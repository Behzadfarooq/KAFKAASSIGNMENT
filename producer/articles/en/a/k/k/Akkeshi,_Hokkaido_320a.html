Akkeshi (厚岸町, -chō) is a town located in Akkeshi District, Kushiro Subprefecture, Hokkaido Prefecture. As of September 30, 2004 it has an estimated population of 12,117 and an area of 734.82 km².

[edit] History

  • 1900 4 towns and 7 villages combine, forming the town of Akkeshi
  • 1955 the south half of the former Ota Village merges with Akkeshi
