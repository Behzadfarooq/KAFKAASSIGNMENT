Akkad Bakkad Bambey Bo is a TV Series aired on Disney Channel in India. The story revolves around the life of a road side vendor and his upmarket friends who he refers to as babua log . The babua log encounter tough situations related to evil mythical creatures who want to take over the world while performing their day to day activities. They are then saved by the vendor who is a ghost himself. The show enjoys a great viewership in India.

[edit] Characters

Natwarlal "Nutty" Prasad Yadav: The main character of the series, he is a ghost who is a vendor who sells vada paav,a popular Indian snack, by profession. He is the one who saves the lives of his friends when they are in trouble. He was originally from Bihar, a province in northern India. He speaks conventional Hindi and very little English. He and his friends live in Mumbai, India.

Arnav: The smartest of Natwarlal's friends, he has many good attributes. He is smart looking, intelligent and stylish.

Caddy: She is a collage goer who is addicted to her looks.

Sheena: She is also a collage goer, though not as addicted to looks as Caddy. She is a Karate expert and challenges enemies bravely.

Vikram: The least intelligent and greediest of Natwarlal's friends, he provides most of the comic scenes, as all jokes are on him. He does mediocre in studies, and his only interest is food.

Dhruv: He appears only in certain episodes in place of Vikram. Unlike Vikram, he is quite intelligent.

Gappu: An evil ghost and natwar's main enemy.


[edit] Showtimes

The show is broadcast at 15:30 hours Indian Standard Time from Monday to friday on Disney Channel India.
