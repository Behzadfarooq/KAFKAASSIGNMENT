Akkhoykumar Boral

Akkhoykumar Boral (1860-1919), was a Bengali poet and writer. He was born in Kolkata.

[edit] Life

Could not continue study but was a fan of Biharilal, as Rabindranath.

[edit] Works

Akkhoykumar Boral was not influenced by Rabi and as a poet, he had an own style.

His literary works were included in the curriculum of school level, secondary and higher secondary Bengali Literature in Bangladesh.

Poetry

  • Prodip
  • Kanakanjoli
  • Shankha
  • Esha

[edit] Reference

Bangla Sahitya (Bengali Literature), the national text book of intermediate (college) level of Bangladesh published in 1996 by all educational boards.
