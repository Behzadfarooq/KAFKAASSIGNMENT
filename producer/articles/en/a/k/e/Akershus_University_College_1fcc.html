The Akershus University College is a medium sized institution of higher education located outside Oslo, the capital city of Norway. The college is owned and run by the Norwegian state. It has about 2000 full time students and a staff of approximately 240.

The college is divided into four faculties:

  • Faculty of Nursing Education
  • Faculty of Product Design
  • Faculty of Social Education
  • Faculty of Technical and Vocational Teacher Education
