Akeem Agbetu

Akeem Agbetu (born March 10, 1988 in Lagos, Nigeria), is a Nigerian football (soccer) player, who's on loan to Kolding FC from FC Midtjylland in the Danish Superliga. Akeem is a quick and aggressive striker loaded with good dribbling skills and technically sound also credited for his entertaining fancy footwork and flamboyant style of play. He is also effective as a midfielder.

Brought from the Nigerian FC Ebedei football academy to Denmark at a young age, Agbetu joined FC Midtjylland's youth academy. He graduated into the club's senior team, where he was assigned the number 35 jersey. At the senior side, he plays alongside fellow Nigerian Oluwafemi Ajilore. He made his debut for Midtjylland in a Royal League match against FC Copenhagen. He has scored 2 goals so far in the Danish Superliga; one against FC Nordsjælland and one against top club Brøndby IF.
