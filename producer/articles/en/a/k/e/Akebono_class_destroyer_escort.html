Akebono class destroyer escort

The Akebono class destroyer escort (or frigate) of the Japanese Maritime Self-Defense Force is the first post World War II Japanese indigenous frigate class, and its primary task is ASW. It is also called B Type escort and it is compared by 9B Type escort (Ikazuchi class destroyer escort). JMSDF chose Ikazuchi class. Only a single unit was built.

  • Displacement: 1075t standard; 1350t full load
  • Armament
    • 2 3 in/50 main guns
    • 4 40 mm anti-aircraft guns
    • 1 hedgehog
    • 8 DCT (K-guns)
    • 1 Depth charge rack
  • Complement: 193
  • Laid down: 1954
  • Launched: October 30, 1955
  • Comissioned: 1956
  • Decomissioned: 1976
  • Stricken: 1981
