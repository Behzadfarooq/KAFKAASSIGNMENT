Akemi Takada

Self portrait of Takada.

Akemi Takada (高田明美 Takada Akemi) is a Japanese artist and illustrator, and was born on March 31 in Tokyo, Japan. She is a popular anime character designer who attended Tama Art University, and then worked for Tatsunoko Pro following her graduation. She is currently a freelance illustrator with her own studio, though she is also a member of the creative group Headgear. She also has her own designer jewelry store called Diakosmos.
