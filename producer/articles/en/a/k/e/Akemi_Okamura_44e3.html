Akemi Okamura

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Akemi Okamura (岡村 明美, Okamura Akemi?, born on March 12, 1969) is a seiyū who works for Mausu Promotion. She has had at least one song appear on the NHK program Minna no Uta.
