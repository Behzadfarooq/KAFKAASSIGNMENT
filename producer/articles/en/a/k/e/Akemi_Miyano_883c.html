Akemi Miyano

Akemi Miyano (宮野明美 Miyano Akemi) is a fictional character in the anime and manga series Case Closed (Detective Conan). Akemi Miyano is Shiho Miyano's elder sister and her only family after their parents (scientists working for the black organization) died.

[edit] Case Closed Manga Volume 2

Akemi is watched by the black organization most of her life. Then she decides to join under the alias Masami Hirota which is her old university professor's name. She helps them with robbery in order to get her little sister, Shiho, back from them. The robbery is a success, however one of her partners takes the money and escapes. In order to find him she hires Richard Moore (Kogoro Mori), pretending to be the man's highschool-aged daughter. The third robber, a giant, strangles their partner in his apartment. Later, Akemi is tricked by the men in black into poisoning the giant. She meets her contacts, Gin and Vodka. Gin reveals that he knows her real name and shoots her. Conan Edogawa arrives too late and she dies in his arms right after he tells her who he really is: Jimmy Kudo (Shin'ichi Kudo). As the police finds a gun besides her, her death is reported to be a suicide.

[edit] Case Closed Manga File 599

It has been recently revealed in the manga that Akemi actually had a relationship with the FBI agent Shuichi Akai while he was undercover as a Black Organization member. She had to undergo a mission in hopes of getting herself out of the organization, but was lied to and killed despite completing, however, it is revealed that she left one last message for before she died asking if they could have a real relationship if she ever escaped the Black Organization.

[edit] Anime

[edit] Episode 13 and Episode 128

While Episode 13 (The Strange Person Murder Hunt Case)is pretty much a faithful adaptation of the manga, the portion involving the mastermind is where it diverges. Rather than being Gin and Vodka, the mastermind behind the murders is a character known as O'Brian. Aside from the fact that the black organization isn't involved, Masami survives the gunshot.

Since Shiho Miyano's appearance is coming up in the anime, the writers had to remake Episode 13 for Episode 128 (The Black Corps One Billion Yen Robbery Case). This time including Akemi Miyano and Gin/Vodka.
