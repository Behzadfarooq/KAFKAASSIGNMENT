Akemi Satō

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Akemi Satō (佐籐 朱美, Satō Akemi?, born May 16, 1980, Akita Prefecture) is a female Japanese seiyū and works for Aoni Production. Akemi Satō also sang a few songs from Fushigi Yugi like the opening theme, Itooshi Hito no Tame ni, the OVA opening themes, Yo Ga Akeru Mae Ni, Star, and some other songs like Kuroi Tsuki, Shiawase ni Narou ne, Destiny, Everlasting Story, and others.

[edit] Voice roles

  • Mizuiro: Kenji Katase (young) (2003)
  • Tsuri Baka Nisshi: Koitarou Hamazaki (2002)
  • Kanon: Shiori Misaka (2002/2006)
  • La corda d'oro: Fuyuumi Shouko (2006)
