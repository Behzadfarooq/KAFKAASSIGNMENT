Akeno Watanabe

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Akeno Watanabe (渡辺 明乃 Watanabe Akeno, born November 18, 1982) is a seiyū who was born in Chiba.

[edit] Notable anime and video game voice roles

  • Ryofu Housen in Ikki Tousen
  • Konami Sasagure in Godannar
  • Fuko Kuzuha in Strawberry Eggs
  • Anna Heart in Kaleido Star
  • Chachamaru Karakuri in Negima
  • Sena Robin in Witch Hunter Robin
  • Tayuya in Naruto
  • Jessica in Hellsing
  • Slur in Rockman EXE Stream
  • Mao in Tales of Rebirth
  • Villetta Nu in Code Geass
  • Ibis Douglas in Super Robot Wars Alpha 2 and Alpha 3
  • Jo in Bakuretsu Tenshi (Burst Angel)
  • Dori & Gura in Utawarerumono
  • Ikazuchi no Fusanoshin in Onmyou Taisenki
  • Taruru in Keroro Gunso (Sgt. Frog)
  • Jen May-Yen, Tomigai Kei in Zegapain
  • Tiamat in Shakugan no Shana
  • 加賀美麗 in Detective School Q
  • Asagi in Witchblade (anime)
  • Densuke Mifune in Eiken
  • Mirei Tachibana in Stratos 4 Advance OAV
  • Mira Kimishima (Nozomi Weaver/Naomi Kimishima) in Trauma Center: Second Opinion

[edit] Trivia

  • Although she does not star in the Get Backers drama CDs, many of her co-stars have. (In Negima, her character's master is voiced by Yuki Matsuoka, who voiced Otowa Madoka. In Witch Hunter Robin and Godannar, a total of 4 co-stars voiced members of the Kiryuudo. Also, her Burst Angel co-star Tanaka Rie voiced Haruki Kaoru.)
  • Reportedly, she is a rather big video game fan, especially of the Tales series and the Super Robot Wars series. Her role in Code Geass playing a soldier with the same name as a major SRW character (Though not one she herself voiced) may have been intended as a private joke.
