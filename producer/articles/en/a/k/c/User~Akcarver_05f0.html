Hello. I am Alan Keith Carver, and I live in Seattle, WA. I was born on August 7, 1962 (age 44). The current time and date is 19:17, 8 April 2007 (UTC). I work for the nation's largest cable communications company. I am a complete and total fan of Apple computers and products, to the point that if you cut me, I bleed six colors.

The vast majority of my edits on Wikipedia are cleanups of redundant links/refs, poor spelling, extraneous spaces, etc.

I got married on March 17, 2007 to Laurie, my very own Sweetie-pumpkin.
