[edit] Featured Picture Habanero photo

  • Congratulations on your Featured Picture Habanero photo (right). Interestingly, your Featured Picture Habanero photo looks very much like a famous wood carving of a man huddled into an almost cube shape. Looking at your Habanero photo, the man's head is the dark orange portion near the stem. You can see his ear reflecting the light. His back makes up the left side of the Habanero. His right arm is folded between his back and his right leg. His face is hidden in his hands by his right leg. His feet are crossed and he is resting on his bottom. The green stem may say something about the portability (controllability?) of a man huddling into himself against grief or sadness. I wanted to add your Habanero photo in a Wikipedia article next to an artwork showing a huddled man, but could not find the huddled man photo. This is not the wood carving I am thinking of, but its close and does look like your Habanero photo: link. -- Jreferee 18:25, 14 November 2006 (UTC)
