Akash Arora

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

You have new messages (last change).

Akash Arora is an Indian journalist who started his career with the Times of India, and then worked for several years with New Delhi's English-language newspaper, the Hindustan Times. During his stint at the Hindustan Times he wrote extensively on fashion and cinema.
