Akan languages are those languages belonging to the Kwa language family spoken in Ghana and the Côte d'Ivoire:

  • Agona
  • Ahafo
  • Akan
    • Twi
      • Akuapem Twi
      • Asante Twi (Ashanti)
    • Fante (Fanti, Mfantse)
  • Akyem (Akyem Bosome)
  • Andoh
  • Anyi
  • Asen
  • Attié
  • Baoulé
  • Brong
  • Chakosi
  • Dankyira
  • Guang
  • Kwahu
  • Nzema
  • Sefwi

Apart from Brong, these variants all have written forms in the Roman alphabet.
