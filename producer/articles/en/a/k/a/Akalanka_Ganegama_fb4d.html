Akalanka Ganegama (born Withanaarchchige Chamara Akalanka Ganegama on March 29, 1981 in Colombo) is a Sri Lankan cricketer. He is a right-handed batsman and a right-arm medium-fast bowler who played in two One-day internationals for Sri Lanka and has also played Twenty20 and List A cricket. He plays domestic cricket for Nondescripts.
