Akari Kaida

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Akari Kaida (海田明里, Akari Kaida?) (b. 1974-01-10) is a video game music composer. She has composed music (in full or collaboratively) for Breath of Fire III, Mega Man & Bass, the Mega Man Battle Network series, Dino Crisis, Onimusha 3: Demon Siege, Resident Evil, and Ōkami (under the name of Akari Groves).
