Akasaka Station (赤坂駅, -eki?) is a Tokyo Metro station in the Akasaka district of Minato, Tokyo, Japan.

The station is located adjacent to the headquarters of Tokyo Broadcasting System, Inc. Automated platform and on-board announcements identify the station as Akasaka, Akasaka, TBS-mae.
