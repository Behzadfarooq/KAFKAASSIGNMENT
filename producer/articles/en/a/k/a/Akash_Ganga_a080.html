Akash Ganga, it is the hindi name for Milky way. It is roughly translated in Hindi as "The Ganga of the sky", is the ancient Hindu name for the galaxy as viewed from the Earth.


The name Akash Ganga was adopted by an Indian organization that promotes the use of rainwater harvesting. Many parts of India are without reliable sources of water. Rainwater harvesting during the regular monsoon season can collect water to meet needs throughout the rest of the year.


Akash Ganga is also the name given to the sky diving team of the Indian Air Force (IAF). After having formed in 1987, this team has performed all over the world.
