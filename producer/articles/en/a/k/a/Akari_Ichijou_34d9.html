Akari Ichijou is a character in the Last Blade fighting game series.

[edit] Background

When her older sister, Hikari, fell to a disease, Akari became very agitated. At that time, their father called for her. She restlessly sat before her grim-faced father, who was about to give her a lecture about Hell's Gate. Akari found out that Hell's Gate was causing trouble again, and flew out of the room, bent on finding the perfect cure for her sister's disease on her own: sealing Hell's Gate. Her father accused her of not listening to what he said, but she was not home.

[edit] Gameplay

Akari's style is characterised by a number of unique teleport moves. These allow her to appear from the left or right of the screen, and also from above the opponent. After the initial first hit, she is able to link into a variety of rolls and stomps, each of which hit in very different ways. She also possesses an infinite combo, one which many consider to be "cheap", because of how easy it is to do. Luckily, she must be able to corner her opponent before she's able to do this.

[edit] Trivia

  • At 13 years of age, Akari is the youngest character in Last Blade series.
  • Holding start after doing her falling sweep will allow her to sleep on the ground.
  • For some reason, many characters are able to hit Akari with special moves even when she's on the ground.
  • In LB1-2, if Akari is not fighting Juzoh, Juzoh can be seen in Akari's stage.
  • Akari is the only character that doesn't get killed after being hit by a fatal attack. At first, it looks as though she did get brutally killed, but it seems that Akari was able to replace herself with a substitute (which is made of 2 paper charms). It is believed that SNK didn't want a fatal demise for such a young girl and made her death animations comical, even though the other female characters, such as Yuki and Hibiki and even the elderly Okina can actually be killed.
    • After being heavily slashed while in the air, the substitute splits in half and the charms are revealed. Afterwards, a portal opens up and the real Akari falls out of it (she appears to be unconscious, not dead). The same thing happens when she is stabbed on the ground or in the air.
    • If she is heavily slashed while on the ground, Akari will fall to her knees and seems to be split in half, but the charms are revealed and the real Akari appears at the side of the screen (the side in which her copy was on) and stick her tongue out at the opponent and runs away.
