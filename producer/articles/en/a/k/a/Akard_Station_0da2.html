Akard Station is a DART light rail station located in the City Center District of downtown Dallas, Texas on Pacific Avenue, between Akard and Field Streets. It opened on 14 June 1996 and is a station on the Red and Blue lines, serving Elm Place, One Main Place, Renaissance Tower, Thanks-Giving Square and Thanks-Giving Tower.
