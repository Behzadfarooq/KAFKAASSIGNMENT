I am a Computer Programmer living in West Sussex, England and working in London. I have been a Wikipedia editor since 23rd January 2004, and Wikipedia reader for much longer.

My main interests lie in the areas of computers, cars, and heavy metal/rock music.

I am a born-again Evangelical Christian. On www.politicalcompass.org, I score as follows: Economic Left/Right: -0.75, Social Libertarian/Authoritarian: -2.26

I am the founder and a member of the WikiProject:Automobiles. I also work on important articles for the WikiProject: Missing articles, and random other stuff, as it takes my fancy. A recent project is Fort Nelson, Portsmouth and my pet hate is the Crawley Fastway. I am Commons:User:akadruid.

If you wish to contact me, please use the email address thedruid*thedruid,co,uk. Please refer to Wikipedia when contacting me. I am also available on ICQ #7849476 or MSN akadruid*hotmail,com, although I do not accept messages from people not on my list, so please send a contact request mentioning Wikipedia.
