I am starting a list on this page of all the car articles that I have discovered to have been overwritten with complete new (probably copy-vio) articles.

These overwrites are all similar in appearance, and are probably taken from the same source. They are all split into model series, with titles in capital letters. In each case, the entire article is overwritten. In each case, the edit is done by an anonymous IP address. The articles are not encycopedic, being somewhat POV. My guess is that they are taken from a car magazine website somewhere but I have not been able to track down the source.
