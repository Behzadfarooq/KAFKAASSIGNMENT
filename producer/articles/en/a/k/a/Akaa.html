Akaa
Akaa (Finland)

Akaa is a town in Pirkanmaa, Finland. It was created on January 1, 2007 when the towns of Toijala and Viiala were united into a single town.

The town has a population of 13,795 (2007) and covers an area of 115.38 km² of which 13.6 km² is water. The population density is 119.6 inhabitants per km².
