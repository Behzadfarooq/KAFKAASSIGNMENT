[edit] Main characters

[edit] Micaiah

A wielder of Light Magic, Micaiah (ミカヤ, Mikaya?) is one of two main characters of the first part of the game.

Micaiah can speak in Galldr, otherwise known as the "Ancient Tongue" and only spoken by members of the Heron tribe in Fire Emblem: Path of Radiance.[1] The reason for is that she is shown to be a Branded, with one of her ancestors being a Heron.

[edit] Sothe

Sothe is one of two main characters of the first part of the game. He was the first of the Path of Radiance cast announced to return. His starting class is "Rogue". His weapon in the video is a knife called Kard, which was assumed to be his unique weapon, but is just a B Rank knife. Sothe also has a new skill, "Protection," which allows him to take damage for a chosen ally. Sothe is Micaiah's younger brother, although not by blood. He force promotes into Epison in the fourth section of the game.

[edit] Ike

Ike as seen in the Japanese TV Ad

Recent scans and a Japanese commercial confirm that Ike (the main character of Path of Radiance) is in fact returning for Goddess of Dawn. His class is Hero, or Brave and he is equipped with Alondite, which is a different sword to the one used by the Black Knight (which was called Ettard in the Japanese version). The aforementioned trailer also shows the return of the legendary sword Ragnell. He is the main character of the third part. Ike force promotes into Vangaurd.

[edit] Elincia

Recent scans confirmed that the game would be divided into different parts, with Elincia as the main character of the second part. Her class is listed as being Queen Crimea. Her role in the plot is to quell a rebellion in Crimea, partially started by corrupt nobles.

[edit] Returning characters

Spoiler warning: Plot and/or ending details follow.
  • Ilyana
A stat sheet for Ilyana recently surfaced. It has her class listed as 'Thunder Mage.' She joins in Micaiah's chapter, being the second returning character to join after Sothe.
  • Tauroneo
A recent trailer shows the return of Tauroneo, still at Lv. 14. But, it also shows that Tauroneo has 38 HP, 10 HP less compared to Path of Radiance, when he had 48 HP. His class is Glory Dux.
  • Zihark
The same trailer shows Zihark, apparently as a Swordmaster with 30 HP. A strange fact is that Myrmidons have 100 Hit on him, which is a very uncommon thing to happen with Swordmasters. The Myrmidon also had 42 Crit, which is likely obtained from the Killing Edge (30), the class bonus (5) and from a limit of 14 Skill (7). This, of course, does not factor in Zihark's Luck stat. However, he may become as good as in FE PoR considering that a unit can promote twice, as opposed to once.
  • Jill
A Japanese magazine recently released a new issue featuring an older Jill, clad with new armor and longer hair. Her class is listed as Dragon Knight (localized as Wyvern Rider), although she now fights initially with axes.
  • Tormod
Recent scans confirmed the return of Tormod, Muarim's beorc friend. He appears as a 'Fire Sage.' He joins Micaiah's team alongside Muarim.
  • Muarim
An ally Beast Laguz was seen in a screenshot in a Japanese ad. Although it was not confirmed to be Muarim, the character bears striking similarities to him, and is referred to as Muarim by most fans. In the screenshot, he uses his fist to uppercut the enemy soldier while in human form, which may mean Laguz can fight outside of their animal forms in the game. Like Caineghis, he was also equipped with the "Fang" weapon. A recent scan confirmed that this character is indeed Muarim.
  • Marcia

"Marcia, a Pegasus Knight and sister of Makalov, joins Elincia's part as one of the starting units.

  • Nealuchi
Nealuchi, the elderly Raven advisor to Naesala, is a playable character, joining in the second chapter alongsiude Leanne.
  • Leanne
Leanne, Reyson's sister and princess of Serenes, is a playable unit this time around.
  • Haar
In a recent scan from the character select screen, Haar can be seen with the Greil mercenaries. He joins Elinica's part.
  • Geoffrey and Lucia
Geoffrey and Lucia are also slated to return and are in Elincia's story. Lucia returns as a swordmaster, and Geoffrey's class is "Glory Knight." A recent trailer [2] depicted an execution of Lucia at the hands of soldiers in white armor. She is saved by Ike soon after. Geoffrey appears in a second trailer, attacking an enemy in white armor with a new weapon called a Long Lance. Both Lucia and Geoffrey act as the 'main character' for one chapter each for Elincia's team.
  • Nephenee
Nephenee appears in Elincia's part, with her and Brom acting as the 'main characters' for a chapter. She is a promoted Halberdier and her class can equip Long Lances too.
  • Brom
In a recent scan, Brom can be seen in Tibarn's group. He and Nephenee join Elincia's part.
  • Lethe and Mordecai
Mordecai and Lethe both come into the story, readily available, in the part 2.
  • Astrid, Kieran, and Makalov
In a recent scan, Astrid, Kieran, and Makalov can be seen in Tibarn's group. They are part of Elincia's chapter.
  • Devdan
A Japanese trailer of the game confirmed the return of the peace-loving halberdier Devdan. In the trailer, he is seen performing a critical hit against an enemy of the Myrmidon class. For an unexplained reason, Devdan's name was in reverse Japanese hirigana, effectively giving the equivalent spelling of Nadved. In Path of Radiance, Nadved is a picture Devdan drew and voiced in a support conversation with Largo. In the character relations chart it shows two Devdans one being Devdan and the other being Nadved. It showed them as being the same person with a question mark in it.
  • Calill
Calill, a sage, is seen in the same trailer as Devdan. It has been confirmed that she's married to Largo, the berserker of Path of Radiance who now owns a pub and is retired from fighting, and has an adopted daughter name Amy.
  • Titania
The deputy commander of the Greil mercenaries in Path of Radiance. Her class is Great Knight.
  • Soren
Soren appeared in a cut scene assisting other Griel mercenaries defeat the Crimean revolutionaries. He is confirmed to be the son of Ashnard, the mad king of Daein (very likely). Also, he is the son of Amrita, making him related to Kurthnaga and Rajaion. Under set conditions, at the end, Soren is reunited with his mother who identifies him as her son.
  • Oscar, Boyd and Rolf
In a recent scan from the character select screen, Oscar, Boyd and Rolf, the three brothers, can be seen with the other Greil mercenaries. They also appeared in a cut scene assisting the Griel mercenaries defeating what Crimean revolutionists. Oscar is a 'Glory Knight', Boyd a Warrior, and Rolf a Sniper. Rolf still has his personal bow (although anybody can use it now), aptly called "Rolf's Bow".
  • Mist
In recent picture Mist is seen talking to Ike with Titania. She is also shown in a picture with Soren and Titania. Her class is Cleric (Like she was in Path of Radiance). However, she can wield both swords and staves and she comes with her own sword, Florette.
  • Gatrie
Gatrie, the gullible Knight from Path of Radiance, was recently revealed as a returning character. His class in the scan translates as 'Marshell,' This is a third promotion class for Generals. He actually starts off as a 'Glory Dux', or 'Lance General'.
  • Shinon
Gatrie's friend, Shinon, is also slated to return. His class in the scan is 'Divine Archer' or 'Sagittary', but actually starts off as a Sniper. He has 32 Skill, which appears to cap at 40.
  • Rhys
Rhys, a healer in Path of Radiance, joins as a Bishop alongside the other Griel Mercenaries.
  • Mia
A popular Myrmidon in Path of Radiance, Mia now appears as a Swordmaster, equipped with the Wo Dao sword. She is now a member of the Griel Mercenaries.
  • Ranulf
In the opening scene on the Japanese website of the game, Ranulf could be seen standing next to Schriml. He is on the Laguz side in the battle with Begnion, and joins Ike's group in part 3 chapter 3.
  • Reyson
In a recent update of the Japanese website (the Beginner's Guide), Reyson and Leanne are pictured below Rafiel in a chart. Reyson joins Ike's part.
  • Janaff and Ulki
Janaff and Ulki are seen in Tibarn's group on the character select screen. They join Ike's team in the third part.
  • Sigrun and Tanith
Sigrun and Tanith, the commanders of the Holy Guard, fight on the player's side in Ike's part.
  • Sanaki
In recent scans, Apostle Sanaki can be seen on a character relations chart. She escapes Begnion when the senate seizes control of the Empire. She has recently been confirmed to fight in the game, she has been seen as a third tier class character with her class being Empress. It has been confirmed that she fights on your side later in the game along with Sigrun and Tanith and she is also Micaiah's younger sister.
  • Tibarn
During the opening scene of the game, Tibarn is seen jumping off a cliff transforming into a hawk who proceeds to attack the Begnion general Zelgius. He does indeed become a blue unit before the end of the game.
On the character select screen, he is seen leading Elincia's group.
  • Naesala
Naesala appears in the opening video alongside Reyson and Leanne. His role in the story is unknown, except that he is a blue unit in the game.
  • Stefan
Stefan's stat sheet was recently revealed, in addition to a screenshot of him. Like in the last game, he can only be recruited if a certain character stands in a certain area of the map. This time, he only appears before Micaiah. His starting luck appears to be much higher than in Path of Radiance.
  • Bastian
Bastian appears as a playable character late in the game alongside Volke. He starts off as an 'Archsage', a third-tier class.
  • Volke
Volke has been confirmed to return later in the game as the Assassin class.
  • Giffca
Giffca was seen talking to Ranulf and Ike in part three of the game. It has been comfirmed that the black lion will be playable in part 4 of the game.
  • Caineghis
One of the trailers[3] revealed a scene in which Caineghis, the king of Gallia, was engaged in combat with Micaiah. This was proven to be just a demonstration of the game's combat system becuase it has been comfirmed that Caineghis will join in part 4 of the game. He also appears in the opening movie alongside Renning.
It is interesting to note that Caineghis does not use the Beast Laguz weapon of the previous game, Claw, in the clip. Rather, his weapon is a set of teeth, sometimes dubbed "Fang."
  • Renning
Renning was supposedly killed in the fall of Crimea in Path of Radiance, but he appears in the opening video alongside Caineghis. He has been confirmed to be one of Ashnard's "Four Riders", Bertram. He also has been confirmed to be playable near the end of the game.
  • Kurthnaga
Kurthnaga, appears in the opening video. He is is a Partner Unit in an early chapter and will become playable in the final chapter.
  • Ena
Ena appears in the opening video alongside Deghinsea and Kurthnaga. her role in the story is unknown. She sides with you, however, as opposed to Deghinsea.
  • Nasir
Nasir, the white dragon and Ena's grandfather, has been confirmed to return later in the game as an enemy beside the boss of one of the final chapters, the black dragon king Deghinsea. He will join the hero's army later if he is not killed in the chapter.
  • Gareth
The dragon prince's bodyguard. He sides with the dragon king Deghinsea when you battle him.
  • Izuka
A recent scan shows Izuka, a scholar who worked for the late King Ashnard to develop biological weapons, resulting in the Feral Ones. He is also seen talking to Sothe and Micaiah in one screenshot. He places emphasis on Micaiah's skill, "Healing Hand."
  • Daniel and Aimee
Daniel, a merchant who can forge custom weapons, is seen in the same trailer as Calill and Devdan. He still seems to forge custom weapons for the player. Aimee, also a merchant, can be seen as a Partner Unit in one of the early chapters. The rest of Daniel and Aimee's merchant group (consisting of Muston and Daniel's twin brother Jorge) are confirmed to return.
  • Anna
Anna, who has appeared in many Fire Emblem games before (usually as a game-suspender and tutorial giver), was shown in a recent trailer giving a tutorial.
  • Oliver
Duke Oliver Tanas, despite being supposedly killed in the previous game, appears again in Goddess of Dawn. His class is 'Saint', the promotion for Bishop. Surprisingly, he can actually be recruited, but only in Hard or Maniac mode.
  • Largo
Largo, Path of Radiance's only playable beserker, returns as a non-playable character. He is married to Calill, and they have an adopted daughter named Amy. He is the only playable character from Path of Radiance who is not recruitable in this game.
  • Lekain and Hetzel
Two nobles seen briefly in Path of Radiance, they appear as major antagonists and enemy bosses.
  • Rajaion
The game has comfirmed that Rajaion died at the end of Path of Radiance.
  • Zelgius
"The Black Knight... Zelgius!"
Unmasked by Ike in the final chapter, the former Rider of Daein returns in Goddess of Dawn to lead the Begnion Army. He appears to serve Sephiran selflessly, everything he does he does because Sephiran tells him to. One of the branded, it's theorized that Zelgius is Sephiran's son. The game does not confirm this, nor is it ever proven false. He appears as a playable unit in a few select chapters in his guise as the Black Knight.
  • Deghinsea
King Deghinsea appears in the opening video. It has been comfirmed that he is one of the bosses in the final chapter.
  • Sephiran
Sephiran, the prime minister of Begnion and Duke Persis, can be seen on a character relations chart. His role in the story is crucial in the game and he has been comfirmed to be one of the last bosses. It has been comfirmed that he is the mastermind behind the Crimea-Daein war. He has also been confirmed to be the Heron,Lehran, and may fight on your side after he is beaten if Ike battles against the Black Knight in a certain chapter.
  • Ashera
The goddess of Tellius, worshipped by the Begnion Empire. She sleeps in the Guiding Tower in Begnion and she is known to be the final boss.

[edit] New characters

  • エディ - Eddie
Eddie was assumed by some to be a Myrmidon (most likely playing a similar role to Mia in battle), a potential return of the Mercenary class (which was briefly retired in Path of Radiance), or another Lord character (based on his apparent importance in one gameplay video). The former of these three was proven when a recent scan showed his class as Swordfighter (localized as Myrmidon).
  • ノイス - Noys/Noyce
Noyce is a mustached Fighter with red hair. In an older scan, he can be seen as an enemy, but recent screenshots imply he is an ally from the start. Leader of the Dawn Group.
  • レオナルド - Leonardo
Leonardo is a blond Archer with long hair who joins early in the game.
  • ローラ - Laura
Laura is a Priest, and is another member of the group. As with Leonard and Noyce, little else is known. In the chapter in which she was featured, the objective is to escort her to safety, so she may only officially join the "Dawn Group" at a later date. She is a childhood friend of Brad.
  • オルグ - Org
Org is the only non-noble useable unit of the Wolf Laguz. He has the skill demi that keeps him in beast form. Recruited from a information conversation in Part 1 Chapter 5.
  • スクリミル - Skrimir
In a recent trailer of the opening scene[4] a Lion was seen commanding a Laguz army. This Lion was later revealed as Caineghis' nephew, Schriml. He thinks lowly of the beorcs' wisdom and sorcery and will become playable in part 4.
  • ラフィエル - Rafiel
A member of the believed to be wiped out Heron Tribe, which means a probable relation to Reyson and Leanne from Path of Radiance or the heron parent of Micaiah. During the Serenes Massacre he was captured, but escaped to the Desert of Death via Daein. He is an ally.
  • ニケ - Nike
The Queen of a new type of Laguz, the Wolf Tribe. She and her tribe reside in the Desert of Death in the east of Tellius.
  • ペレアス - Pelleas
A youth who holds the key to the revival of the Kingdom of Daein. He is seen holding a dark magic tome. He was claimed to be the son of Ashnard and can even become playable after the first playthrough.
  • ヘザー - Heather
A young Rogue. She is a playable unit in Elincia's story and is the second playable Epison.
  • フリーダ - Frieda
Frieda is a young lance knight. Her father was at one time one of the Four Riders, who apparently died around the time Ashnard took the throne. Frieda was to take his place, but she refused, believing Ashnard had assassinated him.
  • メグ - Meg
Meg is one of Brom's daughters. Her class is 'Sword Armour.'
  • ブラッド - Brad
Brad is a new character of the soldier class. He is a childhood friend of Laura.
  • リィレ - Lyre
Lyre is a Cat laguz. She is also the younger twin sister of Lethe who is discussed in a support conversation between Ranulf and Lethe in Path of Radiance.
  • キサ - Kisa
Kisa is a Tiger laguz and a member of the laguz army. He appears to be one of Ranulf's subordinates.
  • Bize
A member of the Raven Tribe, she is recruited when Tormod is recruited. Only playable female Raven.
