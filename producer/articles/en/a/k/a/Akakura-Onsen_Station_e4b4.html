Akakura-Onsen Station (赤倉温泉駅) is a JR East railway station located in Mogami, Yamagata Prefecture, Japan.

[edit] History

  • 1917-11-01: Station begins operation as Tomizawa Station.
  • 1952-11-15: Station name is changed to Uzen-Akakura Station
  • 1999-12-04: Station name is changed to Akakura-Onsen Station
