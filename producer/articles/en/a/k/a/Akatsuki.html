Akatsuki, 暁, meaning "dawn" in Japanese, may refer to any of the following classes of Japanese naval destroyers:

  • The early 20th century Akatsuki class of destroyers.
  • The early 20th century Akatsuki class destroyer, built in 1901.
  • The mid 20th century Akatsuki class of destroyers.
  • The mid 20th century Akatsuki class destroyer, built in 1932.

It may also refer to:

  • Akatsuki, a Japanese sleeper train operated between Kyoto and Nagasaki.
  • Akatsuki, a fictional organization of villains in the anime and manga series Naruto.
  • ORB-01 Akatsuki, a fictional weapon from the anime series Mobile Suit Gundam SEED Destiny.
  • Sword of Akatsuki, a fictional weapon from the anime series My-Otome.
  • Teruko Akatsuki, a singer from the 1950s. [1]
  • Akatsuki (Website), a popular music sharing community and website.
