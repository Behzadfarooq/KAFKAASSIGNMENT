Akatsuki makes its move (暁、始動, Akatsuki, shidō?) is episode 2 of Naruto: Shippūden, the new name for the anime series Naruto. It is the second episode of the first season.

[edit] Plot

Spoiler warning: Plot and/or ending details follow.

Jiraiya explains to Kakashi Hatake what he has learned about Akatsuki and that his predictions about their activities have been correct. Later, Naruto Uzumaki and Haruno Sakura are challenged by Kakashi with a bell test, in which they must retrieve two bells from Kakashi's waist, at the same training ground where they became Genin in the fourth episode of the original Naruto. The purpose behind the test is to determine the degree to which Naruto and Sakura's skills have improved after two and a half years. As Naruto and Sakura prepare for battle, Kakashi puts his book away, explaining that he believes he should take the fight seriously. To emphasize this, he reveals his Sharingan. And all of the other 8 rookies have graduated to chunnin except naruto

Meanwhile, Akatsuki sends two members, Deidara and Sasori, to Sunagakure in an attempt to capture Gaara, who is now Kazekage, with the help of their sleeper agent Yura.
