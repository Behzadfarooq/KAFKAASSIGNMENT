Akaki Devadze (born 28 November 1971) is a retired Georgian football goalkeeper.

He played for the Georgian national team between 1992 and 2005, and was capped 20 times.

[edit] Clubs

  • 1989-1990 : FC Dila Gori
  • 1990-1991 : FC Iberia Tbilisi
  • 1991-1995 : FC Shevardeni Tbilisi
  • 1995-1996 : Rostselmash Rostov-na-Donu
  • 1996-1997 : Hapoel Taibe
  • 1997-2000 : FC Ashdod
  • 2000-2002 : FC Merani-91 Tbilisi
  • 2003-2006 : FC Spartaki Tbilisi
