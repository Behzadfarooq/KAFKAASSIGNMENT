Akaltara is a town and a nagar panchayat in Janjgir-Champa district in the state of Chhattisgarh, India.

[edit] Geography

Akaltara is located at 22.02° N 82.43° E[1]. It has an average elevation of 283 metres (928 feet).

[edit] Demographics

As of 2001 India censusGRIndia, Akaltara had a population of 20,373. Males constitute 52% of the population and females 48%. Akaltara has an average literacy rate of 69%, higher than the national average of 59.5%; with 59% of the males and 41% of females literate. 14% of the population is under 6 years of age.
