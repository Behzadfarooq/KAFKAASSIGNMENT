Akamaru Unleashed! Who's Top Dog Now? (Japanese: 赤丸参戦!!負け犬はどっちだ？, - Akamaru sansen!! Makeinu wa docchi da?) is episode 44 of the anime Naruto and is a main storyline episode.

[edit] Episode Synopsis

Spoiler warning: Plot and/or ending details follow.

The 6th match is over and the wall screen has decided that for the 7th round in the preliminary match, Naruto will go up against Kiba. Though Kiba thinks that it'll be an easy fight against a goof ball like Naruto, Naruto intends to prove everyone that they shouldn't underestimate him. Kiba uses Four Legs Technique and knocks Naruto down and tells the examiner that he'll be unconsious for a while before Naruto gets up.

Kiba is able to effectively use a number of techniques, all of which pretty much involve acting like a wild beast, to really push Naruto to the corner. Kiba then sends Akamaru at him and it appears that even Kiba's dog can beat Naruto up. Kiba stretches out his arms so that he can catch Akamaru in a hug but strangely, Akamaru bites Kiba. Everyone is shocked at this as Kiba and Akamaru had a long friendship until Akamaru changes into Naruto, spitting and saying: Ugh! You taste like a dog! And the Naruto on the floor is shown getting up and holding a bruised and beaten Akamaru. Kiba feeds Akamaru a soldier pill which turns his skin red and uses Beast Human Clone and then does Double Piercing Fang. However, Naruto is not one to give up, even though it seems clear that Kiba will be the winner.

[edit] Trivia

  • The Japanese title of this episode literally translates to Akamaru joins the fight! Who is the underdog?.
  • Goof: In a flashback back to a time when Naruto had not yet become a Genin (as he failed to do the replication technique properly in it), he nevertheless wears a Konohana forehead protector. He is also shown trying to walk up a tree which he does not learn until kakashi shows team 7 when they are in the land of the waves.
