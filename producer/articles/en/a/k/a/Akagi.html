Akagi means (赤城) "red castle," (赤木) "red tree," or (赤来) "red future" in the Japanese language, and may refer to:

  • Mount Akagi, a volcano in the Kantō region of Japan
  • Akagi, Gunma, a village in Gunma Prefecture
  • Akagi, Shimane (赤来町), a former town in Shimane Prefecture
  • Kei Akagi, a jazz pianist
  • Akagi, a manga by Nobuyuki Fukumoto
  • Takenori Akagi and Haruko Akagi, two sibling characters from the basketball manga and anime Slam Dunk (manga)
  • Dr. Ritsuko Akagi, a character from the anime TV series Neon Genesis Evangelion
  • Two ships of the Imperial Japanese Navy:
    • Japanese gunboat Akagi, fought in the Sino-Japanese War
    • Japanese aircraft carrier Akagi, fought in World War II
  • A Japanese surname
