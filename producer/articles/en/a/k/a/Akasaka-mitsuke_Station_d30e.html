Akasaka-mitsuke Station (赤坂見附駅, -eki?) is a Tokyo Metro station located in Minato, Tokyo, Japan.

[edit] Lines

  • Tokyo Metro Ginza Line(G-05)
  • Tokyo Metro Marunouchi Line(M-13)

The station is also connected by underground passageways to Nagatacho, which is served by the Tokyo Metro Yurakucho Line, Tokyo Metro Hanzomon Line and Tokyo Metro Namboku Line, and it is possible to transfer between the two stations without crossing through the ticket gates.

[edit] Station Layout

There are 2 island platforms, 4-way. The Ginza Line and Marunouchi Line share platforms at this station.

1 ○Ginza Line Shibuya
2 ○Marunouchi Line Shinjuku ・ Ogikubo
3 ○Ginza Line Ginza ・ Ueno ・ Asakusa
4 ○Marunouchi Line Ginza ・ Ikebukuro
