Aka Pella is a fictional character created for the Warner Bros. animated series Histeria! Aside from being the only African-American member of the Kid Chorus, she also stands out as being the only one of them who regularly wears long pants (not unlike Yakko Warner of Animaniacs) with her shirt tucked into it (as opposed to the others letting their shirts hang out), and shoes that are not red and white.

Aka's personality trait is her sassy lingo, often delivering comedically-timed insults to whoever she is currently hanging out with. She is also clearly the best of the Kid Chorus' singers, as she was able to sing a lengthy solo in one particular song.

Compared to her companions, Aka's role on Histeria! was quite minor. Most of her bigger roles came up near the end of the show's run, where she was shown making fun of General Custer at his last stand, and having spitball wars with Froggo during Rosa Parks' bus ride. (Incidentally, she is often a love interest to Froggo in fanfiction.)

Aka's name is obviously a pun on a capella.

Aka's voice was provided by Cree Summer, who was also the voice of Elmyra Duff and fellow African-American Mary Melody on Tiny Toon Adventures.
