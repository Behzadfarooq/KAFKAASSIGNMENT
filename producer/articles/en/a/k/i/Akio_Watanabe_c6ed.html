Akio Watanabe

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Coverart of a Pokopii dōjinshi, illustrated by Poyoyon♡Rock.

Akio Watanabe (渡辺明夫, Watanabe Akio?) is a Japanese animator, illustrator, character designer and animation director. As an illustrator and character designer he often uses the pseudonym Poyoyon♡Rock (ぽよよん♡ろっく Poyoyon♡Rokku). He is also a member of the dōjinshi circle Pokopii.
