Akio Nojima (野島 昭生, Nojima Akio?, born April 6, 1945 in Tokyo, Japan) is a Japanese seiyu (voice actor). He is the father of Hirofumi Nojima and Kenji Nojima and is employed by talent firm agency Sigma Seven.
