Akiko Koumoto (河本明子, Kōmoto Akiko?) is a Japanese voice actress.

[edit] Notable voice roles

  • Midna in The Legend of Zelda: Twilight Princess
  • Cammy, Juni and Juli in Street Fighter Alpha 3
