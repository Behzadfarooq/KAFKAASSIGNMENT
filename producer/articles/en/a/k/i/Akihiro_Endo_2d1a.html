Akihiro Endo

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Akihiro Endo (born 18 September 1975) is a Japanese football player who currently plays for Vissel Kobe of the J-League.

[edit] Clubs

  • 1994-2005: Yokohama F. Marinos
  • 2005-present: Vissel Kobe
