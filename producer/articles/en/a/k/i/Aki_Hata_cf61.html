Aki Hata

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Aki Hata (畑 亜貴, Hata Aki?, born August 13 in Tokyo) is a japanese musician and lyricist. She is noted for having penned and composed songs for various anime and games, including popular titles like Azumanga Daioh, Haibane Renmei and Suzumiya Haruhi no Yūutsu, and for singers and seiyū. She has even composed game soundtracks; formerly, Hata was a composer for Konami games.

Additionally, Aki Hata also sings herself. She is a member of the band Tsukihiko, providing the vocals and keyboard.
