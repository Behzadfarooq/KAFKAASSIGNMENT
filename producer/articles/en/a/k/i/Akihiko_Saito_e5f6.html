Akihiko Saito

Akihiko Saito was a Japanese security guard who was taken hostage by the Jaish Ansar al-Sunna in Iraq in 2005, and later died in captivity of wounds he had received in an earlier gunbattle. Mr. Saito was a retired French Foreign Legionnaire with a distinguished combat career.
