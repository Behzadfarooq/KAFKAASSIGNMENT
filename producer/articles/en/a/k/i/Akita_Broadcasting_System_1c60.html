Akita Broadcasting System, Inc. (ABS, 株式会社秋田放送) is a Japanese broadcasting station in Akita Prefecture. It broadcasts many TV programs and many radio programs. It is a network radio station of Japan Radio Network (JRN) and National Radio Network (NRN), and a network TV station of Nippon News Network (NNN) and Nippon TV Network System (NNS).
