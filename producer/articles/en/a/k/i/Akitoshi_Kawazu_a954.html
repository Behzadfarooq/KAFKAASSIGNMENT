Akitoshi Kawazu

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Akitoshi Kawazu (河津 秋敏, Kawazu Akitoshi?, born 1962) is a Japanese game producer who was born in Kumamoto Prefecture. He is the creator of the SaGa console role-playing game series and is now an Executive Producer of Square Enix.
