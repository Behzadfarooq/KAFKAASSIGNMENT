Akiko Kimura (木村 亜希子 Kimura Akiko) is a seiyū whose birthday is June 28, 1970 in Fukui.

[edit] Notable voice roles

  • Yoko Sagisawa in Mai-HiME
  • Ninomai Kisaragi in Happy Lesson
  • Biske in Hunter × Hunter
  • Rockman.EXE in Rockman EXE series
  • Miroku Yukihiko in Get Backers
  • Aoko Aozaki in Shingetsutan Tsukihime
  • Anabel(Lila) in Pokémon Advanced Generation
  • Ann Tachibana in Prince of Tennis
  • Akane Hoshikawa in Ryusei no Rockman
  • Iris in Outlaw Star
