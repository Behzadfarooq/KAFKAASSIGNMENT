Akizuki class destroyer

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Akizuki class destroyer of the Japanese Maritime Self-Defense Force is the successor to the old Murasame class destroyer. Like its predecessor, the primary task is ASW.

  • Displacement: 2350t standard; 2890t normal
  • Armament:
    • 3 5in/54 main guns
    • 4 3in/50 guns
    • 1 Mk.108 Weapon Alfa,
    • 2 hedgehog
    • 2 DCT
    • 2 ASW torpedo
    • 4 533 mm torpedo tubes (8 torpedoes)
    • 2 Depth charge rack
  • Complement: 330
