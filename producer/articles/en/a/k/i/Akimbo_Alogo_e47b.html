Akimbo Alogo is the first full-length solo album from Canadian singer and guitarist Kim Mitchell. It includes the only song of his to become a hit in the United States, "Go For Soda".

[edit] Track listing

  1. "Go For Soda" (Kim Mitchell, Pye Dubois) – 3:28
  2. "That's A Man" (Mitchell, Dubois) – 3:47
  3. "All We Are" (Mitchell, Dubois) – 4:45
  4. "Diary For Rock 'n' Roll Men" (Mitchell, Dubois) – 4:14
  5. "Love Ties" (Mitchell, Dubois) – 4:18
  6. "Feel It Burn" (Mitchell, Johnson) – 4:14
  7. "Lager And Ale" (Mitchell, Dubois) – 4:05
  8. "Rumour Has It" (Mitchell, Dubois) – 4:09
  9. "Caroline" (Mitchell, Johnson, Dubois) – 3:40
  10. "Called Off" (Mitchell, Dubois) – 4:57

[edit] Personnel

  • Kim Mitchell - lead guitar, vocals
  • Peter Fredette - rhythm guitar, keyboards, vocals
  • Robert Sinclair Wilson - bass, keyboards, vocals
  • Paul Delong - drums
  • Todd Booth - keyboards and lead synthesizer
  • Pye Dubois - lyrics
