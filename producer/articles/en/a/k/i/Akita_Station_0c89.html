Akita Station (秋田駅, Akita Eki?) is a railway station on the JR East. It is located in Akita, Akita, Japan.

  • Akitaekimae Post office
  • AL☆Ve（Shopping/Hotel complex near the east entrance)
  • Kanto festival (It is a five minute walk from the west entrance)
  • Sensyu park
