Akira Itou (伊藤章 Itō Akira) is a mangaka. Itou draws Yu-Gi-Oh! R while the creator of the Yu-Gi-Oh! franchise, Kazuki Takahashi, comes up with the storyline. Itou's drawing style is similar to Takahashi's. He has also worked with Yoshihiro Takahashi to create the book Fang.
