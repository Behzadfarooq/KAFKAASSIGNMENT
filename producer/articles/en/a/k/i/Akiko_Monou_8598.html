Akiko Monou (桃生 亜希子 Monō Akiko, born 2 April 1976 in Kanagawa, Japan) is a Japanese actress and model.

Monou did spots for, amongst others, Nike, before Hiroyuki Nakano caught sight of her in a video clip and cast her in Samurai Fiction, her film debut. She has since worked with Nakano in his Stereo Future and the short film Slow is Beautiful.
