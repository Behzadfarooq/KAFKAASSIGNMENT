Akihiko Matsui

Akihiko Matsui is a video game developer and battle designer working for Square Enix (previously Square Co., Ltd.). He was notably the director for Chrono Trigger with Yoshinori Kitase and Takashi Tokita.

[edit] Games credited

Akihiko Matsui has been credited on the following games:[1]

  • Final Fantasy IV (1991): battle designer
  • Final Fantasy V (1992: battle planner
  • Chrono Trigger (1995): director
  • SaGa Frontier (1997): game data developer
  • Legend of Mana (1999): game designer & system design chief
  • Final Fantasy XI (2002): battle data designer[2]
