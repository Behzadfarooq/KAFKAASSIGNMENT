Akitsu Station (Hiroshima)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Akitsu Station (安芸津駅, Akitsu Eki?) is a JR West Kure Line station located in Higashihiroshima, Hiroshima Prefecture, Japan. Akitsu Station has two sets of tracks and Side platforms.

Akitsu Station started its operation on February 17, 1935. At this time this station was known as Akimitsu Station (安芸三津駅, Akimitsu Eki?) but on November 20, 1949, this station was renamed to Akitsu Station.
