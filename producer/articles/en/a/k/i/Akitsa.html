Akitsa

Akitsa is a black metal band from St. Andre, Quebec. They now consist of only one member, but were originally a duo. They released the short demo "Totale Servitude" in 1999, which was more of an experiment in various black metal sounds. They pulled it together in 2002 for their first album "Goétie", which was raw black metal with RAC and even punk/oï influence. At this point, drummer Néant left the group, leaving O.T. to take over all instruments. He generally kept the same sound, and Akitsa remained an interesting group in the Quebec black metal underground. The lyrical themes are generally about Quebec nationalism and its History, nature and misanthropy among others.
