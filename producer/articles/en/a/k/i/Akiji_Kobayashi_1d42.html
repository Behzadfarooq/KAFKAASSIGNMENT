Akiji Kobayashi (September 6, 1930—August 27, 1996, born in Tokyo, Japan), sometimes credited as Shōji Kobayashi or Issei Mori, was a Japanese actor. He died of lung cancer at the age of 65.

[edit] Partial flmography

  • 1963 - Youth of the Beast (Yaju no seishun)
  • 1965 - Etsuraku
  • 1970 - Buraikan
  • 1972 - Lone Wolf and Cub: Baby Cart at the River Styx
  • 1979 - Urutoraman
  • 1980 - Shogun Assassin
  • 1981 - Kofuku
  • 1993 - Godzilla Versus Mecha Godzilla
  • 1996 - Gamera Attack of Legion
