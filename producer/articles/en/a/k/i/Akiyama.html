  • Tahiro Akiyama, Japanese biologist
  • Takahiko Akiyama, Japanese film director
  • Tamayo Akiyama, Japanese shojo manga author and artist

  • Toyohiro Akiyama, Japanese TV journalist and cosmonaut

  • Yoshihiro Akiyama, Japanese judoka and mixed-martial artist
