Akiko Kawase (川瀬 晶子, Kawase Akiko?, born June 29, 1980 in Tokyo) is a seiyū.

[edit] Notable voice roles

  • Nattsu in Aishiteruze Baby
  • Yuko Kisaragi in Astro Boy (2003)
  • Mai Moritaka in Dear Boys
  • Miyabi Tsukuyomi in Koi Koi 7
  • Plusle in Pocket Monsters Advanced Generation
  • Micchan in Rozen Maiden Träumend
  • Aoi Mishina in Ryusei Sentai Musumet
