Akihito Terui (born August 27, 1976 in Iwate Prefecture, Japan), also known as Milano Collection A.T., is a Japanese professional wrestler.

[edit] Wrestling facts

Finishing and Signature moves

  • IR — Italian Revolution (Fisherman's suplex with wrist around one leg and other around neck)
  • IR II — Italian Revolution II (Jumping double arm facebuster)
  • A.T. Lock (Chickenwing armlock with headscissors)
  • Paradise Lock (Entaglement submission hold)
  • Emporio Armani Shoe (Middle-rope springboard moonsault press)
  • Armani Shoe Exchange (Middle-rope springboard twisting senton)
  • Handspring back elbow smash
  • Enzuigiri
  • STF / Full nelson combo
  • Split-legged Hold (Milano holds the opponent's legs and head and then performs an arm pull submission)
  • A.T.-Dise Lock (Chickenwing armlock with headscissors hung onto the ropes for leverage)

[edit] Championships and accomplishments

  • 1-time Open the Triangle Gate Champion (with YOSSINO, & Anthony W. Mori)
  • 1-time NWA International Light Heavyweight Champion
  • 2-time UWA Six Man Tag Team Champion (with Brother YASSINI & YOSSINO; with YOSSHINO & Condotti Shuji
  • 1-time ICW-ICWA Texarcana World Television Champion
  • 2000 Young Dragons Cup winner
  • 2002 T2P Strongest League winner
  • 2006 Jeff Peterson Memorial Cup winner
