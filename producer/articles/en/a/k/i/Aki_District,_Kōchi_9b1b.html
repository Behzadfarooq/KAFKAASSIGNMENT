Aki District, Kōchi

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Aki (安芸郡, Aki-gun?) is a district located in Kōchi, Japan.

As of 2003, the district has an estimated population of 21,006 and a density of 37.29 persons per km². The total area is 563.33 km².
