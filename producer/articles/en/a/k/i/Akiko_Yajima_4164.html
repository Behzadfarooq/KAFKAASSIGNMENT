Akiko Yajima (矢島 晶子, Yajima Akiko?), née Akiko Ogasawara (小笠原 晶子 Ogasawara Akiko, born May 4, 1967), is a seiyū who was born in Niigata and raised in Kawasaki, Kanagawa.

[edit] Trivia

She has starred alongside Naoko Matsui in at least 2 anime series.

  • In Mobile Suit Gundam Wing, with Matsui as Dorothy Catalonia and Yajima as Relena Darlian / Peacecraft. Here, their characters were at first antagonists (Relena was a pacifist and Dorothy claimed to love wars), later becoming friends.
  • In Gankutsuou, with Matsui as Madam Danglars and Yajima as Haydee (although their characters have no dialogue together). Also, Haydee share some similarities with Relena.
    • Both girls were orphans.
    • Both were of royal blood, and their kingdoms were destroyed.
    • Both were kind and gentle most of the time, but had expressed strong emotions when angered.
    • Both were petite ladies.
