Akizuki Tanenaga (秋月 種長; 1567 - 1614) was a samurai who served under Kuroda Nagamasa during the Korean campaign. He sided with Ishida Mitsunari at the battle of Sekigahara but managed to keep his fief (which was?) after the battle.
