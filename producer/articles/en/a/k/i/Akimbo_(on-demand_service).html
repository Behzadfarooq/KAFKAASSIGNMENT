Akimbo (on-demand service)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Akimbo Logo

Akimbo is a video on demand system that allows subscribers to download television shows, movies, and other video to a set-top box on demand.

Akimbo's hard-drive-equipped set-top box plugs into a broadband connection and stores the chosen full-resolution programs using Akimbo’s TiVo-like guide. The initial content is a bit eccentric — Turkish-language shows, independent films, British dramas from Granada TV, a lot of skin flicks — but 6ABC CNBC Cartoon Network & VH1 Uno are also among the offerings from its 200 content partners, and more are being added.

Akimbo has released a software version of its on-demand system that will run on any computer running Windows XP Media Center Edition 2005. The software version is compatible with media center extenders such as the Xbox and Xbox 360.

Akimbo has also recently released its new RCA Akimbo Player which is a set top box that offers up to 100 hours of recording and is connected to the Akimbo Video On Demand Service [1].
