Dear fellow 1-E students,

Welcome to this special Wikipedia page entitled "A Tribute to Odeza". In honor of a fellow 1-E friend, colleague and fellow pilot, Odeza Bactin, I have set up this page personally in her honor.

As we all know, Odeza is departing for the last time from the doors of CSA on January 20, 2005 and is, like many Filipinos today, headed for Canada in search of greener pastures. As a fellow migrant myself (although to the United States), it is of my greatest pleasure to congratulate Odeza. She finally gets to experience something that probably many Filipinos might never experience: to go and live in another land, to be immersed in a new culture, to live amongst others.

However, her spirit stands resilient. As fellow pilots, it is our duty and our obligation to commemorate a fellow pilot who will be departing us. It is our responsibility to assure all links and bonds are still strong, and that Odeza, no matter where she goes, is still a part of our big 1-E family.

Therefore, it is my honor to create this tribute. Something that, for future use, will come in handy when we, the 1-E pilots, even when broken, want to remember someone who is a part of all of us.

Odeza, I salute you!

Sincerely,

Joshua Lim

[edit] Instructions

So you like the letter, huh? (or not) Anyway, to leave a tribute, follow these simple steps:

  1. Click on the "Edit this page" tab on the top of this page.
  2. Click on the title "Tributes to Odeza" on the bottom of the edit box. From there (on the last equal sign), press Enter to start a new line.
  3. To insert a title, type in three equal signs, followed by your title, then three more equal signs.
  4. Press Enter and type your tribute. It can be as long as you want it to be.
  5. When finished, you can type in an edit summary (you don't have to)
  6. Finally, click on the "Save page" button on the screen. Before saving, though, you can also see what you have written by clicking on the "Show preview" button before clicking "Save page".

Here are also some guidelines on your tributes what must be followed:

  • When leaving your tribute, please write down your name and (for non-1E students), your year level and section.
  • Also, please be appropriate when leaving your tributes.
  • Finally, vandalism of any kind will not be tolerated. Violators of this policy shall be dealt with according to the Wikipedia guidelines. You can be blocked for up to 24 hours or, if you are a Wikipedian, blocked or banned.

So, going on, that ends the instructions on how to leave tributes. Please remember that Wikipedia is a free resource, and as such, is freely editable. Just be responsible and appropriate please with your tributes.
