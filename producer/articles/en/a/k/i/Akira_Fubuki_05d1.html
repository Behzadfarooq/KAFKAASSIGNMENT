[edit] Questions of rape

Of note is a video circulating various download programs claiming that the actress contained in the movie is Akira Fubuki as she is raped by two male porn stars. When one compares the video to known pictures of her, however, the link is clearly false.[citation needed] The clip makes use of pixelization, a genital obscuring technique Japanese pornographers are required to use before a video can be legally sold in Japan. This would suggest that regardless of the identity of the actress, the "rape" is not real, but a rape fantasy intended for legal sale in Japan.

The actual actress that appears in that video is in fact Japanese porn star Sally Yoshino.
