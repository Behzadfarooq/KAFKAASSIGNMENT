Akita-Shirakami Station

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Akita-Shirakami Station (あきた白神駅, Akita Shirakami Eki?) is a station on the Gonō Line in Hachimine, Akita.

[edit] Station Structure

The station have a platforms with a lines. The have the following uses:

1 ■Gonō Line For.Noshiro, Higashi-Noshiro and Akita
For.Iwadate, Fukaura and Hirosaki
  • Akita-Shirakami Station is a simple consignment station, administered by Noshiro Station, and operated by Hachimine municipal authority, with point-of-sales terminal installed. Ordinary tickets, express tickets, and reserved-seat tickets for all JR lines are on sale.
    • Business hours: 8:30 a.m. - 5:00 p.m.
