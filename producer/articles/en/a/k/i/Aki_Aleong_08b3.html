Aki Aleong

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Aki Aleong (born Assing Aleong, December 19, 1934 in Port of Spain, Trinidad and Tobago) is an American character actor who has also been active in songwriting and musical production. He is probably best known for portraying Senator Hidoshi during the first season of the critically acclaimed series Babylon 5. He owned the Gingham Dog fast food restaurant in Hollywood, California circa 1965.
