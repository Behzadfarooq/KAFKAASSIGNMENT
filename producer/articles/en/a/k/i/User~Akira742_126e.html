[edit] Personal

I began my Wikipedian journey on September 16, 2003, 20:20, modifying Hakata, Fukuoka. My primary contributions have been articles pertaining to Japan, railroads, Southern California, or a combination therein.

I live in Altadena, California, am an undergraduate computer science student at UCLA, and intern during the summer at the Jet Propulsion Laboratory in Pasadena, California.

[edit] Politics

By the measure of The Political Compass, I am -3.75 Economic Left/Right and -4.51 Social Libertarian/Authoritarian, which I would say is an accurate description (left libertarian). What can I say? I'm from Los Angeles County, we're practially bleeding blue here. (For those not from the U.S., that means overall we're very much for the Democratic Party as opposed to the Republican Party, but of course there are exceptions.)
