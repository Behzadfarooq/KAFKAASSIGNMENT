Akividu is a town and Panchayat located in West Godavari district, Andhra Pradesh, India. Geographical co-ordinates are 16°36'North,81°23'East. It is located 437 km from Hyderabad, 288 km from Visakhapatnam, 85 km from Vijayawada and 18 km from Bhimavaram.

[edit] History

Akividu is famous for agriculture (rice paddys), and the fish and prawn export business. This town hadto ponds for fish and prawn exporting business.
