Akira Watase (渡瀬晶) (b. July 2, 1980 in Tokyo, Japan) was a very prolific and well-known Japanese adult film actress. She is still considered one of the most popular stars of the genre, however she retired from the industry in mid-2005. Over the course of her adult film career, which started in 2000, she starred in over 100 releases.

She is most renowned for the adult film Black Semen in L.A. (2002), which was shot in Los Angeles, California, in which she engaged in group sex with five African-American men.
