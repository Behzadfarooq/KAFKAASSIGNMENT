Akiko Hiramatsu

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Akiko Hiramatsu (平松 晶子, Hiramatsu Akiko?) is a seiyū who was born on August 31, 1967 in Tokyo, Japan.

[edit] Notable voice roles

  • Haruko Akagi in Slam Dunk
  • Miyuki Kobayakawa in Taiho Shichauzo
  • Bloodberry in the Saber Marionette series
  • Palme in A Tree of Palme
  • Nene Romanova in Bubblegum Crisis and Bubblegum Crash
  • Kyoko in Nuku Nuku
  • Yukari Tanizaki in Azumanga Daioh
  • Minamo "Nyamo" Kurosawa in Azumanga Web Daioh
  • Cinnamon and Hanna-sensei in Little Snow Fairy Sugar
  • BT in .hack//SIGN
  • Miyabi Kagurazaki in Ai Yori Aoshi
  • Mistie in Super Doll★Licca-chan
  • Youko Wakana in Full Metal Panic? Fumoffu
  • Musashi in Pocket Monsters: Advanced Generation (episodes 85-92 only)
  • Calaveras in Sailor Moon
  • Aki Hinata in Sgt. Frog
  • Natsuko in Kinnikuman: Scramble for the Throne
  • Edel in Princess Tutu
  • Fuuko Kirisawa in Flame of Recca
  • Kūkaku Shiba in Bleach
  • Ran Hibiki in the Rival Schools series
  • Hinata Aki in Nee-Chan And The Big Sister
  • Harold Belserius in Tales of Destiny 2
