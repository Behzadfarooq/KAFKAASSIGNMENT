Akihabara48

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Often abbreviated as AKB48, Akihabara48 is a Japanese theater/idol group supposedly composed of 48 members, produced by Akimoto Yasushi. The group presently has 36 members, which are divided into 2 subgroups. They have released 4 singles as of present, and will be releasing their next single in February of 2007.

AKB48's primary place of performance is the 8th floor of Don Quixote, in Akihabara, Japan. Tickets are sold at 2000 yen for adults, and 1000 yen for girls, students and children. Performances are held once every weekday, and twice every weekend.

[edit] History

[edit] Opening Member Audition

The opening audition received an initial application of 7924 girls, 45 of which became successful candidates after the interview selection process. And of the 45 who passed the interview, only 24 made it after screening their dancing and singing abilities. And of the 24 who were successful in the audition, 20 showed up in the initial performance date as team A.

[edit] Second Member Audition

In the second member audition, initial applicants rose to 11892 girls, of which 131 were successful at the initial screening. Of the 131, 54 made it through the TV-phone audition. And of the 54, only 19 made if after the final screening of dancing and singing abilities. Of the 19, 18 showed up on April 1, 2006 as team K.

[edit] Discography

[edit] 桜の花びらたち (Sakura no Hanabiratachi)

Debut Album

  • Released February 1, 2006
  • Event information: The launching of Sakura no Hanabiratachi was held on February 1, 2006, at the Akihabara48 theater. During the said event, a video presentation of the events that occurred during the formation of Team A was presented. 20 Members of AKB48 Team A then performed Sakura no Hanabiratachi and Dear My Teacher, and later introduced themselves one by one to the public. Notable during this event was the absence of Ohe Tomomi (大江朝美) due to school activities.
  • Album Information:
    • All Produced By: Yasushi Akimoto
    • Executive Producer: Yasushi Kubota/Koutarou Shiba
    • Creative Direction and Photography and Edit: Yasumasa Yonehara
    • Photography: Hirohisa Sako
    • Hair and Make Up: Hiromi Kawano
    • Design: Takumi Matsubara
  • Track Listing:
    • 1. 桜の花びらたち (Sakura no Hanabiratachi)
    • 2. Dear my teacher
    • 3. 桜の花びらたち（カラオケバージョン） (Sakura no Hanabiratachi (Karaoke Version))
    • 4. Dear my teacher（カラオケバージョン）

[edit] スカート、ひらり (Skirt, Hirari)

Skirt, Hirari cover
  • Released June 7, 2006
  • Event information: The launching of Skirt, Hirari was held on June 4, 2006 at the UDX Akiba Square. 37 Members from both Team A and Team K performed live during the said event. The show started around 2:00PM, and approximately 1000 fans lined-up even from the previous night just to attend the said performance.
  • In the performance (and the PV) all member wear extra-short skirts and swing them while dancing.
  • Track Listing:
    • 1. スカート、ひらり (Skirt, Hirari)
    • 2. 青空のそばにいて (Aozora no Soba ni Ite)
    • 3. スカート、ひらり（カラオケバージョン） (Skirt, Hirari (Karaoke Version))
    • 4. 青空のそばにいて（カラオケバージョン） (Aozora no Soba ni Ite (Karaoke Version))
