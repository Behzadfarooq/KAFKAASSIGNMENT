Akizuki Tanezane (秋月 種実) was a samurai warrior who was defeated by the Otomo. Sometime after that he joined the Shimazu and fought with them against Toyotomi Hideyoshi in Kyūshū, an island of Japan.

After Sekigahara, he was transferred to Takanabe (in Hyuga, 20,000 koku).
