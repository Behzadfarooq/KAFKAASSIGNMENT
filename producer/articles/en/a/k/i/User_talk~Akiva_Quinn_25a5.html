[edit] Signing your name

When you make comments on talk pages (which is very much encouraged!), please sign your name at the end by typing -~~~~. Doing this, rather than typing your name out, creates a link to your user page and your personal talk page, giving everyone easier access to both. Thanks! -Seth Mahoney 00:11, Jun 18, 2005 (UTC)

[edit] Dates.

If a date is entered as [[22 June]] [[1999]] it will display as 22 June 1999 to you, but to other, depending on their preferences will display as either 22 June 1999 , June 22 1999 , 1999 June 22 or 1999-06-22. See Special:Preferences. See also [Wikipedia:Manual of Style (dates and numbers)]. Rich Farmbrough 08:58, 24 Jun 2005 (UTC)
