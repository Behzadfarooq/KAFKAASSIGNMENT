Akhmatova Orphans (Ахматовские сироты) were a group of Russian poets from Saint Petersburg. Their name was a reference to Anna Akhmatova. They were:

  • Joseph Brodsky
  • Yevgeny Rein
  • Anatoly Naiman
  • Dmitri Bobyshev
