Akher Gharam (Arabic: آخر غرام‎, English: Last Love) is the debut album for the popular Lebanese singer, Amal Hijazi.

[edit] Overview

Akher Gharam was produced by Dilara Master Production and distributed by Music Master. It was anticipatedly released in 2000. The album includes the hit single Rayyah Balak.

[edit] Success

Akher Gharam became one of the biggest selling albums on the year it was released and wowed audiences and critics alike

It was a major success and Hijazi conquered the Arab World and secured her place on the charts when Akher Gharam held the Number #8 position on the Official Sales charts published by the Chart Magazine, an achievement not earned by any debut album. Akher Gharam proved to be a great success scoring 4 Hit Singles for Hijazi's marvellous debut career.

[edit] Chart Performence

Most amazingly, this hit album kept Hijazi on the Top Twenty Charts for more than 6 months.
