Akvan

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Akvan, meaning "Evil Mind", is a Persian div with great powers and strength. He is described as having fangs and horns, and wearing a traditional short skirt which partially hides his tail, and has clawlike toenails. Akvan is mentioned in the Persian epic poem, Shah-Nameh. One unique characteristic of Akvan is that he always does the opposite of what is asked of him, a trait that, if known, can be used against him.
