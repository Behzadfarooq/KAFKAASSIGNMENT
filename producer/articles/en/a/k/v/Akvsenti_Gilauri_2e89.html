Akvsenti Gilauri (Persian: آگوستنی گیلائوری‎ , born August 8, 1979 in Tbilisi, Georgia) is a Georgian football player who currently plays for Esteghlal F.C. in Iran's Premier Football League. He usually plays in the defender position. He is also a former member of the Georgia under-21 national football team.
