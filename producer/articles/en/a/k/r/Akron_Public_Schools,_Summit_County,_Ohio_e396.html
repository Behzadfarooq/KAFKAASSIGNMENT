Akron Public Schools, Summit County, Ohio

Akron Public Schools serves students in Akron, Ohio and nearby communities. This school district is located in the northeastern part of the state and less than 40 miles south of Cleveland and 30 miles north of Canton. As of 2006-2007, it is comprised of seven high schools, 10 middle schools and more than 35 elementary schools.

Currently, Akron Public Schools are the fifth largest school district in Ohio, behind the Toledo Public Schools (Toledo City School District). The current superintendent of the Akron City School District is Sylvester Small, Ed.D.
