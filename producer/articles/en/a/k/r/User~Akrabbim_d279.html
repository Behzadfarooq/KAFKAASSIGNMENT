[edit] My Contributions

My Contributions | my edit count by Essjay's version of Interiot's version of Kate's tool

Because a very large majority of my edits are on topics of modern culture and popular music (to my taste), I occasionally feel guilty for not contributing to more traditional encyclopedic articles in any advanced way, but there are a number of reasons why.

First of all, being a college school student I do not have very much time to do the research required for more sophisticated topics. I also know a lot more about music I listen to than about traditional encyclopedic articles. I can contribute effectively to these articles without spending long amounts of time, which is ideal since a number of my edits are made during down time in between classes.

More mainstream articles such as these are also the object of large amounts of misguided or inconsequential information, as well as frequent vandalism, mostly because of the high "hit-counts" of these articles by generally anonymous non-Users. As a result, these pages are generally poorly written and formatted, and generally far below encyclopedic standards, as they are lacking honest, NPOV Wikipedians that actually have interest in them. Edit wars are common, especially in regards to genre, as many contributors have very biased opinions on what style of music a particular band is, as they are generally uneducated in professional music criticism and NPOV policy. To cite personal experience, Switchfoot, Relient K, and Fall Out Boy have all encountered such extensive edit wars.
