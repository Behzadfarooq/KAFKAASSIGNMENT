Akrar is a village in the Faroe Islands. It is located on Lopransfjørður, an inlet, which itself is part of Vágsfjørður, on the east-side of Suðuroy, and was founded in the year 1817.

[edit] Statistics

  • Population: 28
  • Postal code (Zip): FO 927
  • Location: 61°27′20″N, 6°45′33″W
  • Municipality: Sumbiar
