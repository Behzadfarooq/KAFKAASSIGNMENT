A.P.O. Akratitos is a soccer club that plays in the Fourth Division in Greece.

As of the 2005/06 season Akratitos were relegated to Fourth Division after finishing last in the tables. They had the lowest average attendance of only a few hundred.It also had a low attendance record in First Division in a game against Egaleo which was only attended by 16 fans.[1] The club rose from the lower ranks to the top flight under the stewardship of Yannis Pathiakakis, who in 2002 died on the pitch during a training session. The stadium is now named after him.
