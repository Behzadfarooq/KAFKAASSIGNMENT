Chi Rho

Chi Rho is the Wake Forest University Christian Male A Cappella Ensemble located in Winston-Salem, North Carolina.

[edit] Background

[edit] Discography

  • Egypt, 1993
  • Rejoice, 1995
  • All That I Have, 1997
  • No Greater Love , 1999
  • Free, 2001
  • The Promise, 2003
  • See Through the Rhetoric, 2005

[edit] Awards

  • 2001 Best Male Collegiate Song of the Year, Championships of Collegiate A cappella (ICCA)
  • 2002 Nominated Best A Cappella Male Solist of the Year, Contemporary A cappella society (CASA)

[edit] Places sang

  • The Television Game Show: The Price is Right
  • Christian Radio Stations (including interview and playing of past recordings) in Winston-Salem, Maryland, Atlanta, Tennessee, and other locations
  • Fox & CBS News
  • Touching Lives Ministry on the Trinity Broadcasting Network
  • The White House
  • The Washington National Cathedral in Washington, D.C.
  • Turner Field in Atlanta, GA, Tropicana Field in Tampa, FL, and Oriole Park at Camden Yards in Baltimore, MD
  • The Georgia State Capitol, Atlanta, GA
  • Churches and Organizations in the states and districts of Alabama, Arkansas, California, Florida, Georgia, Illinois, Kentucky, Maryland, Michigan, New York, New Jersey, North Carolina, Pennsylvania, South Carolina, Tennessee, Texas, Virginia, Washington D.C., and West Virginia and many others.
  • They have been on an international tour to Canada, and are currently scheduled to tour Ireland and Great Britain.
  • Backing vocals for Jars of Clay live in concert.
  • Performed as opening act for Shane and Shane.
  • Private concert for Senator Bob Dole.
