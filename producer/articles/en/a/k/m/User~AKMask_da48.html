[edit] About Me

Resident of Juneau, Alaska. Actor, Linux geek, and aspiring journalist.

I am a registered Libertarian. True to the stereotype, I am perfectly capable of being dropped in the woods with nothing and surviving for an extended period of time. I actually occasionally do this for fun.

According to one anti-wikipedian notable eccentric I am a danger to society. See my page of the dealings between myself and google-watch.com/wikipedia-watch.com webmaster Daniel Brandt here.

Despite being a total geek, I am of the opinion that Richard M. Stallman is a dick. Freedom means allowing people to do whatever they want, and even more so not crying and whining when somebody creates code that they dont want to release. I prefer the BSD license to the GPL.

I am an accomplished Social Engineer. I use this ability not only to get information and goods, but also to bring down the system, large monopitalistic corporations and government.
