An AK-22 training rifle.

An AK-22 is a AK type rifle chambered in .22 cal. Most AK-22 guns are used to train troops. Most AK-22 rifles are sold for $200-$500. AK-22's are cheap to shoot because they use .22 cal ammo, which is why armies train with them.
