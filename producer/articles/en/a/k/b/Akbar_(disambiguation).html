Akbar (disambiguation)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The name Akbar may refer to any of the following:

  • The Mughal emperor Akbar of India;
  • The Mughal emperor Akbar II of India;
  • The Star Wars character Admiral Ackbar.
  • One of the comic-strip duo Akbar and Jeff of Life in Hell comics.
  • Akbar, Basilan, a municipality in the Philippines

Akbar is also the Arabic word for great, as used in the takbir.
