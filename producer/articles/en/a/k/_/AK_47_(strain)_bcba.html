AK 47 (strain)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

AK 47 is a strain of Cannabis, and placed 2nd at the 2003 Cannabis Cup in the Indica Cup category. AK 47 is a sativa-indica cross, and is noted for an incredibly pungent odor, and a magnificently powerful effect. It is popular among consumers, but has seen limited production due to a lack of density, and the difficulty that this presents for commercial production. Although it may be called Special K in some circles, this can cause confusion, as the term also refers to Ketamine. AK 47 was presumably named after a world-famous assault rifle, the AK-47. It has also been suggested that the name may have a second meaning related to Afghani Kush, and a 47 day flowering period. Genetics: mostly sativa (see also:cannabis sativa). Indoor clone grow time: 5–10 days. Flowering time: 53–63 days. Yield per m2 (indoors): 350-500 grams. Harvest time (outdoors): 15-31 October.
