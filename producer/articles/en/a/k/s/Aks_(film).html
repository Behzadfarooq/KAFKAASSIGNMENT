Aks (Hindi: अक्स, Urdu: عکس, marketed in English as "The Reflection") is a Bollywood movie that released in 2001. This film was loosely based on the Hollywood films Face/off and Fallen.

[edit] Synopsis

Spoiler warning: Plot and/or ending details follow.

Aks is a supernatural thriller centering on two main characters - Manu Verma, the cop and Raghavan, the shadowy killer. Raghavan is caught,and as he is about to be executed by hanging, his spirit gets into Manu Verma's body and starts to manipulate Manu Verma in performing acts that Raghavan wanted to perform...

[edit] DVD

The DVD of "Aks," released by Eros Entertainment, is notorious for being faulty, and not working in most DVD players.[citation needed] Furthermore, Eros infamously denies issuing replacement copies of any of their discs, unless the purchase is made directly from them, and covered by their standard "return policy".[citation needed]
