[edit] Introduction

Hi! Welcome to my userpage. I made my first edit to wikipedia in November 2005 and have been editing ever since. I am an administrator since October 26, 2006. Leave me a message on my talk page if you need help that requires my attention. You can also find me at #wikipedia, #wikipedia-in or #vandalism-en-wp IRC channels.
