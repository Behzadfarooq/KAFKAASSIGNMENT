AAFT creates a Media Revolution

The Asian Academy of film & TV and Asian School of Media Studies located in film city Noida the first of its kind in the private sector in India. The film school says, Marwah aims to serve this growing need for trained professionals besides offering a world level platform for research and development for films and television. Sandeep Marwah, who has been instrumental in establishing the noida film city way back in 1987 which has now become the hub for News and films for Northern India,.

With a track record of having groomed nearly 4000 of its alumni in the last 13 years through the Asian Academy of film and television 85 percent of whom are already working with one or the other leading channels and film production houses like the Zee, Star,AAJ Tak,NDTV ,balaji telefilms ,sridevi productions ,RGV Films , India TV ,Sahara TV,CNN IBN,BBC,Star Network, Channel 7 etc, AAFT & ASMS are poised for a quantum leap forward in media education now." The fact that AAFT attracts students from 56 countries of the world including countries like the US, Canada etc has already established its international reach and its credibility as an institution of learning at a global level" adds Marwah. Another critical advantage leveraged by AAFT & ASMS is its Ability to draw the best amongst the practicing artistes from the world of films, television and art, audio, visual and print media and even from amongst the best management brains from the corporate world and industry in general. "The outstanding contribution to the Indian film industry by AAFT and a track record AAFT has surely taken the Film Industry in a big way”, is how Bollywood producer Boney Kapoor ,who is also member of the board at AAFT sums it up.The Board of Directors also includes Satish Kaushik ,Madhur Bhandarkar ,Anil Kapoor and many more. With the state of the art training infrastructure including world class studios, theatres, editing labs and other equipment, the institute offers bachelor to masters level courses in radio and TV , cinema, mass communication, media management including the various short term foundation courses (3month and 1 year ) in different art forms have been devised to suit international standards.
