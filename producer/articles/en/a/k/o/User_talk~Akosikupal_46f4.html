[edit] About images and copyright

Please do not upload images found on the web and tag them as beeing licensed under a free license if there is not an explicit statement on said website that this is the case. I have gone over the sites you list as sources for your images and most either have no copyright info (in wich case the default is "all rights reserved" per law in most of the world) or explicitly do state "all rights reseved". As such I have flagged all your uploads as unlicensed and to be deleted. Please be more more carefull when uploading images in the future. Thanks. --Sherool (talk) 08:30, 18 December 2006 (UTC)
