[edit] Welcome to A. Kohler's User Page

This page provides an introduction to me and an overview of my work here. I am a professional writer and editor. I have a broad range of personal interests and knowledge that I hope will benefit the community, including grammar, language, archaelology, computers, and philosophy. I am an avid proponent of free information (free as in freedom and free as in beer), which is why I chose to volunteer here.
