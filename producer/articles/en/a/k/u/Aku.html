Aku

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Aku can mean:

  • An ethnic group in Gambia, descendants of former slaves
  • The romaji spelling of the Japanese word for "evil", written "あく" in hiragana or, more commonly, "悪" in kanji
  • The antagonist of Samurai Jack
  • The skipjack tuna

The abbreviation AKU can stand for:

  • In computer technology, AKU stands for Adaptation Kit Update
  • Amirkabir University of Technology, Tehran, Iran
  • Aga Khan University, Karachi, Pakistan
