Akuma na Eros (悪魔なエロス, Akuma na Eros?) (Virgin Crisis) is a Shōjo manga series by mangaka Mayu Shinjo.

[edit] Characters

  • Miu Sakurai

Sakurai Miu our little wizard wannabe who innocently curses herself into a contract with a demon who calls himself Satan, all for the love of the most popular boy in school.

  • Akuma (Kai)

Kai / Akuma A rather handsome prince of darkness. He's not your typical demon aside from his pointed ears, red eyes, and ram horns - and a crow who talks to him while sitting on his shoulder. His human persona looks normal though, and has appeared at Miu's school so that he can torment her, but he announces to her classmates that he is her older brother.

  • Shion Amamiya

Amamiya Shion The most popular boy in school and the object of Miu's love interest. He is the typical shoujo hero who gets high grades, is super kind, very handsome, great at sports, etc. The only thing he isn't, is cursed by a demon. He seems to be fond of Miu from the beginning but gives her extra attention since Miu's encounter with the Book of Shadows.
