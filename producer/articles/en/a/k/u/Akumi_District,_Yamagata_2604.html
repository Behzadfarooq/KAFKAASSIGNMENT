Akumi District, Yamagata

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Akumi (飽海郡; -gun) is a district located in Yamagata, Japan.

As of 2003, the district has an estimated population of 36,844 and a density of 57.99 persons per km². The total area is 635.31 km².
