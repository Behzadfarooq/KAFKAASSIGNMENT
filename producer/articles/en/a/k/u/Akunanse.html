The Akunanse are a fictional legacy of vampires, native to Africa from White Wolf Game Studio's Vampire: The Masquerade books and role-playing games.

All Akunase are slightly monstrous, often displaying the features of spiders. Likely an offshoot of the Gangrel, the Akunanse are tricksters and storytellers, most closely tied to the animals of the Ebony Kingdom than other Legacies. They practice a discipline called Abombwe that resembles the Protean of the western Kindred.
