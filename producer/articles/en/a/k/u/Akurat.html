Akurat is a Polish band formed in November 1994 in Bielsko-Biała disctrict. Akurat in Polish means even, exactly, just as. Music of the band can be credited as a fusion between punk rock, reggae, ska and pop.

[edit] Members

[edit] Current members

  • Tomasz Kłaptocz - vocal, trumpet
  • Piotr Wróbel - guitar, backing singer
  • Wojciech "Yellow" Żółty - guitar, backing singer
  • Ireneusz Wojnar - bass guitar
  • Przemysław Zwias - saxophone, flute
  • Łukasz Gocal - drums

[edit] Former members

Former members of the band are: "Yoyo", Marcin Stroński, Dariusz Wilk, "Mróva", Jarosław Bandzul, Piotr Fuchs.
