[edit] Bio

A Van Burener who programs with C++.

[edit] Hobbies and Interests

When I'm not programming I'm probably watching an anime like Cowboy Bebop, Fullmetal Alchemist, or Ghost in the Shell. Or possibly trying to learn Japanese, or German. Or maybe reading about history, or watching the History Channel or Mythbusters. Or Slashdoting. Or playing America's Army, Wolfenstein, SimCity 4, The Sims 2, Civilization 4, GalCiv 2, or Stronghold Crusader. And of course all of that is when I'm not in school... more or less.

[edit] Music

From years of being in band I've acquired a taste for classical music. However, I also enjoy jazz, industrial, jpop, and alternative rock.

[edit] Proposed WikiProject:Musical Instruments

The vast majority of musical instrument articles are of good quality, however, these articles tend to lack a uniform appearance and style, additionally, many of these articles are not featured or of featured quality. I believe a project focusing specifically on musical instruments might help to better organize this trove of knowledge. akuyumeTC 02:32, 2 November 2006 (UTC)
