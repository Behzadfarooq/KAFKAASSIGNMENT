I found this article to have about 1/4 real information and 3/4 marketing material, so I cut out the marketing material. However, I feel that something serious should be said about some of the products of this company, which have played important roles both in recording studio technology (particularly the dynamic and condenser microphones of the 1950s through 1970s) and consumer audio. Most of the material that I cut out was about consumer headphones; I felt that it gave excessive detail on just one audiophile-oriented model while ignoring the headphones for which AKG is best known (e.g. the K 240). As soon as time permits, I will sketch in some information about the studio condenser microphones, which I know more about. DSatz 14:38, 14 June 2006 (UTC)
