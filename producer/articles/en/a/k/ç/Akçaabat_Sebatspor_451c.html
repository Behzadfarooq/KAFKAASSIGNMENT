Akçaabat Sebatspor, also known as A. Sebatspor is a Turkish football club located in the Akçaabat district of Trabzon, Turkey. They play their home games in the Akçaabat Fatih Stadyumu. The club was founded in 1923.

[edit] History

  • 1923, Akçaabat Sebatspor was founded as Sebat Idman Yurdu.
  • 1968, the team became professional, renamed as Sebat Gençlik Kulübü.
  • 1970, promoted to Turkish Third League.
  • 1978, promoted to Turkish Second League
  • 1986, renamed as Akçaabat Sebatspor.
  • 1990, relegated to Turkish Third League.
  • 1991, promoted to Turkish Second League.
  • 1993, relegated to Turkish Third League.
  • 2000, promoted to Turkish Second League.
  • 2003, promoted to Turkish First Football League
  • 2005, relegated to Turkish Second League.
