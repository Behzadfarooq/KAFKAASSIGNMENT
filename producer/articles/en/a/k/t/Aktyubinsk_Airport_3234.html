Aktyubinsk Airport (also given as Aktobe) (ICAO: UATT) is an airport in Kazakhstan located 4 km south of Aktobe. It has a small terminal with five airliner parking spots. It has serviced the Ilyushin Il-86.

[edit] Airlines and Destinations

  • Air Astana (Almaty, Astana)
  • Scat Air (Aqtau, Almaty, Astana, Atyrau, Moscow-Domodedovo)
