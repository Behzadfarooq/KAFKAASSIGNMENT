The Ahwatukee Foothills News is a bi-weekly paper serving Ahwatukee, Arizona. It has a circulation of 28,000. First published as the Ahwatukee Sentinnal in 1976, its name was changed to the Ahwatukee News in 1978. In 1988 it once again changed its name, this time to the Ahwatukee Weekly News. The current incarnation is the Ahwatukee Foothill News. [1]
