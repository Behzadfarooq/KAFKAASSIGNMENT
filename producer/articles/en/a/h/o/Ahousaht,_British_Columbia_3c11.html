Ahousaht (also spelled Ahousat) is the principle settlement on Flores Island, in British Columbia, Canada (49°17′N 126°4′W). Accessible only by water or air, Ahousaht is a small community predominantly composed of First Nations people from the Nuu-Chah-Nulth Nation. It has about 1,800 members, almost half of which live in the reserve of Maaqtisiis.
