Ahousaht First Nation

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Ahousaht First Nation is a First Nation government based on the west coast of Vancouver Island in British Columbia, Canada, focussed on the community of Ahousaht, British Columbia. It is a member of the Nuu-chah-nulth Tribal Council.
