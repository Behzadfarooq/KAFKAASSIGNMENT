Ahore, or Ahor, is a town in Jalore District of Rajasthan state in India. Situated 17 km north of the Jalore on Jalore-Jodhpur road it is headquarters of the tehsil by the same name.

Many people from this village are settled in various cities of India and abroad. This village has 8 Jain temples and Shri Godi Parshvanath Jain Mandir is very famous. It's Prathista was done by Shri Rajendrasuriji Maharaj more than 110 years ago.
