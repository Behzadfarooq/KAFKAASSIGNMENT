Hello Ahod, and welcome to Wikipedia. Thank you for your recent edit to The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints, one of the articles that is part of the Latter Day Saint movement WikiProject. I deleted your edits for the following reasons:

  • Power and authority back to authority - power in the priesthood is based on personal rightousness, not the Church.
  • Martyr - While I believe it is true, the Wikipedia community has decided not to refer to Smith's death in this way. It apparently violates WP:NPOV.
  • Hinckley is seen as Smith's successor in the presidency, and the other information was redundant to material elsewhere in the article.

That said, don't let my reverts stop you from editing. We can always use new editors on wikipedia and you've shown an interest. Please continue to edit. Happy editing! -Visorstuff 23:28, 9 January 2007 (UTC)
