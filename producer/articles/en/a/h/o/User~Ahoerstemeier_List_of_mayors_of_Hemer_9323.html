User:Ahoerstemeier/List of mayors of Hemer

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

< User:Ahoerstemeier

Until 1995 the mayor was a honorary post with only representative duties, while the actual head of administration was a Stadtdirektor. This system was introduced in 1946 by the British military government, and was abolished in 1999. However when Reiner Hermann was forced to resign in 1994, shortly after the new system was adopted, Hemer became the first commune of North Rhine-Westphalia to get a professional mayor in 1995.

[edit] Mayor of the Amt

Till 1918 named Amtmann, after that Amtsbürgermeister. After 1946 the mayor was mostly representative, while the administrative head was the Amtsdirektor. In case of Hemer the Stadtdirektor and Amtsdirektor were the same.

  • Hermann Trump - 1900-1919
  • Carl Degen - 1919-1929
  • Wilhelm Langemann - 1929-1945 (at first independent, later NSDAP)
  • Josef Kleffner - 1945
  • Heinz Hoose - 1945-1946 (SPD)
  • Rudolf Deidert - 1946 (SPD)
  • Hermann Arendt - 1946-1948 (SPD)
  • Gustav Teves - 1948-1956 (CDU)
  • Rudolf Deidert - 1956-1961 (SPD)
  • Josef Kleffner - 1961-1966 (CDU)
  • Hans-Ludwig Kremerskothen - 1966-1969 (CDU)
  • Hermann-Josef Geismann - 1969-1974 (CDU)
