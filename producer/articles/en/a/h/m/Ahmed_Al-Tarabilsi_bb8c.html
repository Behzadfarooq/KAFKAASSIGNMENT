Ahmed Al-Tarabilsi (born 22 March 1947) was a famous goalkeeper who played for Al-Kuwait Sports Club and the Kuwaiti National team. Al-Tarabilsi performed well in the 1982 World Cup, specially against Czechoslovakia and England. Al-Tarabilisi didn't concede any goals in the 1974 Gulf Cup in Kuwait. Al-Tarabilsi is originally from Lebanon but was given a Kuwaiti citizenship to play for Kuwait's national team
