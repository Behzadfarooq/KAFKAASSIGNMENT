Ahmed Dokhi Al-Dosari (Arabic: أحمد الدوخي) (born 25 October 1976) is a Saudi Arabian football (soccer) player who currently plays as a defender for Al-Ittihad.

Dokhi was part of the Al Hilal team that won the AFC Club Championship and AFC Super Cup in 2000. He then moved on to Al Ittihad and helped them lift the AFC Champions League in 2005.

He is a member of the Saudi Arabian national team and was recently called up to the squad to participate in the 2006 FIFA World Cup.
