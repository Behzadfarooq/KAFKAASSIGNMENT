Ahmed Bahja (Arabic: أحمد البهجة) (born 21 December 1970) is a retired Moroccan football player, who played for several clubs, including KAC Marrakech and Raja Casablanca.

Bahja played for the Morocco national football team and was a participant at the 1992 Summer Olympics and the 1994 FIFA World Cup.
