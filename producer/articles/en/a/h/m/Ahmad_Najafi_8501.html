Ahmad Nadjafi Shoushtari (Persian: احمد نجفی شوشتری‎ , born in 1948 in Khorramshahr, Iran) is a famous Iranian actor and producer. He has a B.A. in Economics from the Woodbury University in Burbank, California. In 1976 he entered cinema by assisting Massoud Kimiayee in Ghazal. In 1976 he founded a movie distributing company in U.S., and he was the vice-president of IRIB2, distributing manager of "Tel film". He was also the chairman of IRIB in America and the vice-president of "free film making" (Kargah Aazaad Filmsazi) studio; he has also founded "7th art" studio.
