Ahmed Mubarak (born on February 23, 1985) is a Omani footballer who is a midfielder for Al Rayyan. He is a member of the Oman national football team.

In the qualifiers for the 2006 FIFA World Cup, Mubarak was a member of Oman's national squad, who came second in their Stage Two group picking up 10 points, losing only to the 18 points of Japan. scored 2 goals in Oman's 5-1 rout of India.[1] He also picked up a yellow card in Oman's 2-0 defeat of Singapore.[2]
