Ahmed Shobair

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Ahmed Shobair is a former Egyptian Goalkeeper who played for Al-Ahly club & National Egyptian football team. He was born on 28 September 1960. He is the vice-president of The Egyptian Football Association, and a previous Official of Egyptian National Olympic Team. He also is famous as soccer T.V. commentator & Show Anchor.

He is also a member in the Egyptian Parliament, representing his city Tanta, in Al-Gharbiyah Governorate for the term starting 2005 and ending in 2010. He is a member in the ruling National Democratic Party.

[edit] Honours

Personal

  • 7th Best African Footballer by France Football 1989
  • 5th Best African Footballer by France Football 1990
  • Best Egyptian Keeper several times
  • Best Arab Keeper 1987, 1989 & 1996

International

  • Participated in World Cup, Italy 1990
  • Pan Arab Games gold medal & Arab Cup title for 1992

Al-Ahly

  • 7 Egyptian League titles
  • 5 Egyptian Soccer Cup titles
  • 1 African Champions League title 1987
  • 4 African Cup Winners' Cup titles
  • 1 Arab champions league title 1996
  • 1 Arab cup winners' cup title 1995
