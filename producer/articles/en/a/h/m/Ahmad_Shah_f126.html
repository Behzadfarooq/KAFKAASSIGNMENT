Ahmad Shah

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Ahmed Shah is the name of several prominent people, including:

  • Ahmad Shah Qajar, who ruled as Shah of Persia between 1909 and 1925 AD
  • Ahmad Shah Massoud, a prominent commander in the Soviet war in Afghanistan and then leader of the Northern Alliance (an anti-Taliban faction)
  • Ahmad Shah Abdali, also known as Ahmad Shah Durrani, formed the Durrani Empire
  • Ahmad Shah Bahadur, one of the "lesser" Mughal Emperors of northern India, who reigned from 1748 to 1754 AD
  • Ahmad Shah (Malaysia), 5th sultan of Pahang and sometime head-of-state of Malaysia
  • Ahmed Shah of Gujarat, founder of Ahmedabad city
  • Ahmed Shah (field hockey), son of Iqrar Shah, player of Pakistan's team
