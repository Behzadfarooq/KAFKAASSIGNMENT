Ahmad Rasheed Miller (born April 10, 1978 in Bradenton, Florida) is a former defensive tackle for the National Football League. He was chosen with the final pick in the 2002 NFL Draft, giving him the nickname Mr. Irrelevant. After being drafted by the Houston Texans, he went through training camp but was cut prior to their final preseason game. He then signed on to the New York Giants practice squad.
