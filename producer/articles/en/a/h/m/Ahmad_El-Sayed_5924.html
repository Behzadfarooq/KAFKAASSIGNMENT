Ahmad El-Sayed (Arabic: أحمد السيد) (born February 8, 1982) is an Egyptian footballer. He plays the Defender position for Egypt's African Super Cup Championship club Al-Ahlyas well as Egypt's National Football Team. He was in the team list of Egypt that won the African Cup of Nations Egypt 2006, but he didn't participate in games.

Ahmad El-Sayed who is known for his faithfulness for Al-Ahly has cried after knowing of his injury that deprived him from the latest games in CAF Champions League 2006, Al-Ahly got the title of this tournament to be the third CAF Champions League Ahmed achieves. El-Sayed had put his prints on the glorious victory of Al-Ahly in the CAF Champions League 2005, scoring a goal against Ajax Cape Town of South Africa, in the group round. El-Sayed is the Vice-Captain of Al-Ahly. He is known for his strength and speed.
