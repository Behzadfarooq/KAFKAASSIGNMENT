Ahmad Safa

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Ahmed Safa (right) with professor Abdur Razzak, taken in 1995

Ahmed Safa (Bangla: আহমেদ ছফা) was a very famous Bengali novelist and writer. He was born in 1943 in Gachbaria, Chittagong. He was famous for his emotional outcome and unorthodox lifestyle.

[edit] Books

  • Jadyapi Amar Guru
  • Ordhek Nari Ordhek Ishwari
  • Onkar
  • Ekti Nokhotrer Mrittu
