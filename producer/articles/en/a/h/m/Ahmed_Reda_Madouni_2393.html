Ahmed Reda Madouni

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Ahmed Reda Madouni (Arabic: أحمد رضا مادوني‎) (born 4 October 1980, in Casablanca, Morocco) is an Algerian soccer player for Bayer Leverkusen. He played for Montpellier HSC and Borussia Dortmund before joining Bayer Leverkusen.

A former French youth international, Madouni made the switch to join the Algerian national team in June of 2005, as he played his first game for Les Fennecs, a friendly against Mali. He has 2 international caps.
