Ahmed Abou Moslem (born July 25, 1981 in Cairo) is a Egyptian footballer currently playing for RC Strasbourg as a defender.

Ahmed Abou Moslem started his career at Ithad Othman Club in Egypt. He was selected for the Egyptian National Youth Team U20 to play in the FIFA World Youth Championship 2001 in [[Argentina. Egypt reach the semi finals and finally won the 3rd place in the U20 World Cup
