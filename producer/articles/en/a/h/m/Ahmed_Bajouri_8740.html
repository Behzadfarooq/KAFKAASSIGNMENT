Ahmed Bajouri (born May 4, 1982 in Tripoli, Lebanon) was an Lebanese rugby league player for the Wests Tigers and the South Sydney Rabbitohs in the Australian National Rugby League competition. Since 1999 he has also been a regular member of the Lebanon national rugby league team; his position of choice is usually on the wing.
