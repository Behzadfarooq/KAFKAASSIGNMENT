Ahmed El Faleh

Ahmed El Faleh (Arabic: أحمد الفالح) (born February 26 1982 in Iraq & currently living in Arizona, USA) is a singer who rose to popularity around the world after placing fourth in Super Star 3, the pan-Arabic version of Pop Idol.

Ahmed includes Umm Kulthum & Thekra as his main musical inspirations.
