Ahmad Hassan Stakoza (Arabic: أحمد حسن ) (born April 12, 1979) is an Egyptian footballer. He plays the Defensive midfielder position for Egypt's African Super Cup Championship club Al-Ahly. He joined the club in July 2004 from al-Shrqya sporting club. He did not take his chance in the first season. In the 2006-2007 season he appeared in some matches for Al-Ahly and scored the wining goal against Tanta.
