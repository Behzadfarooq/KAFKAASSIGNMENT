Ahmadiyya Mosque Berlin

Ahmadiyya Mosque in Berlin is Germany's oldest mosque, situated on Brienner Straße 7-8 in Berlin-Wilmersdorf. It was designed by K.A. Kermann and was built between 1924 and 1928. Ahmadiyya Mosque Berlin, which has a 32 metre tall minaret, was heavily damaged in World War II.
