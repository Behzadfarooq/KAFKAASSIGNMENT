Ahmed Thasmeen Ali

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Ahmed Thasmeen Ali (born 1967) is the Home Minister of the Maldives. His predecessor is Umar Zahir. He holds a BA degree in Economics from Warwick University in England and a Master's degree in Political Science of the American University in Cairo, Egypt. He has been an MP for Baa atoll since the 1990s. Ahmed Tasmeen Ali became Home minister in 2005. Ahmed Thasmeen Ali owns resorts and other businesses, including Reethi Beach Resort and Fonaddhoo Tuna Products.
