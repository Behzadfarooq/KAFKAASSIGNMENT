Major General Ahmed bin Rashid Al Maktoum (b. 1950 in Bur Dubai, Dubai) is the Deputy Chairman of Dubai Police & Public Security.

He is the fourth and youngest son of the late ruler, Rashid bin Saeed Al Maktoum (1912-1990). His elder brothers Hamdan bin Rashid Al Maktoum and Mohammed bin Rashid Al Maktoum are the Deputy Ruler and Ruler of Dubai respectively.

[edit] Thoroughbred horse racing

Since the 1980s, Sheikh Ahmed's family has been heaving involved in Thoroughbred horse racing. His horse won the 1999 running of the Dubai Golden Shaheen in his native Dubai. Racing in the United Kingdom, his Thoroughbred racehorse Ameerat won the 2001 One Thousand Guineas and Mtoto captured the 1987 and 1988 runnings of the Eclipse Stakes and Prince of Wales's Stakes plus the 1988 King George VI & Queen Elizabeth Diamond Stakes. His horses have also won Group One races in Ireland, France, Germany and Italy including the Irish St Leger, Prix de la Salamandre, Grosser Preis von Baden and the Derby Italiano.
