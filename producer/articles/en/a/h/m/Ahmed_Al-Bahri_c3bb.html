Ahmed Al-Bahri (Arabic: أحمد البحري) (born 18 September 1980) is a Saudi Arabian football (soccer) player who currently plays as a defender for Al-Shabab.

He is a member of the Saudi Arabian national team. He appeared for his country in the 2003 Arab Cup and was a member of the Saudi team at the FIFA World Youth Championship. He was recently called up to the squad to participate in the 2006 FIFA World Cup.
