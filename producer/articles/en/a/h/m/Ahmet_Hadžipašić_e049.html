Ahmet Hadžipašić

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Ahmet Hadžipašić (born June 1, 1952 in Cazin) is the current Prime Minister of Federation of Bosnia and Herzegovina (since February 14, 2003). He is a member of the Bosniak community and Party of Democratic Action. Hadzipasic works as a professor of the University of Sarajevo. He is married and has three children, one is a pediatrician, another dentist and the youngest daughter is in high school.Lives in Zenica, city well-known as a metallurgy center of the former Yugoslavia. .
