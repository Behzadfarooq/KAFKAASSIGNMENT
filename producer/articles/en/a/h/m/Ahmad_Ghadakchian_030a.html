Ahmad Ghadakchian

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Ahamd Ghadakchian (in Persian: احمد قدکچیان), Actor,(1920 -December 8, 2006), Tehran, Iran. The father of director Kamran Ghadakchian.

Some of his works:

  • The Golden Dreams, 1951
  • Marjan, 1956
  • Hashem Khan, 1966
  • The Red Line, 1982
  • Explosion in the Operation Room, 1991
