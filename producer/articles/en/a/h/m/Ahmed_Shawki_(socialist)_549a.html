Ahmed Shawki (socialist)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Ahmed Shawki is an Arab American socialist activist and journalist, the editor of the International Socialist Review and a leader of the International Socialist Organization. He also serves on the steering committee of the National Council of Arab-Americans.

[edit] Selected publications

  • Ahmed Shawki, Black Liberation and Socialism (Haymarket Books, 2006), ISBN 1-931859-26-4.
  • Anthony Arnove, Peter Binns, Tony Cliff, Chris Harman and Ahmed Shawki, Russia From Workers' State to State Capitalism (Haymarket Books, 2006), ISBN 1-931859-06-X.
  • Ahmed Shawki, "Roots of the Civil Rights Movement" in International Socialist Review 45 (January-February 2006).
  • Ahmed Shawki, "China: From Mao to Deng" in International Socialist Review 1 (Summer 1997).
