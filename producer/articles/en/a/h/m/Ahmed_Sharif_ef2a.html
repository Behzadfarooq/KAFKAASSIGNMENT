Ahmed Sharif (Bangla: আহমদ শরীফ) (1921-1999 CE) was an educationist, philosopher, critic, writer and scholar of medieval Bangla literature. Born in Chittagong, Sharif retired as a Professor from University of Dhaka in 1983. Sharif is widely recognized as one of the most outspoken and radical thinkers of Bangladesh.
