Ahmad Bradshaw is a junior running back for the Marshall University Thundering Herd. Bradshaw wears number 44.

Bradshaw only had 462 yards and 3 touchdowns in his freshman season in 2004. But Bradshaw started to break out his Sophomore season in 2005 with 997 yards and 9 touchdowns. After a 5-touchdown performance against UTEP, Bradshaw had 1,413 yards and 18 touchdowns with a game against Southern Miss left.

Ahmad Bradshaw was born in Bluefield, VA and is the son of James Bradshaw and Diana Davis. He is majoring in sports management and marketing.

High School: A SuperPrep and Prep Star All-American out of Graham High...rated as the No. 7 player in Virginia and the No. 28 cornerback prospect in the country by Rivals.com...rushed for 2,282 yards and 27 touchdowns as a junior and 2,557 rushing yards and 31 touchdowns as a senior at Graham...Rushed for 5,265 yards, scored 92 touchdowns and averaged nearly 10 yards a carry during his high school career...Originally signed to play football at the University of Virginia, but never enrolled for the fall semester.
