Ahmad Black is an American Football Cornerback for the University of Florida. Black is an aggressive player.

[edit] High School

While helping lead his team to the Class 5A state championship, Black had 70 tackles and six interceptions. He was also the 2nd leading scorer on his school's varsity basketball team.

Black has been quoted on Scout.com as saying:

"I have real quick feet to play corner. And I'm one of those guys who can play either corner or safety. I just love being out there."[1]

[edit] University

Black is eager to begin his University career stating:

“I want to play early. I’ll play special teams or something. I just want to get out there.”[2]

Black enrolled at the University of Florida in January 2007 and will compete at spring practice.
