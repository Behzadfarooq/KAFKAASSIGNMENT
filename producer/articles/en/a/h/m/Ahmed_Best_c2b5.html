Ahmed Best

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Ahmed Best (born August 19, 1973) is a voice actor most famous (or infamous) for his role as Jar Jar Binks in the Star Wars prequel trilogy (1999-2005), for which he won a Golden Raspberry in 1999. He was cast as Jar Jar after casting director Robin Gurland saw how loose and lanky he was during a performance of musical group STOMP.

He has also been involved in numerous other projects as a live actor and a producer.
