Ahmed Mubarak el-Khaldi has been the Minister of Justice of Palestine since March 2006 when Hamas won the elections and took clear control of the Palestinian Authority. Dr. el-Khaldi is a former professor of law and dean of the College of Law at An-Najah National University [1] in Nablus, Palestine. For several years, he has been instrumental in drafting a permanent Palestinian Constitution. He holds a Ph.D. in Public Law, Political Systems and Constitutional Law from Cairo University, Cairo (Egypt) that was granted in 1979. He has also worked as a lawyer in private commercial practice in Nablus, Palestine.
