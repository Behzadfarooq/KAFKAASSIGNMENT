Ahmad Ali Al Sayegh

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Ahmad Ali Al Sayegh is a prominent citizen of the United Arab Emirates. He serves in a number of key capacities in Abu Dhabi. Al Sayegh started his career at the Abu Dhabi National Oil Corporation and would eventually become its Finance Director. Today , he has left ADNOC for other engagements in Abu Dhabi's corporate sector.

Here are some of the positions he currently holds:

  • Aldar Real Estate Company — Chairman
  • First Gulf Bank — Vice-Chairman
  • The Offsets Group — Vice-Chairman
  • Abu Dhabi Ship Building Company — Vice-Chairman
  • Dolphin Energy — Chief Executive Officer
  • Abu Dhabi Water & Electricity Authority — Board of Directors
