Ahrenviöl (Danish: Arenfjolde, North Frisian: Årnfjål) is a municipality in Nordfriesland district, in northern Germany. It is part of the Amt Viöl.

The municipality covers an area of 10.11 km². Of the total population of 510, 258 are male, and 252 are female (Dec 31, 2005). The population density of the community is 50 inhabitants per km².
