Ahn Sung-ki

Ahn Sung-ki(Korean: 안성기, Hanja: 安聖基, 1 January 1952 –) is known to the public as the "National Best Actor of Korea". He was born in Daegu and was grown up as a child actor. Starting from the 1980s, he participated in nearly 100 movies. He was a graduate of the Vietnamese department of the Hankuk University of Foreign Studies and he can speak several foreign tongues. Therefore, he was appointed by the UNICEF as representatives and you can always see his advertisements if you take a plane travelling to Korea. He acted twice as the President of Korea in the movies the Romantic President (2002) and The Korean Peninsula (2006).

  • Musa (무사, Hanja:武士, 2001)

  • Two Cops (1993)
