Ahn Seung-In

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Ahn Seung-In is a South Korean scouter for Daejeon Citizen. He made his debut on 1999, playing for Bucheon FC. He appeared in 117 games in his whole career, scored 7 goals and made 8 assists. He succeded Han Jung-Gook, ex-scouter, and currently try to find suitable player for Daejeon.
