Hoang Anh Tuan (Hoàng Anh Tuấn, born 12 February 1982) in the Bac Ninh province is a Vietnamese weightlifter.

Tuan participated in the men's -56 kg class at the 2006 World Weightlifting Championships and won the bronze medal, finishing behind Li Zheng and Sergio Alvarez Boulet. He snatched 124 kg and jerked an additional 152 kg for a total of 276 kg, 4 kg behind winner Li.[1]

In some weightlifting statistics he appears misspelled as "Ahn Taun Hoang".
