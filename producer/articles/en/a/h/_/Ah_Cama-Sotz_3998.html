Ah Cama-Sotz is an industrial techno/dark ambient solo project by Belgian musician Herman Klapholz (formerly of Hybryds, also of Pow[d]er Pussy and ¥Π¥). Ah Cama-Sotz has released music on Spectre Records, Hands Productions, Old Europa Cafe, Klanggalerie and Ant-Zen, as well as Klapholz's own Bats & Cats label.

Ah Cama-Sotz incorporates elements of electronic, ambient, atmospheric, and experimental music. Klapholz's earlier works were quite rhythmic and broke into ritualistic ambient. As the music evolved and changed over time, it continued to explore darker themes, including religion, death, plagues and war.
