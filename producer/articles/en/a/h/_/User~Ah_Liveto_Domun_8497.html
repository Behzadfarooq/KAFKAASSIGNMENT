[edit] Who is Ah Liveto Domun?

       e-CmessengerFD (Earth Company's MESSENGER For Diversity)

                        - Ah Liveto Domun - 

                         == Coming soon ==

Ah Liveto Domun - ALD - (21st century) is either a symbolic author or a mythical character created by unidentified philosopher and artist from Brazil, and broadcast through the Internet. He or she - whose creator apparently prefers staying in anonymity - is self-defined as "apologist of diversity", and publishes contents usually signed on behalf of, and addressed to, everyone.

Known by issuing texts enriched with out-of-the-way semiotic resources - in which paragraphs, words, colors, phonemes, and punctuation marks peculiarly carry the reader to unusual meanings - ALD writes in general ways, sometimes comic, but like the focuses of ontology and philosophy of mind.

By ALD´s writing semiotics have been provided with unexplored way to get to the "paralanguage" or "metalanguage" which is behind the apprehensible senses, like it would be the basis of human consciousness, and perhaps fundamentals for the process of everything.

Among several other groups and subjects, politicians and media personnel have reportedly received ALD´s e-mail messages on "meanterror" and "meanpeace".

[edit] Meanterror

Meanterror stands for "terror caused by presumption or greed, afforded by ownership of wealth, arms or info manipulation; killing unlike people by any means" (Ah Liveto Domun, 2004).

[edit] Meanpeace
