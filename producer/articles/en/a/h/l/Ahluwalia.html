Ahluwalias are an ethnic group originally from Kalal community from Punjab.

[edit] History and Traditions

The term Ahluwalia came into use after the rise of a resident of the Ahluwal village, Jassa Singh Ahluwalia as a misldar of the Sikh Kingdom of Punjab. Hence his descendants came to be known by this term. Later, descendants of other residents of the village also came to be known as Ahluwalias.

[edit] Prominent Personalities

The following is the list of the prominent people from this community:

  • Jassa Singh Ahluwalia - (1718-1783) Eighteenth Century Sikh Misldar (Baron)
  • Montek Singh Ahluwalia - Former World Bank economist and Deputy-Chairman of Planning Commission of India.
  • Surendrajeet Singh Ahluwalia - Member of Parliament, Rajya Sabha (Upper House), Govt. of India [ 1986 - date.], Former Urban Development Minister, India. Former Parliamentary Affairs Minister, India.
  • Premindra Singh Bhagat - Lt. General (Retd.) in the Indian Army and Victoria Cross winner.
  • Waris Ahluwalia - Hollywood Actor
  • Harkeerat Singh Ahluwalia - Kaun Banega Crorepati Winner
  • Kiranjit Ahluwalia Ash - Famous female figure portrayed by Ashwariya Rai in the film Provoked.
  • Dr.Jasbir Singh Ahluwalia- Punjabi beaurocrat,acclaimed&awarded by the U.N.(United Nations)as the promoter of sikhism through his literary works like "Liberating Sikhism from The Sikhs"&literary works on The Akal Takht
  • Rajesh Walia- Ex Squadron Leader In the Indian Air Force awarded VM (Vayu Sena Medal ) Gallantry. Gallantry award winner for Kargil conflict. Carried out Day and Night bombing missions during 1999. Deviced a new mode of attack which reduced IAF attrition rate))
  • Milli Ahluwalia - famous New Media Designer in New York City.
  • Baba Kharak Singh, Famous Sikh Leader before and after Partition*
  • Dr. Avtar Singh Paintal,Famous Indian Doctor and Scientist
