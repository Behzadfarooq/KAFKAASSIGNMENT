Ahlström — Gullichsen family

The Ahlström — Gullichsen family is a Finnish family of industrialists, designers and artists. They are known for their cooperation with Alvar Aalto

[edit] Notable Members

  • Antti Ahlström, founder of the A. Ahlström company
  • Walter Ahlström, son on Antti
  • Maire Gullichsen (née Ahlström, 1907-1990), daughter of Walter, founder of Artek, resident at Villa Mairea
  • Alexander Gullichsen, CEO of W. Gutzeit & Co (now Stora Enso)
  • Harry Gullichsen, CEO of A. Ahlström, husband of Maire
  • Kristian Valter Gullichsen (b. 1932), son of Harry & Maire, architect in the company Gullichsen Kairamo Vormala
  • Professor Johan Gullichsen, Chairman of the Board of Directors of A. Ahlström
  • Alvar Gullichsen (b. 1961), artist known for his spoof Bonk Business Inc.
  • Johanna Gullichsen, textile designer
