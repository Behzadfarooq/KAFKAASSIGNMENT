Ahlcon Public School is a public school in East New Delhi, India.

[edit] Subjects and activities

The school teaches three languages, namely, English, Hindi and Sanskrit. It also teaches Mathematics, Science and Social Studies. There are three main streams for study beyond Class 10: science, commerce and humanities.

The school also places emphasis on other co-curricular activities such as art, craft, dance, vocal and instrumental music. Culturally, the school is a hub of activities; it has active Computer, Drama, Quizzing, Yoga, Music (instrumental as well as vocal) and Art Groups, apart from others. The school is continually a host to various inter-school as well as intra-school competitions, and a Charitable Wing for the economically stressed has been founded.
