Ah! Grab Your Dream With That Hand! (Japanese: 「ああっその手で夢をつかまえてっ」 - Aa! Sonotede yume o tsukamaete!) is the eleventh episode of season 2 of the Ah! My Goddess (TV) series first aired 2006-06-15.
