Mode(s) Single player

Quiz: Ah! My Goddess is a trivia game for Oh My Goddess! first released on August 11, 1998, for the Sega Dreamcast. Player plays as Keiichi Morisato answering to questions posed by characters from the anime/manga such as Belldandy, Skuld, Urd, Lind, Peorth
