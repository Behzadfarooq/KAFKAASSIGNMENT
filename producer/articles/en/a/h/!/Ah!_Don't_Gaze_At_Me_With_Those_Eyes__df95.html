Ah! Don't Gaze At Me With Those Eyes? (Japanese: 「ああっそんな瞳でみつめないでっ？」 - Aa! Sonna Hitomi de Mitsumenaide?) is episode number 19 of season 1 of the Oh My Goddess! TV series originally aired 2005-05-19.

Spoiler warning: Plot and/or ending details follow.

Urd prepares a love potion, which Skuld sabotages by adding extra ingredients. While the potion was intended for Keiichi, Belldandy eats one and becomes a very outgoing woman.
