I am a 20 year old student from near Manchester, England.

The majority of articles I contribute to are football related but I don't have any speciality as such. I do try to keep up with world football a little more than the average person.

I only contribute to subjects if I've done some research first and can justify my facts and views. Like most people (hopefully) on Wikipedia, I always contribute in a friendly way and never mean to offend or insult. I'm not even bothered about being shown to be wrong, as long as it's in pursuit of the big Knowledge.


And sometimes I make spelling mistakes.
