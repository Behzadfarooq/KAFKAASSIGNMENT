[edit] General Hospital

Hi, thanks for your help with the General Hospital character pages, but as a note of advice, please be cautious about adding information from personal knowledge. It is also generally not a good idea to add speculation, unless you can provide a source that verifies that this is public speculation. For example, if you see a "future tip" on soapdigest.com, you can include that information on an article, if you also link to the place at soapdigest where you got it. Thanks, and let me know if you have any questions, Elonka 01:04, 13 March 2007 (UTC)
