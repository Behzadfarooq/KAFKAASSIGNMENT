Ahal Province

Ahal is one of the Welayatlar of Turkmenistan. It is in the south of the country, bordering Iran and Afghanistan. Its capital is Änew.

It is divided into 8 etraplar (singular: etrap) and one city. Name changes since 1995 are shown in parentheses: [1][2]

  • Abadan city (formerly Büzmeýin)
  • Akbugdaý etrap (formerly Gäwers)
  • Babadaýhan etrap
  • Baharly etrap (formerly Bäherden)
  • Gökdepe etrap
  • Kaka etrap
  • Ruhabat etrap
  • Sarahs etrap
  • Tejen etrap
