Günther's Whipsnake or Burmese Vine Snake, Ahaetulla fronticincta is a species of tree snake found in Myanmar (Burma) and neighbouring northeastern India (Assam).

[edit] Food

Feeds only on fish. It strikes at a fish in water. The mild venom of the snake renders the fish immobile.
