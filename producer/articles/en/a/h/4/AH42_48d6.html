AH42, 2346 miles (3,754km); Lanzhou, China (on AH5) to Barhi, India (on AH1) AH43 or Asian Highway 43 is a route of the Asian Highway Network, running 2346 miles (3,754km) from Lanzhou on AH5 in China[1] to Barhi on AH1in India. [2].

It passes through China, Nepal,[3] and India

[edit] China

It passes through following cities in China

  • Lanzhou
  • Xining
  • Golmud
  • Lhasa
  • Xhangmu

[edit] Nepal

It passes through following cities in Nepal

  • Kodari
  • Kathmandu
  • Narayangarh on AH2
  • Pathlayia on AH2
  • Birganj

[edit] India

The route share a some portion of various Indian National Highways numberred Number 28A, Number 28 and Number 31. Various cities and towns in the Indian states of Bihar and Jharkhand situated on AH42 are as follows:
