AH46

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

AH46 is a route of the Asian Highway Network, running 946 miles (1,513km) from Kharagpur on Asian Highway 45 in West Bengal state of India to Dhule on Asian Highway 47 in Maharashtra State, India.[1].

It passes through Raipur in Chhatisgarh state and Nagpur on Asian Highway 43 in Maharashtra state of India.

The route share a big portion of Indian National Highway Number 6.

Various cities and towns in the Indian states of West Bengal, Jharkhand, Orissa, Chhatisgarh and Maharashtra lies on AH46 as follows
