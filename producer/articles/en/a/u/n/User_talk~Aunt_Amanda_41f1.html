[edit] Hello

Hi Aunt Amanda, was forwarded your name by BanyanTree as you had an interest in biliary atresia. I'm a gastroenterologist and would be happy to review any additions you make. Here are some suggestions:

  1. Making a new header: ==Header== ===Next level header===. Example: ==Causes== or ==Epidemiology==
  2. Wikify the first instance of any medical term by placing the term withing [[square brackets]]. For example: [[gall bladder]] becomes gall bladder, creating a wiki-link
  3. WP:CITE is the guideline for citations. I would try to reference any addition you make with the following <ref>Authors. Name of article. Journal and citation. PMID number</ref> within the article text. I can help with this.
  4. italics is like this: ''italics''. bold is like this: '''bold'''
  5. I'll keep an eye out for biliary atresia and help wikify things that you add. You sound like you have read a lot about the topic and it would be great if you could add your referenced knowledge base.
  6. Finally, please free to add your name to WikiProject Gastroenterology which is trying to improve gastroenterology based articles.

[edit] Liver Families

Hello. I am the user who proposed the Liver Families article be deleted. I just wanted to drop you a note thanking you for making an attempt to add content to the project. As you have correctly gathered, this encyclopedia is only for articles on subjects that have been covered by reliable third party sources. This excludes many worthwhile subjects from the encyclopedia, but it is also what distinguishes this encyclopedia from a web directory or other type of site. I'd like to wish you the best with what sounds like an outstanding support group and also express my hope you will stay and contribute on the encyclopedic topics you are familiar with.
