[edit] Rudy Aunk

Techno-Cultural Activist, Author and life long student of CosmoLogics (philosophy in the broadest sense of the term). I have a great interest in Orwellian language and its application to the subjects of Cultural Health, Cultural Literacy and Cultural Poisoning.
