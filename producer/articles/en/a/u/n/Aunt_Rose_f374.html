Aunt Rose is a 2005 horror film directed by James Tucker.

[edit] Cast

  • Joshua Nelson
  • Elizabeth Cooke
  • Kevin T. Collins
  • Velocity Chyalld

[edit] Plot

Debbie is a spoiled teenage girl who's quit school and refuses to get a job. Her parents are getting fed up with her attitude and they find out she's a lesbian, it seems as if things couldn't get any worse. Well it can, and it does when three dangerous criminal decide to seek shelter for a couple of days. But the thugs have no idea who's living in the attic...
