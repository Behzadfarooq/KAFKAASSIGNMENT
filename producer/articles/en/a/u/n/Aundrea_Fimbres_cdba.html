Aundrea Aurora Fimbres is a member of the musical group Danity Kane. She was found by Diddy on the MTV reality show Making the Band. Danity Kane's debut album was released on August 22, 2006. Aundrea is known as the baby of the group because she's the shortest; she is 5' 0". She is a soprano and has the highest vocal range of the members in Danity Kane, as confirmed by Dawn Richard. Many of Danity Kane's fans believe, including Making The Band 3 vocal coach Doc Holliday, that Aundrea is the strongest vocalist of the group.[citation needed]

Aundrea is left-handed and is of Mexican descent. She went to Claremont High School in 9th and 10th grade and Upland High School in 11th and 12th grade. Her nicknames include Drea, Little Muff, and Cayanne Pepper.
