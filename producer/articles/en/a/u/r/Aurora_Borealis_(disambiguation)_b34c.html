Aurora Borealis (disambiguation)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Aurora Borealis can mean:

  • For the astronomical phenomenon known as Aurora Borealis, a bright glow observed in the northern night sky see Aurora (astronomy).
  • Aurora Borealis (MCD) an EP by the Norwegian band Einherjer.
  • Aurora Borealis (album) an album by Cloud Cult.
  • Aurora Borealis (film) is a 2006 drama/romance film directed by James C.E. Burke and starring Joshua Jackson, Donald Sutherland, Juliette Lewis, and Louise Fletcher.
