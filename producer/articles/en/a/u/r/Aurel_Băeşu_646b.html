Aurel Băeşu (May 26, 1896—August, 1928) was a Romanian painter.

[edit] Biography

Aurel Băeşu graduated the Academy of Fine Arts from Bucharest in 1919 and the Institute of Fine Arts from Roma in 1922.

His paintings can be admired in museums in Bucharest, Bacău, Piatra Neamţ, Galaţi.

He died of tuberculosis in Piatra Neamţ when he was only 32 years old.
