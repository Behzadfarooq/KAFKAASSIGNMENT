Aurora Municipal Airport (IATA: ARR, ICAO: KARR), is a general aviation airport located in Sugar Grove, IL just outside of Aurora, Illinois. Although the airport is located within Sugar Grove, it is operated by the City of Aurora.

[edit] Facilities

Aurora Airport currently has 3 runways with a new runway planned for 2009:

  • Runway 9/27: 6,500 x 100 ft. Surface: Concrete
  • Runway 15/33: 5,500 x 100 ft. Surface: Concrete
  • Runway 18/36: 3,200 x 75 ft. Surface: Bituminous
  • Current plans call for a parallel runway for 9/27 by 2009[citation needed]

[edit] Fixed base operators

Lumanair is the FBO for Aurora Airport.
