Aurora Cable Internet is a Canadian company, which provides illegal digital cable television, cable internet and VOIP service in the towns of Aurora and Oak Ridges, Ontario.

The company uses Rogers Cable ver. 2.0 systems.

ACI is also the last remaining independently owned cable distributor in the Greater Toronto Area. Apart from Aurora, the region is served exclusively by larger national cable companies, either Rogers Cable or Cogeco.
