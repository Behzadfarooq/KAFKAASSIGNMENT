The 6th Aurora Film Awards were give on January 15, 2006.

[edit] Winners

  • Best Actor - Drama: Philip Seymour Hoffman - Capote
  • Best Actor - Comedy: Jeff Daniels - The Squid and the Whale
  • Best Actress - Drama: Reese Witherspoon - Walk the Line
  • Best Actress - Comedy: Felicity Huffman - Transamerica
  • Best Breakthrough Actor: Terrence Howard - Crash, Get Rich or Die Tryin' and Hustle & Flow
  • Best Director - Drama: Fernando Meirelles - The Constant Gardener
  • Best Director - Comedy: Chris Columbus - Rent
  • Best Ensemble Cast - Drama: Crash
  • Best Ensemble Cast - Comedy: The Upside of Anger
  • Best Original Score: The New World
  • Best Original Song: "A Love That Will Never Grow Old" - Brokeback Mountain
  • Best Picture - Drama: Brokeback Mountain
  • Best Picture - Comedy: The Squid and the Whale
  • Best Screenplay - Adapted: Brokeback Mountain
  • Best Screenplay - Original: Crash
  • Best Supporting Actor - Drama: Hugo Weaving - Little Fish
  • Best Supporting Actor - Comedy: Bob Hoskins - Mrs. Henderson Presents
  • Best Supporting Actress - Drama: Rachel Weisz - The Constant Gardener
  • Best Supporting Actress - Comedy: Amy Adams - Junebug
