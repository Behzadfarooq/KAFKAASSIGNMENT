Aural Vampire is Exo-Chika (vocals/lyrics) and Raveman (others). They hail from Tokyo, Japan and are an electro/new wave/industrial band. The duet is currently on tour in Europe and has been on magazine covers such as Astan Magazine and The Dose. They are currently under an Indie record label.
