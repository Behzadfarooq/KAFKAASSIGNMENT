Aurora State Airport (IATA: UAO, ICAO: KUAO), is a public airport located one miles (1.6 km) northwest of the city of Aurora in Marion County, Oregon, USA.

Primarily a general aviation airport, Aurora has a large amount of business aviation based at the field. In addition the airport serves as the home to two major aviation companies Van's Aircraft and Columbia Helicopters.
