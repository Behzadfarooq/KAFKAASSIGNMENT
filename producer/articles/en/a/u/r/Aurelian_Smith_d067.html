Aurelian Smith is a professional wrestler better known as Grizzly Smith. Aurelian Smith is the father of Jake "The Snake" Roberts with whom he has a strained relationship. He has another son Michael (Sam Houston) and a daughter Robin (Rockin' Robin) from another relationship, who also became wrestlers.

[edit] Profile

  • Trained by: "Tiny" Smith
  • Debut: 1958
  • Previous Gimmicks: Grizzly Smith, The Kentuckian, Sky-Hi Jones, Tiny Anderson
  • Signiature Moves: The Bearhug, The Stump Puller

[edit] Championships and accomplishments

  • National Wrestling Alliance
  • National
  • NWA American Tag Team Championship (1 time) - with Fritz Von Erich
  • NWA Canadian Tag Team Championship (Vancouver version) (1 time) - with Don Leo Jonathan
  • NWA United States Tag Team Championship (Tri-State version) (2 times) - with Luke Brown
  • Regional
  • NWA Texas Heavyweight Championship (1 time)
  • World Wrestling Association (Los Angeles)
  • WWA World Tag Team Championship (1 time) - with Luke Brown
