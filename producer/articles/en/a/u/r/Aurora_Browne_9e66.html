Comedian Aurora Browne

Aurora Browne is a Canadian actress and comedian originally from Thunder Bay, Ontario, currently based out of Toronto. In 2000, she was nominated for the Tim Sims Encouragement Fund Award. Also in that year she joined Toronto's Second City comedy troupe and performed with them for three years. She co-stars in the CTV/Comedy Network production Comedy Inc. She made a guest appearance on Corner Gas.

Aurora had the role of Vampira in the Toronto Fringe Festival production of Ed Wood's classic Plan 9 From Outer Space named Plan Live From Outer Space.
