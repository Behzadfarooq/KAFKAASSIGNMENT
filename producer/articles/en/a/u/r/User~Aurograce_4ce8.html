Dipak Mehta

Born: 1960
Bhavnagar, Gujarat, India
Occupation: Techno-commercial industrialist

Dipak Mehta is a techno-commercial industrialist having vast experience in the field of trade, commerce, industries, consumer affairs etc. He is a self-made person with commendable entrepreneurship qualities.

He is hailing from a moderate Hindu Brahmin family. His father has been Government employee and is retired now. He passed out his B.E. Mechanical degree from Gujarat University in the year 1982 with First class distinction. Subsequently he also acquired degree of Post Graduation in Sales & Marketing. By nature he has been fond of developing human relations, hence he had chosen Industrial marketing as his profession.

He served for large & prominent engineering companies like Larsen & Toubro , Ingersoll Rand, Kirloskar Pneumatics & Fenner India for almost 16 years. During his tenure with these companies, he acquired excellent knowledge & valuable experience while dealing with various types of industries & distributors. Many products were conceptualized, designed & marketed by him for these companies were most successful and even today generating good revenues for them.

The Entrepreneur in him inspired him to start his own venture. A manufacturing plant was started in the year 1998 to produce special purpose Electronic equipments for Dairy & Agro sector. The equipments are considered as good quality products by the industry.

During his varied professional experience of almost 25 years, he has become quite knowledgeable in following fields.

1. In-depth knowledge about working of large scale, medium scale & small-scale industries.

2. Working of Distributors, Whole sellers, Dealers, Traders, Retailers, Franchises etc.

3. Government Licensing policies, procedures & problems related to it. He made presentations about it at State & Central level and suggested many ideas to simplify the process.

4. Consumer relations & affairs is his favorite subject. He had prepared a Software presentation called “ Grahak Suraksha Abhiyan ” for The Controller, Legal metrology dept., Weights & Measure unit, Gov’t. of Gujarat without charging anything for it. This presentation was then widely circulated by the department for increasing awareness among people.

5. Various certification procedures like ISO 9001: 2000, BIS marking, etc.

6. Commercial aspects like Income tax rules, Value added tax rules, Professional taxes, Excise duty rules, General insurance, Labor laws, etc.
