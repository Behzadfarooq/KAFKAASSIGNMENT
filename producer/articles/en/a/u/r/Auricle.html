Auricle has the following meanings:

Auricle of barley (Hordeum vulgare)
  • A small conical pouch that projects from each atrium of the heart.
    • Right auricle
    • Left auricle
  • An older name for the left and right atria of the heart.
  • The external portion of the ear, the pinna.
  • In some grasses it is the name given to ear-like outgrowths at the junction of the leaf blade and the leaf sheath.
  • It can be found in a fetal pig.
