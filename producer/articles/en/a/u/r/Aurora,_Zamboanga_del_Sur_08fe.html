Aurora, Zamboanga del Sur

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Aurora is a 3rd class municipality in the province of Zamboanga del Sur, Philippines. According to the 2000 census, it has a population of 42,820 people in 8,329 households.

[edit] Barangays
