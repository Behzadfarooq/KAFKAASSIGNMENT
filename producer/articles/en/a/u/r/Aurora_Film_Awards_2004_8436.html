The winners of the 5th Aurora Film Awards:

[edit] Winners

  • Best Actor - Drama: Leonardo DiCaprio - The Aviator
  • Best Actor - Comedy: Jamie Foxx - Ray
  • Best Actress - Drama: Hilary Swank - Million Dollar Baby
  • Best Actress - Comedy: Annette Bening - Being Julia
  • Best Director - Drama: Martin Scorsese - The Aviator
  • Best Director - Comedy: Alexander Payne - Sideways
  • Best Picture - Drama: The Aviator
  • Best Picture - Comedy: Sideways
  • Best Supporting Actor - Drama: Clive Owen - Closer
  • Best Supporting Actor - Comedy: Thomas Haden Church - Sideways
  • Best Supporting Actress - Drama: Natalie Portman - Closer
  • Best Supporting Actress - Comedy: Virginia Madsen - Sideways
