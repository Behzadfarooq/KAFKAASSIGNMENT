[edit] General Information

My name is Ben. I'm a lyricist and enjoy music (see below subcategory). I like Wikipedia because it allows me to share my knowledge and pick up some new knowledge. It is my opinion that if school was more like Wikipedia, more would appreciate it. It is my dream to become a bassist in a band.

[edit] Favorite Music

Music plays a huge part in my life. In my spare time, you are most likely to find me listening to music or talking about something music-related.

My favorite musical genres include emo, pop-punk, alternative rock, grunge, and ska. Some of my favorite bands include (but are not limited to) Less Than Jake, Nirvana, Motion City Soundtrack, Fall Out Boy, Gorillaz, Yellowcard, and Sum 41.
