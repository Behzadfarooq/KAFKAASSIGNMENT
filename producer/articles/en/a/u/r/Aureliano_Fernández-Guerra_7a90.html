Aureliano Fernández-Guerra y Orbe (1816 – September 7, 1894) was a Spanish historian, epigrapher and antiquarian, also remembered as a poet and playwright. He became a member of the Real Academia Española from 1860 and served as its Archivist and Librarian from 1872.
