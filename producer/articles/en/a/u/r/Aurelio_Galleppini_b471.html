Aurelio Galleppini

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Aurelio Galleppini, known for his nickname Galep (August 28, 1917 – March 10, 1994) was an Italian comic book artist and illustrator.

Galep is most famous as graphic designer of the Tex Willer, probably the most famous Italian comic book in the 20th century. He illustrated Tex Willer from number #1 and was the illustrator all the cover illustrations of the regular series right up to issue number #400.
