Aurora Borealis is the original demo released by the band Einherjer in 1994. It was released as an MCD in 1996 following the success of the band's EP Leve Vikingånden. The first and last tracks have Norwegian language lyrics, while the middle two have lyrics in English.

[edit] Track listing

  1. "De Sorte Sjøers Land" (The Land of Black Lakes)
  2. "Aurora Borealis"
  3. "Witchking"
  4. "Einherjer"

Music by Storesund/Glesnes/Bjelland. Lyrics by Glesnes/Bjelland.
