Aurora College
Established 1981
President Maurice Evans
Location Fort Smith, Northwest Territories, Canada
Website www.auroracollege.nt.ca

Aurora College, formerly Arctic College, is a college in the Northwest Territories, Canada with campuses in Inuvik, Fort Smith and Yellowknife. They have learning centres in many communities in the NWT.
