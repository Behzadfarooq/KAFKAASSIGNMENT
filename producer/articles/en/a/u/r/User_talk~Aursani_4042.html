[edit] I can't read Sindhi in Devenagri script

I can't read Sindhi in Devenagri script and hence don't know how similar it is and how different it is. It might me containing many words from Hindi, but I am not sure. But what I insist on is that the main Sindhi page of the Wikipedia should be in Sindhi Arabic script, as Sindhi is written, read and understood by millions of Sindhis in Sindhi-Arabic script and not in Sindhi-Devenagri script. Sindhi in its land SINDH is written, read and understood in Sindhi Arabic script and not in Sindhi Devenagri script that is insgnificant even in India. Sindhi was declared official language by the British govt. in Sindhi Arabic script in 1850. Since then, it is used in offices, taught in school and printed in Media in arabic Script only. But unfortunately, the Wikipedia and Wikitionary simply seem to ignore this fact and has failed to provide support for Sindhi Arabic script. While selecting the language option, one doesn't find Sindhi (in Sindhi Arabic script). 193.52.94.2 18:50, 17 February 2006 (UTC) Ahsan Ahmad Ursani
