Aurelio Gonzales

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Aurelio Gonzales (born July 26, 1940) is a Spanish cyclist.

He won the King of the Mountains prize and one stage in the 1968 Tour de France. In 1967 he won the King of the Mountains prize in the Giro d'Italia and in 1969 he won the King of the Mountains prize in the Tour de Suisse.
