Aurtis Whitley (born May 1, 1977) is a Trinidad and Tobagonian footballer who plays as a midfielder for San Juan Jabloteh in his home country.

Whitely has spent all but six months of his career - when he played for Vitoria Setubal in Portugal - in Trinidad and is regarded as one of the strongest player's in its national league. He has been a part of the Trinidad and Tobago national team since 2000 and took part in the 2006 FIFA World Cup in Germany.
