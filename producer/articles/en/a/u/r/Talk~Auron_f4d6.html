[edit] Auron lost his eye?

Although Auron does keep one of his eyes shut most of the time, I've seen times when he's opened it...

For example: look at the cutscene where you first see Auron in the blitzball stadium in Spira. He clearly has both eyes open. I would like somebody to remove that part about him losing his eye unless they have solid proof. In the game, it never mentions a single thing about him "losing his eye." He just got severely injured and died shortly afterwards. Just because he has a little scar on his eye and keeps it shut most (but not all) of the time doesn't mean that he lost his eye. —The preceding unsigned comment was added by 65.60.132.49 (talk) 21:10, 25 January 2007 (UTC).
