Aubrey Woods

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Bill, The Candy Store Owner from the 1971 film, Willy Wonka & the Chocolate Factory.

Aubrey Woods (born 9 April 1928 in London, England) is a British actor.

His television credits include: Z Cars, Up Pompeii!, Doctor Who (in the serial Day of the Daleks), Blake's 7, The Hitchhikers Guide to the Galaxy, Auf Wiedersehen, Pet and Ever Decreasing Circles.

He also played a notable role in Willy Wonka & the Chocolate Factory. He was the Candy Store Owner where he sang the song The Candy Man.
