Aubrey Morgan O'Day (born February 11, 1984) is an American singer, songwriter, model, dancer, and member of the musical group Danity Kane.

O'Day was born in San Francisco, California, and is of Irish descent. As a teen, she performed in various theatrical productions with the On Stage Theatre Company. O'Day graduated in 2001 from La Quinta High School (La Quinta, California). During her high school years, she was on the dance and cheerleading squads and was also in the drama club. O'Day was a member of the Alpha Chi Omega Sorority while attending the University of California, Irvine. O'Day also walked the catwalk at Heatherette's Fall 2007 runway show at Mercedes-Benz Fashion Week held on February 6, 2007.[1]

She was discovered on the MTV reality show Making the Band 3, founded by Diddy. Danity Kane's album debuted on August 22, 2006, at #1 on the Billboard 200 charts. Danity Kane is currently on tour as the opening act for Christina Aguilera on her Back to Basics Tour with the Pussycat Dolls.

[edit] Pregnancy Rumors

In late March of 2007, Mediatakeout.com reported that Miss O'Day was pregnant, and that Diddy was the father. A few days later, as an April Fools' Day joke, O'Day took fellow Danity Kane member D.Woods to a drugstore and proceeded to buy pregnancy tests, diapers, and a lot of baby shampoo, while being videotaped. Since then, O'Day has released two statements pertaining to this matter. The first one was posted on Bad Boy Online.

"I am not pregnant. Recent rumors linking me to Sean Combs, CEO of my label Bad Boy Entertainment are completely untrue and ridiculous."

The second one was a Myspace bulletin that came directly from her official Myspace page.

"In light of the recent and quite absurd rumors of my pregnancy, I would like to state very clearly that I am not and have never been pregnant, nor has my relationship with Diddy been anything more than strictly professional. I am extremely thankful to be a part of Danity Kane and a Bad Boy artist and it is disappointing that one edited photo could challenge my integrity as an artist. These rumors should serve as another unfortunate reminder of how much influence the media can inflict on public opinion. I'd like to thank everyone for their support and for sticking with me through this."

[edit] Trivia

  • She is the youngest member of Danity Kane.
  • She currently has a boyfriend whose name is DJ Cassidy.
  • She is naturally brunette, but she dies her hair blond.
  • She was in all 3 seasons of Making the Band 3 (a feat only matched by fellow Danity Kane member Aundrea Fimbres).
  • She was listed as #81 in Blender (magazine)'s "100 Hottest People" in their September issue and was listed at #86 on the Maxim Hot 100 countdown in 2006.[2] Blender again listed her on their list of hottest women in film and TV in 2006.[3]
  • She appeared in a special celebrity episode of the MTV series Exposed (which first aired on April 7, 2007) with Melissa Smith, who was also on Making The Band 3.
