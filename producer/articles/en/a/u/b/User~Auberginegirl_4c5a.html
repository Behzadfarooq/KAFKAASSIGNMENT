Aubergine Girl (born June 23, 1982 in Denmark) is called Christine in real life. She is currently studying multimedia design somewhere in Denmark. However, the dream is to move to Australia (where some of her family resides) and to eventually get a career in the music industry, either as a performing artist or more behind the scenes.

The following is a list of Christine's various interests:

  • Music – especially most kinds of rock music
  • Singing
  • Writing poetry and lyrics
  • Body modification – she has six piercings and one upcoming tattoo
  • Travelling – especially to Australia
  • Web design
  • Fan fiction – especially Savage Garden slash
