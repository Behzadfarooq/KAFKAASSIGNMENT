Auburn Middle School

Auburn Middle School, also known as AMS, is a school in Auburn, Maine. It contains grades seven and eight. The AMS mascot is the Falcon, and the school colors are maroon and white. The sports program supports baseball, basketball, field hockey, track and field, soccer, softball, and skiing.

Students are split up into numbered teams, then into homerooms on those teams, and then even further into advocacy groups. The children only stay in their advocacy groups for a short period of time. Then after this activity, their day is divided into blocks, sometimes changing between days. Within a team the homerooms are divided alphabetically.
