The district of Aubonne is a district of the canton of Vaud in Switzerland. The seat of the district is the town of Aubonne.

The following municipalities are located in the district:

  • Apples
  • Aubonne
  • Ballens
  • Berolle
  • Bière
  • Bougy-Villars
  • Féchy
  • Gimel
  • Longirod
  • Marchissy
  • Mollens
  • Montherod
  • Pizy
  • Saubraz
  • Saint-George
  • Saint-Livres
  • Saint-Oyens
