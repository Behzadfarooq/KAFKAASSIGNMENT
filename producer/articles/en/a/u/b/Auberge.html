Auberge (a French word meaning "inn") is an album by Chris Rea, released in 1991.

[edit] Track listing

All songs by Chris Rea.

  1. "Auberge"
  2. "Gone Fishing"
  3. "You're Not A Number"
  4. "Heaven"
  5. "Set Me Free"
  6. "Red Shoes"
  7. "Sing A Song Of Love To Me"
  8. "Every Second Counts"
  9. "Looking For The Summer"
  10. "And You My Love"
  11. "The Mention Of Your Name"

Some CD issues also contain the otherwise non-LP single "Winter Song".
