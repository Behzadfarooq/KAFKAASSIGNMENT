Aubrey Dollar

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Aubrey Dollar (born September 23, 1980 in Raleigh, North Carolina) is an American television and film actress.

She studied at Needham B. Broughton High School, where she was classmate of fellow actor Colin Fickes. She also went to prestigious Boston University.

She is better known for her role as Judy Kramer in Point Pleasant a short-lived 2005 series on FOX and for her role as Marcy Bender in Dawson's Creek. She is also known for her role as Marina Nadine Cooper in the soap opera Guiding Light.

Her younger sister, Caroline Dollar, is also an actress.
