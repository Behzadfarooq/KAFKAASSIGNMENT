Aubrey Suwito

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Aubrey Suwito is a Malaysian music composer and producer. Aubrey Suwito had written songs for many artiste, most notably Gemilang for Jaclyn Victor which won multiple awards including the Asian Music Festival and Anugerah Juara Lagu. He was the musical director on Malaysian Idol. Currently, he works as musical director for the hit reality television talent search competition, One in a Million.
