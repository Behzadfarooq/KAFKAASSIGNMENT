Auburn Hospital

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Auburn Hospital is a 155 bed hospital in Auburn, which is in the western suburbs of Sydney, Australia. It provides basic 24-hour emergency facilities, as well as a medical, surgical, paediatrics, and maternity facilities as well as coronary care and intensive care facilities.

This facility is part of the Eastern Cluster of Sydney West Area Health Service.
