Aubrey Meyer is the author of 'Contraction & Convergence: The Global Solution to Climate Change' as discussed on BBC 9 February 2006.

He co-founded the Global Commons Institute in 1990. A CV is here: - http://www.gci.org.uk/AubreyMeyer/CV_Aubrey_Meyer_1.pdf
