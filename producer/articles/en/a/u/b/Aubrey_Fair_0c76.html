Aubrey Fair was an association football player who played mainly as left back for West Ham United.

Fair made his debut for West Ham in an FA Cup game at Leyton on 3 November 1901, but this was his only appearance that season. He never held a permanent spot in the Hammers first team, and failed to appear at all for the club during the 1905-06 season. His last of 33 West Ham appearances was a 2-1 home win against Queens Park Rangers on February 25, 1907.
