Auburn, Victoria

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Auburn Hotel. Built in 1888.

Auburn is a suburb of Melbourne, Australia, in the state of Victoria with a postcode of 3123. It is in the local government areas of the City of Boroondara and generally considered as part of Hawthorn.

The suburb is renowned for its outstanding commercial Victorian architecture, including the Auburn Hotel, a giant 3 storey coffee palace in the Second Empire style built in 1888, part of a well preserved streetscape on Auburn Road.

The area is serviced by Auburn railway station.
