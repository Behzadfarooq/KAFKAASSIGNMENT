Auðunn Blöndal

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Auðunn Blöndal is an Icelandic television personality, actor and comedian. He is best known as the co-host of the comedy shows 70 mínútur and Strákarnir, and as the host of Tekinn, the Icelandic version of Ashton Kutcher's show Punk'd. He also starred in the comedy series Svínasúpan (English: The Pig Soup), and in the Icelandic production of Richard Herring's one-man show Talking Cock, known as Typpatal in Icelandic.
