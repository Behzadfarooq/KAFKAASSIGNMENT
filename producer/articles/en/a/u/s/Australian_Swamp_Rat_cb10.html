The Australian Swamp Rat is a species of rat native to the area around Melbourne. they currently live primarily in the outer urban areas East of Melbourne. They are unrelated to Black or Brown rats and are not problematic. The Swamp Rat can be seen at places like the Healesville Sanctuary, where they live in the grounds. The rangers at Healesville tolerate them, since they (the rats) are native to the area and carry no dangerous diseases.
