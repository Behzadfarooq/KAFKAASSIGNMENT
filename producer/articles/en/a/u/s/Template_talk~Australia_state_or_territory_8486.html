[edit] Maps

With the new maps there is no reason the background of the Map section needs to be white anymore (they have transparent backgrounds). It could be changed to match the grey, like the City info box does. The maps are available at User:Martyman/My Images/Maps. --Martyman-(talk) 12:44, 12 October 2005 (UTC)

[edit] Proposed new look

I have created a new look for this template. This look is meant to help this template more closely resemble (and function like) other geographic infoboxes such as (Template:Infobox Country, Template:Infobox city, and Template:Infobox U.S. state. Those templates all use common css. See Wikipedia:Geographical infoboxes for more information. Also, I think this version is more appealing. I'll make the switch in a few days unless there is a hugh uproar. Comments? —MJCdetroit 02:23, 3 December 2006 (UTC)

Very nice! I've made some minor tweaks. -- Rick Block (talk) 17:45, 3 December 2006 (UTC)

I made the switch and all looks good. I changed the example above to match the new look with some <!--ed notes--> on useage. —MJCdetroit 18:45, 4 December 2006 (UTC)
