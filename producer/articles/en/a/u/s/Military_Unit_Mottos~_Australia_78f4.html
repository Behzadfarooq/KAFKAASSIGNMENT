Military Unit Mottos: Australia

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

  • The Australian Army
    • Corps
      • Royal Australian Army Medical Corps: Paulatim (Little by Little)
      • Royal Australian Corps of Transport: Par Oneri (Equal to the Task)
      • Royal Australian Engineers: Ubique (Everywhere)
      • Royal Australian Corps of Military Police: Praesto et Persto (To Stand Out and to Stand Firm)
      • Royal Regiment of Australian Artillery: Ubique (Everywhere) and Quo Fas Et Gloria Ducunt (Where Right and Glory Leads)
      • Royal Australian Signals Corps: Certa Cito (Swift and Sure)
    • Regiments
      • Royal Australian Regiment: Duty First
      • Royal Queensland Regiment: Pro Aris Et Focis (For Altars and Hearths)
      • 1st Armoured Regiment: Paratus (Ready)
      • 2nd Cavalry Regiment: Courage
      • 4th/19th Prince of Wales's Light Horse Regiment: Ich Dien (I Serve)
      • 10th Light Horse Regiment: Percute et Percute Velociter (Strike, and Strike Swiftly)
      • 12th/16th Hunter River Lancers: Virtutis Fortuna Comes (Fortune is the Companion of Valour)
      • Special Air Service Regiment: Who Dares Wins
      • 1st Commando Regiment: Strike Swiftly
    • Other
      • Australian Army Cadets: Courage, Initiative, Teamwork
    • Historic Light Horse Regiments:
      • 4th Australian Light Horse (Hunter River Lancers) Tenax et Fidelis (Steadfast and Faithful)
      • 6th Australian Light Horse (New England Light Horse) - pre 1907: 'Hearth and Homes', then Virtutis Fortuna Comes(Fortune is the Companion of Valour)
      • 12th Light Horse Regiment Virtutis Fortuna Comes (Fortune is the Companion of Valour).
      • 16th Light Horse Regiment Tenax et Fidelis (Steadfast and Faithful).


  • Royal Australian Air Force: Per Ardua Ad Astra (Through Adversity to the Stars)
    • Commands
      • Air Command: Alert and Ready
      • Training Command: Train to Defend
    • Force Element Groups (FEGs)
      • Air Combat Group: Defend and Strike
      • Air Lift Group: Mobility and Reach
      • Combat Support Group: Project and Protect
      • Surveillance and Response Group: Foremost Sentinel
    • Wings
      • 395 Expeditionary Combat Support Wing: Expeditious Support
      • 396 Combat Support Wing: Steadfast in Support
      • 41 Wing: Pass Not Unseen
      • 42 Wing: Defend From Above
      • 44 Wing: Steadfastness
      • 78 Wing: Fight
      • 81 Wing: Prepared to Fight
      • 82 Wing: Find and Destroy
      • 84 Wing: Guide and Deliver
      • 86 Wing: Precision
      • 92 Wing: Watch and Ward
