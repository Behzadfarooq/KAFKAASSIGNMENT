1st Austin Film Critics Association Awards

2005

Best Picture:
Crash

The 1st Austin Film Critics Association Awards, honoring the best in film making in 2005, were given in 2006.

[edit] Winners

  • Best Picture:
    • Crash
  • Best Actor:
    • Philip Seymour Hoffman - Capote
  • Best Actress:
    • Reese Witherspoon - Walk the Line
  • Best Animated Film:
    • Sin City
  • Best Director:
    • Paul Haggis - Crash
  • Best Screenplay - Adapted:
    • Brokeback Mountain - Larry McMurtry and Diana Ossana
  • Best Screenplay - Original:
    • The Squid and the Whale - Noah Baumbach
  • Best Supporting Actor:
    • William Hurt - A History of Violence
  • Best Supporting Actress:
    • Laura Linney - The Squid and the Whale
  • Best Documentary:
    • Murderball
  • Best Foreign Language Film:
    • Oldboy
  • Best Breakthrough Performance:
    • Terrence Howard - Crash, Hustle & Flow
  • Most Promising Filmmaker:
    • Noah Baumbach - The Squid and the Whale
  • Austin Film Award:
    • Sin City - Robert Rodriguez
