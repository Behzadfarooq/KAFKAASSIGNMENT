Austria has competed at most of the modern Summer Olympic Games, missing only the 1920 Games. The nation was one of 14 to compete at the inaugural 1896 Games.

[edit] Fencing

Austria's first fencing medals came in the second edition of the sport, when the Austrian fencers won the bronze medals in both the amateurs and masters sabre events.
