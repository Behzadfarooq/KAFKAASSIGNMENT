[edit] Renaming yourself on various Wikis

On the wikis which support it, a bureaucrat can rename you and you'll retain your edit history. It's too late on Wikinews as you've already created the username you wish to move to, but for future reference, please see Wikinews:Changing username. --Chiacomo talk 06:13, 28 April 2006 (UTC)
