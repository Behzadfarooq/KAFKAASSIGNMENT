Austin McIntosh (born 5 November 1987 in Sheffield) is a professional footballer who plays as a defender for Mansfield Town in The Football League in England. He made his first-team debut for the Stags in the closing stages of the 2004-05 season, but missed most of 2005-06 with an eye injury. He is regarded as a promising youngster, and signed a professional contract with the Stags in the summer of 2006. McIntosh is primarily used as right-back, but can also play in central defence.
