Austin Kanallakan (born May 23, 1991) is an American figure skater. He is the 2005 Novice national champion. In his first year on the Junior Grand Prix circuit, he qualified for the Junior Grand Prix Final, where he placed second. He later went on to place 13th as a junior at nationals. In 2007, he came back from a disappointing short program to win the free skate and claim the bronze medal on the junior level.
