Austin O'Brien (born May 11, 1981 in Eugene, Oregon) is an American actor. To date, his most famous role is arguably as the comic sidekick co-lead 'Danny Madigan' from the Arnold Schwarzenegger-movie Last Action Hero, followed by his turn into romance as 'Nick Zsigmond' in My Girl 2, and a minor part in both The Lawnmower Man and its sequel.

Austin is the older brother of actor Trever O'Brien. He is also a devoted Christian, coming from a religious family, who always supported him during his childhood.[citation needed]
