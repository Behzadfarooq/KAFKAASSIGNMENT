Athletes who have played Australian rules football at the elite level - the AFL (previously known as VFL) - or at SANFL and WAFL level.

Categories consist of all 16 clubs, as well as sub-categories for SANFL and WAFL players.

Trivia: Out of the 14,027 players to have played VFL/AFL football, not one has had a surname starting with "X."
