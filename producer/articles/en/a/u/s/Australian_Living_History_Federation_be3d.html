[edit] Objectives

The objectives of the Association are:

  • To act as a focus for historical re-enactment and living history societies portraying aspects of World history within Australia
  • To promote the safe, authentic and realistic re-enactment of all periods of World History.
  • To promote the interests of members participating in historical re-enactment and related activities by (for example) obtaining and maintaining Australia-wide insurance coverage for members
  • Instigating and promoting contact, and maintaining liaison with display organisers and Government bodies
  • To hold special observances, meetings, authentic historical re-enactments, promotional and cultural activities.
  • To do such other things as are conducive or incidental to the attainment of the above objects.

[edit] Activities

ALHF and all member groups are involved in some or all of the following activities:

  • Living history: re-creating civilian & military activities of the particular culture and time period portrayed by the group.
  • Historical reenactment: the physical reconstruction of history.
  • Preservation: activities related to the preservation of artefacts, skills, crafts or historical sites.
  • Research: into a particular period or field in world history.
  • Public Performances and displays of our activities.

[edit] Legal Status

The Association is a non-political, non-profit body, incorporated in NSW with member groups and individuals in NSW, Victoria, ACT, South Australia and Queensland.
