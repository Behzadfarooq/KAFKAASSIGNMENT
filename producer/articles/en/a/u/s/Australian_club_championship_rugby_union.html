Australian club championship rugby union

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

A challenge match between the Brisbane and Sydney club premiers was played in a regular basis from 1982 to 1992 (inclusive) and on an ad-hoc basis in various other years. The fixture was abandoned in the mid 1990s when the NSWRU insisted on playing each match at Concord Oval in Sydney, rather than on a rotating basis.

[edit] Winners summary

  • Winners by City
    • 9 wins: Sydney Premiers
    • 6 wins: Brisbane Premiers
  • Multiple Winners
    • 7 wins: Randwick
    • 3 wins: Brothers
