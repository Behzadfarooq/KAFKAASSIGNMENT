The Australian Poker League Pty. Ltd (APL) is an entertainment company involved in the organizing, development and marketing of a ‘Play for Fun’ poker tournament competition, as well as the licensing and sale of branded products and corporate sponsorships. It is the world's largest tournament poker organisation, larger than any other free or 'pay to play' poker organisation in the world.

The concept behind the league and competition is to offer an event where participants with different levels of experience, from novices to professionals, can compete in a friendly environment for fun, experience, and often prizes (for the highest ranking players of the night).

In its short history the APL has become a leader in the industry, as well as a major player in the entertainment arena as well, attracting people from all walks of like seeking a different leisure alternative.

Apart from the league events the APL organizes celebrity, charity and corporate poker tournaments based on its normal event format to increase the company’s reach and brand awareness as well as support important causes, but results do not count towards the league.
