Category:Australian lawyers

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Classification: People: By occupation: Lawyers: By nationality: Australian
also: Australia: People: By occupation: Lawyers


This category is for those who have practiced law in Australia representing and/or advising clients, whether private or government. Technically, anyone with a license to practice law in a specific jurisdiction is a lawyer; however, this category is reserved for those who meet the most obvious connotation of the word. Otherwise, please see Category:Australian judges. More than one designation may apply to certain individuals—please see individual category descriptions for proper application.
