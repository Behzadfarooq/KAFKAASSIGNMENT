Austin Ryan Fuentes (born February 23, 1982, in San Dimas, California), is a billionaire-heir and philanthropist.

Fuentes’ mother died from complications due to her Multiple Sclerosis when he was 12. Fuentes was raised by his mother’s side throughout his life and attended Don Bosco Technical Institute (a poly-technical Catholic high school & college, see St. John Bosco).

Fuentes regularly attends and supports several major charity events and organizations such as the Nancy Davis Race to Erase MS Gala.{See Photo}

Because of the manner in which his holdings are diversified his exact monetary worth is unknown to the media.

Fuentes is also involved heavily in the Hollywood community and promotes pro-latino/Hispanic ideals.

He is often credited as Austin Fuentes.
