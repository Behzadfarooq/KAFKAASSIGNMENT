Portal:Australia/Anniversaries/February/February 9

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

< Portal:Australia | Anniversaries | February
  • 1889 - Peter Lalor, the Commander in Chief of the Eureka Stockade rebellion, dies aged 62.
  • 1914 - Sir Ronald Munro-Ferguson, a landholder and provost of Kirkcaldy appointed as the new Governor-General.
  • 1923 - Stanley Bruce becomes Prime Minister of Australia.
  • 1937 - Cairns is hit by a tropical cyclone.
  • 1986 - Women were ordained as priests in the Anglican Church of Australia for the first time.
  • 2002 - Australian Labor Party, led by Mike Rann wins the South Australian election.
