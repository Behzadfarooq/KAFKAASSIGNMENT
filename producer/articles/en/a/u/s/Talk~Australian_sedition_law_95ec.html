[edit] Source bias

Every "debate" article cited is from a far left perspective. Without passing judgement on the matter one way or another, readers should know that such a one-sided roundup of press articles is hardly "debate" to speak of.

They're mostly just what were the relevant top hits in google (most of which I added simply to bookmark them while composing the article). Frankly, "the right" isn't particularly interested in the issue, and in any case The Age and The Australian are mainstream/rightist sources. That said, feel free to delete redundant or misrepresentative articles, and indeed to reformat the whole "Debate" section in some other fashion; it's not a wikipedia standard, after all.
