Austin Graduate School of Theology

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Austin Graduate School of Theology, formerly known as Institute for Christian Studies, is located in Austin, Texas, in the United States of America. It is a private Christian theological college (although it refers to itself as a seminary, unlike most such institutions associated with the Churches of Christ).

The campus is located in central Austin at the corner of University Avenue and Martin Luther King, Jr. Avenue, roughly midway between the Texas State Capitol and the Littlefield Fountain on the University of Texas at Austin campus.

AGST is accredited by the Commission of Colleges of the Southern Association of Colleges and Schools.
