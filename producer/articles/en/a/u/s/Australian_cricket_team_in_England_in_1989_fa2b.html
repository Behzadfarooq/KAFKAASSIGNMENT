Australian cricket team in England in 1989

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

You have new messages (last change).

Australia won the 1989 Ashes series against England. Australia won four test matches, and two test matches were drawn:

  • 1st Test (Leeds) — Australia won by 210 runs - [1]
  • 2nd Test (Lord's) — Australia won by 6 wickets - [2]
  • 3rd Test (Birmingham) — drawn - [3]
  • 4th Test (Manchester) — Australia won by 9 wickets - [4]
  • 5th Test (Nottingham) — Australia won by an innings and 180 runs - [5]
  • 6th Test (The Oval) — drawn - [6]
