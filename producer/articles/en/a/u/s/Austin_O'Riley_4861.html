Austin O'Riley (born on January 27, 1980 in California) is an American pornographic actress.

O'Riley grew up in Southern California, near Palm Springs and Big Bear. Her family was Irish and strict Roman Catholics.

She reportedly convinced her girflfriend Britney Foster to enter the porn industry as well.

Austin became a popular star almost from her first films in 2001. While this has been attributed to her girl next door looks, more often her popularity stems from her large derriere and much of her movie work involving anal sex.

While no official retirement announcement has been made, O'Riley has not appeared in any new scenes since early 2004 and seems to have left the industry altogether.
