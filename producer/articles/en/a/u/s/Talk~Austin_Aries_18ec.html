Shouldn't the website be for Austin_Starr instead of Austin_Aries, since he is clearly more known by his TNA name than his indy wrestling name? Wikipedia guidlines state to use the most popular name. 12.41.14.138 19:53, 5 December 2006 (UTC)

Other than the fact that long time indies fan will say he is much more known as ARIES, according to Wikipedia's policy he should be known as Austin Starr. It just facts.

The guidlines states the most used name, not the most popular name (Wikipedia guildlines for stage names).
