[edit] Medals

Austria finished in 21st position in the final medal rankings, with one gold medal and four medals overall.

[edit] Gold

  • Elisabeth Theurer and Mon Cherie — Equestrian, Individual Dressage

[edit] Silver

  • Wolfgang Mayrhofer — Sailing, Men's Finn Individual Competition
  • Hubert Raudaschl and Karl Ferstl — Sailing, Star Team Competition (mixed)

[edit] Bronze

  • Gerhard Petritsch — Shooting, 25m Rapid Fire Pistol (60 shots)


[edit] Results by event

[edit] Archery

In its second appearance in archery competition at the Olympics, Austria again entered only one man.

Men's Individual Competition:

  • Peter Mitterer — 2336 points (→ 21st place)

[edit] Athletics

Men's Marathon

  • Josef Steiner
  • Final — 2:24:24 (→ 39th place)
