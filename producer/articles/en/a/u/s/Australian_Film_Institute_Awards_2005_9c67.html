Australian Film Institute Awards 2005

26 November 2005

Best Film:
Look Both Ways

The 2005 Australian Film Institute Awards, given in on November 26, 2005, honored the best in film for 2005.

[edit] Winners

  • Best Actor: Hugo Weaving - Little Fish
  • Best Actress: Cate Blanchett - Little Fish
  • Best Cinematography: The Proposition - Benoit Delhomme
  • Best Costume Design: The Proposition - Margot Wilson
  • Best Director: Sarah Watt - Look Both Ways
  • Best Editor: Denise Haratzis - Look Both Ways
  • Best Film: Look Both Ways
  • Best Production Design: The Proposition - Chris Kennedy
  • Best Score: The Proposition - Nick Cave and Warren Ellis
  • Best Screenplay - Adapted: Three Dollars - Robert Connolly and Elliot Perlman
  • Best Screenplay - Original: Look Both Ways - Sarah Watt
  • Best Sound: Little Fish
  • Best Supporting Actor: Anthony Hayes - Look Both Ways
  • Best Supporting Actress: Noni Hazlehurst - Little Fish
