BLT: BETTER LUCKTOMORROW

Tommy and Austin began playing guitar in yrs 5-6. Once in highschool, they joined a band with older, more experienced guys. Once they learnt more than 4 chords, they began playing with a drummer by the name of Mitch Stanger (heart shot), singer Jocie P and Bass guitarist Rick G. They played under the name of '20% Female', and played at a couple of school performances. In this time, Luke Neely had begun to play drums, and Charlie Hill began Guitar.

After about a year however, 20% Female fell apart, and Tommy and Austin began to jam with Luke, who was picking up drums very quickly. the guys jammed on a regular basis, but needed a bass guitarist. Charlie had approached us about joining the band, but as we already had two guitarists, we suggested he learnt bass. Charlie instantly got into bass and over the next year became the next flea. He picked it up quickly, and in just a year and a half, can play bass as fast as the guys play ing guitar.

Under the name Big Left Toe, or BLT, They began playing at many school related band nights, and won the school battle of the bands competition. BLT continue to play at parties, band gigs and competitions, and just recently took out the battle of the bands comp at Tara.

Anyone need a band for their party? because... WE WILL PLAY AT YOUR PARTIES!!!!!!!!!
