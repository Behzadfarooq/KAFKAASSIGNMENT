The Australian Reptile Park is located at Gosford on the Central Coast, New South Wales in Australia. It has a lot more than reptiles, e.g. roos, cassowaries, Tasmanian devils, koalas, dingos, spiders.

The park is heavily involved in snake and spider venom collection for use in the production of anti-venom.

The park is home to a replica diplodocus named Ploddy, often recognised as being the very first of Australia's Big Things.
