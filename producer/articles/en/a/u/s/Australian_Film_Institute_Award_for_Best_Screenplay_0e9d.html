Australian Film Institute Award for Best Screenplay

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Australian Film Institute Award for Best Screenplay is an award in the annual Australian Film Institute Awards. It has been awarded annually since 1976 as a joint category until 1993 when the award was separated into Best Original Screenplay and Best Adapted Screenplay.

[edit] Best Screenplay (Original or Adapted)

  • 1976: Fred Schepisi (The Devil's Playground)
  • 1977: David Williamson (Don's Party)
  • 1980: Jonathon Hardy, David Stevens, Bruce Beresford (Breaker Morant)
  • 1981: David Williamson (Gallipoli)
  • 1982: Bob Ellis, Denny Lawrence (Goodbye Paradise)
  • 1990: David Parker (The Big Steal)
  • 1991: Jocelyn Moorhouse (Proof)
  • 1992: Baz Luhrmann, Craig Pearce (Strictly Ballroom)
