Australia Fair Shopping Centre

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia



Located in Southport, Australia Fair Shopping Centre is the major shopping centre in the administrative centre of the Gold Coast. Australia Fair is the only undercover and fully air conditioned major shopping centre on the Gold Coast. It includes majors such as Coles, Kmart, Woolworths and The Warehouse and over 230 specialty stores. It has 2 food courts and one overlooks The Broadwater. There is also a 10 screen Birch Caroll and Coyle Senstadium Cinemas and the Fig Tree Courtyard, with a large Moreton Bay Fig in the middle of it.

The centre has majority undercover parking for over 2500 cars with three adjoining public car parks.

The bus interchange at Australia Fair is a major transport hub for Surfside Buslines' TransLink bus routes.
