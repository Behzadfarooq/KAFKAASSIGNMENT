Austrian Actuarial Society

The Austrian Actuarial Society (German: Aktuarvereinigung Österreichs, AVÖ) is the association of actuaries in Austria. The Society was established in 1971. It is a full member of the International Actuarial Association and the Groupe Consultatif. As of 2005, the Society has about 360 members.
