Australian Wikipedians

Board | List | Portal | Projects | To-Do | Tools

This category is for Wikipedia users who come from Australia or are descended from Australians, but who do not necessarily live in Australia currently. For Wikipedians currently living in Australia, see Category:Wikipedians in Australia. Also, feel free to participate in the Australian Wikipedians' notice board.

If you are an administrator, there is also an Australian Administrators Category that you may wish to add yourself to.

The current Australian Collaboration of the Fortnight is Cricket in Australia.
Every fortnight a different Australia-related topic, stub or non-existent article is picked.
Please read the nomination text and improve the article any way you can.
