Australia competed at the 1976 Winter Olympics in Innsbruck, Austria. Colin Coates' sixth place in 10000 metres speed skating was Australia's best result so far at the Winter Olympics.

[edit] Results by event

[edit] Alpine skiing

For more details on this topic, see Alpine skiing at the 1976 Winter Olympics.
Men
  • Downhill: David Griff (22nd/66), Kim Clifford (34th), Rob McIntyre (DNF - fell)
  • Giant slalom: David Griff (did not finish), Kim Clifford (disqualified)
  • Slalom: Kim Clifford (DNF - fell), David Griff (DNF - fell)
Women
  • Downhill: Sally Rodd, Joanne Henke (36th/38)
  • Giant slalom: Sally Rodd (33rd/43), Joanne Henke (40th)
  • Slalom: Joanne Henke (DNF - fell), Sally Rodd (disqualified)

[edit] Figure skating

For more details on this topic, see Figure skating at the 1976 Winter Olympics.
Men
  • William Schober (DNF - withdrew injured after first day of competition)
Women
  • Sharon Burley (20th/20)

[edit] Speed skating

For more details on this topic, see Speed skating at the 1976 Winter Olympics.
  • 500 m: Colin Coates (25th/28)
  • 1000 m: Colin Coates (11th/31)
  • 1500 m: Colin Coates (8th/30)
  • 5000 m: Colin Coates (10th/31)
