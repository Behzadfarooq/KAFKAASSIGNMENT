Aussie (shampoo)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

You have new messages (last change).

Aussie is a brand of shampoo and other hair care products such as hairspray, hair gel, hair mousse, and hair detangler. It is marketed as a product related to Australia, its products are often labeled with a logo of a kangaroo, and its slogan is "Put Some Roo in Your Do". The brand is manufactured by Procter & Gamble, an American business, which bought the brand circa 2003.
