The Australian Arid Lands Botanic Garden is located on the Stuart Highway, 1.4 kilometres north of the Eyre Highway in Port Augusta. The garden was established in the 1980's [1],[2] and opened in September 1996 [3]. The Garden is an initiative of the City of Port Augusta and corporate funding, and very limited State Government support [4].

The Garden has an interpretive centre and cafe. It features Australian and overseas plants from arid environments within its 250 hectares [5].
