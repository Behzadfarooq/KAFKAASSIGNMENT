Category:Australian vocational education and training providers

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Vocational education and training is provided by a range of post-secondary educational providers.

These include private companies, not-for-profit organisations, to government bodies, such as TAFE and the universities.

Generally these organisations will be Registered Training Organisations with the Australian Government Department of Education, Science and Training.
