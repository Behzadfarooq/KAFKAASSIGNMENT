20 to 1: Aussie Parties and Pastimes

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Aussie Parties and Pastimes was an episode of the Australian television series 20 to 1 that screened on February 13, 2007.

  5. Gay & Lesbian Mardi Gras
  6. Root tossing, tuna tossing, thong throwing
  7. City to Surf
  8. Beer Can Regatta / Henley on the Todd
  9. Carols by Candlelight
  10. Sydney to Hobart Yacht Race
  11. Hens' Nights
  12. Bucks' Parties
  13. Schoolies Week
  14. Birdman Rally
  15. Desperate and Dateless
  16. Australia Day
  17. Big Day Out
  18. Cracker Night
  19. Royal Shows
  20. B & S Balls
