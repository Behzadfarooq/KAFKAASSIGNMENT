[edit] Triple J artists

After completing the Triple J Hottest 100 lists, here are the artists on the lists that are red-linked. Some are just spelling or capitalisation issues. But many need articles written! I have bolded the ones that I think are the most important (POV, I know..)

Also note not all of these are Australian artists, but I am leaving them in the list anyway - mainly because some of the earlier ones I'm not sure about, and because they are on the Triple J Hottest 100 lists. -- Chuq 01:52, 19 Nov 2004 (UTC)
