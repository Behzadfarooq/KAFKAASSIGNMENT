Austria competed at the 1976 Summer Olympics in Montreal, Canada.

[edit] Medals

Austria finished in 37th position in the final medal rankings, with one bronze medal.

[edit] Bronze

  • Rudolf Dollinger — Shooting, Men's Free Pistol

[edit] Results by event

[edit] Archery

In its first appearance in archery competition at the Olympics, Austria entered one man.

Men's Individual Competition:

  • Oswald Probts — 2173 points (→ 33rd place)
