Paweł Dembowski, born on April 23, 1983, Poland. I became a Wikipedian in February 2004. I'm a sysop here, and also on the Polish Wikipedia, Polish Wikinews, and Wikimedia Commons,as well as a Wikimedia steward.

My main areas of contribution are science fiction, fantasy, Poland, and Polish science fiction :), but I've been also contributing to other articles. Lately, I've been updating and creating mostly Middle-earth articles - check out my List of Middle-earth articles. My nickname comes from Ausir, one of the nicknames of Dior, son of Beren and Luthien, but I out Google him (hmm.. I think I invented a new word here!)

[edit] Other sites I write for

  • RPG Codex - a computer role playing game news site
  • Circle of Eight - Modding community and news site about Troika Games
  • Duck and Cover - an English-language website about Fallout (computer game)
  • Sierra Army Depot - a Polish website about Fallout (computer game)
