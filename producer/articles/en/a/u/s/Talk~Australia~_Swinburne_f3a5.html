[edit] Hello

I gather this is part of the Future studies project you guys are working on. A few issues: the title needs to be an encyclopedic term for whatever the object of the article is. In this case, it seems to be the Strategic Foresight program (University of Swinbune) or Strategic Foresight graduate program at .... But these seem to be a bit too specific at this stage because we have no counterparts for other programs. I'm also not sure if people will consider one graduate program "notable" enough unless you make a better attempt to establish this in the article. I would recommend either inserting this as a part of the article about the University if one exists. Or you could create another article about the concept of Strategic Foresight or perhaps just link from the main Future studies article. You can change the title by clicking "move" based on whatever you decide. Best, Tfine80 06:24, 31 October 2005 (UTC)
