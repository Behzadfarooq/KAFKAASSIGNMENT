edit

The Austria women's national football team represents Austria in international women's football.

The team is made up mainly of players from the joint Austro-German Women's Bundesliga. Austria's attempt to qualify for the 2007 World Cup has seen it placed in Group E, with France, England, Netherlands and Hungary. The team only won one of its first four games - against Hungary, meaning it is placed second bottom of the group, unlikely to qualify.
