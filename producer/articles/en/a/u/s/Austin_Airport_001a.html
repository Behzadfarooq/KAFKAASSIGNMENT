Austin Airport (IATA: ASQ, FAA LID: 9U3) is a public airport located four miles (6 km) southwest of the central business district of Austin, in Lander County, Nevada, United States.

[edit] Facilities

Austin Airport covers an area of 1,205 acres (488 ha) and has one runway:

  • Runway 18/36: 6,000 x 75 ft. (1,829 x 23 m), Surface: Asphalt
