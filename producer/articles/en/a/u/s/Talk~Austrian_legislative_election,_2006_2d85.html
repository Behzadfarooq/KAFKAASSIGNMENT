[edit] Coalition possibilities

How is a parliamentary majority formed - through numbers of votes, or through numbers of seats? The article states that "the numbers permit no other two party coalition". Presumably this means that it is not possible for either the SPO or the OVP to form a coalition with any party except each other so as to form a majority? --Richardrj talk email 09:41, 10 October 2006 (UTC)

Numbers of seats, of course, which correlates directly to number of votes; and yes, it does mean exactly what it says -- that the only *TWO*-party coalition with the necessary amount of votes/seats is SPÖ/ÖVP. —Nightstallion (?) 15:39, 24 October 2006 (UTC)
