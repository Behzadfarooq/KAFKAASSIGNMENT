Australia national under-21 rugby union team

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Australian national under-21 rugby union team represents Australia on a national level at rugby union at an under-21 year old age. The team has competed at the Under 21 Rugby World Championship, and has featured in three championship finals, though they have never won the event, losing to South Africa in 2002, New Zealand in 2003 and South Africa again in 2005. They finished in fourth place at the 2006 Under 21 Rugby World Championship in France.

[edit] World championship final results

Year Champions Score Runners-up Venue
2002 South Africa 24-21 Australia Johannesburg, South Africa
2003 New Zealand 21-10 Australia Oxford, England
2005 South Africa 20-15 Australia Mendoza, Argentina
