Austin Mast

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Austin R. Mast is a research botanist within the Department of Biological Science at Florida State University.[1] One of his main areas of research is the phylogenetics of Grevilleoideae, a subfamily of Proteaceae. He has shown the genus Banksia to be paraphyletic with respect to Dryandra,[2] and subsequently transferred all Dryandra taxa to Banksia.[3]

The standard author abbreviation A.R.Mast may be used to indicate this person in citing a botanical name.
