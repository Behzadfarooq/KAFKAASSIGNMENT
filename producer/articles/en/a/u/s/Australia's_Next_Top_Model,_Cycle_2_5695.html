[edit] Episode Summary

[edit] Ep One

  • Original airdate: January 04, 2005

The search beings to find the contestants for this season's competition to find Australia's Next Top Model. 13 beautiful all new wannabe models from around the country arrive in Sydney for a shot at becoming Australia's Next Top Model - Game On!! Laura shaved of one of Harinthi's eye brows while she slept in this episode. Madeline ruffled some feather after she kissed Sophie and then tried to kiss Lara in the spa.

  • Eliminated : Sasha Greenoff, Rebecca Pian & Natalie Guiffre

[edit] Ep Two

  • Original airdate: January 11, 2005

It's transformation week. This week the girls get a new hair style and make up tips. As the girls get to know each other, there are also tempers beginning to flair.

  • Eliminated: Sophie Miller

[edit] Ep Three

  • Original airdate: January 18, 2005

Tempers heat up in the house as the competition continues.

  • Eliminated: Sarah Lawrence

[edit] Ep Four

  • Original airdate: January 25, 2005

Team Work is the focus of this weeks task, the girls are left in the middle of bush land. Will they be able to work together as a team and navigate their way to the campsite?

  • Eliminated: Hiranthi Warusevitane

[edit] Ep Five

  • Original airdate: February 01, 2005

This weeks focus in on movement and contol Adam takes the girls to Bondi Iceberg Pool, where they meet Olympic Gold Medalist Elka Graham.Caroline decides to leave this week, disappointed she was in the bottom two.

  • Quit: Caroline Mouflard
  • Eliminated: Lara Cameron

[edit] Ep Six

This eoisode Louise has been eliminated.

[edit] Ep Seven

  • Original airdate: February 15, 2005

Madeline Rose has been eliminated in this episode.

[edit] The Girl Who Becomes Australia's Next Top Model

  • Original airdate: Febaruary 22, 2005

Final 3 are Simone Viljoen, Jessica French and Eboni Stocks. Simone goes first. Jessica and Eboni competed in the final catwalk, then it was Eboni who won becoming Australia's Next Top Model.

[edit] Controversy

In a surprising twist, Sasha Greenoff was eliminated and Madeleine Rose took her place in the competition. Also, in week 5 Eboni left due to a medical condition but was allowed to return in week 6 depsite missing a photoshoot.

After the show, Madeleine, Jessica, and Simone continued modelling. After being dropped from Chic Management, Eboni signed with Elite Modeling Agency (Aus). Currently, she is working as a sales person in a shoe store.
