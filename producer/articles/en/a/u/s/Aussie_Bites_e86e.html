Aussie Bites

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Aussie Bites are a collection of children's novels, aimed at 7-10 years old, written by a variety of Australian authors in Australia. They are published by Puffin Books, a subsidy of Penguin Books.

Aussie Bites are notable for having a bite-mark cut-out on the top right corner of the book's covers. The books also have a colour illustrated front cover, but the illustrations featured inside are in black and white. Aussie Bites retail for about $11.95 AUD

Following the success of Aussie Bites, Puffin Books have also released several more series' called:

  • Aussie Nibbles (Junior readers)
  • Aussie Chomps (Senior readers)
  • Kiwi Bites (New Zealand's version of Aussie Bites)
