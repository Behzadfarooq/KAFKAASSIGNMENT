Australian Institute of Public Safety

Established 1990
Location Melbourne CBD, Victoria, Australia
Campus Flinders Street, Melbourne
Website www.aips.edu.au

The Australian Institute of Public Safety is an Australian owned independant tertiary education provider of courses accredited by the Victorian Department of Education and Training. The current student population numbers around 400. The most popular degree offered by the institution is a Bachelor of Social Science (Criminal Justice).
