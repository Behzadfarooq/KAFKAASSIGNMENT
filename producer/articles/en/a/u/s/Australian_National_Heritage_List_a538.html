Australian National Heritage List

The Australian National Heritage List is a list of places deemed to be of significant heritage value by the Australian Heritage Council. There are currently 29 places on the list.

  1. Budj Bim National Heritage Landscape - Tyrendarra Area
  2. Budj Bim National Heritage Landscape - Mt Eccles Lake Condah Area
  3. Royal Exhibition Building National Historic Place
  4. Dinosaur Stampede National Monument
  5. Kurnell Peninsula
  6. Eureka Stockade Gardens
  7. Castlemaine Diggings National Heritage Park
  8. Mawson Huts and Mawson Hut's Historic site
  9. Brewarrina Aboriginal Fish Traps (Baiames Ngunnhu)
  10. Port Arthur Historic site
  11. Glenrowan Heritage Precinct
  12. Sydney Opera House
  13. Fremantle Prison
  14. First Government House Site
  15. Newman College
  16. Sidney Myer Music Bowl
  17. ICI Building (former)/Orica House
  18. Australian Academy of Science building
  19. Recherche Bay (North East Peninsula) Area
  20. Richmond Bridge
  21. HMVS Cerberus
  22. Melbourne Cricket Ground
  23. South Australia's Parliament House
  24. Tree of Knowledge
  25. Dirk Hartog Landing Site 1616 - Cape Inscription Area
  26. Batavia Shipwreck Site and Survivor Camps Area
  27. Hermannsburg Historic Precinct
  28. Australian War Memorial and Anzac Parade
  29. North Head, Sydney
