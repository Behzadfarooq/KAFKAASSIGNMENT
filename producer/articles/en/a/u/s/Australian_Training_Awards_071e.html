Australian Training Awards

The Australian Training Awards are annual awards given by the Australian government for excellence in a number of occupational training and education areas, to both individuals and organisations.

Each Australian state and territory also provides awards in these areas; it is these winners, the finalists, who compete for the national Australian Training Awards.

The announcement of the 2006 Australian Training Awards is planned for Thursday, 16 November 2006, at a function at the Adelaide Convention Centre, Adelaide.

[edit] Award Categories

The 2006 Awards include the following categories:

[edit] Organisations

  • Australian Training Initiative Award for leading-edge programs or products developed and implemented to provide quality vocational education and training;
  • Employer of the Year Award for medium to large enterprise employee training programs;
  • Prime Minister's Small Business of the Year for small enterprise employee training programs;
  • Large Training Provider of the Year Award for large registered training organisations;
  • Small Training Provider of the Year Award for small registered training organisations;
  • VET in Schools Excellence Award for school student-based vocational education and training.

Additionally, there are a number of industry awards aligned to Industry Skills Councils.

[edit] Individuals

  • New Apprenticeships Apprentice of the Year
  • New Apprenticeships Trainee of the Year
  • Vocational Student of the Year
  • Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander Student of the Year
