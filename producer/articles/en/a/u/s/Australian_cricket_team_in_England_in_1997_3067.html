Australia won the 1997 Ashes series against England. Australia won three test matches, England won two test matches and the other test match was drawn:

  • 1st Test (Birmingham) — England won by 9 wickets - [1]
  • 2nd Test (Lord's) — drawn - [2]
  • 3rd Test (Manchester) — Australia won by 268 runs - [3]
  • 4th Test (Leeds) — Australia won by an innings and 61 runs - [4]
  • 5th Test (Nottingham) — Australia won by 264 runs - [5]
  • 6th Test (The Oval) — England won by 19 runs - [6]
