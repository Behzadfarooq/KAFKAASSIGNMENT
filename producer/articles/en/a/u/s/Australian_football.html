Australian football

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Australian football may refer to:

  • Australian rules football, a code of football played with a prolate spheroid ball, on large oval shaped fields, with four posts at each end
  • Australian Football League, the de facto governing body and national league of Australian rules football
  • Football Federation Australia, the governing body for football (soccer) in Australia
