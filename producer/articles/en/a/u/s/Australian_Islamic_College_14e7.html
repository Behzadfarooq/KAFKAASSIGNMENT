Australian Islamic College is an educational institution in Perth, Western Australia. Its founder is Abdallah Magar. The school was founded in 1986 with only 50 students. Twenty years on, in 2006 the school has three campuses and over 2300 students.

The 3 campuses are located at;

  1. Dianella K-10
  2. Kewdale K-12
  3. Thornlie K-10

[edit] See also

  • Islam in Australia

visit www.aic.wa.edu.au for more information about AIC.

The goal of the school is Success in this life and the Hereafter - a beautiful balance.

The school follows the WA Curriculum, has a excellent TEE program and offers Arabic and Islamic lessons to the students.

Many of the past students have gone on to study Medicine, Dentistry, Pharmacy and more.
