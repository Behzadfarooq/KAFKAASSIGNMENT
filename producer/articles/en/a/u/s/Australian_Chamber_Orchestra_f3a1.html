Australian Chamber Orchestra

The Australian Chamber Orchestra is an orchestra based in Sydney, which tours its programs to most major Australian cities. One of its founders in 1975 was former Sydney Symphony Orchestra principal cellist John Painter. Its Artistic Director since 1989 has been the charismatic Australian violinist Richard Tognetti. Much of its funding comes from corporate support and the Australia Council. In Sydney it performs in the City Recital Hall and the Sydney Opera House.
