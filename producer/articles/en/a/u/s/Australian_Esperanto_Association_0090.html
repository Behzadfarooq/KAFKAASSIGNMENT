[edit] Summer School

The Australian Esperanto Association hosts a summer school of the international language Esperanto for one or two weeks each year. Classes are arranged for beginners, intermediate, and fluent speakers of the language. The summerschool usually occurs in January and everybody is cordially invited to participate. Details are available at the website of the association which also describes its other projects.

[edit] Journal

The official journal of the Australian Esperanto Association is 'Esperanto Sub La Suda Kruco', edited by Donald BROADRIBB, honorary member of the Universal Esperanto Association.

[edit] Radio

Esperanto is broadcast for one hour every Monday at 1pm by the Melbourne Ethnic Community Radio 3ZZZ.

[edit] Book Service

The bookshop of the AEA is based at the Esperanto Centre of Sydney, 143 Lawson St. Redfern. Telephone +61 2 9319 4775 for more information.

[edit] Sub-organisations

Statewide organisations exist in New South Wales, Victoria, Queensland, South Australia, Western Australia, and in Tasmania.

Local Esperanto groups,clubs and societies exist in Brisbane, Sydney, Melbourne, Adelaide, Hobart, Toowoomba, Gold Coast, Warwick, Manly, Newcastle, and Oakleigh.
