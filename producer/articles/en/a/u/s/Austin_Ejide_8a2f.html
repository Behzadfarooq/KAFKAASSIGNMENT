Austin Ejide (born 8 April 1984 in Onitsha, Nigeria) is a Nigerian football goalkeeper.

He played for a few clubs, including Gabros International and SC Bastia in Corsica, which is competing in the French Football League (LFP).

He plays for Nigeria national football team and was a participant at the 2002 FIFA World Cup.
