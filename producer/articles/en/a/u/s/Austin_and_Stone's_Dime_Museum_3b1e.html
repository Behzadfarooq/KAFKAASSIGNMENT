Austin and Stoneâ€™s Dime Museum was an entertainment emporium located in Boston, Massachusetts, USA's famous Scollay Square. It featured a freak show as well as dancing girls for entertainment. The freak show and other exhibits such as two-headed animals cost ten cents, while admission to the girlie show cost an additional dime.
