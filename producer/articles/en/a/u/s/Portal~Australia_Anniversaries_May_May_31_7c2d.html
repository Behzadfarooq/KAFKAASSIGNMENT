  • 1813 - Gregory Blaxland, William Lawson, and William Charles Wentworth arrive at Mount Blaxland and having successfully crossed the Blue Mountains for the first time head back to Sydney.
  • 1886 - The SS Ly-Ee-Moon sinks off Green Cape, New South Wales, with the loss of 71 persons.
  • 1942 - During the Attack on Sydney Harbour, a Japanese midget submarine sinks the converted Sydney ferry, HMAS Kuttabul, killing 21.
