Category:Auto parts

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

This category contains articles relating to essential and/or typical automotive parts and includes some articles that concern parts that are not specifically or only found on automobiles but also on other vehicles. Multi-part technologies are addressed in the parent Category:Automotive technologies. Non-essential, optional parts are the domain of Category:Automotive accessories, while retailers and suppliers of essential and non-essential parts are found in Category:Auto parts suppliers and Category:Automotive part retailers, respectively.
