Autopsy is an upcoming film, set to be released in the year 2008. It is about a group of friends that go to a punk concert. One of them is hit over the head with a bottle. Before his girlfriend can even call for help, a 1950's ambulance arrives. They are taken to a strange hospital where the injured friend is. They must find their friend.

As writer Adam Gierasch puts it:

"It’s a punk-rock horror film," Gierasch tells Fango. "It’s about a group of kids who are at a punk show, and one of them gets pretty badly injured; he gets a bottle cracked over his head. His girlfriend is about to call for medical help, but before she can, this ancient 1950s ambulance emerges from out of the fog, and the kid is picked up by these two strange orderlies. The rest of the movie is his girlfriend and group of friends trying to find their injured pal—this leads them to a spooky hospital, and wacky hijinx ensue."


Kelli Garner has been cast as the lead role in the film.

The movie is currently in pre-production.
