AutoTrader.com

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

For the UK based publication and website, see Auto Trader.

AutoTrader.com is a majority-owned subsidiary of Cox Enterprises. Other investors include ADP, Inc. and the venture capital firm Kleiner Perkins Caufield & Byers. AutoTrader.com is an Internet-based auto classifieds marketplace and consumer information website. It was founded by Manheim Auctions and ADP Dealer Services under the name AutoConnect.com in 1997. In July 1999, AutoConnect and Auto Trader Online merged websites to form AutoTrader.com. The CEO and President is the company's first employee, Chip Perry. The company is privately held and headquartered in Atlanta, Georgia.

The site's headquarters are in Atlanta, Georgia. Regional offices are located in Ft. Lauderdale, Florida; Norfolk, Virginia; Chicago, Illinois; Dallas, Texas; Los Angeles, California; Boston, Massachusetts; and Seattle, Washington.
