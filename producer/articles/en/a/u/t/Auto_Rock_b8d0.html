"Auto Rock" is a song by Glaswegian Post-rock band Mogwai on their album Mr. Beast.

[edit] Musical structure

It features a piano riff played by Barry Burns, and march-like drums. It was, at one point, used as a set opener by Mogwai on their 2006 tour.

[edit] Trivia

  • It is featured on the soundtrack of Miami Vice and has been used at least twice in BBC motoring magazine show, Top Gear.
