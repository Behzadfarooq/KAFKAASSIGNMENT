Autumn in New York is a 2000 romance/drama movie directed by Joan Chen and starring Richard Gere, Winona Ryder, and Anthony LaPaglia.

The movie focuses on Will Keane (Grere), a 50-year old restaraunt owner who falls in love with Charlotte Fielding (Ryder), a sweet, but terminally ill young woman.
