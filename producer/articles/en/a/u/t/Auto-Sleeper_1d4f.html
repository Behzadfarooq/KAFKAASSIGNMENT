Auto-Sleeper

Auto-sleeper are manufacturers of motorhomes, most of which have either a Peugeot Boxer or Ford Transit van chassis. Auto-sleeper were the manufacturers of the very popular and well built Rambler, and have been converting vans since the 1960s. They are well known for their successful monocoque van chassis, which is a one-piece unit unlike the back and front being built separately then joined together like with most coachbuilts, which are also far more prone to leaking.
