Autovía C-14

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The C-14 and at its northernmost numbered the N-145 is a highway in Catalonia, Spain from Andorra to Lleida.

It passes through Balaguer along the valley of the Rio Segre to La Seu d'Urgell.
