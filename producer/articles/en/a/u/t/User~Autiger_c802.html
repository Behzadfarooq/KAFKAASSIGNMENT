AUtiger (born January 26) is an e-business technology consultant living in Midtown, Atlanta, Georgia and working primarily with IBM software technologies such as Lotus Domino. He is a 1992 graduate of Auburn University's Management Information Systems program. He originally began in Auburn's nationally recognized Architecture program and still retains significant interests in architecture, development and city planning.

In his spare time, he is an avid volleyball player and college football fan having attended games in 24 different stadiums. He also attended the 1996 Summer Olympics in Atlanta and the 2000 Summer Olympics in Sydney. He has significant interests in music, both listening and performing, with significant experience as a trumpet player, choral singer, and a DJ and host of the jazz show on WEGL-FM while a student at AU. His hobbies also include photography - now shooting with a Nikon D80 digital SLR - and creating original images for contribution to Wikipedia.
