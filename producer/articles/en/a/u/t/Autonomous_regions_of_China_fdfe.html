A autonomous region (S.Chinese:自治区 or T.Chinese:自治區,Pinyin:zīzhì qǜ) is the first-level administrative subdivision of China, it has their own local government as the other Chinese Provinces, but a autonomous region has more rights on legislation. It is a minority entity and has more populous of minority, There are five autonomous regions in China, Tibetan in Tibet ranks the first of the population in the region andZhuang in Guangxi, Uyghur in Xinjiang, Mongols in Inner Mongolia, Hui in Ningxia rank the second of population in the other four ones.
