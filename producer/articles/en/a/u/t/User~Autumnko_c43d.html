Hello. I am Autumn Oakley. I am 24 years old and I live in Seattle, WA. I grew up in Colville, WA in rural Stevens County, WA. I have a BA in comparative religion from the University of Washington. I am married with no children, straddling somewhere between graduation and graduate school. I don't know what I want to study yet, but I do know that I love to write. I have been a long time end user of Wikipedia, and I think I'm ready to take the next step in our relationship, and become a contributor. I *just* started, so please don't judge me based on my article(s) thus far. I am starting with light topics to get the feel for the Wiki editing system. I am very level headed and rational, but I can become offended just like everyone else. Please keep criticism contructive (ie, I think your sentance structure is a little awkward in X paragraph) rather than destructive (ie, I think you suck and I think your mamma is fat and I wish you would die because you can't write.).

I am interested in a variety of subjects, and, as my major in college was inter-dicsiplinary by its very nature, I have background in a vast array of subjects. I love history, foreign language, grammar, anthropology, sociology, theology (though I personally am not religious), earth sciences, TV and many many many more things.

I thought it might be interesting to do a sampler type list (and so I can play with format and mark up), so here's a list of my articles and a few of my favourite things.
