The Autopista AP-36 is a highway in Spain south of Madrid.

It connects the N-400, Autovía A-4 and Autovía R-4 at Ocaña to the Autovía A-31 at La Roda. It follows the route of the N-301. There are junctions with the N-420 and N-310.The AP-36 was opened in July, 2006
