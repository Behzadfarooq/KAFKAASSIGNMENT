Autos Lobelle de Santiago Fútbol Sala is a futsal club based in Santiago de Compostela, city of the province of A Coruña in the autonomous community of Galicia.

The club was founded in 1987 and her stadium is Estadio Multiusos Fontes Do Sar with capacity of 5,500 seaters.

The club has the sponsorship of Autos Lobelle.
