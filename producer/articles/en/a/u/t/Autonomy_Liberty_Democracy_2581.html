Autonomy Liberty Democracy (Autonomie Liberté Démocratie, ALD) is an electoral list in Italy, active in Aosta Valley.

The list is affiliated to the The Union, the centre-left coalition led by Romano Prodi, and comprises the Democrats of the Left, Democracy is Freedom – Daisy, Lively Aosta Valley, Valdotanian Renewal, the Communist Refoundation Party, the Federation of the Greens and orther minor parties.

In the 2006 general election, ALD elected a deputy, Roberto Rolando Nicco (Democrats of the Left), and a senator, Carlo Perrin (Valdotanian Renewal), defeating respectively 43.4% to 30.7% Marco Viérin (SA, APF)[1] and 44.2% to 32.0% incumbent senator Augusto Rollandin (UV, APF)[2].
