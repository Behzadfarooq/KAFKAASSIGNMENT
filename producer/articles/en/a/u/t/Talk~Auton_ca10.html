Is the Nestene Conciousness one life form - as in the same one each Auton episde - or is it it's own race and each one is different?

Hard to say. It's supposed to be gestalt intelligence - as in, all the parts combine to make up a whole. So in a sense it's just one life form, but in another sense, it's made up of individual parts. Whether the Nestene Consciousness the Doctor meets in Rose is the same one that is sent back to the stars in Terror of the Autons or the same one that is neutralized in Spearhead from Space is not clear.
This is just my own take on it, but I like to think of it like a hive intelligence, and those bits mentioned above simply parts of the same whole - individualistic, but even if you destroy one bit, but as long as there are other bits out there, the Consciousness remains. This is why the Doctor refers to the Nestenes in the plural, even though there seems to be a central intelligence. --khaosworks 19:08, May 25, 2005 (UTC)
