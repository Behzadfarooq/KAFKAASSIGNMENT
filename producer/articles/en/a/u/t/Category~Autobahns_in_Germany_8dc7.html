Autobahns in Germany. One-digit roads are main lines, two-digit roads are geographically distributed by region.

  • 1 East and Berlin
  • 2 North and Northwest, Hamburg, Bremen
  • 3 Center, Hanover
  • 4 West, Dortmund, Düsseldorf, Kassel
  • 5 West and Southwest, Cologne
  • 6 Southwest, Frankfurt, Stuttgart
  • 7 Southeast, Leipzig
  • 8 South
  • 9 South, Munich

Three-digit roads are trunk lines to the corresponding two-digit road.
