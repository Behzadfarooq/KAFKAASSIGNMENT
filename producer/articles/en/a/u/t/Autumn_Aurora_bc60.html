Autumn Aurora is the second album by the Ukrainian black metal band Drudkh, released in 2005. It is the first Drudkh album to incorporate synthesizers.

Some releases of Autumn Aurora have "Summoning the Rain" and "Glare of Autumn" combined as a single track.
