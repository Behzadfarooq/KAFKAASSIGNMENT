Autumn is a russian gothic-doom band formed in 1993.

[edit] Members

  • Yuri "Rottor"- guitars, Vocals
  • Ludmila Emeliashina - Viola
  • Sergey Berdyshev - Drums
  • Felix Vigorov - Bass
  • Roman Il'in - Guitars
  • Svetlana Polezhaeva "Slaviana" - Keyboards, females vocals

[edit] Discography

  • The Druid Autumn - Demo (1995)
  • And We Are Falling Leaves... (1997)
  • Chernye Krylia (2000)
  • Osen Vechna (2003)
