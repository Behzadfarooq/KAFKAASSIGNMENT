Autrey Mill Middle School is a public middle school in Alpharetta, Fulton County, Georgia. The school serves more than 1,200 students. This school is part of the Chattahoochee Cluster. Autrey Mill is a split-feed school, with students feeding into either Northview or Chattahoochee High School depending on where the student lives.

Autrey Mill opened its doors in 2003, under the direction of principal Dr. Ann Ferrell. According to the January 2007 issue of Atlanta Magazine, Autrey Mill was named Best Middle School. In June of 2006, the Atlanta Journal-Constitution named Autrey Mill one of the Top 10 Public Middle Schools in Georgia.
