[edit] Needs a fix

This template in its current form has a problem. It says "add to this page..." and then gives a link to {{Replaceable fair use disputed}}, which has a facility for offering your objections or fair use rationale. While I've edited the markup of {{Replaceable fair use disputed}} to try and make it really obvious not to put your rationale in the template itself, this particular template is practically inviting people to do it, and I just had a new user do just that. Accordingly I would like to replace it with the version currently located at User:Dgies/sandbox. —Dgiest c 16:28, 5 January 2007 (UTC)

Addendum: This change should be made to all 4 templates in Category:Replaceable_fair_use_images
  1. Template:Replaceable fair use
  2. Template:AutoReplaceable fair use
  3. Template:AutoReplaceable fair use people
  4. Template:AutoReplaceable fair use buildings
—Dgiest c 16:30, 5 January 2007 (UTC)
Done. If you have any questions, please contact me at my talk page. Ian Manka 01:59, 6 January 2007 (UTC)

[edit] Questions link

As this tag is placed after it has been selected from the licence selector, can a link to the Wikipedia:Media copyright questions page be added so that users have a relevant point of contact? After using {{rfu}}, the uploader should get a notice on their talk page which links to the questions page, or they can ask the user who left them the message, but with this tag the uploader doesn't get the notice so they don't get the links. Suggested format:

'''Questions''' about this message can be asked at [[Wikipedia:Media copyright questions]].

Please also update Template:AutoReplaceable fair use buildings if this is updated. Thanks, mattbr30 16:29, 24 February 2007 (UTC)
