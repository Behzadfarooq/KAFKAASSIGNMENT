As one who has driven on many autostrada around Roma and Napoli in the last couple of years, I can say that the newer superstrada routes all seem to have it, but many of the autostrada have either a emergency lane/hard shoulder that is frequently broken by obstacles such as bridge piers. Many have no emergency lanes.

[edit] Superstrada

When I've been in Italy, I've come across so-called "superstrada". I don't know if that is the official name, but that is what my Italian colleagues referred to them as. The signage indicated a speed limit of 150kph (about 94mph). As I was in a taxi that happily overtook several policecars whilst travelling at 160kph (just under 100mph), and wasn't stopped, I presume that this wasn't me misreading signs.
