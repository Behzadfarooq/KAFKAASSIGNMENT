The following projects/tasks have been completed using the AutoWikiBrowser, "complete" meaning that the correction has been made across the entire encyclopedia:

Please add more projects to this list.

[edit] Largely completed

  • Bulleting all external links - ~20,000 fixed
  • Standardising section (such as "External links") headers - in progress
  • Adding bold format to the first occurrence of the title in every article - ~15,000 fixed
  • Correcting typos - in progress
  • Removing duplicate word mistakes
  • Adding/removing and changing categories per WP:CFD.
  • Repairing Bad links (at least 9,000).
  • Migration of all aircraft articles to specs template per WP:Air - in progress
  • Correcting 15 most common capitalisation mistakes in headings - Complete
  • Correcting the next 228 most common capitalisation mistakes in headings - Complete
  • Find + fixing untagged/broken disambig pages - ~600 fixed
  • Putting U.S. 2000 census data into past tense - 33,000 fixed
  • Miscellaneous fixes for U.S. 2000 census data articles - 33,000 fixed
  • Removing unnecessary links to days of week or to months (Thousands fixed) - in progress
  • Replacing "passed away" with "died" (Thousands fixed).
  • Updating cleanup to cleanup-date on recently flagged articles for cleanup - >2500 completed - ongoing/daily
  • Substitution of User Talk Templates - ongoing.
  • Replacement of Image:Vatican coa.png with Image:Emblem of the Papacy.svg - 208 instances fixed
  • Reformatting ISBN numbers which didn't allow wikimagic to work .
  • Tagging living person biographies' talk pages with {{WPBiography}} (including {{Blp}}) - ~115,000 done
  • Move all cleanup template messages from talk pages to articles

Some of these projects have used the DataBaseSearchTool to identify the corrections needed.
