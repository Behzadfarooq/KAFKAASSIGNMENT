Automated Species is the 3rd album of Violent Work of Art, it will be released on January 26th on Swedmetal Records. This album is the first record with Tobias Eng since 1996, he sings on "Silence Is The Enemy" a song he also wrote himself.

  1. As the Sky Comes Falling Down - 4:21
  2. A Sad Creation - 4:35
  3. Deranged - 5:26
  4. Stains - 4:11
  5. Silence Is the Enemy - 5:39
  6. Scars - 4:06
  7. Requiem (For Myself) - 4:13
  8. Misery Loves You - 4:48
  9. Lord of Flies - 4:21
  10. Scarecrow 2006 - 4:30

[edit] Album line-up

  • Magnus Karlsson - Vocals
  • Tobias Eng - Rhythm Guitars, Vocals
  • Anders Gyllensten - Lead Guitars
  • Viktor Eng - Bass
  • Maria Simonsson - Synths
