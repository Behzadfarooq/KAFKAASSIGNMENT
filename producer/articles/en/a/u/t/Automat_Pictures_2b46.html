Automat Pictures is an entertainment production company based in Los Angeles, which specializes in EPK/DVD Added Value, as well original television programming and independant feature documentary production. Automat has contributed content for many DVDs, among them A Nightmare on Elm Street, Blue Velvet, Elf (film), Final Destination, Goodfellas, Philadelphia (film), Psycho (1998 film), Resident Evil (film), Rent (film), Snakes on a Plane, and many others.

Automat is also in production on indepedant feature documentaries about B-Movie mogul William Castle and 70's adult film star Jack Wrangler.

In addition, Automat is developing several narrative films, one of which is a biopic about George Kuchar.

Automat was founded by Jeffrey Schwarz in 2000.
