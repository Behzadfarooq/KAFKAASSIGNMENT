Autopsy Torment

The Swedish Oldschool Death Metal band Autopsy Torment was formed in 1989. The band started as a solo band of Thomas Karlsson, who's Autopsy Torment's frontman and singer, with Onkel on guitars. Later on (1991) the band turned into a trio with Karl Vincent (Sexual Goatlicker) on drums and Daniel Nilssen on guitars with the demo Darkest Rituals. After this demo a period of constant change of members took place with Thomas Karlsson and Karl Vincent as the core members.

In 1992 the band split up and Thomas Karlsson started black metal band Pagan Rites and later became the vocalist for the doom band Tristitia. In 1999 the 1991 line-up got back together with the addition of The Demon on bass and they wrote the album Orgy With The Dead which was released on Miriquidi Productions, and started writing the next album, Tormentorium. The band's lyrics are focused on murder, death, and gore. Painkiller Records was the last label the band signed at before they took a break. Thomas Karlsson is still actively working with his own project Devil Lee Rot as well as working on new material for Pagan Rites while The Demon & Daniel is working on their doom project Tormentorium and gearing up for new Autopsy Torment songs.

  • The Demon- Bass (Tormentorium, ex-Devil Lee Rot)

  • Daniel Nilssen - Guitars (Tormentorium, Scyphian Dreams, ex-Lords of the Grief)
