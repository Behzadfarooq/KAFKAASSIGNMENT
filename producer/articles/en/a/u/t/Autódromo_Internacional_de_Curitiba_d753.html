Autódromo Internacional de Curitiba (also known as Autódromo de Pinhais and Circuit Raul Boesel) is a motorsports circuit located in Curitiba, Brazil. It has been host to the World Touring Car Championship, TC2000, Fórmula Truck and Stock Car Brasil racing.

[edit] A1 Grand Prix series

The circuit was to play host to the A1 Grand Prix in February 2006, however, it has since been removed from the schedule.
