Article 116 of the Italian Constitution

contemplates that five of the 20 regions of Italy shall benefit of particular conditions of autonomy. These regions are:

  • Sicily
  • Sardinia
  • Trentino-South Tyrol
  • Friuli-Venezia Giulia
  • Aosta Valley
