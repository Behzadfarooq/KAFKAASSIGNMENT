Autodelta (UK)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Autodelta is an after-market tuning company for Alfa Romeo cars, founded almost 20 years ago. Headquartered in London, England. Company produces tuning parts and also full bodykits for various Alfa Romeo cars. It also produces full tuned cars including: reworked suspension, tuned engines with turbos, superchargers and so on. Autodelta's newest model is Rotrex supercharged Autodelta Brera J5 3.2 C (348 PS, 430 Nm). Company is often mixed up with Alfa Romeo`s own racing department Autodelta established in 1963.
