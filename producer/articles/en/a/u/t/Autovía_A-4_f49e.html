The Autovía A-4 or Autopista AP-4 (also called Autovia del Sur) is a Spanish autovía and autopista route which starts in Madrid and ends in Cádiz.

The Autopista AP-4; with toll, (from Sevilla to Cádiz) has a total length of 123.80 Km.
