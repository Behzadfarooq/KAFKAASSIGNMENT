Automatic Man were a progressive rock quartet which also featured funk and Santana-inspired fusion playing. Formed in late 1975 by guitarist Pat Thrall, drummer Michael Shrieve, keyboardist Todd Cochrane AKA Bayete, and bass player Doni Harvey. Despite a promising 1976 eponymous debut album Automatic Man, the line-up of Automatic Man was not cohesive and after a second album Visitor, the band split in 1978, with Pat Thrall joining Pat Travers band.

[edit] Personnel

  • Pat Thrall - Guitar, vocals
  • Todd Cochrane - Keyboards, vocals
  • Doni Harvey - Bass guitar
  • Michael Shrieve - Drums

Produced by Lou Casabianca and Automatic Man Mixed by Keith Harwood and Chris Kimsey
