Auto Ever (hangul:오토에버시스템즈) is an automobile internet company. Its headquarters is in Seoul, Korea. It was established in 2000. It is owned by the Hyundai Motor Company and Kia Motors to sell used car on portal sites. It is part of Hyundai Motor Group.
