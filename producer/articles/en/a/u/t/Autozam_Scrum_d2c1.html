Autozam Scrum

The Scrum is a tiny minivan and pickup truck sold in Japan by Mazda. First part of the company's Autozam marque in 1990, Mazda continued selling the Scrum until 2003.

[edit] 2000

A consumer version called the Scrum Wagon was added for 2000, while the truck and van were updated.
