Autodromo Riccardo Paletti is a motorsport race track near Varano in the Province of Parma, Italy. The track began as a small 550m oval in 1969, and this was then expanded to a full 1.800 km (1.119 miles), 11 turn race track. This new layout was inaugurated officially on 26 March 1972.

In 2001 the layout was extended to a length of 2.375 km (1.475 miles).

The track is named after F1 driver Riccardo Paletti, who was killed at the 1982 Canadian Grand Prix.
