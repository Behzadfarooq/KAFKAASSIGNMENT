The Autovía A-7 or Autopista AP-7 (also called Autovia del Mediterráneo) is a Spanish autovía and autopista (limited-access highway) which starts in La Jonquera, near the French frontier and ends in Algeciras. The autopista begins in La Jonquera and ends in Cartagena.
