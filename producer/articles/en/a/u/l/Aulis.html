Aulis may be:

  • in Greek mythology, Aulis was both a daughter of King Ogyges and Thebe, and a port in Boeotia where the Greek navy rallied before setting off against Troy
  • in biology, Aulis is a genus of ladybird beetle
  • a UK website [1] that challenges accepted orthodoxy and established views on space exploration, including the nature of human space travel in the future. Without denying that astronauts have landed on the Moon Aulis questions Project Apollo, examining apparent anomalies in the photographic record. The website and its publications also examine the genesis of mankind including possible links between Cydonia on Mars and Avebury/Stonehenge in England. It provides an outlet for articles, books and DVDs.
