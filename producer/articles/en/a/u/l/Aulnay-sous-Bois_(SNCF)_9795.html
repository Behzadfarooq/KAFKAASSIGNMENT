Aulnay-sous-Bois (SNCF) is the major railway station of the commune Aulnay-sous-Bois (département Seine-Saint-Denis).

Railway station of Aulnay-sous-Bois (RER)

Lines serving this station

  • RER B
  • Tram Aulnay-sous-Bois - Bondy
  • SNCF Gare du Nord (Banlieue) - Crépy-en-Valois
