There must be fantasies at work here in addition to these genealogies, since I have never expressed any thoughts about Martial's friend Pudens one way or the other. I have not edited the brief stub here, nor have I complained about the similarly childish genealogical nonsense at the entry Caractacus. The epigram of Martial was sparked by the marriage of his friend, whom he calls "Pudens" to a Claudia [1]; the connections among these various names are imaginary. An elaborate genealogical fantasy is not contempt, merely a description of facile connections of various Pudens, with no basis in inscriptions or texts. Apparently then, to judge from the above, there is no serious basis for drawing these connections, other than modern so-called "legends". --Wetman 06:16, 6 November 2005 (UTC)
