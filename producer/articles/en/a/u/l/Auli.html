Auli

Auli is a recently developed winter Ski Resort in Chamoli district of Uttaranchal, India. Even in the summer months the place is a big attraction for it's panoramic views, and it's green meadows.

[edit] How to Reach

Auli, is a 20 minutes Ropeway ride from Joshimath, which is on the way to the famous Badrinath temple. Joshimath is a 16 hours drive from Delhi. One can also reach Roorkee, Rishikesh or Hardwar by train, and then catch a bus/cab afterwards.

Auli is a very serene hill station. It offers very good treks to places around and is a good ski resort from November - March depending on snowfall. The ropeway from Joshimath to Auli is one of the longest in India covering a distance of 4.15 kms.
