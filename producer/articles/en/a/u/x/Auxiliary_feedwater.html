Auxiliary feedwater

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

You have new messages (last change).

Auxiliary feedwater is a backup water supply system used by nuclear power plants (PWR) that use pressurized water reactors during shutdowns, including accident conditions, and sometimes during startup. This system works by pumping water to the steam generators to remove decay heat from the reactor.
