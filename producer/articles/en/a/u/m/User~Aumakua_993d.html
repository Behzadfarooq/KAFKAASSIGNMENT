[edit] Basic information relevant to Wikipedia editing:

  • Attended California Institute of Technology, 1974-1975.
  • Attended Iowa State University 1975-1976 and 1979-1981.
  • Worked at a nursery for over ten years.
  • Co-authored dnd (computer game) from several locales, 1976-1985.
  • Professional computer software engineer, 1981-1992, writing code in TUTOR, C, Pascal, Assembly and other languages.
  • Enthusiast and player of computer strategy games and games of logic
  • An accomplished photographer, both with 35 mm film and now using digital photography and Adobe Photoshop.
  • An accomplished professional face painter since 1995.
  • Have visited all 48 contiguous states in the U.S., every province in Canada from British Columbia to Nova Scotia (and only the ones directly between), plus Mexico, Guatemala, more than half the departments of France, and 10 other countries in Europe.
