1 Memorable Thoughts

The stars are falling from the sky, Yet no one notices the tears i cry, No one from the many lands, See the pieces of my shattered soal cupped within my dieing hands, A pain that fills my waking day, That echos throw my dreaming way, The darkness grows in my soul, Till a guiding light can pierce its hold.

The sun...the moon...the endless stretch of time...spun in to a web of eternety...all is endless...the neverending cycle of time...a hidden truth in truth...words without voices...all is dark...time has no meaning...life and death...an echo of time...to live is to die...to die is to live...to be born in this world is to die in this world...the endless pattern...to be is to be...nothing is always...always is nothing...an echo...nothing more.

An endless path...surrounded by many. The sun sets many times and the surrounded are fewer. The path is hard yet nothing is around to catch your falls. The path ahead begins to fade until only a memory remains. You sink away and nothing is clear. The road is unclear. The sand beneath your feet is hard and cold. Endless sand. The black water plays at your toes no matter how hard you run. The wind fights you and your body crys. The water rises and your running slows. A sobbing cry dissappears under the rising water. All warmth is gone. You reach for the light, but none is there. You search endlessly on for a life line to come and save you, but none are visible. The darkness increases. You still look but for a lost cause. A few golden lines descend, but you cannot reach. The waters close in and your soal is complete darkness. You know longer look for help from above and push aside what is given. You drown in your sorrow and anger. A life line descends, but you brush it aside. But it does not leave. You push it away, but it remains. It wraps around you and pulls you up from the endless darkness. No matter how you struggle it never lets go. The light above the water splashs in rainbows around you. A glorious angel of hope is by your side guiding you. Hope, truth, and joy are yours. Sorrow has vanished. The path is sandy, but sweet and soft. The road ahead is long, but clear. You have wings of hope to bear you into the sky. No matter what happens next you will never lose sight of the path. The dark waters are mearly a memory and your true dreams are yours. When all seems lost spread your wings and fly into the sky.


To have wings...my greatest wish in the world. Please someone hear my wish. Grant me wings to fly among the birds and sky.
