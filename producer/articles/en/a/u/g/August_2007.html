August 2007

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

August 2007 is the eighth month of that year and has yet to occur. It will begin on a Wednesday and will end after 31 days on a Friday.


[edit] Scheduled events

This article or section contains information about scheduled or expected future events.
It may contain information of a tentative nature and the content may change dramatically as the event approaches and more information becomes available.
  • August 3-August 5 - The Wikimania conference will be held at Taipei, Taiwan, Republic of China.
  • August 4-August 11 - The National Eisteddfod of Wales will be held at Mold, Flintshire, Wales.
  • August 4-August 11 - The 92nd Universal Congress of Esperanto in Yokohama, Japan
  • August 9-August 11 - The Emerging Information Technology Conference will be held in Princeton University, Princeton, New Jersey.
  • August 10-August 12 - The Delmarva Crab Festival In Milton Delaware. Food, fun, education and the future. Mark your calendar for these 2007 festival dates.
  • August 24-September 2 - The 2007 World Championships in Athletics will be held at Nagai Stadium in Osaka, Japan.
  • August 28 - Total lunar eclipse.
  • August 30-September 3 - The 65th World Science Fiction Convention will take place in Yokohama, Japan.
  • August 31 - Finland plans to completely cease analog television broadcasts.
