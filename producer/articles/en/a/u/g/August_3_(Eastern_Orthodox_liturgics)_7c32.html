[edit] Fixed commemorations

All fixed commemorations below are observed on August 16 by Old Calendarists

[edit] Saints

  • Holy Myrrh-bearer Salome
  • Venerable Isaac, Dalmatus, and Faustus, ascetics of the Dalmatian Monastery at Constantinople (5th century)
  • Venerable Anthony the Roman, Abbot in Novgorod (1147)
  • Saint Cosmas of Palestine, eunuch and hermit (6th century)
  • Martyr Razhden of Georgia (457)
  • Saint John of Patalaria Monastery, confessor and abbot
  • Saint Theoctistus the Wonderworker of Optimaton
  • Nun Theodora of Thessalonica

[edit] Other commemorations

  • Repose of Hiero-schemamonk Ignatius of Harbin (1958)
