Augenblick Studios

Augenblick Studios is an independent animation studio located in New York City. Founded in 1999 by Aaron Augenblick, their team of artists has created a wide array of animated shorts for television, film, and the Internet. Clients include the Cartoon Network, Comedy Central, Spike Lee, PBS, MTV, Nickelodeon, AT&T, Gateway Computers, and Cisco Systems. The studio is responsible for much of the animated content on MTV2's Wonder Showzen, as well as Comedy Central's weekly web series Golden Age.
