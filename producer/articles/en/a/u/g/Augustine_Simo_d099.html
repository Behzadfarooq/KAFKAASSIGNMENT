Augustine Simo (born September 18, 1978) was a Cameroonian footballer playing as a midfielder.

Part of his career was spent in France, at AS Saint-Étienne and in Switzerland at FC Aarau. He was member of the Cameroon national football team which participated in 1998 FIFA World Cup.
