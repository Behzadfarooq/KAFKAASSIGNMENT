Auguste-Alexandre Ducrot.

Auguste-Alexandre Ducrot (February 24, 1817, Nevers - August 16, 1882, Versailles) was a French general. Ducrot served in Algeria, in the Italian campaign of 1859, and as a division head in the German War. He was imprisoned for refusing to sign the capitulation treaty of Sedan, but escaped and took part in the defense of Paris when besieged by the Germans.
