[edit] 2005

  • New Calendarists only: Fasting day (Dormition) Fast
  • 8th Monday after Pentecost
  • 1st Corinthians 9:13-18
  • Matthew 16:1-6

[edit] Fixed commemorations

All fixed commemorations below are observed on August 21 by Old Calendarists.

[edit] Saints

  • Saint Emilian of Cyzicus, bishop and Confessor (815)
  • Venerable Gregory of Sinai (14th century)
  • Saint Myron of Crete, bishop (350)
  • Martyrs Eleutherius and Leonides of Constantinople, with many infants as well
  • Martyr Gormizdas of Persia
  • Saint Gregory of Kiev Caves, wonderworker
  • Martyr Triandaphyllus of Thessaly
  • Martyr Athanasios of Macedonia
  • The twelve ascetics of Egypt
  • The two martyrs of Tyre

[edit] Other commemorations

  • "Tolga" Icon of the Most Holy Theotokos
  • Translation of the relics of Venerable Zosimas and Sabbatius of Solovki (1566)
