Augustów County

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Augustów County (Polish: powiat augustowski) is a powiat (county) in eastern Poland, in Podlasie Voivodship. The county seat is the town of Augustów. The population numbers 59,025 (2005). Its area is 1658.27 km².
