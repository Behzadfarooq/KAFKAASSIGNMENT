Aughton Park railway station is a railway station in Aughton, Lancashire, England. It is situated on the Ormskirk branch of the Northern Line of the Merseyrail network.

[edit] Services

Trains operate every 15 minutes (Monday to Saturday daytime) between Ormskirk and Liverpool Central, and every 30 minutes at other times.
