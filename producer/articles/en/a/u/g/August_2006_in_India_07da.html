[edit] 31 August 2006

  • Training camp for the Indian cricket team begins at National Cricket Academy(NCA), Bangalore.
  • Discover of Racket lancer in chennai

[edit] 27 August 2006

  • Start of Ganesh Utsav (Ganpati Festival)
  • Veteran film-maker Hrishikesh Mukherjee dies after prolonged illnes.

[edit] 21 August 2006

  • Noted shehnai maestro Bismillah Khan passed away.

[edit] 20 August 2006

  • Three bogies of Chennai-Hyderabad Express catch fire.

[edit] 19 August 2006

  • UPA government decides against bringing the controversial amendment to the RTI Act.

[edit] 18 August 2006

  • President Kalam signs Office of Profit Bill after a decision to refer the issue to a Joint Parliamentary Committee (JPC) was taken.
  • Hundreds of people flock the Mahim beach after reports of sea-water turning "sweet".
