August 21 (Eastern Orthodox liturgics)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Aug. 20 - Eastern Orthodox Church calendar - Aug. 22

[edit] Fixed commemorations

All fixed commemorations below are observed on September 3 by Old Calendarists

[edit] Saints

  • Holy Apostle Thaddeus of the Seventy (44)
  • Martyrs Bassa and her sons Theogonius, Agapius, and Pistus
  • Venerable Abramius of Smolensk, archimandrite and wonderworker, and his disciple Epraim (1222)
  • Saint Abramius the Lover-of-Labor of the Kiev Caves Monastery (12th century)
  • Saint Theocleta the Wonderworker of Asia Minor (9th century)
  • Saint Cornelius of Paleostrov, abbot, and his disciple Saint Abramius (1420)
  • Saint Isaiah of Mt. Athos
