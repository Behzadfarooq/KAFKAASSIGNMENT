Coat of arms of the Hereditary Order of Armigerous Augustans, an allied association of the Augustan Society

The Augustan Society, headquartered in Daggett, CA, was founded in 1957 (1) to preserve material related to heraldry, genealogy, and orders of chivalry, and (2) to further chivalric ideals in society. The Society's library contains approximately 100,000 items. The Heraldry and Chivalry Collections are some of the most extensive on the West Coast.
