August Kluckhohn

August Kluckhohn (1832-93) was a German historian, born at Bavenhausen. He studied at Heidelberg and Göttingen, and in 1858 went to Munich to become one of the editors of Sybel's Historische Zeitschrift. In 1865 he was appointed professor of history at the Polytechnic School in Munich, and in 1883 he went to the University of Göttingen. His works include:

  • Geschichte des Gottesfriedens (1857)
  • Wilhelm III., Herzog von Bayern-München (1861)
  • Ludwig der Reiche, Herzog von Bayern-Landshut (1865)
  • Friedrich der Fromme, Kurfürst von der Pfalz (1876-79)
  • Luise, Königin von Preussen (1876)
  • Blücher (1879)
  • Ueber L. von Westenrieders Leben und Schriften (1890)
  • Vorträge und Aufsätze (1894)
