[edit] 2005

  • New Calendarists only: Fasting day (for the Dormition)
  • 7th Tuesday after Pentecost
  • 1st Corinthians 6:20-7:12
  • Matthew 14:1-13

[edit] Fixed commemorations

All fixed commemorations below are observed on August 15 by Old Calendarists

[edit] Saints

  • Blessed Basil of Moscow, Fool-for-Christ (1552)
  • Blessed Basil of Kubensk (15th century)
  • Hieromartyr Stephen, Pope of Rome
  • Saint Marco of Belavinsk in Vologda
  • Saint Friardus of Vidumitta in Gaul
  • Martyr Phocas
  • Martyr Theodore

[edit] Other commemorations

  • Translation of the relics of the Protomartyr and Archdeacon Saint Stephen in 428
  • Finding of the relics of righteous Nicodemus, Gamaliel, and Abibas in 428
  • Translation of the relics of martyrs Maximus, Dada, and Quinctilian at Dorostolum in Bulgaria
  • Repose of Priest Alexis Medvedkov of France (1934)
