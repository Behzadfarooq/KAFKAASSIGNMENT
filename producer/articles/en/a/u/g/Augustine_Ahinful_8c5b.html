Augustine Ahinful (born in Accra, November 30, 1974) is a Ghanaian football (soccer) player.

In career he played in Germany, Switzerland, Portugal, Italy and in Turkish Premier Super League for Borussia Dortmund, Grasshoppers, União Desportiva de Leiria, Venezia, Ankaragücü and Trabzonspor.

For Ghana national football team he participated at 1996 Summer Olympics and in 2000 African Cup of Nations.
