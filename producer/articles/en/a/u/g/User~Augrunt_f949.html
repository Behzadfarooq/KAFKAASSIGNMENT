[edit] Introduction to Steven

Esteban (Steven) Lopez --- Taken by a Logitech Sphere/Orbit


I am a constant browser of Wikipedia, It has replaced my Encarta books and I now sometimes just come on to just have a read and learn, I live in (and was born in) Australia but my parents are both from Uruguay. I am Currently 17 and I was born in February 11th 1989. I have recently contributed to a few articles, and have taken it upon myself to now visit Suburbs and take pictures of the City Center to further the Information found in Wikipedia using Photographs... I do believe a picture says a 1000 words...

I have traveled to Uruguay at the Age of 12 and touched down in New Zealand and Argentina before making the approach to the Uruguayan International Airport... After a scary landing at Buenos Aires, I dislike "Aerolíneas Argentinas" due to there "Crap" customer service and "crap" pilot training.

I Love Black Holes!

From there we changed to PLUNA Airlines, Where we flew on a small Boeing 737-200 to finally land at Montevideo and stay for one month and a half... There we took the same route back, Uruguay>Argentina>New Zealand>Australia using the same airlines used in the trip... I felt relieved that I'd be back in my Home Country... as I stepped off the plane, I kissed the ground and thanked god I made it back to my countries soil. Uruguay was and is currently in a State of a depression and continues to this day, it has major debts which effects the entire country all round, With job shortage and various economical defects. It is a sad tale.


I will add more as my Interest deteriorates.
