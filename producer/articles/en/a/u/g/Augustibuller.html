Augustibuller (Swedish for ’August sound pollution’) is a popular festival in Sweden, hosted by Lindesberg, Örebro County. The festival takes place first weekend in August, hence the name. It was first hosted in 1996, featuring only demo bands, mostly local ones. The festival was free of charge until 2001. It has three different stages with artists playing the festival's main genres: punk, hardcore, grindcore and ska.

[edit] History

The festival started out as a free one-day-festival with only a few local bands, but as the festival got more attention it grew. In 2000 it was held at a camping area, but the festival didn't start costing money until the year after, and then costing only 100 Swedish crowns.

After the festival 2006 the crew of Augustibuller thought about not arranging the festival for 2007. The reason for this was the increasing vandalism on the festival. But later that year they announced that an 11th year of Augustibuller would come, with some changes. When the first bands were announced, they also announced that the festival would be held during three days, instead of two.
