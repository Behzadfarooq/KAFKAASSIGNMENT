Aug. 17 - Eastern Orthodox Church calendar - Aug. 19

[edit] Fixed commemorations

All fixed commemorations below are observed on August 31 by Old calendarists

[edit] Saints

  • Martyrs Florus and Laurus of Illyria (2nd century)
  • Repose of Venerable John of Rila, abbot (946)
  • Martyrs Hermes, Serapion, Polyaenus of Rome (2nd century)
  • Hieromartyr Emilian and lay martyrs Hilarion, Dionysius, Hermippus, and about 1000 others in Italy
  • Saints John and George, Patriarchs of Constantinople (674,683)
  • Saints Barnabus and his nephew Sophronius, monks of Mount Melanear Trebizond (412)
  • Saint Christopher of Mt. Mela, abbot (668)
  • Saint Sophronius of St. Anne's skete on Mount Athos
  • Saint Arsenius the New of Paros
  • Martyr Juliana near Strobilus
  • Martyr Leo, drowned near Myra in Lycia
  • 4 holy ascetics, details unknown
