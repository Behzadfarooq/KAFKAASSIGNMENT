Augusto Severo International Airport (IATA: NAT, ICAO: SBNT) is an international airport located at Parnamirim, Rio Grande do Norte, Brazil. It handles national and international air traffic of the metropolitan area of Grande Natal.

[edit] Airlines

  • Air Italy (Rome)
  • TAM (Brasília, Fortaleza, Goiania, Recife, Rio de Janeiro-Galeão, Salvador, São Luis, São Paulo-Congonhas, São Paulo-Guarulhos)
  • TAP Portugal (Lisbon)
  • Fly Brazil (Dusseldorf)
  • Arke Fly (Amsterdam - Charter by TUI)
  • BRA Transportes Aereos (All Brazil and Lisbon - Portugal (Europe))
  • White Airlines (Portugal TAP Charter) (Lisbon - Portugal)
