August 17, 2006 Nanjing UFO Incident

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

You have new messages (last change).

On August 17, 2006, a UFO was spotted and recorded hovering over an apartment building in Nanjing, China. When the UFO reached the highest point of the apartment building, seven white lights turned on around it, and the UFO vanished.
