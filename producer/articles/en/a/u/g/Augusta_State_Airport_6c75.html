Augusta State Airport (IATA: AUG, ICAO: KAUG, FAA LID: AUG) is a public airport located one mile (2 km) northwest of the central business district (CBD) of Augusta, a city in Kennebec County, Maine, USA. The airport is owned by the State of Maine, but managed and operated by the City of Augusta. Augusta State Airport covers 350 acres and has two runways. It is mostly used for general aviation, but is served by one commercial airline. Service is subsidized by the Essential Air Service program.
