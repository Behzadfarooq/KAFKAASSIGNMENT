The Augsburger Panther are a member of the German hockey league (DEL). The team’s home is Augsburg, Germany and the home stadium is called Curt Frenzel Stadion. The team colors are red, green and white. The "AEV" is Germany´s oldest hockey club. It was founded in 1878. http://www.aev-panther.de/uploads/pics/pantherkopf_neu.jpg
