Augustino Viccini Special School

Augustino Viccini Special School
School type School for hearing impaired students
Established 1995
Location Mundamveli, Kerala, India

The Augustino Viccini Special School is a school for hearing impaired students in Mundamveli, Kerala, India. Founded in 1995, and managed by the Diocese of Cochin, it is the only one of its kind in West Kochi.
