Augustus A. Beekman

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

You have new messages (last change).

Augustus A. Beekman was appointed the 23rd Fire Commissioner of the City of New York by Mayor Edward I. Koch on January 17, 1978 and served in that position until his resignation on November 5, 1980.
