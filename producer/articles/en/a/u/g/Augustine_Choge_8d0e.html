Augustine Choge

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Augustine Kiprono Choge (born 21 January 1987) is a Kenyan long distance runner.

Choge was born in 1987 to a family of small-scale famers at Kipsigak, near Kapsabet in the Nandi District of Rift Valley Province, Kenya.

On 20 March 2006, he won the men's 5000 metres event at the 2006 Commonwealth Games with a time of 12 minutes, 56.41 seconds.[1]
