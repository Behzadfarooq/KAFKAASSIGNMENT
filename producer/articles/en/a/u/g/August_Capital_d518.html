August Capital is a private venture capital firm with offices on Menlo Park's famous Sand Hill Road. It has backed such companies as Tumbleweed Communications, PayCycle, Seagate, Splunk, Six Apart, VideoEgg, Evite, and Westbridge Technology.

The investment professionals are: David Hornik, John Johnston, David Marquardt, Vivek Mehra, and Andrew S. Rappaport. Investments made by these partners before the founding of August Capital include: Microsoft, Sun Microsystems, Adaptec, and Grand Junction Networks (acquired by Cisco).
