Maps

As my time permits, I am willing to create location maps (world maps, regional, local, ...) or thematic maps.

Particularly, I am focused on maps for articles relating to:

  • Washington, D.C.
  • New York City
  • Canadian Rockies
  • Wikipedia:WikiProject Protected areas

Other interests include developing a data source and how-to guide, as well as to clarify copyright/use-restrictions for spatial data (an issue for data from the Government of Canada).

The maps below were created using ArcGIS and polished using Adobe Illustrator and Photoshop:

2006 Canadian federal election results
