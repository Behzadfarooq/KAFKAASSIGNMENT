Audiopain

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Audiopain live at "South of Heaven" in Oslo, Norway, January 2, 2007.

Audiopain is a Norwegian thrash metal band, formed in 1996. Fenriz of Darkthrone has written the lyrics for Infuriation Scalp on the EP Revel In Desecration.
