Audrey Trueman was a fictional character in the popular British BBC soap opera EastEnders. She was played by Corinne Skinner-Carter, and made her first appearance on 6 November 2000.

[edit] History

Audrey was a busybody mother to Dr. Anthony Trueman, becoming deeply involved with his life, whilst ignoring her other son, tearaway Paul. She didn't approve of Anthony dating Kat Slater and tried to interfere many times leading to Anthony rebelling against his mother. Audrey's main reason for ignoring Paul was that in the past Paul and Anthony had been in a car crash that killed a person. Anthony was drunk at the time and was driving but Paul took the blame and went to prison. Audrey was unaware of this and continued to ignore Paul despite his attempts at making her notice him. One day, Audrey suffered a blow to the head accidentally and after an argument with Paul during which he revealed Anthony was the one who was driving at the time of the accident, Audrey collapsed and died. Just before she died she told Paul to tell Anthony that she loved him and Paul said "What about me?". Just as Audrey was about to say something, she died. However her will left Paul everything.

[edit] Family

  • Granddaughter: Eleanor Trueman
