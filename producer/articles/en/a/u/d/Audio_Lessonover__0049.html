Audio Lessonover? is the fourth studio album to be released by the British Christian rock band Delirious?. It was released in August 2001.

The first single released from this album, Waiting For The Summer, reached number twenty-six in the British charts. "Take Me Away", originally planned to be the second single, was released to the internet as a free MP3 download.

Once again, mainstream chart success for the band was hindered by the refusal of BBC Radio 1, the UK national pop station, to playlist them. In addition, the album was met with some resistance by Christian fans who were disappointed at the lack of overtly religious lyrics. However, several tracks from the album have become popular live songs at the band's concerts.
