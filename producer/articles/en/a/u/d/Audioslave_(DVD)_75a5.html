Audioslave is the name of a DVD by American rock band Audioslave, released in 2003. It contains the three videos the band had made up to that point, as well as two songs performed live on the David Letterman Show.

[edit] Chapter Listing

  1. Cochise
  2. Like a Stone
  3. Show Me How to Live
  4. Set It Off (live)
  5. Gasoline (live)

[edit] Line-up

  • Chris Cornell - Vocals
  • Tim Commerford - Bass
  • Brad Wilk - Drums
  • Tom Morello - Guitars
