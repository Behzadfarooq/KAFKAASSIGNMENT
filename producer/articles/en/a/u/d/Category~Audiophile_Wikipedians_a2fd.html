Wikipedians who consider themselves to be audiophiles, those who value high fidelity recording and playback of music.

This user is an audiophile.

To add yourself to this category, add {{user audiophile}} or [[Category:Audiophile Wikipedians|YOUR NAME HERE]] to your user page. Replace YOUR NAME HERE with the name you wish to be listed under, either a nickname or your real name, whatever you prefer.

This category is a self-reference and so is part of the Wikipedia project itself rather than the encyclopedic content.
