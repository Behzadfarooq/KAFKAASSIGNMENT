Audrey Glenn McMillian (born August 13, 1962 in Carthage, Texas), is an American former professional football player who played defensive back in 8 NFL seasons from 1985-1993 for the Houston Oilers and Minnesota Vikings. McMillian played football collegiately at the University of Houston and was selected by the New England Patriots in the 3rd round of the 1985 NFL Draft. He was a Pro Bowl selection in 1992 after leading the NFL, along with Buffalo's Henry Jones, with 8 interceptions.
