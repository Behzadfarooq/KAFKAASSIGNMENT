Chris Hambly MA ODE is an educational technologist and the Director of Audiocourses.com, an online distance learning school, for audio and music technology production, London. He is a musician, new media operator and thinker and has been working as an educational professional since 1994, having been a recording studio/PA engineer and a Royal Navy aircraft radio technician previously. He is also the producer and director of AC Podcast, an internet radio show focusing on music technology, the school's culture and activities, reflections on life, and education.
