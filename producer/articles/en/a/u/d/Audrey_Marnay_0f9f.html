Audrey Marnay (born October 14, 1980 in Chartres, France) is a supermodel.

Audrey Marnay began modeling at the age of fifteen. From then on she appeared on many magazine covers such as Vogue and Elle, and in advertisements from Versace to Calvin Klein. She is known for her natural, spontaneity and cute freckled face. She's been photographed by the greatest ones, Steven Meisel, Irving Penn, Herb Ritts, Jean-Baptiste Mondino, Paolo Roversi and Peter Lindbergh. She is the French face in the fashion world and more. After two years in acting school in Paris she realized acting was the way she wanted to continue her career. March 2006 was her debut in acting. She played the role of Laetitia in Bunker Paradise. Audrey is the fiancee of fashion show producer Alexandre de Betak. They have two sons Amaël (2000) and Aidyn (2003).
