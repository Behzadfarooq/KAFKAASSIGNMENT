Audrey J. Walton Stadium

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Audrey J. Walton Stadium on the campus of the University of Missouri is the home of the Missouri Tigers track and field teams and the prestigious Missouri Relays track and field meet. The stadium had a seating capacity of 10,000 and is also the home of the men's and women's soccer teams at the University of Missouri.
