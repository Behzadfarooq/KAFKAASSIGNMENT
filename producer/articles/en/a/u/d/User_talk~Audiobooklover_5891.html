[edit] Recommendation on Recording for the Blind & Dyslexic

As this article stands now, it looks as if you have created an article that was copied en bloc from somewhere else. It has no links to other articles, and is not formatted according to WP:MOS. Copying text from other sites is not allowed by Wikipedia rules - see Wikipedia:Copyright - unless permission by the copyright holder is given. [edit] Copyright issue with Recording for the Blind & Dyslexic

Hello. Concerning your contribution, Recording for the Blind & Dyslexic, we cannot accept copyrighted text or images borrowed from either web sites or printed material. This article or image appears to be a direct copy from http://www.43things.com/things/view/271740. As a copyright violation, Recording for the Blind & Dyslexic appears to qualify for speedy deletion under the speedy deletion criteria. Recording for the Blind & Dyslexic has been tagged for deletion, and may have been deleted by the time you see this message. For text material, please consider rewriting the content and citing the source, provided that it is credible.

If you believe that the article or image is not a copyright violation or if you have permission from the copyright holder to release the content freely under the GFDL, you can comment to that effect on Talk:Recording for the Blind & Dyslexic. If the article or image has already been deleted, but you have a proper release, you can reenter the content at Recording for the Blind & Dyslexic, after describing the release on the talk page. However, for text content, you may want to consider rewriting the content in your own words. Thank you, and please feel free to continue contributing to Wikipedia.
