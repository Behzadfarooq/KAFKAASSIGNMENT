Audri DuBois

Audri Dubois Marchionno (born December 4, 1967 in Los Angeles, California) played Trini in the pilot episode of the Mighty Morphin Power Rangers TV Series. She was replaced by the late Thuy Trang for the inception of the TV Series.

At a young age, She was taken to Mann's Chinese Theatre to watch Fist of Fury. She enjoyed the film, so she wanted to imitate people fling through the air, beating up the bad guys, and fight. She wished to practice martial arts.

[edit] Trivia

  • She now works at Sea World in San Antonio
  • She was a key hair stylist in the show Paparazzi.
  • Her religion is Scientology.
  • She is an avid Bruce Lee Fan.
  • She was cast to play as April Jones in VR Troopers, but things didn't work out.
  • Likes writing, reading, cooking, drawing, crafts (soap and candle making) traveling, golf, working out, and yoga.
  • Her favorite past time is teaching/practicing martial arts.
  • Her favorite movie is Enter the Dragon.
