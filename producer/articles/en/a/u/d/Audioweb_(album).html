Audioweb (album)

Audioweb was the eponymously titled first album from the 1990s' rock/reggae/electronica group based in Manchester, United Kingdom. The album was released in the UK in October 1996.

The album was produced by Kevin Bacon and Jonathan Quarmby with additional production and programming by Audioweb. A special limited edition of the album was released at the same time as original version; this contained a bonus 6 track live CD.

All tracks on the live CD were performed at the Reading Festival in August 1996 except Faker, which was performed at BBC Radio One's Sound City music festival held in Leeds during April 1996.
