Audiogram (label)

Audiogram is a Quebec-based independent record label.

For more than 20 years, the company has been representing artists who have eched a permanent mark on the Quebec cultural scene: Paul Piché, Michel Rivard, Jim Corcoran, Laurence Jalbert, Pierre Flynn, Daniel Bélanger, Jean Leloup, Lhasa de Sela, Mara Tremblay, Loco Locass, Marc Déry, Ariane Moffatt, Pierre Lapointe, Damien Robitaille, and many more keep enriching local and global culture.
