Audley Anderson (born 27 August 1971) is an English singer from Thornton Heath near Croydon and songwriter who has appeared in Fame Academy, on CBBC, RISE and on all the major UK TV networks.

Anderson was a Helpdesk Manager before he got his first break in music by featuring in Fame Academy in 2003. He has been seen at a few high profile shows supporting acts such as Boys 2 Men and Ja Rule.

He is working towards releasing his first single, entitled "Undress U". An album, A Journey into Soul, is also being produced, and will be released at some point in the future.

Audley will be touring the UK in 07 with the theatre production "Ridin on a Soul train" with Jaki graham, Jeffrey Daniel (Shalamar) and Voices with Soul X-factor

Anderson's musical influences include Luther Vandross, Barry White, Jodeci, Tyrese, The Temptations, Stevie Wonder, James Ingram, Omar and Don-E.
