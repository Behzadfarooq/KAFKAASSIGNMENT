Audrey Carville

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Audrey Carville is the host of the BBC World Service radio show Europe Today. She is known for her tough questioning of European public figures on the show. Previously Carville was the host of BBC Radio Ulster in Northern Ireland.
