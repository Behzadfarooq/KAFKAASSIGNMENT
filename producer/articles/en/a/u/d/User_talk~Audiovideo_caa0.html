[edit] Pronouns

Thanks for your comments. No, I don't have a problem with pronouns - as you imply, I have continued their use after the first naming at the beginning of a paragraph. I guess I simply prefer to restate the subject at the beginning of the paragraph and use a consistent style. I appreciate that the surname alone can seem aggressive to some people, but I felt this is better than Mr X, Firstname Lastname or the other variations that I have come across. I'll bear your point in mind and try not to be quite so prescriptive - the main point of my edits has been to reflect that many are no longer MPs.

Thanks again. Mtiedemann 23:48, 12 Apr 2005 (UTC)
