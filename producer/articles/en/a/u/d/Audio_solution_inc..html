Audio Solution Inc. is a Taiwan-based audio and broadcast technology company. It was founded in 1996 by a group led by Allen Chou. At its initial formation, Audio Solution was involved primarily in digital audio technology. The company provides technology solutions for Taiwanese businesses, and supplies turnkey systems for radio stations, TV stations, OB vans and theaters.

[edit] Specialized in solutions for

  • Digital recording studios
  • Radio stations
  • TV stations
  • Post production
  • CD / DVD Audio / SACD Pre-mastering

[edit] History

  • 1996 - Allen Chou and partners founded the company
  • 1996 - Became Digidesign distributor in Taiwan
  • 2004 - Became Lawo distributor in Taiwan
  • 2004 - Became Jutel Radioman distributor in Taiwan
