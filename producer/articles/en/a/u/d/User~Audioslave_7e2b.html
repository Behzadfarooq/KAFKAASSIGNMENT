Audioslave's real name is Laura. She is a middle school student in Oklahoma. Her hobbies are listening to music, going to concerts and shows, playing bass guitar, singing, acting, dancing (hip hop), going to the movies, and shopping.

For Laura, music is life. Her favorite bands are A Perfect Circle, Pearl Jam, Rage Against the Machine, Soundgarden, Tool, Temple of the Dog, Stone Temple Pilots, Audioslave, and Alice in Chains. Her favorite local bands are Dark Crysalis, Blank Stare, and Minutes Too Far. Her musical heroes are Tom Morello, Maynard James Keenan, Eddie Vedder, Chris Cornell, Matt Cameron, and Tim Commerford.She is a big fan of grunge, but doesn't like Nirvana.
