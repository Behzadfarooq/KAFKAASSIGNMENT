Audrey Lindvall (August 11, 1982 – August 2, 2006) was an American fashion model. Audrey was the sister of supermodel Angela Lindvall and the former face of Coach and Ann Taylor.

On August 2, 2006, Lindvall was killed by a truck in Lee’s Summit, Missouri, while out on a bicycle ride with a friend. [1]
