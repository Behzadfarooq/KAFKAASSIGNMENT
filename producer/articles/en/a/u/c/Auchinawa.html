Auchinawa is an anime convention based Glasgow, Scotland first held in 2006 and the first anime convention to be held in Scotland.[1] A winter date means it does not overlap with other UK conventions.

[edit] Auchinawa 2006

The Auchinawa 2006 convention was run between the 30 November and 3 December 2006, at The Quality Hotel (known to some as the Central Hotel), which is located in the very heart of Glasgow's town centre and is partially inside Glasgow Central train station. The number of available spaces was decreased to 450 (totalling 600 people including guests and ancillaries) from over a thousand.

[edit] Guests

  • Jan Scott Frazier [2]
  • Scott Ramsoomair of webcomic VG Cats [3]
  • Dave Cheung and Jamal Joseph Jnr of webcomic Chugworth Academy
  • Paul Gravett
  • Mugenkyo

[edit] Events

The following events were confirmed at Auchinawa 2006:

  • Webcomics Panel (Dave Cheung, Jamal Joseph Jnr and Scott Ramsoomair)
  • Guest Panel (Jan Scott Frazier)
  • Parties (Cosplay Ball, AMV Disco, Anime Disco)
  • Elegant Gothic Lolita Tea Party (run by Ouran High School Host Club cosplayers)
  • Mugenkyo Concert
  • Pub Quiz
  • Games Room (Including Para Para and DDR machines run by DDRUK.com )
  • Other Panels (Cosplay, AMV)

Additionally, standard Anime Convention events such as a Masquerade, dealers' rooms, video rooms, artists' alley, and CCG rooms were part of the convention.

[edit] In the press

Auchinawa made it into the press for being the first Anime Convention in Scotland. This was picked up by the BBC who produced a section on Cosplay which featured in the TV program Video Gaiden.[4][dubious — see talk page] The show was shown on December 5, 2006.
