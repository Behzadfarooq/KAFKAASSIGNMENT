Auckland Girls' Grammar School is a prestigious New Zealand secondary school for girls located in Newton, in the Auckland central business district. Established in 1888, it is one of the oldest and most well-respected secondary institutions in the country.

[edit] Notable Alumni

  • Hon. Georgina Te Heuheu, National MP, former Minister of Courts and Womens' affairts under 1996 National-New Zealand First Government
  • Dr. Penelope Brook Cowen, economist
  • Indira Moala, Top 2 contestant on NZ Idol 2006
  • Lana Coc-Kroft, Miss Universe NZ 1988, television presenter
  • Candy Lane, famous professional dancer and host of Dancing with the Stars (New Zealand TV series)
