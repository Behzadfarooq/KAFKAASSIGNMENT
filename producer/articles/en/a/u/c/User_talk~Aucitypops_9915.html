I presume you are new... a hint for when you sign your entries, use ~~~~ otherwise called 'four tildes' and it will sign your entry automatically, and date stamp it. If you look down under the 'Show changes' button, you will see the 'sign your name' with the four tildes showing. Click on those tildes and it will automatically enter them in for you.
