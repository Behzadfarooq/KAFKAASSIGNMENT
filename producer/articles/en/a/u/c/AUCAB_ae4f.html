[edit] Introduction

The AUCAB is the American Universities and Colleges Association of Bangladesh. The organization was formed in 2006, with the goal to promote networking between its members, who are alumni from American Universities and Colleges and increase contacts, discussion of job opportunities in the country, and in general promote discussion and social networking.

[edit] Current Students

Students from Bangladesh who are currently studying in Colleges and Universities in America are also encouraged to join the AUCAB, where they are encouraged to ask questions about job scope in Bangladesh if they decided to return and other social and work related opportunities.


[edit] Scholarship Program

The AUCAB will also plan to raise funds to support needy students who wish to study in America. As such any donations to the organization are welcome. The funds will be received by qualified students who show need for the funds to the study in the United States.


[edit] Contact Information

A permanent web page will be set up at www.aucab.com, in the meantime the contact address of the organization is ceo@bijoy.net
