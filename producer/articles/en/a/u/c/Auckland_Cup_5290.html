Auckland Cup

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

[edit] Harness Racing/Pacing

The Auckland Cup is a major horse race, held for standardbred horses in Auckland, New Zealand each year.

[edit] Thoroughbred Racing/Galloping

The Sky City Auckland Cup is an annual race held by the Auckland Racing Club (ARC). It is an Open Handicap for thoroughbred racehorses competed on the flat turf over 3200 metres (two miles) at Ellerslie Racecourse in Auckland, New Zealand. The race is graded as a Group One (G1) and was first contested in 1874. The current purse is valued at NZD$600,000.
