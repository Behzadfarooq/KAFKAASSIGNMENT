Auckland usually refers to the metropolitan area of Auckland (Greater Auckland) in New Zealand.

Auckland can also refer to:

[edit]  New Zealand

  • Auckland City (Central Auckland)
  • Auckland Region, a current region of New Zealand
  • Auckland Province, a former province of New Zealand
  • Auckland Islands, a sub-Antarctic archipelago of New Zealand
    • Auckland Island, the main island of the Auckland Islands

[edit]  United Kingdom and  Ireland

  • Baron Auckland and Earl of Auckland in both the Peerage of Ireland and the Peerage of Great Britain; Auckland in New Zealand is named for the only Earl of Auckland, George Eden.
  • Bishop Auckland, a town in County Durham in England
    • Auckland Castle, the official residence of the Bishop of Durham in Bishop Auckland

[edit]  South Africa

  • Auckland Park, Gauteng, a suburb of Johannesburg

[edit]  Republic of Korea

  • Mount Auckland, a peak on Jeju Island usually called Hallasan
