Auchenharvie

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Auchenharvie is a former colliery in Stevenston, Ayrshire, Scotland that was devastated by a pit disaster on August 2, 1895 in which nine lives were lost.

[edit] List of the deceased

  • John Clauchan (35)
  • William Clauchan (age unknown)
  • James Clauchan (21)
  • Henry Clauchan (18)
  • Duncan Gallacher (31)
  • James Mullen (19)
  • Peter Mullen (14)
  • Robert McConn (18)
  • John McGhee (14)

[edit] Trivia

The event has been immortalised in the poem "The Star of Young McGhee".
