This page provides links to the range of articles on districts within the Auckland metropolitan area of northern New Zealand. The term district in this context is taken to mean a definable area of any size (such as a suburb), but not an individual building or street. The Auckland metropolitan area area is made up of Auckland City (excluding the Hauraki Gulf islands), North Shore City, and the urban parts of Waitakere and Manukau cities, along with Papakura District and some nearby urban parts of Rodney and Franklin Districts. For lists of suburbs, see the articles on Auckland's component cities and districts.
