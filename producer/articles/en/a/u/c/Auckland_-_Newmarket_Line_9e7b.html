The Auckland - Newmarket Line, sometimes called the Newmarket Branch, is a railway line in Auckland, New Zealand. It is 2.64 km long and runs between Quay Park Junction, near Britomart, Auckland, and Newmarket. It connects the North Island Main Trunk, which runs into Britomart via the waterfront, and the North Auckland Line, which runs between Westfield and Otiria via Newmarket and Whangarei. It is double track and has a 343 m tunnel under Parnell Road.
