Au Cœur Du Stade is Céline Dion's fifth DVD/video release. This 90-minute concert was recorded live at the famous Stade de France in Paris, during the Let's Talk About Love Tour. It was recorded on June 19, 1999 and released on August 30, 1999.

Dion during the Stade de France concert (1999).

"To Love You More" features Taro Hakase on violin. Jean-Jacques Goldman joined Céline Dion on "J'Irai Où Tu Iras" and Barnev Valsaint (band member) on "I'm Your Angel". Diana King can be seen on a screen during "Treat Her Like a Lady." Dion paid tribute to the Bee Gees singing "Stayin' Alive" and "You Should Be Dancing." The performance of "Tell Him" with Barbra Streisand on a big screen was cut from the DVD.

The Au Cœur Du Stade DVD also includes exclusive footage from the making of S'il Suffisait d'Aimer and Let's Talk About Love. It features an appearance by guest star Sir George Martin, and rarely seen footage of Céline Dion, Barbra Streisand, David Foster, and the "Tell Him" lyricists chatting around the piano. The entire "Tell Him" video is also included on this disk.

Au Cœur Du Stade was certified diamond in France for selling over 100,000 copies. On the French Music Videos chart, which is available since September 2003, Au Cœur Du Stade peaked at #9.

Au Cœur Du Stade CD is also available.

The video was re-released in November 2005 as 2-DVD set with All the Way... A Decade of Song and Video.
