Au Revoir, UFO (안녕!유에프오 Annyeong UFO) is a 2004 South Korean film. It was one of the final films made by Lee Eun-ju before her unexpected suicide in 2005, and was also the directorial debut of Kim Jae-won. The film tells the story of a romance between a man and a blind woman in a town where everyone believes in UFOs.
