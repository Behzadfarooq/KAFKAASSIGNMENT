[edit] Plural words don't need apostrophes

Hi A7X,

Thanks for your contributions to Wikipedia. However, when you're writing words like "eyes", "areas", "temperatures", "attacks", etc, these words don't need apostrophes. Please don't just stick in an apostrophe just because a word ends in S. here is the sort of thing I'm talking about. If you have further questions, please feel free to drop a note on my talk page. Thanks, and happy editing! :) Firsfron of Ronchester 22:29, 2 October 2006 (UTC)

[edit] Images

Please do not upload unfree images for use on your userpage (this includes files that only Wikipedia have permission to use), and also make sure to provide the source information (who created the images and where did you find them?). Images that have not been explicitly released as free content by theyr creator can only be used on Wikipedia as fair use, and then only when used in articles (not user pages). Repeatedly re-uploading such images after they have been deleted can be considered vandalism, so please stop doing that. Thanks. --Sherool (talk) 18:01, 28 October 2006 (UTC)

Images you find in the subcategories of Category:Free images should be ok to use for any purpose (baring the ocational mislabeled image), including userpages. You can also use any imgage found on Commons directly (just include them as any other image). Just don't forget that there is more to Wikipedia than decorating your usepage ;) --Sherool (talk) 02:01, 4 November 2006 (UTC)

[edit] Rock music Wikiproject invitation

Hello WikiProject Metal member.
WikiProject Metal music is important in expanding encyclopedic coverage of the metal. It brings attention to the lesser-known bands, and significantly improves the quality of the famous ones. Five Featured articles and two formers is proof of that.

Rock on.
