The Motorway A7 (Croatian: Autocesta A7) is a motorway in western Croatia. Currently, only short section from Rijeka to the Slovenian border exists (Rijeka bypass doesn't count, as it isn't a motorway, only a 2 lane road) and a very short part from the A1 motorway near Žuta Lokva. The motorway between Rijeka and Žuta Lokva is in planning stage.
