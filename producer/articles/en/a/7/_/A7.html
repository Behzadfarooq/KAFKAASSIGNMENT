A7 roads:

  • The A7 road in the United Kingdom from Edinburgh to Carlisle.
  • The A7, an autoroute in France from Lyon to Marseille.
  • The A7, the longest German Autobahn running all the way from Denmark to Austria
  • The A7 (Croatia), a motorway in northwest Croatia
  • The A7 (Switzerland), a motorway in Switzerland from Winterthur to Kreuzlingen.
  • The A7 (Netherlands), a motorway in the Netherlands from Nieuweschans to Amsterdam. Part of this road is also the European route E22 (running over the Afsluitdijk).
  • The A7 (Spain), a motorway in Spain that runs all along the mediterranean coast, from Algeciras to the border with France at La Jonquera.
  • The A7 road (Luxembourg), a motorway in Luxembourg from Luxembourg City to Clervaux.
  • West Tamar Highway, in Tasmania

In military A7 or A-7 may mean:

  • HMS A7, an A-class submarine of the Royal Navy.
  • USS A-7, a Plunger-class submarine of the United States Navy.
  • The Ling-Temco-Vought A-7 Corsair II, a light attack aircraft.
  • A7, a German rocket design in World War II, never implemented.
  • The Soviet VHF transceiver A7, a radio developed during World War II.
  • UK Joint Force Air Component Headquarters A7- Air Training branch.
  • Skoda 37 mm A7, a tank gun produced before and during World War II by the Skoda Works.
