The Aruban Patriot Movement (Movimento Patriótico Arubano) is a political party in Aruba, formed by dissidents of the Aruban Patriotic Party. At the last elections for the Estates, 23 September 2005, the party won 7 % of the popular vote and 1 out of 21 seats.
