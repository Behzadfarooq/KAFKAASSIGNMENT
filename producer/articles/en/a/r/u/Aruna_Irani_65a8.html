Aruna Irani (Hindi: अरुणा ईरानी, Urdu: اَرُنا ایرانی) is an Indian actress of mixed Irani Zoroastrian and Hindu background. She has acted in about 300 movies. She is the sister of producer-director Indra Kumar and wife of film director Kuku Kohli.

Aruna Irani made her debut in the movie Gunga Jumna (1961). In the 1970s she appeared in many films as a vamp which other actresses such as Helen and Bindu were famous for in the 70s. In the 1980s and 1990s she appeared as a character-actor playing motherly roles.
