Arundel High School is located in Gambrills in Anne Arundel County, Maryland.

The principal is Sharon Stratton and there are currently 2,471 students enrolled. The school, which is over 150 years old, is part of the Anne Arundel County Public School system, and is the primary high school for the Odenton, Gambrills, Crofton, and Piney Orchard areas. The current building in which the school is located was built in 1949 with additions in 1966, 1985, and 2006.
