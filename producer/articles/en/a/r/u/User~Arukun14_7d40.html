You may know what the first part of my ID states, arukun14. It was influenced from the anime Fullmetal Alchemist, from Alphonse Elric, one of the main characters. ARU, is what he is addressed by Edward Elric, his older brother, and -KUN, a honorific in Japan, referring to a man someone close to them. -KUN is usually addressed by their social superiors, like Al and Ed being close brothers. And finally, the number 14. This was the age when I first started using the alias ARU-kun, and it is one of my lucky numbers.

I work here in Wikipedia, TV.com, and my website. I love what I do, and I hope I meet friends along the way.

I am not just credited as arukun14. I have quite the reputation to be called these names: arukun or Arukun, Aru, or Heatblast.
