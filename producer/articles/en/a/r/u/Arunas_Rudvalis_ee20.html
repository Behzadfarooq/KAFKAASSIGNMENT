Arunas Rudvalis (born June 8, 1945) is an Associate Professor of Mathematics at the University of Massachusetts - Amherst. He is best known for the Rudvalis group.

Rudvalis went to the Harvey Mudd College and received his Ph.D. degree in Dartmouth College under direction of Ernst Snapper.
