Arundhati (mythology)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Arundhati (अरुंधति) was the wife of the sage (rishi) Vasishtha, and the mythology identifies her with the morning star. She is invoked during the marriage ceremonies of Hindus, and is a representative of an ideal wife.
