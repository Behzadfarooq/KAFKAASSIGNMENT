[edit] Roman Urdu

Please, make whatever changes or systems you'd like to the Roman Urdu page. What little I did is only suggstions, and it would be very nice to have someone with some experience to work on it. Thank you! Kitabparast 00:09, 3 November 2006 (UTC)

Salaam-e khudavand aap ke sath ho! I noticed your comments on Kitabparast and Fayyaz Abbas's talk pages. It's a pleasure to meet someone who wants to contribute to the article. I typed the Roman Urdu Zabur 23 and the Roman Urdu examples. This standard is based on the one the Kita'b I Muqaddas uses. As I am an Urdu/Hindi speaking Christian, I picked up the Bible from Lucknow, my hometown in India. Fayyaz Abbas introduced another standard he knew and on the talk page we were all discussing which system would be best to use in the article. Your comments on the talk page would be very much appreciated. I saw that you were willing to help on the article. May I request that you format the Perso-Arabic script here in Bible verse form as the Roman Urdu (above) and Devanagari (below) are. Also, if you'd like, you can order a Roman Urdu Bible from The Bible Society of India. They have been in print for a number of years now. Thanks so much for your enthusiasm and willingness to help. Khuda hafiz, AnupamTalk 03:22, 3 November 2006 (UTC).

[edit] link to British

Hello, when you want to link to the article about something British, please do not link to British, as that is a disambiguation page (which nothing should be linked to). Instead link to the one of the options found on that page such as United Kingdom, Great Britain or British English by writing out [[United Kingdom|British]] or [[Great Britain|British]].
