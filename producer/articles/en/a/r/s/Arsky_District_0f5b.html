Arsky District (Russian: А́рский райо́н, Tatar: Арча районы, Arça rayonı), also called Arça District or Archa District, is one of the 43 districts (raions) of the Republic of Tatarstan, Russia. The administrative center of the district is the urban-type settlement of Arsk. The district was established on September 10, 1930 after Arsky Kanton was abolished. In 1990, Atninsky District was split from Arsky District, and the borders of Arsky District have not changed since then. District's population: 51,343 (2002 Census); 61,959 (1989 Census).
