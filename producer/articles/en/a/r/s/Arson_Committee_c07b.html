Arson Committee was formed in 2004 in Bloomington, Illinois by Scott Blair and Tim Nash. The two met at marching band camp in the summer. The band was later joined by Nick Hutchins and Alex Auer. They have released one demo album and performed at several local competitions. Winners of the local Battle of the Bands competition. They are best known for their singles Schedule A and Wonderland.
