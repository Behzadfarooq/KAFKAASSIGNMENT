Arsenal of Megadeth is an anthology of Megadeth's first 20 years. The 2-disc DVD set contains music videos, live performances, interviews, home videos and special appearances. Arsenal Of Megadeth is an EMI/Capitol release.

[edit] Miscellanea

[edit] The Writing on The Bomb

The writing on the bomb is in Hangul, Korean
메가데스 = Megadeth
조병창 = "Military Bank", "Ordnance Depot", "Arsenal"
메가데스 조병창 = "Arsenal of Megadeth" (Loose Translations)

[edit] Penelope Spheeris

Penelope Spheeris directed the "No More Mr. Nice Guy" video which features comedian/radio personality Jeff Duran. This was for the Shocker movie soundtrack. There were two versions of this video that appeared on MTV. This version includes clips from the film.
