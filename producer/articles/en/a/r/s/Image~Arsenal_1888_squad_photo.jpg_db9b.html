[edit] Summary

The squad of Royal Arsenal FC in 1888. From left to right:

Front row: Morris, Babour, Charteris
Seated: Brown, Connolly, Danskin
Standing: Horsington, Wilson, Beardsley, Bates (captain), McBean, Scott
Back: Parr
