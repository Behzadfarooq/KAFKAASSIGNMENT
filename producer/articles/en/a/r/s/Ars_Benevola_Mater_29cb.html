Ars Benevola Mater

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Ars Benevola Mater is an Italian independent record label, born in order to release and promote music, created by artists of different cultural origin with special sight on East-European territory (Poland, Hungary, Czechia, Slovakia, Latvia, etc.). Its spiritual father, founder and owner is Mauro Casagrande. Ars Benevola Mater´s production content all sorts of avantgarde projects from ambient, dark ambient, noise or ambient industrial music up to various movements and forms of contemporary classical music, but it is not belonging to the usual idiomatic clichés. Ars Benevola Mater is not also linked to any political or religious ideal. All production of the label is registered to SIAE (Società Italiana Autori ed Editori). The label was established in 1999.

[edit] Label artists

Atrax Morgue, Novy Svet, Job Karma, Nostalgia, Radio Kuolema, Lagowski, Vladimír Hirsch, Tamás Kátai, Our God Weeps, Oxyd, Caregon Del Diol, Rapid Action Brigade, Anima Mundi, Romowe Rikoito, Santa Biasin Trio, Matt Howden, Synta(xe)rror, Teatro Satanico, Bellborn, Thorn-Agram, Tor Lundvall, etc.
