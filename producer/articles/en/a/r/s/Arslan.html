Arslan (Turkish for "lion"; Ottoman ارسلان arslān and اصلان aṣlān) was used as a title by Seljuk and Ottoman rulers. Arslan is also in use as a surname.

[edit] People

  • Alp Arslan, Seljuk ruler
  • L'Emir Magid Arslan (1908-83) was a political leader in Lebanon
  • Professor Tughrul Arslan holds a chair at the University of Edinburgh

[edit] Fiction

  • Amir Arsalan the celebrated, the protagonist of a Persian epic
  • The Heroic Legend of Arslan is a fantasy novel, and Arslan is the main character.

[edit] Other uses

  • Ghezel Arsalan (3250m) is a peak of the Mount Alvand range
  • Arslan Uniforms is a company based in Cincinnati, Ohio that sells uniforms.
