My real name is Julien and I am a PhD student in computing at Imperial College London. I'm currently working on Image-Based Modeling And Rendering, focusing more on methods that generate an approximative 3D model using a sparse camera coverage.

I'm also interested in hiking, marathon and ultramarathon.

My nickname has nothing to do with the toxic gas, it refers to a lovely place in the Dauphiné Alps (Southern French Alps). A small village, a chalet, a mountain pass, a morainic lake, a glacier and two peaks are named after it.
