Arsonal is a student at Cornell University's College of Engineering and 2005 alumnus of Westfield High School. He enjoys web programming with XHTML/CSS and is interested in learning new programming languages like PHP/MySQL. He is also available as a Wikipedia translator from Indonesian to English. His page on the Indonesian Wikipedia is Rhandjaja.

View Arsonal's editing statistics. He can be contacted at the discussion page.
