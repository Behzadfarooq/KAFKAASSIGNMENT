Arsuk (founded 1805) is a village in southwest Greenland in the municipality of Paamiut. Population is 140 (as of 2005). Location is 61°10′N 48°27′W at the mouth of the Arsuk Fjord which ends in the Arsuk glacier (see image).

Arsuk Glacier, at the end of Arsuk Fjord.

Arsuk is Greenlandic for "the beloved place".
