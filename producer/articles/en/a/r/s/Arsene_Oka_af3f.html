Arsene Oka (born December 20, 1983) is a Ivorian footballer, who currently plays as a left-sided midfielder for the New England Revolution of the MLS. He has played previously for Cote D'Ivoire's Stade d'Abidjan, Stella Club d'Adjame and Africa Sports National before signing a developmental contract with the Revolution in September 2006.

He has appeared for Cote D'Ivoire's U20 and U17 national teams.
