Ary de Sá

Ary Façanha de Sá is a former Brazilian long jumper.

At the 1952 Summer Olympics he finished fourth in the long jump. He also competed at the 1956 Summer Olympics. [1] He became South American long jump champion in 1952, won silver medals in 1956 and 1958 and a bronze medal in 1954. [2] He also won a bronze medal at the 1955 Pan American Games [3] and a gold medal at the 1955 World Student Games. [4]
