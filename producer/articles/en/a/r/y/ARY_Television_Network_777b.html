ARY Television Network is subsidiary of the ARY Group. The ARY Group of companies is a Dubai-based holding company founded by a Pakistani businessman, Haji Abdul Razzak Yaqoob (ARY). The channels owned or affiliated with ARY Television Network include:

  • ARY Digital - Infotainment and Sports
  • ARY One World - 24 hour independent news
  • The Musik - 24 hour music
  • Q tv - Quran TV, religious content
  • Al Jazeera Urdu
  • ARY Bangla
  • The City Channel
  • Fashion TV Pakistan
  • DESH
  • HBO Pakistan
