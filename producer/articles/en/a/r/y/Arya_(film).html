Arya (2007) is a Tamil forthcoming film directed by Love Today fame Balasekaran. The movie stars Madhavan, Bhavana, Prakash Raj, Vadivelu, Praveen Kumar, Tejashree and Devan. The film's music is composed by director, Mani Sharma. It is set to be released on April 14, 2007.
