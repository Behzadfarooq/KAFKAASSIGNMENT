As a native Italian speaker, I usually work at adding articles to the Italian Wikipedia. Sometimes they are original contributions, sometimes translations of English (or Spanish, or French) Wikipedia pages, sometimes a blend of both. So, often my contributions to en.wiki are interwikis to Italian pages ;-)

I sing in a show-choir. Our repertoire is Italian and foreign songs from 1940's and 1950's. Most of my contributions to Wikipedia are bios of singers, musicians, composers, songwriters, et al. from that era.

Some are missing from English Wikipedia. I plan to translate them sooner or later.

For a full list of my contributions, check my Italian userpage.
