Arya Pratinidhi Sabha of Fiji

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

You have new messages (last change).

The Arya Pratinidhi Sabha of Fiji is the national body for all the Arya Samajs in Fiji. At present there are 11 Arya Samajs affiliated to the Sabha. It was formed in 1918 and immediately went about establishing schools in with the first one established in the same year in Lautoka.

At present the Sabha owns and manages 14 pre-schools, 18 primary schools, 7 secondary schools, a commercial school, a religious training centre, a youth development centre and has provided the facilities and resources for the establishment of the University of Fiji.

At present its president is Kamlesh Kumar Arya.
