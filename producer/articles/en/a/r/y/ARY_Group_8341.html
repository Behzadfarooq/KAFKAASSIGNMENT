The ARY Group is a Dubai-based holding company founded by a Pakistani businessman, Haji Abdul Razzak Yaqoob (ARY). Haji Abdul Razzak is the Chairman of the Group. The ARY Group is into various sectors, which include:

  • Retail - Jewellery shops, Cash & Carry's
  • Residential - Estate Agencies in Dubai
  • ARY Television Network - ARY Digital, ARY One World, The Musik, QTV & ARY Sports, which are shown worldwide on six satellites.
  • Retail A Debit Card (Prepaid Card) called "Sahulat Card"
