[edit] About me

I currently attend high school in North Carolina, my favorite subjects being history and lit. For several years now, I have been completely addicted to Star Wars, Lord of the Rings, Harry Potter, and, more recently, Heroes. I am an avid reader, and in my spare time (I never have any because of an enormous load of homework), I love to contribute to Wikipedia and Wookieepedia, though under the name Sword of the sith. I am a strong Christian and proud of it.
