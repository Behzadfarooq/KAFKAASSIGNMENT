Arwut Lab (Secret Weapons) is the second studio album of Christina Aguilar - a very popular Thai artist. The album was released 2 years after her debut album. It break the role that the second album of any artist always unsuccess, this album was also successful like the first. It was well known unless only in Thailand, the album hit Japan. Music video Jing Mai Klua (Don't Mention the Truth) won MTV Asian Viewers Choice Award in 1992. That was the second time Thai artist did it. The album nearly reached 1,000,000 copies.
