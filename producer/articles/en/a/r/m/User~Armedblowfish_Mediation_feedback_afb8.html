Hi, if I recently mediated a dispute you were involved in, please consider leaving me feedback here so that I might become a better mediator. Note that this is completely optional. While you can include any feedback about me that you like, here are some suggested questions to get you started:

  • Did I offend you at any time?
  • At any time, did you feel that I was not being neutral?
  • Was there anything about the mediation you were particularly unsatisfied with?
  • Did you feel that I cared about your problem(s)?
  • Was there anything in particular you thought I handled well?

Things you should not place here:

  • Comments about other parties in the dispute, especially if the comments are of a negative nature.

Note that this is not a talk page, and I reserve the right to move, reorganize, and remove comments where I feel it is appropriate. Also, if you want a response, please say so, and expect me to reply on your talk page, not here.
