Armen Donelian

Armen Donelian is a jazz pianist who was a member of the Jazz fusion group Cosmology. He has performed since 1975 as a member of a group or as a solo act. He has also taught at conservatories in Copenhagen, Paris, Tbilisi, and other cities.

[edit] Partial discography

  • 2006 All or Nothing at All
  • 2005 Grand Ideas, Vol. 3: Full Moon Music
  • 2005 Full Moon Music: Grand Ideas, Vol. 3
  • 2003 Quartet Language
  • 2001 Mystic Heights (Grand Ideas, Vol. 2: Original Compositions for Piano)
  • 2000 Wave
  • 1990 The Wayfarer
  • 1988 Secrets
  • 1987 Trio '87
  • 1984 A Reverie
  • 1980 Stargazer
