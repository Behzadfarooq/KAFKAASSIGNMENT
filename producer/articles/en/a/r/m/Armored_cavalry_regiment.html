An armored cavalry regiment (ACR) is a regiment of the United States Army or United States National Guard organized for the specific purposes of reconnaissance, surveillance, and security. The regiment is equipped with armoured fighting vehicles; hence the terms armored and cavalry.

  • 11th Armored Cavalry Regiment
  • 278th Armored Cavalry Regiment
