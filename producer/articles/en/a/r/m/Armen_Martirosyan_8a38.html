Armen Martirosyan

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Not to be confused with Armen Martirossian.

Armen Martirosyan (born 6 August 1969) is a retired Armenian triple jumper, best known for placing fifth at the 1996 Summer Olympics and third at the 1996 European Indoor Championships. His personal best was 17.41 metres, achieved in June 1998 in Gyumri. This is the Armenian record.
