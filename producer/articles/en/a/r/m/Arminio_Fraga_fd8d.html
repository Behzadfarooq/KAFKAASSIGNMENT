Arminio Fraga

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Arminio Fraga was the former president of the Central Bank of Brazil, from 1999-2002. Also a former associate of George Soros and his Quantum Fund.

In 2003, he founded the Rio de Janeiro based investment company, Gávea Investimentos.
