Armando Efren Begines Avalos (born March 26, 1983 in Guadalajara, Jalisco) is a Mexican soccer defender.

Begines played for C.D. Chivas USA in Major League Soccer in 2005. Begines came to Chivas USA as one of imports from parent club Chivas de Guadalajara, and also played for their other child club, La Piedad. He has been a part of the Chivas system since 1999. Begines was released by Chivas USA in November 2005.
