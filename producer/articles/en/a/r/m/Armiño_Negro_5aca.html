Armiño negro (English language:Black Ermine) is a 1953 Argentine romantic film directed by Carlos Hugo Christensen and written by Rafael Maluenda and Pedro Juan Vignalle. The film premiered on [[]] 1953 in Buenos Aires.

  • Roberto Escalada

  • Aurelia Ferrer

  • Ricardo Galache
  • Laura Hidalgo
