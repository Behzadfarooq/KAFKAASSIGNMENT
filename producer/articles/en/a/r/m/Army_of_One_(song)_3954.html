Army Of One is a song by Cass Fox. Although Cass' second solo single release it was the first under the name Cass Fox. Previous releases had been released under Cass or Cassandra. It was released on 12th June 2006, one week before the re-release of Come Here. The single contained the re-recorded version from the second release of Come Here along with four other re-mixed versions.
