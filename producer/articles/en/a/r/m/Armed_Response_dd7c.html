"Armed Response" is the nineteenth episode in the first season of Murder, She Wrote. It first aired on March 31, 1985 on CBS. Guest stars include Eddie Bracken from The Miracle of Morgan's Creek, Martha Raye from Rhythm on the Range and Kay Lenz from Breezy.

[edit] Synopsis

It's a bitter pill for Jessica Fletcher to swallow when she is forced to stay at the hospital and a fellow patient is murdered.
