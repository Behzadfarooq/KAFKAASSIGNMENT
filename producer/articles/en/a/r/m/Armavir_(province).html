Armavir (Armenian: Արմավիրի մարզ) is a province (marz) of Armenia with the capital in Armavir. It is in the south-west of the country, located in the Ararat valley, between Mount Ararat and Mount Aragats, and shares a 45-mile border with Turkey to the south and west. The provinces also includes Echmiadzin, the center of the Armenian Apostolic Church and the historic city of Sardarapat.

Armavir borders the following marzer:

  • Aragatsotn - north
  • Ararat - southeast

To the northeast, between its boundaries with Aragatsotn and Ararat, Armavir also borders Yerevan.
