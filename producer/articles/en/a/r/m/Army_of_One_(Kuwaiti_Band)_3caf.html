The Army of One is a Hip-Hop/Rnb group from Kuwait. They are widely regarded as the most successful and talented Hip-Hop/Rnb group in the Middle East. In affiliation of the Kuwait-based ALIEN Records, they released their first album, Reprezentin, in 2005. Three of the band members (Kwe, DubG and Spyro) are rappers, the other three (ArabFace, QB and Daffy) are Rnb singers. Including worldwide production, including NG, and Fash (www.mrfash.com) from the UK.
