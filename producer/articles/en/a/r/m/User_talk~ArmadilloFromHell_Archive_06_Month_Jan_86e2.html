[edit] User:12.45.169.249

Thanks for the note about User:12.45.169.249. I have some concerns about the linking to analogstereo.com. Its not clear to me that the site has the right to make those manuals downloadable for free. The PDFs are all copyrighted and the links on the page to the terms of use just redirect to the home page of the site. The site doesn't appear to address the copyright status of the material it make available. If the material is a copyright infringement, then the site shouldn't be linked (see WP:EL). I think this is an interesting case that deserves wider discussion. I'd recommend posting about this site to Wikipedia talk:External links and see what the editors there think. Best, Gwernol 17:23, 1 January 2007 (UTC)

Thanks, the same thing about copyright occured to me also this morning. I'll post there. --ArmadilloFromHellGateBridge 17:25, 1 January 2007 (UTC)
