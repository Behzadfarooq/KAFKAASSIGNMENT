Armand Benneker (born June 25, 1969 in Tongeren) is a Dutch former footballer who retired in 2004. He played as a defender and is currently a coach with Austrian club RW-Rankweil.

[edit] Career

Benneker began his career with Dutch side MVV, before leaving in 1996 to join Scottish side Dundee United. Benneker only played seven league matches for United and was released in 1997, where he moved to Austria Lustenau. Further spells in Austria ensued with Bregenz, another with Austria Lustenau and his final playing days at Rheindorf Altach, where he retired in 2004.
