Arman David Tarzi is the current President of the American Council of Public Policy, a private left-of-center policy research and advocacy organization in Washington D.C.[1] He has served on various local and state campaigns, including the 2004 John Edwards Presidential Campaign.[2] In 2007, Tarzi started a national youth organization, known as Students for John Edwards, to assist Senator John Edwards bid for the White House in 2008.[3]
