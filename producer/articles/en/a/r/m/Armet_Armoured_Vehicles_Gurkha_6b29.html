Armet Armoured Vehicles Gurkha is an armoured military vehicle built by Florida based Armet Armoured Vehicles Incorporated. The 4 X 4 wheeled light armoured vehicle (LAV) and is based on the larger Armet Armoured Vehicles Balkan Mk3 armoured truck. An optional turret is available for both the Balkan Mk3 and the Gurkha.

[edit] Operators

The vehicle has been sold for used by the United States Army and the Canadian Forces.

The vehicle is also manufactured in Canada in Concord, Ontario.
