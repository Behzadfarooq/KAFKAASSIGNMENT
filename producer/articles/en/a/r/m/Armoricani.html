Armoricani

The Armoricani were a tribe in the area now called Brittany. They inhabited the area in the Iron Age, though there is plenty of evidence of earlier settlement. Strabo and Poseidonius describe the Armoricani as belonging to the Belgae. The Armorican's are of the type of peoples called Atlantic Celts. Being part of the great sailing community they knew of the lunar effect on the sea.
