Location of Armo in Italy

Armo is a comune (municipality) in the Province of Imperia in the Italian region Liguria, located about 90 km southwest of Genoa and about 25 km northwest of Imperia. As of 31 December 2004, it had a population of 120 and an area of 9.3 km².[1]

The municipality of Armo contains the frazione (subdivision) Trastanello.

Armo borders the following municipalities: Caprauna, Ormea, Pieve di Teco, and Pornassio.
