[edit] Game

  • Armageddon (MUD), a many-player text-based online game set in a fictional harsh desert world.
  • Armageddon (Warhammer 40,000), a world within the fictional Warhammer 40,000 universe and the three wars centred around this world.
  • Mortal Kombat: Armageddon, the latest game in the Mortal Kombat series, released in 2006.
  • Worms Armageddon, a video game from the Worms series.
  • Armageddon, is a tabletop roleplaying game published by Eden Studios, and is a possible future of Eden's WitchCraft line.
  • Armageddon, a videogame for Commodore 64.
  • Armageddon Man, The, another videogame for Commodore 64.
  • Many games involving magic have some kind of Armageddon spell, generally of highly destructive nature. Examples include Magic: The Gathering and Ultima VI.
