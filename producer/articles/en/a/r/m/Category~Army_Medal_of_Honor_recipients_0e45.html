This page contains recipients of the United States highest military honor — the Medal of Honor — who served in the United States Army.

  • Medal of Honor recipients who served in the United States Army Air Forces (USAAF) and the United States Army Air Corps (USAAC) in the years 1926-1941 are categorized in Category:Air Force Medal of Honor recipients, as these organizations are direct predecessors to the current U.S. Air Force.
