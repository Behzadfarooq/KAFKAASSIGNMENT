Armands Bērziņš (born 27 December 1983 in Riga, U.S.S.R.) is an ice hockey player. 1.92 m tall, weight - 90 kg (198 lb), Berzins plays as center. Never played in NHL, but drafted by Minnesota Wild in 2002 years draft as total number - 155. Now is playing in HC Vsetin (Czech extraleague).
