Armor Wars (Heroclix)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Armor Wars is the ninth Marvel HeroClix set produced by WizKids, released November 2005.

The set consists of the following characters made into figures:

  • Aurora
  • Banshee
  • Black King
  • Cannonball
  • Captain America: Steve Rogers
  • Captain America: John Walker
  • Crimson Dynamo
  • Crystal
  • Dazzler (Ultimate version)
  • Diamond Lil
  • Echo
  • Executioner
  • Ghost
  • Jean Grey - Phoenix
  • Firebrand
  • Iron Man
  • Iron Monger
  • Jocasta
  • Killer Shrike
  • Lorelei (Asgardian)
  • Magma
  • Magneto (from House of M)
  • Marrina
  • Mystique (from House of M)
  • Paladin
  • Psylocke
  • Quicksilver
  • Sentry
  • Shaman
  • Shathra
  • Spider-Man (from House of M)
  • Spider-Woman
  • Spymaster
  • Sunfire
  • Thunderball
  • Thunderbird
  • Titanium Man
  • Ultron-5
  • Ultron-11
  • Ultron-16
  • Ultron-13
  • War Machine
  • Warpath
  • Wendigo
  • Wolverine (from House of M)

[edit] Bystanders

Armor Wars has the following characters as Bystander tokens:

  • Pepper Potts
  • Happy Hogan
  • Justin Hammer
  • H.E.R.B.I.E.
  • Doug Ramsey
  • Wyatt Wingfoot
  • Tony Stark L.M.D.

[edit] Feats

Armor Wars has the following Feats in the set:

  • Alpha Flight - Alt Team
  • Armor Piercing
  • Extended Range
  • Homing Device
  • Life Model Decoy
  • Nanobots
  • Protected
  • Repulsor Shield
  • Saboteur
  • Shellhead

[edit] Battle Field Conditions

Armor Wars has the following Battle Field Conditions in the set:

  • Armor Wars
  • Assembled
  • Inertial Interference Field
  • Isolation
