Armbrae Academy

Armbrae Academy is an independent, university preparatory, co-educational, non-denominational day school from Preschool to Grade 12, accredited by the Canadian Educational Standards Institute and a member of the Canadian Association of Independent Schools. It is located in Halifax, Nova Scotia Canada.

[edit] Facilities

Armbrae Academy has a computer lab full of 18 macs with 20 additional iBooks. The large grounds of the school incorporate a large forest, playground, and small field. Armbrae rents out King's Gym for the PE classes, and uses the church down the street for presentations that cannot fit in the school.
