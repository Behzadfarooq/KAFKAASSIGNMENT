[edit] Loricariidae

Armoured catfish are not Loricariidae, but Loricariidae are armoured catfish. There are several types of armourd catfish including Corydoras (Callichthyid armored catfishes), Loricariidae (Suckermouth Armord catfish) and others.

We're just following FishBase here. If they change, we'll change. It's worth cross-referencing other taxa that may also be called "armored catfish". Stan 19:10, 15 March 2006 (UTC)
Above statement is right - this family is rarely called - the armoured catfish - it's more likely to refer to corydoras or Callichthys callichthys - I suggest changing the title to 'Armoured suckermouth catfish' which is free from confusion. Then 'armoured catfish' could be changed to a disambiguation page.HappyVR 20:51, 27 May 2006 (UTC)
