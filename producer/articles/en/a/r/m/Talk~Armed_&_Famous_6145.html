I'm the one that put that stuff about them using locations around town and saying they're something else. The "Training Facility" is my High School, Muncie Central. I know this because I've been in our wrestling room and the entrance they showed is the door I go in every day. - --68.51.88.109 23:04, 11 January 2007 (UTC)

And this can be verified...how? I'm not in Muncie, but I can say that I am as easily as you can. This is supposed to be an encyclopedia. Encyclopedias contain independently verifiable facts. If what you say is factual, give a reference that people can check out without having to accept the word of a stranger.

That's just something you'll have to trust me on. My school is Muncie Central High School, and I'm certain that's Central. That is Central, I'd swear on a stack of Bibles. --68.51.88.109 23:56, 22 January 2007 (UTC)
