[edit] History of this article

Note to later editors: This article has a somewhat complicated page history. There were originally two separate articles, at Armatoles and at Armatoloi. The first was written mainly by User:Dragases ([1]), the second mainly by User:Deucalionite ([2]), both starting from January 2006. After some rather chaotic merging and cut-and-paste moving in June 2006, the histories of both articles were merged and the whole thing moved to Armatoloi.
