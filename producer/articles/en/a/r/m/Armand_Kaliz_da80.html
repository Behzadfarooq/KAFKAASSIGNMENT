Armand Kaliz

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Armand Kaliz (23 October 1887 Paris, France - 1 February 1941 in Beverly Hills) was a French born American film actor of the silent film and early sound period of the 1930s who began his career in vaudeville. He appeared in films such as The Temptress in 1926 with actresses such as Greta Garbo, making some 82 film appearances between 1917 and 1941.

However after 1933 the majority of his small roles in films went uncredited and he died on 1 February 1941 from a heart attack in Beverly Hlls.
