Armenian diplomatic missions

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Armenian Embassy in Washington DC
Armenian Embassy in Ottawa

Armenia is a landlocked country located in the Southern Caucasus. Over 5 million Armenians reside abroad, with large communities located in Russia, France, Iran, United States, Georgia, Syria, Lebanon, Argentina, and Ukraine. Listed below are Armenian embassies, and consulates general, excluding honorary consulates.
