General Characteristics
Displacement: 14,245 tonnes
Length: 441 ft 6 in (129.81 m) overall, 417 ft 8.75 in (122.82 m) between perpendiculars, 427 ft (125.52 m) waterline
Beam: 57 ft (16.76 m)
Draft: 27 ft 8 7/8 in (8.16 m)
Propulsion: 2500 hp
Speed: 11 knots
Cargo Capacity: 10,674 tonnes dead, 7,219 tonnes gross, 272,978 ft3 (6,937 m3, 64,826 barrels)
Complement: 81 officers and men
Armament: typically one five-inch gun, one three-inch gun, eight 20mm cannon

The Armadillo class of tankers were those Type Z-ET1-S-C3 Liberty tankers that were commissioned into the United States Navy. They were given the hull classification symbols of unclassified miscellaneous vessels.
