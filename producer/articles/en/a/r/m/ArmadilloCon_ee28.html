ArmadilloCon is a science fiction convention held annually in Austin, Texas, USA. It is sponsored by the Fandom Association of Central Texas. ArmadilloCon is known for its emphasis on literary science fiction.

[edit] Traditions of ArmadilloCon

  1. Emphasis on literary sf
  2. Guests of Honor are up-and-coming writers and are frequently first-time GOHs
  3. Program item "Fannish Feud" has been held regularly since ArmadilloCon 4 in 1983. Frequent hosts of this event have been Pat Cadigan and Walter Jon Williams.
  4. Willie Siros has been a dealer every year, either as part of Two Guys from Texas or Adventures in Crime & Space
