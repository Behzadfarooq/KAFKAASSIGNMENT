"Arms Around Me" is a song from The Departure's debut album, Dirty Words. It was released as the third single from that album on 17 October 2005, on a limited edition 7" green vinyl that includes a poster and sticker. The single was also deleted on the day of release.

[edit] Track listing

  1. "Arms Around Me" - 4:21
  2. "Arms Around Me" (acoustic, recorded live for Gideon Coe's show on BBC6 Music)
