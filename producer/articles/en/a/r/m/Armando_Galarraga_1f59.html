Armando Antonio Galarraga (born January 15, 1982 in Caracas, Venezuela) is a pitcher in the Texas Rangers minor league system.

Galarraga originally signed as a free agent with the Montreal Expos on October 31, 1998. He was acquired by the Rangers from the Washington Nationals as part of the deal that sent Alfonso Soriano to the Nationals in 2005. He has yet to make a Major League appearance.
