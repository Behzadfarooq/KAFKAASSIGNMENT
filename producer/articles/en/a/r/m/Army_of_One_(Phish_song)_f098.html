Army of One (Phish song)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Army of One is a Phish song written by Page McConnell. It was released as the fifth song on the 2004 album Undermind.

Army of One is the first Page-written song with lyrics. It made its debut in 2003, a few months before it was recorded for the album.
