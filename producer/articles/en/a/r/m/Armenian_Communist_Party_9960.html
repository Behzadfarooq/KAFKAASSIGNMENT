The Armenian Communist Party (Armenian: Հայաստանի կոմունիստական կուսակցության, abbreviated ՀԿԿ, Hayastani Komunistakan Kusaktsutyun) is a communist political party in Armenia.

HKK is the main communist party in Armenia and it has 18.000 members[1].

The party is led by Ruben Tovmasyan.

HKK publishes Hayastani Komunist and Pravda Armenii.

At the last elections, 25 May 2003, the party won 2.11 % of popular votes and none of 131 seats.
