The following are common phrases, proverbs and sayings within the Armenian community. Some of these may be as old as 2,000 years, some older. Many Armenian phrases make great use of analogies.

[edit] Աա

Աչքիս վրայ:.
achkees vuhrah.
Literally: "On my eye."
Meaning: "Absolutely!"


[edit] Գգ

Գլուխըս մի արդուկէր:.
kuhlookhuhs mee artooger.
Literally: "Don't iron my head."
Meaning: "Don't bother me with that."


[edit] Մմ

Մուխըս մարեցաւ:.
mookhuhs maretsav.
Literally: "My smoke has gone out."
Meaning: "I am exhausted."
