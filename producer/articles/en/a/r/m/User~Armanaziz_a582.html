[edit] Who I am

My full name is Arman Aziz. I was born in 1980 in Bangladesh. Currently I'm working for a major multinational bank in Hong Kong as the Project Manager for implementing Basel II in Asia-Pacific region. I completed my BBA and MBA from Institute of Business Administration, University of Dhaka. I passed CFA Level I examination in 2006.
