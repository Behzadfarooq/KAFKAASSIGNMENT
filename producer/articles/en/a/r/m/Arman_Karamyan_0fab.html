Arman Karamyan (born 14 November 1979) is an Armenian football striker.

He is a member of the national team, and has played 34 matches and scored 5 goals since his debut in 2000 [1].

He is currently awaiting to clarify his situation with Ceahlăul Piatra-Neamţ, in order to join his twin brother, Artavazd Karamyan, at FCU Politehnica Timişoara (Romania).
