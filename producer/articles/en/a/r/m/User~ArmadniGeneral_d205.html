My real name is Alex, or Alexander if you please. I'm sixteen years old and a resident of Logan Township, New Jersey. I attend the Kingsway Regional School District.

My primary areas of study cover computers, politics and government, law, and history. However, these interests are not often reflected in my contributions, as what draws me to Wikipedia is mostly administrative work and small tweaks.

Work on Wikipedia

My work on Wikipedia seems to be split between administrative tasks and minor tweaks. Though I am not currently a Wikipedia Administrator, it is a position I would like to hold when I gain the requisite experience.

I use VandalFighter to actively patrol the most recent edits to Wikipedia. I often revert vandalism, submit for deletion such pages that meet deletion criteria, and welcome new users to the project. On the note of deletion, I do not often vote at WP:AfD, but I stay up-to-date and offer opinions on controversial deletions.
