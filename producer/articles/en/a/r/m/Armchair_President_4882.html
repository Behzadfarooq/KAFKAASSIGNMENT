Armchair President is a podcast by Patrick Fogarty. Patrick Fogarty is currently running for the 2008 presidential elections of the United States of America. He tries to gather support for his nomination through this podcast. In this podcast Patrick Fogarty presents solutions to a great variety of problems that the United States of America is currently facing. Patrick Fogarty is the husband of Mignon Fogarty, the creator of Grammar Girl's Quick and Dirty Tips for Better Writing podcast.
