Armageddon (convention)

Armageddon is a twice annual science fiction and comics convention held in Wellington and Auckland, New Zealand. In recent years it has also showcased gaming, animation, and consumer electronics.

[edit] History

Armageddon was first held in Auckland, New Zealand on the weekend of 2 December 1995 at the Avondale Raceway. In 1997 the convention first came to Wellington, and will hold its first Christchurch event in 2007.

[edit] Venues and Guest appearances

  • 1995 (December 2-3) - Avondale Raceway, Auckland
  • 1996 (August 25) - Freemans Bay Community Centre, Auckland
  • 1997 (March 9) - Freemans Bay Community Centre, Auckland
  • 1997 (August 17) - Freemans Bay Community Centre, Auckland
  • 1997 (August 24) - Victoria University, Wellington
  • 1998 (March 21-22) - Alexander Raceway, Auckland
    • Kurt Busiek, Mark Waid, Devin Grayson (comics)
  • 1998 (March 28-29) - Wellington Raceway, Wellington
    • Kurt Busiek, Mark Waid, Devin Grayson (comics)
  • 1998 (October 3-4) - Aotea Centre, Auckland
    • Joe Kelly, Steven Seagle, Chris Bachalo (comics)
  • 1999 (May 29-30) - Aotea Centre, Auckland
    • Claudia Christian (Babylon 5), Chase Masterson (Star Trek: Deep Space 9), Warren Ellis, Grant Morrison (comics)
  • 1999 (June 5-6) - Melbourne Expo Centre, Melbourne, Australia
    • Claudia Christian (Babylon 5), Chase Masterson (Star Trek: Deep Space 9), Warren Ellis, Grant Morrison (comics), Frazier Hines (Doctor Who)
  • 2000 (March 18-19) - Aotea Centre, Auckland
    • Jeremy Bulloch (Star Wars), Lani Tupu (Farscape), Art Adams, Joyce Chin, Jeph Loeb (Comics)
  • 2001 (February 10-11) - Aotea Centre, Auckland
  • 2001 (September 15-16) - Michael Fowler Centre, Wellington
  • 2002 (April 12-14) - Aotea Centre, Auckland
  • 2002 (November 23-24) - Michael Fowler Centre, Wellington
  • 2003 (April 12-14) - Aotea Centre, Auckland
  • 2003 (September 20-22) - Michael Fowler Centre, Wellington
  • 2004 (April 16-18) - Aotea Centre, Auckland
  • 2004 (September 25-26) - Queens Wharf Events Centre, Wellington
    • John Rhys-Davies, Sean Astin, Andy Serkis (Lord of the Rings)
  • 2005 (April 16-17) - Queens Wharf Events Centre, Wellington
  • 2005 (October 22-24) - Aotea Centre, Auckland
    • 10th Anniversary Event
  • 2006 (April 29-30) - Queens Wharf Events Centre, Wellington
    • Rooster Teeth Productions (Makers of Red vs Blue)
  • 2006 (October 21-23) - Aotea Centre, Auckland

Future events:

  • 2007 (April 14-15) - Christchurch Convention Centre, Christchurch
  • 2007 (April 21-22) - TSB Arena (formerly Queens Wharf Events Centre) - Wellington
  • 2007 (October 20-22) - Aotea Centre, Auckland
