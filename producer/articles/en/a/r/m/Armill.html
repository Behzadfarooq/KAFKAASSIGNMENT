Armills, the so-called bracelets of wisdom and sincerity, are some of the many items of which are included in a regalia. Armrills have been used in many coronations. The oldest armills in the British Royal Ragalia are those of King Charles II, the newest belonging to Her Majesty, Queen Elizabeth II.
