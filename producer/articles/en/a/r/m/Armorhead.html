The Armorheads are a small family, Pentacerotidae, of fishes in the order Perciformes. They are native to the Indo-Pacific and southwestern Atlantic.

Their name, from Greek pente meaning "five" and keras meaning "horn", refers to the prominent sharp spines in their dorsal fin (though these do not number five in all species).

  • Genus Paristiopterus Bleeker, 1876

  • Genus Pseudopentaceros Bleeker, 1876
