Armel Tchakounte (born December 12, 1978) is an Cameroonian professional footballer who plays for QPR. He previously played for Hong Kong clubs Rangers and Kitchee and non league Carshalton Athletic in the 2005/06 season.

Armel has yet to appear in a competitive match for Queens Park Rangers.
