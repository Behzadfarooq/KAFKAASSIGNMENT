[edit] License tagging for Image:Artist George O. Hughes AKA Kwesi Afedzi.JPG

Thanks for uploading Image:Artist George O. Hughes AKA Kwesi Afedzi.JPG. Wikipedia gets thousands of images uploaded every day, and in order to verify that the images can be legally used on Wikipedia, the source and copyright status must be indicated. Images need to have an image tag applied to the image description page indicating the copyright status of the image. This uniform and easy-to-understand method of indicating the license status allows potential re-users of the images to know what they are allowed to do with the images.
