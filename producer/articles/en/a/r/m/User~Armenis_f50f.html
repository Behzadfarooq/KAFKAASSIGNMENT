Aren Armenagian (born August 02, 1990) is a famous and ambitious Armenian student who currently lives in Limassol, Cyprus.


[edit] Early Life

Aren was born in Limassol, Cyprus, to Armenian Cypriot parents Nazaret Armenagian, who is a doctor and Hera Kazandjian, who is a saleswoman. He has a youger sister, Claudia (born January 07 1995) He grew up in Limassol, away from his homecountry Armenia.


[edit] Personal Life

Aren, also known as "Armenis" graduated from the Armenian Junior School of Narek at the age of 11, and soon visited and still visits the Private English Grammar School where he has made an immediate impact. He is literally loved by everyone and his marks are absolutely fantastic.

Aren is a great fan of football. He is a supporter of APOEL Nicosia, Panathinaikos FC, and Manchester United FC. During his free time, he loves playing football, going out with friends or just playing on the computer.

[edit] Ambitions/Dreams

Armenagian dreams that one day he will become an Armenian Onasis. An Onasis who will have a great positive influence to the world. His greatest ambition is to open a navigation company (W.L.S Navigation Company) with a close friend of his, Takis Petevis. Aren wants to study shipping architecture and engineering, whereas Takis dreams himself as a shipping businessman. Both of them dream that one day they will become the owners of the biggest and most successfull multinational naval organisation in the world.
