Armagh (2006 Population 1,613) is a small village in the Bellechasse Regional County Municipality, Quebec, part of the Chaudière-Appalaches administrative region. Its coordinates are 46° 45' N, 70° 35' W.

[edit] Demographics

* Land area: 846.79 km²
