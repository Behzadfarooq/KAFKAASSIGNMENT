[edit] Mission

Strategic deployment of combat-ready air and missile defense units to theatre commands. One command is assigned per theater army.

[edit] Organization and equipment

[edit] Overall

  • Personnel
    • 65 officers
    • 6 warrant officers
    • 73 non-commissioned officers
    • 37 enlisted personnel
    • 181 Total

[edit] Command section

The Command Section consists of the unit commander, his Command Sergeant Major, and his assistants. A single truck is provided for personal transportation of the commander.

  • Personnel (2 Officers,
    • Major General: Commanding General
    • E9: Command Sergeant Major
    • Captain: Aide-de-Camp
    • E6: Executive Admin Assistant
    • E6: Chauffeur
  • Equipment
    • 1 HMMWV

[edit] Chief of Staff section

The Chief of Staff coordinates all staff for the Commander. A single truck is provided for personal transportation.

  • Personnel
    • Colonel: Chief of Staff
    • Major: Secretary General Staff
    • E4: Executive Admin Assistant
  • Equipment
    • 1 HMMWV

[edit] Administration/Logistics section

Admin/Log provides for administrative services, logistics, supply, and transportation for the Command. Two trucks are provided for personal transportation of the Assistant Chiefs of Staff.

  • Personnel
    • Colonel: Assistant Chief of Staff G1 (Administration)
    • Colonel: Assistant Chief of Staff G4 (Logistics)
    • Lt. Colonel: Deputy G1 (Administration)
    • Lt. Colonel: Deputy G4 (Logistics)
    • Major: Plans Officer (Administration)
    • Major: Plans Officer (Logistics)
    • Major: Security/Plans/Operations Officer (Logistics)
    • E9: Chief Personnel Services Sergeant
    • E9: Senior Logistical Services Supervisor
    • E8: Chief Administrative Supervisor
    • E4: Executive Admin Assistant
    • E4: Administrative Specialist
    • E3: Administrative Clerk
  • Equipment
    • 2 HMMWV

[edit] Headquarters Commandant

Provides for coordination of command elements. A single vehicle is provided for transportation.

  • Personnel
    • Major: Headquarters Commandant
    • E8: Operations Sergeant
    • E3: Vehicle Driver
  • Equipment
    • 1 x HMMWV

[edit] Communications/Electronics section

Provides technical expertise to facilitate brigade communications. A single vehicle is provided for the personal transportation of the Communications/Electronics Officer and a second is provided for transportation of personnel and equipment.

  • Personnel
    • Lt. Colonel: Communications/Electronics Officer
    • Major: Assistant Communications/Electronics Officer
    • E9: Chief Signal NCO
    • E8: Information Systems Chief
    • E7: Information Systems Staff NCO
    • E7: Technical Intelligence Analyst
    • E6: Technical Intelligence Analyst
  • Equipment
    • 2 x HMMWV
    • 1 x M101A1 Cargo Trailer
