[edit] Summary

Coat of Arms of Oldham Metropolitan Borough Council. Protected by Crown Copyright, and used with permission. Terms of copyright on the site preclude the Seal from being considered a "free licence" Crown Copyright image. As such, a fair use rationale has been added to retain it on the English-language Wikipedia.

[edit] Rationale

  1. As an official symbol of the Borough, the crest adds significantly to the article on the subject.
  2. No free or public domain images have been located describing the subject of the article.
  3. Due to the nature of the image, it is highly unlikely that any "free licence" alternatives will be found.
