Armageddon is the 11th novel in the Left Behind series by Tim LaHaye and Jerry Jenkins]]. It was first published in 2002.

[edit] Plot summary

Spoiler warning: Plot and/or ending details follow.

In the final days of the Tribulation, Nicolae Carpathia prepares to launch the greatest military conflict the world has ever seen, against none other than Jesus Christ himself. The Tribulation Force manages to leave the now compromised safe house and the Chigaco area just in time before Antichrist orders what's left of Chicago to be obliterated by a nuclear blast. The main Trib Force members find a new safe house with George Sebastian in San Diego. Chang must escape New Babylon before it is destroyed. Chloe falls into the hands of the GC. The sixth Bowl Judgement hits and the Euphrates river dries up to open the way for the East's mighty armies to come and join the forces of the Beast. Tsion, along with Buck, decides to go to Jerusalem and join the fight. Rayford and the others find themselves in the middle of this greatest conflict as the Tribulation Force suffers its greatest casualties. The world is on the brink of total destruction as the tribulation nears its end. Only the imminent return of Jesus Christ can save those who survive. Of the original Trib Force members, only one is alive at the end... but for how long?
