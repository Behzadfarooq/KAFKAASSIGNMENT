Armando Crisóstomo

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Armando Crisóstomo (born 1908) is a silent film Filipino actor who made his film debut in the 1925 film Ang Pagtitipid.

Crisóstomo also known as Armando Cris in his early movie career as silent film star and he also played character roles in numerous films.
