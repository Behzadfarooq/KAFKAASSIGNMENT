Aračinovo (Macedonian: Арачиново; Albanian: Haraçinës) is a municipality in the northern Republic of Macedonia. Aračinovo is also the name of the village where the municipal seat is found.

[edit] Geography

The municipality borders Lipkovo municipality in the north, City of Skopje in the west, Petrovec municipality in the south and Kumanovo municipality in the east.

[edit] Demographics

According to the last census in 2002, [1] the municipality have 11,992 inhabitants, and in the census of 1994 it had 9,960 inhabitants.

The ethnic structure of the municipality is the following: 90.72% are Albanians, 8.23% are Macedonians, 0.54% are Bosniaks, 0.11% are Serbs, 0.01% are Vlachs and 0.39% other.

[edit] Inhabited places

The municipality is rural, meaning that all the inhabited places within it are villages. The number of the inhabited places in Aračinovo Municipality is 4.
