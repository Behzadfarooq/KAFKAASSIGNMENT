[edit] Trivia

  • Aran's highest Scrabble score in a single word in a single turn is 1026. The word is "xylographically" on Row O Column 1 (O1). "x", "p" and the 2nd "y" was placed on the triple word score and "o" and the 2nd "a" was placed on the double letter score. The game was started with two players, each with 20 tiles at first. No cheating was involved.
  • Aran have two major accident so far. The first major accident is a big cut on the right leg when he was Primary Six. Aran was playing Catch during recess when he accidentally trip over something and he fell onto the ground, leaving a huge, oval-shape cut (and he can see the bone!). The wound closed up a week later. The second major accident is a deep cut on the left thumb when he was Secondary Two. It was the first practical lesson of Design & Technology and Aran need to saw the last piece of wood into two. He arm was already tired by then. Suddenly, he lose control of the tenon saw and accidentally cut his left thumb, causing a deep cut. The wound closed up two weeks later, but the "scratch mark" left on the fingernail remains.
  • Aran was stung by a black-coloured bee twice.
