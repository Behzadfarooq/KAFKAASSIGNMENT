Arab Nations Cup 2009

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

You have new messages (last change).

The Arab Nations Cup 2009 is the 9th staging of the Arab Nations Cup, an association football tournament held between Arab countries. The qualifying rounds of the tournament began in December 2006.

[edit] Second Qualifying Round

Teams that enter in this round (plus the three teams from the previous round):

  • Algeria
  • Iraq
  • Egypt
  • Oman
  • Kuwait
  • Saudi Arabia
  • Syria
  • Tunisia
  • Bahrain
  • Morocco
  • Lebanon
  • United Arab Emirates
  • Jordan

The teams are divided into 4 groups of 4 teams from which the group winners and runners-up qualify for the finals in 2009.
