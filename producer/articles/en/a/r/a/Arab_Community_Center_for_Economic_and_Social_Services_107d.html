The Arab Community Center for Economic and Social Services (ACCESS) is an organization formed to support the needs of the Arab American community. A national group, the headquarters is located in Dearborn, Michigan. Founded in 1970, the group built the Arab American National Museum, the first of its kind in the United States, which opened May 5, 2005.
