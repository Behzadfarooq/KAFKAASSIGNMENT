a few comments regarding the translation of a few terms related to this topic:

Tribe is actually "qabila", while "Ashira" fits into the "clan" category as it's smaller and can be composed of several families. "Fukkdh" is larger than a clan and sometimes can be as large as a tribe. Usually, a tribe is composed of several "fukhdhs" along with a "batn" (literally means stomach) which is the center "fukhdh" of the tribe and where the tribe chieftens are from.

federation of tribes is called "sh'ab" in Arabic if the tribes are related or "hulf" if they are not related.

Mashallah it looks like 90% of Iraqis are descendents of the Prophet.
