Ok, it's probably good for an RfC or an RfAr then. You might consider going ahead and requesting arbitration to see if the arbcom will be willing to hear the case at this stage in the game; they do not make rulings about content disputes, but they are able to pass stricter measures to keep users from being disruptive.
