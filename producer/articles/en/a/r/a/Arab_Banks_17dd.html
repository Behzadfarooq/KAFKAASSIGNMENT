Arab Banks

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

You have new messages (last change).

The Modern System of Arab Banks has started in Egypt in the Late 19th Century, with the campaign of Modernizing the Country, Today Arab banks are among the Pioneering in the Developing Countries, and some are competitors to Big Major International Banks. A new System of Banking has also been introduced to the World by the Arab World, to suit its Islamic Laws about certain terms of Banking, thus Islamic Banks were Created.
