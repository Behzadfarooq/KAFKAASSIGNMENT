[edit] Shortening The Amazing Race

Hi. If you have been watching Talk:The Amazing Race, I have come up with an alternative proposal to yours regarding the split up of The Amazing Race. With all due respect, I propose to implement my plan in about a week (by Wednesday, March 14) instead of yours, if there are no serious objections by any users. I thought your proposal was good, but I wanted to split The Amazing Race in a way such that anything split would survive any Wikipedia:Articles for deletion attempt. Please see Wikipedia:Fancruft. I don't want to see any hard work be wasted if an AfD succeeds of any split article, as I feel it might with your proposal. I hope you will support my plan and I hope there will be no hard feelings if I am managing the split and you are not. Tinlinkin 06:51, 6 March 2007 (UTC)

Thank you so much for your support and your comments. I have just one comment: I thought for a moment that The Amazing Race Singapore was a real book based on The Amazing Race. I guess it's your own work that you are posting on your user space. Tinlinkin 18:52, 8 March 2007 (UTC)
