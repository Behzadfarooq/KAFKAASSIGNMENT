Aradan is a fictional character from J.R.R. Tolkien's Middle-earth universe.

Spoiler warning: Plot and/or ending details follow.

He was one of the Edain, the son of Marach. His birth name was Malach. He joined in his people's journey to Estolad, but left Estolad for Hithlum, where he spent fourteen years in the service of Fingolfin and received his Sindarin name (Aradan). He may have been the ruler of those of the house of Marach who travelled to Hithlum.
