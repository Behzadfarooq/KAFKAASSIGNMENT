Arabian Nights (disambiguation)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Arabian Nights may refer to:

  • Arabian Nights, also known as The Book of One Thousand and One Nights, a medieval Middle-Eastern literary epic which tells the story of Scheherazade
  • Arabian Nights (TV miniseries), a 2000 television miniseries adaptation of The Book of One Thousand and One Nights
  • Arabian Nights, a 1974 Italian film also know as Il fiore delle mille e una notte
  • Arabian Nights (1942 film), a 1942 Technicolor film starring Jon Hall and Maria Montez
  • One Thousand and One Arabian Nights (film), a 1969 Japanese animated film.
  • Arabian Nights (video game), a platform-jumping computer game for the C64
  • Arabian Nights (Beck), a song by Beck from his 1999 album Midnite Vultures
  • Arabian Nights (Stormwitch), a song by Stormwitch from his 1985 album Tales Of Terror (Stormwitch)
  • Arabian Nights (Aladdin), the main song from the 1992 animated movie Aladdin
  • Arabian Nights (Magic: The Gathering), an expansion to the Magic: The Gathering collectible card game
  • The New Arabian Nights, a collection of short stories by Robert Louis Stevenson first published between 1877 and 1880
  • Scooby-Doo in Arabian Nights, an animated telefilm based on The Book of One Thousand and One Nights
  • Arabian Nights (And Days), the seventh trade paperback collection of the comic book series Fables
  • The Arabian Nights (play), a play by Mary Zimmerman
  • 1001 Arabian Nights, a 1959 animated movie starring Mr. Magoo
  • The opening number from Disney's Aladdin
