Arashiyama Station (嵐山駅) is a railway station in Kyoto, Japan. It is the terminal station of the Hankyu Arashiyama Line. The station is a short walk from Nakanoshima Park and Togetsukyo Bridge. In spring, Sakura trees lining the station light up in an array of white and pink.
