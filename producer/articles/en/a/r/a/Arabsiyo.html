Arabsiyo is a city in Somaliland, located about 30 km west of the capital Hargeisa. Arabsiyo is known for its major farming area. It is also a resort town popular for special occasions such as weddings and Eid celebrations. Arabsiyo has an estimated population of 15,000, and is growing rapidly.
