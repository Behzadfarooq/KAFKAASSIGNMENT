[edit] Necessity

Unfortunately, the school needs and uses all three gyms, plus the free-weight rooms and pool, almost every period of every day. While it would be nice to see the money go somewhere else, Arapahoe is a balanced-enough school to allow this kind of spending. The interior of the new addition is very functional and serves Arapahoe well.
