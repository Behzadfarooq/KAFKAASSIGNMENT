Aranyer Din Ratri (Days and Nights in the Forest) is a film released in 1970 and directed by Satyajit Ray. It is based upon the novel by Sunil Gangopadhyay.

[edit] Plot

Spoiler warning: Plot and/or ending details follow.

Four urban businessmen leave the city in order to take a vacation in the wilderness. This experience in the forest ultimately forces them to face elements of their personalities which are suppressed in the city.
