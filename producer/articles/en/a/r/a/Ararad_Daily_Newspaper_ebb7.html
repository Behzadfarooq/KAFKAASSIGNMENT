The Ararad Daily Newspaper (Armenian: Արարատ Օրաթերթ) is the official newspaper of the Social Democrat Hunchakian Party (Henchak) in Lebanon. It is published every Friday morning in colour. The present Editor in Chief is Aharon Shekherdemian.

The Ararad Daily Newspaper started publication on 12 November 1937 in Beirut, Lebanon. It used to be published daily until 2000, but since June 2001, it has been published weekly.
