Reading

I mostly read sci-fi/fantasy.

Series I've read (hardly complete and in no particular order):

  • Anne McCaffrey's Dragonriders of Pern series
  • R.A. Salvatore's Drizzt series
  • R.A. Salvatore's Cleric Quintet series
  • Elaine Cunningham's Counselors and Kings and Starlight and Shadows series
  • The three Halo novels (Halo: The Fall of Reach, Halo: The Flood, Halo: First Strike)
  • Almost all of Michael Crichton's novels
  • The first three Warcraft novels (Day of the Dragon, Lord of the Clans, and The Last Guardian)
  • Richard A. Knaak's Warcraft: War of the Ancients Trilogy
  • Almost all of the original six Dune novels (still need to read Children of Dune)
  • The War of the Spider Queen series (quite good, but ending was a bit anticlimactic, methinks).
  • A good portion of Isaac Asimov's works

I've also read quite a few other books that aren't on the list simply because 1) I can't remember them or 2) they don't belong in a series.

By far, my favorite book is Dune. Second would be Catch-22. Oh, and my favorite short story is Asimov's The Last Question
