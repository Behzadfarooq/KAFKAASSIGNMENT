Araya-Shinmachi Station

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Araya-Sinmachi Station (荒屋新町駅, Araya-Sinmachi Eki?) is a railway station on the JR East Hanawa Line. It is located in Hachimantai, Iwate, Japan.

[edit] Station Structure

  • 2-way, 3-track, above-ground station.
Platforms
1 ■Hanawa Line For.Ōbuke and Morioka
2 ■Hanawa Line For.Kazuno-Hanawa and Ōdate
3 ■Hanawa Line (siding)
  • Araya-Sinhachi Station is a simple consignment station, administered by Ōbuke Station, and operated by Co., Ltd. Ordinary tickets, express tickets, and reserved-seat tickets for all JR lines are on sale.
    • Business hours: 8:00 a.m. - 6:00 p.m.
