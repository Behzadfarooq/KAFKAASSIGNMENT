Aram Hakobyan

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Aram Hakobyan (born August 15, 1979 in Yerevan) is an Armenian forward, currently plays for Urkainian club FC Illychivets Mariupol. Aram was a top scorer of Armenian Premier League (APL) of 2006 season with 25 goals and voted as a best player of APL of 2005 season. He is also a member of Armenian national team, got 14 caps and 2 goals since his debut in 2000.
