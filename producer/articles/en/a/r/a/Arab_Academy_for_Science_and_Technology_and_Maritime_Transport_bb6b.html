The Arab Academy for Science & Technology and Maritime Transport (also known as AASTMT or AAST) is a maritime academy that has its main campus located in Alexandria, Egypt. Other campuses are located in Cairo, Syria, Bahrain, Qatar and the United Arab Emirates. It is an Arab League specialized organization.
