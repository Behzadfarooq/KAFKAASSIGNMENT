[edit] Sidenote

I know this isn't a tech support page, but I thought the following would be useful to some ArabTeX users: sometimes the combining ^ and _ characters has no effect. Thus you can't produce a sheen (^s). To correct this, put the following in the preamble after \usepackage{arabtex}:

\catcode`^=7
\catcode`_=8
