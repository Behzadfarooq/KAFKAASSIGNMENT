[edit] Why was this Trivia info removed?

Trivia:

  • In Araña the third iusse, Amun is on the Internet looking for more information about Anya:
    • The name on her birth certificate is Aña Sofia Conazon
    • Her eighth grade final quarter grades: English: A+, Math: B-, History: B+, Science: A-, Shop: A+, Gym:A.
    • Born on 17 June 1989.
    • At Born: weighing 7 lbs 3 ozs.
    • At Born: measuring 19 inches in length.
    • Her birth certificate was signed by Dr Enrique Simoneson.
  • Gil Corazon was born on 24 October 1964, got his MS in Journalism from Columbia University and has previously lived in Puerto Rico and Mexico. His full Name is Gillberto Conazon. Address is 247 Lafeyette Ave. Brookyn_NY 11217. SS# 458-68-6572.

--Brown Shoes22 16:17, 10 November 2005 (UTC)

I didn't remove it, but I can understand why someone did. None of it, except maybe the year she was born and possibly her father's background, is important to an encyclopediac entry. It's not useful information in any sense of the word. Pitr 19:35, 10 November 2005 (UTC)
Well, its what we call fancruft. Jack Cain 00:07, 9 February 2006 (UTC)
It's also ungrammatical and frequently poorly spelled. Some of it (birth info) is of such minor importance (and liable to be retconned as needed) as to be irrelevant. The facts of passing importance (birth date, full name, etc.) could be expressed more efficiently as part of the article, with the issue cited as source. As formatted above, it takes too much space to present largely unimportant and unencyclopedic material. Canonblack 20:34, 22 February 2006 (UTC)

[edit] Plagiarism

"Arana learned that she is not a true hunter. No hunter before her has ever had an exo-skeleton like hers. In a fight with "The Sisterhood of the Wasp" who attempted to resurrect their hunter, she released the power of the Hunter after Miguel died. Afterwards, she has quit WebCorps, though she's committed to fighting for the greater good."

This text clearly plagiarises a bio I wrote fro Marvel.com (which is copyright of Marvel). Here is the original text:

"Recently, Araña learned that she is not a true hunter. No hunter before her has ever had an exoskeleton like hers. In a battle in which the Sisterhood attempted to resurrect their hunter (even Spider-Man got involved), Miguel died and she released the power of the Hunter. Even though she has quit WebCorps, she will never stop fighting for the greater good." Sononsj 02:43, 3 February 2007 (UTC)
