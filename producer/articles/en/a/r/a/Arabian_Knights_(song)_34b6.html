"Arabian Knights" is a song recorded by English rock band Siouxsie & the Banshees in 1981. The track was written by the Banshees and co-produced with Nigel Gray. It was the second and final single released from the Banshees' fourth album Juju.

"Arabian Knights" is a mid-tempo punk/gothic rock song that has been a concert favorite since its release. It's also known for its "I heard a rumour / It was just a rumour / What have you done to her" lyric. The single peaked at number thirty-two on the UK singles chart in 1981.
