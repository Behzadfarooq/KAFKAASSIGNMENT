Arachnophobia (video game)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Arachnophobia is a video game based on the film Arachnophobia. It was developed by BlueSky Software and Disney for the DOS, Commodore 64, Amstrad CPC, and Amiga platforms.

The player assumes the role of Delbert McClintock, a bug-exterminator and character from the original film, fighting to save America from a horde of invading killer spiders.

[edit] Tips

Destroying an eggsack, one of which is found in each location of a town, provides a hint to the location of the queen of that particular town. When the queen is located, the use of a flamethrower, constructed by combining the easily found matches with a spraycan, or your bug-bombs is highly recommended.
