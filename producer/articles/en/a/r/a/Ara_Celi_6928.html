Ara Celi (born 31 May 1974 in El Paso, Texas) is a Latina[1] actress active in American film and television. She had a starring role in the minor 1997 dance movie Looking for Lola, but she is probably best known for her role as Ampata Gutierrez from season two of Buffy the Vampire Slayer (also 1997).

In 1999 she played Raquel Dion Santos in a nine-month attachment to All My Children.[2]

She has made several guest appearances in television series including Saved by the Bell: The College Years, Nip/Tuck, as well as Buffy the Vampire Slayer. She was also been seen in Go Fish, Bruce Almighty, American Beauty and From Dusk Till Dawn 3: The Hangman's Daughter, playing Esmeralda.
