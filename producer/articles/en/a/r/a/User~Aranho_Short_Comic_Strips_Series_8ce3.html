[edit] Comic Strips

[edit] Strip 1: I won $1 million!

This strip is about a maths game show. Three contestants were competing with each other for the grand prize. 30 minutes later, two contestants were left. The host asked them the last question.
Host: Last question. What is 1 + 1?
Contestant 1: I give up.
Host: Congrats, {{{name of contestant 2}}}. You won $1 million!

[edit] Strip 2: The Xbox

This strip is about a person trying to redeem a free Xbox. Brendan was reading a newspaper when he saw a contest.
Brendan: Spend $1000 in Carrefour to win an Xbox worth $345. Wow!
After he had shopped for five hours, he tried to redeem the Xbox buy he soon find out that the free Xbox was fully taken.

[edit] Strip 1: Lottery Number

This strip is about 6 4D numbers: 8888 (lots of good luck), 4444 (lots of bad luck), 1234 (toddler who learned his first 4 numbers), 0999 (Singapore's Police's hotline, 7777 (heaven), and 0748 (You loser).

[edit] Strip 2: Lord of the Rings: The Return of the destroyed ring

This strip is about a quest to go on a journey and return back without destroying the ring. 10 years, 2 months, 5 days, 19 hours, 2 minutes and 6 second later, the journey-er returned to its master with the ring damaged.

[edit] Strip 3: The surgery

This strip is about a surgery. The surgeon is doing a surgery but it turns out that he is fixing a broken toy car.

[edit] Strip 4: The crazy family tree

This strip is about a family tree.

[edit] Strip 5: Miss or Mrs Fit?

This strip is about two people talking about their friend's surgery.
Jack: My friend wanted to be as good-looking as her parents so she went for a plastic surgery.
Jill: And?
Jack: She ended up looking like her father.

[edit] Strip 6: How smart

This strip is about a man faking to be a blind man and he is trying to cheat a woman by asking her to buy a toy. The woman purposely drop the money and the man try to steal the money but was eventually caught stealing.

[edit] Strip 7: The last strip (map)

This strip is about a whole bunch of incident happening in just one single comic box. A total of 3 incidents is happening at the same time.

[edit] Trivia

  • In Strip 3 of Titanic Comic Strips, a RMS Titanic repica was seen in the strip. This shows that Titanic 2 is just above the wreckage of Titanic.
  • In Strip 2 of Short Comic Strips Book 2, Saddam appears to be shorter than George W. Bush.
  • Short Comic Strips Book 5 contains the most number of comic in the whole entire series so far.
  • Short Comic Strips Revisited (Book 6) was the first Short Comic Strips Series not to be a mini-book format.
  • Short Comic Strips Book 7 Halo Edition was the first book in the whole entire "Titanic Book" Collection to have a non-stickman drawing.
  • The original Short Comic Strips Book 8 was cancelled due to lack of creativity.
