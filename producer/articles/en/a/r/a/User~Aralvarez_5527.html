Aralvarez, (b. 8 April 1978, the Bronx, NY), known to his friends and colleagues as Fredo, is a weblogger and self-described "professional homosexual" – that is, a gay person who works for a gay rights organization. He currently wrangles web code for a large non-profit organization in Washington, DC. Previously, he managed the organization's shipping and supply operation, and was briefly the receptionist before that. He also holds a part time retail job for some fruit company.

Previous jobs include stints as a human resources assistant, an arcade game repairman, and a pretzel maker.

In his spare time, he drums in a local bagpipe band, designs web sites, and watches reruns of game shows, Britcoms and the Simpsons. He is a proud Mac user, an photography entuhsiast, and an avid kilt wearer. He shares a rowhouse with his ex-mate, his cat, and his roommate on Capitol Hill.

He's also prone to abusing hyphens.
