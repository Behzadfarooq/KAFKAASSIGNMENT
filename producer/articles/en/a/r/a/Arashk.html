Arashk, a hard/progressive rock music band established in 2001 in Tehran, Iran.

[edit] History

Arashk, established in 2001 in Tehran by Pooyan Khajavi and Shahram Khosraviani. The band line-up is Salim Ghazi Saeedi on Guitars, Keyboards & Synthesizers; Pooyan Khajavi on Guitars & Vocals, Shahram Khosraviani on Drums.

Image:Arashk Band.jpg
Left to Right: Pooyan, Shahram, Salim

[edit] Discography

Arashk's first album, Abrahadabra, is an instrumental blend, representing an impressive debut of instrumental, compositionally strong, electric guitar driven progressive rock music[1] and is now released.

The band's instrumental arrangement is firstly formed by standard rock conventions but hiring digital synthesizers has established the ground toward new explorations especially in promo songs of the album, Sovereign.

It is expected that the band releases its third album, Roar, in the middle of 2007[2].
