Arab News Network (ANN) is a news channel broadcasted on satellite from London. It has faced great financial issues.

ANN is a politically motivated satellite TV station owned by Rifat Al Assad, the Uncle of Syria's current President Bashar Al Assad. The two have been in disagreement over political issues and ANN TV exists to promote Democracy within Syria and other Arabic countries. Output is solely in Arabic language from March 2006. From November 2004 to March 2006 daily English News was running. The desire to remove the programme reflected a change in management. Rifat's son, Ribal Al Assad is currently Director of the London Bureau,he has decided to cancel the English programme because after he took over, he noticed that there was no audience for that programme. Major restructuring has been undergone and the latest technologies(AVID MEDIA,GOOGLE EARTH...) have been acquired by the channel in order to improve its image. ANN is currently broadcasting on Hotbird and Arabsat and will be soon broadcasting on Nilesat.
