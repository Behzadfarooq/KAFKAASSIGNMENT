Arab Chess Federation includes 17 members that are also members in the Arab League, the federation was created under a motto of "Arab as one Nation|Pan Arabism", it was established in 27 july 1975 in Damascus, capital of Syria.the federation was established in the presidence of Mr. Nahed El Khany.
