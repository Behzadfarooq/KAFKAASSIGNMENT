Arar is also the ancient name of the Saône river in France and a Canadian citizen Maher Arar.

Arar (Arabic: عرعر, BGN spelling: `Ar`ar) is the capital of Al Hudud ash Shamaliyah (The Northern Border) province in Saudi Arabia. It has a population of 145,237 (2004 census).

As it is spelled in English "Arar" it also refers to the nickname of a famous Jordanian poet of the twentieth century (his real name is Mustafa Wahbi et Tull (or Tal)).
