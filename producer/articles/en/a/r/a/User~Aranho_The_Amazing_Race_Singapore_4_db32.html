[edit] Prizes

Unless otherwise noted, prizes were awarded to the first team to check in for each leg.

Leg 1 - S$10,000.

Leg 2 - 8 movie tickets for the movie Spider-Man 3 at GV VivoCity.

Leg 3 - Sony Ericsson W300i Walkman Phone with accessories.

Leg 4 - Xbox 360 Premium Package with 5 Xbox 360 games.

Leg 5 - Windows Vista.

Leg 6 - S$1,000,000.

Special - S$100 worth of Adidas Voucher (This prize was given to the team who has the Golden Adidas Logo that was located in the envelope given by the casheir of the Adidas Store located in CityLink Mall).

[edit] Race locations

@ = Additional task

[edit] Leg 1 (Bishan)

  • Bishan, Singapore (Bishan Stadium) (Starting Line)
  • Bishan Swimming Complex @
  • Bishan North Community Centre (Un-use, Un-aired)
  • Longhouse Food Centre

The first Detour was To Get Healthy or To Get Fit. In To Get Healthy, teams had to go to the vegetable plot located in Bishan Park 2 and each team member must eat 1kg of fresh organic lettuce. In To Get Fit, teams had to travel longer to go to the multi-purpose field and each team member must successfully score 5 goals into the goal post using the penalty shoot-out way.

@ The Teams must swim across the pool to Ang Peng Siong to receive their next clue.

[edit] Leg 2 (Bishan → Buangkok)

  • Punggol Park Community Centre
  • Small Section of Lorong Buangkok
  • Sengkang Fire Station (Who want's to get hot?)
  • Buangkok MRT Station

The Roadblock was to put out a huge household fire in a open field.

[edit] Leg 3 (Buangkok → Changi)

  • Control Tower of Singapore Changi Airport
  • Changi Chapel and Museum
  • Changi Village (POSBank ATM Machine)
  • Historical Site of Beach Massacre (Who's ready to dig?)
  • Singapore Changi Airport Terminal 2's entrance.

This leg's Roadblock required a team member to dig in a marked area of sand on the beach for a small wooden surfboard token hidden up to 40cm beneath the sand. In the Detour, teams chose between Fuel and Fowl.

[edit] Leg 4 (Changi → Bukit Timah → Bugis → Kampong Glam)

  • Bukit Timah Community Centre
  • Bukit Timah Nature Reserve Visitor Centre (Who love hide n seek?)
  • Old Ford Motor Factory - Elimination Point
  • Newton MRT Station
  • Istana Kampong Glam (Who is really hungry?) (Venue: Gedung Kuning)
  • National Library @
  • Masjid Sultan

The first Roadblock of this leg was to find 8 huts. The second Roadblock of this leg was to eat eight plates of Mee siam. The Detour was Search For It or Sit Back and Relax. In Search For It, teams will have to search for the marked stall along New Bugis Street and eat 10 seeds of the durian. In Sit Back and Relax, teams will have to watch a short video in the Drama Theatre located in the library and answer 10 questions related to the video.

@ The teams must ride on the DHL Balloon to find the marked building, the National Library.

[edit] Leg 5 (Kampong Glam → HarbourFront)

  • VivoCity's Wading Pool (Who is starving?) (Venue: VivoCity's Kopitiam Food Court) (Un-use)
  • VivoCity's Kopitiam Food Court
  • Sentosa Express Sentosa Station

For the Roadblock, one team member had to eat ten bowls of Tau Suan. The Detour in this leg was Rap This or Sell That. In Rap This, teams had to go to the St. James Power Station and find a marked bar. Once they found it, those teams will have to write a rap about their race around Singapore and they will have to rap it to receive their next clue. In Sell That, teams had to go to Giant store located in VivoMart and sell 40 Nutella Hazelnut Spread.

[edit] Leg 6 (HarbourFront → Sentosa → Raffles Place → City Hall)

  • Merlion at Sentosa (Who got's stamina?)
  • Carlsberg Sky Tower @
  • Sentosa 4D Magix
  • Southernmost Point of Continental Asia @
  • Civilian War Memorial
  • Republic Plaza (Who got's the power?) (Venue: Swissôtel The Stamford)
  • Fountain of Wealth
  • Padang @ (Finish Line)

In the first Roadblock of this leg, one team member had to walk to the mouth of the Merlion and that team member will have to abseil down the Merlion. In the last Detour of the race, teams had to choose between Physical Climb or Human Search. In Physical Search, they had to go to Imbiah Falls and rock-climb up the fall. In Human Search, teams had to go inside Sentosa 4D Magix and search the whole theatre for their next clue while the movie, Pirates 4D, is playing. In the second and last Roadblock of the race, one of the team member must run up the marked stairs to the top of the building and that team member must then abseil down the building.

@ The teams will have to ried on the sky tower's observation deck and find the marked building.

@ The teams must serach the whole island for the departure times and their next clue.

@ Before the teams can proceed to the finish line, the teams must find the marked store, the Adidas store, and collect an envelope from the cashier.
