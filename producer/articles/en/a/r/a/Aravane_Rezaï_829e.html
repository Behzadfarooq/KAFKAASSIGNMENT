Aravane Rezaï (Persian: ارغوان رضايی ‎ , born March 14, 1987) is an Iranian-French tennis player born in St. Etienne, France to Iranian parents. Rezaï took up tennis after a childhood stint as her older brother's ball-girl.

At the 2006 U.S. Open, she reached the fourth round, her career best Grand Slam singles result. As of November 27, 2006, Rezai is 48th in the WTA rankings.

She competed for Iran at the Women's Islamic Games winning Gold in 2001 and 2005; however she professionally represents France as games she plays in Tehran aren't officially sanctioned.
