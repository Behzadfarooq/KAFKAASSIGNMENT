Arappalayam is an area in the Madurai district of Tamil Nadu State, India. It is on the south bank of the River Vaigai. Arappalyam comes under the Madurai West constituency. The PIN Code for Arasaradi-Arappalaym is 625016.

In Tamil, Aaru means river and Palayam means area. As it is situated on the river bank it got the name Arappalayam (aka Aatruppalayam).

[edit] History

Arappalyam has a very long history. Arappalyam is mentioned in one of the 63 Thiruvilayaadalgal of lord Siva. Arappalyam is called as "Pittukku Mann sumantha thiruthalam."

[edit] Bus Stand

Arapplayam Bus Stand is a major junction for the TNSTC, SETC buses going to Bangalore, Salem, Theni, Kambam, Kumuli, Dindugal. Well connected with Railway Station, Maattu Thavani Bus Stand, Anna Bus stand and Periayar Bus Stand. Buses are operated to Thirumangalam, Theppakulam, Anna Nagar and Madurai Kamaraj University from Aarappalyam Bus stand.

  • Midland Theatre
  • Vellaikannu Theatre
  • Thangam Parameswari Theatre,
  • Solamalai Theatre-Arasaradi

Maapilai, Manikka Vinayagar theatres are in Kaalavaasal.
