[edit] Introduction

[edit] Editorial Scope

Arabian Reseller News (ARN) is an industry magazine that covers the business of Technology in the Middle East. It covers IT industry trends, market analysis, cutting-edge technologies, products and IT services to channel and business buyers at regional level throughout the Middle East.

The publication offers an essential insight into the latest IT distribution trends, reseller models and VAR (Value Added Reseller) concepts. It also assists IT vendors to market their products and services through relevant channel partners.

Furthermore, ARN delivers information for distributors and resellers in order to help them track product developments, competitive vendor strategies, mergers, acquisitions and distribution agreements. ARN guides business buyers and resellers to making better buying decisions.

Each section in ARN is tailored to meet the information needs of the VAR in the Middle East. The objective is to further the aspirations of the resellers and to help grow the business further by identifying opportunities in the regional market.

[edit] Frequency

ARN is a monthly publication for IT VARs (Value Added Resellers) in the Middle East, offering analysis of trends and developments in the regional IT market.

As an industry magazine, there is much more to ARN than news. It is a monthly digest of latest developments covering the entire gamut of the IT market consisting of Consumer, SMB and Enterprise markets.

It goes beyond conventional industry magazines that are restricted to news coverage. ARN offers its readers analysis, case studies, sell-side features covering different verticals, interviews that introduce companies and channel personalities, and features product reviews. It also has regular columns from industry experts writing on various topical business and technology issues.

[edit] Editorial Breakdown

[edit] News

The primary focus is coverage of the industry's most significant developments concerning the technologies, products, services and the people shaping the IT world in the Middle East.

[edit] Analysis

This section offers in-depth analysis of some of the latest developments that will have an important bearing on the market in days to come. Going a step beyond straight forward news coverage, it offers a perspective on the connotations of the news covered.

[edit] Features

The features including the Cover Feature provides a near comprehensive understanding of the newsworthy issues on the market. These articles discuss the knock-on effects and offers opinions from industry insiders as they see it.

[edit] Case Studies

This section features real life accounts of how a reseller along with his principal vendor went about implementing an IT solution at a client site. These span different sectors and verticals. The narratives describe the challenges encountered and help the reader develop a perspective on the opportunities available in the different sectors.

[edit] Reviews

We make use of the leading (and only) scientific IT lab in the Middle East (that provides the region's best selling computer title, PC Magazine Middle and Near East, with their reviews) to carry relevant hardware and software evaluations, which are of paramount importance to resellers. It is one of our goals to keep IT resellers in the Middle East informed of the best products available on the market.
