[edit] Red lump

The red lump on the side of the one face looks really gory: Is that something that was put there as a decoration with the fabric under his head or is it stained with blood? — Sebastian 18:00, 26 March 2007 (UTC)

There are three people in the photo, and from what I can see none of them has a red lump on his face. Are you talking about the large wound at the back of the head of the boy in the middle? I'm pretty sure the lump is not so much decorated fabric as it is a very large section of missing skull and the boy's brain matter spilling out. --Ddawn23 08:20, 27 March 2007 (UTC)
Yes, that's what I meant. It just seemed odd to me that they would leave that open, while they gave him a band-aid on the forehead.
BTW, the use of this picture is currently being discussed in WT:SLR#Conflict_over_a_Foto_in_Sri_Lankan_Civil_War. — Sebastian 01:14, 28 March 2007 (UTC)
