Arakawaoki Station (荒川沖駅, Arakawaoki eki?) is a JR East railway station located in Tsuchiura, Ibaraki, Japan.

[edit] Station Layout

There are 2-side platforms, 2-ways.

1 ■Jōban Line Tsuchiura ・ Mito ・ Iwaki
2 ■Jōban Line Toride ・ Kashiwa ・ Ueno

[edit] History

  • 1896-12-25: Station begins operation.
