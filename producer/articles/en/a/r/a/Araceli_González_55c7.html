Araceli González (born June 19, 1967 in Buenos Aires, as Araceli Edith González) is an Argentinian fashion model, actress and TV host.

After marrying at a young age, she was married for a very long time, having her first child, a daughter, in 1988. She also has a son.

Gonzalez was linked romantically to Chayanne when the duo starred in a telenovela. This caused her marriage trouble, and, eventually, she separated from her husband (Adrián Suar) and found work as an actress in Europe. Later on, she returned to Argentina.
