Source: Commons Wikimedia.

  • Non-Arab regions of Morocco, Algeria, Tunisia, Libya, Egypt, Sudan, and Iraq are in lighter shades of green (Berbers in the first five countries, Southern Sudan in Sudan, (other main non-Arab areas of Sudan, like Darfur and the region of Beja people are not indicated on the map) and Kurdistan in Iraq.).
  • Hashed countries are countries generally considered to be Arab, but where Arabs are only a "small" minority. (Mauritania has a 70 % Arab majority and Somalia or Djibouti are not "generally" considered to be Arab, despite they are members of the Arab League.)
