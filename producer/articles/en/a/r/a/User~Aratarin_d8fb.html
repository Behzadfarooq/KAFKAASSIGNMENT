[edit] What is an Aratarin

Well, to start, Aratarin would like to thank you for visiting his page. As the reader, you my be wondering what an Aratarin is. Well, to be truthful, Aratarin wishes he knew what Aratarin means. Even though he has no idea what it means, he has used it since he was first becoming members of websites and playing onlinegames. Of course, it doesn't really matter what Aratarin means, because if it ever did mean anything, it now means me and is one with me.


[edit] About Aratarin

Aratarin is a Wikipedian who enjoys correcting problems and incorrect information that he finds on Wikipedia. He lives in Wisconsin, in the United States. Aratarin is currently a Sophomore at Lakeview Technology Acadamy planning on going into engineering through the Project Lead the Way program. He also enjoys writing about himself and other things on his User Page in third person.


The times that he is most often on are from 7:30 A.M. to 9:00 A.M. Central Time Zone. This is because he is in his Introduction to Engineering Design class, an amazingly easy class that he is in during that time and on the computer, often surfing Wikipedia.


[edit] Hobbies and Activities

Aratarin prefers PC gaming to console. He occasionally enjoys playing World of Warcraft on his main avatar, Caffinegood, a level 60 tauren hunter of the Dalaran server. Other games that he has played online (all with Aratarin as his username) include Halo, Warcraft 3, Starcraft, and Diablo 2. Currently he is actually on Wikipedia even more than World of Warcraft.


[edit] FIRST Robotics

Aratarin is currently a member of Team 1652 of Lakeview Technology Acadamy of Pleasant Prairie, Wisconsin. Him and his other team members are working on their robot for the 2007 FIRST Robotics competition, Rack 'n Roll. Aratarin was helping in the early stages with the designing and drafting of the robot, then joined with the programming group. His team is made entirly of Lakeview students.
