The main article for this category is Arab.

The word Arab is most commonly used to refer to any person of the Middle East or North Africa whose mother tongue is the Arabic language; it is used in this sense for such terms as Arab World, Arab League. In this sense, there are nearly 300 million Arabs.

This category concentrates on Arab people, all cultural related subjects must go into Category:Arabic culture and its subcategories.
