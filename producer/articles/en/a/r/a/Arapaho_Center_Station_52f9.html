Arapaho Center Station is a DART light rail station located in Richardson, Texas (USA) at Greenville Avenue near Arapaho Road. It opened on July 1, 2002 and is a station on the Red Line, serving the Richardson Civic Center, the University of Texas at Dallas and part of Richardson's Telecom Corridor including the facilities for Honeywell and Samsung.
