Arabesque TV

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Arabesque is an Arabic language pay-tv, owned by Arab Digital Distribution.

Arabesque commonly known as ART, broadcasts via Hotbird and Nilesat satellites to the Middle East, North Africa and Europe. It's direct competitors in the Arab regions are Showtime Arabia and Orbit Satellite Television and Radio Network. Access to the full package of channels is via an easily obtainable over the counter viewing card which counts down once activated in a suitable receiver. Cards are available in 3, 6 and 12 month formats.

ART is highly popular for it's sports coverage having up to 6 sports channels showing live English Premiership football matches simultaneously with optional English or Arabic language commentaries.
