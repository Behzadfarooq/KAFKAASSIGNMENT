Aragon Middle School

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Jan H. Aragon Middle School is a middle school located at 16823 West Road in unincorporated Harris County, Texas.

The school, which serves grades 6 through 8, was established in 2001, and is part of the Cypress Fairbanks Independent School District.

The school mascot is the Wildcat.

The school’s namesake was a Cypress-Fairbanks ISD staff member for 35 years. She taught English, history, and sociology at Arnold Junior High School and Cy-Fair High School for 16 years. Ms. Aragon was an assistant principal at Cy-Fair High School before being named the principal of Dean Junior High School. She opened Cook Junior High School as its principal and, in 1989, became the principal of Cypress Creek High School until her retirement in 1996.
