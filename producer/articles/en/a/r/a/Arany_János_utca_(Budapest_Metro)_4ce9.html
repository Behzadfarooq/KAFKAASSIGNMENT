Arany János utca (Arany János Street) is a station on the M3 (North-South) line of the Budapest Metro. It is located in District V under Bajcsy-Zsilinszky Road. Its single vestibule stands in Podmaniczky Frigyes Square.

Saint Stephen's Basilica is a prominent landmark a block south of the station.
