The Aragonese Party (Spanish: Partido Aragonés, or PAR) is a political party which advocates the interests of Aragon within Spain. The party was founded in 1978 under the name Aragonese Regionalist Party, but changed its name in 1990, keeping the initials PAR.

PAR had representation in the European Parliament (1999 - 2004) where it sat in the Greens - EFA group.
