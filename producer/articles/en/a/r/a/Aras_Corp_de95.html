Aras Corporation is an American developer and publisher of product lifecycle management (PLM), Advanced Product Quality Planning (APQP), and new product introduction (NPI) software.

The Aras software is called Aras Innovator.

[edit] Aras Innovator Software

Aras Innovator logo

Aras Innovator is a suite of open source Microsoft .NET enterprise software for managing complicated business processes.

[edit] Open Source Project

Aras Innovator's open source projects are hosted on Microsoft's CodePlex site for collaborative development.

The Aras Innovator project on CodePlex uses the Microsoft Community License.


[edit] Company Information

Aras is a privately held company with institutional investors Oak Investment Partners, Greylock Partners, Matrix Partners and leading New Hampshire angel group eCoast Angels.
