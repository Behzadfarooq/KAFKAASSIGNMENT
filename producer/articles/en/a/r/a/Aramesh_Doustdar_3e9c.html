Aramesh Doustdar (born in Tehran) is an Iranian writer, scholar and a former philosophy lecturer at Tehran University.

Doustdar received a PhD degree in philosophy from University of Bonn. He is known in Iran as a secular Heideggerian philosopher (in contrast to Reza Davari Ardakani who is a religious Heideggerian philosopher).[1]
