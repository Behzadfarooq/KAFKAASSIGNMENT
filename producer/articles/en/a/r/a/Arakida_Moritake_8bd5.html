Arakida Moritake (荒木田守武, Arakida Moritake?) (1473-1549) Arakida Moritake, the son of Negi Morihide, and a Shintoist. It is also known that Moritake excelled very highly in the fields of poetry, waka, renga, and in particular haikai. At the age of 69, Moritake became head priest of the Inner Ise Shrine.
