Arancón is a Spanish municipality of Soria. Located on the foot of the mountain range of "El Almuerzo" and mount "Cencejo". The localities that form it are: Arancón, Tozalmoro, Omeñaca, Calderuela, Nieva de Calderuela and Cortos.

[edit] Monuments

[edit] Civil Monumets

  • Roman road: Via 27 of Antonino, which united Caesaraugusta (Saragossa) with Austica Astorga (Astorga).
  • Medieval source of "La rana" (the frog)"

[edit] Ancient Roman Milliarium

Two Roman milliarium are exposed in a public garden next to the Church. They were used during the Middle Ages to lay the foundations of a bridge. Arancón can be considered milliarium capital in Soria. After the church there're another two milliarium.

[edit] Religious Monuments

  • Asunción romanesque church. (Arancón)
  • Concepción church. (Omeñaca)
  • Saint Bartholomew hermitage ruins.

[edit] Economy

  • Culture of wheat, rye, barley and sunflower.
  • Sheep cattle and pasture rent.
  • Greater hunting boundary

[edit] Flora and fauna

  • Flora: Holm-oak, rivets, steppe, tea, camomile, heather and lavender.
  • Fauna: Wild boar, red deer, roe deer, rabbit, hare and partridge.
