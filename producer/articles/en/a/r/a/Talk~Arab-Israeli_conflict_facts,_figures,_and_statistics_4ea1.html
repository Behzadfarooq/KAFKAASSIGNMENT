[edit] Comments on the accuracy of this article

  • The name of the article is misleading. These are facts and statistics of the Arab counties and Israel and not of their conflict. The name of the article suggests these are facts and figures about the conflict: facts about the history of the conflict, wars,...etc. Since these are not facts of the conflict but only facts and statistics related to these countries, i can't really understand the importance of several items in this article. Is the fertility rate, for example, that related to the conflict?
  • This article is lacking references. There are no references at all cited for several columns in the table. Also there is no reference for notes 2 and 3. Why are these two notes anyway?
  • Total fertility rate values seem to be very different from those provided by the World Fact Book [3].
  • Same applies to infant mortality rates[4], GDP per capita [5]. Some differences also appear in the military expenditure values [6]
  • It appears to me that presence of Netherlands in an article about Arab-Israeli conflict statistics is not justified. I read above it is for comparison. Why is there a need for comparison with another country? Why was Netherlands chosen for comparison? It is neither the highest, the lowest or the median in the aspects of comparison.

--Wedian 18:23, 6 August 2006 (UTC)

I think the name is ok and the table is ok, assuming the figures are right, but its layout with Israel at the top is misleading. It appears to be listed according to freedomhouse.org's estimation of who is 'free' or not based on PR & CR (Political Rights and Civil Liberties), ratings which, (I assume), freedomhouse.org have awarded the particular countries.
Report says: "freedom ratings reflect an overall judgment based on survey results" Is there no greater hint to freedomhouse's methodology than that? I'm not saying that their figures or methodology is wrong im just worried that this may not be the best indicator to include in a table dealing with figures and facts, or at the very least it may not be the best method of sorting the list.
