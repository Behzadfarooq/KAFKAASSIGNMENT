The Araneoidea are a superfamily of araneomorph spiders. They contain families of eight-eyed spiders:

  • Anapidae
  • Araneidae
  • Cyatholipidae
  • Linyphiidae
  • Mysmenidae
  • Nesticidae
  • Pimoidae
  • Symphytognathidae
  • Synaphridae
  • Synotaxidae
  • Tetragnathidae
  • Nephilidae
  • Theridiidae
  • Theridiosomatidae
