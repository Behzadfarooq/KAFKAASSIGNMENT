Arfius Arf (real name Allan Russell Ferguson) (born April 3, 1981) is a British artist.

Arfius Arf was born in the industrial town of St.Helens, Merseyside. He is associated with the Stuckist art movement and The 4 Bullet Theory, a collection of artists and musicians from his hometown.

Arfius Arf, along with two other members (A.K.Devious and James "Jammer" Lunt) of The 4 Bullet Theory, traveled the length of Britain (Lands End To John O' Groats) in a classic Ford Capri for five weeks in mid 2006, in a project called "The Capri Diaries".

His work has been described as simplistic vectorism because of its strong colour and often sharp lines. His main subjects are portraits of people in his life as well as abstract works, including Nevermind The Pollocks, a cross between his love of punk rock music and the works of Jackson Pollock.
