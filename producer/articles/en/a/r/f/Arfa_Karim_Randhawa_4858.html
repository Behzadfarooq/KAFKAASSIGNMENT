Arfa Karim Randhawa from Faisalabad, Pakistan is by now the youngest person ever to pass the Microsoft Certified Application Developer (MCAD) exam.

She was 9 years, 9 months, 11 days and 9 hours when she took the exam. In consequence to this outstanding accomplishment, Microsoft invited her to visit the campus in Redmond on July 15, 2005, where she also met Bill Gates.
