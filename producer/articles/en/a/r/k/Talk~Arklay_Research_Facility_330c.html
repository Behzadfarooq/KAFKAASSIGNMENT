[edit] "Spencer Mansion"

One of the trivia points featured in this article states that "Spencer Mansion" is a fan-made title. I don't believe that this is true. It's somewhere in one of those game files, as I remember referring to it as Spencer Mansion long before reading ANYTHING AT ALL about the RE series outside of the video games themselves. I know I didn't just come up with the name off of the top of my head. Anybody else remember feel this way? Gamer Junkie 20:16, 5 November 2006 (UTC)

Good question, I just read through all the files from each game and it's never referred to as "Spencer Mansion". PS. The trivia section needs sorting out Parjay 22:34, 6 November 2006 (UTC)
I'm reluctant to touch trivia sections, as I'm not always aware of what's true, false, in-between or just not common knowledge in the RE community. The question about Spencer Mansion, though, it's really frustrating me now, I can't believe that it isn't mentioned anywhere at all. Gamer Junkie 04:14, 7 November 2006 (UTC)
The name comes from the non-canon S.D. Perry novels and as far as I know, they never been used in the games. The closest thing I've seen is Nicholai's Report referring to it as "Lord Spencer's estate" in Resident Evil Survivor. The problem is that alot of English-speaking Resident Evil fans tend to mix info from the Perry novels with the games themselves, resulting in mixed info (even though the canon of the games often contradicted those of the novelizations). Jonny2x4 20:00, 15 January 2007 (UTC)
I suppose it doesn't help that I've never read an S.D. Perry novel. The movies were a disappointment, I suppose the novels would be, too. That's why I've never touched them. Gamer Junkie 02:47, 16 January 2007 (UTC)
