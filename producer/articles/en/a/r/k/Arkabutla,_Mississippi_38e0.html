Arkabutla, Mississippi

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Arkabutla is a small town in Northern Mississippi. Arkabutla is sometimes called "Arkabutla Township" and is located in Tate County, Mississippi. It is on the southern side of Arkabutla Lake.

American actor James Earl Jones was born in this town.
