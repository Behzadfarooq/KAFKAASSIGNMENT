Arko

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Arko III is an International show jumper. The horse has had many wins and has represented Britain in the Olympics.

  • Color: Bay
  • Markings: Star, snip, half-sock on right fore, half-cannons on both hind legs
  • Height: 16.2 hh (168 cm)
  • Sex: Stallion
  • Breed: Oldenburg
  • Breeding: by Argentinius (Hanoverian), out of Unika (Oldenburg) by Beach Boy (Oldenburg)
  • Breeder: Mr Schuett, Enge-Sande, Germany
  • Current Rider: Nick Skelton
  • Previous Riders: Andrew Saywell, Robert Whitaker
  • Owner: John R. Hales, Lisa Hales, and Patricia Hales

Born in 1994, Arko is known world-wide for his talent in the show jumping arena. Being both careful and quick, the stallion is one of the best competing in the sport today.

Arko's sire, Argentinius, has sired over 400 show jumpers and 190 dressage horses in Germany alone. His show jumping offspring include AEGON's Autogramme, Anka, Fursa, A-Dur, Argentino, Gigowatt Asmodina and Albatros; his dressage offspring include Nissan Amaretto, Albano, Aleppo-S and Aristocrat. Argentinius is one of the greatest sons of Argentan, a well-known and prolific stallion.
