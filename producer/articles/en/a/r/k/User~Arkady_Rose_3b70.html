[edit] About me

I've been an editor on Wikipedia for a few years now; I was originally introduced to it by my partner, David Gerard. Whilst not as prolific an editor as David, nonetheless I have been reasonably active in the past although I am now returning to semi-active status as an editor after a prolonged wiki-hiatus.

I am also quite involved with the UK Chapter of Wikimedia and on 08/03/2007 was elected Director and Chief Financial Officer of Wiki Educational Resources Ltd.
