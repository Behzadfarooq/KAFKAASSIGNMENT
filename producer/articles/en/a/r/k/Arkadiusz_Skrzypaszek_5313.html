Arkadiusz Skrzypaszek (born April 20, 1968 in Oświęcim) is the most famous Polish modern pentathelete. He won two gold Olympic medals, both in Barcelona, 1992. Skrzypaszek won the individual and the team challenge. After The Barcelona Games he retired.
