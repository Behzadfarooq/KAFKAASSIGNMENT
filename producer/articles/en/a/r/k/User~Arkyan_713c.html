Hi, I'm Arkyan. This is my userpage. There used to be a lot more here but I want to make this page a lot more concise. The old biographical information that was here has been archived at User:Arkyan/Bio for historical purposes. Or of course there is always the page history.

I primarily poke around on AfD and usually only sound off on discussions that I either have a particular interest in or feel should be deleted. Probably the only time you'll see a keep from me is if either I feel strongly on the subject or the article is otherwise headed for a delete. I guess this makes me something of a deletionist.

My contributions to the namespace are primarily things related to the great state of Arizona although I do also try to make edits and improvements to subjects relating to various natural sciences, particularly astronomy, politics and mythology.

Feel free to leave messages on my talk page. I am happy to try and help how I can.
