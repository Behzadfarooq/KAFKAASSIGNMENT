Arkadiusz Głowacki

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Arkadiusz Głowacki (born March 13, 1979 in Poznań) is a Polish footballer who currently plays for Wisła Kraków.

Głowacki is 6'1" in height (1.86 metres) and nearly thirteen stones (78 kilograms) in weight. He first played for SKS 13 Poznań during the 1994/95 season, but was purchased by Lech Poznań in the 1995/96 season. He made his Polish Premier League debut on May 7, 1997 against Amica Wronki and has since appeared in well over 150 games. He participated in the 2002 Football World Cup for Poland.
