Arkady Vyatchanin (born April 4, 1984) is a Russian swimmer who competes in the backstroke events.

Last Name Vyatchanin
First Name Arkady
Gender Male
Height 2.00 m / 6'7"
Weight 85 kg / 187 lbs
Nationality Russian

[edit] Achievements

  • 2006 European Championships - gold medal (100m backstroke)
  • 2006 European Championships - gold medal (200m backstroke)
  • 2005 World Championships - silver medal (4x100 m relay)
  • 2004 Olympic Games - fourth place (4x100 m relay)
  • 2004 World Short Course Championships - bronze medal (200 m backstroke)
  • 2004 World Short Course Championships - bronze medal (4x100 m relay)
  • 2003 World Championships - silver medal (100 m backstroke)
