Arkavathy River (Kannada ಆರ್ಕಾವತಿ ) or Arkavathi River, is a river originating at Nandi Hills of Kolar District. It is a tributary of the Kaveri River, which it joins at Kanakapura after flowing through Kolar District and Bangalore Rural district.
