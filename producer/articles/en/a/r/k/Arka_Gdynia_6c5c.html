[edit] Latest events

Arka Gdynia started the new season in the 1st league:

  • Arka Gdynia - Legia Warszawa 0:0

Effective immiedieatly the team got suspended by PZPN for a month due to corruption. The team will not be allowed to compete in the Orange Extraklasa, League Cup and Polish Cup. All games will be awarded a walkover ( 0:3 ) for the opposite team. Arka will not be able to play in 5 Orange Extraklasa games. Rules state that if a team does not compete in 3 consecutive Extraklasa games it gets expelled and relegated 2 levels of competition (3rd division).
