Arkady Andriasyan (Russian: Аркадий Георгиевич Андреасян) (born August 11, 1947) is a retired Armenian football player.

He played for FC Ararat Yerevan in 1973 when Ararat won the Soviet championship. He also won a bronze medal at the 1972 Summer Olympics for the USSR.
