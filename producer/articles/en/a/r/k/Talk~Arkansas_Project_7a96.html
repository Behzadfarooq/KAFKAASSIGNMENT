[edit] NPOV

While the malevolent intentions and existence of the "Arkansas Project" are proven and established, they are still a tough pill to swallow for a lot of Clinton-haters (and there are many). Granted that, the langugage in this article (unrestrained use of the word "attack") compromises the integrity of the information presented. As is, I don't think it's in line with NPOV. —The preceding unsigned comment was added by Dawsong (talk • contribs). (19 December 2005)

Excuse me for asking what might seem like an impertenent question, but if the use of the word "attack" is accurate in describing the Arkansas Project, how is that NPOV?
