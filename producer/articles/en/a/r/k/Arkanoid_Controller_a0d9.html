The Arkanoid Controller is an optional game controller for the Nintendo Entertainment System (NES) game Arkanoid which enhances the feel and play of the game. The player uses the knob to control the paddle just like in a real video arcade. There are also real NES buttons on the controller so that the player can access the option menu. While it can be theoretically used to play other NES games, it is optimally designed for Arkanoid only.
