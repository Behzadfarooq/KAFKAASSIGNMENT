David Barreda (born May 28, 1990 in Tampico, Mexico) is an PHP programmer who focus his work with Otserv where currently is very known for works like ArkAAC, ArkCMS, Otserv Help Files and eOtserv

[edit] Biography

David Barreda born in Tampico, Tamulipas on May 2, 1990.

He start to study HTML and scripting around the year 1996 when he got his first computer, his main job was in Red-Latina founding the #Scripting channel (Later deleted and founded by other guy in this times), giving away his codes modifications for OpTalk and SDFind services for the well known MP3 Channel, #mp3+ where he was operator and later a great helper with his services at #CService with the nickname of HoOt_HoOt (a while ago).

After some years with the same stuff, he moved from Tampico and he leave the whole internet for about 2 years, in that time he developed some sites (some of them never where uploaded or even tested sucefully), His "more known site" of that times where PumpNet (now offline) a very good resource to get Pump It Up files for PC.

In the year 2001 he discover Otserv, a server for his favorite MMORPG (Tibia) what he start to play at the ends of 1997, Since that time he started to browse the World of Tibia forums and later moved to Otcommunity for a while, later it goes down and he raised with Arkold Thos nickname at Otfans.net where he right now is a senior moderator and is well known for his job there
