Arkansas Baptist College

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Arkansas Baptist College (ABC) is a historically black college (HBCU) located in Little Rock, Arkansas. Founded in 1884 as The Minister's Institute, ABC was initially funded by The Colored Baptists of the State of Arkansas, and is the only Baptist HBCU west of the Mississippi River.
