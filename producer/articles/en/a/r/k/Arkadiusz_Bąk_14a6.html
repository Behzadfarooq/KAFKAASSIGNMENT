Arkadiusz Bąk

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Arkadiusz Bąk (born October 6, 1974) is a Polish footballer who currently plays for Lech Poznań.

Having started in the 1992/93 season, Bąk moved between Ruch Chorzów, Polonia Warszawa, Birmingham City and Widzew Łódź. In the 2002/03 season he transferred to Wronki, and has made thirteen appearances for Poland, including appearances in the 2002 Football World Cup in South Korea.
