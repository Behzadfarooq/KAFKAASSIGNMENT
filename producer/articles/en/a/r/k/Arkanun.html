Arkanun

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Arkanun is one of the oldest and best selling role-playing games in Brazil. It was written by Marcelo Del Debbio in 1995 and published by Daemon Editora.

Arkanun is based on medieval mythology, angels, demons, the war between Templars and the Inquisition. Players take roles as rosacrucian mages, scholars, alchemists or templars and must survive church persecution and the magi politics.
