Arkansas Highway 355

Highway 355 is a north-south highway in southwest Arkansas. Its southern terminus is at an intersection with U.S. Route 67 in Fulton. Its northern terminus is at an intersection with U.S. Route 278 2 miles south of Center Point. Communities along this 32 mile route include Fulton, McNab, Saratoga, Tollelle, and Mineral Springs.

A second section of Highway 355 begins at an intersection with Arkansas Highway 174 6 miles southwest of {{Hope, Arkansas|Hope]] and runs 3 miles southeast, ending at Arkansas Highway 29 at Evening Shade.

A third section begins at an intersection with Arkansas Highway 29 5 miles south of Evening Shade and runs east then southeast 23 miles before ending at an intersection with U.S. Route 371 at Lamartine.
