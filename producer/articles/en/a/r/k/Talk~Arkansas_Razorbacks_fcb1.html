Referring to the track and field teams (and it is plural) as "One of the most successful programs in NCAA history" is not POV. As noted in the John McDonnell (coach) article:

Coach McDonnell led the track team to their first national championship as a member of the now-defunct Southwest Conference at the 1984 NCAA Indoor Championships. Since then, the University of Arkansas has won 41 NCAA championships, including 11 cross country, 18 indoor track and 12 outdoor track. Only 24 other NCAA titles in the three sports combined have been won by other schools during that time.

He has more national championships (41) than any coach in any sport in the history of college athletics. Holford 22:56, 3 January 2006 (UTC)
