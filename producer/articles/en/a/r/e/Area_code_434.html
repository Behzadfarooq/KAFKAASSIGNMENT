The 434 area code was created on June 1, 2001 as a split from the 804 area code. It is located in the south central area of the Commonwealth of Virginia and extends to the North Carolina and Tennessee borders.

[edit] Cities and towns in the 434 area code

  • Chatham, Virginia
  • Charlottesville, Virginia
  • Crozet, Virginia
  • Danville, Virginia
  • Farmville, Virginia
  • Hampden Sydney, Virginia
  • Keswick, Virginia
  • Lynchburg, Virginia
  • Scottsville, Virginia
