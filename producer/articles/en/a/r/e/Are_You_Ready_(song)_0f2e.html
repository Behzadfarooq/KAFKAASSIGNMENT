"Are You Ready" was the third single from Joanne's 2001 debut album, Do Not Disturb.

[edit] Song Information

Are You Ready, released on November 8, 2001. It debuted and peaked at #41 on the ARIA Single Charts, her 6th highest single to date.

The Are You Ready experience reminds Joanne of cameras, smoke machines, lights, costumes, the disco and over sixty dancers crammed into a dance studio. In November '99, this video clip was filmed at Dance World 301 in a ballet studio. Joanne's management team and crew spent many sleepless nights building the set with their own hands. Their aim was to create a mysterious location to set the scene for this high energy clip. Each time Joanne hears this song, she recalls her amazement when she first walked on set to see a dull ballet studio transformed into a futuristic night spot. It was amazing!

[edit] Track Information

Are You Ready features three versions on its single.

The single contains the tracks:
Track #01: Are You Ready [Radio Edit] 3:23
Track #02: Are You Ready [Extended Radio] 5:50
Track #03: Are You Ready [Pump Funk] 4:06

Do Not Disturb features two tracks of Are You Ready.
Track #10 (Extended Pump Funk), Track #14 (Hot Hot Crazy).

The single noted on the front that Joanne was an Aria Award Winner.
