Area codes 419 and 567

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

North American telephone area codes 419 and 567 serve the northwest corner of the State of Ohio. Cities served by this area code include Lima (Bluffton), Toledo (Ottawa Hills, Waterville, Whitehouse, Perrysburg, Sylvania, Bowling Green, Maumee, Holland, Northwood, Oregon, Rossford), Findlay and Mansfield (Ontario). Area code 419 was created as one of the original area codes in October 1947; the overlay Area Code 567 was created January 1, 2002.
