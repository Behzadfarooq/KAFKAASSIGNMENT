Area code 315

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Area code 315 is a telephone area code in central Upstate New York. It stretches from west of Syracuse to Little Falls, north to the Canadian border, east to Massena and south to shortly before Cortland.

[edit] Reference in popular culture

In the greater Rochester area, the term "315er" is an insulting way of referring to residents of the rural area east of Rochester. The term was popularized by Alan Levin, a.k.a. Brother Wease, a host on radio station WCMF in Rochester.[1]
