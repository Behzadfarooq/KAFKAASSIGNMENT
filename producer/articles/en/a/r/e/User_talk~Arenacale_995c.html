[edit] Monster truck questions...

Do you have sources for the ownerships? For instance, I knew that several were owned by Clear Channel, but had seen no indication on Grave Digger and Maximum Destruction. We (all of us on wiki) need to get better about sources.  :) You clearly know more about this than I.  :) Wikibofh 03:29, 7 December 2005 (UTC)

  • Grave Digger trademark and Maximum Destruction trademark. The entire Grave Digger operation is owned by CCE (the Digger website is proof of this), while the physical Maximum Destruction truck is owned by Tom Meents Racing with the name licenced by CCE, and it is classified by most in the sport as a "Clear Channel Truck" because of the trademark. Blacksmith would be a similar example where the trademark is owned by CCE but the truck itself is owned by Pablo Huffaker. Once Christmas break comes in a week and a half I'll try to find some sources for all of this. The problem really is that there are so many sources that it's hard to distinguish them. For the time being I'll switch "Maximum Destruction" to Madusa in the MJ article. Arenacale 03:50, 7 December 2005 (UTC)

[edit] License tagging for Image:Blacksmith05.jpg

Thanks for uploading Image:Blacksmith05.jpg. Wikipedia gets hundreds of images uploaded every day, and in order to verify that the images can be legally used on Wikipedia, the source and copyright status must be indicated. Images need to have an image tag applied to the image description page indicating the copyright status of the image. This uniform and easy-to-understand method of indicating the license status allows potential re-users of the images to know what they are allowed to do with the images.

For more information on using images, see the following pages:

  • Wikipedia:Image use policy
  • Wikipedia:Image copyright tags
