Arena is a Pay-TV General Entertainment channel in Australia, available for viewing on the Foxtel, Austar and Optus TV featuring Foxtel Digital carriers. Arena mainly syndicates American television programs. It is owned and operated by XYZnetworks, which owns and operates many channels for Australian Pay-TV carriers. The female voice over artist for the channel is Zoe Sheridan from The Catch-Up.
