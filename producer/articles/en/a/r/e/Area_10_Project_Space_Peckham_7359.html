[edit] Area 10 Project Space Peckham

Area 10 Project Space Peckham, (also known as Area 10 PSP) is a not-for-profit artist-run initiative that produces and hoasts collaborative events providing a platform for interdisciplinary projects and practices that encourage cross fertilisation across a wide range of divergent practices

Area 10 PSP is a pioneer in the Creative Industries sector, bringing new, experimental and mainstream contemporary art practices to Peckham, London UK.

Area 10 PSP is a platform for education and social cohesion, forming collaborative links with University of the Arts London and Goldsmiths, University of London.

[edit] History

Area 10 Project Space Peckham was initiated in 2002 in Peckham, Southwark, UK. Based in a disused Timber Warehouse called Eagle Wharf.

Area 10 PSP is so called in reference to Brodmann area 10. It is thought to be involved in processes such as abstract thinking, cognitive flexibility, planning, rule acquisition, initiating appropriate actions and inhibiting inappropriate actions, and selecting relevant sensory information.
