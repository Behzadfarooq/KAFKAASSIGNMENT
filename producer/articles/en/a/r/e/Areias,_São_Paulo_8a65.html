Coordinates: 22°34′48″S, 44°41′50″W Areias is a city in the state of São Paulo in Brazil. The population in 2003 is 3,697, its density is 12.04 /km² and the area is 307 km². The elevation is 509 m.

[edit] Demographics

According to the 2000 IBGE Census, the population was 3,600, of which 2,452 were urban and 1,148 are rural. Its life expectancy for the city was 65.57 years. The literacy rate was at 84.97%.
