Are We Nearly There Yet? is the tenth album by indie legends Television Personalities. It was released in the United Kingdom on February 20, 2007. The album features brand new tracks along with covers of Bruce Springsteen and The Killers.

  2. "The Peter Gabriel Song"
  3. "The Eminem Song"
  4. "I Get Scared When I Don't Know Where You Are"
  5. "I See Dead People"
  6. "If I Could Write Poetry"
  7. "If I Should Fall Behind"
  8. "Coltrane's Ghost"
  9. "Mr Brightside"
  10. "All the Midnight Cowboys"
  11. "All the King's Horses"
  12. "You are Loved"
  13. "It's All About the Girl"
