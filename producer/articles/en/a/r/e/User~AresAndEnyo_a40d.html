AresAndEnyo is a user of Wikipedia. Messages can be left however AresAndEnyo leaves to its discretion the actions taken after. AresAndEnyo discourages leaving negative messages on its message page. If AresAndEnyo offends you then it probably apologises.

AresAndEnyo is currently involved in a project to add album/single art and info boxes to his favorite Australian artists. At the moment he has done extensive work on Little Birdy and The Superjesus.

AresAndEnyo is a member of WTOBDW and UARWW.

It has 96 edits of 01:24am 28th of January Western Australian Time (DST)!!

[edit] About It!!

It lives in Perth, Western Australia. Intrests include Computers, Music and being a British Gentleman.
