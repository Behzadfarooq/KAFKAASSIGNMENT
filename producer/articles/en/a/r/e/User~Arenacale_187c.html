Arenacale (born Cale Putnam on August 15, 1985) is a student currently enrolled at Worcester Polytechnic Institute in the class of 2007. He is from South Boston, Massachusetts and is a graduate of Boston Latin School with the class of 2003.

[edit] Interests

Arenacale is a well documented fan of Monster Trucks. He has enjoyed monster trucks since the age of 3. He currently does not have a favorite truck, but his favorite driver is the 2007 Monster Jam World Finals Racing Champion John Seasock.

Arenacale is a fan of several other motorsports. He watches the NASCAR Nextel Cup Series, where Tony Stewart is his favorite driver. He also watches NHRA, CORR, WRC, and most other forms of racing.

Arenacale is also a reknowned truck maker for Monster Truck Madness 2.

[edit] Currently editing

Arenacale currently edits and creates articles in Category:Monster trucks. He would like a lot of help with this! Many well-known and important trucks are not documented and existing articles need further info. Any help would be greatly appreciated!
