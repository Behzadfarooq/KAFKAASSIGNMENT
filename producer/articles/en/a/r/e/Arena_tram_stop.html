Arena tram station is a stop on the Tramlink in the Woodside area of South Norwood. The station is located in the London Borough of Croydon on the edge of South Norwood Country Park, by the Croydon Arena, London, England.

The stop serves the council estates of Longheath Gardens Estate and Tollgate Estate. The track was previously the railway between Elmers End station and Addiscommbe station, although there was no station on its present site. The stop is at ground level and is the point where the Waterlink Way National Cycle Network route 21 crosses the tram lines.

Tramlink Route 1 between Elmers End and Croydon and Route 2 between Beckenham Junction station and Croydon serve the station. The next stops in each direction are Elmers End station (Route 1), Harrington Road tram stop (Route 2) and Woodside tram stop (both routes).
