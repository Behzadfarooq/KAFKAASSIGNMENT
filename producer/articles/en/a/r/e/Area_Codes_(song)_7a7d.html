Area Codes is a 2001 single off Ludacris's third album, Word of Mouf. The song's lyrics display the area codes for the women the singer meets in various places. This song was produced by Jazze Pha.[1]

[edit] Song Information

The song is humorous, light-hearted, and has a fast flowing pace. At five minutes and three seconds, it is the fifth-longest track on the album.

[edit] Billboard Reception

Area Codes was received fairly well on the charts. Its highest position was #7 on the Hot Rap Singles.[2]
