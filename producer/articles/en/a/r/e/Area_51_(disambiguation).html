Area 51 (disambiguation)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Area 51 is name for a secret military facility in Nevada, north of Las Vegas.

Area 51 may also mean:

  • Area 51 (arcade game), is a light gun game
  • Area 51 (first-person shooter), a first-person shooter game
  • Alienware Area 51, a range of computers made by Alienware
  • Hangar 18, Area 51, a song by heavy rock guitarist Yngwie J. Malmsteen
