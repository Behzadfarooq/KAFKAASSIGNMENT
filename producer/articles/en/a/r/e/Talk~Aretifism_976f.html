[edit] Merging?

This article has been marked for merging into Foot fetishism. The article however states explicitly that this is not a foot fetish qua definition, but rather a fetishism for the absence of shoes (thus the counterpart to retifism) -- i.e. the inverse of shoe fetishism.

If it should be merged rather than deleted (google.com says 144 hits, some of which are Wikipedia mirrors), I'd rather suggest to merge it into shoe fetishism, as this seems to be more relevant (a See also link seems to be the more appropriate thing to add to foot fetishism in this case).

As the term is apparently unknown to the interwebs, I'd also like to suggest that whoever started this article should add a better known synonym or references if they want to prevent a successful WP:VfD. -- Ashmodai 20:05, 13 April 2006 (UTC)
