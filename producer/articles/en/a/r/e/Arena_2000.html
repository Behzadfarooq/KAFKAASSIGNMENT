Arena 2000 (ru: Универсальный Культурно-Спортивный Комплекс Арена-2000) is an arena in Yaroslavl, Russia. It is primarily used for ice hockey and concert events, and is the home arena for Lokomotiv Yaroslavl. Arena 2000 opened in 2001 and holds approximately 9,000 people.

The first game in Arena 2000 took place on October 12, 2001, with Lokomotiv Yaroslavl posting a 3-1 win over HC Lada Togliatti.
