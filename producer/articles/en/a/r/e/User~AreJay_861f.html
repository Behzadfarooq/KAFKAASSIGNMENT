I'm 25 years old and work for one of the Big Four Auditors in Atlanta, Georgia. I love archeology especially when it pertains to the Indus Valley Civilization.

As far as entertainment, my favorite movies include The Usual Suspects, The Godfather, Pulp Fiction and The Lord of the Rings. I prefer to listen to progressive rock, my all time favorite prog rock band being Genesis.

I am also a fan of classical music especially requiems and Baroque works and greatly admire Bach. I also admire Beethoven. I also like Jethro Tull, Queen and Pink Floyd. I like professional football, college football and tennis. Big fan of the Purdue Boilermakers, the Florida Gators and the Indianapolis Colts.

I watch a lot of british comedy shows. Some of my favorites include Fawlty Towers, Blackadder, 'Allo 'Allo!, Jeeves and Wooster and Monty Python's Flying Circus. I edited my first article under my current moniker on August 17, 2004, but I had been contributing anonymously prior to that for some time.

I have started to try and contribute regularly to the Indian and American portals. I am quite the magician (although I do say so myself) with Adobe Photoshop so if you need help with maps, image manipulation and/or enhancement, holla (internal, email) at me and I will try and help.
