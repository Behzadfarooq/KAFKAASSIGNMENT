Arena Multiuso

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Arena Multiuso is a multi-use stadium in Salvador, Brazil, that is currently under construction. Once completed in 2007, it will be used mostly for football matches and will host the homes matches of Esporte Clube Vitória. The stadium will have a capacity of 32,000 people. It replaces their current stadium Barradão.
