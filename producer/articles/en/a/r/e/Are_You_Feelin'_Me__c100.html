Are You Feelin' Me? was a song recorded by Aaliyah with guest vocals by the song's producer Timbaland for the Romeo Must Die Soundtrack in 2000. It was also included in 2005 on Aaliyah's second compilation album Ultimate Aaliyah (released in the UK). The song flopped at UK radio stations, possibly due to the fact that it was previously released half-a-decade earlier.

[edit] Further Song Information

The song was used as a theme in Romeo Must Die where in Trish (Aaliyah) & Han (Jet Li) are dancing in the casino or bar. This song was also released in 2005 in the UK as a promo single to promote the greatest hits album "Ultimate Aaliyah" but it failed to chart on the UK charts.
