[edit] Plot synopsis

Ares plans to form an army of children part of his bid to cover the world in war without end. Naturally it falls to Hercules to stop his evil half-brother.

[edit] Quotes and trivia

  • Mark Newham plays Ares in this episode, but the actor would be replaced by the late Kevin Smith in the second season.
