Area code 580

Area code 580 serves the state of Oklahoma. It covers southern and western Oklahoma including the cities of Guymon, Durant, Hugo, Enid, Lawton, Ardmore, and Elk City.

The other two area codes in Oklahoma are 918, which covers northeastern Oklahoma (and includes the city of Tulsa) and 405, which covers central Oklahoma (mostly Oklahoma City and the surrounding area).
