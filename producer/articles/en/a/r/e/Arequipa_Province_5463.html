Arequipa is a province in the Arequipa Region, Peru. It is the most populous province of the region, and its capital, Arequipa, is Peru's second-largest city. It borders the provinces of Islay, Camaná, Caylloma, and the Cusco and Puno regions.

[edit] Political division

The province is divided into twenty-nine districts (Spanish: distritos, singular: distrito).
