[edit] Popularity

Seems like Ares is catching up to eMule as the most downloaded project on sourceforge, though if you count the multitude of eMule spinoffs (both the mods at sourceforge and distros like VeryCD), I'm sure eDonkey still has a much larger user base. Still, for a program that is apparently very popular, I haven't really seen or heard much about it. Can somebody discuss more about the origins of the program? Was it very popular in its days of being a Gnutella client? Cyaugin 07:35, 21 January 2007 (UTC)
