Are We There Yet? is an episode of the U.S. television sitcom Yes, Dear.

[edit] Plot

Spoiler warning: Plot and/or ending details follow.

Jimmy surprises Kim & Greg with a trip to the Grand Canyon. He rents an old RV and tells Greg that he will do the driving and when they wake up they will be there. Jimmy falls asleep and when Greg wakes up they are still at the house. Greg gets carsick and they stop a supermarket. Greg complains and Jimmy throws the keys to him and tells him to drive, but the keys fall down a stormdrain. The keys can't be delivered until the next day. Greg tells everyone he is going to take them to a hotel, but Christine forgot his wallet back at the house (she was holding it to prevent Greg from paying). When the keys come Greg drives and Christine is injured twice before they even leave the parking lot. By the time they get there it is dark and they can't see. Greg throws Jimmy the keys to drive home but they fall in the canyon.
