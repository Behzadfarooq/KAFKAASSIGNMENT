Are You Lonesome Tonight? (album)

Are You Lonesome Tonight? is an album featuring the original London cast of a play by the same name, written by Alan Bleasdale and premiering in 1985 at the Phoenix Theatre .[1]. The album features several songs made popular by Elvis Presley.

[edit] Tracks

The cast recording includes 14 tracks:[2]

  1. Peace in the Valley
  2. Heartbreak Hotel
  3. That's Alright Mama
  4. I Don't Care If the Sun Don't Shine
  5. Loving You
  6. Blue Suede Shoes
  7. Hound Dog
  8. If I Can Dream - Martin Shaw
  9. All My Trials - Martin Shaw
  10. 1968 NBC TV Special: One Night With You/All Shook Up/Jailhouse Rock
  11. You Gave Me a Mountain - Martin Shaw
  12. I Was the One
  13. If We Never Meet Again - Martin Shaw
  14. Are You Lonesome Tonight?
