Aree Davis (Ariel Alexandria Davis) was born February 19, 1991 and is from Park Forest, IL. She is an American actress. Her first big movie role was in The Haunted Mansion alongside Eddie Murphy in 2003. She's also appeared in many television shows such as Everybody Hates Chris (Keisha) and ER. Her younger sister, Dee Dee Davis, stars in the The Bernie Mac Show. Aree has also appeared on a few how-to-make commercials based on "The Haunted Mansion" Film.
