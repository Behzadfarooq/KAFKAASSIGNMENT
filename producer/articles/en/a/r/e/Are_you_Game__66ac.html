Are you Game? is a large gaming convention run in Manchester, annually, starting in 2007, under the name of Game 07. It is held each early summer to allow gamers to play in major tournaments at a time when exams are over and University lodgings are available as cheap accommodation. It is the home of the European Dungeons and Dragons Miniatures Championships.

Features:

  • Miniatures Games
  • Film Festival
  • Fantasy Artists
  • Role-playing games - RPGA events and many more RPG events
  • EberCon - Eberron Convention
  • Card Games
  • Seminars
  • "The Market Place" a try and buy area for traders and distributors to show and sell their wares
  • 24 hour gaming
  • Fancy Dress

The event is managed by "Game Convention Services Ltd.", a company set up to host such a large event within a proper structure.
