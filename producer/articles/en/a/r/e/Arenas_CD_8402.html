Arenas Cultura y Deporte is a football team based in Armilla in the autonomous community of Andalusia.Founded in 1931, its plays in Tercera División - Group 9.Its stadium is Estadio Municipal de Armilla with capacity of 2,000 seaters.

[edit] Seasons

  • 2004/2005: Segunda División B 20th - Relegated
  • 2005/2006: Tercera División 4th
  • 2006/2007: Tercera División
  • 1 seasons in Segunda División B
  • 11 seasons in Tercera División
