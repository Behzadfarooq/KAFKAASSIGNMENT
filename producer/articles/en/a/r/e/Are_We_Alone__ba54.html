Are We Alone? is the SETI Institute's weekly science radio program, hosted by Senior Astronomer Seth Shostak. The show covers a broad range of topics in the sciences, especially as they relate to our understanding of the origin and evolution of life on Earth, and what life elsewhere in the universe might be like. Each week's show focuses on a particular topic and allows scientists on the cutting-edge of research to share their latest findings and speculate on the implications for SETI and society in general.

Molly Bentley is the Executive producer of the radio show and she, along with Seth Shostak, chooses topics, writes scripts and procures guests. She is also a co-host and interviewer on the show. Guests include scientists and researchers from academic, public and private spheres (including NASA), popular science writers, cultural critics and ethicists focused on science, science fiction writers, (and debunkers of junk science and the paranormal on the monthly "Skeptical Sunday").

Bill Oxley, the creator of the show, continues to serve as a consultant.

Archived shows are available via podcast or as .mp3 or WMA files on the SETI Institute's website.
