[edit] Language of a city

Languages Spoken in a City could mean the main languages spoken by a majority of the residents - need not be the mother tongue or native language of the majority of the residents. For cosmo cities like Mumbai and Bangalore, English and Hindi are universally spoken. The state language could be another addition, + languages from neighboring states like gujarati and tamil resp.

Ofcourse, this depends on the exact demographics of the city in question and has nothing to do with the state where the city is located or the native tongue.

The Wikimedia Foundation is very careful about the images included in Wikipedia because of copyright law (see Wikipedia's Copyright policy).

The copyright holder is usually the creator, the creator's employer, or the last person who was transferred ownership rights. Copyright information on images is signified using copyright templates.
