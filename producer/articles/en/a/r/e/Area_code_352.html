Area code 352 is a telephone dialing area around Gainesville, Florida, north of Tampa and northwest of Orlando. It split from area code 904 during the 1990s.

It includes all of Dixie, Gilchrist, Levy, Marion, Citrus, Sumter, Lake, and Hernando counties, and all but the extreme north of Alachua County. It also includes the southern and eastern tips of Bradford County, the west edge of northern Putnam County, the northeast corner of Pasco county, and the Steinhatchee and Tennile communities in extreme southern Taylor County.
