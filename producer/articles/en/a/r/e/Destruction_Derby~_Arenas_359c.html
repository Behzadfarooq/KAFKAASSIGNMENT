Destruction Derby: Arenas is a video game for the PlayStation 2. It was developed by Studio 33 and published by Gathering of Developers. It was released on April 1, 2004. The game is part of the Destruction Derby series. This version allows online play. No cheats available on cheat sites. Get Rough Justice by getting gold on every Destruction Bowl level. Get The Dam by getting gold on very race level. Get bonus cars by completing certain actions or winning races on campaign.

Playable characters are (in numerical order) 2-Dravid Fortune 3-Mako 4-The Underdog 6-The Victim 7-Toffee 8-Lt. Johnson 9-Red Widow 10-Sebastian Noire 13-Scratch 15-Mariah 16-Cadence 17-Canvas 20-Jana Makucha 21-Clarence Carver 22-Danni Brennan 28-Set 33-Jeremiah 75-Yin Yang 99-Lady Cassandra 666-Jared Crane
