Ares IV (launch vehicle) is about a real proposal for a rocket, which has a good chance of being built. This article is about a ficticious spaceship from the Star Trek universe. I would say that the rocket proposal is more notable, and therefore it should have the name Ares IV, and this page should be moved elsewhere, with a disambiguation link on the new Ares IV page linking to it. --GW_SimulationsUser Page | Talk 12:47, 13 January 2007 (UTC)
