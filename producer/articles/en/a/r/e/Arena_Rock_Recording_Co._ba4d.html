Arena Rock Recording Co.

The Arena Rock Recording Company ("Arena Rock" or "ARRCo") is an independent record label formed in 1995 in Brooklyn, NY. ARRCo is currently based in Portland, Oregon and is distributed by Redeye in the United States and Koch in Canada.
