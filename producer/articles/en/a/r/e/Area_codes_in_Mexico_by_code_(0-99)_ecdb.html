Area codes in Mexico by code (0-99)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Area codes 0-99 serve the metropolitan areas of Mexico.

Area code 33 serves the Metropolitan area of Guadalajara, Jalisco, area code 55 serves the Metropolitan area of Mexico City (Estado de México and the Federal District) and area code 81 serves the Metropolitan area of Monterrey, Nuevo León.
