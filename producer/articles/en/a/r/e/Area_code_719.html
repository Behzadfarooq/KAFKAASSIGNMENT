Area code 719 is a telephone area code serving the state of Colorado. It covers Colorado Springs, Pueblo, Leadville, Alamosa, San Luis Valley, Cañon City, Trinidad, Rocky Ford, La Junta and southeastern Colorado. Numbers with the format (719) 474-xxxx are located at Thule Air Force Base, Greenland.
