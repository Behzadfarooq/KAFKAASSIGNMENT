[edit] Academic dress of Loughborough University

This is a very college-specific list that is only information for people within the college, not an encyclopedia audience. Comfort 06:17, 1 January 2006 (UTC)

  • Keep. There is a collection of "Academic dress of..." articles, see Category:Academic dress. I would support merging some of them together, but I don't think they should be deleted. --LesleyW 07:41, 1 January 2006 (UTC)
    • - That category should be for academic dress items, not individual schools! Why should there be an article about the academic dress for every school? That may be information under the school's page. I doubt there's any usefulness in an article for the academic dress of every univeristy that exists! The "academic dress of..." articles should all be deleted! 67.67.236.35
  • Delete Too specific an entry on clothing items. There is no need for every college to create a page for their own congregational robes. -- (aeropagitica)  09:28, 1 January 2006 (UTC)
  • Delete, really too specific and of low encyclopedic value (even photos are missing). If there's something to be salvaged, put it into the university article. Pavel Vozenilek 11:00, 1 January 2006 (UTC)
  • Delete per 67.67.236.35. --Squiddy | (squirt ink?) 11:52, 1 January 2006 (UTC)
  • Keep I certainly wouldn't want the Oxford and Cambridge articles to be deleted, so I guess this one should be retained too. CalJW 11:57, 1 January 2006 (UTC)
  • Merge to Loughborough University. Different case from Oxford or Cambridge who set the fashion, so to speak. Dlyons493 Talk 12:32, 1 January 2006 (UTC)
  • Delete- far two narrow. Reyk 12:48, 1 January 2006 (UTC)
  • Merge and redirect to Loughborough University. Too narrow for a separate article, but entirely valid within University article. - Mgm|(talk) 13:16, 1 January 2006 (UTC)
Be it in a much condensed and edited version. - Mgm|(talk) 13:17, 1 January 2006 (UTC)
  • Delete. If really neccessary can merge condensed information into Loughborough University.--Daveb 15:16, 1 January 2006 (UTC)
  • Delete. Far too specific. This isn't about a style of academic dress, it's about the colours used by graduates of Loughborough University. Add the information to Loughborough University, if you must. Pilatus 16:02, 1 January 2006 (UTC)
  • Delete, far too specific to be useful. â€” JIP | Talk 20:50, 1 January 2006 (UTC)
  • Delete, if you are really interested in the academic dress of Loughborough University you should contact the university however this is far too specific to be of interest to almost any Wikipedian. Cedars 07:02, 3 January 2006 (UTC)
