Artistic Gymnastics at the 2008 Summer Olympics

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Artistic Gymnastics competitions at the Beijing 2008 Summer Olympics will be held from August 9 to August 19, at the National Indoor Stadium.

[edit] Events

14 sets of medals will be awarded in the following events:

  • Team All-Around Men
  • Individual All-Around Men
  • Floor Men
  • Pommel Horse Men
  • Rings Men
  • Vault Men
  • Parallel Bars Men
  • Horizontal Bar Men
  • Team All-Around Women
  • Individual All-Around Women
  • Vault Women
  • Uneven Bars Women
  • Balance Beam Women
  • Floor Women

[edit] Qualification Criteria

An NOC may enter up to 6 men and 6 women athletes. Qualification is based on the results of the 2007 World Artistic Gymnastic Championships held in Stuttgart (GER) from September 1-9, 2007. The places (98 men + 98 women) are awarded as follows:

Event Criterion Qualified Men Qualified Women
Team All-Around Teams places 1-12 6 athletes per team 6 athletes per team
Team All-Around Teams places 13-15 2 athletes per team 2 athletes per team
Team All-Around Teams places 16-18 1 athlete per team 1 athlete per team
Individual All-Around Top athletes from NOCs not already qualified 7 athletes 9 athletes
Individual Apparatus Finals Gold medal winners, if are not already qualified. 6 athletes 4 athletes
FIG Executive Board Invitations Ensure participation of the Host Nation and each continent. 3 athletes 3 athlets
Tripartite Invitation 1 athlete 1 atlete
