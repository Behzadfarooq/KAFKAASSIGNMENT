Arts et Métiers is a station of the Paris Métro, serving Line 3. It takes its name from the Musée des Arts et Métiers, which is served by the station.

To mark the bicentenary of the Conservatoire National des Arts et Métiers in 1994, the station was redesigned by Belgian comics artist François Schuiten in a style reminiscent of the science fiction works of Jules Verne.
