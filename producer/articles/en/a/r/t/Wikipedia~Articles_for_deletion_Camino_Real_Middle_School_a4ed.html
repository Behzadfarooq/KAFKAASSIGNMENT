  • Keep - it's a school, hence it's notable. Markb 13:18, 9 February 2007 (UTC)

  • comment I argue that schools are notable because they have a profound affect on their pupils (this could be several thousand people, depending on the age & size of a school). As such, this makes them notable, not only to their community but to the world at large if former pupils move away. Markb 13:09, 10 February 2007 (UTC)
  • Keep - Agree, a school is notable. Sufficient finds on Google to provide additional verification of notability. •CHILLDOUBT• 15:18, 9 February 2007 (UTC)
    • Such as? Resolute 16:45, 9 February 2007 (UTC)
  • Delete - clearly not notable, as are most schools. -- Bpmullins | Talk 15:37, 9 February 2007 (UTC)
  • Delete Non-notable, nothing here to merge with an article about the town. Inkpaduta 16:08, 9 February 2007 (UTC)
  • Delete Irrelevant information about teachers and generally to little information, as-well as failing WP:SCHOOL —The preceding unsigned comment was added by Tellyaddict (talk • contribs) 16:49, 9 February 2007 (UTC).
  • Delete as non-notable per WP:SCHOOL proposal. Charles Kinbote 17:55, 9 February 2007 (UTC)
  • Speedy Delete Per A1. So tagged.--GCFreak2 18:11, 9 February 2007 (UTC)
  • Delete for notability. Philippe Beaudette 18:50, 9 February 2007 (UTC)
  • keep as education-related information which could potentially be notable. Niffweed17, Destroyer of Chickens 20:54, 9 February 2007 (UTC)
    • It could also be said that this "education-related information" could potentially be non-notable. In its current state the article does not establish the notability of the school. Extraordinary Machine 23:29, 9 February 2007 (UTC)
  • Delete; notability of school not established. Schools are not inherently notable, and of the dozens or even hundreds of articles on Wikipedia considered for deletion (AFD, speedy or otherwise) every day because they don't assert the notability of their subjects, I don't see why this one (or any other article about a questionably notable school) should be kept. Extraordinary Machine 23:29, 9 February 2007 (UTC)
