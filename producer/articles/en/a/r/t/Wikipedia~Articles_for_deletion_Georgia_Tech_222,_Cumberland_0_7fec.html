[edit] Georgia Tech 222, Cumberland 0

Found during stubsorting. Not only is it a bad title, it's something that should be mentioned in the article on the teams involved. If the teams don't deserve articles the game doesn't either. I'm not sure if it can be merged as I'm not aware of naming conventions for these sports teams, but if merging isn't an option, this should be deleted. - Mgm|(talk) 12:14, 5 January 2006 (UTC)
