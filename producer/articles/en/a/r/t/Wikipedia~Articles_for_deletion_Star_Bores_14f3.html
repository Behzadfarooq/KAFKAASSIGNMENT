[edit] Star Bores

  • Delete. I believe the subject is not sufficiently notable to merit its own article in Wikipedia. — Mike (talk • contribs) 16:49, 2 July 2006 (UTC)
  • Keep if referenced. A real book ( [1] ) with presumably a fair number of sales (amazon.co.uk rank 100,000-ish - not JK Rowling territory, but still not negligible); however, references are essential. Tevildo 16:52, 2 July 2006 (UTC)
  • Transwiki WikiBooks. Ste4k 20:58, 2 July 2006 (UTC)
  • Keep major books should have their own article. Jedi6-(need help?) 02:22, 3 July 2006 (UTC)
  • Delete per nom. No notability stated or implied, or documented, in this advertisement. "Major" books? This paperback ranked #769,537 on Amazon as of this date, down from #763,195 yesterday. I think we're safe from needing to argue that distinction. Tychocat 14:19, 4 July 2006 (UTC)
  • Delete unless references can show that it meets the suggested criteria of WP:BK. It exists, it's from a notable publisher but I don't see any indications that it is popular, critically acclaimed or otherwise notable. Eluchil404 01:55, 8 July 2006 (UTC)
