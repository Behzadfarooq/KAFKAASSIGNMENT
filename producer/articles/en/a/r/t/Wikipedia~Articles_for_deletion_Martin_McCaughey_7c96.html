[edit] Martin McCaughey

Martin McCaughey (edit|talk|history|links|watch|logs) – (View log)
This AfD has been listed on WikiProject Irish Republican Army/AfD -- Tyrenius 19:01, 10 February 2007 (UTC)

This article was tagged for notability concerns and a prod, which was removed without improvement to the article, so it's here for AfD. There is no indication in the article of meeting notability requirements. Not everyone who was shot in the "troubles" in Ireland will have their own biographical entry — only those who in some way have achieved wider prominence, which can be established by mainstream coverage, of which there is none cited here. It might merit inclusion in a wider article about the alleged "shoot to kill" policy or whatever, but not, from what is written, as a stand-alone article. Tyrenius 13:24, 8 February 2007 (UTC)
