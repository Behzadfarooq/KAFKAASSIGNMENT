Idolblog (edit|talk|history|links|watch|logs)

Blog site that doesn't assert notability. I'd recommend it for speedy A7, except that the article's been around for a while, and [1] says it won an award, although I don't know how significant that might be. gadfium 21:17, 6 November 2006 (UTC)
