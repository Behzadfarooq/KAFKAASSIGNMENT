[edit] Build a website

Delete: The page is unencyclopediac. It has all the earmarks of a schoolkid's homework assignment. Orphan. No substantive work. Poorly written. Hu 15:44, 6 October 2006 (UTC)

  • Delete Unencyclopaedic and original research.--Húsönd 16:27, 6 October 2006 (UTC)
  • Delete per nom --Mnemeson 16:43, 6 October 2006 (UTC)
  • Before anyone says the obvious, Wikibooks already has Web Hosting, Web Development, Web Design, How To Build and Run a Free Online Games Website, Authoring Webpages, and a whole bookshelf of books on web server tools. It has no need of this. Uncle G 17:11, 6 October 2006 (UTC)
  • Delete. Unencyclopedic tutorial. Prolog 18:25, 6 October 2006 (UTC)
  • Delete. "Unencyclopedic" is very vague when we have a criteria in The WP:NOT an instruction manual criteria. It's also original research and probably WP:NFT. ColourBurst 19:08, 6 October 2006 (UTC)
  • Delete Pavel Vozenilek 23:14, 7 October 2006 (UTC)
  • Delete for all the reasons mentioned above. JubalHarshaw 20:27, 10 October 2006 (UTC)
