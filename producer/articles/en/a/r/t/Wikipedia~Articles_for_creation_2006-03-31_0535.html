[edit] Mozart's horn concertos (?)

Mozart composed four different horn concertos for his good friend Joseph Leutgeb. In each concerto there are three seperate movements. The general feel of these movements usually fast, slow, faster.

The first and last usually have lots of short notes and feel energized, (the musical term would be "staccato"). The second movement is always slower and more smooth, ("legato").

In the first and last movement of each concerto there is a similar pattern of short, smooth, short that is also in a major key, minor key, major key fashion.

[edit] Rhampholeon brevicaudatus

Commonly know as the "bearded leaf chameleon" or commonly "leaf/pygmy chameleon," R. Brevicaudatus originates from the eastern Usambara and Uluguru mountains in northeastern Tanzania. It is easily distiguished from others in the Rampholeon family by the presence of a "beard" below the mouth, consisting of a few raised scales. At a full grown length of only a few inches (~3" or 8cm), it is marked by somewhat drab coloring in comparison to other chameleons, usually assuming a brown or tan coloring. It is quite capable of changing its coloration though, often taking on a shade to blend into the background and becoming darker when under stress. It is also capable of compressing its body laterally and producing a stripe down its side, mimicing a dead leaf. It often assumes this form when sleeping in the open. Males are distinguished by a longer tail, more prominent dorsal crest, slimmer body type, and persistent patterning. Like others in the Chamaeleonidae family, it is distinguished by independently rotating eye sockets and a tongue longer than its body.

[edit] Captive Care

Partial to cooler temperatures than other chameleons, R. Brevicaudatus prefers a temperature between 70-80F with a slight drop at night. It is unkown if they utilize UV light, and many keepers report sucess with simple halogen or florescent lighting. Basking lights may be provided but are generally not necessary, and care must be taken to keep the temperature down. Being quite small, a five to ten gallon glass aquarium can be used. Regular misting at least twice a day is required, with direct misting of the chameleon recomended. R. brevicaudatus feeds on small insects, in captivity they are partial to crickets, wax worms/moths, houseflies, and others. Though considered fairly hardy, care must be taken to prevent stress, as with other chameleons. Lifespan is generally several years.

[edit] HCFF: Hampshire Children and Families Forum

HCFF, Hampshire Children and Families Forum is a Winchester-based charity looking after the needs of parents and children in Hampshire, UK.

HCFF consists of volunteers, and staff who care about Hampshire’s children, including;
- Parents
- Those working in childcare and development
- Health professionals
- Community development officers
- Teachers and head teachers
- Pre-school supervisors
- Social services representatives

They all give their time to promote a common aim – to help all children in Hampshire make the best off their childhood and fulfill their potential by;
- Holding termly meetings within 20 geographical areas of Hampshire
- Holding events to meet as many families and children as possible
- Publishing termly newsletters to keep families informed as to what is going on in their area of Hampshire

The Hampshire map and contact details of each HCFF coordinator can be found at http://www.hcff.org.uk

HCFF staff are there to bring together parents and professionals: to decide what should be done to improve life for their community., and then to make it happen.

In the past, issues raised have included:
- A lack of health checks for 3 year olds
- A shortage of pre-school places in the area
- Insufficient one-to-one helpers for children with special needs
- Difficulty accessing play schemes in the summer
- The lack of safe play areas
- The problems of bullying
- The poor quality and choice of activities for young teenagers in the county

[edit] Grace Bible College

[Grace Bible College http://www.gbcol.edu/] is a fundamentalist Bible college that emphasizes a so-called "hyper-dispensational" interpretation of the Bible. Its approach differs from more standard dispensatinal methods by 1) initiating the beginning of the "invisible Church, the Body of Christ," in the middle of the Book of the Acts of the Apostles (somewhere between Acts 9 and 13) and 2) limiting Biblical revelation that directly applies to the Church to the epistles of Paul. The "invisible Church, the Body of Christ," differs from the institutional church in that it is universal and is not to be identified with any single denomination or tangible church institution. People become "members" of the Body of Christ upon acceptance of Christ as their personal savior. Grace Bible College emphasizes that membership in an institutional church is secondary to membership in the Body of Christ. Membership in an institutional church, though encouraged, cannot "save" you, whereas membership in the Body of Christ occurs at salvation. The typical dispensational approach, which is often associated with Dallas Theological Seminary, begins the Church in Acts 2 and tends to include the gospels and general epistles as revelation that directly applies to the Church. A more-extreme approach to "hyper-dispensationalism" does not begin the Church until the end of Acts and limits the Church epistles to Paul's epistles written after this time (Ephesians, Philippians, Colossians, I & II Timothy, Titus, and Philemon). This approach has often been associated with E.W. Bullinger.

As somewhat of a consequence to its dispensational approach, Grace Bible College does not endorse water baptism as a valid Church practice. Instead, it emphasizes the idea that Christians are "baptized" into the Body of Christ upon belief and that water baptism is a relic of Jesus' "Kingdom" ministry, which was a transition from the Mosiac Law to the Body of Christ.

Other denominational bodies are associated with, though technically independent from, Grace Bible College, including the [Grace Gospel Fellowship http://www.ggfusa.org/] and [Grace Ministries International http://www.gracem.org/].

[edit] Adam Long

Theatre actor and writer and member of the Reduced Shakespeare Company, Long joined the troupe in 1981, when he was asked to stand in for Barbara Reinertson in a 40-minute production of Hamlet. Long continued in the RSC with Daniel Singer in 1983, and co-wrote and performed in a 20-minute version of Romeo & Juliet.

Over the years, the company expanded and contracted, culminating in 1985 in the current three-man format, consisting of Singer, Long and Jess Winfield. Long co-wrote the company's best-known production, The Complete Works Of William Shakespare (abridged) in 1987.

Although now retired from the RSC, Long did particpate in The Reduced Shakespeare Company Radio Show in 1994, and a number of other projects in the 1990s.

Adam Long was born in Manhattan and raised in California, and now resides in London. He is married and has a seven-year-old son.

[edit] FUTA

FUTA (Federal Unemployment Tax Act)(United States) - A tax of 6.2% on $10,000 wage base paid by the employer. - Discounted up to 5.4% if the state pays into a qualified state-level unemployment plan. Note: This was done so that employers would put pressure on their states to create qualified state-level plans so that the discount would be available. Monies from this tax go towards paying for administration of UI (Unemployment Insurance) and Job Service programs in all states that elect to participate, 1/2 the cost of extended unemployment benefits during periods of high unemployment, and loans to states that need to borrow money to pay unemployment benefits.

[edit] Abgeordnetenhaus of Berlin

The Abgeordnetenhaus of Berlin (German for House of Deputies) is the state parliament of the land of Berlin and works also as town council for Berlin regarded as a city. The legal electoral term of the Abgeordnetenhaus lasts five years, self-dissolution is possible. At 60%, the Members of the Abgeordnetenhaus (in German: MdA) are elected under a plurality voting system in the constituencies, at 40% under proportional representation, with a threshold of 5% to receive any seats. The minimum number of 130 members is usually passed over due to overhang and compensation seats. The next election will take place on September 17, 2006.

The current numbers of seats result from the elections of October 21, 2001:

Total 141

The government, the Senate of Berlin, is formed by a coalition of Social Democrats and Left Party.PDS (called "red-red coalition").

[edit] Moosehead Studios

Moosehead Studios was created in 1999 by Daniel Skubal as a comical cover name for the films him and his high school friends produced. Once the word spread about the projects, the name stuck.

Moosehead Studios is located in West Lafayette, Indiana and has become one of the top production studios in that area. Their studio has become well-known for producing low budget films with high production value. Some consider Moosehead Studios to be a Guerilla Filmmaking studio because of their quickly-paced production style and on-the-go production set up.

The studio's growth has brought about many new faces and possibilities for them. Some of these new faces include Chris Voelz (film producer), Cody Carter (production assistant), and Quentin Voglund (cinematographer).

The future of Moosehead Studios is promising. Daniel Skubal has confirmed plans to move the studio out to California upon completion of his academic career at Purdue University.

[edit] National Association of Tax Professionals Helps Tax Preparers Serve Taxpayers

The National Association of Tax Professionals (NATP) is a nonprofit professional association founded in 1979, and committed to excellence in taxation. Headquartered in Appleton, Wisconsin, NATP was formed to serve professionals who work in all areas of tax practice and has over 17,500 members nationwide who serve over eight million taxpayers. Members include individual tax preparers, enrolled agents, certified public accountants, accountants, attorneys, and financial planners. NATP fosters the lifelong development of professionals who provide tax and related financial services by: - Providing education, research, and other services; - Communicating knowledge; - Building resource alliances; and - Advocating for the profession and for public interest.

NATP’s workshops meet the standards set by state accrediting and professional agencies and the IRS’ CPE requirements for enrolled agents. Learn more at www.natptax.com.

[edit] [Little Rock Combined Statistical Area]

The Little Rock Combined Statistical area consists of the Little Rock metropolitan area, the Pine Bluff metropolitan area and the Searcy micropolitan area. Created by the census bureau in 2002, combined statistical area and mircopolitan area are new terms.

The new 2000 population from the census bureau for the Little Rock Combined Statistical area was 785,024 people. The 2004 estimate for the area was 813,160 people.
