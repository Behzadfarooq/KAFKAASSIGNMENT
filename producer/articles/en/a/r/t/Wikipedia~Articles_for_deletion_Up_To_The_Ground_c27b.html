[edit] Up To The Ground

Again, WP:NOT a Crytal Ball. --Shane (talk/contrib) 01:53, 24 July 2006 (UTC)

  • Delete per Bugs, but allw for recreation when more info is present.
    • Never mind. Weak Keep. It won't harm anyone and it won't take lonbg to find out about the plot, characters, etc. -- Chris Ccool2ax contrib. 02:10, 24 July 2006 (UTC)
  • Delete as it stands now, as unverified. Article claims "is filming" but all the cited sources just say "has been signed to appear". Need actual verification that it is filming, not six-month-old articles that say it was going to. Every source I can find is dated in March, with not an update since. Fan-1967 02:16, 24 July 2006 (UTC)
  • Delete I see no inherent value in an article about a movie with "no release date and no announced plot". If indeed more information becomes available regarding this then it can be recreated, but as of now it's just crystalballism. DrunkenSmurf 02:22, 24 July 2006 (UTC)
  • Speedy delete as "empty, no content." What is there is that there is a movie, the production date nobody knows, about a thing no one has heard, that will be released nobody knows when, but here is a title. Borderline pranksterism, and we ain't aint-it-cool-news.com. Geogre 02:28, 24 July 2006 (UTC)
  • Delete Once the movie is actually announced by IMDb or a film company, then it should be created. As of now, it's all speculation. Not a crystal ball. -Royalguard11Talk 02:35, 24 July 2006 (UTC)
  • Comment I wouldn't go by IMDB. They have a really bad record on movies "in development" that never happen. Wait at least for cameras to roll. Fan-1967 02:38, 24 July 2006 (UTC)
  • Speedy delete any article which contains the phrase "upcoming movie with so far no release date and no announced plot". -- MrDolomite | Talk 03:54, 24 July 2006 (UTC)
  • Delete to the Ground, crystal-ballism for film that hasn't even begun production yet. None of the links are to reliable sources. --Coredesat talk. o.o;; 04:01, 24 July 2006 (UTC)
  • Delete per nom. --Bigtop (tk|cb|em|ea) 21:07, 24 July 2006 (UTC)
  • Delete for now maybe recreate the article when (and if) more information about the movie becomes available. --Tuspm(C | @) 22:38, 24 July 2006 (UTC)
