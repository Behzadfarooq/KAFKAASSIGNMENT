Arts Center is an underground MARTA rail station with a central island. The station consists of four levels: the platform level, the mezzanine level with faregates facing onto West Peachtree Street, the bus level with a bus terminal, and the upper level which is located across the street from the Woodruff Arts Center. This is the ninth busiest station in the MARTA system, handling an average of 14,500 boardings per weekday.

[edit] Parking

There is a small parking lot with 33 spaces. Parking is free to patrons. There is also a Flexcar parked in the parking lot.

[edit] Attractions

  • Atlantic Station (via shuttle bus)
  • High Museum of Art
  • Woodruff Arts Center
  • Colony Square
  • Center for Puppetry Arts
  • The CW Music Complex Midtown (Center Stage, The Loft & Vinyl)
  • 14th Street Playhouse
  • DeKalb's House
