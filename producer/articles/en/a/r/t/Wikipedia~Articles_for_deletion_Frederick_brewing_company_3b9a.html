[edit] Frederick brewing company

Prod-ded twice, both times removed. Does not assert notability. Renata 22:34, 12 February 2006 (UTC)

  • Speedy Keep. 40K+ google hits, apparently well-known in microbrewing community, apparently involved in hemp beer litigation [1], cited in tourism guides, etc., etc. Described by independent source as largest brewer of its type in region [2] Its products are treated seriously and reviewed by (beer) consumer guides [3]. Looks notable (and potable!) More significant in brewing industry than Kadee Strickland is in movie industry. Monicasdude 23:05, 12 February 2006 (UTC)
  • Keep, multiple independent coverage on the first page of google hits [4], thus passing WP:BEER and/or WP:CORP as applicable. Kappa 00:28, 13 February 2006 (UTC)
  • Keep. Removal of prod was entirely correct. Massive room for expansion here, but the Hempen Ale story makes this an automatic keeper. -- JJay 01:47, 13 February 2006 (UTC)
  • Keep. Breweries are generally encyclopedic IMO, and the controversy surrounding the hempen ale makes it even more so. Friday (talk) 15:13, 13 February 2006 (UTC)
