[edit] GM camouf

GM camouf (edit|talk|history|links|watch|logs)

During a mass de-prodding of articles by TheFarix on Gundam related articles, I reviewed the various removals of the subst'd {{prod}} and have listed this one, the GM camouf for deletion, as it does not assert its own importance in the Gundam metaseries.—Ryūlóng (竜龍) 01:33, 10 January 2007 (UTC)

  • Note: This debate has been included in the list of Anime and manga-related deletions. -- SkierRMH 03:25, 10 January 2007 (UTC)
  • Delete No sources, no notability. Delete per nom. Edison 06:07, 10 January 2007 (UTC)
  • Delete per my nom in Wikipedia:Articles for deletion/AMX-104 R-Jarja. MER-C 06:52, 10 January 2007 (UTC)
  • Delete per my PROD nomination. Moreschi Deletion! 09:01, 10 January 2007 (UTC)
  • Delete Fails the relevant WP policies for this sort of thing (as stated by other users above). --Folantin 18:11, 10 January 2007 (UTC)
  • Keep While I suspect that I would vote for merge or delete were this article proposed singly, the sheer volume of recent nominations for deletion in this category makes the already short time to assess and/or improve said articles completely inadequate. Edward321 00:31, 11 January 2007 (UTC)
  • Keep pending a more reasonably organized deletion discussion. AfDs in this manner are in bad taste and wastes time on both sides. -- Ned Scott 06:44, 11 January 2007 (UTC)
  • Keep or redirect to MS-06 Zaku II. Redxiv 22:27, 12 January 2007 (UTC)
  • 'Merge into RGM-79 GM. —The preceding unsigned comment was added by Jtrainor (talk • contribs) 05:59, 14 January 2007 (UTC).
  • Keep If contents are arranged definitely, there is not a problem.--shikai shaw 07:26, 14 January 2007 (UTC)
  • Delete. Does not assert notability per Wikipedia:Notability (fiction)#Fiction in Wikipedia. A redirect would be fine, but a merge wouldn't be helpful as this is simply a Gundam guide (unencylcopedic). --maclean 07:21, 16 January 2007 (UTC)
