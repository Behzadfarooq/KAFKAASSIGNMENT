Arts & Crafts is an independent record label based in Toronto, Ontario, Canada. In January 2006 Arts & Crafts opened the Arts & Crafts Europe office in a partnership with City Slang Records.

[edit] Trivia

  • Arts & Crafts was founded by members of its flagship group Broken Social Scene.
  • The Most Serene Republic was the first band unrelated to Broken Social Scene to sign to Arts & Crafts.
