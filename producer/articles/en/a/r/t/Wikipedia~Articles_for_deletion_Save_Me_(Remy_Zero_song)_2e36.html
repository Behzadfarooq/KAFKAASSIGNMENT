[edit] Save Me (Remy Zero song)

Not only is the subject covered in The Golden Hum article, I believe that the use of lyrics is in violation of GFDL The Halo 10:23, 1 June 2006 (UTC)

  • Merge with The Golden Hum. Dismas|(talk) 10:33, 1 June 2006 (UTC)
  • Delete/Merge simply not notable enough.--Andeh 10:34, 1 June 2006 (UTC)
    • If a song that charts and acts as a theme song for a popular television show isn't notable enough, what is? --badlydrawnjeff talk 11:44, 1 June 2006 (UTC)
  • Keep, but clean the copyvio out. It's the theme song to Smallville, was a notable single for the band as well, reaching #27 on the Modern Rock charts. --badlydrawnjeff talk 11:23, 1 June 2006 (UTC)
  • Keep as per bdj - the song is arguably more notable than the album it appears on! But the copyvio needs removing and some more info about the song adding to make it a worthwhile article. Seb Patrick 11:26, 1 June 2006 (UTC)
  • Comment - article rewritten w/infobox, tracklisting, no copyvio. If an admin can clean the history out, that'd be great. --badlydrawnjeff talk 11:53, 1 June 2006 (UTC)
  • Not sure if I am allowed to do this as nom Keep In light of the recent changes by Badlydrawnjeff, this article should now be kept. *Slightly Red Face For Not Knowing All The Facts Before Nominating* The Halo (talk) 12:00, 1 June 2006 (UTC)
    Comment - Make your mind up The Halo.--Andeh 12:08, 1 June 2006 (UTC)
    Comment - Pretty sure you can do a Speedy keep under the rationale Nominator withdraws or something like that. I've seen it done before. Not sure if it applies when there are some delete votes in the discussion, though. Seb Patrick 12:21, 1 June 2006 (UTC)
    Comment As I have said on badlydrawnjeff's talk page, I was not aware that the song was so big in the US, and the recent changes were after I nominated it for deletion. At least the AfD made the artucle a lot better than it was. BTW, thanks Seb Patrick, I'll look into the speedy keep. The Halo (talk) 12:49, 1 June 2006 (UTC)
    Nah, the speedy keep is no good as there as already been delete votes. However, I think that since the copyvio has been removed and the article has recived attention, it is now a worthwhile article. The Halo (talk) 12:53, 1 June 2006 (UTC)
  • Keep, quite noteworthy Timb0h 13:59, 5 June 2006 (UTC)
