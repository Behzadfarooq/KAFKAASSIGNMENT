[edit] Morocco at the 2006 Winter Olympics

Morocco was on the competitors list at the beginning but the athletes didn't compete in the end. So the proposal was to delete this article. And a very similar case is Mexico at the 2006 Winter Olympics. Opened for suggestions. --Tone 19:50, 3 March 2006 (UTC)

Also Pakistan and Peru. Is this AFD about all these country pages?--Nitsansh 22:20, 3 March 2006 (UTC)
I haven't noticed these two. The Torino website states that there were some competitors from Peru, not sure about Pakistan. If the outcome will be Keep, I see no problem in keeping all of them. In fact, I am more inclined to keep myself. --Tone 11:55, 4 March 2006 (UTC)
  • Keep as notable (albeit not very successful). Mexico's an okay stub. Morocco should probably be updated with current information. I don't follow the Olympics. -- Krash (Talk) 20:15, 3 March 2006 (UTC)
  • I don't see any harm in keeping this per Krash. Sandstein 20:29, 3 March 2006 (UTC)
  • Keep it can be mentioned that it's athletes didnt' compete and why. 132.205.45.110 21:35, 3 March 2006 (UTC)
  • Keep this interesting article. It's part of an encyclopedic topic, I see no grounds for deletion. -Andylkl [ talk! | c ] 19:39, 4 March 2006 (UTC)
  • Delete as unencyclopedic. Eusebeus 19:55, 4 March 2006 (UTC)
  • Keep Update for actual happenings. Scranchuse 21:19, 4 March 2006 (UTC)
  • Weak keep per Andylkl. -- SonicAD (talk) 21:31, 4 March 2006 (UTC)
  • Delete. The "<country> at the 2006 Winter Olympics" pages (and all past Games' pages) ought to be reserved for countries that actually competed at them. The best place for this kind of content is the "<country> at the Olympics" (or "<country> at the Winter Olympics") pages, which provide overall history etc. See Australia at the Winter Olympics for a good example of this. Andrwsc 01:47, 9 March 2006 (UTC)
