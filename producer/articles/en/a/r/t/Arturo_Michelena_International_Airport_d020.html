Arturo Michelena International Airport (IATA: VLN, ICAO: SVVA) serves the fast-growing industrial city of Valencia in Venezuela two hours from Caracas (by car). It is an active airport but with few facilities, located at 10° 9' 29.46 North latitude, 67° 55' 36.31 W longitude.

The airport was named for Venezuela's famous painter, Arturo Michelena (1863 - 1918), who was born in Valencia.
