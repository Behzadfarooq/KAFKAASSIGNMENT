[edit] Mariah Stanley

Originally, this article was speedy deleted as a copyvio problem. Now, after arriving at DRV, it has been rewritten, but the subject is still a non-notable young lady. Although an IMDb entry exists, it is not extensive. Also, please note that the article was first begun by its subject, raising some WP:AUTO concerns. Xoloz 21:14, 30 December 2005 (UTC)

    • On IMDB, on a personal profile page, for the main/first list of "cast", the *first* year in brackets (displayed immediately afte the show name), is the year of the *show* not the year the person was on it. There is sometimes (not always) an additional note, which shows which year(s) the person was actually on the show. In such cases, both years will show. You'll see many examples of child stars, showing a credit on a show, with the only year displayed, being a year they weren't even born in. This isn't deception, it's just a default display option on imdb. Also, imdb does let in mistakes, but they do actually review changes before adding them (unlike Wikipedia). Feel free to update the article to make clear which version she was on, and how little airtime she had. --Rob 20:23, 31 December 2005 (UTC)|
      • Thanks for your comment Rob. I've revised my comment above for a more NPOV. —ERcheck @ 21:02, 31 December 2005 (UTC)
