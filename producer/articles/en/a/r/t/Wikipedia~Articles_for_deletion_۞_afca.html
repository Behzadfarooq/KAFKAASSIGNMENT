Yet another article about a single Unicode character. Unless something else can be said about it, it should be deleted or merged into a larger article, list, or table. See also Wikipedia:Articles for deletion/░ for a related AfD. --MarkSweep✍ 06:41, 16 September 2005 (UTC)
