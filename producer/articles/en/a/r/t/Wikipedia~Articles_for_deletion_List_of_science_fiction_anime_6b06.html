[edit] List of science fiction anime

Obsolete, entire list has been categorized into either Category:Science fiction anime or Category:Mecha anime. Deproded by Kappa with no reason given. --TheFarix (Talk) 13:40, 12 August 2006 (UTC)

  • Note: This debate has been included in the list of Japan-related deletions. -- TheFarix (Talk) 13:40, 12 August 2006 (UTC)
  • Keep, allows users to look for alternate names and is better structured than the categories. Kappa 13:54, 12 August 2006 (UTC)
    • Comment: Redirects should be used for alternate names so people can search using the built-in site search function. ···日本穣? · Talk to Nihonjoe 05:46, 18 August 2006 (UTC)
  • Keep, most things that have "list of" can be catgorized by category. This list is no different. It is a NPOV list so I see no problem with keeping it. Valoem talk 13:57, 12 August 2006 (UTC)
    Actually, there was a lot of anime that are not Sci-fi, such as Haibane Renmei and Peacemaker Kurogane, or may contain minor Sci-fi elements, such as ToHeart. To put it bluntly, the list was never really sourced and even when I was categorizing the list based on Anime News Network's database, there were disputes about ANN's accuracy and whether it was a reliable source. --TheFarix (Talk) 15:22, 12 August 2006 (UTC)
  • Keep - "entire list" is not in the catagory - I counted 12 red links. As with all lists, it could be improved to better take advantage of the list format, but that's another matter. LinaMishima 17:19, 12 August 2006 (UTC)
    Redlinks were moved to the centralized Wikipedia:Requested articles/Japan/Anime and Manga as part of the categorization process. --TheFarix (Talk) 18:10, 12 August 2006 (UTC)
  • Delete - For a structured list we have List of anime. For redlinks we have List of anime and Wikipedia:Requested articles/Japan/Anime and Manga. Since there are categories that duplicate the purpose of this list there is no need to have to maintain multiple> lists. Shiroi Hane 22:25, 12 August 2006 (UTC)
    • Comment: Actually, List of anime has been deleted (just a few days ago, in fact) and the various sub-categories of Category:Anime have taken over where the list left off. ···日本穣? · Talk to Nihonjoe 05:45, 18 August 2006 (UTC)
  • Delete, genre lists tend to fail behind easily and are more easily managed as categories. --SeizureDog 01:38, 13 August 2006 (UTC)
  • Keep: Lists and categories are complementary, not mutually exclusive. Dread Lord CyberSkull ✎☠ 15:30, 13 August 2006 (UTC)
  • Note: This debate has been added to the list of anime & manga deletions. Dread Lord CyberSkull ✎☠ 15:33, 13 August 2006 (UTC)
  • Keep as much more manageable and likely to be used than List of anime. ···日本穣? · Talk to Nihonjoe 19:38, 14 August 2006 (UTC) Changed, see below.
  • Delete as much less manageable than Category:Science fiction anime. What does this do that the category doesn't? Two things that I can see: hold redlinks (which are not supposed to be here, per the introduction), and contain cross-references for a handful of entries. I don't see that either justifies keeping a list that's even formatted to look like a category, largely duplicates a category, contains no additional information not present in the category for the vast majority of entries, and must be laboriously maintained, all the more so because the page's two-column design must be organised by hand. — Haeleth Talk 15:13, 16 August 2006 (UTC)
  • Delete Haeleth stole my answer and reformatted it in a way that makes perfect sense. --Kunzite 01:48, 17 August 2006 (UTC)
  • Delete Lists are only justified if they add real value to a category - this doesn't. BlueValour 23:40, 17 August 2006 (UTC)
  • I take that back. Delete per various arguments on this page (especially Haeleth's, above). ···日本穣? · Talk to Nihonjoe 05:45, 18 August 2006 (UTC)
  • comment The 'parent' list, List of anime, has actually been deleted now. Shiroi Hane 01:15, 19 August 2006 (UTC)
 AFD relisted to generate a more thorough discussion so that consensus may be reached.
 Please add new discussions below this notice. Thanks, TheFarix (Talk) 02:28, 20 August 2006 (UTC)
  • Comment — I frequently see that "red link" argument in favor of keeping lists. But the red links could just as easily be added to the category page as a bulleted list, and they're already on the list of requested articles page. Anything that links to this list can then be redirected to the category. — RJH (talk) 15:35, 20 August 2006 (UTC)
  • Delete per Haeleth's excellent argument. This is both redundant with the category and more difficult to maintain properly. Nandesuka 16:31, 20 August 2006 (UTC)
  • Delete per Haeleth. ~ trialsanderrors 00:32, 22 August 2006 (UTC)
  • Delete per nom. -- Ned Scott 12:37, 22 August 2006 (UTC)
  • Delete. Parent category deleted, and as pointed by Haeleth, there already is a category for such anime. No need for a redundant list. –NeoChaosX (talk | contribs) 20:03, 23 August 2006 (UTC)
