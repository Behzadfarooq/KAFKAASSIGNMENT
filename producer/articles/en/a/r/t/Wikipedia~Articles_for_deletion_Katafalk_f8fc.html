[edit] Katafalk

  • Delete: Non-notable band per WP:BAND guidelines. Only one full-length album as opposed to two recommended by WP:BAND. No mentions on either Amazon or Allmusic. Has a discography so I didn't speedy. —Wknight94 (talk) 03:22, 31 January 2006 (UTC)
  • Comment Not sure about this one. It might not be international notable, but seams to have at least some repercution in the netherlands, a decent google hits, and a nice web page. From WP:BAND: a national concert tour. Cons: band is only 3 years old. Mariano(t/c) 08:44, 31 January 2006 (UTC)
  • Weak keep, has/had gigs at multiple festivals in Netherlands, Germany and Belgium. The keep is weak, because they also performed at scores of non-notable venues. - Mgm|(talk) 14:02, 31 January 2006 (UTC)
  • Keep. Performing in different countries raises them far, far above the typical garage band we see here all the time. Turnstep 02:22, 1 February 2006 (UTC)
  • Weak keep as per Mariano. Seems worth keeping. -Tim Rhymeless (Er...let's shimmy) 08:53, 1 February 2006 (UTC)
