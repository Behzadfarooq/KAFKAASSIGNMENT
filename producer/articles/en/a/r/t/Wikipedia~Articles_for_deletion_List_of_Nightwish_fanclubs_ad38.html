[edit] List of Nightwish fanclubs

This is forked over from Nightwish, one of our featured articles, and makes it clear that this a list of official fanclubs of the band rather than unofficial fanclubs looking for free publicity. I'm not voting, but since this had been a speedy candidate I would like some other opinions on it. --Idont Havaname (Talk) 16:07, 3 March 2006 (UTC)

  • Keep its not doing any harm. But it must be getting late - I was just going to vote delete this List of Jewish Nightclubs. Defunkier 17:21, 3 March 2006 (UTC)
  • Delete. Wikipedia is not a mere collection of external links. -- Krash (Talk) 19:31, 3 March 2006 (UTC)
  • Delete, WP is not replacement for company website. Pavel Vozenilek 19:54, 3 March 2006 (UTC)
  • Keep. Given its ties to a featured article there is no logical reason to delete this. There is no reason to weaken our coverage of Nightwish. -- JJay 19:55, 3 March 2006 (UTC)
  • Delete. This would even be a valid speedy (not that I'm suggesting people should do this). It's not an article at all, it's a collection of links. Wikipedia is not a website directory. Friday (talk) 20:14, 3 March 2006 (UTC)
  • Delete, Wikipedia is not a web directory. Sandstein 20:51, 3 March 2006 (UTC)
  • Keep. Looking at the edit histories for both articles, this one appears to have been created to move the official fan clubs out of the main article, to de-clutter the page. Aren't improvements like that part of what makes a good article into a featured article? - Vary | Talk 23:08, 3 March 2006 (UTC)
  • Comment: Getting rid of clutter is good, yes. I believe that's why this article is on Afd. Friday (talk) 23:44, 3 March 2006 (UTC)
If the article's deleted, won't the content wind up right back in the main article? -- Vary | Talk 02:47, 4 March 2006 (UTC)
Not if people watch out for it and remove it when it happens. Undesired links can be removed from the main article without having to have a seperate article for them. Friday (talk) 07:16, 4 March 2006 (UTC)
  • Delete. Simply not encyclopedic. People should be able to find this info on the group's official website, which is appropriately linked to in the Nightwish article. dbtfztalk 23:56, 3 March 2006 (UTC)
  • Merge Belongs under ext link in the band article. Eivind 00:11, 4 March 2006 (UTC)
  • keep please there is no reason to erase it Yuckfoo 00:18, 4 March 2006 (UTC)
  • Delete as per Friday. MCB 03:22, 4 March 2006 (UTC)
  • Delete per Crash and Friday Cursive 23:24, 4 March 2006 (UTC)
  • Merge with Nightwish (place under external links) if these really are official fanclub. Otherwise, delete. —Ruud 05:38, 6 March 2006 (UTC)
