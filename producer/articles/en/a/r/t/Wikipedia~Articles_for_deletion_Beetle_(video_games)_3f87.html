[edit] Beetle (video games)

This article covers a very minor aspect of one level in Sonic Adventure 2, of which the stage itself does not even have a level. It can't be considered a character (such as one that could be listed in Minor characters in Sonic the Hedgehog because it has no personality and is essentially just a robot, a feature of the level.
