[edit] Guide to: Art Class

[edit] Main Plot

Polk's Art class is in debt, so the course is going to be canceled. To save it, they have an art show to raise money. Ned makes a Orange Naked Lady painting by mistake. It gets him in real trouble, but Mr. Hattorff, Polk's art teacher, tells him about painting and criticism. Soon, the painting gets the funding for art class. Cookie hires a bunch of 6th Graders to make keybowls for him for Christmas presents, but he drops one accidentally, causing the 6th graders to throw the keybowls around. Moze takes pictures of people without them knowing and exposes many disturbing facts, and she gets chased around school.

[edit] Trivia

  • This is the first episode to deal with something considerably "more adult": a naked woman.
  • When the kids are laughing at the painting when it's put in the trophy case, look closely and you can see it's only a big splash of orange.
  • Running gag: Evelyn slaps Ned across the face whenever she sees the painting.
  • This is the debut of Mr. Hattorff as a teacher, despite his appearance in "Guide to: Recycling."
  • There is a reference to Rudolph the Red Nosed Reindeer when one of Cookie's "elves" says "I want to be a dentist!"

[edit] Goofs

  • When Moze shows her pictures to Mr. Hattorff the art teacher, he looks at a picture of Cookie smiling in front of lockers. But when Moze took the picture, there were no lockers behind him.

[edit] Guide to: Lost and Found

[edit] Main Plot

Ned discovers a pair of signed Carmelo Anthony sneakers in the lost and found box, and attempts to make sure no one claims them before the end of the day, so they will become his. Moze tries to stop him, so he needs to figure out a way to make her quit. He tries to get back Moze's lost puppybear so she will forget about the sneakers. When he gets them, Ned finds out they are Seth's. He has to give them away by Anthony's orders. Cookie loses an MP3 player with a very embarrassing file on it, and needs to get it back before the entire school hears it. He thinks Loomer has it, but Crubbs has it. They try to figure out who owns the MP3 player, but Crubbs had the P.A. on, causing everyone to hear the embarrassing song.
