The article must not be edited by anyone connected with the company to avoid a conflict of interest. Company members can make suggestions on the talk page.
The article must illustrate notability within one of the guidelines: WP:CORP, WP:MUSIC, etc.
The notability must be sourced with verifiable references. Blogs, myspace, and links of that type are usually discounted as unreliable. Desired are major media notices, magazine articles, and references of that nature. IrishGuy talk 19:06, 6 January 2007 (UTC)
