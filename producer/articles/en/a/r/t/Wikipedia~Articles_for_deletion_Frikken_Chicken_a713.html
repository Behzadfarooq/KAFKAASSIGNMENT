  • 5 steps to a band vanity article, for maximum unverifiability:
  1. Band members known only by given names or pseudonyms – check.
  2. Vague band formation date – check.
  3. "Production problems" with albums, which are self-released, though it's never stated that they are – check.
  4. Band plans to do some touring or some recording or something, sometime in the near, or possibly distant, future – check.
  5. General air of mysteriousness – check.
