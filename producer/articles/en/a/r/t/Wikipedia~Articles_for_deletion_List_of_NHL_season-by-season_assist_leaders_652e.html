[edit] List of NHL season-by-season assist leaders

Listcruft. The article outright states that the NHL doesn't have an award for (or even honor) season assist leaders, as opposed to the goal-scoring and overall scoring leaders, so this isn't rather significant achievement (and most of the players posted on this usually won the scoring title anyway, so it's also redundant). Additionally, it should be stated that Wikipedia is not an indiscriminate collection of information. NeoChaosX [talk | contribs] 00:54, 7 October 2006 (UTC)

  • Delete, not encyclopedic. Akradecki 04:09, 7 October 2006 (UTC)
Anorak tendency listcruft of the most pointless kind. Delete doktorb wordsdeeds 09:35, 7 October 2006 (UTC)
