Artur Żmijewski (movie maker)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

For other persons named Artur Żmijewski, see Artur Żmijewski (disambiguation).

Artur Żmijewski (b. 26 May 1966 in Warsaw) is a Polish filmmaker and photographer. During the years of 1990-1995 he studied at Warsaw Academy of Fine Arts. He is an author of short video movies and photography exhibitions, which were shown all over the world.
