[edit] Moralityphobia

Little used neologism with only 23 hits on Google, created with the explanation "We'll see how long this POV article lasts, as opposed to , say, the homophobia article which has lasted for a long time". Falls under the policy "Do not disrupt Wikipedia to prove a point (or indeed for any other reason)". Warofdreams talk 16:49, 11 October 2005 (UTC)
