[edit] Hawkeye vodka

Unremarkable and unverified. Googled this and found about 200 references, most of which were reminiscing about drinking this while at college in Iowa. I reviewed the history and found that the entire article has been back and forth with a bunch of opinions and unverified silliness about whether this vodka is an inexpensive locally produced masterpiece or lighter fluid. Montco 03:35, 10 March 2006 (UTC)

  • Delete per nom Eivind 03:46, 10 March 2006 (UTC)
  • Delete nn alcohol phenomenon,Blnguyen | Have your say!!! 06:43, 10 March 2006 (UTC)
  • Keep. This page (and the number of Ghits) suggest it's the most purchased vodka in Iowa. Confirmed according to the liquor board of Iowa for Jan 2006: [1]. I think it makes local interest, despite silliness in the page's history -- Samir âˆ™ T C 10:23, 10 March 2006 (UTC)
  • Delete as unverifiable. --Terence Ong 15:17, 10 March 2006 (UTC)
  • delete per nom -- getcrunkjuicecontribs 19:58, 10 March 2006 (UTC)
  • Weak keep -- it's listed on List of vodkas, and other kinds of vodkas are listed there. Maybe Hawkeye vodka would go well with a Spanjo. --Elkman - (talk) 20:37, 10 March 2006 (UTC)
    • If you had checked, you would see that it was added to the List of vodkas by the same anon who created the article. Delete. Slowmover 20:49, 10 March 2006 (UTC)
      • Actually, I didn't go that far back to check. Given that it was created by the same anon who created the article, and they have only one other edit, it's not notable -- and it would go especially well with a Spanjo. I'm changing my vote to delete. --Elkman - (talk) 00:32, 11 March 2006 (UTC)
  • Keep. According to the Des Moines Resister (see [2]), it's the most popular (and cheapest) vodka in Iowa. That makes it notable enough, in my opinion. dbtfztalk 21:09, 10 March 2006 (UTC)
    • Comment. No denying it exists, so keep it on the List of vodkas, but there is no verifiable or significant content to this article except the fact of popularity in Iowa. That doesn't rate a whole page. Slowmover 21:14, 10 March 2006 (UTC)
  • Delete -- rotgutcruft. Haikupoet 04:41, 11 March 2006 (UTC)
