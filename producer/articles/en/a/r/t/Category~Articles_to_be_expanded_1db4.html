From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

This category is for articles that are beyond a stub, but still need to be expanded with additional information or details. These articles should be marked with {{expand}} at the top of the article's talk page. (Consensus is not yet reached on which is preferred. See discussion at Template talk:Expand and Wikipedia:Template locations.) Or add {{expandsect}} at the top of a section of the article that needs expansion. Comments on the additions required may be made on their talk pages.

See also: Wikipedia:Requests for expansion

[edit] By month

For browsing by month, see Category:Articles to be expanded by month. For all articles, see Category:All articles to be expanded.

[edit] By topic

Articles for expansion are automatically sorted by topic and appear on Wikipedia:Pages needing attention subpages.
