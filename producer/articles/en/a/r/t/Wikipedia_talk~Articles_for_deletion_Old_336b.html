[edit] Days Taken Off Early?

What's the correct thing to do if a day's been taken off the list of days with still-unclosed debates before all the debates are closed? At the moment, for instance, 27 September 2006 is no longer listed, but at least one discussion hasn't been closed yet. Do we relist the day as active? Post a notice on the AN and ask an admin to close the debate? What? The Literate Engineer 03:09, 9 October 2006 (UTC)

I think the best thing would be to relist the day on this page, and drop a note to whoever removed the log page asking them to be a bit more careful in future :) If one particular debate is really old then a note on AN might be appropriate, but please only use that for extreme cases as AN is crowded enough as it is. Petros471 10:12, 10 October 2006 (UTC)
Thanks for the advice. The Literate Engineer 02:58, 11 October 2006 (UTC)
