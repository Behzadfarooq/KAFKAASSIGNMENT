Non-notable website, fails WP:WEB. The article makes no claim to notability; the website itself claims about 400 members. AOL homepage, so no Alexa data. A few web directories but no non-trivial coverage available on Google. [1] [2] [3] Melchoir 01:04, 21 April 2006 (UTC)
