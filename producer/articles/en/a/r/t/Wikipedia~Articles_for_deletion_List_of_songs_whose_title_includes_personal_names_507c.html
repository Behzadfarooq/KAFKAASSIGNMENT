Subtrivial listcruft.

  • Delete. Unmaintainable list. --SuperDude 02:56, 2 October 2005 (UTC)
  • Keep Not an unmaintainable list And Many subtitles to this link to make sure and not as dumb of a list as the one a few articles up --JAranda | yeah 03:38, 2 October 2005 (UTC)
  • Keep - this is the kind of thing people are always looking for when they need to make a mix tape. -- BD2412 talk 03:53, 2 October 2005 (UTC)
  • keep interesting and already-well-maintained list. — brighterorange (talk) 04:14, 2 October 2005 (UTC)
  • Keep, valid list. User:Zoe|(talk) 04:22, 2 October 2005 (UTC).
  • Keep. Valid and useful information.--Centauri 06:28, 2 October 2005 (UTC)
  • Delete unmaintainable. --MacRusgail 13:23, 2 October 2005 (UTC)
  • Keep, it wouldn't have been created and maintained if it wasn't of interest. - SimonP 13:52, 2 October 2005 (UTC)
    • N.B.:When I said "unmaintainable", I meant that there are too many songs with personal names in them. The list could go on forever. --MacRusgail 14:57, 2 October 2005 (UTC)
  • Keep. I do not agree that this is unmaintainable. At worst, if it got too large, it could be subdivided into separate pages on specific names. --Jacquelyn Marie 16:47, 2 October 2005 (UTC)
  • Keep as per BD2412. Jkelly 17:22, 2 October 2005 (UTC)
  • Keep, it could get huge, but... I don't think it should be deleted. Some use to some people and harmless. gren グレン 21:12, 2 October 2005 (UTC)
  • Delete This is an encyclopædia, not an indiscriminate collection of information. --Mel Etitis (Μελ Ετητης) 22:17, 2 October 2005 (UTC)
  • Delete as yet more preposterous, unmaintainable listcruft. I'm awaiting the inevitable lists of songs including flavors, colors, days of the week, cities, countries, moods, types of belly-button lint, and pokemons. Apparently this seems to appeal to some editors, but I agree that Wikipedia is not an indiscriminate collection of information. MCB 23:07, 2 October 2005 (UTC)
