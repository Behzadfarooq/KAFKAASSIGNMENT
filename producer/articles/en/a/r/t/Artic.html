Artic

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Artic is a nickname used for

  • Semi-trailer trucks, an abbreviation of "articulated lorry" common in Great Britain and Ireland
  • Articulated buses, jointed buses that have a movable rear section to accommodate turns

Artic Software is the name of a defunct video games developer.

Additionally, "Arctic" is frequently pronounced "artic" and may be misspelled that way.
