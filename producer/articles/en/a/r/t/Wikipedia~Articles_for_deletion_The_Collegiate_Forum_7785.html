I believe that this is a vanity article on a non-notable subject. There are additional factors to consider:

  1. The origional author and major contributer of this article (Vermillian21) has only made two other types of contribution to wikipedia: (1) a copyrighted image that was included on this article, and (2) copy/paist articles currently under copyvio review.
