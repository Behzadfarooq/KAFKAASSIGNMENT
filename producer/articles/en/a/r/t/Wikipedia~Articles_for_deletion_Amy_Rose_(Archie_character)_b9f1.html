[edit] Amy Rose (Archie character)

Amy Rose (Archie character) (edit|talk|history|links|watch|logs) – (View log)

As if the Rouge article wasn't crufty enough, Amy is even more so! Amy is a minor character in the games, and an extremely minor character in the comics. She definitely does not warrant two articles. In addition to that, Amy's comic self is a direct clone of her game self...there's hardly any difference between the versions at all. I'm a devoted reader, and I can't think of any..of course, she's mostly background filler anyway.

Actually, the Sonic Character articles could use a good trimming in numbers. As for this one, everything that could be said here can just as well be said in Amy's game article. GrandMasterGalvatron 15:20, 27 February 2007 (UTC)

  • Merge to Amy Rose or very weak keep, same as the Rouge AfD above. Andrew Lenahan - Starblind 15:35, 27 February 2007 (UTC)
  • Please do be bold and do the merge. FYI, you don't need to go through AFD to merge, as the history stays. —siroχo 19:16, 27 February 2007 (UTC)
