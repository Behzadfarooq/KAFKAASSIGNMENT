[edit] Autoput

Autoput (edit|talk|history|links|watch|logs) – (View log)

Dicdef. There was only one highway at the time of SFR Yugoslavia, and it has its own article, as well as new motorways/freeways in successor countries. That makes the article basically a dicdef: "autoput is Serbian word for a highway, Croats call it autocesta and Slovenians avtocesta." I don't see any encyclopedic value. Duja► 13:38, 5 February 2007 (UTC)
