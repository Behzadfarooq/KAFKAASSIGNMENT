[edit] Tooheys New

NN. Local only. Jwissick(t)(c) 05:23, 29 September 2005 (UTC)

  • Keep - I don't like the stuff, but it's certainly notable, and "local only" is patently untrue. I live 3000km away from the brewery and it's readily available here. jmd 05:26, 29 September 2005 (UTC)
  • Very Strong Keep. It's a major beer in Australia, has sponsored major sports teams and events, and is the leading brand of Lion Nathan. --Apyule 06:46, 29 September 2005 (UTC)
  • Delete and expand in Tooheys#Beers. >: Roby Wayne Talk • Hist 06:49, 29 September 2005 (UTC)
THat would require a merge and redirect instead of a delete. - Mgm|(talk) 09:19, 29 September 2005 (UTC)
  • Keep. Very popular Australian beer. Far more Australians drink New than Foster's Lager which is mainly drunk in other countries. Capitalistroadster 06:52, 29 September 2005 (UTC)
  • Keep. Very popular Australian beer. Cnwb 06:59, 29 September 2005 (UTC)
  • Strong keep. Very notable beer. And while I hate to be nasty, if the nominator had done the slightest bit of research, it would've been abundantly clear that it was not a local beer. Ambi 08:00, 29 September 2005 (UTC)
  • Keep Well, it's local as in it's predominantly drunk in only one country, like - say - Millers. Notable (if not particularly good) beer. Grutness...wha? 09:06, 29 September 2005 (UTC)
  • Keep per above opinion by Australians. - Mgm|(talk) 09:19, 29 September 2005 (UTC)
  • Redirect to Tooheys. While it's the most common beer where I'm from, I don't believe there will be sufficient information available to make it worthy of its own article. - 3mta3 10:09, 29 September 2005 (UTC)
  • Keep It's a big part of the culture of Melbourne pub goers as it represents the Melboure/Sydney rivalry.
  • Keep Notable Australian beer brewed since the 1930s--User:AYArktos | Talk 11:07, 29 September 2005 (UTC)
  • Either keep or merge into Tooheys. - Andre Engels 12:01, 29 September 2005 (UTC)
  • Keep. Al 19:10, 29 September 2005 (UTC)
  • Keep. Popular product. Xtra 03:18, 30 September 2005 (UTC)
  • Keep. 220.253.12.86 08:28, 2 October 2005 (UTC)
  • Keep. Definitely not local only. Slac speak up! 09:40, 2 October 2005 (UTC)
