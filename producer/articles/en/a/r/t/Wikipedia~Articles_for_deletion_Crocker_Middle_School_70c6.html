  • Merge article does not meet the WP:SCHOOLS standard. Deletion of the article, as advocated by nom and JoshuaZ, when it can be the subject of a merge is pure destructive delionist vandalism. Alansohn 22:55, 5 November 2006 (UTC)Strong Keep By searching under "William H. Crocker Middle School" (nor just "Crocker Middle School"), I was able to confirm that the school has been honered by the Blue Ribbon Schools Program, the highest award granted to any school in the nation by the United States Department of Education. For all those who have constitently attempted to trivialize this award, Crocker has been recognized not once, not twice, not three times, but on four separate occasions,: in 1982-83, 1988-89, 1994-96 and again during the 2004-05 school year. As far as I know there are only three other schools nationwide that won the award four times from 1982-2002, and I don't see any winners after 2002 who had won three times before. This school is one of about five in the nation to be so recognized. Alansohn 00:44, 6 November 2006 (UTC)
