Art4Charity is a brand new charity scheme for Artists and craft workers. Its purpose is for artists and those of a creative nature to submit their art or craft so that their work can be sold for charity helping Mexico and those living in countries who live below the poverty level.

Its aim is to create an ongoing working scheme by giving a one-off donation to people who are good at art or had a craft that they can support themselves. The money raised by selling art of craft in England and Europe raises money to buy tools and equipment for people in poorer countries, so they can have a business to successfully run themselves and make the money to live on and continue in their Art or Craft work. For instance, a farmer, carpenter or Visual Artist through a one-off payment can buy their tools, enhance their business and still support the domestic needs. The hand outs that the church of charity workers give is a little amount so the people are just reliant on this and never get the satisfaction or the motivation for anything more.
