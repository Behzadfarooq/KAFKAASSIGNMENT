[edit] DIEGO LEON MONTOYA SANCHEZ

Wikipedia does not serve as a crime bulletin. -- Spring Rubber 09:56, 30 October 2005 (UTC)

  • Delete for reason listed above. Spring Rubber 09:56, 30 October 2005 (UTC)
  • Keep but Cleanup and move to a properly capitalized title. When I put it up for AfD, I didn't realize that this person was on the FBI's Top Ten Most Wanted List. --Spring Rubber 19:14, 30 October 2005 (UTC)
    • I’m glad you brought it to AFD. Otherwise I never would have seen it and had a chance to clean it up a little. Of course that’s not the purpose of AFD, but it worked out well. ♠DanMS 21:32, 30 October 2005 (UTC)
  • Delete, encyclopedias don't post wanted ads. What next, a missing kitten? — JIP | Talk 11:34, 30 October 2005 (UTC)
  • Delete as per nom. Keep and clean-up. Devotchka 16:17, 30 October 2005 (UTC)
  • Keep but clean up and fix the page title. This person is listed on the FBI Ten Most Wanted Fugitives page in Wikipedia. Of the ten persons on that page, six have individual pages in their own name. ♠DanMS 16:49, 30 October 2005 (UTC)
    • Note: I wikified this page somewhat but it still appears to be a direct lift from the wanted poster. If this survives AFD, it needs more info and needs to be moved to a page with the title not in all capitals. ♠DanMS 17:10, 30 October 2005 (UTC)
  • Keep per DanMS and rename. Karol 17:31, 30 October 2005 (UTC)
  • Keep and rename per DanMS. Thelb4 19:00, 30 October 2005 (UTC)
