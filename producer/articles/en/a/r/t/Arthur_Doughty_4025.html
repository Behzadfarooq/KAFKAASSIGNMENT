Sir Arthur George Doughty, FRSC (March 22, 1860 â€“ December 1, 1936) was a Canadian civil servant and Dominion Archivist and Keeper of the Public Records.

Born in England, he came to Canada in 1886 and in 1897 joined the Quebec public service. He was archivist from 1904 to 1935.

In 1927, he was awarded the Royal Society of Canada's Flavelle Medal.
