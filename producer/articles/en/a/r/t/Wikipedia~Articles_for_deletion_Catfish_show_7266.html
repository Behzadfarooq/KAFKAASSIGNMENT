Here we have a podcast with no proof that anyone actually watches it. Pilatus 15:45, 11 October 2005 (UTC)

    • If you prove that it's popular it will be kept. This is not the "Directory of random podcasts". Pilatus 16:02, 11 October 2005 (UTC)
  • Delete unless verifiably assertions of notability added CLW 16:19, 11 October 2005 (UTC)
  • Delete Podcruft. Adam Curry's are listed because he is a pioneer of podcasting and therefore notable. Catfish show is yet another vanity podcast of little notability. Even if it was one of the first ones, it doesn't predate The Daily Source Code. Podcasts are like Blogs: inherently non-notable unless the person(s) producing them are notable or there is something that sets them apart from the thousands of other crufty casts out there.--Isotope23 16:47, 11 October 2005 (UTC)

Feel free to delete it. 81.79.251.53 17:11, 11 October 2005 (UTC)ds81.79.251.53 17:11, 11 October 2005 (UTC)
