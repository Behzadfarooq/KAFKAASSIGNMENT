[edit] List of Taunton, Massachusetts businesses

wikipedia is not the yellow pages Hirudo 12:44, 21 April 2006 (UTC)

  • Delete. Yikes, where to start? Listcruft, Masscruft, a bunch of links alone, and pretty farkin' non-notable. RGTraynor 16:36, 21 April 2006 (UTC)
  • Delete per nom, and pretty much every sub-article linked there could have it's own AfD. I didn't check every single one, but there are alot that fall below WP:CORP from the looks of it.--Isotope23 19:12, 21 April 2006 (UTC)
  • Keep Wikipedia is a reference work, and things like this aren't always simple to compile from public databases. Best to leave it in the hands of locals. --AlexWCovington (talk) 01:15, 22 April 2006 (UTC)
  • Delete per my own comments on the discussion page. No other cities in Mass, much less the rest of the country or the world appear to have the honor of a page like this for them. Consider also that most of it is just a nice long list of red links. Unbelievably non-notable. Keeping this would invite someone to make an article of 'Names of people who live on my street'. 24.62.27.66 01:31, 22 April 2006 (UTC)
  • Delete, per nom and per list criteria at WP:NOT. See also: listcruft, redlinkcruft and cruftmagnet. Kappa really lets rip when he pulls up the current prods page, shame because it's otherwise a great system... Deizio 01:33, 22 April 2006 (UTC)
  • Keep Valid list; cited sources. Listcruft is not a criterion for deletion. Masscruft is not a criterion for deletion. Wikipedia asserts that it is not various things, but it does not assert that it is not the yellow pages. Fg2 01:52, 22 April 2006 (UTC)
  • Comment It does assert that it is WP:NOT for mere collections of external links, internal links or Internet directories. That's policy. There is zero context here, except for a (likely POV) labelling of some businesses as "prominent". Deizio 02:00, 22 April 2006 (UTC)
  • Keep, I don't think I would find a list of notable businesses founded in Taunton in the yellow pages. Kappa 01:55, 22 April 2006 (UTC)
    • Comment, I'm a bit perplexed how a list full of businesses that fail WP:CORP has any value at all and could be considered "notable". By what criteria? Also, list is "selective"... it is a completely arbitrary set of businesses picked by the author. Where is the local McDonalds? Doesn't Tauton have a Walmart? Local restaurants? What is so damn special about these particular businesses?--Isotope23 03:59, 23 April 2006 (UTC)
  • Comment The article doesn't claim or show notability for most of those businesses. Hirudo 04:01, 22 April 2006 (UTC)
  • Delete with extreme predjudice. Wikipedia may not assert that it's not the yellow pages, but it doesn't assert that it's not a fish or a bicycle either. -- RoySmith (talk) 01:16, 23 April 2006 (UTC)
  • Comment: No kidding; neither has Jimbo Wales personally asserted that Wikipedia is not explicitly for a subjective listing of some business that might be notable in a small Massachusetts city. There are just times when common sense should prevail over rules lawyering. RGTraynor 13:44, 24 April 2006 (UTC)
  • Delete, as above "wikipedia is not the yellow pages". San Saba 10:54, 23 April 2006 (UTC)
  • Delete, 'cruft and not notable.--Cini 16:35, 23 April 2006 (UTC)
  • Merge the first section on Taunton-based business into Taunton, Massachusetts, then delete the fluff. Sahasrahla 02:54, 24 April 2006 (UTC)
  • Delete per nom. Eusebeus 11:39, 24 April 2006 (UTC)
  • Strong delete per Isotope FloNight talk 10:59, 26 April 2006 (UTC)
  • Keep > Cleanup Could be useful if made NPOV. Startup account 20:03, 26 April 2006 (UTC)
  • Delete - what next - a description of every person living in Helions Bumpstead, Essex? - Runcorn 22:15, 26 April 2006 (UTC)
