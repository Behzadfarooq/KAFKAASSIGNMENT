  • Delete - despite the warning and the fact that it was "peer reviewed," it should be deleted. the article gives no credible assertion of notability and not much NPOV information. It certainly merits mention under the County Public Schools page but not its own article.Warfieldian 20:19, 24 February 2007 (UTC) Very Weak Keep - Based on other decisions regarding notability, Blue Ribbon Schools are considered notable. This school was recognized back in 1983 and does not have much other notability. Warfieldian 16:48, 25 February 2007 (UTC)
