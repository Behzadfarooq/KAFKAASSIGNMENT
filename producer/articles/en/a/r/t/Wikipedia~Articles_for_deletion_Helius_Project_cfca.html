[edit] Helius Project

Helius Project (edit|talk|history|links|watch|logs)

Article about NN web site that purports to allow visitors to communicate with an extraterrestrial who landed on Earth in 2003. About 130 ghits, mostly WP mirrors. Tubezone 22:36, 19 November 2006 (UTC)
