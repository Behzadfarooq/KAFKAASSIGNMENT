I do not contest that this *may* be a non-notable subject, which would then be worthy of deletion, but rather all I want is just to be given a list of specific things which would satisfy the requirement for notability and more than a few hours to go over it, as not all of us can sit around at 5am on wikipedia preventing ban-happy editors from going roughshod over their work.
