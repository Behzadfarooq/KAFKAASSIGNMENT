[edit] Adolf Hitler's contacts with Jewish people

Adolf Hitler's contacts with Jewish people (edit|talk|history|links|watch|logs)

Bundling these three dealings by Hitler with particular Jews under the title Adolf Hitler's contacts with Jewish people is a clear case of Original Research and therefor does not belong in Wikipedia. As one can see in the reference list none of the sources deal with contacts of Hitler with Jews in general. The three dealings should be and probably are included in the respective biography articles, the interaction with the doctor may also be included under possible sources for Hitler's antisemitism. The latter is also a clear secondary focus of the article. This lack of focus is another reason to delete it. gidonb 03:06, 2 January 2007 (UTC)
