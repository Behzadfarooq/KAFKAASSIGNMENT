[edit] Extra Space Storage and Extra space

Company fails WP:CORP, non-notable company. Adding both listings to this nomination Dipics 14:55, 6 September 2006 (UTC)

  • Extra Space Storage is one of the largest self storage companies in the Unietd States with over six hundred locations. They are a publicly traded REIT and are included in several prestigous REIT funds including the Vanguard Specialized REIT fund (VGSIX). —The preceding unsigned comment was added by Tradeconsult (talk • contribs) 2006-09-06 15:11:58 (UTC)
    • ... none of which has any bearing upon whether the company warrants an encyclopaedia article. Wikipedia is not a business directory. (That's Yellowikis's job.) It is an encyclopaedia. Please cite sources to demonstrate that our WP:CORP criteria are satisfied. The SLT article is a start, but we need more than just 1 article. Uncle G 16:08, 6 September 2006 (UTC)
  • Keep. I added some additional references to Extra Space Storage. I think that the article now passes WP:CORP. But the nominator could have found the same references with a quick search on Google News. I also changed Extra space to a redirect to Extra Space Storage. --TruthbringerToronto (Talk | contribs) 16:17, 6 September 2006 (UTC)
  • keep - article reads well to me, and company has over 31000 google hits - that seems notable to me. --mathewguiver 18:54, 6 September 2006 (UTC)

Thanks for the extra info. I was trying to be very conservative with the articles because a lot of what I found were re-prints of their own press releases. These guys are one of only two comapanies I could find that are using the REIT as a foundation to grow another business (self storage). The other company is a direct competitor Shurgard.

  • Delete Not notable enough, even with the extra info. Aristoi 21:20, 6 September 2006 (UTC)
  • Delete Out of those google hits, it is hard to find any that aren't stock market news (which is available for any publically traded company) and/or press releases. I just don't see this company as notable. Beaner1 18:44, 7 September 2006 (UTC)
  • Weak Keep Traded stocks mean some notability. User:Yy-bo 21:58, 7 September 2006 (UTC)
  • Keep. The other company with this business model, Shurgard Storage Centers, is equally interesting but is listed with no external references with the exception of their own website. tradeconsult14:28, 10 September 2006
  • Weak keep as per Yy-bo. – Alensha  talk 18:57, 11 September 2006 (UTC)
  • Weak keep as per Yy-bo. --Bill.matthews 15:36, 12 September 2006 (UTC)
