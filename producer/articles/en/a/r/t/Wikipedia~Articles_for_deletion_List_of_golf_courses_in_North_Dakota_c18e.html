[edit] List of golf courses in North Dakota

List of golf courses in North Dakota (edit|talk|history|links|watch|logs)

A list of non-notable golf courses in North Dakota. Nomination also includes the golf courses on the list:

  • Apple Creek Country Club
  • Apple Grove Grove Golf Course
  • Bois de Sioux Golf Course
  • Bully Pulpit Golf Course
  • Crossroads Golf Club
  • Devils Lake Town and Country Club
  • Edgewood Golf Course
  • Fargo Country Club
  • Garrison Golf Course
  • Grand Forks Country Club
  • Hawktree Golf Club
  • Heart River Golf Course
  • Jamestown Country Club
  • King's Walk Golf Course
  • Links of North Dakota at Red Mike Resort
  • Mandan Municipal Golf Course
  • Maple River Golf Club, Inc.
  • Minot Country Club
  • Osgood Golf Course
  • Oxbow Golf and Country Club
  • Pebble Creek Golf Course (not Pebble Beach)
  • Pheasant Country Golf Course
  • Prairie West Golf Course
  • Prairiewood Golf Course
  • Ray Richards Golf Course
  • Riverwood Golf Course
  • Rose Creek Golf Course
  • Souris Valley Golf Course
  • Westridge Golf Course

as well as the

  • North Dakota Golf Association,

which fails WP:ORG as a subnational organization not the subject of multiple, independent reliable sources. JChap2007 01:38, 16 November 2006 (UTC)
