This is not "original research"; it is "source-based research", and it is fundamental to writing an encyclopedia. What I am doing is source-based research. I am "collecting and organizing information from existing primary and/or secondary sources". Also, I was not finished. I had a bare outline from the the first sources I was passing through. My next step was to fix some of my wikipedia formatting (this is my first try) pass through the rest of the sources, flesh out the entries, and include a big section of criticisms of the concept.
