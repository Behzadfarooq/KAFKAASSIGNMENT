Arthur Korn

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Arthur Korn (May 20, 1870, Breslau - December 21/22, 1945, Jersey City) was a German born physicist of Jewish ancestry[1] and forerunner of the fax machine[2].

[edit] Literary works

  • Handbuch der Phototelegraphie, 1911
  • Bildrundfunk, 1926 (with E. Nesper)
