The Art Gallery of Sudbury is an art gallery in Greater Sudbury, Ontario.

Established in 1967 by the city's chamber of commerce, the gallery was located in the former mansion of William J. Bell, an early lumber baron in the city. It was originally known as the Laurentian University Museum and Art Centre, or LUMAC, and adopted its current name in 1997.
