Arturo Mercado

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

You have new messages (last change).

Arturo Mercado is a notable Mexican voice actor. He has dubbed a lot of TV and movie characters to Spanish. One of the most memorable in Hispanic community.

[edit] Work

[edit] Movie Characters

  • Voice of George Clooney in One Fine Day, "The Perfect Storm", "Batman & Robin" and the "Ocean's 11" and "Ocean's 12" movies
  • Tom Hanks in Catch Me if You Can
  • Dennis Quaid in The Parent Trap (1998 film)
  • Bert (Dick Van Dyke) in Mary Poppins (film)
  • Pedro (Sean Marshall) in Pedro y el Dragón Elliot
  • Walter "Radar" O'Reilly (Gary Burghoff) in H*M*M*C
  • Tex McCormick (Matt Dillon) in Tex
  • Rusty James (Matt Dillon) in Rumble Fish
  • Dally Winston (Matt Dillon) in The Outsiders
  • Jeffrey Willis (Matt Dillon) in The Flamingo Kid
  • John Bender (Judd Nelson) in The Breakfast Club

[edit] Animated movies

  • Beast from Disney's Beauty and the Beast (1991 film)
  • Adult Simba from Disney's The Lion King
  • Hamm from Toy Story
  • Gill (The scar face fish) from Finding Nemo
  • Wilbur (the pig) from Charlotte's Web
  • Pooh from Winnie the Pooh
  • Lugia from Pokémon: The Power of One

[edit] TV Characters

  • Walter "Radar" O'Reilly from H*M*M*C
  • Walter O'Reilly from W*A*L*T*E*R

  • Phineas Bogg from Voyagers!
