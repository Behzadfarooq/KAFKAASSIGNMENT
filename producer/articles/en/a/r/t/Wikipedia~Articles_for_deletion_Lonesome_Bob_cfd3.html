[edit] Lonesome Bob

Previously speedied (not by me), anon author complained at helpdesk, so I have restored and nominated for Afd instead. Gets some Google hits. No vote. -- Curps 04:22, 15 September 2005 (UTC)

  • Keep, but clean and wikify. Ive heard of the person once or twice, and google gets over 10,000 hits for the exact phrase. A cleanup with some interwiki links would help. Trjn 04:31, 15 September 2005 (UTC)
  • Maybe the people who know how to check a musician's notability can look into him, but Googling the name makes him seem noteworthy enough. I'll say keep (but cleanup and wikify, obviously). --Angr/tɔk tə mi 05:41, 15 September 2005 (UTC)
  • Keep and cleanup. The first Google hit for "Lonesome Bob" "things fall apart" is for allmusic, so that makes him notable. The second hit is a review by Robert Christgau, so definitely notable. User:Zoe|(talk) 05:46, 15 September 2005 (UTC)
  • Keep Ive heard of him, my folks listen to a lot of country and have his CD --Cloveious 05:48, 15 September 2005 (UTC)
  • Keep expand and cleanup. According to Allmusic.com see [1] he has recorded two albums and a national tour as well as playing at the Grand Old Opry in Nashville and opened for Dwight Yoakam in Austin. Capitalistroadster 05:55, 15 September 2005 (UTC)
  • Keep and cleanup. --Andy Janata 15:26, 15 September 2005 (UTC)

Keep and expand.

  • Keep and expand: meets WP:MUSIC as verified by CR. Barno 23:41, 15 September 2005 (UTC)
  • Keep for the reasons stated above. obviously needs some work, but look at all the other articles that do! Adz 07:39, 16 September 2005 (UTC)
