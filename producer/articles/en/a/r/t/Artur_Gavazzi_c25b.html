Artur Gavazzi (October 14, 1861 â€“ March 12, 1944) was an ethnic Italian geographer.

Gavazzi was born in Split (then in Austria-Hungary) and died in Zagreb (then in the Independent State of Croatia).

Gavazzi was the first professor for Geography at the University of Ljubljana where Anton Melik succeeded him. Gavazzi went in 1928 to the University of Zagreb.
