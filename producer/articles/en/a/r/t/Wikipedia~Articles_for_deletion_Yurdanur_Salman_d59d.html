  • Delete - Tough one. First of all she is not the same person as Ildan, let me clear that. She is a well-known professor of Bogazici University, one of the best universities in Turkey. She is also a translation guru, one of the best translators this country has ever seen (imho, of course). She is far more famous than Mehmet Murat İldan, that i can say without hesitation. But, i don't see translation works and academic career as a sign of notability. Because of that i'm for deleting this article, although i voted "Keep" for İldan, the self-promoting one. I know it's not fair, but what is?--Vito Genovese 18:32, 27 January 2007 (UTC)
