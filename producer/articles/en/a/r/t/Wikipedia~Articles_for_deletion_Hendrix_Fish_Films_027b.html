[edit] Hendrix/Fish Films

Non-notable film production group that is apparently just two friends. From the article: "Get a camera, get some friends and shoot a film, forget about catering budgets." You should probably forget about warranting a wikipedia entry for the moment as well. Eusebeus 22:20, 30 August 2006 (UTC)

Doesnt really fail any notability guidelines and can be cleaned up and rewritten (which does need doing).Might get round to a clean tomorrow if i have time --Errant Tmorton166(Talk)(Review me) 23:37, 30 August 2006 (UTC)
  • Delete - does not meet notability criteria of WP:CORP. In attempting to verify, I got 10 unique GHits. Only 8 were in English. Of those, 4 were Wikipedia or mirrors and 1 was a MySpace page. The remaining 3: the IMDB page for The Killbillies, the award page for Horror Dance Film Festival, and a mention in the newsletter page for the Trasharama touring film festival. Not quite "credible, third-party sources with a reputation for fact-checking and accuracy" per WP:V. --Satori Son 21:55, 9 September 2006 (UTC)
