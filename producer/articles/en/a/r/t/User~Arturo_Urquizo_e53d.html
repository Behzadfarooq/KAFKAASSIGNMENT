[edit] Profile

Arturo Urquizo (né Arturo Urquizo Valdés, June 25, 1985, in Lerdo, Durango) a.k.a. Turok, is an average guy residing in Mexico. He was born at the Centro de Salud de Ciudad Lerdo (Health Center of Ciudad Lerdo) at 10:00 AM. He actually attends the Instituto Tecnológico de La Laguna (Institute of Technology of La Laguna), having certain knowledge on programmable automation, materials science, mechanism simulation and computer programming. He is finishing his B.D. in 2007. He is also interested in Chemistry, Polish language and web design. Both of his parents have Spanish roots, more precisely, Basque roots. His mother tongue is Spanish, followed by a regular level of English and French

[edit] Persona

Arturo tends to be a rather conservative guy in different fields. He is a non-smoker and a non-drinker, not even socially. Nonetheless, he has an accentuated addiction for fast and delicious food, turning him into a gourmand. He dislikes sports which involve any kind of compete-and-win (including soccer), excepting jogging. His clothing is informal, based on shirts and jeans. He enjoys hanging around with friends, from going to the theater to BBQ's. He does not like at all going to clubs.

[edit] Locomotion

He owns a blood-red Ford Fiesta Mk IV with a 1.4L Zetec engine. It is equipped with a Sony CDX-GT200 head unit, a pair of JBL GTO-936 full-range speakers at the rear and a pair of JBL P650c component speakers inside the front doors. Over the tailgate, there will be 3 bumper stickers, resembling his passion for japanese animation, videogames and technology. There's a sticker for country identification, which remarks his love for Poland.

[edit] Music

Arturo has a peculiar interest in European Pop bands of the 80's, which is sometimes criticized by his friends who prefer Banda music. Some of them include a-ha, ABBA, Culture Club, Cyndi Lauper, Duran Duran, Mecano, among others. He has also learnt to cope with contemporary music and certain artists have won his appreciation such as Alizée, Matt Costa, Shakira, t.A.T.u. and Gwen Stefani.
