[edit] List of TV networks that air The Amazing Race

There is no reason for this "article to even exist". The information should be part of the article about The Amazing Race and not an out of context list. I placed the information from this list in the main article where it belongs. This article violates Wikipedia:What Wikipedia is not#Wikipedia is not a directory and Wikipedia:What Wikipedia is not. Displaced Brit 03:56, 19 August 2006 (UTC)

  • Delete per nom: the involved networks could be included in a subcategory of Category:The Amazing Race. TrackerTV (CW|Castform|Green Valley) 04:20, 19 August 2006 (UTC)
  • delete if this were kept it would generate a horrible precedent to spawn thousands of nonsensical lists Anlace 05:05, 19 August 2006 (UTC)
  • Merge most american sitcoms or serial's articles do have the network in which it is aired in foreign countries.--Ageo020 05:37, 19 August 2006 (UTC)
  • Redirect to The Amazing Race, purely for GFDL reasons as the list was merged into that article, where it probably belongs. BryanG(talk) 05:39, 19 August 2006 (UTC)
  • Merge back into The Amazing Race in prose form. (For example, it shouldn't take seven separate lines to list all the places where AXN airs this program.) --Metropolitan90 06:10, 19 August 2006 (UTC)
  • Keep. No need to merge contents into the main article; its inclusion exceeds recommendations from Wikipedia:Article size. Note that other television shows and televised events also have a separate article on international broadcasters (Broadcasting of The Simpsons, List of Smallville broadcasters and home video releases, Live 8 broadcasters, etc. --Madchester 15:59, 19 August 2006 (UTC)
  • Keep per above reasoning. --CFIF â˜Ž 19:41, 19 August 2006 (UTC)
  • Delete as proposed. Broadcasting of The Simpsons is a prose article, not a list. I'll be glad to nominate the Smallville list. Gazpacho 22:07, 19 August 2006 (UTC)
  • Keep, or failing that, merge into The Amazing Race. A list of foreign airings of a US TV show is enyclopedic. Kirjtc2 23:20, 19 August 2006 (UTC)
  • Delete outright, no merge, no redirect. If someone wants to include the info in the main article, go crazy. If there's not enough room, then the article was too fracking big to begin with and should be trimmed. --Calton | Talk 03:54, 20 August 2006 (UTC)
  • Merge with the main TV series article, which appears to be the general rule of thumb for this type of list. 23skidoo 02:12, 21 August 2006 (UTC)
  • Keep - this is separate from The Amazing Race for pure size reasons. Please look at the article you're proposing to merge it into - there's not much room! Radagast 15:59, 21 August 2006 (UTC)
