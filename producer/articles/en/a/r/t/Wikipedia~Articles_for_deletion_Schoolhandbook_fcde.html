[edit] Schoolhandbook

Schoolhandbook (edit|talk|history|links|watch|logs) – (View log)

Article about a supposedly popular website giving funny hints to students. There is no indication that the site is that popular, if wikipedia had every little website out there the site would be huge. Borjon22 02:04, 6 April 2007 (UTC)

  • Keep. Notable website. Lorty 02:05, 6 April 2007 (UTC)
  • Delete. No reliably-cited assertion of notability other than a generic and weak-at-best statement that it is "a semi-popular website". Seems to fail WP:WEB badly. DMacks 02:41, 6 April 2007 (UTC)
  • delete unless sources towards meeting WP:WEB are shown to exist. I'm not seeing any: [1], [2]. --W.marsh 02:56, 6 April 2007 (UTC)
  • Delete Even if the site was notable the article is not really even a stub. Also the site itself isn't notable for a number of reasons. Anynobody 03:23, 6 April 2007 (UTC)
  • Delete per nom. Michael 04:04, 6 April 2007 (UTC)
  • Delete - Non notable web site--$UIT 04:06, 6 April 2007 (UTC)
  • Delete Unless I'm missing something here, this is a blog with one entry over two years ago. Clearly not notable. Maxamegalon2000 05:01, 6 April 2007 (UTC)
  • Delete NN, but more over, not useful. If you forgot your pencil, look on the ground for one? Shenme 05:52, 6 April 2007 (UTC)
  • Delete as a non notable website. CattleGirl talk | sign! 07:25, 6 April 2007 (UTC)
  • Delete per nom. SkipSmith 08:17, 6 April 2007 (UTC)
  • Delete per nom. —dima/talk/ 18:16, 7 April 2007 (UTC)
  • Speedy delete - no assertion of notability. So tagged. MER-C 11:45, 8 April 2007 (UTC)
