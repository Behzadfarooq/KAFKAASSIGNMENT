[edit] Reed College Co-op

This is a non-notable college co-op. At best it should be merged with Reed College, but I don't think it even merits that, frankly. Delete JDoorjam 19:24, 21 December 2005 (UTC)

  • Question: how common are these? It's an interesting concept for a dorm that they didn't have when I was in college. If this is the only one, it's worth keeping. If it's a regular thing on college campuses, then this can be deleted. -MisterHand 20:39, 21 December 2005 (UTC)
    • There were a few at my alma mater... I don't think it's uncommon JDoorjam 21:24, 21 December 2005 (UTC)
  • Delete - fascinating as the cheese-purchasing habits of college students is... Barneyboo (Talk) 21:26, 21 December 2005 (UTC)
  • Delete seems similar to the "scholarship halls" at my university, and a common setup elsewhere. The only possible expansion would be vanity. (ESkog)(Talk) 07:13, 22 December 2005 (UTC)
  • Comment: Please note that I created this page to get this crap off of the main Reed College page. It's short-term, trivial, and inane, but who am I to say it doesn't belong on Wikipedia? I don't care, but if it starts showing up on the Reed page again, I'll stuff it over here. -- Gnetwerker 07:33, 22 December 2005 (UTC)
    • Its not belonging on Reed College, if anything, seems to reinforce the idea that it's not notable content. If it shows up on Reed's page again, just... delete it. JDoorjam 18:17, 22 December 2005 (UTC)
  • Delete per nom. Stifle 00:49, 26 December 2005 (UTC)
