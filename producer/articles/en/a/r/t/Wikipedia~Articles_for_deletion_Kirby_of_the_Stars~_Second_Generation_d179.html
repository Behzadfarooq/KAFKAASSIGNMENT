[edit] Kirby of the Stars: Second Generation

This is fancruft. Google search turns up 0 results, and a video game fan fiction created by a fan on a fan forum doesn't seem to satisfy WP:CSD A7 or WP:NN to me. Also violates WP:WEASEL and WP:NPOV. I originally PRODded the article, but the IP that's been editing the article promptly removed it, so I put it up at AFD. Green451 00:27, 20 July 2006 (UTC)
