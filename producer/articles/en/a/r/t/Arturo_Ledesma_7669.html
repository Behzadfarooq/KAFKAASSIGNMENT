Javier Arturo Ledesma (Born on May 25, 1988), is a Mexican Footballer currently playing for both the Mexican Primera División A team Tapatío and for Mexican Primera División's Chivas de Guadalajara as a Defender.

Arturo is a young player that shows a lot of potential. At only 19 years of age he has already debuted with Mexican giant Chivas de Guadalajara in the Clausura 2007 Tournament as well as in the CONCACAF Champions' League. However he can be found getting more playing time with Chivas' Primera División A team Tapatío. Arturo has been training under the Chivas Organization since the 2005-2006 season where he played for Chivas Coras. He quickly climbed through the ranks and can now be found as a regular for Tapatío and as a substitute on Chivas' bench.
