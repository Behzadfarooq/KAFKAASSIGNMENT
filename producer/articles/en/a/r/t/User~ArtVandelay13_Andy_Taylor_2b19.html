Andy Taylor (born September 17, 1982 in Exeter) is a professional footballer currently playing for English Conference club Exeter City.

Taylor, a midfielder, was initially a Manchester United trainee, but was released in 2002 without playing for the first-team. While at Manchester United he suffered an injury to his cruciate ligament that kept him out for a year, limiting his chances with the Premiership club. Taylor then moved to Northwich Victoria where he featured in 31 first team games during the 2002–03 season.

On 21 October 2003, Taylor moved on a free to his hometown club, Exeter City. Since his arrival, Taylor has played in over 80 league matches for the Grecians, mostly as a right winger, although he has also played as a central midfeilder and right back. On 8 January 2005, Taylor played in a 3rd round FA Cup tie against Manchester United, a fixture that not only represented Taylor's return, but his first ever match at Old Trafford. Taylor has also represented England at semi-professional level, for the National Game XI.
