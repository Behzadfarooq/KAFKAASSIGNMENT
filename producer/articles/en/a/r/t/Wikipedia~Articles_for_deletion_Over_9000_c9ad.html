[edit] Over 9000

Over 9000 (edit|talk|history|links|watch|logs) – (View log)

Non-notable internet meme based around a YouTube video. Dave6 05:19, 5 February 2007 (UTC)

  • Please delete! This article is hardly encyclopedic. Bryanw03 05:21, 5 February 2007 (UTC)
  • Delete. Non-notable meme. (Note: I reformatted Bryanw's opinion to conform with Afd conventional usage.) YechielMan 05:27, 5 February 2007 (UTC)
  • Delete: It's OVER NINE THOUSAND!!!!!! a non notable Internet meme that really has no place in Wikipedia. .V. [Talk|Email] 05:46, 5 February 2007 (UTC)
  • Delete. The number of Google hits for "It's Over 9000" and "It's Over Nine Thousand" is not over 9000. No sources provided. In short, non-notable internet meme, as stated by nom. --N Shar 06:02, 5 February 2007 (UTC)
  • Delete per discussion. --Dennisthe2 06:20, 5 February 2007 (UTC)
  • Delete in the spirit of WP:NFT. This meme lacks any verification to indicate actually significance.-- danntm T C 17:43, 5 February 2007 (UTC)
  • What Nine Thousand?! I mean Delete It's on the List of Internet phenomena page (I put it there) and that's the only place it should be at. JuJube 19:29, 5 February 2007 (UTC)
  • Delete OR/COI wrapped up in an allegedly notable meme. Only one of the first ten ghits [1] is relevant and that is the video itself. No assertion of notability and no independent reliable sources cited. -- IslaySolomon | talk 19:42, 5 February 2007 (UTC)
  • Delete per nom. SakotGrimshine 01:07, 6 February 2007 (UTC)
  • It's OVER NINE THOUSAAAAND!!!!. Also delete. No reliable sources... yet.... --- RockMFR 02:54, 7 February 2007 (UTC)
  • Delete. If we kept this, we might as well start cataloging YTMND videos to boot. RFerreira 08:25, 7 February 2007 (UTC)
  • Delete it’s not been reported by any reliable sources. And until that happens, as much as i love over 9000, it has no right to an article El cid the hero 16:59, 7 February 2007 (UTC)
  • Delete. Internet meme which is choking on non-notability, no reliable sources. (jarbarf) 18:29, 7 February 2007 (UTC)
  • Delete. The scouter says that this article's power level is not high enough to overcome WP:WEB. If deleted, expect OVER NINE THOUSAND sockpuppets to clamor for its return. Thunderbunny 00:38, 8 February 2007 (UTC)
