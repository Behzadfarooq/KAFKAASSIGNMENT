[edit] Introduction

Arthur Benson Tourtellot (July 23rd, 1913 – Oct 1977) was an American writer, screenwriter and producer best known for the book Lexington and Concord

[edit] History

Arthur Benson Tourtellot was born July 23rd, 1913. He was a fellow at Middlebury College in 1938 and was at a writer's conference at the Bread Loaf Campus in 1941. He served in the U.S. Coast Guard during World War II. At the end of the War he served as the Associate Producer of two March of Time documentaries. The first, Crusade in Europe was based on Dwight D Eisenhower's book of the same name. He died in October 1977.
