Artisan Park is one of the neighborhoods located in Celebration, Florida. It is located at the end of Celebration Ave., being a literal dead end of the road.

Artisan Park is an upscale area of Celebration including homes, townhomes, and a condo complex. There is also a private Artisan Club with restaurant, health club, locker room, hot tub and heated pool.

[edit] Artisan Park Real Estate

There is a variety of housing in Artisan Park. There are 8 condo buildings located at the dead end of Celebration Avenue. They are 3-5 story buildings which have include 2-3 bedroom condos with 1 car garages. There are also townhomes closer to the Artisan club which have 2 car garages and courtyard backyards as well as single family homes which vary from small cottage homes to large million dollar estates. All homes, condos, and townhomes in Artisan park share the Artisan Club, a clubhouse which includes a heated resort-style pool which includes shaded cabanas, an outdoor kitchen, and outdoor fireplace. Inside the club are pool tables, workout facility, meeting facilities, lockerrooms, seating areas in front of a fireplace and/or flat panel televisions, as well as a bar/restaurant.

Many homes/townhomes/condos are sitting empty in Artisan Park due to a large amount of investors trying to cash in on Florida's once rapid growing population, real estate prices, as well as taking advantage of 'Celebration's last housing development'. The current investors have run out of people to flip these homes to.
