[edit] Jay Wile

Speedy delete. This article is centered around a non-notable person. Also delete Apologia Educational Ministries. SycthosTalk 01:49, 18 January 2006 (UTC)

  • Do not delete: As the primary author of this article, I can definitively state that Dr. Wile is quite notable inside the Christian homeschooling community. His textbooks are used by many families as their means of science instruction, and easily reaches an audience greater than 5,000 people (as per Wikipedia's "notoriety" policy). If his significance as an author is related to the number of textbooks he has published, there are currently ten; including three Advanced Placement courses. And lastly, I can assure you that this article is not autobiographical. :-) LAATi88 18:09, 18 January 2006 (UTC)
  • Delete. Appears non-notable outside of his Apologia thingamabob. I don't like the idea of having every last company CEO or whatnot with their own article. However, that thingamabob is mildly notable inside of Christian whatchamacallit circles. -- Consumed Crustacean | Talk | 01:53, 18 January 2006 (UTC) (Revised -- Consumed Crustacean | Talk | 02:20, 18 January 2006 (UTC))
  • Delete -- article has a promotional tone and doesn't assert that Wile is notable even within Christian whatchamacallit circles. Catamorphism 01:57, 18 January 2006 (UTC)
  • Speedy Delete, non-notable, self-promoting, possibly autobiographical. Will 02:05, 18 January 2006 (UTC)
  • Delete, speedily if possible, although the claim that he published a couple of textbooks may be notable. -- Saberwyn -02:58, 18 January 2006 (UTC)
  • Speedy Delete Per above. SWD316 talk to me 03:24, 18 January 2006 (UTC)
  • Speedy Delete as above. Blnguyen 04:26, 18 January 2006 (UTC)
