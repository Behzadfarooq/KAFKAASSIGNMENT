[edit] List of Roman Catholics

This is an overly broad list, which leads to significant incompleteness in each subcategory. Many more narrowly defined lists of Catholics exist - see Category:Roman Catholics. Should be deleted as a list of loosely associated topics, or made a redirect to Category:Roman Catholics or some other such article. NoSeptember 17:58, 24 July 2005 (UTC)
