Arthur Thomas (footballer)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Arthur Thomas is a football player who played for Linfield in the Irish Premier League. He is Linfield's joint top scorer in European competition, with five goals in the European Cup.Currently living in Sydney, Australia where has lived since the early seventies, he has played for a number of Australian clubs including Pal Helennic. Returned to teaching sport, after finishing playing football. Now happily retired.
