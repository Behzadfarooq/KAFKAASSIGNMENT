[edit] Dell XPS

[*] Keep Dell's XPS is the ultimate gaming computer. I like to read about its updates and history. I think it deservers its own page rather than being crammed into the all-one Dell page. —The preceding unsigned comment was added by 71.192.228.85 (talk • contribs) 17:47, 14 October 2005.

Keep -- Dell's finest line of gaming computers are the XPS. They are like PCs to Microsoft. Please keep this article up. It is helping a lot of people learn. —The preceding unsigned comment was added by 71.195.155.206 (talk • contribs) 08:11, 14 October 2005.

Delete--Dell's products are non-notable and will not be significant 100 years from now Fsdfs 23:03, 13 October 2005 (UTC)

  • Keep Samboy 00:31, 14 October 2005 (UTC)
  • Redirect as per  BD2412 talk. Dlyons493 Talk 03:46, 14 October 2005 (UTC)

  • Keep. Dell's products are notable.--Nicodemus75 04:31, 14 October 2005 (UTC)
  • Keep. There would be overflow on the Dell, Inc. page.
