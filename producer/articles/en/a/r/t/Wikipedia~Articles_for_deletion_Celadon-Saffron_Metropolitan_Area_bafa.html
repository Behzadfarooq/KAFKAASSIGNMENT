[edit] Celadon-Saffron Metropolitan Area

All the information in this article can be found in the articles Saffron City and Celadon City--with much more information too. Additionally, a Google search for Celadon-Saffron Metropolitan Area returns only 115 results, many from mirrors of or links to this Wikipedia article. WindFish 07:58, 10 October 2005 (UTC)
