  • No, we do not. Delete or possibly redirect to the snake pit that is List of Internet slang. Can't sleep, clown will eat me 08:55, 6 May 2006 (UTC)
  • Delete, do not redirect (as it isn't a likely search term) and do not transwiki this crap non notable neologism to Wikt, we don't want it. --Rory096 09:14, 6 May 2006 (UTC)
