Sure, I agree that there should be better sources. That's a reason to clean-up, not to delete. BTW, the word conclusion is not used in WP:OR at all. There are times when conclusions are necessary to write good articles. Mister.Manticore 17:43, 12 December 2006 (UTC)

I'm afraid I must disagree. If we can see lots of uses of the term Redshirt, then clearly it's not a novel idea unique to Wikipedia, or any one person's cause. My advice remains the same, look for sources, don't argue for deletion. Mister.Manticore 19:39, 12 December 2006 (UTC)
