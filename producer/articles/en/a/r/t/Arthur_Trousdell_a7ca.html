Arthur Trousdell (born April 21, 1981) in Lower Hutt, New Zealand, is a professional basketball player. He played for CB Villa de Los Barrios in LEB in Spain in 2006. Currently playing for the Hawkes Bay Hawks.

Arthur Trousdell attended Hastings Boys High School. He has been a member of the New Zealand U/18 and U/20 national teams.He was a member of the New Zealand Tall Blacks squad for 2005.

He played for the Hawkes Bay Hawks 2006 champions of the New Zealand NBL.

He attended Southwest Baptist University playing in the MIAA conference from August 2002 until May 2004. He transferred to Cameron University for his junior in August 2004 and completed in the Lone Star conference. He graduated with a BSc in Physical Education in May 2006.
