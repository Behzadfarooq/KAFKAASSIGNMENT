[edit] Lucy Parker (transsexual)

Lucy Parker (transsexual) (edit|talk|history|links|watch|logs)

I turned down a speedy deletion request on this page a while back. After looking closely at the article though, this page is edited heavily by a User:Lucy Parker and the info in the article is overwhelmingly trivial and/or unverifiable. Whether or not one-time subjects of TV shows are worthy of inclusion has been up for debate many times before on this forum, with mixed results. However, I think we can agree that a subject mentioned in one press release (that doesn't include Lucy's last name) for an upcoming show cannot meet WP:BIO. Delete pending more coverage by reliable sources. Grandmasterka 04:01, 27 December 2006 (UTC)
