Arthur Treadwell Walden

Arthur Treadwell Walden (b. 10 May 1871, d. 26 March 1947) was a Klondike Gold Rush adventurer, dog driver and participant in the first Byrd Antarctic Expedition. He is also known as an author and developer of the Chinook sled dog breed.
