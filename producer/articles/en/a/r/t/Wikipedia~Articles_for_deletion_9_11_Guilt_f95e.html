[edit] 9/11 Guilt

This 9/11 conspiracy video is not notable. Searching the term on Google [1] yields only 338 results. It's also nowhere to be found on IMDB. This compares with other articles, 9-11: The Road to Tyranny, 911 In Plane Site, and Loose Change (video), which are all listed on IMDB and google return thousands of search results. --Aude (talk contribs) 16:21, 15 August 2006 (UTC)
