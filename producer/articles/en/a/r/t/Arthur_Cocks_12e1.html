Arthur F Cocks was an Australian cricket Test match umpire.

He umpired one Test match between Australia and England at Adelaide on 2 February to 8 February 1951. Australia won comfortably with Arthur Morris scoring 206, Jim Burke scoring a century in his first Test, and Bill Johnston taking 7 wickets. Cocksâ€™ partner in his only match was Andy Barlow.
