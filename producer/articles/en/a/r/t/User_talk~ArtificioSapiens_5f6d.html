I had edited your Artificial Being page. Consider it a scaffold from which to work.

Some notes:

1) the wiki is an encylopedia. articles should include a clear definition in the first sentence! follow that up with some history and a detailed description, as appropriate.

2) don't capitalize names of articles, unless it's a proper noun (someone's name for instance). You should use the Move this page function to move it to "artificial being" (note the caps).

3) there's a meta-wiki for longer discussions like what you were doing in the original article. the pages here should be "clinical", void of emotion. Yes, that might sound a little odd, but that's why they have the meta-wiki for just that purpose.

Anyway, enjoy!

Oh, the | is above the Enter key on US keyboards. It is typically shown as two short vertical lines, for historical reasons.
