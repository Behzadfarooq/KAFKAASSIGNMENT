Wikipedia is not a crystal ball. This page was deleted before with the same amount of information. http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wikipedia:Articles_for_deletion/Silent_Hill_5 Even if there is an article where developers cite plans to create the game, right now it is too early in the cycle to even be worthy of mention. The expected release dates are still TBA and even those are subject to rumors. Without more solid information available I recommend DELETE. TotalTommyTerror 18:15, 22 December 2005 (UTC)

If this article isn't worthy of mention, then some many other videog games set to be released aren't worthy of mention and need to be deleted as well. At least this article has two paragraphs, not just two stupid sentences stating something like, "...it is currently in development for sometime that is yet to be announced. The game is a first-person shooter (FPS in game terminology)." If people are picky about the release dates, then they can be deleted and simply put that it's going to be release in the future sometime. Every TBA release is subject to rumours. Can we at least keep the page up with what we already know? The article will indeed resurface once it's released [obviously] and I feel that, if all the "crystal ball speculation" is removed, it should stay up. We could merge what we already know into the Silent Hill article also. With no speculation included, I recommend MERGE. Godzilla 20:56, 22 December 2005 (UTC)
