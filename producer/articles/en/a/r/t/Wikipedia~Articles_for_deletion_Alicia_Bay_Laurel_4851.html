[edit] Alicia Bay Laurel

Alicia Bay Laurel (edit|talk|history|links|watch|logs)

Non-notable person. Cites no WP:RS, so appears to be unverifiable WP:OR. Also fails to satisfy WP:BIO. Valrith 23:05, 15 November 2006 (UTC)

Keep. Very notable late 60s early 70s counterculture writer and artist. 26,300 Ghits isn't bad for somebody who wrote a New York Times bestseller (published by a notable publisher, Random House) 35 years ago.[1] She's mentioned on AOL Music Now [2]. The Honolulu Star-Bulletin [3] and Metroactive Magazine [4] have published online articles about her. As a musician she's received reviews in eJazznews, a notable jazz music website [5]. --Charlene 23:24, 15 November 2006 (UTC)
  • Keep while looking for old NY Times Best Sellers lists, I've found from even the free NY Times previews that they did profiles of this subject and her writings. [6][7] --Oakshade 05:59, 16 November 2006 (UTC)
