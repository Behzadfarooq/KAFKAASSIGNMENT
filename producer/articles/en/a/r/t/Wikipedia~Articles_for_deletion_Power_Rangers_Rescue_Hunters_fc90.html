[edit] Power Rangers Rescue Hunters

Power Rangers Rescue Hunters (edit|talk|history|links|watch|logs)

I can't find any source to verify its existence, even through its own links, and it is partially written in foreign languages. Also it is most likely Power Rangers: Operation Overdrive as that had the same working titles. Laganojunior 23:26, 24 December 2006 (UTC)

  • Delete per nom. If it turns out this is another working title, then redirect to Power Rangers: Operation Overdrive. --- RockMFR 23:57, 24 December 2006 (UTC)
