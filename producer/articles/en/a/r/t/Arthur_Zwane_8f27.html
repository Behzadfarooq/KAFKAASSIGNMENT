Arthur Zwane

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Arthur Zwane (born September 20, 1973 in Soweto, Gauteng) is a South African football (soccer) midfielder for Kaizer Chiefs and South Africa.

In 2004, he was banned for 2 years after testing positive for Methyltestosterone, an anabolic steroid.[1] The ban was later reduced to six months.

  • Previous clubs: Jomo Cosmos, Santos; Brazil, Orlando Pirates, AmaZulu, Real Rovers, Dynamos, Classic
  • Bafana Bafana caps won: 9
