[edit] Differences between iTap and T9

Was tagged for speedy deletion by User:Walter Görlitz as being a repost of deleted content (see Wikipedia:Articles for deletion/Differences between iTap and T9) but it's different than the earlier version. Bringing it to AfD instead. howcheng {chat} 20:25, 7 February 2006 (UTC)

  • Delete And this is encyclopædic because…? Avi 20:30, 7 February 2006 (UTC)
  • Delete. It is not encyclopedic. It is more of an instruction manual than anything else.--Adam (talk) 20:35, 7 February 2006 (UTC)
  • Delete. iCouldCareLess about T9 or iTap.--み使い Mitsukai 21:00, 7 February 2006 (UTC)

I'd say merge with predictive text or expand with comparisons to the other existing systems - maybe with a table format. Diego Moya 21:38, 7 February 2006 (UTC)
  • Delete per Avi. Stifle 18:50, 8 February 2006 (UTC)
  • Merge I actually think this article, iTap and T9 should be merged into one article, perhaps something like 'mobile phone input methods'. This way the two stubs and this offshoot could actually be a useful and robust article instead of little blurbs.Pdurland 19:30, 8 February 2006 (UTC)
