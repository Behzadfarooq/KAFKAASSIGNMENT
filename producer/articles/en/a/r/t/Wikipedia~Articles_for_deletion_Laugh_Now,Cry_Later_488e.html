[edit] Laugh Now,Cry Later

Article about an album to be released next year. WP:ISNOT a crystal ball. howcheng [ t • c • w • e ] 00:55, 17 November 2005 (UTC)

  • Keep "Wikipedia is not a crystal ball" is a slur rather than an argument. You have presented no evidence that the information in the article is incorrect. CalJW 02:48, 17 November 2005 (UTC)
It isn't a slur, it is a policy: WP:ISNOT#Wikipedia is not a crystal ball. If you believe the information is verifiable, add relevant links. Otherwise, Delete. Laszlo Panaflex 04:44, 17 November 2005 (UTC)
Agree. Delete per above. Dottore So 08:03, 17 November 2005 (UTC)
  • Delete per nomination. Burden of proof is on the one making the claim of its existence. Edwardian 06:54, 17 November 2005 (UTC),
  • Abstain for the time-being. I can't find any authoritative references or quotes but fuddle me!'s search shows that it is at least very strongly rumored to be Ice Cube's next album. Edwardian 00:52, 19 November 2005 (UTC)
  • Google turns up a healthy cross-section of people (including a columnist for the Las Vegas Review-Journal) who not only believe that this will be Ice Cube's new album, but that the man is e'en now heavily promoting the album and has released singles. However, there's no mention of it on his official website. fuddlemark (fuddle me!) 11:57, 17 November 2005 (UTC)
  • Keep looks bona fide. JBH 22:22, 18 November 2005 (UTC)
And you[1] look like a sockpuppet. Laszlo Panaflex 23:45, 18 November 2005 (UTC)
  • Keep, heaps of albums like this exist at Category:Unreleased albums. -secfan 18:21, 19 November 2005 (UTC)
