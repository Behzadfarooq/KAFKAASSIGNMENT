Artimaeis Nightshadow is a freshman Music and Mathematics major attending Newberry College.

I have an extreme interest in Physics and plan to pursue graduate studies in it later in life. I edit wikipedia as much as I can because I have taken it on as my service learning for this semester.

I also have interests in Dungeons and Dragons, World of Warcraft, and Computer Games in general. I try to browse through articles related to these subjects and edit as I find the time. The life of a music major at my college leaves few blocks of free time that I can simply dedicate to editing. Instead I am forced to edit in small bursts as frequently as I can manage.
