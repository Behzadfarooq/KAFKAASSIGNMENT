[edit] Exploding cow

  ATTENTION!

If you came here because a post on Dave Barry's web log asked you to "do whatever it is that needs to be done", please note that this is not a vote, but rather a discussion to establish a consensus amongst Wikipedia editors on whether an article is suitable for this encyclopedia. We have policies and guidelines to help us decide this, and deletion decisions are made on the merits of the arguments, not by counting heads, socks or hooves.

You can participate and give your opinion. Please sign your posts on this page by adding ~~~~ at the end. Happy editing!
