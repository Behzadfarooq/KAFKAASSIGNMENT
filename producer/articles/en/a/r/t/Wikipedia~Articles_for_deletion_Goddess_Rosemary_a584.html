Originally Speedy deleted CSD#G11, this is close enough to the original that it could probably be a CSD#G4, but I thought I'd open it up to community review. Ignoring for the moment the fairly clear WP:COI and WP:NPOV problems {which could be addressed with editing), I don't see anything that meets WP:BIO. The subject has been on a few talkshows, but the "reference" section is vague to say the least; it is unclear if these are trivial mentions about the subject in the referenced publications, or more substantial coverage. Without more specific information (issue numbers, etc) it is hard to even verify this coverage actually exists. I'm not a big fan of the "Search Engine Test", but I found very little in the way of content that suggests this person meets any of our notability guidelines. In the end, there simply is not enough evidence of notability here. Delete. Isotope23 13:39, 21 March 2007 (UTC)
