[edit] United Kingdom in the Eurovision Song Contest 2006

Individual articles for each year of the U.K.'s (or any other country's) entries in the Eurovision Song Contest are duplicative and certainly not necessarily notable. There should be a single article for United Kingdom in the Eurovision Song Contest which covers the key points. Any notable groups, who happen to be U.K.'s entry, should have their own articles. Those that are not notable should not be wedged into the Wikipedia by the mechanism of having yearly Eurovision articles by country. Bejnar 05:36, 28 May 2006 (UTC)
