Contested prod. nn fansite, fails WP:WEB. Alexa rank 482,000. Delete. Oldelpaso 07:58, 4 July 2006 (UTC)

The issue is not what Planetside Universe is described as in the article, but rather whether or not Planetside Universe is notable enough to have its own article on Wikipedia. I do not think Planetside Universe meets the criteria in Wikipedia: Notability (web), hence the deletion listing. Oldelpaso 10:04, 4 July 2006 (UTC)
