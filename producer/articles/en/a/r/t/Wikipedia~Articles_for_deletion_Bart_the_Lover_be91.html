[edit] Bart the Lover

Keep - had been previously marked as speedy delete, but no comments to substantiate this. Certainly needs a cleanup though. --Sgkay 16:40, 1 May 2005 (UTC)

  • Tagged for Vfd 5/1, but not listed--adding 5/7. Delete if not cleaned up (someone's working on creating articles for all Simpsons eps that are of much higher quality than this (EG Guess Who's Coming to Criticize Dinner?), so it wouldn't be a real loss to start fresh). Niteowlneils 19:57, 7 May 2005 (UTC)
  • Keep; nothing wrong with this as a one-paragraph plot synopsis, and the Simpsons episode editors will be along to improve it soon enough. Samaritan 00:31, 8 May 2005 (UTC)
  • Delete. I'm inclined to say individual episodes of TV shows should only have articles if there was something particularly notable about that episode (something that gets it mentioned in the press, for example, such as the episode of "Ellen" where she comes out, or the It Hits the Fan episode of South Park. There's nothing special about this episode of the Simpsons. --Angr/comhrá 08:44, 9 May 2005 (UTC)
  • Delete - does not stand alone as an article - Tεxτurε 17:16, 11 May 2005 (UTC)
  • Delete. Bart is too young to have a love life. Jayjg (talk) 21:12, 12 May 2005 (UTC)
