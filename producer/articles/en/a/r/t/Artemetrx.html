Artemetrx [1] is a healthcare analytics and consulting firm based in Lexington, KY. Artemetrx uses technology and clinical expertise to identify and implement blueprints for healthy cost savings. Artemetrx works primarly with large self-insured companies (employers), HMO's and State Medicaid Programs in an effort to make healthcare costs more transparent and ultimately save the health plan money. Artemetrx focuses on developing QN/QP (Quality Neutral/Quality Positive) strategies that reduce healthcare costs for these health plans without affecting the quality of coverage or care given to the health plan's membership.
