[edit] List of Fake Facebook profiles

This list does not meet guidelines for notability or importance. Furthermore, there is no way to cite any sources. The list could become extremely long as there are numerous fake profiles being created. This basically would end up being a list of celebrities, politicians, internet memes ("Limecat"), and other random fictional characters ("Captain Planet"). Sorry to spoil your fun, but this does not belong on Wikipedia. L1AM (talk) 00:50, 20 March 2006 (UTC)
