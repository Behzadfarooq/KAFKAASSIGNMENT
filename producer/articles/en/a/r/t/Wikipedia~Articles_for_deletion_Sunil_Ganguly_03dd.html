  • Delete: and redirect name to Sunil Gangopadhyay (the poet). The musician is non-notable. --Ragib 17:42, 23 March 2007 (UTC)
  • Delete. Fails WP:MUSIC and WP:RS Weak Keep. It seems that he released five albumns on HMV, which consisted of instrumental versions of popular Bollywood songs. The guy passes WP:MUSIC because he has >2 albums with a notable record company. His name is listed among the notable musicians on some sites[1][2]. I could find his mention among notable musicians on some forums, blogs and mailing list (eg.[3][4] etc.). Although the reliable sources are very few[5], and I'm not fully convinced about notability, I'd give a benefit of doubt here. utcursch | talk 13:28, 26 March 2007 (UTC)
  • Delete per nom. - KNM Talk 02:36, 25 March 2007 (UTC)
