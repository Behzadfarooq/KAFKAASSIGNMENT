Patrick Ada (born January 14, 1985 in Yaoundé, Cameroon) is a defender who signed for Exeter City F.C. on the 26th of July 2006. The 21 year old joined on a six month contract.

Ada, who holds a British passport, can play at both right back and center back. His previous clubs include Barnet, Stevenage Borough, and St Albans City.
