[edit] Jack shit

Dictionary definition plus nonsensical original ideas. WP:WINAD and WP:NOR. Erik the Rude 00:48, 28 May 2006 (UTC)

  • Delete. I was new page patrolling and about to do the same. SorryGuy 00:50, 28 May 2006 (UTC)
  • Delete Dictionary definition. Sarahgal 00:57, 28 May 2006 (UTC)
  • Delete WP:NOT a dictionary:
A usage guide or slang and idiom guide. Wikipedia is not in the business of saying how words, idioms, etc. should be used. We aren't teaching people how to talk like a Cockney chimney-sweep. However, it may be important in the context of an encyclopedia article to describe just how a word is used to distinguish among similar, easily confused ideas, as in nation or freedom. In some special cases an article about an essential piece of slang may be appropriate. --Jersey Devil 01:00, 28 May 2006 (UTC)
  • Delete as original research about a dicdef. Kevin 01:35, 28 May 2006 (UTC)
  • Delete Slang dic def, POV. Ande B 01:50, 28 May 2006 (UTC)

  • Delete, Wikipedia is not a dictionary, slang. --Terence Ong 03:59, 28 May 2006 (UTC)

  • Delete per above. —Khoikhoi 04:33, 28 May 2006 (UTC)
