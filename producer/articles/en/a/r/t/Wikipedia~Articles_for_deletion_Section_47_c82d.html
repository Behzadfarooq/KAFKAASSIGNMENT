The following discussion is an archived debate of the proposed deletion of the article below. Please do not modify it. Subsequent comments should be made on the appropriate discussion page (such as the article's talk page or in a deletion review). No further edits should be made to this page.

The result of the debate was delete. There were three users, all of whom voted keep, that have only edited this AfD; another that edited this AfD and the article itself, who also voted keep; and a fifth user (Cptjeff)who edited this AfD and an unrelated article, who also voted keep. Only the latter was included in the tally, and marginally at that. Note that I didn't discount the comments these users made, but they weren't sufficient to sway the result. Mindmatrix 00:23, 29 December 2005 (UTC)
