(edit conflict) You should re-read WP:MOS if you haven't already, because there really is a lot of things needing to be wikified. For example, album names are supposed to be italicized, not bolded. "French" in the first sentence should be capitalized. There are way too many bolded words. Statements such as where the name came from, and who influenced them, should be cited. There shouldn't be any original research either, so you can't just write down what you think. WP:NOT#CRYSTALL, meaning you shouldn't include information so far ahead, especially if it's a year away, in 2008. There's no references and no note on why they're notable. Just because they released two CDs does not make them notable. See WP:Notability. External links only link to their website (twice), and a French wiki.
