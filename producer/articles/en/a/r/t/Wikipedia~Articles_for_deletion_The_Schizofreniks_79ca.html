[edit] The Schizofreniks

The Schizofreniks (edit|talk|history|links|watch|logs)

Non-notable upstart label. Google brings forth 89 unique hits which isn't much. The article is carefully worded but the official site has bios that more clearly show that these individuals worked for companies that worked with notable artists...the "Schizofreniks" themselves may not have. The author RecordLabel has predominately made edits in reference to this subject so a conflict of interest may be at hand. Additionally, the whole article reads like an advertisement.
