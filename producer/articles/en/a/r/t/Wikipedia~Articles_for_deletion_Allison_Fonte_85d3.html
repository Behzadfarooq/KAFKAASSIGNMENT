[edit] Allison Fonte

I would have speedied this as vanity, but the article appears to place her on the razor's edge of notability. What say you, brethren? DS 01:16, 26 August 2005 (UTC)

  • Ugh... this defines borderline. Negligibly weak keep. --Titoxd 01:33, 26 August 2005 (UTC)
    • After the Google Test, I remove the "negligibly weak" part. 26,100 Google hits. [1] --Titoxd 01:36, 26 August 2005 (UTC)
      The Google Test is evil and much misunderstood. Try [2]. 225 hits, you mean. JRM · Talk 01:41, 26 August 2005 (UTC)
  • Comment. Most of the article (as it is) is unverifiable; Wikipedia is not a primary source. It's possible to edit this article down to only the most stringently verifiable facts (Allison Fonte does exist, she did co-found the company mentioned [3], she was a Mouseketeer [4]), but if you're talking notability, I'd say no. I still haven't made up my mind about notability, however, so I can't really vote. How annoying. JRM · Talk 01:41, 26 August 2005 (UTC)
  • Keep and Cleanup. Regular actors in notable television shows are notable. Pburka 01:42, August 26, 2005 (UTC)
  • Rewrite. I think information on people who someone might, someday, want to look up is good — I'm just turned off by the way this article appears to have been written as a (kinda creepy, third-person) personal homepage. (Anon editor, intimate knowledge of her own life.) If a registered user rewrites it from available sources, definitely keep; otherwise, I'd rather it be a stub or deleted. --Quuxplusone 02:21, 26 August 2005 (UTC)
  • A Google search for "Allison Fonte" shows 135 hits see [5] However as WP:BIO provides that: "Well known entertainment figures, such as TV/movie producers, directors, writers, and actors who have starring roles, or a series of minor roles, in commercially distributed work watched by a total audience of 5,000 or more" meet the criterion for articles, then she meets it for her role on the New Mousketeers.Keep Capitalistroadster 04:17, 26 August 2005 (UTC)
  • Rewrite for reasons states by Quuxplusone. Too bad the author of the existing article didn't upload a nice picture of Ms. Fonte as a Mousetekeer.Crypticfirefly 04:20, 26 August 2005 (UTC)
  • Rewrite; and keep | Celcius 04:53, 26 August 2005 (UTC)
  • Keep and Cleanup as per Pburka. 23skidoo 05:02, 26 August 2005 (UTC)
  • Keep I rewrote this, it's probably ok now, she meets WP:BIO Rx StrangeLove 05:38, 26 August 2005 (UTC)
  • Keep. I was compelled to add "cast" in the first sentence so it reads as "was a cast member of The New Mickey Mouse Club". Alf 13:11, 26 August 2005 (UTC)
  • Keep Trollderella 16:41, 26 August 2005 (UTC)
  • Keep. *drew 22:31, 27 August 2005 (UTC)
