[edit] Beth Sholom Day Camp

Completely unnotable. The article is two sentences one, the first has the name and its location, and the second describes the activities offered at the camp, which are typical of all summer camps. Páll (Die pienk olifant) 18:11, 19 May 2006 (UTC)

  • Delete. Its a US summer camp. Unless the article can explain, through the use of verifiable information taken from reliable third-party sources, how this summer camp is significantly different from the standard, run of the mill summer camp, there is nothing we can do that the camp's own site cannot do better. -- saberwyn 03:02, 20 May 2006 (UTC)

  • Keep, I'm concerned about this campaign to delete Jewish camps. --MPerel ( talk | contrib) 19:40, 21 May 2006 (UTC)
