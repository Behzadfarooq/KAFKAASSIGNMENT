Art Radio 88MHz is a Belgrade oldies radio station founded in 2002.

Art Radio claims the most complete CD and LP library of world oldies standards, including some very valuable rare archive records from original sound carriers.

Newer music such as 80s and 90s pop and rock hits are played daily, as well as the newest songs from the world's greatest stars. During the day, there are announcements of cultural happenings in Belgrade including theaters, galleries, cinemas and concerts, for which the station is often a sponsor.
