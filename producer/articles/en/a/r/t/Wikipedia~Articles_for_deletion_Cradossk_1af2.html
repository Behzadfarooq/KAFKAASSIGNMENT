[edit] Cradossk

Direct quote from my brother the Star Wars fanatic: "I wouldn't even put that guy in a Star Wars character guide, let alone an encyclopedia." Delete. ♥purplefeltangel (talk) ♥ (Contributions) 00:16, 19 September 2005 (UTC)

  • Merge into Bossk. I don't think the resulting redirect will cause any harm. -- BD2412 talk 00:19, 19 September 2005 (UTC)
    • Info is already in there; redirect to Minor bounty hunters in Star Wars#Bossk. -- BD2412 talk 00:23, 19 September 2005 (UTC)
      • You can't redirect to sections though, but apart from that I agree. --fvw* 00:29, 19 September 2005 (UTC)
        • Do you mean that in the sense of "it's not technically possible", or in the sense of "it's not allowed"? I'm pretty sure it's technically possible. -- BD2412 talk 02:03, 19 September 2005 (UTC)
          • It's not technically possible. Here, I'll set up an example for you. Go to User:Purplefeltangel/Sandbox2, then click on the link at the top back to the redirect page. Then click "edit." You can see it's supposed to link to a section, but it doesn't. ♥purplefeltangel (talk) ♥ (Contributions) 02:28, 19 September 2005 (UTC)
            • I stand corrected. But darn it, it should be possible. They need to fix that. -- BD2412 talk 03:20, 19 September 2005 (UTC)
              • I think that this used to work a few months ago. Maybe it's a regression in the software? Pburka 03:48, 19 September 2005 (UTC)
  • Delete. Not particularly opposed to a redirect. — Phil Welch 02:39, 19 September 2005 (UTC)
  • I'd rather like to say "delete" as another example of rampant Star Wars cruft, but precedent is against me. Redirect, merging if necessary. --fuddlemark (fuddle me!) 03:05, 19 September 2005 (UTC)
  • Merge into Minor_bounty_hunters_in_Star_Wars#Bossk and then Redirect --Icarus 05:18, 19 September 2005 (UTC)
  • Merge and redirect to the list of minor bounty hunters. Since sections can be renamed without being explicitly logged, redirects to sections are prone to breaking. That's why it's not working. - Mgm|(talk) 09:02, 19 September 2005 (UTC)
  • Merge and redirect into Minor bounty hunters in Star Wars#Bossk.
