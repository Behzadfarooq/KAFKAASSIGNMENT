Art Paul Schlosser

Art Paul Schlosser (born January 4, 1960) has been a street musician and busker as well as an outsider artist on State Street in Madison, Wisconsin[1] since 1986. He is best known for his song "Have A Peanut Butter Sandwich", which made the Funny Five of the Dr Demento radio show four times in 2001.[2][3][4][5]

He usually performs his own songs on an acoustic guitar and kazoo. He also sometimes plays electronic keyboard or ukulele.[6] He sings mostly humorous songs, but has also written on the topics of politics and religion.[7] His music has been played on oddball radio programs, including Greasy Kid Stuff[citation needed] and the Dr. Demento Show, and he has appeared on his own public-access television show[1]
