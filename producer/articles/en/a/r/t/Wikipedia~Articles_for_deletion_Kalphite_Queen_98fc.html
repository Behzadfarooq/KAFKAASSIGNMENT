Also King Black Dragon, Chaos Elemental, Dagannoth

* All are non-notable RuneScape monsters, with indication of notability (individual characters from a relatively popular game - contrast this with the recently deleted individual CounterStrike Maps).

  • Unreferenced (WP:OR) Runecruft. Needs to be merged with RuneScape monsters in order to cut down on the excessive amount of RuneScape articles.
  • Should be completely covered RuneScape monsters article, which I debate it's relevance anyway, but I thought these are the most obvious candidates for removal.
  • Many of the articles head into GameGuide territory (as per WP:NOT), Wikipedia is not a Game Guide
    • From Kalphite Queen - The Queen resides at the very end of the cavern, past several chambers full of Kalphite Workers, Kalphite Soldiers, and Kalphite Guardians. Beyond these creatures is another hole that must be climbed down via rope. In this last chamber, along with the Kalphite Queen, there are several of the smaller Kalphite beings, which pester players attempting to kill the Kalphite Queen, but these are generally ignored.
    • From Dagannoth - As part of the Horror from the Deep quest, players must visit the basement of a lighthouse, where they have to face a level 100 dagannoth.
    • From Chaos Elemental - The Chaos Elemental is located west of the Rogues' Castle, next to the scorpion pit, in the Level 50 through 52 wilderness, an area available only to RuneScape players who pay a monthly fee (P2P).

These have been grouped together since these all cover /very/ similar subjects (individual monsters in the game), as per suggestions on the no consensus close of Wikipedia:Articles for deletion/RuneScape armour (2nd nomination). Another AFD request has been created involving all the articles as per the note on Wikipedia:Articles for deletion/King Black Dragon, note that King Black Dragon never was merged. -Halo 21:32, 24 July 2006 (UTC)
