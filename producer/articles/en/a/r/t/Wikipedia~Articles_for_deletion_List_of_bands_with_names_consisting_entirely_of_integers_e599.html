[edit] List of bands with names consisting entirely of integers

Too idiosyncratic to be encyclopedic. Matt Gabriel

  • Delete, unnecessary duplicate list. Megan1967 10:33, 15 Mar 2005 (UTC)
  • Keep, interesting, verifiable. Kappa 11:56, 15 Mar 2005 (UTC)
  • Delete as arbitrary subset of Category:Bands (can be looked up from there if wanted). Radiant! 13:08, Mar 15, 2005 (UTC)
  • Delete, unless we want List of bands with names that are five letters long or List of bands with names that are palindromes. DaveTheRed 19:57, 15 Mar 2005 (UTC)
  • Do we have List of bands with numbers in the title? If not, move article here and update accordingly, else merge. Since we have List of songs with numbers in the title there should be a similar list for bands. Grue 20:54, 15 Mar 2005 (UTC)
  • Keep, interesting list worthy of note. --GRider\talk 22:01, 15 Mar 2005 (UTC)
  • Weak delete. Arbitrary, trivial. If it were a really good list I might be swayed, but it isn't.
  • Weak delete. Fairly pointless esoteric data. -Sean Curtin 06:18, Mar 16, 2005 (UTC)
  • Agree with grue - move to new, less idiosyncratic page. Grutness|hello? 09:21, 17 Mar 2005 (UTC)
  • Delete. Arbitrary list. Jayjg (talk) 21:12, 17 Mar 2005 (UTC)
  • Delete. Useless cubed. Edeans 20:50, 18 Mar 2005 (UTC)
  • Keep - David Gerard 23:05, 18 Mar 2005 (UTC)
  • keep, move maybE? Yuckfoo 21:11, 19 Mar 2005 (UTC)
  • keep, needs expansion. - SimonP 05:04, Mar 30, 2005 (UTC)
