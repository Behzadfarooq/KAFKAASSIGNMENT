Artsadmin is a UK based charity providing project management to a number of contemporary artists (see below), as well as support and resources for artists at any stage of their career working particularly in the fields of new theatre, dance, music, live art and mixed media work. The organisation has established a range of new opportunities for emerging and unfunded artists with a bursary and mentoring scheme, a full time advisory service, school residencies and a programme of showcases.

Based at Toynbee Studios in East London (part of Toynbee Hall), Artsadmin offers rehearsal studios available for hire and workspaces for artists. The building is currently being refurbished (due for completion in March 2007) and when complete will include six spaces of varying sizes for hire suitable for rehearsals, castings and showcases (including a brand new rooftop studio) and eight additional creative media units, as well as the return of the popular caf√©.

Artsadmin works closely with promoters, producers and other arts organisations in the UK and internationally to develop audiences for the work of its artists, and with other partner organisations on the development of support schemes and resources for artists.
