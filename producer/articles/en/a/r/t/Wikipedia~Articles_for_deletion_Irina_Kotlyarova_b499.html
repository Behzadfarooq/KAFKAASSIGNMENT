Kappa, do you really believe Wikipedia should be a spam ground? Many of these articles are in a mess (Isabekova's is a prime example) and look like a vehicle for promoting "Pop Idol". Let's concentrate on the ones who finished 1st, 2nd and 3rd, not forgetting that "one that got away" (Isabekova). I don't think this is spam, there is nothing overly promotional or POV. I also don't see why wikipedia readers shouldn't be able to read about someone who's been on national television multiple times. Also this one did come third, so where is your "keep" vote? Kappa 09:52, 13 September 2006 (UTC)
