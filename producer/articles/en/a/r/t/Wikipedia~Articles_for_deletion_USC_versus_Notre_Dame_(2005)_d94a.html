[edit] USC versus Notre Dame (2005)

I was about to prod this, but I wanted to get opinions on this. I don't know if it's a memorable game from the broader view. Sure, it was one of the highlights of the 2005 season, but I don't know if it's on the level of the Flutie Hail Mary or other memorable games. Metros232 23:59, 20 May 2006 (UTC)

  • Delete per nom. In the grand scheme of things, this was just a game. This was not Kordell Stewart throwing the ball 75 yards in the air or the band being on the field. It was a great game, but not "forever notable" great. -- Kicking222 00:06, 21 May 2006 (UTC)
  • Delete per nom, but the articles in Category:College rivalry games suggest that at least some of this info might fit well in a future article on the rivalry itself, rather than on any individual game. â€”Zero Gravitas 01:13, 21 May 2006 (UTC)
  • Delete not a significant enough individual game to justify its own page. Zaxem 06:09, 21 May 2006 (UTC)
  • Delete, Wikipedia is not Wikinews. Stifle (talk) 15:05, 21 May 2006 (UTC)
  • Keep even if Brady Quinn and ND won, I'd still vote to keep it. This article, with some minor help, could become a great compliment to the 2006 Rose Bowl Game --fpo 02:57, 22 May 2006 (UTC)
