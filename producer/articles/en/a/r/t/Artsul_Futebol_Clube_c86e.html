Artsul Futebol Clube, usually known simply as Artsul, is a Brazilian football team from the city of Nova Iguaçu, Rio de Janeiro state, founded on June 19, 2001.

[edit] Stadium

The home stadium is Estádio Nivaldo Pereira named after the club founder.

[edit] Colors

The official colors are blue, green and white.
