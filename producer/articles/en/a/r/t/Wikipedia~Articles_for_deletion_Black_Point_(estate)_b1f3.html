[edit] Black Point (estate)

Keep. As of 2007, the house will be a Wisconsin historic site open to the public (notability which the article asserts). I'm aware the main news link has expired, but it was recently in the NYT. There's also an odd little sidebar in a connection to the bribery conviction of former State Senate Majority Leader Chuck Chvala, who doesn't have an article as yet.
