Arturo Erasmo Vidal Pardo (born May 22, 1987) is a Chilean defender, who currently plays on the Chilean club Colo-Colo. Vidal can also play midfield. He scored three goals in Colo-Colo's Copa Sudamericana 2006 campaign. He has represented the Chilean national football team at the sub-20 level at the 2007 South American Youth Championship in Paraguay where he was Chile's leading goal scorer with six.
