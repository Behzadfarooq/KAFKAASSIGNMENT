[edit] Eight "I'd Really Rather You Didn'ts"

Verbatim reproduction of eight "commandments" of a parody "religion", allegedly based on a book that appeared this year. Generally unencyclopedic as textdump, non-notable and nonsense. The existence of these commandments is already noted at Pastafarianism. Contested PROD. Sandstein 04:22, 22 May 2006 (UTC)
