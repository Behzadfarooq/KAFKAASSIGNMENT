Hi, is this an actual listed company or just the self-styling of the game developers? If the latter then the info here could be adequately handled in the Adventure Quest article as this may be considered a vanity article. I ask because the company doesn't appear to have an independent web presence. - Randwicked 05:12, 3 December 2005 (UTC)

The copyright to all the characters, art, codes, and other aspects of AdventureQuest and related materials are copywritten by Atrix Entertainment, which means they have a legal presence at least insofar as they hold copyrights. Also, I believe only legally recognized companies or individuals - not informal organizations - can apply for copyrights. Even if they aren't a legal company, their web presence and multiple projects qualify them as significant in and of themselves. --The reverend 00:12, 28 October 2006 (UTC)
