[edit] Can't be linked to at all

This is a title containing a period which would be interpreted by web browsers, and which can only be seen by using an outside link to Wikipedia. The insoluble problem is the period, not the slash.

  • /.
  • /.
  • /.

The first link is a redirect to Slashdot. The second and third links above are produced by [[:/.]] and [[&#47;&#46;]] respectively: they actually link to the Slash (punctuation) article via a redirect.

[edit] Links to titles with an initial /

See here for full list. For other titles starting with a "/", there are two ways of achieving the desired result:

  1. prepend a ":" to the link (for example [[://Gana language]]) as you would for an inline interlanguage or category link; or
  2. use "&#47;" instead of the "/" (for example [[&#47;/Gana language]]
