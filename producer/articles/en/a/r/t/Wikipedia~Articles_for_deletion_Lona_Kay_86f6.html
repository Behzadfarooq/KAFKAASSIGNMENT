Non-notable author. Her one claim of notability is her book Dialogue with a Demon, published by vanity press Vantage Press. Her name does not occur outside Wikipedia along with the book and it has no amazon.com SalesRank. -Elmer Clark 04:22, 25 October 2006 (UTC)
