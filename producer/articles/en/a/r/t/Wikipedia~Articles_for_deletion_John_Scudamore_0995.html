[edit] John Scudamore

A biography of User:Whaleto, whose name is not a secret. John Scudamore and vaccination gets ~275 ghits (about 10% of what I get), posting prolifically to Usenet does not constitute notability and that is pretty much the sole claim to notability. John apparently doesn't want this article on Wikipedia, but that is not a reason for keeping it. The site (which is a compendium of crankery, see Wikipedia:Requests for comment/Whaleto) does get an Alexa ranking of around 80k, but Wikipedia is the top site linking in, a fact which may not be unrelated to John's contentious addition of his own site to multiple articles.
