          • Just want to note here that many of the first-timers were considerably more qualified to judge this documentary than Calton, the one who nominated for deletion apparently without so much as visiting the official movie site. I'm not defending anyone who was rude or accused Wikipedia as a whole of censorship (obviously, there is no secret council here, and Calton has turned out to be a completely solitary voice in support of deleting the article).
