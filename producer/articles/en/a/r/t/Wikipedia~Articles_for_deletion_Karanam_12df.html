[edit] karanam

No hits on google for this as a caste name. If it is legit, should merge with caste Kghose 04:49, 4 November 2005 (UTC)

  • Delete per nomination. - Kghose 04:50, 4 November 2005 (UTC)
  • Comment Google, searching for Khandayat and Karana has results, but Karanam seems to be a typo, or morphological variation of the caste name. should be merged per nom. jnothman talk 06:34, 4 November 2005 (UTC)
  • Keep – I don't know about the veracity of the text, but my gut feeling is that this is a useful article. It should not be merged with caste, as it appears to be a specific subcaste. =Nichalp «Talk»= 18:19, 5 November 2005 (UTC) Withdrawing my vote. =Nichalp «Talk»= 13:47, 9 November 2005 (UTC)
  • Delete as the description in the article refers to Kayastha caste; Karanam is a surname in Andhra Pradesh (e.g. Karnam Malleswari) and not necessarily a caste name. As late as 1980, the officers who maintained village records and were native of a village were known as karanams. The institution of Karanam is at least around 500 years old. So, while the name definitely exists, it is not a caste name and definitely doesn't fit the content in the article. Seems like an evil hoax.--Gurubrahma 07:44, 6 November 2005 (UTC)
  • Delete as per gurubrahma, but merge whatever can be salvaged into Kayastha. --Pamri • Talk • Reply 15:39, 8 November 2005 (UTC)
    • Good! Some people from south india who know about this :) Thanks for taking time to comment. I will try port the description into Kayastha under caste. Be bold and edit it:). Kghose 15:54, 8 November 2005 (UTC)
