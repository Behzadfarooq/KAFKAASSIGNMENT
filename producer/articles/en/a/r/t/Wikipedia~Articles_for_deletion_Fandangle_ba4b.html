[edit] Fandangle

Fandangle (edit|talk|history|links|watch|logs)

The article claims that the band toured the UK five times, but there is no reference to support that. ShadowHalo 04:59, 19 December 2006 (UTC)

  • Delete, fails WP:MUSIC, unverifiable. Terence Ong 05:26, 19 December 2006 (UTC)
  • Week Keep on condition of immediate referencing - WP:MUSIC allows bands that have done a national tour, as long as it's referenced. From this search, it's looks possible to piece together that they have, and are about to retour UK (including a BBC ref). Chovain 05:41, 19 December 2006 (UTC)
  • Source or delete. MER-C 05:52, 19 December 2006 (UTC)
  • As above source it or lose it. Guy (Help!) 13:48, 19 December 2006 (UTC)
  • Keep, nominate again if it remains unsourced for a while afterwards. -Toptomcat 14:16, 19 December 2006 (UTC)
  • Keep - I found a news article on the East Herts Herald 24 that mentioned their performances and tours, so they pass WP:MUSIC Jayden54 15:28, 19 December 2006 (UTC)
  • Weak delete - do not meet WP:MUSIC enough for me to endorse a keep vote. -- moe.RON Let's talk | done 05:08, 20 December 2006 (UTC)
  • Delete: band promo. Wile E. Heresiarch 18:07, 21 December 2006 (UTC)
  • Keep I (a long with hundreds of others) payed to see them at a show (which was part of a tour), therefore they must be noteworthy. And they've released on the same label as LTJ. Philc TECI 18:20, 25 December 2006 (UTC)
