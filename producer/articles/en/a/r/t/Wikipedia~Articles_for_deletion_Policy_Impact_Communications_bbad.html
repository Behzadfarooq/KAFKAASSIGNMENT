[edit] Policy Impact Communications

Policy Impact Communications (edit|talk|history|links|watch|logs)

Claims notability I suppose, and you might expect it. However, only 150 ghits which seems trivial for such a company [1]; and they only seem to be press releases and own site. So propose deletion for failing WP:CORP, for failing WP:COI as created by a user called PIC, and for reading like a press release. Akihabara 13:28, 28 December 2006 (UTC)

  • Keep because I think that an organization founded by such notable individuals is notable simply through association; but it needs a source for this. Tarinth 13:51, 28 December 2006 (UTC)
Being notable doesn't mean everything you do is notable. An organization like this needs to be able to stand on its own. Akihabara 14:11, 28 December 2006 (UTC)
  • Delete does not pass WP:CORP. Tarinth, this is not a band. There are no clauses in WP:CORP that state having a 'notable individual' as part of the company automatically makes the company notable like WP:BAND states for music groups. --Brian(view my history)/(How am I doing?) 18:08, 28 December 2006 (UTC)
  • Delete. I could not find any published works where this company is the primary subject. They seem to float somewhat below the radar and thus do not appear to pass CORP.--Kubigula (talk) 05:21, 31 December 2006 (UTC)
