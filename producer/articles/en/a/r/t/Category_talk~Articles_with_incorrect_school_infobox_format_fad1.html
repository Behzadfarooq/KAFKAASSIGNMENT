[edit] Backlog cleared, and future

[edit] Repurpose?

It seems like there are a number of transitions that it would be nice to make for Infobox School:

  • "students" or "enrollment"? We should get rid of one.
  • website format?
  • "information" to "contact number"?
  • ...

Might it make sense to rename this category something generic like "Articles needing Infobox School transition", and then reuse it for those?

Or do people consider creating and deleting categories to be lightweight enough to do for each such change?

(BTW, can anybody tell me how Wikipedia finds the articles that are categorized via a template? Does it require that they be edited in order to notice that they're in a new category, or does it scan all articles that include an edited template in order to see if they got recategorized by it, or just periodically rescan the world, or...?)

Jordan Brown 05:12, 21 March 2007 (UTC)

You know, using this article for all maintenance tasks sounds good... and we can sort them using piped category calls. I believe that pages are placed in categories when viewed. A couple of articles have popped up even after all of it was supposedly cleared... presumably they were not viewed. If you want to use this for something else, just tell me, and I'll code it! GracenotesT ยง 22:06, 22 March 2007 (UTC)
Come to think of it, the current name isn't all that wrong for this use. (It does seem a bit negative: the articles were correct before, so calling them "incorrect" seems unfair. "old" would be better.)
