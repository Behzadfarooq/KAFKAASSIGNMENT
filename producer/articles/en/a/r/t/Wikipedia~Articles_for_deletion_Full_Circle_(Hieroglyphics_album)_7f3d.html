  • Logistical comment: Deletion is definitely not going to be the result here. Was I wrong in stepping over the AfD bureaucracy? We don't need AfD for this discussion. (Btw, I have reverted my changes to the other two articles for consistency) ~ Booya Bazooka 22:54, 2 January 2007 (UTC)
