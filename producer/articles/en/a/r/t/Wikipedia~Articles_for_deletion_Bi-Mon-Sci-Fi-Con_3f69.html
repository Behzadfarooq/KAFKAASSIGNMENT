[edit] Bi-Mon-Sci-Fi-Con

Bi-Mon-Sci-Fi-Con (edit|talk|history|links|watch|logs) – (View log)

NN in-universe Simpsons cruft. It appeared briefly in 2 episodes and wasn't a major plot focus in either. Amazingly, this article has somehow survived more than a year and a half (most likely because it is not in any Simpsons categories). Scorpion 05:38, 10 February 2007 (UTC)

  • Delete or redirect to the show or the episodes in which it's featured. I'm not fussy. BigHaz - Schreit mich an 05:44, 10 February 2007 (UTC)
  • Comment It was actually featured very heavily in both episodes it appeared in (about 1/3 of both episodes very held entirely in it). TJ Spyke 05:46, 10 February 2007 (UTC)
  • Yes, but we can't have every single setting and character which has been in the show have their own page. It has had no notability within the show and it was not a key plot point. -- Scorpion 05:49, 10 February 2007 (UTC)
  • Comment Is there a List of Simpsons locations-type article this could be merged into? If not, I say Delete; it may have been relatively prominent in the two episodes, but that doesn't make it as notable as, say, the Kwik-E-Mart. EVula // talk // ☯ // 06:09, 10 February 2007 (UTC)
    Update: Redirect to Recurring jokes in The Simpsons, per Dgies' suggestion below. EVula // talk // ☯ // 19:12, 12 February 2007 (UTC)
    • List of fictional places on The Simpsons, although it seems to mostly contain information on other towns. -- Scorpion 06:13, 10 February 2007 (UTC)
  • Delete. In-universe Simpson cruft. --Maitch 12:00, 10 February 2007 (UTC)
  • Delete. No sources to indicate notability, original research. Nick Graves 16:08, 10 February 2007 (UTC)
  • Redirect it's possible someone might be looking for what show this was in, and it would get them to the right place. SkierRMH 19:17, 10 February 2007 (UTC)
  • Redirect and merge to wherever is found to be the best place for it. Mathmo Talk 10:29, 11 February 2007 (UTC)
  • Delete or possibly redirect to whatever episode this appeared in. (jarbarf) 17:24, 12 February 2007 (UTC)
  • Redirect/merge to Recurring jokes in The Simpsons as it is not a location, per-se. —Dgiest c 19:06, 12 February 2007 (UTC)
    I think that would be a good place for the redirect; previous suggestions have been less than stellar (with it appearing in two episodes, pointing it to just one or the other isn't that great, and List of fictional places on The Simpsons is, as Scorpion pointed out, not quite appropriate). EVula // talk // ☯ // 19:12, 12 February 2007 (UTC)
  • Merge/redirect to either the episode, or the recurring jokes thingy, or to the List of fictional places on The Simpsons. >Radiant< 14:37, 14 February 2007 (UTC)
