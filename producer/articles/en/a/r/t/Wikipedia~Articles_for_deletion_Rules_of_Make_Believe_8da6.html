[edit] Rules of Make Believe

Article for a webcomic, which can be found here. Alexa ranks it at 2.5million+. Although the comic seems to be at a slight hiatus since July of this year, the page rank, if it is a notable comic should be higher than stated. A Google search shows up nothing that would distinguish this from any other random webcomic. Just because it's a 100 strip+ webcomic does not mean it is inherently notable. Are we going to start making articles for 100+ post bloggers now? - Hahnchen 23:58, 24 September 2005 (UTC)
