[edit] List of weapons and items from the Ratchet & Clank series

I've been mulling this over for a while, and there is really no reason for this article. The original point of it was to keep all the weapon discriptions off of the main page, but times have since changed, and any info about weapons can be confortably contained in a gameplay section without a need for extensive lists. Thunderbrand 19:59, 30 October 2006 (UTC)

  • Note: This debate has been added to the list of CVG deletions. Thunderbrand 19:59, 30 October 2006 (UTC)
  • Delete All of the weapon descriptions are basically a summary of what they do in game, making this a game guide, which is prohibited by WP:NOT. ColourBurst 20:47, 30 October 2006 (UTC)
  • Delete - Per nom, well stated. Wickethewok 21:13, 30 October 2006 (UTC)
  • Delete- fancruft and listcruft. Interesting. DoomsDay349 Happy Halloween! 21:35, 30 October 2006 (UTC)
  • Delete per nom. Combination 21:37, 30 October 2006 (UTC)
  • Delete. What little information we need about weapons in any game is usually related to their effect on the story and/or gameplay, as in this case. It makes no sense to have this article around, when the same information could be comfortably summarised to support the main one. Daveydweeb (chat/patch) 23:06, 30 October 2006 (UTC)
  • Delete, Listy guidecruft, or guidy listcruft. L0b0t 02:00, 31 October 2006 (UTC)
  • Delete - Indiscriminate information, listcruft and Game guide. The Kinslayer 11:01, 31 October 2006 (UTC)
  • Merge notable information and then delete I'm mostly along the same lines as the nominator, lists and cruft can be a deadly mix, like bleach and ammonia. The ikiroid (talk·desk·Advise me) 20:13, 31 October 2006 (UTC)
