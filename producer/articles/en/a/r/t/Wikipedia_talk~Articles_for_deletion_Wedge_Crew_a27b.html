Actually we aren't a bunch of kids messing around, we go to a small college preperatory school of about 500 students, and literally are known as the Wedge. We are a serious group at our school and we would like to have this article on here, we are not making this as a joke or to fool around.


One good aspect about the whole free encyclopedia idea is that it is created by the people-the ones that use this website for information the most often. A good way to learn about a certain word or topic is not solely through definitions and textbook situations. The most efficient way to gain knowledge on a specific matter is through experiences. By having experiences, you will never forget what happened, and you will never forget the lessons you learned from your encounters. This post, the Wedge Crew uses a definiton of the word wedge which I have taken from www.dictionary.com


3

  1. Something that intrudes and causes division or disruption: His nomination drove a wedge into party unity.
  2. Something that forces an opening or a beginning: a wedge in the war on poverty.

Let's look at the first definition here. The Wedge Crew's experiences at The Harker School have for the most part caused great disruption and disturbance to the flow of normality at the school. The experiences listed on this topic give examples as to why this is true, and as a result, the readers of this page will be much more knowledge on the definition of the word wedge.

The second definition also is a clear aspect to the Wedge Crew. Before we arrived at Harker, there was a big overall ambience of plainness to the school and its surroundings. With our group's vast and diverse members, it was obvious that we would change this. Each person to the Wedge Crew has brought a unique part of himself which makes the Wedge Crew the diverse force of energy it is today. In it's 4 years of effect, we have done nothing other than create a new opening at Harker. We, in a sense, have created an Option Z to the prospective students to the 9-12 segment of the school. We have created a new beginning which has not only had an enormous impact on the students, but also the faculty and administration of Harker as a whole.

For these reasons, I believe it is nothing other than evident that this post should be kept in the Wikipedia Encyclopedia. I believe the creators of this enormous site can relate to the Wedge Crew on this matter. This website in a sense "Wedged" a new aspect to the Internet. Wikipedia created a new opening to our everyday way of life. The outcome of the idea of the creator of this website now impacts millions of people per day as it is used for research, homework, and other various topics for people who are just wanting to learn more.
