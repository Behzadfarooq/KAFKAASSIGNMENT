Artesia Transit Center is a station on Metro's Harbor Transitway and a large bus transfer station. The station is located at the southwest corner of the interchange of Interstate 110, the Harbor Freeway and California State Route 91, the Gardena Freeway. Entrances to the center are at Cassidy Street and Vermont Avenue and near 182nd Street and Hoover Street. Transitway services that continue southbound, or are traveling northbound from the Carson station enter or exit the I-110 at the entrance and exit near Torrance Boulevard via Figueroa Street.
