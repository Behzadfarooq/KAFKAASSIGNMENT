[edit] Aaron Aulsebrook-Walker

NN infant actor. I renominated the article for deletion as it was first listed with many other child actors and some editors advised me to nominate them individually. Delete. --Tone 18:45, 15 April 2006 (UTC)

  • Keep, regular performer on highly popular television show. GT 18:58, 15 April 2006 (UTC)

  • Delete. Gurgling on one TV show doesn't merit an encyclopedia entry. --Calton | Talk 02:11, 16 April 2006 (UTC)
