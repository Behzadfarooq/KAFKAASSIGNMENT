  • Note: With respect to the most recent edit of the article, the following two references are not the same. The editors of the book, Neale's Disorders of the Foot, decided that the chapter, '"Structure and function of the foot'" should be divided into two new chapters in the 7th edition:
  • Watkins, J. (2006), "Structure and function of the foot", in Lorimer, D.L., French, G., O'Donnell, M. & Burrow, J.G., Neale's Disorders of the Foot (7th ed.), Edinburgh: Churchill-Livingstone.
  • Watkins, J. (2002), "Structure and function of the foot", in Lorimer, D.L., French, G., O'Donnell, M. & Burrow, J.G., Neale's Disorders of the Foot (6th ed.), Edinburgh: Churchill-Livingstone.
