[edit] Early Life

NO Born in Kiev Artor came to live in Holland in 1994. His mother Lukina and himself left Ukraine because his father Andriy who was not a good man. Artor picked up the language pretty well and was fluent in the langauge by time he was 7. His mother moved on and met someone else, Jeffery he was good man. Artor did not like him as he was treated like a child all the time. He left home by 16 to move back to Ukraine where he became an astronaut.

[edit] Personal Life

Now living in England, Artor has decided to take up a career in being a DJ known as DJ Entee. He has a love for Bonkers which features some artists such as Scott Brown who is a friend of his. He is also best friends with Denis Wolf who is footballer currently playing for Fortuna Düsseldorf. In Dutch magazine De Doo Artor made the front page for his new televsion show Look & Find Love which is due to be on air in England in early 2008. The show is going to be about him finding love for the first time.

[edit] Recent Events

He has recently announced to The Sun that he is looking to appear on the next series of Celebrity Big Brother
