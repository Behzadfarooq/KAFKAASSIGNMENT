The following AfD-style discussion occured without proper procedure on the talk page of the Neil Cicierega article. The page did not have an appropriate template, the AfD page was not created, and the discussion lasted almost a year. Since the vast majority of votes in this discussion are keep I am going to be bold and "conclude" it, moving the AfD-related contents of the talk page to here, and adding a "survived pseudo-AfD" notice to the top of the page. --TexasDex 02:11, 9 February 2006 (UTC)
