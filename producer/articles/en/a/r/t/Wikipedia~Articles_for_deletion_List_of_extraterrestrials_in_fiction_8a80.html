[edit] List of extraterrestrials in fiction

Delete List cruft. If a fictional extraterrestrial race is notable enough it'll have an actual page on Wikipedia and be listed in the "Fictional extraterrestrial species" Category. AlistairMcMillan 23:12, 19 May 2006 (UTC)

  • Keep contains some info a category can't; useful navigation; very notable concept and topic for a list. CanadianCaesar Cæsar is turn’d to hear 00:38, 20 May 2006 (UTC)
  • Delete, a category is fine, this is just listcruft. Stifle (talk) 01:30, 20 May 2006 (UTC)
    • Not every list will automatically qualify as listcruft. CanadianCaesar Cæsar is turn’d to hear 01:47, 20 May 2006 (UTC)
      • Indeed, which is why I don't refer to every list as listcruft. This one, however, is. Stifle (talk) 11:08, 20 May 2006 (UTC)
  • Delete as per nom. -- stubblyhead | T/c 01:50, 20 May 2006 (UTC)
  • Keep. There are too many notable ETs out there and not enough editors available to write good pages. Besides, categories do not have the same navigation and comment facilities as a list. --AlainV 01:56, 20 May 2006 (UTC)
  • Keep per AlainV matching aliens with the authors/stories is a good idea. Crazynas 02:21, 20 May 2006 (UTC)
  • Keep per AlainV. --Sparky Lurkdragon 04:21, 20 May 2006 (UTC)
  • Keep per AlainV. --Terence Ong 04:37, 20 May 2006 (UTC)
We also don't have enough good editors to go through the list and filter out all the anon contributions. Are alien species that appear in a single episode of Star Trek notable? For example Angosian, Antedean, Vaadwaur... AlistairMcMillan 10:12, 20 May 2006 (UTC)
  • Keep per AlainV Lady Aleena 13:52, 20 May 2006 (UTC)
  • Comment — Restricting this list to extraterestrials with significant roles in their respective fiction would help winnow it down to a manageable group. I.e. cut out the "scenery" aliens and just keep the important characters. — RJH 17:06, 21 May 2006 (UTC)
  • Keep per AlainV. - CNichols 19:35, 22 May 2006 (UTC)
  • Comment: shouldn't the fictional fandoms have their own list? For example, List of species (Animorphs). Delete this page but keep lists by fandom.--Lkjhgfdsa 21:49, 22 May 2006 (UTC)
