  • Keep. Seems to have appeared in several porn movies. There are lots of porn stars with this name, so eventually it will have to be moved and disambiguated. --Tony Sidaway|Talk 22:36, 10 July 2005 (UTC)

  • Delete. Making a lot of porn movies isn't particularly notable in the industry, where any given "actor" cranks out a few a week.   • Delete not notable advertising. JamesBurns 08:30, 11 July 2005 (UTC)
  • Delete There are at least 8 IMDb Kitties. I try to be cautious with porn, because its impurience might slant anyone's judgment, but, if she can't provide even a surname, the potential for confusion is simply too great. Xoloz 08:58, 11 July 2005 (UTC)
