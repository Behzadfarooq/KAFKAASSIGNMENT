[edit] Landover Baptist Church

Nominator has changed mind to speedy keep/withdraw nomination Kappa 12:55, 20 May 2005 (UTC)

Promoting a commercial website. A Wiki entry that does nothing but summarize the contents of another website does not belong. Internally linked here as a "notable example of satire," alarm bells should go off when you see it next to the likes of Jonathan Swift and Mark Twain. Suggest delete or possibly put into a list of parody/satire/mockery websites --Fazdeconta 05:55, 20 May 2005 (UTC)
