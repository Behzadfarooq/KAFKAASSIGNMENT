The result of the debate was

[edit] Inside Moves

As the article says, it's a "nearly unknown movie". And this article is nothing but a spoiler (and a cast list). Uncle G 11:23, 2005 Jan 12 (UTC)

  • Delete, nothing much going on. Inter 12:13, 12 Jan 2005 (UTC)
  • Delete, no useful content. Speedy material in my opinion. Mgm|(talk) 13:32, Jan 12, 2005 (UTC)
  • Keep. This needed cleanup, not deletion. Xezbeth 15:16, Jan 12, 2005 (UTC)
  • Keep and cleanup. I agree with Xezbeth. It's notable enough, and it's on IMDB here. --Deathphoenix 17:33, 12 Jan 2005 (UTC)
    • Being listed on IMDB is not per se evidence of notability (although IMDB is a reasonably good first-stage filter for non-notability). Moreover, Wikipedia is not an IMDB mirror. Uncle G 19:49, 2005 Jan 14 (UTC)
  • Keep, cleanup would help. Wyss 23:02, 12 Jan 2005 (UTC)
  • Keep and Cleanup. Definitely not speedy material. One of my favorite movies. RickK 00:25, Jan 13, 2005 (UTC)
  • Keep. Academy Award nominee (Best Supporting Actress, as explained in the cleaned-up article now.) Samaritan 21:30, 13 Jan 2005 (UTC)
  • Keep - notable film, though it could use some expansion. The "nearly unknown" comment is incorrect, however I see that's been removed. 23skidoo 07:18, 14 Jan 2005 (UTC)
  • Keep - I find it a notable film a cult film of a few as the imdb entry comments show. vaceituno 13:58, 14 Jan 2005 (UTC)
  • Keep. For the umpteenth time, VfD is not cleanup. GRider\talk 23:36, 14 Jan 2005 (UTC)
