[edit] IronMan

IronMan (edit|talk|history|links|watch|logs)

Unsourced, apart from a blog and an unbuilt website. Full of opinions and original research. I can't tell which parts of the article are vandalism and which aren't. As an invitation-only tournament, this seems intrinsically unnotable. Delete Redirect to Iron Man (disambiguation) as suggested belowgadfium 19:11, 12 December 2006 (UTC)

  • Doesn't look like a notable tournament. Possible WP:COI. Someone typing this in could be looking for the comic book character, or info on triathlons. Redirect to Iron Man (disambiguation). Fan-1967 19:26, 12 December 2006 (UTC)
  • Redirect - As Fan-1967. Fails WP:NN, WP:NPOV and maybe even WP:SPAM. --tgheretford (talk) 19:32, 12 December 2006 (UTC)
  • Redirect per Fan-1967. Danny Lilithborne 20:35, 12 December 2006 (UTC)
  • Delete and edit the dab page directly. We shouldn't link directly to disambiguation pages. -- Bpmullins | Talk 00:31, 13 December 2006 (UTC)
  • Why not? I thought it was perfectly acceptable to have alternate spellings redirect to DABs. Personally, I'd put the DAB page at Iron Man and move that page to something like Iron Man (comic book character). Fan-1967 15:35, 13 December 2006 (UTC)
  • Redirect to Iron Man (disambiguation) (don't forget the Ironman Triathlon!) per User:Fan-1967. B.Wind 19:08, 13 December 2006 (UTC)
  • Delete and Redirect or just Redirect to Iron Man (disambiguation) as likely search term, and with current content being a potential conflict of interest and externally unverifiable through the use of third-party, factchecked, reliable sources. If a redirect to a disambig page is not acceptable, work some funky magic to move the disambig page to Iron Man and redirect there! -- saberwyn 21:26, 13 December 2006 (UTC)
  • It's the popularity of the term in reference to triathlons (as well as that TV commercial resurrecting the old Black Sabbath song) that convinces me that Tony Stark should get moved to a new title and Iron Man should be the DAB page. Fan-1967 22:01, 13 December 2006 (UTC)
