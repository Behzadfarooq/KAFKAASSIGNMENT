This is a list of notable footballers who have played for Liverpool. Generally, this means players that have played 100 or more first-class matches for the club. However, some players who have played fewer matches are also included; players who fell just short of the 100 total but made significant contributions to the club's history (e.g. Gary McAllister).

For a list of all Liverpool players, major or minor, with a Wikipedia article, see Category:Liverpool F.C. players. For player appearance records see Liverpool F.C. - Statistics and for the current squad see the main Liverpool F.C. article.

Players are listed according to the date of their first professional contracted signed with the club. Appearances and goals are for first-team competitive matches only; wartime matches are excluded. Substitute appearances included.

As of November 2, 2006.
