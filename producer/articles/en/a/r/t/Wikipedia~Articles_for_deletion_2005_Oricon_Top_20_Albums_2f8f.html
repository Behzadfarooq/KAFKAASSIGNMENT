[edit] 2005 Oricon Top 20 Albums

Wikipedia is an encyclopedia, not a magazine or some music chart board. This is unencyclopedic by nature and it does not deserve a place here. So speedy delete this article. Terence Ong Talk 11:20, 6 January 2006 (UTC)

  • Keep, like Hot 100 No. 1 Hits of 2005 (USA). A good way for readers to find articles about notable Japanese musicians and songs. Kappa 17:11, 6 January 2006 (UTC)
