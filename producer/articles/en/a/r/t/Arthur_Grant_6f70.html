Arthur Grant (1915-1972) was a British cinematographer. He succeeded Jack Asher as regular Director of Photography for Hammer Film Productions. His films for Hammer included Shadow of the Cat (1961), The Curse of the Werewolf (1961), The Phantom of the Opera (1962), The Plague of the Zombies (1966) and The Devil Rides Out (1968) among others. His final film for the studio was Demons of the Mind in 1972.
