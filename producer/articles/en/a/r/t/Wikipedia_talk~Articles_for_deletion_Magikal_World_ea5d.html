It's a pity that this is going to be deleted. Yes it may not be as notable as Mugglenet but it is in Role Play terms. It's been awarded many awards for the site, and is almost as well known as Mugglenet in the Harry Potter Community. I frequent both sites often, hearing about Magikal World on Mugglenet itself, so it must be of some notability.

Magikal World may not be as big and as famous as Mugglenet but it does look like it's the biggest of all role play forums. Most HP role players know of it. I can't think of any other that is bigger and more notable. Both MuggleNet and Hpana and others praise Magikal World. They mention this site more often then any other role play.

Magikal Network is a household name among those who frequent HP forums. It is often surprising to new members how accessible the site is, considering how much word of mouth it gets. The site is enormous, well-organized, and a giant among RPG sites.

The domain name is attached to the main pages. The other forum is connected to the network, which is a new venture by Magikal World, setting up support for other websites in the HP community, yes there aren't many members on that, but that part of the forum is still young. Clicking on the link on the main pages for Role Play forum will take you to the Magikal Role Play. It was left on the proboards site, to prove that you didnt need money to run a successful website, which Magikal has proven over and over again. I understand that these comments will probably be deleted all over again, but surely we have a right to speak on behalf of the site, and give our opinions to why the page for Magikal World should be kept.

Magikal World is a great Harry Potter site,yes it may not be as big as mugglnet as others have said.But i have looked many times and have not yet found a Harry Potter role play site with as many members as Magikal World.Please dont delte it.
