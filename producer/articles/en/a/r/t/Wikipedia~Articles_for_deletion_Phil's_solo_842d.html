[edit] Phil's solo

No information of this alleged Brand X song can be found on Yahoo. 50+ links on Yahoo, but all related to Phil Collins's solo work and not this song. [1]. Page creator also added this as a track to unrelated albums recorded near a decade before its performance, [2]. Ataricodfish 22:54, 9 July 2006 (UTC)
