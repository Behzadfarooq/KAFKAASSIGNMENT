[edit] Star Wars Fanon

Non-notable wiki for Star Wars fan fiction. Fails WP:WEB. I don't have a problem with Star Wars pages, but this is just a site where fans make up their own stories and characters. No mention in any independent third-party sources I can find. Kafziel Talk 14:51, 13 October 2006 (UTC)

  • Delete I had an edit conflict with you in creating this AfD. :-P EVula 14:55, 13 October 2006 (UTC)
  • Delete While it's great to promote the wiki spirit and all, Wikia alone hosts over 1500 wikis. They aren't all notable, and only a handfull pass WP:WEB, and this doesn't seem to be among them. Andrew Lenahan - Starblind 16:05, 13 October 2006 (UTC)
  • Keep. Oh, and don't group us altogether as "kids". I take that as an insult! Jasca Ducato 18:41, 13 October 2006 (UTC)
    • You need to give a reason for keeping it. This is not a vote. Sorry about the "kids" bit; no offense was intended and I have removed it. Kafziel Talk 18:52, 13 October 2006 (UTC)
  • Delete as failing WP:WEB. JoshuaZ 19:30, 13 October 2006 (UTC)
  • Delete per JoshuaZ. There are many more popular wikias than this. Plus, Jasca Ducato is an ass. Ramiro Rivarez 22:18, 13 October 2006 (UTC)
    WP:CIVIL. Read it. EVula 22:34, 13 October 2006 (UTC)
    • Comment: I'd also like to point out that Mr. Rivarez has been banned from Star Wars Fanon for vandalism and has vandalized my userpage and talk page here on Wikipedia numerous times in retaliation. Just a note to take into consideration when an admin decides to close this...StarNeptune 03:11, 15 October 2006 (UTC)
  • Delete nothing particular about the site to give the slightest sign it passes WP:WEB.--Nilfanion (talk) 00:58, 14 October 2006 (UTC)
  • Delete fictional wikia made up of fanfiction characters, non notable. As per nom. Darthgriz98 01:56, 15 October 2006 (UTC)
  • Delete notability not established and currently written in an unacceptable tone. Khatru2 06:02, 15 October 2006 (UTC)
  • Delete Non-notable wikia. Pages about more popular wikis than this have been deleted as well. 82 20:59, 15 October 2006 (UTC)
    • Comment: In response to the nominator's comment, this wiki has, in fact, been mentioned on Rebelscum forums. 82 21:01, 15 October 2006 (UTC)
      • Comment Well, it still fails the notability requirements. EVula 21:04, 15 October 2006 (UTC)
