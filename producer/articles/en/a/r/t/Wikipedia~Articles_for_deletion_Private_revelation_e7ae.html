[edit] Private revelation

This is almost certainly a copy and paste from somewhere; it reads like an essay, and it is stated in terms which support a single, restricted view of what a "private revelation" might mean. It's part of a fairly active campaign by Vaquero100 (talk • contribs) to promote Roman Catholicism on Wikipedia. If it's not a copy & paste job it is probably original research, since there are precisely zero sources. It's also written in unencyclopaedic tone. Just zis Guy you know? 21:20, 7 July 2006 (UTC)
