[edit] Five Star Painting

The creator of the article (User:MA77) bears a username similar to that of the owner of the business the article is about (Matt Anderson). There are only 177 Google hits for '"Five star painting" Minnesota', and the creator of this page has edited the Painter and decorator article to include this business. [1] This seems very much like an advertisement/vanity page for a small business. — Indi [ talk ] 21:24, 8 February 2006 (UTC)

  • Delete as WP:VSCA. The company's website (Alexa: 4,591,364) doesn't make it seem too notable. Reminds me of the "Five Star Cleaners" down the street from me... which is about as encyclopedic of a subject. --Kinu 21:39, 8 February 2006 (UTC)
  • Strong delete Blatant Advertising Avi 22:01, 8 February 2006 (UTC)
  • Delete - advertising. New, nonnotable business. Not even a listing on BBB. —ERcheck @ 02:49, 9 February 2006 (UTC)
  • Delete - obvious vanity/advertising. Elijya 17:20, 9 February 2006 (UTC)
