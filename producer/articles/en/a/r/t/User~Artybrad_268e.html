Alias 'Artybrad', born 1986, is an art student at Goldsmiths College, part of the University of London and he currently lives in Deptford close to Greenwich. However, he originates from Dorset where he lived in Merley until the age of 15 when he moved to Broadstone, close to Corfe Hills School where he studied GCSEs and A Levels in art, physics and classical civilisations.

My work has recently involved photography and flaneurism of the city.

My most major Wikipedia edit to date has been on the Tate Modern page. I'm still yet to start an article.
