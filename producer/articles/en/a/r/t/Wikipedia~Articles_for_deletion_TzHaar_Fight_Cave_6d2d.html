Keep Tarikochi 05:58, 2 February 2006 (UTC) - I do not think it should be deleted or even be considered for deletion at all. This is a major part of RuneScape, and considered one of the highest honours to complete, giving a necessary reason to keep the article.

Keep The only other thing you can do with this article is to merge it with RuneScape Locations and that would be very very very messy. J.J.Sagnella 07:30, 2 February 2006 (UTC)

It should not be deleted because there is no reason for it to be.You may think that it is unencyclopedic, but the people who actually like to play RuneScape and go and fight these things may need advice or tips on how to beat them,etc.

Tarikochi 01:39, 3 February 2006 (UTC) - I would also like to add that the "TzHaar Fight Cave" is not a location, despite its name. The "Fight Cave" is actually a RuneScape mini-game, furthering the reason why this article should not be deleted.
