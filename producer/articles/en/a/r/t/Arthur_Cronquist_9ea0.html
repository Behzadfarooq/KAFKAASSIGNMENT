Arthur J. Cronquist (1919–1992) was a North American botanist, a specialist on Compositae, most famous for the Cronquist system, laid down in

  • An Integrated System of Classification of Flowering Plants (1981) and
  • The Evolution and Classification of Flowering Plants (1st edition 1968; 2nd edition 1988) ISBN 0-89327-332-5

Two plant genera from the sunflower family (Asteraceae) have been named in his honor :

  • Cronquistia R.M. King (a possible synonym of Carphochaete A. Gray)
  • Cronquistianthus R.M.King & H.Rob. (sometimes included as a group in Eupatorium L.)
The standard author abbreviation Cronquist may be used to indicate this person in citing a botanical name.
