Arguments that it is a hoax:
  1. The WP entry for rabbit doesn't list "cuniculus" as a synonym for rabbit.
  2. Dictionary.com defines "cuniculus" as "The burrow of the itch mite in the skin", or a pacas, which is found in South America (and not a rabbit).
  3. The Gopherus rafinesque is a tortoise, not the "Arctic Goffer [sic]."
  4. There are no Google hits for either "Alfred Vandoogle," "Vandoogle Association of Arctic Mammals", or "West Pacific Sea Pelican".
  5. The anonymous poster's IP address is in Toronto, Ontario, not Seattle. And what would a microbiologist be doing studying rabbits anyway?
