[edit] Summary of assessments

Is there a way to get a summary of the assessments? Something like the score for each assessment done for each article? Displayed on the main assessment page, rather than having to click through to each article's assessment? Or is that something that comes after the assessment period is over? I hope to have time to assess some of these myself, and I would love to see the Wikipedia collection of natural disaster articles eventually worked up to something where a portal could be implemented, if such a thing doesn't already exist. I know there is a Wikibook on Disasters somewhere, but I don't know what happened with that. Carcharoth 20:59, 23 February 2006 (UTC)
