  • Delete per nomination and per Night Gyr's comments. --Dennisthe2 02:24, 16 January 2007 (UTC)
  • Delete - The author may want to try writing about the company who made these lines of boat and then give mention to this particular boat. But right now, it fails Wikipedia:notability. Mkdwtalk 03:03, 16 January 2007 (UTC)

  • Delete for reasons outlined above. BTfromLA 07:30, 16 January 2007 (UTC)

  • Delete per nom. --Magarmach 20:14, 16 January 2007 (UTC)
