[edit] Changing Homes FC

Changing Homes FC (edit|talk|history|links|watch|logs)

Amateur Sunday league club. Chick Bowen 01:15, 28 November 2006 (UTC)

Note: I've prodded the individual players for whom articles were created. Chick Bowen 01:18, 28 November 2006 (UTC)
  • Delete or possible Speedy A7-article doesn't even assert notability and what little information there is is unreferenced. Seraphimblade 01:48, 28 November 2006 (UTC)
  • Delete - As per Seraphimblade. When I first saw the title I thought it was a local version of "Changing Rooms". Now making over a football club, now that's exciting... Spawn Man 02:33, 28 November 2006 (UTC)
  • Delete per above. MER-C 02:53, 28 November 2006 (UTC)
  • Delete - If these played on Saturdays, they would be several levels below accepted the notability criterion. No consensus has been reached on Sunday league teams, other than they are all probably non-notable. This one especially so, they are only in their second season and there is nothing in the article to raise them above the crowd - fchd 07:40, 28 November 2006 (UTC)
  • Delete per precedent, appears that Sunday teams always get red-carded as NN. Also, the players, if their only claim to notability is being on this team, should be added to this AfD so we don't get orphan articles to AfD if the prods are pulled. Tubezone 08:11, 28 November 2006 (UTC)
  • Delete, nn football club. --Terence Ong (C | R) 08:34, 28 November 2006 (UTC)
  • This discussion has been included in WikiProject Football's list of football (soccer) related deletions. ChrisTheDude 11:41, 28 November 2006 (UTC)
  • Delete Sorry lads but the team doesn't come close to meeting the football project's agreed guidelines on team notability. I'm also torn on whether or not to nominate the league they play in (the WSFL WindowMan Championship) for AfD as well.... ChrisTheDude 13:17, 28 November 2006 (UTC)
    • Yeah, I thought of nominating the league. I don't think in general we do Sunday leagues. Chick Bowen 17:46, 28 November 2006 (UTC)
  • Delete the team, league, and any players who haven't played anywhere notable (I doubt they have anyone who's played internationally or in the FA Premiership). It's basically a recreational team in a recreational league. —The preceding unsigned comment was added by 65.212.122.142 (talk • contribs).
