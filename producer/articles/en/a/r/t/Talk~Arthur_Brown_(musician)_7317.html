[edit] "banned in the united states"

i did a pretty zealous scouring of the internet for something on this and found absolutely nothing indicating he was banned from the united states except for an about.com article that linked to a gig history page (the owner of the page asked wikipedia to stop linking to it, for whatever reason. you can find it via google pretty easily though). the webpage cited that he played a show in texas in 2001, so unless he was banned within the last five years... he wasn't and the assertion is completely false. --Friendship hurricane 18:18, 13 September 2006 (UTC)
