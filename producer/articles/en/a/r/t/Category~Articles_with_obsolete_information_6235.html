This category is for articles containing disputed statements which an editor attributes to out-of-date references. Articles may be added to this category with the {{out of date}} template, which is distinct from the similar {{update}} (see Category:Wikipedia articles in need of updating for articles using that template).

A separate list using a different mechanism is described at Wikipedia:Updating information, a more recent and preferred alternative to Wikipedia:As of.
