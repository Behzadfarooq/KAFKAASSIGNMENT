[edit] Arcata High School

Arcata_High_School (edit|talk|history|links|watch|logs) - (View log)
  • This page may not be relevant under Wikipedia's notability standards. Remember that secondary schools are not inherenty notable, and this article fails to prove relevance. DukeOfSquirrels 08:21, 19 February 2007 (UTC)
  • This AfD nomination was incomplete. It is listed now. DumbBOT 12:43, 19 February 2007 (UTC)
  • Delete. Inadequate references/sources. Also, I find no strong evidence of a notability claim. Existence is not notability. WMMartin 13:50, 19 February 2007 (UTC)
  • Keep — Meets my criteria for H.S. notability. — RJH (talk) 20:07, 19 February 2007 (UTC)
  • Delete No notability, no third-party sources, no verification, no passage of SCHOOLS3 (or, as it's now fortunately known, SCHOOLS). -- Kicking222 20:56, 19 February 2007 (UTC)
  • Delete per WMMartin. Shall Wikipedia be advertising for schools??? — Mannfred5 (talk • contribs) has made few or no other edits outside this topic.
  • Delete per my arguments here. Soltak | Talk 23:40, 19 February 2007 (UTC)
  • Keep per my bloviation here. Noroton 16:13, 20 February 2007 (UTC)
  • Delete There is literally nothing notable here--just a list of the standard activities of an ordinarily good HS. DGG 04:43, 21 February 2007 (UTC)
  • Keep 1920s high school in US west coast is quite significant. --Vsion 06:27, 21 February 2007 (UTC)
  • Delete No independent reliable sources means nothing to hang an article on. Shimeru 08:26, 21 February 2007 (UTC)
  • Keep per my belief that secondary schools are notable, as are small towns and villages. Would not be opposed to a merge per WP:LOCAL until additional sources are provided, but the current links do at least verify the existence of the institution. Yamaguchi先生 02:34, 22 February 2007 (UTC)
  • Keep High Schools are generally notable. This one is a major school, with plenty of independent sources that should be available for the 80+ years it has been around. --Aude (talk) 06:55, 23 February 2007 (UTC)
  • Keep as a major school of a significant age, this passes the test for notability. (jarbarf) 00:25, 24 February 2007 (UTC)
  • Keep per Noroton. AntiVan 05:35, 24 February 2007 (UTC)
