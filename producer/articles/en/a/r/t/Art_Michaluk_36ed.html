Art Michaluk

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Art Michaluk was a Canadian ice hockey defenceman, who was born May 4, 1923 in Canmore, Alberta.

He served in the Royal Canadian Air Force during World War II. Michaluk spent most of his career with the Providence Reds of the AHL. He also played with the Chicago Blackhawks of the NHL during the 1947-48 season. After he retired from hockey, he served on the Calgary police force for 23 years.

He died on March 14, 2006, following a battle with cancer.
