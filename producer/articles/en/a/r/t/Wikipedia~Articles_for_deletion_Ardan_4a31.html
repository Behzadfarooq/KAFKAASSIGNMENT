Do I even need to ask? Are gym instructors from the Sootopolis City of the fictional world of Pokémon encyclopedically noteworthy? This character garners 137 unique google hits [1]. Jimbo Wales once said in an interview with the Washington Post: "It is our goal to put the sum of all human knowledge in the form of an encyclopedia in the hands of every single person on the planet for free."
