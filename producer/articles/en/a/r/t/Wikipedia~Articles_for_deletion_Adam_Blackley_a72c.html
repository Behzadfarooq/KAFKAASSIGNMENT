[edit] Adam Blackley

Contested A7 speedy. Article about student film maker. Only claim to fame is an IMDB entry showing that he is an "associate producer" on an upcoming film. A Google search found that this distinction is available to anyone willing to donate $1 to the film.[1] --Allen3 talk 11:49, 4 May 2006 (UTC)

  • Delete. The link does indeed suggest that "associate producer" status is available for a donation. Notability is definitely not established. Zetawoof(ζ) 12:13, 4 May 2006 (UTC)
  • Speedy Delete, A7 nn bio, nothing asserted in article. IMDb entries like this will signal its downfall as a credible source, the project seems to be some kind of stunt and subject's relation to it is laughable, having been bought for a dollar. Deizio talk 14:55, 4 May 2006 (UTC)
  • Speedy delete Buying your own credit in a one-second movie is not notability. --TheKoG (talk|contribs) 14:58, 4 May 2006 (UTC)
  • Speedy Delete I've prod'ed and AfD'ed a few other "producers" from The 1 Second Film. It's a charity fundraiser where anyone can buy a credit. Fan1967 16:02, 4 May 2006 (UTC)
  • Speedy delete I also bought a credit for The 1 Second Film, and you don't see me writing an article about myself on WP. -- Kicking222 16:25, 4 May 2006 (UTC)
