[edit] Rufio

Non-notable per WP:MUSIC Nv8200p talk 03:59, 17 June 2006 (UTC)

  • Delete no assertion of notability AdamBiswanger1 04:25, 17 June 2006 (UTC)
    I didn't realize they were on the Warped Tour, so I'll change my vote to Keep. (Even though each Warped Tour features 100+ bands. AdamBiswanger1 20:58, 17 June 2006 (UTC)
  • Delete. Concur with above. Notability is missing. Interlingua talk 05:32, 17 June 2006 (UTC)
  • Keep. Recordings and tours add up to notability. TruthbringerToronto 05:57, 17 June 2006 (UTC)
  • Strong keep Article states that they were on a national tour (Warped Tour) and they meet others of the notability criteria. Tag the article to have notability asserted instead of deleting it. fuzzy510 06:21, 17 June 2006 (UTC)
  • Weak Delete Doesn't quite reach acceptable levels of notability or WP:MUSIC.--Auger Martel 07:39, 17 June 2006 (UTC)
  • Clear keep. Released two full albums on Nitro Records, and they did appear on Warped Tour (see [1]). Notability is asserted. Punkmorten 07:52, 17 June 2006 (UTC)
  • Keep. Content seems fine to me. David L Rattigan 10:33, 17 June 2006 (UTC)
  • Keep. Seem notable. Just came across this page after seeing a music clip on Australian TV. Cedars 16:27, 17 June 2006 (UTC)
  • Very strong keep, plenty of media attention, charting, and touring. --badlydrawnjeff talk 17:12, 17 June 2006 (UTC)
  • Strong Keep. plentiful references in several mainstream music papers, also per Punkmorten W guice 18:34, 17 June 2006 (UTC)
  • Strong keep Absolutely a notable band, if for nothing else besides touring and being on a notable record label. Plus, unlike 99.9% of bands with articles on WP, I've heard of them. -- Kicking222 23:10, 17 June 2006 (UTC)
  • Keep per Badlydrawnjeff. King rich 13:52, 18 June 2006 (UTC)
  • Strong Keep. This deletion is a joke, there is no point to delete this article. They’re a real band that tours and have a nice fan base. DidYouLoseASock 19:26, 18 June 2006 (UTC)
  • Keep, couple of albums on notable indie labels and a well established tour. Good enough for me per WP:MUSIC. Rockpocket 06:35, 19 June 2006 (UTC)
  • Keep. Notable enough. WesleyDodds 07:34, 21 June 2006 (UTC)
