[edit] Milwaukee Brewers: All-Time Team

Appears to be original research. Googling "Milwaukee Brewers" "all-time team" returns nothing that would indicate either the Brewers or a media outlet named such a team. Also, the members of the All-Time Team appear to change depending on who's editing the article. A Brewers Walk of Fame exists [1], but the membership isn't consistent. If I come across any similar pages for other teams, I'll be listing those as well. djrobgordon 01:29, 10 March 2006 (UTC)
