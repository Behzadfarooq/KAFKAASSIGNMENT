[edit] OrthodoxWiki

NN wiki. --Jeffrey O. Gustafson - Shazaam! - <*> 02:02, 18 December 2005 (UTC)

  • Keep Over 1,300 articles and accelerating. CalJW 03:03, 18 December 2005 (UTC)
  • Keep or merge. How many Orthodox wikis exist? -- JJay 03:23, 18 December 2005 (UTC)
  • Delete. Being a wiki does not procure notability. --Apostrophe 05:19, 18 December 2005 (UTC)
  • Delete - A site's use of MediaWiki is irrelevant in determining notability. FCYTravis 07:00, 18 December 2005 (UTC)
  • Keep - Notable enough. I've went there and I think I've heard of it mentioned online several places.--T. Anthony 10:40, 18 December 2005 (UTC)
  • Keep—I'm one of the project's sysops, so I'm biased (I still get to vote!), but I have heard it being talked about it a good bit even outside of the Internet. —Preost talk contribs 10:54, 18 December 2005 (UTC)
  • Keep Notability is not official Wikipedia policy, merely a guideline by which some but by no means all Wikipedians operate. The site exists and is verifiable. Jcuk 21:57, 18 December 2005 (UTC)
    • Comment Yes, notability is official Wikipedia Policy, Werdna648T/C\@ 12:07, 19 December 2005 (UTC)
      • Sorry but I cant see anything on that page that says anything about notability. Verifiability, yes, but not notability. Jcuk 17:26, 19 December 2005 (UTC)
    • Comment: Notability is a proposed Wikipedia policy. —Preost talk contribs 17:33, 19 December 2005 (UTC)
      • Granted, but as of right now its only proposed policy.Jcuk 21:36, 19 December 2005 (UTC)
  • Keep. A well-maintained, informative place and with broad audience and editorship. Unlike the rest of wikicruft listed here it has notable context in wikipedia. mikka (t) 23:41, 18 December 2005 (UTC)
  • Keep, as per mikkalai. Can't sleep, clown will eat me 08:21, 19 December 2005 (UTC)
  • Keep -- OrthodoxWiki isn't an obscure resource so its article shouldn't be deleted for allegedly being "non-notable". Critias 12:04, 19 December 2005 (UTC)
  • Delete. Not close to being notable, as based on WP:WEB. Traffic Rank for orthodoxwiki.org: 422,224 (not in top 10,000) [1]. 703 active users [2] (not over 5,000). No evidence that it has been the subject or national or international media attention. And having a few links from wikipedia doesn't demonstrate notable context. Agnte 17:39, 19 December 2005 (UTC)
  • Strong Keep Heck, if Wookieepedia (Star Wars) can have it's own page, why can't this? Dumanov 01:28, 21 December 2005
  • Delete nn.  Grue  15:00, 21 December 2005 (UTC)
  • Keep - Looks like a quality resource that can only become more notable in the future. --Pmsyyz 09:11, 24 December 2005 (UTC)
  • keep please this wiki is notable Yuckfoo 07:53, 28 December 2005 (UTC)
