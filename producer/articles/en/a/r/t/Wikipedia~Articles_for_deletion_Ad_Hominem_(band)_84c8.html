[edit] Ad Hominem (band)

Redlink label, no circulation information, no evidence that French Nazi Metal has a sizable following - though they certainly have many albums. - CrazyRussian talk/email 15:02, 17 October 2006 (UTC)

  • Delete. The few bluelinks to the lead singer's other bands appear to go to unrelated articles, which blows out of the water this band's only chance of meeting WP:MUSIC. (French Nazi Metal; who knew? You learn something new every day...) --Aaron 17:32, 17 October 2006 (UTC)
  • Delete "The band is about the eradication of humanity" -- if their philosophy prevails, nobody will be around to buy their albums, or read their Wikipedia article.  ;) NawlinWiki 20:27, 17 October 2006 (UTC)
  • Delete per WP:NOT. =Axlq 04:22, 19 October 2006 (UTC)
  • Keep. I found this Afd as I'm planning to write a stub for Avantgarde Music [1], which is a notable indie record label, and a label through which this band seems to have released one full-length and one EP [2] [3]. I'm pretty sure AMG entry is very rare for a French nazi black metal group too, and their latest album is sold on Amazon. Prolog 16:59, 23 October 2006 (UTC)
    • Comment. As the bluelink shows, I've created a stub for the record label. However, in terms of WP:MUSIC this is a bit borderline case, as the band's second release through the label is an EP. The band hasn't probably toured either, as those can be a handful for one-man bands. Prolog 19:28, 23 October 2006 (UTC)
