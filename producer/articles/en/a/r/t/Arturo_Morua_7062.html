Arturo Morua (born on May 3, 1978) from his hometown of Guadalajara, Mexico, is a professional boxer, whos nickname is "El Silencioso", and fought numerous times on Telefutura and Telemundo.

In October 2001, Morua toke on hard hitting, Cosme Rivera (21-6-2) and defeated him by UD12 to win the WBA Fedecentro Light Welterweight Title.

Titles won:

WBA Fedecentro (Light Welterweight division)

WBO Latino (Light Welterweight division)

WBO Inter-Continental (Light Welterweight division)


"El Silencioso" has been known for fighting good fighters and in 2004 seemed to strike gold with big fights with the following fighters:
