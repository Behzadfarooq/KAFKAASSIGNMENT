  • Keep all; reality show winners are fixtures in our popular culture. Not all of them are celebrities in the sense of being a name most people would recognize, but that does not mean they are not known to many millions of people. In Chip Arndt's case, though, he has legitimate celebrity in the gay community and has used that celebrity consistently to raise money for gay causes even though several years have passed since his win on the Amazing Race. He is still able to make a living as a public speaker. --Mileage 00:48, 9 October 2006 (UTC)
