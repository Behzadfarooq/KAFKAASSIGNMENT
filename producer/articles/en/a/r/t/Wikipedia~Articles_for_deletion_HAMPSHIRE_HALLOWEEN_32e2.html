[edit] HAMPSHIRE HALLOWEEN

Article about a Halloween party. Not notable. DJ Clayworth 18:31, 2 November 2005 (UTC)

  • Delete all non-notable Hallowe'en parties. Tonywalton |Talk 18:39, 2 November 2005 (UTC)
  • Delete as non-notable party... Mwa-ha-ha!--Isotope23 18:45, 2 November 2005 (UTC)
  • Delete as very NN. PJM 18:50, 2 November 2005 (UTC)
  • Delete. If this is a conspicuously important part of Hampshire College student life, the contributors of this article are invited to add something short and appropriate to the article on Hampshire College. HAMPSHIRE HALLOWEEN is not a useful redirect so should not be kept. The work involved in a proper merge-and-delete is too much to put on the shoulders of the closing sysop so I won't propose that. Dpbsmith (talk) 20:11, 2 November 2005 (UTC)
  • Comment Hampshire Haloween was notable at one point because it was written up in Playboy Magazine (I have no cite, this was what people said when I was there) as one of the parties not to miss due to various college party crazinesses. I'll look for some more definitive cites to see if I can back this up & possibly do a small rewrite if notability can be established. Jessamyn 20:27, 2 November 2005 (UTC)
* that said, a merge seems appropriate in any case. Jessamyn 20:28, 2 November 2005 (UTC)
  • Delete as standalone article. However, as user Jessamyn who lives in the region, says it forms a notable part of Hampshire College life, something appropriate should be included in the article. Capitalistroadster 00:00, 3 November 2005 (UTC)
  • Delete Non-notable. *drew 05:07, 5 November 2005 (UTC)
