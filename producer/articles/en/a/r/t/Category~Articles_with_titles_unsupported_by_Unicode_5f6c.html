Category:Articles with titles unsupported by Unicode

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

This category is for articles that use characters not defined in unicode or text styles. Styles such as italic, bold, underline, strikethru and colors are not part of plain text character encoding. An example of an unsupported character would be The Love Symbol Prince has frequently used.

To add articles into this category, use {{no unicode character|title=Correct Title}}.
