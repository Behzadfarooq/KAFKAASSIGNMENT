[edit] Simon Riley WWE

The article is unreferenced and a web search on Simon Riley reveals no articles at all. Even if this article isn't a hoax, the wrestler/trainer looks to be non-notable and the information unverified. Dugwiki 22:02, 17 October 2006 (UTC)

  • Also, fyi, the author of the Simon Riley article also added lines to many WWE diva articles saying that "he had dated/had an affair with/had wild sex with" that Diva. I am removing all uncited references to Simon Riley from the diva and OVW pages. Dugwiki 22:20, 17 October 2006 (UTC)
