Arthur Cecil Bateman (born 30 October 1890 in County Cavan) was an Irish cricketer. A right-handed batsman, he played two first-class matches for Ireland, one in 1913, the other in 1914. Both were against Scotland.

Whilst fighting in the First World War, he was declared missing, presumed dead on 28 March 1918 near the town of Arras,France.
