[edit] Prinadora

With 44 matches on Google for this subject [1], is this merge worthy or should it be deleted? --GRider\talk 20:19, 7 Mar 2005 (UTC)

  • Move to List of Star Trek characters and delete. There sure have been a lot of minor Star Trek characters lately. --Allen3 04:01, Mar 8, 2005 (UTC)
  • Delete and do not move/merge. ComCat 04:17, 8 Mar 2005 (UTC)
  • Merge to "List of minor characters on Star Trek" and redirect, don't delete. I don't think I have ever seen a female Ferengi actually appear on the show, which is obvious considering the Ferengi culture. Thus Prinadora doesn't deserve her own article. — JIP | Talk 06:19, 8 Mar 2005 (UTC)
    • What about Moogie? Anyway, Merge and redirect. Too minor to justify a solo article. 23skidoo 00:06, 11 Mar 2005 (UTC)
  • Merge and Redirect to List of Star Trek characters DaveTheRed 06:59, 8 Mar 2005 (UTC)
  • Merge and redirect to List of Star Trek characters. Megan1967 07:14, 8 Mar 2005 (UTC)
  • Merge or keep, necessary information. Kappa 10:32, 8 Mar 2005 (UTC)
  • Delete, after putting name on List of Star Trek characters. --BM 12:52, 8 Mar 2005 (UTC)
  • Merge and redirect to List of Star Trek characters. If that list starts getting too big, split it into smaller lists, e.g. List of Ferengis etc. Miss Pippa 16:41, 8 Mar 2005 (UTC)
  • Merge. Not sufficiently significant individually, but may as well keep the info somewhere. Average Earthman 19:02, 8 Mar 2005 (UTC)
  • Delete since only appears in dialog, not 'in the flesh'. Second choice, merge. Also, AFAIK Ishka is a female Ferengi who appeared in 5 DS9 episodes. Niteowlneils 17:18, 9 Mar 2005 (UTC)
    • With or without her clothes? — JIP | Talk 05:33, 10 Mar 2005 (UTC)
