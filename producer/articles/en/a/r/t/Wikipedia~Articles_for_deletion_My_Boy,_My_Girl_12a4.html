[edit] My Boy, My Girl

My Boy, My Girl (edit|talk|history|links|watch|logs) – (View log)

These are separate articles for a single story arc of the TV series Love Spell. If you look at the articles, they actually contain little-to-no additional info compared to the episode summaries already on the parent article, and are entirely redundant. Whatever info that can be merged should be merged back into the parent article and the separate articles should be deleted, per them being useless/redundant. Shrumster 19:37, 7 February 2007 (UTC)

I am also nominating the following related pages because of the same reasons given above:

Wanted: Mr. Perfect (edit|talk|history|links|watch|logs)
Charm & Crystal (edit|talk|history|links|watch|logs)
Home Switch Home (edit|talk|history|links|watch|logs)
Pasko na, Santa ko (edit|talk|history|links|watch|logs)

Shrumster 19:40, 7 February 2007 (UTC)

  • Merge and redirect back to the parent article "Love Spell". That way, anybody searching for a specific episode will be dumped at a location where they can find the information they're looking for. -- saberwyn 20:15, 7 February 2007 (UTC)
  • Merge and redirect per saberwyn Bucketsofg 20:20, 7 February 2007 (UTC)
  • Delete - No need to merge. Looks like a page in the director's notebook. Lajbi Holla @ me 02:00, 9 February 2007 (UTC)
  • Delete per Lajbi. Wikipedia doesn't need these. --Howard the Duck 11:50, 10 February 2007 (UTC)
  • Delete - I can't see how individual story arcs are even close to being important enough to include in an encyclopedia. ---J.S (T/C/WRE) 02:04, 13 February 2007 (UTC)
