[edit] Noadiah

  • Delete Non-Notable (Wikipedia is not an indiscriminate collection of information), the name only appears one time (in passing, no less) in the scriptures. I can't imagine how the stub will ever expanded.--WilliamThweatt 03:36, 24 April 2006 (UTC)
  • Keep or merge with Book of Nehemiah. A one-time Biblical character would almost certainly be more notable than an episode of the Simpsons; there's gotta be some kind of secondary literature out there somewhere. If not, just merge/redirect it. CanadianCaesar Et tu, Brute? 05:15, 24 April 2006 (UTC)
  • Merge to Book of Nehemiah, just to give the benefit of the doubt. --Icarus 07:45, 24 April 2006 (UTC)
  • Merge per the Canadian. --Eivindt@c 12:24, 24 April 2006 (UTC)
  • Merge Too brief of mention to warrant entire article StuffOfInterest 18:44, 25 April 2006 (UTC)
  • Merge with Book of Nehemiah per above. --Bachrach44 19:03, 25 April 2006 (UTC)
  • Merge with Book of Nehemiah --JBJ 20:37, 25 April 2006 (UTC)
  • Merge with Book of Nehemiah per above. While CanadianCaesar makes a valid point about secondary sources, I am skeptical such information exists.
