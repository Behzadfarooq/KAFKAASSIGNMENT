[edit] El-Gouna

advertising the resort (malformed AfD - completing. No vote. ViridaeTalk 08:33, 2 July 2006 (UTC))

  • Delete, blatant ad for what seems to be a non-notable resort. --Coredesat talk. o.o;; 08:47, 2 July 2006 (UTC)
  • Delete WP:NPOV and WP:SPAM, not notable. SM247My Talk 10:55, 2 July 2006 (UTC)
  • Weak keep I've edited out the ads and will add a little content when I get some time (there won't be much as it's modern and man-made). Dlyons493 Talk 12:28, 2 July 2006 (UTC)
  • Weak keep Based on current content. It's pretty thin but at least now it doesn't read as a blatant advert. If it read more as a geographical entry I'd say keep rather than weak keep. In any case it needs expansion. Brian 12:48, 2 July 2006 (UTC)btball
  • Delete fails WP:VER and is therefore WP:OR. Ste4k 18:46, 2 July 2006 (UTC)
Comment It's completely verifiable and obviously not original research - just Google it. There are certainly grounds on which one might argue for deletion of this article, but not those ones. Dlyons493 Talk 18:52, 2 July 2006 (UTC)
  • Delete. Obviously it is verifiable and that is not the issue. But an article about an un-notable resort is still spam once you've edited out the most obvious advertisement. Pascal.Tesson 19:08, 2 July 2006 (UTC)
  • Keep as geographic entry (place name), which can be encyclopedically expanded. --MCB 05:30, 3 July 2006 (UTC)
