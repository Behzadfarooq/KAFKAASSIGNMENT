  • Delete, nothing on Google News Archive for maria.swan+model. The sources provided in article and AFD so far are none of them reliable. Fails WP:BIO from what I see. -- Dhartung | Talk 10:35, 16 March 2007 (UTC)

  • Keep Multiple magazine cover appearances: [1], [2], and [3], and an appearance on the German Oliver Geissen Show. Clearly passes WP:BIO: "A person is notable if he or she has been the subject of secondary sources that are reliable, intellectually independent, and independent of the subject." This is a celebrity, not a vanity page. Using WP:N to delete such pages is an abuse of the term "notability." A celebrity, even if one particular editor has not heard of her, is by definition notable. It is Wikipedia's goal to provide unbiased, sourced articles on just such subjects.
