[edit] T-Shirt Hell

Just an online T-shirt shop. Same as every other T-shirt shop out there Phileas 04:26, 7 June 2006 (UTC)

  • Keep. Well known, notable, has been featured on ABC TV. Crystallina 04:47, 7 June 2006 (UTC)
  • Delete, no evidence of notability.--Peta 05:55, 7 June 2006 (UTC)
  • Delete, not notable enough even though it has a news mention (good cite, though). -- Kjkolb 07:23, 7 June 2006 (UTC)
  • Keep In my limited message board experience, this is popular as a humor site as well as a t-shirt site. It's well-known enough to be notable. Ace of Sevens 09:01, 7 June 2006 (UTC)
  • Delete, nn website. --Terence Ong 09:29, 7 June 2006 (UTC)
  • Delete no evidence of notability. Bwithh 10:52, 7 June 2006 (UTC)
  • Keep, notable website even without the media mentions in Melbourne's The Age, a segment on a CNN broadcast, and Philadelphia Weekly, among others. --badlydrawnjeff talk 11:12, 7 June 2006 (UTC)
  • Strong keep I don't like T-Shirt Hell, but it passes WP:WEB. The site has, as shown above, many media references. Its Alexa rank is in the top 5,000 [1], which is certainly high enough for an article, and a huge number of other sites (over 1,600) link to it. The article simply explains what the site is, and does not read like advertising. "T-Shirt Hell" (in quotes) gets 236 thousand Google hits, and if you just Google "t-shirt"- a word that gets over 87 million hits- T-Shirt Hell is the very first link [2]. -- Kicking222 11:50, 7 June 2006 (UTC)
  • Keep - meets WP:WEB per pertinent arguments from Kicking222. definately notable, and verifyable from independant sources. - Peripitus (Talk) 12:38, 7 June 2006 (UTC)
  • Keep per Kicking222. Tevildo 12:47, 7 June 2006 (UTC)
  • Keep All indications are that this site meets WP:WEB.--208.215.25.131 13:35, 7 June 2006 (UTC)
  • Keep per Kicking222. Voice of Treason 13:43, 7 June 2006 (UTC)
  • Keep notable site among t-shirt collectors (I happen to be one) Stev0 15:11, 7 June 2006 (UTC)
  • Keep per Kicking2222. Rob 15:35, 7 June 2006 (UTC)
  • Keep per Kicking222. Reluctantly, it does pass the criteria for such. -- Evanx(tag?) 17:54, 7 June 2006 (UTC)
  • Keep, very notable. I'm pretty sure it made the news (at least once) when the owner received a number of death threats last year. --cholmes75 (chit chat) 19:28, 7 June 2006 (UTC)
  • Keep Notable enough for an article. Deathawk 20:38, 7 June 2006 (UTC)
  • Strong Keep Notable website, also was in the news over the past few years. The main reason i beleive that this article should be kept is because Wiki is supposed to be a network of unbiased facts, so naturally even if you do not agree with the t shirt hell, you should still allow for free information to be circulated user:ryan123004 20:13, 7 June 2006
  • Keep per excellent argument raised by badlydrawnjeff and Kicking222 -- Samir धर्म 00:14, 8 June 2006 (UTC)
  • Keep No doubt notable, and I want to jump on the voting bandwagon here ;-) --Kickstart70-T-C 00:19, 8 June 2006 (UTC)
  • Keep Non-notable my ass. XM 19:48, 12 June 2006 (UTC)
