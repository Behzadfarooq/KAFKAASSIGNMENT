Art of Life Live is an X Japan live album released on March 18, 1998. It is comprised solely of a performance of the song "Art of Life", recorded on December 30, 1993 at the Tokyo Dome. A video recording of the same song, recorded at the following day was later released on DVD and VHS, titled Art of Life 1993.12.31 Tokyo Dome.
