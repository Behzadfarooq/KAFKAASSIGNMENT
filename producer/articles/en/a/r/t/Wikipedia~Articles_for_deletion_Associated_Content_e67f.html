  • Delete per nom. Fails WP:WEB. Tevildo 17:15, 26 June 2006 (UTC)
    • Neutral following updates to article. Not sure whether the linked articles are enough to count against the criteria, so I'll let others more experienced in this issue discuss it further. Tevildo 20:52, 26 June 2006 (UTC)
  • Comment: removed links to Cold Springs Tavern and Saaya Irie in this AfD subpage added by User:212.85.6.26. See history of Wikipedia:Articles for deletion/Associated Content for details. –Dicty (T/C) 17:38, 26 June 2006 (UTC)

Please let me know if there are an additions or clarification needed. Some similar sites that have wiki entries include, about.com, squidoo, flickr, YouTube, Photobucket and tagworld. Thanks, Jcurran 21:29, 26 June 2006 (UTC)

I'm thinking this may be a keep, but will sit on the fence for now. --DaveG12345 07:36, 27 June 2006 (UTC)
  • Comment I had this article on my watchlist because some folks have been mass-adding external links to that site before it was deleted via prod. See [1] [2] [3] [4] [5] [6] And it continued even after the deletion: [7]. Rl 07:53, 27 June 2006 (UTC)
  • Delete Weak keep, fails WP:WEB, heavy vandal magnet. Needs some cleanup. One of the "media references" isn't about the subject at all. --Coredesat talk 05:26, 28 June 2006 (UTC)
  • Weak delete I think it's important to disambiguate the criteria here. As a company, AC does not appear to be notable per nom. As a web site, there aren't any accolades, but there does appear to be coverage by secondary sources. But are these secondary sources noting it for it's innovation? Only one of the media references is working now, the other two appear to have changed perhaps. Ste4k 06:56, 3 July 2006 (UTC)
