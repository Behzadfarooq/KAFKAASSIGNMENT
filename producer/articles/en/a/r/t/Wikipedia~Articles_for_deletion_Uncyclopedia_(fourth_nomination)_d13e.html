Strong Delete. I find it extremely hypocritical that Encyclopædia Dramatica's article be deleted, yet this article persists. Both contain similar subject matter and unverified information. This shouldn't be a political argument on the content on the websites or notability -- both are extremely notable and mentioned on popular websites -- but rather their verifiability and response to Wikipedia's guidelines.
