[edit] Zorn's Law

There has been a discussion as to whether to merge, keep, or delete this. The discussion revealed a few things. This "law" was made up by Eric Zorn and he mentioned it in his blog. It has not been used widely elsewhere. He himself then created the page here in order to publicize it, and even referred to his use of wikipedia for publicity purposes a "whim and a stunt" [1]. I think based on the fact that it is a neologism that has yet to catch on, combined with the fact that it appears to violate WP:NOT, and evidence that the originator is trying to use wikipedia to publicise his own work, I do not even think it is worth a merge - just delete it. Bachrach44 01:54, 9 June 2006 (UTC)
