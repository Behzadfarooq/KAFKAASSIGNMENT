Artificial Intelligence II was a compilation album released on Warp Records on October 5, 1994. The album is the eighth and last release in Warp's Artificial Intelligence series.

[edit] Track listing

[edit] CD version

  1. "Release To The System" - Mark Franklin (Beaumont Hannant remix) (8:37)
  2. "Selinite" - The Higher Intelligence Agency (7:01)
  3. "Arcadian" - Link (9:32)
  4. "Scriptures" - B12 (6:58)
  5. "Chatter" - Autechre (7:36)
  6. "Symmetry" - Speedy J (6:08)
  7. "Utuba" - Beaumont Hannant (8:12)
  8. "Reality Net" - Richard H. Kirk (6:47)
  9. "Parasight" - Balil (7:34)
  10. "Spangle" - Seefeel (7:20)
