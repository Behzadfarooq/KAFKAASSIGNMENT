[edit] New Christgiving

New Christgiving (edit|talk|history|links|watch|logs)

There's no evidence that this event has any particular notability. To that end, google:"new+christgiving" or google:"new+christ+giving" produces 2 hits, whilst google:christgiving produces 65. The New in the title is, for what it's worth, redundant. - Tiswas(t/c) 13:34, 12 December 2006 (UTC)
