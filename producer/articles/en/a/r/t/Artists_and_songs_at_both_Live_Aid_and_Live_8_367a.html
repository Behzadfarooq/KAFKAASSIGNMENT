Artists and songs at both Live Aid and Live 8

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

You have new messages (last change).

There were many of the same artists and songs at both Live Aid and Live 8.

These songs were sung at both Live Aid and Live 8 (although some not by their original artists). Songs are listed with their Live Aid performers, with the artists who sang the songs at Live 8 (if different) in brackets:

Brian Wilson's performance of "Good Vibrations" in Berlin was shown on the big screen in Hyde Park
  • "Driven To Tears" - Sting
  • "Every Breath You Take" - Sting and Phil Collins (Sting)
  • "Good Vibrations" - The Beach Boys (Brian Wilson)
  • "I Don't Like Mondays" - Boomtown Rats (Bob Geldof)
  • "Rat Trap" - Boomtown Rats (Bob Geldof)
  • "Rockin' All Over The World" - Status Quo (Coldplay, chorus sung during their song "In My Place")
  • "Save A Prayer" - Duran Duran
  • "Tears Are Not Enough" - Bryan Adams
  • "Vienna" - Ultravox (Midge Ure)
  • "We Are The Champions" - Queen (Green Day)
  • "We Will Rock You" - Queen (Robbie Williams)
  • "Won't Get Fooled Again" - The Who

These artists did not sing the same songs but still performed at both events:

  • David Gilmour (played with Bryan Ferry in 1985, and as part of Pink Floyd in 2005)
  • DMC (performed as part of Run DMC at Live Aid)
  • Elton John
  • George Michael
  • Duran Duran
  • Madonna
  • Neil Young
  • Paul McCartney
  • U2
