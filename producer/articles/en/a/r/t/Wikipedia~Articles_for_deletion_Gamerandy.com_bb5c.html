[edit] Gamerandy.com

Gaming website which was started last year and seems wholly non-notable with an alexa rank of ~500,000. The claim to notability here is that they have a weekly podcast, which really doesn't cut it per WP:WEB. In addition, the article reads like an advertisement, is unwikified and a dead-end article. - Bobet 11:06, 1 August 2006 (UTC)
