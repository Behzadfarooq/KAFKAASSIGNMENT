[edit] KEEP !

There are several reasons why this article should remain:

1) People claim nobody cares about that forum. Do you really think everybody cares about all articles written in here?

2) Nobody forces you to look "The Suckage" up. So if you find the subject not interesting, don't look it up. When you read a real encyclopedia, do you also read the subjects that do not interest you?

3) As far as I know, no laws were broken when this article was made.

4) The article is neutral. The link to the sucks forum was given only once, and nowhere did the author say he condones to what the Suckage community stands for (spamming other forums, no moderation, etc)

5) Many pieces of text in the article are quotes of the Suckage, that doesn't mean they reflect the author's opinion.

6) There are articles about goatse, tubgirl and shock sites in general.

7) The Suckage as described here has not been mentioned anywhere else

8) The Suckage is a community that has been existing for years now, and deserves an entry because it's not just a simple online community, but it's almost a religion, a culture and most people don't even understand their goals and ideals, so they can refer to this article to understand them better. It's a strange community that not many people understand and this article can help.

I agree the subject should be expanded, but that doesn't mean it should disappear. Where the hell is free speech??
