[edit] King's Cinemas

Non-notable minor chain that is now defunct. Lack of notability compounded by the fact that half of the article details exploits of certain employees. —C.Fred (talk) 03:28, 1 June 2006 (UTC)

  • King's Cinemas was an important part of a declining North area of historical Smyrna during the late 90's. The facts represented are historically accurate and it is important to mark the theater's history which is linked to Cobb Center Mall, the first mall built in Smyrna Ga. —The preceding unsigned comment was added by Rorschachpro (talk • contribs) 03:36, 1 June 2006.
    • Comment. Is it necessary to have its own article, though? If its notability is that it was part of the mall, then this could be a section of the mall's article? I don't see where King's Cinemas stands on its own to deserve an article, in light of WP:CORP. —C.Fred (talk) 03:59, 1 June 2006 (UTC)
  • Delete per nom. RGTraynor 07:11, 1 June 2006 (UTC)
  • Delete per nom. Contains less claim of notability than Rorschachpro's comment above. Paddles TC 08:03, 1 June 2006 (UTC)
  • Delete per nom. --Terence Ong 14:03, 1 June 2006 (UTC)
  • Merge with mall's article as above. Xyrael T 16:49, 1 June 2006 (UTC)
  • Delete per nom Trebor 18:01, 1 June 2006 (UTC)
  • Clean/merge/delete Stripe out the small section related to the mall and merge, delete the article. Two closed cinemas aren't notable. CP/M 22:49, 1 June 2006 (UTC)
  • Merge a paragraph to Cobb Center Mall and delete the rest. --Cedderstk 17:42, 7 June 2006 (UTC)
