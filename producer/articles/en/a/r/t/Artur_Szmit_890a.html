Artur Szmit

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

You have new messages (last change).

Artur Szmit is a former bass guitarist of Polish streetpunk band The Analogs. He had been playing for The Analogs in 1996, as an exchange for imprisoned Paweł Czekała, and for short time in 1999.
