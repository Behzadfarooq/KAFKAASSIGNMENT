Mathias Feist (edit|talk|history|links|watch|logs)

Morsch's article was deleted, so should this one. Contents should be moved into Fritz (chess), if at all. Xiner 21:29, 5 December 2006 (UTC)

  • Keep - A quick Google search found several press mentions ([1], [2], [3]) so he seems somewhat notable as the Deep Fritz programmer. The article needs some serious expansion though. Jayden54 22:07, 5 December 2006 (UTC)

  • Merge the single line of his activities into ChessBase & Fritz (chess).
