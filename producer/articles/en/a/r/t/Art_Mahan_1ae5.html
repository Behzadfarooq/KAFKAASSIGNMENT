Arthur Leo Mahan (born June 8, 1913 in Somerville, Massachusetts) is a former first baseman in Major League Baseball who played for the Philadelphia Phillies during the 1940 season. He batted and threw left handed.

In one-season career, Mahan posted a .244 batting average (133-for-544) with two home runs and 39 RBI in 146 games played, including 55 runs, 24 doubles and five triples.

As of January 2007, Mahan (92) is recognized as one of the oldest living MLB players.
