[edit] Profound Intent

Profound Intent (edit|talk|history|links|watch|logs)

Teenage R&B group; appears to have done nothing but criticize more established stars and release a single "through MySpace.com". Fails WP:MUSIC on every count; no chart hits; no external coverage apart from forums[1] and apparent self-published press releases [2][3]. The one reliable source[4] doesn't mention them at all and seems to be just an attempt to hitch their wagon to a notable producer who was killed recently and thus can't refute it (a new low for wikipedia spammers?).
