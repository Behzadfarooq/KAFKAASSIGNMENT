A.I.: Artificial Insemination is a two-part episode of the television situation comedy Will & Grace, technically the twenty-sixth and twenty-seventh, and final, episodes of season four.

Spoiler warning: Plot and/or ending details follow.

[edit] Plot

Everyone faces big changes in their lives. Will and Grace run into a multitude of troubles as they plan to have a baby, including the choice between natural and artificial creation, and attempts at insemination that go wrong. Karen, under her alias Anastasia Beaverhausen, flirts with an eligible bachelor but finds herself seriously considering having an affair. And Jack faces the possibility of giving up his acting career, until a meeting with a divine entity - in the form of Cher.

[edit] Trivia

This is the last episode for Michael Angarano as a main cast member, having only appeared in a fraction of the season's episodes. He would return to guest star several times.
