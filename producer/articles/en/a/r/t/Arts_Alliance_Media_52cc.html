Arts Alliance Media

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Arts Alliance Media is a media company based in London, United Kingdom , with outposts in Paris, Amsterdam and Kristiansand, Norway. It operates in the following business areas:

  • Digital cinema
  • Video on demand (VOD)
  • Electronic Sell Through (EST)
  • Cinema Services (Newman)
  • Industry Services (LoveFilm Pro)

It is also the single largest shareholder in LoveFilm, an online DVD rental company.

[edit] Digital cinema

Arts Alliance Media subsidiary Arts Alliance Digital Cinema (AADC) is currently rolling out the UK Film Council’s Digital Screen Network (DSN), a $20m contract to install and operate Europe’s largest 2k digital cinema network. Phase 1 (50 screens) was completed in February 2006, and by Spring 2007, it is planned that 240 screens will be installed and operating. All screens are JPEG2000 compatible, in line with the specifications developed by the Digital Cinema Initiatives consortium.

AADC is responsible for installing and maintaining the equipment, and training operators. AADC also offers distributors that use the circuit special rates for content management and duplication services. AADC was forced to undertake a costly swap out of servers half-way through the DSN roll-out when QuVis servers had to be substituted for ones from Doremi. As a result, films mastered by AADC in the QPE format needed to be re-mastered in the JPEG2000 format.

AADC is also expanding its operations into Europe, beginning with a 10 screen digital cinema circuit in Norway as part of the Nordic Digital Alliance.

[edit] Video on demand/Electronic sell through

Arts Alliance Media acts as a content aggregator and technology supplier , offering a package of rights cleared content and a technology service to run digital content download and streaming businesses, and supplies the technology and content behind download services provided by LOVEFiLM and AOL. AAM carries content from Warner Bros., Sony, Universal, Momentum, Tartan, World Cinema, Celluloid Dreams and Icon.


[edit] Cinema Services (Newman)

Arts Alliance Newman is an online service for independent cinemas offering back office operations management and customer relationship management. Newman powers websites and online booking for several independent cinemas, including City Screen Cinemas.


[edit] Industry Services (LoveFilm Pro)

LOVEFiLM Pro is a secure digital network to transport films, trailers and promo reels between producers, sales agents, distributors, exhibitors and festivals.
