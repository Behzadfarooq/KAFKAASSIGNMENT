  • weak keep. Barnett probably has some name recognition as co-anchor of the morning news (not prime-time evening news) for the second station in Philly, which is apparently the 4th market in the US. According to the kyw bio, she has won one award which could warrant a closer look of keeping her article (WP:BIO does say "multiple" however). I do not subscribe to the view that every journalist who has ever appeared on TV news to be sufficiently notable for a wiki article, and the category appears to contain more than its fair share of non-notables (at least two county level reporters and a number of fill-in reporters with close to no name recognition and no awards). Susan Barnett seems to be quite a common name.
