Arthur B. Langlie

Arthur Bernard Langlie (b. July 25, 1900 in Lanesboro, Minnesota; d. July 24, 1966) served as the mayor of Seattle, Washington, 1938-41 and as the governor of Washington, 1941-45, 1949-57.
