[edit] Still Falls the Rain

Individual song does not seem notable. I'd argue that Edith Sitwell's poem should have an article at this space. —Wrathchild (talk) 19:49, 22 May 2006 (UTC)

  • Delete - this article is mostly speculation and does not contain much actual content. Perhaps it would be better served in a music magazine. Páll (Die pienk olifant) 20:04, 22 May 2006 (UTC)
  • Delete per Páll. Master of Puppets That's hot. 20:19, 22 May 2006 (UTC)
  • Keep. RM was one of the seminal bands of the 70s. The article is too good to delete. As for the nom's suggestion, I can't see why there would be any objection if he wants to do an article on the poem. That's why we have disambig pages. -- JJay 23:24, 23 May 2006 (UTC)
    • Comment. I am not suggesting the the band nor the album on which the song appears is not notable, it just doesn't appear to me that this song in particular is that notable. —Wrathchild (talk) 02:40, 25 May 2006 (UTC)
  • Merge with Manifesto (album). It makes some worthwhile observations. Tyrenius 04:40, 29 May 2006 (UTC)
Relisted to generate a more thorough discussion so that consensus may be reached.--Ezeu 03:47, 30 May 2006 (UTC)
  • Merge with Manifesto While it is a good article, it doesn't seem that this was a single or was otherwise notable as a track. Capitalistroadster 07:21, 30 May 2006 (UTC)
  • Merge with Manifesto (album), some mention is better than nothing. --Terence Ong 15:19, 30 May 2006 (UTC)
