Artie, the Strongest Man in the World was a character on the television show The Adventures of Pete & Pete, played by Toby Huss. He was Little Pete's "personal superhero". He appeared in the specials, shorts, and first two seasons of the series, as well as the two five-minute Pete & Pete short films "The Artie Workout" and "Staremaster" which appear on the "Classic Petes" and "School Dazed" Pete & Pete videos, respectively.

The character of Artie was originally created by Huss for one of his No Shame Theatre comedy routines.

[edit] Costume

Artie's "costume" consisted of a red-and-blue striped long-sleeved shirt and red form-fitting pants. He also wore glasses. According to the Pete & Pete short "Artie, the Strongest Man... in the World!", his shirt is made of 60% titanium and 40% cotton.

[edit] Origin

The origin of Artie and how he became Little Pete's own personal superhero will forever be a mystery. However, from dialogue gleaned from the episode "Farewell My Little Viking", it can be inferred that he goes around and helps kids who need him.

[edit] Known Superpowers

  • Artie Vision - Used to help locate Mr. Tastee
  • Artie Boogie - Artie's dancing abilities help the Petes in 'The Nightcrawlers' and 'Day of the Dot'
  • Super Sleep - A special kind of sleep reserved only for superheroes.
  • Super Strength - His most well-known ability, used once to move a house by one inch in "Apocalypse Pete", and is able to lift cows (referenced in Mom's Plate short).
  • Super Speed - Which he once used to locate the soundwaves emitted from the younger Pete's favorite song (Hard Day's Pete)
  • Thermal Heat - By placing small objects in his armpits, such as moist towlettes, Artie Is able to use his superpowers to make the object as hot as "Mexican Toad" in his own words. (Farewell My Little Viking)
  • Impervious to summer heat, and is thus able to walk across really hot asphalt along with the younger Pete (The Call)

[edit] Known Weaknesses

  • Rolling Thunder (When Petes Collide)
  • The sound of a whammy bar (Hard Day's Pete)

[edit] Notable events

Artie aided Little Pete in many of his exploits, including his attempt to break the world record for staying awake. Some of Artie's other notable doings include:

  • Once moving the entire Hickle house one inch. (Apocalypse Pete)
  • Helping Little Pete beat up the Atlantic Ocean as punishment for allowing summer to end. (What We Did On Our Summer Vacation)
  • Artie was once commissioned by Little Pete to use his powers of strength to help win a bowling contest; Artie was subsequently spotted and signed by a professional bowling scout and became a bowling superstar. (New Year's Pete)
  • Artie has an epic battle with Rolling Thunder (The famed Wrigley family bowling ball) to stop its mysterious powers causing the maddening rivalry between the brothers Pete. Despite being the strongest man in the world, he was unfortunately defeated by the bowling ball.(When Petes Collide)

[edit] Other Facts

  • Artie's favorite smell is that of tire air.
  • While he is the Strongest Man in the World, Artie is not invincible. He was once defeated by an evil bowling ball, and is as affected by Whammy Bars and E Guitar Chords as Superman is by Kryptonite.
  • "Love Rollercoaster" by The Ohio Players (and perhaps funk in general) attracts Artie like a "giant funk magnet." Artie regards this song as "Music of the Gods!"
  • Invented the sport of "land canoeing."
  • Artie likes armpit noises, as they remind him of his mother.
  • Artie has a past with Clark (a turtle), Ernest Hemingway, and Gertrude Stein.
  • During the show, Artie lives in an isolated Port-a-john-like "Port-a-Home" when he was not with the Petes. It is actually somewhat like the TARDIS from Doctor Who, because it's apparently bigger on the inside than on the outside, and in "Farewell, My Little Viking" he was seen hosting a party with over a dozen people inside.
  • Artie has an affinity for pressed slacks.
  • Artie, after being tricked by the International Adult Conspiracy, once became an aluminum siding salesman until saved by Dad.
  • Artie thinks of himself as lemon-scented. (Space, Geeks, and Johnny Unitas)
  • Artie has an unofficial constellation named after him.
