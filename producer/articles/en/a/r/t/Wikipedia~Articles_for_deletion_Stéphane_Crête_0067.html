[edit] Stéphane Crête

Stéphane Crête (edit|talk|history|links|watch|logs) – (View log)

Non-notable actor fails to satisfy WP:BIO. Acted in around 20 productions over a 12 year span. No WP:RS cited. Valrith 04:24, 25 March 2007 (UTC)

  • Keep - Principle actor on popular TV show. Just a few seconds of googling and found many 3rd party pieces on him [1], [2] (a TV interview), [3] (a Télévision de Radio-Canada bio on him). Plus an award winner in the Canadian equivlant to the Emmys [4]. The numbers citation above is not a reason to delete. An actor can become "notable" for 1 role in a 100 year span. --Oakshade 04:59, 25 March 2007 (UTC)
  • Keep. Co-lead actor of the No. 1 show in French Canada for three years running. Major Gemini Award winner (equivalent to national Emmys). This isn't Conservapedia - notability on Wikipedia exists irrespective of language or nationality. --Charlene 05:42, 25 March 2007 (UTC)
  • Keep. Seems reasonably notable, within the context of Canada. -Hit bull, win steak(Moo!) 17:03, 26 March 2007 (UTC)
