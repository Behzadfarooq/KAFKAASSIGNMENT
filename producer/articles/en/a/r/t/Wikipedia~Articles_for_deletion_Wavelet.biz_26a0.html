  • Comment In practice, Google testing also winds up being subjective, because Google itself has certain selection biases that need to be accounted for. Measures of Google pagerank often wind up measuring "How much has this web site owner done to optimize his or her Google ranking?" as much or more as "How notable is this web site?" Leaving that aside, Made2Manage Systems, an article you nominated for deletion (in your only other apparent contribution to Wikipedia), also rates a 5 on your proposed test.
