[edit] Luke Martin

Non-notable lower house candidate.

As best I can make out, Luke Martin started a vanity page on 6 May, 2006 with a lowercase "m" [1]. Then on 28 July asked Essjay to change his username from Lukewmartin to Kataposmanson [2]. Essjay obliged and on the next day recreated the current Luke Martin page.

Catchpole believes that the fact that Martin is a current candidate makes him sufficiently notable. I disagree. If he were party spokesperson on some issue at the state level then maybe that would increase his notability but I don't think we can go giving pages to every candidate of every election. Nick 20:11, 1 August 2006 (UTC)
