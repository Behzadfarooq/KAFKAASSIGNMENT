[edit] The Triad alliance

An article describing a non-notable fanmade "alliance" within an already non-notable webgame (which has also been put up for deletion). Should at least be merged and in my opinion be deleted all together. Fails WP:NOR, WP:NOT, WP:V and WP:RS. Peephole 12:47, 1 August 2006 (UTC)

  • Delete. Gaming clans/tribes/alliances are notable only to themselves and sometimes to other players. Not here. Fan-1967 14:05, 1 August 2006 (UTC)
  • Delete. Akradecki 14:07, 1 August 2006 (UTC)
  • Speedy delete surely this can be speedied, similar to a gaming clan. In any case, definitely delete. Even the game itself is non-notable, its clans/players even moreso. Andrew Lenahan - Starblind 14:15, 1 August 2006 (UTC)
  • Delete, I have tried to converse with the original editor but he does not seem to understand our notability guidelines. At least one other clan was deleted already: Free_Trade_Syndicate. -- nae'blis 19:24, 1 August 2006 (UTC)
  • Delete highly non-notable --RMHED 19:47, 1 August 2006 (UTC)
  • Strong delete, the vast majority of gaming clans are not notable. --Coredesat talk. ^_^ 00:27, 2 August 2006 (UTC)
  • Delete, another non-notable gaming clan. WP:VANITY page with WP:V issues. --Kinu t/c 02:30, 2 August 2006 (UTC)
  • Delete nn Matthew Fenton (contribs) 17:51, 4 August 2006 (UTC)
  • Delete per NN and the fact the alliance is a bunch of ninny poopieheads who should all be bountied, :yarr: :yarr:. Sethimothy 21:49, 5 August 2006 (UTC)
