[edit] Mango Racing

Mango Racing (edit|talk|history|links|watch|logs)

The article in question fails to meet notability criteria with regard to available reference sources. It refers to an obscure racing team that completed barely half a season in a sub-class of British F3. I have contacted the original contributor via his talk page, but have received no response.
