Article: The Naked Gun

Details of the assessment method can be found at the main page. Feel free to add comments when you assess an article, or use the talk page for discussion.

[edit] Review by DMurphy

  • Coverage and factuality: 5
Lacks content, no references, little more than a plot description and list of cameos
  • Writing style: 7
Encyclopaedic but dry.
  • Structure: 6
The long list of cameos in the lead belong in a section with a cast list; section titles aren't extremely helpful ("trademark gags" contains all of one "gag")
  • Aesthetics: 8
Could use another picture.
  • Overall: 6

Needs more content and better content. -DMurphy 17:58, 11 March 2006 (UTC)
