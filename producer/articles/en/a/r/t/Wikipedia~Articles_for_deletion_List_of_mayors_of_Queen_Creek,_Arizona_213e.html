[edit] List of mayors of Queen Creek, Arizona

Do we really want an article which lists all of the mayors of a town with less than 5000 people? I have no problem with having an article on every place in the world, but on the mayors of all of those places? I think not. And, as I write this, the only entry is actually linking to somebody else, not the mayor of the town. User:Zoe|(talk) 04:37, 8 October 2006 (UTC)
