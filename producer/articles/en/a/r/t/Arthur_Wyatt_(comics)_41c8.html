Arthur Wyatt (comics)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

You have new messages (last change).

Arthur Wyatt is a writer for British comic 2000ad, creating stories mostly in the Future Shock format. Wyatt was also selected as one of 2005's five best new comic book writers, contributing to the 2000AD Winter Special.

Wyatt is also the founder of small press title FutureQuake. He edited and wrote large parts of the first three issues and continues to contribute scripts.
