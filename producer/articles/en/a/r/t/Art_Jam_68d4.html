[edit] Art Jam

Art Jam is an educational art-chored program of ABS-CBN in the Philippines. It teaches the viewers how to make simple, unique yet environmentally-friendly artworks.

The program is hosted by the son of the King of Comedy, Epy Quizon, and the comedian Tado. The program gives the children some ideas to make artwork.

Art Jam is every Saturday on ABS-CBN Channel 2

[edit] Art Jam Segments

[edit] Playtime

In this segment, they make some unique and beautiful artworks using any artwork tools such as cardboard, pencil, pentel pens, colored papers, cartolina, etc.

[edit] Food Trip

In this segment they decorate food by putting some design on it, using items such as marshmallows, candies, and cream.

[edit] Sketch Pad

In this segment they are giving some knowledgable information about art like when art started, who firstmade the abstract design, etc.

[edit] Junk Art

In this segment they use some recycable materials into fabulous artworks like making a plastic bottle into a Piggy Bank and lot more.
