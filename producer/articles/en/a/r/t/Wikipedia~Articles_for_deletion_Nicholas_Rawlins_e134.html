[edit] Nicholas Rawlins

Nicholas Rawlins (edit|talk|history|links|watch|logs) – (View log)

I pull up 7 hits when I Pubmed search this guy. Does not seem very notable and does not meet WP:BIO. Mnemopis 03:05, 6 February 2007 (UTC)

Please note that, on further investigation, I now believe this article should be kept. This person's publications appear under "Rawlins JN", and not "Rawlins N", as would be expected from his name, Nick Rawlins. The fact that I could not retrieve his list of publications using "Rawlins N" lead me to nominate his page for deletion. However, he does have quite a few publications, as I subsequently found out, and they are fairly well cited, so his article should be kept. Mnemopis 07:52, 12 February 2007 (UTC)

Comment Ok we seem to have validated with primary sources only that the subject of this article is a Professor at Oxford, but in spite of all the keep votes no one has shown how he meets WP:N being a professor is not a notability criteria any place that I can find any Wikipedia policy.
