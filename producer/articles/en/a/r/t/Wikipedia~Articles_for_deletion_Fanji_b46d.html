[edit] Fanji

This is a fictional term (and a poor one at that) or an extremely, extremely rare term. I did a Google search:

Results 1 - 7 of about 9 for fanji anime -encyclopedia -wikipedia. (0.07 seconds) The mius signs tells google to not show results that contain those terms. The narrowing was required to eliminate most of the taichi results (apparently a Chinese proper noun, possibly part of a person's name) and wikipedia mirrors.

  2. Document sans titre Taichi website in French. Not related.
  3. Fanji Guides, Tutorials Wikipedia mirror site.
  4. Engrish - The Jiggies Reference Guide Wikipedia mirror site.
  5. 217.243.191.102 66.235.217.102 66.194.67.102 207.126.49.11 63.251 ... Porn-related machine datafile. Occurance: fanji.com
  6. ORIGIN . $TTL 43200 ; 12 hours infilter.its.is IN SOA dual.snerpa ... Porn-related machine datafile. Occurance: www.fanji.com/ukrainelolitas/lolitas.html

No actual non-Wikipedia occurance of said term found. I will file for a VfD. --69.214.226.102 21:14, 7 May 2005 (UTC)
