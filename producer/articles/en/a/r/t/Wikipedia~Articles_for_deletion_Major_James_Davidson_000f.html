  • Delete Not much online, except for the fact that he's real. Admittedly, ghits aren't a particularly useful barometer for a subject this old, but if there's nothing online, an editor's really going to have to go out of his way to expand this beyond rank and date of death. It's possible there's a good article to be created on Davidson, but nothing is lost by deleting this one. --Djrobgordon 11:42, 25 February 2007 (UTC)
