Arto Koivisto (born December 7, 1948 in Isojoki). is a former Finnish cross country skier who competed in the 1970's. He won the 4 x 10 km gold and the 15 km bronze at the 1976 Winter Olympics in Innsbruck.

Koivisto also won the 15 km event at the Holmenkollen ski festival in 1976.
