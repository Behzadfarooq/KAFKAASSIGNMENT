[edit] Sons Of Nothing

tagged for deletion as non-notable band, but contested. This is a Pink Floyd tribute with some local fame, may or may not meet WP:MUSIC (no real evidence presented that they do). Just zis Guy you know? 09:00, 24 July 2006 (UTC)

  • Delete Fails WP:MUSIC the only Pink Floyd tribute band really worth an entry is Australian Pink Floyd Show who do world tours etc DavidHumphreysSPEAK TO MEABOUTTHE THINGS I MESSED UP 09:06, 24 July 2006 (UTC)
  • Delete. I speedied it yesterday as an nn-band and would support doing that again, but slow deletion for not meeting WP:NMG will do. Stifle (talk) 15:09, 24 July 2006 (UTC)

The band is apparently listed with PollStar [1] if that makes them more notable. I agree the Australian Pink Floyd is clearly more notable internationally. Sons Of Nothing is a regional phenomenon at this point. --166.70.238.250 16:46, 24 July 2006 (UTC)

  • Delete - Does not meet WP:MUSIC, regional touring only. --Joelmills 02:52, 25 July 2006 (UTC)
  • Delete. As the one who originally placed the speedy tag (the article was much smaller then), I can say it fails WP:MUSIC Alphachimp talk 04:23, 26 July 2006 (UTC)
