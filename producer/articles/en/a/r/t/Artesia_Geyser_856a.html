Artesia Geyser

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Artesia Geyser is a geyser in the Lower Geyser Basin of Yellowstone National Park in the United States.

Artesia Geyser is part of the Black Warrior Group which includes Young Hopeful Geyser, Gray Bulger Geyser, and Steady Geyser. Artesia is a perpetual geyser in that is never stops ejecting water from at least one of its two cones, usually no more than 5 feet (1.5 m). During a period in 1999, one of the cones jetted to a distance of 12 feet (3.6 m) while the other emitted only steam.
