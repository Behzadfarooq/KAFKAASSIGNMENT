[edit] Wild Passion (DJ Small & LV Club Mix)

Non-notable remixes of non-notable songs; fails WP:MUSIC. We don't need an article for every song ever written, much less the various remixes of those songs. This AfD also applies to the other songs from the album:

  1. Dance with the Wolves (Harem Disco Mix)
  2. Drum 'n' Dance (DJ Small & LV Club Mix)
  3. Play , Musician! (Treat Brothers Happy Dub)
  4. Wind Song (Michael Nekrasov Zebra Mix)
  5. Wild Dances (DJ Small & LV Club Mix)
  6. The Same Star (Treat Brothers Re-Pop)
  7. The Tango We Used to Dance (DJ Small & LV Club Mix)
  8. Dance with the Wolves (Treat Brothers Remix)
  9. The Same Star (DJ Zebra & Sergei Repin Fusion House Remix)
  10. Wind Song (DJ Small & LV Club Mix)
  11. Wild Dances (C.V.T. vs DJ Nick 2005 Club Edit)

Kafziel Talk 14:39, 25 October 2006 (UTC)

  • Delete all Actually, it would be unfair to categorize all the songs themselves as non-notable ("Wild Dances" being one that screams notability) but remixes do not get separate articles. If any of these remixes is official/documentable/somehow important, it can be mentioned within the article for the song if that exists. GassyGuy 15:54, 25 October 2006 (UTC)
  • Agree Not every song (especially remixed songs) need an own article on wikipedia. -- Luigi-ish 17:05, 25 October 2006 (UTC)
  • Delete all per nom. Kavadi carrier 08:27, 4 November 2006 (UTC)
