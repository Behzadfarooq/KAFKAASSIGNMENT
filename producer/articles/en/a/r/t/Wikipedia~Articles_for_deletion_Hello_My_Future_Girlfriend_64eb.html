Ultimately I decided that imitation is not just the sincerest form of flattery, but also the sincerest measure of popularity, so I let the google hits make this a keep vote. However, this makes me see keepsleeping's point, and I think that we may have to start rethinking our overall stance on internet (and other pop culture related) phenomena. --Bachrach44 00:52, 9 January 2006 (UTC)
