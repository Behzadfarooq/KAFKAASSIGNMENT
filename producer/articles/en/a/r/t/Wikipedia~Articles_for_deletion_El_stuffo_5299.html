[edit] El stuffo

El stuffo (edit|talk|history|links|watch|logs)

It seems that I tagged this article for notability around seven months ago; since then, there's been no improvement. I would prod it, but, when I came across it again today, I found an incomplete afd nom, so I dediced (er, make that decided) to complete it. Anyways, delete per WP:NOT and WP:V. Picaroon 02:48, 2 January 2007 (UTC)

  • Strong delete. No context, no actual (good) explanation of the definition of the term, and what little there is...well, we're not a dictionary. Add on to that the issue of WP:CRYSTAL: speculation that it will migrate to other cities. --Dennisthe2 02:56, 2 January 2007 (UTC)
    • Format change. Doh! --Dennisthe2 02:56, 2 January 2007 (UTC)
  • Delete, as neologism. Phrases such as "used only in" and "high school fad" throw away the word's notability. In addition, "high school fad" brings up the guideline (in progress) WP:SCHOOL. --SYCTHOStalk 03:53, 2 January 2007 (UTC)
    I don't see anything related to this in WP:SCHOOL. Am I missing it, or do you mean another page? Picaroon 21:38, 2 January 2007 (UTC)
  • Delete per WP:NEO, likely WP:NFT. Fan-1967 04:00, 2 January 2007 (UTC)
  • Delete per above. MER-C 05:41, 2 January 2007 (UTC)
  • Delete per above. Sounds like nonsense. Wikikiwi 10:41, 2 January 2007 (UTC)
  • Delete -used only in one town? not notable. --TeckWizTalkContribs@ 23:40, 2 January 2007 (UTC)
  • Delete Even if the word were to spread as "other Anglo-Spanish words that have been created in Southern Spain" have not (according to the quote in the article), it would become a Spanish word, and should be a in a Spanish dictionary, not an English Encylopedia. It does seem like a good start to an article, and I wish it were, but it simply violates WP:CRYSTAL and WP:NOT. Enuja 02:50, 5 January 2007 (UTC)
