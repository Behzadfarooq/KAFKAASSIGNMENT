[edit] Domus Legis Society

College social clubs are not inherently notable and this one has no particulalry notable features. Delete. BlueValour 00:30, 20 August 2006 (UTC)

  • Keep. The fact that it is Canada's oldest law student social society makes it sufficiently notable, in my opinion. Aplomado talk 00:46, 20 August 2006 (UTC)
  • Comment If it were 1865 not 1965 I might agree with you. I would argue that the oldest of something not notable is still not notable unless there is a lot of history which there isn't here. BlueValour 00:57, 20 August 2006 (UTC)
  • Weak keep Hmm. I usually vote delete on single school clubs, but this basically the first social society for law students in Canada. Forty years history, complete references. Teke 01:07, 20 August 2006 (UTC)
  • Delete, I missed that pesky one liner in there about operating without university administration, and I can't find evidence on their website of official administrative approval. That is necessary for legitimacy. Teke 01:12, 20 August 2006 (UTC)
  • Delete Social clubs shouldn't be kept unless they have a large mebership/ have done notable works/ mentioned in the media. --Ageo020 01:16, 20 August 2006 (UTC)
  • Delete Social clubs should not be as Wikipedia's article per Wikipedia policies. Daniel's page ☎ 01:50, 20 August 2006 (UTC)
  • Delete per the above. --Daniel Olsen 02:49, 20 August 2006 (UTC)
  • Delete/Merge not sufficiently noteworthy as a stand-alone article but could be a section in the Dalhousie Law School article. --PullUpYourSocks 03:48, 20 August 2006 (UTC)
  • Delete as a non-notable social club. RandyWang (chat me up/fix me up) 10:37, 20 August 2006 (UTC)
  • Merge and redirect to Dalhousie Law School, as this, like all other student life facets, should be discussed where it can be most reasonably sought. The article itself looks well done enough, but not as a solo entry. Geogre 12:24, 20 August 2006 (UTC)
  • Merge and redirect to Dalhousie Law School, as per Geogre. --Nishkid64 18:19, 20 August 2006 (UTC)
  • Merge and redirect per Geogre. Jon Cates 02:09, 21 August 2006 (UTC)
  • Merge Two marginal articles will become one adequate article by combining the material. StuffOfInterest 19:06, 21 August 2006 (UTC)
  • Merge and perhaps reduce somewhat. Heimstern Läufer 04:15, 22 August 2006 (UTC)
