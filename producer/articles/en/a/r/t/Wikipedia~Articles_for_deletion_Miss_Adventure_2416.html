[edit] Miss Adventure

Using google I have been unable to verify if this comic actaully appears in the Weekly World News - someone in the US could probably verify it more easily. The page has been speedied several times as nonsense. I also don't think that a comic that appears in a spoof newspaper is necessarily encyclopedic - all edits that aren't from the original contributor are fixing disambigs.--nixie 23:05, 20 December 2005 (UTC)

  • Weak keep but clean-up. The character is legitimate though I don't recall if it's actually a comic strip. I thought it was a photo feature. Characters like Bat Boy from the WWN get their own articles, so Miss Adventure I guess deserves one, too. 23skidoo 01:23, 21 December 2005 (UTC)
  • As 23skiddo already said, Miss Adventure is a legitimate character, but is not a comic strip. More of a fictional story that is continued each week. The article was previously deleted, but brought back for the reason of characters like Bat Boy getting their own articles. DaemonDivinus 01:52, 21 December 2005 (UTC)
    • I don't why one justifies the other, the content of the article is unencyclopedic.--nixie 22:21, 21 December 2005 (UTC)
