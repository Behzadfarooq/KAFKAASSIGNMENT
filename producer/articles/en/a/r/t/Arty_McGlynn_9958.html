Arty McGlynn

Arty McGlynn is an Irish guitarist born in Omagh, County Tyrone. In addition to collaborating with different notable groups such as Patrick Street, Planxty, Four Men and a Dog, De Danann and The Van Morrison Band. He has also done duo performances and recordings with his wife Nollaig Casey.
