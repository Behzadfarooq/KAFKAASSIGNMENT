Artificial Intelligence is a 1985 album by John Cale.

The album was produced by John Cale himself. All songs are credited to John Cale/Larry Sloman, except Everytime The Dogs Bark and Vigilante Lover (John Cale/Larry Sloman/David Young).

[edit] Track listing

  • Everytime The Dogs Bark
  • Dying On The Vine
  • The Sleeper
  • Vigilante Lover
  • Chinese Takeaway (Hong Kong 1997)
  • Song Of The Valley
  • Fadeaway Tomorrow
  • Black Rose
  • Satellite Walk
