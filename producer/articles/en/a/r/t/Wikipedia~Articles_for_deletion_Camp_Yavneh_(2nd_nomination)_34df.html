[edit] Camp Yavneh

  • Keep, notable enough, over 60 years old, affiliated with Hebrew College, mentioned in The Jewish Journal. --MPerel ( talk | contrib) 10:38, 22 May 2006 (UTC)
  • Weak Delete. Its a summer camp. Unless the article can explain, through the use of verifiable information taken from reliable third-party sources, how this summer camp is significantly different from the standard, run of the mill summer camp, there is nothing we can do that the camp's own site cannot do better. The article linked to (3rd external link onthe page) seems to be a good external source, but IMO effort can and should be made by those who seek inclusion of this article to cut out the badvertisment and write an externally verifable article with references and/or citations. -- saberwyn 10:46, 22 May 2006 (UTC)
