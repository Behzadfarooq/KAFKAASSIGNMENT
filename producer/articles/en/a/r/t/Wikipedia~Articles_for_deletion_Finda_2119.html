[edit] Finda

Finda (edit|talk|history|links|watch|logs)

Supposedly an info directory site for New Zealand. Alexa ranks ~ 46,000+ [1] Personally I don't see this particular site as being notable enough to warrant its own article. Also I believe that it is only been created in order to generate traffic. I have personally deleted this article already under CSD, however user has recreated it and insists that it is noteworthy.-- malo (tlk) (cntrbtns) 07:42, 12 December 2006 (UTC)

  • Delete - only one non-trivial coverage, doesn't meet WP:WEB: [2]. MER-C 08:49, 12 December 2006 (UTC)
  • Keep This article meets the first criterion of WP:WEB - there are multiple stories about it on major NZ news outlets, as cited in the article. (Alexa rankings are generally not great as evidence, and 46k is actually a decent rank for a site with an audience limited to New Zealand.) Dbdbdb 08:56, 12 December 2006 (UTC)
  • Keep - satisfies WP:V, WP:RS and seems to be notable with New Zealand. -- Whpq 22:21, 12 December 2006 (UTC)
  • Keep - Meets WP:WEB in its current form, I have heard of it in Australia as a NZ competitor to similar Australian based sites.Garrie 01:43, 13 December 2006 (UTC)
