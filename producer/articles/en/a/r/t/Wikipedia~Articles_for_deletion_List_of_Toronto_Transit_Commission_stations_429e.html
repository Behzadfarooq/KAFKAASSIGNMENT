[edit] List of Toronto Transit Commission stations

List of Toronto Transit Commission stations (edit|talk|history|links|watch|logs)

This list is a duplicate of {{TTCstations}}. -- Selmo (talk) 20:22, 24 December 2006 (UTC)

  • Keep - the list article has substantially more information than the template, and information about a currently operating transit system in a major city is certainly notable. Newyorkbrad 23:59, 24 December 2006 (UTC)
  • Keep this list has a well-defined scope, and provides added value of information over the template.-- danntm T C 02:46, 25 December 2006 (UTC)
  • Keep substantial added value compared to category Fg2 07:20, 25 December 2006 (UTC)
  • MergeWikipedia is specifically not designed to be a book of lists. While this is valuable information, the article title should not begin with the word "list." It seems preferable to merge this into the article about Toronto Transit Stations, where it is more likely to be found by a search. --Kevin Murray 15:16, 25 December 2006 (UTC)
  • Keep Contains plenty of useful, verifiable information. Lankybugger 16:36, 27 December 2006 (UTC)
  • Keep Substatial information of a major transit system in the largest city in Canada. --Oakshade 03:06, 28 December 2006 (UTC)
  • Keep per informational comments above; and cleanup so the first word is more palatable. Neier 09:51, 28 December 2006 (UTC)
  • Snowball keep: There is no remotely viable justification for deletion. – joeclark 21:45, 28 December 2006 (UTC)
