    • Comment Just like filing a lawsuit, anybody can start an AfD for any reason valid or invalid, and they usually do. The fact that you and others have decided to nominate schools for deletion is no indication of the merit of these AfDs. I have expanded this article and other similar articles because they deserve to be retained. I never attended Holmen High School, I'm not even quite sure where it's located. But I do know that the article has multiple, non-trivial reliable and verifiable sources to demonstrate notability. Alansohn 11:31, 21 March 2007 (UTC)

But I should add that the original article that I nominated for deletion is very different from its current state (which contains much more information). Robinson weijman 11:51, 21 March 2007 (UTC)
