[edit] Axis of weasel

Yet again another nonce term that will be discarded in a week or so. RickK 08:45, Mar 5, 2005 (UTC)

  • Keep, notable term. 80.255 11:00, 5 Mar 2005 (UTC)
  • Delete. It is just slang, and won't last. Evertype 15:42, 2005 Mar 5 (UTC)
  • Keep as a proof of stupidity of some US politicians. --Pgreenfinch 16:31, 5 Mar 2005
    A vote is a vote, but that one looks a lot like disruption to prove a point. Gazpacho
  • Delete. It can be mentioned in the axis of evil article, no need for a separate topic. BTfromLA 19:59, 5 Mar 2005 (UTC)
  • Merge with axis of evil, Redirect. Rl 22:31, 5 Mar 2005 (UTC)
  • Delete. Given the strong feelings Bush generates and the general catchiness of this phrase, I would expect it, had it actually been in significant use, to garner many more google hits than it does (436 displayed). There's already a mention at axis of evil, and that's enough. —Korath (Talk) 23:24, Mar 5, 2005 (UTC)
    • Whatever happens with this article, by the way, it should be merged with axis of weasels first. —Korath (Talk) 23:29, Mar 5, 2005 (UTC)
  • Delete, non notable phrase. Megan1967 01:37, 6 Mar 2005 (UTC)
  • Merge with axis of evil. This particular bit of U.S. stupidity doesn't deserve its own article. DaveTheRed 03:32, 6 Mar 2005 (UTC)
  • Merge as suggested above. Radiant! 10:55, Mar 6, 2005 (UTC)
  • Merge. This phrase was in common use for a month in 2003 to mark the perceived duplicity of countries such as Germany and France in their opposition to the Iraq War. It was invented by blogger Scott Ott of the Scrappleface website and used by the New York Post. However, it fell out of vogue very quickly afterwards. Capitalistroadster 18:28, 6 Mar 2005 (UTC)
  • Delete, not in wide or continued use--nixie 02:56, 7 Mar 2005 (UTC)
  • Merge/redirect to Axis of evil. Gazpacho 11:19, 7 Mar 2005 (UTC)
  • Note: Wasn't it Axis of Weevil not Weasel. I could be imagining it, but I don't think I am. Redirect --Rentastrawberry 02:00, Mar 8, 2005 (UTC)
  • Merge with Axis of evil. --Carnildo 21:23, 8 Mar 2005 (UTC)
  • Delete, no merge. Not in use. Rossami (talk) 01:43, 9 Mar 2005 (UTC)
  • Merge with Axis of evil. Burschik 16:29, 9 Mar 2005 (UTC)
  • Redirect to Axis of weasels. Nothing to merge, as the info is duplicative. --BD2412 22:25, 9 Mar 2005 (UTC)
