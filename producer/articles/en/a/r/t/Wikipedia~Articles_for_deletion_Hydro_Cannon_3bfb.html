[edit] Hydro Cannon

Hydro Cannon (edit|talk|history|links|watch|logs) – (View log)

This is a description of an attack from the Pokemon games, it is game guide and violates WP:NOT Bhamv 12:14, 24 March 2007 (UTC)

  • Delete and do not redirect. This can certainly be discussed in a relevant pokemon article. A Train take the 16:43, 24 March 2007 (UTC)
Why not redirect? If it's discussed somewhere, a redirect will help a user find that information and discourage recreation of this article. — brighterorange (talk) 18:59, 26 March 2007 (UTC)
Because a description of the features of a single, specific attack from the Pokemon games, including its tactical uses and strengths/weaknesses clearly crosses the line into game guide information, violating WP:NOT. I don't think any Pokemon article on Wikipedia could include such information. Bhamv 04:27, 28 March 2007 (UTC)
  • Delete aggree with A Train -- Diletante 17:55, 24 March 2007 (UTC)
  • Redirect to whatever Pokemon-related article is most appropriate. Crotalus horridus (TALK • CONTRIBS) 18:54, 24 March 2007 (UTC)
  • Delete utter rubbish. David Spart (talk · contribs · logs · block user · block log) 21:52, 24 March 2007 (UTC)
  • Delete Pokécruft. JuJube 23:32, 24 March 2007 (UTC)
  • Delete Per WP:NOT, and it's quite factually inaccurate... SuperDT 23:40, 24 March 2007 (UTC)
  • Note: This debate has been added to the list of CVG deletions. — Kaustuv Chaudhuri 02:25, 26 March 2007 (UTC)
