  • Merge possibly with List of sex positions. --MPerel 16:50, Dec 6, 2004 (UTC)

"Tossed salad" appears as an item on menus and in cookbooks, but the other phrases are deliberately different and usually refer only to the sexual practice. Inky 22:13, 6 Dec 2004 (UTC)
