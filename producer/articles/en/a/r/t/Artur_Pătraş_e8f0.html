Artur Pătraş (born October 1, 1988 in Chişinău) is a Moldavian football player, currently under contract at FCU Politehnica Timişoara, but who mainly plays for the 2nd team. He made his debut in the Romanian Liga 1 in the match against Dinamo Bucharest, in March 2007.

Pătraş is an Under-21 international.
