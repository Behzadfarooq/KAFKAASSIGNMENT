[edit] AIDS Wolf

No evidence of notability per WP:MUSIC Nv8200p talk 03:39, 16 June 2006 (UTC)

  • Comment Even though Pitchfork didn't like them being reviewed by the foremost hipster webzine makes them notable, no? ~ trialsanderrors 03:53, 16 June 2006 (UTC)
  • Keep per existence of multiple non-trivial works featuring AIDS Wolf. This fulfills WP:BAND. [1][2]. They also seem to be more than your neighbor's garage band who plays at the bar on friday nights. AdamBiswanger1 04:02, 16 June 2006 (UTC)
  • Keep The cited sources are enough for me. --djrobgordon 04:03, 16 June 2006 (UTC)
  • Keep per the above. A bit unpleasant tho'.Ac@osr 07:10, 16 June 2006 (UTC)
  • Keep Also recently did a European tour [3] and are on Skin Graft Records, a notable independent label. Kellen T 09:59, 16 June 2006 (UTC)
  • Keep Toured on 2 continents, released record on notable indie label which received press coverage from established sources (Pitchfork, The Wire, etc). Dancarney 23:49, 16 June 2006 (UTC)
  • Keep per WP:MUSIC, "Has gone on an international concert tour, or a national concert tour in at least one large or medium-sized country" I'm pretty sure two continents should cover that. Yanksox (talk) 03:21, 17 June 2006 (UTC)
  • Keep notable enough, well written aricle, little stuff is cheap. Joeyramoney 17:20, 19 June 2006 (UTC)
