[edit] Amphetamine in popular culture

Amphetamine in popular culture (edit|talk|history|links|watch|logs) – (View log)

Another indiscriminate "pop culture" spinoff article. Nothing but a list of every time this particular drug is mentioned in some obscure song or movie; plenty of OR and entries of questionable notability.

I am also nominating the following nearly identical pages for the same reason:

Benzedrine in popular culture (edit|talk|history|links|watch|logs)
Dextroamphetamine in popular culture (edit|talk|history|links|watch|logs)

— Krimpet (talk/review) 00:50, 24 March 2007 (UTC)

  • Delete Most of these examples aren't even significant to the works in question. Croxley 01:36, 24 March 2007 (UTC)
  • Delete all - per nom. Strongly oppose any suggestion that any of this stuff be merged into the articles on the various drugs. Otto4711 01:59, 24 March 2007 (UTC)
  • Delete all per Otto4711. Scienter 04:43, 24 March 2007 (UTC)
  • Delete. "Indie Rock singer-songwriter Elliott Smith mentions Amphetamine use in a song titled "St. Ides Heaven" from his self titled album." You don't say? Some of these "in popular culture" articles are useful, but this certainly isn't one of them. Lankiveil 04:46, 24 March 2007 (UTC).
  • Delete all. Listcruft, or listcrud, whatever. Realkyhick 06:21, 24 March 2007 (UTC)
  • Biased Delete. Find most "In pop culture" list about as usuful as google add farms. This one is no exception. --I already forgot 10:15, 24 March 2007 (UTC)
  • Delete. Unencyclopedic. --Guinnog 13:19, 24 March 2007 (UTC)
  • Delete all anyone who cared would be looking on Google, not Wikipedia anyway. - Iridescenti (talk to me!) 18:50, 24 March 2007 (UTC)
