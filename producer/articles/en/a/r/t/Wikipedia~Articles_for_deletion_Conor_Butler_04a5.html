[edit] Conor Butler

To be honest, I'm not buying the claim in the article. I really don't believe there's a sport called "Pez eating," and a google search on this fellow (including "Conor Butler" +"pez") turns up nothing that seems relevant. This looks like a vanity page to me, but I'm not 100% positive -- thus is why I'm nominating it for AFD and not just sticking a CSD A7 notice on it. FreelanceWizard 02:47, 21 April 2006 (UTC)
