  • Keep, real place, I suppose, but I am highly dubious of much of the info in the rewrite. I removed the prattle about 'no fish' as a quick google search proved that incorrect. In addition, on a lake that small, it's hard to imagine a human-made dam involved, and neither google nor a topo map of the area had any evidence of such a dam (altho' there is a "Little Cranberry Dam" on nearby Cypress Island). Also, if you go to this topo map[1], click the "Large" radio button and select 1:100,000 scale in the dropdown and look at the legend, you will see that there is no way in hell tiny Fidalgo Island could have a '10 mile morain' there--2-3 miles, tops. There's at least four other "Little Cranberry Lake"s in the US alone--I wonder if someone is getting them mixed up. Niteowlneils 19:11, 8 May 2005 (UTC)
