Art Min

Art Min is a game developer whose work has included stints at Looking Glass Studios, Valve Corporation, Eidos and Ion Storm Austin. In 2005, he was linked to the creation of Junction Point Studios.

He is credited with work on the following titles: System Shock, Terra Nova: Strike Force Centauri, FireTeam, Deus Ex and Half-Life 2. In addition, Min has participated in the Indie Game Jam.

Min grew up in California and attended college at MIT, earning a degree in Computer Science.

According to the end game credits, Art Min also provided his face for a character in Half-Life 2.
