Arthur Siskind (born 11 October 1938) has been an executive director of the News Corporation since 1991. He served as their group general counsel from March 1991 until December 2004 and has been the senior advisor to the chairman since January 2005.

Siskind became an adjunct professor of law at Georgetown University Law Center in 2005 and has been a member of the bar of the State of New York since 1962.
