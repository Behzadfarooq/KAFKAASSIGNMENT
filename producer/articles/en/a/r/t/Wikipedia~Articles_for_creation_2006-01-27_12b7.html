[edit] Adam Michael Bied

The man, myth, and legend Adam Michael Bied (born October, 1985). He is widely known as Adam Bied, or simply "Adam." He is a common ficture of american history and myth. Well known for his political fame.Adam P. Bied He should not be confused with Adam Beid the famed English novel.

[edit] Early Life

He began his early life in a small town in Upstate New York. After being born in Albany New York Adam settled down in a small suburb with his family. He breifly attended Albany Academy, an elite private school founded in 1814. Following his breif education at A.A. he moved to Voorheesville New York. Voorheesville is, and was, a rural farm town north west of Albany. In September 1994 Adam matriculated at Clayton A. Bouton Central School. He graduated with high honors in 2004 and later went on to study at Boston College. It is important to note that the location of his birth set the seeds in motion for his political ambitions. The capital of one of the nations most important states. After all the three previous governors were presidential candidates.

[edit] The College Years

2004-Present

At Boston College, a private Catholic Jesuit University, Adam Bied quickly excelled in Biology which he is currently studying. He joined the Mendel Society and is an active researcher in both bio-medical and genetics. He joined the St. Thomas More Society in the fall of his freshman year. Though his time on with this society is breif it has "build character" and shaped his world view. In 2005 he was a researcher in the Wadsworth Laboratory. This became his love. For it combined both genetics and phisiology, his primary scientific interests.

Although he has no technical political affiliation, Bied, often corresponds with former Massachusetts Governor and former Presidential Candidate, Michael Dukakis. He has met with both Senator John Kerry and former-Vice President Al Gore. Currently he does work with the Pro-Life club an anti-abortion rights think tank based in Newton Massachusetts. Early on it became clear that he would persue politics. Driven at an early age by the political center of Albany he was quickly engaged by the forces around him. Bied was a briefly affiliated with the Pataki administration during the Summer of 2004. In 2005 he briefly met with Attorney General Elliot Spitzer (D) who is expected to win the 2006 gubenatorial race. With his first taste of politics his future became clear. He would set out the ambitious goal of uniting America at a time when her people were hopelessly divided. Saddly he sits back watching as the legacy of the men he admirers are slowly being undone by the Clinton-Bush administration. Few things in politics are clear and yet Adam Bied stands alone as a figure. Ask who the greatest man of all time is and you'll will get many answers. [edit] Colloquy

Colloquy is an internet-based organization of individuals who have qualified for membership by virtue of performance on IQ tests and other accepted standardized tests including the ACT, GRE, PSAT and SAT. In online literature the group claims "Colloquy is, to the best of our knowledge, the first high-IQ society based entirely on the Internet." [2] [3].

Through its membership criteria, Colloquy accepts individiduals with an intelligence quotient measured in the top 99.5 percantile. Contrast this with the more popular Mensa International, accepting members in the top 98 percentile, and more "selective" orginizations such as the Triple Nine Society, the Prometheus Society and the Mega Society that accept members in the 99.9 percentile and higher.

[edit] ECITE

ECITE

ECITE is the European Contact Improvisation Teachers Exchange Conference. It started in Amsterdam 1985 and was initiated by Nancy Stark Smith. It is the annual meeting of Contact Improvisation teachers of Europe providing opportunities to exchange on Contact Improvisation as a dance technique, as a way of moving and improvising, and as an approach to being.

ECITE was made possible then by those who started to teach Contact Improvisation in Europe and wanted to deepen its practice by researching and playing with kollegues.

The Conference focuses on methods of teaching and methods of exploring the form. It is for people deeply involved with the form of Contact Improvisation. ECITEs welcome you if you are a teacher of Contact Improvisation.

[edit] Dork! (Comic Book)

DORK! is a comic book created by Evan Dorkin and is published on a semi-regular basis by Slave Labor Graphics.

The comic book has a stream-of-consciousness sense of non-sequitor, composed of dozens of two-to-three frame strips delivering one-shot gags and jokes. Recurring themes include the life of a comic book artist, ska and punk music, everyday annoyances, satire and random mayhem. Evan often depicts himself and his wife, Sarah Dyer as protagonists in various story threads.

The series does have several recurring segments to round out the relatively brief joke strips:

The Eltingville Science Fiction, Fantasy and Roleplaying Club -- A group of four archetypal teenage nerds from New York who take fandom to ridiculous extremes. Later turned into a pilot, Welcome To Eltingville.

Murder Family -- A somewhat tasteless, twisted sitcom (presented with audience responses like laughter and applause) based on the Reilys, a family who solves their day-to-day problems with brutal murders.

Myron The Living Voodoo Doll -- A recurring mini-strip with the same joke. An apparently abandoned voodoo doll is shown being destroyed in some way, and then a cut to a human who is likewise destroyed, thinking "I've got to find that doll!"

The Devil Puppet -- A Svengali-like, charismatic storyteller who happens to be a devil hand puppet. He weaves tales (for various audiences depicted) about cruel irony and ignoble dooms from times gone by. While the puppet talks, his expression never changes, and his host [who appears to be Dorkin himself] never speaks. It has been hinted at that the Devil Puppet is a parasitic lifeform that feeds off his host.

How To Get Sued -- Depicts a famous comic book character is a rather slanderous light. For instance, one showed TinTin as a Hitler Youth.

Song List -- Most copies include a song list as "recommended listening" during reading. Naturally, most selections are punk, pseudo-punk or ska.

The last issue created as of this writing was #10.

[edit] Metro Manila Community Orchestra

The Metro Manila Community Orchestra (MMCO) is a project of the Institute for Orchestral Development in the Philippines (IODP). It is composed of over fifty musicians whose ages range from 15 to 48 years old and who come from different backgrounds. Some are students from schools in and around the city. Others are business professionals, government employees and even retirees. What they all have in common is a passion for music. These avid musicians meet at Miriam College twice a week to work on a vast orchestral repertoire ranging from the classical symphonies of Beethoven, Brahms, and Mozart, to works of traditional Filipino composers like Abelardo, San Pedro and contemporary works of living Filipino composers.

[edit] Cosma Suma

Cosma Suma, whose name was often confused with Karma Sutra, was a five piece New York City rock band with Punk and new wave influences from the late 1990's. They played the Lower East Side and were notable regulars on the Bleecker Street strip at The Elbow Room (often on the same bill with contemporaries The Erratics). In 1998 they recorded their first E.P. 5 Little Nibbles...A Taste of Cosma Suma (see review below). In its two year life span Cosma Suma had a spinal Tap-ish amount of bass players including--John Pollard, Nick Tersi, Danny Cohen, Pat Cioffi, and Bruce McGaughlin. The group broke up during the recording sessions of their second E.P. and the founding members Mike Loss, Damian Eckstein, and Sean Moriarty went on the form the more successful Swami.

Review from nyrock.com:

Cosma Suma's strong points seem to be uncannily balanced by their weaknesses. The music is lively; Mike Loss's vocals are appealing and tuneful; the songwriting is good, intelligent and quirky. On the downside, however, are various blemishes that tend to be somewhat distracting: The Farfisa organ brings back painful memories of The Knack, and the falsetto backup vocals and occasional feigned English accent just don't cut it. Back to the good news: these are actually small items that any good producer could easily weed out, allowing Cosma Suma's inherent potential to shine more brightly, which, I imagine, it will, as the band continues to record and gig around New York City. In addition to Loss, the band consists of Sean Moriarity (guitar), Jason Brown (keyboards, harmonica), and Damian Eckstein (drummer/songwriter).

[edit] Primary peritoneal cancer

Primary Peritoneal Cancer is a cancer of the cells lining the peritoneum, or abdominal cavity.

Prognosis and treatment is the same as for the most common variarion of Ovarian Cancer, epithelial ovarian cancer.

Some studies indicate that between 7 and 20 percent of initially diagnosed epithelial ovarian cancers could be properly reclassified as primary peritoneal cancers.

The cancer does involve mesothelial cells and can be medically describved as mesothelioma; however, this cancer should not be confused with asbestos-related mesothelioma, which requires different treatments and has a much higher mortality rate.

[edit] Jay Mafia

Jay Mafia was born to a 21 year old South Dallas mother of two in 1983. Shortly after his birth, his father died and he began to move around the Dallas metroplex but spent the majority of his life in the North and South Dallas areas. While coming up, he was surrounded by a strong diversity of music and accrued street fame through his various mixtapes selling over 5,000 units independently. Before he could have a taste of success, his life quickly took a turn for the worst when he dropped out of school, moved back to the southern Dallas area, and began establishing ties with the LPG ( Lagow Park Gangstahs ). This would only hurt the young mafioso's ambition by involving him in numerous felonies such as aggravated robberies, aggravated assaults, felony drug distribution and check forgery. After doing a ten month bid in the county and surviving several police integragations, Jay was dismissed of all charges with the exception of two aggravated robberies which were later decreased to two counts of 2nd degree robbery . For this he was sentence to 10 years in the Texas Department Of Criminal Justice and within prison walls he began to formulate the company we know today as Outbreak Records. His music history goes back as far as 8 years, so this assisted him with such hits as "Gangstah Guerrillas" and "7 Concybinz'" written behind the stone cold prison walls in Tennessee Colony. Shortly before his release, he met Steve Brisco aka Yung Gunz and began to paint the finishing touches to Outbreak Records. Although Yung Gunz is currently incarcerated on an 8 year bid for his role in a 2001 aggravated robbery, Jay Mafia has since remained loyal and established "The New and Untouchable" Outbreak Records.

The Bakiga people can be found in the counties of Ndorwa, Rubanda, Rukiga, Kabale, Kinkizi, Rubabo.

Rukiga or Lukiga is their language. Mukiga is one person Bakiga are the people. Bakiga means people of the hills

The Rukiga language is similar to Runyankore, and Dictionaries usually group the two languages together.
