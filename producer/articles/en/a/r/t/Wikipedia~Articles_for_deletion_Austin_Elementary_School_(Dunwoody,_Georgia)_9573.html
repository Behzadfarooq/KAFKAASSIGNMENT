The result of the debate was no consensus to delete the article. Mailer Diablo 02:01, 3 March 2006 (UTC)

[edit] Austin elementary school

  • Is this school eligible for an article on a non-local encyclopedia. If so, please explain. If not, Delete. Georgia guy 21:02, 25 February 2006 (UTC)
  • This is a very accurate article. I know this (laughs) because I am an insider. On whether or not it is elligible for a non-local listing in an encyclopedia, I believe that it is as elligible to stay as any

school listed in Wikipedia. Gigor 21:02, 25 February 2006

  • Keep all schools Jcuk 23:06, 25 February 2006 (UTC)
  • No vote you want to get rid of an article on a shcool? Best of luck. The article on Asbestos might have a list of specialist tailors at the end. Just zis Guy you know? 23:15, 25 February 2006 (UTC)
  • Strong delete as non-notable unencyclopedic school, which should rightly be considered a non-notable corporation. — Adrian Lamo ·· 05:54, 26 February 2006 (UTC)
  • Delete. Non-notable. If kept, do not expand with transient and generic information solely for the sake of expansion. — Rebelguys2 talk 09:27, 26 February 2006 (UTC)
  • Keep. It is a real school. Carioca 20:20, 26 February 2006 (UTC)
The act of existing, while commendable, is not notable. — Adrian Lamo ·· 21:08, 26 February 2006 (UTC)
  • Keep. Not entirely sure if elementary schools should be included, but the precedent to keep them is clear and the article is more than a substub. Sjakkalle (Check!) 08:58, 27 February 2006 (UTC)
  • Keep as per Wikipedia:Schools/Arguments#Keep, article is off to a good start. Silensor 23:26, 27 February 2006 (UTC)
  • Keep as per Wikipedia:Schools/Arguments#Keep  ALKIVAR™ 02:20, 28 February 2006 (UTC)
  • Keep and allow for organic growth. Bahn Mi 02:27, 28 February 2006 (UTC)
  • Keep, suitable for a local encyclopedia or one aiming to be comprehensive. Kappa 02:40, 28 February 2006 (UTC)
  • Delete per Adrian. If this comes in, all schools do, then all churches with schools, and all clubs at schools, and we won't have an encyclopedia we'll have the Internet. Carlossuarez46 02:34, 2 March 2006 (UTC)
