Artificial Mind and Movement (A2M), founded in 1992 in Quebec City, Québec, is one of the largest independent game development studios in Canada, specializing in the production of 2D and 3D action/adventure games. A2M's team consists of over 375 employees, which are involved in creating games for the Xbox 360, PS3, Wii, PS2, GameCube, Xbox, PC, Game Boy Advance, PSP and Nintendo DS.
