  • This most defiently is not a hoax, Petros is actually is for this.—the preceding unsigned comment is by 69.142.195.171 (talk • contribs) 15:55, 1 February 2006 (UTC)

And please make it official policy to delete anything and everything which hosts on myspace! —the preceding unsigned comment is by Kingboyk (talk • contribs) 16:05, 1 February 2006 (UTC) Whoops, forgot to sign. --kingboyk 23:13, 1 February 2006 (UTC)
