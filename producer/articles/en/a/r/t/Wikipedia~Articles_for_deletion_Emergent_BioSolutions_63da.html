[edit] Emergent BioSolutions

Promotional company article. No verifiable references are provided, and it does not pass WP:CORP. An attempt at a prod was simply removed by an anonymous editor, so moving along to AfD. Recommend speedy deletion. Elonka 20:44, 27 October 2006 (UTC)

  • Keep While it's too early to say if their Antrhrax vacine is of practical wide use, this company has certainly generated alot of mainstream press. Here are some just from the last month : [1] [2] [3] --Marriedtofilm 20:58, 27 October 2006 (UTC)
  • Keep Perhaps the article could use some copyeditting, but they are know for their anthrax vaccine, and they have been reported on by independent news sources as shown by Marriedtofilm. -- Whpq 21:00, 27 October 2006 (UTC)
  • Keep Seems notable enough. --Kf4bdy talk contribs 22:26, 27 October 2006 (UTC)
  • Keep Merely getting (even a lot of) press coverage doesn't in itself mean anything much in terms of encyclopedic notability. Notability here comes from the fact that this is the sole licensed manufacturer of anthrax vaccine in the US, and one of a small handful in the world due to tight state control of such operations (for obvious reasons). Article does need cleanup though Bwithh 23:35, 27 October 2006 (UTC)
