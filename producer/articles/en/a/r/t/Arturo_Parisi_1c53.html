Arturo Parisi

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Arturo Parisi (born 13 September 1940 in San Mango Piemonte, Salerno) is an Italian politician, member of the Daisy—Democracy is Freedom party and currently the Italian Minister of Defence.

A well-known strong supporter of Romano Prodi and the Olive Tree, Parisi is head of the Federal Assembly of the Daisy party since 2004.
