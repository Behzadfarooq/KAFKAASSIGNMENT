[edit] Neaera (band)

Another dubious CSD A7 candidate. The latest version mentions that they're signed to a major record label Metal Blade Records, and have released a couple of albums under that label, which would make them notable per WP:MUSIC. However, all previous versions failed to assert notability of the band. Thus I'm bringing here to seek a consensus to keep or to delete. Please don't tag this as either CSD G4 or A7. Awyong Jeffrey Mordecai Salleh 18:06, 30 August 2006 (UTC)

  • Neutral. Band generates quite a number of hits on Google, most of which are relevant to the band. The band also links to other Wiki pages, but the band members itself are not notable in their own aspect. --Nishkid64 18:23, 30 August 2006 (UTC)
  • Neutral. Band is not very notable and article is poorly done. But, the band does have many hits on Google, so they must have some undeground following.—The preceding unsigned comment was added by Worthlessboy1420 (talk • contribs).
  • Delete. not notable. no sources other than personal website of band. poorly written article. Anlace 22:10, 30 August 2006 (UTC)
  • Keep Meets WP:MUSIC with two albums on Metal Blade Records, who have plenty of notable acts, including The Black Dahlia Murder and King Diamond, and formerly Slayer and GWAR. Verifiable through allmusic. I'll add the link to the article to meet WP:V. --Joelmills 03:19, 31 August 2006 (UTC)
  • Keep per Joelmills. - Mgm|(talk) 09:06, 31 August 2006 (UTC)
  • Keep, two albums and signed to one of the biggest metal labels. Prolog 19:58, 5 September 2006 (UTC)
