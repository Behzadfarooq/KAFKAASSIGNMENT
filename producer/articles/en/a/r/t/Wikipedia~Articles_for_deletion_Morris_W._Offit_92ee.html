  • Delete. Agree with Jcbarr. The rest should be looked at. Merely serving on a corporate board is insufficient, even for a major corporation, unless the people have other claims to notability. Fan1967 17:49, 23 May 2006 (UTC)
  • Keep and cleanup. As important as Google hits are, more important from our point of view is reliable third party sources such as this Forbes profile see [1]. Article is a mess though and needs to be rewritten. Capitalistroadster 19:54, 23 May 2006 (UTC)
