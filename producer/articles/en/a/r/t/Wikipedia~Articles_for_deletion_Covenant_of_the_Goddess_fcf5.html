[edit] Covenant of the Goddess

This is both non-notable and spammy JianLi 19:44, 24 July 2006 (UTC)

  • Delete unverified and sourceless, official site has Alexa rank of 1,111,293. Andrew Lenahan - Starblind 19:54, 24 July 2006 (UTC)
  • Keep. ALthough the article does need some sources, the group itself is quite notable, receiving 40,600 hits on Google, 717 of which are unique - quite a feat when put into perspective. As for the above comment, I don't think that the Alexa statistic should really come into pay here, as the article is about the organization, not about the organization's website. For example, the official website of the redcross recieves an ALexa ranking of only 13,254. tmopkisn tlka 20:49, 24 July 2006 (UTC)
  • Keep, notable neopagan organization both in longevity and size. Article could use some neutralizing and sourcing, but they deserve an article. Will add to WikiProject_Neopaganism. -- nae'blis (talk) 21:13, 25 July 2006 (UTC)
  • Keep -- guideline should not be WP:WEB here; this shouldn't be an article about a website. Organisation is arguably the most important Neopagan organisation in the United States and inarguably in the top five. See the 1979 book Drawing Down the Moon by Margot Adler for more information about the organisation and its importance, or more recent surveys of Neopaganism in the U.S. Jkelly 21:49, 25 July 2006 (UTC)
