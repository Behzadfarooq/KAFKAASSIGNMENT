[edit] Pokémon Brown

This is a fan hack. I'm pretty sure we mostly don't list those for notability reasons. It says it's widely known as the most popular Pokémon ROM hack, but provides no evidence of this and that isn't exactly a notable category by itself. It gets about 1500 Google hits, but most seem to just be lists of hacked ROMS and the number-two ranked one was the Wikipedia article. Considering the article has no categories and there are few links to it from other articles, this is impressive. Ace of Sevens 11:17, 23 July 2006 (UTC)
