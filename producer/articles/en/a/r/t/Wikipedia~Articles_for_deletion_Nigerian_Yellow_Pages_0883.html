[edit] Nigerian Yellow Pages

'+"Nigerian Yellow Pages"' googles only about 6000 hits, of which I can't find a sensible reference near the top, and I've not found that the company meets WP:CORP (not the primary subject of non-trivial publication, not listed on an important ranking index, value not used in a stock market index). The only reliable references provided appear to me closely affiliated with the company, and certainly not independent, nor separately noteworthy. Many of its incarnations (including its current state at time of nomination) read like an SEO inbound links generator. I bring it here simply to find out whether others agree that it could be deleted. RobertG ♬ talk 14:50, 11 October 2006 (UTC)
