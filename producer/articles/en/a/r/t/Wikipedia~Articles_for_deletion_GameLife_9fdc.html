[edit] GameLife

GameLife (edit|talk|history|links|watch|logs)

Online gaming show. Only real claim to notability is getting hits from being an Awful Link of the Day on Something Awful and getting sponsored to go to E3 by some media company. Only two minor non-first-party sources, one of which is a press-release. Alexa rank 2,596,635 Drat (Talk) 10:25, 8 December 2006 (UTC)
