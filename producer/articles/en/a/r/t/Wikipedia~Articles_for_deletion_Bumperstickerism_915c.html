[edit] Bumperstickerism

The term "bumperstickerism" does not seem to be in widespread use, and is not an appropriate article title. The content may be more at home in the bumper sticker article if appearing on bumper stickers is the defining feature, otherwise epigram, aphorism and the like may be more appropriate --Tabor 00:44, 8 May 2005 (UTC)
