This category is for stub articles relating to arthropods, including myriapoda (centipedes and millipedes), merostomata (horseshoe crabs) and pycnogonids (sea spiders). You can help Wikipedia by expanding them.

To add an article to this category, use {{arthropod-stub}} instead of {{stub}}.

See also {{insect-stub}}, {{arachnid-stub}} and {{crustacean-stub}} for other arthropod-related stubs.
