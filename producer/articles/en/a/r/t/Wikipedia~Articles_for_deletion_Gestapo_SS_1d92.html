Kommando Sturmblut remains the only original member. After the solo effort of Gestapo SS/Resistencia 88 split of Acireman Southern Terrorists taken from the Kicking Down the Door demo. The 2nd demo was entitled Breaking Down the Door featuring Krieghammer88 on vocals and drum prog while Kommando did back ups, keys, guitar, and bass as well as some drum prog. The 3rd Kameradenschaft was live materials featuring Volkh on drums, Krieghammer88 on vocals and guitars, Kommando on guitars, back ups, and bass. All these were released on one full length cd by Clawhammer Records out of Maryland entitled "Vinlandic Stormtroopers". The latest recording for the Tribute to Strikeforce Comp. was the songs "Traitor Crusher 88" and a cover of "Banned from the Pubs" by PATTTB. This line up featured live materials of Kommando on guitar and vocals with Arrogant Drunk on drums and bass added in after the fact. The 2nd full length "Traitor Crusher88" looks to be released on Strong Survive whenever it is recorded since the line up is now pending. The original sounds were hatecore/rac/nsbm. But, now it is basically a complete RAC/Hatecore sound to their style. Many of their recordings have been sloppy, but they are indeed one of Vinland's most blatantly racist white power bands in a long time.
