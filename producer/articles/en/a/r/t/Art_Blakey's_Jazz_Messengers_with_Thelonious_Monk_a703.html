Recorded in 1957, this album was the result of a collaboration of Thelonious Monk with Art Blakey and the Jazz Messengers.

[edit] Track listing

  1. "Evidence" (composed by Monk)
  2. "In Walked Bud" (Monk)
  3. "Blue Monk" (Monk)
  4. "I mean you" (Monk & Coleman Hawkins)
  5. "Rhythm-A-Ning" (Monk)
  6. "Purple Shades" (Johnny Griffin)

[edit] Personnel

  • Art Blakey - Drums
  • Thelonious Monk - Piano
  • Johnny Griffin - Tenor Sax
  • Bill Hardman - Trumpet
  • Spanky Debrest - Bass

It was produced by Nesuhi Ertegun and the sound engineering was by Earl Brown.
