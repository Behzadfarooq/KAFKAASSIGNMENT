[edit] Holy Spirit as Revealed in Acts through Revelation

The title itself is POV - "revealed" is a theological term. The article appears to be entirely original research with no sources given, and only one POV represented throughout. From the layout it appears to be a college essay. There are already several related articles, eg Holy Spirit, Acts etc. I suggest anyone with an interest in the article merge any material they feel is important into other articles; then it can be deleted. David L Rattigan 07:23, 13 June 2006 (UTC)
