Does not seem to meet WP:BIO. From the history, this also appears to be WP:VAIN. I originally slapped a prod on this months ago, but totally forgot about it, so here it is. Delete unless more third-party evidence comes up to explain why he is encyclopedic. --Kinu t/c 02:53, 29 June 2006 (UTC)
