[edit] Eh

Dicdef, already in wiktionary. It is rather lengthy but it really only deals with the usage of this oft-Canadian interjection. Not encyclopedic, eh? Radiant_* 11:02, May 25, 2005 (UTC)

  • Keep. The article should be turned into a more encyclopedia-style entry. If consensus is to delete, redirect → Canadian slang. —Markaci 2005-05-25 T 17:27 Z
  • Keep, though the article is rather messy right now. Also, there are other uses of eh that should be noted (for example, Italian usage); the article should be cleaned up and expanded. Mindmatrix 18:23, 25 May 2005 (UTC)
  • Strong Keep Revolución 19:16, 25 May 2005 (UTC)
  • Keep. An important aspect of a national stereotype, recurs over and over in popular culture. Smerdis of Tlön 19:49, 25 May 2005 (UTC)
  • Keep. While I'd normally suggesting chucking this type of thing at Wiktionary, it really does go into detail on its uses, an can easily be expanded to me more international. --InShaneee 21:00, 25 May 2005 (UTC)
  • Keep and yes, InShaneee, I've just expanded it a little to be more international! :) Grutness...wha? 22:22, 25 May 2005 (UTC)
  • Keep More than just a dictionary entry. siafu 22:24, 25 May 2005 (UTC)
  • Keep goatse has spoken "Eh" Klonimus 23:06, 25 May 2005 (UTC)
  • Keep. A classic stereotype of Canadian culture. Just like an article on "like" or "d'oh". Wiwaxia 12:21, 26 May 2005 (UTC)
    • And of New Zealand culture, too!
  • Keep. - Lucky13pjn 20:25, May 26, 2005 (UTC)
  • Keep Useful info, not just on the Canadian stereotype. BrainyBabe 16:08, 27 May 2005 (UTC)
  • Keep. -- BD2412 talk 01:55, 2005 May 30 (UTC)
