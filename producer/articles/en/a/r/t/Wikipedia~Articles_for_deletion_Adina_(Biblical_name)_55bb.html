[edit] Adina (Biblical name)

Delete this extraordinarily obscure biblical character. Mentioned precisely once in 1 Chr. 11:42, available, among other places, here. WP does not have articles for any of the other hundreds of NN dudes mentioned in that book. Prod remover says, "biblical figures are presumptively notable". You decide! - the.crazy.russian τ ç ë 01:13, 12 April 2006 (UTC)
