[edit] 2026 FIFA World Cup

Nominating for AfD, since the article has been prodded before and the prod was contested. Concern: "I don't suspect we should be speculating this far in advance -- the article cited says: "Nicolas Maingot, an official at FIFA's media department, said FIFA has yet to decide on the sites, or even the continents, for the World Cups beyond 2014." Considering this statement, should this even be an article currently? Wikipedia is not a crystal ball Ian Manka Talk to me! 07:12, 12 July 2006 (UTC)" --Zoz (t) 11:32, 12 July 2006 (UTC)
