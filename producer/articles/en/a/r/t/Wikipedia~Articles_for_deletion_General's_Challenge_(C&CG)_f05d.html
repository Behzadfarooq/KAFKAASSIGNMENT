[edit] General's Challenge (C&CG)

This and the following pages comprise a guide on how to play Command and Conquer. A number of these pages have already been deleted, or are about to be (see here, here, here, and here). So this is a mass-nomination of the few that remain. Wikipedia is most emphatically NOT a how-to guide - this is stated specifically in Wikipedia:What Wikipedia is not - and as per precedent at Wikipedia:Articles for deletion/Structures of the GLA and Wikipedia:Articles for deletion/Structures of the Chinese, this should be deleted forthwith. This is basically an abuse of Wikipedia's free hosting to allow someone to have images on their GameFAQs guide. Closing admin, please also note the vast array of dubious 'fair use' screenshots that are attached to these pages. Proto||type 12:41, 16 June 2006 (UTC)

  • Note - I am also nominating the following related pages because of the same reasons:
    • USA Story
    • GLA Storyline
    • China Storyline
    • United States of America (C&C: Generals)
    • Global Liberation Army (probably could well be redirected to Command and Conquer after deletion, if it occurs)
    • China (C&C: Generals)

That is all.
