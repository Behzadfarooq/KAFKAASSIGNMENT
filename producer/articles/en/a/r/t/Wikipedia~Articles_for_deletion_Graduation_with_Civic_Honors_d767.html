[edit] Graduation with Civic Honors

Vanity/personal essay. Delete as per Wikipedia is not a publisher of original thought. Court Jester 21:15, 17 April 2006 (UTC)

  • If you feel Graduation with Civic Honors is vanity, then delete the article. However, in this case does the topic not exist? Nels Lindahl
  • Delete. Nels Lindahl is the originator of this proposal and the author of the article. Nels, if you're reading this, the proposal might be a good idea -- I personally think so -- but wikipedia is not the place to promote it. --Lockley 21:34, 17 April 2006 (UTC)
  • Delete the page, but the existence of Graduation with Civic Honors is not original thought, students have graduated with civic honors. --Nels Lindahl 21:40, 17 April 2006 (UTC)
  • If an encyclopedic, externally verifiable article can be produced, through the use of reliable, third-party sources, on this topic, then I may be inclined to support its inclusion.
