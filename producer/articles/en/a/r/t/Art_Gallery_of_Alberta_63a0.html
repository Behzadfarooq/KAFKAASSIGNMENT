The Art Gallery of Alberta (formerly the Edmonton Art Gallery) is a public art gallery located in Edmonton, Alberta Canada. Its collection of over 5000 works of art includes historical and contemporary paintings, sculptures, installation works and photographs by Canadian and international artists.

Originally designed in 1968 as a Brutalist building by Don Bittorf, the gallery is currently undergoing an 80 million dollar renovation.
