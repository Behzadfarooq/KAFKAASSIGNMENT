[edit] Anthropos Consulting and Corporate Anthropology

Non-notable company. I am also nominating Corporate Anthropology in with this article as they reference each other and the "corporate anthropology" article seems to be an advert for Anthropos. Andy Saunders - 00:59, 30 January 2006 (UTC)

  • Delete and Delete Attempt to advertise --Bugturd 01:26, 30 January 2006 (UTC)

It is not true that Anthropos Consulting is a non-notable company. If you visit the site www.anthropos.com.br and look at client list you´ll find most of the 500 Fortune companies with branches in Brazil and Latin America. As the first established company in corporate anthropology it´s natural the link. It will be a loss for Wikipedia to not have corporate anthropology and Anthropos Consulting in file. —The preceding unsigned comment was added by Marinsfilho (talk • contribs).

  • The unsigned entry above was placed by the author (and possibly the subject) of the article. The only contributions by this user are the article creations and the comment above. --Bugturd 01:40, 30 January 2006 (UTC)
  • Delete both as badvertising, made worse by trying to use WP to provide a legitimacy to their methodology that is otherwise lacking. Eusebeus 07:32, 30 January 2006 (UTC)
  • IMHO being able ti list notable others as your customers does not make you notable. Consider a sandwich vendor who delivers around the London Stock Exchange. Delete as advertising copy. -- Simon Cursitor 09:03, 30 January 2006 (UTC)
  • Delete as advertising, and delete unless rewritten. Google seems to indicate that "coporate anthropology" is indeed a real term, but the current article is nothing more than badvertising. Zunaid 12:56, 30 January 2006 (UTC)
  • Delete as advertisment. --Terence Ong (恭喜发财) 14:47, 30 January 2006 (UTC)
