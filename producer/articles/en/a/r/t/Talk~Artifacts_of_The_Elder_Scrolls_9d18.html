[edit] Perhaps Separate the Artifacts by Game?

Perhaps there should be separate articles for the artifacts of each of the Elder Scrolls game (i.e. Morrowind, Oblivion) -- to avoid confusion.

Anyone agree/disagree?

First, sign your comments. As for article separation, well, the title is for all of the artifacts in the Elder Scrolls series and it's relatively small, so I don't feel a separation is necessary. Most of the artifacts appear in more than one game, so separation is sort of unnecessary. But, I do feel that some of the artifacts that are Daedra-affiliated should be removed from this section; they're already talked about in the Daedra Artifacts article. Plus, there shouldn't be any confusion. Just read the descriptions of the artifacts. BishopTutu 03:58, 18 December 2006 (UTC)
My apologies, I wrote that last night while quite tired, so in my oversight I forgot. No need to be nasty about it. Anyway it was merely a suggestion -- for artifacts that serve different purposes and are slightly different in the variety of games, perhaps having individual TES game artifact articles would be more effective. --24.190.158.211 16:59, 18 December 2006 (UTC)
First off, I wasn't trying to be nasty. Too many people simply type their comments without signing. Second, there aren't nearly enough artifacts in the Elder Scrolls for it to merit article separation. Yeah, the artifacts do different things, but that's not the focus of the article; it's simply the artifacts of the Elder Scrolls. It's listing the several artifacts of the Elder Scrolls. Most of the artifacts were already separated from the article (the Daedra Artifacts) and, because it's so many of them, thats probably all that can, and needs to be, separated. And, like I said, I feel a few of the listed artifacts should be removed because they are already talked about in the Daedra Artifacts article. Even then, the articles that appear exclusively in one game tell which game they specifically appear it. In the end, the article is fine, even though a few should be removed. BishopTutu 17:16, 18 December 2006 (UTC)
