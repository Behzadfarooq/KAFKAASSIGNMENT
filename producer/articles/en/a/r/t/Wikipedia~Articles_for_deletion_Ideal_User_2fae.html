[edit] Ideal User

Frankly, I'm not sure this should be deleted, but at least it should be moved to back-end pages (or whatever that's called) as an essay or something. I don't think we ought to have an "ideal user" or even this page when we already have loads of guides about how "anyone" can edit wikipedia. On the other hand, I don't want to give anyone a reason to claim we are censoring suggestions, even silly ones.
