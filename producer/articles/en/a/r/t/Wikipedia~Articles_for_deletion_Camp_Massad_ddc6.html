[edit] Camp Massad

To quote User:Saberwyn, this is a US summer camp. Unless the article can explain, through the use of verifiable information taken from reliable third-party sources, how this summer camp is significantly different from the standard, run of the mill summer camp, there is nothing we can do that the camp's own site cannot do better. Entirely non-notable and full of non-verifyable POV statements. Páll (Die pienk olifant) 05:02, 20 May 2006 (UTC)
