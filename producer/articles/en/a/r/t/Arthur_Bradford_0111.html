Arthur H. Bradford (born November 19, 1969, in Boothbay Harbor, Maine) is an American short story author. He has one book of short stories published, Dogwalker (Knopf ISBN 0-375-72669-1). He was a contributor to the McSweeney's publication The Future Dictionary of America.

He was also the director of "How's Your News?", a documentary wherein disabled and handicapped adults interview unsuspecting passersby in a cross-country road trip.

Trivium: Arthur Bradford is a great-great-great-grandson of department-store magnate Abraham Abraham.
