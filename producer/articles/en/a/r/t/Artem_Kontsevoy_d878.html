Artem Kontsevoy (Belarusian: Арцём Канцавы) (born May 20, 1983) is a football player from Belarus. He plays as a striker for FC MTZ-RIPO Minsk and the Belarus national team.

  • Place of Birth: Gomel, Belarus
  • Height: 177 cm
  • Weight: 70 kg

[edit] Previous teams

  • Spartak Moscow
  • RUOR Minsk

[edit] Honors and awards

  • 2005 - Belarusian Cup winner
