[edit] Hippopotomonstrosesquipedaliophobia

WP:OR or WP:NEO, either way, I gave up looking for genuine references for this word after the first 15 pages of Google hits. I don't think every "jocular" (i.e. something someone made up) definition deserves a unique page anyway. Earle Martin [t/c] 13:21, 12 October 2006 (UTC)

  • Delete. The properly spelled term (hippopotomonstrosesquippedaliophobia) does exist, but this article is merely a dictdef. The word already has an entry on Wiktionary, so it should be simply deleted, unless someone can add encyclopedic material (which, judging by google search results, seems improbable). Srose (talk) 15:37, 12 October 2006 (UTC)
    • Note that Hippopotomonstrosesquippedaliophobia (AfD discussion) redirects to -phob-. Uncle G 14:17, 13 October 2006 (UTC)
  • This AfD nomination was incomplete. It is listed now. DumbBOT 13:20, 13 October 2006 (UTC)
  • Thanks for spotting that. -- Earle Martin [t/c] 14:32, 13 October 2006 (UTC)
  • Delete, incorrectly spelled (I think) dicdef. PJM 13:37, 13 October 2006 (UTC)
  • Delete. Hippo-what? Shadow1 (talk) 14:30, 13 October 2006 (UTC)
  • Delete per Srose and because Wikipedia is not a dictionary. The Wictionary entry on the word is very nice, so there is no need to transwiki. TheronJ 15:22, 13 October 2006 (UTC)
  • Delete per Srose. *drew 15:59, 13 October 2006 (UTC)
  • Redirect to -phob- to prevent anybody recreating it by accident, as a mispelling of hippopotomonstrosesquippedaliophobia which is redirected to the same place, and because if anybody does type that all out they deserve an article at the very least. Yomanganitalk 16:43, 13 October 2006 (UTC)
