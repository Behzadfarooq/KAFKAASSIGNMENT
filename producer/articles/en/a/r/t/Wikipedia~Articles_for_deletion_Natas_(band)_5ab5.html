[edit] Natas (band)

Does not seem to meet WP:BAND, dead end, poorly written and no external sources. MER-C 07:42, 20 July 2006 (UTC)

  • Strong Keep - the band exists ([1]) and has released at least one album that I'm aware of (I own it and have never set foot anywhere near Detroit). Additionally, Esham has his own article here already and is generally credited as an influence on performers such as Insane Clown Posse and Eminem. The article should be rewritten considerably to cover this sort of thing, but is definitely something which should be kept. BigHaz 07:55, 20 July 2006 (UTC)
