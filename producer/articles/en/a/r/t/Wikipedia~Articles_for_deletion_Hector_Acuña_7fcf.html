  • Background: The article appears to have been written by the students of a Peruvian mathematics teacher, who apparently requests that it be deleted. Comment: As the article does not fulfill WP:V and WP:NOR, I'm voting to delete. encephalon 19:17, 11 October 2005 (UTC)
  • Speedy as CSD:A7. IMO a math professorship and owning cows doesn't even attempt to establish notability. Factor in the apparent request by the subject for removal... I'd say this is a good speedy candidate. Otherwise, simply delete.--Isotope23 19:19, 11 October 2005 (UTC)
    • FWIW, 1. the article as it stands makes too many claims that can reasonably be taken as assertions of notability. 2. the thoughts of the subject of an article on the article can be important context, but should not enter into decision making over keeping or deleting the article. Kind regards encephalon 19:29, 11 October 2005 (UTC)
      • You're such a stickler enceph  :)--Isotope23 20:21, 11 October 2005 (UTC)
        • Aren't I? :) If it means anything, it's borne out of experience with a functional bureaucracy. When you see very poor articles get speedied, but then nominated for undeletion at VFU and then returned to AFD to get redeleted 20 days later, that sort of becomes a strong incentive to help get things done correctly the first time, if only to save us some time. But you're right, it makes me look like a humorless drone. :) encephalon 20:51, 11 October 2005 (UTC)
