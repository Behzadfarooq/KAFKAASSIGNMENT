[edit] Bilge

Previous version: [1]

Another page pending deletion that should not be deleted. It contained encyclopedic content about how bilge water was dealt with (from buckets to eletric pumps) and the reason for doing so (psychological as well as practical). In addition it mentioned a Khan of that name. This content is not in wikionary [2]. Kappa 13:55, 16 Mar 2005 (UTC)
