[edit] Escape from Samsara

Nomination for deletion No claim made of encyclopedic notability. Wikipedia is not a nightlife guide. Clarification - "Escape from Samsara" was not a nightclub in terms of a physical venue; it was one type of regular party night that used the The Fridge as a venue. I am not questioning the encyclopedic notability of The Fridge. A little more detail could be added to the Escape from Samsara mention there, but a separate article hasn't been justified. I originally attached a prod tag, but I saw that the creator had recently discussed this article, and seems to have created the article in earnest, so I decided to take it here for discussion instead. Bwithh 00:45, 26 October 2006 (UTC)
