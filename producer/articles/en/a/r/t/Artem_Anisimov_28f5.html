Artem Anisimov (born May 24, 1988 in Yaroslavl, U.S.S.R.; now Russia) is a professional ice hockey player.

[edit] Playing career

Anisimov, who plays as a centre, played for Lokomotiv Yaroslavl in the 2005-06 season and was drafted 54th overall in the 2006 NHL Entry Draft by the New York Rangers.
