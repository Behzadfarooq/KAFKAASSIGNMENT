Arthur Longbottom (born Leeds Yorkshire January 30, 1933) was a footballer with QPR.

He signed in 1954 and made his debut in the 0-3 defeat against Leyton Orient in March 1955. He played inside left and went on to play 201 league games for Rangers scoring 62 league goals.

Arthur transferred to Port Vale in 1961 and later had spells at Millwall, Oxford United and Colchester United.
