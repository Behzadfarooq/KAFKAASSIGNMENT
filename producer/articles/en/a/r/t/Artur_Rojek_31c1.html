Artur Rojek

Artur Rojek (born May 6, 1972) is the guitarist and lead singer of the Polish alternative rock group Myslovitz. He and the lead guitarist Wojciech Powaga founded the Mysłowice-based group in 1992.

The band were initially called The Freshmen, after 1990 movie The Freshman, featuring Marlon Brando and Matthew Broderick. However, in 1994 they changed it to Myslovitz, which is German for their town of origin Mysłowice.
