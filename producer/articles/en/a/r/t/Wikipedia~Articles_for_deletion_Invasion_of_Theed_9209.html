  • Significant plot element in notable (although lousy) popular movie from highly-notable series, so merge any non-duplicate but important material per WP:FICT. If the two articles cited by AMIB contain sufficient Invasion-related context for their levels, then just delete and redirect to one of them. Barno 05:52, 26 September 2005 (UTC)
    • The information in this article is already covered in Battle of Naboo. The author of this article even states that the Invasion (Siege) of Theed is usually grouped as one of the actions of the Battle of Naboo, and directs people to the main article. Although it is a major plot point, I don't think there's enough context to justify an individual and seperate article. Saberwyn 06:21, 26 September 2005 (UTC)
  • Redirect to Battle of Naboo if the article's claimm that more details can be found there is true. If there is anything in this article which is not in that article, merge it in. Sjakkalle (Check!) 08:35, 26 September 2005 (UTC)
