Arthur Edward Cresswell (born on 7 August 1917 in Christchurch, and died on 3 August 2002 in Blenheim). He was a New Zealand cricketer who played for Wellington and was one of the first players who played for Central Districts in the early 1950's. He was the younger brother of Fen Cresswell.
