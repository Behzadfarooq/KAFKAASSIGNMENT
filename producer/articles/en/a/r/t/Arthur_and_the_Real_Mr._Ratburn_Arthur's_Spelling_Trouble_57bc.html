"Arthur and the Real Mr. Ratburn/Arthur's Spelling Trubble" [sic] is the second episode of the first season of the children's TV series Arthur. It was preceded by Arthur's Eyes/Francine's Bad Hair Day, and followed by D.W. All Wet/Buster's Dino Dilemma.

[edit] Plot

[edit] Arthur and the Real Mr. Ratburn

Arthur and the gang get a new scary teacher named Mr. Ratburn.

[edit] Arthur's Spelling Trouble

Arthur has trouble with a spelling contest.

[edit] Date Aired

September 3, 1996 (US)

[edit] Trivia

  • Although the episode stats the first appearence of Mr.Ratburn he actually appears as a teacher in the first episode.
