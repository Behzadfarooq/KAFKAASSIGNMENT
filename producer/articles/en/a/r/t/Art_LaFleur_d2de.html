Art LaFleur (born on September 9, 1943 in Gary, Indiana) is an American character actor. He has had many guest-starring roles on television shows, including Angel and JAG, but is probably best known for his appearance as Babe Ruth in The Sandlot. LaFleur also appeared as a baseball player, Chick Gandil, in Field of Dreams, and as the White House's security chief alongside Sinbad in Disney's First Kid. He also played McNulty, Jack Deth's boss in both Trancers (1985) and Trancers II (1991) and played Banes in The Replacements (2000).
