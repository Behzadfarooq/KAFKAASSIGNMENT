[edit] Christina Shei

Christina Shei (edit|talk|history|links|watch|logs)

Article about a NN person, fails both WP:BIO and WP:PORNBIO. Dismas|(talk) 20:57, 12 December 2006 (UTC)

I suggest that it should be kept. Several articles are linking to it. The article is still informative. Several web pages were about her a few of years ago. Facts about her can be found in "The Bare Facts Video Guide 2001 CD-ROM". Perhaps someone has access to it. Longhairadmirer 19:10, 13 December 2006 (UTC)
Regarding articles on Wikipedia that link to this one: two are lists (List of Playboy NSS models S-Z and List of women with very long hair) one of which is AfD'd, and the other three are all relating to this very AfD. Tabercil 22:40, 18 December 2006 (UTC)
  • Delete. Don't see anything about her in the article, just a list of magazine appearances. Only Web pages I can find are of the "insert random name here" type. That sort of thing belongs in List of Playboy NSS models S-Z. Delete or (second choice) merge there. AnonEMouse (squeak) 21:23, 18 December 2006 (UTC)
  • Delete. Article as it stands now does not present anywhere near a strong enough argument for her to be considered notable, and given the relative sparsity of info that comes back if you google her name, expanding it might be difficult without doing original research. Tabercil 22:40, 18 December 2006 (UTC)
  • Delete. Article does not demonstrate subject's notability as it pertains to WP:BIO or WP:PORNBIO. -- Joe Beaudoin Jr. Think out loud 23:07, 18 December 2006 (UTC)
