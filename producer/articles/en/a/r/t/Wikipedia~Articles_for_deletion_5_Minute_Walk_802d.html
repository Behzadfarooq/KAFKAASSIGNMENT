[edit] 5_Minute_Walk

Delete - not notable per WP:MUSIC, vanity entry Funkymuskrat 07:04, 30 January 2006 (UTC)

  • Delete, vanity band. --Terence Ong (恭喜发财) 10:32, 30 January 2006 (UTC)
  • Keep. Read the article carefully; it's not a band, it's a record label, and it's a record label that sports quite a few artists notable enough to have Wikipedia entries. StarryEyes 10:45, 30 January 2006 (UTC)
  • Speedy keep per the difference between a music label and a band. Falls under WP:CORP not WP:MUSIC. - Turnstep 15:03, 30 January 2006 (UTC)
  • Keep -- appears notable. Odd that the official website for the label appears to focus on just one band, which is not even listed in the WP article -- but I found plenty of references to the label. Powers 15:21, 30 January 2006 (UTC)
  • Keep -- Powers, Roper is listed on the Wiki article as the only band currently signed to them. Thus why they only feature Roper on the site. STAREYe 18:38, 30 January 2006 (UTC)
  • Keep - as others have noted this is an independent record label, not a band and therefore doesn't fall under WP:Music. Dan, the CowMan 20:52, 30 January 2006 (UTC)
  • Speedy keep, per Turnstep. Adrian Lamo · (talk) · (mail) · 01:53, 31 January 2006 (UTC)
  • Keep warpozio 10:44, 31 January 2006 (UTC)
