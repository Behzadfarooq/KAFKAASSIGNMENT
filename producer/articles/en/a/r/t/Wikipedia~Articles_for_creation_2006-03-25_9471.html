[edit] Pure Luck

The 1991 odd couple comedy "Pure Luck" stars Martin Short as super-putz Eugene Proctor and Danny Glover as mondo coolio Raymond Campanella. They set off as a pair of mismatched investigators looking for the daughter of business tycoon Mr. Highsmith who seems to have fallen off the face of the planet. A psychologist's hypothesis of Proctor's bad luck assumes that he will be able to lead them straight to Valerie Highsmith (Sheila Kelley) as a result of both Valerie's and Eugene's existence in some cosmological vortex of bad luck. Proctor is led on to believe that he is in charge of the mission and that tough-guy Campanella is his assitant, a role that Campanella reluctantly accepts. As they are led on a wild goose chase as a result of Proctor's bad luck, they are inadvertently led closer and closer to Highsmith's whereabouts, all the while grinding down Proctor and Campanella's already shakey relationship. Finally, while overlooking a Mexican precipice, Proctor and Glover have a fall-out and Proctor attacks his friend. Hilarity ensues and in the process Proctor receives a concussion. The film ends as Campanella carries his skinny friend to a local Mexican hospital to treat said concussion. And it just so happens that Valerie Highsmith is also being treated for a similar concussion at the same hospital.

This film is sure to bring laughs, joy and perhaps even a tear. It courageous display of loyalty, trust and dimwitted relationships speaks volumes to us today. The development of unity between Proctor and Campanella's relationship echoes into soft undertones towards racial unity. We hear loudly director Nadia Tass's call for peace, patience, penitence.

[edit] Social representation theory

Social representation theory is a leading theory in modern social psychology. Originally proposed by French social psychologist Serge Moscovici, the theory is purposefully situated between social psychology and sociology to model human social thought and action.

Unfortunately, the majority of Moscovici's work has been written in the French language and therefore is not as readily accessible to mainstream social psychologists in the United States. As a result, social representations theory thrives more in Europen, though it is gaining more popularity within the United States.

[edit] E164

E164 refers to the numbering plan for telephones around the world. It is e164 that says Canada and USA starts with the code 1 etc...

With the growing popularity of VoIP several countries are now putting all their telephone numbers in DNS systems. Which would allow a DNS lookup of a given telephone number. The official DNS root for this effort is e164.arpa, but e164.org also exists which is meant to provide the same information for free.

e164.org stores the information in DNS and a user wanting to query the database might do a DNS lookup for 0.0.1.2.5.5.5.6.1.4.1.e164.org. This would provide, if available, the calling information for 1-416-555-2100. E164.org will return a URL such as a SIP url which would allow the querying user to directly connect to the queried user without passing via the PSTN.

There are currently several countries who are participating with e164.arpa. The united states is to be testing the deployment of the system this year.

A further glimpse down the high street shows the gangster origins of this now dusty, disinhabited slum. For example, the rich palace that is King's Snooker club flaunts its trade to the many passers by.

Meanwhile in the school itself, Onyubis and his gang are often found making daisy chains with old man warren in room 13 - A real rough area of the school.
