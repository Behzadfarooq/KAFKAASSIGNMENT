Arthur D. Healey

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Arthur Daniel Healey (1889-1948) of Somerville, Massachusetts was a U.S. Representative from Massachusetts from 1933, until he resigned in 1942. He was a Democrat. He was also a Judge of U.S. District Court for Massachusetts, from 1942-1948. He died whilst serving in this office. The Arther D. Healey School in Summerville is a public school which was named after him.
