[edit] Amiot (car manufacturer)

Amiot (car manufacturer) (edit|talk|history|links|watch|logs)

"Amiot-Peneau", "-wikipedia", and "-answers" (as in, "answers.com") only turns up 36 Google hits, all of which appear to be either Wikipedia clones of this article or Wikipedia clones of other Amiot car articles or of articles that have to do with anything ("analytical chemistry"?!). Please note: article was renamed from Amiot to Amiot (car manufacturer) during AfD. The subject of this AfD is Amiot (car manufacturer). Angus McLellan (Talk) 22:23, 9 December 2006 (UTC)
  • Delete, no reliable sources to show notablility; fails Wikipedia:Verifiability.Inner Earth 14:56, 8 December 2006 (UTC)
  • Delete few ghits is a telling sign, no reliable resources cited.¤~Persian Poet Gal (talk) 21:14, 8 December 2006 (UTC)
  • Keep as a dab page (rewritten), we have a few Amiot-thingies to disambiguate, and potentially many more based on articles at de.wiki and fr.wiki, as well as missing Dictionary of Canadian Biography subjects. Angus McLellan (Talk) 19:19, 9 December 2006 (UTC)
    • Since this is no longer about the dab page I made, delete. I had a careful look for anything resembling non-trivial information. I appreciate that there may be a little bit more info in French vintage car magazines and books, but the fact that there is no information on any fansites, whether you search for Amiot-Peneau or Amiot-et-Peneau (the actual name of the company), is pretty telling. The best known French motor industry company called Amiot appears to have been a coachbuilder in Dinan (when I found mentions of carrosserie Amiot, I assumed it was a sideline by the aircraft company, but no). There are no end of French vehicle-builder stubs that could be written (Latil, Laffly, La Licorne, Labourier, just for "L"). This shouldn't be one of them. Fails WP:V. Angus McLellan (Talk) 22:20, 9 December 2006 (UTC)
