[edit] Sanja Nikolić

Sanja Nikolić (edit|talk|history|links|watch|logs) – (View log)

A stub about someone who fainted on TV. That's it. Since when is everything on Youtube automatcally encyclopedic? Pointless. R. fiend 21:26, 10 March 2007 (UTC)

  • Delete No real notability established. --Tainter 21:43, 10 March 2007 (UTC)
  • keep and due rewrite She is clearly more notable than some of these other people on the list, but article is horribly written.--Sefringle 03:06, 13 March 2007 (UTC)
 Relisted to generate a more thorough discussion so that consensus may be reached
 Please add new comments below this notice. Thanks, Quarl (talk) 12:06, 14 March 2007 (UTC)
  • Merge with list of Internet phenomena and cite sources such as when the incident took place.--h i s s p a c e r e s e a r c h 16:00, 14 March 2007 (UTC)
  • Delete. She appears to be one of minor announcers on a (locally major) television; she's not even listed on their own list of "TV faces", i.e. major journalists. Her sole claim to notability is the accident with fainting, and almost all of (~234) Ghits (for "Sanja Nikolić" Pink) I see refer only to the accident. This article is actually an offshoot of List of Internet phenomena, and it used to be listed there; it should be treated in such context (i.e. cruft); Merge there is also an option.
    And, btw, the images are falsely claimed as PD-self; they're schreenshots, i.e. fair use at best. Duja► 16:06, 14 March 2007 (UTC)
  • Delete fails WP:BIO. --MaNeMeBasat 05:42, 16 March 2007 (UTC)
  • Delete. In a week nobody's rewritten this. Absolutely does not meet WP:BIO. Angus McLellan (Talk) 20:25, 19 March 2007 (UTC)
  • Delete without prejudice to proper recreation (if possible). This person may or may not meet WP:BIO. I am inclined to think that she doesn't because her name gets no mentions in a news search. So perhaps the entire episode is limited to publicity on YouTube. If that is the case, then proper recreation is not possible in any case. -- Black Falcon 22:33, 19 March 2007 (UTC)
