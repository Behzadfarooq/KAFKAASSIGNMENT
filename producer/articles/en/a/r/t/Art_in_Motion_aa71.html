Art in Motion

Art In Motion is a privately held publisher of fine art reproductions and a manufacturer of decorative home decor products sold worldwide. The company employs 750 people who work at its 130,000 square-foot facility in Coquitlam's Pacific Reach Business Park, in Canada.

In 2002, the company increased the size of its Coquitlam manufacturing plant by some 38 per cent.
