[edit] Nazism in Russia

  • del An unreferenced rant on the topics already started to be covered in Fascism in Russia and Racism in Russia. The term Nazism is well-defined and refers to Nazi Germany. Its usage in other contexts is a mere political cliche. At best, it could be Neo-Nazism in Russia, but there is nothing "neo". mikka (t) 08:42, 10 November 2005 (UTC)
  • Merge with Fascism in Russia. Capitalistroadster 09:03, 10 November 2005 (UTC)
  • Strong delete. There is as much Nazism in the country that destroyed Hitler as there is anti-Semitism in Israel. Another option is redirect to Fascism in Russia, which should be listed for deletion in its turn. --Ghirlandajo 09:12, 10 November 2005 (UTC)
  • Delete on the grounds that the name incorrectly, implies that the original Nazism involved Russia, and on the grounds of redundancy. A1kmm 09:14, 10 November 2005 (UTC)
  • I am personally for Merge to Fascism in Russia, although the name is indeed ambigous, it is not clear from the name is it refer to Nazi occupation of Russia durin world war II or to the National-Socialist movements in modern Russia. It happens to be the second abakharev 09:18, 10 November 2005 (UTC)
    • Not much of the salvagable content IMHO, but the redirect might be useful abakharev 23:04, 13 November 2005 (UTC)
  • Merge With Fascism in Russia, an interesting subject about Russian political climate.--Molobo 11:57, 10 November 2005 (UTC)
  • Delete, for it looks like user's personal thoughts on this matter (if there is one). KNewman 12:06, 10 November 2005 (UTC)
  • Delete as original research and POV. Just zis Guy, you know? [T]/[C] :: AfD? 12:34, 10 November 2005 (UTC)
  • Delete, poorly written, non-sourced, non-NPOV and the topics in the article are covered better elsewhere on Wikipedia. Keresaspa 14:50, 10 November 2005 (UTC)
  • Delete Fisenko 18:00, 10 November 2005 (UTC)
  • Delete misleading title. Consider merging/improving quality of Fascism in Russia, Racism in Russia, Neo-Nazism #Neo-Nazism in Russia. ←Humus sapiens←ну? 21:05, 10 November 2005 (UTC)
  • Merge to Fascism in Russia. The article is riddled with factual errors (Russian nazis DO adore Hitler like any other nazis) but that's not the reason to delete.  Grue  22:54, 13 November 2005 (UTC)
  • Delete. POV. *drew 01:14, 16 November 2005 (UTC)
