Young Choppa (Born Myron Howard on April 20, 1992) is an American rapper . He was born in Social Circle, Georgia

[edit] Biography

Born in Social Circle, Georgia Howard is the last of three children. Young Choppa's mother tried to keep im in the church where she ministered at, but the street life drifted him and his brother away from it. While Choppa at the age of 14 and his brother at the age of 15, lived in the southside of Monroe in Georgia they ran with a little gang that called their self T.O.P(named oringinated from the apartment complex they lived in, Tall Oaks Players was the whole name). After Being kicked out of the apartment (both choppa and brother cussed out the Rent Lady), the family moved to an even bad part of Monroe called Burk Street.

[edit] Music

Choppa is apart of the unsigned group Gutta-Squad. They are now working on their first CD that will be released sometime in 2007. Choppa first became notcied for his hot lines and freestyles that he spits in a battle. He has one song that he did by himself that has not been herd yet.
