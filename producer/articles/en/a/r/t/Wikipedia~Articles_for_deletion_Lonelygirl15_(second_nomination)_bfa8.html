[edit] Lonelygirl15

A DRV consensus (heavily infested with spammers, but they were ignored) overturned the previous AfD on this subject in light of new evidence (including a New York Times article, published since the last AfD.) Please consult the DRV for the citations to new evidence. The version here is the most recent sourced version in the article history -- other, better versions may be in there for those who wish to check. This matter is resubmitted to AfD for fresh consideration. This is a procedural nomination, so I abstain. Xoloz 15:47, 31 August 2006 (UTC)
