The result was speedy keep since this discussion has zero chance of resulting in deletion if prolonged. As Daniel Olsen points out, towns such as this will, by their very natures, be the subjects of multiple, independent, non-trivial published works, and thus satisfy the primary notability criterion. The article itself at the time of nomination cited one of them (a census report) and now cites two more (a community history, and a publication by Tanadgusix Corporation), thanks to TruthbringerToronto. (Good work.) If you see a town article with only a census citation, follow the example that TruthbringerToronto set here, find more published works about the town, and improve the article.
