  • Keep Merge to Butch and Femme along with lipstick lesbian per systematic bias. Relevant and notable term in the GLBT community. I have no doubt that these are good faith nominations and delete votes but the appearance here of such well known and actualized concepts of a sizable minority seems to scream "Systematic Bias". If properly expanded and source, these articles provide a considerable service to Wikipedia in shedding light on an area of knowledge that most in a straight-dominated world do not see.Agne 17:22, 8 August 2006 (UTC)
