Categories of artillery by calibre. This allows a researcher to find a weapon without knowing what type of artillery it is, as well as see different uses for certain calibres as used for artillery. Note that each category will include weapons that are in that general size class. Weapons of similar caliber may differ in exact caliber (i.e. 76 mm and 76.2 mm will both be under 76 mm artillery). Non metric calibers are placed within the nearest calculated metric category.
