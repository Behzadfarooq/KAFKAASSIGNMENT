[edit] You're a looza, if you don't know what a palooza is...

Personally, I don't see the harm in keeping the article. With the inevitable release of this film from the box office, many people will wonder what the word actually means. Maybe after someone actually informs the author of the definition of Palooza then it could possibly be deleted but I still feel that it would be a tremendous loss to the wikipedia site as, that's what it's here for. —The preceding unsigned comment was added by 82.110.217.226 (talk • contribs) September 28, 2005.
