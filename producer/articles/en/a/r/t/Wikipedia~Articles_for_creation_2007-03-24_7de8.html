[edit] Faceless Joker

Facless Joker are a Punk/Rock/Metal style band from the East Anglian town of Milton Keynes in Buckinghamshire whom formed in 2005 under the name Modern Outrage. They are a relatively small band that have played a few shows amassing to over 200 people in and around their hometown of Milton Keynes. Originally known as Modern Outrage, the band consisted of Lead Guitarist Andrew Brimson, Bassist James Reeve, Drummer Ben Glassock and then-Singer/Guitarist Siobhian Palmer. The band later parted ways with Siobhian and later changed the style in which they played, from Pop-Punk to more Melodic Punk/Rock/Metal. The band later recruited David Foster as their vocalist and have been working with him ever since. www.myspace.com/facelessjoker[1] is the current website that they use to advertise themselves, their top 4 friends are in fact the four band members (David, Andrew, James then Ben).

[edit] Early Days

Faceless Joker started out in January 2006 as their first band practice and created the song 'Intro' in that first day. The band later entered a talent show and had finished Second after playing Intro, losing out to a girl by the name of Siobhian Palmer. Siobhian joined the band 6 months later and the band finished Intro, I'm Sorry and Wishing I Could Tell You with Siobhian and later recorded the three songs. The band released an EP under the name Modern Outrage which they named 'Intro' as Intro was Track 1 and was also the very first track the band ever created, bringing them a larger fanbase after coing 2nd in a local talent show. The band released one copy of 'Intro' under the record Label Zucchini Records, but then pulled the plug on the EP as later they parted ways Siobhian and Zucchini and changed their style and name.

[edit] 2007

2007 was a new era for the band, changing their name to Identity Parade which was found to be an unsuccessful name with their fans they needed a new singer and a better name, the band day after day came up with various names, all of them were rejected, they eventually decided on Faceless Joker after they had appointed David Foster to vocals and rhythm guitar. Their drummer Ben is accredited with creating the name and the name was advertised on myspace via a bulletin sent out by Ben. In March 2007, James decided that the site needed re-forming, he took it upon himself to design the myspace that they currently have, changing the url and the background, also including the logo which David had created for them as their main picture. The band continued to work on the songs they have been creating, and are due to record their Demo around April/May 2007. They are currently in talks with One Of These Days Records, an Indepedant Label based in London, talks appear to be going well.

William E. Conway, Jr. is a Founding Partner and Managing Director of The Carlyle Group, a global private equity firm based in Washington, DC. He serves as chairman of Carlyle's investment committees and is based in Washington, DC.

Prior to forming Carlyle in 1987, Mr. Conway spent three years as the Senior Vice President and Chief Financial Officer of MCI Communications Corporation. Mr. Conway was a Vice President and Treasurer of MCI from 1981 to 1984. While at MCI, Mr. Conway arranged several billion dollars of debt and equity financing, both in the public and private financial markets, and negotiated MCI's most significant acquisitions and divestitures. Prior to joining MCI, Mr. Conway served in a variety of positions for almost ten years with The First National Bank of Chicago in the areas of corporate finance, commercial lending, workout loans and general management.

Mr. Conway is a graduate of Dartmouth College and the University of Chicago Graduate School of Business.

Mr. Conway is former Co-Lead Independent Director of the Board of Directors of Sprint Nextel Corporation, former Chairman of the Board of United Defense Industries, and former Chairman of Nextel Communications, Inc.

Mr. Conway is a board member and active supporter of various local charities including the National Capital Poison Center.

[edit] Pliers (singer)

Pliers is a Jamaican singer born Everton Bonner in Rockhall Hills, Jamaica on April 4, 1963. He was most famous for his duets with fellow Jamaican singer and DJ Chaka Demus. Their hits together included "Murder she Wrote", Tease Me, "She Don't Let Nobody" and "Twist And Shout".

[edit] Sage Salzer

Sage Salzer (born 1976) is a top plus-size model who has appeared in publications including Oprah Magazine, Real Simple, Good Housekeeping, Figure, and Grace magazine.

She has been featured in plus-size advertisements and catalogues of eminent clothing retailers such as Eddie Bauer, Lands' End, Levi Strauss, Nordstroms, Macy's, Old Navy, Playtext, JC Penney's, Tommy Hilfiger, Target and currently appears in the television and print campaigns for Slim Fast and Fruit of the Loom underwear.

A native of Ventura, California, Sage started modeling at the age of fourteen when she won a modeling contest on Good Day Los Angeles as a size 6 model, which included a trip to Paris and a seven year contract with a top modeling agency. During that year, she spent a summer modeling in Tokyo, Japan, and landed several spreads, including a cover, in Seventeen magazine.

Sage found it challenging to keep her weight low enough to pursue straight-size modeling as a career and subsequently shifted her attention to plus-size modeling. Currently, she is a proud advocate of healthy lifestyle choices for women and has been proactive in helping plus-size models break into mainstream print and broadcast media.

She is represented by the top modeling agencies aound the world and currently resides in New York City.
