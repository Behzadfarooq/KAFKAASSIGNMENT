[edit] Harmonics Theory

A previous version of this article and 6 related articles were nominated for deletion on 18 May 2005. (Wikipedia:Votes for deletion/Harmonics Theory). After much reflection, I concluded that the community concensus was to delete the current versions. During the discussion period, two sandbox versions were created. In the previous vote 4 people explicitly endorsed the first sandbox version, however, many of the objections raised about the original article could be said to apply equally to the sandbox version. Concensus on whether Wikipedia should have any article on this topic remained unclear. This is a procedural nomination specifically on the sandbox version(s). I abstain.
