[edit] Environmental Transformation of North America Prior to European Colonization

Environmental Transformation of North America Prior to European Colonization (edit|talk|history|links|watch|logs) – (View log)

see WP:NOR. Seems like an essay. Fisherjs 21:35, 18 March 2007 (UTC)

  • Delete Seems very much like an essay. May quote citations, but they are not easily verified, and besides, the conclusion section reads like original research. Bubba hotep 21:44, 18 March 2007 (UTC)
  • delete per WP:NOR. well written and researched essay that would be excellent for an environmental studies class (at least, in briefly scanning it over) but entirely unencyclopedic. Niffweed17, Destroyer of Chickens 00:11, 19 March 2007 (UTC)
  • Delete A well written persuasive essay would support keeping if some serious work was done to it to make it more encyclopedic —The preceding unsigned comment was added by Irate velociraptor (talk • contribs) 05:12, 20 March 2007 (UTC).

  • Delete unless rewritten with sources to become a non-essay. Looks like someone's research paper. Resurgent insurgent 13:50, 22 March 2007 (UTC)
