[edit] San Jose Las Flores

San Jose Las Flores (edit|talk|history|links|watch|logs) – (View log)

This article about a "city" has absolutely no information at all. It is unclear how important it is, as it could be the most insignificant little village in El Salvador. Delete! R.smithson 21:35, 20 March 2007 (UTC)

Keep Prior consensus has established that named population centers are notable by default. I have no doubt that there are many source regarding this city. No reason to delete the stub it will grow some day. --Daniel J. Leivick 21:54, 20 March 2007 (UTC)
  • Speedy keep. The subject has various ghits, and I found a few websites [1][2][3] detailing its history, although they're in Spanish. The place is notable, as it appears its origins are linked to past conflicts and wars in El Salvador. - Mtmelendez (TALK|UB|HOME) 22:16, 20 March 2007 (UTC)

  • keep as cites automatically notable. ask nominator to do at least the minimum ammount of checking in future.  ⇒ bsnowball  08:43, 21 March 2007 (UTC)

  • Keep. Any town, no matter how small is notable. There are tons of tiny villages in the USA that have articles! 11kowrom 16:14, 24 March 2007 (UTC)
