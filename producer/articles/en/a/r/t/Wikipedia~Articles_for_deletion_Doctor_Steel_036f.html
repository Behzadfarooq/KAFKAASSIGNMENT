Doctor Steel

Doctor Steel (edit|talk|history|links|watch|logs) – (View log)

At any glance, this article is just a major fair use violation. With a partial read, it's impossible to tell if this is about a real individual and his band or a fictional individual used as part of an advertising campaign. Seeing as this does not really fall under any sort of speedy deletion, I'm listing this here as not having neutral verifiable sources as to the actual existence of this individual/band/copyright gallery. The talk page also raises some questions.—Ryūlóng (竜龍) 22:23, 8 February 2007 (UTC)
