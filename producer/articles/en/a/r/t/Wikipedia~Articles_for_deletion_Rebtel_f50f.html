[edit] Rebtel

Rebtel (edit|talk|history|links|watch|logs) – (View log)

Procedural listing. An anon user, 158.143.215.204 (talk • contribs) has been posting messages on this page suggesting it may be primarily intended to promote this company, and from a cursory examination, it may fail notability guidelines. Listing here to get a broader consensus. —dgiestc 18:17, 28 March 2007 (UTC)

  • Weak Delete - reads like advertising. Also, most of the links are to sites affiliated with the company, so no evidence of multiple non-trivial coverage in independent sources to establish notability per WP:CORP. Delete unless appropriate sources are added by the end of this AfD. Walton Vivat Regina! 18:35, 28 March 2007 (UTC)
  • Keep. Could use some more information, but it has been mentioned by USA Today and Reuters UK. flod logic 18:42, 28 March 2007 (UTC)
  • Delete In spite of the USA today article. This is the attempt of an individual company to hijack VOIP and its derivatives, a much more general technology, discussed in dozens of articles, by calling it be its own name. I see no evidence of more general use. DGG 03:46, 29 March 2007 (UTC)
