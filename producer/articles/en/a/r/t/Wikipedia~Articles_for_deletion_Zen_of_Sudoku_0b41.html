  • Delete. WP:SOFTWARE is only a proposed guideline that is currently far from consensus, so it is not fair to cite it as a deletion rationale. However, the current text of this page is not verifiable from reliable sources, and most definitely fails WP:OR and WP:NPOV ("sets the player in a beautiful, relaxing, and comforting environment"). It possibly also fails WP:ADS, although I don't see any proof that Foughtfoo (talk • contribs) is one of the authors of this software. The web-site for the software links to this WP article, which is suspicious. If the article is kept for some reason, it needs significant cleanup. — Kaustuv Chaudhuri 20:37, 13 July 2006 (UTC)
