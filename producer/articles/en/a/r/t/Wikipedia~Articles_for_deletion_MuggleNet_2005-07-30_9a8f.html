[edit] MuggleNet

[edit] Update on 29 Jul 2005 by User:Markl222

Somehow, a link to this VFD got posted on COSForums.com (official MuggleNet forums), so that's why the votes are so one-sided. Something should be done about this. Note the number of anonymous voters and the people who registered just to "save" a worthless article from deletion. This is pathetic.

- User:Markl222 19:57, 29 Jul 2005 (UTC)

comment, not a vote It still has won the vote though. You must accept that. And it has changed greatly for the better. Your bias is greatly unfair. Please accept the vote result. - Jamandell (d69)

Well, I'd recommend, for purposes of validity, not counting all anonymous votes as well as all votes from registered members whose only edit is this page. - User:Markl222 19:57, 29 Jul 2005 (UTC)

A popular site among Harry Potter fans, but not notable enough to merit an article. Contains little content besides the JKR quote and award. Plus, other sites (most notably Harry Potter Lexicon) have also received awards directly from JKR, but do not have their own articles. (The HP-Lexicon link redirects to Harry Potter.)

On June 8, 2005, I put the MuggleNet article up on Votes for Deletion. It received three votes of Keep, due to the article's potential for expansion. Now, as of July 17, 2005, the article has seen no expansion whatsoever; and, as a result, I have relisted the article up on Votes for Deletion.

An alternative to deleting the article entirely is redirecting to Harry Potter Fandom #Unofficial_sites, and moving the information about Mugglenet to that section (as I have already done)

- User:Markl222 22:44, 17 Jul 2005 (UTC)
