[edit] Budchievement

Budchievement (edit|talk|history|links|watch|logs)

This is ridiculous; it's probably something some kid thought up at school. I highly doubt Budchievement is a word. Split Infinity (talk) 23:40, 17 December 2006 (UTC)

  • Speedy Delete No assertion of notability, unsourced neologism. Oh, and Wikipedia isn't Urban Dictionary. —ShadowHalo 23:48, 17 December 2006 (UTC)
  • De... lete... it Danny Lilithborne 00:15, 18 December 2006 (UTC)
  • Speedy Delete. Probably some college student trying to be funny. PullToOpen 01:10, 18 December 2006 (UTC)
  • Speedy Delete No claim of notability. Mytildebang 01:45, 18 December 2006 (UTC)
  • Speedy Delete. Prob qualifies as nonesense. Definitely no notability claim. - WJBscribe (WJB talk) 03:03, 18 December 2006 (UTC)
  • Delete: Note: No claim of notability isn't a criterion for speedy unless it's about a person, group or website. I don't think this article technically qualifies for speedy (it's garbage, but not really patent nonsense), but it should definitely be deleted. Heimstern Läufer 06:47, 18 December 2006 (UTC)
  • Delete per WP:NFT and WP:NEO. Ohconfucius 07:40, 18 December 2006 (UTC)
  • Speedy Delete per nom. Anomo 12:16, 19 December 2006 (UTC)
  • Buds Of course it's not a word, but that doesn't mean it won't one day be. We can be trendsetters here, people...think about it.

When you're old and you hear some young kids having a conversation along the lines of:

Young Kid 1 - "Got my first Budchievement last week." Young Kid 2 - "That's great man...congrats! How was it?" Young Kid 1 - "I can't remember." Young Kid 2 - "Ahhhh, that really is a Budchievement!"

you can have a silent chuckle thinking about how you helped that to happen.

Save Budchievement, save the world.

  • Willie East Side - this deserves a chance, as mentioned above it is not patent nonsense. Perhaps its progentors would care to provide some more substantiation, examples, etc.
