[edit] UCLA Taiwanese American Union

I'm not sure about this page, but I thought I would put it up for debate. It's just a campus club, and it doesn't seem notable enough for inclusion in an encyclopedia. It's also partly self-promotional: see the slippage into the first person ("our") in the middle section. I vote to delete. Emiao 08:28, 18 May 2005 (UTC)

  • If you do a search, you'll find quite a number of student organizations out there. In addition, you'll find student organization itself, and this would be a good example of one. As for the self-promotional slip, that was an accident; it was copied and pasted. I vote to keep. Cgkm 08:48, 18 May 2005 (UTC)
