Artrosis

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Magdalena Stupkiewicz-Dobosz

Artrosis is a Polish gothic-doom band founded in 1995 in Zielona Góra.

[edit] Band members

[edit] Current members

  • Magdalena Stupkiewicz-Dobosz "Medeah" – vocals (1995-)
  • Krystian Kozerawski "MacKozer" – guitar (Sacriversum) (2002-)
  • Remigiusz Mielczarek "Remo" – bass guitar (Sacriversum) (2002-)
  • Łukasz Migdalski "Migdał" – keyboards (2005-)
  • Konrad Biczak "Lombardo" – drums (2005-)

[edit] Former members

  • Maciej Niedzielski – keyboards (1995-2005)
  • Mariusz Kuszewski "Mario" (Sacriversum) – guitar (2003-2004)
  • Rafał Grunt „Grunthell” – guitar (1999-2002)
  • Marcin Pendowski – bass guitar (1998-2001)

[edit] Guests members

  • Zbigniew Robert Promiński "Inferno" – drums on (re-release) „Ukryty Wymiar” and "W Imie Nocy"
  • Magdalena Kogut – violin on (re-release) „Ukryty Wymiar”
  • Magdalena Stelmaszyk – violin on „Ukryty Wymiar”
  • Paweł Słoniowski – bass guitar on „Ukryty Wymiar”
