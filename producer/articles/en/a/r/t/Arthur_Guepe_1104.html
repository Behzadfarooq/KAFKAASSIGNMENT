Arthur Guepe

Arthur Guepe was the head coach of the Virginia Cavaliers football program from 1946 to 1952. He is one of the best coaches in school history, having compiled a 47-17-2(.727) record. His final 3 seasons were his best, wining 8 games in those 3 seasons. He was a quarterback for Vanderbilt. He is also noted for serving as the Ohio Valley Conference's first commissioner from 1963 to 1975.
