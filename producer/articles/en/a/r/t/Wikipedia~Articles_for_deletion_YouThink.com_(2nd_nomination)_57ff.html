[edit] YouThink.com (2nd nomination)

YouThink.com (edit|talk|history|links|watch|logs) – (View log)
  • Discussion at Wikipedia:Articles for deletion/YouThink.com. (first nomination)

Bump from speedy. No opinion. —Quarl (talk) 2007-02-17 20:27Z

  • Delete 16 Google hits, utterly fails WP:WEB. Result of previous AfD should qualify it for speedy deletion under G4.--RWR8189 20:39, 17 February 2007 (UTC)
    Doesn't qualify - different content, and subject may have gained notability since the previous AFD (it's been over a year). —Quarl (talk) 2007-02-17 20:49Z
What does "google hits" mean? Just looking at it, I'd think that would mean when you search "youthink" or "youthink.com", which both are more than a half of a million. (I am really asking) EgyptianSushi 05:48, 18 February 2007 (UTC)
  • Delete apparently no references in reliable sources. Re. ghits: if you search for 'link:youthink.com' you get the sites that link to it. Ignoring those from 'i-am-bored.com', which apparently has links to every entry in one of the sections, there aren't many. JulesH 10:11, 18 February 2007 (UTC)
  • delete notability not established.--Sefringle 05:09, 19 February 2007 (UTC)
  • Delete Although I use to go on this site, I agree with the fact that it is not notable enough for an article. MichaelGriffin 20:14, 20 February 2007 (UTC)
  • Keep As one of the main contributors to this article, I find it frustrating to receive a speedy delete nomination more than a year after the original AFD. I-Am-Bored.com, a mirror of Youthink.com has an Alexa rank of around 4000. Does WP:WEB require Alexa rankings of 5,000? If so, Youthink should be allowed to stay. Furthermore, Youthink quizzes are found on many MySpaces, Livejournals, and Xangas.Electricbassguy 22:23, 20 February 2007 (UTC)
  • Keep I agree with electricbassguy - it seems to me to have a good google and alexa ranking and youthink quizzes can be seen elsewhere on the internet. Thedreamdied 00:17, 21 February 2007 (UTC)
  • Comment/Information The phrase "Brought to you by YouThink.com quizzes and personality tests." returns an estimated 166,000 Google Hits. This wouldn't be seen in Link:Youthink.com because it does not link to YouThink. Electricbassguy 02:40, 21 February 2007 (UTC)
