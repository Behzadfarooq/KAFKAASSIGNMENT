[edit] Huckle buckle beanstalk

Huckle buckle beanstalk (edit|talk|history|links|watch|logs)

Seems to have been made up one day. Non-notable children's game. Contested prod. MER-C 06:37, 2 January 2007 (UTC)

  • Why exactly is this page being considered for deletion? A simple google search will prove it as a well known childhood game. —The preceding unsigned comment was added by EABair (talk • contribs).
  • Alright, I've added a pop-culture reference to suit your needs. EABair 06:53, 2 January 2007 (UTC)
  • Keep. If it was made up one day, that day was at least 50 years ago, and I suspect it is much older. Ideally, this article would be able to tell us just how old this game is.--OinkOink 15:19, 2 January 2007 (UTC)
  • Keep. This appears to meet notability requirements. Note that the game is described in dozens of books, according to a quick Google books search. schi talk 20:21, 2 January 2007 (UTC)
  • Keep and merge Hunt the Thimble with it. Games like this often have a long history and may be discussed by folklorists, anthropologists etc. (I've added a bit.) --HJMG 23:30, 3 January 2007 (UTC)
  • Keep don't see the need necessarily to merge, this meets WP:N as a widespread, relatively well known children's game. SkierRMH,00:24, 8 January 2007 (UTC)
