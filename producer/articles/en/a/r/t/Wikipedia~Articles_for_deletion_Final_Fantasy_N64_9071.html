  • Redirect. The entire thing is a poorly researched bastardization of content already present at Final Fantasy SGI demo: not only does it duplicate information present in the aforementioned article, but it presents fannish speculation as fact. To wit, it is a technical demo, not a "game that was once to be released, but was not," and was never tied to the Nintendo 64. The only connection it had to that console is that the SGI workstations used to produce it had some technical similarities to the N64 hardware. Any statement beyond that, particularly without references to support it, is speculation. – Seancdaug 19:56, 4 October 2005 (UTC)
