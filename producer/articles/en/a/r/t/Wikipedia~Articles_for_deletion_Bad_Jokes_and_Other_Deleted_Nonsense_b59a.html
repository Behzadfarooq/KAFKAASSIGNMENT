[edit] Wikipedia:Bad Jokes and Other Deleted Nonsense

Bad jokes? Deleted nonsense? This has no place on a serious project like Wikipedia. I will explain why in this six part seminar:

  1. Bad. We don't keep bad articles here. Note that Bad Axe, Michigan and Strong Bad should also be deleted.
  2. Jokes. Again, if we had an article on every joke ever made, we'd have 2 million articles. Delete The Aristocrats.
  3. And. We write articles on singular subjects, thank you. Requesting move of United States to fifty iterations of United State.
  4. Other. Again, if this is about other things, then make the article about them.
  5. Deleted. WE DELETE THINGS SO WE DON'T SEE THEM. Which is why this is brought here!
  6. Nonsense. We don't tolerate nonsense here on Wikipedia: Serious Business.

Thank you for your time. Golbez 07:35, 1 April 2006 (UTC)
