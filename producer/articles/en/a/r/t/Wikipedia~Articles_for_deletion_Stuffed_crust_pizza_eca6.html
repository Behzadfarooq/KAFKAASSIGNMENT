The result of the debate was 3 for delete, 6 for merge and direct, 4 Keep, and 1 transwiki. That qualifies for No Consensus. Redwolf24 02:30, 4 August 2005 (UTC)

[edit] Stuffed crust pizza

Trivial. Should be merged with pizza. 67.174.230.30 22:32, 13 July 2005 (UTC)

  • Delete (changed from Merge) -- A single dish from a single franchise (and a dish which may be seen as a "fad" dish nonetheless) shouldn't deserve its own article User:24.9.10.235
  • Merge -- anything useful into pizza and redirect. - Longhair | Talk 12:21, 28 July 2005 (UTC)
  • Merge -- As per request --RN 13:30, 28 July 2005 (UTC)
  • Merge, but VfD is not the place to discuss this. - ulayiti (talk) 17:32, 28 July 2005 (UTC)
  • Comment This is vote for deletion, not vote for merge. If you want to merge something, be bold! But it's not the domain of VfD. --malathion talk 17:39, 28 July 2005 (UTC)
    • Actually, we're directed not to merge articles while a VfD is going on... ^^; Almafeta 18:30, 28 July 2005 (UTC)
  • Delete. In its current form, there is pretty much nothing to merge. Unless this is expanded to talk about how stuffed-crust pizza became a fad that was copied by every pizza chain under the sun, then I'll change my vote to keep. Almafeta 18:30, 28 July 2005 (UTC)
    • Keep The fact the Wikipedia articles don't have to be good to start out with is one of the fundamental reasons why Wikipedia has been a success. CalJW 15:57, 29 July 2005 (UTC)
  • Keep, an adequately notable pizza product in its own right jamesgibbon 18:32, 28 July 2005 (UTC)
  • Delete. Factually incorrect: It's not just Pizza Hut that's making stuffed-crust pizzas, it's every pizza chain out there, and most brands of store-bought frozen pizza. --Carnildo 21:18, 28 July 2005 (UTC)
  • Merge and redirect, stuffed cruft. (Awww, quit your heckling!) the wub "?/!" 08:33, 29 July 2005 (UTC)
  • Transwiki to Wiktionary. Peter Grey 09:53, 29 July 2005 (UTC)
  • Merge and redirect with pizza. This stuff is awesome, but pure awesomeity does not a good Wikipedia article make. Lord Bob 21:12, July 29, 2005 (UTC)
  • Merge and redirect with [[pizza]. My ex sells a lot of this stuff at Pizza Hut. Dave Porter 22:18, 30 July 2005 (UTC)
  • Keep. Not much worse than several other entries in the Pizza Category, to which it should be added. William Avery 15:44, 31 July 2005 (UTC)
  • Eh. If it's possible to fill in the article some more (who invented it? what kinds of stuffings are common, besides cheese? is it associated with any other style of pizza?), then keep it. But if this is all that can be said about it, then merge into the main pizza article. Dr.frog 01:20, 1 August 2005 (UTC)
  • Keep, awesome foodstuff. Kappa 16:45, 2 August 2005 (UTC)
