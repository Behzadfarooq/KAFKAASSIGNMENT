[edit] Metro Camel

Clearly Fails the Test of WP:MUSIC. Seems like an Advertisment to me. --Marwatt 13:51, 14 September 2006 (UTC)

  • This AfD nomination was incomplete. It is listed now. DumbBOT 17:51, 14 September 2006 (UTC)
