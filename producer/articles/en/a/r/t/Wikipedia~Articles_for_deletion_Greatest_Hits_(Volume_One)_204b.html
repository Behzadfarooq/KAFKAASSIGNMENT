[edit] Greatest Hits (Volume One)

It seems that we already have articles on the subject: see Greatest Hits (Queen, Elektra), Greatest Hits (Queen, Hollywood) and Greatest Hits (Queen, Parlophone). Also, this shouldn't be changed into a redirect as the name of the group isn't mentioned in the title. The sales information (the primary content of this article) could be included in the proper articles if it proves to be accurate. -KFP 19:36, 16 August 2005 (UTC)

  • Delete per nominator. ManoaChild 21:19, 16 August 2005 (UTC)
    • Redirect per R. fiend, below. ManoaChild 22:13, 16 August 2005 (UTC)
  • Redirect to Greatest Hits (there's a couple "Volume Ones" there), which needs serious work. There must be thousands of "Greatest Hits" albums, I'm not sure they can be contained in one page. -R. fiend 21:22, 16 August 2005 (UTC)
    • Oh, not that it matters for this VfD, but I thought I'd mention that I combined all those Queen albums into one article at Greatest Hits (Queen), which surprisingly didn't exist. Actually it was a move and 2 redirects, as one article covered all three. -R. fiend 21:38, 16 August 2005 (UTC)
      • Did you notice the articles Greatest Hits III (Queen), Greatest Hits, Vol. II (Queen) and Greatest Hits: We Will Rock You Edition? --KFP 04:14, 17 August 2005 (UTC)
        • Eh, this is getting too confusing. Maybe some of them could use separate articles, if they are completely distinct albums. I mainly objected to two separate articles when the second was identical to the first, but with a couple bonus tracks. I have no real interest in Queen, but if I feel like it I'll take a stab sometime at sorting them all out. I think there could be some cleanup due all around. -R. fiend 17:44, 18 August 2005 (UTC)
  • Disambig Kappa 22:08, 16 August 2005 (UTC)
  • Disambig as per Kappa. Alf 15:48, 21 August 2005 (UTC)
