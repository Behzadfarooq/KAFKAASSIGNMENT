  • While there might be merit in having the information on the subject on one page, this is not a suitable stub nor an NPOV title to build upon. Delete. -- Antaeus Feldspar 23:18, 6 September 2005 (UTC)
  • Agree with conclusion. Was originally created to try to deal with conflict, but appears not to be useful. Delete Robert McClenon 23:48, 6 September 2005 (UTC)

  • Delete. This is a classic example of how not to start an article. It lists one 'argument' in favor of complicity, and none against. Er, well, I guess that proves it, then.... TheMadBaron 10:00, 15 September 2005 (UTC)

How would it do that? Wouldn't it be a far greater cause of righteous indignation if a church did stop a political party from coming to power? Assuming there's a surviving legitimate article existing on the topic, it would already contain the only source (Hitler's Pope) cited here, so no need to merge. Delete.
