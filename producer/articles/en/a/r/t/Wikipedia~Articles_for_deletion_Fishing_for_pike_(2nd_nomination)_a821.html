[edit] Fishing for pike

Fishing for pike (edit|talk|history|links|watch|logs)

Delete as nom. Article reads like something which should be on an uneditable enthusist's website or a fishing wiki. Offers no information as to the subject's notability and does not seem to follow the manual of style. Seems to be a perpetual target for spamming. This is simply not the kind of article for a free encyclopedia. If a more appropriate sister project would be a better location, Transwiki. Ace Class Shadow; My talk. 01:25, 29 November 2006 (UTC)

  • Delete. This is original research (since it's not cited well) and is a how to. -- Mikeblas 01:35, 29 November 2006 (UTC)
  • Merge whatever relevant info is left to Pike (fish) after a MAJOR overhaul. Right now it reads like a Field & Stream article, but once all the POV and "how to" garbage is removed, there actually seems to be some decent content. PS: this article has been around since 2002, and this is the best we've got? Wavy G 02:54, 29 November 2006 (UTC)
  • Transwiki or delete as a how-to, which Wikipedia is not. MER-C 04:56, 29 November 2006 (UTC)
  • Delete, Wikipedia is not a how-to guide. JIP | Talk 08:24, 29 November 2006 (UTC)
  • Merge to pike. How to fish it should be in that article, not outside. --Fred McGarry 10:37, 29 November 2006 (UTC)
  • Wikipedia is not a how-to guide. Transwiki to WikiBooks and leave link in Pike. - Mgm|(talk) 11:28, 29 November 2006 (UTC)
  • Delete - merging this kind of stuff would simply disrupt a perfectly good article on pike, which are more than "things that swim around waiting to be caught". --ElaragirlTalk|Count 14:44, 29 November 2006 (UTC)
  • Transwiki to wikibooks. It sounded like wikibooks material the moment I saw the title. Confirmed by the article. Dolive21
  • Delete As per Elaragirl. Khukri (talk . contribs) 15:17, 29 November 2006 (UTC)
  • Delete - the article is a how-to guide. -- Whpq 15:34, 29 November 2006 (UTC)
  • Delete - it's original research and how-to; by no stretch of the imagination encyclopedic. --Orange Mike 16:14, 29 November 2006 (UTC)
  • Delete this article is not fit for an encyclopedia. Nileena joseph (Talk|Contribs) 16:51, 29 November 2006 (UTC)

*Delete per nom, unencyclopedic fishing lesson.--John Lake 18:58, 29 November 2006 (UTC)

  • Transwiki-After consideration I change my vote to Transwiki to WikiBooks.--John Lake 19:13, 1 December 2006 (UTC)
