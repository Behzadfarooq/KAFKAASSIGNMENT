Life Dynamics is important enough for Planned Parenthood to list it on its "Profiles of 15 Anti-Choice Organizations".[1]

I notice that LDI has been nuked twice, with no debate and not even the courtesy of a "quick delete" warning.

LDI is listed in Pro Life Wisconsin's list of resources.[2] It's listed in the Yahoo Directory.[3] LDI is cited by STOPP.[4] Priests for Life is working in concert with LDI on a project to link abortion facilities with Catholic parishes.[5] The Southern Baptist Convention publicized LDI's efforts to uncover the sale of fetal parts.[6] Covenant News lists LDI as a resource for abortion-injured women.[7] Pro-Choice Press attacks LDI's fetal tissue sales publicity.[8] Working For Change has an article on LDI's activities.[9] The Interim interviewed Mark Crutcher about the organization's activities.[10] Pregnancy Decisions Health Center cites a joint work between David Reardon and LDI.[11] nuvo.net did an article about Crucher and LDI's abortion malpractice activities.[12] The Republican National Coalition for Life addressed LDI's research into failure to report statutory rape.[13] AbortionFacts.com lists LDI as a national organization and provides a link.[14] Defend Life has an article about LDI's "Eyewitness Campaign".[15] Abortion TV promotes a video produced by LDI.[16] Traditional Values Coalition posts about LDI's sex abuse allegations.[17]
