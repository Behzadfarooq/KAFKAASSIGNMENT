Arturo Di Mezza (born 16 July 1969 in Napoli) is an Italian race walker.

[edit] Achievements

Year Tournament Venue Result Event
1988 World Junior Championships Sudbury, Canada 9th 10,000 m
1991 Universiade Sheffield, England 3rd 20 km
1993 World Championships Stuttgart, Germany 13th 20 km
1995 World Championships Gothenburg, Sweden 7th 50 km
Universiade Fukuoka, Japan 3rd 20 km
1996 Olympic Games Atlanta, United States 4th 50 km, 3:44:52 PB
1997 World Championships Athens, Greece 8th 50 km
Universiade Catania, Italy 3rd 20 km
1998 European Championships Budapest, Hungary 7th 50 km
1999 World Championships Seville, Spain 6th 50 km
