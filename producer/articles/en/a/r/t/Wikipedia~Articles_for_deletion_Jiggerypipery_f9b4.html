Notability not established, 1,450 GHits, and fails WP:BAND. Delete. M1ss1ontomars2k4 (T | C | @) 18:48, 22 October 2006 (UTC)

  • Weak Keep They may not be an international phenomenon, but there seem to exist some sources (such as this) that place them on the borderline of notability.--Húsönd 21:26, 22 October 2006 (UTC)

Contains at least one member who was once a part of or later joined a band that is otherwise notable.

Weak Keep because I don't like that criteria... --Onorem 12:35, 23 October 2006 (UTC)
