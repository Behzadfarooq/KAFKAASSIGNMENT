'Mark Stephenson (born January 18, 1985 in Victoria, British Columbia) is currently a student at the University of Victoria. Mark is a singer of pop songs that have become quite well known on Vancouver Island. Mark's breakthrough was a one man cover of "I Want It That Way" by the Backstreet Boys which received heavy airplay on 98.5 The Ocean and 107.3 Kool FM in the Summer of 2005. In the Autumn of 2005 Mark later released an original work called My Love (Is Real) that surpassed the success of I Want It That Way. My Love (Is Real) even broke into the countdowns of these stations reaching #14 on The Ocean and #11 on Kool FM. In March 2006 Mark Stephenson released a single called "Freedom". A self titled EP is expected in June 2006.
