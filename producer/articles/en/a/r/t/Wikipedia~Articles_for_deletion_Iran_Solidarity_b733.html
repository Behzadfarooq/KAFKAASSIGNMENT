[edit] Iran Solidarity

Iran Solidarity (edit|talk|history|links|watch|logs) - (View log)

Defunct British anti-war group with no real assertion of notability. Googling the phrase "Iran Solidarity" is meaningless because these are two common words, but a search for "Iran Solidarity" anti-war group gets only about 400 results of which only about 40 are unique[1]. Browsing the organization's website, I could only find one example of a protest they organized, which managed to attract about 30 people [2]. Given that this organization no longer exists and thus will never become more notable, it should be deleted. GabrielF 06:26, 20 January 2007 (UTC)
