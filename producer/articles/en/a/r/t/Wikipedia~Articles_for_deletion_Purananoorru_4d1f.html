[edit] Purananoorru

This appears to be a hoax. Bachrach44 14:51, 6 January 2006 (UTC)

  • Abstain. I got 188 hits. Not much, but it's likely not something that one person invented. We should contact someone familiar with the issue. Punkmorten 15:48, 6 January 2006 (UTC)
  • Move to Purananuru (that gets 35,000 google hits) and keep the redirect. It looks notable and verifiable enough based on this amazon link. - Bobet 17:53, 6 January 2006 (UTC)
    • Comment: I cleaned the article up a bit to make it more presentable. - Bobet 18:00, 6 January 2006 (UTC)
  • Now keep per Bobet. Punkmorten 10:20, 7 January 2006 (UTC)
  • Delete as random poem collection with little notability. Stifle 01:15, 8 January 2006 (UTC)
    • Comment, how can a 2,000 year old poem anthology that people still care enough about to translate be not notable? - Bobet 01:59, 8 January 2006 (UTC)
  • Keep and move to Purananuru or Purananooru Tintin Talk 03:40, 8 January 2006 (UTC)
