Fails WP:CORP. Tagged {{notability}} since January 2007 with no change in content. Mikeblas 05:45, 28 March 2007 (UTC)

  • The company appears to have had a long history, see [1] (after "begin page 388" line), [2] (halfway in the section "Rami Nadeau and his roads"), and [3] ("The company quickly became the dominant power in transportation throughout Inyo County and remained so for nearly a decade...").
