[edit] Muliebrity

Contested PROD, nothing but a dictionary definition of a neologism. Brian G. Crawford 23:14, 1 April 2006 (UTC)

  • Keep, again, notable word worth expanding. --badlydrawnjeff (WP:MEME?) 23:19, 1 April 2006 (UTC)
  • Transwiki unless more content can be added. Seems to be a reasonably well-established (if obscure) term, but unless it has some significance beyond the definition, I can't see as useful anywhere but Wiktionary. —Cuiviénen, Saturday, 1 April 2006 @ 23:23 (UTC)
  • Transwiki. This is a perfectly wonderful word with the correct definition, but this is just a dicdef. I'm not sure how this would be expanded to be an appropriate WP article. --Deville (Talk) 00:24, 2 April 2006 (UTC)
  • Keep I found this article specifically because I was looking for information about this concept, beyond the dictionary definition. This article clearly needs expansion, as it isn't nuanced enough to give a clear explaination, but it appears to be complex enough to warrant an article. ~CS 04:27, 2 April 2006 (UTC)
  • Delete, unverifiable, unstable neologism, i.e. protologism. Wiktionary won't take it as it hasn't been used for a year. Stifle 22:23, 2 April 2006 (UTC)
  • Delete. Come back next year. Or never. Midgley 23:59, 4 April 2006 (UTC)
  • Delete all neologisms ⇒ SWATJester Ready Aim Fire! 23:59, 6 April 2006 (UTC)
  • Transwiki; this appears to be referenced in a dictionary [1]. - Liberatore(T) 19:07, 7 April 2006 (UTC)
