Arturo Goetz (born June 24, 1944 in Buenos Aires), is an Argentine film actor.

He works in the cinema of Argentina.[1]

[edit] Filmography

  • Cómplices (1998)
  • El Amateur (1999) aka The Amateur
  • El Camino (2000) aka The Road
  • Plan (2000)
  • Four Aims and Flyin' Shoes (2001)
  • Cama adentro (2004) aka Live-In Maid
  • La Niña Santa (2004) aka The Holy Girl
  • Derecho de familia (2006) aka Family Law
  • La Novia errante (2006)
  • El Otro (2007) aka The Other
