[edit] i have a few questions about the school....

1) How many acres is it on, how big is the school? 2) Are there aly clubs at the school, if so could someone tell me about 2 of them? 3) What kind of facilities to they have there?


This isn't the kind of school you are thinking about. It is on about 4 floors of an office building.
There really haven't been any formal clubs, sometimes a production team might pop up outside of class. It is usually just friends hanging out. The size of the school doesn't really make it easy for a club to for since it would likely be the entire class showing up.
The video production has a few small studio spaces. The graphics program has multiple computer labs. Fashion design has classrooms with drafting tables and workrooms for sewing and such. Culinary has kitchens and classrooms in a side building.
