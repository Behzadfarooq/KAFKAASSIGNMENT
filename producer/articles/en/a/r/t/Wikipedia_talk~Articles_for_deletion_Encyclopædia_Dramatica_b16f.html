Normal policies for VfD are:
  1. If a vote is from a banned user (Iasson, for example), it may be struck out, or deleted entirely.
  2. If a vote is from an anon and is unsigned, the edit may be signed using the {{unsigned}} template, or some other matter
  3. If a vote is from a new user or suspected sockpuppet, that may be indicated by a comment after the vote.
  4. In general, editing other people's votes (or other comments) is considered very bad behavior, and has been grounds for sanctions such as a temporary ban for vandalism.
