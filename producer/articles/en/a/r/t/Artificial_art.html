Artificial art is the automatization of art production. Described in literature e.g, (Arroyo 2005).

The art considers a specific activity human, for which it resorts to certain sensorial aesthetic and intellectual faculties . The computers have certain sensorial faculties (sounds by the microphone, tact by the keyboard, etc) and next to the Genetic Programming and Artificial Neuronal Networks (branches of the Artificial intelligence)from where certain level of aesthetic and intellectual faculty is obtained. This is the frame in which we speak of as Artificial Art.
