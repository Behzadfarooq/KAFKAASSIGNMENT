[edit] Locked in Lace

Locked in Lace (edit|talk|history|links|watch|logs) – (View log)

Article claims notability, but nothing is sourced and doesn't seem to meet WP:WEB criteria, at least not that I can discern. RJASE1 Talk 04:45, 27 February 2007 (UTC)

  • Delete. A decent number of hits on Google, but they seem largely self-referential or a part of a list of links. Certainly nothing that can be used as a reliable source regarding notability or importance. Arkyan 16:14, 27 February 2007 (UTC)
  • Delete Gender bending fun and games article lacks reliable and independent sources. Promotes a website. Inkpaduta 18:55, 27 February 2007 (UTC)
  • Delete; unless this has outside media or academic coverage, its not notable in an encyclopaedia. John Vandenberg 03:31, 28 February 2007 (UTC)
  • Strange and apparently directed towards promotion of a website and discussion group, rather than of a recordable and objective phenomenon or event. Also appears to lack objectively independent sourcing. -- Simon Cursitor 07:51, 1 March 2007 (UTC)
  • Delete per nom. --pIrish 22:00, 2 March 2007 (UTC)
