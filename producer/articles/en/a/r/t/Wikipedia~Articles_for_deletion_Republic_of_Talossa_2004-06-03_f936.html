[edit] Yes, it is an edit war...

I am the user that is trying to PUT information on the Republic of Talossa. Mr. Madison, King of the Kingdom of Talossa, is trying to remove as much information as possible on Republic, which seceded from the Kingdom because of his abuses.

I am from the Republic, and I am simply trying to put information on the Republic online.

The same situation is happening with the Talossa keyword. I have put neutral information on the Talossa keyword, but Mr Madison keeps redirecting it to the Kingdom of Talossa keyword.

[edit] On deleting and redirecting.

For the sake of Neutrality, if there was concensus on removal of Republic_of_Talossa, Kingdom_of_talossa and Talossa should also be redirected to Micronation and protected.

Otherwise, there will be content on the Kingdom of Talossa, but not on the Republic of Talossa.
