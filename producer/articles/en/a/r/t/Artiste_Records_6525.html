Artiste Records

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Artiste Records is a Canadian-based record label founded in 1987 by manager and producer Paul Lévesque in Rosemère, Québec. The label produced for years albums from Bruno Pelletier. The management company, Paul Lévesque Management, manages now, not only Bruno Pelletier, but also Marie-Pier Perreault and Philippe Berghella.
