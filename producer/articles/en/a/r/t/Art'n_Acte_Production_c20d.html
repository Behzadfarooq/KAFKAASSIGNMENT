Art'n Acte Production is a French short film released in 2003, produced and directed by Farid Dms Debah. It is the first completed film shot by Farid Dms Debah on 35mm. With Cristiana Réali, Rachid Arhab, Tom Leduc, Benoît Allemane, Khaled, K-Mel, Thierry Beccaro, Henri Guybet, Yazid Aït and Luc Saint-Eloy.

[edit] Synopsis

Christophe Valet is a young film director budding with hopes and dreams. His imagination carries him into a fantasy world of limitless and abundant success. Unfortunately, reality is something far removed. In order to make his first film, he must succumb to the demands of the producers. Christophe thinks he has found an ideal partner in the distinguished European film producer Mr. Hermann. But what he doesn't know is that success comes at a price!
