Cossacks: Art of War is the first expansion pack to the game, Cossacks: European Wars. The game can not be played without purchasing Cossacks: European Wars, whereas Cossacks: Back to War can be played as a standalone. Like the original Cossacks, the game is set in the 17th and 18th centuries, and 8000 units can be controlled. Cossacks: Art of War adds 5 new campaigns, 2 new nations, a map editor, and 16 times larger maps with new terrains.

The two new nations are Denmark and Bavaria, which are both regarded as very good in the online Cossacks community, with Denmark being the popular choice of the two.
