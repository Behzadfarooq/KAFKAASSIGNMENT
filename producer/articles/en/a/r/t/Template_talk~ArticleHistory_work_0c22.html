[edit] First worklist

The following is a list of pages with the following criteria:

  • Begins with 'Wikipedia:Featured article candidate'
  • Is either
    • Not linked from an article talk page with a name included in the page title
      • (Thus Wikipedia:Featured article candidates/Sunset Blvd. (1950 film) is listed despite a link from Sunset Boulevard (1950 film))
    • Is not transcluded in a Featured log or Archived nomination page
  • Is not simply a redirect which is neither linked from TP nor transcluded in log
    • (Thus Wikipedia:Featured article candidates/1755 Lisbon earthquake/Archive 1 is excluded because it is a redirect not linked to from anywhere, nor transcluded in a log)
  • Has a title which contains the name of one featured article
    • (Thus 2003 Invasion of Iraq is listed, because "Invasion" is FA)
    • (Thus Sunset blvd is listed because it matches Sun)

Pages which look like archives are probably older FAC attempts for featured articles. These need to be added to the talk page with {{facfailed|pagelink}}, where pagelink excludes the WP:FAC prefix. If the facfailed links are added (where appropriate), the bot will take care of (most of) the rest.
