[edit] Delta Goodrem: Remixed

Unverifiable, probable hoax. A week ago I left messages on the article's talk page and the talk pages of the editors (an anon IP and an account which has only made edits about this album) asking for sources but none have been forthcoming. No apparent Google hits for the name or for any new Delta Goodrem album of this description. I can't find any references on Sony's site or Delta's official site. The only reference I can find is a thread (which was started the same day the article was created) on her official forum where someone states there is a rumour about such an album and asks people to make mock-up album covers [1]. The person who started the thread later cites this Wiki article as the source of the rumour. And the so-called album cover appears to be a mock-up of a picture on Delta's official site. Sarah Ewart 04:32, 14 January 2006 (UTC)
