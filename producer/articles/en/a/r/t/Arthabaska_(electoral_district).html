Arthabaska (electoral district)

Arthabaska is a provincial electoral district in Quebec, Canada that elects members to the National Assembly of Quebec. The riding was created in 1890 from a part of Drummond-Arthabaska.

[edit] Members of the Legislative Assembly

  1. Joseph-Éna Girouard, Liberal (1890-1898)
  2. Paul Tourigny, Liberal (1900-1916)
  3. Joseph-Édouard Perrault, Liberal (1916-1936)
  4. Joseph-David Gagné, Union Nationale (1936-1939)
  5. Wilfrid Girouard, Liberal (1939-1942)
  6. Pierre-Horace Plourde, Liberal (1944-1948)
  7. Wilfrid Labbé, Union Nationale (1948-1960)
  8. Albert Morissette, Liberal (1960-1966)

[edit] Members of the National Assembly

  1. Roch Gardner, Union Nationale (1966-1970)
  2. Jean-Gilles Massé, Liberal (1970-1976)
  3. Jacques Baril, Parti Québécois (1976-1985)
  4. Laurier Gardner, Liberal (1985-1989)
  5. Jacques Baril, Parti Québécois (1989-2003)
  6. Claude Bachand, Liberal (2003-2007)
  7. Jean-François Roux, Action démocratique (2007-present)
