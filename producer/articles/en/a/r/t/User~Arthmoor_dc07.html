Just your average internet geek, born about 10 years too soon to have any real fun in the digital age.

My internet activities:

Blog - Where I rant about whatever comes to mind. Lots of political stuff, and other nerdy game related things when the mood is right. Not politically correct.
QSF Portal - A community portal and lightweight CMS package.
Quicksilver Forums - PHP and MySQL based forum software.
MudBytes MUD Code Repository - A place for folks interested in MUDs to upload code, and discuss issues.
AFKMud Codebase. Can your code do this?
The FUSS Project - An ongoing project to debug the SMAUG, SWR, and SWFOTE codebases.
IMC2 - An Intermud communications protocol that links several MUDs together for chatting.
Alsherok - My own personal MUD. Not terribly active anymore but it had its day.
Arthmoor Internet Services - MUD hosting for self motivated MUD admins.
MUD Domain - A MUD resource site I help with from time to time.

Used to be a category moderator and regular contributor to Mudmagic as well until Kyndig got a while hair up his ass and banned dozens of people for speaking out.

Along with various other minor PHP/HTML related contributions here and there. Nothing of any real importance though.

Of late I have also taken an interest in the anti-spam movement as a result of the deluge that owning so many domains has cursed me with.
