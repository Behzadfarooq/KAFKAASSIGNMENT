[edit] Damien Kane

Damien Kane (edit|talk|history|links|watch|logs)

Created by user:Damien Kane. I call that pure spam. -- RHaworth 03:59, 22 November 2006 (UTC)

  • Delete as autobiographical. MER-C 04:32, 22 November 2006 (UTC)
  • Delete self-promotion. Seems to be using his userpage that way too. Opabinia regalis 05:48, 22 November 2006 (UTC)
  • Delete per WP:AUTO, though I'm guessing that the person who actually wrote this is more likely to be a fan than the actual person. If the creator of the article was not user:Damien Kane, I'd probably vote keep per notable wrestler. --- RockMFR 06:53, 22 November 2006 (UTC)
  • Notable wrestler, but delete per above. SupaStarGirl 14:20, 22 November 2006 (UTC)
  • Notable wrestler, so Keep. I am the son of Damien Kane that happens to have the same name. I set up the user profile in honor of my father and wanted to transfer that info to an article as a school project because my father was a notable wrestler and I would like for him to be remembered for his contribution to wrestling. Damien Kane
  • Keep: Notable wrestler. Deserves articles regardless of who created it. DCEdwards1966 17:17, 22 November 2006 (UTC)
  • Keep, WP:AUTO is not a criterion for deletion. Period. If that is the only violation, it should be kept. Were it a criterion, we would get the absurd scenario that an article's worth depends on how far its author chooses to identify themself. -Amarkov blahedits 19:30, 22 November 2006 (UTC)
  • Keep per Amarkov. --- RockMFR 19:46, 22 November 2006 (UTC)
  • Keep on condition that someone produces sources. Canadian-Bacon t c 22:05, 22 November 2006 (UTC)
  • Keep per above. --badlydrawnjeff talk 16:52, 24 November 2006 (UTC)
