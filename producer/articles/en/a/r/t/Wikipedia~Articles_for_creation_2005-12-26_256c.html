[edit] P-Series

The P-Series Chevrolet chassis was built for GM's motorhomes. Chevrolet offered a wide range of engines to match the chassis over the years; many included the small-block and big-block V8's. Over the years, it was gradually receiving many refinements, up until 1999, when Chevrolet finally stopped building the chassis because of formidable competition from Ford. Ford introduced the V-10 series motorhome chassis with a "wide-track" stance, whereas the Chevrolet still retained the inward wheel base. However, Chevrolet didn't give up the fight because it offered the 7.4 Liter Big Block Vortec V8 with a class leading 290 horsepower until Ford's V10 received an upgrade in mechanicals, and it produced 310 horsepower. GM sold their rights to the motorhome chassis business to Workhorse Chassis Industries, who still use Chevrolet components, namely the 8.1 Vortec 8100 Series with a currently class leading 340 horsepower, and Workhorse uses the Allison five-speed automatic transmission.

[edit] Dollar Validators

A dollar validator is a small mechanism that paper currency if fed through to validate that it is leagle. These mechanisms are mostly found in vending or change machines. Many use 24-34v and 115-117v to operate. they consist of gears, motors, wheels, springs and almost always a set a bands which the dollar is carried on. A few of the most common manufactures are Coinco, Conlux, Dixie Narco and MARS (MEI).

[edit] Eric Britton

Eric Britton -- full name Francis Eric Knight Britton -- born Boston, Mass, USA on 27 June 1938 and a resident of Paris since 1969, has since completing PhD course work and exams in economics at Columbia University (PhD Cand., 1966) worked internationally in the creation of [[self-organized collaborative network]s to advance understanding in practices in the sustainability agenda. [edit] Maglia Rosa

The term "maglia rosa" or pink jersey denotes the overall leader for the Giro d'Italia or Tour of Italy. Like the famous yellow jersey worn in the Tour de France the "maglia rosa" lets fans and sports writers identify the overall race leader from all others more easily and allows the main sponsor of the tour to show off their support. The overall leader of any grand tour is the rider with the lowest acculumated time over all stages. Other jersey are also used in many of the three grand tours. They are often a King of the Mountains jersey, a Sprinters Jersey and Best young rider, usually riders under the age of 23. Within professional cycling to wear the "maglia rosa" for just one day is considered a great source of pride, much less win the overall race.

[edit] Destructuralism

A fanatical political ideal introduced in the book Warday. Similar to anarchism, calls for the destruction of major social organization and economic structures. Main line of the ideal is that the largest and only allowed social unit would be the family. All political or economic activity would be that between families, which would have total political and economic freedom. Currency would not exist, governemts would not exist. All trade would be barter of goods or services.
