[edit] "I Was There" Wiki Memoir project

This "project" might be an appropriate subject for an article if it becomes widely known, or develops significant results. But the current text is simply an inviatation to participate (with no link or indication on how to actually get to the wiki in question) and a list of links to articles about participants, not about the historical moments which they witnessed or participated in. Most are redlinks, all but one of the remainder are up for speedy deletion, and that is a dab page. Delete unless completely rewritten, with evidence of current notability or significance.
