Article and others listed appear to be candidates for deletion per WP:NOT, #7 plot summaries.  — MrDolomite | Talk 02:01, 26 August 2006 (UTC) I am also nominating the following related pages:

  • Remembrance of Rings Past (Hope & Faith episode)

In the context of this AfD, this is a current TV show which has not established its encyclopedic notability yet and as such, the episode detail information is not warranted. See also Samaster1991's comment below about another way to include this information without individual articles, but allows for future growth. — MrDolomite | Talk 13:36, 3 September 2006 (UTC)
