  • Keep, rename Chucky Mullins (the usual spelling) and rewrite. The current tone of the article is not encyclopedic, but Mullins' injury (during the course of a college football game) and death are certainly notable, and have even been the subject of a film! The College Charity Bowl [1] was founded to help pay Mullins' medical bills [2]. There is also a road named after him in Russellville, Alabama. See also this Ebony magazine article [3], this tribute at thegoal.com, or just check out the Google results for yourself. I know next to nothing about football, so I'm not going to edit this article, but there is no doubt in my mind that it should exist. Perodicticus 11:32, 14 January 2006 (UTC)
There's at least one book about him too. Perodicticus 12:06, 14 January 2006 (UTC)
