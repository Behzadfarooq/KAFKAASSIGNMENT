Hello, welcome to Wikipedia. Here's some tips:

  • If you made any edits before you got an account, you might be interested in assigning those to your username.
  • You can sign your name using three tildes, like ~~~. If you use four, you can add a datestamp too.
  • If you ever think a page or image should be deleted, please list it at the votes for deletion page. There is also a votes for undeletion page if you want to retrieve something that you think should not have been deleted.
  • If you have any questions, see the help pages, add a question to the village pump or ask me on my talk page.

Other useful pages are: how to edit, how to write a great article, naming conventions, manual of style and the Wikipedia policies.

Hi. Thanks for adding your contributions to Wikipedia! Here on Wikipedia we try to write our articles using a neutral point of view. What this means is we don't give our own opinions about things, but try to stick to the facts. If you get a chance, try to read Wikipedia:NPOV. It's kind of long and boring, but here on Wikipedia people take NPOV very seriously. It would also be useful if you added some [[links]] when you write your articles. When you see a useful term, such as a persons name, just add square brackets around it. For instance, you might write: "[[Frida]] is a [[film]] that depicts the life of the famous [[artist]] [[Frida Kahlo]]. [[Salma Hayek]] stars in the title role." Welcome again.
