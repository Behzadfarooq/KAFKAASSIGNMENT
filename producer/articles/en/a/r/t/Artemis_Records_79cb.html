Artemis Records

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Artemis Records is a New York-based independent record label, founded in July 1999 by former chairman/CEO Danny Goldberg.

The label, distributed by Koch Entertainment, is currently home to a diverse group of artists including Crossbreed, Steve Earle, Sugarcult, Better Than Ezra, Boston, dope, Little Barrie, Stephan Smith, Kittie (former label), Fabulous Thunderbirds, Lollipop Lust Kill, Jaguar Wright, Ruff Ryders, John Hiatt, Khia, The Pretenders, and the late Warren Zevon.
