[edit] Metal Maul Wars

Non notable (Google finds 85 hits) spin off of Wintermaul Wars, a Warcraft III custom map. That article is, itself, nominated for deletion. The creator of the Metal Maul Wars article, User:Evilmaster, has only made edits to that article, to Wintermaul Wars, and to Tower Defense (the type of map Metal Maul Wars is classified as), all of which were made within a 40 minute space of time on August 20th, and hasn't made any contributions since. The article is also poorly written. Methnor 21:10, 3 September 2006 (UTC)

  • Delete vanity, refer to WP:NOT and webhosting. User:Yy-bo 21:55, 3 September 2006 (UTC)
  • Delete for non-notability. This doesn't merit any more than a mention in the 'Wintermaul Wars' article, and if that one gets deleted, this certainly should be. Terraxos 17:28, 4 September 2006 (UTC)
  • Note: This debate has been added to the list of CVG deletions. Daveydweeb (chat/patch) 10:11, 4 September 2006 (UTC)
