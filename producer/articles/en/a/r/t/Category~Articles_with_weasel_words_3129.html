The following category contains articles that have weasel words (for instance, "Some critics say..." and "Most people dislike..."). We strongly encourage the sourcing of such statements.

The {{weasel}} tag can be added to the top of an article or section to bring to attention that it contains weasel words.

Alternatively the {{weasel-inline}} or {{weal}} tags can be placed inline with the text next to the words in question.
