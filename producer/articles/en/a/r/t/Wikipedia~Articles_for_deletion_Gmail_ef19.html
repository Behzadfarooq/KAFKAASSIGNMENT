Wikipedia is not a news site. Why do you think Gmail is encyclopedic while it is still in beta testing? Gmail, as it stands now, is news, not encyclopedic content.

  • Keep - I think the "Wikipedia is not a news site" argument is a little lost on this page. Gmail just happens to be a new encyclopaedia stub on Google's mail service. Its only weakness (which is almost certainly temporary) is that details are sketchy, even so it has already caused quite a rumpus for an email service. Just give it a few more days. Whether this is genuine (and I stick out my neck here and say I believe it) or it is a hoax, the page is worthwhile.
