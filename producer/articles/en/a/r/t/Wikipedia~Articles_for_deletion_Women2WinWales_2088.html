  • Weak Keep I believe that an organisation chaired by a Shadow Secretary of State is at least marginally notable.--Anthony.bradbury 00:54, 14 February 2007 (UTC)

  • Move, I agree with Bencherlite in the fact that it needs to be moved into a "Women 2 WIn" page.
