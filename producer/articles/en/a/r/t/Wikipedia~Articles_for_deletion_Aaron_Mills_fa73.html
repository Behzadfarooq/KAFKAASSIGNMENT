[edit] Aaron Mills

Someone tried to AFD this as someone notnotable. Not mine just listing it properly, so No Vote kotepho 07:21, 14 March 2006 (UTC)

  • A non-notable -- there's no reason for this entry to exist. —The preceding unsigned comment was added by 69.142.247.167 (talk • contribs).
  • I don't follow football much, but this player doesn't look particularly notable. There are a lot of college players out there. Zetawoof 07:57, 14 March 2006 (UTC)
    • Oh yeah, and that's a delete. Zetawoof 12:14, 14 March 2006 (UTC)
  • Delete I don't think college sportspeople are notable unless they're clearly standouts MLA 09:40, 14 March 2006 (UTC)
  • Keep He's probably notable to the more hardcore football fans, so why not keep it up for them to enjoy!? —The preceding unsigned comment was added by JimMorrisonFever (talk • contribs).
Note: User:JimMorrisonFever has a total of four edits, two of which are voting on AFDs. Kuzaar 14:39, 14 March 2006 (UTC)
  • Strong Keep If the team is notable enough for inclusion, its players should be allowed articles. Otherwise you end up with a.) countless red links, b.) a complete lack of individual player information that may be of use to a person reading the article about the team, or c.) the disruptive overloading of the team page with aforementioned individual player information...due to the fact that individual player articles aren't allowed. Bobby P. Smith Sr. Jr. 14:08, 14 March 2006 (UTC)
  • Delete per MLA. Kuzaar 14:40, 14 March 2006 (UTC)
  • Keep. He does hold the AFL league record for the longest field goal, and to fans of Arena Football he probably is notable. --Aude (talk | contribs) 15:33, 14 March 2006 (UTC)
  • Keep per Bobby P. Smith Sr. Jr. - Wezzo (talk) (ubx) 16:11, 14 March 2006 (UTC)
  • Keep. Bio of a notable professional athlete. No Guru 17:34, 14 March 2006 (UTC)
  • Keep, professional athlete. Andy Saunders 18:40, 14 March 2006 (UTC)
  • Keep - I've no idea about football, but if a team is notable, all its players should probably be able to have articles. ProhibitOnions 19:45, 14 March 2006 (UTC)
  • Keep. Definitely meets Wikipedia:Notability. —Eternal Equinox | talk 21:43, 14 March 2006 (UTC)
