[edit] Roulette Dares (The Haunt Of)

Not notable enough to require it's own page, any addtional info can be put in the De-loused in the comatorium article Zopwx2 20:29, 1 August 2006 (UTC)

  • Merge into De-loused in the Comatorium. The songs from the album released as singles might warrant their own pages, but this one (verified with an Amazon.com search) was not. --Thorne N. Melcher 00:21, 2 August 2006 (UTC)
  • Delete per nom --Peephole 01:16, 2 August 2006 (UTC)
  • Merge per Thorne N. Melcher, the song is not a single, and the uncited explanatory text included not only doesn't establish notability but may also violate WP:NOR. -- H·G (words/works) 07:41, 2 August 2006 (UTC)
  • If it is merged, it should cite the downloadable storybook that accompanies the album, which provides indepth explanations about what happens in Cerpin Taxt's "dreamworld." But yes, it does look like original research, and thanks for the agreement. --Thorne N. Melcher 00:52, 4 August 2006 (UTC)
  • Merge per Thorne N. Melcher and H·G.--Runcorn 17:12, 6 August 2006 (UTC)
