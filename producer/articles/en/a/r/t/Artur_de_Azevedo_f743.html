Artur de Azevedo

Artur Nabantino Gonçalves de Azevedo is an important Brazilian writer, who was born in São Luiz, state of Maranhão, on July 7, 1855. His most important work is A Capital Federal. He died at Rio de Janeiro, state of Rio de Janeiro, in 1908, when he was 53 years old.
