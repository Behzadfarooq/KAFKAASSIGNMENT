[edit] Zygo, Zygo Energy Vodka

Zygo (edit|talk|history|links|watch|logs) - (View log)
Note; there are two separate articles being nominated:
Zygo - and - Zygo Energy Vodka SkierRMH 23:58, 16 January 2007 (UTC)
  • del Nonnotable brand of vodka. Only 100 unique google hits. No independent references. `'mikka 22:07, 16 January 2007 (UTC)
  • Keep - I didn't get it. It exists. What makes a vodka more notable. Consumption? Alcohol volume? The extent of negative effects on human brain? Lajbi Holla @ me 23:24, 16 January 2007 (UTC)
  • Compare the external coverage of, for example, Skyy vodka with the external coverage of Zygo. I have no opinion as of now, but the whole third-party coverage thing would certainly be helpful. GassyGuy 00:44, 17 January 2007 (UTC)
  • Keep, independent refs are easy to find on Google. Here's two links that offer decent coverage: [1] [2] It however seems to be one product and thus the articles should be merged. hateless 00:51, 17 January 2007 (UTC)
  • Keep (but merge the product into the company article) I'm satisfied there is plenty of external coverage of this brand, for example: The New York Times, the Boston Phoenix, and over 9,600 Google hits. Plus, the nominator does not appear to have listed any rationale or reason for deletion. --Canley 08:54, 17 January 2007 (UTC)
  • Apologies to the nominator, I thought your deletion rationale was one of the suggestions. There's no need to put "delete" in the reasoning, it's kind of assumed that is your suggestion. --Canley 05:28, 19 January 2007 (UTC)
  • Keep and merge per Canley. Obviously notable and verifiable. Could certainly use cleanup, but why would we delete this? schi talk 23:26, 17 January 2007 (UTC)
    • Well, if you are going to keep each and every brand of candies, bagels, pizzas, toys, then what the heck, keep it. Still, IMO it is garbage. `'mikka 17:35, 19 January 2007 (UTC)
  • Merge the two, and rewrite to avoid the wording that makes it borderline advertising. Radagast83 22:40, 20 January 2007 (UTC)
