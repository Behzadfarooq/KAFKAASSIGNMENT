[edit] Mañana Será Otro Dia

This is an old page with no users attempting to justify its place, the film is not a notable release in the English speaking world, nor has the original author attempted to take non-English language information and provide it here. It is a non-notable film outside its home release country. doktorb 21:50, 14 December 2005 (UTC)

  • Keep, it's notable inside its home-release country, and wikipedia is a global encyclopedia not an Anglocentric one. See WP:BIAS Kappa 05:29, 17 December 2005 (UTC)
  • Keep. -Tim Rhymeless (Er...let's shimmy) 10:48, 19 December 2005 (UTC)
  • Thanks, Tim, but for what reason? doktorb | words 15:25, 19 December 2005 (UTC)
  • Keep as per Kappa. Its notability outside its home-release country is unimportant; there are certainly many MANY articles here with little importance outside of the United States. Mind you, I'm not stating that "we should keep a, after all, look at all of the b we have" is a valid argument. Anyway, if you check the IMDB link, the television show had 120 episodes; although this is a short run for (what I think is) a telenovela, it's enough to be notable, whether or not it's of notice to Wikipedia users who speak primarily English. It is very much ideal for Wikipedia to combat systemic bias, especially in media, where the project has an extremely deep, although narrow (mostly English lang.) focus. -Tim Rhymeless (Er...let's shimmy) 04:41, 20 December 2005 (UTC)

This AfD is being relisted to generate a clearer consensus. Please add new discussion below this notice. Thanks! Mo0[talk] 04:27, 20 December 2005 (UTC)

  • Keep, just because it isn't Anglo doesn't make it nn. User:Zoe|(talk) 05:11, 20 December 2005 (UTC)
  • Keep and expand. Overcome systemic bias &c. Smerdis of Tlön 06:02, 20 December 2005 (UTC)
  • Keep and expand. Notable within their respective countires. Capitalistroadster 08:05, 20 December 2005 (UTC)
  • Keep per everyone. Perodicticus 09:31, 20 December 2005 (UTC)
  • Keep. Agree with Zoe and Kappa. Sjakkalle (Check!) 12:01, 20 December 2005 (UTC)
  • Keep, speedy close. Little point in this being relisted. Proto t c 12:16, 20 December 2005 (UTC)
  • Keep and expand. Believe it or not, they make movies and television shows outside the US. --MisterHand 15:25, 20 December 2005 (UTC)
Comment I am actually in the UK. My point is that this is a non-notable movie/tv show for the English language Wiki, in my opinion. doktorb | words 15:49, 20 December 2005 (UTC)
  • Keep, as both are notable in their respective countries. Also, we need more non-English language movies and television shows. Carioca 20:19, 20 December 2005 (UTC)
    • I couldn't agree more. Zoe (216.234.130.130 23:46, 21 December 2005 (UTC))
