[edit] Panic! At the Disco

2005 formed band [1] and with the only, new album, A Fever You Can't Sweat Out. Not notable. feydey 23:59, 12 October 2005 (UTC) After the good work by Capitalistroadster i'd say keep and move to Panic! At the Disco. feydey 14:59, 13 October 2005 (UTC)

  • Hmmm "A Fever You Can't Sweat Out" gets 118,000 google hits, so I'm going vote keep. Kappa 00:16, 13 October 2005 (UTC)
    • I also note that's it's Amazon sales rank is 347. Kappa 00:18, 13 October 2005 (UTC)
  • Keep and cleanup as per WP:music. They appear to be a relatively new band but their album has so far reached number 112 on the Billboard charts, #1 on the Heatseeker chart and top 10 of the Billboard indy chart see [2]. They were Spin's band of the day last week see [3] and are currently undertaking a tour of the US and Canada. Capitalistroadster 01:01, 13 October 2005 (UTC)
    • PS Seem to be attracting plenty of media coverage according to this Google News search. [4] Capitalistroadster 01:05, 13 October 2005 (UTC)
  • Keep/Cleanup as per Capitalistroadster Qaz (talk) 03:22, 13 October 2005 (UTC)
  • Keep and cleanup per above. They're notable. PacknCanes 03:58, 13 October 2005 (UTC)
  • I have cleaned up and expanded this article. No change of vote from Keep. Article should be moved after completion of AfD to Panic! At the Disco. Capitalistroadster 10:04, 13 October 2005 (UTC)
  • Keep, cleanup, move. Meets WP:MUSIC. Jkelly 17:05, 13 October 2005 (UTC)
