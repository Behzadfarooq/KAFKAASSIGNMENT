"Art Breaker" is the 56th episode of That's So Raven.

[edit] Plot

Spoiler warning: Plot and/or ending details follow.

When Chelsea discovers her newfound talent for art, she uses Raven as the inspiration for her next masterpiece which will be displayed at a museum exhibit. Raven then sees that Chelsea's finished project is a statue that shows her with a large beak with feathers. Raven then has a vision that the statue will be destroyed, within seconds later the vision actually happens with Raven accidentally knocking it down. Raven then literally poses as the statue at the exhibit so Chelsea does not notice. Meanwhile, When Cory's school gets rid of unhealthy foods, Tanya sets the same example by making the family eat healthier. Cory and Wiliam then use the opportunity by selling sodas in milk cartons at their school.

Spoilers end here.

[edit] Trivia

  • Actress Johari Johnson makes another brief appearance in the series, this time as a passerby at the museum who is frightened by statue Raven.
