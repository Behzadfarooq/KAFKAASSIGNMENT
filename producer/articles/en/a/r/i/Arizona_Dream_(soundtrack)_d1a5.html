Arizona Dream
Soundtrack by Goran Bregovic
Released 1993
Genre Soundtrack
Length 46:08
Label Mercury

Soundtrack from Emir Kusturica's Arizona Dream by Goran Bregovic featuring the vocals and lyrics of Iggy Pop on tracks 1, 4 & 6 and the lyrics of Emir Kusturica as well as the vocals of Iggy Pop on track 10.

  • 1. In The Deathcar
  • 2. Dreams
  • 3. Old Home Movie
  • 4. TV Screen
  • 5. 7/8 & 11/8
  • 6. Get The Money
  • 7. Gunpowder
  • 8. Gypsy Reggae
  • 9. Death
  • 10. This Is A Film
