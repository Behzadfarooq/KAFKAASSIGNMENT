Ariel "Arik" Zeevi (born January 16, 1977 in Bene-Barak) is an Israeli judoka. He won a bronze medal at the 2004 Summer Olympics in Athens in the men's 100 kg division. He was also 5th at the 2000 Summer Olympics in Sydney.

He was three times European champion (2000, 2003, 2004) and once the silver medalist (2005). In the 2001 World Championship he won the silver medal in the open category. He missed the 2005 World championship in Cairo due to injury and subsequently underwent surgery to repair the damage to his shoulder.
