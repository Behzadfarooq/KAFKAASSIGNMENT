[edit] Proposition 202 - Minimum wage

For official text, see [[the proposed statute]].

Proposition 202, officially known as the "Raise the Minimum Wage for Working Arizonans Act" raised Arizona's minimum wage to $6.75 per hour. It was approved by a margin of 65.4%[1] and went into effect on January 1, 2007.
