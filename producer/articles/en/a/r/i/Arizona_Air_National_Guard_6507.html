–± …… Air National Guard is the branch of the United States Air National Guard operating within the state of Arizona. It was founded by Barry Goldwater, and was integrated two years before President Truman's military integration order.

[edit] Units

  • 162nd Fighter Wing
  • 161st Air Refueling Wing, 107th ACS
