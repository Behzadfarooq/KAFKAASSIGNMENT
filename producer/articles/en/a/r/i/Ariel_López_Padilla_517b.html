Ariel López Padilla (born 12 August 1962 in Guadalajara, Jalisco, Mexico) is a Mexican actor of telenovelas.

He studied to become a ballet dancer. He started working in television in 1991 with La pícara soñadora. He had a daughter in 1996, María Levy Padilla with his deceased ex-wife, the actress Mariana Levy.
