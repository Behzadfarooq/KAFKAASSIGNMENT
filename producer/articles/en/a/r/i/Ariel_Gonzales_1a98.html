Ariel Gonzales

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Ariel Gonzalez (born 22 October 1974) is an Argentinian naturalized Mexican football player.

He currently plays as a forward for Club Universidad Nacional, a Mexico City team commonly known as the Pumas. He joined the Pumas for this season on a loan from Club San Luis, he arrived to Mexican football to play in the Primera División A with Real San Luis, then played with Queretaro FC and Club Irapuato, then won the promotion with the club and began to play in the Mexican top flight, after one season with Irapuato he was transferred to Club América, playing with them only half of a season, after that he moved to Club San Luis to play once again in the second tier division of Mexican football, once again he won the promotion, but this time with San Luis. He played well the past Apertura 2006 scoring 10 goals, only 4 less that the champion strikers of the last tournament Salvador Cabañas and Sebastian Abreu.

[edit] Achievements

Goalscoring Leader

  • Primera División A Verano 2002 with Queretaro FC
