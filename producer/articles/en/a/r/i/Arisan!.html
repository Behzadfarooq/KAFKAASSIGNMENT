Arisan! is a 2003 Indonesian film. It is the first Indonesian film with a gay theme, and the first Indonesian film to use high-definition color enhancement.

A television series as a continuation of the movie with the same title has been airing on antv.

[edit] Plot

The story revolved around a meeting group of high-class female socialites in Jakarta, what is usually called as arisan by locals.

The main character is Memey (Cut Mini Theo), an interior designer with a business of her own. Her husband left her for an actress. When she finally found a new love interest, Nino (Surya Saputra), it turned out that Nino was more interested in her best friend, Sakti.
