Arija Bareikis in Deuce Bigalow: Male Gigolo

Arija Bareikis (born 1966 in Bloomington, Indiana) is an American actress. She starred alongside Rob Schneider as Kate in Deuce Bigalow: Male Gigolo (1999). Arija is of Lithuanian descent.
