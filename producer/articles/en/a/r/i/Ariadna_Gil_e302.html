Ariadna Gil i Giner, (Barcelona; January 23, 1969 - ) is a Spanish actress.

Bigas Luna discovered her for the 1986 movie "Lola". After 4 films in the Catalan language, she took part in an Emilio Martínez Lázaro Castillian-language movie, "Amo tu cama rica" in 1991.

From 1993 to 2007, she has performed in more than 31 films, like "Libertarias", directed by Vicente Aranda; "El laberinto del fauno", by Guillermo del Toro or "Soldados de Salamina"; directed by her boyfriend David Trueba.
