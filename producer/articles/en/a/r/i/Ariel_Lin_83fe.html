Ariel Lin (Chinese: 林依晨) is a Taiwanese actress and singer.

After appearing in several television roles in Taiwanese dramas during the early 2000s, she came to fame for playing the role of Yuan Xiang Qin in the hit drama series It Started With a Kiss. She has recently starred in Tokyo Juliet with Wu Zun and Simon Yam, another drama for which she was highly praised. Lin is currently working on Legend of the Condor Heroes and the sequel to It Started With a Kiss. She attended Taiwan's National Chengchi University, majoring in Korean.
