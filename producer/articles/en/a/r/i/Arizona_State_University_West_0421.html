Established by the Arizona Legislature in 1984, Arizona State University at the West campus is a campus of Arizona State University. Located in northwest Phoenix, Arizona, straddling the Glendale, Arizona city limits, ASU at the West campus has 7,100 students and offers 38 degree programs in five colleges including an Ed.D. from the College of Teacher Education and Leadership. More graduate programs are being considered for this campus.
