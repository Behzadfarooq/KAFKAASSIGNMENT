Arida (有田郡; -gun) is a district located in Wakayama, Japan.

As of 2003, the district has an estimated population of 52,619 and a density of 120.16 persons per km². The total area is 437.92 km².

  • Aridagawa (former Kanaya, Kibi and Shimizu)
  • Hirogawa
  • Yuasa
