Arizona (band)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

You have new messages (last change).

Arizona is a eurodance project from England that consisted of remixer/producers Michael Gray and Jon Pearn (both of Full Intention) and vocalist Zeeteah Massiah (born December 24, 1960 in Barbados). Their 1993 club smash "Slide On the Rhythm" (credited to Arizona featuring Zeitia) went all the way to #1 on Billboard's Hot Dance Music/Club Play chart.
