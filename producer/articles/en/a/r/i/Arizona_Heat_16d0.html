The Arizona Heat is a women's softball team based in Tucson, Arizona. Since the 2004 season, they have played as a member of National Pro Fastpitch. The team's home games are played at historic Hi Corbett Field. Six of the players on the 2006 roster played college softball at the University of Arizona, and two more played for Arizona State University.
