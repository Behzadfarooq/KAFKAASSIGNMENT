Aritao, Nueva Vizcaya

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Aritao is a 3rd class municipality in the province of Nueva Vizcaya, Philippines. According to the 2000 census, it has a population of 31,705 people in 6,276 households.

[edit] Barangays

Aritao is politically subdivided into 22 barangays.
