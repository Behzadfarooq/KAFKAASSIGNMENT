While I do not make major contributions to Wikipedia on a regular basis, I do try and contribute where I can. I have many areas of interest, too many to list, and I find a lot of information about them here on Wikipedia. I can easily spend hours reading this site. I hope to help expand a lot of articles in the future.

My greatest contributions by far have been to the Single-8 film format page and recently I have started and fully expanded the article on the Omnibot.
