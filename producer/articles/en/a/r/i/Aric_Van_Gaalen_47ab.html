Aric Van Gaalen, (born on August 25, 1984 in Edmonton, Alberta) is a professional baseball pitcher and plays for the Seattle Mariners farm club. Before playing for the Mariners, Gaalen was an amateur entry draftee for the Toronto Blue Jays in 2002 and 2003. He played for the St.Albert River-Prospects and was drafted by the Edmonton Cracker-Cats in 2005 before being drafted by the MLB.
