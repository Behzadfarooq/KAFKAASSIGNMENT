[edit] Navy ships

A total of three ships of the United States Navy have borne the name USS Arizona:

  • the steamship USS Arizona (1858)
  • the 3850-ton steam frigate first launched as USS Neshamny
  • the battleship USS Arizona (BB-39), which sank in Pearl Harbor on December 7, 1941 (see also: USS Arizona Memorial)
