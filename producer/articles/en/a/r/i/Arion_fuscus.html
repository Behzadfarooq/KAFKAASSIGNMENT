Arion fuscus (O. F. Müller, 1774) is species of slug from family Arionidae.

Arion subfuscus (Draparnaud, 1805) is to be considered to be partial synonyme for Arion fuscus.[1] (For practical purposes they are synonymes in Germany and in the Czech Republic.)
