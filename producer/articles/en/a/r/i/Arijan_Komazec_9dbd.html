Arijan Komazec

Arijan Komazec(born January 23, 1970 in Zadar) is a Croatian basketball player. He plays both guard and foward. His height is 6'8" and weighs 200 pounds.

He was a member of the Yugoslavian National Team and then the Croatian National Team after they gained independence. He was a part of the Croatian team that won the Silver Medal in Barcelona during the 1992 Olympics. He also won the Bronze Medal with Croatia in 1993 and 1995 at Eurobasket.

He also played in the Greek Cup in 1993 with Panathinaikos. He has also been part of Greek clubs Olympiakos and A.E.K. Athens, the Italian clubs Varese and Kinder (the latter with which he won the Italian Cup in 1997), and the Croatian club of Zadar (which he won the Croatian Cup with in 2000.)

He was briefly part of the Vancouver Grizzlies, an NBA team from the 2000-2001 season, but that had little impact and he has since then kept to European teams.
