Ariza Makukula (born March 4, 1981 in Kinshasa, Zaire) is a Congolese-Portuguese football player, who plays for Gimnàstic de Tarragona on loan from Sevilla FC of the Spanish La Liga. His most usual positions are Striker and Centre Forward.

[edit] International career

He was Portuese U-21s and played in the 2002 UEFA European Under-21 Football Championship.

He decided to represent DR Congo, but FIFA new rule in 2000s said that such change in nationality must apply before player's 21 birthday, so Makukula cannot play for national other than Portugal.
