Arianna Fontana

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Olympic medal record
Women’s Short track speed skating
Bronze 2006 Turin 3000m relay

Arianna Fontanta (born April 14, 1990 in Sondrio) is an Italian short track speed skater, who won bronze in the 3000m relay at the 2006 Winter Olympics. She placed 11th in the 500m and 6th in the 1000m.
