A Punk/Rock/Metal band from Bromsgrove/Worcester. The band is made up of four/five people. Ryan Stowe - Lead Guitar/Vocals, Felix Turner - Rhythm Guitar/Backing Vocals, Laurence Alexander - Bassist/Backing Vocals, (Sometimes) Jack Timmus - Bassist/Backing Vocals, (sometimes) Sean Thomas - Drums/Precussion.

The Band was Formed in 2004 through a love a music and a gathering of musical talent, at the RGS Worcester.  The Band has recently started their Tour of England, supported by the agency Aural Pleasure.
 The Band started out with the Bassist Jack Timmus from Bromsgrove, who accompanied them for their first few practices but then was unavailable for their first gig and other practices however he is an accomplished bassist and the band will continue to use his skills in their style of music.
