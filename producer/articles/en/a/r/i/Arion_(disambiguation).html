Arion (disambiguation)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Arion may refer to:

  • Arion, a legendary poet and citharode in ancient Greece (originally of Lesbos) who lived in the court of Periander, tyrant of Corinth, Greece.
  • Arion, a writer from the Netherlands Antilles.
  • Arion, an immortal, extremely swift horse, offspring of Demeter and Poseidon, in Greek mythology. See Adrastus.
  • Arion, a comic book published by DC Comics about a sorcerer in ancient Atlantis.
  • Arion, an anime movie. In this movie, Arion is kidnapped by Hades as a child, and is raised to believe that his mother's blindness Demeter was a curse from Zeus, and that only Zeus' death could lift the curse.
  • Arion, a genus of slugs.
  • Arion, a minor character from Square's console role-playing game, Final Fantasy Mystic Quest
