Aria C Jalali!

Origin California, USA
Years active 2005 - Present
Genres Indie rock
Labels Filthy Little Angels (UK)
Members Aria Jalali

Aria C Jalali! is a San Francisco-based solo indie artist, originally from Los Angeles, California. His music is considered to fall under indie or indie-pop. While his catalog of releases is limited, Aria has gained notoriety for the fact that his music is self produced. Generally, his songs consist of full rock music instrumentation, with influences of new wave music heard in his use of synthesizers and art rock heard in his diverse set of instruments, including but not limited to the sitar, dulcimer, mandolin, glockenspiel, lute, and accordian.[1] However, all instruments on his recordings are played by himself. When performing live, Aria C Jalali tours with a large live band (usually 8-9 people playing on stage), and often switches instruments mid-performance.[2] His lyrics are generally described as being vague or ambiguous, which are often found in front of vibrant, major scale riff-based melodies.

Aria Jalali (Aria C Jalali! is the formal name under which his albums are released) has been the subject of many blog articles and links, often citing the fact that he is a "one-man band."
