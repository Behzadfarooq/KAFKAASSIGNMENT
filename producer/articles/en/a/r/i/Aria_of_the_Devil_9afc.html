Aria of the Devil
Studio album by Theatre of Hate
Released 1998
Genre Post-punk/New Wave
Length 38:44
Label Original Masters/Snapper Music
Producer(s) Mick Jones
Theatre of Hate chronology
Ten Years After Aria of the Devil


Aria of the Devil is the second of two studio albums by Theatre of Hate released posthumously after the band's dissolution in 1983.

[edit] Track listing

All songs written by Kirk Brandon.

  1. "Ovature" (3:14)
  2. "Aria Of The Devil" (4:18)
  3. "Dreams Of The Poppy" (4:03)
  4. "Omen Of The Times" (3:12)
  5. "The Incinerator" (4:07)
  6. "Nero" (5:10)
  7. "Americanos" (3:51)
  8. "Eastworld" (3:29)
  9. "The Black Madonna" (3:24)
  10. "Solution" (3:35)

[edit] Personnel

  • Kirk Brandon - Vocals and guitar
  • Stan Stammers - Bass
