Arista Nashville
Parent company Arista Records / Sony BMG
Founded 1974
Founder(s) Clive Davis
Distributing label RCA Records Group (US) and BNA Records
Genre(s) Country
Country of origin US
Official Website http://aristanashville.com/

Arista Nashville is an American record label that is a wholly owned subsidiary of Sony BMG, and operates under the RCA Records Group Arista Nashville is a Nashville based country records label.
