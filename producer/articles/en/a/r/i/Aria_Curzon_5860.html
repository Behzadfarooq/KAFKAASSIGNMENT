Aria Noelle Curzon (December 8, 1987) is an award winning American actress who has a successful and varied career in film, television, stage and voiceover. Her most famous voice roles are the effervescent Ducky in The Land Before Time video series, Theresa ("The Cornchip Girl") in Recess, and Mandy Strausberg in the Adventures in Odyssey radio drama. She has received 3 Young Artist of Hollywood Awards, (5 nominations) 2 CARE Awards, and one CLIO Award (The CLIO being for a PSA she voiced).

[edit] Biography

At the tender age of five, Aria Curzon realized her interest in acting and performing while watching one of her favorite Shirley Temple videos. With her momâ€™s help and care, she entered the audtion scene and by the age of six, she began her acting career in a Crayola commercial. Aria has had the love and support of her parents in reaching her goals and today is still a busy performer. She is also steeped in the pursuit of music,singing and learning both guitar and fiddle, as well as being a prize winning Irish Dancer.

This award winning actress has been a guest star on many television shows, including Without A Trace, JAG, The Pretender, Adventures of A.R.K., Sabrina the Teenage Witch and many others as well as films such as Treehouse Hostage (with Jim Varney) and Santa With Muscles, her first film, starring with Hulk Hogan, Adam Wylie, Mila Kunis and a host of other stars.

Aria has excelled particularly in the voiceover field, being dubbed by many of her friends and colleagues as the "voiceover queen", while starring in such animated films as The Land Before Time, playing the ever optimistic Ducky, to doing countless radio commercials and t.v. spots.. Now, at age 18, she has acquired numerous acting credits under her belt, in movies, television, commercials, and voice-over, and is looking forward to more opportunities coming her way. She is a series regular in the animated series The Land Before Time which premiered in March 2007.
