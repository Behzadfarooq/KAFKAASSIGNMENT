Arizona NewsChannel is a 24 hour regional cable news channel based in Phoenix, Arizona. The service is owned by Belo Corp., and is operated by KTVK NewsChannel 3 (3TV) in Phoenix.

The AZ NewsChannel rebroadcasts newscasts from 3TV in a 24 hour format with the ability to cut in for breaking news[1]. The service broadcasts primarily on Cox Communications channel 14 to the Phoenix Metro Area.
