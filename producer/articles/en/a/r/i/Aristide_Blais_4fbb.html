The Hon. Aristide Blais

Aristide Blais (1875-10-18 – 1964-11-10) was a Canadian physician and senator.

Born in Berthier, Quebec, he was summoned to the Canadian Senate in 1940. A Liberal, he represented the senatorial division of St. Albert, Alberta. He served until his death in 1964.
