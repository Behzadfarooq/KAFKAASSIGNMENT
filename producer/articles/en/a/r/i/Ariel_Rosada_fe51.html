Ariel Javier "El Bombon" Rosada (born April 11, 1978 in Campana, Buenos Aires, Argentina) He is an Argentine midfielder, who currently plays for Toluca of the Primera División in México.

Rosada began to play professionally in Argentina in 1995 with Boca Juniors in Primera Division A, in 1998, he joined the Dutch team AZ Alkmaar, one year later in 1999 he returned to Boca Juniors. Then in the same year, he joined another Argentine team Chacarita Juniors in Primera B Nacional until 2003 when joined Newell's Old Boys in Rosario. In 2005, he left with "Diablos Rojos" CD Toluca, and has been playing there since.
