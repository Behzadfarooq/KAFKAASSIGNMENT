[edit] Ruleset question

In the problem named "one move goal" you state "Silver to move could push the gold horse on b3 to c3 with the camel on b4. This would unfreeze the silver rabbit on b2, which could then step to victory via a2 and a1." My question is: Even if you pull the horse out of b3, isn't the horse in c2 still freezing the rabit in b2? Shouldn't you have to remove BOTH horses before moving the rabbit?

I am new to this game. I apologize for writing my question here. If it is not the correct place for questions, remove it.

Excellent question. Yes, the c2-horse is still freezing the b2-rabbit, BUT after pushing, the silver camel is adjacent to the b2-rabbit, which unfreezes it. A frozen piece is no longer frozen if it has any adjacent friendly piece. --Fritzlein 18:11, 29 July 2006 (UTC)
