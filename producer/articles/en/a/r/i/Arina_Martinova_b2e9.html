Arina Martinova or Martynova (Russian: Арина Мартынова) (born February 27, 1990) is a Russian figure skater who just recently entered the international scene with a debut performance at the 2006 World Figure Skating Championships. She won her first senior international medal at the 2006 Nebelhorn Trophy competition, which was silver. Her former coach was Svetlana Sokolovskaya.
