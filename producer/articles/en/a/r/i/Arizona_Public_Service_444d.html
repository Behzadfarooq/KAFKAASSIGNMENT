Arizona Public Service

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Arizona Public Service is an electric utility subsidiary of the public company Pinnacle West, serving customers in 11 counties, mainly in northern and central Arizona.

APS is regulated by the Arizona Corporation Commission (ACC), the state agency that regulates all energy utilities in Arizona.
