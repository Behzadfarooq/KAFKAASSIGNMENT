The Arizona Sundogs are an minor-league ice hockey team based in Prescott Valley, Arizona that began play in 2006. Affiliated with the Central Hockey League, the team plays its home games at Tim's Toyota Center.

[edit] External links

  • Arizona Sundogs

One of the top players for the Sundogs in their inagural season was Brent Kelly. A native of Kitchener, Ontario, he led the CHL in goals scored with 42. Other accomplishments for the 2006-2007 season . . . CHL All-Star, named to the All-CHL Team, finished second in the voting for the CHL MVP. According to an interview given on "The Tri-City Sports Round-Up" Show on radio station The News, 1490 AM, Kelly said his favorite musician is Eminem. His funniest moment in hockey was a game in Toledo when play was held up because a flock of birds living in the arena landed on the ice. Toledo was also Kelly's least favorite place to play.
