Location of Ariano nel Polesine in Italy

Ariano nel Polesine is a comune (municipality) in the Province of Rovigo in the Italian region Veneto, located about 60 km southwest of Venice and about 30 km southeast of Rovigo. As of 31 December 2004, it had a population of 4,870 and an area of 81.0 km².[1]

The municipality of Ariano nel Polesine contains the frazioni (subdivisions, mainly villages and hamlets) Bacucco, Crociara, Gorino Veneto, Grillara, Magazzini, Piano, Rivà, San Basilio, and Santamaria in Punta.

Ariano nel Polesine borders the following municipalities: Berra, Corbola, Goro, Mesola, Papozze, Porto Tolle, Taglio di Po.
