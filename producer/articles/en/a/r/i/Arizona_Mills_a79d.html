Arizona Mills

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Arizona Mills is an indoor outlet mall in Tempe, Arizona. It is owned by The Mills Corporation and managed by The Taubman Company. It opened on November 20, 1997 with 6,000 parking spaces and approximately 200 retailers. It is currently anchored by outlet versions of Saks Fifth Avenue, JCPenney, Sears Appliance, Neiman Marcus, and Burlington Coat Factory.
