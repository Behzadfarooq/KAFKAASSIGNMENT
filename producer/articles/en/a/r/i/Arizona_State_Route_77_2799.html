Arizona State Route 77

State Route 77 (or SR-77) is a state highway in Arizona that traverses much of the state's length, stretching from its northern terminus at the boundary of the Navajo Nation north of Holbrook to its junction with Interstate 10 in Tucson.

Past the Navajo Nation boundary, SR-77 becomes BIA Route 6 northbound towards Keams Canyon. Between Show Low and Globe, this highway is concurrent with U.S. Route 60. Its southernmost reaches were formerly part of U.S. Route 89, except for its terminal segment, the Miracle Mile segment of Business 10.
