Arifwala is a small town in the south east of Lahore nearly 210 km. In the south River Sutluj about 33 km. North Sahiwal is 45 km away. This area is called Neely Bar also. It is situated between Sutluj and Biayas. It is Tehsil Head quarter consisting 30 Union Councils. About 75% population live in villages and only 25% live in municipal urban area. Main occupation is agriculture Total population of Tehsil Arifwala is about 600,000.

Tehsil Arifwala is surrounded by 255 Villages Total Area of Tehsil Arifwala is 295,146 Acres

[edit] History

In nineteenth century Arifwala was only a village named as chak No. 61/EB. Later, it was called Arifwala because of a landlord named "Arif" having Agriculture land in this village. In 1908, the Deputy Governor "Hurbert" founded new city of Arifwala. In 1987, Arifwala was raised to the status of sub-division and first it attached with District Sahiwal but in 1995 Prime minister of Pakistan Mian NAwaz Sharif attached it with Pakpattan district that is draw back for this little beautiful city.

[edit] Climate

The climate of the city is hot and dry. The summer season starts from April and continues till October. May, June and July are the hottest months. The mean maximum and minimum temperature of these months are about 42 and 28 degree centigrade respectively.
