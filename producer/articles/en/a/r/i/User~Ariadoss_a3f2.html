[edit] Short biography

Ariadoss (pronounced: Air-ee-á-dus) is the alias of Danilo Stern-Sapad. Born on January 12, 1987 in Los Angeles, California. He is the creator of numerous open source applications, web sites, and various works of science fiction and fantasy.

[edit] World view

Ariadoss is a Cultural Creative. Cultural Creatives are probably the newest group to enter this realm. They are modern thinkers who tend to shy away from organized religion but still feel as if there is something greater than themselves. They are very spiritual, even if they are not religious. Life has a meaning outside of the rational for them.
