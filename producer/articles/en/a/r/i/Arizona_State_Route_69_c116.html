Arizona State Route 69

State Route 69 (SR 69) is a divided four-lane highway that serves as an access road to Prescott, Arizona from Interstate 17. It begins at Exit 262 of I-17, traveling northwest for a total length of 34 miles.

Prior to the construction of Interstate 17, Arizona 69 continued south to Phoenix, Arizona as the Black Canyon Highway.
