This is a popular melody and ground bass originating in a number by Emilio de Cavalieri in the Florentine Intermedio of 1592. It spread all over Europe and was used as a basis of compositions by numerous other renaissance composers such as Peter Phillips, Sweelinck, Banchieri and of course Anon...
