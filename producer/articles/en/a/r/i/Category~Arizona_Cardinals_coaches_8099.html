This category is for NFL coaches for the Arizona Cardinals. There are separate categories for each era of the franchise:

  • Category:Chicago Cardinals coaches (1920–1959)
  • Category:St. Louis Cardinals (football) coaches (1960–1987)
  • Category:Phoenix Cardinals coaches (1988–1993)
  • Category:Arizona Cardinals coaches (1994–)

Coaches should be included in each category that applies.
