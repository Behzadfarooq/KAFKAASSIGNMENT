Aristonicus of Tarentum

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Aristonicus (Latin; Greek Ἀριστόνικος Aristonikos) of Tarentum was the author of a work on Greek mythology which ancient sources often refer to.[1] He is perhaps the same as the one mentioned by Athenaeus 1.20, but nothing is known about him.

(Roulez, ad Ptolem. Hephaest. p. 148.)
