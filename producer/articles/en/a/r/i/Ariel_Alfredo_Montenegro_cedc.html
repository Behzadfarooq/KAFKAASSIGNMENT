Ariel Alfredo Montenegro Caseya (born 3 November 1975 in Buenos Aires) is an Argentine football (soccer) midfielder. He currently plays for Hércules CF in Spain.

He hold Italian passport.

[edit] Trivia

  • His shirt printed Ariel T.M.T., a given name and emoticon.
  • His surname Montenegro is a country of Europe.
