It may be a bit difficult to fathom my Babel correctly. Well, I have two mother tongues, German and French. But the language I practice better is German, and I can assure you that I do this at a professional level, because I've already written a book in that idiom. So I had to reduce my level in French, and now I have some very small difficulties when I use it, so I didn't introduce myself as a native.

I'm not a native speaker of English, and it's possible that I'll make from time to time a grammatical mistake. But please, do not edit my user page, and don't tell me on my discussion page that I've made this or that fault. But despite all, I am pretty sure that I'm going to have a funny time here ...

You might think that my username seems to be of Spanish origin. It is correct that I chose a name with a southern sound - I like it!
