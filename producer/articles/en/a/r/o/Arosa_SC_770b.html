Arosa Sociedad Cultural is a football team based in Vilagarcía de Arousa in the autonomous community of Galicia. Founded in 1945, it plays in the Tercera División - Group 1. Its stadium is A Lomba with a capacity of 5,000 seats.

[edit] Seasons

  • 2004/2005: Tercera División 15th
  • 2005/2006: Tercera División 7th
  • 2006/2007: Tercera División
  • 1 seasons in Segunda División
  • 7 seasons in Segunda División B
  • 43 seasons in Tercera División
