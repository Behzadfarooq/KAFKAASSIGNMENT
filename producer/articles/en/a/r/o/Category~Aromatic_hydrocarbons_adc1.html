Category:Aromatic hydrocarbons

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The main article for this category is Aromatic hydrocarbon.

An aromatic hydrocarbon or arene is a hydrocarbon, the molecular structure of which incorporates one or more planar sets of six carbon atoms that are connected by delocalised electrons, the simplest of which is benzene.
