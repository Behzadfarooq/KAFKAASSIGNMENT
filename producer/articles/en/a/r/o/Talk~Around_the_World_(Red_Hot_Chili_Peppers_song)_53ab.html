[edit] Profanities in the Lyrics?

When you listen to this song and look at the lyrics in the Cd cover theres a line that is clearly not what it says in the lyrics book. He says something like "...I must warn yah, bout the motherfuckin govern California" but in the lyric book is says "...I must warn yah,Bout the girls, From California " Is that just me or does anyone else hear that when this song is played?
