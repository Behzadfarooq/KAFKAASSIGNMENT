Arosvallen

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Arosvallen is a multi-use stadium in Vasteras, Sweden. It is currently used mostly for football matches. The stadium holds 10,000 people with 1200 seated. The stadium was built in 1932.

The stadium hosted two 1958 FIFA World Cup games were played, Yugoslavia - Scotland and Yugoslavia - France. It also hosted four group matches, a quarterfinal, and a semifinal in the 1995 FIFA Women's World Cup.
