Traveling Around the World is a type of travel where a person seeks to circumnavigate the globe.

"Around the World" is the title of several songs.

  • Around the World, a 1956 song from the movie Around the World in Eighty Days
  • All Around the World, a 1977 song by The Jam
  • All Around the World, a 1989 song by Lisa Stansfield
  • Around the World, a 1996 song by East 17
  • Around the World, a 1997 song by Daft Punk
  • All Around the World, a 1998 song by Oasis
  • Around the World, a 1998 song by Japanese electropop group M.O.V.E., also first theme tune of the anime Initial D.
  • Around the World, a 1999 song by Red Hot Chili Peppers
  • Around the World, a 2000 song by Aqua
  • Around the World (La La La La La), a 2001 song from Planet Pop (album) by ATC
  • All Around The World, a 2004 song by trance artists 4 Strings
  • Around the World, a 2005 song by Japanese singer Ami Suzuki
  • All Around the World, a 2007 song by Lionel Richie

Around the World may also refer to:

  • Around the World (a playground basketball game)
  • Around the World (Ami Suzuki album), an album by Japanese singer Ami Suzuki
  • Around the World (football trick), a trick in which the foot orbits the ball while in mid-air and continues to kick it up after.
  • Around the World (magazine), a magazine of popular geography in Russia and Soviet Union
  • Around the World (Mariah Carey DVD)
  • Around the World (cheerleading), a cheerleading jump.
  • Around the World (Yahel album), an album by Israeli trance artist Yahel Sherman
  • Around the World (yoyo trick), a yoyo trick in which the yoyo performs a full rotation representing something travelling around the world.
