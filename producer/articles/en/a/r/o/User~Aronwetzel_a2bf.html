[edit] Aron Wetzel

Aron Wetzel was born in Indianapolis[1], In on March 4th, 1982. Aron Wetzel is best known as a musician, poet, and a member in the United States Air Force. He was raised in an Irish American family. As a child he was very creative, which also led to misbehavior. Aron enrolled into martial arts, Tang Soo Do Moo Duk Kwan, where he received his black belt in 5 years. In his middle school years Aron quickly became more out spoken, and rambunctious. He began drinking, stealing, fighting, and hustling whatever he could make money off of. Aron earned the nickname Irish and or Crazy Irish. As Aron entered Southport High School, he was very well respected by many people. Then by his junior year he fell in love with an Amanda Roach. After Aron's graduation he moved out, and began college at IUPUI. Shortly after, Aron and Amanda moved in together. On Easter of 2003, Aron was arrested for theft, grand theft, and criminal trespassing. Charges were dropped, and his record would be clean, on the condition he joined the military. Aron quickly joined the USAF in Sept 16, 2003. Where he would then marry Amanda Roach, and have a beautiful baby girl. Aron's daughter, Haleigh Gabrielle, was born on July 22, 2004. Shortly after Haleigh's birth Amanda moved back to Indiana. After this, Aron began a heavy drinking downward spiral. He later then came out of this slump with putting his pain towards his music and poetry. Aron received Editor's Choice Award for his poem, Once A Dream. He is also know for his military history, and serving in Operation Enduring Freedom. Aron Wetzel could never find a true love to replace the woman that had shattered his heart, but he will always be there for his daughter, and his country.
