Aroostook Airways

Aroostook Airways was an airline based in Presque Isle, Maine in the 1970s. It offered commuter airline service throughout Maine and to Boston. The airline also offered charter and air ambulance service.

[edit] Former Destinations Served

  • Maine
    • Presque Isle (Northern Maine Regional Airport at Presque Isle) - hub
    • Augusta (Augusta State Airport)
    • Bangor (Bangor International Airport)
    • Madawaska/Fort Kent (Northern Aroostook Regional Airport)*
    • Portland (Portland International Jetport)
  • Connecticut
    • Hartford (Bradley International Airport)
  • Massachusetts
    • Boston (Logan International Airport)

Those airports marked with an asterisk (*) no longer have commercial airline service.
