Hi, I'm Arowen Half-Elven, daughter of Aragorn and Arwen, King and Queen of Gondor and of Arnor.

Basically, all you need to know about me here, is that I'm a writer and an artist. I write a lot of original stuff, usually in the fantasy genre and dealing with dragons, Elves, and other mythical creatures. I do a lot of work on fanfiction, particularly with "Avatar: The Last Airbender" and the "Kingdom Hearts" series. My stories are on fanfiction.net under the penname "Arowen Half-Elven".

I've posted some of my fan art (the good, the bad, and the don't-even-try) at deviantART.com under the name "Half-Elf". My scanner's not working right, so it's mostly some of my older stuff that I don't consider to be very good...but, hey, it's fun all the same.

I'm not for including shipping info in articles, but just for your information, I'm Zutara (Zuko/Katara), Taang (Toph/Aang), and Sokki (Sokka/Suki) all the way when it comes to Avatar. It is my personal belief that Zuko will join the gaang sometime early/mid-way next season as Aang's Firebending instructor. Pairings...those will probably wait til the end of the series. Oh well, still fun to take a guess!!! My stand on the season II finale...Zuko made a stupid move, and he's gonna regret it later, if he doesn't already. Azula's got designs on his throne, and he'll see it soon. It's only a matter of time before the whole arrangement falls apart.
