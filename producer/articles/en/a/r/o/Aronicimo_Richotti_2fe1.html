[edit] Biography

Aronicimo Richotti in his teenage years.

[edit] Early life

Born on January 9, 1979 in Rahway, New Jersey.

Attended high school at Holy Spirit High School in Absecon, New Jersey and Pinelands Regional School District in Tuckerton, New Jersey.

Worked at a marina in Tuckerton, New Jersey until 1999. Attended college in Atlanta, Georgia.(American InterContinental University)

First trained under Carlos Castro, out of the Doraville Boxing Gym in Doraville, Georgia in early 2000.

Currently trains out of the West End Boxing Club in Katy, Texas.
