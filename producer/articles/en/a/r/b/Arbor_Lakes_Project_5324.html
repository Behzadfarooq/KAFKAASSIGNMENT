The Shoppes at Arbor Lakes is a lifestyle center (inside-out mall) located on Hemlock Lane, Maple Grove, Minnesota, a suburb of Minneapolis, Minnesota. 'The Shoppes' have a number of notable restaurants and stores such as P.F. Chang's, California Pizza Kitchen, Pottery Barn, Williams-Sonoma, and Abercrombie and Fitch.

[edit] Overview

The Shoppes at Arbor Lakes lifestyle center attracts quite a few shoppers because it is surrounded by a number of other strip malls with other popular stores and restaurants such as Barnes and Noble, Byerly's, Best Buy, and a 16-screen cinema by AMC Theatres.
