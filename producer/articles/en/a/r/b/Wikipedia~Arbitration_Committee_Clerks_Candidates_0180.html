[edit] Inactive

Main article: Wikipedia:Arbitration Committee/Clerks

This page is no longer used for the appointment of arbitration committee clerks. There is no formal application process for becoming an arbitration clerk. Interested editors should contact one of the current clerks about assisting with opening and closing cases, who will consult with the arbitrators. Pending tasks will be coordinated through the Clerks' noticeboard. After getting some experience opening and closing cases, if you are interested in an official appointment and are willing to make a small but steady committment of your time, the clerks will submit your name to the arbitration committee.


The following page is kept as an archive. This procedure for selecting arbitration committee clerks is no longer active.


Applicants to become clerks, please list yourselves here. A *brief* summary (no more than 2 or 3 sentences) of your arbcom-related experiences might be helpful too - cases you participated in, or cleaned up evidence pages for. Raul654 16:06, 25 January 2006 (UTC)
