Arbroath Sporting Club

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Arbroath Sporting Club (commonly known as Arbroath SC) is a Scottish junior football club based in Arbroath. Their home ground is Seaton Park.

Up until the end of the 2005/6 season, they played in the Tayside Premier League of the Scottish Junior Football Association's Eastern Region.

The SJFA restructured prior to the 2006/7 season, and SC found themselves in the twelve-team East Region, North Division.

SC's arch-rivals are Arbroath Vics.
