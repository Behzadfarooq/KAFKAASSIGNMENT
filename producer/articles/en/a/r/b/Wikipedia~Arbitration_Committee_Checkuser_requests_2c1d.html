< Wikipedia:Arbitration Committee

The Arbitration Committee has decided to take requests for checkuser access from members of the community. Requests will be taken for a limited time (approximately one week) after which the Arbitration Committee will go over the list of applicants and decide who (if anyone) to give access to.

Requirements: A user must:

  1. Want the job
  2. Be a trusted member of the community in good standing
  3. Have the technical expertise to operate checkuser effectively
  4. Be willing to divulge his/her real name to the Foundation

Please direct any questions to the talk page. Raul654 03:36, 28 March 2007 (UTC)
