[edit] Arbuthnot Baronets of Edinburgh (1823)

Arms of the Arbuthnot Baronets, of Edinburgh

The Arbuthnot Baronetcy of Edinburgh was created in the Baronetage of the United Kingdom 3rd March 1823 for Sir William Arbuthnot, Provost of Edinburgh. The coat of arms of this Baronet includes supporters.

[edit] Incumbents

  • Sir William Arbuthnot, 1st Baronet (1776-1829)
  • Sir Robert Keith Arbuthnot, 2nd Baronet (1801-1873)
  • Sir William Wedderburn Arbuthnot, 3rd Baronet (1831-1889)
  • Sir Robert Keith Arbuthnot, 4th Baronet (1864-1916)
  • Sir Dalrymple Arbuthnot, 5th Baronet (1867-1941)
  • Sir Robert Dalrymple Arbuthnot, 6th Baronet (1919-1944)
  • Sir Hugh Fitzgerald Arbuthnot, 7th Baronet (1922-1983)
  • Sir Keith Robert Charles Arbuthnot, 8th Baronet (1951-)

Heir apparent: Robert Hugh Peter Arbuthnot (1986-)

[edit] Coat of arms

  • Arms: azure, a crescent between three mullets, two and one, argent; the whole within a bordure or, charged with three boar's heads couped gules.
  • Crest: a peacock's head proper.
  • Supporters: dexter, a wyvern vert vomiting flames; sinister, a greyhound argent, collared and line reflexed over the back gules.
  • Motto: Innocent and true.

[edit] Arbuthnot Baronets of Kittybrewster (1964)

Arms of the Arbuthnot Baronets, of Kittybrewster.

The Arbuthnot Baronetcy of Kittybrewster in the County of the City of Aberdeen was created in the Baronetage of the United Kingdom 26th February 1964 for John Sinclair Wemyss Arbuthnot, for services to church and state. The Baronetcy was one of the last to be created until 1991 (when Sir Denis Thatcher was created a Baronet).

[edit] Incumbents

  • Sir John Sinclair Wemyss Arbuthnot, 1st Baronet (1912-1992)
  • Sir William Reierson Arbuthnot, 2nd Baronet (1950-)

Heir presumptive: Rt Hon James Norwich Arbuthnot, MP (1952-)

[edit] Coat of arms

  • Arms: azure a crescent between three mullets argent, a bordure gules charged with two escallops in chief and a buck's head cabossed or in base, and in centre chief (overlapping bordure) an inescutcheon argent.
  • Crest: a peacock's head and neck proper, accompanied on either side by a spray of strawberry leaves vert, each flowered of a cinquefoil argent.
  • Motto: Deum laudans ('Praising God').
