[edit] Workshop

This is a page for working on Arbitration decisions. It provides for suggestions by Arbitrators and other users and for comment by arbitrators, the parties and others. After the analysis of [[Wikipedia:Requests for arbitration/{{{1}}}/Evidence|/Evidence]] here and development of proposed principles, findings of fact, and remedies. Anyone who edits should sign all suggestions and comments. Arbitrators will place proposed items they have confidence in on [[Wikipedia:Requests for arbitration/{{{1}}}/Proposed decision|/Proposed decision]].
