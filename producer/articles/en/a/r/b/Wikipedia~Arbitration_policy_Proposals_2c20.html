[edit] Current proposals

Please use the /Template page contents to draft new proposals and add list them below.

[edit] Discussion

These proposals are being written and discussed. Input is welcome during this stage.

  • /Evidence scope - regarding what evidence sources are appropriate for supporting a claim.
  • /Deferring matters - regarding what should the committee do if it the matter is outside their jurisidiction.
  • /Official mailing list - regarding whether the committee should have jurisdiction over the mailing list.
  • /Removing legalese - to remove legalese from the Arbitration policy (but not to change the policy substantively).
  • /Removing references to individual Wikipedians - to tidy up the policy by removing references to a couple of Wikipedians.

[edit] Preliminary poll

Final wording of these proposals is being considered. Successful proposals will move on to final voting.

[edit] Final vote
