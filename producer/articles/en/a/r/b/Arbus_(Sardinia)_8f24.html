Location of Arbus in Italy

Arbus is a comune (municipality) in the Province of Medio Campidano in the Italian region Sardinia, located about 60 km northwest of Cagliari and about 25 km west of Sanluri. As of 31 December 2004, it had a population of 6,895 and an area of 267.0 km².[1]

The municipality of Arbus contains the frazioni (subdivisions, mainly villages and hamlets) Montevecchio, Ingurtosu, and S.Antonio di Santadi.

Arbus borders the following municipalities: Fluminimaggiore, Gonnosfanadiga, Guspini, Terralba.
