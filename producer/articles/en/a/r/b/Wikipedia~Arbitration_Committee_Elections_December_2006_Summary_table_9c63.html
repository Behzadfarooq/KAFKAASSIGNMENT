[edit] Columns of this table

  • Vote Voting is now closed, but the pages linked will show the votes cast for each candidate.
  • Other links - u = user page; t = user talk page; c = contributions list; m = pages moved; l = permissions (can be used to confirm status, eg. administrator); b = use of blocks; p = pages protected; d = pages deleted. These last three actions only apply to administrators.
  • Note regarding block logs: (the links in some entries for the 'Notes' column) some of these blocks were by mistake or as a test so the existence of this link alone does not reflect on the candidate. Follow the link for more detailed information.
  • Examples: Links to examples where candidate exhibited arbitration skills.
  • Raw Votes: Compiled from the voting pages, where % is the percentage of yes votes.
  • Note regarding ages: there is no requirement to reveal age (or any other personal or identifying information) on Wikipedia. The ages provided here were compiled by reading the answers candidates provided, their responses to questions about their age (where asked) and information provided on their user pages.

[edit] General comments and statistics

  • Edit counts (as of 4 November) for most of the candidates, for those interested, can be found at Wikipedia:List of Wikipedians by number of edits.

37 candidates. 2 former arbitrators. 1 bureaucrat. 27 administrators. 2 former administrators. 8 non-administrators. 2 arbitration committee clerks (one inactive). 2 requests for checkuser clerks. 8 candidates with mediation cabal or mediation committee experience. Candidate age range (where revealed) is 14-56. Arrival years (number of candidates): 2002 (1); 2003 (2); 2004 (10); 2005 (20); 2006 (4).
