Arne Hovde

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Medal record
Men's ski jumping
World Championships
Silver 1934 Sollefteå Individual large hill

Arne Hovde was Norwegian ski jumper who competed in the early 1930's. He won a silver medal in the ski jumping individual large hill at the 1934 FIS Nordic World Ski Championships in Sollefteå.
