Arnaz Jerome Battle (born February 22, 1980 in Dallas, Texas) is an American football wide receiver who currently plays for the San Francisco 49ers. He went to the University of Notre Dame. He was drafted by the 49ers in the 6th round (197th overall) in 2003 NFL Draft.

Battle played quarterback for Notre Dame, but was converted to wide receiver because of his athletic ability.
