Arn Menconi is the District 2 Commissioner in Eagle County, Colorado

Mr. Menconi was elected to the Board of County Commissioners in November 2000 and took office on January 9, 2001. Reelected to a second term in November of 2004, Menconi will serve the county until January of 2009 and served Chairman of the Board for 2005. He was appointed to the board of directors of the Eagle River Water and Sanitation in 2004.

He believes our community's quality of life is sustained through a balance of social capital, economic prosperity and environmental health.

Menconi's efforts in social capital programs are seen through his work on various local, statewide and national boards. His top priority is Early Childhood Development where he plans to bring permanent funding for programs for children zero to eight years old.

He is the Vice Chairman for the Health and Human Services Committee for Colorado Counties, Inc. (CCI) and Chairman for CCI's Early Childhood Development Taskforce. Arn was recently appointed to his fourth term serving on the Human Services and Education Steering Committee for the National Association of Counties (NACo). He is a founding member of the Eagle County Youth Coalition.

Menconi's second priority is planning for and preserving our county's environment as is demonstrated in his positions on land use and water protection. He is the county's representative on the Northwest Colorado Council of Government's water group QQ (Quality and Quantity). He also serves on the Colorado Basin Roundtable, a statewide water planning group. These groups are working on creating a statewide water compact. Menconi also created campaigns against Referendum A and in favor of Recreational Instream Channel Diversions (RICDs). He is one of the most visible county advocates for open space and smart growth.

His vision and planning for economic prosperity is being executed locally through his co-founding of the Economic Council of Eagle County and as an Executive Director of the Vail Valley Chamber and Tourism Bureau. He is the President of the Eagle County Air Terminal Authority and on the board of directors for Eagle County and Roaring Fork Transit Authority. He is developing awareness for environmental health through creation of the "Shaping the Future of the Eagle Valley", an eight week course in partnership with Colorado Mountain College. He is president of the Partnership for Education.

Menconi received the "Best of the Vail Daily" for Civic Leader. Arn has been a resident of Eagle County since 1991 and lives in Eagle Ranch with his wife, Anne, daughter Maya and is expecting a son in July. He is founder and executive director of the Snowboard Outreach Society and has a Master's Degree in Business Administration from the University of Denver. His favorite pastimes are snowboarding, road biking and mountain biking.
