Arnaud Desplechin

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Arnaud Desplechin (born October 31, 1960) is a French film director.

[edit] Filmography

  • (2007) Un conte de Noël (filming)
  • (2004) Kings and Queen
  • (2003) Léo en jouant « Dans la compagnie des hommes »
  • (2000) Esther Kahn
  • (1996) My Sex Life ... or How I Got into an Argument
  • (1992) The Sentinel
  • (1991) La Vie des morts
