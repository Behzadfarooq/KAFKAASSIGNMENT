Arnedo

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Coat of arms of Arnedo

Arnedo, La Rioja, Spain, is the third largest city in La Rioja, near Calahorra, with a population of about 14,000 people.

Its economy is based in the shoe industry. It is a traditional red city, with a good standard of living. It also is acquiring more importance than Calahorra, especially in the area of culture. The "La Quincalla" and "La Cuesta" associations are very good examples of the traditional associational spirit of the city, which has given to the city its cultural character.
