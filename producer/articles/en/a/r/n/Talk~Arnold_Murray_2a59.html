[edit] discernment

I watch the Shepherd's Chapel on television pretty frequently. The man teaches The Trinity; not modalism. I have seen many references to this most inaccurate label of blasphemy on several web sites and it simply is not true.

He has said that Cain is not in Adam's geneology -- and has said that it was Satan's seduction of Eve which resulted in his birth. I suppose the 'Serpent Seed' label is appropriate but I have never heard him use it. He does use the word Kenite to describe the 'Sons of Cain' and he backs these statements with specific references to scripture.

The bulk of the criticisms I read involve disagreement with what is said vis a vis literal interpretation of what is written in The Word.

Arnold Murray also teaches discernment. He espouses cultivation of an ability to experience an interpretation then make up your own mind on the matter and never stands in judgement of others; as so many critics have stood in judgement of him.

You cannot take the Bible in a strictly literal sense due to the translations as well as idioms, colloquialisms, and other figures of speech of the period in time -- as well as the locale at which it was written.

Much ridicule of his teachings have turned out to be based on somebody interpreting literally when a figurative reference is made -- or worse when a reference is misinterpreted.

He never actively solicits donations to his ministry and frequently admonishes people to never tithe or make offerings that adversely affect their finances. He does have tapes, CDs, and books for sale -- but so do other ministers on television. I haven't bought any of the tapes or CDs but I have bought a book or two.

Some of his language does border on coarse; but there by the grace of God go I.

I don't necessarily agree with everything Arnold Murry teaches but find him to be an honest and refreshing oasis of realism in religion today.

As for those who would disagree; there comes a time when all will be known and judgement is not ours. Being incorrect is not a sin. Bearing the false witness of blasphemy is. I'm not perfect, just forgiven.

As a matter of record, the only group of which I am a member is with the "Chosin Few" which is a group of Korean War veterans comprised of various military factions who fought in one of the more famous battles of that war. I am very proud to have served as a U.S. Marine in this severe winter battle in which approximately 12,000 of our troops faced a massive surprise attack by an estimated 120,000 of the Chinese communist enemy.
