Arnhem Highway

Arnhem Highway is signed as State Route 36.

The Arnhem Highway is a highway in the Northern Territory of Australia. It links the mining town of Jabiru, in Kakadu National Park, to the Stuart Highway 22 miles (35km) south of Darwin.
