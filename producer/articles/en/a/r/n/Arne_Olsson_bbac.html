Arne Olsson

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Arne Olsson is a retired pastor in the Lutheran Church of Sweden. On 5 February in Gothenburg, 2005 he was consecrated as bishop of The Mission Province by The Most Reverend Walter Obare, the presiding bishop of the Evangelical Lutheran Church in Kenya.
