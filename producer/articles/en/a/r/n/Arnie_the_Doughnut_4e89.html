Arnie the Doughnut is the third book by American children's author and illustrator Laurie Keller. Released in 2003 by Henry Holt and Co., it is the story of a doughnut named Arnie, who attempts to avoid his fate after being purchased by Mr. Bing.

The book was a choice of the Children's Book of the Month Club.
