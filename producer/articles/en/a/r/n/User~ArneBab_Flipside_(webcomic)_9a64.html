Flipside is a fantasy webcomic written and illustrated by Brion Foulke, about reationships, sex and compromise.

It is about the story of two women: Maytag, who is a bisexual jester girl with great skills with throwing knives and magic; and Bernadette, who is a lesbian swordswoman of unmatched skills with her own unique fighting style, the Split Rose. The two are in love, and their relationship is troubled in many ways. Maytag is a nymphomaniac, she cannot restrain herself from externating her libido; Bernadette's dream is to become a knight of the city of Solstice, where the comic is set, but that order is not open to women, let alone lesbians. Plus, the knight of Solstice fight against all kind of wizardry, and Maytag herself possesses some magic skills, thus theorically putting the two of them on two opposite sides.
