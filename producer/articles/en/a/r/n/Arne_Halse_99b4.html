Arne Halse (October 20, 1887 - July 3, 1975) is a Norwegian athlete who competed mainly in the Javelin.

He competed for a Norway in the 1908 Summer Olympics held in London, Great Britain in the Javelin throw where he won a silver medal. He also competed in the Javelin throw free style where he won the Bronze medal.
