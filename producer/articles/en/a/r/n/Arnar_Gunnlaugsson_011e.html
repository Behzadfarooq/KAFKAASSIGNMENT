Arnar Bergmann Gunnlaugsson (born March 3, 1973 in Akranes) is an Icelandic professional footballer. His position is forward.

During his career he has enjoyed spells at Leicester City, Stoke City, Bolton Wanderers, IA Akranes, Nürnberg, Feyenoord and Dundee United.

He has been capped 32 times and scored 3 goals for Iceland between 1993 and 2003. [1]

His twin brother is Bjarki Gunnlaugsson.
