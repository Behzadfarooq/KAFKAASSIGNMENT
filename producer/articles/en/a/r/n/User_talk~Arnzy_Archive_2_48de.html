CityTrain box

Just a comment on your latest additions to the CityTrain template. I think they are very confusing. The box under the station picture that has something like "Brisbane South" could easily be thought to be the line on which the station is located or something else, unless you're familiar with the network itself. I suggest that it either have an explanation of what it is, or you change it back. Wouldn't the TransLink region be better served as one of the infobox attributes (ie. along with suburb, street, etc.)?
