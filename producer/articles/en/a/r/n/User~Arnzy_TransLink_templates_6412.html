[edit] Nambour and Gympie North Line Stopping Patterns

  • All stops
    • A - All stations to Brisbane Central & vice-versa (early morning, late evening (weekdays))*
    • S - All stations from Nambour to Caboolture & vice-versa. (twice-daily off-peak)
  • Peak inbound & outbound
    • X - All stations to Caboolture, express to Bowen Hills, then all stations to Roma Street
  • Off-peak inbound & outbound
    • O - All stations to Caboolture, express to Petrie, express to Northgate, express to Bowen Hills, then all stations to Roma Street & vice-versa (off-peak)
  • Peak outbound
    • P - Roma Street, Central, Brunswick St, Bowen Hills, express to Northgate, express to Petrie, then all stations from Petrie to Nambour (one afternoon outbound peak service only)
    • G - Roma Street, Central, Brunswick St, Bowen Hills, Elimbah then all stations to Gympie North (stopping at Caboolture to pick-up pax only).

Most services terminate at Roma Street. Shuttles terminate at Caboolture, and early morning and late evening all-station services originate or continue from southbound lines.
