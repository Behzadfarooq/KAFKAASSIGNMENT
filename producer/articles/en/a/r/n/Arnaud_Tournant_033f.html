Arnaud Tournant

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Arnaud Tournant (born 5 April 1978) is a French track cyclist born in Roubaix. He is a twelve time World Champion and won a gold, a silver and a bronze medal at the Summer Olympics.

[edit] Career highlights

  • 1996: 2nd in UCI Track World Championships - sprint (juniors)
  • 1997: 1st in UCI Track World Championships - team sprint
  • 1998: 1st in UCI Track World Championships - 1 km time trial
  • 1998: 1st in UCI Track World Championships - team sprint
  • 1999: 1st in UCI Track World Championships - 1 km time trial
  • 1999: 1st in UCI Track World Championships - team sprint
  • 2000: 1st in 2000 Summer Olympics - 750 m time trial
  • 2000: 1st in UCI Track World Championships - 1 km time trial
  • 2000: 1st in UCI Track World Championships - team sprint
  • 2001: World record 1 km time trial in La Paz, 58.875
  • 2001: 1st in UCI Track World Championships - 1 km time trial
  • 2001: 1st in UCI Track World Championships - sprint
  • 2001: 1st in UCI Track World Championships - team sprint
  • 2002: 2nd in UCI Track World Championships - 1 km time trial
  • 2002: 2nd in World cup classic Sydney - sprint
  • 2002: 2nd in World cup classic Sydney - team sprint
  • 2003: 2nd in French national track cycling championships - 1 km time trial
  • 2003: 3rd in French national track cycling championships - sprint
  • 2003: 3rd in UCI Track World Championships - 1 km time trial
  • 2003: 2nd in UCI Track World Championships - team sprint
  • 2004: 2nd in UCI Track World Championships - 1 km time trial
  • 2004: 1st in UCI Track World Championships - team sprint
  • 2004: 2nd in World cup classic Aguascalientes - 1 km time trial
  • 2004: 1st in World cup classic Aguascalientes - team sprint
  • 2004: 1st in French national track cycling championships - 1 km time trial
  • 2004: 2nd in French national track cycling championships - sprint
  • 2004: 2nd in 2004 Summer Olympics - 1 km time trial
  • 2004: 3rd in 2004 Summer Olympics - team sprint
  • 2005: 2nd in World cup classic Los Angeles - keirin
  • 2005: 2nd in World cup classic Los Angeles - sprint
  • 2005: 3rd in World cup classic Los Angeles - team sprint
  • 2005: 3rd in World cup classic Sydney - sprint
  • 2005: 1st in World cup classic Sydney - team sprint
  • 2005: 1st in French national track cycling championships - sprint
  • 2005: 2nd in French national track cycling championships - keirin
  • 2006: 3rd in World cup classic Sydney - sprint
  • 2006: 2nd in World cup classic Sydney - team sprint
  • 2006: 3rd in UCI Track World Championships - keirin
  • 2006: 1st in UCI Track World Championships - team sprint
  • 2006: 3rd in French national track cycling championships - sprint
  • 2007: 3rd in Masters of Sprint
