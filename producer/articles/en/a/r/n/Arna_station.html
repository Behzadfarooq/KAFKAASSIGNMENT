Arna is the second largest railway station in Bergen, Norway. The station is located in the Arna borough on the east side of Ulriken and between two tunnels. The present station was opened in 1964, when the Ulriken tunnel was completed. The old station, located north of the new station, is still in use for heritage trains on Gamle Vossebanen.

All passenger trains on Bergensbanen stop at Arna. The most frequent trains are shuttle trains which run between the Bergen Railway Station and Arna, providing the fastest connection between downtown Bergen and Arna.
