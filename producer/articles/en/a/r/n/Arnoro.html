Arnoro is a charter airline based in Sarajevo, Bosnia and Herzegovina. It was established in 2004 and plans to operate services from Sarajevo and Banja Luka to Stockholm, Gothenburg, Oslo and Copenhagen in 2005.

[edit] Services

Arnoro currently operates one charter service from Sarajevo to Istanbul (as of January 2005).
