Arne Hoel

Arne Hoel was a Norwegian ski jumper who competed in the 1940s and 1950s.

He won the ski jumping event at the Holmenkollen ski festival three times (1948, 1951 and 1959). Because of his successes, Hoel was awarded the Holmenkollen medal in 1956 (Shared with Borghild Niskin and Arnfinn Bergmann.).

He also finished 6th in the individual large hill event at the 1952 Winter Olympics in Oslo.
