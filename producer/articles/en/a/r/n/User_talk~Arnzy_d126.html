[edit] Melbourne Airport (Air NZ Destinations)

Hi there

Sorry, could I just clarify something please regarding Air New Zealand's flights from Melbourne to San Fransisco and Rarotonga.

I had a look at the like you posted regarding the Airports WikiProject destinations listing guide for direct and non-stop flights. I believe the NZ flights from MEL to SFO and RAR fits the direct flight requirement because they maintain the same flight number throughout, are operated by the same aircraft and passengers may disembark at both the intermediate and final destinations.

NZ008 MEL-AKL-SFO daily B777 (begins March 25 2007) NZ842 MEL-CHC-RAR every Friday A320 (begins March 30 2007)

This information is taken from both Air New Zealand and Melbourne Airport's websites.
