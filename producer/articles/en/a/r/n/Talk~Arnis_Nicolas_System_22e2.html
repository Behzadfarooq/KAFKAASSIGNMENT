This article is terrible. The whole point of wikipedia is to provide an overview and important detail of a subject - not any and all information. Registering with the government is a requirement not anything notable. Why is this art important - that needs to be made clear. Right now it reads like an over enthusiastic practitioner with no thought to structure - trivial points are given equal weight to more imporatant ones and there are no sources. After looking at the article I am left with the feeling that it is small fish trying to swim with the bigger ones. There are plenty of good escrima articles to use as example and a careful rewrite would stop me from listing this as an article for deletion debate.Peter Rehse 01:48, 8 March 2007 (UTC)
