[edit] Plot

Arn Magnusson is a Swedish Knight Templar who goes on the crusade to free Jerusalem.

[edit] Procuction

Filming locations are Västergötland and Morocco.

The Swedish version will be in two parts, and edited together into one film for the international market.

[edit] Cast

  • Joakim Nätterquist as Arn Magnusson.
  • Sofia Helin
  • Stellan Skarsgård
  • Mads Mikkelsen is rumored to play the King of Denmark.
