Arntor is an album by Windir released in 1999 (see 1999 in music). It is the last record released when Windir was still a one-man band.

[edit] Track listing

  1. "Byrjing" (The Beginning) – 3:17
  2. "Arntor, ein windir" (Arntor, a warrior) – 6:56
  3. "Kong Hydnes Haug" (The burial mound of King Hydnes) – 6:37
  4. "Svartesmeden og Lundamyrstrollet" (The Blacksmith and the Troll of Lundamyri) – 9:02
  5. "Kampen" (The Struggle) – 6:36
  6. "Saknet" (The longing) – 10:03
  7. "Ending" – 3:38
