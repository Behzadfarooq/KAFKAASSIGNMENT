Arne Arnardo, born Arne Otto Lorang Andersen (Sarpsborg, October 12, 1912 - 1995), was a Norwegian circus performer and -owner.

Arnardo worked as equilibrist, trapeze artist, contortionist, illusionist and ventriloquist, before founding the circus Arnardo in 1949. He played in the movie Cirkus Fandango in 1954, and wrote the book Sirkusliv (Circus Life) in 1962.
