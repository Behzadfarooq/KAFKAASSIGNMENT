Arnold Kerwanty

Arnold Kerwanty is a rugby league player from Papua New Guinea. He moved to Wagga Wagga, in New South Wales, Australia to start a job at the RAAF base. Whilst based here he played for the Wagga Brothers club.

Kerwanty's greatest achievement was representing Papua New Guinea against Australia in an exhibition match in 1988.
