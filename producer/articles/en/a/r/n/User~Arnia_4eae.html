My name is Joe Geldart and I'm a doctoral candidate at the University of Durham, United Kingdom. My research interests are large and varied ranging from linguistics, especially cognitive linguistics, to research into models of computation. Currently I'm researching computational models of cognition based upon ideas from connectionism.


[edit] Planned articles

As my current research concentration is on self-organizing maps, the following articles suggest themselves at this time:

- Neural Gas (SOM variant)

- Growing Neural Gas (SOM variant)

- Trace Feature Maps (SOM variant)

- Hierarchical Feature Maps (SOM variant)

- Recursive Self-Organizing Maps (SOM variant)
