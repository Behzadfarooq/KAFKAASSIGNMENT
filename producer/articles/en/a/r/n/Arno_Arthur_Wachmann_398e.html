Arno Arthur Wachmann (March 8, 1902 – July 24, 1990) was a German astronomer.

With Arnold Schwassmann he co-discovered the periodic comets 29P/Schwassmann-Wachmann, 31P/Schwassmann-Wachmann and 73P/Schwassmann-Wachmann.
