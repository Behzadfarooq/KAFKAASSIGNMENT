Arnold

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

For other uses, see Arnold (disambiguation).

[edit] People with the surname Arnold

  • Benedict Arnold, American Revolution traitor supporting Britain
  • Dominique Arnold, American hurdling athlete
  • Eberhard Arnold, German Writer, Philosopher, and Theologian
  • Jennette Arnold, Member of the Greater London Assembly
  • Malcolm Arnold, British classical music composer
  • Matthew Arnold, British poet and cultural critic
  • Lucas Arnold, Argentine tennis player
  • Tom Arnold (actor), American actor and comedian
  • Vladimir Arnold, Russian mathematician
  • Russel Arnold, Sri Lankan cricketer

[edit] People with the given name Arnold

  • Arnold Böcklin, symbolist Swiss painter
  • Arnold Brown, the 11th General of The Salvation Army
  • Arnold Fruchtenbaum, theologian and founder of Ariel Ministries
  • Arnold Jackson (1891-1972), British athlete
  • Arnold Palmer, American golfer
  • Arnold Rüütel, Soviet politician, president of Estonia
  • Arnold Schoenberg, composer
  • Arnold Schwarzenegger, Austrian-born American bodybuilder, actor, and politician
  • Saint Arnold, name of several saints

[edit] Fictional

  • Arnold Jackson, Gary Coleman's character in the "Diff'rent Strokes" TV series
  • Arnold Rimmer, a character from Red Dwarf
  • Arnold Shortman, title character from Hey Arnold!
  • Arnold Ziffel, a character on Green Acres
