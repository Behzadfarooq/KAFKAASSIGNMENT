Arlene McCarthy

Arlene McCarthy (born October 10, 1960, Belfast, Northern Ireland) is a Member of the European Parliament for North West England for the Labour Party. She has been a member of the European Parliament since 1994.

She used to work at Kirklees Metropolitan Borough Council.
