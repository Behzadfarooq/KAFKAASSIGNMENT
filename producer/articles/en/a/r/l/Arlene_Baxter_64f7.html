Arlene Baxter (born November 27, 1962 in Oceanside, California) is an American model and actress. She was chosen as Playboy's Playmate of the Month for December 1993.

As a model, Baxter has worked for Victoria Secret, Saks Fifth Avenue, Avon and others.
