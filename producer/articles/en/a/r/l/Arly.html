Mouth Isère River

Basin countries France
Length 38 km

The Arly is a 38 km long river of France. It is a right tributary of the Isère River, which it joins in Albertville.

[edit] Towns crossed by the river

  • Megève
  • Praz-sur-Arly
  • Flumet
  • Saint-Nicolas-la-Chapelle
  • Crest-Voland
  • Héry-sur-Ugine
  • Cohennoz
  • Ugine
  • Marthod
  • Albertville

[edit] Tributaries include

  • Arrondine
  • Flon
  • Chaise
  • Doron de Beaufort
