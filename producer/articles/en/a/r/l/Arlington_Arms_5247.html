Arlington Arms is a seven man indie rock band from Butler, Pennsylvania consisting of Christoper Wagner, Neil Convery, Nathan Renwick, Kenneth Glasgow, Daniel Flecker, Phillip Neyman and Cody Tyler. They have released two EPs and have played in various concerts with bands such as: Emery, Dizmas, The Showdown, and Terra Firma. They are also known as the "A Squared".
