Arlington, Arizona

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Arlington is a small unincorporated community in Maricopa County, Arizona, located about 40 miles west of Phoenix. It follows the street numbering system of Phoenix. Arlington is included in the 85322 zipcode, the population of which was 470 as of the 2000 census.
