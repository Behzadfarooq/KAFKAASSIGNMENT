Arlington Central School District

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Arlington Central School District (also known as the ACSD) is one of thirteen public school districts serving residents of Dutchess County, New York. It has eight elementary schools, three middle schools, one comprehensive high school, and other institutions of various types. As of 2005, it had nearly 10,000 students.

The district's territory covers substantial parts of the Towns of Beekman, La Grange, Pleasant Valley, Poughkeepsie, and Union Vale. The district also includes small parts of the Towns of East Fishkill, Hyde Park, Pawling and Wappinger. The district's offices are located in Canterbury Plaza in the Town of Poughkeepsie, and its current superintendent is Frank V. Pepe Jr.
