'Arlington Senior High' of St. Paul, is a public-education establishment in the US state of Minnesota. Built in 1996, it was to house a teaching program dubbed "smaller learning communities". These smaller learning communities would essentially partition particular student interests into different areas of the school, providing goal/interest oriented learning. Since then, several other high schools have established their own smaller learning communities.
