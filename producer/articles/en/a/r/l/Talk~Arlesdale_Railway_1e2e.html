[edit] Siegfried (basis:Synolda)

Why did you remove Siegfried from this page? He is from Own Character Ideas And Images. Felix 18:46, 20 July 2006

Felix, the answer is the same here as it is everywhere. You cannot include any of those characters in any Wikipedia article as they are fan fiction. Adding any of those characters to an article would be vandalism. Gwernol 23:48, 20 July 2006 (UTC)

I see. Felix 9:25, 21 July 2006

In Own Character Ideas And Images, it's claimed Siegfried is a tender engine and is painted blue (like Bert) and has an Atlantic (4-4-2) wheel format. However, in the Ravenglass and Eskdale Railway, there was a locomotive with a tender, painted blue and has a 4-4-2 wheel configuration. The locomotive is Synolda. I think Siegfried is probably based on Synolda. Felix 18:20, 26 July 2006

[edit] Arlesdale End - possible ref from TV Series?

A recent edit (since removed) noted that:

The railway never appeared in the The TV Series, although a location Arlesdale End was mentioned in the Season 10 episode Toby's New Shed.

According to the entry in Fictional Locations (Thomas the Tank Engine and Friends), Arlesdale was the location of Toby's branch line before he came to Sodor (apparently mentioned in Season 1). In which case there is no real reason to link 'Arlesdale End' with the Arlesdale Railway on Sodor.

However, proof to the contrary is welcome.

EdJogg 13:34, 26 February 2007 (UTC)
