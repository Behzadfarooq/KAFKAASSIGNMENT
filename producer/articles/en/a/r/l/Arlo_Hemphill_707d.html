Arlo Hanlin Hemphill (born October 7, 1971 in Baltimore, Maryland) is a California-based marine biologist and conservationist. Arlo is currently the Vice President for Conservation at Deep Search, a non-profit entity founded by the ocean explorer Dr. Sylvia Earle, and dedicated to the conservation and exploration of the global ocean.
