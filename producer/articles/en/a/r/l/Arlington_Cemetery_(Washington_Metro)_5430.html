Arlington Cemetery is a Washington Metro station in Arlington, Virginia on the Blue Line.

The station is located at the entrance to Arlington Cemetery, near Memorial Drive. There is no public parking near station except at the cemetery, which is reserved for visitors. It is the only station that closes earlier than the rest of the system. Service began on July 1, 1977.
