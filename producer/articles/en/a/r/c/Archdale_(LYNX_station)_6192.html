The Archdale station is a light rail station on the LYNX light rail system in Charlotte, North Carolina.

The station is located along Old Pineville Road at Archdale Drive just to the west of South Boulevard. It has side platforms, which sit on either side of the tracks. Additionally, this station will include 432 commuter parking spaces.[1]
