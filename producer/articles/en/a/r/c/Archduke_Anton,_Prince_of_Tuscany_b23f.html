Princess Ileana of Romania and Archduke Anton of Austria

Archduke Anton of Austria, Prince Imperial of Austria, Prince Royal of Hungary and Bohemia, Prince of Tuscany (1901-1987) was married Princess Ileana of Romania.

They had the following children:

  • Archduke Stefan of Austria-Tuscany (born 1932)
  • Archduchess Maria Ileana of Austria-Tuscany (born 1933)
  • Archduchess Alexandra of Austria-Tuscany (born 1935)
  • Archduke Dominic of Austria-Tuscany (born 1937)
  • Archduchess Maria Magdalena of Austria-Tuscany (born 1939)
  • Archduchess Elisabeth of Austria-Tuscany (born 1942)
