Archie Corrigan is a fictional comic book character from the Marvel Comics universe. He is a friend of Patch and is a pilot. He owns and operates South Seas Skyways, of which he was the entire company. He lives in Madripoor.

[edit] Powers and abilities

Although he had no actual super powers, Archie was a good fighter.

[edit] Notes

  • Archie had an older brother, Burt, who was somewhat insane. Burt would get obsessed with a movie and start living it.
