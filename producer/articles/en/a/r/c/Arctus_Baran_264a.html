Arctus Baran

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Arctus Baran
Gender: male
Rank: Captain
Portrayed by: Richard Lynch

In the fictional Star Trek universe, Arctus Baran was the captain of a mercenary ship in 2370. He was noted for his ruthless tactics and lethal punishments. He was fond of using neural servos, which he implanted in all of the people who served in his crew.

In mid-2370, Baran was hired by members of a Vulcan isolationist movement to retrieve fragments of the fabled Stone of Gol. Baran was killed by his own servo after Jean-Luc Picard changed the transponder codes of the servos.

Arctus Baran was played by Richard Lynch.
