Archbold High School

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Archbold High School is a public high school in Archbold, Ohio, United States. It is the only high school in the Archbold Area Schools district. Their nickname is the Bluestreaks. They are members of the Northwest Ohio Athletic League.

[edit] History

The New Archbold High School was erected in 1990. It sits on LaFayette street, right next to the new elementary school, which was built in 1997. The High School has had several locations around our school district. It was located at the current middle school, which sits on Holland Street, but now sits on Lafayette. Former students used to ride the bus out to the school in Ridgeville. That building currently serves as the "Christ Community Church"

[edit] Notable alumni

  • 2006 Indianapolis 500 and IndyCar Series champion Sam Hornish, Jr. is a graduate of the school.
