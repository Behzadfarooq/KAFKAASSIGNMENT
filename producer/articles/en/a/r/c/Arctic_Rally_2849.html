Arctic Rally

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Arctic Rally, currently Arctic Lapland Rally and also known as Tunturiralli, is an annual rally competition held in Rovaniemi, Lapland, Finland. It has been organized continuously since 1966. The rally was part of the World Rally Championship's FIA Cup for Drivers in 1977 and 1978.[1] Since then it was a round in the European Rally Championship until 2004. Currently, it is a rally in the Finnish national championship.

The rally has been won by five World Rally Champions: Marcus Grönholm, Tommi Mäkinen, Hannu Mikkola, Timo Salonen and Ari Vatanen.[2] In recent years, it has attracted competitors from circuit racing, such as Mika Häkkinen, JJ Lehto, Keke Rosberg and Derek Warwick.
