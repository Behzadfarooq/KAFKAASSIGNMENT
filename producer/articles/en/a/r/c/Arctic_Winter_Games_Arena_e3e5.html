Arctic Winter Games Arena

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Arctic Winter Games Arena is a 2,500-seat multi-purpose arena in Iqaluit, Nunavut, Canada. It is home to the Arctic Winter Games. Hosted CBC's Hockey Day in Canada in 2003.
