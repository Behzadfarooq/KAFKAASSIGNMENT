Arcadia Lake (Oklahoma)

Arcadia Lake is an artificial lake in Northeastern Oklahoma County, Oklahoma, USA. The lake is northeast of Oklahoma City.

The lake has a surface area of 1,820 acres and has about 26 miles of shoreline.

The lake was created in 1987 by an earthen dam on the Deep Fork River begun in 1980. The lake was created to control floods, create a water supply, and provide recreational resources.
