Archibald the Koala is an animatic animated children's television series produced by Millimages. The show began airing in 1998.[1]

[edit] About

This Series is set on a secluded island called Rastepappe it is populated by Koalas and Badgers. Its city Koalaville is filled with all strange characters including a Nervous Mayor, a hopeless Inventor and even a bossy Painter.

There are always strange things going on so Archibald, the trustful Detective is put on the job!
