The Arcata Transit Center is a bus station in Arcata, California. It is located at 925 E Street, between 9th and 10th Streets.

The center is served by four bus companies. Arcata and Mad River Transit offers service around Arcata, Redwood Coast Transit offers service to Crescent City, California, and Thruway Motorcoach stops at the center on its way from the San Joaquins in Martinez to the Arcata-Eureka Airport. The station's Amtrak code is (ARC). Greyhound Lines also calls at the station.
