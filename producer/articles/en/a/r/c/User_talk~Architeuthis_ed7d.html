[edit] Pelevin's new novel title

Excuse me, but how do you know exactly that it should be spelled with initial "E"? The novel hasn't been formally published even in Russian yet, let alone in English. Dart evader 04:34, 22 October 2006 (UTC)

  • It's been distributed on the Web under this title (Empire V, not Ампир В); also, it makes sense - "Empire" is a French word which denotes a certain style in architecture, art and furniture, related to the French empire, and is pronounced "ahm-peer", whereas there is no such word as "Ampir" in either Russian or English. Architeuthis 00:50, 23 October 2006 (UTC)
OK. I thought that the bootlegged text was titled "Ампир В" and the "Empire V" was just a literal translation. So I guessed that "Ампир В" was originally supposed to be an anagram of "Вампир". Perhaps I was wrong. We'll see when the book is officially released. Dart evader 04:48, 23 October 2006 (UTC)
