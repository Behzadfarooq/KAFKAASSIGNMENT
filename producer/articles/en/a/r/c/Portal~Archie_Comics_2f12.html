The Archie Comics Portal

This portal has been created as a way to organize and provide a general entry point for articles relating to Archie Comics. This portal is currently under construction. Any help you can provide would be greatly appreciated.

Archie Comics is an American comic book publisher based in Mamaroneck, New York. The company was originally known as MLJ comics (MLJ being the first initials of its founders), which was best known for its superhero comics featuring The Shield. After seeing the popularity of Archie, the company changed its name to Archie Comics and focused more on the Archie character and his friends.
