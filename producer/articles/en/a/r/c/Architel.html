Architel

Architel Holdings LLC is an American professional services company. Headquartered in Dallas, Texas, Architel provides computer and network support. Architel' wholly owned subsidiary, Big in Japan, is a social media company focused on the development of social tools written using Ruby on Rails.

Architel operates multiple data center and network operation facilities with personnel in more than six countries around the globe. The company's headquarters is located in the Infomart.

Architel has relationships with technology providers including Microsoft Corporation, Dell and Intel amongst others.

[edit] Products

Architel developed Simpleticket, a trouble ticket system written using Ruby on Rails and released it using the GPL open source license.


[edit] Services

  • Network Monitoring
  • Network Support
  • Desktop Support
  • Online Backup
  • Hosted VoIP PBX (Asterisk based)
  • Hosted SPAM filtering
  • Mail and Website Hosting
  • Collocation

[edit] International Presence

Architel offers services exclusively in the United States with personnel in the Philippines, India, Brazil, Argentina, France and Russia. The company is privately owned by partners located in the Dallas, Texas, area.
