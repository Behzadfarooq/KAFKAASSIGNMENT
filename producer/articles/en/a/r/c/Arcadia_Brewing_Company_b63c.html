[edit] History

The Arcadia Brewing Company (also known as Arcadia Ales) is a brewery and pub in Battle Creek, MI. The pub opened in 1996 in Battle Creek, and its products are distributed in Michigan, Illinois, Indiana, Ohio and Wisconsin.


[edit] Awards

1998 World Beer Championships

  • Angler's Ale - Bronze Medal
  • Whitsun - Silver Medal
  • India Pale Ale - Gold Medal

2003 World Beer Championships

  • London Porter - Gold Medal
  • Starboard Stout - Silver Medal
  • Imperial Stout - Silver Medal
