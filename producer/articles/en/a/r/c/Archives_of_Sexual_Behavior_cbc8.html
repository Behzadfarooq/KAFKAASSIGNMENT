Archives of Sexual Behavior is an academic sexology journal and the official publication of the International Academy of Sex Research.

Contributions consist of empirical research (both quantitative and qualitative), theoretical reviews and essays, clinical case reports, letters to the editor, and book reviews.

[edit] Editorial board
