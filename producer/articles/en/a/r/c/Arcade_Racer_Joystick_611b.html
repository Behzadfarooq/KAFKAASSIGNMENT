Arcade Racer was a steering wheel type of joystick for the Sega Saturn video game console, helpful when playing racing games. Unlike most controllers at that time which were digital, the Arcade Racer is analog. This gives the controller a smoother response. The controller works with a variety of Sega Saturn games including:

  • Time Warner Interactive's V.R. Virtua Racing
  • Sega's Daytona USA.
