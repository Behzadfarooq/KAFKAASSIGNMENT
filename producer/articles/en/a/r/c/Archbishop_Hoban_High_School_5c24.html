Archbishop Hoban High School is a private, Catholic high school in Akron, Ohio. It is a part of the Roman Catholic Diocese of Cleveland.

[edit] Notable Alumni

Butch Reynolds - Olympic medalist
Bob Neidert - Former University of Notre Dame defensive end
Chuck Mesko - Former Penn State University defensive back and former Hoban record holder for rushing.
Tyrell Sutton - Starting running back for Northwestern University and all-time record-holder for the state of Ohio in rushing (9,426 yards).

[edit] Ohio High School Athletic Association State Championships

  • Boys Basketball – 1989 [1]
  • Boys Track and Field – 1992, 1993
  • Girls Basketball – 1988
  • Girls Softball – 1983, 1984, 1986, 1987, 1991, 1992 [2]
  • Girls Volleyball – 1980, 1990, 1991, 1992[3]
