Archwood School is a Kindergarten to Grade 8 multi-age school with a student population of 134 students, and 28 dedicated staff members. It also features a Family Centre which offers programs for parents and pre-school aged children. The school also houses a satellite location for the Moreau Avenue Daycare.

[edit] Beliefs

At Archwood School, they believe that all children and adults have the right to learn, work and play in a safe and happy place.
