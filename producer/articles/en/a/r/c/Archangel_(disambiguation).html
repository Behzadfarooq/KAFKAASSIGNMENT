Archangel may refer to:

  • Archangel, a type of angel
  • Arkhangelsk, a city and region in Russia
  • K-525, Minsky Komsomolets, an Oscar class submarine renamed Arkhangelsk
  • Archangel (film), a film by director Guy Maddin
  • Archangel (comics), a superhero in the fictional Marvel Universe
  • Archangel class assault ship, the "base ship" in the anime series Gundam SEED
  • Archangel (Band), a melodic death metal band
  • Archangel (Song), a song by the horror punk band Samhain
  • Archangel (Shinn novel), a novel by Sharon Shinn
  • Archangel (Harris novel), a novel by Robert Harris, adapted into a BBC movie
  • Archangelos, a town of Rhodes Island in Greece
  • The codename for the character Michael Coldsmith Briggs III in the 1980's TV series Airwolf
  • Archangel (operation), a British military operation to support counter-revolutionaries after the Bolshevik revolution
  • Archangel (pigeon), a breed of domestic pigeon
  • Archangel (role-playing game), by David Baxter
