Category:Archbishops of Uppsala

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia


This category contains the names of people having the position of Archbishop of Uppsala, the Lutheran Primate of Sweden.

Please note that some Archbishops are alphabetized by their first name because their last name is the Latin form of their patronymic names, not their family names, and should thus not be used for sorting.
