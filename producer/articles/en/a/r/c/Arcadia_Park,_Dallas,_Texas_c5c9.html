Arcadia Park is a neighborhood in the Oak Cliff neighborhood of Dallas, Texas (USA).

[edit] Education

Arcadia Park residents are assigned to the following Dallas Independent School District schools:

  • Arcadia Park Elementary School
  • Quintanilla Middle School
  • Pinkston High School
