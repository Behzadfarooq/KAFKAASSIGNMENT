The Hon. Archibald McLelan

Archibald Woodbury McLelan, PC (20 December 1824 – 26 June 1890) was a Canadian shipbuilder and politician.

McLelan was born in Londonderry, Nova Scotia, the son of Gloud Wilson McLelan, a member of the Nova Scotia House of Assembly. He was educated in Great Village and joined his father's shipping and retail business. On his father's death in 1858 he succeeded him in the House of Assembly.

A Conservative Member of parliament, McLelan served from 10 December 1885–26 January 1887 as the Minister of Finance in the second administration of Sir John A. Macdonald.
