The Archdeacon of Sodor and Mann is the deputy to the Lord Bishop of Sodor and Mann in the Isle of Man. He was until around 100 years ago an ex officio Member of the Legislative Council.

  • The Venerable William McCrystyn, 1546-1557
  • The Venerable John Kippax, 1727-1760

  • The Venerable Hugh Stowell Gill MA, 1895-?
