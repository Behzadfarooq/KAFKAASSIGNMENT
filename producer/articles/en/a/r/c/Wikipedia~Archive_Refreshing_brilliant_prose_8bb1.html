Beginning mid-June of 2003, a nomination process for Brilliant prose was implemented. Before this process existed, articles were added to Brilliant prose by individuals, and no system of debate, voting, or discussion took place.

We are now holding votes on the articles that were on the Brilliant prose page before the current process was in place. Please take the time to look the articles over, and place your votes. The articles have been broken down into the following categories:

  • Wikipedia:Refreshing brilliant prose - People and culture
  • Wikipedia:Refreshing brilliant prose - History and religion
  • Wikipedia:Refreshing brilliant prose - Others
  • Wikipedia:Refreshing brilliant prose - Science
