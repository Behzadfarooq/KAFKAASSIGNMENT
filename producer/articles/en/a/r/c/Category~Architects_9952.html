Architects

An architect is a person involved in the planning, design and quality control of a building's construction. Others in the profession may be involved in large-scale urban planning. Some architects do not build, but confine their activities to academic projects which serve to advance architectural thought. Over the years, architects have specialized in the design of specific shelter types, such as commercial, industrial, recreational and residential facilities. If you are looking for a particular architect and do not find him in this category, you may find them grouped by nationality.

The main article for this category is Architect.
