Arch Enemy Entertainment is an independent comic book studio based in Los Angeles, California. It was founded in 2004, by William Wilson, with the help of Tone Rodriguez. Arch Enemy Entertainment focuses on publishing comic books and graphic novels.

The company was formed after Wilson decided to start his own comic book company, in which he would create and own his own material. He also started the company to use as a platform to springboard new and emerging talent into the comic book world.

David Soul is inheriting his estranged fathers super-powers. But what will he do when he finds out that every while his power increases over time. So his fathers powers erode.

A high powered action thriller

Alex Bishop is a typical husband and businessman, or so his wife, Jillian thinks. Wait until she finds out her father recruited him into a a covert govenment organisation and made him the Enforcer

  • Karma

A Mafia assassin is cursed to see the physical manifestations of her own, and the population of the worlds, Karma

  • Nadir

A supernatural series, set to debut as a web-comic

a Horror Graphic Novel
