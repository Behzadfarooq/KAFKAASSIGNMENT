Arc Dome Wilderness
IUCN Category Ib (Wilderness Area)
Location: Nevada USA
Nearest city: Tonopah, NV
Coordinates: 38°50′00″N, 117°21′00″W
Governing body: U.S. Forest Service

The Arc Dome Wilderness is a protected wilderness area in the Toiyabe Range of Nye County, in the central section of the state of Nevada in the western United States. It covers an area of approximately 115,000 acres (465 km²), and is administered by the Toiyabe National Forest.
