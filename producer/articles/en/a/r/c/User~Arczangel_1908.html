[edit] Literature

J.D. Salinger is the author of my favorite book, Catcher in the Rye. The protagonist of Catcher is Holden Caulfield, a symbol of teenage angst, according to some. I, however, am less deep than that. No, really. I simply enjoy reading the book. I think it's funny and true.

T.S. Eliot is the author of my favorite poem, The Hollow Men, which contains a reference to Conrad's Heart of Darkness, which I was tested over recently. If you don't know of the poem The Hollow Men, you should stop reading my drivel and go read that instead. It's much better.

Kurt Vonnegut has written a lot of stuff. Mostly good.

Douglas Adams is the author of the Hitchhiker's Guide to the Galaxy Non-Trilogy. Very good stuff. Highly recommended.

J.K. Rowling is, well...everyone knows who she is. Harry Potter rocks my socks. I could talk a while about him, but that would be a waste of 12 or so perfectly good bytes so I won't. I can't wait for the next book to come out. Yay.
