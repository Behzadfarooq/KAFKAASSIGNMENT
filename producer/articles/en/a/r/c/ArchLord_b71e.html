ArchLord is a MMORPG epic fantasy mix, set in the world of Chantra where players are able to play as one of three unique races including Humans, Moon Elves and Orcs and within each race they will be able to choose from one of eight different classes depending on their chosen race. The game accomodates both PvP fans as well as those looking for more solo action in a quest based game.
