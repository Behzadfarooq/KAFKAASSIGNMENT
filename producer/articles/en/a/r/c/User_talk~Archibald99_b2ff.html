[edit] Ibrox Stadium

My amendment to the Ibrox Stadium article is not vandalism. The reference to Castle Grayskull is accurate and verifiable, and does not breach copyright. It may be considered offensive by Rangers fans but that does not make it unsuitable for inclusion in the encyclopaedia. I have consulted several regular users of Wikipedia on this and they agree with me unanimously. There is even a reference to this on the Wikipedia page on Castle Grayskull. If that has been deemed suitable for inclusion on that page, then there is no rational justification not to include it on the Ibrox Stadium page too. —The preceding unsigned comment was added by Youngdegsy (talk • contribs) 17:39, 28 March 2007 (UTC).

Thanks for your reply. However, I do disagree with you and will have to take this to dispute resolution using the formal procedure. This revisal is not unsuitable, as you suggest. I note that you didn't comment on my point about the Castle Grayskull page. One of the main purposes of Wikipedia, or indeed any encyclopaedia, is to provide relevant, factual information. Ibrox is known as Castle Grayskull by fans across Scotland. Whether a reference to it would be unusual or not, or even unique, is scarcely relevant. To suggest that this is neither notable nor encyclopedic is, with respect, a clear indication of bias. Youngdegsy 18:12, 28 March 2007 (UTC)
