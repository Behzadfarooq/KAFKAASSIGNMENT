Archibald Kennedy, JP, DL, FSRGS, 4th Marquess of Ailsa (22 May 1872 – 27 February 1943) was a Scottish peer, the son of Archibald Kennedy, 3rd Marquess of Ailsa.

He married Frances Emily McTaggart-Stewart, daughter of Sir Mark John McTaggart-Stewart, 1st Bt., on 20 April 1903.

Archibald Angus Charles Kennedy, 5th Marquess of Ailsa succeeded to the titles of 15th Earl of Cassillis, 17th Lord Kennedy, 4th Marquess of Ailsa & 4th Baron Ailsa on 9 April 1938.

He died without issue and his titles passed to his younger brother.
