Archdiocese of Cashel and Emly (Roman Catholic)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

You have new messages (last change).

Cashel is one of the four Archdioceses in Ireland. The others are Armagh (seat of the Primate of All Ireland), Dublin (seat of the Primate of Ireland) and Tuam.

[edit] List of Archbishops

  • December 15, 1820 - Patrick Everard, died March 31, 1821
  • 1822 - Richard Laurence
  • 1833 - Michael Slattery, died 1857
  • 1875 - Thomas Croke, the most well-known, in whose honour the Gaelic Athletic Association stadium Croke Park in Dublin is named. Resigned 1896
  • 1902 July 23 - Thomas Fennelly, died 1913
  • 1913 December 2 - John Mary Harty, died September 1, 1946
  • 1946 September 11 - Jerimiah Kinnane, died February 18, 1959
  • 1959 December 21 - Thomas Morris, resigned September 12, 1988
  • 1988 September 12 - Dermot Clifford
