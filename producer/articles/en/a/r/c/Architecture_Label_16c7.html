Architecture Label is an independent record label based in Sydney, Australia.

[edit] Artists with releases on Architecture

  • Belles Will Ring
  • Bit By Bats
  • Death Cab for Cutie
  • Pedro the Lion
  • School of Emotional Engineering
  • Sounds Like Sunset
  • Spod
