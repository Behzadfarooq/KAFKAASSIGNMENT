Hello, world. My real name is Elie Charest and I am a video game designer and an aspiring novelist.

I have worked as game designer/lead designer on the following published console games:

  • Monsters Inc.
  • Scooby-Doo! Mystery Mayhem
  • Scooby-Doo! Unmasked
  • Kim Possible: Kimmunicator
  • Monster House

I also worked for a Role-playing game company called Dream Pod 9, where I contributed to the Heavy Gear series of game books, most notably the Tactical Air Manual and the Operation: Jungle Drums adventure scenario.

I am currently working on my first novel, called Demons and such, and I owe a great deal of gratitude to Wikipedia for helping me with my research. I try to give back a little by contributing to a few articles here and there (if only by checking spelling and the like) as well as monitoring a few others for vandalism.
