Arcadia, Queensland

Arcadia is a village and town of Magnetic Island, Australia. Arcadia is more residential focused with around 97% of the dwellings being Housing or unit type dwellings.

Arcadia is also home to Alma Bay and Geoffrey Bay, which are the Island's most well known beaches. It is also home to Centaur Guest House a World War 2 house built in memory of the hospital ship Centaur.

It is also home to the famous Turnbull mansion which was built in the early 1900's.
