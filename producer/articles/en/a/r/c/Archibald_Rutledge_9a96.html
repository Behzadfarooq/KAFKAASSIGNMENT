(1883-1973) - South Carolina poet laureate. He attended the Porter Military Academy, now the exclusive Porter-Gaud School, in Charleston, SC. His ancestors included a Governor of South Carolina, a chief justice of the US Supreme Court, and a signer of the Declaration of Independence. Later in life, he taught English at Mercersburg Academy in Pennsylvania.
