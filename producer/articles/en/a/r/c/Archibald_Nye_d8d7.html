Archibald Nye

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Lieutenant General Sir Archibald Edward Nye , GCSI, GCIE, KCB, KBE, MC (23 April 1895 - 13 December 1967).

Posts held include:

  • Vice Chief of the Imperial General Staff, 1941 - 1945
  • Governor of Madras, 1946 - 1947
  • UK High Commissioner to India, 1948 - 1952
  • UK High Commissioner to Canada, 1952-1956
