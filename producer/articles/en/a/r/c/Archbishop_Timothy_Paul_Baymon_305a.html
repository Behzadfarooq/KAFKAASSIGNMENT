His Beatitude, TIMOTHY PAUL, Ph.D., D.TH. H.B. TIMOTHY PAUL is Metropolitan Archbishop of the International Communion of the Holy Christian Orthodox Church.

With several earned Doctorates, including two in Philosophy (Human Services and Religious Education) and a Doctorate in Theology, the Archbishop has been named in "2000 Outstanding Scholars of the 21st Century".

Archbishop Baymon is also a founding member and President of the World Bishops Council. Headquartered in New York

[edit] Media

Archbishop Baymon is well known around the world and in his hometown of Springfield, Massachusetts.

He is also a well known speaker for the Family Federation for World Peace and Unification http://www.wewillstand.org/main.html

He has had the honor of meeting Father Moon on several occasions. On one such occasion. in a brief ceremony on stage, the large bible was given to Father and Mother Moon by Archbishop Baymon, representing all the eighty ministers in attendance.

On March 9, 2001, His Beatitude Archbishop Timothy Paul Baymon representing the Christian Orthodox Church of Springfield, Massachusetts and the World Bishop's Council served to introduce the keynote address by Father Moon. In this introduction he expressed his confidence that just as God sent a message of guidance and hope in those days, God has also now raised up a man and a woman who are willing to stand to rebuild His ideal here on the Earth. He identified Father and Mother Moon as two chosen by God.
