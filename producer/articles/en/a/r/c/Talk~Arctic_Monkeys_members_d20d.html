[edit] Split 'Alex Turner (musician)'

Whilst the other band members may not justify their own articles quite yet, Alex Turner definitely does. After all, he was (rather idiotically) named the 'coolest man on the planet' by NME. If the coolest man on the planet doesn't deserve his own article, I don't know which musician does. There's already a two paragraph section, which could easily be expanded in the context of a separate article, with more biographical details, an infobox ({{Infobox musical artist}}), and a picture. I concur... although there is a fair amount of content available about the other band members too. But yeah, I support a split. DJR (T) 13:41, 24 January 2007 (UTC)
