Architecture is a communicative art that situates human activity within a horizon of possibilities – the art and science of designing buildings. A wider definition would include within its scope the design of the total built environment, from the macrolevel of urban planning, urban design, and landscape architecture to the microlevel of furniture and product design.

Discussion of Architecture categories can be made on the talk page of Wikipedia:WikiProject Architecture/Categories
