Since the mid-20th century, it has become common for architects to be known for their work as part of a group in an architecture firm or partnership, as well as the individual architectal practice. (Herzog & de Meuron vs. Le Corbusier). Both types of firms employ a number of professional and technical staff under the direction of a partner or owner.


Refer to the List of architects for the names of individual architects (see also: "Starchitects")
