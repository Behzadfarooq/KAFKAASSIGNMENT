The Archonta are a group of mammals considered a superorder in some classifications.

The Archonta consist of the following orders :

  • Primates
  • Plesiadapiformes (extinct - primate-like archonts)
  • Scandentia (treeshrews)
  • Dermoptera (colugos)
  • Chiroptera (bats)

Genetic analysis has suggested that the bats are not as closely related to the other groups as previously suspected. A revised category, Euarchonta, excluding bats, has been proposed.
