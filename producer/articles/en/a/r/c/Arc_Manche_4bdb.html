Arc Manche

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

You have new messages (last change).

Arc Manche is a transnational region covering parts of southern England and northern France. Its purpose is to encourage collaboration between local authorities and businesses on either side of the English Channel, and to allow local authorities to collectively apply for EU funding.
