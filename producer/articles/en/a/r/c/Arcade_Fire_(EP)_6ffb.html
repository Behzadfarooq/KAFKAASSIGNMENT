Arcade Fire is a self-titled 2003 EP by the indie rock band Arcade Fire. It is also unofficially known as Us Kids Know after a lyric in "No Cars Go."

It was recorded on Mount Desert Island, Maine in a barn. It was remastered for its 2005 re-release.

The song "No Cars Go" was re-recorded for Arcade Fire's sophomore album, Neon Bible.
