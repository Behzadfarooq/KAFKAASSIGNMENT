[edit] Switching to Mac

I have been familiar with and have used a Mac since 1985. My most common experience with a Mac was in various public schools from about the 3rd grade all the way to my junior year of high school. However, my family always had a PC at home because that's what my dad prefered. When I finally bought my first computer, I got an eMahines because they were cheap. From there, I kept buying PCs to I could keep using my existing applications and games.

My interest in Mac was revivied when I heard about OS X. When a friend, Doug, told me he had switched to Mac severak years ago, my interest only became stronger. Finally in November of 2006, I ordered a Mac Book. I had no intention of switching to Mac. In Janurary of 2007, I sold my Dell Desktop and am now waiting for the release of OS X 10.5 to buy an iMac. I will still use Windows XP via Bootcamp, but only so I can play my games.
