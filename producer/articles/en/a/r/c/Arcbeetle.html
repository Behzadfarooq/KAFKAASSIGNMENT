Arcbeetle

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Arcbeetle is the medabot of Space Medafighter X/Phantom Renagade/Hikaru Agata/Henry in the anime Medabots.

[edit] History

Spoiler warning: Plot and/or ending details follow.

At first seen by Spike, Arcbeetle was a mysterious Medabot that showed up and was assumed to strike Spike with lightning (Spike finally is able to explain that he is struck by regular lightning and not by Arcbeetle, as assumed by the Screws). Later shows up and the next episode has him and Space Medafighter X fight off the medabots that took out Metabee, Sumilidon and Kintaroth all together, he defeats them with ease and then disappears with X.

Arcbeetle is shown to be very powerful and faster than Sumilidon, helping Ikki out of many scraps and only saying one word the whole series. He goes with X off the cliff during the defeat of Mega-Emperor and later shows up and is revealed to have a rare medal.

During the world finals, he only shows up when X finally does, to stop Joe's Medabot from hurting Karen, easily blocking the punch from Wigwamo and standing by X as he "debates" with Joe. He fights team U.S.A and Japan wins, due to Koji.

It is later revealed that Arcbeetle is actually the first Metabee, but when Hikaru broke his medal, he lost all his memories. It also has Hikaru saying the Ten Days Of Darkness were ten days long because he was looking for any other way to stop the madness, something he is very guilty over. All this was left out of the dub because it was to intense and it is often a misconseption that Metabee was once Henry's.

[edit] Weapons and Attacks

  • The Prominence- the two spikes on his head and chest channel lightning and than shoot it in a very powerful and explosive blast.
  • Tri Gun barrels on each arm and foot.
  • Very fast and swift in spite of his size. Is said to be able to dodge bullets and catch seeker missiles.
  • The Medaforce when revealed that he had a rare medal.
  • Specialty:Shoots fireballs
