[edit] Sphere 41

Fictional object (Trek) that only appears in one episode. Yes, I know we cover fiction--I have no problem with that. Yes, WP is not paper--I have no real problem with that (as I have noted before, I believe the limiting resource is the ratio between the number of Wikipedians and the number of articles). The problem I have with this level of granularity is that, from a user perspective, having to click to an article that can never be more than a couple of lines, is a complete waste (especially given how sluggish WP currently is). Any relevant info should be merged into Zero Hour (ENT episode), and make Sphere 41 a redir to it. Niteowlneils 05:37, 29 May 2004 (UTC)
