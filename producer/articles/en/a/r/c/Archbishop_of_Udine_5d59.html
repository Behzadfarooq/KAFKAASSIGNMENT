Archbishop of Udine

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

This is a List of Archbishops of Udine. The see is established in 1752 when the Patriarchal see of Aquileia was divided. From 1818 to 1846 it was a suffragan diocese of the Patriarch of Venice.
