[edit] Name?

"The name "Arc" came from intense white light the Luxeon Star LED produced, looking similar to an electric arc light."

Cite? I believe that Arc Flashlight's first product didn't have the Luxeon. This explaination doesn't make sense in that context. —The preceding unsigned comment was added by 71.12.197.93 (talk • contribs) 19:27, 19 May 2006 (UTC).

You're right the first product was the original Arc AAA based on a 5mm LED in May 2001, whereas the first Luxeon-based product was the Arc LS in December 2001 [3]. Changed article accordingly. Joema 14:13, 20 May 2006 (UTC)
