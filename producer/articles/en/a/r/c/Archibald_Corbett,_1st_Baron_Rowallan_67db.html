Archibald Cameron Corbett, 1st Baron Rowallan (23 May 1856 – 19 March 1933) was a Scottish Liberal Party politician.

The son of Thomas Corbett, he first contested North Warwickshire in 1884. At the 1885 general election he was elected as Member of Parliament (MP) for Glasgow Tradeston, holding that seat until his retirement from the House of Commons in 1911, when he was created 1st Baron Rowallan.
