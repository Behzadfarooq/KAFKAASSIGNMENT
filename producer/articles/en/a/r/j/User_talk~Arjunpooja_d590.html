[edit] edits to MOO

Thanks for your email. If you want to allow other users to email you, check the Enable e-mail from other users box under Special:Preferences, otherwise I'll just use your talk page ;).

User:Davidpk212 was the one who removed your edits, as you can see by looking at the history of the article. Your research is certainly of value to the academic community in general, but I'm not sure it is appropriate for inclusion in this wikipedia article. A summary of your findings as complete sentences referencing cited sources is the general format for wikipedia articles. Perhaps a section on WOOs and how they differ from MOOs would be a good place to start again.

I would recommend placing your research (what appears to be a paper written for other purposes?) online and available to the public somewhere off wikipedia, allowing google to index it, and thus making it available to the world.

Any further discussion of MOO should take place at Talk:MOO to involve everyone watching the article. Thanks again for your efforts.
