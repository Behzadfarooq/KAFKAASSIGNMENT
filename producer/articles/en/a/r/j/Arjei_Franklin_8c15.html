Arjei Franklin (born April 25, 1982 in Toronto, Ontario, Canada) is a Canadian professional football wide receiver in the Canadian Football League who has played for the Winnipeg Blue Bombers.

Franklin was also an alumni of Agincourt Collegiate Institute.

[edit] Playing career

Franklin was drafted by the Winnipeg Blue Bombers in the 2006 CFL Canadian Draft, 3rd round, 19th overall, after starring at University of Windsor.

University: All-time leading receiver in Windsor Lancer history. Most touchdowns and all-purpose yards in team history. Three-time Lancer MVP. Windsor Male Athlete of the Year in 2005. All-Canadian and OUA First Team All-Star in 2005.

[edit] Awards and achievements

  • 2004: All-Canadian at inside receiver. OUA First Team All-Star.
  • 2003: OUA Second Team All-Star.
  • 2002: OUA First Team All-Star.
