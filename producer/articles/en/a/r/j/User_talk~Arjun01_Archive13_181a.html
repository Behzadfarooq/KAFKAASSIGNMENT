Your picture

Well, I'll tell you one thing: I'm not a good photographer, so one mistake, and my pictures is bad (and mistakes are common). I don't recommend taking pictures of faraway things (unless you have a special lens, called a telephoto lens). Read the nominations at FPC, it'll help you to know about certain image problems. If possible, read a photography guide/book, or take lessons (although that might be expensive). Also, start off with things that don't move, and you can take all day taking pictures of (it took me three hours and about 150 pictures to get a few good images of that walnut, but at least it wasn't going anywhere). If you're still in doubt, ask an expert, like User:Fir0002, or User:Diliff. Also, only upload your best images (I'd look like a fool if I didn't :).
