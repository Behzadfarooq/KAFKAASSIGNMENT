[edit] Pim Fortuijn

On the matter of the section of the Pim Fortuyn article you removed: I can understand you perhaps saw this as the tarnishing of someone the memory of whom you hold dear, but this issue has reemerged several times. As it was described now, it was basically correct: it stated that the allegations were unproven and that Fortuijn's views on paedophilia were no different from those hold by many Dutchmen. I personally find the the issue rather irrelevant, but if there isn't a section on it, someone else is bound to create one and probably in a less balanced form.

A further point I'd like to make is that it is considered very bad manners on Wikipedia to simply remove entire sections without prior discussion. When I saw you had edited the Talk page, I hoped you were ready to begin such a discussion; I was disappointed to see you had simply removed other people's opinions. That is hardly in the spirit of Fortuijn himself. I don't know your age; but if you're a boy, I'd say: grow up faster and if you're a man: act like one.
