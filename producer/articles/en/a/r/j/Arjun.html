Arjun and Arjuna are popular male given names in India and Sri Lanka

Arjun or Arjuna may refer to:

  • Arjuna, a hero of the Mahabharata, a Hindu epic
  • Arjun MBT, an Indian main battle tank
  • Kartavirya Arjuna, also known as Sahasrabahu, a character in Hindu mythology
  • Earth Girl Arjuna, a 2001 Japanese animated TV series
  • Arjunayana, an ancient people of Northern India
  • Arjun (film), a 2004 Telugu film starring Mahesh Babu

In science:

  • Arjuna asteroid, a class of near-Earth asteroids with Earth-like orbits
  • Terminalia arjuna, a species of tree in the genus Terminalia

Among specific people:

  • Arjun Singh, an Indian politician
  • Arjun Munda, the former chief minister of the Indian state of Jharkhand
  • Arjun Appadurai, a contemporary social theorist
  • Arjun Atwal, a professional golfer
  • Arjun Rampal, a fashion model
  • Arjun (actor), a popular film actor from south India
  • Allu Arjun, an actor of Telugu films
  • Arjun Sreedharan, an expert in computer securities in calicut, kerala ,india.
