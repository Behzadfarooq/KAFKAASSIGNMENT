Arjun Sablok is an Indian film director. He is currently one of the film directors working under the Yashraj Films banner.

Before he foray into film directing he made brief appearances in two of director Yash Chopra's films Vijay (1988) and Dilwale Dulhania Le Jayenge (1995).

He made his directorial debut with Na Tum Jaano Na Hum (2002) starring Hrithik Roshan, Saif Ali Khan and Esha Deol. The film was not a success and he took a break for three years before returning with another film Neal N Nikki (2005) which was produced by Yash Chopra's production company Yash Raj Films. The film starred Yash Chopra's son Uday Chopra and Tanisha. This film was critically panned and also failed at the box office. He is currently working on his yet untitled project which will also be produced by Yashraj films.
