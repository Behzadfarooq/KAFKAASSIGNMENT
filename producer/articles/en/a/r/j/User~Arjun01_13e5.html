[edit] About me

I am a young adult from the beautiful Sunshine State of Florida. I am a follower of the Hindu faith, I make sure that my religious beliefs don't bleed into my article writing. I would greatly appreciate it if you gave me a note if I am not writing from the NPOV, however I am pretty sure do :). I am interested in a variety of things some include philosophy, politics, nature, astrology, photography and Musical Studies. On that note; I am a Guitarist who devotes his life to Music. I am also an aspiring Classical Pianist. I am interested in the Blues particularly though. I also love to read, I own an original set of the Harvard Classics and read whenever I get the chance.

[edit] On Wikipedia

In Wikipedia I can be found in a number of places, such as the regular visitation of the AIV, RFPP, CSD, WP:XFD and other various places. I am a "vandal focused admin", so whenever I am on Wikipedia there is an 80% chance that I am doing some form of RC patrol or NP patrol. If you have any questions about that please let me know. Another thing worth mentioning; if I am online there is a good chance that I am on the IRC. My nickname is Arjun01. I am usually on a variety of channels — some include; #wikipedia-en-admins, #vandalism-en-wp, #wikipedia-in but always on #wikipedia. If you need my assistance with anything just "ping" me and I will reply ASAP. Beware: if my nick is Arjun_away; then don't expect a quick reply ;).

[edit] Notable contributions

I will be the first one to admit that I am a maintenance focused Wikipedian, however I do have some contributions that I am proud of.

  • Hinduism — would like to FA this one some day. This article is one of the first things I worked on when I joined Wikipedia. I have removed and added some sections and do some overall copyediting.
  • List of Stratocaster players — this list is close to my heart, as I am a player too :D. I completely revamped it and added some citations.
  • List of Hindu Festivals — another list that I revamped. I converted it to the modern style wikitable format. I am hoping to work on this list even more.
  • Portal:Guitar — planning to work on this one to get it to featured status. I converted it to the rotation cycle format, this is only the first step though.
  • Portal:Hinduism — my only featured item, I have done a lot of work on this portal.
