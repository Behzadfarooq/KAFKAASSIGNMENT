Arzignano is an industrial town in the province of Vicenza, Veneto, Italy. It is located at around 45°31′0″N, 11°19′60″E.

[edit] Population

It is the hometown of Giovanna D. Cornett

  • 23.085 in 2001
  • .937% annual growth rate.
  • 675 people / km^2 [1]

[edit] Other Details

  • Zip Code : 36071
  • 23km away from Vicenza, (the capital of the province).
  • According to sources, there are many (826) industrial firms which employ ~1/3 of the people in the town and immediate vicinity.[citation needed]
