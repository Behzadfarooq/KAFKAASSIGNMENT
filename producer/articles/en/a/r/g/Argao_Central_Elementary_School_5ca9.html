Argao Central Elementary School

Argao Central Elementary School is a school located in Poblacion Argao, Cebu, Philippines. This school was established in 1928. The current principal is Mr. Anselmo B. Chavez.[1] As of the 2004-05 school year, the school had an enrollment of 887 students.[1]

[edit] Notable alumni

  • Hilario Gelbolingo Davide, Jr., who was the 20th Chief Justice of the Philippines, serving from 1998-2005.
