Argonaute (S636)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The conning tower of Argonaute

Argonaute (S636) is a submarine of Aréthuse class, launched on October 23, 1958 and decommissioned on July 31, 1982. She was the fourth ship of the French Navy to bear the name.

After being refurbished for easy visiting, she is now on display before the Cité des sciences et de l'industrie in Paris.

For other French ships by that name, see French ship Argonaute.
