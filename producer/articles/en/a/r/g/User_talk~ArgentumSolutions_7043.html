[edit] Welcome to Wikipedia!

Welcome, ArgentumSolutions, to Wikipedia!

Wikipedia is an enormous community filled with knowledge and opportunity. Wikipedia currently consists of at least 1,714,956 articles and 3,998,848 users, as well as many other resources. In joining, I hope you will benefit from this amazing supply of knowledge. I also hope you will consider having this community benefit from you and your expertise.

If you ever need help, feel free to contact me on my talk page. Also, placing {{helpme}} on your own talk page will notify advanced users that there is an unanswered question there.

I suggest looking over some of the pages I have listed below before you begin. Be sure to enjoy your new opportunity to learn and educate others!

--ENIAC (Talk) (Current Projects) 18:01, 6 September 2006 (UTC)
