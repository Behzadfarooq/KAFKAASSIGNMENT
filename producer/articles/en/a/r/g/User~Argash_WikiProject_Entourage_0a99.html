Welcome to the Entourage WikiProject Proposal!

Goals
  • Improve Wikipedia's coverage of the HBO dramedy show Entourage.
  • Create guidelines for articles about Entourage (style, formatting, etc.)
Scope
  • The project covers all articles about Entourage; main article, cast articles, episode articles and all related lists

[edit] Open tasks

  • Main Article
    • General cleanup (spellings and such)
    • Move major characters to their own individual articles and flesh out
      • Create a single entry about the cast as a whole for the main page
    • Trim trivia, and move episode-specific items to their respective episode pages
    • Reference the entire article (has begun)
  • Episode Articles
    • Add complete plot summary to each episodes article
    • Add selected quotes to each episodes article
    • Add selected Trivia bits to each episode
  • Images
    • All images used for Entourage related articles MUST have addiquate fair use rational added to their description page.
    • All images used MUST be captioned (complies with fair use)
    • Add needed images
  • List of Celebrity Cameos
    • Fictional Characters need to be merged into the main list and linked to their entry on the List of Recurring Characters
    • Thumbnails need to be added to "beautify" the list
    • Category:Entourage actors should be added to any actors wiki entry that has appeared on the show (Categorizing by performace is no longer "allowed". This is WP:CFD concensus)
