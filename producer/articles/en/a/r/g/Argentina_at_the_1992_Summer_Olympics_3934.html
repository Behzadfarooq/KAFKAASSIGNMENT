[edit] Medals

Argentina finished in 54th position in the final medal rankings, with one bronze medal.

[edit] Bronze

  • Javier Frana and Christian Miniussi — Tennis, Men's doubles team competition

Men's Doubles Competition

  • Javier Frana and Christian Miniussi → Bronze Medal

    3. Quarterfinals — Defeated Jakob Hlasek and Marc Rosset (Switzerland) 2-6, 7-6, 3-6, 6-2, 6-2
    4. Semifinals — Lost to Boris Becker and Michael Stich (Germany) 6-7, 2-6, 7-6, 6-2, 4-6
