[edit] Medals

Argentina finished in 54th position in the final medal rankings, with two silver medals and one bronze medal.

[edit] Silver

  • Carlos Espinola — Sailing, Men's Mistral Individual Competition
  • Matias Almeyda, Roberto Ayala, Christian Bassedas, Carlos Bossio, Pablo Cavallero, José Antonio Chamot, Hernán Crespo, Marcelo Delgado, Marcelo Gallardo, Claudio López, Gustavo López, Hugo Morales, Ariel Ortega, Pablo Paz, Hector Pineda, Roberto Sensini, Diego Simeone, and Javier Zanetti — Football (soccer), Men's Team Competition

[edit] Bronze

  • Pablo Chacón — Boxing, Men's Featherweight
