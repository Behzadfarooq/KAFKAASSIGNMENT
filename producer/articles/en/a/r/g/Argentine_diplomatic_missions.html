Listed below are the embassies and consulates-generals of Argentina (excluding honorary consulates).

It is to be noted that embasies in several countries serve as diplomatic contact for other countries where no permanent representative is available. For example, The embassy in Vienna serving Slovenia, Croatia, Bosnia and Herzegovina, and Serbia.
