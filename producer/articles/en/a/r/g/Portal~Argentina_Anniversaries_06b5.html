  • 1821 – the Supreme of the Republic of Entre Ríos, Francisco Ramírez, fights against the forces of Gregorio Aráoz de La Madrid in the Battle of Rosario.

  • 1985 – Dies the poet and writer César Fernández Moreno.
  • 1989 – In the second presidential elections after the endo of the National Reorganization Process, is elected the justicialist Carlos Menem.
