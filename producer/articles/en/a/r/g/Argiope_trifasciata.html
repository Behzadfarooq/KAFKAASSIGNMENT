Argiope trifasciata, also called banded garden spider in the USA, is a species of spider found around the world, but not in Europe, where the similar looking Argiope bruennichi is common.

Their webs reach a diameter of about 60 centimeter.

[edit] Subspecies

  • Argiope trifasciata deserticola Simon, 1906 (Sudan)
  • Argiope trifasciata kauaiensis Simon, 1900 (Hawaii)
