Argus Rentals

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Argus Rentals is a car rental distributor founded in 1959 in Dublin, Ireland.

Argus Rentals has over 9000 locations in Africa, Australia, Asia, the Caribbean, Europe, the Middle East, North America and South America. As a car rental distributor, it uses a network of major car rental companies in addition to smaller independents in order to serve car rental search results from a host of different suppliers.

Argus Rentals' headquarters are still located in Dublin, and have expanded into the German market.
