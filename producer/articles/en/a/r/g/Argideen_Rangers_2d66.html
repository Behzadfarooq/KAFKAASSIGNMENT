Argideen Rangers

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Argideen Rangers is a Gaelic football and Hurling club based in Timoleague in County Cork. In 2005 the club won the Cork Intermediate Hurling Championship by beating Nemo Rangers in the final. The club plays in West Cork or Carbery Division games in Cork competitions.

[edit] Honours

  • West Cork Junior A Hurling titles 1993, 1996 and 2003
  • West Cork Junior A Football title in 1994
  • West Cork Junior B Hurling title in 1985
  • West Cork Junior B Football titles 1967, 1988, 1993 and 2001.
  • Cork Junior Hurling Championship: Winners 1996, Runners-Up: 2003

In 2003 the club was promoted to compete in the Cork Intermediate Hurling Championship even though it had not won the county Junior Hurling Competition.

  • Cork Junior B Football Championship: Winners 1993, 2001
  • Cork Minor B Football Championship: Runners Up 1992
  • Cork Minor C Hurling Championship: Runners Up 2003

Underage Club

2006: U12 West Cork "D" Football League & Championship Winners. Beat Barryroe in both finals

The club enjoys a very strong but friendly rivalry with neighbours, Barryroe.
