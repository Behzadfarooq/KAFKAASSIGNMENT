Club Atlético Argentino are a football club from Rosario in Santa Fe Province, Argentina. They currently play in Primera C Metropolitana, which is the regionalised fourth tier of the Argentine Football Association league system.

At the end of the 2005/06 season they narrowly missed out on promotion to Primera B Metropolitana, they reached the playoff final but lost 6-0 on aggregate to Excursionistas.

The club have never played at the top level of Argentine football.
