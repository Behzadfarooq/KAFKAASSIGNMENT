Welcome to the regional notice board for Argentina. This is a place for announcements, updates, requests for assistance, etc. on topics belonging or related to Argentina. For questions, comments or suggestions, go to the discussion page.

If you are willing to contribute with articles about Argentina (creating them anew, or improving existing ones), check the WikiProject Argentina page. There are many things to do. We have a collection of resources to assist you in the task.

If you are an Argentinian and haven't do so already, add yourself to the Category:Wikipedians from Argentina. If you're living there, you can additionally use Category:Wikipedians in Argentina. Consider also mentioning the languages you speak (see Wikipedia:Babel).

If you have any question, or any problem with another user/vandal, you can contact Mariano, Sebastian or Pablo, our very own administrators, for assistance.

Important policies: Don't violate copyright. Cite sources and verify them. Do it with style. Be neutral. Link, but only in context. Watch your words.
