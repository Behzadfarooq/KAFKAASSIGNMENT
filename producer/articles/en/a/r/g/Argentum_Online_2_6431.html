Argentum Online 2

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Argentum Online II is a Massively Multiplayer Online Role Playing Game, the prequel of Argentum Online. Argentum 2 will have support for many languages, including Spanish, English, French, Català and Esperanto.

[edit] History

The game is set in two medieval fantasy worlds. One of these worlds has been subjugated by the Lord of Chaos, and most folks were enslaved. However, there are a few who have remained hidden, waiting for a chance to get a magic portal and reach the new world. These magic portals appear and disappear ramdomly along the lands, linking the worlds.

Player characters may choose their own path, embracing the Chaos or struggling against it. Most characters are fleeing from Dovoo, the world in ruins, and begin their adventure in the hopeful new world of Argentum. They are also able to create characters from Argentum.

[edit] The engine

The game is being developed in C/C++ programming language using DirectX8 and MySQL. It has a staggered isometric engine under the terms of LGPL licence.

[edit] System requirements

  • 400 MHz CPU
  • A standard 3d card
  • 64 MB RAM
  • Windows 2000 or later
  • DirectX 8.1 or later
  • Internet connection
