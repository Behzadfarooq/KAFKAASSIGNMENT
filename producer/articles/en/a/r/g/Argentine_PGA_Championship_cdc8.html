The Argentine PGA Championship is a non-tour domestic event which dates back to 1920. It is generally supported by the leading Argentine golfers and its list of champions includes Eduardo Romero (8 times), Vicente Fernández (5 times), José Cóceres (twice) and Ángel Cabrera. However, the record holder is former British Open champion Roberto DeVicenzo who recorded 16 victories between 1944 and 1985.
