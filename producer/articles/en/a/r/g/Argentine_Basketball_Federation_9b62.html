Argentine Basketball Federation

The Argentine Basketball Federation (Spanish: Confederación Argentina de Basquet - CABB) is the governing body of basketball in Argentina. It is also one of the founding members of the International Basketball Federation (FIBA), founded in 1932.

Member of the FIBA Americas, it organizes the National league and other basketball minor divisions, and is responsible for the Argentina national basketball team.

Currently there are approximately 1200 clubs registered with the association, as well as 130,500 male and 11,000 female players.
