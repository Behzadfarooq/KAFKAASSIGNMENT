Argentinísima II is a 1973 Argentine musical documentary film directed and written by Fernando Ayala and Héctor Olivera. It is the sequel to Argentinísima released in July 1973. The film premiered on 21 June 1973 in Buenos Aires.

The film is based on tango dancing, an integral part of Argentine culture.

The film followed on from Argentinísima 1972
