Argentino

Hi. I am proudly Argentinian. I started to edit Wikipedia in June 2005 and since then I've tried to create and expand argentina-related articles. This has provoked someproblems, including the vandalisation of my userpage by a nationalistic falklander. I'm a vandal fighter too: I have Lupin's tool, Vandalproof and a lot of vandals to exterminate. My contributions to this project has helped me to improve my English very much. (I wish I could speak better)

I'm an Administrator of the Latin Wikiquote, where I have translated a lot of the MediaWiki into a dead language (But I'm not too mad, I only wanted to practise and help).

If you want to talk send me a message to my talk page
