[edit] Medals

[edit] Gold

  • Delfo Cabrera — Athletics, Men's Marathon
  • Pascual Pérez — Boxing, Men's Flyweight
  • Rafael Iglesias — Boxing, Men's Heavyweight

[edit] Silver

  • Noëmi Simonetto de Portela — Athletics, Women's Long Jump
  • Carlos Diaz Sáenz — Shooting, Men's Rapid-Fire Pistol
  • Enrique Sieburger, Julio Sieburger, Emilio Homps, Rodolfo Rivademar, and Rufino Rodriguez de la Torre — Sailing, Men's 6 Meter Class Team Competition

[edit] Bronze

  • Maurio Cia — Boxing, Men's Light Heavyweight
