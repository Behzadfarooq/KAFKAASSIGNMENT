The Arquette family is a famous show business family.

[edit] First Generation

  • Cliff Arquette - (Dec. 26, 1904-Sept. 23, 1974) TV actor best known as Charley Weaver, father of Lewis Arquette and grandfather of David, Richmond, Rosanna, Alexis and Patricia.

[edit] Second Generation

  • Lewis Arquette - (Dec. 14 1935-Feb 10, 2001) Movie actor who is the son of Cliff Arquette, and father of the Arquette kids.

[edit] Third Generations

  • Rosanna Arquette - (Aug. 10, 1959) Movie actress
  • Richmond Arquette - (Aug. 21, 1963) Movie actor
  • Patricia Arquette - (Apr. 8, 1968) Movie and TV actress
  • Alexis Arquette - (Jan. 1, 1969) Movie actor
  • David Arquette - (Sept. 8, 1971) Movie actor

[edit] Others

  • James Newton Howard - (June 9, 1951) Composer and orchestrator, ex-husband to Rosanna.(1986-87).
  • Nicholas Cage - (Jan. 7, 1964) Movie actor, ex-husband to Patricia (1995-2001).
  • Courtney Cox - (June 15, 1964) - Movie and former TV actress, current wife of David (1999-present). They have one child, CoCo.
  • Thomas Jane - Actor, current husband of Patricia Arquette.
