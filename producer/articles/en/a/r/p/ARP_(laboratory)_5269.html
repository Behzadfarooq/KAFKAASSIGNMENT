ARP (laboratory)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

ARP stands for Applied Research in Patacriticism. It is a digital humanities lab based at the University of Virginia. ARP's open-source tools include Juxta, IVANHOE, and Collex. Collex is the social software and faceted browsing backbone of the NINES federation. ARP has been awarded funding by the Mellon Foundation.
