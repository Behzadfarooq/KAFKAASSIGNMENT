[edit] Career

Miklos has worked under the direction of such noted directors as John Rutherford, Jerry Douglas, and Chi Chi LaRue, and has been noted as being "one of the most laidback, professional models to work with out there".[1]

A prolific performer, some of the earliest videos Miklos made, The Vampire of Budapest and Comrades in Arms, were with noted director Kristen Bjorn and were both released in 1995. He has made several additional films for Kristen Bjorn Video, including Hungary for Men, Thick as Thieves, and The Isle of Men. The films with Bjorn were shot in locations that included Eastern Europe (Hungary and Russia), the United States, and the Caribbean (Puerto Rico, the Bahamas, the Cayman Islands, and Jamaica).

With Falcon Studios, he has made Beefcake, Bootstrap, Man Made, Taking Flight, Part I, and Defined. Miklos recently worked with Dirk Yates in Jarhead 2 for All Worlds Video; other titles for that studio include Fucking with the Stars, Nomads, Pool Boy, Tune Up, and World Splash Orgy 2005. Lucas Entertainment productions include Bonesucker, Big Blue, Fire Island Cruising 6, and Manhattan Heat.

Miklos has appeared in Humping Iron and Stoked 2 for Raging Stallion Studios and the Hot House Entertainment titles At Your Service, Blue, The Hard Way, Manhunt: The Movie, Private Lowlife, Ram Tough, The Road to Temptation, Skuff II, and Skuff 3. Both studios are noted for a "grittier" no-nonsense approach to gay porn that includes extreme sex acts such as fisting.

In addition to his appearance in the award-winning COLT Studios Group: Buckshot Productions BuckleRoos I, Miklos has also appeared in that studio's Leatherbound. Additional studios with which he has worked include Butch Bear, Catalina Studios, Massive Studio, MSR Videos, Red Devil Entertainment, Studio 2000, Three X Films, and Unzipped Video. He also appeared as Francois Kagylo in an Eastern European film with director Herve Handsome for the High Octane studio.

One of his more memorable scenes was one in BuckleRoos Part I that garnerned two awards for him: he and Ricky Martinez performed autofellatio while lying side-by-side.[5] To date, Miklos has only performed in the top role in his videos.
