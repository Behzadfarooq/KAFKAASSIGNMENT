Ardsley (SEPTA station)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Ardsley is a rail station on SEPTA's R2 Regional Rail Line in Pennsylvania, formerly Reading New Hope branch. is a SEPTA station along SEPTA's former Reading New Hope branch. It is located at the intersection of Jenkintown Road & Tyson Avenue in Ardsley. The station has a parking lot with 45 spaces.
