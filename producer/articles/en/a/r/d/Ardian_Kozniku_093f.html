Ardian Kozniku (born 27 October 1967 in Đakovica, Kosovo, then part of Yugoslavia) is a former Kosovo-born Croatian football player of Albanian descent.

He played for several clubs, including Hajduk Split, Le Havre AC and SC Bastia in France, Dinamo Zagreb, FC Kärnten in Austria, But he started his career at KF Vllaznimi from Đakovica, and later picked up by KF Prishtina.

He played for the Croatia national football team (7 matches/2 goals) and was a participant at the 1998 FIFA World Cup, where Croatia won the bronze medals.
