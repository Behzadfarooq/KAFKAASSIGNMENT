Ardisia (also called Coralberry or Marlberry) is a genus of flowering plants in the family Myrsinaceae (the Myrsine family). The genus includes more than 200 and up to 500 species.

One species, Ardisia japonica (Chinese: 紫金牛; pinyin: zǐjīn niú) is one of the 50 fundamental herbs in traditional Chinese medicine.
