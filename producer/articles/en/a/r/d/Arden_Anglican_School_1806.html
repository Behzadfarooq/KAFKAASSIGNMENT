Arden Anglican School is a coeducational primary school in Beecroft, New South Wales, Australia, with a secondary campus in Epping.

The primary school was originally established in 1922 as a preparatory school of the Presbyterian Ladies' College, Sydney, and the secondary school was opened on the site of the old Australian American International School near Epping railway station in 2003.
