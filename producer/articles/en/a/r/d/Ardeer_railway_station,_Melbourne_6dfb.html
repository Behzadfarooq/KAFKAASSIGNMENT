Ardeer is a railway station in Melbourne, Victoria, Australia, located in the suburb of Ardeer, on the Melton railway line. It is in Metcard Zone 2.

Ardeer station is unmanned.

[edit] Location

The station is located between Forrest Street and Ridgeway Parade.

[edit] Facilities

The station consists of two platforms, in an island format. The island platform contains a small shelter. A small Metcard machine is located inside the shelter, which dispenses only selected ticketing options.

The island platform can be accessed via Forrest Street and Ridgeway Parade.

[edit] History

Ardeer station opened on Wednesday, 2 April 1884.
