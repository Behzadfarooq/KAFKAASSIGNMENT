[edit] Information possibly relevant to the Gospatric article

[edit] Generation No. 1

1. Gospatric of Northumberland (son of Maldred of Dunbar) was born 1040 in Northumberland, England, and died c. 1080 in Norham, Northumberland, England or c. 1075 in Ubbanford, Scotland[citation needed]. He married Aethelreda of England c. 1059 in Dunbar, East Lothian, Scotland. She was born c. 1042 in Dunbar, East Lothian, Scotland[citation needed]. Gospatric was buried 1080 in Norham, Northumberland, England[citation needed].

Children of Gospatric and Aethelreda are:

  • Dolfin, Lord of Cumbria, born c. 1060, Dunbar, East-Lothian, Scotland[citation needed].
  • 2. Waltheof of Dunbar, Lord of Allerdale
  • 3. Gospatric, 2nd Earl of Dunbar
  • 4. Ethelreda of Northumberland
  • Uchtred of Northumberland (citation needed for existence), born c. 1080, Raby, Durham, England.


[edit] Generation No. 2

2. Waltheof of Dunbar, Lord of Allerdale was born c. 1062/1064 in Dunbar, East Lothian, Scotland or Allerdale, Scotland, and died 1138[citation needed]. He married SIGRID UNBEKANNT c. 1098. She was born c. 1075 in Scotland[citation needed].

children:

  • Alan Baron of Allerdale[citation needed] born c. 1106 Dunbar, East Lothian, Scotland[citation needed]
  • Ochtreda (Ethelreda) born c. 1108 Dunbar, East Lothian, Scotland[citation needed]
  • Gospatric of Bolton born c. 1110 Dunbar, East Lothian, Scotland[citation needed]
  • 5. Gunnild of Dunbar born c. 1124/1134 Dunbar, East Lothian, Scotland[citation needed]


3. Gospatric, 2nd Earl of Dunbar (or 1st Earl of Dunbar), was born c 1062 in Dunbar, East-Lothian, Scotland, and died 22 August 1138 in the Battle of the Standard[citation needed]. He married (1)[citation needed] (name unknown) c. 1087 in Dunbar, East-Lothian, Scotland. She was born c. 1069 in Dunbar, East-Lothian, Scotland[citation needed]. He married (2) Sybil Morel c. 1132 in Dunbar, East-Lothian, Scotland. She was born c. 1105 in Dunbar, East-Lothian, Scotland[citation needed].

Children of Gospatric and Sybil are:

  • 6. Gospatric, 3rd Earl of Dunbar, born c. 1090, Dunbar, East-Lothian, Scotland; died c. 1166, Scotland[citation needed].
  • 7. Edgar of Dunbar, born c. 1135, Dunbar, East-Lothian, Scotland[citation needed].
  • Juliana of Dunbar, born c 1135, Dunbar, East-Lothian, Scotland[citation needed].


4. Ethelreda of Northumberland was born c. 1068 in Dunbar, East Lothian, Scotland[citation needed]. She married Duncan II c. 1082 in Scotland[citation needed].

Child of Ethelreda and Duncan II is:

  • 8. William FitzDuncan, born c. 1082, Morayshire, Scotland[citation needed].


[edit] Information irrelevant to the Gospatric article

[edit] Generation No. 3

5. Gunnild of Dunbar was born c. 1124 in Dunbar, East Lothian, Scotland. She married Uchtred of Galloway c. 1156 in Dunbar, East Lothian, Scotland. He was born c. 1118 in Carrick, Ayrshire, Scotland, and died 22 September 1174.

Children of Gunnild and Uchtred are:

  • a daughter, born c. 1157, Carrick, Ayrshire, Scotland.
  • Fergus of Galloway, born c. 1159, Carrick, Ayrshire, Scotland; died 30 September 1185.
  • Lochlann of Galloway, a.k.a. Roland (citation needed that they are in fact the same person), born c. 1161, Carrick, Ayrshire, Scotland; died between 1164 and 1166.
  • Roland of Galloway, probably the same as Lochlann, born c. 1164, Galloway, Perthshire, Scotland. He died December 1200, Northamptonshire, England and was buried December 1200 in the Abbey of St. Andrew, Northamptonshire, England. Roland married Elena de Morville, c. 1185 in Kirkoswald, Cumberland, England. She was born c. 1172 in Kirkoswald, Cumberland, England. She died 11 June 1217 in Kirkcudbright, Kirkcudbrightshire, Scotland and was buried June 1217 at Abbey Dundrennan, Kirkcudbright, Kirkcudbrightshire, Scotland.


6. GOSPATRICK4 DE DUNBAR (GOSPATRIC OF3 NORTHUMBERLAND, GOSPATRIC OF2, MALDRED OF1 DUNBAR) was born c 1090 in Dunbar, East-Lothian, Scotland, and died c 1166 in Scotland. He married DERDERE c 1121 in Dunbar, East-Lothian, Scotland. She was born c 1094 in Dunbar, East-Lothian, Scotland.

Children of GOSPATRICK DE DUNBAR and DERDERE are: i. UNBEKANNT5 DE DUNBAR, b. c 1124, Dunbar, East-Lothian, Scotland; m. GILLBRIDE MORMAER, c 1150, Northumberland, England; b. c 1118, Forfar, Angusshire, Scotland; d. c 1187, Forfar, Angusshire, Scotland.

More About GILLBRIDE MORMAER: A.K,A: Gillbride Earl of Angus

ii. WALDEVE DE DUNBAR, b. c 1125, Dunbar, East-Lothian, Scotland; d. c 1182, Dunbar, East-Lothian, Scotland; m. AELINA, c 1148, Dunbar, East Lothian, Scotland; b. c 1129, Dunbar, East Lothian, Scotland.


7. EDGAR OF4 DUNBAR (GOSPATRIC OF3 NORTHUMBERLAND, GOSPATRIC OF2, MALDRED OF1 DUNBAR) was born c 1135 in Dunbar, East-Lothian, Scotland. He married ALICE DE GREYSTOKE c 1154 in Grimesthorpe, Lincolnshire, England, daughter of WALTER DE GREYSTOKE and BEATRICE DE FOLKETON. She was born c 1134 in Grimesthorpe, Lincolnshire, England.

More About EDGAR OF DUNBAR: A.K.A: Edgar The Dauntless

AGNES DUNBAR, b. 1160, Yanwath, Westmoreland, England; m. ANSALEM FLEMING, c 1180, Yanwath, Westmoreland, England; b. c 1156, Stanton, Lancashire, England; d. 1210 - 1217, Stanton, Lancashire, England.


8. WILLIAM4 FITZDUNCAN (ETHELREDA OF3 SCOTLAND, GOSPATRIC OF2 NORTHUMBERLAND, MALDRED OF1 DUNBAR) was born c 1082 in Morayshire, Scotland. He married ALICE DE MESCHINES c 1121 in Skipton, Yorkshire, England, daughter of RANULPH DE MESCHINES and LUCY MERCIA. She was born c 1094 in Normandie Province, France.
