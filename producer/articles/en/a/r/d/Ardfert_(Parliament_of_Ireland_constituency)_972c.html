[edit] Ardfert (Parliament of Ireland constituency)

[edit] Boundaries and Boundary Changes

This constituency was based in the town of Ardfert in County Kerry.

Ardfert
Corporation constituency
Created: 1639
Post-Union: Disenfranchised
Type: Irish House of Commons

'Ardfert was a constituency was a constituency represented in the Irish House of Commons to 1800.

Following the Act of Union 1800 the borough was disfranchised.

[edit] History

It was incorporated by charter in 1639 with a Provost, 12 Burgesses and freemen. It had a Corporation, and the electorate consisted of 13 burgesses and 50 freemen.
