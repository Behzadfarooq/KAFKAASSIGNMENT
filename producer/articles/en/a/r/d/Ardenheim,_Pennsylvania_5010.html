Ardenheim, Pennsylvania

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Ardenheim is a village in Huntingdon County about 4 miles east of Huntingdon, Pennsylvania. This village is where the Raystown Branch and Frankstown Branch of the Juniata River meet.
