Ards RFC

Ards Rugby Football Club is based in Newtownards, County Down, Northern Ireland. On the 11th April 1928, Ards Rugby Football Club was formed at meeting in Newtownards Academy pavilion. The first game was played on 13th October 1928.

Ards RFC played most of their early years in the Ulster junior leagues, but in 1977-1978 season they were promoted to the Ulster Senior League. After a downturn in fortunes they spent a second period in the junior leagues. However, in 2002-2003 season Ards 1st XV won through the AIB All Ireland Qualifying round robin and were promoted for a second time, to AIB All Ireland League Division 3. They continue to play in AIL senior rugby.

[edit] Distinguished Past Players

  • Paddy Mayne, Ireland and British and Irish Lions
  • W.H.J. Millar, Ireland
  • Nigel Carr, Ireland and British and Irish Lions
  • Philip Matthews, Ireland
  • Kenny Hooks, Ireland
