Ardy Bernardus Wiranata (b. February 10, 1970) was an Indonesian badminton player.

Wiranata won the silver medal in singles at the 1992 Summer Olympics. He also won the silver medal at the 1989 IBF World Championships, the bronze medal in 1991 and 1993, and six consecutive Indonesian Open title.[1]
