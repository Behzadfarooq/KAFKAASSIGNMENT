Ardrossan Montgomerie Pier railway station was a railway station serving the town of Ardrossan, North Ayrshire, Scotland as part of the Lanarkshire and Ayrshire Railway.

[edit] History

The station opened on 30 May 1890 as Ardrossan Pier. It closed on 1 January 1917 due to wartime economy, reopened 1 February 1919, and upon the grouping of the L&AR into the London, Midland and Scottish Railway in 1923, the station was renamed Ardrossan Montgomerie Pier on 2 June 1924. It officially closed to passengers on 6 May 1968, although the last train ran on 25 September 1967. This station was the last to close on this line in Ayrshire, the rest all having been officially closed by 1932.

Today Montgomerie Pier is the site of private flats overlooking the Ardrossan Marina.
