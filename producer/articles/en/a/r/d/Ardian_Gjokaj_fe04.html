Ardian Gjokaj (Montenegrin variations: Адријан Ђокај, Adrijan Đokaj or Ardijan Đok(ov)ić) is a Montenegrin-born Albanian leftwinger currently playing for Turkish side B.B. Ankaraspor. He was born on May 23, 1979 in Titograd, Montenegro, SFR Yugoslavia. He stands at 1,79cm (five feet ten inches) and weighs 66 kg.

He has previously played for FK Dečić, FK Budućnost Podgorica, Mladost, Mallorca, Lleida, Obilic, OFK Belgrad, Red Star Belgrade, and Trabzonspor.
