Ardmore Air Force Base (IATA: ADM, ICAO: KADM) is a former United States Air Force base in Oklahoma.

It is now known as Ardmore Industrial Airpark, one of the industrial parks operated by the Ardmore Development Authority, and is home to various manufacturing and distribution facilities serving the surrounding region.
