[edit] Previous work

[edit] Primary schools in Northern Ireland

I had been adding Primary schools articles for NI, largely stubs, to which new contributors had started to add to and develop etc, but they came under attack by people proposing that all such primary school articles should be deleted as being non-notable etc. You can see the debate at Wikipedia:Articles for deletion/Abercorn Primary School. Following prolonged discussion the articles are being retained. Very many thanks to all who defended the inclusion of Primary schools articles - for once common sense has prevailed.

[edit] The Troubles in Towns/Villages in Northern Ireland

I had added 97 articles relating to incidents of two or more fatalities in towns/villages in Northern Ireland during the Troubles. Many of these were effectively stubs to be added to and developed, with reports on all fatalities, other incidents etc. However, some people do not seem to understand the stub concept and seek to destroy articles rather than build them up and develop them. These articles have been attacked twice - see Wikipedia:Articles for deletion/The Troubles in Moneymore and Wikipedia:Articles for deletion/The Troubles in Tynan. In both cases the Wiki-destructors have been successfully repelled. Very many thanks to all defenders and particularly to those developing and building the articles.
