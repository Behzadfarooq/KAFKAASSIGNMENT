Ardian Budi (born March 27, 1983 in Bandung, Indonesia) is a System Developer, IT Consultant and also as Gadget Consultant. Now he works at Mobilla Netsmedia and also as a student at MM Bina Nusantara University.

Ardian was born in Bandung, He spent his childhood until High school in Bandung, Paris van Java, but since 2001 he moved to Jakarta to take his study at Bina Nusantara University, majoring Information System. He graduated on July 2005 and now he continued his Magister (Magister Management Young Professional General Management) at JWC Bina Nusantara.
