[edit] The eating of cats

I noticed you use alpha-3 as the language codes. I would recommend using the two-letter codes for the languages that do have it since they are the most common.

I stuck with the Alpha-3 codes from ISO 639 simply because there were more of them--the Alpha-2 codes are entirely missing for many languages, like Cherokee, ancient Egyptian, and Filipino. --Ardonik.talk()* 04:21, Apr 4, 2005 (UTC)
