Arda Peak (Vrah Arda \'vr&h 'ar-da\) is located in the east of Livingston Island, Antarctica. The peak rises to approximately 470 m and is located in the Friesland Ridge, Tangra Mountains, overlooking the lower course of Charity Glacier to the northwest and Tarnovo Ice Piedmont to the southeast. The peak is named after the Arda River in Bulgaria.

[edit] Location

Arda Peak is located at 62°44′23″S, 60°17′48″W which is 470 m south of Gabrovo Knoll, 850 m east by north of the summit of Veleka Ridge and 3 km west of Yambol Peak.
