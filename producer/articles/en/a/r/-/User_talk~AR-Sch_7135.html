Hi and welcome! I noticed your new articles like House of Kadies. Good work! Did you know you can make redirects that will send a reader to an article if they type in a related word by going to the top of the page you want to turn into a redirect and typing #REDIRECT [[<name of page you want to redirect to>]]? That way duplicate articles won't get made, which can be a problem if someone changes one and not the others. You can also check out Wikipedia:how to write a great article and WP:MOS for more info. Thanks for contributing and helping out! Please don't hesitate to ask me on my talk page if you have any questions or anything. Peace, delldot | talk 21:04, 20 January 2006 (UTC)
