Arvee Enterprise or also known as ARVEE is a Malaysian company. Arvee Enterprise is an Importer of various types of books & teaching aids. Arvee Enterprise is part of Malaysian Book Distributor & Malaysian Book Importers. With a number of suppliers & retailers procuring books from Arvee, it can be said that we are also a Malaysian Book Wholesaler.

Started as a Sole Proprietorship company by its owner, Ravivarma Sanmugam, in 2001, Arvee ventured into Books distribution to all educational institutes in Malaysia. Arvee first operated from the proprietor's hometown, Seremban, Negeri Sembilan.

Since 2001, Arvee has capitalised on the growing popularity of Unique & Different types of books by importing & stocking both English-language and Tamil-Language books, comics as well as games which is not easily obtained in Malaysian schools for mainly Primary Schools and Secondary School. Steadily, yet confidently Arvee has plans on going global. As a first step towards that, a website, Arvee Books - Malaysia Online Bookstore has been created with all available titles in stock. This website, caters for all those looking for Online Book Store in Malaysia.
