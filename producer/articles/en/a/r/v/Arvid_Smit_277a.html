Arvid Smith (born October 8, 1980 in Heerhugowaard, Noord-Holland) is a Dutch footballer playing for C.S. Marítimo in the Superliga on loan from PSV Eindhoven. His former clubs are De Graafschap, Willem II and PSV Eindhoven.

On January 29, 2007 he played his first Superliga game for Maritimo against Nacional [1]
