Arve Walde (born May 19, 1985 in Bergen) is a footballer from Bergen, Norway. He is a younger brother of former Brann defender Knut Walde.

[edit] Career

Walde debuted for Brann's first team in 2003, but apart from a short period in 2004, he never managed to establish himself in the starting line-up. In 2006, Walde was loaned out to the local club Løv-Ham, where he reunited with his brother Knut who is the skipper on Løv-Ham. The clubs agreed on a one year loan deal, and the plan is for Walde to return to Brann after the 2006 season. Walde also signed a two-year contract with Brann before being loaned out to "The Lions".

[edit] Trivia

  • In a match on September 3, 2006 against the top team Aalesund, both Arve and his brother Knut scored an own goal, causing Løv-Ham to lose 1–2.
