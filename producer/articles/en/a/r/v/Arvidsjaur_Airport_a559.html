Arvidsjaur Airport (IATA: AJR, ICAO: ESNX) is situated 13 km from Arvidsjaur town in Sweden and had 45 000 passengers in 2005.

[edit] Airlines and destinations

  • Scandinavian Airlines
    • operated by Cimber Air (Copenhagen Airport) (seasonal)
  • Skyways Express (Lycksele Airport, Stockholm-Arlanda)

In the winter there are special flights from Frankfurt, Stuttgart, Hannover and München, in Germany, about 2-3 flights per week. Close to these airports there are major German car producers, who perform lot of snow and cold temperature testing in the Arvidsjaur region.
