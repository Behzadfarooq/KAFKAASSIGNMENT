Arvīds Reķis

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Arvīds Reķis (born January 1, 1979 in Jūrmala, USSR) is a Latvian ice hockey player.

The defenceman is 1.80 m tall and weighs 90 kg (198 lb). He is currently playing for German team Augsburger Panther.
