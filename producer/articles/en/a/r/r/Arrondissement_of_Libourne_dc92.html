The arrondissement of Blaye is an arrondissement of France, located in the Gironde department, in the Aquitaine region. It has 9 cantons and 129 communes.

[edit] Composition

[edit] Cantons

The cantons of the arrondissement of Libourne are:

  1. Branne
  2. Castillon-la-Bataille
  3. Coutras
  4. Fronsac
  5. Guîtres
  6. Libourne
  7. Lussac
  8. Pujols
  9. Sainte-Foy-la-Grande
