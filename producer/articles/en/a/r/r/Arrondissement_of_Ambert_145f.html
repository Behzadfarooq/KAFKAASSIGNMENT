The arrondissement of Ambert is an arrondissement of France, located in the Puy-de-Dôme département, in the Auvergne région. It has 8 cantons and 55 communes.

[edit] Composition

[edit] Cantons

The cantons of the arrondissement of Ambert are:

  1. Ambert
  2. Arlanc
  3. Cunlhat
  4. Olliergues
  5. Saint-Amant-Roche-Savine
  6. Saint-Anthème
  7. Saint-Germain-l'Herm
  8. Viverols
