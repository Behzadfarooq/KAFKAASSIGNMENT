The arrondissement of Largentière is an arrondissement of France, located in the Ardèche département, in the Rhône-Alpes région. It has 10 cantons and 103 communes.

[edit] Composition

[edit] Cantons

The cantons of the arrondissement of Largentière are:

  1. Burzet
  2. Coucouron
  3. Joyeuse
  4. Largentière
  5. Montpezat-sous-Bauzon
  6. Saint-Étienne-de-Lugdarès
  7. Thueyts
  8. Valgorge
  9. Vallon-Pont-d'Arc
  10. Les Vans
