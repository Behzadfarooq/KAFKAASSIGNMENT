The arrondissement of Meaux is an arrondissement of France, located in the Seine-et-Marne département, in the Île-de-France région. It has 9 cantons and 146 communes.

[edit] Composition

[edit] Cantons

The cantons of the arrondissement of Meaux are:

  1. Coulommiers
  2. Crécy-la-Chapelle
  3. Dammartin-en-Goële
  4. La Ferté-sous-Jouarre
  5. Lizy-sur-Ourcq
  6. Meaux-Nord
  7. Meaux-Sud
  8. Mitry-Mory
  9. Rebais
