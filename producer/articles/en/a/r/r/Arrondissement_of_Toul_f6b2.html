The arrondissement of Toul is an arrondissement of France, located in the Meurthe-et-Moselle département, in the Lorraine région. It has 5 cantons and 112 communes.

[edit] Composition

[edit] Cantons

The cantons of the arrondissement of Toul are:

  1. Colombey-les-Belles
  2. Domèvre-en-Haye
  3. Thiaucourt-Regniéville
  4. Toul-Nord
  5. Toul-Sud
