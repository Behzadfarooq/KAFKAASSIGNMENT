The arrondissement of Château-Gontier is an arrondissement of France, located in the Mayenne département, in the Pays de la Loire région. It has 7 cantons and 71 communes.

[edit] Composition

[edit] Cantons

The cantons of the arrondissement of Château-Gontier are:

  1. Bierné
  2. Château-Gontier-Est
  3. Château-Gontier-Ouest
  4. Cossé-le-Vivien
  5. Craon
  6. Grez-en-Bouère
  7. Saint-Aignan-sur-Roë
