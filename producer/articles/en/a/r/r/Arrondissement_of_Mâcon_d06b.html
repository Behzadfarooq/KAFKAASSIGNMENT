The arrondissement of Mâcon is an arrondissement of France, located in the Saône-et-Loire département, in the Bourgogne région. It has 10 cantons and 124 communes.

[edit] Composition

[edit] Cantons

The cantons of the arrondissement of Mâcon are:

  1. La Chapelle-de-Guinchay
  2. Cluny
  3. Lugny
  4. Mâcon-Centre
  5. Mâcon-Nord
  6. Mâcon-Sud
  7. Matour
  8. Saint-Gengoux-le-National
  9. Tournus
  10. Tramayes
