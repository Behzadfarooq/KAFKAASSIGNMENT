The arrondissement of Niort is an arrondissement of France, located in the Deux-Sèvres département, in the Poitou-Charentes région. It has 18 cantons and 166 communes.

[edit] Cantons

The cantons of the arrondissement of Niort are:

  1. Beauvoir-sur-Niort
  2. Brioux-sur-Boutonne
  3. Celles-sur-Belle

  5. Chef-Boutonne
  6. Coulonges-sur-l'Autize
  7. Frontenay-Rohan-Rohan

  9. Mauzé-sur-le-Mignon
  10. Melle
  11. La Mothe-Saint-Héray
  12. Niort-Est
  13. Niort-Nord
  14. Niort-Ouest
  15. Prahecq
  16. Saint-Maixent-l'École 1st Canton
  17. Saint-Maixent-l'École 2nd Canton
