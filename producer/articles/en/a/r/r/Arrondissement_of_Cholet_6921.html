The arrondissement of Cholet is an arrondissement of France, located in the Maine-et-Loire département, in the Pays de la Loire région. It has 9 cantons and 78 communes.

[edit] Cantons

The cantons of the arrondissement of Cholet are:

  1. Beaupréau
  2. Champtoceaux
  3. Chemillé
  4. Cholet 1st Canton
  5. Cholet 2nd Canton
  6. Cholet 3rd Canton
  7. Montfaucon-Montigné
  8. Montrevault
  9. Saint-Florent-le-Vieil
