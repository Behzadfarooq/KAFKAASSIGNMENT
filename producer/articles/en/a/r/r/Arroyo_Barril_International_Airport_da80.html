This airport is used mostly for turism. Some airlines make domestic flights and international flights with this airport. In this airport is based the dominican airline Aerodomca; It makes flights to Santo Domingo and Punta Cana, also it makes charter services.


[edit] Airlines and destinations

  • Aeronaves Dominicanas (Santo Domingo, Santiago, Puerto Plata, Punta Cana, Puerto Principe, San Juan[charter service])
  • Air Santo Domingo (Santo Domingo)
  • Caribair (Santo Domingo, Punta Cana)
