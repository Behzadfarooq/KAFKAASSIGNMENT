The arrondissement of Mortagne-au-Perche is an arrondissement of France, located in the Orne département, in the Basse-Normandie région. It has 12 cantons and 146 communes.

[edit] Composition

[edit] Cantons

The cantons of the arrondissement of Mortagne-au-Perche are:

  1. L'Aigle-Est
  2. L'Aigle-Ouest
  3. Bazoches-sur-Hoëne
  4. Bellême
  5. Longny-au-Perche
  6. Mortagne-au-Perche
  7. Moulins-la-Marche
  8. Nocé
  9. Pervenchères
  10. Rémalard
  11. Le Theil
  12. Tourouvre
