The arrondissement of Vire is an arrondissement of France, located in the Calvados department, in the Basse-Normandie region. It has 6 cantons and 88 communes.

[edit] Composition

[edit] Cantons

The cantons of the arrondissement of Vire are:

  1. Aunay-sur-Odon
  2. Le Bény-Bocage
  3. Condé-sur-Noireau
  4. Saint-Sever-Calvados
  5. Vassy
  6. Vire
