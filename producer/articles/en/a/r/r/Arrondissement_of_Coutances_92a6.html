The arrondissement of Coutances is an arrondissement of France, located in the Manche département, in the Basse-Normandie région. It has 10 cantons and 130 communes.

[edit] Composition

[edit] Cantons

The cantons of the arrondissement of Coutances are:

  1. Bréhal
  2. Cerisy-la-Salle
  3. Coutances
  4. Gavray
  5. La Haye-du-Puits
  6. Lessay
  7. Montmartin-sur-Mer
  8. Périers
  9. Saint-Malo-de-la-Lande
  10. Saint-Sauveur-Lendelin
