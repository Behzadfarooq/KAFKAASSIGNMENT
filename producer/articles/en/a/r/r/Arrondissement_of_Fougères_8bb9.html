The arrondissement of Fougères is an arrondissement of France, located in the Ille-et-Vilaine département, in the Bretagne région. It has 6 cantons and 57 communes.

[edit] Composition

[edit] Cantons

The cantons of the arrondissement of Fougères are:

  1. Antrain
  2. Fougères-Nord
  3. Fougères-Sud
  4. Louvigné-du-Désert
  5. Saint-Aubin-du-Cormier
  6. Saint-Brice-en-Coglès
