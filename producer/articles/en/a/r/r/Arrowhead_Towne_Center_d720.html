Arrowhead Towne Center is a regional enclosed shopping mall located in Glendale, Arizona. The mall is managed by The Macerich Company. It is anchored by Macy's, Sears, Dillard's, JCPenney, Mervyn's, and AMC Theatres. It is currently the only enclosed shopping mall in the West Valley suburbs of Phoenix, though several new malls are in the planning stages.

Arrowhead Towne Center will soon be home to the West Valley's first Sephora, J. Jill, and Coach stores. All of these tenants are new to Glendale.

  • AMC Theatres 14 Plex (46,123 sq. ft.)
  • Dillard's (212,706 sq. ft.)
  • JCPenney (160,173 sq. ft.)
  • Macy's (200,000 sq. ft.)
  • Mervyn's (83,232 sq. ft.)
  • Sears (109,416 sq. ft.)
