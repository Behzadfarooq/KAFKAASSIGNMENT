The arrondissement of Limoux is an arrondissement of France, located in the Aude département, in the Languedoc-Roussillon région. It has 8 cantons and 149 communes.

[edit] Composition

[edit] Cantons

The cantons of the arrondissement of Limoux are:

  1. Alaigne
  2. Axat
  3. Belcaire
  4. Chalabre
  5. Couiza
  6. Limoux
  7. Quillan
  8. Saint-Hilaire
