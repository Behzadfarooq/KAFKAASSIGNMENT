The arrondissement of Condom is an arrondissement of France, located in the Gers département, in the Midi-Pyrénées région. It has 11 cantons and 159 communes.

[edit] Cantons

The cantons of the arrondissement of Condom are:

  1. Cazaubon
  2. Condom
  3. Eauze
  4. Fleurance
  5. Lectoure
  6. Mauvezin
  7. Miradoux
  8. Montréal
  9. Nogaro
  10. Saint-Clar
  11. Valence-sur-Baïse
