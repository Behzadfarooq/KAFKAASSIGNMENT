User:ArrEmmDee

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

ArrEmmDee is a guy on the internets. His primary interests are Pokémon, Kirby, Transformers, House, and LOST (not necessarily in that order), but this is entirely irrelevant and most likely not the reason you are on this page. This is an absolute shame because these are really the only things the guy is informed enough on to talk about, since he's just a 17 year old chap in Canada named Mark, son to immigrants from the Balkans. Secretly, he is better known as his alternate persona, Stone Phillips.

You can usually find him at Kirby's Rainbow Resort, where he resides as a member of staff even though he doesn't do all that much very often and usually just sits around complaining about things. He also lurks and occasionally posts on several Pokémon communities such as Smogon and Serebii.net, the latter of which he hates with a burning passion. He also has a website which is never updated. Though if you need to talk to him, arremmdee (at) gmail (dot) com is both an e-mail address and a Windows Live Messenger account!

[edit] Wikipedia Contributions

Most of ArrEmmDee's edits are minor ones, but he has done significant editing on articles such as King Dedede, and started the article on Kirby's Rainbow Resort. He has interest in editing the article on Tropicana Twister since that's all he's been drinking lately, and will also probably mess around with the Pokémon Trading Card Game articles since for the most part they are terrible.

[edit] Name

ArrEmmDee is a hardcore phonetic spelling of RMD, short for RMDDD, short for Robo Mecha Dedede, a large fat mechanical penguin. Robo Mecha refers to ArrEmmDee's admiration for mechas and the sort, and the Dedede refers to King Dedede, the main antagonist in the Kirby series. However, this is an unfitting persona for a rather normal guy, so ArrEmmDee now prefers to have the name refer to himself as opposed to any fan-made character, though that does not mean the character is not still known as ArrEmmDee. Because it still is.

[edit] Pokémon

Ever since getting Pokémon Blue for Christmas, ArrEmmDee has been a Pokémon fanatic, collecting cards, playing games, and just generally spending the majority of the time expanse between that fateful day and the present moment on his most certainly unhealthy obsession. He believes the magnum opus of the series is Pokémon Gold and Silver, but is no longer in possession of any of these games after having a garage sale several years ago when his Game Boy Color decided to act very stupid. However, his current collection now contains Pokémon Red, Pokémon LeafGreen, Pokémon Colosseum and his much despised Pokémon XD.

His favourite Pokémon is Slowbro, with Espeon, Wobbuffet, Exeggutor, Porygon, Mudkip, and several others close behind. The majority of his favourite Pokémon are either Psychic or Water types, but this is a strange coincidence since his favourite type is the Electric type.

He owns two Alt. Color Pokémon, Butterfree and Porygon2, which has spurred him to always be on the lookout for these sorts of Pokémon when playing his games.
