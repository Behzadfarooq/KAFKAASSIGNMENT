The arrondissement of Pamiers is an arrondissement of France, located in the Ariège département, in the Midi-Pyrénées région. It has 7 cantons and 115 communes.

[edit] Composition

[edit] Cantons

The cantons of the arrondissement of Pamiers are:

  1. Le Fossat
  2. Le Mas-d'Azil
  3. Mirepoix
  4. Pamiers-Est
  5. Pamiers-Ouest
  6. Saverdun
  7. Varilhes
