The arrondissement of Louhans is an arrondissement of France, located in the Saône-et-Loire département, in the Bourgogne région. It has 8 cantons and 79 communes.

[edit] Composition

[edit] Cantons

The cantons of the arrondissement of Louhans are:

  1. Beaurepaire-en-Bresse
  2. Cuiseaux
  3. Cuisery
  4. Louhans
  5. Montpont-en-Bresse
  6. Montret
  7. Pierre-de-Bresse
  8. Saint-Germain-du-Bois
