The arrondissement of Montdidier is an arrondissement of France, located in the Somme département, in the Picardie région. It has 5 cantons and 132 communes.

[edit] Composition

[edit] Cantons

The cantons of the arrondissement of Montdidier are:

  1. Ailly-sur-Noye
  2. Montdidier
  3. Moreuil
  4. Rosières-en-Santerre
  5. Roye
