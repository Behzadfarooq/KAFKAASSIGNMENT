The Arrondissement of Bayeux an arrondissement of France, located in the Calvados département, in the Basse-Normandie région. It has 6 cantons and 126 communes.

[edit] Composition

[edit] Cantons

The cantons of the arrondissement of Bayeux are:

  1. Balleroy
  2. Bayeux
  3. Caumont-l'Éventé
  4. Isigny-sur-Mer
  5. Ryes
  6. Trévières
