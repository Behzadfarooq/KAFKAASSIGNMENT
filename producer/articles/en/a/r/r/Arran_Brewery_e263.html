The Arran Brewery is an independent brewery founded in March 2000 in Cladach, near Brodick,on the Isle of Arran in Scotland.

[edit] Brief details

Arran Blonde

The brewery produces cask ale and filtered beer in bottles.


[edit] Availability

The cask ales are available in various pubs around Britain and at British beer festivals. The filtered bottled ales are sold throughout Britain and in North America.

[edit] Beers

The brewery produces three regular beers: Arran Ale 3.8%, Arran Dark 4.3% and the wheat beer Arran Blonde 5.0%; and two seasonals - one in summer and in winter the dark and gingery Arran Fireside. The most popular beer is Arran Blonde.
