The arrondissement of Abbeville is an arrondissement of the Somme département, in the Picardie région of France. It has 12 cantons and 169 communes.

[edit] Composition

[edit] Cantons

The cantons of the arrondissement of Abbeville are:

  1. Abbeville-Nord
  2. Abbeville-Sud
  3. Ailly-le-Haut-Clocher
  4. Ault
  5. Crécy-en-Ponthieu
  6. Friville-Escarbotin
  7. Gamaches
  8. Hallencourt
  9. Moyenneville
  10. Nouvion
  11. Rue
  12. Saint-Valery-sur-Somme
