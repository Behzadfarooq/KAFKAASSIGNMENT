The arrondissement of Foix is an arrondissement of France, located in the Ariège département, in the Midi-Pyrénées région. It has 9 cantons and 135 communes.

[edit] Composition

[edit] Cantons

The cantons of the arrondissement of Foix are:

  1. Ax-les-Thermes
  2. La Bastide-de-Sérou
  3. Les Cabannes
  4. Foix-Rural
  5. Foix-Ville
  6. Lavelanet
  7. Quérigut
  8. Tarascon-sur-Ariège
  9. Vicdessos
