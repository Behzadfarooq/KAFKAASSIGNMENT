The arrondissement of Langon is an arrondissement of France, located in the Gironde department, in the Aquitaine region. It has 13 cantons and 169 communes.

[edit] Composition

[edit] Cantons

The cantons of the arrondissement of Langon are:

  1. Auros
  2. Bazas
  3. Captieux
  4. Grignols
  5. Langon
  6. Monségur
  7. Pellegrue
  8. La Réole
  9. Saint-Macaire
  10. Saint-Symphorien
  11. Sauveterre-de-Guyenne
  12. Targon
  13. Villandraut
