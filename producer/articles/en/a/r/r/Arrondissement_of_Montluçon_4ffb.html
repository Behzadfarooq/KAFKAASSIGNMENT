The arrondissement of Montluçon is an arrondissement of France, located in the Allier département, in the Auvergne région. It has 12 cantons and 106 communes.

[edit] Composition

[edit] Cantons

The cantons of the arrondissement of Montluçon are:

  1. Cérilly
  2. Commentry
  3. Domérat-Montluçon-Nord-Ouest
  4. Ébreuil
  5. Hérisson
  6. Huriel
  7. Marcillat-en-Combraille
  8. Montluçon-Est
  9. Montluçon-Nord-Est
  10. Montluçon-Ouest
  11. Montluçon-Sud
  12. Montmarault
