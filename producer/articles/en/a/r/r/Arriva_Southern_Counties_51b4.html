Arriva Southern Counties is a bus operator in London, Surrey, West Sussex, East Sussex and Kent in England. It is a division of Arriva.

[edit] History

Originally founded in 1995 as Invictaway Ltd., it was renamed Arriva Southern Counties in 1997.

Invictaway Ltd. was the management services company set up by the then owners British Bus to run Maidstone & District (M&D), Kentish Bus and Londonlinks. The offices were (and still are) based in Maidstone. Invictaway was the name of the Maidstone & District commuter services to London, which were renamed Green Line at the same time. Invictaway Ltd. was a renaming of Einkorn Ltd., the company that bought M&D from the National Bus Company in 1986.

Upon the take over of British Bus by Cowie Group and the subsequent renaming of Cowie to Arriva, the operating companies became as follows:

Maidstone & District:

  • Mainly became Arriva Kent & Sussex Ltd.
  • Medway area became Arriva Medway Towns Ltd.

Kentish Bus:

  • Mainly became Arriva Kent Thameside Ltd.
  • Partly became Arriva Kent & Sussex Ltd.

London & Country:

  • Became Guildford & West Surrey Ltd.

LondonLinks:

  • Became Croydon & North Surrey

Current operations in the Southern Counties area are:

  • Arriva Kent & Sussex
  • Arriva Kent Thameside
  • Arriva Medway Towns
  • Arriva Guildford & West Surrey
  • Arriva Maidstone
  • Arriva Southend

Many reorganisations of Arriva operations in the South East have followed since, and today the company has also control of Arriva Guildford & West Surrey Ltd., Arriva West Sussex. and Arriva Southend Ltd. (and, for a time before sale, Arriva Colchester Ltd.). The Croydon & North Surrey subsidiary was largely transferred to Arriva London.

Arriva Colchester was sold in 2003 to Tellings-Golden Miller. The coaching subsidiary, New Enterprise Coaches (Tonbridge) Ltd., inherited from Maidstone & District, remains in operation.

In June 2004, 61 new buses entered the fleet, as part of Arriva's original "Operation Overdrive". This meant that the Medway Towns depot would receive brand new vehicles with latest technology installed to replace old ones. New low floor buses in this depot would be refurbished. "Overdrive 2" entailed the refurbishment of some vehicles at Maidstone depot. Similar "Overdrive" projects have since followed on Merseyside and in County Durham.

[edit] Guildford Park and Ride

Arriva operates the Guildford park and ride for Surrey County Council. The buses are in the same style of livery, with oak tree leaves on the sides, but in different colours.

There are 4 car parks.

  • Artington: Route 200 (Monday - Saturday)
  • Spectrum Lesiure Centre: Route 100 (Monday - Friday)
  • Ladymead: Route 101 (Saturday only)

Note: the Ladymead P&R site replaces the Spectrum Lesiure Centre car park on Saturdays because the Spectrum car park is too small for Park and Ride and the lesuire centre purposes on a Saturaday

  • University of Surrey Car Park 3: Normal Arriva Routes 3, 26, 27, 36, 37 (Saturadys only)

[edit] Fastrack

This is a new scheme to improve the transport around the Kent Thames Gateway area. Arriva run Fastrack service B on behalf of Kent County Council. Service B will link Gravesend, Dartford and the Bluewater Shopping Centre with each other.

Route A was due to start at the end of 2006, but has not yet commenced operation.

[edit] Mercedes-Benz World

Three Mercedes-Benz Citaros were bought for service 436. This route used to be: Guildford - Burpham - Direct on A3 - Ripley - Sendmarsh - Send - Kingfield - Woking - Sheerwater - West Byfleet - Byfleet - Manor Farm - Brooklands M&S/Tescos.

Now it is: Woking - Sheerwater - West Byfleet - Byfleet - Manor Farm - Mercedes-Benz World - Brooklands Museum - Weybridge Station.

Arriva have totally withdrawn the Woking - Guildford section, with Countryliner Coaches making their 463 the 463 and 462. A new route (536), also operated by Countryliner, is a limited shopping bus service, retaining the link from Guildford to Brooklands Tesco.


The Citaros are in the normal style of Arriva livery, see below, but with light blue instead of Cream, and Silver in place of the aquamqrine. Despite being 56 registered, the buses are only Euro 3-engined.
