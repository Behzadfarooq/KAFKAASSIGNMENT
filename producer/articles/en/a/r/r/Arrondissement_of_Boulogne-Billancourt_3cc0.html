The arrondissement of Boulogne-Billancourt is an arrondissement of France, located in the Hauts-de-Seine département, in the Île-de-France région. It has 9 cantons and 9 communes.

[edit] Composition

[edit] Cantons

The cantons of the arrondissement of Boulogne-Billancourt are:

  1. Boulogne-Billancourt-Nord-Est
  2. Boulogne-Billancourt-Nord-Ouest
  3. Boulogne-Billancourt-Sud
  4. Chaville
  5. Issy-les-Moulineaux-Est
  6. Issy-les-Moulineaux-Ouest
  7. Meudon
  8. Saint-Cloud
  9. Sèvres

[edit] Communes

The communes of the arrondissement of Boulogne-Billancourt are:

1. Boulogne-Billancourt (92012) 2. Chaville (92022) 3. Issy-les-Moulineaux (92040) 4. Marnes-la-Coquette (92047)
5. Meudon (92048) 6. Saint-Cloud (92064) 7. Sèvres (92072) 8. Vaucresson (92076)
9. Ville-d'Avray (92077)
