The arrondissement of Morlaix is an arrondissement of France, located in the Finistère department, in the Bretagne region. It has 10 cantons and 60 communes.

[edit] Composition

[edit] Cantons

The cantons of the arrondissement of Morlaix are:

  1. Landivisiau
  2. Lanmeur
  3. Morlaix
  4. Plouescat
  5. Plouigneau
  6. Plouzévédé
  7. Saint-Pol-de-Léon
  8. Saint-Thégonnec
  9. Sizun
  10. Taulé
