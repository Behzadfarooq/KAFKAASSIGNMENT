The arrondissement of L'Haÿ-les-Roses is an arrondissement of France, located in the Val-de-Marne département, in the Île-de-France région. It has 9 cantons and 10 communes.

[edit] Composition

[edit] Cantons

The cantons of the arrondissement of L'Haÿ-les-Roses are:

  1. Arcueil
  2. Cachan
  3. Chevilly-Larue
  4. Fresnes
  5. L'Haÿ-les-Roses
  6. Le Kremlin-Bicêtre
  7. Thiais
  8. Villejuif-Est
  9. Villejuif-Ouest

[edit] Communes

The communes of the arrondissement of L'Haÿ-les-Roses are:

1. Arcueil (94003) 2. Cachan (94016) 3. Chevilly-Larue (94021) 4. Fresnes (94034)
5. Gentilly (94037) 6. L'Haÿ-les-Roses (94038) 7. Le Kremlin-Bicêtre (94043) 8. Rungis (94065)
9. Thiais (94073) 10. Villejuif (94076)
