The arrondissement of Sélestat-Erstein is an arrondissement of France, located in the Bas-Rhin département, in the Alsace région. It has 7 cantons and 101 communes.

[edit] Composition

[edit] Cantons

The cantons of the arrondissement of Sélestat-Erstein are:

  1. Barr
  2. Benfeld
  3. Erstein
  4. Marckolsheim
  5. Obernai
  6. Sélestat
  7. Villé
