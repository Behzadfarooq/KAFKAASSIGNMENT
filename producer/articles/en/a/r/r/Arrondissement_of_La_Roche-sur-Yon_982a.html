The arrondissement of La Roche-sur-Yon is an arrondissement of France, located in the Vendée département, in the Pays de la Loire région. It has 11 cantons and 92 communes.

[edit] Composition

[edit] Cantons

The cantons of the arrondissement of La Roche-sur-Yon are:

  1. Chantonnay
  2. Les Essarts
  3. Les Herbiers
  4. Mareuil-sur-Lay-Dissais
  5. Montaigu
  6. Mortagne-sur-Sèvre
  7. Le Poiré-sur-Vie
  8. Rocheservière
  9. La Roche-sur-Yon-Nord
  10. La Roche-sur-Yon-Sud
  11. Saint-Fulgent
