The arrondissement of Roanne is an arrondissement of France, located in the Loire department, in the Rhône-Alpes region. It has 11 cantons and 115 communes.

[edit] Composition

[edit] Cantons

The cantons of the arrondissement of Roanne are:

  1. Belmont-de-la-Loire
  2. Charlieu
  3. Néronde
  4. La Pacaudière
  5. Perreux
  6. Roanne-Nord
  7. Roanne-Sud
  8. Saint-Germain-Laval
  9. Saint-Haon-le-Châtel
  10. Saint-Just-en-Chevalet
  11. Saint-Symphorien-de-Lay
