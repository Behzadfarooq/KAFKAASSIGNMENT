The arrondissement of Saint-Claude is an arrondissement of France, located in the Jura département, in the Franche-Comté région. It has 5 cantons and 71 communes.

[edit] Composition

[edit] Cantons

The cantons of the arrondissement of Saint-Claude are:

  1. Les Bouchoux
  2. Moirans-en-Montagne
  3. Morez
  4. Saint-Claude
  5. Saint-Laurent-en-Grandvaux
