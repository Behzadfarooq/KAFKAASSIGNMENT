The arrondissement of Bernay is an arrondissement of France, located in the Eure département, in the Haute-Normandie région. It has 15 cantons and 239 communes.

[edit] Cantons

The cantons of the arrondissement of Bernay are:

  1. Beaumesnil
  2. Beaumont-le-Roger
  3. Bernay-Est
  4. Bernay-Ouest
  5. Beuzeville
  6. Bourgtheroulde-Infreville
  7. Brionne
  8. Broglie
  9. Cormeilles
  10. Montfort-sur-Risle
  11. Pont-Audemer
  12. Quillebeuf-sur-Seine
  13. Routot
  14. Saint-Georges-du-Vièvre
  15. Thiberville
