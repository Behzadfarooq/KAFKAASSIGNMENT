The arrondissement of Saint-Omer is an arrondissement of France, located in the Pas-de-Calais département, in the Nord-Pas-de-Calais région. It has 8 cantons and 116 communes.

[edit] Composition

[edit] Cantons

The cantons of the arrondissement of Saint-Omer are:

  1. Aire-sur-la-Lys
  2. Ardres
  3. Arques
  4. Audruicq
  5. Fauquembergues
  6. Lumbres
  7. Saint-Omer-Nord
  8. Saint-Omer-Sud
