The arrondissement of Nogent-sur-Seine is an arrondissement of France, located in the Aube département, in the Champagne-Ardenne région. It has 6 cantons and 82 communes.

[edit] Composition

[edit] Cantons

The cantons of the arrondissement of Nogent-sur-Seine are:

  1. Marcilly-le-Hayer
  2. Méry-sur-Seine
  3. Nogent-sur-Seine
  4. Romilly-sur-Seine 1st Canton
  5. Romilly-sur-Seine 2nd Canton
  6. Villenauxe-la-Grande
