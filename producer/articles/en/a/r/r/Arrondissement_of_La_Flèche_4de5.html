The arrondissement of La Flèche is an arrondissement of France, located in the Sarthe département, in the Pays de la Loire région. It has 7 cantons and 71 communes.

[edit] Composition

[edit] Cantons

The cantons of the arrondissement of La Flèche are:

  1. Brûlon
  2. La Flèche
  3. Le Lude
  4. Malicorne-sur-Sarthe
  5. Mayet
  6. Pontvallain
  7. Sablé-sur-Sarthe
