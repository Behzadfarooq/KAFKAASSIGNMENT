The arrondissement of Rambouillet is an arrondissement of France, located in the Yvelines département, in the Île-de-France région. It has 5 cantons and 81 communes.

[edit] Composition

[edit] Cantons

The cantons of the arrondissement of Rambouillet are:

  1. Chevreuse
  2. Maurepas
  3. Montfort-l'Amaury
  4. Rambouillet
  5. Saint-Arnoult-en-Yvelines
