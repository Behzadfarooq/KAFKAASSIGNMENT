The arrondissement of Cognac is an arrondissement of France, located in the Charente département, in the Poitou-Charentes région. It has 8 cantons and 96 communes.

[edit] Composition

[edit] Cantons

The cantons of the arrondissement of Cognac are:

  1. Baignes-Sainte-Radegonde
  2. Barbezieux-Saint-Hilaire
  3. Brossac
  4. Châteauneuf-sur-Charente
  5. Cognac-Nord
  6. Cognac-Sud
  7. Jarnac
  8. Segonzac
