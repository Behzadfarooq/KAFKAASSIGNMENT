The arrondissement of Mauriac is an arrondissement of France, located in the Cantal département, of the Auvergne région. It has 6 cantons and 55 communes.

[edit] Composition

[edit] Cantons

The cantons of the arrondissement of Mauriac are:

  1. Champs-sur-Tarentaine-Marchal
  2. Mauriac
  3. Pleaux
  4. Riom-ès-Montagnes
  5. Saignes
  6. Salers
