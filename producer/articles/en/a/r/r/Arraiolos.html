Arraiolos (pron. IPA: [ɐʁɐi'ɔluʃ]) is a municipality in Portugal with a total area of 683.0 km² and a total population of 7,389 inhabitants.

The municipality is located in the District of Évora.

The present Mayor is Jerónimo Loios, elected by the Unitarian Democratic Coalition.
