The arrondissement of Le Vigan is an arrondissement of France, located in the Gard department, in the Languedoc-Roussillon region. It has 10 cantons and 75 communes.

[edit] Composition

[edit] Cantons

The cantons of the arrondissement of Le Vigan are:

  1. Alzon
  2. Lasalle
  3. Quissac
  4. Saint-André-de-Valborgne
  5. Saint-Hippolyte-du-Fort
  6. Sauve
  7. Sumène
  8. Trèves
  9. Valleraugue
  10. Le Vigan
