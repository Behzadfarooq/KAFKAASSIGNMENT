The arrondissement of Marmande is an arrondissement of France, located in the Lot-et-Garonne département, in the Aquitaine région. It has 10 cantons and 97 communes.

[edit] Composition

[edit] Cantons

The cantons of the arrondissement of Marmande are:

  1. Bouglon
  2. Castelmoron-sur-Lot
  3. Duras
  4. Lauzun
  5. Marmande-Est
  6. Marmande-Ouest
  7. Le Mas-d'Agenais
  8. Meilhan-sur-Garonne
  9. Seyches
  10. Tonneins
