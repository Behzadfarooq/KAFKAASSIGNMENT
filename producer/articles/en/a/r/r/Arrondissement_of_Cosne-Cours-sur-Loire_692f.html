The arrondissement of Cosne-Cours-sur-Loire is an arrondissement of France, located in the Nièvre département, in the Bourgogne région. It has 7 cantons and 64 communes.

[edit] Composition

[edit] Cantons

The cantons of the arrondissement of Cosne-Cours-sur-Loire are:

  1. La Charité-sur-Loire
  2. Cosne-Cours-sur-Loire-Nord
  3. Cosne-Cours-sur-Loire-Sud
  4. Donzy
  5. Pouilly-sur-Loire
  6. Prémery
  7. Saint-Amand-en-Puisaye
