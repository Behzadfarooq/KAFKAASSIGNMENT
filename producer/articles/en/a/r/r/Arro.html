Arro

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Arro is a French commune situated in the départment of Corse-du-Sud, Corsica. Its area is km² and its population as of 1999 was 51. Its altitude varies from a minimun of 12 metres to a maximum of 867, averaging 150 metres.
