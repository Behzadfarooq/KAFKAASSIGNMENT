The arrondissement of Lens is an arrondissement of northern France, located in the Pas-de-Calais département, in the Nord-Pas de Calais région. It has 15 cantons and 39 communes.

[edit] Cantons

The cantons of the arrondissement of Lens are:

  1. Bully-les-Mines
  2. Carvin
  3. Courrières
  4. Harnes
  5. Hénin-Beaumont
  6. Leforest
  7. Lens-Est
  8. Lens-Nord-Est
  9. Lens-Nord-Ouest
  10. Liévin-Nord
  11. Liévin-Sud
  12. Montigny-en-Gohelle
  13. Noyelles-sous-Lens
  14. Sains-en-Gohelle
  15. Wingles
