The arrondissement of Melun is an arrondissement of France, located in the Seine-et-Marne département, in the Île-de-France région. It has 11 cantons and 113 communes.

[edit] Composition

[edit] Cantons

The cantons of the arrondissement of Melun are:

  1. Brie-Comte-Robert
  2. Le Châtelet-en-Brie
  3. Combs-la-Ville
  4. Le Mée-sur-Seine
  5. Melun-Nord
  6. Melun-Sud
  7. Mormant
  8. Perthes
  9. Rozay-en-Brie
  10. Savigny-le-Temple
  11. Tournan-en-Brie
