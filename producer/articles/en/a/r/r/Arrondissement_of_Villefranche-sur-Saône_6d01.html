The arrondissement of Villefranche-sur-Saône is an arrondissement of France, located in the Rhône department, in the Rhône-Alpes region. It has 11 cantons and 131 communes.

[edit] Composition

[edit] Cantons

The cantons of the arrondissement of Villefranche-sur-Saône are:

  1. Amplepuis
  2. Anse
  3. Beaujeu
  4. Belleville
  5. Le Bois-d'Oingt
  6. Gleizé
  7. Lamure-sur-Azergues
  8. Monsols
  9. Tarare
  10. Thizy
  11. Villefranche-sur-Saône
