The arrondissement of Montmorillon is an arrondissement of France, located in the Vienne département, in the Poitou-Charentes région. It has 11 cantons and 98 communes.

[edit] Cantons

The cantons of the arrondissement of Montmorillon are:

  1. Availles-Limouzine
  2. Charroux
  3. Chauvigny
  4. Civray
  5. Couhé
  6. Gençay
  7. L'Isle-Jourdain
  8. Lussac-les-Châteaux
  9. Montmorillon
  10. Saint-Savin
  11. La Trimouille
