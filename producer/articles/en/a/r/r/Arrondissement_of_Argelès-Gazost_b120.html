The arrondissement of Argelès-Gazost is an arrondissement of France, located in the Hautes-Pyrénées département, in the Midi-Pyrénées région. It has 6 cantons and 89 communes.

[edit] Composition

[edit] Cantons

The cantons of the arrondissement of Argelès-Gazost are:

  1. Argelès-Gazost
  2. Aucun
  3. Lourdes-Est
  4. Lourdes-Ouest
  5. Luz-Saint-Sauveur
  6. Saint-Pé-de-Bigorre
