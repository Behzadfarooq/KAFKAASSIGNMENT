The arrondissement of Nantua is an arrondissement of France, located in the Ain department, in the Rhône-Alpes region. It has 7 cantons and 64 communes.

[edit] Composition

[edit] Cantons

The cantons of the arrondissement of Nantua are:

  1. Bellegarde-sur-Valserine
  2. Brénod
  3. Izernore
  4. Nantua
  5. Oyonnax-Nord
  6. Oyonnax-Sud
  7. Poncin
