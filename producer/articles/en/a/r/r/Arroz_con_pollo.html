Arroz con pollo

Arroz con Pollo "Rice with Chicken" in Spanish. It is a traditional dish that is common throughout Latin America (with regional variations). Common ingredients include rice, sofrito (mixed vegetables and fresh herbs), chicken, and saffron. It is very aromatic. Wikibook has a recipe for this.
