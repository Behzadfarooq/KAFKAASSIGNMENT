The arrondissement of Pontarlier is an arrondissement of France, located in the Doubs département, in the Franche-Comté région. It has 5 cantons and 85 communes.

[edit] Cantons

The cantons of the arrondissement of Pontarlier are:

  1. Levier
  2. Montbenoît
  3. Morteau
  4. Mouthe
  5. Pontarlier
