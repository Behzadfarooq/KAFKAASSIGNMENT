The arrondissement of Tulle is an arrondissement of France, located in the Corrèze département, in the Limousin région. It has 14 cantons and 118 communes.

[edit] Cantons

The cantons of the arrondissement of Tulle are:

  1. Argentat
  2. Corrèze
  3. Égletons
  4. Lapleau
  5. Mercoeur
  6. La Roche-Canillac
  7. Saint-Privat
  8. Seilhac
  9. Treignac
  10. Tulle-Campagne-Nord
  11. Tulle-Campagne-Sud
  12. Tulle-Urbain-Nord
  13. Tulle-Urbain-Sud
  14. Uzerche
