The arrondissement of Mont-de-Marsan is an arrondissement of France, located in the Landes département, in the Aquitaine région. It has 17 cantons and 178 communes.

[edit] Cantons

The cantons of the arrondissement of Mont-de-Marsan are:

  1. Aire-sur-l'Adour
  2. Gabarret
  3. Geaune
  4. Grenade-sur-l'Adour
  5. Hagetmau
  6. Labrit
  7. Mimizan
  8. Mont-de-Marsan-Nord
  9. Mont-de-Marsan-Sud
  10. Morcenx
  11. Parentis-en-Born
  12. Pissos
  13. Roquefort
  14. Sabres
  15. Saint-Sever
  16. Sore
  17. Villeneuve-de-Marsan
