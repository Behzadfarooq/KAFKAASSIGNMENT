The arrondissement of Castelsarrasin is an arrondissement of France, located in the Tarn-et-Garonne département, in the Midi-Pyrénées région. It has 12 cantons and 103 communes.

[edit] Cantons

The cantons of the arrondissement of Castelsarrasin are:

  1. Auvillar
  2. Beaumont-de-Lomagne
  3. Bourg-de-Visa
  4. Castelsarrasin 1st Canton
  5. Castelsarrasin 2nd Canton
  6. Lauzerte
  7. Lavit
  8. Moissac 1st Canton
  9. Moissac 2nd Canton
  10. Montaigu-de-Quercy
  11. Saint-Nicolas-de-la-Grave
  12. Valence
