The arrondissement of Prades is an arrondissement of France, located in the Pyrénées-Orientales département, in the Languedoc-Roussillon région. It has 6 cantons and 100 communes.

[edit] Composition

[edit] Cantons

The cantons of the arrondissement of Prades are:

  1. Mont-Louis
  2. Olette
  3. Prades
  4. Saillagouse
  5. Sournia
  6. Vinça
