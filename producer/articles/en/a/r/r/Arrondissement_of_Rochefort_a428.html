The arrondissement of Rochefort is an arrondissement of France, located in the Charente-Maritime département, of the Poitou-Charentes région. It has 13 cantons and 79 communes.

[edit] Cantons

The cantons of the arrondissement of Rochefort are:

  1. Aigrefeuille-d'Aunis
  2. Le Château-d'Oléron
  3. Marennes
  4. Rochefort-Centre
  5. Rochefort-Nord
  6. Rochefort-Sud
  7. Royan-Est
  8. Royan-Ouest
  9. Saint-Agnant
  10. Saint-Pierre-d'Oléron
  11. Surgères
  12. Tonnay-Charente
  13. La Tremblade
