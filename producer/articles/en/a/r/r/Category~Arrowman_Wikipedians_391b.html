These are Wikipedians who are members of the Order of the Arrow. To add yourself to this list, insert the following on your user page:

[[Category:Arrowman Wikipedians|{{USERNAME}}]]

Aternatively, you may choose one of three OA Wikipedian userboxes. Using the Arrowman or either of the Vigil Brother templates will also add your userpage to this category, so you only need to use one of them. (Careful ... they are case-sensitive!)

Note: Your userpage will also be added to Category:Wikipedians in the Boy Scouts of America by using any of these templates.
