The arrondissement of Mantes-la-Jolie is an arrondissement of France, located in the Yvelines département, in the Île-de-France région. It has 8 cantons and 117 communes.

[edit] Composition

[edit] Cantons

The cantons of the arrondissement of Mantes-la-Jolie are:

  1. Aubergenville
  2. Bonnières-sur-Seine
  3. Guerville
  4. Houdan
  5. Limay
  6. Mantes-la-Jolie
  7. Mantes-la-Ville
  8. Meulan
