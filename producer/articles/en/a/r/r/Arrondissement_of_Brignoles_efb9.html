The arrondissement of Brignoles is an arrondissement of France, located in the Var department, in the Provence-Alpes-Côte d'Azur region. It has 9 cantons and 61 communes.

[edit] Cantons

The cantons of the arrondissement of Brignoles are:

  1. Aups
  2. Barjols
  3. Besse-sur-Issole
  4. Brignoles
  5. Cotignac
  6. Rians
  7. La Roquebrussanne
  8. Saint-Maximin-la-Sainte-Baume
  9. Tavernes
