The arrondissement of Dole is an arrondissement of France, located in the Jura département, in the Franche-Comté région. It has 10 cantons and 130 communes.

[edit] Composition

[edit] Cantons

The cantons of the arrondissement of Dole are:

  1. Chaumergy
  2. Chaussin
  3. Chemin
  4. Dampierre
  5. Dole-Nord-Est
  6. Dole-Sud-Ouest
  7. Gendrey
  8. Montbarrey
  9. Montmirey-le-Château
  10. Rochefort-sur-Nenon
