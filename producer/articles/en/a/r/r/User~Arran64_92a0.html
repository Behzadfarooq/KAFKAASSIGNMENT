Arran Mark Garside, better known by his Wikipedia user name, Arran64 (born January 29, 1991, in Dewsbury, West Yorkshire), is a Wikipedia user, famous for his dashing good looks and ability to refer to himself in 3rd person.

[edit] Early Life

Garside was born to elven parents in the mystical forests of Dewsbury. He had a high magical ability at a very young age, and was able to wield a sword by the age of 7. His first rise to fame was when he destroyed an invading army of orcs, saving the parish of Batley from destruction. He was later destroyed by a Bizarroesque copy of himself, named 'Bizarran'. He was revived by the Justice League at the age of 10, as they wanted to use his incredible powers. However, Garside was too powerful, and accidentally created the Sahara Desert when he destroyed Atlantis. His musical career began when he took the stage name Elvis Presley, and released an album of the same name. He was later hunted by The Empire for parking his murloc on double yellow lines, and was forced to flee to the then-Communist state of Liversedge.

[edit] Comeback Album

In 2004, Garside released a 67th album simply entitled 'Arran Garside'. It didn't sell. At all. Garside followed through on this huge success by announcing a World Tour. It ended when the 268 didn't come. This brought an end to his musical career.

[edit] Garside in the 21st century

On 5 June 2006, Garside joined the ever-expanding religious cult of Wikipedia; his first action being editting article of the "Chef" character from the adult cartoon, South Park. This edit was a huge success, and he performed it in front of these four blokes and the late James Dean. He made millions. He is seen nowadays working on articles about ancient rock bands that you're too cool to have heard of, and correcting spelling and grammar every here and there, adding things to lists, etc. His last reported public sighting was walking home from school a couple of days ago. He hasn't been seen since, and is considered dead.
