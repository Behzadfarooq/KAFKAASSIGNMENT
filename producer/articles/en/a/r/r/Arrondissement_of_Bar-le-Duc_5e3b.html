The arrondissement of Bar-le-Duc is an arrondissement of France, located in the Meuse département, in the Lorraine région. It has 9 cantons and 109 communes.

[edit] Composition

[edit] Cantons

The cantons of the arrondissement of Bar-le-Duc are:

  1. Ancerville
  2. Bar-le-Duc-Nord
  3. Bar-le-Duc-Sud
  4. Ligny-en-Barrois
  5. Montiers-sur-Saulx
  6. Revigny-sur-Ornain
  7. Seuil-d'Argonne
  8. Vaubecourt
  9. Vavincourt
