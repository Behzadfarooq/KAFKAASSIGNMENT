The arrondissement of Saint-Amand-Montrond is an arrondissement of France, located in the Cher département, in the Centre région. It has 11 cantons and 116 communes.

[edit] Composition

[edit] Cantons

The cantons of the arrondissement of Saint-Amand-Montrond are:

  1. Charenton-du-Cher
  2. Châteaumeillant
  3. Châteauneuf-sur-Cher
  4. Le Châtelet
  5. Dun-sur-Auron
  6. La Guerche-sur-l'Aubois
  7. Lignières
  8. Nérondes
  9. Saint-Amand-Montrond
  10. Sancoins
  11. Saulzais-le-Potier
