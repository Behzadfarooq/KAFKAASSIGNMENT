The arrondissement of Châteauroux is an arrondissement of France, located in the Indre département, in the Centre région. It has 11 cantons and 82 communes.

[edit] Composition

[edit] Cantons

The cantons of the arrondissement of Châteauroux are:

  1. Ardentes
  2. Argenton-sur-Creuse
  3. Buzançais
  4. Châteauroux-Centre
  5. Châteauroux-Est
  6. Châteauroux-Ouest
  7. Châteauroux-Sud
  8. Châtillon-sur-Indre
  9. Écueillé
  10. Levroux
  11. Valençay
