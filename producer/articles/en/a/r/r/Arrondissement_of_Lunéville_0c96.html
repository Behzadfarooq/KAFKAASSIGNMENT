The arrondissement of Lunéville is an arrondissement of France, located in the Meurthe-et-Moselle département, in the Lorraine région. It has 9 cantons and 164 communes.

[edit] Composition

[edit] Cantons

The cantons of the arrondissement of Lunéville are:

  1. Arracourt
  2. Baccarat
  3. Badonviller
  4. Bayon
  5. Blâmont
  6. Cirey-sur-Vezouze
  7. Gerbéviller
  8. Lunéville-Nord
  9. Lunéville-Sud
