The arrondissement of Avallon is an arrondissement of France, located in the Yonne département, in the Bourgogne région. It has 10 cantons and 149 communes.

[edit] Composition

[edit] Cantons

The cantons of the arrondissement of Avallon are:

  1. Ancy-le-Franc
  2. Avallon
  3. Cruzy-le-Châtel
  4. Flogny-la-Chapelle
  5. Guillon
  6. L'Isle-sur-Serein
  7. Noyers
  8. Quarré-les-Tombes
  9. Tonnerre
  10. Vézelay
