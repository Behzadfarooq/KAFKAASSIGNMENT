Arron Fray (born May 1, 1987 in England) is a footballer who plays for Crystal Palace in The Championship. He wears the number 31 shirt and is a defender. Arron's name is often misspelt as Aaron.

Arron made a surprise debut for Palace against Italian giants Inter Milan, in July 2005, and, despite playing on the opposite side of defence to his natural position of right-back, he excelled, and performed admirably. He was also given starts in the Carling Cup victories over Walsall and Coventry City, but did not manage to gain a league appearance. However, he continued to impress for the Reserves, enough to be rewarded with his first professional contract, in May 2006.

Arron will now look to make his mark upon The Championship, next season under new manager, the former England under-21 coach Peter Taylor.
