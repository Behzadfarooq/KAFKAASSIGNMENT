The arrondissement of Pithiviers is an arrondissement of France, located in the Loiret département, in the Centre région. It has 5 cantons and 86 communes.

[edit] Composition

[edit] Cantons

The cantons of the arrondissement of Pithiviers are:

  1. Beaune-la-Rolande
  2. Malesherbes
  3. Outarville
  4. Pithiviers
  5. Puiseaux
