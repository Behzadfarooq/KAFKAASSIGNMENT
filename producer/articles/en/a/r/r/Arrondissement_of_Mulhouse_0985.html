The arrondissement of Mulhouse is an arrondissement of France, located in the Haut-Rhin département, in the Alsace région. It has 9 cantons and 73 communes.

[edit] Cantons

The cantons of the arrondissement of Mulhouse are:

  1. Habsheim
  2. Huningue
  3. Illzach
  4. Mulhouse-Est
  5. Mulhouse-Nord
  6. Mulhouse-Ouest
  7. Mulhouse-Sud
  8. Sierentz
  9. Wittenheim
