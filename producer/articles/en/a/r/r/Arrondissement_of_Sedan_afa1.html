The arrondissement of Sedan is an arrondissement of France, located in the Ardennes département, in the Champagne-Ardenne région. It has 6 cantons and 79 communes.

[edit] Composition

[edit] Cantons

The cantons of the arrondissement of Sedan are:

  1. Carignan
  2. Mouzon
  3. Raucourt-et-Flaba
  4. Sedan-Est
  5. Sedan-Nord
  6. Sedan-Ouest
