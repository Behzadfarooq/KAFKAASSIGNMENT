The arrondissement of Montbrison is an arrondissement of France, located in the Loire department, in the Rhône-Alpes region. It has 10 cantons and 138 communes.

[edit] Composition

[edit] Cantons

The cantons of the arrondissement of Montbrison ae:

  1. Boën
  2. Chazelles-sur-Lyon
  3. Feurs
  4. Montbrison
  5. Noirétable
  6. Saint-Bonnet-le-Château
  7. Saint-Galmier
  8. Saint-Georges-en-Couzan
  9. Saint-Jean-Soleymieux
  10. Saint-Just-Saint-Rambert
