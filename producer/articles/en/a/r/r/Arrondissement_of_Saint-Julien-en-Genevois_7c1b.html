The arrondissement of Saint-Julien-en-Genevois is an arrondissement of France, located in the Haute-Savoie department, in the Rhône-Alpes region. It has 7 cantons and 72 communes.

[edit] Composition

[edit] Cantons

The cantons of the arrondissement of Saint-Julien-en-Genevois are:

  1. Annemasse-Nord
  2. Annemasse-Sud
  3. Cruseilles
  4. Frangy
  5. Reignier
  6. Saint-Julien-en-Genevois
  7. Seyssel
