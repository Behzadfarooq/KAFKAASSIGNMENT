The arrondissement of Nontron is an arrondissement of France, located in the Dordogne department, in the Aquitaine region. It has 8 cantons and 80 communes.

[edit] Composition

[edit] Cantons

The cantons of the arrondissement of Nontron are:

  1. Bussière-Badil
  2. Champagnac-de-Belair
  3. Jumilhac-le-Grand
  4. Lanouaille
  5. Mareuil
  6. Nontron
  7. Saint-Pardoux-la-Rivière
  8. Thiviers
