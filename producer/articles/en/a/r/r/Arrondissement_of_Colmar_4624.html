The arrondissement of Colmar is an arrondissement of France, located in the Haut-Rhin département, in the Alsace région. It has 6 cantons and 62 communes.

[edit] Composition

[edit] Cantons

The cantons of the arrondissement of Colmar are:

  1. Andolsheim
  2. Colmar-Nord
  3. Colmar-Sud
  4. Munster
  5. Neuf-Brisach
  6. Wintzenheim
