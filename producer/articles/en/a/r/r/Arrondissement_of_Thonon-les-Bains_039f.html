The arrondissement of Thonon-les-Bains is an arrondissement of France, located in the Haute-Savoie department, in the Rhône-Alpes region. It has 7 cantons and 68 communes.

[edit] Composition

[edit] Cantons

The cantons of the arrondissement of Thonon-les-Bains are:

  1. Abondance
  2. Le Biot
  3. Boëge
  4. Douvaine
  5. Évian-les-Bains
  6. Thonon-les-Bains-Est
  7. Thonon-les-Bains-Ouest
