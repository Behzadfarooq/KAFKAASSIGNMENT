The arrondissement of Mamers is an arrondissement of France, located in the Sarthe département, in the Pays de la Loire région. It has 10 cantons and 134 communes.

[edit] Composition

[edit] Cantons

The cantons of the arrondissement of Mamers are:

  1. Beaumont-sur-Sarthe
  2. Bonnétable
  3. La Ferté-Bernard
  4. La Fresnaye-sur-Chédouet
  5. Fresnay-sur-Sarthe
  6. Mamers
  7. Marolles-les-Braults
  8. Montmirail
  9. Saint-Paterne
  10. Tuffé
