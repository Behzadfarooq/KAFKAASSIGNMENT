[edit] Track listing

All songs written by Steen Mogensen.

  1. "Arrival" – 1:08
  2. "Walked On the Water" – 5:01
  3. "Jungle" – 4:08
  4. "Straight to the Bone" – 5:02
  5. "Top of the World" – 3:27
  6. "Reload" – 4:33
  7. "Gift of Flesh" – 4:01
  8. "Grain of Sand" – 5:26
  9. "Fooled" – 4:25
  10. "I'm Alive" – 6:46
  11. "Basscamp 1" (Instrumental) – 3:13
  • European version (Massacre Records MAS CD0266) omits track 11.

[edit] Personnel

  • Doogie White – lead, backing vocals
  • Steen Mogensen – bass guitar, keyboards and percussion
  • Jacob Kjaer – guitars
  • Allan Sorensen – drums
  • André Andersen – keyboards
  • Toni Rahm – guitar (Tracks 6 and 10)
  • Peter Brander – guitar (Track 11)
  • Kasper Damgaard – guitar (Track 11)
  • Kenny Lubcke – backing vocals
  • Henrik Brockmann – backing vocals

[edit] Production

  • Mixing – Peter Brander
  • Engineer – Steen Mogensen and André Anderson
