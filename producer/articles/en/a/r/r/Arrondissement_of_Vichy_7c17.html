The arrondissement of Vichy is an arrondissement of France, located in the Allier département, in the Auvergne région. It has 11 cantons and 103 communes.

[edit] Composition

[edit] Cantons

The cantons of the arrondissement of Vichy are:

  1. Cusset-Nord
  2. Cusset-Sud
  3. Le Donjon
  4. Escurolles
  5. Gannat
  6. Jaligny-sur-Besbre
  7. Lapalisse
  8. Le Mayet-de-Montagne
  9. Varennes-sur-Allier
  10. Vichy-Nord
  11. Vichy-Sud
