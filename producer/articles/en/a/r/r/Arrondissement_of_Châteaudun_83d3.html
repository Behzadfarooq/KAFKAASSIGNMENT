The arrondissement of Châteaudun is an arrondissement of France, located in the Eure-et-Loir département, in the Centre région. It has 5 cantons and 80 communes.

[edit] Composition

[edit] Cantons

The cantons of the arrondissement of Châteaudun are:

  1. Bonneval
  2. Brou
  3. Châteaudun
  4. Cloyes-sur-le-Loir
  5. Orgères-en-Beauce
