The arrondissement of Mende is an arrondissement of France, located in the Lozère département, in the Languedoc-Roussillon région. It has 18 cantons and 135 communes.

[edit] Cantons

  14. Saint-Alban-sur-Limagnole
  15. Saint-Amans
  16. Saint-Chély-d'Apcher
  17. Saint-Germain-du-Teil
  18. Villefort
