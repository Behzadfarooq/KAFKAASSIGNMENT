The arrondissement of Albertville is an arrondissement of France, located in the Savoie department, in the Rhône-Alpes region. It has 9 cantons and 82 communes.

[edit] Composition

[edit] Cantons

The cantons of the arrondissement of Albertville are:

  1. Aime
  2. Albertville-Nord
  3. Albertville-Sud
  4. Beaufort
  5. Bourg-Saint-Maurice
  6. Bozel
  7. Grésy-sur-Isère
  8. Moûtiers
  9. Ugine
