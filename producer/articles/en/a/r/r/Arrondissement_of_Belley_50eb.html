The arrondissement of Belley is an arrondissement of France, located in the Ain department, in the Rhône-Alpes region. It has 9 cantons and 107 communes.

[edit] Composition

[edit] Cantons

The cantons of the arrondissement of Belley are:

  1. Ambérieu-en-Bugey
  2. Belley
  3. Champagne-en-Valromey
  4. Hauteville-Lompnes
  5. Lagnieu
  6. Lhuis
  7. Saint-Rambert-en-Bugey
  8. Seyssel
  9. Virieu-le-Grand
