The arrondissement of Provins is an arrondissement of France, located in the Seine-et-Marne département, in the Île-de-France région. It has 7 cantons and 125 communes.

[edit] Composition

[edit] Cantons

The cantons of the arrondissement of Provins are:

  1. Bray-sur-Seine
  2. Donnemarie-Dontilly
  3. La Ferté-Gaucher
  4. Montereau-Fault-Yonne
  5. Nangis
  6. Provins
  7. Villiers-Saint-Georges
