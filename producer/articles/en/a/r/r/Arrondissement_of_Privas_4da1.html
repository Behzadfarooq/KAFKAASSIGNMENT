The arrondissement of Privas is an arrondissement of France, located in the Ardèche département, in the Rhône-Alpes région. It has 11 cantons and 110 communes.

[edit] Composition

[edit] Cantons

The cantons of the arrondissement of Privas are:

  1. Antraigues-sur-Volane
  2. Aubenas
  3. Bourg-Saint-Andéol
  4. Chomérac
  5. Privas
  6. Rochemaure
  7. Saint-Pierreville
  8. Vals-les-Bains
  9. Villeneuve-de-Berg
  10. Viviers
  11. La Voulte-sur-Rhône
