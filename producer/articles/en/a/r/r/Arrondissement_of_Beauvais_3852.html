The arrondissement of Beauvais is an arrondissement of France, located in the Oise département, in the Picardie région. It has 14 cantons and 258 communes.

[edit] Cantons

The cantons of the arrondissement of Beauvais are:

  3. Beauvais-Nord-Ouest

  5. Chaumont-en-Vexin

  7. Crèvecoeur-le-Grand
  8. Formerie
  9. Grandvilliers
  10. Marseille-en-Beauvaisis
  11. Méru
  12. Nivillers
  13. Noailles
  14. Songeons
