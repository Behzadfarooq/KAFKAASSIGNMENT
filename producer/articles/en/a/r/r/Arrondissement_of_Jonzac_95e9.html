The arrondissement of Jonzac is an arrondissement of France, located in the Charente-Maritime département, in the Poitou-Charentes région. It has 7 cantons and 114 communes.

[edit] Composition

[edit] Cantons

The cantons of the arrondissement of Jonzac are:

  1. Archiac
  2. Jonzac
  3. Mirambeau
  4. Montendre
  5. Montguyon
  6. Montlieu-la-Garde
  7. Saint-Genis-de-Saintonge
