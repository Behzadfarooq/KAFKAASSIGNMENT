The arrondissement of Senlis is an arrondissement of France, located in the Oise département, in the Picardie région. It has 10 cantons and 133 communes.

[edit] Composition

[edit] Cantons

The cantons of the arrondissement of Senlis are:

  1. Betz
  2. Chantilly
  3. Creil-Nogent-sur-Oise
  4. Creil-Sud
  5. Crépy-en-Valois
  6. Montataire
  7. Nanteuil-le-Haudouin
  8. Neuilly-en-Thelle
  9. Pont-Sainte-Maxence
  10. Senlis
