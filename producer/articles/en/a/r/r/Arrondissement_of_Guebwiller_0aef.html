The arrondissement of Guebwiller is an arrondissement of France, located in the Haut-Rhin département, in the Alsace région. It has 4 cantons and 47 communes.

[edit] Cantons

The cantons of the arrondissement of Guebwiller are:

  1. Ensisheim
  2. Guebwiller
  3. Rouffach
  4. Soultz-Haut-Rhin
