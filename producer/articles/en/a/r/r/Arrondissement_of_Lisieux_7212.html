The arrondissement of Lisieux is an arrondissement of France, located in the Calvados department, in the Basse-Normandie region. It has 13 cantons and 204 communes.

[edit] Cantons

The cantons of the arrondissement of Lisieux are:

  1. Blangy-le-Château
  2. Cambremer
  3. Dozulé
  4. Honfleur
  5. Lisieux 1st Canton

  7. Lisieux 3rd Canton
  8. Livarot
  9. Mézidon-Canon
  10. Orbec
  11. Pont-l'Évêque
  12. Saint-Pierre-sur-Dives
  13. Trouville-sur-Mer
