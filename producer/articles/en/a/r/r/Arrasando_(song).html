"Arrasando" is the third single from Thalía's 2000 Arrasando album, and it was written by herself. Also, it has an English version called "It's My Party".

This mixture of Latin sound and "rap" is one of her greatest hits ever.

[edit] Music Video

The music video was shot in Los Angeles (USA), and it was directed by Simon Brand, who had directed also her two previous videos. Two versions of this video were filmed - Spanish and English.
