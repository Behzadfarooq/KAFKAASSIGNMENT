The arrondissement of Arcachon is an arrondissement of France, located in the south west of Gironde department, in the Aquitaine region. It has 4 cantons and 17 communes.

[edit] Composition

[edit] Cantons

The cantons of the arrondissement of Bordeaux are:

  1. Arcachon
  2. Audenge
  3. Belin-Béliet
  4. La Teste-de-Buch


[edit] Communes

The communes of the arrondissement of Arcachon are:

1. Andernos-les-Bains (33005) 2. Arcachon (33009) 3. Arès (33011) 4. Audenge (33019) 5. Belin-Béliet (33042)
6. Biganos (33051) 7. Gujan-Mestras (33199) 8. La Teste-de-Buch (33529) 9. Le Barp (33029) 10. Le Teich (33527)
11. Lugos (33260) 12. Lège-Cap-Ferret (33236) 13. Marcheprime (33555) 14. Mios (33284) 15. Salles (33498)
16. Saucats (33501) 17. Saumos (33503)
