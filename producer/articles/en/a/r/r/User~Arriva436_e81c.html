My edit summary key:

  • sp. - I have corrected the spelling of something
  • gram. - I have corrected a grammar mistake
  • str. - I have corrected the layout of stricture of something
  • misc. - I have corrected miscaleainious things

My main aim for Wikipedia is to prodive an edit summary for any edit I do, no matter how small, and if you look at my contributions you will see that I do!!! I even have my preferances set to remind me and not save the changes if I forget to.


I am a bus enthusiast and became a user in October 2006. I mostly edit or create bus-related articles but I have done others from time to time. Also, I've done edits before without being a registered user. Happy Surfing

Feel free to leave messages, click "disscusion" above. But, Please do not ask me anything about how to make Wikipiedia's formatting work, because I'm RUBBISH at it!

I hate apostrophe mistakes, and correct them wherever I see them. A quote from Stagecoach in Hampshire:

"It's main bases of operation are Andover, Basingstoke and Winchester."

I soon changed that for "Its"!

However, I am not too worried about slight spelling mistakes in my edit summaries.
