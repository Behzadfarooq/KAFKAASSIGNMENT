The arrondissement of Montreuil is an arrondissement of France, located in the Pas-de-Calais département, in the Nord-Pas-de-Calais région. It has 7 cantons and 140 communes.

[edit] Composition

[edit] Cantons

The cantons of the arrondissement of Montreuil are:

  1. Berck
  2. Campagne-lès-Hesdin
  3. Étaples
  4. Fruges
  5. Hesdin
  6. Hucqueliers
  7. Montreuil
