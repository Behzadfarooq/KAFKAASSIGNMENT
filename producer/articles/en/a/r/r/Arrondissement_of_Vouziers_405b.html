The arrondissement of Vouziers is an arrondissement of France, located in the Ardennes département, in the Champagne-Ardenne région. It has 8 cantons and 123 communes.

[edit] Composition

[edit] Cantons

The cantons of the arrondissement of Vouziers are:

  1. Attigny
  2. Buzancy
  3. Le Chesne
  4. Grandpré
  5. Machault
  6. Monthois
  7. Tourteron
  8. Vouziers
