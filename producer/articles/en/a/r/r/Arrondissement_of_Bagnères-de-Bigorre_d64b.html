The arrondissement of Bagnères-de-Bigorre is an arrondissement of France, located in the Hautes-Pyrénées département, in the Midi-Pyrénées région. It has 9 cantons and 160 communes.

[edit] Composition

[edit] Cantons

The cantons of the arrondissement of Bagnères-de-Bigorre are:

  1. Arreau
  2. Bagnères-de-Bigorre
  3. La Barthe-de-Neste
  4. Bordères-Louron
  5. Campan
  6. Lannemezan
  7. Mauléon-Barousse
  8. Saint-Laurent-de-Neste
  9. Vielle-Aure
