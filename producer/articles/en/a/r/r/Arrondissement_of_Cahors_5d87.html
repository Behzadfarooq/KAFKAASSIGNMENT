The arrondissement of Cahors is an arrondissement of France, located in the Lot département, in the Midi-Pyrénées région. It has 13 cantons and 135 communes.

[edit] Cantons

The cantons of the arrondissement of Cahors are:

  1. Cahors-Nord-Est
  2. Cahors-Nord-Ouest
  3. Cahors-Sud

  5. Catus

  8. Lauzès
  9. Limogne-en-Quercy

  12. Puy-l'Évêque
  13. Saint-Géry
