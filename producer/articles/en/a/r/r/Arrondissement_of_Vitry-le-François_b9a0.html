The arrondissement of Vitry-le-François is an arrondissement of France, located in the Marne département, in the Champagne-Ardenne région. It has 6 cantons and 113 communes.

[edit] Composition

[edit] Cantons

The cantons of the arrondissement of Vitry-le-François are:

  1. Heiltz-le-Maurupt
  2. Saint-Remy-en-Bouzemont-Saint-Genest-et-Isson
  3. Sompuis
  4. Thiéblemont-Farémont
  5. Vitry-le-François-Est
  6. Vitry-le-François-Ouest
