The arrondissement of Les Andelys is an arrondissement of France, located in the Eure department, in the Haute-Normandie region. It has 10 cantons and156 communes.

[edit] Composition

[edit] Cantons

The cantons of the arrondissement of Les Andelys are:

  1. Les Andelys
  2. Écos
  3. Étrépagny
  4. Fleury-sur-Andelle
  5. Gaillon
  6. Gaillon-Campagne
  7. Gisors
  8. Lyons-la-Forêt
  9. Pont-de-l'Arche
  10. Val-de-Reuil
