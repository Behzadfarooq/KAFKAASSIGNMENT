The Arrondissement of Bastogne (French: Arrondissement de Bastogne; Dutch: Arrondissement Bastenaken) is one of the five administrative arrondissements in the Province of Luxembourg, Belgium. It is not a judicial arrondissement. Three of its municipalities, Gouvy, Houffalize and Vielsalm, are part of the Judicial Arrondissement of Marche-en-Famenne, while the rest of its municipalities are part of the Judicial Arrondissement of Neufchâteau.

[edit] Municipalities

The Administrative Arrondissement of Bastogne consists of the following municipalities:

  • Bastogne
  • Bertogne
  • Fauvillers
  • Gouvy
  • Houffalize
  • Sainte-Ode
  • Vaux-sur-Sûre
  • Vielsalm
