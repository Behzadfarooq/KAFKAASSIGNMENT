The arrondissement of Charolles is an arrondissement of France, located in the Saône-et-Loire département, in the Bourgogne région. It has 13 cantons and 136 communes.

[edit] Cantons

The cantons of the arrondissement of Charolles are:

  1. Bourbon-Lancy
  2. Charolles
  3. Chauffailles
  4. La Clayette
  5. Digoin
  6. Gueugnon
  7. La Guiche
  8. Marcigny
  9. Palinges
  10. Paray-le-Monial
  11. Saint-Bonnet-de-Joux
  12. Semur-en-Brionnais
  13. Toulon-sur-Arroux
