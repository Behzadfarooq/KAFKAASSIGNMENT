The arrondissement of Vervins is an arrondissement of France, located in the Aisne department, of the Picardie region. It has 8 cantons and 130 communes.

[edit] Composition

[edit] Cantons

The cantons of the arrondissement of Vervins are:

  1. Aubenton
  2. La Capelle
  3. Guise
  4. Hirson
  5. Le Nouvion-en-Thiérache
  6. Sains-Richaumont
  7. Vervins
  8. Wassigny
