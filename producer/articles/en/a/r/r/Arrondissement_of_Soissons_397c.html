The arrondissement of Soissons is an arrondissement of France, located in the Aisne département, in the Picardie région. It has 7 cantons and 159 communes.

[edit] Composition

[edit] Cantons

The cantons of the arrondissement of Soissons are:

  1. Braine
  2. Oulchy-le-Château
  3. Soissons-Nord
  4. Soissons-Sud
  5. Vailly-sur-Aisne
  6. Vic-sur-Aisne
  7. Villers-Cotterêts
