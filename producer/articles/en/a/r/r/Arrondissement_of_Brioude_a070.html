The arrondissement of Brioude is an arrondissement of France, located in the Haute-Loire département, in the Auvergne région. It has 9 cantons and 100 communes.

[edit] Composition

[edit] Cantons

The cantons of the arrondissement of Brioude are:

  1. Auzon
  2. Blesle
  3. Brioude-Nord
  4. Brioude-Sud
  5. La Chaise-Dieu
  6. Langeac
  7. Lavoûte-Chilhac
  8. Paulhaguet
  9. Pinols
  10. Saugues
