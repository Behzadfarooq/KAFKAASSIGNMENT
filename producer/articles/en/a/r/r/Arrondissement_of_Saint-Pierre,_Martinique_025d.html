The arrondissement of Saint-Pierre is an arrondissement of France, located in the Martinique département, in the Martinique région. It has 5 cantons and 8 communes.

[edit] Composition

[edit] Cantons

The cantons of the arrondissement of Saint-Pierre are:

  1. Le Carbet
  2. Case-Pilote-Bellefontaine
  3. Le Morne-Rouge
  4. Le Prêcheur
  5. Saint-Pierre

[edit] Communes

The communes of the arrondissement of Saint-Pierre are:

1. Bellefontaine (97234) 2. Le Carbet (97204) 3. Case-Pilote (97205) 4. Fonds-Saint-Denis (97208)
5. Le Morne-Rouge (97218) 6. Le Morne-Vert (97233) 7. Le Prêcheur (97219) 8. Saint-Pierre (97225)
