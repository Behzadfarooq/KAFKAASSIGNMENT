The arrondissement of Moulins is an arrondissement of France, located in the Allier département, in the Auvergne région. It has 12 cantons and 111 communes.

[edit] Composition

[edit] Cantons

The cantons of the arrondissement of Moulins are:

  1. Bourbon-l'Archambault
  2. Chantelle
  3. Chevagnes
  4. Dompierre-sur-Besbre
  5. Lurcy-Lévis
  6. Le Montet
  7. Moulins-Ouest
  8. Moulins-Sud
  9. Neuilly-le-Réal
  10. Saint-Pourçain-sur-Sioule
  11. Souvigny
  12. Yzeure
