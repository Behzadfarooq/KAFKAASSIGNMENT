The arrondissement of Fontainebleau is an arrondissement of France, located in the Seine-et-Marne département, in the Île-de-France région. It has 6 cantons and 87 communes.

The arrondissement of Fontainebleau was suppressed from 1926 to 1988.

[edit] Composition

[edit] Cantons

The cantons of the arrondissement of Fontainebleau are:

  1. La Chapelle-la-Reine
  2. Château-Landon
  3. Fontainebleau
  4. Lorrez-le-Bocage-Préaux
  5. Moret-sur-Loing
  6. Nemours
