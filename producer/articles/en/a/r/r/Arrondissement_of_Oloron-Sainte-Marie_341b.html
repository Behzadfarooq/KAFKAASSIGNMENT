The arrondissement of Oloron-Sainte-Marie is an arrondissement of France, located in the Pyrénées-Atlantiques département, in the Aquitaine région. It has 12 cantons and 155 communes.

[edit] Composition

[edit] Cantons

The cantons of the arrondissement of Oloron-Sainte-Marie are:

  1. Accous
  2. Aramits
  3. Arudy
  4. Laruns
  5. Lasseube
  6. Mauléon-Licharre
  7. Monein
  8. Navarrenx
  9. Oloron-Sainte-Marie-Est
  10. Oloron-Sainte-Marie-Ouest
  11. Sauveterre-de-Béarn
  12. Tardets-Sorholus
