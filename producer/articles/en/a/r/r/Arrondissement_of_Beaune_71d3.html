The arrondissement of Beaune is an arrondissement of France, located in the Côte-d'Or département, in the Bourgogne région. It has 10 cantons and 194 communes.

[edit] Composition

[edit] Cantons

The cantons of the arrondissement of Beaune are:

  1. Arnay-le-Duc
  2. Beaune-Nord
  3. Beaune-Sud
  4. Bligny-sur-Ouche
  5. Liernais
  6. Nolay
  7. Nuits-Saint-Georges
  8. Pouilly-en-Auxois
  9. Saint-Jean-de-Losne
  10. Seurre
