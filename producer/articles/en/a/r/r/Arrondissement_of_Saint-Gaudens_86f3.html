The arrondissement of Saint-Gaudens is an arrondissement of France, located in the Haute-Garonne département, in the Midi-Pyrénées région. It has 11 cantons and 236 communes.

[edit] Composition

[edit] Cantons

The cantons of the arrondissement of Saint-Gaudens are:

  1. Aspet
  2. Aurignac
  3. Bagnères-de-Luchon
  4. Barbazan
  5. Boulogne-sur-Gesse
  6. L'Isle-en-Dodon
  7. Montréjeau
  8. Saint-Béat
  9. Saint-Gaudens
  10. Saint-Martory
  11. Salies-du-Salat
