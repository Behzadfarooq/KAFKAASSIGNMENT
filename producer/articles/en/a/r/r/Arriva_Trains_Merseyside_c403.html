Arriva Trains Merseyside was a train operating company, operating passenger trains in Merseyside (UK). The company was previously known as Merseyrail Electrics before it was taken over by Arriva. It was previously franchised to MTL railways, whose parent company was M.T.L. Holdings Ltd.

The franchise was taken over by Merseyrail in 2003.
