The arrondissement of Clermont is an arrondissement of France, located in the Oise département, in the Picardie région. It has 7 cantons and 146 communes.

[edit] Composition

[edit] Cantons

The cantons of the arrondissement of Clermont are:

  1. Breteuil
  2. Clermont
  3. Froissy
  4. Liancourt
  5. Maignelay-Montigny
  6. Mouy
  7. Saint-Just-en-Chaussée
