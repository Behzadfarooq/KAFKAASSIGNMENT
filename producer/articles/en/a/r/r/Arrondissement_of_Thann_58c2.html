The arrondissement of Thann is an arrondissement of France, located in the Haut-Rhin département, in the Alsace région. It has 4 cantons and 52 communes.

[edit] Cantons

The cantons of the arrondissement of Thann are:

  1. Cernay
  2. Masevaux
  3. Saint-Amarin
  4. Thann
