The arrondissement of Annecy is an arrondissement of France, located in the Haute-Savoie department, in the Rhône-Alpes region. It has 10 cantons and 93 communes.

[edit] Composition

[edit] Cantons

The cantons of the arrondissement of Annecy are:

  1. Alby-sur-Chéran
  2. Annecy-Centre
  3. Annecy-le-Vieux
  4. Annecy-Nord-Est
  5. Annecy-Nord-Ouest
  6. Faverges
  7. Rumilly
  8. Seynod
  9. Thônes
  10. Thorens-Glières
