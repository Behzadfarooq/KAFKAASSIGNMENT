The arrondissement of Segré is an arrondissement of France, located in the Maine-et-Loire département, in the Pays de la Loire région. It has 5 cantons and 61 communes.

[edit] Composition

[edit] Cantons

The cantons of the arrondissement of Segré are:

  1. Candé
  2. Châteauneuf-sur-Sarthe
  3. Le Lion-d'Angers
  4. Pouancé
  5. Segré
