The arrondissement of Saintes is an arrondissement of France, located in the Charente-Maritime département, in the Poitou-Charentes région. It has 9 cantons and 107 communes.

[edit] Cantons

The cantons of the arrondissement of Saintes are:

  1. Burie
  2. Cozes
  3. Gémozac
  4. Pons
  5. Saintes-Est
  6. Saintes-Nord
  7. Saintes-Ouest
  8. Saint-Porchaire
  9. Saujon
