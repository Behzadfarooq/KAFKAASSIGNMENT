Arròs negre

Arròs negre (Black rice in Catalan) is a dry rice dish similar to paella. In contrast to the latter, it is cooked with onion and garlic.

It is prepared with cuttlefish and calamari, but the broth may be made from other seafood. Rice is added to that broth, and it's cooked until water evaporates. The dark aspect is because squid ink is actually added.

It's usually served with allioli.
