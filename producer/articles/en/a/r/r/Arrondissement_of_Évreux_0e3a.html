The arrondissement of Évreux is an arrondissement of France, located in the Eure département, in the Haute-Normandie région. It has 18 cantons and 280 communes.

[edit] Cantons

The cantons of the arrondissement of Évreux are:

  2. Breteuil
  3. Conches-en-Ouche

  8. Évreux-Sud

  12. Nonancourt
  13. Pacy-sur-Eure
  14. Rugles
  15. Saint-André-de-l'Eure

  17. Vernon-Nord
  18. Vernon-Sud
