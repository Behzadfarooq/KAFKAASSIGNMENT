The arrondissement of Montargis is an arrondissement of France, located in the Loiret département, in the Centre région. It has 12 cantons and 126 communes.

[edit] Composition

[edit] Cantons

The cantons of the arrondissement of Montargis are:

  1. Amilly
  2. Bellegarde
  3. Briare
  4. Châlette-sur-Loing
  5. Château-Renard
  6. Châtillon-Coligny
  7. Châtillon-sur-Loire
  8. Courtenay
  9. Ferrières-en-Gâtinais
  10. Gien
  11. Lorris
  12. Montargis
