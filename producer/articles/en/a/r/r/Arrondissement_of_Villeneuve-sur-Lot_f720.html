The arrondissement of Villeneuve-sur-Lot is an arrondissement of France, located in the Lot-et-Garonne département, in the Aquitaine région. It has 11 cantons and 92 communes.

[edit] Composition

[edit] Cantons

The cantons of the arrondissement of Villeneuve-sur-Lot are:

  1. Cancon
  2. Castillonnès
  3. Fumel
  4. Monclar
  5. Monflanquin
  6. Penne-d'Agenais
  7. Sainte-Livrade-sur-Lot
  8. Tournon-d'Agenais
  9. Villeneuve-sur-Lot-Nord
  10. Villeneuve-sur-Lot-Sud
  11. Villeréal
