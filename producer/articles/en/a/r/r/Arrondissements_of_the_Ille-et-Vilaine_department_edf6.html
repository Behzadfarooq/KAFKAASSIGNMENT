Arrondissements of the Ille-et-Vilaine department

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The 4 arrondissements of the Ille-et-Vilaine department are:

  1. Arrondissement of Fougères, (subprefecture: Fougères) with 6 cantons and 57 communes. The population of the arrondissement was 75,382 in 1990, and was 75,380 in 1999, a decrease of 0.0%.
  2. Arrondissement of Redon, (subprefecture: Redon) with 7 cantons and 53 communes. The population of the arrondissement was 75,695 in 1990, and was 81,106 in 1999, an increase of 7.15%.
  3. Arrondissement of Rennes, (prefecture of the Ille-et-Vilaine department: Rennes) with 31 cantons and 179 communes. The population of the arrondissement was 512,676 in 1990, and was 569,932 in 1999, an increase of 11.17%.
  4. Arrondissement of Saint-Malo, (subprefecture: Saint-Malo) with 9 cantons and 63 communes. The population of the arrondissement was 134,965 in 1990, and was 141,115 in 1999, an increase of 4.56%.
