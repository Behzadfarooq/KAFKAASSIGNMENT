Arrowsmith is a fantasy comic book by writer Kurt Busiek and penciller Carlos Pacheco that was published by Wildstorm's Cliffhanger imprint.

[edit] Publication history

The original limited series ran for six issues in 2003 and was collected in the trade paperback Arrowsmith, Book 1: So Smart in Their Fine Uniforms (ISBN 1-4012-0299-3) in 2004.

Busiek has stated that he and Pacheco hope to produce a sequel Arrowsmith series eventually. [1]

[edit] Setting

The series envisioned a world where magic is real and the First World War is fought with and by dragons, spells, vampires and all other kinds of magical weapons and beings.

The story follows the protagonist, Fletcher Arrowsmith, as he joins the war effort on the side of the Allies, gets taught the rudiments of sorcery and engages in some brutal battles with the enemy Prussians.
