In this article, an arrondissement is a subdivision of the city of Paris. Paris contains twenty municipal arrondissements. The city's most central arrondissement is numbered as being the first; then, in a clockwise spiral direction, come the following subdivisions that gain in numerical value with each successive subdivision, thus this "around" signification of this sort of subdivision name. The French cities of Lyon and Marseille are also subdivided in this way.

These arrondissements should not be confused with the administrative subdivisions of French départements also known as "arrondissements" (see Arrondissements in France). Paris, as a département, also contains one arrondissement in this meaning (see Arrondissements of the Paris département). But the word "arrondissement", when applied to Paris, refers almost always to the municipal arrondissements listed below.
