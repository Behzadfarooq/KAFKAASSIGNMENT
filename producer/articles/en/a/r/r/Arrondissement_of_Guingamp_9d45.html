The arrondissement of Guingamp is an arrondissement of France, located in the Côtes-d'Armor département, in the Bretagne région. It has 12 cantons and 90 communes.

[edit] Composition

[edit] Cantons

The cantons of the arrondissement of Guingamp are:

  1. Bégard
  2. Belle-Isle-en-Terre
  3. Bourbriac
  4. Callac
  5. Gouarec
  6. Guingamp
  7. Maël-Carhaix
  8. Mûr-de-Bretagne
  9. Plouagat
  10. Pontrieux
  11. Rostrenen
  12. Saint-Nicolas-du-Pélem
