The arrondissement of Dunkirk is an arrondissement of France, located in the Nord department, in the Nord-Pas-de-Calais region. It has 16 cantons and 115 communes.

[edit] Cantons

The cantons of the arrondissement of Dunkirk are:

  1. Bailleul-Nord-Est
  2. Bailleul-Sud-Ouest
  3. Bergues
  4. Bourbourg
  5. Cassel
  6. Coudekerque-Branche
  7. Dunkerque-Est
  8. Dunkerque-Ouest
  9. Grande-Synthe
  10. Gravelines
  11. Hazebrouck-Nord
  12. Hazebrouck-Sud
  13. Hondschoote
  14. Merville
  15. Steenvoorde
  16. Wormhout
