Arriving
Studio album by Chris Tomlin
Released September 21, 2004
Length 46:01
Label Sparrow Records
Producer(s) Ed Cash

Arriving is an album released in 2004 by Chris Tomlin. The album has received RIAA 'Gold' status and peaked at #3 on Billboard's Top Christian Albums chart.
