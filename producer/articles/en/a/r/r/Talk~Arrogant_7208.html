Whats the difference between a conceded person and an arrogant person? Is arrogance a negative thing?

i think a conceded person is an a-hole and knows their an a-hole and and arrogant person is an a-hole but is to full of them self to believe it
No, a conceited person is one who has an excessively favorable opinion of one's abilities, appearance, etc.[1], where as an arrogant person is merely an individual that makes offensive display of superiority or self-importance[2]. Strictly, a person can be arrogant and actually have greater self-worth (arrogance and ability are not related). A genius, laurated Noble Prize winning scientist for instance, can be arrogant about his intelligence. Sure, his belief that he is smarter than everyone else is based on mountains of evidence, but if he is offensive in his display or scorn he shows those of lesser ability he is arrogant. --Xylix 03:12, 20 February 2007 (UTC)
