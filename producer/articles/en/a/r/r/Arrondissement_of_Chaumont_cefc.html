The arrondissement of Chaumont is an arrondissement of France, located in the Haute-Marne département, in the Champagne-Ardenne région. It has 11 cantons and 160 communes.

[edit] Composition

[edit] Cantons

The cantons of the arrondissement of Chaumont are:

  1. Andelot-Blancheville
  2. Arc-en-Barrois
  3. Bourmont
  4. Châteauvillain
  5. Chaumont-Nord
  6. Chaumont-Sud
  7. Clefmont
  8. Juzennecourt
  9. Nogent
  10. Saint-Blin
  11. Vignory
