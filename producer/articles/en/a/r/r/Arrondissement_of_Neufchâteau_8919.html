The arrondissement of Neufchâteau is an arrondissement of France, located in the Vosges département, in the Lorraine région. It has 7 cantons and 174 communes.

[edit] Composition

[edit] Cantons

The cantons of the arrondissement of Neufchâteau are:

  1. Bulgnéville
  2. Châtenois
  3. Coussey
  4. Lamarche
  5. Mirecourt
  6. Neufchâteau
  7. Vittel
