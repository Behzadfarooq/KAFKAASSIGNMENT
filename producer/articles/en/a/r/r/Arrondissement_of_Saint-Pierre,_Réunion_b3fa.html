The arrondissement of Saint-Pierre is an arrondissement of France, located in the Réunion département, in the Réunion région. It has 16 cantons and 8 communes.

[edit] Composition

[edit] Cantons

The cantons of the arrondissement of Saint-Pierre are:

  1. Entre-Deux
  2. Petite-Île
  3. Saint-Joseph 1st Canton
  4. Saint-Joseph 2nd Canton
  5. Saint-Louis 1st Canton
  6. Saint-Louis 2nd Canton
  7. Saint-Louis 3rd Canton
  8. Saint-Philippe
  9. Saint-Pierre 1st Canton
  10. Saint-Pierre 2nd Canton
  11. Saint-Pierre 3rd Canton
  12. Saint-Pierre 4th Canton
  13. Le Tampon 1st Canton
  14. Le Tampon 2nd Canton
  15. Le Tampon 3rd Canton
  16. Le Tampon 4th Canton

[edit] Communes

The communes of the arrondissement of Saint-Pierre are:

1. Cilaos (97424) 2. Entre-Deux (97403) 3. Petite-Île (97405) 4. Saint-Joseph (97412)
5. Saint-Louis (97414) 6. Saint-Philippe (97417) 7. Saint-Pierre (97416) 8. Le Tampon (97422)
