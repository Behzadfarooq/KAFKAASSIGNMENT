The arrondissement of Château-Chinon is an arrondissement of France, located in the Nièvre département, in the Bourgogne région. It has 6 cantons and 72 communes.

[edit] Composition

[edit] Cantons

The cantons of the arrondissement of Château-Chinon are:

  1. Château-Chinon(Ville)
  2. Châtillon-en-Bazois
  3. Fours
  4. Luzy
  5. Montsauche-les-Settons
  6. Moulins-Engilbert
