Arrivederci Roma

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

"Arrivederci Roma" is a popular song.

The music was written by Renato Ranucci, the original Italian lyrics by Pietro Garinei and Sandro Giovannini, with English lyrics by Carl Sigman. The song was published in 1955.

The song was introduced in the movie, Seven Hills of Rome.
