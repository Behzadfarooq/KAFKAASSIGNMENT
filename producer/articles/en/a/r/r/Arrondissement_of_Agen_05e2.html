The arrondissement of Agen is an arrondissement of France, located in the Lot-et-Garonne département, in the Aquitaine région. It has 12 cantons and 71 communes.

[edit] Cantons

The cantons of the arrondissement of Agen are:

  1. Agen-Centre
  2. Agen-Nord
  3. Agen-Nord-Est
  4. Agen-Ouest
  5. Agen-Sud-Est
  6. Astaffort
  7. Beauville
  8. Laplume
  9. Laroque-Timbaut
  10. Port-Sainte-Marie
  11. Prayssas
  12. Puymirol
