The arrondissement of Carpentras is an arrondissement of France, located in the Vaucluse department, in the Provence-Alpes-Côte d'Azur region. It has 8 cantons and 58 communes.

[edit] Composition

[edit] Cantons

The cantons of the arrondissements of Carpentras are:

  1. Beaumes-de-Venise
  2. Carpentras-Nord
  3. Carpentras-Sud
  4. Malaucène
  5. Mormoiron
  6. Pernes-les-Fontaines
  7. Sault
  8. Vaison-la-Romaine
