Arron Yan (Traditional Chinese: 炎亞綸; pinyin: Yán Yǎlún), born Wu Geng Lin (Traditional Chinese: 吳庚霖), is a Taiwanese actor and singer. He is currently a member of the boy band Fahrenheit.

[edit] Biography

Arron Yan is the youngest member of Fahrenheit. He was born on November 20, 1986 in Taiwan. At a young age, his family moved to New York, USA, where he stayed for his primary school education for about five years. He then moved back to Taiwan for secondary school. He can speak and understand Mandarin, English, Taiwanese, and limited Japanese, and Cantonese. He is currently a sophomore at Chinese Culture University, majoring in Journalism.
