The arrondissement of Le Blanc is an arrondissement of France, located in the Indre département, in the Centre région. It has 6 cantons and 56 communes.

[edit] Composition

[edit] Cantons

The cantons of the arrondissement of Le Blanc are:

  1. Bélâbre
  2. Le Blanc
  3. Mézières-en-Brenne
  4. Saint-Benoît-du-Sault
  5. Saint-Gaultier
  6. Tournon-Saint-Martin
