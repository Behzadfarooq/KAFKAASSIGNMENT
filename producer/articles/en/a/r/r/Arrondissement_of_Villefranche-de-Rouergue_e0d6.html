The arrondissement of Villefranche-de-Rouergue is an arrondissement of France, located in the Aveyron département, in the Midi-Pyrénées région. It has 8 cantons and 64 communes.

[edit] Composition

[edit] Cantons

The cantons of the arrondissement of Villefranche-de-Rouergue are:

  1. Aubin
  2. Capdenac-Gare
  3. Decazeville
  4. Montbazens
  5. Najac
  6. Rieupeyroux
  7. Villefranche-de-Rouergue
  8. Villeneuve
