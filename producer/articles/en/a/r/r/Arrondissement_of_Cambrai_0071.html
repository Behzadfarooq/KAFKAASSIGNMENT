The arrondissement of Cambrai is an arrondissement of France, located in the Nord department, in the Nord-Pas-de-Calais region. It has 7 cantons and 116 communes.

[edit] Composition

[edit] Cantons

The cantons of the arrondissement of Cambrai are:

  1. Cambrai-Est
  2. Cambrai-Ouest
  3. Carnières
  4. Le Cateau-Cambrésis
  5. Clary
  6. Marcoing
  7. Solesmes
