The arrondissement of Vendôme is an arrondissement of France, located in the Loir-et-Cher département, in the Centre région. It has 9 cantons and 107 communes.

[edit] Cantons

The cantons of the arrondissement of Vendôme are:

  1. Droué
  2. Mondoubleau
  3. Montoire-sur-le-Loir
  4. Morée
  5. Saint-Amand-Longpré
  6. Savigny-sur-Braye
  7. Selommes
  8. Vendôme 1st Canton
  9. Vendôme 2nd Canton
