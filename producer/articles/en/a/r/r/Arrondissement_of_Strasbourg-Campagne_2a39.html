The arrondissement of Strasbourg-Campagne is an arrondissement of France, located in the Bas-Rhin département, in the Alsace région. It has 8 cantons and 104 communes.

[edit] Cantons

The cantons of the arrondissement of Strasbourg-Campagne are:

  1. Bischheim
  2. Brumath
  3. Geispolsheim
  4. Hochfelden
  5. Illkirch-Graffenstaden
  6. Mundolsheim
  7. Schiltigheim
  8. Truchtersheim
