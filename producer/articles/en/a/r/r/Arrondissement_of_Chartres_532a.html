The arrondissement of Chartres is an arrondissement of France, located in the Eure-et-Loir département, in the Centre région. It has 11 cantons and 162 communes.

[edit] Composition

[edit] Cantons

The cantons of the arrondissement of Chartres are:

  1. Auneau
  2. Chartres-Nord-Est
  3. Chartres-Sud-Est
  4. Chartres-Sud-Ouest
  5. Courville-sur-Eure
  6. Illiers-Combray
  7. Janville
  8. Lucé
  9. Maintenon
  10. Mainvilliers
  11. Voves
