The arrondissement of Compiègne is an arrondissement of France, located in the Oise département, in the Picardie région. It has 10 cantons and 156 communes.

[edit] Composition

[edit] Cantons

The cantons of the arrondissement of Compiègne are:

  1. Attichy
  2. Compiègne-Nord
  3. Compiègne-Sud-Est
  4. Compiègne-Sud-Ouest
  5. Estrées-Saint-Denis
  6. Guiscard
  7. Lassigny
  8. Noyon
  9. Ressons-sur-Matz
  10. Ribécourt-Dreslincourt
