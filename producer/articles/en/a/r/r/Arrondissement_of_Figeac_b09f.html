The arrondissement of Figeac is an arrondissement of France, located in the Lot département, in the Midi-Pyrénées région. It has 9 cantons and 120 communes.

[edit] Composition

[edit] Cantons

The cantons of the arrondissement of Figeac are:

  1. Bretenoux
  2. Cajarc
  3. Figeac-Est
  4. Figeac-Ouest
  5. Lacapelle-Marival
  6. Latronquière
  7. Livernon
  8. Saint-Céré
  9. Sousceyrac
