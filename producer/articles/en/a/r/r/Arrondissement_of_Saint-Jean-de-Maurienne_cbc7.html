The arrondissement of Saint-Jean-de-Maurienne is an arrondissement of France, located in the Savoie department, in the Rhône-Alpes region. It has 6 cantons and 62 communes. The area corresponds to the province of Maurienne.

[edit] Composition

[edit] Cantons

The cantons of the arrondissement of Saint-Jean-de-Maurienne are:

  1. Aiguebelle
  2. La Chambre
  3. Lanslebourg-Mont-Cenis
  4. Modane
  5. Saint-Jean-de-Maurienne
  6. Saint-Michel-de-Maurienne
