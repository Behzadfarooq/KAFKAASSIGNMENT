Arrow (symbol)

An arrow is a graphical symbol like →, ←, used to point or indicate direction, being in its simplest form a line segment with a triangle affixed to one end, and in more complex forms a representation of an actual arrow (e.g. ➵ U+27B5).



[edit] North arrow

North arrow is used in floor plans and maps to indicate the direction to the north.

[edit] Arrows in Unicode

In Unicode, arrow symbols occupy the hexadecimal range 0x2190–0x21ff (8592–8704 decimal), as described below.
