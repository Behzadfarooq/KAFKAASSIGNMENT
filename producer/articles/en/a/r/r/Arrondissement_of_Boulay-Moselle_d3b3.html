The arrondissement of Boulay-Moselle is an arrondissement of France, located in the Moselle département, in the Lorraine région. It has 3 cantons and 96 communes.

[edit] Cantons

The cantons of the arrondissement of Boulay-Moselle are:

  1. Boulay-Moselle
  2. Bouzonville
  3. Faulquemont
