Arrow is horse ridden by American Bruce Davidson and Canadian Graeme Thom in the equestrian sport of eventing.

  • Foaled:
  • Sex: Gelding
  • Color: Bay
  • Markings: n/a
  • Height: 16.2 hh (168cm)
  • Breed: Thoroughbred
  • Breeding: by War Rollick (Dutch Warmblood), out of Never On Monday
  • Rider: Graeme Thom
  • Owner: Graeme Thom

As of February 2006, Arrow was for sale for $75,000. Arrow has competed successfully at the international level, including competing at the Rolex Kentucky Three Day CCI****, finishing 13th, and several times competing at the Fair Hill Three Day Event at the CCI*** level.
