The arrondissement of Sarlat-la-Canéda is an arrondissement in France, located in the Dordogne department, in the Aquitaine region. It has 10 cantons and 122 communes.

[edit] Composition

[edit] Cantons

The cantons of the arrondissement of Sarlat-la-Canéda are:

  1. Belvès
  2. Le Bugue
  3. Carlux
  4. Domme
  5. Montignac
  6. Saint-Cyprien
  7. Salignac-Eyvigues
  8. Sarlat-la-Canéda
  9. Terrasson-Lavilledieu
  10. Villefranche-du-Périgord
