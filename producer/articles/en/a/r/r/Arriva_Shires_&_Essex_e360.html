Arriva Shires & Essex is a division of Arriva and one of many private operators of London Buses. It has operations across Essex, and until recently Colchester. In September 2005, they bought MK Metro. Operations are split between two registered companies, Arriva East Herts & Essex Ltd and Arriva The Shires Ltd.

[edit] Arriva East Herts & Essex Ltd

Arriva East Herts & Essex Ltd are based in Harlow, and operate three bus garages in Harlow, Southend-on-Sea and Ware.

[edit] Harlow

The Harlow garage runs London bus route 500 and LSP routes 505 and 724. The garage was opened 22 May 1963 to serve Harlow New Town and also replace Epping Garage. Harlow was allocated 25 RTs, 16 RMCs (to operate Green Line routes 718, 720 & 720A), seven RFs and two coach RFs.

Bus types in use:

  • Dennis Dart

[edit] Southend-on-Sea

The Southend-on-Sea garage runs all of the Arriva services in the Southend-on-Sea area and is now under control of Arriva Southern Counties. Arriva took over control of the Southend Transport in August 1998 and renamed it Arriva Serving Southend (although the 'Serving Southend' caption has been phased out now).

Bus types in use:

  • Dennis Dart
  • Dennis Dominator
  • Lance/Wright
  • Volvo B6
  • Leyland Olympian

[edit] Ware

The Ware garage runs London bus routes 317, and LSP route 724.

Bus types in use:

  • Dennis Dart

[edit] Arriva The Shires Ltd

Arriva The Shires Ltd is based in Luton and operates two bus garages, in Watford and High Wycombe.

[edit] Watford (Garston)

The Watford garage runs London bus routes 142, 258, 268, 288, 303, 305, 340, H2, H3, H18, H19, LSP route 8, and school routes 142 and H1.

Bus types in use:

  • DAF DB250LF 10.2m/Alexander ALX400
  • DAF DB250LF 10m /Wright Pulsar Gemini
  • Dennis Dart SLF 9.4m/Alexander Dennis Pointer
  • DAF SB120 10.1m/Wright Cadet

[edit] High Wycombe

The High Wycombe garage runs London Buses route U9.

Bus types in use:

  • DAF DB250LF/Alexander ALX400
  • Dennis Dart SLF/Plaxton Pointer
