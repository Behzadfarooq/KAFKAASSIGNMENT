Arrondissements of the Haute-Loire department

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

You have new messages (last change).

The 3 arrondissements of the Haute-Loire department are:

  1. Arrondissement of Brioude, (subprefecture: Brioude) with 9 cantons and 100 communes. The population of the arrondissement was 42,346 in 1990, and was 41,095 in 1999, a decrease of 2.95%.
  2. Arrondissement of Le Puy-en-Velay, (prefecture of the Haute-Loire department: Le Puy-en-Velay) with 17 cantons and 116 communes. The population of the arrondissement was 97,328 in 1990, and was 96,254 in 1999, a decrease of 1.1%.
  3. Arrondissement of Yssingeaux, (subprefecture: Yssingeaux) with 9 cantons and 44 communes. The population of the arrondissement was 66,894 in 1990, and was 71,764 in 1999, an increase of 7.28%.
