The arrondissement of Commercy is an arrondissement of France, located in the Meuse département, in the Lorraine région. It has 7 cantons and 136 communes.

[edit] Composition

[edit] Cantons

The cantons of the arrondissement of Commercy are:

  1. Commercy
  2. Gondrecourt-le-Château
  3. Pierrefitte-sur-Aire
  4. Saint-Mihiel
  5. Vaucouleurs
  6. Vigneulles-lès-Hattonchâtel
  7. Void-Vacon
