Arroz a la cubana

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

You have new messages (last change).

Arroz a la cubana, which means Cuban-style rice, is a Spanish dish consisting of rice cooked black beans and tomato sauce. The dish also seems to be popular in the Philippines and Peru.
