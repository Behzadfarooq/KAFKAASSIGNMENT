The arrondissement of Saumur is an arrondissement of France, located in the Maine-et-Loire département, in the Pays de la Loire région. It has 10 cantons and 112 communes.

[edit] Composition

[edit] Cantons

The cantons of the arrondissement of Saumur are:

  1. Allonnes
  2. Baugé
  3. Doué-la-Fontaine
  4. Gennes
  5. Longué-Jumelles
  6. Montreuil-Bellay
  7. Noyant
  8. Saumur-Nord
  9. Saumur-Sud
  10. Vihiers
