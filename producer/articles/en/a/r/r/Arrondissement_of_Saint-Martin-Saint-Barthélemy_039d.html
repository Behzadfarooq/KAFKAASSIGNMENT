The arrondissement of Saint-Martin-Saint-Barthélemy is a former arrondissement of France, formerly located in the Guadeloupe département, in the Guadeloupe région. It had 3 cantons and 2 communes. It ceased to exist on February 22, 2007 when the communes of Saint-Martin and Saint-Barthélemy were officially detached from Guadeloupe.
