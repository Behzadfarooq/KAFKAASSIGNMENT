The arrondissement of Dreux is an arrondissement of France, located in the Eure-et-Loir département, in the Centre région. It has 9 cantons and 109 communes.

[edit] Composition

[edit] Cantons

The cantons of the arrondissement of Dreux are:

  1. Anet
  2. Brezolles
  3. Châteauneuf-en-Thymerais
  4. Dreux-Est
  5. Dreux-Ouest
  6. Dreux-Sud
  7. La Ferté-Vidame
  8. Nogent-le-Roi
  9. Senonches
