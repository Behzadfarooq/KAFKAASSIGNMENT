The arrondissement of Alès is an arrondissement of France, located in the Gard department, in the Languedoc-Roussillon region. It has 12 cantons and 101 communes.

[edit] Composition

[edit] Cantons

The cantons of the arrondissement of Alès are:

  1. Alès-Nord-Est
  2. Alès-Ouest
  3. Alès-Sud-Est
  4. Anduze
  5. Barjac
  6. Bessèges
  7. Génolhac
  8. La Grand-Combe
  9. Lédignan
  10. Saint-Ambroix
  11. Saint-Jean-du-Gard
  12. Vézénobres
