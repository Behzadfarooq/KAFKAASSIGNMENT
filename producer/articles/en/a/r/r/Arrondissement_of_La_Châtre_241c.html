The arrondissement of La Châtre is an arrondissement of France, located in the Indre département, in the Centre région. It has 5 cantons and 58 communes.

[edit] Composition

[edit] Cantons

The cantons of the arrondissement of La Châtre are:

  1. Aigurande
  2. La Châtre
  3. Éguzon-Chantôme
  4. Neuvy-Saint-Sépulchre
  5. Sainte-Sévère-sur-Indre
