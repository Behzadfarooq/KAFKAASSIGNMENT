The arrondissement of Apt is an arrondissement of France, located in the Vaucluse department, in the Provence-Alpes-Côte d'Azur region. It has 6 cantons and 56 communes.

[edit] Composition

[edit] Cantons

The cantons of the arrondissement of Apt are:

  1. Apt
  2. Bonnieux
  3. Cadenet
  4. Cavaillon
  5. Gordes
  6. Pertuis
