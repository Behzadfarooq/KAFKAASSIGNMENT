The arrondissement of Bar-sur-Aube is an arrondissement of France, located in the Aube département, in the Champagne-Ardenne région. It has 5 cantons and 104 communes.

[edit] Composition

[edit] Cantons

The cantons of the arrondissement of Bar-sur-Aube are:

  1. Bar-sur-Aube
  2. Brienne-le-Château
  3. Chavanges
  4. Soulaines-Dhuys
  5. Vendeuvre-sur-Barse
