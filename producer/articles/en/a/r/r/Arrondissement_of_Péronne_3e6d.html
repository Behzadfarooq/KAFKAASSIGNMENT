The arrondissement of Péronne is an arrondissement of France, located in the Somme département, in the Picardie région. It has 8 cantons and 169 communes.

[edit] Composition

[edit] Cantons

The cantons of the arrondissement of Péronne are:

  1. Albert
  2. Bray-sur-Somme
  3. Chaulnes
  4. Combles
  5. Ham
  6. Nesle
  7. Péronne
  8. Roisel
