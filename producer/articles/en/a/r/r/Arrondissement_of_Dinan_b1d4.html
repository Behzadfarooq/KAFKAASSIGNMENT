The arrondissement of Dinan is an arrondissement of France, located in the Côtes-d'Armor département, in the Bretagne région. It has 12 cantons and 101 communes.

[edit] Composition

[edit] Cantons

The cantons of the arrondissement of Dinan are:

  1. Broons
  2. Caulnes
  3. Collinée
  4. Dinan-Est
  5. Dinan-Ouest
  6. Évran
  7. Jugon-les-Lacs
  8. Matignon
  9. Merdrignac
  10. Plancoët
  11. Plélan-le-Petit
  12. Ploubalay
