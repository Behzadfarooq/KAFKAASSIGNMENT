The arrondissement of Épernay is an arrondissement of France, located in the Marne département, in the Champagne-Ardenne région. It has 10 cantons and 164 communes.

[edit] Cantons

The cantons of the arrondissement of Épernay are:

  1. Anglure
  2. Avize
  3. Dormans
  4. Épernay 1st Canton
  5. Épernay 2nd Canton
  6. Esternay
  7. Fère-Champenoise
  8. Montmirail
  9. Montmort-Lucy
  10. Sézanne
