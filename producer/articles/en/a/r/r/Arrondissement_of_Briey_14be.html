The arrondissement of Briey is an arrondissement of France, located in the Meurthe-et-Moselle département, in the Lorraine région. It has 10 cantons and 130 communes.

[edit] Composition

[edit] Cantons

The cantons of the arrondissement of Briey are:

  1. Audun-le-Roman
  2. Briey
  3. Chambley-Bussières
  4. Conflans-en-Jarnisy
  5. Herserange
  6. Homécourt
  7. Longuyon
  8. Longwy
  9. Mont-Saint-Martin
  10. Villerupt
