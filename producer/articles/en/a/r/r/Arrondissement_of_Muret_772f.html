The arrondissement of Muret is an arrondissement of France, located in the Haute-Garonne département, in the Midi-Pyrénées région. It has 11 cantons and 126 communes.

[edit] Composition

[edit] Cantons

The cantons of the arrondissement of Muret are:

  1. Auterive
  2. Carbonne
  3. Cazères
  4. Cintegabelle
  5. Le Fousseret
  6. Montesquieu-Volvestre
  7. Muret
  8. Portet-sur-Garonne
  9. Rieumes
  10. Rieux
  11. Saint-Lys
