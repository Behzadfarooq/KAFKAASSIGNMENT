The arrondissement of Bonneville is an arrondissement of France, located in the Haute-Savoie department, in the Rhône-Alpes region. It has 10 cantons and 61 communes.

[edit] Composition

[edit] Cantons

The cantons of the arrondissement of Bonneville are:

  1. Bonneville
  2. Chamonix-Mont-Blanc
  3. Cluses
  4. La Roche-sur-Foron
  5. Saint-Gervais-les-Bains
  6. Saint-Jeoire
  7. Sallanches
  8. Samoëns
  9. Scionzier
  10. Taninges
