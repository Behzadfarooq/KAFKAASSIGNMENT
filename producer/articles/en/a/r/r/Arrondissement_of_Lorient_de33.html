The arrondissement of Lorient is an arrondissement of France, located in the Morbihan département, in the Bretagne région. It has 15 cantons and 60 communes.

[edit] Composition

[edit] Cantons

The cantons of the arrondissement of Lorient are:

  1. Auray
  2. Belle-Île
  3. Belz
  4. Groix
  5. Hennebont
  6. Lanester
  7. Lorient-Centre
  8. Lorient-Nord
  9. Lorient-Sud
  10. Ploemeur
  11. Plouay
  12. Pluvigner
  13. Pont-Scorff
  14. Port-Louis
  15. Quiberon
