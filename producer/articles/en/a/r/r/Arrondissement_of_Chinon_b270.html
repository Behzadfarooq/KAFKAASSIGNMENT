The arrondissement of Chinon is an arrondissement of France, located in the Indre-et-Loire département, in the Centre région. It has 7 cantons and 87 communes.

[edit] Composition

[edit] Cantons

The cantons of the arrondissement of Chinon are:

  1. Azay-le-Rideau
  2. Bourgueil
  3. Chinon
  4. L'Île-Bouchard
  5. Langeais
  6. Richelieu
  7. Sainte-Maure-de-Touraine
