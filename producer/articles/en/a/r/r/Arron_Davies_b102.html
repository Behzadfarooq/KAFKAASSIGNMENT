Arron Davies (born June 22, 1984) is a footballer, currently playing as a forward for Yeovil Town. He has earned 1 cap for the Wales national football team, in a friendly against Trinidad and Tobago.

Arron was raised in a little town in Wales called Llantwit major, where he went to Eagleswell Primary and Llantwit Major Comprehensive Schools.
