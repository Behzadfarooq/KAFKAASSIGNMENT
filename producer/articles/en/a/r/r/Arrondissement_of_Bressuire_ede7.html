The arrondissement of Bressuire is an arrondissement of France, located in the Deux-Sèvres département, in the Poitou-Charentes région. It has 7 cantons and 64 communes.

[edit] Composition

[edit] Cantons

The cantons of the arrondissement of Bressuire are:

  1. Argenton-Château
  2. Bressuire
  3. Cerizay
  4. Mauléon
  5. Saint-Varent
  6. Thouars 1st Canton
  7. Thouars 2nd Canton
