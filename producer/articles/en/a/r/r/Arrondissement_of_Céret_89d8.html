The arrondissement of Céret is an arrondissement of France, located in the Pyrénées-Orientales département, in the Languedoc-Roussillon région. It has 5 cantons and 40 communes.

[edit] Composition

[edit] Cantons

The cantons of the arrondissement of Céret are:

  1. Argelès-sur-Mer
  2. Arles-sur-Tech
  3. Céret
  4. Côte Vermeille
  5. Prats-de-Mollo-la-Preste
