A Techno Tribute to Depeche Mode
Tribute album by Various Artists
Released April 13, 2001
Genre Techno
Length 59:57
Label Big Eye Music
Producer(s) Various

A Techno Tribute to Depeche Mode is a 2001 tribute album of Depeche Mode songs. In some releases the title is lengthened to I Just Can't Get Enough: A Techno Tribute to Depeche Mode.

1. Intro (1:49)
2. Audio Science - Strangelove (6:57)
3. Kirk - Behind the Wheel (7:29)
4. Axis 01 - PIMPF (6:28)
5. Blasphemous Garden - Blasphemous Rumours (5:02)
6. Bypass Unit - I Just Can't Get Enough (5:58)
7. Para One - Any Second Now (5:51)
8. Lindum & Lindum - Waiting for the Night (5:57)
9. Godheads - One Caress (3:27)
10. Mirrorman - Little 15 (4:37)
11. The Scarabs - Get the Balance Right (6:22)
