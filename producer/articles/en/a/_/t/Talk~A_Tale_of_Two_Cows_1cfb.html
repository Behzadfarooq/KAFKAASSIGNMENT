[edit] candlelighting

It is incorrect to state that traditionally men never light candles in the Jewish traditions. A single male or a man who is not in the presence of women is supposed to light candles himself. The Shabbat or Festival candles were historically lit by women because they were at home during candlelighting time whereas the men were at the Synagogue. Valley2city 04:42, 15 September 2006 (UTC)

I'll reword the statement.Raymondluxuryacht 04:57, 15 September 2006 (UTC)
