After Hours: a Tribute to the Music of Lou Reed
Studio album by Various Artists
Released 2003
Genre Rock
Label Wampus Multimedia
Producer(s) Mark Doyon (exec)

Eamon Loftus (mastering)

After Hours: a Tribute to the Music of Lou Reed is a tribute album released in 2003 by the American label Wampus Multimedia. It focuses on Reed's pivotal role in the Velvet Underground through his reinventions as glam jester, hardcore pre-punk, lovestruck Romeo, and acerbic rock 'n' roll survivor.
