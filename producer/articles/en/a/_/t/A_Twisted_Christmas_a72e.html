A Twisted Christmas is a Christmas album from US heavy metal group Twisted Sister, released on October 17, 2006.

[edit] Track listing

  1. "Have Yourself a Merry Little Christmas" - 4:48
  2. Oh Come All Ye Faithful - 4:40
  3. White Christmas - 3:56
  4. I'll Be Home for Christmas (featuring Lita Ford) - 4:08
  5. Silver Bells - 5:05
  6. I Saw Mommy Kissing Santa Claus - 3:39
  7. Let It Snow - 3:09
  8. Deck the Halls - 2:52
  9. The Christmas Song (Chestnuts Roasting on an Open Fire) - 3:40
  10. Heavy Metal Christmas (The Twelve Days of Christmas) - 5:14
  11. Bonus Track: We Wish You a Twisted Christmas (parody/remake of We Wish You a Merry Christmas) - 0:36

[edit] Trivia

  • Oh Come All Ye Faithful uses the tunes of We're Not Gonna Take It (the melody of which was inspired by the carol) and Hava Nagila. Its video is in the same vein of We're Not Gonna Take It and I Wanna Rock, and was performed on the Tonight Show on December 13th, 2006
