A Time to Be Born 2004 novel by John Vornholt set in the fictional universe of Star Trek: The Next Generation. It is the 1st in the series "A time to..." novels, depicting the events that took place before Star Trek: Nemesis. It is the first book in the series and prequels A Time to Die

[edit] Plot

In this book, Jean-Luc Picard and crew are assigned to assist in the salvage of ships and bodies from a battle site during the Dominion War. However, danger is all over the Rashanar Battle Site, and soon the legendary crew of the Enterprise find themselves under court martial.

Not all is lost though, since a "friend" from the past now known as the Traveler returns to keep an eye on the legendary crew.
