[edit] Plot

Veronica finally investigates what happened to her the night of Shelly Pomroy's party where she was drugged and date-raped and what she finds out is shocking. It's even more shocking when she arrives home to find that her mother is back. Veronica ditches Logan after finding out that he brought GHB to Shelley's party, but they later reconcile. Aaron, who wants to show Logan that he's reformed, throws a surprise party for his son, where everyone discovers that Veronica and Logan are now together. He tells everyone that if they have a problem with his girlfriend, they can leave. Veronica discovers cameras in Logan's poolhouse and wrongly assumes that he was going to tape them having sex, so at the end of the episode, she yet again ditches him and calls Weevil to come take her home.

[edit] Arc significance

  • Logan and Luke got GHB (also known as "Liquid X") from Mexico and brought it to Shelly Pomroy's party. Luke lent some to Dick who wanted to use it on his girlfriend Madison Sinclair. He drugged her drink, but she, seeing Veronica, spit in it and surreptitiously handed it to her (the "trip to the dentist" of the title). Veronica got drugged from the drink, and Dick brought her upstairs so Beaver could take advantage of her in her unconscious state. Logan decided to create a salt lick when Veronica passed out, but Duncan stopped them. However, his drink had been drugged with GHB by Logan to loosen him up and Veronica and Duncan ended up having sex while in their drugged state.
  • Duncan left Veronica alone the next morning because his mother, Celeste, had told him she was his sister.
  • Keith goes to Las Vegas to talk to a prostitute who was with Abel Koontz at the time of Lilly's murder.
  • Everyone discovers that Logan and Veronica are going out.

[edit] Production details

  • This episode was originally called "Up on the Roofie".
  • This episode has the return of possibly the most one-shot characters in the show. There was Casey Gant from "Drinking the Kool-Aid", Luke from "You Think You Know Somebody", Sean Friedrich from "An Echolls Family Christmas", Carrie Bishop from "Mars vs. Mars", and Cole from "Like a Virgin".
