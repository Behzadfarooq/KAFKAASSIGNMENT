Muddy Water Blues: Tribute To Muddy Waters Album by Paul Rodgers of Bad Company Fame. Contributing artists include Brian May, Buddy Guy, David Gilmour, Jeff Beck, Neal Schon, Richie Sambora, Slash, Steve Miller, Trevor Rabin.
