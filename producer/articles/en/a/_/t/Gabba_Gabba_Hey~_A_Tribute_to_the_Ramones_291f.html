Gabba Gabba Hey: A Tribute to the Ramones
Tribute album by Various artists
Released August 30, 1991
Genre Punk rock
Label Triple X Records

Gabba Gabba Hey: A Tribute to the Ramones is a 1991 tribute album by various artists as a tribute to the Ramones.
