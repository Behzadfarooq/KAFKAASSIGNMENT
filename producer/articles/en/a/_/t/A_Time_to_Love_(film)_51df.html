A Time to Love (film)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

A Time to Love (Simplified Chinese: 情人结; Traditional Chinese: 情人結; pinyin: Qĺngrén jié) is a 2005 film directed by Huo Jianqi. It stars Mainland actress Zhao Wei and actor Lu Yi.
