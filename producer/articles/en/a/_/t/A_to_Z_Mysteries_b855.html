A to Z Mysteries

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

A to Z Mysteries is the name of a children's book series by Ron Roy. There are 26 books in the series, one for each letter of the alphabet. The series ended with The Zombie Zone on 28 April 2005, but a new series, Detective Camp (with the same characters) has started with the first book, Detective Camp, released in May 2006.

[edit] Characters

The three junior detectives in this series are Donald David "Dink" Duncan, Joshua Pinto and Ruth Rose Hathaway. These three are 9 years old and live in Green Lawn, a fictional town in Connecticut. When a mystery comes up, they're on the case to solve it. The adventures they find themselves in are written by children's author Ron Roy. In 2006, a special edition called Detective Camp was launched. "Mayflower treasure hunt", Special Edition 2, will be published by August 2007.
