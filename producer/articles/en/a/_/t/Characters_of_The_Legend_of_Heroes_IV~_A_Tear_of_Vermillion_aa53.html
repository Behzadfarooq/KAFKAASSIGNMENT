[edit] Neutral Characters

[edit] Avin

  • Alignment: Good through the whole game.
  • Affiliations: Knows people working for the Bardus Church. He knows all of the Legendary Sage. He is a becomes a world-renowned mercenary for the Adventurer's Guild.
  • Age: 9 before story; now 17
  • Town: Ourt village and Lookout Cottage
  • Types of Spells: Black Spells; from Chapter 3 on, also Spirit Spells

Avin is the main protagonist of the game. He has a painful memory of the attack on Cathedral. He has a sister, Eimelle. He becomes an adventurer to help find his long-lost sister. Unfortunately, he is subject to many ordeals and attacks. After losing Eimelle for the third time, he searches for the four remaining Sacred Treasures of Bardus.

[edit] Mile

  • Alignment: Good until Chapter 3. Bad (because of mind control) in Chapter 3. Good again in the final chapter.
  • Affiliations: Knows the Sage of Mercy
  • Age: 10 during prologue; now 18, still 18 at two deaths
  • Town: Ourt Village and Lookout Cottage
  • Types of Spells: White Spells; Chapter 4 to end, also black spells

Mile becomes best friends with Avin during the Festival of Nepthys. He is the wiser one. He also becomes an adventurer. Just before chapter 3, he dies trying to protect Eimelle. During Chapter 3, he is under the control of Bellias. He still has his mind, as proven in Chapter 4. He says he know what he did was wrong and helps by telling about the Sealed Land.
