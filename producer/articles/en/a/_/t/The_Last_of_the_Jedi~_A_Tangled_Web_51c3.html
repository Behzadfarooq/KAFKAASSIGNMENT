A Tangled Web is the 5th book in Jude Watson's series The Last of the Jedi. It involves Ferus Olin as the Main character.

[edit] Summary

Since Palpatine's takeover, he believes his power is absolute. He controls everything, including the senate. He is the leader of the evil Sith.

And now, for some unexplainable reason, he wishes to control Ferus. But Ferus does not want to help him, until Palpatine threatens those people closest to his heart. This leaves Ferus with no option but to help Palpatine. Palpatine wishes to use Ferus as a undercover agent - a double-agent. But can Ferus do this and keep his allegiance to the Jedi Order?

This book was released in August 2006.
