A Train or the variant A-Train may mean:

  • A (New York City Subway service), made famous by the jazz song Take the A Train
  • Green Line "A" Branch in Boston, Massachusetts
  • An electric rail line of the Key System that last ran between Oakland and San Francisco in 1958.
  • A-Train, a computer game developed by Artdink in 1992
  • A set of five environmental satellites - see Aqua, Aura, CALIPSO, CloudSat and PARASOL
  • (A-train), an umbrella term covering a range of trains manufactured by Hitachi, Ltd. in Japan. [1]

  • Devon Hughes (also known as "A-Train"; born 1972), a professional wrestler who was formerly known as D-Von Dudley.
  • Anthony Thomas (football) (nicknamed "A-Train"; born 1977), a professional American football player
  • Mike Alstott (nicknamed "A-Train"), a professional American football player
  • Artis Gilmore (nicknamed "A-Train"), former professional basketball player
