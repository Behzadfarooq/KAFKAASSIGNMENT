Project Majestic Mix: A Tribute to Nobuo Uematsu is a fanmade tribute album of video game music in honor of Nobuo Uematsu's work for video games, produced by Square Co., Ltd.. The album is either a Silver Edition or a Gold Edition, and both contain a selection of musical tracks from the games, arranged and performed by KFSS Studios. The Silver Edition was released on May 9, 2002 by NTT Publishing Co., Ltd., DataM and DigiCube, and the Gold Edition was released on June 22, 2002 by NTT Publishing Co., Ltd., DataM and DigiCube. The Silver Edition release bears the catalog number KFSS-702 and the Gold Edition release bears the catalog number KFSS-701, but it's not available for purchase.
