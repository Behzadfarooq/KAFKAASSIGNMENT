The Bridge: A Tribute To Neil Young
Tribute album by Various Artists
Released July, 1989
Recorded 1988-1989
Genre Alternative rock
Length 56:31
Label Caroline Records
Producer(s) Various

The Bridge: A Tribute To Neil Young is a 1989 anthology album that features a variety of alternative rock bands covering songs written by Neil Young. A portion of the profits from the album were donated to The Bridge School, which develops and uses advanced technologies to aid in the instruction of handicapped children.
