Felt: A Tribute to Christina Ricci is the first album by Felt, a side project made up of Slug and MURS. It is entirely produced by Living Legends member The Grouch and it contains scratches provided by Mr. Dibbs. The album is dedicated to actress Christina Ricci as part of a wager between the two rappers over who could have sex with her first. So far, neither has been successful.[1]
