[edit] Now the rubber band is on the other claw

If you remember when Ziodberg attacks Earth he says "now the rubber-band is on the other claw". This is based after the saying "the shoe is on the other foot" meaning instead of Earth attacking Zoidberg, now Zoidberg and his people are attacking Earth. Also the rubber-band and claw idea was from that in seafood sections in supermarket they keep lobsters in a viewing tank with rubber bands on the claw so when cook take them out, they won't pinch the cook.

While this is true we should not take it upon ourselves to explain every joke in the show. Sometimes people will just have to figure it our for themselves. Stardust8212 14:40, 10 January 2007 (UTC)
