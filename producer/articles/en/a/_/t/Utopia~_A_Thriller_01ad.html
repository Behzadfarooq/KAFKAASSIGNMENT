Utopia (ISBN 0-385-50668-6) is a novel by Lincoln Child published in 2003. It is set in a futuristic amusement park called Utopia, a park that relies heavily on holographics and robotics. Dr. Andrew Warne, the man who designed the program that runs the park's robots, is called in to help fix a problem. But when he gets there, he finds out that the park is being held hostage by a mysterious man known as John Doe.

Worlds: Utopia is made up of Worlds modeled after different time eras.

Gaslight: based on Victoria London.

Camelot: a medieval kingdom.

Boardwalk: a reproduction of a turn-of-the-century sea side amusement park.

Callisto: a futurist spaceport above Jupiter's sixth moon.

Atlantis: a water park based on the lost city.
