A Taste of Yesterday's Wine is an album by American country music artists George Jones and Merle Haggard. This album was released in 1982 on the Epic Records label. This was Jones' and Haggard's very first album together, their next album together would not come until 24 years later in 2006 called "Kickin' Out the Footlights...Again" released on October 24, 2006 on the Bandit Records label.
