"A To The B", is a song by the Danish rock/electronica band, Infernal. It reach 45 on the Australian charts, but was not released in the UK.

[edit] Music video

The Music video of "A to the B" illustrates the fictional character, Superman (played by Paw Laggerman) flying throughthe air and then accompanied by several dancing women. Then we see a different woman, wearing a wolf skin standing at the top of a skyscraper (who we assume is supermans lover | Played by Lina Rafn) who is trying to get supermans attention. She jumps of the skyscraper, and is caught by a flying superman. Superman then leaves her and starts dancing with the other women. The woman wearing the wolfskin walks away, and we se her sitting on a bench, picking the petals off a flower. The scene changes and we see the woman tying herself to a traintrack. Superman saves her again and returns to where the women were dancing - but are no longer there. A tv we assume is near narrates "It seems to me that all us strong, independent women make men lazy. All of a sudden, they think they dont have to court us properly! But boys, let me tell you something, there is nothing like a man who knows how to treat a lady." We now see a succession of jumping off the skyscraper from Lina Rafn, but the second time, Superman does not leave her, but takes her to Krypton, (supermans home planet from the comic sierees). Lina seems to walk into crystals, and comes out like superwoman, another character from Superman. Superman is seen flying off and the scene cuts to the Eiffle tower, where a man is just about to get into a shower.

This video seems to suggest that men have forgotten how to look after women, "that you will be mine the old-fashioned way".
