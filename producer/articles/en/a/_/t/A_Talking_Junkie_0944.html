"A Talking Junkie" was the sixth episode of the HBO series Mr. Show to air. It originally aired on November 22, 1996.

[edit] Trivia

  • Fake Special Thanks: Charles Portis
  • DVD Commentary: David Cross, Bob Odenkirk, Jay Johnston, Paul F. Tompkins, Brian Posehn, Bill Odenkirk, Mary-Lynn Rajskub (as herself and as Pat Lemontime, Southern old-fashioned public access host who now resides in Simi Valley)
  • Three Times One Minus One was based on R&B musician R. Kelly.
  • Writers Jay Johnston, Paul F. Tompkins, and Bill Odenkirk went uncredited in this episode due to budget constraints, even though they did, in fact, work on the episode.
  • The sketch "Mom and Pop Porn Shop" came from a premise that was thought of by writer David O. Waterman, who had known David Cross and had been a member of his comedy troupe "Cross Comedy". Waterman gave Cross permission to use the premise for the show.
