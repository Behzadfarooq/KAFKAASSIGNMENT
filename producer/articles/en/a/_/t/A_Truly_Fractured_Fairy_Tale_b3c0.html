"A Truly Fractured Fairy Tale" is the fourth episode of the ABC romantic comedy-drama television series Cupid.

[edit] Plot synopsis

A woman in Claire's group dreams of a male model on a billboard, and Trevor introduces them. The relationship goes well initially, but they end up apart because he's a city boy and she's a country girl. Meanwhile, Claire's faith in romance is restored by anonymous gifts, but it turns out they were inteded for a different woman.
