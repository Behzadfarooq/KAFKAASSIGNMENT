A Tale of A Tale of Two Cities is an action packed movie. It is going to be produced by PAD Studios and Directed by Jake Gryb. This movie is currently being filmed in Charlton MA., and is expected to be released on April 15th.

This movie is based upon the novel by Charles Dickens and is a great alternative to the book. Although considerably shorter than the novel(30 min.) the movie is a fun and a good time. The actors do a great job with the characters.

One problem with the movie is that the majority of the 12 scene movie is dominated by scene 9. This scene is the storming of the bastille and has a touch of modern era guns as well as new age editing and graphics. This movie is surpirsingly well edited and well handled despite its under $100 budget.
