Elliot Goldenthal scored the movie A Time to Kill in 1996, its emotional, sweeping themes can, given the subject matter of thew film, at times be a tad melodramatic but Goldenthal's singular vision ensures that it is never boring or dull.

The track "Pressing Judgment" is used in Goldenthal's score for Titus in a longer version incorperating that films theme.
