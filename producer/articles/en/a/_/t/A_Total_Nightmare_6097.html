A Total Nightmare is the 3rd full-length album from Simi Valley, CA based punk rock band Midget Fan Club. The cover art is taken from a painting by Vincent Van Gogh. The band tried to mix the themes and style of 1970s Classic Rock with the new school Punk Rock roots of the MFC. The songs are engineered to flow into one another, with a divider between what would be the A and B sides of the record. This album is available for free at the band's website. Direct download link.
