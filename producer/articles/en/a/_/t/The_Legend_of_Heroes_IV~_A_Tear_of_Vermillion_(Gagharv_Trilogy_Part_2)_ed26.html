[edit] Story

On his quest for light, will Avin survive the darkness?

In the post-Gagharv era, the land of El Phildin is consumed by a bitter war. As its citizens fight to survive, one will be taken by the darkness. As time passes and light returns, wounds heal and lives move on - except for one.

On his quest to free his sister, will Avin be prepared for what he finds?

[edit] Main Characters

Avin: The main character of this story. He sets out on a journey to find his sister Eimelle who has been separated from him for years.

Eimelle: A kindhearted and modest girl. Avin's young sister and the only family of Avin since being orphaned.

Rutice: A heroine of this tale. A member of the heretics under Bellias, but is seeks the truth.

Mile: Avin's close friend. He is concerned about Avin, and travels together him.

Shannon: A pure and innocent girl. She believes that Mile is the ideal man since being saved by Mile.
