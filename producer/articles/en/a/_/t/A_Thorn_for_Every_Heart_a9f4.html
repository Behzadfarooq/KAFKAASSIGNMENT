A Thorn for Every Heart is an alternative/Post-Hardcore quartet from Chino Hills, California on the Kickball Records label.

[edit] Band members

  • Kelvin Cruz - Lead vocals
  • Jeff Harber - Guitar, piano, vocals
  • Phil Nguyen - Guitar, vocals
  • Justin Powell - Bass

They are currently without a drummer, and are using their friend Matt Thorsen of Downtown Singapore for the upcoming album.
