This is an alphabetical list of notable female movie actors.

A certain number of actors of this list are also well-known because of their roles in television films and series, and are therefore included in both, this list and the list of television actors.

See also:  Actors,  List of male movie actors,  List of male television actors  and  List of female television actors .

Note: In some forms of current English usage, the terms actor or female actor are often used in place of the more widespread actress.
