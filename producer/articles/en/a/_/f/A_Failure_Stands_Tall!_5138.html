A Failure Stands Tall! (Japanese: 憧れの人の目の前で!!, - Akogare no hito no me no mae de!!) is episode 47 of the anime series Naruto. The episode's storyline is from the Chūnin Exam manga arc.

[edit] Plot

Hinata, being encouraged by Naruto, exchanges blows with Neji. Even though the Jūken doesn't look that flashy, it turns out to be very effective. While Hinata was trying to damage Neji's internal organs, Neji was actually sealing Hinata's chakra points, another ability of the Hyūga's ability to use the Byakugan.

Neji tells Hinata that she cannot win, and it is predetermined by fate. Naruto keeps encouraging Hinata, who continues to fight, even without her chakra. Neji deals nearly lethal blows, disrupting her kidneys, heart, and other organs.

Hinata still does not give up, she tells Neji that he is also suffering inside because he knows that he's part of a lesser branch of the clan. This enfuriates Neji, and he rushes at her in a blind rage, intending to kill her. However, at this point, Kakashi, Gai, Kurenai, and Hayate all stop Neji, who claims that since Hinata is part of the Main house, the jōnins will not let her come to harm. Hinata collapses and Naruto, Sakura, and Lee quickly rushes to her side. Neji then calls Naruto out telling him that if he intends to be called a shinobi, he tells him to stop cheering and that once a failure, always a failure. This enrages Naruto, but before he can fight Neji, Rock Lee steps in.

Unfortunately, the injuries to Hinata's heart prove near fatal and she goes into cardiac arrest. Naruto watches with horror as the medics hurriedly carry Hinata off. He kneels down and collects some droplets of her blood in his fist. He faces Neji and shows him his now bloody fist and swears on Hinata's blood he will defeat Neji.

[edit] Trivia

  • Most of the Naruto episodes when aired on Cartoon Network are edited to remove blood, but almost all the blood in this episode was left intact.
  • In the UK it is edited out that Kurenai says Hinata is going into cardiac arrest.
  • It is also edited that Naruto swears on Hinata's blood.
