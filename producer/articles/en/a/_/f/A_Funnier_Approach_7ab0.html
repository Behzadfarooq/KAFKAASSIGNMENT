A Funnier Approach is a Ten-pin bowling book of cartoons that was released in 2000 by Triangle Publications. It was written by Walt Steinsiak, and is a sequel to Balls - Bowling, of Course! and A Funny Approach. The book was followed by another in the series, The Funniest Approach. Every copy of the book was signed by the author.
