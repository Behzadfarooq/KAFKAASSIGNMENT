Evil Dead: A Fistful of Boomstick is a video game for the PlayStation 2 and Xbox video game consoles, and PC. It is the unconnected sequel to the previous game, Evil Dead: Hail to the King, based on the movie trilogy The Evil Dead. Evil Dead: Hail to the King is mentioned part of the sub-plot. Included is the final battle with Evil Ash in Hail to the King.

[edit] Plot

Spoiler warning: Plot and/or ending details follow.

Ash Williams must once again save Dearborn, Michigan from the evil Deadites after Professor Raymond Knowby's tape is played on a live television broadcast, opening a Vortex to the Undead.

[edit] The Characters

  • Ashley J. "Ash" Williams - Our reluctant hero, fully "armed" with his chainsaw, trusty boomstick and witty one-liners.
  • Trisha Pettywood - An overly ambitious journalist out to expose the truth of The Book of the Dead.
  • Professor Alex Elridge - A bestselling occult author, colleague of the late Professor Knowby.
  • Deadites - Zombie-like creatures who attack anything in their way.
  • Hell hounds - Demon dogs from the underworld whose bite and bark are most unpleasant.

[edit] Game Features

  • Cult icon Bruce Campbell returns to voice the role of "Ash"
  • Vortices to the past and future with four noticeably different eras in Dearborn
  • Multiple classes in weaponry from clubs to blades and firearms
  • Two armed attacks allow simultaneous control over multiple weapons
  • Upgradable weapons
  • Combo and Chain attacks
  • Spell casting system
  • Bonus "Making of" video featuring Bruce Campbell and THQ's developmental staff
  • One-liner button
