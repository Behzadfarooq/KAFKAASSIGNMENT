A Fairytale for You (お伽話をあなたに, Otogibanashi wo Anata ni?) is a one-volume manga by mangaka Ayumi Shiina.

It is a collection of 3 short love stories:

  1. A Fairytale for You (お伽話をあなたに, Otogibanashi wo Anata ni?)
  2. A Fairytale for You 2 - The Jewel Princess (, Otogibanashi wo Anata ni 2 - Houseki Hime?)
  3. Carefree Maria (, Kimagure na MARIA?)
