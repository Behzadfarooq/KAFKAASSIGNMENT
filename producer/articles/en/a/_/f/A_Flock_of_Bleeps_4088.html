A Flock of Bleeps
Studio album by Younger Brother
Released 2003

A Flock of Bleeps is Younger Brother's debut album. It is a collaborative psybient/psychill project between English psytrance Producers Simon Posford (Hallucinogen, Shpongle) and Benji Vaughan (Prometheus).
