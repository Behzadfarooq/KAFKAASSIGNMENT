A Fisherman of the Inland Sea is a 1994 collection of short stories by Ursula K. Le Guin. The collection was second in the 1995 Locus Award poll in the collection category.[1]

[edit] Contents

The stories include:

  • "The First Contact with the Gorgonids"
  • "Newton's Sleep"
  • "The Ascent of the North Face"
  • "The Rock That Changed Things"
  • "The Kerastion"
  • "The Shobies' Story"
  • "Dancing to Ganam"
  • "Another Story OR A Fisherman of the Inland Sea"
