A Feather in His Hare is a Warner Brothers Looney Tunes animated short, directed by Charles M. Jones. It was originally released on February 7, 1948. Because the cartoon features an offensive stereotype of an American Indian, this short is seldom screened.


[edit] Trivia

This cartoon was one of 11 pulled from Cartoon Network's annual June Bugs marathon in 2001 [1] as being potentially offensive.
