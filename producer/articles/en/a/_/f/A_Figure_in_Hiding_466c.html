A Figure In Hiding is Volume 16 in the original Hardy Boys book series published by Grosset And Dunlap.

[edit] Plot introduction

While investigating the theft of a valuable idol, Frank and Joe encounter a terrified business woman, a walking mummy, and a weird glass eye.
