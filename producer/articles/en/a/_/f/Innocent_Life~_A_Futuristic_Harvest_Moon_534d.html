Innocent Life: A Futuristic Harvest Moon is a PlayStation Portable game that breaks many of the traditions established in the other Harvest Moon games. Unlike Harvest Moon Portable which is simply a port of the first game, Innocent Life will be a brand new game. It was released for the PlayStation Portable on April 27th, 2006 in Japan.

The game itself will take place on a relic-filled island which can be explored by walking or biking. Players will have the ability to explore ruins and even visit a volcano. All these areas have their own terrain and is said to resemble a tropical paradise.

The game features a new art style that steps away from the traditional style of the previous Harvest Moon games. The lead character of the game is also said to have an in-depth backstory which will help to allude to why he has chosen to become a farmer and eventually reveal the meaning of life as he sees it.
