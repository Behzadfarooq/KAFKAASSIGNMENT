A Funky Thide of Sings is a 1975 studio album by Billy Cobham.

[edit] Musicians

Billy Cobham (synthesizer, percussion); Michael Brecker, Larry Schneider (saxophone); Randy Brecker, Walt Fowler (trumpet); Tom Malone (trombone, piccolo); Glenn Ferris (trombone); Milcho Leviev (keyboards); John Scofield (guitar); Alex Blake (bass); "Rebop" Kwaku Baah (congas).
