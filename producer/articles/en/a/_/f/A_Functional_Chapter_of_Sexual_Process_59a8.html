A Functional Chapter of Sexual Process

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Editing A Functional Chapter of Sexual Process is the 1992 release from German industrial and noise group Genocide Organ. Released in an edition of 80 copies in black and gold acetates. There are 2 different covers for this release, one with a vagina and the other with a penis. Released by Gravestyle.
