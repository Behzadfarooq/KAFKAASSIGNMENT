A Flame in Hali is a fantasy novel written by Marion Zimmer Bradley and Deborah J. Ross as part of the Darkover series and is set in the "Hundred Kingdoms" time period. This book is also part three of the Clingfire Trilogy.

[edit] Plot summary

Spoiler warning: Plot and/or ending details follow.

A Flame in Hali is set in Darkover's "Hundred Kingdoms" era. It is an era blurred in with the Ages of Chaos at the tail end. Breeding programs are no more, but wars are still fought with terrible laran weapons. The events in this book start some years after the end of Zandru's Forge. It overlaps with Two To Conquer as it mentions the Kilghard Wolf and Varzil's visit in Asturias.

[edit] Characters

Main article: Characters in Darkover

  • Eduin MacEarn, youngest son of Rumail Deslucido, seeking revenge against the Hasturs and Varzil Ridenow
  • Saravio, laranzu, was thrown out of Cedestri tower because he didn't agree to the Compact, and because his abilities violated a laranzu's promise not to alter an unwilling mind.
  • Varzil Ridenow (aka Varzil the Good), one of the most powerful laranzu'in of his time,
  • Dyannis Ridenow, sister of Varzil, leronis, trains to be a keeper
