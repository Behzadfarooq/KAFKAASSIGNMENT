A Sort of Homecoming is a bootleg live album recorded at various locations (primarily Madison Square Garden in New York City) by progressive metal band Dream Theater and released by the Dream Theater International Fan Club.

[edit] Track listing

  1. "NYC Radio Ad" – 1:01
  2. "As I Am" – 7:10
  3. "Honor Thy Father" – 9:47
  4. "Caught In A Web Pt. 1" – 3:44
  5. "Mike Portnoy Drum Duet with Scott Rockenfield" – 7:35
  6. "Beyond This Life Improv Jam" – 11:07
  7. "The Oakdale Oddysey" – 5:42 (improvised jam)
  8. "The Spirit of St. Louis" – 6:17 (improvised jam)
  9. "John Petrucci Guitar Spot" – 3:27
  10. "Vacant" – 2:35
  11. "Stream of Consciousness" – 11:02
  12. "Disappear" – 6:10
