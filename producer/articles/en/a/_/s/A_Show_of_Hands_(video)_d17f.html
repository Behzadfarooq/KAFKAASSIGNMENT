A Show of Hands (video)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

A Show of Hands is a videocassette/laserdisc released by the Canadian band Rush. Not to be confused with the album of the same name, the video had a slightly different set list, and contains different performances. It was released in 1989 by PolyGram Records. A DVD was released in the box set Rush Replay X 3.
