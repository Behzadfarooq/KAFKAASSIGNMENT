A Static Lullaby is the third album by post-hardcore band A Static Lullaby. It was released on October 10, 2006 on Fearless Records. This is the first release after the departure of three members of the band's original lineup. The fully-mastered copy was leaked onto P2P clients on September 21st, 2006.
