A Skylit Drive is an American post-hardcore band formed in late 2005, in Lodi/Galt, California. They played their first show in January 2006. A Skylit Drive joined Tragic Hero Records in 2006, and recorded their only formal release, and EP entitled "She Watched the Sky." It was released on January 9, 2006.

A Skylit Drive has toured the United States during 2006 & 2007 with various bands. They are currently on a national tour with A Heartwell Ending, which ends April 28, 2007.
