A Safer Place is the debut album from UK alternative metal band Exhibit A. Released June 28, 2004 by Fulmination Records.

[edit] Track listing

  1. "Breathe" – 2:12
  2. "One Song" – 3:22
  3. "No One / Nothing" – 2:42
  4. "Dismiss" – 2:40
  5. "Fallen" – 2:46
  6. "A Life Beneath" – 2:20

All songs written and performed by Exhibit A

[edit] Personnel

  • Jon Willock - vocals
  • Bez - vocals
  • Mick Korba - guitar
  • Widge - guitar
  • Jon Legge - bass guitar
  • Simon Walklate - drums
