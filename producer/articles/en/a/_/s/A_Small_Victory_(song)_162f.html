"A Small Victory" is a song by Faith No More appearing on their fourth album Angel Dust. When asked about the songs meaning, Mike Patton says "Its kind of about, well my dad was a coach, so I grew up and I always wanted to win. And well, I found out that I just cant win every game...darn it."

[edit] Music Video

In Sempteber, 1993, The music video was nominated for the MTV Video Music Awards for Best Art Direction.
