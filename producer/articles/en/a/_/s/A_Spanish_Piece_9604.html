"A Spanish Piece" is a short track from Pink Floyd's Music from the Film More album running for 1:04 minutes. It is an instrumental using the typical Spanish riff accompanied by vocalisations in the background done by David Gilmour.

  • David Gilmour - Guitar and Vocalisations
