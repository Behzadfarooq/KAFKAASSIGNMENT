The Team: A Season With McLaren is a 7 part TV series produced by the BBC during the 1993 Formula One season and first transmitted during November and December 1993.[1] The series followed the team and regular drivers Ayrton Senna, Michael Andretti, as well as test driver Mika Häkkinen giving a behind the scenes insight to the McLaren team.

The series consisted of 7 Episodes, each approx 30mins in length:

  • A Man For All Seasons
  • The Rookie
  • A Few Good Men
  • The Boy From Brazil
  • Friendly Persuasion
  • The Rules Of The Game
  • Good-bye To All That
