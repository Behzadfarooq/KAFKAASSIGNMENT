A Skateboard Party is a live album by the Dead Kennedys and was released in August 1983 on Starving Missile Records in cooperation with Alternative Tentacles as a limited edition release. It features the famous exchange where Jello Biafra asks what the audience wants, a girl yells, "Autograph, goddammit!", prompting Jello to retort with, "I'll autograph you with my dental chart."

The concert took place in Munich, Germany, on December 13, 1982. On the original vinyl version, Side A was tracks 1-10 and Side B was tracks 11-17
