A Song for You is an album of rapper Bizzy Bone. It is his first major label album in 6 years. The album will be released in 2007, by Virgin Records. Confirmed is that DMX, Mariah Carey, Twista, Drag-On, Chris Notes, Trina and Good Charlotte will be on the album. Bizzy confirmed in an interview that there will be a video for the album.

[edit] Track Listing

  • "Back in the High Life" (featuring Chris Notes)
  • "I Truly Believe"
  • "Druck Up" (featuring Fabolous, Eightball, Drag-On & UGK)
  • "So Lonely" (featuring Mariah Carey, Trina & Twista)
  • "Life in Rock Star" (featuring Good Gharlotte)
