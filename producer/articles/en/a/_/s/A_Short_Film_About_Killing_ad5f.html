A Short Film About Killing (Polish: Krótki film o zabijaniu) is a 1988 film directed by Krzysztof Kieslowski. Set in Warsaw, Poland, it explores murders by the state and by individuals[1]. It is an expanded version of the fifth episode of The Decalogue.
