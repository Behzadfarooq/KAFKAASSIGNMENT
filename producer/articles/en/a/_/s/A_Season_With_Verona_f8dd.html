A Season With Verona is the title of a 2002 book by Verona based British author Tim Parks. It tells the story of a single season following the fortunes of Italian football club Verona, and deals especially with Parks' relationship with the infamous hard core tifosi who make up Verona's travelling support. All the matches are detailed as well as many off-field dealings.
