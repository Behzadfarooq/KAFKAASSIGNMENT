A Spooky Night in Disney's Haunted Mansion (1998 Cassette) The original terrifying tale with scary sounds, chilling chants, horrible hosts, and the song "Grim Grinning Ghosts". Reissue of the 1969 The Haunted Mansion LP. Out of print.
