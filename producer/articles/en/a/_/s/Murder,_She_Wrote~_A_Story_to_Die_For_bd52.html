Murder, She Wrote: A Story to Die For is the second of the four Murder, She Wrote TV movies. It first aired May 18, 2000 on CBS.

[edit] Synopsis

Jessica Fletcher puts her sleuthing skills to work at a writer's convention after a Russian author is found dead.

[edit] Cast

  • Angela Lansbury as Jessica Fletcher
  • Richard Crenna as Warren Pierce
  • Kathryn Morris as Patricia Williams
  • Daniel Dae Kim as Everett Jay
  • Duncan Regehr as Yuri Malenkovich
  • Jay Acovone as Lt. Det. Bob Mankowsky
  • Steven Culp as William Bassby
  • J. Patrick McCormack as Joseph Brennan
