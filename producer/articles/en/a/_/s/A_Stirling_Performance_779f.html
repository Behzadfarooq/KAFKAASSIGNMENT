A Stirling Performance is a documentary about R.E.M.'s three-night stand at Stirling Castle on July 19, 20 and 21, 1999, and the effect it had on Stirling and its residents. It was released in 2000 and was included on R.E.M.'s live performance DVD Perfect Square in 2004.

The documentary premiered in the UK on October 17, 2000, at London's Metro Cinema during the Raindance Film Festival. Its US premiere took place at 7 Stages in Atlanta, Georgia, on June 10, 2001.
