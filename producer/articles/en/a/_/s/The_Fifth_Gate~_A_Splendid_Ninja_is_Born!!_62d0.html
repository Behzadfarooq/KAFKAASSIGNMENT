The Fifth Gate: A Splendid Ninja is Born!! (Japanese: 嗚呼ロック・リー！これが男の生き様よ！！, - Aa Rokku Rī! Kore ga otoko no ikizama yo!!) is episode 50 of the anime series Naruto. The episode's storyline is from the Chūnin Exam manga arc. It details the final part of the Lee vs. Gaara battle.

[edit] Plot

The Episode begins with Lee saying: I cannot be the only one who loses here! After Lee opens up the Life Gate, Kakashi believes that Lee was going to make his move. However, Lee does not, and actually opens the Fourth gate, the Gate of Pain. Lee's speed has increased tremendously, as well as his muscular force. Lee begins to hit Gaara with tremendous force many times, wreaking the entire Armor of Sand. At this stage, Gaara's first defense, the Shield of Sand, can not possibly keep up with the speed at which Lee is moving.

Lee kicks Gaara into the air, and begins to assault Gaara there with a bunch of high speed attacks. Gaara looks like a pinball at the rate Lee is hitting him. Lee remembers of how he wanted to beat Neji Hyuga so badly and thinks in his mind that he will show Neji this move. Might Guy is thinking about that while Lee is hitting Gaara that Gaara cannot even see Lee and that the answer to beating Neji is within the high speed combo. Everyone is thinking something about how strong Lee is.

Finally, Lee opens the Fifth gate, the closing Gate. By now, many of Lee's muscles have torn. Lee attacks Gaara with the Hidden Lotus. Lee hopes to smash Gaara into the ground, and thus either knock out or kill Gaara. However, Gaara transforms his entire gourd into sand, which protects Gaara enough that he can still fight.

By now, Lee is critically injured from using the Lotus too much. Gaara then uses Desert Coffin and Desert Funeral to destroy Lee's left arm and left leg. Gaara prepares to finish Lee off, but Gai steps in to intervene, stopping the sand. Gai remembers how Lee is very much like him, always working hard, and used to be taunted for his lack of skill. However, Lee worked night and day, training himself, just like Gai.

Gaara asks why Gai just stopped his attack, and Gai replies "because he is my student, and also because he is precious to me." The other sand ninja realize that Gaara will probably never understand those words.

Since Gai stepped in, Gaara is automatically the winner. Hayate was about to announce this, but Lee suddenly stands up. Gai starts telling Lee that it is over and he should rest, and all the ninja are surprised that Lee is still trying to fight. However, Gai then suddenly realizes that Lee was unconscious, and was moving purely by muscle reflex.

The Konoha medical team came to get Lee. A doctor tells Gai that even though they will give Lee the best possible treatment, most of his bones have broken and his muscles have been torn, and it is very likely that Lee will never be able to live as a ninja again.

At the end, Kakashi realizes that Gai allowed Lee to use Ura Renge to fulfill Lee's deep ambition, and makes up with Gai.

[edit] Trivia

  • This is the first time we are introduced to Lee's ability to move by muscle reflex while unconscious. The second time is in episode 154, when Lee fought Raiga while unconscious.
  • The fansub name of this episode is Ah Rock Lee! This is the way a man should be!
