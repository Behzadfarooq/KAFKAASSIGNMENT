[edit] 2003

In 2003, we saw the last Song For Europe, the winner being Jemini with "Cry Baby", which finished last in Eurovision the following May.

  1. Jemini - "Cry Baby" 68pts
  2. Emily Reed - "Help Me" 64pts *
  3. Mimi - "Ever Since That Night" 54pts
  4. Simon Chapman - "Now and Forever" 0pts **

* Emily Reed was a backing singer for Daz Sampson in Eurovision Song Contest 2006.

** Simon Chapman was the third singer to sing this song; the original singer was Esther Hart, who represented the Netherlands in Eurovision Song Contest 2003, and the second act to sing this was United Colours of Sound, who pulled out at the last minute.
