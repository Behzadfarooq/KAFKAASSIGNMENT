A Shit Leopard Can't Change Its Spots is the 21st episode of the hit Canadian comedy mockumentary series Trailer Park Boys.


[edit] Trivia

  • This is the first time in the series that Ricky does not go to jail at a season's end.
  • Lahey and Randy go to jail for the first time.
  • This is the first episode Julian goes to prison without Ricky.

[edit] Quotes

Ricky: I've noticed that if you throw something into a water body, like a lake or an ocean, that the next day you come back and it's gone so, somehow it takes it away and filters it through and it just cleans it up, like a garbage compactor or whatever. So it's not really littering if you ask me.
