A Singles Collection
Compilation album by Marillion
Released 1992

A Singles Collection is a collection of Marillion singles from both the Fish era and the Steve Hogarth era. It also includes a cover of Sympathy which was released as a video at that time. It includes all the bands most successful singles of the Fish era, plus all the Steve Hogarth singles up to that year. It was released in two versions, one UK and one U.S. edition, with different names: in the U.S., simply A Singles Collection, in the UK it was called Six of One, Half Dozen of the Other.
