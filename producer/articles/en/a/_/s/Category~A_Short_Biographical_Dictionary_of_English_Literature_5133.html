This category lists articles which were sourced from, as well as existing and coincidental articles on authors listed in A Short Biographical Dictionary of English Literature. The biography is in the Public Domain and served by Project Gutenberg [1].

The main purpose of the category is to provide Wikipedians porting content from the Biography with a quick author lookup; it is anticipated that the category will be deleted once the entire content of the Biography has been transferred.
