A sega nakade (Bulgarian language:А сега накъде, English language: And Where Do We Go from Here? is a 1988 Bulgarian drama film directed by Rangel Vulchanov and written by Georgi Danailov. The film is centres on an actors examination and involves themes of corruption and immorality in important exams.

[edit] Release and acclaim

The film premiered on 17 October 1988 in Bulgaria. The film was produced by Boyana Film.
