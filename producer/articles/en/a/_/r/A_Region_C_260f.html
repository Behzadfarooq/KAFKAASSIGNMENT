Region C is one of the four A regions of the Virginia High School League. It is made up of four districts: the A Hogoheegee District, the A Mountain Empire District, the A Pioneer District, and the A Three Rivers District. The region stretches from the Alleghany Highlands to most of Southwest Virginia, including the Roanoke and Bristol areas. In the upcoming realignment cycle, several of its southern members will be transferred into Region D starting with the 2007-08 school year.
