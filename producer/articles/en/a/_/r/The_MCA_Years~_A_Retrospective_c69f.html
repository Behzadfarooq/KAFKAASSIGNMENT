The MCA Years: A Retrospective was released on MCA in 1993 , and contains songs from her five MCA albums recorded between 1997 and 1991 - Lone Star State Of Mind (1987) , Little Love Affairs (1988) , One Fair Summer Evening (1988) , Storms (1989) and Late Night Grande Hotel (1991).
