Lupe Fiasco & DJ WunderKidd: A Real MC is a 2007 mixtape released by rapper Lupe Fiasco and DJ WunderKidd. It contains popular Lupe tracks, remixed into featuring other artists' vocals and beats by Lupe and DJ WunderKidd.

[edit] Track listing

  1. "Ignorant Shit" (Featuring Lil' Wayne)
  2. "Pressure" (Featuring Tupac Shakur & Jay-Z)
  3. "Lu Yourself" (Featuring Eminem)
  4. "Champ Is Here" (Featuring Jadakiss)
  5. "Mic Check" (Featuring Juelz Santana)
  6. "I’m Runnin’" (Featuring The Game)
  7. "Hood-Hop" (Featuring Mobb Deep)
  8. "Still Tippin’" (Featuring 50 Cent)
  9. "Sunshine" (Featuring Tupac Shakur)
  10. "Skit"
  11. "Freestyle"
  12. "Freestyle 2"
