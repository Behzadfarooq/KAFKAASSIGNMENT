A Rúa (Spanish: La Rúa) is a mostly rural municipality in Galicia, located at 42°24′N 7°06′W, in the east of the province of Ourense, 101 km (63 mi) from Ourense, the provincial capital. An inhabitant of the area is known as a Rués (plural Rueses).

As of 2005, A Rúa has a population of 4996 people. It has an area of 35.9 km² (14 sq mi) and is 298 m (978 ft) above sea level. It contains three parroquias.
