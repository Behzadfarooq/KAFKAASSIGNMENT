A Rose For The Dead was the last release using the doom metal sound Theatre of Tragedy originally adopted. The track "Decades" was a cover version of the song originally performed by Joy Division.

[edit] Track listing

  1. A Rose for the Dead – 5:11
  2. Der Spiegel – 5:17
  3. As the Shadows Dance – 5:28
  4. And When He Falleth (Remix) – 5:13
  5. Black in the Devil Painteth (Remix) – 4:29
  6. Decades (Bonus Track) – 6:36
