A Retrospective
Studio album by Pamela Moore
Released 2004
Genre Pop, Hard Rock
Producer(s) Paul Speer

A Retrospective is a limited edition EP with a collection of songs written by Pamela Moore and, among others, Radar.

The song "Reminds Me Of You" (Moore/Paul Speer) was featured in the independent movie Minimal Knowledge.

[edit] Track listing

  1. "Power Of Money"
  2. "Shot Down"
  3. "Mirror Mirror"
  4. "Lookin' For Love"
  5. "Reminds Me Of You"
  6. "Win Your Heart"
