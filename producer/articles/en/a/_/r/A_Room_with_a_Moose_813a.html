A Room with a Moose is an episode of the animated television series Invader Zim, originally produced as episode 9A and aired as episode 7A on August 17, 2001.

[edit] Plot details

Spoiler warning: Plot and/or ending details follow.

In episode 7a of the first season, the Irken title character Zim attempts to take a bus load of schoolchildren, those he has perceived as having crossed him, and ship them off to get rid of them for good. He sends them through a wormhole to a room with nothing but a moose in it.

As this is happening, the only one who isn't fooled by Zim's illusion is Zim's nemesis, the human boy Dib. Dib attempts to stop the "driver" on the schoolbus only to learn that it is a robot, and that it is actually piloting a school bus-shaped spaceship. He then finds a television screen on the robot's face that shows Zim and his psychotic robot slave, GIR. As Zim talks to Dib, he shows him a disturbing scene where he throws walnuts into the room with the moose. Dib is driven to the brink of psychosis by the noise of the moose chomping away on the walnuts. Zim ends the conversation telling Dib to enjoy his "moosey fate", a phrase suggested by GIR.

Dib runs back towards the passenger part of the bus where he attempts to warn the other students. They mock him and disregard his warnings. Dib hooks up his laptop to the bus to plot out the course of the wormhole, and finds that the trajectory forks at a point further on. The left fork leads to the room with the moose, and the right fork back to Earth. He tries to get his fellow students to change from the left side of the bus to the right so they would tilt the bus and it heads towards the right side and thus back towards Earth. They refuse because they would "have to be near him." Dib, unable to change their minds decided to move to the left side of the bus, resulting in the children moving to the right to avoid being near him. The weight shift causes Dib's plan to succeed and the bus to head back towards Earth.

The bus safely lands and Dib vaunts proudly, while the other students talk behind his back about him being weird.

Spoilers end here.
