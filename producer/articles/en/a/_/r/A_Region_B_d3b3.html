A Region B is one of the four regions in Group A of the Virginia High School League. It is made up of four districts: the A Bull Run District, the A Dogwood District, the A James River District and the A Shenandoah District. The region is one of the larger regions geographically, extending from Clarke County on Virginia's northern border to Pittsylvania County on Virginia's southern border along its north-south axis and from Lynchburg to Richmond along its east-west axis.
