A Restatement of the History of Islam and Muslims CE 570 to 661 is a 1996 book written by Sayed Ali Asgher Razwy, in which most of the history of Islam is retold in the Shi'a perspective. Published by World Federation of KSI Muslim Communities, United Kingdom ISBN 0-9509879-1-3 [1]
