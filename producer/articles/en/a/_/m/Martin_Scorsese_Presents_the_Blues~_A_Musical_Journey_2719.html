Martin Scorsese Presents The Blues: A Musical Journey is a 2003 Various artists box set released on Hip-O Records. It is the soundtrack to the Martin Scorsese PBS documentary series on the American music genre known as the blues. The box set attempts to present a history of the blues from the dawning of recorded music to the present day. It offers a survey of many different blues sub-genres and tangential music styles, as well as a survey of almost all the most notable blues performers over time.
