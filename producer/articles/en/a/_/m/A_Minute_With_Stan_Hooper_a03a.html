A Minute With Stan Hooper

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

A Minute with Stan Hooper, also simply known as Stan Hooper, was a short-lived sitcom on the FOX Network starring Norm Macdonald. The central character, Stan Hooper, was taken from Macdonald's work on Saturday Night Live.
