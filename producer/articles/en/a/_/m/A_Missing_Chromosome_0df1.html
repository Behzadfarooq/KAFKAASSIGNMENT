Released on July 12, 2005 and distributed only to radio stations, A Missing Chromosome is a compilation of various non-LP tracks and B-sides by The Mars Volta. The title comes from the first line of the chorus in "A Plague Upon Your Hissing," "a missing chromosome walks."

[edit] Track listing

  1. "Cut That City" – 5:44
  2. "Concertina" – 4:54
  3. "Eunuch Provocateur" – 8:47
  4. "A Plague Upon Your Hissing" – 4:40
  5. "Ambuletz" – 7:03
  6. "The Bible And The Breathalyzer" – 5:17
  7. "Frances The Mute" – 14:36

[edit] Production

  • Tracks 1-3 recorded in Long Beach, California from October to December 2001.
    Produced by Alex Newport and The Mars Volta for the Tremulant EP.
  • Tracks 4-5 recorded 2002 - 2003 at The Mansion, Los Angeles.
    Produced by Rick Rubin and The Mars Volta for De-loused in the Comatorium; track 4 was left off the album, while track 5 appeared on The Australian Tour edition and the UK release.
  • Tracks 6-7 recorded 2004.
    Produced by The Mars Volta for Frances the Mute; track 6 appears on the "L'Via L'Viaquez" single, while track 7 appears on the "Widow" single (along with a live version of the title track).

[edit] Miscellanea

  • Many of the lyrics for "A Plague Upon Your Hissing" would later appear in the song "Day of the Baphomets", on their 2006 LP Amputechture.
  • "Frances The Mute" was originally meant to be the first track on Frances The Mute. However, due to the time constraints of a compact disc and Universal Records' unwillingness to release a double album, the song was cut from the tracklisting.
