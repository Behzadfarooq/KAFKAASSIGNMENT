I'm never gonna get all of the cruft. I'm never even gonna get it faster than it's made. But it's a windmill to tilt at.

Hi. I know a lot about cartoons, games, and to some extent, comics. This is my personal to-do list to cut down on cruft. (Cruft, in this case, means overspecific articles, which are likely to be unexpandable stubs or chock full of overspecific trivia or plot summary.)

This is largely going to be dealt with merges and redirects, so don't think I'm going to delete stuff about your favorite game.
