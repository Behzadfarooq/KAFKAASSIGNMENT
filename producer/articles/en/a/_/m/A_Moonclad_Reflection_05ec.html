A Moonclad Reflection (released in 1992) was Dark Tranquillity's second demo release after first demo Trail of Life Decayed. The songs in this demo can also be found in the Tranquillity demo (1992), combined with Trail of Life Decayed demo. After twelve years, in their live&compilation album Exposures - In Retrospect And Denial, the band had re-released these two songs re-mastered with Trail of Life Decayed demo and some old unreleased tracks.

  2. "Yesterworld" – 7:56
