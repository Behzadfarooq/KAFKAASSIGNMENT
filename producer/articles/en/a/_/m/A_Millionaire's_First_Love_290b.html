A Millionaire's First Love (백만장자의 첫사랑, Baekmanjangja-ui cheot-sarang) is a Korean movie released in 2006, starring Hyun Bin and the beautiful star Lee Yeon Hee (이연희).

[edit] Cast

Hyun Bin starred as Kang Jae-Krung, Lee Yeon Hee starred as Eun-Whan

Warning: Review/Commentary may include Spoiler!!!

The movie, while falls under the stereotypical Hallyu format (read boy-meets-girl-with-terminal-disease-self-discovery-coming-of-age) definitely also presented many plusses usually absent in sappy, love stories.

First of all, the actual passing of the lead character was implied, not shown so there was no high-decible wailing, tearing of hair scene at all. In fact, towards the end, everything about the movie was very calm, mimicking the readiness of the character's to let go.

Secondly, the male lead played his conflicted role wonderfully. Many viewers may have felt that he was "in and out" of character but in actuality, he merely projected the unexpected reactions in all of us when dealing with the issue of imminent death.

Lastly, there is a twist in their relationship that is to be savored as all the pieces come together. This is one of the better products to have emerged from Korean Cinema.
