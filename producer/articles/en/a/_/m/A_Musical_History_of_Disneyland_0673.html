A Musical History of Disneyland is a CD boxed set collection of music from Disneyland, spanning opening day to the present. Released on May 5, 2005 in honor of Disneyland's 50th Anniversary, it is the biggest release in Walt Disney Records' history.

[edit] Editions

[edit] Standard edition

  • Unlimited run
  • Red cloth covered box
  • The 6 CD audio library
  • The Sounds of Disneyland hardcover book

[edit] Special edition

  • Unlimited run
  • Red cloth covered box
  • The 6 CD audio library
  • The Sounds of Disneyland hardcover book
  • Walt Disney Takes You To Disneyland: A Musical Tour of The Magic Kingdom" record album

Since the limited edition quickly sold so well, it was decided to create a special edition with a unlimited run in which the album would be made of black vinyl instead of gold. [1]

[edit] Limited edition

  • Limited run of 5,000 (out of print)
  • Red cloth covered box
  • The 6 CD audio library
  • The Sounds of Disneyland hardcover book
  • Gold vinyl reproduction of the "Walt Disney Takes You To Disneyland: A Musical Tour of The Magic Kingdom" record

[edit] Limited edition special numbers

  • Number 0001: Sold on eBay by Disney.
  • Number 0050: Sold on eBay by Disney.
  • Number 0885: A copy that was signed by Randy Thornton and won in a contest at MagicMusic.net.

There are numbers higher than 5,000, this was due the use of an 'unsecured' numbering method – a commonly used system. However, there are still only 5,000 copies. [2]

In 2005 Disney sold a test pressing on eBay.
