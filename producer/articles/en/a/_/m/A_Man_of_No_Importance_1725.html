"A Man of no Importance" is the third single released by the UK-based band Cherubs. The track does not appear on their only album at time of release, Uncovered by Heartbeat, but the B-side is a demo version of Hey Bunny on the CD, and Apollo Studios version of The Kiss All Morning is featured on the 7" B-side. It is named after a play.
