[edit] Split A/C into separate articles?

I am thinking of splitting the A-C article into two articles, covering the A and C lines by themselves. Please discuss this before a final decision is made.

I don't feel strongly about it either way. Every C station is also an A station (late nights), so a split would create a lot of duplication. However, the overlap wasn't always the case, so the history of each service becomes a little simpler to describe if the other service isn't included in the same article.
