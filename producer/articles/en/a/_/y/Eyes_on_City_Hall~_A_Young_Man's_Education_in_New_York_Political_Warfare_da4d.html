Eyes on City Hall: A Young Man's Education in New York Political Warfare was written by Evan Mandery, the author of 1997's The Campaign: Rudy Giuliani, Ruth Messinger, Al Sharpton, and the Race to Be Mayor of New York City and 2007's Dreaming of Gwen Stefani.

Eyes on City Hall: A Young Man's Education in New York Political Warfare is a non-fiction book published by Westview Press in 1999. The book was generally met with good reviews.
