A Ass Pocket of Whiskey
Studio album by R. L. Burnside
Released 18 June 1996
Label Matador Records

A Ass Pocket of Whiskey is an album by R. L. Burnside & Jon Spencer Blues Explosion released on Matador Records on 18 June 1996. The genre might be described as lo-fi storytellin' garage-blues-rock with explicit (dirty) lyrics.
