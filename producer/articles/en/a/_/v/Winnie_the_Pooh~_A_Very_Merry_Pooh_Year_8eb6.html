Winnie the Pooh: A Very Merry Pooh Year is a direct to video Winnie the Pooh movie released in 2002 which featured the 1991 Christmas TV special Winnie the Pooh and Christmas Too.

[edit] Plot

Spoiler warning: Plot and/or ending details follow.

It's christmas eve and everyone is gathered for the celebration. They tell Roo the story of Winnie the Pooh and Christmas Too. Then with the countdown to New Year, Rabbit plans a party but then gets angry about the others behaviour and decides to leave the Hundred acre wood. To stop Rabbit from leaving everyone makes a new year resolution to change. But then things go topsy-turvy when Pooh is gloomy, Eeyore is happy and eating hunny, Piglet is bouncing everywhere and Tigger is scared of everything.
