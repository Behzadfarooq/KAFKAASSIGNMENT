[edit] Ace Reporter, Ren Höek: Mr. Horse Returns

[edit] Plot

We find Mr. Horse and his sheep when they return to the United States of America, with Ren as an anchorman asking Mr. Horse questions.

[edit] Notes, Goofs, and Trivia

  • To respond to Ren's 1st question, Mr. Horse says the opposite of his usual catchphrase.

[edit] Quotes

  • Ren: Is that your fiance?

(sheep blinks eyes beautifully at Mr. Horse)

Mr. Horse: No, sir. This is my sheep.

Ren: There you have it. Straight from the horse's mouth. I'm ace reporter, Ren Hoek.

  • Ren: So tell me, are you a celebrity?

Mr. Horse: No sir, I am a real horse!

[edit] Plot

This episode is about a little boy who sends Ren and Stimpy a letter to come and visit him in the United States. The duo receive that letter, then went to Anthony's house. At first, the three have some fun, but next morning, some grief was caused in the bathroom (cartoon characters don't do what they did), then Anthony's dad caused the duo some big trouble.

[edit] Additional Opening Credits

  • Storyboard: Jim Smith

[edit] Trivia

  • The letter shown in the episode is actually from a kid named Anthony who did the voice for his character (you can tell Anthony did his own voice in the credits).
  • In some parts of this episode, you can tell that Billy West did Ren's voice, even though Kricfalusi was Ren's main voice-over.
  • This episode marks the introduction of Victor the bully, who was voiced by Danny Cooksey.

[edit] World Crisis With Mr. Horse

[edit] Plot

Someone tells Mr. Horse some stuff about the world crisis. As Mr. Horse hears them, he tells them to Ren. And, of couse, Mr. Horse didn't feel good about it.

[edit] Notes, Goofs, and Trivia

  • The captions on the DVD says that the person telling the information was Stimpy, though I'm not sure it is Stimpy.
  • Translated at Google translate, it seems that "Las cucarachas entran, pero no pueden salir" doesn't mean "No sir, I don't like it.", it means, "The roaches enter, but they can't leave." The real translation is "Ningún sir, no tengo gusto de él."

[edit] Quotes

  • Mr. Horse (saying "No, Sir. I don't like it" in the Spanish language): Las cucarachas entran, pero no pueden salir.

Ren: No, sir. He doesn't like it.
