"A Very Precious Love" is a popular song.

The music was written by Sammy Fain, the lyrics by Paul Francis Webster. The song was published in 1957.

It was introduced in the movie Marjorie Morningstar and recorded by The Ames Brothers, by Doris Day, and by Bonnie Guitar. It was nominated for the 1958 Oscar.

The recording by The Ames Brothers was released by RCA Victor Records as catalog number 47-7167. It first reached the Billboard magazine charts on March 31, 1958. On the Disk Jockey chart, it peaked at #23; on the composite chart of the top 100 songs, it reached #65.
