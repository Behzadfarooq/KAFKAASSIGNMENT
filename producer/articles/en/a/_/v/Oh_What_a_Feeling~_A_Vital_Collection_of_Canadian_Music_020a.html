Oh What a Feeling: A Vital Collection of Canadian Music

Oh What A Feeling: A Vital Collection of Canadian Music was a 4-CD box set released in 1996 to celebrate the 25th anniversary of the Juno Awards. A second box set, Oh What A Feeling 2, was released in 2001 to mark the awards' 30th anniversary, and a third set, Oh What A Feeling 3, was released in 2006 for the 35th anniversary.

All of the sets feature popular Canadian songs from the 1960s onward. The sets were titled for the song "Oh What A Feeling" by rock band Crowbar.
