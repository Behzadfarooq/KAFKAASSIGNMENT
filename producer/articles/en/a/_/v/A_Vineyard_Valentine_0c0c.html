A Vineyard Valentine is episode 15 of season 6 of the television series Gilmore Girls. It originally aired on February 14, 2006.

Spoiler warning: Plot and/or ending details follow.

[edit] Summary

In this episode, Logan invites Luke, Lorelai and Rory to spend the Valentine's Day weekend with him at his family's house in Martha's Vineyard. On Saturday, the two couples visit the gym and then eat. Later, Lorelai and Luke discuss their upcoming wedding. The following day, Mitchum Huntzberger, Logan's father, reprimands him and commands him to leave.
