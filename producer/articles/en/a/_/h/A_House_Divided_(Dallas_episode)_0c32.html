"A House Divided" is the 25th and final episode in the second season (1979-1980) of the television series Dallas.

[edit] Overview

This is the episode that created the worldwide "Who Shot J.R.?" phenomenon. Dallas went on to finish at #1 in the Nielsen Ratings for three of the next four seasons as a result of the publicity this episode generated.
