A Hot Night in Paris
Live album by The Phil Collins Big Band
Released 1999
Genre Jazz

A Hot Night in Paris, released in 1999, was the only album released by The Phil Collins Big Band. Fronted by pop singer Phil Collins, the album did not contain any singing. Instead, the album consisted of big band renditions of primarily Phil Collins and Genesis songs, with Collins remaining at the drums.

The album did not crack the Billboard 200, although it did reach #2 on the jazz album chart.
