A Haunting is a television series on Discovery Channel that, according to its website[1] chronicles "the terrifying true stories of the paranormal told by those who experienced real-life horror tales." It is about people who lived in haunted houses. You can see A Haunting every Thursday at 8:00 PM on the Discovery Channel. Season 4 of A Haunting is currently being filmed.

[edit] About the Show

A Haunting is a docu-drama series, chronicling the true stories of the paranormal told by those who experienced real-life horror tales, airing on Discovery Channel.

Each one-hour episode dramatizes ghost stories based on true events. Through first-person accounts, the mystery and origin of each haunting is unraveled. In each episode, normal people are drawn into seemingly unexplainable events.

[edit] Summary of A Haunting

When the door between the spirit world and ours is opened, the stuff of nightmares becomes reality. That's according to the introduction of this series which dramatizes the "true" stories of people who have been haunted.

In this docu-drama, the actual people who have experienced the paranormal recount their stories on-camera, as the frightening events of their stories are re-enacted. (Fearing ridicule, some of the witnesses are shown only in silhouette.) "A Haunting" is filmed in a very cinematic style which sets out to frighten.

"A Haunting" began as two specials ("A Haunting in Connecticut" and "A Haunting in Georgia") before being turned into a series.
