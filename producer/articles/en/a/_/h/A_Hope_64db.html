A Hope (Chinese: Chang Jiang Yi Hao) is a science fiction/comedy film directed by Stephen Chow. It was previously known as Yangtze River 7. It is currently being filmed in Ningbo, in the Zhejiang province.

[edit] Plot

Stephen Chow plays a poor father who can't afford a toy for his son. He decides to pick one up from the garbage, but it turns out to be a magical toy from outer space.
