A Hard Road is a 1967 Electric Blues album recorded by John Mayall's Bluesbreakers featuring Peter Green on lead guitar, John McVie on bass, Aynsley Dunbar on drums and John Almond. Tracks 5, 7 and 13 feature the horn section of Alan Skidmore and Ray Warleigh. Peter Green sings lead vocals on "You Don't Love Me" and "The Same Way." The cover art and the original LP sleeve design are by J. Mayall.

In 2003 and 2006 two different expanded versions of the album were released.
