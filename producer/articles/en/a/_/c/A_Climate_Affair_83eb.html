A Climate Affair is a weekly environmental radio programme and podcast produced and presented by Kate Conroy for Planet Radio in Brisbane, Australia. The programme is billed as a "critical analysis of current environmental issues"; each episode includes the latest environmental news, interviews with environmentalists and scientists, and commentary and critical discussion on various environmental and societal issues. Despite the programme's title, A Climate Affair covers more than just environmental issues; as the FAQ points out, climate can refer to many things, including the "political and cultural climate of our lives".

[edit] Kate Conroy

Kate Conroy is a PhD student studying philosophy and cognitive science, who completed her undergraduate degree at the University of Melbourne before being awarded a scholarship at Rutgers University in New Jersey in the United States. She continues to study but now lives in Brisbane.

[edit] Guests

The guests on A Climate Affair include both experts and enthusiasts; among their number have been musicians Vashti Bunyan and Mal Webb, representatives from Australian environmental organisations and environmentally aware companies including The Wilderness Society, econnect and JackGreen, and Steven Gale of the Suitable Transport project.
