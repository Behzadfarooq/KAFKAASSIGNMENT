Retardation: A Celebration is Strangers with Candy's unaired pilot. Paul Dinello and Stephen Colbert play nurses as well as teachers. Amy Sedaris plays the same characater Jerri Blank.

[edit] Plot

Jerri is asked to spy on her locker neighbor, looking for signs of retardation.

  • Sarah Thyre - (Sara Blank)

[edit] Trivia

The plot used in this unaired pilot was recycled in episode four: Who Wants Cake?. The major difference between the episodes is that Jerri works at a nursing home in Retardation: A Celebration and interacts with Paul Dinello and Stephen Colbert as nurses. Who Wants Cake? is set entirely at Flatpoint High.
