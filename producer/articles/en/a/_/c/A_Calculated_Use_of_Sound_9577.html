A Calculated Use of Sound is an EP released by post-hardcore metal band Protest The Hero in 2003. It was later re-released in 2004 with re-mastered tracks, a remade cover with a black color scheme, and an additional song titled "Soft Targets Make Softer Graves".

[edit] Track listing

  1. "Red Stars over the Battle of the Cowshed" – 2:51
  2. "An Apathetic New World" – 3:07
  3. "These Colours Don't Run" – 3:13
  4. "Soft Targets Dig Softer Graves" – 4:29 – Only available on the re-release version.
  5. "Fear and Loathing in Leramie" – 3:28
  6. "Led Astray" – 4:33
  7. "I Am Dmitri Karamazov and the World is My Father" – 3:35
