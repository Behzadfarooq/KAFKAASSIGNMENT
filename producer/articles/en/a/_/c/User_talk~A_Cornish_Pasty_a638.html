[edit] License tagging for Image:Truro45 river.jpg

Thanks for uploading Image:Truro45 river.jpg. Wikipedia gets thousands of images uploaded every day, and in order to verify that the images can be legally used on Wikipedia, the source and copyright status must be indicated. Images need to have an image tag applied to the image description page indicating the copyright status of the image. This uniform and easy-to-understand method of indicating the license status allows potential re-users of the images to know what they are allowed to do with the images.

For more information on using images, see the following pages:

  • Wikipedia:Image use policy
  • Wikipedia:Image copyright tags

This is an automated notice by OrphanBot. If you need help on selecting a tag to use, or in adding the tag to the image description, feel free to post a message at Wikipedia:Media copyright questions. 19:09, 26 September 2006 (UTC)

[edit] Please don't use the St Mary's image

Can 'A Cornish Pasty' tell me if he has taken this particular image from my website at Notes relating to the Dominican Friary and St Mary's Church, Truro as I'm fairly certain that it is due to the mild foxing top right of the image and to the fact that the image was uploaded to Wikipedia at 17:53 on Feb 3rd, and the page on my website was accessed at 17:47 on Feb 3rd by IP 86.147.175.# (I won't give the full IP). This could easily be verified by checking the coding of the actual image as no two scans will be exactly the same. If so then I would request that you delete the image from Wikipedia as I hold the copyright of this particular scan of this particular engraving. The fact that the engraving itself is historical and out of copyright does not mean that any images of that engraving are also out of copyright. Copyright exists on reproductions as it does for books etc featuring reproductions of historical images. I realise that the image isn't used anywhere yet other than on A Cornish Pasty's sandbox and so have not deleted the image. It would have been common courtesy to at least ask whether or not it would be okay to use the image. I put a hell of a lot of unpaid hard work into my website and make the information freely available, and have had to pay good money for the material used on the site, and I am not prepared for my copyright material to be used elsewhere without permission.Cornovia 01:32, 4 February 2007 (UTC)

I will delete it, sorry for the inconvenience A Cornish Pasty 12:30, 4 February 2007 (UTC)
