A Christmas Gift for You from Phil Spector is an album of Christmas songs; a collaboration between many pop/R&B artists that had records produced by Phil Spector. In 2003, the album was ranked number 142 on Rolling Stone magazine's list of the 500 greatest albums of all time.

The album was a relative failure at the time of its release- coming the day of the assassination of President Kennedy. Radio stations throughout the US did not play tracks from the album during the period of mourning.

The Album has been released three times on three different labels, the original 1960s release on Philles , an early 70s release on The Beatles' Apple Records Label (Probably as a personal favor to Spector. Spector had produced several solo Beatle projects and remixed the " Let It Be " LP ),and finally, the current remastered CD release on ABKCO.

Brian Wilson of the Beach Boys has cited this album as his favorite Christmas album of all time.[1]
