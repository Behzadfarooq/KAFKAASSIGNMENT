Knuffle Bunny: A Cautionary Tale is a children's picture book by Mo Willems. Released by Hyperion Books in 2004, an animated version of the book won a Carnegie Medal in 2007. Later in 2007, a sequel, Knuffle Bunny Too: A Case of Mistaken Identity will be published.
