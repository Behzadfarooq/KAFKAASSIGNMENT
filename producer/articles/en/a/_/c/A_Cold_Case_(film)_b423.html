A Cold Case is a thriller starring Academy Award-winning actor Tom Hanks playing the role of investigator Andy Rosenzweig.

Rosenzweig, on the prospect of near-retirement, vows to solve one more crime - the 27-year-old case of the death of his best friend.

The film is scheduled to be directed by Mark Romanek, adapted from the novel by Philip Gourevitch and written by Eric Roth.

This is based on a true story, Andy Rosensweig was a real New York City police cop and later chief investigator for the New York state attorney's office.

Currently being filmed, it is still to be given a release date sometime for 2008.
