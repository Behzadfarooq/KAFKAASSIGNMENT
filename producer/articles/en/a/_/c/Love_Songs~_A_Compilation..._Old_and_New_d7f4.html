Love Songs: A Compilation... Old and New is a compilation composed of all of Phil Collins' greatest love songs during his career, including several new tracks not previously released.

[edit] Track listing

  1. "Tearing and Breaking"
  2. "Do You Remember?"
  3. "One More Night"
  4. "Against All Odds (Take a Look at Me Now)"
  5. "Can't Turn Back the Years"
  6. "Groovy Kind of Love"
  7. "Everyday"
  8. "Don't Let Him Steal Your Heart Away"
  9. "Please Come Out Tonight"
  10. "This Must Be Love"
  11. "It's in Your Eyes"
  12. "Can't Stop Loving You"
  • CD 2
  1. Testify
  2. True Colours (Rehearsal)
  3. You'll Be in My Heart
  4. If Leaving Me Is Easy
  5. I've Been Trying*
  6. I've Forgotten Everything
  7. Somewhere*
  8. Least You Can Do
  9. Two Hearts
  10. Separate Lives (Live)
  11. My Girl (Live)
  12. Always (Live)*
  13. The Way You Look Tonight (Live)

(*= Previously Unreleased)
