A Cool Breeze on the Underground is an upcoming drama/thriller film to be released in 2008 by New Line Cinema. The screenplay was written by J. Mackye Gruber, based on a novel by Don Winslow. It is being directed by Eric Bress and J. Mackye Gruber, and stars Nick Stahl as Neal Carey.
