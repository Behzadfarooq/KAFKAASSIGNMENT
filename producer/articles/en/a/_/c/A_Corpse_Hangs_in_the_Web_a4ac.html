A Corpse Hangs in the Web (Original German Title: Ein Toter hing im Netz, released in the United States as Horrors of Spider Island) is a 1960 horror film directed by Fritz Böttger. The film is about giant spiders terrorizing plane crash survivors that consist of a talent agent and seven gorgeous girls. The English version was featured in the last season of Mystery Science Theater 3000.

The movie begins with a scene in the talent office in New York City, where girls try out by dancing for the man sitting behind the desk. Some are chosen (including one who strips in the office), and others rejected before they start their journey to Singapore with Gary, the talent agent. While flying over the Pacific Ocean, they are involved in a horrific plane crash that involves the plane splitting in half and falling into the sea while burning up. Miraculously, all but the pilot survive aboard a life raft. After five days at sea with no food and little water, they spot land. Gary literally drags the helpless females onto the beach. He discovers a waterfall, but only lets them drink out of it for a minute before taking them into the island to search for signs of life. On the way, they discover "a hammer, with a long handle...probably for excavating some metal, most likely uranium!" Soon after they find a cabin, and inside (gasp!) there is a man hanging from (really standing behind) a gigantic spider web. By the next scene the man disappears and Gary and the girls move into the cabin. Next there are a few scenes involving most of the girls putting some type of move on Gary, to which he responds, "The heat, I can't think straight anymore!" He then proceeds out onto the island where he is bitten by the giant spider, turning himself into some type of spider-man beast. Note: The spider has opposable thumbs. The girls hear gunshots (clearly) from the cabin, but can't find Gary. The next day he kills one of the girls, and she screams (It can be heard plainly.). They begin to get scared, and two have a lengthy wrestling match in the cabin while wearing little clothing. Soon a couple men show up on the island in a small rowboat (who knows how they got to the deserted island in the middle of the Pacific with just a rowboat?) to help the professor with research. Even though the professor is dead, they are not disappointed, as they have their choice of 6 scantily clad, desperate girls. Joe cannot make up his mind, and tries his luck (and is successful) with most of them. Bob has his sights set on Ann, a girl from Minnesota, who hadn't left home before this trip. One thing leads to another, Joe and one of the girls are killed by Gary, and they finally figure out that "the only thing that will destroy him is fire!" This is basically the end of the movie, as Gary is chased into quicksand, sinks, and dies. A ship comes the next day to save the rest of the living.
