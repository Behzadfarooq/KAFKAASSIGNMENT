A Classic Case (1985) is Jethro Tull's only orchestral album, featuring the London Symphony Orchestra conducted by David Palmer. This is also Tull's only entirely instrumental album.

[edit] Notes

  • Recorded during the summer of 1984 at the CBS Studios in London.
  • Released 31 December 1985 in the US, where it reached #93 in the charts.
