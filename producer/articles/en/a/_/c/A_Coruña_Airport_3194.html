A Coruña Airport (IATA: LCG, ICAO: LECO), also known as La Coruña Airport or Alvedro Airport, is the airport serving the Galician city of A Coruña (formerly known as La Coruña) in northwestern Spain.

[edit] Airlines and destinations

  • Clickair (Barcelona, London Heathrow, Seville)
  • easyJet (Madrid)
  • Iberia (Barcelona, Madrid, Paris-Orly)
    • Iberia operated by Air Nostrum (Bilbao)
  • Portugalia (Lisbon)
  • Spanair (Barcelona, Las Palmas, Madrid, Palma Mallorca, Tenerife-Sur)
