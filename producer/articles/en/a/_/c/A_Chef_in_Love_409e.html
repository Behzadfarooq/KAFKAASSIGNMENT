A Chef in Love, შეყვარებული კულინარის 1001 რეცეპტი in Georgian (Shekvarebuli kulinaris ataserti retsepti) is a 1996 Georgian film directed by Nana Dzhordzhadze. It stars Pierre Richard and Nino Kirtadze.

[edit] Synopsis

It is about a French opera singer and chef living in Georgia who opens a restaurant.

[edit] Awards

It was nominated for an Academy Award for Best Foreign Language Film.
