A Challenge of Honour is the Dutch music project of Peter Savelkoul. It is usually classified as martial, industrial, ambient, neofolk and neoclassical.

[edit] Albums

  • Only Stones Remain (2001)
  • The Right Place (2001)
  • Wilhelm Gustloff (2002)
  • Aurore de Gloire (2003)
  • Monuments (2004)
  • Seven Samurai (2005)
