The Gatalog: A Collection Of Chaos: is the massive debut album of underground rapper Celph Titled. It contains many previously heard tracks, inluding Mixtape tracks from previous Mixtapes of his, and other artists'. It also features the full, Extended Mix of Celph's track The Battle, which was featured on Fort Minor's debut album, The Rising Tied, along with the album version of the track Cover And Duck, which was featured on Fort Minor's debut Mixtape, Fort Minor: We Major. This massive debut totals in 75 tracks, although most have been previously released on other LP's including Apathy's "Eastern Philosophy."
