A Chorus Lie is the sixteenth episode of season four of the television situation comedy Will & Grace.

[edit] Plot

Jack gets an audition for the Manhattan Gay Men's Chorus, but on arrival he discovers he has competition in very attractive, tight-shirt wearing Owen. Just as his hopes seem lost, Jack discovers that Owen is actually straight, and only pretending to be gay. Utilising the help of Grace - who fears she has lost her touch with men - Jack sets about trying to 'in' Owen.

Elsewhere, Will accompanies Karen - whose husband is in jail - on her annual Valentine's Day party. While there - to protect her reputation, and beat her rival Beverley Leslie - Karen spreads the word that Will is a gigolo she has hired.
