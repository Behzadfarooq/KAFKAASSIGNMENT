A Carefully Planned Accident is the first full-length album by Israeli/American gypsy/rock band Nanuchka. It was released on June 21, 2006.

  5. "The Promise" (Yula) – 4:11
  6. "Seven" (Nanuchka, Assaf Spector) – 4:40
  7. "Captain Sensible Calling" (Yula, Jack T. Cloth) – 3:59
