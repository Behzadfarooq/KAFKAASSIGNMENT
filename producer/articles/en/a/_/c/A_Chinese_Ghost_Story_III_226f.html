A Chinese Ghost Story III (Sinnui Yauwan III: Do Do Do) is a 1991 Hong Kong comedy horror romance film produced by Tsui Hark and directed by Ching Siu-Tung. It is the sequel to A Chinese Ghost Story and A Chinese Ghost Story II.

[edit] Cast

  • Tony Leung Chiu Wai as Fong
  • Joey Wang as Lotus
  • Jacky Cheung as Yin
  • Nina Li Chi as Butterfly
  • Lau Siu-Ming as Tree Devil
  • Lau Shun as Fong's Master
  • Lau Yuk Ting as Jade
