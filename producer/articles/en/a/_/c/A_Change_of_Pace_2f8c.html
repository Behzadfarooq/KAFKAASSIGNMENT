A Change of Pace is a five piece rock band from Peoria, Arizona. The band was on both the 2005 and 2006 Warped Tours.

[edit] Bio

A Change of Pace formed in 2001 when singer Torry Jasper and drummer Jonathan Kelly began playing together after meeting in a high school English class. In 2003, after a few years of jamming and playing small local shows, bassist Johnny Abdullah and guitarist Adam Rodgers joined the band. From there they put some self-produced recordings on MP3.com where they were discovered by manager Jorge Hernandez. Within a few months they had a deal with Immortal Records and released their EP Change Is The Only Constant. In 2005 the band released their first full-length, An Offer You Can't Refuse and landed a spot on the Vans Warped Tour. Just a year after their first album, on August 15th A Change of Pace released their sophomore record, Prepare The Masses. The band also added a fifth member to the band, Dan Parker (previously in Don't Let Go).

[edit] Band members

  • Torry Jasper: lead vocals
  • Jonathan Kelly: drums
  • Johnny Abdullah: bass, vocals
  • Adam Rodgers: lead guitar
  • Dan Parker: guitar, vocals
