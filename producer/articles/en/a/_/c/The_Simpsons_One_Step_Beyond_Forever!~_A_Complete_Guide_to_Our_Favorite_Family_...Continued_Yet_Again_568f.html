The Simpsons One Step Beyond Forever!: A Complete Guide to Our Favorite Family ...Continued Yet Again is the third sequel to the Simpsons episode guide The Simpsons: A Complete Guide to Our Favorite Family. The book covers seasons 13 and 14.

[edit] Content

This book has an episode-by-episode guide to seasons 13 and 14. Each season starts with production art like Buck McCoys Mansion and Mount Carlmore. The book starts with "Treehouse of Horror XII" and ends with "Moe Baby Blues". Each episode guide has quotes, "stuff you may haved missed in each episode" and references to other films and television. Each episode includes a special character starting with Judge Constance Harm and ends with Dan Castellaneta in "Moe Baby Blues". Special guests that are included are Jane Kaczmarek, Julia Louis-Dreyfus, Delroy Lindo, Ben Stiller, Reese Witherspoon, Dennis Weaver, Olympia Dukakis, Marisa Tomei, Eric Idle, Scott Thompson and Jonathan Taylor Thomas. Also this book includes Character Designs, Couch Gag, Doh's!, Mmmmm's, Itchy & Scratchy, Filmography, "Who does that voice", songs sung by the Simpsons and the Church Marquees.
