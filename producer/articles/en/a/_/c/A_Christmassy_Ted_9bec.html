"A Christmassy Ted" is an episode of the Channel 4 sitcom Father Ted. It is the 17th episode overall and was broadcast between the second and third series.

[edit] Synopsis

Spoiler warning: Plot and/or ending details follow.

After helping seven priests who accidentally wandered into the largest lingerie store in all of Ireland escape from a possibly scandalous fate, Father Ted receives a top Catholic award known as the Golden Cleric. After receiving the award, a priest, who refers to himself as Todd Unctious, claims to be an old friend of Ted's and pays a visit to the Parochial House. He stays overnight and makes an attempt to steal Ted's prized trophy. He is ultimately foiled by Ted, who wakes up in the middle of the night to gloat over his trophy for an invisible audience, catching Todd in the act.

[edit] Trivia

  • This episode is a full hour long, as opposed to the usual half-hour length of most Father Ted episodes. It was aired on Christmas Eve, seven months after the second series had ended.
  • Irish comedian Ed Byrne makes an appearance as one of the rude pranksters who interrupt Ted's phone call to the hotline.
  • The Lingerie section scenes were filmed in a Dunnes department store, Ennis, Co.Clare.[1]
