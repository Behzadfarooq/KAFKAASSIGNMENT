"A Different Kind of Love Song" is the second North American single release from American singer/actress Cher's thirty-third album Living Proof.

[edit] Song information

At the time of the release of this single Cher was on her Living Proof/Farewell tour, which prevented her from promoting the single. The song on its own did manage to enter a couple of Billboard charts, ultimately its only success were in the clubs and in dance single sales. Like Song for the Lonely, A Different Kind of Love Song was not released in Europe due to complications with Cher's record company.

[edit] Music video and promotion

Cher promoting "A Different Kind of Love Song" in Will & Grace.

No music video was made for this song however the song was promoted on the comedy series, Will & Grace. In 2002 Cher made a brief appearance on the comedy show and guest starred as God. During the show Cher appears in the character Jack's dream and suggests to him why he should remain an entertainer. Cher sings the chorus of the song while semi-naked men dressed as angels dance next to her.
