A Dump of My Own

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

"A Dump of My Own " is the 5th episode of season three of the television sitcom Married... with Children.

  • First Aired: Sunday January 8, 1989 on FOX.
Spoiler warning: Plot and/or ending details follow.

[edit] Plot

Al plans on building his dream - an executive's type of bathroom in the garage.

[edit] Guest stars

None
