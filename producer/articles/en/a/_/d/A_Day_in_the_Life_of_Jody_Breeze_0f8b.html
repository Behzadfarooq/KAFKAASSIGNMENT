A Day in the Life of Jody Breeze is the debut album from Jody Breeze the member from the rap group Boyz N Da Hood. He is currently still working on his album with Sho'nuff.

Guests will include Jazze Pha, Juvenile, Ludacris, Trick Daddy, Sean Paul, Pastor Troy, Eightball, Paul Wall, Mike Jones, Slim Thug and more. It will feature production from Jazze Pha, Lil Jon & Mannie Fresh. The first single is "Fast Foward" featuring Freeway.
