[edit] Music

I like almost every type of music, although my favorite genres are rock, alternative, world, new age, r&b, and soundtracks. Green Day, Jewel, Audioslave, Joss Stone, Alicia Keys, O-Zone, Kitaro, Enya, Loreena McKennitt, and Cirque du Soleil are a few of my favorite artist. And of course, you're not legally allowed to live in New Jersey if you don't like Bruce Springsteen.


[edit] Books & Authors

I have several different favorite authors, among them are: Ray Bradbury, William Shakespeare, Mercedes Lackey, J.R.R. Tolkien, Kurt Vonnegut (Slaughterhouse Five was such a weird book, I loved it.), Edgar Allan Poe, F. Scott Fitzgerald, Zora Neale Hurston, and Philip Pullman.


[edit] TV & Movies

I usually try not to watch a lot of television, but it always seems to fail. The shows I can't miss are Gilmore Girls, The Amazing Race, and Sex in the City, preferably as they originally aired on HBO.
