A Decade Under the Influence EP was released by Taking Back Sunday as a single/EP in 2004 by the Chicago-based Victory Records. It was available for $2.97 on itunes on January 17th, 2006. Tracks 2 and 3 were recorded with Long Island producer and friend, Mike Sapone, who has also recorded for Brand New, Crime in Stereo, and Straylight Run.
