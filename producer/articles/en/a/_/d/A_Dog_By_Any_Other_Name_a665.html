"A Dog By Any Other Name" is an episode of That's So Raven.

[edit] Plot

Raven and Chelsea (along with Eddie) are invited to a popular classmate named Amber's party but Amber thinks that they'll come to her party together because they can't get dates. Raven tries to prove her wrong. Raven tries to get a date with a guy who doesn't even know Raven. Raven then has a vision of Chelsea meeting a new guy. Then her vision comes true. However, the problem is that the boy, Sam, is the same name of Chelsea's dog. Chelsea needs a date to Amber's party but she has a hard time putting the two apart so Raven and Chelsea try to give Sam a nickname Chelsea can call him. However, Sam isn't interested in Chelsea as it seems (according to one of Raven's visions). Meanwhile, Eddie must deal with sitting in the front of Mr. Lawler, a teacher who spits every time he talks.

[edit] Visions

  • Raven, Chelsea, and Eddie screaming because of the "spit" seat.
  • Chelsea making friends with a guy named Sam.
  • Chelsea loving her dog Sam.
  • Sam saying Chelsea is not cute.

[edit] Trivia

  • Mike Erwin guest stars as Sam
  • Brenda Song guests stars as the snotty classmate Amber, who is throwing a party. Song has also starred in fellow Disney Channel hit sitcoms Phil Of The Future and The Suite Life of Zack and Cody. Her character in this episode is very similar to hers on TSL.
  • Fifth appearance of Mr. Lawler
  • The name could be from Romeo and Juliet, where Juliet says, "A Rose by any other name"
  • T'Keyah Crystal Keymáh does not appear in this episode.


[edit] Goofs

  • Raven never actually lives a few of her visions, she just see's them in her vision.
