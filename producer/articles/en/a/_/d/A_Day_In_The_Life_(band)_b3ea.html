A Day In The Life was an emo band formed out of Dayton, Ohio. Before changing their name and lineup to the succesful Hawthorne Heights, they released four albums. One of them, Nine Reasons To Say Goodbye, which was later re-issued by Hawthorne Heights' record label, Victory Records, in July, 2006. J.T. Woodruff was the only member to continue in Hawthorne Heights.
