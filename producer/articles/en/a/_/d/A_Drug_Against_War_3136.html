A Drug Against War is a single from KMFDM's 1993 album, Angst. It contains an alternate version of "Blood", with a stronger beat and sans rhythm guitar, as well as two remixes of "A Drug Against War", the "Overdose Mix" being a bit more manic and longer than the original, and the "Hookah Mix" having a significantly slower beat.

[edit] Track listing

  1. "A Drug Against War" – 3:43
  2. "A Drug Against War (Overdose Mix)" – 5:28
  3. "A Drug Against War (Hookah Mix)" – 3:37
  4. "Blood" – 4:01
