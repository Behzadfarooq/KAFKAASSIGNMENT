A Day at the Beach was a film made in 1970, and set in a rundown British seaside resort. It was written by Roman Polański, who was originally intended to be the director, although most of the direction was finally done by Simon Hesera. It starred Mark Burns and Beatie Edney. Peter Sellers makes a cameo appearance.

The film was never released in theatres at the time of its creation, but has seen limited runs at film festivals since that time. A small clip of the film appears in "The Unknown Peter Sellers."
