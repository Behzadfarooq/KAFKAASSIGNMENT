[edit] Racism

As for your so-called racist remarks made towards me, your constant edits concerning Zodiac signs had nothing to do with race. It has been discussed time and time again that categorizing article by Zodiac signs was highly frowned upon. For one, if such were allowed, every person would be in 1 of 12 categories, leaving thousands of articles to be grouped into one category. Second, it is entirely un-encyclopedic. I have yet to see one respected encyclopedia that mentions a person's Zodiac sign -- unless that person was harmed or any event happened was specific to their Zodiac sign. Also, your removing of a category for deletion template is not allowed. It is there for a discussion whether or not the category will stand. It is a highly unanimous decision that it will be deleted when voting is over. You can make your point here if you wish to do so. --Zimbabweed 23:03, 31 October 2006 (UTC)
