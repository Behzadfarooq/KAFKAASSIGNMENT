A Date with Elvis is a 1959 release by Elvis Presley. Much like the earlier release, For LP Fans Only, A Date With Elvis was put out by RCA during Elvis' army tenure, and contains a mix of early Sun recordings and RCA soundtrack cuts.

[edit] Track listing

  1. "Blue Moon Of Kentucky" (Bill Monroe) - 2:05 Sun recording
  2. "Young And Beautiful" (Abner Silver, Aaron Schroeder) - 2:07
  3. "(You're So Square) Baby I Don't Care" (Jerry Leiber, Mike Stoller) - 1:55
  4. "Milkcow Blues Boogie" (Kokomo Arnold) - 2:38 Sun recording
  5. "Baby Let's Play House" (Arthur Gunter) - 2:18 Sun recording
  6. "Good Rockin' Tonight" (Roy Brown) - 2:12 Sun recording
  7. "Is It So Strange" (Faron Young) - 2:32
  8. "We're Gonna Move" (Vera Matson, Elvis Presley) - 2:31
  9. "I Want To Be Free" (Jerry Leiber, Mike Stoller) - 2:16
  10. "I Forgot To Remember To Forget" (Stan Kesler, Charlie Feathers) - 2:27 Sun recording
