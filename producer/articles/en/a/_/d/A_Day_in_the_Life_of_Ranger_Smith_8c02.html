A Day in the Life of Ranger Smith is a cartoon featuring Ranger Smith from Yogi Bear. It was made by Ren and Stimpy creator John Kricfalusi and his company Spümcø for Cartoon Network. The cartoon's original air date (along with the short Boo Boo Runs Wild) was on the evening of September 24, 1999.

[edit] Cast

  • Ranger John Smith: Corey Burton
  • Yogi Bear: Greg Burson
  • Boo-Boo Bear: John Kricfalusi

[edit] Crew

  • Directed by John Kricfalusi
  • Story Sketches by Vincent Waller
  • Layout by Ben Jones, Erik Wiese and Ed Benedict
  • Clean-Up artist: Gabe Swarr
