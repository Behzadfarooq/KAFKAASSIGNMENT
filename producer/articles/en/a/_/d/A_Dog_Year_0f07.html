A Dog Year is a movie directed by first time director George LaVoo starring Jeff Bridges. The film should be released in late 2007.[1]

The film is based on the memoir by Jon Katz and adapted by LaVoo, the story centers on a man having a midlife crisis whose life is turned upside down when he takes in a border collie crazier than he is.[2]
