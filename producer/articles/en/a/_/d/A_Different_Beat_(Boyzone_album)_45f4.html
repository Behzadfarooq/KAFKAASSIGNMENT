A Different Beat is the second album released by Irish boy band Boyzone. It includes their 1994 hit Love Me for a Reason which reached #2 on the UK charts, Words (a Bee Gees cover, reaching #1 UK), A Different Beat (#1 UK) and Isn't It a Wonder (#2 UK). It also includes a cover of the Michael Jackson song Ben.
