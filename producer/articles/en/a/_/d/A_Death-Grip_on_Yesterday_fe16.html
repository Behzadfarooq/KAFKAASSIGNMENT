A Death-Grip on Yesterday is the third studio album from the metalcore quintet Atreyu. It was released on March 28, 2006 through Victory Records.

It was leaked onto P2P and BitTorrent sites on February 24, over a month before its official release.

A week after its release, it had sold more than 69,000 copies and started out at #9 on the Billboard 200 and it has gone on to sell more than 222,000 copies.

[edit] Trivia

  • The song "Her Portrait in Black" is a B-side of this album and is on the Underworld: Evolution soundtrack.
  • The song "Ex's and Oh's" was the first single released from the album, and is featured in EA Sports' Madden NFL 07 video game.
  • "Shameful" was played during the TNA 2006 recap at the end of the December 28, 2006 edition of TNA iMPACT!.
  • An instrumental version of the album, as well as The Curse was released on iTunes on January 9, 2007.
