A Daughter's a Daughter (published in 1952) is a novel written by Agatha Christie. It was the fifth of six novels Christie wrote under the nom-de-plume Mary Westmacott.

Initially a play written by Christie in the 1930s, the plot tells of a daughter's opposition to her mother's plan to remarry.
