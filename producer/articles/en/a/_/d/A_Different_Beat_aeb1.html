A Different Beat is a 1999 album by Gary Moore.

All music and lyrics by Gary Moore except track 4, by Jimi Hendrix.

[edit] Track listing

  1. "Go on Home" – 4:21
  2. "Lost in Your Love" – 5:59
  3. "Worry No More" – 5:07
  4. "Fire" – 2:50 (Jimi Hendrix cover)
  5. "Surrender" – 9:38
  6. "House Full of Blues" – 4:49
  7. "Bring My Baby Back" – 4:50
  8. "Can't Help Myself" – 5:52
  9. "Fat Boy" – 3:27
  10. "We Want Love" – 5:43
  11. "Can't Help Myself" – 12:18
