"A Dios le Pido" (English: I ask God) officially is the lead single from the Spanish studio album Un Día Normal by the latin music singer and songwriter Juanes, released in 2002 in Spain and Latin America. In 2006, the song was re-released in some countries in Europe, right after the success of "La Camisa Negra", which charted in almost every European country in the top five.
