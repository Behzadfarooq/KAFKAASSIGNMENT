A Day in the Life of a Poolshark was the second single from Idiot Pilot's debut album Strange We Should Meet Here. The single was released on January 10, 2006 (in the UK only during their UK tour) and came in two formats, CD and 10" Limited Edition vinyl. There are two additional tracks with exclusive remix from Chino Moreno. Two B-sides are also featured on the album, "Deep Blue" (CD Version Only) and "The Spartan".

There was also a promo released for "A Day in the Life of a Poolshark". This promo CD came in a Black Card sleve and featured three tracks: "A Day in the Life of a Poolshark", and exclusive remix from Chino Moreno, and "Spark Plug".

[edit] Music Video

Poolshark Video

The video for A Day in the Life of a Poolshark was imagined by Daniel Anderson while sitting in a hotel room. The video features the two members of the band members arguing (over what is not known) in a back alley. The argument soon turns into a fight which is the basis of the video. The video was filmed in downtown Los Angeles, directed by Shafei & Levitz. Since its debut, the video has been featured on MTV2 Banned in the UK.
