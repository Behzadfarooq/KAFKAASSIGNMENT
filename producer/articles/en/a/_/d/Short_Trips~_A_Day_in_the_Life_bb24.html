Short Trips: A Day in the Life is a Big Finish original anthology edited by Ian Farrington and based on the long-running British science fiction television series Doctor Who. The collection's theme is the hours of the day. Like all Doctor Who spin-off media, its canonicity in relation to the television series is unclear.
