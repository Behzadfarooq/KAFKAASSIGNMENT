A Dope Trailer Is No Place for a Kitty is the 10th episode of the hit Canadian comedy mockumentary series Trailer Park Boys.


[edit] Quotes

Bubbles: Boys, watch the carts!
Ricky: Bubbles, this guy's no good. He's lazy, and sleeping around and he's not protecting the plants and weed.
Bubbles: Well a dope trailer's no place for a kitty that's why.
