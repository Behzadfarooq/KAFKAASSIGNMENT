"A Neverending Dream" is an Dance cover performed by German group Cascada. This song appears on their debut album, Everytime We Touch (2006). It was the fifth single released in Germany by Cascada in 2006. This song was originally released by X-Perience in 1996. It appears in their album, Magic Fields. The single was released simultaneously with the album in Germany. The single was not as big as "Everytime We Touch" or "How Do You Do" but managed to climb to #11 on German's official singles charts.
