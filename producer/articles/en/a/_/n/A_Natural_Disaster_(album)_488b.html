A Natural Disaster is an album by the British rock band Anathema. It was released on November the 3rd [1] 2003 in UK and on February the 24th [2]2004 through Music For Nations.

[edit] Track listing

  1. "Harmonium" – 5:28
  2. "Balance" – 3:58
  3. "Closer" – 6:20
  4. "Are You There?" – 4:59
  5. "Childhood Dream" – 2:10
  6. "Pulled Under at 2000 Meters per Second" – 5:23
  7. "A Natural Disaster" – 6:27
  8. "Flying" – 5:57
  9. "Electricity" – 3:51
  10. "Violence" – 10:45

[edit] Notes

The verse of the song "Pulled Under at 2000 Metres per Second" is very similar to the verse of Pink Floyd's "Sheep" of Animals.
