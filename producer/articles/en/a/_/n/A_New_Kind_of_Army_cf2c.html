A New Kind of Army is a punk rock CD released by the band Anti-Flag. It was released in 1999. A New Kind of Army is easily one of Anti-Flag's most varied albums. From the childish fun of "Captain Anarchy" to the ska-like vibe of "That's Youth" to the slowed-down protest song "The Consumer's Song" to the satiric "This is NOT a Crass Song".
