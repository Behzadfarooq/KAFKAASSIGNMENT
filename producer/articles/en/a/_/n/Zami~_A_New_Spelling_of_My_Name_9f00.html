Zami: A New Spelling of My Name is an autobiography by African American poet Audre Lorde. It started a new genre that the author calls biomythography.

The book is a very personal inside look at Lorde's life, beginning as a child in Harlem.

Zami is a carriacou name for women who work together as friends and lovers.
