[edit] Removal of performance dates

Janet13 04:18, 25 November 2006 (UTC)

  • I must agree with Janet13, the table is huge and overly repetitive and makes the page excessively long. As a compromise to deleting/restoring the table, the venue, city and country information can be consolidated at the top of the tour section (because this info will never change since Celine is always at Caesers Palace). For example, the top would say:

All shows were perfomed in Las Vegas Nevada in the United States at Caesers Palace.

  • Then the dates of the shows can be listed by being grouped by year (5 groups, 2003-2007 show dates). Therefore, the table will be much more compact and will not look so god damn repetitive.

The text clearly states where and for how long the show is on, plus the link to the official website at the bottom should give all the specific details to those interested attending it. I have removed the list of dates because they (along with quite a lot of the rest of the text actually) are mere promotional material. TVarsa 01:57, 18 December 2006 (UTC)
