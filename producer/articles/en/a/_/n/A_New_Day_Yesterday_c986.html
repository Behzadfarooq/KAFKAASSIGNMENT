A New Day Yesterday

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

25th Anniversary Video
Compilation video by Jethro Tull
Released 2003

A New Day Yesterday (2003) is a stereo DVD remastering of 25th Anniversary Video by Jethro Tull.

also known as Jethro Tull: A New Day Yesterday - 25th Anniversary Collection, 1969-1994.
also known as Jethro Tull - A New Day Yesterday: The 25th Anniversary Collection, 1969-1994.
also known as A New Day Yesterday: 1969-1994 - The 25th Anniversary Collection.
