A Night Out is a 1915 Charlie Chaplin comedy short. It was Edna Purviance's first film with Chaplin. It was also Chaplin's first film with Essanay Film Company in Niles, California. Chaplin's first Essanay film, His New Job, was made in the Chicago studio, but he moved to Niles Studios after that. He found Miss Purviance in San Francisco when he was searching for leading lady for his films. A Night Out also stars Ben Turpin, Leo White and Bud Jamison.
