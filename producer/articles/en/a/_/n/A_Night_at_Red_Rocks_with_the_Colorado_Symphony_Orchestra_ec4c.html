A Night at Red Rocks with the Colorado Symphony Orchestra is an album by The Moody Blues, recorded from a live performance at the Red Rocks Amphitheatre with the Colorado Symphony Orchestra on September 9, 1992 and released in 1993 on Polydor Records (see 1993 in music).
