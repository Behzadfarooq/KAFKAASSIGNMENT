A New Low is a series of underground movies, music and podcasts produced in Horseheads, New York by Eric Thomas Craven. Two movies have been released to date, A New Low and A New Low 2. There is also a video podcast of the same name and a weekly audio podcast, Pirate vs Wolfman on Planet X and various novelty bands including Jefferson Foxfire, September Morn, Murdermore and Reckon For a Dream.

The cast and crew includes Eric Thomas Craven, John Holden, Taem Jones, Wil Smith, Cal Biddle, Tony Shaddock, Yetti, Seth McPizza, London McGrane and others in various stunts, skits, skateboarding, animation and pranks.
