A Northern Chorus is a Canadian band from Hamilton, Ontario known for their mellow tunes. They have released three records on the Sonic Unyon label, Before We All Go To Pieces, Spirit Flags and Bitter Hands Resign. Before We All Go To Pieces was originally released by Black Mountain Music.
