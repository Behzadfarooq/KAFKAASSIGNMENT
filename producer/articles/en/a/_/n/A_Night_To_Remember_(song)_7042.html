A Night To Remember was the fourth single off of the album of the same name by Cyndi Lauper.

Lauper re-recorded the song for a promo released in the U.S. The arrangement is a little different. The is considered a flop and the single only made a dent on the Australian Chart.

A music video was made of the song. It featured shots of Lauper on stage performing the song with her band as well as shots of Lauper on the beach.

The song is about a break-up where Lauper reminisces about the night of a painful break-up.
