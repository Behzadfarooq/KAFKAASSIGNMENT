Armageddon, or Armageddon: A Novel of Berlin is a novel by Leon Uris about post-World War II Berlin and Germany. The novel starts in London during the war, and goes through the Russian occupation of Berlin to the eventual Berlin Airlift and finishes with the end of the airlift.
