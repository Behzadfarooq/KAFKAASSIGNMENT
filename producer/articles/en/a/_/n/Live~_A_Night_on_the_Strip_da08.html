Live: A Night on the Strip is a live album by L.A. Guns. The performance occurred October 7th, 1999 at the Key Club, located on Sunset Boulevard in Hollywood.

  5. "Bitch Is Back"
  6. "Time"
  7. "Long Time Dead"
  8. "Over The Edge"
  9. "Never Enough"
  10. "Nothing Better To Do"
  11. "Guitar Solo"
  12. "Electric Gypsy"
  13. "Ballad Of Jayne"
  14. "Rip N Tear"
