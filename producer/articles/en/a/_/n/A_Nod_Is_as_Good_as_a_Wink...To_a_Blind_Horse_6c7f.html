A Nod Is As Good As A Wink... To A Blind Horse was the third album by British rock group Faces, and their second album of 1971. It contains their biggest hit at home or abroad, the rollicking "Stay With Me".

[edit] Track listing

  1. "Miss Judy's Farm" (Stewart-Wood)
  2. "You're So Rude" (Lane-McLagan)
  3. "Love Lives Here" (Lane-Stewart-Wood)
  4. "Last Orders Please" (Lane)
  5. "Stay With Me" (Stewart-Wood)
  6. "Debris" (Lane)
  7. "Memphis, Tennessee" (Chuck Berry)
  8. "Too Bad" (Stewart-Wood)
  9. "That's All You Need" (Stewart-Wood)

[edit] Group members

  • The Faces - producers
  • Rod Stewart - vocals
  • Ronnie Lane - bass, guitar, vocals
  • Ron Wood - guitar, harmonica
  • Ian McLagan - piano, organ
  • Kenny Jones - drums
  • Harry Fowler - steel drums on "That's All You Need"
  • Glyn Johns - co-producer, engineer
