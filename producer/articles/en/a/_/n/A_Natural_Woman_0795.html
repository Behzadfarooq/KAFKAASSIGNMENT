A Natural Woman is a 1969 (see 1969 in music) album by Peggy Lee.

[edit] Track listing

  1. "(All of a Sudden) My Heart Sings" (Harold Rome)
  2. "Don't Explain" (Billie Holiday, Arthur Herzog Jr.)
  3. "Can I Change My Mind?" (Barry Despenza, Carl Wolfolk)
  4. "Lean On Me" (Peggy Lee, Mundell Lowe, Mike Melvoin)
  5. "(Sittin' On) The Dock of the Bay" (Steve Cropper, Otis Redding)
  6. "(You Make Me Feel Like) A Natural Woman" (Jerry Goffin, Carole King, Henry Wexler)
  7. "Everday People" (Sylvester Stewart)
  8. "Please Send Me Someone to Love" (Percy Mayfield)
  9. "Spinning Wheel" (D.C. Thomas)
  10. "Lving Is Dying Without You" (Al Kasha, Joel Hirschhorn)
  11. "I Think It's Gonna Rain Today" (Randy Newman)
