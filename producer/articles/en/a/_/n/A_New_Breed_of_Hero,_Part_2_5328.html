A New Breed of Hero, Part 2 is the 59th episode of Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Monsters GX (Yu-Gi-Oh! GX). It was first broadcast in Japan on November 16, 2005.

[edit] Synopsis

Aster controls time itself as he calls forth his Destiny Heroes, using them to manipulate every aspect of the duel in his favor. Jaden loses control of his own monsters as Aster's Clocktower Prison ticks towards Jaden's defeat, and the Destiny Hero sleeping inside the Tower begins to stir.

[edit] Major events

  • Jaden duels Aster (Aster wins)
  • Jaden loses his third duel
  • It is revealed that Aster's father was kidnapped (murdered in the Japanese version)
  • Jaden loses his ability to see his cards.
Jaden defeated. (Badly with the lost of his cards.)
