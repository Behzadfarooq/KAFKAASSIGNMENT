[edit] Wikipedia:Neutral point of view (Christian articles)

Hi, sorry to be getting back to you late. I had stopped looking at the TfD page where our discussion was going on. Anyway, although I agree with what you've written at Wikipedia:Neutral point of view (Christian articles), I don't think it needs to be formulated so specifically as a Wikipedia policy or guideline. There's nothing there that isn't already covered in WP:NPOV, and having a separate page for NPOV in Christian articles is just instruction creep. I really think the best way to counter bias in Christian articles is simply to edit them in such a way as to remove the bias. If you find an article where the bias is particularly egregious, slap a {{POV}} tag on the top and then discuss your issues on the talk page. You might also want to consider participating in WikiProject Christianity, if you're not already. That would also be the place to bring attention to any problematic articles. Angr/talk 22:41, 20 March 2006 (UTC)
