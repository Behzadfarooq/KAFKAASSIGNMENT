I/S: A Journal of Law and Policy for the Information Society is an interdisciplinary journal of research and commentary concentrating on the intersection of law, policy, and information technology. I/S is the result of a partnership between the Moritz College of Law at The Ohio State University, and the Heinz School of Public Policy and Management at Carnegie Mellon University. I/S was started in 2004, published its first issue in the winter of 2005, and currently has over 2,000 subscribers.

I/S publishes two or three issues per year, both in hard-copy and subscription-based online versions. I/S commits one issue each year to the topics of cybersecurity and privacy, because there are few outlets for published research on these subjects.

Moritz College of Law

Peter Shane, Joseph S. Platt/Porter Wright Morris & Arthur Professor of Law; Director, Center for Interdisciplinary Law and Policy Studies

Peter Swire, Professor of Law, John Glenn Scholar in Public Policy Research

Heinz School of Public Policy and Management

Ronald Gdovic, Executive Director, Institute for the Study of Information Technology and Society (InSITes)

Ashish Arora, Professor of Economics and Public Policy
