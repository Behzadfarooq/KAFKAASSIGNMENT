Chemistry - A European Journal is an international scientific journal that publishes articles on all areas of chemistry and related fields. It is owned and supported by the Editorial Union of Chemical Societies (EUChemSoc) which is an organization of 14 European chemical societies. It is published by Wiley InterScience. It now publishes 36 issues per year. The impact factor of this journal is 4.91 (2005).[1]
