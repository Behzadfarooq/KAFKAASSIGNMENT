A Person of Interest

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

A Person of Interest is an episode of the television show Law & Order: Criminal Intent. The 23rd episode of the second season, it originally aired May 18, 2003.

[edit] Episode trivia

  • The episode title, "A Person of Interest", has two meanings within the episode. One suspect, named by the police as a "person of interest", is a government scientist who works with anthrax and falsely claims 'covert ops' experience. This accused domestic terrorist is based on Steven Hatfill. Additionally, the return of Nicole Wallace is of interest to both the detectives and viewers alike.
