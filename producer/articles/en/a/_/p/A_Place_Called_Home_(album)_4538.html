A Place Called Home is a full-length album by Californian hardcore punk band Ignite. The record was first released in 2000, but in 2001 BMG Records (Germany) re-released the album in Europe. The re-release contains two bonus tracks.

[edit] Track listing

  2. "Veteran"
  3. "Fill In The Blanks"
  4. "Burned Up"
  5. "No Regrets"
  6. "Run"
  7. "Bullets Included No Thought Required"
  8. "A Place Called Home"
  9. "Hands On Stance"
  10. "By My Side"
  11. "In Moderation"
  12. "Pieter"
  13. "Sunday Bloody Sunday" (U2) (only on the 2001 European re-release)
  14. "Old" (only on the 2001 European re-release)
  15. "A Place Called Home" (Hungarian) (*hidden track)
