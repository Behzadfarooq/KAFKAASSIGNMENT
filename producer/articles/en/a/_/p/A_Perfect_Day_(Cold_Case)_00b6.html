[edit] Summary

The remains of a young girl killed in the 1960's puts the squad on the case. Evidence of abuse leads them to initially suspect the girl's mother, though when it's learned the victim had a twin sister, the truth turns out to be much more complicated.

[edit] The Case

[edit] Side Story

Stillman finds Detective Kat Miller smoking outside. He thanks her for her assistance to the squad on an earlier case (in the previous episode "Honor") and offers her a position in the squad. Kat is interested but admits some reservations because of an incident in which she was shot in Fishtown. She thanks him for the offer and tells him she'll get back to him with an answer soon.

[edit] Response

With this episode, Cold Case achieved its most watched episode to date (19.2 million viewers) and also the highest rated episode of the season (4.4/10 in adults 18-49).
