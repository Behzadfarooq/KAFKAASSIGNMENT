Turbo: A Power Rangers Movie (album)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Turbo: A Power Rangers Movie
Soundtrack
Released 1997
Recorded 1996/1997
Genre Rock
Label Polygram Records

Turbo: A Power Rangers Movie was the companion album for the 1997 release of the second Power Rangers film of the same name. It served a dual purpose, as it not only used much of the music heard in the movie, but also contained several tracks from the 1996 season, Power Rangers: Zeo. Many of the songs towards the end of the album are not remotely featured in the movie.
