"Kuwabara: A Promise Between Men" is the third episode of the anime, Yu Yu Hakusho. The Japanese version is called "Kuwabara in a Corner! A Man's Oath."

[edit] Synopsis

The episode starts off with Yusuke as a ghost, and Botan following Keiko because he feels something bad is going to happen to her. At that moment, Keiko screams because she is being assaulted by students from another school. Kuwabara then shows up, saves Keiko and beats up the students. Kuwabara and his friends then get a lecture from Mr. Akashi about fighting. Mr. Akashi then tells them to not fight for a week or else one of Kuwabara's friends will lose his job and starve. They then leave the school in separate ways telling each other to not punch anyone. While Kuwabara is heading home he is confronted by the guys that were attacking Keiko because of Mr. Akashi telling them to attack him. Kuwabara starts to run away but is called a sissy so he immediately turns around and tells them that he's not going to fight back. After being beat up for a while Kuwabara starts to attack but remembers Okubo and stops. The kids then proceed to beat him up before heading off. The next day at school Kuwabara tells his friends what happened and Okubo tells him he should've fought back. Mr. Akashi then walks by and tells them that he's adding to the stipulations. Each one of them must score over a 50 on their next test. Kuwabara's friends aren't worried about it but Kuwabara got a 7 on his last test. Yusuke remarks that Kuwabara's stupider than he is saying that he got a 12.

Kuwabara immediately starts studying even buying a book at a book store before once again getting beat up. The next night Kuwabara continues studying but falls asleep. Yusuke then gives him an all-night study session in his dream. On the way to school the next day three guys start chasing Kuwabara running through two girls causing one to faint. Yusuke then takes control of her body and proceeds to beating up the three guys hitting one in the head with a bag, knocking the next one out with a punch, and kicking one in the balls three times before finishing him off with a punch. Kuwabara then takes the test and tells his friends he looked over the answers and that he scored 53 points. While grading Mr. Akashi is shocked to see that Kuwabara scored 53 points but he and Mr. Iwimoto decide to erase one of Kuwabara's answers bringing his score down to a 48. Kuwabara finds out about this and is ready to punch Mr. Akashi but stops and heads home. The principal, Mr. Takanaka who overheard Mr. Akashi talking with Mr. Iwimoto in the teachers lounge earlier about their plan orders Akashi to change Kuwabara's score back to a 53. Kuwabara's friends then tell Kuwabara what happened and that Okubo can keep his job. While walking back home Kuwabara turns around and thanks Yusuke for what he did for him thus ending the episode.

[edit] Trivia

  • In the manga Yusuke never possesses a girl, he simply barks out a threat and they leave.
  • In the manga there was no fight scene at the beginning.
  • In the manga Mr. Iwimoto wasn't apart of Mr. Akashi's torment of Kuwabara.
