A Perfect Day (Arabic: Yawmon Akhar, يوم اخر) is a 2005 lebanese film directed by Joana Hadjithomas and Khalil Joreige.

[edit] Plot Summary

A 26 years old man, Malek in pursue of his ex-girlfriend Zeina. The mother Claudia is over protective and waiting for over 15 years the kidnapped father during the civil war.

[edit] Credited Cast

Ziad Saad (Malek), Julia Kassar (Claudia -the mother-), Alexandra Kahwagi (Zeina -the girlfriend-)
