A Present For Everyone (2003) is the second album from the British punk-pop band Busted. It was released on November 17, 2003 in the United Kingdom and debuted at No. 2 in the Top 40 Album Charts. It became a triple platinum album with sales above 1 million. Features the singles "Crashed The Wedding", "Who's David?", "Air Hostess", "3AM" and "She Wants To Be Me".
