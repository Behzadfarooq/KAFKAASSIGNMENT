A Picnic That Ends in Bloodshed
Studio album by Muzzin
Released 2005

A Picnic That Ends in Bloodshed is the second album by the Canadian punk band Muzzin, released in 2005, which features the hit song Oh Winnie.

[edit] Personnel

  • Tim Nantais - Vocals, Bass
  • Steve Fox - Lead Guitar, Additional Vocals
  • Eric Jaber - Drums
  • Hardcore Al - Guitar, Synthesiser
