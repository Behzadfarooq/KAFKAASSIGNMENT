A Peaceable Kingdom was a television drama aired by CBS as part of its 1989 Fall lineup.

A Peaceable Kingdom starred Lindsay Wagner as the recently-hired managing director of the Los Angeles County Zoo, who was also recently widowed with three children. Her zoo staffers included Tom Wopat and David Ackroyd.

A Peaceable Kingdom failed to find an appreciable audience and was cancelled less than two months after its premiere.
