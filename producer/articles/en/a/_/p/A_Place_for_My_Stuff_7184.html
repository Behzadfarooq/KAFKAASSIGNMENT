A Place For My Stuff is United States comedian George Carlin's eighth comedy album. Unlike his previous albums, which consisted entirely of stand-up performance, A Place For My Stuff contains a number of studio-recorded tracks, typically poking fun at the format and candor of community radio, commercials, and television.
