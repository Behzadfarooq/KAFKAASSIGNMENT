A List Before Dying is an episode of the U.S. television sitcom Yes, Dear.

[edit] Plot

Spoiler warning: Plot and/or ending details follow.

Jimmy is nearly killed when a piano almost falls on him at the studio. Worried that he will die before he does everything he would like to in life he sets out do just that. Composing a song, dancing, and hosting a talk show, going in a car with a race car driver (Dale Earnhardt Jr), and being attacked by guard dogs are some of the things from his list. When Jimmy finally finishes he feels empty and then lies in bed. But his children come to make a list and want his help so he decides to help them out.
