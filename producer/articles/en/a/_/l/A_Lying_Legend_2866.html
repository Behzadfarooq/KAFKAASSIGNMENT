[edit] Synopsis

Jaden and the other Key Keepers are summoned to the celestial ship of the Pharaoh Abidos III, the 5th Shadow Rider from Ancient Egypt who's never lost a duel. However, when the duel starts and Jaden begins giving him a run for his money, Abidos begins to question the validity of his past victories.
