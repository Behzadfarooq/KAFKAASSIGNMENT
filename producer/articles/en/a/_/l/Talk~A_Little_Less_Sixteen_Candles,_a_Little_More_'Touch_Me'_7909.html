[edit] The Doors' "Touch Me"

Perhaps the origins of this unique title should be noted? I mean, maybe not a lot of young listeners today would be able to identify "Touch Me" as a song by The Doors. As well, Sixteen Candles is an '80s movie. Even better yet would be if someone could find a quote or something about the title.
