A Little Princess (1973 TV serial)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Little Princess (1973) is a television mini-series directed by Derek Martinus, based upon the novel A Little Princess by Frances Hodgson Burnett. This version is noted for being very faithful to the original novel.

It was originally broadcast in six 25-minute episodes on BBC 1, 18 January to 25 March 1973.

[edit] Cast

  • Ruth Dunning - Miss Minchin
  • Deborah Makepeace - Sara Crewe
  • Gaynor Hodgson - Becky
