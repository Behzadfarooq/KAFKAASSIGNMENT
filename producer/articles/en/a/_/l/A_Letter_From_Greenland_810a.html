A Letter From Greenland is an album in 2000 by S.E.S.. This is S.E.S.' fourth Korean album. It sold approximately 630,000 copies. Singles include "Gam Ssa Ahn Eu Myuh" (a Korean cover of MISIA's "Tsutsumi Komu Youni...") and "Be Natural". Bada performed her solo song "I Will..." sometimes, too.
