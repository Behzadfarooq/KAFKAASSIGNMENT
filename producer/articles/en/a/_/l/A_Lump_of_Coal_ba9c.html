A Lump of Coal
Compilation album by Various artists
Released 1991
Genre Rock

A Lump of Coal is a compilation album of Christmas music produced in 1991. While most of the artists from this disc remain unknown to most, the Hoodoo Gurus, Crash Test Dummies, The Primitives, and Henry Rollins all went on to produce well-known albums and many of the other artists are popular amongst rock fans.

[edit] Track listing

  1. Hoodoo Gurus "Little Drummer Boy"
  2. Crash Test Dummies "The First Noel"
  3. The Wedding Present "Step Into Christmas" (originally recorded by Elton John)
  4. Drunken Boat "Blue X-mas (To Whom It May Concern)" (originally recorded by Miles Davis)
  5. Divine Weeks "O Holy Night"
  6. Carnival Art "Bring a Torch Jeannette Isabella"
  7. The Primitives "Silent Night"
  8. Young Fresh Fellows "O Little Town of Bethlehem"
  9. The Odds "Kings of Orient"
  10. Clockhammer "Here Comes Santa Claus"
  11. Henry Rollins" "'Twas the Night Before Christmas"
