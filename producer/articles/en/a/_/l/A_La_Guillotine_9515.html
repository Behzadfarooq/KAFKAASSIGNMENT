A La Guillotine That's All Very Well, But...


A La Guillotine was the a collection of the first three singles by the band McCarthy. It was released on 29th February 1988.

[edit] Track listing

  1. "Red Sleeping Beauty"
  2. "Something Wrong Somewhere"
  3. "Kill Kill Kill Kill"
  4. "In Purgatory" (studio version)
  5. "From The Damned"
  6. "Frans Hals"
  7. "The Comrade Era"
  8. "God The Father"
  9. "For The Fat Lady"
  10. "The Fall"
