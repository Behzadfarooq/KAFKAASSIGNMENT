A Lesson in Love (Swedish: En lektion i kärlek) is a 1954 film directed by Ingmar Bergman.

The film is a comedy by Ingmar Bergman and one of his early films. It displays the marriage of David (a gynecologist) and his wife Marianne and the little bumps along the way; such as mistresses that are definitely too young, a jealous artist that is definitely too eccentric, a much too neglected daughter and celebrating a birthday at David's parents. A lesson in love...!
