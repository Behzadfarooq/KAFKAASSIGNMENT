A Little Extra Sun is the third studio recording and first EP by American independent singer Megan Slankard, released on September 6, 2005 as the follow up to her sophomore album, Freaky Little Story. This was first released as a limited edition EP exclusively available at CD Baby, with limited distribution available at other music retailers in 2006.

  2. "Sails" (Slankard) – 3:30
  3. "Planets" (Slankard) – 4:16
  4. "You Love Like" (Slankard) – 3:58
  5. "Riley" (Slankard) – 2:58
