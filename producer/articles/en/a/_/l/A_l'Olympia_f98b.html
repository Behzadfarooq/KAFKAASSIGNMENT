À l’Olympia is a live album and DVD by French electronic musician Émilie Simon, due for release in February 2007 (see 2007 in music). It includes songs from all of her albums. The album contains material taken from a concert in December 2006. The DVD contains footage of the making of the album, a video produced by her in Japan for “Dame de lotus”, and all her three music videos to date.
