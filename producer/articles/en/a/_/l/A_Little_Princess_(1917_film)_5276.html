The Little Princess (1917) is a silent film directed by Marshall Neilan based upon the novel, A Little Princess by Frances Hodgson Burnett. This version is notable for having been adapted by famed female screenwriter Frances Marion.

  • Gertrude Short - Ermigarde
  • Gustav von Seyffertitz - Mr. Carrisford
  • Loretta Blake - Lavinia
  • George McDaniel - Ram Dass
