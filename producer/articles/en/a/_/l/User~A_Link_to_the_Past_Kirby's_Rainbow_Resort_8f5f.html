Kirby's Rainbow Resort (or KRR) is the largest Kirby fan site on the internet, with information and walkthroughs on every single video game in the franchise created by HAL Laboratory Inc.'s Masahiro Sakurai. As of 2003, several sections were added about the Kirby anime, which has become one of the most popular sections of the website. In addition to providing information to the interested fan, the site also has many multimedia downloads available, as well as a thriving forum, oekaki, and chat community.

[edit] History

The site was originally created as an AOL homepage by current site administrator kindarspirit in 1997, inspired by the lack of sites providing information on the video games series. This initial endeavour was abandoned, but recreated in 1999 on Xoom. Around this time, she met several people who aided her in the development of the site, until it was soon hosted by Zophar's Domain, a site which was and still is focused on video game emulation and hacking. Feeling as if the site did not fit in there, kindarspirit looked for a new host, and was accepted by classicgaming.com, where it has been hosted since 2000. The site has gone through many layouts and designs, all with a bright color scheme, to bring attention to the equally bright gameplay and graphics provided by the series.

[edit] Community

Though the main site itself averages up to 5000 visits per day, the forum is also one of the main drawing points to the site. The forum has sections dedicated to discussion of the video games and anime, as well as fan creations and miscellaneous discussion. Of note, the anime forum currently has a project focused on translating Japanese episodes and subbing them for download, run by those of the opinion that the official translation provided by 4Kids Entertainment did not do the series justice.
