A Life With Brian
Studio album by Flowered Up
Released 1991

A Life With Brian is an album by British band Flowered Up. It was released in 1991.

  1. "Sunshine" (5:08)
  2. "Take It" (4:32)
  3. "Crackerjack" (4:13)
  4. "Mr Happy Reveller" (3:11)
  5. "Hysterically Blue" (4:11)
  6. "It's On" (4:40)
  7. "Silver Pan" (2:37)
  8. "Phobia" (4:01)
  9. "Egg Rush" (4:48)
  10. "Doris... Is A Little Bit Partial" (5:01)
  11. "Weekender (12" Version)" (12:55) (Included on the 2005 rerelease, note that this album is called The Best of Flowered Up.)
