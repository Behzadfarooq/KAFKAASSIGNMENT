At Sea: A Limited Edition Live Recording is Alli's first live album and was recorded throughout October and November 2006 while touring with Shawn McDonald on his The Ripen Tour. This is a limited edition live album available only at shows and online.

[edit] Notes

  • "At Sea" is a new song.
  • "New Today" is a new song.
  • "Till Kingdom Come" is a cover of Coldplay's song "'Til Kingdom Come" from the X&Y album.
