Mishima: A Life in Four Chapters (soundtrack)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Mishima: A Life in Four Chapters
Soundtrack by Kronos Quartet, Michael Riesman
Released 1985
Genre Classical
Label WEA
Producer(s) Kurt Munkacsi

Mishima: A Life in Four Chapters is the soundtrack to the 1985 film Mishima: A Life in Four Chapters. It features music written by Philip Glass and performed, among others, by Kronos Quartet.

It was produced by Kurt Munkacsi and distributed by WEA.
