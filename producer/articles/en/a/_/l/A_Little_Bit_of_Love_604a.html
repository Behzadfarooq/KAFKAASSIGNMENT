A Little Bit of Love is a song and music video performed and produced by drag icon RuPaul and released February 25, 1997. This was the second single released from the Foxy Lady album. Written by RuPaul and Joe Carrano and released through Rhino Records.

The song reached #28 on the Hot Dance Music/Club Play list. The CD single contained six remixes: Welcome's Extended Original Mix, Moretta Electro-Funk Mix, Welcome's Sly Vocal Extended Mix, Hot Mix Version Mix, Moretta Love Beats Mix, and the Edge Factor Dub Mix. The music video depicted drag queens as aliens out to conquer the world. Besides RuPaul, the video also starred fellow female illusionists Jazzmun and Kandice Kane. The video was nominated by GLAAD for Best Video of 1998. It was directed by Randy Barbato and Fenton Bailey.

Rupaul performed the song that same year on The Ricki Lake Show.
