A Live Record is the first live album by the progressive rock band Camel, released in 1978. It is a double disc, composed of recordings from three different tours.

Disc one features recordings from the Mirage tour in 1974, and the Rain Dances tour, in 1977. Tracks 1-4 on the disc are from the Rain Dances tour, and 5-6 are from the Mirage tour.

Disc 2 features the original lineup all the way, and is devoted to a complete performance of the band's instrumental concept album, The Snow Goose, during the tour for the album in 1975.
