A Life of Surprises is a Big Finish original anthology edited by Paul Cornell, featuring Bernice Summerfield, a character from the spin-off media based on the long-running British science fiction television series Doctor Who. Like all Doctor Who spin-off media, its canonicity in relation to the television series is unclear.
