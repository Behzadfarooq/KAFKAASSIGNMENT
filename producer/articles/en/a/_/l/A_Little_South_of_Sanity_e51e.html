A Little South of Sanity is a live album by American hard rock band Aerosmith, released in 1998 (see 1998 in music). This album was the only Aerosmith album to receive the Parental Advisory sticker, primarily due to lead singer Steven Tyler shouting profanities in between songs and modifying some song lyrics to racier ones.
