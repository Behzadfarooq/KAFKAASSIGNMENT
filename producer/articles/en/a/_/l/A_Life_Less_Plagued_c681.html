A Life Less Plagued
Studio album by Carry On
Released October 2001
Genre Hardcore
Length 18:00
Label Bridge 9 Records

A Life Less Plagued is the debut full length record and swan song for straight edge hardcore punk band Carry On released in October of 2001 on Bridge 9 Records. Many[attribution needed] consider A Life Less Plagued to be the record that put L.A. hardcore back on the map and one of the best/most influential records of the decade.[citation needed]
