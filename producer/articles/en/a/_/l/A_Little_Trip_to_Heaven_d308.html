A Little Trip to Heaven is an Icelandic/United States drama and thriller film from 2005, directed by Icelandic director of The Sea, Baltasar Kormákur. The film is set in the U.S. but was shot in Iceland and Minnesota. Icelandic musician Mugison composed and played the music, except the song A Little Trip to Heaven which is originally by Tom Waits, though performed by Mugison in the movie.
