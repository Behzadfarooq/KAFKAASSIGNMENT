A Little Fall of Rain is a musical number in Les Miserables, which takes place in the second act. The number features Marius and Eponine. This is one of the most well known parts of Les Mis, as it is the song in which Eponine dies in Marius's arms.

A Little Fall of Rain starts in F Major, then modulates numerous times between Ab Major and F Major, before finally ending in Ab Major.
