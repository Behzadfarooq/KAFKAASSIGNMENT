A Life of Faith

A Life of Faith books are based on Martha Finley's Elsie Dinsmore books. They have been adapted to appeal to modern children. The language has been updated, and the story's have been shortened.

There are four series so far in the A Life of Faith series.

[edit] Elsie Series

The Elsie series is based on approximately the first six Elsie Dinsmore books. Some things have been changed such as the names of Elsie's two youngest children. In the original books they were Rosie and Walter. In the new version they are Rosemary and Daniel.

The series has eight books, a diary, and two supplement books.

  • 1.Elsie's Endless Wait
  • 2.Elsie's Impossible Choice
  • 3.Elsie's New Life
  • 4.Elsie's Stolen Heart
  • 5.Elsie's True Love
  • 6.Elsie's Troubled Times
  • 7.Elsie's Tender Mercies
  • 8.Elsie's Great Hope

The supplements are

  • 1.Elsie's Christmas Party Book
  • 2.Elsie's Life Lessons

[edit] Millie Keith

The Millie Keith books are based on the Millie Kieth books written by Martha Finley. Millie Keith is Elsie Dinsmore's cousin.

The Millie Keith book series consist of eight books, a diary, and three supplements.

  • 1.Millie's Unsettled Season
  • 2.Millie's Courageous Days
  • 3.Millie's Remarkable Journey
  • 4.Millie's Faithful Heart
  • 5.Millie's Steadfast Love
  • 6.Millie's Grand Adventure
  • 7.Millie's Reluctant Sacrifice
  • 8.Millie's Fiery Trial

The supplements are

  • 1.Millie's Life Lessons
  • 2.Millie's Prayer Projects
  • 3.Dear Millie

[edit] Violet Travilla

The Violet Travilla books focus on Elsie's second daughter Violet. It starts at approxiamatly book seven of the original series.

The series consist of eight books.

  • 1.Violet's Hidden Doubts
  • 2.Violet's Amazing Summer
  • 3.Violet's Turning Point
  • 4.Violet's Bold Mission
  • 5.Violet's Perplexing Puzzles
  • 6.Violet's Bumpy Ride
  • 7.Violet's Defiant Daughter
  • 8.Violet's Foreign Intrigue

Supplements:

  • 1. (Soon to be released) Violet's Life Lessons
