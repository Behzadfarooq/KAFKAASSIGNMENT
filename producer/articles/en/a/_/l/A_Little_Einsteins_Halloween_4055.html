"A Little Einsteins Halloween" is a Little Einsteins special episode from season one.

Spoiler warning: Plot and/or ending details follow.

Brief Summary: It's Halloween, and the children are in Europe to celebrate the holiday. They plan to do some trick-or-treating at some European castles, until it seems some persistent ghosts attempt to steal their Halloween treats. Will the gang protect their treats from the ghosts?

Setting: Buckingham Palace in London, England; 3 castles various locations (including Neuschwanstein in Germany)

[edit] Plot

This episode starts out with a frightful entrance from Rocket and the team! Leo makes the noteworthy statement that it’s Halloween! The kids are getting ready to go to a big Halloween Costume party! Leo notes that before they can go, they need a lot of Halloween Treats! They are going to go around Europe and visit four great European castles to get all the treats they need! They are going to visit a Mediaeval Castle, Buckingham Palace, Neuschwanstein castle, and the Palace of Resigh! The team heads off in the direction of the Mediaeval Castle! June points it out and Rocket lands near it. Once they land, the team runs to the door. Quincy knocks at the door and Leo asks nicely for some Halloween Treats, but they don’t get any! Annie says that they need to say “Trick-or-treat!” Annie makes up a trick-or-treat song and sings it at the castle and in no time, candy pours out of the castle and into the team’s candy carrying pumpkin! The team got a large portion of candy! Afterwards, the team is visited by a very spooky ghost that will try to take their treats away! The team is in shock of the ghost and quickly makes a run for Rocket.

BLAST OFF!!!

The team flies high, and far away from the castle and the ghost. Once the team knows that the ghost is too far away, Rocket flies in the direction of Buckingham Palace! The team quickly makes it to London and the palace! The team tries to fly to the palace, but a guard stops them! Rocket tries again to get through but the guard still won’t let them pass. The guard then turns around and June takes the chance to sneak past the guard. With some quick thinking, June thinks of a plan to get Rocket past the guard. They are almost caught by the guard twice, but afterwards they make it through the Buckingham Palace gates and to the door! They got past the guard! The team gets off Rocket and walks casually to the door of the palace. Quincy makes a few good knocks and the door opens up. The team sings the trick-or-treat song and many Halloween treats fly out of the castle and into the pumpkin! After they get there treats, the ghost returns to take the treats! But this time another ghost is with him! Annie sheiks and the team makes a run for Rocket and away from the ghosts!

The team flies away from the ghosts and to the direction of Germany and the castle of Neuschwanstein! In what seems like no time at all, the team arrives at the door of Neuschwanstein castle. The kids walk to the door which has a large silver violin near it, and Quincy knocks on the door. The team waits, and the door never opens. Quincy looks at the violin and wonders if he tries to play it, then the door will open up! Quincy tries, but the violin makes no music. Quincy then tries playing it again, pizzicato this time. It works! The door opens up to the music of the violin! The team sings to the trick-or-treat song and bushels of treats are tossed in the treat pumpkin! Now the pumpkin is almost full! Only one more castle to visit! Once again, the same ghosts as before come floating to the kids to try and take the treats away! This time there are three ghosts! The team dashes for Rocket and away from the ghosts!

BLAST OFF!!!

The team flies away from Neuschwanstein castle and the ghosts, but the ghosts stay right behind Rocket! The team flies away and in the direction of the Palace of Resigh! Rocket quickly finds the castle in France! The castle has a large hedge maze in front of it! Rocket flies into the maze! Once there, the kids can hear the ghosts again! The team needs to find a path to take that doesn’t lead to ghosts! It takes three tries but the team does it and finds a path that leads away from the ghosts and right to the door of the palace! Once they arrive at the door, the team walks up to the door and Quincy gets ready to give it a knock. Before Quincy can get to the door, the ghosts appear in front of the door! Leo quickly notices up close that they really aren’t ghosts, but they are really instruments! Leo unmasks the three ghosts finding their friends, Cello, Bassoon, and Clarinet! More ghosts come up and reveal them selves to be their friends Violin, Timpani, and Cymbals! Quincy now knocks on the door of the castle and the door opens up. Everybody, including the instruments plays the trick-or-treat song while the kids sing it! Treats come from the castle and land right in the treat pumpkin! Leo announces that they finally have all the treats they need! But as clever as he is, Leo lets the team know that the party will be held in the Resigh Hall of Mirrors! The team enters the Hall of Mirrors in their costumes to find all of the treats set up for them! Leo is dressed as a spider, June is a dressed as a witch, Quincy is a bat, and Annie is a pumpkin! The instruments fly into the hall of mirrors playing the trick-or-treat song for the team. The team sings and dances along with them in their costumes, and the party begins! Leo pulls out his baton and...

Mission Completion!
