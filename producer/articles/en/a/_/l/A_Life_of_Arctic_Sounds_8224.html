"A Life of Arctic Sounds" is a 1996 single by Modest Mouse.

[edit] Track listing

  1. "A Life of Arctic Sounds" — 2:29
  2. "Medication" — 5:01

Both songs later appeared on the Building Nothing Out of Something compilation album and were left unreleased elsewhere.
