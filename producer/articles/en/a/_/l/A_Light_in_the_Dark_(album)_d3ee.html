A Light in the Dark is a 2006 album by the heavy metal band Metal Church.

[edit] Tracklist

  1. "A Light In The Dark"
  2. "Beyond All Reason"
  3. "Mirror Of Lies"
  4. "Disappear"
  5. "The Believer"
  6. "Temples Of The Sea"
  7. "Pill For The Kill"
  8. "Son Of The Son"
  9. "More Than Your Master"
  10. "Blinded By Life"
  11. "Watch The Children Pray 2006" *(A remake from their song of the same name from their album The Dark, recorded and dedicated to late vocalist David Wayne.)

[edit] Lineup

  • Guitars: Kurdt Vanderhoof, Jay Reynolds
  • Vocals: Ronny Munroe
  • Bass: Steve Unger
  • Drums: Jeff Plate
