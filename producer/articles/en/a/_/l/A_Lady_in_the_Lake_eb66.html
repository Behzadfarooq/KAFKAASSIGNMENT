"A Lady in the Lake" is the seventh episode in season 2 of Murder, She Wrote. It first aired November 10, 1985 on CBS. Guest stars include Tom Bosley, William Windom and Lee Meriwether.

[edit] Synopsis

On Harry Pierce's advice, Jessica Fletcher spends a little bit of time at a lakeside inn where, while birdwatching one morning, she witnesses what appears to be a struggle and a woman falling into the lake. Everyone is convinced that the husband did it, but Jessica isn't quite so sure.
