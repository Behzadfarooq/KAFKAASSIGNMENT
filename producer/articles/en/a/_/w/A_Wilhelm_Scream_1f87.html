A Wilhelm Scream (abbreviated as AWS, and for short known as Wilhelm or the Scream), who formerly were known as Smackin' Isaiah, is a technical punk rock/melodic hardcore band from New Bedford, Massachusetts. Many people have referenced Strung Out, Hot Water Music, Propagandhi, and Strike Anywhere as the bands similar artists. Their name is a reference to the Wilhelm scream, an infamous stock sound effect which mainly is used in films. A Wilhelm Scream is currently planning to record their next album in May of 2007 at the Blasting Room in Fort Collins Colorado with legendary punk producer Bill Stevenson. The first single off the upcoming album will be "5-9".

A Wilhelm Scream are currently signed to Nitro Records.

[edit] Trivia

  • In their song "Knucklehead" they use a quote from the film The Goonies.
  • In their song "You Bendt My Wookie" they use a quote from the film The Kentucky Fried Movie.
  • In their song "Bowling" they use a quote from the film The Big Lebowski.
  • In their song "Mob Life" they use a quote from the film True Romance.
  • In their song "A Chapter of Accidents" they use an edited quote by Richard McLaren, when hostiles were invading the Republic of Texas embassy.
  • In their song "Catharsis for Dummies" they use a quote from the film Casablanca.
  • In their song "Diver" there is a line that reffers to the alternative rock band The Afghan Whigs.
