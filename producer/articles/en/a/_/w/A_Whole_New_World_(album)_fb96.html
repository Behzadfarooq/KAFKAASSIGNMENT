A Whole New World
Studio album by Katie Price & Peter Andre.
Released 2006
Genre Pop
Label Sony BMG

A Whole New World is a duet album by British glamour model Katie Price and her husband, the pop singer Peter Andre.

[edit] Response

The album entered Number 20 on the official UK top 40 album chart. The album sold a total of 180,000 copies in the UK in its first four weeks being on sale, and is expected to be released in Asia, Australia and other countries in 2007. The top 40 album has been certified gold in the UK.

The album has become notable on Amazon's UK page for receiving a deluge of reviews sarcastically praising the record, most of which are absurdly hyperbolic - for example, one reviewer claimed it had restored his sight, and another claimed his cat had been resurrected by the CD.
