A Worm's Life is a 1996 album by Crash Test Dummies. It came after their successful album, God Shuffled His Feet.

[edit] Track listing

All songs by Brad Roberts.

  1. "Overachievers"
  2. "He Liked To Feel It"
  3. "A Worm's Life"
  4. "Our Driver Gestures"
  5. "My Enemies"
  6. "There Are Many Dangers"
  7. "I'm Outlived by That Thing?"
  8. "All of This Ugly"
  9. "An Old Scab"
  10. "My Own Sunrise"
  11. "I'm a Dog"
  12. "Swatting Flies"

[edit] Credits

  • Brad Roberts – lead vocals, guitars
  • Ellen Reid – back-up vocals, piano, keyboards
  • Benjamin Darvill – harmonica, melodica, theramin
  • Dan Roberts – bass guitars, synth bass
  • Mitch Dorge – drums, percussion, digital stuff
  • Murray Pulver – electric guitar solos on I'm A Dog and All Of This Ugly, additional guitars
  • Simon Franglen – string and horn arrangements on My Own Sunrise
  • Terry Manning - Recording Engineer
