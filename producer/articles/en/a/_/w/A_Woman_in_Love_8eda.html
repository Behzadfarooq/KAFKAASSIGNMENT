"A Woman In Love" is a popular song. It was written by Frank Loesser and was published in 1955, introduced in the Broadway musical Guys and Dolls..

The biggest hit version of the song was recorded by The Four Aces, with Frankie Laine also recording a version that scored a big success in the United Kingdom, reaching #1 on the UK Singles Chart. In the UK, Ronnie Hilton also charted, at #30, with another version of the song.
