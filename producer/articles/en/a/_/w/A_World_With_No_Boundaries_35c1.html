A World With No Boundaries was a fictional terrorist organization featured in the video game Ace Combat Zero: The Belkan War. The organization was founded by a group of disillusioned pilots and soldiers from all sides of the Belkan conflict (Including Belka, Osea, Ustio, Sapin, and Yuktobania), who defected from their armies to form the group.

[edit] June 6, 1995

In the game this day was the day that the 7 nuclear weapons were dropped onto belkan soil. This was supposedly the doing of the fictional terrorist group. This group was decieving all other governments for their own gain and used nuclear weapons during a staged firefight to confuse other pilots.

[edit] XB-0 Hresvelgr

The Hresvelgr in flight.

The organization's primary goal was to eliminate all borders between countries and form a global state. Somehow, A World With No Boundaries got hold of the Belkan experimental bomber XB-0 Hresvelgr and activated it for their own purposes, raiding several Allied targets, including Valais Air Base, the staging area of Galm Squadron. The sheer cost of just running the Hresvelgr suggests that the organization held ties to people in positions of power, with access to impressive amounts of funding.

The aircraft was developed by the South Belkan Munitions Factory[1] and was a prototype, meaning that no further models were built. It was propelled by six jet engines, each putting out about 1350.2 kN. Protecting the aircraft was a mix of Anti-Aircraft weaponry, such as AAMs and AA cannons, as well as the Sapin air squadron known as Espada.

The Hresvelgr, along with Espada and several other squadrons protecting it, was shot down by Galm squadron.

[edit] V2

Towards the end of the game, A World With No Boundaries had gained control of the Avalon dam facility in Belka which was being used to develop the V2 weapon. Most likely named for the real-life German V2 rocket of World War II, the weapon was an ICBM containing a multiple independently targetable re-entry vehicle. A World With No Boundaries was preparing to use the missile to plunge the world into a state of anarchy, thus eliminating all borders between countries and creating a world without boundaries. [2] The facility was attacked and neutralized by Galm squadron, however, a single missile was launched by former Galm squadron member Larry "Pixy" Foulke, who was controlling the V2 launch from the cockpit of the experimental ADFX-02 Morgan.

An epic battle ensued, culminating in the defeat of the former Galm Two. The link to the controlling aircraft severed, the final V2 self-destructed, and A World With No Boundaries ceased to exist.
