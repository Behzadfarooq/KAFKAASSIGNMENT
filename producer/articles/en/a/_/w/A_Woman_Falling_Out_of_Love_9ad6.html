A Woman Falling Out of Love is the upcoming studio album to be released by the R&B/soul singer Aretha Franklin. The album is set for a 2007 release. It is being produced by the singer's own Aretha Records. On this album, Franklin will perform duets with country star Faith Hill, gospel icons Shirley Caesar & Karen Clark Sheard[1] and R&B star Fantasia. [2]

Franklin has stated that, despite its title, the album is not a commentary on her romantic status, but rather, "something that all women can relate to."
