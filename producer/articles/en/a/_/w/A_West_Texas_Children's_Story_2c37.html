A West Texas Children's Story is a drama movie set in the 1960's directed by Brad Isaacs, starring Cayden Boyd, AnnaSophia Robb, Lara Flynn Boyle, Val Kilmer and others.

[edit] Plot

Set in the 1960's, a magical tale of 12-year old Ben (played by Cayden Boyd) and 12-year old Cassie (played by AnnaSophia Robb) embarking on an adventure to find new, cool parents and escape their neglected, overly adult, existence.
