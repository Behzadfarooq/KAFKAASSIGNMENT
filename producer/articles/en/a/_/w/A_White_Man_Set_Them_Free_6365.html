"A White Man Set Them Free" was the nineteenth episode of the HBO series Mr. Show to air. It originally aired on November 28, 1997.

[edit] Trivia

  • Fake Special Thanks: Ross McElwee
  • DVD Commentary: Bob Odenkirk, David Cross, Paul F. Tompkins, Jay Johnston, Dino Stamatopoulos, Brian Posehn, Bill Odenkirk, Tom Kenny, Jill Talley, Mike Upchurch
  • Writer Dino Stamatopoulos originally wrote the songs in the sketch "Cracker Barrel Open" for Late Night with Conan O'Brien, but they were deemed too offensive. He then tried to use them on Saturday Night Live and The Dana Carvey Show, but was shot down for the same reason; so, he brought the sketch to Mr. Show.
  • Bill Odenkirk did the drawing for the "Map Link" sketch. He also did drawings for the earlier episode "Peanut Butter, Eggs, and Dice."
