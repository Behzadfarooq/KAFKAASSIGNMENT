Sqanto: A Warrior's Tale is a live action Disney film, released in 1994.

It was written by Darlene Craviato and directed by Xavian Koller.

[edit] Plot

From the Internet Movie Database:

Historically inaccurate chronicle of Squanto's life prior to and including the arrival of the "Mayflower" in 1620.
