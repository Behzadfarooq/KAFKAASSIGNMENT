Bruce Lee: A Warrior's Journey is a documentary on the martial artist Bruce Lee and Jeet Kune Do. Also includes rare footage of Bruce Lee's incomplete film Game of Death that has been released with Bruce Lee's original English dubbing as part of the documentary. Most of the footage which was shot is from what was to be the centre piece of the film.

[edit] Enter the Dragon: Special Edition

'Warrior's Journey' was originally a documentary in its own right, but it can be found as a bonus feature on the release of the 2004 edition of Enter the Dragon on DVD along with the documentary, Bruce Lee: The Curse of the Dragon. This Film is one of the Best Documentary as it strongly emphasizes Bruce Lee's Philosophy , Way of his Life & thinking and also Bruce Lee's Problem's which he faced in America.
