A Brokedown Melody

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

A Brokedown Melody is a 2004 surfing documentary film directed by Chris Malloy and starring Jack Johnson.

Johnson and Matt Costa contributed to the soundtrack, including the single, "Let It Be Sung."
