A Boot and a Shoe is the tenth (studio)album from American singer-songwriter Sam Phillips.

[edit] Personnel

  • Sam Phillips: Vocals, Guitar.
  • Chris Bruce: Guitar.
  • Marc Ribot: Guitar.
  • Patrick Warren: Piano, Pump Organ, String Arrangement
  • T Bone Burnett: Bass.
  • David Piltch: Bass.
  • Mike Elizondo: Bass.
  • Carla Azar: Drums.
  • Jay Bellerose: Drums.
  • Jim Keltner: Drums.

  • Eric Gorfain: Violin
  • Daphne Chen: Violin
  • Richard Dodd: Cello
  • Leah Katz: Viola

[edit] Production notes

  • Produced by T Bone Burnett.
  • Executive Producer: David Bither.
  • Recorded by Mike Piersante at Sunset Sound Recorders (Hollywood, California), The Village (Hollywood, California), Stagg Street Studios (Van Nuys, California).
  • Assisted Engineers Brian Hearity, Jason Wormer, Emile Kelman and Ben Mumphrey. Mixed by TBone Burnett and Mike Piersante.
  • Mastered by Gavin Lurssen.
