A Beautiful World was a 2003 re-release of producer/singer/songwriter Robin Thicke's debut album Cherry Blue Skies. The album features the extra tracks "A Beautiful World" and "She's Gangsta". The woman featured on the cover of the album is Paula Patton, Thicke's wife.

[edit] Chart Performance/Sales

The album managed to reach #152 on the Billboard 200, while it peaked at #4 on Billboard's Heatseekers Album Chart. To date, the album has sold a mere 59,000 copies in the United States.
