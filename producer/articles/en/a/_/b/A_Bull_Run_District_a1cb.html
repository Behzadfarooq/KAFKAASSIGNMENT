The A Bull Run District is a high school conference of the Virginia High School League which draws most of its members from Northern Virginia and in the northern part of the Shenandoah Valley. The schools in the Bull Run District compete in A Region B with the schools of the A Dogwood District, the A James River District and the A Shenandoah District.

[edit] Member Schools

Clarke County Eagles, Berryville

Madison County Mountaineers, Madison

Manassas Park Cougars, Manassas Park

George Mason Mustangs, Falls Church

Rappahannock County Panthers, Washington

Strasburg Rams, Strasburg
