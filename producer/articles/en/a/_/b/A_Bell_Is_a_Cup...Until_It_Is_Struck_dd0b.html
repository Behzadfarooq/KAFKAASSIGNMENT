A Bell Is a Cup . . . Until It Is Struck is a 1988 album by the British art punk/electronic group Wire.

  9. "Follow the Locust"
  10. "A Public Place"
  11. "The Queen of Ur and the King of Um (alternate version)"
  12. "Pieta"
  13. "Over Theirs (live)"
  14. "Drill" (live)"

Note: The last 4 tracks are on the CD only.
