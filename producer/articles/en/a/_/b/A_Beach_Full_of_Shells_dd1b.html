A Beach Full of Shells
Studio album by Al Stewart
Released 2005

A Beach Full of Shells is a 2005 album by Al Stewart. The song on the album, "Class of 58'" is a shorter version of a song of the same title, of length 13:08. This has now been released as a single. In the blurb of the single, it is suggested that the album "A Beach Full of Shells" was originally intended to focus around this song, which describes the life of a musician on the 1950s rock and roll scene.
