1999's A Box of Birds is a cover album of songs by artists who were an influence on The Church's music.

[edit] Track listing

  1. "The Faith Healer" [Alex Harvey]
  2. "It's All Too Much" [The Beatles]
  3. "Hiroshima Mon Amour" [Ultravox]
  4. "The Porpoise Song" [The Monkees]
  5. "Decadence" [Kevin Ayers]
  6. "The Endless Sea" [Iggy Pop]
  7. "Friction" [Television]
  8. "All the Young Dudes" [Mott The Hoople]
  9. "Silver Machine" [Hawkwind]
  10. "Cortez the Killer" [Neil Young]
