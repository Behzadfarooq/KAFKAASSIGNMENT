A BEST 2 is a series of "greatest hits" compilation albums recorded by Ayumi Hamasaki. The series consists of:

  • A BEST 2 -BLACK-
  • A BEST 2 -WHITE-

[edit] Sales and Accomplishments

The sales of A BEST 2, combined, reached approximately 1,000,000 (one million) units sold shortly after one week of release. This makes it her most sales in one week for an Album since RAINBOW back in 2002.

  • 1st time in 36.5 years that a female artist has occupied #1 and #2 in the album charts.
  • 1st time in the United World Chart's history that a female soloist could top the chart with 2 albums landing at #1 and #2 at the same time.
