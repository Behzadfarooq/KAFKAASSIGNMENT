[edit] Merger Proposition

I think it would be beneficial to merge this article with the RPG Maker article. This, although probably being the most popular RPG Maker game, is not of enough interest to have an entire separate article on it. All content could be placed under RPG_Maker#Games_made_with_RPG_Maker, and a subheading could be created for this game. Not all of the information here would be interesting to a person who has no interest in RPG Maker; if they stumble upon this page, they are not going to want to know the entire story, the issues, and the features, but maybe a short summary of all three would be better. Though Gaming World is down for the moment, the game's page could be found here, as long with screenshots, a small bit of information, and a download link. I do not think having to upkeep a completely separate article is feasible, and the merger would be more beneficial for the reader. --imaek 21:23, 15 January 2006 (UTC)

Agreed; this should be done with all RPG Makers games. I'll merge it with the RPG Maker article, and from there on out, drop similar propositions on other RPG Maker game pages. I'm a mergist myself ^_^ Deckiller 17:21, 22 January 2006 (UTC)
