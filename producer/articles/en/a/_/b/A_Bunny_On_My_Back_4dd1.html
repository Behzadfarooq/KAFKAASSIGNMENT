A Bunny On My Back is episode # 1.07 b Brandy & Mr. Whiskers.

[edit] Synopsis

Image:BAMW-A-Bunny-On-My-Back.jpg

Brandy is sunbathing while some of the local denizens are diving off of a cliff into the swimming hole. Ed the Otter dives in and Mr. Whiskers gets ready to dive in, but he is pushed aside by an ocelot names Artaro, whom does an elegant dive into the water, impressing everyone there. Brandy falls in love with him upon first glance, and most of the other females in the jungle also consider him to be very dreamy and attractive, and when Artaro invites Brandy to come for dinner, she accepts and they walk off together. However, Mr. Whiskers personally believes that Artaro is evil and he doesn't believe Brandy should go out on a date with him. Despite how much Mr. Whiskers pleas with Brandy, she refuses to listen to his nonsense, so he decides to go try to find out more information on Artaro. Mr. Whiskers asks around the jungle, though all the females envy Brandy because Artaro is very handsome and they consider him to be very attractive, in which they refuse to believe him. Mr. Whiskers then decides to go consult with Ed to figure out what he should do, which Ed warns him that his feet are stuck in the sticky solution that Ed and Mr. Whiskers were standing around. Mr. Whiskers then overhears Artaro talking to his children, where he mentions that they are going to eat Brandy that night, so Mr. Whiskers returns to the house while unwittingly containing the sticky solution on him, and when he runs so fast that he smacks into Brandy and gets stuck on her, Brandy gets very frustrated with him. Despite Brandy’s best efforts to get Mr. Whiskers off of her, she realizes that she must make do the best she can. She tries to make Mr. Whiskers look like a scarf, in which she goes to her date with Artaro wearing him around her neck. Unfortunately, Mr. Whiskers keeps saying odd things, as well as burping and farting, in which embarrasses her greatly. Artaro decides to go get something to help Brandy’s condition, in which she had no choice but to say she had when Mr. Whiskers burped and farted, though when Artaro leaves, Brandy struggles to try to get Mr. Whiskers off of her. While Brandy cannot get him off, she does tie his ears around his mouth by the time Artaro gets back, which she goes and accepts the drink he provides. However, Artaro’s family is sneaking up behind Brandy, in which Mr. Whiskers finally manages to get Brandy’s attention in which she sees what is occurring and she begins to panic. Brandy backs up against a wall, but Mr. Whiskers uses his excellent lower leg strength that he has since he’s a rabbit and he fights off Artaro and his family. Brandy thanks Mr. Whiskers and they return home, and the episode ends with them still stuck together, though their friendship was increased dramatically.
