A Brief History of Ambient
Compilation album by Various Artists
Released 1993
Genre Ambient
Label Virgin Records

A Brief History of Ambient is a 1993 compilation album released on the Virgin Records label, as part of their Ambient series. The album was issued as a double CD, and was compiled by Simon Hopkins.
