A BEST 2 -BLACK- is one of two greatest hits albums by Ayumi Hamasaki known collectively as "A BEST 2". It was released on February 28, 2007 along with its counterpart A BEST 2 -WHITE-. Both sets were released in 1CD+2DVD versions with limited version covers for each album's color. Both albums feature songs released between the singles "evolution" and "Bold & Delicious/Pride." No songs off her latest album Secret are featured on either album. A BEST 2 -BLACK- includes "dark" songs with "painful/heartbroken" lyrics while A BEST 2 -WHITE- is the opposite, containing "light" songs with "bright/cheerful/upbeat" lyrics. A Best 2 -Black- includes a previously unreleased track, "part of Me" (featured in a new Panasonic CM) and its accompanying music video. Previously released tracks were also remastered. The 1DVD includes all PVs for the songs off the album. 2DVD includes a documentary which chronicles her concert, "Best of Countdown Live 2006-2007 A".

[edit] Secret track

There is a secret track at the end of the last track, "part of Me". The song is the previously-released "Memorial address", which is from her 2003 mini-album of the same name.
