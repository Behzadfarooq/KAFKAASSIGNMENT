a bug's land is a section of Disney's California Adventure at the Disneyland Resort in Anaheim, California. The area is based on the on the Disney-Pixar film A Bug's Life. While part of the land opened in 2001 as "Bountiful Valley Farm", the majority of attractions opened in autumn of 2002 and was re-christened "a bug's land" and sought to offer kid-friendly rides sorely lacking from the park's initial roster.

The area in "a bug's land" is seen from that of a bug, and the character of "Flik" from A Bug's Life. Oversized human items and foodstuffs are scattered around the land. The entrance to the section featuring the rides is a box of "Cowboy Crunchies", a reference to "Woody" from another Pixar film, Toy Story.
