A Break In The Weather is a compilation album released by Ginger (of The Wildhearts) that collects together all the tracks from the first 5 singles from the ill fated[1] "Singles Club", of which there were originally going to be 12, one being released every month. The final 3 tracks are the 6th single that was recorded but remained previously unreleased.

[edit] Trivia

  • "Dying Art Of The Chorus" features "growls" by Mark "Barney" Greenway of the Death metal group Napalm Death[2].
  • The 5 "Singles Club" singles that were released are represented on this compilation by tracks 1, 4 and 7 from Disc 1 and tracks 1 and 4 from Disc 2. The 2 tracks following each of these tracks represent the 2 b-sides that were included on each single.
  • According to the acompanying steeve notes[3], "Energetic O" is about masturbation, the "O" denoting orgasm.
