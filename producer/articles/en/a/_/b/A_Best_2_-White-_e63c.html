A Best 2 -White- is one of the two CDs of the 2nd greatest hits album by Ayumi Hamasaki . It was released on February 28, 2007 along with its counterpart A Best 2 -Black-. Both sets will be released in both 1CD+2DVD versions with limited version covers for each albums color. Both albums will feature songs between the release of her "evolution" single to her "Bold & Delicious/Pride" single. No songs off her latest album "Secret" will be featured on either album. A Best 2 -White- will include "Light" songs with "bright/cheerful/upbeat" lyrics while A Best 2 -Black- will be the opposite including "Dark" songs with "painful/heartbroken" lyrics. The 1DVD will include all PV's for the songs off the album. 2DVD will include her latest Countdown Live "Best of Countdown Live 2006-2007". On January 15 , 2007 this release has been confirmed by her Official website. On January 16 , 2007 track listing for both CDs were officially released. The album covers and DVD track listings were released on February 6th, 2007.
