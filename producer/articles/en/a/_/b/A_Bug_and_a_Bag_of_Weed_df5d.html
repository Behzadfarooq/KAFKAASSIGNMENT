A Bug and a Bag of Weed is a 2006 comedy film directed by David Gonella and starring Sebastian Spence, Chris Cuthbertson, Drew Hagen, Nico Lorenzutti, Laura Kohoot, Nigel Bennett, Ryan Scott Greene, Amy Kerr, John Dunsworth, Veronica Reynolds and Christopher Shyer.
