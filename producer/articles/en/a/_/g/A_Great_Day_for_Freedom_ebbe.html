"A Great Day for Freedom" is a song by progressive rock band Pink Floyd from their 1994 album, The Division Bell. A live version can be found on P*U*L*S*E.

[edit] Personnel

  • David Gilmour - Guitar and Vocals
  • Richard Wright - Piano
  • Guy Pratt - Bass
  • Jon Carin - Programming
  • Nick Mason - Drums and Percussion (Tambourine)
  • Michael Kamen - Orchestral arrangement
