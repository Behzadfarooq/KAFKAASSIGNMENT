A Grizzly Murder is an episode of the American television series CSI: Miami, first broadcast February 26, 2007.

Gimmicks include

  • homocides
    • by injuring the knee of a companion during flight from a bear (implicitly evoking a joke that "I don't need to outrun the bear, i only need to outrun you") and
    • by kneeling on the chest of the person performing fellatio on the killer,
  • the use of a mixture of glycerine and vanilla as a hunting lure for bears, and
  • identifying the owner of a portable digital-music player by interrogating the tune-server for all customers who have ordered a specific combination of tunes.

The prospect of an extended subplot is provided by Horatio Caine's refusal to yield to the demands of a county supervisor for inaction on the supervisor's hiring of a prostitute with a $10,000-fee, and the prospect that the supervisor is financing such expenditures with the proceeds of bribery.
