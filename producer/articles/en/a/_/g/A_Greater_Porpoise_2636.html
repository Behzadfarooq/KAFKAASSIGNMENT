[edit] Synopsis

Jaden, adrift at sea, is pulled into Neo-Space to meet the Neo Spacians. As Neo-Spacian Aqua Dolphin warns him about Sartorius and the Society of Light, an alien attacks, and Jaden must call forth his new Neos monsters to win.

[edit] Major events

  • Jaden's first encounter with the Neo-Spacians
  • Jaden recovers the ability to see his cards
  • Jaden duels the Alien of Light (Jaden wins)
