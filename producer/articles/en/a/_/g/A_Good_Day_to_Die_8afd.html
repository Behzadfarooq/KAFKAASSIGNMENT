A Good Day to Die
Studio album by Absinthe
Released August 24, 1998

A Good Day to Die is a music album by Absinthe of songs released in 1998 on Llanas Records.

[edit] Track listing

  1. "Intro" – 1:18
  2. "Bully On The Corner" – 4:56
  3. "Defeat" – 5:17
  4. "A Good Day To Die" – 4:56
  5. "Spanish Waltz" – 4:47
  6. "It Don't Bother Me" – 4:47
  7. "Messed Up Likes Of Us" – 3:31
  8. "Still Alone" – 5:00
  9. "Angelina" – 4:57
  10. "Dying In My Dreams" – 4:28
  11. "What I Don't Feel" – 5:21
  12. "A Little Bit Of Hell" – 4:12
  13. "Time For Us" – 4:18

[edit] Personnel

  • Sam Llanas: Vocals, Acoustic Guitar
  • Jim Eannelli: Guitars, Bass, Chant
  • Guy Hoffman: Drums, Chant

[edit] Additional musicians

  • Thacher Schmid: Violin, Mandolin (track 3)
  • Gary Tanin: Keyboards (tracks 4, 7, 10, 11, 12)
  • Paul Gmeinder: Cellos (track 5)
  • Tiffany Hood and Janet Planet: Vocals (track 6)
  • Nick Kitsos: additional tom (track 8)
  • Jim Eannelli: Cellos (track 9)
  • Guy Hoffman: Percussion (track 11)
  • Reggie Bordeaux: additional toms (track 12).
