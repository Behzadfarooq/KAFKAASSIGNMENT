A Garage Dayz Nite, is the first E.P. from Beatallica. It contains 7 tracks, made from combinations of Beatles and Metallica songs

[edit] Track listing

  1. "Sgt. Hetfield's Motorbreath Pub Band" ("Sgt. Pepper's Lonely Hearts Club Band" / "Motorbreath")
    • Lyrical References:
      • Motörhead
  2. "A Garage Dayz Nite" ("A Hard Day's Night" / Garage Days Re-Revisited)
    • Lyrical References:
      • "Wherever I May Roam"
      • "Trapped Under Ice"
      • "Love at First Sting"
  3. "For Horsemen" ("For No One" / "The Four Horsemen")
    • Lyrical References:
      • "King Nothing"
      • Winger
  4. "No Remorseful Reply" ("No Reply" / "No Remorse")
    • Lyrical References:
      • "Creeping Death"
  5. "The Thing That Should Not Let It Be" ("Let It Be" / "The Thing That Should Not Be")
    • Musical References:
      • "One"
      • "Fade to Black"
  6. "Everybody's Got a Ticket to Ride Except for Me and My Lightning" ("Everybody's Got Something to Hide Except Me and My Monkey" / "Ticket to Ride" / "Ride the Lightning")
  7. "...And Justice for All My Loving" ("All My Loving" / "...And Justice for All")
    • Lyrical References:
      • "Sad But True"
