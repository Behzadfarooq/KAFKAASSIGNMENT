A Glorious Day is the fourth single from Embrace's fourth studio album, Out Of Nothing. This single was released to coincide with the band's "Glorious Day" open air gigs on May 28 and 29, 2005, at Millennium Square in Leeds, United Kingdom.

The songs Milk and Honey and Feels Like Glue are featured on the b-sides compilation Dry Kids: B-Sides 1997-2005.
