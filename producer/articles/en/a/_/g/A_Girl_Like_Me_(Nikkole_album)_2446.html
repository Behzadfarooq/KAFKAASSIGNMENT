A Girl Like Me is the sophomore album by American Pop/R&B singer Nikkole. The album was released on October 11, 2005 by SE Entertainment Records. The first single from the album is It’s Too Late which features The Emotions of Best Of My Love fame.

The second single, Gon’ Bounce is scheduled to be released on June 06, 2006. The Maxi-Single will contain six mixes of the song including the original album version, several club/dance mixes and an a cappella version.
