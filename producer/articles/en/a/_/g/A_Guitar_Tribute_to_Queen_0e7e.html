A Guitar Tribute to Queen is a tribute album of many famous songs by Queen. It is performed by Dark One Light, a cover band. The album was released on June 22, 2004.

[edit] Track listing

  1. "We Are The Champions"
  2. "Crazy Little Thing Called Love"
  3. "Fat Bottomed Girls"
  4. "We Will Rock You"
  5. "Another One Bites The Dust"
  6. "You're My Best Friend"
  7. "Now I'm Here"
  8. "Under Pressure"
  9. "I Want It All"
  10. "Great Pretender"
