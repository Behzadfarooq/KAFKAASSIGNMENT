"A Goat's Tale" is an episode of That's So Raven

[edit] Summary

Eddie is tired of rival school Jefferson High stealing Bayside's Barracuda mascot before their big games, so Eddie steals their mascot. Chelsea thinks it's wrong to use goats that way. Raven is planning on going to a concert but then the goat eats her tickets. The goat becomes sick and Chelsea nurses it. At the end of the episode, Eddie becomes the new mascot for Jefferson High.

[edit] Episode notes

  • Second time the Boyz N' Motion is mentioned.
  • Aired as part of That's So Raven's Psychic Summer
  • Second appearance of the Bayside mascot.
