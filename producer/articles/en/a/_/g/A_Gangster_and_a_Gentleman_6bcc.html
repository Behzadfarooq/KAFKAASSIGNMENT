A Gangster and a Gentleman is Yonkers, New York rapper Styles P's solo debut after a series of three LOX albums and three Ruff Ryder volumes. Styles is the second to be debuted solo, after Jadakiss, and before the other two members of the LOX J-Hood and Sheek Louch.

The album is dominated by the "Ruff Ryder" mood that the label always propells, as heard in DMX albums. However, this selection of instrumentals can more specifically be described as a reflection of the temperament of Styles P. Duality in the persona of Styles P. is equally presented lyrically and instrumentally throughout his album. The track "A Gangster & A Gentleman" switches back and forth from a hefty beatless hook to a quieter and pensive beat during Styles P's 16 bars. "Soul Clap," "I'm A Ruffryder," and "Y'all Don't Wanna F***" delivers "gangster" with the authority of M.O.P. and "Black Magic," "We Thugs," "Lick Shots," and "My Brother" gentles down to show the positive. But, of course, this template has certainly been followed before. The quality in this album comes from Styles P.'s lyrics.

It was certified platinum by RIAA on October 11, 2002.[1]
