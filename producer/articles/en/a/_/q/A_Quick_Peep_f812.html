'A Quick Peep' is a song from British rock band Oasis' fifth album Heathen Chemistry, written by Andy Bell. This was Bell's first song written for Oasis. The song is an instrumental. According to Gem Archer, this song was written while the band was moving on a Tour Bus through the Alpes while they were on tour in 2001. They had nothing to do and he and Andy Bell started playing guitars. They were doing it for many hours, so this is how this song was written
