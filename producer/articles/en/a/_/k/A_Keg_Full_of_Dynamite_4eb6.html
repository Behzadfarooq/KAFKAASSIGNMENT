A Keg Full of Dynamite is a live album by Pentagram released by Black Widow Records in 2003. It was recorded at The Keg in 1978. Bobby Liebling stated that this is the "first authorized representation of Pentagram during what has come to be known as the High Voltage Era."[1] The final two tracks, "When the Screams Come" and "Livin' in a Ram's Head," were taken from the original test pressing 7" issued on High Voltage Records in 1979.[1]
