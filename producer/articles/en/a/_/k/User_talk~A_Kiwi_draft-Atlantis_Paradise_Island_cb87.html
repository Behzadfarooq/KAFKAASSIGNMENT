Opened in 1963, Ocean Club is the oldest hotel on Paradise Island.

The Ocean Club is owned One & Only Resorts, the management company of Bahamas-based Kerzner International Limited. Ocean Club's offers privacy and quietness, and exceptional service.  Champagne and strawberries brought daily to your suite. And then of course, there is own private butler.

Ocean Club guests have full access to the Kerzner-owned Atlantis Resort and Casino, a ten-minute walk down the beach.

At Atlantis, large outdoor "playpen" filled with all kinds of aquatic entertainment: pools of turtles, large demonstrations of shark feeding, underground tunnels lined with aquariums, more swimming pools and places to eat than you can count, and several waterslides.

Off-site activities such as snorkeling, scuba diving and charter fishing.

Before 1963, 80% of Paradise Island was owned by Merv Griffin. Founded ten years BEFORE Bahamian independence from Great Britain.

Colonial style buildings, beautiful courtyards. white beaches, clear blue water.

Annual Michael Jordan Celebrity Golf Tournament which is held on the private golf course just for Atlantis and Ocean Club guests.
