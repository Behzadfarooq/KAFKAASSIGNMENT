A Kid from Tibet (西藏小子) is a Hong Kong action film starring three of the Seven Little Fortunes: Yuen Biao, Yuen Wah and Jackie Chan (only a cameo appearance). The film is about a monk from Tibet, sent by his master to Hong Kong to recover from a lawyer a magical bottle to which he has the cap. When these items were united long ago they protected Tibet from invasion by the evil Black Section of Esoteric Buddhism. The monk meets and protects a woman who is acting as the agent for the lawyer, and the Black Section fight to gain the magical bottle for themselves.
