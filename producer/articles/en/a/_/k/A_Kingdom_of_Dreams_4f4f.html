A Kingdom of Dreams is a 1989 romance novel written by American author Judith McNaught. It is set during the time of the English invasion of Scotland.

[edit] Plot summary

Spoiler warning: Plot and/or ending details follow.

Jennifer Merrick is the feisty daughter of a Scottish laird and Royce Westmoreland is called the "Wolf" sent by the King of England to wage war against Scotland. When Royce's brother, Stephen kidnaps Jennifer and her stepsister and brings them to Royce's camp, the lives of the two become intertwined.

[edit] Characters

  • Jennifer Merrick – daughter of a Scottish landowner, protagonist
  • Royce Westmoreland – an English fighter sent to Scotland
