A Kiss in Time is Patty Griffin's fourth commercially released album, and her first live album. It was recorded on January 30, 2003, at the Ryman Auditorium in Nashville, Tennessee and released on October 7 of the same year. The package includes a DVD which features behind the scenes footage of Griffin as well as videos of "Chief" and "Rain."
