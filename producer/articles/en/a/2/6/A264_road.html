The A264 is an east-west road in southern England that runs from Pembury in West Kent to Five Oaks in West Sussex.

[edit] Route (with major intersections)

  • Pembury (A21)
  • Tunbridge Wells (A26)
  • East Grinstead (A22)
  • Copthorne (M23)
  • Crawley (A23, M23)
  • Horsham (A24, A281)
  • Broadbridge Heath (A281)
  • Five Oaks (A29)
