A20 is a three-letter acronym that may refer to:

  • A20 line (addressing line 20), a microprocessor connection
  • A20 road, a major road in south-east England, United Kingdom
  • Quebec Autoroute 20 (A-20), in Canada
  • Bundesautobahn 20 autobahn in Germany
  • A-20 Havoc, the US Air Force's designation for the Douglas DB-7 aircraft
  • A-20, a rejected prototype version of the Soviet T-34 medium tank
  • One of the ECO codes for the English Opening in chess
  • A-20 (.hack), a fictional character from the anime .hack//SIGN
  • Aero A.20, a Czech fighter aircraft design from 1923
  • Autoroute A20, French motorway linking Paris to Toulouse and Spain
