The A229 is a major road running through the centre of Kent.

It begins in the Medway town of Rochester (at the A2 Star Hill) before running south over Blue Bell Hill to Maidstone, Staplehurst, Cranbrook and Hawkhurst before terminating at Cooper's Corner, a junction just outside Hurst Green in East Sussex. The road intersects with the A2, the M2, the M20, the A20, the A274 to Tenterden and the A268 at the crossroads in Hawkhurst. It is quite a pleasant road to drive on.
