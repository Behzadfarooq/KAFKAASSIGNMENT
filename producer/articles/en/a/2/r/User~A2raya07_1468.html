I am a proud Assyrian student who is now a senior in High School. I was born and raised in Chicago, IL. I have great interests in who I am and where I come from. I speak fluent Assyrian and I am currently learning how to read and write in the Aramaic language. I plan on majoring as a Civil Engineering with a minor in Syriac Studies and move to Iraq to help rebuild the remaining Assyrian villages that we have left.
