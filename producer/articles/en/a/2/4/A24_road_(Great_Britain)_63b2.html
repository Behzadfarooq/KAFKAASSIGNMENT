The A24 is a major road in England. It runs south from Clapham in southwest London through Morden before entering Surrey and heading through Ewell, Epsom, Ashtead, Leatherhead and Dorking. Once in West Sussex, it bypasses Horsham, Ashington and Findon before terminating in Worthing at the A27.

Between Clapham and Dorking, the A24 closely follows the route of the old Roman road Stane Street.
