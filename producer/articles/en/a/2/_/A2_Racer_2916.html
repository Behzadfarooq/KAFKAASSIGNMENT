Mode(s) Single player, Multiplayer

A2 Racer is a racing computer game, from Dutch game company Davilex where the player has to race over the Dutch A2 highway. In the UK, the games were released as M25 Racer and London Racer. Various A2 Racer sequels were released:

  • A2 Racer 2
  • A2 Racer III - Europa Tour
  • A2 Racer - De Politie slaat terug
  • Europe Racing
  • USA Racer
  • A2 Racer World Challenge
  • A2 Racer Police Madness
  • A2 Racer Destruction Madness

In Germany the games were released as:

  • Autobahn Raser I
  • Autobahn Raser II
  • Autobahn Raser III
  • Autobahn Raser IV
  • Europe Racing
  • USA Racer
  • Autobahn Raser World Challenge
  • Autobahn Raser - Das Spiel zum Film
  • Autobahn Raser Police Madness
  • Autobahn Raser Destruction Madness

In France they use the name Paris-Marseille Racing.
