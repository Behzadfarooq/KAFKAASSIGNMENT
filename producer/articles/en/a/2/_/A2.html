[edit] A2

A2 can refer to:

  • The A2 network, llc Online Company
  • A2 (rocket), a German design of rocket during World War II
  • The Zweikanalton (German) Stereo sound system used in some countries for their analogue television broadcasting.
  • The A-level education qualification in England, Wales and Northern Ireland
  • A street name for the drug benzylpiperazine (BZP).
  • A2 is an international standard paper size (420×594 mm), defined in ISO 216
  • Cielos del Peru IATA code
  • HMS A2, an A-class submarine of the Royal Navy
  • USS Adder (SS-3), a Plunger-class submarine of the United States Navy
  • Radio Station A2 in Lithuania
  • In town and country planning in the UK, A2 is the code for permission to use specific land or premises for financial and professional services.
  • The Audi A2 car
  • An A2 (theater) manages microphones for a performance.
  • The A2 Aviator, an LED flashlight constructed by Surefire
  • The Cowon A2 Portable Multimedia Player from Cowon Systems, Inc.

[edit] A2 roads

There are several roads and highways with the designation A2:

  • A2, a motorway in Austria see Highways section of the "Transportation in Austria" article
  • Hemus motorway (Bulgaria), motorway with the code A2
  • A2 autoroute (France)
  • Bundesautobahn 2 (Germany), autobahn connecting the Ruhr Area to Berlin
  • A2 road (Great Britain), major road connecting London to Dover, partially following the Roman road "Watling Street"
  • Rijksweg 2 (Netherlands), motorway with the code A2 connecting Amsterdam to Maastricht
  • A2 road (Northern Ireland)
  • A2 (Poland), motorway running from west to east through the middle of the country
  • A2 - Auto-estrada do Sul (Portugal), highway connecting Lisbon to the Algarve
  • A2 freeway (Romania), a motorway connecting Bucharest to Constanţa
  • Autovía A-2, is an Spanish autovía connecting Madrid to Barcelona
  • A2 (Slovenia), a motorway in Slovenia see List of highways section of the "Transportation in Slovenia" article
  • A2 (Switzerland), motorway connecting Basel to Chiasso
  • A2 (Croatia), a motorway in Croatia
  • Bass Highway, a state highway in Tasmania, Australia.
