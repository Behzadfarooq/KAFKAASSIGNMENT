The A2G EP by Blackalicious was released in 1999 by Quannum Projects and served as a precursor to Blackalicious' full length debut Nia, which shares three tracks with this EP. Perhaps the EP's most famous track is "Alphabet Aerobics", a collaboration with Jurassic 5's Cut Chemist.

The EP was also released on Mo' Wax with slightly different artwork.

[edit] Track listing

  1. "A to G"
  2. "Clockwork"
  3. "Rock the Spot"
  4. "Back to the Essence"
  5. "Deception"
  6. "Making Progress"
  7. "Alphabet Aerobics (The Cut Chemist 2½ Minute Workout)"

Tracks 1, 5 and 6 can also be found on Nia.
