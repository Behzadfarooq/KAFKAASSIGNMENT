[edit] About Me

My name —which was given by my parents and as in the birth certificate— is Andre Ardiantoro. But a few years ago on all of my social documents they put the last name of my father —Muliana— at the end. So, here we go: Andre Ardiantoro Muliana, a.k.a. amuliana. So, what the hack is a2xm? a2xm is just my own attractive modification of the abbreviation of my full name —AAM → "a" two times → a2x + "m" = a2xm– :-). I'm Chinese by race and Indonesian by birth.

  • Birthdate: 8 June 1971
  • Birthplace: Purbalingga, Indonesia.
  • Interests: Linux, Open source, Internet, Web, Books, Scuba diving, Photography, 3D computer graphics, Digital art, Home theater.
  • Hobbies: Swimming, Cycling, Jogging, Surfing the Web, Watching movies.

You can visit my website at a2xm.com to know more about me. My email is amuliana at gmail dot com.
