[edit] Catherine of Genoa

Hello, Aquilachrysaetos. It appears that you copied and pasted Catherine of Genoa to Saint Catherine of Genoa. Please do not move articles by copying and pasting them because it splits the article's history, which is needed for attribution and is helpful in many other ways. If there is an article that you cannot move yourself using the move link at the top of the page, follow the instructions at Wikipedia:Requested moves. Also, if there are any other articles that you copied and pasted, even if it was a long time ago, please list them at Wikipedia:Cut and paste move repair holding pen. Thank you, TeckWizBot 13:35, 19 March 2007 (UTC)

-- Pastordavid 00:53, 4 February 2007 (UTC)
The preferred form of article titles for saints, according to naming conventions (and also on the saints wikiproject), is without the word "saint" in the title. Please do not move article to add "saint" to the title. Thank you. -- Pastordavid 00:56, 4 February 2007 (UTC)
