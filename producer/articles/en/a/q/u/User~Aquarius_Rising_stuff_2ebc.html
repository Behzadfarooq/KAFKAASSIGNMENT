  • Actinides and lanthanides are collectively known as "rare earth elements" (deprecated). IUPAC does not assign them a group number.
  • Alkali metals, alkaline earth metals, transition metals, actinides, lanthanides, and poor metals are all collectively known as "metals".
  • Halogens and noble gases are also non-metals.

State at standard temperature and pressure

  • those with atomic number in red are gases
  • those with atomic number in green are liquids
  • those with atomic number in black are solid

Natural occurrence

  • those with solid borders have stable isotopes older than the Earth (Primordial elements).
  • those with dashed borders arise naturally from decay of other elements, and have no isotopes with half-life beyond or comparable to the age of the Earth (some can however be found in trace amounts in radioactive ores).
  • those with dotted borders are synthetic elements.
  • those without borders have not been discovered yet.
