[edit] Plot

 This Entourage-related article is a stub. You can help Wikipedia by expanding it.

Vince wants his mother to accompany him to the premiere of "Aquaman". The only problem is, she hates travelling. She agrees to come when Vince calls her on live radio, but later calls him from her car, parked outside the airport, and tells him she can't do it.

Meanwhile, Drama and Turtle give out all the invitations Vince has been permitted, only to then meet two girls who promise to sleep with them if they are invited. They desperately call Ari to get more tickets, and are eventually given two of James Woods'. James Woods finds out, and comes to the house, demanding that they be returned to him. Drama gives him an empty envelope, keeping the tickets himself.

Instead of using the invitations on the girls they met, Eric and Turtle come up with a plan to get Vince's mother to Los Angeles. All the boys' mothers fly over with her, and accompany their sons to the premiere.
