Aqua Knight (水中騎士, Suichuu Kishi?) is a manga by Yukito Kishiro.

[edit] Story

Spoiler warning: Plot and/or ending details follow.

The world of Marmundo is a big ocean, with very few islands, separated from the other half of the planet by a huge waterfall in which a dragon dwells. In one of the islands there's a mystical light, protected by a king, who says it's his wife, and his son who doesn't have a name (his father calls him "little one"); when a young knight, lady Ruhira, who has recently finished the academy and is on her way to prove herself as a brave aqua knight, happens to land on the island, the little boy decides to become her apprentice. The king wants to prove the young knight worthiness, and wearing his old suit (he was a knight too) challenges lady Ruhira to a fight; but while they're fighting the worst happens, and the light is stolen by the (evil?) genius Alcantara, who also takes the boy with him.
