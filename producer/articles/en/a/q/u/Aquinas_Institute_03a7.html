This article is about the high school in Rochester. For the Dominican seminary in St. Louis, see Aquinas Institute of Theology.

The Aquinas Institute is a Catholic, coeducational high school in Rochester, New York since 1902. It has stood at its current location on Dewey Avenue since 1925. Over 18,000 graduates have graduated since its founding. Notable alumni include Bob Wegman, Don Holleder, Dick Buerkle (former world record holder in the Indoor 1 mile)
