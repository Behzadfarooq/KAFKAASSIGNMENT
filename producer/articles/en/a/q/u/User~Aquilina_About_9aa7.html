This is my user page. Here's some stuff about me:

  • I have a user page, which I should use more
  • I edited for a long time anonymously.
  • I joined WP and got a username to translate scientific and sporting articles German <> English.
    • I have so far failed to do this.
  • I strangely quite enjoy copy-editing and playing hunt the tpyo.
  • I like lots of well-thought out headings.
  • I try to be a nice guy.
    • If I'm not, give me a slap.
  • I make lots of mistakes, though I try not to.
    • If I make a mistake, let me know and I'll give myself a slap.
  • I reserve the right to disagree with you, but not the right to edit-war.
    • I trust in the might of the Talk page and WP:RfM.
  • I try not to hate things, but I hate major edits marked as minor ones with a passion.
    • I believe no revert is a minor edit.
  • I edit more than I ought.
