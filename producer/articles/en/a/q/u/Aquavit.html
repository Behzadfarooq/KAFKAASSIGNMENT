Aquavit (from Latin aqua vitae = "water of life") can refer to:

  • akvavit, a scandinavian mid-strength drink (~45% alc). A potato or grain spirit distilled several times and flavored with caraway, anise, coriander, or citrus rind. List of Akvavit producers
  • okovita, a historic Polish/Ukrainian/Russian strong drink that is a precursor and/or a variant of vodka
  • Aquavit Restaurant is a Scandinavian Restaurant located in New York City. It was ranked as one of the top ten restaurants in New York City by New York Magazine. Its sister cafe, the A/Q Cafe, is located at Scandinavia House - The Nordic Center in America in New York City.
