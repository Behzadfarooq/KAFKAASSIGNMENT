Aqualung can refer to:

  • Aqua-lung (often spelled "aqualung" in the United Kingdom), a type of SCUBA diving equipment, and sometimes used generically to refer to SCUBA breathing apparatus. See also scuba set
  • Aqualung (album), an album by Jethro Tull, as well as a hit song, Aqualung, from that album
  • Aqualung (musician), the stage name of Matt Hales, a British musician
  • Aqualung (2002 album), an album by the aforementioned artist
  • Aqualung (venue), a concert venue in the Spanish city of Madrid
  • Aqualung The common name of a famous hobo in Charleston, WV. Name derived from the fact he looks like the man on the cover of the Jethro Tull album of the same name. See also Aqualung (album)
  • For a dinosaur, see its correct spelling Dryptosaurus aquilunguis
