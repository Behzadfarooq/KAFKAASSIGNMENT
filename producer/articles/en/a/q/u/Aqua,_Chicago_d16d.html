The Aqua is an 87-story skyscraper under construction in the Lakeshore East area of Chicago.[1] It will border North Columbus Drive and East Southwater Street.

[edit] Architecture

[edit] Architect

The architect is Jeanne Gang. This will be her first skyscraper project. It will also be the largest project ever awarded to an American firm headed by a woman.[2]

[edit] Design

The façade will be glass with undulating balconies which change slightly from floor to floor giving it a rippling effect. The balconies will extend as far as 12 feet (3.7 meters) from the glass wall in some places. [2]

The building will contain 55,000 square feet (5,100 square meter) of retail and office space, in addition to 210 hotels rooms (floors 1-18), 476 rental residential units (floors 40-52), and 263 condominium units (floors 53-80). Aqua will also be the first downtown building to combine condos, apartments and a hotel. Strategic Hotel & Report has agreed to acquire the first 15 floors of hotel space upon completion of the building. [3]
