[edit] Biography

Aquaria is a symphonic power metal band from Rio de Janeiro, Brazil. The band was formed in 1999, and was originally known as Uirapuru, under which moniker they released two demos: Here Comes The Life and Flames Of Trinity. In 2005, the band released their debut album Luxaeterna through Scarecrow Records / Marquee Avalon. As of the beginning of 2007, they are in the studio, recording its successor, tentatively due in mid-2007.

On January 24:th, 2007, the band announced the addition of guitarist Gustavo di Padua.
