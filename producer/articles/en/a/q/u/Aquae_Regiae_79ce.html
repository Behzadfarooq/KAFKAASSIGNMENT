The Diocese of Aquae Regiae is a Titular See of present-day Tunisia[1] in the Roman Catholic Church[1]. The first Titular Bishop was appointed to this See on 17 March, 1964. In Latin the diocese is known as Aquaregiensis. It is situated in the old Roman province of Byzacena.
