Aqueduct–North Conduit Avenue is a station on the IND Rockaway Line of the New York City Subway, served at all times by the A train. The station has two side platforms, four tracks (only the two outer ones are in revenue service), one mezzanine, and a cross-under at street level. The mezzanine and stairs to the full time area are at the extreme south end of this station. There is an additional exit-only at north end of Rockaway-bound platform that leads to the Aqueduct Racetrack. Evidence shows Long Island Rail Road-type exit steps near the south end. It currently has a chain link fence. The platforms are extra long, about 800 feet in length, which is 200 feet more than a standard IND platform length.
