Aqua Raiders

Aqua Raiders is one of the LEGO Group's new themes for 2007. The setting is in the Bermuda Triangle. A group of divers go down with high tech submarines and other equipment to look for lost treasure and shipwrecks. However, usually they run into sea creatures ranging from sea snakes to giant squid that they must fight in order to safely recover the treasure!

[edit] Set List

  • 7770-Deep Sea Treasure Hunter- Includes 2 sea snakes, diver, rocks, and submarine.
  • 7771-Angler Ambush- Includes Anglerfish, 2 divers, submarine ,and rock formation.
  • 7772-Lobster Strike- Includes giant lobster, 2 divers, and a rover.
  • 7773-Tiger Shark Attack- Includes tiger shark, 2 divers, submarine, and large harpoon gun.
  • 7774-Crab Crusher- Includes giant crab, 2 divers, large submarine, and 2 mini subs.
  • 7775-Aquabase Invasion- Includes giant squid, submarine, sea scooter, 4 divers, and the aquabase.
  • 7776-The Shipwreck- Includes 2 sea snakes, shipwreck, 2 divers, and submarine.
