[edit] Information

Hello. My name is Jennifer Perkins, but most people call me AquafireGal. I'm only 13 years of age but I am extremely intelligent for my age. My edits, submissions and/or page creations are often related to either television or film projects. I am a big fan of children's television. As for films, I am a big fan of horror and spoof movies and I dislike movie sequals.
