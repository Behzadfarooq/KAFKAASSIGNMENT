This happens to be a rather mediocre film according to Rotten Tomatoes and IMDB yet the reviews listed show the film in a very different light.

Yeah, the "production values" weren't very slick but after seeing it for the first time last night, I think it might become one of my top 10 favorite movies ever. It has everything: intimate friendship, realistic dialogue, beautiful scenery and actresses, unselfish devotion to "something greater than oneself", doddering villains. --Uncle Ed 14:51, 15 September 2006 (UTC)
