Aquellos años locos

Aquellos años locos (English language:Those Crazy Years) is a 1971 Argentine musical comedy film directed and written by Enrique Carreras with Norberto Aroldi. The film premiered on 30 July 1971 in Buenos Aires and starred Palito Ortega and Mercedes Carreras.
