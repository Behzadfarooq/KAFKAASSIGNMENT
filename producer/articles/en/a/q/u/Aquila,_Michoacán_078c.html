[edit] Aquila, Michoacan, Mexico

Aquila (ah-KEE-lah), town (2002 pop. 1,915), in extreme SW part of the Mexican state of Michoacán, 23 mi/43 km SE of Tecoman; 18°35'N 103°31'W. Elev. 989 ft/510 m. Harsh terrain, small farming, mainly for subsistence. On paved road S to Mexico Hwy. 200 (5 mi/11 km).

Aquila Municipality consists Ticla, Cuyatlan, Pascuales, Playa Azul, Mohawa, Tinicatita, know the ferel beauty of these areas. Surfing. Sleeping, sightseeing, relaxing, swimming, sunning. Start in Manzanillo, Colima.
