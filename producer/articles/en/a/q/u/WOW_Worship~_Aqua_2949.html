WOW Worship: Aqua is the 6th installment in the WOW Worship series. The collection features 33 powerful worship songs from today's top artists, 8 of which are brand new recordings. WOW Worship: Aqua is ideal for the both praise and worship lover and fans of popular artists in Christian music. The album reached #75 on the Billboard 200 chart. [1]
