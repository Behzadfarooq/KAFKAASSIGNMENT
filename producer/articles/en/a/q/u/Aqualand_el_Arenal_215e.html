Aqualand el Arenal is large waterpark located near the El Arenal beach on the south of Mallorca, the largest of Spain's Balearic Islands.

Aqualand El Arenal is a park of the Aspro Ocio group, which also has Aqualands in France and Portugal and amusement parks under various other names scattered throughout western Europe.

The park features over a score of rides, all water-oriented and most being variations of water slides (Grand Canyon, Devil's Tail, Kamikazee, Black Hole, and others) and also attractions such as Polynesia and Surf Beach, and areas of gardens and trees.
