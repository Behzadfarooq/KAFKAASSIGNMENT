Main article: Aquinas College, Perth


Aquinas College is a Catholic college for boys which was established in 1938. It is located in Salter Point, a suburb of Perth, Western Australia.

It currently has many clubs, associations and groups which are subsidaries or, departments within which have grown to such an extent, they have their own pages.

Since navagating through endless related pages is a hassle when a specific group is needed, this category will assist in finding the article you are looking for.
