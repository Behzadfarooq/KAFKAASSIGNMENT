Aquilino Lopez is a relief pitcher who played in the Major Leagues from 2003 to 2005.

[edit] Career

Lopez was first signed by the Seattle Mariners on July 3 1997 and made his Major League debut on April 2 2003 after being signed by the Toronto Blue Jays on 16 December 2002 as a rule 5 draftee. He made 72 appearances in 2003 for the Blue Jays, posting a 3.42 ERA over 73.2 innings.

In 2004 Aquilino was still in Toronto where he pitched only 21 innings in 18 appearances. Since then Aquilino has been involved with the Los Angeles Dodgers and the Philadelphia Phillies organisations, mostly as a Minor League player and has not pitched at the Major League level at all in 2006.

Lopez currently plays for the AAA Portland Beavers in the San Diego Padres organization.
