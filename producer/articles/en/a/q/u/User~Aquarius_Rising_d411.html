Aquarius Rising has been an editor on English Wikipedia since July 2005.

He is a Fellow of The Institute of Wikipediology.

Most of his edits are in the subject areas of popular culture / law / education / occupations / religion / New Age / Canadian Studies.

Besides doing substantive edits to content, Aquarius Rising has made numerous edits to articles by adding categories, article links and image links and has made numerous redirect pages where they were lacking.
