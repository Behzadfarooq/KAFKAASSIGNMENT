Aquatics at the 2006 Commonwealth Games

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

You have new messages (last change).

The Aquatics at the 2006 Commonwealth Games were held at the Melbourne Sports and Aquatic Centre (MSAC) and were divided into 3 separate disciplines.

  • Diving
  • Swimming
  • Synchronized swimming

which were then split into individual events as follows:

Men's Diving: 1 m springboard | 3 m springboard | 10 m platform | synchronise platform
Men's Swimming 50 m freestyle | 50 m freestyle (EAD) | 100 m freestyle | 100 m freestyle (EAD) | 200 m freestyle | 400 m freestyle | 1500 m freestyle | 50 m backstroke | 100 m backstroke | 200 m backstroke | 50 m breaststroke | 100 m breaststroke | 200 m breaststroke | 50 m butterfly | 100 m butterfly | 200 m butterfly | 200 m individual medley | 400 m individual medley | 4 x 100 m medley relay


Women's Diving: 1 m springboard | 3 m springboard | 10 m platform | synchronise platform
Women's Swimming 50 m freestyle | 50 m freestyle (EAD) | 100 m freestyle | 100 m freestyle (EAD) | 200 m freestyle | 400 m freestyle | 800 m freestyle | 50 m backstroke | 100 m backstroke | 200 m backstroke | 50 m breaststroke | 100 m breaststroke | 200 m breaststroke | 50 m butterfly | 100 m butterfly | 200 m butterfly | 200 m individual medley | 400 m individual medley | 4 x 100 m medley relay
Synchronised Swimming Technical solo | Technical duet | Free solo | Free Duet
