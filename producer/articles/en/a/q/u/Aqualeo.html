Aqualeo is a Houston based hip hop duo signed to Swishahouse Records. One of its members, Acie, was the winner of the UPN reality show, "The Player." The other member of the group is an artist named Priceless who is lesser known. They currently have an album out entitled Star-Struck. The name was coined by the group to represent the two fixed masculine signs in the zodiac, the Leo and the Aquarius. The lion, which represents the Leo, is leading a spiritual revival of thought intertwined with knowledge in the Age of Aquarius. Coming from the city known for its Rockets, Comets, Astros, and NASA, not to mention it being the Lone Star State, you understand that this is not a duplication of anything, but an accumulation of everything. Aqualeo brings a dynamic and versatile style to the Swisha House. They touch on a wide range of subjects over a broad spectrum of beats. Their teaser track Cadillac appeals to the core of southern fans, however they can quickly switch to a song like the first single Samson. "Samson" shows the range and versatility of the groups by combining Southern Rap, Hip Hop and Rock into a sound we call Aquasiatic.
