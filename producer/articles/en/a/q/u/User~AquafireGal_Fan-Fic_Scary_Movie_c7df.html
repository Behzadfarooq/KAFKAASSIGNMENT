Scary Movie (2004),is a American film directed by Keenen Ivory Wayans, is a comedy-gross-out film spoof that parodies the horror and mystery genres. Several horror movies are spoofed, including Scream, The Ring, I Know What You Did Last Summer, Hannibal, House of Wax, and Child's Play.
