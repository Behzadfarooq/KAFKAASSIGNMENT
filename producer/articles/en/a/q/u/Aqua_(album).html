Aqua is the fourth studio album by the progressive rock band Asia, released in 1992. The lineup changed a little bit since Astra in 1985. The new lineup is composed of John Payne (bassist/vocalist) and Al Pitrelli (guitarist), together with founder Geoffrey Downes (keyboardist/vocalist). Carl Palmer (drums) and Steve Howe (guitarist), both also co-founders of the band, guest.
