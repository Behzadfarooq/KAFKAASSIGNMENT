The Aqaba Flagpole in Aqaba, Jordan is the tallest free standing flagpole in the world.

The Aqaba Flagpole in Aqaba, Jordan is the tallest free standing flagpole in the world at a height of 132 meters (430 feet) high.[citation needed].

The Aqaba Flagpole carries the flag of the Arab Revolt.
