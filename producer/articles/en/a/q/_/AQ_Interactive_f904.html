AQ Interactive

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

AQ Interactive (JASDAQ: 3838) is a Japanese game developer and publisher, the create of video games such as Cry On and Vampire's Rain. AQ stands for Artistic Quality.

[edit] History

AQ Interactive was founded on October 1, 2005 to publish games for Artoon, Cavia and Feel Plus. To date, it has released the Xbox 360 launch title Tetris: The Grand Master.

It is currently also working on an action game and a game based off a major comic license, Hajime no Ippo The Fighting, both for Nintendo's Wii[1].
