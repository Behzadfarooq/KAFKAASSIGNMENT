ABV-2

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

ABV-2 is a television station in Melbourne, Victoria. It is affiliated with the Australian Broadcasting Corporation. The station began broadcasting on 18 November 1956. The studios are in Southbank and Elsternwick, and transmitter at Mount Dandenong.
