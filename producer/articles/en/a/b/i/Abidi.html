Abidi or Abedi is the surname for a family belonging to the offsprings of Holy prophet Muhammad's great-grandson Imam Zain-ul-Abideen or Imam Abid whose real name was Ali ibn Husayn. Abidis (or people belonging to this family) can be found all over the world especially in Iran and Indo-Pak subcontinent.
