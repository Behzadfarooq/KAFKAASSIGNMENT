The Abitibi Eskimos are a Tier II Junior "A" ice hockey team from Iroquois Falls, Ontario, Canada. They are a part of the Northern Ontario Junior Hockey League.

The Eskimos will be hosting the 2007 Dudley Hewitt Cup.
