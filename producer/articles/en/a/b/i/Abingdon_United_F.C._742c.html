Abingdon United F.C. are an English football club based in Abingdon, Oxfordshire and currently playing in the Southern League.

The club was founded in 1946 as a rival to the already existing Abingdon Town at the Anchor pub on the bank of the River Thames. After an initial 8 years in the North Berks League, United set their sights higher and entered the Hellenic League. In the 2006-07 season they will be playing in the newly-formed Southern League Division One South and West.

[edit] Honours

  • Hellenic League Premier Division: Runners-Up 1996-97
  • Hellenic League Division One: Winners 1981-82
  • North Berks League: Winners 1953-54
  • Hellenic League Premier Division Cup: Runners-Up 1989-90
  • Hellenic League Division One Cup: Winners 1965-66; Runners-Up 1966-67
  • Berks & Bucks Senior Cup: Runners-Up 1983-83, 2004-05
  • Berks & Bucks Senior Trophy: Winners 1996-97, 1997-98; Runners-Up 1993-94
  • North Berks Charity Shield: Winners 1952-53
  • Hellenic League Floodlit Cup: Winners 1996-97
  • Hellenic - Hungerford Cup: Winners 1996-97, 2001-02
