Ability Plus Software is a global software company, the producer of several low-cost yet full-featured applications. The company's flagship product is Ability Office Professional[2], the premier edition of their award-winning office suite. Ability's worldwide software distribution is managed by Formjet.

[edit] History

The company was founded in the UK, in 1985, and by the end of that decade, had over a quarter of a million registered users. The company's early success was demonstrated when it won the Barclays Bank British Micro Computing Award, in 1987.
