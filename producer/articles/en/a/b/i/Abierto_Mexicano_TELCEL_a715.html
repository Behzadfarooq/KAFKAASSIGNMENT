Abierto Mexicano TELCEL

The Abierto Mexicano TELCEL is a tennis tournament held in Acapulco, Mexico. It is an event on both the ATP (International Series Gold event) and the WTA (Tier III). It has been on the ATP tour since 1993, and began on the WTA in 2001.
