Abigail Elizabeth Mavity (born 4 March 1993 in Arizona) is an American actress who has appeared on a number of television series and commercials, as well as in feature films.

[edit] Biography

This section is a stub. You can help by expanding it.

Abigail Elizabeth Mavity was born as the youngest of ten children (her siblings are: Kevin, Shannon, Sara, Jake, Emily, Justin, Matt, Nate, and Nick). In March 1999 she moved from Arizona to Los Angeles to further her acting career. She has appeared regularly in various films and television shows, as well as numerous commercial roles as well as voiceover roles She was nominated for a Young Artist Award in 2003 for her role as Kathy Martin on the series Haunted.
