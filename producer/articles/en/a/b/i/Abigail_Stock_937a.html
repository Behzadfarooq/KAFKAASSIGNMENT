Abigail Stock is a fictional character in the television series Skins. She is played by Georgina Moffat.

Spoiler warning: Plot and/or ending details follow.

[edit] Characterisation

Abigail is a posh school girl with psychotic tendencies. She is Tony Stonem's mistress. Like her brother, Josh Stock, she is psychoanalysed and medicated by her mother who runs a rehabilitation clinic. She displays an incredible suppressed range and desire to be liked. Her wealth comes with arrogance, and she refers to Tony's girlfriend disparagingly as a "chav", a characterization which Michelle does not fit. Her personality can be erratic and she finds herself willing to do anything Tony asks.

[edit] Character history

In "Tony", she meets Tony at a choir rehearsal at her school where he is singing, and invites him and his friends to her party. She reappears in "Jal", seen kissing Tony who is in a relationship with Michelle at the time. In "Sid", she publicly makes out with Tony at a concert so Michelle would see. Michelle finds her, with Tony holding her breasts, and confronts her. Abigail explodes and angrily spews "I'll kill you, you fucking flat-chested, cock-sucking, spastic horse fucker!"

In "Michelle", after breaking up with Tony over his infidelity with Maxxie, Michelle comes to confront Abigail about it. Abigail's brother, Josh, restrains her and eventually begins a short-lived relationship with Michelle. Angry, Tony asks Abigail to pose naked for him, which she does willingly. He then sends the pictures of Abi to Michelle using Josh's phone, freaking her out.

Despite being credited in the main cast, Georgina Moffat does not appear in the series finale.
