Abid Qayyum Raja (born November 5, 1975) is a Norway Scholar at Wadham College at the University of Oxford.

Raja is a lawyer in Norway and also serves on a government advisory council. He frequently appears on debate programs on TV and regularly writes op-eds in national newspapers. Originally he became known to the public as the spokesman for Norway`s muslim community. Raja is a member of Venstre, the liberal party in Norway, and is running for a seat on the regional council in [Akershus] in the 2007 local election.
