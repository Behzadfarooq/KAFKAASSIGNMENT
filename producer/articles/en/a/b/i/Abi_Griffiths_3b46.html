Abi Griffiths

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Abi Griffiths (born in December 1978) is a British television presenter.

Abi attended the prestigious Millfield School, followed by a BA(hons) in drama from Loughborough University in the UK. Abi has also attended numerous TV Presenting courses at various institutions.

Whilst studying at Loughborough University Abi worked as a runner on Live & Kicking and The National Lottery Show.

[edit] Australian work

After completing her studies Abi moved to Sydney, Australia where she became a television presenter working on The Weather Channel, Not The Movie Show (Channel 31, Australia), and Whatuwant (Channel V, Australia)

[edit] Home & Away

Whilst in Australia Abi also won a role in Home & Away as a British backpacker.


[edit] UK Presenting Work

Since her return to the UK Abi has continued presenting, working for Bid2Win, Quiz TV, The Great British Quiz, and The Making of Dirty Dancing, amongst others. She also works for Sky Sports doing pits interviews for their coverage of the Speedway Elite League.
