Abiko Station (我孫子駅 あびこ駅, Abiko eki?, M27) is a train station located toward the southern end of the Osaka Municipal Subway Midosuji Line, as well as Abikocho Station on the JR Hanwa Line in Sumiyoshi-ku, Osaka, Japan. Abiko is situated on the Southern city limits, with the Yamato River separating it from Sakai city. Abikocho Station is the nearest stop for Osaka City University.
