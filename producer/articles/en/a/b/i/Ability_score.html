Ability score

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

An ability score, in the d20 System, is a numeric description of a character's physical and mental characteristics. Scores for characters range typically range from 3 to 16, but can be much higher.

The ability scores are Strength, Dexterity, Constitution, Intelligence, Wisdom, and Charisma.
