Abiels Ledge is a small barren rock located in Buzzards Bay, within Massachusetts. The rock is just off the southeast coast of Stony Point Dike, near Hog Island Channel and within the town limits of Wareham. This is a good place to catch stripers when they are running[1]

(Coordinates: Lat. = 41.692'N, Lon. = 70.673'W)
