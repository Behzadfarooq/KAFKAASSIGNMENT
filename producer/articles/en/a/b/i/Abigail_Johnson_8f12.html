Abigail Johnson

Abigail Johnson (born January 7, 1962) is an American financial businessperson. She runs Fidelity Investments together with her father Edward Johnson. From the 2006 annual list of 400 richest Americans Abigail Johnson ranks 16th with $13 billion. She was ranked as the 28th richest person in the world in 2005 and the 5th richest woman by Forbes magazine's list of the world's richest people. Forbes calculated her net worth in 2005 at $12 billion.

Johnson is an alumna of Buckingham Browne & Nichols. She earned a bachelor's degree at William Smith College. She later received an MBA from Harvard University. She lives in Milton, Massachusetts.
