Abigail Bryant was born in November, 1991, and regularly competes in science compettitions in her home state of Florida.

[edit] Science Fair

[edit] Thomas Alva Edison Regional Kiwanis Science and Engeneering Fair

Ms. Bryant has so far competed and won in 3 consecutive years at the Thomas Alva Edison Regional Kiwanis Science and Engeneering Fair. In 2007 she receved first place in the Evironmental Analisis category with her project entitled A Study of the Effects of Airborne Volcanic Particulates on the Photosynthesis and Photomorphogenisis of Food Crops

Photosynthesis is the process by which plants make food. Photomorphogenisis is the response of plants to light.


[edit] Science Olympiad

Ms. Bryant is a member of the Science olympiad Team at Dunbar High School.
