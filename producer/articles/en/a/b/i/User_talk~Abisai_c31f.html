[edit] Edit summaries

Hi! I just saw your edit to Matt Anderson. When you make significant changes (or even if you don't!), it's very useful to other users if you take a moment to add an edit summary. I notice that many of your edits don't indicate what you changed. It's also easier to tell quickly which edits are (not) vandalism. Thanks, and happy editing! It's always nice to find a baseball fan on Wikipedia. -Phoenixrod 04:33, 8 June 2006 (UTC)
