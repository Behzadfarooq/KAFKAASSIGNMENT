The Abilene Zoological Gardens is a small zoo located in Abilene, Texas.

Their signature exhibit consists of a bridge crossing over a giraffe habitat, allowing visitors to pet and feed the giraffes.

The Abilene Zoo offers a family pass program on an annual basis. They also participate in a national reciprocal membership program, allowing members to obtain free or reduced admissions at many other zoos.
