Abila is the name of several places of antiquity:

cities
  • Abila (Decapolis), also called Seleucia, currently ca. 13 km northeast of Irbid, Jordan
  • Abila Lysaniou, capital of ancient Abilene, ca. 20 km northwest of Damascus, Syria
  • Abila (Peraea), northeast of the northern shore of the Dead Sea, currently in northwestern Jordan
  • Abila, a Latin name for Ávila, Spain
mountains
  • Abila Mons, the southern of the Pillars of Hercules on the African side of the Strait of Gibraltar, now Monte Hacho, in the Spanish enclave of Ceuta
