Abitibi 70, Ontario

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Abitibi 70 (2006 population 114) ,also known as Wahgoshig or just Abitibi, is an indian reserve on Lake Abitibi in northeastern Ontario, Canada. It is located in Cochrane District.

According to the Canada 2006 Census:

* Population: 114 (-10.2% from 2001)
* Land area: 78.7 km²
* Population density: 1.4 people/km²
* National population rank (Out of 5,008): Ranked 4,364th
* Median age:† N/A (males: N/A, females: N/A)
* Total private dwellings: 39
* Dwellings occupied by permanent residents: 37
