Abbas is a given name, that means 'austere' in Arabic. (Austere: (1) severely simple in appearance. (2) strict, stern)

Abbas or Abbass may refer to:

  • Abbas ibn 'Abdul Muttalib (c.566–c.653), a paternal uncle of Muhammad
  • Abbas ibn Ali, son of Ali ibn Abi Talib and Ummal Baneen; revered in Sh'ia Islam for his loyalty to Husayn ibn Ali, the third Sh'ia Imam
  • Abbas Mirza (1789–1833), a Qajar crown prince of Persia
  • Abbas I of Egypt (1813–1854), Pasha of Egypt
  • Abbas II of Egypt (1874–1944) aka "Abbas Hilmi Pasha", last khedive of Egypt
  • Abbas I of Persia (1571–1629) aka "Abbas the Great", Persian Shah
  • Abbas (photographer) (born 1944), Iranian photojournalist
  • `Abdu'l-Bahá (Sir `Abdu'l-Bahá `Abbás Effendí) (1844–1921), second head of the Bahá'í Faith after his father, Founder of the Faith
  • Ali ibn Abbas al-Majusi (died 994), a Persian scientist
