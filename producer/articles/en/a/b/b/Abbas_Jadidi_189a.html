Abbas Jadidi (born January 13, 1969) is an Iranian wrestler who competed in the Freestyle Heavyweight (90-100 kg) category at the 1996 Summer Olympics, lost controversally to Kurt Angle and won the silver medal.

[edit] Achievements

  • Olympic Silver 1996 Atlanta
  • World Champion 1998
  • World Bronze 1995, 1999
  • World Cup Champion 1998
  • Asian Champion 1993, 1996 1998

[edit] Trivia

Jadidi was once mentioned in a promo by Kurt Angle during his rivalry with Samoa Joe. Angle said he once wrestled an Iranian man during the 1996 Summer Olympics. He talked about doing the honorable thing, and giving the man a rematch; much like the rematch he gave Joe after he ended his undefeated streak.
