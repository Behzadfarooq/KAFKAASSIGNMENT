ABBA Live is a live album by Swedish pop group ABBA, released in 1986 (see 1986 in music). When this LP/CD was released, most interest in ABBA had faded, and this album did not perform very well internationally or domestically. Nevertheless, it still remains the only live album put out by the group. ABBA Live was remastered in 1997.
