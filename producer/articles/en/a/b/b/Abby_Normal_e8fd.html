Abby Normal

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Abby Normal is a musician from North Carolina. He has been performing since 1992, and is best known for his work with Frankenstein Drag Queens From Planet 13 and The Graveyard Boulevard, though he has successfully performed as both a solo artist, and with other bands.

[edit] Musical Career

In 1992, Normal became one of the founding members of Maniac Spider Trash, alongside Wednesday 13, Sicko Zero and Michael Patrick. The band existed until 1996, when they disbanded to allow the formation of Frankenstein Drag Queens From Planet 13 (FDQ). He was with FDQ until 2001.

In 2001, when FDQ disbanded, Normal took some time to record a solo effort, entitled "Midnight Creature Feature Picture Show".

In 2003, alongside other ex-FDQ members Seaweed and Sicko Zero, Normal formed The Graveyard Boulevard.

[edit] Trivia

Normal's name, in light of his association with a Frankenstein-named band, evokes the 1974 film Young Frankenstein, where the character sent to steal a particular brain from an organ bank settles for stealing one labeled "ABNORMAL - Do not use", and diffidently recalls it as being that of "Abby someone".

His album "Midnight Creature Feature Picture Show" evokes the 1975 Frankenstein-evoking film The Rocky Horror Picture Show and its opening song, "Late Night Science Fiction Double Feature Picture Show".
