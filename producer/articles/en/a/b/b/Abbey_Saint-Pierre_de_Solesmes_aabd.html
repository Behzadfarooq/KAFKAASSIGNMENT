Abbey St. Peter in Solesmes

The Abbey Saint-Pierre de Solesmes (Sarthe) is a french Benedictine monastery in Solesmes founded in 1010.

This Abbey is one of the most famous in the world, thanks to the work of Dom Gueranger, who restored in 1833 the Order of Saint Benedict and monastic life in France after it was abolished by the Revolution. It is also a center of the Roman Catholic liturgy and of the Gregorian chant.
