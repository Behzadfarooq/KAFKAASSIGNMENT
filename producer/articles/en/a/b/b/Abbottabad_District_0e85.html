Abbottabad (Urdu: ایبٹ آباد ) is a district in the North-West Frontier Province of Pakistan. It contains the cities of Abbottabad, Havelian Birote Bakote and Kohala (Circle Bakote).It is a beautiful city. Pakistan Military Academy Kakul is also in this city.

[edit] Origin of name

Abbottabad is named after Major James Abbott, an administrator of Hazara Division, a region that contained this district.

[edit] Famous Peaks

Miranjani and Mukeshpuri are the highest peaks in Nathiagali in this district.

[edit] Subdivisions

[edit] Areas of interest

A prominent area of Abbottabad district is Ayubia National Park in the town of Ayubia - it is a major nature reserve, and has many tourists every year.
