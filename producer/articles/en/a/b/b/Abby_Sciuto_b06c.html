Abigail "Abby" Sciuto ("shoe-toe") is a fictional forensic scientist in the NCIS television series by CBS Television, portrayed by Pauley Perrette.

[edit] Personality

A quirky technogeek in the mold of The X-Files' Lone Gunmen, Abby is best known for her gothic style of dress, with at least nine tattoos on her neck, arms, back, ankle and other places which are only hinted at. Her style of clothing is probably best described as Spice Goth. Spice Goths often wear short skirts and platform boots. The hairstyle (two high ponytails) is named 'My Little Pony hair'. She also states several times that she sleeps in a coffin, and in episode Hiatus, she says she has a hearse, which has flat tires all the time. She has a happy-go-lucky attitude and has a sweet personality. Abby is a hearing child of deaf parents and likes her music loud.

[edit] Background

Abby is from New Orleans, Louisiana. Abby graduated with full honors from LSU with a triple major in Sociology, Criminology and Psychology. She earned her Master's Degree from Georgia State University in Criminology and Forensic Science.

Abby is extremely well-versed in her field of study, rarely stumped by the evidentiary puzzles which Gibbs' team present to her; she has been shown to exhibit an almost religious zeal for the field of forensics. Also, she does all the work on the evidence herself, which shows that she is extremely good at her job. She also once said, as a threat against McGee, 'I am one of the few people in the world who can murder you and leave no forensic evidence'. She was in the science club in high school. She hates being called Abigail, but will tolerate it from Ducky in certain situations.

[edit] Relationships

She gets on well with all the rest of the team, particularly Tony and Gibbs and is fond of Ducky who refers to her as "Abigail". She had a relationship with McGee which she stated that she would like to keep casual. Although the relationship ended early in season two, she has exhibited jealousy over the attention that McGee gives to Ziva.

[edit] Traits

She is able to communicate in American Sign Language — as can Gibbs. She invented a new technique to clean up surveillance video in the episode "Terminal Leave". She considers submitting a paper entitled Digital Optical Enhancement of Reflective Surfaces with Scattered Refractive Properties to a forensics journal. She is able to manufacture fake IDs easily, according to the episode "Split Decision": "Please Gibbs, I've been making fake IDs since I was 15." This hints at a misspent youth. She was arrested in 1990 for indecent exposure at a Rolling Stones concert in Los Angeles. Abby is very computer savvy; on par with McGee's abilities.

Abby is addicted to a drink called Caf-Pow, which appears to be orange or pink in color; possibly a soda. The rest of the team are fully aware of this and frequently bribe her with large cups of it.

Abby uses the IM screenname "abbsolute" (as seen in the Season 4 episode "Skeletons").

Abby also has a stuffed collar-wearing hippo named Bert, who farts (introduced in "Twilight", and brought back in Season 3 episodes "Silver War", "Ravenous" and "Untouchable").

During the time the team was having trauma-induced hallucinations of deceased agent Caitlin Todd, Abby's imaginary Caitlin appeared as a goth figure with long, pure white hair.

[edit] Family and Boyfriends

  • She is fluent in sign language because her parents are deaf.
  • Abby has an uncle named Larry, a grandmother who was an Olympic swimmer and a younger brother (no name).
  • She has an impressive list of ex-boyfriends, including a gemologist (Benjamin) and a NASA satellite technician (Ashton).
  • She was briefly stalked by an ex-boyfriend (Mikel Mawher) who was caught in the episode "Bloodbath". She gave him a straitjacket for his birthday.

[edit] Trivia

  • Pauley Perrette was born in Metairie, Louisiana, just outside New Orleans.
  • Perrette also has a Master's Degree in Criminology, and has toured the real NCIS offices to meet with the real forensic specialist.
  • While some of Abby's tattoos are real, the large cross on the back is makeup added for the rare occasions Perrette shows her bare back on camera.[citation needed]
