[edit] A Very Brief Autobiography

Hello all. I'm a 16 year old student attending Phillips Academy.
I adore Musical theatre, but I am perhaps a wee bit pretentious about it. I can't stand people who think the genre starts at Rent and stops at Wicked.
I despise short sleeved turtlenecks, because they make no sense.
I enjoy NYT crossword puzzles, baking cookies, late night phone calls, and cuddling perfect strangers with wild abandon.
