Abby Hayes

Abby Hayes is a sixth-grade student, the protagonist of a series of novels by Anne Mazer.

[edit] Family

Abby is one of four children. Abby has two older siblings, Isabel and Eva Hayes. They are twins and they fight a lot. Isabel loves history and where as Eva is a sports star. Abby often refers to them as her Super Sibs. Abby also has a younger brother named Alex Hayes.Alex loves electronics, especially his computer. Alex is also a genius in math. Abby's parents are Olivia and Paul Hayes. Olivia is a full-time lawyer and a mother of four while Paul has his own webpage company. While Olivia works at her law firm, Paul often stays at home.

[edit] Journal

Abby has kept a journal since just before fifth-grade. The novels contain traditional third-person writing intermixed with portions of Abby's journal written in the first person. Since Abby's favorite color is Purple, she writes in Purple ink. However, starting in "Everything is New Under the Sun" it is revealed that Abby's purple pen ran out of ink. Therefore, the rest of the books are in black ink.

[edit] School

[edit] Fifth Grade

In the fifth grade, Abby goes to Lancaster Clark Elementary School. Abby's favorite teacher is her creative writing teacher, Ms. Bunder, who gave her the nickname "Purple Hayes". Ms. Bunder comes to Abby's class every Thursday. Mrs. Kantor is her regular teacher who is new to the school.

[edit] Sixth Grade

Abby goes to Susan B. Anthony Middle School, where one of her most favorite teachers, Ms. Bean, works as an art teacher. She is similar and friends with Ms. Bunder. Abby is very different then in fifth grade. Abby had the courage to get pierced ears on her own,and she has a new sense of fashion. She makes a new friend, Sophia, who is very good at art and such.

[edit] Peers

Jessica: Jessica is Abby's best friend. Before she moved to Oregon she enjoyed astronomy, and smiley face buttons. However, when she came to visit Abby in "Somethings Never Change" she is a complete new person. Abby stated that Jessica now dresses similarily to Brianna and is a "party person".

Hannah: Hannah is the girl Abby befriends after Jessica moves. She has a younger sister, and lives near Abby's (old) house. Hannah is currently Abby's new best friend.

Natalie:Natalie is another one of Abby's best friends. She loves Harry Potter and doing science experiments. She also enjoys acting ever since she scored the lead in Peter Pan in the book, "Reach for the Stars." In 6th grade, Natalie loses some contact with Abby as she begins to join the jazz band. Some say that she may have turned into one of the emos.

Bethany: Bethany enjoy animals especially her pet hamster Blondie whom she talks about often. She and Abby become close friends after Brianna ditches Bethany for Victoria. Before they were friends Abby often referred to Bethany as a Brianna clone.

Sophia:Sophia is Abby's 6th grade friend. She often carries a sketch book everywhere, and eats with Abby at lunch everyday since Hannah has a different lunch period.

Brianna: Brianna is one of the people Abby hates. Abby often records how many times Brianna brags in her journal. Brianna often brags about her clothes and her talents. She claims to have many contacts, and have her family or herself on TV. In 6th grade, she ditches Victoria for a whole new crowd of friends. She also becomes a bit nicer.

Victoria: Victoria is Brianna's best friend after she ditches Bethany. Like her best friend Brianna, she is also well-dressed, although not as talented. Not only is she the snobbiest girl in school, but is also Abby's archnemisis. Victoria often includes the word "like" in her sentences, as well as "totally".

Mason: Mason was known as "The-Big-Burper" in fifth grade. However, in 6th grade according to Abby he shot up and slimmed down. Mason helps Abby sell her cookies in "That's the Way the Cookie Crumbles". Mason becomes one of Abby's best friends in middle school.

Casey Hoffman: Casey is Abby's best friend who's a boy. He calls her "Hayes" and Abby calls him "Hoffman." He and Abby enjoy shooting hoops together. They became friends in the 5th grade while doing a science project. Brianna and Victoria tease them about being a "couple"

Simon: Simon is Abby's first crush. He is in the 7th grade and has brown, spiky hair and bright blue eyes. He also plays the saxophone and is in the jazz band with Natalie. Simon is also very popular, an athlete, yet barely knows that Abby exists. It is possible that Simon may have a crush on Abby as well.

Zach: Zach is one of Abby's 5th grade classmates. He is obsessed with computer and video games. It is revealed that Brianna likes Zach, and that Zach might like Natalie on account of him "flirting" with her all the time.

Tyler: Tyler is Zach's best friend, and is just as obsessed as computer and video games as Zach. In the beginning, Bethany was revealed to like Tyler.

[edit] The Amazing Days of Abby Hayes books

Fifth Grade:

  1. Every Cloud Has a Silver Lining-This is the first book in the series. Abby is entering 5th grade, and wants to be on the soccer team.
  2. The Declaration of Independence
  3. Reach for the Stars-Abby tries to get a good role in the schools play of Peter Pan. Instead, she is found with the part of the crummy narrator.
  4. Have Wheels, Will Travel- Abby tries to earn money to buys new rollerblades because her sisters old ones stink. She ends up getting all her money by having a garage sale.
  5. Look Before You Leap- Abby must hide her new cat from her family.
  6. The Pen Is Mightier Than the Sword- Abbys class writes a newspaper and her job is being the advice columnist.
  7. Two Heads Are Better Than One- Abby and Casey are partners for a school science project. Unfortunately they can't decide what to do and spend most of their time arguing.
  8. The More, The Merrier- Abby is throwing a party, and decides to surprise her family and friends by painting everything in her room bright purple.
  9. Out of Sight, Out of Mind- Abby's best friend Jessica goes away to Oregon to live with her father. Abby says she will take care of Jessica's babysitting job. Abby gets fired, because she forgot about the babysitting. Soon, Jessica finds out.
  10. Everything New Under the Sun- Abby's grandmother invited her to visit. Her cousin, Cleo, is going to be there too. At first they don't get along very well until they work together making a book.
  11. Too Close for Comfort- Abby's family goes camping for the summer and she meets a new friend named Hannah.
  12. Good Things Come in Small Packages- Abby's new friend Hannah has moved to the neighborhood. Her class has a project for the holidays. They are supposed to make gifts for poor families, but Ms.Kantor (their teacher) has to go, so they are left with a mean teacher named Ms.Lee who is disliked by the whole class. Abby and her friends have plans, but Brianna has it taken care of, with her "words of persuasion".
  13. Some Things Never Change- Abby's best friend Jessica comes back for a visit, but Jessica isn't the Jessica Abby once knew. She doesn't like astronomy, overalls,and smiley face buttons. Jessica (Jessy) only likes "mad good" parties, boys, and girly things. Abby doesn't think she and Jessica have much in common.

Sixth Grade:

  1. It's Music to My Ears- Abby doesn't understand why girls are so obsessed with boys. Abby doesn't get it until she meets Simon, a saxophone player with bright blue eyes. Abby will do anything to get him to notice her.
  2. Now You See It, Now You Don't- Abby is starting to feel comfortable in Middle School. But there is a new thief who likes stealing the thing you value most. Abby is on the case until she figures out that the thief is someone she knows.
  3. That's the Way the Cookie Crumbles- Abby is offered to go to a writing camp but it costs too much money. She made cookies for some of her friends, and they all loved it. She gets the idea of selling cookies and her friend Mason decides to help but they both get detention from the principal Dr.Trane. Abby is sick of detention, but when she told her parents they didn't get mad. Instead, they told her about how they got in trouble before too.
  4. Home is Where the Heart is - Oliva Hayes is offered a new job at work, so now the Hayeses are moving. Abby leaving her purple paradise, but she is moving into the same neighborhood as Brianna!

[edit] Super Specials

  1. The Best Is Yet to Come- Abby and the rest are finally graduating 5th grade!
  2. Knowledge Is Power- Abby and her friends enter 6th grade. Abby gets her ears pierced (finally) secretly, and meets a new friend.
