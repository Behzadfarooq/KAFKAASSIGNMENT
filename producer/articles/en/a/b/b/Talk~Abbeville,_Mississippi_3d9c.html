[edit] Turning Point of Global Media

Presentation through communication media to various communities directly and other wise creatively, the necessity and importance of conservation of organic and inorganic matter in the world, is another.Affording a training to media artists in this respect.Representing human rights of media artists and all others.. Irrespective of any bar.. we respect all religions and entire humanity.Laying of a main foundation to erase.....off persecution and world terrorism through mass media.Causing through well planed endeavor to exchange among each other all religious back grounds, all social design, all Nationals, every cast and creed, Their music, drama, literature another media cultures.

The media personnel and artistes throughout the world hold a big responsibility in this regard. A responsibility that exceeds all bars and differences, such as nationalities, nation boundaries, marginal conflicts, linguistic and all sorts of other conflicts down to cast and creed. This is solely a humane responsibility. The time has set in to establish peace and its values and to build a world –wide concern among the people of today. It is never by terror alone that terrorism could be overcome...

My endeavor is to create a voice for the people who face torture and masses that are massacred by their own species. To raise against them to eliminate such brutal In addition to the human being facing such disasters I wish to raise a voice for the innocent creatures, the beasts of the earth, the fishes of the waters and fowl of the air,

Who are forced to sacrifice their lives, in tens of thousands every day to fill the bellies of man and to satisfy his greed, Of man, to nourish him and to satisfy his greed. Even at this very moment.

Single-handed I am not at all in a position to step forward to stop this disaster as an individual. Hence I eagerly invite you who spare a couple of minutes from your precious time to glance through this plea to hold hands with me encouragingly with enthusiasm My endeavors to institute a media network for the people of the entire globe, so that these aims can flourish. It is only.

A dream yet that I dream day and night however. Will not be a mere dream any longer when you and thoughtful individuals like you rally round me to make this a ‘dream come true’

Even as a single person you can influence people who would...

Be capable and interested to promote this idea, people like the heads of media institutions various organizations, business establishments, groups of companies, religious and social welfare organizations etc and etc. you may enlighten your friends and relations and even acquaintances whom you feel would be interested to support such a service.

While our lives are fast fading away with, every passing second, let us join hands together to build a free and just global society for all; our children, our grand children and a pleasant environment even for the animals to live peacefully fearlessly and in harmony with each other is the invitation I extend to you eagerly.

Thank you.
