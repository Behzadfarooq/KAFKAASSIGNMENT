Abigail "Abby" Fujimoto is a fictional character on Flight 29 Down, She is played by Tani Lynn Fujimoto

[edit] Character Info

Spoiler warning: Plot and/or ending details follow.
  • She was one of the 11 survivors of the plane crash. She, Ian, and Jory joined Captain Russell in their search through the island for other life.
  • She came back alone and dehydrated. Abby was eventually rescued by the seven survivors who were left behind at the campsite, but later returned to look for the others.
  • After unsuccessfully finding the others, She returned in the episode "Good Luck Abby". Many of the castaways were suprised by her different personality, being much more harsh and arrogant than they remember.
Spoilers end here.
