Abbas Aghaei (Persian: عباس آقایی ‎ , born September 2, 1977 in Iran) is an Iranian football player, currently playing for Pas Tehran in the IPL.

[edit] Club career

He spent most of his career in Pas Tehran. In 2004 he joined another IPL club, Sepahan, but returned to Pas after two seasons.

[edit] International career

He gained his first international cap for Iran on August 16, 2000 in a friendly versus Georgia. His appearances for the national team have been few, the most recent was in October 2006 when he was called up to join Team Melli in a LG cup tournament held in Jordan.
