Abbas Ibn al-Ahnaf

Abbas Ibn al-Ahnaf (750-809) was an Arab Abbasid poet. His work consists solely of love poems.

His work was an acknowledged influence on Abdullah ibn al-Mu'tazz and Abu al-Atahiya.
