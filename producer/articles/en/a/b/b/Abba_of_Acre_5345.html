Abba of Acre (Abba of Acco, Hebrew: אבא דמן עכו) was a Jewish Talmudist, known as an amora who lived in the Land of Israel and who flourished at the end of the 3rd century.

He was greatly respected by Abbahu and praised as an example of modesty (Soṭah, 40a).
