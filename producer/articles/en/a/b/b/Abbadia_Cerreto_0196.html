Location of Abbadia Cerreto in Italy

Abbadia Cerreto is a comune (municipality) in the Province of Lodi in the Italian region Lombardy, located about 40 km southeast of Milan and about 7 km southeast of Lodi. As of 31 December 2004, it had a population of 274 and an area of 6.1 km².[1]

Abbadia Cerreto borders the following municipalities: Bagnolo Cremasco, Crespiatica, Chieve, Corte Palasio, Casaletto Ceredano, Cavenago d'Adda.
