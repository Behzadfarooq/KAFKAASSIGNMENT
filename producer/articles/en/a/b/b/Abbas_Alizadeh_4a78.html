Abbas Alizadeh

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Abbas Alizadeh is an Iranologist and Persian archaeologist.

Alizadeh is currently a professor of ancient archeology at the University of Chicago. According to Science magazine, Alizadeh's hard work and determination have won him Iranian trust.
