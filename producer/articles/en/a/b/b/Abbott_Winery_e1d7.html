Abbott Winery
Location Middletown, California
Founded 1988
Key people Paul and Jo Ellen Abbott, Proprietors
Cases/yr 333
Known for “Salley Gardens”
Varietals Mourvèdre, Syrah, Durif
Website http://www.abbottwinery.com
Distribution by subscription


Abbott Winery is a small family-owned winery in Middleton, Lake County, California. It produces approximately 200 cases per year of its premier wine, a red Rhone-style table wine named after the traditional Irish song Down By The Salley Gardens, transcribed and published by W.B.Yeats. The wine is mostly mourvedre and syrah, with smaller amounts of durif, cinsaut, cabernet sauvignon, terret noir, counoise, roussanne, and vaccarese.
