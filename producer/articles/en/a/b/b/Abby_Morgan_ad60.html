Monica Keena as Abby Morgan

Abby Morgan is a fictional character from the television program, Dawson's Creek, played by Monica Keena.

Abby was a spiteful girl who disliked mostly everyone.

She first appeared in season one, when she shared saturday detention with Dawson, Jen, Joey, and Pacey for tardiness, and again in an episode shortly after when she helped spread a rumor about Joey Potter that she was pregnant.

She played a larger role in season two, when she became friends and drinking buddies with Jen Lindley. At first she seemed enamored with Jen because she came from the big city. She planned on moving to New York City after she graduated. She and Jen Lindley stole some champagne after a failed attempt to crash a local wedding reception, being hosted by Bessie and Joey Potter. Abby and Jen climb up onto a peir, but being intoxicated, Abby falls off into the water, and drowns.

After her death, Jen makes a less-than-moving eulogy at her funueral, only out of spite to her Grams. Abby seems to haunt her fellow classmates, including Jen and Andie McPhee, who scolded both Abby and Jen when they tried to crash the wedding the night Abby died.

A website, along with Abby's journal, is still available online.
