Abba Seafood

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Abba Seafood, formerly Abba AB, with head offices in Kungshamn is a company producing fish preservatives.

The company was established in Bergen 1838 and in the 1850s it moved to Stockholm. 1906 the name AB Br√∂derna Ameln, ABBA, was registered. In 1981 it was merged into Volvo, was sold to Procordia and is since 1995 a part of the Norwegian Orkla ASA.

They are known for having let the pop group ABBA share the name in 1974.
