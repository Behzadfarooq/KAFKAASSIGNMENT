Abbaretz

Abbaretz is at 50 km in the north of Nantes and at 80 km in the south of Rennes. more exactly, the commune is 7.5 km in th east of Nozay, on the road D2, before that it borders the Forêt de l'Arche to arrive to la Meilleraye-de-Bretagne, about ten km after.

The bordering communes are Nozay, Puceul, Saffré, Joué-sur-Erdre, La Meilleraye-de-Bretagne, Issé and Treffieux.
