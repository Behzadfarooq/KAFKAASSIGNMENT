Abbaye aux Dames

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

You have new messages (last change).

William the Conqueror and his wife Matilda both founded the Abbaye aux Dames and the Abbaye aux Hommes. Both abbeys are located in Caen, in Northwestern France. L'Abbaye aux Hommes was dedicated to Saint Stephen and was a male abbey.
