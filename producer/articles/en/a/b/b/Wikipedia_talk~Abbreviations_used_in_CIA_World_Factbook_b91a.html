Fine with me, Docu -- I created it in the first place to make it easier to link/disambiguate all those abbreviations in the "Politics of" articles; I thought it could conceivably be a useful general reference for encyclopedia readers, but moving it's fine too. Catherine 19:59 Apr 21, 2003 (UTC)
It's most useful for this. Even though the list of general acronyms (and abbreviations) includes most of them, a specific List of international organizations and their abbreviations and acronyms would be helpful as general reference. As in the context ICC might refer primarily to the International Criminal Court, rather than the International Chamber of Commerce listed in this table (If a given country has already ratified the ICC-statute, obviously one may aleady redirect there). Thus, it would be preferable to have such a list independent of a given version of the CIA World Factbook. Another advantage of moving this to the wikipedia namespace .. I wont need to disambiguate the links ;-)
