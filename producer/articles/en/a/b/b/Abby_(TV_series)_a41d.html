Abby was an American sitcom that aired on UPN from January 6, 2003 to March 4, 2003. The show's original title was Abby Walker, but was soon changed.

[edit] Overview

The series is centered around Abby, who has dumped her boyfriend Will, but agrees to remain roommates to keep their San Francisco apartment.

Besides this, Abby's life is further complicated by her eccentric family. Jo, her sister, thinks Abby should live her way. She often has clashes with her boss, the overbearing Roger, at her job as a TV producer of the Northwest Sports Report. Plus, her best friend Max has a huge crush on her, but doesn't want to break the news to Abby.

The show had very poor ratings from the beginning, and was soon cancelled. A total of 10 episodes were filmed, but only 9 were aired.
