Abbey church in Czerwińsk nad Wisłą

Abbey church in Czerwińsk nad Wisłą was built in 12th century. Despite gothic and baroque elements added in later centuries, the basilica remains to be one of the most valuable example of the Romanesque architecture in Poland.
