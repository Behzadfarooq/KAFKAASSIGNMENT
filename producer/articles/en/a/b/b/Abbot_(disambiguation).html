  • Ezra Abbot (1819-1894), American Biblical scholar

    • George Abbot (Archbishop of Canterbury) (1562-1633), Archbishop of Canterbury (George Abbot School)
    • George Abbot (English writer) (1603?-1648), English Puritan writer
  • Henry Abbot (died 1597), English Catholic martyr
  • Joel Abbot (1793-1855), American naval officer
  • John Abbot (1751-1840), American entomologist
  • Jude Abbot, British musician
  • Maurice Abbot (died 1540), British merchant, brother of George Abbot the Archbishop
  • Nick Abbot, British radio presenter
  • Robert Abbot, one of several people including
    • Robert Abbot (1560-1617), Bishop of Salisbury
    • Robert Abbot (1588-1622), Puritan theologian
    • Robert Abbott (born 1933), American game designer
    • Robert Sengstacke Abbott (1870-1940), American lawyer and publisher
  • William Abbot (1798-1843), British actor
  • William Abbot, English Catholic martyr William Knight
