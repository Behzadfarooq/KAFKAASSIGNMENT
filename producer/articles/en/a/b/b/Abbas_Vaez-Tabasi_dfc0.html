Ayatollah Abbas Vaez Tabasi is an influential member of the Expediency Discernment Council and the Assembly of Experts of the Islamic Republic of Iran.

Furthermore, Vaez-Tabasi is the head of the Imam Reza Shrine Foundation which runs "auto plants, agricultural businesses, and many other enterprises, and it is worth an estimated $15 billion."[1] This gives him the nickname of Sultan of Khorasan[2] , and his son Naser the Heir to the throne of Khorasan in Iran.[3]

Although during the 2006 Assembly of Experts elections, Tabasi and Rafsanjani were denounced by the Ahmadinejad supporters,[4] he somehow managed to get elected.[5] Nevertheless he is said to be gravely ill and is expected to die very soon.[6]
