[edit] Season 1: 1995-1996

Screencap Title Original airdate #
"Pilot, Part 1" September 24, 1995 01
When an Earth outpost 16 light-years away is destroyed by an unknown alien force, a group of young United States Marine Corps Space Aviators find themselves on the front lines of an interstellar battle in an attempt to save their home planet. 
"Pilot, Part 2" September 24, 1995 02
per above

 
"The Farthest Man from Home" October 1, 1995 03
When the USS Saratoga passes close to the planet where the Earth colonists were attacked, West goes AWOL and flies down to the planet in hopes that his girlfriend, Kylen, somehow survived. 
"The Dark Side of the Sun" October 8, 1995 04
The Wild Cards attempt to secure a fuel ore mining facility, only to find it overrun by A.I. Silicates. 
"Mutiny" October 15, 1995 05
Cooper and McQueen, both In Vitroes, face a difficult decision when the captain of a space cargo hauler decides to sacrifice his In Vitro cargo in order to save his ship after it comes under attack. 
"Ray Butts" October 22, 1995 06
The members of the 58th are suspicious after a tough war veteran, who arrives on the Saratoga with orders to take them on a classified mission, changes the mission's objective once they're behind enemy lines. 
"Eyes" October 29, 1995 07
In an effort to get West to help with an assassination plot, the young marine is told that high-ranking Earth officials knew about the aliens before the colonists were sent to the Vesta Colony. 
"The Enemy" November 5, 1995 08
The members of the 58th turn on each other after they land on the planet Tartarus and encounter a Chig mind-altering weapon that wreaks havoc on the area of the brain that controls fear. 
"Hostile Visit, Part 1" November 17, 1995 09
After the 58th captures an alien bomber, McQueen suggests that it would help raise troop morale if they use the vessel to attack the aliens' home planet - a plan that goes horribly wrong. 
"Choice or Chance, Part 2" November 26, 1995 10
When the 58th crash-lands on a moon deep in enemy territory and Shane, West, Damphousse and Wang are captured by Silicates, West discovers one of the other prisoners is Kylen. 
"Stay with the Dead" December 3, 1995 11
West's plan to lure Chig soldiers into an ambush works, but after he's rescued, no one believes him when insists that the rest of the 58th is still alive and needs to be rescued too. 
"The River of Stars" December 17, 1995 12
When the Marines are stranded in enemy territory on Christmas day, they receive a cryptic transmission which tells them how to "hitch a ride" back to safety on an incoming comet's orbit. 
"Who Monitors the Birds?" January 7, 1996 13
Wounded and all alone in enemy territory, Hawkes struggles to stay alive as he recalls his past In Vitro education and how he was scheduled for termination because he asked too many questions. 
"Level of Necessity" January 14, 1996 14
After experiencing a vision of a soldier's death during a combat mission, Damphousse is investigated by a Psi Corps colonel who is convinced she possesses psychic powers. 
"Never No More, Part 1" February 4, 1996 15
Shane encounters an old boyfriend as both their divisions volunteer to help hunt down a powerful new Chig vessel which is rumored to be equipped with a cloaking device. 
"The Angriest Angel, Part 2" Feburary 11, 1996 16
McQueen, a grounded ex-member of the elite fighter team The Angry Angels, insists that he be allowed to fly a dangerous mission to destroy the Chig's newest spacecraft. 
"Toy Soldiers" February 18, 1996 17
West is upset when his younger brother, who has joined the Marines, shows up on the Saratoga under the command of an inexperienced gung-ho lieutenant. 
"Dear Earth" March 3, 1996 18
The members of the 58th receive letters from home, some with good news, some with bad, while McQueen and Cooper are ordered to appear in a documentary on In Vitroes. 
"Pearly" March 24, 1996 19
On a planet overrun with Chigs, the 58th encounters Major Cyril MacKendrick, the sole survivor of the 8th British Cavalry, and Wang encounters the Silicate who previously tortured him. 
"R&R" April 12, 1996 20
Suffering from combat fatigue, the 58th is granted liberty aboard the Bacchus, a Rest and Recreational pleasure ship where it's said anything can, and does, happen. 
"Stardust" April 19, 1996 21
A mysterious mission the 58th is assigned to carry out nearly turns to disaster when the Earth ship the Corps members are ordered to escort suddenly locks on their Hammerheads and opens fire. 
"Sugar Dirt" April 20, 1996 22
The 58th struggles to survive on a desolate planet held by the Chigs after the Saratoga must abandon the crew in order to launch an assault that could ultimately save millions of lives. 
"And If They Lay Us Down To Rest..., Part 1" May 26, 1996 23
The 58th lands on the moon of the Chig's home planet and encounters an extraterrestrial creature which may be an unarmored Chig or an entirely different life form. 
"...Tell Our Moms We Done Our Best, Part 2" June 2, 1996 24
When peace talks on the Saratoga go awry, the 58th flies one last dangerous mission in order to save POWs trapped in transport carriers crippled by the enemy.
