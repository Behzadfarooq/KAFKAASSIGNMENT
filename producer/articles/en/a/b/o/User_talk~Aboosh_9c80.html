[edit] WP:AGF, WP:NPA, WP:CIVIL, WP:V

Hi Aboosh, I wrote on the discussion page of the AFD that I apologize for WP:BITEing a newcomer, but apparantly this does not really apply to you since you say you have already been editing for a long time [1].

In any case, I'm responding to what you wrote on the sockpuppet page about my assumption of bad faith, and I have already explained and apologized for that. The whole matter will be straightened out when both of you provide your IP addresses. The thing about assumptions of bad faith is that the whole thing is very reciprocal. It doesn't really help to go around accusing someone of bad faith while showing it to other editors. Here are some examples from your recent edits in which you showed bad faith.

  • 1). You wrote: "Dear Beit Or, your attempts to Jewdisizing Wikipedia by including biased sites that explains Islamic issues about Islamic sites from an un-neutral point of view.Aboosh 01:19, 29 October 2006 (UTC)[2]". Just because you don't agree with someone's sources, it does not mean that they are biased. Please see WP:V for a description of acceptable sources. Also, a Jewish point of view (whatever that is) is acceptable on Wikipedia along with other points of view. "Jewish" is not a dirty curse word on Wikipedia, and shouldn't be anywhere, so please stop using it like one. Please see WP:CIVIL.
  • 2). Here is another example of your assumption of bad faith: "Comment. Dear Amoruso, I think whats dubious is your intentions of writing this article in the first place. You cannot continue to shift your argument from the core of the issue like what you showed in your response to Ramellite. Your neutrality towards the issue is questionable. [3]" This was something like your third edit to the encyclopedia.
  • 3) About this edit: "This is an issue that has to have an input from Islamic scholars ONLY. Also, Beit Or, Islam does not open a wide door for discussion and interpertations as many non-muslims wish for it to be.... So, this discussion must be ended [4]," please refer to WP:NPOV. Wikipedia is not a Muslim encyclopedia. If you want to participate in the project, you can't ignore Wikipedia rules, even on Muslim topics. Elizmr 23:31, 29 October 2006 (UTC)
