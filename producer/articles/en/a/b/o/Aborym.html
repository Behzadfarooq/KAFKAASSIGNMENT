Aborym is an Italian industrial black metal band who refer to their style as hard / black alien industrial metal. This terminology is met out in their image by their use of extreme pornographic and erotic theatrics as well as neon corpse paint, and in their music by their use of experimental cut up techniques. Until Bård Faust joined, Aborym used a drum-machine on their albums.

[edit] Current members
