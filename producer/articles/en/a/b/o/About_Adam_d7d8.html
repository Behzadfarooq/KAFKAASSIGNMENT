About Adam is a 2000 Irish-set movie, a romantic comedy about the relation between Adam (Stuart Townsend) and Lucy (Kate Hudson). It is very loosely based on Theorem by Pier Paolo Pasolini.

Spoiler warning: Plot and/or ending details follow.

Adam is a young Dublin serial seducer. Not happy with falling for Lucy (Kate Hudson), he seduces her two sisters Laura (Frances O'Connor) and Alice (Charlotte Bradley) as well.
