About A Boy is an album that was released by Badly Drawn Boy (born Damon Gough) in 2002. The album was the soundtrack to the movie About a Boy. Unusually, Gough was chosen to compose and perform the entire soundtrack for the movie.

In writer Nick Hornby's book 31 Songs, he lists Gough's track "A Minor Incident" as one of the many tracks that changed his life.
