Aborted Films

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Aborted Films is a small independent film production company working in and around Preston. They are run by a group of young film-makers who have worked with actors from Emmerdale, Coronation Street, Grange Hill and the PI Helpline man in their productions so far. They have mainly worked on films dealing with controversial subjects such as rape, incest, murder and prostitution. They are currently working on a short film dealing with the issues of regrettable one night stands.

Aborted Films are: Steven Bradley, Ash Williamson, Daniel Baines, Carl Humphries, Diana Venezia, Ryan McMurray and Conroy.
