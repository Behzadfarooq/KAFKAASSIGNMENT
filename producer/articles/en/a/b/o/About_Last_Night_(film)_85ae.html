About Last Night (film)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

About Last Night is an English-language film released in 1986. The film is 113 minutes long and is a romantic comedy directed by Edward Zwick. It is based on the 1974 David Mamet play Sexual Perversity in Chicago. The film is rated R in the United States.

Tagline: It's about men, women, choices, friendship, love, last night...

[edit] Plot summary

Danny and Bernie are two single men who live on the wild side in Chicago. But when Danny meets Debbie at a bar and the two start a relationship with a one night stand, Danny's life takes a different turn. How does this passionate night become a full affair and what effect will this relationship have on both people and their friends?
