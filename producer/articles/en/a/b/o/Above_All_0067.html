Above All is a British heavy metal band. They released their debut album Domain in 1996 on the Roadrunner Records label. Their lyrics were highly politically motivated and protested against right wing politics in general.

[edit] TV Appearances

They made their debut TV appearance on MTV's Headbangers Ball where they played the song Prison. The program included an interview of the band where thay were asked if they viewed themselves as a Bristish hardcore band, to which they replied that they did not consider.
