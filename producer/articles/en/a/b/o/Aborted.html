Aborted, formed in 1995, is a Belgian deathgrind/goregrind band. Founded by vocalist Sven de Caluwé who was able to find additional members in 1997, and eventually get the band signed to their first deal on UXICON records. Aborted are currently signed to Century Media and have toured with such acts as Cryptopsy and Morbid Angel.
