"About Us" is a Pop/R&B song by Brooke Hogan. It was released as the first single from Brooke's debut album Undiscovered in June 2006 in the US. The music video premiered on June 25, 2006, after the season two finale of 'Hogan Knows Best,' in which Brooke prepares and then films the video for the song. The song features Paul Wall and is the first single released by Scott Storch's record label, Storchaveli. Hogan currently is the first and only person signed to this record label.

DJ E Rock collaborated a Bay Area hyphy remix of the single "About Us". This track features lyrics from E-40 and beats from Traxxamillion.

[edit] Music video

The budget for the video was $300,000 and was shot in California. In the video Brooke does a work-out/dance routine in a gym with a jump rope. In some scenes you can see her with big, rhinestone-encrusted sunglass with a pair of grills that Paul Wall gave her for her 18th birthday.[1] She also is depicted in a night club and has a dance-off with other girls.

The video premiered on Hogan Knows Best on June 25, 2006. The music video also premiered on Total Request Live on July 24, 2006 as a PRE-RL.

[edit] Chart performance

"About Us" debuted on the Billboard Hot 100 for the week ending August 19, 2006. The song was the Hot Shot Debut of the week, landing at number fifty-three (#53). In its second week, the song went from #53 to thirty-three (#33), where it peaked.[2] On the Top 40 Mainstream chart, it peaked at number twenty-two (#22).[3]

The video was #1 on VH1's Top 20 countdown for 3 weeks.
