Above the Rim (Original Soundtrack) is the official movie soundtrack to the film Above the Rim. It had three singles, "Pour Out a Little Liquor" by 2Pac, "Regulate" by Warren G and Nate Dogg, and "Part Time Lover" by H-Town. It also feauters many new acts including B-Rezell, Lord G, DJ Rogers and also the Death Row debut for 2Pac. One of the more acclaimed tracks from the original release of the album, "Pain" by Tupac Shakur was not included on the compact disc rerelease of the album. It was certified double platinum with its 2,142,290 shipped copies. The album won on the 1995 Source Awards as the Soundtrack of The Year.
