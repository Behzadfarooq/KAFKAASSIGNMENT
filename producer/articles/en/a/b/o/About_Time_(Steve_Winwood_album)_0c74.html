About Time is a Steve Winwood album (2003 ).

Steve returns to the music business after 6 years and proves to listeners that he has not lost the sound of Traffic.

[edit] Wincraft

Steve Winwood starts an independent record label called Wincraft Music administered by SCI Fidelity. Two albums have appeared under this label:

  • About Time
  • The Last Great Traffic Jam (with Epic)
