"Eyes" is the seventh episode in the first season of Space: Above and Beyond. It originally aired in North America on November 5, 1995.

[edit] Plot

Spoiler warning: Plot and/or ending details follow.

After the assassination of the UN Secretary General the Wild Cards are assigned to protect the newly assigned leader.
