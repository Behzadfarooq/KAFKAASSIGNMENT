"About a Book Club" is an episode of Hope & Faith where Faith joins Hope's book club but instead of reading with the rest of the group, she takes the group out to a nightclub and ends up getting the whole group thrown in prison. Meanwhile, Charlie takes the children to a video arcade and ends up spending the whole night playing pinball.

The episode first aired on October 24, 2003.

[edit] Triva

  • This is the first Hope and Faith episode that had an opening scene first and then the theme song.
