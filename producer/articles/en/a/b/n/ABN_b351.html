ABN or abn is a three-letter acronym that may refer to:

  • Alaska Board of Nursing
  • IATA airport code for Albina Airport in Albina, Suriname
  • Algemeen Beschaafd Nederlands (meaning "Common Civilized Dutch"), the near-obsolete name for now "Common Dutch"
  • Algemene Bank Nederland, a Dutch bank now part of ABN AMRO
  • Asahi Broadcasting Nagano, a television station in Nagano Prefecture, Japan
  • Assholes by Nature, a rap group
  • Assumed Business Name, in Oregon corporation law, often called Doing business as elsewhere in the United States
  • Australian Business Number, a unique identifier issued to business entities by the Australian Taxation Office
