The ABN AMRO World Tennis Tournament is a tennis tournament held in Rotterdam, Netherlands. Held since 1972, the tournament is played on indoor hard courts and is a part of the ATP Tour schedule.

American Arthur Ashe won the tournament three times (1972, 1975-76) and holds the record for most singles titles. In 1984 the final between Jimmy Connors and Ivan Lendl was interupted due to a bomb alarm. Both players were declared winner.
