The Abnuceals Emuukha Electric Symphony Orchestra is the group name for the recurring studio musicians used during the experimental music scene in the years between 1959-1988. Their first appearance on record was on the Chipmunks single "Alvin's Orchestra". Their last appearance was in 1988 on the Who Framed Roger Rabbit soundtrack album.

[edit] Trivia

  • "Alvin's Orchestra" managed to reach #33 on Billboard's Pop Singles in 1960. This was the Chipmunks' fourth single to chart on the Billboard charts.
  • The Abnuceals Emuukha Electric Symphony Orchestra appeared on Frank Zappa's first solo album, "Lumpy Gravy".
