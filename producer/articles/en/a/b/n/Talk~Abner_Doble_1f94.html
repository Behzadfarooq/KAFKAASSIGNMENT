Yes, I too found it surprising that a run-of-the-mill Wickipedian could have that much original inside knowledge. I have traced the original (longer) source[1] and have inserted the reference in this article. I get the impression that the original author, Stephen Fox himself got a lot of his information from an interview he did of James D. Crank, probably today the world authority on the Dobles and their cars and the only one likely to have this sort of knowledge. Apparently there is a definitive book in preparation and reading between the lines of Jim's recent contributions to the "Steam stuff SACA forum [2] suggests he is involved in it. A couple of years ago there was an appeal for subscribers. I sent my name and eMail address, but have heard no more since.--John of Paris 00:18, 27 November 2006 (UTC)

There is nonetheless a lot of material that could be transferred. Also these articles need to be coordinated with the general Steam car one, which itself is far from brilliant at present.--John of Paris 12:55, 6 December 2006 (UTC)
