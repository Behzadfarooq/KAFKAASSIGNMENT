[edit] Who am us, anyway?

David Abfackeln, also known as DSA, David A. or abfackeln, is a Wikipedia user, software designer, and audio artist. He lives in Vancouver's Chinatown and is a founding member of the G42 Players.

[edit] Random facts and history

  • abfackeln is an ambitious Capricorn but does not care much for money.
  • abfackeln has very severe and cynical political views.
  • The G42 Players hosted a Friday late-night radio show for six years on CiTR Radio called Limp Sink.
  • The music of G42 can really be very irritating.
  • abfackeln is an extreme introvert.
  • abfackeln has used approximately 100 pseudonyms since first using a modem in 1983.
  • David Abfackeln is a pseudonym.
  • abfackeln's first pseudonym was Dalek -- which really did seem distinct at the time.
  • abfackeln's pre-internet posts used the phrase "Oyt be Nayt" in the sig -- the meaning of which is now forgotten.
  • abfackeln was one of the original denizens of the EFnet #industrial channel and occasional vistor on #gothic.
  • abfackeln denies being goth.
  • abfackeln is new to this Wikipedia thing.
