Abdullah al-Tariki (1918 - 1997) ( Arabic: عبدالله الطويرقي ) nicknamed "the red minister", is the first Saudi oil minister appointed by King Saud , co-founder of Organization of Petroleum Exporting Countries (OPEC)and well-known Arab nationalist.
