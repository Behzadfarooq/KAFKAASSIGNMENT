Abdelilah Fahmi

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Abdelilah Fahmi (Arabic: عبدالله فهمي‎) (born 3 August 1973) is a Moroccan football defender who currently plays for Raja Casablanca.

He was part of the Moroccan 2000 African Nations Cup team, who finished third in group D in the first round of competition, thus failing to secure qualification for the quarter-finals.

[edit] Clubs

  • 1991-1999 : Raja Casablanca
  • 1999-2003 : Lille OSC
  • 2003-2005 : RC Strasbourg
  • 2005-2006 : Gaziantepspor
  • 2006: Al-Khor
  • 2006: Al-Arabi Sports Club
  • 2007-present: Raja Casablanca
