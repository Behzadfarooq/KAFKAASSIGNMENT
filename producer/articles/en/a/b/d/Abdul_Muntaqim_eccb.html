Abdul Muntaqim

Born March 17, 2007 (age 0)
Raja Isteri Pengiran Anak Saleha Hospital, Bandar Seri Begawan, Brunei
Other names Yang Teramat Mulia Pengiran Muda'Abdul Muntaqim
Title Prince of Brunei
Parents Al-Muhtadee Billah Bolkiah and Sarah Pengiran Salleh

Yang Teramat Mulia Pengiran Muda'Abdul Muntaqim (born March 17, 2007) is the first born son and thus far only child of Al-Muhtadee Billah Bolkiah and his wife, Sarah Pengiran Salleh.

In honour of the birth of the prince, a 19-gun salute was fired from the cannons on the grounds of Istana Nurul Iman, the official residence of the Sultan of Brunei. The gun-salute is traditionally carried out to mark the births of immediate members of Sultan's family, and serves as an announcement of a royal birth to the Bruneian people.
