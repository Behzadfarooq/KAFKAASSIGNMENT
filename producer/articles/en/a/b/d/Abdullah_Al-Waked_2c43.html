Abdullah Al-Waked Al-Shahrani (Arabic: عبد الله الواكد الشهراني) (born 29 September 1975) is a Saudi Arabian football player.

He played most of his career for Al Shabab and Al Ittihad.

He played for the Saudi Arabia national football team and was a participant at the 2002 FIFA World Cup.
