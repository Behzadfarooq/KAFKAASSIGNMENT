Abdelkader El Mouaziz (born January 1, 1969) is a Moroccan long-distance runner who has the record for the most sub 2:10 marathon runs at 13 (as of October 2005).

El Mouaziz is a two-time winner of the London Marathon, in 1999 and 2001.
