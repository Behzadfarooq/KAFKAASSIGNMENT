Abdul Nasir al-Janabi

Sheikh Abdul Nasir Karim al-Janabi is an Iraqi politician and conservative Sunni cleric. He is a leader of Iraqi Accord Front, the Sunni Arab-majority bloc within the government of Nouri al-Maliki. He is the secretary general of the Iraqi National Dialogue Council.

[edit] Quotes

  • "We came here to solve the security issue, and we made an agreement before the formation of the government to name the ministers of Defense and Interior," [1]
  • “We cannot trust the office of the prime minister.”[2]
