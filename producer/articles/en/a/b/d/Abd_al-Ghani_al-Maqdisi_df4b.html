Abd al-Ghani al-Maqdisi is a classical Sunni Islamic scholar, one of the most prominent Hadith masters [1].

[edit] Biography

He was one of the students of Abu-al-Faraj Ibn Al-Jawzi.

He died 600 AH [2]

[edit] Works

He is the author of many famous works, including [1]:

  • al-Kamal fi ma`rifat al-rijal [3][1]
  • Umdat al-Ahkam [1]
