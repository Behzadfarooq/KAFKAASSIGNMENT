Abdoulrazak Traoré (born 28 December 1988) is a Cote d'Ivoire footballer who plays for Norwegian Premier League club Rosenborg B.K., where he plays as a midfielder.

At Rosenborg, he plays alongside his compatriot and Cote d'Ivoire international Didier Ya Konan, who joined the club in December 2006 from homeland club ASEC Abidjan.

Traore played on Raja Casablanca before he ended his contract and joined Rosenborg B.K. on a free transfer a few months later.
