Abdel Khaliq Sarwat Pasha (1873 - 1928) (Arabic: عبد الخالق ثروت باشا) was an Egyptian political figure. He served as the Prime Minister of Egypt from 1 March 1922 until 30 November 1922, and again between 26 April 1927 and 16 March 1928.
