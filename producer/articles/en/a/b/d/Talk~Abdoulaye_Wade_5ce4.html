It's not surprising to hear him speaking Wolof, as everybody in Senegal speaks Wolof, it's the main language of the country, together with French. So that doesn't has to mean he's Wolof, you could just try to find "Wade" in a list of tribal names as you can commonly recognize the tribe somebody is in by looking at his last name. --ቢትወደድ 20:55, 2 March 2007 (UTC)
