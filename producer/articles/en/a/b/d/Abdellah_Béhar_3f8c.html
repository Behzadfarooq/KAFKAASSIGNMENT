Abdellah Béhar

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Abdellah Béhar (born 5 July 1963 in Souk Arba du Gharb) is a Moroccan-born French runner who specializes in the 5000 metres and cross-country running.

[edit] Achievements

Year Tournament Venue Result Extra
1995 World Championships Gothenburg, Sweden 15th 5000 m
1997 World Championships Athens, Greece 11th 5000 m
1998 European Championships Budapest, Hungary 5th 5000 m
1999 World Cross Country Championships Belfast, Northern Ireland 7th Short race
World Half Marathon Championships Palermo, Italy 4th Half marathon
2000 World Cross Country Championships Vilamoura, Portugal 9th Long race

[edit] Personal bests

  • 3000 metres - 7:35.80 min (1996)
  • 5000 metres - 13:13.33 min (1998)
  • 10,000 metres - 27:42.57 min (1996)
  • Half marathon - 1:01:30 hrs (2000)
  • Marathon - 2:09:05 hrs (2001)
