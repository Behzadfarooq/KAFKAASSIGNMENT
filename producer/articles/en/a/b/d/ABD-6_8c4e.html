ABD-6

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

ABD-6 is a television station in Darwin, Northern Territory. It is affiliated with the Australian Broadcasting Corporation. The station was the first to go to air in Darwin, on 13 August 1971. The studios are in the inner city of Darwin and transmitter is at Blake Street.
