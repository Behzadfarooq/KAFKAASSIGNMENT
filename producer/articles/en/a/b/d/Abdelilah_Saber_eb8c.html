Abdelilah Saber

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Abdelilah Saber (Arabic: عبد الإله صابر) (born April 21, 1974) is a Moroccan football player, who played for Sporting Lisboa in Portugal and SSC Napoli in Italy.

He played for the Morocco national football team and was a participant at the 1998 FIFA World Cup.
