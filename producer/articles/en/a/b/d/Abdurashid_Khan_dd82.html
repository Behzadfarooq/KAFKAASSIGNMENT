Abdurashid Khan was the ruler of Yarkand state in Eastern Turkestan between 1533 and 1560 years A.D.

Khan was a descendent of the first moghul khan-Tughlugh Timur Khan (1347-1363 AD) and came to power in 1533 year when his predecessor Sultan Said Khan died of asthma.

[edit] Achievements

[edit] Treaties

In 1538, Khan concluded the Treaty of Peace and Alliance with Shaybanid Abdulla, who ruled in West Turkestan at the time. He also completed the same treaty with the moghul Empire in India.
