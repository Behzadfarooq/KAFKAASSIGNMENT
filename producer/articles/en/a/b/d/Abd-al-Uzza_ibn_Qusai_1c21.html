In Islam, Abd-al-Uzza ibn Qusai (Arabic: عبدالعزة‎) forms an important link between his father, Qusai ibn Kilab (c.400–480), the great-great-grandfather of Shaiba ibn Hashim (Abdul-Mutallib) and his son, Asad ibn Abd-Al-Uzza, the progenitor of the Banu Asad [1]
