Abdylas Maldybaev

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Abdylas Maldybaev, as shown on the Kyrgyz 1 som note.

Abdylas Maldybaev (Kyrgyz: Абдылас Малдыбаев) (1906-1978) was a Kyrgyz composer, actor, and tenor singer. Maldybaev was the creator of Kyrgyzstan's national anthem and is still renowned for his operatic composition. He helped popularize Kyrgyz music by skillfully using Western European techniques. The Kyrgyz one som banknote pictures him.
