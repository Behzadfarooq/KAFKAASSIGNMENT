Abdul Raouf Khalil's Museum (Arabic: متحف عبدالرؤوف خليل) located in Jeddah, Saudi Arabia.

The museum shows the Saudi's heritage and civilaization. It's diverted into 3 sections:

  • first section: the old Saudi heritager
  • second section: Ottoman Empire
  • third section: european devlopment
