Abd Allah (Zaragoza)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Abd Allah ibn al-Hakam al-Tjibi (Arabic: عبد الله بن الحكم التجيبي‎) was the final member of the Banu Tujibi group of local bosses to rule Zaragoza before they were muscled out of control by the Banu Hud family. He ruled for a small piece of 1039 before the Banu Hud seized control.
