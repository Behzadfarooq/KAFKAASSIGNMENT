Ayatollah Abdollah Javadi-Amoli is an Iranian Friday prayer leader.

Javadi-Amoli believes that the United Nations and Atomic Agency are controlled by the United States and in one of his sermons in 2004 urged American not to vote for George W. Bush[1].
