Abdool Mudassar Samad (born May 3, 1979 in Guyana) is a Canadian cricketer. He has played in 7 one-day Internationals for Canada to date. He has also played one first-class match, scoring 119 against Bermuda in the 2006 ICC Intercontinental Cup.
