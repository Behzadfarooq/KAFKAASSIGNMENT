Abdelnacer Ouadah (Arabic: عبدالناصر عوادة‎) (born September 13, 1975) is an Algerian football player who, as of 2006 was playing for FC Metz but is now playing for CS Sedan Ardennes.

He was part of the Algerian 2004 African Nations Cup team, who finished second in their group in the first round of competition before being defeated by Morocco in the quarter-finals.

His brother, Mohamed, is a renowned soccer trainer in Moselle.
