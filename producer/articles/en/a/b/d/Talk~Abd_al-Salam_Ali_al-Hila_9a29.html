Is this the same as Abdul Al-Salam Al Hilal? Gene Nygaard 15:17, 3 May 2006 (UTC)

I just read through al-Hilal's testimony before his Combatant Status Review Tribunal. I think he would have mentioned it if he had been held in a black site. He didn't. So, I think they are two different individuals. -- Geo Swan 01:39, 4 May 2006 (UTC)
I saw some identification number with another likely duplicate. Do these one or two people have identification numbers? Gene Nygaard 11:42, 5 May 2006 (UTC)
All the detainees whose cases were considered by a CSRT had their ID numbers published on April 20, 2006. The ID numbers of the 200 plus detainees who were released before the CSRT process was initiated have not been made public. Al Hilal is on there. Al Hila is not.
The DoD released the transcripts of the 317 detainees who chose to appear before their Tribunals on March 3, 2006. Those transcripts all had the detainee's ID number in the lower right hand corner. I manually correlated the list of names and ID numbers, with the corresponding anonymous transcripts, through the ID numbers.
Here is my list User:Geo Swan/working/detainees who went through their CSRT. You can see I started at the beginning, and started adding new articles, based on the CSRT transcripts. -- Geo Swan 21:06, 5 May 2006 (UTC)
