Abdalá Bucaram, Jr.

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Abdalá Bucaram Junior is the son of Abdalá Bucaram, a former president of Ecuador.

He is a football player [1] who has played at Emelec and at Chilean club Santiago Morning [2]. He has also played at the Ecuadorian under-20 National Team.
