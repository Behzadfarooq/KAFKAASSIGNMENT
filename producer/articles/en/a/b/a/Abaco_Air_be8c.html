Abaco Air

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Company Logo

Abaco Air LTD is a scheduled and charter airline based in the Bahamas.

[edit] Basic Information

  • Abaco Air home base is in Marsh Harbour, Abaco Islands, Bahamas. It operates scheduled and charter flights and has been in operation for over 30 years.

[edit] Fleet

  • Aero Commander (aircraft)
  • Britten-Norman Islander

[edit] Services

Scheduled services between:

  • Marsh Harbour and Nassau
  • Marsh Harbour and North Eleuthera
  • Marsh Harbour and Moore's Island

Charter operations within the Bahamas and to Florida
