Abadia de Goiás is a small town and municipality in central Goiás state, Brazil, located on the western edge of the Goiânia metropolitan area.

The distance to Goiânia is 27 km. and highway connections are made by BR-060.

Neighboring municipalities are:

  • north and east: Trindade
  • northwest: Goiânia
  • east: Guapó
  • south: Aragoiânia

[edit] Economy

The economy is based on services, government jobs, small industries, cattle raising, poultry, and agriculture.

  • GDP in 2003: R$16.9 million (R$8.1 million dollars at the rate of May 1, 2006)
  • GDP per capita in 2003: 2,910 (1,398 dollars at the rate of May 1, 2006)
  • Financial institutions: none in June 2005
