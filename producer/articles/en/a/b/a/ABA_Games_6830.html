ABA Games

ABA Games is a Japanese creator of freeware video games run by Kenta Cho. Cho is most famous for his shmups, mostly featuring abstract reinterpretations of retro classics with each game having a specific unique feature and using randomly generated levels. He even created BulletML, a custom markup language for defining 2D shooter firing patterns, which has been used in a variety of games. Most of the games were written in the D programming language and use Simple DirectMedia Layer for graphics.
