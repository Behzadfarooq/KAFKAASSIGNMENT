Guys, awards from a site that gets only a few Google hits, and has a far lower Alexa ranking than even Abandonia does, just doesn't equate to note-worthy in my eyes, or in the rules set forth in WP:WEB. While Reloaded may be worth a couple to a few paragraphs on the Abandonia article, it is not yet noteworthy enough, by the criteria set by Wikipedia admins, to have an article of it's own. I'm proposing it for deletion again, and will continue to do so until I'm either proven wrong about the notability of DaFastLane, or Abandonia qualifies under one of the other criteria in WP:WEB.Sephylight 02:27, 29 August 2006 (UTC)

  • Deleting does seem a bit harsh. There's good content here, but the subject itself doesn't yet qualify for an article of it's own. Merge it, I say. 72.161.224.153 03:39, 1 September 2006 (UTC)

Not everything can be measured by an Alexa ranking, Abandonia Reloaded is a site dedicated to freeware games which is in itself a niche market, it is one of the biggest sites in his class providing FREE valuable content to it's visitors through the means of reviews and screenshots. I think this point alone justifies to have Abandonia Reloaded as it's own article. Also even though it might be written by fans (as I imagine is the case for most articles on wikipedia) of the site, the article is written from a neutral point of view.
