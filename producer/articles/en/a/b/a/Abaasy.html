The Abaasy (Abaaht or Abasy) (Sakha: абаасы/абааһы) are demons of the underworld in the mythology of the Sakha (also known as the Yakuts). They are alleged to be the spirits of the long-time deceased who dwell near graves or in deserted places who otherwise travel about causing destruction.[1] Either their entire body or just their teeth are made of iron. The abaasy travel in packs of seven.[2] They serve Arson-Doulai, the ruler of the dead, who also swallows people' souls and gives the living diseases. The abaasy can be appeased by blood sacrifices. .[1]

The concept of the abaasy is so ingrained into Sakha thought that the verb абааһы көр- (to see abaasy) is the everyday term for "to hate" or "to dislike".
