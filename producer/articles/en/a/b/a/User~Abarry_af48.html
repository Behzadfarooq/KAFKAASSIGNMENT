[edit] Wikipedia

I've only recently started to edit Wikipedia rather than just reading it, prompted by the Hello! Project wikiproject. In that case, most of my edits will probably be based around jpop articles, specifically Hello!Project. That's the subject I know the most information about. I'm trying my best to learn the Wiki markup. If I make a mistake, it's really important for you to tell me on my talk page. It'd be even better if you told me how I could fix it rather than just fixing it yourself and not telling me. I learn through trial and error, just like most other people. I will definitely work my best to be the best I can be when it comes to wikipedia. It's helped me countless times before and I figured it was my turn to give back to the community.
My only interest is helping out. ♥ I'm not doing it for my own personal gain or to remember the information myself. I make all the changes to Wikipedia in good faith. So please remember to have WP:FAITH in me.
