Welcome, I'm Tom. I live in Wiltshire, United Kingdom, and have had a particular interest in cars ever since I was old enough to recognise separate brands and models. I own two cars, a 2004 Fiat Stilo Abarth, which is great in all respects, and a newly-acquired Mercedes-Benz E280CDi T-Modell, which has a huge amount of space. Previous to the Mercedes I had a 2005 Chevrolet Corvette C6, which was less than great in just about all aspects. Particular concerns about the Corvette centred around its flimsy build quality, numerous troublesome dealership experiences, and its concerning appetite for tyres and/or light bulbs.

I have a particular interest in automobiles on Wikipedia, especially articles on my own vehicles (Fiat Stilo, which I haven't done yet) and generally TVRs. Right now I'm just too engrossed in my own doings - see TVR Sagaris and Aston Martin DB9 and then revisit them in six months time! I'm also a reader of Autocar, Britain's oldest motoring magazine (founded 1895), which I have a subscription to. I've also got a diecast model collection, which seems to be growing every day, with the newest addition being an AUTOart Porsche 996 GT3 RS.
