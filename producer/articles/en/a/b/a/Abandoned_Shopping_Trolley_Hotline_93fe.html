Abandoned Shopping Trolley Hotline is a 2000 compilation of previously unreleased material, including BBC Radio 1 studio sessions, outtakes and B-sides by UK band Gomez. Some of tracks foreshadow the band's move into electronic-based music on 2002's In Our Gun.
