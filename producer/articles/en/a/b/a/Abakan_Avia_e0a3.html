Abakan Avia (Russian: АБАКАН-АВИА) (IATA: N/A, ICAO: ABG, and Callsign: Abakan Avia) is an airline based in Abakan, Russia. It was established in 1992 and was a former local Aeroflot unit. It operates passenger and cargo trunk services. Its main base is Abakan Airport (ABA). In 2002 a proposed merger with RusAir failed.

As of August 2006 the Abakan Avia fleet includes:
