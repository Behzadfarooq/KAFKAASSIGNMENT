Abaddon is a Biblical Hebrew word meaning "destruction".

Abaddon may refer to:

  • Abaddon (band), a Polish band.
  • Abaddon (demon), chief of the demons of the seventh hierarchy.
  • Abaddon: Fallen Angel, a freeware Role Playing Game for the Personal Computer
  • Abaddon the Despoiler, a character in the Warhammer 40,000 fictional universe
  • Abaddon Books, an imprint of Rebellion Developments

Abaddon may also refer to:

  • In A Wish for Wings (a web comic), a character
  • Abadon El Exterminador, a novel by Argentinian writer Ernesto Sabato
  • "Abaddon's Bolero", the closing track on Emerson Lake & Palmer's "Trilogy" (1972).
  • In the Achaea MUD, a God of Mhaldor
  • In Angel Sanctuary, a son of Lucifer, a monster with a deadly gaze
  • In Battle Angel Alita, a defensive weapon used by the city of Tiphares.
  • In the film Bloody Mallory, a villain attempts to summon the demon Abaddon and plunge the world into darkness.
  • In Castlevania: Dawn of Sorrow, a cricket-like boss located in the Abyss who attacks using locusts. This boss is also present in Castlevania: Portrait of Ruin.
  • In multiple DC Comics series, including The Justice League of America, The Demon, and The Books of Magic, a fallen angel called Abaddon the Destroyer
  • In Diablo II: Lord of Destruction, a dungeon area in Act V
  • In the 2006 Doctor Who episode "The Impossible Planet", The Ood exclaim that "The Beast shall rise from the pit," and that he is known by many names, one of which is Abaddon.
  • In the 2006 Doctor Who spinoff series Torchwood, a demonic entity referred to as Abaddon was released from the Cardiff Rift and was described as 'the Great Devourer', bringing death to anyone that was caught in his shadow in End of Days.
  • In DotA Allstars (a Warcraft III custom game), a Scourge hero titled "Lord of Avernus"
  • In the Final Fantasy series, a monster
  • In Guild Wars, a fiery realm called Abaddon's Mouth
  • In Guild Wars Nightfall, Abbadon is the banished sixth god of Tyria.
  • In The Magic Kingdom of Landover, the demonic realm.
  • Michael Lynn, a technology expert who uses the handle Abaddon
  • In the manga Rebirth, a summoning attack called Abaddon's Kiss.
  • In early drafts of the Return of the Jedi script, the name for the Imperial capital was Had Abaddon.
  • Saga of Pliocene Exile, the character Mark Remillard
  • The Scabbed Wings of Abaddon, a free novel about an alternative afterlife] on a plane ruled by the angel Abaddon
  • In the StarCraft universe, an ash world featured in StarCraft: Ghost
  • Summer in Abaddon, 2004 album by the indie rock band Pinback
  • Pseudonym of Tony Bray, drummer in black metal band Venom
  • In Unreal Tournament 2004, a cybernetic character
  • In the 2006 film, Prairie Home Companion, Abbadon is shown as the Angel of Death who kills an agent of a national radio station to allow the show to continue
  • In the MMORPG Eve-online, a space battleship.
