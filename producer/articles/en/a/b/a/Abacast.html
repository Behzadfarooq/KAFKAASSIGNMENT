Abacast

Abacast is a peer-to-peer audio and video streaming system developed in the early 2000s. It is maintained by Abacast Inc. as a paid-use system, with a variety of additional services for supporting "ad injection" and digital rights management. It is primarily deployed with Windows Media streams, but also supports Shoutcast.

[edit] Stations that use Abacast

  • KING-FM
  • Wisconsin Public Radio
  • Niagara Falls camera streams
  • WTKT - 99.5 FM Syracuse
  • RadioU - 88.7 FM Columbus Ohio
  • Somafm

[edit] See also

  • Other commercial P2P live streaming companies include Rawflow and Octoshape
