Abashera is a first person perspective-maze-platform game. The objective is to find, and sometimes activate, the exit by solving different tasks. The maps are filled with very single minded creatures and much of the point of the game is to learn these monster's behaviors. Different dogs eat each other, frogs jump when you move in front of them,

"Strawgoat", climb-able object

Abashera revamped is the second version of Abashera. There are a lot of differences, such as more natural movement, more realistic landscapes with weather, fractal ground and vegetation. Abashera revamped is also more adventure orientated than its predecessor, players could for instance capture and ride dragons, find the Holy Grail and locate the Ark of the Covenant in some of the mazes.

Abashera is a freeware program made by the artist Max Magnus Norman.
