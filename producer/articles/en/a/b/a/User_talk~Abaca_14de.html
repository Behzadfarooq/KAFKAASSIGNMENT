[edit] Infobox

I appreciate your edits to the Infobox inside Gravity's Rainbow. However, it's generally consensus within the WikiNovels project that whenever information on a book's first edition is available, it takes precedence over other editions. See Wikipedia:WikiProject_Novels/GeneralForum.

Secondly, Released applies to the original publication date (1973 in this case), not the publication date of a given edition (2006, as you suggested). Series refers to an author's series of books, not a publisher's. Spamguy 16:14, 14 May 2006 (UTC)

[edit] License tagging for Image:Vbytrp.jpeg

Thanks for uploading Image:Vbytrp.jpeg. Wikipedia gets thousands of images uploaded every day, and in order to verify that the images can be legally used on Wikipedia, the source and copyright status must be indicated. Images need to have an image tag applied to the image description page indicating the copyright status of the image. This uniform and easy-to-understand method of indicating the license status allows potential re-users of the images to know what they are allowed to do with the images.

For more information on using images, see the following pages:

  • Wikipedia:Image use policy
  • Wikipedia:Image copyright tags

This is an automated notice by OrphanBot. If you need help on selecting a tag to use, or in adding the tag to the image description, feel free to post a message at Wikipedia:Media copyright questions. 01:07, 12 July 2006 (UTC)
