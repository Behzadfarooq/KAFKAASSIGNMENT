ABC Supply Company Incorporated is a roofing supply company based in Beloit, Wisconsin. It also sells windows, gutters, and siding for residential and commercial.

It was founded in 1982 by Ken Hendricks. There are currently 300+ stores nationwide. Ken Hendricks is the #107 richest person in America according to Forbes October 2006 issue.

It currently sponsors A.J. Foyt Enterprises' #14 Indy Racing League car driven by Darren Manning.
