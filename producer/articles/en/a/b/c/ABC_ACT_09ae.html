[edit] ABC Television ACT

ABC TV Canberra is a territory-wide television network, airing the programs of the national broadcaster, the Australian Broadcasting Corporationin addition to a small number of locally produced programs.

The main station is ABC-9 Canberra, with studios in the suburb of Dickson and transmitter at Black Mountain.

The schedule is similar to the national schedule, with some exceptions.

[edit] ABC NEWS: Canberra

A 1/2 hour news program covering local, national and international news sports and weather. It is broadcast 7 days a week at 7pm with updates throughout the evening.

The 7.00pm weekday news bulletin is presented by Virginnia Haussegger

[edit] ABC Radio (666 ABC Canberra)

The ABC local radio station fo the Australian Capital Territory. In addition to broadcasting national ABC programing, 666AM features local programs Afternoons with David Kilby, Mornings with Alex Sloan and Saturday Mornings with Rod Quinn.
