ABC Western

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

ABC Western is a statewide television network, airing the programs of the national broadcaster, the Australian Broadcasting Corporation.

The main station is ABW-2 Perth, with studios in the suburb of East Perth and transmitter at Bickley. The station is transmitted throughout the state via a series of relay transmitters, and via satellite to areas that cannot receive normal terrestrial television signals.

The schedule is similar to the national schedule, with some exceptions.

The 7.00pm news bulletin is presented by Alicia Gorey.
