[edit] Creation of new articles

I noticed your creation of a few new articles and thought I'd give you some pointers. I recommend you read over the pointers, and then improve the articles you have created already, rather than create more.

  1. There is a criterion that allows an article to be immediately deleted if it is very short or provides little or no context. So try to create more substantial entries
  2. Wikipedia:Stub has some information on what should be included in new articles
  3. Wikipedia:Notability has some ideas on what should have an article and what should not.

Consider adding some more facts to the articles, Categories, a stub notice etc. You can view your contributions at Special:Contributions/Abcfan.--Commander Keane 19:09, 18 March 2006 (UTC)

[edit] Lester Holt Live

Hello, and welcome to Wikipedia! We welcome and appreciate your contributions, such as Lester Holt Live, but we regretfully cannot accept copyrighted text or images borrowed from either web sites or printed material. This article appears to be a direct copy from http://www.msnbc.msn.com/id/3688725/, and therefore a copyright violation. The copyrighted text has been or will soon be deleted.

If you believe that the article is not a copyright violation, or if you have permission from the copyright holder to release the content freely under the GNU Free Documentation License (GFDL), you can comment to that effect on Talk:Lester Holt Live. Then you should do one of the following:

  • Make a note on the original website that re-use is permitted under the GFDL and state at Talk:Lester Holt Live where we can find that note; or
  • Send an e-mail from an address associated with the original publication to permissions(at)wikimedia(dot)org or a postal message to the Wikimedia Foundation permitting re-use under the GFDL, and note that you have done so on Talk:Lester Holt Live.

It is also important that the text be modified to have an encyclopedic tone and that it follows Wikipedia article layout. For more information, see Wikipedia's policies and guidelines. Thank you, and please feel welcome to continue contributing to Wikipedia. Happy editing! -- ReyBrujo 22:06, 28 October 2006 (UTC)
