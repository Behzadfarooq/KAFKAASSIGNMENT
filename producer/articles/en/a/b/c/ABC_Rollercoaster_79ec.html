ABC Rollercoaster is a children's television segment run by the Australian Broadcasting Corporation in Australia. Hosted by Elliot Spencer, it is aimed at an older audience than ABC Kids, and is time-shifted between ABC TV and the digital-only ABC2. As of Monday June 19, 2006, Rollercoaster Rewind began on ABC TV, which is a repeat of the programming from 4 months ago in reverse order.

  • 6am-7am ABC TV (Channel 02)
  • 5pm-6pm ABC TV (Channel 02)
  • 6pm-7pm ABC2 (Channel 21)
