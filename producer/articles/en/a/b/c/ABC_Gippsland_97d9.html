ABC Gippsland is the ABC's Local Radio network in Gippsland, Victoria, Australia.

It began operating on 31 October 1935 as the ABC's first radio station in regional Victoria, also boasting the state's most powerful radio transmittor.

Today the network features local news and breakfast content as well as content from the ABC's Melbourne studios 774 ABC Melbourne.
