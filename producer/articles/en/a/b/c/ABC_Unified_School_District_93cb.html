The ABC Unified School District is a school district that is based in Cerritos, California, United States.

ABCUSD serves the cities of Artesia, most of Cerritos, Hawaiian Gardens, the portion of Lakewood east of the San Gabriel River, as well as tiny portions of Long Beach, Norwalk, and La Mirada (the ABC portion of La Mirada contains only business areas, no residents).

"ABC" stands for Artesia (founded in 1875), Bloomfield (founded in 1885) and Carmenita (founded in 1902), three districts which were unified in 1965 [1].

[edit] Elementary Schools

  • Aloha Elementary School
  • Bragg Elementary School educates students in grades kindergarten through sixth. As of 2006, the school principal is Dr. Scott Wilbur. The school mascot is the Bobcat. The school enrolls approximately 650 students each year. The student-teacher ratio is 23:1. 22.6% of students are eligible for the government-sponsored free and reduced lunch program, compared to 35.1% within the district and 48.5% within the state of California.
