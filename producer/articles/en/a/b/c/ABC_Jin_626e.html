ABC is a 2007 album by Chinese-American rapper Jin. ABC is Jin's fourth overall album, which was released on February 20, 2007. The album was done entirely in Cantonese and all the tracks were produced by the Far East Movement. "ABC" stands for "American Born Chinese". The first single from this album is "ABC." A music video was shot for it and debuted on MTV Chi on January 26, 2007.
