ABC islands (Lesser Antilles)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The ABC islands are Aruba, Bonaire and Curaçao, the western islands of the Leeward Antilles; the last two are in the Netherlands Antilles, all are part of the Kingdom of the Netherlands.

They are north of Falcón State, Venezuela, from west to east in the order Aruba, Curaçao, Bonaire.
