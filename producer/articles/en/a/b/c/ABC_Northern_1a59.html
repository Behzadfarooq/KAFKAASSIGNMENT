ABC Northern

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

ABC Northern is a Northern Territory-wide television network, airing the programs of the national broadcaster, the Australian Broadcasting Corporation.

The main station is ABD-6 Darwin, with studios in the inner city of Darwin and transmitter at Blake Street. The station is transmitted throughout the territory via a series of relay transmitters, and via satellite to areas that cannot receive normal terrestrial television signals.

The schedule is similar to the national schedule, with some exceptions.
