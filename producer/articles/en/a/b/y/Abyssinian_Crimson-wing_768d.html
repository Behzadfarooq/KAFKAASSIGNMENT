The Abyssinian Crimson-wing Cryptospiza salvadorii is a common species of estrildid finch found in Africa. It has an estimated global extent of occurrence of 190,000 kmÂ².

It is found in Burundi, The Democratic Republic of the Congo, Ethiopia, Kenya, Rwanda, Sudan,Tanzania and Uganda.
