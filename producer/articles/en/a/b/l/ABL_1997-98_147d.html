ABL 1997-98

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

You have new messages (last change).

The 1997-98 season of the Australian Baseball League saw the Melbourne Reds become the first champions defeating the Gold Coast Cougars 2 games to 0 in the Championship series, infront of a rather small home crowd of 600 at the Melbourne Ballpark.
