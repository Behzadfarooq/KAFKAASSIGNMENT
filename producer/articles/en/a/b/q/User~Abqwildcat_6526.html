From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

I began contributing on February 20, 2004 as a result of repeated linking and the addition of a wikipedia search in my installation of Mozilla FireFox. I'm currently at 655 edits, and still going strong. I spend a fair amount of time each day making sure that the pages I've contributed to are still up to snuff.

I am currently an Engineer living and working in Seattle, Washington. My degree is in Aerospace Engineering with a minor in physics.

I lived in Tucson for 7 years over the course of my stay at the University of Arizona. During my time there, I definitely came to understand that Tucson is hot in the summer, mild in the winter, dusty year-round, and smells like a sewer at night. The Roger Road Sewage Treatment Plant is many miles from where you may be living, but at night the whole city stinks.

I hail originally from Albuquerque, New Mexico. I lived there 18 years and get back whenever I can. That's partially due to the passion that New Mexicans have for our green chile. It's the state pasttime to get together at a great New Mexican restaurant and have good New Mexican food.

Anyway, I may contribute in a lot of different ways - I have a way of both wasting time and knowing a lot about a few things, a little about a lot of things, and nothing about almost nothing. Ok, well almost anyway.
