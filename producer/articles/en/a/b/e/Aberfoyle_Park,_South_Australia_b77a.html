Aberfoyle Park, South Australia

Aberfoyle Park (pronouced ['æbǝˌfoɪl paːk]; located at 35°04′S, 138°36′E) is a suburb in South Australia. It has a mall called The Hub, several primary schools and a high school, one of the biggest in the state.

Aberfoyle Park is part of the City of Onkaparinga. It is in the state electoral district of Fisher, whose current member is Dr. Bob Such and the federal Division of Mayo, whose representative is Alexander Downer, Australia's current foreign affairs minister.

The Happy Valley sports park, which is located near Aberfoyle Park HIgh School, contains the Happy valley football club who are known as the Vikings, Happy Valley tennis club who are known as the Llama's, a lawn bowls club and a BMX track.
