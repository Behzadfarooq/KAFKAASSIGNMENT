Hi! My name is Abel Cavaşi, I live in Romania, I am born in 1965,I am married, licensed in mathematics and crazy about Physics (that, in personal works, I am writing with capital letter). I am considering that, as the programming is a wizard for computer screens, Physics is a wizard for the real world.


I am very lucky that I discovered this wonder named Wikipedia, created by people as wonderful as Wikipedia is. I dream about being able to give a bit of my time and energy for forward this becoming tresurehouse of human knowledge.


Due to the fact that I know mathematics and that I am devoted to Physics, I meditated on reality and I formed a stable conception, about which I am ready to discuss with you. I am publishing in the Romanian magazine of Physics called "Evrika" and I have a web site on the Internet: http://www.geocities.com/abelcavasi. My blog is on http://www.astronomy.ro/blogs/abelcavasi .


I consider that I managed to: -discover a new conservation law in mechanics (the conservation of volumic impulse) -resolve the Ehrenfest paradox -explain how did the Universe appeared -discover the importance of overforce in Physics.


I still have to tell you that i am repelled to (not unjustified) to many modern Physics knowledges as:

-the principle of indetermination and all the quantum mechanics conceptions which propose the world unknowable -the Big-Bang theory -the explanations for the comet tail or for the Earths precession. I am also repelled to those who teach Physics in a manner that don't allow you to see all its lacks, presenting it to you as an untouchable and unchangeable something, discountenancing, through this, any researching trial.



I am greedily reading from Wikipedia, from the Feynman work "Modern Physics", from "The Physics course Berkeley" or from "The General History of Science", leaded by Rene Taton.



The idea of uniting the fields and all its consequences its contusing me. I have a totally different vision about this idea and I take it from a different point of view that you cannot find nowadays. For example, I believe that there is a single field (which I name general or universal field) that can modify the impulse, kinetic moment, volumic impulse and the energy of a system. I also believe that the electric charged is a variable volumic impulse and I also believe that a magnetic field creates moments of forces perpendicularly to kinetic moment, so it is acting over neutral corps from the electric point of view. I don't believe that the uniting of fields can be made only at high energy.
