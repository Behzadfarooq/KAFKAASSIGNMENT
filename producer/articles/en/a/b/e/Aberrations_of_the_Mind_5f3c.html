From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Aberrations of the Mind
Studio album by Morgana Lefay
Released March 19th, 2007
Recorded Studio Soundcreation, Bollnäs, Sweden
Genre Heavy metal
Power metal
Thrash metal
Label Black Mark Records
Producer(s) Morgana Lefay & Per Ryberg
Morgana Lefay chronology
Grand Materia (2005) Aberrations Of The Mind (2007)


Morgana Lefay's 10th studio album, released March 19th, 2007. First album with Pelle Åkerlind on drums.

[edit] Songs

All music & lyrics written by Morgana Lefay

  1. Delusions
  2. Make A Wish
  3. The Rush Of Possession
  4. Depression
  5. Caught In The Treadwheel
  6. Reflections Of War
  7. Face Of Fear
  8. Where I Rule
  9. In Shadows I Reign
  10. Aberration Of Mind
  11. Vultures Devouring
  12. Over And Over Again


The Digipak version of the album has the song "Nightmares Are Made In Hell" as a bonus track.
