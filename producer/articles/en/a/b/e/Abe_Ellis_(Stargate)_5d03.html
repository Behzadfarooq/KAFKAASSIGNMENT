Colonel Abe Ellis is a fictional character in the science fiction television series Stargate Atlantis played by Michael Beach.

[edit] History

Spoiler warning: Plot and/or ending details follow.

Colonel Abe Ellis first appeared as the commander of the new Earth ship Apollo, his mission was to commence a pre-emptive attack on the Asuran homeworld as they have been building ships to attack Atlantis/Earth. The mission is successful, however, the Asurans send a satellite containing a stargate and fire an energy beam at the ship which damages the shields and then the beam is redirected at Atlantis. In a plan to fly Atlantis away from the satellite weapon, Col. Ellis beams up all nonessential personnel aboard the Apollo, and uses a flight of F-302's to send an asteroid into the path of the satellite beam, allowing Atlantis to escape and hyperspace away from Lantea.
