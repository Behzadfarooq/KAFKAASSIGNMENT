Abe Mickal

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Abe Mickal
Date of birth July 9, 1912
Place of birth Talia, Lebanon
Position(s) Running back
College LSU
NFL Draft 1936 / Round 6 / Pick 53

Abe "Miracle" Mickal (July 9, 1912 - September 20, 2001) was an American football player. He was named to the College Football Hall of Fame for his career as a running back at LSU and went on to play in the NFL.
