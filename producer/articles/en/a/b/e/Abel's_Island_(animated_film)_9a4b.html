Abel's Island (animated film)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

You have new messages (last change).

Abel's Island is a 30 minute animated film created in 1988 directed by Michael Sporn. It is based off the children's novel Abel's Island written by William Steig. It won an Emmy Award in 1989 for Most Outstanding Program (For Programming less than one hour).
