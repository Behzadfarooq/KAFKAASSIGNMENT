Aberystwyth Athletic Club or Clwb Athletau Aberystwyth is an amateur athletic club based in the town of Aberystwyth on the coast of mid Wales. The club was formed on the 9th of September 1955 by Ron Cullum.

They host the very popular Aberystwyth 10K race which was first held in 1999. The 2005 race attracted over 300 participants and shows no sign of losing appeal.
