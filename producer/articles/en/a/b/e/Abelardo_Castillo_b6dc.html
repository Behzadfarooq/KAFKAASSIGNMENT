Abelardo Castillo

Abelardo Castillo (b. March 27, 1935) is an Argentine writer, born in the city of San Pedro, Buenos Aires. He practised amateur boxing in his youth. He also directed the literary magazines El Escarabajo de Oro and El Ornitorrinco.
