Abel Santa Maria Airport (IATA: SNU, ICAO: MUSC) is an international airport serving Santa Clara, Cuba.

[edit] Airlines and destinations

  • Air Transat (Calgary, Montreal, Quebec City, Toronto-Pearson)
  • CanJet (Montreal)[1]
  • Cubana de Aviación (Havana)
  • Livingston (Milan-Malpensa)
  • Monarch Airlines (Manchester (UK))
  • Skyservice (Montreal, Toronto-Pearson)[2]
  • Westjet (Montreal)
