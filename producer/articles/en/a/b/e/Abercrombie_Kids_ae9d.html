abercrombie Kids, or in the advertised form, abercrombie, is a childrens version of the popular Abercrombie & Fitch "Casual Luxury" retailer. It is one of their four concept stores, but is mainly targeted towards boys and girls ages 7-14 years old, unlike the other three concepts.

[edit] Comparisons to A&F

[edit] Similarities

Like Abercrombie & Fitch, abercrombie sells the best clothing inspired by the American Lifestyle and is often described as preppy. The designs of both abercrombie and Abercrombie & Fitch are very much alike; both stores have the same architectural design, posters of the current season models are plastered throughout both stores, using a blue color scheme rather than grayscale. In both, Dance Music is blasted and the signature fragrance is sprayed throughout the store.

[edit] Differences

  • Lower price point than Abercrombie & Fitch, and obvious size differences. abercrombie's prices are typically 35% 40% lower than those at Abercrombie & Fitch. Typically, shirts, and tees cost around $15-$29 while jeans cost $39.50-$59.50. Hoodies and sweaters are around $40 to $50, while swimwear is about $25 to $30 per piece (meaning girls' tops and bottoms).
  • The jeans are styled after Abercrombie & Fitch jeans, with the same back pocket stitching, and similar names.
  • abercrombie models depicted on the store's posters are not partly nude as in A&F posters. However, girls' shirts are low-cut and boys' pants are worn very low in the pictures.
  • abercrombie shopping bags are different. The handles are navy blue and not gray like A&F shopping bags.
  • The adult store is usually larger in size, much darker, and much louder. Often Abercrombie and Fitch has shutter like walls at the entrance while abercrombie has windows.
  • The abercrombie kids logo is in all lowercase and the Abercrombie and Fitch logo isn't.

[edit] Trivia

  • Past abercrombie models include Lindsay Lohan and Christina Akatsuka.
  • As of August 14, 2006 there are 164 abercrombie stores throughout the United States, with expansion into Canada. There are currently no stores outside of the United States and Canada.
