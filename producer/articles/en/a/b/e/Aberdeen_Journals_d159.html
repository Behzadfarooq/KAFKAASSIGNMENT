Aberdeen Journals Ltd. is a newspaper publisher based in Aberdeen, Scotland.

The company publishes the Press and Journal and Evening Express newspapers. It was owned by Northcliffe Newspapers Group, which is owned by Daily Mail & General Trust until 2006, when Aberdeen Journals was sold to Dundee based D. C. Thomson & Co. Ltd.
