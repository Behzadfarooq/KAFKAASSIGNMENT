Aberdeen Lock and Dam

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Aberdeen Lock and Dam is part of the Tennessee-Tombigbee Waterway. It impounds Aberdeen Lake.

Aberdeen Lock and Dam is located at 33°49′48″N, 88°31′12″W east of Aberdeen in Monroe County, Mississippi.
