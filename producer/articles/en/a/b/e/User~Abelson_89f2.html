Yours truly in Hollywood, U.S.A.

I am Swedish and live in Lund in southern Sweden with my girlfriend. I have a degree as Master of Science in Computer Engineering from Lund Institute of Technology, and I work as a Software Development Manager for Actix, a company in the telecom industry. I'm a member of IEEE and IEEE Computer Society. I hold three certifications from Sun Microsystems in Java. When not working, I enjoy playing Battlefield 2 online and Lumines on my PSP. Visit my personal web site.

Have I any relation to Hal Abelson? Don't know, but we seem to be in the same line of business.

I joined Wikipedia on July 15, 2004. Since then I have contributed with photos, with articles on Swedish subjects, and with more than 1000 edits to existing articles.
