Abe Masatsugu

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Abe Masatsugu (阿部正次; 1569-1647) was the son of Abe Masakatsu, one of the long lived retainers of Tokugawa Ieyasu.

Masatsugu served under Tokugawa Hidetada (eldest son of Ieyasu), in the Osaka campaigns of 1614 - 1615. In 1620 he was given Odawara in Sagami Province.
