Liverpool Abercromby
Borough constituency
Created: 1885
Abolished: 1918
Type: House of Commons

Liverpool Abercromby was a borough constituency represented in the House of Commons of the Parliament of the United Kingdom. It elected one Member of Parliament (MP) by the first past the post system of election.
