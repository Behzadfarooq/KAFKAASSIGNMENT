Abeno Station (阿倍野駅, Abeno-eki) is a railway station on the Tanimachi Line of the Osaka Municipal Subway, as well as a tram stop on the Hankai Uemachi Line, in Abeno-ku, Osaka, Japan.

[edit] Platforms

Osaka Municipal Subway Tanimachi Line (T28)
  • an island platform and two tracks
  1. for Fuminosato and Yaominami
  2. for Tennoji, Higashi-Umeda and Dainichi
Hankai Uemachi Line
  • two side platforms and two tracks
  • Southbound for Sumiyoshikoen and Abikomichi
  • Northbound for Tennoji-eki-mae

[edit] Establishments around the station

  • Abeno BELTA
  • Tsuji Group (cooking school)
