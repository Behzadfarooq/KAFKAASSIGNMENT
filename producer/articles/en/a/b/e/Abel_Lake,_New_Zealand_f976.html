Abel Lake, New Zealand

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Abel Lake
Location South Island
Coordinates 43.3410° S 170.6383° E
Basin countries New Zealand

Abel Lake is a lake in the Westland region of the South Island of New Zealand. It is located in the Southern Alps and is fed by a mountain stream. Another stream links the lake with the Perth River.
