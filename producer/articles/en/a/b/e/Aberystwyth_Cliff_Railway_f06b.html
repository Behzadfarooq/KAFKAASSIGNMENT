Aberystwyth Cliff Railway

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Aberystwyth Cliff Railway (Welsh: Rheilffordd y Graig) is a funicular railway in Aberystwyth, Wales, opened in 1896. Originally operated on a water balance system, it was electrified in 1921. The railway climbs 430 ft in 778 ft—a gradient steeper than 1:2 (50%).

Its twin carriages are named The Lord Geraint and the Lord Marks.
