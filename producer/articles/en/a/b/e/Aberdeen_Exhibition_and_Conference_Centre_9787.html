Aberdeen Exhibition and Conference Centre

The Aberdeen Exhibition and Conference Centre is a large exhibition and conference complex in Aberdeen, Scotland. The complex is home to a Holiday Inn hotel, conference facilities and the 4,700-seat multi-purpose Press & Journal Arena. The arena currently hosts local sporting events and concerts, including the Aberdeen Cup tennis event. In recent years the AECC underwent a major refurbishment, with the conference facilities being completely re-built. As part of the redevelopment, a large viewing tower was constructed and is one of the tallest structures in Aberdeen.

P!nk played a two date concert during her I'm Not Dead Tour at the arena on the 20th and 21st of November 2006. Other recent performers at the P&J Arena include The Killers, Westlife, Muse and Lionel Richie. The arena has also played host to WWE wrestling events and The X Factor Live Tour.
