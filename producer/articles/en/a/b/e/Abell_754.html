Abell 754

Abell 754 is a cluster of galaxies in the constellation Hydra that was formed from the collision of two smaller clusters. This collision, which began about 300 million years ago, is ongoing, and the system is still disturbed. Eventually, the cluster will reach a level of equlibrium in a few billion years.
