Aberdeen-Matawan is a station on New Jersey Transit's North Jersey Coast Line, 5 miles east of South Amboy and 2 miles west of Hazlet. This station is very popular with commuters, and is the

busiest station on the line, between Bay Head and Rahway.

The station is located at grade on Atlantic Avenue, on the border of Aberdeen Twp. and Matawan Borough, and has two high side platforms and two tracks. A ticket office is located on the New York bound platform.

All trains serve this station.
