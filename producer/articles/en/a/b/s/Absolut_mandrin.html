ABSOLUT MANDRIN

Type Flavored Vodka
Proof 100
Manufacturer V&S Group
Country of Origin Åhus, Skåne Sweden
Introduced 1999
Related products ABSOLUT VODKA

ABSOLUT MANDRIN is a flavored vodka from the Swedish brand of ABSOLUT VODKA, owned by V&S Group. In 1999, seven years after the fourth family member ABSOLUT KURANT was launched, ABSOLUT MANDRIN was introduced to the market.

ABSOLUT MANDRIN is a unique contribution to the citrus-flavored vodkasegment and like all members of the ABSOLUT VODKA family, it is produced in Åhus in the province of Skåne in Sweden.

The taste is complex, smooth and mellow. A fruity character of mandarin and orange mixed with a note of orange peel.

Citrus is the largest flavored-vodka segment. ABSOLUT MANDRIN with its unique taste combination of orange, mandarin and other citrus fruits, brings something new to the segment. ABSOLUT MANDRIN is also a good complement to ABSOLUT CITRON within the citrus segment.

The bottle has, since the launch in 1999, received about six international design awards.
