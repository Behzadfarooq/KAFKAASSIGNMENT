Absolutist Games is an online gaming development and publishing company founded in 2000. A corporate website is a casual gaming portal for multiplatform downloadable and online casual games of different genres with monthly traffic exceeding 1 million visitors. The company has developed over 70 unique software titles, operating on platforms including Windows, Mac OS X, Linux, Palm OS, Windows Mobile, Windows Smartphone, Symbian (Series 60, 80, UIQ). [edit] Products

Absolutist focuses on producing family friendly content, including card games, board games, puzzles and logic games, arcade and word games. The company is working on producing new software titles by taking plot from the real life and combining gaming experience of many nations. The most popular games by Absolutist are the games of the "Bubble Series" - Bubble Shooter Deluxe and Bubble Golden Pack Deluxe. In 2006, the company released Sudoku Pagoda, an electronic remake of the Japanese Sudoku game.

[edit] The most popular games developed by Absolutist:

  • Bubble Shooter
  • Elythril - the Elf Treasure

  • Rotate Mania Deluxe
  • Aquabble Avalanche

[edit] Order process

As a rule, the shareware games provided by Absolutist can be downloaded on the Internet for free as a feature-limited version. The full version of the games is available upon purchase. Many of the shareware games developed by Absolutist have a free online version.

[edit] Development process

The developer team of the company consists of 50 people. The most frequently used programming languages within the company are C++, Java, and Delphi. The team develops various categories of applications - both for consumer and business. Absolutist completes projects concerned with the development of software applications for business and consumer ported for many computer platforms.
