Absolute Garbage is the title for the forthcoming greatest hits album from Garbage. The album is due out on June 4, 2007 in the UK; and presumably a day later in North America.

"It's All Over But the Crying" was rumoured to be the third U.S. single off Bleed Like Me, but was never released due to the band going on hiatus in November of 2005.[citation needed]
