Absolution Tour is a DVD released by the English rock band Muse. The DVD contains footage of most of the band's headline performance at the Glastonbury Festival 2004 (it does not contain the footage of "Citizen Erased" or "Stockholm Syndrome", though Stockholm Syndrome can be seen in the Earls Court footage); it also features some previously unseen footage from the Earls Court Exhibition Centre and Wembley Arena in London, and the Wiltern Theater in Los Angeles. The tracklist includes most of the songs from their third studio album, Absolution, plus performances of some older material. The DVD was also included for free as a bonus disc with the Limited Edition release of "Black Holes and Revelations" in the United States (the only way in which to get an NTSC version of the DVD). The DVD has been criticised for its lack of scope on the tour, and just focusing on the one show.

[edit] Hidden Extras (Easter eggs) and Instructions

  1. "Thoughts Of A Dying Atheist" - (Live at Wembley Arena)
  2. "Endlessly" - (Live at Wembley Arena)

To get to the Hidden Tracks, go to the Extras Menu and select the title "Fury". Press the up key once. Now the word "Back" is highlighted. Then press the left arrow key and the 'X' in Extras will light up. Press enter and the hidden footage will play.
