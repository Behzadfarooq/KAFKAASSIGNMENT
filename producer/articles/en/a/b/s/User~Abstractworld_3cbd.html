'Abstract Thinking[Italic text]

The Hidden Indiviuals" I believe that society has failed a certain amount of indiviuals who perhaps have an amount of intelligence not easy to measure. The creative yet not noticed indiviual who perhaps has a perfect score in abstract thinking on a skills or IQ test for instance. What does a perfect score in performance of completing mazes mean, for example.

Does this mean that a person is an abnormal indiviual, when only one score is this high and other scores average, below average or slightly above average. What does this mean. I myself struggle to understand where I belong, and would love to discuss with others who have the same score, in that particular area. Surely I am not the only one.

I do know know that I would feel the most comfortable learning and gaining an education through a program where an indiviual may learn at an indiviual pace, with both computer and experiental learning. That I fell in love with the SRA learning by passing levels and that this indiviual type of learning combined with other types of learning may be an anwser for some indiviuals who feel that a regular classroom does not seem to be quite the learning environment that is needed to reach full potential. I also really related to the Montessori Method of learning which also is a type of indiviual type of learning. I am concerned about those who perhaps are left to struggle unneccesarily when there are so many alternatives to help indiviual reach their full potential, even as adults or rather especially as adults. Children should be afforded every extra opportunity and so many of us weren't. We are the ones who fall through the cracks yet simply needed the best learning environment suited to our indiviuals needs.

Those who are best able to do so should search out those who have hidden abilities and simply didn't recieve the optimal learning situation. I am looking for those who agree with me or those who simply would like to discuss this with me. Since the brain is active and continually growing new cells and the structure, learning, growth, intelligence and how it works within the brain, and the way we remember and learn are all subject to new research I feel that there is room for those with a hidden talent waiting to be developed and or discovered!
