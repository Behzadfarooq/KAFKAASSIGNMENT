[edit] So you've been to Ushuaia...

I have a fascination for this; I just wanted to discuss the place with ya. What time of year did you go? Do you ever feel like its the end of the world when you are there? --NicAgent 02:21, 17 May 2006 (UTC)

I don't remember exactly, but probably went around January or Feb, since those are the usual 'vacations' months where I live. It was very cold for summer, but makes sense being so far south. Actually it did not feel like the end of the world, but just being there was great, the air was so clean I loved breathing it (this is true for the whole south area of Argentina). Why your fascination? --A/B 'Shipper 女 (talk) 13:54, 19 May 2006 (UTC)
