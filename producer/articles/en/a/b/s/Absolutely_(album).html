[edit] Personnel

  • Suggs – lead vocals
  • Mike Barson – keyboards, harmonica, marimba, percussion
  • Chris Foreman – guitar
  • Mark Bedford – bass guitar
  • Lee Thompson – saxophones
  • Daniel Woodgate – drums
  • Chas Smash – backing vocals, trumpet, lead vocals on track 7

[edit] Notes/trivia

  • The album is named after one of expressions of the band's then tour manager, and sound man Tony Duffield. [1]
