The Absecon Light is a lighthouse located in Atlantic City, New Jersey. It was built in 1857, was deactivated between 1933 and 1997, but it has been reactivated. It was designed by Lt. George Meade.

Listed in the National Register of Historic Places as reference #71000492. Listed in by the Historic American Buildings Survey number NJ-734.
