Absolutely Kosher Records

Absolutely Kosher Records is an independent Californian-based record label founded in 1998 in San Francisco by Cory Brown. The label moved to Berkeley in 2002 and then to Emeryville in October of 2006 when it partnered with Misra Records. The two labels remain separate entities.

It has been erroneously posted on this site that Absolutely Kosher is a member of the RIAA. The label is listed on the RIAA website as a distributed label of a reporting member (Fontana, the label's distributor) and has been so since 2005. Absolutely Kosher is not a member and does not pay dues nor correspond in any fashion with the RIAA.
