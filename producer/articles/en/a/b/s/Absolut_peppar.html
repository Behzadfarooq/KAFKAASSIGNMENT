ABSOLUT PEPPAR

Type Flavored Vodka
Proof 80
Manufacturer V&S Group
Country of Origin Åhus, Skåne Sweden
Introduced 1986
Related products ABSOLUT VODKA

ABSOLUT PEPPAR is a flavored vodka from the Swedish brand of ABSOLUT VODKA, owned by V&S Group. ABSOLUT PEPPAR was introduced in 1986. It is the first flavored vodka in the ABSOLUT VODKA family and it is made with only natural ingredients.

The taste of ABSOLUT PEPPAR is hot and spicy. It has a distinct character of green bell pepper, chili pepper and jalapeño pepper.

In the US, vodka is an ingredient in the popular drink Bloody Mary. ABSOLUT PEPPAR was developed as a base for this drink. It was the first flavored vodka in the world to be sold on the international market.

ABSOLUT PEPPAR is primarily used as a base in drinks and for shots.
