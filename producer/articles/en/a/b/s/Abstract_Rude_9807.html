Abstract Rude

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Abstract Rude (born Aaron Pointer) is an American rapper, from Los Angeles, CA. He is the MC from the hip hop group Abstract Tribe Unique (ATU), which features dancer Zulu Butterfly Prince, DJ Drez, and Underground Hip Hop producer, Fat Jack. Ab Rude is also a member of Haiku D'Etat (with Mikah 9 and Aceyalone) and The A-Team (with Aceyalone). [1] [2]
