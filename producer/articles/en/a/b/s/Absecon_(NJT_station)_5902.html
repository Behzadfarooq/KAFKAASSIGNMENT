Absecon Station is a New Jersey Transit station in Absecon, New Jersey on the Atlantic City Line. Through ticketing from Amtrak is available, although no Amtrak trains actually serve the location. Its Amtrak station code is ABN.

It is located at Shore Road and Ohio Ave.
