Please seek consensus and observe civility

Greetings. This encyclopedia operates by finding consensus as to the content and presentation of information in its articles. Please do not tag articles as disputed or NPOV without first discussing the matter on the article talk page. Also, please observe civility - quick resorts to vulgarity will undermine any views you wish to express to your fellow editors. Cheers! BD2412 T 20:18, 13 December 2005 (UTC)

Personal attacks are not allowed on Wikipedia

Name-calling and personal attacks, like the one you've made on Sherurcij are not allowed by the Wikipedia community. If you wish to contribute to Wikipedia, you must follow this rule like the rest of us. If Wikipedians disagree, we state our positions, talk about them in a civilized manner and try to reach an agreement. Name-calling is not allowed. See: Wikipedia:No personal attacks, and Wikipedia:Etiquette. --Valentinian 21:57, 13 December 2005 (UTC)
