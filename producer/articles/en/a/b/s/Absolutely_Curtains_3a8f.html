"Absolutely Curtains" is a mostly instrumental track (the only vocalisation is a chorus of 'ah-ahhhh!' by all 4 members of the group towards the end of the main part of the track) which closes Pink Floyd's 1972 album Obscured by Clouds. The last part of the track is a chant by the Magupa tribe, recorded for La Vallée, the film to which Obscured by Clouds is the soundtrack.

[edit] Personnel

  • Richard Wright - Organ, Piano, Synthesiser
  • Nick Mason - Percussion and Cymbals
  • Magupa tribe - Vocalisations
