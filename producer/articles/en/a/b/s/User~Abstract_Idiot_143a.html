[edit] About Me Section

Hey, my names Andy but you know me as Abstract Idiot. This account is new to Wikipedia, however I have had other accounts and I have contributated to Anti-Vandalism Efforts using those accounts. I've been using Wikipedia for around four years. I've been seriously editing Wikipedia for about one and a half years. I did alot of copyedit work under an IP Address before then.

While I do enjoy contributating to articles now and then, I feel my main job on Wikipedia is to be a member of the WP:CVU. Right now I use Vandal Fighter and all the people in IRC to help me catch vandals. If I catch you doing vandalism and I leave a warning on your page, nothing against you. I've forgotten your name five minutes later. If I delete your article, again, no offense. I'm just doing a job. People need to keep Wikipedia policy in order and I chose to be one of those people.

If I leave a message on your talk page and you are unsure what it is for, what you did, or how to prevent it in future just drop me a message at any time.

I also take pride in being in the minority of young Wikipedians. I'm not just around 14 or so, I'm 12. I can be very mature, I think, and I am extremly skilled at computers for my age, so I hope you don't judge me any differently.

Thanks!.
