ABS-CBN Interactive

ABS-CBN Interactive or ABSi is the multimedia and interactive subsidiary of ABS-CBN. Established in 2000 in the rise of interactive game shows of some of its weaker rivals, it is responsible for the creation and maintenance of the company's main website as well as the website of some of its shows. It is responsible for the production of interactive quizzes/activities. ABS-CBN interactive manage revenues from its online network game brand Tantra and text messaging revenues for its TV programs.

[edit] ABSi vs. ABS-CBNi

The term ABSi should not be confused with ABS-CBNi, which is another subsidiary of ABS-CBN called ABS-CBN International.
