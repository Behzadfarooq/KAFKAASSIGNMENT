In 1990, ABC released a comprehensive greatest hits package called Absolutely. This covered all the eras up until 1990, and had all their singles together. A video package featuring their promos was also released. One new song called The Look of Love (The 1990 Remix), was also released to promote it, but not with approval of by the band. Other remixes were included on this compilation, including "When Smokey Sings (The Miami Mix)", "Be Near Me (Munich Disco Mix)" and "One Better World" (Pickering-Park Mix).
