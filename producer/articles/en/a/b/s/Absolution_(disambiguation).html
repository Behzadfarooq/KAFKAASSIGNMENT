Absolution can mean:

  • Absolution, the pronouncement of God's forgiveness of sins.
  • Absolution (album), a 2003 musical album by the band Muse.
  • Absolution Tour (DVD), a DVD containing live footage of Muse performing most of the songs on Absolution album
  • Absolution, a hardcore punk band from New York City.
  • Absolution (film), a 1981 British film starring Richard Burton.
  • Absolution (play), a 2002 play starring Brennan Brown.
  • Absolution (play), a 1996 play by Robert William Sherwood.
  • Absolution (story), a short story written by F. Scott Fitzgerald
