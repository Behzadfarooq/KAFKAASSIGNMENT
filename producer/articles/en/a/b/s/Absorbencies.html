“absorbencies” redirects here. For other uses, see absorbencies (disambiguation).

Absorbencies is the brand of diapers that NASA astronauts wear during space flight. Each astronaut is issued three pairs, one for takeoff, one for landing, and an extra, in case the landing is scrubbed.[1]

They are an adult diaper laced with a super absorbent chemical, sodium polyacrylate.
