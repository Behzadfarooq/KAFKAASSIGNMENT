[edit] Addition of featured articles

Hi, please do not add articles to the list of featured articles, as you did with Ottoman Empire and Turkish language. Only articles whose featured status has been discussed and approved beforehand, following a well-defined process, are listed on this page. If you want an article added to the list, here is what you should do:

  1. Make sure that the article satisfies the featured articles criteria, and if not, improve it until it does;
  2. List the article on Wikipedia:Featured article candidates, and try to improve it based on the reviewers' comments and criticism;
  3. If all comments have been taken into account and the article satisfies the criteria, it will added to the list (by someone else than you).

If you have any question about this process, please do not hesitate to ask me. All the best, Schutz 11:55, 13 July 2006 (UTC)

I see that you have followed point 2 of the list above; unfortunately, you seem to have not checked if the article satisties the featured article criteria. Your nomination is likely to be rejected quickly, but you can always renominate once the article has been improved. Cheers, Schutz 13:51, 13 July 2006 (UTC)

[edit] License tagging for Image:Kusun Gozundeki Elazig.jpg

Thanks for uploading Image:Kusun Gozundeki Elazig.jpg. Wikipedia gets thousands of images uploaded every day, and in order to verify that the images can be legally used on Wikipedia, the source and copyright status must be indicated. Images need to have an image tag applied to the image description page indicating the copyright status of the image. This uniform and easy-to-understand method of indicating the license status allows potential re-users of the images to know what they are allowed to do with the images.

For more information on using images, see the following pages:

  • Wikipedia:Image use policy
  • Wikipedia:Image copyright tags

This is an automated notice by OrphanBot. If you need help on selecting a tag to use, or in adding the tag to the image description, feel free to post a message at Wikipedia:Media copyright questions. 14:07, 15 August 2006 (UTC)
