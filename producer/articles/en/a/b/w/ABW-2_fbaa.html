ABW-2 is a television station in Perth, Western Australia. It is affiliated with the Australian Broadcasting Corporation. The station began broadcasting on 7 May 1960. Its studios are in East Perth and its transmitter is at Bickley. It broadcasts analog television on VHF 2 and digital television on VHF 12.

[edit] East Perth Studios

2005 saw ABCTV Perth move from the Adelaide Terrace studios to East Perth.
