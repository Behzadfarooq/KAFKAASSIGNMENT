[edit] What I like in music

I like the particular melancholy of Radiohead or Thom Yorke, its just slowly makes it way (like in Harrowdown Hill for instance)...I like brutal powerful music like Slayer...more thoughtful forms of metal, including progressive metal...I like the mellow pop of Belle & Sebastian...the hypthnotic feeling conveyed by GY!BE...

[edit] My favourite guitar solos...

Marty Friedman's solo in Megadeth's Tornado of Souls is probably my favourite...You have to let it flow through your body, for almost every note in this solo is a little piece of heaven...this is so wonderful when it so happens that I manage to enjoy every note of it...for me it just transcends metal, it is pure magnificent music...

[edit] My favourite drummers...

I really like Dave Lombardo's style of playing the drums, technical, fast, groovy and subtle at the same time. I particularly like his work on the song Seasons in the Abyss. I like Lars Ulrich too, his style is sometimes soberer but I like it. Fenriz is one the grooviest metal drummer for me on A Blaze in the Northern Sky and Goatlord. I like Jason Cooper too.
