"Abuse Me" was the second single off the band Silverchair's second album, Freak Show.

The song reached the number 4 in the United States major charts, beeing the second single by Silverchair to reached a Top 5 in that country. The first one was Frogstomp`s "Tomorrow" getting a #1.
