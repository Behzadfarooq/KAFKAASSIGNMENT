Abu Dhabi Mall is currently the largest mall in the Abu Dhabi Emirate, ahead of the recently renovated, Marina Mall. Abu Dhabi Mall comprises more than 200 stores, including a cineplex, supermarkets, a large hypermarket (Abu Dhabi Co-Operative Society) as well as a large food court. In addition to the foodcourt, the mall has 40 international food outlets and restaurants located throughout the multi-storeyed mall.

Abu Dhabi Mall, which officially opened 15 April 2001, covers a total area of 200,000 sq meters and is owned by Abu Dhabi Trade Center, (property of His Highness Sheikh Suroor Bin Mohammed Al Nahyan).

It houses more than 200 shops, comprising of children's, women's and men's fashion stores, make-up and perfume, music, soft-furnishings, household furniture, electrical goods, gifts, health-food, pharmacies, confectionary, dates, sportswear, books and stationary including the city's preferred grocer Abu Dhabi Co-op. We also boast the very best in dining with 40 international food outlets and restaurants plus an extensive food court. We have entertainment for the whole family with a Cineplex plus two action-packed kid's entertainment centers and visiting artists regularly entertaining the crowds. Our extensive underground parking facilities make Abu Dhabi Mall the perfect shopping destination.

Abu Dhabi Mall works very close to the local community considering its cultural sensitivity and community developments. The Mall is also considered as a perfect place for local and resident families in Abu Dhabi to meet & socialize aided by its friendly ambience and its high quality. At the Mall there are always plenty of activities, besides shopping, to keep the visitors of all ages entertained.

The Mall gives maximum attention, consideration, understanding and cooperation to the local community and look forward to a long lasting relationship. In fact, the Mall organizes special traditional events to coincide with the Holy month of Ramadan. In keeping with the international image of the city the Mall organizes a yearly Charity Fair, Abu Dhabi Mum's events, Art Exhibitions, Performing Artist's, Competitions for Children, Classic Car Show and the Bike Show. Abu Dhabi Mall wish to continue sharing the many opportunities of this unique development with our local community.
