[edit] Findings

I've identified two main types of vandalism from this IP:

  1. - Inserting random obsceneities into artices, usually to the tune of "This is a load of shit"
  2. - Adding sometimes subtle, but always annoying and innapropriate, changes to articles. These include adding random words, phrases, letters, spaces, line breaks, and confusing/ innapropriate subheadings, etc..., all clearly bad-faith edits

Perhaps an isolated incident, I discovered at least one occurrence of page blank vandalism, to Aztec Mythology

Amounts to repeated schoolboy vandalism, does not appear that blocks are having any affect and vandalism continues soon after blocks expire. This needs to be taken up with the school system
