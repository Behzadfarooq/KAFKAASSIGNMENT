Abu Dhabi Aviation is an airline based in Abu Dhabi, United Arab Emirates. It serves oil-fields and economic facilities in the United Arab Emirates and other Arab states. Its main base is Abu Dhabi International Airport.

[edit] Code Data

  • ICAO Code: AXU

[edit] History

The airline was established in 1975 and started operations in March 1976. It began with helicopter operations and added fixed-wing aircraft in 1991. A third-party maintenance facility was opened in 1994. It is owned by Abu Dhabi nationals (70%) and the Abu Dhabi government (30%).

[edit] Fleet

The Abu Dhabi Aviation fleet includes the following aircraft (at August 2006) [1] :

  • 2 Bombardier Dash 8 Q200
  • 2 De Havilland Canada DHC-6 Twin Otter Series 300
