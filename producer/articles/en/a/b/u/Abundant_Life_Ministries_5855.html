Abundant Life Ministries

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Abundant Life Music (ALM) is the record label of the Abundant Life Church in Bradford, England.

[edit] Worship leaders

The Abundant Life worship team is lead by Lara Martin and Mark Stevens, the latter originally from Hillsong Church in Sydney, Australia. Pop Idol runner-up Gareth Gates was also a worship leader on the first two albums.

[edit] Albums

Abundant Life have produced several live praise and worship albums as well a studio-recorded album and a youth EP RPM Live and their first complete album I Surrender All.

[edit] Live praise and worship

  • Higher (2000)
  • Divine Exchange (2002)
  • God Is Here (2003)
  • How Loved (2004)
  • Let the World See Jesus (2005)
  • Send Me to be released May [2007]
