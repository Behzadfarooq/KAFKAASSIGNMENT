[edit] Initial Report

The St Francis Xavier College shared IP address was blocked after persistant vandalism to Wikipedia's pages. This was discovered by one of its students (User:Beno1000) who is a Wikipedian when he attempted to edit an article using his account from a school/college workstation. He then checked the IP's talk page and discovered that it had been persistantly vandalising Wikipedia. He left a message on his talk page regarding the incidents, however could not contact any administrators who had been involved with the vandalism or leave a message on the IP's talk page since the IP address had been blocked. He then reported it to the school librarian and printed off the IP's talk page and several edit comparisons of articles which had been vandalised and was told that she would report it to the network administrator.

At home he left notes on the IP's talk page about the vandalism, and offered to liase between Wikipedia and the college, and to help with the investigation. He then got into contact with User:Petros471, and began to discuss how to deal with the problem.

Later, the contribution summary of the IP address was investigated and several names were found in the edits. These are listed below.
