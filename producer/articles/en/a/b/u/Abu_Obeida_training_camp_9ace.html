Abu Obeida training camp

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Abu Obeida training camp is a military training camp in Afghanistan, near Jalalabad, believed to be associated with al Qaeda.[1][2]

Saeed Ahmed Mohammed Abdullah Sarem Jarabh[1]
  • "The detainee attended training for approximately one week at the Abu Abaida training camp near Jalalabad Afghanistan."
Tariq Mahmoud Ahmed Al Sawah[2]
  • Al Sawah acknowledged providing training.
  • However, the allegations presented to his Tribunal said he had provided training at al Farouq training camp when he really provided that training at Abu Abaida.
