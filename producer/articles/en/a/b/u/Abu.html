Abu may refer to:

  • Abu (father) means "father of" in Arabic.
  • Abu (volcano), a volcano on the island of Honshu in Japan.
  • Mount Abu is the highest mountain in the Indian state of Rajasthan.
  • Abu, Yamaguchi is a town in Japan.
  • Abu is a minor god of vegetation in Sumerian mythology
  • Abu was a character in the animated film version of Aladdin.
  • Abu, another name for Elephantine island: abu (correctly '-b-w) is Ancient Egyptian for "elephant".
  • Abu is the fifth month of the Babylonian calendar
  • In Irish and Scottish Gaelic, "Abu!" is a war-cry. An Irish folk song bears the title "O'Donnell Abu!".
  • ABU, the Air Force's new Airman Battle Uniform.
  • Abu Simbel, an archaelogical site in southern Egypt
  • Abu-Ageila, a strategically important road junction and site of two Israeli-Egyptian battles in the north of the Sinai peninsula.
