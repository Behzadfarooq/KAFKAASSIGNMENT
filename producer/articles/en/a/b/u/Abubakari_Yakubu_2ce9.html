Abubakari Yakubu (born 13 December 1981 in Tema) is a Ghanaian football (soccer) midfielder. He currently plays for Vitesse Arnhem and Ghana national football team.

He also played for Ajax Amsterdam. He was loaned to Vitesse Arnhem in 2004-05 season and signed permanent deal at the end of season, for 4 years.
