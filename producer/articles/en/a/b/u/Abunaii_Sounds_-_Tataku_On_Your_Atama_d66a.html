Abunaii Sounds - Tataku On Your Atama (which roughly translates to English as Dangerous Sounds-Beating On Your Head) is a mix album by Slipknot's DJ, Sid Wilson. The CD includes 14 tracks by the N2O records label, and some of the sounds are taken from "Eyeless" and Slipknot's lives sets.
