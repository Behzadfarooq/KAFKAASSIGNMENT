[edit] Info

I'm the contactor working on Wikipedia:Abuse reports/70.49.111.121, the case to contact the ISP of the Alkivar phone number vandal. I've recieved a reply back from them, asking to see the logs that shows the abuse that connects the IP to the vandalism (this hadn't initially been provided, as most if not all of these edits have been deleted). I responded by undeleting many of the relevant edits and moving them to a new location (admins can find them by going here and looking around). I told them that, although there is only one edit made by the IP, that we're convinced that the other accounts are being run by the same IP, a fact that can be verified by CheckUser. Although they haven't yet replied, I think that they will probably want this data and so am asking for CheckUser verification so that I can move this report along as quickly as possible.

These edits are from May 4 - 8, and I'm unaware if that's within the possible time frame. As time may run out, haste on this case would be greatly appreciated.

BE AWARE! If you provide the CheckUser verification for this, it is possible that your help will be needed to advance the case, and you may need to contact the ISP yourself with the results. Please do not perform this CheckUser if you are not able to or do not wish to make contact with an ISP. (Hopefully, that won't be necessary, but I just don't know.)
