Aburadako (あぶらだこ, Aburadako?) is a Japanese Rock group. A notable oddity is that none of Aburadako's albums have titles and are only distinguished by their packages.

[edit] Discography

[edit] Singles

  • 'Aburadako / Yokujitsu' (9/8/2002) - This is a privately copied CD-R recording of their performance and called "CD-R"
  • 'Aburadako / Yokujitsu' (1/24/2004) - This is called "Kami-jake" because of its paper packaging.

[edit] Albums

  • Aburadako (8/?/1983) - The debut album of Aburadako. It is a 7 inch sono-sheet and is called "ADK Sono-sheet".
  • Aburadako (9/?/1984) - This is a 12 inch LP referred to as "ADK 12 inch"
  • Aburadako (8/?/1985) - The package is a photo of tree in the sunset, called "Ki-ban"
  • Aburadako (12/?/1986) - The package is colored navy blue, called "Ao-ban"
  • Aburadako (4/?/1989) - The package is a photo of a turtle, called "Kame-ban"
  • Aburadako (1/?/1996) - The package is a photo of a man holding a fish, called "Tsuri-ban"
  • Aburadako (12/?/1999) - The package is a drawing of a man, called "OK-ban"
  • Aburadako (10/?/2000) - The package is a photo of a full moon, called "Tsuki-ban"
