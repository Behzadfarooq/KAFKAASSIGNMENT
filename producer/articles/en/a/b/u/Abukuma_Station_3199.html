Abukuma Station

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Abukuma Station (あぶくま駅, Abukuma eki?) is an Abukuma Kyūkō Railway Company railway station located in Marumori, Miyagi Prefecture, Japan.

Station and the entrance to the Abukuma Line

The name of the station was taken from the Abukuma River. There are no villages or houses around the station. The station building sells tickets for the adjacent Abukuma Line's riverport, from where tourist ships depart between April and November.
