The Abukuma Express Line (阿武隈急行線, Abukuma Kyūkō sen?) is a railway line in Japan. It is owned and operated by the Abukuma Kyūkō Railway Company. The line transits from Fukushima Station in Fukushima Prefecture to Tsukinoki Station in Miyagi Prefecture. Both of these stations are also on the Tōhoku Main Line.
