[edit] Summary of Abuse

[edit] Initial Report

Has vandalized a range of articles, the most recent have been US history related. Here's the shortlist of his most recent handy work:

  • Battle of Gettysburg
  • AP US History Exam
  • John Winthrop
  • House of Burgesses
  • Slavery

et cetera...

Vandalism is of the immature schoolboy type, and usually consists of adding an innapropriate statement randomly in the article. For example, in one of his most recent edits, he added the phrase "This a bloody battle!!!!!!!" before a section heading in the Gettysburg article, not only for the sake of putting the phrase in, but also doing so in such a way to stop the Wiki-markup from functioning correctly (weather this is intentional or simply a side effect of the vandalism I'm not sure.)

[edit] Findings

Has been blocked eleven (11) times in the past three months, all for this immature type of vandalism. The vandal took a one month break, from mid june-july but now it appears that the vandal continues as soon as the block ends, and that he or she just isn't taking a hint from the blocks. Judging from the history of vandalism, even though the user took a break, I believe this needs to be taken up with the school.
