The Abraham Lincoln Strike Group or Carrier Strike Group 9 is the collection of United States Navy ships and aircraft built around the aircraft carrier Abraham Lincoln (CVN-72).

The battle group's homeport is in Everett, Washington.

Ships in the battle group as of the 2002-2003 deployment:

  • Abraham Lincoln (CVN-72)
  • Mobile Bay (CG-53)
  • Shiloh (CG-67)
  • Paul Hamilton (DDG-60)
  • Fletcher (DD-992)
  • Crommelin (FFG-37)
  • Reuben James (FFG-57)
  • Honolulu (SSN-718)
  • Camden (AOE-2)

Operations to date:

  • Operation Fiery Vigil
  • Operation Desert Storm
  • Operation Southern Watch
  • Operation Vigilant Sentinel
  • Operation Enduring Freedom
  • Operation Iraqi Freedom
