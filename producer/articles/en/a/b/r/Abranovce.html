Abranovce

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Location of Prešov District in the Prešov Region.

Abranovce is a village and municipality in Prešov District in the Prešov Region of eastern Slovakia.

[edit] History

In historical records the village was first mentioned in 1320.

[edit] Geography

The municipality lies at an altitude of 492 metres and covers an area of 9.581 km². It has a population of about 545 people.
