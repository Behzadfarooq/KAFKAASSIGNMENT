Milton Abramowitz and Irene A. Stegun, eds. (1965). Handbook of Mathematical Functions with Formulas, Graphs, and Mathematical Tables. New York: Dover. ISBN 0-486-61272-4.  (See chapter {{{1}}})

[edit] Usage

This template generates links to scanned pages of A&S.

[edit] Parameters

The first parameter should be a chapter number, and any additional comments, such as an equation number. The second parameter should be the page number. For example:

{{AS ref|3, eqn 3.6.26|16}}

will result in

Milton Abramowitz and Irene A. Stegun, eds. (1965). Handbook of Mathematical Functions with Formulas, Graphs, and Mathematical Tables. New York: Dover. ISBN 0-486-61272-4.  (See chapter 3, eqn 3.6.26)
