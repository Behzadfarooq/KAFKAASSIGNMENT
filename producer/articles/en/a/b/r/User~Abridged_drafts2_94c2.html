[edit] Title

WikiProject on Homeopathy

[edit] Scope

This WikiProject covers all articles about Homeopathy in Wikipedia.

[edit] Goals

  1. Improve existing articles on homeopathy in Wikipedia
  2. Widen existing scope of coverage by addition of articles on relevant topics in homeopathy to Wikipedia
  3. Assure that all homeopathy articles on Wikipedia conform to WP:ATT and WP:NPOV
  4. Maintain an atmosphere consistent with WP:NPA and WP:AGF on talk pages and edit summaries of homeopathy-related articles

This section is designed to allow you to let project members know about current developments that might be of interest.
