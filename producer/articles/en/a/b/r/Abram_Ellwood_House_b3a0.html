The Abram Ellwood House is a home in the DeKalb County, Illinois city of Sycamore. It is located in the 400 block of Somonauk Street and is listed as a contributing structure to the overall historical integrity of the Sycamore Historic District. The historic district joined the National Register of Historic Places in May 1978. It is across the street from the David Syme House.
