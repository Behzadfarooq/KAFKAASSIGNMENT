Abram Moiseyevich Deborin (Joffe) (Russian: Абрам Моисеевич Деборин (Иоффе) (June 16 (O.S. June 4), 1881 - March 8, 1963) was a Soviet Marxist philosopher and academician of the Soviet Academy of Sciences (1929).
