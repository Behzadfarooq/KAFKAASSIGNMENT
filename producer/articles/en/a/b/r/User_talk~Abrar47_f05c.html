[edit] big unexplained edit

Since you are inexperienced I am keeping an eye on your contributions.

This is a big unexplained edit. You expressed some cooperative views a month or so ago. If you want to cooperate with other contributors you should really go to the associated talk page when you make a big edit like this, and explain why you made the edit. Maybe you will convince other editors. And, if you don't, at least they will know why you made the edit. This gives them an opportunity to do some homework and present you with specific reasons why they disagree with you. Who knows, if you explain your reasoning, and they do the right homework before they reply to you, maybe they will convince you.

The other contributor who left you a note mentioned "edit wars". That is where two or more contributors keep making deletions of one anothers contributions -- without explaining their reasoning. It is ugly, and a waste of time.

Please think about how you can explain your reasoning more fully in future.

Cheers! -- Geo Swan 18:51, 5 January 2007 (UTC)
