[edit] Biography

Abraham Soltero III (born December 26, 1981, in Boulder, Colorado), better known by his Radio Name Slim Douglas (or earlier in his career as Kegel), He currently resides in Westminster, Colorado. He works for Frontier Airlines as a baggage movement specialist in which he moves bags from one aircraft to another. He has also been known to work as a baggage loading specialist in which he loads an aircraft with baggage which passengers check in. Most recently he has also been convinced to work as a radio personality with a co-worker known as Nicky Park who founded the website known as Soupjar. Earlier in his hitory Abraham went to school at Iver C. Ranum High School. It was there where he began to enjoy art. He created a few painting but nothing special. From there he moved to Medina, Ohio where he attended school at Cuyahoga Community College. It was here where Abraham took a class in graphic design and drafting and decided on a career in computer graphics. A year later Abraham moved back to Colorado to attend [[The Art Institute of Colorado]] where he recieved a bachelors degree in Media Arts and Animation. Upon graduating from The Art Institute Abraham, looked for work in almost every corner of the industry and was unable to find work. It was then when he decided to begin work for Frontier Airlines, where he decided he would devote his skills. Now working as a [[Ramp Agent]] he is waiting for the opening to move into the Branding department, but while waiting for this opening he met a friend by the name of [[Nicky Park]] who proposed an internet radio show, [[Soup Jar]]. Along with Nicky Park they hope to make a great show.
