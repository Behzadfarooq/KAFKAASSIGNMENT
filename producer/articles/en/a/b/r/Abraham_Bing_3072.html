Rabbi Abraham Bing was born in Frankfurt in 1752. From 1769 to 1778, he served as "Klaus" Rabbi of Offenbach. His other positions were:

  • Dayan of Frankfurt, 1778-1796
  • Rabbi of Heidingsfeld, 1796-1814
  • Rabbi of W端rzburg, 1814-1840

In 1813, Bing was instrumental in re-settling Jews in W端rzburg, where they had been banned for 250 years.

Bing's students included Jacob Ettlinger, Nathan Marcus Adler, Isaac Bernays, and Sligmann Bar Bamberger.

Rabbi Abraham Bing died in W端rzberg in 1841.
