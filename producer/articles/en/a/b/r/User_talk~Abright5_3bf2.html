[edit] WikiProject Narnia

Hi, Abright5, and thanks for your contributions to the article The Chronicles of Narnia: Prince Caspian! I thought you may be care to know that there is a WikiProject working to improve articles about Narnia, and your help would be greatly appreciated. Please consider joining the WikiProject Narnia. Thank you! Fbv65edel / ☑t / ☛c || 15:33, 6 November 2006 (UTC)

Hi, Abright. Good to have you with us! Typically a user box, like {{User Narnia-WP}}, goes on your user page, which in this case is User:Abright5. You haven't made your user page yet, where you can tell a little about yourself, or list a few projects you're involved with, or add more userboxes. See also WP:UP for Wikipedia's guideline on user pages. Click on the red link above to create the page, if you'd like. It's not mandatory to use the userbox, either.
We have yet to start a really big project on the WP Narnia, but our best program right now is to "adopt an article," which is taking an article up for adoption from the list (or finding one on your own, that's fine too), particularly on a topic that you like and are experienced in, and to try to bring it up to good article status, and eventually featured article status. This means articles need to be referenced; in our case this will probably be with Lewis' text alone, but if you want to reference something like a companion novel that's a reliable source, such as Paul Ford's Companion to Narnia, that's fine too. Or, if you don't want to adopt an article, just having your name on the list puts you on call for us if and when we want to send out a newsletter informing members of our next steps. If you have any more questions, feel free to talk to me on my talk page again or on Wikipedia talk:WikiProject Narnia. See you around! --Fbv65edel / ☑t / ☛c || 13:18, 12 January 2007 (UTC)

Article of the Month: Edmund Pevensie
  • Edmund Pevensie is a major character in The Chronicles of Narnia. He appears as a principal character in three of the seven books. Edmund was the second to enter Narnia, although he tried to betray his siblings to the White Witch. However, Edmund managed to help save Narnia by neutralizing the White Witch's most powerful weapon, her wand.

[edit] Nim’s Island

A tag has been placed on Nim’s Island, requesting that it be speedily deleted from Wikipedia. This has been done under the criteria for speedy deletion, because it is a very short article providing little or no context to the reader. Please see Wikipedia:Stub for our minimum information standards for short articles. Also please note that articles must be on notable subjects and should provide references to reliable sources that verify their content.

Please do not remove the speedy deletion tag yourself. If you plan to expand the article, you can request that administrators wait a while for you to add contextual material. To do this, affix the template {{hangon}} to the page and state your intention on the article's talk page. Feel free to leave a note on my talk page if you have any questions about this.
