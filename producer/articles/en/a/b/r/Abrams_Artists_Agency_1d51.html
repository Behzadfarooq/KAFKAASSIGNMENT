Abrams Artists Agency

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

You have new messages (last change).

Abrams Artists Agency (founded in 1977) is a bi-coastal talent and literary agency that represents writers and actors for film, television, commercials, theatre, print and voice-over. They represent over 700 clients and staff around 80 agents, making them one of the largest talent agencies in the country. Technically, they are considered an upper mid-level agency because they do not "package" movies the way the Big five talent agencies do. However, they are still a very prestigious agency and they represent many well-known actors. They have very strong voice-over and commercial departments, and is well known for handle young talent.
