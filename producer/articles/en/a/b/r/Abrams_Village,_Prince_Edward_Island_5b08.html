Abrams Village, Prince Edward Island

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Abrams-Village is a small Acadian French community in Prince County, Prince Edward Island. It is part of the Evangeline Region. As of 2006, their population was 266, which is a 22% decrease from 2001. The village is famous for its Acadian Festival during late August or early September. Daniel Lubbersen grew up here. So did Denise Lubbersen.
