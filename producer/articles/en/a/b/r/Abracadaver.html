Abracadaver is a record label imprint formed by the metal band Cradle of Filth in 2001.

[edit] Release History

  • Bitter Suites to Succubi - Cradle of Filth (2001)
  • Live Bait for the Dead - Cradle of Filth (2002)
  • Damnation and a Day - Cradle of Filth (2003, via Sony)
  • Nymphetamine - Cradle of Filth (2004, via Roadrunner)
  • Thornography - Cradle of Filth (2006, via Roadrunner)
