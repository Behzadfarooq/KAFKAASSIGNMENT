Abrasion may mean:

  • Abrasion (medical), superficial damage to the skin, generally not deeper than the epidermis
  • Abrasion (dental), the loss of tooth structure by mechanical forces from a foreign element
  • Abrasion (geology) (corrasion), mechanical scraping of a rock surface by friction between rocks and moving particles during their transport in wind, glacier, waves, gravity or running water.
  • Abrasion (materials science), abrasive wear is the erosion of material from a solid surface by the action of another solid.

Abrasion may also refer to:

  • Abrasion (BDSM), a human sexual practice involving sexual arousal from contact with abrasive substances
