[edit] Last Robin Murphy before deletion

Dr. Robin Murphy, ND was born August 15, 1950 in Grand Rapids, Michigan. He attended the University of Michigan at Ann Arbor from 1972-1976. In 1976, he entered the National College of Naturopathic Medicine (NCNM) where he was awarded the Hahnemann Scholarship for his thesis Homeopathy and Cancer. During his time at NCNM he studied with Dr. Ravi Sahni and Dr. John Bastyr. From 1980-1984, Dr. Murphy was the director of the homeopathy program at NCNM. He taught at Bastyr University and was chairman of the Homeopathic Department at the National College of Naturopathic Medicine in Portland, OR. He is now director of the Lotus Health Institute.


Dr. Murphy is an honorary patron for the Abha Light Foundation's Abha Light College of Natural Medicine in Kenya.
