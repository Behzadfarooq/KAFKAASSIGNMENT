Abraham González International Airport (IATA: CJS, ICAO: MMCS) is an international airport located in Ciudad Juárez, Chihuahua, Mexico, near the U.S.-Mexico border opposite with El Paso, Texas. It handles national and international air traffic of the city of Ciudad Juárez.

[edit] Airlines

  • Aero California (Mexico City, Torreón)
  • Aeroméxico (Mexico City)
    • Aerolitoral (Chihuahua, Ciudad Obregón, Guadalajara, Hermosillo, La Paz, León, Mazatlán, Monterrey, Tijuana, Torreón)
  • ALMA de Mexico (Chihuahua, Guadalajara, Tijuana, Torreón)
  • Aviacsa (Mexico City, Monterrey)
  • Interjet (Toluca)
  • Líneas Aéreas Azteca (Mexico City, Tijuana, Zacatecas)
  • Mexicana (Mexico City) [starts May 1, 2007]
  • Viva Aerobus (Monterrey)
