Abrams Municipal Airport (FAA LID: 4D0) is a general aviation airport located two miles (3 km) north of the central business district (CBD) of Grand Ledge, a city in Clinton County, Michigan, USA.

[edit] Facilities

Abrams Municipal Airport covers 160 acres and has two runways. Runway 18/36 is closed to fixed-wing aircraft from November through April. The airport is staffed daily from 8AM until dusk.

[edit] Runways

  • Runway 9/27: 3,200 x 75 ft. (976 x 23 m), Surface: Asphalt
  • Runway 18/36: 2,580 x 120 ft. (787 x 37 m), Surface: Turf

[edit] Transit

  • The airport is accessible by road from Eaton Hwy, and is located 2.1 miles south of I-96, just east of M-100.
