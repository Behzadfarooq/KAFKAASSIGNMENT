Abrakebabra

Abrakebabra is a chain of Irish fast food restaurants that stays open late to capitalise on the post-pub crowd. In 2002, they complete revised their menu with the addition of the "Taco Fries", a mainstay on their menu. This helped to turn around the dwindling number of customers at the time and make them one of Ireland's better known domestic restaurant chains.

The restaurant in Waterford was also featured on an RTÉ show by comedian Des Bishop, who still makes frequent references to this trip in his stand-up routine[1].

Since the brand was founded, it has always been, and continues to be to this day, advertised as "your international fast food restaurant". This was parodied in a television commercial between 2002 and 2003 which featured several impersonators of Britney Spears, Ali G, Anthony Hopkins, and others buying food in the restaurant.

The menu consists mainly of kebabs, baguette dishes and fried chicken has recently been added to lend an air of diversity.
