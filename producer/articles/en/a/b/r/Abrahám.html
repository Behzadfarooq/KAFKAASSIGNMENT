Abrahám

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Location of Dunajská Streda District in the Trnava Region

Abrahám is a village and municipality in Galanta District of the Trnava Region of south-west Slovakia.

[edit] History

In historical records the village was first mentioned in 1266.

[edit] Geography

The municipality lies at an elevation of 125 metres and covers an area of 15.78 km². It has a population of about 1075 people.
