Abra-Cadaver is the fifth episode in the third season of the popular American crime drama CSI: Crime Scene Investigation, which is set in Las Vegas, Nevada.

[edit] Plot Summary

Grissom, Warrick and Sara investigate the case of a missing person who turned up in a magicians act only to disappear again. Things are complicated when that same magician burns to death on stage. Catherine and Nick investigate the overdose of a rock-star in his trailer.
