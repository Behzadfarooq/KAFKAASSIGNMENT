Hi! I'm Abhorsen327, known in the real world as Ana Best, and in most other communities as, simply, Abhorsen. My pseudonym is derived from the series of Old Kingdom books by Garth Nix, and the articles in that category are articles which I am planning on editing in the near future, depending on how much work I have to do outside of Wikipedia. Other favorite articles include those on books by Neil Gaiman, such as the Neverwhere article, and articles relating to Greek mythology. Hopefully there will be more soon to come in this space, as I mentally assemble information about myself and increase my contributions to Wikipedia. Feel free to make (friendly and accurate) edits to this page, and leave messages, comments, and suggestions on my talk page.
