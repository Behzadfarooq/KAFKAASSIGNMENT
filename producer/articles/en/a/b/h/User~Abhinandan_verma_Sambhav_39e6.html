Sambhav is a non-profit organization founded by the students of DA-IICT, Gandhinagar, in 2006. Sambhav is actively involved with the denotified tribes of Maninagar. Sambhav identifies itself with the vision "a world where people can live better and happier lives in an equitable, democratic and environmentally sustainable society".[1]
