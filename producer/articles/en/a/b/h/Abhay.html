Abhay is a Sanskrit word meaning "fearless", and a common Indian male given name. It may refer to:

  • Abhayanand, an Indian police officer
  • Abhay Ashtekar, a professor of Physics at Pennsylvania State University
  • Abhay Deol, an Indian actor
  • Abhay Rustum Sopori, an Indian santoor player
  • Hector Abhayavardhana, a Sri Lankan politician

Abhay may also refer to

  • Abhayagiri Buddhist Monastery
  • Abhayagiri Dagaba, an extensive ruins in Anuradhapura, Sri Lanka.
  • Abhayamudra, a pose that Hindu gods are often depicted in
  • Abhayapuri, a town in Assam, India
