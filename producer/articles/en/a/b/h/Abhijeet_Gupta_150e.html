Abhijeet Gupta

Abhijeet Gupta (born 1989) at age of 15 is India's youngest International Master after FIDE, the World Chess Federation, officially awarded him the title in August 2005. He hails from Bhilwara in Rajasthan. In 2002, Abhijeet entered the Limca Book of Records on becoming the youngest National junior (under-19) champion at the age of 13 years and 10 days. Abhijeet has won 14 international medals, including six gold medals from the Asian, Commonwealth and British age-group categories.
