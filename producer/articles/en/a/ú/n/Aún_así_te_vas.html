Aún Asi Te Vas (Spanish: "Even Then You Leave") is the third single released by the Mexican electronic-pop music band Belanova, from their album Cocktail.

The song was released to radio stations in June 2004, during a period of high sales for the album thanks to the popularity of the second single, Suele Pasar. Its popularity with Mexican radio and the August, 2004 release of the music video (directed by Studio Monitor) helped Aún Así Te Vas reach #12 on the Mexican Top 100.

Aún Así Te Vas was picked as the official third single from the album Cocktail. However, Universal Music Mexico decided to promote only two singles simultaneously and released Y... in July, 2004 as a radio single only.

Aún Así Te Vas entered the Mexican Digital Sales Chart in 2006 at #75 and peaked at #68, spending two weeks in the Top 100.

Scene of the music video "Y Aún Así Te Vas" (2004)

[edit] Music Video

Directed by Studio Monitor, Y Aún Así Te Vas was filmed in Mexico City and used special effects and graphic design. The video premiered in August, 2004 on the Mexican channel Telehit. The video reached #8 on MTV Mexico's "Los 10 + Pedidos", #15 on the Top 20, #3 on Telehit's Top Ten, and #5 on VH1's Top 20.
