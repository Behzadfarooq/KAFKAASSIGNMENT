Acklam Grange Secondary School is a specialist college of mathematics and computing. It is located at the end of Lodore Grove in Acklam, Middlesbrough.

[edit] History

The School has gone through many changes, first being Hue Bell School, then Stainsby School and currently Acklam Grange Secondary School.

[edit] Facilities

On the site of the School is the Middlesbrough City Learning Centre, providing specialist ICT facilities for the school and others in the area. The centre is located on the Lodore side of school.

At the Heythrop side of school you can find the Acorn Centre, a sports and inclusion centre.
