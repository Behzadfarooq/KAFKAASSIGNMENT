Acrophobe is the first EP by Bad Astronaut, released in 2001 on the Honest Don's independent record label.

[edit] Current lineup

  • Derrick Plourde: Drums and Incarceration
  • Marko 72: Bass and Lemonade
  • Joey Cape: Vocals, Guitars and Nervous Breakdowns
  • Todd Chapps: Keyboards and Coffee
  • Angus Cooke: Cello, Vocals and Percussion
