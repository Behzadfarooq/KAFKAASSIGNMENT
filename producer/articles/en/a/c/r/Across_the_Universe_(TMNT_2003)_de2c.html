"Across the Universe" is the seventy-second episode of the animated series Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles (2003), which originally aired on March 12, 2005.

[edit] Trivia

  • At the beginning of this episode, we see through Raphael's point of view seconds before the events of Reality Check when Ultimate Drako appears and separates the Turtles and Splinter across time and space.
  • Rapahel comes face to face with the world and characters of Peter Laird and Jim Lawson's highly regarded "Planet Racers" series of graphic novels.
