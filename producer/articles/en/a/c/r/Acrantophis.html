Acrantophis is a genus of terrestrial boas endemic to the island of Madagascar. It is often synonymized with the genus Boa.

[edit] Species

  • Madagascar Ground Boa, Acrantophis madagascariensis
  • Dumeril's Boa, Acrantophis dumerili
  • Madagascar Tree Boa, Sanzinia madagascariensis
