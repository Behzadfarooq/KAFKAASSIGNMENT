Across the Tracks

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Across the Tracks is a 1991 film directed and written by Sandy Tung and stars a young Brad Pitt.

[edit] Selected cast

  • Rick Schroder – Billy Maloney
  • Brad Pitt – Joe Maloney
  • Carrie Snodgress – Rosemary Maloney
  • David Anthony Marshall - Louie
  • Thomas Mikal Ford – Coach Walsh
  • Annie Dylan - Linda
  • Jack McGee - Frank
