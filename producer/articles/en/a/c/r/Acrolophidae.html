Acrolophidae (Burrowing webworm moths) is a family of moths in the Lepidoptera order. In addition, there are about 300 species, which live in the wild ony in the New World.

[edit] Genera

  • Acrolophus
  • Amydria
  • Drastea
  • Exoncotis
  • Ptilopsaltis
