Across Indiana is a weekly 30 minute long documentary-style television program which covers places, people, history and culture across Indiana.

Hosted by Michael Atwood, Across Indiana is a regional Emmy winning program originating on WFYI TV 20 in Indianapolis. The producer is Jim Simmons. The executive producer is Clayton Taylor. It was originally produced and directed by Dave Stoelk, who is no longer with the program. It premiered in 1989.

The theme music was written and recorded by Emmy-winning artist Tim Brickley.

In 1998 a booking of recipes from the Hoosier state was released called Recipes From Across Indiana: The Best of Heartland Cooking edited by Sheila Sampson.
