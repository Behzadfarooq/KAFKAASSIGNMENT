Jan Ryan Cantin was born in the year 1987 on the day October 15th. Jackie Cantin gave birth in their small backyard, before an assembly of woodland creatures. Jan took 7 steps, chewed off his own umbilical cord, and passed out. Robert Cantin was worried about their small, fragile son, but his worries were pacified as the young Jan stirred, and passed gas.

Some scholars believe that Cantin as a youngster, was directly or indirectly responsible for a number of incidents, resulting in at least 4 deaths. These were mentioned in the press as the "magic marker deaths", alluding to the messages written in magic marker on the corpses. Jackie and Robert Cantin feverishly deny that their son could have comitted any of the atrocities, as he was locked in the closet at the time. One of the messages recorded was "I am Jan Cantin and I killed this person, and then wrote this message on them in magic marker so you would know that it's me."

Hiding behind the Young Offenders Act of Canada, a 7 year old Jan was exhiled to Germany. There his reading tended towards Military and Art History, and he briefly visited Vienna and cited this as a shaping experience. He learned to play the violin. His parents visas expired, and so they returned to Canada, hoping it had all blown over.

At age 10, Jan decided his artistic and literary pursuits were a waste of time. Deciding that art and literature are tools of the bourgeoisie, he sought to forget them. Sitting down resolutely, he vowed he would not rise until he had forgotten everything he knew. He promptly forgot it all, including how to speak English, and how to defecate. His rehabilitation moved quickly, however, taking only 3 months.

The next 6 months were spent writing a novel he called “Life of Pi”. Under the pen name Yann Martel.

Jan was involved in a tragic 6 car pileup. 4 of the cars exploded violently. The entire mess of blood, burning metal, and glass, fell 100 feet into a river in the middle of January. Jan rescued his parents and swam to shore. He uttered his first obscenity. He was 11 years old.

When asked about his scarring encounter with Big foot at age 12, Jan comments “Holy Shit his feet are huge!” Thought the details surrounding the encounter are still foggy, some Janaeic Scholars speculate he wrestled the beast to the ground, and in a drunken stupor peed on its face. This may or may not be responsible for the beast’s subsequent fear of mankind. Jan believes “He had it coming.”

Jan has been spotted in 124 countries worldwide, including one session of the United Nations, where he alleged that Norway was crowding him, and called Zimbabwe “a dick.” He has been banned from an overwhelming number of these countries. He spent 2 nights in an Iranian prison, before being politely asked to leave. Jan has, to some extent, damaged relations with the United States of America.
