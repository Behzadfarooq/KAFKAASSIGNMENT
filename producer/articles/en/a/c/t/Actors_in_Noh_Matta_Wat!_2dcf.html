[edit] Kent Clare

Role: Clifford Rowland, government minister and Randy Diego's father. Born: Belize City, September 20, 1961 Previous Dramatic Experience: Writing and performing with former drama group CIA Current Job: Tax Audit Supervisor for the Government of Belize Quote: "If you can't dazzle them with brilliance, baffle them with bullshit."

[edit] Sherlette Lopez

Role: Margaret Diego, mother of the kids. Born: Camalote Village, Cayo District, July 20, 1972 On the show: Sees her character as a rolemodel for single mothers.

[edit] Shackera Samuels

Role: Lisani Diego, Randy's half-sister and Margaret's daughter. Born: Belize City, June 20, 1987 Previous Dramatic Experience: Starring in two local made for TV movies Education: Graduate of SJC Junior College, Business Administration

[edit] Kerret Paulino

Role: Randy Diego, half-brother of Lisani and son of Margaret. Born: Belize City, September 7, 1987 Education: Wesley Junior College Quote: "Never let your problems get you down, always stay focused and hold your ground."

[edit] Carrie Fairweather

Role: Tomasa Diego, the kids' grandmother. Born: Dangriga, March 28, 1933 Previous Dramatic Experience: Wrote a play "The Podiatrist" (performed in Brooklyn, New York). Also sings and writes poetry.

[edit] Hershabeth Ramclam

Role: Rosanna Ramirez, student and Lisani's best friend. Born: Belize City, 1988 Student; and according to her, "a fun and outgoing individual."

[edit] Allison Hemsley

Role: Dan Man, Randy's mentor Born: Belize City, 1973. Previous Dramatic Experience: Singer and Songwriter, some primary school drama Current Job: Musician
