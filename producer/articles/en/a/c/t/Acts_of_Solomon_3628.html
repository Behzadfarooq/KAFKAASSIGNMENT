Acts of Solomon

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The similarly named Biblical book is located at Song of Solomon.

The Acts of Solomon is a lost text that may have been written by the Biblical prophet Iddo, who was the author of other lost texts. The book is described in 1Kings 11:41. The passage reads: "And the rest of the acts of Solomon, and all that he did, and his wisdom, are they not written in the book of the acts of Solomon?"

This text is sometimes called The Book of the Acts of Solomon.[1], [2]
