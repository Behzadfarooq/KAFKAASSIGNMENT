Universal TV's Action Pack is from MCA TV that aired a 2 hour movie each week in 1994. Starting in 1995, the action pack included two hours of various television series distributed by MCA and Universal. From January-June 1995, it included the Hercules: The Legendary Journeys and Vanishing Son series. From September 1995 through January 2000, it included the Hercules: The Legendary Journeys and Xena: Warrior Princess. After Hercules ended its run in January 2000, the Universal Action Pack picked up two thirty minute series: Jack of All Trades and Cleopatra 2525 to air alongside Xena. This lasted until January 2001. For the final six months of the package, Jack of All Trades was dropped and Cleopatra 2525 was increased to an hour long show. After the summer of 2001, when Xena was done airing it's final season's reruns, Cleopatra 2525 also stopped production and the Action Pack was discontinued.

Some of the movies from the 1994 run included:

The Hercules Series:

  • Hercules and the Amazon Women
  • Hercules and the Lost Kingdom
  • Hercules and the Circle of Fire
  • Hercules in the Underworld
  • Hercules in the Maze of the Minotaur

The TekWar movies (based on the books by William Shatner:

  • Tekwar
  • Teklords
  • Teklab
  • TekJustice

The Midnight Run films, which were sequels to the 1988 Robert De Niro film:

  • Another Midnight Run
  • Midnight Runaround
  • Midnight Run for Your Life

The Bandit films

  • Bandit: Bandit's Silver Angel
  • Bandit Goes Country
  • Bandit Bandit
  • Beauty and the Bandit

The Vanishing Son tv movies, which eventually lead to a short-lived television series:

  • Vanishing Son
  • Vanishing Son II
  • Vanishing Son III
  • Vanishing Son IV

And a single movie based off Knight Rider:

  • Knight Rider 2010
