Actionslacks are an American indie rock band based primarily in the San Francisco Bay Area.

[edit] Members

Tim Scanlin - singer-guitarist
Chuck Lindo - lead guitarist
Ross Murray - bassist
Marty Kelly - drummer

[edit] Discography

  • Too Bright, Just Right, Good Night (1996), Skene
  • One Word (1998), Arena Rock Recording Co.
  • The Scene's Out of Sight (2001), Self-Starter Foundation
  • Never Never Shake, Baby (2002), Post-Parlo
  • Full Upright Position (2003), Self Starter
