"Act a Fool" is the official second single from Lil Jon's upcoming album Crunk Rock. Three 6 Mafia are featured on the song, and its production was done by Lil Jon and the punk rock group, Whole Wheat Bread. It was released on Lil Jon's MySpace on Halloween, immediately attracting listeners with approximately 580,000 plays in the first day.

The song was also released in iTunes, just a few days later after its premiere on MySpace.
