This category is for stub articles relating to actors. You can help Wikipedia by expanding them.

To add an article to this category, use {{actor-stub}} instead of {{stub}}.

Please use a more specific stub template where possible:

American actors or actresses, use {{US-actor-stub}} or one of its child stub types:
{{US-film-actor-stub}}, {{US-theat-actor-stub}}, {{US-tv-actor-stub}}, {{US-voice-actor-stub}}
Asian actors or actresses, use {{{Asia-actor-stub}} or one of its child stub types:
{{{HongKong-actor-stub}}, {{India-actor-stub}}, {{Japan-actor-stub}}, {{Korea-actor-stub}}, {{Philippines-actor-stub}}
Australian actors or actresses, use {{Australia-actor-stub}}
Canadian actors or actresses, use {{Canada-actor-stub}}
European actors or actresses, use {{Euro-actor-stub}} or one of its sub-types:

{{UK-actor-stub}}, {{Finland-actor-stub}}, {{France-actor-stub}}, {{Germany-actor-stub}}, {{Ireland-actor-stub}}, {{Italy-actor-stub}}, {{Poland-actor-stub}}, {{Sweden-actor-stub}}

Mexican actors or actresses, use {{Mexico-actor-stub}}
Middle Eastern actors or actresses, use {{MEast-actor-stub}}
New Zealand actors or actresses, use {{NZ-actor-stub}}
South American actors or actresses, use {{SouthAm-actor-stub}}


Actors or actresses with only film credits, use {{film-bio-stub}}
Actors or actresses with only television credits, use {{tv-bio-stub}}
Actors or actresses with only theatre credits, use {{theat-stub}}
Actors or actresses with only voice acting credits, use {{voice-actor-stub}}

The perfect article about an actor gives both a (possibly short) biography and a list of performances. Also, appropriate categories should be added, especially those listed in Category:Actors by medium and Category:Actors by nationality.
