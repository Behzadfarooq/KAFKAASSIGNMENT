Alright - Actin is not a single protein but a whole protein family as so far 6 different actin genes - [ACTA1], [ACTA2], [ACTB], [ACTC], [ACTG1], [ACTG2] - that are active, plus several pseudogenes, have been discovered in humans only (i bet there are many more in the rest of the live world).
