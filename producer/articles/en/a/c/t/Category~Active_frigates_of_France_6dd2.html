Active frigates of France include frigates designed, built, or operated since the end of the Cold War (approximately since 1990).


Since French military parlance does not distinguish between frigates and destroyers, Category:Active destroyers of France redirects here. Ships whose pennant number sport the letter "D" are classified as destroyers by NATO standards ("first rank frigate" in French parlance).
