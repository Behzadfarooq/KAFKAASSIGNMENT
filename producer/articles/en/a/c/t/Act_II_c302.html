Act II is a brand of popcorn in North America that is based on the look and taste of movie theater popcorn. It is made and distributed by ConAgra Foods.

Act II history begins with its predecessor-ACT I, an early microwave popcorn that had to be stored in the refrigerator. In 1984, ACT II, a shelf-stable microwave popcorn, hit the stage. It was the first mass-marketed microwave popcorn.[citation needed]
