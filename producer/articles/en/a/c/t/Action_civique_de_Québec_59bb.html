Action civique de Québec

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Action civique de Québec is a political party in Quebec City, Quebec, Canada that contests municipal elections. It was created in 2001 after the amalgamation of Quebec City and surrounding suburban municipalities.[1] The party currently holds six of the 37 seats on the City Council.[2]
