[edit] Summary

The world as divided by the most active regional blocs. Some colors are used multiple times for different blocs. Dark color means full member; light color means associate member, observer, prospective member, candidate, and so on. Initially created by Alinor. Modified by Nightstallion: added Timor-Leste as prospective member of ASEAN, removed STaP from CEMAC (no official affiliation), added CACM, changed legend to be next to the bloc it refers to, changed CEMAC to UDEAC and EURASEC to EurAsEC, added IGAD, changed Palestine to grey for now, changed Afghanistan to full member, changed ECOWAS' colour to distinguish it from UDEAC.

Note: The Andean Community and Mercosur plan to unite into one trade block, CSN.
