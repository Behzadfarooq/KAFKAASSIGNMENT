Acton Vale (2006 Population 7,797) is a town in south-central Quebec, Canada. It is the seat of the Regional County Municipality of Acton (MRC d'Acton). The town covers an area of 90.96 km². The town grew 6.4% from 2001 to 2006. It was founded in 1862 and is in the Montérégie administrative region.

By road, Acton Vale is 100 km from the province's largest city Montreal, 190 km from the provincial capital Quebec City, and 100 km from the United States border[1].
