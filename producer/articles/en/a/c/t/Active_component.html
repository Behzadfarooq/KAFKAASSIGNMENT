Active component

You have new messages (last change).

In electronics, an active component is one that can be used to provide gain in an electronic circuit.

[edit] Examples

  • transistors
    • Bipolar junction transistors
    • Field effect transistors
      • MOSFETs
      • JFETs
  • operational amplifiers
  • vacuum tubes (also known as thermionic valves)
    • triodes
    • tetrodes
    • pentodes
    • heptodes

[edit] Nonlinear passive components

The following devices do not fit the definition generally ascribed to active components, but because of their nonlinear responses, designs, histories, and uses in circuits, are sometimes presented alongside active components in textbooks and educational materials.

  • junction diodes
  • step recovery diodes
  • thermionic diodes
  • thyristors
  • tunnel diodes
  • varactor diodes
