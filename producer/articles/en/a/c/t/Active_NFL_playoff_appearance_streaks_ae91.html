The following is a list of National Football League teams with the most consecutive (active) seasons appearing in the playoffs. This refers to teams which have qualified and made the playoffs at the conclusion of the 16 game regular season either by winning one of the eight divisions or by winning a wildcard spot (entering 2007 regular season).

[edit] Other streaks of accomplishment

Entering 2007 NFL regular season:

[edit] Most consecutive 7+ win seasons

  • 7 seasons: Denver Broncos
  • 6 seasons: New England Patriots and Seattle Seahawks [1]
  • 5 seasons: Carolina Panthers, Indianapolis Colts, and Kansas City Chiefs
  • 4 seasons: Cincinnati Bengals,
  • 3 seasons: Atlanta Falcons, Jacksonville Jaguars, Pittsburgh Steelers, and San Diego Chargers
  • 2 seasons: Chicago Bears, Dallas Cowboys, and New York Giants
  • 1   season: Baltimore Ravens, Buffalo Bills, Green Bay Packers, New Orleans Saints, New York Jets, Philadelphia Eagles, Saint Louis Rams, and Tennessee Titans

[edit] Most consecutive 8+ win seasons

  • 7 seasons: Denver Broncos
  • 6 seasons: New England Patriots
  • 5 seasons: Indianapolis Colts
  • 4 seasons: Cincinnati Bengals, and Seattle Seahawks
  • 3 seasons: Jacksonville Jaguars, Pittsburgh Steelers, and San Diego Chargers
  • 2 seasons: Carolina Panthers, Chicago Bears, Dallas Cowboys, Kansas City Chiefs, and New York Giants
  • 1   season: Baltimore Ravens, Green Bay Packers, New Orleans Saints, New York Jets, Philadelphia Eagles, Saint Louis Rams, and Tennessee Titans

[edit] Most consecutive 9+ win seasons

  • 6 seasons: New England Patriots
  • 5 seasons: Denver Broncos and Indianapolis Colts
  • 4 seasons: Seattle Seahawks
  • 3 seasons: San Diego Chargers
  • 2 seasons: Chicago Bears, Dallas Cowboys, and Kansas City Chiefs
  • 1   season: Baltimore Ravens, New Orleans Saints, New York Jets, and Philadelphia Eagles

[edit] Most consecutive 10+ win seasons

  • 5 seasons: Indianapolis Colts
  • 4 seasons: New England Patriots
  • 2 seasons: Chicago Bears
  • 1   season: Baltimore Ravens, New Orleans Saints, New York Jets, Philadelphia Eagles and San Diego Chargers

  1. ^ Team was in AFC West in 2001 and moved to NFC West in 2002 due to division realignment
