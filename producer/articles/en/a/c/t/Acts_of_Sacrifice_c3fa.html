"Acts of Sacrifice" is an episode from the second season of the science-fiction television series Babylon 5.

[edit] Plot synopsis

Spoiler warning: Plot and/or ending details follow.

Commander Ivanova attempts to negotiate a diplomatic relationship with the Lumati.

[edit] Arc significance

  • Sheridan and Delenn have begun covertly aiding the Narn refugees.

[edit] Trivia

  • Delenn tells G'Kar that she "was there when our war against Earth began, when our ship encountered an Earth vessel for the first time", and that she "heard the cries for revenge" after the Earth ship attacked and killed their leader. Her disingenuity here and her greater role in that event become clearer much later in the series.
