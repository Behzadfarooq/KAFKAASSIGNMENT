Actual Size is an album by hard rock band Mr. Big released in 2001.

[edit] Track listing

  1. "Lost In America"
  2. "Wake Up"
  3. "Shine"
  4. "Arrow"
  5. "Mary Goes Round"
  6. "Suffocation"
  7. "One World Away"
  8. "I Don't Want to Be Happy"
  9. "Crawl Over Me"
  10. "Cheap Little Thrill"
  11. "How Did I Give Myself Away"
  12. "Nothing Like It In the World"

"Shine" is also the ending theme of the Anime Hellsing.
