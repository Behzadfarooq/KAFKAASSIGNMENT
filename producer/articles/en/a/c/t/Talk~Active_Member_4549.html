[edit] Beat jackin'??

I think that the following two sentences need to be verified since they include accusations that as far as I know are not real: "The group has caused controversy throughout the years, mainly when foreign hip-hop artists accused Active Member for illegally using musical compositions of their own (a practice known as beat jackin', which is rather frowned upon in hip-hop). However, formal lawsuits never occured." I know none foreign hip hop artist that has accused Active Member for beat jackin', and even though is an accusation commonly made (and possibly true) by greek fans of other greek hip hop groups, I do not think that this should stay here unless backed up by some verifiable source.--daydreamer 18:36, 13 September 2006 (UTC)

No proof provided yet, therefore I've removed the above mentioned in italics part.--daydreamer 16:28, 2 October 2006 (UTC)
