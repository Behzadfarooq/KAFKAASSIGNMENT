Acting age

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Acting age is an age, or range of ages, that an actor lists on his or her resume. It is not a claim to an actual chronological age, but a suggestion as to what age or ages the actor considers themselves capable of credibly portraying.


[edit] Notable examples

  • Stacey Dash, who played a 17 year old until the age of 33.
  • Estelle Getty, who played the mother of an actress who was actually older than her.
  • Sidney Poitier played a teenager in Blackboard Jungle when he was 29.
