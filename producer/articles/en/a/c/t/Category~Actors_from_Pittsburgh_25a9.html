Category:Actors from Pittsburgh

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

This category includes actors who were born in, residents of, or otherwise closely associated with the city of Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania and its surrounding metropolitan area, including:

  • Allegheny County, Pennsylvania,
  • Armstrong County, Pennsylvania,
  • Beaver County, Pennsylvania,
  • Butler County, Pennsylvania,
  • Fayette County, Pennsylvania,
  • Washington County, Pennsylvania, and
  • Westmoreland County, Pennsylvania.
