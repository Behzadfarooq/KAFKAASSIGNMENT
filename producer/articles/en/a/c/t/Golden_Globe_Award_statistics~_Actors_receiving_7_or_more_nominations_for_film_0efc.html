  • Meryl Streep (5 wins)

[edit] 17 Nominations

  • Jack Nicholson (6 wins)
    • Easy Rider (1969, supp.), Five Easy Pieces (1970, lead), Carnal Knowledge (1971, lead), The Last Detail (1973, lead), 'Chinatown' (1974, lead), One Flew Over the Cuckoo's Nest (1975, lead), Reds (1981, supp.), Terms of Endearment (1983, supp.), Prizzi's Honor (1985, lead), Ironweed (1987, lead), Batman (1989, lead), A Few Good Men (1992, supp.), Hoffa (1992, lead), As Good as It Gets (1997, lead), About Schmidt (2002, lead), Something's Gotta Give (2003, lead), The Departed (2006, supp.)

(1972, lead), Save the Tiger (1973, lead), The Front Page (1974, lead), The China Syndrome (1979, lead), Tribute (1980, lead), Missing (1982, lead), That's Life! (1986, lead), Dad (1989, lead)

[edit] 10 Nominations

[edit] 7 Nominations

    • Billy Bathgate (1991, supp.), To Die For (1995, lead), The Others (2001, lead), Moulin Rouge! (2001, lead), The Hours (2002, lead), Cold Mountain (2003, lead), Birth (2004, lead)
  • Geraldine Page (2 wins)

  • Ingrid Bergman (3 wins)
