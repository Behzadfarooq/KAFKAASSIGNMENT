[edit] Fair use rationale

Rationale for the fair use of this image in the article Actrise

  1. This image is used to show the appearance of Actrise, the subject of the article. No public domain images exist that could serve this purpose.
  2. The image is used for informative purposes and does not detract from the game guide.
  3. The image does not limit the copyright holder's ability to sell the game guide.
  4. The game guide from which the image was taken has been released to a wide audience. Its inclusion here does not make it significantly more visible.
  5. As a detail of one page of a book, the image is an extremely small portion of the copyrighted work.
