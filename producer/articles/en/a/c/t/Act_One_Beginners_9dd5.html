Act One Beginners is the call used in theatre, given by a stage manager to the cast and, if necessary, orchestra (the crew should already be in place, having been at their stations since the half hour call at the latest). It is a call for all members of the production to assume their places immediately as the show will begin as soon as both clearences have been received. Act One Beginners is called five minutes before the start of the show to ensure that everyone is in position for curtain up (i.e. the start of the production).

Act Two and Act Three Beginners work by the same principles.
