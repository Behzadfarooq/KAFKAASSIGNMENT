Toy Story 2: Action Game is a computer game for children, based around the animated film Toy Story 2.

Overview


You are Buzz Lightyear and you are on a quest to save Woody from the hands of Al. Throughout the game, you collect pizza planet tokens to progress further in the game. For instance, just one token is needed on Level 1 to progress to Level 2 , but you will need 40 to get to Level 15. Pizza Planet tokens can be collected by:

  • Collecting 50 coins or more for Hamm the Pig
  • Completing a timed challenge which is different in each level
  • Completing a puzzle
  • Finding 5 of a missing item for a certain character, e.g in Level 1 you have to find 5 sheep for Little Bo Peep
  • Defeating a mini boss in each level
