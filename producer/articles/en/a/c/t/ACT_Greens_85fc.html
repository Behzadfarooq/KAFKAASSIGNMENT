ACT Greens is a Green Party located in the Australian Capital Territory, a member of the federation of the Australian Greens party.

Dr Deb Foskey represents the ACT Greens in the Legislative Assembly.

[edit] Notes

1The ACT Greens do not formally have a leader. Dr Deb Foskey is the party's only elected member in the ACT and is treated by the media and public as the leader.

[edit] Previous Legislative Assembly members

  • Kerrie Tucker: from 1995 to 2004.
