Action of 18 November 1809 was a battle during the Napoleonic Wars. It took place on 18 November 1809 near Mauritius in the Indian Ocean, when 2 French frigates and a corvette defeated and captured 3 British East Indiamen. Wyndham was captured on 22 November after a long chase, but recaptured by the British frigate Magicienne 36 on 29 December just before she could arrive at Mauritius to meet the other ships.

[edit] Ships involved

[edit] France

  • Vénus 40
  • Manche 40
  • Créole 14

[edit] Britain

  • Wyndham 26 (John Stewart) - Captured by Vénus 22 November
  • Charlton 26 (Charles Mortlock) - Captured by Manche and Créole
  • United Kingdom 26 (William Parker D'Esterre) - Captured by Manche and Créole
