Acton Elementary School is an elementary school in Acton, Maine which educates grades kindergarten through eighth. Acton Elementary enrolls approximately 300 students each year.

The school has a teacher:student ratio of 1:10, below the state average of 1:14. 98% of the students enrolled in the school are white. The school principal is Kyle Rhoads who recently replaced Carol A. Eddy. The schools' mascot is the wildcat and their school colors are blue and white.

  • Lyonel Tracy
  • Donald Gnecco
  • Carol Eddy (1988 – 2003)
  • Kyle Rhoads (2003 – present)
