ACG

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

ACG is a three-letter acronym that may refer to:

  • American College for Girls, which is now called Robert College of Istanbul
  • Angle closure glaucoma
  • Anime, Comics, and Games, a collective term used mainly in Taiwan
  • Ashby Computer Graphics, a video game developer now trading as Rare
  • Azeri-Chirag-Guneshli, an oil field in the Caspian Sea
  • Acocks Green railway station, in the United Kingdom, from its National Railway code
  • A codon of Threonine in genome expression
  • Atcor Medical Holdings Ltd, a Health Care Equipment manufacturer listed on the ASX under code ACG
  • American College of Gastroenterology
  • American College of Greece
  • American Comics Group, a comic-book publisher in the Golden Age of comic books
  • Air Combat Group, an organisational unit of the Royal Australian Air Force
