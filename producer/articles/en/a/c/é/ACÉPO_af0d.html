ACÉPO

The Association des conseils scolaires des écoles publiques de l'Ontario changed its name in 2006 from Association des conseillères et des conseillers des écoles publiques de l'Ontario to better reflect its membership. The Association, whose acronym remains ACÉPO, is composed of 4 different French-language school boards (FLSBs) in the province of Ontario. In 2006, student registration in the provincial Public French-language schools has grown by 20% since its inception in 1998.

  • Conseil des écoles publiques de l'Est de l'Ontario (CÉPEO)
  • Conseil scolaire de district du Centre-Sud-Ouest (CSDCSO)
  • Conseil scolaire public du Grand Nord de l'Ontario (CSDGNO)
  • Conseil scolaire public du Nord-Est de l'Ontario (CSDNE)
