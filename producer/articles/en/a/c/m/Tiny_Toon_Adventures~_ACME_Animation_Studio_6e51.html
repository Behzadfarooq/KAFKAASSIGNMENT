Tiny Toon Adventures: ACME Animation Studio is a game that is supposed to have been released on the Sega Mega Drive/Genesis. Very little is known about this game other than that it was developed and released by Konami in 1994.

Tiny Toon Adventures: ACME Animation Studio is a "cartoon creation" game, allowing the player to create your own Tiny Toon Adventures cartoons, using templates and pre-created animated sequences. It is very much like the NES title, Tiny Toon Adventures Cartoon Workshop.
