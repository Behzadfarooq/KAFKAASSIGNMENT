ACME Animation Factory is a Super Nintendo game released by Sunsoft on November of 1994.

In ACME Animation Factory, the player is given a series of tools to create his own cartoons, using the most famous characters of Looney Tunes, including graphics, animation and music. When the cartoon has been created, it can be saved and later replayed.
