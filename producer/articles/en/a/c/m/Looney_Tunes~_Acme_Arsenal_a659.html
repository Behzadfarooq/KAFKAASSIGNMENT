[edit] Story

No details of the story have been announced yet.

[edit] Characters

There will be seven characters that include Bugs Bunny, Daffy Duck, The Tazmanian Devil, Foghorn Leghorn, Marvin the Martian, Gossamer, and an unnamed secret character

[edit] Melee Stages

Gamers command an arsenal of ACME melee and projectile weapons in outrageous battles and travel to various locations such as Camelot, Ancient Egypt, Mars, The Wild West, and the trenches of World War II.

[edit] Wii Remote Use

The Wii Remote will be used in this game for spin attacks, fighting, smash attacks, and driving.

[edit] History

This game was announced in the March issue of Nintendo Power.
