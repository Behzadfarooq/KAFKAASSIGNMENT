ACM Crossroads is a current information science journal published in both print and electronic forms by the Association for Computing Machinery. The full text is available online, without subscription. The first edition was published in 1994(Jones 2003). According to its publisher, it is run for and by students, and was their first electronically published journal(ACM 2004). They report the hardcopy circulaton to be approximately 20,000(ACM 2005). Issues exist for every quarter since Fall 1994, with an extra, mid-Summer, issue in 2001(ACM 2005b)
