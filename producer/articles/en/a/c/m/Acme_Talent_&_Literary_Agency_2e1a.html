Acme Talent & Literary Agency

Acme Talent & Literary is a "full service" talent and literary agency, based in Los Angeles, that represents writers and actors for film, television, commercials, print and voice-overs. They represent over 250 clients and staff around 9 agents. They are considered a lower mid-level agency. They have a strong commercial department and are well-known for representing children.
