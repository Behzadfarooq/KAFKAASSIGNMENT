Aczel's anti-foundation axiom

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

You have new messages (last change).

Aczel's anti-foundation axiom is an axiom set forth by Paul Aczel in his book Non-Well-Founded Sets. It states that every finite graph corresponds to one or more sets.
