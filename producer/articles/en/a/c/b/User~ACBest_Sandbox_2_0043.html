[edit] Unnofficial Travel Info

Unnofficial Travel Info is a voluntary buisness based in Lincoln, UK. It has not fully opened yet. Unnofficial Travel Info abbreviates into Uti. Also, the mispelling of the word unnofficial is meant to be there.


[edit] About Uti

Unnofficial Travel Info is not a walk-in office. It is only reachable by phone or the internet.

What Uti will provide is travel information in the form of:

• A Text Message

• Phonecall

• Or The Internet, on thier (not yet opened) website.
