[edit] Test5

If you look at the Template:Test5 (edit|talk|history|links|watch|logs) template, you will see that it is a template that admins use to tell users that they have been blocked, as opposed to a straight warning. If you are dealing with repeated vandalism, and the user has been warned with {{test4}}, then the best thing to do is to post about it at WP:AIV, which will draw it to the attention of an admin who can block the offender. --GW_SimulationsUser Page | Talk 11:54, 12 November 2006 (UTC)
