[edit] Userbox grouping

Grouping your userboxes is quite simple. Insert {{userboxtop|?}} above some userboxes, with ? being replaced by what you want to call the group of userboxes. Below the userboxes you want to group, add {{userboxbottom}}. For example:

{{userboxtop|Stuff that has no relation to chocolate milk}}

{{User:UBX/Undecided}}

{{User WPFishes}}

{{userboxbottom}}

Creates:

Stuff that has no relation to chocolate milk
:-|
This user is undecided.
This user is a member of
Wikiproject Fishes

--Gray PorpoisePhocoenidae, not Delphinidae 21:57, 1 November 2006 (UTC)

You're welcome, that looks good. --Gray PorpoisePhocoenidae, not Delphinidae 21:17, 2 November 2006 (UTC)
