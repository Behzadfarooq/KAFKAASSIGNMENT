- A genuine Wikiholic.

Wikipedia Is My Thing.
This user is one or once was of the 20 highest-scoring users on the Are You a Wikipediholic Test.

My score on the test was 1599(aprox.). I rank 15th on the test. To see my exact score, please click on the link on the box above.


I've been hoping to create this page for a long time, but have never gotten around to it. I just a bit lazy in writing it, I suspect. I find that userboxes are much more useful in expressing one's personality on Wikipedia. I'm interested in a vast variety of things, including Western Europe, in particular Luxembourg, Switzerland, Germany, and The Netherlands. I also am interested in Dogs, most notably the Cavalier King Charles Spaniel. When I become older, I would like to live in Zurich, Switzerland. I am also interested in studying Law or Political Sciences. Another job ambition is to become an Anesthesiologist I would like (hopefully), to attend an Ivy League university, but if not, I would like to go to Duke University. This is just a bit about myself, more to come later. Fair Travels on your journey through Wikipedia!
