[edit] Alexa Ranking

I removed the Alexa ranking segment of the intro:

It was the 14th top political blog of 2006, as determined by Alexa Internet [2], making the list just below Wonkette and just above Andrew Sullivan...

When I emailed Ace (the site's main blogger) to show him the improved page, and he emailed me back, saying:

One error-- and I actually went on Wiki to correct it, but didn't see how, and didn't want to screw things up. I think that Alexa rating almost certainly overstates the blog's traffic and whatever. I think Alexa cannot separate out one mu.nu blog from the hundred others -- so that rating is an aggregate of me, MyPetJawa, Confederate Yankee, and a hundred smaller blogs.

I think that warrented removal. --Ultramontane 20:56, 11 January 2007 (UTC)
