Ace Fu Records is an independent record label currently located in downtown Manhattan.

Founded in 1998 by Eric Speck, Ace Fu is known for its support of bands like Man Man, An Albatross, and Aqui, as well as indie acts such as Pinback, Kaiser Chiefs, The Dears, Ted Leo and Annuals.
