[edit] Education

My degree is in Linguistics from Tulane University, New Orleans, Louisiana. The degree is broad in scope, but I spent most of my time on Syntax and Sociolinguistics. I studied abroad twice, once in Canada and once in France and enjoyed it very much. While not a formal part of my degree, I spent most of my remaining electives on history. My history area of interest is the rise of Rome.

[edit] Career

I'm an indentured servant of the Intelligence Community, United States Air Force style. In my past life (that is, before graduation) I had numerous jobs of interest. I served as tech geek for Asa Hutchinson's campaign for US Congress, I did volunteer work with John Boozman's congressional office, and I worked as an online Community Manager for AOL prior to their December 04 realignment of the company.

[edit] Hobbies and Interest

If you didn't guess from my name, I like Card Games (casino card games). However, as you may not have guess from my name, my game of choice is Blackjack, not Poker. I am a certified Blackjack dealer and am working on my poker certificate.

I also keep pet fish, Black Fin Sharks to be more specific. I currently have three: VX, TaNu and BlackDeath the Plague Bringer. The third's name is a lot longer than the others, and is called simply BlackDeath for short.
