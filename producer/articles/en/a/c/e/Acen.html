Acen Razvi (real name Syed Ahsen Razvi) was an early 1990s UK rave and Breakbeat Hardcore techno producer. Acen was known for tracks such as "Close Your Eyes" and "Trip II The Moon" (both 1992). He worked for the Production House stable which also brought fame to Baby D (dance group). He released the album "75 Minutes".

He has also recorded material under the name Appleheads and has collaborated with Baby D (dance group)'s Floyd Dice to form the House Crew

Trip II The Moon (Part 2)[1992]

1 Trip II The Moon (The Darkside) (6:22) 2 The Life And Crimes Of A Ruffneck (6:01) 3 Close Your Eyes (Optikonfusion!) (6:01

Track 3 samples - "Just close your eyes / forget your name / forget the world / forget the people / go insane" from Celebration of the Lizard by The Doors and "overdose" from Mr Kirk's Nightmare by 4 Hero.

Trip II The Moon (Part 2 The Dark Side) [1992]

A Trip II The Moon (The Darkside) AA The Life And Crimes Of A Ruffneck

Inspired by a pitch black forest rave with only the moon as light source." Track A samples strings from "You Only Live Twice" by Nancy Sinatra, from the film of the same name. Also samples Rebel MC and 80 Aum. Track AA strings are taken from "Chi Mai" by Ennio Morricone. The bassline from this tune was later used on "You & Me" by Ruff Rider.
