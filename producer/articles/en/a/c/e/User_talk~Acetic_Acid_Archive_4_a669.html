Re: Pokemon VFDs

I was putting the personality-less gym leaders on VFD, yes, the ones who are currently only two sentences of description and lists of Pokemon teams. I was also planning on expanding Pokémon Ruby and Sapphire to cover the leaders of Team Aqua and Team Magma. You'll note that I haven't put Drake (Orange Islands) or Rudy (Pokémon) or Professor Birch on VFD; those are recurring characters with personalities. On the other hand, Brawly (Pokémon) is a one-off opponent with no personality, role in the story, or backstory.

PAC is doing good work covering the characters who are actually characters, and not simply non-notable obstacles in a long-running game series. The stuff I'm redirecting or VFDing, however, is not the product of PAC's hard work, but instead GameFAQs-esque lists of names and numbers. - A Man In Black (Talk | Contribs) 11:47, 6 August 2005 (UTC)

I'm not throwing up VFDs willy-nilly here. I've got a page open to Serebii, a pair of Google windows open, and a window open editing R&S right now. I'm a fan of the series, but one-off Pokemon characters aren't notable, Pokemon project or otherwise. Brawly, for example, appears once in R/S/E and once in episode 296 of AG (which hasn't even been translated into English yet, as far as I can tell). There's nothing to add to this article other than a summary of the events of episode 296. Bugsy, at least, has been in the already-localized episodes of AG, but, again, only once.
Chuck (Pokémon), in retrospect, I'm going to delist. Looks like he's a recurring character in the manga.
It's not that I hate Pokemon or anything. Wouldn't most of these one-off characters be better served with a List of minor Gym Trainers in Pokémon, anyway? - A Man In Black (Talk | Contribs) 12:01, 6 August 2005 (UTC)
If you want to make a draft for that, I'll start delisting these from VFD. Updating Pokémon Ruby and Sapphire can wait. - A Man In Black (Talk | Contribs) 12:07, 6 August 2005 (UTC)

Looks like this stirred up some strong feelings from WP:PAC. You might want to check out Talk:List of Johto Gym Leaders. - A Man In Black (Talk | Contribs) 15:10, 6 August 2005 (UTC)
