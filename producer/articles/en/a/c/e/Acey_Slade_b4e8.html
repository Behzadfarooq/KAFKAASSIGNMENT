Acey Slade

Acey Slade, born on 15 December 1970 in Pennsylvania, is the lead vocalist and guitarist in the Punk/Rock band Trashlight Vision. He also plays Guitar in the Horror Punk/Glam Metal band Murderdolls. Previous to the Murderdolls, he played bass in the band Dope alongside with guitarist Tripp Eisen who was also part of the Murderdolls. Once Tripp left Dope, Acey was moved to guitar for the album Life. He later left Dope to join Murderdolls, replacing Tripp once again on guitar. Before Murderdolls (alongside his Dope work), he also fronted a band known as Vampire Love Dolls. He's a huge fan of Tim Burton's Nightmare Before Christmas.
