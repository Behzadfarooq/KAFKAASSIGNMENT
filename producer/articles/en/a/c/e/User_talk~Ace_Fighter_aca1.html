[edit] Re:Heyy

Changing a font's color is fairly simply, really. Just type:

<font color="Green">Green text</font>

or

<font color="#008000">Green text</font>

and you'll get this:

Green text

You can take a look at web colors#HTML color names and web colors#X11 color names for more on this. Happy editting. ╫ ２５ ◀RingADing▶ 10:36, 27 January 2007 (UTC) ╫
