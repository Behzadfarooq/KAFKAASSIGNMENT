My name is Jonathan Mooney. I am currently a student (Year 1 of 3) at McGill University pursuing a Bachelor of Science degree in chemistry as well as a Bachelor of Arts degree in history. During the summers of 2002, 2003, and 2004, I worked as a summer employee of the United States Air Force.

I resided in in Saugus, Massachusetts for the first 18 years of my life but have lived in Montréal, Québec since August 2004. I am an alumnus of Malden Catholic High School. I am the webmaster of Classicqueen.com but I also have a personal website and a journal.

I can be reached at acegikmo1@aol.com

[edit] Contributions

I officially joined Wikipedia in December 2003, although there is evidence to suggest that I had been editing articles since late October 2003. At first, I wrote articles on music I liked, Star Trek, and politics. Then, I frequented the Wikipedia: Requested Articles page. Now, I create and edit articles about chemistry, history, music, and science fiction. I also occasionally contribute an article about the Catholic Church.

My article on the New Hampshire primary was "in the news" on the Main Page in January 2004. Thanks to everyone who edited it into excellence.
