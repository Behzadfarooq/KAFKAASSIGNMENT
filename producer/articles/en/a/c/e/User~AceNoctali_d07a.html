Hiya, I'm AceNoctali.


I'm a 23-years old French. My interests are : books (novels, mangas and comicbooks), video games, music, anime and movies.

I have a very wide array of tastes in all those interests. But generally, I love original and/or unknown (or little known) concepts.


My favourite manga titles : Chrno Crusade, Planètes, FullMetal Alchemist, Full Metal Panic, Akira, Azumanga Daioh, Fruits Basket, GetBackers, Dragon Head, Seizon (Life), Samuraï Deeper Kyo, Rurouni Kenshin, and 20th Century Boys.


My fave anime : Uchuu no Stellvia, Mujin Wakusei Survive, and Nanaka 6/17. (add also FMA, Planètes, Chrno Crusade, and FMP into this list)


My 5 first favourite video games, from 1st to 5th : Fire Emblem : Seisen no Keifu, Dr Robotnik’s Mean Bean Machine (Puyo Puyo), Star Ocean : the Second Story, Tokimeki Memorial : Forever with you, Lunar : Silver Star Story.


Other video games I love (among LOTS of titles) : Colony Wars, Bare Knuckle 2 (Streets of Rage 2), Genesis’ Sonic saga, Command & Conquer 95 / Tiberian Sun, Light-Gun Shooters (like Virtua Cop, Time Crisis, and The House of the Dead).


My fave video game genre : Strategy games (be it either Puzzle, Gestion, Wargame, or Tactical-RPG) the most.

I'm a Sega fan at heart (my first, and still fave console is a Sega Mega Drive).


My fave movies : - Alien saga (especially the second one, "Aliens") - Terminator 1 et 2, - Die Hard saga, - Jurassic Park saga, - Back to the Future saga, - Forrest Gump, - Rain Man, - The Deer Hunter, - Saving Private Ryan, - Paradise Road, - Seven Samurai, - Once upon a time in the West, - Léon, - Le Dîner de Cons.


My fave novel : The Count of Monte-Cristo.
