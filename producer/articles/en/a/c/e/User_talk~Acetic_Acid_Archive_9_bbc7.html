Thank you for the welcome

It was good of you to offer the welcome. If it is not too much trouble, I could use some help. I have created a couple of pages. These pages are now gone. They now redirect to other pages that don't have my content. I understand that my contributions may get edited, or even removed; but what are the rules for this?
