Ace and Vis, aka The Troublemakers of Radio, are BBC 1Xtra radio DJs and television presenters from south-east London. They met whilst studying at the BRIT School in London.

After winning a gold Sony Radio Academy Award for their lunchtime show on 1Xtra in 2004, the two now co-present 1Xtra's drivetime show from 4-7pm on weekdays. They also co-present a show on the UK's Channel U.

Recent programmes have seen the two presenters flipping pancakes in the street, attempting to fry eggs on the pavement, and interviewing Jimmy Saville and former 1980's WWF wrestling stars. Guests on their show include musicians and artists such as Chamillionaire, Ne-Yo, Missy Elliott, Beenie Man, Wiley, GZA, Asher D, Chris Brown, T.I., Lupe Fiasco, Jamie Foxx and John Legend.
