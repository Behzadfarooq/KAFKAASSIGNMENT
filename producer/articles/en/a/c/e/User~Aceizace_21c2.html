[edit] Me

Hello, my name is Andrew. I am currently a student at The Universtiy of Bath in England reading Computer Science with Maths. I love computers, games, and things-Japanese. Lovely to meet you.

[edit] Wikipedia

Wikipedia is the best website on the internet. I love knowledge, in all its forms. One of the best things in the world is being able to answer those random questions people ask in passing, the ones in the form of "I wonder why...?" and so on.

[edit] Wikipedia and Me

I'll try and add what I can to Wikipedia. Like I said I will just look on random pages for information, just because it's interesting. If I see something wrong or missing I'll add it, and if I see some vandalism, I'll revert it. But I'm not a massive contributor, yet, I tend to add to discussion pages more than the main article. That being said, I'm slowing picking up pace, checking the recent changes for vandalism is always fun and rewarding. Watch this space.
