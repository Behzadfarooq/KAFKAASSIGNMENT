Ace of Cakes is a reality television show currently (as of 2007) airing on the Food Network. It features Duff Goldman, the owner of Charm City Cakes, a custom cake shop in Baltimore, Maryland. The show focuses on the daily proceedings of the business.

The show has garnered some criticism for being the Food Network's attempt to toughen up their wholesome image. Stuff magazine called Duff one of TV's wimpiest men [1]. Another criticism of the show is that actual edible cakes are not made. Rather, contraptions made with welded metal or bonded plastic and styrofoam are covered with frosting and are typically inedible.[citation needed]
