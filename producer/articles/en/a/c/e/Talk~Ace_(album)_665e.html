[edit] Merge proposal

I support the merge of the song information to this page, at least for now, because:

  • There is very little information residing on this page at present anyway.
  • These songs have done little to distinguish themselves and are not obviously notable subjects of standalone articles
  • There's no loss of information or anything of that sort, and, with redirects in place, it is still easy to find information on a particular song.

Therefore, I believe editors wishing to expand the coverage of the individual songs would do better to include it on this, the page for the album, and make one decent article, rather than set up a lot of short articles about songs which are, at best, of borderline notability.
