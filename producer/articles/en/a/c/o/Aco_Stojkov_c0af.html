Aco Stojkov (born 29 April 1983 in Strumica) is a Macedonian international football (soccer) player.

He played with national team-mate Goran Pandev in F.C. Internazionale Milano Primavera, 2001/02, which won the youth league title. Aco Stojkov plays as a striker and currently plays for Debreceni VSC.
