Acontiinae is a subfamily of moth family Noctuidae.

[edit] Genera of the Acontiinae

The Acontiinae do not hold an overly large number of genera at present (for noctuid moth standards at least). Apart from the tribes listed in the taxobox, there are many genera not presently assigned to a specific tribe as their relationships require further study. These genera incertae sedis are:
