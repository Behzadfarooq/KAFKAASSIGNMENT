Acoustic Hearts of Winter is a Christmas album from pop artists Aly & AJ. The album was released on September 26, 2006 in the United States. The first single from the album was "Greatest Time Of Year" and was used as promotion for The Santa Clause 3 with the music video containing clips of the film. The music video premiered on Disney Channel on October 16, 2006 after the movie Twitches. Clips of the album were first released on the Barnes & Noble website.

The Target Corporation version of this album also has an extra song to it - "We Wish you a Merry Christmas". So far, it has sold 114,204 copies in the U.S.[1]
