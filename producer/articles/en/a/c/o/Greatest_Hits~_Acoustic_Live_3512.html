Greatest Hits: Acoustic Live is a compilation album from Scottish singer-songwriter Donovan. It was released on October 21, 1997 (Laserlight 12976).

[edit] History

Although Beacon Records issued an edited version of Rising only two months before, Laserlight somehow gained the rights to the same set of songs and issued Greatest Hits: Acoustic Live. The Laserlight issue included some of the original songs from Rising not included on Beacon's Performance, but also cut other tracks that were on Performance.
