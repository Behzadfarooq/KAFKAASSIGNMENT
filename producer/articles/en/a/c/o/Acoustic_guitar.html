Acoustic guitar

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Acoustic guitar can refer to the following musical instrument:

  • Nylon and gut stringed guitars:
    • Renaissance guitar
    • Baroque guitar
    • Romantic guitar
    • Classical guitar, the modern version of the original guitar, with nylon strings
    • Flamenco guitar
  • Steel stringed guitars:
    • Steel-string acoustic guitar, also known as western, folk or country guitar
    • Twelve string guitar
    • Resonator guitar (such as the Dobro)
    • Archtop guitar
    • Battente guitar
    • Lap steel guitar
    • Lyre guitar
  • Acoustic bass guitar
  • Russian guitar
  • Novelty instruments:
    • Harp guitar
    • Banjo guitar
    • Guitar lute
  • Acoustic Guitar (magazine), a magazine published in the US
