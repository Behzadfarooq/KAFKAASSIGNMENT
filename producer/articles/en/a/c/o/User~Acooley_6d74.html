Hello! I'm an business and language enthusiast currently residing in Wyandotte, Michigan, USA. Currently I work at Fifth Third Bank as an international banker and attend the University of Michigan to get my MBA. I also run a side business as a real estate entrepreneur.

In addition to native English, I am fluent in Japanese and Portuguese, competent in French, Korean, and Spanish, and can speak a little Mandarin Chinese. (Chinese practice)

I also enjoy classical music, business, strategy games (including real-time strategy, chess, and go), computers, and Japanese literature. Thanks for visiting my page.

Feel free to leave me a message on the discussion page, or e-mail me at aaronsama@yahoo.com.
