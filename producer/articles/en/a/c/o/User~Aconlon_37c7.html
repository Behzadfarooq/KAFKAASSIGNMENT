[edit] Early Years

Andrew (age 8) and Tory

Born January 19,1985 in Alameda, California, Andrew was a normal, relatively bright child having an eighth grade reading level by the time he was in third grade. At that time his family and him moved to Jakarta, Indonesia as his father was and is a consultant. After the two and a half years spent in Indonesia, Andrew came back to California where he entered fifth grade and the school's GATE program. Other points of interest include: acting in Beauty and the Beast and being an all-star tetherball player.












[edit] Middle School

Andrew (age 13)

He went to a public middle school in California where he started to learn spanish, clarinet, and a plethera of mostly useless information. Furthermore, as far as sports went, Andrew played football, basketball, tennis, baseball, and soccer.








[edit] High School

Andrew (age 17)



Upon entering high school, Andrew dropped the majority of his sports - at least everything except tennis - and focused not only on academics but joined both the tennis and diving teams. Throughout high school, Andrew took AP and Honors classes, continued to learn spanish, started to learn how to skateboard and film, and did relatively well academically.





[edit] College Years

Andrew (age 21)

After realizing that although he was relatively proficient in the subjects of mathematics and English, Andrew chose to apply to colleges majoring in film. He got into UCSC, SFSU and UCSD and decided to go to UCSD (University of California, San Diego). He is currently in a film club, called muir movie, which produces a film throughout the year. He will be graduating a year early as he is tired of college and wants to move on in life ie failing repeatedly until, 10 years down the line, he gets a job.
