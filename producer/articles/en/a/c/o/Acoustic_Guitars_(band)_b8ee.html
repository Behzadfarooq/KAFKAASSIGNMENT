Acoustic Guitars (band)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Acoustic Guitars is a Danish musical quintet influenced by Spanish, Arab and Indian music. They where formed by Mikkel Nordsø, Christian Ratzer, and Steen Kyed. Later Klavs Nordsø joined, and recently Ole Theill.

Acoustic guitars are:

  • Mikkel Nordsø: Guitar
  • Christian Ratzer: Guitar
  • Steen Kyed: Guitar
  • Klavs Nordsø: Percussion
  • Ole Theill: Tablas

[edit] Discography

  • Acoustic Guitars (1987)
  • Gajos in disguise (1990)
  • Out of the Blue (1995)
  • Arabesque (2002)
