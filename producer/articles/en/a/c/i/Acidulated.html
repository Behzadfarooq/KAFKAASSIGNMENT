Acidulated

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

You have new messages (last change).

Acidulated describes something that has been rendered acid or sour.

  • Acidulated drops are an old-fashioned candy similar to modern lemon and lime drops.
  • Acidulated water is water to which lemon or lime juice has been added, (used to prevent food from browning when cut).
  • Acidulated malt
  • Acidulated phosphate fluoride
