Aci Sant'Antonio is a comune (municipality) in the Province of Catania in the Italian region Sicily, located about 160 km southeast of Palermo and about 10 km northeast of Catania. As of 31 December 2004, it had a population of 16,692 and an area of 14.3 km².[1]

The municipality of Aci Sant'Antonio contains the frazioni (subdivisions, mainly villages and hamlets) S.Maria la Stella, Monterosso Etneo, Lavinaio, and Lavina.

Aci Sant'Antonio borders the following municipalities: Aci Bonaccorsi, Aci Catena, Acireale, Valverde, Viagrande, Zafferana Etnea.
