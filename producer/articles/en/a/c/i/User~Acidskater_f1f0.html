I currently put a lot of my time into this wikiproject by expanding and editing pages on fraternities. I even create articles on fraternities which don't have any. Every article I create I watch, so go down to my list of pages I watch and you should be able to figure it out. I am a brother of Sigma Chi and highly encourage everyone to join a Greek organization. I do edit many other articles and create other articles. I have been known to add my two cents on the subject of Fair Use photos because I think the current trend of deleting all fair-use photos on the idea that they can be replaced with a free image, even if they won't or it is near impossible, is stupid. Don't get me wrong, I wish it all could be freely licensed, but that just isn't reality. Anyway, feel free to leave me a message on my talk page, I like feedback.
