Acid soul

The Acid Soul is a grind used in Aggressive Inline Skating. It is similar to the Soul grind but differs in that the leading foot is positioned in the 'backside' position (with the heel of the skate pointing towards the object being ground). The trailing foot, like the Soul grind is placed on the sole plate of the skate, like a Makio. Sometimes the Acid Soul is abbreviated to just 'Acid'. Acid Souls can also be performed topside.

[edit] Variants

Several modifications exist for this basic trick.

[edit] Citric Acid

Essentially the same feet positioning, but the leading foot is wrapped around the back of the trailing foot then placed back in front.

[edit] Battery Acid

Alley-oop (backwards) with leading foot only riding on front wheel.
