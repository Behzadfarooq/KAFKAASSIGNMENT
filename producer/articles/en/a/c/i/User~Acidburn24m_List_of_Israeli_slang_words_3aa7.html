[edit] Slang and swear words

Most of the swear words and phrases in Hebrew are, in fact, borrowed from other languages (usually either Arabic, Russian, Yiddish or English).

May it be noted that much of the Israeli slang comes from the army, where most of the Israeli slang is invented.

[edit] Examples of Sexual slang

  • Kus (כוס) - Used to boys/men to describe a female genitalia in a non friendly manner. Considered to be a vulgar word.
  • Pot (פות) - The female genitalia. Not an offensive word.
  • Zayin (זין) - The male genitalia. Considered to be a vulgar word.
  • Pin (פין) - The male genitalia. Not an offensive word.
  • Shadaiim (שדיים) - To describe breast of a women. Not an offensive word.

[edit] Examples of swear words

  • Fraier (פראייר) - means "sucker", "gullible", "loser", a person whom is exploited by others, a person whom give up on his own success to the favor of values which are outdated.
  • Kus emmek (כוס אמק) - taken from Arabic, means "your mother's cunt", is similar in meaning to the English word "fuck". (f.ex "Kus emek, my car broke down").
  • Ben zona (בן זונה) - son of a bitch
  • Inahl Rabak Ars Ya Choosharmuta (אינעל רב רבעק ערס יא'חושרמוטה) - Go to Hell With your Fucking Father
  • Jiffa (ג'יפה) - taken from Arabic, means "garbage", "filth" (Everything that is a little bit dirty is referred to as "Jiffa").
  • Khappes mi ye'na'a'ne'a ot'kha (חפש מי ינענע אותך) - literally means "go search who will jiggle (rock) you"
  • Tembel (טמבל) - stupid moron
  • Lech tizdayen (לך תזדיין) - Go fuck yourself
  • Ya ben sharmuta (יא בן שרמוטה) - Son of a whore
  • Ya chatikhat hara (יא חתיכת חרא) - You piece of shit
  • Ya man maniak (יא מן מנייאק) - You fucking maniac

[edit] Examples of General slang

  • Sababa (סבבה) - means "cool", "great", "no problem", "all-right"
  • Magneev (מגניב) - means "awesome"
  • Bas'a (באסה) - means "bummer".
  • Kusit (כוסית) - sexy woman. Usually refers to the body, not the face.
  • Kuson (כוסון) - the male version of kusit.
  • Totach (תותח) - (Literally means "Cannon") means "Talented", "hot", "alpha male".
  • Nishbar li hazayin (נשבר לי הזין) - (Literally means "my dick has broken") means "I've had it". (i.e. - "What's wrong?" "My dick's broken from this work / relationship / television.")
  • Al Hapanim (על הפנים) - (Literally means "on the face") means "really bad", "sucks". Often used as an adjective for mood. (i.e. - "How are things?" "Don't ask, on the face".)
  • Matzav (מצב) - (Literally means "Cannon") "there is a situation" means "it is foreseeable", or "it is possible that". Used in different ways, see following entries.
  • Ma hamatzav? (?מה המצב) - What's up? (a common question that does not usually require an answer)
  • Yesh matzav (יש מצב) - (Literally means "there is a situation") - used either in the form of a question or as an answer (i.e. - "Is there a situation that we go to a movie today?" "there is a situation")
  • Ein matzav (אין מצב) - (Literally means "there is no situation") - (i.e. - "Is there a situation you'll clean your room for me honey?" "Forget it mom, there isn't a situation.")

[edit] Examples of Ethnic slurs

  • Tzabar (צבר) - is a slang term used to describe a native-born Israeli. The word is derived from the Hebrew name of the "prickly pear" cactus. The allusion is to a tenacious, thorny desert plant with a thick hide that conceals a sweet, softer interior i.e., tough on the outside, sweet and tender on the inside.
  • Ashkenazim (אשכנזים) : the European Jews. AKA 'Tzfonim' (Northerners).
  • Mizrakhim (מזרחים) : the North African Jews. AKA 'Dromim' (Southerners).

Smaller groups:

  • Arsim (ערסים) : is a Israeli slang term for an Israeli sub-culture, which would be parallel to American rednecks. The stereotyical Israeli Ars is usually a Sephardic Jew of Moroccan, Persian or Yemenite descent, known for his bad manners, flashy jewelry, and overall rudeness.
  • Frehot (פרחות) : is the Israeli slang term for bimbos. Mostly referring to women that wear skimpy clothes, high platform shoes and has low intelligence and often speaks bad Hebrew. Its male counterpart is usually the Ars.
  • Chnoonim (חנונים) : is the Israeli slang term for geeks. The stereotype is that the biggest geeks are the Ashkenazi Jews.
  • Yapim (יאפים) - is the Israeli slang term for the northern Ashkenazi Jews. stereotyical Israeli Yapim would bleech their hair, have a single earing, a tattoo on their back, wear foreign designer fashion and wear their designer sun glasses with them wherever they go.
  • Hippim (היפים) - is the Israeli slang term for Hippies.
  • Rockerim (רוקרים) - is the Israeli slang term for rockers. The stereotyical Israeli Rocker is long-haired, likes to listen to heavy-metal music, wears dark clothes and generally believes that the end of the world is near.
  • Dosim (דוסים) - is the Israeli slang term for Haredi Jews.
