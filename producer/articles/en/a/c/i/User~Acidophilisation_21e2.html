[edit] About Me

I'm Karlee, and I guess I'm one of the younger Wikipedia contributors, since I'm 13. I'm in 8th grade, and I live in NJ, but I was born in Quito, Ecuador, so I can speak Spanish fluently (not to mention some German and Italian).

As for my personality, I'm pretty outgoing. I'm known to have a sharp tongue and witty remarks, but it often lands me in trouble at school. @_@ I like to try anything once, and am a bit of a tomboy according to most people I know.

In terms of interests, I'm all over the map. I like working with computer graphics and reading manga, so I'm in the process of writing my own manga, along with some friends. Video games and music are my obessions (so much so that I have waaay too many CDs to count and every penny of my spare money goes towards a new gaming console). Mostly, I like punk and metal music, but I've recently been listening to alot of ska. Other than that, I'm just a typical teenager: I like playing with my band,trying to create the next great energy drink, writing, skateboarding, snowboarding, and virtually every sport but basketball :P.

If you ever want to talk or discuss anything, just drop me a line on my talk page. I won't bite, promise ;]

[edit] My thoughts on Wikipedia

Wikipedia is an ENCYCLOPEDIA. This isn't the place for speculation and "what if?"s. It doesn't matter if the rumors turn out to be true. It's a collection of facts, not a crystal ball! So please,follow the no original research and no speculation policy.

Wikipedia is not for vandalizing. If you feel like trashing someone, do it on your MySpace or whatever the hell you want. But NOT on Wikipedia. Don't trash someone or something just because you feel like it. All you're doing is making yourself a target for endless mockery of your immaturity. Not to mention that trashing someone or something on Wikipedia is one of the lowest things you can do. In short: You WILL look like an immature moron if you do this. You have been warned. I won't bite your head off or anything, but I just think that the human race as a whole is better than that, don't you think? Spread the Wikilove, man.
