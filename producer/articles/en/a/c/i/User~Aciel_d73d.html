User:Aciel

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Aciel (alias for John Oates Woods, III) was born in Alexandria, Virginia in the United States on March 20, 1984. He graduated from St. Stephen's and St. Agnes School in 2002.

He is a member of the Hillcrest Honors Community at Virginia Polytechnic Institute and State University, where he studies computer science, mathematics, philosophy, Russian language, and biology. He will graduate in 2006 with a Bachelor of Science degree in computer science.

His specific research interests include psycholinguistics, education, and knowledge representation. He hopes to study cognitive neuroscience after he completes his undergraduate degree.

Aciel achieved his black belt in Chung Do Kwan Tae Kwon Do, from Khan's Martial Arts Academy in Burke, Virginia, on December 3, 2005. He is an assistant instructor for the Tae Kwon Do Club at Virginia Tech.

Aciel can be reached by email at bamboowarrior on gmail.com.
