[edit] Link Problem

There is a problem with the link on this template for "this page's entry". The text only goes

[[Category:AID candidates|{{PAGENAME}}]]

but the WP:AID page's candidates all have sections called "PageName (# votes, stays until Month #)", so the link doesn't work; it only takes you to the top of the AID page, not the specific section.

-- King of Hearts 00:34, 18 December 2005 (UTC)

I noticed this as well. I changed the heading formats at WP:AID, but I guess that upset their bot or something, so they switched it back. Thus I have removed the broken link. I view this as a less than optimal solution. See discussion at Wikipedia talk:Article Improvement Drive. -lethe talk + 16:29, 7 April 2006 (UTC)
