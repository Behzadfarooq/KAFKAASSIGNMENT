[edit] Response to your comment

I was not responsible for the deletion of your article (as I do not have the power to do that), but I did tag the article for deletion. I did this not out because of a white/heterosexist/conformist/whatever mindset, but because the article did not assert the subject's notability. While being an anti-racism advocate is certainly laudable, it is unfortunately not enough to warrant an entry in an encyclopedia. Being a director of films that are freely distributable via the Web is, again, not enough to establish notability. Perhaps Mr Holden has other accomplishments that would merit an entry--or perhaps he will do something in the future that would establish his notability. But the article as it stood did not assert it. Keep in mind that notability is not the same as importance or relevance.

I am very sorry you have not had a positive experience with Wikipedia thus far. I hope you will reconsider your decision, as I'm sure you would make a valuable contributor to our site. Wikipedia just has a lot of rules that many new users don't know about, and I am sure it is very frustrating. Please let me know if you have any questions or if there is anything I can do to help. If you would like a copy of the article you submitted for Mr Holden, I can have an administrator retrieve it for you. I wish you the best of luck, whatever your endeavors be. Cheers. -- Merope Talk to me/Review me 22:12, 12 September 2006 (UTC)

[edit] Andrew J. Holden

A tag has been placed on Andrew J. Holden, requesting that it be speedily deleted from Wikipedia. This has been done because the article seems to be a biographical account about a person, group of people, or band, but it does not indicate how or why he/she/they is/are notable. If you can indicate why Andrew J. Holden is really notable, I advise you to edit the article promptly, and also put a note on Talk:Andrew J. Holden. Any admin should check for such edits before deleting the article. Feel free to leave a note on my talk page if you have any questions about this. Please read our criteria for speedy deletion, particularly item 7 under Articles. You might also want to read our general biography criteria. Please do not remove the speedy deletion tag yourself. To contest the tagging and request that admins should wait a while for you to assert his/her/their notability, please affix the template {{hangon}} to the page, and then immediately add such an assertion. It is also a very good idea to add citations from reliable sources to ensure that your article will be verifiable. -- Merope 15:01, 6 September 2006 (UTC)
