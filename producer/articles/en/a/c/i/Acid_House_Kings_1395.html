Acid House Kings is a Swedish indie pop band. It was founded in 1991 by Joakim Ödlund (also in the bands Poprace, Double Dan, and Starlet) and brothers Niklas (Red Sleeping Beauty} and Johan Angergård (Club 8, Poprace). In 2001, singer Julia Lannerheim officially joined the band. The group writes catchy melodies with light guitar background, typical of the twee pop style.
