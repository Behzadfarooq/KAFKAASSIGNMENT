Acid On Sunday or simply ~AoS~ is a Canadian rock group from Toronto. They have performed at many local clubs in Toronto, and have developed quite a following becoming a cult hit. They finished recording their EP entitled The Logic In Setting A Couch On Fire in early 2007.

[edit] History

They formed in mid 2006 under the direction of guitarist and current vocalist Matt Campbell and their then vocalist. They soon recruited another guitarist and bassist, although the bassist would soon be replaced by current bassist and keyboardist Woongbin Kang. They also recruited a drummer, although due to complications with the vocalist, he left the band, and current drummer Kale Trinier was supposed to replace him on a temporary basis. Matt, Woongbin and Kale ended up being the only members to stay through Acid On Sunday’s adversity and Matt ended up stepping up to the vocalist position, and has remained at that position ever since. They have played a number clubs around Toronto, and have gained a solid following. Their song Baby Blue Eyes has become a fan favorite, while the song Stick With It, Kid has become a cult hit, and is scheduled to appear in a compilation.
