[edit] A bit about me...

I am currently a part time undergraduate taking BA (hons) Combined Social Studies with Criminology at the University of Leeds with an particular interest in subjects such as Crime, Deviant behaviour (particularly issues surrounding Drugs, Youth culture, Self harm and Recreational drug use)and Social control along with more personal interests in Anarchism, Anti-Capitalism and Environmentalism. I have worked over the last 12 years or so in areas of Youth work, Housing and Student advice.

[edit] Other stuff I do...

My recreational interests are largely related to Techno (which I produce and occasionally play live), the UK Free party scene, along with reading fantasy and science fiction, growing things in pots and buckets and making and sampling a variety of alcoholic concoctions...

More about me and my music , for anyone interested is at: Suburban Acid Saturation Homepage
