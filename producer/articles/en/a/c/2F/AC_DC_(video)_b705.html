AC/DC, released only in Australia in 1989, is a video of the Australian hard Rock band AC/DC. Most of these videos now appear on the DVD Family Jewels.

[edit] Track Listing

  1. "High Voltage"
  2. "Jailbreak"
  3. "Let There Be Rock"
  4. "Riff Raff"
  5. "Dog Eat Dog"
  6. "Highway To Hell"
  7. "Shot Down In Flames"
  8. "Touch Too Much"
  9. "If You Want Blood (You've Got It)"
  • All tracks written by Malcolm Young, Angus Young and Bon Scott.

[edit] Personnel

  • Angus Young - Lead guitar
  • Malcolm Young - Rhythm guitar
  • Bon Scott - Vocals
  • Cliff Williams - Bass guitar
  • Phil Rudd - Drums
  • Mark Evans - Bass guitar on "Jailbreak" and "High Voltage"
