ACQ Kingdom Broadcasting Network is the broadcasting arm of a Philippine television evangelist, Pastor Apollo C. Quiboloy. Based in Davao City, it is available via terrestrial broadcast, cable, and satellite. Its single television channel maintains 24-hour service through regular repetition of a dozen self-produced Kingdom Programs in English and Tagalog. It also maintains a network of radio stations under the name "Sonshine Media Network International" of the Swara Sug Media Network.

Licensed in late 2003 by the Philippine government to operate thirty-eight broadcast channels, they now claim to operate thirty with plans to operate six more. On June 23, 2005, the Philippine National Telecommunications Commission ordered their Davao channel 43 shut down due to interference with another channel. But now it has been already solved and Sonshine TV 43 Davao is on Air 24/7.

[edit] ACQ-KBN programs

  • Batang Kaharian – A 30-minute religious education program.
  • Christmas from the Heart – A Christmas program produced for the benefit of the Children's Joy Foundation, whose goal is "To Feed, To Clothe, and To Send to School One Million Children".
  • Daily Light – Featuring the beautiful Covenant Mountain and Prayer Center, Daily Light helps viewers start their day right by leading them to a mode of prayer and intercession to the Almighty Father, through His Son, as well as praise and worship through the Kingdom songs and music that is played as the program goes on.
  • Give Us This Day - A two-hour live program of Pastor Apollo where progressive revelations of the Word of God are being tackled. GUTD features timely issues with invited guests, inspirational country music sung by the kingdom musicians and viewers' call-ins and messages sent through text.
  • Gospel of the Kingdom - A daily 1-hour and a half signature program of Pastor Apollo C. Quiboloy where he preaches the Word of God.
  • Highest Praise – A program that showcases spiritually-uplifting Kingdom music that truly touches and refreshes one's soul. Highest Praise also features originally-composed Kingdom songs, performed by the Kingdom musicians.
  • I Am the Way – A 30-minute program of discussion of the Kingdom Doctrines based on the preaching of Pastor Quiboloy.
  • Kingdom Up CLose - Gives an update of the latest happenings in the Kingdom, which includes the activities of Pastor Apollo C. Quiboloy.
  • Newsline Mindanao - Sonshine TV's news program for Visayas/Mindanao edition. (Ironically, although the newcast's main language is Bisaya, it is the network's flagship newscast like what TV Patrol World is for ABS-CBN and 24 Oras for GMA-7.)
  • Newsline Pilipinas - Sonshine TV's news program for Filipino edition.
  • Powerline – A 30-minute program where Pastor Apollo C. Quiboloy discusses spiritual matters and answers queries coming from covenant partners. Testimonies and victory reports from daily viewers are also featured.
  • Quiet Moments – An hour of instrumental praise and worship combined with breathtaking scenery. It is a great way for viewers to unwind and get a deep sense of peace that comes from the comforting verses of the Scriptures.
  • Sounds of Worship - The live broadcast of the Kingdom's Thanksgiving and Worship Presentations held in the Philippines and around the world, wherein Pastor Apollo preaches before thousands of people.
  • The Weekender World - Sonshine TV's 30-minute weekend news program.
  • Walk with the Son – An interstitial program that features testimonies, victory reports and life stories of Kingdom members, covenant partners and sympathizers who have been healed, blessed, and changed by God through the programs, concert crusades, and fellowships of Pastor Apollo C. Quiboloy.
  • Way Ahead - A 30-minute youth-oriented show that aims to present the Word of God as preached by Pastor Apollo C. Quiboloy, in way that young people can identify with. It teaches the youth to devote time for their spiritual growth.
