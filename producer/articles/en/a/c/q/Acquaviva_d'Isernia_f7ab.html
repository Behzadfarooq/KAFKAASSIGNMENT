Location of Acquaviva d'Isernia in Italy

Acquaviva d'Isernia is a comune (municipality) in the Province of Isernia in the Italian region Molise, located about 45 km west of Campobasso and about 10 km northwest of Isernia. As of 31 December 2004, it had a population of 474 and an area of 13.7 km².[1]

Acquaviva d'Isernia borders the following municipalities: Cerro al Volturno, Forlì del Sannio, Montenero Val Cocchiara, Rionero Sannitico.
