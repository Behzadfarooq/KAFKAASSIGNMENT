Acqua & Sapone-Caffè Mokambo (UCI Team Code: ASA) is a professional continental cycling team based in Italy and participates in UCI Europe Tour and when selected as a wildcard to UCI ProTour events. They are managed by Palmiro Masciarelli, assisted by directeur sportifs Lorenzo Di Lorenzo, Bruno Cenghialta and Franco Gini. Acqua & Sapone are led on the road by former Giro d'Italia winner Stefano Garzelli and former Liège-Bastogne-Liège and Paris-Nice winner Frank Vandenbroucke.

[edit] 2007 squad

[edit] Major results

UCI ProTour
  • Selected as a wild card team for the 2007 Giro d'Italia, a 2007 UCI ProTour event.
  • Selected as a wild card team for the 2007 Tirreno-Adriatico, a 2007 UCI ProTour event.
    • 1st, Stage 2, Tirreno-Adriatico : Alexander Arekeev
    • 1st, General classification (after Stage 2), Tirreno-Adriatico : Alexander Arekeev
    • 1st, Points classification (after Stage 2), Tirreno-Adriatico : Alexander Arekeev
    • 1st, Team classification (after Stage 2), Tirreno-Adriatico : Team
  • Selected as a wild card team for the 2007 Milan-Sanremo, a 2007 UCI ProTour event.
    • 7th place : Gabriele Balducci
UCI Europe Tour
  • 1st, Clásica de Almería : Giuseppe Muraglia
  • Selected as a team for the 2007 Milano-Torino, a 2006-2007 UCI Europe Tour event.
