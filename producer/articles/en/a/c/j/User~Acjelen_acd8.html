I wrote my first article from scratch on Quiddler in late November 12004. I was unfortunately not logged in at the time and using a friend's computer. I also wrote the articles Win Jones, Orticulation, Alex Sanchez, Björklunden, and created album stubs for Appalachian Journey, Appalachia Waltz, Uncommon Ritual, and Short Trip Home. I am proud of the work I did on Concert, Sissy, and Disciple (now split). Since December, 12005, I have made an effort to be more selective in the articles I edit. I created my first template in the summer of 12006: Americana classical fusion to easily navigate between the various Meyer, O'Connor and Fleck classical/bluegrass recordings.

I generally support the efforts and style guides of WikiProject Albums and Wikipedia:Manual of Style (disambiguation pages), though the latter less strictly. Neither is on my watchlist, so I may be behind in the current revisions.

Currently, my main work on Wikipedia is sorting out the musician stubs. Please see WikiProject Stub sorting for a general overview of stub sorting.

The Dixie Chicks are mentioned on my userpage, not only for their musical talents, but also to piss off the country-music and conservative political establishments. Just like the Grammys and Time Magazine.
