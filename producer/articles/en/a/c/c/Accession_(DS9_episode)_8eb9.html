"Accession" is an episode of Star Trek: Deep Space Nine, the sixteenth episode of the fourth season.

[edit] Plot summary

Spoiler warning: Plot and/or ending details follow.

A 300-year-old Bajoran ship comes through the Wormhole, and its passenger, Akorem, claims that he is the Emissary of the Prophets.

At first, Sisko is happy to give up his position as a religious leader for the Bajorans. Soon, disturbing things come to light. Akorem wishes to return Bajor to a system of caste-based discrimination. This would also result in the Federation denying Bajor for membership.

Sisko must deal with a vision of Kai Opaka, a murder of a 'lower caste' member and his second in command, Kira deciding she should resign her post to follow her caste.

Sisko challenges Akorem and they both enter the wormhole to see what the Prophets think. It turns out Akorem was a challenge for Sisko. Akorem is sent back to his own time, to live out his life with no memory of his three hundred year long trip.
