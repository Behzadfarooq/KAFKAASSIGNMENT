Accademia di Cervo

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

You have new messages (last change).

Founded in 1988 by Professor Arnulf von Arnim and his wife Elfe in the attractive Italian seaside resort town of Cervo, this prestigious music academy on the Ligurian Riviera has attracted the finest performers of classical and contemporary music from scores of countries, as diverse as Germany, Italy, Japan, Lithuania, France, USA, Denmark, Alaska, Argentina, Great Britain, Spain, Israel to name but a few.

Among the regular guests/lecturers are David Geringas, Ulf Hoelscher, Paul Rosenthal, Jean Sulem and Klesie Kelly. Individual master classes are complemented by evening concerts held from September 1st to 12th every year in a near-perfect acoustic venue, the ancient Oratorio di S. Caterina, before an enraptured audience. See also International Chamber Music Festival of Cervo.
