Accept is the debut album released by the Speed Metal band Accept. It was recorded in September and December 1978 at Delta-studio, Wilster. Drums on the album are played by Frank Friedrich, although he had been replaced by Stefan Kaufmann previous to the release of the record. All titles are written and composed by Dirkschneider, Baltes, Hoffmann, Fischer and Friedrich.
