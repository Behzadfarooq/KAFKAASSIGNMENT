Access Media Group also known as AMG (formerly known as Learning & Skills Television of Alberta Ltd.) is a privately held Canadian broadcasting and multimedia company based in Edmonton, Alberta, Canada specializing in learning-based media. It owns four television stations, a multimedia distribution company, and a school for continuing education and personal growth.

At the time of the company's founding in 1994, it was 60% owned by CHUM Limited. On February 15, 2005, CHUM Limited purchased the remainder of the company.
