Acceptable in the 80s

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

"Acceptable in the 80s"
Single by Calvin Harris
Released 2007
Chart positions
  • #10 (UK)

"Acceptable in the 80s" is a 2007 song by Calvin Harris. The video is about the style and culture of the 1980s. The video features many references of the time; the stereotypical large hairstyles, bright dayglo colours and bizarrely, scientists dissecting what appears to be an otter. The song has been used to advertise the third series of Channel 4's Paul O'Grady Show and to advertise celebratory programmes for the tenth birthday of Five.
