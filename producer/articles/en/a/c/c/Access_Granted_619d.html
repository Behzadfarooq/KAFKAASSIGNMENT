Access Granted

Access Granted is a television program on the Black Entertainment Television network (BET) which allows viewers to go behind the scenes of the making of music videos by various artists. Each episode features a different artist. During the last few minutes of the 30-minute show, the video is premiered.

[edit] Rock The Boat (2001)

The most watched episode was the making of Aaliyah's video for "Rock the Boat", in October 2001. This episode featured Aaliyah's last video and her final days before she died in a plane crash shortly after the video was completed. This feat has since been eclipsed by the July 26, 2006 episode which documents Janet Jackson's "Call On Me" video.
