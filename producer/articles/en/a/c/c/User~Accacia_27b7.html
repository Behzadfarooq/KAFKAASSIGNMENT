Accacia "Casey" Hammonds (born July 30, 1986 in Dothan, Alabama)is the daughter of Sonja Hammonds-Wells. She grew up on the eastside of Dothan.

[edit] Biography

The only daughter of Sonja Hammonds and the only grandaughter of Willie Mae Hammonds, Accacia grew up without a father until her mother married Bobby Wells in 1990. Accacia was happy to finally see what it was like to have father figure around. Since her brother's father was around, he already knew what it was like to have a dad. Accacia was a quiet giving girl. She was always trying to impress her grandmother. Her grandmother would tell her, "boys and books don't mix", and Accacia took those words to the heart. She grew up on one of the bad sides of Dothan. Drug dealers were a major part of the community. Accacia was determined to leave this area and become something. She felt as if she would never forget were she had come from but not want to struggle as an adult. Accacia graduated from Dothan High on May 29, 2004.


[edit] Air Force

On January 17, 2005, Accacia left home for United States Air Force.After basic training she started technical school training of Security Forces. After a month in training, she developed Pneumonia. This put her out of trainging for two months. The doctors soon diagnosed her with a serious case of Anemia. After two months of trying to get well, Accacia was able to go back to training on June 7, 2005. After one week in training her condition did not seem to get any better. The doctors gave Accacia two options, one was to stay there for six months to see if her body improves or leave and get a civilian doctor and have better chance at getting back in. She felt like option two would be a lot better.She went home on September 23, 2005.

[edit] Home Again

Accacia was so dissappointed to be home again. She looked at it as a mission tool. She was to stay focused and soon her determination would lead to her dream. Within two weeks of being home, she took on a security job with the government. Her priority was to go to school and go to work. She became interested in doing other such as: real estate, life and health insurance, and investing.

[edit] College

Accacia started her college career January 9, 2006, almost one year after leaving for the military. She had not given up on life yet. Although her dream was the Air Force, she always had a back up plan. She decided to major in Criminal Justice with a minor in Information Systems.
