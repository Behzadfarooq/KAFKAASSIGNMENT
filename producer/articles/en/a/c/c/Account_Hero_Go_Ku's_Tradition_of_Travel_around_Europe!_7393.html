Account Hero Go Ku's Tradition of Travel around Europe! (西遊記ヒーローGo空伝!, Saiyūkihīrō Go Kūden!?) is comics-full-of-gags work of Noriyuki Konishi. A rough story says that various supernatural creature blocks that Warehouse's party goes to India.

[edit] Main characters

  • Goku (悟空, Gokū?)/Sun Wukong
The hero of this work.
  • Wujing (悟浄, Gojō?)/Sha Wujing
  • Vinaya (八戒, Hakkai?)/Zhu Bajie
Goku's and others mediator.
The supernatural creature who is aiming at the meat of Warehouses.
  • Gourd the Devil (ひょうたん魔王, Hyōtanmaō?)
  • Thief (盗賊, Tōzoku?)
  • Horse the Devil (馬魔王, Bamaō?)
The supernatural creature of the cactus who is aiming at the meat of Warehouses and who Warehouse party found in the wilderness. The Naithpick people who are his friends notice Warehouses eaten by him, and are enraged at it.
  • Ikemen Mirror (イケメン鏡, Ikemenkyō?)
One of the supernatural creatures chosen as the excellent work in the supernatural creature contest constantly held in the comic magazine. Incidentally his motto is "winning by carrying out, without fighting."
The wild boar of the same size as Cow the Devil.

[edit] The terms which appeared in the work

  • Cloata (金斗雲, kintoun?)
  • Cloata No. 3 (金斗雲3号, kintoun 3(san) gō?)
This is the thing which Warehouses inserted in and put in the head of Goku before leaving with Goku.
