Acceleration is the first full-length album by Norwegian avantgarde progressive metal band Age Of Silence. It was released on September 14, 2004.

  1. "Auditorium Of Modern Movements" – 3:36
  2. "Acceleration" – 4:31
  3. "The Concept Of Haste" – 4:09

  5. "The Green Office And The Dark Desk Drawer" – 4:18
  6. "The Flow At – 9:30 Am" – 6:25
  7. "Of Concrete And Glass" – 3:14
  8. "90° Angles" – 7:19
  9. "I No Longer Know If I Am Mad" – 2:28
  10. "Synthetic, Fabricated, Calculated" – 4:11

[edit] Recording Line-Up

  • Kobbergaard (K. Haugen) (ex-Winds) - Guitars, Vocals on "I No Longer Know If I Am Mad"
  • Hellhammer (Arcturus, ex-The Kovenant, Mayhem, Winds) - Drums
  • Lars Are "Lazare" Nedland (Solefald, Borknagar, Ásmegin, ex-Carpathian Forest) - Vocals, Lyricist
  • Eikind (ex-Khold, Tulus, Winds) - Bass, backing vocals
  • Andy Winter (Winds) - Piano, keyboards
  • Extant - Guitars
