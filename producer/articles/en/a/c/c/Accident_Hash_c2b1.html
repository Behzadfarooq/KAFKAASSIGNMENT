Accident Hash

Host(s) C.C. Chapman
Website http://www.accidenthash.com
Update Schedule 2-3 times per week
Launch Date January 10, 2005
Genre Podsafe Music, Talk

Accident Hash is a popular award-winning music podcast hosted and produced by C.C. Chapman. It is part of the PodShow group of music podcasts and has been featured on Sirius Satellite Radio's channel 102, Sirius Stars.

Hash serves as a showcase for podsafe music from various genres from around the world. Occasional interviews with indie artists have also been included, such as Danko Jones, Matthew Ebel, and Kristia Di Gregorio.

With its debut on January 10, 2005 - and with over 200 episodes - Hash is one of the longest-running music podcasts in the world. During its run, the show has featured music from over 500 different artists.

Chapman is known for his enthusiastic commentary - such as his popular catch phrase "It's all good!" - about the music, artists, and any other topics he finds noteworthy. His opening greeting of "Hey Home Fries!" has inspired many fans of the show to call themselves "home fries."

[edit] Behind the Scenes

Many of the song selections come from Podshow's Podsafe Music Network. However, Chapman also plays submissions sent directly from artists and record labels. One example of this is Rykodisc, who allowed Chapman to play tracks by the major band Waltham.

The theme song is performed by Madsumo.
