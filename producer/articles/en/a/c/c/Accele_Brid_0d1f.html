Accele Brid is a video game released by Tomy in 1993 for the SNES. You play a robot racing through a pipe shooting everything that moves. The robot can shoot from both arms, jump, kick, and punch. There are also power-ups and bonuses that can be collected to upgrade the robot's weapons and armor.
