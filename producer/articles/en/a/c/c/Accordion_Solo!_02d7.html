Accordion Solo! is a 2005 album by Ten in the Swear Jar. It is the band's third release under their name, a complete discography derived from of all their previous efforts. It is described by Asian Man Records as, "A collection of live recordings, "field recordings" and studio tracks with complex lyrics, beautiful melodies, and diverse instrumentation." The live tracks are acknowledged to be of imperfect quality.
