Acciona, SA, (IBEX-35:ANA) is a Spanish conglomerate group dedicated to civil engineering, construction and infrastructures.

Was founded in 1997 as fusion of Entrecanales y Tavora and Cubiertas y MZOV. The company's headquarters is in Alcobendas, Community of Madrid, Spain. The company's U.S. operations are headquartered in Chicago.
