Accident is the sixth and final episode of the first series of British sitcom Bottom. It was first broadcast on Monday October 28, 1991.

[edit] Synopsis

Richie has an accident on his rather depressing birthday.

[edit] Plot

Spoiler warning: Plot and/or ending details follow.

The episode begins with Eddie sitting down at the breakfast table. Richie then enters the room in his pyjamas singing "Happy Birthday to me" much to Eddie's dismay. Richie then begins his annual birthday "routine" of reading what are obviously forged cards, the same ones he claims to have received last year. After asking Eddie the whereabouts of his birthday gift Eddie - after annoying Richie with a couple of gag-gifts (Richard's comb that he lost last week and the TV remote) - hands him a piece of paper, with the words "Madame Swish 3:30" written on it, Richie -believing it to be a prostitute - is initially excited at the prospect of finally getting sex and heaps praise on Eddie,however it soon comes to light that Madame Swish is actually a horse running in the 3:30 at Kempton. Richie gives Eddie £20 to put on the horse, with odds of 10 to 1.

Eddie arrives back after attending the bookies for the big race, and successfully fools Richie into thinking that the £20 bet Richie put on the race has left him with £2 in winnings. As Eddie tosses over the winnings however Richie falls off the ladder he is standing on and breaks his leg. After trying to overcome the problem with Eddie's assistance (which actually leaves Richie worse off) Richie is forced to go to hospital.

The pair return from the hospital, with Richie bandaged up and in a wheelchair, just in time for 7pm when Richie is expecting his party guests. As the clock strikes 7 there is a knock at the door - however only Eddie's "real" friends Dave Hedgehog and Spudgun await. Richie realises he has no friends and invites the pair to stay. Richie insits the group play "Sardines" - a game in which one person hides and the rest have to find him, and when they find him they hide with him. However, being in a wheelchair, Richie needs the other three to hide him in the upstairs cupboard, and they must do so with their eyes closed. When they have hidden Richie and counted to ten, Eddie, Spudgun and Dave spent prepare to spend the night in front of the television drinking the alcohol Eddie has bought with the winnings he has stolen off of Richie.

Richie is ecstatic after being in the upstairs cupboard for over 5 hours, believing it to be a world record. Richie soon after gets bored of waiting to be found and attempts to rejoin them downstairs, however due to his immobililty he struggles getting down the stairs and ends up breaking his other leg much to the others amusement. A drunken Spudgun vomits on the injured Richie.

Later on Eddie is heading up a party in the flat while Richie is being treated for his mishaps. Eddie is just in the middle of having the crowd in stitches whilst doing impersonations of Richie - with a mop end on his head - when Richie enters the room. After Eddie convinces him that all of his friends have turned up for his party after all Richie cheers up and attempts to boss over the activities. After attempting however to take one of the guests blouses off, he is stopped by the womans boyfriend who then precedes to get everyone together to give Richie the bumps. As they all throw him into the air Richie's casts snap leaving him screaming in pain.

[edit] Notes

  • This episode sees the first appearance of Eddie's "real friends" Spudgun (Steve O'Donnell) and Dave Hedgehog (Christopher Ryan). O'Donnell had worked on several of the Comic Strip Presents... productions with Mayall and Edmondson, while Ryan had famously been one of the Young Ones, Mike, along with Mayall and Edmondson who played Rick and Vyvyan respectively. Spudgun and Hedgehog would return in several other episodes.
