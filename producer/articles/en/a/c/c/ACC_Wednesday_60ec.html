ACC Wednesday appears every Wednesday with the biggest college basketball games in the Atlantic Coast Conference in primetime during the regular season on ESPN. The official name is ACC Wednesday presented by Staples. ACC Wednesday is similar to Throwdown Thursday, Super Tuesday and Big Monday, but unlike the others, ACC Wednesday only has one game due to an NBA game as part of NBA Wednesday immediately following the game, although on occasion the NBA Wednesday game will be preempted as there will be an ACC Wednesday doubleheader. The ACC game of the week starts at 7 p.m. ET with play-by-play commentator Mike Patrick and analyst Dick Vitale announcing the game. On occasion Len Elmore serves as game analyst in place of Vitale.

The studio host is usually either Rece Davis or Stan Verrett along with analysts Hubert Davis and/or Steve Lavin. They deliver the half-time report post game report and live in-game updates. They also appear on College GameNight later that night as part of the Trifecta. Both the game and in studio reports are presented in high definition on ESPN HD.

An oddity of ACC Wednesday is that there generally is a doubleheader on ESPN2 airing concurrently, neither of which feature an ACC team specifically.

In the Southern United States and parts of New England, ACC Wednesday is often blacked out due to other ACC coverage provided on local television stations by Lincoln Financial Sports.
