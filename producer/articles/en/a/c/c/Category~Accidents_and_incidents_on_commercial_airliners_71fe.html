Category:Accidents and incidents on commercial airliners

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

 Disasters Portal

This category is for fatal and non-fatal incidents, accidents, and acts of terrorism which have affected airliners, including scheduled passenger and cargo flights.

This list is not complete. For more exhaustive lists, see the Aircraft Crash Record Office or the Air Safety Network.
