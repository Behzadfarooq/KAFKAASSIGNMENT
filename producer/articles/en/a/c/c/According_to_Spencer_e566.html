According To Spencer, 2001

[edit] Synopsis

According to Spencer is a 2001 movie starring Jesse Bradford, Mia Kirshner, David Krumholtz, Adam Goldberg, and Brad Rowe. The film is centered on Spencer (Bradford), who moves to Los Angeles and (literally) runs into the girl he's been in love with since he was a child (Kirshner). Spencer is plagued by many obstacles in his new life: winning the girl of his dreams, finding success in his new job, and dealing with his two ambiguously-oriented roommates (Krumholtz and Goldberg), who are obsessed with making a pornographic film.

According to Spencer is rated R for language and sexual content by the Motion Picture Association of America.
