[edit] Longevity claims

Acctorp, unless Wikipedia wants to list the 940+ verified 110+ cases, I do strongly suggest not adding longevity claims below age 113. They are far too numerous. Note the 'oldest people' lists generally have a top-10 list, and the list generally goes down to 112 1/2 at the lowest, more often age 113. If someone claims to be 110 and doesn't have the documents, that's not yet encyclopedic-worthy.→ R Young {yakłtalk} 22:43, 31 May 2006 (UTC)
