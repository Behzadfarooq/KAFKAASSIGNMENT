[edit] Section stubs

If you want to mark a section as a stub you can use the tag {{sectstub}}. If you want to request that a section is expanded (i.e. it has all the main information but needs to be expanded to be comprehensive) you can use the template {{expandsect}} and {{expand}} can be used on an article as a whole.

Happy editing. Ksbrown 12:15, 18 November 2006 (UTC)

[edit] WikiProject Western Australia

Hi, I noted your name at WA participants at WikiProject Australia. Please feel free to join the WikiProject Western Australia! As you might know, the WP:WA is a new project whose aims are still being developed.

  • Every one has something to contribute and cooperation between members and other projects can produce good and featured articles.
  • The opportunity for expanding and linking of articles will also be facilitated, contributing to the success of this encyclopedia.

The scope of the project includes articles either extending beyond Wikipedia:WikiProject Perth or have a West Australian element, or absence, in Wikipedia:WikiProject Australia.
