AC Oulu is a Finnish football club, based in Oulu. The club's manager is Miika Juntunen, and it plays its home matches at Castrén. AC Oulu was founded in 2002 to bring top level football back to Oulu (FC Oulu played in Veikkausliiga in the early 90's as the most recent Veikkausliiga team from Oulu). After four years, AC Oulu will be playing in Veikkausliiga in 2007.
