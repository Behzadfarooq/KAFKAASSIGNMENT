Achanuragantha Veedu is a 2006 Malayalam film directed by Lal Jose and produced by Reji Kuthazhath. Set in the 1970s, it tells the tale of Prabhakaran, a student who falls in love with a Christian girl named Lillikutty, and changes his religion in order to win her parents' approval; however, his wife passes away, and he must face hardships alone when his youngest daughter goes missing.[1] Critics described the film as Salim Kumar's "graduation from a comedian to a mature character.[2][3]

[edit] Cast

  • Saleem Kumar as Prabhakaran
  • Samvrutha Sunil as Prabhakaran's daughter
  • Suja Karthika as Prabhakaran's daughter
  • Bhanu (Muktha) as Lisamma, Prabhakaran's youngest daughter[1][4]
