Achkhoy-Martan (Russian: Ачхо́й-Марта́н) is a village in the Chechen Republic, Russia. It is the administrative center of Achkhoy-Martanovsky District. Population: 16,742 (2002 Census); 14,680 (1989 Census). Geographical location: 43°11′N 45°17′E[citation needed].
