Acheron Island is one of the islands South of the Great Palm Island group. The island is half way between Magnetic Island and Palm Island

The island lies near the border of Halifax Bay and Coral Sea, East of Rollingstone and North of Townsville about 40km.

The island is just North of Rattlesnake Island, where the RAAF practices bombing raids.
