Achaios Saravali or Ahaios Saravali (Greek: Γ.Π.Σ. Αχαιός Σαραβαλίου G.P.S Achaios Saravali) is an athletic club in Saravali in southeastern suburban Patras in the Achaia prefecture. The team was first created in 1969. Its colours are blue and white and has a sandy field. The team plays with the EPS Achaias and the EPO number 1581. It currently plays in the EPS Achaia first division.

From Achaia featured a footballer (soccer player) named Dimitris Argyropoulos who played in Panachaiki also plaked in Aalst in Belgium, Patraikos, Panelefsiniakos, Ethnikos and in Panachaiki in the first division.

In the basketball section, the team began and the brother of Dimitri, Nikos who played in the Apollon Patras in the Premier Division and today plays in Olympiacos in Piraeus.

Later, the basketball team now plays in the second division.
