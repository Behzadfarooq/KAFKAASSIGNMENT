Achille et Polyxène

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Achille et Polyxène (Achilles and Polyxena) is an opera with a libretto by Philippe Quinault. The overture and first act have music by Jean-Baptiste Lully, who died before he could complete the score. The remaining acts are the work of his pupil Pascal Collasse. The opera was first performed in 1687.
