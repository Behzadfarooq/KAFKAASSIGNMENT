Achtung! Auto-Diebe! (English language:Warning! Auto thieves!) is a 1930 German crime film directed by Harry Piel and written by Hans Rameau . The film starred Harry Piel and Leopold von Ledebur.

[edit] Cast

  • Harry Piel .... Harry Palen, Auto-Verkäufer
  • Charly Berger

  • Herbert Paulmüller
  • Lydia Potechina
  • Oswald Scheffel
  • Raimondo Van Riel .... Robert Radek
  • Leopold von Ledebur .... Eggert, Kriminalkommissär


[edit] Release

The film premiered on 5 June 1930 in Berlin.
