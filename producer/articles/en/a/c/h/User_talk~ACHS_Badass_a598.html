[edit] License tagging for Image:Cheetahccc.jpg

Thanks for uploading Image:Cheetahccc.jpg. Wikipedia gets hundreds of images uploaded every day, and in order to verify that the images can be legally used on Wikipedia, the source and copyright status must be indicated. Images need to have an image tag applied to the image description page indicating the copyright status of the image. This uniform and easy-to-understand method of indicating the license status allows potential re-users of the images to know what they are allowed to do with the images.

For more information on using images, see the following pages:

  • Wikipedia:Image use policy
  • Wikipedia:Image copyright tags

This is an automated notice by OrphanBot. If you need help on selecting a tag to use, or in adding the tag to the image description, feel free to post a message at Wikipedia:Media copyright questions. 17:07, 4 May 2006 (UTC)

[edit] Wikipedia is not a forum

Recently I came across your user page, and found that you are sharing it as a forum for people at your high school. It is against the Wikipedia rules to host a forum (see What Wikipedia is not) on the site. Also, in my opinion, the classmates are posting some pretty tasteless, sexual text. Please discontinue this forum-like activity. Additionally, Many people will think that Wikipedia's discussion pages are forums. While talk pages are a type of forum, they are only supposed to be referring to stuff on Wikipedia. If you have any further questions, please feel free to leave me a message on my talk page. Cheers, Wikipeedio 21:22, 4 May 2006 (UTC)
