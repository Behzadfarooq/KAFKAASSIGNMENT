Achinsk

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Achinsk - Train station on trans-Siberian railway

Achinsk (Russian: А́чинск) is a city in Krasnoyarsk Krai, Russia, located at the intersection of the Chulym River and Trans-Siberian Railway, at 56°15′N, 90°30′E Population: 118,744 (2002); 85,000 (1969); 32,000 (1939). Achinsk was founded in 1682; town status was granted in 1782. It is served by Achinsk Airport.
