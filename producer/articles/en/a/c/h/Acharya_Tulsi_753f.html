Acharya Tulsi

Acharya Tulsi (1913-June 23, 1997) was a Jainist Acharya (an ascetic). He was the author of 100+ books, and the founder of the Anuvrata and the Vishva Bharati Institute.

[edit] Quotes

  • "A non-violent [person should not] discriminate between rich and poor. Non-violence is the best guarantee of humanity's survival."
