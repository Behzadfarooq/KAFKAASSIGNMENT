ACH is a three-letter acronym that may refer to:

  • Acetylcholine (ACh), a neurotransmitter
  • Achondroplasia, a type of genetic disorder that is a common cause of dwarfism
  • Achnashellach railway station, in the United Kingdom, from the National Railway code
  • Acholi language (ach), as the ISO 639 code for the language
  • Advanced Combat Helmet
  • AC Horsens, a Danish football club
  • ACH Food Companies (AC HUMKO from 1995 to 2000), an American subsidiary of Associated British Foods, previously part of Kraft Foods from 1952 to 1995 [1]
  • Altenrhein Airport, in Switzerland, from its IATA airport code
  • American College of Heraldry, an heraldic organization in the United States
  • Analysis of Competing Hypotheses
  • Angeles Crest Highway
  • Automated Clearing House, a nationwide electronic banking network
  • Arkansas Children's Hospital in Little Rock, Arkansas

Ach may refer to the following rivers in Germany and Austria:

  • Bregenzer Ach, a tributary of Lake Constance
  • Dornbirner Ach, a tributary of Lake Constance
  • Friedberger Ach, a tributary of the Lech
