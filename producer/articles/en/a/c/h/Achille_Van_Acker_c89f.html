Achille Van Acker

Achille Van Acker (April 8, 1898—July 10, 1975) was Prime Minister of Belgium in three different cabinets from 1945 to 1958, for a total period of 7 years.

Terms:

  • February 12, 1945—March 13, 1946
  • March 31, 1946—August 3, 1946
  • April 23, 1954—June 26, 1958

Van Acker is known as the father of Belgian social security.
