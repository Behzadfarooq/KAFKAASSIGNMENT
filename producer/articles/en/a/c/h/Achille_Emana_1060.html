Achille Emana (born 5 June 1982) is a Cameroonian football defender who, as of 2006 was playing for Toulouse FC.

He was a member of the Cameroonian 2006 African Nations Cup team, who exited in the quarter-finals.

[edit] Clubs

  • 1999-2000 : Valencia CF B
  • 2000-present : Toulouse FC
