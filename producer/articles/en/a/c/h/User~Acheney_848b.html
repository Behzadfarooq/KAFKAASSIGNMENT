Hey everyone. I stumbled upon Wikipedia not too long ago and have been an "addict" ever since. It's extremely habit forming!!! Therefore, it only seemed natural that I would want to be a part of such an ambitious project that is this one. I just finished my undergraduate degree in Business Administration at the University of Florida and soon I will find myself in the heart of Castile in Spain in Salamanca pursuing my post-graduate MBA. I am 100% fluent in Spanish and English, but my writing skills are ten times stronger in English then in Spanish. However, I do possess more then an intermediate level in written Spanish and would not be adverse to writing in Spanish. I have yet to truly define my Wikipedia interest in terms of writing articles, but I consider myself to be an avid reader and believe I can lend a hand on articles concerning novels/fiction. Moreover, I have a huge interest in Spain and in pop culture; also being able to help in these fields. I am a newbie and am still learning the ropes!!!
