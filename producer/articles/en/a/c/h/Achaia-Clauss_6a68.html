Achaia-Clauss

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Achaia Clauss is a venerable Greek winery located in Patras, Peloponnese. It was founded in 1861 by the Bavarian Gustav Clauss. It is most famous for its Mavrodaphne.
