ACS Statistician of the Year

The Statistician of the Year was instituted in the year 1987 by the Association of Cricket Statisticians and Historians and is sponsored by Hamlyn.

The winners of the award are :

Year Name Major contributions and areas of work
1987 George Russ
1988 Philip Bailey
1989 Andrew Hignell Glamorgan cricket
1990 Robert Brooke
1991 Richard Lockwood
1992 Les Hatton Sunday league, Worcestershire cricket and County second XI cricket
1993 Kit Bartlett
1994 Philip Bailey, Philip Thorn and Peter Wynne-Thomas Second edition of Who's Who of Cricketers
1995 Howard Milton
1996 Bill Frindall
1997 Vic Isaacs
1998 Ray Webster For his major books on Australian cricket
1999 Joe Webber For his book on W.G. Grace
2000 Brian Croudy
2001 Don Ambrose
2002 no award
2003 Geoff Wilde
2004 Abid Ali Kazi Pakistani first class cricket
2005 Tim McCann Early Sussex cricket
2006 Tony Webb Minor Counties Cricket
2007 Krish Reddy SACBOC/SACB cricket played by non-white South Africans
