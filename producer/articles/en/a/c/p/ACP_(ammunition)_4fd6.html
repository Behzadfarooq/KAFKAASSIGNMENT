ACP (ammunition)

ACP (Automatic Colt Pistol) is the acronym chosen by Colt to denote its involvement in the design and/or marketing of several standard models of pistol cartridge.

[edit] ACP cartridges

  • .25 ACP
  • .32 ACP
  • .380 ACP
  • .38 ACP (Obsolete)
  • .45 ACP
