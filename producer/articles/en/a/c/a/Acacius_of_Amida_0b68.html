Acacius of Amida

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Saint Acacius
Born unknown
Died , Mesopotamia
Venerated in Roman Catholicism
Feast
Saints Portal

Saint Acacius was bishop of Amida (Diarbekir), Mesopotamia. He sold the sacred vessels of his church to aid victims of the Persian persecution. His actions so impressed King Bahram V that he is reported to have ordered an end to the persecution of the Christians.
