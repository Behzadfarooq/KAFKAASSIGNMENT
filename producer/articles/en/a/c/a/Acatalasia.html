Acatalasia (or Takahara's disease) is a peroxisomal disorder caused by a catalase deficiency.

In 1948, Dr.Shigeo Takahara (1908-1994), a Japanese Otolaryngologist reported the new disease. He had examined the patient of the Mouth ulcer with Necrosis. He had spread hydrogen peroxide on the diseased part, but oxygen was not generated.
