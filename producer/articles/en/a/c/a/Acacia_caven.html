Acacia caven (Roman cassie; syn. Acacia adenopa Hook. & Arn., Acacia cavenia (Molina) Hook. & Arn., Acacia farnesiana var. cavenia (Hook. & Arn.) O.Kuntze, Mimosa caven Molina, Vachelia farnesiana (Molina) Speg., Mimosa cavenia Molina) is an ornamental tree in the Fabaceae family, which is native from Argentina, Brasil, Chile, Paraguay, and Uruguay.
