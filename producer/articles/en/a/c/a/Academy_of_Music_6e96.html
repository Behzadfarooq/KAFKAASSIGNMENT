Academy of Music is College or university school of music.

It may refer to:

  • Academy of Music in Budapest, a major center for Hungarian musical innovation
  • Academy of Music (Jerusalem), a musice school in Israel
  • Academy of Music (Manhattan), former theatre and opera house in Manhattan, New York City
  • Academy of Music (Philadelphia), an opera house in Pennsylvania
  • Academy of Ancient Music, an orchestra based in Cambridge
  • Academy of St. Martin in the Fields
  • Baku Academy of Music, a music school in Baku, Azerbaijan
  • Brooklyn Academy of Music, still a concert hall in New York and now a cultural center
  • London Academy of Music and Dramatic Art, University of Kent
  • Montreal Academy of Music, a theater in Montreal, demolished in 1910
  • Royal Swedish Academy of Music, an institution for the promotion of music
  • Royal Academy of Music, a music school in London, England
  • Royal Irish Academy of Music, a college within Dublin City University
  • Royal Scottish Academy of Music and Drama, a conservatory in Glasgow
  • Sarajevo Music Academy, Princeton, New Jersey
