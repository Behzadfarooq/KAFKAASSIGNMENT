Academic WorldQuest (also referred to as WorldQuest) is an annual team-based international affairs, geography, history and culture competiton sponsored by the World Affairs Councils of America. WorldQuest, held every year since 2003, has two different levels open to competitors - high school and adult.

There are ten categories that are changed each year; at the competition, ten questions from each category are asked.

[edit] History

WorldQuest was created in 1995 by Jennifer Watson Roberts of the World Affairs Council of Charlotte. The national competition began in 2003.

Winners: 2006: Thomas Jefferson High School for Science and Technology (World Affairs Council of Washington, DC)

[edit] Regional competitions

In order to compete at the national competition, held each March in Washington, DC, teams must first win at their regional council level (usually held in January or February).
