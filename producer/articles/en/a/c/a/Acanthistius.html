Acanthistius is a genus of groupers, large marine fish.

[edit] Species

  • Brazilian sea bass, Acanthistius brasilianus (Cuvier, 1828)
  • Yellowbanded perch, Acanthistius cinctus (Günther, 1859)
  • Acanthistius fuscus Regan, 1913
  • Acanthistius ocellatus (Günther, 1859)
  • Acanthistius pardalotus Hutchins, 1981
  • Acanthistius patachonicus (Jenyns, 1840)
  • Acanthistius paxtoni Hutchins & Kuiter, 1982
  • Acanthistius pictus (Tschudi, 1846)
  • Koester, Acanthistius sebastoides (Castelnau, 1861)
  • Western wirrah, Acanthistius serratus (Cuvier, 1828)
  • Duskytail grouper, Epinephelus bleekeri (Vaillant, 1878)
  • Leopard coralgrouper, Plectropomus leopardus (Cuvier, 1828)
