Acadian Ambulance

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Acadian Ambulance is an ambulance service that covers most of the state of Louisiana. It was founded in 1971. The main office is based in Lafayette, Louisiana. Acadian Ambulance has a fleet of more than 200 regular ambulances, seven medical transport helicopters which are called Air Med, and four fixed-wing airplanes. Air Med provides fast and sometimes lifesaving transport to a needed medical facility. In addition to emergency medical services, Acadian Ambulance provides an Executive Aircraft Charter Service, either for business or pleasure.

One of seven Acadian Ambulance Air Med medically equipped helicopters.

The company is the largest private rural ambulance service in the United States, with over 2400 employees and more than 200 ground ambulances

Richard Zuschlag is the company's CEO and Chairman of the Board.

The Acadian Ambulance service currently operates a system where they have a PodCast which is available free from iTunes, which is viewable on a PC, MAC or on a iPod. They Operate from a website called yourstudioa.com.
