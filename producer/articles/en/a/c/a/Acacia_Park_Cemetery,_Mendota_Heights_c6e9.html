Acacia Park Cemetery

Acacia Park Cemetery is located in Mendota Heights, Minnesota at 2151 Pilot Knob Road. It consists of 79 acres of land overlooking the confluence of the Minnesota and Mississippi Rivers, with 163 deceased buried here. In addition, Dakota Indians had buried their dead on the same site prior to acquisition by European settlers. [1]
