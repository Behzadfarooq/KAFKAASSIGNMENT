Acartophthalmidae is a family of flies, found in the order Diptera. Only four species are included, all in the Genus Acartophthalmus Czerny. All are Holarctic.

[edit] Biology

Almost unknown. Adults have been found mostly in forests. Larvae have been reared from dead wood and decaying organic material. See also External Links (below).

[edit] Other

There is an uncertain fossil species.
