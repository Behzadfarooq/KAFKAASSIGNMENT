Academy Park High School is a four-year public high school in Sharon Hill, Pennsylvania. Its mascot is the Knight. It is currently the only high school in the Southeast Delco School District.

The school got some national attention when then Vice President hopeful Dick Cheney visited the school in the fall of 2000.

Academy Park is well known for its highly reputed theater program. The students in this program, who hail from not only APHS but also other schools in Southeast Delco, recently performed "Beauty and the Beast". The school's Hi-Q team also ranked 9th/21 Teams in the first round of the 2005-2006 Hi-Q competition. Their field hockey team also has done very well in the past.

However, Academy Park has also drawn criticism due to having low PSSA scores and there has been much criticism of the school board's running of the district. Despite the criticism and rumors; Academy Park students and staff have been able to blow past the crude remarks to make for a welcoming school to enjoy. Under the new rule of Principal Eric Turman, changes are in accord for the school.

On June 14th, 2006, at the 2006 graduation, two students, twin brothers Steven Theveny and David Theveny, stood up during superintendent Trudie Bennett's speech and turned around in order to protest against a rumor for alleged "Senior Afterparty" funds she had taken from the class for her personal benefits. The two students were taken out of the graduation lineup when they lined up to walk onstage, and not given their diplomas at the ceremony. This followed rumors of a protest that were circulated on the school's Myspace board.
