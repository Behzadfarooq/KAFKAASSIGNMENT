[edit] Major project

I was involved in developing articles about and related to Colleges and Universities as a part of Wikipedia:WikiProject Universities. I was particularly active in the development of the Purdue University article.

The project, started on 10 August 2003, the project is attempting to formulate a standard template for College and University articles on Wikipedia. The ultimate goal is to bring all articles on the subject into compliance with a single standard in order to give Wikipedia a more professional look, make cross-referencing easier, expand content of edu-stub articles, and ultimately make Wikipedia a more useful and powerful resource.

[edit] WikiPolitiks

Since I've been experimenting on Wikipedia, I've become somewhat familiar with some of the institutional workings of this place and how this wiki concept tends to play out. One of the biggest (and most confusing and irritating) issues I have come across are those regarding intellectual property, licensing, and other legal nonsense.

I prefer to leave these issues to the lawyers, and I don't particularly care how my contributions are used, so long as they remain free and are never sold for profit (I thought that was the whole idea behind Wikipedia). I am perfectly happy with any free licensing scheme, be it the GFDL or something equally or more free. I see my contributions as charitable donations to Wikipedia, its governing instituiton, the Wikimedia Foundation, and ultimately to mankind.
