Academy Award for Documentary Short Subject

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

This is a list of films by year that have received an Oscar together with the other nominations for best documentary short subject. Following the Academy's practice, the year listed for each film is the year of release: the awards are announced and distributed in March of the following year.

[edit] "Inappropriate" categorizations for Benjy (1951) and Neighbours (1952)

A press release issued by AMPAS states that "Documentary Short Subject winners "Benjy" (1951) and "Neighbours" (1952) are among a group of films that not only competed, but won Academy Awards in what were clearly inappropriate categories." [1]
