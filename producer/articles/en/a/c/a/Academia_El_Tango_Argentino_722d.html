Academia El Tango Argentino (English language:The Argentine Tango Academy) is a 1942 Argentine musical romantic drama film directed and written by Julio Irigoyen and written by Julio Porter. Starring Warly Ceriani and Domingo Conte .

The film is based around tango dancing, an integral part of Argentine culture.
