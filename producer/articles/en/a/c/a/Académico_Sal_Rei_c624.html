Académico Sal Rei (Capeverdean Crioulo, ALUPEC or ALUPEK: Akadémiku Sal Rei or Akadémiku du Sal) is a football (soccer) club that had played in the Premier division and plays in the Boa Vista Island South Zone League in Cape Verde. It is based in the city of Sal Rei in the island of Boa Vista.
