Location of Acadia in Calgary

Acadia is a neighbourhood in the southeast quadrant of Calgary, Alberta. While Acadia proper lies between Heritage Drive and Southland Drive. The area is bounded on the west by MacLeod Trail and on the east by the Bow River.

Lord Beaverbrook High School

The neighbourhood's main street is Fairmount Drive, and many of the area's businesses cluster along the street. The area also contains Lord Beaverbrook High School, the city's largest.
