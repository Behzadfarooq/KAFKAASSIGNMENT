Acaydia School of Aesthetics LLC.

Acaydia is the name of a private aesthetics school located in Provo Utah. The school is located on the main level of the Wells Fargo building on 100 North and University Avenue and is currently in cadidacy status for acreditation by NACCAS.
The product lines carried at Acaydia include Bath Bloomers and Sonya Dakar.
Acaydia school of Aesthetics is a member of the Provo Orem Chamber of Commerce.
