Académie de la Grande Chaumière

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Académie de la Grande Chaumière (academy of the large thatched cottage) is an art school located at 14 rue de la Grande Chaumière in Paris, France. It offers full courses, but also a unique "one-sketch admission" service, for which one pays to draw nude models. *1.

At the turn of the 20th century, it was one of the most famous art schools in Paris.
