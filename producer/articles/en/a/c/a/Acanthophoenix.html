Acanthophoenix is a genus of plants in the Arecaceae family and includes the endangered Acanthophoenix rubra, prized for its edible palm hearts in the Indian Ocean islands.

[edit] Selected species

  • Acanthophoenix grandis Bosschere - syn. Acanthophoenix rubra ? - See World Palm checklist
  • Acanthophoenix rubra (Bory) H.Wendl. - syn. A. crinita H.Wendl.
