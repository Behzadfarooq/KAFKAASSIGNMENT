Acacia Park Cemetery, Chicago

Acacia Park Cemetery is located in Chicago, Illinois at 7800 West Irving Park Road.

[edit] Notable burials

  • Johannes Anderson (1887-1950), World War I Congressional Medal of Honor recipient
  • Ral Donner (1943-1984), singer
  • Red Faber (1888-1976), baseball player
  • Audre Knecht (1930-1989), actress
  • Osborne Theomun Olsen, china decorator
  • Irna Phillips (1901-1973), television producer
  • Alvah Curtis Roebuck (1864-1948), founder of Sears
  • Mike Royko (1932-1997), columnist
