The Academy of Saint Joseph, in Brentwood, New York, is a Catholic college-preparatory school for Kindergarten to grade 12. ASJ was founded in 1856, by the Sisters of Saint Joseph.

Situated on a 200 acre campus, it is accredited by the New York State Board of Regents and by the Middle States Association of Colleges and Schools and holds full membership in the New York State Association of Independent Schools and the National Catholic Educational Association.

The junior high and high school (Grades 7-12) are all girls.
