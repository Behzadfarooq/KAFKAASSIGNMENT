Academic Soceity of Iranians in Japan, abbreviated as ASIJ, is a non-governmental, independent and friendly group of Iranian students and researchers residing in Japan that has been formed apart from political affiliations and with the following objectives:

  • Make and promote friendship between the members
  • Exchange experiences between the members
  • Benefit the members e.g. through establishing relations with other organizations and societies
  • Making a scientific bridge between the academies of Iran and those of other countries including Japan

Membership to ASIJ, is limited to the Iranian researchers and students residing in Japan. ASIJ annually holds a scientifc conference in Tokyo with presentations in Persian, English and Japanese languages. Attendance to this conference, held in Spring every year, is open to all researchers from around the globe.
