Aca Lukas (Аца Лукас) is a popular Serbian turbo folk singer.

[edit] Biography

Aca Lukas was born in Belgrade with the name, Aleksandar Vuksanović. He got the name Aca Lukas from: Aca, nickname for Aleksandar and "Lukas" from the riverboat disco in Novi Beograd, where he spent much time as a teenager.

Aca Lukas is known for being an extreme homophobe. When in the Netherlands, he started a fight with a homosexual, knocked him out and later said to the press: "I hit him so hard, I tried to remember when I hit someone that hard but I couldn't "

[edit] 2003 Arrest

During a very public and massive police action called "Sablja" (English - Saber) that followed after the assassination of Serbian Prime Minister Zoran Djindjic, Lucas was arrested for illegal weapon possession and narcotics possession. Lukas publicly admits to cocaine addiction, and has been known to sing an ode to the drug at the opening of every concert; he had no objection to the drug charges but denied ownership of the handgun in question. This arrest followed the high-profile arrest of singer Ceca Raznatovic.[citation needed]
