Academia Diplomatica del Peru

The Academia Diplomatica del Peru (Peruvian Diplomatic Academy) was created by means of Supreme Decree Nro. 326, the 18 of August of 1955, like superior professional formation and training center, of the Ministry of Outer Relations. His first Director was illustrious internacionalist Don Alberto Ulloa. The inauguration ceremony was carried out in the Audience of the National Library, the 4 of November of the same year.

For more than 45 years, the Academy has had like main target the formation of aspirings to the Diplomatic Service of the Republic and the permanent qualification of its members.

The preparation of the aspiring to the Diplomatic Service of the Republic carefully has been designed so that throughout four semesters it receives a formation updated and modern in specialized areas of the Diplomacy and the International Relations, counting for it with the aid of an educational plant of the highest level. The studies lead to the obtaining of the Title of Career diplomat, in addition to the Academic degree of Masters in Diplomacy and International Relations.

As a form to arrive at diverse sectors, the Diplomatic Academy organizes, in addition, Annual Cycles of Conferences on subjects of foreign policy and international relations and publishes the magazine quarterly "the Political International", that has national and international circulation.

Tagle tower is synonymous of peruanidad, tradition and excellence,values that the Diplomatic Academy of Peru has insisted on promoting from its foundation, in 1955, like center of professional formation of the Peruvian Chancellery.

The Diplomatic Service of the Republic, to which personalities of the stature of Víctor Andrés Belaúnde, Raul have belonged Barrenechea Clubs, Alberto Ulloa, Carlos Garci'a Bedoya and Javier Perez de Cuéllar, has fulfilled from the beginnings of the Republic the important mission to preserve and to promote the national interests.

The only one access road to the Diplomatic Service of the Republic is the Diplomatic Academy of the Peru, the same one that is a center of postgraduate of the highest level in the country.
