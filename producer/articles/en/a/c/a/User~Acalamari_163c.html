Thank you to everyone who gave their input on my RfA. I will listen to everything everyone had to say, and will make sure I avoid the situations all the opposers mentioned. :)

Greetings, my (user) name is Acalamari. I am a 16 year old English immigrant living on the West Coast of the mainland United States of America. I have been living here for eleven years; since February 7th, 1996. I was born on November 29th, 1990.

I joined Wikipedia on October 19th, 2006, though the logs say that I joined on the 20th. That's only because of time differences, since Wikipedia is on a different time to me. The main reason I decided to register with Wikipedia was so I could correct minor errors in articles. I have fixed mistakes that I know I can correct. However, I have done much more than just sort out minor errors, and as a result, my contributions are much, much better than they were.

My major contributions to Wikipedia include heavy editing to the Fergie (singer), Anna Nicole Smith, and Paris Hilton articles. I have also done a lot of editing to the Aly & AJ, Alyson Michalka, and Amanda Michalka articles. I've done many changes to several Star Trek related pages as well, including cleaning up the Other Starfleet ship classes and USS Enterprise NCC-1701-E articles.

I also alerted an administrator about a virus I found on one of the Christina Aguilera sites. This virus has been sorted out, though I do occasionally check the sites I found it on.

Upon hearing about the deaths of Molly Ivins and Anna Nicole Smith, I went straight to those articles to help out. It is sad that some people vandalized these pages when they heard about the deaths.

I have been involved in the deletion discussions of a few articles, and I was in a mediation Case as well. I've been involved in many important discussions. I help deal with sockpuppets and trolls, vandals, and other disruptive users.

I give help to users whenever I can, and if my Wikipedia skills don't cover what a user needs, I will always check the Wikipedia help pages or ask an administrator.

[edit] Origin Of Acalamari

The meaning of my username: Acalamari does not have anything to do with me being a squid. It is named after the animal, and it is also named after a fictional planet called "Mon Calamari." The "A" at the front separates it from "calamari." I also think the name Acalamari just looks and sounds good.

For some reason, some people don't seem to be able to spell my name right. Instead, they just put "Aclamari," "Alcamari," "Alacamari," or "Acamalari." I do not know why they aren't able to spell it correctly. Acalamari is easy to spell, but some users still type it wrong.
