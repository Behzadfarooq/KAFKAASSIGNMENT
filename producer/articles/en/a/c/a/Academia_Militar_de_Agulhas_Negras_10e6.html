The Academia Militar de Agulhas Negras (AMAN), in English, the Military Academy of the Dark Needles, is the biggest among several schools of formation of combatant officers of Brazilian Army. It had origin in 1792, with the creation of the Real Academy of Artillery, Blockhouse and Drawing - first military school of Americas - in the city of Rio de Janeiro, for the Conde de Resende, that one that gave its name to the city where today's hometown of the the AMAN.

[edit] History

With the coming of the prince Dom João VI and its cut for Brazil, was inaugurated, in 23 of April of 1811, the Royal Academy To militate, that the Ten. Gen. had as first commander Carlos Antonio Napion, current protector of the Warlike Material. The Real Academy To militate was created in the same local of the previous one and was also called Train´s House of the Artillery, today National Historical Museum of Rio De Janeiro. The Train´s House was destined to the guard of the “train of artillery”, set of used warlike equipment in the defense of the city, and, later, it sheltered the Armory of War.

In 1812, the Train´s House was transferred to the plaza of San Francisco, place that offered better conditions for the exercise of the art of the war. With the Independence of Brazil, in 1822, passed it to call Imperial Military Academy and, during the Regencial Period, called Military Academy of the Cut. In 1858, was transferred to Praia Vermelha (Red Beach).

Formation in internal area

With the necessity to improve the combatant formation of the officers of the Brazilian Army, it was servant, at the beginning of century XX, the School of War, in Porto Alegre, Rio Grande Do Sul. In 1913, objectifying to unify all the schools of War and Application, was created the Military School of the Royal one, that it almost formed the elite of officers of our Army per forty years.

With the urgency to perfect the formation of the officer for an Army that grew and got operational, was created in Resende, 1 of January of 1944, the Military School of Resende, that became to call, in 1951, Academia Militar das Agulhas Negras, having as its great idealizer Marshal José Pessoa Cavalcanti de Albuquerque.

[edit] AMAN today

Image:Formac aman.jpg
Cadets on Parade

The AMAN currently is located in Resende, Rio de Janeiro state, occupying a total area of 67 km². It contains some constructed complexes, amongst them the Main Complex, the Physical Education Department, the Equestrian Department, the Firearms Department and the Instruction Park. The Main Complex was expanded in 1988 to two times its original size, the expansion consisting mainly of refectories and cadets' lodgings.

The Monumental Gate, in first plan, consists of a postal card of the Academy. In its background part of the Mountain range of the Mantiqueira can be glimpsed, where the Black Needles' peak is located, standing 2.792 meters high.

The AMAN possesss, in support its activities, a Battalion of Command and Services, organized of the following form: Company of Command; Company of Services; Company of Policy of the Army; Company of Guards; Company of Riflemen; e two Company Auxiliary of the Cadet corps. It is the biggest Battalion of the Brazilian Army.

[edit] Structure

Image:Cadazulao.jpg
Cadets of the AMAN

The Cadet corps are constituted by a Commander, a Vice commander, a Bigger State and diverse Courses and peculiar Sections, composites for Officers and Cadets with the diverse missions and characteristics to each one.

To the Courses, it fits to form the official futures in the diverse Weapons and Services:

  • Infantry,
  • Cavalry,
  • Artillery,
  • Engineering,
  • Communications,
  • Service of Intendancy and
  • Bellic Material.

The Sections assist in the formation of the military personality of a cadet, working the most varied attributes of the affective area, by means of military physical training (SEF), lessons in riding (S Ski), exercises using special techniques (SIEsp) and the use of diverse armaments (S Tir).

[edit] The Education

In the Academy the pertinent activities to the professional teaching of the official military man of career are developed, in accordance with the following periods:

  • 1º Period ** Basic Formation - 1º and 2º Years
  • 2º Period: ** Qualification - 3º Year
  • 3º Period: ** Qualification and Intensification of Instruction - 4º Year

In the Basic Formation, the objectives are: to adjust the personality of the cadet to the principles that conduct the military life; to assure the knowledge that qualify him to the continuation of its formation of officer; to form the military character, to prepare the basic combatant, getting acquainted with the execution of techniques and individual tactics of combat, to get physical qualification, and to develop abilities techniques.

The Qualification has as its main objective the qualification in the practice of commanding the small elementary fractions, and the inherent functions of the graduated ones. One consolidates the perfectioning of the individual techniques of the combatant, the raised standard of joined order and the continuous development of the physical capacity. The Qualification and the Intensification of the Military Instruction have as their main objective the inherent qualifications to the holding of positions and functions to the Subordinate Officer and the Captain.

They are inherent activities to the Cadet corps: exercises in the field, peculiar to each course; periods of special instructions training ; conjunct exercises of weapons, service and formation; maneuvering; the Academic Olympics; sporting competitions with the Naval School and the Academy of the Air Force (NAVAMAER) and the South American Festival of Cadets.
