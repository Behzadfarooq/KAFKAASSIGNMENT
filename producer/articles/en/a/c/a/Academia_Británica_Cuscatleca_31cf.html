Academia Brit치nica Cuscatleca is a selective, mixed bi-lingual and bi-cultural school from Pre-Kinder to Grade 12 situated in Santa Tecla, El Salvador. The school has some 1,340 students in the Primary and Secondary Schools. The student body is mainly Salvadorian with a number of international students attending the school.

One third of the teaching staff is British with the remainder largely Salvadorian. The school is accredited by the Council of International Schools and is an I.B. World School. The language of instruction throughout the school is predominantly English with Spanish being used for a small number of classes.

The school follows a British curriculum preparing students for International General Certificate of Secondary Education examinations (grades 9 and 10) followed by International Baccalaureate Diploma in (grades 11 and 12).

The school is accredited by the Council of International Schools.
