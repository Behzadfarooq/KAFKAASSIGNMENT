Hello, I am Barrett Ross, and I hail from the United States, Florida to be exact. I have several goals on Wikipedia you should know about...

  • Infoboxing- I do a lot of work filling out infoboxes, because they really upgrade an article from mere stub to something a little more substansive.
  • Tagging- If a page is crap, I let everyone know. I don't have the time and espectially not the expertise to correct every article, but I let it known the job needs to be done. Also, I'm a member of Wikiproject:Stub sorting
  • Music- Wikipedia lags behind dozens of websites in our music coverage. One article I'm proud of is Down to Earth (Jimmy Buffett album).
  • Criticism Sections- I confess I haven't done enough work on this most important part of many articles. Face it, you always want to know how people are talking smack about this and that. See Zune#Criticism for a good example (I contributed one myself).

That's all for now...
