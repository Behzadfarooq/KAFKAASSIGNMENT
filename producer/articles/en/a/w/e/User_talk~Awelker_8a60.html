[edit] autobiography

welcome. wikipedia is not a good place to write an autobiography.

To corroborate further on this anonymous fellow: Hi, welcome to Wikipedia! I'm Marblespire, one of the losers who keeps this site up and running.  ;) I wanted to drop by and explain the meaning of the "DB-bio" tag you removed from your page (and why, unfortunately, you'll probably find it returning). Wikipedia is an open-source encyclopedia, true, that anyone can edit, but we try to keep it slim and trim. With that in mind, we tend to delete articles about things people are unlikely to look up. For better or for worse, that includes you; you don't meet Wikipedia's standards of notability. The "DB-bio" tag is a simple way of alerting administrators about someone SO obscure that, at this point, we feel his/her article can be deleted out of hand. Don't feel bad; we're picky—there's only, like, 200,000 people in the history of the world who haven't got speedied. Also, this isn't to say that you can't have information about yourself on Wikipedia—we simply prefer that you keep it on your user page (click that link to reach it). In any case, that's the deal. Again, welcome. =) Marblespire 04:39, 23 March 2006 (UTC)

Ok, sorry for being harsh then, but a copyright violation is a copyright violation. If the webpage you get the info from has a copyright statement (usually at the very bottom) the information is copyrighted unless otherwise noted somewhere else.
