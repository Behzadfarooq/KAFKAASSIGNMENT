The Awesome Collective Vol. 1
Compilation album by Various Artists
Released September, 2003
Recorded 2002-2003
Genre R&B, Hip Hop
Label Awesome DKD

The Awesome Collective Vol. 1 is a compilation album of Canada's rising stars in the hip hop and R&B genres.
