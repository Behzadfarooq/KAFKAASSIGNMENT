AWB is a funk-disco album by Average White Band, released in August of 1974 (see 1974 in music). An enormous best-seller, AWB was the Average White Band's breakthrough record, stunning many listeners with its soul and funk coming from a Scottish band.

AWB topped Billboard's Pop Albums and Black Albums charts.
