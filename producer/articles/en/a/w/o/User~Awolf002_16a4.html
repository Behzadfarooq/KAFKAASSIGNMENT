[edit] Introduction

  • Who am I
    • I am a trained physicist with a Ph.D. I now work as a Software Engineer.
  • My interests
    • I obviously work with computers a lot. I also have "played" with lot of electronics, analog and digital. I am a hobby astronomer, and own a Meade LXD55-SC8. I am a Lutheran, and very much into music.
  • Latest Info
    • My little telescope and CCD camera are giving first results. See my home page for minor planet pictures. Should I GFDL some of them for their respective article?
