AWplanet is a 3D Online Multiplayer Science Fiction game. The game is set on a distant planet far from our own galaxy where modern technology and medieval ways are interwoven. AWplanet claims low system requirements and support for systems including PDAs and mobile phones, also claiming that it will work well on slow internet connections.[1]
