Description

en:Australian War Memorial, en:Canberra, en:25 April en:2005 - en:ANZAC en:Dawn Service - 90th anniversary of en:ANZAC Day.

The lightening dawn, with some 20,000 people gathered at the forecourt of the AWM. The trumpeter is seen on the rampart at the front of the AWM having just played en:reveille.

Bishop en:Tom Frame, en:Anglican en:Bishop to the en:Australian Defence Force, gave the address, and talked about remembrance.

I took this picture. Peter Ellis 20:46, 24 Apr 2005 (UTC)
