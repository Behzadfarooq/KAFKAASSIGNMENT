[edit] Documentation

[edit] Purpose

{{award}} — Gives an award to a person


[edit] Usage

{{subst:Award
  |image=the image to use, defaults to Barnstar.png
  |size=The size of the image, defaults to 100px
  |topic=The topic for the barnstar, default to "An Award"
  |text=The description of the award
  |color=The background color of the box, default to #fdffe7
}}


[edit] Example

Code Result 
{{subst:Award
  |topic=The Barnstar of Good Humor
  |image=Barnstar of Humour3.png
  |text=The Barnstar of Good Humor 
may be awarded to 
particularly light-spirited
 Wikipedians who, by their
 unshakably good humor, 
consistently and reliably 
lighten the mood, defuse conflicts,
 and make the Wikipedia a generally
 better place to be.
}}
The Barnstar of Good Humor may be awarded to particularly light-spirited Wikipedians who, by their unshakably good humor, consistently and reliably lighten the mood, defuse conflicts, and make the Wikipedia a generally better place to be.
