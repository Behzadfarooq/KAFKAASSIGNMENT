Awake (Bleed the Dream album)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Awake
EP by Bleed the Dream
Released 2003
Label Noize Pollution

Awake is the first EP release from Bleed the Dream on their own label Noize Pollution in 2003.

[edit] Track listing

  1. "Villain"
  2. "Overcrowded Room"
  3. "Solace"
  4. "Sarcastic Farewell"
  5. "Be There"
  6. "'Pop' Song"

[edit] Personnel

  • David Aguilera
  • Brandon Thomas
  • Scott Gottlieb
  • Keith Thompson
