Awaraonsen Station (芦原温泉駅, Awaraonsen-eki?) is a train station in the city of Awara, Fukui, Japan. It is located on the Hokuriku Main Line.

[edit] Lines

  • JR Hokuriku Main Line

[edit] History

  • 20 September 1897 - Kanazu Station was opened.
  • 15 March 1972 - It was renamed to Awaraonsen Station.
