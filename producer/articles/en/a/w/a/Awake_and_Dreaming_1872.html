Awake and Dreaming is a novel by Canadian author Kit Pearson. It was first published in 1996. The book follows an impoverished, introverted nine-year-old girl named Theo Caffrey, who dreams of living with a "real" family.

[edit] Plot

Spoiler warning: Plot and/or ending details follow.

Theo lives in the slums of Vancouver with her young mother Mary-Rae. When Rae starts dating a man, Theo is forced to go to Victoria, British Columbia to live with her Aunt Sharon. While she and her mother are on the ferry, Theo seems to fall asleep, and meets a "perfect" family, the Kaldors. After seeming to black out, Theo wakes up mysteriously and unexplainably living with the Kaldors. While there, she asks about her former life many times, but no one seems to hear her. Her new life seems to literally fade away, as people start to ignore her. She wakes up after what seemed to be several months, and finds herself back on the ferry with her mother. She goes to live with her kind aunt, haunted by the supposed memory of the Kaldors, unable to figure out whether they were real or just a dream. While living in Victoria, she discovers the Kaldors again, but they don't remember her. She goes to visit them again many times, and is frightened by a mysterious shadowy figure she repeatedly sees. The figure turns out to be the restless spirit of a dead author, who encountered Theo on the ferry and envisioned her living with the Kaldors. After meeting Theo, her spirit moves on after forty years. Theo and her mother are reunited, and are no longer living in poverty.
