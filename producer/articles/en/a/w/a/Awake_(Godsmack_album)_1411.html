Awake is Godsmack's second album. It was released on October 31, 2000. The CD version of Awake was an enhanced CD. The songs "Sick of Life" and "Awake" have been used for the Navy's "Accelerate Your Life" commercials. It also features the song "Goin' Down" that first appeared on All Wound Up.
