To be awake is to have consciousness, the opposite of being asleep. Awake may also refer to:

  • The AWAKE Project, which developed a driver hypovigilance system that wakes up drowsy drivers.
  • Awake! is a general-interest magazine published by Jehovah's Witnesses.
  • In culture:
    • Awakening can refer metaphorically to a change in one's beliefs.
    • The Great Awakening and the Second Great Awakening are names given to two religious revivals that took place in the history of the United States.
  • In literature and films:
    • The Awakening is a novel by Kate Chopin.
    • Awakenings is the title of a book and derived film.
    • Awake is a 2006 film starring Jessica Alba and Hayden Christensen.
  • In music:
    • Awake is the title of several albums:
      • Awake (1994) by Dream Theater.
      • Awake (1996) by Klinik.
      • Awake (1997) by Crematory.
      • Awake (2000) by Godsmack.
      • Awake (2003) by Bleed the Dream.
      • Awake: The Best of Live (2004) by Live.
      • Awake (2005) by L'arc~en~Ciel.
      • Awake (2006) by Josh Groban.
    • "Awake" is the title of a song by Black Rebel Motorcycle Club from their 2001 album BRMC.
