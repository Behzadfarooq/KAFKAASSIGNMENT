Awakening is the name of the 84th episode from the television series Star Trek: Enterprise. It is the eighth episode from the fourth season of the series. It first aired on November 26, 2004 on the American television network UPN.

This episode is part two of a three episode arc involving Captain Archer's clandestine journey through planet Vulcan. To follow the full plot, see the other two episodes of the arc "The Forge", and "Kir'Shara".

V'Las, Administrator of the Vulcan High Command, is determined to strike at the Syrrannites.
