Awakening is the announced second single off Switchfoot's sixth studio album, Oh! Gravity.. The single will be released to radio sometime in March, according to the band's Footsoldiers website [1]. On the album, it is the fourth track. It has been released to Hot AC, Alternative, Active Rock, and Top 40 format radio stations with an impact day of March 26, 2007

Singer/songwriter Jon Foreman says about the song, "Perhaps to be truly reborn death is not optional. Here’s a firsthand story about new life, it always starts at the bottom."

[edit] Music Video

A music video for this song was filmed February 28, 2007 in Toronto, one of the stops on the band's Winter/Spring Tour.[1] Brandon Dickerson (director of You Already Take Me There and New Way to be Human videos[2]) directed the Awakening music video.

The release date for the music video is currently unknown.
