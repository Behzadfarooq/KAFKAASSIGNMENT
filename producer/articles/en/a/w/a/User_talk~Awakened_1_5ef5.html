[edit] Kay'bil B'alam - Kaibil Balam

Hi there Awakened 1. I notice that you have several times now changed Kay'bil B'alam into a redirect to Kaibil Balam. I would be happy to hear any arguments you may have as to why this latter should be the preferred spelling on the relevant talk page, but before you carry out the redirect action again, please. Note that changing one to a redirect of the other is not really the way to go about retitling articles- if you really think it should be moved then the procedure would be to move the article to the new title. If this isn't done then the edit history is lost, and also the talk page becomes disassociated from the article. To do this you would need to firstly put a request for it to be moved at WP:RM, where it can be discussed. In the meantime, I have reset things back to the previous arrangement, until we can come to a consensus on the article title to use. Kind regards, --cjllw | TALK 05:23, 17 January 2007 (UTC)
