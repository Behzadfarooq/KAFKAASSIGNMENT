I am a graduate student in English literature, specializing in eighteenth-century British literature. Currently, I am writing a dissertation on the self and sensibility in eighteenth-century British children's literature.

"The unexamined life is not worth living." - Socrates


I am an active peer-reviewer and FAC-reviewer. If you would like me to review your article for either of these processes or at any other time, please leave a message on my talk page. I will give your page a careful and thoughtful review. Although I have decided not to join the League of Copyeditors because I spend many hours of my life commenting on student papers, I will gladly respond to specific copyediting requests.
