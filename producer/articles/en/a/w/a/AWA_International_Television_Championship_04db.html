The AWA International Television Championship was a short-lived title in the American Wrestling Association from 1987 to 1989. It was filled with a several months long tournament and was defended on their television broadcast on ESPN.

[edit] Title history

Wrestler: Times: Date: Place:
Greg Gagne 1 December 27, 1987 Las Vegas, NV (over Adrian Adonis)
Ron Garvin 1 September 17, 1988 Nashville, TN
Greg Gagne 2 December 13, 1988 Via countout...Garvin left for WWF & would not job for Gagne
Title Retired October 1989 Gagne retired from in-ring competition
