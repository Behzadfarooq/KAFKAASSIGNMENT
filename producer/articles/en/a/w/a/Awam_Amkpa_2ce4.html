Awam Amkpa is a Nigerian actor, playwright, and professor of dramatic arts.

[edit] Background

He received his B.A. in B.A. 1982 in theater from Obafemi Awolowo University, Ile-Ife, Nigeria, his M.A. in 1987 from Ahmadu Bello University, Zaria, Nigeria, and his Ph.D. from in 1993 from University of Bristol, Bristol, England.

[edit] Career

Amkpa is currently a professor of drama at New York University [1]. He has also taught at Mount Holyoke College [2].
