Award Tour was the first single by A Tribe Called Quest's third album Midnight Marauders and features Trugoy from De La Soul. The song features a sample of Weldon Irvine's "We Gettin' Down."

The B-Side of the single is a slightly differed version of Midnight Marauders track "The Chase pt. II", which notably features the first known verse by future Tribe collaborator Consequence.

"Award Tour" remains Tribe's highest charting single to date, experiencing gigantic success in America and overseas.
