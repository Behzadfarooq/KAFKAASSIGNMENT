[edit] Medals

  • Darjah Utama Bakti Cemerlang (Tentera) (Distinguished Service Order (Military))
  • Pingat Gagah Perkasa (Tentera) (Conspicuous Gallantry Medal (Military))
  • Pingat Jasa Gemilang (Tentera) (Meritorious Service Medal (Military))
  • Pingat Pentadbiran Awam, Emas (Tentera) (Public Administration Medal, Gold (Military))
  • Pingat Pentadbiran Awam, Perak (Tentera) (Public Administration Medal, Silver (Military))
  • Pingat Pentadbiran Awam, Gangsa (Tentera) (Public Administration Medal, Bronze (Military))
  • Pingat Penghargaan (Tentera (Tentera) (Commendation Medal (Military))
  • Pingat Berkebolehan (Tentera) (Efficiency Medal (Military))
  • Singapore Armed Forces Overseas Medal (Operational)
  • Singapore Armed Forces Overseas Medal (Non-operational)
  • Singapore Armed Forces Long Service and Good Conduct Medal
  • Singapore Armed Forces Good Service Medal
