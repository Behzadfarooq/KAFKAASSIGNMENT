An award is something given to a person or group of people to recognize excellence in a certain field. Awards may consist of prizes (money or other prizes), and/or plaques, certificates, ribbons, trophies, medals, and other memorabilia of the award. Awards often accompany award lectures, speeches, or other events, and may sometimes be designated by the name of the event.

see also List of prizes, medals, and awards

Types of awards include: civil decorations - honorary titles (including honorary degrees) - orders, decorations, and medals - records - scholarships


For more information, see the article about Award.
