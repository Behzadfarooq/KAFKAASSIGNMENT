AWA British Empire Heavyweight Championship

You have new messages (last change).

The AWA British Empire Heavyweight Championship was a short-lived mid-card professional wrestling title contested for in the American Wrestling Association promotion. The title was primarly defended in Canada, part of the Commonwealth Realm, hence the British Empire name.

[edit] Title history

Wrestler: Times: Date: Location: Notes:
Billy Red Lyons 1 1969
Billy Robinson 1 England Robinson was named champion.
Angelo Mosca 1 March 10, 1978 Winnipeg, Manitoba, Canada
Billy Robinson 2 May 18, 1978 Winnipeg, Manitoba, Canada
Super Destroyer Mark II 2 October 25, 1979 Winnipeg, Manitoba, Canada
Billy Robinson 3 November 12, 1979 Winnipeg, Manitoba, Canada The title was later abandoned.
