[edit] Short Bio

  • Born in Rochester, New York
  • Military Service:
  • U.S. Army Military Police - 21 Years (retired 01/01/92 as Staff Sergeant E-6)
  • Military Service Overseas: Vietnam, West Germany, Panama, Honduras, South Korea
  • Education:
  • Machinist Basic (1968)
  • Military Police Basic (1973)
  • Military Police Advanced NCO (1981)
  • Defense Language Institute (Latin American Spanish - 1982)
  • Swiss L'Abri (May 1978)
  • Bachelor of Science (Bible Ministries - 1995) Philadelphia Biblical University (PBU)
  • Present Employment: Campus Services Coordinator at PBU
  • Family: married with grown daughter and son
  • Religious Affiliation: Bible Baptist Church
