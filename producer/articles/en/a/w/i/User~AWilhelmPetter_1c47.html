My name is Petter Nitter and I'm from Bergen, Norway. I'm a 15 year old student who makes a small amount of money on web and graphic design for bands i support. My nickname AWilhelmPetter originates from A Wilhelm Scream which is the name of my favourite band. I have seen them live and met them once and they are the most awesome bunch of people. I really hope to see them again when they are touring around Europe with Rise Against in September. Appart from A Wilhelm Scream I'm a big fan of bands such as Anchors For Arms (formerly Allstar Otis), Boys Night Out, Much The Same, Protest The Hero, Senses Fail and Strike Anywhere to mention a few.
I have lived in Bergen, Norway most of my life, but from the age of 6 to the age of 8 I lived in Newcastle, England. Since I moved back to Norway I have always wanted to move back to England again. I like England heeps more then Norway and it's just a matter of time until I will be moving back there on my own.

When what has been said has been said, I don't have more to say.

Enjoy Wikipedia and please contribute when ever you can. It's people like you and me who keep this place alive and if you all are to think someone else will take care of it, no one will.
