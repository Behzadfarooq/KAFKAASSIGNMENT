Aju Cold Storage Holdings Co, Ltd. (hangul:아주냉장) is a Korea's ice in logistic and shipping company. headquartered in Sangha-Ri Guseong-Eup Yongin Gyeonggi-do, Korea. it was a cold storage in logistic and express service products. Logistic center is based in Yongin and Hwaseong. and group family by Aju Group.
