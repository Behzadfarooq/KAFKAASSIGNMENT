Aju Avis Co, Ltd. (hangul:아주에이비스) is an automobile rental headquartered in Guro-dong Guro-gu Seoul Korea and established in 1988. Aju Avis is a unit licensed by Avis Rent A Car System Inc., a wholly owned subsidiary of Aju Group.

Aju Avis currently operates branch offices throughout Korea, including locations in Seoul, Incheon, Gyeonggi-do and many others. It is one of the largest vehicle rental agencies in the world[citation needed].

Aju Avis rents cars for out-of-town travellers (as well as local commuters who are temporarily without the use of their own vehicles). The company also provides an assortment of vehicles ranging from cube and panel vans to box trucks, for the purposes of both residential and commercial moving. Aju Avis also operates a number of storage facilities at their various locations.

The "Aju Avis" CEO is Jeong Sang Ho (정상호).
