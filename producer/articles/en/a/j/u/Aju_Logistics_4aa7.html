Aju Logistics Co, Ltd. (hangul:아주택배) is a Korea's logistic and shipping company. headquartered in Yangjae-dong Seocho-gu Seoul, Korea. established in 1998 as formery company name is Hanmi Logistics. it was a local, worldwide in logistic and express service products. Logistic center is based in Seoul and other Korea local. and group family by Aju Group. The "Aju Logistics" CEO is Hong Han Yeong (홍한영).
