AJ Calloway

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

AJ Calloway (born Albert Calloway on August 29, 1974 in Jersey City, New Jersey, USA) is the former host of popular BET music video countdown show 106 and Park: BET's Top 10 Live. After co-hosting the show for five years with Free (Marie Wright), he left the show on July 28, 2005. Calloway decided to leave the show to pursue other endeavors. He can now be seen on the entertainment TV show Extra.When he left 106 and park he cried.

Married to Lao Sealey Calloway as of April 23, 2005.
