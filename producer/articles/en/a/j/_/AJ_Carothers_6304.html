AJ Carothers

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

AJ Carothers was born on October 22, 1931 in Houston, Texas in the United States of America. Most noted for his work with Disney, Carothers is also a noted playwright and TV writer. His most recent work includes writing the book for the Sherman Brothers stage musical Piccadilly (also called Busker Alley). Carothers is sometimes credited as "A.J. Carothers".

  • Emil and the Detectives (1964)
  • Miracle of the White Stallions (1963) also known as The Flight of the White Stallions (UK)
