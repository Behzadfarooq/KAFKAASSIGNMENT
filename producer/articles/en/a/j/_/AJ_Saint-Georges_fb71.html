AJ Saint-Georges

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

AJ Saint-Georges are a French Guianese football club based in the country's capital Cayenne. They have won the French Guyana Championnat National six times: in 1965, 1983, 1984, 1999, 2000 and 2002.

[edit] Achievements

  • French Guyana Championnat National: 6
1965, 1983, 1984, 1999, 2000, 2001
  • Coupe de Guyane: 14
1960, 1965, 1966, 1969, 1971, 1980, 1983, 1984, 1985, 1990, 2000, 2001, 2003, 2004
