Hi!

I'm A.J., and if you're seeing this page, you probably want to know a bit about me.

I live in Beaverton, Oregon, USA and have lived in Oregon all my life. Other than Wikipedia, my interests include 3d graphics, politics, and photography. On Simtropolis, I am Sparkythepenguin, a co-founder of Around the Roses: the Portland and Oregon Project, an attempt to bring real-life buildings into the Sim City 4 video game using the Building Architect Tool (BAT).

[edit] Interests

You'll most likely see me contribute something in the following subjects:

  • Oregon- especially historical aspects.
  • Portland, Oregon
  • Rapid transit
  • Light Rail
  • Metropolitan Area Express (Portland, Oregon) and Transportation in Portland
  • Aviation, Airports, and Airlines
  • Interstate Highways, and Ghost ramps
  • vexillology, the study of flags
  • Heraldry
  • Architecture, especially Architecture in Portland, Oregon

I tend not to edit, but read a lot about the following subjects:

TV shows, history, composers, John Phillip Sousa, Johann Sebastian Bach, Movies, cryptozoology, the paranormal, military, internet memes, languages, Humor, geography, physics, urban planning, politics, and outer space

[edit] Projects

I am a proud member of WikiProject: Oregon (link below somewhere in the Userboxen..)

  • Architecture photos: Getting more good pictures of the buildings around the State of Oregon
  • City seals and flags: Stil waiting on a bunch of replys from Oregon cities requesting use of their city logos and flags.

Lately I've not felt too up to writing articles themselves, but just making them look nice, such as adding info boxes where needed. I also like to categorize.

I often check for bad links, grammar problems, and spelling errors. If you see any that I've made, I really appreciate it if you could correct them! Thanks.
