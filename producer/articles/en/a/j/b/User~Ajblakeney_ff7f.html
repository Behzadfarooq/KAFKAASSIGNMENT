I was born in January 4, 1992 to an Black Indian (of African-American and Cherokee descent) mother and Filipino American father. I am the last and only boy out of six children. I have 3.5 octave range and I'm a Lyric Tenor. I write my own songs. I have recorded over five demo albums. My voice has been trained in opera and soul-gospel vocal exercises.
