Andy Tabar was born in 1986 in Cleveland, Ohio. He moved to Nashville, Tennessee in 2005, upon the demand of a business opportunity and acceptance to the Entrepreneurial Studies program at Belmont University, which sits at the top of Music Row. In 2006, Tabar launched a new business venture, Nashville Creative, serving national clients with online communications, marketing development and advertising for the corporate, creative, arts & entertainment industries.

Tabar is an active entrepreneur who jumpstarted his career at the age of 13. He is the front man for entrepreneurial ventures Andy Tabar & Associates, Nashville Creative, and Club Nash.
