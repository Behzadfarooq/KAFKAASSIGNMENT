I am a wrestler for my school. I am like the best at the sport. I have been wrestling for about five years now. I have been to many tournaments in Indiana. When wreslting for school a always have a good record. Last year I got fifth, and this year I am shoting for third or better. Anyone intersted in the sport should try out. It will get you in shape and show you lots of disipline and self- control. I have greatly benifited from the sport and you should to. So go out for your schools team and start learning about the sport and have a good time wrestling.

In the sport you have to pin your oppent or you could score more points then him. To pin, you need to get both of his shoulder blades on the mat. It is important to get them on the mat. You score points by taking the guy down or puting them on their back. If they are on their back for three seconds you get three points and if you get him on his back for five seconds you get five points. To win you need to either pin him or get a tech. fall. A tech. fall is when you get fifteen points or more. It feels good to win because you feel like you are invicible and strong. It really feels good.
