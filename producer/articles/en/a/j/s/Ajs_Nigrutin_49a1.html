Ajs Nigrutin

Origin Belgrade, Serbia, Yugoslavia
Years active 1996 – present
Genres Hip-hop
Labels One Records

Ajs Nigrutin (Ајс Нигрутин) (born Vladan Aksentijević, Владан Аксентијевић) is a Serbian rapper. In addition to his solo career, he's also a member of Bad Copy and 43zla groups.

Nigrutin developed somewhat of a cult following throughout the Balkans for his irreverent sex, marijuana and alcohol influenced raps as well as a propensity for inventing new slang words. He also works with many other rappers in Serbia. His latest album, Štrokavi pazuh, talks about excitements in his life, which he has a lot.

Ajs Nigrutin resides in the Belgrade suburb of Kotež.
