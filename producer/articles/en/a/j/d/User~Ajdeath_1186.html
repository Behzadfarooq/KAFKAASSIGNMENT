AJ Death (born Alphonso Jarvier Arsenio Morales, October 23, 1989 in Los Angeles, California), is an Mexican-American Amature Wrestler and he is currently the ACW Champion for Amature Championship Wrestling(ACW) on both Monday Night Imact 2.0 and Friday Night Throwdown.

[edit] Amature Championship Wrestling

On October 28, 2005, Aj Death was confronted by a the founder of Amature Championship Wrestling, and was asked if Aj wanted to join the ACW. Aj said that he would join the ACW. Aj was told that the first on-aired Internet show would be on November 14, 2005. The main event would be a Fatal four way match for the first ever ACW Champion.

[edit] ACW Champion

On November 14, 2005, on the first on air show of Monday Night Revenge, their was a fatal four way match to declare the first ever ACW Champion. It was "The King Of Reggeton" Ricardo Manzo vs. AJ Death vs. Jorge Alarcon and vs. Abe the King of Kings.

The match lasted 44 minutes long and the winner was AJ Death, making him the first ever ACW champion. Then on December 12, 2005 their would be a Fatal four way match for the #1 contender for the ACW Championship, it would consist of Abe the King Of Kings, "The King Of Reggeton" Ricardo Manzo, Jorge Alarcon, and Mr. Zapeda. On December 26, 2005 was the Championship match "The King Of Reggeton" Ricardo Manzo would face the ACW Champion AJ Death, The winner was AJ Death, after landing the Death Bomb (Double-Underhook Power Bomb). Then Began the title reign of Aj Death.

Until, April 7, 2006, On the premiere of the first airing of Friday Night Evolution. Thier was a championship match between Ricardo Manzo and Aj Death, Ricardo beat Aj Death with the help of Jorge Alarcon. Aj had earned his title shot when he beat Alarcon, so Ricardo decided to create a tag match, if AJ won he would get the title shot, Aj could pick anyone for a tag partner. On April 28, 2006 Aj was schduled to face Ricardo Manzo and Jorge Alarcon in a tag team match. Aj was supose to find a unknown tag team partner. Ricardo and Jorge had came out already for the match, then Aj's music hit, he came to the ring, when one of his good friends named Vicente came out for his debut match as AJ's tag partner.

[edit] Second Title Reign

On May 1, 2006, Aj had been getting ready for the match when his friend Oscar came to him and said that Ricardo would have Jorge Alarcon at his corner, so AJ decided to have Vicente at his corner. Aj and Vicente came out for the match, Ricardo came out with Jorge. The match was tense, with Jorge getting ready to pounce on AJ when ever the referee wasn't looking. Since Aj had Vicente at his corner he coulnd worry, except the match. Ricardo was going for the Reggeton Slam when, Aj countered it send Ricardo to the ropes back to Aj to send Ricardo for the Death Stunner. After that move Jorge came in and Vicente came in and took him out.

Since the referee was accupied with Vicente and Jorge, out of no where Ricardo gave Aj a low blow, going into a roll up pin. The referee counted when Aj kicked out, Ricardo was shocked, Ricardo send Aj for the Reggeton Slam and pinned him. AJ still kicked out, Ricardo was going to send him for another Reggeton Slam, when Aj countered and send Ricardo for the Death Stunner, then he did the Death Leg Drop. Finally, Aj was setting Ricardo for the Death Power-bomb, he landed it, pinning him and becoming the Second time ACW Champion.

[edit] Third Title Reign

On June 23, 2006, Aj had come out to the ring, to say that he is tired of ACW's Wrestlers cause they were to easy. He was interupted by an unknown wrestler. He challenged Aj to a match that night. AJ accepted the match, if the unknown wrestler won, he would face AJ again for the title. The match was a good, AJ thought he was winning, when out of no where, the unknown wrestler hit AJ with a clotheline. Pinning AJ and becoming the #1 Contender for the Title.

The following week, the match was set and the wrestler was revealed, that night it was schduled "The ACW Champion AJ Death vs. Juan V." The match had not even began and the aren was trebleing with excitement. The match was a classic, AJ was winning at the beginning, when the "Turning Point" of the match was comenced. The refferee was down, out of no where Juan hit AJ with a steel chair right on the head, giving Juan a chance to do his signature move "The Dominator," pinning AJ and becomeing the new ACW Champion.

[edit] Fourth Title Reign

On July 3, 2006, Aj had a #1 Contender match between AJ and Maynor. Maynor won the match thanks to Click Crew. After Maynor failing to become champ, AJ got another chance to win the title back. Fano interupted the match by attacking both AJ and Juan V. ending the match but starting a war with the Click Crew. During the war AJ and Juan had gone so many beatings. After the wars, there was a match made, AJ Death vs. Maynor vs. The ACW Champion Juan V. Maynor won that match after hitting the 323 on AJ and doing a roll up pin, winning the title.

After Kevin Nitro winning the title from Maynor, AJ asked for a title match from Kevin Nitro. The manager of ACW said that there will be a best of three matches, the winner will be the ACW Champion. In the first match, AJ was screwed when the Click Crew interferred. The second match AJ won the match. In the third and final match AJ lost the match with no interuption. One week later, Kevin Nitro gave AJ another chance to try to win the title and even gave AJ to set the rules.

One week after Kevin Nitro challenge, AJ came out face to face with Kevin, Aj said he would accept the match and it will be a TLC Match! On September 11, 2006, the match was on. AJ won the match after he tossed Kevin Nitro off the ladder into two tables, making AJ the new ACW Champion.

[edit] Monday Night Impact 2.0

On September 25, 2006, was the season premiere of Monday Night Impact 2.0, the main event was AJ Death vs. Kevin Nitro in a 3 best of three falls match and if Kevin Nitro lost the match he would not be no longer the number one contender no more. Thanks to the help of Jonny Nitro, AJ was able to win the title from Kevin Nitro.

[edit] Friday Night Throwdown 2.0

On Friday October 6, 2006, was the season premiere of Friday Night Throwdown 2.0 the main event was Kevin Nitro vs. AJ Death in the first ever Steel Cage match for the ACW title. AJ won the match after some help of Alex and Armando and doing the Death- Defyer on Nitro to retain the title.

[edit] ACW Tricontinental Champion

On April 17, 2006, Since Ricardo was denining Aj a championship match, so Aj decided to face the Tricontinentel Champion Jorge Alarcon, for the title. On the following Friday April 21, Aj faced the Tricontinental Champ. Jorge Alarcon. The match lasted about 21 minutes long, but at the end AJ had landed the Death Power Bomb, and pinning Jorge becoming the new Tricontinental Champion. On April 24, Jorge got his rematch clause, Jorge won the match thanks to his good old friend Ricardo Manzo. ending AJ's Tricontinental Title Reign.

On July 17, while Juan V. was champion, AJ came out saying that the ACW title wasn't the only title out in the ACW, so he challenged the Tricontinental champion Ricardo Mora. AJ won the title after hitting the STF, winning his second Tricontinental Title. After a while Johnny Nitro won a #1 Contender's match to face the Tricontinental Title. The match happened 28 of July, Johnny won the title ater hitting the Armwrech-Scissor Kick.

[edit] ACW Tag-team Champion

On May 8, 2006, the ACW champion Aj Death and Vicente decide to try for the Tag titles, the match was organized that it would be AJ Death & Vicente vs. Chris & Sabby, Aj and Vicente won the match and became the #1 Contenders for the ACW Tag-team Championships. May 12 2006, The main event for friday Night Evolution the Challengers ACW Champion AJ Death & Vicente vs. the ACW Tag-team champions Maynor & Mr. Zapeda. They went on for 36 minutes, the challengers were losing after AJ had gotten a lowblow by Maynor while the referee wasn't looking, because Vicente was knocked outside of the ring. Mr. Zapeda and Maynor had done their signature double-team move the Neckbreaker-bomb, when outta no where Vicente came out with a super kick to Mr. Zapeda knocking him outta of the ring, leaving Maynor by himself. Aj stood up, Vicente gave Maynor the V-Drop, Aj gave Maynor the Death Bomb, Then they did the Superkick-jacknifehold, pinning Maynor, becoming the new ACW Tag-team Champions.

On May 19, AJ and Vicente were going to fight Mr. Zapeda and Maynor, for the ACW Tag Team Titles, their rematch claus. The match started off with Aj and Vicente winning, since Maynor is so good at giving low-blows, Aj had countered one of those low-blows, but Mr. Zapeda attacked AJ. Mr. Zapeda and Maynor did the Maynor Z Cutter (Elevated Cutter), knocking out Aj. Leaving Vicente alone to get a Neckbreaker-bomb, pinning him. Maynor and Mr. Zapeda won back thier titles.

[edit] The Hardocore Match of Hell

On May 26, 2006, Aj had the worst hardcore match, in his career. It was Aj Death vs. Kevin Nitro for the ACW Championship in a Hardcore Match. The match lasted 1 hour and 23 minutes. The was a one of the best matches, with Aj countering each move. Aj was about to lose the match until. he came out with a closeline, getting a momentum to do the " 3 moves of death" pinning Nitro retaining his title, as the ACW Champion.

[edit] Aj Death vs. Click Crew

On June 5, 2006, Kevin Nitro had formed a group called "Minute Warning", Kevin Nitro(Leader), Joseph, and Mathew. That night Kevin Nitro, had called out AJ. Aj had gotten ambushed, by KC and DC, they sended Aj for the 3-D(Elvated Cutter), right through a table. The following Friday, Aj had a match against Kevin Nitro for the title, Aj won, then out of no where DC and KC came out to ambush Aj, then the new member of Minute Warning, "The Dominator" Phycho. The thrashing ended by Aj going for a power-bomb through three tables.

[edit] Cypress Park Mafia

On June 23, 2006, AJ and Vicente and Ricardo formed The Cypress Park Mafia. Since the click crew was getting out of hand, they decided to create the Cypress Park Mafia. On August 11, during a 6-man tag team match, CPM vs. Click Crew, AJ was going for the cover when out of no where Ricardo hit him with the Reggeton slam, making the Click Crew win. Later, after the struggle with Ricardo's betrayal, CPM, decided to recruit a couple of new wrestlers to CPM.

[edit] Outside of the ACW

Aj likes to hang out with friends Ricardo Manzo and Oscar Navarro. His favorite singers are Eminem, Rihanna, and Christina Milian. AJ attends Abraham Lincoln High School. He watches World Wrestling Entertainment and Total Nonstop Action Wrestling, his favorite wrestler is Bret " The Hitman" Hart.

[edit] Aj's friends

AJ's best Friends are Oscar Narvarro, Ricardo Manzo, And Vicente Mendoza. Oscar Narvarro has been friends with AJ for 10 years. Ricardo and Aj have been friends for 5 years, they first started as enemys then friends, they've been good friends for now. Vicente was the same thing with Ricardo first enemys then friends.


[edit] Growing Up

AJ grew up in Cypress Park, with his mother(Maria), grandmother(Arcelia), brother(Martin), and his great grandmother(Antonieta). Aj has other places he grew up, he grew up in Whittier, California with his aunt Pat, Imperial Valley, California with his aunt Mya. In Whittier, California AJ grew up with his aunt Pat and his cousin Manuel, he went to Aloleon Elemetary School. In imperial Valley, California, Aj and his grandma Arcelia would always go down to Imperial to visit his aunt but when ever he would leave he would leave good memories.
