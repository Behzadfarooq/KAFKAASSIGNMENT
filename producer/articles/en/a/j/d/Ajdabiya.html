Ajdabiya

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

City centre of Ajdabiya

Ajdabiya (Arabic: إجدابيا) is one of the municipalities of Libya. It is in the north-east of the country. Its capital is Ajdabiya.

In the northeast, Adjabiya has a small shoreline on the Mediterranean Sea. On land, it borders the following municipalities:

  • Al Hizam Al Akhdar - north
  • Al Wahat - east
  • Al Kufrah - southeast
  • Al Jufrah - southwest
  • Surt - west
