Hey, I'm A.J. Halliwell, and this is my user page. My user page keeps annoying me cause I can't get it just right. I came here by way of Memory Alpha, the Star Trek wiki. You don't really want to know more do you?

My mission, as I've chosen to accept it: to upgrade the Garfield Heights, Ohio page (as well as several pages in relation to it) as it is my hometown, and who else besides a resident would want to? I also plan to get more overall wiki-experience by doing a number of things. I'm working on the disambiguation list (such as Sasebo) I also intend to get around to revamping the Birds of Prey (TV series) one of these days.

I'm also involved in the Ohio project. I try to get around to taking pictures for Wikipedia with my DSC-W70, and formerly my Sony Cyber-shot DSC-S600.
