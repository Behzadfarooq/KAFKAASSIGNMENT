Ajnabee Shehr Mein (Urdu: اجنبی شہر میں) is a film being directed by the ace Pakistani ad-film maker Saqib Malik. The film stars Ali Zafar, Shaan, Samina Peerzada and the ramp-model Tooba Siddiqui. It is being produced by the recording industry tycoon Khalid Sadaf. Shanee scores the music. The film is an urban thriller set in the Pakistani metropolis Karachi, and the shooting commenced in April 2006. Sarmad Sultan Khoosat has penned the script. The film is currently in production.

[edit] Synopsis

The film is a coming of age tale of non-native Pakistanis who land in Karachi to discover their destinies. A mystery/thriller angle has also been hinted at by the director.
