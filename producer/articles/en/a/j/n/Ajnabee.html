Ajnabee (English: Stranger) is a 2001 Bollywood film directed by Abbas-Mustan. The film stars Akshay Kumar, Bobby Deol, Kareena Kapoor and Bipasha Basu in the lead roles.

[edit] Synopsis

Spoiler warning: Plot and/or ending details follow.

Raj (Bobby Deol) and Priya Malhotra (Kareena Kapoor) are a new married couple who have moved to Switzerland. They meet there neighbours Vicky (Akshay Kumar) and Sonia Bajaj (Bipasha Basu) who are also married. The couples spend a lot of time together and quickly become friends. They take a trip to Mauritius together but Vicky suggests that he and Raj swap their wives for a night. This angers Raj and he cuts all contact with Vicky and Sonia. However after some time the two become friends again and they all celebrate on Vicky's birthday. Raj incredibly drunk wonders into Vicky's home whilst Vicky goes to Raj's home.

The next day Raj wakes up and imagines that Vicky and Priya are making love and then finds out finds out that Sonia has been murdered. Vicky accuses Raj that he killed her and the evidence soon point to him. Raj knows that Vicky has framed him and flees the authorities to prove his innocence... This is a very exciting movie and it is also regarded as Script writer Partha's greatest acheivement.

Spoilers end here.
