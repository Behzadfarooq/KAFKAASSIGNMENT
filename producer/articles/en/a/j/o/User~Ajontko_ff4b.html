[edit] Who am I?

You probably never heard of me...

Anyway, I am located near Charleston SC. I live along a dirt road out "in the country" on a 3 acre farmlet along with my dear wife, her Arabian horse appropriately and coincidentally named "Celeste", a flock of "Easter Egg" chickens, and an assortment of wild ducks, African Egrets, frogs, cats, and other wildlife...

[edit] What do I do?

I make my living (hah!) and live my passion (ah!) by designing and building pipe organs.

When not doing that, I compose classically-based music for Organ, Carillon, Chorus, and whatever other instrument(s) strike my fancy.

When not doing that, I watch old monochromatic movies on Satellite TV and heap curses on anyone who presumes to colorize them...

And when the spirit moves me, which hasn't been very often lately, I work on my two in-progress murder mysteries wherein a pipe organ builder is the main character. Someday...

I used to fly private planes, but lost my medical... Still in mourning.

[edit] Where'd you find this character?

Born a Yankee in the New York City area,; attended Westminster Choir College and came away with a BMus degree, to everyone's surprise; married; moved South (became a Damyankee); divorced; remarried; still in the South (am now an official Gahdamyankee).

And that's all for now, folks.

And, by the way, I am a stickler on using the English language, and an inveterate procrastina
