Ajmer Division

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Seven divisions of the Districts of Rajasthan.

Ajmer Division is one of the administrative geographical unit, called a division, of Rajasthan state, India. The division comprises four districts, namely, Ajmer, Bhilwara, Nagaur, Tonk.
