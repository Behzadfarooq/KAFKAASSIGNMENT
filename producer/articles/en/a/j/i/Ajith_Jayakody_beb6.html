Ajith Jayakody

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

You have new messages (last change).

Ajith Jayakody is a former Sri Lankan record holder for discus throw. He held the national record for more than 10 years. He currently serves as a Lieutenant Colonel in the Sri Lankan army and continues to play a major role in bringing up young talent within the country.
