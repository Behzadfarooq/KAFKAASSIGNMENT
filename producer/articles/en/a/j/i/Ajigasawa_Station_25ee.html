Ajigasawa Station(鰺ヶ沢駅 -eki) is a railway station located in Ajigasawa, Aomori, Japan.

[edit] Line

  • JR East
    • Gonō Line

[edit] Layout

The station has two platforms with two lines, which are:

  • Track 1: Gonō Line For.Goshogawara, Hirosaki and Aomori
  • Track 2: Gonō Line For.Fukaura and Higashi-Noshiro

[edit] Route bus

  • Kōnan bus
    • For.Fukaura via Kita-Kanegasawa and Ōdose
    • For.Hirosaki Station via Tokoshinai
    • For.Goshogawara Bus office via Morita, Kizukuri and Goshogawara Station
    • For.Kuromori

[edit] Surrounding area

  • Maito Post office
