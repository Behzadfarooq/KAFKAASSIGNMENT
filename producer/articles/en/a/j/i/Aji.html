Aji can refer to:

  • the ají pepper or Peruvian Hot Pepper, or a pepper sauce
  • Al-Jazeera English a Qatar-based popular Arab channel that was originally named Al-Jazeera International
  • a term used in Go, the East Asian strategy game, which represents "potential" or "taste" left in a position. See Aji on Sensei's Library.
  • あじ (味, あじ?) Japanese word for flavor
  • あじ (鯵, あじ?) Japanese word for jack, most commonly jack mackerel (Trachurus japonicus), as may be seen in sushi or sashimi menus
