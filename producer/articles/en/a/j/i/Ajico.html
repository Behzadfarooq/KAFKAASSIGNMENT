Ajico

AJICO is a Japanese popular music group. Its members are UA, Asai Kenichi (of Blankey Jet City), TOKIE (of RIZE), and Shino Kyoichi. In the summer of 1999 after supplying music for two of UA's songs, Asai and UA decided to create a music group, and AJICO was born.
