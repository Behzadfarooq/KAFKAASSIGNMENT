Ajay Chabra

Ajay Chabra is a British actor who is known for playing Anil in The Basil Brush Show, he is also known for his comical Indian accent. He was also a regular on Holby City.

[edit] Filmography

  • Project Huxley (2005)
  • The Basil Brush Show (2003-2004)
  • The Audition (2003)
  • Holby City (2003)
  • Anita and Me (2002)
  • Second Generation (2000)
  • The Bill (1998)
  • Casualty (1998)
