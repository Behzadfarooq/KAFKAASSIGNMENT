Ajay Kumar Sharma pronunciation (help·info) (born April 3, 1964 in Delhi), was a prolific run maker in First Class cricket , mainly for Delhi, scoring over 10,000 runs at the high average of 67.46[1] - but he only played one Test match (in January 1998) . Sharma played 31 One Day Internationals for India scoring 424 runs at an average of 20.19 with a highest score of 59 not out – he also took 15 wickets with his left-arm spin.

In 2000, aged 36, his career ended when he received a life ban from cricket after he was implicated in a match-fixing scandal.[2]
