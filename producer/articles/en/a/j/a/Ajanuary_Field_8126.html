Ajanuary Field is a rock wave project founded by Maethelyiah. It started in 1999 when she used to live in Italy and it continues now in the UK, along with Ivan Flospergher (bass guitars) and Tury (drums). Ajanuary Field matches surreal stories into sexual, aggressive, rough but melodic music.

The discography is:

  • Perverse Spring (album published by I.A.P.)
  • Fetish Virus (EP published by I.A.P.)
