The Ajax GO Station is a train and bus station in the GO Transit network located in Ajax, Ontario, Canada. It is a stop on the Lakeshore East line train service, and is also a stop on GO Transit's Oshawa Highway 2 bus route. The station is located in proximity to Highway 401, at the Westney Road exit, exit number 401.

The station is also the Ajax hub for Durham Region Transit services, with most routes providing timed transfers to inbound (morning) and outbound (afternoon) GO trains. DRT's West operational facility is also located at the station.
