Ajai Sanders, born August 24, 1974, in North Trenton, New Jersey, is an American actress best known for her supporting role as Gina Devereaux in The Cosby Show spinoff, A Different World. She is sometimes credited as Ajai Richards.

[edit] Television

Ms. Sanders has made guest appearances on the sitcoms, Martin, Hangin' with Mr. Cooper, The Fresh Prince of Bel-Air, The Parent 'Hood, The Wayans Brothers, The Jamie Foxx Show, and Moesha. She also guest-starred in a 2001 episode of the Lifetime show, The Division.

[edit] Movies

Ms. Sanders had a major role in the independent urban comedy, High Freakquency. She was credited as Ajai Richards. She had a smaller role in the Michael Keaton Christmas comedy, Jack Frost. She was also in the 2000 urban independent drama, The Playaz Court.
