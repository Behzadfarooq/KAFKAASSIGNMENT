Ajalon (band)

Ajalon is a Christian progressive rock trio from Seattle. The name can be found in the book of Joshua Ch 10 verse 12. The loose meaning of Ajalon is: "Grace in the midst of the fire".

[edit] Personnel

  • Randy George - Various instruments
  • Wil Henderson - Lead vocals
  • Dan Lile - drums

[edit] Discography

  • Light at the End of the Tunnel, Hope Records - released 1996
  • On the Threshold of Eternity, Progrock Records - released 2005
