Ajaz Ahmed

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

"Ajaz Ahmed" redirects here. For the British AKQA co-founder and chairman, see AKQA.

Ajaz Ahmed (b. 1976) is a Pakistani man claiming to be the world's tallest living person. He hails from Bakkhar village in Daryakhan, Punjab, and claims to be 8 feet 4 inches.

He suffers from an orthopaedic problem that afflicted his right leg after he underwent surgery for a hip-bone fracture. He was a farmer from a poor family when he came to the notice of a Pakistani social worker, Talat Mehmood, who helped him get in touch with Qamruddin Ansari, the manager for Ghulam Shabbir, the holder of the Guinness Record for the biggest shoe size (size 74), and the late Alam Channa, the world's tallest man at the time.
