Ajaana UDonto bostu ba Aw-Oo-Baw (Bengali: অজানা উড়ন্ত বস্তু বা আ-উ-ব) (Unidentified Flying Object or UFO) was the second album of the Bangla band Moheener Ghoraguli. It was released in 1978 as a Standard Play 45 rpm disc by Hindusthan Records, and had two songs.

[edit] Track listing

  1. Ajaana UDonto bostu ba Aw-Oo-Baw (Aadho aalo aandhaaray, kono ek nawgoray...)
  2. Shudhijon Shono (Shudhu Aaj Noye Protidin)
