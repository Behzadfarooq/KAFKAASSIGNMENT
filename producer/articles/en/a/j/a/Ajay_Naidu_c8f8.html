Ajay Naidu

Ajay Naidu (born 12 February 1972 in Evanston, Illinois) is an American actor of Indian ancestry.

Naidu was raised in Chicago and is the son of Indian immigrants to the U.S.[1] As an actor, he trained with the American Repertory Theatre's Institute for Advanced Theatre Training at Harvard University.[1]

On screen, Naidu has appeared in the film Office Space, as well as K-Pax, Ï€, Requiem for a Dream, Bad Santa, The War Within, American Chai, and The Guru. He co-starred in the sitcom LateLine and had guest starring roles on the television dramas The Sopranos and The West Wing. In 1997, Naidu was nominated for an Independent Spirit Award for his role in the independent film SubUrbia.[2] He is currently engaged to actress Heather Burns.[3]
