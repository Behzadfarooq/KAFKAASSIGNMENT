Ajax is a vertically scrolling shoot 'em up arcade game released by Konami in 1987. The players controls a helicopter (in the 2D stage) and later a fighter jet (in the 3D stage), and shoots enemies in the air and bomb them on the ground, collecting power-ups and defeating bosses to advance levels.
