[edit] Importance of Bee Gees article

You just rated the Bee Gees article and placed its importance within music as "low". I was wondering whether you could expand, perhaps on the talk page, as to why you rated it as low importance? I would have thought a group such as the Bee Gees would be very important in the music category? Shadow007 15:13, 15 September 2006 (UTC)

Thanks for your reply. I tend to like to give the original rater a chance to explain themself before changing a rating they made. Thanks. Shadow007 23:48, 15 September 2006 (UTC)

Please stop rating musician articles by importance until you have grasped how this works. I'm working through the articles which use {{musician}} now, to migrate them to {{WPBiography}}, and I keep finding one ridiculous rating after another. Bands like the Bee Gees, Aerosmith, Abba and AC/DC are legends in their fields and are Top priority. How can you put the Bee Gees as low?! Barry White ("low") should be High.

Low should be bands that didn't make it, but have just about enough notability to get a Wikipedia article. We can also put reality TV show contestants who didn't win in this category. The legendary bands and artists get Top. Everyone else should be Mid or High. --kingboyk 12:52, 21 September 2006 (UTC)
