Ajattara (band)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

You have new messages (last change).

Ajattara is an atmospheric black/death/doom metal band from Finland. The band got its name from the Ajattara (or Ajatar) of Finnish mythology, which is an evil forest spirit. Ajattara uses heavy guitar sounds and synthesizers to create a grim atmosphere.

Ruoja is actually Pasi Koskinen, ex-singer of Amorphis and singer of Shape of Despair, singer/guitarist of To Separate the Flesh from the Bones and ex-singer/guitarist of St.Mucus .

At the end 2006 Ajattara's long time group broke up, when Pasi Koskinen made (for example) neo-nazi salutes and other same related political statements during the gig. The new line-up is still unknown.
