Ajam (Bahrain)

The Ajam (Arabic: عجم‎) are a community of Persians in Bahrain. Unlike the Huwala Persians in Bahrain who adhere to the Sunni sect, the Ajam of Bahrain are Twelver Shias. They have traditionally been merchants living in a specific quarter of Manama. They are fierce defenders of their Persian identity and language.
