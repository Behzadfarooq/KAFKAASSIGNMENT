[edit] My Info

My name is Alexander Jackl.

I am the Chief Architect for ESP Solutions Group and the Project Director for the Wyoming Department of Education's enterprise data system: WISE

I am the co-chair of the Technical Board of the Schools Interoperability Framework- SIF. SIF is the national k12 data standards organization.

I am, as a volunteer, a Seminar Leader for Landmark Education

I have a deep commitment to education in the world and always looking for opportunities to support and cause that!

Most important of all, I am a father of three (boy, 10; girl, 6; boy, 4), husband to one, brother to two, and friend to many!
