Ajay (Telugu: అజయ్, Hindi: अजय, Urdu: اَجے) is a Telugu film actor.

Ajay was born and raised in [[hi Ajay got his big break with Pawan Kalyan's Kushi. He later got more offers through playing the role of Mahesh Babu's friend in Okkadu.

He got married around January 2006.
