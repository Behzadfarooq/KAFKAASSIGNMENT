Who am I? What am I doing here?

I questioned myself on this prior to quitting Wikipedia on my previous account, Alexzero77. The people I made enemies with, from edit wars, beliefs, and... image copyright problems caused this. In addition, I felt that this website was doomed from the start. The pointless, excess articles no one has rarely touched, the vandalism, lack of donations, and opposition of the ability for anyone to edit.

Yet, after peeping at some of my work, I saw it loosing quality. I decided to quickly edit it, but then after seeing it reverted, I decided to log on to my old account. Before quitting, I blindly changed my password to something I would never guess, so I was screwed. I also quoted in my main page...

"Wikipedia was created by a pornographer who stole the idea from Encyclopedia Dramatica to create a wannabe-encyclopedia which caters to terminally boring entries on useless topics like higher mathematics, social psychology and international politics that really have no relevence to the internets and internet culture. All Wikipedia content could easily be found through Google and other mundane forms of reference material, with the exception of their A+ Lame Edit Wars, which contain a level of pathetic dedication to the insignificant only possible on the Internets."

This is from Encyclopedia Dramatica, a website of the Wiki family that Wikipedia itself, wants to hide. I highly disagree.

So, I'm back with a new account and wish to do the best I can in this hellhole. Or else... [I'm talking about you Captaintruth!]
