This category contains subcategories of WikiProject Aztec-related articles according to their currently assessed quality/class in the overall WP Mesoamerica article assessment and rating scheme. These subcategories form a subset of the corresponding Mesoamerica articles by quality subcategories.

Articles are automatically placed in these subcategories according to the particular class (or quality) rating assigned.

This is done by setting an appropriate value to the class parameter in the WikiProject Aztec project banner {{WP Aztec}}, which appears on these articles' talk pages). Articles should not be assigned to this category directly.

Please see the article rating and assessment scheme for more information, or enquire at the Project's main discussion board for assistance.

This category is a self-reference, and as such does not pertain to encyclopaedic content, but rather is used by the Project to categorise its workload.
