Aztec or Aztek could mean:

  • The Aztec civilization of Mesoamerica
  • The Nahuatl language, also known as "Aztec"
  • Aztec, New Mexico, a city in the United States
    • the Aztec meteorite of 1938, which fell in New Mexico, United States (see Meteorite falls)
  • Aztec-C, a programming language for a variety of platforms including MS-DOS, Apple DOS 3.3, Macintosh and Amiga
  • Aztec, a novel by Gary Jennings, ISBN 0-689-11045-6 Hardcover; ISBN 0-8125-2146-3 Paperback
  • The Aztecs, a serial in the Doctor Who television series
  • Cadbury's Aztec, a nougat-filled chocolate bar of the 1960s and 1970s.
  • The Pontiac Aztek, a sport utility vehicle
  • The Piper Aztec, a small twin-engine aircraft
  • The Aztecs, an Australian rock band of the 1960s and 1970s
  • The "Aztecs" collegiate athletics teams of San Diego State University (California, United States) (see SDSU Aztecs)
  • Aztek was a character in the DC Universe whose short-lived series led to his joining the Justice League
  • The Aztecs is a team in the card game Fightball
  • de aztec, a map in the first person shooter game Counter-Strike
  • Aztek is a rapper who is signed to Jay-Z's Roc La Familia Records
  • Aztec, a game for the Apple II personal computer
