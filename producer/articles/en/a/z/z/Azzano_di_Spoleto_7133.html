Azzano di Spoleto

Azzano is a hamlet in central Umbria, a frazione of Spoleto in the upper floodplain of the Clitunno river at 42°49N49N 12°29E, at 229 m above sea-level; its population is about 100. Traces of the ancient Roman Via Flaminia can still be made out near the village, the chief monuments of which are the three medieval churches of S. Maria, S. Paolo, and S. Lorenzo.
