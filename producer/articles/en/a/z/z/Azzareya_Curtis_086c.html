Azzareya Crystal Curtis, sometimes known simply as Azzareya, is a Hip Hop model of African American, Egyptian, and Brazilian ethnicity.

[edit] Introduction

At the age of 23 (as of December 2004), Azzareya Curtis has a number of Hip Hop music videos under her belt. She has also done modeling work with appearances in magazines, in addition to television appearances, such as Nick Cannon's Wild 'N Out.

She is also an exotic dancer with Erotic Angels NY.
