Location of Azzanello in Italy

Azzanello is a comune (municipality) in the Province of Cremona in the Italian region Lombardy, located about 60 km southeast of Milan and about 20 km northwest of Cremona. As of 31 December 2004, it had a population of 702 and an area of 11.1 km².[1]

Azzanello borders the following municipalities: Borgo San Giacomo, Casalmorano, Castelvisconti, Genivolta, Villachiara.
