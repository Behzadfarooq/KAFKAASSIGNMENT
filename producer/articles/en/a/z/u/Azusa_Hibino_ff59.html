Azusa Hibino {日比野梓} born 1990-07-26), is a Japanese model. Photographed by Garo Aida, she commonly poses in magazines with Mao Kobayashi as well as other models.


Azusa burst into the modeling scene at 13 years old when she had a movie done at the Villa Jatayu.
