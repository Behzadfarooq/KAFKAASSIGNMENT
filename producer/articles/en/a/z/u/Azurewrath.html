Azurewrath is a mythical blade in the Diablo mythos. It is an angelic runeblade wielded by Izual (Archangel Tyrael's lieutenant and close friend). Izual went into the forges of Hell to stop the forging of the evil equivilent sword Shadowfang (against Tyrael's will). Izual and his men underestimated the amount of enemies in Hell and they were overwhelmed.

The usable weapon Azurewrath is a high level weapon (requires the player to be level 85) and grants many different character buffs. Any character can use the weapon after meeting the requirements, but it is best suited for the Paladin since it grants an extra stacking aura and is a fast one-handed weapon, beneficial to Paladins.

The stats for Azurewrath include the following:

Required Dexterity: 136

+5-10 To All Attributes (varies)
Level 10-13 Sanctuary Aura When Equipped (varies)
Indestructible
+3 To Light Radius
(Ladder Only)
(Only Spawns In Patch 1.10 or later)


There is also a low-level version of the Azurewrath that is availiable in the early game.
