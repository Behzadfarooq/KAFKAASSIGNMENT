Azuga is a small resort town nestled in the mountains of Prahova county in the Wallachian region of Romania. Azuga is located at the foot of the Bucegi mountains, and contains the longest ski run in Romania, the Sorica. The town also has a famous beer factory that brews Azuga Beer.

[edit] Tourism

The town is an emerging ski resort, that (as of 2006-2007) sees tremendous development in tourism infrastructure.
