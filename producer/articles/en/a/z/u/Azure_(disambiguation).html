Azure may refer to any of the following:

  • Azure, a blue tincture used in heraldry.
  • Azure (color) is a color between blue and cyan on the color wheel.
  • An alternate term for lapis lazuli, a blue mineral
  • Azure, Montana.
  • Azure (building), a residential tower in Dallas, Texas (USA)
  • Azure (journal) is a quarterly journal published by the Shalem Center.
  • Azure (song) is a song of the Japanese Rock band, Duel Jewel.
  • Bentley Azure, a luxury car.
