Azubuike Oliseh (born 18 November 1978) is the Nigerian soccer midfielder, currently playing for AEK Larnaca FC in the Cypriot First Division, the highest football league in Cyprus. He is the younger brother of ex Nigeria captain Sunday Oliseh and the older brother of former AS Nancy and QPR midfielder Egutu Oliseh.
