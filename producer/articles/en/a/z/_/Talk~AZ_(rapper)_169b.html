[edit] Cult following?

I know he's got a few intense fans, but does cult following really make sense here? Read the definition of a cult... Fans of AZ's music hardly qualify as cult leaders.


I think the phrase "cult following" is generally used in a way that's distinct from the meaning of "cult." Like various campy B-movies are said to have "cult followings" but that doesn't mean there's an actual cult based around the movie; just that they have a small but highly dedicated (maybe obsessive) fanbase that sticks around over time.

I'm not sure that meaning really applies to AZ either though.

[edit] E-40 Beef?

Anyone know that happened and should it be added? Brunbb 10:59, 21 October 2006 (UTC)

back in 1996, AZ was interviewed in a magazine and had said that he doesn't thing E-40 can rap and that E-40 doesn't deserve the respect that he gets. E-40 fired back at him in october of that year on his "hall of game" album with "record haters". 40 basically dissed AZ and said that "I was selling tapes out my trunk while you were runnin around drinkin Similac." and "all in your fake a-- videos knowing damn well you ain't have no mils". AZ never responded, as to why, i'm not sure. it would've been good lyrically but oh well. i take it they squashed the beef by now.
