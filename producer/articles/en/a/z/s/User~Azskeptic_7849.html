Azskeptic is a consumer advocate who speaks and writes on international medical school issues. He is the state medical board moderator for www.valuemd.com and has spoke in front of the Administrators in medicine and in front of state medical boards plus international boards. His concern is fraud and questionable medical schools.

his website is www.internetmedicalschool.homestead.com
