Azmaria Hendric

Azmaria Hendric is a fictional character from the Chrono Crusade anime and manga series. She is known as the Apostle of Charity and has an amazing singing voice.

[edit] History

Spoiler warning: Plot and/or ending details follow.

[edit] Early life

Azmaria was born in Fátima, Portugal on March 12, 1912. She immigrated to the United States after her parents died in World War I. There she traveled with a band of singers. After a while, a man named Ricardo Henderic took Azmaria away from them. The band of singers was killed off by a demon. She was then adopted by Ricardo Henderic. Ricardo regarded Azmaria as a mere tool for his own selfish purposes. He charged Viscount Lerajie to be her enforcer and as a symbol of her imprisonment.

[edit] Rescue

Years passed and one night Chrono and Rosette made their way into Ricardo’s casino on a mission. As Azmaria hid in a maintenance closet, Rosette suddenly barged in and introduced herself as a friend even though Azmaria was clearly fearful of her presence. The attendants found the two. Azmaria begged Rosette to run, but Rosette had other plans for the girl.

The two escaped by breaking out of a window. Viscount Lerajie tried to stop them and ended up destroying part of the building, causing it to collapse. When the dust cleared, numerous people were hurt. Saddened and thinking that this was her fault, she started to use her power by singing. As her angel wings grew, a shining light emulated from her and the people touching it were instantly healed. Though she had this power to heal people, she always treated her power as a curse. The power, she believes, would cause misfortune and bring bad things to her and those close to her.

Azmaria spent her time at the Order. While under protective custody, she remains to be a helpful figure, trying to win favor by helping people. She overheard a conversation between Father Remington and Sister Kate in which they mentioned that the band in which Azmaria used to sing were all dead. Azmaria ran away, crying. When Rosette and Chrono went to her, she pretended that nothing was happening. Seeing that she was crying, Rosette took her to a nearby lake to talk and to have fun.

Azmaria and friends after the battle

After a long discussion between Rosette and Azmaria, Viscount found their location and seriously wounded Chrono. In order to save both Chrono and Rosette, Azmaria agreed to go with Viscount. Viscount then took her to the blimp in which it was manned by Ricardo and his men as he planned to use Azmaria to call upon the Astral Line and re-energize his body.

The Astral Line opened and Viscount betrayed Ricardo at the last minute. As Ricardo’s tried to restrain Viscount to no avail, and as his body turned into dust, Viscount prepared to attack Azmaria. However Chrono, in his transformed state, attacked Viscount and the two battled. Rosette soon arrived at the scene and explained to Azmaria about the situation.

After a fierce battle, Chrono was able to overcome Viscount and the blimp started to explode. Suddenly, Azmaria started to grow wings and with Rosette and Chrono flew over to safety.

After the battle, Azmaria had lost her power temporarily and was unable to use them until later in the series.

[edit] Friends and behavior

Azmaria usually hangs out with Chrono and Rosette and with Rosette’s friends. She looks up to Rosette as someone she wants to be like when she grows up.

She is an extremely helpful person and is always thinking about other people. Her favourite word is 'sorry' as she is apologetic to everyone when situations occur. She has a beautiful soprano voice and she enjoys her singing hobby.

[edit] Anime ending

Azmaria Hendric: Years Later

Azmaria was extremely close to Chrono and Rosette, and upon learning of their deaths, was devastated. After being alone for a few minutes, she finally understood that Rosette and Chrono were finally at peace, and was happy for them.

In the final scenes of the anime, Azmaria has apparently joined the Magdalene Order. She lays the pocket watch by Rosette’s tomb, a sign that Azmaria still cares for two of her best friends.

[edit] Manga version

In the manga, Azmaria and all other Apostles lose their powers due to the Battle of Pandaemonium. She created a movie about the life and events after the battle in the Astraline for Satella. There was a frame in the manga which shows Azmaria and Joshua holding hands with rings on their fingers, which suggested that they were married. In the manga, it is shown that she has grown to old age, and had established a Hendric Foundation, which her grandson continued after her death and has unfrozen Satella.
