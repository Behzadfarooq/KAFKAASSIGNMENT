Azrieal a professional wrestler (born September 23, 1981) who currently works in Jersey All-Pro Wrestling, Full Impact Pro and Pro Wrestling Unplugged. He was also a member of Special K (under the name Angeldust) in Ring of Honor. He apparently took his name from Azrael, the Angel of Death.

[edit] History

Azrieal lost his Jersey All Pro Wrestling Light Heavyweight title to Jay Lethal in a four-way match at Revolution on December 10th, 2004, after Lethal hit a Spinning Butterlfy Brainbuster. The two faced off again four months later at the next JAPW show, Awesome Sacrifice, in a No Disqualification match, which saw Lethal retain his belt. At JAPW's 7th Anniversary show on September 18th, 2004, Azrieal got another chance at the Light Heavyweight belt in a ladder match that saw the two combatants brawl all over the arena, at one point fighting in front of Lethal's ever attendant parents. Azrieal felt the need to throw a cup of water on Mr. Lethal and received a slap from the old man for his troubles, eventually losing the match. For this reason, Azrieal sought legal action against Mr. Lethal and the Lethals were not allowed to attend shows.

Azrieal and Lethal would later go on to feud over JAPW's Heavyweight title as well.

[edit] Japan


Azrieal recently completed a tour of Japan with the Dragon Gate promotion.
