[edit] Genetically enhanced?

Does anybody know if it was ever actually stated that Azrael was genetically enhanced? From my reading, it seems as if an Azrael feotus is raised in animal amniotic fluids and fed animal nutrients, which might have some effect on muscle growth or something (I have honestly no clue what it would do), but it definitely wouldn't have an affect on his genes.D1Puck1T 16:29, 22 June 2006 (UTC)

According to Azrael #30, the guy had traces of animal genes in his DNA, so he is genetically enhanced. --Neodammerung 03:53, 1 July 2006 (UTC)
