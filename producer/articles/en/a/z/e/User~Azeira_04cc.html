[edit] About Me

Indie rock elitists seem to have it that all you need to know about people are their musical tastes, as the first thing they'll ask you is what your favorite act is, and depending on how obscure your answer is, they'll either peer down their noses at you, make a condescending noise, and walk derisively away (if it is known to more than 50 people) or embrace you as their new soulmate in hipness. Therefore, I shall divulge no personal details about myself apart from this list of a few albums that I favor (in chronological order, because that is the only way a proper elitist should organize a music collection):

The Beatles - Abbey Road

David Bowie - Aladdin Sane

Queen - Sheer Heart Attack

Pink Floyd - Wish You Were Here

(Yes, they're all British and from the '70s. Shut up.)
