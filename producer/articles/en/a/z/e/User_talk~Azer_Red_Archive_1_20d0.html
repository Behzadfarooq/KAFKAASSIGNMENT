[edit] American Nihilist Underground Society

Have you heard the expression "stub-ifiy" used with regards to a wikipedia article? It consists of taking out everything that cannot be supported by a reliable source and placing the {{stub}} tag at the bottom of the page. It's exactly what every article should look like anyway, but for those times when you're concerned about promotion/aggrandizement/succor-to-troll/whatever it is particulary useful. It's also almost bullet-proof: The removal of uncited material is chapter and verse for one of the foundation policies of wikipedia.
brenneman 00:20, 2 January 2007 (UTC)
