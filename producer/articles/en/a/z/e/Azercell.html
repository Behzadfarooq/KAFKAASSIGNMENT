Azercell

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Azercell Telecom is a mobile telecommunications company, located in Baku, Azerbaijan.

[edit] Founders

Azercell Telecom was established on January 19th 1996. The co-founders were Turkcell İletişim Hizmetleri A.S. and the Ministry of Communications of Azerbaijan Republic.

[edit] Beginning of Service

Azercell Telecom started its providing Azerbaijan with postpaid mobile telecommunications on December 15, 1996. In 1998, a prepaid system was launched with a tariff package "SimSim".

As being the leader of the market and very dynamic company it’s known as the trend setter in the mobile telecommunications market in Azerbaijan, applying new technologies and offering various tariff packages, products and services to its customers.

As of February 2007, 78% of the Azerbaijan Republic was under Azercell coverage and has a total suscriber base of 2 333 387.

[edit] Director

The General director of Azercell Telecom Joint Venture is A. Halim Atesh.
