Azerbaijani diplomatic missions

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

You have new messages (last change).

Azerbaijan has a number of diplomatic missions in Europe, Asia and the Middle East. Notably it does not have an embassy in Armenia, a neighbouring country with which it has been in conflict.
