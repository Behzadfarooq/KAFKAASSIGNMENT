Azerbaijan Cup

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Azerbaijan Cup is a major football (soccer) competition in Azerbaijan. In its original form, it started in 1936, when Azerbaijan was a republic of the Soviet Union; it was not disputed by Azeri teams in the Soviet league pyramid.

[edit] Cup Winners

  • 1992: Inshaatchi Baku 2-1 (EX) vs. Kur Mingachevir
  • 1992-1993: FK Qarabag Agdam 1-0 (EX) vs. Inshaatchi Sabirabad
  • 1993-1994: FK Kapaz Gandja 2-0 vs. FK Khazar Lenkoran
  • 1994-1995: FK Neftchi Bakou 1-0 vs. Kur-Nur Mingachevir
  • 1995-1996: FK Neftchi Bakou 3-0 vs. FK Qarabag Agdam
  • 1996-1997: FK Kapaz Gandja 1-0 vs. Khazri Buzovna
  • 1997-1998: FK Kapaz Gandja 2-0 vs. FK Qarabag Agdam
  • 1998-1999: FK Neftchi Baku 0-0 (5-4 after kick-off penalty) vs. FK Shamkir
  • 1999-2000: FK Kapaz Gandja 2-1 vs. FK Qarabag Agdam
  • 2000-2001: Safa Bakou 2-1 vs. FK Neftchi Baku
  • 2001-2002: FK Neftchi Baku xxx FK Shamkir (FK Shamkir retired)
  • 2002-2003: not played
  • 2003-2004: FK Neftchi Baku 1-0 FK Shamkir
  • 2004-2005: FK Baku 2-1 (EX) vs. FK Inter Baku
