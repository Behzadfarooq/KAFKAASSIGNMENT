Azela Jacqueline Robinson Cañedo (born August 26, 1965 in London, England) is a Mexican actress.

[edit] Biography

Born in the United Kingdom to an English father and a Mexican mother, Azela Robinson studied in the Old Vin Drama Workshop (1980 - 1982) and soon after, she enrolled in the National Theatre Workshop (1982 - 1983). Years later, She had to leave her country and her family behind to seek new job opportunities. That is when she arrived in Mexico, where she began her career as an actress and participated in countless telenovelas. The television has not been the only medium in which the actress has developed therefore she has also appeared in films and on stage. In 2005, Azela returned to the small-screen in the telenovela Contra viento y marea,in the role of "Apolonia".
