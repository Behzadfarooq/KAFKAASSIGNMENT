[edit] Speedy deletions

First, other editors decide whether an article is a candidate for speedy deletion, I must decide whether they were right. In MinnesotaLindy case, with 25,000 Google-hits, and with sentences like

  • "Minnesota is also home to several regular great dancing joints. "
  • "While this is primarily a restaurant, they have a small floor and allow us to dance to great bands"

, I was convinced that it is not notable enough to have an article on Wikipedia. In the case of SwingMonkey, with 938 Google-hits, it is not notable enough. Please read Wikipedia:Notability.

The system works fine, if you don't agree with me, you can request the undeletion of the articles here. Or if you want to, I can restore the whole page for you to be able to work on it. Thank you for your appreciation. NCurse work 20:22, 30 October 2006 (UTC)

PS: I don't have to be an expert of Lindy Hop, I must know our policies. That's why I've made these deletions. NCurse work 20:25, 30 October 2006 (UTC)
