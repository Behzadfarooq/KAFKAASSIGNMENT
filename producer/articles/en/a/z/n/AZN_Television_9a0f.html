AZN Television promotes itself as "the network for Asian America." It is run by International Networks which in turn is a wholly owned subsidiary of Comcast Corporation. The channelâ€™s programming targets the fast-growing, young, affluent and English-speaking Asian American community. Genres include the most popular Asian films, dramas, documentaries, music and anime as well as a diverse slate of original programming. It competes in certain markets with ImaginAsian Television.
