Aznar II Galíndez was a Count of Aragón 867 – 893, son and successor of Galindo I Aznárez.

Married with a daughter of the king of Pamplona Garcia Iñíguez, has two children: his successor, Galindo II Aznárez, and Sancha who married Muhammad al Tawil, wali of Huesca.

Deceased in 893.
