Please do not remove speedy deletion tags from articles. If you do not believe the article deserves to be deleted, then please place {{hangon}} on the page and make your case on the article's talk page. Administrators will look at your reasoning before deciding what to do with the article. Thank you. AmiDaniel (talk) 08:31, 20 May 2006 (UTC)

As I see you've now removed the PROD tag from the article as well without any explanation for your actions, I thought I'd drop you a brief note explaining why the topic does not merit inclusion before I bring the matter to WP:AFD. Wikipedia upholds strict policies of inclusion based upon a subject's notability. For a band like Forever Darkness to merit inclusion on Wikipedia, it must have received air-time on a reputable radio station, released notable albums, or received press coverage. According to the article, Forever Darkness has not yet even released a demo disc, and its only reference is a MySpace profile. Please read over Wikipedia's notability requirements for musical ensembles. I wish your band luck; however, Wikipedia is not a vessel for self-promotion and advertising, but rather it is an encyclopedia. I'm going to wait a day before bringing this to WP:AfD, to see if you may be able to address these concerns. If you have any questions about the deletion process on Wikipedia, or why the band Forever Darkness is non-notable by Wikipedia's standards, please contact me. Thanks.
