Aznil bin Hj Nawawi or better known as Aznil Hj Nawawi (born November 6, 1962) is a TV host in Malaysia. He is now 44 years old and have been hosting for 16 years. He graduated from University of Malaya with a Bachelor of Economics. He had two children, a girl and a boy. He is also an actor and a producer of his own talkshow. He started his career in 1990 as a broadcaster and a media reporter with TV3. He has been working with almost all TV stations in Malaysia since his involvement in media industry. He gain back his popularity since joining Akademi Fantasia, one of the most successful reality TV show as the host and is now consider as the top host in Malaysia.
