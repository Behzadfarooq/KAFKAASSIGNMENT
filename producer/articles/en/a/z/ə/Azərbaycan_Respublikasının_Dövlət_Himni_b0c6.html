Azərbaycan Respublikasının Dövlət Himni is the national anthem of Azerbaijan, the original title of which is March of Azerbaijan (Azərbaycan marşı). The words were written by the poet Ahmed Javad, and the music was composed by the legendary Azeri composer Uzeyir Hajibeyov.
