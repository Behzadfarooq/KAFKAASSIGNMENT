Hi, I'm AZCactus1 hailing obviously from the great state of Arizona. I'm a huge fan of everything PAC-10 (it is the Conference of Champions), especially Arizona, UCLA, and 'SC.

I am a graduate of Georgetown University in Washington, DC (Hoya Saxa!), yes, just like Bill Clinton. And I've heard all the jokes like, "Georgetown isn't exactly Ivy, it's more like Moss." It's a great place with a beautiful campus...highly recommended.

Best beaches I've ever experienced: The Golden Corridor between Cabo San Lucas and San Jose del Cabo on the Baja in Mexico.

Most scenic drive: The Boston Mountains of northwestern Arkansas and the Green Mountains of Vermont.

Most inspiring political leader: Winston Churchill with his speeches to the British Parliament during and around the time of the Battle of Britain (summer 1940).
