Azilises
Indo-Scythian king
Silver tetradrachm of Azilises (9.38 g), minted in Gandhara.
Reign Indo-Scythians: 57 BC - 35 BC
Predecessor Azes I
Successor Azes II

Azilises was an Indo-Scythian king who ruled in the area of Gandhara.
