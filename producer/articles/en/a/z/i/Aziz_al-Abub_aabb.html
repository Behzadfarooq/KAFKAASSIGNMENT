Aziz al-Abub (also known as Ibrahim al-Nadhir or al-Nahdhir, Arabic: ابراهيم النظير) Lebanese Hezbollah psychiatrist and mind control expert.

Aziz is a graduate of the Soviet Union's People's Friendship University.

Aziz al-Abub used drugs and physical torture on hostage William Buckley over a period of 444 days during his captivity in Beirut.
