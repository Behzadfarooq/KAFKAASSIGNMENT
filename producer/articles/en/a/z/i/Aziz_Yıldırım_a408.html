Aziz Yıldırım

Aziz Yıldırım (born 2 November 1952 in Diyarbakır, Turkey) is currently the 52nd Chairman of Türkiye Süper Ligi club Fenerbahçe SK. He is currently serving his fourth two-year term since coming to power.

Chairman of Turkish Super League team Fenerbahçe. During his presidence he made significant changes in the club's structure and infrastructure; Şükrü Saracoğlu Stadium was extended to 52.500, %15 of the club went public and Fenerbahçe name became a trademark. He started Fenerium chain manifacturing Fenerbahçe's official products. There are 45 Fenerium stores in Turkey. For now more than 52.500 Fenerbahçe cards are sold and the target number is 100.000 for this year which is the 100th year of Fenerbahçe Sports Club. The average income is expected to be US$20.000.000 from these Supporter Cards.He has put the club in a very competitive position with regard to Galatasaray and Besiktas.
