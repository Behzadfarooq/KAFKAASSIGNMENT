The Aziz Bhatti Park is located in Gulshan Town in Karachi, Sindh, Pakistan. Aziz Bhatti Park was named after Major Aziz Bhatti of Punjab Regiment, Pakistan Army. Major Bhatti valiantly defended Burki sector of Lahore on 6 September 1965, against Indian attack in the Indo-Pakistani War of 1965.
