Azita Hajian

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Azita Hijian (Persian: آزیتا حاجیان ‎ , born 1957 in Tehran) is an Iranian actress.

Graduated in theatre acting and directing from the Faculty of Dramatic Arts in Tehran, Azita Hajian started stage acting in 1975 and film acting in 1989 with Thief of Dolls (Mohamad Reza Honarmand). Due to her theatrical background in the field of children and teenagers, her first films were in the same field. She has also played in some TV series.

[edit] Selected filmography

  • The Extraordinary Journey, 1990
  • Avinar, 1991
  • Dayanbakh, 1992
  • The Path to Glory
  • The Snowman, 1994 (released in ’97)
  • The Red Ribbon, 1998
  • The Dead Wave, 2000.
