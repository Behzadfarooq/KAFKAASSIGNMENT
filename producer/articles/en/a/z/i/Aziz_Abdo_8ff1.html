Aziz Abdo
Background information
Origin Beirut, Lebanon
Genre(s) World, Arabic
Occupation(s) Singer
Website www.azizabdo.com

Aziz Abdo (Arabic:عزيز عبدو) is a Lebanese singer of Arab Music. Born in Beirut during the Lebanese Civil War, he moved to Sweden as a young boy and at age 16 became active in modeling. After earning the 2001 Mr. Lebanon contest, Abdo started recording songs and eventually became popular in the Lebanese music scene.
