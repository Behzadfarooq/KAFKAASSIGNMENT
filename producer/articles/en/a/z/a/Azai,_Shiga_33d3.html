Azai (浅井町; -cho) was a town located in Higashiazai District, Shiga Prefecture, Japan.

As of 2003, the town had an estimated population of 13,055 and a density of 149.90 persons per km². The total area was 87.09 km².

[edit] Sister town

  • Wentworth, New South Wales, Australia

[edit] Merger

On February 13, 2006, the town of Azai merged into the city of Nagahama.
