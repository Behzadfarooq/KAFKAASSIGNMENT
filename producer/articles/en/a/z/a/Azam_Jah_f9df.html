Nawab Mir Himayat Ali Khan, Azam Jah, Prince of Berar (1907-1970) was the eldest son of the seventh and last Nizam of Hyderabad, Mir Osman Ali Khan Asif Jah VII.

His wife was Princess Durr-e-Shevar who was one of the daughters of Abdul Mejid II, the last Caliph and Sultan of the Ottoman Empire. His oldest son, Barkat Ali Khan Mukarram Jah is the current pretender to the throne of Hyderabad.
