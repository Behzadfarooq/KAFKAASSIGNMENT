[edit] Information

Azasuke Wind is a popular Internet based Hentai artist from Japan. His artwork can be seen on many Japanese and Hentai image boards, most notably more popular boards such as 4Chan. His artwork generally involves characters from popular doujinshi and manga comics, put into pornographic situations. He is currently one of the most popular Hentai artists on the Internet, and his site has received well over 15 million hits, currently sitting at 15,943,597.

[edit] Artwork

Stylistically, Azasuke doesn't stick to a particular genre, or fetish. Art can range from Guro, Scat, Bondage, Rape through to plain, consentual intercourse.

Characters drawn by Azasuke Wind are varied. Some notable examples include:

  • Nina Williams from the Tekken Series.
  • Tifa Lockhart from Final Fantasy VII.
  • Lei-Fang from the Dead or Alive series.
  • Ivy from the Soul Calibur series.

And more recently Revy from the Black Lagoon Manga/Anime series.
