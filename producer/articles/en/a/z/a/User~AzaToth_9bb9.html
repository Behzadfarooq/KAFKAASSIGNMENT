[edit] What I have done

Or I could say What have I done!?. First and most I have been doing some work in the template namespace, I have created the following templates:

  • Template:Qif (edit|talk|history|links|watch|logs)
  • Template:Switch (edit|talk|history|links|watch|logs)
  • Template:Booleq (edit|talk|history|links|watch|logs) and boolne
  • Template:Booland (edit|talk|history|links|watch|logs) and boolor, boolxor, boolnot, boolnand, boolnxor, boolnor
  • Template:Exists (edit|talk|history|links|watch|logs) as generalization
  • Template:If defined (edit|talk|history|links|watch|logs) and undefined
  • Template:Ifndef (edit|talk|history|links|watch|logs) and the now removed ifdef
  • The now deleted foreach, see m:Template:Foreach
  • Template:Tfd top (edit|talk|history|links|watch|logs) and bottom, also debate top and bottom as generalization
  • Template:Tdeprecated (edit|talk|history|links|watch|logs)
  • Template:Esoteric (edit|talk|history|links|watch|logs)
  • and perhaps some more that I have forgot about

Major work has been done to

  • Template:Infobox Aircraft (edit|talk|history|links|watch|logs)
  • Template:Infobox University (edit|talk|history|links|watch|logs)

As for articles there are no specific article that I have worked extensivly on, so that's blank here for now.
