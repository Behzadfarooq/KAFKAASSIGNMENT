Azadi
Studio album by Junoon
Released 1997

Azadi (Urdu: آزادی) was an album released by Pakistani rock band, Junoon. It was released in 1997. It was a patriotic album that expressed the Partition of India during the year of 1947. The album was a huge success and created a huge reputation for Junoon. There were many singles on this album that were chart toppers that made it a very successful album in Pakistan.
