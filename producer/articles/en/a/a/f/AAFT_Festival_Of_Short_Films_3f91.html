Asian Academy Of Film And Television, a film and TV institute in India has launched its International Film and Television Club under the leadership of world renowned film maker Sandeep Marwah from the film city Noida. The organisation has its annual programme of having festival of short films.The festival is divided into two parts. First the films made in India and the other one is films made all over the world.The institution has 63 centres all over the world and the alumni of the academy handles its business every year.

Its been 13 years since this festival has been attracting the film makers of all sorts from all over the world.The next film festival is going to be held in the month of Feb.07 at Marwah Studios popular as An Island Of Excellence.

The most interesting part of the whole event is that the winner gets an opportunity to make another short film at the cost of the centre. The complete infrastructure of film and TV is at the disposal of that film maker.The organisation believes that cash prices can never cover the cost of the new film.A creative person must be given more chances to show there calibre.
