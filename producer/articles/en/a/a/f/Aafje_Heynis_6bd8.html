Aafje Heynis

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

You have new messages (last change).

Aafje Heynis (born 2 May 1924) is a Dutch contralto. In 1961, she was awarded the Harriet Cohen International Music Award. A tea rose, hybridised by Buisman 1964, was named after her.
