[edit] Human Rights Watch

HRW provides no evidence whatsoever for their claim that Siddiqui "may have once been held" in a CIA prison. On the contrary, they emphasize that info about CIA programs is hard to get, from which they deduce that there may be other secret detainees. There might. And there might be a crashed UFO stored at Roswell.

They say that Anas al-Liby too "may have once been held" by CIA. If he was, he still is. Personally I doubt that HRW knows anything at all about Anas al-Liby. In the case of Siddiqui, HRW's claim might be based on bombastic fiction that they have read in Pakistani newspapers. I've read it too.
