Aaftab Shivdasani

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Aftab Shivdasani (born 25 June 1978 at Mumbai, India) is an Indian film actor.

[edit] Biography

Aftab Shivdasani graduated from HR College of Commerce in Mumbai. Aaftab was a fairly well-known child star appearing in films such as Mr India, Shahenshah, and Insaniyat but first broke ice in Bollywood with a starring role in 1999's Mast. In this Ram Gopal Verma-directed film, he starred opposite Hindi actress Urmila Matondkar. Nevertheless, Aaftab's film career is not considered extremely successful. This could be attributed to his choice of roles and films as well as his shying away from serious characters. Despite these setbacks, he has appeared in hits like Awara Paagal Deewana (2002), Hungama (2003) and Masti (2004). Aftab also won the best new comer award for his debutant movie Mast in 1999. Aaftab's height is 5'11.

[edit] Personal life

Aftab's parents are of different religious backgrounds. His mother is Muslim and his father is Hindu. He is also of half Sindhi descent. Shivdasani is currently said to be dating Czech actor Yana Gupta.
