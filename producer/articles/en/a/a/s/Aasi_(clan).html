Aasi (clan)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

“Aasi” redirects here. For other uses, see Aasi (disambiguation).

Aasi is a subtribe of major Punjabi Rajput/ Jutt clan: the Chadhars of central Punjab. Aasi people are mostly the inhabitants of districts Sargodha, Jhang, Mandi Bahauddin, and tehsil Sarai Alamgir of district Gujrat.

Aasis are mostly the rustic inhabitants of the rural hinterland of district Sargodha in general and tehsil Bhalwal of district Sargodha in particular. Uppi Rawana is one of the major hamlets of Aasis.
