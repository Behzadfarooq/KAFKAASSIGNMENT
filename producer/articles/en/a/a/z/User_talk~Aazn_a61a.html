[edit] re: Comments at the top versus comments at the bottom

I guess it's a matter of convention. In email, replies usually show up at the top and the mail thread accumulates below. On Wikipedia talk pages, the convention is to add new comments at the bottom, so you get chronological order if you read from top to bottom. Jim Douglas 20:49, 14 September 2006 (UTC)
