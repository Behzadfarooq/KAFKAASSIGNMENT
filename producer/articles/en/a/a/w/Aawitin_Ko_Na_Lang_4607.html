Aawitin Ko Na Lang is the sixth studio album by Ariel Rivera. The album was released during the Teen pop/Bubblegum pop explosion. In the Philippines, Ariel showed no signs of slowing down. His album proved to be successful earning him another #1 album and earned him a Gold album.
