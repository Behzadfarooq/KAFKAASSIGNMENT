Aavakaaya

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Aavakaaya is a variety of Indian Pickle originated in Andhra Pradesh in India. The main ingredients are raw, fully mature, fiberous, very sour mangos and a combination of spices.

The mangos are cut into eight or ten pieces with the stone intact. Now, the chopped mangos are washed and completely dried. They are then pickled in porcelain jars with gingelly oil, salt and a mixture of chilli powder, turmeric powder, mustard powder and fenugreek powder. The mixture is then allowed to mature for over four to eight weeks, taking care to periodically mixing the content to ensure uniform marinade.

This pickle is very popular in Southern India, especially in the states of Andhra Pradesh and Tamil Nadu.
