AAE or AaE is a three-letter acronym that may refer to:

  • Above Aerodrome Elevation, an altitude given as above the nearest aerodrome or airport
  • American Association of Endodontists, a professional organization
  • Australian Air Express, a cargo airline based in Melbourne, Australia
  • IATA airport code for Rabah Bitat Airport in Annaba, Algeria
  • ICAO airline designator for Express Air in the United States
  • Affirmative Action Employer, in advertisements
  • Armando Alejandro Estrada, stage name of professional wrestling manager, Hazim Ali
  • Anthropomorphic Arts and Education
  • All About Eve
