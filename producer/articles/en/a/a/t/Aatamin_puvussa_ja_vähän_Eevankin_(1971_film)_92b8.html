Aatamin puvussa ja vähän Eevankin was a 1971 Finnish comedy film directed and written by Matti Kassila starring Heikki Kinnunen and Juha Hyppönen. It ran for 88 minutes.

[edit] Plot Summary

Two young men, Heikki and Jussi, and the local railway stationmaster cause havoc in a small village
