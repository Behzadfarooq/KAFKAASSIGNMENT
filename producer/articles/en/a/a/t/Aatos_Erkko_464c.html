Aatos Erkko

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Aatos Juho Michel Erkko (born 16 September 1933 in Helsinki) is a Finnish journalist and the main owner of SanomaWSOY and its Helsingin Sanomat newspaper. Erkko has a Master of Science degree and he has studied journalism in the Columbia College of Columbia University. Aatos Erkko is married to Mrs. Jane Erkko. His grandfather Eero Erkko was a Finnish journalist and founder of the Helsingin Sanomat newspaper.

In 2005, Erkko was listed in the tabloid Iltalehti as the richest man in Finland, with a (taxable) fortune of 192 million euros, falling to position #11 in 2006 with 52 million euros. His true wealth is considerably larger, as he after a large sale of stock on the 1.3.2005 owned directly or indirectly 24,06% of all the Sanoma WSOY stocks, which at the current trading price (10 February 2007) alone amounts to a worth of more than 800 million euros.[1]
