Aatamin puvussa ja vähän Eevankin (1931 film)

Aatamin puvussa ja vähän Eevankin was the original 1931 Finnish comedy film directed and written by Jaakko Korhonen based on the novel by Yrjö Soini. The film lasted 104 minutes.
