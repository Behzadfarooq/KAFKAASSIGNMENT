Aatma

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

You have new messages (last change).

Aatma is a religious series broadcast on STAR Utsav in which devotees of ISKCON discuss shlokas of the Bhagavad Gita. Each shloka is explained and interpreted in relation to current daily life.
