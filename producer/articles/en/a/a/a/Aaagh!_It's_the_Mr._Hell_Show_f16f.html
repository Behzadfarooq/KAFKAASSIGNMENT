Aaagh! It's The Mr Hell Show was an adult animated comedy show which ran for one season of thirteen episodes in 2001/2002, produced by a British-Canadian collaboration. The basic format was a series of sketches linked by the eponymous Mr. Hell, a Satan-esque host voiced by comedian Bob Monkhouse.

Notable characters in the series include Josh, who would like to talk to you about reincarnation, and Serge the seal of death, who wants to take revenge on the fashion industry for killing his parents.

The Complete Series DVD with all 13 episodes and extras was released on April 02, 2007 distributed in UK by MVM Films. There will be both a PAL and NTSC release.
