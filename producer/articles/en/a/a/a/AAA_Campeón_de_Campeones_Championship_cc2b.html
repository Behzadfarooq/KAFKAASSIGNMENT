The AAA Campeón de Campeones (Champion of Champions) Championship is a championship contested for in the Mexican lucha libre promotion Asistencia Asesoría y Administración (AAA). As the name of the title suggests, the holder was to be held in regard as a higher-regarded champion among all of the AAA title holders.
