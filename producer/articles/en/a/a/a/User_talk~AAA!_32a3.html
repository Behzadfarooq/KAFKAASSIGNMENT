Hi, AAA!, and Welcome to Wikipedia!
I go by the nick Werdna here, but you can call me Andrew if you like. Thanks for deciding to get involved in Wikipedia, the largest encyclopedia in the world, created entirely by volunteers, and that anyone can edit. I just thought I'd drop by your talk page to say Hi, and to give you a few pointers on working around Wikipedia. One of the most important things is to sign your posts on talk pages, with four tildes (~~~~). This helps us figure out who said what and when on the talk pages. Another thing you should do is to fill in the edit summary field, which is just above the Save page button. We have a policy of not biting the newbies — please make an effort to know and understand our policies and guidelines, but if you don't understand them, just ignore them, and use your own common sense. There's stacks for you to do here, so I'll leave you with a few pointers as to where you can get started. — Werdna talk criticism 06:26, 14 September 2006 (UTC)
