AAA Americas Trios Championship

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The AAA Americas Trios Championship is the six-man tag team title contested for in the Mexican lucha libre promotion Asistencia Asesoría y Administración (AAA).

[edit] Title history

Wrestler: Times: Date: Location: Notes:
Los Villanos
(Villano III, Villano IV and Villano V)		 1 March 8, 1996 Nezahualcóyotl, México Defeated Cien Caras, Heavy Metal and Latin Lover to become the first champions.
Guardia, Oficial and Vigilante 1 November 19, 1996 Naucalpan, México Defeated Cien Caras, Heavy Metal and Latin Lover to become the first champions.
Los Villanos 2 1996 Naucalpan, México Los Villanos are still recognized as champions as of their last defense on June 1, 1997 in Naucalpan, México, however none of the three currently work for AAA.
