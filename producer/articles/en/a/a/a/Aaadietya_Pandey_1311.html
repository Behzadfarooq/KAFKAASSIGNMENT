Aaadietya Pandey

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Pt. Aaadietya Pandey (born on January 30, 1985) is an astrological numerologist. He is practices the pseudoscientific fields of physiognomy, graphology, psychoanalysis, Chinese and Japanese numerology and in Vedas and Rudrakshas. His comments and predictions have been printed in newspapers like The Times of India, Dainik Jagran. His columns are regularly published on sites like oneindia.com, boloji.com, tajonline.com

He is an ardent devotee of Maa Kaali.
