AAA Reina de Reinas Championship

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The AAA Reina de Reinas (Queen of Queens) Championship is a women's title contested for by in the Mexican lucha libre promotion Asistencia Asesoría y Administración (AAA). It is on the line yearly in AAA's Reina de Reinas Tournament, as well as defended regularly.

[edit] Reina de Reinas

  • 1999 – Xóchitl Hamada
  • 2000 – Rossy Moreno
  • 2001 – Lady Apache
  • 2002 – Esther Moreno
  • 2003 – Martha Villalobos
  • 2004 – No tournament held
  • 2005 – Tiffany
  • 2006 – Miss Janeth

[edit] Title history

Wrestler: Times: Date: Location: Notes:
Xóchitl Hamada 1 February 19, 1999 Puebla, Puebla Defeated Miss Janeth, Rossy Moreno and Esther Moreno in the four-way final of the first Reina de Reinas tournament to become the first champion.
Esther Moreno 1 October 6, 1999 San Luis Potosí, San Luis Potosí
Title vacated Moreno vacated the title due to injury.
Rossy Moreno 1 February 20, 2000 Morelia, Michoacán Defeated Xóchitl Hamada, Martha Villalobos and Miss Janeth in the four-way final of the second Reina de Reinas tournament to win the vacant title.
Lady Apache 1 February 17, 2001 Veracruz, Veracruz Defeated Miss Janeth, Tiffany, and Alda Moreno in the four-way final of the third Reina de Reinas tournament to win the title.
Esther Moreno 2 February 23, 2002 Veracruz, Veracruz Defeated Miss Janeth, Martha Villalobos, and Lady Apache in the four-way final of the fourth Reina de Reinas tournament to win the title.
Martha Villalobos 1 May 4, 2002 Reynosa, Tamaulipas Defeated Lady Apache in the final of the fifth-annual Reina de Reinas tournament in February 2003 to win the title.
Tiffany 1 2003–2004
Lady Apache 2 February 1, 2004 Zapopan, Jalisco
Tiffany 2 February 20, 2005 Mexico City, Distrito Federal Defeated Lady Apache in the final of the sixth Reina de Reinas tournament to win the title.
Miss Janeth 1 February 18, 2006 Orizaba, Veracruz Defeated Martha Villalobos, Cynthia Moreno and La Diabólica in the four-way elimination final of the seventh Reina de Reinas tournament to win the title.
