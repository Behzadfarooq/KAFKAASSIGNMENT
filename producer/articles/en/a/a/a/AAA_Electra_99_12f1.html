AAA Electra 99 is art museum, gallery and performance space, located in Anaheim, Orange County, California. It is Orange County's longest running (and only) co-op art gallery/museum.

AAA Electra 99's mission is to display any type of art â€“ without the hassles of having to sell, someone dictating what to display or what not to display, or artists getting ripped off; to act as a permanent venue for artists of all kinds to express themselves in a community setting.
