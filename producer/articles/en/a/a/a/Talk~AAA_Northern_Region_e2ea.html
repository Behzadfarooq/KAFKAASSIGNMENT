[edit] Current District Format

In 1993, the Northern Region realigned its districts based on school size to equalize football competition in particular. The Division 6 schools were in the Concorde and Patriot Districts, and the Division 5 schools were in the Liberty and National Districts. These were the original members of each district in 1994, and NOT the current membership of the districts today as additional members have been added since then:

AAA Concorde District (Division 6)

  • Centreville
  • Chantilly
  • Herndon
  • Jefferson
  • Oakton
  • South Lakes
  • Woodson

AAA Patriot District (Division 6)

  • Annandale
  • Hayfield
  • Lake Braddock
  • Robinson
  • West Springfield
  • T.C. Williams

AAA Liberty District (Division 5)

  • Fairfax
  • Falls Church
  • Langley
  • Lee

AAA National District (Division 5)

  • Edison

  • West Potomac
  • Yorktown
