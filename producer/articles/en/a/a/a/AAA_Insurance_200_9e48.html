AAA Insurance 200

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The AAA Insurance 200 is a NASCAR Craftsman Truck Series race that takes place at Dover International Speedway.

Past Winners:

  • 2006 Mark Martin
  • 2005 Kyle Busch
  • 2004 Chad Chaffin
  • 2003 Jason Leffler
  • 2002 Ted Musgrave
  • 2001 Scott Riggs
  • 2000 Kurt Busch


Previous race:

City of Mansfield 250

 NASCAR Races Next race:

Sam's Town 400
