AAA Dominion District

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The AAA Dominion District is a high school conference in the state of Virginia that includes schools from the Greater Richmond Region. AAA is the largest enrollment class of the Virginia High School League and also typically the most competitive level in Virginia high school sports.

The Dominion District schools compete in the AAA Central Region with schools from the AAA Capital District, AAA Central District, and the AAA Colonial District

[edit] Facts About the District

The Dominion District schools are located in the central part of the Greater Richmond Region south of the James River, which includes schools in Chesterfield County and Richmond City.

  • Manchester High School of Midlothian, Virginia
  • Midlothian High School of Midlothian, Virginia

  • George Wythe High School of Richmond, Virginia
