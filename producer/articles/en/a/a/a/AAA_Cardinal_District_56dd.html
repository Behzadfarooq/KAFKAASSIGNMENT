AAA Cardinal District

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The AAA Cardinal District is a high school conference in the state of Virginia that includes schools exclusively from Prince William county. AAA is the largest enrollment class of the Virginia High School League and also typically the most competitive level in Virginia high school sports.

The Cardinal District schools compete in the AAA Northwest Region with the schools from the AAA Cedar Run District, the AAA Commonwealth District, and the AAA Western Valley District.

[edit] Facts about the district

All Cardinal District schools are currently located in eastern Prince William County of Northern Virginia which draws students who live in Woodbridge, Dale City, and Dumfries. Currently, Prince William County schools are split between the Cardinal and the Cedar Run Districts due to the high amount of growth in the county, but all Prince William schools were in the Cardinal District until 2001. The smallest school in the Cardinal is Potomac with an enrollment at around 1,400 and the largest member is Woodbridge with an enrollment at around 2,600. Potomac has a AA enrollment, but chooses to play up in AAA to maintain rivalries rather than play in the AA Northwestern District or AA Battlefield District whose member schools are farther away. Potomac's enrollment is also projected to increase back into the AAA level in the near future.

The Cardinal District holds one of the perennial state football powerhouses in C. D. Hylton High School, and also one of the top boys basketball programs in the state in Potomac Senior High School.
