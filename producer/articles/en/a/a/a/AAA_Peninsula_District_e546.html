AAA Peninsula District

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Peninsula District is one of the four districts in the AAA Eastern Region of the Virginia High School League. It consists of the public high schools in the cities of Hampton, Newport News and Gloucester High School in Gloucester County.

Schools in the Peninsula District are also divided into Divisions 5 and 6, for football competition within the AAA level. Division 5 schools are the smaller half of schools within the Peninsula District and the Eastern Region; and Division 6 schools consist of the larger half.

  • Hampton High School, Division 5
  • Kecoughtan High School, Division 5
  • Phoebus High School, Division 5

[edit] Newport News

  • Denbigh High School, Division 5
  • Heritage High School, Division 5
  • Menchville High School, Division 6
  • Warwick High School, Division 5
  • Woodside High School, Division 5

At this time, only Gloucester High School and the five Newport News Schools compete in Volleyball. The Hampton schools hope to join in within the next year or so.

Gymnastics has been eliminated from most schools in the Peninsula District. Only Denbigh, Gloucester, Heritage, Menchville, and Warwick had Gymnastics teams last year.
