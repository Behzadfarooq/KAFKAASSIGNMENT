Aaahh!! Real Monsters is a platform arcade style video game released in 1995 for the Sega Mega Drive/Genesis and SNES based on Nickelodeon's cartoon program of the same name.

[edit] Description

In the game, you take control of Ickis, Oblina, and Krumm in their attempt to pass the Monster Midterm Exam at Monster Academy. You explore various locales collecting specific items and scaring specific people the Gromble instructs you to.

Players can switch between each character at will and each has their own special stats that are often used to solve puzzles and overcome obstacles: Ickis can jump higher, Oblina can reach higher ledges, and Krumm can scan off-screen areas. The monsters can also perform team moves, such as stacking on top of one another to reach items normally out of reach. Your main weapon is trash, which you can hurl at enemies. Collecting "monster manuals" will allow to perform a "scare" defeating any enemies on screen. The goal of the game is to finish all levels and find all the items to pass the exam. The game features over 25 levels.
