The AAA World Mixed Tag Team Championship is a tag team title contested for in the Mexican lucha libre promotion Asistencia Asesoría y Administración (AAA). The title is for teams comprised of one male wrestler and one female wrestler.

[edit] Title history

Wrestler: Times: Date: Location: Notes:
Electro Shock and Lady Apache 1 June 15, 2003 Naucalpan, México Defeated the teams of Gran Apache & Fabi Apache, El Brazo & Martha Villalobos and Chessman & Tiffany in an elimination match to become the first champions.
Chessman and Tiffany 1 September 16, 2003 San Luis Potosí, San Luis Potosí
Gran Apache and Faby Apache 1 August 1, 2004 Guadalupe, Puebla Defeated the teams of Electro Shock & Lady Apache, Chessman & Tiffany and Orientál & Cynthia Moreno in a four-way match.
Title vacated July 17, 2005 Title vacated when Fabi Apache no-shows a title defense.
Orientál and Cynthia Moreno 1 December 10, 2005 Guadalajara, Jalisco Defeated Chessman and La Diabólica in a tournament final to win the title.
