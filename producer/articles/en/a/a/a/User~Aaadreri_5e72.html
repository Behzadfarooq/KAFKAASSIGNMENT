[edit] Me

My full name is Adrian Dybfest Eriksen. Dybfest is my mother's surname and Eriksen is my stepdad's surname. My biological father's surname is Schancke. I was born on 29 September 1989 in a small community called Ørland right outside Trøndelag, Norway. I was born under the Libra. Currently I live in Trondheim, Norway and I am studying in a secondary school called Brundalen VGS.
