AAH or Aah is a three-letter acronym that may refer to:

  • Action Against Hunger
  • AAH Pharmaceuticals the UK's largest pharmaceutical wholesaler
  • Aquatic ape hypothesis a theory from evolutionary biology that human ancestors at some point lived partially in water
  • Advanced Attack Helicopter: see AH-64 Apache
  • IATA airport code for Merzbruck Airport near Aachen, Germany
  • ICAO airline designator for Aloha Airlines, Inc. of the United States
  • An interjection that one may make when in pain or relaxing
