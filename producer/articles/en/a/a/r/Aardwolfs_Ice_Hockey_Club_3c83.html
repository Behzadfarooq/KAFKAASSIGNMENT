Aardwolfs Ice Hockey Club

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Aardwolfs Ice Hockey Club is based in Christchurch, New Zealand. The club was created in 1998 by Glen Cameron and Tony Oston. The Aardwolfs now compete as part of the Christchurch Senior Non-Checking (SNC) Ice Hockey League.

[edit] Southern Migration

The Aardwolfs Ice Hockey Club are known for their annual 'southern migration', a social trip around the Otago and Southland Regions of New Zealand. Games are held against many of the local teams during their travels including against the Dunedin Ice Hockey Association.
