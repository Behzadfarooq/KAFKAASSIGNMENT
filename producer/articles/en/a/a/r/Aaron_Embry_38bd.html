Aaron Embry

Huntington Beach, California

Aaron Embry (born 11 November 1975, in Huntington Beach, California) is an American musician and actor. From summer through December of 1999 he was the keyboardist/guitarist "Skszp" in the L.A. production of "Hedwig and the Angry Inch" In May - June of 2000, he toured with Elliott Smith playing the piano. He was also on the April 21, 2000 episode of "Late Night with Conan O'Brian." He played keyboards on the song Pretty (Ugly Before) on Elliott Smith's 2004 album From a Basement on the Hill. He is the brother of actor Ethan Embry and actress Kessia Randall.
