Aaron Michael Gray, (born December 7, 1984 in Tarzana, California) is an American college basketball center for the Pittsburgh Panthers, where he is a senior. He is considered a top prospect in the 2007 NBA Draft, to be held June 28, 2007 in New York City.

In March 2007, Gray was named an Associated Press "All-American", after helping lead the University of Pittsburgh to the final 16 in the 2007 NCAA Men's Division I Basketball Tournament, also held in March 2007.

Gray played high school basketball at Emmaus High School in Pennsylvania's Lehigh Valley Conference.

He is 7'0 and weighs 270 pounds.
