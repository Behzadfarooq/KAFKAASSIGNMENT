Aaron Brown (born 23 June 1983, England) is a professional footballer with Reading. A defender who had previously played for Stafford Rangers and Tamworth, Aaron joined Reading on 23 November 2005 on loan, before signing permanently in January 2006.

Aaron played for the England non-league team while with Tamworth.
