Aaron Pitchkolan (born March 14, 1983 in Aurora, Colorado) is an American football (soccer) midfielder, who currently plays for FC Dallas of Major League Soccer.

Pitchkolan played college soccer from 2001-04 at University of Tampa and then West Virginia University, and played in the USL Premier Development League for Boulder Rapids Reserve, but was bypassed in the 2005 MLS SuperDraft. Instead, he was taken by Dallas in the second round of the Supplemental Draft. Midway through the year, Pitchkolan became a starter for the club.
