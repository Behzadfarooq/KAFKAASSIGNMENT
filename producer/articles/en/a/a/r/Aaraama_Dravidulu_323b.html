Aaraama Dravidulu is a sub-sect of Telugu Brahmins of Andhra Pradesh in South India. They primarily reside in East Godavari and West Godavari districts of Andhra Pradesh, though they can found all over Andhra Pradesh and other parts of India and world now. They are Brahmins who migrated from the Tamil country into Andhra.
