Aaron "Zeke" Dauner is best known as the drummer of the Alternative Rock band Breaking Point. He replaced former drummer Jody Abbott after the recording of the band's sophomore album Beautiful Disorder released in 2005.1 Dauner was discovered by former Creed (band) bassist Brett Hestla while at a studio in Orlando2. Hestla then referred Dauner to the band and it was an immediate hit.

[edit] Biography

Aaron Dauner was born to parents Janet Schultz and Dean Dauner in 1975. He was born in Minnesota and also spent some of his childhood in North Dakota. He then moved to Chicago and later Tennessee to pursue his career in music. He played in various bands before being referred to Breaking Point by Brett Hestla after meeting in Orlando.3
