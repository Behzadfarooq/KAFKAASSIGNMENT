Aaradhana (Aaradhna) Patel (born: December 1983), is a New Zealand R&B artist under the music label Dawn Raid.

[edit] Biography

Aaradhna first showed singer aspirations at the young age of 12 singing along with her mother to traditional Samoan and country songs. When her father was home she would try to imitate the songs she heard with her dad on Bollywood movies, even though she couldn’t understand what they were saying. Aaradhna began writing her own songs at the age of 13; she entered her first talent quest the same year making it in to the finals. Soon after she joined the school choir at Porirua College and then deciding to take her dream further, she went on to form a five piece girl group called “Lovera”. Her mother is of Samoan descent and her father is Indian.

[edit] Compilation appearances

Aaradhna has appeared on a few compilations and soundtracks since 2004 in New Zealand and Australia. The following is a list of these albums that have featured her music.

  • (2004) - Now That's What I Call Music 16 (EMI) - Getting Stronger (with Adeaze)
  • (2005) - Now That's What I Call Music 19 (EMI) - They Don't Know (with Savage)
  • (2005) - The R&B Collection III (Sony BMG) - Getting Stronger (with Adeaze
  • (2006) - Sione's Wedding (Dawn Raid Entertainment) - Forever (with Kevin Soul), Knowing You, & They Don't Know (with Savage)
  • (2006) - Now That's What I Call Music 21 (EMI) - Down Time

[edit] Featured appearances

Aaradhna also appears in albums recorded by other artists, including Savage, Adeaze and Che Fu. The following is a list of albums by other artists that Aaradhna has appeared in.

  • (2004) - Always & For Real by Adeaze (Dawn Raid Entertainment) - Getting Stronger
  • (2006) - Hi-Score: The Best of Che-Fu by Che Fu (Sony BMG) - Spin 1
  • (2005) - Moonshine by Savage (Dawn Raid Entertainment) - They Don't Know
