Aaron Ruell (born June 23, 1976) is an American actor who grew up in Clovis, California, and is most noted for his performance as Kipland Ronald Dynamite (or Kip) in the indie movie Napoleon Dynamite. He also shot all of the promotional photography for the film as well as designing the opening title sequence to the film.

Ruell stars in, On the Road With Judas, premiering in the Dramatic Competition at the 2007 Sundance Film Festival, where he plays a successful New York businessman who leads a double life as a computer thief.

Ruell is an accomplished photographer, and directs commercials. He was recently declared one of the most promising new directors in the world of advertising by Shoot magazine, as well as, "one of the emerging directors to know now" by Creativity. His photography has been seen in nearly every major publication and a book of his photography is being published by Nazraeli Press which will be released later in the year.

He was the only filmmaker to have two films, Everything's Gone Green and Mary, which he wrote and directed, premiere at the 2005 Sundance Film Festival.
