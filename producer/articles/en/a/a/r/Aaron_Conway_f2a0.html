Aaron Conway (born 29 March 1985 in Dundee) is a Scottish footballer who currently plays as a striker for Carnoustie Panmure in the Scottish Junior Football East Super League.

[edit] Career

Conway started his career with Dundee United but made just two appearances for the Terrors, appearing in games late in each of 2002–03 and 2003–04. Conway played ten games for Clyde before a two-game spell with Dundee, followed by ten appearances for Forfar Athletic. In 2006, Conway moved out of senior football and joined Carnoustie.
