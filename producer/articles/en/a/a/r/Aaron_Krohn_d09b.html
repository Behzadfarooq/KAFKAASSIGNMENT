Aaron Christopher Krohn (Born May 7, 1968 in Houston, Texas) is a former Texas-based voice actor who primarily worked on the properties of ADV Films. Aaron was formerly a voice actor but he has since moved to New York City and is currently working as a stage actor.

His most well known role is that of Ryoji Kaji on the hugely successful anime series Neon Genesis Evangelion. In addition to Kaji, he also played Kaworu Nagisa in the other Evangelion movies.
