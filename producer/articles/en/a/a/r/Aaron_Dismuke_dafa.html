Aaron Mitchell Dismuke (IPA pronunciation: [dɪzmjuːk]) is an American voice actor, known for anime roles in FUNimation titles. He was around eleven years old when he voiced the role of Alphonse Elric in Fullmetal Alchemist and is a cousin of voice actor and director Justin Cook. Aaron is one of few child voice actors in the voice acting field.

Dismuke was born in Tarrant County, Texas
