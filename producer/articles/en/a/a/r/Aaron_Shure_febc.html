Aaron Shure is a TV writer/producer and two-time Emmy winner. Shure was an Executive Producer of Everybody Loves Raymond for which he won two Emmy awards in the "Outstanding Comedy Series" category. He was recently a Consulting Producer and writer for the HBO sitcom Lucky Louie, and he sometimes blogs on the Huffington Post.

Born in Colorado, Shure has had a varied career, logging hours as a karaoke host, street performer, stand-up comedian, radio commentator and circus clown. His early years in theater were spent at SAK Comedy Lab as an improviser.

He has appeared on the The Late Show With David Letterman during a "Stupid Human Tricks" segment. He set a mouse trap off on his tongue.
