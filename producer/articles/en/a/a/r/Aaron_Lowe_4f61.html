Aaron Lowe (born October 12, 1974 in Vancouver, British Columbia) is a Canadian figure skater, competing in ice dance with Megan Wing since 1986. The pair has won six bronze medals and four silver medals at the Canadian Figure Skating Championships. In 2006, the pair competed in the Winter Olympics, where they finished 11th overall.

In April 25 2006, Aaron and Megan announced officially their retirement from competitive skating.
