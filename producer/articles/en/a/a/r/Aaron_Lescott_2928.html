Aaron Lescott (born 2 December 1978 in Birmingham) is a professional footballer who currently plays as a midfielder for Bristol Rovers.

Aaron started his career as a trainee with Aston Villa making only one first team appearance for them (in the FA Cup third round tie against Hull City). He had a loan spell with Lincoln City in 2000 before signing for Sheffield Wednesday the same year.

He made 43 appearances for Sheffield Wednesday before signing for Stockport County in 2001 making 81 appearances and scoring 1 goal before leaving for Bristol Rovers in 2004.

He is the brother of Everton defender Joleon Lescott.
