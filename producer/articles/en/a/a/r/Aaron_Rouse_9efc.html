Aaron Rouse, (born on January 8, 1984), is a strong safety for Virginia Tech. He is currently a prospect for the 2007 NFL Draft.

[edit] High School Career

Aaron Rouse attended First Colonial HS in Va. where he excelled at many positions, including safety. He was named first-team Group AAA by The Associated Press and second-team by the state coaches as a defensive back. He also lettered in basketball.

[edit] College Career

Rouse redshirted his freshman year, and eventually played in every game his sophomore year. Rouse has declared for the 2007 NFL Draft and is projected to go in the second round.
