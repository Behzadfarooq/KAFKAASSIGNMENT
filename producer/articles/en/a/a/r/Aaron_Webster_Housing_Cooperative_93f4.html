Aaron Webster Housing Cooperative

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Aaron Webster Housing Cooperative (formerly Cityview Co-op) is a housing cooperative located in Vancouver, British Columbia, Canada.

The co-op, located on Pender Street, was completed in 1987 and comprises one four-storey building complex with thirty-one suites, four of which are wheelchair accessible.

The co-op was renamed in 2005 in honour of a former member, Aaron Webster, who was murdered by a group of youths in Stanley Park in 2001. Webster had been serving as the co-op's president at the time of his murder.
