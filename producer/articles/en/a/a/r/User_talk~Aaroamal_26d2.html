[edit] List of famous Ezhavas

193.130.175.134 has removed the name of SK Pottekad from the list. Do you know if he is Ezhava ? I thought he was, but not sure. Tintin 10:19, 5 August 2005 (UTC)

  • It was only yesterday that I noted this question posted here, but could not immediately put it in context to early discussion. I regret the oversight. Yes Pottekad is. However, my view is that these community-based lists are not fit for an encyclopedia like Wiki. The information is not imparting anything valuable to a global user group of the encyclopedia. Who really cares if ONV or MT are Nairs, or Pottekad or OV Vijayan were Ezhavas, they have all grown intellectually and culturally above all these low barriers and their heads tower high above a different cloud altogether. These lists, if at all, should be at the respective community's web sites. Also these lists become quite subjective, as a petty starlet who showed her face in a TV serial will get listed, but names of many unsung and forgotten heros and notables that worked for the uplift of the community itself get edited out, because of lack of historical knowledge among Wiki users. Without some criteria, for listing, these lists can also become endless; for if you simply put the four largest communities in Kerala together, then that's all about having a who's who for all Malayalees! Also most occassional and anonymous Wiki users are only interested in adding names to these lists, but not building properly researched articles on the back of those names. Nowadays I have taken some of these articles off my watchlist, as I have noted that they are only points of contention, rather than anything worthwhile spending time on. Aaroamal 06:26, 30 January 2007 (UTC)
