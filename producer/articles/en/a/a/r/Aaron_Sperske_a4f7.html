Aaron Sperske

Aaron Sperske is an American drummer, who has played in the several bands, including Beachwood Sparks, Lilys, The Miracle Workers, and The Pernice Brothers. He also played drums on the Elliott Smith song Coast to Coast which was featured on the From a Basement on the Hill album.
