Aaron Wall

Aaron Wall (born 1979) is a writer and search engine optimization expert. He operates a well-known forum for SEO experts, Threadwatch.[1] and is a frequent speaker at the Search Engine Strategies, and Pubcon [2] conferences.

In 2005 Wall was sued by Traffic Power for defamation and publication of trade secrets.[3] According to Wired Magazine, Traffic Power had been "banned from Google for allegedly rigging search engine results."[4] The case was watched by many bloggers because it addressed the murky legal question of who's liable for comments posted on blogs.[5] The case was dismissed for lack of personal jurisdiction, and Traffic Power failed to appeal within the allowed time.[6]
