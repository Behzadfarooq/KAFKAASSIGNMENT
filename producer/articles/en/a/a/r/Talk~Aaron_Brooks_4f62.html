[edit] Opinion vs. Facts

8/06: I recently noticed that a lot of opinion was injected on this page, about Brooks' smiles and sunflower seed chewing and allusions to Brooks not caring; some casual sarcastic remarks were included, and some poor grammar as well. All of this was either unsubstantiated speculation, irrelevant to this person's career and performance, or both. I removed some of these statements, but left all the clinical and factual information intact. I was also responsible for the recent re-order of some of the paragraphs, which were presented somewhat by chronological order, but not consistently so. Brooks' accomplishments from the 2000 season were completely missing, and I added that content. This is a living person. I hope we can keep things about him neutral. [[MattBattison 18:31, 27 August 2006 (UTC)]]
