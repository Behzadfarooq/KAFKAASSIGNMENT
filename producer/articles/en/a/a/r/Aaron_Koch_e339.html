Aaron Paul Koch (born February 21, 1978 in Portland, Oregon) is a former NFL offensive lineman for the Jacksonville Jaguars from 2000-2001. He played college football at Oregon State University and was signed by the Jaguars after graduation. He played in 24 games over two seasons.
