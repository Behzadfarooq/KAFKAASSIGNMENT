Aarón Díaz (born Aarón Díaz Spencer March 7, 1982) is a Mexican actor, from Puerto Vallarta, Jalisco, Mexico.

[edit] Biography

He lived several years in San Francisco, California. He finished high school in San Diego, before returning to Mexico to study at the exclusive Televisa Acting School Centro de Educación Artística CEA.

He made his acting debut in the popular teen drama Clase 406. In 2004, he followed Clase with another teen drama titled Corazones al límite co-starring Sara Maldonado and Arturo Peniche. Díaz's latest work was in the telenovela Barrera de amor starring Yadhira Carrillo. He is currently starring along former flame Sherlyn in Mexico's own version of Grease entitled "Vaselina" Apart from acting, Díaz is also a singer and hopes to release a hip hop album sometime in the future. Diaz has just recently signed on(2006) with Pedro Damian again, (creator of Clase 406 and Rebelde) for the teen telenovela called "Lola". "Lola .. Erase Una Vez" premired on February 26, 2007 at 6 p.m. on Televisa in Mexico.
