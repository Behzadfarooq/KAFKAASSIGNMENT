Aaron Turner is an influential musician, graphic artist, and founder of label Hydra Head Records.

[edit] Artwork

Turner has created album cover and liner note artwork for a variety of artists and bands, many of whom are signed to his Hydrahead Records label.

The cover to Pelican's Australasia is typical of Turner's style.
