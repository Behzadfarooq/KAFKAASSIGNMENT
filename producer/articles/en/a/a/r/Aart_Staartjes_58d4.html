Aart Staartjes (born March 1, 1938) is a Dutch actor, director, television presenter and documentary maker from Amsterdam. .

He plays or has played roles in the following programmes:

  • De Fabeltjeskrant (as Rocus de Vrije Vogel)
  • De Stratemakeropzeeshow
  • J.J. de Bom, voorheen de Kindervriend
  • Het Klokhuis (among others as Fetze Alsvanouds)
  • Sesamstraat
  • De Film van Ome Willem
  • Waltz (series)

Staartjes used to present the annual arrival of Sinterklaas from Spain in The Netherlands. As of 2005, he retired from this job.
