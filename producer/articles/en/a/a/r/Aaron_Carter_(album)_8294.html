Aaron Carter is the self-titled debut of Aaron Carter, brother of Backstreet Boy Nick Carter. It was released in December 1997 and re-released the next year with a new song and a remix. The original version was also available as a limited edition including a poster and a pen that has written on the side, "I Love Aaron" (red, yellow, green or blue).

[edit] Other versions

The Japanese version of the album includes stickers and bonus tracks:

  • "Crush On You" (Remix)
  • "Crazy Little Party Girl" (One Day Mix)
  • "I'm Gonna Miss You Forever" (Dreamix)
  • "Shake It" (Nick Carter Radio Remix)

The Canadian version has a different cover and includes a poster (different to the one that came with the German version) and has the same track listing but without the Intro.

There was also a Limited Edition Bonus CD Pack available, and with it you got cards, stickers and a bonus CD featuring:

  • "Crush On You" (Gary's Mix)
  • "Crazy Little Party Girl" (Main Mix)
  • "I'm Gonna Miss You Forever" (Dreamix)
  • "Shake It" (Nick Carter Radio Remix)
  • "Surfin' USA" (Album Version)
  • "Crazy Little Interview"
