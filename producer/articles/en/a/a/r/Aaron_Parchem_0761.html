Aaron Parchem (born June 6, 1977 in Columbus, Ohio) is an American figure skater. He competes in pairs, and since April 2003, his partner has been Marcy Hinzmann. In 2005, they won the bronze medal at the U.S. Figure Skating Championships. In 2006 they won the silver medal at the U.S. Figure Skating Championships and represented the United States at the 2006 Winter Olympics in Torino, Italy. He was previously paired with Stephanie Kalesavich.

Parchem is married to his choreographer, Zuzanna Szwed.
