In 1998, Aaron M. Bertram (April 3rd, 1981) started the seven piece third-wave ska band Suburban Legends while attending Huntington Beach High School. The popular Orange County, California ska band toured the US many times, as well as a brief UK tour. His role in the band included back-up vocals and trumpet.

While maintaining the band, Aaron also found time to work at the Disneyland Resort as well as attend Cal State Fullerton. In May of 2005 Aaron left the band to marry his fianc√©, Rachel Charest, and was replaced by Luis Beza.

On April 6th, 2006, Aaron and Rachel were marriaged at the Downtown Disney Chapel. Although retired from Suburban Legends, Aaron still can be contacted through his joint myspace account with his wife. [1]
