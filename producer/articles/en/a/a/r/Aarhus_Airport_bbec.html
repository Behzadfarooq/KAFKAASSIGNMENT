Aarhus Airport (IATA: AAR, ICAO: EKAH) is a civilian airport located in Kolind, Denmark, which is 19.4 nautical miles (40 km) northeast of Aarhus.

[edit] Transportation

  • Taxi — There are taxis traveling to the city available from outside the terminal building.
  • Bus — There is an airport bus service that takes passengers from the airport to Aarhus and back, the service is available 24 hours a day. Also, route 212 between Ebeltoft and Randers stops at the airport.
  • Road — There are several options available to travelers arriving and leaving the airport: car; bus; and taxi services.

[edit] Directions

The airport is easily accessible from Aarhus, just take the route 15 from the city, and continue on route 15 towards the airport. Once on route 15, just follow the signs to the airport.

[edit] Airport facilities

  • Airport Info: A tourist help desk is found in the arrival terminal of the airport.
  • Money: The airport has a bank; a bureaux de change; and several ATMs.
  • More Services: There is a first aid station located in the terminal building of the airport, there are also baby changing rooms found in the airport.
  • Eating & Drinking: There are a range of shops found in the airport, including a duty free shop; a newsagent; a tobacconist; travel agent; and a Gift Shop.
  • Shopping: The airport has a few restaurants; cafeterias; and bars situated in the terminal building, they offer a wide range of refreshments catering to anyhting a tourists could be looking for.
  • Business Facilities: The airport has a VIP lounge that offers some business services, including fax; Internet; and telephones.
  • Disabled Facilities: The airport is easily accessible by people with disabilities and wheelchair accessible toilets are also found throughout the airport. If passengers with disabilities have any special requirements, they should inform their airline before traveling.
