Aaron Pike (b. Aaron Pike Shainwald, September 29, 1981, New York City) was raised outside of Princeton, New Jersey.

On May 21, 2004, Aaron Pike Shainwald married his long-time boyfriend. The wedding and the events leading up to the wedding were the focus of MTV's True Life: I'm Gay and I'm Getting Married, which aired throughout the 2004 season on the MTV networks around the globe. They were the 44th gay couple to be married in the United States of America.

He has appeared in The Advocate, TV Guide, Boston Globe, The New York Times, Metro, Boston Herald, Inside Track, and in several other national and international media outlets. The show aired several times throughout the 2004 MTV season and now currently plays on LOGO, MTV's sister channel.

The show won the 2005 GLAAD Outstanding Documentary Media award and was presented to the couple in March 2005 in New York City's Marriot Marquee, Times Square.

In September 2006, Aaron Schonberg and his husband, Stephen, filed for divorce for irreconcilable differences and the divorce proceedings were finalized on January 18, 2007.

Aaron currently resides happily in Boston's South End neighborhood and has a career in healthcare marketing.
