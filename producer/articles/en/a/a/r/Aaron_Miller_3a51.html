Aaron Miller (born August 11, 1971 in Buffalo, New York) is a professional ice hockey defenceman who currently plays for the Los Angeles Kings of the NHL.

[edit] Playing career

Miller was drafted in the 5th round, 88th overall by the New York Rangers in the 1989 NHL Entry Draft.
