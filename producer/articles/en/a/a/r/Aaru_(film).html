Aaru (Tamil : ஆறு) is a 2005 high-budget Tamil movie. The movie stars Surya and Trisha. The film is directed by Hari of Saamy fame. The score and soundtrack, composed by Devi Sri Prasad, was released on December 2005.

[edit] Plot

Aarumugham, or as everyone calls him Aaru, (Surya) is a thug who makes or unmakes political deals with his band of boys, handpicked from the ghettos or the slums of Chennai. Aaru’s mentor Viswanathan (Asish Vidyarthi) has a reason to cultivate him. Aaru gives him the violent undercover he needs.

But when Vishwanthan guns for his bitter rival Reddy (Jaiparakash Reddy), Aaru acoompanies him and gets exploited by the man whom he respected as his god, and his mento Vishwanath. Things turn horribly wrong, The protest that Aaru orchestrates for his political master ends in the death of five of his trusted Friends.

Now Aaru understands the real evil intent of Viswanathan and vows to Destroy his Political Image, Man Power, and his Gang. The rest, as they, is the climax. Interwoven into the story is the love of the college girl Maha (Trisha) with the rowdyish Aaru.

[edit] Soundtrack

The Soundtrack of the Movie was Composed by Devi Sri Prasad. The Album had 6 Tracks. The Songs were an average hit among the Tamil and Telugu Masses.

  • Soda Bottle – Shankar Mahadevan & Saaki Mukhesh
  • Paakatha – Tippu & Sumangali
  • Freeya Vudu – Mukhesh, Karthikeyan, Vadivelu, Jessie Gift & Giresh
  • Thottutea – Karthik, & Sunathi Sarathy
  • Dhrogam – Hariharan
  • Nenjam Enum – Srinivas & Kalpana

[edit] Box Office

The Film had an Impressive Opening thanks to Surya's last movie Ghajini which was an all out Blockbuster. Although Aaru couldn't sustain the opening, the movie still managed to have a solid 150 day run in most theaters, and Celebrated 200 days in Devi Theater in Chennai. The movie is undoubtedly a Hit.
