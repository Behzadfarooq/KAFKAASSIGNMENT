Aaron Rosenberg is an American novelist and game designer.

Aaron is originally from New Jersey and New York. He returned to New York City in 1996 after stints in New Orleans and Kansas. He has taught college-level English and worked in corporate graphics and book publishing.

Aaron has written novels for Pocket’s Star Trek: Starfleet Corps of Engineers, White Wolf’s Exalted, and Games Workshop’s Warhammer lines. He also writes educational books and roleplaying games and has his own game company, Clockworks (www.clockworksgames.com).

Aaron lives in New York City with his family.
