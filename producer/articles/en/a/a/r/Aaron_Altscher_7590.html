Aaron Altscher (born May 26, 1981 in Ellicott City, Maryland) was a candidate for the sixth season of reality show, The Apprentice, where candidates compete in several tasks as an extended "interview" for a position in one of Donald Trump's companies. (He was fired from the interview in week five.) He is known as The Adapter.

[edit] Early life

Aaron was born in Ellicott City, Maryland and attended Wilde Lake High School in Columbia, Maryland, graduating in 1999. While at Wilde Lake, he participated on the varsity football, wrestling and baseball teams. He attended the University of Mary Washington in Fredericksburg, Virginia, receiving a Bachelor's degree in business and All-American honors in baseball, where he played left-fielder.
