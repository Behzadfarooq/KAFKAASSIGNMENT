[edit] Nice job!

Fascinating new article on the Family Feud broadcast history. My brother, father, wife, former brother-in-law and sister-in-law played with Ray Combs in '89. They won two games but, sadly, not the big money. I met Ray briefly as well as some of the Goodson-Todman crew. Wonderful folks all. I was deeply saddened at Combs' passing since he treated my wife particularly nice. He had played a joke on her by tweaking her nose on camera; she got him back later with a terrific tweak of her own which brought down the house...but which didn't make it to air. Wonderful memory. - Lucky 6.9 04:18, 19 December 2006 (UTC)
