Aaron Johnson (April 30, 1983) in Port Hawkesbury, Nova Scotia is a professional ice hockey player who currently plays defense for the National Hockey League's Columbus Blue Jackets.

[edit] Playing career

He was drafted by the Columbus Blue Jackets in the 2001 NHL Entry Draft. He was the chosen 85th overall in the 3rd round.
