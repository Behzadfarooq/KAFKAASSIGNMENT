Aaron Miller (cantor)

Aaron Miller (1911 – 2000) was a Polish/American cantor of Bobover descent. Born in Oświęcim, Poland, the son of Reb Chaim Shimon, a Chazzan and an expert mohel, and Gittel, he immigrated to America after World War II. He died in Brooklyn, New York in 2000.

He is the father of the Cantor Benzion Miller.
