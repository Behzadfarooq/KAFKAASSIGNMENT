Aaron Kamin

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Aaron Kamin (born Aaron Kamm Kamin, on 10 August 1977, in Studio City, California) is an American guitarist and songwriter. He is a founding member of the band The Calling.

Kamin, the son of an attorney and a teacher, attended high school in the San Fernando Valley.

Kamin formed The Calling in his family's garage with Alex Band. They released two albums: Camino Palmero and Two as well as a number of hit singles including "Wherever You Will Go." The band also won MTV's award for "Best New Band".

The Calling is now on hiatus, as Alex Band has decided to pursue a solo career. Kamin is currently producing and writing songs for several different bands in the Los Angeles area, including Hodges Taylor.
