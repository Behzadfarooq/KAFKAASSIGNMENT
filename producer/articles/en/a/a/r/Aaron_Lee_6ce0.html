Aaron Lee Soon Yong (born 1972 in Malaysia) is a Singaporean prize-winning poet who writes in English. His first collection of poems, A Visitation of Sunlight, was named one of the best books of 1997 by The Straits Times. In 1999 the title poem of his book was selected for the National Arts Council’s "Poems on the Move" programme, a national initiative to bring poetry to the masses on public transport.

Lee’s work has been anthologised in such publications as Rhythms: a Millennial Anthology of Poetry (Singapore), the New Straits Times (Malaysia) and Anglistik (Germany).

Lee is the co-editor of No Other City: the Ethos Anthology of Urban Poetry and Love Gathers All: the Philippines- Singapore Anthology of Love Poetry (for which the editors were given an award by the Singapore International Foundation). He is active in the literary scene in mentoring young poets and conducting school workshops and seminars on creative writing. He is married to an artist and educationist, Namiko Chan.
