Aaron Hosack is a free agent NFL wide receiver. He graduated from Chino High School in Chino, California and played college football for the Minnesota Golden Gophers. Hosack played with the Frankfurt Galaxy inNFL Europe during the 2006 season. The team won the World Bowl and he was named to the All-NFL Europe team. Hosack was cut by the Minnesota Vikings during the 2006 preseason.[1]
