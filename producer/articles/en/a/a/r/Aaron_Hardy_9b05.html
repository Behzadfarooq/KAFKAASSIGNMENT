Aaron Hardy is a professional footballer for Huddersfield Town in Coca-Cola League One.

He made his debut for Town on 17 October 2006 in their 2-1 home defeat against Doncaster Rovers in Round 1 of the Johnstone's Paint Trophy. He got many plaudits for his play at right-back in an otherwise poor team performance.

Then 4 days later, he played his first league match in Town's 2-1 win at Port Vale.

In the televised encounter on 5 January 2007 at Yeovil Town, substitute Hardy scored an own goal in Yeovil's 3-1 win when he turned Lee Morris's cross passed Matt Glennon.
