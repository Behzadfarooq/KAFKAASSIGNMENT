Aaron Booth

Aaron Booth is a Canadian indie pop singer/songwriter and producer, living in Calgary. He has released two full-length albums and two EPs.

Transparent was released independently in 2002 and reached #6 on the Canadian campus radio chart. The album was co-produced by Aaron and celebrated Canadian producer, Dave Draves, who is known for his work with Julie Doiron, Kathleen Edwards, Howe Gelb, Kepler and Wooden Stars.

Our Last Escape was released by Endearing Records in 2004 to critical acclaim in Canada and abroad. It was also co-produced with Draves.

Aaron has toured extensively throughout Canada and has performed in the United Kingdom, Japan, Finland, Sweden and the United States. Members of Aaron's touring band have included musicians from Arcade Fire, The Weakerthans and Wooden Stars.

He also records and performs with the Toronto band Lake Holiday and Pigeonbooth, a collaboration with Calgary band, Woodpigeon.
