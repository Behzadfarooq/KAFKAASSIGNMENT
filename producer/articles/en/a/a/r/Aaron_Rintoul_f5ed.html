Aaron Rintoul.

Aaron Rintoul owns and runs Sublight Records an experimental electronic music label started in 2004 and based out of Winnipeg, Manitoba, Canada. Named among the top 25 indie record labels of 2006 by XLR8R magazine, Sublight has released 40 albums in only three years by artists such as Venetian Snares, Richard Devine, Datach'i, Not Breathing, Doormouse, Bong-Ra, Somatic Responses, Fanny, The Gasman, Enduser, The Flashbulb, Wisp, Belladonnakillz, Bill Laswell's Method of Defiance project and many others of the IDM, breakcore, drill and bass fields of experimental electronic music.
