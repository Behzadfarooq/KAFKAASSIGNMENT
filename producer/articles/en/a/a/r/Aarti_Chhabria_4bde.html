Aarti Chhabria, born November 21, 1982, is an Indian Bollywood actress and model.

She is fluent in English, Hindi, Marathi, Sindhi and Gujarati (Source: IMDb). She debuted in a Hindi movie Tum Se Achcha Kaun Hai.
