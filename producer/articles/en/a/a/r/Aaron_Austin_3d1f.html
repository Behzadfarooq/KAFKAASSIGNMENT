Aaron Austin is a bisexual American pornographic actor (porn star) who appeared primarily in gay pornographic films but appeared in some bisexual films as well.

In some of the gay films he appeared in Austin only masturbated, but in others he engaged in anal intercourse as the top. In the 1993 film Jock-A-Holics, he bottomed for both Phil Bradley and Eric Houston. His most recent appearance in a pornographic film was for Jock Trade Solos, released by Sports & Recreation Video in 2000, a collection of scenes of men masturbating.[1]

In addition to his film credits, Austin is listed at the Internet Movie Database (IMDb) as an electrician for the 2006 movie Cherry Crush and as a member of the sound department for the 2003 movie Who's Your Daddy?.
