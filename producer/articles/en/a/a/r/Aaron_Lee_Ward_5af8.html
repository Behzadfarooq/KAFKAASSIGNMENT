Aaron Lee Ward

Aaron Lee Ward was born August 28, 1896. A former infielder for the New York Yankees, he died 30 January 1961, in the Veterans Administration Hospital in New Orleans, Louisiana, after a long illness. He was 64 years old.

Aaron Lee Ward was born near Booneville, Arkansas and was a standout at second base when he was performing for the Yankees under Miller Huggins. He won a World Series ring in 1923. He also played for the Chicago White Sox in 1927 and Cleveland Indians in 1928.
