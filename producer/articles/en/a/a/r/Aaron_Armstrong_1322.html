Aaron Armstrong

Aaron Armstrong (born 14 October 1977) is a sprinter from Trinidad and Tobago who mainly competes over 200 metres.

[edit] Personal bests

As of August 1414th August 2006

  • 100 m - 10.04 (2005)
  • 200 m - 20.08 (1999)
  • 400 mH - 51.68 (2002)

[edit] Achievements

  • 2006 Commonwealth Games - fifth place
  • 3rd IAAF World Athletics Final - sixth place
  • 2005 Central American and Caribbean Championships - silver medal
