[edit] Original meaning of aaronu

The definition of aaronu was first created by the BBC television program 'Shooting stars' which starred the comic duo Vic Reeves & Bob Mortomer. The word was used in part of the show in a game of true and false, but rather than using true and false the words aaronu and uvarvu were used, aaronu meaning true and uvarvu meaning false.


[edit] aaronu the artist

This word is also in use by the artist R Aaron Sidwell, during his time at art college in Loughborough (UK) the show 'shooting stars' was popular and as a result the name was given to the artist as a nick name. After graduating from University in 2003 Aaron began work as an artist and began to use the name aaronu as part of his identity. The artsits work can be found on www.aaronu.co.uk

Aaron was born on 28th August 1978 in Leicester (UK) where he lived until he was 12 when he moved to the country where he spent most of his time drawing. He later attended Art college and then Served a mission for the church of Jesus Christ of Latter day saints. After returning from his mission to the south west of england he studied Graphic design and advertising at BCUC in Buckinghamshire. When he graduated he spent a short amount of time working in a warehouse with clothing and then some time as an IT assistant. It was there where he took the confidence to take his love for art and design to the next level and began to sell his work through the popular website eBay. He found much success there and began to build up a world wide customer base and has become a succesful artist.

The art

The idea behind Aaron Sidwells artwork began when he was at art college and then later at University. When at art college Aaron studied in depth the importance of light and its effects on what we see and how we see it. He began to see that the world is not made up of cartoon shapes, but shapes of light and dark 'when one looks at a tree you don't se a bouble you she light and shadow. With this new way of looking at the world Aaron studied Graphic illustration which helped him define his style. With this new style he began to start painting his work and sold it on ebay. Aarons work began to be very popular not only with lovers of art but also with lovers of money, people bagan to see an oportunity to make money as they saw that they could copy Aarons style with ease and come up with painting of their own in the same style. Now when you look on ebay the art has taken over as many have taken on the style and are painting many painting in the same style, however Aaron still sees much success and finds that his work still is one of the definative styles in the art world.
