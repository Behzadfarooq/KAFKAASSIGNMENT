Aaron Himelstein

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Aaron Himelstein as Friedman in Joan of Arcadia

Aaron Himelstein (born 1986 in Buffalo Grove, Illinois) is an American actor who is best known for playing a younger version of Austin Powers in Austin Powers in Goldmember and Friedman, Luke Girardi's best friend, in Joan of Arcadia. He also wrote, directed and edited the short film, Sugar Mountain.

Aaron Himelstein as Luke Palmeiro in House

He has made guest appearances in numerous different series such as Cupid, Boston Public, North Shore and House and appeared in the film High Fidelity.

Aaron Himelstein and Michael Welch in Joan of Arcadia

He is also a very close friend of Joan of Arcadia stars Michael Welch and Chris Marquette. He and Welch had known each other for several years but they did not become good friends until the series began in 2003. [1]
