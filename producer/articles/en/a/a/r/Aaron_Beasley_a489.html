Aaron Bruce Beasley (born July 7, 1973) is a defensive back player in the National Football League. He was drafted by the Jacksonville Jaguars in the 1996 draft as the 63rd pick overall, and has also played for the New York Jets and Atlanta Falcons. In his career he played in 121 games (105 as a starter) and amassed 427 solo tackles and 24 interceptions.
