Aaron David Ledesma (born June 3, 1971 in Union City, California, USA) was an infielder for the Major League Baseball team the New York Mets, Baltimore Orioles, Tampa Bay Devil Rays, and Colorado Rockies. He was drafted in the second round of the 1990 amateur draft by the Mets. Ledesma hit two home runs in his career, both for the Baltimore Orioles during the 1997 season.
