Aarti Tikoo

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Aarti Tikoo is a print journalist who worked in conflict ridden Jammu and Kashmir state of India for seven years. She was senior correspondent with India's leading and the largest selling newspaper, The Times of India. As a media fellow with WISCOMP (Women In Security, Conflict Management and Peace) she conducted research on the impact of insurgency on education and children in Kashmir. She is currently a student of Masters in International Affairs at Columbia University, New York, USA.
