Aaron Norris (b. November 23, 1951) is an American film producer, director, actor, and stunt co-ordinator. He is the brother of Chuck Norris.

Aaron Norris was born on November 23, 1951 in Gardena, California. He is half Irish and and half Cherokee. Norris served in Vietnam along with his older brother Weiland who was killed in action.

He began working in the movies as an uncredited stunt man. A few years later he began acting in small roles in films like, Good Guys Wear Black (1978), A Force of One (1979), The Octagon (1980), and Lone Wolf McQuade (1983). In the mid-1980s he moved on to writing and directing.

Norris has directed many films including, Braddock: Missing in Action III, Platoon Leader, Delta Force 2: The Colombian Connection, The Hitman, Hellbound, Top Dog and several Walker Texas Ranger episodes.

Recently he was a producer of his brother's starring film The Cutter in which he also appeared as an actor.
