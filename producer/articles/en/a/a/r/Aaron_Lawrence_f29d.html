Aaron Lawrence is an American gay porn star, escort, author, and webmaster. He received a Bachelor of Science degree in psychology from Iowa State University in 1993 and a Master of Arts in Student Affairs Administration from Michigan State University in 1995. He currently resides in Berkeley Heights, New Jersey, with his partner Jeff, whom he met in 1992 and married in 1999.

Describing himself as versatile[citation needed], Lawrence performs in both the top and bottom roles. His website discloses that while he is comfortable with very nearly anything to be described as "mainstream" gay sex, he is not comfortable nor skilled in some of the activities more often described as "kink", such as BDSM.

[edit] Escorting

Lawrence worked as an escort for the first time in 1993, and escorted regularly for a brief time in 1995. He stopped escorting that year at the request of his husband-to-be, Jeff, but returned to escorting in mid-1996. He eventually spent less and less time escorting as he was producing films, and announced a one-year hiatus from escorting in 2002. He returned to escorting 8 months later, only to announce his retirement from the industry less than 2 months later. After an absence of 3-1/2 years, he returned to escorting in September 2006.

[edit] Film

In 1997 Lawrence appeared in a low-budget porn film titled Pushover and began recording and releasing his own amateur videos that same year. His first self-produced film, Aaron Jacks Off, was followed by Aaron's Boys Tell All in 1998. The first of his "travels" films, Aaron's American Tour 1 and Aaron's American Tour 2 were filmed in 2000 while traveling in the United States. He filmed his first foreign-country films in 2001, the two-part Aaron's Adventures in Amsterdam 1 and Aaron's Adventures in Amsterdam 2. Eventually, he would record films in Russia (where members of the Russian military appeared in his videos), London, Thailand, Ukraine, Latvia, Czech Republic, and Slovakia. He produced a total of 36 amateur films, ending with Aaron's Bareback Orgy in 2003. While Lawrence's website states that he always plays safely, Orgy is a bareback film in which the performers have anal sex without wearing condoms.

During the production of his own amateur films, he continued to perform in professional productions for other studios; in 1999 he appeared in Virgin No More, a film produced by All Worlds Video. For his role in Studio 2000's Dream Team, he was nominated for "Best Supporting Actor" at the GayVN Awards. While Thomas Lloyd took that award, the film garnered four, among them "Best Gay Video" and "Best Director". He also appeared in Eric Magyar Goes West, an amateur film he did not produce.

His film Aaron's Russian Boy Orgy received a nomination for "Best Amateur Video" at the 2002 Gay Entertainment Awards; Lawrence was nominated for "Best Amateur Performer" and "Best Gay Author".

[edit] Books

Lawrence is also the author of two books. Suburban Hustler: Stories of a Hi-Tech Callboy, published in 1999, is an account of his own experiences as a male escort. In 2000, Aaron's second book, The Male Escort's Handbook: Your Guide to Getting Rich the Hard Way, was published. The Handbook is a "how-to" guide for those considering work as companions-for-hire. He has also written articles for several publications, including Anything That Moves and Unzipped. In 2004, he began another project as webmaster for "Gay Geek," a website which lists and reviews gay-oriented adult websites.
