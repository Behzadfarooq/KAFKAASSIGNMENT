Aaron Jermaine Ross (born September 15, 1982, in San Antonio, Texas) was an American college football player. He played defensive back for the University of Texas Longhorns, winning the Jim Thorpe Award for the nation's top defensive back in 2006. He was named the defensive “most-valuable player” of the 2006 Alamo Bowl.
