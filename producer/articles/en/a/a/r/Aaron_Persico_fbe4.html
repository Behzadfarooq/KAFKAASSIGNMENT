Aaron Persico (born 29 March 1978 in Wellington, New Zealand) is an Italian rugby union footballer.

One of the most experienced players in the Azzurri set-up having made in excess of 50 appearances since his Italy debut against Scotland in the 2000 Six Nations.

Persico has represented his adopted country in every Six Nations tournament and was also part of their World Cup squad in 2003. The flanker left Italy in 2003 to join English side Leeds Tykes before moving to French Top 14 outfit Agen.
