Aaron Wilbraham (born October 21, 1979, Knutsford, England) is a striker for English League Two team MK Dons.

[edit] Early professional career

Aaron started his career at Stockport County where he made 118 league appearances. During that time he also went out on loan to Norwegian club Moss F.K.. Afterwards Aaron went to Hull City for £100,000 and made just 10 league appearances during the season that he spent there. He was subsequently released and MK Dons snapped him up on the same day. In the 2005-06 season, Aaron was loaned out to Bradford City for 2 months. In 2006-07 season, Aaron Wilbraham has become a first team regular for the MK Dons. His emergence as a first team regular has coincided with manager Martin Allen's decision to play Wilbraham as an attacking midfielder rather than an out and out striker. In January 2007 he was rewarded for his performances earlier in the season with a new contract which will keep him at the MK Dons until 2009.
