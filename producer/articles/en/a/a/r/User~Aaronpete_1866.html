[edit] Aaron Peterson

Born on the 8th day of May in the year 1989, he is currently 16 years old. He was born in raised in Coppell, Texas. He plays the sport of soccer, frequently. He currently plays for Everton 89 Boys and on April 22nd recieved a yellow card for shouting profanity in the direction of the referee. When going to state with his high school varsity team, Peterson went to a hair salon and added a "power stripe" to his head. Although his mother found it quite charming, his father said that he "resembeled an indian". Historians record him as the "greatest player to grace the green grasses of this grand Earth." Aaron Peterson often finds himself going to school, during the weekdays, of which he participates on the Coppell High School Varsity Soccer Team. He also activly involves himself in the arts, of witchcraft, at Hogwarts. Although he has not made that many great achievements in his lifetime, his legend has to be Sarah Sanders, soon to be world-famous female soccer player living in England and appearing on many EPL fields. Peterson trained her viciously from the very begining that is without a doubt his number one life achivement. A palm reader is rumored to have told Peterson that he "will someday rule the world with his ability to make people laugh and his extreme sexual appeal." He has been labeled as many things including "Tall and built" and has been rumored to follow as the next Brawny Man. It has also been reported that Peterson has an excessivly overweight grandmother, and a border collie named Bingo (although the song does not specify the breed).
Aaron frequently wears "crazy" attire, such as plaid shorts and zamba pants. He also loves new york. So much that he bought five shirts that say he loves it. He is the epitome of the word "pirate", as he often pirates music and also owns an actual pirate hat.
Peterson's trademark is his booming laugh, which can be heard for miles. He is also known for his "golden locks" which have been reported to "fly about" on the soccer field.
