[edit] Welcome to Wikipedia!!!

Hello Aa35te! Welcome to Wikipedia! Thank you for your contributions. If you decide that you need help, check out Wikipedia:Where to ask a question, ask me on my talk page, or place {{helpme}} on your talk page and someone will show up shortly to answer your questions. Please remember to sign your name on talk pages using four tildes (~~~~); this will automatically produce your name and the date. You may also push the signature button located above the edit window. Finally, please do your best to always fill in the edit summary field. This is considered an important guideline in Wikipedia. Even a short summary is better than no summary. [edit] Don't fool around

Remember that this is an encyclopedia and the sandbox isn't really a toy. Whenever you add nonsense to your user pages, it attracts the attention of vandal fighters who've seen real vandals do the things you were doing many, many times before. So I hope you've learnt your lesson not to even look like a vandal. -- Netsnipe ► 19:19, 4 September 2006 (UTC)

This isn't an entirely fair message. It is incumbent upon vandal fighters to make sure what they are seeing, reverting and blocking is definitely vandalism. Sure, it is incumbent upon editors to, well, edit, but they should be safe in the knowledge that people aren't going to block them for editing their userpage and the sandbox.
