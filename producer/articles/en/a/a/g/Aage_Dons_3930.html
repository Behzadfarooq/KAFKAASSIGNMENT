Aage Dons (19 August 1903 - 20 October 1993) was a Danish writer.

He studied at University in 1919 and made his writing debut with Walpurgisnacht in 1931.

[edit] Notable works

1935 "Koncerten"
1936 "Soldaterbrønden. Roman"
1938 "De uønskede"
