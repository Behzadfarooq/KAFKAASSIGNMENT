Aage Kvalbein

Aage Kvalbein (born Oslo 1947) is a Norwegian cellist and a professor in cello at the Norwegian Academy of Music. He is one of the most well-renowned musicians in Norway, both as a soloist, chamber musician and as a pedagogue.

He's published approximately 40 records with music from various composers.

At age 33, he became the first professor of cello in Norway. He is married to the Norwegian actress Mari Maurstad.
