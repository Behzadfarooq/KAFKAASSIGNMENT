Aappilattoq (old spelling: Augpilagtoq) is a village in the southernmost part of Greenland located at approximately 60°08′N 44°18′W, roughly 50 km from Kap Farvel. Population ca. 160. It is situated near 'some of the world's most awesome fjords' according to Lonely Planet.
