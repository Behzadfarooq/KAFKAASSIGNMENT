Aaptos aaptos

Aaptos aaptos is a sea sponge belonging to the Phylum Porifera.

This particular species is known to contain adrenoreceptor-blocking compounds. While it is highly toxic to fish, it is known to be preyed upon by the hawksbill turtle, Eretmochelys imbricata.[1]
