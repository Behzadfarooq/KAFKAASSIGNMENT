Whether a class of storage is commonly called memory or storage depends on several factors, not the least of them being tradition. Here are some common characteristics:

Likely called Technology Latency Volatility Capacity Distance
Memory Semiconductor Fast (less than 1 ms) Volatile Relatively small (<1-1000 MB) Primary
Storage Magnetic or optical Slow (more than 10 ms) Non-volatile Relatively large (>1 GB) Not primary
