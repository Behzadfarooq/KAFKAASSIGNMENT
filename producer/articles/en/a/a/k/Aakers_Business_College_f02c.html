Aakers Business College is a private two-year college in Fargo, North Dakota with a satellite campus in Bismarck, North Dakota. The school offers associate degrees in several fields.

Aakers was originally founded in Fargo in 1902 and a second location was opened in Grand Forks, North Dakota in 1905. The Fargo location was sold in 1912 and the Grand Forks location became the only Aakers for decades. In 1998, the Grand Forks location was closed and operations were again moved to Fargo. The Bismarck campus opened in 2003.
