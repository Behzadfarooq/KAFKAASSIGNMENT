Aaken ja Sakun kesäkeittiö is a 1999 Finnish cookery television series that was shown on Sundays during the summer of 1999. It first aired on June 20, 1999 on MTV3 and lasted one season.

The series was written and directed by Ari Lohenoja, presented by Aake Kalliala and Sakari Kuosmanen.
