The AA Southwest District is a high school conference of the Virginia High School League that includes schools from Southwest Virginia, United States, and is mostly in the Bluefield, West Virginia area. The schools of the Southwest District compete in AA Region IV with the schools in the AA Highlands District and the AA Piedmont District. The Highlands District is expected to dissolve after the 2006-2007 school year with the remaining three schools joining the Southwest District.

[edit] Member Schools

Carroll County Cavaliers, Hillsville

Graham G-Men, Bluefield

Grundy Golden Wave, Grundy

Richlands Blue Tornadoes, Richlands

Tazewell Bulldogs, Tazewell
