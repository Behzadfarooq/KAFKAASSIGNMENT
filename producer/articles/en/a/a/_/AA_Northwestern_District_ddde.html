The AA Northwestern District is a high school conference in the state of Virginia that currently stretches from Shenandoah County to Prince William County. The Northwestern District member schools compete in Region II with the schools from the AA Dulles District, AA Jefferson District, and AA Valley District.

The Northwestern District was not known for being strong in football, but things have changed. The district was one of the strongest in AA this season with the dominance of Sherando and Liberty and the emergence of James Wood, Millbrook, and Brentsville as solid football teams. Even with Liberty's departure coming next year, the district will boast 4 teams that finished at .500 or better. In basketball, Millbrook finished second in the region and falling in the state quarterfinals in 2005, one year after a last place finish in the district in their inaugural year.

[edit] Changes in 2007-2008

Because of increased enrollment, Liberty High School is now classified as a AAA school and will move to the AAA Cedar Run District of the AAA Northwest Region. Warren County High School is also classified as a Group AAA school due to increased enrollment, but will remain in the district, because a new high school is expected to open in the 2008-2009 school year.
