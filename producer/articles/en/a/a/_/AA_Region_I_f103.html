AA Region I

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Region I is one of the four AA regions of the Virginia High School League. It is made up of four districts: the AA Battlefield District, the AA Bay Rivers District, the A/AA Eastern Shore District, and the AA Southside District. The region stretches the entire eastern section of the Commonwealth.
