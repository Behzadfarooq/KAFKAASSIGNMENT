AA Region II

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Region II is one of the four AA regions in the Virginia High School League. It is made up of four districts:the AA Dulles District, the AA Jefferson District, the AA Northwestern District, and the AA Valley District.

The geography of the region stretches from Northern Virginia, primarily in Loudoun County down to Charlottesville and the Shenandoah Valley.

Due to rapidly-growing populations in the area requiring new schools and expanding enrollment of schools previously in Group A, the Region now has more schools than any other VHSL Group AA region. In the 2007-09 redistricting plan, the Valley District is tenatively moving to Region III. Region II has lost several members to Group AAA over the last 10 years. In 2007, Liberty High School will be moving up to Group AAA. Warren County High School would move up to Group AAA, but the opening of Skyline High School in the fall of 2007 will allow both schools will be able to remain in Group AA.
