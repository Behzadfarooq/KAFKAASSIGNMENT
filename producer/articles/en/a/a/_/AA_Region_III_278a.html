Region III is one of the four AA regions in the Virginia High School League. It is made up of three districts: the AA Blue Ridge District, the AA Seminole District, and the AA River Ridge District. Currently, the region consists largely of schools around Roanoke, Virginia and Lynchburg, Virginia. In an upcoming realignment, the River Ridge District will be placed into Region IV, which is the smallest region because many of its member schools in Southwest Virginia have lost enrollment and dropped into Group A. In turn, Region II's AA Valley District will be placed into Region III as two new districts named AA Southern Valley and AA Massanutten. This comes into effect the 2007-2008 athletic seasons.
