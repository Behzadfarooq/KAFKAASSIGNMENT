Aa Ii na! (あぁ いいな!, Aa Ii na!?) is the second single of the J-pop duo, W. It was released on August 4, 2004 on the zetima label.

[edit] Track listing

  1. Aa Ii na! (あぁ いいな!, Aa Ii na!?) - 3:51
  2. Otome no Keitai Denwa no Himitsu (乙女の携帯電話の秘密, Otome no Keitai Denwa no Himitsu?) - 3:52
  3. Aa Iina! (Instrumental) (あぁ いいな! (Instrumental), Aa Iina! (Instrumental)?) - 3:48
