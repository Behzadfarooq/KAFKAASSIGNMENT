The AA Piedmont District is a high school conference of the Virginia High School League that includes schools from Southwest Virginia mostly in the Martinsville and the Danville areas. The schools of the Piedmont District compete in AA Region IV with the schools in the AA Highlands District and the AA Southwest District. Until the 1990s, the AA Piedmont District was a member of AA Region III but was moved to balance the number of schools in the VHSL's AA regions.

The Piedmont District has traditionally been strong at boys' basketball, with both Martinsville High School and Magna Vista High School having won multiple State Championships. Martinsville High School has won more State Championships (a total of 13) than any other school in the Commonwealth of Virginia.
