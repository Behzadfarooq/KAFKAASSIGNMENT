The AA Battlefield District is one of the four districts of AA Region I of the Virginia High School League which consists of schools in Fredericksburg and surrounding area. Most of the schools in the district are in Spotsylvania County, which is one of fastest growing counties in Virginia. The district is named for the Civil War battlefields around Fredericksburg.

The schools in the Battlefield District compete in Region I with the schools in the AA Bay Rivers District, A/AA Eastern Shore District, and the AA Southside District. Courtland, Spotsylvania, and James Monroe won multiple football state titles during the 1980s and 1990s.

In 2007, Riverbend High School will leave the Battlefield District and join the AAA Commonwealth District.

[edit] District Championships

  • 2006-2007 Boys winter track-Riverbend
  • 2006-2007 Scholastic Bowl- James Monroe
  • 2005-2006 Scholastic Bowl- Caroline
  • 2004-2005 Boys' Tennis- James Monroe
  • 2004 Girls Spring Track State Champions - Courtland
