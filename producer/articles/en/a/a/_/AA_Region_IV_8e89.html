AA Region IV

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

AA Region IV is one of the four AA regions in the Virginia High School League. It is made up of three districts: the AA Highlands District, the AA Piedmont District, and the AA Southwest District.

The members of Region IV are all located in Southwest Virginia and is also the smallest region in terms of membership in AA, due to many of its members losing membership and dropping into Group A. Gate City High School, Lebanon High School, John S. Battle High School, Radford High School, Giles High School, and George Wythe High School are some of the schools that have dropped in classification since the 1980's. In Henry County, Virginia, Laurel Park High School and Fieldale-Collinsville High School closed after the 2001-2002 school year, with their student populations consolidated into Magna Vista and Bassett High Schools. Some schools, such as Graham High School and Virginia High School, have Group A sized student bodies but currently choose to play in Group AA. In addition, Lee High School is over 90 minutes from their closest Group AA opponent, leading to travel difficulties. Because of this, in the 2007-2009 playing cycle, there will be a considerable shifting among AA districts to different regions, including the moving of the AA River Ridge District into Region IV from Region III, and the declaration of a travel hardship for Lee, allowing them to play in Group A. The AA Piedmont District was also a member of Region III until the 1990s. The remaining three schools in the Highlands District are expected to join the Southwest District.

[edit] Member Schools

AA Highlands District

  • Abingdon High School of Abingdon, Virginia
  • Lee High School of Jonesville, Virginia
  • Marion Senior High School of Marion, Virginia
  • Virginia High School of Bristol, Virginia

AA Piedmont District

  • Bassett High School of Bassett, Virginia
  • Dan River High School of Ringgold, Virginia
  • Magna Vista High School of Ridgeway, Virginia
  • Martinsville High School of Martinsville, Virginia
  • Patrick County High School of Stuart, Virginia
  • Tunstall High School of Dry Fork, Virginia

AA Southwest District

  • Carroll County High School of Hillsville, Virginia
  • Graham High School of Bluefield, Virginia
  • Grundy Senior High School of Grundy, Virginia
  • Richlands High School of Richlands, Virginia
  • Tazewell High School of Tazewell, Virginia
