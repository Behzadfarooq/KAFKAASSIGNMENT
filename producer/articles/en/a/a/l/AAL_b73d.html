AAL is a three-letter acronym that may refer to:

  • Anglo American (mining), AAL is the stock symbol for the large mining enterprise
  • ICAO airline designator for American Airlines
  • Antalya Anadolu Lisesi, Antalya Anatolian High School
  • ATM Adaptation Layers, a technology used in computing information transfer
  • Australian Air League, Australian Air Cadet Organisation
  • Anatomical Automatic Labeling, a program and digital human brain atlas
  • IATA airport code for Aalborg Airport in Aalborg, Denmark
  • Aid Association for Lutherans, the name of Thrivent Financial for Lutherans before its name change in June, 2002
  • Anterior Axillary Line, a vertical line along the anterior axillary (underarm) fold
  • Asociación Argentina de Letras, the Argentine chapter of the Asociación de Academias de la Lengua Española (Association of Spanish Language Academies)

Aal may also refer to:

  • Noni, also known as Great morinda (Morinda citrifolia)
