[edit] About

Aaliyah Stevens is my name, convert to Islam without shame; no man, or husband can you blame, from my own intelligence, my conversion came, by the way Wiki entries on my religion are really lame! But now that I am here I will do my best, and life won't be the same!

My area of focus is, to date, almost exclusively Islam, Islamism, and the War on Terror related articles. Although I do intend to contribute to other articles related to security, philosophy, sociology, and politics, I'm rather tied up in these issues because of what I believe are a few individuals poisoning the factual accuracy of many articles in this field. Many Muslim students have complained to me about the bias and sometimes lies published in wikipedia. I hope to contribute positively to wikipedia, in which I firmly believe in as a system, and a philosophy of open source knowledge, so that it gains a reputation for reliable and verifiable information on Islamism and Islam.

[edit] Conversion

My conversion, after having stereotypes, myths and bias about Islam obliterated by feminist Islamists, was largely due to studying Al-Ghazali's and Al-Juwayni's Kalam Arguments academically, proving to me the viability of Islam, Allah or the one god, and his messenger Muhammad(saw). Many Muslims are unaware of thier rich philosophical history, especially in using rationalism in harmony with and to establish Yaqeen or conviction in Iman and tawheed.
