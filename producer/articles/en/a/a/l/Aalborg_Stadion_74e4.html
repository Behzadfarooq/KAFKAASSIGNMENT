Aalborg Stadion is a football stadium and the home ground of Aalborg Boldspilklub. It has a capacity of 16,000.

The stadium consists of 4 stands:

  • Two long-side stands:
    • The NORDJYSKE stand
    • The Sonofon stand (formerly Siemens stand)
  • Two end stands:
    • The Spar Nord stand
    • The Faxe Kondi stand
