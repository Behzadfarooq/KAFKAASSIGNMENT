Dr. Aamir Liaquat Hussain (born July 5, 1971 in Karachi ) is a Pakistani minister of state for religious affairs. He is an active member of the Muttahida Qaumi Movement or MQM. He is best known in South Asia as an anchor of a program called 'Aalim Online' which is broadcasted on GEO TV. He is known for his dramatic style of anchoring and recitation.

[edit] Early Life and Career

Aamir Liaquat studied medicine at the University of Sindh.

He served as an editor of "The Daily Parcham" and as program director for Radio Asia in the UAE.

Aamer Liaquat Hussain, has the distinction of being GEO's first newscaster & has subsequently became the channels leading anchor and host. He is a household name and his program Aalim Online has completely revolutionized the concept of religious programming in the region. By placing scholars from different sects of Islam on the same panel, Aalim online has gone a long way in creating an environment of sectarian harmony.

Born on July 5th, 1971 in Karachi, Aamer Liaquat Hussain belongs to a family that has been prominent in politics. He studied medicine at the University of Sindh and while still a student excelled in debates, journalism, broadcasting & campus politics.

It was during his studies that Aamer Liaquat Hussain became an icon of the debating fraternity winning the “Best Debator of Asia” award bestowed by the International Youth Congress, United Nations. In addition he was declared “Best Debator of Pakistan” {Eight Times}, Sindh {Thrice} & Karachi, {17 times}.

His interest in journalism flowered in the nineties when he joined “The Daily Parcham” as its Editor. Under his stewardship the newspaper attained new heights. Aamer Liaquat worked for “Radio Asia” in the UAE, as program director and was the named best Urdu Newsreader by the Government of Ras Al Khaima, United Arab Emirates.

Prior to joining Geo as a Director Religious Affairs, Aamer Liaquat worked in the social sectors as program manager of the Teachers Resource Centre, a well known NGO devoted to education.

Aamer has the distinction of presenting a marathon National Solidarity Program which he conducted for 14 hours without a break. This was a milestone in Pakistan’s broadcast history.

Aamer has a sensitive nature and his poems reflect this side of his personality. A collection of his poetry was published under the title “Sarey Zamaney Ki Sada Hai” {My voice is the voice of times}.

Aamer Liaquat Hussain was elected to the National Assembly in 2002 and is a legislator of distinction.

The President of Pakistan, General Parvaiz Musharaf is a self confessed fan of Aamer Liaquat and his program Aalim Online. To quote the president;

“Aalim on line is doing a great service for Islam. Aamer Liaquat is amazing. I like to watch him and hear him. He is doing a great service for Islam through Geo. I am the biggest fan of Dr. Aamer Liaquat Hussain.”

Praise indeed, from the most powerful in Pakistan. Such fulsome praise has been showered on him by fans that are from every class, every gender and every age.

[edit] Fake degree controversy

He has also been accused of acquiring a fake post-graduate degree, due to this reason he is some times referred to as "Dr. Fake" After further investigations by the media and the Higher Education Commission of Pakistan, it was discovered that Mr Liaquat had also submitted fake bachelor's degrees when he stood for election. Because of his fake personality some people also call his program "Jahil Online".
