Aamna Shariff

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Aamna Shariff born 16 July was a lead actress in the Balaji Telefilm-sponsored serial (soap opera) Kahiin To Hoga. Her show is a hit and her character is very popular among Indian TV viewers.

It is rumoured that she is going out with Rajeev Khandelwal ( Ex- Sujal of the Show Kahiin To Hoga)
