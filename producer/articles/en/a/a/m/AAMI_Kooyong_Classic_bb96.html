AAMI Kooyong Classic

The AAMI Kooyong Classic is an international men's tennis tournament held in Kooyong, a suburb of Melbourne, Australia. The tournament is not related to the ATP Tour and players participating in the tournament will not gain points for the ATP rankings.

In the tournament eight high ranked players from the ATP Tour will face each other in a promotion/relegation type format. Each of the eight participants play three matches over four days.
