Aadavari Matalaku Ardhalu Verule (Telugu: ఆడవారి మాటాలకు అర్ధాలు వేరులే , English: The Words of Women Have Different Meanings) is a forthcoming Telugu language film, to be directed by Selvaraghavan starring Venkatesh, Trisha Krishnan and Srikanth in lead roles. It also stars Kota Srinivasa Rao, Nadia Moidu and Sivaji in a supporting roles. The film has been said to be a mature love triangle story set in a metropolis revolving around three people [1].

According to reports, Trisha will be seen essaying the role of a modern and aggressive working lady [2]. Nothing else has been revealed about the story.

Yuvan Shankar Raja will be composing the music for the film. Editing will be handled by Anthony whilst cinematography is handled by Arvind Krishna who also previously worked with Raghavan in Pudhupettai. Selvaraghavan writes the film. The Paruchuri Brothers handle the dialogues.

The entire film will be shot at several locations in India including Chennai, Hyderabad and Bangalore. The shooting for the film has just recently begun. The first schedule of the film was shot at studios at the Ramanaidu Cine Village in Nanakramguda, Hyderabad [3]. Shooting is in full swing. The producers plan to release the movie in March or April of 2007.

[edit] Trivia

  • Jyothika was actually the first choice to play the heroine when the film was announced in 2005. She later dropped out of the film due to her marriage being near. She was subsequently replaced with Trisha [2].
  • The title for this film was actually taken from a song from N. T. Rama Rao's Missamma.
  • The film has erected a 25 lakh(Rs.only) set at the Ramanaidu Studio for some major scenes.
