Aadnya Borkar (born September 15, 1987 in Mumbai, India), is an Indian figure skater currently living and practicing in Oman. She is coached by Michael Fernandes. Borkar is the 2005-2006 Indian national champion and has competed internationally for three seasons on the Junior Grand Prix.
