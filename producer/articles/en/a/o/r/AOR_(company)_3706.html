AOR (company)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

AOR, Ltd. ( Authority on Radio communications Ltd.) is a Japanese based manufacturer of radio equipment, including transceivers, scanners, antennas and frequency monitors.

Established in 1977 when two radio amateurs decided to go professional. Based in Tokyo, Japan, they also have offices in the U.K. and the U.S., and manufacturing facilities in Japan and in the U.K..
