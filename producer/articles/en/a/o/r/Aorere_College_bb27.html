Aorere College is a co-educational state secondary school (years 9-13) that was established in 1964 in Papatoetoe, Manukau, Auckland, New Zealand. It is overseen by the Manukau City local authority, with M J Williams as principal. As of 2005 there were 1,215 students enrolled.


[edit] Aorere Rise

The school song "Aorere Rise" was written by then-Aorere College student Agnes S. in 2006, as part of a competition which it won "hands down."
