AO Kavala (Greek: Athlitikos Omilos Kavala), the Athletic Club Kavala, is a professional football (soccer) club based in the city of Kavala, Greece.

was formed in 1965 from the merger of Phillipoi Kavala, Iraklis Kavala and A.E. Kavala. They have had a few runs in the top division of the Greek League, Alpha Ethniki: (1970 - 1975, 1977 - 1982, 1997 - 2000).

Team also owns Kavala Stadium
