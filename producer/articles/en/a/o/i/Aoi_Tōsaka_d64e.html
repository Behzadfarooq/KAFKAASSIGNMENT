Aoi Tōsaka is a character from the Japanese novel Fate/Zero, the prequel to Fate/stay night.

[edit] Character

Aoi Tōsaka

Aoi Zenjō is wife of Tokiomi Tōsaka and the childhood friend of Kariya Matō. She is the mother of Tokiomi's two daughters, Rin and Sakura.

Aoi is a passive and loyal woman who does not interfere with her husband's affairs, described by Kirei Kotomine as the ideal version of a traditional wife. She accepted Tokiomi's proposal with full knowledge of what it meant to marry a mage. While Kariya loved her as well, he peacefully gave up pursuing her affections because she seemed happy accepting Tokiomi's proposal. It is implied that Aoi and Kariya remained in touch, given that her daughters, especialy Sakura, were fond of Kariya's company. [1]

Tokiomi's sudden decision to give Sakura away to Zōken Matō comes as a shock to Aoi, though she does not openly oppose his decision. She leaves at the beginning of the Holy Grail War with Rin to live at her parents' home for the duration of the conflict.
