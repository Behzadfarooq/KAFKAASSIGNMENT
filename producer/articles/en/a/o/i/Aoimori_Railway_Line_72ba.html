The Aoimori Railway Line (青い森鉄道線, Aoimori Tetsudō-sen?) is a rail line in Japan. Part of the Aoimori Railway Company, it runs from Metoki Station in Sannohe to Hachinohe Station in Hachinohe, Aomori Prefecture.

Formerly part of the JR Tohoku Main Line, it was separated from JR in 2002. Freight and express trains continue to use the line.
