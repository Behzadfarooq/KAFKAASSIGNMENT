[edit] What do I do here?

In the past, I was a regular contributor to Wikipedia, especially in the area of Hawaiiana (particularly history and geography), and United States geography. However, my priorities have shifted far away from Wikipedia (and my life as become ridiculously busy), so for the time being I will likely only contribute minor edits (e.g. grammar, reverting vandalism, etc.).
