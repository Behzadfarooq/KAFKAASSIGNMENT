The tall white-and-black building is the Aon Center in downtown Chicago. To the right, in front, is the Fairmont Hotel. Behind it is Two Prudential Plaza. At the base of the buildings, the three levels of Columbus Drive can be seen. The construction in the foreground is the Lakeshore East development.

I, Knowledge Seeker, took this picture on March 5, 2005. It was taken from Upper Wacker Drive. The view faces southwest.
