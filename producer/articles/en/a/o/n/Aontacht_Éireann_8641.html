Aontacht Éireann

Aontacht Éireann or Irish Unity was a short lived Irish political party founded by Kevin Boland (former Fianna Fáil government minister) after his resignation from that party in 1971.

The party had a hawkish line on the existence of Northern Ireland and failed to get any TDs elected to Dáil Éireann.
