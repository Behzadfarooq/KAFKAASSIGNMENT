Aomori Station

Aomori Station is a railway and bus station located in Aomori, Aomori, Japan.

    • Tsugaru Line（Tsugaru-Kaikyo Line）
      • For.Kanita,Minmaya,Hakodate and Sapporo

[edit] Route　Bus

  • Aomori municipal bus
    • For.Aomori Airport（Getting off is impossible to Aomori Airport
    • For.Sannai Maruyama remains
    • For.Namioka Station via Sinjyo and Daishaka
    • For.Asamushi-Onsen Station or Asamushi aquarium
  • Kōnan Bus
    • For.Yadamae
    • For.Kuroishi Station via Sinjyo, Daishaka and Namioka（For.The Hirosaki is transferring in Namioka）
    • For.Goshogawara Station via Sinjyo, Daishaka and Harako
  • JR Bus Tōhoku
    • For.Lake Towada via Sukayu and Yakeyama
    • For.Asamushi-Onsen Station via Nonai
  • Towada Kankō Bus
    • For.Towada via Shichinohe（Limited Express Bus）
  • Shimokita Bus
    • For.Aomori（Only for getting off）
    ※Mutsu line please take it from "Kanko Bussankan".
