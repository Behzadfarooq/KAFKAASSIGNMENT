Aomori Television

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Aomori Television Broadcasting Co.,Ltd. (株式会社青森テレビ, Kabushiki-gaisha Aomori TV?), also known as ATV, is a Japanese broadcast network affiliated with the JNN. Their headquarters are located in Aomori Prefecture.

[edit] History

  • 1969 December 1 It was set up second broadcasting station of Aomori Prefecture.
  • 2006 July 1 Digital terrestrial television was started(Aomori main station).
