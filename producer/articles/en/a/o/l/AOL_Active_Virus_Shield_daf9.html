AOL Active Virus Shield
Developer: AOL (Underlying engine created by Kaspersky)
OS: Microsoft Windows
Use: Antivirus
License: freeware
Website: http://www.activevirusshield.com/

AOL Active Virus Shield (AVS) is a free antivirus utility made available by AOL. Its engine is based on the one used by Kaspersky Antivirus 6. Active Virus Shield offers real-time protection.

[edit] Pros and cons

Active Virus Shield" is light compared to other commercial anti-virus products. It also does not consume much memory during startup. However, it does lack some features of Kaspersky 6.0 (such as proactive protection and HTTP protection), and the license provided is only valid for one year.
