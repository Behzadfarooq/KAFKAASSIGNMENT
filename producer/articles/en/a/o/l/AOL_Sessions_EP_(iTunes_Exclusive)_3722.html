AOL Sessions EP (iTunes Exclusive) is Dashboard Confessional's fourth EP released exclusively on iTunes. It contains three recordings from AOL Music Sessions (found free online) and an acoustic version of "Stolen" previously released with pre-orders of Dusk and Summer purchased on iTunes. Accompanying these four tracks were videos of the three AOL Sessions tracks, though it is now being sold with a video only for "Rooftops and Invitations." The following is the track listing currently being sold:

[edit] Track listing

  1. "Don't Wait (Live)" – 4:08
  2. "Rooftops and Invitations (Live) (Video)" – 3:58
  3. "Rooftops and Invitations (Live)" – 3:53
  4. "The Secret's In The Telling (Live)" – 3:17
  5. "Stolen (Live Acoustic in Los Angeles)" – 4:26
