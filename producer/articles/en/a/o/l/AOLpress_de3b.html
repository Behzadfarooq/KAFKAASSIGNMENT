AOLpress

AOLpress was an HTML editor available from AOL. It was originally developed as NaviPress by the company NaviSoft before being bought by AOL. It was discontinued in 2000. However, the last version (2.0) can still be found on some websites. The HTML code used is very outdated and may not display more recent websites.

It actively pushed AOL's PrimeHost service.
