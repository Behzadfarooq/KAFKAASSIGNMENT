Aozora ni Yureru Mitsugetsu no Kobune. (蒼空に揺れる蜜月の小舟。, Aozora ni Yureru Mitsugetsu no Kobune.?) is Yukari Tamura's third album, released on November 6, 2003.

[edit] Track listing

  1. Primary Tale
    • Lyrics and composition: marhy
    • Arrangement: Tetsuya Uchida and marhy
    • Opening theme song of her radio show, Snuggery of Black Bunny (田村ゆかりの黒うさぎの小部屋, Tamura Yukari no Kuro Usagi no Kobeya?).
  2. Lovely Magic
    • Lyrics and composition: Mika Watanabe
    • Arrangement: Mika Watanabe and Kanichirou Kubo
    • Opening theme song of her radio show, Snuggery of Black Bunny (田村ゆかりの黒うさぎの小部屋, Tamura Yukari no Kuro Usagi no Kobeya?).
  3. 大好きと涙 (, Daisuki to Namida?)
    • Lyrics and composition: Kazuyuki Shibayama
    • Arrangement: Hirokazu Inuyama
  4. フルーツ (, FURUTSU?)
    • Lyrics: Mika Watanabe and Rie Yoshihara
    • Composition: Mika Watanabe
    • Arrangement: Mika Watanabe, Kanichirou Kubo
  5. きらら時間旅行 (, Kirara Jikan Ryokou?)
    • Lyrics: 成田景
    • Composition: Tomonori Hotta
    • Arrangement: Yasunari Nakamura
    • Ending theme song of her radio show, Snuggery of Black Bunny (田村ゆかりの黒うさぎの小部屋, Tamura Yukari no Kuro Usagi no Kobeya?).
  6. Snow bird
    • Lyrics, arrangement and composition:Masatomo Ota
  7. 月華 (, Tsukihana?)
    • Lyrics: Tomomi Haraguchi
    • Arrangement and compositio: Masahiro Inada

    • Arrangement and composition: Tsugumi Kataoka
  9. 虹の奇跡 (, Niji no Kiseki?)
    • Lyrics and composition: marhy
    • Arrangement: Tetsuya Uchida and marhy
  10. 恋歌姫　～蜜月夜バージョン～ (, Koi Utahime ~Mitsugetsu Yoru BAAJON~?)
    • Lyrics, arrangement and composition: Masatomo Ota
  11. 不可触な愛 (, Fukasawa na Ai?)
    • Lyrics: Kaori Kawaguchi
  12. Honey Moon
    • Lyrics: Yukari Tamura
    • Arrangement and composition: Masatomo Ota
