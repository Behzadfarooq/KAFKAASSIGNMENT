The Aotearoa Student Press Association (ASPA) is an association of thirteen student newspapers and magazines that are published by the student associations of universities and polytechnics in Aotearoa (New Zealand).

The members of ASPA are:

  • Caclin, Lincoln University
  • Canta, University of Canterbury
  • Chaff, Massey University, Palmerston North campus
  • Craccum, University of Auckland
  • Crew, Universal College of Learning
  • Critic, University of Otago
  • Debate, Auckland University of Technology
  • gYRo, Otago Polytechnic
  • In Unison, Unitec
  • Magneto, Massey University, Wellington campus
  • Nexus, Waikato University
  • Salient, Victoria University of Wellington
  • Satellite, Massey University, Albany campus

ASPA has had an associate membership in the New Zealand House of Representatives press gallery since 2005, and runs a collaborative news wire.

The association has held an annual awards ceremony since 2002; since 2004 the awards have been held in association with the magazine the New Zealand Listener, with prizes including subscriptions and an internship with the magazine. The ASPA Awards are judged by a collection of New Zealand media experts and commentators - previous judges have included Chris Knox, Nicky Hager, Raybon Kan, and John Campbell.
