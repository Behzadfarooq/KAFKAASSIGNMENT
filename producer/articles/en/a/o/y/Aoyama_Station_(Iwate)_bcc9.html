Aoyama Station (青山駅, Aoyama Eki?) is a railway station on the Iwate Ginga Railway Iwate Ginga Railway Line. It is located in Morioka, Iwate, Japan.

[edit] Station Structure

  • 2-way, 2-track, above-ground station.
Platforms
1 ■Iwate Ginga Railway Line For.Iwate-Numakunai, Ninohe and Hachinohe
■Hanawa Line For.Ōbuke, Araya-Shinmachi and Kazuno-Hanawa
2 ■■Iwate Ginga Railway Line For.Morioka and Kitakami
  • Aoyama Station is a simple consignment station, administered by Morioka Station.
    • Business hours: 6:50 a.m. - 7:30 p.m.
