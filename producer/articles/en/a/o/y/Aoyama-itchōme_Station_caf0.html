Aoyama-itchōme Station

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Aoyama-itchōme Station (青山一丁目駅, -eki?) is a railway station in Minato, Tokyo, Japan.

[edit] Lines

  • Toei Oedo Line (E-24)
  • Tokyo Metro Ginza Line (G-04)
  • Tokyo Metro Hanzomon Line (Z-03)

[edit] Station Layout

There is a report passage between Hanzomon Line and Oedo Line. The transfer from Ginza Line to Oedo Line should pass the platform of Hanzomon Line.

[edit] History

November 18, 1938 Opened as a Ginza Line Station.
August 1, 1978 Hanzomon Line opened.
December 12, 2000 Oedo Line opened.
