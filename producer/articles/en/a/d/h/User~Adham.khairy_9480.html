Full Name: Adham Khairy Abdeen Mahmoud Aziz Birth Date: Monday 07-June-1982 Place of Birth: White Crescent Hospital - Alexandria, Egypt Education: High school diploma from University Schools - Dhahran, Kingdom of Saudi Arabia B.S in Architectural Engineering - College of Environmental Design, K.F.U.P.M., Dhahran, Kingdom of Saudi Arabia

e-mail address: adham.khairy@yahoo.com, adham.khairy@hotmail.com

website: obscure and melancholic This web log created to advocate Adham's ideas regarding social reforms and women and human rights in Egypt and in the Arab world in general. Adham is also a strong supporter of the peace agreement between Egypt and Israel and that Arab countries should use such agreements to focus more on the social and economic reforms in their own countries. He also calls for banning of woman circumcision and the elimination of illiteracy which according to him is the main obstacle in front of any democratic attempt in Egypt.

Attempted a master's degree in structural engineering in K.F.U.P.M. but two years in the program changed his plans and went to Egypt to serve in the military and according to him to "participate in the social reforms" in his home country.

Professionally, Adham's architectural designs shows strong admiration for exposed structural elements, clean outlines and natural ventilation and lighting. Adham got interested in the concept of Green buildings early on on his college studies.

Being obese since childhood, Adham tried to lose weight several times through his college years but failed miserably, mainly due to a persistence depression that peaked three months before his coming back to Egypt on the 7th of Dec 2006. Listens to almost all kinds of music from Metallica to Tchaikovsky's swan lake, he also plays guitar. Some of his other hobbies include freehand sketching, photography and watching movies.
