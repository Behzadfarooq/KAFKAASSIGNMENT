Adhemir de Barros best known as Paraná (born in Cambará, Paraná, March 21, 1942) was a Brazilian footballers. He played at São Bento, São Paulo, Londrina, and Brazil national football team, which had participated at 1966 FIFA World Cup. Currently he is a district functionary of the Sorocaba prefecture.
