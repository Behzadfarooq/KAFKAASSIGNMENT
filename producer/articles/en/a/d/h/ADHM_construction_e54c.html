ADHM construction

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The ADHM construction is the construction of all instantons by Michael Atiyah, Vladimir G. Drinfel'd, Nigel. J. Hitchin, Yuri I. Manin in their paper Construction of Instantons.

[edit] References

  • Construction of Instantons, Michael Atiyah, Vladimir G. Drinfel'd, Nigel. J. Hitchin, Yuri I. Manin reproduced in Instantons in Gauge Theory by M. Shifman.
