[edit] Help:Edit summary

Hi. The text at the bottom of all pages coming from Meta says quite clearly "You are welcome to copy the exact wikitext from the master page at Meta and paste it into this page at any time". It also says "Copies of this page on other projects, for other languages see Meta".

The text is designed to be an exact copy, so users (and bots) can easily copy and paste the text without having to worry about making changes specifically for their project. There are two templates associated with these pages, which automatically display when the page is viewed. Template:Ph:Edit summary is the main one for the edit summary page. I will paste the links there, surrounded by <includeonly> tags. If this doesn't work I will delete them, based on the fact Help:Edit summary is meant to be an exact copy and links to other languages exist on Meta. Thanks. -- Gareth Aus 05:46, 27 May 2006 (UTC)

Well it works. If you intend to create interlanguage links, please use the templates (with <includeonly> tags) in the future. Thanks. -- Gareth Aus 05:56, 27 May 2006 (UTC)
