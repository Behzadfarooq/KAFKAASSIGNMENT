Adnan Ćustović (born April 14, 1978 in Mostar) is a Bosnian footballer currently playing at R.E. Mouscron. Before joining Mouscron, Custovic mainly played or French division 1 and 2 teams Le Havre AC, Stade Lavallois and Amiens SC.

He made his national team debut against Norway in 24 March 2007.
