Hi,

My name is Adam. I live in Canberra, Australia. I'm 28 years old.

My wiki interests include:

  • aviation - particularly commercial airlines and civilian airports.
  • towns and cities
  • politics and geo-politics
  • transport
  • any combination of the above
  • I also like sorting out categories and finding inter-wiki links.

I created the Flight West article and contributed to the Wrocław International Airport article before creating my wiki username. I've contributed to Canberra, various Canberra suburbs, Cairns, various airports pages, various Brisbane related pages, particularly Brisbane suburbs and various airline pages, including Impulse Airlines.

Photos I have uploaded: County Court of Victoria Australian Treasury building Brisbane CityCat Old Melbourne Gaol Brisbane Powerhouse Brisbane Powerhouse again Queenslander (house) and another one and another one Regatta Hotel, Brisbane Russell St Police HQ Toowong Tuggeranong a few pics of Ainslie, ACT and Dickson ACT and other places around Canberra.
