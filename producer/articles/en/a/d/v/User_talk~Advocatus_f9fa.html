[edit] Introduction

Welcome!

Hello, Advocatus, and welcome to Wikipedia! Thank you for your contributions. I hope you like the place and decide to stay. Here are some pages that you might find helpful:

  • The five pillars of Wikipedia
  • How to edit a page
  • Help pages
  • Tutorial
  • How to write a great article
  • Manual of Style

I hope you enjoy editing here and being a Wikipedian! Please sign your name on talk pages using four tildes (~~~~); this will automatically produce your name and the date. If you need help, check out Wikipedia:Questions, ask me on my talk page, or place {{helpme}} on your talk page and someone will show up shortly to answer your questions. Again, welcome!

Wikipedia is not the place to discuss plans with your friend(s). Wikipedia user talk and article talk pages are meant to be used for messages relating to the content of our encyclopedia. For instance, you should not post messages asking whether your real life friend is going to the YMCA. The proper place to ask such questions is on e-mail, the phone, or an instant messanger service. On Wikipedia, personal messages are not tolerated. All of your interaction with other users on this website should be related to the encyclopedia's content, not your afternoon plans. Have a great day!
