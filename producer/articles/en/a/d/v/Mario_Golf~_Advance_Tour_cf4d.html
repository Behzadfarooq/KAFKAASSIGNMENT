Mario Golf: Advance Tour, known in Japan as Mario Golf: GBA Tour (マリオゴルフ ＧＢＡツアー, Mario Golf: GBA Tour?), is a video game that was developed by Camelot Software Planning and published by Nintendo for the Game Boy Advance and released in 2004. It is the sequel to the Game Boy Color version of Mario Golf. The music was composed by well known video game composer Motoi Sakuraba.
