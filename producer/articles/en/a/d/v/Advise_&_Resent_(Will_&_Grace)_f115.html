"Advise & Resent" is the fifteenth episode of season two of the television situation comedy Will & Grace.

Spoiler warning: Plot and/or ending details follow.

[edit] Plot

Will puts up with Jack's advice as he considers going on a blind date organized by Ben; and Karen advises Grace on what to do with her wimpy boyfriend Josh.
