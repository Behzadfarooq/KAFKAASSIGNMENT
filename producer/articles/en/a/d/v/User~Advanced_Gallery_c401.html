Hello! Thanks for coming to visit my image gallery. This gallery consists of All the pictures I have uploaded. Almost all of the pictures are available for usage on the Wikimedia Commons.

The photos consist mostly of Public transport in Singapore, buildings and places in the city-state of which I call home, scenes in other countries that I have visited before, such as Malaysia, and many other misc. images.

All the photos shown can be used anywhere on Wikipedia. They are licensed mostly under CC-BY-SA-2.5, GFDL, or both. Feel free to use the images for any other purposes. However, I would really appreciate it if you drop a note on my main talk page. I'd be glad to see how they are used.=)
