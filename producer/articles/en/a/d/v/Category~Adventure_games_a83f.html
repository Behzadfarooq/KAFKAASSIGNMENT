The main article for this category is Adventure games.

Adventure games normally require the player to solve various puzzles and find various artifacts. The earliest adventure games were textual, then a hybrid of visual display with textual input, then a "point-n-click" visual interface. Mass market adventure games are less prevalent today, and many new entries into the genre are fan-created interactive fiction games and amateur adventure games.
