Adventure District

One important neighbourhood of Northeast Oklahoma City, USA is known as the Adventure District. Located roughly at NE 50th Street and Martin Luther King Jr. Avenue, the Adventure District is the city's first tourist area and is home to many of the city's great museums and major attractions.

[edit] Attractions

  • Oklahoma City Zoological Park
  • Six Flags Frontier City
  • Omniplex Science Museum
    • OmniDome Theatre
  • National Softball Hall of Fame and Museum
  • Oklahoma State Firefighters Museum
  • National Cowboy and Western Heritage Museum
  • Remington Park
  • 45 Division Infantry Museum
  • Tinseltown-USA
  • Coles Garden
