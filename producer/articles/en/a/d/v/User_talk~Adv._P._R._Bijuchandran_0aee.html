[edit] Happy Diwali

Happy Diwali!!!
Known as the "Festival of Lights" in India, Diwali symbolises the victory of good over evil. It represents celebration of hope and life, and is an occasion to strengthen relationships. This year, it is celebrated on April 2, 2007. I take this as an occasion to wish you the best in the days to come. May this Diwali invigorate you, bringing you more happiness and content.
