Adventures by Disney

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Adventures by Disney is the operating name for the series of all inclusive guided vacation tour packages run by Walt Disney Parks and Resorts, a unit of The Walt Disney Company.

Trialled during the summer months of 2005 in Wyoming and Hawaii (both USA), guests were taken on themed expeditions throughout natural scenery, on 'adventures'. The company, which is jointly managed between Walt Disney Parks and Resorts and The Walt Disney Travel Company, offers twelve specialized excursions that include the following four major areas:

North America with Mid-Atlantic, American Southwest, and Wyoming adventures

Latin America with Costa Rica adventures

The British Isles & Europe with Ireland, England & France, and Austria & Czech Republic adventures

Southern Europe with Spain, Southern Spain, Italy, Tuscany, and Southern Italy adventures
