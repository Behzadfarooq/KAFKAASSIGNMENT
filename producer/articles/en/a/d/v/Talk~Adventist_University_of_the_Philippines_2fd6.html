The University of Gheelh (UG) is situated in Pulong Yantok barangay, Angat municipality, Bulacan province of the Philippines. It is the center for agricultural education in the country. It has several campuses:

   * Pulong Yantok, Angat, Bulacan
         o UG-Talbac Campus (main)
         o UG-Sentinela I Campus
         o UG-Sentinela II Campus
         o UG-Bungad Campus

   * Pulong Buhangin, Santa Maria, Bulacan
         o UG-Garden Campus
         o UG-Perez Campus
         o UG-Manggahan Campus
         o UG-Caybalat Campus (College of Accountancy)
         o UG-Tierra in Daffodil St.
         o UG-Tierra in Camellia St.
         o UG Tierra in Camachile

   * Norzagaray, Bulacan
         o UG-Partida Garay Campus
         o UG-Diliman Campus

   * Doña Remedios Trinidad, Bulacan
         o UG-DRT Campus

   * Special Centers Cluster in CityLand Pulong Buhangin, Santa Maria, Bulacan:
         o UG-Conservatory of Music
         o UG-Center for English Language
         o UG-Open University

Affiliations with other schools:

   * StarsBorn Institute
   * University of Bermillo
   * Ryan College

The university is in partnership with the following large universities:

   * University of the Philippines (Diliman, Los Baños, Visayas)
