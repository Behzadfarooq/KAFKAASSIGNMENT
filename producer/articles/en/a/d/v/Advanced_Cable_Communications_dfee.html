Advanced Cable Communications is a division of Schurz Communications providing cable television, cable telephone, DVR, and broadband services to customers in the Florida communities of Coral Springs and Weston.[1] It operated independently as Coral Springs Cablevision prior to 1978, when it was purchased by Shurz.[2]
