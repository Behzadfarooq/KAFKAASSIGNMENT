[edit] Seiryo's original voice actor in Tenchi Muyo!

This is one of the voices in the Tenchi dub cast that I'm still not quite sure on. Some sources credit him to "John Lee", a name that appears in the VHS dub cast credits for episode 13 (but keep in mind that this was a list of names, not credited to the role). I am pretty certain that John Lee is John Rafter Lee, since he is a regular voice actor in dubs directed by Jack Fletcher. From what I remember hearing him as Aristotle in Reign, though, he has a pretty deep voice that is much closer to how Azusa Jurai sounds than does Seiryo. On the other hand, I still cannot be sure John DeMita (who is also in the VHS credits for the episode) is voicing Seiryo either. His other roles in the Tenchiverse which he has been attributed to in databases are uncredited, and don't know him too much from elsewhere (Dosu Kinuta in Naruto doesn't really sound similar; may have to look at his Princess Mononoke role again). --EmperorBrandon 18:21, 19 May 2006 (UTC)

[edit] Voice actors in Tenchi Muyo!

As you may have noticed, I added voice actors to the character profiles on the latest additions, because this is the preferred method of mentioning them in series articles (rather than a list). I am going to go ahead and remove the remaining lists and reattach the VA's to their characters in a consistent format. Be sure to message me if you have any feedback about the format. Placing them breaked after the kanji/kana seems to look nicely IMO, but I'm open if you have some different ideas on what to do. --EmperorBrandon 03:34, 1 June 2006 (UTC)

I do have one thing in particular to ask about Tenchi Muyo! GPX on aliases. Now, there is some concern about outing voice actors connecting aliases due to ethical concerns regarding the SAG. However, it seems several people editing Wikipedia (you're not the only one) are really intent on connecting them anyway. I don't really have much of a problem with it. It's only natural for fans to want to know the full body of work of a particular actor or actress. I am concerned a little bit, but I guess it should not be too much of a problem in the case of Tenchi Muyo! GXP. When FUNimation posted the cast list on the original website, they even told us to check databases for aliases. A few are not really surprising at all since they appeared in some of the other Tenchi series (all but the early OVA episodes and GXP are SAG-sanctioned) with their real names credited with the same voice.
So, I guess connecting voices to their real names is assumed when I edit the information in. I'm not really sure on some things, though, the format [real name] (credited as "alias") just seems to add a lot of garbage (IMO) that's not really needed since most of this information has been edited into the actor pages. I'm really wanting to do just plain [real name] when mentioning the English VA, with no mention of the alias at all. There is a kind of compromise position where I could use a piped link [real name|alias], thus the alias is the only thing that appears on the page but the alternate text shows the real name and it links to that page. Do you have any preference on what I do? GXP is going to be a pretty big edit, so I want to know beforehand. --EmperorBrandon 05:12, 1 June 2006 (UTC)
