The Advisor Answer is a talk radio show on WGOW FM on Saturday Mornings at 7:00am. Your host, Richard Cox, answers all types of financial questions and keeps you updated on the world of finance.


== Headline text == Your Host

Richard L. Cox Sr., CFP®, QFP, ChFC, CLU, MFP, CWM, RFC, ATA, FAAFM

Started his financial services career in 1976, He has earned the prestigious CERTIFIED FINANCIAL PLANNER™ (CFP®) certification from the Certified Financial Planner Board of Standards, Denver, CO, the Chartered Financial Consultant (ChFC®) and the Chartered Life Underwriter (CLU®) designations from the American College, Bryn Mawr, PA, the Accredited Tax Advisor (ATA®) designation from the Accreditation Council for Accountancy & Taxation, Alexandria, VA, the Registered Financial Consultant (RFC®) designation from the International Association of Registered Financial Consultants, and the Qualified Financial Planner (QFP) designation from the International Association of Qualified Financial Planners. Richard has also been accepted as a Fellow of the American Academy of Financial Management (FAAFM™) and has been awarded the Board Certified designations of Master Financial Professional™ (MFP™) and Chartered Wealth Manager™ (CWM™) by the Academy and has also been designated as a Fellow of the Life Underwriters Training Council, by the National Association of Insurance and Financial Advisors (LUTCF).

He is a current member of the following Industry Associations; the Financial Planning Association (FPA), the International Association of Qualified Financial Planners (IAQFP), the National Society of Accountants (NSA), the Estate Planning Council of Chattanooga (EPCC), the International Association of Registered Financial Consultants (IARFC), The American Academy of Financial Management (AAFM), The Christian Financial Professionals Network (CFPN), The Financial Services Institute (FSI), the Society of Financial Service Professionals (SFSP) and the National Association of Insurance and Financial Advisors (NAIFA).

Mr. Cox is currently serving on the Board of Directors of the following organizations; The Society of Financial Service Professionals, Chattanooga Chapter (Past President 2005-2006) (SFSP), and the Chattanooga Resource Foundation (Former Chairman 2004 - 2006) (CRF).

Richard and his wife Renee’ are Elders of River of Life Church, Rossville, GA (ROLC) and he serves as President of the Church Elder Board.

Mr. Cox is a Registered Principal of FSC Securities Corporation (FSC) and has served on its Field Advisory Board from 1995-2001. He is also an outside consultant to many financial services firms, and a contributing author to trade journals that service the financial planning industry nationwide.

Mr. Cox conducts a widely popular series of financial workshops throughout the year that help people understand the financial planning process, and how to make better informed financial decisions, including a special Biblically Based financial workshop to church congregations on financial stewardship from a Biblical perspective.

Mr. Cox is a well-known and respected author with local articles distributed through “The Chattanooga Times Free Press”, “Woman’s Way Magazine” and “Commercial Network Magazine”. He is a highly sought after speaker on a wide range of financial subjects, and is host of a local call-in radio program called, The Advisor An$wer ©which is broadcast Saturdays at 7:00 am EST on Station 102.3 FM Talk Radio.

== Headline text == The Radio Show The Beginning

Airing on Saturday September 11, 1999, he met the radio market with a storm of new hope for a market already saturated in the day-to-day "get rich quick" financial situation. Richard teaches and looks on the long and storm term effects of how the markets will impact your financial future.



== Headline text == Tuesday Night Lights

The 7pm home for the Advisor Answer has been the staple on Tuesday nights for many years. With that time slot move from saturday came much needed rest and a new market, the listeners that were just getting off work. The whole new crew of listeners brought a whole new base of clients. From then on we led the groups of listeners that other financial advisors left out in the cold or had no idea on how to need their needs.


== Headline text == Back To Saturday Mornings

Currently the show is back to the chicken hour of 7:00am. Richard has established himself as a figurehead in the Chattanooga area as far as finance goes. So he has turned his show into an arena where other professionals come in and share their experiences with the public.
