An adventure is an undertaking into the unknown, often connotating danger and excitement.

Adventure may refer to:

In film:

  • Adventure film, a film genre
  • Adventure (1945 film), a 1945 movie starring Clark Gable

In gaming:

  • Adventure game, a genre of computer games focusing on puzzle-solving and character interaction
  • Adventure or Colossal Cave Adventure, a landmark 1976 text-based adventure game
  • Adventure (Atari 2600), a 1978 video game for the Atari 2600
  • Adventure!, a pulp fiction tabletop role-playing game by White Wolf
  • Adventure (role-playing games), a specific pre-written plotline in role-playing games
    • Adventure (Dungeons & Dragons), a module or scenario for the Dungeons & Dragons role-playing game
  • The Adventure Company, a Canadian video game publisher of adventure games

Adventure can also refer to:

  • Adventure novel, a literary genre
  • HMS Adventure, several ships of the Royal Navy
  • Adventure (album), a 1978 album by the punk rock band Television

  • Adventure Unlimited, a Christian Science organization
  • Mitsubishi Adventure, a compact MPV produced and sold in the Philippines
