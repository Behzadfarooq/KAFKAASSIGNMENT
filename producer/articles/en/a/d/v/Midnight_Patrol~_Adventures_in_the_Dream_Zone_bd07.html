Midnight Patrol: Adventures in the Dream Zone

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Midnight Patrol: Adventures in the Dream Zone, is an animated series produced by Hanna-Barbera is conjunction with Sleepy Kids PLC., known as Potsworth & Co. in the UK (which is reflected in the theme song's lyrics in the UK broadcast). Potsworth and his 4 friends, Carter his owner, Keiko, Rosie, and Nick are appointed by the Grand Dozer to protect the Dream Zone, and are given their missions by the Snooze Patrol. Whilst they are in the Dream Zone, they have special powers. Keiko, an Asian American girl, has a flying skateboard; Carter, an African American, can draw anything and then have it come to life; Nick is able to fly (in addition his stuffed toy Murphy comes to life) whilst Rosie his cynical sister tags along.

Premiering on The Funtastic World of Hanna-Barbera in 1990, Midnight Patrol: Adventures in the Dream Zone lasted only 13 episodes.

The show had more of a fanbase in the UK than the US, and for a time, a comic strip based on the show featured in the Dandy.

[edit] Midnight Patrol member profiles

Potsworth (Age 10, 60 in human years): The sarcastic dog who usually has a stern voice when he enters the Dream Zone.

Nick (Age 6): The little boy who is the flying superhero when he's in the Dream Zone (thus, he is also known as "Super Nick"). He is assisted by his stuffed toy dinosaur Murphy.

Rosie (Age 8): Nick's big sister. An annoying brat who gives the group a very hard time, despite her usefulness. Whenever she yells at him, she calls Nick by his full name, Nicholas.

Keiko (Age 9): The Asian-American girl who rides a flying skateboard. She is energetic, very optomistic, and sounds a bit more like the leader of the group.

Carter (Age 10): The African-American who is an expert artist. In the Dream Zone his magic paintbrush can bring many things to life. He is Potsworth's owner.
