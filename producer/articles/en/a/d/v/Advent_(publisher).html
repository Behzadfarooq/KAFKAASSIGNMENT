Advent (publisher)

Advent: Publishers is a publishing house founded by Earl Kemp and other members of the University of Chicago Science Fiction Club in 1956, to publish criticism, history, and bibliography of the science fiction field, beginning with James Blish's The Issue at Hand. Other authors in the field who have either written or edited Advent books, or been the subject of an Advent book, include:

  • Cy Chauvin
  • Reginald Bretnor
  • Theodore Cogswell
  • Robert Heinlein
  • Cyril Kornbluth
  • Alfred Bester
  • Robert Bloch
  • L. Sprague de Camp
  • Howard DeVore
  • E. E. Smith
  • Ron Ellik
  • Lloyd Arthur Eshbach
  • Damon Knight
  • Alexei Panshin
  • Donald H. Tuck
