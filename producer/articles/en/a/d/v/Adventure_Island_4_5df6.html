Adventure Island 4 (Takahashi Meijin no Bouken Jima IV in Japan) is a video game designed for the Famicom console in 1994 by Hudson Soft. It was only released in Japan.

Unlike most of the entries in the Adventure Island series, Adventure Island IV plays less like a straight platformer and more of an action/adventure platformer, simliar to the later Wonder Boy games.
