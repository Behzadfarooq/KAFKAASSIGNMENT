Adventures of Dean Martin and Jerry Lewis

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Adventures of Dean Martin and Jerry Lewis is the title of a comic book published by DC Comics featuring the popular team of comedians Dean Martin and Jerry Lewis. The series ran for 40 issues from 1952 through 1957, at which time the title was renamed because of the factual breakup of the team. The title was continued as The Adventures of Jerry Lewis thereafter for issues #41-124.
