Completed in 2001, Adventure of the Seas is the smallest Voyager-class cruise ships from Royal Caribbean International. Voyager-class ships are the third largest passenger ships in the world; currently, only Cunard's Queen Mary 2 and Royal Caribbean International's Freedom of the Seas are larger. She was built in Aker Finnyards in Turku, Finland. Currently, she sails in the Southern Caribbean and departs weekly from the port of San Juan, Puerto Rico.
