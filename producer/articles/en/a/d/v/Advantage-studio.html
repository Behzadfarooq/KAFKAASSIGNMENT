[edit] About Advantage Studio

The Advantage-studio, created in 1987, is a French independent production company specialised in producing creation documentaries with themes such as nature, the animal world and contemporary societies either as full producer or as executive producer. The foremost French channels (TF1, France Télévision…) are among its regular broadcasters.

[edit] Presenting the Company

Advantage-studio

The Advantage-studio company was created on 1 December 1987. At first specialised in institutional video, the company quickly moved on to producing creation documentaries with themes such as nature, the animal world and contemporary societies either as full producer or as executive producer.

Advantage-studio is an independent production company endowed with a legal personality free to define its own business policy; no radio or televising organisation has in its capital any freezing minority. And vice versa, the company is not linked to any broadcasting organisation and does not possess, either directly or indirectly, any freezing minority in any broadcasting organisation.

Advantage-studio also enjoys a total autonomy in means of production (wide screen 16:9 shooting units and editing room) and a solid ground experience which allows for a quick projection on just about any theatre of operation.

Thanks to its independence, Advantage-studio can fully benefit from national or international support such as those of the European Union Media program, the Council of Europe’s Eurimage program, French National Centre for Cinematography’s Support Account (CNC) or regional aids, in particular that of the Rhône-Alpes region.

[edit] Current Projects

Advantage-studio current projects include:
Adoption, des chaînes et des passions, a 52 minutes documentary film about international adoption;
Militsia au jour le jour, a 52 minutes documentary film about the Russian police;
Russian Ballets, a 52 minutes documentary film about the life of a Russian dance ballet troup;
