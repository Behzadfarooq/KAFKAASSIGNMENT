Adventure A: Planet of Death was a 1981 adventure game from Artic Computing for the ZX81 and ZX Spectrum computers. In this adventure, the player find themselves stranded on an alien planet. The aim is to escape from this planet by finding their captured and disabled spaceship. The player communicated with the program by typing instructions into a simple Verb-Noun parser.
