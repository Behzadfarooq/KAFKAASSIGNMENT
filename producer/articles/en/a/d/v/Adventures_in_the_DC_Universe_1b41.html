Adventures in the DC Universe

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Adventures in the DC Universe is the title of a comic book published by DC Comics.

Following on from Batman Adventures and Superman Adventures, Adventures in the DCU used the same "animated style" seen in the DC Animated Universe but instead focused on a rotating cast of characters from throughout the DCU.

It is interesting to note that the "animated" versions of many characters that would later appear in Justice League and Justice League Unlimited first appeared here. However, the appearances of those characters made this comic series non-canon.
