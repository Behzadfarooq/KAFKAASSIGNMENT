Advanced Ceramic Technology is a ceramic manufacturing and machining company. It has been in business since 1983 and is located in Orange, California. The company produces precision ceramic parts primarily for the aerospace, semiconductor, and defense industries.

Primary materials include aluminum oxide, aluminum nitride, zirconia, ferrites, silicon carbide, silicon nitride, boron carbide, boron nitride, sapphire, cordierite, and mullite.
