Beacon is a lowly resourced production group that thrives on volunteers and enthusiasm, and has done since it began in 1987. In April 2004 the club received a grant for its work from the lottery scheme Awards for All of Â£5,000 which has refurbished all of the clubs equipment and props. The club also now has its own purpose fitted studio. It continues to unite local people and train students up in a variety of media related jobs.
