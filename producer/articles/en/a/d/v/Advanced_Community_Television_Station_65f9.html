Advance Community Television Station (ACTS) is local a television station in the Republic of Trinidad and Tobago. It is broadcast on channel 25 on the UHF band and on 9 through Columbus Communications' cable TV service.

The station focuses on social and spiritual development, and it operates a 24 hour schedule. The station's Chief Executive Officer is Reverand Nelson Sammy Guilante.

ACTS25 was officially launched on Tuesday 15 August 2006, although it ran a test transmission since 2005. It features religious programmes, as well as talk shows and movies.

The station's studios are located in San Fernando in the southern part of the island of Trinidad.
