Adventures in Troll-Sitting and Fu Dog Takes a Walk are two episodes of American Dragon: Jake Long, usually shown back to back.

[edit] Synopses

[edit] Adventures in Troll-Sitting

Stan the Sewer Troll needs to hide from the sun, and asks for Jake's help in giving him shelter. This proves to be a problem, when it comes to keeping his existence from his father, and his visiting Aunt Patchouli (Wendie Malick).

[edit] Fu Dog Takes a Walk

Fu Dog wins tickets to a baseball game in a section of seats for magical creatures. While trying to get to the game, he's captured by a wannabe dog catcher(Diane Delano), who in reality is using captured stray dogs to create a cure for dog allergies, including her own. And this cure is extremley bad for dogs.
