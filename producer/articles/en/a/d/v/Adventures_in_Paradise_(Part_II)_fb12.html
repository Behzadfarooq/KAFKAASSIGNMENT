Adventures in Paradise (Part I) is episode 8 in season 2 of Frasier. It was originally aired on 15th November 1994. The first part of this saga was aired a week previously.

[edit] Synopsis

After seeing a magazine feature on eligible singles in Seattle, Roz is bold when Frasier admires one, and a promising romance results - only to result in a major shock for Frasier.
