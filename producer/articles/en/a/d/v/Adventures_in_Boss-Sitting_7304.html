[edit] Plot

Spoiler warning: Plot and/or ending details follow.

When Donna Cabonna's assistant, Tiffany is sent on a business trip, Donna hires Raven as her backup assistant until Tiffany gets back. Devon calls Raven and tells her that he's coming tonight, but on that same night, Donna comes to Raven's house devastated because her boyfriend broke up with her. Raven tries to meet Donna's every need and be with Devon at the same time. Meanwhile, Cory and Cindy decide that they need to see other people. Cory and Cindy end up going to the same movie with their dates, and while watching the movie, Cory realizes how much he misses Cindy and they get back together.

Spoilers end here.
