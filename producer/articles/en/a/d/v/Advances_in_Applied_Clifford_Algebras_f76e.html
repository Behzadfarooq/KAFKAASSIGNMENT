The journal Advances in Applied Clifford Algebras is a peer-reviewed journal that publishes original research papers and also notes, expository and survery articles, book reviews, reproduces abstracts and also reports on conferences and workshops in the area of Clifford Algebras and their applications to other branches of mathematics and physics, and in certain cognate areas.

The Journal was established in 1991; the current editor is Jaime Keller, professor at Universidad Nacional Autónoma de México.
