User:Addict 2006/Intolerance

< User:Addict 2006
This page is intended as a way for the Addict to gain your attention about the things he hates about certain behavior that has occurred on Wikipedia. It will most likely fit with one or more Wikipedia policies or guidelines. It illustrates standards or conduct that should not be accepted by the Wikipedia community.
The content is as of March 13, 2007.

The Addict is most displeased with some users' apparent lack of... thinking... about the edits they make. While he does try to be friendly and really doesn't want to argue with people about something that is not really that important, he also cares about the quality of several articles he patrols and wanders around. Most matters may be related to being vandalized or being trolled upon. It becomes annoying to the Addict if the same mistakes are always made twice... Or more. The Addict wonders if people, including him, really do assume good faith and try to look up past reverts on vandalism in the corresponding articles in the history...

[edit] ALERT! vandalism

See also: User:Addict 2006#Defeating vandals

[edit] 24.190.12.249

In November 2006, 24.190.12.249(contribs) kept posting Wow! Wow! Wubbzy! text and a false "zero heroes" show in unrelated places, like Noggin (TV channel), PBS (and related), Nick Jr., and a few others, which mainly are of cartoon networks in general. In fact, that act of vandalism is how I first heard about Wubbzy and how another Nickelodeon voice actress I heard of also did voices in that show. Anyway, he has been blocked twice and has apparently stopped on his IP address since then.

[edit] Kgb23

In February 2006, 68.60.251.223(contribs) aka User:Bbb00723(contribs) aka User:Kgb23(contribs), was caught using copyrighted Net Nanny pictures for vandalism. He tried striking back by putting once-only-warnings on innocent users, such as me. And he also claimed that righteousness is vandalism, when in fact, vandalism stays vandalism. And he claimed the site was his once. It is not known when or where he may strike again, because he has just announced that he created another account. And we all know doing that is making sockpuppets on Wikipedia if they are all used for vandalism.

[edit] PBS idents

Have you ever watched the US Public Broadcasting Service, aka PBS? Have you ever finished watching your favorite PBS Kids show or something and saw those cool but scary PBS logos at the end of the credits and stuff? Well, lately, 67.84.187.57(contribs) insisted that PBS idents, which have expired many years ago with the creation of those anew, still were majorly in effect until 2006. Which is not true by the way. As stated on the vandal's talk page, without bolding and italics:

“ Okay, so this 6th ident starts up in so-and-so time, and it plays for almost every show they play on that channel. And when PBS makes up a new ident, they stop regularly playing the previous ident for mostly every other program. Why sure it may play on rare occasion for a few programs here and there, but overall, they move on to the next ident. ”

—Addict 2006, on 67.84.187.57's talk page

Also, there have been hoaxes about an alleged non-existent partnership between Nick Jr. and PBS, through idents they both made. Not. I even believed it once. Really. Because I don't watch TV that often now.

The preceding page has been inspired by User:Yume no Kishi/TMM-hate, and the Addict does hate these actions too, even if he never saw them first-hand.
