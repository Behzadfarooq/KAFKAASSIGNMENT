Adda Djeziri (born 3 August 1988) is a Danish footballer of Algerian origin who currently plays for Rangers, on loan from BK Frem.

Djeziri will play in the Rangers reserve and under-19 teams until the end of the season with a view to winning a permanent transfer deal. He would command a transfer fee of around £125,000.[1]
