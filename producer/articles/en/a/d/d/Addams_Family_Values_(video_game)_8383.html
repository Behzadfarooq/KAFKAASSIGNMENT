Addams Family Values was a game for the Super NES. It was released on April 16, 1994 by Oceansoft. It was also released for the Sega Mega Drive in Europe.

Loosely based on the movie of the same name, the protagonist, Uncle Fester, searches for the recently kidnapped baby Pubert. On the way he receives help from The Addams Family and a host of non-canon players. This is a spoof of the Zelda game series.

The game is a hybrid Action RPG.
