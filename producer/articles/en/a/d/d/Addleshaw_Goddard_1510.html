Addleshaw Goddard

Addleshaw Goddard is a UK-based law firm (established as an LLP) with almost 600 lawyers, based in Leeds, London and Manchester. It was formed on 1 May 2003 by the merger of Addleshaw Booth & Co with Theodore Goddard.

According to The Lawyer, a UK legal newspaper, Addleshaw Goddard was ranked 15th largest law firm in the UK by turnover in 2006. It was ranked 91st in the world by the same periodical.

The firm was also placed 100th in The Times newspaper's 'Top 100 Graduate Employers' for 2006/2007. In March 2007 it was placed 83rd in the Sunday Times newspaper's '100 Best Companies to Work For'.
