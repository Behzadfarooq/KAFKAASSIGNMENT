The Addams Family is a video game which was released for Sega Game Gear in 1993. It is based on the popular movie franchise of the 1990s, which was based on the 1960s situation comedy known as The Addams Family, featuring the recurring characters from Charles Addams's panel cartoons from The New Yorker magazine.

The game play involves Gomez Addams searching for missing family members.
