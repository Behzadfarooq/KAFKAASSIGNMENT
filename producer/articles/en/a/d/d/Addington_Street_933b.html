Addington Street is a very short road in central London. It is located just off York Road and it constitutes the County Hall roundabout, along with Westminster Bridge Road.

The York Road section of the roundabout was taken out of commission, when the road became a sort of magic roundabout, with traffic flowing both ways around the remaining sections. With the demolition of the County Hall Annexe, the road was restored to its original form.

Station Approach is one of the exits from the roundabout and leads under the Eurostar tracks to Waterloo station.
