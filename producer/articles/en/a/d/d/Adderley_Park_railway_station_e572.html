Adderley Park railway station serves the Adderley Park area in the east of Birmingham, England. The station, and all trains serving it, are operated by Central Trains. It was threatened with closure in 2004, but has now been given a reprieve. The station will become the main railway station for the City of Birmingham Stadium, if that is constructed.

It lies on Bordesley Green Road, part of the B4145.
