"Addicted to You" was Utada Hikaru's fifth single, released on November 10, 1999. It sold 1,784,000 copies and hit number one on the Oricon charts, selling 1,067,510 in its first week.

The single was produced by Jimmy Jam and Terry Lewis for Flyte Tyme Productions. In addition, the cover photos of the single were taken by legendary photographer, Richard Avedon.

[edit] Track listing

  1. "Addicted to You" (Up-in-Heaven mix)*
  2. "Addicted to You" (Underwater mix)
  3. "Addicted to You" (Up-in-Heaven mix) (instrumental)*
  4. "Addicted to You" (Underwater mix) (instrumental)

The tracks with (*) were produced by Jimmy Jam and Terry Lewis.
