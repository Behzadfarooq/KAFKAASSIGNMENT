[edit] Username's origin and alternate name

I came up with this nickname (or username) because I used to call myself a "bubbob" addict (a term seemingly made up from the game Bubble Bobble, or the characters Bub and Bob).

My other nickname is Superslinger in other sites. While playing a former Lego.com Bionicle game called Huai Snowball Sling, a preview of a GBA game, the game said that Lego.com members could be able to post their high scores. I then decided to get a Lego.com account. After a while of pondering, I settled on that nickname with the idea of "slinging" from that game.
