Addicted is a song sung by the Canadian rock band Simple Plan. This song is about the pain of breakup, and how the singer is totally "addicted" to the object of his devotion. The song could also be about unrequited love. It was first released on the album No Pads, No Helmets...Just Balls in 2002.

The song has been edited in radio stations when the line, "I'm Addict", sounds exactly like "I'm a dick". This was the plan of the band, as they succeeded in being the first band to ever get the word "dick" on MuchMusic, and MTV.

[edit] Charts

"Addicted" became Simple Plan's first top 50 hit. It peaked at the #45 position on Billboard Hot 100. Later, It became a 2004 release in Australia where it peaked into the top 10.
