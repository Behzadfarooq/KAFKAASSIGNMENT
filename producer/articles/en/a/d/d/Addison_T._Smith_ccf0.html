Addison Taylor Smith (September 5, 1862 – July 5, 1956) was a United States Representative from Idaho. Smith served as a Republican in the House from 1913 to 1933.

Born near Cambridge, Guernsey County, Ohio, Smith was originally elected as one of two at-large members from Idaho, representing the entire state. After 1919 he represented the state's Second Congressional District.

Smith died from lung cancer in Washington, D.C. at the age of 94.
