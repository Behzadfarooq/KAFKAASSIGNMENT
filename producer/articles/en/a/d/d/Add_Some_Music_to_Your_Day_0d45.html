"Add Some Music to Your Day" is a song written by Brian Wilson, Joe Knott and Mike Love for the American pop band The Beach Boys. It was released on their 1970 album Sunflower. It was also released as a single, with the B-side of the single being "Susie Cincinnati". The single peaked at #64 in the U.S. and it was never released as a single in the U.K.
