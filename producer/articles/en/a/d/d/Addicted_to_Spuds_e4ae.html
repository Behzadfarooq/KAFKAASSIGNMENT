Addicted To Spuds is a song by "Weird Al" Yankovic. It is a parody of "Addicted to Love" by Robert Palmer. It is mostly about a person's obsessive love for all types of potato related foods.

[edit] Music Video

According to the The "Weird Al" Yankovic Anthology, a music video was never made to the song, although a parody of the video was later inserted in Al's UHF video: In the Addicted to Love video, Palmer sang the song in front of a group of identical mini-skirted women. In the UHF video, Al sings in front of a group of women who have glasses and moustaches similar to those of Yankovic.
