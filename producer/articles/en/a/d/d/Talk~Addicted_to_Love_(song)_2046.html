[edit] Weird Al Yankovic Parody

I made a change to reflect the 2 different ways that Weird Al parodied this song. In the video for UHF, he did a parody of the style of the video. He later did a parody of the song itself called "Addicted To Spuds" (There was no video made for this song). 72.161.165.250 22:07, 27 July 2006 (UTC)
