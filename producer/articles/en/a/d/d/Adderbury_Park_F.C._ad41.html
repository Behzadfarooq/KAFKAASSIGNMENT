Adderbury Park F.C. is a football club based in Adderbury, near Banbury, Oxfordshire, England. They were formed as a merger between Adderbury F.C. and AP Sports & Social F.C. in 1995. In the 2004-05 season, they were relegated from the Hellenic League after finishing at the bottom of the league.
