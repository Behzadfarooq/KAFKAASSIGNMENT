"Addicted to You" is Anthony Callea's second single from his new album A New Chapter. The single was released on radio on December 12, 2006.[1] "Addicted to You", the follow up to "Live for Love", is a slick departure from the previous single into a more contemporary pop-rock sound, a genre Callea explores in his new album. Sony BMG confirmed that the single will be released on February 3, 2007. The single includes two B-sides, "Try" and "Meant for Love".[2]

Anthony performed the song at the Robina Instore at the Gold Coast on November 25, 2006, the day of the release of A New Chapter and also on Sunrise on November 24.[3][4]

The music video for the song showed Callea and his band playing on the roof of the fifteen storey AWA Tower in Sydney.[5]
