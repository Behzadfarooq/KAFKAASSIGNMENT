Addicted (album)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Addicted
Studio album by Jade Villalon
Released March 1, 2006
Korean cover

Addicted is the 10th album by singer Jade Villalon of Sweetbox and was released on March 1, 2006 in Japan. The First Press edition included a bonus cardboard slipcase. In Japan the album sold over 100,000 copies and reached the Gold Status. The Korean version of the album includes a bonus DVD featuring 5 live performances.

This album was originally going to be titled Bold & Delicious, but has been rumoured to have changed to avoid confusion with Ayumi Hamasaki's Japanese version of the same song.

In Addicted, Jade's seems to have an influence she had back in the days of Classified, as this album is probably the most upbeat, in its music and also in its lyrics. This album may have the least amount of Classical influences.

The song, "Happy Tears" was originally written for the Classified album.
