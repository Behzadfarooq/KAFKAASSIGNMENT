[edit] Army Trail High School vs. Lake High School

When I was working on working on an AutoCAD project for the I&T dept, I saw and handled the original Blue-prints for the school. On those plans, the south wing of the buidling was not included in the design. It must have been added on later.

Also, the plans clearly said that the school was supposed to be named "Army Trail High School" just like the Army Trail Blvd that forms its southern border.
