Municipalities and townships of Gallia County.

Addison Township is one of the fifteen townships of Gallia County, Ohio, United States. It is the only Addison Township in Ohio.

It borders the following townships:

  • Cheshire Township - north
  • Morgan Township - northwest corner
  • Springfield Township - west
  • Green Township - southwest corner
  • Gallipolis Township - south

To the east, Addison Township borders West Virginia across the Ohio River. It is located upstream of four of Gallia County's five other Ohio River townships.
