Addison Transit Center is a bus-only station located along Quorum Drive and Addison Road in Addison, Texas (USA). This transit center will become a future rail station on the Cotton Belt as part of DART's 2030 Transit System Plan.[1]

The bus routes within the transit center serves Addison Circle Park as well as The Galleria of Dallas and Valley View Center.
