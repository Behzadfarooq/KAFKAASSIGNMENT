Ad Herbal

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

For the Berber king, see Adherbal.

Adherbal or Ad Herbal (died 230 BC) was Admiral of the Carthaginian fleet who battled for domination of the Mediterranean Sea for Carthage in the First Punic War against Rome, 264 BC-241 BC. It is known that he was in command until at least 249 BC, during the Battle of Drepana, which was a major victory for Carthage.
