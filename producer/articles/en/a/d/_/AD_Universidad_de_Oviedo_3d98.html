Asociación Deportiva Universidad de Oviedo is a football team based in Oviedo in the autonomous community of Asturias. Founded in 1962, its plays in Segunda División B - Group 2. Her stadium is Estadio Universitario de Oviedo with capacity of 4,000 seaters.

[edit] Seasons

  • 2004/2005: Tercera División 8th
  • 2005/2006: Tercera División 1st - Promoted
  • 2006/2007: Segunda División B
  • 2 seasons in Segunda División B
  • 14 seasons in Tercera División
