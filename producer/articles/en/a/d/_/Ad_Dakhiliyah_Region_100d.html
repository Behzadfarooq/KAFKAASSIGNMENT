Ad Dakhiliyah (Arabic: الداخلية) is one of the regions (mintaqah) of Oman with Nizwa town as the regional center.

Ad Dakhiliyah Region consists of eight wilayats: Nizwa, Samail, Bahla, Adam, Al Hamra, Manah, Izki and Bid Bid.
