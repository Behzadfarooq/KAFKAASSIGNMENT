Agrupación Deportiva Alcorcón is a football team based in Alcorcón in the autonomous community of Madrid. Founded in 1971, the club plays in the Segunda División B - Group 1. Alcorcón's stadium is the Municipal de Santo Domingo with a 3,000 seat capacity.
