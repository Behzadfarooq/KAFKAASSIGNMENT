Ad Fundum is a 1993 Belgian drama film directed by Erik Van Looy. It tells the story of some students living at the university of Leuven, and who participate in some initiation rites. The main characters are freshman at the UNiversity of Leuven and join a "fraternity" otherwise known as a student association. THey are having a great time, drinking and what not. until one of their friends dies in a seemingly questionable accident.

Ad Fundum (meaning to the bottom) refers to a term used among students when they empty their glass of beer in one time.
