Asociación Deportiva Ceuta is a football team based in Ceuta in the autonomous city of Ceuta. Founded in 1996, it plays in Segunda División B - Group 4. Its stadium is Estadio Alfonso Murube with a capacity of 6,500.

[edit] Seasons

  • 2004/2005: Segunda División B 3rd
  • 2005/2006: Segunda División B 10th
  • 2006/2007: Segunda División B
  • 8 seasons in Segunda División B
  • 2 seasons in Tercera División
