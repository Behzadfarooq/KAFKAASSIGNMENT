Ad astra may refer to:

  • Ad astra (phrase), a Latin phrase meaning "To the stars", and the motto of several organizations

  • Ad Astra, anthology of science fiction short stories by Croatian authors, edited by Tomislav Šakić and Aleksandar Žiljak (ISBN 953-7113-22-1)
  • Ad Astra, a painting by Akseli Gallen-Kallela
  • Ad Astra, a song by Arcturus (band), on the album La Masquerade Infernale
  • Ad Astra, a music album by the band Spiritual Beggars.
