Aduaneiros sem fronteiras is a Galician design project focused on social, political and cultural analysis. It is published in a weblog format and usually a new design is posted every 15 days.

Obra Social is more participative and open part of the website to involve the community on the project, developing initiatives like a collection of galician typography and the first edition of a photographic contest called "Galician Anecdotal Photographic Contest".
