An Adult Porn Link List (abbreviated LL) is a common type of categorized website that provides links to free and pay Internet pornography.

[edit] How It Works

When a "link"â€”a small text string is clicked, it typically leads to a website that contains subject matter related to the link description. Adult Porn Link Lists are a compilation of web pages that contain hypertext links to pornographic sites containing photos and/or movies in many categories or niches. Visiting these sites is free: their purpose is advertising. The adult material is provided by a commercial subscription-based pornographic website (a paysite), which offers additional adult subject matter that may be of interest to the surfer.

Adult Porn Link Lists are usually operated by individual owners ("affiliates") who are paid by the commercial, or sponsor sites for web traffic and for subscriptions sold. This system of website content promotion is the main advertising vehicle for Internet pornography on the web today.

An Adult Porn Link List is a large, constantly updated and categorized list of links to an assortment of free and pay adult websites. Some Adult Porn Link Lists provide simple text links while others advertise adult paysite by means of horizontal or full page banners. The Adult Porn Link List owner makes money by offering links to quality adult paysites through the use of free picture and/or movie links.

Listing a free site on an Adult Porn Link List usually requires a recripocal link to the Link List, in order to engage in fair traffic trading methodologies. Adult free site owners face the task of having to submit their sites to hundreds Adult Porn Link Lists in order to receive sufficient return traffic to make their efforts profitable.

Before the advent of Adult Porn Link Lists in the second half of the 1990s, free advertising porn sites usually relied on the major search engines for their traffic. Ranking high enough on these sites was difficult, and remains so today. This resulted in the creation of many Adult Porn Link Lists which would provide a mechanism for exposure of free adult porn sites. An example of an Adult Porn Link List is Adult Sex Sites Network, which was established in the year 2000.

[edit] Traffic Trading

Adult Porn Link Lists employ a variety of methods to trade traffic with other Link Lists and free sites; signing up for these trades is usually easy and free for the free adult site owners. Perhaps the most basic trade is to link to other Adult Porn Link List sites. Another common practice is for owners of Adult Porn Link Lists to trade recipricol links with each other, on their index, category and submit pages. This cooperation increases the traffic flow to all links lists involved, and benefits all link list owners equally.
