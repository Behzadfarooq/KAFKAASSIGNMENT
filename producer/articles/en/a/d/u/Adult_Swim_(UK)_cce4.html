Adult Swim is a block of television programming produced in the United Kingdom that airs on the cable network Bravo in the UK. It should be noted that the UK's Bravo channel is owned by Virgin Media Television, unlike other parts of the world where Adult Swim is seen as a block on Time Warner-owned Cartoon Network. Shows that are a part of the UK's Adult Swim block are Robot Chicken, Aqua Teen Hunger Force, Sealab 2021, The Brak Show, Tom Goes to the Mayor, Space Ghost Coast to Coast & The Venture Bros.[1] Non-Williams Street shows on the block include Stripperella and Kid Notorious.

[edit] On Demand

A subscription based on demand service is available in the UK via adultswim.co.uk, similar to the American adult swim fix it features many of the programmes mentioned above. It should be noted that unlike [adult swim] on Virgin Media owned Bravo the on demand service is owned by Time Warner.
