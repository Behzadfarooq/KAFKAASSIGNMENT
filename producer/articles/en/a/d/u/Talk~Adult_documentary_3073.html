[edit] Citing Password Protected Areas?

The term "adult documentary" originated in editor forums that are always password protected. It started with the difficulty DMOZ.org had with categorizing such sites. Once other web directories began to have the same issue, it became a major concern for the editors of sexually-realted information and discussion sites. The term "Adult Documentary" was a term that evolved over time from the phrases "Adult Site" and "Educational Documentary". Since these types of sites did not quite fit into either category, the newer and more accurate descriptive term became accepted within the editor forums for such sites.
