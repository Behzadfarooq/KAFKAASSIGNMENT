[edit] Andrew Simpson (aka adpsimpson)

New to Wikipedia as a contributor, but not as a reader. A brief history of me would go something like this...

[edit] History of me

  • Born in Edinburgh, Scotland in October 1981.
  • Lived in and around Edinburgh till January 2000 when I moved to live in Nazareth for 9 months.
    • Worked in the Nazareth Hospital (mentioned in the list of hospitals in Israel), in the maintenance department.
    • Learned basic spoken Arabic during my time there
  • Started University in Glasgow in October 2000, studying Aeronautical Engineering, a 5 year undergrduate masters course.
    • Spent time on placement at the VKI for 3 months in 2003 and 4 months in 2005.
    • Worked for 4 weeks in 2004 at the AAIB.
    • Graduated (finally) in July 2005.

[edit] Current life

I now live in Dodoma, the supposed capital of Tanzania, where I'm working for 9 months as an aircraft maintenance engineer with Mission Aviation Fellowship.
