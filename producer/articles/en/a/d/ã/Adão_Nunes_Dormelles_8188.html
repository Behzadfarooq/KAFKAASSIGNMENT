Adão Nunes Dormelles

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Adão Nunes Dormelles, better known as Adãozinho (born in Porto Alegre, April 2, 1923 – August 30, 1991) was a Brazilian footballer in striker role.

For the Brazilian team he played three matches. He participated at 1950 FIFA World Cup, without playing a game. He died at 68 years old.

[edit] Clubs

  • Diário Official F. C.: 1932 - 1942
  • Internacional: 1943 - 1951
  • Flamengo: 1951 - 1953
  • XV de Jaú: 1951 - 1953

[edit] Honours

  • Campeonato Gaúcho: five times (1944, 1945, 1947, 1948, 1950)
  • Campeonato Carioca: 1953.
  • FIFA World Cup: Runner-up 1950
