Adi Azar is a PhD student at the department of Computer Engineering in the University Of Southern California (USC). Adi Azar received his Master Of Science in Computer Engineering from USC, and his bachelor from Yarmouk University in the beloved homeland; Jordan. Although it is beloved home land, I really enjoy not living outside it.

I was born in Irbid - Jordan. I lived in Jordan most my life before moving to the US.

Adi Azar is multi passion person. I am in love with Computer Engineering( Specifically NoC), I am more than excited to search engine and I am skilled software engineer and I want to be software engineer till death.

Adi Azar started his professional life in Lille 1 University in France. He had a six month internship, he worked as programmer intern (Jan 2003 - July 2003). Then, he was hired by his undergraduate university for his excellent and distinguished GPA in Jordan for eight months (Feb 2004- Aug 2004) as a full time engineer. Then, he moved to USA and started career as a part time programmer in USC. In Summer 2005 he joined Interwoven Inc. in the SF Bay area (the silicon valley) and worked as full time programmer intern. After that, Adi went back to Los Angeles and Joined Cooking.com as a part time programmer (Sep 2005 - June 2006). After that, Adi received his M.Sc from USC and joined AT&T YellowPages as a full time software engineer.

In August 2006 Adi Azar started his PhD. You can visit Adi Azar blog : http://adiazar.blogspot.com or email him at adiazar@usc.edu
