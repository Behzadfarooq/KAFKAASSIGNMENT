Adisumarmo International Airport (IATA: SOC, ICAO: WARQ) is an airport located on Solo, Central Java, Indonesia.

It was the sole international level airport in Southern Central Java, until the upgrading of the Adisucipto International Airport in Yogyakarta and the Achmad Yani International Airport in Semarang.

[edit] Airlines

The following destinations are served from Solo (as of February 2007):

  • Adam Air (Jakarta)
  • Air Asia (Kuala Lumpur)
  • Garuda Indonesia (Jakarta)
  • Lion Air (Jakarta)
  • Singapore Airlines
    • Silk Air (Singapore)
  • Sriwijaya Air (Jakarta)
