Adi Raval is a Baltimore area radio personality and comedian. Currently Adi is developing a television pilot, managing local band's, and preparing for his motion picture debut. Adi is a graduate of Towson University and resides in Bel Air, MD. He is the self proclaimed "Mayor of Bel Air" and has also gone by numerous nicknames including "Captain Charisma" and "the People's Champion" (for his striking resemblance to The Rock).

[edit] TV/Radio

Adi has worked at 105.7 WHFS, Mix 106.5 WWMX, and ESPN Radio 1300 WJFK (AM). Prior to this Adi was working for 91.1 WHFC in Bel Air, MD and U-92 in Morgantown, WV. Adi has also worked for ESPN 2 TV as a production assistant covering the West Virginia Mountaineer's football games during their 2002-2003 season.

Adi is best known for his talk radio career, he was the producer for various shows on ESPN Radio 1300 from late 2004-early 2006. Since Fall 2004, Adi has been the co-host of the widely acclaimed talk radio comedy show, "The Adi and Heavy B Show [1]." The show was a major hit locally but the cast went on an indefinite hiatus in May 2005 due to equipment problems. In the summer of 2005, Adi appeared on the "Basement Biatches", an internet talk radio show, as a special guest host. Adi has also produced and/or made appearances on the Chuck Harmon Sports Show, Out to Lunch w/ Miles & Thrill, Big O & Dukes, and E-Mortgage Solution's radio show.

In 2006, Adi worked for Sony/BMG at Epic Records in New York City.

[edit] Music

Adi is the founder of the comedy folk metal band Spyrogyra. Spyrogyra recorded their debut album "Banana Milkshake" in Fall 2001. The album contained 9 tracks that featured both rap and rock, but was deemed too inappropriate for a wide distribution. With very little touring the band still managed to create a large following of fans. In 2004, Spyrogyra added Cautious C and Delicious D to the group. Spyrogyra is currently working on brand new material for a 2007 follow up album.

[edit] Film

Adi will begin filming his first motion picture in the summer of 2007. The film is entitled "Transition" and is going to be independently produced. Adi will be playing a character based off of himself.
