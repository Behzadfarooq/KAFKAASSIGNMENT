Locale New York
Dates of operation 1992 – present
Track gauge 4 ft 8½ in (1435 mm) (standard gauge)
Headquarters Thendara, New York

The Adirondack Scenic Railroad (AAR reporting marks ADCX) is a heritage railway in the Adirondacks, headquartered in Thendara, New York. The railroad is operated by the Adirondack Railway Preservation Society (AAR reporting marks ADIX).

The railroad travels from Utica, New York to Thendara, and from Lake Placid to Saranac Lake. The two sections will be linked in the future.
