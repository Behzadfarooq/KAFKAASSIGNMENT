Adichunchanagiri Institute of Technology(AIT) is one of the engineering colleges in Karnataka,India under the VTU (Visveswaraiah Technological University). It was founded in 1980. AIT college present Principal is Dr. C K Subbaraya

[edit] Location

AIT College is located in Jyothinagar extension of Chikmagalur town. Chikmagalur is a District in Karnataka, 250 Kilometers from Bangalore city.

[edit] Courses Offered

  • Civil Engineering
  • Computer Science & Engineering
  • Electrical & Electronics Engineering
  • Electronics & Communication Engineering
  • Environmental Engineering
  • Industrial & Production Engineering
  • Information Science & Engineering
  • Mechanical Engineering
