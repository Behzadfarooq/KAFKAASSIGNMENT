Her Royal Highness Princess Adityadhornkitikhun or commonly known as 'Ong Tid' (born 5 May 1984), is the second daughter of HRH Princess Chulabhorn and Royal Thai Air Force Flight Lieutenant Virayudh Didyasarin (now, Air Vice Marshal).
