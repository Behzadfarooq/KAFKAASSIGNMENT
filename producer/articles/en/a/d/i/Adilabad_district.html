Adilabad is a district in Andhra Pradesh, India. It has a population of 2,488,003 of which 26.53% is urban as of 2001. [1]

It has a famous Temple of the Goddess Saraswathi, who is the Goddess of Education. It is located in the village Basar. The Saraswathi temple is only in two places in India.
