[edit] Rise & Fall Of The Nations: In the light of the Holy Quran

Translation: Adil Mahmood MALIK Year: 2006

The decline of the Nation of Prophet Noh(Elah-e-Salam) Reason: Pride and superiority

Surha Hud 27 “In reply to him, the leaders of his nation who refused to his talks, said, in our view point you are nothing but just like a human like us, and we are seeing that a very little of us who are inferior, have followed you, and we do not see anything which makes you superior to us rather you call us liar”


Prophet Noh Elah-e-Salam was the first Prophet of Allah who was sent as a Messenger of Allah. He is also called Adam-e-Sani – The second Adam. Because the his offspring continued the human being . His Era of Messengership covers about 4000 years BC. He and his nation are mentioned about 43 times in Quran – a Holy Book of Allah. However Surah Araf, Hud, Mominoon, Sua`ra, Qamar, and Nuh describes in his details. Prophet Nuh Elah-e-Salam preached his nation for about 950 years. His nation was indulged in polytheism by Idol worshiping. He invited them to “Tuheed – oneness of Allah” and clearly announced to them that if you follow me you will be blessed. “I am not in demand of anything from you”. Only a few people who were poor and unaware followed his preaching. The majority of his nation and the leaders were in proud and taunted the politeness of the Prophet Nuh and his followers. His wife and one son named Kanàn were also deprived to follow him. However, the other three sons, Saam, Haam, and Yafas follow him. When the nation of Prophet Nuh reached up to their maximum peak of ignorance and ….. He prayed to Allah for His help. Allah ordered Prophet Nuh to construct a boat. With the will of Allah, there started rain and fountains from aside. With the will of Allah, Prophet Nuh, with his followers and a set of all the creatures boarded on that boat. It became a strong flood from the heavy rains and the fountains from the earth. In that flood his son and wife along with the non-believers crashed. The Prophet Nuh and his Followers were saved by the Will of Allah and the human being continued in generations…


To consider others inferior is a proud and those who do will not be allowed to enter the Heaven. (Sahè-Al-Muslim)
