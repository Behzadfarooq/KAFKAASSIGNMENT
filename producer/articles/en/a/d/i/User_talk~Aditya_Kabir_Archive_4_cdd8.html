Help me - 3

How do I put my transwiki user pages on my user page here? - Aditya Kabir 15:36, 15 December 2006 (UTC)

You can link to a page on another Wikimedia wiki using its interlanguage prefix; a colon, two letters, and another colon for a different language (for instance :it:Utente:ais523 for my userpage on the Italian Wikipedia), or one letter and a colon for a different project (for instance m:User:ais523 for my userpage on Meta). You can pipe the link as normal to make it display something else. (You can also use the 'in other languages' list down the side, if you link a language without the first colon.) --ais523 16:31, 15 December 2006 (UTC)
