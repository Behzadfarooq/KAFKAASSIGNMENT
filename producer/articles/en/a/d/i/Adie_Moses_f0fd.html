Adie Moses, born 4 May 1975, is an English footballer, currently playing for Lincoln City F.C., in the English Football League 2. Before joining Lincoln Moses played for Crewe Alexandra F.C., Huddersfield Town F.C. and most notably Barnsley F.C. in the Premier League. He is naturally a defender who can either play in the center or on the right. He has two England U-21 caps to his name.
