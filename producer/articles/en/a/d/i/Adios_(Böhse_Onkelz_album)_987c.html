Adios is the last studio album of the German Hard Rock band Böhse Onkelz. It was released in 2004.

[edit] Track Listing

  1. Feuer (Fire)
  2. Immer auf der Suche (Always on the lookout...)
  3. Superstar (A song against the pop-castingshows in television like "Popstars" or "Deutschland sucht den Superstar".)
  4. Sowas hat man... (What someone has)
  5. Ja, Ja (Yeah, yeah) (Refers to an idiom, which means "Kiss my ass".)
  6. Lass mich gehn (Let me go)
  7. Fang mich (Catch me)
  8. Einmal (Once)
  9. Kinder dieser Zeit (Children of this time) (Whether hip hop, metal or other fans - all should stand united.)
  10. Hass-tler (Hate-ler) (The title is a wordplay from Hass (Hate) and Hitler. "You are the Hass-tler: Paranoid and confused / You are the Hass-tler: One time pierced through brain")
  11. Onkelz vs. Jesus (Ironic review about 24 years Onkelz)
  12. Überstimuliert (Overstimulated)
  13. Prinz Valium (Prince valium)
  14. Ihr hättet es wissen müssen (You should have known)
  15. A.D.I.O.Z.
