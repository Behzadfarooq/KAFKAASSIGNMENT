Adil Ramzi (Arabic: عادل رمزي) (born July 14, 1977 in Marrakech) is a Moroccan football striker. He has played for his current team FC Utrecht since January 2006. So far, he has played 9 international matches for Morocco, and scored once. He also played in 1997 FIFA World Youth Championship.
