Adile Naşit

Adile Naşit (born June 17, 1930 in Istanbul, Turkey; died December 11, 1987) was a Turkish actress who played in Hababam sinifi (Hababam Class), Neşeli Günler (Happy Days) and many other movies. She also starred in a children's programme called Uykudan Önce (Before Sleep) as a storyteller.

She is the daughter of comedian Nasit Özcan and Armenian theater actress Amelya Hanım, and sister of theater actor Selim Nasit Ozcan.
