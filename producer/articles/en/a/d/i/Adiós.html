Adiós is Spanish for "goodbye". Literally translated, the word is the contraction of "a" (to) and "Dios" (God), making it equivelent to "Godspeed" or "Go with God". Adios may refer to:

  • Adios (racehorse), a champion harness racing sire
  • Adiós, Navarre, a municaplity in Navarra, Spain
  • Automatic Distillation of Structure (ADIOS), an algorithm that can teach itself grammar by analysing text
  • A pair of Adio skate shoes, or other apparel made by the company.
  • Adios LiveCD, a Linux distribution by Fedora Core
