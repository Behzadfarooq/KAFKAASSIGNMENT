Adisa Cizmic

Adisa Cizmic - musician, photo model, actress, architect.

Adisa is a frontman of the band AXA [1] - Detroit, USA.

[edit] BIOGRAPHY

She was born in 1976 in Sarajevo, Bosnia. At the age of 17, she was elected Miss Charming of Sarajevo Under Siege. Her first public appearances were in magazines and TV (1995-1997). By 1998, Adisa was on many European magazine Covers as a young rockstar. In 1998, she won an Award on the International music Festival "Forte98". Her first CD "Therion Confidential" was released in 1999 by RCS in Europe and Orchard [2] in USA. She has performed for a movie soundtrack in 1999 (Escaping Otis, Sundance festival '99)[3]. As one of best performers in the Country, Adisa was introduced to President Bill Clinton in 1999 [4] and they had a chat with other members of her band *AXA

In 2000, Adisa is moving to the U.S. She is signed for Traque management International, Inc. [5] and established in fashion & model world. Her face is on magazine covers (Insight, NineModels - Model of the Month...), she is doing runways and fashion shows. Adisa was in music videos in Europe and USA, but in 2005 she got her first role in a featuring movie ("Lucky Charm"). In the interviews for magazines and newspapers, Adisa likes to mention her professional dedication to architecture. With her three careers going on and overlaping, she is shooting her second movie and recording her new CD (in 2006)
