Adi Mulahalilovic (born November 02, 1967, Sarajevo, Bosnia-Herzegovina), composer and producer.

Since 1986. Adi composed and produced music for theatre, short movies, new age music and pop music for singers and bands in countries of ex-Yugoslavia and Scandinavia. (Hari Mata Hari, Jasna Gospić, Edo Mulahalilović, Davorin Popović, Tifa, Nera, Laura Voutilainen, Isto Hiltunen, Janne Hurme, Johanna Siekinen, Petri Salminen, Mika Pohjonen, Tony Montana etc.) 1994.

For the Eurovision song contest in Dublin, Adi composed a song “Ostani kraj mene” ("Stay with me”) in co-operation with his brother Edo, to represents Bosnia and Herzegovina.

Adi Mulahalilović is a member of GRAMEX and TEOSTO (Finnish composers copyright society).
