Comic book artist Adi Granov

Adi Granov is a Bosnian-born comic book artist and conceptual designer. He was named in August 2004 as one of Marvel Comics' "Young Guns", a group of artists who have the qualities that make "a future superstar penciller", according to Marvel editor-in-chief Joe Quesada.[citation needed] He recently teamed with comic book writer Warren Ellis for the post-Avengers Disassembled relaunch of Iron Man. He will also help design the Iron Man suit for Jon Favreau's 2008 Iron Man film.
