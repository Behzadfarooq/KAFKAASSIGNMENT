Adicta is a Synthpop/Synth rock Argentine Band, formed by Adrián Nievas (nicknamed Toto) and Rudie Martinez in 1999. Their first album, Shh (2000), was accepted by the public and the critics. Then, they edited an EP, called Poco a Poco. They started growing, until 2002, when they came out with Miedo, and the numer one hit, "Tu Mal". In 2005, they prepared their next great album, called Día de la Fiebre, with a new video, "Soy Animal"

[edit] Discography

  • Shh (2000)
  • Poco a Poco (Single) (2001)
  • Miedo (2003)
  • Tu Mal (EP) (2003)
  • Día de la Fiebre (2005)
  • Dosis? (EP) (TBA)
