Adiel Paananen

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Medal record
Men's cross country skiing
World Championships
Bronze 1930 Oslo 50 km

Adiel Paananen was a Finnish cross country skier in the late 1920's and early 1930's. He won a bronze medal at the 1930 FIS Nordic World Ski Championships in the 50 km.
