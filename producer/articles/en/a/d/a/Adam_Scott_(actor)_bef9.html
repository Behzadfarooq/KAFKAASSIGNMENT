Adam Scott (born April 3, 1973 in Santa Cruz, California) is an American actor.

Scott has appeared in several feature films, including Monster-in-Law (2005), The Aviator (2004) and High Crimes (2002).

On television he appeared as Griff in the television series Boy Meets World and Josh on Party of Five, as well as David's love interest Ben Cooper on Six Feet Under.
