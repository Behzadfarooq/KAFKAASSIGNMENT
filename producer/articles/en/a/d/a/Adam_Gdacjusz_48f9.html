Adam Gdacjusz

Adam Gdacjusz or Gdacius or Gdak, also called Rey of Silesia (1615–1688) was a Polish writer and a Lutheran pastor, since [1644]] was a deacon and, later, a parish priest in Kluczbork. He was writing preaching and moralizing literature in Polish and Latin, mainly in the years 1674–1687.

  • O pańskim i szlacheckim stanie albo rycerskim dyszkurs

  • Trojaki o ołtarzach, obrazach i organach dyszkurs
  • Kwestyja o zmartwychwstaniu
  • Kwestyjej o Pannie Maryjej
  • Dyszkurs o dobrych uczynkach
