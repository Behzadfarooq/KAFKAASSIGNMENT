Adam Stenavich (born March 11, 1983) is an offensive lineman for the Green Bay Packers.

[edit] College Career

Stenavich took over as starting left tackle his sophmore year and kept the spot through his senior year. He named to the All Big 10 first team in 2004 and the All Big 10 second tem in 2005

[edit] Professional Career

After completing his career at the University of Michigan, Stenavich went undrafted in the 2006 NFL draft, but was signed within hours by the Carolina Panthers. Stenavich was cut in training camp, but was signed to the Green Bay Packers practice squad durring the 2006-2007 season. The Packers designated Stenavich elegible for play in NFL Europe in 2007 and he was drafted in the first round by the Amsterdam Admirals.
