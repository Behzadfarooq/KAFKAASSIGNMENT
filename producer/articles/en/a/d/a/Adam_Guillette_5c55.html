Adam Guillette

Adam Guillette is the Deputy Dean of Faculty for the Leadership Institute , a DC-based conservative training organization, and leads a nationwide campaign to help students get American flags for their classrooms.. He is also Director of Development for America's Choice Title Company.

[edit] Past Activities

  • Member of the Mayor's Alliance for the Prevention of Alcoholism and Drug Abuse, East Brunswick, NJ / 1998-1999
  • Chairman of the Liberty Project PAC, Gainesville, FL / 2000-2002
  • Candidate for Mayor of Gainesville, Florida, finishing in 3rd place out of 6 candidates / 2001
  • Chair College Republicans, University of Florida / 2002
  • Member of Alachua County Republican Executive Committee, Gainesville, FL / 2002
  • Member of the Citizens Advisory Committee for Community Development, Gainesville, FL / 2002
  • Chairman of the Freedom Foundation, a 501(c)3 non-partisan conservative educational organization / 2002-2004
  • Published the Gator Standard newspaper / 2002-2003
  • Student Senator at the University of Florida / 2003
  • Gainesville, Florida City Commission candidate / 2003
  • Helped organize a campaign culminating in placing American Flags in every public school classroom in the state of Florida / 2002-2005
  • Has been featured in the New York Times, National Review, and the Alan Colmes Radio Show
