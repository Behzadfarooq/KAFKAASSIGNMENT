Adam Matthew Lamberg (born September 14, 1984) is an American actor. He is perhaps best known playing David "Gordo" Gordon in the Disney Channel series Lizzie McGuire from 2001 to 2004.

Lamberg was born Adam Matthew Lamberg in New York City to Suzanne and Marc Lamberg; he is Jewish. In New York, he has appeared in plays such as Nine Armenians, Asylum, The Gathering and Adam Baum and the Jew Movie. He was Kirstie Alley's son in the ABC TV movie Radiant City, a voice on Phred on your Head, and had roles in the feature films I'm Not Rappaport and Max Keeble's Big Move. Other roles include Lord Willy in the ABC TV miniseries Dead Man's Walk, and Tad Lincoln in TNT's The Day Lincoln Was Shot. He also performed in Macbeth (Young MacDuff) at New York's Public Theater with Angela Bassett and Alec Baldwin. His most current movie is When Do We Eat? (April 2006).

Lamberg is currently filming Beautiful Loser in which he is the lead. As of 2007, he is still attending the University of California, Berkeley and majoring in Geography.
