Adam Chandler III

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Adam "Little Adam" Chandler III is a character from the soap opera All My Children. He is the son of J.R. Chandler and Babe Chandler. He was kidnapped by his mother's ex-husband Paul Cramer and given to Paul's half-sister Kelly Cramer Buchanan and her husband Kevin Buchanan, who raised him (on the soap opera One Life to Live) for approximately a year as Asa "Ace" Buchanan II. He is known as the nickname "Little A", given to him by his parents.
