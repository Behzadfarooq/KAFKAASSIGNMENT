[edit] List of films shot in Budapest – a source for extension

Hi, I'm very glad that you started the article List of films shot in Budapest. I proposed this idea, too, in this August in the Hungarian Wikipedia (see "Magyarországon forgatott külföldi filmek listája" -- ?) but we didn't find an good solution for harmonizing the diverse aspects. You can, however, find some new films for this list from the above Hungarian page. I'm looking forward to hearing about your ideas. -- Adam78 19:29, 14 November 2005 (UTC)

Thanks for pointing that out, I will see the list and try to incorporate that into the entry. I thought of this entry partly because I have already did some research on the topic in 2004, and partly because simply collecting films that were shot in Hungary regardless of the type of production (that would have meant a list of all Hungarian films ever made) wouldn't have made sense and wouldn't have made a good encyclopaedia entry.

As you may have seen in the page history, I changed "non-Hungarian films" to "internationally distributed films". I think it's not as a broad extension of the scope as if we included all the Hungarian films ever shot, but it may let non-Hungarian people find Hungarian films where recognizable scenes/venues of Budapest are shown. For example, if someone visited Budapest and happened to see Lőrinc pap tér in Józsefváros, they may be glad to find that this is the square shown in Fateless. Or if they saw the Eastern Railway Station, they may be glad to recognize its building painted in Willy the Sparrow. – Anyway, it may be worth making specific sub-sections for Hungarian films under the individual main listings. What do you think? -- Adam78 23:32, 14 November 2005 (UTC)

Quite okay, I liked your additions but I really wanted this article only to have films that are or may be internationally known. There are so many Hungarian films that were shot in Budapest or have parts in it (like nearly all non-low budget fiction movies since 1990, or possibly 1970 or even more back), that would have much more sense in a list of Hungarian films. -- AdamDobay 07:46, 15 November 2005 (UTC)

You may be interested to browse this: Category:Lists of films, including List of movies based on location, into which I inserted your article. Do you think it could be renamed to the standard form (according to this collection) "List of movies set in Budapest" or it would considerably affect its content? -- Adam78 09:36, 15 November 2005 (UTC)

Will check that out. I don't think it would fit in list of movies set because one part of that list is actually set there. If you wanted to fit it in you would have to divide the entry into two. I don't know if that is necessary. AdamDobay 16:53, 15 November 2005 (UTC)

Do you think that the film An American Rhapsody belongs in this list? Based on its IMDb entry, it seems to me that it does. -- Adam78 18:04, 23 November 2005 (UTC)

It does, and it's in. I included it ten days ago. :) AdamDobay 20:05, 23 November 2005 (UTC)
