Adama Tamboura (born May 18, 1985) is a Malian football player currently playing for Helsingborgs IF. Until the summer of 2006 he was playing for Djoliba AC.

He was part of the Malian 2004 Olympic football team, who exited in the quarter finals, finishing top of group A, but losing to Italy in the next round.

On August 22, 2006, he signed a loan deal ending on November 30 with Helsingborgs IF. He has also been testing for Feyenoord. On November 23, the HIF board announced that Tamboura had signed a three-year-contract with the club.
