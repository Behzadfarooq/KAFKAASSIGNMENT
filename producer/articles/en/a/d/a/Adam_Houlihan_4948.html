Adam L. Houlihan (born 6 April 1978), an Australian rules footballer, made his debut in 1996 with the Geelong Cats, before moving to Richmond, where he made a name for himself as a forward, wearing the number 44 guernsey. At the end of the 2004 season, Houlihan was delisted by Richmond, and now appears in the media. At 26 years of age, Houlihan decided that his career was already over and he ruled out playing AFL football again. In 2005, he hosted a horse-racing tips show with Brett Phillips and Nick Evans on SEN 1116.
