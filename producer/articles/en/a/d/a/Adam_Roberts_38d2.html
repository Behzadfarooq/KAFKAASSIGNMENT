Adam Roberts

Adam Roberts (born 1965) is an academic, critic and science fiction novelist. He also writes parodies under the pseudonym of A. R. R. R. Roberts (as a play on words of J. R. R. Tolkien.) He has a degree in English and Classics from the University of Aberdeen and a PhD from Cambridge University on Robert Browning and the Classics. He teaches English literature and creative writing at Royal Holloway, University of London.
