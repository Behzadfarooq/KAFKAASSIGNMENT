[edit] Intro in a nutshell

Hi,

I'm a Hungarian Wikipedian, 27 years old. I'm interested in languages, informatics, literature, sciences, mathematics, social sciences, psychology, music and many other things.

[edit] Announcement & request

Please only ask me questions if you have enough grounds that I may be the best person around to answer them. Please don't ask me to write or expand articles about this or that topic. Recently, I have little time for Wikipedia and I prefer to spend it spontaneously. Sorry everybody!
