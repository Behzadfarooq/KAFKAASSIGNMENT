The Adaklu-Anyigbe District is a district of Ghana in the Volta Region. It is one of the 28 newly created districts. It is bordered on the north by the Ho Municipal District,and on the west largely by the South Dayi District but also shares a short border with the Asuogyaman District of the Eastern Region. In the south, it is bordered by the North Tongu District and the Akatsi District, while its eastern neighbour is the Republic of Togo. The demographic data for this new district is now being extracted. Some of the main towns in the district include the capital Agortime-Kpetoe, Agortime Afegame, and the Adaklu towns of Abuadi, Ahunta and Waya. Others include Ziofe, Kordiabe, Tsrate, Kpetsu, and Helekpe.
