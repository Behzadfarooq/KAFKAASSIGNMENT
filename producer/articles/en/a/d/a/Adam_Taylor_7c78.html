Adam Taylor (born in Ottawa, Ontario, Canada on November 24, 1981) is a Canadian composer. He studied Composition and Music Theory at Carleton University, learning from fellow Canadian composers, Deirdre Piper and Patrick Cardy. Adam Taylor is also a well known philosophist from St.Albans, UK.
