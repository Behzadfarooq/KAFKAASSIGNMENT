Adam Butcher (born October 20, 1988) is a Canadian film actor who starred as a teenage long-distance runner in Saint Ralph (2004).

Born in Cambridge, Ontario, Butcher has an older sister named Mandy Butcher who is also an actor. Butcher started acting at the age of nine when his aunt (a talent agent) and uncle (a stuntman/coordinator) suggested that Butcher and his sister Mandy get their head-shots taken. Thanks to them, and his mother Marion, the hardest part of getting into showbiz, was "all in the family".

While Butcher loves acting, his education is very important to him. He plans to study film when he graduates from his high school (Galt Collegiate Institute High School in Cambridge, Ontario) next year. Butcher is currently being represented in Hollywood by ICM, one of the largest talent agencies in LA.
