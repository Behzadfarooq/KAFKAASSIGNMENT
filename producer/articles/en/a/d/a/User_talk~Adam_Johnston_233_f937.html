[edit] Listing Gary Doer in populism

Hi there! I'm assuming you're the anonymous user who added Doer to populism because that anonymous user also edited your user page. Could you back up the assertion with a reasonable number of appropriate third-party sources, if asked to? You may be a populist who supports Gary Doer - I'd be too, if I still lived there - but we've got to watch not to add our own opinions, where they may readily be contested, to Wikipedia, unless we've got the backing of credible outside sources such as appropriate newspaper articles, columns, scholarly publications, etc. Samaritan 21:30, 25 March 2006 (UTC)
