[edit] AUTO SIGNING

  1. Go to Special:Preferences
  2. Change Nickname to ~~~~
  3. Check Raw signature
  4. Click save (alt +s)
  5. Go to the place you want autosign (where you want --~~~~ to appear)
And insert ~~~
Optional change your signature back


For it to work you must insert the template with {{subst:template}
