[edit] Alternate writings

  • 足立: "standing leg" or "standing foot"
  • 足達: "legs" or "feet"
  • 安立: "peaceful standing"
  • 安達: "multiple peace"
  • あだち (hiragana)

[edit] Real People

  • Adachi clan, a family of samurai
    • Morinaga Adachi (1135 - 1200), Japanese warrior from the Adachi clan
    • Kagemori Adachi (? - 1248), Japanese warrior from the Adachi clan and was a son of Morinaga
  • Hatazo Adachi (1884 - 1947), Japanese General
  • Jeff Adachi (born 1959), Public Defender of San Francisco
  • Katsuji Adachi, (better known as Mr. Hito) retired Japanese professional wrestler
  • Masao Adachi (born 1939), Japanese screenwriter and director
  • Mitsuru Adachi, a Japanese mangaka
  • Osamu Adachi (born 1987), Japanese actor and singer
  • Tsutomu Adachi (1947 - 2004), Japanese mangaka and the older brother of Mitsuru Adachi
  • Yuji Adachi, male Japanese popular music guitarist
  • Yumi Adachi, a Japanese actress
