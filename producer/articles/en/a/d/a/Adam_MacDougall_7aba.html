Adam MacDougall (born May 8, 1975 in Sydney, New South Wales) is an Australian rugby league player. His positions of choice are centre or wing. MacDougall will be playing again for the Newcastle Knights in the 2007 National Rugby League season after being released by the South Sydney Rabbitohs. He has already enjoyed a long and successful career with the Knights, having played with them from 1996 to 2003 before switching to the Rabbitohs. He also previously played for the Sydney City Roosters, with whom he made his debut in 1995. Adam's brother, Luke MacDougall, also plays in the NRL.

[edit] Career highlights

  • Junior Club: Harbord Diggers, Cronulla-Caringbah
  • Career Stats: 114 career games to date scoring 60 tries

[edit] Representative games

  • State Of Origin: Played 5 games in total for New South Wales
  • International: Played 5 tests for Australia including the 2000 World Cup
