Adam Proudlock (born May 9, 1981 in Wellington in Telford, Shropshire) is a footballer who currently plays for Stockport. He was sacked by Sheffield Wednesday early in the 2005/06 season for what was called a breach of club discipline, but was reinstated after an appeal by the PFA. This allowed him to join Ipswich on a short-term basis, before being released. Adam has previously played for Clyde, Nottingham Forest, Tranmere and Wolves.

[edit] Sheffield Wednesday

Adam Proudlock spent nearly 2 years at Sheffield Wednesday. In the 2004-2005 season Proudlock scored 6 goals in 14 games, then broke his leg in training. 7 months later he was cut from Sheffield Wednesday, and found himself without a club.
