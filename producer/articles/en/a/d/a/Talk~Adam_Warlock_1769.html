[edit] Powers Section Out-of-Date

The section describing Warlock's powers looks to be gleaned from the 1980's Marvel Universe "Book of the Dead". That data's over 20 years old, and in Warlock's case is definitely outdated considering the number of times he's coccooned-up since then and emerged stronger than before.

During the Infinity Gauntlet series, it was stated that he, Gamora, and Pip all received augmented levels of strength. This was further elaborated on in The Infinity Watch series to indicate that Pip--by far the weakest--had been augmented to Spider-Man's level of strength, while Gamora was in Iron Man's class. Warlock is at least as strong as Gamora. --SteveG 10:55, 20 March 2007 (UTC)
