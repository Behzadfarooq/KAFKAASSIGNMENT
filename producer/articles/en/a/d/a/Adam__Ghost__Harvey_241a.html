Adam "Ghost" Harvey started rapping as an underground artist in 2003. In 2006, he started producing for Baltimore artists: JAX and Scolla. He is 21 years old and resides in Baltimore. He is credited as a producer on the upcoming JAX solo album, and the upcoming Scolla solo album.

[edit] History

In the summer of 2003, Ghost, JAX, and Ace Money started an underground rap group called M.O.B. In 2007, he was signed onto Pain Is Temporary Entertainment, by JAX (CEO). Signed to Pain Is Temporary Entertainment is Scolla, Ace Money, JR, and Booghetty. Their first album to be released is the JAX solo album, being released on May 1st, 2007. There are also future releases planned for Scolla , Ace Money, and JR.
