Adam Terry (born September 1, 1982) is a National Football League offensive tackle for the Baltimore Ravens.

[edit] High School Years

Adam Terry attended Queensbury High School in Queensbury, New York. As a junior, he was a first team All-Conference pick and a first team All-State selection, and was named an All-East selection by Prep Football Report.
