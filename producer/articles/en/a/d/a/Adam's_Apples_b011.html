Adam's Apples (Danish: Adams Ã¦bler) is a 2005 Danish movie, directed by Anders Thomas Jensen.

The main roles are played by Ulrich Thomsen (Adam) and Mads Mikkelsen (Ivan).

[edit] Plot

Adam is a Neo-Nazi sent to a church for resocialization after being released from prison. While there, he is given a task that by the end of his visit there, requires him to bake an apple cake as a personal goal.
