Adam Davidson

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Adam Davidson born 1964 in Los Angeles, California is an American actor and director. Davidson has appeared in the following films, The Day Trippers, A Match Made in Heaven, Návrat ztraceného ráje (aka Return to Paradise Lost), Way Past Cool, Nature Boy and Pop Life. In addition to acting, Davidson has also directed for several modern-day television programs which include Lost, Grey's Anatomy and Six Feet Under.
