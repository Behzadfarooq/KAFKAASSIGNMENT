Adam Tuominen

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Adam Tuominen (born in 1980 in Adelaide, South Australia) is an Australian actor. He studied drama at St. Peter's Collegiate and studied directing at Adelaide's Finders Drama Centre and is a 2001 graduate of the National Institute of Dramatic Art in Sydney. He is best known as Hunter Bradley/Crimson Thunder Ranger in the Power Rangers series. He is 6' 1" high, and he has a twin brother.
