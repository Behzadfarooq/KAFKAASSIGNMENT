Adam Watt (October 10, 1967) is an Australian boxer and kickboxer. He has studied kickboxing, Seido-kaikan karate, and boxing, and is called "Aussie sniper" because of his high level boxing skills. He has won many titles and reached as high as 19th in the W.B.C. Cruiserweight.
