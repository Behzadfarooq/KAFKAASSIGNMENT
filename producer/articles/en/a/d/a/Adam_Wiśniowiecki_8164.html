Adam Wiśniowiecki (ca.1566-1622) - Polish-Lithuanian Commonwealth szlachcic and magnate. Supported False Dmitriy I during the Muscovy Time of Trouble, famous, together with Konstanty Wiśniowiecki (1564-1641), for being the 'finders' of the False Dmitriy I.

Married Aleksandra Chodkiewicz before 1601. Daughter Krystyna Wiśniowiecka born in 1602.
