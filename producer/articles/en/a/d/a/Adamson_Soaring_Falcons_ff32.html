The Adamson Soaring Falcons is the varsity team of Adamson University in the University Athletic Association of the Philippines.

[edit] Basketball

[edit] Notable players

  • Marlou Aquino
  • Kenneth Duremdes (PBA MVP)
  • Edward Joseph Feihl
  • Hector Calma
  • Eddie Laure (MBA MVP)
  • Gherome Ejercito
  • Karl Kenneth Bono (UAAP MVP)
