Adam Sieff.

Adam Sieff is a record producer, guitar player, studio owner and more recently Director of Jazz for Sony Music.

Sieff has worked with many bands and artists including Wasted Youth, James Brown, The Bad Plus, Herbie Hancock, Alexis Korner, Hugh Masekela, Big Country, Wynton Marsalis, Spitting Image, Clare Teal and is currently working with British band Second Person as A&R representative for their The Elements album. He is also currently working for the online company Sellaband helping to launch bands by allowing fans to invest in their future recordings.
