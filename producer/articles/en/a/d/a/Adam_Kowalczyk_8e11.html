Adam Kowalczyk

Adam Kowalczyk is a touring rhythm guitarist with the band Live. His brother is Ed Kowalczyk, the lead singer and guitarist of Live. He has led two of his own bands, Portion and Adam and the Weight, but both have broken up. Kowalczyk still plays some shows of his own at smaller venues across the country. He recently released an LP of his own material ironically titled The Dream EP.
