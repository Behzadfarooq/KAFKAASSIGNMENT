Adam Mesh

Adam Mesh was the runner-up on the NBC reality television show Average Joe. He then was the star of his own show, Average Joe: Adam Returns, where he got to go on dates and then send women home. In the end, he chose Samantha Trenk. Part way through the show, a bus arrived with some attractive women in bikinis. He asked the producers to send them home, which they promptly agreed to do.

Mesh married his girlfriend Jessica Malca on May 28, 2006, in Miami, Florida. The couple had been dating since 2004 after Mesh broke up with Trenk.
