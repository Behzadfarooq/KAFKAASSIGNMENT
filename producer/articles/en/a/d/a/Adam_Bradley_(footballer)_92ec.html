Adam Bradley (born on 25 August 1988 in Carlisle) is a young goalkeeper who plays for Carlisle United and has been a regular in the reserve and youth team for "The Cumbrians". In the 2005-2006 season he has been on the bench for the 1st team but yet to make his debut. His contract runs out in Summer 2007.
