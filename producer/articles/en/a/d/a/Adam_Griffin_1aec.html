Adam Griffin was born on the 26 August 1984 in Salford, Greater Manchester, and is a professional footballer who currently plays as a midfielder for Stockport County.

Adam started his career with Oldham Athletic making his debut on the 20 April 2002 against Queens Park Rangers. While at Oldham Athletic Griffin and team mate David Beharall were victims of a carjacking.[1]

He had loan spells with Chester City and Oxford United before signing for Stockport County in 2006.
