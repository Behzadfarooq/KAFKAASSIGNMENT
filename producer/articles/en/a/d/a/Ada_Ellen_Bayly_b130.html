Ada Ellen Bayly

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Ada Ellen Bayly (March 25, 1857 - February 8, 1903), was a novelist.

Bayly wrote several popular stories under the name of "Edna Lyall". They include Autobiography of a Slander, Donovan, Hope the Hermit, In the Golden Days, To Right the Wrong, We Two, and Won by Waiting.
