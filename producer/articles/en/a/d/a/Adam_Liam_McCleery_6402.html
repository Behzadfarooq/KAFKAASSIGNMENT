Adam Liam McCleery (born 4 September 1981) is an Irish American fashion model.

Notable Haute Couture print model best known for his controversial femme-boy image in front of the fashion lens. Famed print pet of such colossal fashion kings & queens: Calvin Klein, Hedi Slimane, Karl Lagerfeld, Miuccia Prada, Valentino Garavani, Donatella Versace. In addition to being a world class fine artist, NEXT model's sensation has caught the eye of many of the industry's best. Last seen beside Boy George in The M·A·C AIDS Fund, New York, February 11, 2004 MAC (cosmetics) and the 2004 Dior Homme campaign.

Adam Liam McCleery L'Uomo Vogue, 2004

American born, Adam Liam (real name: Adam William McCleery) grew up in Glastonbury, Connecticut. Having spent the better part of a decade based between County Claire & Dublin, Ireland, he has quoted himself partial to Irish descent. In 1999, McCleery was approached by Sheila Anderson, a booker from Abercrombie and Fitch while visiting Quincy Market in Boston with an offer to contract test shoot a ready-to-wear kids campaign. Signed, booked and printed; Within the following year, DNA Models, NYC approached McCleery with a sign-on offer. A slow start led to Adam attending SCAD in Savannah, Georgia to study film and acting. Adam did not achieve a name until 2002-2003 where a successful audition with NEXT models executives granted him an exclusive contract with the AEFFE fashion group throughout Europe. (Jean Paul Gaultier, Moschino, Narciso Rodriguez.

McCleery has been personally linked to musicians Lenny Kravitz and Madonna for contracted art. He's had alleged romantic links with actress Natalie Portman & Bijou Phillips but this is still unverified.
