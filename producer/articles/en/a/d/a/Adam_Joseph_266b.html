Adam Joseph

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Adam Joseph came to WPVI in April 2005. Adam is currently the weekend evening meteorologist. He completed his Bachelor of Science in Meteorology from Lyndon State College in 2000, and is in fact the only WPVI weathercaster who has a full meteorological degree. He also holds the American Meteorological Society seal of approval. In addition to his forecasting duties he contributes "Science Notebook" reports on Action News.
