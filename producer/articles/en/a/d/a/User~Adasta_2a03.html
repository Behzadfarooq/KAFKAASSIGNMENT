About Me

Adasta - I am currently working for a prominent English building society after travelling for roughly 2 months; I visited Japan, Australia, Fiji, and the U.S. over the course of May , June, and the early days of July. I am planning another excursion to Canada, more specifically Quebec City and Montreal.

I shall begin studying at the University of Sussex this September, reading English as an undergraduate. Most of my time is spent attempting to read all the books in my personal book collection, as I tend to buy books faster than I can read them. Aside from this, I can also be found wasting my life on the internet, sweating profusely at the gym, trying to woo fair maidens, or trying to recover from putting my foot in my mouth. I'm not a major Wikipedia player, and I'm happy with that; however, you can see what projects "take my fancy" by looking at the "Projects" column to the right.
