AdaCore is a computer software company that develops an Ada compiler, tools and libraries.

All AdaCore software products are licensed either under the GPL or the GMGPL license. Special releases are made for academic or open source use.

The company was founded in 1994, and was originally named Ada Core Technologies (ACT). In 2004 it was renamed to AdaCore.
