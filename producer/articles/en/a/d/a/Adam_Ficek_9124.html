Adam Ficek

Adam Ficek is the drummer for the London rock band Babyshambles. He joined the band in January 2005, following the departure of Gemma Clarke. Prior to joining Babyshambles, Ficek used to teach drum lessons in schools and played drums for The White Sport.

Adam also drums and sings with the Mains Ignition collective and has played vibes, piano and drums on two crossover albums with various musicians on the London jazz scene.
