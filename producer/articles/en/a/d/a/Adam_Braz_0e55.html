Adam Braz (born June 7, 1981 in Montreal, Quebec, Canada) is a Canadian soccer player. He currently plays as a defender for Toronto FC and Canada.

Braz played for Fairfield University in Fairfield, CT from 1999 to 2001. In 2000, Braz was an All-MAAC First Team defender and helped the Stags earn a #15 national ranking.

In 2002 Braz signed for the Montreal Impact and played an important role in the Impact defence helping the team make the playoffs. In 2003 Braz signed for Västerås SK of Sweden, where he failed to make an impact like he did with Montreal. Braz returned to Montreal in 2004 and helped the team win the USL First Division Championship. A year later Braz continued to be strong in the Montreal defence.

In July 2005 Braz hard work paid off he was invited to play for his country Canada, in the Gold Cup in 2005 where he played all three games. After the Gold Cup Braz become a main stay in the Canadian defence so far he has earned 7 caps for Canada.
