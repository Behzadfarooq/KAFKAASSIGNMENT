Adam Nawałka (born 23 October 1957 in Kraków) is a retired Polish football player.

He played most of his career for Wisła Kraków.

He played for the Polish national team (32 matches) and was a participant at the 1978 FIFA World Cup.

Nawałka later jumped on a coaching career and now coaches Wisła Kraków.
