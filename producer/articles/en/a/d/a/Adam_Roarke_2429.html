Adam Roarke (August 8, 1937-April 27, 1996) was an American actor and film director.

Roarke was born Richard Jordan Gerler in Brooklyn, New York, where he was a street gang member during his youth.[1] His father was a vaudeville comedian and his mother was a chorine.[2]

Roarke died in Euless, Texas of an apparent heart attack.
