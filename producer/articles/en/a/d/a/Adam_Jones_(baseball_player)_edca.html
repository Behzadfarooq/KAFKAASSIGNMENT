Adam La Marque Jones (born August 1, 1985 in San Diego, California) is a center fielder for the Seattle Mariners. Originally drafted with the 37th pick of the 2003 Major League Baseball Draft, Jones has shot through the Mariners minor league system in a relatively short amount of time. On July 12, 2006 he was called up to the Mariners replace the injured Jeremy Reed.

On August 9, 2006, Jones hit his first major league home run against the Texas Rangers.
