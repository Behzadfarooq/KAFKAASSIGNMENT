Adam Childe was a fictional character in the popular BBC soap opera EastEnders. He was played by Matthew Ashforde.

[edit] History

Childe is a duty solicitor at Walford Police Station, who was assigned to represent Sonia Fowler when she was arrested on suspicion of the murder of her ex-mother-in-law,Pauline, although he first thought she was Sonia Bailey, arrested for shop- lifting. He was a nervous solicitor, and made comments about wanting to murder his mother, ironic to the situation. Sonia was relieved when Phil Mitchell paid for Ritchie Scott to represent her in stead of Adam.
