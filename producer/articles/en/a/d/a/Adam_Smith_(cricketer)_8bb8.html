Adam Smith was born on April 6th 1976 in Greensbrough Australia.

Adam Smith has captained Australia's at under 19 level at Test and One Day on three occassions each.

Statistics

In his 3 first class games in which he has played he has scored 89 runs at an average of 14.83, with his highest score being 36. he has also taken 3 first class wickets.

In his three List A games his played he has scored 71 runs at an average of 35.50. His highest score is 41*.

All of the first class and List A games that his has played was for Victoria.
