"A grassroots organization founded in 2002 in honor of the great Adam Solomon and his revolutionary Procedure to solidify the Vast Right Wing Conspiracy.

Mr. Solomon, plagued by legions of hot young communists who found themselves irrestibly drawn to him, began to look for this trend in others like himself. He soon realized that, in their infatuation, these communists were more than willing to bend their ideology to meet his acceptance.

Acknowledging the unparalleled sexiness of young conservatives, Solomon realized that this attractiveness could be used for the greater good, and so developed his three-step Adam Solomon Procedure (ASP)- Reform, Bosh, Repeat (or as many prefer, the somewhat-altered Bosh, Reform, Repeat).

If you (man or woman), like the legendary Mr. Solomon, have had similar experiences in which liberals are inexplicably drawn to your GOP boff-ability, feel free to join the Adam Solomon Committee (colloquially known as the 'Chicks Dig Conservatives' or 'Guys Dig GOPs' movement), and help reform the world one Demmie at a time!"
