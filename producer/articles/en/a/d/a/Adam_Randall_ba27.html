Adam Randall (born Adam Lewis, 1979) is an actor from the Rhondda Valley, South Wales. He trained at East 15 Acting School and has appeared in several productions, including Sgripts Cymru's controversial play Franco's Bastard and played Gethin in the BBC series First Degree.

[edit] Trivia

  • He played the cyclops in the video of A Quiet Man by Jim Noir.
  • He is also a composer, arranger and music producer under the names Adam Lewis and Mr Ronz.
