Recently Adam Baer wrote the Op-ed for the Los Angeles Times called "Fighting to Say No To Chemo," a reaction to the Abraham Cherrix alternative medicine vs. chemotherapy case in Chincoteague, VA. This article contributed to a national debate on whether or not a county has the right to force a family to treat their children with one particular form of medication. It also questioned the definition of "alternative" medicine.


I have been following Adam Baer's work, from his musical criticism to his recent writings on patient advocay. I found his reviews both insightful and refreshing and this recent Op-ed a much needed spotlight on the limits of choice of medicine--a truly frightening issue. MPT Chicago, IL 128.125.12.140
