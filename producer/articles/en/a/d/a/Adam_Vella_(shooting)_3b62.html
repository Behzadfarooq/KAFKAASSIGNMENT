Adam Joseph Vella (born June 12, 1971) is a professional target shooter from Australia. Vella was born in Melbourne and is a Commonwealth Games gold medalist and an Olympic Bronze medalist. He works as a plumber when not competing.

Adam is of Maltese descent.
