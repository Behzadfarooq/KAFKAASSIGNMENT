Adam C. Beamish is a graphic designer and was a founding member of Remedy Music's the Midway State.

Adam was born in Oakville, Ontario, Canada on February 12, 1985 where he lived until the age of 16. Following the death of his father in 2001, Adam moved to the small town of Collingwood, where he attended Jean Vanier Catholic Secondary School. It was here that Adam met Daenen Bramberger, who attended the local public high school. Together, they formed Nexus, a melodic hardcore band with vocalist Samantha Thomas. While performing at a local concert, Adam was introduced to schoolmate Nathan Ferraro. The two performed together in the winter of 2002, and shortly therafter, the Midway State was formed.

In the Summer of 2003, Adam left Nexus to pursue the Midway State more seriously. Following high school end extensive touring, the Midway State relocated to Toronto, Ontario in January of 2005. In September 2005, Adam Beamish and guitarist Ryan Horning were asked to leave the band. Adam re-enrolled at the University of Western Ontario where he currently studies mechanical engineering and philosophy.

Adam is an accomplished, self-taught, musician who plays guitar, bass, and drums. He has shared the stage with many prominent performers including Grade, Alexisonfire, Silverstein, and Dallas Green. He has performed at various Canadian music festivals including the SCENE Music Festival, Canadian Music Week, and the Hillside Festival

Adam designs under the pseudonym COИVOKЄ![1] He has designed websites for Black Box Recordings, Ten Second Epic, and the Midway State.
