Adam Schefter is an American sports writer and television analyst. He is currently employed by the NFL Network.

[edit] Career

A graduate of the University of Michigan and Northwestern University's Medill School of Journalism, Schefter covered the Denver Broncos for the Denver Post for 15 years. He began a relationship with many major Bronco athletes and coaches, and has subsequently helped write their biographies. These include Bill Romanowski, Mike Shanahan and Terrell Davis. While with the Post, Schefter won the Colorado Sportswriter of the year award in 2002 and 2003.

Schefter joined the NFL Network in 2004 and appears on NFL Total Access (hosted by Rich Eisen and also writes for NFL.COM).

In addition, Schefter is a guest on numerous radio programs as an NFL insider, including 850 KOA in Denver, Colorado.
