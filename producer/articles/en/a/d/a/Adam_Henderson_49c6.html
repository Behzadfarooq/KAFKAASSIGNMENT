Adam Henderson is a Canadian voice actor who works for Ocean Studios in Vancouver, British Columbia, Canada. He has played several roles in anime, most notably Ledonir Kisaka in Gundam Seed and Gundam Seed Destiny.

[edit] Anime Roles

  • A Wind Named Amnesia (movie) as Wataru
  • AD Police Files (OAV) as Leon
  • Dragon Drive (TV) as Agent S
  • Galaxy Angel Z (TV) as TV Cameraman
  • Inu Yasha (TV) as Gatenmaru
  • Mobile Suit Gundam Seed (TV) as Ledonir Kisaka
  • Mobile Suit Gundam Seed Destiny (TV) as Ledonir Kisaka
  • New Dominion Tank Police (OAV) as Al
  • Roujin Z (OAV) as Maeda
  • X (movie) as Fuuma Monou
