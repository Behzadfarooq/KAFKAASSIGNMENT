Adam Carson is the original and current drummer of the punk/rock band AFI. He is one of the two original members left in the lineup, the other being singer Davey Havok. Carson also filled in as drummer for the band Tiger Army. His drumming can be found on Tiger Army's "The Early Years EP" as well as their debut album "Tiger Army". Adam was also a member of Influence 13 - a band formed by Nick 13, Jade Puget (who joined AFI in 1998), Geoff Kresge (who left AFI in 1996), and two other friends.

[edit] Trivia

  • Carson's tattoo include a Blue Rose and Spider Web on left arm, and pirate ship.
  • Carson is a vegetarian.
  • Carson is left handed.
  • Within the AFI fan community, Carson is often associated with Pirates, though he dislikes this association.
  • Carson used to smoke, but does not anymore.
  • Carson featured in the video for the song Truth has Failed by Love Equals Death. This video can be seen on their Myspace profile.
