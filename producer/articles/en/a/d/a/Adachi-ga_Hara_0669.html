Adachi-ga Hara is the fifth anime episode in the Lion Books series. This episode is the only one in the series released to theater first, making it NOT an OVA[1].

[edit] Story

The story is a sci-fi fantasy about an old women who meets the young space pilot Jes, exiled for trying to overthrow Earth's pro-indenpendence presient Phippo and pining of his girlfriend Anny. The story is based on the classical Japanese noh theatre drama Ôshû Adachi-ga-Hara, (The Ballad of Sodehagi).

[edit] DVD

It was re-released as a DVD in March 21, 2003 on the same disk as Akuemon.
