Adam Griffiths (born 21st August 1979 in Sydney) is an Australian professional football (soccer) player currently playing in England for Brentford. Griffiths generally plays as a central defender, but his versatility means that he can also play at right or left full back, or in central midfield.

Prior to joining Brentford on 11th July 2006, Griffiths played for, among others, Northern Spirit, Oostende and Watford.

As of March 26, 2007 speculation is mounting that Griffiths is soon to return to his former club Newcastle Jets. There he would play alongside his brother Joel.
