Adam Brooks (b. July 3, 1979) is a Canadian painter, filmmaker, and musician.

[edit] Biography

Adam lives and works in Winnipeg, Canada. He drew comic books until he discovered oil paint and the work of Philip Guston, Francis Bacon, and Vincent Van Gogh. In 2002 he graduated from the University of Manitoba with a Bachelor of Fine Arts Honours degree in Painting. He has since produced five short movies, one CD, and a body of work on canvas and paper.
