Adama Coulibaly

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Adama Coulibaly (born September 10, 1980 in Bamako) is a Malian football (soccer) defender. Beginning his career in local club Djoliba, Coulibaly has played for his current team RC Lens since 1999. He has played international matches for Mali.

[edit] Playing career

  • 1998-99 : Djoliba AC
  • 1999-00 : Lens 5/0
  • 2001-02 : Lens 22/2
  • 2002-03 : Lens 25/0
  • 2003-04 : Lens 22/1
  • 2004-05 : Lens 18/0
  • 2005-06 : Lens

[edit] Honors and awards

  • FIFA World Youth Championship 3rd place: 1999
