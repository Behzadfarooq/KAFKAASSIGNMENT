[edit] Vital statistics taken from actor Adam West

The data (height, DoB, etc.) on Adam West (Family Guy) appear to be taken from Adam West, which would be assuming too much — this has been brough up (by me) on the Talk page. While I think the deletion is premature, we probably shouldn't keep it unless there's verification that Adam West (Family Guy) == Adam West, at least as far as vital stats go. This could be either a mention on the show of the characters birthdate (lot to ask for), or one of the show's producers saying the rules for the character is that the vital stats are taken from the real-life actor.

Possibilities:

  1. Mayor West's DoB and/or height have been mentioned on the show.
  2. Family Guy producers say they intend the Adam West character to resemble actor Adam West in every way.
  3. Family Guy producers say the character doesn't mirror the real-life West.
  4. It is unknown — no one who would know the rules for this character has revealed them.

If ...

  1. keep the infobox stats and add cite episode.
  2. keep the infobox stats and add citations.
  3. delete the statistics from the infobox, add this info to article with citation.
  4. delete the statistics from the infobox and mention this in the article.

It would be helpful if someone who has been through all the DVD commentary and more than a couple interviews can comment on this. I am certainly not an expert on this subject. / edgarde 06:48, 14 January 2007 (UTC)

Well, having typed out all that, it seems that mayoradamwest.com, FOX-owned, gives "September 19, 1928 (the birthday of the real Adam West)" as Mayor West's birthdate. This may not be canonical, but it's a good reason to keep "real Adam West" stats in this article for now. / edgarde 07:10, 14 January 2007 (UTC)
