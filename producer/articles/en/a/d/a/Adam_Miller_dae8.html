Adam Miller

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Adam W. Miller (born November 26, 1984 in Plano, Texas) is an American baseball player in the Cleveland Indians organization. Since being drafted by the organization in 2003, Miller has played Minor League Baseball as a member of the Burlington Indians, Lake County Captains, Mahoning Valley Scrappers, Kinston Indians, Akron Aeros, and Buffalo Bisons. A pitcher and a 2007 non-roster invitee to spring training, Miller throws and bats right-handed. He is expected to make his Major League Baseball debut in 2007.
