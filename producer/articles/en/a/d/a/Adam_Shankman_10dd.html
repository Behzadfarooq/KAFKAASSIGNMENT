Adam Shankman born November 27, 1964 is an American film director, dancer, actor, and choreographer. He directed A Walk to Remember, Bringing Down the House, The Pacifier, and Cheaper by the Dozen 2. He also danced in videos for Paula Abdul and Janet Jackson. He officiated the wedding of Freddie Prinze, Jr. and Sarah Michelle Gellar, who he worked with while choreographing Buffy the Vampire Slayer.[1]

His next project is the 2007 version of Hairspray.
