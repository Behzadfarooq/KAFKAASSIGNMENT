Adam Storke

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Adam Storke (b. August 18, 1962 in New York City, New York, USA) is an American actor who has starred in television and film. He is best known for playing Julia Roberts' love interest in the 1988 hit film Mystic Pizza and as Larry Underwood in the 1994 hit Stephen King mini series The Stand.

His well-known television role is in the soap opera Search for Tomorrow as Andrew Ryder in 1985 and in the short lived TV series in 1998 Prey. Adam has appeared in some TV movies and has made some guest appearances on TV shows; some of those shows range from Miami Vice, L.A. Law, American Dreams, Law & Order: Criminal Intent, and 2005's Over There.

Adam Storke is the son of stage actress Angela Thornton.
