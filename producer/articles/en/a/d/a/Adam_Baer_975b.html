Adam Baer is an American writer.

[edit] Magazine journalism

He is a staff correspondent for Travel + Leisure Magazine.

He began his writing career at NPR and then wrote arts criticism for The New Republic, Washington Post, Slate, New York Sun, and New York Times Book Review, among other publications. He also wrote articles for The New Yorker, Men's Health, Maxim, and GQ. He contributes frequently to the Los Angeles Times. His articles have been published in Wired, the New York Times, the Atlantic, and Men's Journal.

[edit] Books

His stories have appeared in books such as "Before and After: Stories from New York" (W.W. Norton), a book of essays by New Yorkers about 9/11, and "A Leaky Tent is a Piece of Paradise," (UC Press, Sierra Club Books).

[edit] Weblog

Baer maintains a weblog at www.glasshallot.com.
