Adam Christopher Nowland (born 6 July 1981 in Preston, Lancashire) is a professional footballer who currently plays as a midfielder for Preston North End.

Adam started his career at Blackpool where he made his professional debut at the age of 17.

Despite making 80 appearances for Blackpool he was released by manager Steve McMahon in 2001.

He signed for Milton Keynes Dons but only made 34 league starts in 3 years, he then played for West Ham United and Nottingham Forest and Gillingham F.C. on loan before joining Preston North End on a free transfer in 2005.

He made a good start for Preston North End, scoring 3 goals in 9 starts, but then suffered a broken leg playing against Norwich City after a bad challenge by Dickson Etuhu- a player still on loan from Preston at the time of the incident.

He is expected to return to the first team fold around October
