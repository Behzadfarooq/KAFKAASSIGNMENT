Adam Chandler is a fictional character from the soap opera All My Children, played by David Canary from fall 1984 to present. Adam is one of the richest men in the community of Pine Valley, Pennsylvania. He can be considered one of the most devious individuals on the show. His business dealings can and have gotten him into a lot of trouble. However, he has a close relationship with his family. He is especially close to his twin brother, Stuart; however, Stuart's tendency to act the part of Adam's conscience can be wearing. Adam is perhaps one of Pine Valley's most frequently married characters, as shown below. He resides at Chandler Mansion (300 River Road) and since September 21, 2005, has been married to Krystal Carey. He has had a long-standing feud with Palmer Cortlandt; although that front has somewhat quieted down, since Palmer isn't seen very often.
