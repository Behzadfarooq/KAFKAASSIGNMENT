Adam Polakoff was the Saxophone player in the ska-punk band Reel Big Fish for the album Everything Sucks (Reel Big Fish album). He is a graduate of the University of California at Santa Cruz and is to graduate with a J.D. from the University of California at Hastings in Spring 2007.
