Adam LaClave

Adam LaClave is the lead singer and primary songwriter of the bands MACROSICK and Club Of The Sons. Prior to starting up MACROSICK and Club Of The Sons, LaClave was co-founder and lead singer of the alternative band Earthsuit. Adam's baritone voice is often identified by its similarities to those of David Bowie and David Byrne of Talking Heads. Adam also won a Dove Award in 2005 as co-writer of Mute Math's "Control" which won for "Modern Rock Recorded Song of the Year".[1] LaClave is also the founder of independent label Spacefarm Records.
