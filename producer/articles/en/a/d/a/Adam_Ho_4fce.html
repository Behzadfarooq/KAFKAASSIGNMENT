Adam Ho (born February 6, 1989) is an American singer/guitarist. He appeared on America's Most Talented Kids in 2004 and has gone on to other shows such as Paris by Night. He was also featured on the Disney Channel's Check This Kid Out.

Ho, a Vietnamese American, was born in Dallas, Texas. He lives in Orange County, California.
