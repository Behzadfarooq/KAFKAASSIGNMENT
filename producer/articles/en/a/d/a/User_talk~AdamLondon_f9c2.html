[edit] Great minds think alike?

Hello - only last week did I think "I know, I'll create an infobox for magazines", but didn't have time to do it immediately. I see you have, though - looks pretty good, too. In my scribblings before I got otherwise busy, I had a few other pieces of info in my theoretical box to yours. These were: Publisher (which I guess is your Owners, but I think Publisher is probably a better name), First issue (date), Last issue (date, #), Country. Would it be OK for me to add/change these? What do you think? Stephenb 16:39, 2 August 2005 (UTC) (Talk)

  • I agree, publisher is better suited the other changes sound good too, how about adding the cover price too?
    • Thanks! It's an idea, but cover price isn't particularly notable, and could look like advertising (and changes too frequently for an encyclopedia to recognise) - probably best left to the web site of the magazine in question. I've half-changed my mind on publisher, as strictly speaking that is a person, so I've currently got Company on my prototype (see my user page) - your thoughts welcome! NB: I've also dropped "Location" as this relates to the company rather than the magazine, really, so simply "Country" is more relevant. I'll give it a day or two while I think about it, then I'll change the infobox and start spreading it more widely. So, if you've any strong opinions, let me know before I start making changes! Stephenb 20:41, 2 August 2005 (UTC)
