[edit] Summer's brother

I removed the section that talked about Adam X being considered a brother to Cyclops and Havok because it lacked a verb to explain what exactly was revealed. If you can add the relevant details, feel free to reinsert it. CovenantD 14:49, 21 April 2006 (UTC)

Why could Vulcan not be the brother Sinister was referring to? Dr Archeville 02:30, 13 May 2006 (UTC)

Sinister was talking about not wanting the Summers brothers to get the Legacy Virus. Vulcan was "dead" at the time, if Sinister even knew about him. - SoM 16:49, 13 May 2006 (UTC)
Did you actually mean to say that Phillip Summers was Katherine's father-in-law? If not, then it seems that there is no point in making note of the fact that the word "your" was used instead of "Summers". In that case it would be quite evident to many of the other readers that Adam X could still be included even if it was the "Summers brothers" reference that had been used, and even if Katherine was the only parent he shared with the other three, since she herself would be a Summers. Just checking! Fuyutaka 09:10, 21 January 2007 (UTC)
