Adamstown Heights is a small southern suburb in Newcastle, New South Wales, Australia, and is located just off Pacific Highway 8 km south-west of Newcastle's central business district (CBD).

Adamstown Heights contains two schools, Kotara High School, established in 1968 and containing 3 hectares of native bushland, as well Belair Primary School. The suburb is served by Westfield Kotara, formerly Garden City Kotara, on its northern border.

Adamstown Heights is located on several bus routes and Kotara and Adamstown railway stations are nearby.
