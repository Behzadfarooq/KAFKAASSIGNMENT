Adam Casey (born 1 May 1986 in Australia) is a professional football (soccer) player who currently plays as a striker for Sydney FC in the A-League.

Adam is a product of the Australian Institute of Sport system, having come through the Australian age group teams before signing for the Wollongong Wolves and then for the New Zealand Knights. Adam is a former Australian under 17 and current under 23 international player and most recently represented his country on a tour of Vietnam with the Australian Under-23 national team in April 2006 and is also a member of Graham Arnold's squad for the 2008 Summer Olympics. Adam scored no fewer than six goals in qualifying games for the 2003 Under 17 FIFA World Cup.
