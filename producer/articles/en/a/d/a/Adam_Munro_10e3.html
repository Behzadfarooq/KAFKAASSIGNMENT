Adam Munro (born November 12, 1982 in St. George, Ontario) is a professional ice hockey goaltender who currently plays for the Chicago Blackhawks of the NHL.

Munro was drafted in the 1st round, 29th overall by the Chicago Blackhawks in the 2001 NHL Entry Draft. He was signed as free agent with HC Fribourg-Gotteron (Switzerland) on July 7, 2006.
