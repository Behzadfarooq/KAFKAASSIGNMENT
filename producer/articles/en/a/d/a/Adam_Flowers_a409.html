Adam Flowers

Adam Flowers is an emerging American Lyric Tenor (Tenore lirico) who is based in San Francisco, California. Adam sings major lyric and Spinto lirico roles in Opera houses across the Western United States.

Adam was in the resident company of Opera San Jose from 2001-2006 and appeared there in 22 productions, in many leading roles.
