Adama Family

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

In the fictional Battlestar Galactica universe, the Adama family is the family of Commander Adama, commander of the Battlestar Galactica, and they are some of the core characters of the series. The composition of the family varies from the original 1978 series and the the 2003 remake.
