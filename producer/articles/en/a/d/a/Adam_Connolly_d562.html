Adam Connolly (born April 10, 1986 in Manchester)is an English footballer, who plays in midfield for Cheltenham Town.

He was an academy player for Cheltenham and signed professionally in 2004. He is not considered to be a big part of the squad and has only played six times in two years, scoring once.
