Adam Benjamin Elliot (2 January 1972 â€”) is an animator from Melbourne, Australia, best known for his Academy-Award winning claymation Harvie Krumpet. Elliot attended high school at Haileybury, Melbourne.

[edit] Other films

Elliot has made four other claymation films, three of them seemingly autobiographical: Brother (his sibling who died at a young age), Uncle (his uncle's odd life), Cousin (about his relative, who suffered cerebral palsy), and Human Behavioral Case Studies One. All of the films were narrated by Willian McInnes, and besides the narration, were mostly silent.
