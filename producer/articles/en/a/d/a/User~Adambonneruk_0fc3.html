[edit] About Me

Not much to say, I'm a geek who is interested in this whole "internet" thing, I like building computers and did one for myself a while back. I also enjoy computer programming, web design and computer games. A recent interest of mine has been open source software, for too long we have put up with microsoft's half hearted buggy attempts and these appliction communities are the answer, programs such as openoffice.org have inspired and changed alot of peoples minds. I believe that this is only the beggining though, one only has to look at what mozilla firefox has done.

I love to learn about computers and at the minute im trying to learn the Pascal programming language and a bit about css (Cascading Style Sheets).

I also enjoy helping out whereever i can with the development of opensource material, mainly by helping seed the main torrent releses of said software, ive also just created anew icon for 7zip.

I'm currently in my second year of sixth form in Ormskirk School, my subjects include Computer Studies and Psychology.
