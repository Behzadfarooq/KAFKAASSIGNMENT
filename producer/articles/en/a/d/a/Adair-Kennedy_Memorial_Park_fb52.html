Adair-Kennedy Memorial Park

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Adair-Kennedy Memorial Park is a sports facility complex in Juneau, Alaska, and adjacent to Floyd Dryden Middle School. It was named after Richard James Adair and Jimmy Earl Kennedy, two Juneau Police Department officers who died in the line of duty in April 17, 1979.[1] A motion before the city's Parks and Recreation Advisory Committee to name the football field at the park Reilly Richey Stadium at Adair-Kennedy Memorial Park, after Reilly Richey, who was a coach for the local high school football team that passed away recently, was proposed on April 5, 2005[2], but delayed at the request of the Adair family.[2]
