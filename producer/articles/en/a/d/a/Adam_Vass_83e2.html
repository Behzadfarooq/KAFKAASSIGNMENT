Ádám Vass (born 9 September 1988 in Kápolnásnyék) is a Hungarian footballer who is currently playing for Stoke City.[1] He plays as a central midfielder.

Vass was signed from Ferencvaros in November 2004.[2]

Vass has played for Hungary at under-17 and under-19 levels. On 15th November 2006, Vass made his debut for the Hungary senior side against Canada. He became the youngest ever Stoke City player to become a full international.[3] He earned his second cap on 6 February 2007 against Cyprus, but has still not had his first senior game at club level.[4]
