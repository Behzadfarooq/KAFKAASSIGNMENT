Adam Lynn born (November 16, 1977 in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania) is an American professional mixed martial arts (MMA) fighter. He is currently fighting for the Southern California Condors of the International Fight League.

[edit] Bio

At twenty years old Adam joined the Marine Corps. While in the Marines Lynn discovered a passion for mixed martial arts fighting, which he learned from a fellow soldier. Once he left the service he set his sights on becoming a professional fighter.
