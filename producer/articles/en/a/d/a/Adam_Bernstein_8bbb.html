Adam Bernstein (born May 7, 1960) is an American film and television director and scriptwriter. He was born in Princeton, New Jersey.

[edit] Select TV work

  • 30 Rock (2006-)
  • Scrubs (2001-2006)
  • Ed (2001-2003)
  • Entourage (2004)
  • The Job (2002)
  • Action (1999-2000)
  • Strangers with Candy (1998)

[edit] Theatrical film work

  • Six Ways To Sunday (1997) (also writer)
  • It's Pat (1994)
