Adam Crozier

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Adam Crozier (born 26 January 1964) is the current chief executive of the Royal Mail.

Brought up on the Isle of Bute[1] he was educated at Heriot-Watt University. He formerly worked for advertising agency Saatchi and Saatchi, and was chief executive of the Football Association from 2000 to 2002.
