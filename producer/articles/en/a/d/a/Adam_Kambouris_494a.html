Adam Kambouris

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Adam Kambouris is an Australian Rugby League player who currently plays for North Queensland Cowboys' feeder club the Young Guns. He was signed for the 2007 season from Queensland Cup rivals Norths Devils. Due to his Greek heritage, Adam has represented Greece on several occasions.
