Adam Bernero

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia


Adam Gino Bernero (born November 28, 1976 in Los Gatos, California) is a Major League Baseball pitcher. Bernero has pitched in the majors for the Detroit Tigers, Colorado Rockies, Atlanta Braves, Philadelphia Phillies, Kansas City Royals, and recently signed a minor league contract with the Boston Red Sox. He has a career record of 11-27. In his first start with the Royals he pitched 5.2 innings allowing 7 hits and only one run, while striking out 5. He got the no-decision.In his second start with the Royals in the 2006 season, he pitched 6 innings,allowed no runs and struck out 6.

Bernero will miss the entirety of the 2007 season after undergoing Tommy John surgery on March 1, 2007.[1]
