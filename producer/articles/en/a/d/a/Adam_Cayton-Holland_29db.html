Adam Cayton-Holland is a newspaper columnist and stand-up comedian in Denver, Colorado.

His column, "What's So Funny," appears weekly in Westword, a Denver alternative newspaper. His article "Word of Mouth", about Denver's mobile burrito vendors, won the 2006 Bert Greene Award for Excellence in Food Journalism from the International Association of Culinary Professionals.

Cayton-Holland also appears onstage frequently at Comedy Works, a popular comedy club in Denver.

Cayton-Holland, a long-time Denver resident, has lived with his partner Stephen, and their two prize winning show-dogs, for the last 3 years.
