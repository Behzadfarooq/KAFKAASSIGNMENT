Adam Donegar (b. December 5, 1980 in Hewlett, New York) is an American lacrosse player. He attended and played his college lacrosse at Johns Hopkins University where he was team Captain as a senior and a two-time First Team All-American. [1]

Doneger played for the 2002 U.S. Men's National Team in World Lacrosse Championship that won the Gold Medal.[2]

Donegar is a midfielder with the New Jersey Pride in Major League Lacrosse. He won the Major League Lacrosse Rookie of the Year Award in 2003.
