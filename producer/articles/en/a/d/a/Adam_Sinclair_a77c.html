Adam Antony Sinclair
Born: Feb 29, 1984
Coimbatore, India
Occupation: Hockey player
Website: http://www.indiahockey.com

Adam Sinclair (born February 29, 1984) was a member of the Indian field hockey team at the 2004 Summer Olympics in Athens, and is a resident of Coimbatore. He made his international debut in May 2004 during a Four Nation Tournament in Gifu (Japan). Sinclair currently plays for the Chennai Veerans.


Sinclair was the best in Field Hockey at Stanes Higher Secondary School, Coimbatore.HE was the captain of the school team and under his capitancy the school won many tournaments. He was elected School Pupil Leader of the School in 2001. He was also leader of his 'house' ,"Panthers" and led them to victory in the Intra-School Sports Event in 2001. He did his futher studies in PSG College of arts and science, Coimbarore.

Sinclair has also won accolades and medals as an athlete in the triple jump and long distance running events.
