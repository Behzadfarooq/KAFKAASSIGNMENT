Adam Darragh (born August 16, 1979 in Gold Coast, Queensland,) is an Australian basketball player, currently playing guard for the New Zealand Breakers in the Australian NBL. Darragh attended college at Martin Methodist College in Tennessee, USA in 2001-02. He previously played in the Australian NBL for the Brisbane Bullets from 2004-2006 and the West Sydney Razorbacks during the end of the 2006 NBL season.He signed as a free agent for the New Zealand Breakers for the start of the 2006-2007 Australian NBL season.
