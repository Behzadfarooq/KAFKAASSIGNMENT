Adam Rabinowitz (Hebrew: אדם רבינוביץ, Born 1973) is an artist working and leaving in the city of Tel Aviv.

Rabinowitz Studied through 1994-98 in The Bezalel Academy of Art and Design, Jerusalem, Art Department.

Prizes - 2002 Prize for a young artist, Ministry of Science, Culture and Sport; 2003 Shmuel Givon Prize, Tel Aviv Museum.

He has presented solo exhbition works in several galleries and museums such as: Dvir Gallery [1], Givon Gallery [2], and Museum Haus Esters, Krefeld [3].
