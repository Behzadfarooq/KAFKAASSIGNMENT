Adam Barlow is a fictional character in the British soap opera Coronation Street. He first appeared aged thirteen when visiting his granddad Ken and uncle Peter, with his mother Susan. It was revealed that he was the son of Mike Baldwin, something neither he nor Mike knew. Susan died shortly afterwards in a road accident and Adam went to live with Mike. Adam left Coronation Street to go to boarding school in Scotland, but returned when doing his A-levels, having dropped out of school.

[edit] Storylines

On August Bank Holiday 2005, Adam bought a car from an auction, but in an embarrassing turn of events, after filling his car up with six gallons of petrol on a day out with his girlfriend Candice Stowe, his car ignited by the roadside. The cause of the fire was a mystery, until a policewoman told him his car ran on diesel, not petrol.

He worked at his father Mike's factory, Underworld, and annoyed his new found half-brother, Danny, by being the apple of his father's eye. He was dumped by ambitious Candice Stowe via letter, had a short fling with Kelly Crabtree, and had a small crush on Sarah Platt when he first returned to the Street. Adam began dating shy factory worker Joanne Jackson after they flirted at his birthday party.

Following Mike's death, he is determined to bring brother Danny down after he stole his inheritance. Adam is unaware that the will is invalid because Mike's last will, the real one, left everything to him. Only Leanne Battersby and Danny knew it exists, and were determined that Adam not get his inheritance.

However, following a split with Danny, Leanne gave a photocopy of the real will to Adam, and it is now possible he will claim inheritance, and press criminal charges on Danny. After the interrogation of Danny by the police, he decided to offer Adam 40% of the shares in the factory. Acting on Ken's advice Adam accepted.

In September 2006, Adam sold his 40% of Underworld to Liam Connor. Liam's brother Paul bought the remaining 60% in January 2007.

In early February 2007 Joanne Jackson dumped Adam by text, after not even seeing him for months.
