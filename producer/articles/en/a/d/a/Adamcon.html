Adamcon

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

You have new messages (last change).

Adamcon is an annual gathering of North American Coleco Adam computer enthusiasts. The convention has been meeting since 1988 and is held in a different location each year. The 2006 Adamcon was held in Chicago, Illinois. The 2007 event will be in Ottawa, Canada.
