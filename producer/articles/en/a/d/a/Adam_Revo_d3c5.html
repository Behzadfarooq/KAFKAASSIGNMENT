Adam Revo is a city car manufactured by Adam Motor Company in Pakistan. It is the first indigenously Pakistani built car with all of its parts being produced in Pakistan, however the engine is temporarily being imported from China.

At the moment two variants are available equipped with different engines: Revo 80 (0.8L) and Revo 105 (1.05L).

The Revo is about 10-20% cheaper than its foreign competitors. The car's target audience is Pakistan's middle class car buyer. The affordable price, low running costs, fuel efficiency, ready availability of parts at countrywide 3S and 2S dealership network, larger cabin and trunk size – the Revo is a very good addition to Pakistan's car Market.

[edit] Specification

Engine

  • Revo 80: 797cc straight-4 OHC petrol with carburetor — 35 ps at 5500 rpm, 52 N·m at 3000 rpm
  • Revo 105: 1051cc straight-4 OHC petrol with EFI — 56 ps at 5500 rpm, 83 N·m at 3000 rpm

Suspension

  • Front: Strut with Coil spring
  • Rear: Leaf spring with Shock absorber

Brakes

  • Front: Disc with booster
  • Rear: Drum
