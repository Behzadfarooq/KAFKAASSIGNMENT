Привет, よい日, and howdy! My name is adam12901...formerly known as User:Adam Riley. I'm a 23 year old geography student from Northern New York. I'm interested in Geography, Photography, and Russia. I contribute mostly to articles relating to New York State, as I know most about here than anywhere else.

I've been with Wikipedia since March, 2006. I make corrections on things like spelling errors, and some grammar stuff. I've recently become a newpage patroller, watching for vandalism in my spare time (which is not very often). I think that being a newpage patroller is finally a place where I fit well into the Wikipedia community, and hope to possibly one day become more involved.

Off of Wikipedia, my claim to fame is proving that one can literally walk into the United States from another country, such as when I walked from Hemmingford, Quebec to Mooers, New York, with only passing a sign saying "Arretez - Frontiere des Etats Unis".
