Adam Legzdins (born November 28, 1986) is a footballer, currently playing for Birmingham City. He has previously played on loan for Alfreton Town, Halifax Town, Oldham Athletic and Macclesfield Town. Legzdins has a one-year contract that will expire in the 07/08 football season. He was handed a squad number early in the season because of an injury to first choice keeper Maik Taylor.
