Adam Hamilton (musician)

Adam Hamilton is the current bassist of L.A. Guns. So far he has played on the albums Waking the Dead, Rips the Covers Off, Tales from the Strip, and Loud and Dangerous: Live from Hollywood.

He has also played in Joey C. Jones And The Glory Hounds, Needle Park, and Dokken.

He also plays other instruments besides bass.

Adam was born on November 11th.

Adam has been credited for the movies: The Bourne Identity, What Lies Beneath, Boys and Girls, Final Destination, Girlfight, Pushing Tin, Urban Legend, Breaking Up, and Melinda and Melinda.

Adam has been credited for in TV shows: Lost, The Simpsons, Six Feet Under,Law and Order, Party of Five, MTV Real World,Road Rules, Sweet Sixteen, Navy CSI, SNL, The Osbournes, and Touched by an Angel

Adam has been credited for the documentaries: The Fire Within (score) and Chasing The Horizon (score
