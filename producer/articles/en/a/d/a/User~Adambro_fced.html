Introduction

My name is Adam Brookes and I'm an Electronic Engineering student from Huddersfield in the United Kingdom. I started contributing to Wikipedia in mid 2004.

My work on Wikipedia

I spend a lot of time my time patrolling recent edits looking for vandalism. I find it very rewarding knowing that I'm helping to protect Wikipedia. I also look at new pages and improve them where possible or propose them for deletion when appropriate.

Most of my edits are involved in maintenance tasks, such repairing links to disambiguation pages, but I do contribute content whenever possible.

If you've come to this page because you've noticed a problem with one of my edits then please let me know. I accept that I do make the occasional mistake and would appreciate any advice.
