This was nominated for speedy deletion on the grounds that it is not notable. Notability is not a grounds on which an article can be speedy deleted. This school is not one of the leading schools in Delhi, however it is well known in the Mayur Vihar area because of its being located across a large local market. Searches on google turned up nothing notable. Many private schools in North India focus on enhancing their infrastructure through studuent donations, are a good business, and do answer social needs of providing education in English. They also generally follow the relevant Education laws. However very few have anything notable to contribute in the field of education or have come into being through a new vision.
