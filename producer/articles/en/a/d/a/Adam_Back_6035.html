Adam Back

Dr Adam Back is a well know expert in cryptography as well as a software hacker based in the UK. He is the inventor of hashcash [1], a denial-of-service counter measure tool based on the concept of proof-of-work done by the sender, as well as the developper of the Eternity service [2], a distributed data haven. Dr Back is also well know for the RSA export-a-crypto-system munitions T-shirt [3].
