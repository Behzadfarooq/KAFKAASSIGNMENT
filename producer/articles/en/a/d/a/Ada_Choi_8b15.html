Ada Choi

Ada Choi Siu Fun (蔡少芬) is a Hong Kong actress best known for her work for TVB television, and to a lesser extent, for her film work. Born in September 17, 1973, she was the second runner-up in the 1991 Miss Hong Kong beauty pageant.

  • English Name: Ada Choi
  • Cantonese Name: Choi Siu Fun
  • Chinese Name: Cai Shao Fen
  • Vietnamese name: Thái Thiếu Phấn
  • Nickname: Cha Siu Fun (BBQ Pork Rice), Mei Nu Fen (Pretty Fun), Bor Lor Fun (Pineapple Fun)
  • Languages spoken: Cantonese, Mandarin, Teochew, English
  • Dialect: Chiuchow (Teochew)
  • Favorite Role Of Hers: Jackie in Healing Hands
  • Height: 173 cm
  • Weight: 110 lb
  • Vitals: 33-24-35
