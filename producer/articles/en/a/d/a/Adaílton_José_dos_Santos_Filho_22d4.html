Adaílton José dos Santos Filho (born 14 April 1983 in Salvador, Bahia) is a Brazilian Central Defender. He currently plays for Santos Futebol Clube.

[edit] Honours

  • Bahia State League: 1999, 2000, 2002, 2003
  • Nordeste Cup: 1999, 2003
  • World Cup (U 20): 2003

[edit] Contract

  • 3rd January 2007 to 31st January 2007
