Adaílton Martins Bolzan, best known as Adaílton (born in Santiago, Brazil, 24 January 1977) is a Brazilian footballer in striker role.

Top goalscorer and winner adidas Golden Shoe in 1997 FIFA World Youth Championship, he was played in career (started in 1994) with Juventude and Guarani, in France with Paris Saint Germain and, in Italian Serie A, with Parma, Genoa and Hellas Verona.
