ADAP Galo Maringá Futebol Clube, usually known as Galo/Adap, Galo Adap or ADAP Galo Maringá, is a Brazilian football club from Maringá, Paraná state.

[edit] History

On November 25, 2006, two Paraná state clubs, ADAP, of Campo Mourão city, and Galo Maringá, of Maringá city, fused. [1] The club kept the same colors and kits of Galo Maringá, but the official club's foundation date was ADAP's one, 1999. ADAP Galo Maringá's logo is similar to Galo Maringá's, but including the words "ADAP" and "1999" and at the same time keeping the name "Galo Maringá". [2]

On January 14, 2007, ADAP Galo Maringá will play its first official match, against Nacional of Rolândia, at Willie Davids Stadium, Maringá. This will also be the club's first Campeonato Paranaense match.

[edit] Stadium

ADAP Galo Maringá's home stadium is Willie Davids stadium[1], inaugurated in 1957, with a maximum capacity of 23,000 people. [3]

The club also owns a training ground, in Campo Mourão.[1]

[edit] Club colors

Galo/Adap's colors are black and white, which were the colors of Galo Maringá. [2]
