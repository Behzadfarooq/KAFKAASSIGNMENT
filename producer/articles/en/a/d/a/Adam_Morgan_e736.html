Adam Morgan (born October 28, 1981) is an Australian rules footballer in the Australian Football League.

Recruited to the Western Bulldogs from Port Adelaide during the 2003 trade period, he has managed a total of 14 senior games since 1999.

He was recruited by the Bulldogs as a key position player that can play at either end, but following two successive season ending injuries in 2005 and 2006, Morgan was delisted by the Bullldogs at the end of the 2006 season.
