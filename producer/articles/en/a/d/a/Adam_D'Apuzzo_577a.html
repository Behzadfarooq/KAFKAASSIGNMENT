Adam D'Apuzzo

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Adam D'Apuzzo (born October 20, 1986) is an Australian football (soccer) player. He currently plays as a central midfielder for the Australian A-League club Newcastle United Jets. He has previously played for the Marconi Stallions in the NSW Premier League competition. Adam used to attend Trinity Grammar School which is in Summer Hill, Sydney, Australia and was the Captain of Soccer.
