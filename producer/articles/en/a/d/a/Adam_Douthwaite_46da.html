    Adam Douthwaite (born November 13, 1985) is an English football player who currently plays for Waitakere United (since september 2006). Before he moved to New Zealand, he played in the English lower leagues for Altrincham F.C.. Adam is 1.70 m tall and weighs 66 kg. He currently wears the number 25 shirt for Waitakere United.
