Adam Campbell (born January 25, 1985) is an Australian rules footballer for the Fremantle Dockers in the Australian Football League.

[edit] Fremantle Dockers

Coming from a rugby union background in the town of Hamilton, Campbell represented New Zealand in rugby at junior level before moving to Australia at age 16 where he began playing Aussie rules in 2001, quickly proving a standout talent in country football, playing full forward at the North Ballarat Rebels.

Nominated as the overall No. 27 selection at the 2003 National AFL Draft, he is a key position prospect who spent the 2004 & 2005 seasons as a development player.

Adam debuted in Round 10, 2006 against Richmond playing off the bench having spent some time in the reserves at West Perth.
