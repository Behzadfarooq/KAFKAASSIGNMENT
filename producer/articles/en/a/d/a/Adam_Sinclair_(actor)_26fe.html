Adam Sinclair (actor)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Adam Sinclair is a Scottish film and television actor.

[edit] Personal life

Sinclair married girlfriend Michelle Kath in the Edinburgh City Chambers in 2004, they also had a second wedding in California for their American friends and family. He met Michelle in Hawaii on the set of To End All Wars, when he was taping with her stepdad, Kiefer Sutherland. Sinclair is originally from East Kilbride but now lives near Venice Beach, California with his DJ wife and young son Hamish.

[edit] Career

Sinclair has had many small roles in television and film although he is perhaps best remembered for his role in the British television series Mile High, as the gay, self-destructive cabin crew team leader Will O'Brien.

He plans to start an international production company.[citation needed]
