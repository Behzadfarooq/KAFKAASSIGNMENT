Adachi District, Fukushima

Adachi (安達郡; -gun) is a district located in Fukushima, Japan. As of January 2007, the district has a population of 8,577 and an area of 79.46 km², for a population density of 107.9 per km².

[edit] Towns and villages

  • Adachi
  • Iwashiro
  • Otama
  • Towa

[edit] History

On January 1, 2007, the village of Shirasawa and the town of Motomiya, formerly part of Adachi District, merged to form the city of Motomiya, leaving the district. Prior to the merger, as of 2003, the district had an estimated population of 68,436 and a density of 178.99 persons per km². The total area was 382.34 km².
