Adam Kokoszka (born on October 6, 1986 in Andrychów, Poland) is a promising, young Polish footballer (central defender) currently playing for Wisła Kraków. Although he has made only a few appearances for his club, he was noticed by Leo Beenhakker and debuted for the Poland national football team a short while later, in a friendly versus the United Arab Emirates on December 6, 2006 in Abu Zabi. In the next friendly match against Estonia he scored his first ever goal for Poland.

His debut for Wisła Kraków came on August 4, 2006 in the game against Arka Gdynia. In the winter break 2006/2007 he was connected with some clubs like Zagłębie Lubin , but he stayed in Wisła Kraków


[edit] Trivia
