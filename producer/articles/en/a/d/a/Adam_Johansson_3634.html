Adam Johansson (born 21 February 1983 in Jönköping) is a Swedish football defender.

After playing for two local clubs and Västra Frölunda IF, he joined IFK Göteborg in 2005.

[edit] Clubs

  • Slottskogen/Godhem IF, Azalea/Kungsladugård, Västra Frölunda IF (-2005)
  • IFK Göteborg (2005-)
