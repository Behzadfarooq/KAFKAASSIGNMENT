Adam Michał Darski (b. June 10, 1977 in Gdynia), stage name Nergal, is a Polish death metal musician.

[edit] Biography

Nergal is the founder, lyricist, main composer, frontman and manager of blackened death metal band Behemoth, which he started when he was fourteen. He also plays on lead, rhythm and acoustic guitar. Nergal likes to interact with the crowd during performances. He is known for contribution with following bands: Hermh, Nile, Damnation, Vader, Sweet Noise, Mastiphal, December's Fire, Mess Age, Corruption, Hangover, The Wolverines and Hefeystos.

Nergal has completed six years of history and one year of Latin at Gdańsk University, and is qualified to be a museum curator.
