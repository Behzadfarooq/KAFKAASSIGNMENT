Adam Resurrected is an upcoming dramatic film, scheduled for release in 2008. It concerns an ex-circus performer who, after World War II, becomes the ringleader at an asylum for Holocaust survivors. Adam Resurrected is currently in the process of casting, which is being conducted by Anja Dihrberg.

Jeff Goldblum will star as Adam, the movie will also star Willem Dafoe[1].
