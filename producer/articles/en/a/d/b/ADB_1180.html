ADB

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

ADB is a three-letter acronym that may refer to:

  • ADB (file format), used by Microsoft Access
  • Absolute Debugger, a general purpose debugger for UNIX platforms
  • Active Dimension Battle, a battle system in computer gaming
  • Adnan Menderes Airport, the IATA airport code for the airport in Izmir, Turkey
  • Advanced Digital Broadcast, supplier of digital TV set-top-boxes for cable, satellite, terrestrial and telecommunications
  • African Development Bank, a non-profit financial institution whose aim is to help developing African countries
  • Agricultural Development Bank, a name shared by several financial institutions, including:
    • Agricultural Development Bank of Trinidad and Tobago
    • Agricultural Development Bank of Pakistan
  • Allgemeine Deutsche Biographie, a biographical reference work in the German language
  • Apple Desktop Bus, a bit-serial computer bus for connecting low-speed devices to computers
  • Asian Development Bank, a non-profit financial institution whose aim is to help developing Asian countries
  • Australian Dictionary of Biography, Australia's pre-eminent dictionary of national biography.
