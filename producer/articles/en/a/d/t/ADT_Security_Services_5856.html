ADT Security Services also known as ADT fire and security or simply ADT is a division of Tyco International, Ltd. and a major worldwide supplier of electronic security and fire alarm systems, communication systems and integrated building management systems.

[edit] Company overview

ADT is the leading providers of burglar and fire alarms, access control and video surveillance systems.

With over 20 million customers nationwide (US) ADT has grown, mostly through the acquisition of other companies, to become the largest alarm company in the United States.

[edit] Products and services

  • Home security
  • Fire detection
  • Public address and voice alarm (PA/VA)
  • Fixed fire extinguishing and suppression systems
  • Access control systems
  • CCTV
  • Care communications (Nurse call, disabled refuge and toilet alarms, lone worker protection, staff attack alert systems)
  • Electronic article surveillance (Commonly known as tagging)
  • Building management systems
  • 24 hour alarm Monitoring
  • Security systems service and maintenance

ADT UK has also a specialist vehicle division tasked to create and maintain CCTV and riot control vehicles for police forces around the country.

[edit] The Americas

ADT has branches covering the United states, Canada and Latin America.

[edit] Europe

ADT has branches in at least 19 countries in Europe. ADT UK and Ireland has 41 branches employing around 6000 people. ADT Europe has an annual turnover of at least £800 million.

[edit] Africa

ADT has a branch in South Africa.

[edit] Asia

ADT has branches in at least 7 countries in Asia.

[edit] Australasia

ADT has branches in Australia and New Zealand. They have the contract for maintaining Cityrail's CCTV system[2].

[edit] About ADT systems

ADT's main product is the installation and monitoring of alarm systems, whereby they maintain a centralized monitoring station that receives electronic signals from an alarm system when an alarm event is triggered, such as in the case of an intrusion. A live monitoring specialist examines the data being received from the alarm system and attempts to determine the type of response the alarm signal warrants. In most cases the monitoring specialist will promptly attempt to contact the residence via telephone to investigate further and determine if the signal is a false alarm. Beyond that, if necessary, the monitoring specialist can notify the local police department and/or an authorized security response service of the alarm status and the event address. ADT maintains five monitoring stations in the United States.

[edit] History

All text in this section is based on ADT sales literature, so while not being untruthful, will likely not be impartial.

[edit] 1874

In America in the 1800s there were many small telegraph delivery companies, in 1887 a large number of these companies affiliated and became American District Telegraph

[edit] 1890s

With the advent of advanced call boxes and multi-signal electric protection systems in the early 1890s, ADT gained a solid reputation in security protections.

[edit] 1899

With the increase in telephone usage in the late 1800s ADTs messenger business slowly declined in popularity. ADT tried branching out and developing their signalling business while still maintaining their telegraph business as primary income source.

[edit] 1901

ADT incorporated into Western Union. Many developments were made in this year in the, now, primary field of signalling. ADT separated its messenger business from its main signalling business. This was the beginning of the end for the old ADT company.

[edit] 1909

Western Union (and ADT) came under the control of AT&T.

[edit] 1910-1930

As technology improved and new ideas came into practise ADT grew its business into new areas like Fire alarms and hold-up alarms.

[edit] 1930-1940

What with the Second world war and the great depression and the increased industrialisation they brought security became more important to businesses. ADT grew and capitalised on this new age.

[edit] 1960

ADT becomes a publicly owned company.

[edit] 1980-1990

In the late 1980s ADT shareholders agreed to change the corporate name from American District Telegraph to simply ADT, Inc. By the mid 1990s ADT surpassed the 1 million customer milestone.

[edit] 1997

ADT was purchased by Tyco

[edit] 2000-Present

ADT currently have branches in 30 different countries, with over 20,000 employees. ADT had turnover of US$7.2 billion in 2006. In 2006, ADT created the Custom Home Services Division.
