[edit] Request a coach :: Coaching volunteers :: Update your status

Thank you for your interest in admin coaching. Hopefully you won't have to wait too long! We're in the process of re-organising at the moment and are starting a new system of requests.

New requests should be entered in the following syntax to the bottom of the list:

  • #{{user|USERNAME}} <very short comment> ~~~~

Coaches: If you wish, you may contact a user at or near the top of the list, without going through the coordinator. Leave a message at that person's name here, indicating you have contacted that person. If you are coaching him/her, remove that name from this list, then update your status.

Current requestors: Some of these requests are quite old, and we need to know if you are still interested. If you are, copy/paste --''still interested as of ~~~~~'' after your name.
