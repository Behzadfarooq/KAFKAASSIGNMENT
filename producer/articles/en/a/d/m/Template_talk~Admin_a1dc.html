[edit] Usage

  • Links to the user page, user talk page, and contributions, block, protect, delete and move logs of an editor. Thus
{{admin|Example}}
produces
Example (talk • contribs • blocks • protects • deletions • moves)
  • To reduce the text space used by this template, you can also use Template:Admin-abbr which produces the same links but abbreviates the words.
{{admin-abbr|Jimbo Wales|Jimbo_Wales}}
produces
Jimbo Wales (t c b p d m)

[edit] Not working for me

See Ta bu shi da yu (talk • contribs • blocks • protects • deletions • moves). Why are the external links not working? - Ta bu shi da yu 03:58, 13 April 2006 (UTC)

Hello Tabu. You may have figured this out by now, but if not, the reason is that when internal link templates are used for items with more than a single word, an underscore must always be used in place of the spaces between words. For example, {{admin|Ta bu shi da yu}} produces Ta bu shi da yu (talk • contribs • blocks • protects • deletions • moves); correctly using underscores, however, works the requisite magic: Ta_bu_shi_da_yu (talk • contribs • blocks • protects • deletions • moves). If you want the first link (to your userpage) to appear without the underscores, you can use {{admin|Ta bu shi da yu|Ta_bu_shi_da_yu}}, which gives Ta bu shi da yu (talk • contribs • blocks • protects • deletions • moves) —Encephalon 07:31, 17 April 2006 (UTC)
This bug has been fixed. The second parameter is no longer necessary, or even accepted at all. — May. 26, '06 [10:27] <freak|talk>
