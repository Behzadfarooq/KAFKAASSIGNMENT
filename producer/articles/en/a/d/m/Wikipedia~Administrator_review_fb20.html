Note: Admins could kickstart this process, although it may be just as well to self certify a request for comment.

[edit] Rationale

The idea is to create something similar to Wikipedia:Editor review, a voluntary forum where admins could come and list themselves and then be open to review. It could be used as the forum of choice for those admins open to recall. It could also be useful to those admins considering a run for bureaucrat.

[edit] Form

The suggested form would be almost exactly similar to the setup used at Wikipedia:Editor review.

[edit] Issues

Issues which need to be debated include whether a review would be open to all users, or only admins.
