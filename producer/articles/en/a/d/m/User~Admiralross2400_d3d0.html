[edit] LIFE AND TIMES OF CHARLES ROBERT ROSS Esq

[edit] History

Charles Ross was born on 21st July 1986 in Doncaster Royal Infirmary at 3.29am. His mother was Margaret Ross and his father was also called Charles Ross. At the time of his birth, his father was employed as a Corporal in the RAF. His father maintained and repaired the aircraft as a mechanic. Soon after he was born, they moved away to Geilenkirchen as his father was posted to RAF Wildenwrath. Here they stayed until 1991. It was also here that Charles' brother George was born on 30th April 1989. In 1991, they moved to RAF Lossiemouth where they remained until mid 1992 when they moved to Armadale. Until recently, they all lived here, but as he started attending Edinburgh University, Charles moved away to Edinburgh where he remains to this day.

[edit] Present

Charles currently studies Computational Physics BSc at University of Edinburgh and is in his 2nd year there. He is in a long term relationship with his Girlfriend and has been for the past 8 months. He can be contacted at: Rocky_Horror_Show'at'hotmail(dot)com
