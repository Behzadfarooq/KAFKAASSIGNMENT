Administrator of the Turks of Caicos

Administrator of the Turks of Caicos was the admnistrator of the Turks and Caicos from 1859 to 1973. It replaced the previous post of Commissioner of the Turks and Caicos and was replaced by the Governor of the Turks and Caicos after 1973.

A list of Commissioners:

  • Geoffrey Colin Guy (1921-) 1959-1965
  • Robert Everard Wainwright (1913-1990) 1965, 1967-1971
  • John Anthony Golding 1965-1967
  • Alexander Graham Mitchell (1923-) 1971-1973
