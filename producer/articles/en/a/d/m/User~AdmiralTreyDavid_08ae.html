[edit] Awesomeness

I am AdmiralTreyDavid (Trey). I'm a 18 year old mofo from Georgia. I'm a Sophomore in college majoring in Secondary English Education and minoring in Spanish as a foreign language. I LOVE music, it's a huge influence in my life. I mostly like progressive rock bands such as Dream Theater, Rush, Symphony X, Theocracy, Aviab, Savatage, and Pink Floyd; thrash bands like Megadeth, Tourniquet, Anthrax, old Metallica; and power metal bands like Vision Divine, Iron Maiden, and Pyramaze;and I also love classical music and solo guitar artists.

I'm currently in two rock bands, and am loosely affiliated with several others by either being friends with them, having taught their lead guitarists, or being asked to join them. I've been teaching guitar lessons for over three years now, and several of my students are in rock bands playing shows, and are awesome musicians in their own rights, which is even better than seeing myself grow.

I love to drink Mountain Dew, and started a religion called Mofoism to promote it, among other things ;D. This is not a 'major religion' so there cannot be a mofoism article on Wikipedia, which is ridiculous and stupid, for Mofoism now has over 200 'followers', so everybody should petition for a Mofoism Wiki.

[edit] Contributions

All in all, I'll randomly contribute random things to Wikipedia, mostly to things affiliated with music; rock bands, musical theory pages, musical history, ect. I'll also contribute to sports pages whenever I can. I'm also known to vandalize every now and then... I consider this to be constructive vandalism. Normally if I vandalize you now, I'm pointing out how ridiculously incorrect a page is, which should be considered helpful, not evil ;D But sometimes I do go on minor rampages, which is my right as an amazing [Mofo].

[edit] Personal Things

I love reading. I'm 6'3". I played high school varsity baseball for all four years and played all nine positions during my career. I hit one HR my Sophomore year, three my Junior year, and one my senior year. I also stole 34 bases my Junior year. I hold the supposed longest HR in school history, at an estimated 450 feet, but that's not official because it's hard to measure at a poor and crappy high school... But it cleared a 30 foot net over the 10 foot center field fence which is 400 feet away, and also cleared a playground behind it... so it was pretty far. I guess that balances out my 10,000,000,000,000,000,000,000,000,000 strikeouts. I played JV basketball my Sophomore year and averaged 5-6 blocks and 11 rebounds per game, including 14 blocks in a single game as a league high, but didn't score hardly any points... I think I only scored in double digits one time. I also played varsity my senior year but didn't get enough playing time to make stats worth putting up here. I hold a first degree black belt in Tae Kwon Do, and I am a sophomore at Columbus State University, where I'm majoring in Secondary English Education. I'm also in two rock bands right now, and I do a lot of my own stuff. I just recently got real recording stuff so I should be able to record some good music soon.
