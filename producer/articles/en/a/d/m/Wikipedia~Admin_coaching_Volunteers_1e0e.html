[edit] Request a coach :: Coaching volunteers :: Update your status

[edit] Instructions

  1. Add your name to the bottom of the list with ":#~~~~"
  2. Go to the requests page and contact one of the requestors at or near the top of the list.
  3. Once your coaching is "official", add yourself and your student to the status page.
  4. Start coaching.
