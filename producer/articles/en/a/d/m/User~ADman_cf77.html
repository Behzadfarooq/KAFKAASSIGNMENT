[edit] Biography

Adam Jones was born on March 18, 1989 in Shinnston, West Virginia. He is currently a senior at Bridgeport High School in Bridgeport, West Virginia. He is very musical and is involved in many band programs at school. He plays lead trumpet in his school's Jazz Band and is also a member of the Symphonic Band, Marching Band, Pep Band, and others. He also successfully auditioned for West Virginia's All-State Band. He play drums in the bands Jonestown and Click It or Tick It. In the past he has performed with One Knight Stand. His girlfriend, Tiffany McGary, currently attends West Virginia University in Morgantown, West Virginia. Adam also enjoys watching his favorite sports teams, finding information on his computer, using his DVD player, and eating candy.
