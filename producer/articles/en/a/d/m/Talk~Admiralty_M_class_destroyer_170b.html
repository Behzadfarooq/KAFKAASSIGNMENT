This is a listing of Moon Class Destroyers, They were constructed in Great Britain between 1914 and 1916, and were retired from Royal Navy service in 1921. In 1920 two vessels were transferred to the Canadian Navy and were scrapped in 1929.

HMS Mons. Launched May 1st 1915. Sold for scrap November 1921.

HMS Marne. Launched May 29th 1915. Sold for scrap September 1921.

HMS Mystic. Launched June 26th 1915. Sold for scrap November 1921.

HMS Maenad. Launched August 10th 1915. Sold for scrap September 1921.

HMS Manners. Launched June 15th 1915. Sold for scrap October 1921.

HMS Mandate. Launched April 27th 1915. Sold for scrap September 1921.

HMS Magic. Launched September 10th 1915. Sold for scrap September 1921.

HMS Moresby. Launched November 20th 1915. Sold for scrap May 1921.

HMS Marmion. Launched May 28th 1915. Sunk after colliding with another vessel on October 21st 1917.

HMS Martial. Launched July 1st 1915. Sold for scrap May 1921.

HMS Mary Rose. Launched October 8th 1915. Sunk on 17th October 1917 by German cruisers Brummer and Bremse.

HMS Menace. Launched November 9th 1915. Sold for scrap in 1921.

HMS Michael. Launched May 19th 1915. One of four Thornycroft specials. Sold for scrap September 1921.

HMS Milbrook. Launched July 12th 1915. One of four Thornycroft specials. Sold for scrap September 1921.

HMS Minion. Launched September 11th 1915. One of four Thornycroft specials. Sold for scrap November 1921.

HMS Munster. Launched November 24th 1915. One of four Thornycroft specials. Sold for scrap November 1921.

HMS Moon. Launched April 23rd 1915. One of four Yarrow specials. Sold for scrap May 1921.

HMS Morning Star. Launched June 26th 1915. One of four Yarrow specials. Sold for scrap December 1921.

HMS Mounsey. Launched September 11th 1915. One of four Yarrow specials. Sold for scrap November 1921.

HMS Musketeer. Launched November 12th 1915. One of four Yarrow specials. Sold for scrap November 1921.

HMS Mameluke. Launched August 14th 1915. Sold for scrap September 1921.

HMS Marvel. Launched October 7th 1915. Sold for scrap May 1921.

HMS Mischief. Launched October 12th 1915. Sold for scrap November 1921.

HMS Mindful. Launched August 24th 1915 Sold for scrap September 1921.

HMS Nonsuch. Launched December 8th 1915. Sold for scrap May 1921.

HMS Negro. Launched March 8th 1915. Sunk after colliding with HMS Hoste in the North Sea on 21st December 1916. Depth charges from Hoste exploded and blew out her hull plating, this accident cost many lives.

HMS Nessus. Launched August 24th 1915. Sunk after colliding with HMS Amphitrite in the North Sea on September 8th 1918.

HMS Nepean. Launched January 22nd 1916. One of two Thornycroft specials. Sold for scrap November 1921.

HMS Nereus. Launched February 24th 1916. One of two Thornycroft specials. Sold for scrap November 1921.

HMS Nerissa. Launched February 9th 1916. Sold for scrap November 1921.

HMS Noble. Launched November 25th 1915. Sold for scrap November 1921.

HMS Nizam. Launched April 6th 1916. Sold for scrap May 1921.

HMS Nomad. Launched February 7th 1916. Sunk on May 31st 1916 at Battle of Jutland.

HMS Nonpareil. Launched May 16th 1916. Sold for scrap May 1921.

HMS Norman. Launched March 20th 1916. Sold for scrap May 1921.

HMS Northesk. Launched July 5th 1916. Sold for scrap May 1921.

HMS North Star. Launched November 9th 1916. Sunk on April 23rd 1918 at Zeebrugge.

HMS Nugent. Launched January 23rd 1916. Sold for scrap May 1921.

HMS Obedient. Launched November 6th 1916. Sold for scrap November 1921.

HMS Obdurate. Launched November 21st 1916. Sold for scrap November 1921.

HMS Onslaught. Launched December 4th 1915. Sold for scrap November 1921.

HMS Onslow. Launched February 15th 1916. Sold for scrap October 1921.

HMS Opal. Launched September 11th 1916. Wrecked on January 21st 1918.

HMS Ophelia. Launched October 13th 1916. Sold for scrap November 1921.

HMS Opportune. Launched November 20th 1915. Sold for scrap December 1923.

HMS Oracle. Launched December 23rd 1915. Sold for scrap September 1921.

HMS Orestes. Launched March 21st 1916. Sold for scrap September 1921.

HMS Orford. Launched April 19th 1916. Sold for scrap September 1921.

HMS Orpheus. Launched June 17th 1916. Sold for scrap September 1921.

HMS Octavia. Launched June 21st 1916. Sold for scrap November 1921.

HMS Ossory. Launched October 9th 1915. Sold for scrap November 1921.

HMS Nestor. Launched October 9th 1915. Sunk on May 31st 1916 at Battle of Jutland.

HMS Napier. Launched November 27th 1915. Sold for scrap November 1921.

HMS Narborough. Launched March 2nd 1916. Wrecked on January 12th 1918.

HMS Narwhal. Launched December 30th 1915. Collided with another vessel in 1919 and broken for scrap in 1920.

HMS Nicator. Launched February 3rd 1916. Sold for scrap May 1921.

HMS Norseman. Launced August 15th 1916. Sold for scrap May 1921.

HMS Oberon. Launched September 29th 1916. Sold for scrap May 1921.

HMS Observer. Launched May 1st 1916. Sold for scrap October 1921.

HMS Offa. Launched June 7th 1916. Sold for scrap October 1921.

HMS Orcadia. Launched July 26th 1916. Sold for scrap October 1921.

HMS Oriana. Launched September 23rd 1916. Sold for scrap October 1921.

HMS Oriole. Launched July 31st 1916. Sold for scrap May 1921.

HMS Osiris. Launched September 28th 1916. Sold for scrap May 1921.

HMS Paladin. Launched March 27th 1916. Sold for scrap May 1921.

HMS Parthian. Launched July 3rd 1916. Sold for scrapping November 1921.

HMS Partridge. Launched March 4th 1916. Sunk on December 12th 1917 by gunfire from German destroyers.

HMS Pasley. Launched April 15th 1916. Sold for scrap May 1921.

HMS Patrician. Launched June 5th 1916. Transferred to Canadian Navy in 1920 subsequently scrapped in 1929.

HMS Patriot. Launched April 20th 1916. Transferred to Canadian Navy in 1920 subsequently scrapped in 1929.

HMS Plucky. Launched April 21st 1916. Sold for scrap May 1921.

HMS Portia. Launched August 10th 1916. Sold for scrap May 1921.

HMS Pheasant. Launched October 23rd 1916. Mined on March 1st 1917.

HMS Phoebe. Launched November 20th 1916. Sold for scrap November 1921.

HMS Pigeon. Launched March 3rd 1916. Sold for scrap May 1921.

HMS Plover. Launched March 3rd 1916. Sold for scrap May 1921.

HMS Penn. Launched April 8th 1916. Sold for scrap October 1921.

HMS Peregrine. Launched May 29th 1916. Sold for scrap November 1921.

HMS Pelican. Launched March 18th 1916. Sold for scrap November 1921.

HMS Pellew. Launched May 8th 1916. Sold for scrap May 1921.

HMS Petard. Launched March 24th 1916. Sold for scrap May 1921.

HMS Peyton. Launched May 2nd 1916. Sold for scrap May 1921.

HMS Prince Launched July 26th 1916. Sold for scrap May 1921.

HMS Pylades. Launched October 28th 1916. Sold for scrap May 1921.

HMS Medina. Launched March 8th 1916. Sold for scrap May 1921.

HMS Medway. Launched April 19th 1916. Sold for scrap May 1921.

HMS Rapid. Launched July 15th 1916. Sold for scrap April 1927.

HMS Ready. Launched August 26th 1916. Sold for scrap July 1926.

HMS Relentless. Launched April 15th 1916. Sold for scrap November 1926.

HMS Rival. Information Unknown. It is possible that she might have been cancelled before production was completed, hence no launch or scrapping date.
