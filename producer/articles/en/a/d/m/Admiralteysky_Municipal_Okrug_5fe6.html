Admiralteysky Municipal Okrug (Russian: Адмиралте́йский муниципа́льный о́круг) is a municipal okrug of Admiralteysky District of the federal city of Saint Petersburg, Russia. Municipal okrug's population: 30,533 (2002 Census).

The okrug borders the Neva in the north, New Admiralty Canal and Kryukov Canal in the west, Fontanka in the south, and Voznesensky Prospekt and Moyka in the east.

Places of interest include the Admiralty Board, Saint Isaac's Cathedral, Mariinsky Palace, Decembrists Square, and Mariinsky Theatre.
