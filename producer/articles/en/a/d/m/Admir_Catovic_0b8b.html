Admir Catovic is the youngest player in today's AIK, or Almänna Idrottsklubben, football team. He was born on September 5, 1987, in the city of Gacko in Bosnia-Herzegovina. He weighs in at 71 kilograms and stands 177 centimeters tall. He scored his first goal as senior against Frölunda when he gave the team the lead after nine minutes in a 3-0 victory.

After the 2006 season, he was released as a free transfer by AIK, since his contract was not extended.
