The Admiral Petre Bărbuneanu class frigate is a patrol frigate (corvette by NATO standards), and they are quite old and obsolete, to be replaced by Eustaţiu Sebastian class.

Available information indicates that only 2 of all frigates in this class, have remained in service out of a total of 4 in the Romanian Navy.
