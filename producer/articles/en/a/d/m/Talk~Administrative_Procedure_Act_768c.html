(2/2/05) walshga: I took out "recorded" from the line that read "the APA is recorded under Title 5 of the United States Code" and replaced it with "the text of the APA can be found under Title 5 . . . " Federal statutes, such as the APA, are "codified" in the USC. I want to avoid the legalistic term "codified," so I chose to use the simpler "text . . .can be found." See, e.g., Oates,The Legal Writing Handbook, at 381-2 (1993)(discussing statutes as being "found" in code compilations).
