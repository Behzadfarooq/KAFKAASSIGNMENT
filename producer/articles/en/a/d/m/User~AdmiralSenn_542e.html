AdmiralSenn is one of those crazy nutjob Christians you hear about sometimes. He believes things without having a really good reason to, other than some really bizarre (and very convincing) personal experiences that have led him to believe that there is a God and his son was Jesus Christ, died on a cross, rose three days later, salvation for mankind, and so on.

That being said:

AdmiralSenn owns a 1990 Volvo 740GL. It is white, and is a remarkable vehicle considering that it has nearly 200,000 miles on it. He aspires to someday drop a 5.0 or 5.4 liter Mustang engine and T5 transmission into it. Someday.

AdmiralSenn is also in the process of purchasing a wonderful example of a DeLorean DMC-12, which has been modified by the car's current owner to produce approximately double the factory horsepower rating.

He drives like a maniac without letting anyone else on the road know. You feel as if you're going to die in his car, but nobody else notices anything amiss when he drives. Interesting, no?

He also has a love of reading (and a current addiction to Stephen King) and has lots and lots of hobbies. One of these, modeling and animating in 3D, is going to be his job in a few years.

AdmiralSenn thinks Nicholas Cage is really cool, and he has no reason why.
