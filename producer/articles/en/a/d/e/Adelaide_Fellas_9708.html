The Adelaide Fellas are an Australian Women's Basketball League (WNBL) team based in Adelaide, Australia.

[edit] History

The Adelaide Fellas (formerly Adelaide Lightning) was formed in 1993.

The Adelaide Fellas have won the WNBL Championship four times.

They acquired the rather incongruous moniker in a corporate naming rights agreement in 2005. They will resume use of the "Lightning" moniker in the 2006-07 season.
