Adeleas Namelle

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

An Aes Sedai of the Brown Ajah. A character from the Wheel of Time Series by Robert Jordan. Twin sister to Vandene Namelle. She was a good friend of Moraine Damodred.

She was slain by Careane Fransi of the Black Ajah as she accompanied Elayne towards Caemlyn leaving her twin sister to seek out her killer.
