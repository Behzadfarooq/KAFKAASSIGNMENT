[edit] About ToyoShow

ToyoShow is an Adelaide based car show, for Toyota Enthusiasts. Entrance is available for both Toyota built cars, and 'Toyota aspirated cars', which are cars built by other manufacturers, but fitted with major mechanical components built by Toyota, such as engines and gearboxes. There are many classes of entry catering from everything from stock examples, daily drivers and all out show cars.

The first ToyoShow was held in February in 2006, and attracted approximately 40 entrants to its debut cruise and show and shine.

[edit] History

ToyoShow was started in 2006.

The first year was held at Birdwood Motor Musseum, in South Australia. The Show was held over 2 days, with a cruise on the saturday and a show and shine on the Sunday.

The show was started and organized by Sean Basford, to be run with the help of Celica Car Club of South Australia but due to some organisational problems, the show was later organized by Toyota Car Club.net.

[edit] This year

In 2007 the show will be held from the 2nd of February till the 4th of February.

The ToyoShow Supercruise is to be held on Friday 2nd of February at 18:00.

The Dyno Challenge is to be held on Saturday 3rd of February at 09:00 at BoostWorx.

The Show'n'Shine is to be held on Sunday 4th of February at 09:00 at Wigley Reserve.

The show has expanded to provide both entrants, spectators and sponsors more value, including a shift to Wigley Reserve in Glenelg and the inclusion of a Dyno Day. As always, spectators are free.

[edit] Toyoshow Judging Classes

ORIGINAL up to 1985 This category is for Toyotas that are 20 years or older (up to 1985) and in original or restored to original condition. Allowances will be made for non-standard equipment or minor changes that do not detract from the cars originality such as a stereo fitted to the original location or speakers fitted to original holes.

ORIGINAL 1986 to 2000 The category suits Toyotas built 1986 - 2000 and in stock or near stock condition. Allowances will be made for non-standard equipment or changes that do not detract from the cars stock appearance.

MILLENIUM 2001 on This category suits Toyotas built 2001 or later and are in showroom or near showroom condition. To be eligible for this category the car must be fitted with all standard or optioned equipment as it left the dealership.

STREET This category is for Toyotas fitted with aftermarket accessories like Mags, sports steering wheel, exhaust, stereo etc but still retain the factory engine. Allowances will be made for cars fitted with twin-cam engines provided they were available as a factory option for that model e.g. 18RG in RA23, 16 valve 4AGE in Sprinter etc.

MODIFIED This category suits Toyotas fitted with non-optioned, larger capacity or turbo/ supercharged engines in addition to other major changes to body, suspension and interior.

IMPORT This category was created for cars built and sold overseas.

COMMERCIAL & 4WD This category is for Commercial & 4WD Toyotas eg: Vans, Utes and Trucks.

ASPIRATED This category was created to cater for the increasing number of Toyotas fitted with non-Toyota piston engines or cars of other manufacture fitted with Toyota engines. Examples of Aspirated are: Celica fitted with Chev V8, Corolla fitted with Nissan SR20, Commodore fitted with 1JZGTE or Cortina fitted with 1UZFE.

MATRON OF MODIFIED This is a female only category. The car must be owned & registered in the females name.

SHOW CLASS This category is for hi class Toyotas that are well built and up to show standard.

TOYOCHAMP This category is for the Best of the best from all classes with the highest points in Cruising, Dyno & Show n Shine jugging. Toyochamp is one award we think is the best Toyota of TOYOSHOW. And with TOYOCHAMP there can only be one winner.
