Adelaide Roberts, a well-known pianist, is currently faculty of The Juilliard School Pre-College Division. Her undergraduate degree is from georgian court College, her gradate work from Syracuse University


She has performed in more than 400 recitals and orchestral concerts on one and two pianos with late husband Edgar Roberts, in England, Denmark, Japan, Italy, Mexico, Hawaii, and the U.S A number of her recording have been released by Sony and other labels.
