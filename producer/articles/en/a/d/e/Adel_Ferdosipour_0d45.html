Adel Ferdosipour is an Iranian journalist, football commentator, and television show host.

He is famous for his commentary, despite his unremarkable voice, and his controversial yet popular program, Navad, broadcasted by Channel 3 in Iran. He is sometimes dubbed: The John Motson of Iran. He has a B.Sc and M.Sc. in Industrial Engineering from the Sharif University of Technology. He is also a facutly member in the Industrial Engineering department at his Sharif University of Technology and teaches some English classes.
