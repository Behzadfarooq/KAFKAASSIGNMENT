Ader Jamat is the name of a book in the fictional Myst universe. It was written by Veovis after 16 years of writing, earning him the first Korfah V'jah in almost 200 years. The Fall came shortly after it was accepted into the canon of the Guild of Writers, so this was the last one. Myst: The Book of Ti'ana gives no description of the age, but mentions a creature known as a "Reekoo" that comes from it.
