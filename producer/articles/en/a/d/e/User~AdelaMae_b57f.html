[edit] Greetings from AdelaMae!

(Leave me a message.)

Hello, and welcome to my user page. I enjoy Wikipedia immensely, so I do the best I can to help where I can. I've learned a lot from both reading and editing Wikipedia articles and I want to pass it on.

I'm marginally knowledgeable about philosophy, Greek mythology, and queer studies, moderately knowledgeable about neopaganism and giftedness, and more knowledgeable than most about Hellenic polytheism. For everything else, well, I know how to research.

[edit] Interlanguage Collaboration

I do minor editing on the following Wikipedias:

  • Greek Wikipedia (my userpage)
  • Italian Wikipedia (my userpage)
  • Spanish Wikipedia (my userpage)

I have extremely limited knowledge of Greek and Italian, though I am making an effort to pick up some Greek. I can read Spanish moderately well and am willing to help with Spanish-to-English translations, especially after-translation cleanup.
