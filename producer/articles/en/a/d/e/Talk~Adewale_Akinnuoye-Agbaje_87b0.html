[edit] Doctor Who and Abisola Agbaje

In this week's episode of Doctor Who ("Fear Her"), a young girl named Chloe Webber is played by Abisola Agbaje.

Are Adewale and her related?

I have been thinking exactly the same thing. While I do not know if she is related, in an interview with her:

http://news.bbc.co.uk/cbbcnews/hi/newsid_5090000/newsid_5098400/5098406.stm she says that she would like a part in 'Lost'. Coincidence? SergeantBolt 09:12, 24 June 2006 (UTC)

Do you know how racially insenstive that sounds? What if they were two hispanic actors with the last name of Lopez? Would you naturally assume they are related? --68.103.154.140 03:09, 3 September 2006 (UTC)

I think you are reading way too much into an innocent comment. I would imagine there are very few nigerian actors 'using the name Agbaje' (as apposed to a screen name). the chances of the two being related (with Adewale helping Abisola get into the business) seem pretty reasonable to me. comparing Agbaje to Lopez when talking about last names of actors is just silly.
