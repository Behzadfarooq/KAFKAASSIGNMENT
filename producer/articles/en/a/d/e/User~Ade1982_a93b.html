I am Welsh, from a town called Merthyr Tydfil and have been around for 24 years. I currently hold two Masters degrees in Computer Science - one being an Master of Engineering from the University of Wales, Swansea, and also I am completing a Master of Philosophy at the University of Manchester, where I am currently a student.
