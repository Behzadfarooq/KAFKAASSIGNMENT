Adelbert Mühlschlegel

Adelbert Mühlschlegel (1897–1980) was a prominent German Bahá'í from a Protestant family. He became a Bahá'í in 1920, translated Bahá'í literature and served as a member of the National Spiritual Assembly of Germany.

He was appointed a Hand of the Cause by Shoghi Effendi in February 1952, after which he travelled to visit the Bahá'ís in many countries. He died in Athens, Greece.
