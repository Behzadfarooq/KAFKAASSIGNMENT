[edit] Monster Profiles

  • Unless stated otherwise, each wyvern will have the following attacks: A tail swipe, a charge attack where it runs at the player and sometimes spits fireballs and a fireball attack (which can consist of other elements).


[edit] Returning Monsters (With Videos)

Note: Videos are courtesy of YouTube

Kirin

Online Event only monster. A big white unicorn with the ability to possess electricity at will.

  • Battle Video1 : [[6]]
  • Battle Video2 : [[7]]


Rathalos

A large red wyvern; male. The main wyvern of Monster Hunter, Rathalos is considered a rite-of-passage of upcoming hunters. Spends a lot of time flying, and will fly away at random times, making the hunter go after it. There are Blue and Silver colored variations; the silver version is called Silversoul. Its claws have poison in them.

  • Battle Video : [[8]]


Rathian

Green and brown wyvern; the female version of Rathalos. Prefers to stay on the ground, rather than fly. There are Pink and Gold colored variations; the gold version is called Goldheart. Spits 3 consecutive fireballs.

  • Battle Video1 : [[9]]
  • Battle Video2 : [[10]]


Gravios

An adult Basarios. Has a sleep-sac that makes the hunter fall asleep right under it with its gas attack.

  • Battle Video : [[11]]


Plesioth

Aquatic monster with water power. Also one of the biggest monsters in Monster Hunter. Has a green colored variation. Its breath weapon is a high pressure water jet.

  • Battle Video : [[12]]

Gypcerous

A strange wyvern that acts like the Kut-Ku. Can steal items and blind the player with the crest on its head. Also has a pink-colored variation. Spits poison instead of fire.


Yian Kut-Ku

Small, pink, bird-like wyvern with large ears. Also has a blue variation. Possibly one of the first wyverns that a new hunter will kill, and possibly the wyvern that gets slain the most.

  • Battle Video1 : [[13]]
  • Battle Video2 : [[14]]


Khezu

Cave monster with sickly white fleshy skin. Has the electricity element. Also has a red variation.

  • Battle Video : [[15]]


Fatalis

Online "main boss" in Monster Hunter. Large flying dark dragon with powerful fire blasts.

  • Battle Video : [[16]]


Lao Shan Lung

A giant red dragon with no wings. Also has a blue variation.

  • Intro Video : [[17]]
  • Battle Video : [[18]]

[edit] Returning Monsters (No Video)

Basarios

A juvinile wyvern made of rock. Emits two gas attacks: one that poisons the player, and one that puts the player to sleep. Enjoys the charge attack.

Melynx

A variation of the Felyne, it has black fur, and will try to steal items from hunters that don't carry felvine. Items can be re-obtained by attacking the Felyne, or by going into the Felyne village and searching for one’s items.


Felyne

A small white cat which is peaceful, unless attacked, when it then hits you with its cane and runs at you with small barrel bombs.


Bullfango

A vicious wild boar-type monster that will charge-attack on sight. Bullfango are notorious for annoying most players due to the fact that they tend to come in groups of three or more, and rarely give the player a chance to get up.


Velociprey

A velociraptor-type monster, it is blue and will attack on sight. Variations are Genprey and Ioprey. Genprey have the "stun/paralyze" element, while the ioprey have the "Poison" element. If a killing blow is delivered while they are in the air, they will separate into two pieces and disappear.


Genprey

A certain breed of Velociprey. They are yellow and their claws can stun hunters.


Ioprey

A certain breed of Velociprey. The strongest of all breeds. They are large, and they can spit poison at hunters.


Vespoid

A flying monster similar to a giant wasp, about half the size of a hunter. They also carry the "paralyze" element, but only if they stab the hunter in the back. In most areas, they will return after being killed. When killed, they either explode, or stay in one piece, depending on the amount of damage inflicted, but only can be "harvested" when in one piece.


Hornetaur

Similar to the vespoid, this monster is like a large beetle that crawls slowly, yet will perform quick jump attacks.


Aptonoth

Similar to cows, they are not aggressive, but may run if one of their herd is attacked/killed. They may attack, but it is slow, easy to avoid and does not deal too much damage.


Kelbi

Not aggressive, this animal resembles a deer with two straight horns. They will not leave the area, unlike the aptonoths, but they will run away from hunters. Only the males will attack if provoked, but again, this attack doesn't hurt much, and can be easily avoided.


Velocidrome

A raptor-like monster. The alpha-male of the pack. Also has a silver-colored variation. They are very large with an orange crest on their head.


Gendrome

The alpha-male of the Genprey. They are very large and have 2 crests on the top of their head.


Iodrome

The alpha-male of the Ioprey. They are very large and powerful, with giant crests on the top of their head.
