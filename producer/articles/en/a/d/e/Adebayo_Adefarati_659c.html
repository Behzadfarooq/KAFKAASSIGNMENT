Adebayo Adefarati (February 14, 1931[1] â€“ March 29, 2007) was a Nigerian politician. He was governor of Ondo State from 1999 to 2003, but was defeated when he ran for re-election in the latter year. He was a presidential candidate for the Alliance for Democracy in the April 2007 election,[2] but died in Owo, Ondo State at the age of 76 of an undisclosed ailment a few weeks before the election.[1] He had not been considered a major candidate. His death raised the possibility that the election would be delayed, but a spokesman for the Independent National Electoral Commission said that this would not happen, and that the party could name a replacement candidate.[2]
