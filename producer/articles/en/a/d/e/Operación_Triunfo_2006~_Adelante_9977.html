Operación Triunfo 2006: Adelante
Studio album by Operación Triunfo
Released December 4, 2006 (SPN)
Recorded 2006
Genre Pop

Operación Triunfo 2006: Adelante, is the first album from the 5th Operacion Triunfo Generation in the Telecinco Spanish TV Show. The album was produced for the academy's director Kike Santander and collaborated with Bono, La Guardia, Rosa López, David Bisbal, Diego Torres, Pasión Vega and Sergio Rivero.

  • December 19 the concursant was signed the albums in a diferents locations in Spain
