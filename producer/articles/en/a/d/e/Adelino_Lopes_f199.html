Adelino 'Lino' Lopes (born July 15, 1976 in Bissau) is a Guinea-Bissau footballer and defender for PFC Beroe.


[edit] Career

  • 1995-1998 Varzim S.C.
  • 1998-2003 C.S. Marítimo
  • 2003-2004 Portimonense S.C.
  • 2004-2005 Assyriska Föreningen
  • 2006 PFC Cherno more
  • 2006-present PFC Beroe
