Adelaide Ferreira is a famous Portuguese singer. She was born in Porto de Mós, Portugal on 23 September 1959.

She started as a rock singer with the hit "Baby suicida". After that, she has sung ballads. In 1985, she represented Portugal in the Eurovision Song Contest with the song "Penso em ti (Eu sei)" which came 18th with 9 points.

She is also an actress on television having roles in many portuguese telenovelas.
