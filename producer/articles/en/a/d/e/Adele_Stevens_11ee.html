Adele Stevens, born on July 29, 1969, in Wakefield, Yorkshire, England is a model and erotic actress. She is sometimes credited as Adele Stephens.

She began her career as a Page Three Girl, then as a Ralph Lauren model. From there, Adele progressed to nude modeling and appeared in a wide range of magazines and videos, as listed below.
