Adelaide Remand Centre

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Adelaide Remand Centre
Location: Currie Street Adelaide, South Australia
Status: Operational
Security class: Maximum
Capacity: 247(male only)
Opened: 1986
Closed: still in use
Managed by: South Australian, Department for Correctional Services

The Adelaide Remand Centre is a maximum-security prison facility located in Adelaide, South Australia used primarily to hold prisoners on remand pending trial.
