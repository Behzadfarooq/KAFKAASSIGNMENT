[edit] re:ESRB Rating

"Game has been created before the formation of the ESRB, or the ESRB has chosen not to rate the game for one or more factors." Since there are two cases here (pre-ESRB and ESRB choosing not to rate a game,) and the ESRB never officially chose not to rate games released before they existed, I feel that it would make sense to make a distinction between the two. Thank you for pointing that out though, and just so you know, if you decide to revert, I'll leave it since I see a case for using a uniform NR. Y0u (Y0ur talk page) (Y0ur contributions) 20:21, July 20, 2005 (UTC)

 ratings += '[[Office of Film and Literature Classification (Australia)|OFLC]]: ' + x.oflc.value

if (x.loc1.value == 'United States')
