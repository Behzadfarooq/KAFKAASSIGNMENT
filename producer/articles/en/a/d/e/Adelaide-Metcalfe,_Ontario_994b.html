Adelaide-Metcalfe (2006 population 3,117) is a township in western Ontario, Canada, in the County of Middlesex.

According to the Canada 2006 Census:

* Population: 3,117 (0.3% from 2001)
* Land area: 331.26 km²
* Population density: 9.4 people/km²
* National population rank (Out of 5,008): Ranked 966

* Total private dwellings: 1,017
