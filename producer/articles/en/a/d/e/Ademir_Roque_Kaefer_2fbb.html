Ademir Roque Kaefer (born in Toledo, Paranà State, Brazil, January 6, 1960) was a former brazilian football (soccer) player.

In his career (1978–1995) he played for Toledo, Racing Club (Argentina), Matsubara, Santo André and Cruzeiro. He won four Campeonato Gaúcho (1981, 1982, 1983, 1984), three Campeonato Mineiro (1987, 1990, 1994), one Brazilian Cup (1993) and one Supercopa Sudamericana (1991). With Brazilian Olympic Team he won silver medal at the 1984 Summer Olympics, and with the major team played 5 games in 1988.

In same year was on the roster for the 1988 Summer Olympics, and won another silver medal. Never played in the FIFA World Cup. He retired after a 16 year career.
