Adelaide Arpad

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Adelaide Arpad (c. 1039 - 1061) was a daughter of Andrew I of Hungary and Anastasia Yaroslavna, daughter of Yaroslav the Great, Prince of Kiev. In 1057 she was maried to Vratislav II of Bohemia, and was the mother of Bretislaus II of Bohemia and Judith Premyslid.
