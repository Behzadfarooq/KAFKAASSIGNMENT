Adept

A screenshot of Adept
Developer: Peter Rockai
Latest release: 2.1.1 / 20 juillet 2006
OS: GNU/Linux
Available language(s): mostly English
Use: GUI for APT
License: BSD Licence
Website: http://web.mornfall.net/adept.html

Adept is a GUI to the Advanced Packaging Tool for KDE. Adept is developed by Peter Ročkai and is sponsored by Canonical Ltd. through the Kubuntu project. Current release is 2.1.
