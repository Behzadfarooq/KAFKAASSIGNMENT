The Adelaide United FFSA Federation Cup is a knockout football (soccer) competition played in South Australia. It is run by the Football Federation of South Australia and the makor sponsors are A-League club Adelaide United FC. When South Australian football was run by the South Australian Soccer Federation (SASF) it was simply known as the Federation Cup.

The reigning champions are White City Woodville who defeated Adelaide Raiders to win the 2005 Cup.
