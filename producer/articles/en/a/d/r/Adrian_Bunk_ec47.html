Adrian Bunk

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Adrian Bunk is a Linux Kernel developer. Since August 2006 he is the maintainer of the 2.6.16 Linux Kernel.[1] The 2.6.16 kernel is the base for a stable series (2.6.16.y), which follows development rules similar to the 2.4 stable branch.[2] Adrian Bunk is also a former contributor to the Debian Project, from which he retired in January 2002.[3]
