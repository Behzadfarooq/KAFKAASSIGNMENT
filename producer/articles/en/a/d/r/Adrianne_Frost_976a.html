Adrianne Frost (born October 16, 1972) is an American comedian, author and actress. She is best for her work on Comedy Central's The Daily Show and VH1's Best Week Ever.

She was born in Lincoln, Nebraska. Frost grew up in the state of Louisiana. She attended school at the University of Southwestern Louisiana [1].

In 2000, Frost married Asaf Ronen. In 2002, she became a correspondent on The Daily Show, but left the show later that year. In 2004, she became a panelist on Best Week Ever, on which she still works today. In 2006, she published her first book I Hate Other People's Kids and also divorced Asaf Ronen. She is still working on Best Week Ever and is currently writing her second book.
