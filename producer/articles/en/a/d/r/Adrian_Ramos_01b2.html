Adrian "Adis!" Ramos (born on September 8, 1978) is the author of the award winning webcomic Count Your Sheep, which is hosted on Keenspot. In addition, he is the creator of No Room for Magic, also on Keenspot, as well as The Wisdom of Moo, available on Girlamatic.
