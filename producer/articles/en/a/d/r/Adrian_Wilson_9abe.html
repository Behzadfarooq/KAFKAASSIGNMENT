Adrian Wilson (born on October 12, 1979 in High Point, North Carolina) is a safety for the National Football League's Arizona Cardinals. He was selected by the Cardinals in the 3rd round of the 2001 NFL draft. In his career, he has recorded 404.0 solo tackles and 14 interceptions. During the 2005-2006 season, he set an NFL record for the most sacks ever by a defensive back, with eight.

[edit] College career

Adrian "The Assassin" Wilson attended North Carolina State University. During his career, he posted three interceptions, 248 tackles (eleven for losses), and eleven pass deflections. His 248 tackles rank second in school history for tackles in a career.

[edit] NFL career

In 2004, Wilson openly admitted that he wanted to leave the struggling Cardinal organization. Wilson, in his record breaking season in 2005 did not get an invitation to the pro bowl, this angered many Cardinal faithfuls, because Roy Williams had made it over Wilson, and Williams had been consistently beat by Tight Ends and receivers all season. This year however, Wilson's talents have earned him a trip to the coveted Pro Bowl in Honolulu after he had argubly his best season.

In 2007, a video appeared on Youtube of Adrian jumping over a bar 66 inches above the ground. This provoked a deluge of responses by users of the site.
