Adrian Olah (born April 30, 1981 in Târgu-Mureş) is a Romanian football player, who currently plays for FCU Politehnica Timisoara. He can be a Defender or a Midfielder right.

Olah made his debut in the Divizia A during the 1998/1999 season at FC Naţional where he kept on playing until the summer of 2005, when he moved to Poli as part of the deal which had brought Gigel Coman, Gabriel Cânu, Marius Popa and Gabriel Caramarin to Timişoara earlier that year.
