Adriano Fabiano Rossato (born on 27 August 1977 in Vila Velha) is a Brazilian football defender currently playing for U.D. Leiria of Portugal on loan from Real Sociedad.

Adriano has been linked with a transfer move to English club Leeds United.

He also hold Italian nationality.
