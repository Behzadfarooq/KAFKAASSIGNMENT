Adriana Málková (born 11 October 1976) is a nude model from the Czech Republic. Her modeling has been featured in leading pornographic magazines and Internet websites. Although Adriana has never officially modeled under the stage name Adrian Tyler, she is often mistakenly believed to be American as a result of a website featuring her photos under that stage name.

Málková was born in Prague, where she still resides. She has one younger brother, and can speak Czech, Russian, and English.

She started out in the modeling business on a trip to the United States in 1996 through the request of her then-boyfriend, with whom she recorded her only boy-girl pornographic film performance so far, featured in the 1996 release Sexhibition 3. She also modeled for the AlsScan fetish website. Although she is heterosexual, she was often featured in softcore lesbian photo sets with Monika Hájková. Early in her modeling career Málková decided to continue with softcore modeling and ended her relationship with her boyfriend. She worked almost exclusively for a Dutch agent in the Netherlands until 2003, but since then has moved on to work for model agencies throughout Europe.

Her modeling career has included high-profile adult modeling work such as Hustler (April 1997, the "Perfect Woman" issue), Penthouse in the Netherlands and France, various features for Paul Raymond Publications, J. Stephen Hicks, Suze Randall, Twistys.com, Michael Ancher, Christine le Duc, Carstuckgirls.com and others. Her popularity has increased as she has matured, and she has appeared on many adult websites. Countries she has modeled in include France, Germany, Italy, the Netherlands, Portugal, Spain, Thailand, and the United States.

In addition to her modeling, Málková also works as a non-nude being the superstar Go-Go dancer in Prague nightclubs, as well as being a hair stylist.[citation needed]
