Adrianne Kangas

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Adrianne Irene Kangas (October 5, 1992 - ) is a television actress, known for her role as Linda on Barney & Friends and for numerous commercials. She is also a talented singer, and her singing has been featured on Barney.

Adrianne was born on in Virginia, and made her debut on Barney & Friends when she was 5 years old. She has subsequently performed in commercials and been featured in advertisements for such things as a divorce hotline, JCPenney, Little Tykes, Pizza Hut, and more.

As of 2006, Adrianne is living in Aledo, Texas with her mom, dad, sister Lindsay, and 2 dogs. She attends school in the seventh grade. Her hobbies include playing the flute, singing, listening to her iPod, and chatting on the computer. Her favorite foods include Oreos, marshmallows, spaghetti, or whatever else she's hungry for. Her favorite movie is Phantom of the Opera.
