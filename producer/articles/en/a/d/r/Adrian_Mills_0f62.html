Adrian Mills

Adrian Mills is a British television presenter. He appeared in the 1980s on That's Life! with Esther Rantzen, and in later years was a host on TV Travel Shop.

He is now a regular presenter for the holiday teleshopping channel "Sky Travel Shop", a part of Sky Travel.

In 1982 he appeared as Aris in the Doctor Who story Kinda.
