Adrian Rawlins

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Adrian Rawlins as James Potter in Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone

Adrian Rawlins (born March 27, 1958 in Stoke-on-Trent) is a British actor. He has appeared in several films including Wilbur Wants to Kill Himself, and also has a minor role in the Harry Potter film series as Harry's father, James Potter.
