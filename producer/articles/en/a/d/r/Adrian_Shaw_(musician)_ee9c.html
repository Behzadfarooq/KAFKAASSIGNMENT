Adrian Shaw is a Barnsley-born artist and musician. He is a member of The Teenbeat, The Beale and The Venus Pollution Project.

He appeared on the British Channel 5 TV series Jerry Sadowitz VS The People and on television narrating for programmes. He recently narrated the Channel 4 documentary on the artist David Shrigley. He also organises the music for the Late at The Tate Britain gallery event and has appeared in art galleries and exhibitions over the last 10 years. He has also curated exhibitions.

His music has been played on Radio 1, played live on Resonance FM. His latest album reviewed in The Sunday Times Newspaper (July 2005), receiving 4 out of 5 stars in a review by Stewart Lee. Cartoonist - designed the guidance signs for children that you will find hanging on the walls in the Tate Britain gallery, London.
