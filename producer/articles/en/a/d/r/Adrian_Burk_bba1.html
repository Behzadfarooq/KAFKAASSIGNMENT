Adrian Burk was a quarterback in the NFL. He is one of only five NFL quarterbacks who share the record of seven touchdown passes in one regular season game. Adrian Burk threw seven touchdown pases on October 17 1954 when the Philadelphia Eagles won 49-21 over the Washington Redskins. Three of his touchdowns passes were to Eagles receiver Pete Pihos.
