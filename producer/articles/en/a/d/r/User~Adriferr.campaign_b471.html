[edit] The Departure

It's a cold winter morning. The sun shines bright on the face of the Wall, and has just started creeping into the Northwest end of the courtyard. You are standing there, holding rope and facing the Staute. Some people open their windows, others start carrying pails or lumber from one place to another. Many of them look at you in curiosity, some in resentment.

[edit] Encounter 1 - Fiona, and the Town

The central courtyard, South of the Wall and Statue. If they try to take down the statue, some will stop and look menacing. Fiona "Fi" will come bursting out and confront the PCs. A crowd starts to gather, including Doyle McTann, who is a big pushover. Fiona: resents the party doing anything to her home. Distrusts them. Doyle: Trusts Mauro, paladin. Moreover, is intimidated by them. XP for satisfactory completion (200 XP)

They not only need the blessings, but possibly some gold from the townsfolk. DC 20 +-__ for willing donations. (amount of gold = Diplomacy check * 5).

[edit] Rules
  • Diplomacy Battle. DC 15 for the crowd, +1 per previous failure. Fi has Diplomacy +5.
    • Starting Attitudes: Crowd is Indifferent, Fiona is Unfriendly, Doyle is Friendly.
    • SRD: Diplomacy

[edit] The Journey to Arcanix

It's around _____, but the morning cold is still there. The sky is greying a little. As you set out of the South gates, the townsfolk have slowly formed a solemn farewell party. At the front of the crowd, you see Fiona, arms crossed. Doyle leans against the gate and waves you off. A gust picks up, and most of the crowd is lost from view as the fresh snow is blown across the plains.

They should try to get to Arcanix, with the statue. On the way, bandits (that are aware of the amount of gold and valuables that they are carrying), attack them.

[edit] Encounter 6 - The Student

As you approach the inn's front deck, a robed old man calls " Can I help you? I am ___ ir'Trannick." He says with a wide grin.

He says he is willing to help them, if they would do something for him: get Blue Hyphea from a cave in the southern mountains. Questions:

  • Why not you? I can't be bothered to waste my time on such things, especially now that the Entrance Exams are coming up.
  • Where? Did you see the mountain range to the SW? There.
  • And afterwards? Meet me here, in one week. I will help free your friend.

He is actually a sneaky prospective student, who is trying to turn away some of the competition. He believes that anyone that is stupid enough to be fooled shouldn't be a part of the great Congress.

[edit] General Notes

[edit] Incentives

Improvement: Magic items, reasonably large XP rewards (equal to a standard encounter).

Hard Combat: Insert gratuitous but lively battle against slightly inferior opponents.

Tactics and Planning: Create a dangerous situation that can be circumvented by use fo a clever scheme.

Puzzle Solving: Add a puzzle.

Favored Role: Tailored scenario.

Coolness/Stoicism: NPC awe.

Story: Insert a scene that advances an ongoing storyline.

Psychodrama: Introduce a character for the PC to interact with, one echoing the character's inner struggle.

Irresponsibility:Provide a buffoonish authority figure to harmlessly bedevil, or an impressive structure to spectacularly demolish.

Setting Exploration: Insert a travelogue moment.

Outliers: No need to do much.

Lurkers: Let them contribute from the shadows.
