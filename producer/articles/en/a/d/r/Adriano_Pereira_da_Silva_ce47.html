Adriano Pereira da Silva, known as Adriano Pereira (born 3 April 1982 in Salvador de Bahia) is a Brazilian football defender.

[edit] Teams and clubs

  • 2001-2004: Grêmio
  • 2004: Palermo 1 game
  • 2005-present: Atalanta co-ownership with Palermo
