Adrian Moore (composer)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Adrian Moore is an electroacoustic music composer born January 21, 1969 in Nottingham, UK, and currently living in Sheffield, UK.

[edit] Recordings

  • Rêve de l'aube (empreintes DIGITALes, IMED 0684, 2006)
  • Traces (empreintes DIGITALes, IMED 0053, 2000)
