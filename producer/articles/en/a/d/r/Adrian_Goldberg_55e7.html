Adrian Goldberg

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Adrian Goldberg (born in Birmingham) was a BBC journalist and presenter of the Breakfast Show on BBC Radio WM. He also presented The Politics Show on BBC TV for the Midlands region, and was a reporter on the BBC TV consumer programme Watchdog.

Adrian is now editor of The Stirrer and has made a film about disquiet in the world of football - Manchester DisUnited.[1] Other film projects are planned in the future.

Goldberg has a degree in English from Birmingham University. He is a supporter of West Bromwich Albion.[1]
