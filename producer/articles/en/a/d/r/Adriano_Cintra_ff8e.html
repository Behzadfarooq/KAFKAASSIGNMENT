Adriano Ferreira Cintra (São Paulo, May 21, 1972) is a Brazilian multi-instrumentist and producer of Portuguese, caboclo and native Brazilian stock, also known as a C-list underground and club celebrity in his hometown. He was guitarist and singer for the garage rock power trio Thee Butchers' Orchestra, and among his many solo projects are Ultrasom and Caxabaxa. He was also in the band The Books On The Table in his native São Paulo. Cintra is also a composer for fashion shows, and was the recording engineer for Tom Zé's 1998 album Com Defeito de Fabricação.


[edit] Cansei de Ser Sexy

His latest and most famous band is the indie-electro project CSS. In 2003, Adriano formed the group with some friends as a joke. He plays the drums and produces the band, besides eventually playing bass, guitar and singing. CSS signed to indie label Sub Pop in the beginning of 2006 to release their first international album, and toured U.S. and Canada in the same year. Another international tour, scheduled for September 2006, is planned, when CSS is going to open the shows of Ladytron.
