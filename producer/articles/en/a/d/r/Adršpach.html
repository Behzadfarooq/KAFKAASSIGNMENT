Adršpach

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Adršpach Manor House

Adršpach is a village in the Hradec Králové Region of the Czech Republic. It has around 520 inhabitants.

The village consist from administrative parts Dolní Adršpach and Horní Adršpach (Lower and Upper Adršpach). It is located on border with Poland.

The place is known for Adršpach-Teplice Rocks (or Adršpach Rocks, [1]), ruins of Adršpach Castle (Adršpašský hrad) and Adršpach Manor House (Adršpašský zámek).
