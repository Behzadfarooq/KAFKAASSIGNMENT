Adria Montgomery-Klein and Natalie Montgomery-Carroll

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Adria Montgomery-Klein and Natalie Montgomery-Carroll (born June 24, 1974 in Birmingham, Alabama)[1][2] are American twin sisters, who are fitness competitors, fitness trainers, professional models, and reality TV contestants. They gained widespread national exposure on Big Brother 5, a hit reality TV show airing on CBS in 2004.

[edit] Early life

The twins attended Midfield High School, where they played softball.[2] After graduating high school in 1992, they went to Troy State University in Troy, Alabama, where they each received a Bachelor of Science degree in Computer Information Science and Minors of Business and American Sign Language.[2] They continued playing softball during college.[2] After graduating they both went to work at Electronic Data Systems.[2] While still working for EDS, they began participating in fitness competitions.[2]

Adria married Lenny Klein in 1998.[2] Natalie married Breck Carroll in 2000.[2]

The twins' first significant success as competitors was in 1998, when Natalie placed first and Adria placed second in the Ms. Fitness Kentucky event.[2] They quit EDS to focus entirely on fitness training in 2000.[2]

[edit] Fitness competition and training

The twins on Go For It! TV

Starting in 2000, the twins signed up with HealthSouth Corporation, to go on a "Roadshow Nationwide Tour" called "Go For It!", teaching fitness and nutrition to people, particularly middle school students.[2] Along with this was a TV show for students called Go For It! TV, which they appeared on as competition coaches.[2] After doing "Go For It!" for a three years (the TV show for two), they left that, and continued with fitness competitions. [2]

The twins have since opened the Fitness Twins Elite Training Studio. [2]

[edit] Big Brother 5

In 2004, the twins appeared on Big Brother 5 reality TV show, as a single person named "Adria Okins".[3] In the show, a group of contestants compete against one another, as one person is voted out each week, by fellow contestants. The fact "Adria Okins" was really two different people, was kept secret to fellow contestants for five weeks. [3] This was done, as part of the season's theme of "Do Not Assume (DNA)". Once the secret was revealed, they competed openly as two separate people.[4] However, the negative feelings towards being deceived in addition to the two being perceived as an aligned unit rather than two separate players helped cause the two to be evicted not long after, with Natalie being the first twin removed, and Adria next. Six other contestants remained after their eviction.
