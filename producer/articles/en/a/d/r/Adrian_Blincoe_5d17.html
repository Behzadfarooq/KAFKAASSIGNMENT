Adrian Blincoe (born 4 November 1979) is a New Zealand middle distance runner who specializes in the 1500 metres.

[edit] Achievements

Year Tournament Venue Result Extra
2003 World Athletics Final Monte Carlo, Monaco 10th 1500 m
2006 Commonwealth Games Melbourne, Australia 10th 1500 m
World Cup Athens, Greece 8th 5000 m

[edit] Personal bests

  • 800 metres - 1:48.69 min (2004)
  • 1500 metres - 3:35.50 min (2005)
  • 3000 metres - 7:46.49 min (2005)
  • 5000 metres - 13:21.73 min (2005)
