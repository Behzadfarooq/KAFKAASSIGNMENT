Adrian Francis Branch (born November 17, 1963, in Washington, D.C.) is an American former professional basketball player. He played in the NBA as well as in Australia for the NBL's Canberra Cannons.

In 2004 he became a television color analyst for the NBA's Charlotte Bobcats games.[1]
