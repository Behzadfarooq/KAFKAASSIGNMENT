Born January 22 in Hollywood, CA, is currently a sophomore at Westchester Senior High School. Adrian has multiple interests, among his main interests are music (both listening and singing), wrestling (both watching it and doing it), and shopping. After leaving school, Adrian works at A Child's Place, a daycare center located in Hawthorne, CA after school, where he is an Executive Administrator.

[edit] Background

Adrian was born in Hollywood, CA at Kaiser Sunset Hospital on January 22 at 7:32 PM to mother Verna Mae Latchman (now Latchman-Gardner) and father Calvin Denton. Since birth, Adrian has lived in many different cities in California, from Los Angeles to Inglewood to San Francisco to Hawthorne and even New York City.

Many people do not know Adrian's heritage and often comment on him about not being "black" enough. This is not true however, since Adrian is NOT ONLY BLACK! Adrian is more Hispanic than anything else, having four different Latin bloodlines (all of which are on his mother's side, of course). He is 20% Mexican, 20% Spanish, 20% Honduran, 20% Belizean, 10% Black, 5% Indian (yes from India), and 5% White. While it may not be known commonly, Adrian's native language is Spanish, and he is fluent in it, though he prefers to speak in English.

[edit] Music

As do many teenagers, Adrian has a major interest in music.

"Music is part of who I am. I can define myself through the music I listen to. And, what I really like about music is that there is something for whatever mood you are in. For example, if you are having Love problems (or are in love) you could listen to "Be Without You" by Mary J. Blige. There is so much variety and I think that it's a shame that people go around saying that Rock or Hip-Hop isn't music, because that is not true. In fact, when you get down to it, rock is really the only real music these days. They use REAL instruments not just beats, so they can actually play live! It just depends on your personal preference, I guess." -Adrian speaks on music.

Adrian's main playlist includes artists such as Zebrahead, The All-American Rejects, The Pussycat Dolls, Papa Roach, Drowning Pool, The Jonas Brothers, Evanescence, and Shakira. He does, however, frequently venture outside of this playlist, always looking to broaden his interests.

[edit] Wrestling

Adrian is a 'HUGE fan of Professional Wrestling. His favorite wrestlers include (in order of preference) Christian Cage, Edge, The Undertaker, Rey Mysterio, [[Phil Brooks|CM Punk], Kane and Shawn Michaels. As far as his preference between WWE and TNA, Adrian says that he has no personal preference, stating that the are both great promotions and that they both have a lot to offer in their own ways.

[edit] ACP Enterprises

In May of 2005, Adrian's mother opened a daycare center, A Child's Place in Hawthorne, under ACP Enterprises as her corporation name. Adrian is currently the Executive Assistant and the heir to the company, as of June 2005.
