Adrian Mark Sanders (born 25 April 1959, Paignton) is a politician in the United Kingdom. He is Liberal Democrat Member of Parliament for Torbay in Devon, and was first elected in 1997, winning with a majority of 12, beating Conservative Rupert Allason.

Sanders was a Torbay Borough councillor 1984-86.
