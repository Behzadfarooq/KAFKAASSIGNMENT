Adrienne Moreau (born 5 July 1941 in Trenton, New Jersey) is an French-American model who is best known for being Playboy magazine's Playmate of the Month for its March 1963 issue. Her centerfold was photographed by Frank Eck.

Adrienne attended Rutgers University for two years, and later worked as a 'gal Friday' for an executive at a New York City advertising agency. She went into modeling and also served as an interpreter at that city's World's Fair in 1964-65.
