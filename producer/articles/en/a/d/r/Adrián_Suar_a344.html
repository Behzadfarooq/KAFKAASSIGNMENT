Adrián Kirzner Schwartz, better known as Adrián Suar (born March 25, 1968) is an Argentine former actor, and a current media producer and businessman, better known as the programme director of Canal 13.

Even though he was born in New York City to Argentine parents, they moved back to Argentina in 1970.

At the early age of 13 started working in the TV series Pelito.

He then worked in other TV shows such as La banda del Golden Rocket, Poliladron, Por el Nombre de Dios, 22, El Loco, and Sin Código.

And films such as Comodines, Cohen vs Rossi, Apariencias, El hijo de la novia, El día que me amen and 18-J.

Together with Fernando Blanco, he created in 1994 Pol-Ka, one of the three most important TV producing companies in Argentina, with shows that have been exported to other countries.

Among the series and films produced by Pol-Ka are Por el Nombre de Dios, Comodines, El Hijo de la Novia and Luna de Avellaneda.

He married, and later divorced actress Araceli González, with whom he had a child, Tomás.
