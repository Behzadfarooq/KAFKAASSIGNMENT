Adrien-Maurice-Victurnien-Mathieu, 8th duc de Noailles

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Adrien-Maurice-Victurnien-Mathieu, 8th duc de Noailles (22 September 1869 – 23 October 1953), son of Jules-Charles-Victurnien, 7th duc de Noailles, acceded to the title of duc de Noailles on his father's death in 1895. He was succeeded by his nephew, François, 9th duc de Noailles.
