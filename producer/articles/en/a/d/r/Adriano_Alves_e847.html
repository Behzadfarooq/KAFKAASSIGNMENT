Adriano Alves (Persian: آدریانو آلوز‎ , born January 5, 1977 in Brazil) is a Brazilian footballer currentley playing for Mes Kerman in Iran's Premier Football League. He acquired Iranian citizenship in October 2004.[citation needed] Alves was born to a Brazilian father and a Brazilian mother of Lebanese origin.

[edit] Club career

Alves played for Esteghlal Ahvaz F.C. for four years. In 2006, he was transferred to the newly promoted Iranian Premier League side Mes Kerman F.C. in which he has played a crucial role in the Persian Gulf Cup (2006-2007) season.
