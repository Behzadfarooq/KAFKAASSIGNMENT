Adriano
Gender: Male
Origin: Brazilian, Portuguese and Italian

Adriano is a popular Brazilian, Portuguese and Italian given name, the equivalent in those cultures of Adrian.

[edit] People with the given name Adriano

  • Adriano Banchieri, an Italian composer, music theorist, organist and poet
  • Adriano Bernareggi,an Italian bishop of Roman Catholic church
  • Adriano Castellesi, an Italian cardinal and writer
  • Adriano Celentano, an Italian entertainer
  • Adriano Correia de Oliveira, Portuguese singer and composer
  • Adriano Espaillat, Dominican-American politician
  • Adriano Olivetti, Italian entrepreneur
  • Adriano Panatta, Italian former professional tennis player
  • Adriano Sofri, Italian politician and journalist
  • Adriano Visconti, Italian air force major
  • Adrian Willaert, a composer, who in sources was sometimes referred to simply as "Adriano". [1]

[edit] Brazilian footballers

  • Adriano Leite Ribeiro (born 1982), striker for Inter Milan and the Brazil national football team
  • Adriano Correia Claro (born 1984), left-sided defender for Sevilla FC, Brazil internationals in 2000s
  • Carlos Adriano de Souza Vieira (born 1977), midfielder for Inter Porto Alegre, once capped for Brazil in Confederations Cup 2003
  • Adriano Pereira da Silva (born 1982), defender
  • Luiz Adriano de Souza da Silva (born 1987), striker
  • Adriano Duarte Mansur da Silva (born 1984), central defender for Nantes
  • Adriano Fabiano Rossato, defender
  • Adriano Ferreira Pinto , shirt name Ferreira Pinto, striker
  • Adriano Pimenta (born 1982), midfielder, Champions League 2005-06 player
  • Adriano Vieira Louzada (born 1979), FC Porto footballer
