"Adrift" is an episode of the science fiction television series Stargate Atlantis. This will be the fourth-season premiere. [1]

Spoiler warning: Plot and/or ending details follow.

[edit] Plot

After escaping into hyperspace from a Replicator attack on Lantea, Atlantis' stardrive shuts down without warning or reason. With only 24 hours of power left the cityship drifts in space out of contact with Earth and the Apollo. With no way to realign their Stargate and no idea of what their position is, the team must once again save Atlantis (and themselves) from certain doom.
