Adrian Fisher

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

For the U.S. Lawyer and Diplomat see Adrian S. Fisher.

Adrian Fisher is internationally recognised as a leading maze designer. His mazes can be found in all corners of the globe.

The Adrian Fisher Mazes Ltd team based in Dorset, England, has earned six entries in the Guinness Book of Records, won twelve design and export awards, and created over 500 mazes in 23 countries.
