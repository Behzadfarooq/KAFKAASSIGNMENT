Adriana Sabrina Diaz (born 1985) is a beauty queen from The Bronx, New York who has competed in the Miss USA pageant.

Diaz won the Miss New York USA 2006 title in a state pageant held in late 2005. She competed for the title of Miss USA in Baltimore, MD on April 21, 2006, but failed to place. The pageant was won by Tara Conner of Kentucky.

Diaz is no stranger to pageants, as won the title Miss New York Teen USA 2003. She competed at Miss Teen USA 2003, held at Palm Springs, California, but did not place. That pageant was won by Tami Farrell.

Diaz graduated from Stuyvesant High School in 2002 and pursued a Bachelor of Arts degree from Princeton University, majoring in Public and International Affairs. In 2004 she spent a semester studying in Paris, France. Diaz had previously wished to attend medical school, and was accepted into the Mount Sinai School of Medicine Early Acceptance Program, but turned down the offer.

Her interests include playing the violin, playing tennis, soccer, acting, dance and yoga.
