Adrian Zackheim

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Adrian Zackheim is an American business book editor. Currently he is at Portfolio, and heads Sentinel HC a division of Penguin Books.

At Portfolio, Zackheim has edited such titles as Purple Cow: Transform Your Business by Being Remarkable; Trading Up: The Power of Mass Luxury Brands; and First In, Last Out: Leadership Lessons from the New York Fire Department.

Zackheim is also publisher of Sentinel, a conservative imprint within Penguin. There, he published The Truth About Hillary, a controversial biography about Senator and former First Lady Hillary Clinton.

[edit] Other Noted Works

Previously, Zackheim was with Harper Collins, William Morrow, Doubleday, and St. Martin's Press. He has edited many best sellers including Good to Great by Jim Collins, Die Broke by Stephen Pollan and Mark Levine, The Dilbert Principle by Scott Adams, The HP Way by David Packard, Head to Head by Lester Thurow, and Swim with the Sharks without being Eaten Alive by Harvey Mackay.
