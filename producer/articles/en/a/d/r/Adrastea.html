Adrastea may refer to:

  • Adrasteia, daughter of Melisseus, King of Helicon in western Boeotia. She and her sister Ide (Ida) were the nurses of Zeus.
  • Adrasteia, goddess of necessity, and daughter of Zeus (Jupiter) and Ananke.
  • Adrasteia, a mountain goddess worshipped in Phrygia
  • Adrastea (moon), the second of Jupiter's known moons
  • Adrastea was the informal name borne by Jupiter XII (now Ananke) until 1975
  • 239 Adrastea, an asteroid
