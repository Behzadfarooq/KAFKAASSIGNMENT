Adrien Beard is an American voice actor. He can currently be heard on the animated cartoon TV series South Park. Adrien is the voice of Token Black on South Park. In addition to being a voice actor, Adrien Beard also works as the art director and lead storyboarder on the show. South Park creator Trey Parker has said that Beard voices Token because he is the only black guy in the building while needed during Cartman's Silly Hate Crime 2000 as neither Parker or Matt Stone could do it.[citation needed]
