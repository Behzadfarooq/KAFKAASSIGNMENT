[edit] Trentino-Alto Adige/Sudtirol

Hello, I would like there to be a serious discussion on this region and how the names have been changed. I am from this region and have both Italian and Austrian/German blood. I care for this region like no other. How the names have been manipulated is offensive. To go to a page and see the Province of Bolzano-Bozen now listed as South Tyrol is getting to the point of being ridiculous. Any basic list of provinces of Italy lists Bolzano-Bozen, Trento, etc. Also, any and all cities in the region have both the Italian and German name listed. Taalo 21:33, 1 October 2006 (UTC)
