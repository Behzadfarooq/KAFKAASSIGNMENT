Adrianne Leon (born March 15, 1986 in San Diego, California) is an Emmy Award-nominated actress and musician who performed two songs for the documentary Hollywood's Magical Island: Catalina (2003), "Everlasting Dream" and "She Heals You". She performed three songs for the "Snow White" DVD. Adrianne is the lead singer of the band Caught Crimson.

She is best known for her role as Brook Lynn Ashton on TV's General Hospital from May 2004 until January 2006 for which she was nominated for a Soap Opera Digest Outstanding Female Newcomer Award in 2005. She was also nominated for a 2005 Daytime Emmy for Outstanding Younger Actress. Prior to joining General Hospital in the role of Brook Lynn she co-wrote and performed many songs for the show (one such song was "Redeem Me").

Since January 18, 2006 Leon has portrayed the role of Colleen Carlton on The Young and the Restless, taking over from actress Lyndsy Fonseca.
