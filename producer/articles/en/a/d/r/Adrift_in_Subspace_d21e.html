[edit] Prologue

Space is likened to the sea with its many dangers. Despite many deaths, "Sailors" keep sailing because of the many opportunities for discovery.

[edit] Plot

Spoiler warning: Plot and/or ending details follow.

Gene makes a daring dive into the first checkpoint and avoids the space wave. At the first pitt stop in the race, Heifong V, Gene confronts Harry MacDougall who claims no knowledge of the XGP15A-II or the incident at Farfallus. In the next leg of the race, Aisha plans on attacking the Outlaw Star. Finding her ship weaponless, she settles on subterfuge. Pretending to be stranded in space in only her spacesuit, she is brought onboard. After finally being convinced that Gene knows nothing about the Galactic Leyline, she decides to stay on the Outlaw Star. As they prepare to make a sub ether jump, they are attacked by Harry MacDougall and their FTL drive is damaged. In order to stay in the race, Gene is forced to ride an ether stream, something impossible for a normal ship. They finish the race fourth, but first in the privateer class, and they break even in their deal with Fred Luo.
