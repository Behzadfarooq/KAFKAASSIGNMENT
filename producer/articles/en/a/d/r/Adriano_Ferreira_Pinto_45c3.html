Adriano Ferreira Pinto (born 10 December 1979 in Quinta do Sol) is a Brazilian football striker. Currently plays for Atalanta B.C..

He also played for São José Esporte Clube (1999-2001), S.S. Lanciano (02-04) on Serie C1, Perugia Calcio (04/05) and A.C. Cesena (05/06) on Serie B.
