Adrian Bogdanovich Mikhalchishin (or Mihalcisin or Mihalčišin, born November 18, 1954 in Lvov) is a Ukrainian chess grandmaster now playing for Slovenia. He shared 1st place at Nikolaev tournament in 1983 and was 2nd at Hastings 1985-86.
