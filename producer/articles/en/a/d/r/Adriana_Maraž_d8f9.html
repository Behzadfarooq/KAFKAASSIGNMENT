Adriana Mara탑 (born 1931) is a Slovene artist who studied painting and graphic art at the Academy of Fine Arts (Akademija za likovno umetnost) (ALU) in Ljubljana. She has had many solo exhibitions and has participated in the Graphic Art Bienales in Ljubljana. She won the Grand Prix at the second Biennale of Graphic Art in Freddenkstad.
