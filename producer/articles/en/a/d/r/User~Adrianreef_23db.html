Adrian Reef , born November, 1985 in San Giovanni Rotondo, Italy, is one of the fastest rhythmic guitar players of the world.

Guitarist Adrian Reef received his first guitar on his 15th birthday and it changed is whole life . Adrian began teaching himself through some guitar methods just to have an idea of "how it works" but he later decided that he didn't need them and started studying technics improvising melodic exercises that he later "translated" into compositions . He always loved to compose . He was about 6 years old when he started "composing" little things using a small piano of just two octaves . During his learning curve he started listening METAL and HARD ROCK bands from "Pantera" to "Skid Row" , but the band that changed his life was "Death" . Some of Adrian ? s early influences include : Kiko Loureiro , Paul Gilbert , Steve Vai , Joe Satriani , Chuck Schuldiner , Dimebag Darrel , Shawn Lane plus bands as Sinphony X , Pantera , Skid Row , TNT , Europe , Queen , Sadist , Death , Anthrax , Dimmu Borgir , Testament ,At The Gates , Fates Warning , Dream Theater , Vision Divine , Labyrinth , Angra and TOO many others . There where times when Adrian was pulled in so many different band projects but they all crashed due to the fact that he always loved to play is own compositions instead of making covers . So every project fell in "archive" . Instead he started composing alone using drum loops and recording all the other parts that could be useful in a song , playing bass guitar , keyboard and even violin if you can believe that ...as guitar indeed . Nowadays he is looking for some SERIOUS band that may hire him in making one of his songs and one of his dream reality : playing something that people may love and come to see just to listen to it . By the way ... now he is composing various songs specializig himself in all metal genres ( as you will notice listening to his songs ) , he made a BRUTAL song with the help of a friend of his that made on a text and a "gourgeous" GROWL VOICE . However , someone told : "Power is nothing without Control" . So , taking this in mind , he realized that his technic had to be "upgraded" so he started studying things as sweep picking and two-handed tapping , and he is studying ever , every day and every moment , he is studying them even when you are reading this , cause ( as he would say ) "...is always a joy to play ,to compose and studying some that you love...is one of the most beautiful things that someone can do...cause my guitar is me...and I play myself on "HER"...without ""HER" and without my music...I will have only to die...cause I will have nothing to live for anymore..."

                                                                      (text taken from his BIO)

Many of his songs are available at Adrian Reef's Official Website[1]
