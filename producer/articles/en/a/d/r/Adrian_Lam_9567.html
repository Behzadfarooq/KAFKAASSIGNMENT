Adrian Lam (born 25 August 1971) is an assistant coach with the Cronulla Sharks National Rugby League (NRL) team and former Papua New Guinea rugby league football player.

Lam had a fertile career with 14 State of Origin football matches for the Queensland side, 147 club games for the Sydney Roosters as well as 107 first team games for Wigan Warriors.

He made his debut for the Papua New Guinea national side in 1994 and went on to win 21 test football caps. He captained the Kumuls to the quarter finals in the 2000 Rugby League World Cup.

Adrian Lam also had a "personal" video released to several Adult Shops a few years ago. He did not release this video. It was stolen by persons unknown. In a later statement, Adrian said he regretted ever making the video, and did not intend for it to be released. He also stated that he wouldn't make further comments about the video.
