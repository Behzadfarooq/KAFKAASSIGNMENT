Adrie Koster

Adrie Koster (born November 18, 1954 in Zierikzee, Zeeland) is a former football winger from the Netherlands, who obtained three caps (no goals) for the Netherlands national football team in 1978. He played for Roda JC (1977-1979) and PSV Eindhoven (1979-1983).

He played his last match in the Eredivisie on October 2, 1982, replacing Jurrie Koolhof, and later became a football manager, who started to work as an assistant for FC Eindhoven (1985-1986). Since then he worked for Willem II Tilburg (1986-1991), Roda JC (1991-1993), Helmond Sport (1993-1995), TOP Oss (1995-1997), Excelsior Rotterdam (1997-2003), VVV Venlo (2004-2005), and RKC Waalwijk (2005-2007). At the last club he was fired due to poor perfomances on November 27, 2006.
