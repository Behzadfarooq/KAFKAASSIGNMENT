Adrienne Pierce

Addriene Pierce is a Canadian singer from Vancouver. She has two albums, an iTunes EP, and a single currently released. Her song "Fools Gold" was featured in Grey's Anatomy, and her song "Lost and Found" was featured exclusively in Veronica Mars and was released on the original soundtrack for the series.

Her second album, Faultline, was released on January 30th, 2007.
