Adriana Cataño

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Adriana Cataño is a Hispanic actress and television host. She has acted in Latin soap operas and has hosted programs on Univision and Telefutura.

[edit] Biography

Adriana Cataño was born in Miami, Florida and is of Colombian origin. She studied Broadcast Journalism at Florida International University. She speaks both Spanish and English and has starred in Latin American as well as US Television Programs

[edit] Career

Adriana Cataño has acted in numerous programs. She has starred in Latin soap operas, such as Belinda, Secreto de Amor, Morelia, MariElena, Amor Latino and Cadena Braga. She has also been in American films and TV Shows, such as Bad Boys, Holy Man, and the ABC program Moon Over Miami.

Outside of Television, she has been featured on the front page of various magazines and newspapers, such as FHM, Selecta, Ocean Drive, People, Open Your Eyes, TV y Novelas, TV Notas, MIRA, La Opinion, Men’s Fitness, El Norte, El Reforma, and The Miami Herald.
