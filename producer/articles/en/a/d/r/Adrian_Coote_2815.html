Adrian Coote

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Adrian Coote (b. 30 September 1978 in Belton, nr Great Yarmouth) is a former professional footballer.

Coote, a striker, came through the youth system at Norwich City, for whom he scored 3 goals in 61 appearances. While at Carrow Road, he won full international caps with Northern Ireland.

Coote was unable to establish himself in Norwich's first team and left during the 2001-02 season to join Colchester United. Coote was not a success at Colchester either, and after a loan spell at Bristol Rovers he was released by Colchester shortly after the start of the 2003-04 season.

Since leaving Layer Road, Coote has played for a series of non-league clubs in Norfolk and is currently on the books of Acle Rangers FC, a sunday league side.

Coote's Mother is a cousin of his former manager at Norwich, Bryan Hamilton.
