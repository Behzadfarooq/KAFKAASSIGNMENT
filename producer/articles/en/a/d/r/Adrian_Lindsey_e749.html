Adrian Lindsey was the head coach of the Oklahoma Sooners football program from 1927 to 1931. He compiled a 19-19-6(.500)record, becoming one of only several coaches in NCAA history with a tied record. However, his teams have outscored their opponents 506-435, while he was the Sooners head coach. He also has compiled a mediocre record in Conference play standing at 11-12-2.
