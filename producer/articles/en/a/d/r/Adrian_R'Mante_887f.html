Adrian R'Mante (born February 3, 1978) is an actor best known for his role as Esteban Ramírez on the Disney Channel show The Suite Life of Zack and Cody. R'Mante also occasionally works as a substitute teacher.

[edit] Biography

R'Mante is a native of Florida, born in Tampa. He currently lives in greater Los Angeles metropolitan area. He graduated from the University of Central Florida in 1996, with a bachelor's degree in Theatre Performance. He got his first break as the host for the game show Game Lab in Orlando, Florida. He has also hosted other shows on Nickelodeon and the Discovery Channel.

[edit] Appearances

He is also well known for being a minor character on the once-popular (now cancelled) show, Summerland. He is currently playing Esteban on The Suite Life of Zack and Cody with Cole and Dylan Sprouse. He was also a guest star in the second episode of season six of 24 (TV Series). He starred in a movie with Abigail Spencer called Graduation Night.
