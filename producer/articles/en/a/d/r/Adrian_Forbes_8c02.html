Adrian Forbes (born January 23, 1979 in Greenford, England) is an English professional footballer who currently plays for the Football League One team Blackpool. His position is as a right midfielder.

Forbes began his career at Norwich City. He played under several different managers in his six years at Carrow Road, including Martin O'Neill, Mike Walker and Bruce Rioch.

In the summer of 2001 he moved to Luton in a £60,000 deal. After three seasons at Kenilworth Road, Forbes signed for Swansea City on a free transfer.

Two years later, he was on the move again, this time to Simon Grayson's Blackpool, on another free.

He scored his first goal for Blackpool on March 6, 2007, in a 2-0 win over Bournemouth at Bloomfield Road.[1]
