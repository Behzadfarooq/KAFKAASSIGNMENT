Adrian Sarkissian

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Adrian Sarkissian (born February 13, 1979 in Montevideo) is a Uruguayan footballer of Armenian descent. His position is midfield.

[edit] Career

  • 1997-2001 : CA River Plate
  • 2001-2003 : CD Veracruz
  • 2003-2005 : CA River Plate
  • 2005-present : AS Nancy-Lorraine
