Adie, or Adrienne Camp, n√©e Liesching, (born July 12, 1981) was the lead singer of the europop rock band The Benjamin Gate before the group disbanded in 2003. She briefly contributed to other religious albums, namely her duet with rapper John Reuben featured on Professional Rapper and her background vocals on husband Jeremy Camp's albums Restored and Live Unplugged, after The Benjamin Gate disbanded.

She now goes by "Adie" and released her debut solo album called Don't Wait on September 26, 2006. The album was co-produced by her husband Jeremy Camp.

She just finished touring with husband Jeremy Camp on his Beyond Measure tour.

[edit] Personal life

In December, 2003, Liesching married Jeremy Camp. The couple have two children, Isabella Rose (born September 25, 2004), and Arianne Mae (born April 5, 2006).
