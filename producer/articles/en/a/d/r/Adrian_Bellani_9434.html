Adrian Bellani

Adrian Bellani (also known as Gerardo Celasco, born April 8, 1983 in Miami, Florida) is an American actor.

He was born in Miami of Italian and Salvadoran descent, and as a child moved to El Salvador with his family. He received his schooling at the American School of El Salvador (Escuela Americana), and later returned to the United States to study finance at SMU (Southern Methodist University). He is most recognizable as being the second actor to play the role of Miguel Lopez-Fitzgerald (a role originated in 1999 by Jesse Metcalfe of Desperate Housewives fame) on the NBC daytime drama, Passions.
