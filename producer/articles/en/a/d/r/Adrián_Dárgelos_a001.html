Adrián Dárgelos (Adrián Rodríguez), Argentine actor and singer of the musical group Babasónicos, that was created in 1991 in Lanus. dargelos is known for his stage presence , swagger and his lyrics.

[edit] Discography with Babasónicos

  • Pasto (1992)
  • Trance Zomba (1994)
  • Dopádromo (1996)
  • Babasónica (1997)
  • Miami (1999)
  • Jessico (2001)
  • Infame (2003)
  • Anoche (2005)
