Adriano Correia Claro, best known as Adriano (born October 26, 1984 in Curitiba, Paraná) is a Brazilian football left midfielder or left back, currently playing for Sevilla FC in the Spanish La Liga. He used to play for his hometown club, Coritiba Foot Ball Club. One of few players in the world who is genuinely two-footed, Adriano is therefore also capable of playing on the right flank too.
