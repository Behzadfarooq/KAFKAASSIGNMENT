Adriana Aizemberg

Adriana Aizemberg (born c. 1940s) is an Argentine film and television actress.[1]

She has appeared in film since the year 1967 making her debut in El ABC del amor making some sixteen appearances to date.

She appeared in the popular 2004 Daniel Burman film, El abrazo partido alongside Uruguayan actor Daniel Hendler and more recently in A través de tus ojos in the role of Nilda in 2006.
