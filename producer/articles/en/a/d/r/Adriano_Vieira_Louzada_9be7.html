Adriano Vieira Louzada or Adriano (born in Rio Branco, January 3, 1979) is a Brazilian International who started playing in 1998 for Portuguesa, Palmeiras, CD Nacional and Cruzeiro. He is famous for scoring the game winning goal in the Portuguese Cup final. His current club is the portuguese giants FC Porto.

Adriano rejected a loan offer to Panathinaikos FC in January 3 2007, because he didn't wanted to play in Greek Superleague. Sporting de Braga were interested in Adriano as well, but it looks like FC Porto decided to keep the player in their books.

[edit] Honours

  • Portuguese Championship: 2005/06
  • Cup of Portugal: 2005/06
  • SuperCup Cândido de Oliveira: 2006/07
  • Rio-São Paulo Tournament: 2000
  • Brazilian Champions Cup: 2000
