Adrian Bamforth

Adrian Bamforth is a British comic book artist who has worked for 2000 AD as well as producing work for British small press comics like FutureQuake.

[edit] Bibliography

Comics work includes:

  • Judge Dredd:
    • "Pret-a-porker" (in Zarjaz volume 1 #1, 2001) [1]
    • "Married with juves" (with Gordon Rennie, in 2000 AD #1262, 2001) [2]
  • Judge Anderson: "Not All In The Mind" (with Alan Grant, in Zarjaz volume 1 #2, 2002) [3]
  • Sinister Dexter:
    • "U R Here" (with Dan Abnett, in 2000 AD #1279, 2002) [4]
    • "Narked for Death" (with Dan Abnett, in 2000 AD #1281, 2002) [5]
  • Past Imprefect: "The Red Menace" (with Gordon Rennie and inks: Lee Townsend, in 2000 AD #1318, 2002) [6]
  • "Emigration" (with Arthur Wyatt, in FutureQuake #1, 2003) [7]
  • "Down The Tubes" (with Arthur Wyatt, in FutureQuake #2, 2004) [8]
  • "Outer Space" (with Arthur Wyatt, in FutureQuake #3, 2004) [9]
  • "The Other side" (art and script, in Something Wicked #1, 2006)
  • Tharg's Future Shocks: "Optimal" (with Al Ewing, in 2000 AD #1496, 2006)
  • Tharg's Future Shorts: "Stasis" (art and script, in 2000 AD #1510, 2006)
