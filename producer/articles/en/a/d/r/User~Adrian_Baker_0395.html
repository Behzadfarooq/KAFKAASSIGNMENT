[edit] About me

This is me racing my T20 Suzuki

I'm in my early 40s and am a Physics teacher by trade (currently Head of Physics at Bishop's Stortford College). However, my main interest is motorcycles and I restore, ride and race motorcycles - particularly old classic ones. I'm a lover of I.T. too, and I have combined both of these interests with my website t20suzuki.com. My other main interest is a fascination with the History of Science and Astronomy. Other interests are: Gardening, DIY, Walking in the countryside, wildlife and birds, anything nerdy, some sports (eg bike racing and football, but also ANY sport involving a petrol engine), politics and current affairs and other stuff too.


[edit] Where?

I'm from Coventry in England originally, but have since moved 'darn sarf' to Hertfordshire - in a small village near to Ware.
