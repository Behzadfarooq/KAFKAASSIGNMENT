Adrian Michael Ward (b. July 1, 1982, in Berkeley, California) is an NFL cornerback for the Minnesota Vikings. He played college football for the University of Texas at El Paso, after attending Chabot Coummnity College in Hayward, Ca. Has two brothers, Michael and Jason, and one sister, Erin.
