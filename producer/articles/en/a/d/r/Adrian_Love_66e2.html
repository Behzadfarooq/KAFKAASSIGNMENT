Adrian Love (3 August 1944â€“11 March 1999) was a British radio presenter. He was the son of musician Geoff Love.

He began his career in 1966, working for Radio City. He joined the BBC in 1967 and worked there until 1973 when he moved into commercial radio when he moved to LBC. He later returned to the BBC, appearing on Radio One, Radio Two and Radio Four. He also continued to appear on other stations, including BFBS and Classic FM. His last four years as a presenter were at BBC Southern Counties Radio.

He was gravely injured in a car crash in December 1997 and never completely recovered. He eventually succumbed to a collapsed lung.
