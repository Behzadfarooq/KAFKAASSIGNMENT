Adolfo Ferreira Caminha, more commonly known as Adolfo Caminha, is an Brazilian writer, who was born in Aracati city, state of Ceará, on May 29, 1867. He died in Rio de Janeiro, state of Rio de Janeiro, on January 1, 1897.

[edit] Bibliography

  • Judite, short story, 1887
  • Lágrimas de um crente, short story, 1887
  • A normalista, novel, 1891
  • No país dos ianques, 1894
  • Bom-Crioulo, novel, 1848
  • Cartas literárias, novel, 1895
  • Tentação, novel, 1896
  • Ângelo, novel, unfinished work
  • O emigrado, novel, unfinished work
