Adorf [ˈadɔʁf] is a small town in the Vogtlandkreis in the south-west of the Free State of Saxony, Germany. In Gettengrün, a belonging village, it borders the Czech Republic.

As of february 2007, it has 5,777 inhabitants.

Other belonging villages include Arnsgrün, Remtengrün, Hermsgrün, Rebersreuth, Leubetha, Freiberg and Jugelsburg. Gettengrün is, despite it's minor size devided in Obergettengrün and Untergettengrün.

Adorf is conterminous with Bad Elster, Eichigt, Markneukirchen and Mühlental.
