Adolphus W. Brower House
(U.S. National Register of Historic Places)
The Italianate Adolphus W. Brower House in Sycamore, Illinois.
Location: Sycamore, DeKalb County, Illinois, USA
Coordinates: 41°59′2″N, 88°41′39″W
Built/Founded: 1876[1]
Added to NRHP: February 14, 1979[2]
Governing body: Private ownership

The Adolphus W. Brower House, in Sycamore, Illinois, has been listed on the National Register of Historic Places since Valentine's Day, 1979. The home is located on Illinois Route 64 as it passes through the DeKalb County seat of Sycamore as DeKalb Avenue. The Italianate structure was erected in 1876 by Sycamore merchant Adolphus W. Brower.[1]
