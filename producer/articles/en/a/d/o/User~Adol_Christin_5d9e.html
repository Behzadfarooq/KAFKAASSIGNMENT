Hi.

I'm an 18 year old who was born in Bettendorf, Iowa. Right now, I'm an open major at the University of Iowa in Iowa City. I'm a bit of a hardware geek, listen to music constantly, and am decidedly left-wing; in other words, I'm a pretty bad match for Iowa (but a perfect match for most of the internet!).

In any case, I'll probably wind up focusing on stuff that I'm interested in, namely electronics, music, and games, along with the occasional edits to pages related to things that I'm studying.

Toodles.
