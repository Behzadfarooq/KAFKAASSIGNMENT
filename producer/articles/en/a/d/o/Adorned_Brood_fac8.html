Adorned Brood is a Pagan/Folk/Black/Viking metal band from Neuss, Germany.

[edit] Line-up

[edit] Current line-up

  • Frost - vocals, bass guitar
  • Ingeborg Anna - vocals, flute (1996-present)
  • Benjamin - guitar (2001-present)
  • Thorsten - guitar (2004-present)
  • Tim - drums, piano (1998-present)

[edit] Original members

  • Frost - vocals, bass guitar
  • Oberon - guitar (1993-1997)
  • Pagan - guitar (1993-2001)
  • Ariovist - drums (1993-1998)

[edit] Other members

  • Andreas - guitar (1997-2004)
