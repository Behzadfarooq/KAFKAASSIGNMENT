Adobe Acrobat Connect (formerly Macromedia Breeze) is software used with Microsoft PowerPoint to create information and general presentations, online training materials, web conferencing, and learning modules. Supported by Adobe Flash multimedia interaction is available.

Adobe Acrobat Connect is part of the Adobe Acrobat family.

The former Macromedia Breeze included four applications: Breeze Presenter, Breeze Training, Breeze Meeting, and Breeze Events. Following the acquisition by Adobe, Macromedia Breeze Meeting is now rebranded as Adobe Acrobat Connect Professional, and includes rebranded versions of Breeze Training, Breeze Meeting and Breeze Events. Acrobat Connect Professional features the following in addition to Acrobat Connect:

  • Adobe Connect Training (formerly Breeze Training)
  • Adobe Connect Events (formerly Breeze Events)
  • Adobe Presenter (formerly Breeze Presenter)
  • Unlimited and customizable meeting rooms
  • Multiple meeting rooms per user
  • VoIP
  • Audio integration
  • Meeting recording
  • User management, administration and reporting
  • Polling
  • Central content library
  • Collaboration Builder SDK

[edit] History and marketplace competition

Prior to September 2006 and being added to the Acrobat family of Adobe products, Adobe Connect was Macromedia Breeze. [1] It was renamed after Adobe's purchase of former competitor Macromedia. The following lists the rebranded Breeze products:

  • Breeze is now Adobe Acrobat Connect
  • Breeze Meeting is now Adobe Acrobat Connect Professional
    • Breeze Training is now Adobe Connect Training (part of Adobe Acrobat Connect Professional)
    • Breeze Events is now Adobe Connect Events (part of Adobe Acrobat Connect Professional)
    • Breeze Presenter is now Adobe Presenter (part of Adobe Acrobat Connect Professional)
  • Breeze Server is now Adobe Connect Enterprise Server
  • Breeze Edge Server is now Adobe Connect Edge Server

This product competes with Microsoft Live Meeting and is designed for creating live presentations that can also be replayed "on demand". This product's audience is similar to that of Macromedia Captivate.
