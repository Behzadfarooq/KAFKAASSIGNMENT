Adolf Grabowsky

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Adolf Grabowsky (August 31, 1880, Berlin - August 23, 1969, Arlesheim, Switzerland) was a German political scientist and author of several books about geopolitics and political theory, including "Democracy and Dictatorship" (1949).
