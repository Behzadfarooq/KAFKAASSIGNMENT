[edit] Geography

Adora, West Bank is a Jewish settlement on the West Bank.

[edit] Name and its origin

Adora is a girl's name.

There are many languages that use this name:

English: Short for adorable

French: Means the beloved

Spanish: short for Adoracion

Latin: Means the adored one or much adored [Ibo(Nigerian)]:Derived from 'Adaora' which means first daughter of the people

[edit] Literature

Adora is the title of a romance novel by Beatrice Small
This is a classic novel with all of the romance stereotypes: Adora is a princess who is raised in a convent, married to a sultan, and kidnapped by a pirate. Lots of hot steamy love scenes, intrigue and really evil people coming to a nasty end.

Adora is a town mentioned in the Apocrypha
According to the International Standard Bible Encyclopedia [1]: "a'-dor, a-do'-ra (Adora): In Idumaea, mentioned in Ant, XIII, ix, 1 as one of the cities captured by Hyrcanus, and referred to in..." the first book of Maccabees 13:20.
It is thought to be present Dura, a village about five miles to the W of Hebron.

[edit] Music

The French rock band Indochine have a song with that name in their album Alice et June.

Adora [2] is a Finnish female rock duo.

Adora is also an American metalcore band. [3]

[edit] Information Technology

ADORA is an approach to object-oriented modeling of software (ADORA stands for Analysis and Description of Requirements and Architecture).

[edit] Science

ADORA (Atmospheric Dispersion of Reactive Agents) is an Environmental and Safety offsite Consequence Analysis tool available for use by organizations involved with environmental impact assessments for intentional or accidental discharge of hazardous chemicals that react with air, moisture or each other.

Adora is a potato bred by A.D. Mulder from (Primura x Alcmaria) in Enkhuizen (Netherlands).

[edit] People and characters with the name Adora

Princess Adora is the name of the popular 80s cartoon character also known as She-Ra.

Adora is a character in the Magna Carta Video Game.

Adora is one of the characters in Hans Christian Andersen's The Little Mermaid.

Adora Dupree [4] is an African American actress, writer, director and ordained minister.

Adora is the stage name of a popular drag queen in Miami.

Adora Svitak is a child prodigy [5] known for her poetry and blogs.
