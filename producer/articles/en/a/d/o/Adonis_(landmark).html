Adonis is one of the most famous landmarks in Timog Avenue, Quezon City in the Philippines. It is a gay bar famous for its lively nighttime entertainment. They are also famous for their Philippine holiday specials, most notably the National Heroes' Tribute given during the Philippine Independence Day.
