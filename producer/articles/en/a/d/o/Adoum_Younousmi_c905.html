Adoum Younousmi

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Adoum Younousmi is a Chadian politician who briefly served as interim head of the cabinet of Chad in February 2007.[1] Younousmi became minister of state for infrastructure in August 2005; previously he was minister of public works and transport.[2] When Prime Minister Pascal Yoadimnadji was flown to France for medical treatment on February 21, 2007, Younousmi became head of the cabinet in his absence;[3] Yoadimnadji died on February 23, leaving Younousmi as interim head of the cabinet[1] until Delwa Kassiré Koumakoye became the new prime minister on 26 February 2007.
