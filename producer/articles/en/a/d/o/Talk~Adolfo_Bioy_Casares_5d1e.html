[edit] Odiousness

A recent anon edit without comment changed the title Los que aman, odian (Those Who Love, Hate, 1946) to Los que aman, nunca odian (Those Who Love, Never Hate, 1946). I don't know the work in question, so I don't know whether this is a correction or vandalism. -- Jmabel | Talk 22:24, Oct 28, 2004 (UTC)

Something should be added to the description of The Invention of Morel to mention how the novel was inspired by Bioy's fascination with cinema recordings of Louise Brooks, since the protagonist's fascination with a recording of a woman is the main plotline. -- anon visitor :)
