The Adonis Blue (Polyommatus bellargus) is a butterfly in the family Lycaenidae.

[edit] Habitat

The adonis blue loves to live in grasslands with short vegetation.

[edit] Species decline

The Adonis Blue has been rapidly declining. This has been due to their habitat (grasslands) disappearing or becoming coarse and shady through a lack of grazing. There have been many people trying to get them to reappear.
