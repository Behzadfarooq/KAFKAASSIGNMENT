Adolfo Zamora y Hernandez (1910 - 1987) was a sedevacantist Catholic bishop from Mexico.

Ordained a Roman Catholic priest for the Mercedarians in 1939 by Bishop Pedro Vera y Zuris, he was consecrated a bishop on October 17, 1981 at Toulon, France, by Mgr. Pierre Martin Ngô Đình Thục.
