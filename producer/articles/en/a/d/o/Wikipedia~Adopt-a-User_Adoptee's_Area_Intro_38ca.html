The Adoptee's Area is the section of the Adopt-a-User Program set aside for invaluable information for current or prospective Adoptees (users who have been adopted). Adopters (users who have adopted) should see Wikipedia:Adopt-a-User/Adopter's Area

If you have any immediate or urgent queries please leave a message on the main Adopt-a-User talk page.

Getting Adopted

Joining the Adoption program is easy, just add the template {{Adoptme}} to your user page, and normally within 24 hours an experienced user will come along and offer to adopt you. Alternatively check out the list of Adopters who are actively seeking new Adoptees here; if you find one you like, contact them on their talk page.

Once you have had an adoption offer the {{Adoptme}} will be changed to {{Adoptoffer}}. If you are not happy with the Adoption offer made please replace the {{Adoptme}} on your user page or contact an Adopter who is actively seeking new Adoptees here.
