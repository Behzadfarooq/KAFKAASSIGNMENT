Adolf I of Altena, count of Altena, 1st count von der Mark (born 1194, died 1249), son of Friedrich I of Altena.

He buys the castle Mark in 1198 and becomes count von der Mark (in 1208). He re-unites Altena after the banishment of his cousin Friedrich II of Isenberg.

He married (1st) Lutgardis (possibly a daughter of Gerhard von Looz) and (2nd) before 7 Apr 1210 Irmgard of Geldern (died after 1230). They had issue:
