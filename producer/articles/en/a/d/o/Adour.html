The Adour (Basque: Aturri) is a river in southwestern France, rising in High-Bigorre (Pyrenees) and flowing into the Atlantic Ocean (Bay of Biscay) near Bayonne. It is 335 km long.

[edit] Places along the river

Départements and towns along the river include:

  • Hautes-Pyrénées: Bagnères-de-Bigorre, Tarbes, Maubourguet
  • Gers: Riscle
  • Landes: Aire-sur-l'Adour, Dax, Tarnos
  • Pyrénées-Atlantiques: Bayonne

[edit] Tributaries

Tributaries include:

  • Échez
  • Arros
  • Gave de Pau
  • Nive
