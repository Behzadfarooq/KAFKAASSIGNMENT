Adolfo Bermudez is an American professional wrestler, better known as the pseudo-Native American Dances With Dudley. He used the Moonsault as a finishing move.

[edit] Career

Bermudez's first national exposure was as Dances With Dudley, part of The Dudley Family stable in Extreme Championship Wrestling. He debuted in ECW in 1995, replacing the injured Snot Dudley as the tag team partner of Dudley Dudley. Dudley Dudley claimed that Bermudez was his half-brother, and a son of (the fictitious) Big Daddy Dudley. Dances With Dudley's mother was supposedly a Native American women who Big Daddy had met while visiting a Cheyenne reservation in Oklahoma some years before. Dances With Dudley could only speak Spanish, or sing the song "One little, two little, three little Indians". Bermudez left ECW after growing unhappy at how he was being utilised. The kayfabe explanation for his departure was that he suffered a leg injury, and was brutally attacked by the debuting D-Von Dudley as he was stretchered from the arena. Bermudez later wrestled for the New York Wrestling Connection as Chief Dudley.
