At first, a large number of edits I did were just minor typos here and there, but then the Wiki-bug hit, and I became addicted. For a while, I enjoyed RC patrolling for vandalism, or finding new articles to try to help. I did a lot of work with WikiProject The Simpsons trying to cleanup and organize the mass of Simpsons information here on Wikipedia. I currently only have limited internet access, and so my number of edits have slowed down considerably. I recently have started creating articles for musical albums. As I learn more about Wikipedia, and Wiki markup, I am making larger and larger edits, and am finally starting to be bold.
