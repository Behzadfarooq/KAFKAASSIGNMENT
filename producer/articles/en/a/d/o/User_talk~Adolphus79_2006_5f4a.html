Merging and redirecting

I just noticed that you merged a couple of articles with List of neologisms on The Simpsons and then tagged the old article titles for deletion. Rather than deleting them, it's preferable to simply change them into redirects. There are two main reasons for this; GFDL compliance (the edit history of the merged material gets "left behind" at the old title) and convenience for people performing searches for or linking to the content. Redirects should normally only need to be deleted if they're blatantly inappropriate or useless. Bryan 03:32, 31 May 2006 (UTC)
