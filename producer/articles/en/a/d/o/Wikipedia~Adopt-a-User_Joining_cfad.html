Being adopted is easy and fun. You can add the template {{Adoptme}} to your userpage, which adds you to Category:Wikipedians seeking to be adopted in Adopt-a-user, and before long an experienced user will come along and offer to adopt you. Alternatively check out the list of Adopters who are actively seeking new Adoptees here, if you find one you like, contact them on their talk page.

If you are wishing to adopt a new user, check that you meet the criteria below and then contact one or more of the new users in the seeking adoption category. If there are no users presently seeking adoption, put {{Adopting}} on your user page and add your details to our list of Adopters.

On offering adoption, replace the {{Adoptme}} on the Adoptee's user page with {{Adoptoffer}}, and once adoption is confirmed change to {{Adoptee}} and add {{Adopter}} to your user page. Also, you can welcome your new Adoptee by adding {{subst:Adoptmessage|your username}} or {{AdoptCongrats!}} to their talk page.

If you think that this program can be improved upon, discuss it on the talk page or just go ahead, be bold, and fix it yourself.
