Adolfas Večerskis - born September 4, 1949 (in Kretinga), is Lithuanian movie and stage actor, director and translator.

[edit] Studies

He studied in Lithuanian State Conservatory, 1973 graduated actor studies, 1988 – finished manger studies for theatre director. 1993 Večerskis did study in international New York art institute art management, and 1995 had finished manager studies in America center. 1996 he did receive cerificate at Baltic studies center.


[edit] Career

Began his career with some films in Lithuanian, but later chose to play mainly on the stage.

  • 1973-1991 he perfomed in Lithuanian National Drama Theatre.
  • 1992 Večerskis did open his own theatre - Vaidilos ainiai (1997 renamed to A. Večerskis theatre).
  • 1995-2000 was manager in international project theatre without borders
  • 1996 he was appointed Baltic manager found.
  • 1998 manager of Kretinga theatre festival
  • 1998 he was appointed as Lithuanian National Drama Theatre programs chief.


He is a popular actor in Lithuanian theater. He is also directing plays in Vilnius, in the Lithuanian national drama theater. His directed play by Ray Cooney Run for your Wife is popular play.

[edit] Trivia

He lives in Vilnius, where he is General Director of the Lithuanian national drama theatre. He lives with his family, wife Viola and their junior daughter Juta. Hes older daughter lives in Vilnius too, he has two granddaughters.
