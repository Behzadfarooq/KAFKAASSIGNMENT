{{AdopteeGrad|adopter's user name}} results in:

This user has graduated from the adoption program! Kudos! from [[User:{{{1}}}|{{{1}}}]].




This template is for when an adopted user is "graduated" by an experienced editor. It will automatically keep them in Category:Wikipedians involved with Adopt-a-user as well as add them to Category:Wikipedians who have graduated from Adopt-a-user. Be sure to remove the "Adoptee" user box.

This template is used in the user adoption process.
