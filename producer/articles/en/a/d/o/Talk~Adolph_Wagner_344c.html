[edit] anti-semitism

From the Clark article, Wagner's own statement: "The anti-Semitic tendency of Stoecker and myself, and of those whom we represent, has not proceeded against the Jews as a religious party, but against the evil practices in economic and social life which unfortunately spring in large part from our Jewish fellow-citizens." There are more statements of him in the article, but this will suffice. Intangible 22:07, 1 November 2006 (UTC)

2. you're only source is a 1940 article, that seems to be calling a national socialist a man that died in 1917...

And about calling Wagner a "national socialist," that doesn't seem to be far off. He advocated State Socialism along with nationalism, semitism and imperialism. Intangible 00:38, 2 November 2006 (UTC)
To call Wagner a national socialist is not only really ignorant but also really dumb, because that way you generalize whatit is to be a nazi too much.
