A56 (software)

The correct title of this article is a56. The initial letter is shown capitalized due to technical restrictions.

a56 is an assembler for the Motorola DSP56000 and DSP56001 digital signal processors by Quinn Jensen (email). Version 1.1 is available from an Usenet's alt.sources archive or [1].
