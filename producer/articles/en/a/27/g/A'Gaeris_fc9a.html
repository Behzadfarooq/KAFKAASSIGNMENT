A'Gaeris was a D'ni philosopher, who had once been an important public figure but had fallen into disgrace after breaking D'ni laws. He had been exiled from the upper classes and was forced to live under the low poor of the D'ni society. A'Gaeris grew to hate D'ni and all it stood for.

After the arrival of the human Anna in D'ni, and the subsequent falling out between the former friends Veovis and Aitrus, A'Gaeris saw his chance for revenge. He approached both and played them against each other. The result of this was that Veovis was falsely accused of attempting to Write illegal Books, and was framed for murder. Veovis was imprisoned based on the testimony of Aitrus.

A'Gaeris now used this to make Veovis his accomplice: he helped Veovis escape, and together they planned their revenge on D'ni. After a series of terrorist attacks on the city, they released biological poison gas in the Cavern; a genocidal attack which killed most of the population. Not content with this, A'Gaeris and Veovis Linked diseased corpses into all Ages they could find, killing most of the refugees as well.

However, A'Gaeris' plans were foiled when Veovis refused to Write an Age where he and A'Gaeris could live as Gods. Enraged, A'Gaeris murdered Veovis. Aitrus had caught up with them by now, and managed to lure A'Gaeris into a Death Age, Ederat, but only by Linking there himself, in essence committing suicide.
