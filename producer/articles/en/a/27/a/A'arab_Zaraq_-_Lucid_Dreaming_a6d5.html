A'arab Zaraq - Lucid Dreaming is sixth full-length musical album by Swedish heavy metal band Therion released in year that the band celebrated their tenth anniversary. The record contains a few unused songs from Theli, a few cover songs the band had made, plus a full soundtrack Christofer Johnsson had made independently for the film called ”The Golden Embrace”.
