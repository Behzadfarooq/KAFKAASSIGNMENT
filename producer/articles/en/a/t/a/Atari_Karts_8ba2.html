Atari Karts is a Mario Kart style racing game for Atari Jaguar published by Atari Games and developed by Miracle Designs from 1993 to 1995.[1] The graphics were above average for the time.[2] The game is critised for its bug that causes the player to get stuck in a wall.[3] The game music was composed in 1994 by the tracker musician Fabrice Gillet on an Amiga.[4]
