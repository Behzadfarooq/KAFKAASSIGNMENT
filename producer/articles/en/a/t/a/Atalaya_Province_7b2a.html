Atalaya is a province in the Ucayali Region, in the central Amazon rainforest of Peru. Its capital is the town of Atalaya.

[edit] Political division

The province is divided into four districts (Spanish: distritos, singular: distrito), each of which is headed by a mayor (alcalde). The districts, with their capitals in parenthesis, are:

  • Raymondi (Atalaya)
  • Sepahua (Sepahua)
  • Tahuania (Bolognesi)
  • Yurua (Breu)
