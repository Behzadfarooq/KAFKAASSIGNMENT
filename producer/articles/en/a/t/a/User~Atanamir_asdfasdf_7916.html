[edit] Summary

The Kentucky Revised Statue 177.020(1) [1] [2] provides that the Department of Highways is responsible for the establishment and classification of a State Primary Road System which includes the state primary routes, interstate highways, parkways and toll roads, state secondary routes, rural secondary routes and supplemental roads. These routes are listed below.

[edit] History

Kentucky began a numbered route system in the 1920's. Originally numbered so that odd numbers ran from north to south, increasing to the west and even numbers ran east to west, increasing to the south, this pattern was only followed for KY 1 through KY 100. Routes numbered KY 101 and higher are in sequential order as designated by the Department of Highways.

[edit] Primary Interstate Routes

Interstate 24 Begins at the Illinois border near Paducah, Kentucky and stretches 94 miles (152km) south east to the Tennessee border near Oak Grove, Kentucky.
Interstate 64 Begins at the Indiana border in Louisville, Kentucky and travels 191 miles (308km) east to the West Virginia border near Catlettsburg, Kentucky.
Interstate 65 Begins at the Tennessee border near Franklin, Kentucky and travels 137 miles (222km) north to the Indiana border in Louisville, Kentucky.
Interstate 71 Begins in Louisville, Kentucky and stretches north east 96 miles (154km) into Ohio while converging in Walton, Kentucky with I-75.
Interstate 75 Begins at the Tennessee border near Williamsburg, Kentucky heading north 192 miles (311km) to Covington, Kentucky.

[edit] Secondary Interstate Routes

[edit] Routes from Interstate 24

No spur routes for Interstate 24 in Kentucky.

[edit] Routes from Interstate 64

Interstate 264 Begins west of Louisville, Kentucky at I-64 and loops to the south for 23 miles (37km) northeast of Louisville to I-71.

[edit] Routes from Interstate 65

Interstate 265 Begins southwest of Louisville, Kentucky at Dixie Highway looping around the city to the south for 37 miles (59km) to northeastern Jefferson County, Kentucky at I-71.

[edit] Routes from Interstate 71

No spur routes for Interstate 71 in Kentucky.

[edit] Routes from Interstate 75

Interstate 275 Forming a complete beltway around Cincinnati, Ohio, the Kentucky portion runs from Petersburg, Kentucky in the west to Alexandria, Kentucky in the east. Officially, I-275 begins and ends in Erlanger, Kentucky.
