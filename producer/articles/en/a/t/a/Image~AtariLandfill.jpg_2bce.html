[edit] Fair use in E.T. the Extra-Terrestrial (Atari 2600 video game)

It is believed that this image falls under the blanket of fair use because:

  • It is one of the only known photographs of the Atari landfill;
  • It is needed to give weight to the claim that the newspaper article exists;
  • It is needed to give weight to the claim that the Atari landfill exists;
  • Future pictures of the Atari landfill are an impossibility;
  • The image is of low quality.
