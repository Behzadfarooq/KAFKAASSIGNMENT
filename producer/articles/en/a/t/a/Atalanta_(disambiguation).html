Atalanta is a heroine in Greek mythology.

Atalanta can also refer to:

  • Atalanta (opera), an opera by Handel.
  • Atalanta, a character in Handel's opera Serse
  • Atalanta (island), an island in the Opuntian Gulf in Greece
  • HMS Atalanta, the name of six ships in the British navy
  • Atalanta B.C., an Italian football (soccer) club
  • Armstrong Whitworth Atalanta (A.W. 15), a British airplane of the 1930s manufactured by Armstrong Whitworth
  • Lockheed P-38 Lightning, known as the Lockheed Atalanta in design, a World War II era fighter plane
  • Atalanta, Santa Catarina, a town in Brazil
  • Atalanta (1915 automobile), a British car made from 1915 to 1917.
  • Atalanta (1937 automobile), a British sports car made between 1937 and 1939.
  • Atalanta is a member of the Pantheon, a fictional group from Marvel Comics.
