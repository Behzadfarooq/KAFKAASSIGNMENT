Atari System 2

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Very soon after the introduction of the Atari System 1, the Atari System 2 was introduced. The System 2 platform was used for the games:

  • 720°
  • Paperboy
  • Championship Sprint
  • Super Sprint
  • APB : All Points Bulletin

Probably the most noticable difference between the System 2 and System 1 games was the fact that the System 2 used higher resolution graphics. The video resolution was 512x384 and as such a medium resolution monitor was used (System 1 used low resolution).

The hardware was similar to its predecessor in the fact that it used 2 main circuit boards. In this case it used a "CPU board" and a "Video Board". The EPROMs were split between both boards.

The main CPU was a DEC T11 running at 10mhz.

The sound CPU was a MOS Technology 6502 running at 2.2mhz, and the sound chips used were a Yamaha YM2151 running at approx. 3.57mhz, 2 POKEYs at approx. 1.78mhz and a TMS5220 running at 625khz.
