Atami Station

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Atami Station (熱海駅, -eki) is a train station in Atami, Shizuoka Japan.

[edit] Lines

This station is served by following lines.

  • East Japan Railway Company
    • Tokaido Line
    • Ito Line
  • Central Japan Railway Company
    • Tokaido Shinkansen
    • Tokaido Line
