Ataxin 1 is a protein found in the brain, heart, muscle, and liver. Recent studdies show that Ataxin 1 is also found in the Galbladder, Spleen, and Cornea.

It is associated with a condition known as both olivopontocerebellar atrophy type 1 and spinocerebellar ataxia type 1.
