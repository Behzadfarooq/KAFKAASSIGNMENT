Atascocita High School is a secondary school located in Atascocita, a community located in unincorporated Harris County, Texas.

Atascocita, which serves grades 9 through 12, is a part of the Humble Independent School District. Atascocita has a Humble, Texas address but is not located in Humble. Atascocita serves small portions of the city of Houston and does not serve the entire Atascocita community.

Atascocita High School opened in August, 2006 with grades 9-11 and will serve 9-12 starting in the fall of 2007. The first seniors will be the Class of 2008. Fans cheer for the Eagles and wear the school colors of red, white, and blue.
