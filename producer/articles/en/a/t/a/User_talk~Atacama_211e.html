[edit] Yerba mate

Why do you think yerba máte is the right spelling? In Spanish, the accent is both wrong and unnecessary, as is a palabra grave that ends in 'e'. In Portuguese is mate. In English, it is either mate or maté (see article for details). In French is maté. And FWIW in Guaraní/Avañe'ẽ it's Caä. Let me know, otherwise I'll revert. Regards, User:Ejrrjs says What? 01:12, 22 January 2006 (UTC)

im from chile and there its máte MAHteh not maté m'ahTEH

En Chile, como en el resto de Latinoamérica, valen las mismas reglas gramaticales para el español. Saludos, User:Ejrrjs says What? 01:55, 22 January 2006 (UTC) oye güey, todas las marcas de máte, p.ej. Taragüí y Ñandú lo escriben así.
