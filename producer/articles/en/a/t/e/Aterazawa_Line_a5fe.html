The Aterazawa Line (左沢線, Aterazawa sen?) is a rail line in Japan. Part of the East Japan Railway Company (JR East) system, it runs from Kita-Yamagata Station in Yamagata, Yamagata Prefecture to Aterazawa Station in Ōe, acting as a spur line from nearby Yamagata Station
