Atelje Trag (established 1998 by Marko Perunicic and Nebojsa Arezina) is one of the most superior songwriting/production teams in Serbia, with over 40 published projects for bestselling artists in the region.

Perunicic and Arezina (composers, beatmakers and producers of 187, Jelena Karleuša, Zdravko Čolić, Luna, Djogani, Zana, Keba, D'n'd, Biber, Kerber and many other groups/artists with hundreds of thousands copies sold worldwide), made their talent and creativity the primary choice for many superstars in former Yugoslavia.

After 7 years of collaboration with biggest names in the record industry, TV and Radio stations, advertising agencies and companies in Serbia and it's neighbourhood, an idea of building one of the best equipped, most competitive recording studios in the Balkans appeared in 2005. It almost fully became the reality today. Purchasing the Solid State Logic 5000M (fully customized) console and tons of high end studio equipment (best of the old world meets the best of the new) for their Belgrade based recording/mixing studio, ensured Atelje Trag Group's leading position in the regional music industry. The doors of this facility will be opened in spring 2007.

In 2006, they also started a high end speaker building business under the name "Emmett Brown Technologies", a member of Atelje Trag Group ltd. The first line of their ultimate Recording/Mastering Speakers (based on new, revolutionary modification methods) will show up in Europe during spring 2007. The first 2 pairs are already installed at their Belgrade based recording studio.
