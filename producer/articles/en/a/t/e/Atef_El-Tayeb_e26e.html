Atef El-Tayeb (Arabic:عاطف الطيب) (born 1947-June 23, 1995) was an Egyptian film director[1][2].

Alternative transliterations of his name are: Atef Al-Tayeb, Attef El Taieb and Atef E-Taieb[2].

[edit] Filmograpy

El-Tayeb has directed a number of films, many of which have been translated into English.[2]

[edit] As director

  1. Leila Sakhina (A Hot Night) (1994)
  2. Did el Hokouma (Against the Government) (1992)
  3. Nagi El-Ali (1991)
  4. El Heroob (Escape) (1988)
  5. El Zamar (The Piper) (1985)
  6. Al Hob Fawk Habadet al Haram (Love on the Pyramids Plateau) (1984)
  7. The Cell (1983)
  8. Sawak al-utubis (Bus Driver)(1982)

[edit] As assistant director

  1. Gallipoli (1981) (assistant director for Egyptian version)
  2. The Awakening (1980)
