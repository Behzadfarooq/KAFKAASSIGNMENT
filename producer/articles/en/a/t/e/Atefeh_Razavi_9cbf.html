Atefeh Razavi (Persian: عاطفه رضوی ‎ , born 1967 in Tehran, Iran) is an Iranian actress and make-up artist.

[edit] Filmography (as an actress)

  • An Soo-ye Atash (Across the Fire), 1986
  • Zir-e Bamha-ye Shahr, 1989
  • Nargess, 1992
  • Yek Mard, Yek Khers (A Man, A Bear), 1993
  • Zinat, 1993
  • Chehreh, 1995
  • Nejat Yaftegan (The Rescued), 1995
  • Banoo-ye Ordibehesht (The May Lady), 1997
  • Stories of Kish (The Greek Ship episode), 1998
  • Dance of Death, 2000
  • Gahi Be Aseman Negah Kon, 2002
