At Home With Love

At Home with Love is the name of a 2006 television programme shown in Hong Kong. It was one of the last series to be broadcasted before the TVB 39th Anniversary Awards.

[edit] Synopsis

Zhong Bong(Chung King Fai) left his real estate business [Trustworthy Property] to his son Zhong Chi Leung(Lawrence Ng). The business has been well established in the area. Through Bong, Ling (Yoyo Mung) becomes a real estate agent working for Leung and they soon fall in love and becomes his girlfriend. The business has been doing well until a large competiting firm enters their area, attempting to force them out of business. Leung soon faces many problems: his business, his health, his relationship with his father, and his girlfriend..will he be able to make it through?
