At Your Best (You Are Love) was originally a radio hit by The Isley Brothers in 1976.


  • Ronald Isley: lead and background vocals
  • O'Kelly Isley, Jr. and Rudolph Isley: background vocals
  • Ernie Isley: guitar, drums
  • Marvin Isley: bass
  • Chris Jasper: piano, keyboards, synthesizers
  • Produced, written, composed and arranged by The Isley Brothers and Chris Jasper

Eighteen years later, the song was revived by teen singer Aaliyah, and became a charted hit reaching number six on the pop singles chart and number two on the R&B singles chart though reaching number twenty-seven in the UK pop charts. In of all the artists who covered the song, her version was the most successful.

[edit] Remix

The song became a hit based on a remix version Aaliyah cut with R. Kelly featured on the song chanting this is for the steppers and earlier saying, 1-2, checker, baby, lemme know what's up before Aaliyah began singing.

[edit] Chart

It became another Aaliyah successful hit.Peaking number six on Billboard Hot 100, number four in ARC Weekly Top 40 for two weeks and number two on Hot R&B/Hip-Hop Songs.It was unsuccessful outside the US, peaking number twenty-seven on UK Singles Chart, number fifty-six on ARIA Australia and number thirty on Irish Singles Chart.
