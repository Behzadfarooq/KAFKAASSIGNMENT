At full power! Burn Choji (Japanese: パワー全開！燃えろチョウジ, - Pawā zenkai! Moero Chōji) is episode 113 of the anime series Naruto. The episode's storyline is from the Sasuke Retrieval manga arc.

[edit] Plot

After breaking free of Jirobo’s trap, Team Shikamaru reverts to type, with Naruto and Kiba making separate, failed attacks on the Sound ninja. Jirobo uses his Earth Release: Earth Flow Rampart attack, but Choji blocks it with his Multi-Size technique. The team uses the ensuing dust cloud to cover a tactical retreat.

Shikamaru proposes that the team split into two groups; one to fight Jirobo and one to go after Sasuke. Shikamaru and Naruto will stay behind while the rest, under Neji’s command, will go on. However, Jirobo easily evades Shikamaru’s Shadow Imitation technique, and counters with Earth Release: Earth Mausoleum Dumpling. After some back-and-forth with Jirobo, Choji decides to override Shikamaru’s decision and take on Jirobo by himself. He gives the team his supply of Soldier Pills, then reveals the Three Colored Pills (三色の丸薬, Sanshoku no Ganyaku?). The pills, colored green (spinach), yellow (curry), and red (pepper), in order of increasing potency, were designed by his clan, the Akimichi. They will each give Choji a temporary but extreme chakra boost. He takes the green pill and stops the charging Jirobo in his tracks.

Reluctantly, the team leaves Choji and continues the pursuit of Sasuke. Shikamaru distributes Soldier Pills to everyone, while thinking of Choji and the harmful side-effects of the special pills.

One side-effect quickly makes its’ presence known in the form of extreme pain. But Choji battles through, using Spiked Human Bullet Tank to attack Jirobo, even breaking through the Sound ninja’s Earth Release: Earth Shore Return technique. Jirobo releases his Curse-Seal to Level One, sending Choji flying with a powerful palm strike.

The remaining members of Team Shikamaru express their doubts as to Choji’s ability to take on the powerful Sound ninja, but Shikamaru, both as Choji’s teammate and childhood friend, knows that Choji lacks only self-confidence, not talent.

Despite taking a pounding from Jirobo, Choji fights back and takes the yellow pill. He quickly powers up and displays his Partial Multi-Size: One Arm technique. End of episode.
