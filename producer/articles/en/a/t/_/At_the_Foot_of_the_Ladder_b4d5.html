At the Foot of the Ladder was a 1912 American silent short film romantic drama written by Lloyd Lonergan. The film starred Mignon Anderson, William Garwood, Carey L. Hastings, Harry Chamberlain and Riley Chamberlain.

The film starred William Garwood

Mignon Anderson played the lead role
