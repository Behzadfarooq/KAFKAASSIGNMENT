At KROQ is a CD-single by the Smiths frontman Morrissey, released on September 18, 1991.

All songs were recorded in 1991 visiting the L.A. radio station KROQ when Morrissey was touring with his second album Kill Uncle.

[edit] Track listing

  1. "There's A Place In Hell For Me And My Friends"
  2. "My Love Life"
  3. "Sing Your Life"
