At Eternity's Gate
Vincent van Gogh, 1890
Oil on canvas
80 × 64 cm
Kröller-Müller Museum, Otterlo

At Eternity's Gate is painting by Vincent van Gogh, executed in 1890 in Saint-Rémy after his own lithograph of 1882.[1]

  1. ^ The title On the Threshold of Eternity is probably a unhappy re-translation from the German.
