At The Speed of Life is rapper Xzibit's debut album. The album peaked at number 74 on the Billboard 200 music chart. The album has sold over 100,000 copies to date. The single Paparazzi was featured on the Tony Hawk's Pro Skater 3 video game soundtrack. An instrumental version of the same song was also featured during the final scene of Sopranos sixth episode "Pax Soprana".
