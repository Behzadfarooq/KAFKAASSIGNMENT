At the Zoo (Married... with Children episode)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

" At The Zoo" is the 10th episode of season four of the television sitcom Married... with Children.

  • First Aired: November 10, 1989, on FOX.
Spoiler warning: Plot and/or ending details follow.

[edit] Plot

Ever since Steve was fired from the bank, he begins spending his time at the zoo, which angers the working Marcy.

[edit] Cast

  • Reporter — Matt McKenzie
  • TV News Anchor — Lisa Moss
  • Courtney — Jennifer Banko
  • Sonny — Dan Tullis Jr.
  • Norris — Frank Lloyd
  • Chelsea — Trudy Adams
