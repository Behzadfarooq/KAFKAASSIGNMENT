At Last is a 2003 album by Cyndi Lauper. It made the top 40 of the album charts in both the US and Australia.

The album features a duet with Tony Bennett on "Makin' Whoopee". Lauper co-produced the album with Russ Titelman.

The US long box was available only at Costco or Sam's Club shops within the first two weeks when it was released. These are huge warehouse discount stores in the USA, and a yearly membership card is needed to shop there.
