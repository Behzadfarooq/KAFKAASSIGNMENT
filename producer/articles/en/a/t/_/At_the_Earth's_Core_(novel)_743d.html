At the Earth's Core is a 1914 science fiction novel by Edgar Rice Burroughs, the first in a series about the fictional "hollow earth" land of Pellucidar.

The novel was filmed as At the Earth's Core (1976), directed by Kevin Connor and starring Doug McClure as David Innes and Peter Cushing as Abner Perry.
