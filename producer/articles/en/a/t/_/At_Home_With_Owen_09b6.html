At Home With Owen is the fifth studio album by Chicago artist Mike Kinsella under the name Owen. It was released on November 7, 2006 on Polyvinyl Records.

[edit] Guest musicians

  • Kristina Dutton: Violin
  • Andy Rader: Stand-up Bass
  • Jamie Burns: Vocals
  • Gillian Lisee: Vocals

[edit] Trivia

  • Track seven "Femme Fatale" is a cover of a Velvet Underground song from the album The Velvet Underground and Nico.
