At Last is a hip hop a cappella (also known as hiphopapella) group from Los Angeles, California. They first gained national recognition as finalists on the first season of America's Got Talent, having won their spot through viewer votes. The members of the group are Hans Cho, Michael H. Lee, Justin Fong and DJ Say. Actress Ming-Na, and her husband, screenwriter Eric Zee, are the group's managers.

On America's Got Talent, At Last covered R&B songs from the 1970s such as "Let's Stay Together" and "Ain't No Sunshine". The songs they cover are given a hip hop beat, as beatboxing is a main component of the group's sound.
