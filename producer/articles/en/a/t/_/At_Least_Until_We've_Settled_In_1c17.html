At Least Until We've Settled In is the debut release from The Maple State, which was released on February 14, 2005 by Punktastic Recordings.

[edit] Track listing

  1. "You Can't Put Two Hotels On Mayfair" – 3:39
  2. "The City Is Under Martial Law Until We Have Annihilated Them" – 4:06
  3. "Mightiest Monster Mightiest Melodrama Of Them All" – 2:54
  4. "The Fall Of Shi Gau Rui" – 3:25
  5. "The First Law Of Thermodynamics" – 4:31
  6. "Hazards Of Russian Convoys" – 5:10
  7. "Oh No, Figaro" – 2:54

[edit] About

At Least Until We've Settled In is powerful record with some original ideas and some warm, heart on your sleeve lyrics. Songs like "You Can't Put Two Hotels On Mayfair", "The City Is Under Martial Law Until We Have Annihilated Them" and "Oh No, Figaro" are great examples of the band at their finest.
