At The Movies is the title of an episode of the MTV animated series Beavis and Butt-Head.

[edit] Synopsis

Spoiler warning: Plot and/or ending details follow.

The episode begins with Beavis and Butt-Head watching a documentary about the destruction of the South American rainforests, and the people of said area. The two title characters seem uninterested concerning the actually message of the documentary, and are more interested in the breasts of the nude tribeswomen and looking in awe at the tools of destruction applied on the rainforests.

For reasons not revealed, the duo then pay a visit to the drive-in theater from "Beware of the Butt". After making crude jokes in the facility's lavatories, Butt-Head ignites an explosive, later described as a "quarter-stick of dynamite", and flushes it down a toilet. The two then rush to the snack bar, where, apparently according to plan, a pipe at the till has burst and created a huge mess. In the ensuing chaos, Beavis and Butt-Head make off with a good deal of snacks. They then proceed to watch a playing film with their loot.

It is not long before the two are confronted by a security guard of questionable competence. He attempts to draw his gun, but cannot remove it from its holster before it discharges, blasting his foot. The duo joke as they watch the incapacitation of him. They then tie and gag him and continue to watch their film, while it can be visibly seen that his foot is bleeding.

Spoilers end here.
