AT&T Champions Classic

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The AT&T Champions Classic is a golf tournament on the Champions Tour. It is played annually in March in Valencia, California at the Valencia Country Club. AT&T is the main sponsor of the tournament.

The purse for the 2007 tournament was US$1,600,000, with $240,000 going to the winner. The tournament was founded in 1990 as the Security Pacific Senior Classic.
