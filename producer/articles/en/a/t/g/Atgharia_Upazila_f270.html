Atgharia (Bengali: আটগড়িয়া) is an Upazila of Pabna District in the Division of Rajshahi, Bangladesh.

[edit] Geography

Atgharia is located at 24.1333° N 89.2500° E . It has 22339 units of house hold and total area 186.15 km².

[edit] Demographics

As of 1991 Bangladesh censusGRBangladeh, Atgharia has a population of 124454. Males constitute are 51.41% of the population, and females 48.59%. This Upazila's eighteen up population is 59721. Atgharia has an average literacy rate of 21.7% (7+ years), and the national average of 32.4% literate. [1]

[edit] Administrative

Atgharia has 5 Unions/Wards, 111 Mauzas/Mahallas, and 132 vilages.
