Atlas Group

The Atlas Group was laid in 1962, with the establishment of Shirazi Investments (Private) Limited. Atlas Group is a diversified dealing in engineering, financial services and trading. It consists of seven public limited companies out of which six are quoted on the Stock Exchanges in Pakistan, and five private limited companies.
