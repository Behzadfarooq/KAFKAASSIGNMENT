The Atlantic Reporter is a regional case law reporter published by West Publishing in the United States. It contains select opinions of state supreme courts and lower appellate courts from the following states:

  • Connecticut
  • Delaware
  • District of Columbia
  • Maine
  • Maryland
  • New Hampshire
  • New Jersey
  • Pennsylvania
  • Rhode Island
  • Vermont

The first series of the Atlantic Reporter was published from 1895 until 1938, for 200 volumes. The Atlantic Reporter, Second Series, published from 1939 until the present, has reached over 900 volumes. The citation forms for these series are A. and A.2d, respectively.
