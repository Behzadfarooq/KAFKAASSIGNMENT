The Atlanta Vision is an American Basketball Association (ABA) team based in Atlanta. The team began play in the fall of 2004. They finished the 2005 season with a 5-13 record. They missed the playoffs. However, in the 2006-07 season, they finished 15-7, good for second place in the Barnes-Malone division (they were given first place when the Florida Pit Bulls left). They also made it to the ABA's Great 8 tournament at the Blue Cross Arena in Rochester, New York, losing in the quarterfinals to the host team, the Rochester Razorsharks.
