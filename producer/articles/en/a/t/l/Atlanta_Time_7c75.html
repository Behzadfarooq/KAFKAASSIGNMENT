Atlanta/Time is a promo single by the string quartet bond and was released in 2003 after Shine. It contains an audio CD and a DVD with 3 promo videos. Atlanta/Time is only available in Japan and internet shopping sites like Amazon and HMV.

[edit] Track listing

  1. "Atlanta"
  2. "Time"
  3. "Fuego" (Caliente Mix)
  4. "Shine" (Indian Summer Mix)
  5. "Speed" (Crash Club Mix - Radio Edit)
