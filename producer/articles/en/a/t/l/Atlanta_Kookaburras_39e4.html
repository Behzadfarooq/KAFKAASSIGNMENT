The Atlanta Kookaburras are an Australian rules football club based in Atlanta, Georgia, United States.

The club competes in men's and women's divisions in the MAAFL and SEAFL and USAFL National Championships.

[edit] History

The club was founded by Mike Powers (who had previously been involved with Louisville and Nashville clubs), with the first practice being held in Marietta in August of 1998.

Atlanta have been premiers of the SEAFL competition in 2001, 2002, 2003 and 2004.

Also in 2005, the Kookaburras established a local Metro Footy league, the Atlanta Metro Footy League.

The club also a fields a women's team known as the Lady Kookaburras.

Atlanta were Women's Footy national champions in the inaugural women's division of the USAFL National Championships in 2005.
