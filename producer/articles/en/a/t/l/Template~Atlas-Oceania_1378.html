This template is part of a series of navigation templates used in the map entries that form together the Wiki-Atlas at Portal:Atlas. The Wiki-Atlas will be a set of entries in Wikipedia, holding maps of the specific regions and or countries. The template series consists out of template:Atlas, template:Atlas-Africa, template:Atlas-Americas, template:Atlas-Asia, template:Atlas-Europe and template:Atlas-Oceania
