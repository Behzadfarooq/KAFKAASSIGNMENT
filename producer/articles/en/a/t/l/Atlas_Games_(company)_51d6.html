Atlas Games (company)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Atlas Games is a company which publishes role-playing games and card games (both plain games and collectible card games). Its current president is John Nephew, an alumnus of Carleton College.

Many of their games are available in multiple languages.

  • Ars Magica
  • Dungeoneer
  • Cthulhu 500
  • Gloom
  • Feng Shui
  • Furry Pirates
  • Lunch Money
  • Once Upon a Time
  • Over the Edge
  • Unknown Armies
  • Recess (game)
  • Pieces of Eight (game)
  • Seismic (game)
  • Official adventures for Cyberpunk 2020
