The Atlantic Collegiate Baseball League (ACBL) is a baseball league located in the Mid-Atlantic region of the United States. The league has experienced moderate success in having players appear in Major League Baseball, and 13 players that played in the majors in the 2006 season were ACBL graduates as of July 20th. The ACBL is one of eight leagues in the National Alliance of Summer Baseball.
