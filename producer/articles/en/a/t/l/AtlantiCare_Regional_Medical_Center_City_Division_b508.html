AtlantiCare Regional Medical Center is a health system based in Atlantic County, NJ. It includes two hospitals; the Atlantic City Campus in Atlantic City, New Jersey and the Mainland Campus in Pomona, New Jersey. It has Atlantic City's only cancer institute, heart institute, and neonatal intensive care unit.
