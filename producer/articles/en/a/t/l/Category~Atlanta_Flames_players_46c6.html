This is a list of players who played Professional Hockey for the Atlanta Flames of the National Hockey League.

There is also a list at List of Atlanta Flames players.

Note: The Atlanta Flames transferred to Calgary and were re-named the Calgary Flames. So a player may appear on the roster for more than one of these teams.
