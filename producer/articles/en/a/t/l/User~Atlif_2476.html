[edit] About me

My name is Atli Freyr Steinþórsson (IPA pronunciation: [ˡahtlɪ ˌfrei:r ˡstei:nθoursɔn]). I was born in 1984 and am currently reading for a BA in Icelandic at the University of Iceland.

During my not-so-ample spare time, I work as a continuity speaker and newsreader at RÚV (Icelandic National Broadcasting Service) Radio 1, where I also present the odd programme of my own.

In addition to that, I am a freelance editorial assistant with The Old Icelandic Text Society (Hið íslenzka fornritafélag).
