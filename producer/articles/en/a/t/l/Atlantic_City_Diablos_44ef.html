Atlantic City Diablos are an American soccer team, founded in 2007. The team is a member of the National Premier Soccer League (NPSL), the fourth tier of the American Soccer Pyramid, and will make their debut in the brand new Eastern Conference in 2007.

They will play their home games at the stadium on the grounds of St. Augustine Prep School in the city of Richland, New Jersey, 29 miles west of downtown Atlantic City. The team is affiliated with the WPSL team of the same name, and falls under the umbrella of the long-established Team Dynamics LLC, a highly successful organization dedicated to soccer education and player development at all age levels.
