Atlantic FM, is a radio station based in and serving Cornwall, United Kingdom. The station, owned by Atlantic Broadcasting Ltd, began broadcasting on July 6, 2006 at 0730 BST, and is broadcast from St Agnes. The station, aimed at 25 to 54 year olds, mainly plays Adult Contemporary music, and features national and local news and information, including surf reports for Cornish towns and locations such as Newquay and Polzeath on the North Cornwall coast, and Whitsands Bay on the South Cornwall coast.

The station can be found on 105.1 and 107.0 FM, and there is also a live stream1 on Atlantic's website.

The current presenter line up includes: Jane Friggens, Dean Moore, Chris Edgecombe, Tim Ley, Nigel Barker, Chris Gerry, Robin Parris, Alison Johns, Tony Townsend, and Matthew Bunt.
