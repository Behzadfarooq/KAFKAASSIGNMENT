Atlantic Boulevard/Atlantic Avenue is a major north-south thoroughfare in Los Angeles County. It passes through Alhambra, Monterey Park, East Los Angeles, Commerce, Vernon, Maywood, Bell, Cudahy, South Gate, Lynwood, Compton, and Long Beach. It starts off on Huntington Drive in Northern Alhambra and ends on East Ocean Boulevard in Downtown Long Beach.

Through most of its route, Atlantic travels next to the Long Beach Freeway and the Los Angeles River.

South of Randolph Street, Atlantic Boulevard becomes Atlantic Avenue

LACMTA Gold Line is due to connect to Atlantic at Pomona Boulevard in late 2009, connecting East Los Angeles to the other LACMTA rail lines.
