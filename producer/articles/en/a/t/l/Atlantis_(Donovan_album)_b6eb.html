Atlantis is a compilation album from Scottish singer-songwriter Donovan. It was released in the Netherlands on November 12, 2002 (Superior 540777).

[edit] History

In late 2002, Superior Records issued Donovan's 2 disc UK album Rising Again (itself an expanded version of Donovan's 1990 album Rising) as Atlantis for the European continent market. The album featured a different cover.
