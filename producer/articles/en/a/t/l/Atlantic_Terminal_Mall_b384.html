Atlantic Terminal Mall is a shopping mall located on Atlantic Avenue surrounded by Hanson Place, Fort Greene Place and Flatbush Avenue in Brooklyn, New York.

It is also across the street from Atlantic Center (via a small enclosed bridge from Target) which was developed by the same real estate company. It is also an office building and part of the ticket office of the Long Island Rail Road's Atlantic Terminal, which has been been under the process of renovation since the mall was built. Parts of Atlantic Center were also renovated to compete with the new mall. The mall has 3 floors and a basement (LIRR and New York City Subway are also technically in the basement). It features stores like Daffy's, Circuit City, Victoria's Secret, Avenue, Mandee, DSW, GameStop, Men's Wearhouse, The Children's Place, and most notably Target. It also features eateries like Cold Stone Creamery, McDonald's, TCBY/Mrs. Fields, Starbucks (one is located in Target while the other is in the ticket office area of Atlantic Terminal), Houlihans, Pizza Hut Express (also located in Target) and a Chuck E. Cheese's. It also has a dentist's office. It also houses a Department of Motor Vehicles a Burlington Coat Factory and Pathmark Supermarket.
