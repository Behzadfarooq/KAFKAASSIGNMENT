[edit] Awards

[edit] MVP Award

John Evers (1914)

Robert Elliot (1947)

Hank Aaron (1957)

Dale Murphy (1982, 1983)

Terry Pendleton (1991)

Chipper Jones (1999)

[edit] Cy Young Award

Warren Spahn, (1957)

Tom Glavine (1991, 1998)

Greg Maddux (1993, 1994, 1995) (Also won the award for the Cubs in 1992)

John Smoltz (1996)

[edit] Rookie Of The Year Award

Alvin Dark (1948)

Sam Jethroe (1950)

Earl Williams (1971)

Bob Horner (1978)

David Justice (1990)

Rafael Furcal (2000)

[edit] Comeback Player Of The Year Award

Davey Johnson (1973)

Lonnie Smith (1989)

Terry Pendleton (1991)

Andres Galarraga (2000) (Also won the award for the Rockies in 1993)

Javy Lopez (2003)

[edit] Manager of the Year Award

Bobby Cox (1991, 2004, 2005)(Also won the award for the Blue Jays in 1985)

[edit] Silver Slugger Award

Dale Murphy (1982, 1983, 1984, 1985)

Tom Glavine (1991, 1995, 1996, 1998)

Ron Gant (1991)

Fred McGriff (1993)

David Justice (1993)

John Smoltz (1997)

Jeff Blauser (1997)

Chipper Jones (1999, 2000)

Gary Sheffield (2003)

Javy Lopez (2003)

Mike Hampton (2003)

Johnny Estrada (2004)

Andruw Jones (2005)

Brian McCann (2006)

[edit] Hank Aaron Award

Andruw Jones (2005)

[edit] Roberto Clemente Award

Phil Niekro (1980)

Dale Murphy (1988)

John Smoltz (2005)

Lew Burdette (1957)

Tom Glavine (1995)

[edit] All-Star Game MVP

Fred McGriff (1994)

[edit] Lou Gehrig Memorial Award

Warren Spahn (1961)

Hank Aaron (1970)

Phil Niekro (1979)

John Smoltz (2005)

[edit] Gold Glove Award

Del Crandall (1958, 1959, 1960, 1962)

Hank Aaron (1958, 1959, 1960)

Joe Torre (1965)

Felix Millan (1969, 1972)

Clete Boyer (1969)

Phil Niekro (1978, 1979, 1980, 1982, 1983)

Dale Murphy (1982, 1983, 1984, 1985, 1986)

Terry Pendleton (1992) (Also won the award for St. Louis in 1987, 1989)

Greg Maddux (1993, 1994, 1995, 1996, 1997, 1998, 1999, 2000, 2001, 2002) (Also won the award for Chicago in 1990, 1991, 1992, 2004, 2005, 2006)

Marquis Grissom (1995, 1996) (Also won the award for Montreal in 1993, 1994)

Mike Hampton (2003)

Andruw Jones (1998, 1999, 2000, 2001, 2002, 2003, 2004, 2005, 2006)
