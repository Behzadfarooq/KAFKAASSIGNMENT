Good Day
I currently am a student at Bates College in Lewiston, Maine. I was born and grew up in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania, and I currently spend a bulk of my time on Great Diamond Island in Portland, Maine. I am a DJ and board member at college radio station WRBC. You can listen to my show on Tuesdays from 6-8 AM EST using the webstream. I am the principal vocalist for The Band and Brad, and back in the day I also played the Clarinet, Drums, Bagpipes, Mandolin, Ukelele, and Tenor Recorder. My passions are studio art and theater.
