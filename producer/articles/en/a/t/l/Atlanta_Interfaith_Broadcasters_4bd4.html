Atlanta Interfaith Broadcasters (or AIB) is a religious cable channel distributed to cable television systems in and around Atlanta. Their studios and offices are located in Midtown Atlanta.

Many local and regional organizations of multiple faiths and religions provide programming to AIB.

Programming is aired in the 15 counties surrounding Atlanta on cable systems from Comcast, Charter and BellSouth Americast.
