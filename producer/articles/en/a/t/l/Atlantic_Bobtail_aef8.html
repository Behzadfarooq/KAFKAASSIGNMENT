The Atlantic Bobtail (Sepiola atlantica) is a species of bobtail squid native to the northeastern Atlantic Ocean (65ºN to 35ºN), from Iceland, the Faeroe Islands and western Norway to the Moroccan coast. Additionally, there exists one record of this species from the Mediterranean Sea.[1]

Sepiola atlantica appears on stamp FO 409 of the Faroe Islands.

Both sexes of S. atlantica grow to around 21 mm in mantle length.[1]

The type specimen was collected in the Bay of Biscay and is deposited at the Muséum National d'Histoire Naturelle in Paris.[2]
