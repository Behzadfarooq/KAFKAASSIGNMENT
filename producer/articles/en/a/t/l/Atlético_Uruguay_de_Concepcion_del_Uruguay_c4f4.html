Atlético Uruguay are an Argentina Football club, their home town is Concepcion del Uruguay, in the Province of Entre Ríos in Argentina. They currently play in Zone B of the regionalised 4th level of Argentinian football Torneo Argentino B.

The club have played at the highest level of Argentine football on only one occasion. they qualified to play in the National tournament of 1984. The club finished bottom of their group and only managed one draw in their six games.They were beaten 5-0 and 6-0 by River Plate and 7-0 by Huracán.
