Atlas Honda CG 125

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Atlas Honda 125 is a 125cc bike manufactured in Pakistan. It is a 4-stroke, all-purpose naked bike with a top speed of around 105Km/h (65 mph) and a single cylinder engine capable of generating 11 horse power. It's performance and cheap price make it a good option for people in East Asian countries.

It has been in production since mid 80s, and is still being produced. A new model is released every year, although there's nothing new in it performance wise, only graphics on fuel tank and other labels are changed. Performance changes are not so fast here, since it's production it has shifted from Point Ignitoin to CDI (Capacitor discharge Ignition) only.

Some of it's present characteristics are summarised below:
