Atlanta Brewing Company is a brewery located in Atlanta, Georgia. One of Atlanta's first craft breweries, the company opened in 1993, with Red Brick Ale. Blonde and Pale ales were introduced shortly thereafter.

Atlanta Brewing Company also brews Numbers Ale, which is exclusively offered at Atlanta-area restaurants owned by Concentrics Hospitality Group, and Reverend Mudbone Ale, exclusively offered at Jim 'n' Nicks, a barbeque restaurant chain. ABC beers were only available in the metropolitan Atlanta area until 2006, when the line expanded into Alabama, Tennessee and Florida. ABC anticipates offering its beers in the Carolinas sometime in 2007.

ABC brewing company hosts tours of their premises every Wednesday, Friday, and Sunday starting at 5:00 PM until 7:00 PM. Tours are $6 and include an ABC pint glass for tasting the many fine brews on tap.

ABC is in the process of moving to their new location at 2323 Defoor Hills Rd., N.W. Atlanta, GA 30318 but will continue to offer "tastings" at the 1219 Williams St. location until the end of March 2007.
