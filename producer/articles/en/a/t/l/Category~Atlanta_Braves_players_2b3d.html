This category includes baseball players, past and present, for the franchise known as the Atlanta Braves since 1966. This franchise also has been known as:

  • Boston Red Stockings (1871-1875)
  • Boston Red Caps (1876-1882)
  • Boston Beaneaters (1883-1906)
  • Boston Doves (1907-1910)
  • Boston Rustlers (1911)
  • Boston Braves (1912-1935 / 1941-1952)
  • Boston Bees (1936-1940)
  • Milwaukee Braves (1953-1965)
