[edit] Summary

I created this image to replace the fair use image used for the Atlas Award. evrik 15:31, 25 March 2006 (UTC) The Atlas Award for Raking the Sandbox Clean was created by User:Pedant to be given to editors who remove anything particularly offensive from the wikipedia sandbox. "The sandbox is not a litterbox" The idea is that, since the sandbox is reset often, yet is one of the first pages many new editors experience, anyone cleaning the sandbox is going way above the call of duty to keep the wikipedia presentable. They deserve an award if anyone does.
