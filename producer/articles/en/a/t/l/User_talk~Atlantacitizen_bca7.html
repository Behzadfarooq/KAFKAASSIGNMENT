[edit] Bellsouth building pic

Atlantacitizen, thanks for your efforts to add original pictures to Wikipedia. I reverted instances where you replaced an existing unencumbered picture of the Bellsouth building because the existing image, though not great in composition, still better displays the building than your new image due to the better color saturation and constrast. Your pics were taken on an overcast day which really lowers those attributes on your image and does not illustrate the building very well. Please keep taking photos, but look for brighter cloudless days for architectural images.

If you're interested in photography and improving your skills/eye, watch Wikipedia:Featured picture candidates and go through the archives for Wikipedia:Picture of the day. Keep in mind that the folks that are commenting at candidates page are being exceptionally critical (because it is a very high standard to be PotD) as your review, but you can definitely learn what makes a great picture and the things you should work toward even to produce good pictures. Just looking at a good/great pics helps. Good luck and I'm looking forward to seeing more pics from you in the future. AUTiger ʃ talk/work 03:59, 28 October 2006 (UTC)
