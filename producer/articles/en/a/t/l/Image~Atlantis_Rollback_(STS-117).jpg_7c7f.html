[edit] Summary

Description

Kennedy Space Centre, Florida - Space Shuttle Atlantis rolls back to the VAB for repairs following a hailstorm on February 26, which formed 1000-2000 divots in the External Tank and minor surface damage to 26 of the heat-proof tiles on Atlantis's left wing.
Source

http://www.nasa.gov/images/content/170897main_07pd0558.jpg

4/3/2007
