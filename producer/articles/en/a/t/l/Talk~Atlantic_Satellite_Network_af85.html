[edit] Affilation of ASN

I need a little help in determining if the various affilations ASN currently is a part of (CTV, A-Channel, and Citytv) are all secondary or otherwise. MapleLeafFan04 17:08, 20 April 2006 (UTC)

At this point I would say none. While it's certainly worth noting it carries programming from all of these, it's no more an affiliate than NTV is an affiliate of Global (the present consensus there is that it's independent). — stickguy (:^›)— home - talk - 12:35, 21 April 2006 (UTC)

[edit] Adding info

I think it's important to add the point that all cabe companies in Atlantic Canada are required to carry ASN on basic cable. I would add it myself, but I am not 100% sure of this but I do believe that it is true. 70.31.137.12 23:26, 27 November 2006 (UTC)

Actually, and interestingly, they aren't. A sample condition of licence for EastLink Halifax: "The licensee is authorized to distribute, at its option, the programming service Atlantic Satellite Network (ASN), received via satellite, provided that it is distributed on an unrestricted channel of the basic service." (emphasis added) [1] As far as I know, it's the same in the rest of Atlantic Canada. — stickguy (:^›)— || talk || 02:44, 28 November 2006 (UTC)
