[edit] Bishop William Wayne Pugh

Since 1972, Bishop W. Wayne Pugh has been “Preaching the WORD OF FAITH and demonstrating the POWER OF GOD!” Thousands have been Saved, Healed and Blessed in his Miracle Faith Crusades in North America and around the World. Bishop Pugh ministers in the Apostolic Office of the Prophet and is used mightily of God in the Gifts of the Spirit, primarily the Gift of Faith and Prophecy.

Bishop W. Wayne Pugh has ministered the Gospel of Jesus Christ as an International Missionary Evangelist since June of 1974. He has made over 100 Mission Trips to five continents and 57 nations. God has honored His Word as Bishop Pugh preaches the Word of Faith and then Demonstrates the Power of God. Many are being Saved, Healed, Delivered, Blessed and Set Free by God's Power in Miracle Faith Crusades across America. Due to the missionary trips of Bishop Pugh to the many nations, Miracle Faith has currently built 17 churches around the world. Miracle Faith Centers are covering the globe!

Below are some of the nations that Bishop Pugh has ministered in:

Antigua, Aruba, Austria, Bahama Islands, Barbados, Barbuda, Belize, Bonaire, Bulgaria, Canada, Cayman Islands, Costa Rica, Cuba, Curacao, Egypt, England, France, Germany, Greece, Grenada, Guam, Guyana, Haiti, Holland, Honduras, Israel, Jamaica, Japan, Jordan, Macedonia, Mexico, Micronesia, Netherlands, Nevis, Nigeria, Palau, Peru, Philippine Islands, Puerto Rico, Saint Kitts, Saint Lucia, Saint Martin (Dutch) and Sint Maarten (French), Saint Vincent, Scotland, Siapan, Suriname, Switzerland, Trinidad, Turkey, Turks and Cacaos Islands, St. John & St. Thomas, Virgin Islands, Wales, Yugoslavia and all across the United States of America.

Bishop W. Wayne Pugh received his formal education at Tomlinson College and Bible Training Institute in Cleveland, TN. He holds an honorary Doctorate of Divinity from Christian Theological Institute. He has been an Ordained Minister of the Gospel since 1972 and was Consecrated and Elevated to the office of Bishop on August 6, 2000.

Bishop Pugh and wife Marie have three adult children, Aaron, Noel and Nicholas. They reside in the foothills of the North Georgia Mountains, near Atlanta, Georgia.
