Atlantic Sampler is a promotional single by the band King Crimson, released in 1973. Its tracks are from the album Larks' Tongues in Aspic (1973).

[edit] Track listing

  1. "Easy Money" (Robert Fripp, Richard Palmer-James, John Wetton)
  2. "Exiles" (David Cross, Fripp, Palmer-James )
  3. "Larks' Tongues in Aspic (Part II)" (Fripp)

[edit] Personnel

  • Robert Fripp - guitar, mellotron
  • John Wetton - bass guitar, vocals
  • David Cross - violin, viola, mellotron
  • Bill Bruford - drums
  • Jamie Muir - percussion
