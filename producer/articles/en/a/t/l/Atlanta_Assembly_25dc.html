Atlanta Assembly

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Former Atlanta Assembly plant photographed on I-75 in Hapeville, Georgia on January 12, 2007

Atlanta Assembly is an automobile factory owned by Ford Motor Company in Hapeville, Georgia. Harbour Consulting rated it as the most efficient auto plant in North America in 2006. As part of The Way Forward plan, the plant's last operations were October 27, 2006. [1].

Products:

  • Ford Taurus
  • Mercury Sable
