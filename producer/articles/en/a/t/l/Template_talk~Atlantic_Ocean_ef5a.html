Technically incorrect? I wouldn't say so. The Atlantic Ocean is not a superset of the Mediterranean Sea. See both articles and here in p.13 to see that the Atlantic borders the Med. Spain and France do of course have an Atlantic coast. I agree with your point though, just couldnt resist nit-picking... --Guinnog 08:19, 3 November 2006 (UTC)

[edit] This template purpose

To be honest, I find this template of very limited usefulness (especially if it’s littered on all country pages, some have over 8 now). From Wikipedia:Navigational_templates#General navigation templates I get that templates should retain a "tightly focused relationship between the articles", which I don’t think the Atlantic Ocean is (A template should be added to help the reader navigate quickly, not because it’s possible).--Van helsing 11:53, 2 November 2006 (UTC)
