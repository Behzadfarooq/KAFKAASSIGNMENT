This user is an Esperanzian
This user is an Esperanzian, which is someone who is a member of Wikipedia:Esperanza. Esperanza is an association of Wikipedians dedicated to strengthening Wikipedia's sense of community. It is primarily involved with establishing and maintaining a support network for its members, who want to build a place where Wikipedians can go for hope and reassurance. Esperanza is designed to be an approachable, small community designed to recognize situations where hope, reassurance, or help are needed, and match those who desire help with those who offer it.
