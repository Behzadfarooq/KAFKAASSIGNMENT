ATM Bersama is one of the many interbank networks in Indonesia, connecting the ATM networks of twenty-one banks in Indonesia. It was established in 1993 and is based on the model adopted by MegaLink, an interbank network in the Philippines.

At present, ATM Bersama has over 2,400 ATMs over all of Indonesia's provinces and fifty of Indonesia's cities. The network is owned, organized and operated by PT Aplikanusa Lintasarta.
