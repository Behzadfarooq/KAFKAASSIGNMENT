Category atmospheric radiation is an umbrella term for studies of atmospheric optics, radiative transfer in the atmosphere, light scattering on hydrometeors and mulecules, band models of atmospheric transmission. Atmospheric radiation plays crucial role in studies of atmospheric remote sensing.
