The Atmore Station is an Amtrak station in Atmore, Alabama on the Sunset Limited. Its Amtrak station code is ATR.

The station is located at 107 East Louisville Street. It features an enclosed waiting area, payphones, and partial wheelchair accessibility.

Because of the effects of Hurricane Katrina the Sunset Limited terminates at New Orleans, and Atmore is closed.
