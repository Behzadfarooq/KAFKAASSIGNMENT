Atmosphere/She's Lost Control is a single released by Joy Division following the death of lead singer Ian Curtis.

The song "She's Lost Control" is an alternative version to the one that appears on the debut album Unknown Pleasures. This version, along with "Atmosphere" appears on the compilation Substance

Although the list changes constantly, the single is often in the top 4 of the Rate Your Music Top 1000 Singles Of All-time

In 2000, the single's A-side "Atmosphere" was voted the greatest song of the millennium by listener's of John Peel's Radio 1 programme.[1]
