atm⁵ is an interbank network in Singapore, connecting the ATMs of five of Singapore's six qualifying full banks, with the lone exception of BNP Paribas. As of 2006, there are 143 atm⁵ ATMs island-wide. The network was established in April of 2005.

atm⁵ is also one of the few interbank networks that does not charge its customers for transactions via another member bank's ATM, having removed all interbank transaction charges on April 4, 2006.
