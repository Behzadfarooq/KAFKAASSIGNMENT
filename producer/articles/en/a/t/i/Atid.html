Atid (Hungarian: Etéd) is a commune in Harghita County, Romania comprising of 5 villages:

  • Atid/Etéd
  • Crişeni/Kőrispatak
  • Cuşmod/Küsmöd
  • Inlăceni/Énlaka
  • Şiclod/Siklód

[edit] Demographics

The commune has an absolute Székely (Hungarian) majority. According to the 2002 census it has a population of 2,837 of which 98.48% or 2,794 are Hungarian.
