Ati may refer to:

  • Ati (tribe), the earliest inhabitants of the Visayas in the Philippines
  • Ati-Atihan Festival, an annual celebration held in the province of Aklan in the Philippines.
  • Ati, Chad, a town in Chad.
  • Ati (queen), Queen of Punt ca. 1490 BCE.

The three-letter acronym ATI may stand for:

  • Allegheny Technologies Inc., a steel company that trades on the New York Stock Exchange under the symbol 'ATI'.
  • ATI Technologies Inc., a subsidiary of AMD that manufactures graphics cards.
  • Auckland Technical Institute, now Auckland University of Technology
  • Advanced Training Institute, a home education program developed by Institute in Basic Life Principles.
  • Automation Technology Inc., a provider of web-based collaborative outage work-scope planning software for the electrical power generation industry.
  • Air Transport International, a worldwide charter cargo airline.
  • Allied Telesis Inc., a manufacturer of computer network products, formerly known as Allied Telesyn.
  • A. Tarricone, Inc., a fuel company operating a chain of gas stations in parts of New York State.
  • ATI (group), a musical group.
  • Agricultural Technical Institute, a campus located in Wooster, Ohio part of The Ohio State University system.
  • A. T. I., a 1944 collection of poems by A. P. Herbert
  • Tunisian Internet Agency (Agence tunisienne d'Internet), a Tunisian ISP
