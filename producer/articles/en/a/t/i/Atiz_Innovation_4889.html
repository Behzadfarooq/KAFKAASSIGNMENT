Atiz Innovation is a private corporation, specializing in book scanning and digital camera-based scanning.

The company produces a number of book scanning products. One of its first product line is its BookDrive, an automatic page-turning scanner in the size of about 2 PCs that can scan some subset of books at a speed of 500 pages an hour. Another noted product line is its BookDrive DIY, which is a platform that mounts 2 digital cameras to photograph the page images of a book cradled in a V-shaped, adjustable book cradle.

Sarasin Booppanon was a founder of the company. He started working on Atiz in 2003 while pursuing his doctorate at George Mason University. Fed up with having to commute from home to school to spend countless hours photocopying books assigned by the professors, he envisioned a device that would digitize books automatically.

Sarasin contacted Jackrit Suthakorn, a professor in robotics and printed materials, to assist in bringing his idea to life. In 2004 Atiz Innovation Co., Ltd. was found. The main research office is located in Bangkok, Thailand. Its sales and marketing office in the United States is currently headed by Nick Warnock, former finalist from the popular "The Apprentice" TV show.
