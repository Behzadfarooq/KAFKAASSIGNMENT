[edit] Merge proposal - God Eater and Atum

God Eater (comics) and Atum (comics) - they're the same character (just different aspects, in an almost Banner/Hulk way...) and there's a lot of duplication on the two pages. Better to merge them and keep one as a refirect. Since the character's "default" state is Atum, that's the one I'd keep... --Mrph 10:08, 21 October 2006 (UTC)

[edit] Survey

  • Merge any additional info into Atum (comics) --Mrph 10:08, 21 October 2006 (UTC)
  • Merge- for the reasons pointed above. -Wilfredo Martinez 14:45, 21 October 2006 (UTC)
  • Rename/redirect- The information is identical. Just rename one article and turn the other into a redirect. --NewtΨΦ 15:17, 21 October 2006 (UTC)
  • Merge. Stephen Day 22:16, 21 October 2006 (UTC)
  • Merge per above. This material just isn't lengthy enough to warrant separate articles, even if it weren't identical. So in practical terms, kill one and redirect to the other. Doczilla 05:38, 22 October 2006 (UTC)

[edit] Discussion
