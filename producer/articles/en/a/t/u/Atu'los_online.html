Atu’los Online (ah-tool-los on-lie-ne)is a free isometric online medieval game MMORPG. The game is still in its infancy and currently has well over 5,000 members. The client size is 34mb.

[edit] Plot

(from the website)

In the Ancient times, a fearsome ruler in the line of Arkahron ruled with an Iron Fist. This ruler was called 'Atu'los', because in the Ancient tongue this meant that he was both wise and evil. Atu'los used his wisdom to study the dark arts, a gift that according to legend, was sometimes found in the lineage of Arkahron. Atu'los awoke many great and terrible forces, and became more powerful than any other that has lived.

Atu'los conquered all the lands of the Ancient world, and ruled them for many years. But Atu'los was not immortal. During a war with a band of mountain dwelling rebels, Atu'los was struck in the neck by an arrow. Nearing death Atu'los retreated to his castle. There Atu'los died and was placed in a burial chamber that became enchanted by powers still unknown.

Over the centuries the region was named after its ancient ruler ... Atu'los.

[edit] Gameplay

The gameplay is most intriguing. The game is separated into different dungeons and one arena where players can battle each other. In each dungeon, you have to kill a certain creature, to get the amulet key to open the chest with the amulet. The main story is for the player to collect the amulets, 6 altogether, in order to prepare themselves for the first major round boss battle. After this the game becomes much bigger offering a major quest for the Orbs of Arphax and more encounters with round bosses. Of course, players can battle each other outside of the arena, just move next to them and right click.

Players earn experience points from battling monsters in the dungeons and defeating them. A finite amount of xp is awarded after killing a type of monster (there are around 15 different types of standard monsters of varying difficulty and an array of difficult round boss battles) and over time the player goes up in level, able to purchase greater weapons and armour. After defeating the first boss, there are many new shops and items to purchase (provided they have the money – earned through defeating monsters and opening chests) . What is curious to many is that it doesn’t become more difficult to gain levels, almost the same amounts of enemies are killed in succession to level up again, hence, there is no higher difficulty curb. This results in a high levels being earned, in the thousands.

Actual combat against NPCs is as follows: The player is required to left click next to the monster and the player’s avatar automatically attacks, however the player can use potions and attempt to run if they are close to death. Players can pick mushrooms in a forest and have them converted to health potions, adding another element to the game.

[edit] Premier Quests

There is also a Premier addition to Atu'los that allows players to become mercenaries for the Army of Arphax. This sends the player on a huge series of quests, and to new Areas such as the Palace of Kings. There are also new shops and items in the Premier verision of Atu'los.

[edit] Character Classes

The game presents the players with a ‘variety’ of classes, where each class has an advantage of it's own. For example the Guardian class attacks faster, but with less damage and the Mage class has a higher attack+wisdom combination through the use of spells, but is more expensive to develop. Each class has specific items in shops that only that class can purchase.

The Knight of Earth: The knight of earth is skillfull with a sword. Using accuracy rather than force to win a fight.

The Knight of Fire: The knight of fire is skillfull with a sword. Using force rather than skill to win a fight. The shield the knight uses is heavy and strong.

The Mage of Earth: The mage of Earth uses a staff that is charged with the magic of earth. The robe that the mage wears is also magical, deflecting some of the attack from an opponent.

The Mage of Fire: The Mage of Fire uses a staff that is charged with the magic of fire. The robe that the mage wears is also magical, deflecting some of the attack from an opponent.

The Dwarf of Earth: The Dwarf of Earth uses an axe with a balance of force and skill. A stout warrior, the dwarf is well protected by his shield.

The Dwarf of Fire: The Dwarf of Fire uses an axe with a more force than skill. A stout warrior, the dwarf is well protected by his shield.

The Warrior of Earth: The Warrior of Earth uses an axe with brute strength, hacking an oppponent into submission. The warrior wears a brace to protect his striking arm.

The Guardian of Fire: The Guardian of Fire has no rival with the sword. Skill and cunning see many a stronger opponent beaten. The shield of the guardian is light but strong.

[edit] Administrators

As users explore the Atulos world they may encounter Atulos administrators who are individuals with very high levels (over 1000)and some control over the game. The 10 or so Administrators appear to be around to help players and to police the game. Many people seem to use the program as an animated chat room, including the administrators, so they can be found in the game regularly if you have a problem.

[edit] Creators

The game was created by Timothy Camm, a programmer hobbyist. Its graphics were worked on by Dascribe, Tiles and himself.
