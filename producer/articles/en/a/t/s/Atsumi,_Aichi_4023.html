Former Town of Atsumi

Atsumi (渥美町; -chou) was a town located in Atsumi District, Aichi, Japan.

On October 1, 2005 the Town of Atsumi merged into the City of Tahara.

As of 2003, the town had an estimated population of 21,841 and a density of 265.77 persons per km². The total area was 82.18 km².
