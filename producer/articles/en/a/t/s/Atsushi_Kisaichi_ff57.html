Atsushi Kisaichi

Atsushi Kisaichi (私市 淳 Kisaichi Atsushi, born February 23, 1971) is a seiyū who was born in Tokyo. He works for Aoni Production. His hobbies are baseball and tennis.

[edit] Notable voice roles

  • Kirby series: Meta Knight
  • Kage Kara Mamoru: Mamoru Kagemori (2006)
  • This Ugly and Beautiful World: Shin'ichi Asakura (2004)
  • Piano: The Melody of a Young Girl's Heart: Nagasawa (2002)
  • Kanon: Yuuichi Aizawa (2002)
  • Mahoromatic: Kiyomi Kawahara (2001)
  • His and Her Circumstances: Hideaki Asaba (1998)
  • Dragon Ball GT: Uub (1996)
  • Yu-Gi-Oh!: Heida (1998)
  • Future GPX Cyber Formula SAGA: Phill Fritz (1996) & Future GPX Cyber Formula SIN (1998)
  • Hoshi no Kaabii: Meta Knight (2001 and 2007, respectively)
  • Saint Seiya: Papillon Myu 2002
  • Gundam Seed: Special Edition: Rusty Mackenzie
  • GetBackers drama CDs: Sariel
  • Sukisho: Gaku Ishikawa
  • Dragon Shadow Spell: Kiriyama Laika (video game)
  • Hana-Kimi drama CDs: Izumi Sano
