Atsushi Shindo (新堂 敦士 Shindō Atsushi) is a singer/songwriter who is known for him creation of music for the Pop'n Music line of Bemani games. He has also written songs for artists such as Hello! Project's Melon Kinenbi. Having signed a five-year contract with Konami (which has recently expired), his songs are mainly folk songs with the genre "power folk".

However, all these tunes were deleted in the game for the plagiarism problem(The following description).

[edit] A large amount of plagiarism

A large amount of plagiarism came to light since about 2002. The activity was stopped as a charge period to study music in July, 2004.

Because it was unnamed, it was not reported at all though the plagiarism reached tens of.

It upped and about doing in October, 2005.

It has not explained and has not apologized at all for the plagiarism. up to now.
