Atsuhiro Miura

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Atsuhiro Miura (三浦淳宏, born 24 June 1974) is a Japanese football midfielder.

He was capped 25 times and scored 1 goal for the Japanese national team between 1999 and 2005. He was a member of the Japanese 2000 Summer Olympics squad.
