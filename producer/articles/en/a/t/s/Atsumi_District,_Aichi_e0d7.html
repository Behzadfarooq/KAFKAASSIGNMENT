Atsumi (渥美郡; -gun) was a district located in Aichi Prefecture, Japan.

As of 2003, the district had an estimated population of 21,841 and a density of 265.77 persons per km². The total area was 82.18 km².

[edit] Former towns and villages

  • Atsumi

[edit] Merger

On October 1, 2005 the town of Atsumi merged into the city of Tahara. Atsumi District was dissolved as a result of this merger.
