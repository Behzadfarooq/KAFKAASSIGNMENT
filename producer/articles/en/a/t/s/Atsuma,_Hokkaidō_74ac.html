Atsuma, Hokkaidō

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Atsuma (厚真町; -chō) is a town located in Yūfutsu District, Iburi, Hokkaidō, Japan.

As of 2004, the town has an estimated population of 5,211 and a density of 12.88 persons per km². The total area is 404.56 km².
