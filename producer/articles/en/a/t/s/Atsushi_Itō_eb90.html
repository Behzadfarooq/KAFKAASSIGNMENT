Atsushi Itō

Atsushi Itō (伊藤淳史, Itō Atsushi?)(also Atsushi Itoh) is a Japanese actor, born November 25, 1983 in Chiba Prefecture, Japan. He started acting at the age of three in education programs. He is 162 centimeters tall and a Sagittarius. He is currently studying at Hosei University Business Faculty. He is frequently cast as geeky or otaku characters.
