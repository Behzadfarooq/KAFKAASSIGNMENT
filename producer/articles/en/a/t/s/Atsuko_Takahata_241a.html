Atsuko Takahata (高畑 淳子, Takahata Atsuko?) is a seiyū who was born on October 11, 1954 in Kagawa.

[edit] Notable voice roles

  • Kanoe in X/1999 the movie
  • Debonair in Magic Knight Rayearth

[edit] Live-action roles

  • Space Witch Gilza in Juspion
  • Maribaron in Kamen Rider BLACK RX
  • Calra/Miyuki Fukano in Tokkei Winspector(Episode 27)
  • Hidemi Takeuchi in Tokusou Exceedraft(Episode 6)
  • Reiko Ayanokouji in Tokusou Robo Janperson
  • An actress in the Movie Ringu Zero
