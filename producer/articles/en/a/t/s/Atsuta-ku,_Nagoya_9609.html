Atsuta Shrine

Atsuta (熱田区, -ku) is one of the wards of the city of Nagoya in Aichi Prefecture, Japan. Atsuta is a place that prospers as a shrine town in Atsuta Shrine. There is a headquarters of large rolling stock manufacturer Nippon Sharyo.
