Atsuko Tanaka (田中 敦子, Tanaka Atsuko?), born on November 14, 1962 in Gunma is a seiyū who works for Mausu Promotion.

[edit] Notable voice roles

  • Motoko Kusanagi in Ghost in the Shell, Ghost in the Shell 2: Innocence, and Ghost in the Shell: Stand Alone Complex
  • Ateria, in The Adventures of Tweeny Witches
  • Soi in Fushigi Yūgi
  • Saki Sawanoguchi in Magic User's Club OVA
  • Hades Goddess Gorgon in Mahou Sentai Magiranger
  • Claire in Trigun
  • Yumi Haneishi in Fancy Lala
  • Puru in Super Doll★Licca-chan
  • Rosso in Dirge of Cerberus: Final Fantasy VII
  • Caster in Fate/stay night
  • Mika in Kyoshiro to Towa no Sora
  • Chun-Li and Regina in Namco X Capcom
  • Viletta Badim in Super Robot Wars
  • Yuca Meilasch in Mobile Suit Victory Gundam
  • Sayoko Nanamori in RahXephon
  • Karurauatsuurei (Karura) in Utawarerumono
  • Ahly and Leone in Valkyrie Profile 2: Silmeria
  • Maiko Kaji in Hataraki Man
