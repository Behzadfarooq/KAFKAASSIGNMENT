Atsuko Enomoto

Atsuko Enomoto (榎本 温子, Enomoto Atsuko?, born November 1, 1979 in Tokyo) is a seiyū who works for 81 Produce (transferred from I'm Enterprise on September 1, 2005). Her debut role is Yukino Miyazawa from Kare Kano.
