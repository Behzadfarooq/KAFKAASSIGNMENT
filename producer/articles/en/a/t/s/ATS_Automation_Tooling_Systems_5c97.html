ATS Automation Tooling Systems

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

ATS Automation Tooling Systems (TSX: ATA) is a Cambridge, Ontario based company which designs and builds factory automation solutions. They have designed and built more than 10,000 automation systems in the areas of medical devices, pharmaceuticals, telecommunications, semiconductor, fiber optics, automotive, computers, solar energy and consumer products. ATS employs approximately 3,900 people worldwide, with more than 1.5 million ft² (140,000 m²) of manufacturing space in 25 manufacturing facilities.

Business Units

  • Automation Systems Group
  • Precision Component Manufacturing Group
  • Advanced Manufacturing Group (component assembly, build to print automation, repeat equipment build and private label manufacturing)
