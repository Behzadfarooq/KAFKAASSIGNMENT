Attila Elek (born on December 6, 1982 in Budapest) is a Hungarian ice dancer. He competes with Nora Hoffmann. They are five-time Hungarian national champions, two-time World Junior silver medalists, and recently placed 17th in Torino. Elek's younger brother Gyorgy Elek competes in ice dance with Zsuzsanna Nagy
