Attic Records was a Canadian independent record label, founded in 1974 by Alexander Mair and Tom Williams.

Attic Records ceased to exist as an independent company in 1999, when it was merged with TMP (The Music Publisher) and Oasis Entertainment Distribution to form Song Corporation.
