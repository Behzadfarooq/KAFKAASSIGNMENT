[edit] Introduction

Hi I am AtTheGatesOfBodom which is an alias I made combining the bands, At the Gates and Children of Bodom which are a couple bands that I like highly.

[edit] Life

I was born in York, PA in 1991. I moved later to Chester County, PA and in 4th grade moved to Downingtown, PA. I am still in high school and attend Downingtown East. Basically all I do is hang out with my friends, play guitar and go on the computer, regular teenager stuff. My mom lives in Mill Valley, CA and I visit her a lot and visit my friends there too. I am planning on going to UCSF after graduation and becoming a nurse.
