Attack

Attack can mean:

  • In military strategy, an attempt to actively strike at the enemy, as opposed to a defensive plan of stopping enemy attacks. The attempt may be to conquer (or deny to enemy use) enemy territory, installations, personnel, or equipment.
  • Criticism, to point out the flaws in something.
  • In music, the onset of a note (also called an "attack-point").
  • In audio and radio, the reaction time of an envelope detector in audio level compression.

Attack can also refer to:

  • Attack!, a 2003 board game developed by Eagle Games.
  • Attack! (film), a 1956 war film
  • "Attack (song)", a song by the band System of a Down.
  • Attack (fencing), an offensive movement.
  • Attack!! (album), a 2002 album by Yngwie J. Malmsteen.
  • The Attack (band), a mid-60s band from England.
  • Myocardial infarction, a heart attack, a disease in which the blood supply to the heart is interrupted.
  • Attack Records (UK), a vanity record label of Sanctuary Records for the singer Morrisey.
  • Attack No. 1, a Japanese manga and anime about volleyball.
  • Attack 25, a TV Quiz by TV Asahi.
  • Attack!, a Japanese laundry detergent made by the Kao Corporation.
  • Attack, a video game for the TI-99 4/a home computer, with a ship killing large spiders.
  • Der Angriff (English: "The Attack"), a newspaper published by Berlin Gau of NSDAP since 1927.
