The Attack on Tantive IV was the first conflict seen in the Star Wars saga, seen in A New Hope.

After Kyle Katarn stole the Death Star plans during the Battle of Dantua from Star Wars: Dark Forces, he beamed them to Princess Leia and a group of soldiers, who were on Ralltiir. They soon departed in their Blockade Runner, the Tantive IV.

Meanwhile, the troopers of the 501st Legion were attacking a small Rebel outpost in the Polis Massa system, where intelligence "falsely" tracked the plans. They found information from a Rebel holodisk that the plans were beamed to the Tantive.

While en route to Alderaan, their Tantive's trip was interrupted by the Devastator. After it was captured, the ship was drawn by a tractor beam into the docking bay, where Darth Vader and the 501st Legion forced their way into the frigate.

Imperial Stormtroopers of the 501st Legion engaged the rebels in a firefight aboard the ship. After Darth Vader destroyed the security mainframes, he came across some access codes that allowed his troops to gain access to the rest of the ship.

After most of the Rebels were killed and captured, Vader strangled Captain Antilles, owner of C-3PO and R2-D2, to death after interrogating him about the location of the Death Star plans.

Princess Leia transferred the plans into R2, who escaped the Tantive with C-3P0 in an escape pod. Leia was captured, and the Empire was victorious. However, the Death Star's schematics were no where to be found.
