Attavyros

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Aerial view of Attavyros mountain and municipality

Attavyros (Αττάβυρος) is a municipality on the island of Rhodes, in the Dodecanese, Greece. Population 3,225 (2001). The seat of the municipality is in Empona.
