Attalea is a large genus of palms native to the Caribbean, Central and South America. This pinnately-leaved genus includes some of the largest neotropical palms. According to the Arecaceae section of the Royal Botanic Gardens, Kew: World Checklist of Selected Plant Families,[1] there are 67 recognised species in this genus.

Common Names: Cohune palm, rain tree, American oil palm, corozo palm, manaca palm.
