Attack of the Weirdos

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Attack of the Weirdos
EP by Bizarre
Released 1997
Genre Hip hop

Attack of the Weirdos is an EP recorded by Detroit rapper Bizarre in late 1997. The tracks were produced by local Detroit producers and DJs including DJ Head, who was D12's DJ at the time, Mad Chemist, Hush (of Da Ruckus), DJ Lenn Swann and the now acclaimed producer Jay Dee of Slum Village. The EP earned Bizarre the Inner City Flava Of The Year award in 1998.

[edit] Track listing

  1. Rap Guys - produced by Hush
  2. What, What [featuring Da Brigade (Kon Artis & Kuniva of D12)] - produced by Denaun Porter a.k.a Kon Artis
  3. Trife Thieves [featuring Eminem & Fuzz] - produced by DJ Head
  4. Down Low - produced by Mad Chemist
  5. Over React - produced by DJ Head, co-produced by DJ Lenn Swann (all cutz and skratches.)
  6. Butterfly [featuring Dea Day] - produced by Jay Dee
  7. Get the Dick (Raw Mix) [featuring The Outsidaz] - produced by DJ Head
