[edit] History

Attitudes was a pop/rock quartet from Los Angeles made up of some well-known names — producer/composer/arranger David Foster on keyboards, guitarist Danny Kortchmar (who has worked extensively with James Taylor and Don Henley), bassist Paul Stallworth, and noted session drummer Jim Keltner. The group was signed to George Harrison's Dark Horse Records label and made two albums in the mid-'70s; a single, "Sweet Summer Music", charted at number 94 in 1976
