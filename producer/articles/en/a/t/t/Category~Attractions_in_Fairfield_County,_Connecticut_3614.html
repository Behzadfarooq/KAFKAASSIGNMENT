Category:Attractions in Fairfield County, Connecticut

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Attractions in Fairfield County, Connecticut includes museums, parks, lighthouses, sports and entertainment venues, sports teams and organizations as well as regular events.

The dozen or so largest and most notable attractions, of any sort, are also listed in the Fairfield County category both for ease of navigation and to avoid possible confusion with the general term "attraction".
