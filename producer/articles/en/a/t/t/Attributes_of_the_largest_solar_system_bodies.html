Attributes of the largest solar system bodies

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

These tables list the attributes of the solar system's largest objects (excluding the Sun).

See also: table of planets in the solar system.

Mass, equatorial diameter, orbital radius, orbital inclination and day are measured relative to the Earth. See the Earth article for absolute values.

Objects are listed in order of distance from the Sun.
