Attila Zubor

Attila Zubor (born March 12, 1975 in Budapest) is a former freestyle swimmer from Hungary, who competed in three consequentive Summer Olympics for his native country, starting in 1996. He was trained by the famous Tamás Széchy, who also guided the great Tamás Darnyi for instance.
