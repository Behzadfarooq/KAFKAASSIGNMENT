This image is copyrighted; however, the copyright holder allows the image to be freely redistributed, modified, used commercially and for any other purpose, provided that their authorship is attributed.

This template can be used in three ways:

  • with no copyright holder specified (as seen above): {{attribution}}
  • with a copyright holder specified who has a page on Wikipedia: {{attribution|User:Someuser}} or {{attribution|Microsoft Corporation}}
  • or the same as previous but with specified link text: {{attribution|User:Someuser|My full name}}
