[edit] Opening Narrative

Renton narrates a cynical critique of modern society through a series of him and he and Sick Boy away from a robbery, the main five characters playing a match of soccer with random opponents, a scene introducing Mother Superior's, the Heroin den he and most of his friends frequent, and a scene showing those in Renton's life who disapprove of his habit.

The opposition to his listless drug addiction nearly makes him convinced into quitting his habit, but repents as the opening title frame appears.

[edit] London

Faced with the anomie of his post-junk life, Renton escapes to London to become a Real Estate agent, trying to find a purpose in the hustle and bustle of the big city.
