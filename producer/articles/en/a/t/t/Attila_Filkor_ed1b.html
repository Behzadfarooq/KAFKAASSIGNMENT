Attila Filkor (born July 12, 1988, Budapest Hungary) is an attacking midfielder who can also play as a striker plays for Inter Milan in Italy's Serie A. He joined them in the summer of 2006 from Maltese club Pietà Hotspurs FC and has been called-up for the Hungary national football team. He was on the bench against Canada national football team, 15 November 2006 and made his debut on February 7, 2007 in the last 5 minutes of a 2-0 win against Latvia in a friendly.

He has also captained the Hungarian U-19 side.

He made his first team debut in the Italian Cup against F.C. Messina Peloro, and also played in the second leg.
