Attilio Demaría (March 19, 1909 – November 11, 1990) was an Italian-Argentine football (soccer) player, who played for Argentina in the 1930 World Cup and for Italy in the 1934 World Cup.

He was born in Buenos Aires. He played 295 games for Internazionale of Italy between 1931 and 1943, scoring 86 goals for the club.

Demaría died in Haedo.
