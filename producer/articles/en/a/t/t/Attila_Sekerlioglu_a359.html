Attila Sekerlioglu (born January 27, 1965 in Vienna) is a retired footballer of Austrian and Turkish descent. He played in midfield. Since retirement, he has moved into coaching and management.

During his career he played for Elektra Vienna, Austria Vienna (1988-1995), VSE St. PÃ¶lten, FC Tirol Innsbruck (1995), St Johnstone (1995-1998), SC Untersiebenbrunn and FC Oslip.

In the early 2000s, Sekerlioglu managed Maccabi Vienna, SC Himberg and, until January 2005, SV Horn.

Sekerlioglu is currently the manager of Austrian regional league club ASK Schwadorf.[1] Schwadorf hosted English giants Arsenal in a pre-season friendly on July 31, 2006. The visitors won 8-1.[2]

[edit] Trivia

  • "Sekerlioglu" means "Sugar Boy" in English.[3]
