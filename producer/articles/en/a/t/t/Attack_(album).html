ATTACK is an album by the Japanese group of entertainers known as AAA: Attack All Around. It was released on avex trax on January 1, 2006 in three different editions: CD, CD accompanied by a DVD, and a CD which featured only a selection of the songs from the album. The album contained only one top 10 hit and the band being a group of performers of both sexes picked from auditions, it was no big surprise that the album debuted at #16 in the Japanese albums chart with about 36,000 sold copies and has since continued to drop in the chart.

With a wide range of songwriters and arrangers involved in the production, the album presents a variety of modern day Japanese pop music, drawing both inspiration from both from Japanese and international music. Many styles are represented: m.o.v.e.-like Eurobeat ("BLOOD on FIRE", "VIRGIN F"), Tokyo Ska Paradise-reminiscent ska ("DRAGON FIRE"), freestyle ("Welcome to This World"), funk (きれいな空 ("Beautiful Sky", kirei na sora?) & "Get or Lose"), Ricky Martin'esque Latin pop (最強 Babe ("Strongest Babe", saikyō bēbu?)), bubble gum pop (出会いのチカラ ("The power of dating", deai no chikara?) & 地球に抱かれて ("sakyū ni dakerete", 地球に抱かれて?)), disco ("Friday Party") and slow jams (ボクラノテ ("My hands", bokura no te?)).
