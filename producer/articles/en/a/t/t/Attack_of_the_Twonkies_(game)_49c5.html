Attack of the Twonkies (game)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Adventures of Jimmy Neutron Boy Genius: Attack of the Twonkies
Developer(s) THQ Studio Australia
Publisher(s) THQ
Release date(s) September 17, 2004
Genre(s) Action adventure
Mode(s) Single player
Rating(s) ESRB: Everyone (6+)
Platform(s) GameCube, Game Boy Advance, PlayStation 2

Attack of the Twonkies is a video game released in 2004 based on the animated TV series The Adventures of Jimmy Neutron: Boy Genius. In the game, the player controls Jimmy Neutron in a quest to save earth from the alien twonkies using a variety of gadgets and inventions.
