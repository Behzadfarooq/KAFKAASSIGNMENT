AtTask, Inc. is a project management software company based in Utah. The firm was founded in 2001.

Its flagship product, @task 4, is a platform-independent, web-based project management suite which includes task management, issue tracking, document management, time tracking, and portfolio management.[1]

[edit] History

AtTask began in 2001 when a group of software developers, working from various geographic locations on a variety of projects, needed a collaborative solution that could aid in project planning, project status reporting, and could keep clients in their feedback loop. The result of this initiative was @task, a browser-based project management solution.

[edit] Software

In 2006, AtTask released a complete rewrite of its product. The rewrite delivered new technologies such as: an "approachable" simplified user interface[2] that utilizes AJAX[3] for responsiveness and improved page loading times, a JMS based event model, Cluster configuration, i18n compliance, SaaS options, and Web Services for product integration.

AtTask claims to be the first SaaS based project management solution to allow users to bring their own data in-house.

In 2007, Joe Cardenas, former VP and CIO of Salesforce.com joined AtTask's board
