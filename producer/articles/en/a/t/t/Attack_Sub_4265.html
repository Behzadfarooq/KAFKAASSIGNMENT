"Attack Sub" is an episode of The Emperor's New School.

[edit] Plot

Kuzco succeeds in getting Mr. Moleguaco a day off. However, the substitute, Mr. Notaempa, is not who he thought he was. Kuzco also succeeds in getting the class more work than they have ever had. Kuzco at first decides to run away and never come back. Malina tells Kuzco that they should get Mr. Moleguaco back but Kuzco implies that he was thinking it before he said it. After he returns, it is revealed that Mr. Notaempa is Mr. Moleguaco's cousin who works every year when Mr. Moleguaco is unable to teach. Then Mr. Moleguaco decides to teach them that there is more to life than work by taking them on a vacation.

[edit] Trivia

  • We learn that Mr. Moleguaco gets seasick
  • Yzma makes no appearance but the name "Principal Amzy" is mentioned.
