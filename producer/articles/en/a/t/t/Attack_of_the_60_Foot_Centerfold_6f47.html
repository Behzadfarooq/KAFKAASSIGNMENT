Attack of the 60 Foot Centerfold is a 1995 science fiction film starring J.J. North, Ted Monte, Tammy Parks, Raelyn Saalman, Nikki Fritz, John Lazar, Michelle Bauer, Tim Abell, and Jay Richardson. This movie is a parody of "Attack of the 50 Foot Woman".

[edit] Plot

Spoiler warning: Plot and/or ending details follow.

The finalists for the Plaything Centerfold of the Month are chosen between Inga (Raelyn Saalman), Betty (Tammy Parks) and Angel Grace (J.J. North). Angel wants to win more than anything else in the world so she enlists the help of Dr. Lindstrom in a highly experimental "beauty enhancement program" which is tested on a rat. Although she was warned of a high risk that the potion might have a dangerous side effect, she took a case full of the experimental drug.

Angel was instructed to take one drink a day. Each time she drank one vial, her breasts would grow.

During the photo shoot, the effects of the beauty potion began to wear off. Angel decided to increase the dosage in hope of gaining an edge over the competition. Later that night, something strange happened to her when she took another vial. Her face started stretching into something hideous, then retracted and then she fainted.

Next morning, it worked but then she felt strange as the side effects of the beauty potion took over. Her body began to expand. Her clothes ripped as she kept growing and growing to a gorgeous giant who was 60-feet tall. She was now nude and she went to show her friends. The problem was that there were no clothes large enough fit her, so she got a giant white shroud.

The founder of Plaything magazine plotted to exploit her for money and turn her over to the government for experimentation. The shy photographer's assistant fell in love with the colossal blonde and became determined to protect her.

Her spiteful rival Betty (Tammy Parks) finds out Angel's secret, gets a giant black shroud, steals one of the vials and takes the formula herself, growing to an equally large giant. The two behemoths then battle it out in Hollywood for position as Centerfold of the Month. As the two gorgeous giants trash most of L.A. in the process, Dr. Lindstrom races to find a cure. When he finds it, he tests the gas on the rat. The shrinking is successful. The assistant then sprays the girls with the gas in a machine gun-like sprayer while they are fighting downtown. Angel and Betty were amazed by what was happening to them. As they shrunk, their clothes didn't. So once they were back to normal size, they had their huge shrouds wrapped around their bare bodies. Then, off-screen, Betty explodes because she did not have the proper stabilizing formula.

[edit] Merchandise

Strangely enough for a B-movie, Attack of the 60 Foot Centerfold actually spawned a small number of movie memorabilia, including its own trading card set.

When the film was released on Laserdisc, it contained a number of extras, including film tests, and even a commentary by the director.

A limited edition of 100, uncut trading card sheets, signed by J J North, were also put out for sale.
