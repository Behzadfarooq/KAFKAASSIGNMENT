Attack of the Killer B's is the name of a compilation album of B-sides, covers and rarities by the thrash metal band Anthrax. The album was released in June of 1991 by Megaforce Worldwide/Island Entertainment. The band produced the album alone, which includes the single, "Bring the Noise". Attack of the Killer B's was certified gold by the RIAA. These two live tracks of "Keep It In The Family" and "Belly Of The Beast" were recorded live in 1990 of the Persistence Of Time tour. Also included one remix of "I'm The Man" and released 3-songs from the 1989 EP of the State Of Euphoria sessions Penikufesin. Included on the album is the song "Startin' Up A Posse", which attacks the PMRC.

"Bring the Noise"was a collaboration with legendary rappers Public Enemy, technically making this song the first rap-metal collaboration. The song appears on the skateboard-game Tony Hawk's Pro Skater 2.
