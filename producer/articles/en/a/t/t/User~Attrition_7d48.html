Attrition, known outside of the internet as Andrew Runciman, is a 20 year-old engineering student currently residing in Kingsport, Tennessee. His contributions to Wikipedia have been limited, but reading Wikipedia is an activity that consumes a vast portion of his generally limited free time.

[edit] Background

k4k4k4k4k4, 1 w45 4 n00b.

Andrew Christopher Runciman was born in Myrtle Beach, South Carolina on May 27th, 1986. His father, Thomas Runciman, was an RN working for the local hospital, his mother, Kristina, was a copy editor for Waccamaw Corp. On March 9th, 1988, Andrew's only sibling, Katie Runciman, was born.

Andrew attended preschool at a local church, and then kindergarden and the first grade at the area's public elementary school. In 1992, Kristina started her own business, at the time called Lifeforce Publishing, which manufactured coupon books to give to others as gifts, with redeemable offers of the giver's time or energy. Working from home, Kristina was able to homeschool Andrew and Katie, and did so for a number of years. In 1994, Andrew's family moved to Winter Haven, Florida, due to his father's recieving a better job there. Andrew and the rest of his family hated living in Winter Haven, and a year later moved to St. Augustine, Florida. The family took to St. Augustine much better and stayed there until 1997, when the house Andrew's parents were renting was bought by someone incredibly obnoxious and they couldn't find another house that was affordable without being in the suburbs. After his father was offered a job by Blue Cross and Blue Shield Association, Andrew's family moved to Pensacola, Florida. In 2000, Kristina's business became successful enough for it to move out of the house. It became incorporated under the new name of Lifeforce Glass, Inc., reflecting the company's recent change to glass products, which were significantly more successful than the paper ones. Around this time, Andrew's father, Tom, quit his job at Blue Cross to work at Lifeforce.

Rocking the mutton chops.

In 2002, Andrew stoped homeschooling and enrolled at the Pensacola Private School of Liberal Arts. He graduated Class President with a 3.92 GPA in 2004. The following Fall he began attending Pensacola Junior College with the intention of being an art major. The next semester he switched to the University of West Florida. In the Summer of 2005, Andrew went on a two week school trip to Italy. After the long period of time he had to reflect during the numerous bus, plane, and boat rides, Andrew decided he would rather be an engineer than an artist. In 2006, Andrew's parents decided to move to Tennessee following Hurricane Ivan's near destruction of Lifeforce Glass's new office to be. After months of difficult construction work by the whole family repairing the building and a number of other hurricanes following Ivan, a location with less extreme weather seemed better for Lifeforce Glass's new office.

The man, the legend.

[edit] Present, Future

Needing to get out of Pensacola and having no intention of living in Tennessee for any longer than the summer, Andrew has applied to Waterloo University and the University of Central Florida. Andrew was rejected from Waterloo (:`( ), and is currently arranged to start going to UCF in the fall. Currently he is in Kingsport, working pretty much all of the time and occasionally goes out to try and find intelligent life in a sea of redneck assholes. So far he has not been successful in his search.
