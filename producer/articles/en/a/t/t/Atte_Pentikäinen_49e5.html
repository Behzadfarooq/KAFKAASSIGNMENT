Atte Pentikäinen (born December 12, 1982, in Finland), is a professional ice hockey defender. He is currently a defenseman for the Färjestads BK in the Swedish Elite League. Before he signed with Färjestad in September 2006 did he play with Tappara (2003-04) and Ässät (2004-06) in the Finnsh SM-Liiga .
