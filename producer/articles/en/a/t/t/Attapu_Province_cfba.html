Attapu (also Attopeu, Lao ອັດຕະປື) is a province of Laos, located in the south-east of the country.

[edit] Administrative divisions

The province is made up of the following districts:

  1. Phouvong (17-05)
  2. Samakkhixay (17-02)
  3. Sanamxay (17-03)
  4. Sanxay (17-04)
  5. Xaysetha (17-01)
