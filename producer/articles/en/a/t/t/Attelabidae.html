The Attelabidae or leaf-rolling weevils are a widespread family of weevils. There are more than 2000 species. The phylogenetic position of the family within the Curculionoidea based on 18S ribosomal DNA and morphological data is suggested as below:[1]


Nemonychidae


Anthribidae




Belidae



Attelabidae



Caridae



Brentidae


Curculionidae







Members of this family have long necks and some species are known as Giraffe weevils. A few species are minor agricultural pests.
