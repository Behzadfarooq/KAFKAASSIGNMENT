I'm from Rome, Italy, but spending much time in my sea house in Gaeta, Southern Latium. I'm currently degreeing in automation engineering, and I wrote several horror-fantastic short stories... still searching for somebody to publish them.

I'm interested in many (maybe too many?) fields. These include:

  • History (favourite characters: Frederick II of Sicily, sigismondo Pandolfo Malatesta)
  • Art (favourite painter: Giorgio De Chirico)
  • Astronomy (favourite planet: Neptune; favourite star: Zeta Reticuli)
  • Cinema (favourites: Stanley Kubrick, Sergio Leone, Nicole Kidman, Naomi Watts)
  • Comics (favourite artist: Kevin Maguire, Adam Hughes, John Bolton)
  • Music (favourites: progressive rock and Italian singers like Francesco Guccini, Angelo Branduardi, Franco Battiato, Roberto Vecchioni)
  • Science fiction and, in general, fantastic literature (favourites: Jorge Luis Borges, Gabriel Garcia Marquez, Josè Saramago)
