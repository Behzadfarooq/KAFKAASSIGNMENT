"Attached At The Hip" is a song by CKY released in 2003 on the album Infiltrate•Destroy•Rebuild.

[edit] Music video

A video was directed by Bam Margera and features the band playing together in various places, including outside near a hole which used to be a beer mill somewhere near Philladelphia, and Bam's basement. The video shows no connection between the lyrics of the song.
