"Attack of the Mousers" is the third episode of the animated series Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles (2003), which originally aired on February 22, 2003.

[edit] Voice-Over Introduction

Michelangelo: A very wise frog once said, "It's not easy being green," but believe me, he didn't know the half of it. In the past 48 hours my brothers and I have been attacked by killer robots, had our home caved in on us, and had a serious butt-kicking ninja battle with some deadly ninja assassins.

We made a shell raising escape in an armored car, found a new place to live...

Donatello: This is beyond awesome!

Michelangelo: ...and were attacked by even more killer robots. Oh, and we also managed to perform our very first ninja rescue. This is the closest any of us have ever been to a human being, and it raises one very big question:

Michelangelo: Can we keep her?

[edit] Plot Synopsis

After fainting from their initial encounter, April O'Neil wakes up in the Turtles' lair. It is here April learns of the Turtles' origin, and they all agree that Baxter Stockman and his rampaging Mousers must be stopped.

[edit] Miscellanea

  • The two cops in this episode are modeled after series creators Kevin Eastman and Peter Laird.
  • Michelangelo said the same thing about April O'Neil in the first epsiode of the 1987 series.
  • When the turtles are caught in Stockman's factory, the alarms and doors are triggered by Stockman pressing a button. His hand has white (caucasian) skin when he presses the button, although he is of Afro-American ethnicity.
