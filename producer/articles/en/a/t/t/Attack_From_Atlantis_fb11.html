Attack From Atlantis is a Science fiction paperback book created and written by author Lester del Rey in 1953. The story takes place underwater involving the new Triton submarine on her maiden voyage. While trouble surrounds them they come face to face with the inhabitants of the underwater city Atlantis.

[edit] Book Synopsis

  • 'Undersea Quest' - It was an official test run...nothing dangerous, nothing unplanned. Just a chance to show the brass and the press how the new atomic sub Triton took the depths. Don Miller was thrilled to be aboard. It was the chance of a lifetime, and he wouldn't have missed it for the world. But at 500 Fathoms, there was trouble with the tanks; at 700, there was even more...and there was a limit to the pressure even a sub like the Triton could take. Suddenly it became obvious that things were not happening accidentally, that at a depth never before possible for a man to reach, the Triton was being sabotaged!
