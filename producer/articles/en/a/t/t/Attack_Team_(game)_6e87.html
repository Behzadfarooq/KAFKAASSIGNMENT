Attack Team (AT) is a single player computer RPG set in a fictional war in North America in 1999. You play as Sergeant Sam Jones, a member of AT (attack team) 7 under leadership of Master Sergeant Brookbank.

[edit] Gameplay

In the game there are 3 ground locations LA , Washington, D.C and NYC. Two air locations: airspace over NYC and Mt Rushmore. Also there is a ship raid on a ship near LA harbor this level involves 4 missions :

Sailing to the ship

Storming the ship

Explore the Bridge

Evacuating The Ship

Throughout the game you work as a team sometimes taking orders sometimes giving them. In one mission you work undercover although you are not with the rest of your team they are in contact with you over the radio and storm the building at the end of the mission. Throughout the course of the game new weapons amour and Non-playable characters are available.

At the end of the game after the death of Master Sergeant Brookbank you are given the rank of Master Sergeant. At this point the president sends you to area 51 for special assignment.

[edit] Extra Facts

The game was for unknown reasons not popular and only sold at all in the USA.

The original price off $29.99 was dropped to $9.99 after a month. After a further 3 months most stores took the games off the shelves and returned them to the distributer.
