Attero Dominatus is the third album by Swedish power metal band Sabaton.

As with the Primo Victoria album, the lyrics on Attero Dominatus deals with different war subjects. "Nuclear Attack" deals with the bombing of atomic bombings of Hiroshima and Nagasaki, "Rise of Evil" with the rise of the third reich and Adolf Hitler, "In the Name of God" about Osama bin Laden. The song "Back in Control" deals with the Falklands War.

A video was shot for the titletrack in Umeå, Sweden done by Nocturnal Rites' drummer Owe Lingvall.

  1. "Attero Dominatus" – 3:43 - About the Battle of Berlin.
  2. "Nuclear Attack" – 4:10 - About Hiroshima and Nagasaki.
  3. "Rise of Evil" – 8:19 - About the Third Reich and Adolf Hitler.
  4. "In the Name of God" – 4:06 - About the War on terror and Osama bin Laden
  5. "We Burn" – 2:55 - About the Yugoslavian civil war
  6. "Angels Calling" – 5:57 - About World war one.
  7. "Back in Control" – 3:14 - About the Falklands war.
  8. "Light in the Black" – 4:52 - About the Vietnam war.
  9. "Metal Crüe" – 3:42 - About heavy metal, it is built up enterily by famous band names such like Queen, In Flames, Iron Maiden, Accept, Venom, etc.
