Attack Records (UK)

Attack Records ran originally from 1969 to 1980 as an imprint of Trojan Records. Notable artists included The Pioneers, Gregory Isaacs, I-Roy, Big Youth and The Upsetters.

In 2003 it was revived for British singer Morrissey and the label Sanctuary Records, to become his vanity label. Current artists on the Attack roster include Nancy Sinatra, Jobriath, James Maker and of course, Morrissey. Morrissey's 2004 album, You Are the Quarry has sold in excess of 700,000 copies for the label.
