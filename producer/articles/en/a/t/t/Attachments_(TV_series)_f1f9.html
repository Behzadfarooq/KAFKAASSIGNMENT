Attachments is a BBC TV Series that ran for two seasons from 2000 to 2002, a total of 26 episodes. It focuses on a group of young professionals in London that work for an Internet startup company called "seethru" during the dot com boom.[1] The fictional company ran an internet portal website at seethru.co.uk updated as the show progressed, and still exists today. The show was criticised for being too offensive[2]. While the show was originally released on VHS, there are no mentions that it is to be released on DVD.

[edit] Characters

Seethru was started by Mike (Justine Pierre) and his wife Luce (Claudia Harrison). Other major characters include Jake (David Walliams), the site designer, Sophie (Amanda Ryan), the content writer and Brandon (Iddo Goldberg), the nerdy technology expert.[3]
