"Attack of the Radioactive Hamsters from a Planet near Mars" is a song by "Weird Al" Yankovic. It is a comic, albeit darkly comic, tale of giant hamsters invading the Earth in UFOs shaped like Cuban cigars. The narrator states that, although "They're gonna stomp us into jelly and conquer the world... You gotta admit, they're really kinda cute."

[edit] Trivia

  • According to the song they are so large that one's "As big as a blimp", and one is "The size of Central Park".
  • Supposedly the hamsters aren't very fond of Burger King salad bars.
  • According to the narrator, the hamster maliciously keep him up playing their electric guitars all night.
