The word attic has various meanings:

  • An attic is an area above a house.
  • Attic (always capitalised) is an adjective for something or someone coming from or associated with Attica or Athens, for example:
    • The Attic dialect of the Greek language.
    • The ten Attic orators, ancient Athenian speechwriters.
    • The Attic style refers to a storey or low wall above the cornice of a classical façade.
  • Attic Entertainment Software, a defunct German computer game developer and publisher.
  • Attic Records (Canada), a record label.
  • Attic Records (UK), a record label.
