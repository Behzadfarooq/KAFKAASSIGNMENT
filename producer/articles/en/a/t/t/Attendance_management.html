Attendance management

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

You have new messages (last change).

Attendance management is the act of managing and or controlling attendance or presence in a worksetting to minimize loss due to employee downtime.

Attendance management is managed by several means including

  • Time clocks
  • Timesheets
  • Time tracking software
