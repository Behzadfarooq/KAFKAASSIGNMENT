If wikipedia and or you the user disagree I understand and will have them removed from the article, as I do not want to hurt the integrity of wikipedia.

It's good to have an article about short attention span (the condition) and about short attention span (the band). But it's not good to have one article about both. It's a real encyclopedia: one subject, one article. You can write an article about each, but you'd probably run into two problems. For the former (the condition), you'd have to thoroughly make references to reputable sources. The wikipedia science community really doesn't mess around. Also I think an attention span article already exists. For the latter (the band), you'd probably have a difficult establishing notability. There's like a million bands, what makes SAS notable? If you think you can do both, you should definitely give it a shot. You'll find plenty of people willing to help. -Taco325i 22:35, 11 January 2007 (UTC)
