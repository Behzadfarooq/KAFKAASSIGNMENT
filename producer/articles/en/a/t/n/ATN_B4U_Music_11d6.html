ATN B4U Music is a Canadian category 2 Hindi music digital cable specialty channel that is owned by Asian Television Network International Limited. It broadcasts programming from B4U Music as well as Canadian content. Programming consists of music from Bollywood movies and other music videos, interviews, request shows, and much more.
