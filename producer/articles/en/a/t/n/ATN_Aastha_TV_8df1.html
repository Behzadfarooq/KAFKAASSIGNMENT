ATN Aastha is a Canadian category 2 digital cable specialty channel that is owned by Asian Television Network International Limited (ATN). ATN Aastha broadcasts programming from Aastha TV as well as Canadian content.

The channel features religious and spiritual programming in Hindi, Gujarati, and English, focusing mainly on the teachings and principles of Hinduism. This programming includes discourses, devotional music, meditation and yoga techniques, coverage of religious festivals, and ancient Indian scientific practices such as ayurveda.
