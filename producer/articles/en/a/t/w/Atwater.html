Atwater is the name of several places in the United States of America:

  • Atwater, California, a city in Merced County, California
  • Atwater Village, Los Angeles, California, a neighborhood of the city of Los Angeles in Los Angeles County, California
  • Atwater, Georgia
  • Atwater, Illinois
  • Atwater, Minnesota
  • Atwater, Ohio
  • Atwater, Wisconsin

Atwater is also:

  • Lee Atwater, a U.S. Republican political strategist.
  • Atwater, a station on the Green Line of the Montreal Metro
  • Atwater Avenue, a street in Montreal
  • Atwater Library[1], an independent library in Montreal operated by the Mechanics' Institute of Montreal
  • Atwater Market, a public market in Montreal
  • Edwin Atwater (1808–1874), a municipal alderman in Montreal for the district of Saint-Antoine.
  • Steve Atwater, former Denver Broncos and New York Jets Free Safety.
