At3g04890

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

In the plant Arabidopsis thaliana, At3g04890 is a gene locus for which there is one gene model, At3g04890.1.

[edit] Properties

The gene product has 217 amino-acid residues and an estimated molecular weight of 24432.0. It is a relatively neutral protein, with a isoelectric point pI of 7.2.

[edit] Localization

Computational predictions suggest that it is targeted to the chloroplast. Based on its alignment with cyanobacterial homologs, its transit peptide is roughly 70 residues long.

[edit] Function

This expressed protein is currently of unknown function.
