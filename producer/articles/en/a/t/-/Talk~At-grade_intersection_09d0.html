[edit] From Texas & NM DOTs

I received this e-mail from the Texas Department of Transportation. I have not yet heard from the New Mexico DOT:

Thank you for your question. There are no freeway frontage roads on I-40 in Texas from the New Mexico state line to about 20 miles into Texas. There are several ranches in this area, and they have direct access to the freeway. The answer to your question is, yes, we do have at-grade crossings along I-40 in West Texas. I hope this helps.

Rt66lt 14:39, 26 January 2006 (UTC)

Yes, there exist at-grade crossings in West Texas, but this one might be in New Mexico, especially when the photographer says he took it in New Mexico, assuming good faith. There is another photo of an identical at-grade crossing taken by a different photographer who also claims he took it in New Mexico -- [2] from [3]. See Image talk:Grade intersection on I-40.jpg. There is also the possibility of the at-grade crossings in West Texas looking similar to those in New Mexico. -- Paddu 03:59, 31 January 2006 (UTC)

Do you know if the sign shown in the attached picture exists on our stretch of I-40? I’ve never seen it. Please advise. Thanks!

[edit] Old photo?

I wonder if this sign existed earlier but was then removed. This seems to be consistent with the 2000/2003 Usenet threads and photographers talking about the sign in NM and the Wikipedians and the people from the DOTs who say this doesn't exist in NM in 2005-6. -- Paddu 07:21, 22 March 2006 (UTC)

As a reference, here are the links to the various opinions:
  • 2000 Usenet thread and 2003 Usenet thread saying it is in NM.
  • Image talk:Grade intersection on I-40.jpg and User talk:Alanhwiki -- the photographer of this photo saying he took the photo in NM. His webpage says these photos were taken between October 2003-present. He uploaded the image into Wikipedia in May 2005 so the photo was taken between October 2003 and May 2005.
  • Another photographer saying he took a similar photo in NM, in June 2003.
  • Image talk:Grade intersection on I-40.jpg and this page -- User:Rt66lt saying he/she has NOT see this sign in NM but did see an identical one in TX, since August 2005.
  • this page -- NMDOT saying this sign is NOT in NM, in February 2006.
  • User talk:Paddu/Archive1#At-Grade Crossings -- User:Camerafiend saying he/she has NOT seen this sign near Albuquerque, in February 2006.
