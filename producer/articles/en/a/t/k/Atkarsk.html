Atkarsk (Russian: Аткарск) is a town in Saratov Oblast, Russia, located at the confluence of the Atkara and Medveditsa Rivers, 92 km northwest of Saratov. Population: 27,907 (2002 Census); 28,877 (1989 Census).

It was founded in place of a Tatar settlement at the mouth of the Atkar River. Town status was granted to it in 1780.
