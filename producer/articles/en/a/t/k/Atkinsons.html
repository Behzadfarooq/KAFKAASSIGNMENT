Atkinsons

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Atkinsons is a family-owned department store located on The Moor in Sheffield, England which sells retail items of clothing, furnishings, lightings, gifts, jewellery, toys, cookers and televisions. Atkinsons has been running for 130 years, and recently as of 2004 has seen an extension to the store occupying more space. The department store also includes a restaurant and café.
