Atrium on Bay

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

North West Corner of Yonge and Dundas in 1926 - Future site of Atrium on Bay

The Atrium on Bay is a retail and office complex on the former site of the Ford Hotel on Dundas Street West, extending from Yonge Street to Bay Street. The 14-floor building was built in 1981 with two floors of retail stores. As part of the PATH network, it has underground connections to Dundas subway station and the Toronto Bus Terminal.

A major tenant of the Atrium is CIBC.
