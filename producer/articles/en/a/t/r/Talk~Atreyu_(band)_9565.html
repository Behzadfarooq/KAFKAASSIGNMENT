[edit] Atreyu

A semi-new band from Orange Country, California. Their music from the newest album really reminds me of oldschool Metallica. They have a very unique sound that few bands seem to obtain which combines 3 genre's together consisting of hardcore,emo,and metal. Made up of Alex Varkatzas, Brandon Saller, Dan Jacobs, and Marc McKnight. Currently signed to Victory records, they have two released EP's(Visions, and Fractures in the Facade of your Porcelain Beauty), and three full-length albums(Suicide Notes & Butterfly Kisses, The Curse, A Deathgrip on Yesterday). This is one of the few bands that are good enough to make me headbang like they do, while keeping a good set of singing mixed with awesome drum and guitar play. I suggest this band to anyone into metal/hardcore. —The preceding unsigned comment was added by Gotron (talk • contribs) .
