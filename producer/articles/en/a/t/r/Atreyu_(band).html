Atreyu is a metalcore band, consisting of vocalist Alex Varkatzas, guitarists Dan Jacobs and Travis Miguel, bassist Marc McKnight and drummer/vocalist Brandon Saller. The band was originally named "Retribution" but later changed their name to "Atreyu" after the character from The Neverending Story of the same name. Atreyu was formerly on Victory Records, but in November 2006 signed with major record label Hollywood Records.

[edit] Former members

  • Kyle Stanley – bass guitar (1997/1999-2000(?); played on Visions), played in A Storybook Ending, Hideki and The Electric Youth.
  • Brian O'Donnell – bass guitar (1998/1999-2001(?); played on Visions).
  • Chris Thompson – bass guitar (2001 – 2003; played on Suicide Notes and Butterfly Kisses), now plays in the band Cold War.
