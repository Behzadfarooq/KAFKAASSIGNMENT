Atco is a small unincorporated community on the southwestern side of Cartersville, Georgia, USA. It is located in the southern end of Bartow County. The population is estimated at around 700. There are numerous baseball and soccer complexes in the area, primarily along Sugar Valley and Cassville Roads, making it a popular destination for subdivisions.
