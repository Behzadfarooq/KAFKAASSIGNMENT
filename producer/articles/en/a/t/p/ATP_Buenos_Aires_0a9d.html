The ATP Buenos Aires (Spanish Abierto de Buenos Aires) is an annual tennis event for male tennis players held in Buenos Aires, Argentina. The tournament is an International Series tennis event on the Association of Tennis Professionals (ATP) tour and it is played on outdoor clay courts at the 5,500 capacity Buenos Aires Lawn Tennis Club, in the Palermo neighbourhood. Usually heald in February, it includes both a men's singles and a men's doubles tournament.

Its sponsored name is currently Copa Telmex. Previously the event was known as South American Open and Copa AT&T.
