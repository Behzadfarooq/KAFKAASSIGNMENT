Atonement is a 2007 film directed by Joe Wright. It is adapted from Ian McEwan's novel of the same name. It stars Keira Knightley and James McAvoy in the lead roles. The film is scheduled to be released in North America on December 7, 2007.

[edit] Filming Locations

  • Redcar, England
  • Grimsby, England (the docks area).
  • London, England
  • Shropshire, England

The above four are all confirmed filming locations; it is unclear whether another English filming location is expected.
