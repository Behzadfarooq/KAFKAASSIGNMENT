[edit] About Me

AtomicFire, aka Atomic, aka Chris Daly, is a chemistry student who resides in the UK. Born in Chester, England, he currently splits his time between his home in Hope, North Wales and Leicester, England.

He is currently studying for a Masters (MChem) degree at the University of Leicester, and in his spare time he practices Seishin Mizu Ryu Jiu Jitsu under Jade Banks, Sensei.
