The Hill-Soto Theory on Time Travel

At the time of this writing mankind has made tremendous advances in all of the Sciences. Researchers, Engineers and Scientist are constantly breaking new ground in the areas of science that pertains to Computer Technology.

As a member of the general public and only having access to mainstream information systems, i.e. Mass Media and the Internet I have come to the realization that at some point in our very near future Humans gain the ability to travel at will through time using technology unknown to most. I am theorizing that we are on the outskirts of a technological breakthrough that ultimately will change the future and quite possibly continue to rewrite our past until we get right the information needed to get us to the next level of our evolution.

I have recently been exposed to information that at first glance appears to be subtle and non-threatening almost elusive or transparent until you look at the entire pool of information all the bits and pieces start to fit together like clues or puzzle pieces just waiting to be combined to expose itself in its entirety.

The first thing I noticed is often I feel like I am receiving a subliminal message in the form of numerous amounts of information from many sources. All of the facts point toward our ability to achieve anything we put our minds to. If we can dream it: We can achieve it, would probably best sum it up. I have been looking at this vast amount of information and how it fits together in awe and I believe we will one day fulfill our destiny as set forth by our ancestors from the far future in the past. Stay with me now…


Which came first, the future or the past?

They have gone by and no longer exist accept in our minds and recorded documentations. We can reasonably control our futures even though we are not guaranteed one.
