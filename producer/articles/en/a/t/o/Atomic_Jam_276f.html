Atomic Jam is the largest and one of the most influential Techno events in the United Kingdom.

The event was founded in Birmingham UK, in 1995 and the first event was at the Que Club, Birmingham in August of that year. Richie Hawtin was headlining the event..

Every major techno DJ on the planet has performed including Carl Cox, Jeff Mills, Derrick May, Dave Clarke, and DJ Rush have performed for Atomic Jam.

Residents are:

Chris Finke Ade Fenton Ratty Fallout

The event takes place in various venues across the Birmingham area and showcases other cities across Europe.
