Atopia (film studio)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Atopia is an independent film company based in Montréal, Canada.

Since its inception in 2000, Atopia has produced a string of critically-acclaimed feature films including: S.P.I.T.: Squeegee Punks In Traffic (2001), A Silent Love (2003), Jimmywork (2004) and Sur la trace d'Igor Rizzi (2006) programmed at major film festivals such as Sundance, Toronto and Venice. Since 2004, Atopia has been operating as a fully integrated company, handling projects from development all the way through to worldwide sales and theatrical distribution in North America. Atopia's films, shot around the world in various languages, are reflective of today’s global awareness and sensibilities.

Atopia's distribution branch recently acquired North American rights to a string of feature length theatrical documentaries including Panache (2006) and The Short Life of José Antonio Gutierrez (2006).
