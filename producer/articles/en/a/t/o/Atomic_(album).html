Atomic was Lit's third album, released on October 16, 2001.It peaked at #36 on The billboard top 200

[edit] Bonus CD

There was a limited edition release of Atomic that contained three tracks of bands from Lit's Dirty Martini label.

  1. Lit - "Down (Acoustic)" – 3:46
  2. Handsome Devil - "Makin' Money" (clean version) (Walker) – 3:30
  3. The Color Red - "Sore Throat" (demo version) (Meyer, Schartoff, The Color Red, Verloop, J. Zamora, M. Zamora) – 5:52
