Atokos (Greek: Άτοκος), is a Greek island in the Echinades. It is administered by the municipality of Ithaca and is northeast of the island.

[edit] Nearest islands and islets

  • Ithaca, southwest
  • Arkoudi, northwest
  • Kalamos, east-northeast
  • Kastos, northeast
