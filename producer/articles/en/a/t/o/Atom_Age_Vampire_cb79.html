Atom Age Vampire (aka Seddok, l'erede di Satana) is a 1963 Italian science fiction film directed by Anton Giulio Majano and starring Alberto Lupo. When a singer is horribly disfigured in a car accident a scientist comes up with a treatment which can restore her beauty by injecting her with the glands of a murdered woman. While performing the treatment though he falls in love with her. Despite the title, the film has little to do with the so called atom age.

This film is now one of many which are considered to be in the public domain and as such is freely available to download legally from the Internet.
