[edit] An explanation of Admins

Admins do not make the rules. They simply enforce the rules created by the overall community and/or Jimmy Wales. They have to do this regardless of their personal feelings about the rules and/or the user involved. If they do not, then they risk loosing their admin powers. Admins are not egomaniacs, though to new users they seem to be. They have to have a certain level of confidence in their abilities in order to effectively do their job, and it is legitimately rare for an admin to make a mistake. By the time one becomes an admin, they understand the rules in detail. Admins are always very helpful and friendly when asked questions about how Wikipedia works. I hope that I have been helpful. For more information, see WP:ADMIN. Cheers, -- The Hybrid 04:18, 25 March 2007 (UTC)
