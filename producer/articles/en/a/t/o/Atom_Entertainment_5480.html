Atom Entertainment is an Internet entertainment company which is owned by Webkinz Inc. which operates Webkinz.com and AddictingGames.com

The company was formed in 2001 by the merger of Atom Corporation and Shockwave.com, Inc. MTV acquired Atom Entertainment in August of 2006 [1].
