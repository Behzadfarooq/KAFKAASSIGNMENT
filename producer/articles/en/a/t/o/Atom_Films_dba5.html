Atom Films

Atom Films is a broadband entertainment network offering original short subject films, animations, and series by independent creators. Hit AtomFilms titles include Angry Kid, Star Wars fan films, JibJab's This Land, I Can't Afford My Gasoline, and many more. AtomFilms is available on the Internet at www.atomfilms.com, and on Verizon's VCast mobile entertainment service.

[edit] Buyout

On August 10, 2006, Atom Films was bought by MTV Networks with all its properties, including Addicting Games, Addicting Clips and Shockwave.com.[1]
