Our site, atomicarchive.com is a National Science Foundation funded effort, and is not a 'commercial' site in the strict sense. -Chris Griffith

Thanks for taking the time to respond. However, links to a website that you own or maintain (unless it is the official site of the subject of the article) should not be included. If it is relevant and informative, mention it as a possible link on the talk page and wait for someone else to include it, or include the information directly in the article. For more information, please see Wikipedia' external link guidelines. NaconKantari e|t||c|m 22:47, 17 February 2006 (UTC)

  • I sympathize with you, in part because I know that atomicarchive is a good site with good links (much better than some of them which are on the pages).
