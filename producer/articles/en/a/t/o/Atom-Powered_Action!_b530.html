Atom-Powered Action! was the first Bis release on label mainstay Wiiija Records. It could be up to debate as to if this release is a single or an EP considering that "Starbright Boy" showed up on the New Transister Heroes album. It was released on 7 inch vinyl, cassette tape and compact disc.

[edit] Tracks

  1. "Starbright Boy" (3:39)
  2. "Wee Love" (1:24)
  3. "Team Theme" (2:42)
  4. "Cliquesuck" (2:50)

[edit] Production

Recorded by: Jim Brady
Recorded at: Apollo Studios, Glasgow, Scotland in August of 1996.
