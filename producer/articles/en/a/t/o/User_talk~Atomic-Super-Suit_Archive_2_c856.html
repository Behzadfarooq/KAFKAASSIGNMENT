Hitchhiker's Guide articles

If you're going to make major changes, I would REALLY suggest joining the Wikipedia:WikiProject HHGTTG and discuss it with us, rather than making a bunch of changes unilaterally. The episode guide was forked off by a member of the British TV shows project, who eventually agreed with something I had said on the HHGTTG project talk page - namely that the episode guides by themselves really cannot stand alone in their own article (too short). Also, when you DO fork things off, please spell them correctly. Further, when asking about forking off things like Slartibartfast, it IS generally a good idea to WAIT and give people a chance to see what you've proposed, and VOTE on it. As I noted in an edit summary, one vote does NOT make a consensus. I'm all for being bold, but when there is a project organized about these things, at least come ask us? --JohnDBuell 02:48, 16 October 2006 (UTC)
