The Pole Vault at the 2004 Summer Olympics as part of the athletics program was held at the Athens Olympic Stadium August 21 and August 24.

Gold: Silver: Bronze:
Yelena Isinbayeva, Russia Svetlana Feofanova, Russia Anna Rogowska, Poland
