[edit] Athena Buensuceso

Athena Cabrera Buensuceso is a first year college student in the University of the Philippines, Diliman [1] taking up BS Psychology. She lives in Layac, Dinalupihan, Bataan. She was born to Mr. and Mrs. Benhur and Syncletica Buensuceso.

Buensuceso graduated valedictorian from Saint John Academy [2], Dinalupihan, Bataan. She had been involved in many activities during her high school years. Some of her achievements, and organizations in high school are as follows:

  • The Herald- Editor-in-chief
  • Student Council- 3rd year, vice president; 1st year, 2nd year representative
  • Science Club- President
  • Teatro San Juan- member
  • Math Club- member
  • Kulay Club- member
  • Regional Schools Press Conference (Copyreading and Headlining, English; Radioscript Writing and Broadcasting)
  • Division Schools Press Conference- 2nd place (Copyreading and Headlining, English)
  • Diocesan Schools Speech Fest- 1st place (Speech Choir, "The Cobbler and =The Rich Man")
  • Best in Science -4th year
  • Best in Math- 4th year
  • Best in Computer- 4th year
  • Journalist of the Year awardee- 4th year
  • JS Promemade '06- Ms. Senior
  • CSAT- percentile rank: 97

Buensuceso is a member of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints.
