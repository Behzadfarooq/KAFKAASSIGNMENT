Athletics at the 1908 Summer Olympics - Men's 400 metre hurdles

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

You have new messages (last change).

The men's 400 metres hurdles was the longer of two hurdling events at the 1908 Summer Olympics in London. It was the third time the event had been featured at the Olympics. The Olympic record was beat three times in the course of the Games.

Ten sets of hurdles were set on the course. The hurdles were 3 feet tall and were placed 35 metres apart beginning 45 metres from the starting line.

[edit] Second round

[edit] Second round, heat 1

Hillman won easily, becoming the second American to set a new Olympic record at the event. Koops fell on a hurdle and did not finish.

Rank Name Nation Time
1 Harry Hillman United States 56.4 seconds (OR)
2 H. L. Coe United States
DNF E. Koops Holland

[edit] Second round, heat 2

Bacon started off fast, and the other two runners gave up halfway through the race.

Rank Name Nation Time
1 Charles Bacon United States 58.8 seconds
DNF O. Groenings Great Britain and Ireland
DNF N. Kovacs Hungary

[edit] Second round, heat 3

Burton led after the hurdles, and finished first despite losing ground in the straight.

Rank Name Nation Time
1 Leslie Burton Great Britain and Ireland
2 F. W. Harmer Great Britain and Ireland
3 E. W. Gould Great Britain and Ireland

[edit] Second round, heat 4

Burton did not finish the race, allowing Tremeer to win with a jog.

Rank Name Nation Time
1 Leonard Tremeer Great Britain and Ireland 60.6
DNF G. Burton Great Britain and Ireland

[edit] Final

Halfway into the race, it was clear that the only question was which American would win. Bacon and Hillman were even all the way until the straight, with Bacon just barely pulling ahead. He won by less than two metres, as both of the Americans came in under the Olympic record set by Hillman in the second round.

Rank Name Nation Time
1 Charles Bacon United States 55.0 seconds (OR)
2 Harry Hillman United States 55.3 seconds
3 Leonard Tremeer Great Britain and Ireland 57.0 seconds
4 Leslie Burton Great Britain and Ireland 58.0 seconds
