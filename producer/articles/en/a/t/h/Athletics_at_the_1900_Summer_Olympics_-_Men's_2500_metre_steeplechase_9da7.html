Athletics at the 1900 Summer Olympics - Men's 2500 metre steeplechase

The men's 2,500 metre steeplechase was a track & field athletics event at the 1900 Summer Olympics in Paris. It was held on 15 July 1900. Six athletes from six nations competed in the shorter of the two steeplechase events.

The competition involved five laps of the 500 metre track, complete with standard hurdles as well as stone fences and a water jump.

Orton had been in fourth place for most of the race, ahead of only Wraschtil and Dühne. He passed the three leaders in the final straight of the fifth lap.
