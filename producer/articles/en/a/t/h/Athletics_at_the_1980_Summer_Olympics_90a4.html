Athletics at the 1980 Summer Olympics

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

You have new messages (last change).

Athletics at the 1980 Summer Olympics was represented by 38 events. They were held in the Grand Arena of the Central Lenin Stadium at Luzhniki (south-western part of Moscow) between July 24 and August 1.
