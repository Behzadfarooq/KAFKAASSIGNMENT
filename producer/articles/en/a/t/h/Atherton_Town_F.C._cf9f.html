Atherton Town F.C. is a football club based in Atherton, England. They were established in 1964 and was originally named Briarcroft Youth Club. They joined the Manchester Football League in 1987. For the 2006-07 season, they are members of the Manchester Football League Premier Division. Their home colours are yellow and green.
