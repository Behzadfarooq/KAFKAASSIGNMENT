The 1,500 metres at the 2004 Summer Olympics as part of the athletics program were held at the Athens Olympic Stadium August 20 to August 24.

[edit] Records

World Record 3:26.00 Hicham El Geurrouj Morocco Rome, Italy 14 July 1998
Olympic Record 3:32.07 Noah Ngeny Kenya Sydney, Australia 29 September 2000

[edit] Medals

Gold: Silver: Bronze:
Hicham El Guerrouj, Morocco Bernard Lagat, Kenya Rui Silva, Portugal


[edit] Event Summary

[edit] Qualifying

The first round comprised of three heats with the first five in each plus the next nine fastest losers progressing to the two semi-finals. Here the first five and the two fastest losers went to the final.
