Athy Rugby Club

Athy Rugby Football Club was founded in 1880 and plays its home games at The Showgrounds, Athy, County Kildare. The club currently play in the RE/MAX Leinster League Division 2 and field a 1st, 2nd and 3rd XV's as well as an U20's and youth teams from U18's to U8's.

[edit] Honours

The club has contested 15 Provincial Towns Cup finals, winning the coveted trophy on five occasions:

  • 1928 Runner-up
  • 1929 Runner-up
  • 1930 Runner-up
  • 1938 Winner
  • 1940 Winner
  • 1943 Runner-up
  • 1948 Runner-up
  • 1962 Runner-up
  • 1965 Runner-up
  • 1977 Runner-up
  • 1978 Winner
  • 1981 Winner
  • 1983 Runner-up
  • 1984 Winner
  • 1991 Runner-up
