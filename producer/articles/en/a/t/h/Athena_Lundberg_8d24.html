Athena Lundberg (born April 12, 1986) is an American model. She was Playboy Playmate of the Month for January 2006.

Lundberg has also been featured in other male-interest websites and products. She can be seen at the Rookie Babe website, in the Girls Gone Wild Island video, and on the cover of the GGW video, Daddy's Little Girls.
