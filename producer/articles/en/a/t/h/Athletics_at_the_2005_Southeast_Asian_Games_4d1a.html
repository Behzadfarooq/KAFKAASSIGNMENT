Athletics at the 2005 Southeast Asian Games

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Athletics (track and field) events at the 2005 Southeast Asian Games were held at the Rizal Memorial Sports Complex in Malate, Manila, Philippines. The Marathon event were ran from the Diosdado Macapagal Boulevard, Pasay City, Philippines.
