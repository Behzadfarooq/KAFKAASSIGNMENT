The 3,000 metres steeplechase at the 2004 Summer Olympics as part of the athletics program were held at the Athens Olympic Stadium.

[edit] Records

World Record 7:53.63 Saif Saaeed Shaheen Qatar Brussels, Belgium 03 September 2004
Olympic Record 8:05.52 Julius Kariuki Kenya Seoul, Korea 30 September 1988

[edit] Medals

Gold: Silver: Bronze:
Ezekiel Kemboi, Kenya Brimin Kipruto, Kenya Paul Kipsiele Koech, Kenya

Going into the event the top ten IAAF ranked athletes were:

  1. Saif Saaeed Shaheen
  2. Paul Kipsiele Koech
  3. Ezekiel Kemboi

[edit] Qualifying

From the initial three heats the first three in each plus six fastest losers progressed through to the final.
