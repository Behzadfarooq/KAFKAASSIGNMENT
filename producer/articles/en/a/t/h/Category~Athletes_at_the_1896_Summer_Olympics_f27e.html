This category includes those people who competed in athletics (track and field) at the 1896 Summer Olympics in Athens.


This category should only be used for Olympians participating in athletics (track and field).
All other Olympians should be categorized into Category:Competitors at the 1896 Summer Olympics (or an appropriate subcategory). Thanks!
