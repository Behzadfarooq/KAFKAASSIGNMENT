Atherinomorus is a genus of silversides in the family Atherinidae.

[edit] Species

  • Spatular-scale silverside, Atherinomorus aetholepis Kimura, Iwatsuki & Yoshino, 2002
  • Balabac Island silverside, Atherinomorus balabacensis (Seale, 1910)
  • Capricorn silverside, Atherinomorus capricornensis (Woodland, 1961)
  • Tropical silverside, Atherinomorus duodecimalis (Valenciennes, 1835)
  • Eendracht Land silverside, Atherinomorus endrachtensis (Quoy & Gaimard, 1825)
  • Hawaiian Islands silverside, Atherinomorus insularum (Jordan & Evermann, 1903)
  • Hardyhead silverside, Atherinomorus lacunosus (Forster, 1801)
  • Lined silverside, Atherinomorus lineatus (Günther, 1872)
  • Culion silverside, Atherinomorus regina (Seale, 1910)
  • Hardhead silverside, Atherinomorus stipes (Müller & Troschel, 1848)
  • Ogilby's hardyhead, Atherinomorus vaigiensis (Quoy & Gaimard, 1825)
