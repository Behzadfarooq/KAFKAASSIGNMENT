The 5,000 metres at the 2004 Summer Olympics as part of the athletics program were held at the Athens Olympic Stadium on August 25 and August 28.

[edit] Records

World Record 12:37.35 Kenenisa Bekele Ethiopia Hengelo, Netherlands 31 May 2004
Olympic Record 13:05.59 Saïd Aouita Morocco Los Angeles, USA 11 August 1984

[edit] Medals

Gold: Silver: Bronze:
Hicham El Guerrouj, Morocco Kenenisa Bekele, Ethiopia Eliud Kipchoge, Kenya
