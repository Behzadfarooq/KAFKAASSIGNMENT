The 20 km walk at the 2004 Summer Olympics as part of the athletics program was held on the streets of Athens with the start and finish at the Athens Olympic Stadium.

[edit] Records

World Record 1:17:21 Jefferson Pérez Ecuador Saint-Denis, France 23 August 2003
Olympic Record 1:18:59 Robert Korzeniowski Poland Sydney, Australia 22 September 2000
