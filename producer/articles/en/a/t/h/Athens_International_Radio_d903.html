Athens International Radio (AIR 104.4 FM) is an Athens radio station aiming at a non-Greek speaking listenership. It broadcasts on 104.4 FM in thirteen languages (not simultaneously), including English, French, German, Spanish, Italian, Russian, Arabic, Albanian, Polish, Bulgarian, Romanian, and Tagalog.

The station produces more than than ten hours of locally-produced programmes daily, consisting of news, music, traffic, weather, chat, comment and entertainment tips.

The station broadcasts content from the BBC World Service, Radio France Internationale and Deutsche Welle.

Athens International Radio is a project of Athena 98.4 FM.
