The official results of the Women's Marathon at the 1988 Summer Olympics in Seoul, South Korea, held on Friday September 23, 1988. The race started on 09.30h local time. A total number of 64 athletes completed the race, with Mariana Ysrael from Guam finishing in last position in 3:42:23. There were 69 competitors from 39 countries. Five of them did not finish.
