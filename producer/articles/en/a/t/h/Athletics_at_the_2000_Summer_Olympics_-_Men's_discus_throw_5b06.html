[edit] Finals

Each thrower was given three throws. The eight best in those three received another three throws, keeping all six.

Frantz Kruger took an early lead, until Lars Riedel, then Virgilijus Alekna raised it in the third round. Alekna never lost his lead, even extending it in the 5th round.
