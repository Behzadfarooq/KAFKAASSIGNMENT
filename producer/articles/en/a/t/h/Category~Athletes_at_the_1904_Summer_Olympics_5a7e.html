This category includes those people who competed in track and field athletics at the 1904 Summer Olympics in Paris.


This category should only be used for Olympians participating in athletics (i.e., track and field).
All other Olympians should be categorized into Category:Competitors at the 1904 Summer Olympics (or an appropriate subcategory). Thanks!
