Athletics at the 1988 Summer Olympics - Men's 20 km walk

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Men's 20 km Race Walk at the 1988 Summer Olympics in Seoul, South Korea had an entrylist of 53 competitors. Three athletes were disqualified, while one walker did not finish the race, held on Friday September 23, 1988.
