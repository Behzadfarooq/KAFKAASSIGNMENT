Athirson Mazzoli e Oliveira (born January 16, 1977) is a Brazilian football player (left back). He was born in Rio de Janeiro and has played most of his career at Flamengo. He has also played for Santos FC, Juventus and Cruzeiro. He is currently playing for Bayer Leverkusen.

He has played 17 times for the Brazilian National Team, having scored 2 goals. He was in the squad of 1997 FIFA World Youth Championship.
