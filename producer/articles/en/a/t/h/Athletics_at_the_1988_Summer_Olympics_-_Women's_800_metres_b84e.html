Athletics at the 1988 Summer Olympics - Women's 800 metres

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

You have new messages (last change).

The Women's 800m at the 1988 Summer Olympics in Seoul, South Korea had an entrylist of 32 competitors, with four qualifying heats (32) and two semi-finals (16), before the final (8) took off on Monday September 26, 1988.
