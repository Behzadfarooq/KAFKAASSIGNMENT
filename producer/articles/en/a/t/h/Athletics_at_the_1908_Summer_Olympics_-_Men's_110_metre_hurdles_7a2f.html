Athletics at the 1908 Summer Olympics - Men's 110 metre hurdles

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The men's 110 metres hurdles was the shorter of two hurdling events at the 1908 Summer Olympics in London. It was dominated by the American runners.

Ten sets of hurdles were set on the course. The hurdles were 3 feet, 6 inches tall and were placed ten yards apart beginning fifteen yards from the starting line.

[edit] Results

[edit] Heats

The first round heats were held on 23 July 1908, making the short hurdles one of the last events to begin. The Americans won every race in which they competed. The British team also had a strong showing, winning any time that there was no American in the race.

[edit] Heat 1

Healey won by four yards after leading from the start.

Place Name Nation Time
1 Alfred Healey Great Britain 15.8 seconds
2 Henry S. Murray Australasia 16.3 seconds
3 Douglas Stupart South Africa Unknown

[edit] Heat 2

Garrels won by a comfortable seven-yard margin.

Place Name Nation Time
1 John Garrels United States 16.2 seconds
2 Arthur Halligan Great Britain 17.1 seconds

[edit] Heat 3

Groenings led by five yards at the finish.

Place Name Nation Time
1 Oswald Groenings Great Britain 16.4 seconds
2 Georgios Skoutarides Greece 17.0 seconds

[edit] Heat 4

Kiely had no competition in the first round.

Place Name Nation Time
1 Laurence Kiely Great Britain Walkover

[edit] Heat 5

Rand and Powell were even for most of the race. Powell hit the ninth hurdle, slowing him just enough for Rand to take the lead and win.

Place Name Nation Time
1 William Rand United States 15.8 seconds
2 Kenneth Powell Great Britain 16.2 seconds
3 Frank Savage Canada Unknown

[edit] Heat 6

Lemming fell during the race, allowing Walters to claim an easy victory.

Place Name Nation Time
1 David W. Walters Great Britain 17.8 seconds
— Oscar Lemming Sweden Did not finish

[edit] Heat 7

The seventh heat was another in which there was no competition.

Place Name Nation Time
1 William Knyvett Great Britain Walkover

[edit] Heat 8

Halbart won without competition.

Place Name Nation Time
1 Fernand Halbert Belgium Walkover

[edit] Heat 9

Ahearne was the fourth runner to win without a contest.

Place Name Nation Time
1 Timothy Ahearne Great Britain Walkover

[edit] Heat 10

Kovacs had barely cleared the final hurdle when Smithson crossed the finish line.

Place Name Nation Time
1 Forrest Smithson United States 15.8 seconds

[edit] Heat 11

Blijstad fell and did not finish the race.

Place Name Nation Time

[edit] Heat 12

Kinahan had an easy time in this heat, winning by ten yards.

Place Name Nation Time
1 Cecil Kinahan Great Britain 16.8 seconds
2 Oscar Guttormsen Norway 18.2 seconds

[edit] Heat 13

Both runners cleared each set of hurdles simultaneously. Howe was slightly quicker transitioning from the final hurdle to the finishing sprint, edging out Leader for the victory.

Place Name Nation Time
1 Leonard Howe United States 15.8 seconds
2 Edward Leader Great Britain 16.1 seconds

[edit] Heat 14

Shaw won without a contest.

Place Name Nation Time
1 Arthur Shaw United States Walkover

[edit] Semifinals

The semifinal round was held on 24 July 1908. Each of the four heats of the second round were won by American runners.

[edit] Semifinal 1

Shaw took the lead at the first set of hurdles, finishing about five metres ahead of Hussy.

Place Name Nation Time
1 Arthur Shaw United States 15.6 seconds
2 Eric Hussey Great Britain 16.5 seconds
3 David W. Walters Great Britain 16.9 seconds
4 Oswald Groenings Great Britain Unknown

[edit] Semifinal 2

Smithson had no trouble winning this heat, tying the Olympic record as he did so.

Place Name Nation Time
1 Forrest Smithson United States 15.4 seconds =OR
2 William Knyvett Great Britain 15.6 seconds
3 Leonard Howe United States 15.8 seconds

[edit] Semifinal 3

Healey led for most of the race, with Rand close behind. On the ninth hurdle, Healey was not quite able to clear the obstacle cleanly. This allowed Rand to pass him and win the closest of the second round races.

Place Name Nation Time
1 William Rand United States 15.8 seconds
2 Alfred Healey Great Britain 15.9 seconds
3 Laurence Kiely Great Britain Unknown
4 Timothy Ahearne Great Britain Unknown

[edit] Semifinal 4

Garrels won easily, with Kinahan nearer to the last hurdles than to the finish line when Garrels crossed.

Place Name Nation Time
1 John Garrels United States 15.8 seconds
2 C. E. Kinahan Great Britain 17.5 seconds
— Fernand Halbert Belgium Did not finish

[edit] Final

Smithson won relatively easily, setting a world record as he pulled away from his countrymen at every set of hurdles.

Place Name Nation Time
1 Forrest Smithson United States 15.0 seconds WR
2 John Garrels United States 15.7 seconds
3 Arthur Shaw United States 15.8 seconds
4 William Rand United States 16.0 seconds
