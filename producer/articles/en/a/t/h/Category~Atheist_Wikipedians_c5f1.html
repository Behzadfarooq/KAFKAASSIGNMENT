Category:Atheist Wikipedians

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

This page contains Wikipedians who have identified themselves as being atheists, and thus as not believing in the existence of any deity, unlike theist Wikipedians. Atheists may still be religious or spiritual; atheism is not synonymous with lacking religion, though in its broader definition, it is synonymous with nontheism.
