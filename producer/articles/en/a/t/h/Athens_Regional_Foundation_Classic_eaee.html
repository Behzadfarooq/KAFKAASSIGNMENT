The Athens Regional Foundation Classic is a golf tournament on the Nationwide Tour. It is played annually at Jennings Mill Country Club in Bogart, Georgia, United States. The Athens Regional Foundation is the host organization for the event and the tournament aims to support its goals of providing for the long-term health care of area citizens.

The 2006 purse was $500,000, with $90,000 going to the winner.
