Athidhi (Telugu: అతిథీ) is a 2007 Telugu film starring Mahesh Babu and Amrita Rao.[2] Surender Reddy will be directing this movie. G. Ramesh Babu produces it under their banner Krishna Pictures Private Limited. It is scheduled to release on August 9, 2007,[1] which happens to be Mahesh Babu's birthday.
