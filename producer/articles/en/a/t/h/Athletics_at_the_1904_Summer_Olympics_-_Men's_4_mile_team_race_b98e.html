Athletics at the 1904 Summer Olympics - Men's 4 mile team race

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

You have new messages (last change).

The men's 4 mile team race was a track and field athletics event held as part of the Athletics at the 1904 Summer Olympics programme. It was the first time the event was held, though the 1900 Summer Olympics had featured a similar event in the 5000 metre team race. 2 teams of 5 athletes each, all from the United States, competed.

[edit] Individual standings

[edit] Team standings

The scores from each of the individual team members were summed, with the lowest-scoring team winning.
