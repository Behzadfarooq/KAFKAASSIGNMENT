The 400 m hurdles at the 2004 Summer Olympics as part of the athletics program were held at the Athens Olympic Stadium from August 23 to August 26.

[edit] Records

World Record 46.78 Kevin Young USA Barcelona(ESP) 6 August 1992
Olympic Record 46.78 Kevin Young USA Barcelona(ESP) 6 August 1992

[edit] Results

Gold: Silver: Bronze:
Felix Sanchez, Dominican Republic Danny McFarlane, Jamaica Naman Keita, France

[edit] Qualifying

From the initial five heats the first four in each plus four fastest losers progressed through to the semi-finals.

The final was made up of the first two finishers from each of the semi-finals plus two fastest losers.
