The 400 metres hurdles at the 2004 Summer Olympics as part of the athletics program were held at the Athens Olympic Stadium August 21 to August 25.

[edit] Records

World Record 52.34 Yuliya Pechonkina Russia Tula, Russia 8 August 2003
Olympic Record 52.77 Fani Halkia Greece Athens, Greece 22 August 2004

[edit] Medals

Gold: Silver: Bronze:
Fani Halkia, Greece Ionela Tirlea-Manolache, Romania Tetiana Tereshchuk-Antipova, Ukraine
