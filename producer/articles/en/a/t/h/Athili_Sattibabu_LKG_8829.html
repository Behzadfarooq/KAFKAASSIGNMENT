Athili Sattibabu LKG is the announced title for the upcoming telugu movie starring Allari Naresh and Kousha , under the direction of EVV Satyanarayana.This movie will release in April 6th, 2007.[1]

Allari Naresh is playing a Pawnbroker in the film, while Raghubabu and Suneel play his wayward brothers.

The first schedule will be held from January 1 to 25 at Ramoji Film City,Hyderabad. The penultimate 4-day schedule will take place in January-end in Hyderabad, followed by a major schedule, from February 1 to 25, in Rajahmundry. The film is slated for a April 2007 release. Of the five songs in the film, one will be shot on sets, one in Rajahmundry and three of them abroad.[2]
