Created: 1614

Athy was a constituency represented in the Irish House of Commons to 1800.

Following the Act of Union 1800 the borough was disfranchised.

[edit] Members of Parliament

  • 1768-1769, John St Ledger (10 April 1726 - March 1769)
  • 1798-1800: William Hare
