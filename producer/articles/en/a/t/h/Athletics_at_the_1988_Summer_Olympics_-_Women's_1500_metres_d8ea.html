Athletics at the 1988 Summer Olympics - Women's 1500 metres

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Women's 1500m at the 1988 Summer Olympics in Seoul, South Korea had an entrylist of 28 competitors, with two qualifying heats (28) before the final (12) took place on Saturday October 1, 1988.
