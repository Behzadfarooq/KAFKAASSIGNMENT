Athletes

Athletes (UK English) or track and field athletes (U.S. English) are specialists in the sport of athletics, which is called track and field in some countries.

See also: List of Olympic medalists in athletics (men) and List of Olympic medalists in athletics (women).

For participants in other sports, see Sportspeople by sport and Olympic competitors by country.
