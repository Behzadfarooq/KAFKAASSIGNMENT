Athletics at the 1988 Summer Olympics - Men's 200 metres

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

You have new messages (last change).

The Men's 200m at the 1988 Summer Olympics in Seoul, South Korea had an entrylist of 76 competitors, with ten qualifying heats (76), five second-round races (40) and two semi-finals (16), before the final (8) took off on Wednesday September 28, 1988.
