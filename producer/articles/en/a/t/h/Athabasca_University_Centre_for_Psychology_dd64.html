Athabasca University Centre for Psychology

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Athabasca University Centre for Psychology offers programs leading to a Bachelor of Arts degree with majors in psychology, a cerificiate in Career Development, or even a diploma in Inclusive Education, of three and four-year lengths.

The Centre was set up at Athabasca University in Athabasca, Canada and they have 2000 students enroll in psychology courses each year at the Centre, and they have 200 students majoring in psychology.
