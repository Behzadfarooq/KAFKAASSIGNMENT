Athletics at the 2006 Central American and Caribbean Games

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

You have new messages (last change).

The athletics competition at the 2006 Central American and Caribbean Games lasted from July 25 to July 29. The events were dominated by Cuban athletes, whereas teams like Jamaica did not send their strongest athletes.
