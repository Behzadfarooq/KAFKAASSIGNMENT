Athletics at the 1992 Summer Olympics - Women's Marathon

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The official results of the Women's Marathon at the 1992 Summer Olympics in Barcelona, Spain, held on Saturday August 1, 1992. The race started on 18.30h local time. A total number of 37 athletes completed the race, with Bakombo Kungu from Zaire finishing in last position in 3:29:10. There were 47 competitors from 31 countries. Nine of them did not finish.
