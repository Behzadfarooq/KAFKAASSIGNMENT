Athelstan Rendall

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Athelstan Rendall (16 November 1871 – 12 July 1948) was a Liberal Party politician in the United Kingdom.

He was elected as Member of Parliament (MP) for the Thornbury constituency in Gloucestershire at the 1906 general election, and held the seat until his defeat at the 1922 general election by the Conservative Party candidate Herbert Charles Woodcock. He regained the seat at the 1923 general election, but was defeated again at the 1924 general election. He did not stand for Parliament again.
