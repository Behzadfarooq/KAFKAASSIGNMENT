Athyriaceae is a family of 12-15 genera of terrestrial ferns, with a cosmopolitan distribution.

  • Acystopteris
  • Anisocampium
  • Athyrium
  • Cornopteris

  • Deparia
  • Diplaziopsis
  • Diplazium
  • Gymnocarpium
  • Hypodematium
  • Pentarhizidium
  • Rhachidosorus
  • Woodsia
