Athletics at the 1908 Summer Olympics - Men's javelin throw

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

You have new messages (last change).

The men's javelin throw was one of six throwing events on the Athletics at the 1908 Summer Olympics programme in London. The javelin was required to be held in the middle.
