The official results of the Men's Marathon at the 1992 Summer Olympics in Barcelona, Spain, held on Sunday August 9, 1992. The race started on 18.30h local time. A total number of 87 athletes completed the race, with Pyambuu Tuul from Mongolia finishing in last position in 4:00:44. There were 112 competitors from 72 countries. Thirtheen of them did not finish.
