The 20 km walk at the 2004 Summer Olympics as part of the athletics program was held on August 23.


[edit] Records

World Record 1:26:22 Wang Yan CHN Guangzhou(CHN) November 19, 2001
Olympic Record 1:29:05 Wang Liping CHN Sydney(AUS) September 28, 2000
