Athletics at the 1988 Summer Olympics - Men's 110 metres Hurdles

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Men's 110 metres Hurdles at the 1988 Summer Olympics in Seoul, South Korea had an entrylist of 43 competitors, with six qualifying heats (43 runners), four second-round races (32) and two semifinals (16) before the final (8) took place on Monday September 26, 1988.
