The 50 km walk at the 2004 Summer Olympics as part of the athletics program was held on August 27.


[edit] Records

World Record 3:35:29 Denis Nizhegorodov RUS Cheboksary(RUS) June 13, 2004
Olympic Record 3:38:29 Vyacheslav Ivanenko USR Seoul(KOR) September 30, 1988

[edit] Results

Gold: Silver: Bronze:
Robert Korzeniowski, Poland Denis Nizhegorodov, Russia Aleksey Voyevodin, Russia
