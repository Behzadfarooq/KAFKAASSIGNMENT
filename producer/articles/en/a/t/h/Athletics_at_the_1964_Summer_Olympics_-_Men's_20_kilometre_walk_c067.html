Athletics at the 1964 Summer Olympics - Men's 20 kilometre walk

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The men's 20 kilometre walk was the shorter of the two men's racewalking events on the Athletics at the 1964 Summer Olympics program in Tokyo. It was held on 15 October 1964. 35 athletes from 15 nations entered, with 30 starting and 26 finishing.
