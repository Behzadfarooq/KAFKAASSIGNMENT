The Women's 100 m hurdles at the 2004 Summer Olympics as part of the athletics program were held at the Athens Olympic Stadium from August 22 to August 24.

[edit] Records

World Record 12.21 Yordanka Donkova Bulgaria Stara Zagora, Bulgaria 20 August 1988
Olympic Record 12.37 Joanna Hayes USA Athens, Greece 24 August 2004
