Athens Stock Exchange

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Athens Stock Exchange or ASE (Greek: Χρηματιστήριο Αξιών Αθηνών or ΧΑΑ) is a stock exchange located in Athens, Greece. It was first opened in 1876. It's located in Psiris, an area near the city centre and it's on Sofocleous Street this is why it is also called Sofocleous. It is planned that in 2007 it will have moved in its new headquarters designed by Babis Vovos Constructions.
