Athina Sat is a Cypriot digital satellite pay television service owned by Athina Sat TV Ltd.. Athina Sat launched in May 2005 and is the first Cypriot-owned DTH satellite provider in Cyprus. It is one of two satellite platforms, the other being NOVA Cyprus.

Athina Sat offers Cypriot subscribers an array of programming choices from News, Sports, Movies, Children's, Entertainment as well as popular Cypriot TV networks.
