Athletics at the 1988 Summer Olympics - Men's 3000 metres Steeplechase

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

You have new messages (last change).

The Men's 3.000 metres Steeplechase at the 1988 Summer Olympics in Seoul, South Korea had an entrylist of 33 competitors, with three qualifying heats (33 runners) and two semifinals (26) before the final (13) took place on Friday September 30, 1988.
