Athletics at the 2006 Lusophony Games

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Athletics at the 2006 Lusophony Games were held in the Macau Stadium on October 11 and 12. Brazil was the dominating delegation conquering 19 out of 30 gold medals (63% of the total!), while Sri Lanka managed to grab its 3 gold medals at this sport.
