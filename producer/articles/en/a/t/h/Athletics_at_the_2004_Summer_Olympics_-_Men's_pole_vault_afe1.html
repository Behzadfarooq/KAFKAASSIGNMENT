The pole vault at the 2004 Summer Olympics as part of the athletics program was held at the Athens Olympic Stadium on August 25 and August 27.

[edit] Records

World Record 6.14 Sergey Bubka Ukraine Sestrière, Italy 31 July 1994
Olympic Record 5.95 Tim Mack USA Athens, Greece 27 August 2004

[edit] Medals

Gold: Silver: Bronze:
