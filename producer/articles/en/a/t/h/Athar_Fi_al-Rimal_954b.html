Athar Fi al-Rimal (Arabic: أثار في الرمال‎, English: Traces in the Sand) is a 1954 Egyptian drama film directed by Gamal Madkoor. It starred Imad Hamdi and Faten Hamama.

Faten Hamama plays Ragia, a woman who helps Ibrahim (Imad Hamdi) retain his memory and tell the story of his sister's death.
