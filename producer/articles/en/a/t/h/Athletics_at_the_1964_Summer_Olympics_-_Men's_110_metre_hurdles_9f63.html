Athletics at the 1964 Summer Olympics - Men's 110 metre hurdles

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The men's 110 metre hurdles was the shorter of the men's hurdle races in the Athletics at the 1964 Summer Olympics program in Tokyo. It was held on 17 October and 18 October 1964. 38 athletes from 24 nations entered, with 1 not starting in the first round. The first round was held on 17 October, with the semifinals and the final on 18 October.
