Athletics Weekly

Athletics Weekly is a weekly athletics magazine published in the United Kingdom by Descartes Publishing. Started in 1945, it reached its 60th anniversary in December 2005.

Athletics Weekly covers track and field, cross-country and road races. It mainly covers British events and British athletics, but also covers major international events. The magazine also contains coaching information and advice, and a section dealing with listing results and fixtures.

The magazine covers many interesting sections, eg. rising stars in this section it covers the latest young athlete who could make it big. As well as this it gives good hints and tricks on techniques for doing things such as steeplechase. Athletics weekly also includes questions you can send in and answers from top qualified coaches. Athletics Weekly is the top athletics magazine.
