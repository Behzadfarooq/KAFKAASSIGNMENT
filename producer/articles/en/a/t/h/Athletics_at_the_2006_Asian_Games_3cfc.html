Athletics at the 2006 Asian Games

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

You have new messages (last change).

Athletics, known in the United States as Track and Field, will be contested at the 2006 Asian Games in Doha, Qatar from December 7 to December 12. Twenty-three events are being contested for the men while 22 are on the slate for the women. Only the 3000 Meter Steeplechase is not contested for by the women. All events, except Race Walk and the marathon, will be held at Khalifa Stadium.
