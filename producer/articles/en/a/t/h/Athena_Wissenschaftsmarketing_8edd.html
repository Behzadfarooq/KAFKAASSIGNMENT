Athena Wissenschaftsmarketing is a full-service agency specialized in international student recruitment, release and branding of international programs and academic institutions. The company was founded in October 2006 as spin-off from the Heidelberg Center for American Studies (HCA). As one among very few spin-offs with a humanities background Athena Wissenschaftsmarketing is supported by the Research Department of the Ruprecht Karls University Heidelberg.

[edit] Corporate profile

Athena Wissenschaftsmarketing is an independent academic marketing, consulting and evaluation services company that combines a market-focused approach to academia with expertise and global experience in the international educational market on both sides of the Atlantic.
