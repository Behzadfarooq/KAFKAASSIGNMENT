Athletics at the 1908 Summer Olympics - Men's 3200 metre steeplechase

You have new messages (last change).

The men's 3,200 metres steeplechase was held for the first and only time at the 1908 Summer Olympics in London.

[edit] Medallists

Gold: Silver: Bronze:
Arthur Russell, Great Britain and Ireland Archie Robertson, Great Britain and Ireland John Eisele, United States

[edit] Results

[edit] First round

[edit] First round, heat 1

Russell won easily, with Cartasegna being the only other competitor to finish. Downing was disqualified at the first water jump, Carr pulled up injured, and Ragueneau retired after two laps.

Rank Name Nation Time
1 Arthur Russell Great Britain and Ireland 10:56.2
2 N. Cartasegna Italy
DNF A. G. Ragueneau France
DNF E. P. Carr United States
DQ T. Downing Great Britain and Ireland

[edit] First round, heat 2

Eisele was the only one to finish. Fleurac led early, but was caught before the water jump and retired.

Rank Name Nation Time
1 John Eisele United States 11:13.6
DNF A. Lovass Hungary
DNF L. de B. de Fleurac France
DNF F. J. Buckley Great Britain and Ireland
DNF J. C. English Great Britain and Ireland

[edit] First round, heat 3

Barker did not finish, leaving Galbraith to win uncontested.

Rank Name Nation Time
1 William Galbraith Canada 11:12.4
DNF H. Barker Great Britain and Ireland

[edit] First round, heat 4

With Yorke disqualifying and Bonhag retiring, the race was between Robertson and Dull. Dull led at first, but Robertson passed him and won by two hundred yards.

Rank Name Nation Time
1 Archie Robertson Great Britain and Ireland 11:10.0
2 G. A. Dull United States
DNF G. N. Bonhag United States
DQ R. F. C. Yorke Great Britain and Ireland

[edit] First round, heat 5

The Americans had little chance of catching up with Holdaway and Kinchin. Kinchin was himself a hundred yard behind his countryman at the finish.

Rank Name Nation Time
1 Guy Holdaway Great Britain and Ireland
2 J. W. Kinchin Great Britain and Ireland
3 R. A. Spitzer United States
4 C. L. Hall United States

[edit] First round, heat 6

By far the closest of the six first round heats, Sewell and Lightbody competed right up to the finish. Sewell pulled away only at the very end, winning by less than ten yards.

Rank Name Nation Time
1 Harold Sewell Great Britain and Ireland
2 Jim Lightbody United States
DNF J. F. Fitzgerald Canada
DNF W. Grantham Great Britain and Ireland

[edit] Final

Holdaway was the first to lead, but did not do so for long. Galbraith and Russell were the leaders for most of the first half of the race, until Galbraith fell back and Eisele joined Russell at the front. After the bell, Robertson passed Eisele and nearly caught Russell, trailing by only two yards when the two crossed the finish line.

Rank Name Nation Time
1 Arthur Russell Great Britain and Ireland 10:47.8
2 Archie Robertson Great Britain and Ireland 10:48.4
3 John Eisele United States 11:00.8
4 Guy Holdaway Great Britain and Ireland 11:26.0
5 Harold Sewell Great Britain and Ireland
6 William Galbraith Canada
