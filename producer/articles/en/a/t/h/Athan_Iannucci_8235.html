Athan Iannucci (b. April 15, 1982 in New Westminster, British Columbia) is a professional lacrosse player.

Iannucci attended Hofstra University. In 2006, he was Hofstra University’s second-leading scorer with 80 points (62G, 18A) in 19 games.

Iannucci was a First Round Draft pick (8th Overall) by the Philadelphia Wings in the 2007 NLL Entry Draft. He was awarded "Rookie of the Week Honors in Week 5 of the 2007 Season.

He was selected 1st Overall in the 2007 Major League Lacrosse Supplemental Draft by the Chicago Machine.
