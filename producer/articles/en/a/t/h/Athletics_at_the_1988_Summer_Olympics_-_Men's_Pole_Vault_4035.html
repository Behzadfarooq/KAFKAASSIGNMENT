Athletics at the 1988 Summer Olympics - Men's Pole Vault

The Men's Pole Vault at the 1988 Summer Olympics in Seoul, South Korea had an entrylist of 21 competitors, with two qualifying groups (21 jumpers) before the final (15) took place on Wednesday September 28, 1988.
