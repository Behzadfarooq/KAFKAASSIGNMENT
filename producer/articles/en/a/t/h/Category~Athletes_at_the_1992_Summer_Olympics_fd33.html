This is a list of athletes who participated in the 1992 Summer Olympics in Barcelona, Spain.


This category should only be used for Olympians participating in athletics (track and field).
All other Olympians should be categorized into Category:Competitors at the 1992 Summer Olympics (or an appropriate subcategory). Thanks!
