Athletics at the 1988 Summer Olympics - Women's 400 metres Hurdles

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

You have new messages (last change).

The Women's 400 metres Hurdles at the 1988 Summer Olympics in Seoul, South Korea had an entrylist of 35 competitors, with five qualifying heats (38 runners) and two semifinals (16) before the final (8) took place on Wednesday September 28, 1988.
