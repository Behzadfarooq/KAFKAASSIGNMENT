The heptathlon at the 2000 Summer Olympics as part of the athletics program was held at the Stadium Australia on Wednesday September 23 and Thursday September 24.

[edit] Results

[edit] Medals

The medals were distributed as following:

Gold: Silver: Bronze:
 Denise Lewis
Great Britain (GBR)		  Yelena Prokhorova
Russia (RUS)		  Natallia Sazanovich
Belarus (BLR)

Both Lewis and Sazanovich had won medals (bronze and silver respectively) at the previous Olympics in 1996. Prokhorova would go on to win the 2001 World Championships. At the next Olympic Games in 2004, Prokhorova finished fifth while Lewis did not finish because of injury.
