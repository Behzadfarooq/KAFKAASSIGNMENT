Athletics at the 1988 Summer Olympics - Women's 3000 metres

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Women's 3.000m at the 1988 Summer Olympics in Seoul, South Korea had an entrylist of 35 competitors, with two qualifying heats (35) before the final (15) took place on Sunday September 25, 1988.
