Athletics at the 1996 Summer Olympics - Men's Marathon

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The official results of the Men's Marathon at the 1996 Summer Olympics in Atlanta, Georgia, held on Sunday August 4, 1996. The race started on 07.05h local time. A total number of 111 athletes completed the race, with Wasiqi Abdel Baser from Afghanistan finishing in last position in 4:24:17. There were 124 competitors from 79 countries. Thirtheen of them did not finish.
