Athletics at the 2006 Commonwealth Games

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

You have new messages (last change).

The Athletics at the 2006 Commonwealth Games were held in the newly adapted Melbourne Cricket Ground, except for the racewalking and marathon which were held on street courses running through the city. The marathon began and ended in the Melbourne Cricket Ground.
