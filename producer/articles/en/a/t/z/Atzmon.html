Atzmon

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

For other uses, see Atzmon (disambiguation).

Atzmon (Hebrew: עצמון‎) is a communal settlement in Gush Segev on the Israel Galilee area. It was established in 1975, and settled beside the Yavor junction in 1978. The current location is less than 1 km west from the Misgav Regional Council, and was occupied by the founders on 1983.

374 members inhabit Atzmon in 194 Families. They were settled in 7 stages. The 8th stage is currently in advanced planning phase, with about 40 more families which are expected to join Atzmon during 2007.
