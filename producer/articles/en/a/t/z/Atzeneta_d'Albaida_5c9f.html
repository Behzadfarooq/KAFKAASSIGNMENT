Atzeneta d'Albaida (Spanish: Adzaneta de Albaida) is a municipality in the comarca of Vall d'Albaida, in the province of Valencia, in the Valencian Community, Spain.

[edit] Monuments

  • Church of San Juan Bautista (18th century)
  • Hermitage of Cristo del Calvario.
  • Nevera de Dalt.
  • Nevera de Baix (snow wells)
