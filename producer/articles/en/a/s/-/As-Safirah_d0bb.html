As-Safirah

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

As-Safirah (Arabic: السفيرة‎) is a Syrian city administratively belonging to the Aleppo Governorate. As-Safirah has an altitude of 348 meters. It has a population of 106,382 as of 2007, making it the 11th largest city per geographical entity in Syria.

Coordinates: 34°6′N, 36°46′E
