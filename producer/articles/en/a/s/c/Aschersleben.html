Aschersleben is a town in the Aschersleben-Staßfurt district, in Saxony-Anhalt, Germany. It is situated approx. 22 km east of Quedlinburg, and 45 km northwest of Halle (Saale).

Aschersleben is also the seat of the Verwaltungsgemeinschaft ("collective municipality") Aschersleben-Land, which consists of the following municipalities (population in 2005 between brackets):

  • Aschersleben * (26.438)
  • Freckleben (768)
  • Drohndorf (529)
  • Groß Schierstedt (649)
  • Mehringen (1.157)
  • Schackenthal (328)
  • Westdorf (806)
