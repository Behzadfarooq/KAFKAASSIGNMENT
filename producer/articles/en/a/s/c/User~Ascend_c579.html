Who am I

I'm just a person who got addicted to Wikipedia. I find editing to be quite entertaining. Apparently I have gotten obsessed with getting my edit count high. However, I can't seem to find much time to do that yet...

I'm currently obsessed with reverting edits made by vandals. It's quite funny what people write on articles. From just ridiculous nonsense to mildly profane language, it never really gets old.



Fear me vandals. I am currently armed with Navigation Popups
What do I do?

Nothing much now... I ski a bit and I swim. I also enjoy reverting vandalism (cause it's just so damn entertaining). Currently, I'm working on memorizing rubik's cube algorithms. I'll get to solve it in a minute or less eventually. I also spin pens and do some other relatively dexterous things. I do play some instruments.

I guess that's it.
