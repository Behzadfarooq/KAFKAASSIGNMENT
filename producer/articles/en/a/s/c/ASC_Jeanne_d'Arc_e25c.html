ASC Jeanne d'Arc is an Senegalese football club based in Dakar. It was founded in 1921. They play at the Stade Léopold Sédar Senghor.

[edit] Achievements

  • Senegal Premier League: 10

1960, 1969, 1973, 1985, 1986, 1988, 1999, 2001, 2002, 2003

  • Senegal FA Cup: 6

1962, 1969, 1974, 1980, 1984, 1987

  • Senegal Assemblée Nationale Cup: 3
1986, 1989, 2001
  • CAF Cup:

Finalist : 1998
