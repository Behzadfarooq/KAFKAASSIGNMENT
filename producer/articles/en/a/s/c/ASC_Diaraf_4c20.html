ASC Diaraf is a Senegalese football club based in Dakar. They play in the top division in Senegalese football. Their home stadium is Stade de Diaraf.

[edit] Achievements

  • Senegal Premier League: 7
1968, 1970, 1975, 1976, 1977, 1982, 1989
  • Senegal FA Cup: 12
1967, 1968, 1970, 1973, 1975, 1982, 1983, 1985, 1991, 1993, 1994, 1995
  • Senegal Assemblée Nationale Cup: 3
1987, 1991, 2003
