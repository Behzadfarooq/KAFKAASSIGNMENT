Generally, an ascension is the act of ascending, usually to a significantly higher personal state, as the ascension of a king to the throne. A physical act of ascending, such as in mountain climbing is more commonly called an ascent.

In Christianity, Ascension refers to the bodily ascension of Jesus into Heaven and an annual feast commemorating the event.

Ascension may also refer to:

In religion and spirituality:

  • Enoch's, Elijah's, or Muhammad's disappearance into heaven or Shantideva's disappearance or Shakyamuni Buddha's entrance into parinirvana
  • In Theosophy, and some New Age groups, a process of enlightenment whereby any person may escape the physical plane of existence to become an ascended master
  • Ishayas' Ascension, a form of meditation

In geography:

  • Ascension Island, an island in the southern Atlantic Ocean
  • Ascension Parish, Louisiana
  • Highway 160 (PE) or Ascension Road, a 2-lane collector highway in western Prince County, Prince Edward Island, Canada

In fiction:

  • Ascension (Stargate), in the Stargate universe, a process to become a noncorporeal god-like entity on a higher plane of existence
    • Ascension (Stargate SG-1), a Stargate SG-1 episode
  • Ascension (The X-Files), an episode from season 2 of The X-Files
  • Ascension (comics), a comic book series created by David Finch and published by Top Cow Productions

In games:

  • Mage: The Ascension, a role-playing game
  • Ultima IX: Ascension, the final part of the computer role-playing game series Ultima
  • In the games NetHack and Kingdom of Loathing, a term used for winning
  • Ascension (Game), an upcoming computer game, styled after the Tribes series of games, created by RenWerX Studios on the Torque Game Engine Advanced

In music:

  • Ascension (album), an album by John Coltrane
  • Ascension (DJ duo), an alias of trance music DJs Ricky Simmons and Stephen Jones, who also record as Lustral, Space Brothers, Oxygen and Chakra
  • ASCENSION (Misia album), 7th original album by Japanese singer MISIA
  • The Ascension (Glenn Branca album), the sophomore album by No Wave musician Glenn Branca
  • The_Ascension, an album by band Otep
  • "Ascension Day", a song by Talk Talk from their 1991 album Laughing Stock

In other:

  • Right ascension, in astronomy, a term used to describe positions in the sky
  • Ascension (climbing), a term in climbing and mountaineering
