Ascot Road tube station

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Ascot Road tube station is a proposed tube station in Watford, Hertfordshire, England. It is the first station to be built on the Metropolitan Line diversion to Watford Junction. This station is to replace the abandoned station of Croxley Green. It is also to replace the current Watford tube station and cease the branch. The station is projected to become an interchange with bicycle facilities and local transportation [1]
