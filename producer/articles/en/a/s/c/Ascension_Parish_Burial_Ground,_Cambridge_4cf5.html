Ascension Parish Burial Ground, Cambridge

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The chapel of the Ascension Parish Burial Ground, Cambridge

The Ascension Parish Burial Ground, formerly St Giles's Parish, is a cemetery located on Huntingdon Road in the north-west of Cambridge, England. It is home to the graves of many Cambridge academics and non-conformists from the 19th and early 20th century. A small chapel is also situated on the grounds.

[edit] Selected graves

  • John Couch Adams
  • Hugh Kerr Anderson
  • Arthur Christopher Benson
  • John Cockcroft
  • Frances Cornford
  • Francis Darwin
  • Horace Darwin
  • Arthur Eddington
  • James Frazer
  • Roberto Gerhard
  • Sir Richard Jebb
The gravestone of Ludwig Wittgenstein has started to attract small tributes in recent years.
  • Horace Lamb
  • Desmond MacCarthy
  • Alfred Marshall
  • George Edward Moore
  • Frank Ramsey
  • Walter William Rouse Ball
  • Ludwig Wittgenstein
