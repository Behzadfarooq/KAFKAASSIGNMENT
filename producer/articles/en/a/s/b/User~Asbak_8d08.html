Ash Dreelinck, born in Ronse (Belgium) on 2 May 1986, and still living in the same town. José and Hilde are his parents. Ex-student of the Royal Athenaeum Oudenaarde with A2 Technlogical Informatics Degree and 2x certificate Company Management (2004 and 2005).


In his free time he works on:

  • Websites
  • Visual Basic applications
  • Servercontrol-solutions
  • As DJ (external links)


He is active as a member in:

  • Youth house Zoobidoobie (leader)
  • Youth house Canavia (technician)


The nickname Asbak has his origin with my first name. Ash was named regulary by the nickname 'Ash bak', with 'bak' as a dutch word to strenghten the first name. When I got a domain name, I suggested 'Asbak', because it's much easier (asbak is dutch for ashtray) and ever since then everybody who I can consider as a friend is calling me 'Asbak', in stead of 'Ash bak'.
