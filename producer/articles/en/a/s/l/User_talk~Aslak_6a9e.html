[edit] Sukkah picture

Hi, I'm happy to add a license to my picture, but I have no idea how to do that. It was hard enough figuring out how to upload the image in a regular way, not on Wikipedia Commons. Could you advise? Yoninah 11:38, 9 January 2006 (UTC)

Sure. "If you upload a file here to which you hold the copyright, you must license it under the GNU Free Documentation License or release it into the public domain." I recommend the GFDL: simply edit the page Image:Jerusalemsukkas.jpg and license it by adding there {{GFDL-self}}. (I noticed that there is a GFD License in your Image:Porchsukka.jpg.) -Easlak 11:55, 9 January 2006 (UTC)
Okay, I figured out how to upload it to Wikimedia Commons, and gave it a license there. It is also named Image:Jerusalemsukkas.jpg. Thanks, Yoninah 20:31, 9 January 2006 (UTC)
I fixed the description page on Image:Porchsukka.jpg too. Both pictures were taken on the same day on the same street. Yoninah 21:23, 9 January 2006 (UTC)
One more question: I found a picture on Wikimedia Commons that I would like to use on a Wikipedia article page. How do I write it into the article? If I write Image:XYZ.jpg (in double brackets), nothing comes up. Yoninah 21:42, 9 January 2006 (UTC)
Hm, it should work? Are you sure that you have the name in correct form (it can be .JPG, for example). -Easlak 21:48, 9 January 2006 (UTC)
You're right, I had the wrong name. Thanks, Yoninah 08:50, 10 January 2006 (UTC)
