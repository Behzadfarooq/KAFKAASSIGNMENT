Asli Bayram (born April 1, 1981 in Darmstadt), Miss Germany 2005, is a German actress.

[edit] Biography

Asli Bayram comes from a Turkish family. Her father, Ali Bayram, a successful businessman and entrepreneur from Ankara, was murdered in 1994 in Darmstadt by a right-wing extremist who was a neighbour of the family. Asli herself was shot and seriously injured in the incident.

The motto of Asli Bayram is: "You can do anything if you really want to".

[edit] Education

School-leaving certificate (Abitur) at the Max Beckmann School in Frankfurt am Main;

Studying law in Frankfurt am Main since 2003;

Drama training at the BKM (Besiktas Kültür Merkezi) in Istanbul.
