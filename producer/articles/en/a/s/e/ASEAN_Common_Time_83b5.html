ASEAN Common Time

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

ASEAN Common Time is an idea by ASEAN to adopt a standard time for all member countries.

As of 2006 time zones used by ASEAN countries are UTC+6:30, UTC+7, UTC+8 and UTC+9.

  •  Indonesia UTC+7, UTC+8, UTC+9
  •  Brunei Darussalam UTC+8
  •  Malaysia UTC+8
  •  Philippines UTC+8
  •  Singapore UTC+8
  •  Cambodia UTC+7
  •  Laos UTC+7
  •  Thailand UTC+7
  •  Vietnam UTC+7
  •  Myanmar UTC+6:30
