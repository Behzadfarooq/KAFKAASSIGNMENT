BIOGRAPHY:

Chus Liberata (Mondo Paradiso, Pamplona, Spain)


Chus Star to play in some clubs pubs of you city in 1989, in 1991 enter Resident dj in Liberata Club, now last over 15 year he buy this Club “Liberata Club” and now he’s a new owner of this famous Club.

In this moment Chus start to make some Tours in some parts of Spain, working and playing with some deejays on differents Clubs and events in Spain, and making some shows and gig’s in France in Importants Clubs like, Le Bpm in Pàu, James in Toulosse andin others important Clubs in France (Some Clubs)

In 1999 he make the First Big Event, called “Mondo Paradiso” this event is The Best Festival & More Important Party of the Country (Spain), with some guest dj’s like: de MARCO V , RON VAN DEN BEUKEN, DAVE 202 ,M.I.D.O.R , RANDY KATANA , MARCEL WOODS , CARL COX info www.mondoparadiso.net


In these days, Chus Liberata, are making some gig’s and Tour in differents countrys like: POLAND,HOLLAND,FRANCE in some Festivals like SUNRISE FESTIVAL, DANCE PARADE IN ROTTERDAM and all South France Festivals & Clubs, At the moment Chus are making a Tour in differents parts and countrys of the World.
