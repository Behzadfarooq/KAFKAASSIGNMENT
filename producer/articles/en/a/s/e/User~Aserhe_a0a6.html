Hello, my name is Andreas Victorsson. I am currently residing in Rancho Santa Margarita, California, but used to live outside Stockholm, Sweden. I love my wife Beth very much, and our half chihuahua, half shitzu dog Widdy.

I enjoy living in California a lot, I do miss the snow at home and the ability to purchase and fire off your own fireworks. I love to eat at Chili's, Jack in the Box and In-n-Out

My favorite TV shows are, The Office, King of the Hill, South Park, Family Guy and Futurama.

Me and Beth spend a lot of our time chilling at home, cruising in our black convertible Volkswagen beetle, or we're out taking photo's of anything and nothing.

Why I picked the name Aserhe for my Wikipedia user was because it's one of my favorite aliases, I used to be called Aserhe Ostaho in the Massive Multiplayer Online Role Playing Game (MMORPG) called Star Wars Galaxies (SWG), I used to play on the Scylla Game Server, and that's where I met Beth!
