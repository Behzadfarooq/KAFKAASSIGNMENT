As Heard On Radio Soulwax Pt. 6 is an unofficial compilation album, attributed to (but probably not mixed by) 2 Many DJs (the pseudonym of the Soulwax Dewaele brothers). The origins of this bootleg are unclear. It contains material of various Radio Soulwax shows and other Soulwax remixes.

The album contains the song "A Stroke Of Genius", originally mixed by The Freelance Hellraiser, which was the first major underground hit for the bastard pop genre.
