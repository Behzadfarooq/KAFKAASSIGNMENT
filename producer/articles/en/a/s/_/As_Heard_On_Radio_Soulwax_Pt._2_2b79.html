As Heard On Radio Soulwax Pt. 2 is the first album to be released by Soulwax alter-egos 2 Many DJs. It consists of 45 remixed tracks by a series of diverse artists including Dolly Parton, Basement Jaxx and 10cc.

Some of these tracks are simply remixes. However, some are more traditional mashup affairs, with the a cappella of one song placed over the instrumental part of another.

On Compact Disc copies of the album, it is possible to rewind to before the first track to access "Can't Get You Out Of My Head (Soulwax Elektronic Mix)" by Kylie Minogue. Press play, then rewind for 4 minutes and 17 seconds and allow the album to play. After this song the album moves straight into "Peter Gunn"/"Where's Your Head At"?
