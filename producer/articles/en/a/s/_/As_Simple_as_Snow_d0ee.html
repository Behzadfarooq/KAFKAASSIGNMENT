As Simple as Snow

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

As Simple As Snow is a young adult mystery novel by Gregory Galloway. It tells the story of a high-school age unnamed narrator who meets a Gothic girl, Anna Cayne. Through postcards, a shortwave radio, her various mix-CDs, and her other erratic interests, she eventually wins his heart. However, a week before Valentine's Day, she goes missing, leaving only a dress on the ice and secret codes to help the narrator and the reader find out where she has gone.

Although Galloway never tells the reader where Anna disappears to, or solves any other mysteries in the story, he does hint at the possibility of the reader figuring out this by himself. Such clues may be left in maps, Houdini, and various songs.
