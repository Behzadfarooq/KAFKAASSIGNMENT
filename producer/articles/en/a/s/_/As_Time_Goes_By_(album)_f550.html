As Time Goes By
Studio album by Harpers Bizarre
Released 1976

As Time Goes By is an album by Harpers Bizarre.

[edit] Track listing

  1. "Introduction/Cowboy" (Gene Bob Smith)
  2. "As Time Goes By" (Herman Hupfeld)
  3. "Down at Papa Joe's" (Jerry Dean Smith)
  4. "Everynight" (Paul McCartney)
  5. "Society Strut" (Misha Mack/Jack Van Gleason)
  6. "Lullaby of Broadway" (Harry Warren/Al Dubin)
  7. "Speak Low" (Kurt Weill/Ogden Nash)
  8. "Banana King Louie" (Lord John)
  9. "My Melancholy Baby" (Ernie Burnett/George Norton; Ray Keller/Jack Van Gleason arrangement))
  10. "Beechwood 4-5789" (William "Mickey" Stevenson/Berry Gordy/Marvin Gaye)
  11. "That's the Way It Was (from Dvorak's "New World Symphony"" (Joseph Bocci/Robert Frost; Ray Keller/Jack Van Gleason arrangement)
  12. "Back in the Saddle Again" (RayWhitley/Gene Autry)
