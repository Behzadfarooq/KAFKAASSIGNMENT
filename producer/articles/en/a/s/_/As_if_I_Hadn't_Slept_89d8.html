"As if I Hadn't Slept" is a song by Scottish rock band Idlewild and is featured on their 2005 album Warnings/Promises. It was planned to be released 23 January 2006 as the final single from that album before the band announced 21 November 2005 they had parted ways with Parlophone/EMI (see 2005 in British music). The planned single has since been deleted.
