AS ADEMA Analamanga is a Malagasy football club based in Antananarivo, Madagascar. They have won the Malagasy Premier League championship on two occasions, in 2002 and 2006. In 2002 they also advanced to the quarterfinals of the CAF Cup before losing to Al-Masry of Egypt.[1]

AS ADEMA won a Malagasy Premier League match against SOE Antananarivo by the amazing score of 149-0 on October 31, 2002. SOE scored 149 own goals in protest of a refereeing decision.[2]
