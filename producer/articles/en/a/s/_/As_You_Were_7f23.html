As You Were is a 2005 live album by English acoustic roots duo Show of Hands.

[edit] Track listing

All songs written by Steve Knightley, except where noted.

  1. "Longdog" – 3:25
  2. "You're Mine" – 4:38
  3. "Willin'" (Lowell George) – 4:06
  4. "Mary From Dungloe" (Trad) / "The Setting" (Ralph McTell) – 4:02
  5. "The Oak" – 4:18
  6. "The Blue Cockade" (Trad) – 6:05
  7. "Crazy Boy" – 5:43
  8. "Crow On The Cradle" (Sydney Carter) – 4:37
  9. "I Promise You" – 4:39
  10. "Maybe" – 4:11
  11. "Be Lucky" – 5:26
  12. "The Blind Fiddler" (Trad) / "Galway Farmer" – 8:27
  13. "Captains" – 4:33
  14. "Corrina, Corrina" (Bob Dylan) – 3:38
  15. "Smile She Said" – 4:39
  16. "Crooked Man" – 4:05
  17. "Are We Alright" – 3:31
  18. "Cousin Jack" – 6:09
  19. "The Train" / "Santiago" / "The Soldier's Joy" (Trad) – 11:46
  20. "Widecombe Fair" – 4:19
  21. "Country Life" – 8:25
  22. "Don't Be A Stranger" – 16:18 (see Notes)

[edit] Notes

Track 22 is comprised of 5:03 of "Don't Be A Stranger", 3:33 of silence, and then 7:42 of live between-song banter.

[edit] Credits

  • Steve Knightley - vocals, guitar, mandocello, cuatro
  • Phil Beer - vocals, guitar, slide guitar, fiddle, mandolin, cuatro
  • Miranda Sykes - vocals, double bass
  • Jenna Witts - vocals and keyboards on tracks 15 & 16
