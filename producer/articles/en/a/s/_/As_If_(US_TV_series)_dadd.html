As If was a short-lived comedy-teen-drama that aired on UPN. It was based on successful UK series of the same name.

The series focused on six friends in Los Angeles, with full episodes shot entirely from one perspective. Each episode presented different modern topics that applied to many teens of the day.

The series had extraordinary low rating from the beginning. Seven episodes were produced, but only two were aired.
