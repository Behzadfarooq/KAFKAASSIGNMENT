As One is a Korean pop duo consisting of Korean Americans Lee Min Young and Crystal Chae. They were discovered by one of the former members of critically acclaimed Korean R&B group SOLID who attended their church one day and heard them sing[1]. Their first hit was "Day By Day," released in 1999 on Rock Records and their latest release was in 2004 entitled Restoration. Between their fourth and fifth albums, their contract with Rock Records expired and they signed with EMI Korea, a label which also manages other k-pop stars like DJ DOC and Baby VOX. They have released their last album 12 Tears of Farewell in November of 2006, and have started their promotional activities with the lead song "십이야" (12 Nights).
