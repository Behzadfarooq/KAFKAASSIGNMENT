"As for One Day" was the eighteenth single of J-pop idol group Morning Musume and was released 23 April 2003. It sold a total of 129,893 copies. This single also marks the graduation of second generation member Kei Yasuda, who is still currently in the Hello! Project. This was the last single to hit #1 until 2006's "Aruiteru".
