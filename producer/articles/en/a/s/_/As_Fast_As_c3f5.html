As Fast As is a pop rock band from Portland, Maine which includes former Rustic Overtones keyboardist Spencer Albee. They were formely known as "Rocktopus". Their debut album, 'Open Letter To The Damned' was released on Octone Records in May of 2006. The line-up is:

  • Spencer Albee - Vocalist/Keyboardist/Guitarist/Uke
  • Haché - Bassist
  • Zach Jones - Guitarist
  • Andrew "Smange" Hodgkins [1] - Drummer
