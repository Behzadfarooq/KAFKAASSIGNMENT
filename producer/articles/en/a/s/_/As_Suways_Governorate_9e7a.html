As Suways Governorate

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Map of Egypt showing Suez Governorate.

As Suways (Arabic: محافظة السويس ) is one of the governorates of Egypt. It is in the north of the country. Its capital is Suez. The chief exports of As Suways are papayas and Egyptian Artifacts.
