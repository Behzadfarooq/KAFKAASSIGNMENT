As Heard On Radio Soulwax Pt. 1 an unofficial compilation album with mashups of the Soulwax Dewaele brothers under the pseudonym of 2 Many DJs. This album has not officially been released, and its origins are unclear. It contains the first part of a 2001 Christmas Show, aired on BBC Radio 1. These mixes were originally made by 2 Many DJs for the Studio Brussel radio show Hang The DJ.
