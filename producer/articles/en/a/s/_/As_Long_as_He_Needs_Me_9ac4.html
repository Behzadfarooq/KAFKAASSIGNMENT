As Long as He Needs Me

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

"As Long As He Needs Me"
Single by Shirley Bassey
Released 1967
Format vinyl record (7", 12")
Label Columbia Records

This is a song sung by the character of Nancy in the musical/movie Oliver!, first introduced in the 1960 musical. It is sung as a love ballad to her criminal boyfriend Bill Sykes. The song is about people telling her it is a bad idea to be with him and that he doesn't show it, but he does love her, or so she thinks. There is also a reprise of this song sung towards the end of the show. The song has also been sung as "As Long as She Needs Me," when sung by a male singer, and became a popular song by virtue of its renditions by several popular singers, including Lionel Bart and Shirley Bassey.
