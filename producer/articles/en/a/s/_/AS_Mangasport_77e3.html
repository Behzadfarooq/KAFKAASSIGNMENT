Association Sportive Mangasport (AS Mangasport) is a Gabonese football club based in Moanda. It was founded in 1962. They play at the Stade Henri Sylvoz.

[edit] Achivements

  • Gabon Championnat National D1: 5
1995, 2000, 2004, 2005, 2006
  • Coupe du Gabon Interclubs: 3
1994, 2001, 2005
