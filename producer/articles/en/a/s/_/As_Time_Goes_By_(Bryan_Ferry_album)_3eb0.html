As Time Goes By (Bryan Ferry album)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

As Time Goes By
Studio album by Bryan Ferry
Released 1999
Label Virgin

As Time Goes By is an album by British singer Bryan Ferry, released on Virgin Records in 1999.

It is co-produced by Rhett Davies and Bryan Ferry.
