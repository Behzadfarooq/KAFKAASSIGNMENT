Gold Digging: As Sampled By Kanye West is a 2006 2-Disc mixtape released by rapper and producer Kanye West, and by the Harmless label. It contains one Mixed CD, and one Un-Mixed CD.

  2. "Common (David Ruffin)"

  7. "There Is Nothing This World (Tom Brock)"
  8. "Khusara Khusara (Hossam Ramzy)"
  9. "The Big Beat (Billy Squier)"
  10. "That’s How Long I Love You (Chi Lites)"
  11. "Unknown"
