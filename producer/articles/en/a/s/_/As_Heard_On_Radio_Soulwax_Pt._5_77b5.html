As Heard On Radio Soulwax Pt. 5 is the fifth installment in the unofficial (bar Pt. 2) Radio Soulwax album series, mixed by 2 Many DJs, alter egos of Soulwax's Dewaele brothers. This album has not officially been released, and its origins are unclear. It contains mixes made by 2 Many DJs for the first series of the Studio Brussel radio show Hang The DJ.
