As Heard On Radio Soulwax Pt. 4 is an unofficial mashup album with mixes of 2 Many DJs - the counterparts of the alternative rock band Soulwax. This album has not officially been released, and its origins are unclear. It contains mixes made by 2 Many DJs for the first series of the Studio Brussel radio show Hang The DJ.
