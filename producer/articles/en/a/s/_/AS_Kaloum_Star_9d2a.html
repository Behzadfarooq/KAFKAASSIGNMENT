AS Kaloum Star is a Football club based in Conakry, Guinea.

[edit] Achievements

  • CAF Cup: 1
    • Finalist : 1995
  • Guinée Championnat National: 8
    • 1980, 1981, 1984, 1987, 1993, 1995, 1996, 1998
  • Guinée Coupe Nationale: 5
    • 1985, 1997, 1998, 2001, 2005
