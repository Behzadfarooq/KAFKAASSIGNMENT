The Association Sportive de Saint Priest is a French association football team founded in 1945. They are based in Saint-Priest, France and are currently playing in the Championnat de France Amateurs Group B. They play at the Stade Jean Jaurès in Saint-Priest, which has a capacity of 3,000.
