"As Jy Met Vuur Speel Sal Jy Brand", directly translated to "if you play with fire you will burn," is the debut CD of the South African punk rock band Fokofpolisiekar. It is an eight track EP releaed in 2003 by Rhythm Records in South Africa.

Tracklisting

1. "Hemel op die Platteland"-2:40 (translated "Heaven on the Countryside")

2. "Maak of Braak"-2:26 (translated "Make it or vomit", a wordplay on "Make or break")

3. "Hemel op die Platteland (Acoustic)"- 3:20

4. "Tygerberg Vliegtuig"- 10:03 (translated "Tygerberg Aeroplane")

5. "Verklaar"- 2:46 (translated "Clarify")

6. "Vernietig Jouself"-1:59 (translated "Destroy Yourself")

7. "As Jy Met Vuur Speel Sal Jy Brand"-2:26 (translated "If You Play With Fire You Will Burn)

8. Hidden Track "Fokofpolisiekar"-2:35 (translated "Fuck off police car")
