[edit] How to warn vandals

In order for administrators to take action against persistent vandals, vandals need to be warned via the appropriate templates. Simply posting a personal comment, as you did with 172.164.167.252, will not be considered a warning for administrative blocking purposes. The warning templates are located in chart form at WP:VAND. You can just choose the appropriate one for the situation and paste it in. MSJapan 17:34, 3 February 2007 (UTC)
