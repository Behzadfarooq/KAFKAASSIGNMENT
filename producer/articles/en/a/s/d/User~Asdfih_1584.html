[edit] About me

  • I am a twenty-one year old Brazilian guy. I am about to major in Economics for Universidade Federal do Rio de Janeiro.
  • I am particularly interested in Japan and her culture, history, language and economy. My graduation thesis was about the Japanese Industrialization in the Meiji Era. I intend to go on studying this theme for my Master's Course.
  • I love Japanese music (Jpop and Jrock, specially Vkei). I also am fond of Korean music.
  • I love learning languages, and I can speak English, Japanese, French, and some Spanish, besides Portuguese, my mother tongue. I have Cambridge CAE diploma, and also JLPT 2kyuu, and have been trying the 1kyuu for two years. I have never taken any French examinations, although I could achieve a DELF level.
  • Finally, I am also interested in History of Economic Thought (HET), Economic History and Compared Development Studies.
