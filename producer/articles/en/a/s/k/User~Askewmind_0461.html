I joined the comunity of wikipedians on February 10th 2005, but I have always supported the idea of making knowledge available to everyone.

[edit] Biography

I am a physicist with far too much time to procrastinate. I consider myself to be a secular humanist. I currently live in the Switzerland and when I'm not writing for my thesis, I can be found with my friends, watching classic sci-fi shows, travelling around or reading. I am bilingual in Spanish and English.
