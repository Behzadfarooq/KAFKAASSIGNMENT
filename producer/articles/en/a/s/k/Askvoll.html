Askvoll is a municipality in the county of Sogn og Fjordane, Norway.

The village Gjelsvik, in Askvoll.


[edit] The name

The municipality (originally the parish) is named after the farm Askvoll (Norse Askvöllr), since the first church was built there. The first element is askr m 'ash tree (Fraxinus excelsior)', the last element is völlr m 'meadow'.

[edit] Coat-of-arms

The coat-of-arms is from modern times (1990). It shows an old stone cross (Korssundkrossen).
