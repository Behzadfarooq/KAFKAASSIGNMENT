The Askern Branch Line is a railway line in South, North and West Yorkshire in England

It runs from Askern Junction near Thorpe in Balne on the East Coast Main Line between Doncaster and York or Selby then runs through Askern, Norton, Womersley, Ferrybridge and finally Knottingley.
