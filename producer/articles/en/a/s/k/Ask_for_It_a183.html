Ask for It is an EP by the band Hole, containing tracks from a November 19,1991 John Peel session and a spring 1992 session. "Pale Blue Eyes" was recorded live at the Whisky a Go Go in West Hollywood, California in March 1992. "Over The Edge" also appears on the Tim/Kerr Records tribute to Greg Sage and The Wipers.

[edit] Track listing

  1. "Over the Edge" (Greg Sage) – 2:47
  2. "Pale Blue Eyes" (Lou Reed) – 3:56
  3. "Drown Soda" (Hole) – 3:51
  4. "Doll Parts" (Hole) – 2:21
  5. "Violet" (Hole) – 3:36
  6. "Forming/Hot Chocolate Boy" (The Germs/Beat Happening) – 1:32
