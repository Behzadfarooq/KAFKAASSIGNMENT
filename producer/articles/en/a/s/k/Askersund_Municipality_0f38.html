Askersund Municipality is a municipality, in Örebro County, in central Sweden where the town Askersund (pop. 4,000) is seat.

[edit] Geography

The city is located in the northern bay of Lake Vättern.

[edit] Towns

  • Askersund
  • Zinkgruvan
  • Lerbäck
  • Mariedamm
  • Åmmeberg
  • Åsbro

The town Askersund traces its origin from the 14th century. In 1643 it was significant enough to received its charter as one of the cities in Sweden.

In the 19th century, Askersund was planned to be the site of one of the two supply fortresses for Karlsborg Fortress, located on an island in lake Vättern, now belonging to Karlsborg Municipality.
