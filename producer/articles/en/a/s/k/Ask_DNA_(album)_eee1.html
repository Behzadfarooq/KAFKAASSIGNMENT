Ask DNA is a mini-album by The Seatbelts, released in 2001. It consists of music from Cowboy Bebop: The Movie, including the title theme, "Ask DNA".

    • Lyrics: Raj Ramayya [[3]] [[4]]
  4. "Hamduche" – 1:49
    • Vocals: Hassan Bomhide
