Asker S.K. is a Norwegian football club from Asker. Asker's women's football team is one of the most successful clubs ever in Norway, with 6 Premier League championships and 5 cup championships. The men currently play in the 3rd division (level four), and have a cup silver from 1951 as their best achievement.

[edit] Unbeaten seasons

Asker S.K. become historic in 1998, when the women's team won all its Premier League matches. Incidentally, Asker S.K.'s men did the same thing in 2005, though in the 3rd division, and not the Premier League.
