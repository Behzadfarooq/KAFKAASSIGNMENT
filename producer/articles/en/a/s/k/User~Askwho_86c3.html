Thomas Askew is Welsh by birth and half Welsh half English by heritage. (Change of person) I was born in Cardiff, my mother having been sent to her parents there because of the outbreak of gulf war in Saudi Arabia where she was living at the time. It was not long before we moved back to Saudi Arabia, where we stayed for a number of years, making our first international friends (most of whom are now living in the uk, including these), the Nickelsons. The next country we lived in was Singapore, which was very clean, if a little restrictive, there wasn’t much to do there, but we did make our second international friends there, the Tailors (who also moved to the uk). The following country we lived in is, and I suspect always will be, my personal favorite, Australia (which I am visiting again in December of this year, yay!) it was lovely and warm in the Christmas period, and refreshingly cool in the middle of the year, (a much better way of going about things), this was also the place where we met the last, but most certainly not least, of our international friends, the Gerrys. Finally we moved to England where I have been living for just under 8 years.

I am a Scientist at heart, I live for physics, with chemistry ranking second and math’s following very close behind, unfortunately I appear to be quite the minority in that respect. I also have Dysgraphia, but I try not to let it hold me back

I am a strong atheist, which anyone who knows me will know, which I proclaim in my continuing quest to keep religion out of science classrooms.
