Askola (IPA: /ˈaskola/) is a municipality of Finland.

It is located in the province of Southern Finland and is part of the Eastern Uusimaa region. The municipality has a population of 4,530 (2004-12-31) and covers an area of 218.42 km² of which 5.18 km² is water. The population density is 21.24 inhabitants per km².

The municipality is unilingually Finnish.
