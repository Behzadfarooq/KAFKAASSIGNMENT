Ashley singer/songwriter and actress from England. She was previously a part of the pop music band, Soda Club. Since then, Ashley who has drawn comparison to Christina Aguilera, released her solo debut album, "Dreaming" in the U.S. and abroad in 2005 on Water Music Records. Mainly because of weak promotional pushes and lack of strong public relations, the album did not get a fair chance at charting on the Billboard 200 Albums chart. "Dreaming" features the chart hits, "Let Me Be Your Fantasy", which is a cover of the Rave classic, by Baby D. The albums second single, "On The Run" became even more popular than the previous single. The third single from "Dreaming" is "Set You Free."
