Ashish is a popular Nepali and Indian male first name that means "blessing". Among the persons with this first name are:

  • Ashish Juwa, first student from south Asian country (Nepal) to be honored with STARR foundation Scholarship
  • Ashish Gupta, first website developer of UIET (Panjab University).
  • Ashish Kumar, First GRC Support person from India.
  • Ashish Bagai, a Canadian cricketer, a batsman and wicketkeeper.
  • Ashish Gulhati, an online activist and open source hacker.
  • Ashish Nehra, an Indian cricketer, a bowler of fast-medium pace.
  • Ashish Patel, a Canadian cricketer, a medium pace bowler.
  • Ashish Vidyarthi, a National Award winning actor in India.
  • Ashish Advani, an American actor, popular from work on MTV.
  • Ashish Kumar Saha, a Bangali Computer Engineer, popular for founding the First ever advertisement based website of Bangladesh.
