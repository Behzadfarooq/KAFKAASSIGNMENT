Ashley Hall (musician)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

You have new messages (last change).
Not to be confused with the South Carolina school, Ashley Hall.

Ashley Hall was a musician who did the singing voice of Cooler in 1988's Pound Puppies and the Legend of Big Paw. He also composed the scores for Million Dollar Mystery (1987) and Happily Ever After (1993). [1]
