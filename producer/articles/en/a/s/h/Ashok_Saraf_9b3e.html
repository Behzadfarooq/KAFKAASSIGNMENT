Ashok Saraf ( born January 1 ) is an Indian actor and Comedian. He has appeared in many Hindi and Marathi movies.

Ashok Saraf has been well-known for his great comedies and is considered to be one of the best Marathi comedians that Bollywood has seen so far. He has also starred in famous Hindi serials like "Yeh Choti Badi Baatein" and "Hum Paanch".
