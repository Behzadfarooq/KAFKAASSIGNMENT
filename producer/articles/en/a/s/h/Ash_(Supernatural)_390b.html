Ash is a fictional character on The CW Television Network's Supernatural portrayed by Chad Lindberg.

[edit] Biography

Spoiler warning: Plot and/or ending details follow.

Ash attended the Massachusetts Institute of Technology before he was kicked out for fighting. It is unknown when he met Ellen and Jo Harvelle, but he lives in the Roadhouse Saloon, which Ellen runs. He has a room in the Roadhouse with a sign on the door reading, "Dr. Badass is" with the option of "in" or "out." He owns a homemade laptop, which he uses to track the paranormal, particularly The Demon, with the information that John Winchester and his sons, Sam and Dean, have gathered. Jo refers to him as a genius.

Spoilers end here.
