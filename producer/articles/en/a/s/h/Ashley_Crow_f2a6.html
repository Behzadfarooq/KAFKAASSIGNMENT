Ashley Crow (born on August 25, 1960 in Birmingham, Alabama) is an American actress. A notable role she has is Sandra Bennet on Heroes.

[edit] Trivia

  • Ms. Crow's real-life husband is Heroes cast member Matthew John Armstrong, who plays the role of Ted Sprague, a man with radioactive power.
