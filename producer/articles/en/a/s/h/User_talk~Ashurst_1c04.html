[edit] User:Ashurst

Please delete all material relating to your law firm from your user page. Wikipedia:Userpage#What_can_I_not_have_on_my_user_page prohibits "substantial content on your user page that is unrelated to Wikipedia." I'm sorry that I seem so heavy handed, but these policies are there from preventing everyone from turning Wikipedia into their own advertising service -- you're not the first one (and won't be the last).

Regarding the "advert" notice, please do not remove it until you expand the article. It is there to remind everyone that more work needs to be done on this article in order to remain in an encyclopedia. Articles that fall short of our standards will be deleted. Don't just talk about what your firm offers. Write about its history, famous cases it's won or loss, references to press coverage does wonders for an article's chances at survival. Since you started this article, you're now obliged to work on it. Wikipedia isn't a free ride.

PS: You can reply to any of these messages and ask for help by editing this very page. Please don't remove anything though, because that is against policy. Cheers,  Netsnipe  (Talk)  18:00, 11 July 2006 (UTC)
