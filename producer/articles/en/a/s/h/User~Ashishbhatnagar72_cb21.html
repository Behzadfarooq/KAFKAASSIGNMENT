[edit] Ashish Bhatnagar

Ashish Bhatnagar

Last update:--Ashishbhatnagar72 11:16, 29 September 2006 (UTC)

  • Ashish Bhatnagar was born on Nov 26,1972 at 02:35 Hrs at Lucknow, Capital of Uttar Pradesh(UP) state of India. Presently he is residing at New Delhi, the capital of INDIA.

He was born to Sh. Kamal Prakash Bhatnagar (S/o Lt. Sh. Mohan Lal Bhatnagar & Smt. Rajwati Devi) and Smt. Poonam Bhatnagar (D/o Lt. Sh. Rajeshwar Swaroop Kashiva & Lt.Smt. Chandrakala Kashiva {both formerly Bhatnagar}).
He is having one sister Akanksha Bhatnagar, Married to Sh. Amit Kumar Bhatnagar (S/o Sh. Bhatnagar & Smt. Bina Roy Bhatnagar, of Bhind, Gwalior, U.P., currently residing at Ambikapur, Chhattisgarh,India), who is residing at Andheri,Mumbai, with their son, Amish Bhatnagar, born on 7 Nov, 2004 at New Delhi.
He finished his schooling from Tagore International School, East of Kailash, New Delhi and Dayawati Modi Public School,Modinagar, Distt. Ghaziabad-201204, UP, India. Then he did his B.Tech.(Electronics & Communications) from Delhi College of Engineering, Delhi, India.

He is currently employed with Airports Authority of India [1], presently posted at Dte. of Communications, Corporate Headquarters, Rajiv Gandhi Bhawan, Safdarjung Airport, NewDelhi - 110 003.
