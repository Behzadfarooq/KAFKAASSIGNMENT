Ashley Paske (born 19 July 1970 in Nowra, New South Wales, Australia) is an actor, best known for his role in the television series Neighbours. He played Matt Robinson in the series from 1989 to 1991.

He had previously been a regular cast member on the short-lived soap opera Richmond Hill.

His most recent work was a guest role in All Saints.
