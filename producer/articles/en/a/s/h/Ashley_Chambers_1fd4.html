Ashley Chambers (born 1 March 1990 in Leicester) is an English footballer currently playing for Leicester City.

Chambers has been at the club since he was eight,and has been tipped for a great future alongside other young players at Leicester like James Wesolowski, Richard Stearman, Alan Sheehan, Levi Porter, Conrad Logan, and Joe Mattock.

He has already represented England at Under-17 level and scored a Hat-Trick on his debut against the Faroe Islands in the Nordic tournament in the summer of 2005.

After being a consistent perfomer for the Leicester Under-18s and reserves,he made history on 20 September 2005, becoming Leicester's youngest ever player, aged 15 years and 203 days as he came on as a substitute in the Foxes' Carling Cup second round win over Blackpool.
