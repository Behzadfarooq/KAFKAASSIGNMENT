Ashok (Telugu: అశోక్) is a 2006 Telugu film which released on July 13, 2006. Surender Reddy(Athanokkade fame), is directed this film. Jr. NTR and Sameera Reddy are played the main leads. Music by Mani Sharma was a big hit among the audiences.[1] The film released with 228 prints.[2]

[edit] Story

Spoiler warning: Plot and/or ending details follow.

A auto mobile mechanic Ashok (Jr. NTR) is thrown out of the house by his rough acts which kills his grand mother (Ramaprabha). His father (Prakash Raj) is compelled to through out Ashok. Anjali (Sameera Reddy) is a neighbour and Ashok falls in love with her. K.K.(Sonu Sood) sees Anjali in an accident and he also falls in love with her. While saving Anjali from the clutches of K.K, Ashok's friend Raja (Rajeev Kanakala) is killed by K.K.'s gang. This angers Ashok who is sent off to Delhi according to a plan laid by K.K, K.K.'s mother(Vadipukarassi) and Ashok's father and he rushes back to Hyderabad to save his family and Anjali. While Ashok is fighting with K.K., K.K.'s mother enters the seen to shoot Ashok. But the bullet hits K.K. and K.K.'s mother falls down. Ashok takes away Anjali.

Spoilers end here.

[edit] Trivia

Jr. NTR and Sameera Reddy as featured in the film
  • This film collected 10 crores in the opening week, about $2 million.[3]
  • This film was also given an 'A' certificate, along with other Telugu releases in 2006 such as Bangaram, Pokiri, and Vikramarkudu.
  • The film was shot in Super 35 format.
  • Indian Idol runner-up Karunya sang a song in this film.
