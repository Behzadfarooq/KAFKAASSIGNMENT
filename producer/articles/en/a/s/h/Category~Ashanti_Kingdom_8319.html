The Ashanti Kingdom today is located in Ghana, Africa. The people of the Ashanti Kingdom are called the Ashanti, their ruler is the Asantehene. The royal residence city of the Ashanti Kingdom is Kumasi. There, the royal regalia such as the Golden Stool is also kept.

For a detailed history of the Ashanti Kingdom, please see: Ashanti Confederacy.

For a list of the Asantehene rulers, please see: Asantehene.
