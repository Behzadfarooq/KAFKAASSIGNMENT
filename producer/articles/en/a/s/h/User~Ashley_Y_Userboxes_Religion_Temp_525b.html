[edit] Other spiritual and mystical beliefs and philosophies

For general beliefs in or philosophies regarding the divine, deities, the supernatural, mysticism, superstitions, and other areas that aren't qualified as organized "religions". This includes belief in the soul, in guardian angels, in New Age traditions, or in vampires. It also includes philosophies characterized by a lack of belief in those things. For Zodiac-related templates, see Wikipedia:Userboxes/Zodiac.
