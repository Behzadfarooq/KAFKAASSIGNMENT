[edit] Education

  • B.E in Computer Science from Motilal Nehru National Institute of Technology, Allahabad, 1993-1997 Batch.
  • M.S in Software Systems from BITS Pilani in 1999-2000.


[edit] Work Profile

  • My current research is in area of embedded systems, Converged Services and Seamless Mobility at Motorola in Bangalore, India for future prototype development of VOIP applications on products using Java, OCAP:OpenCable Application Platform and SIP.
  • Prior experience includes work in areas of call processing, switching, ISDN,DECT and SIP technologies in companies like C-DOT, Sasken, Nortel Canada and Varaha Systems.

[edit] Awards and Appreciation

  • All India Rank 142 in National Science Talent Search Examination in March-1992.
  • Got the group award for excellence in C-DOT May-2000.
  • Got Q Star and Smart Project Awards in Sasken for customer appreciation in 2001 and 2003.
