Ashley Blue (born July 8, 1981) is an American pornographic actress most famous for her rough anal sex scenes (sometimes dubbed as The Anal Queen) and deep throat blowjobs, as well as pushing her own hand down her throat. [2]

Prior to entering the adult film industry, she had several jobs, and was "[g]etting fired from every job I've had. I was a receptionist. I was a waitress. I got fired as a hostess at a restaurant for sleeping." [2]

Ashley Blue has won numerous Adult Video News awards, including 2004 Female Performer of the Year and 2005 Best Supporting Actress. [3] She is currently a contract performer for JM Productions and is the star of the Girlvert series.

According to Blue, "Girlvert is a mockery of my life. I ran away from home. I started doing porno. I hate my mom. I hate my dad. Everything that has happened to me in porn [is in the series]. Me getting together with Trent. Me dumping him. Me calling him a fag. Every time I get gonorrhea, we throw it into the story." [2]

Blue has also been romantically linked to actors within the industry, including Trent Tesoro and Jonni Darkko. There was also an attempt by a friend of hers to get her on The Surreal Life, though this failed mainly because the producers of the series were attempting to steer away from using pornographic actors.
