Ashley Margaret Anne Leggat (born September 26, 1986 in Hamilton, Ontario, Canada) is a Canadian actress who is known for her roles as "Casey MacDonald" in the Canadian series Life with Derek,"Brittany MacMillan" in the Discovery Kids series Darcy's Wild Life and as "Kelly" in the CBS series 11 Cameras

[edit] Trivia

  • Has three cats named Baby, Punk, and Furbee.
  • She has 4 older brothers, Rob, Brett, Todd and Bram.
  • She started dancing when she was 2.
  • A student of ballet, pointe, tap, jazz, lyrical, musical theatre and hip hop, she has been honored with awards and accolades at several prestigious North American competitions.
  • In 2004, she garnered the senior title of Miss Dance at the Dance, Dance, Dance competition; top senior dancer at Flash Dance, and Senior Title and Dancer of the Year at 5-6-7-8 Showtime, as well as being named the top senior dancer at the Can Dance competition.
