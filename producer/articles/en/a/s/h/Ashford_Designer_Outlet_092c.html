[edit] Information

The McArthur Glen Ashford Designer Outlet was designed by architect Lord Richard Rogers, and opened in March of 2000. It is located near Ashford town centre, adjacent to the Ashford International Eurostar station and a few minutes’ drive from Junction 10 of the M20 motorway.

Designed by world-famous architect Richard Rogers, Ashford Designer Outlet is one of Europe’s most spectacular shopping destinations, and it boasts an equally impressive line-up of over 120 top designer brands.

There is a regular bus that visits each Ashford Town, International Station and Ashford Designer Outlet, on a regular cycle.
