Ashigarashimo District, Kanagawa

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Ashigarashimo (足柄下郡; -gun) is a district located in Kanagawa, Japan.

As of 2003, the district has an estimated population of 50,862 and a density of 361.16 persons per km². The total area is 140.83 km².
