Ashburton Park is a park located in Woodside, London. The park is located next to Woodside tram stop in the London Borough of Croydon. Tramlink Routes 1 and 2 service the park. It is on the junction of Lower Addiscombe Road and Spring Lane. The park covers an area of 18½ acres (7.49 hectares). The park includes a village green and a café.

[edit] Facilities

The facilities in the park include:

  • Car park (entrance in Tenterden Road)
  • Childrens playground
  • Toilets
  • Shelter
  • Bowling green & pavilion
  • Netball & basketball court
  • Tennis courts
  • Petanque terrain

There are also recycling facilities for paper and bottles in the park. The ground is enclosed and locked at nights.
