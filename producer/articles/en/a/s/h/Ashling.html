Ashling is a science fiction novel by Isobelle Carmody, set in a post apocalyptic world. It is the third book in the Obernewtyn Chronicles.

[edit] Plot summary

Spoiler warning: Plot and/or ending details follow.

"Sometimes dreams were gateways through which messages might come. Beasts called them ashlings: dreams that called..."

In order to seal an alliance between the Misfit community at Obernewtyn and rebel forces, the powerful young farseeker, Elspeth Gordie, disguises herself as a gypsy and travels to dangerous Sutrium, seat of the oppressive ruling Councilcourt.

She journeys anxiously, fearing the long-awaited dream summons from the OldOnes to fulfil her secret destiny will interrupt her task for the Master of Obernewtyn. But through encounters with slavers, Herder priests, rebels and enigmatic nomads from the distant lands of Sador, Elspeth learns that even now she does the bidding of the OldOnes.

She is reassured, until she discovers that she is being stalked through her dreams. Her only protection is the ravaged, half mad, telepathic cat, Maruman...

[edit] Characters in "Ashling"

The story is told through the first person point of view of Elspeth Gordie.

  • Elspeth Gordie, A talented Misfit, Guildmistress of the Farseekers Guild.
  • Rushton Seraphim, A misfit with latent talents, heir to and Master of Obernewtyn.
  • Matthew (obernewtyn chronicles), A talented Misfit, Guilden of the Farseekers
  • Dameon, A blind talented Misfit, Guildmaster of the Empath Guild.
  • Maryon, A talented Misfit, a Futureteller.
  • Maruman, an enigmatic cat with prophetic tendencies.
  • Gahltha, A black stallion, self appointed guardian of Elspeth Gordie.
  • Atthis, Eldar of the Agyllians.
  • Iriny, An injured half-breed gypsy woman.
  • Kella, A talented Misfit, a Healer, bonded to Domick and living in Sutrium.
  • Domick, A talented Misfit, a Coercer, bonded to Kella, and spy in the Councilcourt, his alias is Mika.
  • Dragon, A half wild talented Misfit with no memory of her origins, a coercer-empath, in love with Matthew.
  • Brydda Llewellyn, A notorious rebel and Seditioner; also known as the Black Dog.
  • Daffyd, A talented Misfit, formerly a bondsman of Henry Druid, tracking down the slaver Salamander.
  • Swallow, A full blooded twentyfamilies Gypsy.
  • Malik, A rebel leader from Guanette, highly prejudiced against Misfits.
  • Jakoby, A tribeswoman from Sador.
Spoilers end here.
