Ashfield F.C.

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Ashfield Football Club are a Scottish football (soccer) club from Possilpark in the North of Glasgow. Formed in 1886, they are based at Saracen Park, which they share with the Glasgow Tigers speedway team. They compete in the West Region of the Scottish Junior Football Association and wear black and white hooped strips (uniforms).

They used to be one of the top Junior clubs in the country but are now one of the smaller teams in their region. They suffered a set back in October, 2004 when there was a fire at Saracen Park and they lost a lot of records and strips.

Glasgow Perthshire F.C. are located very close to Ashfield F.C.

[edit] Honours

[edit] Scottish Junior Cup

  • Winners: 1893-94, 1894-95, 1904-05, 1909-10
  • Runners-up: 1913-14, 1920-21

[edit] Other Honours

  • West of Scotland Junior Cup winners: 1927-28, 1928-29
  • Glasgow Junior Football League winners: 1905-06, 1906-07, 1907-08, 1908-09, 1909-10, 1920-21
  • Scottish Intermediate Football League winners: 1928-29
  • Central Junior Football League champions: 1952-53, 1954-55
  • Central League C Division winners: 1968-69
  • Central Division Two winners: 1990-91
  • Glasgow Junior Cup: 1893-94, 1905-06, 1906-07, 1907-08, 1908-09, 1909-10, 1914-15, 1925-26, 1951-52, 1952-53, 1962-63
  • Glasgow Dryburgh Cup: 1926-27, 1928-29, 1946-47, 1952-53, 1962-63
