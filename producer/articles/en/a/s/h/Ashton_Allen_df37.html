Ashton Allen is an American singer/songwriter from Jacksonville, Florida. Ashton ventured out on his solo career in 2005 as his band Big Sky split up. September 27, 2005 saw the release of Dewdrops on Atlanta record company Livewire Recordings. Produced by veteran producer Colin Cobb, Dewdrops showcases Ashton's honesty and creativity in this 14 track album.

In July 2005, Ashton Allen and Tour Manager Michael Grosvenor embarked on a 250 date tour in support of Dewdrops. It featured an in-store performance tour in conjunction with a promotional campaign with retail giant Barnes & Noble. That tour wrapped up in the Fall of 2006 with a 6 week full band U.S. Tour.

Ashton has shared a stage with Brian Vander Ark of The Verve Pipe, Duncan Sheik, RobinElla, and Peter Bradley Adams. His music has also been featured on two ABC TV shows "What About Brian" and "Six Degrees".
