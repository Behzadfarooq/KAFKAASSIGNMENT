Ashlea Evans (born April 24, 1983 in Washington, D.C.) was the first contestant voted off the sixth season of the CBS reality show Big Brother. Before the show, Ashlea was a fashion design student living in Plantation, Florida.

[edit] Big Brother 6

In 2005, Ashlea was one of fourteen Big Brother 6 contestants.

Ashlea was nominated the first week, along with Kaysar. The other houseguests said they had found her to be annoying, and she was subsequently evicted in a vote of nine to two, by the other house guests. In a twist where one of the first four evicted contestants could come back, the house was told that Ashlea would not be eligible to return because she opted to leave the sequester house.
