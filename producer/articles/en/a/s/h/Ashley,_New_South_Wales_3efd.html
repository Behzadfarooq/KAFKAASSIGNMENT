Ashley, New South Wales

Ashley (29°18′S 149°49′E) is a small community of 250 people 10 km north of Moree, New South Wales, Australia. It is a major cotton centre with two large cotton gins already operating in the district. Other agriculture such as sheep, wool, wheat and beef cattle are also found operating in the Ashley area. There is a hall, a park and a small fire service.
