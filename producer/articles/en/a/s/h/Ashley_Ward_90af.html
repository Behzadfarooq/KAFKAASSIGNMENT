Ashley Ward

Ashley Ward (born November 24, 1970 in Manchester) is a retired English footballer. He was a centre forward, noted for his strength and ability to retain control of the ball.

He played for at least a dozen different clubs in a long career, including Crewe Alexandra, where he made his name, Norwich City, Blackburn Rovers and Bradford City. He retired in the summer of 2005.

He now runs a luxury property development company, specialising in the building and renovation of luxury homes in Cheshire. He has built many footballers' homes, including those of Kevin Campbell and Wayne Rooney.

[edit] Trivia

Ward holds the dubious honour of being the player relegated most times from the Premiership, happening on a total of four occasions (with Norwich City in 1995; Barnsley in 1998; Blackburn Rovers, 1999; and Bradford City in 2001).[citation needed]
