[edit] Elmina

Regarding the sentence, "In 1863 the Ashanti again invaded the British coastal possessions, but were rebuffed and the important coastal town of Elmina fell to the British.", I can find no sources regarding Elmina in 1863. I assume this sentence means that the Ashanti controlled Elmina in 1863, but fought the British and their allies and lost control of it. I can find no sources citing this happening. Please cite a source for this if you put it back. Mr. Know-It-All 18:51, 14 January 2006 (UTC)
