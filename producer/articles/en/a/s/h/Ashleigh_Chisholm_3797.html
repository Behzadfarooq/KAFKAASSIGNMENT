Ashleigh Chisholm (b. 6 June 1990) starred as Felicity 'Fliss' Sidebotham in The Sleepover Club, a children's television show about five pre-teenage girls. Her character, Fliss, was a fashion purist, and in love with the hottest boy in school.

Ashleigh also had a guest appearance as Jennelle on an episode of Home and Away.
