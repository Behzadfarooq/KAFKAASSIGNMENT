P.S. Regarding your changes to Adagio and Remo Giazotto: Please note that Giazotto's Adagio, famous as it is, doesn't belong in the Adagio article. Quoting Tempo: Generally, composers (or music publishers) will name movements of classical compositions (and in some cases individual compositions) after their tempo (and/or mood) marking, as for instance in Samuel Barber's Adagio for Strings. Similarly, Giazotto's work is referred to as Adagio per flauto, archi, e organo.

Also, according to wiki-syntax, you should use

''text''

instead of <i>text</i>. Many people (including me:-) agree however, that for hyperlinked titles additional emphasis is often unnecessary. Thus, I would leave [[Adagio]] as it is. --Glimz

Point taken—Trevor Caira 22:14, 29 Nov 2004 (UTC)
