Ashley the Smoking Car

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Ashley is the smoking car from Starlight Express. She's sweet and loving but has a bad habit when it comes to smoking. Even though she doesn't play a large part, she is very popular.

Erica Metz as Ashley

[edit] Musical Numbers

When Belle was taken out, she took over the vocals in "Rolling Stock." She also sings along with the cars during "Freight" and "A Lotta Locomotion". During "Girls' Rolling Stock" and "U.N.C.O.U.P.L.E.D" she sings along with Buffy.

Ashley on Ice.

[edit] Outfit

Since Ashley's outfit is very dark, it is very hard to see her. It consits mainly of dark browns. Her skirt is like a lampshade with a floral pattern. Her bust is ashtrays and most of her skin is covered with black fish net. Her hair is shaped like a chimney.

On Starlight on Ice, Ashley's outfit is desgined to look like a cigarette. On her skirt is a purple box that reads 'smoking' in big white letters. Her hair is wispy instead of shaped like before. She has metal rims around her shoulders.
