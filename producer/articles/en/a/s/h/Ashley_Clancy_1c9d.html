Ashley Clancy (born June 17, 1980) is an Australian rules footballer. Ashley drafted to Fremantle's rookie list in 1999 and elevated to the Senior List for the 2000 and 2001 seasons. Due to a series of unfortunate injuries at his time at Fremantle he was unable to make his league debut and reach his potential for the football club.
