[edit] Ashutosh on Wiki

Ashutosh is a well known star, who is easily recognized by many. He was born to a teacher-cum-supervisor and an overseas property manager. He was enrolled into Kinderland in 1996 and was in Mrs Sarada's nursery and K1 class. In 1998, he graduated from the school.

His parents then enrolled him in St Michael's School where he was placed in Mrs Segar's 1F. He left the school in 2004, with a PSLE score of 242. He was admitted into SJI, under the care of Ms Wong in class 108.

After his streaming examinations in 2006, he was placed in 335 in 2007.
