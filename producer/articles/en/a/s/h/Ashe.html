Ashe is a kebele name. It is one of the kebeles (or peasant associations, PAs) of Hadiya Zone

Ashe is a surname.

Ashe may also refer to:

  • Ashe, a male creator in Yoruba mythology
  • Ashe, village in Hampshire, England
  • Campsea Ashe, village in Suffolk, England
  • Ashe, a character in the game Final Fantasy XII
  • Ashe is a character in a series of books called Symphony of Ages see [Ashe]
