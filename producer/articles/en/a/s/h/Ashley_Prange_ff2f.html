Ashley Prange (born November 24, 1981 in Newport Beach, California) is a professional golfer and winner of the reality television program The Big Break V. She won two Futures Tour events in 2006. In 2007, she became a non-exempt member of the LPGA Tour and currently competes on both the LPGA Tour and the Futures Tour. Prange credits her father Bob Prange, who has been her “main influence, backbone and rock,” as the individual most influencing her career.[1]

Prange is a 2004 graduate of the University of North Carolina where she won three events. Prange won two collegiate events and recorded 15 top-10 finishes during her career. She was a 2003 NCAA All-America Honorable Mention and 2004 NCAA First-Team All-American.

Prange lives in Noblesville, Indiana.
