Ashley Williams (Soccer Player)

Ashley Williams is a Welsh professional football player. A product of the Newport County youth academy, Williams is a powerful midfield player who can also operate at right back. He reached a century of first team appearances for Newport County while still in his teens and he has been capped by Wales at schoolboy international level.
