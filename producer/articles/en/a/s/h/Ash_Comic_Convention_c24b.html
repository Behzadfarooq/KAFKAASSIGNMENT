Ash Comics, also known as Ash Comics & Shows, is a convention for fans of comics.

The convention usually takes place every other month (February, April, June, August, October and December) of the year at the Indianapolis Holiday Inn East.

Some of the attendees cosplay while attending the convention, but most are there to buy comics, art, and anime. The convention advertises "60+ tables of Toys, Videos, non-sports cards, posters, gaming, Guests, Fun and 1000's & 1000's of Comics from the 1930s to now! Something for every collector." Comics are priced from 25 cents to thousands of dollars.

The Convention boasts an Anime room sponsored by the Indianapolis Anime Club. At the show, the anime shown encompasses many types and art styles from science fiction to mystery to horror to something for the children and the entire family.

One notable guest is Stuart Sayger, the author of the Shiver in the Dark comic book series. Previous guests have included luminaries from the "Golden Age", "Silver Age" and recent artist, writers and publishers. Past guests have included: Julius Schwartz, Murphy Anderson, Don Rosa, Ron Frenz, Willie Peppers, Denny O'Neil, Mark Waid, Scott Rosema, Shelly Moldoff, Mart Nodell, Gary Barker and many others, including artists of Superman and the X-Men.
