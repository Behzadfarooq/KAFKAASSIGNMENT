Ashley Erin Aull (born October 9, 1985) is a beauty queen from Lansing, Kansas who has competed in the Miss USA pageant.

Aull won the Miss Kansas USA 2006 title in a state pageant held in late 2005. It was her second attempt at the title as she had placed fourth runner-up to Rachel Saunders in 2005. Aull comes from Lansing, Kansas. She represented Kansas in the Miss USA 2006 pageant broadcast live from Baltimore, Maryland in April 2006, but did not place. The pageant was won by Tara Conner of Kentucky.

Aull graduated from Lansing High School in 2004. She is currently studying an Associates degree in General Education from Johnson County Community College and hopes to go on to four-year college. She also works as a waitress.

Aull is involved in a number of sports, and is an accomplished volleyball player, having won the state championship. She has enjoyed snow skiing, water skiing and parasailing. Aull also enjoys modeling, and hopes to model professionally and abroad.
