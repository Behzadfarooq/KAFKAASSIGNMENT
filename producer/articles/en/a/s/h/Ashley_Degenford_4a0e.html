Ashley Degenford

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Ashley Dengeford on The X Show

Ashley Degenford (born Peoria, Illinois, 1977) is an American model who is probably best known for winning Perfect 10 Magazine's first annual model search in 1998. She also appeared dressed in lingerie as a movie premiere correspondent who interviewed Hollywood stars on the red carpet and later as an adult video critic, alongside John Webber, on The X Show on FX. Ashley has continued her association with Perfect 10, most recently singing the national anthem at one of Perfect 10 's "Model Boxing" events in 2004.

Although Ashley is now known as a nude model, she actually started out as a fashion model at a very young age, spending much of her time travelling between Miami and Paris. By high school she was signed with the Elite modeling agency.

[edit] Biography

She attended Washington Middle School, a Peoria public school for gifted students.[citation needed]
