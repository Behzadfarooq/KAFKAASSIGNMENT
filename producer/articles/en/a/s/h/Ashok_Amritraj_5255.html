Ashok Amritraj

Ashok Amritraj is a Hollywood producer and a former tennis player. His brothers are also tennis players, Vijay Amritraj and Anand Amritraj. He has produced over 80 films including, Jeans and hit hollywood films such as "Antitrust (film)" and "Bringing Down the House (film)".
