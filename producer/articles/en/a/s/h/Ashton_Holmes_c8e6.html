Ashton Holmes (born February 17, 1983 in Albany, New York) is an American actor of film and television and a former soap opera actor. At present, he is probably best known for the role of Jack Stall in A History of Violence.

At a young age he began taking acting lessons and appeared in community theatre. He attended The Albany Academy.
