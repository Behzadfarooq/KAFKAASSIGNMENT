Ahes Of Dusk is a Experimental band from Romania.

Started in february 2002 under the name of Catacombs ... originally consisting of three members (Ash - Guitars, Reep - Drums and Spook - Vocals) ... being on a road to nowhere the band changed it's name to Konflikt[ED] and adopted a softer acoustic sound ... they once again fail to gain public atention so in september 2005 the band splits up ... later that year Ash and Reep give music one more try coming up with a very early acoustic version of the song "Seventh Day" that will be the basis of the later "Ashes Of Dusk" project ... due to some personal issues, Reep can no longer carry on drumming for the project so apparently the band takes part once more ... Ash decided to keep the project alive by his own becoming an one man band ... ASHES OF DUSK ...
