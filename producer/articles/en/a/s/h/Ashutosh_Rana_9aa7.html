Ashutosh Rana is an Indian actor working in the Bollywood film industry.

[edit] Biography

A very good actor and he is very fluent in Hindi. He is married to Renuka Shahane (actress by Profession).
Birthday: January 26
Address: 23, Bharat Petroleum Colony Aziz Baug, Chembur, Mumbai - 74
Tel: 557 3439/639 2973

[edit] Film career

He started his career with popular serial Swabhimaan. He became known on Indian Cinema scene after his movie Dushman where he played a cold blooded murderer and psychopath killer.
