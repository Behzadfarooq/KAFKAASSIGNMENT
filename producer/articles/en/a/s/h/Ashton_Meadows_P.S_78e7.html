Ashton Meadows Public School (Also known as A.M.P.S, AMPS, or simply Ashton) is a public, bilingual English and French elemantary school in Markham, Ontario, Canada. It was named after the beautiful Ashton Meadows Park.

Ashton Meadows Public School is part of the Trudeau family of schools. Along with Castlemore Public School, and Stonebridge Public School.

[edit] History

Ashton Meadows P.S. was officially opened in 1997. Prior to being named the school was known as "Buttonville" north in honour of its location and in reference to the holding school, Buttonville P.S. Ashton is located in the western area of Markham. The school attendance area is bounded on the south by Sixteenth Avenue, on the west by Woodbine Avenue, on the east by Warden Avenue, and on the north by the homes on Personna Boulevard.

A child-care centre, Ashton Meadows Child Care, owned and operated by Upper Canada Child Care is located at Ashton Meadows P.S. The centre staff work closely with teachers and parents and administration to support students attending the school.


[edit] Growth And Demographics

Ashton Meadows current population totals 390 students. Fifty three percent are male and forty seven percent are female. Cantonese is the primary non-English speaking language in the community with 21.5 % of the students indicating that this is the primary language spoken at home. There are several other languages represented as well. Approximately 17.3% of the students are ESL stage 1-4. We have 7.5% of the students identified with special education learning needs.


[edit] Identity

The students of Ashton Meadows are collectively known as the Falcons.


[edit] School Events

Ashton Meadows Public School takes great pride in hosting as well as organizing several school and community events. The most notable ones include The Remembarance Day Assembly and The May Show. Other events include The Winter Electives, The Annual BBQ, The Halloween Dance and The Graduation Dance. Seperate events are held for the Child Care center.

The Remembrance Day Assembly is Directed by Francine Klein the Drama,History and English teacher at Ashton Meadows Public School along with the Techies help this event is always a huge Success. The May show is also Directed by Mrs.Francine Klein.


[edit] Arts

Drama at Ashton Meadows is a big part of the arts program. For events like Café Français and the Arts Night, it is mainly the grades 7 and 8 students who get to participate. The head drama teacher is Ms. Klein. Without her none of the drama productions would be possible.

Music is another big part of the arts program. The music teacher at Ashton Meadows is Mrs. Arnokouros. Mrs. Arnokouros teaches music to students in grades 6-8. She conducts all the band practices and organizes the music trips/performances.

Visual Arts is the third part of the arts program at Ashton Meadows.
