I have a BA (Hons) in German and linguistics from Brighton University in East Sussex.

I work as a groom at a riding school and am training for my BHS examinations.

My interests are:

  • Horses. I have ridden for over 12 years and had my own for 6 years.
  • Music - rock, metal, punk, grunge, EBM, some other stuff now and again...
  • Social topics such as class, racism and censorship.
  • Politics
  • Language and linguistics, particularly psycholinguistics. I speak fluent German and have a small knowledge of French and Swedish.
  • Film - from an analytical point of view. My Favourite film is M by Fritz Lang.

I have worked on the horse care page. I created Livery, British Horse Society, Grass Sickness, Recurrent Airway Obstruction, Sweet itch, Headshaking, Nick Skelton, Robert Whitaker and Riding instructor pages.
