Ash-cum-Ridley

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Ash-cum-Ridley is a civil parish in the Sevenoaks district of Kent, England. According to the 2001 census the parish had a population of 7,070. The parish includes:

  • Ash,
  • New Ash Green,
  • Hodsoll Street and
  • Ridley.
