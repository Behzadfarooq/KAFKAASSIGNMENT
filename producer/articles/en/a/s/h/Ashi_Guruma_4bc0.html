Ashi Guruma

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Image:Ej km ashi-guruma 69245-69395 69465-69511.gif

Ashi Guruma is one of the original 40 throws of Judo as developed by Jigoro Kano. It belongs to the third group, Sankyo, of the traditional throwing list, Gokyo (no waza), of Kodokan Judo. It is also part of the current 67 Throws of Kodokan Judo. It is classified as a foot technique, Ashi-waza.
