Asher D (born June 30th 1982), real name Ashley Walters) [1]is a British rapper and actor, who is known for his work within the group So Solid Crew. After the group split up, he went on to focus on other projects.

Asher D also acts, appearing in films such as Bullet Boy where he played the title role, and Get Rich or Die Tryin' (2005 film) starring 50 Cent. Bullet Boy was completed in 2004 and released in 2005. He recently played the role as "Wolf" in a new teenage spy film: Stormbreaker (film), and "Danny" in the film Life and Lyrics. And it has been confirmed that he will star alongside the rest of the con-artist team in the fourth series of BBC Drama Hustle, replacing Adrian Lester who plays Mickey Bricks. He was also cast as Bugzy on MTVS series top buzzer. He has two children.
