The Ashland Daily Tidings is a newspaper published in Ashland, Oregon, United States. Like the Mail Tribune in nearby Medford, it is part of the Ottaway Community Newspapers chain, a subsidiary of Dow Jones.

The Daily Tidings is printed six afternoons a week, from Monday to Saturday. It is one of Oregon's smallest-circulation dailies, along with the Baker City Herald in the state's northeast region.
