Ash Valley Golf is a golf course located in Hertfordshire, England, near to the village of Little Hadham. It is a failry open course and is often cited as a type of links course. The par is 71 but at one stage was par 72 as the 18th was briefly a par 5. A new clubhouse was opened recently replacing the old wooden hut where one payed your green fee. A few more bunkers have been added as has a few more water hazards.
