Ashley Lauren (born October 18, 1972) is an adult model. She began her nude modeling career in 1990 with photographer Suze Randall and has appeared in Swank, Penthouse, Hustler, and Club. She holds the record for appearing the most times on the cover of Swank when she was actively modeling.

She is pictured on a t-shirt worn by actor Matthew McConaughey in the 1999 Ron Howard film, Edtv.

She is retired now. However, you can still download photos from Suze.net and there are a couple active fan sites.
