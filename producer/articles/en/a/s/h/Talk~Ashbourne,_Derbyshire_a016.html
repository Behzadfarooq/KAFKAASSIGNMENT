I've found very little information in the literature. However there are a couple of pages in Chris Banks' Nottingham and Derbyshire (British Railways Past and Present)
Buxton- Parsley Hay opened 1894, to Ashbourne 1899 (Didn't it then go on to Uttoxeter?)
"The line closed to passenger traffic in 1954 but continued to be used by excursion traffic to Buxton (presumably from Ashbourne but it doesnt say) Closed completely in 1963, but the connection to the C&HPR remained in use until 1967."
There is a 1957 photo of an excursion special "to Buxton from the Midlands" standing at Alsop-en-le-Dale station. --Chevin 09:54, 1 February 2007 (UTC)
I have found confirmation among the C&HPR stuff in Clive Hardy's Derbyshire Railways: Passenger services finished in 1954, the line continued until 1964, (slight difference here) the station finally closing in 1965.
