Ashlie Rhey (born August 17, 1961 in Wheeling, West Virginia) is an American actress, B-Movie actress and model. She has appeared in many low to medium-budget feature films in many genres, including: horror, comedy and softcore sex films. Alternate screen names include: Ashlie Rhey, Ashley Rhey and Ashlee Rhey.

[edit] Background

Ashlie Rhey was born in West Virginia, but grew up in Ohio. She began acting in films in 1990 after working as a model and returning to Hollywood, California from Europe. Most of her film roles are 'nudity required' roles in B-movies and are released directly to DVD / Video or appear on cable television channels such as Showtime, Cinemax, and Playboy TV. She has appeared in over 48 films. She is most well known for her starring role in "Bikini Drive In" which was directed by Fred Olen Ray. In addition, she has appeared in 7 editions of "Playboy's NSS's", "Draculina", "Femme Fatales", "Celebrity Skin", High Society" and "Scream Queens Illustrated".
