Ashley Road Primary School is a co-educational school located on Ashley Road in Aberdeen, Scotland. It is owned and run by Aberdeen City Council. It is a feeder school of Aberdeen Grammar School.

It has a nursery section and a primary school section. The school was built of gray granite in Victorian times. Its design is very similar to that of another Aberdeen Grammar feeder school, Mile-End School.
