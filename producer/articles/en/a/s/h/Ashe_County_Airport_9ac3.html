Ashe County Airport (ICAO: KGEV, FAA LID: GEV) is a public airport located three miles (5 km) east of the central business district (CBD) of Jefferson, a town in Ashe County, North Carolina, USA. This general aviation airport covers 106 acres and has one runway.

Although most U.S. airports use the same three-letter location identifier for the FAA and IATA, Ashe County Airport is assigned GEV[1] by the FAA but has no designation from the IATA (which assigned GEV to Gällivare Airport in Gällivare, Sweden[2]). The airport's ICAO identifier is KGEV.[3]
