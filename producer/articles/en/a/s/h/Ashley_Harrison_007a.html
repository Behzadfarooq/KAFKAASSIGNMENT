Ashley Harrison (born May 18, 1981 in Brisbane, Queensland) is an Australian Rugby League player who plays for the Sydney Roosters in the National Rugby League competition. He has previously captained the South Sydney Rabbitohs and played for the Brisbane Broncos.

When he left the Rabbitohs for the Roosters he was quoted as saying, "I want to go to a club where I can play finals football and further my representative career." Ironically The Roosters missed the finals and he still only has one rep game game to his name.

[edit] Career Highlights

Played 1 Game for Queensland in 2005.

Has Played 139 First Grade Games (Scored 40 Tries for 160 Points)
