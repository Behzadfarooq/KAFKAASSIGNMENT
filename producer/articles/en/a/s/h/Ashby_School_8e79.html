Ashby School, formerly known as Ashby Grammar School, is a co-educational day and boys' boarding comprehensive school in Ashby-de-la-Zouch, Leicestershire, United Kingdom. It was founded in 1567 and is now a specialist technology and language school.

[edit] Infrastructure

Ashby School is based on three main sites (A, B and C blocks), based on adjacent roads.

The school has grown rapidly in recent years, with the aim of Headteacher Vivien Keller-Garnett to replace all the mobile classrooms. The school has spent considerable funds on the construction of a new science block, new rooms in the design department, and more recently, a new block built to accommodate music, art, media studies and English. Work is scheduled to begin on a new Sixth Form Centre this Easter.

[edit] Organisation

There are around 134 teaching staff. Due to good results, the new intake is oversubscribed every year.

The school has been key in the Ashby Family of Schools, a local effort to link schools of different ages ranges and make the transition between schools easier for pupils. Various departments work with teachers from local primary schools to provide masterclasses and workshops for the younger children.

[edit] 'Da Vinci' and 'Tip Tops'

Da Vinci is the school's current gifted and talented system. In response to the national talk about gifted and talented schemes, Da Vinci provides opportunities for its members to further their abilities, and monitors the performance of all pupils.

The Tip Tops is a related group for primary pupils in years 5 and 6. The pupils meet after school on Fridays and are taught advanced mathematics, literacy, and science, and and generally encouraged to think outside the box and begin to develop their own opinions. Da Vinci pupils are encouraged to help the younger Tip Tops.

[edit] Houses

There are six houses situated within the school: Hastings, Loudon, Ferrers, Bullen, Crewe and Erdington.

Each house chooses a charity for the year for which it raises money. They also take part in sports days and inter-house competitions.

[edit] School Council

The school has a Student Council run by the student body with elected representatives.
