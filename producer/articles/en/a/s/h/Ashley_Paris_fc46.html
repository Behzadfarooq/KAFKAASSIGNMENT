Ashley Paris, twin sister of Courtney Paris, has been nationally recognized for her basketball achievements.

On May 21, 2006, she was named a 2006 USA U20 National Team finalist.

[edit] Personal life

Ashley Paris was born on September 21, 1987, in San Jose, California to Lyn Harris and William "Bubba" Paris. Her family includes four brothers — Wayne, David, Austin and Brandon — and a twin sister, 2006 USA U20 National Team Trials participant, Courtney.
