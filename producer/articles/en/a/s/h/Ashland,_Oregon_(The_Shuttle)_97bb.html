The Oregon Department of Transportation operates a shuttle bus from Klamath Falls Amtrak station to Ashland, Oregon. The bus meets the Coast Starlight daily and takes passengers on to Ashland, which has the Amtrak station code AHL. The bus stops at the Safeway Market in Ashland.
