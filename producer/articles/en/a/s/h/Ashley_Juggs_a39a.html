Ashley Juggs, born Cynthia Gonzalez, is a glamour model widely known for her large bust. She models for Danni.com and has appeared in a number of large-breast themed adult magazines, including those of the Score imprint. She is multiethnic, the product of a Jamaican/Italian mom and a Hispanic father.[1]
