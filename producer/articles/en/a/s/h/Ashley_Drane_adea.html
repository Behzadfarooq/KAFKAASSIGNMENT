Maria Ashley Drane (born September 22, 1981 in Louisville, Kentucky, United States and raised in Orlando, Florida) is a TV actress best known to audiences of Blue Collar TV. To younger audiences, she plays as a minor character named Muffy on Disney Channel's That's So Raven. Drane also had a small role on one episode in Nickelodeon show Drake & Josh as Susan, Drake's girlfriend.

She married Major League Baseball player and 2006 World Series MVP David Eckstein on November 26, 2005, in David's hometown of Sanford, Florida, followed by a reception at Walt Disney World.
