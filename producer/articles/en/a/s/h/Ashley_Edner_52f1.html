Ashley Louise Edner (born December 17, 1990 in Downey, California) is an American actress.

Ashley Edner is the younger sister to actor Bobby Edner. Her credits include Malcolm in the Middle, MAD TV, The Amanda Show, Martial Law, Party of Five, The X Files, Star Trek: Voyager, Will & Grace, Listen Up, and The Twilight Zone.

She was asked to appear as a young Britney Spears in the opening of her stage performance on the 42nd Grammy Awards days before it aired on February 23, 2000.
