Ashland/63 is a station on the Chicago Transit Authority's 'L' system, located in Chicago, Illinois and serving the Green Line's Ashland branch. It is situated at 6315 S Ashland Avenue. It opened on May 6, 1969. This station is the southern terminal of the Ashland branch of the Green Line.
