Ashley Foyle (born September 17, 1986 in Sheffield) is an English football defender currently playing for Northern Premier League First Division side Belper Town.

He signed for Accrington in August 2006, leaving his only previous professional club, Chesterfield.From there he went on loan to Belper and has now signed for Unibond First Division leaders Buxton FC.
