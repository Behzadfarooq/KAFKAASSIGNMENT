Ashita no Prism (明日のプリズム, Ashita no Prism?) is a CD single by Japanese singer and seiyū Aya Hirano. It was first released on September 6, 2006 and was produced by Lantis.

[edit] Track listing

  1. "Ashita no Prism" (明日のプリズム, "Ashita no Prism"?)
  2. "Yorokobi no Uta" (ヨロコビの歌, "Yorokobi no Uta"?)
  3. "Ashita no Prism" (off vocal) (明日のプリズム, "Ashita no Prism" (off vocal)?)
  4. "Yorokobi no Uta" (off vocal) (ヨロコビの歌, "Yorokobi no Uta" (off vocal)?)
