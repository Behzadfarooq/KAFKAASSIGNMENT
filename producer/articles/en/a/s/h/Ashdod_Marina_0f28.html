Ashdod Marina

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

"Blue Marina" in Ashdod
In building

Blue Marina in Ashdod is a newest one in Israel. It is located close to the city center in the middle of beach zone. The marina can berths for nearly 550 craft. The surrounding area is in development process and will have hotels, restaurants, artificial lake and promenade.

The "Blue Marina" is a center of sea sports. There are surfing, sailing and diving schools located in site.

Latitude: 31° 48" N; Longtitude: 34° 37" E; Number of berths: 4.
