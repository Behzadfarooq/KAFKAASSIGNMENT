Ashalii

Ashalii is an arcade puzzle game for Mac OS X. Playing involves color matching as well as shape matching. While fitting various shapes into the board, a certain number of matching pieces must be connected to remove them.

The game is set in a trap in outer space, and the name Ashalii refers to the alien race who created these traps. The player attempts to escape to freedom by passing through progressively difficult levels.
