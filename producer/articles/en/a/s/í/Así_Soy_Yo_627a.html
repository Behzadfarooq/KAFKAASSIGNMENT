Así Soy Yo is the debut album from latin music singer and winner of the second season of "Objetivo Fama" Anaís. It was released in the U.S. on April 18, 2006. All the songs features on the album are selfwritten by Anaís, some with help of Alacranes Musical, Voltio, Bimbo or La Sister. The first single of the album, "Lo que son las cosas" peaked at number 79 in the Billboard Hot 100 and number one in the Hot Latin Tracks chart.
