Aster (cable)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Aster (cable) is a company that provides cable speed Internet and digital basic channels and family cable television in the country of the Dominican Republic. Aster is one of the most important cable and internet providers of the Dominican Republic. Aster's competitors are Verizon, Inc. recently changed to CLARO Codetel and Telecable Nacional owned by Tricom in the Dominican Republic.

[edit] Services

[edit] Zona Central

Central Zone which is the Santo Domingo metropolitan area. In Santo Domingo they Provide Cable internet speeds for residency at up to 512 KbPS and to business at 1200 KbPS or more.

[edit] Zona Norte

North Zone is the Zone in Santiago De Los Caballeros or Sorroundings. Aster Provides Cable TV signal and It too provides Cable Internet in Some Areas Around the City. La Vega And Licey are other areas under this Zone.

[edit] Zona Este

La Romana Is the area were Aster Provides Cable Signaling in The East Of The Country. Hato Mayor, Higuey, And San Pedro too make part of the Regional East Zone.
