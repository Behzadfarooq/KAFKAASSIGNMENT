Astro (Motorola)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

You have new messages (last change).

Refers to the name brand given to radios from the manufacturer Motorola that are capable of digital voice transmission. Early models used the VSELP vocoder while the latest models use the IMBE vocoder which is also specified in the APCO-25 Common Air Interface.
