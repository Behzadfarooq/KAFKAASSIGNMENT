Astronomical society

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

There are numerous groups devoted to promoting astronomy research and education. See, for example:

  • Ayrshire Astronomical Society, from Ayrshire, Scotland
  • Astronomical League, an umbrella organization of U.S. amateur astronomy societies.
  • American Association of Variable Star Observers, a pro-am organization studying stars that vary in brightness for a multitude of reasons.
  • Association of Lunar and Planetary Observers, an amateur organization specializing in planetary, lunar, and other solar system observations.
  • American Meteor Society, an amateur organization specializing in meteor observations.
  • Birmingham Astronomical Society - located in Birmingham, Alabama.
  • International Meteor Organization, an international organization dealing in meteor observations.
  • Federation of Astronomical Societies
  • Royal Astronomical Society of Canada
  • Category: Astronomy organizations by name
  • Category: Amateur astronomy organizations by geographic location
  • Kopernik Space Center
  • Kosmandu Astronomical Society, an amateur organization from Nepal, working for promotion of astronomy in Nepal.
  • San Francisco Amateur Astronomers http://www.sfaa-astronomy.org/
  • http://www.space-india.org - S.P.A.C.E. - Science Popularization Association of Communicators & Educators. An Indian-based NGO working in astronomy among school kids in India
