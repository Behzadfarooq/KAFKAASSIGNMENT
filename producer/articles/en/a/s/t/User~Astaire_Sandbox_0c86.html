Flora Nwapa
Born: January 18, 1931
Oguta, East Central State, Nigeria
Died: October 16, 1993

Occupation: novelist, short story writer, poet
Nationality: Nigerian

Flora Nwanzuruahu Nkiru Nwapa (January 18, 1931 - October 16, 1993) was the first female Nigerian novelist.
