TV Guide cover from August 7, 1971, with the cast of As the World Turns.

Bottom row (from left to right): Santos Ortega, Helen Wagner, Don MacLaughlin, William Johnstone.

Second row (from left to right): Peter Galman, Eileen Fulton, Phoebe Dorin, Rita McLaughlin.

Third row (from left to right): Ethel Remey, Fran Carlon, Henderson Forsythe, Marie Masters.

Top row (from left to right): Nina Hart, Jerry Lacy, Larry Bryggman, Swoosie Kurtz.
