Astor Broadcast Group is an independent radio company in Southern California, with studios in Carlsbad, California, headquarters in Anaheim, California.

Astor Broadcast Group owns the following radio stations:

  • KCEO AM 1000: financial talk Vista, California [1]
  • KFSD AM 1450: Adult standards Escondido, California [2]
  • KSPA AM 1510: Adult standards Ontario, California [3]
