In many campaign settings for the Dungeons & Dragons role-playing game, Astilabor is the dragon deity of hoards.

She represents the desire in all dragons to acquire wealth and power. However, she dislikes greed. She cannot abide theivery unless such an act is done in the name of building ones horde.

[edit] Clergy and Temples

All of Astilabor's followers are neutral in some some aspect of their alignment. She discourages her clerics from becoming involved in draconic politics and seek to reward dragons with unusually impressive hoards.
