Astronaut Group 3

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Astronaut Group 3, was the third group of Astronauts selected by NASA in October 1963. All of the third group (except those who died) flew on the Apollo program - Aldrin, Bean, Cernan and Scott walked on the Moon. Five of them (Aldrin, Cernan, Collins, Gordon and Scott) also flew missions during the Gemini program. Bassett, Chaffee, Freeman and Williams all died before they could fly in space - Chaffee in the Apollo 1 fire, the rest in plane crashes.

Main article: List of astronauts by selection
  • Buzz Aldrin
Lunar Module Pilot, first manned lunar landing. Second man to walk on the moon. Flew on Gemini 12 and Apollo 11.
  • William Anders
Lunar Module Pilot, first manned flight to orbit the moon, Apollo 8. Since no LM on mission, Anders handled the spacecraft's electrical systems and was responsible for significant photography.
  • Charles Bassett
Selected as a pilot for Gemini 9, but died in a plane crash before the mission.
  • Alan Bean
Lunar Module Pilot, second manned lunar landing. Fourth man to walk on the moon. Flew on Apollo 12 and Skylab 3 as commander.
  • Eugene Cernan
Lunar Module Pilot, lunar module test in lunar orbit, highest velocity attained by manned vehicle. Commander, (as of 2006) final manned mission to the moon. As of 2006, last man to walk on the moon. Flew on Gemini 9A, Apollo 10, Apollo 17.
  • Roger Chaffee
Trained as a Lunar Module Pilot, died in the Apollo 1 fire.
  • Michael Collins
Command Module Pilot, first manned lunar landing. Flew on Gemini 10 and Apollo 11.
  • Walter Cunningham
Lunar Module Pilot, first Apollo manned mission in earth orbit, Apollo 7. The LM was not flown for this mission.
  • Donn Eisele
Command Module Pilot, first Apollo manned mission in earth orbit, Apollo 7.
  • Theodore Freeman
Killed in jet trainer crash in 1964 before being selected for any flight assignment.
  • Richard Gordon
Command Module Pilot, second manned lunar landing. Flew on Gemini 11 and Apollo 12.
  • Russell Schweickart
Lunar Module Pilot, first flight of LM in earth orbit, Apollo 9.
  • David Scott
Commander, fourth manned lunar landing. Seventh man to walk on the moon. Flew on Gemini 8, Apollo 9, Apollo 15.
  • Clifton Williams
Served on the backup crew for Gemini 10. Selected as a member of the backup crew for Apollo 9, but died in a plane crash before the mission.
