About me

Hi! Welcome to my user page. I created my account a few months ago, and I am now quite experienced on Wkipedia. My Username is AstroHurricane001, because I am interested in Astronomy, Weather, and various other subjects. I moved my Userboxes onto a separate subpage (see below). I once got a score of about 700 on the Are You A Wikipediholic Test even before my first edit. I have also created a user talk page, where you can post comments. I have been reading articles on Wikipedia for a long time, and I think it is a great site. I have created several articles, especially ones about meteor showers and comets. Please ask me for permission before editing my page, or post a comment on my User talk page (see above), although I usually don't mind if someone makes a small spelling/grammar correction, etc. My user page has been vandalized 1, 2, 3 times.
