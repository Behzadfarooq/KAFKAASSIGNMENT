[edit] new snowstorm

There is a new storm across Canada. The one in the east is bringing snow, up to 20 cm, rain, up to 3 inches, wind, up to 110 k/h, and the low itself could deepen to 960 hPa. The one in the west is bringing up to 60 cm of snow, especially in the Smithers area. Places like Calgary could see up to 7 inches of snow. Tens of thousands of people are without electricity in northern BC. AstroHurricane001 16:52, 29 October 2006 (UTC)

There could soon be flooding in places like Smithers snd Burns Lake, because if the snow melts, it will probably overflow rivers, andif it doesn't melt, more snow could pile on to of it, and cause flooding when it does melt. Also, there has been recent flooding in other places of the world, like Batman, Turkey and Amsterdam, the Netherlands. AstroHurricane001 18:23, 3 November 2006 (UTC)
