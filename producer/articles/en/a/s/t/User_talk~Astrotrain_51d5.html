[edit] WP:BLP

You seem to be having some trouble with our policy on bios of living people. The policy states that all claims regarding living people (with particular attention to contentious or controversial claims) must be properly sourced. It does not require that those sources be available online, although I think given the choice of two sources we'd take the online one over the other, all else being equal. If you encounter a dead link that has other relevant information on the source as it appeared in print, the best course of action is to de-link the text and add a note on the talk page that the link was dead on such-and-such date. Let me know if you have any questions. | Mr. Darcy talk 23:18, 1 March 2007 (UTC)
