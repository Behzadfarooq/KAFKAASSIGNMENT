Aston Croall (born July 10, 1984) is an English Rugby Union player. He usually plays at Hooker or Loosehead prop for Harlequins. He has represented England at Under 16, 18, 19 and 21 level, and was part of the England Under 21 squad that competed in the IRB Under 21 World Championships in June 2005.
