Around Aston Park, there is a lot of countryside and also a lot of industry.

Aston Park is a small, residential village in Flintshire, North Wales. It is near to Queensferry and Shotton, alongside the A55 expressway, and it is often referred to as Higher Shotton. It is situated in a fairly urban area but there is still a lot of countryside - Wepre Country Park is only a short distance away.

In the village there are some shops, a hairdresser, an Indian take-away, a bowling green, a park, a primary school and a hospital (Deeside Community Hospital).
