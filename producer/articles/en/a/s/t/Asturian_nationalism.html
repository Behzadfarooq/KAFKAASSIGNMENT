Asturian nationalism

Nationalism and regionalism is present in the politics spectrum of the Principality of Asturias. Although asturian nationalist parties like Partíu Asturianista did have institutional representation in the Asturian parliament and government and others like Andecha Astur have also political representation in some municipalities nowadays, the nationalist movement can be sometimes seen more at a level of social movement than an element with a clear institutional representation.

These movements are usually rooted in the several stages of political sovereignty that Asturias has lived, with the time of Kingdom of Asturias between the years 718 and 925, the declaration of sovereignty of The General Assembly of the Principality of Asturias of 1808, and Sovereign Assembly of Asturias and Leon of 1937 as highlights.

Present Asturian nationalism includes political parties and organizations from the whole political spectrum, having Andecha Astur/Darréu, Izquierda Asturiana and Bloque por Asturies in the left-wing and Partíu Asturianista and Unión Asturianista on the center-right wing.

[edit] Political parties and organizations

  • Andecha Astur, leftist nationalist political party.
  • Bloque por Asturies, leftist nationalist political party. Member of the Asturian government in a coalition.
  • Darréu, leftist nationalist youth organization, related to Andecha Astur
  • Izquierda Asturiana, leftist nationalist political party.
  • Partíu Asturianista, centrist nationalist political party.
  • Unión Asturianista, centrist electoral coalition of the centrist nationalist Partíu Asturianista and the centrist autonomist Unión Renovadora Asturiana.
