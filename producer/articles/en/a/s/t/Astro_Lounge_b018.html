Astro Lounge, released in 1999, is the second full length album from the San Jose, California rock band Smash Mouth. It includes the single "All Star", which reached number four on the U.S. billboard Hot 100, and was also featured on the soundtrack to the movie Shrek (the song is played during the film's opening credits). Several other songs were released from the album, including "Waste" and "Then the Morning Comes".

[edit] Then The Morning Comes

This song was used in a TV commercial by Nissan. It was Smash Mouth's first single to enter the Billboard charts, reaching #8, though it was somewhat eclipsed by the success of "All Star" from the same album.

[edit] Can't Get Enough Of You Baby

Can't Get Enough of You Baby was the third single released from Astro Lounge. It was originally by The Four Seasons and was later covered by ? and the Mysterians and The Colourfield, making Smash Mouth the fourth band to record the song. It was the last song on the album and also the shortest (by one second).

Smash Mouth recorded Can't Get Enough of You Baby in 1998 for the movie Can't Hardly Wait. The song is track two on the soundtrack.

In early 2007, Pizza Hut aired a commercial featuring the song in the background.
