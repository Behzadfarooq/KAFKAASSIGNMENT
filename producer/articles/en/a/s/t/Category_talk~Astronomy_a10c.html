[edit] Astrophysics as a subcategory

I think making "astrophysics" a subcategory of "astronomy" is inappropriate. In most astronomy research in the past 100 years, the distinction between "astronomy" and "astrophysics" has been very gray. The two terms may be used interchangably in modern astrophysics. May I suggest merging Category:Astrophysics into this category? I know it will cause problems, but I think that many of the articles in both this category and the astrophysics category can be subcategorized relatively easily. (People seem to slap "Category:Astronomy" on anything.) George J. Bendo 18:20, 19 November 2006 (UTC)
