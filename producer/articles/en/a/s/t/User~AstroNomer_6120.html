Capitalization oldwiki style of Astronomer, referring to one of the first contributors to Wikipedia.

I started, long ago, the Astronomy and astrophysics page later renamed to Astronomy, and I was responsible for its overall structure and most of its content. I have made or added to several related pages, both in physics and astronomy and other areas.

I actually created the page Wikipedia:wikipediholic, though I'm not sure if I should be proud or worried by the fact (84 points...that's too much).
