[edit] Merge proposal

  • Oppose: I would say that this page fulfils a definite purpose as a separate page - it describes the live action films in detail; I would support (and may put up for official debate) a proposal to rename it to "Asterix films (live)" or the equivalent. The List of Asterix films and videogames is just that: a list items on it of greater interest than a footnote have (or should have) their own article and this article serves that purpose for the live films.--Invisifan 23:05, 11 October 2006 (UTC)

[edit] rearrangements

I've recently split "List of Asterix films and videogames" into "List of Asterix films" and "List of Asterix games" - there is also an "Asterix movies" (which redirects to "List of Asterix films") ... Since no one else has expressed an opinion, I am moving this article to "Asterix films (live)" to make the content & rationale clear (and bring the capitalization up to standard) and removing the merge tag.. --Invisifan
