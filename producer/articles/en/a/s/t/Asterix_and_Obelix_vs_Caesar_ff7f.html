Asterix and Obelix vs Caesar (Aust.) or Asterix and Obelix take on Caesar (UK) (French: Astérix et Obélix contre César) is a Franco-Italo-German movie, created by Claude Zidi, and released by 1999.

The film is based on the fictitious comic characters of Asterix and Obelix created by René Goscinny (writer) and Albert Uderzo (illustrator). Uderzo has continued the series since the death of Goscinny in 1977. Obelix is a tall fat person with a small brain (or a brain of a child) played by Gérard Depardieu. Asterix is Obelix's small friend who is clever and cautious.
