Astrid Carolina Herrera

Astrid Carolina Herrera Irrazábal is a famous Venezuelan telenovela actress. She won the 1984 Miss World contest, representing Venezuela. She became the third woman from Venezuela to win the title. The pageant was held in London, United Kingdom.
