Aston Taminsyah

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Aston Taminsyah (b. 1997) from Indonesia, is a chess prodigy who started playing chess when he was five. Much of his chess training from the prominent Chess School in Indonesia (Sekolah Catur Utut Adianto), a school named after the # 1 chess player GM Utut Adianto in Indonesia. He currently belongs to the Indonesian National Youth Squad.

[edit] Awards

  • In 2003, he won the Hong Kong National Chess Championship Under-7 Category.
  • In 2004, he won the Singapore National Chess Championship Under-7 Category.
  • In 2005, April he won his first World School Chess Championship, U8 Category, in Chalkidiki, Greece. [1]
  • In 2006, April he won his second World School Chess Championship, under-9 category, in Chalkidiki, Greece. [2]
