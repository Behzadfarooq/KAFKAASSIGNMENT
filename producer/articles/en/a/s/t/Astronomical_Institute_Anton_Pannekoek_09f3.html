The Anton Pannekoek Astronomical Institute is a department of the University of Amsterdam at the Amsterdam Science Park. It is named after the Dutch astronomer Antonie Pannekoek. The principle areas of research at the group are:

  1. X-ray/High-energy astrophysics
  2. The formation and evolution of single and binary stars
  3. The gamma-ray bursts
  4. infrared astronomy
  5. Massive stars and stellar wind
  6. Radio pulsars
  7. Cosmology and the early universe
  8. Faint variable sources
