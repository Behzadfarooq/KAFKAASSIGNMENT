Astrid Schulz (born 12 September 1939 in Heemstede) is a Dutch model and actress who was Playboy magazine's Playmate of the Month for the September 1964 issue. Her centerfold was photographed by Mario Casilli.

Before her appearance in Playboy, Astrid had modelled in France, Belgium, and her native Netherlands. She spoke several languages, including French, English and German.
