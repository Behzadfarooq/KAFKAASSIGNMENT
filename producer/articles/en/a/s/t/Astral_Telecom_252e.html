Astral Telecom S.A. is a Romanian telecommunications company, a cable television network, internet service provider and telephone operator.

Founded in Cluj-Napoca in 1993, it is currently the owner of the second-largest cable network in Romania, behind RCS&RDS and is also one of the most important Romanian ISPs, having about 900,000 customers (2005) and generating 25% of the web traffic in Romania.

In 2005, Astral Telecom S.A. was bought for $420 million by UGC Europe.
