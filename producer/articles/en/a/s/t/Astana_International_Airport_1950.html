Astana International Airport (IATA: TSE, ICAO: UACC) is an airport in Astana, the capital of Kazakhstan. It is located 14 km outside of the city center of Astana. The airport traces its history to 1931 when Astana's first airfield was constructed. In 2005, this version of the airport underwent a major reconstruction to bring it up to international standards.
