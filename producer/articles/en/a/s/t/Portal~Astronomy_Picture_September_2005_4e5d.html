2 September 2005 (edit )

Maat Mons is the highest volcano on the planet Venus, with its summit lying 8km above the mean planetary radius. Evidence from some Venus landers such as the Pioneer Venus probes has suggested that it may still be an active volcano. This image is derived from radar data taken by the Magellan probe.
