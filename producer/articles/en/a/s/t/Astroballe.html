Astroballe

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Astroballe is an indoor sporting arena located in Villeurbanne, France. The capacity of the arena is 5,600 people. It is currently home to the Adecco ASVEL Lyon-Villeurbanne basketball team.
