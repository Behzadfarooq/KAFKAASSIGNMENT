[edit] January

edit
  • 1 January 1925 - The American astronomer Edwin Hubble announces the discovery of galaxies outside the Milky Way.
  • 2 January 1860 - The discovery of the planet Vulcan is announced at a meeting of the Académie des Sciences in Paris.
  • 2 January 2004 - Stardust successfully flies past Comet Wild 2, collecting samples that it will return to Earth two years later.
  • 3 January 1888 - The 91 cm refracting telescope at Lick Observatory is used for the first time. It was the largest telescope in the world at the time.
