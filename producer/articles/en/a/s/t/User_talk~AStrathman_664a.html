[edit] Thanks and note

Thank you for the many networks and graph theory articles you wrote in a short span of time. And I have a few style tips.

First, links and section names should be in lower case, so community structure rather than Community Structure, BA model rather than BA Model, and

 ==Average path length==

rather than

 ==Average Path Length==


And then I have a request. Can the article Hierarchical clustering: networks be renamed to something else? I think it kind of sounds clumsy. Thanks! I hope you will be back for more good work. Oleg Alexandrov (talk) 16:08, 28 January 2007 (UTC)
