Astronaut High School is located in Brevard County, Florida 32796 in the city of Titusville, Florida, USA. It is part of the Brevard County School District. The school name comes from its location, near the Kennedy Space Center. The school was built in 1972. Astronaut was Class 4A Girls Basketball Champions in 2006. Astronaut's theater program performed Seussical the Musical in March 2006.
