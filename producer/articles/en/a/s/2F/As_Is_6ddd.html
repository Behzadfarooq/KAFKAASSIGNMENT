As/Is is live album by John Mayer, available for download from iTunes and also available as a double-CD release. Notable about these recordings is the sheer volume included (four albums' worth from iTunes), the interaction John has with the audience, and the blues jamming.

As of December 12, 2006, the four concert-specific albums are still available on iTunes in the United States. Each ten-track album had its own artwork, each (like the compilation album) featured rabbits in out-of-character situations on their covers.

[edit] Performing band

These songs all feature Mayer's touring band of the time:

  • David LaBruyere (DayLa) - Bass Guitar
  • David Ryan Harris - Guitar & Backing Vocals
  • JJ Johnson - Drums and percussion
  • Michael Charvis - Guitar & Keyboards
  • Eric Jacobson - Trumpet & Dected Flugel Horn
  • Chris Carliff - Saxophone & Flute
