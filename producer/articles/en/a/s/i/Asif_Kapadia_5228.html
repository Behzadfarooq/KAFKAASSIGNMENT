Asif Kapadia (born 1972) is a British filmmaker of Indian origin.

[edit] Filmography

  • True North (2007)
  • The Return (2006 film) (2006)
  • The Warrior (2001)
  • The Sheep Thief (1997)
  • Wild West (1996)
  • The Waiting Room (1996)
  • Indian Tales (1994)
