Asia

The continent of Asia is defined by subtracting Europe and Africa from the great land mass of Africa-Eurasia. About 60% of the world's population live in Asia. The region of Asia is the continent of Asia plus nearby islands in the Indian and Pacific Oceans. It also includes Indonesia.
