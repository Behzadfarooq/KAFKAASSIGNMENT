The Asian mosques contain the most well known mosques.

The most well known are the Saudi Arabian mosques, since they contain the two well known mosques of Medina and Mecca.

The Chinese mosques stand out from the the other mosques of the world through their unique architecture.

The Great Mosque of Tongxin, Ningxia, China
Masjid al-Haram, Mecca, Saudi Arabia
Agha Bozorg Mosque, Kashan,Iran
