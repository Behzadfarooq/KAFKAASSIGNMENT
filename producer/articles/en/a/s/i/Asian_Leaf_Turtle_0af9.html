The Asian Leaf Turtle Cyclemys dentata is a species of turtle commonly found in pet trade. The turtle be found in North India, Bangladesh, Myanmar (Burma), Thailand, Cambodia, Vietnam, West Malaysia, Indonesia (Sumatra, Java, Borneo, Bali), Philippines (Palawan: Calamian Islands etc.), and China.
