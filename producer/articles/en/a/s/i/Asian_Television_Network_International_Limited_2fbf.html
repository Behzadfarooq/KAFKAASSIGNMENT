Asian Television Network International Limited (oftenly just referred to as Asian Television Network or ATN) is a Canadian licensed television broadcaster providing premium programming in several South Asian languages to communities across Canada. ATN has programming alliances with leading broadcasters such as Zee TV, Star Plus, B4U, Sony Entertainment Television Asia, Alpha ETC Punjabi, ARY Digital, and Jaya TV. It has been broadcasting since 1997 and is a publicly traded company on the TSX Venture Exchange. The company is headquartered in Newmarket, Ontario, and is headed by longtime Indo-Canadian broadcasting stalwart Shan Chandrasekar.

[edit] Channels

ATN operates 14 channels and has licenses for an additional 14 channels. Among these are licenses for two cricket channels. ATN is a major distributor of cricket programming in Canada, as it possesses the rights for many of the top cricket events in the world.

[edit] On the air

  • ATN Aastha TV (Hindi)
  • ATN Alpha ETC Punjabi (Punjabi)
  • ATN ARY Digital (Urdu)
  • ATN B4U Movies (Hindi)
  • ATN B4U Music (Hindi)
  • ATN Bangla (Bengali)
  • ATN Channel (Hindi, other South Asian languages)
  • ATN Cricket Plus (Sports channel- English)
  • ATN Jaya TV (Tamil)
  • ATN NDTV 24x7 (English)
  • ATN Zee Cinema (Hindi)
  • ATN Zee Gujarati (Gujarati)
  • CBN (English)
  • Although Sony Entertainment Television Asia (Hindi) is listed on [1] ATN's website, it is not a channel owned by ATN. SET Asia is a foreign channel from India, but ATN owns its Canadian distribution rights.
