Asia Miles (Chinese: 亞洲萬里通) is the frequent flyer programme of both Cathay Pacific Airways and Dragonair; the The Marco Polo Club is the loyalty programme associated with it. Members join for free and can earn miles by flying with Cathay Pacific, Dragonair or any of the 16 partner airlines (including all oneworld members). Mileage is accrued at a rate dependent on the number of actual miles and the fare class flown.

In addition to flying, members can earn miles through certain retail channels and services. These include staying at hotels, dining, shopping and spending on credit cards. Miles can also be purchased or transferred by one member to another, both at a certain fee.

Mileage can be redeemed for free flights, upgrades, companion tickets, holidays and other lifestyle rewards.

[edit] Earning miles

[edit] Flying

[edit] Cathay Pacific & Dragonair

  • First class: 150% of actual miles flown
  • Business class: 125% of actual miles flown
  • Economy class: 100% of actual miles flown (Y, B, H, K, M, L, & V class tickets - classes are shown in the order of increasing restrictions on the ticket)
  • Heavily discounted economy class: No mileage accrual (S, G, O, Q and other class tickets)

[edit] Other ways to accrue miles

  • Mileage transfer: a minimum of 10,000 miles may be transferred from one member to another at a service fee of USD100.00, and further blocks of 5,000 miles at a service fee of USD50.00
  • Miles exchange: members living in the USA or Canada can exchange reward programme currencies between Asia Miles and other loyalty programmes.
  • Asia Miles top-up: members who have at least 70% of the Asia Miles required for a desired award can purchase the remaining amount in blocks of 2,000 Asia Miles at USD60 per block.
