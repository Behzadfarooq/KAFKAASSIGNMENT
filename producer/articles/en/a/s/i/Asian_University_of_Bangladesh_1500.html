Asian University of Bangladesh is a private university of Bangladesh. It was established in 1996. AUB has three campuses in Uttara, Dhanmondi, and Rajshahi. AUB comprises five schools:

  • The School of Business.
  • The School of Sciences and Engineering (previously The School of Sciences and Technology).
  • The School of Social Sciences.
  • The School of Arts.
  • The School of Education and Training.

The University is managed by Asian University Trust. AUB is committed to provide high quality education in Bangladesh.
