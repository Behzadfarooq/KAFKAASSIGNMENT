Asia-Pacific Touring Car Championship

The FIA Asia-Pacific Touring Car Championship was a motorsport championship for Super Touring racing cars, it ran in 1994 for 1 season only. The only champion was Joachim Winkelhock who drove for Schnitzer Motorsport.

[edit] Schedule

The origianl schedule contained six rounds, but due to logistical and financial problems, only three rounds, Japan, Macau and New Zealand were run. The series was aborted after just one season.

Original schedule:

Date Country Circuit
Oct 30 Fuji Speedway
Nov 20 Guia Circuit
Nov 27 Sentul International Circuit
Dec 4 Wellington
Dec 11 Calder Park Raceway
Dec 18 Chennai
