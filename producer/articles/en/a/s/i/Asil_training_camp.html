Asil training camp

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Asil training camp is a military training camp in Afghanistan believed to be associated with al Qaeda.[1]

Bandar Ahmad Mubarak Al Jabri is held in extrajudicial detention in the Guantanamo Bay detainment camps, in Cuba, in part because he is alleged to have received training in the Asil training camp.[1]
