As far as I'm concerned, being any gender is a drag. -Patti Smith

i am a female-bodied and female-identified gender deviant. i like to take every opportunity to explore and challenge my relationship with gender, and to encourage those around me to do the same. gender, to me, is a social construct, and something that far too many people accept without question - our identity ought to be constructed around something more than just our genitalia.

I believe in God, only I spell it Nature. -Frank Lloyd Wright

my beliefs can best be summed up as a combination of animism, paganism, pantheism, shamanism, and taoism - but i try my best to stay open to all spiritual teachings and philosophies.

Everyone has talent. What is rare is the courage to follow that talent to the dark place where it leads. -Erica Jong

i am a writer - i primarily write and perform slam poetry, but i refuse to limit myself and like to try my hand at other styles of creating as well. most of my writing deals with personal experience, politics, spirituality, and my fascination with etymology. i'm in the process of writing my biomythography.

All truly great thoughts are conceived by walking. -Friedrich Nietzsche

i use my own two legs to get around. i love walking, whether with purpose to reach a destination, or just to enjoy the scenery and spend some time with myself. i feel that there are far too many cars in this world, and that the majority of them are unnecessary - they do far more harm than good.

As long as people will shed the blood of innocent creatures there can be no peace, no liberty, no harmony between people. Slaughter and justice cannot dwell together. -Isaac Bashevis Singer

i am a vegan for many reasons... one being that i don't feel that i have any right to contribute to the painful and unnecessary deaths of any other living creatures. i can exist happily and healthily without the exploitation of animals. so can you.
