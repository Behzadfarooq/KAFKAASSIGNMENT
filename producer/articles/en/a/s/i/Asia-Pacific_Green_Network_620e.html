The Asia-Pacific Green Network is a federation of national Green parties in countries in the Pacific Ocean and Asia, and is a member of the Global Greens. Parties from 13 member nations met recently (February 2005) in Kyoto, Japan to found the network.

Member nations and parties include:

  • Australia: Australian Greens
  • Japan: Rainbow and Greens
  • Mongolia: Mongolian Green Party
  • New Caledonia: The Pacific Greens Les Verts Pacifique
  • New Zealand: Green Party of Aotearoa New Zealand
  • Nepal: Green Nepal Party
  • Papua New Guinea: Papua New Guinea Green Party
  • Pakistan: Green Party of Pakistan (Pakistan Greens)
  • Polynesia: Heiura Les Verts Polynesiens
  • Saudi Arabia: Kingdom of Saudi Arabia Green Party
  • South Korea: Korea Greens
  • Sri Lanka: Green Party of Sri Lanka
  • Taiwan: Green Party Taiwan
  • Vanuatu: The Confederation of Greens of Vanuatu La Confédération des Verts du Vanuatu
