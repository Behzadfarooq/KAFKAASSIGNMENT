AsiaPac International is a provider of corporate international real estate services headquartered in San Jose, California, USA.

[edit] History

The company was founded in 2001 by its CEO, Patrick Moultrup, who also founded Staubach International, which Forbes Magazine recognized as the "#1 Real Estate Firm in the World in 2001."[1]

[edit] Services

Services offered by AsiaPac International include advisory, brokerage, construction management, planning, interior design, project management, and sale-leaseback consulting.

[edit] Coverage

AsiaPac International has local representation in 60 markets around the world. Its offices are divided in nine Regional Service Centers (Mexico City, Caracas, Sao Paulo, London, Seoul, Delhi, Singapore, Shanghai, and Sydney) and twenty affiliated offices (Monterrey, Santo Domingo, San Juan, Lima, Curitiba, Buenos Aires, Beijing, Ho Chi Minh City, Hong Kong, Shenzhen, Tokyo, Bangkok, Bangalore, Chennai, Hyderabad, Mumbai, Pune, Melbourne, Brisbane, and all Europe).[2]

[edit] Partners

AsiaPac international is strategically partnered with nine premier U.S. real estate firms representing the interests of over 16,000 real estate professionals and their respective corporate clients. [3]
