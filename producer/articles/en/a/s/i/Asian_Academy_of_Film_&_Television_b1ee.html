The Asian Academy of Film & Television is the pioneer most Film school in this part of the world. It is affiliated with the International Film & Television Research Center and Marwah Films & Video Studios. It is located in India's Noida Film City .AAFT has been ranked as the top 10 best film schools in the world by Cine Premiere Magazine , Hollywood. The success of AAFT alumni has made it as one of the leading film schools in the world .

[edit] History

  • Founded in 1993.
  • Intended to be an alternative to formal film schools.
  • Approved as an international-level training center by the City & Guilds in 2002.
