[edit] Shoko Asahara

I reverted the title of Shoko Asahara. The general consensus in our discussion has been that Japanese names should be first-last, and that Hepburn romanization should be used with long vowels not reflected in the article title. See Wikipedia:Manual_of_Style_for_Japan-related_articles -- Sekicho 23:04, Jun 23, 2004 (UTC) (Yes, I'm the same Sekicho.)
