Asia Corporate News Network

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Asia Corporate News Network disseminates full-text news announcements from companies and organizations worldwide to news media, financial markets, disclosure systems, investors, information web sites, databases and other audiences.

Publishing in five languages throughout Asia, the ACN Newswire distributes Company Announcements, Press Releases and Regulatory Disclosure for Investor Relations Professionals from any Listed and Non-Listed business entity worldwide.

Asia Corporate News Network ("ACN") distributes Pan-Asian Disclosure to the real time News Terminals, such as Bloomberg and the Internet in Chinese Simplified, Chinese Traditional, Korean, Japanese and English simultaneously.

ACN translates from English into Asian Languages and from Asian Languages into English using Accredited Translators who possess subject matter knowledge relevant to the Finance sector.

Its main competitors are PR Newswire and Business Wire.

[edit] History

Asia Corporate News Network was founded on September 24, 2004 by CEO Timothy J Mckinnon.

On June 10 2005, ACN began publishing simultaneously in 5 Asian Languages to Bloomberg, which led to the opening of a Sydney office in September 2005.
