Asiana AAS Airport Services Co, Ltd. (hangul:아시아나아스공항서비스) is a Korea's airport in service company. headquartered in Gwahae-dong Gangseo-gu Seoul Korea. established in 1988 as under previous name is Kumho Airport Services Co, Ltd. (hangul:금호공항서비스) Asiana AAS Airport Services and airport service are units of licensed by Asiana Airlines, a airport service wholly owned subsidiary of group family by Kumho Asiana Group.

Asiana AAS Airport Services currently operates over all branch offices of Gimpo Airport, Incheon Airport, Gimhae Airport, Jeju Airport, and is one of the Korea local largest airport service in the world.

[edit] Business service

  • Ground works - Ramp Service
  • Cargo works - Cargo Service, Terminal Service
  • Fuel works - Fueling Service, Fuel Quality Control
  • Other works - GSE Maintenance Service, Manpower Assist Service, Laundry Service, GSE Rental Service
