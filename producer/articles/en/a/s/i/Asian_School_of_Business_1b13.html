Asian School of Business,is a business school located in Trivandrum, Kerala in India. The school was established in 2005.

Its aim is to facilitate and promote studies & research with focus on professional education and its programme is called Post Graduation Programme in Management (PGPM), in emerging areas of higher education in disciplines of marketing, finance, human resources, Systems and operations and so on. The admissions to these programmes are through a Common Entrance Test followed by GD and interview.

The school is presently situated at Technopark in Kerala. It plans to shift to the new campus which is approximately 16 acres of land in size, with modern and top class facilities. Late Mr MNV Nair was the Professor Emeritus and Prof. S Jagadish was the first Director of the school.
