[edit] Summary

I (Hariharan91) have acquired permission to use this image from the webmaster at nowRUNNING.com. The webmaster has agreed to release this image under the Creative Commons License.

This is the email I sent to them.

Hello nowRUNNING. The reason I am writing this email is to  require your permission to use one of the images found on your 
site on another site called Wikipedia.
 
Here is a link to Wikipedia:
 
http://www.wikipedia.com
 
You see, I want this images to illustrate the actress Asin on her respective pages on Wikipedia. I was hoping you could  
release the image under a free license like the Creative Commons Attribution license. Also, it requires all republishers to 
credit the photographer, company or website the image was found on, so a link back to your website will definitely be 
included on the image page. Here's a link of what the license will allow me to use the image for : 
 
http://creativecommons.org/licenses/by/2.5/
 
The image I want to use on Wikipedia is: 
 
http://nowrunning.com/download_1.asp?file=http://nowrunning.com/wallpapers/main/Asin_2_1024x768.jpg
 
Your co-operation would be really mean a lot to me. I eagerly await your reply. Thanking you in advance.
 
 
Yours Sincerely,
Hariharan Nambiar


Excerpts from the reply :

Hi Hariharan,

Yes, you can use the image under that license. Our team created that wallpaper themselves so you will not face any problems 
using that particular image. We would also appreciate it if a link back to our website is provided.

Thanks,
John
