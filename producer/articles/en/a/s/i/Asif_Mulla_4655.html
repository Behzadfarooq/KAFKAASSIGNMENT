Asif Abdulhai Mulla [1] (born May 5, 1980 in Gujurat) is a Indian-born Canadian cricketer. He is a right-handed batsman and a wicketkeeper.

He made his One-Day International debut for Canada in Potchefstroom against the Netherlands in 2006-07, although he had previously played for the USA's under 16 side.

[edit] Notes

  1. ^ His first name is commonly spelt Asif, but his passport spells it Ashif [1]. The former spelling is used by the Canadian Cricket Association website and CricketArchive, and the latter by Cricinfo.
