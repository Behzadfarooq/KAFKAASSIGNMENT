Asian Handball Women Championship

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

AHF Logo

The Asian Handball Women Championship is the official competition for senior women's national handball teams of Asia, and takes place every two years. In addition to crowning the Asian champions, the tournament also serves as a qualifying tournament for the World Championship or the Olympic Games.
