Asi

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Asi can refer to several different subjects:

  • Actuarial Society Of India
  • Archaeological Survey of India
  • American Share Insurance
  • In architecture and construction, ASI refers to architect's supplemental instructions.
  • ASI is the name of the Italian Space Agency (Agenzia Spaziale Italiana).
  • Asynchronous serial interface is a technique for transmission of binary data.
  • Asi, a language spoken by Bantoanons from the island of Banton Philippines.
  • Asi, another name for the Orontes River.
  • Asi or AS-i, a name for AS-Interface, Actuator sensor Interface.
  • Asi, a sanskrit word meaning sword. According to Kautilya the people who depended on ‘Asi’ (sword) for their living were known as Asiagh in India.
  • Asi, Creek language for the Black drink brewed by Native Americans of the Southeastern United States from the leaves of the yaupon holly.
