This article is about the society in Calcutta. For other uses, see Asiatic Society.

The Asiatic Society was founded by Sir William Jones (1746-1794) on 15 January 1784 in Calcutta, the capital of British India, to enhance and further the cause of Oriental research. It was formerly known as "The Royal Asiatic Society of Bengal."
