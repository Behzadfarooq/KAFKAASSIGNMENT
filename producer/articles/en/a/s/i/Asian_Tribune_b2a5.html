The Asian Tribune is an online newspaper that provides news and editorial on the current affairs of Asia, with a special emphasis on South Asia but mostly concentrates its news on the current Civil conflict in Sri Lanka.

Publication began in 2002 and was based in Bangkok, Thailand [1] and has currently moved to Sweden. [2]

[edit] The Editor

The editor, K.T. Rajasingham, a minority Sri Lankan Tamil journalist who had worked for Sri Lanka Daily News newspaper .He was a politician in Sri Lanka[3]. He unsuccessfully ran for the mainstream SLFP or Sri Lanka Freedom Party in the Jaffna peninsula during the 1970s in opposition to the TULF party.[4]
