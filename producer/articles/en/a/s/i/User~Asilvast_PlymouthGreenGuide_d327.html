Vegetarian Britain:

Plymouth Art Centre is a long established vegetarian restaurant, based in arts centre which also houses gallery and a cinema. Light refreshments all day, lunch from 12-14 daily, evening meal 17-20 Mon-Wed, 17-20.30 Thu-Sun. Plenty of vegan dishes. 20 % student discount on Tuesday evenings. Address: 38 Looe Street, Plymouth, Devon PL4 0EB. Tel: 01752-202 61. Web: http://www.plymouthac.org.uk

Veggie Perrin’s is an Indian restaurant and takeaway with vegetarian and vegan dishes. Open Mon-Sat 12-14, 18-22, closed Sun expect for private functions. Address: 97 Mayflower Street Plymouth Devon. Tel: 01752-252 888. Web: http://www.veggieperrin.com

C2 (or Tooleys?) is a Chinese take-away. Huge vegetarian menu and a cook that is a pure Buddhist. Open Mon-Sat 17.30-22.30, closed Sun. Address: The Ridgeway, Plympton, Plymouth. Tel: 01752-342211.
