Asia aluminum

Asia Aluminum Holdings Limited
Type Private
Founded 1992
Headquarters Hong Kong, China
Key people Kwong Wui Chun (Chairman)
Area served Global
Industry Aluminum Extrusion
Products Aluminum Extruded and Fabricated Products
Website http://www.asiaalum.com/

Asia Aluminum is the largest aluminum extrusion group in Asia with home base in China. Its annual designed capacity is 350,000 metric tons.[1]
