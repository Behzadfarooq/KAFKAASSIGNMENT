The national federations of the UCI form confederations by continent.

In Asia, this body is the Asian Cycling Confederation, also shortened to ACC.


[edit] Presidents of the Asian Cycling Confederation

Dr. Khaled Al-Turki (KSA) - 1993 - 1996

Tan Sri Dato' Seri Darshan Singh Gill (MAS) - 1996 - 2005

Mr. Hee Wok Cho (KOR) - 2005 - 2008
