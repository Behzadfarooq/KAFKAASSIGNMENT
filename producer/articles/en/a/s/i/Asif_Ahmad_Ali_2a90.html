Asif Ahmad Ali

Sardar Asif Ahmad Ali (born in 1940, in Lahore, Punjab, Pakistan) attended Government College Lahore and St. John’s College, Oxford, where he studied philosophy, economics and politics. Sardar Asif Ahmad Ali is member of Pakistan Peoples Party. He was the foreign minister of Pakistan.
