Asiana IDT Co, Ltd. (hangul:아시아나IDT, 아시아나아이디티) is a Korea's telecommunication company. headquartered in Sejongro Jongro-gu Seoul, Korea. established in 1991. the Asiana IDT's service by telecommunication largest providers of IT system service in internet access and computer, IT solution and PDA network system business products. The company is one of the largest computer telecommunication companies lines, computer and internet IT system service licensed to provide local computer, IT solution, PDA and internet access. A division of the company known as computer internet system controls a large portion of the South Korean information technology market. at network tried unsuccessfully to merge with Korean telecommunication companies, but then was acquired by IT system service in computer tellecommunication systems. Asiana IDT and IT solution and service are units of licensed by Asiana Airlines, a wholly owned subsidiary of group family by Kumho Asiana Group. company name of "Asiana IDT" stands for Asiana's International Digital Communications. Its primary company simillar and competitor is Aju IT Holdings and IT Plus Networks. the "Asiana IDT" CEO is Bak Keun Sik (박근식).
