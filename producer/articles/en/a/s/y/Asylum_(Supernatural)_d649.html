[edit] Plot synopsis

Spoiler warning: Plot and/or ending details follow.

After receiving a tip from their father, John Winchester, Sam and Dean Winchester travel to Rockford, Illinois to investigate the haunted Roosevelt Asylum. Inside, Dean repeatedly makes references to Jack Nicholson and The Shining, to his brother's chagrin. The spirit of a psychiatrist who experimented on his patients causes Sam to lose his sanity and turn against his brother.

Spoilers end here.

[edit] Production notes

  • The episode was filmed in a real closed-down asylum that is rumored to be actually haunted.[citation needed]
  • This episode features the first time Dean made reference to Jennifer Love Hewitt by her character Melinda Gordon in the show Ghost Whisperer, the second time is in the season 2 episode Roadkill.
