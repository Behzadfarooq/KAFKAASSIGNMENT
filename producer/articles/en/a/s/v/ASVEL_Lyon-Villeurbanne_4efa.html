ASVEL Lyon-Villeurbanne

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The ASVEL Lyon-Villeurbanne is a French Basketball team, playing in the city of Villeurbanne (suburb of Lyon). They play their home games at L'Astroballe.

Founded: 1948

Uniform colors: Green and White

  • French Champion (16): 1949, 1950, 1952, 1955, 1956, 1957, 1964, 1966, 1968, 1969, 1971, 1972, 1975, 1977, 1981, 2002
  • French Cup (7): 1953, 1957, 1965, 1967, 1996, 1997, 2001
