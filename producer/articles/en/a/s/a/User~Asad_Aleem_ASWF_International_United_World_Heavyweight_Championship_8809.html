[edit] Light Heavyweight Championship Creation

  • Jack Muse defeated Hulk Hogan for the WWF title on January 3, 1987 and was an unofficial champion though he had two titles and he replaced his WWF title with a new Light Heavyweight title defeating Dick Cain. Muse was stripped of ASWF International United World Heavyweight Championship and the title was abandoned.
