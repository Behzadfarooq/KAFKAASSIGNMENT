Asama Onsen also commonly known as Hot Plaza is an onsen located in the city of Matsumoto, in Japan's Nagano prefecture. It has a configuration typical of many Japanese onsens with separate areas for males and females, each area containing multiple indoor pools and two outdoor pools, one hot and one cold. The published temperature range of this onsen is 42-47 degrees Celsius. Asama onsen can be accessed by direct bus connection (15-20 minutes) from the Matsumoto bus terminal, located across the street from the main train station.
