Asahi Broadcasting Nagano Co.,Ltd. (長野朝日放送株式会社, Nagano Asahi Hōsō Kabushiki-gaisha?), also known as abn, is a Japanese broadcast network affiliated with the ANN. Their headquarters are located in Nagano Prefecture.

[edit] History

  • 1991 April 1 It was set up fourth broadcasting station of Nagano Prefecture.
  • 2006 October 1 Digital terrestrial television was started(Utsukushigahara Main Station,Zenkoji-daira Station,Matsumoto Station,Okaya-Suwa Station,Ina Station and Iida Station).
