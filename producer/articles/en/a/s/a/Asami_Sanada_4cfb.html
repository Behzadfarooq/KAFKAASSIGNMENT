Asami Sanada

Asami Sanada (真田アサミ, Sanada Asami?, born September 8, 1977 in Nagano, Japan) is a seiyū for several anime characters. She is 5 foot in height (1.53m).

[edit] Voice Roles

  • Dejiko - Di Gi Charat & Winter Garden
  • Michiru Ogawa - Bleach
  • Seiya Yaboshi - Daa! Daa! Daa!
  • Serena - Elemental Gelade
  • Waitress Robo - Galaxy Angel (Season 3 Episode 9)
  • Louis Fort Drake - Galaxy Railways
  • Vita - Magical Girl Lyrical Nanoha A's
  • Chizuko Ooe - Mahoromatic
  • Domiko - Nanaka 6/17
  • Shiina Tamai - Narutaru
  • Jun Sakurada - Rozen Maiden
  • Mari Nishino - This Ugly and Beautiful World
  • Phil - Tiny Snow Fairy Sugar
  • Mewt Randell - Radio Version of Final Fantasy Tactics Advance
