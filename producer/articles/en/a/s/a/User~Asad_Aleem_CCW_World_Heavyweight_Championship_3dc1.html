The Cagemaster Championship Wrestling (CCW) World Heavyweight Championship is the primary professional wrestling title in the Cagemaster Championship Wrestling show. It was created by Cagemaster for himself but later, several wrestlers took it.

[edit] Title history

[edit] Cagemaster

Cagemaster created his new show Cagemaster Championship Wrestling in April 2039 when he came to FWE. He started a tournament for the new CCW World Heavyweight Championship where Cagemaster and Shawn Ital met in the finals and Cagemaster pinned him in a Steel Cage match after a Cage Edge.

  • Tournament results:
    • 1st round:
      • Rock Rude def. Pro Gamma
      • Nickfury def. Kevin Texan
      • Shawn Ital def. Kenny Martin
      • Adeel Mughal def. Kenny Serpent
      • Shane Clerk def. Roger Hart
      • Cagemaster def. Shan Pung
    • Quarterfinals:
      • Rock Rude def. Nickfury
      • Shawn Ital def. Adeel Mughal
      • Cagemaster def. Shane Clerk
    • Semifinals:
      • Shawn Ital def. Rock Rude
      • Cagemaster to a BYE
    • Finals:
      • Cagemaster def. Shawn Ital

[edit] Rock Rude

Cagemaster wrestled in several Steel Cage Matches in his life and was credited as an undefeated wrestler but got his first defeat in a FWE Championship Royal Rumble match when he was eliminated by Rock Rude, the son of Rick Rude, a member of DX.

Cagemaster challenged Rude of a One-On-One match at CCW and then it was easy for him to win after he delivered a Cage Edge to Rude in the Steel Cage Match and later, Cagemaster climbed over the turnbuckles where Rude delivered him a Low Blow and pinned him after a roll-up and then a rematch occurred for the CCW title and Rude won it by chokeslam.

[edit] Captain America

After one month, Rock Rude faced his biggest challenge against the American army officer and superhero Captain America. Capt. America delivered a RKO to Rude and then pinned him to become the CCW World Heavyweight Champion.

[edit] Rock Rude (2)

Rock Rude was very angry and then a rematch occurred for the CCW title where Rude attacked Capt. America and went for the pinfall but Capt. kicked out at 2 and that kickout was not good for Rude. He then knocked out the referee by hitting him with the chair and he also hit Capt. America with the chair and then hit him with the CCW title belt and delivered him a Chokeslam and then pinned him and got his second CCW title.

[edit] Shan Pung

Shan Pung couldn't forget his revenge with Cagemaster when he got defeated by Cagemaster in the 1st round of the CCW title tournament. Pung went on to compete in a #1 Contender's Match where Kenny Martin was scheduled to wrestle with Cagemaster but was injured by Pung and then Pung attacked Cagemaster from behind by spearing him and then delivered him a Japan Bomb and pinned him and then competed with the champion Rock Rude where he was distracted by Cagemaster but he attacked on Rude and pinned him to get the victory and the title.

[edit] Rock Rude (3)

For a third time, Rock Rude came to take his revenge and he had several matches with Shan Pung for the title but couldn't win. He competed in a non-title match with Pung and won the match and both of them became rivals for the title. It was a tag team match where Rude got the victory by pinning Pung and in that match, Rude's partner was Cagemaster and Pung's partner was Pam Pung.

[edit] Kevin Texan

A member of Representatives and the FWE Intercontinental Champion Kevin Texan came up to challenge Rude and then he got the best of Rude and pinned him by a roll-up and got the victory and won the CCW World Heavyweight Championship.

[edit] Shawn Ital

Shawn Ital couldn't forget his revenge with Kevin Texan that he lost the Intercontinental title to Texan. He was a partner to Rick Ital in those days. Shawn delivered a Elevated DDT to Texan and then he climbed over the turnbuckles but was distracted by Kevin's friend Adeel.

It was Shawn's bad luck and he climbed over Adeel and then went into the ring and pinned Kevin and got his first CCW title by a success.

[edit] Kenny Serpent

Kenny Serpent has been a rival of Shawn Ital since the tag team of Joey and Mac appeared in FWE. A Six-Pack Challenge was held on an edition of CCW where Shawn defended the title against his rival Kenny, Kevin Texan, Adeel Mughal, Pro Gamma and Cagemaster.

Cagemaster delivered Cage Edge to Adeel, Kevin and the champion Shawn and the remaining were Kenny and Pro Gamma who attacked Cagemaster by a Standing Drophit and he fell out of the ring and then they both delivered him a suplex turn by turn and then Pro Gamma was rolled-up by Kenny and then Kenny won the match and the title.

[edit] Chris Wayne

Chris Wayne, a former TCW Champion wrestled in CCW defeating Kevin Texan in several matches and then went on to compete for the CCW World Heavyweight Championship. He became a rival to Kenny Serpent and defeated him after a Masterlock to get the title.

[edit] Shan Pung (2)

Just after one week, Shan Pung challenged Chris Wayne and then he had a match with Wayne where Wayne was going to get the victory but Pung came with a chair aand threw it in the ring and he entered the ring and Wayne picked up the chair but Pung speared him and then pinned him to get the CCW title.

[edit] Shawn Ital (2)

Shan Pung was thinking that all of his challengers were easy but when he came to defend his title against Shawn Ital, he was very surprised. Ital dropped Pung on the pin and started wrestling a smart match and went for the pin and Pung kicked out at 2 which threw Ital over the turnbuckles.

Ital was taken into Missile dropkick but he reversed it after delivering Pung a Low Blow and then locked him into the Masterlock and delivered him a Pedigree. Ital just pinned Pung and then got his second CCW title.

[edit] Kevin Texan (2)

Kevin Texan was the man who earlier defeated Shawn Ital for the Intercontinental Championship and now for the CCW World Heavyweight Championship. He defeated Ital in a No Disqualification Match by hitting Ital with the chairs and then his friends came by the ringside and then all the three attacked Ital with a supplex. Kevin pinned Ital and got his second CCW title.

[edit] Shan Pung (3)

Shan Pung lost a match for the CCW title to Kevin Texan but a rematch occurred and it was a No Holds Barred for Pung but not for Texan. Kevin attacked Pung several times to get the pin but all the times, Pung kicked out and when it came to Pung, he threw Kevin very badly and then pinned him and got his third CCW title.

[edit] Kenny Martin

Kenny Martin, a jobber and Shan Pung was not a competition. Pung just kicked Martin who fell down on the ring. Pung went for the pin but the referee was knockedout by Kevin Texan. Pung became very angry at this. He kindly delivered a Powerbomb to Texan and Martin gave Pung a Low Blow.

Martin hit Pung with the Standing Drophit and then pinned Pung and then he became the CCW World Heavyweight Champion.

[edit] Pro Gamma

The new champion Kenny Martin reigned as the champion just for one week because one week later, Pro Gamma challenged him for the CCW World Heavyweight Championship. Gamma punched Martin and he fell down and then Gamma just pinned Martin and got the CCW World Heavyweight Championship.

[edit] Shan Pung (4)

Pro Gamma felt easy when he defeated Kenny Martin but when he defended his title against Shan Pung, he was very upset. Pung upset him and then Pung kicked Gamma and kept on beating him. Pung came from the top rope and gave a flying jump to Gamma and then Powerbombed him and pinned him to get his fourth CCW title. He became the first wrestler to hold the CCW World Heavyweight Championship for fourth time.

[edit] Shawn Ital (3)

Shawn Ital defeated Shan Pung for his second CCW title and he also defeated Pung for his fourth title. He kicked Pung very badly and then kept on beating him. In the end, he delivered Pung a Superkick and went for the pin and his third CCW title.

[edit] The Rookie

The best wrestler in the world of professional wrestling, The Rookie is credited to be the best wrestler who has defeated many legends and many strong wrestlers in title or non-title matches. Shawn Ital was also easy for Rookie.

He did his best but couldn't get the win. When he delivered a Body slam to Ital, the match became easy for him and then he pinned Ital to get his first CCW title. He reigned as the CCW World Heavyweight Champion for 34 days (1 month, 3 days) and was credited to be the longest reigning CCW Champion.

[edit] Pro Gamma (2)

Pro Gamma and The Rookie, two rivals in history exchanged the CCW title for the first time. In the beginning, it was difficult for Rookie to get the win but later, he thought to himself that he could win and he wrestled and got the match in his control but Pro Gamma's assistant Gory came with a chair and entered the ring and then The Rookie stopped Gory and from behind, Pro Gamma went for the roll-up and then got the CCW title.

[edit] Shan Pung (5)

For the both times, Pro Gamma lost his title to Shan Pung. Pung this time controlled Gory and Pro Gamma by Powerbombing both of them after wrestling the match just for 10 minutes and then he pinned Pro Gamma to get his fifth CCW title. He became the first wrestler to hold the CCW World Heavyweight Championship for five times.

[edit] The Rookie (2)

Shan Pung, The Rookie and Ilyas Tufail squared off against each other in a 3-Way Elimination Match where Rookie delivered Pung a supplex and then Pung had to suffer the Disco Bomb and the champion was pinned by Ilyas. He got up after doing his pinfall but after that, Rookie attacked on Ilyas and started keeping on.

Rookie delivered a back supplex to Ilyas but Ilyas reversed it into DDT and went for the pin but Rookie kicked out at 2 and then Ilyas went for his submission move Got Ya! Babe. Rookie was to lock in but he had a roll-up and then he won the match and his second CCW title.

[edit] James Carl

James Carl made a rare appearance in CCW for the CCW world title in a match with The Rookie. Rookie controlled much of the match and was credited to be the Man of the Match but his luck was noat good because Carl did his pinfall for his title.

The match started off as Rookie had the match in his control and he delivered a back supplex to Carl and then decided to do pinfall but Carl kicked out at 2 and then Carl did his attack and he started beating Rookie. He kicked Rookie and then climbed the turnbuckles and gave up a jump.

Carl then delivered Spinebuster to Rookie and then went for the pin and his CCW title.

[edit] Minor Man

Minor Man, the best jobber ever seen in FWE was a former FWE Light Heavyweight Champion and now it was the time for the CCW World Heavyweight Championship. He had a match with The Rookie where Rookie kicked Minor Man and then came from the ropes to do a jump but this jobber got on side and this hurt Rookie.

Rookie and Minor Man both of them were doing their best to win and Rookie was just going to regain his title but this time, Minor Man didn't wrestle as a jobber even he used his hands and his power and then he kicked Rookie who was almost dying and then Minor Man pinned him and got his CCW title.

[edit] The Rookie (3)

Rookie challenged Minor Man a few days later for the CCW title and then he defeated Minor Man in just 32 seconds and set the quickest record of the CCW title victory and got his third CCW title.

[edit] Rocky Jones

Rocky Jones became an opponent of The Rookie for the CCW World Heavyweight Championship. He wrestled with Rookie for the title and then beat him. He climbed on the turnbuckles but Rookie pulled him and threw him very badly.

Rookie was going to pin but was distracted by Thunder Gang and then Rookie came outside the ring to look over them and controlled of them but when he entered the ring, Rocky chokeslammed him and then won the match and his first CCW World Heavyweight Championship.

[edit] Nickfury

Nickfury, who earlier participated in the CCW World Heavyweight Championship tournament also needed an opportunity for the title. He had a match with Rocky. People were supporting Nickfury but they thought that Rocky would win by cheating but they were wrong.

They were wrong because when once again, the Thunder Gang appeared, Nick Fury went inside the ring and he didn't concentrate towards them even he threw Rocky over all of them. He then threw Rocky inside the ring and kicked him several times and then climbed over the turnbuckles and did a bad jump on Rocky and then he pinned Rocky to get his first CCW World Heavyweight Championship.

Nick Fury and Rocky would become long-time rivals for the CCW World Heavyweight Championship and would exchange the title for six times.

[edit] Rocky Jones (2)

As in the Nickfury section of this article, it is mentioned that he and Rocky Jones exchanged the title for six times. It was for the second time these both wrestlers were exchanging the CCW World Heavyweight Championship.

Rocky challenged Nick for the title and this time, he was successful in defeating Nick. He speared Nick from behind and then attacked on him and kicked him brutally. He threw Nick on the steel and then he threw him into the ring. Rocky started punching him and threw him very badly over the steel outside the ring. He climbed over the turnbuckles and jumped on Nick Fury.

Rocky started beating him and until his friends, Thunder Gang came to help and then Hassaan Tariq, one of the members of Thunder Gang from behind opened the turnbuckles and the referee was closing them. He then picked up a chair and then hit Nick Fury with the chair. Rocky threw Nick Fury into the ring and pinned him for his second CCW World Heavyweight Championship.

[edit] Nick Fury (2)

Nick Fury challenged Rocky for a match for the CCW World Heavyweight Championship and then defeated him in a dark match on FWE Monday Night Trend to get his second CCW World Heavyweight Championship. It was for the third time, these both wrestlers were exchanging the title.

[edit] Rocky Jones (3)

Rocky Jones challenged Nick Fury for a No Disqualification Match. This time, the match seemed to be in Nick's control. He beat Rocky in the beginning but when it came to turnbuckles, Rocky protected himself and Nick's jump hurt him. Rocky went out of the ring and then picked up a chair and threw it into the ring and hit Nick with the chair.

He came from the ropes and suddenly the referee was knocked out and then he kicked Nick. Now Hassaan Tariq once again came for the help and then both of them attacked Nick Fury with six chairs and then Rocky pinned Nick Fury and another referee came to do pinfall and then this gave Rocky, his third CCW World Heavyweight Championship and a fourth exchange of title.

[edit] Nick Fury (3)

Nick Fury, Rocky Jones, Kevin Texan, Cagemaster, Shawn Ital, Chris Wayne, Rock Rude and Minor Man wrestled in a 8-Way Elimination Steel Cage Match. Both long-time rivals, Nick and the champion Rocky wrestled with each other. Shawn Ital controlled on Kevin Texan and then Cagemaster stepped towards Rock Rude.

Chris Wayne punched Minor Man who fell down and then pinned him and then Minor Man was eliminated and then he came from the ropes and Shawn Ital hit him with a Legbreaker and tried to pin Wayne but Wayne kicked out at 2. Ital threw Wayne over the turnbuckles and from behind, Kevin Texan hit him with a Standing Drophit. He started beating Ital and threw him.

Texan climbed on turnbuckles and Wayne pushed him which threw Texan. Cagemaster took Ital over the ropes and came from the ropes and then knocked him in the steel cage. He picked up Ital and delivered him a Chokebomb and then pinned him and Ital was also eliminated. Here, Cagemaster felt very happy and then he attacked on Texan and then this made Texan fainted and unconscious.

Rock Rude gave a Low Blow to Rocky and then tried to do a roll-up but Nick Fury jumped on both of them. He threw Rude out of the ring and then pinned his rival and the champion Rocky who was very angry for losing his title and then he delivered a Spinebuster to Nick Fury. Rock Rude entered the ring and then he hit Cagemaster with a Standing Drophit and then Kevin Texan and Rock Rude both of them threw Cagemaster over Chris Wayne.

Texan hit Rude with his elbow and then kicked him and threw him over the steel cage. He then pulled Rude and pinned him and Rude was eliminated. He came from the ropes and then hit Cagemaster with the Standing Drophit and from behind, Nick Fury eliminated Texan by a roll-up and then he attacked on Chris Wayne and then kept on beating him and threw him over the turnbuckles and pinned Wayne.

Now left in the ring were two former champions, Nick Fury and Cagemaster. Cagemaster started beating Fury and then put him over the Steel Cage. Cagemaster came from the ropes to hit Fury but Fury protected himself and Cagemaster was knocked out into the steel cage. Nick Fury gave a Low Blow to Cagemaster and then pinned him after a roll-up to get his third CCW World Heavyweight Championship in a fifth exchange of his title with Rocky.

[edit] Rocky Jones (4)

Rocky could remember that he was eliminated by Nick Fury (who was the champion at the time) in the 8-Man Elimination Match. Rocky took his revenge with Nick Fury in a world title match. He attacked on Fury and then kept on beating him and showed his anger to Fury. He delivered a Spinebuster to Fury and then pinned him to get his fourth CCW World Heavyweight Championship.

This was the sixth exchange of Rocky Jones and Nick Fury of the CCW World Heavyweight Championship and this match was the end of the long-time rivalry between Rocky and Nick.

[edit] The Rookie (4)

The Rookie was the man whom Rocky Jones defeated for his first CCW World Heavyweight Championship. Rookie remembered that match and then he challenged Rocky for a title match. The match was a match of revenge where Rookie attacked Rocky and then beat him very well. This time, Thunder Gang once again tried to save Rocky but Rookie didn't care.

He attacked on Rocky and hit him with the elbow. He kicked Rocky very badly and then delivered him a Spinebuster and then Rookie pinned Rocky to get his fourth CCW World Heavyweight Championship.

[edit] Rock Rude (4)

Rock Rude made a surprising return in CCW World Heavyweight Championship. After a long time, he found himself in his match for the CCW title. He wrestled with The Rookie and he ended the match after a Chokeslam and then pinned Rookie to get his fourth CCW World Heavyweight Championship.

[edit] The Rookie (5)

Rookie took his revenge with Rock Rude by challenging him for a match for the CCW World Heavyweight Championship. When Rookie entered into the ring, Rock Rude kept on beating him and then kicked him and threw him over the turnbuckles. He was coming from the ropes to do an attack but Rookie protected himself.

Rookie felt himself tired but he didn't lose his efforts. He gave Rock a Backbreaker and then pinned him to get his fifth CCW World Heavyweight Championship.

[edit] Ironman

Ironman, the superhero, was a third superhero to become the CCW World Heavyweight Champion. He wrestled with The Rookie in a match. Rookie did his best to win and broke Ironman's leg and Ironman couldn't wrestle in that match very well. Rookie climbed over the turnbuckles and took advantage of the situation by jumping on Ironman but was caught by Ironman and then Ironman threw him and then slapped in Crossface Chicken Wing. Rookie had to submit and the lame Ironman got his CCW World Heavyweight Championship.

[edit] Rocky Jones (5)

Rocky Jones who had been in severe rivalry with superheroes for the CCW World Heavyweight Championship chased Ironman and then at last, Rocky caught Ironman and then got his best and defeated him to get his fifth CCW World Heavyweight Championship. The title was vacated because Rocky was injured in a match with Shawn Ital for the title.
