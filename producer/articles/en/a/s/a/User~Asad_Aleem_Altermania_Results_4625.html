This article is a list of the pay-per-view event Altermania and in this article, we are going to show you the results of that event. It was promoted as Donaldmania under the Pacific Northwest Wrestling banner.

[edit] Donaldmania

[edit] Donaldmania I: Journey Towards September Gold

Donaldmania I: Journey Towards September Gold
Details

Date July 18, 1989
Venue Cow Palace
City San Fransisco, California
Attendance 13,000

Donaldmania I: Journey Towards September Gold was the first Donaldmania/Altermania pay-per-view event held on July 18, 1989 and it was an event by Pacific Northwest Wrestling.

  • Tatsumi Fujinami defeated The Grappler (03:21)
  • Carl Styles defeated Top Gun (04:16)
  • Curtis Thompson defeated Rex King (05:29)
  • Steve Doll defeated Billy Jack Haynes (06:01)
  • Billy Jack Haynes defeated Killer Brooks (06:42)
  • Thomas Couch and Brady Boone fought to no contest (07:15)
  • Tournament semifinal for the vacant NWA Pacific Northwest Heavyweight Championship-Carl Styles defeated Larry Oliver (07:39)
  • Tournament semifinal main event: Scotty The Body defeated Scott Peterson (08:28)
