Mount Asahi
旭岳 Asahi-dake

Mount Asahi, the tallest peak in Hokkaidō
Elevation 2290.3 metres
Location Hokkaidō, Japan
Coordinates 43°39′N 142°51′E
Type Stratovolcano

Mount Asahi (旭岳, Asahi-dake?) is the tallest mountain in Hokkaidō, Japan. Part of the Daisetsuzan Volcanic Group, it is located in the northern part of the Daisetsuzan National Park.

The mountain is popular with hikers in the summer and can be easily reached from Asahidake Onsen via a ropeway.

Sugatami Pond, directly below the peak, is famous for its reflection of the peaks, snow, and steam escaping from the volcanic vents.
