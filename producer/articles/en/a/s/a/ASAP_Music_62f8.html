ASAP Music is the record label of the ABS-CBN's variety show, ASAP. Hosts of ASAP come together to produce quality music for all to enjoy. It is said that producing an ASAP album could take over a year for the reason that most of their hosts are already signed with other record labels.

[edit] Hotsilog

A 70s icon had a comeback on October 8, 2006. Hotdog, one of the most popular bands of the "jeprox" era, had dominate the airwaves again as their trademark ‘Manila sound’ had given a contemporary twist by ASAP 06’s very own stars in Hotsilog. Hotsilog, the pilot compilation album that signals the show’s groundbreaking foray into the recording field, presents Piolo Pascual, Sarah Geronimo, Gary Valenciano, Zsa Zsa Padilla, Toni Gonzaga, Sam Milby, Nina, among many others, as they lend their vocals to the band’s most memorable hits such as the carrier single "Ikaw Ang Miss Universe ng Buhay Ko," "Pers Lab," "Manila," "I Can’t Forget You," "Annie Batungbakal," "Langit Na Naman," and "O Lumapit Ka." The first ever band to use "Taglish" in the 70s, Hotdog’s vast collection of songs has become a timeless benchmark in OPM. ASAP 06 shadows the band’s legacy by becoming the first ever variety show in the country to produce its own album, setting the bar for what total entertainment is really all about.
