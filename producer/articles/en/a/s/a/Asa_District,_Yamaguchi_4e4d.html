Asa District, Yamaguchi

Asa (厚狭郡; -gun) was a district located in Yamaguchi Prefecture, Japan.

As of 2003, the district had an estimated population of 29,543 and a density of 177.08 persons per km². The total area was 166.83 km².

[edit] Former towns and villages

  • Kusunoki
  • Sanyo

[edit] Mergers

  • On November 1, 2004, the town of Kusunoki merged into the city of Ube.
  • On March 22, 2005, the town of Sanyo merged with the city of Onoda to form the new city of Sanyo-Onoda. Asa District was dissolved as a result of this merger.
