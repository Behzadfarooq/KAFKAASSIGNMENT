Mark Joseph Rage (April 18, 1970 - May 25, 1993) was an American professional wrestler and footballer who was the third son of the legendary wrestler and the mixed martial artist, the late Donald Rage (1942-2007).

[edit] Early life

Mark Rage was born to Donald Rage on April 18, 1970. He spent his early childhood in watching professional wrestling because he liked it a lot. His father was also a wrestler during those days and Mark always went with his father to the arena to see and he never missed any wrestling match of his father. He received his studies in Miami (Ohio)

At the age of 16 in 1986, Mark debuted as a footballer who played American football for the New York Giants from 1986 to 1989. He was a linebacker and he gained popularity in 1987. He became one of the top and notable football players in the New York Giants football team which played for the National Football League.

Here, in 1988, Mark's leg was fractured in a football match and he lost his popularity. He then left New York Giants and went to play for New York Jets so he could get his popularity back. He spent even one year so that he could become the best footballer, the World Champion of football but in 1989, he was fired from New York Jets and then he got retirement from football and was interested in wrestling.

[edit] Professional wrestling (1989-1993)

[edit] Early career

After retiring from football in 1989, Mark became a wrestler at the age 19 and started wrestling for American Wrestling Association. He appeared in AWA managing Larry Zbyszko. He later left AWA to join Continental Wrestling Association.

In CWA, Mark won the CWA Memphis World Heavyweight Championship. Afterwards, he left CWA and then went to wrestle for World Wrestling Council where he captured the WWC Caribbean Heavyweight Championship. Thus, he became the first of Donald Rage's sons to capture any championship.

[edit] New Japan Pro Wrestling (1989-1993)

[edit] 1989

After leaving WWC, Mark left for New Japan Pro Wrestling where his elder brothers Rick and David were wrestling. Mark teamed with Rick and then they both defeated Shinya Hashimoto and Salman Hashimikov. These both brothers defeated several wrestlers in tag team matches until August 1989.

By August, Mark and Rick wrestled for the IWGP World Tag Team Championship and feud with the-then World Tag Team Champions Shinya Hashimoto and Masa Saito for the titles several times but couldn't win any match. They won a match but that was by countout thus Hashimoto and Saito regained the titles.

[edit] 1990

Mark and Rick wrestled on January 5, 1990 with Hashimoto and Saito for the World Tag Team titles. This time, Mark and Rick had the match in control but Mark turned on Rick by kicking him. Mark had his mother Jenna Rage by his side to support him and now Mark turned into heel.

Mark started feuding with his brother Rick and teamed up with David who was also feuding with Rick at the time. Both brothers cost Rick a match on February 24, 1990 when Rick was having an IWGP World Heavyweight Championship title match with the then-champion Vader. They came for the rescue of the champion and attacked their elder brother Rick.

Surprisingly, popular wrestling star World Champion came to rescue Rick and speared Mark and David. Mark and David wrestled in a match with Rick and World Champion which they lost. Later, David was defeated by Rick in a match and was forced to quit New Japan which left Mark alone and then Mark's brother Rick feuded with him.

Mark had also a feud with junior heavyweights and was distracted by Rick in all of his matches so Mark lost. Mark and Rick wrestled in a final match on June 16, 1990 to end feud. Mark lost that match and then he defeated Akira Nogami and then he feuded with Shinya Hashimoto once again.

[edit] 1991

Mark was feeling very easy in 1991 because David had returned to New Japan and then both brothers feuded with Rick once again but their brother Eric came to Rick's support. Mark and David tried to include Eric in their team but he wrestled as Rick's partner.

Mark and David wrestled as heel and feuded with Rick and Eric until April 1991 because on April 15, 1991, a tag team match occurred where Mark was without his brother and partner David and then he was defeated very easily by Rick and Eric. Later, it was discovered that David had died and all the brothers felt very sad including Mark.

All of them took leap from New Japan and returned to New Japan in August 1991. They reunited and paid tribute to their respective brother David. Mark now turned into face. Mark and Rick defeated the then-IWGP World Tag Team Champions Steiner Brothers in a non-title match and then feuded with them for the titles but Steiners didn't surrender them titles. Later, on November 5, 1991 when the Steiners defended their tag team titles against Hiroshi Hase and Keiji Mutoh, Rick and Mark came and injured Scott Steiner.

Later, Steiner Brothers challenged Rick and Mark of a tag team match and won the match by defeating both brothers.

[edit] 1992

Mark and Rick defeated Steiner Brothers in a rematch on January 7, 1992 which was the greatest victory they ever had. After that match, Mark announced that he was going to compete on singles on his own and then he left the team and feuded with Jushin Liger for the junior heavyweight title.

Mark left the feud with Liger and then he feuded with Ultimo Dragon. He defeated Dragon in a No-Disqualification Match and became fan favorite. The feud would continue until May 1992 and after that, on June 3, 1992, Mark paid tribute to his late brother David who died in the last year.

He defeated El Samurai on June 3 paying tribute to his brother and then he was invited to wrestle a match for World Championship Wrestling on June 15, 1992 as a New Japan representative defeating Kevin Nash. He later wrestled for Smoky Mountain Wrestling and represented New Japan in SMW.

He made several appearances for SMW defeating Chris Candido and most notably Brian Lee.

[edit] 1993

Mark started 1993 feuding with Pegasus Kid until March 1993. Mark's closest brother was Eric who had died in January of that year. Mark paid tribute to Eric by defeating his rival Pegasus Kid and then teamed up with his brother Rick for two months. The tag team ended in May 1993 when Mark left the team and died.

[edit] Death

On May 25, 1993, Mark felt very sad because his brother Eric had died and then Mark shot himself with a gun and became the second son of Mr. Donald Rage who committed suicide at the age 23.
