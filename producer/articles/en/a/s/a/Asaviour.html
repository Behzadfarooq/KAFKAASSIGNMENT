Asaviour (b. Nigel Benjamin Cudjoe on 8th January 1979) hails from Huddersfield (West Yorkshire) England, he is a UK hip-hop artist most noted for featuring on tracks alongside well-established british rapper Jehst, such as the UK Hip-Hop classic "People Under The Weather". Asaviour's first known release was "Savoir Faire EP" 2003. His acclaimed debut album was released in March of 2006, under the title "Borrowed Ladder". The album features well-known UK artists Braintax, Kyza & Yungun.

[edit] Discography

  • Featured on Jehsts debut release "Premonitions" (1999) - YNR Productions
  • Featured on "Full Force" (2001) Dominant 3rd Records
  • Featured on the track "People Under The Weather" on Jehsts 12" release "Return Of The Drifter - Low Life Records
  • Releases the "Savoir Faire" EP (2003) - Low Life Records
  • Releases the Single "Homecoming/Money in the Bank RMX" (2006) - Ynr Productions
  • Releases the Album "Borrowed Ladder" (2006) - Low Life Records
