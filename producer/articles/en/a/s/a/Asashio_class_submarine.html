The Asashio class submarine is a submarine class of Japanese Maritime Self-Defense Force. This class is the successor of JDS Oshio.

[edit] Specifications

  • Displacement:
    • 1,650 tons standard
    • 2,150 tons submerged
  • Dimensions:
    • Length: 88 m
    • Beam: 8.2 m
    • Draft: 4.9 m
    • Depth: 7.5 m
  • Maximum speed
    • 14 knots surfaced
    • 18 knots submerged
  • Propulsion: 2 shaft diesel electric
  • Armament
    • Torpedoes: 6 × 533 mm torpedo tubes (fore). 2 × 533 mm torpedo tubes (aft).
  • Complement: 80.
