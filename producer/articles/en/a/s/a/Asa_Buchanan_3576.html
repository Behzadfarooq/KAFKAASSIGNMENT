Asa Jeb Stuart Buchanan is a fictional character on the ABC soap opera, One Life to Live. The role was originated in late 1979 by Philip Carey, who made a guest appearance as the father of the newly arrived Clint and Bo Buchanan. In early 1980, Carey reprised the role full time.

The patriarch of one of Llanview's most prominent families, the Buchanan’s, Asa is a self-made billionaire and Chief Executive Officer of Buchanan Enterprises.

Born and raised in Texas, Buchanan moved the headquarters of his company to Llanview in 1980 to stay close to his sons, Clint and Bo. He is also the most married man in daytime television, having been married 14 times to 11 different women, although several of the marriages were invalid[1]. Asa resides at the Buchanan Mansion.

Asa will leave the show in June 2007, due to the fact that Phil Carey has turned down the show's offer to place him on recurring status. [1]
