Asaka Kubo

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Asaka Kubo (久保亜沙香, Kubo Asaka?, born January 20, 1979) is a Japanese gravure idol, a Seiyū and a singer. She belongs to the show-business production PLATINUM-S.
