Asasekiryū Tarō (Japanese: 朝赤龍 太郎, born August 7, 1981 as Badarch Dashnyam (Бадарч Дашням) in Ulan Bator, Mongolia), is a sumo wrestler. The highest rank he has reached is komusubi.

His father was a Mongolian wrestler, holding a rank roughly equivalent to sumo's komusubi.

Asasekiryū came to Japan in 1997 and fought his first professional sumo bout in January 2000. He achieved jūryō (second division) rank in July 2002, and makuuchi (first division) rank in March 2003. He was promoted to komusubi for the July 2006 tournament, but was forced to retire partway through the basho due to injury and hasn't achieved sanyaku status since.
