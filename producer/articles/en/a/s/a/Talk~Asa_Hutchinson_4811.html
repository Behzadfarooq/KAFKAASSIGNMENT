[edit] Name Pronunciation

How do you pronounce "Asa"? Until I saw his picture, I thought this was a woman! (Asa sound like a girl name to anyone else?) (This comment was left unsigned, but was left by Meekermariner 07:36, 24 May 2006 (UTC))

It is pronounced Ay-suh, the first "A" sounding like the end of the word "today", and the second "A" sounding like "uh" such as in the word "up". It is a Biblical name [1], and is used as a male name in the Bible. I suppose it could be used as either male or female, but all references I have ever heard are for the previously mentioned male one from the Bible. In the future, please sign your comments. (by putting 4 ~'s at the end of your comment, and it will make it look like mine at the end, with my name and time) (Cardsplayer4life 11:11, 24 May 2006 (UTC))
