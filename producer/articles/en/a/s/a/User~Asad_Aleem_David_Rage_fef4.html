David Joseph Rage (October 3, 1968 - April 15, 1991) was an American professional wrestler who was the second son of the legendary wrestler and mixed martial artist, the late Donald Rage (1942-2007) who wrestled briefly in New Japan Pro Wrestling and World Wrestling Federation.

[edit] Early life

David got his training as a wrestler in his college and then he was trained by his father, Donald Rage and then David started wrestling in 1987.

[edit] Professional wrestling (1987-1991)

[edit] New Japan Pro Wrestling (1987-1989)

[edit] 1987

David started wrestling in New Japan Pro Wrestling at the age of 19 as Davey Rage. He wrestled for NJPW in the mid-1987 and teamed up with his elder brother Rick Rage and feuded with the IWGP World Tag Team Champions Yoshiaki Fujiwara and Kazuo Yamazaki for the titles but they failed.

After his elder brother Rick defeated Fujiwara in a singles match, David defeated Yamazaki in a singles match. Now Rick and David were wrestling towards success and had a good luck. They both received their greatest victory as their tag team on December 28, 1987 by defeating Antonio Inoki and Tatsumi Fujinami.

[edit] 1988

David started 1988 as Dave Rage feuding with junior heavyweights and later, he turned heel in May 1988 feuding with his returning brother Rick. He lost to Rick several times but on July 13, 1988, he had a good match which was a No Disqualification Match.

David was having his mother Jenna Rage on the side and Rick was having no one. David suffered a chair shot by Rick but when Rick went for the pin, their mother distracted Rick and then David gave a Low Blow to Rick and pinned him which made Rick lose the match.

They both now put their eyes on Tatsumi Fujinami's IWGP World Heavyweight Championship and then they both shook hands with each other so that they could get Fujinami's title. They both wrestled in a Triple Threat Match where Fujinami defended his title against both brothers. After they both attacked Fujinami, Rick went for the pin but David kicked there.

Both brothers quarrelled there and Fujinami gave Low Blow to both of them. Later, David lost a match to Rick which was Rick's revenge. These both brothers feuded with each other once again in the late 1988.

[edit] 1989

David wrestled in 1989 under his real name David Rage. He formed a tag team with Big Van Vader and kept on winning several tag team matches. Throughout March 1989 and April 1989, David feuded once again with his brother Rick. He started feuding with Salman Hashimikov for May and June.

He later feuded with Vader for the IWGP World Heavyweight Championship and defeated Vader in a non-title match. He kept on feuding with Akira Nogami and most notably Pegasus Kid. David also wrestled for New Japan in early 1990 teaming with his brother Mark Rage.

David and Mark were once speared by World Champion who supported Rick and then Rick and World Champion defeated them on March 3, 1990. David later lost a match to Rick and he was so injured that he was forced to quit New Japan and then he left for WWF.

[edit] World Wrestling Federation (1990)

David signed a contract with WWF in June 1990. He wrestled for WWF as a jobber. He lost the matches to several wrestlers but received his first victory amazingly in October 1990 by defeating Marty Jannetty. He feuded with The Rockers in October and later with Texas Tornado.

[edit] New Japan Pro Wrestling (1991)

After wrestling for almost a year in WWF, David returned to New Japan in 1991 feuding with his brother Rick once again and teaming with Mark once again. Their brother Eric debuted and they tried to include Eric in their team but they failed and he stayed in Rick's team.

David and Mark started feuding with Rick and Eric and wrestled with them in several tag team matches. Their feud ended on April 15, 1991 when these both teams were having a last match in their feud (not in New Japan). On that day, David didn't come and then Mark was easily defeated by Rick and Eric and this match had become a handicap match. Later, it was announced that for being absent in a match, David was fired.

[edit] Death

On March 24, 1991, David was not feeling well and later, doctors found out that he couldn't breathe. The Rage family claimed that he was an asthmetic from his childhood. Later, on April 15, 1991, while being hospitalized, doctors said that the popular wrestling star from the rage family, David died at the age 22.
