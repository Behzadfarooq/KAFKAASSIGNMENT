Altermania is an annual professional wrestling pay-per-view event where several wrestlers wrestle and it is promoted by famous mixed martial artist and legendary wrestler Donald Rage. This PPV is held in every July since 1989.

[edit] History

Altermania was started in July 1989 as Donaldmania when the World Wrestling Federation and NWA World Championship Wrestling were being top promotions in the world and were having best pay-per-view events.

WWF was gaining more popularity and at the same time, the professional wrestling legend, Donald Rage who was a superstar wrestling for Pacific Northwest Wrestling for the time started promoting Altermania as an answer to WWF's WrestleMania and WCW's Wrestle War. It was also considered to be a top pay-per-view event of PNW until July 1992.

In 1993, the event became Altermania from Donaldmania and it remains as Altermania from that day. In 2000, it became the only pay-per-view of the new Portland Wrestling.
