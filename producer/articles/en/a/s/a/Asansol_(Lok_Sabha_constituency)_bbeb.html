[edit] Assembly segments

For the 2004 Genaral Elections, Asansol Lok Sabha constituency was composed of the following assembly segments [3]:

  • Asansol
  • Hirapur
  • Kulti
  • Barabani
  • Raniganj
  • Jamuria
  • Ukhra
