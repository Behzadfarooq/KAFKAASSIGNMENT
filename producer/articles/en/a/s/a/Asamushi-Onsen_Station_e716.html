Asamushi-Onsen Station (浅虫温泉駅, Asamushi Onsen Eki?) is a railway station on the JR East Tōhoku Main Line. It is located in Aomori, Aomori, Japan.

[edit] Station Structure

  • 2-way, 3-track, above-ground station.
1 ■Tōhoku Main Line For.Hachinohe
2 ■Tōhoku Main Line (siding)
3 ■Tōhoku Main Line For.Aomori
  • Asamushi-Onsen Station is a simple consignment station, administered by Aomori Station, and operated by Jaster Co., Ltd. Ordinary tickets, express tickets, and reserved-seat tickets for all JR lines are on sale.
