Asahikawa Airport (旭川空港, Asahikawa Kūkō?) (IATA: AKJ, ICAO: RJEC), is a single-runway regional airport in Hokkaidō, Japan, near the city of Asahikawa. It is a second class airport.

[edit] Airlines and destinations

  • Air Do (Tokyo-Haneda)
  • All Nippon Airways (Nagoya-Chubu, Tokyo-Haneda)
  • Asiana Airlines (Seoul-Incheon)
  • Hokkaido Air System (Hakodate, Kushiro)
  • Japan Airlines (Osaka-Itami, Osaka-Kansai, Tokyo-Haneda)
