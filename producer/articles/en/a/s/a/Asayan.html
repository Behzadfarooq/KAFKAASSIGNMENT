ASAYAN, originally known as 浅草橋ヤング洋品店 (Asakusabashi Young Youhinten, 浅草橋ヤング洋品店?), was a talent search variety show that appeared on TV Tokyo. Many Hello! Project artists and groups were discovered through ASAYAN auditions produced by Tsunku.
