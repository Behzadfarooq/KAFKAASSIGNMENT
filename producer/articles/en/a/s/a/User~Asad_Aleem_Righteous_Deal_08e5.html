Righteous Deal is a fictional professional wrestling heel stable in the animated series Ring Bout. The stable was formed at Fighting Doom pay-per-view event. The team was made when Jack and Mike won the DWF World Tag Team Championship.

[edit] History

[edit] 1998

Two wrestlers Jack Barney and Michael "Mike" Ranger became friends as a tag team in 1998 who started wrestling in the promotion DWF. They debuted in DWF on Fighting Doom pay-per-view and won the DWF World Tag Team Championship as Champs of Doom.

They became fan favorite and kept the titles with themselves. They kept on defeating challengers and then later their tag team was joined by Johnny Daniels. Later, Jack and Mike lost their championships and then Champs of Doom went to wrestle for SWF.

They defeated several wrestlers in SWF and then captured the SWF 6-Man Tag Team Championship but when Jack won the SWF Heavyweight Championship, so Mike and Johnny kicked Jack out of the team and vacated the SWF 6-Man Tag Team Championship but they later included Jack when he lost the SWF World Heavyweight Championship.

Afterwards, they competed in MWF where Jack and Johnny captured the MWF World Tag Team Championships and they wrestled in MWF as JMJ (Jack, Mike & Johnny).

[edit] 1999

In 1999, JMJ feuded with Region of Doom which was a spin-off of Legion of Doom in MWF. Mike became the leader of JMJ and then he defeated the leader of ROD, Human who was the MWF World Heavyweight Champion.

Later, he defeated Human to get the MWF World Heavyweight Championship and then JMJ kept on defeating challengers for their MWF World Tag Team Championship. They became the longest reigning MWF World Tag Team Champions for 8 months and 2 weeks.

They lost their World Tag Team Championship to Region of Doom members Daddy Pops and Orville Riger. They got their second Tag Team title by defeating ROD.

[edit] 2000

In 2000, an I Quit Match was held between JMJ and ROD for the World Tag Team Championship which JMJ lost and then they left for OWF. They captured the OWF World Tag Team Championship but they got a bad attitude over the OWF roster and then they were stripped of the title and they were fired from OWF.

They returned to their home promotion DWF where they were managed as Manager Devil Ricas as they were managed by Ricas in DWF. They defeated many wrestlers and became popular once again and were known in DWF as Night Deaths. They kept on defeating several wrestlers and gaining victories.

Jack became the DWF World Heavyweight Champion and defeated many challengers and he survived battle royals and many brutal matches.

[edit] 2001

In 2001, Night Deaths were joined by Jeffrey Evans. Evans captured the DWF World Tag Team Championship with Johnny and they all together got the 50% shares of the DWF.
