Kerry Joseph Rage (March 24, 1975 - November 18, 1998) was an American professional wrestler who was the fifth son of the legendary wrestler and the mixed martial artist, the late Donald Rage (1942-2007). He is best known for standing 8 ft 0 inch tall and weighing from 400 to 507 pounds.

[edit] Early life

Kerry was very tall in his childhood who stood 5 ft 0 in at the age of 10. His height increased and he weighed 364 pounds during that time which was his natural problem.

[edit] Professional wrestling (1994-1998)

[edit] United States Wrestling Association (1994-1995)

[edit] Starting off

Kerry started wrestling for the United States Wrestling Association in 1994 at the age of 19. He started his career feuding with Brian Christopher and then he defeated Buddy Landel in a match. After that, he started wrestling for the USWA Heavyweight Championship.

He feuded with the then champion Brian Christopher and became a very successful wrestler. He defeated Christopher on several occassions in non-title matches.

[edit] The Defeat Of Tommy Rich

Kerry defeated Tommy Rich on August 15, 1994 to become the #1 Contender for the Southern Heavyweight Championship. Kerry had a match for the Southern title but thanks to Rich who made Kerry lose the match. This defeat of Tommy Rich lead Kerry to a feud and this heaviest wrestler in USWA weighing 400 pounds got defeated by Rich.

He started feuding with Rich and they both had a very long rivalry. Kerry later increased his weight to 507 pounds because of his rivalry and then he defeated Rich on November 8, 1994 and then he went to compete for the Unified world title.

[edit] World Title Match

Kerry defeated Billy Jack Haynes on January 23, 1995 to become the #1 Contender for the Unified world title of USWA. He wrestled with Jerry "The King" Lawler for the USWA title on February 2, 1995 but he lost the match.

[edit] Defeat of Colorado Kid

Kerry felt that he was the true world's champion. He defeated Colorado Kid on February 24, 1995 and then feuded with Colorado Kid and was a heavyweight and a giant wrestler to wrestle with Colorado standing 8 ft 0 in.

[edit] Smoky Mountain Wrestling (1995)

Kerry had also a brief run in Smoky Mountain Wrestling after he left AWA. He wrestled in SMW as Kerry the Great because of his height. He debuted in SMW on Friday Night pay-per-view held on April 8, 1995 where he defeated Cactus Jack and had a great defeat.

[edit] SMW Television Champion

Kerry became the SMW "Beat The Champ" Television Champion by defeating Bobby Blaze who was in his second reign on Summer Blast 1995.

Kerry defended it against Blaze several times and then both wrestlers feuded for the TV title and had a final match for the title on Halloween Scream where Kerry was defeated by Blaze and Kerry lost his TV title to Blaze.

[edit] United States Wrestling Association (1995-1996)

[edit] USWA Television Champion

Kerry returned to USWA feuding with Wolfie D and defeated Wolfie on several occassions. By 1996, he wrestled as Tony Falk and put his eyes on the USWA Television Championship and feuded with the then-TV champion Jesse James Armstrong.

Tony defeated Armstrong on May 4, 1996 in Memphis, Tony defeated Armstrong to become the TV champion but lost the title to Armstrong on May 19, 1996. It was a loser leaves town match and then Tony had to quit USWA.

[edit] Extreme Championship Wrestling (1996)

[edit] Quest for ECW Television Championship

After leaving USWA, Kerry wrestled in Extreme Championship Wrestling as Kerry the Giant who was a super-heavyweight and was considered to be the tallest and the heaviest wrestler in ECW. He feuded with Shane Douglas for the ECW Television Championship who was then champion.

[edit] World Championship Wrestling (1996-1997)

Kerry started wrestling in World Championship Wrestling as a heel who feuded with Hulk Hogan for the WCW Championship in 1996. He later feuded with "Macho Man" Randy Savage in 1997.

[edit] World Wrestling Federation (1997-1998)

Kerry debuted in World Wrestling Federation in 1997 and feuded wtih Goldust. He later defeated Steve Austin to become the #1 Contender for the WWF Championship. He defeated Bret Hart and then feuded with Triple H in 1998.

[edit] AWA Superstars of Wrestling (1998)

Kerry later joined in for AWA Superstars of Wrestling in 1998 and then he defeated Jonnie Stewart to debut in AWA. He later had his final match in AWA against Jonnie Stewart which he lost.

[edit] Death

On November 18, 1998, Kerry was hospitalized because of his height and weight and he was in a very bad condition. He was said to take rest and after that, he didn't get up. Doctors revealed that Kerry died because he was too tall and weighed till 507 pounds and died at the age 23.
