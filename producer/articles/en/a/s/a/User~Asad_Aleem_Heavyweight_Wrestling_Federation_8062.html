Heavyweight Wrestling Federation is an American professional wrestling promotion which was founded in 1998. It was the third promotion to use the acronym NWA.

[edit] History

This promotion was founded by the legendary wrestler Donald Rage in 1998 as Donald Rage Wrestling or most notably DRW. It was based in London. The promotion was having many popular wrestlers such as Cyclinder, House of Dining, Mark Knight and The Deathtaker.

The promotion later changed its name as National Wrestling Arena in 2001 because of its arena was a modern arena. The promotion later changed its name to New York Wrestling Corporation when it was shifted to New York in 2004. In 2006, the name of the promotion was World Heavyweight Wrestling which was used until August 2006 and then the promotion was renamed as Heavyweight Wrestling Federation.
