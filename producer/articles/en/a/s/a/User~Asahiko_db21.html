[edit] Name

A photo of me, as seen from 17 light years away.
See also: tanabata

朝 means "morning". The Japanese name for Altair is 彦星.

Put it together and you get 朝彦. Asahiko. Morning Altair.

Not that you can actually see Altair in the morning, though.

Note: This is only a wordplay!
