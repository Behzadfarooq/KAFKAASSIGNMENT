Asamati

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Asamāti (á-sāmaati "having no equal, unparalleled") is a minor deity invoked in RV 10.60. According ot the Anukramani, it is also the name of a king, with the patronymic Râthaprośṭha.
