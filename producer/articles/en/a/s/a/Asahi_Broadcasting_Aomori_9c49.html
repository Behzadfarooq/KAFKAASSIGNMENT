Asahi Broadcasting Aomori

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Asahi Broadcasting Aomori Co.,Ltd. (青森朝日放送株式会社, Aomori Asahi Hōsō Kabushiki-gaisha?), also known as ABA, is a Japanese broadcast network affiliated with the ANN. Their headquarters are located in Aomori Prefecture.

[edit] History

  • 1991 October 1 It was set up third broadcasting station of Aomori Prefecture.
  • 2006 July 1 Digital terrestrial television was started(Aomori main station).
