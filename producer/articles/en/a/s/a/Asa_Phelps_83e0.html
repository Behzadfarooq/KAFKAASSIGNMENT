Asa Phelps is a fictional character that appears in an episode of the animated television series The Simpsons. Asa Phelps was a member of a fictional unit of American soldiers known as "The Flying Hellfish" during World War II. Abe Simpson, also a member of the Flying Hellfish, receives a letter towards the beginning of the episode (entitled "Raging Abe Simpson and His Grumbling Grandson in "The Curse of the Flying Hellfish"") that simply contains the sentence "Asa Phelps has died." The significance of this message comes from the fact that the last surviving member of the Flying Hellfish is entitled to a cache of priceless paintings confiscated during the war. Upon the death of Asa Phelps, Abe Simpson and C. Montgomery Burns are the only two members of the Flying Hellfish who remain alive. This is the set up for the plot of the episode.

  • "Asa Phelps is Dead" is the title of the fourth track from the album Ghost Stories by Chicago punk band The Lawrence Arms.
