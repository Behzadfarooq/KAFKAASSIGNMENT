Asad Chowdhury is a prominent poet, writer, translator, radio and TV personality, journalist as well as an cultural activist in Bangladesh. He is Master in Bengali ( 1964 ) from Dhaka university. He was born in 11 February, 1943 in Ulania, Bangladesh. He is the father of three sons and one daughter.

Professionally Mr. Asad is a former Director : Bangla Academy, Dhaka and worked as editor in Bengali service of Deutsche Welle after retiriment. He started his profession as a lecturer in Bengali language and literature Brahmanbaria College ( 1973). During the libiration war of bangladesh Mr. Asad was a contributor/ broadcaster of Swadhin Bangla Betar Kendra in Kalkata 1971. However he played role as assistant editor in “the Joybangla” (Kalkata 1971) and in “the Daily Janapada” ( Dhaka, February-March 1973 ). Correspondent, The daily Purbodesh (For Brahmanbaria) 1968 to March 1971.
