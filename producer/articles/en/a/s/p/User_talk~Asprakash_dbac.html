[edit] Anti-Hindi agitations

Vanakkam Asprakash,

I'm Zojoji. I'm a Thamizhan, and I've been adding stuff to the article on Anti-Hindi agitations, such as adding link to Tamil Tribune. While I'm happy that you have taken this important issue and put it on Wikipedia, it's not good to have opinionated comments like "The rat should be our national animal, the crow should be our national bird". Wikipedia is an encyclopedia, and there's no place for emotional comments there. What we should do is to present the facts on Anti-Hindi agitations, and let the reader form his or her own opinions. If we put POV (point of view) comments, administrators may block the page, and we won't be able to communicate the facts of the Anti-Hindi agitations, and that would be bad.

Nanri, Zojoji 03:47, 5 August 2006 (UTC)

Vanakkam,

Thank you a lot for your comments. Please be informed that this Crow story was told by Annadurai in 1962 in the parliament of India and this is not my personal comments

A S Prakash

Zojoji. That rat story is a quote which has been recorded in the parliamentary records and hence there is nothing wrong in reproducing that 'in toto'Doctor Bruno 17:59, 12 August 2006 (UTC)
