Aspatria Railway Station is a train station serving the village of Aspatria in Cumbria, England. It is a request stop on part of the scenic Cumbrian Coast Line. Some through trains to the Furness Line and to Sunderland stop here. It is operated by Northern Rail who provide all passenger train services.
