Aspen-Pitkin County Airport (IATA: ASE, ICAO: KASE), also known as Sardy Field, is a public airport located three miles (5 km) northwest of the central business district (CBD) of Aspen, a city in Pitkin County, Colorado, USA. The airport covers 573 acres and has a single runway.
