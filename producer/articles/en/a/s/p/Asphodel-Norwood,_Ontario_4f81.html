Asphodel-Norwood (2006 population 4,247) is a township in central-eastern Ontario, Canada in Peterborough County. It is located on the Trent-Severn Waterway.

According to the 2006 Statistics Canada Census:

  • Population: 4,247
  • % Change (2001 – 2006): 6.6
  • Dwellings: 2,077
  • Area (km².): 160.85
  • Density (persons per km².): 26.4
