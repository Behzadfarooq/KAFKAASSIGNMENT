[edit] Lachesis Falling

I'm sorry to hear that you're taking the deletion discussion for Lachesis Falling so hard. Please understand that Wikipedia's "prod" and "afd" deletion process is not driven by one user; on the contrary, both the prod tag (which I originally added), and the AfD deletion listing is designed to generate discussion by multiple editors to determine whether an article should stay or go. The idea is to hopefully attract a wide range of regular WP editors who may have knowledge on the topic that the nominator (in this case, myself) does not.

Notability isn't an exact science, but there are agreed upon guidelines. The one I referenced is WP:WEB (<-- that's a clickable link, btw), but you can also see WP:NOTABILITY for other guidelines.

The difference between Rose Colored Glass and Lachesis Falling is that RCG was at one point quite notable, whereas it appears that LF is yet to be notable. If after perusing the guidelines you feel that LF is truly notable, however, feel free to chime in at the discussion (here), citing appropriate links or other evidence of notability.

Again, please try not to take this too personally. Many a Wikipedian has started off by creating an article about themselves or their website, only to have it deleted. Regardless of the outcome of this AfD discussion, I hope you decide to stay and make other contributions to Wikipedia. Please let me know if you have any other questions or concerns --AbsolutDan (talk) 05:04, 16 June 2006 (UTC)
