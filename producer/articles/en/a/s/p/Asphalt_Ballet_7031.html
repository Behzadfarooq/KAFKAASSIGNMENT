Asphalt Ballet

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Asphalt Ballet was a blues influenced hard rock band from San Diego, California. They formed in 1988 and parted ways shortly after the relase of their 1993 album Pigs. After unsuccessfully trying to achieve commercial success with their third album by adapting their sound to the changing music scene of the early 1990s, the group split.


[edit] Band members

  • Gary Jeffries (vocals) - left in 1991, replaced by Tommy Dean
  • Danny Clarke (guitar)
  • Julius J. Ulrich (guitar)
  • Terry Phillips (bass guitar)
  • Mikki Kiner (drums)
