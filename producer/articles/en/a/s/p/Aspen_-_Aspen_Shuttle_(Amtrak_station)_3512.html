Aspen - Aspen Shuttle (Amtrak station)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

A shuttle service to Aspen, Colorado is available to Amtrak passengers arriving at Glenwood Springs (Amtrak station). The service uses minivans and will bring passengers to and from lodging anywhere in Aspen. The Amtrak station code for the shuttle is ASX.
