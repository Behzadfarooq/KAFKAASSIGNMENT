Aso National Park
阿蘇くじゅう国立公園
IUCN Category II (National Park)
Location: Kyūshū, Japan
Area: 72,678 ha
Established: December 4, 1934

Aso Kujū National Park (阿蘇くじゅう国立公園, Aso Kujū Kokuritsu Kōen?) is a national park on the island of Kyūshū in Japan.
