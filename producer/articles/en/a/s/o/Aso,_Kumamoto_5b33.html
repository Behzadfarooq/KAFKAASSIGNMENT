Aso (阿蘇市; -shi) is a city located in Kumamoto Prefecture, Japan.

The city was formed on February 11, 2005 from the merger of the former town of Aso with the municipalities of Ichinomiya and Namino from Aso District.

As of October 2005, the city has an estimated population of 30,150 and a density of 91.95 persons per km². The total area is 376.25 km².
