The Asociación Guías Scouts del Paraguay (roughly Guide Scout Association of Paraguay) is the national Guiding organization of Paraguay. It serves 445 members (as of 2003). Founded in 1923, the coeducational organization became an associate member of the World Association of Girl Guides and Girl Scouts in 1966 and a full member in 1978.
