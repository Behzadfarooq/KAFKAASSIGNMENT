JJ Urquiza are a football club from Loma Hermosa in Buenos Aires Province, Argentina. They currently play in Primera C Metropolitana, which is the regionalised fourth tier of the Argentine Football Association league system.

The club are named in honour of Justo José de Urquiza, Argentine general and president of the Argentine Confederation (1854-1860).
