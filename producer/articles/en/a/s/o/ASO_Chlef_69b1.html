ASO Chlef (Association Sportive Olympique de Chlef) (Arabic: جمعية أولمبي الشلف) is an Algerian soccer club based in Chlef, founded in 1947. The club colours are red and white. Their venue, Boumezrag Mohamed Stadium has a capacity of some 30,000.

[edit] Achievements

  • Algerian Cup: 1
2005
