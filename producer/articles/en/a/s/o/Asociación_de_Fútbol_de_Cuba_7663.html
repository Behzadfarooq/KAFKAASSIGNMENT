Asociación de Fútbol de Cuba

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia
Founded 1924
FIFA affiliation 1932
CONCACAF affiliation 1961
President
Luis Hernández
Coach
González Triana

The Asociación de Fútbol de Cuba is the official football (soccer) organization in Cuba and is in charge of the Cuban national team.
