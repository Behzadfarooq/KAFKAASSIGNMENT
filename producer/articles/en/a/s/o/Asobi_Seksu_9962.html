Asobi Seksu is a shoegazing rock band based in New York City with heavy elements of pop music.

Singer Yuki Chikudate often incorporates Japanese lyrics into her songs.

The band's name in Japanese consists of two words, "asobi" (遊び) meaning "play" or "for fun", and "sekkusu" (セックス), the Japanese imported word referring to "sex" as in sexual intercourse.
