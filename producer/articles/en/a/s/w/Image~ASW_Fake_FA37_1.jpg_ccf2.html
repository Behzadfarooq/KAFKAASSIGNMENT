[edit] Summary

F/A-37 Talon during the shooting the film Stealth (2005). Caption provided by the U.S. Navy:

040618-N-8497H-031 Pacific Ocean (June 18, 2004) – Sailors assigned to the Air Department division aboard the Nimitz-class aircraft carrier USS Abraham Lincoln (CVN 72), move a fictitious jet aircraft during recent filming of the motion picture "Stealth.” "Stealth," starring Jessica Biel, Josh Lucas and Jamie Foxx, is due to be released next summer. Lincoln is conducting local operations in preparation for an upcoming scheduled deployment after 10 months of dry docked Planned Incremental Availability (PIA). U.S. Navy photo by Photographer’s Mate 3rd Class Tyler J. Clements (RELEASED)
