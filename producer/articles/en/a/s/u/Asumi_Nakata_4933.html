Asumi Nakata

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Asumi Nakata (中田あすみ, Nakata Asumi?, born April 5, 1988) is a female seiyū and a Japanese child model who was born in Tokyo, Japan. She is 173cm (5 feet and 8.1 inches) tall.

She belonged to the talent agency 'Crayon', and after that to 'Five Eight'. At present, she belongs to 'Switch 58'.

She used to called "Asumi Nakada" on TV, for instance, on the TV program 'Tensai Terebi-kun MAX'. But now she is called "Asumi Nakata".

[edit] Career

When she was a three-year-old girl, she started to show business with her younger sister. At her age of nine, she debuted as a singer of the duo 'crybaby' (Pony Canyon, produced by Seiji Toda) with Ayano Shiraishi in 1997.

She performed as voice actress for the first time in Japanese anime Mermaid Melody Pichi Pichi Pitch series that broadcasted from April 2003 to December 2004. In two of the anime series, she role-played the heroine Lucia Nanami.

In August 14 of 2005, she was invited to the Sixth Manga Fair (漫畫博覽會) in Taiwan. She sang a theme song of Mermaid Melody Pichi Pichi Pitch "Legend of Mermaid" there.

She also role-plays Yusura in Japanese anime series Himawari! that broadcasted from April 2006.
