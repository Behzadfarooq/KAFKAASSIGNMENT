Asukai Gayu

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

This is a Japanese name; the family name is Asukai.

Asukai Gayu also known as Asukai Masaari (飛鳥井雅有, asukai gayū/masaari?, 1241 – 1301) was a Kamakura period nobleman and poet. He lived in Kamakura and occupied a high position in the Shogunate (幕府, bakufu?).

Eighty six of his poems are represented in the official collection Zokukokinshū (俗古金集, Zokukokinshū?). He also has a personal collection, The Woman Next Door (隣女和歌集, rinjo wakashū?).
