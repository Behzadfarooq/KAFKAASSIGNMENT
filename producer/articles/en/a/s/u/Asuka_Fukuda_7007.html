Asuka Fukuda (福田 明日香, Fukuda Asuka?, born December 17, 1984) is a Japanese singer, and she was a member of Morning Musume until April 18, 1999. Her nickname is Asukacchi.

Asuka was born in Tokyo. She joined with Yuko Nakazawa, Aya Ishiguro, Natsumi Abe and Kaori Iida back in 1997. Although she was the youngest member at that time, she was the first to graduate in April of 1999. She left to continue her schooling and rumors of a return to a musical career have not been realized.

Her current job (as of July 2005) is working as an assistant at her father's night club, Philippine, in Tokyo.
