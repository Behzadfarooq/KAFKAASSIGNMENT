Location of Asuni in Italy

Asuni is a comune (municipality) in the Province of Oristano in the Italian region Sardinia, located about 70 km north of Cagliari and about 30 km east of Oristano. As of 31 December 2004, it had a population of 416 and an area of 21.2 km².[1]

Asuni borders the following municipalities: Laconi, Ruinas, Samugheo, Senis, Villa Sant'Antonio.
