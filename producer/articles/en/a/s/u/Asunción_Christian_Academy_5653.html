Asunción Christian Academy

Asunción Christian Academy is an American Christian school in Asunción, Paraguay. It offers a complete US based program from grades Pre-K through 12 which is accredited by two different associations. All classes are taught in English. Students in the High School must earn 24 Carnegie Credits to graduate.

The school also offers a Spanish language program which is recognized by the Paraguayan Ministry of Education. The Spanish program is optional and is available after school. It currently consists of 180 students. The program includes Spanish language and grammar, Guaraní (Paraguay's second official language), and Paraguayan History and Geography.
