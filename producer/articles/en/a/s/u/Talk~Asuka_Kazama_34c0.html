[edit] Regarding 12/24 Edits

To whomever edited out half of the page:

The Tekken series does not have many solid "facts" about characters, per se. Much of its storyline (such as the Devil Gene entry, is based upon observations and guesswork made by fans of the series. As Asuka has only been in one game (and one revision), her parallelism to Jun Kazama cannot have many facts to support their theories, thus making it possibly theory rather than simple fancruft. However, at the same time, this is true for half of the cast. I don't see the point in editing out all of that information and then rewording it with your own text. King Zeal 22:31, 25 December 2005 (UTC)

I originally made the change from Miko uniform to Aikido uniform because her bio makes no mention of her being a miko, nor does it state that her family runs or is associated with a Shinto temple. Also, her alternate custom does not bare the traditional colors of a miko (white and red), where as it does for an Aikido uniform (white and black). Also, if you go the Tekken Official website and look up her bio (http://www.tekken-official.jp/tekkenps2/characters/asuka_e.html) when you scroll through the story data scenes you'll notice that the practitioners of her family's dojo are dressed in Aikido uniforms like her alternate costume. - Raishin

I personally don't see why it's a big deal. I added the Miko note in the first place because the two uniforms are rather similar. The hakama page even notes that traditional Aikido and Miko uniforms are nearly identical aside from color. In fact, it also states that the hakama is RARELY worn for the purposes of Aikido. But, since you were technically correct, I didn't edit out your change. I just added to it. Basically, I don't see what it'll hurt providing informtion on both. King Zeal 01:25, 26 August 2005 (UTC)
Hmmm, well, when you put it like that, I guess it wouldn't hurt to put info on both. No worries then. - Raishin
