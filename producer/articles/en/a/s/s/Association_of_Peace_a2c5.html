An Association of Peace is an official grouping of conservative laestadians. In Europe there are 214 Associations of Peace: 199 in Finland, eight in Russia, five in Sweden, one in Norway and one in Estonia. There are also three central organizations: SRK in Finland, SFC in Sweden and Eesti luterlik rahuühendus in Estonia. The Laestadian Lutheran Church does mission work worldwide in collaboration with Association of Peace organizations.
