Lube Banca Marche Macerata is a professional Volleyball team of Associazione Sportiva Volley Lube based in Macerata, Italy. It plays in Italian Volleyball League.

[edit] Achievements

  • Italian Volleyball League: 1
  • Italian Volleyball Cup: 2
  • Italian Volleyball SuperCup: 1
  • CEV Champions League: 1
  • CEV Cup: 3
