[edit] Article naming

Please be careful in naming articles for this TV network, as ABC can mean "American Broadcasting Company", "Australian Broadcasting Corporation" and many more.

Also, don't use "ABC" per se, because in this case, "ABC" has a meaning, that is "Associated Broadcasting Company". You can use acnomyns as article names if the acronyms don't mean a thing, such as CBS and KFC (they've both dropped the meaning of their acronyms and go by with their TLAs. Thanks. --Howard the Duck 13:14, 16 August 2006 (UTC)
