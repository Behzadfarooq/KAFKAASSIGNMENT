Associação Atlética Coruripe, usually known simply as Coruripe, is a Brazilian football team, from the city of Coruripe, Alagoas state.

[edit] History

On March 1, 2003, Associação Atlética Coruripe was founded.

In 2003, the club won its first title, the Campeonato Alagoano Second Division, after beating Sete de Setembro 3-1, at Gerson Amaral Stadium. Coruripe was also promoted to the following year's first division.

In 2004, the club was Campeonato Alagoano's runner-up. In the final the club was defeated by Corinthians Alagoano. On May 5, Coruripe won the first leg 3-2, but on May 9 the club was defeated 2-0. [1] The club disputed the Campeonato Brasileiro Série C for the first time in that year. Coruripe, however, finished in the third position, being eliminated in that stage. The club finished only ahead of Corinthians Alagoano, but behind of Porto (PE) and Itacuruba. [2]

In 2005, the club was again Campeonato Alagoano's runner-up. The club was defeated by ASA, which won both stages of the competition (the first stage, called Copa Maceió, and the second stage, named Copa Alagoas). [3] Coruripe has also disputed the Copa do Brasil in that year. However, the club was eliminated in the first round by Fortaleza. [4] Coruripe has also disputed that year's Campeonato Brasileiro Série C. The club reached the competition's third stage. In the first stage, the team finished in the first place, ahead of América de Natal, Nacional de Patos and Vitória (PE). In the second stage, Coruripe beat Icasa. In the third stage the club was eliminated by América de Natal. [5]

2006 Coruripe's home and away kits.

In 2006, Coruripe won the Campeonato Alagoano. In the final the club beat CSA. The club won the first leg 1-0 [6], but was defeated 1-0 in the second leg. In the penalty shootout the club won 6-5. [7]

[edit] Titles

  • Campeonato Alagoano: 2006
  • Campeonato Alagoano Second Division: 2003
  • Torneio Início do Alagoano: 2004

[edit] Stadium

The club's home matches are usually played at Gerson Amaral stadium, which has a maximum capacity of 2,000 people.

[edit] Club colors

The club's colors are green, blue and yellow. [8]

[edit] Mascot

Coruripe's mascot is Marvel Comics super-hero The Incredible Hulk. [9]
