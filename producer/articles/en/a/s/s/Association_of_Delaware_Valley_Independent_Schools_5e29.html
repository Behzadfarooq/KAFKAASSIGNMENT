The Association of Delaware Valley Independent Schools (ADVIS) is a voluntary, non-profit consortium of independent schools in the Delaware Valley area. With headquarters in Bryn Mawr, Pennsylvania, the Association currently has 134 members located throughout eastern Pennsylvania, northern Delaware, and central and southern New Jersey.

Working together, ADVIS members promote independent education and strive to make their schools accessible to families of all backgrounds, regardless of income, religion, or ethnicity. The Association's mission is to "support and strengthen the administrative and financial activities of independent member schools by providing professional development, cooperative efforts, and inter-school collaboration." [1]
