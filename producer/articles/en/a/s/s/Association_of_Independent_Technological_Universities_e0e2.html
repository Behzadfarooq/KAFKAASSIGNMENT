Association of Independent Technological Universities

You have new messages (last change).

The Association of Independent Technological Universities is a group of independent American engineering colleges. The purpose of the association is to further the needs of these independent technology schools.

Current Members:

  • California Institute of Technology
  • Carnegie Mellon University
  • Case Western Reserve University
  • Clarkson University
  • Cooper Union for the Advancement of Science and Art
  • Drexel University
  • Embry-Riddle Aeronautical University
  • Franklin W. Olin College of Engineering
  • Harvey Mudd College
  • Illinois Institute of Technology
  • Kettering University
  • Massachusetts Institute of Technology
  • Milwaukee School of Engineering
  • Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute
  • Rochester Institute of Technology
  • Rose-Hulman Institute of Technology
  • Stevens Institute of Technology
  • Webb Institute
  • Worcester Polytechnic Institute
