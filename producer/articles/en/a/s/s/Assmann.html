Assmann

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Assmann is the surname of:

  • Aleida Assmann (b. 1947), German Egyptologist and linguist, see de:Aleida Assmann
  • Arno Assmann (1908-1979), a German actor and film director
  • Hans Assmann (1923-1998), an alleged KGB spy
  • Hans Erasmus Aßmann, Freiherr von Abschatz (1646–1699), German poet and translator
  • Jan Assmann (b. 1938), German Egyptologist, see de:Jan Assmann
  • Johannes Maria Assmann (1833-1903), catholic priest, see de:Johannes Maria Assmann
  • Richard Aßmann (1845–1918), German meteorologist, see de:Richard Aßmann

[edit] Origin

Aßman or alternatively spelled Assmann is a German language surname derived from the name Erasmus.
