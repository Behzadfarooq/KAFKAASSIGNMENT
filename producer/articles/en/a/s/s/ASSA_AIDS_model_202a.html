ASSA AIDS model

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The ASSA AIDS models are a series of mathematical models developed to assist the actuarial profession and the Actuarial Society of South Africa in assessing and addressing the impact of the AIDS epidemic in South Africa.

The models have been developed by the AIDS Committee of the society and the Centre for Actuarial Research (CARE) at the University of Cape Town.

[edit] Models

The models released are:

  • ASSA500 model (1996)
  • ASSA600 model (1998)
  • ASSA2000 suite of models (2002/3)
  • ASSA2002 suite of models (2004)
  • ASSA2003 suite of models (2005)
