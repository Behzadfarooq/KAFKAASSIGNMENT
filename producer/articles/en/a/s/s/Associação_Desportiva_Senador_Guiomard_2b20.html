Associação Desportiva Senador Guiomard or simply ADESG is a Brazilian football team from the municipality of Senador Guiomard in the state of Acre. The club was founded on the 26 January 1982. The team's home kit is a black and white vertical striped shirt, black shorts and black socks. ADESG's mascot is a lion and they play their home games at the Estádio Naborzão which has a capacity of 2,000. The team is currently playing in the Campeonato Acreano and they are the current holders of the championship having won the title for the first time in their history in 2006.
