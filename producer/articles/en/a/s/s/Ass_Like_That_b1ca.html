"Ass Like That" (Edited version referred to as "A** Like That", "Like That", or "Butt Like That") is a song by the rapper Eminem, released in 2005 as a single. It was from his worldwide hit album, Encore, which was released in 2004.

Eminem puts on an accent for this song, fantasising about females with an "ass like that", making his "pee pee/Slinky" go "duh do-ing do-ing do-ing" which is a reference to erection, but also trying to be innocent with the police. The females include Beyoncé (Oww B),Jessica Simpson, Mary-Kate and Ashley Olsen, Britney Spears, Hilary Duff, JoJo (who was only thirteen at the time the song was recorded), Janet Jackson and Gwen Stefani. The song also contains lyrics which mock R. Kelly, Pee-Wee Herman, Michael Jackson, and Arnold Schwarzenegger. Dr. Dre makes a very brief and uncredited appearance at the end of the song, asking Eminem, "What the fuck is wrong with you?!". Eminem responds with "AH!"

In the music video, recorded and released in 2005, he is mocked by "Triumph the Puppet Dog" (Triumph, the Insult Comic Dog), and it switches between the "real world" and the "puppet world". There are puppet versions of many of the celebrities Eminem makes fun of (listed above), as well as people like Nick Lachey.The puppets from Crank Yankers also make an appearance.
