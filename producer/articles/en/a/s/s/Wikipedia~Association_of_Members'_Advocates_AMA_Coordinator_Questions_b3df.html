[edit] What is an Advocate supposed to do?

I had an Advocate for a while but it did not seem very helpful. The Advocate did on a few occasions politely ask people to stop harassing me. However, this was inconsistant and as the Advocate never contacted me I usually learned about these interventions accidently and long after the fact. The reasoning behind the interventions was never explained to me (the policies, guidelines etc.) so I was not learning ways of taking care of myself. I asked for feedback on my own behavior but received none. The Advocate did not answer email, never initiated contact with me, never suggested or explained anything although I requested this as my primary objective in my original application and subsequently in email and post to my Advocate.

Are there Advocates who do interact with the persons they are advocating for? Or am I considered a "difficut case" since after I received my Advocate an "unbiased" Arbitrator was appointed (who had a history of supporting my attackers) and the first thing he did was file an ANI against me on behalf of my attackers labelled "Mattisse Redux", making me a joke. Am I too stigmatized to receive a helpful Advocate?

Is there another process (perhaps AMA is not the proper place) to get questions and honest feedback such as I asked for in my origninal AMA request? Or am I asking for the impossible at Wikipedia? (I cannot do it publically as I am usually stalked at places like the Techical Pump or my question is used against me a new ANI.) Thank you. Sincerely, Mattisse 20:04, 14 December 2006 (UTC)
