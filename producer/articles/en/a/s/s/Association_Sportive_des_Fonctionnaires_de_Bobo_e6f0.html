Association Sportive des Fonctionnaires de Bobo (ASF Bobo-Dioulasso) is a Burkinabé football club based in Bobo Dioulasso. They play their home games at the Stade Municipal.

The club plays in yellow and black. It was founded on 20 January 1948.

[edit] Achievements

  • Burkinabé Premier League: 2
1961, 1966
  • Coupe du Faso: 5
1986, 1989, 1997, 1998, 2004
  • Burkinabé Leaders Cup: 1
1992
  • Burkinabé SuperCup: 4
1992/93, 1996/97, 2000/01, 2003/04
