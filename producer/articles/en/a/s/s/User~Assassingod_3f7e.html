Hello, my name is Assassingod (Also known as Kazenone, previously FFmaster2). My real name is Steven, and I am a student of Computing and ICT (Computer Sciences). I have many interests, but my time revolves around playing Final Fantasy, programming PHP and watching Anime. I have been a web developer for almost 7-years, having learnt HTML, XHTML, CSS, PHP and MySQL in the process. I also have studied Visual Basic and Delphi.

I have a huge obsession with Final Fantasy. I have been a fan since 1997, when Final Fantasy VII was released in the U.K.. I have since then important many collectables from Japan related to Final Fantasy, and every game available for purchase. Words cannot express my devotion to Final Fantasy.

In my spare time, I like to watch Anime and play other games such as Age of Empires III, Nintendo DS and Shadow of the Colossus. I was also a moderator for the PHP-powered forum-software, vBulletin.
