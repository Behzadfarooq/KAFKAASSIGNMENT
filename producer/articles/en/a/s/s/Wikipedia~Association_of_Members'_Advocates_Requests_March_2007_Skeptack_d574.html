[edit] Summary:

User JonaWiki has added a section to the Star Wars Galaxies page, about me. First, I dispute the accuracy of the statement itself. I feel the user is speculating, posting conjectured conclusions, and even the quotes are taken out of context from several sources, rearranged, and patched-together in order to provide support for his point of view. I'm simply not important enough to have ever been quoted, or described in a reliable source. But he wants to include me based on his misinterpretation of a blog post and web forum postings by me or about me. Looking at his talk page, it seems this is normal behavior for him here. http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/User_talk:Jonawiki

This is what it says:

Former developer repudiates NGE Just as the NGE was about to launch, Star Wars Galaxies game designer Jeff Freeman, who played a limited role in the development of the NGE, took the initiative to provide an enthusiastic public endorsement of it on his personal blog but then retracted his posting as public outrage over the game changes erupted.[38][39][40] After leaving Sony Online Entertainment to work at MMORPG start-up Spacetime Studios[41], he posted on MMORPG.com that the NGE being successful "wasn't even really a close call", admitted that he was not "blameless" for the "mess I (and others... with supervision) made", agreed that he "made some poor choices and did some poor work" and criticized the behavior of Sony Online Entertainment in giving so little advanced notice of the NGE as "shitty".[42]
