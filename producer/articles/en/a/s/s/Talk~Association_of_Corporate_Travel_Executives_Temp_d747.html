The Association of Corporate Travel Executives (ACTE) is a not-for-profit professional association established by business travel leaders to provide executive-level global education and networking.

[edit] Membership

ACTE members are located in 49 countries and include corporate travel buyers, agencies and suppliers. ACTE's equal membership policy accords all sectors equal membership.[1]


[edit] Education, Training and Networking

ACTE provides Global and Regional education events that bring travel industry stakeholders into contact with industry experts and educators in an effort to identify and train attendees on emerging issues. Events include: Webcasts, PowerTalks, Executive Forums, and intensive ACTE Global or Regional Education Conferences. [2]

[edit] ACTE Corporate Social Responsibility Initiative

ACTE launched the first Low-Carbon Business Travel Conference to Raise Climate [3] ACTE, in its continuous efforts to reduce the carbon footprint of the business travel industry, selects Fairmont Hotel as venue for Canada Education Conference. [4]
