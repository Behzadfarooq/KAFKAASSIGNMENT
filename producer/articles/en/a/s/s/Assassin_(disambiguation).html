An assassin is a murderer who is politically motivated or contracted.

Assassin may also refer to

  • Hashshashin, the historical Muslim sect of Alamut
  • Hitman, a murderer who is motivated by money

In fiction:

  • Assassins (film), a 1995 film written by the Wachowski brothers
  • Assassins (musical), a 1991 musical by Stephen Sondheim and John Weidman
  • Assassins (novel), the sixth book of the Left Behind series by Jerry B. Jenkins and Tim Lahaye

In gaming:

  • Assassin (character class), a character class found in many roleplaying games
  • Assassin (Dungeons & Dragons), a prestige class in the Dungeons & Dragons roleplaying game
  • Assassin (game), a live-action role-playing game in which players assassinate each other with imitation weaponry

In music:

  • Assassins (album), a 1988 album by Into A Circle
  • Assassin (deejay), a dancehall deejay
  • Assassin (rap crew), a French rap crew
  • Assassin, a song by British band, Muse on the album Black Holes and Revelations

Other:

  • Reduviidae a large family of predatory insects including the assassin bugs
