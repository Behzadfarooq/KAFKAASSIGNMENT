Assault and Flattery is Toronto's sixth and final album, released in 1985. The album features only two original band members, Annie "Holly" Woods and Scott Kreyer, perhaps explaining its release as "Holly Woods and Toronto". The CD release includes the bonus track "Where Are We Now?"

  3. "Sometimes Change"

  7. "Assault and Flattery"
  8. "Cats & Dogs (Stealin')"
  9. "No More Cliches"
  10. "Where Are We Now?" *
