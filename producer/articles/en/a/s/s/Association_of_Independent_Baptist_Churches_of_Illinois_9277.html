The Association of Independent Baptist Churches of Illinois is a voluntary fellowship of independent Baptist churches in the U. S. state of Illinois. Headquarters is located in Paxton, Illinois. The association was composed of about 75 churches in 1994. The Baptist Messenger is their official publication.
