Association of British Lourdes Pilgrimage Hospitalités

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Association of British Lourdes Pilgrimage Hospitalités (ABLPH) is an association of many groups and Hospitalités that go to Lourdes from Britain. It was formed to share good practice, experience and fellowship between pilgrimage Hospitalités. It meets once a year over a February weekend to discuss issues of mutual concern, to encourage communication, and to meet with representatives of the Sanctuaries and the Hospitalité Notre Dame de Lourdes.
