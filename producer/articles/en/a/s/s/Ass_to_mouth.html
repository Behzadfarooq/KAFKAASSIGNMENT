[edit] Alternate terminology

It is also known as A2M, ATM, or Arse-to-Mouth. Ass to mouth generally excludes cleaning the penis or other object after its removal from the anus and before its insertion into the mouth for the sake of increased sexual excitement. A2OGM, for "ass to other girl's mouth", specifically describes the variant of the act where the penis is moved from one female's anus to another female partner's mouth. The term has been used since at least January 1995 and clearly defined since at least August 1996.[2] Despite its prevalence in pornographic films, it can be a health risk.[3][4]

[edit] Prevalence in pornographic videos

Ass to mouth became something of a fad around 2003 or 2004 in American pornographic videos.[5]

The release of a Heatwave/Horizon film simply titled "Ass to Mouth" in 2000 coincided with a growing focus on this act in mainstream pornography.[citation needed] Heatwave/Horizon debuted a second similar series, "A.T.M. Girls" later the same year. The act billed prominently in multiple films, and lead to the debut of several new series focused specifically on the act, such as "A2M" (2003, Anabolic) and "ATM Machine" (2003, Digital Sin). The original "Ass to Mouth" series from Heatwave/Horizon was suspended in 2005 after "Ass to Mouth #15", but the decline in niche films reflects the fact that this act has become part of the standard repertoire. Among the more prominent porn actors and actresses, Flower Tucci, Bobby Dryden, Olivia O'Lovely, Tony Livsey, Tory Lane, Shy Love, Brianna Banks, Tiffany Mynx, Sasha Grey and Jessica Darlin are also known for performing this fetish in their adult movies.

[edit] Health risks

The health risks of ass-to-mouth include the risks of both oral sex and anal-oral contact. Unprotected oral-genital contact involves the risk of contracting sexually transmitted diseases, such as genital herpes or intestinal parasites. The recipient of ass-to-mouth is also at risk of infection by Hepatitis A. It is unknown whether thoroughly washing the anal area and applying an enema results in reduced risk of disease. Porn-industry performers do use enemas prior to filming anal sex sequences; however, the purpose is to eliminate the possibility of any fecal matter appearing on film, rather than disease prevention.[6]
