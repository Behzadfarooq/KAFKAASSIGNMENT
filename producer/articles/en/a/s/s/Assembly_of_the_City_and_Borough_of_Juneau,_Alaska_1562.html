The Assembly of the City and Borough of Juneau, Alaska is the local government of Juneau, Alaska, United States, and the surrounding area. Headed by the mayor of Juneau, the assembly consists of nine members, elected in rotating tranches to three-year terms. Elections are held on the first Tuesday every October; the 2006 election was on October 3, 2006.
