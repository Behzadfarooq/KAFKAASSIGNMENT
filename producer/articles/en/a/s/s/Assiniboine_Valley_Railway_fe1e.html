Assiniboine Valley Railway is a private minimum gauge railway located in the Charleswood community near the Assiniboine Park in Winnipeg, Manitoba, Canada. It started construction in 1995 and has been in operation since the fall of 1996.[1] The owner of the railway is William Taylor. The railway is located on a private nine-acre property.

The railway is open to public during the second weekend of the month between June and October. In addition special Christmas Light Runs have been arranged.[2]

As of February 2007 the railway has six operating locomotives and 22 wagons, with three more wagons under construction.[3]
