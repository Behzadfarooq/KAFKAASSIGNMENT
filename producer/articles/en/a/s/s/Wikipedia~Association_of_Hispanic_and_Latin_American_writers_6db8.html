[edit] Purpose

The Association of Hispanic and Latin American writers main purpose is to have Hispanic or Latin American writers sharing assignments with one another. For example, if a Bolivian member asks AntonioMartin to writer about a Bolivian topic, AntonioMartin will ask that member to write about a Puerto Rican topic. This is to help expand knowledge about the Hispanic-Latin American race and culture as a whole, between members of the association.

Furthermore, the association hopes to become an example of Hispanic and Latin American unity to other Hispanics or Latin Americans who use the Internet.
