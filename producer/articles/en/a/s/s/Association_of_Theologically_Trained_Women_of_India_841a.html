Association of Theologically Trained Women of India (ATTWI)[1][2] is an association of Indian Women Theologians. It is an associated body[3] of the Senate of Serampore College (University) as well as an affiliate member[4] of the National Council of Churches in India (NCCI).

[edit] Membership

Membership is open to women who studied in colleges and seminaries affiliated to the Senate of Serampore College (University). Attwi members are spread across the globe.
