AssaultMecha is a tactical simulation game from Artix Entertainment that is currently in development. Though the project was declared officially cancelled, there is a chance the project may be revived. In a recent interview, Adam Bohn said that he wanted to continue work on AssaultMecha, describing the concept as "so over the top, old school anime style, non-stop destruction."[1]

As of August 14, 2002, it was listed on Artix Entertainment's teaser website as "currently under development" for the web browser and PocketPC. It appears to use robots similar to those of the BattleTech universe and portrays 3D graphics distinctly different from other flash-driven projects produced before by Artix Entertainment. Other than the teaser website, the only other mention of AssaultMecha is an equipment named AssaultMecha in AdventureQuest which allows the character to pilot a robot similar to the one on the teaser site.
