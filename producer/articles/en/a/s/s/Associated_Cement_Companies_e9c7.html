The Associated Cement Companies Limited(ACC), an Indian company in cement manufacturing business. It's registered office is called Cement House. It is located on Maharishi Karve Road, Mumbai. It is a blue chip company. The stock price of this company contributes in calculating BSE Sensex

The management control of the company was taken over by Swiss cement major Holcim in 2004. On 1st September 2006 the name of The Associated Cement Companies Limited was changed to ACC Limited. The company is the only cement company to get the Superbrand status in India.
