Associates Triangular Series in Kenya in 2006-07

The Associates Triangular Series in Kenya was a One-day International tournament involving the national teams of Canada, Kenya and Scotland, held in Mombasa. Each team played each other twice. The event took plave at the Mombasa Sports Club.

[edit] Schedule

  • 17 Jan: Kenya 328 for 5 (50 overs; David Obuya 73, Paul Hoffman 2/45) beat Scotland 138 (36.2 overs; John Blain 22, Nehemiah Odhiambo 3/25) by 190 runs.
  • 18 Jan: Scotland 293 for 8 (49.5 overs; Ryan Watson 123*, George Codrington 2/38) beat Canada 292 for 5 (50 overs; Qaiser Ali 70, Dougie Brown 2/73) by 2 wickets.
  • 20 Jan: Kenya beat Canada by walkover.
  • 21 Jan: Kenya 259 for 9 (50 overs; Ravindu Shah 113, Dougie Brown 3/37) beat Scotland 253 for 8 (50 overs; Fraser Watts 59, Steve Tikolo 4/41) by 6 runs.
  • 23 Jan: Scotland 209 for 8 (47.2 overs; Majid Haj 45, Sunil Dhaniram 2/24) beat Canada 208 (44.3 overs; Sunil Dhaniram 46, Craig Wright 4/29) by 2 wickets.
  • 24 Jan: Canada 213 for 9 (50 overs; Abdool Samad 50, Hiren Varaiya 3/36) beat Kenya 144 (35.1 overs, Ravindu Shah 48, Kevin Sandher 3/24) by 69 runs.
