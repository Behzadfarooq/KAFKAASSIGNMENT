Association of National Olympic Committees of Africa

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Association of National Olympic Committees of Africa (acronym: ANOCA; French: Association des Comités Nationaux Olympiques d'Afrique, ACNOA) is an international organization that united the 53 national Olympic committees (NOCs) of Africa.

It often assembles with other continental NOCs in the form of the Association of National Olympic Committees (ANOC).
