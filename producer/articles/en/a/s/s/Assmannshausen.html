Assmannshausen

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Assmannshausen is, since the incorporation in 1977, a quarter of the "Rüdesheim on the Rhine" in Germany. It has a lithium spring, baths and a Kurhaus, and is famed for its red wine (Assmannshauser), which resembles light Burgundy. From here a railway ascends the Niederwald.
