The Associated Public Schools of Victoria (APS) are a group of eleven independent schools in Victoria, Australia. Since 1900 the schools have been competing in sporting competitions, including the Head of the River rowing regattas, the APS Combined Swimming & Diving and the APS Athletics. The headquarters of the APS is situated on Morrison Street, Hawthorn, Victoria.

[edit] APS/AGSV Sport Competition

In 2001 the five co-educational Associated Public Schools of Victoria, together with the five co-educational Associated Grammar Schools of Victoria, combined, and now play the following sports on Saturdays at Senior, Intermediate and Junior levels.

  • Badminton
  • Basketball
  • Hockey
  • Netball
  • Soccer
  • Softball
  • Tennis
  • Volleyball
