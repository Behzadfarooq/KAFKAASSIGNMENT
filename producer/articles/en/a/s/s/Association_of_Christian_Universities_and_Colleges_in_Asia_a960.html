Association of Christian Universities and Colleges in Asia

The Association of Christian Universities and Colleges in Asia (ACUCA) is an organization of Christian universities and colleges in Asia, dedicated to Christian witness and service in the field of education. It consists of forty-eight institutions: three in Hong Kong, eight in Indonesia, ten in Japan, ten in Korea, seven in the Philippines, six in Taiwan and four in Thailand.
