Associated Bio-Engineers & Consultants, or ABEC, Inc., designs and manufactures bioreactors, fermentors, the control systems used to manage these in processes, and certain related equipment. The target customer base is the biopharmaceutical and specialty biochemicals manufacturing sector. ABEC is one of several biotech companies clustered in the Lehigh Valley area of Pennsylvania (2).

[edit] History

ABEC was founded in 1975 by Jack Wilson. He is one of 424 contributors to the Encyclopedia of Bioprocess Technology, listed as contributing in the area of fermentation design (1).
