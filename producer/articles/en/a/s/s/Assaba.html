Assaba

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Assaba (Arabic: ولاية العصابة) is a region in southern Mauritania. Its capital is Kifa. Other major cities/towns include Kankossa. The region border the Mauritanian regions of Brakna and Tagant to the north, the Mauritanian region of Hodh El Gharbi to the east, Mali to the south and the Mauritanian regions of Gorgol and Guidimaka to the west.

Departments of Assaba

Assaba is divided into 5 departments:

  • Aftout
  • Boumdeid
  • Guerou
  • Kankossa
  • Kiffa
