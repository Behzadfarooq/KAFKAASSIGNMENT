Assyrian Universal Alliance (Syriac: ܚܘܝܕܐ ܬܒ̣ܝܠܝܐ ܐܬܘܪܝܐ, Arabic: الاتحاد الأشوري العالمي; Farsi: اتحادیه جهانی آشوریها) is an ethnic Assyrian political party in Iraq. The Assyrian Universal Alliance is an international alliance made up of different sectors of the Assyrian federations and organizations throughout the world. [1] The Secretary General position is currently vacant after the resignation of Emanuel Kamber.

In 1968, the Assyrian Universal Alliance was created as a world-wide organisation seeking to spread, uphold and enhance the Assyrian name in the world, to secure the human rights of the Assyrian people in their homeland and to attain an autonomous state in the Assyrian ancestral homeland. In 1991, the Assyrian Universal Alliance became a Member of the UNPO, Unrepresented Nations and Peoples Organization. [2]

[edit] Sectors of the AUA

  • Assyrian American National Federation
  • Assyrian National Congress of Georgia
  • Assyrian National counsel of Iran
  • Assyrian Association of Armenia
  • Assyrian Australian National Federation
  • Assyrian Federation of Russia
