Association Sportive du Faso-Yennenga is a Burkinabé football club based in Ouagadougou. Their home games are played at Stade du 4-Août.

[edit] Achievements

  • Burkinabé Premier League: 8
1973, 1989, 1995, 1999, 2002, 2003, 2004, 2006
  • Coupe du Faso: 1
1991
  • Burkinabé Leaders Cup: 7
1989, 1990, 1994, 1995, 2000, 2001, 2002
  • Burkinabé SuperCup: 1
2001/02
