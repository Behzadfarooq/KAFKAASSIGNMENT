Ass Shaker/Justify/Face Down EP is an EP by The Red Jumpsuit Apparatus created about a year before their first major-label CD release Don't You Fake It.

[edit] Track Listing

  1. "Ass Shaker"
  2. "Home Improvement"
  3. "Love Seat"
  4. "Face Down"
  5. "Cat And Mouse"
  6. "Justify"
  7. "Kins And Carrol"
  8. "Angels Cry"
  9. "Twenty Hour Drive"
  10. "Disconnected"
  11. "The Acoustic Song"
  12. "Grim Goodbye"

[edit] Trivia

  • The first track, "Ass Shaker", is a slightly different, early recording of "In Fate's Hands".
  • The last track, "The Acoustic Song", is an acoustic version of "Your Guardian Angel".

[edit] Personnel

  • Ronnie Winter, lead vocalist
  • Elias Reidy, backing vocalist and guitarist
  • Duke Kitchens, backing vocalist and guitarist
  • Joey Westwood, backing vocalist and bassist
  • Jon Wilkes, drummer and backing vocalist
