The Association of Life-Giving Churches is an association of U.S. churches organized by the New Life Church founded by Ted Haggard. It is listed as a denomination by the U.S. National Association of Evangelicals[1] As of 2006, Harper's Magazine reported that it comprised some 300 congregations. [2]
