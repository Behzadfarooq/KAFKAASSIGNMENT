Assiniboine Regional Health Authority

Assiniboine Regional Health Authority is the governing body for healthcare regulation in an area of the Canadian province of Manitoba. The area region includes the communities of:

  • Baldur
  • Minnedosa
  • Birtle
  • Neepawa
  • Boissevain
  • Carberry
  • Rivers
  • Cartwright
  • Deloraine
  • Russell
  • Elkhorn
  • Erickson
  • Glenboro
  • Souris
  • Hamiota
  • Treherne
  • Hartney
  • Virden
  • Killarney
  • Melita
