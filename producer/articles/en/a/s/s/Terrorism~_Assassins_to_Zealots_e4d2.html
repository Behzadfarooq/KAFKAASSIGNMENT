Terrorism: Assassins to Zealots is a 2003 book by Sean Kendall Anderson and Stephen Sloan. It is a historical dictionary of groups, individuals, incidences, tactics, and ideas associated with terrorism.

It is a revised and updated, paperback version of the Historical Dictionary of Terrorism. The authors seek provide "a foundation for the understanding of what terrorism is, who practices it, and who is victimized by terrorist missions."

The book was published by The Scarecrow Press, Inc. (Lanham, Maryland) in 2003 as a 468-page paperback (ISBN 0-8108-4589-X).
