[edit] Overview

Assam Engineering College was established in 1955, the first under graduate engineering college in the North-Eastern India. It started with Civil Engineering discipline, and within a few years other departments came into existence. Currently, it provides Bachelor's Degree in eight different engineering traits and also provides Master's Degree in Computer Application, Civil Engineering and Industrial and Production Engineering. There is a scheme for Doctorate Degrees in Soil Mechanics & Hydraulics as well. It's annual festival called the "Pyrokinesis" attracts students from the entire region. With a well spread out lush green campus, AEC has been the hub centre of many intellectual and extra-curricular activities in Assam for almost half a century.

[edit] Location

Assam Engineering College is situated at Jalukbari in South-West Guwahati, which is a landmark place in the colourful and chequered history of Assam. Bounded by the Gauhati University on the north, the Deepor Beel on the south, the Government Ayurvedic College on the east, and the Assam Police Radio Organisation on the west, Assam Engineering College spells a magical impact on the visitors. Well connected to the major city centres by open and wide roads, the College campus is about 9 Km. away from the Guwahati Main Railway Station, 5 Km. from the Inter-State Bus Terminus and 8 Km. from Lokapriya Gopinath Bordoloi International Airport, Borjhar.

[edit] Hostels

In all there are 9 hostels of which 8 are boys hostel and 1 is a girls hostel. Each Hostel is self-contained with amenities such as a reading room, an indoor games room, a lounge and a dining hall with mess. All rooms are provided with internet access as well. The administrative head of each hostel, the Superintendent, is a senior faculty member. There are Hostel Committees at the Hostel level. The Hostel Committee of each hostel is composed of Superintendent, Block Monitors, Mess Secretaries, Sports Secretary, Cultural Secretary, Magazine Secretary, Welfare Secretary and Auditors. It decides the pattern and the pace of life of each Hostel.

[edit] Mechanical Engineering Department

The department of Mechanical Engineering, established in 1957 was one of the first departments of the college. It imparts B.E. courses of four years with an annual intake of 60 students and the student to teacher ratio of 5:2.

Equipped with adequate laboratory facilities, library and highly experienced and motivated faculty, this branch has produced engineers who have proved their potential to face varied challenges of the technical world.

Head of the Department:- Dr. Ranjit Kumar Dutta

[edit] Civil Engineering Department

The department of Civil Engineering, established in 1956, was the first branch introduced in this college. The department boasts of highly qualified faculty and a well-equipped labs for field work.

The annual intake capacity is 60 students per semester in B.E. degree course and 25 students in the M.E. degree course. This department also offers a Ph.D. degree in Soil Mechanics & Hydraulics. However, it has a variable intake. The student to teacher ratio for each semester is 3:1.

Head of the Department:- Dr. Bibha Das Saikia

[edit] Electrical Engineering Department

The Electrical Engineering Branch established its roots in Assam Engineering College in 1957 , as the second functioning branch.

The branch offers Bachelor of Engineering(B.E.) degree and has an annual intake of 60 students. Experienced professors and lecturers strengthen the department, which creates a healthy environment with student-teacher ratio of 5:1.

The curriculum of the department is mainly oriented to cater the needs of IT- Industry, Software Controlled Heavy and Light Electrical and Power Systems . Although no Post-graduate courses are offered at present , a proposal for the opening of two Post-graduate courses has been submitted viz. - Electric Energy Systems and Control & Instrumentation.

Head of the Department:- Dr. Damodar Agarwal

[edit] Electronics And Telecommunications Engineering Department

Established in 1984, it is the only department providing Bachelor of Engineering (B.E.) degree in Electronics & Telecommunication Engineering under the state government.

It has an annual intake of 30 students, and a highly qualified teaching faculty, the ratio being 5:1. The department offers syllabus covering the major thrust areas in the field of electronics and telecommunication. Regarding the achievements of our students, they have always been in the brighter side and we hope to be on a brighter side in the near future.

Head of the Department:- Prof. Dinesh Pegu

[edit] Computer Science And Engineering Department

The Department of Computer Science and Engineering at Assam Engineering College, Guwahati was established on August 25, 1998. Presently, the department is attached with the Electronics & Telecommunications Department.

The course available is Bachelor of Engineering (B.E.). The department has quality faculty members and a sophisticated computer centre. The duration of B.E. is 4 years having 8 semesters.

Head of the Department:- Prof. Dinesh Pegu

A view of Deepor Beel in the vicinity of AEC

[edit] Chemical Engineering Department

The Chemical Engineering Department was started in 1963 and has an annual intake capacity of 30 students in B. E. degree. The students to faculty ratio is 3:1 in each semester. This is the only department in the whole of North-east India offering an under-graduate course in chemical engineering. Recently the Gauhati University has recognized the laboratories of this department for research work in chemical engineering leading to a Ph.d degree under this University.

The department has been providing a four-year under-graduate course for Chemical Engineering. The core subjects includes Process Calculation, Chemical Technology, Chemical Engineering, Thermodynamics, Mass Transfer Operations, Chemical Reaction Engineering, Process Dynamics & Control, Petroleum Refinery Engineering & Petrochemicals, Process Equipment Design & Drawing, Fluid Flow Operation, Transport Phenomenon, Heat Transfer Operation, Process Utilities, Process Instrumentation.

Head of the Department:- Dr. Makhan Lal Das

[edit] Industrial And Production Engineering Department

The branch of Industrial and Production Engineering was newly introduced in 1998 due to high demand of production engineers in various oil sectors, companies etc. The branch was recognised to AICTE in 2001.

Head of the Department:-Dr. R.K.Dutta

[edit] Instrumentation Engineering Department

The branch of Instrumentation Engineering was newly introduced in 1998 due to high demand of instrumentation engineers in various industries, companies etc. The branch was recognised to AICTE in 2001.

Head of the Department:- Dr. D. Agarwal

[edit] Annual College Fest- Pyrokinesis

Pyrokinesis, the annual national level technical cum cultural festival of Assam Engineering College has curved a niche among all academic institutions of the north east in the last couple of years due to its unique composition and fostering of friendship among the participating institutions.


'Pyrokinesis kindles the creative essence of the versatile young mind providing a perfect platform to unite its ‘technical’ genius with the vibrant ‘cultural' quotient.'

Pyrokinesis is a word not found in the dictionary. Neither has it meant something substantial. If the word is broken into two, it may mean something like "moving fire" or "setting things ablaze".Just shows that AECians wants to put ablaze their creativity and talents.


The three day long event showcase the various technical and cultural extravaganza.

The events include:

1. Tecnokinesis (the technical section of the event)

Big-Gyan, the seminar,

Technocracy, the technical exhibition,

Wrangler, the debating contest,

Grey Cells, the quizzing contest,

Brainstorm, the school quiz,

Codewar, the software contest,

Skoar, the gaming contest,

Different Strokes, the painting contest,

Visual Impact, the photography contest.

2. The cultural section of the event includes

Mars and Venus, the ultimate personality contest

Pyrotechnix, the rock contest

Blue Suede Shoes, the DJ night

Sanskriti, a performance by school-children,

Iron Man, body-building contest,

Cynosure, the main event,

Dance Mania, DJ night.


Technokinesis the technical section of this fest, provides a common arena to all budding technocrats to prove their mettle in a combat of wits that ignites the intellect and mobilizes the inquisitive mind. It also gives the wannabe ‘techies’ an insight into the latest cutting–edge technology and contemporary path breaking research through various lecture-sessions, workshop and discussion forums conducted by the stalwarts in this field.
