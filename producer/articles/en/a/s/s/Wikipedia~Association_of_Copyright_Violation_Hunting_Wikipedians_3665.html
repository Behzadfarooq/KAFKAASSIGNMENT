Wikipedia:Association of Copyright Violation Hunting Wikipedians

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

[edit] Who We Are

Our visions and missions are simple.

Frankly, we are a Search and Destroy group. We sift through the miles and miles of Wikis to find articles that are in direct violation of a copyright. We are the snitches and narcs of Wikipedia; our reason for existing is to sniff out plagerized and content that hasn't been permitted by the holder of the copyright.
