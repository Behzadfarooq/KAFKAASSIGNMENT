Golgo 13: Kûron no kubi (or Golgo 13: Kolwoon Assignment) is a 1977 action movie starring Sonny Chiba as the international assassin Golgo 13. The movie was based on the popular manga series Golgo 13.

[edit] Plot

Spoiler warning: Plot and/or ending details follow.
Golgo and Smith Face Off

Golgo 13, Sonny Chiba, is given an assignment to track down and kill Hong Kong's most notorious crime boss. Throught the film he manages to stay one step ahead of Detective Smith (Callan Leung) and evade arrest. After the crime boss is killed (by a female assassin not Golgo) Golgo proceeds to track down his boss eventually shooting him from 600 yards away in a moving helicopter hanging from the side of a cliff.
