  • Answer:Yes

How would you describe the nature of this dispute? (policy violation, content dispute, personal attack, other)

  • Answer: Content Dispute and Personal Attack

What methods of Dispute Resolution have you tried so far? If you can, please provide wikilinks so that the Advocate looking over this case can see what you have done.

  • Answer:I was insulted two times. I see no avenue to resort to dispute resoltuon except Arbitration. Conduct of above-mentioned users and generally Armenia and Azerbaijani related pages are now being considered by Arbcom

  • Answer:Proper editing and punishment of users who constantly insult

[edit] Summary:

On page Armenian Revolutionary Federation I made edit based on the referenced information. I wanted add more sources and more information but the page got protected. Without much discussion user Fadix insulted me 2 times [1] and threatened with edit revenge on other Wiki pages [2]. User Fedayee also insulted me, in supporting Fadix claim [3]. I filed 2 complains about personal attacks but no measure has been taken except warning for 1st insults after which Fadix insulted a second time.
