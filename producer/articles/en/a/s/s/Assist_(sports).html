Assist (sports)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

A number of sports have a statistic known as an assist:

  • An assist (basketball) goes to the player whose pass facilitated a basket.
  • Much like basketball, an assist (ice hockey) goes to the player or players who helped set up a goal.
  • Much like ice hockey, an assist (soccer) goes to the player or players who helped set up a goal.
  • An assist (baseball) is awarded to every defensive player who touches the ball (after it has been hit by the batter) prior to the recording of a putout.
  • Much like in soccer, an assist in water polo is awarded to the player who passes directly to the teammate who scores a goal.
