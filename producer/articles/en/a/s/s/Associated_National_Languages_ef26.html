ANL is the provider of the best solution for the language learners, the well established language center was founded in 1999 Taiwan. The wide choice of language options includes English, French, German, Italian and Spanish enable ANL to lead the Language education industry in Taiwan. The programs are designed for different age group and needs, which are cataloged by kids, teens, adults and business. The qualification of all staffs is one of our major concerns, hence, the native speaking and professionalism are the entry requirement to all ANL language instructors. ANL language center offers customized services and tailor made services for tourism industry, the trading company, and kindergarten and language institutions.

In addition to the language classes, ANL also act as a distributor for various international publications for language learning materials. The carefully chosen publications are introduced from all over the world for providing a better variety of language learning materials to the Chinese speaking countries.

ANL have established three schools in Taiwan and an office in Shanghi since its existence. In the near future, the group will expand further in the major cities in Mainland China. In order to meet domestic demand for European languages, ANL will continuously increase the number of the schools and aim to be the leader of the language education field.
