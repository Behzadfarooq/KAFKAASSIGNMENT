Association of European Airlines

The Association of European Airlines (AEA) includes 31 European airlines. The aim of the association is to represent the airlines in within the European Union and other agencies. Altogether 346,475,239 passengers travelled on these airlines in 2005.

The organisation has beginnings in 1952 when the presidents of Air France, KLM, Sabena Airlines and Swissair formed a joint study group.

It includes many major airlines such as British Airways, Deutsche Lufthansa, Air France, KLM, Iberia Airlines and Scandinavian Airlines.
