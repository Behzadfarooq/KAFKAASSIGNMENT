Coded Arms: Assault is a first-person shooter for the PlayStation 3 which is being developed by Konami. It is a sequel to Coded Arms.

It was first revealed at the Sony E3 2006 Press Conference.

The trailer showed a number of shots of the environment transforming. A lot of computer code faded into a full city landscape as the camera panned around the lead character. This could be indicative of the game once again including randomly generated environments, though no information that specific has been given.

[edit] Rumored Cancellation

The game is strongly believe to have been canceled, but Konami has not shed any light on the issue nor gave an official announcement. A report from Famitsu claims that the game was canceled. A list of canceled Konami games was released, but oddly enough Coded Arms Assault was not on the list. Coded Arms 2: Contagion is still on track for it's late March release.
