Associated Wholesale Grocers (AWG) is the second-largest retailer-owned grocery wholesaler in the United States. The cooperative serves over 1,500 stores in 21 states. It was founded in 1924 as Associated Grocers of Kansas City and is based in Kansas City, Kansas. Associated Wholesale Grocers produces two brands of grocery products, Best Choice and Always Save.

Homeland is among its subsidiaries.
