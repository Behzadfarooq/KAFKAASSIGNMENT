Genre Comedy, Mecha, Police
OVA
Directed by Ayumi Chibuki
Studio Tohokushinsha
No. of episodes 2
Released 1990
Runtime 60 mins

Assemble Insert is a 2 episode anime Original Video Animation (OVA). The main character is a young would-be idol singer and high school student, Maron Namikaze, who has superhuman strength. She battles a mecha organization, Demon Seed, which has been terrorizing Tokyo.

The series is a parody of Japanese pop culture.
