The Assam cricket team is a Ranji Trophy plate group team based in Assam. It has not seen much success in the Ranji Trophy circuit and failed to win a match in the 2005/06 season. It is thus considered one of the weaker teams in the Plate Group. It is categorized into the East Zone.
