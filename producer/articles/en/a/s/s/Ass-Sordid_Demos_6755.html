Ass-Sordid Demos (1990-1996) is an album of demos by the Canadian metal musician Devin Townsend, released in 2000 on his own Hevy Devy label (see 2000 in music).

The title is a pun on 'assorted', reflecting Devin Townsend's sense of humour somewhat.
