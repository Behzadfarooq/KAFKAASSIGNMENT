"Assaulted Nuts" is the third episode of the second season of the sitcom The King of Queens. It was aired on CBS on October 4, 1999.

[edit] Plot

Spoiler warning: Plot and/or ending details follow.

Doug, while shooting staples into a coffee can, accidentally ends up with a staple wound to his crotch. Even worse, he is to meet Carrie at the bank to apply for a small mortgage for Carries office. After waiting in the ER for his injuries and having to get to the bank soon, Doug cannot have his staple removed just yet. And after a painfully long meeting at the bank with Carrie, Doug finally comes clean and admits what happened to Carrie. He insists that they should wait until the end of the meeting to get it taken care of.

Spoilers end here.
