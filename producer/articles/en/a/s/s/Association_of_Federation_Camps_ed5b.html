The Association of Federation Camps (AFC] is a partnership of the 11 UJA-Federation NY area overnight non-profit Jewish Summer Camps. The AFC seeks to promote the Jewish Federation Camps and acts as a collegial planning group for the camps Directors. The group includes overnight camps, special needs camps and adult camps.
