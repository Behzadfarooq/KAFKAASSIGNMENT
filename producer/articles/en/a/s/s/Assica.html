Assica is a progressive rock, hard rock, and heavy metal band composed of bassist Jeremy Andrzejewski, drummer Andrew Trapani, lead guitarist Steven Trapani, and rhythm guitarist Garrett Watts. The band was formed in the summer of 2006 by lead guitarist Steven Trapani and bassist Jeremy Andrzejewski.

[edit] Band Members

  • Jeremy Andrzejewski - Bass guitar
  • Andrew Trapani - Drums
  • Steven Trapani - Lead acoustic and electric guitars
  • Garrett Watts - Rhythm acoustic and electric guitars

[edit] Musical Style

Being an up and coming band Assica has yet to really define their style. Many band influenced Assica's different sounds, from classic rock to metalcore.

Jeremy lists his influences as: Avenged Sevenfold, Disturbed, In Flames, Jimi Hendrix, Metallica, Rammstein, SOiL, Slipknot, and Three Days Grace.
Andrew lists his influences as: Pink Floyd, Rush, and U2.
Steven lists his influences as: Eric Clapton, In Flames, Jimi Hendrix, Led Zeppelin, Metallica, Pink Floyd, Rush, Steve Vai, and Stevie Ray Vaughan.
Garrett lists his influences as: Avenged Sevenfold, The Bronx, In Flames, Incredibad, Pink Floyd, Pixies, Rage Against the Machine, SOiL, We Are Scientists, and Soviet
