[edit] My own constructed language: Aingeljã

In July 2005 I wrote an article in Wikipedia about my own constructed language: Aingeljã. Unfortunately this article was deemed not to be "notable" enough to be included here, despite there are other "not so notable" conlangs detailed here.

Aingeljã or Angelian is a constructed language created by myself just for fun. I began inventing it in 1986, when I was 9 years old, when I had never heard about esperanto nor J.R.R. Tolkien. This artificial language is based on the romance languages. The pronunciation of the name of language is /ajNge"lja/ according to X-SAMPA representation.

In 2005 Aingeljã ranked 118 of most popular conlangs, according to the prestigious web Langmaker. There were more than 1400 conlangs participating in this survey.

Here you have a sample of text in Aingeljã: Our Father


Nou Pare, qwe ets nou cell,
qwe santificat eh vou nome,
qwe vene a nos vou reixĩ.
Qwe eh façata va voluntà,
tant na terra com nou cell.
Donat-nus hogge nou pã qwotiggãn 
e pardonat nas ofensas,
com tantbain nos pardonèms qwĩns ofenden-nus.
E nõ deixat-nus cadre na tentacjõn
e lluwerat-nus dou Mau. 
Voul'eh ou reixĩ,
voul'ou potre et a glorja,
pur sempre. AMAIN.
