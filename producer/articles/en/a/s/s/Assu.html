Assu

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Assu or Açu is a municipality (município) in the state of Rio Grande do Norte in Brazil. See Açu for more details.

Assu is a seventh month in the Nanakshahi calendar, which governs the Sikh tradition. This month coincides with September/October of the Western/Gregorian/Julian Calendar and is 30 days long.

Important Events during this month:

September :

15 = 1 Assu

16 Joti Jot Guru Amar Das Ji / 2 Assu

16 Gur Gadi Guru Ram Das Ji

16 Joti Jot Guru Ram Das Ji

16 Gur Gadi Guru Arjan Dev Ji

18 Gur Gadi Guru Angad Dev Ji / 4 Assu

22 Joti Jot Guru Nanak Dev Ji / 8 Assu

October:

9 Birth Guru Ram Das Ji / 25 Assu

15= 1 Katak
