Assorted Jellybeans was a ska punk band originally from Riverside, California. The band was actually the first release for Kung Fu Records, with their highly successful self-titled debut having sold over 25,000 copies.

Their song, "Rebel Yell", was featured on the soundtrack of the video game Tony Hawk's Underground.
