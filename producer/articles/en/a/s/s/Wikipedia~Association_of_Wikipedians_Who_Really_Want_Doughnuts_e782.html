The Association of Wikipedians Who Really Want Doughnuts (AOWWRWD) is a group of Wikipedians dedicated to bringing doughnuts to all users. Editing Wikipedia can be stressful at times, and the crispy and delicious goodness of doughnuts is a sure-fire way to bring morale and general attitude up.

Remember to always deliver the goods to other users whilst they are still warm.

Members of the society must go through a slew of tests, both physical and mental, to join the exclusive club. Doughnut hole jumping, deep sea frying, and Crisco lacrosse are just a few of the many strenuous activities that must be completed in order to join.

...Or you could just add your name to the members list. It doesn't matter either way, really.
