This is the "Arbitration Team", a team created by AMA advocates "specialized" and interested in ArbCom cases. This team should be a place for people needing an advocate for their ArbCom cases and don't know where to begin or whom to ask or simply want to contact an advocate more experienced on arbitrations rather than filling a "normal" request. But, also this team should be a place for other fellow advocates to ask information on the abritration process or some guidance for the cases they're in.

The advocates from this team are all volunteers; no one is forced to be part of this team to attend an arbitration case, of course.

[edit] Asking for request

To ask a request for advocacy, currently you should do it in our common requesting system until we design a better way to do it. Please, state clearly on the case's summary that you're interested on having an answer from the member's of this specific team (or other teams too).

If you're another advocate, wanting for some help in an arbitration case, either contact one of members or write your concerns in the talk page.
