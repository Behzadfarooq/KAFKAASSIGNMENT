The Parliament of the Macedonia, the Assembly (Sobranie), has 120 members, elected for a four year term, by proportional representation.

On August the 1st 2006, the Macedonian assembly elected Mr. Ljubisa Georgievski from VMRO-DPMNE to be the new parliamentary speaker for the term 2006-2010.
