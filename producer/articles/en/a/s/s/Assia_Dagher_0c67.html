Assia Dagher

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Assia Dagher (Arabic: آسيا داغر‎) was an Egyptian actress and film producer. She was born in Lebanon in 1912. She moved to Cairo with her sister Mary, and niece Mary Queeny. She stayed with Asaad Dagher, her cousin, who was a writer and journalist at the famous Al-Ahram newspaper. She got the Egyptian nationality in 1933. She died in 1986.
