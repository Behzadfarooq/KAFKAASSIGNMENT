ASFA Soccer League is the top division of the American Samoa Football Association in American Samoa.

[edit] ASFA Soccer League - Clubs (2005-2006)

  • South Pacific Academy Dolphins
  • Faga'itua
  • Leone High School Lions
  • Tafuna Warriors
  • Kanana Fou
  • Samoana Sharks

[edit] Previous Winners

  • 1992: Nuu'uli FC
  • 1993-1996: unknown champion
  • 1997: Pago Eagles
  • 1998: unknown champion
  • 1999: Konica Machine FC
  • 2000: PanSa Soccer Club and Wild Wild West (shared)
  • 2001: PanSa Soccer Club
  • 2002: PanSa Soccer Club
