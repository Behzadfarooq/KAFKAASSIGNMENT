[edit] Asgardian density

I know this goes very much to the realm of original research, but here goes...

I don't think that the density of Asgardian matter has ever been established more accurately than "about three times heavier" than their Earthly counterparts. However, based on the rather large sample available I'd put the multiplier at 2.5 (so with this formula that seemingly 125 lb/57 kg woman of the example would actually weigh c. 313 lb/142 kg).

So I made up this table, including pretty much every Asgardian I could find the statistics for.

Note that I've included some characters that aren't proper Asgardians: The Executioner is an Asgardian/frost giant hybrid, Loki is a full-blooded frost giant despite his size and I understand this goes for Hela too; Ulik is a troll, Kurse used to be a Dark elf, and I've got the impression that Thor Girl is a unique being with Asgardian physiology. Also, Karnilla is from Nornheim, but technically the same species as Asgardians and the Vanir. But all characters are from the Nine Worlds, or there's reason to believe that they're made from the same "stuff" as the rest

The "Midgard equivalent" is based on my assumption of density at 2.5 times human tissue. The tricky thing is to find a multiplier that fits them all. Try to find a common factor for the Executioner and Volla, for example.

Perhaps Balder was the most disagreeable one; though maybe that weight is for the time when he was suffering from obesity? Another sources say 320 lb (145 kg) for him, but that doesn't compute at all. I found two weights for Hogun as well, and included the more agreeable one.

And there are at least two characters that don't seem to share the same density as the others, yet they probably should. Malekith the Accursed of the dark elves is 6 ft 9 in (2.06 m) tall and weighs 185 lb (84 kg) link; and Vidar, an Asgardian/storm giant hybrid, is 10 ft (3.05 m) tall and weighs 690 lb (313 kg) link – a 6 ft (1.83 m) tall person with the same proportions would weigh 149 lb (68 kg).

The main reference links point to The Immortal Thor of Asgard website, where all of these characters can be found. There are also a few links to Spider-Bob's Comic Book Encyclopedia website. Other reference sites include the Marvel Comics official site and the Marvel Directory website.
