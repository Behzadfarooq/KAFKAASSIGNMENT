Asnallar (May 18, 1981 - ) is a graduate of BA European Languages from the University of the Philippines in Diliman, Quezon City. He majored in Spanish and his secondary discipline was Comparative Literature and English. He is fairly new to HTML and in the last few months has expressed interest in learning more about web and magazine publishing. Aside from Spanish, he speaks and writes in English and Tagalog.

Asnallar is an anagram of his name. He has been using this as an alias for his creative ventures in the last five years. The pseudonym was created by his best friend in Christmas 2001.
