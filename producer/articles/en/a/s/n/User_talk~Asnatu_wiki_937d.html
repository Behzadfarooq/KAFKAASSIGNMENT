[edit] Speedy Deletion Tags

Hi, it is great to see you are interested in helping maintain Wikipedia. However your use of deletion tags is a little worrying. You should not use the {{db}} or {{delete}} tag. Take a look at WP:SPEEDY for more information. Your edits to Randolo Birnelli were very unhelpful as you actually removed the correct tag, {{db-bio}} and replaced it with {{delete}}. If you need any help then please let me know. --Wisden17 19:16, 20 June 2006 (UTC)
