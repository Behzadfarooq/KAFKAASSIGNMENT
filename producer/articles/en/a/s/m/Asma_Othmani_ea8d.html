Asma Othmani.

Asma Othmani (Arabic: أسماء العثماني) (born June 24, 1987 in Tunis, Tunisia) is a singer who rose to popularity around the world after placing fifth in Super Star 3, the pan-Arabic version of Pop Idol.

Asma includes Julia Boutros as her main musical inspiration.
