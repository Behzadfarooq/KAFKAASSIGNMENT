Asmodée Éditions

Asmodée Éditions is a French publisher of board games, card games and role-playing games. The company was founded in 1995 by former employees of the RPG publisher Siroz Productions. The company publishes its own games, as well as French language editions of other publishers' games. Notable games published by Asmodée include:

  • Diplomacy
  • Dungeon Twister
  • Liar's Dice
  • The Werewolves of Miller's Hollow

Asmodée holds the French publishing rights to the WizKids and Pokémon lines of games.

Asmodée is currently distributing games under the imprints Descartes Editeur and Eurogames, acquired when they purchased Descartes. However, they have not used these imprints for any original publications since the purchase.
