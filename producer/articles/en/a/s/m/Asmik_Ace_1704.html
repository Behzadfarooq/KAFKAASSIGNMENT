Asmik Ace

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

It has been suggested that this article or section be merged with Asmik Ace Entertainment. (Discuss)

Asmik Ace is a Japanese software developer and publisher for several platforms, including the Famicom, Super Famicom, Sega Saturn and Sony Playstation. The company mainly distributes titles in Japan although a few has made it on the U.S. market.

Released titles include:

  • Cosmic Epsilon (Japan only)
  • Deep Dungeon IV (Japan only)
  • Gambler Jiko Chuushinha (Japan only)
  • Gambler Jiko Chuushinha 2 (Japan only)
  • Jumping Kid (Japan only - planned US release as Jack and the Beanstalk)
  • Jyuouki (port of arcade game known outside of Japan as Altered Beast)
  • Meimon! Tako Nishiouendan (Japan only)
  • Nippon Ichi no Nakantoku (Japan only)
  • Oh No! (Japan only)

Asmik Ace has also been the distributor of Mind Game, an animated film.
