A.WOLF

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

A.WOLF (also known as Avatar Wolf; born Anthony Cozzo on July 4, 1979) is a San Francisco bay area rapper who has released two albums; Miseries Company in 2004, and a self-titled effort in 2006. He has also recorded with artists such as Yukmouth, San Quinn, Z-Man, and The Lean Years. He has received airplay on San Francisco-based KYLD and KMEL as a successful battle rapper. [1]

He was also featured on the December 2004 cover of the hip hop periodical, Downlow Magazine.
