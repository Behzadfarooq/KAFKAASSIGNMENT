A.W.O.L. is a music album by the rapper, AZ. It was recorded after Final Call, an album that was shelved because of a two month early leak on the internet. While people thought A.W.O.L. would be worse than Final Call, the disc was a surprise underground hit when it came out. Because of constant record label problems, A.W.O.L. is the first AZ album which was released on his own Quiet Money Records imprint. In a couple of months after the release, A.W.O.L. 1.5, which included a bonus disc featuring acapella and instrumental versions to the songs on A.W.O.L., was released.

[edit] Miscellanea

  • The tracks "Live Wire", "Magic Hour", and "The Truth" were all featured on AZ's Final Call album. The album was shelved, however, due to piracy.

A special 2-disc edition of the album was realeased called A.W.O.L. 1.5 It contained "Live Wire", "Magic Hour", and "The Truth" as bonus tracks and disc 2 contained all the tracks' instrumentals as well as achapella versions.
