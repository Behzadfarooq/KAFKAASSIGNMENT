A.P. Warrior is a thoroughbred race horse. As a foal of 2003, he was a possible contender for the Triple Crown in 2006.

[edit] Connections

A.P. Warrior is owned by Stan Fulton and trained by John Shirreffs. He was previously trained by Eoin Harty. Ridden by Corey Nakatani, he was bred in Kentucky by Jim Fleming.

Starting in the 2007 season, A.P. Warrior stands at Stonewall Farm in Versailles, Kentucky.

[edit] Breeding

His sire is A.P. Indy while his dam is Warrior Queen. I n his breeding line are such horses as Dr. Fager, Secretariat, Bold Ruler and Mr. Prospector.
