A.E.K. Arena is a multi-use stadium Project in Athens, Greece.

It will be used mostly for football matches and will host the home matches of AEK Athens FC. The stadium will have a capacity of approximately 50.000 according to chairman Demis Nikolaidis. It will replace their former stadium, "Nikos Goumas," which was torn down in 2003. AEK currently host their home games in the Athens Olympic Stadium.
