A.T.O.M (A Twist Of Modern) is a downloadable album by Carbon/Silicon, a group consisting of Mick Jones from The Clash and Tony James from Genaration X. It was officially released on July 28, 2006.

  10. "Oilwell" - 6:16
  11. "It's Not Over Yet" - 5:44

  • All tracks by Tony James and Mick Jones

[edit] Notes/trivia

  • Demo versions were available since beginning of 2006. The remastered versions were released on July 28 and some of them had different titles than the demos.
  • "War on Culture" had been previously known as "The Global War on Culture"
  • "Eye Scan" had been previously known as "Are You Terrified?"
  • "Nothing But the Truth" had been previously known as "The Whole Truth"
