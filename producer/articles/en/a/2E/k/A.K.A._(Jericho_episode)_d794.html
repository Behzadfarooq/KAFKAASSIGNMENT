"A.K.A" is the eighteenth episode of season one of the fictional CBS drama Jericho.

Spoiler warning: Plot and/or ending details follow.

[edit] Synopsis

Jake holds Hawkins hostage when he discovers that Hawkins might not have been completely honest about his background before arriving in Jericho.
