a.k.a. Cartoon (or a.k.a. cartoon or a.k.a. CARTOON) is a Canadian animation company located in Vancouver, BC. It was founded on April 1, 1994, by Danny Antonucci.

a.k.a. created and produced The Brothers Grunt for MTV but is best known for Ed, Edd n Eddy, an animated series that currently airs on Cartoon Network. The company's motto is: "Dedicated to producing animation for everyone, whether they want it or not!"
