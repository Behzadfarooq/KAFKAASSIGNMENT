A.Konduru is a village and Mandal in Krishna District, Andhra Pradesh, India.

[edit] Villages in A.Konduru Mandal

The following is the list of villages in this Mandal. [1]

  1. Atlapragada
  2. Cheemalapadu
  3. Gollamandala
  4. Kambampadu
  5. Koduru
  6. A.Konduru
  7. Kummarakuntla
  8. Madhavaram (east)
  9. Madhavaram (west)
  10. Marepalle
  11. Polisettipadu
  12. Repudi
  13. Vallampatla
