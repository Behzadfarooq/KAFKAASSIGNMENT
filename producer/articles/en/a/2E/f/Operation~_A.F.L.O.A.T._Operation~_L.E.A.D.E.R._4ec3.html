Operation: A.F.L.O.A.T./Operation: L.E.A.D.E.R. (Agents Feared Lost On Awful Tide/Lizzie Enthusiastically Attempts Daring Executive Role) are the second episodes of the third season of (twenty-eighth episodes) Codename: Kids Next Door. They premiered June 18, 2004.

[edit] Operation: A.F.L.O.A.T

[edit] Plot/Sypnosis

Toilenator arrives at Mr. Wink and Mr. Fibb's house. He tells Mr. Boss, Wink, and Fibb that he is late for the "We Hate the Kids Next Door" barbecue. Mr. Boss furiously asked Wink and Fibb that if they sent Toilenator the fake invitation. The two answers him that they sent Toilenator the fake invitation, which they accidentally got the right invitation to him. Toilenator smells the burgers, and gives Mr. Boss asparagus. Mr. Boss knocks the asparagus off Toilenator, and tells him that asparagus were used by adults to feed the kids with.

Sector V looks at the villains that they were having a relaxation. Numbuh 4 became noisy, and the villains spotted them. Then, the villains chases the team around. Mr. Boss thinks that Sector V couldn't hide at his garage, but he forgot that he has no garage. The team uses the MEGARAGE to escape. Stickybeard shoots bubble gum at the MEGARAGE. One of them clogs the engine. The team crushes down to an ocean.

The team wakes up, and finds that they were stranded at an ocean. The team finds out that they were stranded on a sea of asparagus. Numbuh 1 orders Numbuh 2 to fix the engine. Numbuh 4 became hungry, and Numbuh 5 isn't complaining that they were stranded. Later, Numbuh 4 is starving, and Numbuh 1 tells 2 to hurry fixing the engine. Numbuh 2 can't fix the engine, because of the bubble gum. Numbuh 4 begins to have a hallucination that one of Numbuh 3's Rainbow Monkeys is a cheeseburger. 4 begins to chase 3 around until a monster asparagus attacks Numbuh 2, and eats him. Then, the monster eats Numbuh 3, and her Rainbow Monker was left behind, which Numbuh 4 gets to it. Numbuh 1 orders Numbuh 4 to come back, but refuses to get the cheeseburger. The monster asparagus eats Numbuh 1. Then, the monster eats Numbuh 4, who chases after it to get the Rainbow Monkey, but 4 forgot that he can't swim.

Numbuh 5 worried that the sea monster will eat her. Just before the monster can eat Numbuh 5, a bubble gum fires at its mouth. Stickybeard orders the pirates to slay the monster, and Numbuh 5 faints. Numbuh 5 wakes up to find that she is at Stickybeard's ship. She begins to attack the pirates, and Stickybeard tells her that he saved her and the rest of the team, because he couldn't just leave them to die alone. One of the pirates brings Numbuhs 1-4 at safety, and let's them eat some candy. Stickbeard tells Numbuh 5 that once he and Sector V are out of the sea of asparagus, they will get back as mortal enemies. As the ship sails off into the sunset, Stickybeard asks Numbuh 5 if she can keep quiet about him saving her and her friends, because then the adults wouldn't invite the pirates to next year's barbecue.

[edit] Operation: L.E.A.D.E.R.

[edit] Plot/Synopsis

The story begins when the KND are beating up the lesser Ice Cream Men at an ice cream parlor. After the fight, Numbuh 1 tells the team they did a great job and that another Ice Cream Parlor is safe for kids worldwide. An ice cream cone falls on Numbuh 1's head and covers his head in ice cream. The KND head back to their treehouse and as Numbuh 1 heads to his room to clean himself up, he runs into Lizzie, and Lizzie tells him that Numbuh 1 was to take her to the football game at the middle school. Numbuh 1 tells Lizzie he can do it another time and heads into his room to clean up.

The KND receive a message from Numbuh 65.3 and he tells Lizzie that he has mission specs for Numbuh 1. Deciding Numbuh 1 needs a break, Lizzie decides to take over Numbuh 1's position as the leader and takes the mission specs instead and takes the KND out for ice cream before the mission, not to mention she took Numbuh 1's sunglasses with her. Numbuh 1 comes out of the bathroom and Numbuh 65.3 appears on the view screen and wonders where Lizzie went and asks Numbuh 1 why he's still there, because he already gave the mission specs to Lizzie. The Jr. High football team has been practicing anti-kid maneuvers all week and it's up to the KND to find out what they are up too. Numbuh 1 thinks they're practicing for a football game and 65.3 decides to cancel the mission, although, Numbuh 1 heads for the Jr. High School's locker room to rescue Lizzie and his team.

Lizzie takes the Sector V Team to a beauty parlor to prepare for the mission. Numbuh 2 and Numbuh 4 complain about it, but Numbuh 3 and Numbuh 5 don't. Numbuh 1 sneaks around the locker room and looks at a blackboard with X's and O's and discovers that those weren't football plays but an invasion plan against KND Moonbase and leading the invasion is Numbuh 274, who prefers to be called Chad and Chad was given a powerful explosive football, a Footbomb by Coach Wetterhahn, capable of destroying the KND Moonbase 110%, and Chad's going to take Numbuh 1 out of the game first! The football players start snapping their gym towels at Numbuh 1, have him tied up and stuffed into a locker.

While the KND cruise in the COOL-BUS, they start complaining about their new hairstyles and outfits and Lizzie starts to get on their nerves. The football players turn the stadium into a rocket and it blasts off. The COOL-BUS crashes into the stadium and the KND all end up inside the rocket. Chad and the football players are about to beat up the KND and Lizzie hides in the locker Numbuh 1 is locked up in for cover. Lizzie unties Numbuh 1, gives his glasses back and orders the KND to attack. They manage to retrieve the Footbomb and do evacuation plan X-42, but end up in the football stadium and Chad and the football players play football with the KND, with the Footbomb as the ball! Numbuh 4 eventually gets the Footbomb into the end zone and throws it on the ground, blasting the stadium and causing it to crash! After the blast, Coach Wetterhahn yells at the football team, saying it's the last time he lets a junior varsity football team plan invasions against the KND Moonbase and orders the Jets to do 6,000 push-ups.

Back at the Treehouse, Lizzie apologizes to Numbuh 1 for taking over his position, because she had no idea how hard his job was. Numbuh 1 tells Lizzie it's okay and she'll promise not to do it again. Numbuh 1 goes out for ice cream until Numbuh 65. 3 appears on the communicator, saying another mission has come up. Lizzie tells Numbuh 65.3 that he can transmit the specs to her and she'll make sure Numbuh 1 gets them.
