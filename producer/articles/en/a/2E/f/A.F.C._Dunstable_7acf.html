A.F.C. Dunstable are a football club based in Dunstable, England.

[edit] History

The club was established in 1981 as Old Dunstablians, and were a team for old boys of the defunct Dunstable Grammar School. They joined the Spartan South Midlands Football League Division One in 1995. In 2004, the club changed their name to AFC Dunstable. For the 2005-06 season, they are members of the Spartan South Midlands Football League Division Two.
