A.F.C. Aldermaston is a football club based in the village of Aldermaston, near Tadley in Berkshire, England. They joined the Hellenic Football League Division One in 1979, spending seven seasons in that league. After a spell back in local football, they joined the Hampshire League in 1991, and became members of the Wessex League when the Hampshire and Wessex Leagues merged in 2004. For the 2006-07 season, they are members of the Wessex League Division Two.
