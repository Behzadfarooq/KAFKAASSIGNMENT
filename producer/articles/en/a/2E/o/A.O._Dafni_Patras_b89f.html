Dafni Patras AU (Greek: Α.G. Δάφνη Πάτρας A.Ο. Dafni Patras) is an athletic club in Zarouchleika neighbourhood in south suburban Patras in the Achaia prefecture. The team was first created in 1965. Its colours are white-yellow-blue. It is named after the famous laurel tree. It has the number EPO 1148. They play in the Achaia Football Guild Union and won the Achaia championship two times.
