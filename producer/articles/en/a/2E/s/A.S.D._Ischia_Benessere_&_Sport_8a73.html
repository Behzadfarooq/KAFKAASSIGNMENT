Associazione Sportiva Dilettante Ischia Benessere & Sport[1] is an Italian football club located in Ischia, Campania. It currently plays in Serie D. Its colors are yellow and blue.

The team was previously known as Ischia Isolaverde, and played Serie C2 from 1983 to 1987, and then Serie C1 until 1990, when Ischia relegated to Serie C2. Ischia returned to Serie C1 the next year, and played to the third-highest ranked Italian division until 1998, when the club was cancelled by the federation due to financial troubles.
