Associazione Sportiva Dilettante Figline[1] is an Italian football club located in Figline Valdarno, Tuscany. It currently plays in Serie D. Its colors are yellow and blue.

Attacking midfielder Anselmo Robbiati, formerly of Internazionale and Fiorentina, is a player for the team.
