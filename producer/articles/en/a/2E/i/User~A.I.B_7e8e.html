[edit] AIB Project

AIB is an automated internet bot designed to analyze certain pages for vandalism.


[edit] Algorithm

In order to accurately monitor vandalism AIB has a watch list. The pages on the WatchList are downloaded every 5 minutes and analyzed.

If there is a sudden drop in the size of the page (i.e more than 25%) AIB will list the page on another page/site.


[edit] Reversion

I dont know yet if AIB will revert pages back, this might be seen as a bit zealous.
