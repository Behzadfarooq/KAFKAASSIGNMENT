A. H. Albut

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

You have new messages (last change).

A.H. Albut was the first full-time Secretary of Newton Heath (the term 'football manager' was not used at Manchester United until the arrival of Jack Robson) and Manager of the club. He started in 1892 and remained in that position until James West took over in 1900.
