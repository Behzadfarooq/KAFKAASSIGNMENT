A. M. Rathnam

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

A.M. Rathnam(born February 4) is a South Indian film producer.He belongs to Nellore, a Sea Shore Town in Andhra Pradesh , India. His Banner Sri Surya Movies, is very popular for Quality Films. He produced blockbusters like Indian, Sivakasi, Kushi, Ghilli, and Khushi. He is having Two sons Jyothi Krishna , A talented director and Ravi Krishna , An extra ordinary actor, who got critics appreciations with his film 7/G Rainbow Colony in Tamil which was dubbed into Telugu as 7/G Brundavana Colony.

7/g is simultaneously made in telugu with character roles being replaced.

1. He Started his career as personal Makeup man to the popular Tollywood Actress Vijayashanti.[citation needed].

2. He Settled in the Tollywood with the Success of Kartavyam , Which brought National Award to the Actress Vijayashanti.

3. He likes to use the Latest Technology in his films , Also he releases his Films in Tamil , Telugu and Hindi , which safe guards his movies.

4. His Latest Film Kedi,Jaadoo in telugu , is directed by his elder son A.M.Jyothi Krishna , and his younger son A.M Ravi Krishna starred as a hero.
