[edit] General comments

Suggested check-user priorities:
So far, there appear to be lots of different spammers and POV-pushers hitting this article over a 3 year period and using socks.

  • The best use of checkuser time may be to focus on the very disruptive Medha Hari socks as a first priority.
  • The very active Kalakendra sock army may have been stopped by Kalakendra.com's recent blacklisting.
  • Some of the sock groups may not prove to be worth checking out, having faded away at some point in the past.
