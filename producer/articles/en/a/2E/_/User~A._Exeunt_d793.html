[edit] Opinion on Wikipedia

I find it amazing that, for such a popular site, I had no idea that it existed. It was through a Descent-related website that I happen to come across Wikipedia. Since then, I have found Wikipedia to be an excellent and very useful source for school research and nearly everything else that concerns research.

As a free encyclopedia, I understand that it is subject to vandalism from time to time. It would be a shame, in my opinion, that Wikipedia might eventually shut down due to overexcessive vandalism (face it, there are many hackers and vandals on the Internet, and there are sites that shut down due to vandalism). I will do my best to prevent vandalism in all articles I have edited, especially those that are of my interest.

[edit] Article Editing Style

When editing articles or uploading pictures on the English Wikipedia, I try as much as possible to do the following:

  • Ensure that proper English is used
  • Ensure proper punctuation
  • Cite my sources, where applicable
  • Not vandalise
  • Redirect vandals to Uncyclopedia
  • Follow the neutral point of view
  • Revert any and all vandalism
  • Remove any and all original research
  • Remove any and all points of view
  • Remove any unsourced claims, where applicable

On talk pages, I try to:

  • Assume good faith
  • Avoid attacking anyone personally

[edit] Article Editing Range

Although I would usually rectify errors on every page that I visit, pages related to the following get a higher priority and I would check on them more often:

  • Pokémon
