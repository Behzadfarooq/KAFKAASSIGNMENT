[edit] Centiare update

Note: the commenter above is, in fact, the Jimbo-banned AND community-banned (can't hurt to make sure) User:MyWikiBiz. --Calton | Talk 00:26, 25 January 2007 (UTC)

He's having about as much luck in the spamdexing community as he did in ours. This "black hat" SEO forum exchange is worth reading. It starts with a discussion (which you can skip) of Wikipedia's new nofollow implementation for external links. On page 2, post #13, a brand-new forum member chimes in with a post titled "Centiare to the rescue!". By the end of page 4 he's thoroughly unimpressed his potential customers. One of them dryly points out he doesn't even realize his new Wikipedia alternative, running on Wikia software, still has its own links coded "nofollow", making it useless for the SEO (search engine optimization) people he's marketing it to. Uninhibited by WP:CIVIL, the other forum members' responses to his pitch for Centiare make interesting reading for anyone here on Wikipedia who was on the receiving end of JossBuckle Swami's screeds. --A. B. (talk) 16:15, 7 February 2007 (UTC)
