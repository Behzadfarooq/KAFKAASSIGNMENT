[edit] Semi-serious

Here's how it works: Wikipedia has legions of patrollers tracking vandalism around the clock who can undo your edits, no matter how painstaking or stealthy, in the click of a button. It's very trivial and very easy to remove every trace of your editing to the site, and in the end your vandalism will not be seen nor remembered.

For your sake, please invest your time in something more constructive. The best that will come of your vandalism is that you will be blocked by an administrator in a short while. Your graffiti of Wikipedia articles serves no purpose but to waste your time and ours, so please stop now and consider your motivations for doing this and what the end product will be. ~~~~
