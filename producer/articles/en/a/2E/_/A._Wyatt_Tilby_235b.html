Aubrey Wyatt Tilby was a journalist and an author of history and philosopy. He was born in Addiscombe, Surrey on 1 April 1880 and died in 1948. Married to his wife Kathleen for thirty-nine years, he had three children and eight grand-children.

  • Lord John Russell (Cassell & Company, 1930)
  • Right, A Study in Physical and Moral Order (Williams and Norgate, 1933)
