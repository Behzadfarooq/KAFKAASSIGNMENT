A.Le Coq Arena (Lilleküla Stadium) is a football stadium in Tallinn, Estonia.

It is the home ground of the Estonia national football team and FC Flora Tallinn. The stadium has a capacity of 9,300 [1] but can be expanded to 10,300 with extra seats[2]. It is named after the Estonian beer called A. Le Coq. The address of the stadium is Asula 4c.
