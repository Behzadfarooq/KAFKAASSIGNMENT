A. Vivaldi “Guitar Concertos”
Studio album by Ahmet Kanneci
Released 1986
Recorded 1986 Hacettepe University Ankara State Conservatory 50th Anniversary Concert
Genre Classical Music
Length 40:36
Label Turkofon
Producer(s) Hacettepe University

A. Vivaldi “Guitar Concertos” is Ahmet Kanneci's first album. It was recorded during the 50th Anniversary Concert of Hacettepe University Ankara State Conservatory.
