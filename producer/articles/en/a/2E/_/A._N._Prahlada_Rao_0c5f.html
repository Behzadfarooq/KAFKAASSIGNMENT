A. N. Prahlada Rao from Bangalore is a Kannada crossword writer. He has created 17000 crosswords in Indiain-Kannada language for various kannada daily Newspapers and periodicals. A.N.Prahlada Rao was born on 24 July 1953 at Abbani in Kolar district of Karnataka in India. He started writing Kannada crosswords in 1984. He has created different crosswords covering General, Cinema, Crime, Sports, Mythology, Science and on other subjects. ```A. N. Prahlada Rao``` is believed to a crossword maker with highest number of crosswords in India. His crosswords are published in local publications like Prajavani, Vijayakarnataka, Kannadaprabha, Samyuktakarnataka, Aragini, Mangala, Balamangala, Chitra, Priyanka, Karmaveera, Tharanga, Vijayachitra,Vanita, Janapada, Vignanasangati, Policenews, Policefile, Sanchu, Udayaraga, Dehalivarte, Rangavalli, Hotel Patrike, Cheluve, Chitratara, Kandayavarte, Vydyaloka, Kannadamma, Udayakala and some more magazines. Every day A. N. Prahlada Rao creates 3 crosswords. Three Kannada Dailies i.e Vijayakarnataka, Samyukta Karnataka and Kannadaprabha publishing his crosswords daily. He also creating Quiz for some periodicles.

```A. N. Prahlada Rao``` is also an author of a book named Bangaradamanushya which is the chronicle of Dr. Rajkumar, who is a popular Kannada Film actor. Government of Karnataka has published this book on the occation of Dr. Rajkumars 50 years entry to Filmland.
