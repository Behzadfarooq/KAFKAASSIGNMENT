A. Lorne Cassidy Elementary School is located in the community of Stittsville, in the west end of Ottawa, Ontario, Canada.

The school offers students a choice of regular English and Early French Immersion programs in Kindergarten through Grade 8.

Construction of the school began in 1991. Before it had its formal name, the school was known as the New Crossing Bridge School. The school is named after a former Carleton Board Superintendent of Schools whose contribution to the development of special education programs was recognized throughout the province.
