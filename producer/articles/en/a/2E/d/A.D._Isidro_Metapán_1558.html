Asociación Deportiva Isidro Metapán or simply Metapán is a Salvadoran football club in Metapán, El Salvador. The club currently plays in the Primera División de Fútbol Profesional, which is the top tier football league in the country.

The club's mascot is the jaguar and plays its home matches in the Estadio Jorge Calero Suárez.
