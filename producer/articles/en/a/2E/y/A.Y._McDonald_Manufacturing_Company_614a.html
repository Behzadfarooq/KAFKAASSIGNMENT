A.Y. McDonald Manufacturing Company

The A.Y. McDonald Manufacturing Company is a corporation headquartered in Dubuque, Iowa. The company manufactures plumbing fixtures, pumps, brass, and other water systems. The company also manufactures gas valves designed for high pressure operations, and meter equipment.

A.Y. McDonald traces its history back to 1856, when Scottish immigrant Andrew Young McDoanld founded the company. His family has continued to be involved in the company over the years.

The company was originally located in downtown Dubuque. In 1983, the company moved to a new facility in a new industrial park on Dubuque's west end. In 1996 the plant's foundry was expanded so that production of brass castings could be increased. The company also maintains facilities in Albia, Iowa, Elizabethton, Tennessee, and Sparks, Nevada.

Recently the company made the news when it was revealed that Tonight Show host Jay Leno would be coming to Dubuque to exclusively entertain the company's employees in the fall of 2006. This is part of the company's 150th anniversary celebrations.
