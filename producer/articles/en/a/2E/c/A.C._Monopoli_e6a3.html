A.C. Monopoli is an Italian football club from Monopoli, Apulia. The club was refounded in 2003, and has played Serie D/H in 2005/2006, ending it in second place, and successively reaching the Serie D playoff finals; therefore, Monopoli will play 2006/2007 in Serie C2.
