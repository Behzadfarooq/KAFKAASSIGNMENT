Associazione Calcio Martina is an Italian football club, based in Martina Franca, Apulia. The club was founded in 1947. Martina currently plays in Serie C1/B. The team's colors are blue and white.

The side played against Juventus F.C. for the first time in the first match ot the 2006 Italian Cup.
