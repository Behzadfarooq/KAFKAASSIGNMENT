[edit] Summary

So far the 2006/2007 season has had its ups and downs for Milan. Having started the season with an 8 point penalty due to the Calciopoli Scandal a bad run of results where Milan failed to win for 9 matches in a row had left them in a lowly 15th place on only 11 points. After two consecutive losses against Atalanta and Roma, Milan had a streak of 7 consecutive matches without loss. 2 wins against Catania and Udinese on Matchday 17 and Matchday 18 respectively have left Milan only 6 points behind 4th place and qualification for the Champions League. After showing a bad physical condition throughout most Autumn, the team is looking forward for a January retreat in Malta, to regain condition and try to achieve a 4th place finish in Serie A and further progression in the Champions League.

Milan's performances in the Champions League have altogether been a different story. Drawn in a group described as easy by some pundits, with AEK Athens FC, Lille OSC and RSC Anderlecht, Milan comfortably won the group after Matchday 5, even after losing to AEK Athens in that round. Kaká himself has been in particular good form for Milan in the Champions League having scored 4 goals and is currently in joint lead for this years Champions League Golden Boot.
