Associazione Calcio Forlì is an Italian football club, based in Forlì, Emilia-Romagna. The club was founded in 1919, having last been in Serie B in 1947. Forlì placed last in 2005/2006 Serie C2/B, being relegated to Serie D, but was further excluded by the federation because of financial troubles. A new club, Associazione Calcio Sporting Forlì, is now representing the city of Forlì in the Promozione division. The team's colors were red and white.
