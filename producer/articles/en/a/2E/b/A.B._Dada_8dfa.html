A.B. Dada is an anarcho-capitalist author and entrepreneur based out of the Chicago, Illinois region. Dada has a business history of bringing IT development to the contracting field since the early 90s. He was born on 17 February 1974 in Park Ridge, Illinois. He is the older brother of Shiraz Dada, bass player in Maps And Atlases. He is the second cousin of Fareed Zakaria on his father's side, as well as the second cousin of Rafique Dada, the President of the Bombay Bar Association. Dada's full name is Adam Brian Dada.

In 1987 he founded The Melting Point BBS, one of Chicago's largest gaming BBSes during the BBS era, and the only BBS running the famous Tele-Arena BBS multi-node door in the entire Midwest. In 1995, he founded Deep Productions, one of the first 3D animation studios in Chicago, which spun off into Deep Labs and Design-Engine. He founded No Copyright Studios in 2006 to sign and produce modern bands and artists who will create, distribute and perform music and other art forms with absolutely no copyright or licensing restrictions.

Dada invented the term Unanimocracy relating to the anarcho-capitalist vision of an apolitical structure for the apolitical anarcho-capitalist movement. He is also the sponsor of www.unanimocracy.com and privately funds numerous other pro-liberty websites. Along with the term Unanimocracy he also invented the term immigflation to discuss the idea of masking fiat currency inflation through additional immigration. Dada invented the Godfather Dada (cocktail) recipe at Crobar Chicago in September 1998. This cocktail is currently listed on numerous bartending websites and printed manuals.

Dada is a revolutionary Christian who espouses service and humility rather than judgement and power, which has caused him to be excommunicated from a variety of congregations in his home region of Lake County, Illinois.

Dada also has corporate headquarters for his holding company, Dada Networking Group, in Mumbai, India and Gdańsk, Poland.
