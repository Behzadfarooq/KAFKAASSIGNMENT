A.H. Brightwell

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

A.H. Harry Brightwell (born 4 August 1932 in Matheson, Ontario) was a member of the Canadian House of Commons from 1984 to 1993. His background was in veterinary medicine.

He was elected in the 1984 federal election at the Perth electoral district for the Progressive Conservative party. He served in the 33rd Canadian Parliament and was re-elected in the Perth—Wellington—Waterloo electoral district in the 1988 federal election. He lost the riding to John Richardson of the Liberal Party in the 1993 federal election.
