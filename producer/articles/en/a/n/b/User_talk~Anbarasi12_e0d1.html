[edit] Re: Hi

Unfortunately, I'm a very active administrator -- I generally place several blocks each day. I can't say anything more specific without knowing the contents of the "you've been blocked" infobox (IP address and block reason; I'd normally need your account name and the blocking admin, but I have those, at the moment). Now, I see on your talk page a note about Singapore; if you're in Singapore, that actually gives me a major clue. StarHub, the ISP that a great many Singaporeans connect through, only uses a few IP addresses, and as a result, users on that ISP will very often find themselves hit by collateral damage in the form of an autoblock. I highly encourage you to read up on autoblocks, and to make good use of the {{subst:tl:unblock-auto}} template in the future, should you get hit by one again. Regards, Luna Santin 22:56, 1 January 2007 (UTC)
