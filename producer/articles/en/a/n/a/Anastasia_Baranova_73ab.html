Anastasia Baranova

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Anastasia Baranova (born on April 23, 1989 in Moscow, Russia) is an actress who is perhaps best known for starring role as Jennifer "Scout" Lauer in the Discovery Kids/NBC series Scout's Safari. She has also guest-starred on 7th Heaven, Drake & Josh, Joan of Arcadia, Lizzie McGuire, Malcolm in the Middle and Veronica Mars.
