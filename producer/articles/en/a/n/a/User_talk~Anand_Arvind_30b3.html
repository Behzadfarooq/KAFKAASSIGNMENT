[edit] Vithoba

Hi Anand, I like some of your edits. But the addition to Vithoba is wrong in grammer. It should be full sentence. "Varkari and Bhakti spiritual movements in these states, and a large amount..." is not a full sentence. It should be "Vithoba was a major focus of the Bhakti and Varkari movements in these states." That is a full sentence and very clear.

Now, regarding 'incarnation' and 'manifestation'. The word 'incarnation' means 'avatar', like Rama/Krishna, who were born and grew up on earth. 'Manifestation' means 'sakshatkar' or 'swaroop' which is where God gives darshan to the devotee (like Vithoba appeared before Pundalik). I am sure you agree with all this. Please edit and expand the article to make it nice. Regards, ImpuMozhi 00:22, 9 May 2006 (UTC)
