Ananda Lewis (born March 21, 1973 in San Diego, California) is an American model and television personality.

She co-hosted a morning daily radio show with John Salley on Los Angeles radio Station KKBT, when Salley was dumped in favor of another DJ Tom Joyner, she moved to doing the 9a-12p slot on KKBT, however left that position in November 2006.

She is known for being the host of the program Teen Summit on the BET network, after which she went on to become a VJ for MTV. In 2001, she hosted her own talk show The Ananda Lewis Show.

She attended Howard University, as well as the San Diego School of Creative and Performing Arts.

She is a mixture of Black and Native American (Creek).
