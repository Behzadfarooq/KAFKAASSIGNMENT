Ana Hickmann (born March 1, 1981 in Santa Cruz do Sul, Rio Grande do Sul, Brazil) is a professional supermodel.

She distributes her own clothing and cosmetics lines, manages a photo studio and a DJ agency and is also a television hostess on the programme "Hoje em Dia", aired by Brazilian TV network Rede Record.

Hickmann was once listed in the Guinness Book of World Records for being "The model with the longest legs", measuring 48" (123 cm) out of a total height of 73" (6'1" / 185cm). Since 2002, the Guinness Book of World Records no longer lists this title.

She was ranked #47 and #85 on the Maxim Hot 100 Women of 2004[1] and 2005,[2] respectively.
