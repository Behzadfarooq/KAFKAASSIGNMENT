[edit] About Me

My name is Wa|ker. Anaraug is guarana spelled backwards. I like caffeine.

I like music, alot. I play several instruments. The Wikipedia article on the instrument I am possibly most proficient at, the euphonium, states that most euphoniumists can trace their "teacher ancestry" back to a relatively few famous players. At one point I took euphonium lessons from Robert Nunez, who at one point took tuba lessons from Arnold Jacobs. Later I took euphonium from Masahito Kuroda, who, IIRC, was a student of Earle Louder. Along with euphonium, my most often played instruments are guitar and trumpet.

I may be somewhat of an otaku; my favorite ongoing anime/manga is BLEACH. As a result, i've picked up tiny amounts of Japanese, but not in any serious way. On the other hand I've taken classes in Spanish, but I'm very rusty and wouldn't trust myself to use that skill in editing the Wikipedia.

I sometimes identify myself as a Gnostic Christian Discordian, but in most cases I feel that the term "Thomas Christian" would be more accurate than Gnostic. Also, as far as moral worldviews go, I follow the way of the Three Golden Triangles in addition to Christianity. Some of my favorite spiritual books are Matthew, Mark, Luke, Thomas, Ecclesiastes, Psalms, The Principia Discordia, Enoch, and 101 Zen Koans.
