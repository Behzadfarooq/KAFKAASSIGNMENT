Anastasia Gimazetdinova (Cyrillic: Анастасия Гимазтдинова) (born May 5, 1980 in Tashkent, Uzbekistan) is an Uzbekistani figure skater. She is the three-time Uzbek national champion. She is coached by Peter Kiprushev.
