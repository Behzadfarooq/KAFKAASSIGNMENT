Anata to Scandal (あなたとスキャンダル , Anata to Scandal?) is a shōjo manga by mangaka Ayumi Shiina and published by Shueisha. It ran in Ribon from 1994-1995 and was also released in 5 tankōbon.

[edit] Characters

  • Tomoka Takasaki
  • Arata Miwazawa
  • Yuuki Serika
  • Takemi Ogura
  • Tametsu Hakagama
