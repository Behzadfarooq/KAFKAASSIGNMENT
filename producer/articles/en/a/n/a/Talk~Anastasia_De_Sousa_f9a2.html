[edit] Is THIS page necessary?

While the event has certainly generated much controversy, I do not know if Anastasia should have her own article for being killed in the shootings. We can of course LIST here name under the GNU I believe, but she is otherwise irrelevant to history. It is sad that she died, but many other articles on subjects like the article on Gill say something like "killed x number of people," not each and every one of their names. If anything, this article needs deleted and any reference to Anastasia moved to the bottom of the page in a link to another source that already printed something on her with it marked "The only fatality at the school itself" (as its unknown how many others will survive) (for example).

Wikipedia is an ENCYCLOPEDIA, and it already takes a few liberties (like writing correctly and without faults everywhere like I do). Naming and giving articles to each and every person who has ever been shot at school is not encyclopedic and do not represent the guideline of Wikipedia.

The fact that anyone wants to delete a small paragraph about a girl who passed away is a disgrace. How, in any way, does having this small article available affect anyone? Her innocent life was taken and she SHOULD be mentioned and remembered. There is no way this article should be deleted....end of story

[edit] Educate Yourself

Encyclopedia's are about information on history. Anastasia De Sousa made a huge difference in this historical event. What most of you don't know because it is not mentioned in the articles and was hardly mentioned in the news is that if it weren't for Anastasia De Sousa there would have been a severe number of casualties added to this event. In my eyes that would make her a hero and there's always room in an encyclopedia for a hero. I'm sure I would find Superman if I searched it.

Anastasia De Sousa saved other lives when she was shot. Her boyfriend was right next to her at the time she got shot and took the time to talk to the killer and ask why he was doing this. While they were talking Gill asked if she was dead, the boyfriend replied "no" so he shot her 8 more times. But the time the boyfriend took to speak to Gill and the attention on Anastasia De Sousa allowed the police to get there and control the situation before Gill was able to shoot more innocent victims. Anyone who disagrees with her name being on this website could probably never relate to her story and would not understand. —The preceding unsigned comment was added by 69.70.3.190 (talk • contribs) .
