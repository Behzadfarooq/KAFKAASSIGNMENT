Ana Bertha Lepe (born Ana Berta Lepe Jiménez in Tecolotán, Jalisco on September 12, 1934) is a Mexican actress and third runner up at the Miss Universe contest in 1953.

She obtained a role in 1952 in La justicia del lobo her first of many films, she then entered and won the Miss Mexico contest. In 1955 she filmed in Cuba Una gallega en La Habana. In 1959 she participated in Señoritas with Christiane Martel, the Miss Universe 1953 in the year Lepe obtained fourth place. The following year, in 1960, Lepe's father shot and killed her fiancé, actor Agustín de Anda and disappeared from showbusiness for several years.

In 1977 she acted in the first of many telenovelas, Pacto de amor. She was briefly married later on but never had children.
