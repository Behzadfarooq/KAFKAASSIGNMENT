Ana Paula Araujo (Date of Birth: October 25, 1981) was born in Roraima, Brazil and is a model who appeared in the 2007 Sports Illustrated Swimsuit Issue. [1]She is Brazilian, and has done advertisements for such notable clients as Liz Claiborne.
