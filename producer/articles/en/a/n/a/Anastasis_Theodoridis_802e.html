Anastasis Theodoridis is a Greek journalist from Thessaloniki. He broadcasts his weekly TV show ΕΛΛΑΔΑ ΚΑΙ ΟΡΘΟΔΟΞΙΑ, "Greece and Orthodox Faith" at the Greek TV Channel EURO. He is an "Original Orthodox Christian" which means that he follows the Julian calendar. In his TV-show he supports his claims about the Orthodox faith and opposes both the Greek Orthodox Church and the "heresies" (Catholicism, Protestantism, etc.). He also organises pilgrim excursions to monasteries as well as protests against Tzimis Panousis and the Da Vinci Code.
