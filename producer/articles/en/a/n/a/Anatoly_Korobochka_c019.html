Anatoly Korobochka (born in Odessa,[1] USSR, now Ukraine) is the interim head coach of Heart of Midlothian football club in Scotland.[2] Now in his 50s, he played for Tavriya Simferopol and CSKA Moscow, where he also had a football development role.

He arrived at the club in 2006 as sporting director along with Eduard Malofeev, the consultant who himself had an unsuccessful six-game spell as interim head coach in October 2006.[3]
