Anatoli Semyonovich Levchenko (born on May 5, 1941 in Krasnokutsk, Kharkiv Oblast of Ukrainian SSR - August 6, 1988 in Moscow, Russian SFSR) was a Soviet cosmonaut of Ukrainian descent. Flew as Research Cosmonaut on Soyuz TM-4 mission.

Selected as a cosmonaut on July 30, 1980. Married with one child. Died because of brain tumor.
