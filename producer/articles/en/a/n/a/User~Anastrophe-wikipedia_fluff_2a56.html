[edit] Who I am

I'm a full-disclosure kind of guy. i have philosophical problems with anonymity, but each person has their reasons, and - depending upon their behavior, actions, and intent - i can respect it. Anonymous Assholes however get no slack from me.
So again, who i am: Paul Theodoropoulos. b. 24 October 1959, in Redwood City, California. My father was greek, my mother is a W.A.S.P.. I'm left-handed. I can recite pi to about 65 digits. I've taken all manner of licit and illicit drugs, albeit in my indiscrete youth. I now confine myself to strong coffee, inexpensive red wine, the occasional martini, ouzo, single-malt scotch, and more and more aspirin as my creaking body ages.
i have an extremely broad range of interests, thus, wikipedia captures my attention. i am married, we have a dog and a cat, and live in a house, on a street, in a city.
