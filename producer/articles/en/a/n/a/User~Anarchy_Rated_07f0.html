[edit] History

The group was formed in mid-summer of 2006. At that period of time the group's name was "Anti-Talent". Anti-Talent was composed by: BrainDMG , CostyBoy aka The Imitator , Jeezus , Bogdy , pZdRc , Cata aka Doggy , Doru aka DoRk and Paul aka Junky. The group did short sketch movies with mobile phones that were later published on the internet , the group also tried to make a album "The Best Of 2006 - Summer Edition" , witch featured a serise of sketch movies , and a future album "Anti-Talent" recorded at BrainDMG's house featuring CostyBoy. The song of legendary singer Diana Ross "I Will Survive" , intitled "I Won'T Survive". The project was later abandoned. After a serise of conflicts the group split in late August 2006.

[edit] Anarchy Rated

This time things changed. A new group would take over by surprise , Anarchy Rated. The groups members are: PunkRocker(formerly known as CostyBoy) , Bobletz(formerly known as Bogdy) , Jeezus , DoRk and RzVA. The group will oficially launched it's self in april 2007.
