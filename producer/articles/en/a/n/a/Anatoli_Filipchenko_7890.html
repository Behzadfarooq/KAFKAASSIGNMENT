Anatoli Vassilyevich Filipchenko (Russian: Анатолий Васильевич Филипченко; born February 26, 1928 in Davydovka) was a Soviet cosmonaut of Ukrainian descent. He flew on the Soyuz 7 and Soyuz 16 missions.

After leaving the space programme in 1982 Filipchenko became the Deputy Director of the OKB in Kharkov.
