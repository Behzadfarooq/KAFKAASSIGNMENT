Ana Dolidze

Ana Dolidze (Georgian: ანა დოლიძე)is a well- known Georgian democracy activist. Currently, a Visiting Fellow at the Harriman Institute of the Columbia University [1] till 2007 Dolidze was a Chair of an influential Georgian non-governmental organization, the Georgian Young Lawyers' Association [2] (GYLA). Dolidze has graduated with a law degree from Tbilisi State University [3]and has completed extensive studies in Europe, including receiving a Master's degree in Public International Law at Leiden University in the Netherlands [4] In addition to her activities with the GYLA, Dolidze speaks in the Western world about the current situation in Georgia [5]. Dolidze frequently criticizes the government for human rights abuses and lack of committement to rule of law [6]. Minister of Defence of Georgia and other public officials have attempted to mount a campaign against Dolidze, who has been defended by more than hundred Georgian civil organizations [7].
