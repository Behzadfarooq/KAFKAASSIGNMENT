Anata is a death metal band formed in Varberg, Sweden in 1993.

To date, they have released four albums and a split album with Bethzaida. In 2005 they completed recordings for an album titled "The Conductor's Departure", released on June 12th, 2006 on Earache Records Wicked World label.
