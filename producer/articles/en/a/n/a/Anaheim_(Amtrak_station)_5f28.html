Anaheim Station is an Amtrak and Metrolink rail station south of Los Angeles Union Station in the city of Anaheim. It serves both Amtrak's Pacific Surfliner from San Luis Obispo to San Diego and Metrolink's Orange County Line from Los Angeles to Oceanside.

The station is located on the north side of the Angel Stadium parking lot. The station address is 2150 E. Katella Avenue, Anaheim, CA 92806.
