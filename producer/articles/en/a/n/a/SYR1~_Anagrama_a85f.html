SYR1: Anagrama is an EP by Sonic Youth. It was the first in a series of mostly instrumental and experimental releases put out on the band's own SYR label. SYR1 follows the band's tradition of releasing their experimental work in foreign languages, in this case French.

[edit] Track listing

  1. "Anagrama" – 9:31
  2. "Improvisation Ajoutée" – 2:54 (translation: "Extra Improvisation")
  3. "Tremens" – 3:22 (translation [from Latin]: "Trembling")
  4. "Mieux: De Corrosion" – 6:54 (translation: "Better: Of Corrosion")
