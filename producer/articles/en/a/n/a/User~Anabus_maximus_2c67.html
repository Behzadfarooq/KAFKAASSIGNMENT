[edit] An explanation of my name because no one seems to get it

I subscribe to EGM. When they first gave a preview of Xbox Live, I read that they thought the name AnubisMaximus would be a cool gamertag. Fast-forward a few months to my first time playing Halo and I needed a name for my profile. I remembered the name that I had read in the magazine, so I thought I'd use that. However, I didn't remember correctly and thus, anabus was born (Halo's technical restrictions did not allow the entire anabus maximus I would've liked). Later, after discovering the joy of multiplayer computer games like Jedi Outcast, Battlefield 1942 and Starcraft, the underscore was added to form anabus_maximus. There, now you get it.
