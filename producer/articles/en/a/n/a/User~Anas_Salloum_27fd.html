Who am I?

I'm a Canadian male of Syrian descent. I joined Wikipedia on 8 October, 2006. I'm currently living in Riyadh, KSA. In Wikipedia I contribute mostly to obscure articles, which seem somehow to be interesting. The Syrian articles, too, are quite a mess, and most of them are stubs, I hope that I can help fix some of them. For that reason, I have started a dedicated wikiproject. WikiProject Syria will be my main focus in the time being. We need some members, so if you're interested, do join.
