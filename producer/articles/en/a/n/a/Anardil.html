In J. R. R. Tolkien's universe of Middle-earth, Anardil was the sixth King of Gondor.

He ascended the throne after the death of his father Eärendil in 324 T.A. No notable events were recorded for his reign.

Anardil died in 411 T.A. and was succeeded by his son Ostoher.
