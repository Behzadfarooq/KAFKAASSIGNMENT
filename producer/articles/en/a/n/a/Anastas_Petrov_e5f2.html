Anastas Petrov (Bulgarian: Анастас Петров) (born 24 June 1973) is a Bulgarian footballer currently (as of July 2006) playing for Botev Plovdiv as an midfielder. Petrov born in Pazardzhik and started your career in local team PFC Hebar.
