Anastasia Kelesidou (Greek: Αναστασία Κελεσίδου, born November 28, 1972 in Hamburg, Germany) is a retired Greek discus thrower best known for winning silver medals at the 2000 and 2004 Summer Olympics. During her career she set seven Greek records in discus throw, the best being 67.70 metres.

Her only international victory came at the 1997 Mediterranean Games.
