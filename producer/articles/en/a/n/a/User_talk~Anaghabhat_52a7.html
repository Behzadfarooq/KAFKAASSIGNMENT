[edit] Pyaar Ke Side Effects -- Plagiarism

Hello, Anaghabhat,

you are completely right. Parts of the summary from PKSE are taken from the blog. Plagiarism is prohibited, since it is violating the author of the original text. If the blogger and editor are the same person, it's different, of course. Generally, I'd inform the editor of the Wiki entry that plagiarism isn't the way to go. Since we try to assume good faith, maybe it happened by accident. Just change the text and write a summary with your own words (or I do it, if you don't have the time). If those kind of things happen again with the same editor, consider to warn him twice before informing an administrator who will have him blocked. If you have any question on how to tidy up the article, just ask. If you don't have the time to tidy it up, let me know and I'll do it. Thanks for the heads-up. :) Best regards, --Plumcouch Talk2Me 19:01, 5 October 2006 (UTC)
