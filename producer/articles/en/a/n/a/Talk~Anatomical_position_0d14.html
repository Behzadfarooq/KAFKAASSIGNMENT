[edit] Specificity

I think in many instances this article is too specific. For example, there are infinitely many transverse, coronal and sagittal planes. Also, terms of comparison like superior and inferior don't refer to objects above and below the transverse plane, they just refer to things relatively closer to or further from the top of the head.
