    [edit] User name

    My user name is misleading. It is not an indication of my political leanings. It is my Puzzle Pirates name.

    [edit] About me

    I have a number of different roles:

    • PhD student - I am working on a thesis on practical reason.
    • Part-time fixed term lecturer - I have taught epistemology, metaphysics, philosophy of mind, informal logic, ethics, and philosophy of science.
    • Chair of a health and disability ethics committee - engaged in the independent review of health related research.
    • Senior officer in the crew Iliad on the Puzzle Pirates Viridian ocean

    I live in New Zealand with my husband and daughters.
