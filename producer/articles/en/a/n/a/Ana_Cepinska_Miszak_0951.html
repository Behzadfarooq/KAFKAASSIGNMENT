Ana Cepinska Miszak

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Anna Cepinska Miszak (born on April 11, 1978) is a Venezuela-born model and fourth place (third runner-up) at the 1996 Miss World pageant. She is of Polish-descent and has lived in Mexico since 2004 to work in television. She became the first full-nude model of Playboy-Mexico in April 2007 [1]. She was the representative of Nueva Esparta in the Miss Venezuela of 1996.
