Category:Anaheim Angels managers

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

This category includes baseball managers for the franchise formerly known as the Anaheim Angels. This franchise has been known by several names:

  • Los Angeles Angels (1961-1964)
  • California Angels (1965-1996)
  • Anaheim Angels (1997-2004)
  • Los Angeles Angels of Anaheim (2005-present)
