Ananya Pratinav(born 15 May 1985) is an Indian Wikipedian, registered and active since 09 January 2007. Currently, he is pursuing his bachelor's degree in engineering from D.Y.P.I.E.T,Pune.

[edit] Early life

He was born in Patna(Bihar). He is the eldest of the three children born to his parents Sunit Kumar and Kavita Kumar who live in Patna,Bihar(India). He has two brothers Akshay Pratinav and Paritosh Pratinav. Ananya did his schooling from Patna.He completed his school education (10th standard) from Don Bosco Academy,Patna and (12th Standard) from St Michael's High School,Patna.
