Anand Tucker

Anand Tucker (born 24 June 1963) is a film director and producer. He began his career directing factual television programming and adverts. He co-owns the production company Archer Street.

Tucker was born in Bangkok to an Indian father and German mother, but is based in the UK. He was scheduled to direct Philip Pullman's "His Dark Materials trilogy", but in May 2006 was replaced by Chris Weitz.

It is rumored that he will be directing the upcoming film adaptation of Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince set for release in November 2008.

[edit] Selected director credits

  • The Vampire's Life (1993)
  • The Boys of Sunday (1994)
  • Saint-Ex (1996)
  • Hilary and Jackie (1998)
  • Shopgirl (2005)
  • And When Did You Last See Your Father? (2007) (post-production)
  • Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince (2008) (pre-production) (rumored)
