Anarchy Comics was a series underground comic books published by Last Gasp between 1978 and 1987.

"Cartoon strips, art, and stories from various international anarchist comic artists, including Clifford Harper, Ruby Ray, Jay Kinney, and Paul Mavrides. A class mixture of history (Durruti, Makhno, Kronstadt, Jimmy Hayes, etc.), comment, and fiction (such as the seminal "A Punk In Utopia")."

Presently, issue #2-4 are out of print.


[edit] Creators

  • Jay Kinney, editor, #1-3; associate editor, #4.
  • Paul Mavrides, associate editor, #3 ; editor, #4.
  • Adam Cornford, associate editor, #3.


[edit] Publications

  • Anarchy Comics #1 (September 1978).
  • Anarchy Comics #2 (December 1979).
  • Anarchy Comics #3 (July 1981).
  • Anarchy Comics #4 (1987).
