Hi Anabanana, welcome, and I hope everything works out great for you here! Regarding your speedy delete on Sharpstown Middle School, I don't think that's a speedy delete. Usually, speedy delete is used for total nonsense or for vanity pages. The Sharpstown school articles is a bad article, but (1) it does contain sensible information such as the date of founding etc. and (2) there is and has been a whole long debate about whether schools should get their own articles, some say yes, some say no, I don't know if there is a policy. Anyway, maybe the article should be deleted, but it should go through the standard AfD (Articles for Deletion) process so that other people can review it. The AfD process is here: WP:AFD. In the meantime I removed your speedy tag. By all means you nominate the article for AfD if you want to. Cheers! Herostratus 09:13, 17 December 2005 (UTC)
