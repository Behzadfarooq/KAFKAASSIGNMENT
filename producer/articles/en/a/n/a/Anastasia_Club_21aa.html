Anastasia Club is a manga series by Chiho Saito, which was published in Japanese by Shogakukan Productions Co., Ltd. in Flower Comics.

[edit] Story

The main character, Tsubaki Kamui, is the granddaughter of an antiquarian. A mysterious man named Sena Takuto appears in her store one day. He is trying to gather proof that he is the grandson of the last surviving member of the Russian Empire, Princess Anastasia Nikolayevna Romanov, the daughter of Tzar Nicholas II.
