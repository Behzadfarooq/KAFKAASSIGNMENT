This article is useless and basically looking for a reason to exist :

  • There is only one group listed, so the article should be titled Anarchist group.
    • One anarchist group can easily be added to the anarchism article.
  • The first paragraph (added by Sam Francis with the reasoning "added paragraph that might be better beginning anarchist organisation, but we'll develop it and see") is heavily opinionated.
    • The organization of anarchists is unimportant to nearly all people.
    • Anarchy is not a method of organization, it is a political philosophy.
    • Many anarchists engage in indirect action, especially those that became anarchists for practical rather than moral reasons.
    • Even if the first paragraph was factual, it would be better placed in the anarchism article, since it would help to enlighten people about anarchism and would not be extraneous.
