Anatoli Papanov

Anatoli Papanov (Russian: Анатолий Дмитриевич Папанов; 31 October 1922 in Vyazma, Smolensk Oblast, Russia - 7 August 1987 in Moscow, Russia) was a Russian actor.

Papanov starred in some of the best and well-known Soviet films, often together with Andrei Mironov. Mostly known for his great performance in comedies, he also had serious and dramatic roles. Anatoli Papanov was honored as People's Artist of the USSR (1973).

[edit] Selected filmography

  • Nu, pogodi! (voice) (1969-1987)
  • Beware of the Automobile (Берегись автомобиля) (1966)
  • The Diamond Arm (Бриллиантовая рука) (1968)
  • The Twelve Chairs (Двенадцать стульев) (1976)
  • Cold Summer of 1953 (Холодное лето пятьдесят третьего) (1987)
