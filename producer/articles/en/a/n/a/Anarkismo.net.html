Anarkismo.net is an international anarchist-communist and platformist news and discussion website.

[edit] Purpose

The website aims to collect and distribute news and analyses produced by various platformist, anarchist-communist and especifista anarchist groups from throughout the world. By doing so, those involved hope to facilitate debate and discussion of platformist and anarchist-communist ideas, and improve communication between such organisations globally. They also hope to use the site bring their ideas to a wider audience and encourage more people interested in social change to join and form platformist and anarchist communist groups.

[edit] Affiliated groups

Organisations that support the site goals and editorial statement of Anarkismo.net, or at least have had some input into it include:

  • Federation of Anarchists of Greece (Greece)
  • Federazione dei Comunisti Anarchici (Italy)
  • Coletivo pró Organização Anarquista em Goiás (Brazil)
  • NEFAC (USA and Canada)
  • Organización Socialista Libertaria (Argentina)
  • ACL (Mexico)
  • Anarşist Komünist İnisiyatif (Turkey)
  • Alternative Libertaire (France)
  • Grupo Qhispikay Llaqta (Peru)
  • Workers Solidarity Movement (Ireland)
  • OCL (Chile)
  • Zabalaza Anarchist Communist Federation (South Africa)
