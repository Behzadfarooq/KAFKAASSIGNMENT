[edit] Anarkeya

Hi! I just became a Wikipedia editor in July 2006. I noticed that the articles relating to my favorite book series, the (Dragon Prince and Dragon Star trilogies by Melanie Rawn), were underdeveloped and wanted to expand the the knowledge of its wonderful characters, Sunrunners, and universe.

I occassionally flit around and make small edits to other articles or clean up flagged ones. I usually stick to literature and film articles as I've found Wikipedia to be a great source of information on books, characters, movies, and shows (as well as other things). I plan to expand the pages that I have created for the Dragon Prince series and also to work on Melanie Rawn's Exiles trilogy - though this will probably be sometime in the future.
