Anakaputhur is a town in Kancheepuram district in the state of Tamil Nadu, India. Though situated in Kancheepuram district, the town is actually a suburb of Chennai. As of 2004, it is the only water providing town to the city of Chennai.[citation needed]

[edit] Demographics

As of 2001 India censusGRIndia, Anakaputhur had a population of 31,733. Males constitute 51% of the population and females 49%. Anakaputhur has an average literacy rate of 74%, higher than the national average of 59.5%; with 54% of the males and 46% of females literate. 12% of the population is under 6 years of age.
