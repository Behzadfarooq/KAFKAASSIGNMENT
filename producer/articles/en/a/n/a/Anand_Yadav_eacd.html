Anand Yadav is a writer in the Marathi style. He was born on the 30 November 1935 in a small village near Kolhapur, Maharastra. His father, a farmer, opposed Anand's education. After running away from home, Anand achieved a Ph.D. at Pune University. His writing began a new era in Marathi literature called "Gramin Sahitya".

Yadav's novel Zombi (lit. 'Fight against all odds') won the Sahitya Akademi Award in 1990.

Zombi is a realistic novel that touches the soul. It narrates the story of small 'andya', his eagerness to learn, his life in sheer poverty, his loving mother and illeterate rural father.........

People like Dr. Anand Yadav, who , against all odds, fought, won and reached up to this point, are gift to Maharashtra. We honour them and bow before them

His other novel is called "Nangarni" (meaning cultivation of the soil) which is extension of "Zombi". It narrates his struggle during youth period and the challenges he faced in settling down in life. Author has been successful in writing all whatever was happening in his mind that point of time and makes the reader experience that phase of his life.

Third novel that continues his autobiography is "Gharbhinti". This one describes his Adult life with references to Marriage, Daughters' education and support of family back in Kolhapur.

He faced all odds against posed by the external situations in those times. All three books are must read to really understand Rural life in India.
