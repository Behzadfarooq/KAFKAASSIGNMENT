Anacortes Airport (FAA LID: 74S) is a public airport located two miles (3 km) west of the city of Anacortes, in Skagit County, Washington, USA.

[edit] Facilities

Anacortes Airport covers 120 acres and has one runway:

  • Runway 18/36: 3,015 x 60 ft. (919 x 18 m), Surface: Asphalt

[edit] Airline

  • San Juan Airlines (Eastsound, Friday Harbor, Lopez, Roche Harbor)
