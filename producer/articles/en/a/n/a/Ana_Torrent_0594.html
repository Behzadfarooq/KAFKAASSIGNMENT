Ana Torrent

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Ana Torrent Bertrán de Lis (born July 12, 1966) is a Spanish film actress.

[edit] Biography

[edit] Early life

Ana was born in Madrid.

[edit] Career

Torrent in The Spirit of the Beehive, 1973

Her debut came in 1973 with the starring role as "Ana" in the film El espíritu de la colmena (The Spirit of the Beehive) by Víctor Erice, when she was five years old. This was followed by yet another memorable role in which she played the lead character's younger self in Cría cuervos (1976) by Carlos Saura.

In 1996, she received numerous awards and nominations, including a Goya Award nomination for her lead actress role in Alejandro Amenábar's film Tesis.

By the end of the 90's she received critical acclaim when she played a Basque nationalist murdered for quitting ETA, in the film Yoyes (1999) by Helena Taberna.

As of 2006, she remains a busy actress and will soon appear as Catherine of Aragon in the film The Other Boleyn Girl (2007).
