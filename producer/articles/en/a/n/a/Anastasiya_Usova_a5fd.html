Anastasiya Usova

Anastasiya Usova (Kaz: Анастасия Усова) (born 1988 in Aqtöbe, Kazakh, Soviet Union (present Kazakhstan)) is a Kazakh singer who rose to popularity after placing second in SuperStar KZ 3, the Kazakh version of Pop Idol. She had fewest votes to Nurzhan Kermenbayev.

Anastasiya includes Dilnaz Akhmadieva, Whitney Houston & Irina Dubtsova among her main musical influences.
