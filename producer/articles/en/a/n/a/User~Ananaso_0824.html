[edit] Ananaso

Greetings! I'm Ananaso, or Damon M. Lord, to use my real name. I'm 26, and I'm a linguanaut, an explorer of languages. I am currently employed by 118 118, so I have added a bit to that page, and created the redirect page 118118.

I'm from Cardiff, South Wales, and I'm a committed member of the Conservative Party (UK). Until November 2005, I was Chair for Conservative Future, South Wales Central Area, but stepped down due to ill health. I also supported and voted for David Cameron during the recent Conservative Party leadership elections.

In addition to full time employment, I'm also a student with the Open University, Milton Keynes. I am currently finishing my degree BA (Hons) Humanities with English Language. I am also currently considering my post-graduate studies.

I enjoy writing, am working on a novel, and also enjoy languages, particularly conlangs.

My website: www.wessisc.co.uk/damon
