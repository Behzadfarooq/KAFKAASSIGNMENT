  • March 2, 2007: The eviction of Ungdomshuset by Danish police results in days of rioting in Nørrebro, Copenhagen.
  • February 22, 2007: Thousands of students march through Athens, Greece and clash with police to protest government plans to reform higher education and introduce private universities.
  • January 20, 2007: The World Social Forum opens in Nairobi, Kenya.
Protest in Oaxaca City center
  • January 16, 2007: The Peoples Popular Assembly of Oaxaca (APPO) and the Trique people create the Free and Autonomous Municipality of San Juan Copala in the eastern region of Oaxaca, Mexico.
  • December 30, 2006: The 6th encuentro of the Zapatista Army of National Liberation begins in Oventik, Chiapas, Mexico.
  • October 29, 2006: Mexican federal and military police, supported by army troops, seize the center of Oaxaca, which had served as the headquarters for the five-month protest occupation of the city. Protestors had demanded the resignation of state governor Ulises Ruiz Ortiz, after he sent a brigade of over 3000 police to the area to break the strike of a teachers' union in June.
