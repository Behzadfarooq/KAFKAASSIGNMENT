Ana Mathis

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Ana Mathis was born in Northumberland, United Kingdom on December 23, 1959. She started becoming a voice artist after college in 1985. The roles she played include Rugrats, Wake, Rattle and Roll, Dennis the Menace, Twisted Tales of Felix the Cat and Madeline. Ana was 34 and lives with her husband Christopher Hallowell and moved to New York since 1981.
