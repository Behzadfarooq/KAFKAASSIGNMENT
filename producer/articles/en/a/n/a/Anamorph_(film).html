Anamorph is an independent film scheduled to be released in 2007, directed by Henry S. Miller and starring Willem Dafoe. Dafoe plays a seasoned detective named Stan Aubray, who notices that a case he's been assigned to bears a striking similarity to a previous case of his. The psychological thriller is based on the concept of anamorphosis, a painting technique that manipulates the laws of perspective to create two competing images on a single canvas.
