Asim Naqvi is a final year M.Sc. in Internetworking (MINT) student at University of Alberta. He has six years professional experience with City of Edmonton, SIEMENS and FRAMENET. He has professional career reflecting strong leadership qualifications coupled with “hands-on” IS and networking expertise. He worked with Chinese Optical Fiber manufacturers in designing and implementing the pilot project of OFAN (Optical Fiber Access Network) for PTCL (Pakistan Telecommunication Co. Ltd.). He maintains focus on achieving bottom line results while formulating and implementing advanced technology and business solutions to meet a diversity of needs. He has superior record of delivering simultaneous large-scale, mission-critical projects on time and under budget. He was trained by SIEMENS in Germany and Dubai. His area of interest and research is based on QoS of 3G Wireless Cellular Systems in WCDMA (UMTS). He is member of PEC, IEC and IEEE.
