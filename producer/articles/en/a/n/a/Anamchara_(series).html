[edit] Blurb

The year is 1682. The Great Essex Witch scare ended long ago, leaving behind many grieving witches who are determined to keep the old ways alive. And also forces of evil determined to destory them. When a particular coven of witches is targetted, the witches need to go on the run... before their enemies- whoever they are- succeed. Before they die.

[edit] Characters

The main characters in this series are Raeyn, Mary, Theresa and Colm.

[edit] What is Anamchara?

Back in the 1600s, four witches receive visions foretelling their deaths. Casting a spell to keep them together no matter what, the alterations in strength cause the spell to last a lot longer than intended. Cut to the 21st century when four teenagers discover that they are not only the descendants of these four witches, but also their reincarnations. However, even though their previous battle is long over, evil holds a grudge...

[edit] Where Did Anamchara Come From?

Forever having a love for almost anything to do with Wicca and witchcraft, Nisha Vyas spent years writing short stories about witches in hopes of finding that one special set of characters that would have enough power to create that book series she always wanted to write. Upon the creation of Raeyn, Mary, Theresa and Colm, she was inspired by themes of the Witch Trials, reincarnation and the special bond between friends. Combining these ideas with her experiences as a Wiccan, she created Anamchara. Anamchara translates as "soul friend"- a person who is meant to be in your life because your souls are connected. It is also the name of a Celtic friendship rite.

[edit] About The Author

Nisha Vyas is originally from Essex. At 19 years old, she is currently studying for a degree in Performing Arts and writing part time. With the help of fellow writers and Lulu Publishing, she was finally able to publish her work. Nisha has been a practising Wiccan since her early teens and she aims to combine fantasy, drama and real life Wicca and witchcraft into her book series.
