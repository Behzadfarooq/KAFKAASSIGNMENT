Anabar (district)

Anabar is a district in the country Nauru, located in the northeast of the island. It covers an area of 1.5 km², and has a population of 810.

Anabar is also a Nauruan constituency, made up of the districts Anabar, Anibare and Ijuw. It covers an area of 5.1 km², and has a population of 1,240. It returns 2 members to the Nauruan Parliament in Yaren.
