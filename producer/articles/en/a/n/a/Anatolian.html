Anatolian means of or pertaining to Anatolia (in what is now Turkey), or a person from Anatolia, including:

  • The Anatolian languages, a group of extinct languages once spoken in Anatolia.
  • The Anatolian Plate, a tectonic plate on which Turkey sits.
  • Anatolian rock, a genre of rock music from Turkey.
  • The Anatolian beyliks, medieval Turkish principalities in Anatolia.
  • The Anatolian Shepherd Dog, an ancient breed of dog from Anatolia.
