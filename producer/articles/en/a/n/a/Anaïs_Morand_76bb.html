Anais Morand (born March 10, 1993 in Aigle, Switzerland) is a Swiss pair skater. She competes with Antoine Dorsaz. They are the 2007 Swiss junior national champions and have competed internationally on the Junior Grand Prix circuit and at the World Junior Figure Skating Championships. Their programs are choreographed by Ekaterina Gordeeva.
