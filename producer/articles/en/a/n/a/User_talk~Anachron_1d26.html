[edit] I edited your comments

Hello User:Anachron, I edited your instructions at the top of Wikipedia:WikiProject Cycling#Participants. The main change is to more strongly encourage project members to place {{WikiProject Cycling}} on their user pages, because that will automatically categorize them into Category:WikiProject Cycling members, thereby reducing the chance of someone who is already a project member receiving an unneeded greeting from another project member who is on a recruiting drive. Since I edited your instructions, I removed your signature, so it would not appear I was distorting your words. In any case, the wikigods tell us not to sign our work on non-talk pages (WP:SIG#When signatures should be used). --Teratornis 19:30, 3 February 2007 (UTC)
