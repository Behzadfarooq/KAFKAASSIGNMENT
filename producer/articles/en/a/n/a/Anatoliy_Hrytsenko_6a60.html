Anatoliy Hrytsenko (Ukrainian: Анатолій Степанович Гриценко; Russian: Анатолий Степанович Гриценко), (born October 25, 1957) is the current Minister of Defence of Ukraine.

[edit] Education

  • June 23, 1979 graduated with honors from Kyiv Higher Military Aviation Engineering School[3]
  • December 10, 1984 assigned Candidate of Science (Ph.D.) degree from Kyiv Higher Military Aviation Engineering School[4]
  • 1993 graduated from Defense Language Institute, U.S. Department of Defense
  • June 6, 1994 graduated from Resident Program of U.S. Air War College[5]
  • October 30, 1995 graduated from Academy of the Armed Forces of Ukraine[6]
