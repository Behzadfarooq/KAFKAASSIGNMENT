Anarchist Party of Canada (Groucho-Marxist) was a dadaist-flavoured political party based in Vancouver, British Columbia best known for hitting famous people with pies. Some of the people thus assaulted include U.S. president Jimmy Carter's brother Billy, the federal opposition leader, Joe Clark, future B.C. premier Bill Vander Zalm from Globe & Mail, and Eldridge Cleaver. The mottoes of the Anarchist Party of Canada were "vote with a pie" and "The whole world Canada". They were loosely affiliated, or shared many members with, the short-lived Rhinoceros Party branch in British Columbia. Like the latter, they are considered to have run out of steam when they started taking themselves too seriously.
