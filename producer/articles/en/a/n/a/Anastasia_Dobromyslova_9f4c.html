Anastasia Dobromyslova

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Dobromyslova while throwing darts

Anastasia Dobromyslova (born 26 September 1984) is a professional darts player from Russia. She started to play darts at the age of eleven, and has become Russian champion six times. According to Anastasia, it was quite easy because hardly anyone played the game.

Her most notable performances include beating Trina Gulliver on her debut on the Open Dutch Championships in 2004 and even winning the same tournament in 2005. She has recently moved to the Netherlands, to become a professional darts player.
