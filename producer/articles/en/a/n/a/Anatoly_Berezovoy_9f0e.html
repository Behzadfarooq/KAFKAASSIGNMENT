Anatoly Nikolayevich Berezovoy was born in Enem, Adygeya Autonomous Oblast, Russian SFSR on April 11, 1942. Married with two children. he graduated from Air Force Academy

He was selected as a cosmonaut on April 27, 1970. He flew as Commander on Soyuz T-5.

He retired on October 31, 1992 due to medical reasons after injuries he got as a victim of an armed robbery.

From 1992 to 1999, he was a Deputy President Russian Space Federation.
