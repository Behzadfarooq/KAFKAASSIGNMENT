Analogue Bubblebath 2 (also known as Analogue Bubblebath Vol.2) was the second EP released by IDM artist Richard D. James, under his commonly known alias, AFX. It is also the second release in the Analogue Bubblebath series. It was his second release under this alias.

The EP consists of three tracks, all done in the acid house techno style which was popular at the time. It was released in 1992 on Rabbit City Records in the 12" vinyl format. No official CD copy exists.

"Didgeridoo" can be found in its original form on the R&S Records compilation Classics, though both mixes are similar.
