Analyse-it is a statistical analysis add-in for Microsoft Excel. Analyse-it is the successor to Astute, developed in 1992 for Excel 4 and the first statistical analysis add-in for Microsoft Excel.

The base module provides a range of standard parametric and non-parametric procedures, including Descriptive statistics, ANOVA, and Linear regression.

The Clinical Laboratory module provides a range of procedures for evaluating clincial and diagnostic methods. Procedures include Bias plots, Deming regression and Passing Bablok for method comparison, Precision, Linearity, and Receiver operating characteristic analysis (see references below).
