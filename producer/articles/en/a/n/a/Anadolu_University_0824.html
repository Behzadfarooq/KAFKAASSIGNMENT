Anadolu University (Turkish: Anadolu Üniveristesi) is located in Eskişehir, in the Eskişehir Province of Turkey. It has a well-organized campus including a Japanese garden. Since there is a large student body, the city of Eskişehir is influenced by the university, making it a "university town". There are many cafes at the heart of the city, Adalar, to serve the students.
