Distance Learning And Online Learning

Distance learning, and online learning;or what is popularly known as e learning are generally regarded as synonymous terms.This is due to the enormous popularity of distance education, which has given learners all over the world to study at:any place, pace or time.So distance education has rapidly progressed from the print media to the computer network, which again enables people to learn in a flexible manner.This is especially true for the working adult, who more often than not has to 'switch' between working time and studying time.Online learning and e learning enables the learner to study both synchronously or asynchronously, that is in real time or at leisure. In online learning the lectures could be classroom oriented, like traditional teaching or the teacher might build in innovative formats like group discussion, quiz, puzzles, role play etc into his/her teaching.What makes online or e learning popular is the 'speed', the alacrity with which one can interact with the teacher.Again, there is flexibility, because of the asynchronicity that I have mentioned earlier. Furthermore it bridges'distances' hence:distance learning.Distance, online and e learning are complementary to each other, and thus are parts of the whole.
