Anastasia (band)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

"Melourgia" cover (Libra Music).

Anastasia is the name of a music band from the Republic of Macedonia. Formed in 1990, their members are:

  • Goran Trajkoski (ex Saraceni, Padot na Vizantija, Mizar, Aporea) — lead vocal, bagpipes, flute
  • Zlatkonash Oridjanski (ex Lola V. Stain) — guitar, mandolin, flute, back vocal
  • Zoran Spasovski (ex Mizar, Aporea) — drums, percussion, keyboards, back vocal

Their music is a blend combining Byzantine past, through Eastern Orthodox Church music with a rich gamut of ethnic rhythms. They play acoustic instruments typical for the area where they come from and dwell, such as: kaval (flute), gajda (bagpipes), tapan (drum), and also use modern technology - computers, samplers, synthesizers, etc.

Anastasia have made hardly know music scores for films, theater performances, disney shows, and TV programs. Their soundtrack for the movie Before the Rain bearing Polygram label has been sold in thousands of copies worldwide.
