Academic map:

Dr. Ananda Das Gupta has been engaged in teaching/Research for more than eighteen years in different universities/Institute across the country like North Eastern Hill University (Central university) as Head of the Department of Commerce at Aizawl, as Academic Counsellor to Indira Gandhi National Open University (North Eastern Regional Centre) as Associate Faculty at Vivekananda Centre for Indian Management (VCIM) in Indore, and currently is holding Faculty- Chair in Business Law, OD, HRD and Business Ethics at Indian Institute of Plantation Management, Bangalore- a national level sectoral management school set up at the initiative of Ministry of Commerce, Government of India, and approved by AICTE.

He did his Doctoral work from Faculty of Commerce and Business Management, University of Patna as a UGC Research Fellow on the Operational Problems of the Small Scale sector in India with special reference to Bihar State wherein he conducted a number of field surveys, by administering structured questionnaires on the target group, having attached mainly to:A.N.Sinha Institute of Social Studies,Patna, Social Science Centre,Calcutta, NIESBUD, New Delhi etc. and did gather data on an extensive scale to probe into the problems focused.

Findings: It has been found in the research work that the lack of three vital aspects-namely: Human Resource Management of the Labour force in the informal sector, value orientation of the entrepreneurs and overall forward-looking management system leads to a total chaos that is quantified by the lackadaisical measures taken by the Government for the real development that has yet to take place instead of over-publicized political gimmicks and statistical jugglery to turn the entropy into an ordered system.

A Life Fellow of Indian Academy of Social Sciences and a Member of the Indian Society of Labour Economics, he is a recognized Doctoral Supervisor of the following Universities-Manipur University (the erstwhile Post Graduate Campus of JNU), Imphal, Aligarh Muslim University, U.P. , Vidyasagar University , West Bengal and Amaravati University, Maharastra.
