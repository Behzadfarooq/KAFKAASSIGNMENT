Anacapri is a commune in the Island of Capri, in the province of Naples. It has a separate status from the City of Capri proper. The most important sight in the village is the Villa San Michele.

As opposed to the City of Capri, Anacapri is much cheaper to visit and stay at. The shop of the famed Antonio Viva, whose sandals have graced the feet of Jackie O and other prominent members of society, is located in Anacapri.

The bus you take from the Marina or Capri up to Anacapri is no doubt scary. For Americans who are used to wide roads to accommodate their big cars, be prepared to hold your breath when passing another bus on the edge of the cliffs.
