[edit] Summary

Promotional image of Ana Lucia Cortez played by Michelle Rodriguez in the second season of the Emmy and Golden Globe Award-winning Touchstone Television series on ABC 'LOST' by Grass Skirt and Bad Robot Productions. Photograph taken on August 6th, 2005 and uploaded to ABC Medianet on August 25th, 2005.

[edit] Source

Retrieved from http://gallery.lost-media.com/displayimage.php?album=674&pos=3 on Boxing Day, 2006.

[edit] Fair use rationale

I, thedemonhog, the uploader of this image believe that it is covered by the U.S. fair use laws because:

  • It is of lower resolution than the original image
  • It identifies the subject of the article
  • It was released to promote the character in the show
  • It does not limit the copyright owner's rights to advertise, sell or promote the show in any way
  • It is accompanied by commentary on the character
  • It identifies the character, not the actress, as she appeared in the show

--theDemonHog 21:12, 26 December 2006 (UTC)
