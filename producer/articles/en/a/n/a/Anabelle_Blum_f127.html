Anabelle Blum

Anabelle Blum (born February 4, 1977 in Caracas, Venezuela) is a radio personality and occasional TV presenter and model. She studied acting and singing since early age in the Herzl Bialik school in Caracas. She then graduated in communications from Universidad Catolica Andres Bello with a minor in advertising. Anabelle Blum is very well known in the Venezuelan radio industry for her solid career with more than 10 years of experience.
