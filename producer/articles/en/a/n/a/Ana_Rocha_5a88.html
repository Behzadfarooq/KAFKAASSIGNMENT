Ana Rocha (born December 28, 1984 in Azores, Portugal) is an American female professional wrestler from Boston, Massachusetts. She currently goes under the ring name of Ariel.

[edit] Profile

  • Real Name: Anna Rocha
  • Height: 5 ft 2 in
  • Weight: 130
  • Birthday: December 28, 1984
  • Hometown: Boston, Massachusetts
  • Trained by: Brickhouse Baker, Scott Ashworth, Jose Perez
  • Debut: May 12, 2001 as a manager; July 21, 2001 as a wrestler.

Finishing and signature moves:

  • Ariel Go Round (360 Spin Drop)
  • Dariel (modified Diamond Cutter)


[edit] Championships and accomplishments

  • Defiant Pro Wrestling
  • DPW Women's Championship (1 time) - defeated Mercedes Martinez (February 10, 2007)
  • IWA Mid-South
  • IWA Mid-South/NWA Midwest Women's title (1 time) - defeated Mercedes Martinez and Hayley Skye (January 15, 2005)
  • JWA
  • JWA Women's Championship (1 time) - won an all-girl battle royal (October 26, 2002)
  • South Coast Championship Wrestling
  • SCCW Women's Championship (1 time) - defeated Alicia and Tara Charisma (March 28, 2003)
