Les Très Riches Heures du duc de Berry, Anatomical Man

This is ASTROLOGICAL man too. Look at the signs of the zodiac. They correspond to each part of the body, starting with pisces, the feet, and working up their way to the head, with aries, the ram, that has sacred connotations. the Musée Condé, Chantilly. 15th century.
