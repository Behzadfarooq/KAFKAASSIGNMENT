Anadenanthera colubrina (Vell.) Brenan var. cebil (Griseb.) Altschul is a timber tree native to Caatinga and Cerrado vegetation in Brazil. This plant is also used as a honey plant; medicinal plant; and ornamental plant; especially as an urban tree.
