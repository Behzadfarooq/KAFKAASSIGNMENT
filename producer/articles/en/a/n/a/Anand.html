Anand is an Indian word that means "bliss", "happiness" or "joy."

Anand may refer to:

  • Anand Narayanan, well-known Editor in the Television media in South India. At present, he is with Kairali-People TV in Kerala.
  • Anand (film), the name of a 1970 Bollywood movie directed by Hrishikesh Mukherjee.
  • Anand, Gujarat, a town in Gujarat, India.
  • Anand, one of the clans of Kukhran community who have their origin in Punjab region of the Indian subcontinent
  • Viswanathan Anand, the Indian chess player, an International Grand Master (IGM) born in Chennai (Madras), India.
  • Anand Panyarachun, a former prime minister of Thailand.
  • Dev Anand, a classic Hindi film actor
  • Chetan Anand, Dev Anand's brother and classic Hindi film director/producer
  • Anand Lal Shimpi, the person behind AnandTech
  • Anand (2004 film), 2004 Tollywood film starring Raja and Kamalinee Mukherjee.
  • Murugananthan, Anandha murugan - other names of Lord Murugan, worshipped predominantly in south India
  • Anand Satyanand, Governor-General of New Zealand as of August 2006.
  • J.Anand,Former Formula 3 Racecar Driver
