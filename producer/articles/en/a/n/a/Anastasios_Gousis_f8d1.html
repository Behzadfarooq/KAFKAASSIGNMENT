Anastasios Gousis

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Anastásios Goúsis (born July 7, 1979) is a Greek sprint athlete.

He competed in the 200 m and 400 m in the 2000 Olympics, but did not progress out of the heats in either event. At the 2004 Olympics he made the semi-finals in the 200 m and competed for Greece in the 4 x 400 m relay.

He finished 8th in the 200 m final at the 2006 European Athletics Championships in Gothenburg.
