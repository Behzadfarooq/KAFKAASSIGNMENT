Anuthin Wongsunkakon อนุทิน วงศ์สรรคกร(Bangkok, Thailand 1973), Type designer and Graphic designer. He became a type expert under the guardianship of type design legend Tony Dispigna. Anuthin is also one of the founding partners of Behaviour Group Inc. (1998), a design firm based in Bangkok, Thailand and considered Thailand's first design studio with full typographic service. It offers custom type design and specializes in modern Thai letter form. Anuthin is one of a few Thai type designers in Thai custom type design business. He has been designing Thai custom fonts for major Thai clients like AIS (Thailand's largest mobile phone operator) and Wallpaper* Magazine Thai edition (with Christian Schwartz). He also provided original Thai typeface for MTV Thailand, Men'sHealth magazine Thai edition, Arena magazine Thai edition, SiamSport Publishing PLC and Thai Wattana Panich, two widely known publishing companies in Thailand.

His Latin fonts have been used in local and American magazines such as Blue, Wired and SnowBoarding. Anuthin's font have been used on book covers and consumer products including record covers for Matchbox20 and Five for Fighting. His works has created identity for worldwide company such as Choice Hotel Group (Quality Inn and Comfort Inn). Creative Technology of Singapore enlisted him to design a Bitmap font for use on their products.

His typographic work has been recognized by Type Director Club New York.
