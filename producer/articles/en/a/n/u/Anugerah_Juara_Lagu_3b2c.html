Anugerah Juara Lagu (AJL) (Malay for "Champion of Songs Awards") is a popular annual music competition in Malaysia. It features the best musical and lyrical compositions of each year it is held. Nominees are derived from a list of mostly-Malay songs which have garnered the most public votes in Muzik Muzik throughout the year, and then progress into the semifinals, from which twelve songs â€” four songs from each of three categories (Pop/Rock, Balada, and Irama Malaysia & Etnik Kreatif) will be nominated by a panel of judges to enter the Juara Lagu.

AJL, as it is also known in short form, honours the composers and lyricists of the songs rather than the performing artistes.

[edit] List of champions

The following are for each year of the competition, the winning song, the performer(s), composers and lyricists of the song:

  • 1987 - Menaruh Harapan - Zaiton Sameon; A. Ali; Habsah Hassan
  • 1988 - Kau Kunci Cintaku - Ramlah Ram; Ahmad Nawab; Wielid
  • 1989 - Isabella - Search; Search; Bob Lokman
  • 1990 - Janji Manismu - Aishah; Adam Ahmad; Wan Aishah
  • 1991 - Takdir Dan Waktu - Mega; Rahim Othman; Juwie
  • 1992 - Pada Syurga Di Wajahmu - Nash; Fauzi Marzuki; Bob Lokman
  • 1993 - Teratai Layu Di Tasik Madu - Fauziah Latiff; Adam Ahmad; S. Amin Shahab
  • 1994 - Tanya Sama Itu Hud Hud - M. Nasir; M. Nasir; M. Nasir
  • 1995 - Cinta Beralih Arah - Aishah; Jari; Habsah Hassan
  • 1996 - Jerat Percintaan - Siti Nurhaliza; Adnan Abu Hassan; Hani M. J.
  • 1997 - Ghazal Untuk Rabiah - M. Nasir and Jamal Abdillah; M. Nasir; S. Amin Shahab
  • 1998 - Cindai - Siti Nurhaliza; Pak Ngah; Hairul Anwar Harun
  • 1999 - Andalusia - M. Nasir; M. Nasir; Loloq
  • 2000 - Balqis - Siti Nurhaliza; Pak Ngah; Hairul Anwar Harun
  • 2001 - Mimpi Laila - Yassin; Yassin; Yassin
  • 2002 - Keliru - Nurul and Ajai; Aidit Alfian; Aidit Alfian
  • 2003 - Bunga Bunga Cinta - Misha Omar; Adnan Abu Hassan; Adnan Abu Hassan
  • 2004 - Rela Ku Pujuk - Spider; Tam (band vocalist); Keon
  • 2005 - Gemilang - Jaclyn Victor; Aubrey Suwito; Asmin Mudin
  • 2006 - Terlalu Istimewa - Adibah Noor; Azlan Abu Hassan; Adibah Noor
