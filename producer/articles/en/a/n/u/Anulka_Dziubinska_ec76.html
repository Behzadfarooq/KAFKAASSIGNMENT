Anulka Dziubinska (born December 14, 1950 in Preston, England) was Playboy magazine's Playmate of the Month for May 1973. Her centerfold was photographed by Pompeo Posar. She is sometimes credited as Anulka or Anulka Dubinska.

Of Polish descent, Anulka first appeared in Playboy in the August 1972 "Girls of Munich" pictorial - fully clothed save for a sheer white shirt. She also was a Page Three girl in London. After her Playmate appearance, she became an actress on both film and television. A particularly striking role was as a lesbian vampire in the exploitation classic Vampyres (1974). She has also worked as a restaurateur, a florist and a tree vendor from Scotland to Iran.
