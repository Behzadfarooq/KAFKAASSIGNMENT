Anhalt-Dornburg

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Anhalt-Dornburg was a principality located in Germany. It was created in 1667 following the death of Prince John VI and the partition of Anhalt-Zerbst with Anhalt-Mühlingen being created along with Anhalt-Dornburg for the younger sons of Prince John VI. The principality lasted until 1742 when Princes Christian August and John Louis II inherited Anhalt-Zerbst.

[edit] Princes of Anhalt-Dornburg 1667-1742

  • John Louis I 1667-1704
  • John Louis II 1704-1742
  • Christian August 1704-1742

United with Anhalt-Zerbst in 1742.
