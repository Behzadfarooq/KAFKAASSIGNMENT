Anhui University of Traditional Chinese Medicine (TCM) is a multidisciplinary higher institution with traditional Chinese medicine science as its focus; it also touches the fields of pharmacy, western medicine, nursing, psychology technology, management and economics. The University has become a major base for the higher education of traditional Chinese medicine, medical service and scientific research in Anhui province.

It has 7 schools offering 3 key courses of the provincial level, 1 key course of the national level,16 Master’s degree-awarding spots,4 bases for cultivating the doctorial students cooperating with other medical universities, 6 hi-tech TCM research institutes, 1 key laboratory of the provincial level, 2 affiliated hospitals of the provincial level,1 TCM clinic and 1 hospital specializing in the combination of Chinese and Western medicine. It has 22 majors leading to a Bachelor’s degree, such as Traditional Chinese Medicine, pharmacy, MBBS, nursing, Acupuncture, Osteology, Rehabilitation,etc. There are nearly 8,000 students in the University. It has a qualified faculty, 300 of whom are professors and associate professors and 100 of whom are entitled to supervise students studying for a Master’s or a doctral degree. There are more than 300,000 volumes of books in the library, which has an e- reading room with 200 booths. The website of the University has been connected to China Education and Research Network and the Internet. The Exhibition Hall of Xin’an Medical Thought School, the Sample Center of Chinese Herbs, the Plantation of Medical Herbs, the TCM Clinic with famous Chinese physicians, and the Storing Room of Ancient Chinese Medical Books are all visited by overseas guests more than 1,000 times per year.

Set up in 1959, the University has inherited the two outstanding medical traditions in Anhui Province. One is from the southern part of Anhui, which is called Xin’an; The other one is from the northern part which is called Huatuo. Through 46 years of development since its founding, the University has grown into a mature higher education institution which not only offers undergraduate and graduate education but also offers continuing and international education. At present it has created thousands of advanced medical talents, who have spread over all the China and the world, who function as the cornerstone in the everyday practice, anagement and academic study in their respective fields.

With the coming of worldwide trends to studying Chinese medicine, Chinese pharmacy, Acupuncture and Moxibustion, the University’s external exchanges and cooperation are booming. In recent years, the University has signed scores of cooperative agreements with overseas higher education institutions including a cooperation agreement with Canadian Institute of Postgraduate Studies (Canada) and a student exchange agreement with Hanseo University (Korea). Other foreign partners include Hong Kong University, University of East-West Medicine (California, USA), The Medical School of Columbia University (USA), New York Traditional Chinese Medicine Research Institute (USA), Jamaican Cultural Center of Chinese Medicine (Jamaica), Sydney University of Traditional Chinese Medicine (Australia), International University of Traditional Chinese Medicine of Vancouver (Canada), Omega Institute of International Studies (Nepal) and etc. Since 1996, when the University was authorized to enroll international students, and 1997, when to enroll the students from the districts of Hong Kong, Macau and Taiwan, there have been hundreds of overseas students from dozens of countries and regions having studied at our University. In 2004, six Singaporean students at our University successfully passed their thesis defense for a Master’s degree and obtained it. At present, nearly a hundred international students and those from the districts of Hong Kong, Macau and Taiwan are pursuing their Bachelor’s or Master’s Degree here. There are also many international students coming for their clinical observations and internships each year. Through ten years’ practice of international cooperation and education, the University has accumulated abundant experience in international exchanges and education. Gradually a competitive faculty, which is professional in educating international students, has formed. The University also created a sound campus service system and a high-efficient school administration for the students. As a result, more and more international students and visitors are attracted to come here from the entire world. The University has become an important base for the international exchanges and education in Anhui, even in whole China.
