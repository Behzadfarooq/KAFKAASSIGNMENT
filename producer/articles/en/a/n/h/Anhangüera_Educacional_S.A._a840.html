Anhangüera Educacional S.A. is a private educational holding company with headquarters in Valinhos, in the region of Campinas, state of São Paulo, Brazil. The main shareholder (60%) is Banco Pátria, a Brazilian investment bank, with funds provided by the International Finance Corporation (IFC), the private arm of World Bank.

The company maintains colleges in the following cities:

  • Campinas (4 campuses)
  • Valinhos
  • Vinhedo
  • Jundiai
  • Santa Bárbara d'Oeste
  • Limeira
  • Leme
  • Pirassununga
  • Matão
  • Taubaté
  • São José dos Campos
  • Sorocaba
  • Piracicaba
  • Indaiatuba
  • Rio Claro
