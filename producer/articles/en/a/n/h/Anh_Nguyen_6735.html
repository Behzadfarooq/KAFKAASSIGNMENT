[edit] Biography

Anh was born and raised in San Gabriel Valley, California. He attended Sierra Vista High School in Baldwin Park, California and Citrus College in Azusa, California.

[edit] Minor Roles

Anh so far has been only minor characters in two films. In Americanese he played the guy in the park and in Freedom Writers he plays Sindy's boyfriend (Jaclyn Ngan).

[edit] Quotes
