Anhellica (born 1981) is a female resident of Montreal, Quebec. She is a full time employee of Aquarius Records, and a part time DJ on CJLO.

Her weekly show BVST is in its sixth year, and plays "the best and the worst in rock and roll, country, punk and metal". It airs every Wednesday from 7 to 9 PM EST.

Anhellica enjoys rock and roll in all its various forms. She spends her free time working on graphic design, playing videogames, and doing online research, for which Wikipedia is an obviously invaluable tool.
