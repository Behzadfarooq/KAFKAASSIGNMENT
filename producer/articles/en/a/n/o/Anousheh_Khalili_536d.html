Anousheh Khalili

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Anousheh Khalili performing live on 25 June 2006

Anousheh Khalili is a Richmond, Virginia-based singer/songwriter of Persian background. She is known for providing vocal talent and appearing in the videos for Deep Dish's "Flashdance" and "Say Hello", the latter of which was nominated for a Grammy in 2006 in the Best Dance Recording category.

She recorded her debut album, Let the Ground Know Who's Standing on Him, under the supervision of Triple Stamp Records. Her piano and vocal skills have been compared to the likes of Fiona Apple and Neko Case.[1]
