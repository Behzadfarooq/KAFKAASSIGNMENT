Anouk Leblanc-Boucher (born on October 21, 1984 in Prévost, Quebec) is a Canadian Short track speed skating athlete at the 2006 Winter Olympics.

She won a bronze medal in the 500m short track speed skating event on February 15 at the Winter Olympics. She finished ahead of fellow Canadian Kalyna Roberge with a time of 0:44.759.

On February 22, along with Alanna Kraus, Tania Vicent, Amanda Overland and Kalyna Roberge, Leblanc-Boucher helped win a silver medal for Canada in the women's 3000m relay.
