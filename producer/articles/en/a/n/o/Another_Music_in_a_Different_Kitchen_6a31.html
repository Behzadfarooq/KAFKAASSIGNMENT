Another Music in a Different Kitchen was Buzzcocks first album, released in 1978 and includes the hit single "I Don't Mind" which reached No. 55 in the UK chart in May 1978. This was the second line-up of the Buzzcocks, with Pete Shelley singing, following the departure of original leader Howard Devoto.

The album's second track, No Reply, was covered by SS Decontrol on their 1983 EP Get It Away. The third track, You Tear Me Up, is one of the earliest examples of the D-beat punk subgenre.
