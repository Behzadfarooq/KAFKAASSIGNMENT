[edit] Answers to your questions

I take it that you must be taking some sort of poll, eh?

Question 1. Are you an Administrator?

No, I am not.

Question 2a. If you are when did you become an administrator?

As above, I am not an administrator.

Question 2b. If you're not has anyone nominated you to become one?

I'm not aware of having been nominated.

Question 3. Would you like to become an administrator someday?

At this point in my wikilife, no. While it would be nice to have the added capabilities, I don't currently want to have the added responsibilities that come along with them. I don't want to shut the door permanently on ever being an administrator, but I don't see myself seeking the position anytime soon.

Now, if you don't mind, may I ask you if you picked me for your poll randomly, or if you thought I might be interested in becoming an admin, or something else? I'm just curious. --Tachikoma 13:33, 12 May 2006 (UTC)
