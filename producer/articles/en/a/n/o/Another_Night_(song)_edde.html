"Another Night" is a dance song by Real McCoy from their album Another Night. It was one of their biggest hits, reaching Number Three in the American Billboard Hot 100 Singles chart and Number Two on the Official U.K. Singles chart. The single was certified Platinum in America, spending 40 weeks in the Top 40, and became the best-selling single by a German act in American rock history.
