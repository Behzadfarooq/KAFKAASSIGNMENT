Anouck Lepere (born February 15, 1980 in Antwerp, Belgium) is a Belgian supermodel. Anouck was in Antwerp, studying architecture when she was persuaded to try modelling by friends Dries van Noten and Olivier Theyskens. She first appeared on the runways of Paris in 2000 and is currently signed with IMG Models. She had also appeared as a model for the Hugo Boss "Woman" and "Deep Red" perfume. She has worked with Steven Meisel, Mario Testino, Ines van Lamsweerde and Vinoodh Matadin. She has been on the covers of Vogue, Harper's Bazaar, and Marie Claire. She also has her own line of jewelery.

Since 2005, she has been dating Jefferson Hack, father of Kate Moss' daughter Lila Grace.
