"Another Stranger Me" is a single taken from their 2006 album, A Twist In The Myth. It features two previously unreleased tracks: "All The King’s Horses" and "Dream A Little Dream Of Me" and it also contains the video for "Another Stranger Me"

[edit] Track listing

  1. "Another Stranger Me" - 04:35
  2. "All The King’s Horses" - 04:11
  3. "Dream A Little Dream Of Me" - 03:22
  4. "Lionheart (Demo Version)" - 04:10
  5. "The Edge (Demo Version)"- 04:27
