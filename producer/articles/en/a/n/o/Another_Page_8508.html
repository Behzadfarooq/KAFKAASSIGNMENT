Another Page is Christopher Cross' second album, recorded in 1983.

All songs by Christopher Cross, except Deal 'Em Again, by Christopher Cross and Michael Maben.

  7. "Talking In My Sleep" - 3:34
  8. "Nature Of The Game" - 3:55
  9. "Long World" - 3:32
  10. "Words Of Wisdom" - 5:52
