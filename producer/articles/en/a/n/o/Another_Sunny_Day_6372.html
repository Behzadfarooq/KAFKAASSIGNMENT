Another Sunny Day was a London-based indie band on Sarah Records, best known for the somewhat Smiths-esque single "You Should All Be Murdered" and for the single whose title perhaps best epitomises the Sarah Records output "I'm In Love With A Girl Who Doesn't Know I Exist". They recorded one album, London Weekend, which was released by Sarah. The frontman, Harvey Williams, later went on to record several albums under his own name.
