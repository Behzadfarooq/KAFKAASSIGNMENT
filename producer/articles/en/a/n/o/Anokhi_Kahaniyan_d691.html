Anokhi Kahaniyan's Title (June 2006)

Anokhi Kahaniyan is a popular children's magazine in Pakistan. It is published and edited by Mehboob Elahi Makhmoor since 1991. First issue of Anokhi Kahaniyan comes out on 10th August 1991. First three issues was jointly edited by Mr. Mustufa Hashmi, Mr. Rauf Aslam Araeen, Shahid Ali Sahar and Mehboob Elahi Makhmoor. Later in December 1991, whole responsibilty of Anokhi Kahaniyan was taken by Mr. Elahi Bax Chiti, Noor Muhammad Hashmi and Mehboob Elahi Makhmoor. Late Mr. Elahi Bax Chiti, father of Mehboob Elahi Makhmoor, had a great contribution in establishment of Anokhi Kahaniyan. In early stages, Anokhi Kahaniyan suffers alots of finacial problems but Mr. Bax do not let his son to worry about money. At this time, Anokhi Kahaniyan is in the ranking of the most popular children' magazines of Pakistan. Many popular writer for Children's litrature had start there writing from Anokhi Kahaniyan.
