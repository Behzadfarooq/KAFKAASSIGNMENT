Another World (Gerry Rafferty album)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Another World
Studio album by Gerry Rafferty
Released 2003
Genre Rock

Another World is the eighth studio album by Gerry Rafferty. The album was released in 2003 and got good reviews by critics and fans. It did not sound the same as his 1970s/1980s work, but still produced some great songs. "Keep It To Yourself" was released a single in Europe and the UK.
