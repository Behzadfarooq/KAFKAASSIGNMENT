Attacks attributed to the Abu Nidal Organization

An asterix means the attack was claimed by Black September
but is nevertheless believed to have been the ANO.

Sep 05, 1973 : Occupied the Saudi Embassy, Paris.
Sep 26, 1976: Occupied the Semiramis Hotel, Damascus.
Oct 11, 1976: Attacks Syrian embassies, Islamabad, Rome.
Nov 17, 1976: Attack, Intercontinental Hotel, Amman.
Dec 13, 1976: Assassination attempt, Syrian foreign minister,
Damascus; failed attack on the Syrian Embassy, Istanbul.
Oct 25, 1977: Assassinaation attempt, Syrian foreign
minister, Abu Dhabi; UAE minister killed.
Jan 04, 1978 : Assassination: Said Hammami, PLO, London.
Feb 18, 1978: Assassination: journalist Youssef al-Seba'i.
Jun 15, 1978: Assassination: Ali Yassin, PLO, Kuwait.
Aug 03, 1978 : Assassination: Izz al-Din al-Kalak, PLO rep.
in Paris and his assistant.
Aug 05, 1978 : Attack on PLO offices, Islamabad.
Jan 17, 1980: Assassination: Yussouf Mubarak, director of
the Palestinian library-shop, Paris.
Jul 27, 1980: Attack on Jewish school, Antwerpen.
Assassination: Israeli commercial attaché, Brussels.
May 01, 1981 : Assassination: Heinz Nittel, President of the
Austrian-Israeli Friendship Association, Vienna.
Jun 01, 1981 : Assassination: Naim Khader, PLO, Brussels.
Aug 29, 1981: Machine-gun attack, Vienna synagogue.
Jun 03, 1982 : Attempted assassination: Shlomo Argov
Israeli amb to the UK; triggers Israeli invasion of Lebanon.
Aug 09, 1982 : Machine-gun attack: Jewish restaurant, Paris.
Aug 26, 1982: Attempted assassination: UAE consul, Bombay;
attempted assassination: Kuwaiti diplomat in Karachi.
Sep 16, 1982: Assassination: Kuwaiti diplomat, Madrid.
Sep 19, 1982: Attack on a synagogue in Brussels.
Oct 09, 1982 : Child killed and ten adults injured in
attack on the central synagogue in Rome.
Apr 10, 1983: Assassination: Issam Sartawi, PLO, Lisbon.
Attempted assassination: Jordanian ambassador to Italy.
Oct 25, 1983: Assassination: Jordanian ambassador, New Delhi.
Oct 26, 1983: Assassination: Jordanian ambassador, Rome.
Nov 07, 1983 : Assassination: guard, Jordanian embassy, Athens.
Dec 1983  : Bombing: French cultural center, Izmir, Turkey.
Dec 29, 1983: Assassination: Jordanian ambassador, Madrid.
Feb 08, 1984 : Assassination: UAE ambassador, Paris.
Mar 1984  : Assassination: British diplomat, Athens.
Mar 24, 1984: Bomb found: Intercontinental Hotel, Amman
during a visit of Queen Elizabeth II.
Jun 05, 1984 : Attempted assassination: Isr diplomat, Cairo.
Oct 27, 1984: Attempted assassination: UAE diplomat, Rome.
Nov 27, 1984: Assassination: Br. High Commissioner, Bombay.
Nov 29, 1984: Bombing: British Airways offices, Beirut.
Attempted assassination: Jordanian diplomat, Athens.
Dec 04, 1984 : Assassination: Jordanian diplomat, Bucharest.*
Dec 26, 1984: Bombing: PLO Hani al-Hassan's home, Amman.*
Dec 29, 1984: Assassination: Fahed Kawasmeh, PLO, Amman.*
Mar 21, 1985: Bombings: Royal Jordanian Airlines (ALIA)
offices in Rome, Athens, and Nicosia.*
Mar 25, 1985: Kidnap in Beirut of British journalist Alec Collett.
Collett's fate was never determined.
Apr 03, 1985 : Rockets fired at ALIA airliner, Athens.
Apr 03, 1985 : Rockets fired against Jordanian embassy, Rome.
Jul 22, 1985: Failed bombing: American embassy, Cairo.
Jul 1985  : Bombing: British Airways office in Madrid;
1 dead, 27 injured. Attack on ALIA offices; 2 injured.
Grenade attack against the Café de Paris, Rome; 38 injured.
Nov 23, 1985: Hijacking of an Egyptian plane to Malta; 66 die
during rescue attempt by Egyptian military forces.
Dec 27, 1985: Major attacks on Rome and Vienna airports;
18 dead, 120 injured.
Sep 05, 1986 : Attempted hijack: PA 73, Karachi, 22 die.
Sep 06, 1986 : Attacks Neve-Shalom synagogue, Istanbul; 22 die.
Nov 1987  : Hijack of a yacht; Belgian occupants taken to Libya.
Mar 1988  : Gunman attacks Alitalia airlines crew, Bombay.
May 1988  : Attacks on Acropole Hotel and Sudan Club,
Khartoum, eight dead, 21 injured.
Jul 1988  : Gunmen board the City of Poros;
nine die, 98 injured.
Dec 21, 1988: Suspected involvement in bombing of
PA 103 over Lockerbie, Scotland; 270 die.
Jan 14, 1991: Assassination: Abu Iyad, No. 2, PLO
and Abu el-Hol leader, Fatah's Western sector forces, Tunis.
