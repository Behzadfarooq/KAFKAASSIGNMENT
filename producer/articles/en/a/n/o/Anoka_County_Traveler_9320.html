Anoka County Traveler

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Anoka County Traveler is a system in Minnesota, United States operated for the Met Council by a private company. It currently has two routes (805 and 831). These routes currently serve Blaine, Minnesota, Coon Rapids, Minnesota, and Anoka, Minnesota.
