another mind is the debut release from Hiromi Uehara, a jazz and jazz fusion pianist. It was released in 2003 and received the award for foreign jazz album of the year in in the 2004 Japan Annual Gold Disc Awards.

  2. "ダブル・パーソナリティ" (Double Personality)
  3. "サマー・レイン" (Summer Rain)

  5. "010101（バイナリー・システム）"(010101 - Binary System)
  6. "トゥルース・アンド・ライズ" (Truth and Lies)
