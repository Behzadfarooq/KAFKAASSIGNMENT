Anorrhinus is a genus of the Bucerotidae Family (hornbills). Species included in the genus are:

  • Brown Hornbill, Anorrhinus austeni
  • Rusty-cheeked Hornbill, Anorrhinus tickelli
  • Bushy-crested Hornbill, Anorrhinus galeritus

Anorrhinus tickelli was split into A. tickelli and A. austeni following Rasmussen and Anderton (2005).
