Anonymus (band)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

You have new messages (last change).

Anonymus is a Quebec speed metal band founded in 1989. They are known for their collaboration on the album L'Académie du Massacre featuring songs by Quebec satirical singer Mononc' Serge.

[edit] Members

  • Carlos Araya (drums)
  • Daniel Souto (guitar)
  • Oscar Souto (bass and vocals)
  • Jef Fortin (guitar)

[edit] Discography

  • Ni vu, ni connu (1994)
  • Stress (1997)
  • Instinct (1999)
  • Daemonium (2002)
  • L'Académie du Massacre (with Serge Robert) (2003)
  • Chapter Chaos Begins (2006)
