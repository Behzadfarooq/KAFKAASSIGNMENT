[edit] Welcome!

Welcome to Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia! You don't have to log in to read or edit articles on Wikipedia, but you may wish to create an account. It is free, requires no personal information, and provides many benefits. Your IP address will no longer be visible to other users, and you will be able to:

  • Monitor changes to pages of interest with your own watchlist;
  • Create new pages;
  • Choose your own username, provided it is appropriate;
  • Customize the appearance and behavior of the website;
  • Edit semi-protected pages;
  • Upload images;
  • Rename pages; and
  • Participate fully in Articles for deletion and other Wikipedia discussions.

Feel free to ask me any questions you may have on my talk page. By the way, remember to sign and date your comments with four tildes (~~~~).
