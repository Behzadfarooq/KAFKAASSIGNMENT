"Another Brick in the Wall" is a cover song featured on Korn's Greatest Hits, Volume 1, consisting of several songs (all three parts of Another Brick in the Wall and Goodbye Cruel World from Pink Floyd's The Wall). Another Brick In The Wall was a regular part of Korn's setlist in 2004 and 2005. It was a highlight of the shows, played before the encores, with Jonathan Davis carrying a torch in the beginning. Korn's cover of Another Brick In The Wall was also released on "Word Up!" maxi-single in live version and on compilation titled "Live and Rare" (released through Sony BMG on May 9, 2006. There was also video for this song which was live performance at Projekt Revolution Tour 2004, directed by Bill Yukich, and released on November 12, 2004.
