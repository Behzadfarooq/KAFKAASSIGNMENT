Anonymous is the 3rd album by Tomahawk. It is set to be released on June 19th, 2007, Ipecac Recordings. The album is said to have more of an organic, stripped vibe, abandoning the traditional Tomahawk sound. This will also be the first Tomahawk album without Kevin Rutmanis, as he left the band during the time of making Anonymous[citation needed]. Reasons for his departure are unknown, and also who his replacement will be.
