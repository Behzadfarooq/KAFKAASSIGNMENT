Another Gay Movie is a 2006 feature film, directed by Todd Stephens, humorously exploring gay coming of age through the lives of four male friends. Its plot is largely a parody of American Pie.

[edit] Homages

This film gives homage to a number of movies, with particular emphasis on the entire genre of gay coming out movies

[edit] American Pie

  • The boys make a pact to have anal sex.
  • Someone tries to have sex with food after being told anal sex feels like quiche.
  • The experienced friend is named Muffler instead of Stifler. This character is complete with a younger teen version.
  • Instead of having sex with Muffler's mom, a character has sex with her grandfather.

[edit] Mommie Dearest

  • At the beginning of the film, John Epperson goes to the closet and sees a wire hanger in the closet and she cringes, then gains her composure. This is a reference to the film Mommie Dearest starring Faye Dunaway.
  • There is a Faye Dunaway autograph.
  • Another reference is made when John Epperson quotes a famous line from Mommie Dearest "I'm not mad at you, I'm mad at the crumbs!" and starts shaking the bleach disinfectant cleanser everywhere.

[edit] Queer as Folk

  • Andy says "Queer as Folk, eat your fucking heart out!"
  • Stephanie McVay comes out dressed as Sharon Gless's Queer as Folk character.

[edit] Edge of Seventeen

  • Actress Stephanie McVay reprises her role as the clueless mother from Edge of Seventeen

[edit] Broken Hearts Club (2000 USA)

  • Entire softball scene including the batter picking up on a member of the other team (character played by Dean Cain in Broken Hearts Club)

[edit] Get Real (1999 British)

  • Public restroom glory hole scene
  • Swimming pool scene

[edit] Adam & Steve

  • Entire scene with Richard Hatch and an unfortunately-timed bowel movement

[edit] Beautiful Thing (British)

  • The dancing scene during the final credits is a direct reference to the last scene of Beautiful Thing.

[edit] Trick

  • (deleted scene) The bathroom scene with the drag queen talking to the boy at the urinal is taken from the movie Trick, just with different dialog

[edit] Trivia

  • The theme song, "Another Gay Sunshine Day," is sung by gay icon Nancy Sinatra.
  • The primary setting for the film, San Torum High School, is a play on the neologism "santorum" stemming from the Santorum controversy.
  • Gay porn actor Matthew Rush and television presenter Graham Norton have cameos in the film.
  • The mascot for the High School is the San Torum Donkeys.

[edit] Sequel

A sequel for the movie, entitled Another Gay Movie: Gays Gone Wild has been announced and is scheduled for release in Spring 2007, featuring many of the original cast members from the first film.[1]
