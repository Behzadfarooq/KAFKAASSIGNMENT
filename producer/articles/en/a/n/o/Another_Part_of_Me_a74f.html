"Another Part of Me" was a popular hit for singer Michael Jackson. Released in 1988, it broke the streak of the five #1 songs Jackson achieved on the pop charts with the song peaking at #11 pop although it hit #1 on the Billboard R&B Singles Chart. The song was originally from Jackson's Captain EO 3D film made in 1986. It was not intended to go on the Bad album, but producer Quincy Jones decided to replace the song "Street Walker" with it. The latter of the songs was later released on the 2001 special edition re-release of Bad.
