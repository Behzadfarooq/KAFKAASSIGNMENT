Another Side is the debut album from High School Musical singer Corbin Bleu. It will be released by Hollywood Records on May 1, 2007. The album will be released after the debut of his DCOM movie Jump In!, but before High School Musical 2 premeires. His first single off the album, "Push It to the Limit", was recorded and will appear on the soundtrack for Jump In!. Bleu has stated that the album will have a "pop-R&B feel". Vanessa Hudgens has been confirmed as a featured artist on the track called "Still There For Me".

Bleu shot the music video for the album's official first single Deal With It on March 16, 2007. The video was directed by Lenny Bass and was choreographed by Laurie Ann Gibson.[1] A shortened version of the music video premiered on Disney Channel on April 8, 2007.
