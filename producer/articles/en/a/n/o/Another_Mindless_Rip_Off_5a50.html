Another Mindless Rip Off is the newest EP by New York punk/industrial band Mindless Self Indulgence. It was released exclusively through Hot Topic stores and Hot Topic's website. The original release was limited edition and only 15,000 copies were printed. Many stores sold out within days, however some locations still have copies in stock. On it are five new songs as well as five remixes of the song "What Do They Know?" from their album You'll Rebel to Anything.

[edit] Track listing

  1. "My World" – 3:19
  2. "Pre-Teen Violence" – 3:15
  3. "Frying Pan" – 2:48
  4. "Lush" – 2:37
  5. "Born To Be Beheaded" – 2:12
  6. "What Do They Know? – Mindless Self Indulgence vs. Julien-K & Chester Bennington" – 5:26
  7. "What Do They Know? – ProCon (Backstabber's Delight Mix)" – 4:37
  8. "What Do They Know? – The Perfects (Hollowboy Extended Pleasure Mix)" – 5:22
  9. "What Do They Know? – VNV Nation (Maelstrom Mix)" – 5:42
  10. "What Do They Know? – Kenny Muhammad, The Human Orchestra (The Final Word Mix)" – 2:25

[edit] Personnel

  • Little Jimmy Urine – Lead Vocals, Programming
  • Steve, Righ? – Lead Guitar, Backup Vocals
  • Lyn-Z – Bass Guitar
  • Kitty – Drums

Although the persons listed above are indeed the members of the band, the 5 new tracks on this EP were created by Urine & Galus (the James sisters), with Righ? only contributing to track 4.
