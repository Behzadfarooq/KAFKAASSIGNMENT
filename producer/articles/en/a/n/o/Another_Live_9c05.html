Another Live (released in 1975) is the second album released by Utopia, as well as their first fully live album.

[edit] Track listing

  1. "Another Life" (Rundgren/Shuckett) - 7:37
  2. "The Wheel" (Rundgren) - 7:04
  3. "The Seven Rays" (Rundgren, Siegler) - 8:52
  4. "Intro/Mister Triscuits" (Powell) - 5:27
  5. "Something's Coming" (Bernstein/Sondheim) - 2:51
  6. "Heavy Metal Kids" (Rundgren) - 4:16
  7. "Do Ya" (Lynne) - 4:12
  8. "Just One Victory" (Rundgren) - 5:37
