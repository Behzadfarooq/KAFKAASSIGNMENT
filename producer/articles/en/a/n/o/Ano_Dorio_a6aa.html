Ano Dorio, or Ano Dorion, is a settlement in the prefecture of Messinia, Greece. According to Athens2004.com, Ano Dorio is close to the larger town of Dorio, and has a population of 192[1] [].

An essay posted on the website of the town's gymnasium, describes the participation of the locals in the Greek War of Independence. [2].
