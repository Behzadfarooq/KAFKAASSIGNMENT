If you have a follow up question use {{helpme}} again an we will return.--Commander Keane 13:34, 5 April 2006 (UTC)
Thanks Anonymous anonymous 13:37, 5 April 2006 (UTC)

To delete pages, you need to be an admin. If an article is clearly junk, use the speedy deletion tag, and it will soon be picked up by an admin, if it's arguable, use AfD, jimfbleak 14:03, 5 April 2006 (UTC)

[edit] Admin

I thought that might be the next question! You need to establish a track record as a reliable and trustworthy editor. There's no fixed period, but as a new contributor tou will need to get in at least a few weeks' more contributions. You then ask an admin to nominate you (or they may suggest it), preferably someone who works on similar articles and will know your edits, and then the nomination will be listed to give people to say yea or nay. jimfbleak 14:14, 5 April 2006 (UTC)
