Another View is a five track EP by Maureen Tucker. It was originally released on 12" vinyl.

[edit] Tracklisting

  1. "I'm Sticking With You" (Lou Reed}
    • from the Around And Around / Will You Love Me Tomorrow? single.
  2. "Will You Love Me Tomorrow" (Gerry Goffin, Carole King)
    • from the Around And Around / Will You Love Me Tomorrow? single (without tambourine track).
  3. "Foggy Notion" (Reed)
    • Mix 2, new vocal. Recorded in 1977 in Arlington, re-mixed in Newton during 1978, and finally remixed on March 1, 1985. Features Willie Alexander on backing vocals , John JHovorka of The Turbines on bass and Paul Robinson on drums.
  4. "Foggy Notion" (Reed)
    • Recorded live on December 27, 1978 at The Club, Cambridge, Massachusetts.
  5. "Foggy Notion" (Reed)
    • Recorded live on November 25, 1979 at Paradise in Boston, New York.
