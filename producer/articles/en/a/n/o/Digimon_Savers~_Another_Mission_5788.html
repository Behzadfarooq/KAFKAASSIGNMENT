Digimon Savers: Another Mission (デジモンセイバーズ アナザーミッション Dejimon Seibāzu Anazā Misshon) is a Digimon video game for the PlayStation 2. The game is set in the Digimon Savers universe, and revolves around the Seven Great Demon Lords. In this game the player will be able to control the four main Savers characters, Masaru, Tohma, Yoshino, and Ikuto along with their respective Digimon partners. The graphics for this game are cel-shaded and the battle system is similar to the Battle Terminal. Also this game will be classified as a "special genre", called a "Dramatic RPG", which means that the Digimon partners are effected by how you, the player, treat them, the way your Digimon evolves will be similar to Digimon World, the Digimon will be affected by how you take care of it, and depending on that digivolves into different types Digimon, in Digimon Savers: Another Mission, a new type of digivolution method is used, called the Galactica Evolution System, this will determine what Digimon your partner will evolve into. There will also be new, original characters for this game only, meaning that these characters are exclusive to DSAM, and will not appear in any other Digimon Savers media, which includes the anime, games, etc., they are Kagura Yuma, who has a Renamon as her partner, Katsura Kosaburo, who has an extremely clumsy, female Biyomon as his partner, Kagura Tsukasa, who is Yuma's older brother, and the new DATS technician, who graduated from the same academy as Tohma, Nitta Masaki, who is said to be apart of DATS' past, but is currently missing, and Nitta Manami, Masaki's daughter.
