Anon Optics (pronounced 'an-nun') is a ski and snowboard goggle brand owned by Burton Snowboards of the U.S. state of Vermont. Although they were initially produced in the Smith Optics factory, they are now produced in Burton's factory. Currently the produce goggles and sunglasses

[edit] Goggles

Anon currently produces four different models of goggles. Each model comes in a variety of frames, lenses, and bands.

  • Realm
  • Figment
  • Subrosa
  • Majestic (Ladies)

[edit] Sunglasses

Anon came out with a line of sunglasses May 11, 2005. They currently have twelve different models. Each model comes in a variety of different frames and lenses.

  • Allie
  • Amos
  • Convict
  • Crusher
  • Indie
  • Josie
  • Legion
  • Luce
  • Rico
  • Rufus
  • Truth
  • Pimpin Rufus (Brian Boesen Pro Model)
  • Pimpin Rico (Mason Miller Remix)
