"Another Place to Fall" is a song by Scottish singer-songwriter KT Tunstall and is featured on her debut album, Eye to the Telescope. It was released 13 March 2006 as the final single from that album, charting at #52 in the UK Singles Chart but could have been higher if it had been one of the first tracks released from the album (see 2006 in British music).
