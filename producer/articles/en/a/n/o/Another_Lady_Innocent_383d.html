Another Lady Innocent (フロントイノセント, Front Innocent?) is the title of an H anime directed by Satoshi Urushihara in 2004, based on his artbook "Lady Innocent" and was released in Japan under the title "Front Innocent".

The protagonist of this anime is the young woman Faye, who had returned to her plantation home where she discovers her friends John and the maid Sophie had become lovers. Intrigued, Faye eagerly joins the two. But soon it becomes apparent that an unscrupulous landowner has his eyes on Faye as well. How will she be able to cope with this new interloper?
