Ansu Martin Sesay (born July 29, 1976 in Greensboro, North Carolina) is an American professional basketball player, formerly in the National Basketball Association (NBA). He now plays for Italian club Eldo Napoli.

He was selected 30th overall by the Dallas Mavericks in the 1998 NBA Draft and went on to play for the Seattle SuperSonics and the Golden State Warriors from 2002â€“2005.

[edit] Amateur career

Sesay attended Willowridge High School in Houston, Texas.Lead his team to school's first state title in basketball. He graduated from the University of Mississippi in 1998.[1] He averaged 13 points per game on 42% field goal shooting.

[edit] Trivia

Ansu Sesay also played as an "import" in the Philippine Basketball Association.
