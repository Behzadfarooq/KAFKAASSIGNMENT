  • Anna Nicole Smith
  • ANS (box), a box set from the British band Coil
  • ANS (CD single), a single by the British band Coil
  • ANS synthesizer, a Russian photoelectric musical instrument

  • Armée Nationale Sihanoukiste, a Cambodian resistance group
  • Astronomische Nederlandse Satelliet, a Dutch X-ray satellite
  • Autonomic nervous system
