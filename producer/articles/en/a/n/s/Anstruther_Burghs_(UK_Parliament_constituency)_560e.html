Anstruther Burghs was a district of burghs constituency of the House of Commons of the Parliament of Great Britain from 1708 to 1801 and of the Parliament of the United Kingdom from 1801 to 1832.

[edit] Boundaries

The constituency comprised the burghs of Anstruther Easter and Wester, Pittenweem, Crail and Kilrenny, in Fife.

In 1832, the burghs were combined with Cupar and St Andrews, which were previously components of Perth Burghs, to form St Andrews Burghs.
