Anssi Kippo

Anssi Kippo (born 1976) is a Finnish producer that founded Astia Studios in Finland. He has produced and engineered numerous acts including Behexen, Children of Bodom, Horna, Impaled Nazarene, Insomnium, Norther and Twilightning.
