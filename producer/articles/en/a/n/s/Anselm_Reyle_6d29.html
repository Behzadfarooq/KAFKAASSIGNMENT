Anselm Reyle (born 1970, Tubingen, Germany) is an artist based in Berlin.

He has shown work in many exhibitions including “Expanded Painting” at the 2nd Prague Biennial [1], “Minimalism and After III” at Daimler-Chrysler Contemporary [2] in Berlin and “Sculpture Now!” at Galerie Michael Neff [3] in Frankfurt. Reyle has shown internationally at museums and galleries such as Kunsthalle Zurich, the Los Angeles Museum of Contemporary Art and Centro Cultural Andratx [4]. He is represented by Galerie Giti Nourbaksch [5] in Berlin, Gavin Brown’s Enterprise [6] in New York and Andersen_s Contemporary Art [7] in Copenhagen.
