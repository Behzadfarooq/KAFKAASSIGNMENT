[edit] Summary

Español: Entrega del premio

Description: A symbolic $10 million check is presented to the winners of the Ansari X Prize on 6 November 2004.

Caption: Representatives of the Ansari X Prize Foundation symbolically present the ten million dollar prize to Burt Rutan and Paul Allen of Mojave Aerospace Ventures. The prize trophy is on the left.

Persons on the dais are, left to right: Erik Lindbergh, Richard C. D. Fleming, Burt Rutan, Gregg Maryniak, Paul Allen, Robert Weiss, Dr. Peter Diamandis. Also present on the dais but hidden in this view: Amir Ansari
