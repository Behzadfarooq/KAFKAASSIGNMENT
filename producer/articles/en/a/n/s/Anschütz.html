J. G. Anschütz is a firearms company based in Ulm, Germany, that makes small-bore rifles (.17, .22, .222 Rem. caliber, and .177 air rifles and air pistols) as well as small-bore shotguns, for target shooting and hunting. Their rifles are used by many competitive shooters participating in the 50 m Rifle event at the Summer Olympics and Winter Olympics biathlon.

The ahg-Anschütz company (a "twin" company from J. G. Anschütz) is mostly dedicated to the international trade of shooting accessories like shooting jackets and boots.

J. G. Anschütz company is also the major shareholder of the Austrian target weapons manufacturer Steyr Sportwaffen GmbH.
