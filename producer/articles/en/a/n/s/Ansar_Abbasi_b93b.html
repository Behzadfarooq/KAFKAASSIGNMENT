Ansar Abbasi is an Investigative editor for one of the leading newspapers from Pakistan, The News International.

He has been involved in a recent debate with Pakistan Law Minister Muhammad Wasi Zafar, where the law minister started abusing him on live radio on the controversial removal of the Chief Justice of Pakistan (Justice Iftikhar Chaudhry)

Ansar has taken great risks to expose the true calibre of the current regime under Pervez Musharraf in Pakistan, and has put himself in a position of grave risk to ensure that the correct information is relayed to the people of Pakistan.
