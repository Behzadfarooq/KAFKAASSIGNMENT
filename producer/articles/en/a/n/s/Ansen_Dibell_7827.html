Ansen Dibell (September 8, 1942 - March 7, 2006) was the penname used by Nancy Ann Dibble, an American science fiction author, who also published books about fiction writing.

[edit] Works

  • Pursuit of the Screamer, DAW Books, June 1978, ISBN 0-87997-386-2
  • Circle, Crescent, Star, DAW Books, February 1981, ISBN 0-87997-603-9
  • Summerfair, DAW Books, July 1982, ISBN 0-87997-759-0
