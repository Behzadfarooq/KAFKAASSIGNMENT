Anson Mount (b. February 25, 1973 in White Bluff, Tennessee) is a U.S. actor who appeared in the Britney Spears movie Crossroads.

Mount played Jim Steele on the short-lived NBC series, Conviction. Mount is a graduate of the University of the South.

He appeared in the independent movie Tully with Julianne Nicholson, as the title character Tully Coates, a playboy whose ways are changed when he meets Ella Smalley (Julianne Nicholson), a friend of his brother.
