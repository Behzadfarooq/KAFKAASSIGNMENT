Ansbertus was a Gallo-Roman senator. His wife was a woman named Blithilde [parentage uncertain] and they were the parents of Arnoldus, Bishop of Metz and Margrave of Schelde.

Through Arnoldus, they were the paternal grandparents of Saint Itta, wife of Pippin of Landen.
