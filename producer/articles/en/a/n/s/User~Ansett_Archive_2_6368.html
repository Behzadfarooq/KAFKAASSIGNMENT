[edit] My Images

I am wanting to upload some images that I have taken, to add value to articles. Which image license gives most protection/control (over how its used) to the original creator, that includes "No Derivative Works" and that can be used on Wikipedia? Ansett 11:36, 5 July 2006 (UTC)

Hello Ansett, there is no such license that is compatible with Wikipedia. Creative Commons does have a no-derivatives license, but the only CC licenses usable on Wikipedia are cc-by-sa (attribution/share alike) and cc-by (attribution). The other licenses are similarly non-restrictive, as they are meant to be on Wikipedia. Cheers, Tangotango 11:47, 5 July 2006 (UTC)
