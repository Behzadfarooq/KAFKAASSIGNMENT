Anssi Koivuranta (born July 3, 1988 in Kuusamo) is a Nordic combined athlete from Finland who won the gold medal in the 4 x 5 km team event at the 2007 FIS Nordic World Ski Championships in Sapporo. There he also earned a bronze medal in the 15 km Gundersen race.

Koivuranta also earned bronze medal in the 4 x 5 team event at the 2006 Winter Olympics in Turin.
