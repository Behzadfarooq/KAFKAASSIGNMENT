Anstead is an outer suburb of Brisbane, Australia located 16 km south-west of the Brisbane CBD.

Anstead is named after the original land owner John Anstead[2]

Locals often refer to Anstead by the colloquial title of "The 'Stead"

Anstead was officially Gazetted in 1975, prior to which it was part of the suburb of Moggill.

[edit] Geography

Anstead is located on the Brisbane River.
