Ansung Women's Polytechnic College is South Korea's only women's polytechnic college, and one of a shrinking number of sex-segregated institutions of higher education in the country. It is located in Anseong City, Gyeonggi province, in the Seoul metropolitan area. It contains departments of nanotechnology, digital design, CAD and computer modelling, internet media, and jewelry technology. Unlike most technical colleges, Ansung Women's Polytechnic includes a dormitory, which can house as many as 420 students. The school graduates about 600 students per year from its technical training programs.
