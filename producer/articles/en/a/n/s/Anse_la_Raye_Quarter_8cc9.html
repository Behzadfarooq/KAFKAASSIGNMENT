The Quarter of Anse la Raye

Anse la Raye is a Quarter on the West coast of St. Lucia in the Caribbean Sea. In 2001, the population of the region was 6,071 people, and they are mainly fishers and agricultural workers. The main town in the area takes the same name as the Quarter and is located at 13°55′N 61°01′W, a mere 20 minutes drive from Castries, the capital of the country.
