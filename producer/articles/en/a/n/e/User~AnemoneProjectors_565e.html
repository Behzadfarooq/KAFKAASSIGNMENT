Hello. My name is "AnemoneProjectors" and I have been a Wikipedian since 11 June 2006 and an administrator since 11 February 2007. My RFA passed with a result of 46/0/1. Following this, I went through a patch of bad Wikistress due to having too many pages on my watchlist. As a result, I have removed about 500 pages from my watchlist. So if you vandalise Les Dennis or Peter Brame, it's unlikely that you'll be reverted by me.

I contribute mainly to articles relating to EastEnders and The X Factor. I am a member of the EastEnders WikiProject and created the Wikipedia EastEnders portal. I am also a member of WikiProjects on Doctor Who and Big Brother. At the moment most of my edits are minor ones.
