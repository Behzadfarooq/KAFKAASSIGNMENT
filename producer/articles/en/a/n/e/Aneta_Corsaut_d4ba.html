Aneta Corsaut (b. November 3, 1933, Hutchinson, Kansas - d. November 6, 1995, Studio City, California) was an American television actress. She is best known for playing Helen Crump on The Andy Griffith Show.

Corsaut also appeared in several episodes of Matlock (television series), a later show also starring Andy Griffith. She and Steve McQueen both had their film debuts in the 1958 film, The Blob as well as a supporting role as Head Nurse Bradley in the 1980s sitcom House Calls.

She died on November 6, 1995 from cancer, aged 62.
