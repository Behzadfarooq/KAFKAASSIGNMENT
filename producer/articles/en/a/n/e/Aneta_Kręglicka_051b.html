Aneta Kręglicka

Aneta Beata Kręglicka (b. 23 March 1965) won the Miss World contest on 22 November 1989 in Hongkong, representing Poland. She became the first woman from Poland to win the title, when the pageant was held in Hong Kong. In the same year, she also landed as first runner-up in the Miss International pageant, held in Kanazawa, Japan. In 2006, she took part in a popular world show, the third season of the Polish version of Dancing With The Stars.

She graduated economy at Gdańsk University and started studying on Szkoła Główna Handlowa in Poland (Warsaw School of Economics).

In 2006 she took a part in Dancing with the Stars Polish Edition.
