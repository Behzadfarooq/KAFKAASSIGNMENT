Re: Moving pages

Please do not move pages by cutting and pasting their contents, as you did with Beware of the Dog. When a cut-and-paste move is done, the page history of an article or talk page can be split among two or more different pages. This is a Very Bad Thing, because we need to keep the history with the content for copyright reasons. Please use the "move" tab in future. Thanks — AnemoneProjectors (talk) 21:06, 13 December 2006 (UTC)

OK, I'm sorry. Just did not see an other way to get it to the "Beware of the Dog" article, as the name is supposed to be. Again sorry and thanks for telling me. --Luigi-ish 21:55, 13 December 2006 (UTC)
No problem. You should be able to move a page over a redirect if the redirect page hasn't been edited since it was moved to the existing page... if that makes sense. For more information take a look at WP:MOVE. If you need to move a page but cannot, you can list it at WP:RM or add the {{db-move}} template to the page with the correct title. — AnemoneProjectors (talk) 22:20, 13 December 2006 (UTC)
