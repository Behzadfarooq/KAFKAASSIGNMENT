Ane Hansdatter Kismul (born in 1980 in Mosjøen, Norway) is a Norwegian environmentalist and was chairman of Natur og Ungdom between 2003 and 2005, the only to have served three full years. Before being chairman she had been deputy chairman since 2000. Since she became general secretary of the Norwegian Wind Energy Association and from march 2007 she works as an advisor for the Centre Party fraction in the Standing Committee on Energy and the Environment in the Norwegian legislature.
