Anett Schuck

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Olympic medal record
Women's canoeing
Gold 1996 Atlanta K-4 500 m
Gold 2000 Sydney K-4 500 m

Anett Schuck (born 11 April 1970) is a German flatwater canoer.

She won two Olympic gold medals in the K-4 500 m event, in 1996 and 2000. Birgit Fischer and Manuela Mucke were among her teammates on both occasions.
