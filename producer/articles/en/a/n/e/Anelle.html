In the fictional Marvel Comics Universe, Anelle was a Skrull princess, the only child of Emperor Dorrek VII and Empress R´Klll, and the heir to the Skrull Empire.

From the beginning, she was depicted as being frequently opposed to her father's policies, and preferring peace to the aggressive military policy that her father enacted.

She was in love with Warlord Morrat, but he was executed for treason by firing squad after he attempted a failed coup d'état against Emperor Dorrek VII. She leapt in front of him to try to save him, but the Invisible Woman surrounded Anelle with a force field, saving her life.

The Super-Skrull, Kl'rt, fell in love with her and was a suitor for her hand, but she saw nothing in him and rejected him.

Later, during the Kree-Skrull War, when the Kree Captain Marvel was captured, the two fell in love and had an illicit relationship. Hulkling was the product of that relationship, hidden on Earth to prevent scandal.

After Anelle's mother R´Klll murdered her husband to become ruling Empress of the Skrull Empire, Anelle died when the Skrull Throneworld was devoured by Galactus.
