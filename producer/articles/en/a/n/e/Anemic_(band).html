Anemic is a four person rock band from Bristol, England. They are currently on a tour to promote their new album The Void, released via Sugar Shack Records. They are a hard rock band, and they express much stress in their music. They dress like a punk band, but in reality, they are a hard rock band. Their main influence was Rock N' Roll as stated on the band's myspace. They are in a UK Tour from May 23rd to June 3rd, 2007, in a band battle against fellow UK band Dear Superstar.

[edit] Members

  • Kevin Peters - Vocals
  • Daniel Peters - Guitars
  • Andrew Parslow - Bass
  • Alex Rae - Drums
