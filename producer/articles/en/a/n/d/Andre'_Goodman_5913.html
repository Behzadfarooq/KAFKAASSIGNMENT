Andre' Goodman (born August 11, 1978 in Greenville, South Carolina) is an American football cornerback who currently plays for the Miami Dolphins of the National Football League. He started left cornerback as a senior at the South Carolina.

[edit] Personal

  • Married (Shana) with sons, Fabian and Andre' II, and a daughter, Kennedy.
