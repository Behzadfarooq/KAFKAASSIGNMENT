Andrychów

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Andrychów ( listen) is a town in Wadowice County in southern Poland with 23,100 inhabitants as of 1995. It has been situated in the Lesser Poland Voivodeship since 1999. Previously, it was located in Bielsko-Biała Voivodeship (1975-1998).
