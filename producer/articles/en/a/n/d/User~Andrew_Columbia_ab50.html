User:Andrew Columbia

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Andrew Columbia was born on January 22, 1989 in St. Vincent's hospital located in Staten Island, New York. He was raised in Staten Island and attended P.S. 60 Elementary school. He stayed there until the end of his fifth grade year. When he was about the age of 12 he moved with his family to Clark, New Jersey. While in Clark, Andrew attended Carl H. Kumpf Middle School. Andrew and his family only lived in Clark for about two years until his fathers job caused them to move out to Bucks County, Pennsylvania. When the move first took place Andrew attended Holicong middle school in Buckingham Pennsylvania. After that, he went on and attended Central Bucks West high school for a short while until the new school Central Bucks South high school was built. Andrew currently resides in Doylestown township, and is an 18 year old senior attending Central Bucks South high school. Andrew also goes to Middle Bucks institute of Technology trade school for half of the day. The program he attends is called Multimedia Technology. The program deals with learning photography, shooting film, editing, manipulating images, creating computer graphics, and completing personal and group projects. This year in the program Andrew plans to become certified in Apple Final Cut Pro and "As many other applications as possible" as he puts it. His intent is to gain as much knowledge as he possibly can from this program so he can get all it has to offer. Andrew's aspiration is to become a famous movie director and move to Hollywood, California after college. Andrew also sings and writes songs. He has a love for music as well that he would like to get more involved with somewhere down the road. However, as of now his true passion is his love for film and theater. Andrew will be graduating from Central Bucks South in June, 2007. His plans after high school are to either attend a college in Philadelphia, (Temple University) or New York City (School of Visual Arts) for film and cinema studies. Another one of his college choices is Full Sail located in Winter-park, Orlando Florida. Andrew has two younger brothers Steve, and Matt Columbia. Steven is 16 years old and Matthew is 12. He also has a cocker spaniel female dog named Sidney.
