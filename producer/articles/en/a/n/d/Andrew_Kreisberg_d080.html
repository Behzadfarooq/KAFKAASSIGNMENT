Andrew Kreisberg

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Andrew Kreisberg (born April 23, 1971) is an american television writer. His first job was on the short-lived animated sitcom Mission Hill. Since it's cancellation, he has written for Justice League, The Simpsons and Hope & Faith. Currently, he is writing for Boston Legal. He entertains America 360 days a year and would just like 5 days in which he can relax.
