Anders Glenmark (b. November 20, 1953 in Tomelilla, Sweden) is a Swedish pop and rock singer. He is the brother of Karin Glenmark and nephew of Bruno Glenmark.

Anders Glenmark has been a member of several grouos, as the family group Glenmarks and the siblings duo Gemini. Since 1994 he is a member of the group GES together with Orup and Niklas Strömstedt.

He has participated in Melodifestivalen six thmes. Together with Glenmarks 1973 with the song En liten sång som alla andra (4th), I annorlunda land 1974 (8th plats) and Lady Antoinette (6th plats) 1975. In 1981 he participated alone with the song Det är mitt liv - det är jag (4th), and in 1984 together with the sister Karin, before took the name Gemini, with the song Kall som is (4th place). In 1989 he and Orup participated with the song Upp över mina öron, which finished 2nd.
