Ando Media “Ando” is a company formed in 2004 by a leading technology company, Ando Media, Inc. and Aritaur Communications, Inc. an owner of radio stations and Internet related ventures. Ando provides a number of products which it licenses to the internet radio and content delivery industry that assist stations in monetizing their audience. These products include Webcast Metrics (audience measurement), Ad Injector (ad injection prior to or into a stream including impression measurement), and PodLoc (creates podcasts of any audio on the fly and make available for download as well as measurement).

Ando currently numbers it clients at over 1,700 internet radio stations (as of 1/20/2007) and includes many of the largest terrestrial broadcasting companies including CBS Radio, ABC Radio, Radio Disney, ESPN Radio and more than a thousand Internet radio brands as well.

Ando Media is an industry leader in providing internet software solutions which allow radio stations to monetize their audience. Webcast Metrics provides the necessary audience data allowing more powerful engagement with potential advertisers. Once advertising campaigns are sold, Ando Media’s Ad Injector enables the scheduling and insertion of ads prior to or inside the audio stream and reports in real time the impressions created from this advertising campaign.
