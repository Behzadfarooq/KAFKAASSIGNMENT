Andy Newmark

Andrew 'Andy' Newmark (born 14 July 1950) is an American musician, best known as the drummer for influential funk band Sly & the Family Stone from 1973 to 1975. The album Fresh is considered to be one of the great funk drumming performances. Newmark has performed and recorded with many top musical artists since the 1970s, including John Lennon, B.B. King, Eric Clapton, David Bowie, George Harrison, Rickie Lee Jones, Pink Floyd, Roger Waters, David Gilmour, Roxy Music, Laura Nyro, Elkie Brooks and George Benson. He is featured on Carly Simon's 1971 album, Anticipation and Gary Wright's 1975 album, The Dream Weaver.
