Andes is an intelligent tutoring system designed to help students to solve physics problems.

[edit] Topics covered

Andes is designed to be the homework component of an introductory physics course. It covers all of the topics in a standard two-semester introductory physics course, except for modern physics and thermodynamics. It does not include problems involving calculus.
