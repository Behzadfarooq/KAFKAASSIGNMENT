Andrea Coda

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Andrea Coda (born 25 April 1985 in Massa) is an Italian football (soccer) player. He plays defender for Udinese and Italian U21s.

He started his youth period at A.S. Lucchese-Libertas, and started professional career at Empoli F.C..

Udinese bought 50% rights at January, 2006 and remain at Empoli until officially as a Udinese player in the summer.
