Andre Frazier (born June 29, 1982 in Cincinnati, Ohio) is an American Football player who plays linebacker for the Cincinnati Bengals.

[edit] High School

Frazier attended high school at Hughes High in Cincinnati, Ohio from 1996-2000. He was first-team all-city both his junior and senior years, and helped lead his team to two league titles.

[edit] College

Frazier played his College Football at the University of Cincinnati from 2000-2004.

  • Conference USA All-Freshman in 2001
  • Conference USA First-Team in 2003
  • Conference USA First-Team in 2004

[edit] NFL

Frazier signed with the Pittsburgh Steelers as an undrafted free agent. He was activated from the practice squad to the regular 53-man roster prior to the first game. He played in 11 of the teams games, primarily on special teams, and won a Super Bowl ring from Super Bowl XL.

Prior to the start of the 2006 season, Frazier was cut by the Steelers. On September 28th, 2006 Frazier was signed by his hometown team, the Cincinnati Bengals.
