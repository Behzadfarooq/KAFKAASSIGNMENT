Andrew Rabutla

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Andrew "Jaws" Rabutla (b. 21st November 1971, Tzaneen, South Africa) is a South African soccer player currently playing as a defender for Jomo Cosmos. He has nineteen caps for the South African national team, scoring one goal.
