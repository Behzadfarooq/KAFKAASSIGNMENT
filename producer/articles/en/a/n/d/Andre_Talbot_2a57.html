Andre Talbot (born May 3, 1978 in Toronto, Ontario) is a current wide receiver for the Toronto Argonauts. The 2006 season was his 6th year in the CFL. Talbot played university football at Wilfrid Laurier University where he was a Two Time All-Canadian and was drafted by the Toronto Argonauts in 2001. He has developed over the years into a top talent receiver for the Argonauts. During his first year as a member of the Toronto Argonauts he worked part-time at The Score Television Network and has since developed his media work experience at Corus Entertainment and the Jayne Hancock Group Canada throughout the football off seasons. He currently promotes and markets a grassroots football community website called - TheGridiron.ca.

Receiver Andre Talbot has been named the Reader’s Choice Silver Winner as one of Toronto’s favourite Pro Sports Players. Talbot was nominated by the public in the Favourite Entertainment, Media & Sports Personalities category and finished second behind Gold Winner Mats Sundin and ahead of Bronze Winner, Toronto’s other favourite number 4, Chris Bosh.

Andre Talbot recently married local Toronto fashion designer Melanie Bozzo. Melanie and her sister Kristina own, operate and design - common cloth; the line was featured in Flare's Top Ten Fashion Stars, titling the common cloth look "simplicity personified", and was named as Highrise Magazine's "favourite new label" of the year.
