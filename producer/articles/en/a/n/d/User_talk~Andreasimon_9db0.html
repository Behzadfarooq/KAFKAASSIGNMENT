[edit] TheContentWrangler.com

[THECONTENTWRANGLER.COM]


Scott Abel is a content management strategist and structured content evangelist. He works with organizations to improve the way they author, maintain, publish, and archive their information assets. Scott’s website, TheContentWrangler.com, is an online resource for technical writers with an interest in content management. Scott has experience in XML-based enterprise content management strategy, information architecture, software tool selection, high tech marketing communications, business process analysis, technical writing and editing, usability, content localization, and designing single source solutions. Scott’s formal journalism education is combined with 14 years experience as a technical writer.


Previous roles provided Scott with experience authoring HTML-based online help systems, software documentation, standard operating procedures, marketing collateral, email newsletters, pharmaceutical and other life sciences regulatory compliance and validation documents, instructional/training materials, web site content, structured document templates, and large document collections. Scott is also an editor of technical, medical and scientific documents, white papers, and books, and a frequent contributor to a variety of trade publications including recent articles in e-Doc Magazine, The Rockley Bulletin, CMSWatch, SlashDot, and Expert Access.


A founding member of Content Management Professionals (CM Pros), Scott currently serves as Executive Director of the organization.


[edit] More Information on CM Pros:

In 2004, a group of content management professionals came together with the idea of forming a peer organization focusing on the practice of content management. The organization today is the international community, with members and communities worldwide. Visit http://www.cmpros.org/about-cm-pros/mission to learn about their mission.

In addition, Scott travels globally to speak to organizations and governments about content management as a process, not a product.
