Andrejs Štolcers (born August 7, 1974 in Rīga, Latvia) is a professional football player who currently plays for Skonto FC.

His international debut came in the 0-0 draw against Denmark on August 26, 1992. Since then, he has represented his country on 81 matches and has scored 7 goals.he has also played in the 2004 European Championships in Portugal.

The attack-minded Latvian has played for home side Olimpija Rīga, Skonto Riga, Shakhtar Donetsk, Spartak Moscow, Fulham and Yeovil Town football clubs, and earned a free transfer to Azerbaijan's top team FK Baku on July 8, 2005, in time for the 2005/06 football season. In 2006 he has again returned to Skonto FC
