Andrea Ferro

Andrea Ferro (born August 19, 1973) is the male vocalist of Italian gothic metal band Lacuna Coil.

Ferro was born in Arona, Italy. He started playing music and singing from a very young age and was one of the founding members of Lacuna Coil. He was originally in the band Sleep Of Right when they hired vocalist Cristina Scabbia to do some backing vocals. Cristina later became the permament female singer for the band and Lacuna Coil was formed.
