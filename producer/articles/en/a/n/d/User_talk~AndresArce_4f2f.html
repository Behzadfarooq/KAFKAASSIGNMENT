You're quite prolific. (And this bio effort is for a person I've never even heard of.) With all due respect to someone who is generating real content (vs. the dabbler-editors out there), you should spend a few minutes reviewing Wikipedia:Manual of Style (biographies) to see how bios should be formatted. A recent example in the ACW space that is pretty good is William Tecumseh Sherman, although you don't have to go to the extent of using that big tabular picture box on the upper right. Other examples are the 120+ I have on my user page. I have written a number of formatting and style hints in my page User:Hlj/CWediting and you are welcome to take advantage of them if you wish to be similar in style to other Civil War articles.
