Andrea Lowell (born January 17, 1983 in Long Beach, California) is an American actress and model most recognizable from her Playboy magazine pictorials and on air work for a variety of Playboy TV programs.

Growing up the youngest of three sisters in the Los Angeles area, Andrea began stage acting at six and modeling at eleven. To date she has appeared in Playboy over twelve times, Playboy Special Editions at least a dozen times, Perfect 10 magazine once, starred on several Playboy TV shows, appeared as a host in Girls Gone Wild, and appeared in several television commercials, television shows, and movies. In 2006 Andrea appeared as a cast member in the sixth season of the reality television series, The Surreal Life. In 2007 she made another appearance on the popular VH1 franchise, by staring in the The Surreal Life: Fame Games.

Andrea was given the nickname, "The Natural," by her Playboy and pin-up fans, a nickname that refers to her natural (nonaugmented) breasts, as well as her "girl next door" persona. Lowell maintains contact with fans by checking into her personal website, www.BeautifulAndrea.com, and yahoo group, to chat and answer questions. Many fans on the internet call her "Anz" for short, a nickname given to her by her family and close friends.

Andrea graduated from Long Beach Polytechnic High School in 2001, where she was a member of Zayn Welfare Sorority. During her junior year, she was in two drama productions, the play Chicago and Rehearsal for Murder. Andrea attended University of California, Irvine [1], where she studied biology.

Other media appearances including: VH1's The Surreal Life #6" (Cast Member), On the Record with Greta Van Susteren (interview on Fox News Channel about Girls Gone Wild PPV special), The Howard Stern Show (Radio Guest), Guest Host on Playboy's "Night Calls" radio show (on Sirius Satellite Radio) and FoxxyNews.com (anchor). Her Playboy TV appearance include: Sexcetera (anchor), Totally Busted (cast member) and Night Calls (as the 'Hub Girl'). Her most popular stint on the channel was as the 'Weather/Gadget Girl' on The Weekend Flash (a live weekend naked news cast) where she began filling in as the Entertainment and Sports anchor when needed.


Currently, Andrea is persuing a career in, acting, broadcasting, and spokesmodeling. She will be starring in the new Playboy TV program, Canoga Park, which begins filming in November of 2006. In 2007, Andrea will make her feature film debut, in the motion picture Jason and Ross. On the broadcasting front, Andrea is the new co-host of the Playboy Morning Show on SIRIUS 198, as well as Miss BetUS.com 2006, BetUS.com is an online gambling site, which she spokesmodels for.
