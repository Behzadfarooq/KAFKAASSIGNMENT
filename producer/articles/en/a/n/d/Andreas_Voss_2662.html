Andreas Voss

Andreas Voss (born February 27, 1979 in Stolberg) is a German footballer. He currently plays as a midfielder for MSV Duisburg. His previous clubs include Bayer 04 Leverkusen and VfL Wolfsburg.

He represented Germany at the 1999 FIFA World Youth Championship.
