Andrew 'Buggs' Combs (b. December 31, 1977) is an American lacrosse player. He attended the University of Maryland earning All-American honors at Attack. [1]

In 2006, Combs joined the Los Angeles Riptide in Major League Lacrosse.

Combs began his pro lacrosse career in 2003 with the Baltimore Bayhawks. He also spent 2004 with the Bayhawks. In 2005, Combs joined the Rochester Rattlers, where he was honored with the Major League Lacrosse Most Improved Player of the Year Award. [2]

Combs is also the Offensive Cordinator, under head coach Tony Seaman, for the Towson University Men's Lacrosse team. [3]
