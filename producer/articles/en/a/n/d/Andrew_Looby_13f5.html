Andrew Looby (born February 20, 1974) is an Irish radio presenter. He was born in Tipperary, Ireland. Looby currently works at Clare FM.

Andrew joined Tipperary Mid West Radio in the 1990's where he presented a selection of programs before moving to TippFM Radio in Clonmel to present an afternoon show. His current show on Clare FM is broadcast 12 - 4 Monday to Friday and is County Clare's most popular afternoon radio programme.[citation needed]

Looby is married to Elaine and together they have a son named Drew.
