[edit] Interests

A few of my interests, at least the ones that are relevant here:

  • Classical music, mostly Baroque, Classical, and Romantic.
  • GNU/Linux and free software: I use and recommend Ubuntu Linux.
  • Computer programming (mainly in Python and GTK+).

[edit] Goals

I have a few vague goals for my time on Wikipedia.

  • Adding to classical music articles, specifically Bruckner and working on polishing existing articles and making them more consistent in layout and minimum content (e.g. pages for specific pieces).
  • Making Wikipedia the go-to source for music information, at least as far as my uses go. When I have time, I will add any information I find from other sources on its corresponding Wikipedia page.
  • Helping to keep articles on the various niches of free software (mainly Linux and related projects) current and informative.
