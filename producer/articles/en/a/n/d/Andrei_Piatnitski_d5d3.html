Andrei Piatnitski (Russian: Андрей Владимирович Пятницкий) (born 27 September 1967) is a retired Russian football midfielder. Currently he is the manager of FC Vityaz Podolsk in the Russian Second Division.

He played for a few clubs, including Spartak Moscow and Sokol Saratov.

He played for Russia national football team and was a participant at the 1994 FIFA World Cup.

[edit] Honours

  • UEFA U-21 Championship 1990 winner
  • Russian Premier League champion 1992, 1993, 1994, 1995, 1996, 1997
