Andrew Bernal (born 16 July 1966 in Canberra, Australia) is an Australian former professional football defender, and later football agent and personal assistant for David Beckham.

Bernal played most of his football at Reading, where he was part of the team that narrowly missed out on promotion to the Premier League in the 1994-95 season. Bernal has also played for the Australia national team.
