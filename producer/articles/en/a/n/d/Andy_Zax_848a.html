Andy Zax

Andy Zax is a former co-star of the Comedy Central game show Beat the Geeks on which he played the Music Geek. He is also a music historian and producer of CD box sets and reissues by Talking Heads, Echo & The Bunnymen, Television, Little Richard, Fats Domino, Judee Sill, John Cale, the Neon Philharmonic and many others.

He was also a contestant on the very first episode of the FOX game show Greed on November 4th 1999. When asked what he'd do with the top prize if he were to win, he replied that he'd use it to finish his novel.

Currently he is a recurring panelist on the live stage revival of What's My Line? in Los Angeles. In October, 2006, he began hosting a weekly radio program, Archives Of Oblivion, which is broadcast on www.luxuriamusic.com every Tuesday from 2PM to 5PM PST.
