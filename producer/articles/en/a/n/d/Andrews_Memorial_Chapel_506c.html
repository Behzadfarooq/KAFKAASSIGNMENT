Andrews Memorial Chapel
(U.S. National Register of Historic Places)
Location: Dunedin, Florida
Added to NRHP: July 31, 1972

The Andrews Memorial Chapel is a historic chapel in Dunedin, Florida, United States. It is located at 1899 San Mateo Drive. On July 31, 1972, it was added to the U.S. National Register of Historic Places.
