Andrejs Piedels (born in Jekabpils, September 17, 1970) is a Latvian football (soccer) goalkeeper who currently played for Skonto Riga.

In career was played also for Pārdaugava, Daugava, Amstrig and DAG. He got 14 caps for Latvia national football team and he participated at Euro 2004.
