Andrea Boardman

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

For the Canadian dancer of the same name, see Andrea Boardman (dancer)

Andrea Boardman (born 6 November 1967 in Liverpool) is an English television presenter.

She started out presenting the UK version of the Disney Channel, before progressing to other, mainly ITV based children's TV shows.

The daughter of Liverpudlian comedian Stan Boardman, she lived with actor Darren Day for three years up until 1994, and also dated Toby Anstis
