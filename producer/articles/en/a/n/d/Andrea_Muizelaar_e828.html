Andrea Muizelaar is a Canadian fashion model (born February 10 1987). Muizelaar is famous for becoming the winner of the reality TV show Canada's Next Top Model. Before winning the competition, Muizelaar worked as a cashier in a dollar store in her hometown of Whitby, Ontario, and attended George Brown College in Toronto, Ontario for Hotel Management.

[edit] Audience Reaction

Throughout the season, polls on the official CNTM website showed that the audience frequently strongly disagreed with the judges' decisions. At the end of the season the poll asked, "Did Andrea deserve to win?" The majority of fans voted against her, with the top poll answer being, "No – She’s too skinny and the crying gets annoying", which more than doubled the votes received for "Yes – She’s the best candidate for couture".[1]

[edit] After CNTM

Muizelaar has stated she has given up on fashion because she realized that her future does not lie in the fashion industry. [1]
