Andrea Lehmann (born 1975, Düsseldorf) is an artist based in Germany.

She began attending the Kunstakademie [1] in Düsseldorf in 1995 and attained her MA in 2000.

She has shown work in many exhibitions including “Stuffed Diamonds” at Anna Klinkhammer [2] in Düsseldorf, “Happy Days” at Kulturministerium, Prague. Lehmann has exhibited internationally at venues such as The Rubell Family Collection [3], at Jack Tilton/NY, Michael+Susan Hort Collection/NY, Saatchi Gallery/London, Mera + Don Rubell/Miami, Museum of Glass/Tacoma, Kravetz/Wehby Gallery NY, (selection). She is represented by Anna Klinkhammer Gallery [4] in Düsseldorf.
