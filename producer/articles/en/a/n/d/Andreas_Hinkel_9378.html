Andreas Hinkel

Andreas Hinkel (born March 26, 1982 in Backnang) is a professional German footballer, who plays for Spanish La Liga club Sevilla FC.

Hinkel plays as a defender, starting his career in VfB Stuttgart of Germany. He has represented his country on 17 occasions, making his debut in 2003 against Serbia and Montenegro, but has yet to open his scoring account. Hinkel was not part of the German National Team for 2006 FIFA World Cup.

On June 23, 2006 he signed a 4-year contract with Sevilla FC. The transfer fee was estimated to be around 4 mil. €
