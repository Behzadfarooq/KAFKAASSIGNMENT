Ontronik "Andy" Khachaturian is the former singer for The Apex Theory. Ontronik left the band in November 2002 due to musical differences. He is also the former drummer for System of a Down; he left the band after the third demo due to a hand injury.

Currently, Ontronik is the singer for the band VoKEE. He also has a solo project called Ontronik. Through Ontronik, he has collaborated with the likes of Alien Ant Farm's Dryden Mitchell and Dredg's Gavin Hayes.
