Andy Hillenburg (born April 30, 1963), is an American race car driver. He was born in Indianapolis, Indiana) and began his racing career at age 11, when he competed in the Indianapolis soap box derby. He won the state quarter midget championships in 1975-1979.

He served as a test driver for the IROC series. He competed in the 2000 Indianapolis 500, finishing 28th, and has sixteen Winston Cup starts.

As of 2006 he operates the Fast Track Racing School and is running a team for Canadian racing driver Pierre Bourque in a combined ARCA / NASCAR Busch East schedule of stock car races.

He has also acted in a few movies. Including 3 The Dale Earnhardt Story from ESPN.
