Andrew Kinlochan

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Andrew Kinlochan (born November 12, 1978, Hertfordshire, England) is an English pop singer. From 2003 to 2005 he was a member of the boy band Phixx.

Kinlochan came to prominence when in October 2003 he came out to the British public, becoming the first boyband member to be openly gay from the start of his career. (Boyzone's Stephen Gately came out to head off a magazine scandal after his band had already achieved massive success; his boyfriend was coincidentally a member of the Dutch boyband, Caught In The Act.) Kinlochan has since received attention from the gay community, featured in Attitude and on the cover of Gay Times.

In July 2005, he appeared on the Sky One reality tv series The Only Gays ln The Village where he spent 4 weeks living with 3 other celebrity gay men on a farm in Derbyshire.
