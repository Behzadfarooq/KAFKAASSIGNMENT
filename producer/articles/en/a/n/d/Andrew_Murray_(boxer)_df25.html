Andrew Murray (born 10 September 1982 in Cavan, Ireland) is a professional boxer.[1] He currently fights at Lightweight division but could compete at divisions either side.

Murray boxed as an amateur for Ireland before turning professional in March 2005, winning his first fight at the King's Hall, Belfast, County Antrim, Northern Ireland, in which he beat Welshman Jonathan Jones in a fourth round knockout on a card that included Jim Rock, Neil Sinclair and Eamonn Magee.[2]

Murray, who now fights out of St. Albans near London, England, has won his first seven pro fights and three by knockout.
