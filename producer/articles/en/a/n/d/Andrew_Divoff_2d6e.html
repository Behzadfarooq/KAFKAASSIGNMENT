Andrew Divoff is an actor who was born on July 2, 1955 in San Tome, Venezuela. He has played many villains in film and on television and is best known for playing the nefarious Djinn in the first two films of the Wishmaster series. His other films include Another 48 Hrs., The Hunt for Red October, and Toy Soldiers.

Andrew Divoff's TV guest appearances include The A-Team, JAG and Highlander: The Series. On Highlander, Divoff appeared in the 1st season episode as a mortal named Bryan Slade and in a 5th season episode as an Immortal named Gavriel Larca.

Divoff recently played Mikhail Bakunin on the hit television series Lost, a Ukrainian man with an eyepatch who establishes brief visual contact with Locke and the other castaways. He reprised the role in the Lost episode "Enter 77", which aired on March 7th 2007 and "Par Avion" which aired the following week.

Divoff can speak eight languages: English, Spanish, Italian, French, German, Catalan, Portuguese and Russian.
