Andy Holdsworth is a professional football player for Huddersfield Town in Coca-Cola League One. He currently wears the number 2 shirt for the team and plays at right-back.

Since entering the professional ranks at Town, aged 19, he has started to hold his place at right-back and become one of Town's more important and vital players.

Since Town's promotion season, the only reasons that Holdsworth has been out of the squad is by suspension or injury.

In 2006, he was named on the list of Town's Fans' Favourites.
