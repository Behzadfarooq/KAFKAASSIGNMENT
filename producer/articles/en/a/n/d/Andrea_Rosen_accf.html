Andrea Rosen

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Andrea Rosen, Central Park, New York City.

Andrea Rosen is an American comedian and actress most notable for her work with comedy troupes Stella and Variety Shac, and for her appearances in numerous TV commercials. She was born February 2, 1974 in Delmar, New York. She is Alice's cousin.

A regular on the Brooklyn comedy circuit, Ms. Rosen has written and performed 2 one-woman shows, "Fast and Loose" and "Room for Rent". She co-wrote and performed "It's a Detective Agency and Everyone's English", a play at the NYC Fringe Festival.
