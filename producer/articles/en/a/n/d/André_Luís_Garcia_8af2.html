André Luís Garcia, best known as André Luís (born 31 July 1979 in Bagé) is a Brazilian Football (soccer) player in defender role.

With Brazil national football team he participated at 2000 Summer Olympics.

[edit] Clubs

  • 2000-2001 : Santos FC
  • 2001-2002 : Fluminense FC
  • 2002-2004 : Santos FC
  • 2004-2005 : SL Benfica
  • 2005-2006 : Olympique de Marseille (on loan from SL Benfica)
  • 2006-2007 : Cruzeiro Esporte Clube

[edit] Honours

  • 2002: Brazilian League champion with Santos FC
  • 2004: Brazilian League champion with Santos FC
