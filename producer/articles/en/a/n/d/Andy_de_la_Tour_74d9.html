Andy de la Tour

Andy de la Tour is a British actor and screenwriter. He has appeared in such films as Plenty, Notting Hill and the Roman Polański remake of Oliver Twist. He has also written for such television series as Boon, Peak Practice and Kavanagh QC. He started his career as an alternative stand-up comedian, often appearing on the same bill as Rik Mayall, Ben Elton and Norman Lovett.

He is the brother of Frances de la Tour.
