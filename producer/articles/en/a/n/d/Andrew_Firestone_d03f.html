Andrew Firestone
Born July 10, 1975 (age 31)
Santa Barbara, California

Andrew Firestone (born July 10, 1975) is the second son of Brooks Firestone, and the great grandson of Firestone founder, Harvey Firestone. Firestone graduated from the University of San Diego with a degree in business administration in 1998. He was born in Santa Barbara, California. Andrew is now dating actress/model Ivana Bozilovic. He is also a part-owner of Confidential, a restaurant in San Diego and General Manager of Firestone Vineyard

[edit] The Bachelor

Firestone was the third bachelor on the American Broadcasting Company reality series The Bachelor. On the show, Firestone courted 25 bachelorettes, progressivly eliminating them, and eventually selecting one of the group for a continuing relationship. Andrew selected Jennifer Schefft but the couple decided to go their separate ways after the show. Jen participated in season 3 of a very similar series, The Bachelorette, soon after the break up.

[edit] Other Television Appearances

In 2005, Firestone participated in the fifth Celebrity Poker Showdown tournament on Bravo. He won his first round game, but placed third in the tournament championship. Firestone played for the charity Direct Relief International.

In 2006, Firestone appeared as a contestant in an episode of VH1's Celebrity Paranormal Project.

Firestone is slated to be the host of an upcoming lifestyles HD series entitled Billionaires Car Club, distributed by American Television Distribution, to be aired on iNHD in the Spring of 2007.
