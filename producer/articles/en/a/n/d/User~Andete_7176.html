From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

At the age of 14-15 I played ELITE on my MSX computer for hours and hours. I was amazed by the huge amount of names for all the planets, till i recognized a patern in it. I wrote my own program in Z80 assembler to generate pages of names from a few word-parts. After quite a long time printing on my 8-pin needle printer, I started reading the result, and was instantly struck by the coolness of the name Andete. I therefore adapted it as my nickname. As far as I know it has no meaning in any language.
