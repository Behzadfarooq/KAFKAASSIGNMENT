Andrej Komac (born 4 December 1979) is a Slovenian footballer player from Nova Gorica, Slovenia, who gained his reputation with HiT Gorica. He also played for NK Primorje, Olimpija Ljubljana and CS Marítimo where he has gained attraction from clubs like Sporting Lisbon, Newcastle United, and Borussia Dortmund. He is also widely compared to Zlatko Zahovič for their similar style and the fact that both were Slovenian. He's currently playing for Swedish team Djurgårdens IF.

[edit] National Team

Komac has represented Slovenia on many occasions. from Under-15 to senior level he was also part of the World Cup 2006 team that failed to make it to Germany. He currently has 22 caps for senior team.
