Andy Impey (born September 30, 1971 in Hammersmith, London) is an English footballer who is currently without a club.

Andy signed for QPR in 1990 and made his debut against Norwich in October 1991. Andy has been recently linked with Australian A-League side Sydney FC.

A Sydney FC supporters Firm, located below the Qantas sign at the South East end of Aussie Stadium, is named the Andy Impey Memorial Power Posse.
