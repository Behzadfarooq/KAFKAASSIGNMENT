[edit] Video game years

Hi, Andrevan. My sources for release dates on NES games has been http://www.planetnintendo.com/nindb/nes/ . You have brought up an excellent point, though, as how to deal with release dates in different parts of the world (it has been confusing for me as well). I tend to lean towards the Japan (Famicom) release dates as they are normally the earliest. I gave SMB3 the "1988" category based on both its Japan release and its actual game copyright (which is far less important). I'm not certain that SMB2 was released here in 1987; the Planet Nintendo DB says it was October 1988 (same year as SMB3 !). I personally remember SMB2 being released here in 1988, as it was the first featured game in "Nintendo Power" magazine here, which I know also debuted in 1988. If you have any more thoughts on the issue, I'm more than happy to hear from you. :) -bumm13 07:40, 6 Oct 2004 (UTC)

Hi, Andrevan. I corrected the year for Super Mario Brothers 3, as I believe it was a worldwide release in 1990. Hope this helps a bit. -bumm13 14:40, 6 Oct 2004 (UTC)
Hello again, Andrevan. You may be interested in the following URL- http://www.nesplayer.com/features/doki/doki.htm It is a page about the game Doki Doki Panic, which Nintendo of America edited into SMB2. Hope you find it interesting (I did). -bumm13 15:40, 6 Oct 2004 (UTC)
