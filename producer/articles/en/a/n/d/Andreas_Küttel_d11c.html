Andreas Küttel (born 25 April 1979) is a Swiss ski jumper. He grew up in Einsiedeln and still lives there. He won the World Cup competitions on 3 December 2005 in Lillehammer and on 9 December 2005 in Harrachov and placed third in the overall championship.
