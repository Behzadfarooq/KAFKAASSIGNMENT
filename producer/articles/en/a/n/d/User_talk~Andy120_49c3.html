[edit] Question on Titles

Is it possible to edit titles of an article, and if so how is this done? Thanks!

It sure is. You move the article using the "move" button (to the right of the "edit this page" button). Your account must be 4 days old to move pages. If the move is controversial or impossible, visit Wikipedia:Requested moves.--Commander Keane 17:47, 17 August 2006 (UTC)
Thanks! Andy120 17:50, 17 August 2006 (UTC)
