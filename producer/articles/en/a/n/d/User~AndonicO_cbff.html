My Opinion of Wikipedia

Avoiding any obvious details, (website, encyclopedia, a wiki, ect.), I will tell you what I think Wikipedia is. Wikipedia is a place where people of all ages can both learn and help others learn, enjoy themselves in a healthy manner, and become severely addicted to something good. It can also be said that Wikipedia is constantly evolving, (due to the large numbers of addicted users), it's knowledge base multiplying almost daily, so that never will a person get bored. In other words, thanks Mr. Wales!
