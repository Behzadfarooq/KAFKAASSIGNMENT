Andrew (アンドリュー, Andrew?) is a fictional character from SNK Playmore's Samurai Shodown series of fighting games. He's one of the main protagonists and new characters from the recent Samurai Spirits: Tenkaichi Kenkakuden.

[edit] About Andrew

He's an American soldier who goes to Japan to stop the new menace over his young country.

[edit] Trivia

  • He's based on Andrew Jackson, the former president of the USA.
  • Also, is the first American character in Samurai Shodown series (since Galford D. Weller and Earthquake are from Mexico in that time), as the first who uses a bayonet as weapon.
  • In the game, Andrew befriended Yoshitora Tokugawa, representing the closeness of the two cultures in the series. Also they appears together in the recent SNK vs. Capcom: Card Fighters DS as an Action card.
  • As stated in his profile, Andrew's unable to deal with women (possibly from growing up with little contact with them).
