Andrey Tereshin

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Andrey Tereshin (born 15 December 1982) is a Russian high jumper.

His personal best is 2.32 metres, though he has jumped 2.36 metres indoor in February 2006 in Moscow.[1]

[edit] Achievements

Year Tournament Venue Result Extra
2003 Universiade Daegu, South Korea 6th
2005 European Indoor Championships Madrid, Spain 8th
2006 World Indoor Championships Moscow, Russia 2nd 2.36m PBi
2007 European Indoor Championships Birmingham, England 7th
