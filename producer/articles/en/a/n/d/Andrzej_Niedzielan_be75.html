Andrzej Niedzielan (born February 27, 1979 in Żary, Poland) is a striker for the Poland national football team. His career began in 1997, when he joined the team Promien Żary. After he played for Zagłębie Lubin, where he did not impress. He was twice loaned out, first to Odra Opole, and subsequently to Chrobry Głogów. In the spring of 2001 he was transferred to Górnik Zabrze for a nominal fee. At Górnik, Andrzej Niedzielan finally found his stride. After playing for half a season he was sold to Groclin Grodzisk Wielkopolski. At Groclin his career took off, and after one year he received an offer from NEC Nijmegen.
