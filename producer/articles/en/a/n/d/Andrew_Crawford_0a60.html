Andrew Crawford

Andrew Crawford was an announcer and newsreader. He worked on BBC Radio 4 from 1988 to 1999, and has been on air occasionally as a freelance since then. In 1997, he was the news reader who announced the death of Diana, Princess of Wales in the early hours of 31 August.

He reads the opening and closing announcements to I'm Sorry I Haven't A Clue.
