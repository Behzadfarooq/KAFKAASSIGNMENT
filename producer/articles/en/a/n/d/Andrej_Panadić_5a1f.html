Andrej Panadić (born 9 March 1969) is a retired Croatian football player.

He played for Dinamo Zagreb, Chemnitzer FC (Germany), KFC Uerdingen 05 (Germany), Hamburger SV (Germany) and Nagoya Grampus Eight in Japan.

He played for the Yugoslavia national football team and was a participant at the 1990 FIFA World Cup.
