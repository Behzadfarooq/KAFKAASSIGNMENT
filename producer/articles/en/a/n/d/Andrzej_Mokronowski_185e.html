Andrzej Mokronowski (1713-1784) was a notable member of the Polish szlachta, politician and general of the Polish Army. Knight of the Order of the White Eagle, awarded in 1776.

He was voivode of Masovian Voivodship since 1781. Marshal of the Sejm on August 26 - October 31, 1776 in Warsaw.

First Polish Mason and founder of the first Masonic Lodge in Poland, opened in 1774 in Warsaw.
