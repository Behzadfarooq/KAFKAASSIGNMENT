[edit] Goals

  1. To start or expand the length and then improve the quality of the above mentioned articles.
  2. To utilise other WikiProjects like WikiProject Albums and WikiProject Songs to its maximum potential and also keeping in line with the two projects.
  3. To make sure that all facts in the articles are correct and backed up with citations and errors rectified.
  4. To eventually get at least one of those articles up to Featured Article status.

As of now only a few articles look decent in my opinion, the rest lacks enough information to give readers a better understanding of the topic and its relation to Enigma. Mostly it's the background of the topic and its impact and reception within the public that's lacking here. In line with the goals as mentioned above, this WikiProject also aims to give other articles like the ones related to The Beatles a run for their money! --Andylkl [ talk! | c ] 18:52, 1 March 2006 (UTC)
