Andrei Nazarov (born May 22, 1974 in Chelyabinsk, U.S.S.R) is a Russian professional ice hockey player who most recently played for the Minnesota Wild of the National Hockey League, playing a handful of games during the 2005-2006 season. He has also played for the San Jose Sharks, Tampa Bay Lightning, Calgary Flames, Anaheim Mighty Ducks, Boston Bruins and Phoenix Coyotes.
