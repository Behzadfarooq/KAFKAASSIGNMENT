Andrea Ciliberti is a beauty queen from Kansas City, Missouri who has competed in the Miss USA pageant.

Ciliberti won the Miss Missouri USA 2005 title in a state pageant held in Kansas City, Missouri in December 2004. She had previously placed second runner-up at Miss Missouri Teen USA 2002. Ciliberti succeeded Ashley Litton, who had become Miss Missouri USA after the original titleholder, Shandi Finnessey, won the Miss USA title in April 2004.

Ciliberti represented Missouri in the Miss USA 2005 pageant broadcast live from Baltimore, Maryland in April 2005. She did not place in the nationally televised pageant, which was won by Chelsea Cooley of North Carolina.

Ciliberti graduated from Winnetonka High School in 2001 and is an accounting student at Maple Woods Community College.[1] In 2007 she appeared on the third season of reality television show Beauty and the Geek and was eliminated in the third week.[2]
