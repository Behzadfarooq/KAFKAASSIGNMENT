And the Rock Cried Out, No Hiding Place is an episode from the third season of the science-fiction television series Babylon 5.

[edit] Plot synopsis

Spoiler warning: Plot and/or ending details follow.

Ambassador Londo Mollari arranges the death of Lord Refa. John and Delenn share the 2nd half of a "first kiss."

[edit] Arc significance

  • Lord Refa is dead, killed by a conspiracy of convenience between Londo and G'Kar.
  • The White Star fleet is unveiled, followed by Sheridan and Delenn's mutual feelings for each other.
  • Sheridan has deduced the Shadows' battle plan - to push as many refugees as possible into a single area to strike and deal a devastating blow to the morale of the alliance.
