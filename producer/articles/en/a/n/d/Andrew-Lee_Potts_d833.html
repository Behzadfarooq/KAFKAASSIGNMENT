Andrew-Lee Potts (sometimes credited as Andrew Potts) is an English actor who is known primarily for his roles in British television.

He attended Leeds Performing Arts School, and began his career in Musical Theatre. He has appeared in many British television shows, including Children's Ward, Heartbeat, Daziel and Pascoe, Strange, Foyle's War, Fat Friends and Taggart.

He is currently starring as cast regular Connor Temple in ITV's science fiction series Primeval, appearing alongside his real life girlfriend, Hannah Spearritt.

He is the brother of actress Sarah-Jane Potts.
