Andrzej Ziemianski

Andrzej Ziemiański (January 17, 1960 in Wrocław, Poland) is a Polish science fiction writer. he was the winner of the Janusz A. Zajdel Award in 2001 for his short story Autobahn nach Poznań and in 2003 for his short story Zapach szkła (The Smell of Glass).
