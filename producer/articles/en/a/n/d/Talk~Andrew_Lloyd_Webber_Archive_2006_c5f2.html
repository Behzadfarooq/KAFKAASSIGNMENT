Controversey

There was a section on the controversey of Andrew Lloyd Webber's music. Is it removed? AQu01rius (User | Talk | Websites)  01:44, 17 October 2006 (UTC)

The note about the plagiarism of Pink Floyd's "Echoes" keeps getting removed, I get the feeling some of the editors are biased. --81.242.224.238 22:16, 24 October 2006 (UTC)

I'm sorry, I should have explained my reason for removing this more clearly (I really don't like to edit out other people's work). There are two problems with the inclusion of this: one is that Andrew Lloyd Webber has become something of a target for claims of this kind: eg. Repps also made a claim on some Phantom music (he lost his case in court). The second is that Waters and Repps don't own the chromatic scale. It's true that I don't think there is any validity to these claims so you could say I am biased in that respect. But I don't think that labelling ALW a plagiarist is particularly objective either. I hope this explains my reason more effectively.
