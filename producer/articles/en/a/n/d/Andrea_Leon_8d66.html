Andrea Leon

Andrea Leon is a popular Latin actress who has appeared in such television series as La Ley del silencio [1] (Telemundo), All Saints [2] (Australia's 7 Network) and Young Lions [3] (Nine Network Australia), as well as the feature films Hood of Horror, Man-Thing [4] (Marvel/Lionsgate) and Shiver. [5]

Born and raised in Sydney, Australia, she attended the Actors College in Sydney and had a successful career in soap operas and modeling before relocating to the United States.

Her hobbies include yoga, running, pilates and boxing. She recently competed in the 2007 New York City Golden Gloves boxing tournament (trained by Greg Ardon, who also trained actress Hilary Swank for her Academy Award winning role in Million Dollar Baby).
