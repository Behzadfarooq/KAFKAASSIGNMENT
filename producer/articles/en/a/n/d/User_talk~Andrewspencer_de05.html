This article still has NPOV issues. "The very way in which NetSuite pulls everything together for your business into the one platform could be seen to be a high risk -- what if the platform goes down, is attacked, or the company running the services is bankrupted?" is one of the most blatant examples. It needs a bunch of work. Reading the article more closely, it seems to be attacking NetSuite more than advertising for them. I know that you have battled over this article in the past and removed an {{NPOV}} tag before, but I am putting one back in.
