[edit] Removal of supposed Hebrew derivation

I have removed the sentence The name Andrea in Hebrew means "Beautiful, wondrous, creation of God" because I can find no support for this in any reputable source about the etymology of names. This looks like one of those "implications" of name meanings that are sometimes found in certain baby name books, not a real historical etymology. Cleveland Kent Evans

[edit] From this article:
