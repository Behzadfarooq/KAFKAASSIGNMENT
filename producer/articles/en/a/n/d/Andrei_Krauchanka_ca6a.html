Andrei Krauchanka (born January 4, 1986) is a Belarusian decathlete.

His personal best score is 8013 points, achieved in May 2006 in Götzis.

[edit] Achievements

Year Tournament Venue Result Extra
2003 World Youth Championships Sherbrooke, Canada 2nd Octathlon
2004 World Junior Championships Grosseto, Italy 1st Decathlon
2005 European Junior Championships Kaunas, Lithuania 1st Decathlon
2007 European Indoor Championships Birmingham, England 3rd Heptathlon, 6090 PBi
