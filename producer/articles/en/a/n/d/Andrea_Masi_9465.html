Andrea Masi

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Andrea Masi (born March 30, 1981 in L'Aquila) is an Italian rugby union footballer. His usual position is in the centres. He plays for Italian club Viadana. Masi has also been capped for the national team, and was a part of their squad at the 2003 Rugby World Cup in Australia.

Masi was born in L'Aquila, Abruzzo. He was recognized as a talented player at an early age, and he made his debut for Viadana at the age of 17. He was soon called up for national selection, and made his international debut for Italy against Spain in 1999. He became a regular in the Italian squad, but did not feature in the Six Nations until 2003 when he appeared against England. He was also included in Italy's squad for the World Cup in Australia that year. He had his 2005 season disrupted by injury.
