[edit] Baha'i Faith

I regret I am no longer contributing to the Baha'i pages on wikipedia. Most of the published secondary source information has been produced by Baha'is themselves or by enemies setting out to discredit the Baha'i Faith.

Therefore I think it is impossible to create neutral articles on subjects like Baha'i Elections or Baha'i Administration whilst still adhering to the policies on No Original Research.
