I have created a few articles in Wikipedia. I have a special interest in Yoga, Meditation and Christianity. I studied and practiced Yoga and meditation under the supervision of Reverend Father Cesar Davila Gavilanes. I had the priviledge of being a member of the Association for Self-realization(Asociacion Escuela de Autorrealizacion) for many years , before moving to the United States of America. I believe that now a days man wonders in the dark. Relying in oneself we fall short of being truly happy. But in God, fulfillment quickly becomes our utmost realization. Man becomes whole.

I am also very interested in Family Medicine, obstetrics, geriatrics and advocacy of minorities.
