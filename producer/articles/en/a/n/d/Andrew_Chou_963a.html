Andrew Chou is the youngest member of the Taiwanese hip-hop group Machi. He was a student at both Coronal del Mar High School and Taipei American School, and is currently enrolled at Monte Vista High School. While Andrew does not predominantly read or speak Chinese, his efforts have endeared him to the hearts of female adolescents and since his debut in "Machi" he has been one of the most popular members of Machi. He released a solo album entitled "Oh My!!!" in 2003.

Andrew's has recently turned towards rock music as his latest solo endeavour and his latest demo, My Valentine, has reached the top 10 of Soundclick's Punk Rock charts.
