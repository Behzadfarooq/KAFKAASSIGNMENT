Andre Schembri (born on May 26, 1986 in Pietà, Malta) is a Maltese footballer midfielder currently playing for Marsaxlokk. His former club was Hibernians.

[edit] International career

He is a member of Malta national football team. He scored twice in the game versus Hungary in the autumn 2006.
