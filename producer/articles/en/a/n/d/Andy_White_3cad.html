Andy White

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Two notable musicians have been named Andy White:

  • Andy White (drummer), an American session drummer best known for the Beatles' "Love Me Do".
  • Andy White (singer-songwriter), an Irish singer-songwriter known for political protest songs with a folk-rock or punk sound.

Outside of music there are:

  • Andy White (footballer), an English football player.
  • Andy White, a cartoonist in Fort Worth, TX, USA.
