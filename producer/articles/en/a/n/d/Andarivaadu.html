Andarivaadu (Telugu: అందరివాడు) is a 2005 Tollywood comedy film directed by Srinu Vytla. The film stars Chiranjeevi, Rimi Sen, Tabu and Prakash Raj. Chiranjeevi acted in a dual role in this movie.

[edit] Story

Spoiler warning: Plot and/or ending details follow.

Govinda Rajulu (Chiranjeevi) is a 'mesthri' who drinks a lot and lives a care-free life. His son Siddartha (Chiranjeevi) on the other hand is a popular TV show host and is a more disciplined man and is the opposite of his dad. The son gets his father married to another woman (Tabu), to sombre him down. He himself is in love with Shwetha (Rimi Sen), who is the daughter of a big-time contractor, Prakash Raj. The rich man agrees to get his daughter married, but tries to create a difference between the father-son duo as he does not like the father's influence on the son. The situation comes where the Shwetha's engagement is called off and she turns revengeful to separate the family. She moves into the house and makes sketches to create differences, but fails all the time. The rest of the film is about if she succeeds or not.

Spoilers end here.
