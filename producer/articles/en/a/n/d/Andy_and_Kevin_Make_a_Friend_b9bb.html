"Andy and Kevin Make a Friend" (or "One Bang for Two Brothers") was the fifth produced episode of Mission Hill (though it was the third to air). It originally aired on June 25, 2000. It was written by Michael Panes.

[edit] Plot summary

Spoiler warning: Plot and/or ending details follow.

George Bang's sister comes to town for a science fiction convention and causes a rift between Andy and Kevin as they both vie for her affection. Andy arranges for Kevin to invite Toby, George and George's sister to sleep over at the apartment one night and George's sister ultimately ends up having a physical encounter with Andy, much to Kevin's jealous disgust.

The next day at the convention, Andy and Kevin have an epic fight (reminiscent of a scene from Star Wars Episode V: The Empire Strikes Back) that ends when George's sister angrily lectures the brothers about their childishness.

Meanwhile, Gus refuses to remove a knife embedded in his forehead until Wally makes him realize how embarrassing it is.

Spoilers end here.
