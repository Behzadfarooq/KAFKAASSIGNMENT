Andrew James Lawrence (born January 12, 1988 in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania) is an American actor. He is sometimes credited as Andy Lawrence or Andy J. Lawrence. He is the younger brother of the actors Joey Lawrence and Matthew Lawrence.
