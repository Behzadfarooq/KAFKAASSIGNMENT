Andrea Goldsmith

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Andrea Goldsmith, (born March 24, 1950), in Melbourne, Victoria, Australia) is a writer, and novelist. She graduated originally trained as a specch pathologist, and from 1987 taught creative writing at Deakin University.


[edit] Awards

The Miles Franklin Award The Prosperous Thief, shortlisted 2003
NBC Banjo Awards, NBC Lysbeth Cohen Memorial Prize Modern Interiors, shortlisted 1993
