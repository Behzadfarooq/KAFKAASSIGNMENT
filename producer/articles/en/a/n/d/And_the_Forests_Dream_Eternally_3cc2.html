And the Forests Dream Eternally is an EP released in 1993 by Polish blackened death metal band Behemoth on the Italian label Enthopy Records. The album was later re-released in 1997 by Last Episode Records on a split EP with the Forbidden Spaces by Polish black metal band Damnation. This EP was re-released again in 2005, with the tracks from the Bewitching the Pomerania EP as bonus tracks. And the Forests Dream Eternally features a pure black metal style that the band later strayed from beginning with the release of Pandemonic Incantations.

"Pure Evil and Hate" is one of the band's most popular songs and is often a closer for live performances.
