[edit] Biography

Hi, I'm Andy. I was born on November 5, 1985 in Massillon, Ohio and am currently 20 years old and living in Brewster, Ohio. I've been poking around Wikipedia for several months now (list of anon edits: Special:Contributions/24.140.57.34, and there should be an|some IP[s] in the 130.101.0.0/16 block somewhere but I can't figure out what it was) and just (on July 4, 2005) got around to making an account. I'm an administrator at WikiTI, a TI graphing calculator wiki (WikiTI:User:AndyJ).

I'm currently a student at the University of Akron, in Akron, Ohio for Computer Science. In my free time I like to play CounterStrike: Source or work on my map for it, write programs for my TI-83+ or TI-89 Titanium, watch scifi shows on television (specifically Stargate SG-1, Stargate Atlantis, Star Trek: The Next Generation and the rest of the Star Trek series, and Gene Roddenberry's Andromeda) , The Amazing Race on GSN, and [adult swim]. I also sometimes watch MythBusters, Junkyard Wars (what happened to that show anyway?), Wheel of Fortune, Jeopardy!, and whatever else I feel like. See also User:Andy Janata/Television.

I'm also a semi-avid anime watcher. I've watched Fullmetal Alchemist and Hikaru no Go, and have Naruto sitting around waiting for me to have time. Fullmetal Alchemist is what started my anime craze; it's currently my overall favorite show ever. These shows have gotten me majorly into JPop, of which most of the music I listen to anymore is. I also listen to country music. See also User:Andy Janata/Anime and User:Andy Janata/Music.
