Andrew "Andy" Frank Millen (born 10 June 1965, in Glasgow) is a Scottish professional footballer who plays in defence. He currently plays for St Mirren in the Scottish Premier League.

Millen is currently the oldest registered player in the SPL. He has previously played for St Johnstone, Alloa Athletic, Hamilton Academical, Kilmarnock, Hibernian, Raith Rovers, Ayr United, Greenock Morton and Clyde.
