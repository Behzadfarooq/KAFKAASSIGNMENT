Andrea Ferrari

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Andrea Ferrari (born 1 June 1986 in Milan) is an Italian football (soccer) goalkeeper. Despite having been designated the number 1 shirt, he is still the third choice goalkeeper at Atalanta B.C..
