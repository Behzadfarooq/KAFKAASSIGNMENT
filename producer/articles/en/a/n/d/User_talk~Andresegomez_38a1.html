[edit] Delicious King’s Tree or DKT

DKT is a combination of drinking games created by Andres Gomez and Joe Ziegler. It involves King’s Cup, Palm Tree and Three Man. You will need a deck of cards, two dice, and a can or bottle. Place the can in the middle of the table and spread the cards around evenly. All the cards should be touching.


[edit] RULES

The player to the left of the dealer should draw first. Each card has a meaning(see below) that the player must do. To “break the circle” means a player removed a card that caused the ring of cards around the can to no longer be in a ring. This is a three drink penalty. Leave your card in front of you until it is your turn again. At this time you must stack your card on the can with two corners facing out, or hanging off the ledge. You are allowed to knock one card off, but you must restack it. If you knock more then one card off the game is over and you must finish the beer in the middle.


[edit] Card Values

2-is for you, give two drinks to any player

3-is for me, take three drinks

4-is for whores, all women playing the game must drink, if all male players a role of one dice and drink the total. If you roll a four you get to pass

5-is Dice Date Jive, or DD Jive, player picks another player to roll the dice with, the total of both dice is the drink total, however if doubles are rolled ie 2 sixes or 2 twos, then the players can pass the dice to 2 other players. If they roll doubles the dice are passed back and they roll again, but the total would now double i.e. a roll of 9 becomes 18 drinks

6- is for dicks, all male players drink, if no males see card 4 rules

7-catergory, pick a category like movies or types of beer, go around the table. The first person to repeat or comes up with no response must take a drink

8-Crazy Eights or Spinaroonie, pick someone to count to 8 for you. The player who drew the card must stand and spin around while drinking. The player can stop only when the counter reaches 8. You may attempt a Spinaroonie instead of the Crazy eights

9-Bust a Rhyme, Players must rhyme the first word said. Go around the table until a player repeats a word or can not think of a new one. Words that do not rhyme can’t be used like month or orange.

10-Waterfall, All players starts drinking at the same time. The person who drew the card stops first followed by the person who draws after. Go until all players have stopped the last person should be the person who last drew. Jack-Rule, create a rule. The rule can not change any card rule. It can not reduce drinks.

Queen-question, Player asks a question to another player and that player must answer with a question. The players must make eye contact. If you laugh from the question you lose and must drink. You can not answer the question asked of you before you ask your question.

King-Roll the Dice, roll both dice and drink the total. If you roll doubles then pass both dice to a player of your choice. If they roll doubles it passes back to you and your total will now be doubled. If you pass back that players total will be tripled

Ace- Social, all players cheers and drink

If there are three or less players you can the Suit Rule.


[edit] Suit Rule

When two players have the same suit of card like two Spades then players drink value of cards. A 2 of spades and a 10 of spades both drink. One player drinks ten the other two. Jack is 11 drinks, Queen is 12, King is 13, and Ace 14.
