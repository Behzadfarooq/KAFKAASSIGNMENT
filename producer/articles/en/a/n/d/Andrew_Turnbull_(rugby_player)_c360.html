Andrew Turnbull (b. 5th April 1982, Edinburgh) is a Scottish Rugby player,

Turnbull has represented both Scotland and GB at Rugby Sevens, including the 2006 Commonwealth Games, and has scored more trys for Scotland in Sevens than any other player, including 19 in 2005.

Turnbull also plays for Watsonians and the Scottish Institute of Sport, and has played for Scotland U19, Scotland U21, and the Barbarian F.C..
