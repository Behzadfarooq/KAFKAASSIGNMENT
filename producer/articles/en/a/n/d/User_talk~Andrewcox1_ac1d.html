[edit] Notes on Speedy Deletions

Hi, I meant to leave this right after I tagged the articles you created, but I got a little delayed. Sorry about that. In any case, Wikipedia has a lot of requirements for creating new separate articles. In general, individual tracks on an album probably don't meet Wikipedia:Notability requirements, unless there is significant content that can be backed up with attributable sources. You may want to consider compiling information privately or adding it to the appropriate album or band article until such time that you accumulate enough to warrant a separate article. In general, you should post one or two sentence starter articles. If you plan to add a lot to them, it's better to just wait on creating until there's more. Otherwise, you'll probably run into the same problems with other editors tagging them. Just some pointers. If you have any questions or need help with anything, let me know. Thanks! -Cquan (talk, AMA Desk) 18:25, 27 March 2007 (UTC)
