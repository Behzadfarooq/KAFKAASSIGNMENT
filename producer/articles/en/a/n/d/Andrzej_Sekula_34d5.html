Andrzej Sekuła

Born: 1954
Wrocław, Poland
Occupation: Cinematographer, Director

Andrzej Sekuła (born 1954 in Wrocław, Poland) is a Polish cinematographer and film director. He left his native Poland in 1980 and currently lives in Los Angeles.
