Andrei Maximishin

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Andrei Maximishin is a Russian ice dancer. He previously competed with Jana Khokhlova and Olga Orlova. He teamed up with Anastasia Platonova in 2003. Together, they placed 6th at the World Junior Figure Skating Championships in 2005 and 5th in 2006, and had some success on the Junior Grand Prix circuit, qualifying for and competing twice at the Junior Grand Prix Final. In their senior Grand Prix debut, they placed 5th at Skate Canada International.
