Andrew Ornoch aka Andrzej Mateusz Ornoch,(born August 21,1985 in Warsaw, Poland) is a professional Footballer who currently plays for Lombard-Pápa TFC Division 2 in the Borsodi Liga in Hungary.

Standing at 190cm, 81kg, a midfielder, began playing the Beautiful Game for the renowned North Scarborough Soccer Club. Known for producing international talents Julian De Guzman of Deportivo de La Coruña, Jonathan De Guzman of Feyenoord and Dave Simpson of AC Sparta Praha. From there he went on to Erin Mills, one of Canada's leading clubs that also produced talents such as Dave Simpson of AC Sparta Praha. After which earned an Athletic Scholarship to the University of Detroit Mercy where he was named Horizon League player of the year and offensive player of the year in 2005 on the way to leading the team to its first ever Horizon league Championship. Departing from school a year early Andrew went on to professional soccer in Hungary, but remains to this day the 4th All-Time top scorer in the History of the University of Detroit Mercy.

In 2006 Andrew was called up to the Canadian Mens National Soccer Team making his First Cap on November 15, 2006 against Hungary.
