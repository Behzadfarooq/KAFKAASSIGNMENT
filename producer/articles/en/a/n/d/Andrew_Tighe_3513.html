Andrew Tighe is an Australian actor.

As well as other stage roles, Andrew Tighe appeared in the stage production of "The Importance of Being Earnest" as "Algernon Moncrieff", with Andrew Tighe joining the cast in 1990. The production was so popular that it was an ongoing stage production between 1988 and 1992, and was televised by the ABC.
