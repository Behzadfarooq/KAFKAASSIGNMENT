Andrejs Perepļotkins or Andrey Pereplotkin (born December 27, 1984) is a footballer. He plays internationally for Latvia and for Skonto FC at club level.

He had played many foreign clubs before joined Skonto FC. He was player of Bohemian F.C., Southampton F.C., R.S.C. Anderlecht, Fili Moscow and FC Metalist Kharkiv.
