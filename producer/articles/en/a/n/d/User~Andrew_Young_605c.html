[edit] Introduction

Me and my daughter at Rawhide in Phoenix, Arizona

I was born on November 26, 1980 in San Antonio, Texas. I have been interested in computers and computer programming for as long as I can remember. I wrote my first programs on an Atari 800XL and an IBM AT in BASIC. I went to Business Careers High School from 1995-1999. After that I moved to Austin, Texas in order to attend The University of Texas. However, I only took one class - Introduction to Philosophy - before taking an extended leave. I returned to school in 2004 to study Computer Science. However, I am currently studying Linguistics instead.

[edit] Career

I generally consider myself a computer programmer as far as my career is concerned. I program mostly in Java, but I also program in C#, Perl, Bash, and various other programming languages. I am a Sun Certified Professional and I hold the Programmer, Developer, and Web Component Developer certifications.

Some of the programming subjects which I am especially knowledgeable about include:

  • Internationalization
    • Unicode
    • Legacy character encodings, especially related to CJKV
  • XML processing
  • Enterprise application design, especially with J2EE
  • Object-oriented programming

[edit] Work History

  • June, 1996 - August, 1999: Texas.Net, a local Internet service provider
    • Helpdesk Engineer
    • Jr. Network Admin
  • September, 1999 - December, 2005: IBM - Software group, Global e-Business Transformation (GeT) unit, Support Systems team
    • Helped develop IBM's software support website
  • January, 2006 - Present: ARM Ltd - Processor Division, Design Methodology team
    • Working on tools used during the processor design process
