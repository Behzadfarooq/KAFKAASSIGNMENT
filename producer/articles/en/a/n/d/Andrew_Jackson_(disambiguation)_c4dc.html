Andrew Jackson (disambiguation)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Andrew Jackson was the seventh President of the United States (1829-1837)

Andrew Jackson can also refer to:

  • Andrew Jackson (actor), Canadian film and television actor.
  • Andrew Jackson Donelson, nephew and secretary of Andrew Jackson, candidate for Vice President of the United States in 1856
  • USS Andrew Jackson (SSBN-619), a Lafayette-class nuclear-powered ballistic missile submarine.
  • Andrew Jackson University, a private, medium-sized university located in Birmingham, Alabama.
  • Andrew Jackson State Park, a 360 acre state park established in South Carolina in 1952.
  • Andrew Jackson Middle School, a middle school in Lancaster, SC
