The idea of pansexual and eunuch being related terms doesn't seem to make a whole heck of a lot of sense. A eunuch is simply someone born with male genitals who has undergone orchidectomy. Pansexual is a sexuality, not a gender identity. One can be a pansexual androgyne in a male body, or just as easily a heterosexual androgyne in a female body. Genitalia/chromosomal makeup, gender identity, and sexuality are three different things ~ what you are, who you are, and who you're attracted to. 68.233.12.105 05:53, 1 February 2006 (UTC)
