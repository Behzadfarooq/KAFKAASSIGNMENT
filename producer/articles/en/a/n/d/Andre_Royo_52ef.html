Andre Royo (born in 1968 in the Bronx, New York) is an American actor of Cuban heritage.[1] He is best known for his role on the HBO program The Wire as "Bubbles".

Andre attended Mount St. Michael Academy in the Bronx, NY from 1982 through 1986 alongside such notables as Sean "Diddy" Combs and Craig Grant (Oz). The character Bubbles is the antithesis of what Andre is about. Always upbeat, never down, a pleasure to be around and anti-drug, Bubbles is certainly the hardest character he'll ever have to portray.
