Andromeda may be:

  • Andromeda (mythology), the daughter of Cepheus and Cassiopeia, wife of Perseus
  • Andromeda Galaxy
  • Andromeda (constellation), in which the Andromeda Galaxy is located
  • Andromeda polifolia the monotypic genus containing bog-rosemary, a plant of the heath family
  • Andromeda, the common name for several species of plant in genus Pieris
  • Andromeda (TV series) a science fiction television series
  • Mount Andromeda in Canada and the Antarctic
  • Andromeda (British band), a 1960's psychedelic hard rock band featuring John Du Cann
  • Andromeda (band), a progressive metal band from Sweden
  • Alesis Andromeda synthesizer
  • Andromeda Tonks, a character in the Harry Potter series of books, by J.K. Rowling
  • Andromeda (novel) a.k.a. Andromeda Nebula, a science fiction novel by Ivan Yefremov
  • Andromedans, a fictional race of beings from the Andromeda Galaxy in the Star Fleet Universe
