Page protection

I saw that you removed the {{sprotect}} tag from Zinedine Zidane earlier. Just for future reference, remember that the tag itself doesn't affect page protection in any way – it's there to tell people why they can't edit and to let the community better keep track of which pages are and aren't protected. Check the logs of Zinedine Zidane, and you'll see that it's been semi-protected since 19:20, 10 July 2006, when Samuel Blanning protected it with the rationale, "still being vandalised every few minutes by different IPs."

Page protection and unprotection is done through a special feature only administrators can access. Adding and removing tags is only for informational purposes, and a useful one at that. — Rebelguys2 talk 14:38, 12 July 2006 (UTC)
