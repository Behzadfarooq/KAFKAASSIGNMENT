Andy Croft (b.1966 in Lancashire UK).

Educated in the standard working class system.

Started in the entertainment business by the way of a promotions and modelling in the North of England.

He enrolled at Whitakers Drama school and studied the craft which he had a natural talent for.

Very quickly moving upwards in the business, he moved to London to join Central Casting. On the very first day of joiningCCA he was cast in "A Very British Coup". That was the start he needed.

The roles continued with movies including, "Batman", "Indiana Jones" and "Charlie". With the big Hollywood blockbusters under his belt, roles in the North of England came flooding in.

Yorkshire TV cast him in "Till We Meet Again". 

Granada TV, he was cast in a less successful soap named "Families ".

The North of England was not the place to be for Andy. Returning to London, he was straight back into the swing of things working with his friend Dennis Quaid in "Great Balls of Fire".

"The Krays" and "Dangerous Love" followed.

He started living the celeb lifestyle with a string of jet-set pals. Hitting the clubs of London and LA seemed more important than acting.

He still worked on "Dealers" "Three Musketeers" and "Henry V".

He had enough of the party lifestyle and the movies, and decided to quit and buy a nightclub and a wine bars in the North of England.

Now in 2007 he is back !

Recently starring as David in the forth coming movie Lilith. Based on the book of the same name written by Paul Kidd.

I will keep this site posted of Andy's work. You will hear about hear first
