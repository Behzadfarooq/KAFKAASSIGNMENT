Andrew E. is a Filipino rap artist & comedic actor.

[edit] Biography

Andrew E (the 'E' stands for his last name, Espiritu) began his career in 1991 when he released his first single "Humanap Ka Ng Panget". The combination of his catchy storytelling rap style, irresistible beats, naughty and often hilarious lyrics made him unique in Philippine pop culture.

Due to his double-entendre lyrics, some of his songs had been criticized for its allegedly suggestive lyrics.

Andrew's career branched out as he began to star in comedy films based on his songs. He has more than 25 films under his belt.

In the mid 90s, Andrew founded Dongalo Wreckords for the purpose of discovering and producing new Philippine rap talents. One popular rap group that got its start with Dongalo is Salbakuta. Their song S2pid Luv became a big hit and was made into a popular movie which starred Andrew.

Andrew spent some time in Japan in the late 1990s, performing in clubs and eventually releasing a song in Japanese about the misadventures of a tourist in Tokyo.

He is married and lives in Don Galo, Paranaque (where his record label was named from) with his wife and son.
