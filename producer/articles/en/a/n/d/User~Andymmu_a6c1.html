I am a Computer Science undergraduate student at Manchester Metropolitan University and am very involved in the Students' Union. I am currently the treasurer of the university's Cricket Club and also play Cricket for the university and for Cheriton Fitzpaine Cricket Club in Devon in the United Kingdom where I have a permanent home.

I am a world record holder with Cheriton Fitzpaine Cricket Club as a player in the World's longest cricket marathon. We played for 27 hours and 34 minutes.

I am a member of the Scout Association and attended the 20th World Scout Jamboree in Thailand as a participant. I will be a member of the International Service Team at the 21st World Scout Jamboree at Hylands Park in Essex.

I share my birthday with Wikipedia (15 January), although I am 16 years older, having been born in 1985.
