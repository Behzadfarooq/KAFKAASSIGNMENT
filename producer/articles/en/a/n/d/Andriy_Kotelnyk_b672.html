Andreas Kotelnik (born December 29, 1977 in Lviv, Ukraine) is an Ukrainian boxer who won the Lightweight Silver Medal at the 2000 Summer Olympics.

Kotelnik turned pro in 2000 and won his first 23 bouts prior to losing to Souleymane M'baye in 2004. In 2005 he lost to Junior Witter, but later in the year defeated former Olympian Mohamad Abdulaev.
