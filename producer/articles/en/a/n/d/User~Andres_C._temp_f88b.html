Do Not Feed the Trolls
Feeding a troll, by engaging in such highly productive activities as responding to insults hurled bravely from the security of a distant computer, defending yourself against a personal attack from a complete stranger, holding heated debates with fanatics for weeks on end, taking part in edit wars, or engaging in any activity which may indicate to a troll that he has got your attention, may turn your experience in Wikipedia into a living hell, causing you to lose all interest in doing what you came here for in the first place, and wishing you never had opened a user account on this site.


Some Good Advice from Wikipedia:
  • Follow the Five Pillars of Wikipedia
  • Respect Wikiquette
  • Be civil to others
  • Assume good faith
  • Do not make personal attacks
  • Stay cool
  • Do not feed the trolls
  • Reach Consensus
  • Don't be a fanatic
  • Don't be a dick (from Meta)

and finally:

  • Remember Hanlon's razor
