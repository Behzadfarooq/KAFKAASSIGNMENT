Born: May 19th, 1977

Nationality: American

Andy Greenwald is a writer of social commentary, specifically about popular music. He is currently a senior contributing writer at Spin Magazine, and has also written for such publications as The Washington Post, Blender, Entertainment Weekly, The Village Voice, MTV Magazine, Complex, and Magnet. He is the author of the books Nothing Feels Good: Punk Rock, Teenagers and Emo and Miss Misery: A Novel.

Greenwald grew up in Philadelphia and currently lives in Brooklyn, NY. He graduated from Friends' Central School in Philadelphia and Brown University in Providence.
