[edit] Proposed move to "Andromeda"

  • Support. No single use seems to deserve primary disambiguation, and redirecting Andromeda to the disambiguation page is silly--as long as the dab page gets primary disambiguation, it should be at Andromeda. Niteowlneils 00:41, 22 July 2005 (UTC)
  • Support, per Niteowlneils. —Lowellian (talk) 04:17, July 22, 2005 (UTC)
  • Support, Dragons flight 15:44, July 22, 2005 (UTC)
  • Oppose. The primary meaning is clearly the mythological Andromeda. -- Naive cynic 15:12, 23 July 2005 (UTC)
  • Oppose I agree with cynic. All other Andromedas are named after her. —Michael Z. 2005-07-26 16:13 Z
  • Support. The galaxy and the constellation are both as known as the mythological figure. -Hapsiainen 22:02, 10 October 2005 (UTC)
  • Support since Andromeda redirects to Andromeda (disambiguation). Vegaswikian 01:25, 31 October 2005 (UTC)
  • Support When someone types "andromedia" into the search box and hits go it is reasonable to assume they are expecting the astronomical features to come up on their screen equally as well as the mythological figure. Plus this is a reflexive redirect so it should be moved for technical reasons.—jiy (talk) 17:49, 8 January 2006 (UTC)
