Andrijevica (Cyrillic: Андријевица) is a town in north-eastern Montenegro. It has a population of 1,073 (2003 census).

Andrijevica is the centre of the municipality of the same name (population of 5,785) and is considered unofficial centre of Vasojevići region.

[edit] Population

Andrijevica is administrative centre of Andrijevica municipality, which in 2003 had a population of 5,785. The town of Andrijevica itself has 1,073 citizens.

[edit] Transport

Andrijevica is connected with rest of Montenegro by two-laned motorways. Local roads connect Andrijevica with Berane and Kolašin (both around 30 km away), where local roads merge with E65/E80 road, which is main road connection of Montenegro's coast and Podgorica with northern Montenegro and Serbia.

Podgorica Airport is around 120 km away, and has regular flights to major European destinations.
