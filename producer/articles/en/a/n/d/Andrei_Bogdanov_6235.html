Andrei Bogdanov

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Andrei Ivanovich Bogdanov (Russian: Андрей Иванович Богданов) (1692 - September 11, 1766) was one of the first Russian bibliographers and ethnographers.

Andrei Bogdanov had studied at Academician gimnasium (a gimnasium of Russian Academy of Sciences). His father was a gunpowder-maker and Andrei had assisted him in his work. In 1727 according to Peter the Great's edict Bogdanov began work at the field of typographical art.
