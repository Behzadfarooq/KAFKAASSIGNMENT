Andrew Thompson (musician)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Andrew Thompson is a musician from New York, New York[1].

[edit] Biography

Before signing with Lewis Recordings, Andrew gained some publicity on-line through his track, "We're In Business" which, along with the accompanying video, tell of a love/hate relationship between Andrew and a robot. This track became the 2nd most requested song for 2005, at Western Kentucky University's college radio station, WWHR Revolution 91.7[1].

[edit] Collaborators

Accompanying Band Members (a.k.a. the Barnyard Animals)[1]:

  • Vinnie Sperrazza - drums
  • Lori Bingel - bass
  • Sean Moran - guitar
  • Mechelle Chestnutt - viola
  • Brian Drye - synth/trombone
