Andrew Corporation is an American multinational producer of communications devices. Andrew is a global designer, manufacturer, and supplier of communications equipment, services, and systems. Andrew products and expertise are found in communications systems throughout the world, including wireless and distributed communications, land mobile radio, cellular and personal communications, broadcast, radar, and navigation.

[edit] History

Dr. Victor J. "Doc" Andrew was a physicist by training, but a radio engineer by preference when he founded the company on January 1, 1937. Initial product offerings consisted principally of antenna tuning and transmission line equipment for broadcast stations. Early on, he identified "antenna systems" as an area of technological specialization, establishing the focus of his company and marking its beginnings as a leading designer and producer of electronic communications products, systems and services.[1]
