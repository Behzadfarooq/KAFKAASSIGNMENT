Andrew Preece (born March 27, 1967 in Evesham) is an English former professional footballer. Now a manager, he is currently in charge of Worcester City.

As a player, he found success at Stockport County, where he impressed enough (including scoring the winning goal in a giant-killing feat against QPR in the FA Cup) that he signed for Crystal Palace, where he was strike partner to Chris Armstrong. He later played for Blackpool.

His assistant at Worcester City is former Manchester City captain, Andy Morrison.
