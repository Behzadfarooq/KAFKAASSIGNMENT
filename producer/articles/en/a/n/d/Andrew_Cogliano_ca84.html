Andrew Cogliano (born June 14, 1987, in Woodbridge, Ontario) is a Canadian ice hockey player. He is 5 ft 10 intall, weighs 180 lb, shoots left and plays forward. He attended St. Michael's College School and played for the Buzzers hockey team.

Cogliano was drafted in the first round, 25th overall, by the Edmonton Oilers in the 2005 NHL Entry Draft.
