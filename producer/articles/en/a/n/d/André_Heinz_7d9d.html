André Heinz

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

André Heinz (born 1969) is the son of Teresa Heinz-Kerry and the late Henry John Heinz III. He is an American of German and Portuguese-Mozambican descent. His brothers are Henry John Heinz IV and Christopher Heinz. When his mother remarried after the death of his father in 1995, Heinz became the stepson of Senator John Kerry. An outspoken environmentalist, he has frequently campaigned for his stepfather as he ran for president in 2004. He currently splits his time between Sweden and Paris, France.
