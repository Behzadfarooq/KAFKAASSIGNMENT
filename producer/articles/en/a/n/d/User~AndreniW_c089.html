[edit] Early life

AndreniW was born in the state of Illinois. He is an only child, but enjoys spending time with his family. At the age of two, he was able to read, and by the age of four, he was already programming computers with GW-BASIC.

[edit] Computer age

AndreniW has always enjoyed being on computers. He has twelve computers, seven of which connect to the Internet. He uses all of them to contribute to Wikipedia.

[edit] Wiki life

This user is one or once was of the 20 highest-scoring users on the Are You a Wikipediholic Test.
