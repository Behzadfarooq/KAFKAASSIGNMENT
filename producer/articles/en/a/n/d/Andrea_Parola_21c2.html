Andrea Parola (born 22 April 1979) is an Italian football midfielder.

[edit] Teams and clubs

  • 1997-1999: Pisa
  • 1999-2000: Poggibonsi on loan from Pisa
  • 2000-2001: PFC Naftex Burgas on loan from Pisa
  • 2001-2002: Pisa
  • 2002-2003: Grosseto
  • 2003-2005: Triestina
  • 2005-2006: Ascoli (Jan-June co-ownership with Udinese)
  • 2006-prsent: Sampdoria
