Andrei Volokitin (born 18 June 1986 in Lviv) is an Ukrainian chess player and International Grandmaster of Chess.

He was under-12 champion of the world in 1998 and achieved the grandmaster title in 2001, when only 15 years old. In 2004 he entered the top 100 on the FIDE world ranking list, then won the 73rd Ukrainian Championship (2004).
