[edit] RFB

Please be aware that many Wikipedians consider it poor form to make edits to one's own RFA or RFB. Some would express concern even at such mechanical changes as the updates of totals. The Uninvited Co., Inc. 02:24, 30 August 2005 (UTC)

I suppose everyone who objects to such things has their own rationale. Some feel that it is a sign of overeagerness, others are concerned with an attempt to manipulate the process. I myself don't care, but would hate to see opposition to your request on such grounds. The Uninvited Co., Inc. 02:40, 30 August 2005 (UTC)
