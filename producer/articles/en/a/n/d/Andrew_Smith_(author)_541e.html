Andrew Smith (author)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Andrew Smith is the author of (2005) Moondust : In Search of the Men Who Fell to Earth. HarperCollins. ISBN 0-00-715541-7. , which tells the story of the twelve (12) U.S. astronauts who journeyed to the moon from 1969 to 1972.
