[edit] Nice to look at, hey, these are all very pleasant company

2005 Fender Jaguar Baritone Custom. Note the two pickups, presence of the upper circuit, and fixed bridge tailpiece. Also it's too short for the Jazz Bass case! I love the Fender Jaguar electrics, other than that I like to keep it very simple.

Two electric 12 strings, both fairly interesting. Top is a Shergold Modulator 12. Bottom is a Maton Magnetone TB36/12, only 37 built (including some with three pickups).

Anne-Marie, my musical partner since 1973.

Two electric guitars. See Talk:Electric guitar#Bad picture redux, Talk:Electric guitar#Rosa Hurricane and Talk:Electric guitar#Gotta Say for some, um, discussion of them.

Eston fretless ABG. Yeah, I did say fretless.

The electric mandolin (left) would probably be called semi-acoustic by many, but it has a better acoustic sound than the bowl mandolin next to it, and a better electric sound than the electric mandolin I had played since the 1970s. I wasn't shopping for mandolins really, but I came back the next day and bought it.


I don't have a baryton and can't play it anyway, but isn't it lovely?
