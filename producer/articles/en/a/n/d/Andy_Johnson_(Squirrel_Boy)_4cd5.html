Andrew "Andy" Johnson is fictional character in the animated series Squirrel Boy. He is voiced by voice actress Pamela Adlon.

[edit] Andy's profile

Andy is a nine-year old boy who owns friend and pet squirrel Rodney J. Squirrel. Sometimes they get into arguments, but they somehow work it out. They also have their own secret language. Mr. Johnson (Bob Johnson) and Mrs. Johnson (Lucille Johnson) are his parents. Kyle Finkster is Andy's nemesis (along with Salty Mike). Oscar is his human friend, who is protected from getting dirty or getting hurt by his parents. Andy loves to collect combs. He is on a Little League team called The Fighting Worms, which is coached by Mr. J. (later by Rodney). He is allergic to cashews. His neighbor, Martha, has shown some signs that she has a crush on him in both episodes she appears in: Outta Sight,Birthday Boy and Trouble Date. Outta Sight shows some signs of her affection, but she shows it toward him in the episode "Trouble Date".
