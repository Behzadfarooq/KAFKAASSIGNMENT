Andrew Frampton (born September 3, 1979) is a professional footballer currently playing for Brentford. He is a tough tackling left full back who can also play in the centre of defence. He played for Crystal Palace F.C. from 1998 until 2003. He played 35 games for Palace in all competitions.

Frampton has been appointed Brentford's vice-captain for the 2006/7 season.
