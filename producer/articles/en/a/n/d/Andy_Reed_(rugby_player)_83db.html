Andy Reed (rugby player)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Andy Reed (born 4 May 1969) is a former Cornish rugby union player who played lock for Bodmin RFC, Camborne RFC, Bath Rugby, London Wasps and represented Scotland 18 times at international level between 1993 to 1999. He also represented the British and Irish Lions against New Zealand in 1993 and against Argentina in 1994.
