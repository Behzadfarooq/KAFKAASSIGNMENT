Andreas Johansson (hockey)

Andreas Johansson (born May 19, 1973 in Hofors, Sweden) is a Swedish ice hockey player. He played in the NHL for New York Islanders (1995-96), Pittsburgh Penguins (1996-98), Ottawa Senators (1998-99), Tampa Bay Lightning (1999), Calgary Flames (1999-2000), New York Rangers (2001-02) and Nashville Predators (2002-04). In the 2000/01 season he played with the Swiss club SC Bern and after the 2003/04 season he went back to Switzerland and signed with Genève-Servette HC and played with them for two seasons. In 2006/07 he will play for the Swedish club Färjestads BK, which he played for before he played in the NHL.
