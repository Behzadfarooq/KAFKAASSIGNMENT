[edit] WikiProject SpongeBob SquarePants November 2006 Newsletter

WikiProject SpongeBob SquarePants Newsletter
November 2006 Volume 1, Issue 3
About
The WikiProject SpongeBob SquarePants newsletter is a monthly newsletter posted on the first of the month. AMK152 writes and publish it. If you have any questions or comments please post them here or on AMK152's talk page. In addition, the project needs at least one more newsletter writer. If you are interested, please see the WikiProject's talkpage.
Interviews
Starting in December 2006, the Project will interview its members. The interviews will be published in the newsletter each month, and hopefully this can improve the SpongeBob SquarePants articles. See the talk page for more details.
Concerns and questions
Please leave a message with AMK152 if you have questions concerning the project. You may also post at the WikiProject's talkpage and someone will answer.
Featured articles
We are trying to make several articles good or featured articles. We would like your help reaching featured article status. We are currently working on SpongeBob SquarePants, Krusty Krab, and Bikini Bottom. Please help us improve these articles, based on the peer reviews and your knowledge of Wikipedia featured articles.
Project objectives
  • Episode Pages:
  • Infoboxes need to be complete on each episode article. (See Template:SpongeBob episode)
  • A screenshot of each title card for each episode needs to be complete on each episode article to improve it.
  • Please try to help incorperate all trivia into the rest of the articles.
  • Try to incorperate non notable quotes to Wikiquote
  • Progress Report: All infoboxes have been completed on the season one and two articles. Ghost Host, That's No Lady, Driven to Tears, Rule of Dumb, Born to Be Wild, Best Frenemies, The Pink Purlonier, and Squidwood need title cards.
  • Games: The SpongeBob game articles need to be expanded, especially the stubs. See here: Template:SpongeBob Games.
  • SpongeBob Books - On the List of SpongeBob SquarePants Media, books needed to be listed and a description of the book.
  • Featured and Good Articles - If you believe it's good enough, feel free to nominate it. Some of these articles are just great but aren't getting nominated. Adding content to let it reach the status is just as important.
Signed...
AMK152
13:54, 24 November 2006 (UTC)
Although having the newsletter appear on everyone's userpage is desired, this may not be ideal for everyone. If, in the future, you wish to receive a link to the newsletter, rather than the newsletter itself, you may add yourself to the Opt Out List.
