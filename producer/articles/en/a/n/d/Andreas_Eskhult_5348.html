Andreas Eskhult

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Andreas Eskhult (born April 1, 1977) is a Swedish Bandy player who currently plays for Hammarby IF as a midfielder. Andreas is one of the more creative members of the Hammarby team. Andreas has played for the Swedish national bandy team in the early 21st century.

Andreas has played for three different clubs. His list of clubs are as follows-

IK Sirius (1994-1997)
Hammarby IF (1998-2004)
Raketa (2004-2005)
Hammarby IF (2005-)
