Andrea Cocco (born 8 April 1986 in Cagliari) is an Italian football (soccer) striker who currently plays for Cagliari Calcio. Andrea made his Serie A debut on 21 December 2005 for Cagliari in a 1-0 defeat away to Parma F.C..
