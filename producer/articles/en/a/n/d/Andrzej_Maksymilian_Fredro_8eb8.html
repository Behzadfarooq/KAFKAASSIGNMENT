Andrzej Maksymilian Fredro (c.1620-1679) was a Polish szlachcic, writer.

He was castellan of Lwów since 1654 and voivode of Podole Voivodship since 1676.

== Marshal of the Sejm (zwyczajnego) on January 26 - March 11, 1652 in Warsaw.
