Andreas Nodl (born February 28, 1987 in Vienna, Austria) is an Austrian ice hockey player. He currently plays for the St. Cloud State University Huskies.

He was drafted in the 2nd round, 39th overall in the 2006 NHL Entry Draft by the Philadelphia Flyers.

Nodl played two seasons with the Sioux Falls Stampede of the United States Hockey League before moving on to the Huskies. In his second season with the Stampdede, he led the team with 59 points in 58 games. Incidentally, Nodl is not the only Austrian-born player to play for the Stampede. Thomas Vanek, now of the Buffalo Sabres, spent three years with the club.
