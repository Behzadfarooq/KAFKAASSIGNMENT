Andrew Strempler

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Andrew Strempler is President and Chief Executive Officer of Mediplan Health Consulting Inc. and RxNorth.com which he founded in 1999. Strempler also helped establish the Canadian International Pharmacy Association (CIPA) in November 2002.

He also helped form the Manitoba Internet Pharmacists Association (MIPA) in 2003 and sits as Vice-Chairman on the Board of Directors for the Manitoba Chambers of Commerce. In 2003, Andrew was awarded the Ernst and Young Entrepreneur of the Year Award and has been featured in The Wall Street Journal, New York Times and Fortune Magazine.
