Andrea Beccari

Andrea Beccari (born 12 June 1978 in Moncalieri, Torino) is an Italian freestyle swimmer. He won several medals mainly as member of the Italian 4 x 200 m freestyle relay. He participated for Italy in the Summer Olympic of Sydney 2000 and Athens 2004.
