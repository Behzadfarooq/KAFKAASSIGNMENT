Andrew Jarecki

Andrew Jarecki is an American documentary filmmaker, best known for Capturing the Friedmans.

Co-founder/CEO of Moviefone, which provides film schedules over the telephone and was sold to AOL in 1999, Andrew was encouraged to shoot Capturing the Friedmans by Albert Maysles.

He is the brother of documentarian Eugene Jarecki and attended Princeton University, as did his brother. He displays his relatively unknown musical ability in the theme song of Felicity, "New Version of You", which he cowrote with producer J. J. Abrams.
