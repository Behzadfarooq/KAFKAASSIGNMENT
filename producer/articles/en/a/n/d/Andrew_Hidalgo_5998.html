Andrew Hidalgo (born May 11, 1956) is the CEO and founder of WPCS International Incorporated. WPCS International Incorporated was established on May 24, 2002 and is an engineering company specializing in the design and deployment of wireless networks. Hidalgo is responsible for the company's operations and strategic direction. Since inception, the company has grown to become a multi-million dollar organization traded publicly on the NASDAQ Exchange under the trading symbol (NASDAQ: WPCS). Hidalgo was born in Bridgeport, Connecticut and attended Fairfield University.
