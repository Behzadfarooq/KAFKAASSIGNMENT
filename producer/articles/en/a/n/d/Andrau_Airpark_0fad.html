Andrau Airpark

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Andrau Airpark (IATA: AAP, ICAO: KAAP) was a public use airport located in Alief, Texas from the mid-1950's through 1998. The airport was almost directly south of the intersection of Westheimer road and Kirkwood road. The airport area today (2006) is a upscale housing development and golf course.

[edit] Airport History

Entry for Andrau Airpark in the 1985 Texas Airport Directory

Andrau Airpark opened in the 1950's.

The airport was closed on December 23, 1998.
