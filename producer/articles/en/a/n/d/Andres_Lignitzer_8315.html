Andres Lignitzer (Andreas Liegnitzer, Andrew of Liegnitz) was an early 15th or late 14th century German fencing master, a member of the "society of Liechtenauer. His teachings are preserved by the mid 15th century masters Peter von Danzig and Paulus Kal. Paulus Kal name him together with his brother Jacob as members of the "Liechtenauer society".

He may be identical with Andres Jud ("Andres the Jew") named in 3227a.
