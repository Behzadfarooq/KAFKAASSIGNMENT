Hello, and welcome to my user page. My name is Andrew William Hampe, and I am a fifteen-year-old male. I live in a small town in Wisconsin called White Lake. I have Asperger's Syndrome, so I have a lot of trouble making friends and dealing with the day-to-day sensory annoyinaces that people normally not even notice. I enjoy watching TV, playing video games, working with computers, and landscaping, preferably not digging. I am a Star Trek fan, and watch Star Trek: The Next Generation every night if I haven’t already seen it.
