André Luiz Moreira (born in São Paulo, January 11, 1974) is a Brazilian footballer whose last club was Santos FC. He is currently a free agent and without a club in 2007.

In career, started in 1993, was played in Brazil (São Paulo, Corinthians, Cruzeiro, Fluminense), Spain (Tenerife), and France (Olympique de Marseille, Paris Saint-Germain FC and AC Ajaccio). He won CONMEBOL Supercup, Copa Libertadores and Intercontinental Cup in 1993, two Recopa in 1993 and 1994, and São Paulo State Championship in 1997. For Brazilian team he played 19 matches and scored two goals. He won bronze medal at 1996 Summer Olympics.
