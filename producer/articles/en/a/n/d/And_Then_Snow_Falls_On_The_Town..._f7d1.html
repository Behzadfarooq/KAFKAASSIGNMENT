And Then Snow Falls On The Town... (Japanese: そして街に雪が降り…, - Soshite machi ni yuki ga furi...) is the twelfth episode of the anime series D.Gray-man.

[edit] Plot

Spoiler warning: Plot and/or ending details follow.

Accepting Allen's challenge, Road sends the remaining Akuma to attack the two exorcists. While they were fighting, Allen's wounds begin to return so he return back to Miranda to get healed while Lenalee holds back the attacks. The boy then realizes that if they stray too far from Miranda, their wounds will return. He informs Lenalee about this as well as the fact that Road is a human, not an Akuma. Road, who also comes to the same conclusion about Miranda, calls back the Akuma. The Noah then informs Allen that the Millennium Earl has told her everything about him: how he was cursed by his father and how he wanted to save the Akuma's souls. This is why she decides to choose him if she wants to ruin someone's life.

She immediately orders one of the Akuma to self-destruct and explains that if an Akuma is destroyed by any other means besides an Innocence, the soul cannot be saved. Alarmed, Allen rushes over to the Akuma with an intention to destroy it, but Lenalee prevents him by pushing him away from the explosion. As Allen watches in horror, he sees the soul's crying to be saved and his scar on his left eye starts to bleed. Upset with Lenalee for stopping him, he snaps at her. In return, the girl slaps him, saying that she did it because they are friends. During this time, Road laughs with glee, which infuriates Allen. Before he can attack her, Road informs the exorcists that the last Akuma is after Miranda. Luckily, Lenalee is able to make it on time and destroys it. Seeing that she is defeated, Road decides to leave and creates a portal. However, Allen points his anti-Akuma weapon at the back of her head.

The Noah decides to mock him by daring him to shoot her since she knows that he hates her for what she did, but at the same time, knowing that he is too kind to do the deed. Since she notices that Allen is crying, Road warns him that it is not good for an exorcist to cry over an Akuma's death since he will be isolated from others. As she bids farewell to Allen, she tells him that she cannot wait to play with him again in the Earl's scenario. As soon as Road disappears, the room begins to crumble so Allen tries to rush over to Miranda and Lenalee. However, the floor collapses below him, and he finds himself in a world of wrapped presents. When he opens his eyes again, he is back in Miranda's room and concludes the dimension they were in earlier is a product of Road's power. Suddenly, Lenalee cries out that something is wrong with Miranda so he immediately runs over to them. An exhausted Miranda explains that she is trying her best to activate her Innocence still because if she did not, the exorcists' wounds will reappear. Then their "Thank you's" will be meaningless. The two exorcists disagree, explaining that they are grateful that they are still alive because of her power. Allen also adds that even though they are wounded, the wounds will heal. Hearing this, Miranda relents and everyone returns to their former states.

Meanwhile, Miranda immediately runs downstairs to get medical help. She also meets Toma who helps her. When Allen wakes up some time later in the hospital, he notices Komui is in the room. He asks about Lenalee so the Head Officer explains that Lenalee suffered from nerve damage. A newcomer then assures Allen that his grandfather is taking good care of her. The boy introduces himself as Lavi and gives Allen a letter from Miranda. In the letter, Miranda writes that her clock is back to normal and everyone in town is ignorant about what happened. She also believes that her Innocence was testing her, but because of this and the two exorcists, she is able to find where she belongs. The next time Allen and Lenalee will see her, she will be an exorcist.
