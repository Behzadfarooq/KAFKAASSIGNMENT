Andrew Hendy

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

You have new messages (last change).

Andrew Hendy was a hereditary chief of the Miskito Nation on the Caribbean coast of what is now Honduras and Nicaragua from 1888 to 1889, when he abdicated in favour of his cousin. He died at Rayapura, Rio Wangki, some time after 1905.
