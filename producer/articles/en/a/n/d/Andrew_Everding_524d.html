Andrew Everding

Andrew Everding (born November 13, 1979) is the keyboardist for the post-hardcore band Thursday. He was previously a drummer in Syracuse, NY for Lavender Disaster and The Mandate of Heaven. At first, Everding simply played keyboards on War All the Time, but was soon added as a full time member of the band. A parody of Thursday's "At This Velocity" was created on Thursday's message boards dubbed "At Andrew's Velocity". This song has introduced and popularized the word Andrewalized.[1]
