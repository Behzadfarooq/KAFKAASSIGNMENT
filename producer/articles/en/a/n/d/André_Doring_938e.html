André Doring

André Doring, is a Brazilian footballer who plays as a goalkeeper. He was born in Venâncio Aires, Rio Grande do Sul, on June 25, 1972. He currently plays for Esporte Clube Juventude.

[edit] Previous Clubs

  • Internacional: 1992 - 1999
  • Cruzeiro: 1999 - 2003
  • Internacional: 2003 - 2005
  • Juventude: 2006 - Current

[edit] Honours

  • Campeonato Gaúcho: 1991, 1992, 1994, 1997, 2004, 2005.
  • Campeonato Mineiro: 2003.
  • Copa Sul-Minas: 2001.
  • Brazil Cup: 1992, 2000, 2003.
