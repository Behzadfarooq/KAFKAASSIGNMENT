Andrea Lewis (Microsoft)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Microsoft staff photo from December 7, 1978.
Top row: Steve Wood (left), Bob Wallace, Jim Lane. Middle row: Bob O'Rear, Bob Greenberg, Marc McDonald, Gordon Letwin. Bottom row: Bill Gates, Andrea Lewis, Marla Wood, Paul Allen.

Andrea Lewis was Microsoft's first technical writer. She left the company in 1983. Her financial worth is estimated to be around $2 million. She is now a freelance writer and she helped build a literary center in the former home of poet Richard Hugo.
