Andres, Illinois

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Andres is an unincorporated community in Will County, Illinois United States. It is located south of Frankfort, Illinois and a little west of Peotone on route 45.

[edit] City Info

Andres has a population of about 50 people. There is no library or any city services. It has many old buildings. There are no city streets. It is located about 50 miles away from Chicago.
