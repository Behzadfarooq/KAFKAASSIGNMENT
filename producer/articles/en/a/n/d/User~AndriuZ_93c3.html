[edit] The Tree of Life wikiproject

..,focusing on all organisms on the planet, continues to be the largest of all Wikipedia projects. It has contributors and content in many languages, and many thousands of articles to its name. // The board met online in September to discuss Wikispecies [1] (http://species.wikipedia.org/wiki/Main_Page), a proposed biological project, related to the Tree of Life, that aims to provide data on every species. This resource will feed into Wikipedias of all languages. The project, which was created later that month, will work closely with the Tree of Life Wikiproject, and similar projects across the Wikipedias.
