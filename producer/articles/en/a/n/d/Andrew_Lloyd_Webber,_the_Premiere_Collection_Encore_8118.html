Andrew Lloyd Webber: the Premiere Collection Encore

The Premiere Collection Encore is one of Andrew Lloyd Webber's classic musical collection. It was released in 1992.

Songs:

  • The Phantom of the Opera by Sarah Brightman and Steve Harley
  • Music of the Night by Michael Crawford
  • All I ask of you by Sarah Brightman and Cliff Richard
  • Tell me on a Sunday by Sarah Brightman
  • Pie Jesu by Sarah Brightman and Paul Miles-Kingston
  • Amigos Para Siempre (Friends for life) by Sarah Brightman and José Carreras
  • Love Changes Everything by Michael Ball
  • Wishing you were somehow here again by Sarah Brightman
  • Oh what a circus by David Essex
  • Any dream will do by Jason Donovan
  • Close every door by Phillip Schofield
  • The first man you remembered by Michael Ball and Diana Morrison
  • Anything but lonely by Sarah Brightman
  • Joseph Mega-remix by Jason Donovan and cast.
