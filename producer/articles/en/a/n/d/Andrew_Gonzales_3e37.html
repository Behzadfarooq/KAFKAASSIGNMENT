Andrew Gonzales

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

This article is for the former drummer of Reel Big Fish. For the Lasallian Brother, see Andrew Gonzalez. For the artist, see A. Andrew Gonzalez.

Andrew Gonzales was the original drummer for the Ska/Punk band Reel Big Fish. He recorded the drums on the albums "Everything Sucks", "Turn the Radio Off" and "Why Do They Rock So Hard?". After the album "Why Do They Rock So Hard?" Andrew left the band to spend more time with family. He recommended former Suburban Rhythm drummer Carlos de la Garza to replace him for the album "Cheer Up!".

Andrew is currently in the band Super Leo.
