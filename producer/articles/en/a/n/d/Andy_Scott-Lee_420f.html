Andy Scott-Lee

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Andy Scott-Lee (born Robert Andrew J. Scott-Lee[1] on 29 March 1980 in Bodelwyddan, North Wales) is a former British pop star of a quarter Chinese extraction, who is an ex-member of the band 3SL. After the band broke up, he appeared in the second series of Pop Idol, making it to the final seven.

More recently, he took part in the qualifying rounds to represent the United Kingdom in the 2005 Eurovision Song Contest, where he was up against, Javine, Katie Price, Tricolure and Gina G. Andy got the third most votes, behind Javine and Katie Price.

Scott-Lee's wife is the Liberty X member, Michelle Heaton and he is the brother of the former Steps singer Lisa Scott-Lee. He has also been seen as part of his sister's MTV reality TV show Totally Scott-Lee.

Andy appeared as a celebrity guest on ITV's The Mint.
