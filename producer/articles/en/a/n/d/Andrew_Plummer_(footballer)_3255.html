Andrew Plummer (born 3 October 1989) is a professional footballer who currently "plays" for Ipswich Town.

Andrew attended Stowupland High School for three years whilst working his way through the Ipswich Academy. He is currently Ipswich's third choice keeper.

Andrew was born in Ipswich but was bought up in Finningham with his parents. He was spotted by Town scouts whilst playing for the Suffolk County U13s. He also received offers from Norwich City and Colchester United but he opted to sign for Ipswich Town as he does not wish to go so far from his parents.
