Andy King (born October 29, 1988 in Devon) is a Welsh football midfielder who plays for Leicester City in the English Football League Championship.

A product of the youth academy he has yet to make a first team appearance for Leicester City, but has been given a squad number this season.

He is also a Wales U19 international. He is a midfielder with a great passing ability and also a keen eye for goal. He is admired greatly by a member on Foxestalk named Thracian, who predicts a bright future for Andy King.
