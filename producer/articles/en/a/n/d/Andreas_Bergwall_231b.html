Andreas Bergwall

Andreas Bergwall (born June 10, 1974) is a Swedish Bandy player who currently plays for Hammarby IF Bandy as a goalkeeper. Andreas is the younger brother of teammate Marcus Bergwall. Andreas has played many games for Sweden's national team and has played in several Bandy World Championships and is regarded as one of, if not the best goalkeeper in the world.

Andreas has played for several different clubs. His list of clubs are as follows-

Lesjöfors IF
IFK Kungälv (1996-1998)
Västerås SK (1998-2004)
Vetlanda BK (2004-2005)
Hammarby IF Bandy (2005-)
