Android Lust is the Industrial solo project of Shikhee, combining elements of rock, industrial, gothic and classical styles into a blend of music that she describes as "electronic and dark." Her music is embraced by the gothic culture for its themes of hurt, loneliness, lust, rejection, impatience and anger, though Shikhee dislikes the 'goth' label.

Additional Live Members:

  • Christopher Jon: Drums, Keys, Backing Vocals
  • Bret Calder: Bass, Guitar, Backing Vocals

[edit] Official Bio

 The neutrality of this section is disputed.
Please see the discussion on the talk page.

Android Lust is the vision of its sole creator, Shikhee. Born in Bangladesh, schooled in England and in the United States, Shikhee's background is as varied as the music she creates: running the emotional gamut from loss to discovery, from regret to carnivorous loathing. Borrowing elements from a variety of styles Android Lust presents a blend of electronic rock music that is dark, icy, and fiercely emotional.

After two independent label releases (including 1998's critically acclaimed debut Resolution) Shikhee released The Dividing on her own, bringing her to the attention of Projekt Records who quickly signed and re-released it to eager music fans all over the world. Outburn Magazine calls The Dividing "unconventional and adventurous - 10 out of 10" while The Boston Phoenix says "The Dividing never misfires; it feels almost - but not quite - slick, its arranging smart, its themes ingenious". Jane Magazine said "The Dividing is filled with intensely passionate lyrics delivered in low whispers and startling, penetrating screaming". They even gave away copies of the album to 30 lucky readers.

In addition, a video was shot for the song "Stained" by director Dan Ouellette (whose credits include production design on David Bowie's "Dead Man Walking" video). "Stained" has received regular airplay on music video shows and channels across the United States, as well as 120 Minutes on MTV Latin America and Australia's music video show "rage". "Stained" and other songs from The Dividing have also been featured heavily on CBS' "NCIS" (including an on-air mention of the band's name) and MTV's The Real World and Road Rules, as well as receiving club and radio play all over the world.

Shikhee's third full length album, Devour, Rise, and Take Flight, was recently released on February 21st, 2006. "Dragonfly", the first single for the album was released on October 18th. The video was again directed by Dan Ouellette and keeps the bizarre style of her "Stained" video, using futuristic, yet disturbing, mechanical pieces.

[edit] About Shikhee

Originally from Bangladesh, Shikhee was sent away from the unstable political environment by her parents for schooling in England. In 1999 she moved to New York City. She also has a cat Motu named after her MOTU hard disk recording system.

[edit] Equipment

Shikhee uses a dual 867 processor G4 running Mac OS X, and a 400 MHz G4 running Mac OS 9. Running Digital Performer as an audio/MIDI sequencing software and the modular soft synthesiser Reaktor as her main synthesiser, as well as an Access Virus, Ensoniq ESQ-1, Synthesizers.com modular, and Korg Z1. For vocals Shikhee uses a Neuman TLM 103 microphone, a Great River pre amp, and Empirical Labs Distressor, which is an outboard compressor. [1]
