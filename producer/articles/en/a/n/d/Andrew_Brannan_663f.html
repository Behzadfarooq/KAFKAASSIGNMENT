Andrew Brannan (born May 12, 1981) is a Canadian professional wrestler. Currently working for World Wrestling Entertainment, Brannan is known for a wonderful reputation to sell wrestling moves but is sometimes reported about his bad attitude backstage.

[edit] World Wrestling Entertainment

Andrew Brannan formed a tag team with Calum Davidson named The Future Shock in Ohio Valley Wrestling.

Brannan is continuely wrestling in Ohio Valley Wrestling for World Wrestling Entertainment along with his best friend Calum Davidson he is involved in a major storyline in a stable with Calum Davidson and Ryan Simpson, He is also a former OVW Champion but only held it for 2 weeks and suddenly lost the championship to Kenny Dykstra he is also a former 1 time OVW tag team champion with Calum Davidson.
