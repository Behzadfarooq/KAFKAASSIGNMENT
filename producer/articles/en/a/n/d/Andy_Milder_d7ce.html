Andy Milder is an American actor and voice actor. He has appeared in such films as Apollo 13, Armageddon, Rumor Has It, and Domino. He has also appeared in such television shows as Weeds, Fame L.A, Star Trek: Voyager, Star Trek: Deep Space Nine, and Six Feet Under. Milder provided narration for Ballroom Bootcamp, 101 Most Starlicious Make-Overs, and Wrecks to Riches. His voice can be heard on commercials for Big Lots, Carvel, The Hartford, and Denny's. He provides the voice of Lightning Lad in the 2006 animated series Legion of Super Heroes.

Born in Omaha, Nebraska, Milder moved to Santa Monica, California at a young age. He went to the University of California, Berkeley, majoring in Economics before studying at the American Conservatory Theater in San Francisco. While at Berkeley, Andy, became a brother of the Alpha Epsilon Pi Fraternity.

Milder currently lives in Manhattan Beach, California with his wife, Dr. Betty Lee.
