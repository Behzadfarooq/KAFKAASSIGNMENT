Andrew McKenna

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Andy McKenna is the chairman of the Illinois Republican Party and the president of Schwarz Paper Company. His father, of the same name, is Non-Executive Chairman of McDonald's Corporation

[edit] Early life and education

Andy graduated from La Lumiere School, a small Catholic boarding school near LaPorte, Indiana. The same school where John G. Roberts attended. He received his bachelor's degree from the University of Notre Dame.

In 1980, Andy married his wife, Mary. They have four children: Madeleine, Shawn, Drew and Mary.
