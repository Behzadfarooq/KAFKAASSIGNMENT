Andrew Neiderman

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Andrew Neiderman (born 1940) is a American author. He became the ghost writer for V. C. Andrews following her death in 1986. He is married to the former model Diane Wilson. They have two children, Melissa, a head teacher in Palm Springs, and Erik, a Jet pilot Captain. They have three grandchildren, Dustin, a budding new Bob Dylan, Hannah Rose and Emily Grace, both models in LA. Neiderman is best known as the author of The Devil's Advocate, and the screen writer of V. C. Andrews' Rain.
