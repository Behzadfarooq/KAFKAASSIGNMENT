Andrés Romero

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Andrés Romero (born 8 May, 1981) is an Argentine golfer who plays on the European Tour. He won his tour card by finishing 14th on the Challenge Tour rankings in 2005. He had a successful rookie season highlighted by a tied second finish at the Scottish Open and a tied eighth finish in The Open Championship, and finished placed 35th on the Order of Merit.

In July 2006 he broke into the top 100 of the Official World Golf Rankings for the first time.

[edit] Professional wins

  • 2003 Cable and Wireless Masters Panama, Abierto de Medellin (Argentine)
  • 2005 Roberto de Vicenzo Classic (Argentina), Morson International Pro-Am Challenge (Challenge Tour)
  • 2006 Argentine Torneo de Maestros (Tour of the Americas)
