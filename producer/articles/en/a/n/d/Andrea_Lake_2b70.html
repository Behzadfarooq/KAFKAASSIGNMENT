Andrea Lake

Andrea Lake is a 32 year old high school graduate and business owner. She was on The Apprentice 5. She has made millions of dollars between her five companies. Lake is from San Diego, California. She attended Grossmont High School in El Cajon and now is a resident of Santa Fe, New Mexico.

Although she is not a college graduate, she nonetheless managed to start her own businesses. Her first, Rhythm Styx, is a toy company and helped inspire her to start a clothing distribution company for teens. She now runs a sticker company, a t-shirt company, a clothing company, an online community linking authors with readers, and a wedding planning service. She has an older brother named Dave Lake.

[edit] Apprentice candidate

Lake was a candidate on The Apprentice 5. She was on Team Synergy and won the challenge both times she was project manager. She was fired on Week 9 because she poorly designed the brochures and lacked good team chemistry, especially with her current project manager, Allie Jablon. On her second to last episode, Andrea left Sean's team reporting that she was coughing up blood. She did return the next episode citing she had broken a blood vessel and would be alright.
