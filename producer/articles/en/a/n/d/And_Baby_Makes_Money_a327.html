"And Baby Makes Money" is the 11th episode of season five of the television sitcom Married... with Children. It premiered on December 16, 1990.

[edit] Plot summary

A Bundy uncle dies, and the Bundy family attends the reading of his will. It is stipulated in his will that the first Bundy relatives to give birth to a boy and name it after him shall inherit his fortune. The race for a Bundy boy begins, as Al and Peggy start making love on an hourly basis. Al tosses aside his discomfort of having sex with his wife and can't wait to get his hands on that money, while Peggy takes drastic measures to get what she wants out of the deal.

[edit] Guest stars

Eugene Bundy: Charlie Brill
