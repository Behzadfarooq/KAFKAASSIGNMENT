Andreas Zelinka

Andreas Zelinka (born 23 February 1802 in Vyškov, now the Czech Republic, and died 21 January 1868 in Vienna) served as the mayor of Vienna, Austria from 1861 to 1868. The citizens of Vienna called him Papa Zelinka.
