Andreas Gerdin

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Andreas 'Gordon' Gerdin (born January 11, 1976) is a Swedish guitarist, currently playing for the band Machinae Supremacy. Gerdin provided the Commodore 64-style sounds for the tracks using a SIDStation, alongside using traditional musical keyboards. He played the keyboard since Robert Stjärnström played guitar. However, after their line-up changed, he started to play the guitar.
