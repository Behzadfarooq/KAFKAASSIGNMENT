Andrey Nazário Afonso is a Brazilian Football Goalkeeper who currently plays for Steaua Bucureşti in the Liga 1 the top flight of Romania Football.

Andrey was born in Porto Alegre, Brazil playing in the top division. He was brought to the club to help with Steaua Bucureşti in the UEFA Cup.

He made his debut with Steaua on the 1st of March, 2007, in the quarter-final cup match against Oţelul Galaţi, when Steaua won 8-7 on penalties. He saved one penalty and was considered the man of the match.


Clubs:
2003-16/03/2005: Grêmio-RS
17/03/2005-19/12/2005: Atlético-PR
20/12/2005-16/12/2006: Figueirense-SC
Since 17/12/2006: Steaua Bucharest - Romania


Club titles:
