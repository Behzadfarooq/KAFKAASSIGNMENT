Andrei Travin

Andrei Travin (also known as Andrey Travin) is a Kazakh midfielder and defender. He was born on April 27, 1979. He plays for FC Almaty. Andrei also plays for the Kazakhstan national football team. His height is 171 cm and his weight is 70 kg.

[edit] Career

[edit] Kairat Almaty

Andrei Travin played for the club Kairat Almaty from 1998 - 2000, totalling 3 years. In 1998, he scored 2 points out of 4 games. In 1999, he scored 1 point out of 20 games, and in 2000, he scored 0 points out of 12 games. This totals 3 points in 36 games.

[edit] Vostok Oskemen

In 2001, Andrei played for the club Vostok Oskemen. There, he scored 6 points out of 30 games.

[edit] Jenis Astana

Travin played for the club Jenis Astana in the year 2002. There he scored no points out of 21 games.

[edit] Cesna Almaty

Andrei Traving played for the club Cesna Almaty in 2003. There he scored no points out of 7 games.

[edit] FC Almaty

Andrei Travin currently plays for the club FC Almaty. He has played there from 2004. In 2004, he scored 3 points out of 31 games. In 2005, Andrei scored 2 points of 23 games. In 2006 and 2007, he has not played in any games and consequently, has not scored any points. Andrei Travin scored a total of 5 points for FC Almaty.

[edit] National Team

Andrei Travin has played under the number 6 for the Kazakhstan national football team since 2005.
