Andy Wilkinson (born on August 6, 1984 in Stone, England) is a footballer, currently playing for Stoke City. During 2006, he was on loan to Blackpool. Between January and february 2007 andy enjoyed a run in the stoke first team, impressing fans with his committed style of play.
