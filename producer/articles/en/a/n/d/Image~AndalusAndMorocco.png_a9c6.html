Image cropped from en:Image:Spain_topo.jpg - The arrow points to the mouth of the Guadalquivir river delta and Sanlúcar de Barrameda, where Ferdinand Magellan stayed in 1520. The upper part of the image is Andalusia; the lower part of the image is Morocco. Gibraltar lies to the east, on Strait of Gibraltar, which divides Andalusia from Morocco.
