Andriy Borzukov

Andriy Borzukov (Ukrainian: Андрій Барзуков), born 20 October 1971 in Kherson, is a retired Ukrainian flatwater canoer and former world champion.

He represented his country for over a decade as a member of the four-man (K4) kayak. In 2003 they won the world championship over 200m at Gainesville, USA. This was Ukraine's first world gold in the men's kayak events since independence. Borzukov retired in 2005.

[edit] Medals

  • K4 200m gold medal - 2003 World Championships - Gainesville
  • K4 200m bronze medal - 2001 World Championships - Poznań
  • K4 200m silver medal - 2000 European Championships - Poznań
  • K4 200m silver medal - 1999 European Championships - Zagreb
  • K4 200m bronze medal - 1994 World Championships - Mexico City
