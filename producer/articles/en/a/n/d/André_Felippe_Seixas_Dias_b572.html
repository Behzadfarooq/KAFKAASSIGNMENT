André Felippe Seixas Dias or simply André Dias (born March 11, 1981 in Montes Claros, is a Striker. He currently plays for Vasco on loan from Iraty for the 2007 season.

[edit] Honours

  • Brazilian League: 2002
  • South American U-20 Championship 2001

[edit] Contract

2nd January 2007 to 31st December 2007
