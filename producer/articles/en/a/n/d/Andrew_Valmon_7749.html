Andrew Valmon (born January 1, 1965 in Toms River, New Jersey but raised in Manchester Township, New Jersey) was an American 400 meters runner who won the silver medal at the World Indoor Athletics Championships 1991 in Seville. In 1992 he won a gold medal with the American 4x400 m relay team at the Olympic Games in Barcelona. The same year, Valmon set his personal best of 44.28 s. He now coaches for the Maryland Terps and coaches a summer track and field camp and the University. Married Meredith Raiey also an Olympic runner.
