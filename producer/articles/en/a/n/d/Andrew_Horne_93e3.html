Andrew Horne

Andrew Horne is a 44-year-old United States Marine Corps Reserves Lieutenant Colonel who served in the Persian Gulf War and the Iraq War. He initially supported the 2003 invasion of Iraq but has since revised his stance on the matter, stating "Iraq is a symptom of what's wrong with this Administration."

In 2006, Horne, also an attorney from Louisville, Kentucky, was a candidate for the Democratic nomination for Kentucky's 3rd congressional district seat. According to the Louisville Courier Journal, Horne declared his candidacy on Wednesday, December 14, 2005, and was subsequently endorsed by Ohio politician, Iraq War veteran and former Congressional candidate Paul Hackett.

In a Primary with three other cadidates Horne (and two others) lost to John Yarmuth in the Democratic Primary on May 16, 2006.
