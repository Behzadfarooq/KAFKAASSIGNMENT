[edit] Added Visual Effects

Added visual effects to the list of Bazin's "dislikes". Perhaps "compositing" would be a better word. For example, Bazin's admiration for the balloon in Le Ballon Rouge is admiration for a visual effect - but Bazin enjoys the effect insofar as it was produced "on location" or "in front of the camera", (so to speak), rather than through any subsequent manipulation of the image (eg. through compositing). So it is not visual effects per se, but the manner in which they are made, ie. "after the fact", that attracts Bazin's criticism.
