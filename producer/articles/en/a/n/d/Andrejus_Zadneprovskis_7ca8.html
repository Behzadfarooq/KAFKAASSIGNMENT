Andrejus Zadneprovskis (born August 31, 1974 in Kaliningrad, Russian SFSR) is a Lithuanian athlete who won the silver medal in the Modern Pentathlon at the 2004 Summer Olympics in Athens, Greece.

He also won the silver medal in the Modern Pentathlon World Championships 2006 in Guatemala.
