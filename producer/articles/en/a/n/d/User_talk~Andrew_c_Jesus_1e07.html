We need here to combine the Christian views bio and NT views bio into one somewhat uncondensed biography, then summarize it in the Jesus article. I am totally confused. How can you say that the Jesus bio section is "hugely condensed" when it is the most detailed and indepth of the 3. And what I have already done, is to include even more information not found in any of the 3, but instead in the more in depth articles found in the Template:Gospel Jesus. My purpose is to create a bio of Jesus, that is more in depth than what is there now, but less in depth than the individual topical articles (linked from the template). This bio will go in the NT views article. The Christian views article won't have a bio. And the Jesus article will have a summarized version of the NT view article. For the most part, I think we are on the same page, but a couple of your comments confused me so I want to clear things up if possible.--Andrew c 23:16, 20 July 2006 (UTC)
