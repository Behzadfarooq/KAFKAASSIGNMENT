Andersen Co., Ltd. (株式会社アンデルセン, Kabushiki-gaisha Andersen?) is a Japanese Bakery based in Hiroshima, Japan.

"Andersen" is the "restaurants with bakery" of the ANDERSEN GROUP.

The 1st flag ship restaurant was opened in the A-Bombed building bought from Mitsui Bank-Hiroshima at Hon-dori in Hiroshima in October 1967. The building was built in 1925.
