Andrew Imrie (born February 26, 1981 in Canberra, Australia) is a professional footballer currently playing with Nationwide Conference side Tamworth, where he plays as a Midfielder.

During his time with Taringa Rovers, Imrie was described as arguably their fittest player, but many seen this as no surprise due to the fact that Andrew had studied exercise science at the University of Queensland[1].

As of March 2007, Imrie joined Nationwide Conference side Tamworth. He had been training with the club for 3 weeks prior to signing. Imrie had little problem obtaining International clearance as he owns a British passport[2].
