Andy Pratt (baseball)

Andrew Elias Pratt (born August 27, 1979 in Mesa, Arizona) is a Major League Baseball pitcher with Milwaukee Brewers organization and has a career earned run average of 15.00 in 5 career appearances with the Atlanta Braves and Chicago Cubs. He was selected by the Texas Rangers in the 9th round of the 1998 amateur draft.
