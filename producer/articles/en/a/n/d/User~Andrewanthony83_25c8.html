[edit] Employment

Andrew currently works for a financial services company Thinc Group (previously Thinc Destini Group) where he holds the posistion of Group I.T. Network Administrator. Andrew works from the Telford office.

[edit] Volunteer Work

Andrew has been Divisional Superintendent for Priory Green Quadrilateral division of "Priory Green St. John Ambulance" in Pendeford since May 2005. Andrew is overall responsible for the running of the Quad. Craig Alsop and Richard Rushforth are Assistant Youth Leaders for the Cadets (10-18yrs) section of the Quad. Also included in "Priory Green St. John Ambulance" is Woodland Badger Sett. Woodland Badgers is run by Angela Anthony & Victoria Anthony.

[edit] Education

After finishing compulsory education in 1999 at Aldersley High School in Codsall, Andrew stayed on to study in 6th Form graduating in 2001. When graduating from Aldersley High 6th Form Andrew attended The University of Wolverhampton where he studied Computer Science. He has since Graduated as a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science.

[edit] Driving History

Andrew passed his test on 17th January 2002 in Wolverhampton. Since then Andrew has taken an additional test recognised by St. John Ambulance to allow him to drive St. John fleet of vehicles including Minibuses and Ambulances.
