André Douzet (July 24, 1947-) is a French researcher and author, currently living near Perpignan, France.

[edit] Authorship

André Douzet is the author of several books in the French language. He has three English titles to his name:

  • Saunière’s model and the Secret of Rennes-le-Château: The priest’s final legacy that unveils the location of his terrifying discovery (2001)
  • The Secret Vault: The secret societies’ manipulation of Saunière and the secret sanctuary of Notre-Dame-de-Marceille (with Philip Coppens) (2006)
  • The Wanderings of the Grail: The Cathars, the search for the Grail and the discovery of Egyptian relics in the French Pyrenees (2006)

[edit] Career

André Douzet is best known for his discovery of the so-called Saunière's model, discovered in 1994, with public revelation in 1995, mapping in part the landscape of the abandoned village of Perillos, near Perpignan, France.

[edit] Media appearances

André Douzet appeared in ProSieben's documentary on The Da Vinci Code, as well a Tellus Film documentary on the Grail.
