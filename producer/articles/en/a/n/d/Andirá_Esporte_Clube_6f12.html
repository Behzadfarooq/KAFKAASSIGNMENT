Andirá Esporte Clube is a Brazilian football team which is situated in the city of Rio Branco in the state of Acre. It was founded on the 1 November 1964. Andirá Esporte Clube's home kit is a shirt with vertical black and green stripes, black shorts and black socks. The team mascot is a bat and they play their home matches at Estádio José de Melo which has a capacity of 8000. They are currently playing in the Campeonato Acreano which they have never won in their history.
