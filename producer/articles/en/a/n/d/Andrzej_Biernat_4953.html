Andrzej Biernat
born 1960
Function: member of Sejm 2005-2009
Party: Platforma Obywatelska

Andrzej Biernat (born August 11, 1960 in Urzędów) is a Polish politician. He was elected to Sejm on September 25, 2005 getting 6244 votes in 11 Sieradz district, candidating from Platforma Obywatelska list.
