Andreas Schroeder

Andreas Schroeder (born November 26, 1946) is a German-born Canadian poet, novelist, and nonfiction writer who lives in the small town of Roberts Creek, British Columbia. For many years he appeared on the CBC radio show Basic Black doing a segment on humorous crimes and criminals.

Schroeder teaches Creative Non-Fiction in the University of British Columbia's Creative Writing program.
