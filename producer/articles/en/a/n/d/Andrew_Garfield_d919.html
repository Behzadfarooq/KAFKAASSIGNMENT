Andrew Garfield (born c. 1984) is a British/American actor.

Garfield was born in the USA and moved to the UK with his family as an infant. He attended City of London Freemens School in Ashtead, Surrey and trained at the Central School of Speech and Drama.

He is primarily a stage actor, but had a significant supporting role in the first series of teen Channel 4 drama Sugar Rush.

In 2005 he won a MEN Theatre Award for his performance in Romeo and Juliet at Manchester's Royal Exchange, and won the outstanding newcomer award at the Evening Standard Theatre Awards 2006.

He is to appear in the 2007 Doctor Who episode "Daleks in Manhattan".
