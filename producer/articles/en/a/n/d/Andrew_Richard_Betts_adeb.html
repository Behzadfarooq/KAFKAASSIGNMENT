Career Notes Played college basketball at C.W. Post University (1994-97) and later at Long Beach State University (1997-98)...selected in the 1998 NBA Draft by Charlotte Hornets (#50)...moved to Italy for the 1998-99 season, signed by Fortitudo Pallacanestro Bologna...for the 1999-00 season moved to Pallacanestro Reggiana...signed for the Spanish ACB League's 1999-00 playoffs by Real Madrid...moved to Greece for the 2000-01 season, signed by AEK Athens (Greece)...played there till the 2002-03 championship...moved to Spain for the 2003-03 season, signed by Tau Vitoria.

Miscellaneous Won the 1999-00 Spanish National Championship with Real Madrid...won the 2001-02 Greek National Championship with AEK Athens...won the 2001 Greek National Cup with AEK Athens...won the 2004 Spanish National Cup (King's Cup) with Tau Cerámica Saski Baskonia Vitoria...named to the 1998 Portsmouth Invitational Tournament All-Tournament Team...named to the 1998 First Team All-Big West Conference selection...named to the 1996 Second Team All-New York City Athletic Conference...has been member of the English Junior National team...member of the English National Team...played at the World University Games.

Personal Married to Michelle Jones, former Long Beach State volleyball player. Andy and Michelle have two daughters.
