Andrew Gregory Reid, popularly known as Reidos (born September 7, 1985) is an amateur golfer from Waterloo, Ontario, Canada. He is known across Southwestern Ontario as the 20-year-old University of Waterloo student who hit two hole-in-ones in a single round of golf. He achieved the accomplishment on August 16, 2006 at the Grey Silo Golf course located at RIM Park in Waterloo, Ontario. The word of his amazing feat spread across media outlets around Southwestern Ontario, as Reid appeared on several sports radio stations including the Fan 590 and had a feature on the front page of The Record.
