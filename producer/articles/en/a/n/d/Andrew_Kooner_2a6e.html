Andrew Singh Kooner (born May 11, 1979 in Kettering, Northamptonshire, England) is a Canadian boxer. He moved to Tecumseh Ontario at a young age and began boxing at age 13 out of a Windsor boxing club. He is a member of the Sikh religion. At the 2000 Summer Olympics he lost in the 2nd round in the bantamweight division. At the 2004 Summer Olympics he finished tied for fifth in the bantamweight (54kg) division. Kooner has recently turned pro and has a 6-1 Record. In 2002 he won the silver medal at the Commonwealth Games.
