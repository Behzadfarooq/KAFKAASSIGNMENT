Andrew Lande is a world traveler and international Food and Wine expert and writer, Andrew Lande was educated at the University of California and Trinity College, Dublin.

[edit] Personal life

He and his wife, Michelle Strickland, the respected Cordon Bleu chef, are the founders of MA’s Homemade Gourmet , specializing in organic specialty foods. He is the son of noted author and journalist, Nathaniel Lande, and producer Linda Hope, daughter of the legendary Bob Hope.[1] Andrew Lande lives in North Hollywood, California.

[edit] Bibliography

The Cigar Connoisseur written as a joint father son effort.

He is co-author of The 10 Best of Everything, an Ultimate Guide for Travelers, published by National Geographic Books, and The Cigar Connoisseur, An Illustrated History and Guide to the World’s Finest Cigars, published by Clarkson Potter, an imprint of Random House. He also wrote the acclaimed A&E Biography special: “Bob Hope: America's Entertainer” drawing on his relationship with his grandfather. [1]
