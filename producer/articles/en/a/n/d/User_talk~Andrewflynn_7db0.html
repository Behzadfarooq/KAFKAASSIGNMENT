[edit] Copyright issue with Laundry Room Studios

Hello. Concerning your contribution, Laundry Room Studios, we cannot accept copyrighted text or images borrowed from either web sites or printed material. This article or image appears to be a direct copy from http://www.laundryroomstudio.com/home.html. As a copyright violation, Laundry Room Studios appears to qualify for speedy deletion under the speedy deletion criteria. Laundry Room Studios has been tagged for deletion, and may have been deleted by the time you see this message. For text material, please consider rewriting the content and citing the source, provided that it is credible.

If you believe that the article or image is not a copyright violation or if you have permission from the copyright holder to release the content freely under the GFDL, you can comment to that effect on Talk:Laundry Room Studios. If the article or image has already been deleted, but you have a proper release, you can reenter the content at Laundry Room Studios, after describing the release on the talk page. However, for text content, you may want to consider rewriting the content in your own words. Thank you, and please feel free to continue contributing to Wikipedia. Butseriouslyfolks 03:35, 5 February 2007 (UTC)

[edit] Please do not remove deletion tags on your own writing

Welcome to Wikipedia. Please do not remove speedy deletion tags from articles that you have created yourself. If you do not believe the article deserves to be deleted, then please place {{hangon}} on the page (Please do not remove any existing speedy deletion tag) and make your case on the article's talk page. Administrators will look at your reasoning before deciding what to do with the article. Thank you. -- Butseriouslyfolks 03:37, 5 February 2007 (UTC)
