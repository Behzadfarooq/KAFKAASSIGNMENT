[edit] Lightbulb joke

Hella is a slang term which came out of Northern California and has become one of those litmus-test words which identifies someone who uses it (genuinely) as a Northern Californian. It basically means "a lot" or "very much". Very, very many people in the Bay Area use it at least occasionally.
