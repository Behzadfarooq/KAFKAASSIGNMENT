Andi Muise (born January 12, 1987), is a Canadian supermodel.

[edit] Early life and discovery

Born on January 12, 1987 in North Bay, Ontario, she was discovered at the age of 14 in a local mall by Michèle Miller, owner of the International Model Management agency, and moved to New York City at the age of 17.

[edit] Career

Muise has modelled for major brands, such as Emporio Armani, Hockley and FCUK, as well as shooting for GQ and Allure magazines, and advertising for Bloomingdale's, Intimissimi, Kenneth Cole, and Urban Outfitters; in 2005, she appeared in Victoria's Secret 10th and annual fashion show, also walking the runway for them in the year 2006. In 2006, Fashion Magazine shot Muise for her first major Canadian magazine spread, and Fashion Television documented a day in her life, by having cameras follow her around for a day.

She has walked for shows that include Abaete, Anait Bian, Lacoste, Lela Rose, Yigal Azrouel, Three As Four, Nanette Lepore, Oscar de la Renta, Adam+Eve, Trovata, and also Dolce and Gabbana. She also appeared on Project Runway, walking for designer Santino Rice on the season finale.

In the recent season of Spring 2007, Andi walked 11 shows in New York alone, her agencies not sending her out to Milan, Paris, or London for the rest of the season.

[edit] Personal life

Andi lives in Brooklyn, New York with her photographer boyfriend, Jeffrey Graetsch, of whom she has dated since she was 17. Her mother Lynn and her father Rob support her decision to become a model, and have created a sort of Brag Wall of hers, plastering mint copies of their daughter’s pictures onto a guest wall inside their home, the Wall dates back to her very first photoshoot.
