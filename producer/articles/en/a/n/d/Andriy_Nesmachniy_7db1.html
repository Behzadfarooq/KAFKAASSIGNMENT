Andriy Nesmachniy (Ukrainian: Андрій Несмачний, born February 28, 1979 in Bryansk, Russia) is a Ukrainian professional football player for FC Dynamo Kyiv in the Ukrainian Premier League. Nesmachniy is 182 cm (5'11") tall. He weighs 72 kg (158 lb). Playing as a defender under the number 26, he is also a starter for the Ukrainian national football team. It should be noted that "Nesmachniy" is a homynym for the Ukrainian "not tasty".

[edit] Accomplishments

  • Nesmachniy won the Ukrainian Championship 4 times with Dynamo (2000, 2001, 2003, 2004)
  • He also won the Ukrainian cup 3 times with Dynamo (2000, 2003, 2005)
