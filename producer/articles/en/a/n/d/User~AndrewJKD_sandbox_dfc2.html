[edit] Tragedy (9/23/03)

A woman with a high risk pregnancy is kidnapped close to her risky delivery date. Benson and Stabler are given more than one suspect that is willing to be punished for the crime.

[edit] Maniac (9/30/03)

Benson and Stabler need Huang's help to discover a connection between the murder of two teenage high school students and an illegal campaign being done by a large pharmaceutical company.

[edit] Mother (10/7/03)

Benson and Stabler are have problems with issues about doctor-patient confidentiality during an investigating of a beating and almost murder of a psychiatrist, who is suspected to be attacked by a patient, while also trying to like child abuse and unsolved homicide to the case.

[edit] Loss (10/14/03)

Benson and Stabler suspect a Colombian drug cartel is related to the rape and murder of an undercover federal agent, a case in which they must be carefurl to protect the identities of other undercover agents. As the trial approaches, Cabot is sent death threats, and feels the burden to give the case over to federal authorities.

[edit] Serendipity (10/21/03)

A dead baby sends Benson and Stabler searching for a mother, who soon turns up dead her self. Clues point the finder at a dermatologist who has matching DNA to a child molestation case gone cold. Meanwhile, Casey Novak, Cabot's replacement ADA, resists against the detectives' handling og the murders. She also feels unsure of her place on the SVU team.

[edit] Coerced (10/28/03)

During the investigation of a young schizophrenic boy's kidnapping, Stabler, with the help of Huang, discovers more crimes at an adult group home.

[edit] Choice (11/4/03)

Benson feels affected personally when she discovers that there are many cases of pregnancy negligence after a man, arrested for harrassing his pregnant wife for drinking during her pregnancy, wants to press charges against her.

[edit] Abomination (11/11/03)

After the discovery of a dead gay representative for a sexual re-education group, a minister who flooded the victim with hate mail and death threats is the number one suspect. However, the activist's alibi checks out and the detectives focus on a professor who disagress with the victim's notion on the failure of sexual re-education groups.

[edit] Control (11/18/03)

A subway commuter is found mutilated makes the detectives believe that the commuter is the perp in kidnapping women a making them live in his dungeon for years as a bride. On the way to arrest the commuter, Benson discovers him death and is pressured in court because of turning away a four-year victim because she didn't believe his death.

[edit] Shaken (11/25/03)

After a beaten baby is discovered, Stabler and Cragen search for the pedophile attacker. Tests show that it the baby's symptons are than of Shaken Baby Syndrome, Stabler turns to the closest people in the baby's life: the mother, the nannies and a boyfriend of the mother.

[edit] Escape (12/2/03)

A convicted pedophile is desperate to be proclaimed innocent before his mother dies, and escapes from prison to kidnap his accuser by forcing him to recant his story. Benson feels herself entagled in the middle of the situation and begins to believe his story. After being disarmed, Benson goes over his case and discovers a web of corruption and deceit, causing a wrongful conviction and reuniting him with mother and son.

[edit] Brotherhood (1/6/04)

A local fraternity master's murder takes Benson and Stabler to the victim's pornographic web page. It is also revealed that members of the fraternity aren't cooperating, and a code of conduct for prospective pledges.

[edit] Hate (1/13/04)

Jack Hanna's Animal Adventures is a popular syndicated adventure series, hosted by Jack Hanna, with his daughter Kathaleen. It has aired weekly since 1992.

Each week, Jack Hanna takes his viewers on an exciting adventure to discover a different animal species and their habitat, often going to extremes. Among places the series has visited include Panama, Africa, Alaska, and Norway.

Jack Hanna is also working on a second adventure production, Jack Hanna's Into the Wild', also expected to premier in syndication in fall 2007.
