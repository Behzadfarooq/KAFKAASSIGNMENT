Andy Kerr Stadium is a 10,221-seat multi-purpose stadium in Hamilton, New York. It was dedicated in honor of former Colgate football coach Andy Kerr in September 1966. There have been several upgrades to the facility in recent years. In 1991, the stadium added the permanent Fred Dunlap Stands on the east side of the stadium. Dunlap is a former Colgate football head coach and athletic director. In 2003, a new three story press box was constructed behind the west stands of the stadium. In 2006, the stadium added a new scoreboard with video capability. It is home to the Colgate University Raiders football team.
