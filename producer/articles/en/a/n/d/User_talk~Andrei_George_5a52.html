[edit] Uprising of Khotin

Hi Andrei George! It's true that in the Uprising of Khotin case, User:Irpen did act without too much consideration when continually reverting your addition of the tag. However, it is clear from his edit summary that he did act in good faith and genuinely tried to solve the dispute by adding references and the like. In any case, technically he did not breach 3RR because he only reverted each of his versions twice. The first two times, he added references, which you reverted, and then he reverted again. That's two reverts. Later, after you added the tag again, he reverted to a tag-less version, which was a different version to the initial one before this dispute. A more detailed explanation of the technicalities was offered at WP:ANI, where you made the complaint. In any case, it would've been helpful if you had justified your addition of the tag more clearly. Hopefully a resolution can be reached on this matter. Thanks, Ronline ✉ 10:24, 23 April 2006 (UTC)
