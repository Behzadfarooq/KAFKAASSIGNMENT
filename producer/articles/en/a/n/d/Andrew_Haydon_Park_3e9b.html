Bandshell at Andrew Haydon Park

Andrew Haydon Park is a park on the Ottawa River in the city of Ottawa. It is situated on Carling Avenue at Holly Acres Road.

There is a bandshell for outdoor concerts and a picnic area. The Nepean Sailing Club is located nearby at Dick Bell Park.

Migrating Canada Geese and shorebirds stop over along the marshy edges of the river near the park.

The park was named after Andrew Haydon, former reeve of Nepean.
