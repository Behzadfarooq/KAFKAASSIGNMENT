[edit] Interests

My range of interests is pretty wide, but currently I'm focused in Stargate, specially Stargate Atlantis. I've got also an interest in science-fiction in general, ancient history, royal genealogy, drama television shows and DC and Wildstorm comics.

[edit] Projects

  • Ongoing: Updates on Stargate Atlantis character profiles; completing SGC personnel in Stargate SG-1.
  • When times allows: Updating on The Authority, Titans (comics) and related articles. Updating the Barcelona article.
