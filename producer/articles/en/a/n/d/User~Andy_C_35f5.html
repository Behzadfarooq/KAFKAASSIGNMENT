[edit] Basic Information

Name: Andy Connolly
Age: 25
Lives: Wallsend, Tyne & Wear. The end of the Roman Wall!
Occupation: VAT Accountant

[edit] Education

I was schooled entirely within Wallsend, and attended, in order:

  • Redesdale First School
  • High Farm Middle School
  • Burnside Community High School

I was fortunate enough to gain a good set of results, and went on to gain four A-Levels from Tynemouth College, and a BA Degree in Accounting and Finance from Newcastle University. Currently I am debating as to whether it is the right time for me to gain professional qualifications and become a Chartered Accountant through CIMA.

Non-standard qualifications included NCFE Accounting with Computers, RSA CLAIT I & II, and rugby coaching qualifications.

[edit] Working History

In my time in employment I have worked for Currys, B&Q, and Halfords in retail sales positions. After graduating I sold insurance for the AA, before moving to the Inland Revenue (working during its transition into Revenue & Customs, and finally to P&G, where I am currently a VAT Accountant, handling VAT, Intrastat, and EC Sales List declarations. Hopefully, this broad working knowledge will allow me to add to a vast number of different articles here!

[edit] Interests

I am interested in many things, particularly cars. I currently drive a Vauxhall Omega, complete with some Irmscher additions. Previously I have owned a Nissan Almera and a Rover 400.
