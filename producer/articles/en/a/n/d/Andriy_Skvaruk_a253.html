Andriy Skvaruk

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Andriy Skvaruk (born 9 March 1967 in Brodivski) is a Ukrainian hammer thrower.

His personal best throw is 82.62 metres, achieved in April 2002 in Koncha Zaspa.
