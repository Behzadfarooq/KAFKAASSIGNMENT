Andreas Zingerle

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Olympic medal record
Men's biathlon
Bronze 1988 Calgary 4 x 7.5 km relay

Andreas Zingerle (born November 25, 1961) is a former Italian biathlete. At the 1988 Olympics in Calgary, Zingerle won a bronze medal in the relay.
