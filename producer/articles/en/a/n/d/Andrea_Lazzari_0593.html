Andrea Lazzari (born 3 December 1984 in Bergamo) is an Italian football (soccer) midfielder.

In 2006/07 season, he plays for A.C. Cesena of Serie B, on loan from Atalanta B.C., with Adriano Ferreira Pinto went to opposite direction. In January ,2007 he was loaned to Piacenza Calcio.
