Andrei Vadimovich Makarevich (Russian: Андре́й Вади́мович Макаре́вич (born December 11, 1953, Moscow) is a Russian bard, musician, composer, poet, writer, graphics artist and producer. He is a cofounder and a leader of cult rock group Mashina Vremeni.

Makarevich graduated from Moscow Institute of Architecture as a graphics artist.

Since 1969, when he cofounded the rock group Mashina Vremeni, he authored most of its music and lyrics.

Makarevich also released 8 solo albums, participated in regular TV shows and assisted other groups and artists. He authored several poetry collections and two volumes of memoirs.
