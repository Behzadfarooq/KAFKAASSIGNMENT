Hi everyone! I am just here to look around Wikipedia looking for things that need editing or doing some general cleaning up (which I can do myself or with the help of the AutoWikiBrowser and VandalProof software).

I am currently a 20-something year old student from London (which can be proved by my knowledge of the London Underground). As well as London, I have also visited Paris, Rome, Venice, New York City, Orlando and Miami, as well as the Lake District, Peak District and Yorkshire Dales. I came across and eventually joined Wikipedia in January 2004 while I was doing some general research with Google. My first edit was to the Foreign relations of the United Kingdom article (though I think I may have made two or three anonymous edits beforehand). If you are interested, I have written a page listing my milestone edits.

I consider myself to be a WikiGnome. I don't clamour for attention here, but rather prefer to make small edits (such as correcting spelling, grammar etc.) which help to build Wikipedia's reputation as one of the web's premier sources of information on pretty much any subject.

I can be contacted by e-mail and MSN Messenger using Wikipedia's e-mail form. Information about me can be found in my userboxes.

All hail Darth Elephant!
