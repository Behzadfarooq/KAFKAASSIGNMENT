[edit] Andross in StarFox 2

In Star Fox 2. Andross appears as a main villain and a final boss. As both, he has a metal thingy on his head, making him a cyborg. And as the final boss, he is just a cube that doesn't do anything and has Andross's head on all sides. This is probably a beta version stand-in for the final boss.

A lot of the stuff in this article sounds like fan fiction. I have not heard of many of the things described in official material. If it's from an official guide (I said OFFICIAL!) other than the actual games or instruction manuals, please cite it. I cleaned up the Star Fox 64 section a bit too.

[edit] Andross's background

This quote: "After Andross fought with the "Star Fox Team" (apparently in the original Star Fox), he dedicated his remaining days to planet Venom, wanted to leave some sort of legacy. Thus Andross hid his secret device on Titania where no one would ever think to look, leaving behind one of his bioweapons to guard over the device."

The first sentence I don't think is accurate. The original StarFox isn't canon insofar as StarFox 64 replaced its place in the story. I think what Krystal was referring to was James, Peppy, and Pigma. After Andross fought with them, he dedicated his remaining days to Venom (etc..) --Gamehiker 19:14, 11 September 2006 (UTC)
