Andriy Pyatov (born on June 28, 1984 in Ukraine) is a professional Ukrainian football player for FC Vorskla Poltava on loan from FC Shakhtar Donetsk. He was bought by Shakhtar from Vorskla for approximately £640,000 on December 13, 2006. He will spend the rest of the 2006/2007 season on loan at Vorskla Poltava.

Andriy was a part of Ukraine's 2006 World Cup Squad as well as a member of the Ukrainian national under-21 football team.
