Silver 1948 St. Moritz Pairs

Andrea Kékesy was a Hungarian figure skater. She and Ede Király won the pair skating silver medal at the 1948 Winter Olympics. They placed 2nd at the 1948 World Championship, and 1st in 1949. They were also European champions in 1948 and 1949.
