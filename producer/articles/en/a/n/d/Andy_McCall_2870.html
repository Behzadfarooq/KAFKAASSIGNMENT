Andy McCall (b. March 15, 1925 in Hamilton, Scotland) was a Scottish professional footballer. His son, Stuart McCall, was also a footballer.

[edit] Playing career

Team Nation Years Apps Goals
Blackpool 1947-1951
West Bromwich Albion 1951-1952
Leeds United 1952-1955
Halifax Town 1955-1960

[edit] The Bradford City Fire

On 11th May 1985, Andy was hurt in the Bradford City disaster at Valley Parade while watching his son Stuart McCall play.
