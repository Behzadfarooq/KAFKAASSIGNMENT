Andrei Iordan (Андрей Андреевич Иордан) (22 December 1934 in Klarus, Podlesnovsky district, Saratov province, Soviet Union to 20 January 2006 in Bishkek, Kyrgyzstan) served as the State Secretary of Kyrgyzstan and temporarily exercised the duties of Prime Minister from 29 November 1991 to 10 February 1992. He served as Minister of Industry and Foreign Trade and later as an adviser to the Prime Minister.
