[edit] Who I am

I am physics/computer science student at NDSU in Fargo, ND, USA. I speak english and can use GIS Software. I am a participant in the Wikipedia:WikiProject Geographical coordinates, and have categorized and refined the coordinates for approximately 500 towns, mostly in Nigeria, and Italy, if you're wondering why I chose these two locations just open up Google Earth with Stefan Kühn's Wikipedia layer and check the cities with unknown number of inhabitants display, you will be amazed by how many points there are in Europe, Italy especially.
