Athos () is the Home planet of the Athosians in Stargate Atlantis. It was one of the planets culled every few hundred years by the Wraith. The Athosians once lived in a ruined city on the planet and were technologically advanced. They still possess some advanced technology, but due to the threat by the Wraith, they were living in a pre-industrial culture, in portable camps which they moved to confuse the Wraith.

Athos was the first planet visited by the Atlantis expedition, when they were looking for a safe site to evacuate the city. However, the planet was atacked by the Wraith, and the survivors were evacuated to Atlantis. They lived in the city for 3 months, but eventually moved to the planet's mainland.
