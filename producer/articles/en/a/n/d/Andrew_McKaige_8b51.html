Andrew McKaige

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Andrew McKaige is an Australian actor.

In addition to being an original cast member of the 1980s soap opera Sons and Daughters (as Bill Todd), he is the second actor to play the role of Marty Jackson in Prisoner - succeeding Ronald Korosy and preceding Michael Winchester.

More recently, he has appeared in Paradise Beach, Blue Heelers, Neighbours, All Saints and The Secret Life of Us, and is a voice actor for an Australian marine in the video games Halo: Combat Evolved, Halo 2 and Halo 3.
