Andrew Forsman (b. 1985) is the current drummer for the Mukilteo, Washington based prog-rock/post-hardcore band The Fall of Troy.

[edit] Equipment

  • Drums
    • Custom Creation Drums
    • Kit Style: Walnut with Black Hardware
    • Kit Components: Snare, Bass, Floor Tom, Tom-Tom
    • Various Cymbals, usually Zildjian Crash (left side), Zildjian Crash/Ride (right side), Zildjian Hi-Hat Top/Bottom, Zildjian Zil-Bel. Has been seen with Sabian cymbals.

[edit] Notes

  • Andrew positions his Zildjian Zil-Bel upside-down atop his Crash/Ride. It can be heard in about half of The Fall of Troy's songs, most often in the new songs, "A Man, A Plan, A Canal Panama" and "Problem!?"
