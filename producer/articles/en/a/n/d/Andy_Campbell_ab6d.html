Andy Campbell (born April 18, 1979) is a footballer, currently playing for Halifax Town.

Career highlights include scoring against Manchester United in the F.A cup in a 2-0 win for Middlesboro and the winning goal in the 2001 2nd division playoff final for Cardiff Vs QPR. ( Final score 1-0 )
