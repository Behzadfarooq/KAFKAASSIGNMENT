Andy Bishop (born October 19, 1982 in Walsall, England) is an English football player for Bury, playing the position of striker.

Bishop started his career at Blackburn Rovers and has also played for Walsall, Kidderminster Harriers on loan, Rochdale on loan, Yeovil Town on loan, and York City.

Bishop scored 23 goals for York City in the Conference National during the 2005/06 season, which made him the leagues top scorer. He also netted 2 goals in the FA Cup for York during that season.

He signed for Bury on May 8, 2006 on a free transfer from York City.
