[edit] Friedrich Kellner

I hope you can help me, please. I translated the Friedrich Kellner article into Italian using an automatic translator. A few people in Italy helped to correct the first half of the article, but there are still approximately 700 words that need to be double-checked. I have placed the text on my user page at this site: Rskellner. Would you have some time to look at that page and help me improve the Italian? I would greatly appreciate it. Thank you, Scott (Dr. Robert Scott Kellner, College Station, Texas) --Rskellner 23:50, 10 February 2007 (UTC)--Rskellner 23:01, 10 February 2007 (UTC)

Thank you very much for your improvements to the article, Andrea. It was kind of you to act so quickly upon my request. I will add some Wikilinks to the article, and then place it in Italian Wiki.
