Andrea Masiello (born 5 February 1986 in Viareggio, Lucca) is an Italian footballer. As of 2007 plays for Genoa.

He made his Serie A debut at Siena, losing to Inter, 0-1 in home legs, 20 April 2005.

Masiello was on loan to Avellino in 2005, with Boudianski.

Masiello was sold to Siena in 2006 (in co-ownership deal), with Gastaldello, Konko, Bartolucci and Packer on loan.

After scoring no presence in the first half of the 2006 season, Masiello decided to leave Siena for Genoa. Although Genoa plays in the second league, the player is looking forward to start the new year in a team competing for the promotion in serie A in front of very demanding and passionate supporters.

And at 25 January 2007, Juventus sold its 50% rights of Masiello to Genoa, (which previously owned by Siena and Juventus, after became Genoa and Siena) worth for €1.25million, for part of the deal of Domenico Criscito.
