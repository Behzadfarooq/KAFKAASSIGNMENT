Andy Liddell (born June 28, 1973 in Leeds) is a Scottish professional football forward who currently plays for Oldham Athletic . He weighs 10st 9lb and is 5ft 8in tall. Before joining Oldham, he played with Barnsley, Sheffield United, and Wigan Athletic.

Liddell was the longest serving Wigan Athletic player when he left them to join Sheffield United in 2004, he played just under 250 games for Wigan, and was the clubs leading scorer with 70 goals, 4 more than that of David Lowe the previous all time leading scorer.

In his final season for Wigan Athletic he played 38 games and scored 9 goals, and when told he may not get first team football next season, made a move to Sheffield United, where he played 32 games and scored 6 goals, before moving again to his current team Oldham Athletic.
