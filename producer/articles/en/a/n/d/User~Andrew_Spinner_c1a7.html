[edit] Articles I take an interest in editing

[edit] Musical

I'm interested in the blues as a genre. I've assisted with articles related to the great guitarist Freddie King such as The Stumble.

I'm also something of a Dire Straits fan and have edited and created articles relating to them. I started the Alan Clark, Romeo and Juliet (song), Walk of Life (Dire Straits), Telegraph Road (song) and the On The Night articles.

[edit] Other

I've done a fair amount of work on the Super Soaker article. I also occasionally edit articles that I see need changing, on such diverse subject materials as cricket and ballroom dancing.
