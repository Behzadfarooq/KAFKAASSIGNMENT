Anderson-Frank House
(U.S. National Register of Historic Places)
Location: Tampa, Florida  United States
Built/Founded: 1898
Added to NRHP: April 22, 1982

The Anderson-Frank House (also known as the James Buchannan Anderson House) is a historic home in Tampa, Florida, United States. It is located at 341 Plant Avenue. On April 22, 1982, it was added to the U.S. National Register of Historic Places.
