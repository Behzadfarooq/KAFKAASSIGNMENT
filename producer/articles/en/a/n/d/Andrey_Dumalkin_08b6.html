Andrey Dumalkin (born November 3, 1972 in Moscow, Russia) is a Russian rugby league player currently playing for RC Lokomotiv Moscow in the Championship of Russia competition. His position of choice is usually in the forwards at second-row.

Gavrilin has also represented the Russian national side on several occasions most notably at the 2000 World Cup.
