Hi everyone. This little corner of cyberspace is occupied by a college student at Case Western Reserve University in Cleveland, Ohio who is majoring in physics and hence pretends to know a little bit about everything. I joined Wikipedia when I got annoyed that some articles didn't have certain facts in them so I figured I'd add them myself.

In the real world I am an amateur astronomer, webzine writer, Ham radio operator, and I play violin. Someday I have dreams of either being an astrophysicist of sorts or the owner of a widget factory, whichever is more viable.

Alright, that's it for now! I'll catch you on the flipside...


For your great work (and in this case, inspired by your adding actually useful content to George W. Bush), you are awarded this "Minor Barnstar" (even though there's nothing minor about your work). -Nlu
