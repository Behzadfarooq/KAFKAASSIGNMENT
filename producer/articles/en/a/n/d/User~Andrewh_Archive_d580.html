[edit] Responses to major world events

[edit] World Cup 2006

Come on you guys! Do us proud!

My World Cup subpage


[edit] 2006 Commonwealth Games

I'm wishing England luck in the 2006 Commonwealth Games. We've done well so far, so let's keep it up!

We came second!


[edit] 2005 Hertfordshire Oil Storage Terminal fire

All I can say is that it's a good thing nobody was killed in these explosions.


[edit] Bali Bombings 2005

I really can't believe that terrorists have struck again. My sympathies go out to all victims.


[edit] Hurricanes Katrina and Rita

I am taking this opportunity to show my sympathy to the victims of Hurricane Katrina and Hurricane Rita, which have caused enormous damage to New Orleans, Gulfport, parts of Texas and other areas of the southern United States.


[edit] London bombings

On a less positive note, I would just like to express my condolences as a Londoner for those that have been killed or injured in the London bombings, and their families and friends. Let's just be thankful that there were no major casualties in the 21 July 2005 London explosions.


[edit] 2012 Olympics

As a supporter of London's bid for the 2012 Summer Olympics, I just wish to congratulate all of those who were involved in helping London to get the Games. GO GB!
