Andrew Kaufman

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

This article is about the Canadian writer. For the American entertainer, see Andy Kaufman.

Andrew Kaufman is a Canadian writer, film director, and radio producer.

His short film Aberistiwith was screened at festivals across Europe and Canada. Andrew Kaufman is also a revolving cast member of the Perpetual Motion Roadshow, and now works as a producer for CBC Radio in Toronto.

His novella All My Friends Are Superheroes is a humorous love story between a normal man and a superheroine, The Perfectionist, set in the Toronto superhero milieu, in which the bizarre characters (The Seeker, The Inverse, BusinessMan etc.) actually personify different human types; it has been translated into Italian and French.
