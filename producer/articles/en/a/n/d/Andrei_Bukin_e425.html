Andrei Anatolievich Bukin (Russian: Андрей Анатольевич Букин) (born June 10, 1957 in Moscow) was a Soviet figure skater. He competed in ice dance with Natalia Bestemianova. They were trained by the famous coach Tatiana Tarasova. Andrei Bukin was the USSR Olympic Team Flag Bearer at the 1988 Winter Olympics. The duo capped their lengthy career by winning the gold medal there and at that year's World Figure Skating Championships.
