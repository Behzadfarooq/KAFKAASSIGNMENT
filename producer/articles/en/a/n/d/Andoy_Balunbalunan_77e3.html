Andoy Balunbalunan

Andoy Balunbalunan (born 1909) is a pre-war Filipino actor and comedian. He appeared in Vaudeville and other stage plays. He is married to Dely Atay-Atayan, a comedienne. Balunbalunan is the grandfather of Ann Villegas and Armi Villegas.

After World War II, he stopped making movies but his partner continued to make movies until 1980s. He is also a recording artists in the 1950s.
