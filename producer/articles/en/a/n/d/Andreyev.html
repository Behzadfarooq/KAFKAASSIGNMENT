Andreyev

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Andreyev (Russian: Андреев), also spelled as Andreev or Andrejew, in feminine form Andreyeva (Russian: Андреевa), is a common Russian last name and may refer to:

  • André Andrejew (1887-1967), French-Russian production designer, a classic of the film decor building
  • Andrei Andreyev (1895-1971), Soviet Politician
  • Anatole Andrejew (b.1914), French scientist, biochemist of Russian origine
  • Boris Andreyev (1915-1982), Soviet film actor, People's Artist of the USSR
  • Anatole Andrejew, French scientist of Russian origine
  • Catherine Andreyev (b.1955), British historian at Christ Church Oxford, daughter of Nikolay E.Andreyev
  • Daniil Andreyev (1906-1959), Russian writer, author of ´The Rose of The World´ , son of the writer Leonid Andreyev
  • Igor Andreev (b. 1983), Russian tennis player
  • Leonid Andreyev (writer) (1871-1919), Russian writer, one of the most important Russian modern writers
  • Ljuba Andrejewa-Delmas, Russian mezzospran opera singer, * 1884
  • Leonid Andreyev (doctor) (1891-1941), Soviet physiologist and surgeon
  • Mikhail Andreyev (1873-1948), Russian ethnographer and linguist
  • Nikolay Andreyevich Andreyev (sculptor) (1873-1932), Russian sculptor and graphic artist, member of Wanderers (Peredvizhniki)
  • Nikolay Andreyev (critic) (1892-1942), Soviet literary critic and student of folklore
  • Nikolay Andreyev (physicist) (1880-1970), Soviet physicist and academician
  • Nikolay Yefremovich Andreyev (historian) (1908-1982), Russian historian medieavilist, Russian lector at Cambridge, author of respected memoirs
  • Olga Andrejew Carlisle (b. 1931), American writer, granddaughter of the writer Leonid Andrejew, daughter of the poet Vadim Andreyev
  • Pavel Andreyev (1874-1950), Russian bass-baritone opera singer
  • Piotr Andrejew (b. 1947), Polish film director
  • Stepan Andreyev, 18th century Russian polar explorer
  • Vadim Andreyev (1906-1959), Russian poet exiled in Berlin and Paris, author of Childhood, son of Leonid Andreyev
  • Vasily Andreyev (1861-1918), Russian musician and balalaika virtuoso
  • Vasily Andreyev-Burlak (1843-1888), Russian actor
