Andrew Whitworth (born December 12, 1981) is a current American Football offensive tackle for the Cincinnati Bengals. He was drafted in the second round of the 2006 NFL Draft. He played college football at Louisiana State University. He played high school football at West Monroe High School in Louisiana, where he helped them to a state and national championship in 2000.
