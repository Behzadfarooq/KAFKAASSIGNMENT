Andy Dominianni

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia


Andy Dominianni (b. 1972) is a television news anchor of Italian-American descent in Columbus, Ohio. He joined WCMH-TV in Columbus in October, 2004 as co-anchor of the top-rated "NBC4 Today" and "NBC4 Midday" newscasts. Andy is a former tv news anchor at WCCO-TV in Minneapolis, WCCB-TV in Charlotte, WWMT-TV in Kalamazoo, Michigan, WILX-TV in Lansing, Michigan and WBKB-TV Alpena, Michigan. Andy attended Kalamazoo College earning a Bachelor's degree in Psychology.

He is married to Erin with a daughter Riley and son Will. Andy appeared in the 1988 feature film Eight Men Out starring Charlie Sheen.

Andy is related to Kevin M. Miller, founder of Super Bowl Houses For Rent, LLC.
