Andrew Casric

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Andrew Casric is a DJ from Birmingham in the UK. He has appeared in DJ Magazine, IDJ and Mixmag and on the internet.

Casric was born on the 24th April 1979 in Birmingham, UK, has a Business Studies degree and plays the violin. He has a regular slot on Bay FM in South Africa 107.6 FM in the Port Elizabeth area. He was a runner-up in the Teknasia.com DJ Competition 2005.
