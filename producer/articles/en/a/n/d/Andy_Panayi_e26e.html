Andy Panayi

Andy Panayi is a current jazz musician, skilled in performance, composition and arranging. He plays all the flutes and all the saxophones and leads a selection of jazz and classical groups. He also writes commissioned works and self commisioned work for his own arranging, composing & transcribing business ALP Music.

[edit] British Jazz Scene

Andy is a leading member of the current British jazz scene having performed and recorded with a selection of artists including Shirley Bassey, Paul McCartney, Salena Jones & Helen Shapiro.

He's also worked alongside or supported many otjher leading musicians and groups including The Moscow City Ballet, Ronnie Scott & his Side-men, BBC Radio Big Band, John Dankworth & Cleo Laine, Humphrey Lyttelton and others.

Born in Islington in north London and interesetd in music from an early age, Andy studied at the Trinity College of Music before embarking on a profressional mucic career.

He now works with the Associated Board of the Royal Schools of Music (ABRSM) as a consultant specialising in developing the Jazz syllabus for future examinations.

[edit] Awards

Andy has won several awards during his career including; The Marty Paich Arranging Award, The John Dankworth Soloist Award, The Worshipful Company of Musicians Jazz Medal , British Jazz Awards for Jazz Flute.
