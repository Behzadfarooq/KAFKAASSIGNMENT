Andrew Charles Cyrille

Andrew Charles Cyrille is a jazz drummer.

[edit] Biography

Andrew Cyrille was born on November 10, 1939 in Brooklyn, New York. He joined the Cecil Taylor unit in the mid-sixties for about 10 years and eventually went on to do drum duos with Milford Graves. In addition to recording as a bandleader, he has recorded and/or performed with musicians like David Murray, Irène Schweizer, Marilyn Crispell, Carla Bley, Butch Morris and Reggie Workman among others.
