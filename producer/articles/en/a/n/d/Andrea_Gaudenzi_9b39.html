Andrea Gaudenzi (born July 30, 1973) is a former tennis player from Italy, who turned professional in 1990.

Gaudenzi was born in Faenza, Emilia-Romagna. A righthander, he represented his native country at the 1996 Summer Olympics in Atlanta, Georgia, where he was defeated in the third round by America's eventual winner Andre Agassi. Gaudenzi reached his highest singles ATP-ranking on February 27, 1995, when he became the number 18 of the world.
