Andorra in the Eurovision Song Contest 2007

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Anonymous will represent Andorra at Eurovision.

Andorra chose their entry for the Eurovision Song Contest 2007 using an internal selection called 'Projecte Eurovisió'. Radio i Televisió d'Andorra (RTVA) have confirmed that the Andorran representative at the 2007 Eurovision Song Contest will be the young punk-rock band Anonymous with their song "Salvar El Món" (Save The World). Anonymous beat off competition from over eighty other candidates, and were unanimously selected by a 10-member jury.

The song was be premiered on March 1st on a special gala broadcast by Andorra Televisió from the Auditori Claror del Centre Cultural i de Congressos Lauredià in the town of Sant Julia de Loria.

Anonymous will take part in the semi-final of Eurovision on May 10, hoping to qualify to the final. They will perform in the 21st position following Malta's Olivia Lewis with "Vertigo" and preceding Hungary's Magdi Rúzsa with "Unsubstantial Blues".

This year's entry is the first Andorran entry to contain lyrics in English.
