Andriy Rusol (born January 16, 1983 in Kirovohrad, Ukraine) is a Ukrainian football player. He is 182 cm tall and weighs 75 kg.

[edit] Career

Rusol is a defender. He started his career with FC Zirka Kirovohrad in the 1998/99 season. He then transferred to FC Kryvbas Kryvyi Rih for the 1999/2000 season. He played for Kryvbas until 2003, when he was transferred to FC Dnipro Dnipropetrovsk. He played 94 matches for Dnipro and scored 1 goal.

[edit] National team

Rusol debuted for the Ukrainian national football team on March 31, 2004. Since then he played 26 matches and scored 2 goals (as of June 23, 2006).

[edit] 2006 World Cup

Rusol scored Ukraine's first ever World Cup goal in a game against Saudi Arabia on the 4th minute.

[edit] Personal life

At this moment, he is a bachelor. At an interview, he said that he wanted to keep his freedom. He likes to listen jazz and read good books. He is an intellectual player.
