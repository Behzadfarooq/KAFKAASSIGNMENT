Andrew Something (real name: Andrew) is an average soon-to-be high school student who is a big enough loser to start a user page on Wikipedia and to write the entire page in third-person Wikipedia style. Though he is not one to excessively write or edit Wikipedia, he has a strong stance against vandalizing articles and will immediately delete any vandalism on Wikipedia. Unfortunately, he has to do this quite often, as he has a large host of friends who enjoy Wikipedia pranks.

[edit] Life

Andrew Something lives in a state on the East Coast of the United States. He is somewhat paranoid about revealing personal information on the internet, so this page is written very vaguely. He is something of an outcast at his school, though he is doing his best to improve this social status. However, he gets straight A's and is a good student. Though very talented with computer software (and, occasionally, hardware), he is an avid video gamer and enjoys instant messaging, and generally spending time with, his friends.
