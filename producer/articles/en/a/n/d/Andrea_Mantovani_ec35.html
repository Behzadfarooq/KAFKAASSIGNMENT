Andrea Mantovani (born 22 June 1984 in Turin) is an Italian footballer.

[edit] Teams and clubs

  • 2001-2003: Torino
  • 2003-2004: Triestina on loan from Torino
  • 2004-2005: Torino
  • 2005-2006: Chievo co-ownership with Juventus
  • 2006-present: Chievo

[edit] Honours and awards

  • 2003 UEFA U-19 Championship
