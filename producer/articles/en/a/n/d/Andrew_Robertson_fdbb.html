Andrew Robertson is a British actor remembered for his performances on television.

He appeared as Mr. Fibuli in the 1978 Doctor Who serial The Pirate Planet. Other credits include: Adam Adamant Lives!, The Borderers, Oil Strike North, Secret Army, Blake's 7, The Onedin Line, All Creatures Great and Small, One By One, Rumpole of the Bailey, The Bill and Casualty.
