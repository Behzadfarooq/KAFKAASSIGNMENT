André Siegfried

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

André Siegfried (April 21, 1875 – 1959) was a French academic and political writer best known for his commentaries on American, Canadian, and British politics.

He was born in Le Havre, France, to Jules Siegfried, the French minister of commerce.

  • The Race Question in Canada
  • America Comes of Age
