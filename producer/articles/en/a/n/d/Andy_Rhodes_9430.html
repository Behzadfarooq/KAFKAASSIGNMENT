Andrew "Andy" Charles Rhodes (born 23 August 1964 in Doncaster) is an English footballer. He is the current Ipswich Town goalkeeping coach and second reserve goalkeeper.

In his hay-day, Rhodes was a legendary shot stopper for Barnsley and his name is still chanted around the stands.

Rhodes spent three years with Perth side St. Johnstone.
