Andrew D. Bernstein

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

This article is about the photographer; for the philosopher, see Andrew Bernstein.

Andrew D. Bernstein, is the official photographer of the NBA. Bernstein grew up in Brooklyn, New York and is an avid sports fan. He loved photography and his portfolio earned him a scholarship to Art Center College of Design in Pasadena, California. He became the official photographer of the NBA in 1986. His company, Andrew D. Bernstein Associates Photography is the official photographer of the Los Angeles Lakers, Los Angeles Clippers, Los Angeles Kings, and the Los Angeles Dodgers. In addition, Bernstein is the director of photography at STAPLES Center.

He currently resides in San Marino, California with his two children.
