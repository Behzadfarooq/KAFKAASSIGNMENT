Andrew Shinnie (born July 17, 1989 in Aberdeen) is a Scottish professional footballer currently playing for Rangers in the Scottish Premier League.

Shinnie, a striker, made his first team debut for Rangers on March 17, 2007, coming on as a substitute for Dado Pršo in a Scottish Premier League match against Aberdeen.[1] On the same day he scored two goals for Rangers Under 19 team against St. Mirren Under 19s.[2]

Shinnie has also previously been part of the Scotland national Under 18 squad.[3]
