[edit] Overview

Andrew93093 is a user for Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia. His name is, of course, Andrew. He first stumbled upon Wikipedia in the Spring of 2005. An ancestry research project required that Andrew to find out a lot about the year he was born. Desperate for information, he typed in "online encyclopedia" in Google and came upon Wikipedia. Ever since, Andrew has been using Wikipedia to learn more facts about everything.

[edit] Personality

Personality-wise, Andrew is a charmer. He is kind to people he is not familiar, but as a relationship develops, he starts revealing what is within. As described by family members, he is "goofy, smart, and crazy." But by acquaintances, he is described "quiet, smart, nice." Andrew rarely finds feelings for a girl unless they meet certain standards.

[edit] Occupation

Andrew is currently a student at a middle school. He is taking Gym, Medicine, Geometry, Biology, Civics, English, and Renaissance.
