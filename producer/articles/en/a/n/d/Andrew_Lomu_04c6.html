Andrew Lomu

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Andrew Lomu (born February 28, 1979 in Sydney, New South Wales) is an rugby league of Tongan origin player for the Brisbane Broncos in the National Rugby League competition and also for Tonga. His position of choice is prop forward.

He previously played for the Cronulla Sharks but was released early from his contract and he also played for the Sydney Roosters as well as the Canberra Raiders

[edit] Representative games

  • International: Played 5 games in total for Tonga including the 2000 World Cup
