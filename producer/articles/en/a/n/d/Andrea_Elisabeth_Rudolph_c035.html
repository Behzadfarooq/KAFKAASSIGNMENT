Andrea Elisabeth Rudolph

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Andrea Elisabeth Rudolph (born July 1, 1976) is a Danish TV and radio host. She was previously a co-host in the morning show Morgenhyrderne on Radio 100FM along with Lasse Rimmer and Lars Hjortshøj. She resigned in March 2006 and was replaced by Signe Muusman.

She is also host on the TV show Vild med Dans (Dancing With The Stars), but she will likely not be the host for the 3rd season, due to the fact that she announced in the end of 2005 that she is pregnant.

She was voted "Babe of the year 2004" by the readers of "Se og Hør", a Danish tabloid.
