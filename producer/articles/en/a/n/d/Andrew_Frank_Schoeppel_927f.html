Andrew Frank Schoeppel (November 23, 1894 – January 21, 1962) was the Governor of Kansas from 1943 to 1947 and a U.S. Senator from 1949 until his death. He was born in 1894 in Claflin, Kansas, he was a Republican. He died of abdominal cancer at the National Naval Medical Center at Bethesda, Maryland.
