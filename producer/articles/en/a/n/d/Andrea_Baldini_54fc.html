Andrea Baldini

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Andrea Baldini (born 19 December 1985 in Livorno) is an Italian foil fencer.

Baldini won the silver medal at the foil 2006 World Fencing Championships after he lost the final 15-14 versus Peter Joppich.
