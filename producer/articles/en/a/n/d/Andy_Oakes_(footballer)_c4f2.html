Andy Oakes (born on January 11, 1977 in Northwich) is a football player who plays in goal for Swansea City. His former clubs include Macclesfield Town, Winsford United, Hull City, Derby County, Port Vale (loan), Bolton Wanderers (loan) and Walsall.
