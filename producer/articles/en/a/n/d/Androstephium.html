The genus Androstephium (funnellilies) belongs to the order Asparagales and contains the following species.

  • Androstephium breviflorum - pink funnel lily
  • Androstephium caeruleum - Blue Funnel Lily
  • Androstephium coeruleum - Blue Funnel Lily
