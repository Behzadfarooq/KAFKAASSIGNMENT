Andy Sutcliffe (born May 9, 1947 in Mildenhall, Suffolk) is a British former racing driver from England. After a promising start in Formula 3, his single attempt at Formula One was at the 1977 British Grand Prix with the RAM team, in a March 761. He failed to pre-qualify.
