[edit] Administrator open to recall

If at least six editors in good standing (each with at least 500 main space edits, 6 months since their first edit, and blocked for no more than 48 hours total), or at least four administrators, agree that a particular incident necessitates the revocation of my admin priveleges, I will consent to a reconfirmation process of the same type as RfA, and if consensus rejects my reconfirmation, I will resign as an admin.
