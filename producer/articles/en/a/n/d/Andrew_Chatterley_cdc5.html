Andrew (Andy) Chatterley (born 1973) is a record producer. He is a Grammy nominated remixer under the alias Skylark, The Droyds, Edison, and The Buick Project.

[edit] Life and career

Born in Jersey, Channel Islands, Chatterley is married to British singer-songwriter Nerina Pallot. Both currently live in London.

Remixes include, Muse, Blondie, Justin Timberlake, Justice VS Simian, Deep Dish, Level 42, Boy George, Siobhan Fahey, Tracey Thorne, Michael Grey, Kylie, Warren G, Adam & The Ants, [[Cherish, Billie Ray Martin, Nelly Furtado, Shapeshifters, Peter Bjorn & John, Tiefschawartz, Rulers Of The Deep, Underworld, Sander Kleinenberg, Blur, Red Hot Chilli Peppers

Chatterley co-runs the leading live internet music venue www.liveroom.tv, and was involved with the now defunct 23 movement.

He co-owns seminal house music label Saved Records with DJ Nic Fanciulli

He has recently produced tracks for Blondelle, Deaf Stereo, The Pussy Cat Dolls, Theoretical Girl, The Priscillas, The Droyds and The Buick Project. He released a single with Blondie (Blondie vs Edison - Heart Of Glass) in November 2006.

He is also a song writer, co-writing with Siobhan Fahey, Anthony Killhoffer, Toddy, Boy George, Clare Kenny.
