This article has information on the DVD releases of science fiction television series Andromeda (TV series)

[edit] DVD Overview

Andromeda has been released on both Region 1 and Region 2 DVD. Andromeda was initially released on volumes and then shortly followed by a complete season collection in North America. For Region 2, Andromeda was originally released in volumes and complete season boxsets are being released in 2006. A Complete Boxset of all 5 seasons has been released on region 2 DVD and is due for release on Region 1 DVD in Fall 2006, the Region 2 version was called Andromeda The Complete Boxset whereas the Region 1 Version is called Andromeda The Slipstream Collection
