Andrew Christian LaRoche (born August 13, 1983 in Fort Scott, Kansas), is a highly touted infield prospect who plays baseball in the Los Angeles Dodgers system. He is, as of July 15, 2006, playing for the Class AAA Las Vegas 51s.

Son of Dave LaRoche, a former Major League Baseball pitcher for the California Angels, his brother, Adam LaRoche, is a first baseman for the Pittsburgh Pirates.
