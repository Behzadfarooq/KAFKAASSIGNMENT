Collaborative Drawing (Work in progress)
The goal of this project is to make a site where users can work together to create one big picture out of many small ones. This is a remake of a simple site I made once.
Pastebin v4
Once upon a time (almost two and a half years ago) I made a simple pastebin. Then I decided to make a better one (version 2). Then I decided I could make it better still (version 3). And then, one year ago I began work on version 4. I'm still working on it now and then, but it's fully functional. I intended to make a pastebin which was better than even the most popular ones, and I believe that I succeeded in some parts. However, I could never be bothered to take it further and make it known to people, so only a handful of people ever saw it. Maybe one day I'll pick it up again, who knows?
