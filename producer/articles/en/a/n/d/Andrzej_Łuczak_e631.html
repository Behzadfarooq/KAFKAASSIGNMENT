Andrzej Łuczak

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Andrzej Łuczak at work
The exhibition of Łuczak's works in Inowrocław

Andrzej Łuczak (born on July 24, 1955) is a Polish stucco decorator, painter, photographer, art teacher, lover of the Pałuki region and the Tatra Mountains. He was born and still lives in the town of Żnin in central Poland. According to the Żnin County website Łuczak is one of the most meritorious people for the town and the Żnin County in the field of art. Łuczak has been dealing with different forms of art since childhood and with painting since 1977. His favourite themes are portraits and expressive landscapes, in particular maritime motifs, sailing and panoramas of Żnin and Pałuki region. Since 1989 Łuczak has been collaborating with Polish newspapers and magazines as a reporter and a photographer. For years lots of Łuczak's photographs have been used in books and brochures promoting Żnin and Pałuki region, as well as web sites. History, Pałuki regional culture, tourism and crosswords are his interests as well. Łuczak is the author of lots of crossword puzzles published in Polish publications.
