Andy Legg (born 28 July 1966 in Neath) is a Welsh International footballer for Conference North side Hucknall Town., having starting the 2006/07 season at Llanelli. He has previously played for Swansea City, Notts County, Birmingham City, Ipswich Town, Reading, Peterborough United, Cardiff City and Newport County.

In January 2007, he was appointed as the manager of Hucknall Town.
