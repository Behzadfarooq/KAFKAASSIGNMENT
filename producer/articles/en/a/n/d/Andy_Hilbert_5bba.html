Andy Hilbert (born on February 6, 1981 in Lansing, Michigan) is a professional ice hockey forward who currently plays for the New York Islanders of the NHL.

[edit] Playing career

  • Drafted by the Boston Bruins in the 2nd round (37th overall) in the 2000 NHL Entry Draft.
  • Was traded in November of 2005 to the Chicago Blackhawks for a 5th round selection in the 2006 NHL Entry Draft.
  • Was claimed off waivers by the Penguins on March 9, 2006.
  • Was signed by the New York Islanders on a 1 year contract July 4, 2006.

[edit] Awards

  • 2000-01: NCAA West First All-American Team
  • 2000-01: CCHA First All-Star Team
  • 2001-02: AHL All-Rookie Team
  • 2004-05: AHL Second All-Star Team
