Anderson Luiz de Carvalho, called Nené (27-9-81, in Jundiai, São Paulo, Brazil) is a football player who currently plays for Celta de Vigo of the Spanish Primera Division.

He started played professionally in the Paulista Jundiai, at the age of 19. After that, he played for Palmeiras and Santos. He started playing in Spain with RCD Mallorca, and before for Deportivo Alaves.
