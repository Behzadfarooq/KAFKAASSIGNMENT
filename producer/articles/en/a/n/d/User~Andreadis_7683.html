Personal life

Stavros Andreadis (born September 14, 1990 in georgia)is a greek basketball player and is one of the premiere centers in the National Basketball Association. He is the only son of his father 6-foot-10 (2.08 m) Pavlos Andreadis and his mother 6-foot-2 (1.88 m) Xenia Abdreadou.Both Stavros and Pavlos were greek national team basketball players. At 7'6" (2.29 m)he plays center for the AEK academy in the greek basketball league.Stavros Andreadis is genetically tall. He does not have pituitary gigantism or acromegaly, a condition afflicting his fellow countryman and basketball hopeful Kremmydas Dionysis At 7'6" he is amongst the top 25 tallest basketball players in the world and is amongst the world's tallest men.
