Andrew Moss (born 1984) is an English television actor. He is currently best know for playing Rhys Ashworth in the Channel 4 teenage soap opera Hollyoaks since 2005.

[edit] TV Credits

Hollyoaks is Moss's first regular television role, though he had previously played Craig Gutteridge in the BBC drama series Cutting It, in addition to minor roles in Merseybeat, Doctors and two episodes of Coronation Street.[1] [2]

Moss trained at the North Cheshire Theatre College, where he gained a Diploma in Drama. He also attended Sheena Simon College, where he obtained a National Diploma in Musical Theatre.[3]
