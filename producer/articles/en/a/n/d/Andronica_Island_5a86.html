Andronica Island

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Andronica Island is one of the Shumagin Islands in the Gulf of Alaska south of the Alaska Peninsula in Aleutians East Borough of Alaska, USA. It lies east of Popof Island, southeast of Korovin Island, and northwest of Nagai Island. The island has a land area of 14.661 kmÂ² (5.6607 sq mi) and is uninhabited.
