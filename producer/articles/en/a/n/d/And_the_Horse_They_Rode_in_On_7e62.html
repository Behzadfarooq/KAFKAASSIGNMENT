And the Horse They Rode in On is an album by Minneapolis rockers Soul Asylum. It was produced by Steve Jordan with Joe Blaney. The song "We 3" was included on the soundtrack of the film Chasing Amy.

The vinyl, cassette, and CD versions of the album have different, but related, cover art.

[edit] Miscellaneous

A promotional picture disc CD of And the Horse They Rode In On included three unreleased bonus tracks; "One Way Conversation", "Little House on the Edge", and "Village Idiot". This CD was given to some radio stations to promote the album and came in a plastic sleeve with a sticker of the track listing on it.
