[edit] Basic information

Well, I'm Andi. I'm 19 and currently live in Cardiff, Wales where I attend UWIC. I was born in Newtownards which is a town in County Down in Northern Ireland.

I'm in first year at UWIC studying politics. I've been interested in politics for quite a long time and hope to have a career involving political journalism. I left Northern Ireland to study politics because I disliked the idea of learning about a subject in a country, which in my opinion politics is essentially non-existant due to relations between the Protestant and Catholic communities.

I've an atheist and have been since I can remember. The idea of a higher being existing has always seemed like wishful thinking to me. This is however, just my opinion and I feel that everyone is perfetcly entitled to their own opinion and should be allowed to believe what they wish.

I've only been living in Cardiff since September, 2006 and I love it here already. I was quite unhappy when I lived in Northern Ireland but I still go back there quite regularly because my friends and family all live there.

[edit] Music

I play the guitar, bass and am attempting to learn how to play the keyboard.

I have a rather varied taste in music but my favourite genre of music is gothic rock. My favourite bands are The Cure, London After Midnight and The Birthday Massacre. I'm also a large fan of the bands The Smashing Pumpkins, Rob Zombie/White Zombie, Scissor Sisters and D'espairs Ray.

[edit] Reading

When I was younger, I used to hate reading but now I really enjoy it. I am currently reading The Last Day of Socrates by Plato. My favourite book is Memoirs of a Geisha by Arthur Golden but I also really enjoyed the book Junk by Melvin Burgess
