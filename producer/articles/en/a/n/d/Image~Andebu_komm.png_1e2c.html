Description
English: Coat of arms for the municipality of Andebu (kommune), Norway - The arms were granted on December 12, 1986. The arms show three triangles, each for one of the three villages in the municipality Andebu, Høyfjord and Kodal. The three triangles also form a the letter A, the initial of the municipality.
