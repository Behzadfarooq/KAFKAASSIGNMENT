Andrew J. Ferrell Middle Magnet School was dedicated in 2001 on North 24th Street in Tampa, Florida. The school is located on the site of the original Middleton High School, Hillsborough County's first black high school, built between 1933 and 1935.

The school is named in honor of Andrew J. Ferrell, Middleton High School's last principal.

The school colors are maroon and gold.

The school mascot is the Tiger.
