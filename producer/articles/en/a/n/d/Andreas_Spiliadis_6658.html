G. Andreas Spiliadis (May 8, 1967), often called Spilly, is the school support specialist for the Baltimore Urban Debate League. He was one of the founding coaches of the league, and helps run, judge, and facilitate its tournaments.He was born Baltimore and graduated Baltimore City College. He got his bachelors degree from Haverford College.

[edit] Trivia

  • Spilly plays music, paints, and blogs on a website [1].
  • He is also co-owner of his family’s Greek restaurant, the Black Olive, in Fells Point and is active in his local community of Charles Village.
