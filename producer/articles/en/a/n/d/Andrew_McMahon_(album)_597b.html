Andrew McMahon (album)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Andrew McMahon
EP by Andrew McMahon
Released 1999
Genre Rock, piano rock, pop punk
Length 15:10
Producer(s) E.J. Stanton

Andrew McMahon is an eponymous demo EP, recorded and self-released by Andrew McMahon in 1999. It is unclear as to how many copies of the CD were printed and where to obtain these supposedly few, as well as where and when exactly the songs were recorded, since the liner notes are fairly unenlightening. Three of the CD's songs were later re-recorded by McMahon's band Something Corporate for Ready... Break (2000) and The Galaxy Sessions (2001).

[edit] Track listing

(all songs written by Andrew McMahon)

  1. "Airports" – 5:30
  2. "Babies of the 80's" – 2:58
  3. "Spin" – 3:03
  4. "Drunk Girl" – 3:39
