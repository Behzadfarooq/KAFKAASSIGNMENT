André "Andy" Egli (born 8 May 1958 in Bäretswil) is a retired Swiss football defender and current coach.

He was capped 77 times and scored 8 goals for the Swiss national team between 1979 and 1994. He was an unused substitute at the 1994 FIFA World Cup.
