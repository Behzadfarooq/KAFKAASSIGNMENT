André Luciano da Silva , or simply Pinga (born on April 27, 1981 in Aracati, Brazil), is a Brazilian attacking-midfielder. He is currently playing for Sport Club Internacional of Porto Alegre. He has also played for Italy's Treviso, Torino and Siena.

Pinga was signed by Inter Porto Alegre in four year contract to fill in the gap left by midfielder Tinga after the Libertadores Cup title won in August 2006.
