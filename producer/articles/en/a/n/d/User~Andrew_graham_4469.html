Andrew Graham is a Wikipedia user and contributor from Maze, near Lisburn in Northern Ireland. He was born in 1986 in Belfast and no longer attends Wallace High School where he was studying for is A levels. He is now trying to re-learn German.

[edit] 'Andrew Graham'

My contributions are wide and varied, reflecting my interests. If I see an article that can be brought to a higher standard, I will do my best to do this. I take Wikipedia perhaps a little too seriously, as I am crusading for the removal of all the crappy bulls*** pages about nothing. Recently, I have found myself too busy/ignorant to make many significant contributions other than adding tags to articles.
