Andrew Molera State Park

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Andrew Molera State Park is a state park located in Monterey County, California, United States.

Located in the Big Sur area, this park is still relatively undeveloped and offers visitors great hiking, fishing and beachcombing. Miles of trails wind through meadows, beaches and hilltops. A primitive walk-in trail camp, popular with hikers and bikers, is located approximately one third of a mile from the parking area.

[edit] Andrew Molera SP Trail Camp

There are 24 identified sites. Camping is allowed only at these sites with a maximum of four people per site. Registration is on a first-come, first-served basis.

The establishment of 24 sites with a limit of four people per site allows for maximum availability of individual campsites, yet continues to meet the required carrying capacity.

[edit] Location

The park is 20 miles south of Carmel on California State Route 1.

[edit] Climate

The weather can be changeable. Layered clothing is advised.
