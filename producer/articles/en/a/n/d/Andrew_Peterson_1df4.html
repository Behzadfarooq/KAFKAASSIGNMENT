Andrew Peterson is a musician who plays Christian folk music. As of 2006 he has released seven albums, Walk, Carried Along, Clear to Venus, Love and Thunder, Behold the Lamb of God, The Far Country and Appendix A: Bootlegs & B-sides. Peterson was signed to Watershed/Essential Records from 1999 to early 2004. "Carried Along" featured the top ten radio single, “Nothing to Say.” Peterson is also a founding member of the Square Peg Alliance.

Peterson was heavily influenced by Rich Mullins.
