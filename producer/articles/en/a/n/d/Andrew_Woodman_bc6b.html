Andrew (Andy) Woodman is a worship leader based in Milton, north of Cambridge, England.

Andy has been leading Christian bands and worship in Christian settings since the age of 15. After a period spent at the Soul Survivor Church in Watford in 2002, alongside other worship leaders such as Matt Redman, Tim Hughes and Martyn Layzell, he returned to his home town. He is currently involved in a variety of projects including All Saints at 7 (AS@7), a contemporary service based in the Parish Church in Milton ([1]) and youth and pastoral work. Andy is also involved in leading a city-wide band formed for the CRAVE project, an initiative to reach those aged 18-25 who are no longer in education. He also often visits other churches and bands to teach them about worship, presenting seminars on topics such as Band Dynamics.

Andy has led worship all around the country, as well as abroad, at high-profile events such as the Living Water festival, held every August in Norwich, which regularly attracts 5000 people. He has been with his girlfriend, Jo, for two and a half years.

Andy's debut album, Empty Handed, was released in 2004, containing 9 tracks.
