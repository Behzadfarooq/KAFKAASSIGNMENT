Andrew Marth is a retired Australian football (soccer) player who represented Australia 16 times.

Marth spent all of his club career in Australia and mainly at the Melbourne Knights, where he was involved in their two National Soccer League titles, as well as becoming captain of the club. Marth was the Joe Marston medallist in the 1995/96 NSL Grand Final.
