Andres Oper (born November 7, 1977 in Tallinn) is an Estonian forward, who currently plays for Roda JC of Holland. He has also played for Torpedo Moscow, Aalborg BK and FC Flora Tallinn. Oper is Estonia's national football team's top scorer with 32 goals in 102 matches.
