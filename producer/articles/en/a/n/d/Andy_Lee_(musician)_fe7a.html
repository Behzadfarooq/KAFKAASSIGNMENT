Andy Lee (musician)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Shinhwa 8th

Andy Lee (Korean: 이선호, Hanja: 李先皓, born December 21, 1981) is a rapper in the Korean band Shinhwa.

[edit] Profile

  • Siblings: One elder sister
  • Religion: Christian

[edit] Music Career

Andy is the second member to join Shinhwa. He was originally planning to be a member of H.O.T. (another Korean boy band) with his friend, Tony An. However, his parents did not let him join H.O.T. because they thought he was too young. When he was older, they let him join Shinhwa. During the fourth album, Andy took some time out from Shinhwa to study in US, but he returned for their fifth album, 'Perfect Man'. During the year when Shinhwa members were doing their solo activities, Andy surprised everyone by being an actor on various dramas. Recently, beside being a Shinhwa member, he is the MC on SBS Inkigayo and MBC Goldfish.

In January 2007, Andy decided to step down from being MC on both SBS Inkigayo and MBC Goldfish in a period of 3 days (SBS Inkigayo on January 28 & MBC Goldfish on January 31).

[edit] Acting Career

Surprising everyone when he began acting, Andy starred in the popular drama Lovers In Prague with a minor role. He began appearing in many banjun drama alongside Lee Jin. The couple created some popularity among the fans.

[edit] Trivia

  • Andy and his co-star/partner, Lee Jin (from Fin.K.L), acted together and as a couple for 6 months, longer than any other couple or actors/actresses did on the show segment. They gained a lot of popularity due to their perfect match and chemistry.
  • Although Andy had a very minor role in the hit drama Lovers in Prague, it gained many viewers because of Andy and his role as a complete different, serious person.
  • Andy's trademark "heart-dance" became very famous as it was performed very often in variety shows like X-Man, Love Letter and Heroine 6.

[edit] MC

  • SBS Inkigayo (June 2005-2007): Andy took over as MC of SBS Inkigayo when group member, Kim Dong Wan, stepped down from the job after 2+ long years of hosting the show.
  • MBC Goldfish (November 2006-2007): Andy has taken the opportunity to MC on MBC Goldfish Theatre beside popular comedians, like Kang Ho Dong, Shin Jung Hwan, etc. He is the youngest MC of the large group.
