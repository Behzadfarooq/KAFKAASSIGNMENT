Andy Chambers

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Andy Chambers was an author and game designer for Games Workshop. He worked extensively on various Warhammer 40,000 rulebooks and sourcebooks, and also authored at least one fiction novel set in the same universe. [1]

In 2003, he joined Mongoose Publishing as the lead designer of the company's development team for the Starship Troopers tabletop miniatures game.[2]
