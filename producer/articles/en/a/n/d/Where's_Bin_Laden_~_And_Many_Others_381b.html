Where's Bin Laden?: And Many Others

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Where's Bin Laden? is a pastiche of the Where's Wally? series of books. It was created by Daniel Lilic and was published by New Holland Publishers on September 30, 2006. The ISBN is 1741103320.

Where's Bin Laden?, like Where's Wally? (Where's Waldo? in the USA), takes the reader through a series of full page illustrations where the main character, in this case Osama Bin Laden, is hidden among a vast crowd of people. Other characters include an CIA agent, Bin Laden's posse and bodyguards, and weapons of mass destruction. Each page depicts a different world city, including Paris, New York, and Amsterdam.
