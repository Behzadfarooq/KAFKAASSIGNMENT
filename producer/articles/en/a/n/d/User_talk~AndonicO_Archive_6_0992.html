[edit] Appreciation Week

It had been one of Esperanza's goals to recognize those who had made major contributions, particularly considering that several of the individuals who may have made such contributions might have made them in such a way, perhaps by contributions to a variety of separate things, that they are not recognized. I'm afraid I don't know specifically what you meant by "mass awards", but possibly, I would personally have no real objections to having the individual projects give out awards to whomever they see to have earned them, even if that were to be only a comparatively small award. Exactly what sort of and how many awards might be given out would I think clearly be at the discretion of the individual groups, and really not my place to say. But, I guess, one of my primary reasons for proposing this is that a lot of editors have over the years noticed that the efforts of several valuable contributors have gone unnoticed, and that, in some cases, this might have lead to the reduced activity in wikipedia and possibly in some cases leaving of wikipedia by those parties. Again, I really don't know of any specific details I could offer, because I don't think that it would be my place at this point to make any such detailed proposals. Whether the awards would be given out to major accomplishments, length of service, or at all would be really up to the discretion of the individual groups. Also, one thing that might be true in some cases is that some of these groups might not exactly look to act in this way very often, and maybe receiving a prompt like this would perhaps make several of their members ultimately feel more valuable and appreciated, which wouldn't be a bad thing. I hope that this rather incoherent response satisfies you at least a little. But, again, I'm really at this point only proposing the idea, it wouldn't be my place to say exactly how it should be enacted, if at all. Badbilltucker 17:21, 29 December 2006 (UTC)

[edit] Hello from a main page regular

Recent vandal attacks on the main page where an 'interesting' picture has been snuck in via unprotected subparts of the main page have been annoying at the least - Yes it's true. It has been one or two lapses of concentration by admins who forgot to protect something that they placed on the front page templates. Admins are human, they do make mistakes. Everyone has been poked and reminded. I'm not saying that it won't happen again, but everything that should be done has.

However.

What should happen when such a vandal attacks? Within minutes someone with admin ability spots it, quietly reverts it and covers the cracks that the vandal oozed in via. The vandal is blocked, defeated and deflated, they move on.

What does happen when such a vandal attacks? Everyone, their dog, cat, hamster and rabbits swamp this talk page with "OMG!11!! I'M OFFENDED!! ARGH! ARGH! MY EYES WTF!" style messages of outrage and indignation. The vandal says "HAR HAR PWND! I EMS TEH 1337", posts several taunting messages about how good they are and revels in the afterglow of their nuclear attack.

Eventually, within minutes someone with admin ability spots it, reverts it and covers the cracks that the vandal oozed in via. The vandal is blocked and moves on with a self-satified smug smile and sticky underpants.

People. If you get all up in arms about it, you're feeding the vandal's ego. Just let it pass. It will be reverted within minutes. --Monotonehell 10:38, 5 January 2007 (UTC)
