Hi Andie, can you license these images (below)? If you're usure about what license you want, I tend to license mine under the GFDL and CC-BY-SA licenses, the default. These mean anybody may use re-use them, but when they do they must acknowledge the photographer and make it clear to others that they may also re-use them under those terms. You could also make them public domain, but this allows people to use them without acknowledging you and without telling others that they're free to use them. I also recommend using Wikimedia Commons [1]. Images uploaded there will automatically also be on Wikipedia, but at commons we can create galleries and categorise photos (I found your images when I was updating the English counties galleries).
