André Hediger

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

André Hediger (born April 28, 1941) is a Swiss politician, member of the Swiss Labour Party. He is a member of the government of the City of Geneva, Switzerland (conseil administratif), responsible for Security and Sports. He currently holds the rotating function of mayor (maire). His previous turns as mayor were in 1990, 1994, 1998 and 2002.
