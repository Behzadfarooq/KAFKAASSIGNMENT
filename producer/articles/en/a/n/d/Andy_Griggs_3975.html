Andy Griggs

Andy Griggs is a Louisiana-born country musician. Born 8/13/73 in the city of Monroe, Andy began his adult life as a minister, playing guitar on the side. By 1997, he had written several songs and was playing in the Nashville area and released You Won't Ever Be Lonely in 1999. The album was a chart success, with three Top 10 singles. His second album was 2002's Freedom, led by the best-selling "Tonight I Wanna Be Your Man". His third album was This I Gotta See from 2004 with the hits "She Thinks She Needs Me" and "If Heaven."

After a few albums, Griggs parted ways with RCA. He has since signed a new record deal with a new label in Nashville, Montage Music.
