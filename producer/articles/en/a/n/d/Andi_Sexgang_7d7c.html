Andi Sexgang

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

You have new messages (last change).

Andi Sexgang is vocalist of the band Sex Gang Children who were a prominent act in the Gothic Rock movement of the 1980s, his band frequently played at the infamous Batcave club. He has also recorded as an experimental solo artist.
