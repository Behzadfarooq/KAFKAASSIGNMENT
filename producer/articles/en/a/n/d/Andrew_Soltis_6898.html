Andrew Soltis (born May 28, 1947) is a Polish-American chess author and columnist as well as a Grandmaster. His humorous column Chess to Enjoy is one of the longest running in Chess Life, the publication of the United States Chess Federation. He has authored or co-authored around thirty books. He is a reporter for the New York Post.
