Guly, real name Andrés Guglielminpietro (born 10 April 1974 in San Nicolas) is an Argentine football player.

[edit] International career

He has been capped for Argentina national football team, but not played in 1998 and 2002 FIFA World Cup.

[edit] Honors and awards

  • Italian Champions: 1999
  • Copa Sudamericana: 2004
