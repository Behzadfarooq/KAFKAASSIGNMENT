Andrzej Buncol

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Andrzej Buncol (born September 21, 1959 in Gliwice) is a retired Polish football player.

He played for clubs such as Ruch Chorzów, Legia Warszawa, FC Homburg (West Germany), Bayer Leverkusen (Germany) and Fortuna Düsseldorf (Germany).

He played for the Polish national team.

He was a participant at the 1982 FIFA World Cup (where Poland won third place) and the 1986 FIFA World Cup. After the 1986 World Cup he emigrated to West Germany.
