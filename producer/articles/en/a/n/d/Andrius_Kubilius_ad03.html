Andrius Kubilius (b. December 8, 1956, Vilnius) is a Lithuanian politician and former Prime Minister of Lithuania, currently leader of a Lithuanian party, Homeland Union.

[edit] Family

His wife is a violinist in the Lithuanian National Symphonic Orchestra; the couple have two sons.

He has been praised as an "unsung hero" in The Economist. [1]
