Wikipedia:Image_use_policy#User-created_images this is the particular policy that addresses the addition of Author details. Gnangarra 13:53, 22 February 2007 (UTC)
With images you take its best to upload them into Wikimedia Commons the advantage is that other language wikipedias can use them as well then, just create an account there with the same as you use here. The process for uploading is the same, all the MT Barker images would go into a category call "Mount Barker, Western Australia" then instead of a gallery in this article you link to the commons category and voila a complete gallery. see the bottom of Banksia epica for how this works.
