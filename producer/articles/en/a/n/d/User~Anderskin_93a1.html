[edit] Why I do what I do

After using the site for more than a year and making occasional contributions for half that time, Anderskin became a registered user in December 2004. Although I'm a non-joiner by nature (even when I believe in the cause), I received some encouragement to do so from Wikipedians and a computer geek friend.

My specialities and interests are somewhat diverse. They include:

  • the Hamilton area, a highly underrated part of Ontario and Canada
  • Germany, the good, the bad and the ugly
  • la Francophonie, especially French-speaking minorities
  • military history, labour history and women's history, especially in Canada and the rest of the Commonwealth
  • novelty and comedy music
  • vexillology and other flag-related topics
  • playing cards, board games and other semi-educational pastimes
  • cycling, camping and hiking (sometimes combined in one trip).

If these interest you, please feel free to comments on this page.
