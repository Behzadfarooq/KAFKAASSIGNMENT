[edit] Disruptive edits on Jesus

Linking to your own website is a violation of WP:COI - conflict of interest. Please do not make disruptive edits or insert inappropriate external links (see WP:EL for guidelines.) Edits that violate these guidelines will likely be reverted. You are invited to contribute constructively in any way you can. Nposs 06:37, 16 January 2007 (UTC)

Upon further review, it appears that your edits were not so much disruptive as in violation of WP:COI. I have removed the references to your books that you have inserted. If you believe that they are worth including in the article, the proper course of action is to propose their inclusion on the respective talk pages of the articles in question. Let the other editors discuss the matter and decide if the links and references are worth including.
