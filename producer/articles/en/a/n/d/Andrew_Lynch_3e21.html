Born in Soldotna, Alaska, Andrew Lynch is a singer-songwriter and keyboard player who lives in Los Angeles, California. In 2004, he released his debut album Samaritopia with the help of Pat Boone and Bill Hudson. The album features Sebastian Steinberg and The Section Quartet, who he frequently performs with at Largo (nightclub). He is currently a member of The Black Pine.
