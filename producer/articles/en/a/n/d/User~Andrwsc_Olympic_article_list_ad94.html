There are 230 nations for which we need to write a combination of "Nation at the Olympics" and possibly "Nation at the Summer Olympics" and maybe "Nation at the Winter Olympics" articles.

I want to get these articles created at "start level" instead of as just stubs, so here is what I think they need to contain:

  • standard infobox
  • brief intro, with mention of first appearance and any subsequent boycotts etc.
  • mention of the NOC
  • mention of hosted Games, if any
  • mention of predecessor/successor nations, if any (e.g. Germany, USSR, Yugoslavia, etc.)
  • number of competitors and sports participated per Games (this data is not currently available so will be added later)
  • table of medals won per-Games and per-sport
  • list of medalists

Not surprisingly, a "one size fits all" approach is inappropriate (compare the Olympic history of the United States to Kiribati, for example...). For some nations (USA, URS, etc.), a list of all medalists is impractical, so I would suggest a list of notable medalists instead (for example, multiple winners). For nations that have never won any medals, I think it is best to exclude the tables, as they would just be a large list of zeroes and not really contribute anything.

As for which articles are created, we have three choices:

  1. Some nations have rich histories for both Summer and Winter Games, so I will create both articles, with the "...at the Olympics" article serving as a disambiguation page.
  2. Some nations have long histories at the Summer Games, but not so much for the Winter Games (e.g. a handful of appearances with a very small number of competitors each time), so I will combine everything into a single "...at the Olympics" article. Redirects will be created from the Summer and Winter links.
  3. Some nations have never been to the Winter Games, so they will only have a single "...at the Olympics" article and a single redirect from the "... at the Summer Olympics" link.

Here are the list of nations by each of these categories.
