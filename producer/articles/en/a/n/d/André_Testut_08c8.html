Andre Testut (April 13, 1926 - September 24, 2005, Lyon) was a racing driver from Lyon, France. He entered 2 Formula One Grands Prix, both in Monaco, in 1958 and 1959. On both occasions he drove his Maserati 250F and both times he failed to qualify.
