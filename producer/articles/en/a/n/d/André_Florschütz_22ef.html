André Florschütz

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Olympic medal record
Men's Luge
Silver 2006 Turin Doubles

André Florschütz (born August 6, 1976 in Sonneberg) is a German luger. Together with Torsten Wustlich he won the silver medal in the Men's Doubles event at the 2006 Winter Olympics in Turin, Italy.
