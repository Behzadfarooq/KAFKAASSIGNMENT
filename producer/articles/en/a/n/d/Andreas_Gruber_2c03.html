Andreas Gruber

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Andreas Gruber (born 1954-11-02) is an Austrian screenwriter and director.

From 1974 to 1982 he studied screenwriting and directing at the Hochschule für Musik und darstellende Kunst in Vienna.

In 1979 he was directing assistant to Axel Corti.

In 2000 he won the Golden Romy for best directing.

[edit] Filmography

  • 1983 Drinnen & draußen. TV-Film. Screenplay and directing.
  • 1989 Schalom, General. TV-Film. Screenplay and directing.
  • 1991 Erste Wahrnehmung. TV-Film. Screenplay and directing.
  • 1994 Hasenjagd – Vor lauter Feigheit gibt es kein Erbarmen. Screenplay and directing.
  • 2004 Welcome Home. Film. Screenplay and directing.
