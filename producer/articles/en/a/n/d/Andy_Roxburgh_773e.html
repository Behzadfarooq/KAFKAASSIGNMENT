Andy Roxburgh (born 1 August 1943) is a former Scottish football player and coach. He has been UEFA Technical Director since 1994.

In his playing days Roxburgh appeared for Queens Park, Partick Thistle and Falkirk. His only managerial role was with the Scottish national team, whom he led between 1986 to 1993. guiding them to the 1990 World Cup and the 1992 European Football Championship.

In between his playing and managerial career, Roxburgh was employed as a school teacher.
