Andenne

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

the coat of arms of the municipality of Andenne

Andenne is a Belgian municipality located in the Walloon province of Namur. On January 1, 2006 Andenne had a total population of 24,407. The total area is 86.17 km² which gives a population density of 283 inhabitants per km².
