Andrew Dickman born 1975 in Chester is Head of Sport at Channel M

Andy joined MUTV in 1999 from Wolves TV, a weekly channel dedicated to Wolverhampton Wanderers F.C.

Prior to this, Andy spent time on work experience on This Morning with Richard and Judy.

Andrew was the first person to interview David Beckham after he was named England captain in 2000

He was often the Touchline Reporter at M.U.T.V.'s coverage of the F.A. Premier Reserve League.

However, on 25 February 2007, Andrew worked his last shift for MUTV as a producer before joining Channel M as Head of Sport the following week.

[edit] Trivia about Andy and Hobbies

Andy currently resides in Saughall Massey, Wirral.

Andy is a life-long supporter of Chester City F.C.

Andy's father was a Football League Linesman in between 1978 and 1981 officiating at grounds such as Anfield and Old Trafford

Andy has a collection of over 3,000 Football Programmes varying from League of Wales to the 1966 and 2006 World Cup Finals
