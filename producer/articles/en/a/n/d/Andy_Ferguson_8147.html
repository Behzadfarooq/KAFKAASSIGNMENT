Andrew David Ferguson (born 24 March 1985 in Glasgow), is a Scottish football striker currently playing for Clyde. Ferguson began his career with Ayr United, where he scored 11 times in 55 league games

In the summer of 2005, Ferguson got a big move to Dundee, but failed to make a great impact, on making two starts, whilst getting two goals all season.

Ferguson signed for Clyde in the summer 2006, and made his debut in the Scottish Football League First Division against Partick Thistle.
