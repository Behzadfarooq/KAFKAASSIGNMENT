WILD DISCOVERY S.A.L. TRAVEL & TOURISM[1]

Founded in 1997 and with a head office in Beirut, Wild Discovery is today one of the leading travel & tourism providers in Lebanon, Syria & Dubai. Its regional network of travel agencies is entirely IATA accredited and offers some of the widest choices of services and products in the market, ranging from a simple ticket issuance to the organization of the most comprehensive Holiday experience.

A spirit of Innovation, Teamwork, Client Focus, Integrity and Efficiency is commonly shared by a team of Travel professionals which Knowledge of the business and expertise have made of the Wild Discovery brand what it means today at the regional level: Professionalism, Choice, Expertise and Service.

Wild Discovery is today one of the fastest growing travel providers in the region, has great expansion plans in challenging new markets and offers some of the most exciting products over the five continents: from Chile to Japan, Australia to Russia, Canada to South Africa and passing by Botswana, Kenya, Oman, Spain, Morocco, no destination is far enough for Wild Discovery to find the details that make the difference.

Exploring Okavango Delta in Botswana, taking a Romantic escape in Rome, going on a Gorilla Trekking in Uganda, Cruising onboard the largest Mega Ships in the world, participating in an Expedition to Antarctica or getting sun-tanned in the Maldives….No theme is too simple or too challenging, we Know how to plan it ALL !
