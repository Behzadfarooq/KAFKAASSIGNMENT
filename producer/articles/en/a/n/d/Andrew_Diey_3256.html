Andrew William Langmanis Diey (b. October 31, 1973 in Islington, London) is a British electronic musician, sound designer and record producer. As a solo artist, he is probably best known as Black Faction, or his previous moniker Foreign Terrain.

Diey [pronounced Day] first came to prominence as a musician on the Skam Records Manchester, offshoot label MASK 300, under his Foreign Terrain moniker, and later afterwards he moved onto using the more known Black Faction name for various labels. His career as a sound designer is also note worthy, his works have appeared on many high profile Computer Games, UK TV broadcasts within television and also on BBC Radio.

[edit] History

Diey first became involved in electro acoustic music, at the age of 18. He attended the Electronic Music Studio in Stockholm, Sweden in 1993. He moved back from Sweden in 1995, to attend a HND in Music Technology at City College in Manchester, England. In 1997 he attended Salford University, on the Electro Acoustics BEng undergraduate course. In 1998 he attended IRCAM in Paris, as a sound designer, and working with IRCAM software.

[edit] Professional

His works have been released on CDs for critical acclaim, he received a 5 album contract from his first demo tapes with Soleilmoon Portland, Oregon, he has created audio content for over 20 Game Development titles, produced 100s of hours of broadcast music and sound including BAFTA nominated material. His most recent works have been for National Geographic HD Channel – Inside the Ultimate, producing the music and sound design using surround sound techniques.


BBC Sound Designer In 2006 Diey won the position of New Talent BBC Sound Designer competition, which was open to UK residents. Working in-house as a BBC sound designer on radio drama.

http://www.bbc.co.uk/newtalent/drama/sound_designer/

2007 Diey has been working with D&AD, and also developing a series of produtions for various interactive companies, brand agencies within sound branding, and sonification.

[edit] Research

Diey is currently involved in research in the interdisciplinary field of sonification and sound in products, interface sound design for products, and user interface sound design.
