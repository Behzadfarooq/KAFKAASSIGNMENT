André Venceslau Valentim Macanga, better known as André (born 14 May 1978 in Luanda, Angola), is an Angolan football midfielder who currently, as of May 2006, is a part time footballer, his other job is a postman.

He is a member of the National team, and was called up to the 2006 World Cup.

André is known in the Angola national football team, as the "defensive lung" of the team.
