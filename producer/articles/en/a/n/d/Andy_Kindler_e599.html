Andy Kindler

Andy Kindler is an American stand-up comedian from New York who now currently resides in Los Angeles.

His material usually covers the comedy industry itself, criticizing other comedians for being too predictable. He played the character "Andy" a fellow sportswriter and friend of Raymond on the TV show "Everybody Loves Raymond", is a regular guest on The Late Show with David Letterman, contributor to The Daily Show, has performed on HBO and made numerous appearances on Dr. Katz and Home Movies. He is also known for his annual "State of the Industry" address at the "Just for Laughs Comedy Festival" in Montreal. Kindler is often wrongly credited as having worked for the World Wrestling Federation.
