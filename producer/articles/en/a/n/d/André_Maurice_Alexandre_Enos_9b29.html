André Maurice Alexandre Enos

André Maurice Alexandre Enos (born 1913 - ?) was an Old Catholic bishop. He was ordained a Roman Catholic priest on June 29, 1939 by Alphonse-Paul-Desire Gaudron, Roman Catholic Bishop of Evreux, France. He left the Roman Catholic Church in 1950.

He was consecrated bishop on November 4, 1962 by Julien Erni, Bishop of the Siège Ecuménique Ecclésial de Genève. He was then re-consecrated conditionally by Christian Datessen.

He resides at Mont Saint Amand, Belgium and at Paris, France.
