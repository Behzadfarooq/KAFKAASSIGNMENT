András Törőcsik

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

András Törőcsik (born May 1, 1955 in Budapest, Hungary) is a former Hungarian football player of the 1970's and 1980's. From 1977 to 1984 he made 45 appearances and scored 12 goals for the Hungarian National Football Team. He appeared at the 1978 FIFA World Cup (where he was infamously sent off against hosts Argentina) and the 1982 FIFA World Cup. He played for Ujpest Dozsa, Montpellier, Volan SC and MTK Budapest.
