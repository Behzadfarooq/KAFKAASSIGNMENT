Andy Anderson (born 18 July 1947 in Wellington, New Zealand) is an actor best known for his roles on Australian television.

He has appeared regularly in The Sullivans (as Jim Sullivan), Gloss (as Matt Winter), Prisoner (as Rick Manning), Fire (as John Kennedy), and The Bob Morrison Show (as Steve Morrison). Guest appearances on television include: The Flying Doctors, A Country Practice, Xena: Warrior Princess, Hercules: The Legendary Journeys, Heartbreak High, Water Rats, All Saints, Blue Heelers, Neighbours and Stingers.

He is also a musician, having recorded an album called "If I'd Known I'd Live This Long..."
