[edit] Failed Featured Picture Candidates Nominations

These are the pictures which I nominated, but were not promoted to Featured status. They are still nice pictures, good enough to earn a subpage. I have learned much with each nomination about photography, and my choices should soon be of better quality. Please note that I could not have taken even these pictures, as I am a mediocre photographer.


Please also visit my Successful Featured Picture Nominations, and Pictures I have taken and Uploaded to Wikipedia subpages.


Failed because of: jpeg artifacts; grain; overexposure. - The Hagia Sophia.

Failed because of: lighting; only one brand; unexeptional. - Varied chocolates.

Failed because of: a chick Feature picture already exists. - 2 one-day-old chicks.

Failed because of: jpeg artifacts. - This is an F-22 Raptor.

Failed because of: NASA might make a better image. - Neil Armstrong about to take the first step on the moon.

Failed because of: stiching problems; similar to another astronomy Featured picture. - A star cluster that will eventually break apart.

Failed because of: bad lighting; unencyclopedic; reflections; ugly wall. - A water droplet creating ripples.

Failed because of: blown highlights. - A foot path near Diepenbeek.
