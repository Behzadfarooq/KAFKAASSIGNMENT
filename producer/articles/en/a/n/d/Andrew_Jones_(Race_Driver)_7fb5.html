Andrew Jones is a racing driver for Tasman Motorsport in the V8 Supercar series.

Born on 24 June 1980 in Albury, Australia, Jones has had success in a number of forms of motorsport in Australia.

He is currently driving for Team BOC/Brad Jones Racing with team mate Brad Jones and is looking for more success in 2007 in the V8 supercar Championship Series after two frustrating years with Holden.

In 2004 Andrew won the V8 Development Series Championship, the 2nd tier series, and went on in 2005 to secure a drive with the Garry Rogers Motorsport team. In 2006, he joined Tasman Motorsport, replacing Jamie Whincup but had a frustrating and dissapointing year. It was announced that he was moving back to Albury and re-join his uncle and father's team, Brad Jones Racing, replacing John Bowe.
