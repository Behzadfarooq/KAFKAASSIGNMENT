Andrew McBain (born February 18, 1965 in Toronto, Ontario) is a retired former professional ice hockey player who played 608 career National Hockey League games for the Winnipeg Jets, Pittsburgh Penguins, Vancouver Canucks and Ottawa Senators. He retired in 1996.
