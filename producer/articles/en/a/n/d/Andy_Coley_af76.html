Andy Coley

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Andy Coley, born July 7, 1978) is a rugby player who plays for Salford City Reds. He plays as a prop. Andy was born in Warrington, and signed for Swinton Lions, and in 2001 signed for Salford City Reds as a second row forward prior to the 2001 season, but has since moved up into the front row. He mysteriously hasn't been picked for Great Britain national rugby league team, but should get his chance.
