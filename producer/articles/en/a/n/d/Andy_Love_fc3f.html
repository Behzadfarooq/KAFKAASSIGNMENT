Andrew McCulloch Love (born 21 March 1949, Greenock) is a politician in the United Kingdom. He has been Labour and Co-operative member of Parliament for Edmonton since 1997.

Before entering Parliament, Love worked as Parliamentary officer of the co-operative movement. He served as a Haringey councillor for two terms from 1980 and chaired the council's Finance Committee and Housing Committee. He also was formerly a member of the North East Thames Regional Health Authority.

In Parliament he is involved in many backbench groups and campaigns and served as Parliamentary Private Secretary to Jacqui Smith.
