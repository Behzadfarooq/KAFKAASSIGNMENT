Andreas Görlitz (born January 31, 1982 in Weilheim, Bavaria, Germany) is a German footballer who currently plays as a defender for Bayern Munich.

[edit] Career

[edit] TSV 1860 Munich

He began his Bundesliga career as a midfielder for intracity rival TSV 1860 Munich. He shifted to defender in the 2003-04 season where he has played ever since.

[edit] Bayern Munich

He transferred over to Bayern Munich in 2004. He was injured on November 3, 2004 during a Champions League match against Juventus F.C.. On February 11, 2007 he made his comeback in the Bundesliga.
