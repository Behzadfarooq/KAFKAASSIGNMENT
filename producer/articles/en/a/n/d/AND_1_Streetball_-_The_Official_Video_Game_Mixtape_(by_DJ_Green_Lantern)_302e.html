AND 1 Streetball - The Official Video Game Mixtape is the official mixtape to the video game AND 1 Streetball. Being a promotional a mixtape, it was mixed by hip hop DJ and record producer, DJ Green Lantern, who also mixed and produced the official AND1 Soundtrack.

The mixtape could be purchased separately in addition with a purchase of the game as a pre-order bonus.
