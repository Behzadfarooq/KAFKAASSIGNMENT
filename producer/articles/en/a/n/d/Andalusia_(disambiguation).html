Andalusia (disambiguation)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Andalusia is an autonomous community of Spain (see also Al-Andalus).

In addition, the following places in the United States of America are named Andalusia, mainly named for the Spanish region:

  • Andalusia, Alabama
  • Andalusia, Florida
  • Andalusia, Illinois
  • Andalusia, Pennsylvania
  • Andalusia Township, Illinois
  • Andalusia, Georgia

There is also

  • Andalucia, Colombia in South America

In music

  • Andalucia, a song by John Cale, which features on the album Paris 1919
  • Andalucia (album), a 2002 album by Tito & Tarantula.
  • Andalucia Suite by Ernesto Lecuona
