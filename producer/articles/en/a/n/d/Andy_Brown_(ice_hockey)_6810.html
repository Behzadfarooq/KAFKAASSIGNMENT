Andy Brown (ice hockey)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Andy Brown (born February 15, 1944 in Hamilton, Ontario, Canada) is a retired Canadian ice hockey goaltender.

Brown started his professional hockey career with the Detroit Red Wings of the National Hockey League in 1972. He would also play with the Pittsburgh Penguins. He would also play with the World Hockey Associations Indianapolis Racers. Brown, along with Gump Worsley, was the last goalie to play without a goalie mask.

Andy Brown played the last game of a pro-goalie without a mask in 1974.
