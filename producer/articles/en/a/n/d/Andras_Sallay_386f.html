Andras Sallay

Silver 1980 Lake Placid Ice dancing

Andras Sallay (bron December 15, 1953) was a Hungarian figure skater. He competed in ice dance with Krisztina Regöczy. The pair won the gold medal at the 1980 World Figure Skating Championships and the silver at that year's Winter Olympics.
