Andy Hughes is an electronic music producer. He is most known for his work with The Orb where he helped mix and produce Pomme Fritz, Orbus Terrarum, Orblivion, and Cydonia as well as The Orb's singles from this period. Hughes left The Orb after Cydonia. He has done some additional original production work and performed remixes.
