Andrius Velička (born April 5, 1979, in Kaunas) is a Lithuanian footballer, currently on loan at Scottish Premier League club Hearts. He is on a season-long loan from FBK Kaunas, having also previously played in Russia with Anzhi Makhachkala.

On October 15, 2006 Velička scored two goals in the Edinburgh derby against Hibernian, earning 10-man Hearts a draw.[1] He also scored in the 1-1 draw with Dunfermline Athletic on October 28. He netted yet another Hearts goal at Celtic Park, in a 2-1 defeat to the league champions, however Velička became Hearts top goalscorer as a result of his goal. He scored his first hat-trick for Hearts in a 4-0 Scottish Cup third round win against Stranraer on 6 January.
