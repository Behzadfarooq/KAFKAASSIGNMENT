Andres Vasquez

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Andres Vasquez (often misspelled Vasques[1]) (born 16 July 1987 in Gothenburg) is a Swedish football midfielder. He is of Peruvian descent.

After playing for local clubs, he joined IFK Göteborg in 1997, a club he has played for since.
