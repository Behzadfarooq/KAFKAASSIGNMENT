Andrew Siciliano

Andrew Siciliano is a sports anchor for Fox Sports. He can be seen in FSN Final Score, where he is a co-anchor, and on GameTime Live, which airs on FOX Sports Radio from 7:00 to 10:00 PM ET.

Siciliano is also the play-by-play announcer for the Los Angeles Avengers of the Arena Football League[1] and has previously called games for the Las Vegas Gladiators. In addition, he hosts the Red Zone Channel, which is part of the Superfan supplement to the NFL Sunday Ticket pay-per-view package on DirecTV.

Previous assignments included the Ultimate Fantasy Football show, and AFL Weekly, both on FSN, and a morning show on FSR hosted by Tony Bruno.

Mr.Siciliano is a regular host of the Jim Rome Show when Jim is in the "basement".

Andrew Siciliano is a graduate of Syracuse University, which he has mentioned in passing during Final Score.
