Andrés Tambornino

Andrés Tambornino is an Argentine film editor, film director, and screenplay writer.[1]

He works in the cinema of Argentina.

[edit] Filmography

Director and writer

  • Aqueronte (1994)
  • Dónde y cómo Oliveira perdió a Achala (1995)
  • El Descanso (2002)

Editor

  • Pizza, birra, faso (1998) aka Pizza, Beer, and Cigarettes
  • Buenos Aires 100 kilómetros (2004)
  • Camisea (2005)

Production designer

  • Mundo grúa (1999) aka Crane World
