Anders Bruun

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Anders Bruun (born May 15, 1979) is a Swedish Bandy player who currently plays for Västerås SK as a defender. Anders was a youth product of IFK Motala. Anders has played for the Swedish national bandy team.

Anders has only played for two clubs. His list of clubs are as follows-

IFK Motala (1997-2002)
Västerås SK (2002-)
