Andrew Jackson Bryant (October 30, 1823 - May 11, 1882) was the 17th Mayor of San Francisco serving from December 6, 1875 to November 30, 1879.

Bryant was born in Ettingham, New Hampshire. He was elected mayor of San Francisco in 1875 to replace James Otis. Just two and a half years later on May 11, 1882, Bryant was crossing the San Francisco Bay on a ferry. Bryant fell off the ferry and into the bay, and drowned. It is not known if he is still in the Bay or if he was pulled out.
