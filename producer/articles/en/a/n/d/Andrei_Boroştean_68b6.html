Andrei Ionuţ Boroştean (born 25 February 1987 in Cluj-Napoca) is a Romanian footballer currently under contract for Romanian first league side CFR Ecomax Cluj. Boroştean is a player raised by CFR, and he is a left midfielder. He is currently playing at CFR's B team, Unirea Dej.

Below are Boroştean's career statistics:

  • 2002-2003 CFR Cluj 6B / 1 game
  • 2003-2004 CFR Cluj 1B
  • 2004-2005 Unirea Dej 13B / 2 games
  • 2004-2005 CFR Cluj 11A / 2 games
  • 2005-2006 Unirea Dej 12B / 17 games
  • 2006-2007 Unirea Dej B
