I'm a fairly avid television watcher; I make it a point to either watch "live" or tape several shows. I also watch other shows if I stumble across them or am awake when they are on, and there are some shows I miss. All times for shows that I watch are in Eastern Standard Time or Eastern Daylight Time, depending on whether Daylight Saving Time is active.

[edit] Shows I Make a Point to Watch

  • Stargate SG-1 - SciFi 8:00 PM
  • Stargate Atlantis - SciFi 9:00 PM
  • The Amazing Race - Currently watching old season reruns on GSN at 9:00 PM or 12:00 AM, depending on when I can; Planning on watching the new season on CBS when it airs.
  • Family Guy -- new shows on FOX
  • American Dad -- ditto

[edit] Shows I Watch if I'm Awake

  • Most shows on the [adult swim] block, especially:
    • Futurama
    • Family Guy
    • Aqua Teen Hunger Force (I used to hate this show, but my roommate freshman year at college converted me by force :P )
    • Fullmetal Alchemist (See also my Anime page.)
    • Harvey Birdman Attorney at Law
    • Paranoia Agent -- sometimes, that show is just completely screwed up
    • FLCL -- again, sometimes, first time I saw it I had no idea what it was
    • The Oblongs -- not very often

[edit] Shows I Watch if I Stumble into Them

  • MythBusters
  • Star Trek, Star Trek: The Next Generation, Star Trek: Deep Space 9, and I'd watch Star Trek: Voyager and Star Trek: Enterprise if I could find them
  • some other stuff that I can't think of right now

[edit] Shows I Miss

  • JAG -- really good show, at least it ran to a natural end
  • Star Trek: Enterprise -- I was one of the apparant few that liked the show, yes they didn't do it very well (mucking with all sorts of stuff they shouldn't have) but take it at face value with a grain of salt and it's a good show
