Andrius Skerla (born April 29, 1977 in Vilnius) is a Lithuanian professional football defender, currently playing for Russian Premier League club FC Tom' Tomsk. He is 185 cm tall and weighs 74 kg.

Skerla marked his 50th appearance for Lithuania on October 7, 2006 with a first international goal against the Faroe Islands.

His former teams include FK Žalgiris Vilnius, PSV Eindhoven and Dunfermline Athletic.
