[edit] Biography of Andrew Campbell

Andrew Campbell is a recognized Canadian authority on business management. He has worked locally, nationally and internationally in consulting, training & development and on special 'speaker' assignments. His writing(s) has been published on 5 continents. He is the founder of Andrew Campbell & Associates Inc. based in Oakville, Ontario (near Toronto) Canada. Through Visionary Business Leadership activities, his approach transforms companies so that they out-perform their competition.Andrew Gordon Campbell 16:10, 13 October 2006 (UTC)

[edit] Consulting

Andrew specializes in facilitating "Strategic Planning" workshops during which clients prepare their strategic and business plans and define growth strategies. He helps identify and resolve obstacles to performance in the areas of production, finance, human resources, marketing, R & D and sales ... incorporating aspects of Total Quality Management.

[edit] Training

Andrew helps clients clarify their roles and goals, build teams and deal with issues of leadership, conflict, communications, controls, training and structure. He and his Associates facilitate training seminars in Communications Skills, Supervisor Skills, Management Skills and Total Quality Management Skills and many more.

[edit] Executive Leadership

Andrew facilitates discussion groups (i.e. monthly meetings) in which executives develop and hone their business acumen. Executives confidentially discuss their business strategies and challenges with fellow executives, who in turn, offer their objective advice and proven experience.

[edit] Conference Speaker

He is in great demand as a speaker / seminar leader and has addressed many conferences throughout the world ... and in one year, he spoke to audiences totalling over 10,000 people.

[edit] Writer

He has published articles in recognized magazines (e.g., Globe & Mail, Drake Business Review, Small Business Magazine) that have been distributed worldwide. He has published a book "One Step Ahead" (McGraw-Hill Ryerson) that outlines the dominat 5 management techniques to being a high performance company.

[edit] Special National Assignments

Andrew was a chief advisor to the Canada Awards for Business Excellence and helped develop the criteria for Excellence in Business Management ... the criteria by which leading Canadian companies were evaluated for national recognition as one of the best managed companies in Canada. In this role, he served in the capacity of Chairman of the judging panel and a member of the Grand Jury; Panel Judge in the Productivity and in the Quality Category(s). As recongition of his leadership role in Canada, he was a chosen to be a participant on the 1987 Governor General of Canada Study Tour.

[edit] Special International Assignments

Andrew was Canada's representative on the 8th Japan Study Tour for International Executives that studied Japanese Management processes and systems ... together with 11 other representative(s)each from from Australia(1), USA)(3), Germany(1), Italy(1) Brussels(1), England(1), etc. He was a participant on a (company president's) Trade Mission to Europe to examine the impact of Europe Union on Canadian business.

[edit] Educational Background

Andrew has twenty-five years experience in financial and human resource management. He holds a Degree in Commerce and is a Chartered Accountant. He specializes in 'getting Results'. Bridging Western and Eastern thinking, he is a Reiki Master (Usui). He is a recognized authority on management and has worked with organizations in a number of industries. While he has worked with multi-nationals e.g. Toyota and Prudential, he is a specialist in family business - his second book is to be published on this topic focusing on the 4 major challenges that family business faces as it grows and develops.

Andrew(b. 1948)grew up in Montreal. He was educated at Lower Canada College, then at Concordia (B Comm) and McGill University(CA). He is functionally bilingual.
