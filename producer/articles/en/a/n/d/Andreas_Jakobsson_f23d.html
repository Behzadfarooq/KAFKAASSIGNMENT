Andreas Jakobsson (born October 6, 1972 in Teckomatorp) is a Swedish football defender. He has played 36 international matches for the Sweden national team, and was a squad player for the 2002 FIFA World Cup and the Euro 2004.

He was a successful central defender for Landskrona and Helsingborgs IF, where he gradually became a star in the Swedish league. He played in Germany and Denmark, and in 2004 he joined Southampton F.C.. Upon the relegation of Southampton he returned to Helsingborg.
