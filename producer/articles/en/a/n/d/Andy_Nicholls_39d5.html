Andy Nicholls (b. 1963), well known to every football intelligence officer in the UK, is an Everton supporter and former soccer hooligan for over twenty-five years as a leading figure among the violent followers of Everton Football Club.

Andy was classified as a Category C thug-the worst kind. He has been arrested more than twenty times for football related offences and has been deported from Belgium, Iceland and Sweden. He created the terrace fanzine: Get Into Them which was closed down by authorities.

Andy is banned from every ground in the UK; that includes Goodison Park. He is banned from there for life.

[edit] Scally

In October 2002, Andy Produced a book; Scally which deals head on with the victim knife attacks perpetrated by the shadowy County Road Cutters, chronicles the bitter Manchester-Merseyside battles that left hundreds injured. In his book, Andy also confronts the alleged problems of racism at Goodison Park and he describes the rivalries with the gangs of Aberdeen, Chelsea, Millwall, Middlesbrough and many others.

Scally caused tons of controversy in its first publication. It is widely acknowledged as the most revealing, most shocking book ever written about Football Hoolaginism in Britain.
