Andy's Page


Lets see, what to put on here without being too predictable. Ah sod it. Andy is 31 and originally from Wolverhampton, although he now lives in Didcot with his wife and daughter.

Professionally he is an Audio Visual Engineer with a penchant for Videoconferencing.

He'll happily chat about:

  • Red Dwarf
  • James Bond
  • Jaffa Cakes
  • Porcupine Tree

He will start ignoring you if you talk about:

  • Any TV programme that asks to to call in to vote for who you want to stay. So recently that has been Big Brother. Shudder. And X Factor, and anything with Graham Norton.
  • Holby City
  • Richard Madeley
  • James Blunt
