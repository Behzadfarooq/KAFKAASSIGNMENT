Andy Hargrove

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Andy Hargrove was a fictional character on The WB (and later The CW) show One Tree Hill. He was Karen's business professor and then lover. he was portrayed by Kieren Hutchison.

Spoiler warning: Plot and/or ending details follow.


[edit] Season Two

When Karen and he met for the first time, he pretended to be a student and scared her by saying that the teacher was a bully, but it turned out he was the teacher. He persecuted Karen a little, but it soon became clear that he was in love with her. And Karen soon fell for him, healing from her love issue, but she was scared by the age gap between them. It was further evidenced when she saw him chatting like a friend with Lucas. They stayed together however, even though it was a bit of a forbidden love. They had several troubles: for instance, Karen's grades maybe being love driven and Andy's female acquaintance, who turned out to be a woman he cared for since he had crippled her with his car while going to work, which was the reason he gave up his multi-million dollar company.

They soon had troubles with Dan Scott, Karen's former lover, who wanted Andy out of the picture. They began investigating on him and found out Jules' secret, which led to her breaking up with Keith Scott. Andy then helped Lucas take Dan down because of his conning, but that was a complete failure, as Deb Scott would have fallen too, since he made sure to set things so she would look as guilty as him. Later, Andy and Karen left together to care for Andy's ill mother, but he never came back; Karen broke up with him since she did not feel like becoming the mother of his children and set him free.
