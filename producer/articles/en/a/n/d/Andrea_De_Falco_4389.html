Andrea De Falco (born 19, June, 1986 in Ancona) is an Italian football (soccer) midfielder. He currently plays for Pescara Calcio.

He start his career at native club A.C. Ancona. After made 7 Serie A appearances for the team, he was signed by new promoted team Fiorentina, which Ancona faced bankruptcy and condemnation to Serie C2.

He was loaned to Pisa of Serie C1 in the second half of 2004-05 season.

After played 1 and a half season for the Primavera team, he was sold to Pescara in co-ownership deal, because he became 20 year-old.
