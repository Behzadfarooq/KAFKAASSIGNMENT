Andrew Daniels AKA Andrew David Daniels or Andy Daniels is a UK based Composer and Pianist who has scored for Film and TV projects, various Jazz ensembles and the Concert Hall.

[edit] Film scores

Andrew has composed for production companies in the US and UK. Film work includes the scores for Searching For Wooden Watermelons, The Program, Limbo and Two Play. Recent Films have included Blood Ties and Sweet Dreams.

[edit] Jazz

He has performed with many artists from the UK Jazz scene, including Dick Heckstall-Smith as part of DHSS, On the Corner, Adrian Fry, Paul Young as well as his own group playing original material. He has played at many Jazz clubs and festivals in the UK and abroad, including the London Jazz Festival.

[edit] Contemporary Music

Andrew has also worked with several UK ensembles. Duende was played by London Sinfonietta and recorded by BBC Radio 3. Other works include Reflections at Dawn played by Ensemble Firebird and Recall/Derivatives given its premiere performance by Ixion ensemble.
