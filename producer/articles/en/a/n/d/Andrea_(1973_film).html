Andrea is a 1973 Argentine musical comedy film directed by Carlos Rinaldi and written by Ulises Petit de Murat.

[edit] Release

The film premiered in Argentina on 4 October 1973.

[edit] Cast

  • Andrea Del Boca as Andrea
  • Ángel Magaña
  • Raúl Aubel
  • Julieta Magaña
  • Paquita Más
  • Mario Passano
