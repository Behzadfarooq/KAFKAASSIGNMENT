Andrew Chalmers

Andrew Chalmers is a child actor who portrays the voice of Harry in the television series Harry and His Bucket Full of Dinosaurs. He is also in Chicks with Sticks and the television series Darcy's Wild Life on Disney's ABC Family Channel, he plays Jack Adams.
