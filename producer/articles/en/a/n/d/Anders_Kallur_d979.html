Anders Kallur, (born June 6, 1952 in Ludvika, Sweden) is a Swedish former professional ice hockey player.

He was signed by New York Islanders in 1979 and played his entire North American career with that team (except for two games with Indianapolis Checkers).

He played with Islanders during their entire amazing 4 Stanley Cup wins from 1980 to 1983.

Together with fellow swede Stefan Persson he was the first European player to win the Stanley Cup.

Kallur is currently a scout within the New York Islanders organization.

Anders Kallur is the father of Swedish athletes Jenny and Susanna Kallur.
