Andrea Gasbarroni (born 6 August 1981 Turin) is an Italian football midfielder, currently plays for Parma, on loan from Juventus F.C. He is known for his dribbling skills.

[edit] Teams and clubs

  • 1998-2001: Juventus F.C.
  • 2001-2002: Varese on loan from Juventus
  • 2002-2003: U.C. Sampdoria co-ownership with Juventus
  • 2003-2004: Palermo co-ownership with Juventus
  • 2005-2006: Sampdoria on loan from Palermo and Juventus
  • 2006-2007: Parma F.C. on loan from Juventus

[edit] National team

Gasbarroni was in the squad of bronze medal team of 2004 Summer Olympics football tournament.
