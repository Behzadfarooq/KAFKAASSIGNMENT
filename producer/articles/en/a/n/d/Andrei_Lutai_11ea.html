Andrei Lutai (Russian: Андрей Лутай) (born July 24, 1986 in Belgorod, Russia) is a Russian figure skater. He was silver medalist at the 2007 Russian national championships, having placed seventh the year before. Lutai is coached by Alexei Mishin.
