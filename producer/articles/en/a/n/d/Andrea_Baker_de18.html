Andrea Baker

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Andrea Baker is an American-born actor and voice actor performed in minor roles in movies and television.

Her credits are 1990's independent films like Pleasantville and studio movies like Panic and Paparazzi. Baker had a vocal role as the character "inner voices" of minor role actresses in the 2000 movie What Women Want. Baker made brief appearances on 1990's television shows include Beverly Hills 90210, Charmed and Buffy the Vampire Slayer.

Baker does cartoon character voices on animated shows, such as the voice of Clover on Totally Spies. Baker has narrated hundreds of TV and radio commercials for North American companies, as well in a small number of television documentaries.
