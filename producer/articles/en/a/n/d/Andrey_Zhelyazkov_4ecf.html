Andrey Zhelyazkov

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Andrey Zhelyazkov (born July 9, 1952 in Radnevo) is a former Bulgarian football (soccer) player in midfielder role.

He played mostly in France with RC Strasbourg and for Bulgaria national football team he participated at 1986 FIFA World Cup.
