The Andalucia regional football team is the official football team for the Andalucia, Spain. They are not affiliated with FIFA or UEFA.

The project for a football selection-team of Andulucia born in 1928, and started officially during the celebration of the Iberoamerican Expo in Sevilla, 1929, when Andalucia played a friendly match against Club Atlético Boca Juniors in Estadio de Heliopolis, known today as Estadio Manuel Ruiz de Lopera.

They didn't played anymore till 1965, during the 50 years celebration of the Federación Andaluza de Fútbol, when they played against Paraguay national football team in Estadio Sánchez Pizjuán, Sevilla.
