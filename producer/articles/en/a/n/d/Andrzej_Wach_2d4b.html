Andrzej Wach

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Andrzej Wach.

Andrzej Wach is a General Director and a chairman of Polskie Koleje Państwowe S.A. He has worked for Polish railways since 1980, and previously held the position of Chairman in PKP Energetyka.

He received an Electrotechnics degree from Warsaw University of Technology and a degree of Law and Administration from Warsaw University.

Wach also holds a position of Chairman of Supervisory Board of PKP Przewozy Regionalne company.
