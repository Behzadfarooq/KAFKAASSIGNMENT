Andrew Demetriou

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Andrew Demetriou (born April 14, 1961) is the CEO of the Australian Football League and a former Australian rules footballer of Greek-Cypriot heritage. He has two brothers called Steve and Jim who reside in Sydney NSW.
