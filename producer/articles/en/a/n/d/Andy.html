Andy

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Andy
Gender: Unisex
Origin: Greek
Meaning: "Man"


Andy is predominantly a diminutive version of the male given name Andrew, based on the Scottish "ie" diminutive ending. It is derived from the Greek name Andreas, meaning "man". It is also occasionally used as a dimunutive for the female given name Andrea, the Portuguese and Spanish feminine version of the name Andrew.

It is also occasionally spelt as Andie, and is the 217th most popular name in the USA, with less than 0.001% of the people with this name being female.
