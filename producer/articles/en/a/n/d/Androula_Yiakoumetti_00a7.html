Androula Yiakoumetti

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

You have new messages (last change).

Androula Yiakoumetti is a young Cypriot dialectologist currently based at the University of Cambridge. Research focuses on Greek language and socio-cultural factors that influence language acquisition.
