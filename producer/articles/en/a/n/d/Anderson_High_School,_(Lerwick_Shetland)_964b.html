Anderson High School, (Lerwick Shetland)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Anderson High School is a United Kingdom secondary school based in the Shetland Islands in the north of Scotland. The school was founded by businessman Arthur Anderson

The school is involved in several international projects under the umbrella of Global Classroom.
