Andrew K

Andrew K - Photo taken at Nokia Totally Board Festival, 2004

Andrew K is a Greek DJ and record producer. He has released over 30 records in a variety of well-respected labels including Armada, Mo-Do, Pure Substance, Vapour, Babylon Records and more. As a DJ, he has appeared in many countries across the globe. He is also working as A&R manager for Pure Substance Records and Underground Lessons.
