And the Word Was..... is a compilation of the first recordings of the English band Genesis. The album was released in January 1987 on London Records.These recordings were produced by an early Genesis line-up in the late sixties; namely Michael Rutherford (Bass, Guitar, Vocals); Anthony Phillips (Guitar, Vocals); Peter Gabriel (Vocals, Flute); Tony Banks (Keyboards, Vocals) and Chris Stewart (Drums).
