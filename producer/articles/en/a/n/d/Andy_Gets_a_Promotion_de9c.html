"Andy Gets a Promotion" (or "How to Get Head in Business Without Really Trying") was the sixth produced episode of Mission Hill (though it was the fifth to air). It originally aired on July 9, 2000. It was written by Michael Panes.

[edit] Synopsis

When Andy accepts Ron's offer to become assistant manager, he is introduced to Ron's lifestyle of excess and debauchery, but at the cost of his dreams to become a cartoonist.
