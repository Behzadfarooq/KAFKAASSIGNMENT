[edit] Hello!

Good day; I am not the people who come up when one searches "Andy Wang" in Google. When I created an account, I never thought that a name was not necessary. I started editing in early October of 2005. As of April 2007, I have about 3800 edits on the English Wikipedia. Here, you may see my edit history. My user page has been vandalised 7 times. My user talk page has been vandalised 9 times. I have indirectly adopted four users, who are good friends.

I am currently a student with interests in mathematics, natural sciences (physics and chemistry), computer science, engineering (particularly civil), and classical music, especially the Romantic period. My favorite composer is Gustav Mahler. I have played the piano for eight years and have composed and arranged music for my own enjoyment. Numerous other users edit Wikipedia that need not be mentioned.
