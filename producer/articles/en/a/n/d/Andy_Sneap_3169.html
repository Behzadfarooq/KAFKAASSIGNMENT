Andy Sneap

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Andy Sneap (1969) is a British record producer, recording engineer, and mixer. He is best known for producing and mixing many acclaimed heavy metal albums. Andy was also the guitarist for the classic thrash metal band Sabbat. Currently he works out of his own studio, "Backstage Recording", which he founded in 1994.

Andy received a Swedish Grammy for his work on Opeth's Deliverance album, as well as a US Grammy nomination for his work on Killswitch Engage's The End of Heartache.
