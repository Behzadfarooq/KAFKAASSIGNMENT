[edit] Soybeans, etc.

Hello, AndyCanada, and thank you for contacting me on my talk page. I do not have any interest in seeing any changes to the articles in question in either way, to start off. I often review recent changes by new contributors to watch for vandalism or other abuse, and it appears that I discovered a pretty serious dispute between yourself and Yankee76. On reviewing the page history, it looks like all you two are debating over is the inclusion of certain statements about soy protein's nutritional value. If you could explain the exact nature of your dispute on my talk page, it would help me get my head around the dispute more easily.

As far as sockpuppetry goes, having one more than one account, as you said, is not explicitly prohibited per WP:SOCK. However, notable exceptions to this are sock puppets created for the purposes of evading the 3-revert rule (WP:3RR), abusive sockpuppets, and attempting to make a minority view appear to have wider support than it actually does. Using multiple accounts is strongly cautioned against, and in the case that multiple accounts are used to get around rules, most times the administrative approach is to block all but the original account. Please be careful and civil in disputes in the future and try to engage in debate on talk pages instead of edit warring- it's much more conducive to writing an encyclopedia. Please let me know of any more questions you have on my talk page. --Kuzaar-T-C- 17:48, 27 October 2006 (UTC)
