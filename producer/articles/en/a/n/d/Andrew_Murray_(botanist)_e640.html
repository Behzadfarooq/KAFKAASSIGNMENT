Andrew Murray (botanist)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Andrew Murray FRSE FLS (19 February 1812–10 January 1878) was a Scottish botanist.


The standard author abbreviation A.Murray may be used to indicate this person in citing a botanical name.
