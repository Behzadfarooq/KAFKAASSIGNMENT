Andreyevich

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Andreyevich can refer to:

In politics:

  • Alexey Andreyevich Arakcheyev, Russian general and statesman under the reign of Tsar Alexander I
  • Vasily Andreyevich Dolgorukov, Russian statesman
  • Andrei Andreyevich Gromyko, Minister for Foreign Affairs for the Soviet Union and Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union
  • Ivan Andreyevich Khovansky, Russian boyar who led the Streltsy during the Moscow Uprising of 1682
  • Mikhail Andreyevich Miloradovich, Russian general prominent during the Napoleonic wars
  • Pyotr Andreyevich Shuvalov, influential Russian statesman and a counselor to Tsar Alexander II
  • Ivan Andreyevich Tolstoy, Russian officer at Peter I times
  • Pyotr Andreyevich Tolstoy, Russian statesman prominent during and after the reign of Peter the Great

In literature:

  • Ivan Andreyevich Krylov, the most famous Russian fabulist
  • Vasily Andreyevich Zhukovsky, the foremost Russian poet of the 1810s

In music:

  • Nikolai Andreyevich Rimsky-Korsakov, Russian composer and teacher of harmony and orchestration
  • Nikolai Andreyevich Roslavets, Ukrainian composer whose music was largely suppressed after he was declared a "non-person" by the soviet regime

In other fields:

  • Pavel Andreyevich Chekov, character from the fictional Star Trek universe
  • Alexander Andreyevich Ivanov, Russian painter who adhered to the waning tradition of Neoclassicism but found little sympathy with his contemporaries
