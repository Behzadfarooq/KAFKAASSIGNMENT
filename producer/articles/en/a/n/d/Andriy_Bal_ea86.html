Andriy Mikhaylovich Bal (Ukrainian: Андрій Міхайлович Баль) (born on 16 January 1958) was a Soviet (Ukrainian) footballer (midfielder), and a participant of the 1982 FIFA World Cup and the 1986 FIFA World Cup. He played for Dinamo Kiev at that time . Now he helps Oleg Blokhin to coach the Ukraine national football team.
