Andreas Lasnik (born November 9, 1983) is an Austrian football player, who currently plays for FK Austria Wien.

Lasnik came up through the youth system of FK Lankowitz. He broke into the professional level in 2001 with ASK Köflach. In 2001 he was signed by SV Ried of the Austrian first division, the Österreichische Fußball-Bundesliga. He would appear in over 80 matches with the club and net 14 goals. In 2005 he was signed by FK Austria Wien, where he currently plays.
