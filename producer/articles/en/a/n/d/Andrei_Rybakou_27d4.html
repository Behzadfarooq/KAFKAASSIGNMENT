Andrei Rybakou (born March 4, 1982) is a Belarusian weightlifter who competed in the Men's 85 kg at the 2004 Summer Olympics where he won a silver medal.

In April 2006 he won the European Championship. Later that year, during the 2006 World Weightlifting Championships he was crowned as the World Champion..
