Andrew DeClercq

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Andrew DeClercq (born 1 February 1973 in Detroit, Michigan) is a professional basketball player in the NBA.

DeClercq was drafted 34th overall (5th pick of the 2nd round) following a career at the University of Florida, and began his professional career with the Golden State Warriors. He's gone on to play for the Boston Celtics, the Cleveland Cavaliers and the Orlando Magic, averaging 4.8 points and 4.2 rebounds per game.
