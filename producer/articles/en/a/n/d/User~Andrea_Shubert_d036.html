[edit] Introduction In The Third Person

Andrea Jennifer Shubert is the owner of Blue Sky Red Design LLC. She is also the executive producer of Chron X, the first online collectable card game. She is 31 years old, and currently resides in British Columbia.

[edit] Ludography

  • Acrophobia was released by Berkeley Systems in November 1997 on the Bezerk network.
  • Get The Picture was released by Berkeley Systems in 1998 on the Bezerk network.
  • Star Trek: Conquest Online was developed with Genetic Anomalies and published by Activision in March 2000. An expansion set named A Matter Of Time was released in June 2001.
  • WWF With Authority! was developed with Genetic Anomalies and published by THQ and Jakks Pacific in February 2001. It was the first online wrestling trading card game. Several expansions were released over the next two years. In early 2002, the game was renamed "WWE With Authority!"
  • Chron X was released in May 1997 by Genetic Anomalies. After beta testing the product, I was hired as associate producer one year later. When Genetic Anomalies closed in January 2003, I formed a company to continue the product. The game remains online now, having celebrated its ninth anniversary in May 2006.

[edit] The Name Change

I am transgender. Specifically, a male-to-female transgendered woman, diagnosed with gender identity disorder in 1999. I have been transitioning since 2001.

I was born "Anthony Paul Shubert" in March 1975. I didn't feel "right" as early as age two, but didn't pursue those feelings until age 19. Several years of denial and therapy later, I've found a better place. I went public with my transition in 2003, and legally changed my name in January 2004.

Unfortunately, many former customers of my games don't understand my transition. The only reason this page exists is because in its absence, someone posted incorrect "facts" and I fear that would continue.
