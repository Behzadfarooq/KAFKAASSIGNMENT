Andy Selva (born May 25, 1976 in Rome) is the greatest star of the San Marino national football team. He is currently playing for the Italian Serie C1/A club Sassuolo. He is a forward. He has appeared 29 times for the national team and has scored 6 goals, making him the leading scorer for the San Marino National team. He also scored the only goal in the only game which the team have ever won, a 1-0 win over Liechtenstein.
