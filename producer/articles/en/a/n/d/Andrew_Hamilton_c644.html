Andrew Hamilton may refer to:

  • Andrew Hamilton (New Jersey) was colonial governor of New Jersey, 1692–1697 and 1699–1703.
  • Andrew Hamilton (lawyer) (c. 1676–1741), attorney for John Peter Zenger in libel case, served as Attorney General of Pennsylvania.
  • Andrew D. Hamilton is the Provost of Yale University, 2004–present
  • Andrew Hamilton (composer), Irish composer of contemporary music.
  • Andrew H. Hamilton (Indiana) (1834–1895), was a member of the United States House of Representatives.
  • Andrew J. Hamilton (1815–1875), was a member of the U.S. House of Representatives and provisional governor of Texas.
  • Andrew Hamilton was a juror in the Haymarket Riot trial of Illinois v. August Spies (1886). At the time, he was the owner of a hardware store in Chicago.
