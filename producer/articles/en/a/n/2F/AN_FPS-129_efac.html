AN/FPS-129

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The AN/FPS-129 HAVE STARE radar is a space surveillance system deployed by the United States.

It can detect objects in the 1-10 cm size range.

Its TPO is 200 kW.

One radar of this type was previously deployed in Vandenberg Air Force Base, California and has been moved to Vardø, Norway.
