Alchemy: An Index of Possibilities is David Sylvian's second solo album, released in 1985. Produced by David Sylvian and Nigel Walker. The album was originally released in 1985 only on cassette. The remastered 2003 CD release includes two additional tracks – "The Stigma of Childhood (Kin)", and "A Brief Conversation Ending in Divorce".

[edit] Track listing (CD)

  1. "Words with the Shaman Pt.1 Ancient Evening" (David Sylvian, Jon Hassell) – 5:15
  2. "Words with the Shaman Pt.2 Incantation" (Sylvian, Hassell) – 3:29
  3. "Words with the Shaman Pt.3 Awakening (Songs from the Treetops)" (Sylvian, Hassell, Steve Jansen) – 5:18
  4. "Preparations for a Journey" (Sylvian) – 3:40
  5. "The Stigma Of Childhood (Kin)" (Sylvian) – 8:30
  6. "A Brief Conversation Ending In Divorce" (Sylvian) – 3:31
  7. "Steel Cathedrals" (Sylvian, Ryuichi Sakamoto) – 18:58
  • Tracks 1–3 recorded in London 1985, track 4 recorded in Tokyo 1984, track 5 recording undated, track 6 recorded in London 1989, and track 7 recorded in Tokyo and London 1984/1985.
