An Encounter with Faces is an award-winning 1978 Indian documentary film directed and produced by Vidhu Vinod Chopra. It was nominated in 1979 for an Academy Award for Documentary Short Subject.

[edit] Awards & nominations

  • 1979: Nominated, Academy Award for Documentary Short Subject.
  • 1980: Winner, Grand Prix, Tampere International Short Film Festival
