An Evening with John Petrucci and Jordan Rudess is an album by fellow Dream Theater bandmates John Petrucci (guitars) and Jordan Rudess (keyboards). This album is unusual in being performed live (except for the last track) by just these two musicians, and only guitar & piano.

[edit] Track listing

All songs by John Petrucci & Jordan Rudess, except where noted.

  1. "Furia Taurina" – 10:10
  2. "Truth" – 9:48
  3. "Fife and Drum" – 9:30
  4. "State of Grace" – 5:45
  5. "Hang 11" – 11:38
  6. "From Within" (Jordan Rudess) – 5:21
  7. "The Rena Song" (John Petrucci) – 7:03
  8. "In the Moment" – 6:27
  9. "Black Ice" – 10:54
  10. "Bite of the Mosquito (studio version)" – 1:53

[edit] Notes

  • Tracks 4, 7, and 10 appear only on the rerelease.
  • "State of Grace" was first released by Liquid Tension Experiment on their first album.
