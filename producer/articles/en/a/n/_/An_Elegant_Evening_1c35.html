An Elegant Evening is a 1985 (see 1985 in music) album by the American jazz singer Mel Tormé, accompanied by George Shearing.

[edit] Track listing

1. "I'll Be Seeing You" (Sammy Fain, Irving Kahal) 3:29
2. Medley: "Love and the Moon"/"Oh You Crazy Moon"/"No Moon at All" (Mel Torme), (Johnny Brke, Jimmy Van Heusen), (Redd Evans, David Mann) 4:17
3. "After the Waltz Is Over" (Mel Torme, Bob Wells) 5:07
4. "This Time the Dream's on Me" (Harold Arlen, Johnny Mercer) 3:23
5. "Last Night When We Were Young" (Harold Arlen, Yip Harburg) 5:17
6. "You Changed My Life" (George Shearing, George David Weiss) 2:31
7. "I Had the Craziest Dream"/"Darn That Dream" (Mack Gordon, Harry Warren) 4:20
8. "Brigg Fair" (Traditional) 4:19
9. "My Foolish Heart" (Ned Washington, Victor Young) 6:10
10. "You're Driving Me Crazy" (Walter Donaldson) 4:41

[edit] Personnel

Recorded May 1985, in Los Angeles, U.S.:

  • Mel Tormé - vocals
  • George Shearing - piano
