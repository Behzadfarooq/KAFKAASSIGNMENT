An Ji Hwan

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

An Ji Hwan (Hangul: 안지환, born July 24, 1969 in Seoul, Korea) is a Korean voice actor.

He joined the Munhwa Broadcasting Corporation's voice acting division in 1993, and has also worked for the Seoul Broadcasting System.
