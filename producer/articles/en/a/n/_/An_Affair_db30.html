An Affair (정사 Jeongsa) is a 1998 South Korean film. The quiet film about a woman who falls in love with her sister's fiance was the 7th-highest grossing Korean film of 1998 and won the Best Asian Film award at the 1999 Newport Beach International Film Festival.
