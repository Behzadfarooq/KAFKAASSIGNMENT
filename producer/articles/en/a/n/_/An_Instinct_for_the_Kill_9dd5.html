An Instinct for the Kill

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

An Instinct for the Kill is the second of Antonella Gambotto-Burke's books and her second anthology. Published in 1997 by HarperCollins, it features an introduction by Dr. Edward de Bono and interviews with Tori Amos, Jeffrey Archer, Deepak Chopra, Jerry Hall, Charlton Heston, HRH Princess Haya bint al-Hussein (daughter of Hussein of Jordan), Erica Jong, Colleen McCullough, and Gene Simmons of KISS.
