Heart Full of Soul: An Inspirational Memoir About Finding Your Voice and Finding Your Way is an autobiography of American Idol 2006 winner Taylor Hicks.

In August 2006, it was announced that Hicks received $750,000 (USD) to write a memoir of his life. The book will be ghostwritten by Rolling Stone writer David Wild. It is slated for release on April 3, 2007. The title is derived from the song "Heart Full of Soul" by The Yardbirds.
