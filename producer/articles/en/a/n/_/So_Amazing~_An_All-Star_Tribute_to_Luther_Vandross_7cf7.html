So Amazing: An All-Star Tribute to Luther Vandross is a tribute album to Luther Vandross, recorded by R&B and/or soul artists, besides rapper Wyclef Jean and pop singers Elton John and Celine Dion. It was made when Vandross died on July 1, 2005.

This album has been released with the Copy Control protection system in some regions.

[edit] Awards and nominations

[edit] Awards

2006

  • Grammy Awards: "Best R&B Performance by a Duo or Group with Vocals" for "So Amazing" and "Best Traditional R&B Vocal Performance" for "A House Is Not a Home"

[edit] Nominations

2006

  • Grammy Awards: "Best Male R&B Vocal Performance" for "Creepin'" and "Superstar", and "Best R&B Performance by a Duo or Group with Vocals" for "If This World Were Mine"
