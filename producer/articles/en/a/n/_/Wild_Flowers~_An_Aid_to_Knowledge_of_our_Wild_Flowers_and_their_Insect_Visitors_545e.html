Wild Flowers: An Aid to Knowledge of our Wild Flowers and their Insect Visitors

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Wild Flowers: An Aid to Knowledge of our Wild Flowers and their Insect Visitors is a book published in 1916 by Neltje Blanchan. After that, this book is also published by the Project Gutenberg Literary Archive Foundation in 2002.

[edit] References

  • Blanchan, Neltje (2002). Wild Flowers: An Aid to Knowledge of Our Wild Flowers and Their Insect Visitors.. Project Gutenberg Literary Archive Foundation.
