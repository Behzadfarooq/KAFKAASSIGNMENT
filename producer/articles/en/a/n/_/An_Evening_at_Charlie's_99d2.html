An Evening at Charlie's is a 1983 (see 1983 in music) live album by the American jazz singer Mel Tormé, accompanied by George Shearing.

6. "Caught in the Middle of My Years"/"Welcome to the Club" (Mel Tormé, George Shearing)

6:02

8. "Chase Me Charlie" (Noel Coward) 4:04

[edit] Personnel

Recorded October 1983, in Los Angeles, U.S.:

  • Mel Tormé - vocals
  • George Shearing - piano
  • Don Thompson - double bass
  • Donny Osbourne - drums
