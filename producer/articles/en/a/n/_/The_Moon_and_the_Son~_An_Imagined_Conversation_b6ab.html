The Moon and the Son: An Imagined Conversation

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Moon and the Son: An Imagined Conversation is a 2005 Academy Award-winning animated short film.

On March 5, 2006 it won the Academy Award for Animated Short Film. Filmakers John Canemaker and Peggy Stern utilize a combination of animation, home movies and photos to present an imagined conversation between a son (voiced by John Turturro) and an abusive, late father (voiced by Eli Wallach).
