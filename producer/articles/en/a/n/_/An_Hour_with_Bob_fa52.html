An Hour with Bob

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Bob Venturini (right) with guest, comedian Ace Acedo on An Hour with Bob.

An Hour with Bob is a variety show, in a studio setting, hosted by Bob Venturini. The show airs on Cox Cable channel 13 Thursdays at 11:00pm, Fridays at 10:00pm, and Saturdays at 1:00pm.

The subject matter varies greatly for Bob's interviews, as he highlights local businesses, people in the news, and often gives the stage over to new local talent. Shows are likely to include music, food, art, travel, movies, sports, astrology and wildlife. There are shows that introduce new products, interviews with local heroes, and segments on new developments in medicine.

An Hour with Bob is filmed in Cox Studios in Pawtucket, Rhode Island on a bi-weekly basis. Shows are recorded in realtime (live-to-tape format) which means that the show is filmed as if it was on live television with no editing of the content in the show, unless there is a true need.

The studio control room is equipt with a chroma-key device for the bluescreen effect; digitally inserted backgrounds for the show include stationary video of the providence skyline, and a virtual studio set. The show is recorded and then sent to the statewide playback location for Cox Cable, and on the air within three days.
