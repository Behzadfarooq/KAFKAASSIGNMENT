An Chéim [1] (Collaborative Higher Education Information Management) was established by the Department of Education and Science in Ireland(DoES) along with the Institutes of Technology. An Chéim, from the Irish for "degree" or "step", encompasses the management of Student, Library, Finance, Timetabling and HR information in the Institutes of Technology.

Since its establishment in 2000 An Chéim has transformed the way the institutes meet the needs of their students and staff with the benefit of four sophisticated applications in the MIS Programme;

Agresso - Finance

Banner - Student

Core - HR/ Payroll

Millennium - Library

and the more recent addition in 2005 of the timetabling package

Syllabus Plus

These applications have been modified specifically for the Institutes of Technology and Tipperary Institute. Not only does it improve communication and services to students and staff, it supports new models of education delivery and management. These enable the Institutes to be more effective in their operations in delivering high – quality education to a diverse community of students.


The technical services area of an Chéim provides 3 main functions to the Institutes of Technology and Tipperary Institute.

1. Technical support of the development and centrally hosted environment.

2. Functional support to the Institutes of Technology in the pre-production and live environment (both distributed and centrally hosted).

3. Infrastructure setup for project implementations in the pre-production environment.
