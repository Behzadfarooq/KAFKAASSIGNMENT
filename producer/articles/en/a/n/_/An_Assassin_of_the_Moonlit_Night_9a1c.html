An Assassin of the Moonlit Night (Japanese: 月夜の暗殺者, - Tsukiyo no ansatsusha) is episode 76 of the anime series Naruto. The episode's storyline is from the Invasion of Konoha manga arc.

[edit] Plot

Spoiler warning: Plot and/or ending details follow.

Sasuke is unable to fight, due to overuse of his chakra and Orochimaru's curse-seal, so Naruto is forced to fight Gaara. Gaara traps Sakura while she was trying to protect Sasuke. Her actions haunt Gaara and in a flashback, Gaara relives his childhood, and how his caretaker, Yashamaru, betrayed him and tried to kill him.
