An Adventure in Make-Believe

An Adventure in Make-Believe is the 15th episode in season 2 in the Barney & Friends television show which airs on the Public Broadcasting Service. The episode features Bob West as the voice of Barney, West was featured on the show from 1992 - 2001.

[edit] Plot

When BJ needs to learn to use his imagination, Barney & Friends help him imagine a pretend jungle. They even save the fair princess Baby Bop!
