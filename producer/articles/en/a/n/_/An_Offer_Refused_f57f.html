[edit] Plot

As Vince and Turtle break out the credit cards to decorate the new mansion, Eric worries that the 'Aquaman' offer isn't in writing. Ari does all that he can to close for his client, but he just can't seal the deal. The only person in the house unconcerned about the state of financial flux is Drama, who is preoccupied with his calves and is considering augmentation-- regardless of the $10K price tag. Working the phones frantically, Ari and Eric believe they have finally gotten Vince his popcorn movie... but their glee is short-lived when they find out that it's still not official--and while they are in the club celebrating, they find out that Leonardo DiCaprio has gone off to Europe to talk to James Cameron "about some fish movie".
