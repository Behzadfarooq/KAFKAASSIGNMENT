An Old-Fashioned Piano Party is the twentieth episode of season three of the television situation comedy Will & Grace.

Spoiler warning: Plot and/or ending details follow.

[edit] Plot

To stop her and Will from growing apart, Grace buys an expensive piano to cement their bond. And after being dumped, Jack starts writing trashy romance novels, gaining Karen's obsessive interest.

[edit] Trivia

  • Grace helps Will practice his piano skills by having him play "In My Life" by The Beatles. Later in that episode, Grace and Larry are seated at the piano, singing and playing the climax of the Donna Summer/Barbra Streisand song "No More Tears (Enough Is Enough)." At the end of the episode, Will sings Love Will Keep Us Together by Captain & Tenille.
