The Electric Joe Satriani: An Anthology is an album by guitarist Joe Satriani, released in 2003. It is comprised of two CDs consisting of a collection of the best of Satriani's electric guitar tracks remastered. It also contains previously unreleased song "Slick" and "The Eight Steps" which was previously only available in Japan.
