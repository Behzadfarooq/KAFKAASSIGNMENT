What Was Asked of Us: An Oral History of the Iraq War by the Soldiers Who Fought It is a 2006 book presenting the oral history of soldiers who participated in the Iraq War. It was written by Trish Wood, an award-winning author who has been honoured by the Canadian Association of Journalism, among other organizations. Its interviews are a qualitative approach to documenting the war.
