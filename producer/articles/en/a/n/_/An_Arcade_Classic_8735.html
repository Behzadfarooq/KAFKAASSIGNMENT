[edit] Biography

An Arcade Classic is the acoustic/expermental project of Pennsylvania native Alex Swisher. The project's name was inspired by Swisher's father, who worked in arcades during most of Alex's childhood. The noises of the games intriqued Alex, and at the age of 14 he began to incorporate them into his songwriting. An Arcade Classic is currently recording its first release due out late spring/early summer 2007.

[edit] Live Show

Swisher has never mentioned anything about live performances. It is doubtful, though, because the music would be incredibly hard to duplicate live without numerous other performers.

[edit] Trivia

All of An Arcade Classic's song titled are derived from classic video games such as Sinistar, Joust, and Berzerk.
