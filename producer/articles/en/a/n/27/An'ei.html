An'ei

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

An'ei (安永) was a Japanese era after Meiwa and before Tenmei and spanned from 1772 to 1781. The reigning emperors were Go-Momozono and Kōkaku.

[edit] Change of era

Due to the enthronement of Emperor Go-Momozono and continuing damages from fires and storms, on Meiwa 9, known as the Trouble Year, the era became the An'ei (Peaceful eternity).
