  • I never have enough money/time, even if I do. I planned to play Painkiller: Battle out of Hell all day, but instead I started expanding my Wikipedia profile. Yet tomorrow I'll complain about this decision. Hm, big deal.
  • I have many dreams concerning my life, as well as some goals, but I strongly wish and aim to leave Finland before 25 years old and to be able to start a great enough, disease-free life in some English country, perhaps Canada or even the United States.
  • My outfit and hair doesn't match what I want at all, but I'm too lazy and poor to change it. Also, I mean changing it to how I want me to look like, your opinion doesn't count now.
  • According to some people on mainly supervised situations, I am funny, friendly, kind and all that jazz. I don't know what people really think about me. It's kinda odd, actually. Then again, I don't know what people think about you either.
  • I really like Hymn of the Soviet Union. People can draw their own conclusions, but I simply think it's outstanding and majestic national anthem. I wouldn't salute it any more than The Imperial March, though.
