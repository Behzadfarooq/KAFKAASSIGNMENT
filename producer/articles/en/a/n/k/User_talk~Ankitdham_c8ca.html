Good morning everybody , I am ……………….. and today I am going to speak on the topic right to information act 2005.


First of all let us see . WHAT IS RTI ACT?

   #INTRODUCTION

THE RTI ACT 2005 IS A LAW ENACTED BY PARLIAMENT OF INDIA GIVING INDIANS ACCESS TO GOVERNMENT RECORDS. ANY PARSON MAY REQUEST INFORMATION FROM A PUBLIC AUTHORITY.THE ACT ALSO REQUIRES EVERY PUBLIC AUTHORITY TO COMPUTERISE THEIR RECORDS FOR WIDE DISSEMIATION & TO PROACTIVELY PUBLISH CERTAIN CATEGORIES OF INFORMATIONS SO THAT THE CITIZENS NEED MINIMUM RECOURSE TO REQUEST FOR INFORMATION FORMALLY.

     #INFORMATION DEFINED

IN TERMS OF THE SECTION 2(f) OF THE ACT, INFORMATION HAS BEEN DEFINED AS ANY MATERIAL IN ANY FORM. p(THROUGH THIS SECTION WE CAN ACCESS ANY INFORMATION RELATING TO THE PRIVATE BODIES AND WHICH CAN BE ACCESSED BY THE GOVERNMENT) AND INFORMATION RELATING TO ANY private body which can be accessed by a public authority under any OTHER LAW FOR THE TIME BEING IN FORCE.

    #RIGHT UNDER THE ACT

now let us look what is right ACCORDING TO SECTION 2J THE RIGHTS UNDER THE ACTS ARE AS FOLLOWS

  • InspectION OF works, documents, records.
  • TakING notes, extracts or certified copies of documents or records.
  • TakING certified samples of Materials


WHEN IT WAS ENACTED

  THIS LAW WAS PASEED BY PARLIAMENT ON 15TH JUNE 2005 AND CAME IN TO FORCE ON 13TH OCTOBER.

FEES (In just 10 rs. We can access any information we require ,) FOR CENTRAL DEPARTMENT AS OF 2006, THERE IS just A FEE OF RS10 FOR FILLING UP REQUEST ,RS2 PER PAGE OF INFORMATION AND RS5 FOR EACH HOUR OF INSPECTION AFTER THE FIRST HOUR.

Here the question arises WHY WE NEED A LAW ON RTI?

        Let us look at its #BENEFITS
        -



    *TRANSPARENCY
                       THIS LAW PROMOTES TRANSPARENCY & ACCOUNTABILITY AND ENABLE PEOPLE’S PARTIPATION IN GOVERNANCE.

      *MINIMISE CORRUPTION
                          BECOS OF THE TRANSPERENCY IN THE SYSTEM EVERYTHING IS WATCHED BY THE CITIZENS.
   
      *MAINTAIN RECORDS
                            LEGISLATION WILL HELP SET UP SYSTEMS AND MECHANISM IN THE DIFFERENT DEPARTMENTS THAT FACILITATE EASY ACCESS TO INFORMATION.



  #EFFECTS

now let us look at its effectiveness IN FIRST YEAR OF NATIONALRTI,42876 APPLICATIONS FOR INFORMATION WERE FILED TO CENTRAL PUBLIC AUTHORITIES.OF THESE 878 WERE DISPUTED AT THE FINAL APPELLATE STAGE-THE CENTRAL INFORMATION COMMISSION.A FEW OF THESE DECISION HAVE THERE AFTER BEEN MIRED IN FURTHER LEGAL CONTROVERSY IN THE VARIOUS HIGH COURTS OF INDIA.
