Anka Romensky (Born September 16, 1980 in Kiev, Ukraine) is a Ukrainian model now living in Miami, Florida. She was Playboy's Playmate of the Month for February 2002. When she was 10 years old, her family moved to the United States. Anka had been modelling for five years before she was discovered by Playboy.
