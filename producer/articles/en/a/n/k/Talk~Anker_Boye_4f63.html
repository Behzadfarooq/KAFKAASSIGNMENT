[edit] Bad linkage

Partisan linkage should not be used as a source of information. It is a large mistake to think that a link to a conservative homepage could justify information on social democratic politician. I am also very disappointed with the non-bias focus in the article as well.

ChocoFanten 14:36 30/7/2006

Jan and Anker arguing about the "47 committees" was a pretty frequent topic in the last election campaign. This criticism was probably first raised by Jan Boye, but also repeated by Fyens Stiftstidende, and mentioned in TV2/Fyn. The Tina Turner business was headline news on both national tv channels and it seems beyond doubt that it was influential in the election. It was also the topic of a large number of letters to the editor in Fyens Stiftstidende. On the other hand, Fyens Stiftstidende's ' focus on the struggle between Jan Boye and Anker Boye was a clear help to both of them at the expense of all other political parties. The election was a decisive defeat for Anker Boye (and consequently for the social democrats in Odense) with his number of personal votes down from 36,000 [1] to 18,400 [2], just like the election was catastrophic to Venstre in the same city (17,000 [3] votes slashed to 6,800 [4]). Jan Boye simply crushed both Venstre and the Social Democrats in Odense. Valentinian (talk) 14:19, 30 June 2006 (UTC)

Well I agree on a great deal of what you mention. But it still does not justify the use of linkage to a conservative homepage for information on this subject. And by the way, only uses appalling few lines to describe Anker Boyes twelve years as mayor of Odense. --ChocoFanten 20:55, 1 July 2006 (UTC)
