Anke Möhring (born August 28, 1969 in Magdeburg, Saxony-Anhalt) is a former freestyle swimmer from East Germany, who won the bronze medal in the 400m Freestyle at the 1988 Summer Olympics in Seoul, South Korea. She was named Swimming World's European Swimmers of the Year in 1989.
