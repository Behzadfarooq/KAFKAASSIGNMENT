Ankkarock
Location(s) Vantaa, Finland
Years active 2000 – present
Date(s) June
Genre(s) Rock, metal
Website Ankkarock.fi

Ankkarock is a rock and metal festival held annually in Finland since 2000. The 2007 edition will take place on August 4 and August 5 at Korso in Vantaa.
