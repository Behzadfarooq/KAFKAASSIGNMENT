Anke Katrin Eißmann

Anke Katrin Eißmann (b. 1977) is a German illustrator and graphic designer known for her illustrations of J. R. R. Tolkien's legendarium. She studied visual communication at Bauhaus University in Weimar. Eißmann has also made a number of short films.
