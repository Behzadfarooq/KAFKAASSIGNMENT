Anke Pietrangeli

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Anke Pietrangeli (born; 15 November 1982) is a South African singer who was the winner of the second season of popular talent search series Idols in South Africa in 2003. Like her predesscor, Heinz Winckler, Anke also had a nickname: The Kimberley Diamond. Pietrangeli was convinced by her brother, Sven, who was always certain that his sister would be a star.
