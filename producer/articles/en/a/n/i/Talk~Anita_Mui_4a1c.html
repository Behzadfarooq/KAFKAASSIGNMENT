[edit] Comparison to Madonna

How come there is no discussion on this talk page, and no history for this page either?

Suggest deleting unsupported comments re Madonna in Trivia section, as the point is already made (albeit more briefly) in the section "Music career". Fayenatic london 14:46, 15 October 2006 (UTC)

Having taken the liberty of doing what Fayenatic suggested, I don't think it's really relevant to the article to have a bits of information in the trivia section about her comparison to Madonna. As it is anyway, comparing her to Madonna is taking too much of a Western-centric viewpoint. Anita was one of the greatest stars in HK history, and I find it a little demeaning to having to keep comparing HK's stars to Western ones. However, on another level, I think we can draw a relationship between Madonna and Anita, as yes, they may both have a similar status in their respective countries, and yes, they are both popular, wealthy and etc; but since that's pretty much just a matter of opinion, I don't think we really need to put it in the article. It's just not objective nor factual. That's just my opinion though. With that being said, if you really do want to highlight their similarities and differences, do it properly, not like the half-hearted attempt that I cleaned up. Dagari 10:53, 27 January 2007 (UTC)
