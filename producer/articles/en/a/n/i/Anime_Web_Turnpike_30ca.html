Anime Web Turnpike

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Anime Web Turnpike (also known as Anipike) is an online anime web site that has a large database of links to various anime sites. Anyone can add their own website to the database by setting up a username on the site and adding it to the category it applies to. The website also has services such as a community forum, chat room and a magazine although these don't seem to get noticed by the large amount of people who visit the website.

The site and its creator were featured in the 2003 documentary film Otaku Unite.
