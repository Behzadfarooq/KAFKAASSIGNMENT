Anisul Hoque(Bengali: আনিসুল হক), (1965) is a Bangladeshi author, novelist, dramatist, and journalist.

[edit] Life

Anisul Hoque was born in Rangpur in 1965. His father is late Mofazzal Hoque and mother is Mst. Anwara Begum. He was the student of Rangpur PTI primary school, Rangpur Zilla School, Rangpur Carmichael College. Hoque graduated from Bangladesh University of Engineering and Technology, trained as a civil engineer. His inspiration in journalism and writing started during his student life. After his graduation he joined to serve as a government employee but resigined only after 15 days. Later, He started working as a journalist. Currently, Hoque is working as a Deputy Editor of the Bengali language daily Prothom Alo.
