Animal (2005 film)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Animal is a 2005 action adventure direct-to-video film starring Ving Rhames, Terrence Howard, Jim Brown and Chazz Palminteri. It was directed by David J. Burke and written by David C. Johnson.
