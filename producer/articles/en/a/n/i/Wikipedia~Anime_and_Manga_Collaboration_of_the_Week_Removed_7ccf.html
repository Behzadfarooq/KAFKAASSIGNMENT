[edit] 4Kids Entertainment

Nominated on 23 September 2006; Failed to gain enough votes to be reinstated or given AMCOTW status; Removed on 7 October 2006.

Reason 
  • This article needs attention because the following things are wrong with the page:

Criticism and controversy is a mess. Most of the section needs a complete rewrite and is unstable. An anonymous user is trying to excise a huge chunk of info without giving a valid reason as to why it should be edited (in his words "Link to the article in the external links section") when it should be intergrated into the section itself. Examples of 4Kids edits is rife and a target for fancruft and needs to have several firm examples. No corporate history. It needs a fresh set of editors to bring this article, no matter how hated the company is, to being stable and neutral.

[edit] List of anime

Nominated on 13 June 2006; No substantial problems were actually cited. Removed on 3 July.

Reason 
  • I know, right now you're thinking "huh? That's not even an anime" but let me state my point. Part of the problem with our past collaberations is that, often times, they're on anime most of us have never seen before, so the page can't really improve much. My goal with nominating List of anime is that EVERYBODY can contribute to this one, with no excuse. I think if we pool our efforts, we should be able to create a very near-complete list that conforms to a set style throughout. Plus, if we can get a complete list to see what all anime we have missing, so much the better for expanding outwards further.
Support 
  1. SeizureDog 21:10, 13 June 2006 (UTC)
  2. Haku raja 06:01, 24 December 2006 (UTC)
Comments 
  • No list will ever be complete. But exactly what is wrong with the list that needs to be fix? If you can't give one, then the nomination will have to be pulled. --TheFarix (Talk) 21:27, 13 June 2006 (UTC)
  • SeizureDog didn't say there's anything wrong with the list. He said that he thought it would be useful for everyone to contribute to the list to make it more complete. By having everyone work on it for a week, adding titles to the list, the list is that much more likely to be closer to complete. ···日本穣? · Talk to Nihonjoe 21:53, 13 June 2006 (UTC)
  • Exactly. Ideally, I'd like to build it up to be as complete a list as, say, ANN's. Also, the consistance of titles having romaji is staggered, and the whole format would look better if we could work it into a table. In any case, I think by having CotWs that everyone can work on will help prevent those 1 KB improvements like we saw last week. Nominating lesser known titles isn't working out for us. We need to start nominating articles that either apply to anime as a whole or popular titles that 80% of us have already seen some of if want to make it an actual collaberation. As it is, the CotW doesn't seem to get more than a couple of people who were already working on it anyways. --SeizureDog 23:56, 13 June 2006 (UTC)
  • To me, this is a good candidate for categorization and deletion (or just plain deletion since the titles are likely categorized already.) There was a previous list that here and that list was removed. While a good idea... It might be good to clear redlinks or improve articles at the (more descriminating) 100 Anime list. --Kunzite 23:45, 13 June 2006 (UTC)
  • A list of english dubbed anime was already started (by myself actually) then later deleted for the reason that it should be a category, not a list. The same is true, if not more true, of this complete list of anime. By clicking on the anime category at the bottom of the page, the user is brought to an automatically generated list which is far more complete than any list manually put together could be, even if it doesn't include anime that does not have an article yet...but that's what article suggestion pages are for. -Dave 18:01, 21 June 2006 (UTC)

[edit] June 2006

[edit] Neon Genesis Evangelion

Nominated on 13 June 2006; Was removed on 13 June as it was decided this article did not fit the WP:AMCOTW nomination requirements.

Reason 
  • This article needs attention because the following things are wrong with the page: In sum, the article is a laundry list of fan reactions and speculations, providing only modest overview of the topic itself. Specific issues: it contains tons of material that could be better massaged into the text, deployed in daughter articles or cut altogether as cruft; it is poorly organized; it has absolutely no citations worthy of the name and no critical reviews, not even from the DVD liner notes or merchant websites; it's too long and repetitive but with no discrete section for production issues. I'm inclined to think that a week or two's worth of collaborative googling and database-scrounging could whip this article into shape. See its peer review.
Support 
  1. Monocrat 00:49, 13 June 2006 (UTC)
Comments 
  • The article is already of good quality and has a B-rating. AMCOTW is about taking nonexisting articles, stubs, or starts and turning them into B-rated or better articles, not polishing B-rated or better articles. --TheFarix (Talk) 01:43, 13 June 2006 (UTC)
    • Fair enough. Is there a way to withdraw nominations, or should I just delete? Apologies for the trouble!--Monocrat 01:56, 13 June 2006 (UTC)
