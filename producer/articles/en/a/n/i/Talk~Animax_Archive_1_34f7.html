About information on the Animax Asia channel

It should be noted that the views expressed here regarding the Animax Asia channel are very similar to the complaints made elsewhere by the hardcore anime fans of philippines that amounts to a very small number of the total viewers of Animax Asia. While it is indeed true that recently Animax Asia has had some screwups in their program lineup in the supermaniax block ,we cannot accept the statement that Animax Asia is catering only for kids under 10 by any means just because the anime is aired in English dubbed sound tracks.
