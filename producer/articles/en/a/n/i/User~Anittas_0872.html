I am Anittas, a Romanian from Moldavia. I took this nickname after the second king of the Hittites. I am male. The best article started by me is Battle of Vaslui. Between June 5, 2006 and February 23, 2007, I was banned. Jimbo Wales had banned me because of my refusal to apologize for a photo that I had posted, and which he considered as offensive. During this time, my contribution on Wiki was very limited:—I sometimes used proxy editors that would post my material.
