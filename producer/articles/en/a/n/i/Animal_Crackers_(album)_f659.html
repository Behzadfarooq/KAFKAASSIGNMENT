Animal Crackers (album)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Animal Crackers
Studio album by Wee Hairy Beasties
Released 24 October 2006
Label Bloodshot Records

Animal Crackers is the first album by children's music group Wee Hairy Beasties. It was released on 24 October 2006 by Bloodshot Records.

  11. "Buzz Buzz Buzz"
  12. "Cyril The Karaoke Squirrel"
  13. "Toenail Moon"
