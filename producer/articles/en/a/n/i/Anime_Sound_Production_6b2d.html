Anime Sound Production (株式会社アニメ・サウンド・プロダクション, Kabushiki gaisha Anime Saundo Purodakushon?) is a sound effects company working in the television, movie, radio, video, CD, cassette and game animation industry in Japan.

[edit] Company history

The company was founded in 1976 by Shoji Kato (加藤 昭二, Katō Shōji?), a former sound effects artist from Fizz Sound Creation (formerly Ishida Sound Productions, Inc.).
