Animaritime is an anime convention hosted by the Mount Allison University anime club. The convention was first held on the university campus in Sackville, New Brunswick in 2004.[2] It was the first anime convention to be held in the Canadian Maritimes and the first in North America to be held in the Atlantic Time Zone.[3]

Animaritime was so successful that it was held again from February 25 through 27, 2005 and attendance doubled.[4][5] Animaritime 2006 was held between February 24 and February 26, 2006.[6]

On September 15, 2006, it was announced that Animaritime 2007 would be held at the Ramada Hotel at Crystal Palace in Dieppe, New Brunswick.[3] It is scheduled for February 23 through 25, 2007.[1]
