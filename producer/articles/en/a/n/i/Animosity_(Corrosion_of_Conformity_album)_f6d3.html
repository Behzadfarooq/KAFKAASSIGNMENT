[edit] Trivia

Metallica, long time fans of Corrosion of Conformity, performed the song "Holier" at least twice live in concert (Los Angeles and Donnington both in 1995).

Monster Voodoo Machine covered the song "Holier" on their 1998 release Direct Reaction Now! (it was the hidden track).

The artwork for the album cover was created by legendary punk artist Pushead.
