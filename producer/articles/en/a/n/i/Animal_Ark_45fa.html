Animal Ark is a children's book series. They were previously written by Lucy Daniels, but are now written by Ben M. Baglio. The general plot of each book featured Mandy, the daughter of the local veterinarian, finding animals in trouble and trying to help them with the assistance of her best friend James and other people in the village.

The name Animal Ark, is also the name of the vet surgery.

It has since been turned into a TV series and a magazine.
