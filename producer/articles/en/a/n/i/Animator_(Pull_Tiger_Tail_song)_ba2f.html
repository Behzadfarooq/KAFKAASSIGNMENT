"Animator" is a song by Pull Tiger Tail, initially released as their debut single by the Young and Lost Club Records label on September 25, 2006.

The single release is now a rarity due to only 1000 hand-stamped copies being released, 300 on CD and 700 on 7", with the latter featuring exclusive band artwork.

The song was runner up single of the week in NME.

[edit] Track Listing

  1. "Animator" - 3:31
  2. "Even Good Kids Make Bad Sports" - 4:07
  3. "L'animateur [Demo]" - 3:42

[edit] Music Video

The music video for "Animator" features the members of the band ghost riding their specially made, orange Range Rover whilst playing the song alongside the vehicle. The video was shot all in one take, on a long straight section of road.

[edit] Trivia

  • The B-side, "L'animateur [Demo]", a cover of "Animator" sung in French, was written entirely with the use of an online language translator and one of the band member's A-Level knowledge of the subject. The band have since given out the lyrics to their fans in the hope that someone will help with a more accurate translation for the final version of the song.
