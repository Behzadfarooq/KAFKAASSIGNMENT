[edit] Which is right?

The first animals in space were fruit flies

The first animal in orbit was the Soviet dog Laika onboard Sputnik 2 on 3 November, 1957. Laika died during the flight. At least 10 other dogs were launched into orbit and numerous others on sub-orbital flights before April 1961 when Yuri Gagarin became the first man in space. (See Russian space dogs)
