The Anicuns Microregion

The Anicuns Microregion is a geographical division in the state of Goiás, Brazil. It is made up of 13 municipalities located near Anicuns, a small city and municipality west of Goiânia. For a map see [1]

[edit] Important Data (all figures are from 2000)

  • Population: 103, 276 habitantes
  • Cities: 13
  • Total Area: 5, 483.0 km²
  • Population Density: 18.84 inhab/km²
