Animosity is a deathcore band from San Francisco, California. They released their debut album, Shut It Down on Tribunal Records in 2003, when the members of the band ranged from ages 16-17. In 2005, they released their sophomore disc Empires on Metal Blade imprint Black Market Activities and toured the United States with Origin and Malevolent Creation. They were previously on tour with Ed Gein and The Number Twelve Looks Like You. They have just ended their tour with, Unearth, Bleeding Through, Terror, and Through the Eyes of the Dead, which started on September 7, 2006 and ended on September 30, 2006. Their new album, Animal, will be produced by Kurt Ballou of Converge and released in summer 2007.
