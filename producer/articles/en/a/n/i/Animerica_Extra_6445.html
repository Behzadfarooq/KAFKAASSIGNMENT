Animerica Extra was a monthly shōjo manga magazine that was published in North America by Viz, LCC (now VIZ Media) from 1998 to 2004.

Animerica Extra complemented Animerica. A mature themes title published by Viz called Pulp also complemented Animerica Extra.

The titles published in Animerica Extra included:

  • Banana Fish
  • Chicago
  • Fushigi Yūgi
  • Marionette Generation
  • Revolutionary Girl Utena
  • Short Program
  • Steam Detectives
  • Video Girl Ai
  • X/1999
