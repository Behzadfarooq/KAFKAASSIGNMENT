Animal (band)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Animal is an instrumental rock band based in Brooklyn, NY. Formed in the summer of 2004, the group began in San Francisco. Moving between New York City and San Francisco, Animal began to develop a unique brand of math rock. In the fall of 2005, Animal was signed to Unfun Records, an independent music label from Berkeley, CA. Recently affiliated with the Lumberjack Mordam distribution group.

In the summer of 2006 Animal toured the West Coast of the U.S. with Archaeopteryx, also of Brooklyn, NY. The groups went from Seattle, WA down to San Diego, CA. In August, Animal toured as they drove back to New York City. Animal has played with numerous other notable bands in the independent music world, including the Two Gallants, Knot Feedere (ex Don Caballero), Dirty Projectors, Paradise Islands (ex Erase Errata) and others.

The group consists of Patrick Higgins on guitar (ex/ Mayfair, Inner Struggle) and Alexander Perrelli on drums (ex/ Sidekicks, Miso Militia). Working within the constraints of the duo format, Animal has extended and elaborated on the traditional possibilities of the two-person band. Using complex layering, looping, phasing and improvisation, coupled with frenetic drumming and fluid time signatures, Animal's music recalls many elements of free jazz and the avant garde while still commiting itself to the post-rock and post-hardcore aesthetic. Some of the bands' influences are Hella, Don Caballero, Battles, Ruins, Lightning Bolt, and a compendium of experimental music composers.

"The Animal LP is composed around a central group of melodic and harmonic motifs, which are explored, transformed, and pulled apart throughout each of the tracks. Despite the composition of specific motifs and patterns, Animal uses an improvisational method in the arrangement and realization of each piece. The duo have created a sound that uses both subtle and drastic shifts in tone, tempo, and intensity, while maintaining an aggressive edge and drive. The LP has over an hour of intricate guitar tapestries and frenetic drumming, off-set with thickets of noise. The record was recorded by Paul at Capture Sound Studios in Williamsburg, Brooklyn in April of 2005. Mastering by Brandon Abhorrance at Thrashmasters.com in Santa Cruz, CA." -amazon.com

"Animal play intricate, noisy, beautiful music that will make you take your pants off and think about your mother's best friend from high school. enjoy[ed] this record... Rating: 5 of 5 Stars!" -Customer Review from unfunrecords.com
