Anita Blond (born May 27, 1976, in Budapest, Hungary) is a former pornographic actress.

She has started her career as a Penthouse model, then in 1995 with some acts for the Private Media Group. She also modeled concurrently with her adult film career, modeling for such magazines like Perfect 10.

She left the porn industry in 2001, starring in an estimated 100 movies.
