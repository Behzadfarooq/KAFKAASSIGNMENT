[edit] About Myself

Anirudh B. Vedam is a smart kid who goes to Kennedy Middle School,in Cupertino, California. He likes many sports and his favorite is baseball. His favorite team is the SF Giants. When he was in kindergarten he won a reading award, and his Destination Imagination team went to the state finals. His baseball team went to the finals. His pet peeves include restricted behavior, less free time, weird games, and perverts(extremely gross and sick minded people). He is the co-CEO of BASI, a company that specializes in Bay Area Sports Newsletters. He achieved the 5th highest Current Events score in his school. He has two blogs called http://avedsgames.blogspot.com/ and http://beat-la.blogspot.com/. --Ani vedam 05:14, 3 December 2006 (UTC)
