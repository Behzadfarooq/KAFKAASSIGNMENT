[edit] About Me

I was born on December 14 1990. I'm a hyperactive, young, cheerful 16 year old boy. Growing up, I never knew my big brother Kyle very well. I always thought he was my "neighbor" who came to visit me once in a while. It wasn't until I was 10 that I learnt of my real relation to him(That I was his little brother).

My big brother was 13 when I was born(Making me the "younger" brother quite literally). He never knew our dad as well as I did. He lived with his mom in Hawaii(He's my half-brother but we love each other like full siblings). I don't remember much about my past but what I DO believe is that when we were younger, my brother would take care of me and we'd play together when he came to visit us. I look up to my bro and love him a whole lot. Probably even more than he knows. He's the best big bro ever and I'm really glad to be the little brother of such a great guy.

[edit] Manga I've made

I've drawn manga since 7th Grade. The manga(s) I consider my greatest masterpieces are Dragon Kids and Mother Lode. Dragon Kids is a fantasy/shounen manga about two young brothers named Kenta(age eight) and Sasuke(age seven) who take a detour home from school and find a shrine. But inside the shrine is a portal to another world called "Dragon World". However, there's more to this world than they think as they have a connection to it. If you're intrested in anymore of my ideas, check out the "Your Own Manga" topic on Anime News Network. My username is "Shonen Bat". [edit] My attempt at a Dragon Kids article

Dragon Kids (Kodomotachi no Ryuu in Japanese) is a 150 chapter series written by Animeboye. Currently the series is on its sixth chapter. The creator already has two sequels planned(Check out Anime News Network for more information). If anyone could post the kanji for Ryu no Chibi or Dragon Kids, it'd be appreciated.

[edit] Story

The story revolves around two little boys known as Kenta and Sasuke. One day, after stepping in to protect Sasuke from a bully who was beating him up during a school soccer event(Not AT the event though. Behind the school), Kenta and his little brother Sasuke take a detour home and come across a shrine. Inside this shrine is a path to another world known as Dragonworld. Being the curious children they are, the brothers walk through the portal and arrive in this new world. However, there's more to this world than meets the eye as the brothers have a connection to it. One that could change their lives forever.

[edit] Characters

I'm only going to list the brothers. I don't want to give too much away.

Kenta Honda- The eldest of the two siblings. Kenta has brown hair and brown eyes. He is also the one who takes care of Sasuke. He protects his baby brother from bullies and is extremely protective of him. His birthday is December 14, 1993. Kenta is eight during the beginning, ten near the end(I rhymed!), thirteen during the sequel, and seventeen during the second and final sequel.

Sasuke Honda-Kenta's little brother. Sasuke is kindhearted, hyper, cheerful, and loves his brother more than anyone else(Note to Yaoi fangirls, it's BROTHERLY love not gay love so please no KentaxSasuke parings if this DOES get made into a manga). He's also a bit of a crybaby at times. He clings to Kenta a lot and looks up to him which is kind of expected since he's just a little child. He has blonde hair and brown eyes. His birthday is May 5, 1994. Author's note:May 5th is Children's Day in Japan. It shows that no mAtter how old Sasuke gets, he's always going to be a little kid deep down.
