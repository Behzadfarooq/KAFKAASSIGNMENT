Ani (I am) Yochanan (Yochanan).

I am a WikiFan. For quite a while now, and over the course of various
moves and job-changes, I have been a reader and contributor here at
Wikipedia. Recently it has come to my attention that someone from my
new IP (or IP block, however that works) has been engaged in frivolous
posting/editing activities. Pursuant to a clear separation of identity,
I thought it in my (and Wikipedia's) best interests to create a Login
and stick with it, abandoning forever the semblance of anonymity and its
accompanying false (yet oddly comforting) sense of security.
