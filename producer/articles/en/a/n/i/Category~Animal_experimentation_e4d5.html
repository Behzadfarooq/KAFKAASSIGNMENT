Category:Animal experimentation

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Animal experimentation refers to vivisection (dissection of a living animal) or any other experimentation to animals for the purpose of physiological or pathological investigation. For more information, see Animal testing.

The main article for this category is Animal testing.
