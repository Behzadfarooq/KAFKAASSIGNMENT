This category is for sub-categories of images from anime and manga. Because most if not all of the images in these sub-categories are fair use images of DVDs, manga, TV, etc., all of the sub-categories should be tagged with the magic word __NOGALLERY__. This is per fair use criterion No. 9, which states that "Fair use images may be used only in the article namespace. Used outside article space, they are not covered under the fair use doctrine."

Please do not categorize individual images with this category. Instead, when the number of images used to illustrate the articles related to an anime and/or manga series on Wikipedia becomes large enough that a category would be useful to track them, create a new sub-category and add it to here.
