Anita Dark (born April 11, 1975) is a Hungarian pornographic actress. Her earlier work featured her with black hair, hence her name (as opposed to fellow Hungarian porn actress Anita Blonde, who usually had blonde hair). She used to work as a model in Budapest where she was crowned as Miss Budapest in 1994. According to the Internet Movie Database, Dark's real name is Anita Perger.[1]

As seen in the Private video Casting X 7, she made her first encounter with pornographer Pierre Woodman in July, 1994. She had a breast reconstruction surgery and had her areolae reduced in size. In her early films, such as those by Mario Salieri, she can be seen with the original shape of her breasts. From 1994 on, she appeared in several European and American pornographic titles, in heterosexual and lesbian scenes. In 1996 she moved to the United States.

Dark declares her enthusiasm for anal sex at her website, but has only appeared in a limited number of anal and double penetration scenes, mainly at the beginning of her career. At her official website, she asserts that her best experience in her pornographic career is a scene with Sean Michaels in the 1997 film Sodomania # 20.

Recently, Dark has established herself in Orlando, Florida, dyed her hair blonde, and started to work primarily as a glamour model and feature dancer. Her onscreen sexual activities seem to include exclusively lesbian sex. She has freckles covering her face, shoulders, and upper chest area, visible unless they are hidden with makeup.
