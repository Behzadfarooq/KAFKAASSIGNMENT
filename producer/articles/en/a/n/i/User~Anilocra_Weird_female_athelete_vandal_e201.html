The Female cyclist vandal persistently attacked pages related to athletes, athletic competitions, and various other articles usually related in some way. These edits have been primarily of a childish nature, such as replacing text and dates in articles with inappropriate or inaccurate ones, or blanking pages and writing a random message or text. An example of his work can be seen here [1].
