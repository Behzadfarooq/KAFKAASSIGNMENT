Anita Harris (born 3 June 1942, Midsomer Norton, Somerset, England) is a British actress, singer and entertainer.

Best known in Britain as a singer and entertainer, Anita Harris had a number of chart hits in the 1960s. She became known as a variety entertainer, and appeared in a number of the Carry On films, notably Carry on Doctor.

She co-hosted The David Nixon Show in the 1970s.
