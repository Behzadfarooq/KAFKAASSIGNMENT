"Animal Act" is an episode of the second season season of The New Batman Adventures. It aired on September 26, 1998 and was written by Hilary J. Bader and directed by Curt Geda.

[edit] Plot

Spoiler warning: Plot and/or ending details follow.

The arrival of Dick Grayson's old circus in town coincides with bizarre thefts perpetrated by circus animals. Is Nightwing's former family to blame?
