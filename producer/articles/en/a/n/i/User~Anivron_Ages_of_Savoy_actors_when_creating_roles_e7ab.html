This page serves as a reference on the casting decisions in all 14 operas by Gilbert and Sullivan, listing (by opera) each role, the actor or actress who created it, and their age at the time of creation.

The age listed is the age of the actor or actress on their most recent birthday at the time of the premiere. If the exact birthdate is not known, but a year or approximate year is given, the estimated age is given in parentheses.
