Anindo Ghosh is a Mumbai-based photographer specializing in night photography / low-light and location photography.

Anindo's images have been published in Air India's "Namaskar" magazine, British Airways' "Free & Easy" magazine, The Times of India's "Xplore" magazine, the Taj "Mumbai City Guide", and other magazines and publications. Limited Edition Art Prints of his work are owned by photography and art collectors across India.

Recent write-ups and articles about Anindo's work have appeared in The Times of India Mumbai, Time Out Mumbai, Daily News & Analysis and Mumbai Mirror.

Anindo Ghosh has been involved with photography since he was six, starting out with a hand-me-down Kodak Baby Brownie camera owned by his father. Since 2003, he has moved exclusively into digital photography.

In January 2007, Anindo Ghosh held a debut solo exhibition of his works, themed "Through the Night... and Through the Years...", consisting of 2' x 3' gallery prints of digital images taken between 2003 and 2006.

Anindo Ghosh lives in Bandra, Mumbai, India.
