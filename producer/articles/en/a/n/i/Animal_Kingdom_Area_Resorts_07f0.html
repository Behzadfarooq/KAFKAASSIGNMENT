Animal Kingdom Area Resorts

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Disney's Pop Century Resort entrance

The Animal Kingdom Area Resorts are six resorts near Disney's Animal Kingdom in Walt Disney World. The Animal Kingdom Area Resorts consist of Disney's Animal Kingdom Lodge, a deluxe resort, which closest of all to the Animal Kingdom, Disney's Coronado Springs Resort, a moderate resort, Disney's All-Star Movies Resort, Disney's All-Star Music Resort, Disney's All-Star Sports Resort, and Disney's Pop Century Resort, which are all value resorts.
