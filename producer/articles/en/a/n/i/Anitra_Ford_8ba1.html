Anitra Ford

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Anitra Ford (b. 1942-, born Anitra Joy Weinstein) was a "Barker's Beauty" on The Price Is Right from its beginning in 1972 until the last days of 1976. She was replaced by Holly Hallstrom. She left The Price Is Right to go into real estate.
