For over ten years Anita Stansfield has been one of the most popular LDS romantic fiction authors. Her appealing storytelling attracts readers of all ages as she focuses deeply on difficult and emotional challenges of life and the lessons learned. Stansfield draws her readers in as she covers different time periods in her writings. From the eighteenth century all the way to present, Anita creates an in-depth understanding of the characters in her stories and their pasts. One of the trademarks of Anita Stansfield’s books is how each book is specifically written to stand on its own, whether it’s in a series or parts of sets. Reading the books in or out of order won’t confuse the reader. However, in some cases unanswered questions will be revealed in the following book, but normally all the questions are answered by the end of the book. Stansfield’s books will always guarantee a happy comfortable ending no matter how drastic the story may seem!

The publicist for Covenant Communications said, "with sales approaching half a million, Anita Stansfield has clearly found a niche in this market." Along with this statement, Stansfield says that, "I always write for women like me. It's through my interaction with other women that I've found there are some personal and emotional struggles that are typical, I hope my writing makes women feel better about themselves and shows them these real problems can be solved through gospel principles."

Even now Stansfield is working diligently to create a historic project in the LDS market which may be called “In Search of Heaven”. Stansfield has already finished her first book of this series and is working on the next two volumes. As the years continue to go by, and Stansfield's popularity continues to flourish, there is not doubt that her fans will always remember the remarkable characters portrayed, the lives they led, and the incredible lessons learned in Anita Stansfield's books. From heart-warming to heart-stopping, hundereds of readers will agree that Anita Stansfield's books will always be remembered.

Some books written by Stansfield include: A Dance to Remember, By Love and Grace, A Christmas Melody, Dancing in the Light, and First Love and Forever. There are many more that can be found on her web site www.anitastansfield.com
