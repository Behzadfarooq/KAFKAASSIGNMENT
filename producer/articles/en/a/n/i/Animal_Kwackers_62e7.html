The Animal Kwackers: (from l to r) Rory, Twang, Bongo, and Boots

Animal Kwackers was a children's television series produced by Yorkshire Television and broadcast on ITV. The Animal Kwackers were a four piece pop band consisting of Rory, a lion; Twang, a monkey; Bongo, a dog; and Boots, a tiger. The characters were played by actors in costumes. The show was similar in many ways to the successful US series, Banana Splits.

[edit] Cast

  • Rory, on guitar - Bev Doyle
  • Twang, on bass - Step Morley
  • Bongo, on drums - Atalanta Harmsworth
  • Boots, on guitar - John Basset
