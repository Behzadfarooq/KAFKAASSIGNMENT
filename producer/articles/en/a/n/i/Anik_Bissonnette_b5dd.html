Anik Bissonnette

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Anik Bissonnette (born February 9, 1962, Montreal) is a Canadian ballet dancer and principal dancer, since 1990, with Les Grands Ballets Canadiens.

She is married to Louis Robitaille, a ballet dancer whom she has danced with.

In 1995, she was made an Officer of the Order of Canada. In 1996, she was made a Knight of the National Order of Quebec.
