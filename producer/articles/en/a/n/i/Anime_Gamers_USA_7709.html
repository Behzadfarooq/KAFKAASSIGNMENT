Anime Gamers USA, Inc. is a California corporation that produces anime and manga products to the United States. It is a part of parent company BROCCOLI. One of Anime Gamers USA Inc.'s subdivisions is Synch-Point. It has an online retail website business that was formerly located at OmochaBox.com but has since moved to http://www.animegamersusa.com

[edit] Products

Anime Gamers USA Inc. sells both imported from Japan and domestic manga and anime in the form of DVDs. It also sells anime music CDs, figurines, snacks, stationary, apparel, posters, calendars, trading cards and accessories such as cell phone straps and keychains.

Because it is a division of BROCCOLI, many BROCCOLI produce anime characters are used in their advertising, such as characters from Di Gi Charat and Galaxy Angel. Anime Gamers USA Inc. is one of North America's main distributors of BROCCOLI Books, that also has its own section on the site.
