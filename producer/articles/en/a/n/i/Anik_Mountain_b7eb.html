Anik Mountain
Elevation 1,933 metres (6,341 feet)
Location Primorsky Krai, Russia
Range Sikhote-Alin Mountains
Coordinates 47°15′36″N, 137°01′19″E

Anik Mountain (Russian: гора Аник) is the highest peak of Primorsky Krai, Russia. It is located in the north of Primorsky Krai on the border with Khabarovsk Krai. Anik Mountain is a part of the Sikhote-Alin mountain system.
