Anime International Company Inc. (株式会社アニメ・インターナショナルカンパニー, Kabushiki gaisha Anime Intānashonaru Kanpanī?), often abbreviated as AIC, is a Japanese anime studio based in Tokyo and founded on July 15, 1982. Their works include:

  • AD Police
  • Ah! My Goddess series
  • Ai no Kusabi
  • Armitage III OVA series
  • Battle Athletes
  • Battle Programmer Shirase
  • Be Boy Kidnapp'n Idol
  • Blue Gender
  • Bubblegum Crisis series
  • Burn Up series
  • Call Me Tonight
  • Dangaioh
  • De Vadasy
  • Detonator Orgun
  • Dragon Century
  • Dual!
  • El Hazard series
  • Explorer Woman Ray
  • Gall Force
  • Genesis Survivor Gaiarth
  • Godannar (co-produced with Oriental Light and Magic)
  • Great Dangaioh
  • Gun X Sword
  • Hades Project Zeorymer
  • Hongildon
  • Legend of Black Heaven
  • Lovely Idol
  • Megazone 23 III
  • Moldiver
  • Myth 2: Soulblighter
  • Ninja Cadets
  • Nightwalker
  • Now and Then, Here and There
  • Outlanders (animated for Tatsunoko)
  • Parasite Dolls
  • Photon
  • Petite Princess Yucie
  • Riding Bean
  • Ryokunohara Meikyuu
  • Sol Bianca
  • Solty Rei (co-produced with GONZO)
  • Tenchi Muyo! series
  • The Hakkenden
  • To Heart ~Remember My Memories~
  • Tokimeki Memorial ~Only Love~
  • Virgin Fleet
  • Vampire Princess Miyu series

In addition to producing anime for domestic consumption in Japan, AIC has provided animation assistance on other animated series such as Inspector Gadget. AIC has two divisions named "AIC Spirits" and "AIC A.S.T.A."

As of June 2006, AIC has commenced a joint-project with Singapore's NTU to develop the CACANI system, a software that generates inbetweens from keyframes.
