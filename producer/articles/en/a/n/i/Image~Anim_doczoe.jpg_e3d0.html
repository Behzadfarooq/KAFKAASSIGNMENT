[edit] Fair use for Doctor Who and The Invasion (Doctor Who)

This image, Anim doczoe.jpg, is being linked here; though the image is copyright © to the BBC, I (Josiah Rowe (talk • contribs) 01:21, 1 August 2006 (UTC)) feel that it is covered by the U.S. fair use laws because:

  1. it is a low-resolution screenshot from the programme in question;
  2. it is being used for identification and critical commentary on the programme Doctor Who, the status of missing episodes of that programme and the BBC's production of an animated reconstruction of the missing episodes of this serial;
  3. no free alternative exists or can exist;
  4. the use of the image in this article will in no way infringe on the BBC's copyright, or ability to market the programme or ancillary works.
