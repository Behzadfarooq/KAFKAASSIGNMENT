This category is for stub articles on types of animals, or things relating to animals. You can help Wikipedia by expanding them. To add an article to this category, use {{animal-stub}} rather than {{Stub}}, but please use the following categories for different types of animal:

  • {{mammal-stub}} for mammals, including breeds of domestic mammals
    • {{bat-stub}} for bats
    • {{carnivora-stub}} for members of the Order Carnivora
      • {{cat-stub}} for breeds of cats
      • {{dog-stub}} for for breeds of dogs
    • {{even-toed-ungulate-stub}} for members of the Order Artiodactyla
    • {{insectivora-stub}} for members of the Order Insectivora
    • {{horse-stub}} for horses (not horseracing)
    • {{primate-stub}} for primates
    • {{rodent-stub}} for rodents
  • {{bird-stub}} for birds
    • {{passerine-stub}} for passerines
    • {{parrot-stub}} for parrots
    • {{paleo-bird-stub}} for prehistoric birds
    • For ornithologists, use {{ornithologist-stub}}.
  • {{reptile-stub}} for reptiles
    • {{turtle-stub}} for turtles, including tortoises
    • {{lizard-stub}} for lizards
    • {{snake-stub}} for snakes
    • {{dinosaur-stub}} for dinosaurs
  • {{amphibian-stub}} for amphibians
  • {{fish-stub}} for fish
    • {{Shark-stub}} for sharks
  • {{invertebrate-stub}} for those invertebrates not covered by the following:
    • {{arthropod-stub}} for arthropods
      • {{arachnid-stub}} for arachnids
      • {{crustacean-stub}} for crustaceans
      • {{insect-stub}} for insects
        • {{butterfly-stub}} for butterflies
        • {{moth-stub}} for moths
        • {{beetle-stub}} for beetles
        • {{ant-stub}} for ants

      • {{cephalopod-stub}} for cephalopods
