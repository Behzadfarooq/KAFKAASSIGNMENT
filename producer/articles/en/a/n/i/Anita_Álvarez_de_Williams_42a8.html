Anita Álvarez de Williams

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

You have new messages (last change).

Anthropologist, photographer and historian. Born in Calexico, California in 1931.

Her work has been of great importance to understand the past of Baja California. She has published "The Cocopah People" (1974), "Travelers among the Cucapa" (1975), and "Primeros pobladores de la Baja California. Introducción a la antropología de la península" (1975), among several books and academic journals both in Mexico and the United States.
