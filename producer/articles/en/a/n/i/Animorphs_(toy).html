Animorphs (toy)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

You have new messages (last change).

Animorphs toys are animals which transform into characters from the Animorphs book and TV series. They were introduced in 1999 by Hasbro. They were marketed as part of the Transformers series, however, there is no connection between the Transformers storyline and that of the Animorphs. Several toys planned to be part of the Animorphs line, however, were released as part of the last few figures of the Beast Wars under the name Mutant Beast Wars.
