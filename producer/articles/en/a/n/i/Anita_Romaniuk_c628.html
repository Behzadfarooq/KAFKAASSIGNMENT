Anita Romaniuk is a Canadian politician. She was born in Port Alberni, British Columbia. She received a B.Sc., and a M.Sc. at the University of Victoria and a M.B.A. at University of British Columbia.

She is currently an elected member of the Vancouver Parks Board. She is the Parks Board representative on the Vancouver City Planning Commission and the Vancouver Library Board.

Before being elected she was on the Board of Directors of the Douglas Park Community Association for six years. In 1994 she founded the Douglas Park Arts Committee. Also, she has been heavily involved in organizing fundraiser events for the Booth House Shelter for Battered Women.
