Animakitty (or Anima whenever possible) is the pseudonym of an American amateur author from Ohio. His main interests are online roleplaying, books, computer and video games, anime, furries, vore, modding Nerf guns, and propping.


  • His fiction is furry, erotic and general-audience both, the former almost exclusively centered around Vore and its related fetishes such as unbirth.
  • Top life influences include: A Christian upbringing by a married heterosexual couple, Christian schooling from Kindergarten to College Freshman years, Tom Swift, Danny Dunn, 1980's cartoons, and good 80's movies like "Explorers" and "Ghostbusters."
  • Anima is currently employed by the Air_Force_Research_Laboratory.
  • Anima the Fursona is a large non-anthropomorphic black panther.
