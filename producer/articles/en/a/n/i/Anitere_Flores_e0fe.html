Anitere Flores (born September 8, 1976) is a Representative in the House of Representatives of the U.S. state of Florida.

She graduated from Florida International University in 1997 and the University of Florida School of Law in 2001.

Representative Flores was first elected in 2004 as a Republican and represents the 114th district in Miami-Dade County.
