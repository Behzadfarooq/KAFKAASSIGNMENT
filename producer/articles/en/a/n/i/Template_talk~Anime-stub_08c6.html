[edit] Proposition for new template format

I'd like to propose a new centralized format to change the current one used in this template, and was wondering what the other editors think:

 This anime/manga-related article is a stub. You can help Wikipedia and WikiProject Anime and manga by expanding it.

Personally I'd prefer a more centralized, boxed format, so I've designed the above with that idea in mind, similar to the stub templates used in the JA Wikipedia (example). Please feel free to share your opinions. ~ Ganryuu (talk) 11:20, 30 August 2006 (UTC)
