Anis Ayari (Arabic: أنيس العياري) (born February 16, 1982) is a Tunisian football player who currently plays for Lorient. He was a member of the Tunisian 2004 Olympic football team, who exited in the first round, finishing third in group C, behind group and gold medal winners Argentina and runners-up Australia.
