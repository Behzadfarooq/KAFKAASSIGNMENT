Aniline Blue WS

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Water blue
Methyl blue

Aniline Blue WS, also called aniline blue, China blue, or Soluble blue, is a mixture of methyl blue and water blue. It may also be either one of them.

Aniline blue or its constituents are used to stain collagen, as the fibre stain in Masson's trichrome.

It can be used in the Mallory's connective tissue stain and Gomori's one-step trichrome stain. It is used in differential staining.
