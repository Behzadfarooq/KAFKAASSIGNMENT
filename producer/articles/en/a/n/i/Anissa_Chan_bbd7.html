Dr. Anissa Chan Wong Lai-kuen (Traditional Chinese: 陳黃麗娟) has been principal of St. Paul's Co-educational College since 2004, as well as the supervisor of two affiliated primary schools: St. Paul's Co-educational (Kennedy Road) Primary School and St. Paul's Co-educational (Macdonnell Road) Primary School. She has over twenty years experience and is a leader in the educational field. She was formerly the chairman of Hong Kong Subsidized Secondary Schools Council, a member of the Education Commission and the Advisory Committee on Teacher Education and Qualifications of Hong Kong government, and a member of the Hong Kong Examinations and Assessment Authority.

In October 2006, her left eye was injured when she was playing badminton and the retina was detached.
