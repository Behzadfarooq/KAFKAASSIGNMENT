Animal Bag

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Animal Bag was an alternative metal band from Charlotte, North Carolina. The band members were Luke Edwards (vocals and guitar), Rich Paris (guitar), Otis Hughes (bass guitar), and Boo Duckworth (drums and percussion). Boo died on June 5, 2002 from natural causes at age 35.

Their self-titled initial release mixed acoustic and electric rock; the 1994 album Offering was all acoustic. A return to the harder-edged music can be heard on the unreleased 1996 album Image Damage.

[edit] Discography

  • Animal Bag (1992)
  • Offering (1994)
  • Image Damage (recorded 1996, unreleased)
  • Misc. Recordings (recorded 1998, unreleased)
