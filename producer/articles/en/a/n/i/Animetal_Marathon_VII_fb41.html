Animetal Marathon VII: Fight! Metal Hero (アニメタル・マラソンVII ～戦え!メタル・ヒーロー～, Animetaru Marason Sebun ~Tatakae! Metaru Hīrō?) is a 2005 album by the Japanese heavy metal band Animetal.

Lineup:

Eizo Sakamoto: vocals
Syu: guitars
Masaki: bass
Katsuji: drums
