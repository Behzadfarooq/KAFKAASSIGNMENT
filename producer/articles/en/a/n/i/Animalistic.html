Animalistic is the third studio album by American singer Nivea, released in Japan on November 15, 2006 (see 2006 in music) by Formula Recordings. It was released February 20, 2007 and (February 19 in the UK), with more tracks added and some replaced. It was primarily produced by her husband, The Dream, with additional production from Tricky Stewart and Bryan Michael Cox.
