The Anime Network, a subsidiary of A.D. Vision, Inc. (parent company of ADV Films and Newtype USA) is a cable channel in North America dedicated to anime. The network launched in North America in late 2002 and is marketed to multi-system operators (MSOs) as both a stand-alone 24-hour linear network and as a VOD programming service.

[edit] Anime Network Distribution

The Anime network's content is distributed in two formats.

  • as a 24/7 Linear Feed.
  • a VOD service

Following is a selected list of providers that either carry the Linear feed or distribute the VOD content.

    • Time Warner Cable
    • AT&T U-verse
    • Cablevision
    • Cox
    • Comcast
    • Rogers Communications
    • Shaw Digital TV
    • Verizon FiOS
