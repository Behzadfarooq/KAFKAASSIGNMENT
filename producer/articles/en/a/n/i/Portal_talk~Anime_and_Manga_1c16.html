[edit] AnimeNfo.com article in danger.

I am having a dispute with some people who consider my article on animenfo.com as spam or advertising (and they are being quite condescendent about it). That website is reffered to in many anime articles, as a search for animenfo confirms. It is a useful database and review website for the anime scene, and much like imdb.com deserves an article on wikipedia. Similar websites for the equally "unencyclopedic" topic of videogames have large articles, such as gamespot.com, gamespy, etc. If you would like to help, please visit the animenfo.com article and post on the Discussion to help convince people about the usefulness of this article. Beltz 05:31, 10 April 2006 (UTC)

Just found the A&M portal page today, but quite glad that the AFD failed. I rather think that animenfo.com seems to be one of the more useful anime sites out there. (My main one's animeyume, but we won't go there...) ~Kylu (u|t) 02:29, 12 June 2006 (UTC)
While it is a very useful databae for anime information, it does not warrant its own Wiki article. It is best served as an External Link from various anime related articles. KyuuA4 16:45, 5 October 2006 (UTC)

[edit] Fair use images

I removed the images in the selected article, introduction, selected biography section, as they are violations of Wikipedia's fair use policy. Please remember that fair use images can only be used in the article main spaces (the main page though is an exception)--☆TBC☆ (aka Tree Biting Conspiracy) 12:18, 10 June 2006 (UTC)

I'd always considered Portals to be "encyclopedia content", just not articles. If this concept is correct, then shouldn't WP:FAIR be amended to allow for fair-use pictures which are used to substantially add to the encyclopedic content in portals? While not technically "articles" they're reader-facing pages, I think. Comment encouraged. :) ~Kylu (u|t) 00:33, 29 June 2006 (UTC)
If you think that the policy should be ammended and that portals should be allowed the usage of fair use images, feel free to propose it on WP:FAIR. Until then, fair use images are not allowed on portals (thus I'm removing that image of Ed currently in the biography section).--TBCTaLk?!? 08:24, 8 September 2006 (UTC)

An amendment is now being discussed here: Wikipedia:Fair use/Amendment/Fair use images in portals --GunnarRene 14:39, 10 December 2006 (UTC)
