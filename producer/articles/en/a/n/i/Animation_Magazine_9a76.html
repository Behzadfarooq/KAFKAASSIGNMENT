Animation Magazine is a monthly publication covering the animation industry, including aspects of film and television animation, visual effects and video games.

Founded in 1986, the print magazine is published monthly and features articles on current animated movies, television series, short films, interactive titles and software releases from both major studios and independent producers. Editorial covers all forms of animation, including 2D, 3D and stop-motion, and profiles the artists behind the productions. Digital versions of the print editions were introduced In 2006.

A history of talented art directors have also contributed to Animation Magazine's popularity. They include among others Adam Arnold, Rhonda Zayas and Gregory Van Zuyen.

[edit] Website

Daily animation news is updated every weekday at [1]. The site is also home to World Animation Celebration Online (WAC-O) [2], an online film festival featuring animated shorts from around the world. Films are free to view and visitors can vote for their favorites to decide quarterly award winners.
