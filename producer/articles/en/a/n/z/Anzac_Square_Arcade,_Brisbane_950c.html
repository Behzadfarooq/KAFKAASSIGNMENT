Anzac Square Arcade
( corner Edward and Adelaide Streets )

Anzac Square Arcade, in Brisbane, Queensland, Australia, is on the corner of Edward Street and Adelaide Street, adjacent to Anzac Square (from which the Arcade derives its name). The building is diagonally opposite to Queens Plaza

Anzac Square Arcade is a shopping centre, with speciality shops, a newsagent, a book store, a dental surgery, and a food court.

Anzac Square Arcade also avails easy access to Central Station, and has CityTrain timetables displayed within Anzac Square Arcade.
