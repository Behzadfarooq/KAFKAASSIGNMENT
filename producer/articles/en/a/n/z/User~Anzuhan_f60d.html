I've started contributing to the English Wikipedia in May 2005.

My interests include guitar playing, heavy metal music (thrash & speed mostly), sports, programming (and computers generally, IRC etc.), powerlifting, bodybuilding, miniature painting and Warhammer Fantasy Battle, literature, history, sci-fi as well as fantasy literature, physics and mathematics.
