Map of the Anzhu Islands, part of the New Siberian Islands archipelago.

Anzhu Islands (Острова Анжу in Russian) are the biggest islands in a group of the New Siberian Islands. They include the Kotelny Island, Faddeyevsky Island, Novaya Sibir, and Belkovsky Island. Area: approx. 29,000 km². The Anzhu Islands are named after a Russian explorer Pyotr Anzhu.
