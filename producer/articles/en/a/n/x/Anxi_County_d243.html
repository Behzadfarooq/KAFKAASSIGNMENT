Anxi County (Chinese: 安溪县; pinyin: Ānxī Xiàn), Fujian Province, China, is a county level city in the Quanzhou prefecture-level city. It is well-known for its ancient and famous tea, the Tieguanyin Oolong. Tie Guan Yin in English is Iron Goddess of Mercy.

[edit] Attractions

Qingshui Yan (Chinese: 清水岩; pinyin: Qingshui Yan) is a mountain hosting a very large Buddhist temple.


China Tea Capital (Chinese: 中国茶都 pinyin: Zhongguo Chadu) is a large center dedicated to showcasing Anxi County's famous oolong tea.
