The Anchorage School District (ASD) manages all public schools within the Municipality of Anchorage in the U.S. state of Alaska.

It is the 81st largest school district in the United States, serving approximately 50,000 students at 93 schools. As one of the United States' largest districts, it is often subject to greater amounts of standardized testing.

The current superintendent is Carol Comeau.

[edit] Demographics

An estimated 42% of Anchorage School District students are members of ethnic minority groups.

  • 58.6% White
  • 12.8% Alaska Native/American Indian
  • 10.7% Asian/Pacific Islander
  • 8.4% African-American
  • 6.1% Hispanic
  • 3.4% Other

There are over 120 languages spoken by Anchorage School District students.
