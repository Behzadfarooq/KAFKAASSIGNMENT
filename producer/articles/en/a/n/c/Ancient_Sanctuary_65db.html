Ancient Sanctuary

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

You have new messages (last change).

Ancient Sanctuary (AST) is the eleventh English booster to be released in the Yu-Gi-Oh! Trading Card Game and the last one to follow the original English booster format (two Secret Rares, ten Ultra Rares, ten Super Rares). It is the last pack to contain Secret Rare cards until Strike of Neos. It is comprised of the Japanese sets Sanctuary of the Sky and Pharaonic Heritage. It focuses mainly on Fairy and Machine-type Monsters. Some of the Fairies include the Agent Monsters, who have powerful effects when the card "Sanctuary in the Sky" is in play. Highly sought after cards include Blowback Dragon, Enemy Controller and Zaborg the Thunder Monarch. It contains two Secret Rares, ten Ultra Rares, ten Super Rares, seventeen Rares, and seventy-three Commons.
