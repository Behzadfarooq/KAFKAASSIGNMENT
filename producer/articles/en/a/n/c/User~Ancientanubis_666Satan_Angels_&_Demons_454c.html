Throughout the entire 666 Satan universe, there are angels and demons (some of which are sealed within humans) with amazing powers, all of which are being searched for by the Stea Republic (angels) and the Zenom Organization (demons) in order to awaken what is assumed to be a weapon within a Kabbalah===and a reverse Kabbalah that are found on the North and South Pole respectively.

In the case of the Stea Government, almost all of the angels have been found and inserted into the kabbalah, with two uncaptured angels being protagonists in the main storyline.
