[edit] Ancient Mamodo

The Ancient Mamodo are mamodo who fought for the position of King 1000 years before the present time in the series. The fight was relatively the same as the current competition for King, except for the inclusion of one deadly mamodo. This mamodo, Goren of the Stone, had the ability to cast any mamodo into a stone block, effectively winning the fight and preventing said mamodo from returning to the mamodo world. This scenario played out all over the world, with Goren sealing mamodo after mamodo into stone. A thousand years after this event, the stones were gathered from all parts of the world in a grand scheme perpertrated by the mamodo Zofis. Zofis used his psychic abilities as well as the moonlight stone to simultaneously free the mamodo and force them to fight.

The bookkeepers are descendants of the original bookkeepers, brainwashed and deprived of all emotions but anger by Zofis.

In episode 77, a large majority of the Ancient Mamodo were sent back to the Mamodo world by Brago with one attack, Baberuga Gravidon.

[edit] The King

The Mamodo King (sillhouetted, panel 5)

The winner of the last mamodo battle is an immense thunder elementalist with two children, Zatch and Zeno. He is the instigator of the current mamodo battle to determine his heir. Although he originally appeared cruel, he actually chose to separate his two sons due to Zatch inheriting his Baou power, which turned out for the better due to his knowledge of Zeno's ease of gaining a corruptible, detestable heart that would end up using Baou out of his own anger. Since Zatch was seen as a kinder child, he separated the two, hoping to protect him from becoming corrupted from the knowledge of his ultimate power and what he can do with it, even if this separation lead to the miserable upbringing for both twins, Zatch for the horrible treatment of his foster parents, and Zeno's resentment and training due to not having the Baou power.

Although he is a mamodo from a thousand years ago, he is not included in the previous list because he is the current king, not one of Goren's stone victims.
