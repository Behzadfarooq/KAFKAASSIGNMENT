Anchorvale is a neighbourhood of Sengkang New Town, and is located between Punggol River and Compassvale. The house numbers of the public apartment blocks in Anchorvale begin with the number '3' (3xx). These housing estates were fully completed by the Housing and Development Board (HDB) in 2001.

[edit] Public transport

The Anchorvale neighbourhood is linked to Sengkang Bus Interchange at the town centre via the SBS Transit feeder bus service 372, and to the Sengkang MRT Station via the LRT stations along the Sengkang LRT Line. There are also trunk services to Yishun, Changi, Pasir Ris, Woodlands, Hougang and other parts of Singapore serving the area.
