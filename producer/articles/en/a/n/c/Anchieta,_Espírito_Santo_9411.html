Anchieta, Espírito Santo

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Coordinates: 20°43′19″S, 40°46′55″W Anchieta is a municipality in the Brazilian state of Espírito Santo. Its population was 21,834 in 2005 and its area is 404.88 km²[1]. Its average elevation is 2m above sea level. Formerly known as Reritiba, the city was renamed after the Jesuit missionary José de Anchieta.
