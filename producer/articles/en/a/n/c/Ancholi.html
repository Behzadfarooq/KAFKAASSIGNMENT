Ancholi is one of the neighborhoods of Gulberg Town in Karachi, Sindh, Pakistan.

There are several ethnic groups in Gulberg Town including Urdu speakers, Punjabis, Sindhis, Kashmiris, Seraikis, Pakhtuns, Balochs, Memons, Bohras, Ismailis. Over 99% of the population is Muslim. The population of Gulberg Town is estimated to be nearly one million.
