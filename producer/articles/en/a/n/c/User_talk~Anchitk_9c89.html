[edit] Wikitravel

I find that you are linking pages with wikitravel. Do you really think that they are really required? --Bhadani 02:58, 29 October 2006 (UTC)

Wikipedia is more than about travel, if you want to link anyway, please do not put your link at the top of the external links, and too many links to a particular site in this style may be treated as promotional. --Bhadani 03:00, 29 October 2006 (UTC)
You linked a page: http://wikitravel.org/en/Indore%2C_India to this page - http://en.wikipedia.org/w/index.php?title=Indore%2C_India&diff=84350206&oldid=84349752 and see that where it leads - to a blank page on wikitravel. Are you using the privilege to edit to make fun and ensuring that times is wasted by our users? I assume good faith about your edits, but before linking please think and understand better to which you are linking. --Bhadani 03:09, 29 October 2006 (UTC)
Well done! I thank you for complete reply. I understand your point of view and the reasons. You do as you wish to add value to wikipedia. I think gradually you will diversify to more things. Everyone, including me, was new once upon a time! Please be around. I am predicting: you shall emerge as an asset to Wikipedia! All the best! Only one suggestion: you may avoid linking blank pages - when the page is developed, you may decide. Thanks and regards. --Bhadani 03:49, 29 October 2006 (UTC)
Oh, please write few general words about you on your user page - you need not disclose your identity. Thanks. --Bhadani 03:51, 29 October 2006 (UTC)
