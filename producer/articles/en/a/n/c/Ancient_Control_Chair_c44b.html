Ancient Control Chair

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Atlantis' Ancient Control Chair used by Sheppard.

In the fictional television series Stargate SG-1 and Stargate Atlantis, the Ancient Control Chair is an Ancient control device which functions as a mental interface for those with the Ancient gene. [1] The chair has been encountered on various Ancient outposts.

[edit] Function

Drones being launched from Atlantis.

Control Chairs are mostly responsible for defense. All the control chairs thusfar encountered were used for launching Ancient Drone Weapons at enemy targets. The number of drones available can only be checked when the chair is powered up. [2] However, Aurora class battleships do not use control chairs to fire drones. [3]

Besides being used as a defense platform, the chair can also interface with holographic systems, allowing the user to project maps into the air [4], display information gathered by the sensors [5], cross-referencing information in the user's memory with existing information in the database, or even compensating maps for continental drift. [4] They can also be used to control Puddle Jumpers remotely. This however is not a designed function and controlling the drones of the Jumper is used to help fly the ship. [2] The chair can also be used to manage and control other Ancient systems such as power management with ZPMs. [6] It was also discovered that the chair in Atlantis allows a person to activate the stardrive of the city and thus fly it away from a location and into hyperspace. Once in hyperspace, the individual may get out of the chair without the city exiting hyperspace. [7]

The Chair is normally powered by a Zero Point Module, although the members of the Atlantis Expedition have been successful in powering the device with a Mark II Naqahdah reactor in "controlled overload", which provides just enough energy to activate the chair although the reactor's power can only activate the chair about three times before permanent power depletion. [2]

[edit] Mental Interface

The chair is controlled directly by the user's thoughts. The device is hard to operate, and requires a considerable amount of concentration for most users. However, some people, like John Sheppard, can operate the unit intuitively, even without much practice. [1]

Not only is concentration instrumental in controlling the device, but the chair also requires (as does a lot of Ancient technology) the user to have the ATA gene, originally only found in Ancients. However, due to crossbreeding between Ancients who fled back to Earth and humans, several members of the Atlantis Expedition have the gene. [1] Also, using gene therapy, the gene can be transferred to "ordinary" humans, [8] but the gene does not take with all subjects.

[edit] Encountered Chairs

In the Milky Way, the chairs have been encountered on Proclarush Taonas and inside the Ancient outpost in Antarctica on Earth. [4] In the Pegasus Galaxy, chairs are located in the tower of Atlantis [2] and it's sister cities. [5]

More recent Ancient outposts, such as the research base on the Dorandan homeworld, did not contain a control chair, indicating that by the Ancient retreat to Earth, the chair might have been replaced by more efficient control systems. [9]

[edit] Ori Chair

An Ori Control Chair.

The Ori, being essentially the same race as the Ancients, also use control chairs to operate their battlecruisers. However, instead of requiring an ordinary human with the ATA Gene, this chair has to be operated by a Prior, and is probably keyed to their unique brain physiology. It has been demonstrated however that both the Orici Adria [10], a human-form Ori, and ordinary Priors can also control the vessel without actually sitting down in the chair. [11]

Also, this type of chair is smaller and has no manual controls. The only encountered chair thus far was shaped into the symbol of Origin, the Ori religion, although this is probably only for cosmetic reasons. [10]
