[edit] About me

Hi, I'm Sien. A little about me... I have been a part of Wikipedia since May of 2006, when I decided to create a page for Raine Sage. However, I've been thinking about Wikipedia for a while, especially after rambling on to friends about how mind-blowingly awesome it is (it knows just about as much about Futurama as I do!). I wouldn't exactly consider myself a raving intellectual, but I try and help edit pages with obscure information as much as possible.

By the way, sorry if I accidently mess something up while I'm editing/creating something. Forgive me--I'm a little slow sometimes.
