Ancient may refer to:

  • Ancient history — the span of recorded history starting roughly 5,000–5,500 years ago.
  • Ancient (band) — the progressive Black Metal musical group.
  • Ancient (company) — the Japanese software developer (see also: Yuzo Koshiro).
  • Ancient — used to refer to a standard-bearer or ensign in medieval times, appearing in texts such as Shakespeare's Othello.

Ancients may refer to:

  • Ancients (art group) — a group of English artists in the 1820s and 30s who were influenced by William Blake and served as a model for the Pre-Raphaelite Brotherhood.
  • Ancients — a name often used in science fiction, fantasy, and related genres for ancient races who are usually mysterious, long-gone and extremely advanced, only leaving behind scattered artifacts.
    • Ancient (Stargate) — a name given to the Alterans, the race who built, among other things, the Stargates in the Stargate-universe.
    • Ancient (Traveller) — the mysterious race that once dominated the galaxy in the Traveller role-playing game.
    • Ancients — a name sometimes given to the Cetra from Final Fantasy VII
    • Ancients (Eternal Darkness) — a god-like race from Eternal Darkness: Sanity's Requiem
    • Ancients (Legacy of Kain) — a race from the Legacy of Kain games.
    • Ancients (Farscape) — the race responsible with implanting John Crichton with Wormhole knowledge in the Farscape TV series.
    • Ancients — an advanced species in the Freespace space simulation computer game series.
    • The Five Ancients, a group of five Trollz who helped banished the evil Simon away 3000 years ago and now mentor a new group of trollz from the animated series Trollz.
