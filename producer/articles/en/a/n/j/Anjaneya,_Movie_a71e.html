Anjaneya (2003) is a Tamil film directed by Maharajan. The film stars Ajith, Meera Jasmine, Raghuvaran, Ramesh Khanna, and Sarala. The film's score and soundtrack are composed by Mani Sharma.

[edit] Plot

Spoiler warning: Plot and/or ending details follow.

DCP Paramaguru (Ajith) a nail police officer who fights with the scum of the society. He later masquerades as a thief to infiltrate into the underworld. The bad guys are surprised to find that Paramguru is the DCP, out to get them. So they all gang up against him. In this process a thrilling encounter takes place between the good and the evil. Paramaguru is helped in his fight against injustice by Divya (Meera Jasmine) who falls in love with him.

[edit] Box Office

  • Initially scheduled for release in late 2002, this film was deferred multiple times until its release in early 2003. It opened in 210 screens worldwide. Pitted against stiff competition and crippled by poor marketing, it still grossed grossed Rs.33 million in the first week of release. [1]
  • The film's total gross is estimated at Rs.100 million
  • The film's Telugu rights were sold to Dr.Rajasekar for astonishing Rs.8.8 million
