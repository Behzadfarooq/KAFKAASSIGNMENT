Anjelika Krylova (born July 4, 1973 in Moscow) is a Russian figure skater. With partner Oleg Ovsyannikov, she captured a silver medal at the 1998 Winter Olympics and won the World title in 1998 and 1999.

Prior to partnering with Ovsyannikov in 1994, she skated with Vladimir Fedorov.
