Anja Rubik (born June 11, 1985) is a Polish model, currently living in New York City. Rubik lived in Greece, Canada, and South Africa as a young girl. She was introduced to modeling at a young age. She was in a British high school in Paris at the time. She wanted to finish her schooling before officially becoming a model, therefore she only worked on school holidays. Shortly after graduating she began her career, and was featured on the cover of various magazines, including Vogue Italy, Numero, Flair, and more. She has modeled for Givenchy, Chloe, Christian Dior, Hermès, Chanel, Balenciaga, Louis Vuitton, and many other top designers. She has also done haute couture for Valentino, Chanel, and Elie Saab. Besides modeling she enjoys art, reading, cinema, scuba diving, and traveling. [1]
