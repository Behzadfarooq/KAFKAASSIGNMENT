Anjula Mutanda

Anjula Mutanda is a British psychologist, life coach and therapist. She trained at Durham University and has since appeared on many television programmes giving psychological analysis, such as Big Brother, GMTV, Kilroy, Have I Been Here Before? and RI:SE. She is also the current psychologist on This Morning and the current agony aunt in Mizz Magazine. Anjula is currently featuring in CiTV's Feel the Fear.
