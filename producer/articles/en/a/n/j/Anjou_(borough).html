This article discusses the government of the Borough of Anjou. For information on the borough itself, see Anjou, Quebec.

Anjou is a borough (arrondissement) of the city of Montreal, Quebec, Canada. It consists of the former city of Anjou.

The current borough mayor is Luis Miranda.

The borough is divided into three districts:

  • Anjou-Centre
  • Anjou-Est
  • Anjou-Ouest

The current city councillors are Luis Miranda and Carol Beaupré; the additional borough councillor is Andrée Henault.
