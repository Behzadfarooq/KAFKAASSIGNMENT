Anjō (安城市; -shi) is a city located in Aichi, Japan.

As of 2003, the city has an estimated population of 164,043 and the density of 1,907.25 persons per km². The total area is 86.01 km².

The city was founded on May 5, 1952.

The city is known as the 'Denmark' of Japan, and produces its own microbrew known as den-beer.
