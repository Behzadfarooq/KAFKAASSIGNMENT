Anja Juliette Laval (born June 6, 1981) is a former German porn actress.

She originally came from eastern Germany, but in 1997 relocated to North Rhine-Westphalia with her boyfriend. After some having some experinces with swinging, she entered the porn industry in 2001, signing with the German porn company Magmafilm. At first, she did exclusively girl-girl scenes, plus boy-girl scenes with her now ex-boyfriend, however, she soon began doing heterosexual scenes with other actors as well.

In 2003, she was honored with at the European X Awards with an award for Best German Adult Actress. That same year, she announced that she was leaving the pornography and completely withdrew from the industry.

As of 2005, she was living in Krefeld.
