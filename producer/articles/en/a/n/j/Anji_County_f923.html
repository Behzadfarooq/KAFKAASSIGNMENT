Anji County (Chinese: 安吉县; pinyin: Ānjí Xiàn) is a county in the prefecture-level city of Huzhou in Zhejiang, People's Republic of China, with a population of 450,000. Famous for producing bamboo, it is sometimes called the "Town of Bamboo". It currently manufactures nearly one-third of the office chairs produced in the People's Republic of China.
