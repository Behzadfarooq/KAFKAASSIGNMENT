Anloo (53°3′N 6°42′E) is a town in the Dutch province of Drenthe. It is a part of the municipality of Aa en Hunze, and lies about 10 km east of Assen.

In 2001, the town of Anloo had 225 inhabitants. The built-up area of the town was 0.12 km², and contained 98 residences.[1] The statistical area "Anloo", which also can include the surrounding countryside, has a population of around 380.[2]

Until 1998, Anloo was a separate municipality.
