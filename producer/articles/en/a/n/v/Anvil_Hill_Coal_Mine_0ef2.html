Anvil Hill Coal Mine

Main article: Coal Mining in Australia

The Anvil Hill Coal Mine is proposed for the upper Hunter Valley in NSW. It will impact the Hunter River. Burning the coal will cause Climate change and Global warming. Climate change will have severe negative effects on Australia
