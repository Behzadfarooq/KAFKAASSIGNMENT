Any Dream Will Do is a 2007 talent show-themed television series produced by the BBC in the United Kingdom. The series was commissioned after the success of similar BBC series, How Do You Solve A Problem Like Maria?. It will be searching for a new, unknown male star to play Joseph in the Andrew Lloyd Webber musical Joseph and the Amazing Technicolor Dreamcoat.

The audition process is currently under way and is open to any male who can play a character on stage with an age of 17-30.

Lord Lloyd-Webber, Zoe Tyler, John Barrowman, Denise Van Outen and Bill Kenwright are to be the show's judges. It will be hosted by Graham Norton.
