Any Day Now (aka Vandaag of morgen) is a 1976 science fiction film directed by Roeland Kerbosch and starring Ansje van Brandenberg. The film features a dystopian near-future world which has been split into three. The Third World are the owners of all of the worlds resources and are now able to hold the United States and Europe to ransom.
