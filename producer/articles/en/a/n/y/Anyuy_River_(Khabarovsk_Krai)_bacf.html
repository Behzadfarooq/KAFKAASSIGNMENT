Anyuy River (Khabarovsk Krai)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Anyuy River, also Onyuy River and Dondon River (Анюй, Онюй, Дондон in Russian) is a river in the Khabarovsk Krai in Russia. It is a right tributary of the Amur River. It originates in the Sikhote-Alin mountain range. The length of the river is 393 km. The area of its basin is 12,700 sq km. The Manoma River is a main tributary of the Anuyu River.
