Anybody You is a song written and performed by Jewel that she sang on Nashville Star and released exclusively on iTunes on March 6, 2007. This is also her first Country single from her upcoming Country Album.[1]

More than two weeks after it's release it has yet to debut in iTunes 100 country songs and the Billboard Hot 100.
