Anytime is a 1997 album by Brian McKnight. This was the last record McKnight recorded with Mercury Records before moving to Motown.

[edit] Track listing

  1. "Anytime"
  2. "Could"
  3. "You Should Be Mine (Don't Waste Your Time)" featuring Ma$e
  4. "Show Me The Way Back To Your Heart"
  5. "Every Time We Say Goodbye"
  6. "You Got The Bomb"
  7. "Hold Me"
  8. "The Only One For Me"
  9. "Til I Get Over You"
  10. "I Belong To You"
  11. "Jam Knock"
  12. "When The Chariot Comes"
