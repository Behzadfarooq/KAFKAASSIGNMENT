"Anything But Down" is a 1999 single by American rock star Sheryl Crow. Released as the third single from her 1998 album The Globe Sessions, it fared better than its predecessor "There Goes the Neighborhood". "Down" peaked at #49 on the Billboard Hot 100.

[edit] Video

The video for this single was directed by Floria Sigismondi, and was later released in the DVD The Very Best of Sheryl Crow.
