Anyone is a band from Southern California that emerged from the short lived Sylvia project. They have described their music using the term maximum acid, as a subgenre of psychedelic rock. Anyone is largely the brainchild of Riz Story, the band's lead singer and guitarist, and also the only member of the band that has remained since its inception.

Their influences include Jane's Addiction and Tool.

Their official website claims that their second, long-awaited album, Sip the Pleasure of Days, will be released in April 2007, and that the band have signed to a record label, Delinquent Recordz.

[edit] Current Members

  • Riz Story: lead vocals, guitars
  • Miki Black: guitars, keyboards
  • Miles Martin: bass
  • Lincoln Nesto: drums

[edit] Former members

2001-2004 period:

  • Static (1998-2003) Bassist on Rats Live on no Evil Star, Live Acid, and Anyone.
  • Dave Murray (Nipples) (1996-2002) Drummer on Rats Live on no Evil Star and Live Acid.
  • Johnny Ransom (2002-2004) Drummer on Anyone.

2004-2006 period:

  • Boano (2004-2006) drums
  • Miki Black (2004-2006) guitars and keyboards

[edit] Discography

  • A Little Sip [EP, taster for the upcoming album] (2006)
