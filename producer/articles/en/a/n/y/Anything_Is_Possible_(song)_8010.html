"Anything Is Possible" is the first single from Debbie Gibson's third album of the same title. While the original album version of the song was used for the single release in countries such as the U.S., Australia and Japan, in the U.K. and Europe an edited version of a remix by Harding and Curnow of PWL Records was used.
