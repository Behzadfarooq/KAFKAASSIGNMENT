Anything Can Happen in the Next Half Hour is the third demo EP by Enter Shikari. It was released in 2004, then re-mastered in 2005, and sold only at shows/via website.

[edit] Track listing

  1. "Jonny Sniper" – 3:33
  2. "Anything Can Happen in the Next Half Hour" – 4:31

[edit] Band line-up

  • Roughton "Rou" Reynolds (vocals, electronics)
  • Liam "Rory" Clewlow (guitar)
  • Chris Batten (bass, vocals)
  • Rob Rolfe (Drums)
