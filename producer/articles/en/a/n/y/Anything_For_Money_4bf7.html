Anything For Money (1984-1985) was a television game show hosted by Fred Travalena and announced by Johnny Gilbert. Pranks were done by Christopher Callen and Ralph Harris. Contestants watched film clips in which the prankster tried to get random people on the street to go along with their goofy hi-jinks by offering them increasing amounts of money. The host introduced the clips and then once the premise of the prank was known the contestants guessed whether or not they would go for it, and if so, for how much dough.
