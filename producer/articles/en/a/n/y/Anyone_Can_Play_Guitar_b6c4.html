"Anyone Can Play Guitar" was a single taken from Radiohead's first album Pablo Honey, released just in advance of the album. It was the band's second single, and their first to receive a wide hearing ("Creep" had been issued in limited quantities some months earlier, had failed to chart, but would soon become a hit and ultimately be rereleased later in 1993). "Anyone Can Play Guitar" made relatively little impression on the charts, but it remained a staple of the band's live sets throughout the early-mid nineties.

Thom Yorke got the idea for the famous line "I wanna be Jim Morrison" after seeing The Doors movie and is meant to convey Yorke's dissatisfaction with the mythical status Morrison is given. This was made clear in the band's appearance at the MTV Beach House, where they played "Anyone Can Play Guitar", and Thom Yorke sang "Maybe if I grow my hair I can become Jim Morrison," before shouting "Fat! Ugly! Dead!". However, the band also described this track as genuinely hopeful and optimistic.[citation needed]
