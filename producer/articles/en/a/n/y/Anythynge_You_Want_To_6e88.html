Anythynge You Want To is a 2001 comedy album by The Firesign Theatre. It takes the form of a play, under the conceit of being a lost work of Shakespeare, using language, plot structure, and characters which parody Shakespeare's original works. It was originally recorded in 1980 as a program for National Public Radio's Earplay, and was previously released in edited form in 1982 on LP as Shakespeare's Lost Comedie.
