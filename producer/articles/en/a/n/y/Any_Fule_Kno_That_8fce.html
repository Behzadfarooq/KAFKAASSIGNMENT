Any Fule Kno That is the first song on the 1998 album Abandon by Deep Purple. Vocalist Ian Gillan takes a spoken word approach on the song, comparable to rapping. It also one of the rare occasions in which his lyrics contain an expletive, in this case the word 'shit'.
