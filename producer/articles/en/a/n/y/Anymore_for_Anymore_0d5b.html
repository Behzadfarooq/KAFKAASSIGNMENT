Anymore for Anymore is the debut solo album by Ronnie Lane, one of the founding members of Small Faces and Faces. The recording sessions started in 1973 at his hundred acre farm in Wales with his band "Slim Chance". The album has more of a rootsy, folk-rock sound than his early recordings with Faces.
