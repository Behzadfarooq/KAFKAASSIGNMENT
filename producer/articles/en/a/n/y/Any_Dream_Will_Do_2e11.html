Any Dream Will Do is a popular song written by Andrew Lloyd Webber and Tim Rice for the 1968 musical "Joseph and the Amazing Technicolour Dreamcoat."

It is one of the opening songs in the west-end version of the show and is sung by the character of Joseph. Numerous acts have sung this song both on stage and on record including Donny Osmond, Andy Gibb and Darren Day. The most successful version of the song is the version from the 1991 revival by Jason Donovan which reached number one on the UK singles chart for two weeks in June 1991.
