[edit] Song information and Music video

Janet in "Any Time, Any Place".

The song was based around a theme of sexual liberation, similar to the music of Barry White and Marvin Gaye. Jackson sings about she and her love having public displays of affection and sexual intercourse. The song held the number-one position on the Billboard Hot R&B/Hip-Hop Singles and Tracks for ten weeks (a record at the time) and became Jackson's biggest hit on the chart; it peaked at number-two on the Billboard Hot 100. The single was a moderate success in Europe and Australia. The single contains two b-sides, the janet. track "Throb" and the unreleased (at the time) "And On And On." "And On And On" received airplay itself and charted on Billboard Hot 100 Airplay. "And On And On" was a song about summer fun, similar to "Whoops Now".

The video for "Any Time, Any Place" was directed by Keir McFarlane. Jackson plays sex games with the man across the hall, and gets in touch with her sexy side. The video acted as a safe sex campaign and has alternate version of the video with the R.Kelly Remix with sexier footage. At the end of the video, the screen fades to black, and a message fades in:

"any time, any place ......be responsible"
