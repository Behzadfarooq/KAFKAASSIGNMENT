Anwil Wloclawek is a Polish basketball team, based in Włocławek, playing in Era Basket Liga. Their home arena is Hala Mistrzow.

[edit] 2003/2004 season

Anwil has won 4th place in EBL, being defeated in 3rd place playoffs by Polonia Warbud Warszawa.

[edit] 2004/2005 season

The team will play in EBL next season.

[edit] Best achievements

Anwil Wloclawek was PLK (previous name of EBL) champion in season 2002/2003.
