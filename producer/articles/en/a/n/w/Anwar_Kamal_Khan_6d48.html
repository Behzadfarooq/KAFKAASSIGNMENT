Anwar Kamal Khan Mina Khel is the current Parliamentary Leader of Pakistan Muslim League (Nawaz) in the NWFP's Provincial Assembly. He is the elder son of Khan Habibullah Khan and older brother of a senior Pakistani Civil Servant Akhter Munir Khan Marwat[citation needed]. Earlier he has also remained a Senator in addition to being a Senior Provincial Minister.[1][2]

Anwar Kamal Khan

He has been leader of Qabaili Lashkar (group of tribesmen), many times and is the only leader who commanded them when they have consisted of 15,000 - 20,000 men[citation needed].

He also was leader of a Lashkar group of tribesmen that succeeded in gaining the release of Ghazni Khel teachers held captive by Arsalla Khan. This resulted in popularising him even in Ghazni Khel whose leaders failed to gain the release of captives from their own clan.[citation needed]
