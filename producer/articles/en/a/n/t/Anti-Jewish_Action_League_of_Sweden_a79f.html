Anti-Jewish Action League of Sweden (in Swedish: Sveriges Antijudiska Kampförbund) was an antisemitic political organization in Sweden. The group was founded in 1941 by veteran antisemite Einar Åberg. Åbergs propagandistic activities eventually provoked the adoption of a legislation banning propaganda that foments ethnic hatred (Lagen om hets mot folkgrupp).

The organization was later disbanded, but Åberg continued his political work.
