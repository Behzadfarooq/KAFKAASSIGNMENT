Antar (also Anthar) Yahia (Arabic: عنتر يحيى‎) (born March 21, 1982 in Mulhouse) is a French-born Algerian football player who plays for german club VfL Bochum.

He was a member of the Algerian 2004 African Nations Cup team, who finished second in their group in the first round of competition before being defeated by Morocco in the quarter-finals.

On January 30, 2007 he played his first Bundesliga match for VfL Bochum against Bayern Munchen [1]
