Antártica (comuna)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Antártica is one of two communes ("comunas") of the Province of Chilean Antarctica, XII Region of Magallanes and Chilean Antarctica, which form the Chilean Antarctic Territory.

Although the Chilean constitution permits communes to be grouped for administation with others, it currently is the only one which is administered by a different municipality, being grouped with the comuna of Cape Horn.

The comuna of Antarctica was created on 11 July 1961, and was dependent on the Province of Magallanes until 1975, when the Province of Antarctica was created. It was dependent administratively on Puerto Williams, the capital of the province.

The official population figure for the area, as of the census of 2002, is given as 130 (115 male, 15 female). This does not include the staff of non-Chilean bases in the area. During summer, the population swells to 350.

The largest population centre is located on King George Island and consists of President Eduardo Frei Montalva Station, which has an airport, and which is connected to the village of Villa Las Estrellas, which has a town hall, hotel, day-care centre, school, scientific equipment, hospital, post office and bank. This enclave is a centre of logistical support for the other eight countries with scientific bases on King George Island. The Antarctic Institute of the Ministry of Foreign Relations controls Professor Julio Escudero Base, the main Chilean scientific facility in Antarctica.
