Anthony Goldwire (born on September 6, 1971 in West Palm Beach, Florida) is an American basketball player, formerly in the NBA. He played for the University of Houston and was drafted by the Phoenix Suns in the 2nd round (52nd overall) in 1994. Goldwire is currently with Panellinios Gymnastikos Syllogos of the Greek basketball league.

[edit] Awards

  • He was on the CBA Championship Team in 1994-95 season.
  • He won the Second All-Rookie Team for the CBA 1994-95 season.
