Antonio Lopez

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Antonio Lopez is also the name of:

  • Antonio López de Santa Anna, a Mexican general, famous for leading Mexican forces to victory at the Battle of the Alamo.
  • Antonio López, a Spanish astronomer.
  • Antonio Lopez, an American actor.
  • Antonio López García, a Spanish realist painter and sculptor.
  • Antonio López Guerrero, a Spanish football player.
  • Antonio López-Istúriz White, a Spanish politician.
  • Antonio López Nieto, a Spanish football referee.
  • Antonio Garcia Lopez (criminal), a Puerto Rican criminal also known as "Toño Bicicleta".
  • Carlos Antonio López (politician), former president of Paraguay between 1844 and 1862.
  • Juan Antonio Lopez, a Mexican boxer.
