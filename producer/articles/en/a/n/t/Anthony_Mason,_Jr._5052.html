Anthony Mason, Jr. (born January 25, 1987) is a 6'7", 203 lb forward on the St. John's University basketball team, Red Storm.

Mason attended Fairley High School located in Memphis, Tennessee. He is the son of former National Basketball Association player Anthony Mason.
