[edit] Double redirects

Hi there. Thanks for moving Islamic Society of Sydney Boys' High to the preferred spelling Islamic Society of Sydney Boys High. Just wanted to let you know that you forgot or weren't aware of how to correct double redirects. Because ISSBH and Islamic Society of Sydney Boys' High School redirected to the spelling with the apostrophe, a user clicking on those spellings would find themselves at a redirect page, not redirected to the right page.

If you don't know how to correct double redirects, just click "what links here" on the left side of the main article. Check to make sure each link marked "redirect page" redirects directly to the preferred spelling and not to another redirect.

Not a big deal, I already corrected them, but I thought you might want to know for future reference. See you around! NickelShoe 00:00, 11 January 2006 (UTC)
