Antowan Nothling is a South African voice artist, actor and radio personality. Born 21 October 1977 in Pretoria, South Africa. He currently lives in Centurion. It was recently confirmed he will make an appearance in a new MNet production coming to South African screens later in 2007.

[edit] Radio Stations he has worked for

  • Radio Rippel 90.5
  • Tuks FM 107.2
  • Kosmos Digitaal
  • Kosmos Namibia
  • Jacaranda 94.2 (currently)

[edit] Voice over art

Antowan has done voice work for major companies in South Africa including most of the major banks, restaurants and many in the entertainment industry.

[edit] Trivia

Antowan does many character voices for the translated German series Berlyn Distrik 40
