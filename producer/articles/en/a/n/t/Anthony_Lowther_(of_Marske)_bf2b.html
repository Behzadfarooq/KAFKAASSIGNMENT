Anthony Lowther (of Marske)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Anthony Lowther, FRS (c. 1641 – 27 January 1693) was an English landowner, of Marske, and Member of Parliament. He was the eldest son of Robert Lowther (d. 1655), an alderman of London; Robert's brother was Sir John Lowther of Lowther.

He was one of the original Fellows of the Royal Society, from its chartered inception in 1663.[1]

In February 1667, he married Margaret Penn, daughter of Sir William Penn, and had one son:

  • Sir William Lowther, 1st Baronet (1670–1705)
