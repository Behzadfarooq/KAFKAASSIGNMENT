Antonello Cuccureddu (born 4 October 1949) is an Italian football (soccer) coach and former player.

During his playing career he played for Brescia (1968-69), Juventus (1969-81, winning six Serie A titles), and Fiorentina (1981-83). He earned 13 caps for the Italy national football team, and played in the 1978 FIFA World Cup.

He is currently the coach of U.S. Grosseto F.C.
