Antwerpen International Airport (Deurne) (IATA: ANR, ICAO: EBAW) is located 2 km from the City of Antwerp, Belgium. It has a maintenance hangar of VLM Airlines. In 2005 it served as many as about 105,937 passengers (only counted: Scheduled Flights, Business Flights and Charter Flights).

[edit] Airlines and destinations

  • VLM Airlines (London, Manchester) with onward connections to Isle of Man, Jersey and Liverpool,
  • Welcome Air (Rotterdam [Saturdays only])
  • Abelag
  • Flying Group
