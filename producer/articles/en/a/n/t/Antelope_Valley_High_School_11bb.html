Antelope Valley High School

Antelope Valley High School is located in Lancaster, California and is part of the Antelope Valley Union High School District. It was founded in 1912[1]. It is located in the Mojave Desert. During the 1960s, the high school served as meeting ground for musicians Frank Zappa, Captain Beefheart (Don Van Vliet), [1] Rockette Morton (Mark Boston) and Zoot Horn Rollo (Bill Harkleroad).

[edit] Notable alumni

Notable alumni include:

  • Kevin Appier[2]
  • Priscilla Barnes of Three's Company[3]
  • Steve Chilcott, number one pick in the 1966 Major League Baseball draft
  • Sean Douglass[4]
  • Dwayne Murphy[5]
  • Marcy Walker, soap opera actress
  • Frank Zappa[6]

Don Van Vliet (born Don Glen Vliet on January 15, 1941 in Glendale, California) is a musician and visual artist, best known by the pseudonym Captain Beefheart.
