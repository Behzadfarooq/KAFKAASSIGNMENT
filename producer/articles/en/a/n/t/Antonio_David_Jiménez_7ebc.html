Antonio David Jiménez (born February 18, 1977, Sevilla) is a Spanish long-distance runner.

He won the gold medal in the 3000m steeplechase at the 2002 European Athletics Championships in Munich. He was a finalist in the same event at the 2004 Olympics.

He finished 5th in the 300m steeplechase final at the 2006 European Athletics Championships in Gothenburg.
