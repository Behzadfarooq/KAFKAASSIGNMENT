Anterrabae is a metalcore/hardcore punk band from Long Island, New York, USA. Their first album, Shakedown Tonight, was released in March 2004. Their second studio album, And Our Heart Beat In Our Fingertips, Without Reason came out in June 2006. They have since disbanded.
