Anthology is a 2-disc greatest hits album by released by funk/R&B group Cameo in 2002. The collection is arguably the best representation of Cameo released to date, containing 23 of their 26 Top-40 R&B hits. Strangely though, the top 5 "You Make Me Work" is not present. This collection was repackaged and retitled Gold three years later.
