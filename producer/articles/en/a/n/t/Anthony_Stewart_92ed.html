Anthony Stewart (born January 5, 1985 in LaSalle, Quebec, Canada) is a professional ice hockey player. He is 6'1", 225 lbs, shoots right and plays Right Wing for the Florida Panthers of the NHL. He was drafted by the Panthers in the first round, 25th overall, in the 2003 NHL Entry Draft. Anthony's younger brother Chris is a player with the OHL's Kingston Frontenacs, and was drafted by The Colorado Avalanche 18th overall in the 2006 NHL Entry Draft.
