Anthem (disambiguation)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Anthem may refer to several things, besides the definition and etymology at anthem:

  • Anthem (novella) is a novella by Ayn Rand.
  • Anthem (band), one of several 80's Japanese metal bands.
  • Anthem, Arizona is a town in The United States.
  • Anthem (Toyah album) an album by Toyah.
  • Anthem (album) an album by ska band Less Than Jake.
  • "The Anthem", a song by Good Charlotte.
  • Anthem Blue Cross and Blue Shield, is an affiliate of the Blue Cross Blue Shield Association providing health insurance in several US States.
  • Anthem is a song by progressive rock band Rush.
  • "Anthem", a song by Deep Purple from their 1969 album The Book of Taliesyn
  • Anthem (We Are the Fire) is a song by metalcore band Trivium
  • Anthem is an album by Irish composer Ronan Hardiman.
  • Anthem Records, a record label.
