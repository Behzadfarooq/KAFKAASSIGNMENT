The Aquarium Fishes WikiProject Newsletter
Issue IV - March 2007
News
  • Please welcome our new members: Antony1103 (talk • contribs), riftreef (talk • contribs), Roan Art (talk • contribs)
  • Pufferfish has been retitled as Tetraodontidae. See discussion here.
  • Various goldfish pictures have been deleted due to copyrights issues. This left many goldfish articles with no pictures at all. See Celestial Eye, Pompom (goldfish), Ranchu, Ryukin, Veiltail.
  • User:Melanochromis is attempting to organize uncategorized uploaded pictures (excluding pictures from wikicommons as those are already categorized in wikicommons). If you see uncategorized pictures, please add appropriate categories such as Category:Fish images, Category:Cypriniformes images, Category:Barb images, Category:Perciformes images, Category:Cichlid images, Category:Gourami images, Category:Tetraodontiformes images
  • Articles recently created: Celestichthys margaritatus (Microrasbora sp. 'Galaxy'), Nimbochromis polystigma, Cuckoo catfish, Metriaclima, Thick-Lipped Gourami, Colisa
  • Articles recently expanded or revised: Bolivian ram, Maylandia, Protein skimmer, Giraffe catfish, List of freshwater aquarium fish species, Calico goldfish, Banjo catfish, Cypriniformes
  • See or edit the current tasklist here
Discussions
  • User:MiltonT has proposed to combine some goldfish breed articles. Discuss here.
  • Anyone wants to design the "aquarium box"?
  • Websites references and/or external links. What do you consider appropriate websites and what're not? What about Planet Catfish, Loach Onlines and the likes? Discuss here

[edit] The Aquarium Fishes Newsletter: April 2007

The Aquarium Fishes WikiProject Newsletter
Issue V - April 2007
News
  • Please welcome our new members: Toniher (talk • contribs), MiltonT (talk • contribs), Regani.de (talk • contribs), Codenametiger (talk • contribs)
  • User:DSWebb has created "breedbox" which replaced the taxobox in all goldfish breed articles. See our discussion here.
  • User:MiltonT has created the "Aquarium and Fishkeeping Hobby" box which has been placed in many articles in the category:fishkeeping. See discussion here.
  • In the past two weeks, four different articles have achieved the good article status. They are Oscar, Pelvicachromis pulcher, Fish, Cichlid. User:MidgleyDJ deserves our praise for arduously contributing to most of these improvements.
  • Kribensis has become a disambiguation page. The original contents were moved to Pelvicachromis pulcher.
  • The Portal:Fish has added a new feature - the Fish Quiz. Feel free to come test your knowledge, interact with other fish editors, try to win the game and collect a special prize.
  • The project now has an award program for outstanding and exceptional editors.
  • Articles recently created:List of brackish aquarium fish species, Red-tail dwarf puffer, Chromis viridis, Chromis atripectoralis, Filter (aquarium)
  • Articles recently expanded or revised: Cyprinid, Dwarf pufferfish, Fathead minnow, Rainbow darter, Green sunfish, Oscar, Cichlid, Convict cichlid, Pseudoplatystoma
  • See or edit the current tasklist here
Discussions
  • Our parent wikiproject, the WikiProject Fishes, is having an important discussion about the policy of "Titles of fish species articles". This policy covers the selection of scientific and common names as article titles. It will have a significant impact on a large number of fish articles. See or join the discussion here. Related to this discussion is the title change of the article Mayan cichlid to Cichlasoma urophthalmus.
  • Aquarium, a featured article since 2005, has been proposed for a review which may lead to the article being de-featured. See the proposal and discuss here. Discuss how to improve the article here.

To subscribe or unsubscribe this newsletter, or if you would like to to add news to the next issue, please see here.

--Melanochromis 22:16, 4 April 2007 (UTC)
