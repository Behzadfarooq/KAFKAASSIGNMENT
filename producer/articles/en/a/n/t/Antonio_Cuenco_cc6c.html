Antonio Cuenco

Rep. Antonio "Tony" Veloso Cuenco (born on March 26, 1936) is the current Congressman (see Philippine Congress) representing Cebu City South. Currently, he lies as the Chairman of Dangerous Drugs Committee and Foreign Affairs.
