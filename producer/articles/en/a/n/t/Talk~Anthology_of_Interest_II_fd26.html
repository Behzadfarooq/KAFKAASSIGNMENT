[edit] Kansas and New New York

Doesn't Leela say "Nibbler, I don't think we're in New New York anymore?" while at the end the "Professor" tells Leela to click the shoes three time and wish she was back at Kansas? Go Futurama! User:Sp3000 05:29, 20 December 2006 (UTC)

It as a reference to The Wizard of Oz. In fact, that whole segment is a spoof of The Wizard of Oz. I doubt it would make much sense (or more importantly: be funny) to someone without prior knowledge of the original story. Kranak 17:00, 8 February 2007 (UTC)
