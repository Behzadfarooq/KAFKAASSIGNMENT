Anton Pierre (born 23 September 1977 in Port of Spain) is a football defender from Trinidad and Tobago. He currently plays as for Defence Force. He got 52 caps and scored 1 goal for the national team between 1996 and 2005 [1]. In May 2005 he was left out of the national team by new coach Leo Beenhakker, but he was called up as a standby player to the 2006 World Cup.
