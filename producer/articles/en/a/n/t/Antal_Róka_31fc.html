Antal Róka (June 25, 1927 - September 16, 1970) was a Hungarian athlete who competed mainly in the 50 kilometre walk.

He competed for a Hungary in the 1952 Summer Olympics held in Helsinki, Finland in the 50 kilometre walk where he won the bronze medal.
