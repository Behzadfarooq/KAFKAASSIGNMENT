Userspace is King!
(userspace)

Userpage design;

All of my previous userpages have consisted of elements from all over the encyclopedia; the current is owed mostly to Tariqabjotu and Essjay, whose userpages are in themselves something else. The most recent was also owed to Tariqabjotu, as well as the meta main page and just my personal code bank.

I believe no userpage is complete without symbolic, or nice-looking, pictures interspersed. My current userpage is on the theme of the desert, with - for those of you who do English Studies - a figurative symbolism of a "Desert of Incivility" - i.e. my aim to refrain from incivil behaviour.

I've got no qualms about any copy+pasting at all from my userpages or subpages - in fact, attribution doesn't bother me...it's a Wiki, and anyway, a lot of it was copied from another user, who borrowed it from another, who loaned it from yet another, who...you get the drift :P
