Antti Akseli Laaksonen (born October 3, 1973 in Tammela, Finland) is a professional ice hockey left winger who currently plays for the Colorado Avalanche of the NHL.

[edit] Playing career

Laaksonen left Finland in 1993 to get an education and play hockey at the University of Denver where he played four seasons for the Pioneers. Laaksonen was selected 191st overall by the Boston Bruins in the 1997 NHL Entry Draft. In 2000, Laaksonen joined the expansion Minnesota Wild via free agency.
