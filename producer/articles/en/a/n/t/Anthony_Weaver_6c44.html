Anthony Weaver (born July 28, 1980) is an American football player who plays defensive end for the Houston Texans.

Born in Abilene, Texas, Weaver was drafted by the Ravens in 2002 after four years at the University of Notre Dame, which was preceded by a four-year stint at Saratoga Springs High School. He started all 16 games of the 2003 season and was one of two rookies to start every game played the previous season (the other being safety Ed Reed).

In 2005, Weaver became a free agent and signed with the Houston Texans.

Anthony is also among a host of Samoan football players in the NFL.
