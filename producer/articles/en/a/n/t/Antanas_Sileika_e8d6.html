Antanas Sileika is a Canadian novelist and critic.

He was born in Weston, Ontario.

His second novel, Buying Time (1997) was nominated for both the City of Toronto Book Award and the Stephen Leacock Award for Humour, and was serialized on CBC Radio's Between the Covers.

Sileika is the artistic director for the Humber School for Writers and is a past winner of a National Magazine Award.

[edit] Bibliography

  • Dinner at the End of the World. Oakville, Ontario: Mosaic, 1994.
  • Buying on Time. Erin, Ontario: Porcupine's Quill, 1997.
  • Woman in Bronze. Toronto: Random House Canada, 2004.
