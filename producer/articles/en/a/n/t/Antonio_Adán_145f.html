Antonio Adán Garrido (born 13 May 1987 in Madrid) is a Spanish football goalkeeper, who currently plays for Real Madrid Castilla.

He was selected as a member of Real Madrid's U.S. tour in August 2006.

Adán's dream is to play in the Primera División, as a Real Madrid player if possible, while his role model Íker Casillas continues to be the first-choice goalkeeper.


[edit] National team

  • In the summer of 2006, Adán captained the U-19 Spanish squad all the way to winning the European Championship alongside four other Real Madrid products: defensive midfielder Javi García, midfielder Esteban Granero, and strikers Juan Mata and Alberto Bueno.
  • Adán was called up to the U-21 Spanish squad for European U-21 Championship play-off against Italy in October 2006, but was benched. Spain lost to Italy 1-2 on aggregate.
