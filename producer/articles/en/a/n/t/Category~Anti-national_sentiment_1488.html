Category:Anti-national sentiment

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

This category contains articles about criticism of or unfavorable sentiment directed at a particular nationality. It differs from category:persecution in that it need not involve persecution, and ethnism in that many major states (for instance, Brazil) do not have a one-to-one correspondence with ethnicities.
