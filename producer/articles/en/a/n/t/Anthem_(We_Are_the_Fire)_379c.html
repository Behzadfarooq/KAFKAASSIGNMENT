Anthem (We Are the Fire) is a single by the American band, Trivium. It was the second single from their 2006 album, The Crusade. The single release for the song included two B-sides from The Crusade. It made 40th in the UK Singles Chart, and number 1 in UK Rock Singles chart. However, it only achieved 89th in the US Modern Rock Chart.
