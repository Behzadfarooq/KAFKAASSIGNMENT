Anty♥ (real name Antoine Théodore Cousteau) gained notoriety within the Action Half-Life community in the early 00's for his refusal to discontinue use of the shotgun. He continues to be banned from computer game servers for a wide variety of offences, including but not limited to talking, swearing, using the wrong gun, not swearing, having a pink name and being banned.

There have been numerous credible reports that Anty♥ is an alcoholic and suffers from a broad spectrum of mental health problems. He is believed to be fluent in at least five languages: English, Danish, Latin, Russian and Japanese.

Anty♥ is famous for his extravagant tastes in designer clothes, haircuts, original artwork and gourmet foods. How he funds these affectations remains unclear.
