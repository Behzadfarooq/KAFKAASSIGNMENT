Antonio De Poli

Antonio De Poli (born on 4 October 1960 in Vicenza) is an Italian politician and Member of the European Parliament for North-East with the Cristiani Democratici Uniti, part of the European People's Party and is vice-chair of the European Parliament's Committee on Fisheries and its Committee on Employment and Social Affairs.

He is also a member of the Delegation for relations with Canada and a substitute for the Delegation for relations with the United States.
