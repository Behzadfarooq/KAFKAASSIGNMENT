Antigone is a character in Greek Mythology. The following have been named after her, or bear her name:

  • Antigone of Macedonia, mother of Berenice I of Egypt.
  • Antigone of Epirus, wife of Pyrrhus of Epirus.
  • Antigone, the famous play by Sophocles, based on the myth.
  • Antigone, Jean Anouilh's play, also based on the myth.
  • Antigone, George Judy's play, an adaptation of the play by Sophocles.
  • Antigone, the opera adaptation of the myth by Carl Orff.
  • Antigone, the album of a Metalcore band Heaven Shall Burn.
  • 129 Antigone, an asteroid.
  • Antigone Rising, an all-girl rock band.
  • Antigone District, a redeveloped urban area in Montpellier, France, by Ricardo Bofill
