Antoni Żabko-Potopowicz

Antoni Żabko-Potopowicz (b. 9 June 1895 in Oczesa-Rudnia, gubernya of Czernihów - d. 26 April 1980 in Warsaw) was economist of agriculture, economic historian, professor of Warsaw Agricultural University, member of Towarzystwo Naukowe Warszawskie (Polish for Polish Society of Science), co-founder of reactivated in 1945 Polskie Towarzystwo Ekonomiczne (Polish Economic Society).
