Anthony Obodai (born 8 May 1982 in Accra [1]) is a Ghanian football player, currently playing for RKC Waalwijk.

[edit] Career

[edit] Ajax

Anthony signed for Ajax Amsterdam in 2001 [2], but did not make his league debut until 2004, against SC Heerenveen [3].

[edit] Sparta

Anthony signed for Sparta Rotterdam in 2005 [4], after a proposed move to English side Luton Town collapsed [5]. He was made club captain [6]. However, on 29th January 2007 it was announced that he had had a training-ground bust-up with Sparta coach Wiljan Vloet and wanted to leave the club [7]; teams including PSV, Feyenoord, RKC Waalwijk and OGC Nice were all said to be interested [8].

[edit] RKC Waalwijk

On 31 January 2007 it was announced that Anthony had signed for RKC Waalwijk on a contract which runs until June 2010 [9]

On February 3 he played his first Eredivisie match for RKC against NAC Breda [10]
