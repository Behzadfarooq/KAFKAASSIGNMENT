Antonio Gutiérrez de la Fuente (1796 – 1878) was a Peruvian politician who also served in the Peruvian military. He was briefly President of Peru from June to September 1829.

Gutiérrez de la Fuente was born in the city of Huantajaya, Tarapacá, Peru (now Chile), in 1796. He activley participated in the movement to overthrow the government of José de La Mar being named vice president and taking the position of the President of the Republic until the General Agustín Gamarra took possession of the job.

He died in Tarapacá in 1878.
