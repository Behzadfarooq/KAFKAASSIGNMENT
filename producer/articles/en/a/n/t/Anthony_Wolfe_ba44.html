Anthony Wolfe (born December 23, 1983 in Manzanilla) is a Trinidadian footballer who plays as a midfielder for San Juan Jabloteh in Trinidad and Tobago.

He is considered a surprise inclusion in the Trinidad and Tobago national team for the 2006 FIFA World Cup. He was picked due to his good goalscoring record in the Trinidad league.

He has played 4 times for his country (as of June 10, 2006), the first being on July 3, 2003 against Venezuela.
