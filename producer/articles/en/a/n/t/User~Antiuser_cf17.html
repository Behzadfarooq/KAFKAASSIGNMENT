Hi, I'm antiuser, also known in the real world as Adriano. I'm a Brazilian-born Italian who lives in Seattle, Washington, United States. I work as a Graphic Designer and joined Wikipedia some time in 2004 (I think). I used to play Guitar in a Weezer tribute band.

I try to contribute mostly with Brazilian Music-related articles. Most of them are stubs, or quasi-stubs, which I hope to expand when I have more time. Lately I've also been patrolling Recent Changes and helping fight Vandalism. If I've mistakenly reverted or removed one of your edits as vandalism, please let me know.
