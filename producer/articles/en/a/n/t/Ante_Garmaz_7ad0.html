Antonio Jorge Garmaz best known as Ante Garmaz (Zupa, Croatia, January 5, 1928), is an Argentine model, fashion designer, TV host, and actor. He took part in movies like Los hombres sólo piensan en eso (Men only think of that) with Alberto Olmedo and Susana Giménez.

He was declared in bankruptcy in 2004 with 136.506,69 argentine pesos debt.
