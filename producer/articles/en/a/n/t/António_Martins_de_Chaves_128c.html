António Martins de Chaves

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

His eminence António Martins de Chaves (died 6th July 1447) was a Cardinal of the Catholic Church. He was bishop of Porto in Portugal.

He was made cardinal on the 18th December 1439 by Pope Eugenius IV.
