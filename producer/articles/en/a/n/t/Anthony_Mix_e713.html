Anthony Mix (born January 20, 1983 in Bay Minette, Alabama) is an American football player who plays wide receiver, most recently for the New York Giants. Mix played collegiately with Auburn before being signed as an undrafted free agent following the 2006 NFL Draft, where two fellow Auburn receivers (Devin Aromashodu and Ben Obomanu) were drafted.
