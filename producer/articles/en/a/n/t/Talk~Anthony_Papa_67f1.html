[edit] NPOV

Whoever did this article obviously holds Papa in very high esteem:

  • "His art became a weapon used to fight the failed war on drugs in the United States."
  • Papa became a symbol of the Nation's war on drugs and generated hundreds of news articles profiling its human interest aspect.*
  • In 2004 Papaâ€™s memoire "15 to Life: How I Painted My Way To Freedom" (Feral House) was released. It is a personal odyssey about transcendence that takes readers inside the harsh world of a maximum-security prison, where Papa spent twelve years fighting for his freedom while struggling to survive the violence and degradation of life behind bars. It tells how Papa came to find solace through painting, and how it was ultimately his art that set him free and gave birth to his political awarness that compelled Papa to become an agent of change and transformation.
