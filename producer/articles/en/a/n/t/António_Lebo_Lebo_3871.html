António Lebo Lebo, better known as Lebo Lebo or Lebo-Lebo (born 29 May 1977 in Malanje, Angola), is an Angolan football central defender who currently, as of May 2006, plays for Petro Atletico.

He is a member of the national team, and was called up to the 2006 World Cup.
