Ant, Ants, or ANT can refer to:

  • Ant, one of the most successful groups of insects
    • Ant-hill, a pile of materials that build up at the entrances of the subterranean dwellings of ant colonies as they are excavated
    • The Ants, a Pulitzer Prize-winning reference book about ants
  • Ant (chair), a modern chair designed by Arne Jacobsen
  • Antlia (constellation), standard astronomical abbreviation
  • ANT, an early airplane series produced by Tupolev design bureau in the Soviet Union
  • Actor-network theory, a contemporary sociological theory
  • An ant is a name of a group of enemies on Crash Bandicoot

People named Ant:

  • Ant, aka Anthony Phillips (b. 1951), British guitarist with the band Genesis
  • Ant (comedian), aka Anthony Kalloniatis (b. 1967), American actor and comedian
  • ANT, aka Antony Harding, British musician, and solo project of Hefner drummer
  • Ant (producer), aka Anthony Davis, American record producer and member of the Rhymesayers hip-hop collective
  • Ant & Dec, the British television presenter duo, Anthony McPartlin and Declan Donnelly.
  • Adam Ant (b. 1954), British musician, and lead singer of Adam and the Ants.

In science and computing:

  • Apache Ant, a Java-based computer build system
  • ANts P2P, a peer to peer filesharing network.
  • Langton's ant, a two-dimensional Turing machine with a very simple set of rules
  • Ants (computer game), a game for the Amiga computer system
  • Microsoft Ants, a free multiplayer PC game
  • ANT (network), a wireless personal area network protocol
  • ANT (typesetting system), a typesetting system inspired by TeX
  • ANTS (conference) is a symposium on Algorithmic Number Theory.

In geography:

  • River Ant, in Norfolk, England
  • Ant (Island), in Micronesia
  • Antigua and Barbuda, IOC Country code

In arts and entertainment:

  • Les Fourmis, or The Ants, a series of French novels (Les Fourmis) by Bernard Werber
  • Empire of the Ants (1991 novel) the first novel in the series by Bernard Werber
  • Antz, an animated film
  • Ant (fictional), a character from Image comics
  • American Negro Theater, formed in Harlem on June 5, 1940
