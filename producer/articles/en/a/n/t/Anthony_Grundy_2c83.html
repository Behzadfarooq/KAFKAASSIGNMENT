Anthony Montreace Grundy (born April 15, 1979, in Louisville, Kentucky) is an American professional basketball player. A guard, standing 6'3" (1.91 m) and weighing 185 lbs (83.9 kg), he played with the National Basketball Association's Atlanta Hawks in the 2004-05 NBA season. Prior to his professional career he attended Warren Central High School in Bowling Green, Kentucky, and then North Carolina State University.
