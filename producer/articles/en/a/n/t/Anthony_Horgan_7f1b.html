Anthony Horgan

Anthony Horgan (born 15 November 1976) is an Irish rugby player. His province is Munster. He was a part of Ireland's squad at the 2003 Rugby World Cup in Australia. His usual position is on the wing. He made his Test debut for Ireland against Samoa in June of 2003.
