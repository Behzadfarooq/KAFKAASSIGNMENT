Anton Antonov-Ovseenko (born 1920) is Russian historian and writer.

He is the son of a Bolshevik military leader Vladimir Antonov-Ovseenko. He was arrested in 1940 and spent 13 years in labor camps.

[edit] His books

  • The Time of Stalin: Portrait of a Tyranny, Harper & Row, 1981, ISBN 0-060-10148-2
  • Teater of Iosef Stalin Moscow. "Grėgori-Pėĭdzh", 1995. ISBN 5-900-49315-6
  • Enemy of the people, Moscow. Intellekt, 1996. Russian text online
  • Beria (Russian) Moscow, AST, 1999. Russian text online
