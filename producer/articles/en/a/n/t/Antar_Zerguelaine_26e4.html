Antar Zerguelaine

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Antar Zerguelaine (born 4 January 1985 in Jijel) is an Algerian middle distance runner who specializes in the 1500 metres.

[edit] Achievements

Year Tournament Venue Result Extra
2004 World Junior Championships Grosseto, Italy 5th 1500 m
2005 World Athletics Final Monte Carlo, Monaco 7th 1500 m

[edit] Personal bests

  • 800 metres - 1:46.56 min (2005)
  • 1500 metres - 3:31.95 min (2005)
