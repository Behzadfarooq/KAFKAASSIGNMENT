Antoinette de Bourbon

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Antoinette de Bourbon (25 December 1493 - 22 January 1583. She was a daughter of François, Count of Vendôme and Marie de Luxembourg. She married Claude of Lorraine on 9 June 1513. They had 12 children.
