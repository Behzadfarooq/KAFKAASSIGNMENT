Anthony Wayne High School is a high school located in Whitehouse, Ohio, a suburb of Toledo. The school has an enrollment of approximately 1350 students in grades 9-12. The school is named for American General Anthony Wayne, who led troops in the nearby Battle of Fallen Timbers during the Northwest Indian War.

[edit] Administration

  • Principal: James Conner
  • Assistant Principals: Jim Fritz, Jeri Hoellrich, & Paul Holan (also Athletic Department)
  • Dean of Students: Steve Mossing (also Physical Education teacher)

[edit] Band

Anthony Wayne High School is most famous for its marching band, the Marching Generals. This high-stepping band has participated in several large-scale events, such as the London New Years parade, Rose Bowl, Orange Bowl, Fiesta Bowl and many others.

[edit] Athletics

Anthony Wayne High School competes in Ohio Division I sports and has won the following Ohio High School Athletic Association state championships:

  • Girls Softball- 2003 [1]

They have also won:

  • Cheerleading State Champions 2003-2006
  • Many Marching Band Contests[citation needed]

[edit] Mascot & colors

The team mascot is the general and the school colors are royal blue and white.
