Antenna may refer to:

In science and engineering:

  • Antenna (radio), the component designed to send and receive radio waves; (plural: antennas)
  • Antenna (biology), the sensing organs of several arthropods; (plural: antennae)
  • Antennae galaxies, the name of two colliding galaxies NGC 4038 and NGC 4039
  • Antenna may refer to a radio telescope
  • Antenna, in IC design can refer charge collecting behaviour of a wire during fabrication. Hence the term "Antenna Rules", which are the rules which ensure that the buildup of charge above a threshold (which can damage the oxide in the transistor gates) is prevented.

In music:

  • Antenna (album), an album by the rock group ZZ Top
  • produced by Rich Costey Antenna album by Cave In, 2003

In broadcasting:
