Anthony Senter (b. 1955) was a member of the DeMeo crew, a faction of the Gambino crime family, one of the five Italian Mafia families in New York City. The DeMeo crew was notorious for their violence, suspected of between 75-200 murders through the mid 1970s to the early 1980s, when the majority of the crew members still alive were brought to justice.

Anthony Senter was one half of the Gemini Twins, a nickname given to him and fellow DeMeo crew member Joseph Testa, due to the fact that they were always around each other and the primary hangout of the DeMeo crew was the Gemini Lounge in New York.

Along with his fellow Gemini Twin Joseph Testa, Anthony Senter was sentenced to life imprisonment on September 14, 1989. He is currently serving out this sentence at Allenwood Federal Correctional Complex, located in Allenwood, Pennsylvania.
