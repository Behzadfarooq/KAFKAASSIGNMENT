Anthony Lakes Ski Area is located in eastern Oregon, near the city of La Grande. The resort has one triple chair lift, and a vertical drop of 900 ft (270 m) from summit to base. The summit elevation is 8000 ft (2440 m), and the terrain is mostly of an intermediate and expert level. The resort also offers Cat skiing on nearby peaks, and includes a boardercross course as well.
