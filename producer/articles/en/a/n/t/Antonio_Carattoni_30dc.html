Antonio Carattoni

Antonio Carratoni (born 1945) became Captain Regent of San Marino on October 1, 2006 along with Roberto Giorgetti. He is scheduled to hold office until April 1, 2007. Carattoni is a member of the Party of Socialists and Democrats.
