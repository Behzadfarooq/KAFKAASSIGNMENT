Antonio González Izquierdo (born October 13, 1969 in Santander, Cantabria) is a former field hockey player from Spain, who won the silver medal with the Men's National Team at the 1996 Summer Olympics in Atlanta, Georgia. He also participated in the 1992 Summer Olympics in Barcelona.
