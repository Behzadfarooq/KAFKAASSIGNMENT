Anthony Lloyd (born March 14, 1984 in Taunton) is an English professional footballer, currently playing as a full-back for York City.

[edit] Football career

[edit] Huddersfield Town

Lloyd began his career as a trainee with Huddersfield Town, turning professional in August 2002. Lloyd made his league debut on September 27, 2003 in a 3-0 win at home to Leyton Orient and went on to make 42 league appearances for Huddersfield Town.

Lloyd joined Torquay United in November 2005 on loan,[2] and this was extended until the end of the season.[3]

[edit] York City

Lloyd joined York City in July 2006.[4] Lloyd said he would relish having an exciting run in the FA Cup this season with York City, after twice facing Birmingham City last season with Torquay United.[5]
