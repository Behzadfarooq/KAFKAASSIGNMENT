António Reis

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

António Reis, a Portuguese film director, was born in 1927 and died in 1991. He was married to Margarida Cordeiro, co-director in most of his films. He is considered as one of the most important directors of his country, due to the originality of his style.

[edit] Filmography

  • 1959 : Auto de Floripes (co-director)
  • 1963 : Painéis do Porto
  • 1964 : Do Rio ao Céu (co-director with César Guerra Leal- pt)
  • 1966 : Alto do Rabagão (co-director with César Guerra Leal)
  • 1974 : Jaime (co-director with Margarida Cordeiro). Jaime at "The Art Film Festival"
  • 1976 : Trás-os-Montes (co-director with Margarida Cordeiro)
  • 1985 : Ana (co-director with Margarida Cordeiro)
  • 1989 : Rosa de Areia (co-director with Margarida Cordeiro)
