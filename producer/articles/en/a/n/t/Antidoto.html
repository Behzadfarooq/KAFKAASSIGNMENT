Antidoto is the name of a Greek album by singer Anna Vissi. It was released in Greece and Cyprus in 1998 and went 3X Platinum. It also included the video clip "Erotevmenaki".

  2. "S' Eho Epithimisei"
  3. "Methismeni Mou Kardia"
  4. "Gazi"
  5. "Antidoto"
  6. "Magava Tout"
  7. "Pali Yia Sena"
  8. "O Teleftaios Stathmos"
  9. "Gi 'Alla"
  10. "Mou Anikeis"
  11. "O Ponos Tis Agapis"
  12. "Denomai"
  13. "Tilefonaki"
  14. "Na Ton Agapas"

[edit] Music

Music and lyrics are by Nikos Karvelas, Natalia Germanou (Track 14 - Lyrics) and Apostolos Diavolikis (Track 07 - Lyrics)
