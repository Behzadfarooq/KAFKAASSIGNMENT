[edit] Instructions on use:

PLEASE FEEL FREE TO IMPROVE THIS TEMPLATE!

Parameters:

  1. Title of article
  2. Template to be called
  3. Extra text to be added to the end.

Link for testing: Wikipedia:Sandbox

Important: Whenever you change the output of the template, you should update the sample here!

You must use subst before you call this template. Example call:

{{subst:AntiVandal|Article|uw-vandalism3|Thank you,}}--~~~~
gives you:
