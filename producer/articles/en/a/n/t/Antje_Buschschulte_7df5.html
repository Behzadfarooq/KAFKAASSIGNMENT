Antje Buschschulte (born 27 December 1978 in Berlin) is a German swimmer. Her best disciplines are the short distance freestyle and backstroke races. Buschschulte swims for the sporting club SC Magdeburg. Up to now, she has won 24 German championships.

[edit] International Results

  • 1996 – Bronze medal 4 x 100 m freestyle relay, Atlanta Olympic Games
  • 1997 – European champion 100 m backstroke; second place European championship 200 m backstroke
  • 1999 – European chamion 4 x 100 m freestyle relay
  • 2000 – Bronze medal 4 x 200 m freestyle relay, Sydney Olympic Games
  • 2001 – World champion 4 x 100 m freestyle relay; second place World championship 50 m backstroke
  • 2002 – European chamion 4 x 100 m freestyle relay
  • 2003 – World champion 100 m backstroke
  • 2004 – Bronze medals 200 m backstroke, 4 x 200 m freestyle relay, 4 x 100 m medley relay 2004 Olympic Games
  • 2005 – Second place World championship 4 x 100 m freestyle relay
  • 2005 – Second place World championship 100 m backstroke
  • 2005 – Third place World championship 50 m backstroke
  • 2005 – Third place World championship 4 x 100 m medley relay
