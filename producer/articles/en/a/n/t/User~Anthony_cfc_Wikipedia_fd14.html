Wikimedia Activities;
I am a fairly active editor on various Wikimedia projects. I have just transferred from a full to a semi-wikibreak from Wikipedia English - I am currently trying to participate in the various other Wikimedia Projects. Deserving a special mention is simple.wikipedia, an English wikipedia written using simple vocabulary for children, readers with a poor grasp of English or those with Learning Difficulties. I believe that Simple deserves a place side-by-side with wikipedia.english, and I am striving to assist the project in any way I can.
Wikipedia.English;
As I have mentioned, I've just completed a self-reflection Wikibreak away from Wikipedia; as a result, I am now spending more time at the various other projects (including a non-Wikimedia wiki where I have been entrusted with the ABCO designation - gained in that order, incidentally.) However, I have - after much deliberation - decided to ease back into Wikipedia English, with a strict focus on dispute resolution, XfD debates and - most importantly - article writing and maintenance, including achieving Cambuslang railway station GA status, and assisting with the GA Candidate backlog as much as I can.
