Anthony Charles De Longis, born March 23, 1950, in Glendale, California, United States, is an American actor, stuntman, and choreographer. Anthony is well known for his recurring role on Star Trek Voyager, in Season 1 and early Season 2, as First Maje Jal Culluh, leader of the Kazon-Nistrim. An experienced swordsman, he is also known for the his roles on Highlander, in Season 3 as Lymon Kurlow in the episode "Blackmail," and in Season 5 as Octavio Consone in the episode "Duende."

He appeared as Claus Van Zandt in 1985 on the soap opera Days of Our Lives, and as Leo Mitchell in 1989 on Santa Barbara.

Anthony has starred in films, his best known role the character Blade in the 1987 movie Masters of the Universe, in which he was also Frank Langella's stunt double.

He has made several TV guest appearances, on Battlestar Galactica (original series) Babylon 5, Queen Of Swords, MacGyver, and many others. Anthony provided his voice in a "Star Trek" video game.
