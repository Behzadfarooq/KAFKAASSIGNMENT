Anthony Charles (born March 11, 1981 in Isleworth) is a professional footballer with Barnet F.C. A defender, Charles began his career with Crewe Alexandra, but failed to make a first-team apparances in two spells with the club before finally leaving in 2001. He returned south in 2001, and played for several non-league clubs before joining Barnet during their Conference promotion season of 2004/05. On February 20, 2007, he joined Aldershot Town on loan untill the end of the season.
