Bryan Adams' 2005 Anthology contains songs he recorded from 1978 through 2005. The three disc set includes two discs of songs from 1980-2005, and a third disc which is a live concert which was recorded in mid 2005. All the songs are in chronological order of their release, except the last song on the first disc, which was released in 1999. The album's enclosed booklet has notes about the process of the recordings and the credits for each track.

The Taiwanese and Latin American versions of the album contained the original version of the song "When You're Gone" featuring Melanie C. and also the new song "I'm Not The Man You Think I Am" from the movie Colour Me Kubrick, but omitted the third disc.
