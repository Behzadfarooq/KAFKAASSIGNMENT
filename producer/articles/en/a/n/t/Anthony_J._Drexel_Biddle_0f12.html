Anthony Joseph Drexel Biddle I (1875 - 1948) also known as Anthony Joseph Drexel Biddle, Sr. was the man upon whom the play and film The Happiest Millionaire was based.

His daughter, Cordelia Biddle, married Angier Buchanan Duke, the son of Benjamin Newton Duke. His son was Anthony Joseph Drexel Biddle, Jr..
