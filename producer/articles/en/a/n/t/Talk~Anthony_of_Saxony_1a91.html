[edit] Article name

I moved the article from Anthony Clement of Saxony to Anthony of Saxony, since he rarely seems to be called "Anthony Clement". I did not include the ordinal, since it rarely seems be used.

  • "Anthony I of Saxony"
  • "Anthony of Saxony"
  • "Anton I. von Sachsen"
  • "Anton von Sachsen"

I am also moving his full name (Anton Clemens Theodor Maria Joseph Johann Evangelista Johann Nepomuk Franz Xaver Aloys Januar) to the talk page to clean up the article appearance. Olessi 01:43, 28 January 2007 (UTC)
