[edit] Who I am

I'm Anthony. I go to school. I come home from school. etc... Watch this space for more "fun" stuff. ;)

Health

I'm not fat, that's the first thing. In fact I (think) I'm quite fit for a teen. However, I have been diagnosed with Obsessive Compulsive Disorder and spend a considerable amount of time alphebetising my school locker, taking 11 steps between my bed and the door, flicking every unused wall socket off, etc... It's me, and if anyone doesn't like me... well I really don't care. Honest.

School

Now in 4th Year at St.Aloysius College, Glasgow. I commute from Cambuslang station to Glasgow Central on the Argyle Line then walk up to school with my mates. This years exams are 8 Intermediate Twos and 1 GCSE...eek!

Home

Home Sweet Home is in the village of Cambuslang, up in Langlea. House: 4 Bedrooms, Detached. View: road/railway. As you can see, groundbreaking stuff.
