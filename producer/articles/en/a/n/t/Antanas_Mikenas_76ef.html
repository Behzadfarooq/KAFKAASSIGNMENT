Antanas Mikenas

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Olympic medal record
Men’s athletics
Silver 1956 Melbourne 20 kilometer walk

Antanas Mikėnas (24 February 1924 - 1994) was a Soviet athlete who competed mainly in the 20 kilometer walk. He trained at Spartak in Vilnius.

He competed for the USSR in the 1956 Summer Olympics held in Melbourne, Australia in the 20 kilometer walk where he won the silver medal.
