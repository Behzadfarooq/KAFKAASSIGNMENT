Dr António Roseiro

António Henrique Rodrigues Roseiro , Lisbon, 1940 is a Portuguese Dentist and inventor. Founder and president of VITAE - Association of Solidarity and International Development.

Dr. Honoris-cause in Dental Sciences for the University Pro-Deo - New York Former Vice-Director in the area of academic cooperation of ISCTEM (Superior Institute of Sciences and Technology of Mozambique) from January of 2002 to August of 2004.

Doctorating of FCUC - College of Sciences of the University of Coimbra. Investigator and regent of "Preventive Dental Medicine" and of Verbal Public Health in ISCTEM .

[edit] Awards

  • Founder of the - CESPU - Cooperative of Polytechnical and University Superior Education.
  • Member of the Installing Commission of the Institute of Sciences of Health - South.
  • Member of the Installing Commission of the New University of Mozambique.
  • Director of the Department of Dental Sciences of the New University of Mozambique.
  • Associate of the Portuguese Society of Geography.
  • Associate of the Cooperative of Superior Education Egas Moniz.
  • Gold Medal of the World-wide hall of Inventions and 1º Prize exequo SPAD/Information Dentaire *Gold Medal of the World-wide Hall of New Technologies and Innovations - Brussels in the project (Eureka)
  • "Oscar of the public" in the world-wide hall of inventions of Brussels
  • Honorary of the Association of Chinese Inventors - World-wide Hall of the Inventors of Pequim
  • Associate of the Brazilian Association of Hipnodontia - Rio De Janeiro
  • Comendador and knight of the Order of S. John of Jerusalem.
