Anthony Pateras is a musician and composer based in Melbourne, Australia. He has released material on labels such as Tzadik, Synaesthesia and Editions Mego.

Pateras works in a number of groups, the most prominent being The Pateras/Baxter/Brown Trio with Pateras on prepared piano and electronics, Sean Baxter on percussion and David Brown on prepared guitar.
