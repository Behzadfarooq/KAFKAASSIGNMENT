 Hibiscus rosa-sinensis
 Contributing
I contribute to Wikipedia because it is enjoyable, and because I believe it is a worthwhile project. When contributing here stops being enoyable, I stop contributing.
Although I am mainly a maintenance editor, I do also contribute to some railway articles; my specialty includes Glasgow railway stations and cleanup of general Scotland/U.K. railway articles; I assisted in the promotion of Paddington station to GA status, and Cambuslang railway station is currently aiming for GA status.
 Where to find me

Mostly, I'm either at WP:GAC, WP:REF, WP:AN, WP:POST, WP:FC, meta, commons or simple.english wikipedia. No matter where I am, I've always got the other eye on english, so bump be a message and you should get a response in a minute or two.
