Anthem Records is an independent record label based in Toronto, Ontario. The company was formed in the May 1977 by Ray Danniels and Vic Wilson with initial recording artists Rush, Max Webster, Liverpool and A Foot in Cold Water. The three members of Rush, Geddy Lee, Neil Peart and Alex Lifeson became associate directors of Anthem. The company was named after the novel Anthem by Ayn Rand.
