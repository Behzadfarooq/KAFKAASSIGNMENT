Antigonish Bulldogs
City: Antigonish, Nova Scotia
League: Maritime Junior A Hockey League
Founded: 1986
Home Arena: Antigonish Arena
Colours: Yellow, Black, and White

The Antigonish Bulldogs are a Tier-II Junior "A" team based out of Antigonish, Nova Scotia. They play out of the Maritime Junior A Hockey League. Their home rink is the Antigonish Arena.
