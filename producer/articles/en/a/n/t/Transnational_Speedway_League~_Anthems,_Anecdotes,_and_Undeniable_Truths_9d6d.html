Transnational Speedway League: Anthems, Anecdotes, and Undeniable Truths is the first full-length album by rock band Clutch. It was released in 1993 (see 1993 in music), and contains 11 songs.

  1. "A Shogun Named Marcus"
  2. "El Jefe Speaks"
  3. "Binge And Purge"
  4. "12 Ounce Epilogue"
  5. "Bacchanal"
  6. "Milk Of Human Kindness"
  7. "Rats"
  8. "Earthworm"
  9. "Heirloom 13"
  10. "Walking In The Great Shining Path Of Monster Trucks"
  11. "Effigy"
