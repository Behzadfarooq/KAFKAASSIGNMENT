Anthony Roberson (born February 14, 1983, in Saginaw, Michigan) is an American professional basketball player formerly of the NBA's Golden State Warriors. He spent part of the 2006-07 season with the Warriors before being waived in early January 2007.[1]

A 6'2" point guard, Roberson attended the University of Florida. Undrafted, he signed with the Memphis Grizzlies in August 2005. Throughout the 2005-06 NBA season, he split his time between the Grizzlies and the Arkansas RimRockers, the Grizzlies' D-League affiliate. In October 2006 he was was signed by Golden State to be evaluated during training camp.

His cousin, Terrance Roberson, played for the Charlotte Hornets in 2000.
