Antischism

Antischism were an anarchist crust punk band from Columbia, South Carolina. For most of their existence, the band consisted of:

  • Scott Cooper (drums, vocals, lyrics)
  • Lyz (vocals)
  • Kevin Byrd (guitar)
  • Alan (bass)

An additional male vocalist named Brent sang on the Still Life LP. Brent then moved to New York City and became a drum and bass DJ by the name of DJ Chrome. He also helped form the political art/dance/DJ collective known as Black Kat. Antischism broke up and then reformed in Austin, TX, as Initial State. After Initial State, Kevin went on to play in .Fuckingcom (with Lyz) and Guyana Punch Line. Scott moved to Savannah, GA, and played in Damad and Karst, and now plays in Chronicle A/D.
