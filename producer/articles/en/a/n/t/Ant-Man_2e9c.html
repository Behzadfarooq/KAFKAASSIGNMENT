Ant-Man may refer to:

  • Henry Pym, the first fictional character to use the title.
  • Scott Lang, a character who took Pym's equipment and replaced him.
  • Eric O'Grady, a new character and the unofficial replacement for Lang.
  • Ant-Man (film), a movie starring on Pym and Lang.
