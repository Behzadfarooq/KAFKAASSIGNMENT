Anthony Tyler Quinn (born July 25, 1964 in New London, Connecticut, USA) is an American actor.

  • Abandoned and Deceived (1995)
  • Air Wolf (1984 series) – Everett
  • Peyton Place (1985 series remake) – Joey Harrington
