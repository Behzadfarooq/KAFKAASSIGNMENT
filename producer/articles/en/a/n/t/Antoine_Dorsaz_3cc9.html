Antoine Dorsaz (born March 02, 1989 in Martigny, Switzerland) is a Swiss pair skater. He competes with Anais Morand. They are the 2007 Swiss junior national champions and have competed internationally on the Junior Grand Prix circuit and at the World Junior Figure Skating Championships. Their programs are choreographed by Ekaterina Gordeeva.
