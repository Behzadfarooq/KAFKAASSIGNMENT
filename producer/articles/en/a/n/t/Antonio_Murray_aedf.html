Antonio Murray

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Antonio Murray (born September 15, 1984 in Cambridge, England) is a professional football player who is currently playing for Histon.

Growing up in a 'Football' household Antonio always had an excitement for the game. As a youngster he played for Chelsea F.C. youth team while studying at school before moving to Ipswich Town F.C.

He was transferred from Ipswich Town to Hibernian FC in January 2005 to replace the departing Craig Rocastle, and went on to make 13 appearances before being released in the October 2006.

He had played in the middle of the park till he signed to Histon. He has a more attacking position at Histon.

Despite being born in England, he is eligible to play for Scotland through his Scottish father, although he is yet to be given the call. His mother is Italian.
