Air Chief Marshal Sir Anthony Wilkinson Heward, KCB, OBE, DFC and bar, AFC (born 1 July 1918) was a senior Royal Air Force commander. In 1944 he married Clare Myfanwy Wainwright, the daughter of Major-General C B Wainwright, CB. They have two children.

  • Group Captain RAF 1957
  • IDC 1962
  • Air Commodore 1963
  • Director of Operations (Bomber & Reconnaissance) MoD RAF 1963
  • Air Vice Marshal 1966
  • Deputy Commander RAF Germany 1966-69
  • AOA HQ RAF Air Support Command 1969-70
  • Chief of Staff RAF Strike Command 1970-72
  • AOC No.18 (Maritime) Group 1972-73
  • Air Member for Supply and Organisation, MoD 1973-76
  • Air Chief Marshal 1974.
  • County councillor, Wiltshire 1981.
