Anthony Tonkin (born Jan 17, 1980 in Cornwall) is an English professional football (soccer) defender, currently playing for Yeovil Town.

He began his career at Yeovil Town in 1999 and after three years, 95 appearances and a goal, moved to Stockport County. He spent a year at Stockport, until August 2003 when he was transferred to Crewe Alexandra for a reported fee of Â£150,000. After more than 90 appearances in under three years, he was released for free back to Yeovil.

On 2 March 2007, he joined Grays Athletic on loan for the remainder of the season.[1]
