Antti Sumiala (born February 20, 1974 in Pori) is a Finnish footballer who currently plays for Porin Palloilijat in Kakkonen, Finland's third highest division. He plays as a striker.

Sumiala has appeared 38 times for the Finnish national team, scoring nine goals.
