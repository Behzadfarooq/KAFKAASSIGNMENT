Welcome! This page is a talk page, for interacting with Anthony cfc on non-controversial topics/articles. When conversing with me, please remember the simple guidelines below; note that links are in the same colour as the surrounding text:
  • I will refrain from commenting on controversial issues, and will not involve myself with inter-editor disputes. This is to strive to keep myself out of conflict on the encyclopedia, as set out in my neutrality policy.
  • I am an obsessive and archive at irregular intervals, and whenever the feeling takes me; as such, it's quite likely if you posted a comment previously and it's gone, I have archived it. Feel free to bring it back to recommence discussion - simply follow the steps as directed at the archives.
  • Trolling and vandalism will be removed, and replaced with a notice informing of the reasons why: please - say it nice, or not at all.
  • If you're here to begin a dispute, or regarding a dispute...stop! - read WP:DR first! Then, post a civil post outlining the problem with relevant links; I may take time to reply - I will either be gathering my facts, or withdrawing from the dispute until it's cooled down. Please see my dispute policy for the full outline.
  • Please remain civil at all times.
  • Please use a descriptive header; if possible, include links.
  • Indent your replies with the colon; do not use "*"!
  • Don't use "re:-" in your header! It drives me crazy!

If you are here on dispute matters (not dispute resolution matters) and it directly involves me, then I will treat your post with the utmost priority - however, in return for this, please remain civil and don't make personal attacks at any time.

You may also find it convenient to email me via Wikipedia email or externally at anthony(dot)cfc(at)gmail(dot)com. This is best done for private matters, or to request my AMA assistance if you are under block. Although emails will under normal circumstances remain private, I reserve the right to disclose any as I see fit.

Ensure that your request belongs here! Chances are, if it's an official process - eg suspected sockpuppets, it belongs somewhere. However, Wikipedia is too big a place to get the right place first time - just pop me a message, and I'll be sure to point you in the right direction. Lastly, ensure you sign your posts, in accordance with WP:SIG! Beware HagermanBot!
