Antonio Isasi-Isasmendi (1927- ) is a Spanish film director and producer.

He was born in Madrid on March 22, 1927 and began working in the production firm Emisora Films as an assistant manager, film editor, scriptwriter, lead producer, and finally director. In 1955 he founded his own production company, Producciones Isasi, in Barcelona. He also founded another firm, Moon Films, in Madrid. From his relationship with the actress Marisa Paredes he has one daughter, María Carlota.
