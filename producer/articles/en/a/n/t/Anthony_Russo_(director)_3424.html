Anthony Russo is an American television and film producer, writer, and director, who usually works with his brother, Joe Russo.

[edit] Filmography

NOTE: All credits are shared with Joe Russo, unless specifically mentioned.

Year Title Role Other notes
2007 Dad Can't Lose director
2007 Triggerfish director
2007 Most Likely to Succeed director, writer
2006 Crashing executive producer
2006 You, Me and Dupree director
2002 Welcome to Collinwood director, writer
2001 The Kiss director, producer, writer short film
1997 Pieces director, editor, producer, writer
