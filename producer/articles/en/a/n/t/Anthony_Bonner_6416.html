Anthony Bonner (born June 8, 1968 in St. Louis, Missouri) is an American former professional basketball player who was selected by the Sacramento Kings in the 1st round (23rd overall pick) of the 1990 NBA Draft. He played six seasons in the NBA for the Kings, New York Knicks, and Orlando Magic averaging 6.9 ppg in his career. Bonner played college basketball at Saint Louis University. He is the Billikens' all-time leading scorer with 1,972 points.

Bonner is currently the head boys basketball coach for Vashon High School in St. Louis.
