Anthony is an unincorporated locality in Marion County, Florida, United States. It is located on County Road 200A. The Anthony post office has zip code 32617. The most famous resident of Anthony is John Travolta.


[edit] Geography

Anthony is located at 29°17′21″N, 82°06′43″W (29.2892, -82.1119).GR1
