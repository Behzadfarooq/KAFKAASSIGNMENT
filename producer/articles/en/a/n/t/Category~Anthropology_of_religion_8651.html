Category:Anthropology of religion

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Many anthropologists have specialized in studying the religious, spiritual and ceremonial aspects of culture. The anthropology of religion is a sub-sub-discipline of cultural anthropology. These anthropologists study such things as ritual, myth, sacred texts, sacred landscape, transpersonal experience, altered states of consciousness, shamanism, ghosts, spirits, religious hierarchy, so forth.
