Antigonish—Guysborough

Antigonish—Guysborough was a federal electoral district in Nova Scotia, Canada, that was represented in the Canadian House of Commons from 1917 to 1968.

This riding was created in 1914 and combined the former ridings of Antigonish and Guysborough. Antigonish—Guysborough was abolished under redistribution in 1966 forming parts of Cape Breton Highlands—Canso and Central Nova.

[edit] Members of Parliament

This riding has elected the following Members of Parliament:

  • John Howard Sinclair, Laurier Liberals (1917-1921)
  • Colin Francis McIsaac, Liberal (1921-1925)
  • Edward Edward MacDonald, Liberal (1925-1926)
  • John C. Douglas, Conservative Party of Canada (1926-1927)
  • William Duff, Liberal (1927-1936)
  • James Ralph Kirk, Liberal (1936-1958)
  • Angus Ronald MacDonald, Progressive Conservative (1957-1958)
  • Clement O'Leary, Progressive Conservative (1958-1962)
  • John B. Stewart, Liberal (1962-1968)
