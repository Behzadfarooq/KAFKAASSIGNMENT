Anton Devcich

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Anton Paul Devcich (born 28 September 1985 in Hamilton) is a New Zealand cricketer. While at Hamilton Boys' High School he won the Gillette Cup twice and he played in the Under-19 Cricket World Cup in 2004.

His family is of Croat origin.
