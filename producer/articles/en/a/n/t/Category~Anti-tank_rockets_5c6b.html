Category:Anti-tank rockets

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Anti-tank rockets are man-portable rockets designed to be able to defeat armored vehicles. Usually they are less capable than anti-tank guns and missiles, but are useful against various targets including buildings and fortifications.

The main article for this category is Anti-tank rockets.
