Anthony Newman (born November 21, 1965 in Bellingham, Washington) is a retired defensive back for the NFL. Newman played for the Los Angeles Rams from 1988-1994, New Orleans Saints from 1995-1997, and the Oakland Raiders from 1998-1999.
