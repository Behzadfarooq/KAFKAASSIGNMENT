Antonio Jesus Garcia Gonzalez, known as Toñito (born 24 February 1977 in Tenerife) is a Spanish footballer who currently is with NK Rijeka.

[edit] Clubs

  • 1996-1997: CD Tenerife
  • 1997-1999: Vitória Setúbal
  • 1999-2001: Sporting Lisboa
  • 2001-2002: CD Santa Clara
  • 2002-2004: Sporting Lisboa
  • 2004-2005: Boavista FC
  • 2005-2006: CD Tenerife
  • 2006–present: NK Rijeka
