Anthony Charles Telford (born March 6, 1966 in San Jose, California) is a Major League baseball player who has played for the Baltimore Orioles, Montreal Expos, and Texas Rangers.

Telford began playing professionally for the MLB on August 19, 1990, with the Baltimore Orioles. He is a graduate of San José State University and Silver Creek High School.
