Antye Greie aka AGF is a vocalist, musician, producer, and artist. Since 1996 permanently based in Berlin. She was born and raised in East Germany and developed an early interest for music and poetry and philosophy. Recently she has been exploring speech and spoken word combined with electronic music, as well as working on sound installations, pop songs, calligraphy and her website poemproducer.

In 2006 she gave birth to a daughter fathered by partner Vladislav Delay.

Greie performs as AGF (solo), Delay/AGF (with Vladislav Delay), The Dolls (with Vladislav Delay and Craig Armstrong), Laub (with Jotka), and The Lappetites (with Elaine Radigue, Kaffe Matthews and Ryoko Kuwajima) .
