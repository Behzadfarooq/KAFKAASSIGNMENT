Antichristian Phenomenon is an EP released in 2001 by Polish band Behemoth. It was released on the European "Act Of Rebellion" Tour, and only 1000 copies exist. Maciej Niedzielski (form the band Artrosis) is a guest on tracks 1-5; Bony (from the band Devilyn) and Adam Siezega (from the band Lost Soul) are guests on track 6. The EP also contains a multimedia track, a live video of the song "Christians To The Lions", featured on the Thelema.6 album.
