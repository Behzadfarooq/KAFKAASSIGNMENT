Anthony Terpiloff was a British TV-playwright active in the period 1961-1978. He is particularly known for The Poet Game (1972) and his high quality contributions to Gerry Anderson's science fiction series Space 1999. His writing for Space: 1999 included Earthbound, Death's Other Dominion, Collision Course, The Infernal Machine and Catacombs of the Moon.
