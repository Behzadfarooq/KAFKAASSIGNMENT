Antoine Karam

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Antoine Karam (born February 21, 1950 in Cayenne) is the President of the Regional Council (Conseil régional) of French Guiana, a région d'outre-mer of France.

Karam has been president of the Council since March 22, 1992. As a member of the left-wing Guianese Socialist Party (PSG), allied to the French Socialist Party (PS), he is also French Guiana’s single representative in the French Senate.

The election to choose the Council President is held every four years by the 31-member council.
