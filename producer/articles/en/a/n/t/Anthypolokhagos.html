Anthypolokhagos

Anthypolokhagos is used in the Greek language to mean "Second Lieutenant". In the modern Hellenic Army the rank is the lowest commissioned officer rank and is inferior to an Ypolokhagos (First Lieutenant). The insignia consists of a single silver star (or a star and a bar for reserve officers). Officers holding this rank should be addressed as "Kyrie Anthipolokhage" (Κύριε Ανθυπολοχαγέ) (stressed on the last syllable) by their subordinates, or "Anthipolokhage + family name" by their superior officers.
