Anthracite iron

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Anthracite iron is the substance created by the smelting together of anthracite coal and iron ore. The process of making anthracite iron was first performed by David Thomas on February 5, 1837 in the Swansea Valley of Wales.
