 WelCom and AAU

I am an active Welcoming Agent in the Welcoming Committee (WelCom); I see it as every Wikipedian's duty to welcome our newest editors, make them feel welcome and give them a helping hand until they find their feet on the encyclopedia. Wikipedia is a complicated place, with plenty of policies, guidelines and essays to keep you going, as well as all sorts of different users that need to be approached in all different styles, and we have a charge to pass on our knowledge to the next generation.

I have taken this a step further, and taken two (relatively) new users under my wing; we have our own dedicated "classroom" where we can discuss anything that is puzzling them. Whenever they are getting on fine, I gently teach a curriculum of avoiding disputes and civility, which I see as two key guidelines on Wikipedia.

Further reading: Wikipedia:Adopt-a-User; WP:WC.
