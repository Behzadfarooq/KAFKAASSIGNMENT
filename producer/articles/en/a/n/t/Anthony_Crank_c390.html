Anthony Crank

Anthony Crank is a British television presenter, best known for working on Channel 4's T4 and MTV.

Crank has been celebrity editor for magazines such as OK!, Miss and More and has also written a piece for Attitude.

Crank has also presented on MTV and has made guest appearances on Big Brother's EForum and ITV1's Loose Women.

Anthony is currently presenting BBC Holiday and a range of shows for the Eat Cinema channel.
