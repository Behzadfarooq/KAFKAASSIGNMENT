[edit] Anthropometrics and clothing sizing

There are many universities studying range of sizes in clothing. It is not just "scientists" as this page leads you to believe:

"outside academia, scientists working for private companies and government agencies conduct anthropometric studies to determine what range of sizes clothing and other items need to be manufactured in."

TC2 www.tc2.org is a non-profit organization that is facilitating anthropometric studies by supporting research done at universities with 3D body scanners (Cornell University, NC State, Auburn University, University of Missouri).

I know this first hand bc I am a student at Auburn University using anthropometric data to improve the fit of clothing for tween girls in my thesis.
