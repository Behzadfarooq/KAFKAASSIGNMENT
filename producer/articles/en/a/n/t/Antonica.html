Antonica

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Map of Antonica. Sony Online Entertainment image.

Antonica is a continent of Norrath in the EverQuest universe. It is the largest and most populated continent on the planet. The continent was known as Tunaria, until it was renamed by Antonius Bayle. It is on Antonica where Tunare first placed the High Elves. Antonica is also the birthplace of the Halflings, Trolls, Ogres, and Humans. The Dark Elves and Barbarians also call Antonica their home.
