[edit] POV-pushing

As you can tell from the current state of the AfD debate, I think an article on Anti-homosexualism should exist in Wikipedia. However, this article in its current state has maybe 5% of its space devoted to the concept and 95% of its space devoted to selected examples. This is problematic because the selection of examples is always a matter of opinion and accusations of POV-pushing are inevitable and understandable depending on the examples chosen. An encyclopedia should have an article explaining the term and its usage using cited references, and an essay-writer or historian could then select examples using the term. Rather than selecting examples, I hope your first level of categorization is according to "types" as done in the Anti-semitism article. I hope you can find a reference or two that supports the categorization you choose. Another alternative I hope you consider is a much shorter article as in the Judeophobia article. Flying Jazz 15:57, 5 September 2006 (UTC)
