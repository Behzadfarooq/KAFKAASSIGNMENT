Anthony Laffranchi (born November 16, 1980 in Murwillumbah, New South Wales) is an Australian rugby league player for the Gold Coast Titans in the National Rugby League. His position of choice is at Second Row though he can also play Prop forward.

[edit] Career highlights

  • Junior Club: Murwillumbah Colts
  • First Grade Debut: Round 26, Wests Tigers v Sharks at Leichhardt Oval, September 2, 2001
  • First Grade Record: 82 appearances scoring 14 tries

[edit] Rape allegations

On March 6, 2007, New South Wales police charged Anthony Laffranchi with the sexual assault of a 26-year-old woman. Laffranchi professes his innocence, and has vowed to fight the allegations. The trial is continuing.[1]

The rape allegations came at a bad time for Australia's NRL, which has suffered damage to its image from a series of incidents involving demeaning behaviour to women over the previous five years.[2]
