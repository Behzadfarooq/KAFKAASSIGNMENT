Welcome to the Nuclear Testing Facility Sandbox :)

Welcome to my Sandbox; sandboxes are "testing areas" for Wikipedians' future edits. Rather than posting them straight to the mainspace or userspace, editors test them out here first so that any collateral damage is contained, rather than spoil our encyclopedia.
• • •
Although anybody is welcome to use the code in sandboxes, I do ask you strongly to refrain from editing it - the point of the projects not being in the mainspace is that they are not viewable to visitors because they are not yet ready. Despite that, I can't see myself having these pages protected - that goes against the spirit of Wiki for no real reason. Just don't be an arse and vandalise it :)
