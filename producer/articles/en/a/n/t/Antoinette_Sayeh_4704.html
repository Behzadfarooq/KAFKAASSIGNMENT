Antoinette Sayeh

Dr. Antoinette M. Sayeh is a politician in Liberia and since January 2006, Minister of Finance under Ellen Johnson-Sirleaf. Sayeh was born on July 12, 1958 in Monrovia, Liberia. Sayeh is a graduate of Swarthmore College and the Fletcher School of Law and Diplomacy. Sayeh has also worked for the World Bank as country director for Benin, Niger, and Togo. According to one source, Sayeh has "delighted international financial institutions" as Minister of Finance She has improved the Liberian economy. [1]. Sayeh has one son named Dekonti.
