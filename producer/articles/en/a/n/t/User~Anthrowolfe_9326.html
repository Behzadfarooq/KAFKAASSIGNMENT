What is Anthrowolfe?



Aside from being a user on various internet sites, Anthrowolfe is also an assumed name for an amateur artist.Anthrowolfe has other interests than art which include skiing, reading, music, foreign languages, and animals, to name a few. Anthrowolfe is the owner of four dogs, one cat, and one rabbit.

[edit] Signed Musicians
    1. Fleetwood Mac
      Quite possibly my favorite band. While some may think they have no talent and are simply old has-beens from the 70's, everyone in the band has true talent for music. While I believe that Lindsey Buckingham and Mick Fleetwood are the most talented of the group, all of them have an above average abilty or a rare intrinsic understaning of musical expression.
    2. Barenaked Ladies
      The Barenaked Ladies are one of the most idiosyncratic group of musicians that I have ever had the pleasure of listening and unerstanding. They consistantly create albums that feature non-sensical songs right along side of meaningful songs about the human condition. Perhaps not the most brilliant of all groups, musically speaking, but they do evolve and create a distinct sound with every album that is uniquely their own, something rarely found in modern music.
    3. Gigi Dover
      A realatively obscure female artist who is curently touring with The Big Love (as last I heard). While she is realatively young and is signed to a small label, Gigi is one of the most powerful singers I have ever heard. She has the power of a jazz or big band singer, but doesn't allow her sound or talent to drown out her personality in her work. Most of the songs concern bumps in the road of life with a southern twist. When it comes to genere she defines herself as "sultry southern soul." Gigidover.com
    4. Pink Floyd
