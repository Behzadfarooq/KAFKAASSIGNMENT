[edit] creating page

i would like to create a page or stub as the case may be for a transit company in canada and so far everytime i have tried it gets deleted claiming that i am advertising or spamming or the company is not notable... most places have stubs for things like this even your rapid transit and bus company list requests us to build that stuff up t he one i was trying to create for is Timmins Transit if you can help me it would be greatly appreciated (Yonzie1972 23:20, 20 November 2006 (UTC))

Regarding Timmins Transit; I am a Public Transport enthusiast and I have never come across Timmins Transit. However, having said that it may be notable enough to consitute a Wikipedia article. The criteria for notability over companies is located at a subpage of WP:NOTABILITY (a guideline for notable of Wikipedia Articles). The subpage is Wikipedia:Notability/Companies and Corporations;


"...A product or service is notable if it meets any of the following criteria:(1)The product or service has been the subject of multiple non-trivial published works whose source is independent of the company itself. (2)This criterion includes published works in all forms, such as newspaper articles, books, television documentaries, and published reports by consumer watchdog organizations5 except for the following: (A) Media re-prints of press releases, other publications where the company or corporation talks about its products or services, and advertising for the product or service. Newspaper stories that do not credit a reporter or a news service and simply present company news in an uncritical or positive way may be treated as press releases unless there is evidence to the contrary.(B) Works carrying merely trivial coverage, such as simple price listings in product catalogues. (2)The product or service is so well-known that its trademark has suffered from genericization..." —Wikipedia:Notability/Companies and Corporations
