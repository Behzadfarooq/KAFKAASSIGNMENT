Antonio Álvarez Pérez "Ito", (born 21 January 1975 in Almendralejo) is a football (soccer) player from Spain. He plays midfielder for RCD Espanyol in La Liga.

[edit] Clubs

  • 1991-97: Extremadura
  • 1997-98: Celta de Vigo
  • 1998-04: Betis
  • 2004-Present: Espanyol

[edit] Honours

  • Winner Copa del Rey (Spanish League Cup) in 2006 with RCD Espanyol
