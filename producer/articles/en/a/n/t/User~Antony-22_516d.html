I'm a student at Caltech with a research interest in nanotechnology. I've been reading Wikipedia for about two years now, and I've decided it's time to put something back in. I generally spend my time improving the structure of existing articles (i.e. moving sections around) as well as adding information if it is relevant.

My other interests/hobbies include Star Trek, Latin, and amateur coin collecting.

[edit] Main article

When I first encountered the Nanotechnology article it had a lot of information but it was very unorganized and was biased more towards societal implications than the hard science. I've been spending a bunch of time lately giving the article a better structure and adding relevant information.
