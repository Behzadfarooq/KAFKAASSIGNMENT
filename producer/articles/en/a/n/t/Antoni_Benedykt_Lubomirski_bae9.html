Prince Antoni Benedykt Konstanty Lubomirski (?-1761) was a Polish szlachcic.

Owner of Połonne, Międzyrzecz and Miropol. General Major of the Crown Army, Lieutenant General since 1753, Great Sword-bearer of the Crown since 1754.

Marshal of the Sejm (zwyczajnego), October 3 - November 14, 1746.

Awarded with the Order of the White Eagle on August 3, 1757 in Warsaw
