Anthony Nash (born 1985) is an Irish sportsperson who is a member of the Cork senior hurling team.

Anthony Nash was born in Kanturk, County Cork in 1985. He plays hurling with his local Kanturk club as well as being a member of the Cork senior hurling panel as understudy to goalkeeper Donal Óg Cusack. He has yet to make his debut. Nash has had some success at inter-county level, winning two All-Ireland Intermediate medal in 2004 and 2006, as well as a Munster under-21 medal in 2005.
