Ante Čović [ˈaːntɛ ˈtʃɔːʋitɕ] (born June 13, 1975) is an Australian football (soccer) player of Croatian descent. He is a goalkeeper who currently plays for Newcastle United Jets in the Australian A-League. He formerly played for Hammarby in Sweden, as well as Marconi Stallions and Sydney Olympic in Australia's old National Soccer League, PAOK Salonika and AO Kavala in Greece and Dinamo Zagreb in Croatia. He was part of the Australian squad for the 2006 World Cup qualifiers against Uruguay and was Australia's third choice goalkeeper behind Mark Schwarzer and Željko Kalac for the 2006 World Cup in Germany. He made his debut for Australia against Bahrain on February 22, 2006 in an Asian Cup qualifier and, to date, this is his sole international cap.

Covic made his A-League debut in a 3-2 loss to Adelaide United in December 2006 at Hindmarsh Stadium.
