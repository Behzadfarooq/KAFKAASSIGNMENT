Anthony S. Fell is a Canadian businessman and chairman of RBC Capital Markets.

From 1980 to 1998, he was the Chief Executive Officer of RBC Dominion Securities Limited. He currently sits on the board of directors of multiple corporations, including Bell Canada Enterprises, CAE, and Loblaw Companies Ltd. In 2001, he was made an Officer of the Order of Canada.
