Fairly active editor of pages on Christianity and especially Eastern Christianity. I work quite a bit on the Eastern Orthodoxy WikiProject, including the overhaul of the Eastern Christianity Portal. I can sometimes be seen working through the Orthodoxy Stubs to help fill in missing material.

I alternate between life as an Exopedian and an Metapedian, depending on what is needed.
