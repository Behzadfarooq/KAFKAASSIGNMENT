Anthony Guidera

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Anthony Guidera is an American (US) actor from San Francisco who has appeared in many films and television series.

His first role was as a bodyguard in The Godfather Part III. He has also appeared in films such as Species, The Rock, [[The Postman]], Heist and Armageddon.

Guidera has also guest starred in television series such as Renegade, Baywatch, Star Trek: Deep Space Nine and Angel (in the episodes "The Circle" and "The Ring").
