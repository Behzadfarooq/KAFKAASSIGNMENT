Euforia Lifestyle TV, also known as Antena 4, is a television channel based in Bucharest, Romania. Launched in February 2006, it is part of the Intact group, owned by controversial businessman and politician Dan Voiculescu.

The TV channel is dedicated to femei de success, femei independente ("successful women, independent women"), being in direct competition with Media PRO Acasă TV channel.
