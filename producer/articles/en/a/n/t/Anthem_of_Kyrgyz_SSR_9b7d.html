The State Anthem of the Kyrgyz SSR (Kyrgyz: Кыргыз ССР Мамлекеттик Гимни) was the national anthem of Kyrgyzstan when it was a republic of the Soviet Union and known as the Kyrgyz SSR.

[edit] Background

The music was composed by V. Vlasov, A. Maldybaev, and V. Fere; and the words were written by K. Malikov, T. Sydykbekov, M. Tokobaev and A. Tokombaev.
