Antonio Rodriguez San Juan, (born April 12, 1957 in Maracaibo, state of Zulia) is a Venezuela Politician. He is the governor of the state of Vargas from 2000. He is a member of the Movimiento Quinta Republica (MVR) of Venezuela's president, Hugo Chávez.
