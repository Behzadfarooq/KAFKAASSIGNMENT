Antonio Madiera (born in February 1981) is a gay pornographic actor (porn star) from Los Angeles, United States (originally from Miami), who makes pornographic movies for a variety of gay porn studios.

Madiera is one of the most popular "twink" models in gay porn in the early 2000s, mostly due to his smooth and tanned body, his baby face (i.e. a face that resembles that of a young male rather than that of an adult man), giving him an overall youthful appearance. He is popular among certain viewers due to his above average sized penis, a little over 7 1/2 inches. He performs as both the top and bottom in anal sex.

Antonio had his porn debut in A Young Man's World, a movie released in 2001 by Delta Productions, and directed by Kevin Clarke. Clarke specializes in videos with young men, and Madiera was a perfect fit in that category. After his career start at Delta Video, Madiera performed in several videos directed by his boyfriend and fellow porn star Chad Donovan, for a variety of studios. As of September 2006, Antonio had performed in eighteen movies.

The October 2002 issue of Freshmen magazine had Antonio as both the cover and centerfold model. In addition to his porn career, Antonio studies biology.
