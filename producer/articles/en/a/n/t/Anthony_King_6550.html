Anthony King (born June 6, 1975, in Myrtle Beach, South Carolina, U.S.A.) is an American writer, director, and comedian based in New York City. He is the Artistic Director of the Upright Citizens Brigade Theatre in New York City.

King wrote the off-Broadway musical Gutenberg! The Musical! with Scott Brown.

King attended the University of North Carolina in Chapel Hill, North Carolina.
