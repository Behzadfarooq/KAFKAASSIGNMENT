ANTICONFORMITY Live CD/DVD
Live album by Krystal Meyers
Genre Christian rock

ANTICONFORMITY Live CD/DVD is a live performance captured on CD and DVD from Christian punk/rock artist Krystal Meyers. Release date is not yet confirmed but expected around spring 2007. The CD will feature the full concert (15 tracks) plus five bonus acoustic studio sessions. The DVD will feature the full concert plus a music video reel.
