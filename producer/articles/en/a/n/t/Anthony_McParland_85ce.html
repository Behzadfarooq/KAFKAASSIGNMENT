Anthony McParland (born September 20, 1982 in Rutherglen) is a Scotland football player currently playing for Wycombe Wanderers.

He is a Defender or midfielder formerly played for Celtic F.C. In the 2006 January transfer window he completed a transfer to Barnsley. He appeared in eight substitute appearances for Barnsley and joined Wycombe initially on loan in January 2007 before making the move permanent later in the month.

He is a former Scotland under 17,18 and 21 international.
