Antonieta is a 1982 film by spanish director Carlos Saura, starring Isabelle Adjani and Hanna Schygulla, based on the novel by Andrés Henestrosa.

The plot is about an investigation made by a French psychologist on famous suicidal women. She finds the case of Antonieta Rivas Mercado, a Mexican writer who died inside Paris' Notre Dame in 1931.
