Anthony Wong (Chinese: 黃耀明), born on June 16, 1962, is a Hong Kong alternative singer, composer and producer who was a member of a well-known duo Tat Ming Pair in the 80’s.

[edit] Biography

Anthony Wong began his career as a DJ at Commercial Radio Hong Kong in 1984. In the following year, he answered a singer-wanted advertisement in a music magazine posted by a composer and guitarist Tats Lau Yi Tat. They then formed the group Tat Ming Pair and later was signed by Polygram. After the group was disbanded in 1990, he continued his musical career as a solo singer and later also as a producer. In the mid 90’s, he reached the peak of his musical career. With songs like ‘’Blow Up’’ (春光乍洩), 每日一禁果 (literally “A Forbidden Fruit-Once a Day”), he received many prestigious musical awards.

In 1999, he founded a music production company called People Mountain People Sea (人山人海) with a group of very talented artists and musicians in Hong Kong. The company produced and published albums not only for high-profiled Pop singers like Leslie Cheung, Faye Wong, Sandy Lam, Eason Chan , Nicholas Tse and Miriam Yeung, but also published works for other relatively “alternative/ experimental” singers, composers, bands and groups, such as at17 and PixelToy.
