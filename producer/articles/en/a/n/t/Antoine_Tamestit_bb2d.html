Antoine Tamestit

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Antoine Tamestit is a French violist.

Tamestit, who studied at the Paris Conservatory, has performed at such venues as the Royal Concertgebouw, the Vienna Musikverein, and Carnegie Hall. He won first prize at the 2000 Maurice Vieux International Viola Competition, 2001 William Primrose International Viola Competition, and 2004 Munich International Music Competition.
