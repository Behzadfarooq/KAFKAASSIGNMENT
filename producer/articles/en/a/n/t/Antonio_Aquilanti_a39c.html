Antonio Aquilanti (born 8 November 1985 in Lanciano) is an Italian football (soccer) defender. He currently plays for Pescara.

[edit] Football career

He started his professional career at Pescara Calcio, also in Abruzzo.

He was signed by Fiorentina in co-ownership deal, on summer 2004, played with their Primavera team.

He return to Pescara in summer 2005, and Pescara bought back him at summer 2006.

[edit] International career

He was the member of Italian U-20 team at 2005 FIFA World Youth Championship.
