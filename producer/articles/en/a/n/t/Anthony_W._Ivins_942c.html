Anthony Ivins

Anthony Woodward Ivins (September 16, 1852 – September 23, 1934) born in Toms River, New Jersey, was a high-ranking official of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints.

He was ordained an apostle on October 6, 1907, after the passing of George Teasdale. He was succeeded by Alonzo A. Hinckley.

Ivins was an avid Democrat, and active in the Utah Democratic Party.
