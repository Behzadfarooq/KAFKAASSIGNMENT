[edit] About myself

My name is Anthony Ivanoff, I'm 18 years old. I live in Chelyabinsk and study at the South Ural State University, one of the largest in Russia. I use Linux and I love AMD. From time to time, I make small edits in wikipedia.

My English is still quite bad, so feel free to correct my edits.
