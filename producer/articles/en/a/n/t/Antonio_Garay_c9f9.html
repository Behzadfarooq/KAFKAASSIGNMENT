Antonio Garay (born November 30, 1979 in Rahway, New Jersey) is an American football defensive tackle on the Chicago Bears.

He was originally selected with the 22nd pick of the sixth round of the 2003 NFL Draft by the Cleveland Browns out of Boston College.

His parents are from Peru and Puerto Rico.[citation needed]

[edit] High School Years

Garay attended Rahway High School in Rahway, New Jersey, and was a standout Offensive and Defensive lineman in football and wrestling.

In football, he was a Prep Star All-American.

In wrestling, he was the 1998 275 pound class New Jersey State and National Champion.
