Antigonish Arena

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Antigonish Arena is a 2,290-seat multi-purpose arena in Antigonish, Nova Scotia, Canada. It is home to both the Junior A and Junior B Antigonish Bulldogs hockey teams. The arena was home to the St. Francis Xavier X-Men and X-Women hockey teams before they moved into a new on-campus arena. It Is an awesome rink. Some teams who play there are: Pee-Wee AAA, AA, A, Bantam AAA, AA, A, And Minor and Major Midget.
