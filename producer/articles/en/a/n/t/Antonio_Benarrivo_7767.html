Antonio Benarrivo (born 21 August 1968 in Brindisi) is a retired Italian footballer. Primarily a full-back, he played many times on both flanks, and was also frequently employed as a makeshift wide midfielder due to his pace and technique.

He played most of his career for AC Parma.

He played for Italy national football team (23 matches) and was a participant at the 1994 FIFA World Cup, where Italy were the losing finalists.
