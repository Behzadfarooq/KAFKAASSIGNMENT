António Folha

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

António Folha (born May 21, 1971) is a former Portuguese football (soccer) player, currently assistant coach of F.C. Penafiel.

In career he played mostly for F.C. Porto. Folha has also played for AEK Athens FC in the 2001/02 season. For Portugal national football team he was in roster for Euro 1996.
