[edit] Summary

A rally in favor of the the "yes" option in the 2004 recall referendum against Hugo Chávez in the capital, Caracas.

The photographer is Carlos Granier-Phelps. He was contacted via email on 12/6/05 to confirm Creative Commons Attribution ShareAlike License v. 2.5. More pictures and license information on at http://www.carlanga.com/
