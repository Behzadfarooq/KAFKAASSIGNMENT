Philip the Arab. 244-249 AD. AR Antoninianus (4.30 gm). Struck 248 AD.

IMP PHILIPPVS AVG, radiate, draped and cuirassed bust right.
SAECVLVM NOVVM, Roma seated within hexastyle temple.

RIC IV 25b; RSC 198.

Coin from Wildwinds.
