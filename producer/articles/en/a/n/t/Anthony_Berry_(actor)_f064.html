Anthony Berry is an American actor.

[edit] Chappelle Show

He made a few appearances on Chappelle Show, once as the "Holla Guy", another time as the internet penis growth doctor, and several others, in season one and two. He also served as Jeb Bush in the Black Bush skit, though he is referred to as "Some Black Dude".
