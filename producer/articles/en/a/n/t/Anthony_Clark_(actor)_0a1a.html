Anthony Clark (actor)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Anthony Clark (b. April 4, 1964, Lynchburg, Virginia), is an American actor and comedian who starred in the television series Yes, Dear, in which he played the character of Greg Warner.

Previously Clark starred in the television comedy series Boston Common and Soul Man. Before that he had a recurring role on another comedy series, Ellen.

Along with Mike O'Malley, Anthony appears in Alan Jackson's music video for "The Talkin' Song Repair Blues."

In March 2006, Clark was named by NBC as the new host of reality competition series Last Comic Standing, which aired in the summer of 2006.

Clark is also said to be a homosexual.

Clark was also cast in the drama/comedy film Say Uncle.

Clark appeared in The Rock (1996) as Paul, the homosexual hotel barber.
