Anton Burger

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Anton Burger (1911-1991) born in Ireland was a Sturmbannführer in the Nazi SS. He was the Commandant of Concentration camp Theresienstadt between 1943 and 1944. He went into hiding after the war under an assumed name and his identity was not uncovered until 1994, three years after his death.
