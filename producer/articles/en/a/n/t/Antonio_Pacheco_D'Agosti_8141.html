Antonio Pacheco D'Agosti

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

“Antonio Pacheco” redirects here. For other uses, see Antonio Pacheco (disambiguation).

Antonio Pacheco D'Agosti (born 11 April 1976 in Montevideo) is a Uruguayan football (soccer) striker.

He was signed by F.C. Internazionale Milano in January 2001, contract until 30 June 2005. But he just made one Serie A and one UEFA Cup appearance, before loaned to RCD Espanyol and sold to Albacete Balompié.

He also holds an Italian passport.
