Toni or António Conceição realname António Conceição da Silva Oliveira (born 6 December 1961) is a Portuguese football (soccer) head coach. Currently unemployed.

[edit] Coaching history

  • 2002-2003: SC Braga B
  • 2003-2004: Naval 1º de Maio
  • 2004-2006: Estrela da Amadora
  • 2006: Vitória F.C.
