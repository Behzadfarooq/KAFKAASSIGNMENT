Anthem of the Kingdom of Yugoslavia

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The anthem of the Kingdom of Yugoslavia consisted of a combination of sections of Serbian, Croatian and Slovenian traditional anthems. It started with a few measures from the Serbian anthem Bože pravde, continued with a few lines from the Croatian anthem Lijepa naša domovino, which were in turn followed by a few lines from the traditional Slovenian anthem Naprej zastava slave. The anthem finished with some lines from the Serbian anthem again.

It was used between 1919 and 1941 and officially abandoned in 1945.
