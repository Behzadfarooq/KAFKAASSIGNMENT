Anthony DeRosa

Anthony DeRosa is a Disney animator. He attended the Character Animation program at the California Institute of the Arts.

[edit] Supervising Animator

  • Adult Nala - The Lion King (1994)
  • Nakoma - Pocahontas (1995)
  • Zeus, Hera - Hercules (1997)
  • Sprite (Firebird Suite) - Fantasia 2000
  • Moliere - Atlantis: The Lost Empire

[edit] Assistant Animator

  • The Black Cauldron (1985)
  • Oliver & Company (1988
  • The Little Mermaid (1989)
  • Beauty and the Beast (1991)
