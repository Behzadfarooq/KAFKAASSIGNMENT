Ants In The Pantry (1936) is the 12th of Columbia Pictures' 190 short subjects starring the comedy team of the Three Stooges.

[edit] Plot

The Stooges are pest exterminators who decide to drum up business by planting mice, moths, and ants in an unsuspecting house. The Stooges select in a fancy mansion where a high society dinner party is being held. After successfully infesting the house with pests, the Stooges are hired to clean up their own mess without interrupting the party.

[edit] Notes

This is the first short in which Moe attempts to kiss a woman and accidentally kisses Curly instead, followed by Moe screaming, "I'm poisoned!" and striking Curly. This would be a recurring joke in Stooge shorts.
