Antoine-Denis Chaudet

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Chaudet's Cupid in the Louvre

Antoine-Denis Chaudet (1763 - 1810) was a French sculptor who worked in a neoclassical style.


[edit] Works

  • Joseph Sold by his Brothers (bas-relief, 1784)
  • The infant Oedipus and Phorbas (1799)Shown at the Salon of 1801, it was completed by Pierre Cartellier (1757–1831) and Louis-Marie Dupaty (1771–1825) after Chaudet's death.
  • Peace (c. 1800 - 1810)
  • Busts of Emperor Napoleon and Empress Joséphine (exhibited 1811)
  • L'Amour (Cupid and the Butterfly), completed posthumously by Pierre Cartellier (1817), (Louvre Museum)
