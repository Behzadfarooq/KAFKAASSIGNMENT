[edit] Australian judges

To avoid needlessly replicating discussion across many talk pages, I'll post it here once.

The fact that the High Court is the highest in the Australian court hierarchy is useful and relevant information, which provides context to the articles on the judges. It's particularly important for those unfamiliar with the Australian court system. Further, the fact that article A includes something that article B does not is not a good reason to remove it from A, but rather a good reason to add it to B. --bainer (talk) 13:01, 15 July 2006 (UTC)
