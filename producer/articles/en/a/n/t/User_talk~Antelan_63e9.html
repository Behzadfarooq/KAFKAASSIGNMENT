[edit] Nominating articles for deletion with pre-existing debates

Hi. I notice that you were trying to append the closed debate Wikipedia:Articles for deletion/Autocoitus to today's AfD roster. If you do intend to renominate the article for deletion, please follow the instructions on WP:AFD for renomination. In brief,

  1. Add {{subst:afdx|2nd}} on Autocoitus,
  2. Use the "preloaded debate" link on the resulting box to write your nomination, and
  3. On Wikipedia:Articles for deletion/Log/2007 March 29, use {{Wikipedia:Articles for deletion/Autocoitus (2nd nomination)}} instead of {{Wikipedia:Articles for deletion/Autocoitus}}.

As a general rule, closed debate pages should not be edited. â€” Kaustuv Chaudhuri 06:29, 29 March 2007 (UTC)
