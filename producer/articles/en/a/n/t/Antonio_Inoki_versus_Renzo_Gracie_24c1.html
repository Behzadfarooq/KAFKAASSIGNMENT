Antonio Inoki versus Renzo Gracie was a professional wrestling match that was held on the 2000 edition of the "Inoki Bom-Ba-Ye" event on December 31st, as the main event. It was also the first and only worked match featuring Renzo Gracie. Till now it is the only worked match involving someone from the Gracie family, as the Gracie family actually despise professional wrestling and worked matches. The match lasted mere several minutes and had ended in a draw. The card had featured a total of nine pro-wrestling matches, in contrast to later Inoki Bom-Ba-Ye events which featured only mixed martial arts matches.

The card featured other matches such as:

  • 1. Yoshiaki Fujiwara vs. Justin McCully
  • 2. Great Sasuke and Daijiro Matsui vs. Caol Uno and Akira Shoji
  • 3. Bas Rutten and Alexander Otsuka vs. Naoki Sano and Ricco Rodriguez
  • 4. Shinya Hashimoto vs. Gary Goodridge
  • 5. Naoya Ogawa vs. Tadao Yasuda
  • 6. Mark Coleman and Mark Kerr vs. Yuji Nagata and Takashi Iizuka
  • 7. Kazushi Sakuraba vs. Kendo Ka Shin
  • 8. Keiji Mutoh and Nobuhiko Takada vs. Ken Shamrock and Don Frye
