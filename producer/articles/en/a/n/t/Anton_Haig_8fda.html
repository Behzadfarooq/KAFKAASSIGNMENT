Anton Haig

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Anton Jason Haig (born 8 May 1986) is a South African golfer.

He turned professional on 1 September 2004 and plays on Sunshine Tour and the Asian Tour. He has won four times worldwide with his biggest win to date occuring at the multi-tour sanctioned Johnnie Walker Classic in March 2007. This victory earned him full European Tour membership and propelled Haig into the top 100 of the Official World Golf Rankings. [1]

[edit] Professional wins

  • 2005 Seekers Travel Pro-Am (Sunshine Tour)
  • 2006 Malaysian Masters (Asian Tour), MTC Namibia PGA Championship (Sunshine Tour)
  • 2007 Johnnie Walker Classic (co-sanctioned by European Tour, Asian Tour, Australasian Tour)
