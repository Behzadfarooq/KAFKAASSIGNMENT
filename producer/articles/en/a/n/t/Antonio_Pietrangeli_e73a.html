Antonio Pietrangeli

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Antonio Pietrangeli ( Rome, January 19, 1919 - Gaeta, July 12, 1968 ) was a Italian film director.

[edit] Biografia

He started writting film reviews for famous Italian Cinema magazines such as Bianco e nero and Cinema (magazine). Pietrangeli was script writer in Ossessione directed by Visconti, Fabiola by Blasetti, Europa '51 by Rossellini or La lupa di Lattuada.

His film debut is Il sole negli occhi (1953).

Pietrangeli was a major film making of the Italian genre Commedia all'italiana He died filming in 1969 Come, quando, perché
