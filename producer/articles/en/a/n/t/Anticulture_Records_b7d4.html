Founded 2003

Genre(s) Heavy metal
Country of origin England
Location Northampton, England and Tours, France
Official Website http://www.anticulture.co.uk

Anticulture Records are a record label based in Northampton, England which deals mostly with heavy metal music. In 2006 a European office was started in Tours, France.

The first release for Anticulture was Gutworm's 'Ruin The Memory' album in April 2004.
