[edit] Timetable images

Hello,

You uploaded a bunch of images of airline timetables and marked them as {{PD}}, saying that timetableimages.com allows users to use them as free. There are a few issues with that:

  • If timetableimages.com allowed users to use them for free, the tag would be {{CopyrightedFreeUse}}, not {{PD}}.
  • timetableimages.com's copyright statement actually reads as:

You may use our images (marked BL or DZ) on another website. Then please credit them as being from the collections of either Björn Larsson or David Zekria, and preferably provide a link to our site. Thank you! Please note that an image of a timetable or other item marked as belonging to another collector may not be used without prior permission from its owner.

  • timetableimages.com doesn't own the copyright to those images in the first place -- the timetables were created by the airlines. There may be a fair use claim on those images, but they are certainly not public domain if created after 1923.

Please let me know if you have any questions. Thanks. kmccoy (talk) 17:57, 1 August 2005 (UTC)

I'll be happy to answer any more questions you might have about images, to the best of my ability. :)
