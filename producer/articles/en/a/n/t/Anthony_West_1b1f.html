Anthony West (August 4, 1914 - December 27, 1987) was a British author, the illegitimate son of British authors Rebecca West and H. G. Wells. Anthony West's best-known book is H.G. Wells: Aspects of a Life, a biography of his father. Not to be confused with the Irish author Anthony C. West. There is also a professional motorcycle rider of the same name - see Anthony West (motorcycle racer).
