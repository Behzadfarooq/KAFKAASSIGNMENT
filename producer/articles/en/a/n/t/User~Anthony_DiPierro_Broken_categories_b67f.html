[edit] Intro

Unlike broken article links, broken category links are never acceptable. All categories should either have a parent, or be listed under category:fundamental. As an interim solution some categories sit under category:orphaned categories awaiting further categorization.

If you fix something (either by parenting the category or by removing the category reference in the article), feel free to strike it out or delete it altogether. But don't update the time below, that represents the last time the entire database was scanned.
