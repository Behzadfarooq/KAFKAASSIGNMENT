Antiquities and Monuments Office

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Antiquities and Monuments Office (Chinese: 古物古蹟辦事處; pinyin: Gǔwù Gǔjī Bànshìchù) was established when the Antiquities and Monuments Ordinance was enforced in 1976 which to be preserve Hong Kong's monuments are under appropriate protection.Its head office is now housed in Former Kowloon British School.

Leisure and Cultural Service Department now manages the Office.

Hong Kong Heritage Discovery Centre is under the management of the office.
