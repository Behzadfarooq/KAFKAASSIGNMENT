Anthony Petit

Anthony Petit (d. 1946) was an American lighthouse keeper. In 1945 he was assigned to the Scotch Cap Light in Alaska as the head of a five-man crew. All of the men were killed on April 1, 1946 when a massive tsunami struck the station, destroying it.

The United States Coast Guard has named a USCG Coastal Buoy Tender (WLM 558) based in Ketchikan, Alaska in his honor.
