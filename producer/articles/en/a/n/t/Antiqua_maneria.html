Antiqua maneria

The Antiqua maneria were the original 17 manors belonging to of the Earldom of Cornwall.

After March 1337 these manors passed to the new Duchy of Cornwall which was created by King Edward III to give financial support to his son Edward, the Black Prince (1330 - 1376).

[edit] The list of 17 Antiqua maneria

  • Rillaton in Linkinhorne Parish
  • Stoke Climsland
  • Liskeard
  • Helston in Trigg
  • Tybesta, in Creed
  • Tywarnhaile in St Agnes Parish
  • Talskiddy in St Columb Major Parish
  • Penmayne
  • Calstock
  • Trematon Castle
  • Restormel Castle
  • Penketh
  • Tewington in Treverbyn Parish
  • Helstone in Kirrier
  • Tintagel
  • Moresk near Truro
