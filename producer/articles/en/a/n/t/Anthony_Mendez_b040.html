Anthony Mendez is a Hispanic-American voice-over actor. He was born in the Bronx, New York in 1973. A bilingual (Spanish and English) voice-actor, he was the voice for the nationally syndicated TV show "American Latino TV" in its first season. A baritone, Mendez has voiced several commercial advertisements and non-broadcast projects for organizations like Western Union, in English, and campaigns for the New York City Department of Health, in Spanish. Most recently, he voiced a TV commercial for X Games 12 (in Spanish).

A New-York-based voice-over, Mendez lives in New Jersey with his family, works out of his recording studio in Jersey City, as well as several other studios in New York City. He also works on projects via ISDN, which allows him the ability to digitally deliver his voice in real-time to any recording studio in the world.

Tech-savvy, the former architecture student, turned music engineer, turned voice-over actor, designed and maintains his own website. He has been quoted as saying, "I realize that everything in life happens for a reason... if it wasn't for my studying architecture, I would have never developed the ability to speak without fear and see things with a fresh eye... if it weren't for the people I met in college, I would have never entered into the music business... and if it weren't for the people I met in music, especially publicists, I would have never landed my first TV voice-over gig... things definitely have a way of leading you in the right direction - you're responsibility is just to make sure your headlights are on."
