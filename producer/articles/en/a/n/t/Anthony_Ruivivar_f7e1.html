Anthony Ruivivar

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Anthony Michael Ruivivar (born November 4, 1970 in Honolulu, Hawaii) is an American actor best known for his role in the TV series Third Watch. He is married to Yvonne Jung (January 1998 - present), his Third Watch show partner and they have 2 children.

[edit] Trivia

Son, Kainoa born 28 August 2002 and daughter Levi was born on 3 May 2006.

His father is of Filipino, Chinese and Spanish descent, and his mother is German and Scottish.

He received his Bachelor of Fine Arts from Boston University.
