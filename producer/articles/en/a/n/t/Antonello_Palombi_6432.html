Antonello Palombi

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

You have new messages (last change).

Antonello Palombi took over the role of Radames at the opening of the 2006-07 season at La Scala on 7 December 2006 in the new production of Aida by Franco Zeffirelli. During the second performance (10 December 2006), Roberto Alagna was "booed" and "whistled" by the loggione (opera fans who sit in the less-expensive seats at the very back of the Scala), and walked off the stage. Palombi entered on stage wearing jeans and a black shirt.
