Antonio Mora (born December 14, 1957 in Havana, Cuba) has been a news anchor at WBBM-TV in Chicago since 2002 and is the first Hispanic to serve as a main anchor at an English language Chicago network-owned station. Prior to joining WBBM, he worked at ABC, where he was the news anchor for "Good Morning America". He was the original anchor for Good Day L.A. on KTTV in Los Angeles.

He also had a small cameo appearance in the 1994 film Speed starring Sandra Bullock.
