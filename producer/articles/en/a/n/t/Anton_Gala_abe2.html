Anton Gala (March 12, 1891, Kunovice – August 29, 1977, Bratislava) was a Slovak university professor and scientist specializing in ophthalmology.

Gala studied gymnasium at Uherské Hradiště (1910), then medicine at the Charles University in Prague (1916). During World War I he served on a hospital ship. From 1919 to 1961 he worked at the faculty of medicine of Charles University in Bratislava; from 1931 as professor, in 1937/38 as dean of the faculty.
