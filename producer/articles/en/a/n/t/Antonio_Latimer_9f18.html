Antonio Latimer (born in November 26, 1978 in Rio Piedras) is a Puerto Rican professional basketball player. Latimer has played in the NCAA and the National Superior Basketball League of Puerto Rico (BSN) with Coamo Marathon Runners, Myaguez Tainos, and Bayamon Cowboys. Latimer has also played internationally in Israel and Spain. Latimer was a member of the Puerto Rican National Basketball Team.

[edit] Brief Biography

Latimer spent his college years playing for Southern Missouri. He has played professionally in the National Superior Basketball League of Puerto Rico since 1994. Latimer has been a member of Puerto Rican National Basketball Team in 2002 and 2006.
