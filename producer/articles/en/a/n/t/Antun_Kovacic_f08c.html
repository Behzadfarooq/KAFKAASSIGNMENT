Antun Kovacic (born July 10, 1981) is an Australian football (soccer) player who currently plays central defence for the Melbourne Victory in the Australian A-League.

The Victory recruited Kovacic from Victorian Premier League club Oakleigh Cannons as a short-term replacement for the injured Daniel Piorkowski.

The 25-year-old has had severeal years in the now defunct National Soccer League with the Melbourne Knights playing along side current Victory stars such Rodrigo Vargas, Adrian Leijer, Steve Pantelidis and Piorkowski.

At the end of the 2006-07 home and away season, Kovacic has made two appearances, both of the substitutes bench.
