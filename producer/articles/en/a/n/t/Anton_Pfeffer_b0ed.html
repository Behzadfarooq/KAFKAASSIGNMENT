Anton Pfeffer

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Anton Pfeffer (born August 17, 1965 in Lilienfeld) is a retired Austrian footballer.

He participated in the World Cups in 1990 and 1998.

[edit] Clubs

  • youth-1985: SV Türnitz
  • 1985-1998: Austria Wien
