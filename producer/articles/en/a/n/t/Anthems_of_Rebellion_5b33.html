Anthems of Rebellion is the fifth studio album by the Swedish melodic death metal band Arch Enemy. It is the second album to feature the vocals of Angela Gossow.

[edit] Bonus Disc

In numerous regions, a bonus dvd was supplied with the initial release in 3 forms. Track listings would be identical, save the EU jewel case edition, which omitted Exist to Exit (5.1). The U.S. jewel case release and EU digipak included all tracks available on the releases.
