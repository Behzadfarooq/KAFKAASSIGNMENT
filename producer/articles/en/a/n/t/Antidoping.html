Antidoping

Antidoping is a mexican reggae band formed in the summer of 1992 in Mexico City. It fuses roots reggae, ska and caribbean rhythms with an urban flavor.

Antidoping has been an active member of the Razteca reggae movement in Mexico, sharing the stage on several occasions with up to 18,000 thousand spectators. It has also toured Europe with bands of great international renown.
