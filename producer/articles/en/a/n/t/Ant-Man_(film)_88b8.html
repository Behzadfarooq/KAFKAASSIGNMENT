Ant-Man is an announced superhero film based on the fictional Marvel Comics character Ant-Man. Ant-Man will be directed by Edgar Wright of Shaun of the Dead fame. The script is written by Wright and Joe Cornish. The movie will be produced by Avi Arad, financed by Marvel Studios, and released by Paramount Pictures.

[edit] Plot outline

Edgar Wright plans to direct an action-adventure film with comedic elements, insisting that Ant-Man would not be a spoof.[1] Wright also plans to include both Henry Pym and Scott Lang as major characters, with Pym as Ant-Man in the 60's in Tales to Astonish style, and a flashforward to Lang as Ant-Man's successor in contemporary times.[2]
