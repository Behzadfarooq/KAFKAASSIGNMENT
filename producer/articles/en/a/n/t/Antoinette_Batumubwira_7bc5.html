Antoinette Batumubwira (born 23 May 1956) is a Burundian politician. She has been a foreign minister of the country since August, 2005. She travelled in Paris on October 9th 2006 where she met Mrs. Brigitte Giradin, currently Ministre déléguée à la Coopération, au Développement et à la Francophonie
