Anthony "Raptor" Raso (born April 17, 1987) is an Australian rules footballer in the Australian Football League.

He was recruited as the number 77 draft pick in the 2004 AFL Draft from Mt. Eliza. Aged only seventeen at the time, he was regarded as highly skilled, but clearly not yet built to play senior football. Nevertheless, there was speculation that he would be taken high in the following draft, so the Carlton Football Club took him on that basis. He spent almost all of the 2005 and the first half of 2006 playing for the Northern Bullants reserves team, although gladular fever ruined his preparation. He earned a place in the Bullants seniors in mid 2006, but he is yet to play for Carlton.
