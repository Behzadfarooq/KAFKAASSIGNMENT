Anthony Houston Moran is a 13-year old boy who lives in Norfolk, Virginia. He is currently a user of Wikipedia, and owns a Nintendo GameCube.


[edit] Summary

 Anthony was born on July 21, 1993.

His known relatives are Robbin Moran(mother),Justine Moran (sister),Jason Dascoli(brother),Isabella (niece who was named after Hurricane Isabel but is mostly referred to as Bella), and Mellisa Jobes (sister-in-law). His other relatives are unknown. Anthony makes his own series based on himself with famous video game characters. In the story, he bears a quick resemblence to Link and even has a sword called the Moran Sword (ironically, it looks like Link's Master Sword).

[edit] AMAQ series

The letters AMAQ stand for Anthony Moran's Amazing Quest, the series that Anthony makes himself. There is only 1 out of 10 books completed, but will be completed in 2007.He teams up with many famous video game stars from different series. He also fights some of their villians.
