Antonia de Sancha (born September 14, 1961) is an actress known to have had an affair with British Conservative Member of Parliament David Mellor.

She sold her story of her affair with Mellor for Â£30,000, but the furore that followed its publication left her deeply unhappy. "It was emotional rape," de Sancha told an interviewer ten years after the scandal. She has scarcely had any work as an actress since the scandal, and considers the scandal as the reason a subsequent marriage failed.
