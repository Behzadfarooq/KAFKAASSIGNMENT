George Anthony Hylton (born 27 April 1957) is a Jamaican politician currently serving as Foreign minister. Hylton has been Member of Parliament with the People's National Party from Western Saint Thomas Parish, Jamaica since 1993. He has held other portfolios including Minister Foreign trade as well as Mining and Energy. He was educated at, among others, Georgetown University Law center and Morgan State University.
