Ants'hillvania is an Agapeland related album that was written by Jimmy and Carol Owens with Cherry Boone O'Neil. It is well known for being full of "Ant"-puns. It was nominated for a Grammy Award for Best Album for Children for the Grammy Awards of 1982 [1]. It had a sequal titled Ants'hillvania II: The Honeydew Adventure.
