Ante Ledić is Croatian businessman and politician from Zagreb in Croatia.

He was never a member of a political party, but he was very close to Franjo Tuđman and Croatian Democratic Union. His first entry to politics happened during the Zagreb Crisis when he was proposed by Tuđman as a compromise candidate for Zagreb mayor.

In 2000 he ran for Croatian president as independent candidate, finishing at 5th place.
