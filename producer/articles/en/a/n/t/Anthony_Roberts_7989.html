Anthony John Roberts (b. 19 April 1970) is an Australian politician and member of the New South Wales Legislative Assembly.

Roberts was educated at Saint Ignatius' College, Riverview and the University of Technology, Sydney (UTS). He worked in the office of the Prime Minister, John Howard from 1992 to 1996. He served on Lane Cove Council between 1995 and 2003, including a period serving as Mayor. He has represented Lane Cove for the Liberal Party of Australia since 2003.[1]
