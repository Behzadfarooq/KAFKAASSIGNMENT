Antara Mali is an Indian actress who stars in Bollywood films. She is the daughter of noted photographer Jagdish Mali. Mali made her acting debut in Dhoondte Reh Jaaoge. She would later play in movies like Premakatha [1] (directed by Ram Gopal Varma [2]). Varma was her mentor and made many movies with her, as he did with Urmila Matondkar.
