The Anonymex is a group of Wikipedia vandals that was started by Anonymous6 in early 2005. Their symbol is a picture of Colin Mochrie next to a picture of Darth Vader. These pictures were used for vandalism by Anonymous6 or one of his associates. His vandalism ended soon; however, after he was permanetly banned from Wikipedia. Most of his helpers decided to stop their vandalism after this event. Anonymous6 is considered a very minor vandal of Wikipedia. Anonymex has refered to anyone who is against it as an "imbentern". This is a portmanteau of the word "imbecile" and the German word "Hintern," which means "buttocks".

[edit] Rumors

There is a rumor that Annoymous6 will return under a new name when an insider of his has become an administrator, at which point the insider will attempt to ban all other administrators and control Wikipedia with Anonymous6. This is; however, an absurd plan as there are protections against such things.
