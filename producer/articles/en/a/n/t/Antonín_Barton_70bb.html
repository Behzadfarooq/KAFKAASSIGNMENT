Antonín Barton was a Czechoslovakian nordic skier who competed in the 1930's. He won two silver medals at the 1933 FIS Nordic World Ski Championships in Innsbruck (Nordic combined and 4 x 10 km).

Barton finished 6th in the nordic combined event at the 1932 Winter Olympics in Lake Placid, New York.
