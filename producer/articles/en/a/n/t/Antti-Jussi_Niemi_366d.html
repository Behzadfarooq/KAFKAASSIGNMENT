Antti-Jussi Niemi (born September 22, 1977 in Vantaa, Finland) is a Finnish professional ice hockey defenceman. He was drafted by the Ottawa Senators as their fourth-round pick, #81 overall, in the 1996 NHL Entry Draft.

Niemi, a defensive minded rearguard who plays with an edge, has won two SM-liiga gold medals with Jokerit, the Elitserien championship with Frölunda HC in 2005, and has represented Finland at several Ice Hockey World Championships, as well as the 2006 Winter Olympics. He has played 29 NHL games with the Mighty Ducks of Anaheim.
