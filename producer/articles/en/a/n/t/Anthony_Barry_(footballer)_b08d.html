Anthony Barry (born May 29, 1986) is an English footballer (soccer player). He plays midfield.

He signed for Everton as a trainee and when released signed up with Coventry City where he was a regular in the Reserves. He joined Accrington Stanley in 2005 before his move to Yeovil Town.
