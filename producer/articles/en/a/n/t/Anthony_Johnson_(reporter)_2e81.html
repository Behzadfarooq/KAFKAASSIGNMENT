Anthony Johnson (reporter)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Anthony Johnson on WABC in 2006.

Anthony Johnson is a New Jersey reporter for WABC-TV in New York. In December 2005, a deranged fan of the Opie and Anthony radio programme, attacked him while he was doing a live shot. He is married to WNBC reporter Pat Battle. He joined WABC-TV in January 2001 from 1010 WINS radio where he was a reporter from 1992-2001.
