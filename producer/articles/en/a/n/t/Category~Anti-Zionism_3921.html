Category:Anti-Zionism

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

See also Categories: Antisemitism


Anti-Zionism is a term used to describe several different political and religious points of view. Anti-Zionist positions have in common some form of opposition to Zionism, but their diversity in terms of motivation and expression is so great that anti-Zionism cannot be seen as a single phenomenon.
