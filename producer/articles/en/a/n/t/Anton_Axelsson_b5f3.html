Per Roger Anton Axelsson (born January 16, 1986, in Gothenburg, Sweden), is a professional Swedish ice hockey player. He is currently a left winger for the Frölunda Indians in the Swedish Elite League.

Anton is the younger brother of Per Johan Axelsson.

[edit] Playing career

Axelsson was drafted 192th overall in the 2004 NHL Entry Draft by the Detroit Red Wings while playing for Frölunda HC U-20 team.

He advanced to the senior team for the 2005-2006 season.

He is often compared to his older brother, strong on his skates, good hockey sense and plays responsible two way game.
