Anthony Stewart (rugby league footballer)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Anthony Stewart (born 05/03/79) is a winger for the Salford City Reds rugby league team.

He began at Hindley Juniors before joining Saints.

Anthony signed for Salford on loan from St Helens in 2004. He signed permanently in 2005. He has represented Ireland and Lancashire.
