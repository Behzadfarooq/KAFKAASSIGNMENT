Antonio López-Istúriz White

Antonio López-Istúriz White (born on 1 April 1970 in Pamplona) is a Spanish politician and Member of the European Parliament with the People's Party, Member of the Bureau of the European People's Party and sits on the European Parliament's Committee on Legal Affairs.

He is a substitute for the Committee on Transport and Tourism, a member of the Delegation to the ACP-EU Joint Parliamentary Assembly and a substitute for the Delegation for relations with the countries of south-east Europe.
