Anthony da Silva, nickname Tony (b. 20 December 1980 in Le Creusot, France) is a France born Portuguese football player who played for CFR Cluj in defender role.

He started his career at G.D. Chaves a team from his family's hometown of Chaves, Portugal and moved his way up to the Superliga.

On 31st of May 2006 he was rumoured to be in talks with Steaua Bucharest, but finally jointed CFR Cluj in January, 2007.
