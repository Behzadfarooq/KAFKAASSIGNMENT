Antti Aalto

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Antti Aalto (b. 4 March, 1975 in Lappeenranta, Finland) was a professional ice hockey player in the National Hockey League.

[edit] Playing career

Antti Aalto was drafted by the Mighty Ducks of Anaheim in the 1993 NHL Entry Draft in the 6th round, 134th overall. He continued to play in Finland for TPS until the 1997-98 season when he joined the Ducks' minor hockey team, the Cincinnati Mighty Ducks for 29 games and the Ducks for three games. He continued to play in the NHL until the 2000-01 season. The next season, 2001-02, saw Aalto return to Finland, which is where he continues to play.
