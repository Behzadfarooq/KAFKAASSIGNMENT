Anthropax is a user from the UK. He attended Monmouth School, but now studies for his BA Theology at the University of Wales, Lampeter.

  • He is a Catholic Anglican not of the Affirming Catholicism variety, but he is not a member of Forward in Faith just yet.
  • He is exploring a call into Holy Orders
  • He thinks that Oscar Romero is a saint, and should be proclaimed as such by the Pope
  • He keeps a blog [1]
  • He edits all over the place, but has created four articles (all small stubs): One on the Canterbury cap, another on his local bunch of nuns, The Society of the Sacred Cross, and a third on the College of the Resurrection. Recently added is St. Michael's College, Llandaff. If things continue, then he'll have to create a list of stubs.
  • When he drinks too much, he is known to sing Marian hymns very loudly, and with great gusto
  • He recomends that you come and join the debate on The Ship of Fools
  • He is a member of the Society of St. David, The Society of Our Lady of Walsingham and The Society of King Charles the Martyr.
