Antanas Sireika (born May 11, 1956 in Bazilionai near Šiauliai) - professional basketball coach.

Sireika was a Lithuania national basketball team head coach from 2001 till 2006. The biggest achievement of his career was winning Eurobasket 2003. Until March 27, 2006 he was coaching Žalgiris Kaunas in Euroleague. In June of the same year he agreed to a one year contract with russian UNICS Kazan.

[edit] Career

  • 1995-2002: Šiauliai head coach
  • 2002-2006: Žalgiris Kaunas head coach
  • 2006-????: UNICS Kazan head coach
  • 1997-2001: Lithuania national team assistant coach
  • 2001-2006: Lithuania national team head coach

[edit] Wins

  • Champion of Eurobasket 2003
  • Olympic Bronze medalist - 2000
  • LKL champion - 2003, 2004, 2005
  • SEB BBL champion - 2005
