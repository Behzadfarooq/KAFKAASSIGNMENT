Anthony Paul Taylor (born November 30, 1965, in Los Angeles, California), is an American former professional basketball player who was selected by the Atlanta Hawks in the 2nd round (44th overall) of the 1988 NBA Draft. A 6'4" guard from the University of Oregon, Taylor played for only one year (1988-89) with the Miami Heat, their inaugural NBA season.

In his NBA career, Taylor appeared in 21 games and scored a total of 144 points.
