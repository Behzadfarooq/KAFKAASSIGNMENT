[edit] Coronation Street characters

Glad to see you're still working hard with your edits! Have you read WP:FICTION? It is a collection of guidelines about how we write for Wikipedia when the subject is fictional. I draw your attention specifically to minor characters and what it says about them. A number of new articles have appeared recently about characters such as Doreen Fenwick and Roger Stiles which contain only a line of information. As far as we are aware these characters are not permanent and are only very minor, any information should ideally be merged into respective character profiles which are larger (for instance Rita Sullivan or Janice Battersby). Keep up the good work and try to read things like WP:FICTION to help you with your edits and to understand what should be included and what shouldn't! Ben 02:06, 17 March 2007 (UTC)

[edit] Coronation Street articles

Hello, Ant Parker. I have given you a few pointers in the past about your edits to Wikipedia, especially the pages you are creating. Could I please ask you to think carefully before creating a page? More character articles have popped up recently for very minor characters, as well as one or two articles for fictional places that are somehow related to the programme, such as Rosamund Street.
