Antony Kay (born 21 October 1982 in Barnsley) is an English professional footballer for Barnsley and wears the number 15 shirt.

Antony has played for Barnsley from his school years and rose through the ranks to make his professional debut for the club on 12 August 2000 at Oakwell against Norwich City.

He has since been an integral part of the squad, playing as a defender or as a midfielder and is vice-captain of the squad. He scored the fourth and final Barnsley penalty in the Play Off Final shootout against Swansea.
