[edit] About Me

Hello, my name is Anthony D'Elia, I'm 16 and I'm from Toronto, Canada. I attend Maple High School. My favourite things to do are listen to music and play sports. My favourite genre of music is punk rock and rock and my favourite band is Good Charlotte, and finally my favourite sport is track and field in the events of high jump and long jump. I am currently ranked 5th in high jump and 1st in long jump in Ontario.
