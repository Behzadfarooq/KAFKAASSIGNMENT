 Personal Policies

I currently apply several personal guidelines to my Wikipedia activities, especially with regards to contravertial articles or pages. Although they are not official policy, I do follow them rigidly and, except with the odd exception, will strive to not violate them. For quick reference, I've built a quick summary of my personal policies; the toolbar with the complete list is available near the top of this page.

  • User:Anthony cfc/Neutrality - not commenting, participating or otherwise passing judgement - non-verbal or otherwise - to contavertial pages in all namespaces, except with regards to RCP or AMA/Mediation duties.
  • User:Anthony cfc/Mediation - personal style with regards to Mediation Cabal and, most recently, the Mediation Committee or other DR processes.
  • User:Anthony cfc/AMA - similar to the mediation policy, but related to what the users I advocate for the AMA should expect from me with regards to conduct and dedication, and likewise what I expect from them with regards to civility, being a catalyst and striving to undertake processes earlier down the DR chain until they have each been exausted in turn.
  • User:Anthony cfc/Dispute Policy - the name says it all, but just to clarify it is how I conduct myself in disputes that I am personally involved in as a first/second party, rather than third party (mediator) or external editor.
  • User:Anthony cfc/Vandalism Policy - how I deal with vandalism and vandals I come across with regards to both conduct and technical issues.

Further reading:Dispute Resolution.
