Anthony Martinez was the last drummer for Black Flag. He did not record any studio material, but can be heard on the live album Who's Got the 10½? and live EP Annihilate This Week. He played with the band until they broke up in 1986.

Martinez also played drums in the 1980s underground rock band from Los Angeles, Pigmy Love Circus.
