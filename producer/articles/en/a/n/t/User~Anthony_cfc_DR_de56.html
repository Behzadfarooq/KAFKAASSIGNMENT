 Dispute Resolution

During last August I slowly shifted my contributions over to the Wikipedia namespace, with a particular focus on Dispute Resolution (DR).

Since then, I have Mediated numerous cases for the Mediation Cabal, which provides Mediation on an informal basis.

I have also acted as a Member's Advocate for the AMA, although I find I can get more done at the MedCabal. However, I'll gladly take on your case if you wish.

On a further note, I also mediated a Mediation Committee RfM case, WP:RfM/Jews for Jesus which was closed successfully. The MedCom is similar to the MedCabal, but is on an official basis and Committee members are appointed through nominations.
