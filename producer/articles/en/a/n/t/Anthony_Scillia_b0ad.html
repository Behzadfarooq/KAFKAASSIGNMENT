Anthony Scillia aka “Tony Mangia” (b. July 10, 1982 in Hackensack, New Jersey) is a radio and television personality.

[edit] Bio

Anthony was raised in his father’s hometown of Lodi, New Jersey. Lodi was a town that was made up of predominately Italian-Americans. Anthony was instilled with the values, traditions, culture and heritage that have shaped him into the man he is today. He is grateful for his whole family and all they have done for him.

[edit] Radio

He is most recently known for his radio show on WPSC 88.7 FM, The Tony Mangia Show. It is a positive Italian/Italian-American themed show that feeds off of Anthony’s love of his culture and heritage. Anthony has featured such guests as Italian singer Giada Valenti, Sicilian-American Folk Singer Michela Musolino and singer Anthony Fiumano. The show has been airing live since October 2006. He plays a wide variety of Italian and Italian-American music, old and new. Anthony also hosts MANGIA IN THE MORNING, which is a Classic/Garage/Jersey Rock show that has also been airing since October 2006.

[edit] Lodi Late Nite

Anthony was co-creator and host of Lodi Late Nite, which was a cult classic Public Access Show that aired from December 1999 until September 2000. The show followed a similar format to that of David Letterman. Richard Moschella was co-creator and co-host. Louis Jasmine was co-creator. The show also had such recurrent guests as Artie Magliannico, Johnny Paparazzi and the one and only Andy Martin (Lounge Singer Extraordinaire & Secret Member of the Rat Pack).

[edit] Independent Variety Showcaze

Anthony also created and hosted Independent Variety Showcaze (2004-2005). The Showcaze, as it was called, was a Public Access Show that featured people, places and events within North Jersey. The Showcaze’s guests included Kevin Knight and Camp IWF Wrestling School, psychic Jane Doherty, make-up artist Jeremy Selenfriend and Johnny De Carlo (Goomba Aficionado and Importer of Italian goods) to name a few.

[edit] Film

Anthony has also directed 3 short student films including To Each His Own (2002), Silence is Golden (2002) and Willow Creek (2005). He has also written a short script entitled Bocce that is currently trying to produce.
