[edit] Military Rank

  • MG = Major General
  • BG = Brigadier General
  • Col = Colonel
  • Ltc = Lieutenant Colonel
  • Maj = Major
  • Cpt = Captain
  • Lt = Lieutenant

[edit] Other

  • w=wounded
  • mw=mortally wounded
  • k=killed
