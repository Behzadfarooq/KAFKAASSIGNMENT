Antonio Buero Vallejo

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Antonio Buero Vallejo (September 29, 1916 – April 28, 2000) was a Spanish playwright. He was born in Guadalajara and died in Madrid.

During his career he won three National Theatre Prizes (in 1957, 1958 & 1959) and the Miguel de Cervantes Prize, Spain's highest literary honour, in 1986. From 1971 until his death he was a member of the Real Academia Española.
