[edit] Fair use for Antediluvian.jpg

The image, Antediluvian.jpg, is being linked here. though the picture is subject to copyright I (Occasionaluser) feel it is covered by the U.S. fair use laws because:

  1. it does not limit the copyright owner's rights to sell the CD in any way;
  2. on hard copies of the cd, the owner disavows all copyright;
  3. it is a low resolution copy of a CD cover and cannot be meaningfully reproduced;
  4. copies could not be used to make illegal copies of the album artwork on another CD;
  5. the image on the cover is revant because it was made by a known artist, Antediluvian Rocking Horse.
