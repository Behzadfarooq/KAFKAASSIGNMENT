Antidote
Studio album by Deadly Venoms
Released 1998
Genre Hip-hop
Label A&M/PolyGram Records

Antidote was the first album by the Wu-Tang Clan-affiliated hip-hop group Deadly Venoms. It was never released because of label politics. Its catalog number is 540 962.

  4. "Counterfeiters"
  5. "Ladies Room"
  6. "What, What"
  7. "Pockets Stay Deeply"
  8. "The Way We Feel"
  9. "D-Evils"
  10. "Word Life"
  11. "Everything"
