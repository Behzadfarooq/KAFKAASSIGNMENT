Antoni Hardonk (born May 2, 1976) is a Dutch mixed martial arts fighter and kickboxer who has competed in K-1 and the UFC, where he made his debut at UFC 65, defeating Sherman Pandergarst by technical knockout due to leg kicks. He is associated with Vos Gym in Amsterdam. He will fight at UFC Fight Night 9 against Justin McCully, a late-notice replacement for Hardonk's original opponent, former UFC Heavyweight Champion Frank Mir, as he had to drop out due to a Shoulder Injury (see Frank Mir).
