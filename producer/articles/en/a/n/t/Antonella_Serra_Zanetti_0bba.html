Antonella Serra Zanetti

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Antonella Serra Zanetti (born July 25, 1980 in Modena, Italy) is a professional female tennis player from Italy.

On January 30, 2006, Serra Zanetti achieved her career-high singles ranking: World No. 60.
