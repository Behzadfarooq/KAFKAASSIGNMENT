Anthony McMahon (born March 24, 1986 in Bishop Auckland) is an English footballer who currently plays for Middlesbrough as a defender.

He was the captain of the Middlesbrough side who won the FA Youth Cup during the 2003-04 season. He has represented England at under-17 and under-18 level, and made first-team appearances during the 2005-06 preseason.

Lately McMahon has had a torrid time with injuries; suffering shoulder injuries and knee ligament damage in the past few seasons. He is currently recovering from a broken leg suffered in a reserve game versus Liverpool.
