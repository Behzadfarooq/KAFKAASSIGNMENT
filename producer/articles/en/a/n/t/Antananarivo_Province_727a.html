Antananarivo Province

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Antananarivo is an autonomous province of Madagascar with an area of 58,283 km². It has a population of 4,580,788 (July, 2001). Its capital is Antananarivo.

Except for Antsiranana, Antananarivo Province borders all of the country's other provinces:

  • Mahajanga - north
  • Toamasina - east
  • Fianarantsoa - south
  • Toliara - west

Antananarivo is the only landlocked province of Madagascar.

President (PDS - Président de la Délégation Spéciale) of the province: Emile Rakotomalala.
