Anne Hunter - legendary long-time undergraduate and M.Eng. administrator in Course 6 (Electrical Engineering and Computer Science), the most heavily populated major at the Massachusetts Institute of Technology in Cambridge, Massachusetts. Hunter, who once described herself as the "queen of email at MIT," administers two heavily-trafficked mailing lists which reach over 2,000 students at MIT, Wellesley, and Harvard.

"Advising at MIT" Discovery Project, a collection of oral interviews with MIT advisors and administrators, includes a digest of a January 2001 interview with Hunter.
