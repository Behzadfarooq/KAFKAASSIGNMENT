Annarita Sidoti

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Annarita Sidoti (born July 25, 1969) is an Italian race walker.

[edit] Achievements

Year Tournament Venue Result Event
1990 European Indoor Championships Glasgow, Scotland 3rd 3000 m
European Championships Split, Yugoslavia 1st 10 km
1994 European Indoor Championships Paris, France 1st 3000 m
European Championships Helsinki, Finland 2nd 10 km
1997 World Championships Athens, Greece 1st 10 km
1998 European Championships Budapest, Hungary 1st 10 km
