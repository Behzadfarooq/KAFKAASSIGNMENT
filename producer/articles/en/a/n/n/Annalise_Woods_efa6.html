Annalise is an actress. She has appeared in The Lost Word, Legacy, and See No Evil; but she is most known for her role as Sara in The Sleepover Club.

Annalise attended Palm Beach Currumbin State High School in Palm Beach on the Gold Coast, Australia. She graduated this year (2006) from Grade Twelve. She was born in 1989.

Annalise has played a strong part in the drama industry of the school. She has performed in Drama Festival plays including - A Midsummer Night's Dream and Celebrating Humanity. Last year she appeared as Sonya in the musical Godspell. Her ambition is to be a professional film actor and with this list of credits she is sure to pick up where Nicole and Naomi leave off. She also played Miss Sherman in this years production of Fame.

Annalise is well known for her role as Sara in the Sleepover Club. She is jealous that she never got in their club and with her best friend Alana (Ashleigh Brewer) they do everything in their power to insult the Sleepover Club. Some of these things include telling lies to the girls and running for Class President alongside Frankie (Caitlin Stasey).

Annalise was a well respected member of the school community. She was School Captain with Sam McDonald in 2006. She was admired by fellow students and looked up to for her talents. She has been linked to fellow student, Shantanu Starrick, who appeared alongside her in the schools production of Fame. She has also been an active participant in school fundraising activities, including the schools slave auction.
