Ann Marie

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Ann Marie (born 1953, Wood River, Illinois, USA) is the stage name of the now retired big-bust model, stripper and actress, Kathy Ayers. She attended the University of Illinois before dropping out to pursue a career in nightclub dancing in Sarasota, Florida in 1974. As a feature dancer she earned up to US$3,500 a week.

In 1975, she appeared as the "Fisherman's wife" and "Pink bikini woman" in the Russ Meyer movie Supervixens. In 1979, she appeared in another Russ Meyer film, Beneath the Valley of the Ultra-Vixens, playing a religious disc jockey named Eufaula Roop. In the early 1980s she made a low budget softcore film entitled For Your Breasts Only. This was a straight-to-video release. Her whereabouts since the making of this film are currently unknown by the general public.

In her recent years, she has lived in Norway with her family, in a little town near Oslo. She loves playing with her fishes, although she is a catperson.[citation needed]
