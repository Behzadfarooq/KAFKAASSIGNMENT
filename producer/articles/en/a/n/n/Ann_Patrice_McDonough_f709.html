Ann Patrice McDonough (born May 25, 1985 in Osan, Korea) is a former American competitive figure skater. Her familiy moved to the USA in 1986. She won the World Junior Championships in 2002 and was the US National pewter medalist in 2003. She represented the Broadmoor SC. Her Coach was Tom Zakrajsek. McDonough chose to stop skating in 2004.
