Anne Ottenbrite-Muylaert (born May 12, 1966 in Bowmanville, Ontario) is a former breaststroke swimmer from Canada, who competed for her native country at the 1984 Summer Olympics in Los Angeles, California. There she won three medals: gold (200m Breaststroke), silver (100m Breaststroke), and bronze (4x100 m medley relay). In the last event she was accompanied by Reema Abdo, Michelle MacPherson, and Pamela Rai.
