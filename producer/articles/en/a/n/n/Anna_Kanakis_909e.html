Anna Kanakis

Anna Kanakis (born in Messina, Italy) is an Italian actress and model. She was Miss Italy in 1977. She has acted in many Italian television sitcoms and movies.

  • Money (1991) -Anna Lupino
  • Oceano (1989) -Mucena Chavez
