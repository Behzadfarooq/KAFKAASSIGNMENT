Anna Devane is a fictional character on the soap operas General Hospital (1985-1992, 1995, 2006-present) and All My Children (2001-2003). She is played by British actress Finola Hughes.

Anna was formerly a spy with the WSB. She currently is with a different agency. Anna was married to WSB agent Robert Scorpio. Although currently divorced, they have a daughter, Robin Scorpio together. Anna was also married to nightclub owner and erstwhile mobster, Duke Lavery, who broke ties with the mob for love of Anna. In so doing, he brought the organization to its knees, and he eventually gave up his life to protect Anna and Robin. Anna and Duke wanted children badly, but lost the baby Anna was carrying due to an elevator crash staged by mob princess Olivia Jerome. Anna and Robert later remarried, but were presumed dead in an explosion in 1992. However, Anna resurfaced in Pine Valley in 2001 on All My Children. She was married to Dr. David Hayward on that show and had a baby daughter, Leora, with him. The baby died of a heart defect. The stress of that loss broke up her marriage to David. She also briefly reunited with Robin while on All My Children. Anna then left Pine Valley in 2003. She resurfaced in the Markaam Islands in 2006, reuniting with Robert for the first time since 1992. She unceremoniously greeted him with a few good shots to the face. She returned to Port Charles in 2006 to reconnect with Robin and Robert. All the while, she is on assignment for her agency, investigating Lorenzo Alcazar.
