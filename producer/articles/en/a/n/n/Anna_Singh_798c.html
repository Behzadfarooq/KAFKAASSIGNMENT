Anna Singh is an Indian fashion designer, mainly working for the Indian movie industry Bollywood. She has designed costumes for over fifty films and has been working since 1989.

[edit] Career

Starting her career in 1989, she had designed clothes for some of Indias most famous actors, among them Shahrukh Khan, Aamir Khan, Rani Mukherjee, Hrithik Roshan and Madhuri Dixit. In 2010, she will design the clothes for the actors that participate in the opening ceremony of the Commonwealth Games which will be hosted in India then.[1]
