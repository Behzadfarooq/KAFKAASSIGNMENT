Doctor Annie Grey was a fictional character in the popular BBC soap opera EastEnders. She was played by Kate Anthony.

[edit] Biography

Consultant paediatrician Doctor Annie Grey works at Walford General Hospital, and advised Billy and Honey Mitchell about what to do when they discovered that their baby, Janet had Down syndrome. She gave them leaflets but Honey snapped at her.
