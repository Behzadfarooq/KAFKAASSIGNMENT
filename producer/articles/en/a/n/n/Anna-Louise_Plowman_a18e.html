Anna-Louise Plowman

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Anna-Louise Plowman (born 1975 in New Zealand) is a British actress. Her family comes from New Zealand. She is prominent for her role(s) as Dr. Sarah Gardner and the Goa'uld Osiris in Stargate SG-1. She attended the London Academy of Music and Dramatic Art. She married actor Toby Stephens in London in 2002 and thus has actress Maggie Smith as her mother-in-law. She played Diana Goddard in the Doctor Who story Dalek in 2005.
