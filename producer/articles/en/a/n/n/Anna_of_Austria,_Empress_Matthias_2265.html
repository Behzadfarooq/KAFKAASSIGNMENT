Anna, Archduchess of Austria, was the daughter of Ferdinand, Archduke of Further Austria and Count of Tyrol, and Anna Caterina Gonzaga, daughter of Duke William of Mantua. She was born 4 October 1585 in Innsbr√ºck, and married her first cousin, Matthias, King of Hungary and Bohemia, on 4 December 1611. With his election as Emperor in 1612, she became Empress of the Holy Roman Empire. They had no surviving children. Anna of Austria died 14 December 1618 in Vienna, predeceasing her husband by less than four months.
