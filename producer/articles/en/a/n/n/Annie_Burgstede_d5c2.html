Anna Louise Burgstede (born September 18, 1983 in Waupaca, Wisconsin) is an American actress.

Annie initially began her run on Days of our Lives in October in a recurring capacity, but after only a few episodes was upgraded to contract status. Initially known only as Willow, the character's last name was revealed to be Stark in January 2007.
