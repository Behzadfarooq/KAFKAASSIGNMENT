Anne Marie Pohtamo (born August 15, 1955), from Helsinki, Finland, was the second woman from Finland to capture the Miss Universe crown. She claimed the Miss Universe crown in 1975 at the pageant held in El Salvador. Pohtamo was crowned by Miss Universe 1972 Kerry Anne Wells, not by her predecessor Amparo Muñoz, as Muñoz was dethroned shortly before her reign ended. Pohtamo became the second Finn to claim the title of Miss Universe, 23 years after Armi Kuusela claimed it.

She pursued a modeling and movie career, being credited either as Anne Pohtamo or as Anne Pohtamo-Hietanen. Miss Finland 1975

She married Arto Hietanen in 1980, and is a mother of four.
