Annie Gallup

Annie Gallup is an American folk singer/songwriter who lives in Ann Arbor, Michigan. She attended the University of Michigan School of Art. Her song Circle was covered by Chuck Brodsky on his album "Radio". She covered Rachel Bissex's song "Angel" on the tribute-record Remembering Rachel: Songs Of Rachel Bissex (2005).
