Anna Svärd, (born 30 October 1973 in Sveg as Anna Bergström), is a Swedish curler. At the 2006 Winter Olympics, in Turin, Italy, she was the lead for the Swedish team who won the gold-medal after a thrilling final game. She now currently lives in Stockholm, and plays for Härnösands CK, Härnösand.

Anna Svärd is also a fictitious person and the title of a novel from 1928 by the Swedish author and Nobelist Selma Lagerlöf
