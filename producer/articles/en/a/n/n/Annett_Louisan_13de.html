Annett Louisan (born Annett Päge on April 2, 1977 in Havelberg) is a German singer. She lives with a large part of her family in Hamburg. Louisan is her stage name, derived from the name of her grandmother, Louise. Concerning her birth year, there is contradicting testimony, with the official date being given as 1979. In 2004 she married Gazi Isikatli, a Turkish business student. Her first album, Bohème, has been listed on German charts for almost a year, with a peak ranking of 3rd place [1].
