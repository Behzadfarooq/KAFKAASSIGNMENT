Anne Heaton is an American jazz & pop -influenced folk singer/songwriter and pianist from New York City. She majored in liberal studies at the Notre Dame, and grew up in the Chicago suburb of Wilmette. She currently lives in New York, and generally tours and performs with multi-instrumentalist Frank Marotta, Jr.
