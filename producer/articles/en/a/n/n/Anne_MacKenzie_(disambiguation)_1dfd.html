Anne MacKenzie (disambiguation)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Anne MacKenzie can refer to several people with the surname MacKenzie:

  • Anne MacKenzie British television journalist
  • Anne Gladstone (1772-1835) (nee Anne MacKenzie Robertson), the mother of British PM William Ewart Gladstone or Gladstone's maternal grandmother Anne MacKenzie (1740-1823) [1]
  • Judith-Anne MacKenzie, British legal author
  • Lady Anne Mackenzie (1620s-1707), wife of 1) Alexander Lindsay, 1st Earl of Balcarres and 2) Archibald Campbell, 9th Earl of Argyll [2]
  • Anne Mackenzie, 19th century British writer, editor of all 31 years of The Net Cast in Many Waters; Sketches from the Life of Missionaries, London, 1866-1896; unmarried sister to Charles Frederick Mackenzie (1825-1862), Anglican priest and bishop over British Central Africa. Biographed in Frances Awdry, An Elder Sister. A short sketch of Anne Mackenzie, and her brother the Missionary Bishop, London, 1878, 3rd ed. 1904.
