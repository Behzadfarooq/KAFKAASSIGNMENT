Here is a list of Annual sporting events broadcast on ESPN. Most of the following are yearly events like bowl games and championship games are either seen once a year on ESPN, ESPN2 or ESPNU.

  • Alamo Bowl
  • Allstate 400 at the Brickyard
  • Australian Open (also on The Tennis Channel)
  • Bowl Selection Show
  • Championship Week
  • Champs Sports Bowl
  • Chick-fil-A Bowl
  • College World Series
  • 2K Sports Classic benefiting Coaches vs. Cancer
  • East-West Shrine Game
  • Emerald Bowl
  • Feast Week
  • French Open (also on The Tennis Channel)
  • Bell Helicopter Armed Forces Bowl
  • Music City Bowl
  • GMAC Bowl
  • Hawaii Bowl
  • Heisman Trophy Presentation
  • Home Run Derby
  • Independence Bowl
  • International Bowl
  • Jimmy V Classic
  • Judgement Week
  • Liberty Bowl
  • Meineke Car Care Bowl
  • Motor City Bowl
  • MPC Computers Bowl
  • NBA Draft
  • NBA Playoffs
  • New Orleans Bowl
  • NFL Draft (also on NFL Network)
  • NCAA Division 1-AA National Football Championship
  • NCAA Men's Ice Hockey Championship
  • NCAA Men's Lacrosse Championship
  • NCAA Tournament Selection Show
  • NCAA Women's Division I Basketball Championship
  • NIT
  • Outback Bowl
  • Pacific Life Holiday Bowl
  • Pioneer PureVision Las Vegas Bowl
  • Rivalry Week
  • San Diego County Credit Union Poinsettia Bowl
  • Women's College World Series
  • World Baseball Classic (held in 2006, next scheduled for 2009)
  • World Cup (soccer)
  • Wimbledon
  • UEFA Champions League
  • X Games

[edit] Annual sporting events no longer airing on ESPN

  • 12 Hours of Sebring (now on Speed Channel)
  • 24 Hours of Daytona (now on Speed Channel)
  • Division Series (now on TBS)
  • Insight Bowl(now on NFL Network)
  • Pro Bowl(rotates between FOX, CBS, and NBC)
  • Stanley Cup Playoffs(now on Versus and NBC)
