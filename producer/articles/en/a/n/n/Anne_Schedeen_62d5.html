Anne Schedeen

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Anne Schedeen in the Season 1 title sequence from Alf

Anne Schedeen (born on January 7, 1949 in Portland, Oregon) is an actress who starred as Kate Tanner in the sitcom ALF. She has also made appearances in TV shows such as Three's Company and Judging Amy.

She is married to Christopher Barrett. Her daughter Taylor was born in 1989. She is currently living in LA and is working as an antiquer and decorator; also coaches comedy actors part-time.
