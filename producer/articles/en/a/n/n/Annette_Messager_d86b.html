Annette Messager is a French artist who was born in 1943. She is known mainly for her installation work which often incorporates photographs and various materials. Messager has exhibited and published her work extensively. She is married to artist Christian Boltanski.

In 2005 her work was featured in the French Pavilion of the Venice Biennale.
