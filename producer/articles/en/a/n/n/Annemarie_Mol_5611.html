Annemarie Mol

Annemarie Mol is a Dutch ethnographer and philosopher who has written and worked with a range of scholars including John Law. She has helped to develop post-ANT/feminist understandings of science, technology and medicine.

[edit] Prizes

She received The Ludwik Fleck Prize, in 2004 for her book, The Body Multiple.
