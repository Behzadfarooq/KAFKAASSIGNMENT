Anni Ribelli

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Anni Ribelli is a 1996 Italian Argentine romantic drama film directed and written by Rosalia Polizzi. The film starred Leticia Brédice, Massimo Dapporto, Esther Goris and Alessandra Acciai. Actress Leticia Brédice one a silver condor award for best actress in 1997 for her performance.
