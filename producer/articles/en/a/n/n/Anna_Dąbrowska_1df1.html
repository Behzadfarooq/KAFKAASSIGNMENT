Anna Dąbrowska ['anna dɔm'brɔfska] (stage name Ania), born 07.01.1981 in Chełm, is a Polish singer and composer. She participated in the first edition of Idol. She has collaborated with Andrzej Smolik, Krzysztof Krawczyk and Artur Rojek. Her records are published by BMG

[edit] Discography

  • 2004 Samotność po zmierzchu (Solitude after dusk) #1 PL, 1x Gold
  • 2006 Kilka historii na ten sam temat (Several stories with the same topic) #1 PL, 1x Platinum
