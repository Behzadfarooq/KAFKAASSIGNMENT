Annie is a soundtrack album to the film of the same name, released in 1982.

[edit] Track listing

  1. Aileen Quinn & the Orphans: "Tomorrow"
  2. Aileen Quinn, Toni Ann Gisondi & Chorus: "It's The Hard-Knock Life"
  3. Aileen Quinn: "Maybe"
  4. Aileen Quinn: "Dumb Dog"
  5. Aileen Quinn & the Orphans: "Sandy"
  6. Aileen Quinn & Ann Reinking: "I Think I'm Gonna Like It Here"
  7. Carol Burnett: "Little Girls"
  8. Ann Reinking, Lu Leonard, Geoffrey Holder & Roger Minami: "We Got Annie"
  9. Aileen Quinn, Ann Reinking, Albert Finney & Chorus: "Let's Go to the Movies"
  10. Carol Burnett & Albert Finney: "Sign"
  11. Peter Marshall, the Cast and the Orphans: "You're Never Fully Dressed Without A Smile"
  12. Carol Burnett, Tim Curry & Bernadette Peters: "Easy Street"
  13. Aileen Quinn, Albert Finney, Lois deBanzie & Edward Hermann: "Tomorrow (White House Version)"
  14. Aileen Quinn & Albert Finney: "Maybe (Reprise)"
  15. Aileen Quinn, Albert Finney, Chorus and the Orphans: "Finale/I Don't Need Anything But You/We Got Annie/Tomorrow".
