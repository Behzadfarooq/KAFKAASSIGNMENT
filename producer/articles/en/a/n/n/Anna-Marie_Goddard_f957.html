Anna-Marie Goddard (born Anna Marie Lampe on 13 January 1970 in Ysbrechtum, The Netherlands) is a Dutch-Frisian model and actress.

She is married to American screenwriter/model Collin Goddard. She has a son who was born on July 29, 1998, and had her second child, a daughter, in 2005. She was chosen as the U.S. Playboy's 40th Anniversary Playmate of the Month in January 1994. Goddard was also the Playmate of the Year for the Dutch edition of Playboy. She has appeared in numerous Playboy videos.
