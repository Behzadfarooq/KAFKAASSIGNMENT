Anna Nova (born 5 June 1975 Magdeburg, Germany) is an erotic actress.

[edit] Biography

Before Anna Nova entered the pornography industry, she was a dental assistant. She has two younger brothers and in her early teens she moved from Magdeburg to Berlin where she started stripping and modeling.

Nova started out her porn career in 2000 in Germany when she signed an exclusive contract for German porn label "Goldlight" under her former stage name "Tara Young". She did (according to herself) about 20 movies. In 2001 she won the Venus Award for "Best New Female Starlet" as Tara Young.

In 2004 she continued her career in the U.S. and because of copyright conflicts she changed her stage name to "Ana Nova". She partly lives in Los Angeles and Berlin. She is known for her rough anal and gangbang scenes (including double anal) and interracial scenes.

She's still filming in L.A and is also available for shoots in Europe when she's over there. While in the US she is represented by L.A. Direct Models
