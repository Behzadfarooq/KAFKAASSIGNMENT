Notices

  • A list of written sources, sorted by subject area and WikiProject, is being compiled at Wikipedia:List of sources. Feel free to add your own sources.
  • The Wikipedia:Version 1.0 Editorial Team announces the release of Wikipedia 0.5. Leave feedback on the talk page
  • For those who use welcome templates, a new set has been created, and can be seen at Wikipedia:Welcoming committee/Welcome templates.
  • After a Fund-drive/poll, the Linguist List has decided to hire a graduate student to review the English Wikipedia entries on linguistics and to organize a community effort to update and expand these sections.
  • A new proposal for providing help to editors has been proposed, comments, suggestions, and those wishing to help are welcome.
  • A new guideline for reviewing good articles has been proposed, feel free to comment or leave a suggestion.
