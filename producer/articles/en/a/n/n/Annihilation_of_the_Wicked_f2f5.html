Annihilation of the Wicked is the fourth full length album by American death metal band Nile. It was released on May 23, 2005 through Relapse Records. The album continues in the same thematic vein of Nile's previous works, drawing heavy inspiration on ancient Egyptian history and the writings of H.P. Lovecraft. Annihilation of the Wicked produced one single: "Lashed to the Slave Stick".
