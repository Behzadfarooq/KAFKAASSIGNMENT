Anna of Saxony (disambiguation)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Anna of Saxony may refer to:

  • Anna of Saxony (d. 1426), daughter of Elector Wenzel of Saxony and wife of Duke Frederick of Braunschweig-Lüneburg
  • Anna of Saxony (d. 1462), daughter of Frederick I, Margrave of Meissen and wife of Landgrave Louis III of Hesse
  • Anna of Saxony (d. 1512), daughter of Frederick II, Elector of Saxony and wife of Albert III, Margrave of Brandenburg
  • Anna of Saxony (d. 1577), daughter of Maurice, Elector of Saxony and wife of William the Silent
