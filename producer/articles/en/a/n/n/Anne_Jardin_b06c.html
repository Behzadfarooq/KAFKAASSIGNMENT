Anne Jardin-Alexander (born July 26, 1959 in Montréal, Quebec) is a former international freestyle swimmer from Canada, who won two bronze medals at the 1976 Summer Olympics in her hometown Montreal.

She finished third in the Women's Women's 4x100 Freestyle Relay, alongside Becky Smith, Gail Amundrud, and Barbara Clark, and also ended up in third place in the 4x100 Medley Relay, with Wendy Cook, Robin Corsiglia, and Susan Smith. She broke the world record in the 50m free on August 19, 1978 in Etobicoke, Ontario.
