Annwyn, Beneath the Waves is the sophomore album by Faith and the Muse.

  6. "Arianrhod"
  7. "Branwen Slayne"
  8. "Hob Y Derri Dando"
  9. "Cernunnos"
  10. "The Hand of Man"
  11. "The Sea Angler"
  12. "The Birds of Rhiannon"
  13. "Rise and Forget"
  14. "Apparition"
