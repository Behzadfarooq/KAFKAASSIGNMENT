Anna Jones is a fictional character. She first appeared in The Young Indiana Jones Chronicles TV series, and was portrayed by Ruth de Sosa.

The mother of renowned archaeologist Indiana Jones, Anna Jones died when the young Indiana Jones was just 13, after contracting scarlet fever.
