Anne Fletcher

You have new messages (last change).

Anne Fletcher (born 1 May 1966 in Detroit, Michigan) is well known for her job as director for many films starring many famous people worldwide. She has been also well-known for her choreography for over 10 films. Anne Fletcher's latest film for director is a dance/romance movie called Step Up featuring songs with many well-known singers such as Ciara, Chris Brown, Sean Paul, Keyshia Cole, Kelis, Yung Joc and many more.
