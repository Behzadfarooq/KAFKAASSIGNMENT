Ann Katherine "Annie" McNamara is a character on the American television series Nip/Tuck. She is the daughter of Julia and Sean McNamara. Annie is played by Kelsey Batelaan.

Spoiler warning: Plot and/or ending details follow.

[edit] Character History

Annie first appeared on the show in the Pilot episode. Here she was shown as going back to school, this allowing her mother Julia to go back to medical school. She also adopts the class pet gerbil, Frisky. Julia later kills the pet, and does not tell Annie. Instead she bought her a new one.

In Season 2 Annie, at the age of 8, started puberty. This caused Julia and Sean to become self-aware of their own aging. Annie's friend's mother throws Annie a "Princess Menses" party and invites her little friends to celebrate the passage to womanhood.

In Season 3 Annie had to talk to Family Services about her father's anger problem. She led them to believe, unintentionally, that he may be physically abusive and that he has emotionally affected her. This caused her to be taken away from Sean until further notice. She was then put to stay with her mother Julia.

In season 4 Annie learns her mother is going to give birth to a boy with ectrodactyly, which is a webbing of the fingers and toes. Julia and Sean are horrified at this birth defect. Annie in a sincere attempt to understand this birth defect tapes her fingers together to understand how her brother would feel and alters her dolls to have the same appearance. Sean and Julia interpret this as mocking behavior, and Julia forces Annie to throw out her toys after she alters their hands. After her brother's birth she is uncomfortable with him because of his handicap. She is taken away by her mother when Julia leaves for NYC towards the end of the Season.

[edit] Future

In 'Conor McNamara, 2026' we are able to see Annie at age 30 (played by Jennifer Elise Cox). Years of being the ignored middle child and separation from her father has resulted in her being afflicted with Borderline Personality Disorder. A troublesome person, she has issues with bulimia, claims everything is always about Conor, and is caught stealing painkillers from the hospital where Conor is having his surgery. She attempts to shock her family by dating a African-American Bisexual man and announcing at dinner that she just had an abortion. Throughout the episode she also chainsmokes and mentions several of the incidents above such as when Julia flushed her gerbil, throwing away her dolls, the party for her period, and Sean running out on the family.
