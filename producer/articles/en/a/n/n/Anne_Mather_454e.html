Anne Mather

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Anne Mather is a popular English author of over 150 romance novels.

Mather began to write down stories in her childhood years. The first novel that she actually finished, Caroline, was also her first book to be published. Her novel, Leopard in the Snow, was developed into a 1978 movie.

Mather has two grown children and several grandchildren.
