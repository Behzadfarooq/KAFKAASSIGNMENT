Anna Maria of Ostfriesland

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Anna Maria of Ostfriesland (23 June 1601 â€“ 15 February 1634) was a German noblewoman.

She was a daughter of Count Enno III of Ostfriesland and Anna of Holstein-Gottorp. Her paternal grandmother was Katharina of Sweden, a daughter of Gustav I of Sweden and his second wife Margareta Leijonhufvud.

On 4 September 1622 she married Adolf Friedrich I (1588-1658), Duke of Mecklenburg-Schwerin. They had the following children:

  1. Christian Ludwig I, Duke of Mecklenburg-Schwerin (1623-1692)
  2. Anna Maria of Mecklenburg-Schwerin (1 July 1627 - 11 December 1669) (ancestor of Queen Victoria).
