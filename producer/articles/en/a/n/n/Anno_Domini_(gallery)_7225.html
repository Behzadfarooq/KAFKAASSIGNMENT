Anno Domini is a contemporary art gallery in San Jose, California. It was started by graphic designers Cherri Lakey and Brian Eder. Its strapline is "The second coming of Art and Design".

The gallery has featured the collaborative work "FriendMakers" by the artists Don Pendleton, Keith Knight, and Michael Sieben. The gallery also use to carry a considerable share of solo exhibits, such as "Stray Doll" by Kari Byron. Almost every event at the gallery are free in the spirit of keeping an as wide range of artists as possible comfortable with them.
