Annan Rugby Football Club are a rugby union side based in Annan in Dumfries and Galloway, Scotland.

Originally established in 1879, the current club was founded in 1969.

A former member of the Border League and Glasgow District League, Annan currently play in National League Division 2.

[edit] Honours

  • SRU BT Bowl Winners - 2002-2003
  • National League Division 2 Champions - 1997-1998
  • National League Division 3 Champions - 1996-1997
  • National League Division 4 Champions - 1995-1996
  • National League Division 6 Champions - 1994-1995
  • National League Division 7 Champions - 1993-1994
  • Glasgow District League Division 1 Champions - 1992-1993
  • Glasgow District League Division 2 Champions - 1991-1992
