Annate

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

You have new messages (last change).

The first fruits, or first year's revenue of an ecclesiastical benefice paid to the Papal Curia, a certain papal tax derived from one's income.

A tax that has been generally known as annates, a term comprehending all money taxes paid into the Apostolic Camera (papal treasury)
