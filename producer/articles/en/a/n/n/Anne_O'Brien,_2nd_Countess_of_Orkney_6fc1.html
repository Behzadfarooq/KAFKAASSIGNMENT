Anne O'Brien, 2nd Countess of Orkney

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Lady Anne Douglas-Hamilton (d. 1756) was the 2nd Countess of Orkney in the Peerage of Scotland (3rd Creation, 1696), and through marriage the to Countess Inchiquin in the Peerage of Ireland.

She was the eldest daughter and heiress of Field Marshal Sir George Douglas-Hamilton, 1st Earl of Orkney, and Elizabeth Villiers.

She married her first cousin William O'Brien, 4th Earl of Inchiquin, 'the O Brien', Chief of the Name, Prince of Thomond, and Lord of Dál gCais. They had a daughter Mary circa. 1721.
