Annona montana (Mountain soursop, Boszuurzak, Corosselier batard, Guanabana de monte, Guanabana de perro, Wild custard apple, Wild soursop) is an edible fruit and medicinal plant in the Annonaceae family native to Central and South America, specially in Brazil and Peru.
