Anna Pappas was a fictional character of Greek Cypriot descent in the popular BBC soap opera EastEnders in February 2006. She was played by Anna Savva.

Anna appeared as the mother of Nico, Carly Wicks's husband. Anna did not approve of Carly, and vocally said this. She also hated Carly because she couldn't speak Greek.

Yanis and Carly's father, Kevin, arranged a family liaison meeting at 31 Albert Square, but it ended up in almighty brawl, where Kevin and Yanis exchanged punches, and Anna fought with Carly and her brother Deano.

  • Husband: Yanis Pappas
