Anna Lena Hansen

Anna Lena Hansen became the second representative of Norway to win the Miss International pageant in 1995 in Tokyo, Japan, after Catherine Alexandra Gude in 1988.

A year before her victory, she competed in the Miss Norway pageant, as the delegate of Troms. Hence, she was sent to the 1994 Miss World pageant in Sun City, South Africa as Norway's representative where she was unplaced.

As part of Bollywood's global expansion, she recently figured, along with Indian model Aman Dhalliwal in a music video of Indian Idol finalist Ravinder Ravi. The video was shot in Barcelona, Spain.
