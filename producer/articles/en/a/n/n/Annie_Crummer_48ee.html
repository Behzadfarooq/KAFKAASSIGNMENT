Annie Crummer is a New Zealand singer and songwriter, who has not only a successful solo career but also as part of many musical groups.

She grew up in West Auckland. Her father, a Cook Islander, was one of her strongest musical influences. She is not only fluent in English but in both Cook Island and New Zealand Māori.

She has worked with Netherworld Dancing Toys, When The Cat's Away, Herbs and The Katene Sisters. She joined Split Enz and the New Zealand Symphony Orchestra for ENZSO. She has supported John Farnham and Jimmy Barnes.

On stage, she was the soloist in Rent in Australia and played Killer Queen in We Will Rock You in Melbourne.
