Annette Island Airport (IATA: ANN, ICAO: PANT, FAA LID: ANN) is located on Annette Island in the U.S. state of Alaska.

[edit] Facilities

Annette Island Airport has two runways:

  • Runway 12/30: 7,493 x 150 ft. (2,284 x 46 m), Surface: Asphalt
  • Runway 2/20: 5,709 x 150 ft. (1,740 x 46 m), Surface: Gravel
