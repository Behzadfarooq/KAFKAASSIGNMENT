The Baroness von Wurtzburg

Hon. Anne Theresa Bickerton Lyons was born in 1815, to Edmund Lyons (later 1st Lord Lyons) and Augusta Louisa Lyons (née Rogers). In Athens, Greece on December 24, 1839 she married Philipp Hartmann Veit von Würtzburg (1811-1897), Baron von Würtzburg in Bavaria.

Anne (or Annie as she was more commonly known), Baroness von Würtzburg died on June 11, 1894 in Bamberg, Bavaria and was buried at the Würtzburg family estate in Mitwitz, Bavaria.
