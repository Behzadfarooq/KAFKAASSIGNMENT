Ann-Helen Moen, born 1969, is a Norwegian soprano from Molde, now living in the United Kingdom.

She studied at the Grieg Academy (Bergen University) and the Opera Academy in Copenhagen. She was a soloist at the opera in Graz from 2001-2004. In 2006, she sang the role of Solveig in Bentein Baardson's production of Peer Gynt in front of the Sphinx of Giza.

Prizes and Awards

Belvedere Competition, Vienna: special prize.
Queen Sonja International Music Competition, 1999: beste Norwegian artist.
Esso Prize, 2000
