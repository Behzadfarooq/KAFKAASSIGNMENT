Anne van Keppel (24 June 1703 â€“ 20 October 1789) was born Lady Anne Lennox, the daughter of the 1st Duke of Richmond and a granddaughter of King Charles II. On 21 February 1722, she married the 2nd Earl of Albemarle at Caversham, Oxfordshire (now Berkshire), whereupon she became Countess of Albemarle. From 1725 to 1737, she was a Lady of the Bedchamber to Queen Caroline. Anne died in 1789 at Admiralty House, aged 86.
