Anna ("Annie") Johanna Geertruida Maria Borckink (born October 17, 1951 in Hupsel) is a former ice speed skater from the Netherlands, who represented her native country at the 1980 Winter Olympics in Lake Placid, United States. There she won the gold medal in the women's 1500 metres, before het Dutch teammate Ria Visser. For that perfomance Borckink was named 1980 Dutch Sportswoman of the Year.
