Anna Maria Corazza Bildt

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Countess Anna Maria Corazza Bildt (born 1963 in Rome) is an Italian-Swedish politician of the Moderate Party. She is a former member of the Forza Italia Party.

In 1998, she married Carl Bildt, former Prime Minister of Sweden and former leader of the Swedish Moderate Party. The two met in the Balkans where they both worked for the United Nations following the Yugoslav wars. They now have one child together.
