Anna Söderberg (born 11 June 1973 in Knista) is a Swedish discus thrower.

Her personal best throw is 64.54 metres, achieved in July 1999 in Aremark.

[edit] Achievements

Year Tournament Venue Result Extra
1997 World Championships Athens, Greece 11th
2003 World Championships Paris, France 8th
2005 World Championships Helsinki, Finland 11th
2006 European Championships Gothenburg, Sweden 11th
World Athletics Final Stuttgart, Germany 3rd
