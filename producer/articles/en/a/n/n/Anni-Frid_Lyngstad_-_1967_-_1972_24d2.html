Anni-Frid Lyngstad - 1967 - 1972
Compilation album by Anni-Frid Lyngstad
Released 1997
Label EMI Svenska

Anni-Frid Lyngstad - 1967 - 1972 is a double album compilation CD released in Sweden in 1997 by Anni-Frid Lyngstad on EMI Svenska AB..
