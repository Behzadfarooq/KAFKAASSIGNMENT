Anna Notaras

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Anna Notaras (Greek Άννα Νοταρά) was the daughter of Loukas Notaras, the last Megas Doux of the Byzantine Empire. She escaped the fall of Constantinople and, with the fortune her father had wisely invested abroad, became the center of the Byzantine expatriate community in Venice.
