Hi Anneb and welcome to wikipedia! It looks like you've had a rough start -- I hope you don't give up on us!!

I'm a romance nut and I stumbled across the Nicole Jordan page while I was creating pages for other authors. I did a quick cleanup of the page to make it more wikified, and then I added a bunch of biography stuff from her website and a few interviews I found easily. If you look at the page now, it should be a decent example of what wikipedia is looking for (although I am not claiming that it's perfect -- I'm still learning too!). If you have or can find more Nicole Jordan information, please include it on the page -- anything and everything is good, but you will need to make sure that you source the statements pretty well.

You might want to read this page on citing sources, and this one has the general citation templates that make it easy to do.

Good luck, and if you have any questions on what I did, please let me know on my talk page. Karanacs
