[edit] Notes to Self

Before I heard about WikiProject Albums and their preference that premptive disambiguation be avoided, I created a few album pages with just such disambiguation. These are:

  • The Arms Dealer's Daughter (album),which I've moved to The Arms Dealer's Daughter
  • Faux mouvement (album), moved to Faux mouvement
    • Faux Mouvement (album), which is taken care of by the redirect of Faux Mouvement to Faux mouvement
  • L'Échappée belle (album), moved to L'Échappée belle
    • L'Échappée Belle (album), taken care of by redirect of L'Échappée Belle to L'Échappée belle
  • Live at Selwyn Hall (album), moved to Live at Selwyn Hall
  • Radical Mestizo (album), moved to Radical Mestizo

I'm keeping the redirects that involve mere variants of capitalization, as there is some disagreement on how to capitalize titles, especially in foreign languages. See WikiProject Albums: Style (under "Capitalization"), and Capitalization of foreign-language titles on the talk page
