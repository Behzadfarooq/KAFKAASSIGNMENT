Anne of Geierstein, or The Maiden of the Mist (1829) is one of Walter Scott's novels. Along with Ivanhoe, this is one of Scott's English set novels, with the action taking place in the 15th century.

[edit] Plot

The story concerns exiled Lancastrian supporters become involved with the defeat of Charles the Bold, Duke of Burgundy, by the Swiss.
