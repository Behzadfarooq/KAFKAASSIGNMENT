Anna Rice (born 19 August 1980 in North Vancouver, British Columbia) is a female badminton player from Canada.

Rice competed in badminton at the 2004 Summer Olympics in women's doubles with partner Denyse Julien. They were defeated by Saralee Thungthongkam and Sathinee Chankrachangwong of Thailand in the round of 32.

Anna is coached by Julia Chen and Michael Kjeldsen.
