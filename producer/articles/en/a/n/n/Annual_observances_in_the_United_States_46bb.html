Annual observances in the United States

This is the list of observances recognized in the United States and no more than two other independent countries.

[edit] Days

  • January 13 - Stephen Foster Memorial Day
  • February 1 - National Freedom Day [1]
  • March 14 - Pi Day
  • April 13 - Thomas Jefferson's birthday
  • May 1 - Law Day, U.S.A. [2]
  • May 1 - Loyalty Day [3]
  • 1st Thursday in May - National Day of Prayer [4]
  • 2nd Sunday in May - Mother's Day [5]
  • 3rd Saturday in May - Armed Forces Day
  • 3rd Friday in May - National Defense Transportation Day [6]
  • last Monday in May - Memorial Day [7]
  • May 15 - Peace Officers Memorial Day [8]
  • May 22 - National Maritime(US) Day [9]
  • May 25 - Towel Day
  • June 14 - Flag Day [10]
  • 3rd Sunday in June - Father's Day [11]
  • June 25 - National Catfish Day (1987)
  • 4th Sunday in July - Parent's Day [12]
  • August 19 - National Aviation Day [13]
  • 1st Monday. in September - Labor Day
  • 1st Saturday after Labor Day - Carl Garner Federal Lands Cleanup Day (US)
  • 1st Sunday after Labor Day - National Grandparents Day [14]
  • September 11 - Patriot Day [15]
  • September 17 - Citizenship Day [16]
  • September 19 - International Talk Like a Pirate Day [17]
  • last Sunday in September - Gold Star Mother's Day [18]
  • 1st Monday in October - Child Health Day [19]
  • October 6 - German-American Day
  • October 9 - Leif Erikson Day [20]
  • October 13 - Navy Day
  • 2nd Monday in October - Columbus Day [21]
  • October 15 - White Cane Safety Day [22]
  • October 23 - National Mole Day
  • November 12 - National Pizza with the Works (except anchovies) Day
  • November 15 - America Recycles Day [23]
  • December 7 - National Pearl Harbor Remembrance Day [24]
  • December 17 - Pan American Aviation Day [25]
  • December 17 - Wright Brothers Day [26]

[edit] Weeks

  • 3rd in February - National Brotherhood Week
  • 1st in March - Save Your Vision Week [27]
  • 3rd in March - National Poison Prevention Week [28]
  • week of May 15 - Police Week [29]
  • week ending Fri. before Memorial Day - National Safe Boating Week [30]
  • week of 3rd Fri. in May - National Transportation Week [31]
  • week of June 14 - National Flag Week [32]
  • June 14 (Flag Day) to July 4 (Independence Day) - Honor America Days [33]
  • September 17 to September 23 - Constitution Week [34]
  • 2nd Sun. in October - National School Lunch Week [35]
  • 3rd Sun. in October - National Forest Products Week [36]

[edit] Months

  • February - Black History Month
  • February - American Heart Month [37]
  • March - National Nutrition Month, Frozen Foods Month
  • March - Women's History Month
  • April - National Poetry Month [38]
  • April - Confederate History Month (Limited to some state and local governments)
  • April - Cancer Control Month [39]
  • May - Asian Pacific American Heritage Month [40]
  • May - Steelmark Month [41]
  • June - Lesbian and Gay pride Month
  • September 15 to October 15 - National Hispanic Heritage Month[42]
  • October - Filipino American History Month
  • October - National Disability Employment Awareness Month [43]
  • October - Mexican-American History Month
