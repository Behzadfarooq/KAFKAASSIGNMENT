Anna Taggaro was a recurring character on WB show One Tree Hill who is bisexual. She was portrayed by Daniella Alonso.

Spoiler warning: Plot and/or ending details follow.


[edit] Season Two

Anna was first introduced as a girl who saved Lucas during one of Felix Taggaro's games. It was then revealed that she was his sister. She fell for Lucas and they started hanging out together much to her brother's annoyement. They relationship got strained when she tried to sleep with him at the prom night and he said that he wasn't sure to be in love with her. It turned out to be true as he left her for Brooke Davis. She was then really mad at him but not for the reason everyone suspected.

Actually Anna was struggling with her bisexuality and felt Lucas helped her "stay straight" and she was afraid rumors would start again about her which was why she had left her former school. She then fell for Peyton but her feelings weren't shared by the latter. Mouth also knew as she had asked him to erase a part of her time capsule video where she revealed her sexuality, changing it to where she said she hated labels. Towards the end of the season, her girlfriend came to Tree Hill and motivated her enough to not only tell her parents about her sexuality but also to come back to her former school, no longer hiding herself.
