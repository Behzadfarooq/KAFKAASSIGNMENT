Annie Lee aka "the Tse" (pronounced like "tease") is a Korean-Canadian rapper, dancer and model who was born on April 16, 1975.

Lee was originally slated to become a member of the Korean female pop group Fin.K.L. but decided to leave the group before it made its debut. Her spot in the group was then taken by Hyori Lee.

She then formed Tashannie, a hip-hop and R&B duo, with T(Tasha Reid) in 1999 where she was the primary vocalist.

[edit] Facts and Trivia

  • Her Korean name is Lee Su Ah
  • She has worked as backup dancer for Coolio, many artists, along with the NBA, while also working as a print model and acting in Los Angeles and Toronto.
  • She attended college at Ryerson University. She attended Magee Secondary school in Vancouver, and had dreams of being a ballerina and owning her own performing arts school.

Began choreographing for many top R&B singers in Korea after deciding that the music industry was not something she wanted to pursue. Started her own "How to dance" tv series which aired 3 times daily. Opened up the first entertainment/ performing arts academy in Korea in 2002. Started designing a street fashion men's brand called "Know1edge" in 2004.
