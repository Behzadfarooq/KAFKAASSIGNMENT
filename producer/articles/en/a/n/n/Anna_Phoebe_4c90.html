Anna Phoebe (b. Germany, 1981) is a London-based violinist, who is known for her proficiency in many musical genres, including rock, folk, jazz, Celtic, and Middle Eastern music.

[edit] Session Musician

As a session musician, Anna has been hired by many musicians, including Nitin Sawhney, LeAnn Rimes, George Michael and Sean "P Diddy" Combs. She has appeared on UK TV shows, including Top of the Pops, MTV, and The Michael Parkinson Show, as well as music festivals, including the Glastonbury Festival, and The Big Chill. Anna also features in the theme tune for the Turner Prize 2005 exhibition.

[edit] Trans-Siberian Orchestra

For the last three years, Anna has also been the String Director and Solo Violinist for the Trans-Siberian Orchestra. Anna first joined the TSO West Coast Band in 2004. She was invited back for 2006, making her the first String Director and Soloist to return for a third year running, and hopes to return in 2007 as part of the band. As part of the TSO, Anna tours for three months every Christmas through 26 states around West and Central USA. She performs nearly 60 dates in arenas to audiences of up to 15,000 people. Venues have included the Phoenix Arena, American Airline Center in Dallas, the Pepsi Center in Denver, and the Orleans Arena in Las Vegas

[edit] Solo career

Anna has recently finished her debut solo rock violin albumette 'Gypsy', co-written with TSO rock guitarist, Angus Clark, and mixed and recorded by Nick Wollage. It also features a track written with Christian Henson, a TV and film composer and producer who has composed and produced pieces for numerous film soundtracks, including Severance (on which Anna featured as violinist). This year Anna is the featured guest soloist for Jethro Tull , touring the UK, Europe, Scandanavia, USA and Canada between March and October, before she returns for the 2007 TSO tour.
