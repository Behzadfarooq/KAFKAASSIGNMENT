Anne-Marie Cortez is a fictional mutant character in the Marvel Comics Universe who first appeared in X-Men vol. 2, #1

[edit] Fictional character biography

Little is known of Anne-Marie Cortez apart from that she is the sister of Fabian Cortez, one of the better-known Acolytes. She was among the first mutants who joined Magneto as one of his Acolytes but later died when Asteroid M, the homebase of the Acolytes, crashed from orbit.[1]

[edit] Notes

Cortez' powers were never seen or mentioned on-panel in the comics. Marvel RPG states she had psychic powers.
