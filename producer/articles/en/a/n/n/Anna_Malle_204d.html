Anna Malle (September 9, 1967 – January 25, 2006) was an American pornographic actress.

She was born Anna Hotop in Havana, Illinois and raised in Fort Madison, Iowa.

[edit] Career

She appeared in one of the "Dirty Debutantes" series and some Max Hardcore productions. During her career, she performed in over 300 films, frequently with her husband Hank Armstrong as co-star.

She also performed in more mainstream circles, including appearances on HBO and the Playboy Channel.

Malle retired from filmmaking in 2005.

[edit] Death

She was killed in a car accident near Las Vegas, Nevada on January 25, 2006. She was not wearing a seat belt.[1]
