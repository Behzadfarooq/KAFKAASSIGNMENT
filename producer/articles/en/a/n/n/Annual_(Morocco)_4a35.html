Annual (Morocco)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

You have new messages (last change).

Annual is a settlement in northeastern Morocco about 120 km west of Melilla. There, during the Rif War or War of Melilla, on July 22, 1921, the Spanish army suffered a grave military defeat, known as the "Battle of Annual".
