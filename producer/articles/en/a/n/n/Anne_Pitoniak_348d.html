Anne Pitoniak (born Westfield, Massachusetts, USA, March 30, 1922) is an American Actress.

She was nominated twice for Broadway's Tony Award: as Best Actress (Play) in 1983, for 'night, Mother, and as Best Actress (Featured Role - Play) in 1994, for a revival of William Inge's Picnic.
