Annette Dytrt (born September 7, 1983 in Landshut, Germany) is a German figure skater. She competed and won in the 1999 Czech National Championships under the name Annette Dytrt√≤va before competing for Germany. She placed 1st at German National Figure Skating Championships between 2003 and 2006. She also has made numerous appearances at the European and World Figure Skating Championships. 2006 she tryed pair skating with skating partner Norman Jeschke. However, Norman Jeschke finished his carreer after appearing in the TV-show "Stars auf Eis", a German version of Dancing on Ice. Annette Dytrt participated in the same show, but returned afterwards to single skating. She is now coached by Karel Fajfr in Oberstdorf.
