Anne Sinnemäki

Anni Milja Maaria Sinnemäki (b. July 20, 1973) is a Finnish politician and member of Finnish Parliament, representing the Green League. She was first elected to the parliament in 1999.

Sinnemäki was born in Helsinki, Finland. She has a B.A. degree in Russian literature from the University of Helsinki. She has one child, Siiri (b. 1990). She has written lyrics for Ultra Bra.
