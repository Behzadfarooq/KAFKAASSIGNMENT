Anna Nakagawa

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Anna Nakagawa (中川安奈, Nakagawa An'na?), real name Anna Kuriyama (栗山安奈, Kuriyama An'na?, born August 30, 1965 in Suginami, Tokyo, Japan) is an actress.
