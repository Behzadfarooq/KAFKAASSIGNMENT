Anna Sedokova

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia


Anna Sedokova (Анна Седокова, sometimes known as Аня Седакова, Anya Sedakova) is a Ukrainian pop singer, born on 16 December 1981 in Kiev.

Sedokova was part of the group Via Gra (known as Nu Virgos outside of the CIS territories), which she left when she married Valentin Belkevich, a midfielder from the football team FC Dynamo Kyiv. At this time she also became pregnant with her first child, Alina.

Sedokova was replaced in the group by Svetlana Loboda.

As of April 2006, Sedokova is attempting to relaunch her musical career, under the pseudonym 'Annabell'. Her first solo video, 'Moe Serdtse', has just been released.
