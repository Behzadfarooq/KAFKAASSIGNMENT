Anne Suzuki (鈴木杏 Suzuki An, born April 27, 1987) is a Japanese actress. She is sometimes referenced as An Suzuki.

A model since the age of three, she got an early break in her acting career at 12 when she starred in the Hollywood film Snow Falling on Cedars with Ethan Hawke. She was next featured in the action movie Returner opposite Takeshi Kaneshiro, and won the Best New Actor at the 26th Japan Academy Awards. She could also be seen in Hana and Alice, the new film from director Shunji Iwai, and Juvenile. She has also provided voice casting for the main character in Katsuhiro Otomo's 2004 anime film, Steamboy, and also takes the main female role of Natsuki Mogi in the Hong Kong race movie Initial D (頭文字 D) - based on the manga and anime series with the same title.
