Annabelle Apsion born 1963 is a British film and television actress.

She is best-known for playing "Joy Wilton" in Soldier Soldier, "Betty" in My Good Friend, "Beverly" in The Lakes, "Patricia Hillman" in Coronation Street, and "Monica Gallagher" in Shameless, and as "Mrs. Beach" in Michelle Magorian's Goodnight Mister Tom.
