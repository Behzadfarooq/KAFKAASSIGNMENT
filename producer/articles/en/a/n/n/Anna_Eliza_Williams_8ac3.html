Anna Eliza Williams

Anna Eliza (Davies) Williams was born in Gloucestershire, England, on June 2, 1873, and died in Swansea, Wales, on December 27, 1987 aged 114. She broke the UK longevity record in 1985. In 1987 she became the oldest undisputed person to date when she reached age 113 years 284 days. In June the same year, Anna reached age 114, becoming the first undisputed woman to do so. Her age was probably surpassed in 1989 by Jeanne Calment. Her United Kingdom record of 114 years 208 days was broken by Charlotte Hughes in 1992.
