Annulaires Afrique (French), or AfricaPhonebooks (English), is a group of African online telephone directories owned by The Global Super Pages. It currently serves the following areas:

  • Benin
  • Burkina Faso
  • Burundi
  • Cameroon
  • Chad
  • Central African Republic
  • Congo (Brazzaville)
  • Côte d'Ivoire
  • Djibouti
  • Equatorial Guinea
  • Gabon
  • Guinea
  • Mali
  • Mauritania
  • Niger
  • Rwanda
  • Senegal
  • Togo

Service is planned for the following countries.

  • Algeria
  • Angola
  • Comoros
  • French Polynesia
  • Madagascar
  • Mauritius
  • Morocco
  • Tunisia

The Global Super Pages' Asian service now comprises Cambodia, Greater Bangkok, Lao PDR, Malaysia and Myanmar.
