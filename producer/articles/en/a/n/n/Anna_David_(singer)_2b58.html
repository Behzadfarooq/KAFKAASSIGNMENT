Anna David (born December 2, 1984 in Århus, Denmark) is a Danish Pop singer. At the age of 14 she had her first recording contract in Germany, where she released 5 singles. Her First Album with the title Anna David appeared in September 2005 in Danish and went Gold in her homeland. The Song Fuck Dig held first place, in the Charts in Denmark,for twelve weeks. In October 2006, Anna re-recorded Fuck dig in German, under the title Fick Dich , which topped the single Charts both in Germany and Austria. Simultaneously, she recorded Fuck You , an English version of the song, which received the Danish Payload Prize 2005 ,as the most downloaded song of the year

In 2006 she participated in the Danish version of Strictly Come Dancing (Danish: Vild med dans)
