Annie Martin

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Annie Martin (born September 7, 1981 in Sherbrooke, Quebec) is a Canadian beach volleyball player. She and her partner Guylaine Dumont tied for fifth in the women's beach volleyball event at the 2004 Summer Olympics, after losing to the American pair of Kerri Walsh and Misty May in the quarterfinals.
