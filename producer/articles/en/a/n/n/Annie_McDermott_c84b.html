Annie McDermott is a character on the soap opera All My Children who made her first appearance on July 10, 2006. Annie is played by Melissa Claire Egan.

[edit] Background

Little is known of Annie McDermott's past, save that she divorced her ex-husband Terry when she learned he had a penchant for seducing teenage virgin girls. Fearing what he would do to their daughter Emma when she reached puberty, she sued for custody but lost thanks to Terry's impeccable record. Desperate, Annie kidnapped Emma and took her on the run. With help from Erin Lavery and Jamal Cudahy, Annie hid out from Terry and the authorities until September 2006 when Terry was killed by Jonathan Lavery. Prior to Terry's death, Annie mistook Jonathan's brother Ryan Lavery for Terry and shot him. Ryan survived the shooting.

Annie's ex-husband Terry, as it turns out, was not Emma's birth father because Annie had been artificially inseminated. A DNA test done during the divorce and custody proceedings showed that Annie was not Emma's biological mother. The evil David Hayward told Dixie Cooney-Martin that he had run a DNA test on Emma and, lying to Dixie (in order to get her hopes up and to see him as a savior), found out that Emma was actually Dixie's and Tad Martin's lost daughter Kate. Annie became angry at Dixie's belief that Emma was Kate, but refused to take a DNA test because she knew the results of the earlier DNA test done during her divorce from Terry. She later relented and another DNA test was done (without Hayward's involvement and which used samples from Tad, Dixie, and Annie) which proved that Emma was not Kate. Relieved at the result, Annie then learned from Ryan Lavery (who had become her friend) that she may possess a DNA condition called chimera, which meant that she had two sets of DNA. More DNA tests were run at various labs, and finally one lab proved that Annie finally was the biological mother of Emma. In the process, Annie and Ryan became closer and have since gone on their first date. Annie has since confided to Kendall Hart that she is falling in love with Ryan. Kendall is somewhat perturbed because 1) before leaving, David Hayward told her that Emma is actually Ryan's own daughter; and 2) now Annie is a rival for Ryan's affections, even though Kendall is married to Zach Slater and has a son, Spike, conceived via artificial insemination with Ryan. Kendall has since asked her half-brother, Dr. Josh Madden, to run a DNA test to see if Ryan truly is Emma's biological father. On the December 28, 2006 episode, Kendall learned that Ryan is Emma's biological father, and now faces a choice on whether or not to tell Ryan of this. Ryan discovers he is Emma's biological father, when he and Josh Madden drug Zach Slater with truth serum. He hesitates to tell Annie that he is the father of her daughter, but finally tells Annie the truth. He and Annie get closer.
