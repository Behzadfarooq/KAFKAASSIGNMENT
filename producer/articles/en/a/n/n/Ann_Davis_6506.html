Ann Davis (born 17 June 1938) is an American model. She was Playboy magazine's Playmate of the Month for its September 1960 issue. Her centerfold was photographed by Don Bronstein. Her pictorial was also noteworthy because the magazine had several prominent artists, including LeRoy Neiman, produce their own illustrations of Davis.

Ann attended the University of Cincinnati.

She is not to be confused with the actress Ann B. Davis, best known for her role as Alice on "The Brady Bunch".
