Anna Benson

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Anna Benson (born February 12, 1976 in Atlanta, Georgia) is an American model and wife of Baltimore Orioles pitcher Kris Benson. Benson is known for her looks and controversial, outspoken personality.

[edit] Early years

Anna Benson was born outside of Atlanta in 1976. In 1992, Anna moved out of her parents' house when she was 16, because she didn't get along with them. [1] In 1993, Anna married a 19-year old dentist at 17. She and her husband had a child together, but they divorced in late 1994.

In 1998, Anna met then-minor league pitcher Kris Benson. The couple dated and then married. She and Kris did a spicy article for Penthouse. In 1999, Kris was called up to the majors and pitched for the Pittsburgh Pirates. The couple has 3 children.

[edit] Life in New York

On July 30, 2004, the Pirates traded Kris to the New York Mets. In November 2004, Anna posed for FHM magazine. FHM called her baseball's hottest wife. The combination of the photo shoot and a Howard Stern interview made Anna more popular in New York.

In July 2005, she began playing at the 2005 World Series of Poker in Las Vegas under the nickname "Gold Digger".

In November 2005, Anna was supposedly negotiating to pose nude for Playboy, but the two sides couldn't come to an agreement over money. A month later, Anna generated more controversy by attending the New York Mets Christmas party wearing a low-cut Santa outfit red dress. Benson created more controversy with her statement that Met VP Omar Minaya was "putting together an all-Latino team." On January 21, 2006, the Mets traded Kris to the Baltimore Orioles for pitchers Jorge Julio and John Maine. Some think Anna is the reason why Benson was traded from the Mets to Orioles for the 2006 season[citation needed]. She held that it was strictly a business decision but also maintained that she would not change her behavior with her husband pitching for the Baltimore Orioles, seeming to suggest that she might have recognized that her outspoken personality might have had something to do with her husband's departure from New York, whether she wanted to admit it or not. During the baseball season, Anna went on Howard Stern's radio show and said that she would have sex with all members of the New York Mets organization, including the grounds crew, if she were ever to learn he had an affair.

On March 30, 2006, Anna was served papers for a divorce. The divorce petition for divorce claimed the marriage is "irretrievably broken." But, on April 4th, Kris withdrew the petition.

Anna posed for the April 2006 issue of FHM magazine, which was a baseball season preview and an interview of Anna and Kris. On June 17, 2006, Kris pitched against the Mets in an interleague game at Shea Stadium. Met fans taunted Kris and chanted Let's Go, Anna!! On August 18, 2006, Anna posed naked on the cover of Sporting News.

Currently, Anna lives in Atlanta.
