Anna Wilson-Jones

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Anna Wilson-Jones is a British actress. Most recently she has appeared in the Sky One UK television series Hex, as Jo Watkins, and the Channel 4 adaptation of the novel Sugar Rush, as Anna. She has also appeared in As If, Monarch of the Glen and Spaced.

Anna Wilson-Jones features in the ITV parapsychology drama Afterlife in which she plays Jude Bridge, the ex-wife of Robert Bridge, played by Andrew Lincoln.
