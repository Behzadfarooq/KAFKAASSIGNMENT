Annapolis High School is located in Anne Arundel County, Maryland on Riva Road, near the city of Annapolis.

Annapolis is the second oldest high school in the county, having existed since 1898, after Arundel High School (1854).

It is also one of the older public secondary schools in the nation. The student body is 1,712 as of the 2005 - 2006 school year. It has Black (44.5%), White (39.6%), Hispanic (13.0%), and Asian (2.9%), and 2 Native American students. It is a host school of the International Baccalaureate program. The other two IB schools in the county are Meade High School and Old Mill High School. AHS feeder middle schools are Bates Middle School, and Annapolis Middle School; however, because of its magnet program, students are also bused in from other parts of the county.
