Anne of Burgundy

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Anne de Bourgogne, or Anne of Burgundy (died 14 November 1432) was the daughter of John "the Fearless", Duke of Burgundy 1404-1419 and his wife Margaret of Bavaria.

In June 1423 at Troyes, she married John of Lancaster, Duke of Bedford. She died in childbed in Paris and was buried there at the Church of the Celestines.
