Ann Kristin Aarønes (born in Ålesund, Norway, January 19, 1973) is a Norwegian football (soccer) player. She is now retired. She played first for Spjelkavik, then for Trondheims-Ørn and the Norwegian National Team. Later she played for the WUSA's New York Power, during the first season. She is probably most famous for being the top scorer at the 1995 Women's World Cup. She retired in 2001.
