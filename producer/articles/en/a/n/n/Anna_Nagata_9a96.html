Anna Nagata (永田杏奈, Nagata Anna?, born March 29, 1982) is a Japanese actress.

Nagata is best known for her role as Hirono Shimizu in Battle Royale. She also starred as Yuzuki Misaki in Kamen Rider Kabuto and as Yoko Okazaki in One Missed Call (2003).
