[edit] Balsam Fir at Spanish wiki

Hi AnnaP - I saw you added this in Spanish as es:Abeto de Navidad - when I checked in some plant dictionaries, Abies balsamea is given as abeto balsámico in Spanish, whereas abeto de Navidad is a general term equating in English to Christmas tree, not a particular species. I have edited es:Abeto de Navidad accordingly, but (as I don't have a Spanish wiki account) can't move the page to es:abeto balsámico. Also at the Spanish wiki, maybe es:Pinus halepensis should be moved to its Spanish name es:Pino carrasco? (currently a redirect to es:Pinus halepensis) - MPF 13:44, 25 Nov 2004 (UTC)

[edit] Image:Ottoman small animation.gif

Hi Anna, happy new year! About this image: there is short answer and a more complicated answer. The short one is that images from EB are protected by copyright and can not be used for derivative work, hence a copyright violation. The complicated answer is that a simple map of Europe outlining the continent is probably too simple to be considered a creative work, hence not a copyright violation. It all depends on what the original map looked like. Copyright is difficult and there is no clear answer here. Perhaps the best would be if somebody created a replacement map and placed it under a free license? Regards, Thuresson 18:30, 17 January 2006 (UTC)
