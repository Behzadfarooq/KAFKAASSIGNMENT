Anne of Green Gables (1972 film)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Anne of Green Gables (1972) is a made-for-television British mini-series directed by Joan Craft based upon the novel, Anne of Green Gables by Lucy Maud Montgomery.

[edit] Cast

  • Kim Braden - Anne Shirley
  • Christopher Blake - Gilbert Blythe
  • Barbara Hamilton - Marilla Cuthbert
  • Elliott Sullivan - Matthew Cuthbert
