Annunciation Greek Orthodox Church

Annunciation Greek Orthodox Church in Milwaukee, Wisconsin was designed by famous architect Frank Lloyd Wright in 1956, and completed in 1961. It is listed on the National Register of Historic Places.

Annunciation Greek Orthodox Church is one of Wright's last works. Its shallow scalloped dome echos his Marin County Civic Center.
