Anna of Masovia

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Princess Anna of Masovia (1498-c.1557) of the Piast dynasty.

Anna was the daughter of Duke Konrad III Rudy and Princess Anna Radziwiłł. She married Stanisław Odrowąż in February 1536.

Children: Zofia Odrowąż
