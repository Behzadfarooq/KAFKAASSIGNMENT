Anny Road End is a song sung by Liverpool Supporters who are in the Anfield Road End at Anfield.

We're all mad, we're all round the bend
We are the Anny road end,
We're not fucking Kopite Gobshites,
We're going down the Road end for the big fight, Na na na

The reference to 'the big fight' comes from the fact that the away fans are placed in the Anfield Road End.
