Anna Nitschmann

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Anna Caritas Nitschmann born (November 24, 1715 Kunewalde near Fulnek, Moravia - died May 21, 1760, Herrnhut, Lusatia) was a Moravian Brethren missionary (Missionarin), lyrical poetess, and the second wife of Nicolaus Ludwig Zinzendorf.
