Anne Marie Howard

Anne Marie Howard (born on March 31, 1960) is an actress and former spokeswoman for Ditech.com.

Anne Marie is the oldest of five children and was born in San Diego, California at the Balboa Naval Hospital where her dad was a dentist in the navy. At the age of two they moved to Ottumwa, Iowa and when she was fourteen the family moved to Davenport, Iowa.

She has appeared in over 100 commercials including Lifesavers, the George Foreman Grill, Quaker Oats, and AARP. Anne's television guest appearances include Cold Case, ER, Judging Amy, Boston Public, Another World and Days of Our Lives. Her film appearances include The Weather Man with Nicholas Cage, Prince of Darkness and Model Behavior.
