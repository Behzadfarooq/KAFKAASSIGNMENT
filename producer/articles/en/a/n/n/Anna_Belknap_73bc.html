Anna Belknap (born May 22, 1972 in Damariscotta, Maine, USA) is an American actress. She joined the cast of CSI: NY during the second season starting in the episode "Zoo York". She has also acted in NBC's Law & Order: Special Victims Unit (as a rape victim in the episode "Manhunt"), Without a Trace ("Endgame" and "Showdown"), Medical Investigation, and The Handler.

  • Belknap is a member of the Rude Mechanicals Theater Company. (rudemechanicals.org)
  • Belknap received her B.A. from Middlebury College in Vermont, and a masters in Acting from the American Conservatory Theater.
  • She is married to Eric Siegel, who is also a member of the Rude Mechanicals. They welcomed their first child, a daughter, on January 14, 2007.
