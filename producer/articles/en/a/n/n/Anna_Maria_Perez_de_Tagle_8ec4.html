Anna Maria Perez de Tagle (born on December 23, 1990 in San Francisco, California) is a Filipino American actress, model, and singer.

[edit] Personal life

She was born in San Francisco, but was raised in San Jose. She moved to southern California with her family when she accepted the role of Ashley Dewitt in the Disney Channel show Hannah Montana. She is a sophomore in high school. She learned how to sing from her grandmother, Sylvia La Torre. She is also a Filipina.

[edit] Career

She competed on Star Search 2 in 2003, singing the song "Somewhere" from the movie West Side Story. Anna Maria also had a bit part in the movie "Bee Season". She has starred in local productions of Cinderella, Bugsy Malone, and The King and I. She is probably best known for playing Ashley Dewitt in the Disney Channel show Hannah Montana.

Anna Maria has also done much modeling work. She has been featured in many catalogs, including Macys and Mervyns. She was also on the cover of the magazine Filipinas, which featured a two page article about her.

Anna Maria also stars as Miracle Ross in the CBS Saturday morning show Cake, as well as providing the voice of the Safety Patrol Kid in Higglytown Heroes

Anna Maria has a unique, distinguishing part in TV history. She appears on two Saturday morning U.S. network TV shows at the same time. She is a cast member of the CBS/KOL show Cake and a recurring role on ABC/Disney Channel's Hannah Montana.
