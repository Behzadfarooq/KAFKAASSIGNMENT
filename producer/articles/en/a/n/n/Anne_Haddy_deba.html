[edit] Biography

Born in Quorn, South Australia, she is probably most famous for her role as Helen Daniels in the popular Australian soap opera Neighbours. She played this role from the very first episode in 1985 to episode 2,965 in 1997 when she was forced to give up the role due to ill health.

Other programmes in which Haddy appeared include Dynasty (the 1970-71 Australian television series), Prisoner, Punishment and Sons and Daughters. She also appeared in the film They're a Weird Mob (1966).

After suffering from stomach cancer and various other heart and kidney ailments, she reluctantly quit Neighbours in 1997. In 1999, she died peacefully, with her actor husband, James Condon beside her. She had five children and six grandchildren.
