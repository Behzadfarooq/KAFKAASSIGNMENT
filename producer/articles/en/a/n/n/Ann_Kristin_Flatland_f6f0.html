Ann Kristin Flatland (born November 6, 1982 in Oslo). Is a Norwegian biathlete. Ann Kristin Flatland debuted in the World Cup in 2003 in the finnish Kontiolahti, there she came 56th in the sprint event already in the second world cup weekend in Hochfilzen she debuted on the norwegian relay team who finished 5th. Her best world cup placing so far is a 15th place from the 15 km in Khanty-Mansiysk in 2004. She has also been a part of taking a third place in the relay in (Hochfilzen 2006). She represented Norway at the World Championship 2005, in Hochfilzen and came 22nd in the sprint and 37th in the pursuit
