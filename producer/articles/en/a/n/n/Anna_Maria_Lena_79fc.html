Anna Maria Lena (Greek script: Άννα Μαρία Λένα) was the Cypriot entry in the Eurovision Song Contest 1984, performed in Greek by Andy Paul.
The song was performed seventh on the night (following the United Kingdom's Belle & The Devotions with Love Games and preceding Belgium's Jacques Zegers with Avanti La Vie). At the close of voting, it had received 31 points, placing 15th in a field of 19.

The song is a love ballad directed towards the tititular character, with Paul singing "your name is all I sing , Anna".

It was succeeded as Cypriot representative at the 1985 Contest by Lia Vissi with To Katalava Arga.
