Annika Suthe

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Annika Suthe (born October 15, 1985) is a German javelin thrower.

She won the silver medal at the 2004 World Junior Championships finished eighth at the 2006 European Athletics Championships in Gothenburg. She also competed in the 2004 Olympics.

Her personal best throw is 61.38 metres, achieved in May 2004 in Halle. This ranks her sixth among German javelin throwers, behind Christina Obergföll, Tanja Damaske, Steffi Nerius, Karen Forkel and Dörthe Friedrich. [1]
