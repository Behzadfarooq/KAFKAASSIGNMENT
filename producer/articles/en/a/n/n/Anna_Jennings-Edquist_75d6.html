Anna Jennings-Edquist (born April 13, 1985) is an Australian actor who played Tahnee Coppin on the Australian soap opera Neighbours.

[edit] Trivia

  • Anna wrote and directed The Throwback Players' 2006 production of Heat. She also directed the 2006 University of Melbourne Law Revue.[1]
  • She is dating Australian comedian Hamish Blake.
