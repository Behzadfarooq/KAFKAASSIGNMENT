Anne Dudek (b. March 22, 1975, Boston, Massachusetts) is an actress from Newton, Massachusetts.

She played the role of cruise line heiress Tiffany Wilson in the 2004 comedy film White Chicks. She also starred in the Channel 4 sitcom, The Book Group; she has appeared as a guest star on Desperate Housewives (as Karl Mayer's girlfriend in Season 1), Friends, Charmed, Bones and Six Feet Under, as well as playing a disposable part in the pilot episode of Psych. Most recently, she played a sex offending teacher on Law and Order: CI.
