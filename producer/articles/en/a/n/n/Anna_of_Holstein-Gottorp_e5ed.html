Anna of Holstein-Gottorp

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Anna of Holstein-Gottorp (27 February 1575 â€“ 24 April 1610) was a German noblewoman.

She was a daughter of Adolf of Holstein-Gottorp and Langravine Christine of Hesse-Kassel (daughter of Landgrave Philip I of Hesse).

On 28 January 1598 she married Count Enno III of Ostfriesland. They had the following children:

  1. Anna Maria, Countess of Ostfriesland (1601-1633), married Adolf Friedrich I, Duke of Mecklenburg-Schwerin (1588-1658)
  2. Ullrich II, Count of Ostfriesland (1605-1648)
