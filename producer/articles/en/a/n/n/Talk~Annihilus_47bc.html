[edit] Ultimate Annihilus

The Ultimate Annihilus section is a mess. While it is interesting to ponder that the Ultimate Fantastic Four made a crossover, of sorts, to the standard Marvel Universe by entering the same Negative Zone that the 616 Fantastic Four have traveled to, the article as it is now is a head-ache to read. Offcially the Ultimate Universe is Earth 1610 and the classic Marvel Universe is 616. In the Ultimate comic they are told by Nihil that the Ultimate FF are from the universe above his. The Ultimate N-Zone is then presumably Marvel Universe 1609. That is to say, there is an N-Zone *and* an Ultimate N-Zone. I'm just going to alter the aticle now under this assumption. If it really bothers anyone, revert it, and at least give me an arguement against mine. --TylerXKJ 03:47, 18 June 2006 (UTC)
