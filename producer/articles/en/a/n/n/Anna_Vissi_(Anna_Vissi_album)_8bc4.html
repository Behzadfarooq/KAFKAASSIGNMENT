Anna Vissi is the name of a Greek album by singer Anna Vissi. It was also re-released in 1982 again. It was released in Greece and Cyprus were it reached Platinum. The 1982 re-release included the bonus song Mono H Agapi which represented Cyprus in Eurovision 1982.

[edit] Music

Music of the album is by N.Leonardos, Anna Vissi, and Afoi Tzavara. Lyrics of the album are by Giannis Parios, N. Leonardos, Anna Vissi, and N. Leonardos


[edit] Trivia

    • Nikos Leornados is the "alias" name of Nikos Karvelas.
    • The album was re-relased in 1982.
    • The album was released in compact disk for the first time in 2006.
