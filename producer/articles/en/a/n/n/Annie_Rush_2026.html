Annie Rush

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Annie Rush is an artist and game designer. She has written several games for the Wicked Dead Brewing Company and has been called "...a designer to watch." by Kenneth Hite.

Among the games she has published through WDBC are Run Robot Red, The Secret Lives of Gingerbread Men, and InTERRORgation.

After great success publishing with the Wicked Dead Brewing Company, Annie ventured on her own with Itesser, Inc., self-publishing her own games.

She is married to artist Reagan Lodge.
