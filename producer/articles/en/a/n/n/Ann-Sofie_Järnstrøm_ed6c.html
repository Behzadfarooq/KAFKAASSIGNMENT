Ann-Sofie Järnstrøm

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Ann-Sofie Järnstrøm (born July 16, 1949) is a former ice speed skater from Sweden, who represented her native country at three consecutive Winter Olympics, starting in 1972 in Sapporo, Japan.
