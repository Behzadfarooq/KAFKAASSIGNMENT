Annville is a census-designated place (CDP) in Lebanon County, Pennsylvania, United States. The population was 4,518 at the 2000 census.

[edit] Geography

Border detail of Annville and surrounding municipalities

Annville is located at 40°19′49″N, 76°30′45″W (40.330162, -76.512533)GR1.

According to the United States Census Bureau, the CDP has a total area of 4.1 km² (1.6 mi²). 4.1 km² (1.6 mi²) of it is land and 0.63% is water.

The township is bordered to the north and west by North Annville Township (3.2 mi), to the south and west by South Annville Township (3 mi), and to the east by Cleona (0.47 mi) and North Cornwall Township (0.25 mi).

[edit] Demographics

As of the censusGR2 of 2000, there were 4,518 people, 1,452 households, and 917 families residing in the CDP. The population density was 1,104.1/km² (2,864.6/mi²). There were 1,556 housing units at an average density of 380.2/km² (986.6/mi²). The racial makeup of the CDP was 95.40% White, 1.08% African American, 0.02% Native American, 1.24% Asian, 0.11% Pacific Islander, 0.97% from other races, and 1.17% from two or more races. Hispanic or Latino of any race were 1.79% of the population.

There were 1,452 households out of which 27.8% had children under the age of 18 living with them, 51.0% were married couples living together, 8.3% had a female householder with no husband present, and 36.8% were non-families. 29.1% of all households were made up of individuals and 11.8% had someone living alone who was 65 years of age or older. The average household size was 2.35 and the average family size was 2.87.

In the CDP the population was spread out with 16.3% under the age of 18, 31.2% from 18 to 24, 23.1% from 25 to 44, 15.9% from 45 to 64, and 13.5% who were 65 years of age or older. The median age was 28 years. For every 100 females there were 88.1 males. For every 100 females age 18 and over, there were 85.3 males.

The median income for a household in the CDP was $37,415, and the median income for a family was $44,737. Males had a median income of $35,057 versus $23,458 for females. The per capita income for the CDP was $16,586. About 1.5% of families and 6.3% of the population were below the poverty line, including 3.6% of those under age 18 and 6.0% of those age 65 or over.

[edit] Points of Interest

  • The Allen Theatre, a historic theater and coffee house
  • Lebanon Valley College, a private four-year institution with an enrollment of approximately 1,700 undergraduate students.

[edit] Notable residents

  • John W. Killinger, (1824-1896), born in Annville, lawyer, prosecuting attorney, member of the Pennsylvania House of Representatives, Pennsylvania Senate, and United States House of Representatives. [1]
  • Steve Blackman, professional wrestler
  • Brian Aungst, Mayor of Clearwater, Florida
