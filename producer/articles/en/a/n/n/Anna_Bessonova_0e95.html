Anna Bessonova Ukrainian: Ганна Володїмїріівна Безсонова Ganna Volodymyriivna Bezsonova / Russian: Анна Владимировна Бессонова Anna Vladimirovna Biessonova is an Individual Rhythmic Gymnast born July 29, 1984 in Kyiv, Ukraine. Her father is the famous Dynamo Kiev football player Vladimir Bessonov and her mother, Viktoria, was a two time world champion group rhythmic gymnast.

Anna was put into rhythmic gymnastics at age 5, her mother would've rather seen her daughter on a ballet stage but, Anna said she wanted to do rhythmic gymnastics. She is coached by the mother/daughter combination of Albina and Irina Deriugina at the Deriugins School in Kyiv.

Anna is the number one rhythmic gymnast in Ukraine and she finished 3rd at the 2004 Athens Olympics with a total of 106.700 (Ribbon 26.725, Clubs 26.950, Ball 26.525, Hoop 26.500).
