Anne Stuart

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

For Anne Stuart, Queen of Britain, see Anne of Great Britain

Anne Stuart is an enormously popular and prolific romance novelist with a career spanning over 30 years. She has written over 40 novels and was the recipient of the Romance Writers of Americaâ€™s Lifetime Achievement Award. [1].

Her first book was published in 1974 by Ballantine Press when she was 25 years old. Since then, her books have been published by numerous publishers such as Dell, Doubleday, St. Martin's Press and currently Harlequin.
