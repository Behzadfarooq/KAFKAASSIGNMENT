Anne Marie Hoogland

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Anne Marie Hoogland (1966) is a Dutch politician from Amsterdam. She is currently chairman of the Westerpark district. She ran for the chairmanship of the Dutch Labour Party in 2005 and finished third.

[edit] Tessa-case

In 2001, she was embarrassed by the fact that she had hidden the rape of a 13-year-old mentally retarded girl by 14 boys from the neighourhood and media, because most of the boys were of Moroccan descent. Later, it became known that even more sex crimes were hidden by her and the district. The affair became known as the Tessa-case.

Hoogland denied "with her hand on her heart" that she had hidden the affair from the people because the rapists were foreigners. Yet former district chairman M. Voster (also of the Labour party) said that it was "policy" to hide sex crimes committed by Moroccans from the neighourhood, "because that occurs so often lately."
