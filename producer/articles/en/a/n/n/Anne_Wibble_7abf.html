Anne Wibble

Anne Wibble (born Anne Ohlin, 13 October 1943 - 14 March 2000) was a Swedish politician (member of the Liberal People's Party). From 1991 to 1994 she served as Minister for Finance. She was the daughter of Bertil Ohlin, a Nobel laureate.
