Ann Marie Crooks

Ann Marie Crooks is a former female bodybuilder and professional wrestler. As a bodybuilder, Crooks won the Ms. Sunshine State competition in 1992 and placed well in several other bodybuilding events through 1998, finishing as high as 2nd place in the 1994 NPC Nationals (heavyweights).

In 1999, she began a short career in with the WCW, wrestling under the name 'Midnight'. She was valet to Booker T and Stevie Ray there and trained at the WCW Power Plant. Her last match was in 2001 when she wrestled Nicole Bass.

[edit] Contest history (bodybuilding)

  • 1992 Ms. Sunshine State - 1st
  • 1993 Ms. Florida - 1st
  • 1993 NPC Nationals - 4th (HW)
  • 1993 IFBB North American Championship - 2nd (HW)
  • 1994 NPC Nationals - 2nd (HW)
  • 1996 NPC Nationals - 3rd (HW)
  • 1998 NPC USA Championship - 4th (HW)
  • 1998 NPC Nationals - 8th (HW)
