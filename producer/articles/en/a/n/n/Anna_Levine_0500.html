From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Anna Levine
Born September 18, 1955 (age 51)
New York City, United States

Anna Levine is an American actress. She has also been credited as Anna Levine Thompson, Anna Thompson, Anna Levine Thomson and Anna Thomson.

[edit] Partial filmography

  • Bridget (2002) as Bridget
  • Water Drops on Burning Rocks (2000) as Véra
  • Six Ways to Sunday (1997) as Annibelle
  • Sue (1997) as Sue
  • Bad Boys (1995 film) (1995) as Francine (credited as Anna Thomson)
  • The Crow (1994) as Darla
  • True Romance (1993) as Lucy
  • Unforgiven (1992) as Delilah Fitzgerald
  • Talk Radio (1988) as Woman at basketball game/Denise
  • Desperately Seeking Susan (1985) as Crystal
