Anna Blundy (born 1970 in London), is a British author and journalist, educated at City of London School for Girls and Westminster School. Every Time We Say Goodbye, her memoir of her father, the foreign correspondent David Blundy (March 21, 1945 â€“ November 11, 1989), was published in 1998. Her series of novels about her female war correspondent heroine, Faith Zanetti, started with The Bad News Bible in 2004. The second in a series, Faith Without Doubt, was published in September 2005. The third in the series, Neat Vodka was published in September 2006 by Little Brown.

Anna Blundy studied Russian at Oxford University and is a former columnist , going on to be Moscow Bureau Chief for the London Times during the 1998-9 financial crisis. She has appeared since 2006 on BBC Television's Newsnight Review.
