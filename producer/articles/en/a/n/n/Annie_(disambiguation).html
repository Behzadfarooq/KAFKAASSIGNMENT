Annie (disambiguation)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Annie is the diminutive form of the feminine given names Anna, Ann, Anne, and other variations.

Use of the name Annie may refer to:

  • Little Orphan Annie, the star of the comic strip of the same name
  • Annie, the 1977 stage musical, its 1982 film, 1999 television adaptation, and sequels in all three formats
  • Anne Lilia Berge Strand, a Norwegian pop singer known by the stage name Annie
  • Annie Ghazikhanian, a character in the X-Men comic book series
  • The Annie Award for animation honors.
  • Rescue Annie, a doll used to teach CPR/EAR
  • Annie Leibovitz, American portrait photographer.
  • Annie Hamilton, a fictional character in the Power Instinct series of fighting games.
  • Annie Sprinkle American performance artist and sex educator
This disambiguation page lists articles associated with the same title. If an internal link led you here, you may wish to change the link to point directly to the intended article.
