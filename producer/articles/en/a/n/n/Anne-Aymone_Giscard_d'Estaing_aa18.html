Anne-Aymone Giscard d'Estaing

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Anne-Aymone Marie Josèphe Christiane Sauvage de Brantès (b. 10 April 1933) is the wife and distant cousin of former President of France Valéry Giscard d'Estaing.

Though she bears no noble title, she is a daughter of Count François Marie Joseph Abel Henri Sauvage de Brantès, son of the Marquis de Brantès, who died in on Mauthausen-Gusen concentration camp on 8 May 1944, and his wife, the former Princess Aymone Marie Sylvie Renée de Faucigny-Lucinge et Coligny, a great-great-granddaughter of Charles Ferdinand, duc de Berry by his mistress Amy Brown. She also is a great-niece of the Cuban-born French designer and architect José Emilio Terry y Dorticos and the sister of Paul Dominique Marie Joseph Sauvage de Brantès, the present Marquis de Brantès.
