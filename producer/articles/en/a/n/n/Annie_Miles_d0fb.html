Annie Miles (born 5 May 1958 in Tyneside, England) is an actress who is best known for her roles in television soap opera.

She played Sue Sullivan (nee Harper) in Brookside from 1987 to 1991, and Maria Simons Starr in Family Affairs from 1997 to 2000.
