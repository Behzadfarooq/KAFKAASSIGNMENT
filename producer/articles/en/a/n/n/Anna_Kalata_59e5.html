Anna Kalata

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Anna Kalata (born May 10, 1964) in Milanówek in Poland) is a Polish politician, member of the populist Samoobrona party. In Jarosław Kaczyński's cabinet she is the minister of labour and social policy.
