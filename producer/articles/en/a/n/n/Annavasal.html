Annavasal is a panchayat town in Pudukkottai district in the state of Tamil Nadu, India.

[edit] Geography

Annavasal is located at 10.47° N 78.7° E[1]. It has an average elevation of 128 metres (419 feet).

[edit] Demographics

As of 2001 India censusGRIndia, Annavasal had a population of 7630. Males constitute 49% of the population and females 51%. Annavasal has an average literacy rate of 67%, higher than the national average of 59.5%; with 55% of the males and 45% of females literate. 12% of the population is under 6 years of age.
