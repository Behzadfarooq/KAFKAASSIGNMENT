Annie's Lane is a wine label from the Clare Valley of South Australia. Named after a local Clare Valley legend, Annie Wayman[1], Annie's Lane released its first wine in 1996[1], and has since won over three-hundred-and-fifty awards at local and international wine shows. The Annie's Lane label is part of the Foster's Group/Beringer Blass Wine Estates Limited[2][3].

Annie's Lane is based at the Quelltaler Winery, a heritage listed property, in Watervale in the south of Clare Valley.

The winery's site manager is Peter Pawelski and the head wine maker is Mark Robertson.

[edit] Wine range

Annie's Lane releases wine under the two major labels, the Annie's Lane Copper Trail Range and the Annie's Lane Clare Valley range[4].
