Ann Gora is the star reporter for Kat's Eye News and is voiced by Candi Milo in the animated television series SWAT Kats: The Radical Squadron. Her name is a pun on the name of the cat breed angora.

Ann Gora is endlessly curious and always on the hunt for a big, exciting story. She hates covering mundane events like the grand opening of the Megakat Tower in the episode Destructive Nature (and later its re-opening in The Ci-Kat-A) and the Manx Invitational Golf Tournament in Chaos in Crystal, despite the fact that things rarely stay mundane for very long. Although a good reporter, Ann is unfortunately also quite reckless and doesn't weigh the potential consequences before she leaps into the middle of a situation, a fact that usually tends to get her into trouble.

Examples of this include her pushing Al to fly the Kat's Eye News helicopter closer to the Doomsday Express in The Wrath of Dark Kat, which resulted in them being shot down by the airship's heat beam, and when she disguised herself as a miner in Caverns of Horror in order to accompany Lt. Felina Feral and The Enforcers into the mines of the Megakat Metallurgical Company, which resulted in her being captured by a giant, mutant scorpion.

However, Ann has come through at times and proven herself to be an extremely capable and valuable provider of information, such as when she alerted Commander Feral to the fact that Deputy Mayor Callie Briggs was trapped within the Megakat Tower during its takeover by Dr. Viper and the Plantimals in Destructive Nature (which likely saved the Deputy Mayor's life as it prevented the Enforcers from simply destroying the building - although, to be fair, Mayor Manx would've stopped that even if Miss Briggs hadn't been trapped inside), and when she and her cameraman Jonny stumbled upon the invasion plans of the Ci-Kat-A at the M.A.S.A. Space Center and were able to alert the Swat Kats in the episode The Ci-Kat-A.
