Annie Parkhouse

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Annie Parkhouse has been one of the leading letterers in British comics for over 30 years. Beginning her career working on Lion for IPC magazines, she has since provided dialogue for many DC Comics titles and 2000AD, working on scripts by writers and artists such as Alan Moore and Garry Leach (on Marvelman). Annie has been the recipient of an Eagle Award for her lettering. She is married to British writer/artist Steve Parkhouse, and continues to work for 2000AD, Rugrats comic and Hellblazer, among others.

Following the death of Tom Frame, Parkhouse became the lead letterer on the Judge Dredd strip.
