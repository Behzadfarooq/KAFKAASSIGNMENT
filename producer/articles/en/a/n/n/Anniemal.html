Anniemal is the debut album by Norwegian pop artist Annie, first released in the United Kingdom and Europe on March 7, 2005 and in the United States on June 7, 2005 (see 2005 in music). The album featured the hit singles "Chewing Gum" and "Heartbeat".

Annie has developed somewhat of a cult following in colleges across the United States especially those in the northeast.[citation needed]
