Anne-Marie Sim is a Hong Kong television newsreader and journalist working for ATV news. She presents the "Main News" on ATV World at 19:30 in tandem with Nick Waters.

[edit] Career

Anne-Marie Sim has Journalism as well as English Literature degrees from both Cardiff University and Buckingham University. After working for various in-house, television and press organisations, she joined ATV in 2000. [[1]]
