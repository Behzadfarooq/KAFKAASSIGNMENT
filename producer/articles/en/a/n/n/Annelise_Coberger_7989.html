Annelise Coberger (born September 16, 1971 in Christchurch) is a former New Zealand alpine skier. She became the first person from the Southern Hemisphere to win a medal at the Winter Olympics when she won silver in the slalom at Albertville in France in 1992.


[edit] Achievements

1992 Winter Olympics in Albertville:

  • Silver at Slalom

Alpine skiing World Championship 1991 in Saalbach-Hinterglemm:

  • thirteenth place at Combined

1 World Cup race victories at Slalom
