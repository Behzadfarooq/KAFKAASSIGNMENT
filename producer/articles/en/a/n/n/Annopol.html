Annopol ( listen) is a small town in south-eastern Poland with 2,679 (2004) inhabitants, in Kraśnik County. It has been situated in the Lublin Voivodeship (since 1999) previously in Tarnobrzeg Voivodeship (1975-1998). It is also the birthplace of Dorota Barbara Jabłonowska, wife of Prince Józef Klemens Czartoryski (1760-1844).

[edit] History

Annopol received city rights in 1761, lost these in 1869 and received these again in 1969. The arms show St. Anna, the patron saint of the town (the name means the field of Anna).
