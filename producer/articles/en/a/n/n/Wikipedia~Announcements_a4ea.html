Announcement page
 
This page is for brief summaries of news items about the Wikipedia website and the Wikimedia foundation. Longer articles can be found at Wikipedia:Signpost, the weekly newspaper about Wikipedia. News items that are not about Wikipedia can be found at Wikinews. See also: this page's archive and its RSS feed.
 
What should be featured?
  • Important milestones, statistics and Alexa ranking news concerning the English Wikipedia (see Special:Statistics).
  • Any news concerning the Wikimedia Foundation that affects the English Wikipedia.
What shouldn't be featured!
  • News and happenings in the Wikipedia community. Visit instead Wikipedia:News or the Wikipedia:Community Portal.
  • Information about Wikipedia in the news. Visit instead Wikipedia:Press coverage.
  • This is not a page for comments about news and updates. Visit instead the Wikipedia:Village pump (news).

[edit] Software status

Special:Version shows which release of MediaWiki the site is currently running on. Report bugs to MediaZilla using the instructions at Wikipedia:Bug reports.

For a log of recent software and configuration work, see the server admin log.

If your announcement might be of interest to other language Wikipedia editions, please place a copy on the "Wikimedia News" page of our multilanguage discussion site. Announcements having no direct connection with the English language Wikipedia, such as those concerning sister projects should be made on Wikimedia News and/or the article milestones statistics page.

[edit] Announcements

[edit] 21 February

Wikipedia's daily Alexa ranking hit 8 for the first time, a new high. [1]

[edit] 11 January

The English Wikipedia’s revision counter, indicating the total number of edits, has passed 100,000,000.

[edit] 7 January

Wikipedia's weekly Alexa ranking hit 10 for the first time. Meanwhile, its daily ranking hit 9, a new high.

[edit] 3 January

Wikipedia's daily Alexa ranking hit 10 again today. Of the total traffic to all Wikipedia sites, the share attributable to en: has now fallen to 54%, demonstrating the rapid growth of the many other Wikipedia language editions, while growth in en: continues unchecked.

[edit] 2 January

Wikipedia entered 2007 with over 6 million articles in 251 languages, 1560143 of them in English. During 2006, the English Wikipedia contributed 666,906 of the almost 3 million new articles project-wide, which almost doubled in 12 months. Full statistics are now available.

[edit] 26 December

Wikipedia's Alexa 3-monthly average traffic rank hit a new high of 12, hitting daily rank of 10 twice during December.
