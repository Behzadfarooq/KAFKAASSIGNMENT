Anna-Lisa Björling

Anna-Lisa Björling, nee Emy Anna-Lisa Berg (March 15, 1910 – November 24, 2006), was a Swedish opera singer (soprano) and actress. Married to legendary opera tenor Jussi Björling 1935-1960 (his death).

Born in Stockholm, Anna-Lisa Björling was herself a well-trained quality soprano singer in her own right who studied at the Royal Swedish Academy of Music. After her marriage to Jussi Björling in 1935 Anna-Lisa for a period of ten years focused on the family life and their children, but when the children had grown up she resumed her music training again and actually made a very successful debut at the Royal Swedish Opera in 1948 and she came to perform at the opera in various roles until in the late 1960s.

Back in Sweden - and occasionally abroad - she also performed now and then in duets with husband Jussi Björling, often at special occasions (such as TV shows/specials) and at different celebrations. She also appeared in some early Swedish 1930s films, both as an actress and singer.

Sometimes credited as Anna-Lisa Berg or Anna-Lisa Björling Barkman.

She passed away in Stockholm in 2006, at age 96.
