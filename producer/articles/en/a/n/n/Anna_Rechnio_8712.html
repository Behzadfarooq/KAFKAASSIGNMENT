Anna Rechnio (born December 11, 1977) was a Polish figure skater.

Born in Warsaw. She was a career athlete, beginning to skate in 1982, during her competitive career in the 1990s she lived and trained in New England. She currently coaches in Poland.

She was National Champion in 1994, 1997 and 1998. She has competed in the Olympics in 1994 (10th) and 1998 (19th), the World Championships in 1994 (15th), 1995 (14th), 1998 (5th), 1999 (6th) and 2000 (16th), the European Championships in 1993 (15th), 1994 (7th), 1995 (9th), 1996 (12th) and 1997 (13th). She also competed in many less prestigious competitions.
