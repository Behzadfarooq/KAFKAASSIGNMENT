[edit] About Annie

She is at times overbearing and bossy, due her spoiled upbringing. However she loves animals (and as she mentions in Power Instinct 2, a weakness to small children).

[edit] Special Moves

  • Swan Clash - Annie throws a massive golden swan at the opponent. In Matrimelee, the strong version arcs upward.
  • Photon Burst - When done in midair, Annie throws a ball of energy down at the opponent.
  • Rainbow Barrier - Annie swings her arm down, unleashing a rainbow colored barrier. She only had this move in the first Power Instinct.
  • Annie Dynamic - Annie throws a leg down to the ground with massive force.
  • Rainbow Rise - Annie flies into the air with an arm, unleashing a rainbow of energy in her wake. She gained this move in Power Instinct 2, and can be done while dashing.
  • Nature's Orb - Annie powers up an orb of energy into her hand that can be used to add an additional hit to a punch attack and more strength to her Swan Clash and Rainbow Rise (which uses up the orb). She had this move in Power Instinct Legends.
  • Cosmic Warp/Psychedelic Throw - Annie grabs the opponent and hurls him/her through space and time before s/he lands fiercely on the ground.
  • Angel's Blaster - Annie throws a ring of energy at the opponent. If it hits, Annie will kneel and an angel will rise out of her back, firing a powerful beam of energy into her foe.

[edit] Trivia

  • Annie currently has 6 dogs, 4 cats, 3 horses and a turtle named Kensington.
  • It is speculated that she was a bit of a parody of the 'nature girl' persona in fighting games, also including Nakoruru or Jun Kazama, considering that it is said she draws her powers from nature.
  • Her name was changed to "Anny" in Power Instinct Matrimelee.
  • Also to note in Matrimelee, her personality was slightly changed. In the last three games, she had a somewhat 'nature girl' type of personality while also was slightly vain. In Matrimelee, it leaned more on her being vain and the 'nature girl' theme dwindled, becoming somewhat akin to a sexy "Femme Fatale" stereotype.
  • Two of her dogs have appeared in her endings. Dante in Power Instinct 2 and Andy in Power Instinct Matrimelee. However, since both dogs look alike, it is possible that it is one character, and that his name was changed in Matrimelee.
