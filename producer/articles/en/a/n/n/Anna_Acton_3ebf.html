Anna Acton is a United Kingdom based actor last seen in television series The Bill as Rochelle Barratt, the wife of a Borough Commander.

Prior to appearing in The Bill, Acton played the long-suffering yet bubbly Geri Evans in soap opera Family Affairs for three and a half years.

Anna’s passion for acting started in her friend’s back garden where she ran a little drama group.

Originally from Marlow, Anna now lives in London with her boyfriend, actor Ben Hull.

Anna also appeared in an episode of Doctors as Rachel Cook (April 2006) and Hollyoaks (October 2006).
