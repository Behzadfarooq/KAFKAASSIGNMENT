Anne Elvebakk

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Anne Elisabeth Elvebakk (born May 10, 1966) is a former biathlete from Norway. She has received five World championship medals bronze in the 1987 sprint event. And in 1988 she won the biathlon world cup and the same year she took a bronze medal in the sprint at the World Championship and also silver at the 15 km in the same championship. In 1989 she won a gold medal at the World and she also won a silver medal at the 15 km in the same world Championship. And the following year she won the sprint event in the World Championships.
