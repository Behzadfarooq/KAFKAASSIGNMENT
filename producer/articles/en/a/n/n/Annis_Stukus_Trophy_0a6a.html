The Annis Stukus Trophy is a Canadian Football League trophy, which is presented annually by the Edmonton Eskimos Alumni Association to the Coach of the Year, as determined by the members of the Football Reporters of Canada. The Trophy is named after Annis Stukus.

The Stukus Trophy is typically the last trophy to be awarded in the CFL season, being not awarded until the following calendar year and months before the following season.
