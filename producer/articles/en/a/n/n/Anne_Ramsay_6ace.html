Anne Elizabeth Ramsay (born September 11, 1960), is an American actress best known for her role as Lisa Stemple on Mad About You, for which she shared a nomination for Best Ensemble in a Comedy series for the Screen Actors Guild Awards.

[edit] Early life

She was born and raised in Southern California. [1] She studied theater at UCLA and graduated with a baccalaureate. She worked with an acting group called The Continum, which featured UCLA alumni, through the acting group she got her big break and an agent.

[edit] Film Credits

She has many notable feature credits including A League of Their Own and Planet of the Apes.

[edit] Television Credits

Anne Ramsay is well known for her role as Lisa Stemple on the 7 year series Mad About You . Lisa Stemple is the loveable, spacy, perpetually single sister of Jamie Buchman Helen Hunt . She has also made numerous TV show guest appearances on such series as Six Feet Under, Chicago Hope, CSI, Dharma & Greg and The L Word. She was most recently seen on The WB series, Related.
