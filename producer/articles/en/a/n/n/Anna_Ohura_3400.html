[edit] Life and career

Anna Ohura was born in Hokkaidō, the northern-most main island of Japan, on May 30, 1980 to a French father and Japanese mother. She is left-handed and and has poor eyesight, wearing contact lenses. Her favorite subjects in school included social studies and Japanese, while English and mathematics were her worst. As a child, she wished to become a comic book writer, and she has commented that if she had not gone into the adult entertainment field, she would like to have worked as an assistant to a comic book writer.[4] Her debut AV was the February 24, 2000 release Tawawana Fruit (たわわな果実 - Tawawana Kajitsu).[5] She has appeared in several hardcore adult movies, all of which are censored, as required by Japanese law. She has a mole on her right breast, which is sometimes airbrushed out in photos.
