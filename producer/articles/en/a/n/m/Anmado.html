Anma-do (Korean: 안마도) is an island off the coast of Jeollanam-do province, South Korea. Anma-do covers an area of 6.09 km² and is home to approximately 200 residents. The landscape features several peaks: Duit-san (177 m), Mak-bong (167 m), and Geon-san (145 m). Anma-do is suited for agriculture, and many of its residents work as farmers. Its main agricultural products include rice, wheat, corn, garlic, bean, sesame, and pepper. There is currently one elementary school located on the island. [1]
