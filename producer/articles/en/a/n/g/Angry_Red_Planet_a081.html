Angry Red Planet is the name of two (at least) bands:

  • The Angry Red Planet is a 1959 science fiction film directed by Ib Melchior and produced by Sidney W. Pink.
  • Angry Red Planet (punk band), a three piece punk rock band formed in Detroit in 1982; they were signed to Touch & Go Records.
  • Angry Red Planet (electronica band), an electronica band started in 2001.
