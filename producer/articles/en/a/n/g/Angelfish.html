Angelfish

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Angelfish may refer to:

  • Several groups of fishes:
    • Freshwater angelfishes, tropical cichlids of the genus Pterophyllum.
    • Marine angelfishes of the family Pomacanthidae.
    • Angel sharks of the family Squatinidae.
    • Rhinobatos hynnicephalus, a species of guitarfish.
  • Angelfish, a short-lived Scottish alternative rock band, former band of Garbage's Shirley Manson.
