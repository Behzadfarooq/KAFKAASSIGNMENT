Angela Tsai is an Asian-American actress and host based in New York City. She hosts Comcast's "Dating On Demand" network and their spin-off reality show "On A Date", seen on CN8. She also hosts AZN Television's "NBA Timeout" and is a correspondent for NBA TV.

Angela Tsai is the first-ever Asian-American female correspondent for any professional sport league.[citation needed] She hosts news updates and player interviews and features for the NBA.

Angela is a graduate of Duke University. She majored in Public Policy Studies and minored in Visual Arts. When she first moved to New York City after college, she worked as a full-time graphic designer for the entertainment industry, designing websites for musical artists such as David Bowie and Moby.[citation needed] It wasn't until the past few years that she has made the transition to performer.

Angela Tsai was featured on the cover of Woman International Magazine and noted as an "Up and Coming Actor" in Audrey Magazine.
