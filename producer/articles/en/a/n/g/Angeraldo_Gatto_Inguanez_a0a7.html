Angeraldo Gatto Inguanez (died 1472) was the 5th Barony of Djar-il-Bniet e Buqana and a Baron of Budaq in Malta. His parents were Baroness Imperia Gatto and Spanish Baron Antonio Inguanez. After his fathers death in 1458 Angeraldo inherited the fiefs of Djar-il Bniet, Buqana, and Budaq. Later that same year he married Betta dei Baroni de Caro with issue. They had two children, a son Antonio Angeraldo Inguanez who went on to become the 6th Baron of Djar-il-Bneit and Buqana in 1472, and a daughter named Sibilla Inguanez who married the 4th Barone di Culeja Angelo de Vaccaro.
