Angela Featherstone

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Angela Featherstone (born on 3 April 1965 in Hamilton, Ontario, Canada) is a Canadian actress.

[edit] Selected filmography

  • Caved In: Prehistoric Terror (2006)
  • 200 Cigarettes (1999)
  • The Wedding Singer (1998)
  • Cracker (1997) (TV series)
  • Con Air (1997)
  • Dark Angel: The Ascent (1994)
  • Army of Darkness (1993)

[edit] TV

Made an appearance in Friends as Chloe from the copy place, who Ross slept with, leading to his break up with Rachel
