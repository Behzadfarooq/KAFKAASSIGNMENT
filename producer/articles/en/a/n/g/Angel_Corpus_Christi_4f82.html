Angel Corpus Christi

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Angel Corpus Christi (real name Andrea Ross) is a San Francisco based singer, songwriter and accordionist. As well as writing and performing her own songs she has recorded albums of cover versions. She has also recorded with Dean Wareham formerly of Galaxie 500 and Luna and she also played accordion on Spiritualized's 1997 album Ladies and Gentlemen We Are Floating in Space.
