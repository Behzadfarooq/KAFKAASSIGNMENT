Angela Watson

Angela Christine Watson (born November 12, 1975 in Danville, Illinois) is an American model and actress best known for her role on the American sitcom Step by Step, where she played one of the daughters of Suzanne Somers' character.

[edit] Early years

Watson is the youngest daughter of a Barbara and Allen Watson, who were farmers in the small town of Danville, Illinois, until 1985. Her family then moved to Cape Coral, Florida, where her parents entered her into beauty pagents, where she won 60 crowns and 200 trophies. She also won Model of the Year though the Face Finders Model Search in Dallas, Texas in 1988.

[edit] Acting

Like many others, Watson then moved on to local theatre and commercials. Her success in these areas enabled her family to be able to use some of her earnings to move to Los Angeles. She was first casted in a recurring role on Davis Rules. She then went on to her best known role as ditzy wanna-be valley girl Karen Foster on Step by Step. Karen, like Watson, was an aspiring model. Unfortunately, Watson has not had much success in landing roles after Step by Step ended its seven-year run in 1998.

[edit] Filmography

  • Junior Pilot (2006) as Millie Smith
  • "Step by Step" (1991-1998) as Karen Foster
  • "Davis Rules" (1991) as Alice Hansen

[edit] Child Actors Supporting Themselves (CAST)

In 2000 Watson started Child Actors Supporting Themselves (CAST), an organization to help child actors and athletes handle their finances. She was motivated to do this after discovering that her parents spent all of her earnings from her years on Step by Step.
