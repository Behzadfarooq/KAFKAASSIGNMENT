Angleton High School (AHS) is located at 1201 Henderson Road in Angleton, Texas.

For 2006-2007, the School Principal is Gordon Smith and the Assistant Principals are William Blair, Dennis Chaloupka, Elizabeth Comeaux, Herbert Mays, and Christina Todd.

[edit] History

Angleton High School is part of the Angleton Independent School District (AISD). Several previous school locations were destroyed by hurricanes. The High School was once located in what is now the Early Childhood Campus.

[edit] Traditions

The Angleton High School Marching Band is known as the Purple Pride.

[edit] Mascots

The Mascot for all Angleton ISD schools is the Wildcat.

[edit] Bonfire

Each Fall, the students and parents build and burn a large Bonfire before the football homecoming game. The Bonfire is currently held near the Brazoria County Fire Field.

[edit] Senior Serve

An elegant catered dinner served by Senior students, with live entertainment and a silent auction.
