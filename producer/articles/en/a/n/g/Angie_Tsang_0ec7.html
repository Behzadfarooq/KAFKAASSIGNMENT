Angie Tsang (曾思敏 or Tsang Sze-Man, b. 1980) is a Hong Kong wushu athlete. She portrayed a young Wong Fei Hung in the 1993 Hong Kong martial arts film Iron Monkey.

As a member of Hong Kong's national wushu team, Tsang won a gold medal in the women's nangun (southern staff) event of the World Wushu Championships in 2005 in Hanoi, Vietnam. She was a bronze-medal winner in the same event in 2003 in Hong Kong.[1]

She joined the Hong Kong Police Force in 2003 and has retired from competitive wushu.[2]
