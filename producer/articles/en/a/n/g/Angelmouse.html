Angelmouse

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Angelmouse is a children's television programme which is produced and broadcast by the BBC. It is aired on CBeebies (both the separate channel and CBeebies on BBC One and BBC Two). It has also been broadcast on ABC Kids.

The title character is a mouse who is an angel; he has a halo, meaning he must always be good to stop it from slipping, and receives instructions from God.
