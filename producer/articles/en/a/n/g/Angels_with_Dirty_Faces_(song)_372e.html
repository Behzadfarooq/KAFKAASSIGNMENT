"Angels with Dirty Faces" is the third single from the Sugababes's second studio album Angels with Dirty Faces. In the UK, the song peaked at number four on the UK Singles Chart.

[edit] Music video

Sugababes in the music video for "Angels With Dirty Faces". From left to right: Keisha Buchanan, Mutya Buena and Heidi Range.

The animated music video for "Angels with Dirty Faces" was created by the same studio behind the animated series The Powerpuff Girls.

It sees Townsville under attack from miniature robots disguised as raindrops.

After the Powerpuff Girls' failure to defeat the robots, which included Bubbles dancing around in a puddle, Professor Utonium genetically alters the Sugababes who then go on to fight and defeat the villain.

Here Mutya Buena takes the role of Blossom, Keisha Buchanan as Buttercup and Heidi Range as Bubbles.
