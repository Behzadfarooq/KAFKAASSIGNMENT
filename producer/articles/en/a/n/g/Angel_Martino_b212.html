Angel Martino (born April 25, 1967 in Tuscaloosa, Alabama) is a former American swimmer. Over her career, she won three relay Olympic gold medals and three Olympic bronze medals.

In addition to her Olympic medals, she won twelve Pan Pacific medals, seven Goodwill Games gold medals. She was a captain of the 1996 U.S. Women's Olympic Swimming team.
