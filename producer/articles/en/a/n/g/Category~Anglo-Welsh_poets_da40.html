Category:Anglo-Welsh poets

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Anglo-Welsh poets are poets of Welsh origin or residence, writing in the English language.

Neither Gerard Manley Hopkins nor Robert Graves seem to merit inclusion as Welsh poets under the above definition

Or Wendy Cope

Wendy Cope is not Welsh and has never lived in Wales.
