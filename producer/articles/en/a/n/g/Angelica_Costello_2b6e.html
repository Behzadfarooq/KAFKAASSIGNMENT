Angelica Costello (born June 5, 1978 in Plattsburgh, New York) is a porn star who was the Penthouse Pet of the month for June 1999. She is sometimes credited as Venus Rising or Crystal Craft.

This leggy brunette started her career by dancing at a club in Las Vegas. She left Las Vegas for Los Angeles, working first as a runway model and then an adult magazine model, before she began acting in adult films in 1999.
