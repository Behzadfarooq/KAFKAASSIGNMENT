Angel Rat is the sixth album released by the Canadian thrash metal/progressive metal band Voivod. Produced by Terry Brown of Rush fame, it was released in 1991 by Mechanic/MCA Records. Bass player Jean-Yves Thériault (Blacky) subsequently departed from the group after this record.
