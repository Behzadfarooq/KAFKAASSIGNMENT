Angels Cry is the debut album of Brazilian Heavy metal group Angra. It was released in 1993 and recorded in Germany at Kai Hansen's studios in Hamburg.

Despite being featured in the inlays and photo shoots for the album, drummer Ricardo Confessori did not play on the album as he was not a member of the band at the time of recording, with the majority of drum duties handled by Alex Holzwarth, whilst Thomas Nack (at the time a Gamma Ray member) played on one track.
