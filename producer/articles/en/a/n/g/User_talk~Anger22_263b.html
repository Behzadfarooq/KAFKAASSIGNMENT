[edit] President or 500/5?

Hi Anger22, I've been messaged by User:Vera, Chuck & Dave with reference to the Stuart Sutcliffe article. Someone is saying that it's a 500/5 and not a President bass, although the link states that it's a President. I'm not that familiar with basses or archtops for that matter, but I've always thought a 500/5 had two pickups - will you please take a look [1] I'd value your opinion. Hope your'e well, Cheers!
