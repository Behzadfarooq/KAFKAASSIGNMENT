Anggun is the Japanese edition to her international English debut Snow on the Sahara.

[edit] History

The album contains 19 tracks and were exclusively released in Japan by Epic Records and Sony Music Japan.

[edit] Album Cover

The album cover for Anggun is similar to Au Nom de la Lune. This is because both albums were released in the same year.
