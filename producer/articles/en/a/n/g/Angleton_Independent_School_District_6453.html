Angleton ISD is a school district in Angleton, Texas (USA), comprised of 5 levels of education. Established in 1897, AISD encompasses 396 square miles in Brazoria County, serving Angleton as well as the Village of Bonney and parts of Rosharon. The mascot of all AISD schools is the Wildcat.
