Angus Peter McDonald

Angus Peter McDonald (1813 â€“ January 22, 1889) was an Ontario contractor and political figure. He represented Middlesex West in the 1st Canadian Parliament as a Conservative member.

He lived in Glencoe, Ontario. In 1857, he was elected to represented the West riding of Middlesex in the Legislative Assembly of the Province of Canada.
