Anglesey League

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Anglesey Football League (currently the Stena Line Anglesey Football League, for sponsorship reasons) is a football league and is equivalent to the fifth level of the Welsh football league system in North Wales.

The league also runs four cup competitions which are the Dargie Cup, Elias Cup, Megan Cup and the Thomas & Williams Cup.

League members also compete in the North Wales Coast Junior Cup against fellow league members and members from the Caernarfon & District League and the Clwyd League.
