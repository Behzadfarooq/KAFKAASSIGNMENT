Angel is a fictional character in the Tekken fighting game series. She is Devil's final boss in Tekken 2.

[edit] History

[edit] Tekken 2

Angel vies with Devil over Kazuya Mishima's soul. Angel represents Kazuya's good half. She failed to convert Kazuya to the good side during Tekken 2, and she lost to Devil.

[edit] Tekken Tag Tournament

Angel is a playable character in Tekken Tag Tournament, but Tekken Tag Tournament is a non-canonical game. Not much else is said about Angel or mentioned beyond Tekken 2.

[edit] Trivia

  • Angel is a costume swap for Devil in Tekken 2 and Tekken Tag Tournament, sharing the same moves as Devil, as well as the same win animations. However, she has her own game endings.
  • The opening of Tekken 2 shows Angel with an arm around Kazuya's waist, as if protecting him. Kazuya's eyes are red during this appearance, providing further evidence for the connection between Devil and Angel.
