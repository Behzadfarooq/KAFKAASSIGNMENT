Angelgrinder is a project, formed by Gerhard & Frode of the Viking metal band Einherjer and the thrash metal band Battered. Angelgrinder is very different from their main band. The music of Angelgrinder is a mixture of black & death metal.

[edit] Members

  • Gerhard Storesund (Skarphedin) - Drums , Guitars
  • Frode Glesnes(Skallegrim) - Bass , Guitars , Vocals
