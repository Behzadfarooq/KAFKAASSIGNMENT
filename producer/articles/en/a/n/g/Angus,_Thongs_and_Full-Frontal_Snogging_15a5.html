Angus, Thongs, and Full-Frontal Snogging is the first novel from the Confessions of Georgia Nicolson series written by Louise Rennison in. First published in 2001 in the UK, the book follows the life of 14 year old Georgia Nicolson in her quest to the Sex God's (aka Robbie) heart.

[edit] Book Description

Angus:
My mixed-breed cat, half domestic tabby, half Scottish wildcat. The size of a small Labrador, only mad.

Thongs:
Stupid underwear. What's the point of them, anyway? They just go up your bum, as far as I can tell.

Full-Frontal Snogging:
Kissing with all the trimmings, lip to lip, open mouth, tongues ... everything.

Her dad's got the mentality of a Teletubby (only not so developed). Her cat, Angus, is trying to eat the poodle next door. And her best friend thinks she looks like an alien -- just because she accidentally shaved off her eyebrows. Ergghhhlack. Still, add a little boy-stalking, teacher-baiting, and full-frontal snogging with a Sex God, and Georgia's year just might turn out to be the most fabbitty fab fab ever!

[edit] Characters

  • Mr. Bob Nicolson (AKA Vati) – Georgia’s father
  • Mrs. Connie Nicolson (AKA Mutti) – Georgia’s mother
  • Jas - Georgia's best friend.
  • Rosie (Rosemary Mees) – Georgia’s pal and Sven’s girlfriend
  • Jools (Julia) – Georgia’s friend
  • Ellen - Georgia’s friend
  • Mabs (Mary) - Georgia’s friend
  • Robbie the "Sex God" Jennings – Georgia’s first proper love. (Former) Lead singer of the Stiff Dylans
  • Mark the Big Gob - The second person Georgia kissed. He has a huge mouth, hence the name Mark Big Gob.
  • Pete the Whelk (Peter Dyer) - The first person Georgia kissed
  • Libby - Georgia's sister
  • Uncle Eddie - Georgia's uncle.
  • Herr Kamyer - Teacher of German and Physics.
  • "Slim" - Overweight headmistress of Georgia's school (known as Stalag 14).
  • "Hawkeye" Heaton - A horrible teacher at Georgia's school.
  • Miss Stamp aka "Adolfa" - Physical Education teacher
  • Tom Jennings - Robbie (the Sex God)'s younger brother and boyfriend of Jas.
  • Sven - Rosie's giant Swedish boyfriend
  • Wet Lindsay - Robbie's ex-girlfriend, whom Georgia hates.
  • Nauseating Pamela (P.) Green - A repulsive girl in school who breeds hamsters and wears large glasses.
  • The Bummer Twins (Allison and Jackie)- Tarty and common girl bullies from Georgia's school (who are not actually twins) who always smoke and wear tons of make-up.
  • Elvis Attwood - The grumpy caretaker at Georgia's school, called Elvis because he once threw his back out while dancing.
  • Angus - Georgia's cat, who is half tabby and half Scottish wildcat (and about the size of a small Labrador).

[edit] The Snogging Scale

1) Holding hands
2) Arm around the shoulder
3) Good night kiss
4) Kiss lasting over 3 minutes without a breath
5) Open mouth kissing
6) Kissing with tongues
7) Upper body fondling (Outdoors)
8) Upper body fondling (Indoors or in bed)
9) Below Waist Activity (or B.W.A.)
10) "The Full Monty"

This "Snogging Scale" is later updated in other books of the series to include many other "activities".
