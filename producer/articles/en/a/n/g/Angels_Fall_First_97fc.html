Angels Fall First is Nightwish's debut album, released in 1997 by Spinefarm Records. The limited edition —500 copies— features only seven tracks, two of which aren't on the regular edition.

Elvenpath uses audio-clips from the prologue of The Lord of the Rings, directed by Ralph Bakshi.
