Angelica Mandy

Angelica Mandy as Gabrielle Delacour in Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire.

Angelica Mandy (born August 25, 1992 in Bath, England) is an English actress best known for playing Gabrielle Delacour in Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire. She Love's Shopping [Harrods,TopShop,Chanel to name a few] and Make-Up. She has several fears some include the fear of gaining weight, Spiders and Heights.
