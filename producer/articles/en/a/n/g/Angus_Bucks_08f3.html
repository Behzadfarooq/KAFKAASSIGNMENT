Angus Bucks

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

"Angus Bucks" that were dropped on the audience during The Razors Edge tour.

Angus Bucks, Angus Bills or AC/DC Dollars are fake one dollar bills featuring Angus Young, the lead guitarist from the Austrailian rock 'n' roll band AC/DC, on the front in place of the president. On the back of the bill, in place of the Great Seal and the Bald Eagle are the cannon from the band's 1981 album For Those About to Rock We Salute You and a large iron bell representing the song Hells Bells from their 1980 album Back In Black, respectively. Thousands were dropped on the audience as a theatrical gimmick at the end of the song Moneytalks during the band's tour to promote their 1990 album The Razors Edge. They were also featured in the music video for the same song which can be found on their DVD, Family Jewels, or here. In 1992, copies of AC/DC's Live album included both a Live Poster and an Angus Buck. They are now collector's items and can be found online from private sellers.
