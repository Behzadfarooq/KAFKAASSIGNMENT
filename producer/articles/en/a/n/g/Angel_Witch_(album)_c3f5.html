Angel Witch is the first album by NWOBHM band Angel Witch. The album was released in 1980 through Bronze Records, and re-released in three editions.

[edit] Track listing

  1. "Angel Witch"
  2. "Atlantis"
  3. "White Witch"
  4. "Confused"
  5. "Sorcerers"
  6. "Gorgon"
  7. "Sweet Danger"
  8. "Free Man"
  9. "Angel of Death"
  10. "Devil's Tower"

All songs written by Kevin Heybourne.
