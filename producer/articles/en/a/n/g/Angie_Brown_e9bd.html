Angie Brown

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Angie Brown is a female singer/songwriter from Clapham, in South London.

Brown, whose vocals have been featured on tracks of diverse acts ranging from Fatboy Slim to Lisa Stansfield & The Wah Brothers, is more famous as the featured vocalist on Bizarre Inc.'s two #1 Hot Dance Music/Club Play hits ("I'm Gonna Get You" and "Took My Love," both from 1993) and as the featured singer on Motiv8's top ten UK track from 1994, "Rockin' for Myself." Before this, she was a member of the group The Doves, and performed lead vocals on their single "Beaten Up In Love Again".

One of Brown's most notable appearances was on the ITV programme "Club Reps" in which Angie was the star attraction in the resort during that evening. Angie or "Ange" as she was often called put on a remarkable performance during her brief spell at the Greek resort.
