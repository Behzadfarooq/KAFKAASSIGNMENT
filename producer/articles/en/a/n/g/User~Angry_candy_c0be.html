[edit] About this Wikipedian

"... the Cambridge ladies do not care, above Cambridge if sometimes in its box of sky lavender and cornerless, the moon rattles like a fragment of angry candy" - E. E. Cummings

Hello there. My name for the purposes of Wikipedia editing is Angry_Candy. It comes from the above stansa of an e.e. Cummings poem called The Cambridge Ladies Who Live in Furnished Souls and by extension a collection of short speculative fiction stories by Harlan Ellison. In the real world, I work in libraries and write for small magazines.
