Angoulême International Comics Festival Prize Awarded by the Audience

This Prize Awarded by the Audience is awarded to comics authors at the Angoulême International Comics Festival since 1989. The winner is in bold, the (long) list of nominees follows.

[edit] 1980s

  • 1989: Le grand pouvoir de Chninkel by Rosinski and Jean Van Hamme, Casterman

[edit] 1990s

  • 1990: Broussaille: La nuit du chat by Frank Pé and Bom, Dupuis
  • 1991: Le dernier chant des Malaterre by François Bourgeon, Casterman
  • 1992: Peter Pan: Londres by Régis Loisel, Vents d'Ouest
  • 1993: Théodore Poussin: Un passager porté disparu by Frank Le Gall, Dupuis
  • 1994: Jeux pour mourir by Jacques Tardi, Casterman
  • 1995: Peter Pan part 3 by Régis Loisel, Vents d'Ouest
  • (1996: no award in this category)
  • 1997: Blake and Mortimer: L’affaire Francis Blake by Ted Benoit and Jean Van Hamme, after Edgar Pierre Jacobs, Blake et Mortimer
  • (1998: no award in this category)
  • (1999: no award in this category)
