[edit] New and Improved

New and Improved is a Code Lyoko story trilogy currently being written by Matthew Rozier with some ideas being provided by myself. The premise is this: the five primary protagonists from the regular series get two new additions to their group: Elisabeth Delmas and Jim Moralés, the school snob and PE teacher respectively.

This story takes place in an alternate timeline from the Code Lyoko television show, as numerous differences appear between the two. These include Jim acting more loving and Sissi being Odd's girlfriend.

[edit] New Lyoko Warriors

  • Jim—Jim has become a lot nicer and has renounced his strictly-authoritarian ways in favor of being more friendly and caring. He acts as the group's friend and father figure, and has grown to be a very caring man around the students at Kadic. He loves to ruffle his students' hair, especially Jeremie's. In fact, he treats Jeremie like his own son. He also acts as a father figure to Samantha, the girl from the first season episode Rock Bottom. His powers on Lyoko are based around electricity, his weapon is a conductive metal bo, and his vehicle is a tank called the "UnderTank", which reflects his military service from many years ago.
  • Sissi Delmas—Sissi is now Odd's girlfriend. She has grown to be very kind and less bratty. She loves Odd a lot. She is friends with the five main members; she now considers Ulrich to be "just a friend" and has dropped her rivalry with Yumi. She has also become friends with Odd's ex-girlfriend, Samantha. Her powers on Lyoko are anti-gravity-based, her weapon is also a bo (that can convert itself into a javelin and has the ability to fire darts similar to Odd's) and her vehicle is a customized Overboard.
  • Name: Emily Moralés
  • Sex: Female
  • Age: 13
  • Dreams: Singing, becoming a star, martial arts fighting
  • Nightmares: Becoming XANA's queen, seeing her family and friends in danger

Emily is the only daughter of Jim and Suzanne Moralés, two professors at Kadic Junior High School, a boarding academy she attends as a day student. Emily, or Lili as her friends and family call her, holds two valuable assets. Unfortunately, she's underage, so we must discuss her other two special talents: one is her golden voice, and the other is her pure spirit and her inner and outer beauty. Lili has talent, and she knows it, but the problem is convincing the world. Her first shot at stardom came in the form of the "Virtual Dreams" show at Moonbeam Tower, but a strange nervousness kept her at bay. It took an adventure with her family and friends to bring out the courage she held within all along, and she was finally able to let her gift be known to the world.

[edit] Deep Freeze of Doom

Code Lyoko fanfiction inspired by the episode Cold War. In an attempt to make the Warriors' jobs a little harder, XANA decides to cast another freezing spell. Instead of just France falling prey to the spell, the whole world is frozen in a second Ice Age. Unfortunately, the Warriors need heat to expend their energy and use their powers, but Jim finds a way to spur them on...motivation, encouragement and unconditional love.
