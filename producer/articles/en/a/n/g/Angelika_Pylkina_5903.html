Angelika Pylkina (born August 9, 1990, in St. Petersburg, Russia) is a Swedish pairs figure skater. She competes with partner Niklas Hogner. They are the first Swedish pairs team to compete internationally since 1962. They twice placed 5th at the World Junior Figure Skating Championships and have won three bronze medals on the Junior Grand Prix circuit. They recently won the bronze medal at the Nebelhorn Trophy and won the Nordic Championships.
