Angela Chang (Simplified Chinese: 张韶涵; Traditional Chinese: 張韶涵; pinyin: Zhāng Sháohán, born in January 19, 1982) is a Taiwanese singer and also actress. Angela specializes pop in her albums.

[edit] Biography

Angela was born in Taiwan. Her family immigrated to Canada when Angela was 12. As the oldest sibling in her family, Angela has a younger sister and a younger brother.

She attended many singing contests when she lived in Canada. At a Chinese-Canadian singing contest "BCC Star", Angela won first place by singing Zhang Yusheng's popular song "The Day without Cigar (没有烟抽的日子)". She was subsequently offered the chance to go to Taiwan for the final, which she did not attend due to school commitments.

After she graduated from high school (Sir Winston Churchill Secondary School), Angela started her career in 2000. She became popular after starring as "Tian Yuxi (田羽希)" in the TV series "My MVP Valentine (MVP情人)."

Angela's first language is Mandarin, but she also speaks fluent English.

[edit] Rumored Relationships

She was once rumored with Vic Zhou due to the fact that they released their albums together. The two seemed to work a lot before, but later were proven to be just friends.

She was also rumored to be in a relationship with Johnny Yan, who was also reportedly interested in actress Joe Chen Qiao En. That interest was short-lived because of company restrictions, but Johnny has also claimed that Angela and he are only the best of friends, but nothing more.
