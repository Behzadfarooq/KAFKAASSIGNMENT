"Angel" is the second European single from Pharrell's debut album, In My Mind (2006). It debuted on November 29, 2005 in the UK and was backed heavily by BBC Radio 1 where at one point it was their most played record. It was also made Record of the Week by UK DJ Scott Mills.

The single was originally due for release on December 5, 2005 but due to the fact the album In My Mind was pushed back, the single was released on January 21, 2006. The single became Pharrell's third top 20 hit in the UK, peaking at #15 there, his lowest chart peak to date. It also only stayed in the UK top 75 single chart for 4 weeks.

The single was not released in the U.S. or Australia due to the underperformance of "Can I Have It Like That" in both countries. [1]

[edit] Music Video

There are two versions of the "Angel" video. The first, which was never released to television, combined both animation and live action shots . The second video, directed by Hype Williams, was the version released to television in the UK and is the more widely known version .
