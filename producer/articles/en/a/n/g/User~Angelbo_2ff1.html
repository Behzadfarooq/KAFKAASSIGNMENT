Hi, I'm Angelbo

...and I'm a Danish Wikipedian from the city of Odense. I am new to Wikipedia as a contributor (since December 14, 2005), but I've enjoyed reading it for a long time.

About me: my real name is Mads Angelbo Pedersen and I´m studying finance management and economy. I also have a bit of interest in computers, and information technology in general and open source technology in particular. My political conviction is liberal and I am a member of the Danish Liberal party, Venstre, and its youth wing Venstres Ungdom. Besides this, I am an avid amateur photographer and enjoy walking in nature and perhaps shooting a still or two.
