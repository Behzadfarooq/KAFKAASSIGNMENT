[edit] Breif History

Born in Portsmouth, VA on June 6, 1989. My parents had met in the Navy and also had my little brother, Josh (September 16, 1992). About 2 years after my birth my father was shipped off to Japan and my mother and I went with him. We lived in Nagoya for about a year and later moved to Yokosuka. We lived here for another 2 years and went back to the U.S. We lived in North Chicago, IL, where I attended Lakeshore Catholic Academy until third grade. Then we moved to Beach Park, IL and I went to St. Patrick's school from fourth to seventh grade. My parents then got divorsed and my brother and I went with mother to live with my grand parents in Stockbridge, WI. For eighth grade I went to Stockbridge school. Of the three of us, no one liked it there so we moved to Appleton, WI. I have attended Appleton North High School since freshman year. On July 28, 2006, my brother (now 14) left the stove on when he left the house when he was home alone. He came back three hours later and the house was in flames. We now live with my mother's fiance', Jeff, in New Holstien, WI. I still attend Appleton North and plan to for the rest of my senior year.
