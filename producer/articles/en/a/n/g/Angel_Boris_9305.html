Angel Lynn Boris (born August 2, 1974 in Ft. Lauderdale, Florida) is an American model.

While working for Hawaiian Tropic, she appeared in a Playboy pictorial featuring the women of Hawaiian Tropic, and in July 1996 she became Playboy's Playmate of the Month. She appeared in many Special Editions of the magazine and has also appeared nude in other magazines and websites. She has also acted in several films and has appeared on numerous television shows.

She worked as a booth babe for Eidos Interactive at the 1999 Electronic Entertainment Expo (E3).

She is married and recently had her first child, a baby boy.
