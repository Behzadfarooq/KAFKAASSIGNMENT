Angel Fire Resort is an alpine ski resort in Angel Fire, New Mexico, USA. The resort opened in 1966[1] and offers both winter and summer activities.

[edit] Activities

In the winter season there is terrain for

  • Skiing
  • Snowboarding
  • Snowmobiling
  • Inner tubing
  • Sleighriding

During the summer, the resort offers[2]

  • Mountain biking
  • An 18-hole PGA Championship golf course
  • ATV tours
  • Scenic chairlift rides
  • Rock climbing
  • Rafting
  • Horseback riding
  • Hiking
  • Tennis
  • Wildlife tours
