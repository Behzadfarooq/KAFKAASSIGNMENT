Angels & Vampires - Volume II
Studio album by Sananda Maitreya
Released 2005

Angels & Vampires - Volume II is part of Sananda Maitreya's sixth album, the first 6 songs were released on October 8 2005. The fully finished and mastered album is released on April 29, 2006 on his own official site.

For Maitreya, it is a step into new directions. It introduces a more organic, stripped-down sound, quite different from his earlier albums that had heavily taken advantage of electronic equipment.
