Angelos Sakketos

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Aggelos Sakketos

Angelos Sakketos (Άγγελος Σακκέτος) is a Greek writer from Thessaloniki. He has written various books about Greek history and Christianity. In his books, he promotes the claim that Hellenism and Orthodox faith are strongly bound. He also claims that Greek element covers a major part of the Holy Bible by suggesting that many of the people participating in the events that take place, including the followers of Jesus were Greeks. Another topic of concern is ancient Greek technology where he promotes the claim that most of western scientific progress happened after western Europe came in contact with Greek philosophy and science. Most of his books are published by Dimosthenis Liakopoulos. Most famous of them are:

  • Οι Έλληνες της Βίβλου (The Greeks of the Holy Bible)
  • Ελληνικός Χριστιανισμός (Hellenic Christianity)
  • Χριστιανικός Ελληνισμός (Christian Hellenism)
  • Πού είναι τα αρχαία ελληνικά χειρόγραφα; (Where are the ancient Greek manuscripts?)
  • Ιησούς Χριστός Ελληνισμός - Χριστιανισμός (Jesus Christ Hellenism - Christianity)
  • Η Ελληνική Παιδεία του Ιησού (The Greek Education of Jesus)
