Angelo Palombo (born 25 September 1981, in Ferentino, Province of Frosinone) is an Italian footballer. A midfielder, he currently plays for U.C. Sampdoria.

Palombo started his career at AC Fiorentina. He moved to U.C. Sampdoria on free transfer after Fiorentina's bankruptcy in 2002.

Palombo made his Italy national team debut with Sampdoria teamate Falcone, Delvecchio, Terlizzi, lose to Croatia on 0-2, August 16, 2006, by substitute Liverani in 58min.

[edit] Clubs

  • 1998-2002: AC Fiorentina
  • 2002-present: U.C. Sampdoria

[edit] National team

Palombo was in the Italy U-21 team that won the 2004 European Under-21 Football Championship and achieved bronze medal of the 2004 Summer Olympics football tournament.
