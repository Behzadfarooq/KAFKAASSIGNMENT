Angela Griffin (born July 19, 1976 in Leeds) is an English actress.

[edit] Acting career

She appeared in the popular long-running British soap operas Emmerdale and most famously in Coronation Street as Fiona Middleton from 1993 to 1998. Subsequently she joined the original cast of the BBC medical drama series Holby City and left in 2001. She has also appearanced in the BBC dramas Cutting It and Down To Earth.

In October 2001, she presented a programme on the UK's Channel 4 on the subject of mixed race people in British society.

Recently in 2006 she appeared as a teacher, alongside former Cutting It co-star Jason Merrells in the BBC school drama series Waterloo Road.

[edit] Personal life

In 2006 she married her boyfriend Jason Milligan with whom she has a 2 year old daughter Tallulah and is currently five months pregnant with their second child. She has 2 brothers.
