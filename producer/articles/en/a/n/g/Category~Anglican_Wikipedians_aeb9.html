Category:Anglican Wikipedians

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

This page contains Wikipedians who have identified themselves on Wikipedia as being members of one of the 44 churches of the Anglican Communion or adherents to Anglican Christianity. Among these churches there are several known as Episcopal.

Please only specify your user page in this category, and not any sub-pages. A userbox Anglican Wikipedians may use is {{user anglican}}, which displays as:

This user is an adherent of Anglican Christianity.
