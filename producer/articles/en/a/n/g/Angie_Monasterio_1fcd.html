Angie Monasterio is an Argentine R&B/pop singer and songwriter. She was born in El Bolson, Rio Negro, Argentina.

'Anything But Monday' (2006)

[edit] Facts

  • She belongs to the American female Pop/R&B group Anything But Monday.
  • She appears on Lenny the Wonder Dog (2004).
  • To appear on TV show '30 Days Til I'm Famous', along with 4 other singers hosted by rapper Fat Joe.
  • Besides being a gifted singer, she is also talented at web design and illustration.
