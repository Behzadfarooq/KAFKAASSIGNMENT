Angus Holden, 3rd Baron Holden

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Angus William Eden Holden( Lord Holden). Born 1st August 1898 was the eldest son of Baron Angus Holden of Alston. He was the 3rd Baron Holden and the last. The Barony became extinct on the day of his death 6.7.1951 and the Baronetcy was passed on to his next kinsman, Sir Isaac Holden, 5th Baronet. He was a Speaker and Deputy Chairman in The House of Lords 1947.
