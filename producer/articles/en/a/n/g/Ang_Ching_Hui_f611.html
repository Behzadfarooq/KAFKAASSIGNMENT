Ang Ching Hui (Chinese: 翁沁慧; born 29 July 1993) is a teenage actress in Singapore who starred as Lee Xiao Yun in the television series Police and Thief. She is studying at the River Valley High School in Singapore.[1] She received a Star Awards nomination in 2002 for her performance in The Vagrant.[2]
