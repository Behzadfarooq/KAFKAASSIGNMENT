[edit] About Me

My Real name is Emily and I am a 22 year old Female. I live in Southend-on-Sea, Essex in the UK and I am of English decent. I am a university student and I am studying Fine Art (BA Hons). I use Brittish English when writing.

[edit] Writing problems

I suffer from mild Dyslexia, meaning while I can still read and write, my writing skills are below standards and I tend to do more mistakes then the average person my age would. I apologise for my grammar and spelling mistakes in Wikipedia, it is very hard for me to spot my own mistakes even at the best of times. I try to proof read and double check spelling but cannot always spot my own mistakes.

I also suffer from depression so I apologise if I get emotional on wikipedia or a little argumentative from time to time. I don't do this on purpose, I do it unknowingly.

[edit] Interests

I love Science fiction and I love the Dune series of books as well as Babylon 5.

I'm big on Manga and Anime and currently I'm interested in the most is the series One Piece. I'm also am a fan of Outlaw Star as well as the series Devilman.

I am also fond of comedy shows (mostly tradional style) and enjoy watching Red Dwarf, Dad's Army, M*A*S*H, Have I got news for you and Yes minster (and Yes Prime Minster).

I also have an interest in ancient civilisations and art.

[edit] Pages I watch

Right now I'm trying to help bring the One Piece pages up to standard. I have a lot of time on my hands to do it in and I'm hoping it will encourage others to try to bring these up to Wikipedias standards. I am a general editor I do things here and there but I mainly focus on fixing vandalism, removing/rewriting speculations, referencing information from episodes, supplying suitable pictures for pages and checking information is correct.

I have a habit of telling people to get things done... Then doing it myself it seems. Which considering I'm dyslexic and poor at organising things... Is a bad thing. Why do I do it? Because at least I know it's DONE.
