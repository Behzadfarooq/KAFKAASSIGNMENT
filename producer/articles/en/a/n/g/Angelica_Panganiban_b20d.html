Angelica Panganiban (born November 4, 1986 in the Philippines) is a Filipina actress. Panganiban is a member of ABS-CBN's elite circle of homegrown talents properly named Star Magic. Lately she has appeared in a calendar, in Maxim Magazine and graces the March 2007 issue of FHM cover. There has been an issue on whether her pictures in the alcoholic beverage calendar has been edited which she flatly denied.
