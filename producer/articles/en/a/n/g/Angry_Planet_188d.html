Angry Planet is a reality television program broadcast in Canada on OLN that features the adventures of storm chaser George Kourounis, starting in March 2007. Angry Planet is produced by Pinewood Films.

Each episode focuses on a different force of nature as Kourounis gets up close to investigate and document the most fierce natural phenomenon the Earth has to dish out.

Season one consists of 13 half hour episodes which include:

  • Tornado Chase
  • Hurricanes
  • Wild Weather Retrospective
  • Yukon Wildfires
  • African Volcano Climb
  • Arizona Monsoon Lightning
