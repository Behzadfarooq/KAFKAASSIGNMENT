[edit] Welcome Angrypepper

Hi there Angrypepper. Welcome to Wikipedia. When you get a chance, drop us a note at Wikipedia:New user log to introduce yourself we always like to welcome new users.

You can sign your name on talk pages by using " ~~~ " for your username and " ~~~~ " for your username and a timestamp.. Signing your name is important on all talk pages because it helps people get in touch with you.

Also remember to indent your comments using : so that other users can follow the flow of discussions

You might like to put a short introduction on your user page so that other wikipedians can get to know you.

  • Welcome is a good place to start.
  • Wikipedia:Tutorial runs through all the basics.
  • Wikipedia:How does one edit a page gives editing help.
  • Wikipedia:Manual of Style gives formatting info.
  • Wikipedia:Policies and guidelines tell about the principles we operate on. It's important, but don't try to read it all now.
  • Wikipedia:Help covers a broad range of useful topics.
  • Wikipedia:Help desk is a place to ask questions.
  • Wikipedia:Show preview explains how to double-check your edits before saving.

As well as editing wikipedia you could also think about taking part in many other essential activities that make wikipedia a friendly place. For example you could welcome new users to wikipedia (for this you are welcome to use my welcome template here) or you could try mediating discussions. Plus there are loads of other options at the Wikipedia:Community Portal.

You should also feel free to drop me a question on my talk page. I'll answer as soon as possible

Happy editing,  Tmorton166 (Errant Emote)  talk 21:46, 22 May 2006 (UTC)
