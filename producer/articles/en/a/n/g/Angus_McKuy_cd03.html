Angus Gregory McKuy (born August 7, 1957) is an American editor, screenwriter and former journalist and freelance writer.

McKuy covered politics for the Associated Press and Reuters in the 1980s and early 1990s, and has written as a freelancer and stringer for such publications as The New York Times and Rolling Stone Magazine.

McKuy's television credits include work on SCTV, A Year at the Top and Coach. Screenwriting work includes The Last Polka and Mystery Men.
