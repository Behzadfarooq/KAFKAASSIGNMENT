[edit] Summary

This image is used by 20th Century Fox/Pocket Books to promote the television series, Angel.

It features Angel actors as their characters, from left to right: Charles Gunn (played by J. August Richards), Cordelia Chase (played by Charisma Carpenter), Angel (played by David Boreanaz), Wesley Wyndam-Pryce (played by Alexis Denisof) and Winifred Burkle (played by Amy Acker).

[edit] Source Information

This image was taken from the official guidebook, Angel: Casefiles Volume I (between page 118 and 119). 20th Century Fox licensed the book as official Angel guide.

The television series, Angel', is owned by 20th Century Fox, Mutant Enemy Inc., The WB Network, and UPN.
