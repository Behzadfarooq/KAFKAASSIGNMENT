Angela Bulloch

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Angela Bulloch (born 1966 in Rainy River, Ontario, Canada) is a London and Berlin based sculptor, installation - and sound artist who is recognised as one of the Young British Artists. She studied at Goldsmiths' College, London (1985â€“1988) and was included in the Freeze Exhibition, 1988. Bulloch has used video, sound and light to explore pre-edited systems. Many of her works make use of bio-feedback systems.
