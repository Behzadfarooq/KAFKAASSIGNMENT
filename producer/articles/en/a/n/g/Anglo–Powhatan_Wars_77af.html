The Anglo–Powhatan Wars were two wars fought between English settlers and Native Americans of the Powhatan Confederacy in Virginia.The First War lasted from 1609–1613 and ended in a peace settlement.The Second War lasted from 1644-1646 and ended in complete defeat for the Native Americans and they were forced off their land.
