Anglican High School(Abbreviation: AHS) is an autonomous, SAP and Government-aided secondary school in Tanah Merah, Singapore. The school has just celebrated its 51st anniversary.

[edit] History

AHS was founded in 1956 by the Chinese-speaking congregations of the Anglican Churches in Singapore. It was the only Anglican school which had Chinese as its medium of instruction. All other schools of the Anglican church were English schools.

When AHS formally opened in January 1956, it had a Principal, three Chinese teachers, three English teachers, and 123 male students. Lessons were conducted in several classrooms at St Andrew's Secondary School.

It acquired the present Upper Changi Road site and erected the first school building in 1960 which comprised of 16 classrooms, 3 science laboratories, an administration-cum-staff room, an assembly hall and a symbolic clock-tower.

[edit] Core Values

Holiness
Aspiring towards purity in body, mind and soul in reverence to God.
No "dirty" thoughts and unwholesome words.
No smoking and drinking.
Do not visit pornographic sites and do not have premarital sex.
Be pleasing to God.

Righteousness
Upholding personal integrity in thoughts, words and deeds at all times.
Able to differentiate right from wrong and stay in the right path.
Be honest - no foul play behind referee's back, no cheating in tests/exams.
Stand up for what is right and tell the truth.
Admit and amend our mistakes.

Respect
Doing unto others as you would have them unto you.
Be polite and considerate to others, care about their feelings and self-esteem.
Listen to others, respect their opinions and not make negative comments or gossip.
Obey authorities and follow rules and regulations.
Be filial to parents and elders.

Loyalty
Being true and committed to the goals and aspirations of school and nation.
Support the activities of our class, CCA, and school.
Work hard and do well in competitions and studies.
Do not leave the country when the country is in hardship.
Love and serve our school and country.

Responsibility
Taking pride and finding joy fulfilling our roles
Complete our projects/hand in our assignments and homework on time.
Do with all our heart and to the best of our ability whatever we are assigned to do.
Do not shun away from duty, do not blame and bear consequences when job is not well done.
Create an interest in what we are learning and doing.

Perseverance
Pressing on and overcoming difficulties to achieve our goals.
Do not let criticisms or bad circumstances discourage us.
Face the problems, do not run away from them.
Get back to our feet no matter how many times we have fallen.
Have patience, determination and confidence to hold on and move on to reach our goals.

[edit] Motto, Vision and Mission

Motto:

Aspiring towards Holiness And Righteousness

Vision:

where talents soar

Mission Statement:

To nurture creative thinkers, lifelong learners and compassionate leaders who honour tradition and aspire towards holiness and righteousness - Accomplished, Honourable, Servant leaders

目标 Mission Objectives

To create a nurturing and challenging environment,
To develop well-rounded individuals who are effectively bilingual, appreciate Chinese culture and value diversity.
To build a learning community of active and responsible citizens of sound character imbibed with values based on Christian teachings.
To develop talents and leadership qualities.

[edit] Alumni

An alumni Association exists to foster close relations between the school and graduates and also to promote social , cultural and recreational activities among its members and to keep the graduates updated on the activities and programmes of Anglican High School.
