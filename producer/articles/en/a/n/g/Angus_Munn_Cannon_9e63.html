Angus Munn Cannon

Angus Munn Cannon (May 17, 1834 - June 7, 1915) served as mayor of St. George, Utah, in 1861. Angus was 50 years old when he married 27-year-old physician and suffragist Martha Maria Hughes on October 6, 1884.
