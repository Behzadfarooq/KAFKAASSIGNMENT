Angela Ann Knight (born 31 October 1950) is a British Conservative politician.

Knight was educated at Bristol University and became an engineer. She served as a councillor on Sheffield City Council 1987-92.

Knight was MP for Erewash from 1992 to 1997, when she lost the seat to Labour's Liz Blackman. She was Economic Secretary to the Treasury from 1995 to 1997.
