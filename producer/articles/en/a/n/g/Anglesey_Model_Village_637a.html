Follow the winding path through a world of make believe and see models of Anglesey's many historical landmarks in miniature. Every twist and turn reveals a different aspect of Anglesey. From the bustle of a busy market town to the peaceful setting of Llynnon Mill with its slowly turning sails to the sound of nearby waterfalls.

Set in one acre of beautifully landscaped gardens, the model village is completely built to one twelfth (1:12) full size with water features - including the Ynys Mon Express which circles the lake pausing only at the World famous station of Llanfairpwllgwyngyllgogerychwyrndrobwllllantysiliogogogoch.

Younger guests can ride a self drive railway around the picnic and play area.

Tea room, play and picnic areas.
