Angie is a common diminutive of the name Angela and may refer to:

  • Angie (TV series), an ABC television sitcom
  • "Angie Baby", a 1974 single by Helen Reddy
  • Angie (album), a 1978 Angela Bofill album
  • Angie (film), a film starring Geena Davis
  • "Angie" (also known as "Angi" or "Anji"), a guitar instrumental written by Davey Graham
