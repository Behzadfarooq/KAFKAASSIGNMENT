Angie Elisabeth Brooks

Angie Elisabeth Brooks (b. August 1928 in Virginia, Liberia) is a diplomat in Liberia. She is notable for being the second female President of the United Nations General Assembly.

In 1954 she became Liberia's representative to the United Nations. In 1969, she was chosen as the President of the General Assembly and took office in 1970. Angie Brooks has two sons.
