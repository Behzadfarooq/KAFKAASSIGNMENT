Angelique Seriese (born 12 July 1968 in Zevenbergen) is a former Dutch judoka, who became world champion in the 78-kg category at the 1995 World Judo Championships.

Seriese won the gold medal at the 1988 Summer Olympics when women's judo was introduced as a demonstration sport. Other medals include eight European championship victories, bronze at the 1987 World Championships in Essen and silver at the 1993 World Championships in Hamilton.
