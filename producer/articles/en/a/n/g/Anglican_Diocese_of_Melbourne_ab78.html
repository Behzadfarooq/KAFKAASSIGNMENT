The Anglican Diocese of Melbourne is the metropolitan diocese of the Province of Victoria in the Anglican Church of Australia. The diocese includes both greater Melbourne and Geelong. The cathedral church of the diocese is St Paul's Cathedral, Melbourne. The Archbishop of Melbourne, the Right Reverend Dr Philip Freier was previously the Bishop of The Northern Territory and was installed as Archbishop of Melbourne on 16 December 2006.
