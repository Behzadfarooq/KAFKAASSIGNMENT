Angel By My Side is the third single of Dutch singer Do off her debut album, Do. The song was co-written by Engllsh songwriters Alistair Griffin and James and Tom Martin. It peaked at #10 on the Dutch singles chart.

It was used as the title song for the film "Ellis in Glamourland".
