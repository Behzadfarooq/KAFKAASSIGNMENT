Angel Dark (born Viktoria Knezova on April 11, 1982) is a Slovak pornographic actress and nude model.

[edit] Biography

Dark was born in Bratislava, the capital city of Slovakia, and began her career in the sex industry in 2001. Her filmography consists of nearly 100 movies, including her first film Blow Bang 7. Her films fall into the categories hardcore, gonzo, A2M, lesbian, and anal. She has also posed for numerous websites and online pictorials.

According to the Internet Movie Database, Dark chose the name Angel as a stage moniker after giving birth to a baby boy whom she gave up for adoption; the couple who adopted him had called her an angel. She chose the surname Dark in reference to the Goth subculture, of which she is fond.[1]

In early 2006, Angel Dark was on hiatus from modeling and acting due to hospitalization and recovery from kidney failure in late 2005. She is represented by Model Mania, a modelling agency that represents mostly European models and actresses. In the latter part of 2006, according to EBI, Angel Dark returned to active status as an erotic model, including doing male - female (HC) work.
