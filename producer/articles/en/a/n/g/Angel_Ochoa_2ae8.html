Angel Eduardo Ochoa Uriarte (born March 4, 1989 in Culiacan, Sinaloa) is a Mexican Football player, who currently plays for Dorados de Sinaloa of the Primera División A de México.

Ochoa came to prominece playing for UAS of Tercera Fuerza in México, before he went to school.
