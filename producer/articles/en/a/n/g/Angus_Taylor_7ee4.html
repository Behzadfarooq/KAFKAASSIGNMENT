Angus Taylor is a Canadian writer and philosopher specializing in animal rights. He lectures in philosophy at the University of Victoria in British Columbia, and is the author of Animals and Ethics (2003).

[edit] Works

Taylor, A. Animals and Ethics. Broadview Press, 2003. ISBN 1-55111-569-7
