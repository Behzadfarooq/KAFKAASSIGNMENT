Angela Eileen Watkinson (born 18 November 1941, Leytonstone) is a politician in the United Kingdom. She is Conservative Party Member of Parliament for Upminster, and was first elected in 2001, beating Keith Darvill who had taken the seat from the Conservatives in 1997. She was re-elected with an increased majority in 2005.

She lives in Wickford, Essex.
