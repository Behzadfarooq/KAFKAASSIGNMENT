Angela Melini (born July 25, 1969 in Saigon, Vietnam) is an American model, actress and entrepreneur. She was chosen Playmate of the Month for the June, 1992 issue of Playboy magazine, and her centerfold was photographed by Arny Freytag. Melini has appeared in several Playboy newsstand special editions and videos, working steadily for Playboy for more than ten years after she graced the magazine's fold-out page, first as a nude glamour and pin-up model, and then as a promotional model and a feature reporter for the Playboy Channel.

Melini's mother is Vietnamese, and her father was an Italo-American soldier who, prior to the birth of Melini and her twin brother, was tragically a casualty of war, killed in action during the Second Indochina War (more commonly known as the Vietnam War). It is this mixed ancestry that gives Melini her exotic, eurasian appearance. In 1975, as part of Operation Frequent Wind, Melini, then age 5 and together with her mother, emigrated to the United States, settling in the suburbs near Olympia, Washington. Her brother was forced to remain in Vietnam, being one of the millions of Vietnamese people who were put to work in that government's collectivization of farms and factories, a policy implemented to restore its economy devastated by war.

Melini is a 1987 graduate of Timberline High School in Lacey, Washington. Melini earned a Bachelor of Arts degree in Early Childhood Education from the University of Nevada, Las Vegas.

Melini owns and operates two commercial websites -- her personal adult-themed website, AngelaMelini.com, and a mult-model adult-themed website, MarquisModels.com (see below). In addition, Melini appears at various fan conventions, most notably Comic-Con International and Glamourcon, where she greets her fans and members of her websites, autographs and sells various Playboy memorabilia, and recruits female nude and pin-up models for MarquisModels.com.

As of January, 2007, Melini resides in Las Vegas, Nevada with her husband and two children, a son, age 5, and a daughter, age 2.
