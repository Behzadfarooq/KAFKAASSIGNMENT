Angel Orsini is a Professional wrestler who previously wrestled in Ohio Valley Wrestling, a developmental territory of World Wrestling Entertainment. Previously she wrestled in the Professional Girl Wrestling Association (PGWA), winning the PGWA Championship in 2000.

[edit] Profile

  • Height: 5 ft 4 in
  • Weight: 150 lb
  • Birthday: August 26, 1969
  • Hometown: Fort Lauderdale, Florida
  • Trained by: Liz Chase
  • Professional debut: October 1996
  • Previous Identities: Riptide, Prodigette

[edit] Finishing and signature moves

  • Angelsault (Moonsault)
  • Angel Lock (Crossface)
  • Reverse STO

[edit] Championships and Accomplishments

  • Professional Girl Wrestling Association (PGWA)
  • PGWA Champion (1 time)
