Anglicans Online (sometimes abbreviated as AO) is an unofficial weekly newsmagazine of the Anglican Communion. Its editorial staff is private and unaffiliated. A project of the Society of Archbishop Justus founded in 1994, AO says that it includes more than 15,000 links and has more than 200,000 readers. Revealer calls it 'a kind voice in what has increasingly become a shrill, angry denomination. Anglicans Online is intellectual and populist, liberal and alert to the virtues of conservative thought, plugged into the world and wholly dedicated to chronicling the life and times of "God's frozen people."'

The site includes comprehensive listings of Anglican organizations, official websites and parish websites. It is updated each Sunday night by a volunteer staff who also write a weekly editorial letter commenting on matters of church history, theology or politics.
