Angie Gregory (born January 25, 1975) is an American actress from Draffenville, Kentucky. Angie had a lead role in an episode of Diary of An Affair which originally aired on E! Channel and Style Network in 2005. Other credits include Day of the Dead 2: Contagium, Death Valley: The Revenge of Bloody Bill, The Memory Thief, and Guilty or Innocent.
