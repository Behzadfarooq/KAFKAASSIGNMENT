Maria Lourdes Egger dela Cruz, better known by her screen name Angelika dela Cruz (born October 29, 1981), is a Filipino actress and singer of Filipino-Austrian descent. She has appeared in a lot of television shows and movies, mostly with ABS-CBN, and has recorded a couple of albums as well.

[edit] Trivia

  • She has dark brown hair and eyes.
  • Her father, Ernesto, is Filipino while her mother, german language teacher Angelika Egger, is Austrian-Italian. She got her screen name from the latter.
  • She currently resides in Malabon, Metro Manila.
  • Her younger half-sister Mariko is an up-and-coming child actress.
  • She has two younger brothers: Edward and Erick. Edward is the lead singer and guitarist for the boy band Freshmen.
  • Her father, Ernesto dela Cruz, is openly gay.
  • Height: 5'2"(1.57m)
  • Started her career in 1995.
  • She was considered for the role of Eden in 'Panday' but the role went to Heart Evangelista.
  • She was offered the role of Alena in the GMA 7's 'Encantadia' but she turned down the role because of her contract to ABS-CBN. The role went to Karylle.
  • She was offered the role of Pacita in 'Super Inggo' but she turned it down due to the role itself. The role eventually went to Angelu De Leon.
  • Season 1 winner of "Shall We Dance? The Celebrity Dance Challenge," a ballroom dancing contest.
  • Became the girlfriend of Jericho Rosales, Victor Neri and the late Mico Sotto. She is currently dating a non-showbiz guy named Orion.
