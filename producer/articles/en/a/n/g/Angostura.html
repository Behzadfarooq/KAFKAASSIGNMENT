The name Angostura may refer to

  • Angostura bitters
  • Angostura Ltd, a company manufacturing angostura bitters.
  • Angostura bark, a spice made from the bark of the tree Cusparia febrifuga. The bitter spice is used as a herbal medicine. It is not necessarily an ingredient in angostura bitters.

The Spanish word Angostura means "narrows" and so often refers to a narrowing or crossing on a river. Hence, Angostura is the name of a number of places in Latin America:

  • Angostura, Sinaloa is a municipality in northwestern Mexico.
  • Venezuela's Ciudad Bolívar used to be named "Angostura".
  • Villa La Angostura is a mountain town in Neuquén, Argentina.
  • La Angostura is a small neighborhood in the city of Ica, Peru.
