Siem Reap-Angkor International Airport (IATA: REP, ICAO: VDSR) is the second largest airport in Cambodia and is located in Siem Reap, home of Angkor Wat. Official inauguration of Siem Reap-Angkor International Airport's new terminal was in August 28, 2006.
