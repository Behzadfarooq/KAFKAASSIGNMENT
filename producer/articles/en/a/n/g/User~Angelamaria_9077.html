[edit] Various Subjects Interested In

These are various subjects that I am interested in, which I have felt (at least at some point) that I wanted to contribute to the wiki regarding these subjects. Some of them I've already fiddled with, and for others, I am just toying with the idea of fiddling with them. Note: This is obviously not an exhaustive list of things I'm interested in -- only those I've felt I wanted to work with in terms of the Wiki.

  • Old Kingdom Trilogy - This is the most recent YA fantasy series I've read, and I've always had an affinity for these.
  • The Prydain Chronicles - One of the first YA fantasy series I've read. I'm still debating whether or not I want to devote time to the Wikipedia articles for it.
  • The Song of the Lioness Quartet/Tortallan universe - Another YA fantasy series I liked when I was younger; currently working on these articles actively (see Category:Tortallan_Books).
