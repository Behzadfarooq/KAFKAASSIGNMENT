Angel's Egg (album)

A jazz-rock / psychedelic / space rock album by Gong, recorded and originally released in 1973.

Recorded using the Manor Mobile at Pavillon du Hay, France, and mixed at The Manor, Oxfordshire. Produced by Gong under the directon of Giorgio Gomelski.

"Angel's Egg" is the second of the "Radio Gnome Trilogy" of albums, following "Flying Teapot" and preceding "You".
