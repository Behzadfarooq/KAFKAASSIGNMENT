Angel Air is an independent record label established in 1997 by Peter Purnell in England.

With over 250 releases under their belt, Angel Air can boast a roster that includes such diverse acts as Mott The Hoople, Stackridge, Gillan, Maggie Bell, Freedom, GoWest, Mike Hurst, Medicine Head, Atomic Rooster, RMS and Mo Foster.
