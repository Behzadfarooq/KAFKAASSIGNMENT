Angle (disambiguation)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

See also: Angel (disambiguation) and Angles (disambiguation)

The word angle has a number of meanings. See:

Look up angle, Angle in Wiktionary, the free dictionary.
  • Angle for a corner in geometry.
  • Fishing for the technique of using a bait (luring substance) and hook to catch fish.
  • Angles for the Germanic tribe that moved to Britain.
  • Angle, Pembrokeshire for the place in Wales.
  • Angle is a character's motivating story in professional wrestling.
  • Angles are the four Cardinal points of an astrological chart: the Ascendant, the Midheaven, the Descendant and the Imum Coeli.
  • "angle" describes an attribute of a work of journalism.
  • "The Angle" refers an important strategic place and moment at the Battle of Gettysburg in the American Civil War. It was the objective of Pickett's Charge.
  • A slang term, "He knew all the angles", meaning he was streetwise. This usage is heard in the opening scene of Woody Allen's famous movie Manhattan.
  • TNA Wrestler Kurt Angle
  • Angle (rib)
