Angela is a female given name. It is derived from the Greek word ángelos (αγγελος), meaning "angel". In the United States, the name "Angela" peaked in popularity during the mid-1970s. It was the fifth most popular name for girls born between 1974 and 1976 [1].

The following people or things are known mainly by the name Angela alone (normally without an attached surname):

  • angela (band) - is a Japanese band
  • Angela (comics) - is a character in an eponymous comic book in the Spawn universe.
  • Angela (film) - is a movie directed by Rebecca Miller.
  • Angela (Battlestar Galactica) - is a fictional character in the Galactica 1980 episode The Return of Starbuck.
  • Angela (Inheritance) by: Christopher Paolini - A fictional character, who is a wisewoman in the Eragon and Eldest books.
  • Angel-A - a movie by Luc Besson
  • Angela, the theme for the Taxi series
