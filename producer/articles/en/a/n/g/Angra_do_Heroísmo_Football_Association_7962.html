The Angra do Heroísmo Football Association (Associação de Futebol de Angra do Heroísmo, abrv. AF Angra do Heroísmo) is the district governing body for the all football competitions in the former Portuguese district of Angra do Heroísmo. This organization regulates football in the Azorean islands of Terceira, São Jorge and Graciosa. It is also the regulator of the clubs registered in the district. It is located at 17 Av. Tenente Coronel José Agostinho.
