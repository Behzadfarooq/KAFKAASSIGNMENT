Hey, I'm Angus Lepper. I hail from the east coast of Scotland. At present, I'm a student at high school with a view to moving to the United States to attend university - and indeed, hopefully to stay there thereafter! I do a lot of programming, with my current projects being centred around the idea of emergent behaviour. I'm a pedant, and a great believer in exacting accuracy in all things.
