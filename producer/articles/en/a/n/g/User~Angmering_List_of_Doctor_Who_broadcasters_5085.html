The British television science-fiction series Doctor Who has been shown by many broadcasters in various countries around the world since its inception in 1963. This table attempts to list all such broadcasters, noting the years of broadcast. It also notes the series shown (the original 1963–89 run, the 1996 television movie, the 2005–present series, or any two or all three). Television showings of the two 1960s feature film versions of the programme starring Peter Cushing are not noted.
