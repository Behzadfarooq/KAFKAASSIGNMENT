The Angelg (アンジュルグ) is a fictional robot in the Super Robot Wars series. It has appeared as a playable unit Super Robot Wars Advance and Super Robot Taisen: Original Generation 2.

[edit] Appearance

The Angelg, a super robot type, is a distinctively feminine robot (which is sometimes joked about by fans). Its color scheme is a combination of pink, white and gold and it is commonly pictured holding its bow. Its design is based on the Simurgh Splendid from Super Robot Wars 64, which looked like an angel.

[edit] Technical And Historical

The Angelg is equipped with two weapons: a sword (either a metal sword or a beam rapier, depending on the game) and a bow (its main weapon). It has the ability to create afterimages of itself because of its affinity for illusions. In both games it has appeared in, when the pilot reaches 130 Will or kiryoku, the Angelg gets a 50% chance to dodge enemy attacks, even if the chance to hit is 100%.

The Angelg performs the Phantom Phoenix attack

[edit] Specifications

Model Number:SMSC

Height:47.3 meters

Weight:98.2 tons

Weapons Systems:

  • Shadow Lancer
    The Angelg shoots several green darts from the shield on its left arm.
  • Mirage Sword
    The Angelg takes out a metal sword and stabs the enemy several times in a fury. In Original Generation 2, it is a beam rapier instead, and the Angelg only slashes at the enemy twice.
  • Illusion Arrow
    The Angelg takes out its bow and shoots a blue arrow at the enemy. While the arrow flies, it leaves behind a trail of blue dust.
  • Mirage Sign
    The Angelg takes out its sword, carves two star shapes in the air and strikes through them, right through the enemy. In Original Generation 2, it uses a beam rapier and does five slashes on the enemy, which creates a pentagram. It then finishes it off by doing a final vertical slash.
  • Phantom Phoenix
    The Angelg pulls out its bow and shoots a pink arrow. As the arrow is about to hit the enemy, it turns into a phoenix, and rams through the enemy. This is the Angelg's strongest attack.

[edit] Other

The Angelg's model number is SMSC, which stands for SiMurgh Splendid Custom. Most things about it are (as of now) still unknown, but its controls are registered so that only Lamia Loveless can pilot it. The Angelg is based on the Simurgh Splendid, an original robot featured in Super Robot Wars 64. Some fans speculate that the original Simurgh will appear in the Original Generation universe.

There has been some debate among fans over what the SM part of SMSC actually stands for. Although the official romanization is Sweemurg, a number of fans have argued that the name Simurgh makes more sense and that Banpresto has been known to have used some very odd romanizations before. However, while it is said to have officially come down that SMSC means 'Shadow Mirror Simurgh Custom', this has not yet been entirely confirmed.

If one plays as Axel Almer in Super Robot Wars Advance, the Shadow Mirror forces will deploy Angelgs against him.
