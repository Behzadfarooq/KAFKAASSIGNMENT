Angell Conwell (born 2 August 1983 in Orangeburg, South Carolina) is an American actress and model.

Angell moved to Columbia, South Carolina at the age of 2. She attended Seven Oaks Elementary School in Columbia where she was the first African-American student body president. In 1994 she moved to Los Angeles to film the TV pilot "On Our Own". She also starred in movies such as Soul Plane and Baby Boy and appeared in TV productions and series like What About Your Friends: Weekend Getaway, One on One and Cuts.
