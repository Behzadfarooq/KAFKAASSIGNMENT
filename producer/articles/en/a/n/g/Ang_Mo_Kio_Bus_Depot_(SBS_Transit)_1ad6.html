Ang Mo Kio Bus Depot (SBS Transit)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

SBS Transit Ang Mo Kio Bus Depot is a bus depot located in Ang Mo Kio, Singapore. This bus depot is also the starting points for Service 45 & two Ang Mo Kio intra-town services, namely 265 and 268.
