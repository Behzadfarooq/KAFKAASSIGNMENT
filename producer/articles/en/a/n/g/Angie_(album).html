Angie
Studio album by Angela Bofill
Released 1978

Angie is the first album by Angela Bofill, released by GRP Records in 1978.

  4. "Rough Times"
  5. "The Only Thing I Would Wish For"
  6. "Summer Days
  7. "Share Your Love"
  8. "Children Of The World United"
