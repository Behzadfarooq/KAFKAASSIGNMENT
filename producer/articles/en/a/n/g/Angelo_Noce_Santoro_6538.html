Angelo Noce Santoro is a Dutch guitarist and vocalist who has been in a number of jazz and rock bands since the late 1960s.

Santoro began his music career as a member in the 1960s Dutch rock band Cosmic Dealer, which produced one album, titled Crystallization. He also played in a Dutch Brazilian band, Palmeira, produced numerous solo albums and currently (2004) is a member of the band Oriéntal Guitars.
