This is a advice page for anything from restaurants to video games. I've decided to make a page where people can come to see my opinions on these topics. Any new video game or Restaurants I go to I will update the page with a full explantion. Each of these will be judged out of 5 stars.


RESTAURANTS!

Joe's Crab Shack ****

  This nice seafood restaurant i have never been disapointed with. I have been to 2 of these restaurants in the US. MY favorite in San Diego was decorated...interestingly. I enjoyed it greatly, it was funny to see fishing nets and a boat sticking out of the ceiling. 
  I had the snow crab legs and they were great! A good full meal if i say so myself. About 2 crabs worth of legs served with a side (fries for me) and butter. It had decent pricing so i was able to get my lunch without spending more then $12. It was so good i got a desert, the ultimate chocolate cake. It was amazing, i still dream of it here in DC.


Sakura *****

  The mix of elegance and relaxing feeling in this restaurant makes it top of my list. There is no need watsoever to dress up for this restaurant, the restaurant is very flexable with the dress of their customers. This restaurant is much like other Japanese restaurants, but the menu fits America perfectly. My family and I were hungry one Friday night and we decided to go to the Japanese restaurant we had heard of from a friend. When i came in i was greated courtiously by the waitress' and waiters who quickly showed us to our tables, pausing to look at some of the Japanese decorations like a samurai armor and Samurai swords, i saw the table and wondered wat they were planning to do. 
  We were sat at around a large metal stove in the middle of us. The waitress came and took our drink orders and we ordered our food. The menu, having many of my favorites, was full of filet minion, shrimp, and other seafood. They also have a sushi bar in the corner which i looked at greedily for our next visit. A japanese cook came out and checked our orders, and cooked everything right in front of us! i enjoyed the bursts of flame from the shrimp flambe alot. They cook it like a show with lots of tricks with their knives and my personal favorite, the onion volcano. I definatly had fresh food, the filet minion rivaled my grandmother's. I definatly left with a full stomach that night.
