Angel Luis Juarbe, Jr. (May 1, 1966 â€” September 11, 2001) was a New York City firefighter who won the 2001 reality TV game show Murder in Small Town X, which was broadcast during the summer of 2001, the last episode airing on September 8, 2001. Juarbe was killed in the aftermath of the September 11, 2001 attacks a few days after the final episode was aired; he was one of the firefighters who died in the collapse of the World Trade Center towers.[1] [2]
