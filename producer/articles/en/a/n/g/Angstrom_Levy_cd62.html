Angstrom Levy is a fictional character, a comic book supervillain in the Image Comics series Invincible. His first appearance was in Invincible #16.

[edit] Character History

Little is known about Angstrom Levy's background; including how and when he developed the power to open up portals to alternate realities. At some point Levy set out to amass information about the various alternate Earths so as to be able to travel through them more safely. To this end he contacted alternate versions of himself from hundreds of parallel Earths, and convinced them to help him. Next Levy freed the supervillains the Mauler Twins from prison on the condition that they help him create a machine that would allow him to copy the memories of his alternate reality counterparts into his own brain.

With the aid of versions of themselves from other realities, the Mauler Twins were able to construct the machine. Levy was in the process of uploading the memories of his alternate selves when the process was interrupted by Invincible who was trying to apprehend the Maulers. The Maulers were on the verge of killing Invincible when Levy halted the machine to try and stop them, causing it to overload and explode. Unbeknownst to Invincible, Levy survived the explosion but was horribly mutated and suffered internal organ damage.

Surgeons were able to rebuild and even enhance Levy’s body but they were unable to correct his monstrous appearance. Levy was left with a burning hatred of Invincible whom he blamed for the accident. He was able to discover that Invincible was really Mark Grayson by travelling to realities where his identity was public knowledge. Levy then kidnapped Mark’s mother and his baby brother to lure him into a trap. When Mark arrived to rescue his family Levy used his powers to send him through a series of dangerous realities in the hope of wearing him down enough to kill him. However, the plan backfired and after Levy threatened Mark’s mother, Mark became enraged and beat Levy to a bloody pulp, apparently killing him.
