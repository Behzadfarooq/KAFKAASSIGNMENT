Angelo Rossi (musician)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Angelo Rossi was a guitar player and vocalist for the California smooth rock band Pablo Cruise. Rossi joined the band in 1981 for their last album, Reflector, when they decided to add another guitar player. Rossi left the band after the album was made, and was replaced by Stef Birnbaum for reasons unknown.
