[edit] Little Bit of About Myself

I was born December 23, 1983 in Inglewood, California and was raised in the High Desert. I have graduated with a B.A. in Sociology-Social Work Program and a B.A. in Liberal Studies-concentration in Sociology December 10, 2005 from Cal State University of San Bernardino. I am currently preparing to move to Oahu, Hawaii for my graduate degree in Social Work (MSW).
