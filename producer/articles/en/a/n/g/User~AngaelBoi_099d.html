[edit] Biography

Disclaimer: The following biography may contain witticism, so believe at your own risk.

L. Scott Thibodeaux is a man of mystique and glamour who spent his teenage years under the alias of "Angael" as the lead singer of a boyband while courting Britney Spears. Now that he's accepted his sexuality, like all good boy band members should, and settled into a more comfortable lifestyle with his new boyfriend away from the spotlight, things are a little quieter. This reprieve has left him time for more casual pursuits like writing, drawing, and destroying Western civilization before 4PM while looking fabulous. L has made his home in the swampland of Louisiana ever since his childhood, and would probably have it just about any other way. His only-child status and lack of proper induction into peer cliques by way of T-ball and Boy Scouts left him aloof for many of his early years, confiding only in adults and developing advanced critical thinking skills while maturing long before his age. In high school, he was an active participant on the national debate circuit and managed to place ninth in the nation before retiring. If you were wondering, this all means L is much, MUCH smarter than you are.

All the same, between boy bands and boyfriends, L can be found haunting his private dorm room or organizing yet another of those dastardly Pride Society events on campus. When the weekend rolls around, it's time for quality time with the significant other. Just when you thought the world thought it couldn't make something any queerer, however, it took this gay Catholic and made him a pinko, as L styles himself as a bit of a communist. When he can find the spare time to stop slacking off and act serious, he takes care of duties like "classwork" for his university courses. He is enrolled at the University of Louisiana at Lafayette as a visual arts major concentrating in computer animation. He has already obtained a minor in computer science and continues to take core courses for video game development. You can send fan mail to L about his incredible singing or sparkling repartee at angaelboi@oneofthemany.net.
