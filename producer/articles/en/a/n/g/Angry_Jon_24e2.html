Angry Jon is a Phoenix, Arizona based rock n' roll cover band that was formed in 1997. The current band members include Jon Lorenc (vocals), Edward Dalley (bass/vocals), Charlie Sutter (drums/vocals) and Will Pierce (guitars), and are perhaps best known for their musical diversity and entertaining live shows. The self-proclaimed "channel-surfing" band's setlist includes songs by artists such as Tool, Rick James, Billy Joel, Rage Against the Machine, Sublime, Prince, The Beatles, and everything in between. Asked why he is so angry, lead vocalist Jon Lorenc remarks, "I've been married twice, have 4 daughters, and started losing hair at 18. You do the math!"

Angry Jon has played well over 800 gigs since their inception and have even started their own charity, Angry Against Aids. They have been featured on various local news and radio shows and have played gigs from Flagstaff, Arizona to Puerto Penasco, Mexico. The band feels that the entertainment value is just as important as how the music is played and sounds.
