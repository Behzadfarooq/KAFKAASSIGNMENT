Angel 7 (also called Seventh Angel or 7th Angel) is a Christian death metal band from Ukraine. Currently, Black And White the band consisted of only two members, Nikolay Kiriljuk, who is responsible for all Guitars, and Slava Malinin, who plays keyboards, bass guitar, drums, vocals, and is in charge of programming.
