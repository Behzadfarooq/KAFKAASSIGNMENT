Angels and Daemons at Play is the sixth full-length studio album by the Norwegian rock-band Motorpsycho.

The album was originally released as three EP's; "Babyscooter" (released January 27), "Have Spacesuit Will Travel" (February 3) and "Lovelight" (February 10). There were only pressed 500 copies of each.

It was later also released as a double-LP.

The cover art was made by Kim Hiorthøy.
