The PFF Ang Liga or simply Ang Liga (The League) is the top league of the Philippine Football Federation open to all university teams, college teams, club teams and others. A sister league, Ang Liga Pilipina, is Ang Liga's counterpart for women's football.

[edit] Ang Liga Clubs (2006)

  • Ateneo de Manila University FC
  • De La Salle-College of Saint Benilde FC
  • EDSA FC
  • Mendiola FC 1991
  • Philippine Air Force FC
  • Philippine Army FC
  • Philippine Men's Senior National Team (guest)
  • Philippine Men's U23 National Team (guest)
  • PCU - Cheng Hua FC
  • San Beda College FC
  • University of the East FC
  • University of Santo Tomas FC

[edit] Previous Winners

  • 2003: San Beda FC
  • 2004: San Beda FC
  • 2005: St. Benilde FC
  • 2006: Army FC
