Angela Christian

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Angela Christian is an American theater actress and singer.

Christian was born in Birmingham, Alabama, but grew up in San Antonio, Texas, where she graduated from Oliver Wendell Holmes High School, and was drum major of the band. She trained at The Boston Conservatory and was the first to play several roles on Broadway. She most recently appeared as Anne Catherick in the Andrew Lloyd Webber musical The Woman in White.

[edit] Roles

  • Anne Catherick, The Woman in White (West End and Broadway) (first to play role)
  • Miss Dorothy Brown, Thoroughly Modern Millie (Broadway) (first to play role)
  • Lily, James Joyce's The Dead (Broadway)
  • Miss Molly Ivors, James Joyce's The Dead (Kennedy Center, Washington, D.C.)
