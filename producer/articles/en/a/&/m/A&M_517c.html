A&M is a three-letter acronym that may refer to:

  • A&M was originally used to differentiate between state schools and Agricultural and Mechanical schools in a certain state before the University qualification was installed. Today A&M has no meaning.
  • Texas A&M University, the flagship institution of the Texas A&M University System (the "A" and "M" no longer officially stand for anything)
  • A&M Records, a record label formed in 1962 by Herb Alpert and Jerry Moss
  • Any university with "A&M" in its name
