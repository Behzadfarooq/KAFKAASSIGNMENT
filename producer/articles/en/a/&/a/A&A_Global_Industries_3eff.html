A&A Global Industries

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

A&A Global Industries is a prominent manufacturer corporation and supplier of bulk vending machines and merchandise. It was founded in 1938 and today has more than 200 employees. Their products, mostly stickers and some candies, are sold in over 10,000 locations throughout the United States. They have more than 50% marketshare in the bulk vending industry, and have been family owned since the beginning.

[edit] History

The company began when a man named Irv Kovens repaired stamp vending machines to make money in 1938. He started out in a basement, but later moved to a busy storefront under the name of Parkway Machine Company. This later became A&A Global Industries.

As business was good when he repaired stamp machines, he started to make folders for the stamps, and then started to not only distribute vending machines, but also the contents thereof. Edward B. Kovens is the president of the company presently. He was Irv's son, and today works with his two sons to run the business.
