A&F Fragrances

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

This is a list of fragrances from Abercrombie & Fitch Co. and its subsidiaries. The fragrances from Abercrombie & Fitch, abercrombie, and Hollister Co., and RUEHL 925 are either described as sexy, casual, and/or seductive.

[edit] Abercrombie & Fitch

Colognes and Perfumes are positioned by the brand as 'Casual, Sexy and Rugged'

Colognes:

  • Fierce - FIERCE was the previous Abercrombie & Fitch Signature Cologne, it was sprayed throughout the store hourly until PROOF was introduced in Winter 2006; but is now the room spray once again and is Abercrombie & Fitch Signature Cologne again.[citation needed]
  • Ezra Fitch Cologne - Packaged in an expensive casing and wrapped in a sleek leather sleeve, Ezra Fitch Cologne is the most expensive of the men's cologne, and was named after the co-founder, Ezra Fitch.
  • PROOF - The newest A&F Fragrance, Proof comes in a sleek, circular silver/glass bottle and has the image of Winter 2005 model, Matt Ratliff on the box. It was introduced in Winter 2006.

Perfumes:

  • CLASSIC - The newest perfume for the Abercrombie & Fitch woman, Classic comes in a tall, glass bottle, and was introduced in Winter 2006.
  • 8 - One of A&F's more classical perfumes, 8 is captivated by collegiate women for its more 'upscale' scent.
  • Ready - A more fruity scent, Ready was introduced with Fierce in Winter 2004 and remains a top-selling perfume.[citation needed]
  • EZRA Eau de Parfum - Sister of the Ezra Fitch scent, Ezra parfum also comes in a leather sleeve and has a unique metal and gem cap.
  • EZRA Parfum - Sold only at exclusive store locations (as well as the online store), Ezra Parfum is the most expensive of all Abercrombie & Fitch fragrances, selling for a remarkable $108.00 a bottle.[citation needed]

[edit] abercrombie

Fragrances from abercrombie are positioned as athletic, flirty, etc. Since the fragrances are designed for children, they are less sensual than its sister brands.

Colognes:

  • CHASE - "You can't help but give into the chase."
  • abercrombie - "The perfect scent for impressing the girls sitting next to you in class."

Perfumes:

  • SPIRIT - "Go ahead and capture the spirit."
  • abercrombie - "Designed with our classic girl in mind."

[edit] Hollister Co.

Fragrances from Hollister Co. are described having a California, surfer-like attitude.

Colognes:

  • HCO22 - "The cologne of surf destiny...created by J.M.Hollister to capture the dream and flow of the tide."
  • JAKE - "Jake is the essence of a rugged, young California surfer..."
  • DRIFT - "Fresh from the beaches of California."

Perfumes:

  • MALAIA - "...the allure of the ocean and the California sun."
  • RYDER - "...named after the one girl who could charm the heart of J.M. Hollister."
  • AUGUST - "...for the playful, brazen beach girl."

[edit] RUEHL 925

Cologne and perfume from Ruehl come in an antique, ink-bottle and have a more upscale scent than the rest of the brands.

Colognes:

  • RUEHL 925 cologne - This scent smells very mature, and musk infused; it is sprayed in the store every half an hour. It now comes in a small-ounce bottle which sells for $32.00 and a larger bottle is also available.

Perfumes:

  • RUEHL 925 perfume - This is a fruity scent which is found throughout the store. The design resembles an ink-bottle and sells for $68.00.
