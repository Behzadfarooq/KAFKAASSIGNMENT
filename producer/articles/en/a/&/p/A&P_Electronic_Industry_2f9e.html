A&P Electronic Industry Co, Ltd. (hangul:에이앤피 전자산업, A&P전자산업) is a Korea's engineering and electronics company. headquartered in Hyangsan-Ri Gochon-Myeon Gimpo Gyeonggi-do, Korea. established in 2001. It is a RF, CDMA, antenna, telephone, electronic, engineering parts products. and electronic and engineering parts release from LG Electronics and Samsung Electronics.The company is commonly referred to as "A&P" (Spelling your Automation electronic & Parts system).
