Location of Ailano in Italy

Ailano is a comune (municipality) in the Province of Caserta in the Italian region Campania, located about 60 km north of Naples and about 35 km northwest of Caserta. As of 31 December 2004, it had a population of 1,443 and an area of 15.5 km².[1]

Ailano borders the following municipalities: Prata Sannita, Pratella, Raviscanina, Vairano Patenora.
