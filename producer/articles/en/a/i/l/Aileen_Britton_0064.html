Aileen Britton

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Aileen Britton (1916 - 19 April 1986) was an Australian actress.

Britton made her acting debut in Tall Timbers in 1937. She then worked on stage until the reemergence of Australian cinema in the mid-1970s.
