Air Force Systems Command

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

AFSC shield

Air Force Systems Command (AFSC) was a former United States Air Force command. Its headquarters was located at Andrews Air Force Base, Maryland. On July 1, 1992, AFSC and Air Force Logistics Command were merged to form the Air Force Materiel Command, located at Wright-Patterson AFB, Ohio.
