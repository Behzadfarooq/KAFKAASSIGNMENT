"Air Force Ones" is a 2002 song written and performed by the St. Louis hip hop artist Nelly (featuring Kyjuan, Ali & Murphy Lee), from his 2002 album Nellyville. In the song, Nelly and the St. Lunatics purchase Nike Air Force Ones.
