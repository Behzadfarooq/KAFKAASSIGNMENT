Airest

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

You have new messages (last change).

Airest (AS Airest) is an Estonian-owned airline that started in January 2002, operating regular cargo and charter passenger flights on a Tallinn-Helsinki route, since 1995 for UPS as the only UPS link to Estonia.

[edit] Fleet

As of August 2006 the Airest fleet includes as per GSA worldwide Nordic European Aviation

  • 3 Let L-410 UVP-E registered ES-LLA, ES-LLB and ES-LLC
  • 1 Antonov 26B leased for the Tallinn - Helsinki route, registration EW-245TG
