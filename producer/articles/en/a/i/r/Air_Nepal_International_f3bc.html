Air Nepal International

Air Nepal International was an airline based in Kathmandu, Nepal, operating international services.

[edit] History

The airline started services on July 24, 2005.

All of its flights were suspended from December 22, 2005 without prior notice leaving 53 passengers stranded in Kathmandu. [1]

[edit] Code Data

  • IATA Code: SZ
  • ICAO Code: NPL
  • Callsign: AIR NEPAL

[edit] Former destinations

Since they have ceased all operations, all these are former destinations that they used to fly.

[edit] Asia

[edit] South East Asia

  • Thailand
    • Bangkok (Bangkok International Airport) (Twice weekly)
  • Malaysia
    • Kuala Lumpur (Kuala Lumpur International Airport (Thrice weekly)

[edit] South Asia

  • Nepal
    • Kathmandu (Tribhuwan International Airport)

[edit] West Asia

  • United Arab Emirates
    • Dubai (Dubai International Airport) (Thrice weekly)
  • Qatar
    • Doha (Doha International Airport) (Thrice weekly)

[edit] Fleet

It used the Boeing 767-300 aircraft, leased from PB Air with a capacity of 245 seats, 24 being Business class and 221 Economy class.[citation needed]
