Airbus Military SL (Sociedad Limitada) is a subsidiary of EADS created for the management of the A400M project, taking over from Euroflag.

The company was established in January 1999 as the Airbus Military Company SAS. However the company was restructured as Airbus Military SL prior to the signature of the production contract in May 2003.
