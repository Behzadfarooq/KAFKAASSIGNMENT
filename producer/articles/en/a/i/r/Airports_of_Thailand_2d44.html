Airports of Thailand plc. (abbreviated AOT) is a large Thai public company, handling several national airports.

Established on September 30th, 2002 privatised from state-owned Airports Authority of Thailand and worth 14,285,700,000 Baht, the company is owned by the Royal Thai government with 70 % of their stock. [1] In January, 2006 the company was valued 203,345,996 Million Baht. [2] Current CEO is General Saprang Kalyanamitra. The company's seat is Don Mueang, Bangkok.

[edit] Operated Airports

AOT operates the following airports in Thailand:

  • Suvarnabhumi Airport, Bangkok
  • Don Mueang International Airport, Bangkok
  • Phuket International Airport
  • Chiang Mai International Airport
  • Hat Yai International Airport
  • Chiang Rai International Airport

[edit] Other fields

AOT offers a limousine chauffeur service at Suvarnabhumi Airport, Bangkok.
