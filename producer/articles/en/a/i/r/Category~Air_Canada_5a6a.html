Category:Air Canada

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Air Canada, (IATA: AC, ICAO: ACA, and Callsign: Air Canada) is owned by ACE Aviation Holdings Inc. TSX: ACE.RV and is Canada's flag air carrier, headquartered in Montreal, Quebec.

This category includes articles about Air Canada, ACE Aviation Holdings, and their subsidiaries.
