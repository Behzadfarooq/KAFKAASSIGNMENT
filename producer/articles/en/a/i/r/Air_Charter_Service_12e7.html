Air Charter Service is an Air Charter airline based in Costa Rica. It is compromised of two different divisions:

Aviones Taxi Aereo

and Taxi Aereo Centroamericano

[edit] History

Air Charter Service has been in operation 1970. The airline is based at Juan Santamaría International Airport, San José, Costa Rica.


[edit] Fleet

Piper Aztec 250

Piper PA-31 Navajo
