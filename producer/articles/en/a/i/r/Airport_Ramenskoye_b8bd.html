Airport Ramenskoye (ICAO: UUBW) is an airport in Moscow Oblast, Russia located 40 km southeast of Moscow and near the town of Ramenskoye. It has served as a major aircraft testing facility since the Cold War years. It is now mostly used by Ministry of Extraordinary Situations of Russia and cargo transportation.

Also known as Ramenskoye Airfield and Zhukovsky Airfield

The airport is a part of Gromov Flight Research Institute.
