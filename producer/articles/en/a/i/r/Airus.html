Airus is a rock band from Elgin, Illinois. The band consists of five members, three of which are siblings. Brian Garbrecht (vocals, tambourine), Aaron Colon (bass, back up vocals), and siblings Brooke (guitar, piano), Kevin (drums) and Max O'Neill (guitar). The band originally formed back in 2004. Although Airus is considered a rock band they have many influences including progressive, experimental, hardcore, metal, classical, piano rock, jazz, blues, and many more. Airus began recording their full-length debut in March 2006 at Forgotten City Recordings, which will be released in the early fall of the same year. At the same time the band is working on their second cd that will be released sometime in 2007. The plan is to have a dvd out with the cd. David Lind has traveled with the band and been recording videos for the release, as well as other videos that the band and friends have recorded.

Airus has played at nationally-known venues like CBGB in New York, The Rave and The Eagles Ballroom, both in Milwaukee, WI. The Double Door and The Note, both in Chicago, The House Cafe in Dekalb, IL and The Clearwater Theater in West Dundee, IL.

They have also played with nationally known acts like The Mars Volta, Dark New Day, Shindig, and H2O.

Airus was one of the bands of the month for the months of July, August, and September of 2006 on Musiqtone.com. They wrote: "Their sound is nearly reminscent of Pink Floyd, with the dreamy sounds and overall dreamy landscape with plenty of influences from hardcore and metal with a pinch of the contemplativeness of Coldplay." -Musiqtone.com

Airus was interviewed by the Chicago Sun-Times in an article that will run sometime during the fall of 2006. They have also been interviewed on the radio by Elmhurst College radio, and Jstreet Radio in Lake County, IL.
