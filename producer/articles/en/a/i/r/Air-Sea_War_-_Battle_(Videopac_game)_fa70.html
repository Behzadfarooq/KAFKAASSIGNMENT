This is cartridge number 4 in the official Magnavox/Philips line of games for the Philips Videopac. It contains two games: Air-Sea War and Battle.

[edit] Air-Sea War

Air-Sea War is a two-player game with player one controlling an aeroplane which is constantly moving from the right to the left of the screen while trying to drop bombs onto a boat controlled by player two, which is moving from left to right, and trying to fire missiles up at the plane. There are also two neutral craft in the water. A point is added for each hit on the enemy, and one is deducted for each hit on a neutral craft, although scores never go negative. The player with the most points at the end of three minutes is the winner.

[edit] Battle

Battle is also a two-player game, in which each player controls a tank around walls and mines, with the aim being to fire the most shots on your opponent's tank in three minutes, a point is deducted for hitting a mine, but scores never drops below zero.
