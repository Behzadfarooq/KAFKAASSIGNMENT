Air Åland was founded on January 14, 2005 with a broad base of owners within the trade and business of the Åland islands. The goal of the owners is to maintain air traffic with good quality for a low price to mainland Finland and Sweden.

Air Åland does not have its own aircraft. All the flights are operated by Nordic Solutions Air Services.

[edit] Destinations

  • Finland
    • Helsinki (Helsinki-Vantaa Airport)
  • Sweden
    • Stockholm (Stockholm-Arlanda Airport)
  • Åland
    • Mariehamn (Mariehamn Airport)

[edit] Fleet

No fleet of its own. 2 Saab 340 are operated by Nordic Solutions Air Services.
