Airway Lanes is the 2006 debut extended play by Melbourne rock band Airway Lanes which features five tracks by the band.

[edit] Making of the Album

Airway Lanes entered the Sing Sing studios in Melbourne with Jimi Maroudas to produce their self-titled debut EP Airway Lanes early 2006. Maroudas had previously produced records for Pete Murray and The Living End. The extended play was recorded in 2006, January and then released on 3 April through Stomp.

"Desire", from the EP, started to receive radio airplay early August on Nova FM. Airway Lanes also has many other catchy songs including the more heavier "Ships are Sinking" and the melodic beautiful end track "Down to Earth".

[edit] Track listing

  2. "Second Chance" – 3:35
