The Air Staff is Headed by the Chief of Staff of the United States Air Force (currently General T. Michael Moseley). The Air Staff is a body of high ranking U.S. Air Force officials who assist the Chief of Staff in carrying out his duties. The Chief of Staff transmits Air Staff plans and recommendations to the Secretary of the Air Force and acts as the secretary's agent in carrying them out.
