Air Southwest Ltd

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Air Southwest Logo

Air Southwest Ltd is an airline based in Chilliwack, British Columbia, Canada. It provides chartered flights throughout Western British Columbia, as well as a flight training school. Its main base is Chilliwack Municipal Airport.

[edit] Code Data

  • ICAO Code: ASW
  • Callsign: AirSouthWest

[edit] History

The airline has been in operation since 1983. It is a fully owned and operated subsidiary company of Emil Anderson Construction Company Ltd.

[edit] Services

Air Southwest operates scheduled flights to Tipella, Silver River and Bear Creek airstrips located at the shores of Harrison Lake. Scheduled flights leave Chilliwack on Monday evenings and Wednesday or Thursday afternoons.
