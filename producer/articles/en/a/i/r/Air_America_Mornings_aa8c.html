Air America Mornings was the umbrella name used by the Air America Radio network for its morning drive-time shows. The name covered two shows: The Mark Riley Show hosted by Mark Riley (who previously co-hosted Morning Sedition) and The Rachel Maddow Show hosted by Rachel Maddow (previously a one-hour show running from 5AM - 6AM ET).

The show started on January 2, 2006 and aired every weekday from 5AM EST - 9AM EST on some Air America affiliates. The use of the name ended on September 15, 2006 when the morning lineup changed.
