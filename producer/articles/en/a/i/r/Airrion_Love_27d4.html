Airrion Love (born 8 August 1949, in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania) is a member of The Stylistics, a soul group of the seventies.

The Stylistics were formed in 1968 from two Philadelphia groups, the Monarchs (Airrion Love, Russell Thompkins (lead singer), and James Smith), and the Percussions (James Dunn and Herbie Murrell). Their hits included: "You Are Everything," "Betcha by Golly, Wow," "I'm Stone in Love With You," "Break Up to Make Up," and "You Make Me Feel Brand New."

The group's second lead, Love is known for singing the baritone part of "You Make Me Feel Brand New"
