Airwaves is a Canadian television dramedy which aired on the CBC from 1986 to 1987.

The Toronto-filmed show starred Roberta Maxwell as Jean Lipton, a radio talk show host and single mother, who lived with her daughter Zoe, played by Ingrid Veninger and her father Bob, played by Roland Hewgill. Maxwell has indicated that Canadian journalist-activist June Callwood was a basis for her portrayal of Jean.[1]

The series repeated on Vision TV from 1989 to 1991.[citation needed]
