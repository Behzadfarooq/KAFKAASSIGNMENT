Air Comet (former Air Plus Comet) is a scheduled and charter airline based in Spain and is a sister company of Aerolineas Argentinas. It operates scheduled services within Europe and holiday flights to North America, South America and the Caribbean.

[edit] History

The airline was established in December 1996 and started operations in March 1997. It is wholly owned by Grupo Marsans.

[edit] Services

Air Comet operates the following services (at September 2006):

  • Domestic scheduled destinations: Madrid and Málaga.
  • International scheduled destinations: Buenos Aires, Bogotá, Lima, London, Montego Bay, New York, Paris, Puerto Plata, Punta Cana, Rome, Santo Domingo and Shanghai.
  • Charter services include: Madrid to Cancún, Punta Cana and Tenerife.

[edit] Fleet

The Air Comet fleet includes the following aircraft (at March 2007):

  • 3 Airbus A310-300 (EC-GMU, GOT, IPT)
  • 1 Airbus A320-200 (EC-KBM)
  • 2 Airbus A340-300 (EC-KAJ, KCT)
  • 1 Boeing 747-200 (EC-IZL)
  • 1 McDonnell Douglas MD-83 (EC-KBA)
