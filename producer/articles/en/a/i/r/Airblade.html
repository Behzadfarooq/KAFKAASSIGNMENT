Airblade is a futuristic sports game developed by Criterion Games and Namco. It was released on January 28, 2002 for the PlayStation 2 platform.

Airblade is quite interesting taking the idea of futuristic skateboarding as seen in the hoverboarding sequences in the film Back to the Future Part II. Unfortunately, the game sold very poorly as it could not match games such as Tony Hawk Pro Skater 4 and SSX.
