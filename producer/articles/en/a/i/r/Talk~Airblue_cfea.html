[edit] "Airblue destinations" doesn't warrant a separate article

Usually a separate article is created when the main article becomes too large like Boeing 747-400 separate from Boeing 747, Pakistan International Airlines destinations (that has a list of 90+ destinations) separated from Pakistan International Airlines etc. This airblue article is nothing like that, it hardly has 7 destinations and 1 Future route. I suggest the Airblue destinations article be merged back with the main Airblue article. Waqas.usman 14:46, 15 April 2006 (UTC)

Agreed, i have replaced the destinations back into this article. Fast track 27 April 2006 17:48 (UTC)

[edit] jetBlue

To whoever removed the jetblue redirect: It's there for people who confuse the names. While the airlines have nothing in common, they are both airlines with "blue" in the name. I've personally talked to people here in the US that, when talking about jetblue, call it "air blue" or "fly blue" or something equivilant, but not correct. I am replacing the link. --KPWM_Spotter 19:34, 25 July 2006 (UTC)
