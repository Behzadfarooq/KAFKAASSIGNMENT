[edit] Signature;

  1. Go to Special:Preferences
  2. Locate the Nickname text box
  3. Make sure the "Raw Signatures" checkbox has a check in it
  4. Enter your signature code into the Nickname text box
  5. Click save.

An example of a custom signature could be, say,

[[User:Airforceguy|Airforceguy]][[User talk:Airforceguy|<sup>Scramble!</sup>]]

AirforceguyScramble! Hope that helped! Mopper Speak! 05:05, 16 May 2006 (UTC)
