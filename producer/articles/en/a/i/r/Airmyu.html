Air Gear Musical
Opened January 7, 2007
Producer(s) MMV - Marvelous Entertainment Inc.
Official website

Air Gear Musical, also known as "Musical Air Gear" or "Airmyu," is a series of live-action stage musicals based on the anime and manga series, Air Gear. The first musical opened on January 7th, 2007 and ended on January 21st, 2007, but due to the success and popularity of the musical's first run, the musical will have a re-run in May 2007 titled, Musical Air Gear vs. Bacchus Super Range Remix (ミュージカル「エア・ギア」vs.バッカス Super Range Remix, Musical Air Gear vs. Bacchus Super Range Remix?) with the original cast reprising their roles.

The cast features Kenta Kamakari, KENN, and Kenjiro Tsuda reprising their roles from the Air Gear anime as Ikki, Kazu, and Spitfire, respectively; with Masaki Kaji, RUN&GUN, etc. as their co-stars. Of the castmembers, Kenta Kamakari, KENN and Masaki Kaji had previously worked together in the Prince of Tennis musicals.

The musical is not related to the story of the series, but is in fact, loosely adapted from the manga and is similar to the Prince of Tennis musicals in that all female roles (ex. Simca, Ringo, etc.) are eliminated from the plot.

[edit] Performances

The series has two kinds of performances: plot or story musicals (that loosely follow the Air Gear story) and special re-runs. The shows are, in chronological order:

Year Title Info
2007 Musical Air Gear Loose adaptation of the musical with original material as it only follows the battle between Kogarasumaru, the main team of Air Gear and Bacchus, an original team for the musical.
  • Ran from January 7th to January 14th, 2007 in Tokyo at the Space Zero Theater and from January 19th to January 21st, 2007 in Osaka at Theater BRAVA.
2007 Musical Air Gear vs. Bacchus Super Range Remix Rerun of the first musical.
  • Will run from May 3rd to May 8th, 2007 at the Nihon Seinenkan Dai Hall in Tokyo.
