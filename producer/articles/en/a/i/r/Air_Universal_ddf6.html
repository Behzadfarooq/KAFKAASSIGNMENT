Air Universal

Air Universal is an airline incorporated in January 2002 as a commercial air operator. The headquarters of Air Universal are located in Amman, Jordan. Air Universal is a private airline providing aviation services in the Middle East.

Air Universal is registered in the Hashemite Kingdom of Jordan, Ministry of Industry and Trade. It holds an AOC as commercial operator / scheduled and chartered operations for both passengers and cargo and specializes in the value added leasing and chartering of passenger and cargo aircraft to flag operators and other domestic and international operators. In addition, Air Universal provides maintenance, flight crews, insurance and airline support.

[edit] Code data

  • ICAO Code: UVS
  • Callsign: Uni-Leone [1]

[edit] Fleet

As of August 2006 the Air Universal fleet includes [2] :

  • 5 Boeing 747-200
  • 1 Boeing 747-200 Combi
  • 1 Lockheed L-1011-100 Tristar
  • 1 Lockheed L-1011-250 Tristar
