Air Hokkaido Co., Ltd. (エアー北海道株式会社, Eā Hokkaidō Kabushiki-gaisha?) was an airline based in Hakodate, Hokkaidō, Japan. It operated domestic feeder flights in Hokkaidō from its main base at Hakodate Airport (HKD).

  • ICAO Code: ADK
  • Call sign：Air Dream

[edit] History

The airline was established on 5 April 1994 and started operations on 22 July 1994. It is wholly owned by All Nippon Airways subsidiary Air Nippon.

The airline ceased operations on 31 March 2006 as their sole aircraft was no longer airworthy. Its sole route, Hakodate-Okushiri, was taken over the next day by Hokkaido Air System, which is owned by competitor Japan Airlines.

[edit] Fleet

The Air Hokkaido fleet consisted of 1 DHC-6 Twin Otter Series 300 aircraft (at January 2005).
