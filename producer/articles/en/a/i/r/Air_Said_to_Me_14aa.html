"Air Said To Me" is the fifth track of Shine, the 2005 release of Trey Anastasio. It was recorded in early 2005 at the Southern Tracks Recording Studio in Atlanta, Georgia. It was debuted live on April 26, 2005 at the Landmark Theater in Richmond, Virginia.

[edit] Personnel

  • Guitar, Vocals - Trey Anastasio
  • Drums - Kenny Aronoff
  • Bass, Keyboards, Backing Vocals - Brendan O'Brien
  • Percussion - Cyro Baptista
  • Cover Photos - Phil Knott
