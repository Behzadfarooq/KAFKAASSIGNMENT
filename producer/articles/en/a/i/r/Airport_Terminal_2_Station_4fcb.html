Airport Terminal 2 Station (空港第２ビル駅 Kūkō-daini-biru-eki) is a railway station located in Terminal 2 of Narita International Airport in Narita, Chiba Prefecture, Japan.

The station is shared between JR East and Keisei Electric Railway. There is a common concourse on level B1F of the main Terminal 2 building and then platforms below, using the double track originally intended for the Narita Shinkansen. JR East has the northern platform and Keisei the southern platform. The station opened on 3rd December 1992.

There is a security checkpoint between the railway station and the terminal building proper where baggage may be inspected and identification (such as a passport) must be shown.

The station is linked to Higashi-Narita Station by a 500m underground passage.

[edit] Lines

  • JR East Narita Line to Narita Station, Narita Airport Station
  • Keisei Main Line to Keisei Narita Station, Narita Airport Station

[edit] Trivia

  • This was the first station in Japan to contain Hindu-Arabic numerals (rather than Chinese numerals) in its name.
