[edit] Tagline

Bigger than the Titanic. Heavier than the Eiffel Tower. Lighter than Air.

[edit] Plot Outline

Matt Cruse works as a cabin boy on the passenger airship Aurora. When pirates attack the ship, he sets off with Kate de Vries on an adventure that takes them to an uncharted island that was once explored by Kate's grandfather.

[edit] Characters

  • Liam Aiken is starring as Matt Cruse.
