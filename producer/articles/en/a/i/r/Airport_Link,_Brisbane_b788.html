The Airport Link is a tunnelled motorway grade road which is planned for the northern suburbs of Brisbane, Queensland, Australia. It will connect the Brisbane central business district and the North-South Bypass Tunnel to the East-West Arterial Road which leads to the Brisbane Airport. It is proposed to be built in conjunction with the Northern Busway in approximately the same corridor.
