This article is about the international industrial gas company. For the electronic band, see Air Liquide (band)

L'Air Liquide S.A. (Air Liquide) (Euronext: AI) is a company supplying industrial gases and services to various industries including medical, chemical and electronic manufacturers. Founded in 1902 it is second on the world market in its field, operating in over 70 countries. It is headquartered in Paris, France. They owned the patent for Aqua-Lung until it time-expired.
