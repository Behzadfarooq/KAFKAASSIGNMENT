Airlinair is a scheduled airline based in France. It provides wet lease services for major carriers, including Air France, using regional aircraft. Its main bases are Orly Airport (ORY), Paris and Charles de Gaulle International Airport (CDG), Paris.

  • IATA Code: A5

[edit] History

The airline was established in 1998 and started operations in May 1999. It was founded by four principal shareholders and remains wholly owned by private investors.

[edit] Services

Airlinair operates services to the following scheduled domestic destinations (at December 2006):

From Beziers: Bastia
From Lyon: La Rochelle via Poitiers
From Paris - Orly Airport: Aurillac, Beziers, Brive-la-Gaillarde and Castres.

Airlinair service are also operated by Chalair on these routes:

From Le Havre: Amsterdam, Toulouse
From Mulhouse: Rennes

[edit] Fleet

  • 5 ATR 42-300
  • 1 ATR 42-320
  • 11 ATR 42-500
  • 5 ATR 72-200
  • 1 ATR 72-210
