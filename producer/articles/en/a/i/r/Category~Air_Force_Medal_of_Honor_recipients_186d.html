This page contains recipients of the United States highest military honor — the Medal of Honor — who served in the United States Air Force. Also included are those who served in the United States Army Air Forces (USAAF) and the United States Army Air Corps (USAAC) in the years 1926-1941, as these organizations are direct predecessors to the current U.S. Air Force.
