Aira was born in Holland, but her family moved to Java, specifically to Jakarta and resided in a big mansion which belongs to her ancestor, a Dutch nobleman. Aira's mother is the descedant of the nobleman, and Aira herself. However, hardly has she spoken Dutch, nonetheless she has mastered English very well, and also a bit of Javanese and of course, Indonesian.

Aira’s father is an owner of a fairly flourishing electricity industry, that’s why she has a quite prosperous life. She has 3 older siblings, and the oldest is Faither, presently studying electric engineering in a university in German, so in the future she can carry on her father’s effort. While her 2nd sister, Maple is staying in Yogyakarta, studying in Universitas Indonesia (UI) taking the accounting course, while her older sister Kanna, is still studying in High School in her last semester.

When Aira’s still a toddler, her father used to brought her along with her siblings to his industry and have a look on the machinery and workers. That’s when she’s starting to develop her fondness to electronics and since then she spends her free time reading books about electric engineering. But, when her father wasn’t keeping his eye on her, she just ran off inside the industry and touched an electric cable, -which deliver high intensity of electricity- and electrified her, causing her to faint. Fortunately, a worker found her body; nonetheless she’s in her critical moment. Her father anxiously brought Aira to the hospital and she was sent to the emergency room and received medications. It’s a success; however Aira hadn’t woken up yet.

Finally after 2 whole weeks resting, Aira finally awaken however she in some way looked different. Her body started to glow and both her eyes and hair are changing color to bright yellow, and the most extraordinary thing is when she touched a lamp, it turned on even without using electricity in front of her family. Her parents were shocked about their daughter’s abnormality, but they kept hushed on it and treated Aira just like a normal girl.

As days gone by, her Alice is getting stronger not only she can turned on light bulbs, she can even turned a computer on with her touch. Realizing that Aira has an extraordinary power which is named as an ‘Alice’ they agreed to send her to Japan, to the place called Alice Academy.
