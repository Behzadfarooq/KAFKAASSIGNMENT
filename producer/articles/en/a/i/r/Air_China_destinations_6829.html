On selected trunk routes, they codeshare with Shanghai Airlines, China Eastern and China Southern. Air China also offers frequent flights to Hong Kong, thanks to codeshare agreement with Cathay Pacific and Dragonair. Its Macau service is codeshared by Air Macau.

[edit] United Kingdom

  • London (London Heathrow Airport)

Air China codeshares with Austrian Airlines to Vienna, with Finnair to Helsinki, with Scandinavian Airlines System to Copenhagen, with Alitalia to Milan, with Turkish Airlines to Istanbul and with Virgin Atlantic to London(Heathrow). It also codeshares with Lufthansa to Barcelona and Bilbao of Spain, and to Berlin, Cologne/Bonn, Düsseldorf, Frankfurt, Hamburg, Hanover, Leipzig, Munich, Nuremberg and Stuttgart of Germany.
