The Airport Link Company Pty Ltd owns and operates four railway stations on the airport railway line in Sydney, Australia. The tunnels, track and rolling stock is owned and operated by CityRail.

Airport Link owns the International Airport, Domestic Airport, Mascot and Green Square stations. Its chief source of revenue is charging a fee, called a "GatePass" on top of the regular train fare.

As of 2004, with passenger numbers lower than expected, the company was in financial difficulties.
