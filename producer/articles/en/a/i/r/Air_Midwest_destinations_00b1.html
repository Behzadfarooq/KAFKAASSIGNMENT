This is a list of destinations and airports that Air Midwest currently serves. As of February 2007, the airline has scheduled service to 55 cities in 19 states.

[edit] Arizona

  • Lake Havasu City (Lake Havasu City Airport)
  • Phoenix (Sky Harbor International Airport)

  • Hot Springs (Hot Springs Memorial Field Airport)
  • Jonesboro (Jonesboro Municipal Airport)
  • Little Rock (Little Rock National Airport)
