Airspeed is a 1998 Canadian film from Lions Gate Films & Melenny Productions. The cast include Elisha Cuthbert as Nicole Stone, Joe Mantegna as Raymond Stone, Roc Lafortune as Captain Lopez and Bronwen Booth, Lynne Adams, Russell Yuen, Gordon Masten, Don Jordan, Martin Lacroix, Yvan Ponton, and Charles Powell.

[edit] Plot

Spoiler warning: Plot and/or ending details follow.

When the spoiled 13 years old Nicole is expelled from school for the third time, her super rich daddy has her brought home in his private 727. However in a storm the airplane gets damaged and the pilots and personnel are knocked out by loss of oxygen. So it's up to Nicole to fight for her life. Her only help is a young air traffic controller who talks to her by radio.
