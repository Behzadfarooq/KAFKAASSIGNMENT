[edit] Air Force career

Air Force Amy served in the United States Air Force as an airbase ground defense instructor and as an anti-terrorism specialist[citation needed]. During her career she was awarded the Meritorious Service Medal as well as the John Levitow Award[citation needed]. Amy was also a member of the security detail which escorted Ferdinand Marcos out of the Philippines. Amy was honorably discharged from the Air Force in 1990[citation needed]. She reportedly has a service-connected injury, which allows her to claim disabled veteran status[citation needed].

[edit] Adult career

Three months prior to being discharged, Amy, who was stationed at Nellis Air Force Base in Las Vegas, got a job at the nearby Chicken Ranch as a sex worker[citation needed]. During the next ten years Amy worked at the Chicken Ranch, the Mustang Ranch, the Sagebrush Ranch, the Old Bridge Ranch, the Cherry Patch, Sheri's Ranch, the Moonlite BunnyRanch[1] and the Wild Horse Ranch. At present, Amy is one of the feature attractions at the Kit Kat Guest Ranch. As a legal Nevada prostitute, she works as an independent contractor who turns in 50% of her money made at the brothel to the house. She is currently working at getting away from the traditional form of revue selection, known as the lineup and is trying to work by appointment only[citation needed].

Around the year 2000 Amy began to branch out in her adult career by beginning to do adult magazines, appearing in over ten different magazines in roughly four years.[citation needed]}

Amy has appeared in HBO's documentary Cathouse (2002) and Cathouse: The Series (2005).[2] According to the Internet Movie Database (as of May 2006) her only other credits are the movie Lesbian Ho'Down at the Bunnyranch and the video Pornstar Pets.[2]
