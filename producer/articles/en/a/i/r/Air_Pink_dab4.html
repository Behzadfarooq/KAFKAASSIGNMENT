Air Pink is an airline based in Belgrade, Serbia. The airline company was officially introduced in 2004. Its aircraft can fit about 6-8 people inside. Air Pink is airline company specialized for business and VIP airtaxi service. It is part of Serbia's private TV corporation PINK.

Air Pink's motto: Serbian: Vaš avion je spreman.
English: Your airplane is ready.
