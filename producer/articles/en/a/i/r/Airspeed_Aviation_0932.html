Airpseed Aviation is a small regional carrier based in Abbotsford, British Columbia.

Airspeed Aviation has provided business and pleasure travelers with air service between Abbotsford and Victoria, British Columbia since 1986 operating a corporate style twin engine [[]]Cessna aircraft.
