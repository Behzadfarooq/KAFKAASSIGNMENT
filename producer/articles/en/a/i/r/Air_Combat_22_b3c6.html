Air Combat 22 is an arcade game released by Namco in 1995 that was the sequel to Air Combat. This game was named Air Combat '22' because it ran on Namco's Super System '22' hardware.

Andrew Mercier holds the official world record for this game with a maximum possible 55 639 points. [1]
