Airport Myachkovo (ICAO: UUBM) is an airport in Moscow Oblast, Russia located 31 km southeast of Moscow. The runway length including overrun is 1650 m. The facility services transport aircraft up to the size of Ilyushin Il-76 cargo jets. It is owned by Finpromko company.

It is the largest of 19 airports for small planes, helicopters and private flights in Moscow Oblast.[1]
