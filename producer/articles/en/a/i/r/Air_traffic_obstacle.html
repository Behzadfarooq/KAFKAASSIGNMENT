An Air traffic obstacle is a tall structure which can endanger air traffic. Air traffic obstacles have to be marked in most cases with red and white colored marking and with red lights at night-times. At larger structures blinking lights are required.

In earlier days sometimes lamps similar like those used on lighthouses were sometimes in use. There are still such lamps on Fernsehturm Stuttgart at Stuttgart, Germany.

A special way for marking was used at the mast of Deutschlandsender Herzberg/Elster in 1939. Its lense-like top was lit by rotating skybeamers. By this no electrical installation were requred on this mast, which showed as insulated mast radator a high voltage against ground.
