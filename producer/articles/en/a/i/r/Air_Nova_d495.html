From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Air Nova was an airline based in Enfield, Nova Scotia, Canada and became part of Air Canada in 2001.

[edit] History

Air Nova was established in July 1986 with two DHC Dash 8-100 aircraft flying services to five destinations in Canada's north-east region. Further Dash 8s were acquired as the airline became successful and BAe 146 jets were introduced as routes opened up in Canada and the USA. In the spring of 1999 Air Alliance and Air Nova consolidated under the Air Nova name.

Air Nova was a member of Star Alliance.
