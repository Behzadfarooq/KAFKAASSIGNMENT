Air Assault on Crete.

Air Assault on Crete is a wargame published by the Avalon Hill game company in 1977. It also includes a bonus game entilted Invasion of Malta-1942. Each game has its own pieces and mapboards and share a minimum of equipment between them. The main game depicts the Battle of Crete, the first totally airborne invasion in history, by Germany on 20 May 1941 of Crete while occupied by Allied Forces.
