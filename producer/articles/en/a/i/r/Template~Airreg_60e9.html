[edit] Usage

  • {{airreg|N|328KF}} gives N328KF
  • {{airreg|C|GAUN|agency}} gives (TC: C-GAUN)
  • {{airreg|G|BOAC|agency}} gives (CAA: G-BOAC)
  • {{airreg|9M|MRA}} gives 9M-MRA
  • {{airreg|F|BTSC|disaster}} gives F-BTSC (linking into www.airdisaster.com database)

[edit] Nations supported

If a nation is supported, then at the least, it shows the abbreviation for their national authority, or possibly even links directly to the authority's web site entry for a particular registration.

  • Australia
  • Brazil
  • Canada
  • Hungary
  • New Zealand
  • Sweden
  • Switzerland
  • United Kingdom
  • United States of America

Unsupported nations are linked through to http://www.airfleets.net/.
