Airimba Wireless

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Airimba Wireless is a Richardson, Texas based company that provides high-speed wireless Internet services in the southern United States. A product of two companies, Clear Sky Networks and Wi-Fi Texas, and assembled by venture capital firms, Airimba offers service in various North Carolina cities, as well as in Clemson, South Carolina, Austin, Texas, Gainesville, Florida, Athens, Georgia, and West Lafayette, Indiana.
