Airport Station (TransLink)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Wide shot of Airport Station, facing northwest.

Airport Station is a major public transit exchange point in Richmond, British Columbia. It is part of the TransLink system in the Greater Vancouver Regional District.

[edit] History and purpose

Airport Station facing northeast, towards Lulu Island.

It was built around 2000, mainly for the 98 B-Line, a bus rapid transit line from Richmond to Vancouver. Located at the foot of the Airport Connector Bridge near the intersection of Russ Baker Way and Miller Road, it is the transfer point to the #424 Airport/Airport Station bus, the only daytime/evening route to the Vancouver International Airport main terminal. The N10 route provides service between Airport Station and the main terminal late night and early morning when the 424 is not in service.

Airport Station also serves community shuttles going to the nearby community of Burkeville and the south terminal of the airport, which serves local and smaller airlines. IKEA also sends a shuttle bus to Airport Station to pick up customers.

The transit exchange is scheduled to be replaced by Templeton Station of the rapid transit Canada Line in 2009.

[edit] Routes

Airport Station, facing south towards the south terminal and Russ Baker Way.

The following routes meet at Airport Station:

  • #98 B-Line Burrard Station/Richmond Centre(express)
  • #100 22nd Street Station
  • #424 Airport
  • #491 Burrard Station/One Road (peak hours, express, Monday to Friday)
  • #496 Burrard Station/Railway (peak hours, express, Monday to Friday)
  • #620 Tsawwassen Ferry
  • #N10 Downtown/Richmond Centre (night bus)
  • #C90 Sea Island North (community shuttle, limited service)
  • #C92 Sea Island South (community shuttle, limited service)

Airport Station used to service these routes as well:

  • #301 Airport Station/Newton Exchange (now terminates at Richmond Centre)
  • #404 Airport Station/Ladner Exchange (now terminates at Richmond Centre)
  • #425 Airport Station/South Terminal (replaced by the #C92)
