Airport Development Law

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Airport Development Law (空港整備法, Kuko Seibi Hou) of Japan was passed as Law No. 80 on April 20, 1956, which covers matters concerning the establishment, management, and sharing of airport costs, and contribute to promoting development of civil aviation.
