Air Tractor

You have new messages (last change).

The Air Tractor is a series of agricultural aircraft, used for crop dusting. Leland Snow established Air Tractor Inc. in order to manufacture a new agricultural aircraft derived from the S-2B aircraft (designed by his previous company Snow Aeronautical Company). Designated Model AT-301 Air Tractor, the aircraft first flew in 1973. Other variants include the turboprop AT-502, and the 2-seat AT-503A. Over 1700 have been built.

The Aircraft can accommodate a pilot, and a chemical hopper. In later variants, a passenger or observer can also be carried.

[edit] Specifications

[edit] AT-301

  • Length: 8.23m (27ft)
  • Wing Span: 13.75m (45ft, 2in)
  • Height: 2.59m (8ft 6in)
  • Engine: 1 Pratt & Whitney R-1340 Radial engine
  • Propeller: 2 blade
  • Empty Weight: 1656kg (3650lb)
  • MTOW: 3563kg (7860lb)
  • Max Speed: 266km/h (144 knots)
  • Range: 869km (469nm)
  • Capacity: Pilot & 1210 litre chemical hopper

[edit] AT-301A/B

As AT-301, except:

  • Capacity: Pilot & 1515 litre chemical hopper

[edit] AT-302

As AT-301, except

  • Engine: 1 Avco Lycoming LTP 101-600 Turboprop
  • Propeller: 5 blade

[edit] AT-400

As AT-302, Except:

  • Engine: 1 Pratt & Whitney PT6A Turboprop
  • Capacity: Pilot & 1514 litre chemical hopper

[edit] AT-401

  • Length: 8.23m (27ft)
  • Wing Span: 13.75m (45ft, 2in)
  • Height: 2.59m (8ft 6in)
  • Engine: 1 Pratt & Whitney R-1340 Radial engine (also recertified using the Orenda OE600)
  • Propeller: 3 blade
  • Empty Weight: 1950kg (4300lb)
  • MTOW: 4082kg (9000lb)
  • Max Speed: 251km/h (135 knots)
  • Range: 1015km (547nm)
  • Capacity: Pilot & 1515 litre chemical hopper

[edit] AT-402

As AT-400, except:

  • Wing Span: 13.75m (45ft, 2in)

[edit] AT-501

As AT-401 except:

  • Wing Span: 15.24m (50ft)

[edit] AT-502

  • Length: 9.91m (32ft 6in)
  • Wing Span: 15.24m (50ft)
  • Height: 2.99m (9ft 10in)
  • Engine: 1 Pratt & Whitney PT6A Turboprop
  • Propeller: 5 blade
  • Empty Weight: 1820kg (4123lb)
  • MTOW: 4975kg (9200lb)
  • Max Speed: 290km/h (155 knots)
  • Range: 998km (538nm)
  • Capacity: Pilot & 1892 litre chemical hopper

[edit] AT-503

As AT-502, except:

  • Capacity: Pilot, observer & 1892 litre chemical hopper

[edit] AT-602

As AT-502, except:

  • MTOW: 5600kg (12000lb)
  • Capacity: Pilot & 2381 litre chemical hopper

[edit] AT-802

  • Length: 10.87m (35ft 8in)
  • Wing Span: 18.06m (59.3ft)
  • Height: 3.35m (11ft)
  • Engine: 1 Pratt & Whitney PT6A Turboprop
  • Propeller: 5 blade
  • Empty Weight: 2903kg (6400lb)
  • MTOW: 7257kg (16000lb)
  • Max Speed: 338km/h (182 knots)
  • Range: 805km (434nm)
  • Capacity: Pilot, Observer & 3066 litre chemical hopper

[edit] AT-802A

As AT-802, except

  • Capacity: Pilot & 3066 litre chemical hopper

[edit] AT-802AF
