Air Link International Airways

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Air Link International Airways is an airline based in Pasay City, Philippines. It operates charter services and a flying school with small aircraft. Its main base is Ninoy Aquino International Airport, Manila.

[edit] History

The airline was established on 10 October 1983 and started operations in 1984.

The Air Link International Airways fleet includes (at August 2006):
