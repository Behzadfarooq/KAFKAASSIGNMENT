Airport Station is a OC Transpo bus stop in Ottawa that serves the Ottawa Macdonald-Cartier International Airport. It is located in the south end of Ottawa.

[edit] Service

See also: OC Transpo Routes

Route 97 is the only bus that services this station. It is the terminus for this route.

Route 97 takes 6 minutes to reach South Keys Station, 18 minutes to reach Hurdman Station, 45 minutes to reach Lincoln Fields Station, and 1 hour 16 minutes to reach Bayshore Station.

Note: Early morning trips towards downtown travel via Uplands, Breadner, Canadair, and Hunt Club.
