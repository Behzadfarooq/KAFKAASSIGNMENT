Air (Japanese band)

This page concerns the Japanese band AIR, not to be confused with the French band Air.
This article on a Japanese band or other musical ensemble is a stub. You can help by expanding it.

AIR is a name under which Kurumatani Koji releases his songs. He started AIR two months after SPIRAL LIFE broke up in April 1996.
