Airliners is a U.S. magazine dedicated to the airline industry, first published by World Transport Press in 1988. Since the 100th issue (July/August 2006) it has been published by Airliners Publications, LLC. Six 80-page issues are published each year.

It includes articles about the world of commercial aircraft and air carriers, including new low cost airlines, changes at legacy companies, jetliners such as the Airbus A380 and Boeing 787, trips around the world, historical airlines, adventurous flights, airliner crashes and pictures of airliner liveries.

Regular contributors include Robbie Shaw, Sebastian Schmitz, Dave Nichols, John McCollister and Jay Selman.

The magazine's founder and original editor was John Wegg. Subsequent editors were Bryant Pettit, Nick Veronico, Jon Proctor and David Kaufman. The editor is Jay Selman.
