Air Tahiti Nui is French Polynesia's flag carrier airline with its headquarters in Papeete, Tahiti. The airline operates from Faa'a International Airport on the island of Tahiti.

[edit] History

The airline was established on 31 October 1996 and commenced flight operations on 20 November 1998. It is the first international airline based in Papeete, Tahiti, formed to develop inbound tourism. The Government of French Polynesia is the major shareholder (61.4%) along with other local investors.

[edit] Fleet

The Air Tahiti Nui fleet consists of the following aircraft (at August 2006) [1] :

  • 5 Airbus A340-300
  • A possible 6 A340-300 has been deferred for some time until Air Tahiti Nui can resolve their financial crisis.
  • There is a possibility that Air Tahiti Nui will become one of the customers of the Airbus A350. [2]
