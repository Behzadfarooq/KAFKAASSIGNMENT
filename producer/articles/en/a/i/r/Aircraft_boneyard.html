Aircraft boneyards provide rest for aircraft that are retired from years of service or lack thereof.

[edit] Locations of notable aircraft boneyards

  • Pinal Airpark, Arizona
  • Aerospace Maintenance and Regeneration Center, Davis-Monthan Air Force Base, Arizona
  • Scrapper's Row, Oklahoma
  • Mojave Spaceport, California
