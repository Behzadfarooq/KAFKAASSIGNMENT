[edit] Me:Airridi

Hi. My name is "Airridi." (you can pronounce it just like 'EYE-rid-ee.')
I am an 20-year-old resident of Seoul, Korea and a sophomore at Seoul National University, majoring in English linguistics and literature. I began editing Korean Wikipedia in April 2006, and I have edited mainly some materials about languages in Korean Wikipedia. And I will try to contribute more language-related stuffs to Korean Wikipedia.

[edit] Etymology:Airridi

"Airridi" is a Elue word. Elue is an artificial language which I created.

  • "air-" : means "booklet" and it implies "short story"
  • "rid" : means "water" and it is a conventional expression of "life" in Elue language
  • "-i" : is just plural marker

So "Airridi" literally means "small booklets about water," but it refers "short stories on life."
