Ainturk

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Ainturk (Arabic: عين ترك) nearby Bouira (Algeria) has the biggest viaduct (in construction) of Africa with 200 meters spans and 104 meters stems.

The viaduct has a total length of 765 meters and it is, geographically, in the middle of the National Algerian Highway Project.
