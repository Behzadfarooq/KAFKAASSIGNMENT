Ain't Gonna Worry, released in 1990, marked the end of Crystal Gayle's run of Billboard chart appearances (as of 2005). One of its tracks, "Never Ending Song of Love," would only rise as high as #72 on the Country Singles chart. The album itself did not even make the charts. Her only album on the Capitol Records label, it included covers of "Once in a Very Blue Moon" and "Faithless Love," songs previously associated with Nanci Griffith and Linda Ronstadt, respectively.
