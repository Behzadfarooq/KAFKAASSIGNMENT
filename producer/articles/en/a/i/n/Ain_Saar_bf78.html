Ain Saar (1968) is a Võro punk rocker, and freedom fighter, the leader of the group Vaba-Sõltumatu Noorte Kolonn Nr.1 (Free Independent Youth Column No. 1) of December 20, 1987, which struggled against "Pamyat", national bolsheviks and stalinists. He was exiled by authorities of Estonian SSR in 1988.

As of 2006, Ain Saar resides in Skarpnäck, Stockholm, Sweden.
