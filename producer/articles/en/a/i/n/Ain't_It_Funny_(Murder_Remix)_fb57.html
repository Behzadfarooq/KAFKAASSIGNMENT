"Ain't It Funny (Murder Remix)" is an R&B-Hip-Hop song written by American singer-songwriter Jennifer Lopez, Ja Rule, Caddillac Tah, Ashanti Douglas, Irv Gotti, Cory Rooney, 7 Aurelius, Easy Mo Bee, and Craig Mack for remix album, J To Tha L-O!: The Remixes (2002).

[edit] Song information

Released in 2002, the single, which features Ja Rule, peaked at number-one in the U.S. becoming Lopez's third number-one single; it also peaked at number-four in the UK. Caddillac Tah also performs on this song and appears on the song's music video, and even has a longer part than Ja Rule.

The original version appears on Lopez' 2001 second album J. Lo, and was also a single of its own in 2001 (see "Ain't It Funny"). The original version of the song was written by Cory Rooney for the soundtrack of Lopez' film The Wedding Planner, but it was felt that the timbales gave it too much of a Latin sound, and the song was not used for the soundtrack. After the success of "I'm Real (Murder Remix)", a previous Lopez-Ja Rule duet, they decided to remix "Ain't It Funny" as a duet and release it as a follow up. The remix samples Craig Mack's smash 1994 hit single Flava In Ya Ear.

The song spent six weeks at the top of the Billboard Hot 100 from March 9, and followed another Ja Rule duet, "Always On Time" with Ashanti. Ashanti's song "Foolish" replaced it at the number one spot. Ashanti also receives writing credit for the track and appears in the video alongside Jennifer Lopez, Ja Rule, Irv Gotti, Caddillac Tah and other Inc. members.
