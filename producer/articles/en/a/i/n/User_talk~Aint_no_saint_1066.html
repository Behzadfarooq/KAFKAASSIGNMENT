[edit] Buffy episode edits

Thank you for adding useful information to Real Me (Buffy episode) and Buffy vs. Dracula (Buffy episode). However, it didn't quite display correctly. Might I ask that you preview your edits before you save them? For the credits, you need to put the names inside the [[ ]] brackets (not to the right of them) for them to be displayed in the article. For the quotes, they should follow one of two common formatting styles while editing:

* "Single character quote." — Character
-or-
:'''Character 1''': First dialog line.
:'''Character 2''': Second dialog line.

(The second format uses three apostophes (''') on either side of the character name to make it bold.) These look like the following when displayed:

  • "Single character quote." — Character
-or-
Character 1: First dialog line.
Character 2: Second dialog line.

Also, you shouldn't add text on the same line as a heading (the text surrounded by == ==), as it can cause malformatting. I've updated those two articles to provide correct formatting. You can see the differences by clicking on the History tab at the top of the article and comparing the old and new versions. I also urge you to include brief notes in the Edit summary of your edits so that people examining the article history have an easier time understanding the changes without having to read every single edit. If you have any questions, you can post them to my talk page. I hope you find this info helpful. ~ Jeff Q (talk) 20:16, 22 October 2005 (UTC)
