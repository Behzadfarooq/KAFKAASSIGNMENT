Ain't No 401K For A Hustler is the second mixtape of 2007 to be released by the Swishahouse. This is supposed to be the official mixtape before Paul Wall's sophomore album (entitled Get Money, Stay True and is set to release on April 3, 2007). Disc one is regular speed and features mainly flows from Paul Wall. Disc two is the same songs as disc one, except they've been given the screwed & chopped treatment from DJ Michael "5000" Watts
