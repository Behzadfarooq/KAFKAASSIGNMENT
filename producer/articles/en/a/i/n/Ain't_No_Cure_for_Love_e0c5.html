"Ain't No Cure for Love" is a pop song by singer/songwriter Leonard Cohen. It was first released by Jennifer Warnes on her 1987 tribute album Famous Blue Raincoat.

The song subsequently appeared as the second track on Cohen's own 1988 album I'm Your Man. This recording is notable for the intro featuring the only saxophone heard throughout the album.
