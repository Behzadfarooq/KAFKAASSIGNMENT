Ain't Life Grand is the second and last album by Slash's Snakepit, released on October 10, 2000. The original line-up who recorded the material for It's Five O'Clock Somewhere disbanded thus Slash had to find new members.

Ryan Roxie was replaced by Keri Kelli after the recording of Ain't Life Grand.
