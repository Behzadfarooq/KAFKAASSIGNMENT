[edit] Two Music Videos

The first time I saw the video for the song, the opening sequence (before Christina starts to sing) was in a club with a circular stage and there were dancers and trumpet players up on the stage. Christina arrived in back and spent pretty much the whole time on that stage. Only one dress (well, she tore that off), rather than multiple outfits. I don't recall if it had the torch song at the end.

Ever since then, everytime I see the video, it is a different one with her arriving in a car, several sets with different outfits. But I see no mention of this here. Does anyone know what the deal is with this? Should there be some mention in the article? Thanks. PerlKnitter 12:11, 1 July 2006 (UTC)
