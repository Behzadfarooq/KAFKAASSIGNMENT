Ainsworth (surname)

Ainsworth is a surname with its origins in the North West of England. The origin of the word ainsworth is from the anglo saxon name for an 'Enclosure'.

An Ainsworth crest

  • Mary Ainsworth (1913-1999), an American developmental psychologist
  • Kacey Ainsworth (b.1971), an English actress
  • Laban Ainsworth (1757-1858), an American clergyman and pastor
  • Ray Ainsworth, an Australian rugby league player
  • Robert Ainsworth (1660-1743), an English Latin lexicographer
  • Robert "Bob" Ainsworth (b.1952), a British politician and MP

  • William Harrison Ainsworth (1805-1882), an English historical novelist
