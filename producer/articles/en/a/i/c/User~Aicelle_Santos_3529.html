Aicelle Anne Santos is the eldest daughter of Ramon ‘Ongie’ and Leonila ‘Onnie’ Santos. She was born on February 24, 1985 at Quezon City. Aicelle have a sister and two brothers named, Anelle, Aaron and Abraham David respectively. She comes from a very religious family. Aicelle studied at St. Scholastica’s College and transferred to St. Theresa’s College in grade school. She remained at the same school all throughout High School. After graduation, she enrolled at Pamantasan ng Lungsod ng Maynila, taking up Bachelor of Science in Psychology. She is now on her 3rd year and is planning to enroll this coming semester.

Aicelle Anne Santos’ favorite color is yellow, orange and blue. She loves to eat pasta and her most favorite cartoon character of all is Ariel from the Little Mermaid. She has a very comfortable clothing style. A pair of jeans and sneakers is perfect for her


Her favorite TV programs are Pinoy Pop Superstar and SOP. She loves watching the movie MULAN. She likes most African-American singers like Aretha Franklin, Lauryn Hill and Alicia Keys. The type of music she prefers listening to are soul, jazz and R n’ B.

Aicelle was inspired by Ariel of The Little Mermaid when she was (7) seven years old, because of her powerful and enchanting voice. She joined different contests and became one of the contestants of Star in a Million in ABS-CBN. She also joined Pinoy Pop Superstar and lost on her first try singing Climb Every Mountain but she didn’t give up and tried her luck again. Luckily, she won on her first week singing ALL THE MAN THAT I NEED and continued winning for eight weeks becoming the FIRST UNDEFEATED SUPERSTAR of Pinoy Pop Superstar. She became the 1st Runner of Pinoy Pop Superstar Year 2. Now Aicelle Santos is one of the mainstay artists of SOP every Sunday at GMA 7. She has been so blessed for the past year and now Aicelle is preparing for her Battle in Astana Music Festival as a representative of the Philippines this September 2006 at Kazakhstan.
