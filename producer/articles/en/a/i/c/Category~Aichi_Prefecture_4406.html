Category:Aichi Prefecture

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Aichi Prefecture (愛知県 Aichi-ken) is a prefecture located in the Chubu region, Chūkyō region of Japan. The capital is Nagoya.

The main article for this category is Aichi Prefecture.
