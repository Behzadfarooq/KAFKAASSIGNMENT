Aicelle Santos is a Filipino singer and recording artist, who appears in the hit GMA Network show SOP, and in his own TV show Bitoy's Funniest Videos. She is known for being the First Undefeated Pinoy Pop Superstar, having won for eight straight weeks in Pinoy Pop Superstar, one of the highest-rating shows in GMA.

[edit] Background

Aicelle Anne Santos is the eldest daughter of Ramon ‘Ongie’ and Leonila ‘Onnie’ Santos. She was born on February 24, 1985 at Quezon City. Aicelle have a sister and two brothers named, Anelle, Aaron and Abraham David respectively. She comes from a very religious family. Aicelle studied at St. Scholastica’s College and transferred to St. Theresa’s College in grade school. She remained at the same school all throughout High School. After graduation, she enrolled at Pamantasan ng Lungsod ng Maynila, taking up Bachelor of Science in Psychology.

Aicelle was inspired by Ariel of The Little Mermaid when she was (7) seven years old, because of her powerful and enchanting voice. She joined different contests and became one of the contestants of Star in a Million in ABS-CBN. She also joined Pinoy Pop Superstar and lost on her first try singing Climb Every Mountain but she didn’t give up and tried her luck again. Luckily, she won on her first week singing All The Man That I Need and continued winning for eight weeks. She became the 1st Runner-up of Pinoy Pop Superstar Year 2. She also won one of the major awards in Astana Music Festival in Kazakhstan for the category of Best of Own Country Song. She has been so blessed for the past year and now currently planning for the album within this year.
