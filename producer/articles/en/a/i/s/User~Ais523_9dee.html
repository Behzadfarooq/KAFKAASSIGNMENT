 Hello! I'm ais523, an incrementalist metapedian. I'm happy to help new users (or experienced users, for that matter); just leave a note on my talk page; in particular, I can help with wikimarkup (especially template wikimarkup) and signatures. However, I'm not always online; if you don't need help from me in particular, then placing {{helpme}} on your own user-talk page is likely to produce faster results. I mainly dabble in recent change and new-page patrol, deletion debates (particularly TfD, but also MfD, RfD, and AfD), and practicing advanced template wikimarkup (see an example to the left). I am a fan of esoteric programming languages. I run a bot, Bot523.
WikiDefCon alerts users when extra anti-vandalism patrolling is needed.
Prepare for April Fools' Day. Vandal fighters, have your batons at the ready. Be vigilant! CAT:CSD still has a heavy backlog.
Some of my user subpages:
  • List of bot-generated summaries: A list of the summaries produced by other user's bots of AfD, prods, and RFA.
  • List of unclosed AfDs: An attempt to help sort out the AfD backlog by identifying AfDs left open for more than a week.
  • My edit counter: An HTML-scraping user-script edit counter.
