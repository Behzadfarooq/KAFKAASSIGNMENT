This list lists AfD debates that have been in the category for over a week, with the timestamp they were added. It is generated using the same process used to preselect candidate pages for Bot523 to check. Note that this includes malformed nominations and relisted nominations, as well as ones which ought to be closed and haven't been. As the list attempts to figure out the name of the article from the name of the debate, it may occasionaly give a broken link to the article (for instance, a debate with a name ending in ' 2' may be a second nomination or a nomination of an article ending in ' 2', and I've seen both in the same debate before). The list is sorted and given subheadings by a separate program.
