Aishite ne Motto (愛してねもっと, Aishite ne Motto?) is Aiko Kayo's second single. It was released on April 14, 2004, by AVEX Records.

Aishite ne Motto was used as the ending theme for the anime Tenjou Tenge, while the c/w track Eyes, was used as the ending theme for the TV drama Eko Eko Azarak ~Me~, in which Aiko played the character Misuzu Kurahashi. First pressings of the single included a sticker.
