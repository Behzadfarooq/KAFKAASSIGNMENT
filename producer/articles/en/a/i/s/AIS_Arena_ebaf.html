AIS Arena is an indoor sporting arena located in Canberra, Australia. It is the home arena of the AIS Canberra Darters netball team and the Canberra Capitals and Australian Institute of Sport basketball teams also play games at the Arena. It was home to former NBL team the Canberra Cannons. The capacity of the arena is 5,050 and was built in 1981.
