"Aisa Des Hai Mera" (Hindi: ऐसा डेस है मेरा, Urdu: ایسا ڈیس ہے میرا) is a song from the movie Veer-Zaara, directed by Yash Chopra.

[edit] About the song

The music was composed by the Late Madan Mohan and recreated by his own son Sanjeev Kohli and is sung by Udit Narayan and Lata Mangeshkar, Gurdas Mann and Pritha Majumder.

[edit] Music Video

The music video is set on Shahrukh Khan and Preity Zinta in the beautiful villages of India.

[edit] Trivia

  • The man in blue dress who sings and dances with Shahrukh Khan durring the song is the singer Gurdas Mann and sings his own part.
  • This song marks the unity of India and Pakistan as the singer sings the following:

Zaara: Here is the same evening and morning
Same evening and morning
My country is same as your country is

Veer: My country is same as your country is

Zaara: My country is same as your country is

Veer: My country is same

Zaara: As my country is
