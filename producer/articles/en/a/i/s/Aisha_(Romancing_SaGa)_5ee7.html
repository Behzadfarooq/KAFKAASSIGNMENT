Aisha (Romancing SaGa)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

 Aisha
Default Class: Herbalist
Proficiencies: Terrology, Gathering, Survival
Age: 16
Birthplace: Dry Lands

Aisha is a character from the video game Romancing SaGa. She is a member of the Taralians, a tribe of nomads who wander the Steppes of Galessa. She lost both parents at a young age, and was raised by her grandfather Nizam, elder of the tribe. Growing up among several peace-loving and pure-minded people, she developed a curious and cheerful personality.

Aisha can be found in the Amut Temple of North Estamir during the "Free the Slaves" quest and travels between North and South Estamir and Tarmitta if not the main character.
