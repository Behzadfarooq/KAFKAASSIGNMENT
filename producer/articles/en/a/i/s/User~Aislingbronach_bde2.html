User:Aislingbronach

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Aisling Bronach's interests include anthropology, archaeology, folklore, cultural traditions, and history with an emphasis on Celtic cultures - more specifically Irish and Welsh. Her areas of expertise include Celtic folklore, Paganism, and post-Neolithic European religion and sorcery.

She has been active within the Pagan community for over 20 years and received her degree in Anthropology from the University of South Florida.
