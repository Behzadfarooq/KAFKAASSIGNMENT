Aisai (愛西市; -shi), or Aisai City, is a city located in the Owari Region of Aichi Prefecture, Japan.

On April 1, 2005, Saya and Saori Towns and Hachikai and Tatsuta Villages of Ama District were merged into Aisai City.

As of March 15, 2005, the city has an estimated population of 65,597 and a density of 984 persons per km². The total area is 66.63 km².
