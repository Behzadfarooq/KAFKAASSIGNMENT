Aiseau-Presles (Walloon: Åjhô-Préle) is a Belgian municipality located in the Walloon province of Hainaut. On January 1, 2006 Aiseau-Presles had a total population of 10,754. The total area is 22.19 km² which gives a population density of 485 inhabitants per km². It is located in the neighbourhood of Charleroi. Its postal area is: 6250

Cities and communes bordering: Châtelet - Farciennes - Fosses-la-Ville - Gerpinnes - Sambreville
