Aidan Collins (born 18 October 1986 in Harlow) is an English footballer, currently signed to Nationwide Conference side Cambridge United. Having spent the early years of his career at Ipswich Town FC (wot an idiot), he made his debut aged just 16, in May 2003, when Town beat Derby County 4-1. Due to injury and illness he never made a major impact on the senior squad, however played in the very successful youth team. Though Collins made 3 league appearances in the 2005/06 season for Ipswich, he was put up for a free transfer and duly snapped up by Cambridge in January 2007.
