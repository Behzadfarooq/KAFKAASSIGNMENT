Aidan Devane is currently a resident of the fictional town of Pine Valley on ABC's soap opera, All My Children. He is currently played by Aiden Turner from June 6th, 2002 to Present. The character has also been temporarily played by Tom Archdeacon from May 13, 2004 to May 28, 2004 for 6 episodes.
