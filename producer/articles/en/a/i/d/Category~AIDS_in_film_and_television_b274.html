Category:AIDS in film and television

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

A category for films (theatrical and television) and television programs in which AIDS or HIV is a significant plot element or which include one or more characters with AIDS or HIV. This category is not for "very special episodes" or one-shot characters.
