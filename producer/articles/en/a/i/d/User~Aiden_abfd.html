[edit] Interests

I am avidly interested in history, religion, and politics. In particular, I am most interested in history relating to the Middle-East, specifically Israel and the Arab-Israeli Conflict, and history and practices of religions, specifically Abrahamic religions. I am also interested in the history and culture of the Far East, particularly that of Japan. However, I often find myself taken down the inevitable rabbit holes of Wikipedia, reading and researching a wide variety of subjects.
