Aidan Girt (born July 18[1]) is a Canadian drummer for the Montreal-based post-rock collectives, Godspeed You! Black Emperor and Exhaust. He is also a solo electronic artist under the moniker 1-Speed Bike and occasionally Bottleskup Flenkenkenmike.

Girt has also played drums as a guest musician on the debut album of A Silver Mt. Zion.
