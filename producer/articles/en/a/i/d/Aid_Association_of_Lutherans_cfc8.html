Aid Association of Lutherans

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

You have new messages (last change).

The Aid Association for Lutherans was founded in 1902 in Wisconsin as a fraternal benefit society aimed at providing low-cost life insurance to its members and supporting Lutheran communities in America.

The group merged its insurance and financial services functions with the Lutheran Brotherhood to form Thrivent Financial for Lutherans.
