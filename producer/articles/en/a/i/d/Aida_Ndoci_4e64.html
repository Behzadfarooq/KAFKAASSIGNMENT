Aida Ndoci

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Aida Ndoci is an Albanian singer who will represent her country at the Eurovision Song Contest 2007 with the song "Balada e Gurit "(Ballad of Stone). She will perform it together with her husband, Frederik Ndoci.
