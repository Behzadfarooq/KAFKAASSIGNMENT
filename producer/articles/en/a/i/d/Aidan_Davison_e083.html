Aidan Davison (born May 11, 1968 in Sedgefield, County Durham) is an English-born Northern Irish professional footballer currently playing for Colchester United. His previous clubs include Grimsby Town, Bradford City, Notts County and Millwall. Davison has been capped three times by Northern Ireland.
