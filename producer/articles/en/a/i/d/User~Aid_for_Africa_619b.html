[edit] Aid to Africa

Why does Bono want MY tax money that was supposed to be spent on keeping criminals off MY streets to some group of hungry africans? Most of the criminals here have just got off the banana boat from these countries, but Bono insists on giving these potential criminals the means not only to prolong their lives, but to sail to the nearest port so they can fall asleep on MY doorstep, send their 500 children to school to disrupt classes (guess who pays) and be a general canker on society. Instead of feeding these ruffians, I suggest we allow them to die a humane and dignified death in their countries of origin. Cancel all aid and place floating mines around the shores of their countries. This will force them to stop having children at every possible opportunity, force them to stay off my streets (unless they're bombproof) and improve both their and my quality of life.
