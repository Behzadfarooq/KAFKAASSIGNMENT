Aiden O'Keefe (footballer)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Aidan O'Keefe is a football player for Bohemians FC in the Eircom League. He is a Striker. O'Keefe was signed from St. Patrick's Athletic, and has also played with Kilkenny City and Dublin City on loan spells.
