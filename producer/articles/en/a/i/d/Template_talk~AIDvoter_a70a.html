This template is used to tag user talk pages of people who vote for the winning candidate on Article improvement drive. It inserts its own header and should be added at the end of the talk page, like any normal comment.

To allow for future (weekly) updates of the template, the text of the template should be added by transclusion and not by reference:

{{subst:AIDvoter|foo}}

which inserts the following text:

==AID project==

On this week's Article Improvement Drive voting, an article you supported was selected. This week we focus on improving foo to featured article status. Hope you can help.

-- the wub (talk) 13:03, 17 Jun 2005 (UTC)

[edit] This template does not work properly

I tried contacting people to notify them that Volcano was this week's AID winner, but found they had already been notified a week before. I think this is because they also voted for last week's AID winner, which was George Washington. This situation occurs due to the construction:

You helped choose {{IDRIVEtopic article}} as this week's [[WP:AID]] winner, which changes as soon as IDRIVEtopic article is changed. Can someone have a look at this, please? Errabee 12:00, 4 September 2006 (UTC)
