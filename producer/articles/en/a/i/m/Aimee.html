Aimee
Gender: Female
Origin: French
Meaning: "Beloved"


Aimee (or Aimée) is a given name and a version of Amy. Both names come from Aimée, which means beloved in French, from Old French amede, from Latin amāta, feminine singular past participle of amāre "to love".
