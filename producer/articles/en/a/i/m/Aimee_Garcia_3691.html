Aimee Garcia

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Aimee Garcia (born November 28, 1978 in Chicago, Illinois, USA) is an American actress.

Garcia is of Mexican and Puerto Rican descent. She was to appear in the Global Frequency television series as Aleph, but only a pilot was produced, and it was not picked up. She had a role on Cadet Kelly. In 2006, she appeared on The George Lopez Show as George Lopez's spoiled niece, who inherited a million dollars. She was only suppose to be on for 4 episodes, but because of the popularity of character, she has been picked up for the sitcom. She also co-starred alongside Anthony Anderson in the TV series "All About the Andersons" where she played the energetic, Latina med-student, Lydia.
