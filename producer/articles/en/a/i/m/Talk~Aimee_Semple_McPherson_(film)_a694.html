Although this 2005 independent film is entitled "Aimee Semple McPherson" the wikipedia article on the movie is entitled "Aimee Semple McPherson movie" to avoid confusion with the biographical article on Aimee Semple McPherson.

The naming convention here would be to call it "Aimee Semple McPherson (film)" or Aimee Semple McPherson (2005 film)" if there are others by the same title. -Will Beback 00:40, 22 March 2006 (UTC)

[edit] Update

ASM has become an international phenomenon due to the religious controversy and attempts to ban the film by the International Church of the Foursquare Gospel. For further research on religious opposition to the film, visit www.foursquare.org and see church statement on "Richard Rossi movie." Gina Lindley
