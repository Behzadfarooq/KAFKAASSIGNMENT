[edit] FasTrak

Selamat sejahtera (hello), Aiman abmajid. I was thinking about creating an article about a ceased toll system in Malaysia called FasTrak so that people will actually know what it means. Could you help me find some information regarding FasTrak and why isn't it used in Malaysia any more? Thank you and I would really appreciate it if you help me out. Acs4b 09:51, 21 December 2006 (UTC)

Selamat sejahtera (hello), Acs4b. Ok, Fastrak was a non-stop (Electronic toll collection) ETC system that already being used in every Gamuda Expressway Networks like Damansara-Puchong Expressway (Litrak-LDP) (opened on 1995}, Shah Alam Expressway (Kesas-SAE) (opened on 1995), and Sprint Expressway (Sprint) (opened on 2001). The Shah Alam Expressway (Kesas-SAE) was the first Gamuda Expressway to used the system on 1995 followed by Damansara-Puchong Expressway (Litrak-LDP) and Sprint Expressway (Sprint). However on 1 July 2004, This non-stop ETC system was no longer being used and replaced by Smart TAG and Touch 'n Go ETC system following the instructions of Works Minister, Dato' Seri S Samy Vellu that the implementation of a single ETC nationwide as a form of standardisation and convenience of use for road users. The directive requires all major toll operators in Malaysia to have a minimum of 2 Touch 'n Go and 1 SmartTAG lanes per direction at every plazas. That's all about information of Fastrak ETC system! Highway specialist in Malaysia 12:22, 22 December 2006 (UTC)
