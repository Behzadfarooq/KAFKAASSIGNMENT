Aimee Sweet (May 14, 1977, Providence, Rhode Island, United States) is an American glamour model and a porn star.

Since the age of 6, she began performing in stage plays and singing, as well as developed an interest in sports such as hockey, softball, swim and cross country, which she continued to do through high school. She also was very involved with her school's student council.

Sweet's career began at age 19 when she had a chance meeting with Suze Randall. Ironically, she went to Randall's studio to accompany one of her friends, who aspired to be a model. When the photographer met Aimee, Aimee was chosen instead.

She began modeling for Randall, and soon afterwards was chosen for a centerfold spread in Penthouse magazine. Much to her surprise, she became Penthouse Pet for August 1998, which lead to appearances in other men's magazines, including Perfect 10, Club, High Society, and Leg World. She was also considered as one of the candidates for Penthouse Pet of the Millennium.

In 1999, Sweet moved to Milan, Italy, as a high-fashion model for the famous Elite modeling agency. Upon returning to the U.S. several months later, she started her own business and launched her own website, Aimee Sweet - Official Site of Redhead Centerfold Model.

While running her own website, she also modeled for companies such as Ruplemintze, Trashy Lingerie, Mystique-Magazine, and Mac & Bumble.

She has branched out into the field of television and entertainment, including a stint as a hostess of a gameshow on Fox Sports. She has appeared in music videos for KoЯn and Marilyn Manson. For films, she apparently is included in the independent film Anytown, USA.

Having recently moved from San Diego, California, she currently resides in Los Angeles, California, USA.

She has also worked in Canada, Italy, Mexico, and London, England.
