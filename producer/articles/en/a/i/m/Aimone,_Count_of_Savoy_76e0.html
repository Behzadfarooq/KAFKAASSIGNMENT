Aimone (1291-1343), surnamed the Peaceful, was Count of Savoy from 1329 to 1343. He was the younger brother of Edward, Count of Savoy

He married to Yolande Palaeologina of Montferrato and had 2 children

1. Amadeus VI (Amadeo VI) (1334-1383)
2. Blanche (Bianca) (1336-1388), married in 1350 to Galenzo II di Viscounti, Lord of Milan.
