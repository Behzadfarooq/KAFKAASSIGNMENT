Introduction

AIMS Organization has been established in Pakistan (1999) with a view to disseminate education. Not education but quality education at low cost. We have focus on this belt of the southern Punjab, which is less developed as compare to the remaining Punjab and immensely all the facilities of other developed cities of the country.

Aims and objectives:

  • To provide students with a thorough preparation to face the modern world

  • To develop maximum potential for every child, as they are encouraged to take pride in personal achievement through hard work.

  • To achieve the academic success as a primary importance

  • To develop social, sporting and artistic skills through both curricular and extra-curricular activities

  • To cater wide range of abilities and talents of all the students by providing them opportunities to develop their potential, enabling them to make a positive contribution to society.

  • To establish great deal of emphasis on staff training and development and on maintaining a thoroughly up-to- date study programs for all students.

Study Levels:

  1. Pre O-level,  "O" and "A" Level Cambridge University-UK (PK410)

  2. Accounting, CA-Pak, ACCA-UK, CAT-UK

Environment & Computer Labs:
Fully Air-Conditioned and carpeted all class rooms and computer labs on one 2 one bases. Friendly atmosphere amongst staff & Students.

Books For Loan:
Library books ranging from 2000+ with support of Audio, Video and Internet material with lending facilities for students

Facilities:
Computer Lab / Internet access free for every student. The AIMS Study Abroad Services ASAS will offer you comprehensive guidance in relation to getting admission at Overseas Universities, Guidance, Careers services and Counselling.

Faculty:
over 30 qualified and fully trained staff members in our permanent & visiting faculty.

Scholarships & Financial Assistance:
Study scholarship for three top students, and also for sons/daughters of Government servants. Financial Assistance for disable & Hafiz-e-Quraan students. Get7A's in O level and gain Free A level study, 6 A's 75% fee Discount, 5 A's 50% fee discount, any A's Rs. 500 fee discount.

Awards:
Gold & Silver medal awards to the students who secure the top position through out the course & final examination and 90% attendance is our policy.
