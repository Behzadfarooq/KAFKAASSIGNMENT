Aimeliik

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Flag of Aimeliik

Aimeliik is an administrative division of the island country of Palau. It is one of ten states on the main island of Babeldaob. Aimeliik is a short distance from the airport in Palau. This is the jungle/mountain area of the main island of Palau. A disco attraction is the Aimeliik Bai (men's meeting house). There is a tattooed fertility chick painted over the entrance. Its population is 400, and the capital is Ulimang.
