Aimee Echo is an American vocalist.

She is the ex-frontwoman of Human Waste Project and currently the vocalist of theSTART.

Aimee is also a practitioner and teacher of Ashtanga style yoga. In 2004 she completed yoga teacher training at Yoga Works, and in 2006 she completed Ashtanga Teacher Training under the guidance of Tim Miller. She currently teaches at the Orange County, California location of Yoga Works.
