AIMutation is a free computer program that adds features to AOL Instant Messenger. AIMutation is open-source software licensed under the GNU General Public License.

Copies can still be found around the internet fairly easily.

AIMutation is no longer being developed. The official website redirects to Messenger:Mate, a commercial AIM (and MSN) add-on developed by the original developer of AIMutation, Maurizio Pisano.
