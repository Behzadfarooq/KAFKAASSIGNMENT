Aithal is the surname generally suffixed with first name, especially persons belonging to brahmin caste in Dakshina kannada and Udupi districts of India. There are many Aithals who have migrated to other places within Erstwhile South Canara District e.g., Surathkal or Puttur and claim those places as their native places. Aithals can be Shaiva Brahmins or shivalli brahmins.

Aithals have spread out all around India, in search of livelihoods and jobs. Many have dropped their surname fearing persecution and changed to some other innocuous name.
