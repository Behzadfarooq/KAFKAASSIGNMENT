Aitor López Rekarte aka López Rekarte (born 18 August 1975 in Mondragón, Basque Country) is Spanish football player who currently plays for Real Sociedad of the Spanish La Liga. His usual demarcation is Defender. He now is the captain of Real Sociedad football team after Agustín Aranzábal's exit.
