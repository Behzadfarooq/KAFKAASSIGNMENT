"Ai~n Taisou" (アイ～ン体操, "Ai~n Taisou"?) is the fifth single of the subgroup Minimoni. It was released on April 24, 2002 and sold 212.230 copies.

[edit] Track listing

The lyricist and composer of the songs is Tsunku.

  1. "Ai~n Taisou" (アイ～ン体操, "Ai~n Taisou"?)
  2. "Ai~n! Dance no Uta" (アイ～ン！ダンスの唄, "Ai~n! Dance no Uta"?)
  3. "Ai~n Taisou (Instrumental)" (アイ～ン体操 (Instrumental), "Ai~n Taisou (Instrumental)"?)
  4. "Ai~n! Dance no Uta (Instrumental)" (アイ～ン！ダンスの唄 (Instrumental), "Ai~n! Dance no Uta (Instrumental)"?)
