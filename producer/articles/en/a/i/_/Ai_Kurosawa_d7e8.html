[edit] Life and Career

Born in Shiga Prefecture, Ai Kurosawa was very active in volleyball during high school.[2] Her current hobbies include collecting Sanrio's Hello Kitty goods.[3] Her favorite colors include red and pink.

She made her professional debut in 2001 and has starred in over 40 films since then. As of October, 2006, Japanese Amazon shows that she has appeared in 125 DVDs, over 50 VHS tapes, and 21 photo books.[4] Her popularity led to her winning the "Main AV Actress Award" at Takeshi Kitano's second Entertainment Awards ceremony.[5]. She retired in January 2006, and continues working as stripper.[6]
