Ai no Imi wo Oshiete! (愛の意味を教えて！, Ai no Imi wo Oshiete!?) is the 5th single of the Hello! Project group W (Double You), released on May 18, 2005. The song has a Devo-inspired synthesized pop beat, and is one of W's original, non-LP releases. The English translation of the title is "Teach Me the Meaning of Love."
