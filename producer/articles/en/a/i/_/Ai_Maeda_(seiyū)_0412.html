Ai Maeda (seiyū)

Ai Maeda (前田 愛 Maeda Ai) (April 19, 1975) is a seiyū born in Kobe, Japan. She is also a singer under the name AiM and a songwriter under the name ai. She sung most of the Endings and Movie Themes for the Digimon series, as well as several Image Themes and other Digimon related songs.

She is not to be confused with the actress of the same name.

[edit] Notable anime and video game voice roles

  • Mimi Tachikawa in Digimon Adventure
  • Shion Uzuki in Xenosaga
  • Oichi and Samurai Woman in Samurai Warriors
  • Megumi Oumi in Konjiki no Gash Bell!! (Zatch Bell!)
  • Emiru Nagakura in Sentimental Graffiti
  • Range in the Spectral Force 2 ~Eien naru Kiseki~ OVA
  • Haruhi Nishimoto in Tokimeki Memorial Girl's Side 2nd Kiss
  • Karen Minazuki/Cure Aqua in Yes! PreCure 5
