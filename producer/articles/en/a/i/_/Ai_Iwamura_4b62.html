Ai Iwamura

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Ai Iwamura as Mai in the film Battle Royale

Ai Iwamura (岩村愛, Iwamura Ai?, born March 4, 1984) is a Japanese actress.

Iwamura is best known for her role as Mai in Battle Royale. Iwamura also fulfills some "stand-in" roles as Izumi Kanai, Noriko Nakagawa, Hirono Shimizu, and Haruka Tanizawa [1]. Iwamura also stars as Mai in Battle Royale II: Requiem. She also voiced MarineAngemon in the anime Digimon Tamers.
