Ai Kato (加藤あい Katō Ai; born December 12, 1982 in Nagoya, Aichi Prefecture) is a Japanese actress and model. Around 2003, a video of her nude in an onsen (hot spring) surfaced on the internet. It was taken with a hidden camera and is believed to have been a botched extortion attempt by the Yakuza. [1]
