Ai (アイ, Ai?) is a fictional character from SNK Playmore's fighting game Neo Geo Battle Coliseum, being one of the 3 new and inedit characters of the game (along with Yuki and Goodman).

[edit] History

"Your mission is to enter this 'Battle Coliseum' with Yuki and expose the identity of the WAREZ exclusive sponsoring the event". That was the order given to Ai in her position as an agent of the Federal Government.

A casual glance of Ai left one with the impression of a delicate female, but her combat strength was nothing to be underestimated when she puts her modified Neo Geo Pocket to use. Although Ai was known to neglect her duties in preference of playing the lastest game, she insists she's the one who "keeps tabs on Yuki whenever he flies off the handle".

Now all she's concerned with is bringing this mission to a quick close, but the real reason for this is because "the latest game is coming out in a few days..." [1]

[edit] Tribute moves

Ai, like her partner Yuki, has based all her moves in old SNK Playmore's Neo-Geo games, like in direct references to various icons of this company. These are some of her moves and their connection with the SNK world:

  • Robo Arm: The name says everything. Based in Robo Army beat'em up.
  • Baseball Stars, Baseball Stars Professional and Baseball Stars 2: Attacks when Ai hits the opponent with a bat (as can reflect projectiles). Obviously based in the sports games of those names.
  • 2020 Super Catch: A robotic baseball glove who catch projectiles. Reference to the 2020 Super Baseball futuristic baseball game.
  • Last Resort: Appears a ball that shoots the oppnent. References to the shooter game of the same name, where this ball also appears.
  • ASO II - Last Guardian: D-assault attack with Yuki, a hyper who shocks in all the screen. References to the Alpha Mission series, which attack is the Japanese title of the second game.

[edit] Trivia

  • Her name Ai (愛, Ai?) means Love in Japanese.
  • Ai got Neo Poke-Kun (Neo Geo Pocket mascot) as her own mascot and he appears in various of her moves and attacks.
  • Ai's outfit resembles baseball players.
  • In her intro, Ai enters in a robotic car who dissapears in a seconds. That car appears in the Robo Army beat'em up, where the main protagonists collect all the power gems in that game.
  • Ai has the most notable homage to video game history in her attacks Joy Joy Block and Joy Joy Party, which Ai with her Neo Geo Pocket makes to appear Tetris pieces from sky to hit the opponent.
  • Ai appears also in SNK vs. Capcom: Card Fighters DS in 2 cards, as a SNK special character card and in an Action card, along with Bao.
