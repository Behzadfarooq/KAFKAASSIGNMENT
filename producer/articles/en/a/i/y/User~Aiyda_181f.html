[edit] About Me

  • Name: Alys
  • Age: 14
  • Location: Surrey, England

I'm relativity new to Wikipedia, and thought that I really should get round to making a user page. I've been making a small number of little changes now and then including some spelling and grammar edits; which is ironic because I can never see mistakes in my own writing. I may in the near future contribute my first article, but I think I need to learn the ropes first. I've taken recently to patrolling the Recent Changes page and reverting any vandalism I find there using Twinkle.

I am in year 10 at a girls' grammar school (whose students keep vandalising wikipedia) doing my GCSEs. I am taking my maths GCSE and a couple of science modules this May.
