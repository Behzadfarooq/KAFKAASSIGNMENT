Aifric is an Irish-language TV series aimed at young teenagers, directed by Paul Mercier.

The first of the weekly thirteen-part series began broadcasting on 31st October 2006.

The programme charts the progress of fourteen-year-old Aifric (played by newcomer Clíona Ní Chíosain, along with her somewhat eccentric family, as she tries to settle into her new life in Connemara, having recently moved from Dublin.

[edit] Characters

  • Aifric - the protagonist
  • Sophie - Aifric's best friend
  • Claudia - the rich, arrogant, pretentious fellow classmate
  • Traolach - Aifric's younger brother, has a keen interest in Eastern philosophy
  • Mam - Aifric and Traolach's mother
  • Daid - Aifric and Traolach's father, accountant by profession, but rock musician by aspiration

Trivia

  • Over 95% of production budget was spent in the Galway region
  • The production employed over 100 crew and a cast of almost 150 actors
  • In August 2004, there was a national talent search which auditioned over 1000 hopeful teenagers for the many young roles in the show, including that of Aifric.
