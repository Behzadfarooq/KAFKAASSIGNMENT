Aigo is a consumer technology brand manufactured by the Chinese company Beijing Huaqi Information Digital Technology Co., Ltd. It is the most popular MP3 player brand in China.[citation needed]

[edit] Company information

Beijing Huaqi Information Digital Technology Co., Ltd. was established in 1993. The company is a technology company headquartered at the heart of the Zhong Guan Cun in Beijing that is involved in the research and development, support, and service of PC software and hardware products. The company's products include portable storage solutions, monitors, digital products, and computer cases. They are sold abroad in North America, Europe, Southeast Asia and other regions. Aigo portable storage products have the largest market share and have dominated the Chinese market for the past four years. Other products with the highest market share include Aigo monitors, peripherals, and PC cases.[citation needed] Huaqi has over 1400 employees, including over 200 R&D staff. Furthermore, the company has an incorporated national marketing network system, and has developed into one of the primary enterprises in the Chinese information technology industry.[citation needed]
