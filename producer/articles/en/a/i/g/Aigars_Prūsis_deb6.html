Aigars Prūsis

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Aigars Prūsis (b. January 5, 1976, in Liepāja) was the leader of Latvia's National Power Union, a far-right nationalist political party, in 2003—2006.

He runs latvians.lv [1] - an Internet portal for far-right Latvian nationalist groups.
