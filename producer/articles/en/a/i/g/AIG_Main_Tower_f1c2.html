AIG Main Tower

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

This article or section contains information about expected future buildings or structures.
It is likely to contain information of a speculative nature, and the content may change as building construction begins and new information becomes available.

The AIG Main Tower is an approved supertall skyscraper set for construction in Seoul, South Korea. This tower will be 300 meters tall and have 80 floors. It will be the first tower of the Seoul International Finance Center or Youido AIG's SIFC. A second 50 floor tower, AIG Tower 2, is approved also.
