Aigars Cipruss

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Aigars Cipruss (born January 12, 1972 in Riga, U.S.S.R.) is a professional ice hockey player.

[edit] Playing career

A veteran centre, Cipruss plays in the Russian Hockey Super League, regularly representing Latvia internationally. Although he has played in the North American leagues, he has never been drafted by an NHL team.
