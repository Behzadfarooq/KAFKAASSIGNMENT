Aiguilles Island

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Aiguilles Island is an uninhabited island just to the northeast of Great Barrier Island in the Northland Region of New Zealand. The island reaches a height of 120 metres and is 47km from the New Zealand mainland.
