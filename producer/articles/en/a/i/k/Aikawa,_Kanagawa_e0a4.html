Aikawa, Kanagawa

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Aikawa (愛川町; -machi) is a town located in Aikō District, Kanagawa, Japan.

As of 2003, the town has an estimated population of 42,847 and a density of 1,249.55 persons per km². The total area is 34.29 km².
