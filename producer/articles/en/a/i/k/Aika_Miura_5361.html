Aika Miura (三浦あいか) (born Yuu Hasegawa (長谷川優) November 2, 1975 in Kanagawa Prefecture, Japan) is a Japanese pornographic actress.

[edit] Life and career

Yuu Hasegawa was born to a conservative family who did not approve of her AV career, but remained close to her throughout her career. When her father once scolded her for making money from pleasure, Miura pointed out that some of her performances involved bondage and S&M, and observed, "It's not as fun as you think!" [2]

Making her AV debut in March 1995 as Aika Miura in Suki Desu (好きです - Suki Desu), she appeared regularly in AVs until taking an eight-month retirement between January and November of 1998. She pursued a career as a stripper simultaneously with her AV activities. In a remarkably long and prolific career for an AV actress, she released about one film a month over a seven year period (excepting 1998), before she finally retired in 2002. [3] In 2001, six years after her debut, she starred in the theatrically-released, widescreen film Aika Miura - Streetcar Ecstasy (三浦あいか　痴漢電車エクスタシー) [4]
