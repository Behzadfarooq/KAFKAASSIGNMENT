Aika Mitsui (光井愛佳, Mitsui Aika?), born on January 12, 1993 in Ōtsu City[1], Shiga Prefecture, Japan, is the newest member of J-pop group Morning Musume, joining as a member of the eighth generation from the audition in Japan during the December of 2006. Mitsui's audition process was documented during the late 2006 episodes of Hello! Morning.

[edit] Biography

On December 10th, 2006, she alone out of five (originally six, but Ayami Masuda dropped out) finalists was chosen to join as the only eighth generation member of Morning Musume.

An article published on December 11 revealed Mitsui wouldn't be present in the 57th edition of Kōhaku Uta Gassen but would instead be featured in the band's 32nd single (Egao YES Nude), which was released on Valentine's Day 2007 [2], following her first public appearance on the last round of the Hello! Project Winter 2007 Tours, ~Shūgetsu 10th Anniversary~ (～集結! 10th Anniversary～, ~Shūgetsu 10th Anniversary~?), which took place between January 27 and January 28 at Saitama Super Arena. She will also be taking part in Morning Musume's 2007 Spring Tour ~Sexy 8 Beat~.

One week after the results of Happy 8ki Audition were announced, Mitsui was featured in a small segment of Hello! Morning which included footage from a press conference given on the eleventh. During the conference Mitsui revealed her goal of captivating the public by means of her cheerful personality and smile, emulating fellow member Koharu Kusumi, whom she admires and strives to equal or surpass.

Following her concert debut, Mitsui was also featured in a small segment of the Ohastar morning programme entitled I want to express my gratitude ("ありがとうを伝えたい", Arigatō o tsutaetai?), which purports to show the viewers a fictionalized account of the events following her first concert appearances, dealing with Mitsui's sadness over the fact she had to part with her former classmates due to her becoming an idol.

Her first appearance at a non-Hello! Project related event took place on February 8, 2007, on Utaban, as part of Morning Musume.
