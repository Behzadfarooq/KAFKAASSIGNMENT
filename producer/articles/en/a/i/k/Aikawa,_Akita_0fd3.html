Aikawa, Akita

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Aikawa (合川町; -machi) was a town located in Kitaakita District, Akita, Japan.

The city of Kita-Akita was founded on March 22, 2005 from the merger of the former towns of Aikawa, Ani, Moriyoshi, and Takanosu, all from Kita-Akita District.

In 2003, the town had an estimated population of 7,673 and a density of 68.02 persons per km². The total area was 112.80 km².
