Aiko Kayo

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Aiko Kayo (嘉陽愛子, Kayō Aiko?, born December 11, 1985) is a popular idol singer on the avex record label in Japan. She was born and raised in Kanagawa Prefecture.
