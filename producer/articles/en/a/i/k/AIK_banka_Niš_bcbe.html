AIK banka Niš (full name: Agroindustrijsko komercijalna banka a.d. Niš) (BELEX: AIKB) is a bank based in Niš, Serbia.

It was founded in 1976 as the internal bank of the Agro-industrial Combine Niš. AIK Bank officially began operating as a bank after it obtain a license of the National Bank of Yugoslavia (NBJ). AIK Bank was registered as a joint-stock company at 1995. The Bank built its business reputation of a strong dealer-broker company.

[edit] Acquisition

On 11.09.2006. ATEbank based in Greece announce, it has acquired 24.99 pct of common and 24.99 pct of preferential shares of Serbia's Aik Bank, Nis for an undisclosed amount. The transaction need approvals from the central banks of Greece and Serbia, which is expected by the end of this month. At the Belgrade Stock Exchange shares of AIK Bank 14.09.2006 gained historical maximum price of 2.750.
