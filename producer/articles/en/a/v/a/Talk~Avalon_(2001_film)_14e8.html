Story I have a few problems.. "In a distant future" I don't think that this is necessarily the case. To me this film evoked that fantastical industrialism of final fantasy seven. The battle's and so on, the roles of each player/actor. Maybe "alternate universe", or something similar, would be more mysterious than "distant future". Then the next sentance begins "In a kind of.." which sounds repetitive and well.. tired. It really is a story peppered with contradictions and therefore quite difficult to eloquently re-tell. I have trouble putting into words what that movie was about.. but I know it was damn cool. "Especially in higher levels the spirit of the player may stay inside the game, and the body stays vegetating in hospitals in the real world." I think more points like these would be good. Is it ok for me to edit the article a little bit?--Tapsell 03:01, 21 July 2006 (UTC)

You don't need permission as "Be Bold" is one of the credos around here. I'd advise to steer clear of speculation, however as that's a no-no. If the film doesn't specify an alternate universe then we shouldn't say so; similarly the film never really specifies if it takes place in the future, either. Describe what is seen on screen and you'll do fine. Of course if you can provide a cited article or other source in which the writer/director makes comments about what it all means (especially the little girl), please add that as well. 23skidoo 03:41, 21 July 2006 (UTC)
