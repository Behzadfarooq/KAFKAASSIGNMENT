[edit] A little bit about me

Hi! I am a 16 year old kid and live in Massachusetts. Video games, as well as conventional tabletop games such as dungeons and dragons and Shadow run are personal passions of mine. I love Sci-Fi and Fantasy books, and can often be found hiding in my room (which is a complete mess) reading them when not slaving away to both parents and school :-) I consider myself very computer proficient and enjoy programming. I enjoy Sad/tragic music Ranging widely and wildly throughout almost all genre's of music (Barring country, gospel, and most rap.) I am obsessed with Magic The Gathering as well, and often wish to play Warhammer 40,000 although I have not played a tabletop game to date.I am Considered by many of my politically inclined friends that I am a liberal, as I am exceedingly open minded. I am now registered on 4 different wiki's and regularly visit and edit all of them :-) I Have voted Weird Al's "White and Nerdy" and Simon and Garfunkel's "I am a Rock" to be my two joint theme songs. I am obsessed with spellcasting characters, primarily arcane in nature, and have a large number of them made, spanning all level ranges. yes, I have THAT much time on my hands.
