My name is Ava :-) I'm a regular user of wikipedia, casual contributor (you know spelling errors and the like). I'm not really fond of giving lots of details about myself online, although I do run an internet based design business (and I'm not plugging by the way :-) just fyi) that I do with a friend of mine also converse with such stuff ^_^. If you want to look at that website of mine in the mean time it's at www.rayzor-sharp.co.uk.

In other news it must be said I do spend an awful lot of time on wikipedia surfing through articles. Of particular interest to me are Chernobyl, Nuclear Physics, Trance Music and Entropia Universe amongst other things.

I also enjoy poking around on the edit histories of pages targetted by vandalism, and take a bit of interest in counter-vandalism activities by the wikipedia regulars. It's like a warfield out there!
