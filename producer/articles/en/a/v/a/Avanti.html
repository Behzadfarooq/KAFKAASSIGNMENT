Avanti

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Avanti (Italian for ahead or forward) may refer to:

  • The Avanti Kart. A racing go-kart manufactured in Italy by JM Racing. Winner of many races and championships in the United States and Central America.
  • The Avanti was a model of car built by the Studebaker corporation of South Bend, Indiana. It is now built by the Avanti Motor Corporation
  • Avanti Ciné Vidéo, a Quebec-based television production company
  • an Austrian company (http://www.avanti.at) which operates filling stations in Austria and Eastern European countries that are marked with a prancing horse logo which is nearly identical to Ferrari's
  • Avanti Corporation, an electronic design automation company, acquired by Synopsys in 2002.
  • Avanti!, a 1972 film.
  • Piaggio P-180 Avanti is a canard pusher business aircraft with two turboprop engines.
  • Avanti! (Italian newspaper), the official newspaper of the Italian Socialist Party.
  • Avanti (India), a historic kingdom with its capital at Ujjain, and one of the Mahajanapadas.
  • Avanti! (orchestra)[1], a symphony orchestra based in Helsinki, Finland.
  • Durex Avanti non-latex polyurethane condoms.
  • Avanti Screenmedia, a satellite operator.
  • Avanti Products[2], a consumer appliance manufacturer.
  • Avanti Polar Lipids, Inc.[3], a biotech company producing lipids for research and pharmaceutical use.
