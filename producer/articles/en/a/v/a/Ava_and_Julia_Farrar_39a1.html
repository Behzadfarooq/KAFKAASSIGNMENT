Ava and Julia Farrar

Ava and Julia Farrar are child actresses. They have shared the role of Spike Lavery on the US soap opera All My Children since August 2, 2006 through August and September, they shared the role with other babies. They took over the entirely role of Spike Lavery on October 5, 2006 to present.
