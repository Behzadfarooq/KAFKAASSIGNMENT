Advanced Dungeons & Dragons 1st Edition (usually abriviated as: AD&D or AD&D 1e was an version of the Dungeons & Dragons Role Playing Game, that was published by TSR, Inc. from 1977 to 1989. The game was a large revision, and update of the previous boxed games, that were independetly published between 1974 and 1977.
