Avatar is a death metal band from Sweden, founded in 2001. Its first EP 4 Reasons to Die was released in 2004, and its first album Thoughts of No Tomorrow in 2006.

[edit] Discography

  • Personal Observations (demo, 2003)
  • 4 Reasons to Die (EP, 2004)
  • My Shining Star (single, 2005)
  • And I Bid You Farewell (single, 2005)
  • Thoughts of No Tomorrow (2006)
