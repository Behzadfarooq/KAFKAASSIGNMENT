O2: Avalon Remixed is Avalon's sixth release, the group's first remix album. Originally scheduled to be released concurrently with Oxygen, it was eventually released on March 29, 2002. The album has 12 remixes of previous tracks, including one done by DJ Tiesto. "The Best Thing (Euro Stack Mix)" was a replacement for a planned 3-track medley.[citation needed]
