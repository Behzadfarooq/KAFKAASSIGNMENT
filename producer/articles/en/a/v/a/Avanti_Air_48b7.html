Avanti Air

Avanti Air is an airline based at the southern part of county Siegen-Wittgenstein, Germany. It operates ad hoc charter services, scheduled services on behalf of other airlines and fleet management. Its main base is Siegerland Airport (SGE).

[edit] Code Data

  • ICAO Code: EEX
  • Callsign: EuroExpress [1]

[edit] History

The airline was established in 1994 and started operations on 1 July 1994. It was founded by former pilots Markus Baumann (now Managing Director) and Stefan Kissinger (now Chief Executive) as an aircraft management company. It entered the charter market in 1996 with a Beech 1900C aircraft. The company is owned by Gerhard Mahler (50%), Markus Baumann (25%) and Stefan Kissinger (25%).

[edit] Services

Avanti Air, on behalf of Meridiana operate services between Sicily and Lampedusa and Pantelleria. The contract has been extended to March 2007.

[edit] Fleet

The Avanti Air fleet includes the following aircraft (at August 2006):

  • 1 ATR 72-200
  • 2 ATR 42-300
  • 1 Raytheon Beech 1900C Airliner
  • 2 Raytheon Beech 1900D Airliner [2]


As of January 2005:

  • 2 Raytheon Beech King Air B200

The airline is reportedly planning to add an ATR 72 aircraft.
