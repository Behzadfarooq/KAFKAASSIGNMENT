[edit] History

Avanade, Inc. is a joint venture between Accenture and Microsoft which was founded in April 4, 2000. It refers to itself as "the leading technology integrator specialized in providing information technology services on the Microsoft enterprise platform." It is a privately held firm with close relationships with the companies that founded it. It is unclear the level of day to day control the founders have in the companies policies and activities. It is a global firm with around 38 offices in approximately 20 countries.

[edit] Services

Avanade sells services accross a range of IT and business areas, including:

  • Application Portfolio Transformation
  • Application Management
  • Application Renewal
  • Application Development
  • Mobile Applications
  • Enterprise Integration
  • Business Intelligence and Performance Management
  • Customer Relationship Management
  • Enterprise Resource Planning
  • Enterprise Collaboration
  • Infrastructure
  • IT controls, IT audit, and SARBOX data management.
  • Platform Migration
  • Software Engineering
  • Service-oriented architecture
  • Microsoft for SAP as IT platform
  • Integration Microsoft Office and SAP
