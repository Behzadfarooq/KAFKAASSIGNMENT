[edit] Current

Erik Dean Drzazinski. (Born November 20th, 1991), is best known as the lead vocals/guitarist in the up and coming Punk Band Copyright Infringement#Band.

[edit] Biography

[edit] Early Life

Erik was born in Corona, California and resided there for 2 years until his family decided to move somewhere less active Burbank, Illinois. Erik has 2 other brothers, Dave (16), and Eddie (23). He went through grade school at Burbank School.

[edit] Musical Scene

As Erik entered his teen years the outlook of his life changed, being a standard kid before he now became out-going and "Popular." Erik started to play guitar at the age 13 in the 7th grade. He continues to play and "hone" his skills with friends Ryan Seper and Mike Gotsch.

[edit] Copyright Infringement

Erik plays a Squier Bullet that he recieved from his brother as a Christmas gift. Being envious of his brothers ability to play guitar he started playing. He was intending on playing bass, but decided not to when his brother Dave constantly teased him on how when a string snapped it would leave a welt on his arm. Having the dream inside to start a band, he requested that his best friend "Ryan Seper" learn to play the Bass. The band had went through several drummer changes from Seth M. and Mike Gotsch, Mike is the drummer as of date. The name Copyright Infringement didn't come with ease. They argued back and worth on what the name should be, some of the intended names include, "Saving Summer, Skeptic, and Poker Playing Dogs." One day in class they were still thinking of a name when Ryan said jokingly "We should just call ourselves Blink 182." Erik then replied "I'm pretty sure that's Copyright Infringement." Thus the name was born.

[edit] Instruments

As of the current date Erik plays on his Standard Squier Bullet.(Electron Blue) He has noted several times that he wants to explore a new guitar because he feels he has outgrown his current one. He looks to start playing a Fender Stratocaster or a Epiphone Les Paul.
