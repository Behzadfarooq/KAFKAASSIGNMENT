Just a quick note, I am Michael Stanton (formerly referred to on this page as Mike Stainton). On the first two Avant Garde demos, Eric Ridel (Kevin Ridel's brother) was the 2nd lead guitar player, and I came in for the 3rd demo tape.

I thought "Later of Microsoft (band)" was funny. No, I left the music world and many years later got a job at Microsoft the company. I was puzzled when I first saw that, but I guess it is because everybody else had a "later of X" after their name, so for symmetry, the same was done for me.
