Avari Towers

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Avari Towers hotel is situated on Shahrah-Fatima Jinnah, in Karachi, Pakistan. Avari Towers hotel is one of the major hotel in Pakistan. It was founded by Dinshaw Avari and is now run by his son Byram Dinshawji Avari, the CEO of Avari Hotels Limited. The tallest hotel in Pakistan is located in downtown Karachi, Just 20 minutes from the Airport and 7 minutes from the Karachi Port.
