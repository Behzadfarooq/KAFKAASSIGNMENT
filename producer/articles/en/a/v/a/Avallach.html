Avallach

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

In Welsh mythology, Avalloc was the father of Modron. He may have been the King of Avalon.

[edit] Etymology

This theonym appears to be derived from Proto-Celtic *aballo- meaning "apple".[1]
