It begins with Eddy's hands holding up the sign saying 25$ A RIDE. Edd is sitting inside the circle of the tire while Ed is playing with the tire. Eddy is unhappy and argues. Ed pushes the tire, shocking Edd. Eddy turns happy and says "I pay a quarter for that!". Edd is shocked and scared. The cul-de-sac kids see Edd in the tire. The tire falls into the water. Rolf, Nazz, Jonny and Plank get happy and excited about the tire in the water. Ed, Eddy, Rolf, Nazz, Jonny, Plank, Jimmy, Kevin and Sarah watch Double D in the tire. Eddy dicusses to the kids, Eddy points his thumb to the tire. Ed, Eddy see Double D. The kids except Eddy and Ed are not happy. Jonny and Jimmy get happy. The kids shout a excited cheer. Eddy tells Ed a secret about the new scam. Ed shows two fingers on his left hand. Ed in the entrance in the Creek Cruise throws the paper turners and throws tickets at Rolf. Jimmy, Jonny and Plank are chosen as passengers with Captain Eddy waiting in the tire boat. The entrance to the tire boat falls into the water and Jimmy and Jonny get shocked and Captain Eddy and Edd save Jimmy and Jonny and Plank. Eddy gives Jimmy and Jonny Eddy's Creek Cruise towel. Jonny explains to Eddy. Sailor Ed comes out from the water with things for Jimmy and Jonny and Plank to use to have fun. Edd sings a bottle song, succesful. Eddy gives an argument. Jonny and Jimmy get excited. Sailor Ed helps the tire boat go fast and stops it to keep the speed of boats or ships. Edd puts water in the coffee cups. Jimmy and Jonny drink water. Everybody on the tire boat are excited. In the Trailer Park, the Kankers are working, their own work. May is about to eat the look-alike hot dog, later interfered by everybody on the tire boat getting past the Kankers. The Kankers caught the Eds, the Kankers get happy. Ed checks up on top of the tire boat with a red telescope while Eddy is putting his voice at his speech, impressing Jimmy and Jonny and Plank. Jimmy and Jonny clap their hands. Edd doesn't look happy. Edd performs music with his hands doing the music and then reading a red book with his legs doing the music. Jonny splashes some water. Jimmy tells Eddy about something. Jonny, unhappy points at something in the Creek. Ed uses the telescope (he's seeing it in reverse of the telescope) to see what's in the creek, Ed says there's a boat in the creek. Eddy bears a telescope. Ed throws Eddy a telescope. Eddy uses the telescope, and sees that it's the Kankers and Eddy screams. Everyone in the ride are horrifed. Ed gets the boat faster to attempt to lose the tail of the Kankers. Lee still sees the tire boat. Jonny is happy while Jimmy shouts. The Kankers get close to the ride. Eddy orders Ed to get the boat faster and faster. Ed acts as a engine. The ride gets into the speed of powerboats. Everyone in the ride (except Jonny and Plank) get shocked and Jimmy gets scared. Jonny shows up his left arm. The Kankers get mad. A splash wets everyone in the ride (even Jonny and Plank). The Kankers show their lips to the Eds that they are going to kiss the Eds. [edit] Eds' Boats, the boats in AVAST YE EDS

[edit] Tire Boat

The Tire Boat is discovered by Ed, Edd and Eddy in modern-day. The Tire Boat is a tire in the water which allows the tire to float. However, the Kankers terrorized Eddy's Creek Cruise and the tire turned tiny and went underwater, and Ed, Edd, Eddy, Jonny, Plank and Jimmy too.

[edit] Kankers' Bed Ship

A bed transformed into a bed ship. May Kanker suceeds by turning the tire boat tiny, making everyone in the tire underwater.
