[edit] Print Publication

There was an attempt to publish Avalon in print format. The publisher was Maverick Camel, and the book was a collection of the strips from Avalon's first year. The resulting book was of very poor quality. The binding job was not a professional one, resulting in pages falling out easily. The print quality was inconsistent, with black-and-white pages printed by laser, and colour pages printed by ink-jet; the black ink on the cover and the colour pages faded to green quite rapidly (in fact, several customers reported the cover to have already faded when the book was received). The book was sold exclusively through the publisher, and sales were halted after complaints about the book's quality. Sales never resumed. No ISBN number was registered for the book. The only online references to the book that I can find are from Avalon's news postings and in the site's forum.

A few years later, Keenspot approached me about publishing an Avalon collection, but I never completed the contract (I didn't believe I could provide the content in due time). Nevertheless, an ISBN was registered for the book, and it was advertised on Amazon.com. The book was never printed.

This information could go into the article proper if it weren't for a lack of independent sources. Staedtler 05:12, 23 February 2007 (UTC)
