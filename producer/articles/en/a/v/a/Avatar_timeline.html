This Avatar timeline, recording fictional events from the television series Avatar: The Last Airbender, revolves around the birth of Avatar Aang, year 1.

288 Before Birth of Aang--Avatar Kuruk dies and Avatar Kyoshi of the Earth Kingdom is born (accepting the figure of four hundred years stated in The Warriors of Kyoshi as correct).

200s BBA--Kyoshi kills Chin the Great and creates Kyoshi Island.

58 BBA--Accepting the figure of two hundred and thirty years as Avatar Kyoshi's lifespan, Avatar Kyoshi dies and Avatar Roku of the Fire Nation is born.

1 After Birth of Aang--Avatar Roku dies and Avatar Aang is born, presumably at the Southern Air Temple.

12 ABA--Fire Lord Sozin takes the throne at a very young age. Aang flees from the nomads around this time. Sozin uses the power from his eponymous comet to wipe out the Air Nomads.

Around 60s or 70s ABA: General Iroh and Fire Lord Ozai are born around this time.

83 ABA--Sozin dies and his son Azulon takes the throne.

96 ABA--Presumed to be the date of birth of Prince Zuko.

97 ABA--Sokka is born.

98 ABA--Katara and Princess Azula are born.

100 ABA--Toph is born blind, as stated in The Blind Bandit.

104 or 105 ABA--Iroh begins his siege at Ba Sing Se, which lasts 600 days.

106 ABA--The flashback sequences of Zuko Alone occur. Iroh's son Lu Ten is killed in battle at Ba Sing Se. Iroh gives up his siege at the city despite breaching the outer wall. Azulon dies under mysterious circumstances, and Ozai takes the throne from his older brother Iroh, backed with the statement that Azulon wanted him to have it. Ozai's wife, Ursa, disappears mysteriously.

110 ABA--According to The Boy in the Iceberg, the men of the Southern Water Tribe leave to fight the Fire Nation. As revealed in The Storm, Zuko speaks out against the slaughter of the Fire Nation's 41st Division as bait to help a sneak attack. Ozai meets him in Agni Kai.Zuko refuses to fight his father, who scars his eye and gives him his "futile" quest to find the Avatar. Iroh acconpanies him.

112 ABA--The events of the show begin. Katara accidentally reawakens Aang. The Fire Nation discovers Aang is alive and Zuko, Iroh, and the crew chase him North.

113 ABA (Assuming the end of a calendar year shortly after the events of "Avatar Roku (Winter Solstice, Part 2)"). Admiral Zhao tries to kill Zuko and appears to succeed. Iroh joins Zhao's expedition to wipe out the Northern Water Tribe. The Fire Nation attacks with fire missiles and ground forces for 2 days (The Siege of the North), and Zhao briefly kills the moon spirit. Aang becomes one with the spirit of the ocean and wipes out the attack, including Zhao, who was defeated by Zuko.

Aang travels to Ba Sing Se along with Sokka, Katara, and Toph Bei Fong, to tell the Earth King of a solar eclipse that will leave the Fire Nation helpless. The Earth King agrees and invasion plans are made. Azula, Ty Lee, and Mai defeat the Kyoshi Warriors in battle, inpersonate them, and conquer Ba Sing Se from the inside. The Earth Kingdom falls to the Fire Nation.
