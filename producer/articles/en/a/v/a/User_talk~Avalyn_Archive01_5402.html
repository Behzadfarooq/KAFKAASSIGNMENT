[edit] Cannabis allergy

Hi, again. Your user page made a passing reference to your allergy, which I am only familiar with anecdotally. If you have more information on the subject, would you be interested in adding data to cannabis (drug), or recommend where I can find more information? Thanks. —Viriditas | Talk 01:47, 23 April 2006 (UTC)

I'd be happy to impart some knowledge of how cannabis allergy works, but I really don't have any idea. When I've had allergic reactions to it, the effects were the same, but they could be totally different with other people, and I've never heard from anyone else who has admitted to being allergic, so I can't compare reaction effects. The best I could do would be to add anecdotal reports from my own experiences, which is obviously a no-go. I'll see what I can find, though. Avalyn 08:58, 23 April 2006 (UTC)
Thanks, it's a very interesting subject. Out of curiousity, what would happen if you smoked/ingested it? Do you get a reaction from smelling or touching it? Another question: are you allergic to any other plants, such as sage? —Viriditas | Talk 09:28, 23 April 2006 (UTC)
Well, I would react negatively to it only if I had a lot, like a whole bowl (I never bothered with joints; just bongs). My heart rate would increase to 160-190 BPM, I'd lose feeling in my arms and legs, and I'd have a lot of trouble breathing. It landed me in the emergency room once, and they did tests (which ones I don't recall because I'd been sedated), but they gave the results to my parents and I never saw them. Second-hand pot smoke doesn't really affect me unless there's lot of it in a small crowded room, in which case the aforementioned symptoms start up again, but subside a while after I get away from the smoke. I'm not sure about touching it as I haven't even seen any (any that hasn't been prepared for smoking, anyway; i.e., not "raw") in over 10 years. The only other plants I'm allergic to are plants that pollenate, and even then it's just the pollen allergens that affect me. Avalyn 10:17, 23 April 2006 (UTC)
Ok, this is starting to make sense, now. It sounds to me like a cannabis sensitivity, although I'm wondering if you had come across something else altogether. There was a story in the January 27, 2006 issue of The Province about pot-laced crystal meth, and I've heard other stories about pot-laced PCP; I'm certain the former is true, but the latter seems to be either rare or the stuff of urban legends. I'm glad you're ok, though. Thanks for sharing that info. —Viriditas | Talk 11:42, 23 April 2006 (UTC)
I was thinking the same thing. :) Pot laced with crystal meth does this in most people. I have also heard of pot laced with PCP, I know someone who has supposedly tried it.SZadeh 03:59, 28 April 2006 (UTC)
