Avalanche is an arcade game of skill released by Atari in 1978. You control a multi-storied platform with a dial. Your goal is to prevent the falling rocks from ever reaching the ground. There are six rows of rocks to deal with. You start with a six-storied platform and you lose one platform per row of rocks cleared. You score points for those rocks you prevent from reaching the ground. The farther the row of rocks, the smaller and faster they become. Your ultimate goal is to get enough points so that you can continue your game should you lose your first one.

[edit] Trivia

Due to the immense popularity of Space Invaders at the arcades in 1978, Avalanche did not do well.
