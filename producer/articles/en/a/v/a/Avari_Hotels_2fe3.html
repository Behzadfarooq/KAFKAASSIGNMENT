Avari Hotels

Avari Hotels is one of the major chains of hotels in Pakistan. It was founded by Dinshaw Avari and is now run by his son Byram Dinshawji Avari, the CEO of Avari Hotels Ltd, and his two sons Dinshaw and Xerxes. Avari Hotels manages hotels in Pakistan, Dubai and Toronto, Canada.

Portraits of Dinshaw Avari and his wife Khorshed hang in the entrance lobby of the Avari Towers Hotel in Karachi, where the two largest function rooms are named after them - Dinshaw Mahal and Khorshed Mahal. The Avari Towers Hotel, at 17 floors, is the tallest hotel in Pakistan.

Hotels include:

  • Ramada Hotel, Toronto, Canada
  • Avari Hotel, Dubai, UAE
  • Avari Hotel, Lahore, Pakistan
  • Avari Towers, Karachi, Pakistan
  • Beach Luxury Hotel, Karachi, Pakistan
