Avadi is a city and a municipality in Thiruvallur district in the state of Tamil Nadu, India. Avadi is an acronym for "Armoured Vehicles and Ammunition Depot of India". Situated about 20 km north west of Chennai, it is surrounded by major defence establishments.

[edit] Geography

Avadi is located at 13.12° N 80.1° E[2]. It has an average elevation of 17 metres (55 feet).

[edit] Military establishments

The major military establishments having divisions in Avadi are Indian Air Force, Indian Army, Indian Navy, HVF, CVRDE, CRPF, CVD (Central Vehicle Depot) and OCF.

The Indian Air Force has a Mechanical Transport Training Institute Technical (MTTI) here for training its personnel on driving and repairing vehicles. CVRDE stands for "Combat Vehicle Research and Development Establishment", wherein, major research on battle tanks like T-72 and T-90 are undertaken. The Heavy Vehicles Factory produces indigenous version of T-72 tank known as Ajaya. The production of T-90S Bhishma tanks will start in 2006–2007.

[edit] Transport

Avadi is connected to Chennai through NH205. Avadi is a station at the Chennai-Arakkonam railway line. Suburban trains stop at Avadi and Siding E depot. Avadi is nearly 20 km far from Chennai Central station, making it isolated from city.

[edit] Demographics

As of 2001 India censusGRIndia, Avadi had a population of 230,913. Males constitute 52% of the population and females 48%. Avadi has an average literacy rate of 79%, higher than the national average of 59.5%; with 55% of the males and 45% of females literate. 10% of the population is under 6 years of age.

Nearby residential areas are The Vellanur Panchayat and Vellanur Village, Muthapudupettai, Defence Enclave, Veerapuram, Mittanemallli, Kovil Padaghai, Brindavan Nagar, Rajiv Nagar, Kamaraj Nagar and Sabhi Nagar, wherein most of employees of the defence organizations live with their families. People from different ethnicities live in Avadi.

[edit] Education

Avadi has a number of engineering colleges like St. Peters Engineering College,S.A Engineering College, Aalim Mohammed Engg. College, Vel Tech Engg. College.
