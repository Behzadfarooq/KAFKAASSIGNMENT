Avalon Sutra / As Long as I Can Hold My Breath is a double album by Harold Budd which is said to be his final musical work, although both Music for 'Fragments from the Inside' and Mysterious Skin - Music from the Film were released a few months after this album. The first disc is titled Avalon Sutra, and the second is titled As Long as I Can Hold My Breath.
