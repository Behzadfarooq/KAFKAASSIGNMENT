Avarua

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Main street in Avarua

Avarua is a town in the north of Rarotonga Island and the national capital of Cook Islands. The town is located at 21°12′S, 159°46′W at sea level, below the towering peaks of Rarotonga (altitude 208 m or 682 ft). The town is served by Rarotonga International Airport (IATA Airport Code: RAR). The Population of Avarua is 10,000. The town is made up of six of the 12 districts of Rarotonga:

  • Pue - Matavera
  • Tupapa - Maraerenga
  • Takuvaine - Parekura
  • Tutakimoa - Teotue (central downtown district)
  • Avatiu - Ruatonga
  • Nikao - Panama
