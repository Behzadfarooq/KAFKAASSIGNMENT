[edit] Bermuda Triangle page

Just to let you know, and it's extremely minor, when ever a book, magazine, or film is mentioned, the title is always placed in italics; any article within such a work and mentioned in a paragraph is placed in quotation marks, which is a standard method of writing found in a Harbrace manual that a lot of college kids are forced to use. Just don't worry too much about the changes you made or my feelings (which are not hurt, by the way!), I'm very appreciative of any help, including your's, to make it look and read a lot better.
