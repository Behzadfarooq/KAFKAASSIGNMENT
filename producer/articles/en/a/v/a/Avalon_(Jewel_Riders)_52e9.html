Avalon is a fictional land in the Princess Gwenevere and the Jewel Riders animates series.

[edit] Geography

[edit] Riverdells

The Riverdells are where most of the human folk of Avalon live. It's a nice hilly region excellent for farming and growing orchards. A big craft faire is held in the Riverdells every year, and people come from all over Avalon to sell or perform their crafts.

Also in the Riverdells is Rainbow Falls, named for the rainbows created in the mist from the waterfall. The rainbows created a powerful magical field in a cave behind the waterfall.

[edit] Great Forests

The Great Forests are the biggest county of Avalon, from above they look like a wide green carpet. There are legends about the Great Forests, like the one about the little faery wraiths who are shy but will come out and dance for people who have pure hearts or people in love. Another legend is about a great beast called the Prince of the Forest.

Part of the forest, Ravenwood, belongs to Lady Kale, and her Dark Stone twisted the peaceful forest into the nasty Thornwoods.

[edit] Burning Deserts

The Burning Deserts on the western border of the kingdom are home to lots of snakes, lizards, and cacti. Nobody has explored too far into the deserts because there isn't enough water to keep an expedition going. Strangely, Travel Trees do grow in the desert, in the shape of palm trees.

The Great Deserts contain many portholes that lead to many different lands. The entrance to the Fairyland is one these.

[edit] Jungle

The Jungle is mostly impassable, hot and humid and overgrown with vines. The crown jewel that controls this part of Avalon was taken over by Morgana many years ago and she made the jungle grow out of control.

The Jungle is just a normal jungle full of trees -- except for the spot that has a secret porthole to Morgana's lair.

[edit] Great Plains

The Great Plains are gentle hills covered with long grass that waves in the wind. There are some towns there. Heartland Farms is near one of them. The most magical part of the Great Plains is the Dreamfields, where the crown jewel was created. People who fall asleep here can share each others' dreams.

[edit] Crystal Cliffs

The Crystal Cliffs form the northern border of Avalon. Most people of Avalon only see them as tall white spikes against the horizon. The mountains are made of ice that has frozen so hard it couldn't be melted by any heat.

Long, long ago the wizards mined the Crystal Cliffs and dug out the Wizard's Peak, where ancient wizards of the past used to come and study in the Hall of Wizards.

[edit] Misty Moors

The Misty Moors are the most mysterious part of Avalon. The mist rising from the bogs and drifting in from the sea carries strange magic. It's easy to get lost in the Moors. The Misty Rose Jewel with powers of transformation was created here.

[edit] The eighth land

The eighth land of Avalon has no name. It was discovered by Gwenevere and Drake when they got lost and blundered through the border mist. The eighth land is very strange -- the grass is bright neon green and bushes walk. Some of the strangeness probably wore off when the Fortune Jewel was put together and taken to the Jewel Keep, but this is still an unexplored land where anything can happen.

[edit] Places

Castle Rivermist is a beautiful castle in the Misty Moors. A small man named Merry lived there, and he once found the Misty Rose Crown Jewel and turned himself into a rhyming troll, and the castle into a cottage.

Castle Thornwoods is the Lady Kale's domain. Here, she likes to sit on her throne planning her evil schemes.

Crystal Palace was built by Merlin on the heart of Avalon, in the capital, New Camelot. There's a Jewel Keep, contaning the jewels and device enabling to control the magic of Avalon. Just outside is the Friendship Ring, the most powerful place in Avalon.

Faeryland is an enchanted land of tiny faeries. It is ruled by King Oberon. His daughter is Princess Wisp.

Heartland Farm is owned by Tamara's parents, Doc and Charity. The baby magical animals are raised and trained here.

Rainbow Falls is located in the Riverdells. It is a beautiful waterfall and a great place to visit in Avalon. There is a secret passageway behind the falls that created the Rainbow Crown Jewel.

Travel Trees rings are four trees shaped like a square. If one has an enchanted jewel, the Travel Trees will crystallize and open a porthole to enter the wild magic.

Morgana's lair was where Morgana, the evil wizard, created Kale's Dark Stone. Her home endlessly floats around in nothingness. If anyone else removes something from it, the entire place will fall apart and reconstruct somewhere else.

Wizard's Peak now holds clues to why the wizards disappeared and what they were like. However, a genie guards the room and obeys only what wizards tell him to do.
