Ava Benton

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Ava Benton is a character on the American soap opera All My Children. She is played by actress Leven Rambin, who also plays Ava's half-sister, Lily Montgomery. The character first appearance on March 30, 2007.

Details about Ava's past are currently unknown, she was first encountered by Aidan, who was working on a stakeout in Philadelphia. He mistoke her for Lily, but soon discovered otherwise. Ava offered to take him on a date, he accepted, Ava took him to her apartment where she ate Chinese food, she only wanted his money however, but when Aidan offered her some money, she took off out the door. Aidan delivered the news to Jack, determining that they must get the girl off the streets.

[edit] Character Lifeline

  • Ava was born January 4, 1987 in El Paso, Texas. (According to April 5, 2007 episode of AMC)

[edit] Occupation

  • Work a Steak Joint for 2 months
  • Formerly High School Student but drop out in 2003. Got C's and D's before drop out.

[edit] Family

  • Lily Montgomery (paternal half-sister)
  • Dennis "Denny" Benton (biological father; deceased)
  • Unnamed woman (mother)
