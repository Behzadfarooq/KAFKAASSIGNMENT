Ava Devine (born January 22, 1974) is an American pornographic actress known primarily for her surgically enlarged breasts, "dirty talk" and a willingness to perform a variety of sex acts during sex scenes. She has been called the "lady of the lake" of porn, in that she embodies the myth of the promiscuous woman willing to have sex with anyone at any time.[1]

In 2005, she won a prestigious AVN award in the category Best Oral Sex Scene for her performance in Francesca Le's Cum Swallowing Whores 2. Presently, Ava is represented by LA Direct Models.

In 2005, Ava had what she called "malpractice surgery (surgery gone bad)" which put a hold on her film career. In October 2005, she underwent breast reduction surgery and had a lift, reducing her bust to 36DD. She resumed working in Jan 2006 with plans to continue. [2]
