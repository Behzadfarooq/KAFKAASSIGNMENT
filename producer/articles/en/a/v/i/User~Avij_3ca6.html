Hi, I'm avij and I'm the current maintainer of EuroBillTracker, a currency bill tracking website. Because of this, most of my work at Wikipedia is centered around Euro and Euro banknotes in particular. I try to maintain the NPOV in my edits, and I sincerely hope that other people working with those pages would do the same.

For more boring details, see my homepage at http://miuku.net/
