Avi

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Avi can have the following meanings:

Name:

  • Indian given name, it means the Sun.
  • Hebrew given name (see Ab (father))
  • A Hebrew name as a shortened version of Avraham (Hebrew for Abraham of the Old Testament) or literally translated from Hebrew means "my father" (and can have the same meaning as Abraham).
  • Avi, pen name of Edward Irving Wortis
  • Avi Shlaim
  • Avie Tevanian
  • Avi Stern

Other uses:

  • Avi Resort & Casino, Laughlin, Nevada
  • AVI, an acronym for Audio Video Interleave, a video file format
  • Advanced Visual Interfaces, a biennial academic conference in Italy.
