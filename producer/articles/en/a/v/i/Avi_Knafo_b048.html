Avi Knafo (Hebrew: אבי כנפו), (born August 4, 1982) is an Israeli footballer currently plying his trade at Hapoel Kfar Saba. Knafo began his career in the youth team of Beitar Nes Tubruk and joined to the first team of Hapoel Tel Aviv in 2001/2002 season. Knafo has also 7 appearrences in the Israel national under-21 football team. His first appearance was on August 15, 2001 against Turkey.
