Aviation/LAX is a Metro Rail station on the Green Line located on Aviation Boulevard at Imperial Highway, just south of Century Freeway in El Segundo, California. A free shuttle bus connects this station to Los Angeles International Airport. The original name for this station was Aviation/I-105 and it may still be used in some places.
