Aviron Bayonnais Football Club is a French association football team founded in 1935. They are based in Bayonne, Pyrénées-Atlantiques, France and are currently playing in the Championnat de France Amateurs Group C. They play at the Stade Didier Deschamps in Bayonne, which has a capacity of 3,500.

They are part of the same sports club as rugby union club Aviron Bayonnais.
