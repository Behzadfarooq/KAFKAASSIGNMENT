[edit] Evorgreen

Evorgreen is a jam and alternative style acoustic band, started in the basement studio of Michael Wien in Upper Saddle River, New Jersey in the summer of 2005. The band consists of three main band members: Michael Wien, Alex Pildes, and Michael Rosen and an occasional auxiliary drummer: Jack McCarthy. Though the band did not take on the name Evorgreen until late 2005, they have been together for over a year previously and performed together in the Spring talent show at Northern Highlands Regional High School; they played the song Red Eye Beach an original song, and used the band name The Jack Guerin Experience, for the first and only time in their on stage careers.

[edit] Michael Wien

Michael Wien is currently a Senior at Northern Highlands Regional High School, he was born on June 17, in _____ he plays the acoustic and electric guitar, the occasional bongos and can be heard as the lead vocalist for Evorgreen. He has appeared in the Winter and Spring talent shows in 2005 at his local high school, Northern Highlands Regional High School, in Allendale, New Jersey. In the Winter talent show along with Alex Pildes he played and sung Lying in Our Graves by Dave Matthews Band. In the Spring talent show he appeared along with the rest of The Jack Guerin Experience performing [[Red Eye Beach]].

[edit] Alex Pildes

Alex Pildes is currently a Senior at Northern Highlands Regional High School. He was born on June 4, in NYC. He plays the acoustic and electric guitar where he can be heard providing rhythm, and performing original solos. He has appeared in the Winter and Spring talent shows in 2005 at Northern Highlands Regional High School, in Allendale, New Jersey. In the Winter talent show along with Michael Wien he played and performed a solo in the song Lying in Our Graves by Dave Matthews Band. In the Spring talent show he appeared and soloed along with the rest of The Jack Guerin Experience performing [[Red Eye Beach]].

[edit] Michael Rosen

Michael Rosen plays both the acoustic and electric guitar as Alex and Mike, and can also be heard on the base, harmonica, and in the near future piano, as well as a singer in some of the songs.
