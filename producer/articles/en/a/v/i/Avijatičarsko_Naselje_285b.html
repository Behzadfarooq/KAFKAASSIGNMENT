Avijatičarsko Naselje (Авијатичарско Насеље), also known as Avijacija (Авијација), is a quarter of the city of Novi Sad in Serbia.

[edit] Borders

The south-eastern border of Avijatičarsko Naselje is Ulica Oblačića Rada (Oblačića Rada Street), the north-eastern border is Rumenački put (Rumenka Road), and the western border is future Subotički bulevar (Subotica Boulevard), which will be built in 2006/2007.

[edit] Neighbouring city quarters

The neighbouring city quarters are: Jugovićevo in the west, Detelinara in the south-east, and Industrijska Zona Jug in the north-east.

[edit] Name and history

Construction of the settlement started in 1948 and it was named after Avijatičarski put (Avijatičar Road), a road that lead to the local airport.

[edit] Famous citizens

The famous citizens of Avijatičarsko Naselje were literates Miroslav Antić and Ferenc Deak.
