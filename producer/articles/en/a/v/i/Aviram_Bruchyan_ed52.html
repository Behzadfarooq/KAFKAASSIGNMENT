Aviram Bruchian (Hebrew: אבירם ברוכיאן) (born March 20, 1985) in Jerusalem is an Israeli football player.

He has been playing for Beitar Jerusalem since Youth level. In the 2002-2003 season he moved to the Senior team. Bruchian plays as a creative midfielder.

He wears the number 8 jersey which is the same number that his famous uncle wore. His uncle is former Beitar legend Uri Malmilian. When Avi Nimni played in Beitar, Bruchian handed him the number 8 since it is also Nimni's preferred number. Bruchian wore temporarily the number 16.
