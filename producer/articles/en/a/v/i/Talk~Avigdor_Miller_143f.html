[edit] Rav Millers view on food

can somebody please work on this paragraph and place it in the article?

Rabbi Miller's view on food was different from that of other Rabbis. While other rabbis, using the Mussar approach, would consider the eating of foods such as pizza as a pleasureable activity which should be minimized, Rabbi Millers view was that such foods, having been created by god, should be eaten and enjoyed to the fullest, so that one should be able to give thanks to god for the delicious delacacies that he created. 14:46, 19 July 2006 (UTC)
