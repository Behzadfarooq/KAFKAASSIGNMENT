Aviation High School, located in the Highline School District, in Seattle, Washington, is an aviation and aerospace theme school and one of the Highline School District's small schools. It has a large focus on college preparatory studies. The school is opened currently to students going into the 9th, 10th, and 11th grades that live in the Pudget Sound. It has a mentor program and connections to businesses, local government, and colleges. It is housed by South Seattle Community College. Currently there are only about three hundred students.

[edit] Admission

Admission is based on grades, 7th grade WASL scores, interest in the theme of aviation, student behavior and family. Each applicant is interviewed. Only 100 students are accepted yearly. In many ways the process is like that of a private institution, except there is no cost.

[edit] Location

It was confirmed in January 2007 that the school would be moving to the Olympic Interim Site, the current location of Mount Rainier High School. After approximately three years, Aviation High School will move into a new site at the Museum of Flight.
