Avies

Avies Air Company is an airline based in Tallinn, Estonia. It was established and started operations in 1991 and operates charter and air taxi services. Its main base is Tallinn Airport[1].

[edit] Code Data

  • IATA Code: U3
  • ICAO Code: AIA
  • Callsign: Avies [2]

[edit] Services

Avies operates the following scheduled lines: (as of January 2005):[citation needed] Tallinn-Kuressaare-Tallinn, Tallinn-Kärdla-Tallinn and Tallinn-Visby-Tallinn.

[edit] Fleet

The Avies fleet includes the following aircraft (at March 2007) [1] :

  • 1 BAe Jetstream 31
  • 1 BAe Jetstream 32EP
  • 1 Bombardier Learjet 60
  • 1 Let L-410 UVP
  • 1 Let L-410 UVP-E
