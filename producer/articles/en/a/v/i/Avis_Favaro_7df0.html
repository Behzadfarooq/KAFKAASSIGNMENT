Avis Favaro

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Avis Favaro is a Canadian medical correspondent for CTV News since 1992. She has won numerous awards including a Gemini Award. Favaro previously worked as medical correspondent for the Global Television Network where she began her career in 1982 as a writer/reporter. She graduated from the University of Western Ontario with a degree in history and received her masters degree in journalism also from UWO.
