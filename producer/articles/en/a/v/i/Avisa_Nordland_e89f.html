Avisa Nordland is a newspaper published in Bodø, Norway. It was established through a controversial merger of the two competing newspapers Nordlandsposten and Nordlands Framtid. As of 2005, it has a circulation of about 25524 copies[1].

The paper has its headquarters in central Bodø, and occupies most of the bank Nordlandsbankens former premises. In addition it has offices in Nordland small towns Fauske, Oppeid and Ørnes.
