[edit] This category really shouldn't exist

This category really shouldn't exist, because Wikipedia is not a dictionary. A Wikipedia article is properly about the thing described by a term, not about the term itself. (See use-mention distinction if the difference is not clear.) Terms have meanings. The terms here should be categorized based on their meanings within Aviation, not on being "terminology".--Srleffler 04:19, 17 January 2007 (UTC)
