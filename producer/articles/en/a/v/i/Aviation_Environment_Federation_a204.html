Aviation Environment Federation

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Aviation Environment Federation (AEF) is the principal UK non-profit making organisation concerned with the environmental effects of aviation. These range from aviation noise issues associated with small airstrips or helipads, to the contribution of airline emissions to climate change.

The AEF was established in 1975 and now has over 120 affiliated members comprising community and environmental groups, local authorities, parish councils, businesses and consultancies.

AEF is a member of Airportwatch, a UK-wide network of conservation and residents’ groups, and Green Skies Alliance, an umbrella organisation comprising all the major European NGOs who hold a stake in aviation policy.
