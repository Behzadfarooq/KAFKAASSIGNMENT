Aviv Maayan is a British professional wrestler. Originally from Wales, Maayan is best known for competing in the Frontier Wrestling Alliance, and other independent promotions in the United Kingdom.

[edit] Career

Mayaan began training at the Frontier Wrestling Alliance Academy on June 28th 2002 under the tutelage of Mark Sloan. Maayan made his pro wrestling debut at FWA:A show Explosion on the February 15, 2003 in a losing effort to Eamon Shrahan.

Since graduating from the FWA:A, Aviv Maayan has worked in over twenty promotions in the United Kingdom including FWA, All Star Wrestling, Premier Promotions, WAW, IPW:UK and many more. He also went on to wrestled in Israel, Germany and France.

On January 23, 2005, Aviv Maayan won IPW:UK's Best of British: High Flyers after a nearly 25 minute match (24:58). This elimination style match contained many top name British high flyers, namely Dragon Phoenix, Jonny Storm, Flaming Red, Spud. The Best of British win would make Maayan #1 contender for the IPW:UK title, Maayan would go on to hold the belt for one night only before losing it to Martin Stone on July 17th.

On October 28, 2005, Aviv Maayan made his Varsity Pro Wrestling debut in a match against fellow FWA:A graduate Max Voltage, Maayan would win this match. During Maayan's first VPW appearance in 2006 he was defeated by Phil Powers. After the match a mystery man attacked Maayan with a powerbomb into a boston crab submission, "The Twisted Genius" Dean Ayass (former manager of WWE's Paul Burchill) then made his way to the ring and introduced the man as his new protégé, Joel Redman. On VPW's first Charity Show Maayan got his chance to face Redman, Maayan won the match even though he was considerabley outsized but this would not end their feud as once again after Aviv Mayaan lost a match to Jorge Castano, Redman and Ayass would once again come to the ring and attack Mayaan.

During this time he would wrestle in other independent promotions in the UK including Real Quality Wrestling where he fueded with The Bulldozer. One match between the two saw a stipulation added by Sam Gibbs, a member of the Real Quality Wrestling Fan Forum, which made the match a 2 out of 3 falls match, Aviv Maayan would win the match 2-1.

Throughout the rest of 2006, Maayan concentrated adding more lean muscle to his frame in hopes of achieving a bigger build to compete in bodybuilding competitions. In late 2006, Mayaan would also turn heel in the IPW:UK promotion, aligning himself with Andy Boy Simmons and LT Summers to form the Hated Heroes; helping Simmons take the IPW:UK title away from Martin Stone.
