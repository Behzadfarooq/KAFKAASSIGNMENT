Aviajet is a Dublin based charter broker. While it is not an airline, it provides flights for tour operators. Its flights are operated by MD83's in the Aviajet livery. Airlines which fly for Aviajet include Mapjet and Spanair. Air Atlanta Icelandic have previously operated A310's for Aviajet.
