The Mishnah Berurah with Rabbi Nebenzahl's commentary

Rabbi Avigdor Nebenzahl is the chief rabbi of the Old City of Jerusalem and is also is on the Yeshivat Hakotel staff. He has authored a commentary to the Mishnah Berurah. He is one of Rabbi Shlomo Zalman Auerbach's leading disciples. He is the senior Rosh Yeshiva at Yeshivat Netiv Aryeh.
