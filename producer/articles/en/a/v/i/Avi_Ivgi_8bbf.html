Avi Ivgi is an Israeli football player. Ivgi was born on 2/10/1978. Ivgi currently plays as a Goalkeeper (football) in Beitar Jerusalem FC. He was raised in the Hapoel Nazareth Illit Youth squad. on the 96/97 season Ivgi made his debut in the senior squad for Hapoel Nazareth Illit vs. Hapoel Maj'dl al-Karoum. in 2001 Ivgi moved to Bnei Yehuda Tel Aviv and helped them get promoted in the 01/02 season to Ligat ha'Al. In 2003 Ivgi moved to Hapoel Kfar Saba who were in the Liga Leumit, and they finished in the 4'th place nearly promoting to Ligat ha'Al. in the next season (04/05) Ivgi and Hapoel Kfar Saba won the Liga Leumit championship and promoted to Ligat ha'Al. After a season with Hapoel Kfar Saba in Ligat ha'Al Ivgi made the move to Beitar Jerusalem FC.
