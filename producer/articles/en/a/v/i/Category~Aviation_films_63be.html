Category:Aviation films

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Films that generally revolve around and feature the main characters flying aircraft as part of civilian or military aviation.

A common plot in aviation-based films involves the testing of a new type of aircraft by the main characters.

See also: Category:Films set on an airplane
