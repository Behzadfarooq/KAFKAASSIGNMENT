Avis Miller (born November 4, 1945 in Ohio) is an American model. She was Playboy magazine's Playmate of the Month for the November 1970 issue. Her centerfold was photographed by Dwight Hooker.

Avis was also known for her status as a "Jet Bunny", a Bunny who served as a flight attendant on Hugh Hefner's personal DC-9 jet airplane, named the "Big Bunny". She served primarily during Hefner's extended tour (alongside Barbi Benton) to Europe and Africa in 1970. Miller also was a "grounded" Bunny at the Playboy Clubs in Phoenix, San Francisco and Los Angeles.

She is a graduate of Arizona State University.
