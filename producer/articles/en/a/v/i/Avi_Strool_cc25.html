Avi Strool (Hebrew: אבי סטרול) (born September 18, 1980) is an Israeli football player who plays as a defender for Maccabi Netanya and is a member of the Israel national football team player pool.

Strool grew up in the youth system of Maccabi Tel Aviv of Ligat ha'Al and has spent 7 years there. During the summer of 2006 he transferred to Maccabi Netanya.

Being of Romanian descent, the player holds two passports, Israeli and Romanian.
