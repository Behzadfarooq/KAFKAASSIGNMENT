Avi Resort & Casino

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Avi Resort & Casino is a resort located on the banks of the Colorado River between Laughlin, Nevada, and Needles, California it is owned by the Fort Mojave Tribe. It has 465 recently remodelled rooms, a 25,000 square foot casino, and a 260 space RV park with internet access.

[edit] History

The casino opened on February 17, 1995 and was built at at cost of $60 million.
