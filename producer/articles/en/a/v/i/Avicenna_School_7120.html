The Avicenna School, is an accounting school located in Clifton, Karachi, Sindh, Pakistan.

The Avicenna School, was founded in 1996, and sponsored by the College of Accounting and Management Sciences (CAMS). It is a co-educational institution, founded with the aim to abolish private tuitions for Advanced Level (A-level) as they were expensive. In 2000, Ordinary Level (O-level) was introduced, and later junior classes were started as well.

[edit] Campuses

  • The Avicenna 'A' Levels School, Block-2, Clifton
  • The Avicenna 'O' Levels School, Block-2, Clifton
  • The Avicenna School, Juniors Branch (Nursery – Grade VIII), Block -2, Clifton
  • Avicenna Girls O & A Levels School, Al-Hamra Cooperative Housing Society (AHCHS), Main Tipu Sultan Road.
