Aviaenergo

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Aviaenergo is an airline based in Moscow, Russia. It was established in 1994 and operates charters within Europe, the CIS and other countries. It is wholly owned by the Russian State Energy Company. Its main base is Sheremetyevo International Airport, Moscow[1].

Aviaenergo aircraft RA 85809 departs Sheremetyevo Airport, Moscow during a beautiful sunset.
