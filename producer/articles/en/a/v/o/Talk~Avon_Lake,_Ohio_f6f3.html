There's nothing wrong with the city not changing. In fact, I prefer it that way. I don't have to deal with inordinate amounts of traffic all the time, noise is not a problem, etc.

Now that Avon is expanding, they can absorb all of the development burden, and I can drive a mere five minutes to get to all the shopping areas that used to be a half hour away.

And don't complain about the internet at school blocking your jokes and videos. While the taxpayers of the city are paying for your education, you can spend it getting educated, not being entertained. Once you're paying for your own education, you can do whatever you want (when you go to college).

If you think the high school is bad, you should have been there 5 years ago when they ripped the roof and walls off sections of the school. It was a lot colder than 55 then!
