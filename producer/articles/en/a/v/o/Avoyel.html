Avoyel

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Avoyel or Avoyelles was a small Natchesan tribe in the neighborhood of the present Marksville, Louisiana. Numbering 280 in 1698, by 1805 they were believed to have been reduced to only two or three women.
