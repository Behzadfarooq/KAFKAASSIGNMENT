Avon Grove School District is a school district located in West Grove, Pennsylvania, 19390 [1] . Around 4,773 students go to Avon Grove schools from kindergarten to twelfth grade. Avon Grove Charter School used to be Avon Grove Elementary until the Avon Grove Intermediate School was built in 2002. All students attending Avon Grove Elementary School were moved to the Avon Grove Intermediate School at the end of the 2002 - 2003 school year. [edit] Schools within the district

Schools:Penn London Elementary School, Fred S. Engle Middle School, Avon Grove Intermediate School, Avon Grove High School, and Avon Grove Charter School.

Avon Grove High School holds grades 9-12, Fred S. Engle Middle School holds grades 7 & 8, Avon Grove Intermediate holds grades 3-6, Penn London Elementary holds grades K-2. Avon Grove Charter School holds grades K-11
