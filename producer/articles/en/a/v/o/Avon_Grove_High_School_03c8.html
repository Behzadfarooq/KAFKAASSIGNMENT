Avon Grove High School is a public high school located in Southern Chester County, Pennsylvania.

AGHS Principal Thomas M.Alexander

[edit] School

Avon Grove High School enrolls about 1,800 students in grades 9-12. It is neighbored by Fred S. Engle Middle School, which houses grades 7 and 8. The school mascot is the Red Devil, and the school colors are maroon and gold.

[edit] Sports

Avon Grove teams have one state title, the 1979 PIAA Division II Men's Basketball team.[citation needed] A varsity football program was recently created and is now participating in the Chestmont league.
