Avon River in Stratford, Ontario

The Avon River is a river in Perth County, Ontario, Canada. The river was named after the River Avon in England when the town of Stratford was founded on its banks in 1832. The Avon River rises northeast of Stratford and flows southwest, entering the North branch of the River Thames near St. Mary's. It was originally known as the Little Thames River.
