Wikipedia:Avoid using WikiProject talk page templates

WP:AUWPTPT
This proposal was rejected by the community. It has not gained consensus and seems unlikely to do so.

Recently (or not so recently) there has been a push by many Wikipedia:WikiProjects to put templates - like {{U.S. Highway WikiProject}} - on the talk pages of all articles within their scope. While this may help newbies find other like-minded editors, it has a serious flaw. One can no longer find out if there is discussion on a talk page from the color of the "discussion" tab. --SPUI (T - C) 00:39, 24 June 2006 (UTC)
