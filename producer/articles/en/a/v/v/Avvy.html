Avvy

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Avvy (born April 22, 1984) is a young talented songwriter and performer included in the list of Bahamian musicians who contribute to the art of Bahamian folk music (Junkanoo & Rake-and-Scrape). He has received accolades for reviving native Bahamian music with innovative storytelling, upbeat rhythms and energetic performances. [1]
