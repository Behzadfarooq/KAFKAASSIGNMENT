Avy Scott (born November 2, 1981 in Tampa, Florida) is an American pornographic actress. Formerly a business and psychology major in college,[2] she was doing live masturbation shows online. She subsequently dropped out of college and entered the adult film world in November of 2001. Her first film was Four Finger Club #20, while her first boy-girl scene was in Real Sex Magazine #47.[2]

Her unusual first name is pronounced as if saying the letters "A-V"[1] (rhyming with "navy"), and is a shortened form of the French word "Avril".[2]

She has appeared in over 100 adult films and has directed four.[3]
