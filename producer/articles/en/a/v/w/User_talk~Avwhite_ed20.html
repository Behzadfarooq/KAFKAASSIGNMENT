[edit] Niagara article notability

Hey there,

could you take a quick look at Niagara Hostel? It seems a bit like a marginally-notable business advertisement. Does the building have any interesting history or anything else that would make it encyclopedia-worthy?

The author's only edits are creating that article and linking it up in a couple of other Niagara articles. I'm pondering listing this on Articles for Deletion but would like a local's opinion first. --Qviri (talk) 05:32, 24 February 2006 (UTC)

He added it to an unnecessary section in the Niagara Falls article as well, so it's pretty clear he's advertising. I say we step in an try and clean up the hostel article to make it a bit more NPOV / brochure-styled. If the author starts trying to revert the changes than it will be pretty clear that this is just advertising and a good article for deletion. For what it's worth, I've never even heard of this building before. I knew the Falls had a hostel though, so it may be worth keeping in some form. Avwhite 13:16, 24 February 2006 (UTC)
