[edit] About Me

Some Info
This user comes from Canada.
This user comes from the Niagara Region.
This user enjoys punk rock.
This user enjoys ska.

I'm from Niagara Falls, Ontario. I've just returned to my hometown after living in Guelph, Ontario, where I finished a degree in computer science and studied sociology at the University of Guelph. I work in Information Technology as a programmer / consultant by day and co-run independent record label Punknews Records by night.

I'm also one of the editors and reviewers on popular punk rock music news website Punknews.org. Tweaking articles on punk music is what got me initially involved in writing here on the Wiki.
