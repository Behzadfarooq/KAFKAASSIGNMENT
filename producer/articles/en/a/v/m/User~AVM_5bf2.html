Briefing: Venezuelan (Caracas). Degree in Industrial Engineering from UCAB (Andres Bello Catholic University), Caracas. Administration degree from UCV (Universidad Central de Venezuela). Several diplomas in music. Fluent in Spanish, English, less in Portuguese and German, and some Hebrew. Related to the Magyar culture since early childhood, but gave up learning the Hungarian language, as that is utterly hopeless. I emphatically declare to be an American, since I was born in the American Continent.

Main Interests: Music, Software/Computing Engineering, Science, Astronomy, Religion, and latin american cultures. A lover of the Spanish Guitar, the Brazilian guitar (Violão), and the Organ. An admirer of Arturo Uslar Pietri, Jorge Luis Borges, Mario Vargas Llosa, Arthur C. Clarke, Carl Sagan, and ETC. (And that's a big "ETC"). An advocate of the International System of Units, and a loather of the English System of Units. Also, last but not least, a lover of Australia and a devoted lifelong fan of Kylie Minogue.

Wikipedia Goals: Contributing to the enrichment of the Venezuelan portal, especially concerning Venezuelan musicians; attempting to build, or help to build, reliable contributions to other topics; the latter applies both to the English and Spanish versions of Wikipedia. Also: contributing (if such goal has any hope at all) to make Wikipedia a more democratic place. I admire the dedication of most Admins, but some of them (especially a few gruesome Admins I have met in the Spanish WK) sometimes act like police rookies, or as if they owned the place. I feel sorry for them: they should seek to get a real life.
