AVN (Adult Video News) magazine is the standard trade magazine of the pornography industry in the United States. It is headquartered in the San Fernando Valley. It is owned by Paul Fishbein.

Since 1984, AVN has produced the annual AVN Awards where awards are given to honor achievement in the field of adult video.
