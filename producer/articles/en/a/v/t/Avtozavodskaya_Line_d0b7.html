Avtozavodskaya Line (Belarusian: Аўтазаводзкая; Russian: Автозаво́дская) is a line of the Minsk Metro. The line opened in 1990 and crosses the city on a Northwest-Southeast axis. Currently it comprises 14 stations and 18.1 kilometres of track.

[edit] Rolling stock

The line is served by the Mogilyovskoe depot (№ 2), and currently has 27 four carriage 81-717/714 and the modernised 81-717.5M/714.5M trains assigned to it.

[edit] Recent developments and future plans

The line is complete, no further extensions are planned in the near future.
