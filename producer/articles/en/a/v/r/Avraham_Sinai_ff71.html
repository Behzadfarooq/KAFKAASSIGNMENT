Avraham Sinai (b. 1962) is a Lebanese former Shi'ite who converted to Judaism. According to an interview[1] he served as an informant for the Israelis while serving in Hezbollah, until his actions were uncovered. He fled to Israel and subsequently converted.

Avraham Sinai now resides in Tzfat, Israel with his wife and seven children. He has recently authored a book titled A Martyr from Lebanon: Life in the Shadow of Danger describing some of his experiences.
