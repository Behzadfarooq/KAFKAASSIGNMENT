Avraam Papadopoulos (born in December 3, 1984 in Sydney, Australia) is a football player, currently playing for Aris Thessaloniki

Avraam Papadopoulos is considered to be one of the most talented young players in Greece. He is a member of Aris Thessaloniki since 2001, when he signed his first contract for the Aris youth team.
