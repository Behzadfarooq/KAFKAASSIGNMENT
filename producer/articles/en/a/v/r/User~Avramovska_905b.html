Elizabeta Avramovska, artist, professor at the Faculty of Architecture at "St. Cyril and Methodius" University in Skopje, R. Macedonia

CURRICULUM VITAE

BIOGRAPHY:

Name ELIZABETA AVRAMOVSKA 1956 born in Skopje, R. of Macedonia. 1984 BA diploma, Faculty of Fine Arts, 'Sts. Cyrill and Metodius' University, Skopje, R. of Macedonia. 1986 Graduate at the Faculty of Architecture, 'Sts. Cyrill and Metodius' University, Skopje, R. of Macedonia. 1985 Member of Fine Art Association of R. of Macedonia. 1995 MA in History and Philosophy of Art and Architecture, The Central European University, Prague, Czech Republic. Since 1986 employed as a Proffessor on the Fine Art subjects at the Faculty of Architecture at the 'Sts.Cyril and Metodius' University in Skopje, R. of Macedonia. Experience in the fields of: painting, graphic design, stage and costume design, architectural design, teaching and photography.
