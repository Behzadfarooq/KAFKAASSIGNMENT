AVRI APOCALYPSE

Artist History:

Born in Louisville, Kentucky in year 1980. Ant'juan Coleman Avri moved to Toledo, Ohio in search of unknown family and has even soared the streets of Chicago's south side in 1998. As a little child he has been enrolled in many different schools due to unstable living situations with family. Ant'juan always had his eye on hip hop and was forced by his step mother to learn to write. He would later write about being abused as a child by his different stepfathers and being bullied, picked on in school, and how he lived in a foster home away from brothers and sisters and other family members. Ant'juan had run ins with the police and often experimented with drugs and alcohol and would often dream of a better way of life. At a young age he and his family was forced by step father to become members of Jehova's witness and attend services at the local Kindom halls and not celebrate any holidays. He and brothers and sisters later decided to follow the christian way of life. Ant'juan later decided not to be affiliated with any religon at all and spoke his beliefs through his music. He has played parts in many hip hop groups that didn't make it from the ground up. He even helped build a company known as "E-double entertainment" along with Jon "Snoop" Mitchell and a few other hip hop fans. He now works for himself recording under the imprint of "Dream realm entertainment". Look for him to turn some some heads with the independent release of his solo ep album under alias: Shaydie Tha Phantom, titled "ETERNAL BURN: THE ARMAGEDDON". Followed up by "AVRI: The art of blasphemy and reprocussion-mixtape."
