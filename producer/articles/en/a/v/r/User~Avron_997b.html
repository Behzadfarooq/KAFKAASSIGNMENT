I spend far too much time online, in one form or another. Recently a lot of that has been at Memory Alpha again after a year long vacation. Most of the time I spend here at wikipedia however ends up being a constant string of links being followed as I tangent off from whatever initially caught my interest. As a direct consequence of that I'm not likely to be doing a lot of editing as I won't often know enough about what I'm currently reading to notice problems. I may still be in a position to deal with typos and other minor issues though.

At about the same time I went back to Memory Alpha I also started using the Robot Co-op services at All Consuming and Lists of Bests to keep track of what I read and watch. Unsurprisingly (to me at least) there has been a significant number of Star Trek related books and DVDs on the lists.
