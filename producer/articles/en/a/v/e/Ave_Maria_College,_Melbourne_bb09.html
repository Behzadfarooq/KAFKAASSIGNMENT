Ave Maria College, Melbourne

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Ave Maria College is a Catholic secondary college for girls, and was established in 1963 by the Franciscian Missionaries of Mary (FMM). Ave Maria College is located in Melbourne, Victoria, Australia. The Principal of Ave Maria as of 2006 is Mrs Olwen Bell.
