Avenches is a district of the Canton of Vaud, Switzerland. The seat of the district is the town of Avenches.

The following municipalities are located in the district:

  • Avenches
  • Bellerive
  • Chabrey
  • Constantine
  • Cudrefin
  • Donatyre
  • Faoug
  • Montmagny
  • Mur
  • Oleyres
  • Vallamand
  • Villars-le-Grand
