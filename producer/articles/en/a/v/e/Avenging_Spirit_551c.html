Avenging Spirit is a 2-player platform arcade game released by Jaleco in 1991.

[edit] Plot

During a walk with your girlfriend you are ambushed by unknown guys who take your girl away and shoot you dead! Now, as a wandering spirit with the ability to possess almost anybody you come across, you are summoned by your girlfriend's father (who is researching ghost energy) and given a mission to save her from the mysterious crime syndicate that holds her hostage so that you can rest in peace.

[edit] Gameplay

You can possess one of four characters with unique abilities. Shoot (or possess!) enemies, collect power-ups, and defeat bosses to advance.
