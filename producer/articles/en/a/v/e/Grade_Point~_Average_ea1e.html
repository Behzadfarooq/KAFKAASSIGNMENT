"Grade Point: Average" is an episode of Life with Derek.

[edit] Synopsis

Casey discovers she has a reputation as a grade-grubber and wants to rid herself of it by picking a slacker to work with on a group assignment - like Derek. Derek is on the brink of failing English and wants to pick someone smart to work with on the same group assignment - like Casey.
