Avebury Manor & Garden is a National Trust property consisting of an early 16th-century manor house and its surrounding garden. Avebury Manor & Garden is located in Avebury, near Marlborough, Wiltshire, England.

The manor house is privately occupied, and part is open to the general public. The garden was completely redesigned in the early 20th century]. The topiary and other formal gardens are contained within walls and clipped box, creating numerous "rooms".
