[edit] Fair use problem with Image:BizzyD.PNG

I notice you uploaded the image Image:BizzyD.PNG with the {{stamp}} fair use image tag. However, it appears that this tag is totally unrelated to the content of the image, and as a result it may qualify for speedy deletion. Image:BizzyD.PNG has been tagged for deletion, and may have been deleted by the time you see this message.

If you can find a valid tag that expresses why Image:BizzyD.PNG can be used under the fair use guidelines, please replace the {{stamp}} tag that you have placed on it with that tag. If no such tag exists, please add the {{fairusein|article name that the image is used in}} tag, along with a brief explanation of why this constitutes fair use of the image. If the image has been deleted, you can reupload it but please ensure you place the correct tag on it. However, you must not remove the speedy deletion notice. Thank you, and please feel free to continue contributing to Wikipedia. --Fritz Saalfeld (Talk) 15:00, 21 July 2006 (UTC)
