Aven Amentza

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Aven Amentza is a Romanian non-government organisation, established in 1997, that seeks to improve the social position of the country's Roma minority, while also enhacing Roma rights and combating anti-Roma discrimination. It is also involved in research activities that analyse the situation of the Roma in Romania.
