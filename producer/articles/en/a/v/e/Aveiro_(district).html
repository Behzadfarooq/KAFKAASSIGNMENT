The District of Aveiro (pron. IPA: [a'vɐiɾu]) is located in the central coastal of Portugal, the District Capital is the city of Aveiro.

The district is composed by 19 municipalities:

  • Águeda
  • Albergaria-a-Velha
  • Anadia
  • Arouca
  • Aveiro
  • Castelo de Paiva
  • Espinho
  • Estarreja
  • Ílhavo
  • Mealhada
  • Murtosa
  • Oliveira de Azeméis
  • Oliveira do Bairro
  • Ovar
  • Santa Maria da Feira
  • São João da Madeira
  • Sever do Vouga
  • Vagos
  • Vale de Cambra
