"AveMaria by Giulio Caccini" is a popular and much recorded (notably by Andrea Bocelli, Charlotte Church, Sumi Jo et al.) aria.

It is a composition by Vladimir Vavilov, a 20th-century Russian lutenist-composer, the author of numerous musical hoaxes. It was composed ca. 1970. Vavilov himself published and recorded with the ascription to "Anonymous" in 1973. It is unclear how the work received ascription to Giulio Caccini, as it is entirely foreign to real Caccini's style.
