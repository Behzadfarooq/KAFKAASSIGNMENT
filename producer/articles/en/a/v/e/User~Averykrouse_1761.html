[edit] Welcome Statement

Hello there! Wikipedia is a place of learning, knowledge, and community. I hope that my contributions to this encyclopedia have made it a better, stronger place. I believe that if we all work together, seeking to build a community of civility and humility through scholarship, desiring to expand ourselves and our access to information, and pledging to act toward one another how we would like others to act toward us, we will leave this place must better than we found it. Please take a moment to explore my user page. Here you will find links to various facets of my life and experience as well as more information about me, your humble editor and scholar-to-be.
