Avenir ~Mirai~ (アヴニール ～未来～, Avenir ~Mirai~?) is Kaori Iida's third album as a solo artist of Hello! Project, and her first album in Japanese. It was released on December 29, 2004, when she was still a member of the idol group, Morning Musume.
