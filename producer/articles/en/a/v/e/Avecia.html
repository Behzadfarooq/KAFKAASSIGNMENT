Avecia

Avecia are a leading global fine and specialty chemical company, with their International Headquarters in Blackley, Manchester, England. Avecia is the new name for the former Zeneca Specialties business following the completion of the buyout from AstraZeneca, jointly financed by Cinven and Investcorp. Avecia have several business, based on various sites around the U.S. and UK. These include: Biologics, DNA Medicines, Electrophotography, Ink Jet Printing Materials and Pharmaceuticals. Until recently Avecia also owned a Fine Chemicals facility based in Grangemouth in Scotland, however this was sold to Finnish company KemFine Ltd. on 19th September 2005. The current CEO of Avecia is Adrian Buckmaster.
