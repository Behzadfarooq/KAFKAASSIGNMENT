Avengers in Galactic Storm is a one-on-one beat 'em up arcade game released by Data East in 1995. It features a single player story mode or multiplayer one-on-one mode. The game is based on characters in the Marvel universe and the storyline is from the Operation: Galactic Storm series.

[edit] Selectable characters

  • The Avengers
    • Black Knight
    • Captain America
    • Crystal
    • Thunderstrike
  • The Kree
    • Dr. Minerva
    • Korath
    • Shatterax
    • Supremor
