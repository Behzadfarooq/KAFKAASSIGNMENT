i'll identify the remaining characters by who surrounds them:

1. the blond figure on the left side of the image, surrounded by Rage, Network, Green Goblin, Aegis, Swordsman, Gargoyle, and Rocket Racer

2. above that one and to the slight right are three unnamed figures; a dark-haired man, a dark-haired woman, and a cowled man; these three are surrounded by the following characters, clockwise: Death Adder, Aqueduct, Blade, Firebird, Ajaxis, and Micromax

3. to the right of them in the top center of the picture is a dark-haired man surrounded by Diamondback, Silhouette, Tarantula, Black Cat, Humbug, Rockslide, and Slyde

4. below him and to the right is a woman with a sword, surrounded by Shang-Chi, Doc Samson, Shooting Star, Ares, Black Cat, and Humbug

5. slightly up from her and to the right are two more unnamed figures, a woman with short-cropped hair and a blond man, surrounded by Mach-IV, Asylum, Radioactive Man, Nightwing, Hellcat, Captain Britain, Human Torch, Bloodstrike, X-Ray, Sage, and Deadpool

6. slightly down and right from that blond man is another blond man, surrounded by Angela del Toro, Wiccan, Puma, Radioactive Man, Asylum, and Tiger Shark
