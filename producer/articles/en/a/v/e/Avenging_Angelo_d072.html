Avenging Angelo is a 2002 film directed by Martyn Burke which stars Sylvester Stallone and Madeleine Stowe. This is the last film that Anthony Quinn appeared in. It was released a few months after his death.

Tagline: Her life is his job.

  • Raoul Bova - Marcello/Johnny Carboni
  • Harry Van Gorkum - Kip Barrett
