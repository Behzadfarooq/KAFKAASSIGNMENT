Just a note about posting links to Fan Sites on Wiki articles and in particular with regard to the Calvin & Hobbes article ...

Links to fan sites have been removed for having a small number of scanned comic strips which possibly fall under fair use provisions, and possibly not.

However, if you are going to remove these links, then you need to remove all links because all fan sites contain scanned strips, or quotes from the book, or scanned newspaper clipings. Under a strict definition of copyright, all of these are 'illegal' and therefore nothing should be included. This is obviously a stupid policy and one which hurts the fans. Better to allow any fan site to link and only remove those that grossly infringe copyright such as if you had every scanned strip on the site.

I wasn't totally thrilled with this, but, hey, it was better than having NO LINKS to fan sites. I run a Calvin and Hobbes Fan site myself (The Calvin and Hobbes Online Museum) so I have seen basically every C&H site there is to see :-). The links that we decided should be removed were those that contained lots of copyrighted comic strips, and those that were nothing but fluff. The reason I removed the link to Cool Calvin and Hobbes Collection is beacuse it had a lot of copyrighted comic strips. But, hey, it doesn't seem like anybody is griping about it right now, so let's keep it! I think it's a great site!
