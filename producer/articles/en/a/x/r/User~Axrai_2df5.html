Greetings, and Assalamualaikum Waramatullahi Wabarakatuh!

I am a journalist from Malaysia, and am eager to help contribute to this wonderful site - both in terms of content and in terms of translation (to Bahasa Melayu). I taught piano for a very short while, and am interested in Western Classical music - specifically the music of Prokofiev, Shostakovich and other Russian composers (Kabalevsky, Khachaturian, Schehedrin, Schnittke, etc.)
