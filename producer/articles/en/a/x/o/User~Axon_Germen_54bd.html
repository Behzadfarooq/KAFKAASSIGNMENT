[edit] Statement by Axon

Germen has been blocked for edit warring and vandalism three times in the last two months[3], all in relation to the Islamophobia page and other Islam-related articles. He has also attempted to re-create deleted articles[4] and disrupting Wikipedia to make a point by creating alternative pages for his POV all of which must be subsequently put up for VfDs [5] [6] in which Germen aggressively harrassses and contradicts voters[7] [8] that vote against him. He also makes bad faith remarks and edits about other editors because of their alleged religious beliefs (including the compilation of a list of muslims, probable and not, on his user page [9] [10]).

Attempts at dispute resolution are fruitless as Germen remains unapologetic for his behaviour[11][12], insisting on debating even basic Wikipedia policy on WP:VAND, WP:3RR, WP:NPOV and WP:NOR, rule-lawyering Wikipedia policy rather than working towards it's spirit[13], claiming his POV is NPOV [14], backing up his uncited assertions with original research[15][16] and modifying loosely related pages with uncited information to back up his edits. This has resulted in the Islamophobia article being protected for a month without any progress being made on the talk page.

Other inappropriate behaviour includes possible use of sockpuppets to circumvent admin blocking and double-voting [17] [18] [19], continually marking reverts and controversial edits as minor and without edit comments [20] [21] despite several warnings, prematurely removing dispute tags [22], deleting legitimate comments from talk pages [23] and even vandalising evidence and user's comments and signatures on his own RfC page [24] [25].
