IIRC, he was born William Rose, changed to William Bailey at two years old when his mother remarried, changed back to William Rose at 17 when he discovered the identity of his biological father, added Axl as his middle name, and subsequently shortened his first name to just "W" because of his unwillingness to share a first name with his real father. Some reports give that at some point he was called William Bruce Bailey, but whether Bruce was his given middle name at birth (William Bruce Rose?) is unclear. It is also possible that his first name was shortened before the Axl was added, that will have to be verified as well. Currently, his name is W Axl Rose. P 16:21, 9 August 2005 (UTC)

I googled Axl Rose Bios and I got this info:

  • http://www.mygnr.com/members/axl.html - Axl Rose was once known by the name of Bill Bailey. Bailey being his stepfathers name. He took the name of Rose when he found out when he found out it was his real father's name.
  • http://www.imdb.com/name/nm0741730/bio - Birth Name: William Bruce Rose
  • http://www.thestudentzone.com/articles/axlrose.html - Mother Sharon E Bailey and father William Rose split when he was two, with Rose Snr disappearing, and Sharon later marrying L Stephen Bailey. Later he shortened his first name to W ("Because William was an asshole") and then added the Axl after a pre-GNR band name.
  • http://www.livedaily.com/artists/bio/1437.html - William Bruce Rose was born on February 6, 1962, in Lafayette, IN, and suffered sexual abuse from his biological father and physical abuse from his eventual stepfather at an early age (Rose changed his name to William Bailey after his mother remarried).
