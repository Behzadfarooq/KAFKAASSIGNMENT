I was wondering about the wording used in this article describing Axl's 2nd ending vs Zappa and relation to I-no. With regard to Axl's 2nd ending, I don't think he sees S-ko, so it's not like, "Whoa a girl that looks like Megu-- Hey, I wonder what Megumi's doing right now..." Rather, he just seems to "suddenly" wonder about Megumi. The way that ending plays out is a bit vague, so I'm not discounting the Megumi = S-ko theory - I just think you shouldn't say that Megumi = S-ko is implied. (By the way, it *is* official that S-ko committed suicide after being apparently betrayed by her lover - see the Plot FAQ at www.romancancel.com or www.thegearproject.org .) And with regard to I-no, I don't think it's correct to say that Axl recognizes I-no, per se - rather, as he says in one of his fight-opening line: "There's something about your face... I've seen it a lot..." This doesn't imply recognition, merely familiarity. -RTL 13 May 2005
I know. Don't worry, I know that the S-Ko=Megumi thing is fan speculation. That's why I said "Possibly" rather than simply "Because." And in the thing about I-No, to be honest, I don't see how recognition and familiarity differ in this case. He says he recognizes her, so I put it in the article.Digital Watches 21:35, 14 May 2005 (UTC)
