[edit] Axl's Status as a Maverick Hunter

In the Wiki article, I noticed that it is said that 'Axl is not officially employed as a Maverick Hunter, although he is allowed to work alongside them'. According to the Mega Man X8 manual, however, Axl is described as someone who 'left Red Alert to become a Maverick Hunter'. I think that that particular point about Axl not being one should be removed, on those grounds and the logical reasoning that the Maverick Hunters frown on vigilantes (as depicted by Signas' disapproval of Red Alert in Mega Man X7's dialouge, who said that they were a group of 'wannabe vigilantes'), so, therefore, why would they have vigilantes working for them?

It says, specically: 'left Red Alert to become a Maverick Hunter'. That is not as plausible as saying he became one. Also, remember the fact, X and Zero are essentially the head of the organization, so they can make just about anything acceptable. -ZeroTalk 18:17, 1 April 2006 (UTC)
