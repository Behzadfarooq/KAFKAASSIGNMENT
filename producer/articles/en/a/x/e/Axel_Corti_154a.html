Axel Corti (* May 7, 1933 in Paris; † December 29, 1993 in Oberndorf, Austria, was an Austrian writer and director.

[edit] Life

Axel Corti worked at the Austrian Television from 1953 onwards. Beginning with the works on "Schalldämpfer", he made Austrian radio history. Later he became a professor at Austrian academy of film (in 1972).

Axel Corti was the father of three sons, one of them Sebastian Corti.

[edit] Work

  • "Kaiser Joseph und die Bahnwärterstochter", 1963 (with Hans Holt, Hans Moser and Inge Konradi)
  • "Ein junger Mann aus dem Innviertel", 1973
  • "Totstellen", 1975
  • "An uns glaubt Gott nicht mehr", 1982
  • "Herrenjahre", 1983 (with Lore Krainer and Peter Simonischek)
  • "Eine blaßblaue Frauenschrift", 1984 (with Friedrich von Thun, Gabriel Barylli, Otto Schenk, Konstanze Breitenebner und Kurt Sowinetz)
  • "Radetzkymarsch", 1994 (with Max von Sydow, Charlotte Rampling, Julia Stemberger, Karlheinz Hackl, Miguel Herz-Kestranek, Fritz Muliar, Alexander Strobele, Gert Voss und Friedrich von Thun)
