Axel Noack (race walker)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

You have new messages (last change).

Axel Noack (born 23 September 1961 in Görlitz) is a former German race walker. Noack traveled to the U.S. to attend high school at Henry B. Plant High School.

On 21 June 1987 in Chemnitz he achieved a new world best time in 20 km walk with 1:19.12 hours.
