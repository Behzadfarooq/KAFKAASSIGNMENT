The croclings is about little creatures known as the croclings. The male characters are Colin, Skipper, Timmy, Frosty, Lakey, Tony, Nipper, Milo, Sammy and Squiggles, the female characters are Melanie, Penelope, Winona, Sandy, Fransika and Kaiko. The croclings like adventures and will some day be on tv. Is there hope in the future? The croclings all started with Colin, a purple crocodile, he went on lots of adventures, then later on Skipper, Timmy, Melanie and Nipper were created, then Winona, then Sandy and Sammy. Then Frosty and then Lakey. Colin appears to be the mainest as he was the first invented and the crocling that started it all, Colin's always seems to get his friends in a lot of adventures. His best friends are Skipper and Melanie.

[edit] Main Characters

[edit] Colin Cooper

Gender:Male

Colin is the mainest crocling and was the first crocling to be invented. He is close friends with Skipper and Melanie. Colin seems to have a crush on the Crocling Town princess Melanie.


[edit] Wanda Whipper

Gender:Female

Wanda was the second crocling to be invented, she was once considered to be Colin's mother, who wore an apron to carry Colin in, now she's a western cow girl that likes to whip her friends (,especially Colin) with her whip. She also has a laso that can be handy for when Skipper blows away as seen in Fly Away Skipper.


[edit] Skipper Paymo

Gender:Male

Skipper was one of the first croclings to be croclings, he's a very close friend to Fransika and wears a little pin wheel hat and red shirt. His hat has the ability to lift him off the ground and make him float around, which can take him high into the sky, making him unable to get down when it's windy as seen in Fly Away Skipper. He's the second youngest crocling.


[edit] Melanie Melmer

Gender:Female

House:Palace

Melanie is also one of the first croclings to be created and she lives in a palace. She seems to be best friends with Penelope, WInona and Colin. She also has a baby sister named Fransika.

[edit] Sammy Soppa

Gender:Male

House:Blue Tent

Sammy seems to really like hot chocolate and visits Milo's hot chocolate cafe. Sammy's favorite holiday seems to be halloween, as he owns a pumpkin patch, he uses it to make jackolaturns and he loves halloween candy. His favorite halloween costume is his pumpkin.

[edit] Sandy Soppa

Gender:Female

House: Blue Tent

Sandy is Sammy's twin, but unlike Sammy, she is allergic to candy, but she really likes sand and gold. She still goes trick or treating but would never eat the candy. She appears to be best friends with Kaiko. She has golden hair and a fish shaped pony tail, she also owns a gold mine under her blue tent. She thinks candy is a waste of time, except on halloween.

[edit] Squiggles

Gender:Male

House:Blue Tent

Squiggles closest friend is Sandy, Squiggles is made of solid gold and Sandy found him in the episode Squiggly Critter.

[edit] Timmy Paymo

Gender:Male

House:Salon

Timmy is the smartest crocling in town, except his eyesight is completly rubbish if he is not wearing glasses. He is the crocling town scientist and a know it all.

[edit] Penelope Paymo

Gender:Female

House:Salon

Penelope is smart like her one year old brother Timmy, except Penelope spends more time reading books, She is Timmy's research assitint. Unlike Timmy, Penelope can see without glasses on, but she can't read without glasses. Penelope has a fondness for trying new hairdos, she also enjoys basketball.

[edit] Frosty

Gender:Male

Frosty is said to be not a real crocling, he lives on a snow mountain and is allergic to almost everything, he is Lakey's best friend. He calls himself the world's best ice hockey champion, but in the episode Frosty VS Timmy, he lost, as sometimes he lose focusness and gloat, which made Timmy win by breaking his leg. Timmy's closest friends are Lakey and Kaiko and his worst enime is Timmy. He is said to be half crocling, half yeti. He is also part of an evil gang. His favorite holiday is christmas.

[edit] Lakey

Gender:Male

Lakey is completly made of water, he likes to wear a swim suit and flippers. He likes to surf, swim, water ski and do water tricks. He loves the rain and can turn to ice in the winter. His favorite color is turquise and he loves to go to the beach in the summer time, except he doesn't like to hot or he'll dry up. He also crys alot and can flood the place with tears.



[edit] Nipper

Gender:Male

Nipper was one of the first croclings to be created and he always seems to nip the croclings, he also turns into a werewolf at a full moon.





[edit] Tony

Gender:Male

Tony is flirtatious and flirts with every girl he sees except Fransika. No crocling really likes him, as he is Female crazy.





[edit] Kaiko

Gender:Female

Kaiko comes out everynight to do spanish dancing, sometimes the croclings come out to see her dance. She will dance to almost every little noise she hears with out even wanting to know what it is, as seen in Dance Kaiko dance, when her passion for dancing made her dance to the sound of a bee.






[edit] Winona

Gender:Female

Winona loves fassion and jewerly, she is the crocling movie star. Her biggest fan seems to be Sandy, as seen in the episode Star Sandy, Snooze Sammy.




[edit] Milo

Gender:Male

House:Cafe

Milo owns the crocling town cafe, he has chocolate covered hair and makes crocling towns best hot chocolate. Sammy is a total fan of his hot chocolate.

[edit] Other Characters

[edit] Choco

Gender: Male

Choco is Milo's big brother, he raised Milo after their parants died and then Milo left his home to go to crocling town. Choco taught Milo everything he knows.

[edit] Glacia

Gender:Female

Glacia is Frosty's cousin, though she's not really half crocling, half yeti. She enjoys to play with Frosty in the snow, she used to be Frosty's worst enime.


[edit] Avalaunch

Gender: Male

He is the leader of the evil gang that Frosty is part of. He is a whole yeti and he is evil, he lives on the evil gang.



[edit] Icy and Icyicle

Gender: Males

Icy and Icyicle are members of the evil gang, they are identical twins. Like Frosty, they are half crocling, half yeti.
