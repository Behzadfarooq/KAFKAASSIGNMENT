Axel Miller

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Axel Miller (b. 20 February 1965, Uccle, Belgium) is a Belgian businessman. Since January 2006, he is the chief executive officer and chairman of the Management Board of Dexia S.A.

[edit] Education

In 1987, Axel Miller obtained a Master degree in Law from the Université Libre de Bruxelles (ULB), Brussels, Belgium.

[edit] Career

Axel Miller started his career in 1987 as a lawyer working for the legal firm Simont & Simont. In September 1990, he joined Davis Polk & Wardwell, first in New York (United States) then in Paris (France). He went to work as a lawyer at the the bar in Brussels in January 1991. In December 1991, he stated working for Stibbe Simont Monahan Duhot in Brussels, where he became a partner in 1996, a member of the recruitment committee and then member of the Management Board. In May 1999, he joined Clifford Chance in Brussels as an associate.

He joined the Dexia Group in 2001 as General Counsel. In January 2002, he became a member of the Management Board of Dexia Bank, and in January 2003 he became Chairman of the Management Board of Dexia Bank and Head of Personal Financial Services at Group level. Axel Miller is Vice-President of the Belgian Banking Association and a member of the Board of Directors of Febelfin. He is also a Director of several Dexia Group companies, of Ethias, of Crédit du Nord and of LVI Holding (Groupe Carmeuse).
