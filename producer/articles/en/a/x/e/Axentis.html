Axentis is the leading provider of enterprise governance, risk and compliance (GRC) software.[1]

Axentis Enterprise™(Ae) boasts the world's largest GRC user group, finding use in over 100 countries[2]. With over 50,000 Sarbanes-Oxley users, Axentis Enterprise is also the world's most widely adopted Sarbanes-Oxley solution. [3]

Gartner named Axentis to its “Cool Vendors in IT Services and Outsourcing 2006” in its Feb 2006 report for application of SaaS to compliance management.[4]

[edit] Products

Axentis Enterprise™(Ae)

The principal mandate the product addresses is the SOX 404 Compliance, with considerable support for SOX 302, HIPAA, CobiT.

Ae is offered excusively as a managed service, often referred to as Software as a Service (SaaS).

[edit] Clients

Clients include Bombardier Inc., Rockwell Collins, BP Corporation, AstraZeneca Pharmaceuticals LP, Novartis Pharmaceuticals Corporation , Tyco International, Schering-Plough, Armstrong World Industries, Fortune Brands,Arthur J. Gallagher & Co., First Data
