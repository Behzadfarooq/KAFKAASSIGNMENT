The Axe Valley Community is a non-selective mixed community college that specialises in business, located in Axminster in the South west of England.

[edit] Recent News

Since March 20th the houses have expanded their points system to include both Sports and Academic Achievements. The Houses now have Captains and Deputies, who co-ordinate the collection of points and make appearances in assemblies.
