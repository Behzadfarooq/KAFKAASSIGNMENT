[edit] AXN India and Korea

An AXN India does exist, and so does an AXN Korea. AXN India airs in the South Asian region, AXN Korea airs in South Korea. AXN Asia airs in the East Asian region.

Heres the official website of AXN India - http://www.axn-india.com/ and here is the official webisite of AXN Korea - http://www.axntv.co.kr/, and this is the official website of AXN Asia - http://www.axn-asia.com/.

And AXN India and AXN Asia have totally different schedules, AXN India Schedule - http://www.axn-india.com/schedule/index.php, and AXN Asia Schedule - http://www.axn-asia.com/schedule/index.php. (Unsigned comment)
