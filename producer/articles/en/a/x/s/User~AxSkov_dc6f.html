[edit] Who am I?

G'day my name is Axel Skovdam (pronounced: /æksəl sgɔudəm/) and I live in a town in the Macedon Ranges (near Melbourne), Victoria, Australia. The things I do in Wikipedia are: create pages, add information to pages, correct spelling, reword, fix grammar, fix/update links (to avoid redirects) and participate in votes.

My wikiname is a combination of my first and last names, and I go by the signature Axman.

For more about me, see my Userboxes.
