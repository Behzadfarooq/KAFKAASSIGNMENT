The Amman National School (ANS) is a private educational institution located in (Dabouq) Amman, Jordan. It was established in 1985 in an old building and included only Junior Grades, and expanded since then to a new building, all 12 grades plus Kindergarten for pre-education.

ANS is Accredited by the Commission on International and Trans-Regional Accreditation (CITA).

ANS Offered IGCSE exams until 2006/2007. Starting from the school year 2007/2008, it will start offering the IB Diploma Program for grades 11-12.
