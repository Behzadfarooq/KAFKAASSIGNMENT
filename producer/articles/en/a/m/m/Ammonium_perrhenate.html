Ammonium perrhenate (APR) is a salt of rhenium, NH4ReO4.

This is the most common form in which rhenium is traded. Rhenium content of APR is around 69.0 - 69.4%. Pure rhenium powder can be produced from APR simply by reducing it with hydrogen.
