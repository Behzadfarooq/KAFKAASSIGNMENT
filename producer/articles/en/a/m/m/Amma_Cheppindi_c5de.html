Amma Cheppindi(Telugu: అమ్మ చెప్పింది,English: Mother Said So) is a 2006 Tollywood film written and directed by Gangaraju Gunnam. Sharwanand, Shriya Reddy, and Suhasini play important roles with Nagendra Babu with a vital role.

[edit] Synopsis

Spoiler warning: Plot and/or ending details follow.

Bose (Sharwanand) is a 21 year old boy who is mentally half grown. He is the son of a scientist (Pavan Malhotra). Bose’s mother (Suhasini) loves his son and takes care of him. Bose’s younger brother is a genius who stands as a topper all the time. The family lives in a defense research colony. Only a few in the colony likes Bose. Many dislike him for his ignorance and foolishness. Bose almost behaves like a 10 year old guy. But finally, Bose accomplishes something great. He bags praises not only from the people of his colony but also gets salutes from everyone in the country. Thus, the film proves that even the mentally retarded individuals can serve the nation.
