AMT AutoMag IV

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The AMT Automag IV is a large, single action semi-automatic handgun. It was created by Harry Sanford, who also invented the original automag. This model fires the .45 Winchester Magnum round. It has a 7 shot magazine and is, like all of the AMT guns, made of stainless steel.

[[1]]

  • Weight: 2.9 lbs (1.3 kg)
  • Caliber: .45 Winchester Magnum
  • Barrel: 6.5 inch (16.7 cm)
  • Action: Single action
  • Range: 165 ft (50 m)
  • Payload: 7 + 1
  • Cost: $ 700 (USD)
  • Made in: USA
  • Special: Only in stainless steel, adjustable sights standard.
