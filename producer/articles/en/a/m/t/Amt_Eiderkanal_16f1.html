Eiderkanal is a municipality in the district of Rendsburg-Eckernförde, in Schleswig-Holstein, Germany. It is situated on the Kiel Canal, approx. 2 km southeast of Rendsburg.

Osterrönfeld is also the seat of the Amt ("collective municipality") Osterrönfeld, which consists of the following municipalities:

  1. Bovenau
  2. Haßmoor
  3. Ostenfeld (Rendsburg)
  4. Osterrönfeld
  5. Rade b. Rendsburg
  6. Schacht-Audorf
  7. Schülldorf
