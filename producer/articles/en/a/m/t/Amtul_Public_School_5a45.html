St Amtul Public School Nainital is a residential school providing public school education.

[edit] Overview

Amtul Public School Nainital was established in 1983. Founder Haji Abdul Wajid Saheb, an egalitarian by virtue, lived a life consecrated to the cause of education and upliftment of the down trodden in the society. Knowing quality modern education is the sole constituent of a healthy society, he established Amtul’s Public School in 1983.

[edit] Mission

Amtul’s Public School, aims at imparting education with a firm moral base. Learning through better teaching facilities. Academic excellence by offering wide syllabus. Inculcating a sense of nationality, unity in diversity and a desire for integration of regional, linguistic and ideological heterogeneity. Physical fitness through various sports, thus developing all facets of a child’s personality. Our motto “Lead Kindly Light”.

[edit] Campus

Amtul's Public School boasts of a sprawling campus with an edifice reminiscent of a bygone era, surrounded by many modern infrastructures which fulfill all the requisites of CBSE pattern of English Medium Schools in India. The campus consists of a large Class Room Block, Laboratories, Hostel, Mess, Library, Administrative block, Health Center, Principal’s Residence and Staff Quarters. Sufficient facilities have been provided for a well furnished Library, Laboratories, Art & Music Room, a large Audio Visual Room and for Outdoor & Indoor Games.

[edit] Sports

An integral part of the school curriculum is Physical Education. This education is imparted by a qualified and trained instructor. He is also assisted by specialized coaches. It is compulsory for every student to be present at these classes. It is also offered as an optional subject in classes XI and XII. The boarders of the school are put through daily routines, twice a day - weather permitting.

With emphasis on physical fitness Amtul’s Public School pays special attention in training boys in indoor and outdoor games under the supervision of P.T.I.'s and coaches. Boys are trained in Cricket, Football, Hockey, Basketball, Volleyball, Badminton, Karate and Taekwondo. Besides these many indoor games like Table Tennis, Billiards, Snooker, Chess and Carom are also played. The school participates in all the competitive events organized by the local sports authority, and also in Inter-school meets. The school holds several cherished running trophies in Inter-school Tournaments.
