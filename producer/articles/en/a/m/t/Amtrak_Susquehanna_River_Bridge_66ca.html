Amtrak Susquehanna River Bridge
Carries Amtrak Northeast Corridor
Crosses Susquehanna River
Locale Havre de Grace, Maryland and Perryville, Maryland

The Amtrak Susquehanna River Bridge carries Amtrak's Northeast Corridor line across the Susquehanna River between Havre de Grace, Maryland and Perryville, Maryland
