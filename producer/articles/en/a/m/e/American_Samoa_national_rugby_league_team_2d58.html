The American Samoa national rugby league team has been participating in international competition since 1988. This team is not be confused with the Samoan (or Western Samoan) rugby league teams.

[edit] Competitions

American Samoa has participated in:

  • Pacific Cup (since 1988)
  • World Sevens (1996, 1997; entered the Qualification Tournament in 2003 and 2004) and the VB Sevens in 2005
