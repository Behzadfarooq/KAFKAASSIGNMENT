[edit] Zombie flava

This article is called America First Party (1944) but the text states that it was founded in 1943. This page [2] states that it was founded some time between 1939 and 1941. The article does not say what the party stood for. It does not give any details of the party's manifesto. It does not mention the America First Committee. Ourcampaigns.com is described as a "well-respected website" yet the cited article about the party [3] is credited to a writer called pimpalicious, and the site is clearly an obscure wiki of some kind. -Ashley Pomeroy 18:08, 4 January 2007 (UTC)
