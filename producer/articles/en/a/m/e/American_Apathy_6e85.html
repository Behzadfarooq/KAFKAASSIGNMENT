American Apathy is the fourth full-length studio album by the band dope. It was released July 26, 2005 on Artemis Records.

[edit] Trivia

If purchased at Best Buy, the American Apathy limited edition is a single-disc album featuring all 23 tracks from the two discs listed above. A DVD featuring dope videos and concert footage is also included in the package.
