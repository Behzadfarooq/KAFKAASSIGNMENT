Americano do Brasil is a small town and municipality in eastern Goiás state, Brazil.

  • Population: 4,978 (2005 estimate)
  • Total area: 134.0 km²
  • Elevation: The elevation of the town was 890 meters
  • Became a city: 1980.
  • Postal code: 76165-000

Americano do Brasil is located in the Anicuns Microregion, which is northwest of the state capital, Goiânia. It is 37 kilometers southwest of Itaberaí and 113 kilometers northwest of Goiânia.

Highway connections from Goiânia are made by GO-060 / Trindade / GO-326 / Anicuns / GO-156. [1]

Neighboring municipalities are:

  • north and west: Mossâmedes
  • south: Anicuns
  • east: Itaberaí and Itauçu

Economy

The main economic activities are cattle raising (19,054 head in 2003) and agriculture, especially corn, sugarcane, rice, and bananas.

  • bananas: 80 hectares
  • rice: 300 hectares
  • sugarcane: 1,890 hectares
  • manioc: 430 hectares
  • corn: 900 hectares

Source: [2]

  • Gross Domestic Product: 18.15 million Reais, which was 0.06% of the state GDP of 31.298 in 2002.
  • GDP per capita: 3,664 Reais, which was lower than the state GDP per capita of 5,921 Reais in 2002. For information on the municipalities in Goiás see Portal Sepin

The major entrepreneurial activities employing workers were: 9 units of transformation employing 19 workers and 68 units of commerce employing 139 workers.
