Welcome!

Hello, AmericanAtl, and welcome to Wikipedia! Thank you for your contributions. I hope you like the place and decide to stay. If you are stuck, and looking for help, please come to the Wikipedia Boot Camp, where experienced Wikipedians can answer any queries you have! Or, you can just type {{helpme}} on your user page, and someone will show up shortly to answer your questions.

Here are a few more good links for to help you get started at Wikipedia:

  • How to edit a page
  • Help pages
  • Tutorial

It appears that you are interested in editing our road articles. If you are, then here is a list of pages that can help you get started:

  • Highways Project (worldwide)
  • U.S. Roads Project (U.S. only)
  • Canada Roads Project (Canada only)
  • U.S. state highway naming conventions
    • Pipe trick (used with the link above)

Additional road article-related questions can be answered at the following links: United States, Canada, or other countries. You may also ask questions at my talk page.

I hope you enjoy editing here and being a Wikipedian and a roadfan! Please sign your name on talk pages using four tildes (~~~~); this will automatically produce your name and the date. Again, welcome!  --Rschen7754 (talk - contribs) 20:22, 10 February 2007 (UTC)
