American Garage is the second album by the Pat Metheny Group, released in 1980.

[edit] Track listing

All tracks by Lyle Mays and Pat Metheny:

  1. "(Cross the) Heartland" – 6:55
  2. "Airstream" – 6:20
  3. "The Search" – 4:54
  4. "American Garage" – 4:13
  5. "The Epic" – 12:59

[edit] Personnel

  • Pat Metheny - Guitar, 12 String Guitar, Bass Guitar
  • Lyle Mays - Piano, Keyboards, Organ, Oberheim, Autoharp
  • Mark Egan - Bass Guitar
  • Dan Gottlieb - Drums
