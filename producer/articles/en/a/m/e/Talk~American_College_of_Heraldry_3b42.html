[edit] Pressing Needs

Um, are there really "pressing needs" for heraldry?--12.172.37.45

For those of us with knowledge of our family history, and for those of us who are fortunate enough to have a "heraldic past," yes there is indeed a need for an organization to act as a repository and to assist individuals who would like more information about the history and art of heraldry.--64.12.116.72
