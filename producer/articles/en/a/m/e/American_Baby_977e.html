"American Baby" is a song by the Dave Matthews Band from their 2005 album Stand Up. It was the first single released from the album. It is also featured on the Dave Matthews Band album The Best of What's Around Vol. 1.

The song, written the day after U.S. president George W. Bush won re-election in 2004, encourages Americans not to lose their culture in the midst of political and social trouble.

Although American Baby is preceeded by American Baby Intro on Stand Up, that song is not actaully an introduction into American Baby per se. In concert, Intro is often played by itself as an all instrumental song that begins with a violin solo by Boyd Tinsley and grows until it reaches a frenetic pace featuring Carter Beauford's work on the drums and Dave's screaming.
