This category is for American civilians who are associated with or had a significant impact on World War II; or military personnel at the strategic level of operations or who impacted World War II without actually participating in the war (i.e. served as stateside intelligence analyst, etc.). For individuals who actually fought in World War II, see Category:American World War II veterans
