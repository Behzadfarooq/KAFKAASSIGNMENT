America Now is a former politics and business TV program on CNBC with Lawrence Kudlow and James Cramer.

The program's name was later changed to Kudlow & Cramer.

America Now: the Anthropology of a Changing Culture was the original title of Why Nothing Works: The Anthropology of Daily Life (ISBN 0-671-63577-8), a book by the anthropologist Marvin Harris on American sociology.
