American Music Award nominations for Whitney Houston

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

You have new messages (last change).

Following is a list of American Music Award nominations and wins for pop/R&B icon Whitney Houston. Wins are in bold. Currently, Houston is the solo and female artist with the most wins at the awards show, with a total of 20 awards.

[edit] 1986

  1. Favorite Soul/R&B Single: "You Give Good Love"
  2. Favorite Soul/R&B Video: "Saving All My Love For You"
  3. Favorite Pop/Rock Female Artist
  4. Favorite Soul/R&B Female Artist
  5. Favorite Soul/R&B Video Artist
  6. Favorite Soul/R&B Album (Whitney Houston)

[edit] 1987

  1. Favorite Pop/Rock Female Artist
  2. Favorite Pop/Rock Album (Whitney Houston)
  3. Favorite Soul/R&B Album (Whitney Houston)
  4. Favorite Soul/R&B Female Artist
  5. Favorite Soul/R&B Video ("Greatest Love of All")
  6. Favorite Pop/Rock Video Artist
  7. Favorite Soul/R&B Video Artist

[edit] 1988

  1. Favorite Pop/Rock Female Artist
  2. Favorite Pop/Rock Single ("I Wanna Dance with Somebody (Who Loves Me)")
  3. Favorite Soul/R&B Female Artist

[edit] 1989

  1. Favorite Pop/Rock Female Artist
  2. Favorite Soul/R&B Female Artist

[edit] 1992

  1. Favorite Pop/Rock Female Artist
  2. Favorite Soul/R&B Female Artist
  3. Favorite Soul/R&B Album (I'm Your Baby Tonight)
  4. Favorite Adult Contemporary Artist
  5. Favorite Adult Contemporary Album (I'm Your Baby Tonight)

[edit] 1994

  1. Favorite Pop/Rock Female Artist

  3. Favorite Pop/Rock Album (The Bodyguard)
  4. Favorite Soul/R&B Female Artist
  5. Favorite Soul/R&B Single ("I Will Always Love You")
  6. Favorite Soul/R&B Album (The Bodyguard)
  7. Favorite Adult Contemporary Artist
  8. Favorite Adult Contemporary Album (The Bodyguard)
  9. Award of Merit

[edit] 1997

  1. Favorite Adult Contemporary Artist

[edit] 2000

  1. Favorite Pop/Rock Female Artist
  2. Favorite Soul/R&B Female Artist
  3. Favorite Soul/R&B Album (My Love Is Your Love)

[edit] 2001

  1. Favorite Soul/R&B Female Artist
