The American Hellenic Educational Progressive Association is a service organization founded on 26 July. 1922 in Atlanta, Georgia.

Its goal is to support Greek-American charities, causes, and communities. It works closely with the Greek Orthodox Church.

Its lady's auxiliary, Daughters of Penelope was founded in 1929. It supports two youth groups, the Sons of Pericles (established in 1926) and the Maids of Athena (1930).
