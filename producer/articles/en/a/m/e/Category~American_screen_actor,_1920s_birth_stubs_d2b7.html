This category is for stub articles relating to United States film and television actors born in the 1920s. You can help Wikipedia by expanding them.

To add an article to this category, use {{US-screen-actor-1920s-stub}} instead of {{stub}}.

If an American actor is known for specifically for either film or television please use instead one of:

  • {{US-film-actor-1920s-stub}}
  • {{US-tv-actor-1920s-stub}}

The perfect article about an actor gives both a (possibly short) biography and a list of performances. Also, appropriate categories should be added, especially those listed in Category:Actors by medium and Category:American actors.
