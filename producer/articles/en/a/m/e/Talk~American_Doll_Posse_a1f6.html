[edit] Unconfirmed Track Listings:

Please stop posting track listings until it can be sourced from an official site. Such sites would include www.toriamos.com, www.everythingtori.com, or other official Sony/Epic web sites. The track list that has been posted up here several times has been confirmed at this point by one person who claims to have spoken to a representative of Sony in the Netherlands. While this may very well be true it is hardly an official track list and should not be regarded as anything but rumor until confirmed by official sources. —The preceding unsigned comment was added by Hazrek (talk • contribs) 21:35, 9 March 2007 (UTC).
