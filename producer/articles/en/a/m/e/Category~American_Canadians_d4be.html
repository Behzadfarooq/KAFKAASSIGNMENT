This page lists Canadian citizens of full American origin - with American parents. It also includes those born in the U.S. to a Canadian parent or parents who later obtained Canadian citizenship.

For Canadian-born Canadians of partial American origin (one parent, grandparent, etc.) see Category:Canadians of American descent.
