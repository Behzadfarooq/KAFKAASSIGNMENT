Amelia Bence

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Amelia Belce (b.13 November 1919 Buenos Aires ) is an Argentine film actress.

[edit] Career

A major actress in Argentina she has worked consistently throughout 8 decades of film ever since 1933 making her last appearance in 2004.

In 1947 she appeared in A sangre fría with actor Tito Alonso.
