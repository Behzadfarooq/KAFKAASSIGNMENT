An early solo concert from Lou Reed, recorded live at the Hampstead Theatre on Boxing Day - 12-26-72, during the Transformer tour. Features material from that album, The Velvet Underground, his 1972 solo debut Lou Reed. The version of "Berlin" is from that debut album. 2001 release.

Cover photo from Mick Rock, evidently from the same shoot that yielded the Transformer cover.
