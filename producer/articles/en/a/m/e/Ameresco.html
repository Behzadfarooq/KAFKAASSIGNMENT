Ameresco is an energy services company, headquartered in Framingham, MA, recognized as one of the leading firms in the energy services industry.

[edit] History

[edit] Beginnings of the company

Begun in 2000 by George Sakellaris, Ameresco has grown to be one of the leading energy servicing providers in North America.

Since it's inception in 2000, Ameresco has acquired 5 companies. In 2000, Ameresco had acquired Citizen's Conservation Services. In 2002, the company had acquired DukeSolutions. In 2003, Ameresco had bought both LG & E Enertech and E-Three. Their latest acquisition of Planergy Housing was in 2004.
