[edit] Amber Alert International TM (AAI)

A free education and warning system by JM Thrasher. Designed in 2003 and brought on line in 2004. In it's first two years, it has been shown to be 180 times faster response than the current Amber Alert System. See [1] for more information. "Amber" in our "Amber Alert International" stands for "All Missing Bodies Equaly Represented"

== GLOBAL EDUCATION ONLINE & STATE OF THE ART ID SYSTEM == Now used by families in over 65 countries to educate their kids to prevent abductions. In the website, you see the AMBER ALERT ID CARD TM / AMBER ALERT CARD TM [2] and the AMBER ALERT ID KIT / AMBER ALERT CHILD ID KIT TM [3] and other kits/cards created by the founder.

Trademarks filed with the USPTO and AAI has many web domains to build one of the largest systems for child safety. See the web for our trademarks or ebay.

When we wanted to join forces with the group NCMEC, they said we would have to donate $500,000 to be a partner. WOW!!! Simply - we did not have it. So we moved forward without them.

Two years later when we tried to give them our CPPIDS TM system (at cost of deveolpment $40,000), they said no and that they would never work with AAI, or a any small group like us, so we will keep moving forward. Our heart is in the right place and our hand is not deep in UNCLE SAMS POCKET.


[edit] National Center For Missing Children And Adults TM (NCMCAA)

Now, without any big group, every parent can list every MISSING CHILDREN and ADULTS in real time [4], and on 911ACCESS.COM [5] and others without waiting or pleading for their child or loved one to be listed. NCMCAA is not part of AAI, but developed by the same founder. Still FREE.

== Our network is FREE ==. You can enter missing persons FREE, NO DELAY!!

Emergency Shelters, FEMA, Family, Employers and Police can contact displaced families, leaving messages to and from members.
