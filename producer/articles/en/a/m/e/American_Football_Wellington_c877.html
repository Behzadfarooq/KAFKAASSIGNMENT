American Football Wellington

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

American Football Wellington(AFW) is a fully-kitted American football league in New Zealand. Founded in 1980 as the American Football Southern Conference, it consists of four member clubs. The league generally runs from November through February ending with two teams competing for the Capital Bowl.
