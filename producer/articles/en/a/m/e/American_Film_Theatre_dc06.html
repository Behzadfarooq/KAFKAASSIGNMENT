American Film Theatre

American Film Theatre is a limited run series of filmed plays, produced by Ely A. Landau. The films were contracted to be exhibited in 500 theaters in 400 cities, admission based on subscription to the entire series[1].

[edit] Releases

  • The Man in the Glass Booth (1975)
  • In Celebration (1975)
  • Galileo (1975)
  • Jacques Brel Is Alive and Well and Living in Paris (1975)
  • Philadelphia, Here I Come (1975)
  • Lost in the Stars (1974)
  • Rhinoceros (1974)
  • Butley (1974)
  • The Maids (1974)
  • A Delicate Balance (1973)
  • The Homecoming (1973)
  • The Iceman Cometh (1973)
  • Luther (1973)
  • Three Sisters (1970) (US release by AFT in 1974)

The films were released on DVD by Kino Video.
