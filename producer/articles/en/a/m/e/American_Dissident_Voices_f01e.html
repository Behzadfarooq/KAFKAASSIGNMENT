American Dissident Voices is the name of two different radio and internet radio broadcasts. One broadcast is delivered weekly by the National Alliance. The other broadcast is also delivered weekly by National Vanguard. Kevin Strom was fired from the National Alliance in April, 2005, where upon he formed his own group and continued a weekly program under the same name. His show became less regular, from once a week to once a month and was finally discontinued in late 2006. Some critiques claimed the Kevin Strom stole the show, while other critiques claim that Shaun Walker stole the show. Strom's show was never aired over the radio, only the Internet.

Both groups have accurately been accused of anti-Semitism, white supremacy, and homophobia, amongst other crimes.

Show is heard on the Internet at:

http://www.natall.com


This program was started in 1992, is 30-minutes long, a new one every Saturday.

Host of Program for National Alliance:

1997 - 2002 Dr. William Pierce (died July, 2002)

2002-2005 (2nd time) Kevin Alfred Strom

2005 - 2006 Shaun Walker

2006 - Present Erich Gliebe


Host of Program for National Vanguard:

2005 - 2006 Kevin Alfred Strom
