American Film Institute's 50 Greatest Screen Legends

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Greatest Screen Legends were actors and actresses selected by the American Film Institute for their popularity, craftmanship in their career, star quality, legacy and their role in society. The Top 50 Greatest Screen Legends was composed of 25 men and 25 women selected from a list of 250 men and 250 women nominees.

Below is the list of the 50 Greatest Screen Legends:

  1. Katharine Hepburn / Humphrey Bogart
  2. Bette Davis / Cary Grant
  3. Audrey Hepburn / James Stewart
  4. Ingrid Bergman / Marlon Brando
  5. Greta Garbo / Fred Astaire
  6. Marilyn Monroe / Henry Fonda
  7. Elizabeth Taylor / Clark Gable
  8. Judy Garland / James Cagney
  9. Marlene Dietrich / Spencer Tracy
  10. Joan Crawford / Charlie Chaplin
  11. Barbara Stanwyck / Gary Cooper
  12. Claudette Colbert / Gregory Peck
  13. Grace Kelly / John Wayne
  14. Ginger Rogers / Laurence Olivier
  15. Mae West / Gene Kelly
  16. Vivien Leigh / Orson Welles
  17. Lillian Gish / Kirk Douglas
  18. Shirley Temple / James Dean
  19. Rita Hayworth / Burt Lancaster
  20. Lauren Bacall / The Marx Brothers
  21. Sophia Loren / Buster Keaton
  22. Jean Harlow / Sidney Poitier
  23. Carole Lombard / Robert Mitchum
  24. Mary Pickford / Edward G. Robinson
  25. Ava Gardner / William Holden
