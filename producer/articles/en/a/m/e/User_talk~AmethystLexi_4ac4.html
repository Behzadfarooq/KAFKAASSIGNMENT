[edit] Secret (Ayumi Hamasaki album)

Please do not convert names to uppercase. This has been discussed several times, you can see the main discussion here, plus other smaller discussions here (WikiProject Japan) and here (J-pop article), here (WikiProject Songs) and here (WikiProject Albums). Also at Amuro Namie and Zard. In the end, the consensus is that we should fit the names according to our manual of style for trademarks and the naming conventions for albums and bands, that request us to use proper capitalization regardless of how it has been trademarked. -- ReyBrujo 16:59, 20 January 2007 (UTC)

During months we have been working on this. Usually anonymous users change everything from proper case to uppercase because the album cover features the name in uppercase. As I said, it has been agreed that we should follow Wikipedia conventions. Thus, if you find articles where song or album names are in uppercase, you can convert them into lowercase. Thanks for understanding. -- ReyBrujo 17:17, 20 January 2007 (UTC)
