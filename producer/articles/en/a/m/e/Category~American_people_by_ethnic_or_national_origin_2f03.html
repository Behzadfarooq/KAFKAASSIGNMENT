This category includes mostly subcategories. It also includes articles on aboriginal Americans.

This category does NOT include articles on American people who have emigrated to other countries.

Style note: There is no consensus to add a hyphen to those categories which lack it ("Foo Americans"); there was some consensus to add a hyphen to those categories that have it ("Foo-Americans").
