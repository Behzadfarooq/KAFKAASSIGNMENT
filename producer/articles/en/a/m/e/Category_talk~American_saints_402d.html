[edit] List of American Saints

A list of American Saints was published in the 12 Oct 2006 edition of USA Today; this list contains the following names (year of canonization indicated):

  • St. Katharine Drexel (2000)
  • St. Rose Philippine Duchesne (1988)
  • St. John Neumann (1977)
  • St. Elizabeth Ann Seton (1975)
  • St. Frances Xavier Cabrini (1946)
  • St. Isaac Jogues (1930)
  • St. Rene Goupil (1930)

Citation: The news article cites five sources in the table containing the information here. Kim, Theodore, Robert King. "Path to sainthood: Indiana nun's amazing story is recognized", USA Today, 2006-10-12, p. 7D. Retrieved on October 22, 2006. --User:Ceyockey (talk to me) 23:44, 22 October 2006 (UTC)
