This page is about language and includes a bunch of articles about language and the English language in particular.

  • Linguistics - the study of language
  • Linguistic universal - things common to all human languages

-

  • Phonology - the study of the sounds in a spoken language
  • Phonotactic - Rules of a language as to what sounds can be used
  • International phonetic alphabet - alphabet of all possible sounds
  • Phonetics - the study of human made sounds
  • Articulatory phonetics - where the sounds are made

-

  • English phonology - the rules and sounds of the English language
  • English pronunciation - interesting description of which sounds can go where
  • General American - pronunciation table for USA
  • American English - with various pronunciation rules
  • English language - ideas on stress and alternate meanings
  • IPA chart for English - very nice IPA chart for English

-

  • Voice (phonetics) - list of voiced and voiceless consonants
  • Sorority Sequencing Principle - quiet to loud vowel to quiet creates a single syllable
  • Sonority Hierarchy - how loud the various sounds
  • Sonorant - a sound that can be held continuously

-

  • Segment - smallest discrete bit of sound in a conversation
  • Syllable - a vowel sound and any association consonant sounds that comprise a word or part of a word
  • Vowel - a sound made with the vocal cords that comprises the middle of a syllable
  • Consonant - a sound tied to a vowel
  • Consonant cluster - groups of consonant in a row, between vowel sounds
  • Phoneme - a group of very similar sounds that are considered to be the same or equivalent in a spoken language
  • Phone - a single speech sound

-

  • Verb_Object_Agent - apparently the most rare of the six possible orderings of agent verb and object
  • Writing Systems
  • List_of_writing_systems
