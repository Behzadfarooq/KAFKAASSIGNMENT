American Top Team, commonly refered to as ATT, is located in Florida. It is one of the top teams in mixed martial arts. Coaches and instructors include Brazilian jiu-jitsu specialist Ricardo Liborio and 1976 Olympic gold medalist Howard Davis Jr. ATT features professional fighters in the UFC and PRIDE Fighting Championships, among others.

Although the team was founded by some former members of Brazilian Top Team, ATT has no formal affiliation with them.[1]
