Category:American colonial people

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

This category incloudes people who, prior to the American Revolution, lived in the colonies that would become the United States.

People who participated in the Revolution are included in Category:People of the American Revolution.

This category requires continual maintenance to avoid becoming too large. It should list very few—if any—article pages directly, and should contain primarily sub-categories.
Please diffuse articles into relevant subcategories as needed.
