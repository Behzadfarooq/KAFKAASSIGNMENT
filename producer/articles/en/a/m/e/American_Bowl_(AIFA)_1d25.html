American Bowl (AIFA)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

American Bowl Championship Trophy won by Canton Legends in 2006

The American Bowl is the main championship game of the American Indoor Football Association. In 2005, it was played between the two remaining teams (since the league had six teams). Now, it's played between the champions of the Northern and Southern Conferences.

In the league's first year, the championship game was called the Governor's Cup.
