The America East Conference Men's Basketball Tournament is the annual concluding tournament for the NCAA college basketball in the America East Conference:

  • 1980: 1st round at campus sites; rest at Cumberland County Civic Center, Portland, Maine- Holy Cross
  • 1981: 1st round at campus sites; rest at Cabot Gym, Boston, Massachusetts - Northeastern
  • 1982: first rounds at campus sites; championship at Matthews Arena, Boston- Northeastern
  • 1983: first two rounds at campus sites; last two at Matthews Arena- Boston University
  • 1984: first rounds at campus sites; championship at Matthews Arena- Northeastern
  • 1985: same- Northeastern
  • 1986: same- Northeastern
  • 1987: same- Boston University
  • 1988: Hartford Civic Center, Hartford, Connecticut- Boston University
  • 1989: same- Siena
  • 1990: same- Boston University
  • 1991: first rounds at campus sites; championship at Matthews Arena- Northeastern
  • 1992: first rounds at campus sites; championship at Delaware Field House, Newark, Delaware- Delaware
  • 1993: first rounds at campus sites; championship at Drexel Athletic Center, Philadelphia, Pennsylvania- Delaware
  • 1994: same- Drexel
  • 1995: same- Drexel
  • 1996: same- Drexel
  • 1997: first rounds at Bob Carpenter Center, Newark; championship at Case Gym, Boston- Boston University
  • 1998: Bob Carpenter Center- Delaware
  • 1999: same- Delaware
  • 2000: first rounds at Bob Carpenter Center; championship at Hofstra Arena, Hempstead, New York- Hofstra
  • 2001: same- Hofstra
  • 2002: first rounds at Matthews Arena; championship at Case Gym- Boston University
  • 2003: first rounds at Walter Brown Arena, Boston; championship at Case Gym, Boston-Vermont
  • 2004: same- Vermont
  • 2005: first rounds at Binghamton University Events Center, Vestal, New York; rest at Patrick Gym, Burlington, Vermont- Vermont
  • 2006: first rounds at BU Events Center, championship at Recreation and Convocation Center, Albany, New York- Albany
  • 2007: first rounds scheduled for Agganis Arena, Boston; championship at Patrick Gym- Albany
