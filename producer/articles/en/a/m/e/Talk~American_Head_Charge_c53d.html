[edit] Genre

I know, this is always a POV issue, but I really disagree with their being labeled industrial metal. Their synthesizers aren't very prevalent, as they are with most industrial bands, and I think the ferocity of their music and the themes they use (the heavy, death metal-esque vocals mainly) place this band in the metalcore genre, maybe under moshcore (while I've never been to an AHC concert, I'll bet there's quite a bit of moshing going on). Lizardking42 19:16, 2 January 2007 (UTC)

Never mind, I checked their website and they list themselves as industrial heavy metal. Lizardking42 23:12, 17 January 2007 (UTC)

metalcore? they have mentioned themselves on their old webpage and in interviews that they are a nu-metal band, with "organic-industrial" influences. I think their original idea was to be a mix between the "organic" nature of traditional Metal and the "synthetic" industrial metal genre.
