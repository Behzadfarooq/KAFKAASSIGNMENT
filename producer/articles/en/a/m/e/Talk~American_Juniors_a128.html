[edit] Merge into American Idol article?

15:58, 6 December 2005 User:UrineForGas proposed that this article be merged into American Idol.

This would be totally inappropriate. American Juniors was a different series, with a different set of rules, a different kind of contestant, different judges, etc. Standard Wikipedia usage is to create new articles for spinoff TV series. See the Law & Order articles, the CSI articles, etc. for examples. I've removed the merge proposal tag. Wasted Time R 16:30, 6 December 2005 (UTC)
