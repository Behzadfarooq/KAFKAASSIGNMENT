AmeriPlanÂ® is a health care discount plan based in Plano, Texas. Ameriplan utilizes Multi-level marketing to promote and sell its fee-for-service plan. Independent Business Owners (IBOs) act as brokers for these plans and recruit other IBOs and members. Members are people who buy the actual discount plan service.

Ameriplan is a founding board member of the Consumer Health Alliance (CHA).
