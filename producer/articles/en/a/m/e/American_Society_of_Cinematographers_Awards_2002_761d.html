American Society of Cinematographers Awards 2002

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

17th American Society of Cinematographers Awards

February 16, 2003

The 17th American Society of Cinematographers Awards, given on 16 February 2003, honored the best cinematographers of 2002.

[edit] Winners

  • Outstanding Achievement in Cinematography in Theatrical Releases:
    • Road to Perdition - Conrad L. Hall (posthumously)
  • Outstanding Achievement in Cinematography in Movies of the Week/Mini-Series/Pilot for Network or Basic Broadcast TV:
    • CSI: Miami - "Cross Jurisdiction" - Michael Barrett
  • Outstanding Achievement in Cinematography in Episodic TV Series:
    • MDs - "Wing and a Prayer" - Robert Primes
  • Outstanding Achievement in Cinematography in Movies of the Week/Mini-Series/Pilot for Basic or Pay TV:
    • Last Call - Jeff Jur
  • Special Achievement Award:
    • Roger Ebert
  • Lifetime Achievement Award:
    • Bill Butler
  • Board of the Governors Award:
    • Norman Jewison
  • International Award:
    • Witold Sobocinski
  • President's Award:
    • Ralph Woolsey
