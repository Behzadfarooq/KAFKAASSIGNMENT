Amenhotep is the name of several Ancient Egyptian pharaohs of the 18th Dynasty:

  • Amenhotep I
  • Amenhotep II
  • Amenhotep III
  • Amenhotep IV (Akhenaten)

Amenhotep may also be:

  • Amenhotep son of Hapu, deified Ancient Egyptian architect
