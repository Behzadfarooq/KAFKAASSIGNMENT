This template allows stylized uniforms like those found with Template:Football kit. Editors can customize colors and patterns of uniforms. The elements of the uniform include:

  • Helmet
  • Jersey
    • Sleeves
    • Body
  • Pants

[edit] Syntax

[edit] Example home uniform

　
Notre Dame 
{{American football uniform|
  helmet=aaaa66|
  body=000033|
  pattern_b=_af_whitenumbers|
  leftarm=000033|
  rightarm=000033|
  pants=aaaa66|
  title=Notre Dame|
}}

[edit] Example away uniform

　
Rutgers 
{{American football uniform|
  helmet=990000|
  body=990000|
  pattern_b=_af_numbersonwhite|
  leftarm=ffffff|
  rightarm=ffffff|
  pants=ffffff|
  title=Rutgers|
}}

[edit] American football patterns

To create new patterns, add images as shown below.

[edit] Helmets

At this time, it is easiest if helmets only show main colors. Currently, the main helmet color (indicated by the helmet parameter, matches the facemask color.

_af


[edit] Jerseys

Jerseys are made up of two components: arms and body. Arms are a pair of images, in the form kit_leftarm_af_descriptor, where descriptor is something like "bluestripes," for example.

[edit] Sleeves/arms

[edit] Body

Jersey bodies should remain simple.

_af_numbersonblack


[edit] Pants

Pants should also remain simple. There are some ideas at Template:Baseball uniform that may be useful, however (e.g., colored belts).
