[edit] Availability

I don't intend to add this so people know where to buy these..but as info as to where they are sold...what do you think of this:

"The books still being published are made available through The Way's bookstore program or for private sale by some ex-members on sites such as Ebay. These books however are not made available through libraries or regular bookstores." Lsjzl 13:57, 4 January 2007 (UTC)

My questions are:
1) What books are still being published? Amazon counts all of VPW's books as "out of print." As far as I can tell, they're only available on the used book market (for prices up to $200 and more). But the other day at the Way's website, I noticed that they were offering Jesus Christ Our Promised Seed for sale to the general public.
And, 2) What's the "The Way's bookstore program"? When I tried to buy books over the phone, some time back, I was informed that I had to be a "follower."
The current policy of The Way International, of restricting access to its publications, is strange. Most publishers would like to sell as many books as possible, and with the used prices of VPW's books there must be a market for them. When I was involved, back when VPW was alive, the ministry had a program to sell American Christian Press books door-to-door. Since he died this publisher has become an odd duck. User:Jstanley01 16:44, 5 April 2007 (UTC)
