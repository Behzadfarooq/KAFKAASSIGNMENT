[edit] Amerime

First off, Wikipedia is not for advertising. The amerime article deals with American-based anime-style animation. If you were going to advertise, you picked the wrong page (should have picked Amerimanga). Second, Wikipedia is not for advertising. If you wish to discuss your works, you will have to create articles about your company and its works, not linkspam other articles.

For the record, I'm quite aware of your company and what it does. If you'll note on my user page, I'm trying to get an amerimanga published myself. But here, I have to act as a Wikipedia editor, and that means removing links that have no bearing on the article. My recommendation is to create an article about your company and individual pages about your series. But other companies have done the linkspam route and it's always ended up setting off other editors.

Perhaps I came down too hard, and if so, I apologize. But I can honestly say that your company isn't the first one that has done what you did, and has made a mess of things as a result. Your best bet is what I outlined above. If you need assistance with that, please let me know.--み使い Mitsukai 05:03, 11 March 2006 (UTC)
