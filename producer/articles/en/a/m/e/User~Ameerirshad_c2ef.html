[edit] Introduction | Who am I?

I'm a Dutchman, born 1976 (so calculate my age yourself). I have studied Human Geography, specialised in political & cultural geography in which geopolitics, environmental conflict studies and cultural studies are my main interest. I'm very spiritual, studied Judaism, Christianity, Buddhism (tibetan & zen), Taoism and Islam. In 2002 I reverted to Islam, for me a natural step in my development, which you can read on one of my blogs.
