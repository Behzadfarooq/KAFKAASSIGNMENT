[edit] Contestants' surnames

See also talk page of series

These have appeared for all contestants, but there's as yet no source..... How do we know how accurate this information is? Many of these have been inserted by IP editors, of which some have been accused of vandalism, and thus I deem potentially highly unreliable. All surnames have been removed for the moment. Kindly source adequately Ohconfucius 03:21, 23 February 2007 (UTC)

  • Some editors insist on making changes to the contestants' surnames without telling us where they got this information from. The surnames in the article now agree with the source once again. Any changes to those in the article should be properly and adequately sourced. Ohconfucius 10:22, 15 March 2007 (UTC)
  • That poptower website must have gotten their surname information from here. Cassandra's last name is Watson yet they still list her as Laing. They still list Renee's as DeWitt even though somebody here admitted that they just made it up. People keep listing Brittany's as Carrigher when a yearbook photo has shown that it is Hatch. Not to mention the website does not have a name for Felicia, nor does Wikipedia. Also, it still lists Jade and Danielle from Cycle 6 as Jade Roda and Danielle Mahoney. They are actually Jade Cole and Danielle Evans. That source is not reliable. 4.241.33.159 18:30, 19 March 2007 (UTC)
  • In the absence of reliable sources for surnames, they have once again been removed. Editors here have admitted to making these names up, or relied on grapevine sources. In addition, the discussions both here and on the main series' talk page indicate that neither poptower nor realityworld.com are reliable, as they appear to have taken the surnames from wikipedia. Using these, therefore would fail WP:SELF and also violate WP:A. I ask editors NOT TO REINSTATE these unless their surnames are sourced such from, say, mainstream media articles. Ohconfucius 02:08, 4 April 2007 (UTC)
