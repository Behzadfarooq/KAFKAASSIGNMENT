American Life Promo Tour

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Image:American Life Promo Tour.jpg
Madonna performing on the American Life Promo Tour

The American Life Promo Tour, several brief concert-like performances by Madonna, took place in 2003, between the months of April and May. The American Life Promo Tour promoted Madonna's ninth studio album, American Life, and preceded her sold-out 2004 Re-Invention Tour.

[edit] Venues

Madonna performed at several different locations during the Promo Tour.

  • MTV Times Square Studio, ("Madonna: On Stage and On The Record"), New York City, on April 22nd (same day as release of American Life)
  • Tower Records, New York City, on April 23rd
  • RTL Studios, ("Absolute Madonna"), Cologne, Germany, on April 30th
  • BBC 'Jonathan Ross', London, UK, on May 1st
  • ITV Studios, London, on May 3rd
  • Carerre Studios, Paris, France, on May 6th
  • La Cantine Du Faubourg, Paris, France on May 7th
  • HMV, London, UK, on May 9th
  • BBC (Top of the Pops), London, on May 15th.

[edit] Set list

Madonna performed several songs, not just from her most recent album, but from her previous Music album, as well as a few classics.


  • American Life
  • Hollywood
  • Nothing Fails
  • X-Static Process
  • Mother And Father
  • Music
  • Don't Tell Me
  • Like a Prayer
  • Like a Virgin (This song was only performed at Tower Records, by fan request, and in Paris.)
