American Global University School of Medicine

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The American Global University School of Medicine is an unaccredited Central American medical school (it has a mailing address in Belmopan, Belize) offering the Doctor of Medicine (M.D.) degree. The school's admission office and headquarters are in Canal Winchester, Ohio.

American Global University School of Medicine is not accredited by any recognised accreditation body.[1] As such, its degrees may not be acceptable to employers or other institutions, and use of degree titles may be restricted or illegal in some jurisdictions. It is listed in FAIMER's IMED as of 2006.[2].
