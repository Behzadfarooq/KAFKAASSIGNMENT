This category is for stub articles relating to American actors. You can help Wikipedia by expanding them.

To add an article to this category, use {{US-actor-stub}} instead of {{stub}}.

If an American actor is known for a specific type of acting please use instead one of:

  • {{US-theat-actor-stub}}
  • {{US-screen-actor-stub}}
    • {{US-film-actor-stub}}
    • {{US-tv-actor-stub}}
  • {{US-voice-actor-stub}}

The perfect article about an actor gives both a (possibly short) biography and a list of performances. Also, appropriate categories should be added, especially those listed in Category:Actors by medium and Category:American actors.
