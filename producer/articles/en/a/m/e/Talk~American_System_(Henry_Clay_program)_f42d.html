This page was created to direct readers to Henry Clay's specific program entitled the "American System" that developed from the Hamiltonian economic principles that Clay and the Whig Party embraced and not to be confused from the broader American System or School economic philosophy that describes the Hamiltonian economic theory as practiced in the United States and elsewhere under the names "National System", "Protective System" etc. --Northmeister 03:26, 24 May 2006 (UTC)
