American Teen is a new movie directed by Nanette Burstein (On the Ropes, The Kid Stays in the Picture). It is set in Warsaw, Indiana and focuses on graduating high school seniors struggling through school and life. This movie is currently set for a 2007 release. Originally, it was going to be a TV movie for A&E, but it was bought by MTV, and will now be nationally released in theaters.

This rumor now appears to be false, according to the filmmaker.

The movie was filmed at Warsaw Community High School, in Warsaw, Indiana.
