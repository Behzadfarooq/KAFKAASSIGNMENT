Americans who have advocated revolution, whether during the American Revolutionary period or at other times and places. This includes both people who advocated revolt against Britain to achieve American independence, people who advocated revolt against the American government, and Americans who advocated revolt against other governments.

For articles about the full range of people involved in the American Revolution, including those who opposed it, see Category:People of the American Revolution.
