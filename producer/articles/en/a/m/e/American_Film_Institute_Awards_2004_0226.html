The AFI Awards 2004 honored the best 10 Movies and 10 Television Programs of the year.

[edit] Movies

  • Aviator, The
  • Collateral
  • Eternal Sunshine of the Spotless Mind
  • Friday Night Lights
  • Incredibles, The
  • Kinsey
  • Maria Full of Grace
  • Million Dollar Baby *Academy Award Winner for Best Picture*
  • Sideways
  • Spider-Man 2
