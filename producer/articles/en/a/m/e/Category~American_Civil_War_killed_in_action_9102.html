Category:American Civil War killed in action

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Over 215,000 American soldiers, marines, and sailors were killed in action or mortally wounded during the American Civil War. This category is for some of the more notable military personnel who perished in combat. It does not include those who died of disease or accident during the war, or as a result of lingering complications from battlefield wounds.
