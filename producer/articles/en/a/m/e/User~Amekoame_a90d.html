[edit] About Me

I was born on January 30, 1986, in Surrey, England. My interests include Japanese culture, photography, French cinema, computer graphics, web design, conspiracy theories, the paranormal, philosophy, history, politics, and law. In fact, I will soon be studying for my law degree. In my free time, I mostly like to watch anime, listen to music, chat with friends, swim, and draw. My favourite anime titles are: Elfen Lied, Fruits Basket, Card Captor Sakura, Fullmetal Alchemist, Kimagure Orange Road, Sailor Moon, Air, Speed Grapher, Monster, Aria, Gundam Wing, Fushigi Yuugi and Ranma.
