American Gangster (TV series)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

For the upcoming 2007 film starring Denzel Washington, see American Gangster.

American Gangster is a documentary television series aired on BET which features some of black America's most infamous and powerful gangsters. It is narrated by Ving Rhames. The series premiered on November 28, 2006. The show has gained a massive following and each episode has produced around 1,000,000 viewers. The first season ended on January 9, 2007. It is unknown whether or not a second season will be aired.


[edit] American Gangsters featured

  • Stanley "Tookie" Williams
  • Ricky Ross
  • Lorenzo "Fat Cat" Nichols
  • Leroy "Nicky" Barnes
  • The Smith Brothers
  • The Chambers Brothers (crime)
