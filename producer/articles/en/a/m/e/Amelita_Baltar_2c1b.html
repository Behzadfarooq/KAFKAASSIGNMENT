María Amelia Baltar, know as Amelita Baltar (Buenos Aires, September 24, 1940) is an Argentine tango singer.

Baltar became famous in the earlies 1970s, when she gave her voice to the songs of Ástor Piazzolla and Horacio Ferrer.

Her most famous interpretation is Balada para un loco.
