Hey there. I am from Denmark but have lived in more countries than most - whether that is a good thing or a bad one depends on when you ask. Mostly, it's good to have had the experience, although of course not all of the experiences were equally good.

Here is a list of the places I've lived. It also gives you an idea of how old I am, but that's an unintended side effect and as such can and should be ignored.

* 1966 - 1983 Denmark, in Herning
* 1983 - 1986 Germany, in Flensburg
* 1986 - 1995 Denmark, in Copenhagen
* 1995 - 1999 Uk, in London. Actually, in Kilburn
* 1999 - 2001 France, Paris
* 2001 - 2003 Canada, Ottawa
* 2003 -      Uk, London. This time, East Sheen

I don't intend to move to any more new countries - in fact, I'd like to stay right where I am for now :)

I work in R&D for Peregrine Systems, which is a US company that produces enterprise software. That is also mostly good, although it has had its ups and downs: Accounting scandals of 2002. In December 2005, Peregrine was acquired by Hewlett Packard.
