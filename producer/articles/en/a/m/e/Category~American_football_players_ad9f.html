Category:American football players

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

This is a category of American football players.

No individuals should be in this main category; instead, individuals should be placed in the following subcategories that pertain to them:

  • Position(s) played
  • NFL team or league (if not in the NFL)
  • Nationality (if not from the United States)
