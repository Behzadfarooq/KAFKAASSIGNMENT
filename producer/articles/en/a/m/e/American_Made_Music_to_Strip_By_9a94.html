American Made Music to Strip By is a remix album by Rob Zombie containing remixes of the tracks off the Hellbilly Deluxe album. It is worth noting that the "Si Non Oscillas, Noli Tintinnare Mix" of Dragula is exactly the same song as the "Hot Rod Herman Mix", which has featured prominently in a wide number of films and video games.
