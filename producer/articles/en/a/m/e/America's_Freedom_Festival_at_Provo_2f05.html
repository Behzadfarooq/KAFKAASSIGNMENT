America's Freedom Festival at Provo

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

America's Freedom Festival at Provo (also America's Freedom Festival, Provo Freedom Festival, or Freedom Festival) is a Fourth of July celebrations held annually in Provo, Utah. It is said to be one of the largest Fourth of July celebrations in the United States[citation needed]. Many events are a part of it, most notably, the Stadium of Fire. The Pentagon has chosen to broadcast parts of this festival to the armed forces abroad for four years[1].

The festivities usually include a patriotic service, a balloon launch, and a parade[2].
