[edit] Disambiguation Page Requested

I found a website for the American Democracy Project. It is at http://www.aascu.org/programs/adp/about/default.htm .

The ADP is active on over 200 college campus in 47 U.S. States and territories (D.C. and Puerto Rico are included).

“The goal of the project is to produce graduates who understand and are committed to engaging in meaningful actions as citizens in a democracy.” (Quoted from the above website.)

The AASCU is the American Association of State Colleges and Universities. The campuses participating in the ADP are members of AASCU.

I suggest that a new article about the national organization be created. Along with this article, a disambiguation page should be created, to allow Wikipedia users to distinguish between the national organization and the one covered by this article.
