20th American Society of Cinematographers Awards

February 26, 2006

Theatrical Releases:
Memoirs of a Geisha

The 20th American Society of Cinematographers Awards, given on 26 February 2006, honored the best cinematographers of 2005.

    • 1.1 Film

[edit] Winners

[edit] Film

  • Outstanding Achievement in Cinematography in Theatrical Releases:
    • Memoirs of a Geisha - Dion Beebe

[edit] Television

  • Outstanding Achievement in Cinematography in Movies of the Week/Mini-Series/Pilot:
    • Warm Springs - Robbie Greenberg
  • Outstanding Achievement in Cinematography in Episodic TV Series:
    • CSI: Crime Scene Investigation - "Who Shot Sherlock?" - Nathan Hope

[edit] Nominees

  • Outstanding Achievement in Cinematography in Theatrical Releases:
    • Good Night, and Good Luck. - Robert Elswit
    • King Kong - Andrew Lesnie
    • Batman Begins - Wally Pfister
    • Brokeback Mountain - Rodrigo Prieto
  • Outstanding Achievement in Cinematography in Movies of the Week/Mini-Series/Pilot:
    • Into the West - "Wheel to the Stars" - Alan Caso
    • Code Breakers - Thomas Del Ruth
    • Faith of My Fathers - Bill Roe
    • Reefer Madness: The Movie Musical - Jan Kiesser
  • Outstanding Achievement in Cinematography in Episodic TV Series:
    • Carnivàle - "Los Moscos" - Jeff Jur
    • Las Vegas - "Everything Old is You Again" - John Newby
    • Smallville - "Sacred" - Glen Winter
    • Without a Trace - "Freefall" - John Aronson
