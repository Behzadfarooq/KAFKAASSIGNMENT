The American Colony Hotel is a historical, luxury hotel located in East Jerusalem. It is located inside a 19th century Arabic palace near the Old City of Jerusalem. The palace was originally built as a private residence for Rageeb Husayni, father of Mohammad Amin al-Husayni. The hotel frequently gets strong four-star and even five-star reviews. It features all the amenities one would expect in a high-end hotel. Room rates can run anywhere from $255 to $700 per night, or more, depending on occupancy and room selection.
