[edit] Re:My Chemical Romance

There is a source on I believe two MTV websites that state that Disenchanted is going to be released as a CD along with I Don't Love You. Since it says these two will be released as a CD there are people assuming and believing this could mean that they are the next singles. However as this information has not been confirmed by the band and you need to do some assuming to get the information I don't believe at the moment that this is true. Of course, it could be and I'm not saying I would be unhappy it's just at the moment the information is too vague to use. This is why whenever it is added on any page that it is the next single I remove it and count it as vandalism under WP:HOAX since it is unsournced information. This information has been popping up for quite some time but I wouldn't believe anything until it is stated by the band or I suppose Reprise comes out and says it. Hope this helps!  Orfen User Talk | Contribs 22:05, 27 January 2007 (UTC)
