[edit] Requested move

The following discussion is an archived debate of the proposal. Please do not modify it. Subsequent comments should be made in a new section on the talk page. No further edits should be made to this section.

The result of the debate was PAGE MOVED per discussion below. -GTBacchus(talk) 04:46, 24 February 2007 (UTC)

Amer Delić → Amer Delic — American tennis player improperly moved to misspelled name, redirect with history prevents move. This players own fansite cited in article is "Welcome to Amer Delic Online" and it uses the Delic spelling throughout. All other sources mentioned in the article use the "Delic" spelling, as do sources in other articles which link to this one. Gene Nygaard 13:12, 18 February 2007 (UTC)
