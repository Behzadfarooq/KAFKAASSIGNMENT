America's Top 10

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

American Top 10 was a television program of music hits based on Billboard's Hot 100 that was hosted by Casey Kasem and others in the 1980's and early 1990s.

[edit] Should've Been a Top 10 Hit

One segment in the show is when Casey would pick out a song that never made it to the top 10 (peaking anywhere from #11 down), but that he feels should've been based on a number of different things. He would explain many things about the song that explains why he feels it should've been a top ten hit as the video for the song is played. He would end the segment by repeating the name of the song and say what position it peaked at; "That's (song title) by (artist). A number XX song that should've been a top ten hit."
