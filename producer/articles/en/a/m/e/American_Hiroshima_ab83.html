"American Hiroshima" is a term for a proposed nuclear attack on the United States, mentioned in documents captured from apparent Al-Qaeda sources.

Several books and films discuss the American Hiroshima threat, including:

  • American Hiroshima by David Dionisi [1]
  • Nuclear Terrorism by Harvard's Graham Allison
  • Countdown to Terror by Congressman Curt Weldon
  • Last Best Chance a docudrama produced by the Nuclear Threat Initiative
  • King of Bombs: A Novel About Nuclear Terrorism by Sheldon Filger
