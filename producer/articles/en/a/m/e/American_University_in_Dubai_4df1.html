The American University in Dubai (AUD) (in Arabic:الجامعة الأمريكية في دبي ) is a private educational institute located in Dubai, United Arab Emirates.

[edit] Academics

The University is divided into numerous schools:

  • School of Business (offering a MBA)
  • School of Engineering (offering bachelor of science in civil, computer, and electrical engineering)
  • Department of Information Technology
  • Department of Visual Communication
  • Department of Interior Design
  • Division of Liberal Arts
  • Center for English Proficiency.

[edit] The Campus

The campus is located in Dubai City nearby Dubai Media City and Dubai Internet City.
