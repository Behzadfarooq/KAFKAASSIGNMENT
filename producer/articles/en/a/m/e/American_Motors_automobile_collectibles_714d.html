[edit] Collectibles

Not every car gets made into a toy. The Gremlin was probably the most famous and most made into objects. Yet, despite the modest sales, more toys and models were made of the AMC Matador than some better selling AMC models such as the Hornet, certainly more than the striking AMC Marlin, and there are practically no known models of the slight larger AMC Ambassador.

[edit] Matador

  • Aurora AFX slot cars made both the boxy stock car resembling the factory Penske car, and the 1974 coupe.
  • Cox made a gas car of the 4-door sedan.
  • Cox also made a large size stock car of the 1974 coupe.
  • AMT made a model of the 1974 coupe stock car driven by Allison, and one with customized body parts.
  • The Matador is not as common subject for Pinewood Derby as the Superbird, but at least one has been made.
