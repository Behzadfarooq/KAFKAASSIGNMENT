The American University of Puerto Rico (AUPR) is a private university in Puerto Rico with campuses in Bayamon and Manati. The university was founded in 1963 as the American Business College, and offers undergraduate studies in arts, business administration, education, and sciences, and graduate studies in criminal justice.

AUPR is accredited by the Council of Higher Education of Puerto Rico and the Middle States Association of Colleges and Schools.
