The American Lacrosse Conference, also known as the ALC, is a NCAA Division I Women's Lacrosse-only college athletic conference whose members are located in the Midwest and East Coast states from Illinois to North Carolina.

If extended an invitation, In 2009 The University of South Carolina may become the newest member of the ALC.

In 2010 The University of Florida will become a full member of the ALC
