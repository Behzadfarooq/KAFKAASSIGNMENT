AmeriDarts is a darts arcade game released by Ameri in 1989.

There are five different game variations:

  • Flash (A Bull's Eye Game)
  • Sector Shoot-Out (a numbered target game)
  • 301 (the traditional darts game)
  • High Score
  • Cricket.
