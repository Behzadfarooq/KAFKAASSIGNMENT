This category is for biographical stub articles relating to American football coaches. You can help Wikipedia by expanding them.

To add an article to this category, use {{amfoot-coach-stub}} instead of {{stub}}, {{Americanfootball-stub}}, {{Amfootbio-stub}} or {{sportbio-stub}}.

Because the vast majority of participants in American football are from the United States, do not double stub with the {{US-bio-stub}}, though indicating the origin of those from other countries is appropriate.
