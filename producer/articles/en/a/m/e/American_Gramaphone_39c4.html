American Gramaphone

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

You have new messages (last change).

American Gramaphone is an American record company, and was founded in 1984 by Chip Davis. It is best-known for releasing Davis' solo and Mannheim Steamroller albums. It is based in Omaha, Nebraska.
