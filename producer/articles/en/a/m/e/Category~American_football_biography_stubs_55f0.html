This category is for biographical stub articles relating to American football players, coaches, or other figures. You can help Wikipedia by expanding them.

To add an article to this category, use {{Amfoot-bio-stub}} instead of {{stub}}, {{Americanfootball-stub}}, or {{sportbio-stub}}.

Because the vast majority of participants in American football are from the United States, do not double stub with the {{US-bio-stub}}, though indicating the origin of those from other countries is appropriate.
