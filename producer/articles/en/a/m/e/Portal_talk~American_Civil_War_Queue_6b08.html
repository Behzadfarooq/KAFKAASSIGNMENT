The American Civil War portal Queue is for managing dynamic sections that will change automatically throughout the year. This talk page is for hashing out the problems. The main discussion area is at Portal talk:American Civil War.

We also have the Newsroom, which is to manage Portal:American Civil War/American Civil War news.

Oh, hey BusterD. Thanks for taking interest in doing dynamics on the ACW Portal. The scheme I'm thinking of uses the variables, {{CURRENTWEEK}} (0..53) and {{CURRENTMONTH}} (01..12). I'm thinking we can just set it up like a ring that lines up with anniversaries of events on the American Civil War timeline (Which may not exist yet, unfortunately). Once we build a whole year's worth in, it'll run the ring changing weekly/monthly year after year. That way, we wont have to archive anything except maybe ACW News.
We should probably first compile lists of possible selections so we can determin how often to change them. For example, If we only have three featured articles, we might set them up to change each month and repeat the sequence each quarter. The Grand Parade of the States on the other hand has 23 articles to date, that we can draw from. We could actually change those daily. See Portal talk:American Civil War/Grand Parade of the States. Please stay tuned... • CQ 13:28, 20 September 2006 (UTC)
