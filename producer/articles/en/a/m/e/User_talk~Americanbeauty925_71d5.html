[edit] subcategories

You create the category. In the category page, you put in the parent category. That category is then a subcategory of the parent. Like, for instance, suppose there was a category Category:People from Boston and you wanted to create Category:People from South Boston as a sub. You would create the category just a you create an article (by typing Category:People from South Boston in the article name box, then when it says No such category, you create it and type Category:People from Boston as the only content of the page. The subcategory now exists, and you can add Category:People from South Boston to the bottom of any article. Also note that articles should not belong to both a parent category and any of its subcategories (an article should not in both People from Boston and People from South Boston, the parent category is assumed.)
