Amerikaz Nightmare is a 2004 rap album by Hip-Hop duo Mobb Deep. It is their 6th studio effort. It features the singles "Got It Twisted" and "Real Gangstaz". The album was a commercial disappointment for the duo, selling only 547,000 copies, leading to their departure from Jive.
