14th American Society of Cinematographers Awards

February 20, 2000

The 14th American Society of Cinematographers Awards, given on 20 February 2000, honored the best cinematographers of 1999.

[edit] Winners

[edit] Film

  • Outstanding Achievement in Cinematography in Theatrical Releases:
    • American Beauty - Conrad L. Hall

[edit] Television

  • Outstanding Achievement in Cinematography in Movies of the Week/Mini-Series:
    • Introducing Dorothy Dandridge - Robbie Greenberg
  • Outstanding Achievement in Cinematography in Regular Series:
    • The X Files - "Agua Mala" - Bill Roe

[edit] Other

  • ASC Heritage Award (Note: This year the award is named for Gregg Toland)
    • Andrew Huebscher
    • Christopher Popp
  • Lifetime Achievement Award:
    • William A. Fraker
  • Board of the Governors Award:
    • Warren Beatty
  • International Award:
    • Oswald Morris
  • President's Award:
    • Guy Green

[edit] Film

  • Outstanding Achievement in Cinematography in Theatrical Releases:
    • The Insider - Dante Spinotti
    • The Sixth Sense - Tak Fujimoto
    • Sleepy Hollow - Emmanuel Lubezki
    • Snow Falling on Cedars - Robert Richardson
