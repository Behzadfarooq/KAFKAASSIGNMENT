[edit] Overview

Amelia's notebooks are a series of children's books written and illustrated by Marissa Moss. The books, targeted at children between 9 and 12, are a series of notebooks and journals written by the character Amelia, who writes about her life, thoughts, and memories. They are profusely illustrated and designed to resemble actual journals. Amelia is a character that many young people identify with. She has problems that many kids face like gossip, moving or crushes. Amelia has also written guides such as Dr. Amelia's Boredom Survival Guide, Amelia's Guide to Gossip, Amelia's School Survival Guide, Amelia's Easy-as-Pie Drawing Guide, a step-by-step guide on how to draw like Amelia, and My Notebook (With Help from Amelia), an Amelia-style notebook for you to use.

[edit] Titles

  • Amelia's Notebook
    • Amelia's moving, and her mom gives her a notebook to calm her down. Turns out, expressing herself in her notebook helps more than she thought it would...
  • Amelia Writes Again
    • The follow-up to Amelia's Notebook.
  • The All-New Amelia
    • When a new girl named Charisse moves into town from London and becomes popular Amelia tries to be popular too.
  • Amelia Hits the Road
    • Amelia and her family are on the road, and they're visiting her friend Nadia when it's all over. During the book Amelia wonders if Nadia will be the same as she was before.
  • Amelia Lends a Hand
    • Amelia meets a deaf boy named Enzo. She won't let Enzo being deaf get in the way of their friendship!
  • Amelia Takes Command
    • Amelia goes to Space Camp with her friend Nadia and tries to prove that girls should have just as important roles as boys.
  • Amelia Works it Out
    • Amelia's short on cash, and she really wants new shoes. But which job is right?
  • Amelia's 6th Grade Notebook
    • Amelia is finally in 6th grade, but she is suffering from a cruel teacher named Mr. Lambaste and her friends not being in her class.
  • Amelia's Book Of Notes & Note Passing
    • A new cool girl moves to the middle school and becomes friends with Carly, much to Amelia's dismay.
  • Amelia's Best Year Ever
  • Amelia's Easy-As-Pie Drawing Guide
  • Amelia's Family Ties
    • Amelia goes to visit her divorced father.
  • Amelia's School Survival Guide
  • Dr. Amelia's Boredom Survival Guide
  • Luv, Amelia Luv, Nadia
    • Amelia and Nadia write letters to each other.
  • Madame Amelia Tells All
  • Oh Boy, Amelia!
    • Amelia's sister Cleo has a boyfriend, and starts doing strange things, like batting her eyelashes and wearing make-up. Is this what it takes to impress boys?
  • Amelia's Longest, Biggest, Most-Fights-Ever Family Reunion
    • Amelia goes to a family reunion with her father and meets all his relatives for the first time.
  • Amelia's Most Unforgettable Embarrassing Moments
    • Amelia is excited to go on her school overnight-until she finds out who her student guide will be. It's her older sister Cleo!!
