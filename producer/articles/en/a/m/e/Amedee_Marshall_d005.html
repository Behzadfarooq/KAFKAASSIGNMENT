Amedee Marshall is an ice hockey player born on November 23, 1985. He is currently signed to the Hazelton Wolverines of the Central Interior Hockey League (CIHL). He is a skilled mid-sized forward who is quick on his feet, and has very good puck control.

[edit] Playing career

Marshall started playing with the Prince George Spruce Kings in 2002. He played for the Spruce Kings until the 2004-05 season where he was a key player on the first line. He then injured his wrist near the end of the 04-05 season which kept him out of play for the most part of the 05-06 season. He came back near the half way point of the BCHL's 2005-2006 season, only to be traded the the AJHL's Fort McMurray Oil Barons where he went on to win the AJHL title with the team. He has currently just finished playing a season with the senior "AA" Hazelton Wolverines of the Central Interior Hockey League(CIHL). In 20 games Amedee led the team with 38 points and was high in the league standings as well.
