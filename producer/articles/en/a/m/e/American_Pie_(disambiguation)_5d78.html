American Pie is the title of several works:

  • "American Pie", a 1971 hit song by Don McLean; it was also recorded by Madonna in 2000.
  • American Pie (album), the Don McLean album containing the above song;
  • American Pie (film), a 1999 film, and its sequels:
    • American Pie 2, (2001)
    • American Wedding, (2003) also titled American Pie: The Wedding in Europe and Australia.
    • American Pie Presents: Band Camp, (2005) (spin-off).
    • American Pie Presents: The Naked Mile, (2006) (spin-off).
