Category:American captives in Bagram

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

In addition to the more famous American military interrogation centre at the Guantanamo Bay detainment camps, in Cuba, they maintain another interrogation centre at the Bagram Airfield detention facility. Bagram contains approximately the same number of captives, but in more primitive conditions. Captives who have been held in both camps say that their interrogations were more brutal in Bagram. American GIs in Bagram beat two prisoners to death in December 2002.
