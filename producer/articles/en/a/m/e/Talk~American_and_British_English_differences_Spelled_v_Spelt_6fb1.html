[edit] Spelled v Spelt

I reverted the change from "spelled" to "spelt" as:

  • "The forms with -ed are also common in Commonwealth usage."
  • "spelled" is the most common spelling globally (453,000 v 99,500)
  • "spelled" (45,000) is more common in the UK than "spelt" (35,200)
  • "spelled" (14,000) is as common in Australia as "spelt" (14,500)
  • "spelled" (2,980) is as common in New Zealand as "spelt" (2,790)
  • "spelled" (3,320) is more common in Ireland than "spelt" (2,470)
  • "spelled" (39,200) is more common in Canada than "spelt" (2,470)
  • "spelled" is the de facto standard on Wikipedia (261 v 45)
  • the only "Commonwealth" country listed in the article that significantly favours "spelt" (1,500) over "spelled" (851) is South Africa
