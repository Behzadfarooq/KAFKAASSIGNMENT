American Idiot can be the title of:

  • American Idiot (album), Green Day's seventh studio album and their first "rock opera".
  • American Idiot (song), a song from the album, released as a single with over 13 million copies sold.
  • American Idiot (film), a rumored film based on the storyline of the album.
  • American Idiot, a parody of American Idol on Nickelodeon's show All That.
