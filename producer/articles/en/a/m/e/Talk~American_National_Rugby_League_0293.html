[edit] Boston Braves

The Boston Braves were originally called the Boston Wolfhounds and were a part of the Boston Irish Wolfhounds club. Participation by members of other local clubs led to the name change. I'm going to add something to that effect into the article. If anyone thinks that it's unnecessary, feel free to remove it and explain why on the talk page.
