Amft is a German fashion label with headquarters in Berlin. International investors founded it in 2000. The Creative Director since 2002 has been Torsten Amft. Amft produced and relaunched the style of "Elitist Futurism" worldwide.

Torsten Amft has established a stylish collection since 2001 for this label, using a mixture of luxury materials and quality workmanship to create a look intended to emphasize the aura and personality of its wearer. Amft collections incorporate the design principle that each new piece could be combined with the previous fashion designs from Amft, to create a harmonious continuity of style. It also emphasizes the Amft label's ecological and cultural sense. The clothes themselves are tailored skirts, coats, many more. Fabrics include tweeds, leathers, chiffons, pinstripe flannels, and cashmeres.
