AMF Futsal World Cup 2003

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

You have new messages (last change).

The eighth edition of the AMF Futsal World Cup for football was held in Paraguay in 2003. Twenty teams were involved, with the host nation eventually emerging as champion.
