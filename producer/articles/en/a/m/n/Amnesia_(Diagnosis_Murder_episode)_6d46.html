[edit] Plot

Security is increased at the hospital when Senator Robert Bassett undergoes an operation. Dr's. Sloan and Stewart are preoccupied when a beautiful young amnesia patient is brought in. Unbeknownst to them, their beautiful young patient is a professional killer. Ironically this is one episode where dr. sloan did not solve the mystery
