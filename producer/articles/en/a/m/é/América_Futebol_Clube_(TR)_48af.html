América Futebol Clube, usually known simply as América, or as América de Três Rios, América-TR or América (TR), is a Brazilian football club from Três Rios, Rio de Janeiro state.

[edit] History

On May 14, 1929, América Futebol Clube was founded. [1] The club was named after América Football Club.

In 1989, América de Três Rios won the Campeonato Carioca Segunda Divisão, ans thus was promoted to the following year's first level. [2]

In 1990, the club disputed the Campeonato Carioca for the first time. The club finished in the 8th position. [3]

In 1991, América de Três Rios disputed again the Rio de Janeiro state championship. In Taça Guanabara, the club finished in the 10th position, and in Taça Rio, the club finished in the 7th position. [4]

In 1992, the club disputed again the Campeonato Carioca. It was the club´s all-time best campaign, finishing in the 4th position, ahead of Botafogo, and only behind the other three Rio de Janeiro big teams, Vasco da Gama, Flamengo and Fluminense. [5]

In 1993, América de Três Rios disputed the state championship for the last time. The club was relegated to the second level after finishing in the last position in the competition. In Taça Guanabara, the club lost 10 points due to the utilization of an ineligible player named Juarez, finishing the competition with -7 points. [6]

As of 2007, the club´s football section is closed [7]

[edit] Titles

  • Campeonato Carioca Segunda Divisão: 1989

[edit] Stadium

América de Três Rios 's home stadium is Estádio Arthur Sebastião de Toledo Ribas, nicknamed Tievão [8], with a maximum capacity of 5,000 people. [9]

[edit] Adílio

In 1994, Adílio, better known as the 1981 Intercontinental Cup champion defending Flamengo, played for the club. [10]

[edit] Rival

Entrerriense Futebol Clube, which is one of the other two Três Rios' football clubs (the other being Três Rios Futebol Clube), is América de Três Rios greatest rival.

[edit] Club colors

The club colors are red and white. [8] The home kit is composed of red shirts, white shorts and white socks, and the away kit is composed of white shirts, red shorts and red socks. [1]

[edit] Other sports

América de Três basketball team is still active, disputing mostly state championships. [11]
