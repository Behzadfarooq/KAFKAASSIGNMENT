Amédée Louis Michel Lepeletier de Saint Fargeau

Amédée Louis Michel Lepeletier de Saint Fargeau (October 9, 1770 – August 23, 1845) was a French entomologist, and specialist in the Hymenoptera.

From the year of 1833 until his death, he served as president of the Société entomologique de France.
