Amélie Helga Lundahl

Amélie Helga Lundahl (1870)

Amélie Helga Lundahl (Oulu, May 26, 1850 - Helsinki, August 20, 1914) was a Finnish painter.

She studied in Stockholm Fine Arts School and she obtained a student grant to go on with her studies in Académie Julian in Paris. Due to a visit to Bretagne in the 1870's, she decided to move there and paint her typical landscapes and portraits.
