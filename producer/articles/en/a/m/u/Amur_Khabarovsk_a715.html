Amur Khabarovsk (Ru: Амур Хабаровск) is an Russian ice hockey team playing in the Russian Hockey Super League. They are based in Khabarovsk and are named after the Amur river.

Despite being a perennial cellar-dweller in the Russian Hockey Super League, Amur Khabarovsk enjoys one of the best attendances of European clubs. In the recent IIHF ranking of fan attendances, Amur was 13th on the list with an average home crowd of 7,100 (in an arena with the capacity of 7,100).
