Amor a La Mexicana was the sixth album released by Mexican superstar Thalía. The album become one of Thalía's best to date, especially for the album's first single "Amor a La Mexicana" which proved the evolution of Thalía as a musician.

[edit] Por Amor

In France it was released as Por Amor, including 2 bonus tracks ("Piel morena" and "Amor a la mexicana") that were remixed especially for that edition. The remix of "Amor a la mexicana" became the main single of the album.
