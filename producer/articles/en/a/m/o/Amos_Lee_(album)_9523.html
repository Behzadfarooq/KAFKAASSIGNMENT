Amos Lee is the self-titled debut album by American singer-songwriter Amos Lee, released in the US by Blue Note Records on March 1, 2005. The album was produced by Lee Alexander, the bassist in Norah Jones' band. The songs on the album incorporate themes of folk music, soul music, and jazz.

[edit] In popular culture

"Colors" was featured on the soundtrack of the Dreamworks film Just Like Heaven, on the 'Hunting' episode of House, and on the two-hour season finale of the ABC show Grey's Anatomy on May 14, 2006. "Keep it Loose, Keep it Tight" was featured on the NBC show Conviction on April 7, 2006.
