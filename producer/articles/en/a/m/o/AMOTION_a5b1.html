aMOTION is a DVD and remix CD released by American rock band A Perfect Circle. It was released on November 16, 2004.

The aMOTION DVD consists of music videos for singles such as "Judith", "3 Libras" and "Weak and Powerless" as well as some previously unreleased videos for singles such as "Blue" and "Thinking Of You".

The set also includes an audio-only CD containing remixes that were previously only available as b-sides on singles or were previously unavailable altogether. The CD includes tracks remixed by band members Paz Lenchantin, Troy Van Leeuwen, James Iha, Danny Lohner (Renholdër) and Robert Del Naja of Massive Attack.

aMOTION was certified Platinum by the RIAA on 17 December, 2004.
