Amos Arbour

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Amos "Butch" Arbour - (born January 26, 1895 in Waubaushene, Ontario - died November 1, 1943) was a Canadian professional ice hockey left winger who played 6 seasons in the National Hockey League for the Montreal Canadiens, Hamilton Tigers and Toronto St. Pats.
