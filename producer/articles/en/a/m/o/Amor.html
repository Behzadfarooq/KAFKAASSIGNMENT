Amor has several meanings:

  • Amor is Latin, Spanish and Portuguese for love.
  • Amor ("love") is an alternate name for Cupid, the Roman god of love. His equivalent is Eros in Greek mythology.
  • Amor asteroids, a group of near-Earth asteroids named after the asteroid 1221 Amor, the first such asteroid to be discovered
  • Amor (automobile), a German car
  • Amusing Misuse of Resources, a program in KDE toys
  • Amor Vincit Omnia (Caravaggio), 1600s painting
