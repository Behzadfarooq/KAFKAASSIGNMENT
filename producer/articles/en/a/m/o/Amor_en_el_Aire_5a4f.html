Amor en el aire (English language:Love in Flight) is a 1967 Spanish Argentine romantic comedy film directed and written by Luis César Amadori with Jesús María de Arozamena.

[edit] Release

The film premired in Spain on 25 December 1967 and in Argentina on 29 August 1968.
