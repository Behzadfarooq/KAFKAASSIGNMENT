[edit] About Me

My name is Andrew and I am from Minnesota. Right now I live in Brainerd but I plan on moving to Cushing, MN where I recently bought some land back in an old development called Enchanted Lakes (I'll have to make that page sometime!)

While I grew up in Brainerd most of my life, I have spent a good deal of time over the years in Morrison County where much of my family lives. My ancestral history within Morrison County dates back to the mid-1800's when my first relatives came here, there has never been a period in time since that someone from my family hasn't occupied residence in Morrison County.

[edit] Hobbies

  • Coin collecting
  • Rock collecting (specifically Minnesota agates)
  • Photography (using my obsolete Sony P52 digital camera)
