[edit] Plot introduction

The plot deals with the life of an African-American prince named At-mun. When he was 15 or 16 he was sold into slavery, learned to read and write, and bought his freedom at age 59. He went around to houses with woman slaves in them. He would ask the master to buy her and would go work for the money. He would then go and buy the slave to marry her. He said he wanted for them to die freely. The first two he married died within the year, but the third, Violate, lived with him and their daughter Celia, for many happy years. They rented a piece of land and they opened a small tannery. After many hard-working years, he managed to finally buy a farm.
