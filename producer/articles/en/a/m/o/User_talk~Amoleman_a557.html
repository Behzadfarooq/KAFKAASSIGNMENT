[edit] Lavaeth Church

A tag has been placed on Lavaeth Church, requesting that it be speedily deleted from Wikipedia. This has been done under the criteria for speedy deletion, because it is an article providing little or no context to the reader. If you plan to add more material to the article, I advise you to do so immediately. Also please note that articles must be on notable subjects and should provide references to reliable sources which verify their content. Please do not remove the speedy deletion tag yourself. To contest the tagging and request that administrators wait a while for you to add contextual material, please affix the template {{hangon}} to the page and state your intention on the article's talk page. Feel free to leave a note on my talk page if you have any questions about this. DMacks 17:04, 29 December 2006 (UTC)
