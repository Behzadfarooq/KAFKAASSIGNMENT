Amodra

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Amodra is a small village of Una[[1]] Taluka of Junagadh District in the Indian state of Gujarat.
Population  : More than 5000.
Relative location: Amodra, Taluka: Una, District: Junagadha, Gujarat State, India
PIN (Postal Index Number) 362 560
Telephone code  : 0091-2875-28**** (International code-area code-local no.)
Available cellphone coverage: Hutch,BSNL,Reliance India Mobile,Tata Indicom,Airtel, Idea.

[edit] Demographics

People speak Gujarati language (typically pure Kathiawadi - Kathiawad). Most of the people are farmers and government servant.

[edit] Festivals

Diwali, Navaratri (Dandiya), Dussera, Rakhi (Raksha Bandhan), Holi, Uttarayana (Kite Festival), Krishna Janmaashtami are main festivals.

[edit] Education

Amodra is a developing village. Here students can learn up to 10th. But after that they have to go for Una city or at other place for further study. The main educational institutes in Amodra are,

  • Amodra Prathamik Shala (From Std 1 to 7).
  • Vinay Mandir School for Boys.
  • Vinay Mandir School for Girls.
