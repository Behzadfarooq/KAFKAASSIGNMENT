Amor fati

Amor fati is a Latin phrase, which loosely translates to "Love of fate". It is used to describe the attitude that everything which occurs in one's life, including suffering and loss, is good. That is, that everything that happens to one is destiny's way of reaching its ultimate purpose for one, and so should be considered good. Moreover, it is characterized by an acceptance of the events that occur in one's life.
