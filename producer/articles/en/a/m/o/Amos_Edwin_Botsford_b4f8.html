Amos Edwin Botsford, PC (September 5, 1804 â€“ March 22, 1894) was a Canadian farmer, judge, politician, and businessman.

Born in Saint John, New Brunswick, in 1867, he was appointed to the Senate representing the senatorial division of New Brunswick. A Conservative member, he died in office in 1894. He is buried in Fernhill Cemetery.
