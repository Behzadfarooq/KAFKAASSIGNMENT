Amon (demon)

In demonology, Amon is a Marquis of Hell. He controls forty legions of spirits. He appears as a wolf with a serpent's tail, and breaths fire, or appears as a man with dog's teeth in the head of a raven, or simply as a man with a raven's head. He tells of things past and future, and reconciles feuds and controversies between friends.

[edit] References in Popular Culture

  • In the MMORPG Final Fantasy XI, Marquis Amon is a "Notorious Monster" found in the demon stronghold of Castle Zvahl.
  • In the 2006 Dungeons & Dragons role-playing game supplement Tome of Magic: Pact, Shadow, and True Name Magic, Amon appears as a "vestige" with whom characters can make a pact in return for power.
  • In the Shadow Hearts series, Amon manifests as a demon whom the character Yuri Hyuga is able to metamorphose into, after defeating him in battle.
  • In the anime Witch Hunter Robin Amon is one of the main characters.
  • In the manga Devilman, the character Akira Fudo is possessed by a demon called Amon.
  • In the album Them by King Diamond, the house is referred to as 'the House of Amon'.
  • The Death Metal band Deicide was once referred to as Amon.
  • In in J.R.R. Tolkien's Middle-earth, the Sindarin name of Mount Doom is Amon Amarth and could be referred to Amon. As well, the death metal band Amon Amarth (band) uses Amon in their name.
