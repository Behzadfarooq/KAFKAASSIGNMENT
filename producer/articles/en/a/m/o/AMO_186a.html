AMO

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Amo - "I am going to" is a word that was created by Joseph C Kriesher III who attends The Pennsylvania State University. When speaking with friends, Joseph felt he didn't need to say every word when expressing certain ideas. While he didn't realize this talent at first, his friends, Matthew Massaro and Kevin Skelley, politely told him about his use of amo. When he thought about it, he realized he had cut "I am going to" into three letters, one short phrase. A good example of the use of amo is, "Amo win it."
