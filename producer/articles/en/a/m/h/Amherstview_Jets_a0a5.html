The Amherstview Jets are a Junior "C" ice hockey team based out of Amherstview, Ontario. They play out of the Empire B Junior C Hockey League.

Ernestown Jets 1989 - 1999
Amherstview Jets 1999 to Present

[edit] History

The Jets joined the Eastern "C" league in 1989. The league became the Empire B "C" in 1995, and the Jets have been a member since inception. In the team's first ten seasons, the Jets played in Ernestown, Ontario.
