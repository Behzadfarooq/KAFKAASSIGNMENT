Amherst is an Amtrak station in Amherst, Massachusetts on the Vermonter.

[edit] Notable Places Nearby

The station is close to several colleges.

  • Amherst College
  • Smith College
  • Mount Holyoke College
  • Hampshire College
  • University of Massachusetts Amherst
