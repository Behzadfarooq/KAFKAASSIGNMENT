Amanda Swisten

Amanda Swisten (born December 20, 1980 in New York City, New York) is an American actress of Swedish/Eastern-European descent who has appeared in various hit films and TV shows.

Her first credited appearance was in the film American Wedding in 2003. After which she appeared in films such as The Last Run (2004), The Girl Next Door (2004), and Freezerburn (2005). She has had guest appearances on TV series such as I'm with Her, Two and a Half Men, Quintuplets, and Joey. Her free time is spent dealing with significant charities and travelling.

Swisten was ranked #99 on the Maxim Hot 100 Women of 2004.[1]
