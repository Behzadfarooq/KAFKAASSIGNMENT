[edit] Happy New Year 2007

Happy New Year 2007 !!!
Dear Amartyabag,
New Year is the time to enjoy, party and rejuvenate oneself with a new spirit and enthusiasm. It is also the time to forget the past and begin a new life. Let us make the New Year’s resolution to:
  • Contribute to the Wikipedia in any way possible.
  • Create as many as possible FA articles.
  • Fight vandalism.
  • Follow Wikiquette
  • Create a feeling of brotherhood with all Wikipedians and everybody on this Earth.

I take this as an occasion to wish you and your family the best in the days to come on Wikipedia and in real life. May this New Year invigorate you, bringing you more happiness and content. Happy New Year 2007!!! and Happy Wikipediaing -- Amartyabag (Talk) 05:58, 24 December 2006 (UTC)
