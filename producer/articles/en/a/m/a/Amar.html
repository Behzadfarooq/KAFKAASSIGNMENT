Look up amar in Wiktionary, the free dictionary.
  • Amar is a male Hindu name meaning "immortal" or "deathless" in Sanskrit.
  • Amar, Akhbar, Anthony is the name of a comedy film released in India in 1977.
  • "Amar Javan", meaning 'immortal warrior', is written on a cenotaph in the India Gate, New Delhi, India.
  • Amarkhel (Tribe of Amar) is the name of a Pashtun tribe in Afghanistan.
  • Ludu Daw Amar is a prolific female writer in Myanmar (formerly Burma).
  • In Arabic, Amar means 'moon'.
  • In the Burmese language, it means 'strong one' or the 'hard one'.
  • In the Mongolian language, it means calm, tranquil and peaceful.
