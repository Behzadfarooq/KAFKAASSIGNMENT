Amanda Bloom is a fictional character from the ABC Family series Kyle XY and is portrayed by Kirsten Prout.

[edit] About Amanda

Amanda lives next door to the Tragers. Her father, who passed away a year ago, was an artist. Kyle first meets Amanda by being lured to her playing of Pachelbel's Canon. Kyle then begins to feel sadness and starts crying. Amanda hears and notices him. Startled, Kyle runs back to the Trager residence. Amanda and her mother go to the Tragers that night. Amanda's mother, Carol, explains to Nicole that Kyle had entered their house uninvited. Kyle is there with Nicole, and so he and Amanda begin to develop a crush.

After Kyle calms a stray dog by staring it in the eye, Amanda, looking out the window, is surprised. "You're like... the Dog Whisperer." Amanda then explains to Kyle about "down time" and how his family would have to be searching for him. "You didn't just fall from the sky."

Amanda, while driving home, is surprised to see Kyle jumping off the roof. This causes her to crash into some trash cans. Amanda asks Kyle if she should conceal the damage done to the car or admit to the damage. Kyle then tells her to simply admit it. Amanda then tells Kyle to wish her luck. Later, Carol is upset to hear that the car is damaged. Kyle then arrives to defend Amanda, lying that he had caused the wreck. "This lie was easy, because I knew it was the right thing to do."
