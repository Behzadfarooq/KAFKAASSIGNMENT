[edit] Season

Note that I've violated my usual rule of thumb about not listing the season of occurrence for a mushroom species. A mushroom that occurs across a broad geographical area can fruit at very different times of year depending on when the local mushroom season is. However, the geographical range of Amanita velosa is rather narrow (its restricted to the California floristic province), hence, the season listed is accurate throughout its range.
