Aman Yatan Verma, is an established TV show anchor and an actor. He co-hosted with Mini Mathur in the first season of Indian Idol on Sony Entertainment Television and is currently hosting Zee TV's Zee Cinestars.

Verma and the Bollywood elite have vehemently denied their involvement with any casting couch mentality.
