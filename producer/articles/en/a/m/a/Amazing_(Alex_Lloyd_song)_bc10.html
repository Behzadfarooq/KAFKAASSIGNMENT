"Amazing" is a single released by Australian singer, Alex Lloyd in 2001. It was a successful single, which reached the top of the New Zealand chart, and topped the poll in youth radio station Triple J's Hottest 100 in 2001.

In 2004 the song was utilized in advertisements for the Ford Territory SUV.
