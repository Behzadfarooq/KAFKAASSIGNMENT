Amanda Levete (born 1955, Bridgend) is a British architect and partner in Future Systems with Jan Kaplický.

[edit] Buildings

  • 1994–Lord's Media Centre

[edit] Career

  • 1980–1981 worked with Alsop & Lyall
  • 1982–1984 worked with YRM Architects
  • 1983–1986 worked with Powis & Levette
  • 1984–1989 worked with Richard Rogers & Partners
  • 1989 Partner in Future Systems
