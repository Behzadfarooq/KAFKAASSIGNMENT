Amara Traoré

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Amara Traoré (born September 25, 1965) is a Senegalese football player.

He played for several teams, including SC Bastia, FC Gueugnon, FC Metz, all in France.

He played for Senegal national football team and was a participant at the 2002 FIFA World Cup.
