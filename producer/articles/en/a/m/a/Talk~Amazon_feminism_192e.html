[edit] Amazon feminism exists

I identify as an Amazon feminist, someone who seeks physical expression of feminism, seeks to strengthen the physical world of women and supports female heroism. Certainly, the rise of women in sports, military combat and other expressions of female strength are an expression of Amazon feminism. We are really only now starting to evolve after thousands of years of oppression. I don't think it's going to happen overnight, but if you compare women now to women a few hundred years ago, you'll see we're evolving into stronger beings. I think in the next few hundred years we'll evolve even more. It's already started and gained good momentum, I'm proud of it and honored to be part of it. All feminists do not cringe at jokes in the workplace, but many do like to treat people with respect and expect the same in return. That is not weakness, but strength and I'm tired of hearing slurs hurled toward feminism when the things people criticize are actually great virtues. The liberal value of speaking to people with some respect is a strength and a discipline. Anyone who's studied martial arts knows that honor and respect for other people is one of the highest virtues you can aspire to.

The idea of strengthening our physical selves would solve a lot of problems for women - domestic violence, sexual assault and give us the arsenal to protect our children and families. Also physical strength and the confidence to express it, does something to your mental world. It gives you confidence even in non-combative situations to express yourself and defend your deepest truths freely with the security of your physical strength to back you up. I do believe that there's an instinctive fear that women are accustomed to, and this fear is the cause of many women who tend to adapt rather than fight for what's important to them. Those of us who gain the confidence of inner and outer strength, feel freer to express difference of opinion without instinctive recitince or fear. It's just been my personal experience that it lifts the habitual carefulness in the way women move in the world. Amazon feminism is the exciting possibility for a future direction of feminism. We've accomplished some goals, this is one of the frontiers we could further explore. I think it's an inspiring branch of feminism.
