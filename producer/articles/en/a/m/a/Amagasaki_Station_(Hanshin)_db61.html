Amagasaki Station (尼崎駅, Amagasaki-eki?) is a train station on the Hanshin Electric Railway in Amagasaki, Hyogo Prefecture, Japan. The station is undergoing renovations to accommodate the Nishi-Osaka Line extension to Kintetsu Namba Station and through train service with Kintetsu. When the renovation is complete, Amagasaki will become the junction point between Umeda-bound and Namba-bound trains.

[edit] Platforms

The station currently consists of five active tracks and three island platforms, with Track 5 sharing platforms with both Track 4 and Track 6.

During current upgrade
1, 2 Main Line (eastbound) for Osaka (Umeda)
4 Nishi-Osaka Line for Nishikujo
5, 6 Main Line (westbound) for Koshien, Nishinomiya, Ashiya, Kobe, Akashi and Himeji

When the station upgrade is complete, a new island platform would be added on the eastbound side, and the current Tracks 1 and 2 would become the new Tracks 2 and 3, respectively. Tracks 2 Track 5 will share connecting platforms with tracks on both sides.

After completion (late 2008)
1, 2 Main Line (eastbound) for Osaka (Umeda)
3 Nishi-Osaka Line (eastbound) for Nishikujo, Kintetsu Namba and Kintetsu Nara
4 Nishi-Osaka Line (westbound) for Koshien, Nishinomiya, Ashiya, Kobe, Akashi and Himeji
5, 6 Main Line (westbound) for Koshien, Nishinomiya, Ashiya, Kobe, Akashi and Himeji
