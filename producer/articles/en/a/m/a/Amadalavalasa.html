Amadalavalasa or Amudalavalasa is a Municipality and Mandal headquarters in Srikakulam district in the state of Andhra Pradesh, India.

There is a large scale sugar factory and Amadalavalasa and also some other industries are located at that town.

It is 9 kilometers far from Srikakulam town. 'Srikakulam Road' railway station is located at Amadalavalasa.

[edit] Geography

Amudalavalasa is located at 18.4167° N 83.9000° E.[1] It has an average elevation of 29 meters (98 feet).

[edit] Demographics

As of 2001 India census,GRIndia Amadalavalasa had a population of 37,852. Males constitute 51% of the population and females 49%. Amadalavalasa has an average literacy rate of 65%, higher than the national average of 59.5%; with 58% of the males and 42% of females literate. 11% of the population is under 6 years of age.

[edit] Assembly Constituency

Amadalavalasa is an assembly constituency in Andhra Pradesh. There are 1,32,314 registered voters in this constituency in 1999 elections.

List of Elected Members:[2]

  • 1978 and 1989 - Pydi Sree Rama Murthy
  • 1983, 1985, 1994 and 1999 - Thammineni Seetharam
  • 2004 - Boddepalli Satyavathi
