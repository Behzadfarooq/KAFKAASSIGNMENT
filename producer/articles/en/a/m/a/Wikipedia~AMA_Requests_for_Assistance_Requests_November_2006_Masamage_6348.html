[edit] Questions:

Have you read the AMA FAQ?

  • Answer: Yes.

How would you describe the nature of this dispute? (policy violation, content dispute, personal attack, other)

  • Answer: Ongoing rudeness and incivility.

What methods of Dispute Resolution have you tried so far? If you can, please provide wikilinks so that the Advocate looking over this case can see what you have done.

  • Answer: Just asking him to stop, mainly. Many others have had a similar problem. Somebody brought it up at WP:ANI at one point, but it got no responses.

What do you expect to get from Advocacy?

  • Answer: I would like help with making a Request For Comment.

[edit] Summary:

This user has carefully avoided breaking WP:NPA, which is commendable, but he seems very angry all the time and refuses to participate in polite dicussion. He frequently accuses WP editors of pro-Mormon bias while clearly having an extremely strong anti-Mormon bias of his own. He makes belittling comments about others' intentions and comments, even when they are extremely polite to him. From his talk page, it is very clear that others have had a problem with him in the past; I'm not even aware of the full extent of his behavior.

I first became aware of him in the first area linked above (this one), where he continued to imply that I was lying about my source, even after I scanned in the page I was citing and higlighted the relevant words. He contemptuously addressed me as "Lady" and later said that I don't know what verification is. He was drippingly sarcastic when I made a mistake. In the next few sections, when people started to answer his questions about the history of the church, he dropped out of the discussion altogether.

The second thing I linked, in his talk page, is a recent discussion with various people. He continues to strike me as outrageously disrespectful. I do not want this behavior to continue unabated.

I would like to file an RFC against him, because I don't believe that editors should be permitted to act this way; he makes me feel terrible every time we interact. On the other hand, I am somewhat afraid of the inherent drama of starting an RFC, and I know it will get me more abuse from him, so I'd like help to make sure that I don't do anything wrong.

The specific policies and guidelines I believe he has broken are:

  • Civility
  • Etiquette
  • Resolving disputes
  • Assume good faith
