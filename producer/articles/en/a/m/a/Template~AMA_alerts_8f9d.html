AMA Alert posted: There are 16 cases that need Advocacy (view by date filed). A discussion on how to restructure and improve the AMA is going on at AMA talk page and meeting points are being drafted for the next Meeting. Everyone is invited to help build a consensus as to how to improve and correct issues with the AMA.

[edit] Use

This template is for the AMA to pass along messages to its members and is generally placed at the top of an Advocate's talk page. It consists of 2 parts:

  1. An automatic case counter via Martinp23's AMA Bot that will automatically display if the number of cases reported at {{Wikipedia:Association of Members' Advocates/AMA Alerts/content/num}} is > 0.
  2. A content pane to post announcements. The content pane can be found at Wikipedia:Association of Members' Advocates/AMA Alerts/content.

If there are no new cases reported by AMA Bot, and there is nothing posted in the content pane, this template becomes invisible. :-) אמר Steve Caruso (desk/AMA) 17:10, 25 September 2006 (UTC)
