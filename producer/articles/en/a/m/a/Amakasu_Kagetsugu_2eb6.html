Amakasu Kagetsugu (甘糟景継, Amakasu Kagetsugu?) (????-????) is the name of an elite Samurai officer who served under the Uesugi clan.

Later on under the orders of Uesugi Kenshin, Kagetsugu became the head of the Amakasu clan. Kagetsugu was known as a very valiant warrior, being very skilled in swordsmanship along with spears. Kagetsugu also contributed to Dewa's occupation.
