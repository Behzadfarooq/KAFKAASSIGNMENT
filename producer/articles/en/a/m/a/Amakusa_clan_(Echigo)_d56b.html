Amakusa clan a minor clan during the Sengoku period (16th century) of Japan. This branch of the Amakusa clan had originated in the province of Echigo. Due to this fact, the Amakusa had little choice other than to become retainers under the powerful Nagao clan ( a clan that would later evolve into the Uesugi clan) of Echigo. After Uesugi Kenshin had died during the year of 1578, power within the Amakusa clan had greatly wavered. The Amakusa clan's history is unknown through this point.
