Aman/Research Department top secret report (marked urgent) of October 6, 1973, where the Egyptian and Syrian plan of attack was sketched out by a source (Dr. Ashraf Marwan) later thought to have been a double agent. Scanned from Ronen Bergman and Gil Meltzer's Yom Kippur War, Real Time: The Updated Edition (Yediot Ahronoth/Hemed Books, 2004), p. 47. ISBN 965511597-6)

[edit] Translation

Prime Minister
Defense Minister
Chief of Staff
Operations Director
Intelligence Director
Head of Research
Head of South Branch (M) Head of North Branch (P)
1,2,3,6,(3),5,7, Head of External Communications Unit
Head of Operations Directorate/Operations (Personal)
Unit Head/Field Security
Head of Operative Air Unit (P) Head of Operative Sea Unit (P) Head of Mossad
Top Secret
To Addressee Only
URGENT
Intelligence Directorate/Research
Branch 6
Date Oct. 6 73
Hour 0730
Ordinal Number 429
Copy No. 11
This document includes _____ pages
Intelligence Collection URGENT — Military

Under Discussion: Egyptian-Syrian Plan to Renew the Fighting
  1. The Source [sic.?] sources ... [illegible/censored?]
  2. According to a source (1) assessed as having access to information sources, the Egyptians intend to renew the fighting toward sunset today, Oct. 6 '73.
  3. Herein are the principal points of the attack plan:
a. The target — Occupying the canal's eastern bank, the Sinai front up to the passageways (inclusive) and the occupation of Sharm el-Sheikh.
b. Timetable — The [illegible] hour will be on the [illegible] day before sundown. Operation estimated [to last] 5-7 days.
c. General method — Coordinated and synchronized attack by Egyptian and Syrian armies on the first night of the war. The Egyptian army will cross the canal on a wide front, will occupy the eastern bank as far as Egypt (and as far in general), will occupy Sharm el-Sheikh (landing air and sea forces and deploying a mechanized brigade at the canal's axis), while [undertaking] air attacks deep within Israel (Ashdod, Haifa [,] Acre, Eilat) and attacking military targets in Sinai (oil installations) via 270 fighter planes and bombers. The Syrian army will occupy the Golan Heights and will deploy fighter planes as tactical support to the ground offensive and for attacks deep within Israel (Tel-Aviv), and AF bases (Ramat David, Hatzor, Hatserim 2).
Sensitive Material
Due to the sensitivity of the sources from which this information was obtained, these items are not to appear in their entirety on any additional intelligence publications.
They Are Made Privy to the Addressees Only.
Do not report [the contents of this document] through open cable or wireless communications. Its operative usage – via its rendering into orders, its treatment and study – [is to be undertaken] by those authorized to do so only. It is to be kept at utmost security conditions. There is a total prohibition on creating additional copies from it. For any security failure — report immediately to the Intelligence Coprs[?] security officer

Translator notes:
1. The reference is to Dr. Ashraf Marwan (Hebrew), later thought to have been a double agent.
2. Ramat David Air Force Base, Hatzor Air Force Base, and Hatserim Air Force Base, respectively.
