Amazing Thrills! in 3-Dimension
EP by Man or Astro-man?
Released 1993
Genre surf rock
Label Estrus Records

Amazing Thrills! in 3-Dimension is a Man or Astro-man? promo 7" that was given away with the first 1,000 copies of their debut album, Is It ... Man or Astroman?.[1] It was released on Estrus Records in 1993 on black vinyl only. This is the first time Man or Astro-man's cover of the Mystery Science Theater 3000 theme song was released. It was later re-released on Destroy All Astromen!.
