Amanda Lamb presents A Place in the Sun

Amanda Lamb (born 1972 in Portsmouth, Hampshire, UK) is a British television presenter and former model. She worked as an estate agent for five years and then became a model. In 2001, she became the main presenter of the Channel 4 programme A Place in the Sun. She was the face of the Scottish Widows advertising campaign but was replaced in 2005.

Amanda presented "Hot Shots", an Epson funded programme shown on Discovery Real Time on digital photography which was produced by Twofour. Each programme in the series featured a different photographer teaching a group of amateurs tricks of the trade and is notable as being the last television appearance of Patrick Lichfield.

She competed in the reality television show The Games in March 2006. Amanda regularly appears on UK T.V chatshows.
