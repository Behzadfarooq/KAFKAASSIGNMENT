Amanda Ooms

Amanda Ooms (born September 5, 1964 in Kalmar, Sweden) is a Swedish actress and novelist. She has acted in both film and TV in Sweden and internationally. For many years, she was in a relationship with the Swedish rock star Joakim Thåström

[edit] Partial filmography

  • Sök(2006)
  • Harrys döttrar (2005)
  • Så vit som en snö (2001) as the pioneer Swedish aviatrix Elsa Andersson
  • Mesmer (1994)
  • Kvinnorna på taket (1989)
  • Hotel St. Pauli (1988)
  • Bröderna Mozart(aka The Mozart Brothers)(1986) film debut
