[edit] Deleted for notability concerns

There seems to be a sudden uprising of anonymous users complaining about this locked page, so I figured I'd address it here:

The article was deleted for notability reasons (see Wikipedia:Articles for deletion/Amazing Racist).

It only got locked because a user repeatedly recreated this page in violation of Wikipedia policies (see its deletion log).

If you are concerned about this page being locked, and believe that this comedy act is notable per WP:BIO or WP:WEB, then perhaps the best course of action is for one of you to create a user account, create a proposed Amazing Racist page as a user subpage (see WP:USER), and then come back to this talk page and provide a link to it. However, if you can't find evidence that the subject meets the notability guidelines linked above as WP:BIO or WP:WEB, then you probably shouldn't bother: the article would almost certainly be deleted again on these grounds.

Hope this helps. Vslashg (talk) 19:01, 28 March 2006 (UTC)
