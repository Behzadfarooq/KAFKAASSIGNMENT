You have new messages (last change).

For over a month I have been working on the living biography of Jung Myung Seok. As a student of his religious teachings my aim is to see that only factual and verifiable cited information be included, and that the article be free from conjecture and opinion. Invariably I am successful, that is, until two "opposing" editors undo my edits.

The two editors in question both oppose Jung Myung Seok and his teachings and created the article as a vehicle to, as near as I can tell, spread a malicious form of propaganda against him and his teachings. While I readily admit that there exists a certain controversy over the person and circumstances of Jung Myung Seok, I also remind my "opponents" that being that it is a legal matter, the man retains his innocence.

My only aim is to see the article presented accurately and from a NPOV. I understand that a NPOV article will include factual information that contains negative publicity.

My reason for writing here is that lately the discussion page has turned manipulative. In an attempt to "out" me, the two editors in question called me by what they believed to be my actual name. They were wrong, but it marked an ugly turn of events in our discussion and frankly, quite frightened me. I took one week off from working on the article, only to come back to more of the same.

Clearly there needs somone who can provide some independent help here. While Jung Myung Seok may be entirely unknown to 99.9% of the world, because it is a living biography and because those who come into contact with him and his students generally come here for what they believe is independent research, it is critical that the infromation they recieve be free of malicious intent.

Any help would be greatly appreciated.

Much thanks,

Uptional 06:11, 22 December 2006 (UTC)
