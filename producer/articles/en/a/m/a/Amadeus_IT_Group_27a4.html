Amadeus IT Group

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Amadeus Logo

Amadeus was founded in 1987. Amadeus is the world leader in provision of solutions to the travel industry to manage the distribution and selling of travel services.

Its solutions and services are used by its customer groups in different ways. Close to 84,000 travel agencies and more than 27,170 airline sales offices use the Amadeus System to run their business. Many of the industry's other leading travel service providers use its modular technology to optimise their distribution and internal operational requirements.

The traditional core of its business is travel distribution. Worldwide Amadeus Global Distribution System has the most extensive international distribution network and more international bookings are made through Amadeus than through any other system.

Amadeus was formed by an alliance between Air France, Lufthansa, Iberia Airlines, and Scandinavian Airlines System. It specialises in IT solutions to manage airline, train, cruise ship, rental car, hotel and travel bookings.

Amadeus enables making bookings with:

  • 490 airlines, representing more than 95% of the world's scheduled airline seats
  • 73,000 hotel properties
  • 27 car rental companies, serving over 36,000 locations
  • Other travel provider groups (ferry, rail, cruise, insurance companies and tour operators)

Amadeus has subscribers in more than 217 markets (i.e. countries) worldwide; covering these different markets by delivering localized solutions for marketing, customer services and support through a network of over 70 local Amadeus Commercial Organisations (ACOs).

Over 7,600 people work in the Amadeus worldwide team covering the principal locations and consolidated ACOs. There are 95 nationalities working in Amadeus sites worldwide. US headquarters are in Miami.

The company's headquarters is located in Madrid, Spain. Other major offices are in Sophia Antipolis, France and Erding, Germany. There are also IT services centres in London, UK and Sydney, Australia.

Amadeus is a member of IATA its code is 1A.

[edit] Amadeus GDS

The Amadeus Global Travel Distribution System is the world's largest electronic reservation system by market share. It is used by airlines, hotels, travel agents and other related businesses in the travel industry.
