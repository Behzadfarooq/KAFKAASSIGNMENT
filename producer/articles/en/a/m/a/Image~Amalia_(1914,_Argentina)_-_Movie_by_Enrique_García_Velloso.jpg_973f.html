Shot from the Movie "Amalia" directed by Enrique García Velloso.

  • Director: Enrique García Velloso (died 1938)
  • Script: Enrique García Velloso
  • Producer: Max Glücksmann (died 1946)
  • Camera: Eugenio Py (died 1924)

For more information on the movie, see: http://www.cinenacional.com/peliculas/index.php?pelicula=2893 and http://www.us.imdb.com/title/tt0188405/
