Amaravathinagar (or Amaravathi Nagar) is in the foothills of Annamalai Hills in Coimbatore district, Tamil Nadu, India; about 22 km from Udumalaipettai and around 90 km from Coimbatore. The three major parts in this village are the Amaravathi Reservoir & dam, Sainik School and the Glucose Factory.

All activities in this village revolve around these three factors. The dam has fishing, a park, and a canal system for agriculture.

This place is in valley in the Western Ghats of south India. There are many hills, including Pambu malai and Idilimalai (single stone mountain).

Thoovanam falls is one of the four trekking routes in the Amaravathy range of the Indira Gandhi Wildlife Sanctuary and National Park.
