Hijazi dressed up as a bride for Joe Raed

Before becoming an extremely celebrated pop-star, Lebanese singer, Amal Hijazi started her media career as a highlu successful model. On 2005, Hijazi was exclusively chosen by the famous fashion designer Joe Raed to promote his new line of wedding dresses to be released in the summer season. Hijazi modelled and surprised her fans by her elegant and graceful style, dressed as a bride.

Her ravishing star model appearences

From the beginning, Hijazi worked as a highly successful model and soon her ravishing pop-star appearances were noted by different directors and song-producers and soon, her first single was produced which was called "Halan" ("Right Away")in 2000, it was followed by her second single "Rayyah Balak" ("Put Your Mind at Ease"). Both of them were were surprising hits and on the same year her debut album "Akher Gharam" was anticipatedly released.
