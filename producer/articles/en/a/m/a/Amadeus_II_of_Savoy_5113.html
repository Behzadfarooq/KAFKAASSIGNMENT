Amadeus II of Savoy

Amadeus II (d. 1080), Count of Savoy 1060-1080, jointly ruled with Peter until 1078). He married Joan (Giovanna in Italian) of Geneva and had four children:

  1. Humbert II of Savoy
  2. Adelaide (d. 1090)
  3. Ausilia, married Umberto di Beaujeu
  4. Constance, married Otto II of Montferrat
