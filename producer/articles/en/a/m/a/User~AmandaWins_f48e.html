[edit] About ME!

Why AmandaWins, you might ask? Well, I really don't know why myself. It started in eighth grade in my Civics class, and that's all I know. Short-term memory loss much? I'm new to Wikipedia (I've been a user since March 2007) and I don't even know that much about editing yet. I don't know how to start any fancy-schmancy Wikipedia thingies, so I mostly just copy and paste, for now. My userpage is kind of my sandbox for editing, so there might be mistakes, or you might see a copious amount of edits.

[edit] Articles I Have Edited, and Ones I Probably, Someday, Eventually, Will

I'm obesessed with the FOX program Bones (TV series) so I've made a lot of changes to that article and other ones related to it. I also may edit to the Lake Braddock Secondary School article and Thomas Jefferson High School For Science and Technology articles considering that I am currently attending the former and will be attending the latter. I am absolutely addicted to books, (I am one of those people who the checkout people at the library detest because I bring them so many books) and I'll probably edit to articles about books and authors I've read.
