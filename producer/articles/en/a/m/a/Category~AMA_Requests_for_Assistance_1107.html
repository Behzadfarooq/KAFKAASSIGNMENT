[edit] Requesting advocacy

Members' Advocates offer their assistance in representing you or assisting the presentation of your side of an existing dispute. If you are looking for Mediation or Arbitration, you should start that process in the appropriate manner, and then contact the AMA if you want someone to help represent your side. Members' advocates will also assist in other personal conflicts between users prior to mediation or arbitration stages.

  • Before making a request for Advocacy you must read and understand the Members' Advocacy Information and FAQ pages so that you understand what the AMA is all about.
  • If you are merely looking to get a fellow editor banned rather than take the effort to resolve your dispute, the AMA does not leverage Administrator privileges and thus cannot help you.
  • Consider also to make your request targeted to a specific AMA Team (AMAT) instead. These teams were created for joining advocates having more experience or interest in a specific type of dispute or topic. For this, follow the link given above, search the team most suitable for your case and then make the request following the same instructions below but clearly stating somewhere that you prefer to have an answer from an advocate belonging to the team you wanted.

With all of this in mind, to make a request please follow these steps as closely as possible:

[edit] Step 1

Copy the following text verbatim into the Advocacy Request Form below
Wikipedia:Association of Members' Advocates/Requests/March 2007/USERNAME
and replace USERNAME with your username. Make sure that the month name and year are part of the request.

If you've already made an AMA request in the past, add the number of your current request after USERNAME.


For example, if my username is The Thadman, the text field should read:
Wikipedia:Association of Members' Advocates/Requests/March 2007/The_Thadman


And if I have already made a previous request for assistance:
Wikipedia:Association of Members' Advocates/Requests/March 2007/The_Thadman 2

[edit] Step 2

Click the "Begin request application" button and you will be provided with further instructions.
