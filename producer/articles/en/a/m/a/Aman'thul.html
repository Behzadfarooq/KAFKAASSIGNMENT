[edit] Biography

Aman'thul is one of the Titans that visited the world of Azeroth to bring order. They defeated the Old Gods who roamed the planet, and imprisoned them. Aman'thul is Sargeras' brother and leader of the Pantheon. His body is made of platinum and he carries a staff with a thunder symbol on it. He is the eldest and most powerful of the Titans, and most likely the mightiest being in the universe.

When the Titans completed their task on Azeroth, they charged the great dragons with watching over the newly formed supercontinent Kalimdor. Aman`thul imbued a portion of his power into Nozdormu to guard time itself and police the ever-spinning pathways of fate and destiny.

Aman'Thul's lightning powers were obviously inspired by the Greek god Zeus, but his personality and title are taken from the Viking chief god, Odin the Allfather.
