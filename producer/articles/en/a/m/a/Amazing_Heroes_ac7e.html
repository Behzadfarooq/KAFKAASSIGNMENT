Amazing Heroes

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Amazing Heroes cover by Alan Davis.

''Amazing Heroes'' was a magazine about the comic book medium that was published by Fantagraphics Books from 1981 to 1992.

Unlike its companion title, Comics Journal, Amazing Heroes took a somewhat lighter tone to its examination of comics from a hobbyist's point of view as opposed to an intellectual one.

The regular content included industry news, comics creator interviews, in-depth histories of comic book characters and reviews.

It also featured, among other humor features, the regular comic strip "The Cartoonist" by Teri Sue Wood.

There were regular special editions for previews of upcoming comics to be published and swimsuit editions in which various comic artists do individual pictures of characters, both established and original in bikinis and similar beach apparel.
