[edit] Information

"Amaenbo" (甘えんぼ, spoiled child?) was Ai Otsuka's third single, and the title track was used in an television advertisement for Sato Healthcare's cold relief tablets Stona Rhini S (ストナリニS, sutona rini S?). It also contained a new version of "Sakuranbo" (さくらんぼ, "Sakuranbo"?) ("Cherry") and a new song, "Ame iro parasol" (雨色パラソル, ame iro parasoru?) ("Rain-coloured parasol").
