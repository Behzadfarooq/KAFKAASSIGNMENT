Amanda Brooke and Caitlin Ashley Fein (born 4 September 1992) are twin American actresses.

Their first movie appearance was in Matilda in 1996, playing Matilda as a toddler. Since then they have been in quite a few movies including Deep Impact, Baby Geniuses, and Follow the Stars Home. Since then they have been in various commercials and student films. They have also done work as voice overs in movies such as Santa Clause 3.They were in The X-Files when they were eight years old. The twins have appeared in commercials for General Motors and Cingular.
