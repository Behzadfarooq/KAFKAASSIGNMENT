Amarillo Records is a defunct independent record label that operated out of San Francisco in the 1990s. It was owned by Gregg Turkington. The label specialized in releasing experimental post-punk avant-garde rock and was also known for releasing several solo recordings by Church Of Satan founder Anton LaVey.
