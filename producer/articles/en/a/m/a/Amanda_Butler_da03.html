Amanda Butler

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Amanda Butler is an American basketball player and coach, and is currently the head women's basketball coach at the University of North Carolina at Charlotte.

[edit] Career

Butler, a native of Mt. Juliet, Tennessee, became the sixth head coach of the Charlotte 49ers women's basketball program on April 19, 2005. She was named Atlantic 10 Conference Coach of the Year after 20 wins and a share of the regular season championship in her first year as coach.[1] Her first job as an assistant coach was at the University of Florida, where she played from 1990 to 1994. After leaving her job with Florida in 1997, she coached at Austin Peay (1997-2001), and served as the associate head coach for former Charlotte head coach Katie Meier from 2001 to 2005.
