Amanda Nichole Wilkinson (born January 17, 1982 in Belleville, Ontario, Canada) is a well known country singer. Her first hit solo song was entitled "No More Me and You". She is also a member of the country group The Wilkinsons, which includes her brother Tyler and her father Steve.

She had a number one hit in Canada with her second single, "It's Okay To Cry". Her third single, entitled "Walk Away" has so far peaked at 21 on the Canadian country charts.

In 2006, Amanda was nominated for Single of the Year, CMT Video of the Year, Female Artist of the Year and Album of the Year at the Canadian Country Muisc awards.
