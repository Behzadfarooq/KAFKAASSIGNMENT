Amazonas is the name of four subnational entities in various South American nations, named after the Amazon River:

  • Amazonas (Brazilian state)
  • Amazonas (Venezuelan state)
  • Amazonas Department in Colombia
  • Amazonas Region in Peru
  • Diocese of Amazones - Predecessor of the Archdiocese of Manaus
