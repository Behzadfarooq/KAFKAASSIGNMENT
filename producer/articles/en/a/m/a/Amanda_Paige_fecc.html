Amanda Paige (also known as Paige Gellar) was born on October 28, 1984 in North Carolina, USA and is an American model. She was found by Playboy scouts while attending the University of Virginia where she was a sociology major. She was Playboy’s Playmate of the month in October 2005. Prior to being a Playmate, she was Playboy Cyber Club's Coed of the Month for April 2005. She modeled under the name Paige Gellar. Her real name is Amanda Blair.
