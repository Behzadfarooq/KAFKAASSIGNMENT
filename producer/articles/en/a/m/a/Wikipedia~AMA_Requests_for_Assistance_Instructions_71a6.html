[edit] Step 3: Other Wikipedians this pertains to

In the text box at the bottom of this page, you will notice that there is a heading that reads Other Wikipedians this pertains to.

In this section, list the names of the other Wikipedians that are involved in the "other side" of this dispute. This can most easily be done by using the example listed, replacing USERNAME with their username.

For example, if I wanted to list The Thadman and Pedant, I would do the following:

'''Other Wikipedians this pertains to:'''
<!-- Follow STEP 3 below -->
* {{user|The Thadman}}
* {{user|Pedant}}

[edit] Step 4: Wikipedia pages this pertains to

Next you will see the section that reads Wikipedia pages this pertains to.

In this section, list links to the pages on Wikipedia where this dispute has taken place. These can be articles, talk pages, user pages, or whatever else it may have taken place on. The easiest way to do this is to simply use {{La|Name of Page}} for articles and {{Lat|Name of Page}} for article talk pages, and {{Ln|Namespace|Name of Page}} for other non-talk pages and {{Lnt|Namespace|Name of Page}} for other talk pages. The other namespaces are Template, Wikipedia, User, Category, Image, and Portal.

For example, if I wanted to list User talk:The Thadman, Wikipedia:Association of Members' Advocates and the article Dispute, I would do the following:

'''Wikipedia pages this pertains to:'''
<!-- Follow STEP 4 below -->
* {{Lnt|User|The Thadman}}
* {{Ln|Wikipedia|Association of Members' Advocates}}
* {{La|Dispute}}

And it would produce this:
Wikipedia pages this pertains to:

  • User talk:The Thadman (edit|user|history|links|watch|logs)
  • Wikipedia:Association of Members' Advocates (edit|talk|history|links|watch|logs)
  • Dispute (edit|talk|history|links|watch|logs)

[edit] Step 5: Questions

Next you will see the section that reads Questions:.

In this section, all you need to do is read over the questions that are listed and write your replies next to where it says Answer:. These questions help us better understand your situation and give us the ability to better help you out.

[edit] Step 6: Summary

Finally, you will see the section that reads Summary:.

In this section, the last thing you need to do is write out a decription of the dispute so that we can see exactly what "your side" of the problem is. This can be as long as you want it to be (although describing it in too many words will make things difficult for your Advocate), but you shuld be as thorough as possible.

Once you're finished, doublecheck over all of your information, make sure that the Watch this page checkbox is checked and hit the Save page button to submit your request!

[edit] Where things progress from here

Once your request is recieved, an Advocate will be assigned to you and contact you as soon as possible. The Discussion section on this page is the place on this page for you and your Advocate to talk about the case as well as a place for your Advocate to keep you updated on what they have been up to.

Please be patient as your case is assigned and good luck!
