AMAN Jordan is a human rights organization located in Jordan, mostly targeting women's rights cases and domestic violence against women.

[edit] Site's hacking incident

The Hacking message

In around August 2006, the website was hacked to remove all information and content on the site, and leave the racist message against Arabs:

arab lamers

It appears now to have been restored.
