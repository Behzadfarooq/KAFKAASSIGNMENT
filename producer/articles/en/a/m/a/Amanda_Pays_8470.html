Amanda Pays
Born June 6, 1959 (age 47)

Amanda Pays (born on June 6, 1959, in London, England) is an English actress.

Pays is best known for her roles as Theora Jones in the movie and television series Max Headroom and as Christina "Tina" McGee in The Flash. Pays is an interior designer and hosts Fine Living's The Breathing Room.

She married American actor Corbin Bernsen on November 19, 1988, with whom she has four sons: Oliver, Henry, Angus, and Finley.

She is the niece of former child actress Mandy Miller.

She played Sarah in the 1985 miniseries A.D. and narrated the 1988 documentary Computer Dreams.

[edit] Trivia

Pays had a guest role in a Season 1 episode of The X-Files, playing the role of Phoebe Green.
