Amager Strand station

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

This article or section contains information about a planned or expected future railway station.

Amager Strand station is a future station on the Copenhagen Metro located on northeast Amager. It is currently under construction and is scheduled to open in October 2007.

The station will service the M2 line. It is located in zone 3.
