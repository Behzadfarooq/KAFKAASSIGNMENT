Amazon (band)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Amazon is a Brazilian heavy metal band from Valinhos City, about 80 kilometers from São Paulo metropolis. Formed in 2002 by guitar player Renato Angelo (previously of Counterparts and Mighty Athena) and by the vocalist Sabrina Todt, Amazon is today also lined up by bassist Danilo Angelo and the drummer Marcos Frassão.

Amazon has so far released a single ("While There Is Time"), a promotional edition and a studio edition of the album "Victoria Regia" and the DVD "Ao Vivo em São Paulo" ("Live in São Paulo") recorded during a show in 5 December 2005, when the band shared the stage with Nightwish.

Amazon's web page can be located at Amazon's Official Home Page and contain several information on the band and its agenda.
