Amazon was a syndicated television show created by Peter Benchley. The drama series focused on the survivors of a crashed commercial airlines in the Brazilian Amazon jungle. It was developed by Canadian production companies Alliance Atlantis Communications, WIC Entertainment and German company Beta Film GmbH. The 22 episodes of the series were in first-run syndication between 1999 and 2000.

A novelization of the 2-hour pilot was written by Rob MacGregor.
