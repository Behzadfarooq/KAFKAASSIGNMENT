Amasing Hill (Indonesian: Bukit Amasing) consists of three small andesitic volcanoes. The volcanic complex is located in Bacan island at the west of Halmahera island, Indonesia. The two volcanoes, Cakasuanggi and Dua Saudara, were constructed in the southeast. The other volcano, Sibela Mountains, is a metamorphic complex of volcano.[1]
