Amar Sin Mentiras
Studio album by Marc Anthony
Released 06/08/2004
Recorded Midnight Blue Studios, Miami, Florida.
Genre Rock/Pop/Latin/Latin Pop/Latin Vocal
Label Sony Discos Inc
Producer(s) Estefano

Amar Sin Mentiras is a latin pop album by Marc Anthony released in 2004. It also features the vocals of Jennifer López. Andrew Synowieck (electric guitar), Julio Reyes (piano), Guillermo Vadala (bass guitar), Daniel Avila (drums), Juan Jose "Chaqueno" Martinez, Eduardo Avena (percussion), Alex Batista, Claudio Ledda, Diana Pereyra, Ana Karfi, Dorita, Chavez, Vicky Echeverri, Jose Luis Pagan (background vocals). It won Best Latin Pop Album at the 2005 Grammy Awards.
