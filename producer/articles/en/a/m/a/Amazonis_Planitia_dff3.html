Topography of Amazonis Planitia

Amazonis Planitia is one of the smoothest plains on Mars. It is located between the Tharsis and Elysium volcanic provinces to the west of Olympus Mons in the Valles Marineris region of the Memnonia quadrangle. Its topography exhibits extremely smooth features at several different lengths of scale.[1]
