Amanda Aday

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Amanda Aday in Carnivàle

Amanda Lee Aday (born January 21, 1981 in New York, New York, USA) is an American actress best known for her recurring role as Dora Mae Dreifuss on the first season of the 2003-2005 HBO series Carnivàle.

Aday is the daughter of actor/musician Meat Loaf and Leslie Aday. She attended the Stagedoor Manor Performing Arts Training Center, a summer theatre/dance camp in the Catskill Mountains in New York, from 1990 through 1996, and then graduated from the prestigious Idyllwild Arts Academy in California. She then majored in theatre at the California Institute of the Arts. She is a regular performer in the Blue Sphere Alliance, a theatrical company in Los Angeles that film stars Neve Campbell and Matt Lillard helped start.

She appeared in the 2006 film release, Pledge This!, starring Paris Hilton.
