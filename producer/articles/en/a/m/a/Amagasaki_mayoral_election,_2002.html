Amagasaki mayoral election, 2002

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Amagasaki City held a mayoral election on November 17, 2002. Shirai Aya, backed by Japanese Communist Party (JCP) and the local group Amagasaki Residents Group for Democratic City Administration defeted Miyata Yoshio.
