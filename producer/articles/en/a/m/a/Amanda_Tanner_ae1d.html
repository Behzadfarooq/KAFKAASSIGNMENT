Amanda Tanner is a fictional character in the American dramedy series Ugly Betty. She is played by Becki Newton.

[edit] Background

Amanda Tanner is the attractive receptionist at MODE magazine and is in allegiance with both Wilhelmina Slater and her assistant Marc when it comes to taking down the two major thorns in Wilhelmina's side: Betty Suarez and her boss Daniel Meade (whom she is attracted to). She is the daughter of an investment banker and has hinted in one episode that he is an embezzler while telling Marc what she was planning to do that evening when she left work to go home.

Amanda also has a tendency to overeat or go on non-stop binges whenever she feels threatened as a way to control her fears and insecurities. This issue is expected to be addressed in future episodes, where it is learned that her past involves her uncontrollable eating habits and her attempts to be the "Perfect" woman. Despite her somewhat conceited exterior she isn't that bright and also has a tendency to butcher the Spanish language, using mangled pronounciations like HO-la and Bare-rio.

[edit] Connections

  • Betty Suarez - Assistant to the Editor in Chief at MODE; Amanda's true goal is to take out Betty, by any and all means, so she can get her job and hopefully be the one who Daniel picks. After Betty agreed to take Sofia's job offer, Amanda filled that position, but don't expect this to last either, for upcoming episodes are hinting that she could forced out of hers if Wilhelmina takes over as MODE's editor-in-chief. In the episode "In or Out" she realized that she was indeed overqualifed and gave Betty her job back, but at the sake of her future with the magazine.
  • Daniel Meade - Editor-In-Chief at MODE; He sees Amanda as another one-night stand--he has slept with her but forgot about what happened the following day--and Amanda is none too happy about his womanizing ways, making it more frustrating for her to win him. In the end, it resulted in her finally giving up on going after Daniel after criticizing him on his lustful habits, but did make amends by agreeing on a working relationship with Daniel. She even came close to having a three-way with Daniel and his ex-girlfriend Arien, but nothing came to it. Her feelings for Daniel has put her at odds when Marc forces her to declare her loyalty by sabotaging Daniel's date with a model.
  • Marc St. James - Wilhelmina's Assistant at MODE; A loyal ally who both have a dislike for Betty by humiliating her. After the two took advantage of Wilhelmina's office and after placing a prank phone call to the bandaged woman she and Marc found themselves in hot water, but with Marc making a deal with Wilhelmina, she just learned from him that because of this he told her that it will cost her, and he lived up to that promise by forcing Amanda to sabotage Daniel's date so he can convince Wilhelmina that she is on team Wilhelmina.
  • Christina McKinney - Seamstress at MODE; Amanda frequently calls her "Braveheart" because of her Scottish heritage. In the tenth episode she told "Santa Claus" that she wishes that Amanda had "..A Brain" because she knows how clueless she is when she's around her.
  • Sofia Reyes - Editor in Chief at MYW; In Trust, Lust, and Must she was the catalyst that resulted in Amanda losing her chance to win Daniel's heart. Upon discovering that Sofia was actually using Daniel as the subject of a article for her new magazine in Sofia's Choice, she took her revenge out on her at the elevator (where it all started in "Trust") by beating her up.
