Amarillo National Bank (ANB) is the largest financial institution serving the city of Amarillo, Texas, and is one of the largest family-owned banks in the United States.

ANB was founded in 1892 and was purchased by the grandfather of current chairman Tol Ware in 1909. The Ware family has managed ANB for nearly 100 years, a rare feat in Texas where many banks have been purchased by out-of-state owners.

As of the second quarter of 2006, ANB reports having $1.95 billion in assets. ANB claims to have the most deposits of any financial institution in Amarillo since 1987, a major accomplishment considering that ANB competes with several out-of-state bank companies. According to Ag Lender magazine, ANB is Texas' largest independent cattle lender.

Under the slogan "Absolutely Everywhere", ANB operates 10 full-service banks, 3 drive-up banks, and over 60 automated teller machines (four of which can accept deposits) throughout Amarillo and the nearby cities of Borger and Canyon. ANB also delivers bank statements via e-mail, internet banking, and bank by telephone services.

During the Christmas holiday, ANB gives each of its 500+ employees a $100 check to deliver to the charity of the employee's choice, requesting only that the check be hand-delivered by the employee to the charity.
