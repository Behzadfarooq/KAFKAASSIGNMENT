Amanda Billing, born in 1976, is best known for her role as Doctor Sarah Potts on New Zealand soap opera Shortland Street.

In casting Amanda Billing as Dr Sarah Potts, Shortland Street's producers are continuing the show's tradition of turning talented unknowns into stars.

The former high school teacher has had no formal training as an actor but is already impressing viewers with her work as part of the core cast of Shortland Street.

Amanda Billing grew up in Masterton, and spent her university years in Christchurch. After graduating with a Bachelor of Arts with 1st Class Honours in Geography from the University of Canterbury, she trained at the Christchurch College of Education and became a high school teacher.

Billing was involved in drama throughout her teaching years and recently acted in a number of stage productions including Cloud Nine and The Country Wife both at Auckland's SiLO Theatre. She has worked at several schools throughout Auckland teaching Geography, English and Social Studies, most recently at Rangitoto College.
