[edit] KDE?

amaroK doesn't require KDE, only kdelibs and qt. It can run under Gnome, floxbox, etc... Granted, most people who use it use it under KDE, but it is not -FOR- KDE. I think it would be prudent to remove things such as the picture caption, which reads 'amaroK for KDE' Kunwon1 22:32, 17 March 2006 (UTC)
