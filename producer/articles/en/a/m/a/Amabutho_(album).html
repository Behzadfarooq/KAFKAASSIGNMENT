Amabutho was the debut release by the South African isicathamiya group Ladysmith Black Mambazo. It was released to great acclaim in South Africa, and featured signature tracks like Nomathemba, Nkosi Yamakhosi, among others. The LP sold over 40,000 copies (thus making it the first release by black artists in South Africa to receive gold status).
