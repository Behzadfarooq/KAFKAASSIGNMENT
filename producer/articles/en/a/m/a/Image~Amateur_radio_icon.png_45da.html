[edit] Summary

This small image captures a portion of the front panel of an Elecraft K2 transceiver that was used at the amateur radio station of Tom Whiteside N5TW in Georgetown, Texas, USA during the 2002 ARRL International DX Contest, Phone. The photo was taken on February 10, 2002 by Kenneth E. Harker WM5R of Austin, Texas, USA.
