The Amateur Section A is a Northern Irish football league, it comes under the first 3 Irish league Divisions, however there is no promotion or relegation between those divisions.

[edit] Current Amateur Division A Clubs

  • Albert Foundry F.C.
  • Barn Utd F.C.
  • Comber Rec F.C.
  • Downpatrick F.C.
  • Drumaness Mills F.C.
  • Dunmurry Rec F.C.
  • Islandmagee F.C.
  • Killyleagh YC F.C.
  • Kilmore Rec F.C.
  • Larne Tech. O.B.
  • Knockbreda F.C.
  • Malachians F.C.
  • Newington YC F.C.
  • Nortel F.C.
  • Wellington Rec F.C.
