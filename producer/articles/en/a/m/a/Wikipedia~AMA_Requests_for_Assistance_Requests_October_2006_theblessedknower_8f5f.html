[edit] Summary:

From my experience, wikipedia is not set up for personal tirades, political vendettas, or opinion. So in short, the information listed as to the purpose of the united nation of islam being established is clear and evident without going into a theological discussion. The activities that the UNOI has become known for are well documented in numerous newspapers, and communities across the country. The negative information that was posted by masonuc is unvierfiable as it is untrue and based on tabloid style interviews with former members of the UNOI and no direct contact with the UNOI or its officals.
