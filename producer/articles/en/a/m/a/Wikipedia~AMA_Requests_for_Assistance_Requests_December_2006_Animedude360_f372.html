[edit] Summary:

There is some debate over the page Human (Dragon Ball). A few users (who may in fact be the same person) have contiunually merged it with Dragon World, without first reaching a consensus on the talk page, or even adressing it there. The page has been reverted twice and when asked to atleast discuss the issue on the talk page before such a radical change, these users ignore this request and re-merge the article. Numerous posts on the talk page of Human (Dragon Ball) suggested that a majority of people see the page a useful within the context of races shown on the show. Not to mention numerous other pages of its kind exists, where a medai deals with human beings who differ from real ones and co-exist with diffrent sentient races. This includes the pages Human (Star Wars), Human (Star Trek) and even Man (Middle-earth). I would like for the above listed users to stop merging the page without concent, so that an actually discussion on wheter it should be merged can take place.
