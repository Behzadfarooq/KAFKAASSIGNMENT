Amantes Sunt Amentes (Lovers are Lunatics), is the fourth album by the Mexican rock band, Panda. It was released on October 2, 2006.

The first single is called "Narcisista Por Excelencia" and the video has already been premiered on MTV.

"Amantes sunt amentes" contains a 12 tracks:

  1. "La estrategia perdida"
  2. "So violento, so macabro"
  3. "El infame"
  4. "Estoy más soloh que ayer, pero menos que mañana"
  5. "Narcisista por excelencia"
  6. "Procedimientos para llegar a un común acuerdo" (esta trata sobre sexo casual)
  7. "Tripulación, armar toboganes!"
  8. "Pathetica"
  9. "Los malaventurados no lloran"
  10. "Tus palabras punzocortantes"
  11. "Atractivo encontramos en lo más repugnante"
  12. "Ah pero cómo vendo cassettes!"

The band clames that "[t]his was an unexpected album" in the bonus disc, "it was born in Pepe with a tie and two untuned guitars" (The bonus disc comes with the album), It all began between January and February 2006, but it was finally finished on the May 23, 2006.[citation needed]
