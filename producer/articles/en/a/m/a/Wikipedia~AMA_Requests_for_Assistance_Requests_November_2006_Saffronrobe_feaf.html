[edit] Discussion:

Hello. I don't understand why I am being accused of spamming. When I first joined I did but I hadn't read the instructions and now I have. The external link I am trying to add is to my blog which assists people with questions about the Five Tibetan Rites. I also post articles that help people practice and understand them better. The blog acts as a forum and advice centre for many people all over the world who are interested in doing (or are doing) the Five Tibetan Rites. My blog is www.thefivetibetans.blogspot.com I am a teacher and author of the Rites and have looked at all the external links listed and can't see why I am being excluded. Here are the external links on that page already and why they are either no different to me or are infringing the author and publishers copyright:

  1. http://www.tibetanyoga.com/five_tibetan_rites.html - This is a site that offers classes on the Tibetan Rites which is exactly what I do
  2. http://www.mkprojects.com/pf_TibetanRites.htm - this website has copied the images directly out of the book "Ancient Secret of the Fountain of Youth" ISBN 0385491670 Doubleday; 1st Doubleday Ed edition (January 19, 1999) - she also sells a booklet which is marketed extensively on the web which is a direct copy of the author's book which is still in copyright (1939)
  3. http://www.night-thunder.com/fivesecret.html - images also copied from the book as above
  4. http://www.totallyok.com/yoga/5tibetans.htm - this one is genuine
  5. http://www.lifeevents.org/5-tibetans-energy-rejuvenation-exercises.htm - this one is genuine
  6. http://www.tkdtutor.com/11Training/Rites/TibetanRites01.htm - this one has infringed copyright of the images from an earlier version of the Peter Kelder's books.

Unfortunately, external links to personal blogs violate wikipedia policy (WP:EL, WP:COI and WP:OR), hence why they've been removed. Unfortunately there is'nt muich the AMA can do for you in this case - the policy is quite certain on the issue. Thanks Martinp23 21:49, 2 December 2006 (UTC)
