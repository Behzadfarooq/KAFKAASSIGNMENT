20 to 1 : Amazing Moments in Music was the fourth episode of the fourth season of 20 to 1, an Australian TV show. It counts down the 20 most amazing moments in the history of music. Unlike a similar countdown episode of the series, "20 to 1: Greatest Songs of All Time", this episode focused on the impact the songs had in terms of their video clips and cultural significance.

  1. "Sledgehammer" - Peter Gabriel (1986)
  2. "Thriller" - Michael Jackson (1983)
  3. "Come Into My World" - Kylie Minogue (2002)
  4. "Money for Nothing" - Dire Straits (1985)
  5. "Like a Prayer" - Madonna (1989)
  6. "Addicted to Love" - Robert Palmer (1986)
  7. "Girls on Film" - Duran Duran (1981)
  8. "Take on Me" - a-Ha (1985)
  9. "Rock DJ" - Robbie Williams (2000)
  10. "Get Back" - The Beatles (1969) also features "Where the Streets Have No Name" - U2 (1987)
  11. "Freedom '90" - George Michael (1990)
  12. "U Can't Touch This" - MC Hammer (1990)
  13. "Close to Me" - The Cure (1985)
  14. "Jailhouse Rock" - Elvis Presley (1957)
  15. "...Baby One More Time - Britney Spears (1999)
  16. "Bohemian Rhapsody" - Queen (1975)
  17. "Physical" - Olivia Newton-John (1981)
  18. "Subterranean Homesick Blues" - Bob Dylan (1965)
  19. "Weapon of Choice" - Fatboy Slim (2001)
  20. "If I Could Turn Back Time" - Cher (1989)
