Your change on "Montage (film")

Hi, about your change here: [1]. I agree that the article "montage" is about the film making process, that it is not a film by itself. However, I don't think that a) the tag "filming" properly describes the topic "film making" because it only refers to shooting film, and b) I think that the topic of film is a reasonable closed one that we don't need two extensions to refer to it. You did similar edits to "scene", "Sequence", "Shot", "Wrap party" etc. Do you mind if I change those edits back? Peter S. 15:48, 8 July 2006 (UTC)

I think we are in agreement that those things are not a film, and probably shouldn't be marked as such. I'm a stub-sorter, and {tl|film-stub}} is much different than {{filming-stub}}, so I thought that the articles should match that. At the time, it seemed like a good idea. However, you do have a good point about having two different extensions for essentially the same topic. I don't think I would have a huge problem with you changing them back. However, I've already changed all the articles that linked to them to use 'filming', so you'll have to change them all back. Thanks for letting me know about this, and I think that your reasoning is valid. Have a great day and happy editing! ~ Amalas rawr=^_^= 20:54, 8 July 2006 (UTC)
Thanks for your nice reply! I will change the ones back that I can find and have time for :-) Did you use a tool to automatically change all them or did you do it manually? Cheers! Peter S. 21:14, 8 July 2006 (UTC)
I changed them manually by going to the "What links here" link. There's actually not as many of them as I thought, so it shouldn't be too bad. ~ Amalas rawr =^_^= 00:45, 9 July 2006 (UTC)
Ok, I changed them back. The borders between "film" (as a viewer or knowledgable film geek) and "filming" (as a creator or part of the movie industry) are quickly disappearing. Example Night-for-night - something film geeks know about, but also something film creators care about. I would suggest merging the two. Just my 2 cents, though, I'm no stub-sorter. Thank your for your help! :-) Peter S. 01:06, 9 July 2006 (UTC)
