Amanda Hope neé Campbell (born August 23, 1969) is an American model and actress. She was chosen as Playboy's Playmate of the Month in July, 1992 and has appeared in some Playboy videos. Mrs. Hope seemed to not strike a chord with readers and rarely appeared in the magazine's pages after her initial appearance.

Prior to her appearance in Playboy, Mrs. Hope served two tours in the U.S. Army as a clarinet player. She was stationed at Fort Hood, Texas, with the 1st Cavalry Division and in Bad Kreuznach, Germany, with the 8th Infantry Division.
