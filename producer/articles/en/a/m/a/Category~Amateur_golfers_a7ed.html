Category:Amateur golfers

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

This category contains articles about golfers who either never turned professional or won a professional major championship as an amateur. Most distinguished career amateur golfers belong to the pre-Second World War era, but from time to time this category may also contain articles about leading young players who are yet to turn professional. Only elite standard competitors should be included, not people famous for other reasons who play recreational golf as a hobby.
