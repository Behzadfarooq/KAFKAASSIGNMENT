[edit] Woman in wrestling

There is a mention of how many wrestling programs are scrapped because there are not enough women playing. This allso reffers to other 'men's sports' such as swimming, track, and golf. As a female wrestler and swimmer myself, I beg to differ. Golf, track, swimming and yes, even wrestling are not men's sports. Men's swimming and Women's swimming teams compete in seprete compititions, but competitive swiming in general is not a men's sport. Thus, I have changed other programs for men like golf, track and swimming are believed to be similiarly affected

to the more appropriate:

It is believed by some that when schools cannot add enough opportunities for women they choose to scrap their wrestling programs (other programs that have a primary target of men, such as golf and men's swimming, are believed to be similarly affected.).--Broken 03:18, 29 October 2005 (UTC)
