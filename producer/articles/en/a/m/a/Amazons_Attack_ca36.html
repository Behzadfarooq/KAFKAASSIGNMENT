Amazons Attack

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Cover art to Amazons Attack. Art by Pete Woods.

Amazons Attack is a comics format six-part limited series produced through DC Comics scheduled to be released April 25, 2007. Writer: Will Pfeifer, Art: Pete Woods, Editor: Matt Idelson, Associate Editor: Nachie Castro.

[edit] Plot

After leaving the DC Universe during the events of the Infinite Crisis storyline the Amazons return to attack Washington, D.C. in retaliation to the attack previously made to Themyscira as well as for the illegal prison detainment of Wonder Woman by the Department of Metahuman Affairs.

[edit] Story

The Amazons teleport to Washington, D.C. as a battalion of warriors along with mythological creatures to aid them such as Chimeras, winged horses, hydras and several giant Cyclops. Two Amazons manage to enter the White House in an attempt to assassinate the President of the United States but are stopped by Black Lightning before they could complete their task.

[edit] Trivia

  • The Amazon Artemis will be depicted as the head military leader.
  • Promotional art was released with the Newsarama interview (see below) but was later pulled for unknown reasons.
  • A proposed story titled Amazons Attack was originally slated as another mini-series to lead into the Infinite Crisis storyline before it was cancelled due to timing constraints. Though that project and this one share the same title name, it was a completely different storyline that involved the U.S. military invading Themyscira.
  • This is the first time the Amazons have been depicted as putting aside their traditional garbs for modern militant uniforms.
