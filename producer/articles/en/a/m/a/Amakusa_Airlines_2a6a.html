Amakusa Airlines Co., Ltd. (天草エアライン株式会社, Amakusa Earain Kabushiki-gaisha?) is an airline based in Kumamoto, Japan. It operates a regional service from Amakusa.

[edit] History

The airline was established on 9 October 1998 and started operations on 23 March 2000. It is owned by the municipal government (80%) and Japan Airlines Domestic.
