Amalas the name
The name Amalas comes from a faux-Elvish name generator site [1] Supposedly, Ama- means "beauty" and -las means "wild", resulting in "wild beauty".

Other meanings:

  • Lithuanian: lightning without thunder lt:Amalas
  • Hebrew: toiling
  • Spanish (Ámalas): love them / commanding to love a plurality of female subjects
Amalas the person
I am a twenty-something female graduate of the University of Missouri–Rolla. I majored in Information Science and Technology, with a minor in Business, and an emphasis in Human-Computer Interaction. Say that 5 times fast... I am married to another UMR grad (Math and Computer Science).
