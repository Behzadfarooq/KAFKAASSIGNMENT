Amal Dynasty

The Amal Dynasty was the first and greatest of the dynasties of Ostrogothic kings. The first of the dynasty was Theodemir, the greatest Theodoric the Great, and the last Theodahad. They reigned from before 474 to 536.

The Amal kings are:

  • Theodemir d.474
  • Theodoric the Great 476-526
  • Athalaric 526-534
  • Theodahad 534-536
