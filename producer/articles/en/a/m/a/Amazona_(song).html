Amazona is the third track from Roxy Music's November 1973 album Stranded. It features a very high pitched guitar solo from Phil Manzanera. It was the first Roxy Music song not only written by Bryan Ferry, because it was co-written by Phil Manzanera.

[edit] Used as sample

The opening guitar has been used by Ice T for his song 'That's How I'm Living.
