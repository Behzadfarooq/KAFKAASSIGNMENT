Amanda O'Dell

Amanda O'Dell, Born 1986 to Doreen and Malcolm O'Dell, was a 20-year-old Australian woman whose remains were found by timber cutters off Camp Rd in the Tamban State Forest near Frederickton, 15km north of Kempsey on Saturday 13 May 2006. [1]

She was last seen alive by friends when they dropped her off at approximately 10:30pm on 8 April 2006 at her home in Polwood Street, West Kempsey.

O'Dell was born in Kempsey, went to school at Kempsey High School and was employed as a cashier at Woolworths Kempsey at the time of her disappearance.

Amanda's father, Malcolm O'Dell, made an emotional plea on 20 April 2006, appealing for her safe return. [2]

On 15 June 2006, after a tip from the public and a search of bushland, police recovered the clothes that Ms O'Dell was last seen wearing.

On 13 October 2006, Richard Broeder of Kempsey, an old boyfriend of Ms O'Dell was arrested and charged with Murder.[3]
