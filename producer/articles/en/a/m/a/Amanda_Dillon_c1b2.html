[edit] Brief Character History

Amanda Dillon is the daughter of Trevor Dillon and ex-convict Janet Green Dillon. The Dillon family moved to Colorado. Amanda returned in 2005 to attend school and reconnect with childhood friends J.R. Chandler and Jamie Martin.

When drunken JR hit Amanda with his car, Babe took the fall for JR, so Amanda believed Babe was responsible for hitting her, only adding to the enmity between them. Amanda began experiencing blackouts, and during these times odd events began occurring: Babe was pushed down a flight of stairs, the Chandlers' Thanksgiving soup was poisoned, Little Adam Chandler was kidnapped. Amanda secretly feared that she was responsible for these events and couldn't remember, all the while defending herself against Babe and JR's accusations. Jamie, himself suspecting Amanda, played her champion to get information.

Amanda confessed her blackouts to Josh Madden, who hired her as his assistant at Erica Kane's talk show New Beginnings. Josh began to sabotage Erica, even going so far as drugging her, and framed Amanda. By this time, Amanda realized Janet was responsible for the crimes around Pine Valley, but she protected her mother out of maternal loyalty. As Amanda tried to convince her mother to seek counseling, she was thrown in prison for drugging Erica. Her innocence later proven, Amanda realized her mother was planning on blowing up a charity event and rushed to stop her. Amanda was unable to, and people died in the explosion.

After kidnapping Babe, Janet Dillon was caught but Amanda learned that in her madness, Janet had killed Trevor. Amanda, depressed and ostracized for protecting Janet for so long, was given a job by Greg Madden. Greg, a fertility doctor, convinced a vulnerable Amanda to become a surrogate mother, secretly motivated by his own nefarious intentions. However, Greg was buried in the park and died before he could even get Amanda fertilized with an egg.

Amanda soon focused her attention on keeping Lily Montgomery, her one-time stepsister, and Jonathan Lavery, Lily's new husband, together. Jonathan, who had mentally suffered from the removal of a brain tumor for months, got his mentality back. However, he begged Amanda to keep it a secret when she found out because he didn't want to hurt the autistic Lily. However, Lily found out and soon dumped Jonathan, breaking his heart, while Amanda realized she fallen for Jonathan. She eventually seduced him and they made love. Amanda's life has rebounded in recent months, securing a job at ConFusion and renewing her friendship with Babe. She is also living at Wildwind at Julia Santos' invitation.
