Amazing God is the 25th album produced by Christian singer Marcos Witt. The album was recorded live from Houston, Texas. This was Witt's first English language album recorded.

  7. "I Will Sing of Your Love" (feat. Cindy Cruse-Ratcliff)
  8. "Thank You"
  9. "Aleluya to Our God"
