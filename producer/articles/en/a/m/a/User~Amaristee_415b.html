As you can probably tell, I am somewhat single-pronged in my attack at this beast called Wikipedia. I'm fine with that. If my work did not have such a narrow scope, I'd spend my entire life here!

NOTE: I obviously welcome edits to my articles ... no one's perfect, and my grasp of WikiFormatting is sometimes less than great. Plus, we're a community - not a dictatorship. However, I would appreciate it if you would have some clue as to what you're writing about before you change information I have submitted! Despite the fact that I am a skinny, pale, Goth-type chick, I actually have first-hand knowledge of my topic and know of what I speak.

No, really. I do!

P.S. - Not everyone believes that Stoner Rock and Stoner Metal are the same. When I write Stoner Metal, I've written it for a reason ... not because I'm ignorant ... not because I mistyped ... not because I meant Stoner Rock ... but because whatever I am referring to has been defined as such - whether it be by the artist themselves or by popular consensus. I realize that Wikipedia lumps these two terms together - and I do not at any point contradict that idea - but please know that when I use the phrase Stoner Metal, it's there for a reason. Kindly refrain from changing it to Stoner Rock or deleting it entirely. I'm not being redundant or retarded - I'm being accurate.

Thank you! =)
