Amalarius of Metz

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

You have new messages (last change).

Amalarius of Metz was a liturgist. He wrote extensively on the Mass and was involved in the great Medieval debates regarding predestination. While the exact date of his death is not known, it is believed that he died around 850.
