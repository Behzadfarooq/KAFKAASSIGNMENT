Amazon or Amazons may refer to:

  • Amazons, members of a legendary nation of female warriors in Greek mythology
  • Amazon Rainforest, a moist broadleaf forest in the Amazon Basin of South America
  • Amazon River, the largest river in the world by volume
  • Amazon Basin, the part of South America drained by the Amazon River and its tributaries

In commerce:

  • Amazon.com, an American electronic commerce company based in Seattle, Washington
  • Amazon Bookstore Cooperative, an "independent feminist bookstore" in Minneapolis, Minnesota

In literature:

  • Amazons (comics), legendary warrior women from the DC Comics universe associated with the superheroine Wonder Woman
  • Swallows and Amazons (series), a series by Arthur Ransome about two girl sailors and would-be pirates
  • Amazon (comics), also known as Man-Killer, a villain in the Marvel Comics universe
  • The Amazon (book), a book by Frederick Smith, 3rd Earl of Birkenhead

In music:

  • Amazon (band), a Brazilian heavy metal band formed in 2002
  • Amazon (Ruslana song), song by the winner of the 2004 Eurovision , Ruslana

In television and film:

  • Amazons (1986 film), a 1986 Argentine adventure science fiction fantasy epic
  • Amazon (TV series), a 1999 TV series adaptation of a Peter Benchley story
  • Amazon Women on the Moon, a 1987 film written by comedy duo Michael Barrie and Jim Mulholland

In vehicles:

  • HMS Amazon, the name of four ships of the British Royal Navy
  • Volvo Amazon, a car model from Volvo produced from 1956-1970
  • Amazon, a GWR Iron Duke Class steam locomotive

Other:

  • Amazon parrot, a species of parrot from the Americas
  • Dahomey Amazons, an all-female regiment of the African kingdom of Dahomey
  • Amazon feminism, dedicated to the image of the female hero in fiction and in fact
  • Game of the Amazons, a board game
