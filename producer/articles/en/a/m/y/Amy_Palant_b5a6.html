Amy Palant

Amy Palant is an American voice actress, who works on the properties of 4Kids Entertainment.

She is best known for playing the voice of Miles "Tails" Prower in the newest Sonic the Hedgehog games, such as Shadow the Hedgehog, and sometimes credits herself as Vanessa Yardley.
