Amy Aquino is an award winning American television and stage Actress.

[edit] Biography

Amy Aquino has been a steadyworking actress for over ten years. After graduating from Harvard University in 1979 with a degree in Biology she took on drama at Yale School of Drama. She has since gained notability for playing many tough, no-nonsense characters. Having recurring roles in several hit television shows such as ER, Felicity, Crossing Jordan, Peggy on Everybody Loves Raymond, (in one episode beating up Ray Barone (Ray Romano)), an award winning character on Picket Fences, and more, she has gained a fan following. As she has starred in comedy films such as A Lot Like Love and In Good Company she has also guest starred on the hit comedy series Weeds.

[edit] Personal life

Amy Aquino has been married to Drew McCoy since 1995.
