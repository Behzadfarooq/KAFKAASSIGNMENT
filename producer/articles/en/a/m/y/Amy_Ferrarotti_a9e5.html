Amy L. Ferrarotti born December 17, 1979 in Waterbury, CT, is a popular Television and Radio voice in the state of Connecticut. She began her radio career in early 1998 at Litchfield, CT's radio station 97.3 WZBG. She has had a long career in Marketing Promotions, and radio producing throughout the radio medium. Also known as Jodi Scott, she has worked on several other Connecticut radio stations, making her one of the most broadcasted voices in Connecticut. Currently, she is the Promotions Director with WZBG, and is the On-Air afternoon DJ.

[edit] Early life and career

She received her bachelor's degree from Quinnipiac University in 2001, with a degree in Communications. In early 1998, she began an internship with Litchfield, CT radio station FM 97.3 WZBG. Today, joins Dale Jones as the on-air talent broadcasting to Northern Connecticut.
