Amy O'Neill

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Amy O'Neill (born July 8, 1971) is an actress/performer. She is probably best known for her role as Amy Szalinski in Honey, I Shrunk the Kids.

[edit] Biography

Amy Kendra O'Neill was born to Tom O'Neill and his wife Ginny. She has quoted she had a great childhood. She also had five siblings: Casey, Katie, Hugh, and Barry. She is the neice of Hugh O'Neill, Esq, former Deputy Chief Counsel to The Secretary of the Navy, John Lehman She first appeared in series like Mama's Family, Matt Houston, Night Court, and The Twilight Zone. Then she landed her big role in the 1989 hit Honey, I Shrunk the Kids and it's 1992 sequel Honey, I Blew Up the Kid. After White Wolves: A Cry in the Wild II, she appeared in series and then post-poned the acting business. She now lives in LA and is 1/3 of the circus trio "Girls on stilts".
