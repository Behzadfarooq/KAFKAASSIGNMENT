Amy Roloff
Born September 17, 1963

Amy Roloff (b. 1963), is best known for appearing with her family in the reality television program Little People, Big World seen on TLC. Amy Roloff is a dwarf, or little person, as is her husband (Matthew Roloff) and one of her four children.
