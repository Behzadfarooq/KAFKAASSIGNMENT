Amy Yeager

Amy Yeager, known as Ayumi Yoshida (吉田歩美, Yoshida Ayumi?) in the original Japanese anime and manga, is a character of the anime and manga Case Closed, known in Japan as Detective Conan (名探偵コナン Meitantei Conan).

Amy is introduced into the series after Conan Edogawa joins her class. Soon after, she, along with Conan Edogawa, George Kojima, and Mitch Tsuburaya, form a group they name the Detective Boys. After Ai Haibara is introduced to the series, Ai also joins their group. The badges Hiroshi Agasa makes for the Detective Boys contain a voice transmitter as well as a tracker which can be seen on Conan's "radar glasses".

Amy has a personality much like any other normal schoolgirl, and is obsessed with popular trends in her school as Masked Yaiba and Japanese monster films.

[edit] Trivia

  • It has been implied that Amy has a crush on Conan. In the first movie, she openly tells Rachel of her love for him.
  • Starting in volume 39 of the manga, Amy becomes the only one of the children who uses Ai Haibara's first name, calling her "Ai-chan" in the Japanese version. Mitch and George call her "Haibara-san", while Conan simply calls her "Haibara."
