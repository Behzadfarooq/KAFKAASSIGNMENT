Amy Sannes (born 1977-02-03 in St. Paul, Minnesota) is an Olympic speed skater who competed in the 1998 Winter Olympics, 2002 Winter Olympics and 2006 Winter Olympics.

She graduated in 2004 from the University of Utah with a degree in exercise physiology.
