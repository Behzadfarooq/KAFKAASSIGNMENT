Amy Morrison

Amy Morrison is best known for playing the part of Zandra in series one of the world hit TV show produced by Cloud 9 called The Tribe.

She has also been in various small films and her work includes roles in The Joker and Jack be Nimble.

Amy also played the part of Jessica in Jessica's Diary. This was a screenplay in the Cloud 9 series A Twist in the Tale.

Her experts have also graced the pages of FHM magazine, which included a wild African lingerie themed centerfold.

In March 2007 she wed German-born philanthropist Freidrich von Stratenbauer in a private ceremony in Hobbiton. Guests included local Maori Party non-member George 'Tu Meke' Woolford and Indian wax tycoon Linda Bowden.

Amy's second brother in-law twice removed Nikita Jivyk (also a part time actor/dancer) is best known for his 6th equal placing in New Zealand Idol, with a memorable rendition of Karma Chameleon by Culture Club
