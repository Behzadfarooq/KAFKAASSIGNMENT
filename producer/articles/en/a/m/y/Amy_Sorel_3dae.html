Amy Sorel (エイミ・ソレル, Eimi Soreru?) is a fictional character designed for the Soul Series of fighting games. Amy made her first appearance in Soul Calibur II (albeit only in the opening video sequence) and has returned as a playable bonus character for Soul Calibur III and as a full main character in Soul Calibur III: Arcade Edition.

[edit] Biography

Amy was a poor orphan who lived on the streets of Rouen. Her parents died of a plague that had spread across all of Europe during this time period. She had no other family to go to after the loss of her parents. One day she came across a man named Raphael, who was being chased by the local authorities. She hid Raphael from the soldiers who were pursuing him; through only in spite of the soldiers, who ran her poverty stricken town. As Raphael had never owed his life to anyone before, he felt he must protect and repay her. He felt something he never had before for Amy, so took her in to his home and looked after her as his own daughter, to repay her for the kindness she had shown to him.

One day, Raphael returned from a vicious battle with the wielder of Soul Edge, Nightmare. Raphael collapsed before Amy's eyes and she nursed him back to consciousness. When he came round he told her that through the battle he had become infected by the evil seed, and by handling his blood, she was also infected. Amy had always found it hard to open her heart to the world, but now she couldn't even try because she was too different from everyone else. She was no longer human. She went with Raphael to the castle he bought in Romania. She sat in a dim room seeing the torches and troops gathering there. Raphael left, saying he will be out for a while. He took care of the troops and the noises ceased. They eventually returned, but he didn't. He left her... And now the noises and light spread violently in the world he made, bringing it to ruins. She stood up and went to the exit. She was bitten the words from those days and smiled. For she must protect this world that he made.

[edit] Soul Calibur 3 ending

Because she isn't a official character she has no SC3 ending. However she appears in both of Raphael's. The one where the buttons aren't pressed, she is healed by Kilik while an infected Raphael heads their way. If the buttons are pressed then he will arrive to a still cursed Amy with plans to make her a world.

[edit] Weapons

-- Albion

Amy wields a rapier named Albion. Aside from its name, nothing else is known about it. It was probably given to her by Raphael. However, it closely resembles the Queen's Guard, Raphael's ultimate weapon from Soul Calibur II.

[edit] Stages

-- Romanian Valley - Castle Siege (SC3:AE)

Amy shares this stage with Raphael. It has been more than 100 years since Vlad the Impaler led his army against the Ottoman Turks and crossed swords with the Sultan in order to protect this land and his home country of Walachia. Located somewhere in the deep mists of the southern Carpathian Mountains, this castle was not originally designed for a battle of that nature, but it was now about to face exactly that. The army of the Duchy of Walachia surrounded the castle and, seeing that the master of the castle was not going to give up without a fight, began their attack. No one knows why the national army initiated such a large-scale move, but the rumors say that a contagious disease that brings with it mysterious symptoms was spreading from beneath the castle, and the country had moved to destroy it at the source.

[edit] Trivia

Amy's 1P and 2P costumes.
  • According to some artworks, Amy has a serious scar in her leg, which could be a weak spot or a serious problem for her mobility.
  • Amy is a Bonus Character made using original (not unlockable) parts from Character Creation; she even has her own body build and a credited voice actor. She represents the custom discipline "Rapier".
  • Amy can be found on Tales of Souls mode as a "Prepare to Defend Yourself" challenge in the Romanian Valley - Castle Siege, Raphael's stage. In the Arcade Edition, it turns into Amy's level as well.
  • Amy appears as a standard character along with fellow Bonus Characters Hwang and Li Long in the arcade version of Soul Calibur III, "Soul Calibur III: Arcade Edition". She has an updated movelist and a completely new 2P costume, replacing the Color Edit she has in the console version.
  • Amy's Destined Battle (in Quick Play) is Raphael.
  • Amy is rumored to become a main character in Soul Calibur 4.
  • Amy would be the third youngest playable character in the series, being older than Astaroth, who, was created less than ten years ago, and Charade, who appears to have been created only recently.
  • She has a cameo in the opening intro on the scene of Raphael in Soul Calibur II, and it is known that Raphael took her in family as adoption.
  • Although it's hard to tell since she has no dialogue during battle, she has the same voice actor as Lynette
