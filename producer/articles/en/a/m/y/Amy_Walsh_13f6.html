Amy Walsh

Amy Heather Walsh (born September 13, 1977 in Saint-Bruno, Québec) has been a midfielder for the Canada women's national soccer team since 1998. She attended the University of Nebraska, where she was twice named first team All-Conference and once first-team All-Central Region. She has played professionally for the Montreal Xtreme, and currently plays for the Laval Comètes of the W-League.
