Amy Robbins is an English actor born in 1971 at Higher Bebington, The Wirral, England.

Robbins is currently appearing as Dr. Jill Weatherill in the ITV1 drama series The Royal.

She came from an acting family and trained at RADA. She also gained an English and Drama degree from Goldsmiths College, London University.

Robbins is married to her co-star Robert Daws who plays Dr. Gordon Ormerod in the series. They have two daughters.

She is a second cousin of ex-Beatle Paul McCartney.

Prior to her current role she played Police Sergeant Rachel James in the BBC One hospital drama Casualty for two series.

Robbins also appeared on film, playing the part of Sylvie in the 2002 film Killing Me Softly.
