Amy Lalonde

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Amy Ciupak Lalonde is a Canadian-born television personality for Scream.

She has made guest appearances in Mutant X, Queer as Folk, Kevin Hill, Beautiful People, Battlestar Galactica, Jeff Ltd and Love Bites. Amy has appeared in numerous commercials including Molson's "It Starts Here" ad campaign as Allison the cowgirl on the airplane. Amy currently has been seen in commercials for "Kit Kat" (playing a snobby socialite daughter) as well as in a commercial for "Special K" cereal (as a devastated girl who just broke up with her boyfriend) and also a Leons commercial.

Amy is represented by B&M Model Management for modelling and Amanda Rosenthal Talent for acting.[citation needed]. She was previously a high school teacher at Notre Dame College School in Welland, Ontario and New Zealand.

[edit] Filmography

Amy completed her first two feature films in 2006: Heartstopper and 5ive Girls. Her TV Movies of the Week include, Murder in the Hamptons, Earthstorm, and CBC's "Victor- the Victor Davis Story"- set to air in March of 2007. Amy is presently filming George A Romero's "Diary of the Dead", where she landed a lead role as Tracy Thurman. This feature film is set to be released in 2007.
