[edit] About Amyeis

Amyeis is many things, but above all she is herself. Even if bestowed with a myriad of epithets, no given title could possibly convey her true nature. Indeed, how could a human soul be translated into such a flawed and limited vehicle as written language, without that creative flair which lies in the heart of all people? That poetic zeal, that lust for life that gives birth to a dancing star. That dionysian ethic which drives us to love, to hate, to madness and insanity which we have learnt to smother with apollonian anti-morals of order. Nay, it is not the idea which moves mountains but the ideal! Only with the strength of conviction can you begin to transcend your humanity; When you refuse to give up with all your heart and press on into the abyss.
