Amy Bruckner (born Amelia Ellen Bruckner on March 28, 1991 in Conifer, Colorado) is an American actress who is famous for her participation in the Disney Channel shows Phil of the Future and American Dragon: Jake Long.

[edit] Trivia

  • Amy is a tap dancer.
  • Has one sister named Annye and a dog named Tilly (short for Matilda).
  • Is 5'3".
  • She loves to sing.
  • She is a sophomore at Athens Academy in Athens, Georgia.
