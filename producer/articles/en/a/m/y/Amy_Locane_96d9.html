Amy Locane

Amy Locane (born December 19, 1971 in Trenton, New Jersey) is an American actress who starred on the hit TV series Melrose Place. She also played a lead role in the film Cry-Baby (1990), starring Johnny Depp.

On January 23, 2007, Amy Locane and her husband Mark Bovenizer welcomed a baby daughter, named Paige Cricket. Source: Celebrity Baby Names Blog.
