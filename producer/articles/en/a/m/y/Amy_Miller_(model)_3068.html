Amy Miller is an American actress and model. She has been featured on the cover of several Playboy Special Editions, starred in the Las Vegas Cabaret show X: An Erotic Adventure, been a spokesmodel for a number of products, and was Playboy's Cyber Girl of the Week for the second week of November 2000.
