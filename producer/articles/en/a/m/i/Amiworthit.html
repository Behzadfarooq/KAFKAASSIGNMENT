[edit] About

Amiworthit.com is an online social network (See List of social networking websites) for students, into which applicants must be accepted by current members.[1][2]

[edit] History

Amiworthit was started by two (ex)Oxford students, Kirill Makharinsky (one time Graduate of the Year (UK)) and Matt Foster, who is in his final year.

[edit] Media

The site has featured in The Sunday Times (UK)[3], the Oxford Student[4], Cherwell (newspaper)[5], Oxide Radio, The Cambridge Student, Varsity (Cambridge) [6] and many other student media.
