Amityville 1992: It's About Time! was the sixth installment to the Amityville Horror saga. It was released direct-to-video in 1992

[edit] Plot

Jacob Sterling brings home a mysterious clock from the infamous Amityville house, not knowing that it's haunted by demonic spirits.
