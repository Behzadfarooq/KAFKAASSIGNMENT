I am an Australian editor located in Sydney, New South Wales. I primarily do 3 things:

  • Work on building, structure and landmark articles for the inner-west of Sydney and on general articles Australia-wide.
  • Spend a lot of time on Wikimedia Commons, here contributing numerous photographs for articles.
  • Anti-vandal work using Vandal Proof and Popups.


If I have recently reverted your vandalism and you are here to take revenge on my user page, please consider writing something clever or funny rather than calling me an obscenity or just blanking the page. Your handywork will only be visible for a very short time so I would prefer to marvel at your creativity rather than be offended by it.
