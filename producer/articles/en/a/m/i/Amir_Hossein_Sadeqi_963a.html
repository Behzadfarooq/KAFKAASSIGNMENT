Amir Hossein Sadeqi (Persian: امیرحسین صادقی‎ , born September 6, 1981 in Tehran, Iran) is an Iranian football player who currently plays for Esteghlal F.C. in Iran's Premier Football League and the Iran national football team. He usally plays the defender position.

He was named one of 23 members of Iranian national football team in 2006 World Cup in Germany.
