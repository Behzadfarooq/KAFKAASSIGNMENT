Ohad Shem Tov (born 1979) is one of the leaders of the Israeli Ale Yarok (Green Leaf) party, and founder of the Ale Yarok Cell (student group) in the Hebrew University. In the national legislative election in 2006 he was placed third in the party list. With 40353 votes, Ale Yarok didn't pass the 2% barrier required to enter the Knesset.

Shem-Tov as a claimed political figure in Israel is responsible for many demonstrations and speeches, favouring the use and the legalization of marihuana.

He appeared several times in front of the Knesset, and was interviewed on Israeli television.
