[edit] Introduction

This page contains details of the proposed cleanup of the category Power stations in Australia.

The proposed changes are as follows:

  • Standardise article names to the format [Power Station Name], [State].
  • Addition of the Power Station infobox to all articles.
  • Creation of stubs for missing articles.
