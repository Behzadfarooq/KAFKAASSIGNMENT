Ami James (April 6, 1972), is an Israeli-born American tattoo artist. He is the co-owner of Miami Ink, a tattoo shop located in Miami, Florida and the subject matter of the eponymous TLC reality television program.

James began tattooing at age 20 and is now the co-owner of Miami ink with Chris Nu単ez. In addition to his career as a tattoo artist, James served in the Israel Defense Forces and co-owns Deville USA, a clothing design company, along with Nu単ez and Jesse Fleet. He has also created designs for Motorola's RAZR V3 mobile phones.[1]
