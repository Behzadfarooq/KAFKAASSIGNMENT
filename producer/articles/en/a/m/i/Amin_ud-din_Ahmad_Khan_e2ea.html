Amin ud-din Ahmad Khan

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

You have new messages (last change).

Amin ud-din Ahmad Khan was the last Nawab of princely state of Loharu. He was the governor of Himachal Pradesh and Punjab. Before that he was a member of Legislative Assembly of Rajasthan state. He was a leader of Indian National Congress party.

He briefly served the Indian army and saw action during the liberation of Portuguese India in 1961 .

He donated the famous and valuable library collection of his family to the Raza Library in Rampur.
