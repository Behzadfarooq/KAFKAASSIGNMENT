Amina Annabi

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Amina Annabi (born 5 March 1962 in Carthage, Tunisia) is a French-Tunisian singer-songwriter and actress, most famous for finishing second in the tied Eurovision Song Contest 1991.

[edit] Discography

  • Nomad - Best Of, 2001
  • Amina Annabi, Polygram, 1999
  • Wa di yé, Philips, 1992
  • Yalil, Philips, 1989
