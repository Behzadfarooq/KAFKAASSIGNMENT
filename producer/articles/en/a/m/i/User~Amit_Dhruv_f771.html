Amit Dhruv

Amit was born in beautiful Indian Island Diu in 1980 in Dhruv family. His father is Mr Jayendra.N.Dhruv who is Head Master at Catholic School NMHS and a respectable citizen of Island, Mother is Mrs Jasuben J Dhruv who is also Teacher at Govt School in Diu. He has 2 Sisters and 1 brother. Jigna is eldest, Lt.Damini(1978-1995) was also elder than Amit and younger brother Rahul. Amit married to beautiful Jalpa on 20th January 2005 at Bhavnagar. He loves his family and condsider himslef as lucky to have such a wonderful parents, sis&bro and Wife.

Amit is Hindu and follow Vaishnav routes of Hinduism formed by Lord Krishna. He respect all relegions and support all ideas of Mahatma Gandhi. Being Vaishnav he do not consume Liquor or Non-Vegetarian food, he also do not smoke. Diu is his favourite place on earth. He has done his major MBA project on Diu Tourism.

He got his intial education upto SSC at NMHS school in Diu. He went to Ahmedabad for his further education, he did his HSC at Holy Child School specialisg in Commerce. He had lot of support from his cousin brothers Pareshbhai and Hiteshbhai in terms of education and career. They guided Amit to real and true path of success. He did Graduation in Commerce from HK Commerce College, Ahmedabad. During his graduation he was living at Shri Sthanakwasi Jain Hostel, were he mate his two very good friends Krunal Shah and Vivek Shah, known as Don and Dav respectevly. Hostel was located on prime location the C.G.Road.

Being always motivated by his father Amit got ambition in life and joined MBA at Bhavnagar. He joined MBA with aim of getting good education and a good job. He specialized in Marketing and was University 2nd. These two years were his best time of his life so faar, he good result, nice training, good job @ Wiptect, his dreamgirl Jalpa and Best Mate Mahendra Zala. He was liked by all at department except couple of persons. He finished MBA in 2002.

Not having any luck in finding good job in India he went UK for further short Course and further career. He selected Thames Valley University, London for his Graduate Cerificate in Hospitality. His Cousin sister Rajuben helped him to come to UK. After 6 months of class room studies he got Jon Placement at Thistle Hotels as F&B Supervisor and HR Administrator. This was his first Uk Hospitality Experience.

Career moved further in right direction and he got job with [Hilton Hotels ]as Revenue Co-ordinator.

At present he is happily living in UK with his wife Jalpa.
