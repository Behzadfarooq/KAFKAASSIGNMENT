[edit] Instructions

Before posting here make sure that you have contacted the vandalproof user.

To Report Abuse copy and paste the following onto a new line at the bottom of the page:

  • =={{vandal|username}}==

Infomation to include are the following:

  1. include a description of the incident
  2. add links to edits where you consider VP has been used abusively.
  3. sign your posts! (use ~~~~.)
  4. Try to be as polite as possible!
