Amity International is a higher secondary school in Sector-44, NOIDA. It was founded in 1994 by Dr. Ashok K Chauhan and Dr. Amita Chauhan.

[edit] History and Traditions

Amity International Noida, like other Amity educational intitutions, is controlled by Ritnand Balved Education Foundation (RBEF).
