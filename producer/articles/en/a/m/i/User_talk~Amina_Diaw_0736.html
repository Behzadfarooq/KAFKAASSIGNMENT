[edit] What is Shea butter?

Some products like Shea butter shown to be superb moisturizer, with special healing properties for the skin and the hair. Vitamins in this natural product are important for improving a number of skin conditions. They have goods ingredients that heal many skin allergies and a number of other skin forms. The natural products like Shea butter are very essential for skin beauty, for outer beauty also matters. [1] They play a vital role in our daily lives, and this product is also the body’s best friend.

[edit] The composition of Shea butter.

Shea butter is an unrefined vegetable based triglyceride which offers high performance in terms of skin feel and low melting point. It has been used in Africa for many centuries to ease problems of the skin and scalp. [2] It is commonly know as karite which is derived from the Shea nut tree that grows in the western part of Africa. The fruits of this tree contain a nut which is dried and ground, the powder is then boiled in water to release an oily substance which rises to the top and hardens to create Shea butter.


[edit] The best place to find Shea butter is Africa.

[edit] Historical background

It has been used in Africa for many centuries to ease problems of the skin and scalp. [2] It is commonly know as karite which is derived from the Shea nut tree that grows in the western part of Africa. The fruits of this tree contain a nut which is dried and ground, the powder is then boiled in water to release an oily substance which rises to the top and hardens to create Shea butter. This butter have been used for ages in Africa to moisturizes and protect the skin from sun, wind and salt water. It can be used for all form of massage as it creates a frictionless surface allowing for the smoothes and most healing deep tissue work. It cannot steal from the skin it natural oil instead it adds essential oil to the skin. Over the past decades, Shea butter has played a very significant role to the women of West Africa, especial in the preparation of traditional medicines, to cook food, to massage newly born babies and to soften wrinkles [1] This Shea nut tree where it is derived from is often referred to the tree of life by most African women.

[edit] The reasons for why African women use Shea butter

Moreover, this wonderful healing and skin care product is also used for speeding up healing of the umbilical cord and after circumcision. It is however; mostly for skincare that Shea butter is hailed for its protecting and emollient properties. Protecting the skin and the hair from harsh African climate is fundamental, and this naturally product does so beautifully. Also, new born babies are welcomed by a powerful massage with Shea butter to product them from bad weather. In the past years scientist have observed that, within the population using Shea butter, the occurrence of skin diseases was rare and the population’s skin was remarkably agile and smooth. Shea butter is extremely high in vitamins A, E, and F, Shea butter provides skin with all the essential elements it needs for good balance, elasticity and tone.[4]

[edit] Reports from specialists about Shea butter

The dermatologist Irmin Durand 2004 argues that the relationship of Africa with Shea butter is intimate [5] in nature and that “the secret of African beauty lies in it natural skin produced”. In the west, Shea butter is becoming a miraculous, flexible product that promises soft, moisturized skin, effective sun protection, skin aging treatment and healthy scalp. After three month study of Shea butter, the secretary of the women’s club at Suffolk University Dakar Campus reported that 86% of Senegalese family uses karite for skin protection, to strengthen hair and for massaging. Shea butter is becoming a highly demanded skin care product in Senegal.

Moreover some researches have been done by students from different schools including Suffolk university Dakar Campus .The study consisted of 10 students from Suffolk university, 15 from (Institut Superieur de Management) and 10 from (School of Management) both male and female. Most of these students that were placed on survey have been using Shea butter on their skin and hair, whilst others used product like Clear Essence lotion and some other used Skin Success lotion. These students were grouped in three groups according to their schools and the product they are going to used and as well as divided them into two sections and studied them by sex as given in the result data below. The three groups which comprises of both sexes, and two sections which discriminated both sex were required to use the products for a period of one month to see the benefit and essential effect on their skin and hair. Questionnaires were given to these students at the end of the one month and questions were answered swiftly for 30 minutes or more. The three groups were examined carefully. Each student expressed their opinion about the product they used and talked about why they liked the product, while few say what they dislike. The questionnaire shown a very important uses of Shea butter and benefit one can gain. As I questioned a girl who was among those that started using the product few months ago, she said to me “I needed help with my acne and dry hair, a friend told me about Shea butter and bought it. The first few weeks of using it was impressive and has helped me with my decision. After using this product for the month you asked us to used, I saw dramatic changes in my skin and hair. The blemishes on my face are disappearing, thanks to Shea moisturizer my skin has a natural health glow to it and my hair love for perm and does not break anymore”. A high percentage of students, recommended the use Shea butter for healthy, glowing and supple skin. Whereas,a minoriy opposes it because they don’t like the smell, and most of these students said it makes them feel uncomfortable, so they prefer the use of the other product. It was a debated issue though, because those that used other products said they would prefer Shea butter which is natural, whilst few people disagree. And when I observed carefully the two distinguish sexes (that uses Shea butter), I found out that the essential effects it has on the female is the same for the male students and also the difference in the benefit that was obtained was not a huge gap. The reason why women benefit mostly from the Shea butter is because female skin is not the same as male. Girls have a more sensitive, soft, soothing and supple skin unlike boys.[3] So the effect is more likely to be seen in female than male.
