Amidohydrolase

Amidohydrolases (or amidases) are a type of hydrolase that acts upon amide bonds.

They are categorized under EC number EC 3.5.1 and 3.5.2.

Examples include:

  • Beta-lactamase
  • Histone deacetylase
  • Urease
