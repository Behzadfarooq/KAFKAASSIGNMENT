Amistad

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

"Amistad" is a noun in Spanish meaning friendship. It may refer in English to:

  • La Amistad, a 19th century Spanish schooner
    • The Amistad, a 1841 United States court case concerning a slave rebellion on that ship.
    • Amistad (1997 film), a movie about the 19th century ship and court case.
  • Amistad/Amitie, an 18th century schooner, which transported Acadians from France to Louisiana.
  • Amistad National Recreation Area, including the Amistad Reservoir, Texas.
  • La Amistad International Park, a large International Park in Panama and Costa Rica.
