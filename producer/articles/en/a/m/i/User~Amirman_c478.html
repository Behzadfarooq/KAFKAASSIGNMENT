[edit] About Me

I took the Myers Briggs Personality test and it turns out i am "the visionary",my type is ENTP (Extraverted iNtuitive Thinking Perceiving). i like to make things that do things. I prefer muffins to cupcakes. i like doing really quiet and very loud things. I was born in Iran and moved to Canada when i was a baby, then i moved to Eastern Kentucky (see Appalachia) at the age of 7. I get along with almost anyone. I love to rocknroll (I play a lot of string instruments), i love lo-fi recordings and noise shows. I write words that sound nice together sometimes. I play bass in a band called ATTEMPT with some of the best musicians in lexington. As of now my life goals include Owning a Mule (named Alvar Nunez Cabeza de Vaca after a historical hero) which i use to carry things and to go on long adventures in the mountains, i'd like to have a Saint Bernard one day too. i plan on writing books about the systems that guide our lives and how we can control them, making magick happen, build things, play music, move on from industrialism, spend time with my baby boy, eat only food i grow or where i know it was grown. i go on mystical adventures. I HATE fluorescent lights, ALOT.
