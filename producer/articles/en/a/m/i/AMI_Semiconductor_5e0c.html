AMI Semiconductor is a company engaged the design and manufacture of customer specific integrated circuit solutions, including solutions for the Automotive, Medical and Industrial markets. Among its products are products that integrate processing of analog and digital signals. AMI Semiconductor operates globally with headquarters in Pocatello, Idaho, European corporate offices in Oudenaarde, Belgium, and a network of sales and design centers located in the key markets of North America, Europe and the Asia Pacific region.

AMI Semiconductor is a wholly owned subsidiary of AMIS Holdings, Inc., which is quoted on the Nasdaq index under the trading symbol AMIS.

[edit] History

  • 1966 Founded in Santa Clara, CA Pioneered MOS ASICs
  • 1970 Manufacturing moved to Pocatello, ID
  • 1981 Formed AMIS-Philippines
  • 1982 Purchased by Gould/Japan Energy
  • 1988 Moved Worldwide Headquarters to Pocatello, ID
  • 1993 Formed AMIS GmbH
  • 1997 Opened 8" manufacturing facility
  • 2000 Francisco Partners & Citigroup Venture Capital acquire majority interest
  • 2001 Christine King takes helm as President & CEO*2002 AMIS Acquired Alcatel Microelectronics Mixed-Signal Business
  • 2002 AMIS Acquired Microsemi Micro Power Products
  • 2003 NASDAQ, IPO, 30 Million Shares at $20 Share
  • 2004 AMIS Acquired Dspfactory Ltd

[edit] Products

AMIS designs, develops and manufactures a broad range of integrated mixed-signal products. These products are used in a wide variety of applications for a number of end-uses including automotive, medical, industrial, communications, computing, defense, and consumer products.

[edit] Transceivers

AMI Semiconductor's interface transceiver offering includes 1 or 2 wire bus communication CAN, LIN, VAN, and HART. Our products are capable of handling the high-voltage requirements typical in automotive applications, including surge levels and transients.

We also offer sub-GHz low data rate wireless transceivers that are IEEE compliant and low cost FSK or ASK transceivers.

[edit] Motor Controllers

AMIS leverages its unique high-voltage technologies into numerous ASICs for both automotive and industrial applications.

Our motor controller product families target applications that are interfaced by a communication bus (e.g. LIN) and are well-suited to the harsh and demanding environments surrounding automotive and industrial applications.

[edit] DSP Systems

Simplify your design with high-performance, programmable, mixed-signal DSP-based audio systems based on patented second-generation SignaKlara™ technology.

[edit] Image Sensors

AMI Semiconductor's CMOS Image Sensor simplifies the design and implementation of high quality digital camera applications in automotive, industrial, security, and surveillance systems.

[edit] Timers and Clocks

AMI Semiconductor's timer and precision clock products serve a wide variety of high-volume consumer and industrial markets.
