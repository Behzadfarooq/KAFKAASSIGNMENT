[edit] Magsaysay Maritime Corporation

Magsaysay is a leading human resource organization in the Asia-Pacific region. Established over thirty-five years ago in the Philippines, a country abundant in skilled human resources, Magsaysay has been filling the high quality skilled personnel needs of world-class companies in the Maritime, Cruise, Hotel and Healthcare industries among others. Magsaysay's core principle of investing in people and caring for them and their families has been the foundation for its success and growth. The company is proud of its excellent customer base of world class companies, who share its long-term view of developing and caring for their people.

History Founded in 1948, Magsaysay has achieved many milestones through the hard work, creativity and loyalty of its people both aboard and ashore. Magsaysay's Ship management Division, Manning Operations, and Training Center was organized in the 1970's to provide technical, human resource and training services to its fleet and to other ship owners. In 1988, Magsaysay, together with its joint venture partner A.L. Marden, acquired the National Marine Corporation (NMC) from the Philippine government. This allowed Magsaysay's involvement in Philippine interisland and near-sea trades for both dry and liquid cargoes. Magsaysay ventured into domestic tourism in the 1990's with the establishment of Sun Cruises, Inc, which ferried tourists to the historic Corregidor Island, handles guided tours, and operates the Corregidor Inn and Resort. Soon after, Magsaysay Travel and Tours Co., Inc. was organized, promoting adventure tour packages to the different exciting scenic locations in various countries. Together with Mitsui O.S.K Lines, the Magsaysay Institute of Shipping was inaugurated to train seafarers of the partners. Today, strengthened by its people who share a passion for quality, Magsaysay looks to the future with enthusiasm as it continues to fulfill its role as a dynamic prime mover in the development of the Philippine shipping industry. Products and Services Magsaysay provides [human resource] and other related services for the following industries: Shipping, Cruise, Hotels, Entertainment, Casino and Gaming, Computer Graphics, Healthcare, Information Technology, Shipbuilding and Ship Repair, Accounting, Call Centers, Medical Transcription, Port Operations, Logistics, and Tourism.
