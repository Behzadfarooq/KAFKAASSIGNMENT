Hello and welcome to my user page!

I'm a relatively new user and still need lots of help formating things properly.


I am relatively knowledgeable about alternative education, particularly unschooling, and I am well acquainted with the work of radical education writers such as John Caldwell Holt. I'm also a psychology student, so I know a bit about that as well. I am marginally knowledgeable about a variety of topics, from giftedness (I am a child of a former president of the Nebraska Association for the Gifted) to anthropology.
