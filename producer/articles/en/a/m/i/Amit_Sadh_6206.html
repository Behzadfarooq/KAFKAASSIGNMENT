Amit Sadh is an Indian television actor. He quit his education after standard 12 to pursue his acting career; his acknowledged "breakthrough" appearance was in the Neena Gupta production Kyun Hota Hai Pyaar; he went on to star in Kohinoor[1] and appears as a contestant on reality show Big Boss.[2] As of 2005, he was engaged to fellow actress Neeru Bajwa.[3]

As of February 6th, 2007 he is a part of Shobna Desai's Durgesh Nandinii on Sony Entertainment Television. He enters the show as Kshitij, the adopted son of Dharamdas Shrivasata who is a lawyer by profession.
