Amir Vaziri (Persian: امیر وزیری ‎ , born February 5, 1979 in Iran) is an Iranian football player. He currently plays for the IPL club Saipa as a striker.

[edit] Club career

Having played for Zob Ahan FC in the Iranian Premier League for three years, in 2004 he moved to Tehrani club Paykan FC, where he stayed only for season 2004/05. In 2005, he moved to another car industry sponsored club, Saipa FC. In season 2005/06 he made 29 appearances for his club, scoring 7 goals.
