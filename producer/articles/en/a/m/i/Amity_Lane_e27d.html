Amity Lane is a four-piece music group from Montgomery, Alabama. The band features two former members from the gold selling music group Trust Company, in vocalist Kevin Palmer and bassist Josh Moates.

[edit] History

Amity Lane was formed in 2005 by Kevin Palmer and Josh Moates, both members of the now dissolved gold selling music group Trust Company.

Initially a three-piece group featuring Kevin Palmer's wife, Layla Palmer, the band signed on Jason Rash to play noiseboards, before inking a deal with Corporate Punishment Records on May 15, 2006.[1]

The band's debut album The Sound of Regret was released on on October 31, 2006.

[edit] Members

  • Kevin Palmer – Lead Vocals, Guitar
  • Layla Palmer – Backup Vocals, Keyboard
  • Josh Moates – Bass
  • Jason Rash – Noiseboards, Samples, Sounds
