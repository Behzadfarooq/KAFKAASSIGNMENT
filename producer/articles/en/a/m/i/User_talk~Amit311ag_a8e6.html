About the College

Baba Saheb Dr. Bhim Rao Ambedker College of Agriculture Engineering & Technology, Etawah was established during the year 1994-95. The main motto of the college is to make provision for education of mainly rural people of Utter Pradesh and provide good facilities for research and extension in the field of Agricultural Engineering and technology. The college from its very inception had adopted a very progressive policy and extends all facilities to the students for their better education and co curricular activities.

Location

Etawah is well connected with all important cities of U.P. and other parts of the country by road and railways. Most of the trains on Delhi-Howarh main route stop at Etawah Railway station. The campus is divided into two parts by National Highways -2 (Shershah Suri Marg), on one side of it lies the college building while on the other side are girl’s hostels, guest house and residential campus for faculty and staff. The college is located near B.R. Ambedkar Govt. District Hospital and is approximately 2 km from Etawah Railway station and 3 km from Bus station.

Admission Policy

Students are admitted to the under graduate programme through UPSEAT (Uttar Pradesh State Engineering Admission Test) conducted by UPSEAT Cell at Institute of Engineering and Technology, Lucknow, Uttar Pradesh. Seats are allotted according to the merit of students in UPSEAT.

Eligibility

Candidates who have passed intermediate of U.P. Board (10+2) or equivalent with Physics, Chemistry and Mathematics (PCM) or Agriculture (only for B.Tech in Agricultural Engineering). Candidates those are appearing in the qualifying examination or have appeared and the result is awaited, are also eligible to appear in UPSEAT. Top Facilities

Hostel facility: The college campus having two hostels viz. Boy’s and Girl’s Hostel with a capacity of 100 & 40 rooms, respectively. Mess facility is available in each hostel and managed by the students. However respective wardens and chief warden strictly maintain the hostel disciplines. All the students must adhere and abide by the hostel rules. Medical facility: A U.P. Govt. hospital is available near Boy’s hostel in close vicinity of the college campus.

Sports facility: Games and sports facilities are available at the college campus. Time to time inter institute competitions are also organized.

Library facility: Library is the heart of teaching and research. The library of the college covers an area of 600 sq.m.. It remains open from 8.00 AM. to 5.00 PM in all working days.

Training and Placement: There is a training and placement coordinator Cell which arranges the summer field training of one month mandatory for all the students at the end of the second and third year degree programme. The various fields of training are Farm Machinery and Power Engineering, Soil and Water Conservation Engineering, Food Process Engineering and Irrigation and Drainage Engineering at government training centers and reputed private firms.

Financial Assistance: The financial assistance/scholarship is being provided to the meritorious students from the following sources: i. Merit Scholarship: This scholarship is given to the meritorious student of the college. ii. SC/ST/OBC Scholarship: This scholarship is given to the student by Samaj Kalayn Vibhag, Government of U.P. iii. National Scholarship: National scholarship is awarded by the Central Government through Uttar Pradesh Shikha Nideshalay, Lucknow, on the basis of past performance of the student. iv. Other Scholarship: Several other scholarships are offered by the private trusts/agencies announced for time to time.
