Amighetti's Bakery

Amighetti's Bakery is an Italian American bakery and cafe located in the Hill, the large Italian neighborhood of St. Louis, Missouri. They are most known for their Amighetti's Special Sandwich. The Special is made with ham, roast beef, Genoa salami, brick cheese, lettuce, tomato, pickles, onions, pepperoncini and secret recipe Amighetti's dressing on freshly baked Italian bread. They also make and serve fresh pasta.

Amighetti's Bakery was founded in 1921 by Louis Amighetti, Jr. an Italian immigrant.

The Schnucks grocery store chain makes a sandwich called the "King of the Hill" that tries to imitate the Special.
