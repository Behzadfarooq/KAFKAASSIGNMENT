VandalProof version 1.3.0 will be the first interwiki release; however, your help is needed for this to be possible. Below are the current interwiki VandalProof projects under development. If you are familiar with any of the below wikis, or especially if you are an administrator on one of them, and are willing to contribute to the project, please let me know! For each individual interwiki project, I am going to need a handful of users near fluent in both languages, with a decent degree of technical knowledge (though I'll be doing the coding myself, so not to worry), and with a good understanding of how the wiki works. Even if you don't have much time available to contribute, any help you can offer will be much appreciated, including just pointing me in the right direction of help.
