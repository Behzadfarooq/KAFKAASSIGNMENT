Amir El-Falaki (born August 12, 1973 Copenhagen) is a Danish vocalist. He is a member of the band Toy-Box.

Amir is of Moroccon descent and speaks Danish, English, Arabic, and French.[1].

Amir wanted to be a doctor, but became a dance teacher instead. He met Anila Mirza at a New Year's Eve party. The two decided to form Toy-Box. As of 2006, he is planning a solo music career, or a dance teacher career.

Amir was a dancer in Julie Lund's song "Merhaba" at the Danish national Eurovision semi final, Melodi Grand Prix 2007. Their song, however, didn't qualify for the final round.
