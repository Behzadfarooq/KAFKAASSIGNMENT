[edit] Ami Ace Bio

Name: Ami Ace

Real Name: Amsal Ace Susterac

Age: 11

Height: 4 foot 12

Weight: 87 pounds

From: Chicago Ill.

Finishing Move: Ace of Fate/Ace of Diamonds

Career Highlights: World Heavyweight Champion; WWK Championship (3 times); US X-Division Championship (4 times); WWX X-Division champion; World Tag Team Champion (6 times); Intercontinental Championship (3 times); WWK Tag Team Champion (2 times)

WWK Debut: 2002

Current Events: Won the WWK Championship at Bloody Death

Position: WWK Inferno GM/WWK Champ

Favorite Wrestler: John Cena, Matt Hardy, Jeff Hardy

Trained By: Triple S

Associates: Hanna

Favorite Quote: "I am the king of kings!"

Nickname: The Executioner, The main event, the riegn killer


[edit] Career Highlights

February. 12, 2006 - Ami Ace amazingly won the WWK championship on Bloody Death in a triple threat match. He lost it on the same day but got a rematch to be new WWK champ.
