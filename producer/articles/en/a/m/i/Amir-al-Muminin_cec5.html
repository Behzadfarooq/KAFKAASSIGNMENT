Prince of the Faithful (Arabic: Amir-al-Mu'minin أمير المؤمنين). The title was used by caliphs starting with Umar bin-l-Kahttab. Shi'a believe that only Ali is the Amir and no body else should be called with that title. Shi'a name their children Amir to mean Ali.
