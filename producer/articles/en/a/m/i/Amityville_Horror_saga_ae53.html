Amityville Horror is a series of horror films based on and around Amityville and a haunted house.

[edit] The Films

  • The Amityville Horror (1979 original)
  • Amityville II: The Possession (1982)
  • Amityville 3-D (1983, released in 3-D)
  • Amityville 4: The Evil Escapes (1989 TV film)
  • The Amityville Curse (1990 direct-to-video release)
  • Amityville 1992: It's About Time! (1992 direct-to-video release)
  • Amityville: A New Generation (1993 direct-to-video release)
  • Amityville Dollhouse: Evil Never Dies (1996 direct-to-video release)
  • The Amityville Horror (2005 remake)
