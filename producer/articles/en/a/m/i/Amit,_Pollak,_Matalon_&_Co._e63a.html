Amit, Pollak, Matalon & Co. (a.k.a. "APM"), founded in 1956, is one of the largest law firms in Israel.[citation needed] Its offices are in Tel Aviv.
In January 2004, APM merged with the law offices of Ben-Naftali, Erez & Co., to create Amit, Pollak, Matalon & Ben-Naftali, Erez & Co.. But, a year later, the merged firm announced that the merger was unsuccessful, and APM became an independent firm again.
