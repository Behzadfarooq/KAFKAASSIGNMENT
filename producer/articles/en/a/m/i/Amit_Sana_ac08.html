Amit Sana is an Indian singer who appeared in Indian Idol, a show very similar to Pop Idol and American Idol, in 2005 and finished second.

He sung his first Bollywood song for the movie Kalyug. The song's name is Meri Dastaan, music for which is directed by Anu Malik. He is coming up with a new project Sun Zara in which there are four songs, three solo, and a duet with Shreya Ghoshal. His debut album is Chal Diye, first of a three album contract that he signed. He made a special appearance in season 2 of Indian Idol along with Abhijeet Sawant, who was the winner of Indian Idol, where he and Abhijeet along with Aditi Paul performed a song together. He has done a concert in Bangladesh along with Rahul Saxena.
