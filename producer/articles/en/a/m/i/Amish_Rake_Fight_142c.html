Amish Rake Fight

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The logo for Amish Rake Fight

Amish Rake Fight is an electronic solo project from Los Angeles, California by former Machines of Loving Grace (The Machines) keyboardist Mike Fisher. Amish Rake Fight is currently signed to the Chicago based label Positron! Records.
