[edit] My History on Wikipedia

I first discovered Wikipedia way back in 2004, where I used it to get information. I did not understand what the "edit" meant, I was confused.

In late 2005, I started to browse Wikipedia a bit more. I then made my first edits in Late November/Early December, before finally becoming a member on December 2, 2005. I mainly worked on the SpongeBob articles at first, and then went on to work with the "years" articles. (Mainly future) About April 2006, I started to work on Full House articles and became even busier. I started edting Lego articles in May/June 2006.

I joined the SpongeBob SquarePants Wikiproject on June 6th. I started to contribute more to the articles. I created the Lego Wikiproject in late June 2006. I started to work more on the Lego articles. July 2006 is my biggest edting month yet. I went back to working on the "years" articles, and began to help make them easier to browse through. At that point, I have over 8,000 edits; I have been hugly part of WikiProjects Lego and SpongeBob. I am getting really busy, and now I have decided to slow down a bit. I stopped looking over the Full House articles (but will return in the future. Same thing with the years in film. As of summer 2006, I am focused on improving the SpongeBob and Lego WikiProjects, and improving the "years" articles.

On July 17, 2006 i started to use AutoWikiBrowser to make large tasks easier to do. I have used AWB a lot and made 1,254 edits with it.

In early August, I started working more on the 7th Heaven articles, and also slowing down on the Full House articles. I then went to a particular article (SpongeBob article, Bikini Bottom) and attempted to make it better I requested a peer review and got some feed back. Throughout August 2006, I have worked on SpongeBob characters, Bikini Bottom, 7th heaven characters, and some of the years and days articles. As of September 2006, I have been editing on SpogneBob articles and 7th Heaven articles.

In fall 2006, I contributed more heavily to the SpongeBob and 7th Heaven articles. I then went on to the years in topic articles and contributed there.

As of December 2006, I mainly work on teh SpongeBoB articles.
