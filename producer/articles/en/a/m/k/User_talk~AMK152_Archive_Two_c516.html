License tagging for Image:KKMenuMMABBV.JPG

Thanks for uploading Image:KKMenuMMABBV.JPG. Wikipedia gets thousands of images uploaded every day, and in order to verify that the images can be legally used on Wikipedia, the source and copyright status must be indicated. Images need to have an image tag applied to the image description page indicating the copyright status of the image. This uniform and easy-to-understand method of indicating the license status allows potential re-users of the images to know what they are allowed to do with the images.

WikiProject SpongeBob SquarePants Newsletter
September 2006 Volume 1, Issue 1
Introduction
The Wikipedia:Wikiproject SpongeBob SquarePants newsletter is a monthly newsletter posted on the first of the month. AMK152 and ForestH2 write and publish it. If you have any questions or comments please post it on there talk pages.
Concerns and questions
Please leave a message with either ForestH2 or AMK152 if you have questions concerning the project. You may also post to the WikiProject's talkpage and someone will answer.
Featured articles
We are trying to make several articles good or featured articles. We would like your help reaching featured article status. We are currently working on SpongeBob SquarePants, Krusty Krab, and Bikini Bottom. Please help us improve these articles, based on the peer reviews and your knowledge of Wikipedia featured articles.
Special thanks to AMK152 for delievering this newsletter.
