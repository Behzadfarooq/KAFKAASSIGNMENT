Amlwch Town F.C. is a football team, playing in Amlwch on the island of Anglesey in the Gwynedd League (part of the Welsh football league system).

Their highest standard of football ever was two seasons spent in the Cymru Alliance from 2002-04, but were relegated at the end of their second season. They then decided to drop straight past the next level Welsh Alliance to return to the more local Anglesey League, which they won at the second attempt.

In the 1998/99 season Welsh international and former Manchester United and Everton player, Mickey Thomas made several appearances for the club.
