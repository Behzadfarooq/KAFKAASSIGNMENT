[edit] Wikipedia in the NEWS

Apparently in 2006 people with no user-names will not be able to use Wikipedia, I find this a shame really. But then if there is an increased use of Vandalism on Wikipedia, then I hope that people with User Names will see more benefit in Wikipedia than before. Also, it will be easier to find the vandals and people who are using this project to attack people in a slanderous way.

Actually, anons will still be able to edit, they will just not be able to create new pages. We are also looking into heightened protection for a handful of the most frequently vandalised pages. BD2412 T 22:40, 12 December 2005 (UTC)
