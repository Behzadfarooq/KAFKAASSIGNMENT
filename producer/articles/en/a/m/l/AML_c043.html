AML is a three-letter abbreviation with multiple meanings, as described below:

  • Acute myeloid leukemia, a form of cancer
  • Acute monocytic leukemia (AMoL, AML-M5)
  • The Panhard AML, an armored car from France
  • ACPI Machine Language, the language used by the ACPI specification for power management
  • Adaptive Modeling Language, the language developed by TechnoSoft, Inc.
  • Adjustable mortgage loan, another name for an adjustable rate mortgage
  • Algebraic modeling language, programming languages for describing and solving high complexity problems
  • Anti Money Laundering, an acronym used in the financial and legal world.
  • Acton Main Line railway station, a rail station in the United Kingdom
  • ARC Macro Language, a high-level algorithmic language for ArcInfo
  • Aston Martin, English automaker, Aston Martin Lagonda Ltd.
  • Added Main Line Unit, provided additional "pairs" when insufficient cable facilities exist to provide every telephone line with its own pair.
  • Microsoft Assistance Markup Language
  • Anne-Marie Losique, a Quebec television producer, television host and singer (AML is a stage name)
