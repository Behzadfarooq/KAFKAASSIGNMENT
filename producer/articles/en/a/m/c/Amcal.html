Amcal (originally an acronym for Allied Master Chemists of Australia Limited) is an Australasian chain of pharmacies.

[edit] Australia

In Australia, Amcal is operated by Sigma Pharmaceuticals Limited.

[edit] New Zealand

The Amcal New Zealand logo with the "Ask Amcal First" slogan.

In New Zealand, Amcal is a pharmacy franchise. Amcal's marketing is managed by the owner of Amcal New Zealand, Pharmacybrands, a division of Australian Pharmaceutical Industries.
