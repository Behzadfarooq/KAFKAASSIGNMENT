AMCO Constructions Co, Ltd. (Hangul: 엠코건설) is a Korean construction company founded and established in 2002. headquartered in Yangjae-dong Seocho-gu Seoul, South Korea. and as company of apartment brand is AMCO Town, at from apartment construction of Korea. the company CEO is Kim Chang Hee. It is licenced by Hyundai Motor Company and bussiness of construction products.
