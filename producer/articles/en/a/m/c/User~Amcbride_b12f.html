I'm Allen McBride, from Oak Ridge, Tennessee. I've been active on Wikipedia since December 2005, and I often focus on articles about folk music, biology, and politics. I take male pronouns... I know most English speakers can guess that from my name, but it helps me when other editors put their preferred pronouns on their user pages, so I thought I would too.
