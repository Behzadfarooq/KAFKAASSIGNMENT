[edit] Purpose

{{AMCOTW candidate}} â€”

To be placed at the top of a nominated page for AMCOTW.

[edit] Usage

{{AMCOTW candidate
 |Thispage=Nominations
}}

The parameter Thispage is optional but should link to the section name it is listed under on the AMCTOW page.

[edit] Example

Code Result 
{{AMCOTW candidate
 |Thispage=Current successful nomination}}
}}
This article is nominated for Anime and Manga Collaboration of the Week.
Please review this page's entry and voice your support.
