Hi there.

Born Brooklyn, grew up on Long Island, and having studied, worked and lived in Baltimore, New York, Syracuse and Washington, D.C., I am an attorney now living in Chicago, Illinois. I have wide interests, so there is little rhyme or reason to my contributions (though most of my contributions seem to be politics, history and soccer).

This user is a Libertarian.

Following the midterm 2006 elections, I have become more interested in the history of "fusionism" -- the idea, somewhat unique in the US (and to lesser extent Canadian and British) conservative movements, that libertarians (what the rest of the world calls "economic liberals") and traditional conservatives unite politically. To that end I started an article on fusionism, and plan to learn more about the advocates of it.
