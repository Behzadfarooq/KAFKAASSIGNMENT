Rueso (Thai: รือเสาะ) is a district (Amphoe) of Narathiwat Province, southern Thailand.

[edit] Geography

Neighboring districts are (from the northeast clockwise) Bacho, Yi-ngo, Ra-ngae and Si Sakhon of Narathiwat Province, and Bannang Sata and Raman of Yala Province.

[edit] History

Originally the area of the district was tambon Tammangon of Tonyongmon district, the present-day Rangae district. In 1917 it was upgraded to Tammangon minor district (King Amphoe). Later it was renamed to Rueso. 1941 the minor district was upgraded to a full district.

[edit] Administration

The district is subdivided into 9 communes (tambon), which are further subdivided into 71 villages (muban). The township (thesaban tambon) Rueso covers parts of tambon Rueso and Rueso Ok. There are further 9 Tambon administrative organizations.
