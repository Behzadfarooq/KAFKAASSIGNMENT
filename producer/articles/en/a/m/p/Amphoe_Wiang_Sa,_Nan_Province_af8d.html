Wiang Sa (Thai: เวียงสา) is a district (Amphoe) in the central part of Nan Province, northern Thailand.

[edit] Geography

Neighboring are from the south clockwise Na Muen of Nan Province, Rong Kwang, Song of Phrae Province, Ban Luang, Mueang Nan, Phu Phiang and Mae Charim of Nan Province again. And to the east it borders Xaignabouli Province of Laos.

[edit] Administration

The district is subdivided into 17 communes (tambon), which are further subdivided into 126 villages (muban). Klang Wiang is a township (thesaban tambon) and covers parts of tambon Klang Wiang. There are further 15 Tambon administrative organizations (TAO).
