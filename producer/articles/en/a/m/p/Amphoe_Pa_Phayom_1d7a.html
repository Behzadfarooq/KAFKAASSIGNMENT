Pa Phayom (Thai: ป่าพะยอม) is the northernmost district (Amphoe) of Phatthalung Province, southern Thailand.

[edit] Geography

Neighboring districts are (from the southeast clockwise) Khuan Khanun, Si Banphot of Phattalung Province, Huai Yot of Trang Province and Cha-uat of Nakhon Si Thammarat Province.

[edit] History

The minor district (King Amphoe) was established on January 19 1990, when it was split off from Khuan Khanun district. It was upgraded to a full district on September 7 1995.

[edit] Administration

The district is subdivided into 4 communes (tambon), which are further subdivided into 39 villages (muban). There are no municipal (thesaban), and 4 Tambon administrative organizations (TAO).
