I am an author and activist in Buffalo, New York. AMProSoft is the name of the software/shareware business I started in August of 1991. AMProSoft has branched out to become a website and a book publishing enterprise as well.

Recent books I have written include:

  • Relations: McEmpire
  • Relations: SMASH YOUR TV!
  • Invisible Fire: The Poetry of Anna Walsh and Alex Mead
  • The Autobiography of Alex Mead: How To Care About Humans

I am also a performer (see My Rap Name is Alex), and a big time internet nerd (see green.earth.al).

I was born in 1970 and am a lifelong resident of Buffalo, New York.
