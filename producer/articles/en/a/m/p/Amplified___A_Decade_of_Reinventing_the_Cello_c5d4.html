Amplified, released in 2006, is a greatest-hits-like album from cello rock group Apocalyptica containing two new songs: Repressed and Slayer's Angel of Death.

[edit] Track listing

CD 1: Instrumentals

  1. Enter Sandman (from Plays Metallica by Four Cellos) - 3:41
  2. Harmageddon (from Inquisition Symphony) - 4:56
  3. Nothing Else Matters (from Inquisition Symphony) - 4:48
  4. Refuse/Resist (from Inquisition Symphony) - 3:13
  5. Somewhere Around Nothing (from Reflections) - 4:10
  6. Betrayal (from Apocalyptica) - 5:12
  7. Farewell (from Apocalyptica) - 5:32
  8. Master of Puppets (from Plays Metallica by Four Cellos) - 6:03
  9. Hall Of the Mountain King (from Cult) - 3:29
  10. One (from Inquisition Symphony) - 5:45
  11. Heat (from Reflections) - 3:22
  12. Cohkka (from Reflections) - 4:29
  13. Kaamos (from Cult) - 4:44
  14. Deathzone (from Apocalyptica) - 4:36
  15. Angel of Death (Slayer cover) - 3:52

[edit] Limited edition

The album has two versions: regular and limited. Limited edition included the "Heat"-video and bonustrack "Wie Weit". The quickest orders of this edition receive an Apocalyptica key trailer.
