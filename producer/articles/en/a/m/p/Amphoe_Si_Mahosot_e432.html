Si Mahosot (Thai: ศรีมโหสถ) is a district (Amphoe) of Prachinburi Province, eastern Thailand.

[edit] Geography

Neighboring districts are (from the west clockwise) Ban Sang, Mueang Prachinburi and Si Maha Phot of Prachinburi Province and Phanom Sarakham of Chachoengsao Province.

[edit] History

The minor district (King Amphoe) Khok Pip was established on February 27 1970, when it was split off from Si Maha Phot district. It was upgraded to a full district on April 13 1977, and renamed to Si Mahosot on June 3 1993.

[edit] Administration

The district is subdivided into 4 communes (tambon), which are further subdivided into 24 villages (muban). Khok Pip is a township (thesaban tambon) which covers parts of tambon Khok Pip. There are further 3 Tambon administrative organizations (TAO).
