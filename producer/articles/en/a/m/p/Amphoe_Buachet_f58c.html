Buachet (Thai: บัวเชด) is a district (Amphoe) in the southeastern part of Surin Province, northeastern Thailand.

[edit] History

Tambon Buachet was upgraded to be a minor district (King Amphoe) on August 21st, 1978. At first a temporary district office was established at the base of the Patrol Police group of the area. It became a full district on July 27th, 1984.

[edit] Geography

Neighboring districts are (from the west clockwise) Sangkha of Surin Province, Khukhan, Phu Sing of Sisaket Province and Oddar Meancheay of Cambodia.

[edit] Administration

The district is subdivided into 6 communes (tambon), which are further subdivided into 67 villages (muban). Buachet itself has township (thesaban tambon) status and covers parts of the tambon Buachet. There are further 6 Tambon administrative organizations (TAO).
