Khok Si Suphan (Thai: โคกศรีสุพรรณ) is a district (Amphoe) of Sakon Nakhon Province, northeast Thailand.

[edit] History

The minor district (King Amphoe) was split off from Mueang Sakon Nakhon district in 1982. It was upgraded to a full district on May 9 1992.

[edit] Geography

Neighboring districts are (from the south clockwise) Tao Ngoi, Mueang Sakon Nakhon and Phon Na Kaeo of Sakon Nakhon Province, Wang Yang and Na Kae of Nakhon Phanom Province.

[edit] Administration

The district is subdivided into 4 communes (tambon), which are further subdivided into 52 villages (muban). There are no municipal (thesaban) areas, and further 4 Tambon administrative organizations.
