Ampang-Kuala Lumpur Elevated Highway (AKLEH) E 12, is the first elevated highway in Malaysia. It connects Ampang and Kuala Lumpur. This highway was built to reduce traffic jams and make access to the city more convenient. Motorcycles and other vehicles with 2 wheels or less are not allowed on this highway due to safety reasons.

[edit] History

Construction began in 1999 at the banks of Klang River and was completed in 2001. The highway was opened to traffic on 17 May 2001.

[edit] Features

  • Tunnel to Suria KLCC parking.
  • Smooth access to Ampang and Kuala Lumpur.
  • Spectacular sceneries of Kuala Lumpur skyline.
