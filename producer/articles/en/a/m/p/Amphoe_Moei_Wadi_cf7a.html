Moei Wadi (Thai: เมยวดี) is a district (Amphoe) in the northern part of Roi Et Province, northeastern Thailand.

[edit] Geography

Neighboring districts are (from the east clockwise) Nong Phok and Phon Thong of Roi Et Province, Kuchinarai of Kalasin Province and Nong Sung of Mukdahan Province.

[edit] History

The minor district (King Amphoe) Moei Wadi was established on April 1 1978. It was upgraded to a full district on November 1993.

[edit] Administration

The district is subdivided into 4 communes (tambon), which are further subdivided into 43 villages (muban). There are no municipal (thesaban) areas, and 4 Tambon administrative organizations (TAO).
