Ampm

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

For the article on time, see 12-hour clock
The correct title of this article is ampm. The initial letter is shown capitalized due to technical restrictions.

ampm is a convenience store chain with branches located in Arizona, California, Nevada, Oregon, Washington, and Japan. It is owned by BP and, in the US, is usually attached to an ARCO gas station. Franchised units of ampm are also found in parts of Mexico, usually co-located with a Pemex gas station, and in Brazil, usually attached with an Ipiranga station. In Argentina they can be found in Repsol-YPF/ACA stations.
