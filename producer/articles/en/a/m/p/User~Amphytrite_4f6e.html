[edit] Who I am

I'm known in most circles now as Muzz (however, unlike Muzzy, I don't have green hair). I'm a member of the iGeneration and I am a student at a small liberal arts school, where I study Latin. I care for two horses named Jehossee and Dutch and they are my life. If I'm not at the barn and not doing homework, I'm usually found either editing Wikipedia, listening to music that no one has ever heard of, or sleeping. I like sleeping a lot. In my "free" time, I spend a lot of time in Washington, DC and taking pictures with my old school SLR.
Two completely random facts about me: I don't know how to ride a bike and I've been playing mallet percussion for seven years.

[edit] My "job" here

Oh Gettysburg.

I usually poke around or hunt down and fix spelling, punctuational and grammatical errors. I'm a grammar nazi. I's like to help develop the Latin Wikipedia at some point, because I think not enough people appreciate Latin. For the most part, though, I bounce back and forth from one article to another, searching for minor errors. I also categorise a lot. I like to read a bunch of what I edit for content as well.
I'm also a member of Wikiproject: Classical Greece and Rome, as well as an active editor in the Article Creation and Improvement Drive, both of which are great projects.
