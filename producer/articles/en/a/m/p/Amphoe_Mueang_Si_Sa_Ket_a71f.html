Mueang Sisaket (Thai: เมืองศรีสะเกษ) is the capital district (Amphoe Mueang) of Sisaket Province, northeastern Thailand.

[edit] Geography

Neighboring districts are (from the north clockwise) Yang Chum Noi, Kanthararom, Nam Kliang, Phayu, Wang Hin, Uthumphon Phisai and Rasi Salai.

[edit] Administration

The district is subdivided into 18 communes (tambon), which are further subdivided into 165 villages (muban). The town (thesaban mueang) Sisaket covers the tambon Mueang Nuea and Mueang Tai and parts of tambon Nong Khrok, Phon Kha, Ya Plong and Pho. There are further 16 Tambon administrative organizations (TAO).
