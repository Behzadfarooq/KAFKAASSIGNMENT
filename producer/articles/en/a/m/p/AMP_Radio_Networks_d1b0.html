AMP Radio Networks, a wholly owned subsidiary of Astro All Asia Networks plc, is a Malaysian-based radio services company that manages 8 of Malaysia's leading radio stations.

The company operates the following radio stations: ERA, MYFM, Hitz.fm, Mix FM, Lite & Easy FM, Sinar FM, Xfresh FM and THR.fm (acquired in 2005).
