Ampulla of ductus deferens
Prostate with seminal vesicles and seminal ducts, viewed from in front and above.
Vesiculæ seminales and ampullæ of ductus deferentes, seen from the front.
Latin ampulla ductus deferentis
Gray's subject #259 1246
Dorlands/Elsevier a_32/12127859

At the fundus of the bladder the ductus deferens becomes enlarged and tortuous, and this portion is termed the ampulla.
