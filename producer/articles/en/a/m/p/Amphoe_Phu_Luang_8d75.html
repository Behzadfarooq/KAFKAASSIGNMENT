Phu Luang (Thai: ภูหลวง) is a district (Amphoe) of Loei Province, northeastern Thailand.

[edit] Geography

Neighboring districts are (from the south clockwise) Nam Nao of Phetchabun Province, Phu Ruea, Wang Saphung, Nong Hin and Phu Kradueng of Loei Province.

[edit] History

The minor district (King Amphoe) was established on November 28 1980. It was upgraded to a full district on May 9 1992.

[edit] Administration

The district is subdivided into 5 communes (tambon), which are further subdivided into 43 villages (muban). There are no municipal (thesaban) areas, and 5 Tambon administrative organizations (TAO).
