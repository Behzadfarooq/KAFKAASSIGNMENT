Si Banphot (Thai: ศรีบรรพต) is a district (Amphoe) of Phatthalung Province, southern Thailand.

[edit] Geography

Neighboring districts are (from the north clockwise) Pa Phayom, Khuan Khanun and Srinagarindra of Phatthalung Province, Na Yong, Mueang Trang and Huai Yot of Trang Province.

[edit] History

The minor district was established on January 12 1978, when it was split off from Khuan Khanun district. It was upgraded to a full district on November 4 1993.

[edit] Administration

The district is subdivided into 3 communes (tambon), which are further subdivided into 30 villages (muban). There are no municipal (thesaban) areas, and 3 Tambon administrative organizations (TAO).
