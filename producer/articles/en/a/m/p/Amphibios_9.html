Amphibios 9

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Amphibios 9 and a moon

Amphibios 9 is a fictional planet depicted in Futurama, the American animated sitcom created by Matt Groening and David X. Cohen .

This swampy planet is the homeworld of a green-skinned extraterrestrial race of amphibian-like beings. It has at least one moon.

Amphibios 9 is seen in "Kif Gets Knocked Up A Notch", the first episode in season four. In this episide, Lieutenant Kif Kroker comes here in order to give birth to his tadpole-like young.

Other lifeforms shown on Amphibios 9 include:

  • a variety of colorful creatures that look like snakes
  • a flying arthropod that resembles a giant dragonfly from Earth's Carboniferous period but possessing a beak like that of a bird of prey
  • an elephant-sized creature called a "poisonous froad" that resembles a blue poison arrow frog
