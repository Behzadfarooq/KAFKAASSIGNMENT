Sapphaya (Thai: สรรพยา) is the easternmost district (Amphoe) of Chainat Province, central Thailand.

[edit] Geography

Neighboring districts are (from the southwest clockwise) Sankhaburi, Mueang Chainat of Chainat Province, Takhli of Nakhon Sawan Province and In Buri of Sing Buri Province.

[edit] Administration

The district is subdivided into 7 communes (tambon), which are further subdivided into 55 villages (muban). There are two townships (thesaban tambon) - Sapphaya covers parts of tambon Sapphaya, and Pho Nangdam parts of tambon Pho Nangdam Tok. There are further 7 Tambon administrative organizations (TAO).
