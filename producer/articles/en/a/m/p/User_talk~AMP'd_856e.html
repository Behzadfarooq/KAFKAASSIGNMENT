[edit] Additional tips

Here are some extra tips to help you get around in Wikipedia:

  • If you want to play around with your new Wiki skills the Sandbox is for you.
  • You can sign your name using three tildes (~). If you use four, you can add a datestamp too.
  • You may want to add yourself to the new user log.
  • If you ever think a page or image should be deleted, please list it at the votes for deletion page. There is also a votes for undeletion page if you want to retrieve something that you think should not have been deleted.
  • If you're still entirely confused, or would like to get a better grasp of your wikipedia skills, and you have an IRC client (or don't mind getting one), check out the Bootcamp. It's not what it sounds like, but it is fun and can help you with your editing skills.

Hi AMP'd, thanks for the message. This Wikipedia can sometimes be a tough business. I've learned more than a few lessons the hard way too. When someone else says something that's less than ideal, or just completely against the rules (no personal attacks; assume good faith; etc), it's very tempting and all too human to respond in kind. The thing is, that brings us down to the same level, and we're then guilty of the same behaviour that we took exception to in the first place. Your minor lapse was not a big issue in the scheme of things. I'm sure you'll find chinks in my armour down the track. Hopefully they'll be as minor as yours was. Cheers.
