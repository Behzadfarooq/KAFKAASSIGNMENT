Si Rattana (Thai: ศรีรัตนะ) is a district (Amphoe) in the central part of Sisaket Province, northeastern Thailand.

[edit] Geography

Neighboring districts are (from the north clockwise) Phayu, Nam Kliang, Benchalak, Kantharalak, Khun Han and Phrai Bueng.

[edit] History

The minor district (King Amphoe) was created on March 5 1981, when it was split off from Kantharalak district. It was upgraded to a full district on May 26 1989.

[edit] Administration

The district is subdivided into 7 communes (tambon), which are further subdivided into 87 villages (muban). Si Rattana is a township (thesaban tambon), which covers parts of tambon Si Kaeo and Saphung. There are further 7 Tambon administrative organizations (TAO).
