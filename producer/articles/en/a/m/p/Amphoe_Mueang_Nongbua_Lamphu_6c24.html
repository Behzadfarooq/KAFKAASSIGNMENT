Mueang Nongbua Lamphu (Thai: เมืองหนองบัวลำภู) is the capital district (Amphoe Mueang) of Nongbua Lamphu Province, northeastern Thailand.

[edit] Geography

Neighboring districts are (from the south clockwise) Non Sang, Si Bun Rueang, Na Klang of Nongbua Lamphu Province, Kut Chap and Nong Wua So of Udon Thani Province.

[edit] Administration

The district is subdivided into 15 communes (tambon), which are further subdivided into 172 villages (muban). The town (thesaban mueang) Nongbua Lamphu covers parts of the tambon Nong Bua, Pho Chai and the whole tambon Lam Phu. There are three townships (thesaban tambon) - Hua Na, Na Mafueang and Na Kham Hai each cover parts of the same-named tambon. There are further 13 Tambon administrative organizations (TAO).
