Anthonia "Amp" Wayne Lee (born October 1, 1971 in Chipley, Florida), is a former American professional football player who was selected by the San Francisco 49ers in the 2nd round of the 1992 NFL Draft. A 5'11", 200 lbs. running back from Florida State University, Lee played in 9 NFL seasons from 1992-2000. His best year as a pro came during the 1995 season as a member of the Minnesota Vikings when he caught 71 receptions. In 1997 he was named team MVP for the St. Louis Rams.
