Yaha (Thai: ยะหา) is a district (Amphoe) in the western part of Yala Province, southern Thailand.

[edit] History

The area of Yaha was separated from Mueang Yala to create the district in 1907.

[edit] Geography

Neighboring districts are (from the west clockwise) Kabang of Yala province, Saba Yoi of Songkhla Province, Mueang Yala, Krong Pinang, Bannang Sata of Yala province again and the state Kedah of Malaysia.

[edit] Administration

The district is subdivided into 8 communes (tambon), which are further subdivided into 48 villages (muban). Yaha itself has township (thesaban tambon) status and covers parts of the tambon Yaha.
