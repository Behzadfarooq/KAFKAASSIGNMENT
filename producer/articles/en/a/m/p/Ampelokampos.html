Ampelokampos

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Ampelokampos or Ampelokampos (Greek: Αμπελοκήποι), other uses, Abelokabos, Ambelokambos, is a village in the municipality of Amaliada in the northern part of the prefecture of Ilia. Distance from other places 6 km N of Amaliada, 6 km S of Palouki, 2 km E of Savalia, 77 km SE of Patras, and NNW of Pyrgos. The neighbouring communities are Savalia to the west, Gastouni and Koroivos to the southeast, Roupaki ot the north, and Amaliada to the southeast.

The village has a school, a church, a square (plateia). The area is agricultural, most of the land is farmland and of mixed farming while parts of the south and east are forested. There is no train station and the closest is in Amaliada because the train runs west of Ampelokipoi.
