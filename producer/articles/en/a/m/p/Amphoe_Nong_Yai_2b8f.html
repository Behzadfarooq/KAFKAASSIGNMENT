Nong Yai (Thai: หนองใหญ่) is a district (Amphoe) in the province Chonburi, Thailand.

[edit] History

The minor district (King Amphoe) Nong Yai was created on July 14 1981. It was upgraded to a full district the following year.

[edit] Geography

Neighboring districts are (from the south clockwise) Wang Chan and Pluak Daeng of Rayong Province, Si Racha, Ban Bueng and Bo Thong of Chonburi Province.

[edit] Administration

The district is subdivided into 5 communes (tambon), which are further subdivided into 24 villages (muban). Nong Yai is a township (thesaban tambon) which covers the whole tambon Nong Yai. There are further 4 Tambon administrative organizations (TAO).
