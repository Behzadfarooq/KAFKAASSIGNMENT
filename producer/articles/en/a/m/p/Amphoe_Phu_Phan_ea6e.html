Phu Phan (Thai: ภูพาน) is a district (Amphoe) of Sakon Nakhon Province, northeast Thailand.

[edit] History

The minor district (King Amphoe) was created on April 30 1994, when it was split off from Kut Bak district, together with tambon Kok Pla Sio which was split off from Mueang Sakon Nakhon district. On October 11 1997 it was upgraded to a full district.

[edit] Geography

Neighboring districts are (from the north clockwise) Kut Bak, Mueang Sakon Nakhon and Tao Ngoi of Sakon Nakhon Province, Na Khu, Huai Phueng, Somdet and Kham Muang of Kalasin Province.

[edit] Administration

The district is subdivided into 4 communes (tambon), which are further subdivided into 61 villages (muban). There are no municipal (thesaban) areas, and further 4 Tambon administrative organizations.
