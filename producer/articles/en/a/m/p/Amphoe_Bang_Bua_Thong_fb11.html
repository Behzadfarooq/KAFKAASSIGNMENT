Bang Bua Thong (Thai: บางบัวทอง) is a district (Amphoe) of Nonthaburi Province, central Thailand.

[edit] Geography

Neighboring districts are (from north clockwise) Lat Lum Kaeo (Pathum Thani Province), Pak Kret, Mueang Nonthaburi, Sai Noi and Bang Yai.

[edit] Administration

The district is subdivided into 8 communes (tambon), which are further subdivided into 82 villages (muban). Bang Bua Thong has town status (thesaban mueang), which covers parts of the tambon Bang Bua Thong, Bang Rak Yai, Phimon Rat and Bang Rak Phatthana. There are further 7 tambon administrative organizations (TAO).
