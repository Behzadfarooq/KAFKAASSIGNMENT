Ampelos

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Dionysos and Ampelos

In Greek mythology, Ampelos (Αμπελος, "vine") was a beautiful satyr youth, eromenos of Dionysus. As foreseen by Dionysus, the youth was killed in an accident with a bull. After he died, Dionysus turned him into a grape vine and created wine from his blood.

Ampelose (singular: Ampelos) were also a variety of hamadryad.
