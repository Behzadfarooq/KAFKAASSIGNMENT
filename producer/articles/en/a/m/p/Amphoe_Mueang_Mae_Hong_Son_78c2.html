Mueang Mae Hong Son (Thai: เมืองแม่ฮ่องสอน) is the capital district (Amphoe Mueang) of Mae Hong Son Province, northern Thailand.

[edit] History

The Interior Ministry upgraded Mueang Mae Hong Son to be a 4th class city under Monthon Phayap on May 10th, 1900. It had 4 districts included, Muai To (which is Mueang Mae Hong Son district now), Mueang Yuam (now Mae Sariang district), Pai and Khun Yuam.

[edit] Geography

Neighboring are (from south clockwise) Khun Yuam of Mae Hong Son Province, Kayah State of Myanmar, Pangmapha, Pai of Mae Hong Son Province again and Mae Chaem of Chiang Mai Province.

The Namtok Mae Surin National Park office is located in the district.

[edit] Administration

The district is subdivided into 7 communes (tambon), which are further subdivided into 68 villages (muban). Mae Hong Son itself is a town (thesaban mueang) which covers the complete tambon Chong Kham.

1. Chong Kham จองคำ
2. Huai Pong ห้วยโป่ง
3. Pha Bong ผาบ่อง
4. Pang Mu ปางหมู
5. Mok Cham Pae หมอกจำแป่
6. Huai Pha ห้วยผา
9. Huai Pu Ling ห้วยปูลิง

Missing numbers are tambon which now form Pang Mapha district.
