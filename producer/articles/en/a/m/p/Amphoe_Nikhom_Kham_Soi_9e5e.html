Nikhom Kham Soi (Thai: นิคมคำสร้อย) is a district (Amphoe) of Mukdahan Province, northeastern Thailand.

[edit] Geography

Neighboring districts are (from the west clockwise) Nong Sung, Mueang Mukdahan and Don Tan of Mukdahan Province, and Loeng Nok Tha of Yasothon Province.

[edit] History

The minor district (King Amphoe) was established in 1975, when it was split off from Mukdahan district. It was upgraded to a full district in 1979. When Mukdahan Province was created in 1982, Nikhom Kham Soi was one of districts forming the new new province.

[edit] Administrative divisions

The district is subdivided into 7 communes (tambon), which are further subdivided into 79 villages (muban). Nikhom Kham Soi is a township (thesaban tambon) which covers parts of the tambon Nikhom Kham Soi, Na Kok and Chok Chai. There are further 7 Tambon administrative organization (TAO).
