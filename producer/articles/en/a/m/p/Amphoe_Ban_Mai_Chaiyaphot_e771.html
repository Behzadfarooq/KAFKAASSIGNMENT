Ban Mai Chaiyaphot (Thai: บ้านใหม่ไชยพจน์) is a district (Amphoe) of Buriram Province, north-eastern Thailand.

[edit] History

The minor district (King Amphoe) Ban Mai Chaiyapho was created in 1992 by splitting area from Phutthaisong district. In 1997 it was upgraded to a full district.

[edit] Geography

Neighboring districts are (from the east clockwise) Phutthaisong of Buriram Province, Mueang Yang and Prathai of Nakhon Ratchasima Province, and Nong Song Hong of Khon Kaen Province.

[edit] Administration

The district is subdivided into 5 communes (tambon), which are further subdivided into 55 villages (muban). Ban Mai Chaiyaphot is a township (thesaban tambon) which covers parts of tambon Nong Waeng. There are also 5 Tambon administrative organizations (TAO).
