Kaeng Sanam Nang (Thai: แก้งสนามนาง) is the northernmost district (Amphoe) of Nakhon Ratchasima Province, northeastern Thailand.

[edit] History

This area was originally the Tambon Kaeng Sanam Nang of Bua Yai district. It was upgraded to be a minor district (King Amphoe) in 1986. The government officially upgraded it to a full distict in 1993.

[edit] Geography

Neighboring districts are (from the north clockwise) Mueang Chaiyaphum and Khon Sawan of Chaiyaphum Province, Waeng Noi of Khon Kaen Province, Bua Yai and Ban Lueam of Nakhon Ratchasima Province.

[edit] Administration

The district is subdivided into 5 communes (tambon). There are no municipal (thesaban) areas within the district.

1. Kaeng Sanam Nang แก้งสนามนาง
2. Non Samran โนนสำราญ
3. Bueng Phalai บึงพะไล
4. Sisuk สีสุก
5. Bueng Samrong บึงสำโรง
