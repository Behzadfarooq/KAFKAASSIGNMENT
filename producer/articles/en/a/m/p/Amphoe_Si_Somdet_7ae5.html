Si Somdet (Thai: ศรีสมเด็จ) is a district (Amphoe) of Roi Et Province, Thailand.

[edit] Geography

Neighboring districts are (from the north clockwise) Mueang Roi Et and Chaturaphak Phiman of Roi Et Province, Wapi Pathum, Kae Dam and Mueang Maha Sarakham of Maha Sarakham Province.

[edit] History

The district was created on April 1 1987, when it was split off from Mueang Roi Et district.

[edit] Administration

The district is subdivided into 8 communes (tambon), which are further subdivided into 82 villages (muban). There are no municipal (thesaban) areas, and 8 Tambon administrative organization (TAO).
