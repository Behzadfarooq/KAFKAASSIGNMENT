Mueang Uttaradit (Thai: เมืองอุตรดิตถ์) is the capital district (Amphoe Mueang) of Uttaradit Province, northern Thailand.

[edit] Geography

Neighboring districts are (from the east clockwise) Tha Pla, Thong Saen Khan, Tron, Laplae of Uttaradit Province and Den Chai of Phrae Province

[edit] Administration

The district is subdivided into 17 communes (tambon), which are further subdivided into 154 villages (muban). The town (thesaban mueang) Uttaradit covers the whole tambon Tha It. There are two more townships (thesaban tambon) - Wang Ka Phi covers the whole tambon Wang Ka Phi and Ban Dan Na Kham parts of tambon Ban Dan Na Kham. There are further 15 Tambon administrative organizations (TAO).
