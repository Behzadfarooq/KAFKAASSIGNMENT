Khlong Thom (Thai: คลองท่อม) is a district (Amphoe) in Krabi Province, Thailand.

[edit] Geography

Neighboring districts are (from the west clockwise) Ko Lanta, Nuea Khlong, Khao Phanom and Lam Thap of Krabi Province, and Wang Wiset and Sikao of Trang Province. To the southwest is the Andaman Sea.

[edit] Administration

The district is subdivided into 5 communes (tambon), which are further subdivided into 65 villages (muban). There are two townships (thesaban tambon) - Khlong Thom Tai and Khlong Phon, each covering parts of the same-named tambon. Each tambon has a Tambon administrative organization (TAO), responsible for the area not belonging to any of the townships.
