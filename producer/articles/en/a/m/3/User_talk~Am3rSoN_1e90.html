[edit] Eagle River High School

Hi, Thanks for contributions to Wikipedia and concern about the Eagle River High School site. It's important to note that Wikipedia is, of course, the encyclopedia that anyone can edit including people that don't go to your high school. Although I'm sure you have a firsthand perspective on the environment at ERHS if there is an edit dispute please make sure you discuss it on the talk page and come to a consensus about the content. The rationale for reverting the edit you mentioned to me is below:

  • I changed formatting. For example, instead of "2" one writes "two."
  • I deleted mention that the building has potential for growth because that is already mentioned in another section and thus is redundant.
  • I deleted some of the gushy stuff (e.g., "From the first day of school the nice environment of the school started to develop. With the three school values: Excellence, Respect and Community, the staff and students set their goals high.") because it has no place in an encyclopedia.

Let me know if you'd like to discuss any of these specifically and I'm sure we can come to an agreement.

Jarfingle 03:34, 7 March 2007 (UTC)

Sure thing. I added an "in use" template on the page to let other user's know what's going on. In the future, however, please make sure you'll always civil with others. You'll generally find they have good intentions. Also, you can sign your posts on one's talk pages with four tildes (~~~~).
