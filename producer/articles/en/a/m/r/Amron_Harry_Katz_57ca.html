Amron Harry Katz (August 15, 1915 - February 10, 1997) was an US-American physicists and specialist for aerial reconnaissance.

Katz was born in 1915 in the USA. He developed methods for aerial reconnaissance supported by space satellites. His work was used by the military intelligence like for locating disaster victims or resources. Between 1954 and 1969 he worked for the Rand corporation in Santa Monica/California. He died in 1997.
