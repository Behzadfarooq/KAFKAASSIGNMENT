Amritapuri is the main Ashram of Mata Amritanandamayi Devi. It is located in Parayakadavu, Kollam, Kerala about 120km north of Thiruvananthapuram and 120km south of Cochin. Amritapuri is also the name by which the location of the ashram is now known.

[1]

Amritapuri is also one of the campuses of the Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham.
