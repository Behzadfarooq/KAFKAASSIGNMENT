Amrik Bhatia (born March 16, 1967) is an Indian-born Finnish cricketer. He has played for Finland since 2001.

He first took part in the ECC Trophy tournament of 2001, which took place in Austria, during which Finland came in seventh place. He would later represent his adopted country at the 2002 ECC Representative Festival, and, two years later, at the ECC Championship. Most recently he played in Division Four of the newly formed European Championship for his Finnish team.

Generally speaking, Bhatia is an upper-middle order batsman for the Finnish side.
