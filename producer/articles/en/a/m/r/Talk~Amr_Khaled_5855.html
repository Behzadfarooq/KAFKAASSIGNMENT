[edit] Amr Khaled? a saint?

Degrading of women?? In one of the incidents Amr Khaled mentions that in the early days of the Prophet, when the Muslims migrated to Al Madina, the Madina men shared all their wealth with the arriving Muslims, and those who were married to more than one woman gave the extra ones to those Muslims.

Very oftenly Amr Khaled talks of women as objects being distributed and ordered. He emphasizes the importance of male supremacy in very subtle ways.

Amr Khaled refuses the mention of any reformist and liberating Islamic scholars, such as Sheikh Muhammed Abduh, or Jamal al-Din al-Afghani, and instead he backs his arguments with the writings of the conservative extremist Sayyed Qutb.

Also, this wikipedia article does not mention the strong economic benefits Amr Khaled made from advertisement in his programs, and from selling his tapes. —The preceding unsigned comment was added by Mo5tar (talk • contribs).
