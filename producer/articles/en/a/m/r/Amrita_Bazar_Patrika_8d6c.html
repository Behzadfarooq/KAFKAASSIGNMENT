Amrita Bazar Patrika was the oldest newspaper in India [1]. It debuted on 20 February 1868. It was an English daily newspaper published from Kolkata and other locations such as Cuttack, Ranchi and Allahabad [2].

Amrita Bazar which used to be a nationalist newspaper during the British rule, discontinued its publication from 1986. Recently it was announced that the newspaper would be relaunched. [1]
