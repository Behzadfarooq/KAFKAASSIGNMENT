Hi there, welcome to my userpage!

I am mainly interested in computers and video-games, the industry and technology behind them, and the internet culture that has developed around them. Fortunately, many articles about both popular and obscure games are already written, showing the versatility of this encyclopedia.

I am also interested in evolution and hockey, and will occasionally edit articles related to these two topics.

You can find me doing RC patrol at times, reverting vandalism and putting articles up for speedy deletion.
