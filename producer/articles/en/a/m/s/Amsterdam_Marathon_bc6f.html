The Amsterdam Marathon is an annual marathon race over the classic distance of 42km and 1950 meters held in Amsterdam, The Netherlands since 1975. It is held in October of every year since the 24th edition in 1999, and attracts many top athletes from around the world since the late 1990s, when the organisation choosed for a new, more flat parcours. It's one of the twelve top marathons in the world, and part of the Golden League. The event is the second marathon in the Netherlands, after the Rotterdam Marathon.
