Amsterdam Sportsman of the year

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Amsterdam Sportsman and Sportswoman of the Year is an annual election, organised since 1999 by the city's division for topsport named Topsport Amsterdam in cooperation with the city council. The award, named Fanny after four-time Olympic champions Fanny Blankers-Koen (London, 1948), goes to sportspeople who were born, have lived or played in Amsterdam, Zuid-Holland. Also the Coach and the Talent of the Year are being selected every single year.
