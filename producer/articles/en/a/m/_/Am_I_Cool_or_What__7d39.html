Am I Cool or What? is a compact disc featuring songs about Garfield, the fictitious cartoon cat. It was released on July 31, 1991 by GRP Records. The genre is primarily R&B and contemporary jazz. The album features appearances by important genre luminaries, such as B. B. King. It peaked at #23 on the Billboard charts for Top Contemporary Jazz Albums. [1]
