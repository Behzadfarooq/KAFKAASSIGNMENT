The stag Eikþyrnir and the goat Heiðrún on top of Valhalla.

From the 17th century Icelandic manuscript AM 738 4to, now in the care of the Árni Magnússon Institute in Iceland.

This image is from the Árni Magnússon Institute in Iceland. As a photograph of a work whose copyright has expired, it is also in the public domain in the United States (see Bridgeman Art Library v. Corel Corp.). As a courtesy, the Institute requests to be mentioned as their source and, preferably, that the relevant manuscript registration number be stated as well.


The two-dimensional work of art depicted in this image is in the public domain in the United States and in those countries with a copyright term of life of the author plus 100 years. This photograph of the work is also in the public domain in the United States (see Bridgeman Art Library v. Corel Corp.).
