Am Blues vorus
Music DVD by Züri West
Released March 28, 2003
Length approx. 124:00
Producer(s) Annina Furrer & Regula Begert

Am Blues vorus is a documentary film about Bernese rock band Züri West. The main plot is a film crew accompanying the band during the development of their 2001 album Radio zum Glück, from writing the songs to performing them live after album release.

  4. I verabschiedemimau - Live video
  5. (Bitte Baby) blib no chli bi mir - Music video

  8. No'ne Blues - Music video
