Am I Blue? is an episode in Class of 3000

[edit] Plot

The Westley Fall Showcase is coming up, and the group always gets pummeled with rotten tomatoes making them worried. So when they decide they need a gimmick, Philly Phil shows them one of his wacky machines that is a light show as well as a slurpee maker. Before the show, Philly Phil adds too much blueberry flavor syrup to the machine and makes a blast, accidentally turn everyone blue, except Eddie who dodges the blueberry blast. When they show up to perform, the crowd loves their blueness, and turn the gang into instant celebrities.

When the Westley Spring showcase is coming up, the gang's newfound fame and fortune make them ditch practice and blue-less Eddie is the only one practicing. When the Spring Showcase arrives, Eddie isn't allowed to play with the blue gang due to his normality, and watches as the gang forgets everything they know about their instruments and plays horribly. The fans now hate the blue band and pelt them with rotten tomatoes.

Since Eddie was the only one practicing, he performs a great song and the fans now love him. The rest of the gang decide that their music is the most important thing in the band.

  • Philly Phil's stuffed giraffe keeps appearing out of nowhere when something bad happens. The places it appears are:
    • Beating Philly Phil at a tabletop RPG in his basement.
    • In Philly Phil's bedroom after Phil said his giraffe thought that the invention would not work.
    • Next to the curtains on the stage after Philly Phil turned everyone (except Eddie) blue.
    • On Lil'D's bedroom window after he realized he was no longer blue.
    • On top of A Highly Guarded Tower guarding the blueprints.
    • At the end of the episode.
  • There was a reference from Charlie and the Chocolate Factory when Philly Phil"s fixes his machine and turns everyone into blueberries, then Madison tries to eat Lil'd's fingers because he was delicious.
  • Once again, Eddie's crush on Tamika is hinted at when he wants to keep the tomato she threw at him
  • In every episode,(when somebody throws something) a boy says "Ow, my eye!" but in this episode, a girl (with many broken body parts) says "Ow, my eye"
  • In this episode, it shows that Sunny can play the piano and the clarinet.
