Advanced Micro Devices (abbreviated AMD) is an American manufacturer of integrated circuits.

AMD, may also refer to:

  • A Modest Destiny, the webcomic by Sean Howard
  • Acid mine drainage, an ecological problem concerning very acidic water resulting from mining
  • Advanced Medical Directive
  • Age-related macular degeneration, a disease of the eye
  • Alliance for a Democratic Mauritania, or Alliance pour un Mauritanie démocratique, a now-defunct Mauritanian opposition movement
  • Archway metal detector, used at entrances to secured buildings
  • Armenian dram, from its the ISO 4217 symbol
  • Asynchronous message dispatch
  • Angular momentum desaturation (see Mars Climate Orbiter)
  • Berkeley Automounter, a daemon on Unix-like operating systems
  • Association of Media in Digital, a Japanese organization
  • AMD 65 Automata Módosított Deszantfegyver (Automatic Modified Descent), a variation of the AK-47 rifle from Hungary
  • Aircraft Manufacturing and Development, a manufacturer of airplanes
  • Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel International Airport, Ahmedabad, India, from its IATA airport code
