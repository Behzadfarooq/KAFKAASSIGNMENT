The AMD at The Glen is a NASCAR Nextel Cup stock car race held at Watkins Glen International in Watkins Glen, New York. It is one of two road course races in the NASCAR Nextel Cup schedule, the other being the Dodge/Save Mart 350. The race is being called the NASCAR Nextel Cup at the Glen for the 2007 edition.

Race length notes

  • 1957 race 44 laps (101.2 miles) of a 2.3 mile course
  • 1964-1965 races 66 laps (151.8 miles) of a 2.3 mile course
  • 1986-1991 races were run on the 2.428-mile 1971 Six Hours course (90 laps/218.52 miles)
  • 1992-Present, the addition of the Inner Loop chicane lengthened the track to 2.454 miles (90 laps/220.86 miles)
