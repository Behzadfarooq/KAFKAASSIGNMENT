AMD Am9080.

Am9080 is CPU manufactured by AMD. It was originally produced without license as a clone of the Intel 8080. Later, an agreement was made with Intel to manufacture it. The first versions of the Am9080 available in April of 1974. This CPU operated at a speed of 2 MHz.
