AMG

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

AMG is a three-letter abbreviation with multiple meanings, as described below:

  • Mercedes-AMG, the racing team and tuning division of Mercedes-Benz
  • AB Motorfabriken i Göteborg, a former Swedish automobile manufacturer
    • AMG (automobile), an automobile manufactured between 1903 and 1905
  • Access Media Group, a Television and Multimedia Company owned by CHUM Limited
  • Academy Music Group, an operator of music venues in the United Kingdom
  • Algebraic Multigrid Method, a numerical method for solving differential equations and sparse matrices
  • All Media Guide, a music, movie, and game metadata database company
    • All Music Guide, the music component of All Media Guide
  • Athletic Model Guild, a pioneering male model and publishing organization founded in the 1940s
  • AM General, a manufacturer of speciality and military vehicles
  • AMG (rapper), a rapper from California
  • A Mulher Gorila, an avant-garde circus from Argentina
  • Ah! My Goddess, an alternative name for Oh My Goddess!
  • Angel Music Group, a record label
