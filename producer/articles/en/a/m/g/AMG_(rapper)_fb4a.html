AMG (rapper)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

AMG (born Jason Lewis in Brooklyn, New York on September 29, 1970) is a West Coast rapper from Compton, California known for the sexually explicit — albeit relatively good-natured and humorous — nature of his lyrics and best known for his songs "Bitch Betta Have My Money" and "Tha Booty Up." The British electronica group Hardknox later sampled "Bitch Betta Have My Money" on their most famous single, "Who's Money?". AMG is currently in the process of recording an album with DJ Quik under the name "The Fixxers". It will be called "The Midnight Life" and is scheduled to release summer 2007.
