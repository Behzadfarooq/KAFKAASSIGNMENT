Ambyr Childers (born 1988 in Murrieta, California) is an American actress.

Aside from her role as Barbie in the 2003 film Dickie Roberts: Former Child Star, she is known mostly for her role on the daytime soap opera All My Children as Colby Chandler, which she's portrayed since July 2006. Having appeared in a multitude of national television commercials, she was also seen in the series S Club 7. She also has been noted for her resemblance to Hilary Duff of Lizzie McGuire fame.

An avid athlete, Childers likes to play golf, snow board, wake board, play basketball and horseback ride.
