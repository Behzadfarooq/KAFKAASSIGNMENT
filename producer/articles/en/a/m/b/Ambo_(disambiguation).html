Ambo (disambiguation)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Ambo is a town in central Ethiopia.

Ambo may also refer to the following places:

  • Kom Ombo, a town in Egypt known for its ancient Greek temple
  • Huttons Ambo, referring collectively to North Yorkshire villages High Hutton and Low Hutton
  • Lillings Ambo, referring collectively to Yorkshire villages East Lilling and West Lilling

Ambo, in a religious context, may also refer to :

  • Ambo, a pulpit that also serves as a lectern
  • Ambo (liturgy), in the Eastern Orthodox and Eastern Catholic churces refers to the elevated walkway in front of the Iconostasis.

Ambo may also refer to:

  • Ambo, a slang term for an ambulance
  • Ambo, a music project of computer game designer Robyn Miller
