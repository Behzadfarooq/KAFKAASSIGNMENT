Ambese Tolosa (born 28 August 1977 in Shewa) is an Ethiopian long-distance runner who specializes in the marathon race.

[edit] Achievements

Year Tournament Venue Result Event
1999 World Championships Seville, Spain 13th Marathon
2000 World Cross Country Championships Vilamoura 2nd Team competition
2003 World Championships Paris, France 19th Marathon
2004 Olympic Games Athens, Greece 15th Marathon
Paris Marathon Paris, France 1st Marathon, 2:08:56 PB
2005 World Championships Helsinki, Finland 19th Marathon
2006 Rock 'n' Roll Marathon San Diego, California, USA 1st Marathon
Tokyo Marathon Tokyo, Japan 1st Marathon
Honolulu Marathon Honolulu, Hawaii 1st Marathon

[edit] Personal bests

  • Half marathon - 1:01:18 hrs (2005)
  • Marathon - 2:08:56 hrs (2004)
