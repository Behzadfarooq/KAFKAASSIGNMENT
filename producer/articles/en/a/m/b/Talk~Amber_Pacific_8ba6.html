[edit] Truth in Sencerity release date

After User:Crazyman50000 ordered the CD off hopelessrecords.com on January 25th 2006, they debited my bank account for the CD on January 29th 2006, with a promised delivery on release. This insights that due to them taking payment for the CD - meaning it's been despatched; and it taking 3 weeks to arrive, the CD will be released 3rd week in February and as most CDs are released on a Friday, it's a fair guess that the date is 16th Feb.

However, due to there being no concrete release date, this information could be subject to change.

I can post proof of my account and proof or order though I don't really want to do this.

Best bet's to simply wait until Feb 16th and see if my logic was correct.

Update

The CD never came...

For what it's worth, CDs are usually released on Tuesdays. And a band on Hopeless Records would probably publicize an official release date for a sophomore LP. Chubbles 23:18, 18 February 2007 (UTC)
