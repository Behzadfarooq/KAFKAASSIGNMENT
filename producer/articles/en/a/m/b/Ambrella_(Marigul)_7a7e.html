[edit] Company Profile

Ambrella is a video game development company who works as a second-party developer for Nintendo.

Ambrella is a part of Marigul Management, Inc., a company created by Nintendo and the Japanese telecommunications company Recruit for the purpose of enlisting smaller design teams to focus their energies on original game design, rather than worrying about creating big-time sellers.

Ambrella was responsible for the Nintendo 64 voice recognition game, Hey You, Pikachu! in 1998, then they developed Pokémon Channel for the Nintendo GameCube in 2003, and then developed Pokémon Dash for the Nintendo DS in 2004.
