Amber Nichole Miller (born December 5, 1977 in Denver, Colorado) (often just "Amber Nichole") is a model and actress.

Miller began modeling at the age of five. After graduating high school, she moved with her family to Las Vegas, Nevada and resumed her modeling career. She models for Ultimate Fighting Championship and formerly modeled for Venus Swimwear. She has had minor roles in 3000 Miles to Graceland, Ocean's Eleven, and Rush Hour 2 as well as various television shows, independent films, and commercials.
