Amber Rain (born November 30, 1982 in Inland Empire, California) is an American pornographic actress. Her first video was in 2003, as part of the Dirty Debutantes series for Ed Powers. Before then she worked as a waitress. She is popularly known for her youthful looks and sexual exuberance.

(Often confused with porn star Amber Rayne, whose stage name is phonetically identical.)
