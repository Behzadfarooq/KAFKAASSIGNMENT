Scouting Barnstar

The Scouting Barnstar may be awarded for distinguished efforts in Scouting-related articles. This barnstar is intended for those who show the Scouting spirit in order to advance Wikipedia's coverage of Scouting.

It can also be used to award people who have shown the principals of Scouting in their activities on Wikipedia.

See Scouting Barnstar or Scouting Project for more information. A list of recipients is also at Wikipedia:WikiProject Scouting/Recognitions.

This award was introduced by the WikiProject Scouting on 10 February 2006 by evrik and was designed by User:Mang_kiko.
