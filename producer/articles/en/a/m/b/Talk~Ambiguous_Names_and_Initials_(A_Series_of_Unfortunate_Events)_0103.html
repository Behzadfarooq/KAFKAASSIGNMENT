[edit] Move

I was thinking, wouldn't it be better if we moved this article to Ambiguous Names and Initials (A Series of Unfortunate Events)? After all, the names and initials' meanings have been stated in the books; they are just unclear, but not mysterious. Also, it would be better if we deleted the original research, as it is both unverified and irrelevant, due to the fact that it states the origin of the names, not their ambiguity.--Orthologist 17:16, 15 February 2007 (UTC)

Support. (If the opinion of an IP editor counts for anything...) [edit] What is this article meant to be?

Right now, this article contains literary allusions, random speculations on the family tree, and matches up any random pair of initials to any random thing in the book. Seriously, guys, LS for LittleSt elf and Latch String? The only really ambiguous sets of initials in the series are V.F.D. and J.S., and we already have an article for the former. Literary allusions can be moved to character pages, the family tree doesn't make sense any more and is irrelevant besides. Does somebody want to come up with a consistent definition of what's meant to be on this page, or perhaps instead it can be deleted and replaced with J.S. (A Series of Unfortunate Events).

This one is completely unnecessary. The "names" section is helpful, as it lists linguistic references in the books which can be quite helpful, but the rest of the article is pointless.--Orthologist 22:02, 28 March 2007 (UTC)
