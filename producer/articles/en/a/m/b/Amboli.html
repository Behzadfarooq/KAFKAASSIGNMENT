[edit] Amboli

Amboli is the Famous hill station in south Maharashatra.

[edit] Nearest City

  • Savantwadi
  • Kolhapur

Amboli at an altitude of 690 m. is the last mountain resort before the coastal highlands of Goa and a relatively unexplored one. Perhaps the distance from Mumbai & Pune and the proximity to Goa has left this glorious jewel of Maharashtra high and wet.

EcoMantra organises jungle camping in the Amboli, that falls in the western ghats biosphere.

[edit] Nearest Railway Station

  • Savantwadi Road
