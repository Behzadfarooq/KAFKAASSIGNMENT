This is a list of ambassadors from Israel.

The incumbents change from time to time; sometimes a post starts or stops being temporarily headed by a lower ranking diplomat. Occasionally, a post is created or abolished.

[edit] Notes

  1. ^ The ambassador to Angola also serves as Israel's non-resident Ambassador to Mozambique and São Tomé and Príncipe.
  2. ^ The ambassador to Argentina also serves as Israel's non-resident Ambassador to Paraguay.
  3. ^ The ambassador to Australia also serves as Israel's non-resident Ambassador to Fiji, New Zealand and Papua New Guinea.
  4. ^ The ambassador to Belgium also serves as Israel's non-resident Ambassador to Luxembourg.
  5. ^ The ambassador to Cameroon also serves as Israel's non-resident Ambassador to Central African Republic,Congo, Equatorial Guineaand Gabon.
  6. ^ The ambassador to China also serves as Israel's non-resident Ambassador to Mongolia.
  7. ^ The ambassador to Costa Rica also serves as Israel's non-resident Ambassador to Nicaragua.
  8. ^ The ambassador to Côte d'Ivoire also serves as Israel's non-resident Ambassador to Benin, Burkina Faso, Liberia and Togo.
  9. ^ The ambassador to the Dominican Republic also serves as Israel's non-resident Ambassador to Antigua and Barbuda, Dominica, Grenada, Haiti, Jamaica, Saint Kitts and Nevis, Saint Lucia, and Saint Vincent and the Grenadines.
  10. ^ The ambassador to El Salvador also serves as Israel's non-resident Ambassador to Belize.
  11. ^ The ambassador to Ethiopia also serves as Israel's non-resident Ambassador to Burundi and Rwanda.
  12. ^ The ambassador to Finland also serves as Israel's non-resident Ambassador to Estonia.
  13. ^ The ambassador to Guatemala also serves as Israel's non-resident Ambassador to Honduras.
  14. ^ The ambassador to India also serves as Israel's non-resident Ambassador to Sri Lanka.
  15. ^ The ambassador to Italy also serves as Israel's non-resident Ambassador to Malta and San Marino.
  16. ^ The ambassador to Kenya also serves as Israel's representative to UN organizations in Nairobi and non-resident Ambassador to Malawi, Seychelles, Tanzania, Uganda and Zambia.
  17. ^ The ambassador to Italy also serves as Israel's non-resident Ambassador to Lithuania.
  18. ^ The ambassador to Mexico also serves as Israel's non-resident Ambassador to Bahamas.
  19. ^ The ambassador to Nigeria also serves as Israel's non-resident Ambassador to Ghana.
  20. ^ The ambassador to Norway also serves as Israel's non-resident Ambassador to Iceland.
  21. ^ The ambassador to Senegal also serves as Israel's non-resident Ambassador to Cape Verde, Gambia, Guinea-Bissau and Sierra Leone.
  22. ^ The ambassador to Serbia also serves as Israel's non-resident Ambassador to Montenegro.
  23. ^ The ambassador to Singapore also serves as Israel's non-resident Ambassador to Timor-Leste.
  24. ^ The ambassador to South Africa also serves as Israel's non-resident Ambassador to Botswana, Lesotho, Namibia, Swaziland and Zimbabwe.
  25. ^ The ambassador to Spain also serves as Israel's non-resident Ambassador to Andorra.
  26. ^ The ambassador to Switzerland also serves as Israel's non-resident Ambassador to Liechtenstein.
  27. ^ The ambassador to Thailand also serves as Israel's non-resident Ambassador to Cambodia.
  28. ^ The ambassador to Ukraine also serves as Israel's non-resident Ambassador to Moldova.
  29. ^ The ambassador to Uzbekistan also serves as Israel's non-resident Ambassador to Tajikistan.
  30. ^ The ambassador to Venezuela also serves as Israel's non-resident Ambassador to Barbados, Guyana, Suriname and Trinidad and Tobago.
  31. ^ The ambassador to Vietnam also serves as Israel's non-resident Ambassador to Laos.
  32. ^ The consul general to Marsaille also serves as Israel's non-resident Ambassador to Monaco.
