Ambika (in Kakhi Chaddai with Kamal Haasan)

Ambika is an Indian film actress. She has acted in a number of South Indian films. She started her career with the Malayalam movie Mamangam in 1979. Within a couple of years she became a sought after actress not only in Malayalam, but also in Tamil and Kannada as well.
