Amber Pacific is a pop punk band that formed in 2002 as a trio of high school juniors who performed locally in their Seattle hometown just for kicks under the name Follow Through. They participated on the Warped Tour in 2004, 2005, and 2006, and toured with Matchbook Romance on the Sub City Take Action Tour in spring of 2006.

Ben Harper (originally in Yellowcard) joined Amber Pacific and joined the recording of their 3rd studio album. Recording started 16th October 2006; Harper left the band before the end of the year.

On February 4th, 2007, Amber Pacific confirmed that they will be playing the entire Warped Tour 2007 on the Hurley Stage.[1]

As of 4:30 AM February 28, Amber Pacific stated in their MySpace blog that the new CD is done. The first song released off the album, which is titled Truth in Sincerity, is called Fall Back Into My Life. The song appears on the TMNT (2007 film) soundtrack.
