Amba Bongo is a writer and advocate for refugees from the Democratic Republic of the Congo-(Kinshasa. Bongo had been involved in the Congolese Ministry of Education, but had to flee after 1991 because she denounced its corruption. She applied for asylum status in the United Kingdom soon after fleeing the Congo.
