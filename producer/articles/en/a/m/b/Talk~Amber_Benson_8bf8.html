Chance is Amber Benson’s first independent feature film, which was her debut as a writer, director, and producer. Amber also played the main character Chance. Plot Summary: Chance is a young woman living in Los Angeles who tries to get by in life any way she can. Narrating her own personal story with voice-over, as well as direct-at-camera narration, she opens the movie by explaining how life is like a movie with someone’s finger pressed on fast-forward...
