Ambiguous image

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Ambiguous images are an optical illusion images which are crafted to exploit graphical similarities and other properties of visual system interpretation between two or more distinct image forms. These are famous for inducing the phenomenon of multistable perception.

(Some often used synonymous terms are reversal images, puzzle images and perceptual rivalry)
