Fievel from An American Tail, Amblimation's mascot.

Amblimation was the animation production arm of Steven Spielberg's Amblin Entertainment, and was active from 1991 until about 1997.

The feature animation studio was located in London, England, United Kingdom. The studio was folded into DreamWorks' animation division, later spun-off into DreamWorks Animation.

The company's mascot was Fievel from An American Tail, who appears in its production logo.

[edit] Films and television series

  • An American Tail: Fievel Goes West (1991)
  • A Wish for Wings That Work (television special based on the comic strip Bloom County) (1991)
  • Back to the Future: The Animated Series (1991-1992)
  • Fievel's American Tails (television series) (1992-1993)
  • We're Back! A Dinosaur's Story (1993)
  • Balto (1996)
