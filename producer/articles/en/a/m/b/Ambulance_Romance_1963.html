Ambulance Romance is an EP by Post-Hardcore band The Bled. It was a one-sided 12" vinyl record, with four songs on it, released in 2002 by Ambit Records but there was never an attempt to sell it. Production was limited to only 666 copies, with spray-painted on album art and hand written numbers. Some of the albums were dyed a swirly red. It was recorded before current singer James Munoz joined the band.

[edit] Trivia

  • "Ambulance Romance" was later re-recorded and re-sung as "I Don't Keep With Liars Anymore" on Found In The Flood.

[edit] Rarity

Ambulance Romance is a rare vinyl EP released on an obscure record company, and thus as an EP is very hard to find. However, the songs were released, along with the His First Crush EP, on Pass the Flask (Reissue).
