Amber Rose also known as Amber, The Lesbian Queefer (born July 7, 1979 in San Francisco, California, USA) is a pornographic actress specialized in queefing movies.

In 2002, she was the runner-up in the Miss Howard Stern pageant in Las Vegas, which led to a date with Mickey Rourke and a weekend at Gary Busey's house in Malibu, California.[1] This exposure lead to her subsequently making the lesbian adult film Amber the Lesbian Queefer[1] with veteran actress Mimi Miyagi in 2002.[1]
