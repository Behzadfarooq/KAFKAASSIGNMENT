AMBUCS, Inc. (American Business Clubs), is a national charitable service organization. The goal of AMBUCS is to create mobility and independence for people with disabilities.

The AMBUCS logo

The AMBUCS mission is fullfilled by members of local chapters who;

  • Perform Community Service
  • Provide AmTryke therapeutic tricycles to children with disabilities, and
  • Provide scholarships to therapists

Many AMBUCS members consider the organization a fun way to serve in their community.

[edit] Youth Auxiliaries

AMBUCS Youth Auxiliary chapters are made up of college or high school students that care about the AMBUCS mission.
