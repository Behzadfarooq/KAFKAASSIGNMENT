This category contains all current and former ambassadors of the United States. Ambassadors are also listed by diplomatic post in Category:Lists of United States ambassadors.

Current ambassadors of the United States to all nations are listed in Ambassadors from the United States.

For foreign ambassadors to the United States, see Category:Ambassadors to the United States.
