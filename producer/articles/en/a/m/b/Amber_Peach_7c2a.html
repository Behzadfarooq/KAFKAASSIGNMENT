[edit] Early life

Born in Mobile, Alabama, Amber Peach graduated from Irvington, Alabama's Alma Bryant High School in 2002 with good standing. Prior to her career in pornography, she worked many jobs, including one in a library, one as a stripper and one at a Burger King fast food restaurant.[2]

Due to her youthful appearance, she often portrays "teen" or "barely legal" women.
