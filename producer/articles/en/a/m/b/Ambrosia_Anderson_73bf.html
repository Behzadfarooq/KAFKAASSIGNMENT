Ambrosia Anderson (born March 14, 1984 Hawaii) is a professional basketball player in the WNBA for New York Liberty. She is 6ft 1in.

Anderson attended college at Brigham Young University and graduated in 2006. Following her collegiate career, she was selected 17th overall in the 2006 WNBA Draft by the Detroit Shock and was then dealt to the Lynx for Jacqueline Batteast on draft day. She was waived by the Minnesota Lynx on May 9, 2006, and signed by the Connecticut Sun on May 11, 2006. Soon in 2006, she was traded to the New York Liberty.
