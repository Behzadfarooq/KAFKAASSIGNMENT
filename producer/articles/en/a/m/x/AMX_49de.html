AMX is a three-letter abbreviation with multiple meanings, as described below:

  • The AMX, a car made by American Motors Corporation
  • The AMX International AMX, a fighter aircraft
  • AMX International, the company that makes the aircraft
  • AMX Mod a server-side modification for Half-Life games
  • AMX LLC, a manufacturer of audio-visual control devices
  • AMX 30, AMX 13 and AMX 10 tanks produced by GIAT Industries
  • AMX index, a stock market index of Euronext Amsterdam
  • AMX192, a lighting control standard
  • Kadak AMX, a real-time operating system [1]
  • AMX Mouse, a mouse input device for the BBC Micro
  • AMX - Aeroméxico's ICAO airline designator
