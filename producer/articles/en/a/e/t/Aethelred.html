Several persons of Anglo-Saxon England were named Æthelred or Ethelred ("well advised"):

  • Æthelred of Mercia (d. 716)
  • Earl Aethelred of Mercia (d. 911)
  • Aethelred II of England "the Unready"
  • Ethelred, Earl of Fife
  • Æthelred I of East Anglia (fl 760s-780s)
  • Æthelred II of East Anglia (fl 870s)
  • Æthelred I of Northumbria
