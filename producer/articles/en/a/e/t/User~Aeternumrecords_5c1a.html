This is a list of Portland and Seattle based goth, darkwave, post punk, shoegaze, synthpop, electronic and industrial music bands. This list includes former and currently active bands in the entire Pacific Northwest region and hopefully can be used to give people an idea of the significance of this region to these genres of music.

Artist: is the band name (and will be linked to the bands Wikipedia article if there is one), side projects or other names should have their own entries in the list

Last Residence: this is the last or current residence of bands (notice this list includes bands that are no longer in the Pacific Northwest)

Number of Releases: this number is the number of regular releases (bands that perform live only might have no releases)
