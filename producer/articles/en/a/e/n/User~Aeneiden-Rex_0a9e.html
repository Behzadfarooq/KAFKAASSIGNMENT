[edit] Me

I'm a student, from Stockholm, Sweden. My favourite videogames are Tekken, Street Fighter (series), Silent Hill, Resident Evil and Final Fantasy X. My other interests are Athletics & Music.


Michael Jackson "The true King of Pop, Rock & Soul" is innocent!!. I'm a hardcore Michael Jackson-fan and I always will be, I believe he's innocent. My favourite album is Thriller, closely followed by Off the Wall and Bad. I'm a hardcore Prince-fan also and my favourite albums so far are: 1999, Purple Rain, Batman, The Gold Experience, The Rainbow Children, 3121.
