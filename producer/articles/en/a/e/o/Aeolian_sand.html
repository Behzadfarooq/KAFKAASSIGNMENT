Aeolian sand is formed by windblown small particles. It is related to Loess which is windblown silt particles. The difference exists in the texture of the material being transferred. Sand ranges from 2.00 mm to .05 mm which is the determinant for Aeolian sand classification. The word Aeolian comes from Aeolus, the god of the winds in Greek mythology.
