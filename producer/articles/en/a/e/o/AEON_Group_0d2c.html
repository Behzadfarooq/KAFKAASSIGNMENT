ÆON Group or AEON Group (イオングループ, Ion Gurūpu?) is a group of retail and financial services companies based in Japan, which is centered on ÆON Co., Ltd. It has sister companies in Hong Kong and Taiwan, and also trades under the JUSCO name. It claims to be Japan's largest retail group in terms of sales. (1)
