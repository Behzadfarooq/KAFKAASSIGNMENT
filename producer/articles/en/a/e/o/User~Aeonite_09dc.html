Aeonite (aka aeon) is Michael Fiegel, a writer and graphic designer best known as the creator of Ninja Burger. He is the founding member of aethereal FORGE, a loose coalition of artists and writers responsible collectively for Ninja Burger and several other RPG projects.

Aeonite was an early member of Fark.com and created many of that site's graphics during its fledgling years, including the logo. From 2002 to 2006 he was the Lead Columns Editor for RPG.net, and has written extensively for a variety of Role-Playing Game companies, most notably the R. Talsorian Games website. He is also a regular book reviewer for Slashdot.org and writes for Dragon.

Aeonite was born in Buffalo, New York and resides in San Francisco, California.
