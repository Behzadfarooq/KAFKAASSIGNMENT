Aeon (disambiguation)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Aeon or AEON may refer to:

  • Aeon, another spelling of Aion, a particular classical deity.
  • aeon, a Greek word meaning "an age", or sometimes "forever", and thence is a concept in Gnostic theology.
  • Aeon (Thelema), large-scale historical units, of which the current is the "Aeon of Horus", found within the doctrine of Thelema.
  • Æon Flux, animated series.
  • Æon Flux (film), science fiction film.
  • Aeons (Final Fantasy), fictional summoned creatures from the PlayStation 2 video game Final Fantasy X.
  • AEON Group, a Japanese retailer chain.
  • AEON (eikaiwa), a Japanese English language school.
  • aeon or Giga-annum (Ga) is sometimes used to signify a billion (109) years.
  • Aeon Illuminate, a faction in the 2007 RTS Supreme Commander.
