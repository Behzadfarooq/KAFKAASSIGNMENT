[edit] Physical

For those of you who care, I'm about 1/2 German. As of June 28, 2006, I am 17 years old. I have wavy brown hair, blue eyes, and I was last measured at five feet, six inches. My current shoe size is a 9, and that should do it for the rest of my life - I hope. (Don't ask how much I weigh; I don't know and don't really care.) I do my best to keep in shape...which isn't easy, since I don't get out very much. I've been trying out different kinds of haircuts, but they've all been short. Oh yeah - and I have a small scar on my right elbow. It's not really visible unless you're looking for it.

[edit] Personality

I am usually very cheerful, I love to tell stories, and I also like just talking. A lot!! Oh, and all the above is only when I'm at ease. When I'm tense I get held-in and only speak when spoken to, and that very little. Part of that is that I never know what to talk about around other teens. I'm actually looking forward to the school year so I can try again at making more friends there. (I only have one.) Regardless, I sometimes put things away where I think they'll be safe, only to be unable to find them again! So, mostly, I'm a messy person. My parents get very angry with me because any time they give me instructions to carry out in, say, fifteen minutes, I would forget about it. Maybe I should write things down more often...


[edit] Family

I have two little brothers and a twin sister. She and I can only be told apart by the length of our hair. (Mine is short, hers is long.) She also wears glasses. My brothers are, let's see...by the time 2006 is over, they'll be 9 and 6. The nine-year-old is autistic. We're trying to teach him to tell us what he wants and to use the bathroom, and in my opinion it's slow progress. He pretty well has the hang of "I want ---- please," but that's it. As for the bathroom...well, it's currently a halfway victory. He'll rush in to urinate, but he still dumps in his underwear. Sometimes I have to help clean him up. (Annoying.) The six-year-old is my "battered brother." He had heart surgery as a baby, so he has a bunch of scars from that. Also, he has a scar on his thigh from a time when I accidentally spilled SpaghettiO's on him. (That might not have scarred, but he had an allergic reaction to the lotion Mom used.) He also seems to get scraped up more than Autistic.


[edit] Recreation/Hobbies

I play volleyball every summer - my game needs work, but I'm making progress. I also enjoy bike riding, but I cannot do stunts. (remember my mentioning a scar? That was going too fast on a stunt course.) I generally prefer quieter activities like watching cartoons (only on saturday mornings; we don't have cable) and making up stories to tell my sister. I have all kinds of ideas for stories! Sometimes I try to put my stories down on paper, but sooner or later it fizzles out. My history teacher likes the myths that I write - she read one of them to the entire class!


[edit] Food

I am far from a picky eater. The only things I won't eat are pickles or raw olives, and there is nothing that I won't try at least once!


[edit] Religion/Politics

I am a devoted Christian. If you have a problem with that, tough! Currently, I'm not involved in politics and I hope I never am!

[edit] Xanadu

This is a sci-fi/spiritual fanfiction I am writing with the occasional idea from Jeremie@theLab. It's effectively the same plot and the same characters as Code Lyoko, but it starts back before CL does (and with fewer questions) and the characters have different names and show up in a different order. Currently, there are four long chapters and a fifth one in process, and the only exact equivilants to CL's characters to show up so far are Jeremie, Ulrich, Yumi, Samantha, and Franz Hopper. There will be an equivilant to Aelita, but she'll only show up briefly in one of the chapters. I have so many ideas for this story that there's going to be several sequals to hold them all!
