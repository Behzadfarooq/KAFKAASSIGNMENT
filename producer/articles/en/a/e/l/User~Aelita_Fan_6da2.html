[edit] Physical

I have shoulder-length wavy brown hair, and tanned skin. Blue eyes are behind my glasses (I'm nearsighted), and my height is about 5'6". My shoe size is 8, and will stay so forever. Since June 28, I've been 17 years old.




[edit] Personality

I'm usually a very laid back, easygoing girl. I don't stir myself much for almost anything. I do tend to forget certain things from time to time (such as chores), but I always manage to get the job done - eventually. When I'm nervous, I get upset very, VERY easily, especially if I think I've done something wrong.




[edit] Family

I have two younger brothers and a twin sister. I'm still amazed that we're mixed up, because nowadays we don't look a thing alike! The older of my two brothers has autism. Very annoying, but tolerable. My youngest brother has scars from his heart surgery, and a burn on his left leg. Nevertheless, he's full of energy; he runs practically everywhere!




[edit] Hobbies

I love to knit. I can knit all sorts of animals - whether they exist or not - with no pattern! I can crochet, too...but not as often as knitting.



[edit] Food

I won't eat pickles or raw olives, and spicy things I only tolerate. But I'll try anything at least once.



[edit] Religion/Politics

I'm a straight Christian; have been since this last summer. And as for politics...who cares? I'd probably be a Republican.



[edit] Funny Things that I Remember Happening

When I was ten or maybe thirteen, I was at a swimming pool, and either I chose to, or someone dared me, to jump off the high diving board (at least eight feet above the water, which was fifteen feet deep there) backwards! So I was standing on the very edge of the board. I give this little hop off the end...and fell...and fell..and hit the water on my back! After I hit, I was wondering what had happened. My sister was watching from the pool, and she said that with the sun silhouetting me up there, it looked like I fell off. And what was really funny? The minute I got to the surface again (I floated to the surface; less work) I yelled, "I'm okay!" and backstroked over to the edge. After I got out of the pool, though...MAN! My back was smarting! Looking back, though, that was funny.
