[edit] Neo-Eldarin articles

Hi Mr. Hostetter and Mr. Fauskanger. I would like to remark that the articles concerning Neo-Eldarin should better have references, according to the Wikipedia policies. Of course I agree when I read 'some scholars believe that, but others disagree and accuse the others' or 'the term mature and immature for that period of Elvish was used but not any more' etc because I have witnessed such events. However ALL those statements should be sourced. I have seen less serious articles being butchered according WP:NOR for less serious reasons. Pictureuploader 19:02, 1 July 2006 (UTC)
