Aelurillus is a spider genus of the Salticidae family (jumping spiders).

Aelurillus catches and feeds on ants (myrmecophagy).

[edit] Distribution

Aelurillus occurs in the Palaearctic and Africa, with a few species known from India (A. improvisus, A. minimontanus) and Sri Lanka (A. kronestedti, A. quadrimaculatus). Aelurillus subfestivus and A. subfestivus are found in Japan.
