AEL Arena

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

This article or section contains information about scheduled or expected future events.

AEL Arena is a football stadium in Larissa, Greece, that is currently under construction. Once completed in 2007, it will be used mostly for football matches and will host the home matches of AEL 1964. The stadium will have a capacity of 21,000 people. It replaces their current stadium Alkazar.
