Aeba Koson

Aeba Koson, (饗庭篁村; 1855 August 15 - 1922 June 20) was a Japanese author.

Among his works is his translation of Edgar Allan Poe's, The Murders in the Rue Morgue.

He is buried in Somei cemetery in Tokyo.
