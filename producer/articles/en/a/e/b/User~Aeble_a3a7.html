[edit] Axel Eble

[edit] Education

  • Born in 1968 in Freiburg,
  • Elementary School at the Emil-Gött-Grundschule in Zähringen
  • Droste-Hülshoff-Gymnasium in Herdern
  • started Chemistry studies at the Albert-Ludwigs-Universität Freiburg
    • worked as a student employee doing Unix system administration at the CS department and the
    • Institute for Medical Biometry and Medical Computer Sciences
  • aborted the Chemistry studies and moved on to study computer sciences at the Hochschule Furtwangen University.
  • graduated with a Diplom-Informatiker (FH) degree in autumn 1998

[edit] Work

  • 1991-1995 worked as a student employee doing Unix system administration at the CS department and the
  • 1991-1995 Institute for Medical Biometry and Medical Computer Sciences
  • started work at Controlware Communication Systems in de:Dietzenbach in autumn 1998
  • self-employed contractor from 04/2000 until 12/2003
  • moved to de:Kempten (Allgäu) 01/2004 to work for a logistics company.

[edit] Family

  • married since 1998
  • two sons:
    • Linus, born 2001
    • Magnus, born 2003
