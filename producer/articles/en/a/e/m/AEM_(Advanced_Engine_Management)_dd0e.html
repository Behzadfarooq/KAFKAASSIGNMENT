AEM (Advanced Engine Management)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

AEM Company Logo

Advanced Engine Management (AEM), the parent company of DC Sports and Brute Force, is a popular after market performance manufacturer based in Hawthorne, California. AEM pioneered the Cold Air Intake for sport compacts, created the first hybrid intake systems for cars with engine swaps, created the first stand-alone programmable engine management system, and was the first to introduce a gauge with a built-in wideband UEGO controller and gauge readout.

In 2004 AEM acquired DC Sports.

AEM's products are frequently featured in automotive performance industry publications and websites, including Motor Trend, Car & Driver, Road & Track, Turbo, Sport Compact Car, Super Street, Modified, 5.0 Mustang & Super Fords, Race Pages, Muscle Mustangs & Fast Fords, Truckin’, Off Road, Sport Truck, 4Wheel Drive & Sport Utility, 4Wheel & Off Road, Temple of VTEC, EvolutionM.net, FreshAlloy.com, Nissanperformancemag.com, k20a.org, turbomustangs.com, tenmagazines.com, and off-road.com.

[edit] Racing

AEM Racing’s drag Civic, the claimed World’s Quickest Honda boast a 6.52 seconds quarter mile time and uses off the shelf AEM parts. Their Drift Honda S2000, piloted by Stephan Papadakis, qualified for four of the six events it attended in only its first season, with Stephan Papadakis achieving Rookie of the Year Honors in the FormulaD Drift Series.

[edit] Company Vision

AEM's Company Vision

"AEM will become the top competitor in the performance bolt-on and performance electronics segments of the North American automotive aftermarket. We will achieve a market leadership position through continued product innovation, the highest level of product integrity and by being a price/value leader. We will always put the customer first and set the standard for customer service in our industry.

The following values are essential to the growth of AEM:

  • The customer comes first
  • A strong work ethic and professional approach
  • Honesty, integrity and respect for coworkers
  • Clear, concise communication
  • An open mind and open door policy
  • An apolitical attitude focusing on teamwork and common goals

Realizing our vision will ensure continued company growth, which creates individual opportunities for all employees. The results of these efforts are a strong presence throughout the industry, and one that sends a clear, consistent message: AEM products are Engineered To Outperform."
