[edit] Biography

Andrew Edwards (Born 22 September 1989) is a British American high school student whose current place of residence is Wilmington, Delaware in the United States.

[edit] Wikipedia

Andrew became a member of the Wikipedia community on 27 March 2005. From then on he has made a series of contributions.

On 12 May 2005, Andrew became a member of the Doctor Who WikiProject
