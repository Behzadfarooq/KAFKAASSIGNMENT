Aerovis Airlines

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Aerovis Airlines' logo

Aerovis Airlines is an airline based in Kiev, Ukraine. It was established in 2003 and operates charter cargo services within Ukraine and to neighboring countries.

[edit] Code Data

  • ICAO Code: VIZ
  • Callsign: AEROVIS [1]

[edit] Fleet

As of August 2006 the Aerovis Airlines fleet includes:

  • 5 Antonov An-12 [2]
