Aerosmith's 2007 World Tour (or The Tour Heard 'Round the World) is a concert tour by American hard rock band Aerosmith that will see the band performing outside North America or Japan for the first time in about 8 years (since the Nine Lives Tour), and in some countries, the first time in 14 years (since the "Get a Grip Tour"). The band will also visit some countries, such as India and the United Arab Emirates, for the first time ever.

The concert tour, as of now, is slated to begin in Spring of 2007 in Latin America. The band will then do a second leg of dates in Eurasia in the early summer of 2007. The band is also likely to hit North America in late summer and early fall.

The tour is likely being put on in support of a new album the band is scheduled to release sometime in 2007. The tour will likely bear the name of the album once its title is announced.
