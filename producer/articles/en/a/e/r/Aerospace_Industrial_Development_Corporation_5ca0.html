Aerospace Industrial Development Corporation (漢翔航空工業股份有限公司) is an aerospace company based in Taichung which developed the AIDC Ching-kuo aircraft. The company is commonly referred to as AIDC.

[edit] About Aerospace Industrial Development Corporation

AIDC was established in 1969. In 1983,it was transferred to the Chung-Shan Institute of Science and Technology. In 2000, AIDC was divided into four units which are:

  • Aerostructures Division
  • Engine Division
  • Technology Division
  • Administration Division

[edit] Products

  • AIDC F-CK Indigenous Defence Fighter
  • AT-3 (jet trainer)
  • T-CH-1 (Basic Trainer)
  • PL-1 (Primary Trainer)
