[edit] About me

I am currently a graduate student at the University of Regina, studying Computer Science. I discovered wikipedia almost by accident, and it is quickly taking over my life, as I try to absorb as much knowledge as I can, while contributing where I am able to. Currently, I kind of float around wikipedia, browsing articles that intrigue me, while fixing any grammar or spelling mistakes that I might see.

Recently, I have taken to improving the articles The Count of Monte Cristo, the related Edmond Dantès, and other articles concerned with the novel The Count of Monte Cristo, which I consider to be the greatest novel ever written.
