Aeroquay Crescent

Aeroquay Crescent was an access road within Toronto's Malton Airport in Mississauga, Ontario. The road was to have circled by three circular aeroquay terminals, similar to the old Terminal 1 at Toronto Pearson International Airport. The road was connected with the then Airport Expressway and Dixon Road (portion now renamed Airport Road). The road's name was eventually erased with nameless access ramps and service roads, as well as the construction of the new Terminal 1.

Buildings that use to be served by Aeroquay Cresent:

  • the old Airport Administration Building
  • old Terminal 1 via underground tunnel
  • Terminal 2 and old outdoor parking lot
  • open reduced rate parking lot
  • Hertz Car Rental
