Aero L-159 Alca (Advanced light combat aircraft) is a Czech-built military training aircraft. It is used by the Czech Air Force (71 aircrafts, 1 lost in an accident).

[edit] Specifications (L-159A)

  • Crew: 1 pilot
  • Length: 12,73 m
  • Wingspan: 9,54 m
  • Height: 4,77 m
  • Empty weight: 4 360 kg
  • Max takeoff weight: 8 000 kg
  • Powerplant: 1x Honeywell / ITEC F124-GA-100 (28 kN)
  • Auxiliary power unit: SAFIR 5F

[edit] Performance

  • Maximum speed: 935 km/h
  • Range: - 1 570 km (2 530 km with fuel tanks)
  • Service ceiling: 13 200 m
  • Rate of climb: 48 m/s
  • Take-off roll: 440 m
  • Landing roll: 725 m
