I'm just a voracious reader who likes encyclopedias.

Suffice to say my interests are varied. I also am pretty good in geography and tend to be a stickler for spelling.

I also like Latin. But I tend to stick to the English Wikipedia, seeing as I know very little of other languages and probably would have to run it through a translator to correct it or understand it anyway.
