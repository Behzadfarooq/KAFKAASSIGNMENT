Aeromar (Transportes Aeromar, SA de CV) is an airline based in Mexico City, Mexico. It operates mainly domestic routes, its main base is Mexico City International Airport, Mexico City.

[edit] History

The airline was established on 29 January 1987 and started operations on 5 November 1987. It is owned by Grupo Aeromar (publicly listed).
