AeroGauge is a futuristic hovercraft racing game for the Nintendo 64 released in 1998 (1997 in Japan).

Ascii's AeroGauge runs basically along the same conceptual lines of play as Psygnosis' Wipeout XL or Acclaim's Extreme G. the main difference is that the veichles in the game fly instead of staying a few meters over the track so it's possible to maneuver them in the air, unlike games as wipeout or f-zero.
