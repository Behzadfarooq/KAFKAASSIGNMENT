AERO97ONE is a non -commerical FM station serving the several markets. WLXZ (Licensed to Auburn, New York) broadcasts on 97.1 MHz, while WLXQ (Licensed to Oswego, New York) operates at 97.7MHz, both on the FM dial. AERO97ONE also simulcasts on the AM frequency with transmitters in the towns of Constantia, New York, and Binghamton, New York.

[edit] History

WLXQ, whose previous format was LIPS106, flipped formats on June 1st, 2007. This is the fourth station format change in 3 years. The AM transmitters are W971AQ Constantia, New York and W97AX located in Binghamton, New York. AERO97ONE is expected to be a national network of radio stations.
