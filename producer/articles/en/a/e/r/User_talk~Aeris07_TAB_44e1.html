485 years ago a goddess named Lady Belledandy had two infant goddesses .They needed to develop their holy powers from year to year .But one day the gods and goddesses were in a war with a evil demon such as Naraku and other unknown evil demons .At that time Belledandy had fallen in love with a wolf demon named Lord Braska. Also known as the prince of darkness . Belledandy was also well known because she healed the sick and the wounded and was the daughter of the sun god .hint*Belledandy the light Braska the darkness and born the twilight princesses*Futher more in the story Belledandy tripped on one of the demons.(at that time she was holding her children) She had lost one of her children when a demon knocked it out of her hand .Belledandy tried to grasp her daughter again but lost sight of her .She soon came to realize that she was in the middle of a battlefield .When a ill queen was passing through to see if there were any survivors .She had notice Belledandy and ordered her guards to stop the carriage immediately and ran towards Belledandy .Belledandy looks up at the queen and healed her and handed her the child (Belledandy has given the queen her goddess soul) .The queen was about to thank Belledandy and ask her why she handed her the child .But Belledandy had left from the queen's sight .Before the queen left she said “May the light guide you on your tremendous journey mi lady.” Belledandy was walking through the battlefield and heard a distant cry .She knew it was her other child and quickly followed that small sound. When Belledandy had found her child it was bleeding a lot so she used what was left of her holy powers and healed her child .She felt weak when out of nowhere a demon with silverish long hair that flows straight down his back with golden-amber eyes .His armor has an armadillo rim-like piece around his left shoulder and going out onto his upper torso .Both his armor and his long flowing sash have a decided "Mainland". His Kimono is mostly white with a red honeycomb and flower crest at the collar, sleeves, and shoulder, most likely the crest of his family clan .He wears Sashinuki Hakama, (a type of traditional flowing Japanese "pants"), which are gathered at the ankles producing the "ballooning" effect. His footwear are flat pointed ankle-high boots, also reminiscent of Mainland influence .On his right arm, he has a fluffy cloth .On his arms are two maroon narrow slashes, also on his face is another pair of such slashes, and has a navy-blue crescent on his forehead easily shown by his parted bangs. He carries Belledandy off to his castle so that she could be healed and regain her strength.
