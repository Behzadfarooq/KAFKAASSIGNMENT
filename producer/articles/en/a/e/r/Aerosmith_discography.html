Main Article: Aerosmith

Since 1973, American hard rock band Aerosmith has released 14 studio albums. Additionally, the band has released 5 live albums and 8 official compilations (2 of which are box sets). This combines for a total of at least 27 official albums. According to the RIAA, of these 27 albums, 24 have gone gold, 18 platinum, and 12 have achieved multi-platinum status. Aerosmith have sold 66.5 million records in the United States and are estimated to have sold well over 140 million records around the world.

Aerosmith have made a number of singles over the years, some officially released, others album cuts. 21 of their songs have reached the Top 40 of the Billboard Hot 100 and the band has been a longtime stalwart of the Mainstream Rock Tracks chart, achieving nine #1 hits on that chart to date. Additionally, the band have had over 27 Top 10 singles worldwide.

The band has also released a number of video releases and appeared in a few movies and on several soundtracks.
