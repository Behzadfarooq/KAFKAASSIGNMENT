Aeron Valderrama is a graduating BS Chemistry major at the Institute of Chemistry, University of the Philippines in Diliman, Quezon City.

[edit] Name

Aeron Valderrama, is originally named Aeron Paul Valderrama. His second given name was accidentally dropped, when the midwife mistaken it for the Filipino word "po" (a particle showing respect to elders).

[edit] Education

  • Elementary School: Jacinto Zamora (Pandacan, Manila)
  • Secondary School: Manila Science High School (Ermita, Manila)
  • University: University of the Philippines (Diliman, Quezon City).

He also studies different languages, such as Irish and French.

[edit] Interests

  • Sports - an avid supporter of the UP Fighting Maroons, San Antonio Spurs, L.A. Lakers, Purefoods Chunkee Giants, and the Philippine National Team. He likewise admires the talents of Emanuel David Ginobili, Paul-Henri Mathieu, Mikhail Youzhny, Andy Roddick, Mario Ančić, Ian Thorpe, Park Tae Hwan, Cristiano Ronaldo, and Raphael Evan Grabador.
  • Travel - an avid watcher of the TV series, The Amazing Race and shows interest in foreign cultures through study of different languages
  • Music - loves the music of contemporary Irish artists like The Corrs, Westlife, and Boyzone. His taste varies from classical -- Josh Groban and Christian Bautista -- to rock -- Linkin Park and Silverchair.
  • Movies - likes Korean, Filipino movies along with some UK and US made ones.
  • Literature - loves anything "intellectually-stimulating" and "emotionally-moving": from the Harry Potter series to A Walk to Remember
