Aero (Melody Guthrie) is a fictional character appearing in the Marvel Comics universe who first appeared in New Mutants #42.

[edit] Fictional character biography

Melody is the younger sister of Cannonball, Husk and Icarus. Melody also has several other siblings including Jeb, who can project electrical blasts from his eyes and Elizabeth, who although has not yet come into her powers in the Mainstream Reality, was seen in The Age of Apocalyse as 'Amazon', who was able to alter her body mass and height. Aero had the ability to produce an aura which allowed her to fly, however she was depowered on M-Day. She was the only known Guthrie to have lost their powers in that manner.

Though she saw what was happening around her on M-Day, she believed that she was one of the few who retained their powers, and leapt off the roof in an attempt to prove to Emma Frost that she could still fly. Luckily she was saved by Beast.
