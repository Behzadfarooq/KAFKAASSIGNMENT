I am a Canadian in my mid-20s. I am currently enrolled in the Teacher Education program at the University of Ottawa in Ottawa, Ontario, Canada. I have a B.A. in Japanese Studies and Multimedia from McMaster University and I spent two years in Taiwan (Fengyuan and Taichung). My interests include absurdism, CAT III and Mr. Vampire flicks. [edit] Wikipedia

Like most others, I use Wikipedia as a launching point for topics I want to learn more about and once I get started I often get stuck for hours running from point-to-point. I have been using Wikipedia since 1999 acting primarily as a WikiGnome. I am slowly but surely working my way through how to do more than just minor edits here and there. My first ground-up article was for Bowie Tsang.
