[edit] Notable people who could have become notable at football (soccer) but who became notable elsewhere in life

  • Niels Bohr - Goalkeeper for Akademisk Boldklub in Denmark.
  • Harald Bohr - Played for Akademisk Boldklub and the Danish national football team, won silver at the 1908 Summer Olympics.
  • Albert Camus - Goalkeeper for University of Algiers, had to end his career after catching tuberculosis. Would likely have made the Algeria national football team.
  • Goran Ivanisevic - Played for Hajduk Split's youth academy, but decided to play tennis instead.
  • Pope John Paul II - Goalkeeper in his early days, quit football in order to focus on his religion.
  • Vladimir Nabokov - Goalkeeper during his time at Cambridge
  • Dmitri Shostakovich - Qualified football referee
