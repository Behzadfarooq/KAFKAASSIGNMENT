Yes, this is Aecis, a 23-year old Dutchman. The reason for my wikipedianship? I want to add my two cents' worth to wikipedia. I'm mostly working on stub sorting and other maintenance/janitorial work, but you can occasionally find me writing an article, when I can find the time. I joined wikipedia in March 2004, and became an administrator in January 2006. My main focus at this moment is User:Aecis/The Netherlands, a personal project to create articles and fill in the redlinks on the Netherlands on wikipedia. I'm also active for WikiProject Stub sorting. For an overview of what I've done on wikipedia, see User:Aecis/Hall of Fame.
