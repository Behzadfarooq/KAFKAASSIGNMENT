[edit] Where to list open proxies

Please list possible open proxies at WP:OP, not at WP:AN/I. This way, the open proxies will be tested by someone who knows how to check them, and then proven proxies will get listed at the main project over at Meta, where the bans will spread to all Wikimedia projects. Listing open proxies at WP:AN/I will leave the other WMF projects exposed. Jesse Viviano 03:49, 18 October 2006 (UTC)
