Aegir (software)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Aegir

Aegir was originally developed by Hong Kong Linux Center under the name of Nadmin Studio but was released to the free software community under the GNU General Public License in 2003.

Aegir has become a part of the Midgard CMS project. "Aegir" is also the sea god of Nordic mythology. The name was chosen because most initial developers of Aegir come from the Baltic region.
