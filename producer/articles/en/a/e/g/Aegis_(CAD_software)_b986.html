Aegis (CAD software)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Aegis Public Safety Suite is a type of Computer Aided Dispatch (CAD) software used by emergency services in the United States. It is published by New World Systems and contains modules for Computer Aided Dispatch, Police Records, and Jail Management.
