Aegon UK is a financial services organisation based in Edinburgh, Scotland, with assets under administration of £45.2 billion. It employs around 4,000 staff. Comprised of ten subsidiaries, the company is wholly owned by Aegon N.V. of the Netherlands. The AEGON Group is one of the world’s largest listed insurers and has assets under management of £245 billion.[1]
