Aegilops triuncialis (Barbed goatgrass, Barb goat grass, Jointed goat grass; syn. Aegilops squarrosa L., Triticum persicum (Boiss.) Aitch. & Hemsley, Triticum triunciale (L.) Raspail) is an invasive species in the Poaceae family.
