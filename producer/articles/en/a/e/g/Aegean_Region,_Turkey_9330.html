Aegean Region

Aegean Region (Ege Bölgesi), is one of the 7 census-defined regions of Turkey.
It is located in the west part of the country, bounded by Aegean sea (Ege Denizi) on the west, Marmara region on the north, Mediterranean region on the south & southwest and Central Anatolia region on the east.
