The Aegis School of Business offers business management courses in Mumbai, India.

With the support of Bharti (aka AirTel), Aegis founded the Aegis School of Telecommunication Management in 2002 to offer a Masters in Telecommunication Management. Aegis then created the Aegis School of Business, with offerings that include a one year, full-time MBA program, and a two year, part-time Executive MBA.

[edit] Claims

  • One of only five top one year MBA programs in India (per the Economic Times) along with IIM Ahmdebad, SP Jain and ISB.
  • MBA program was developed in consultation with India's top firms.
  • One year Masters Program in Telecommunication Management
  • International academic alliance with Schiller International University and Instituto de Empresa Business School.
  • Maintains fees at an affordable level of $7,000.
  • Starting salary for MBA/PGP 2006 batch is INR 850,000 (8.5 lacs)
  • Offers scholarships to potential Indian candidates.
  • Number of scholarships for potential western candidates.
  • Of the many uses of the term aegis, the shield of Zeus is the inspration for their use of the name.
