This category is for geographical articles related to the Aegean region of Turkey.

To add an article to this category, use {{AegeanTR-geo-stub}} instead of {{Turkey-geo-stub}}, {{geo-stub}}, or {{stub}}.

Provinces included in this region are:

  1. Afyonkarahisar Province (Afyon)
  2. Aydın Province
  3. Denizli Province
  4. Kütahya Province
  5. Manisa Province
  6. Muğla Province
  7. Uşak Province
  8. İzmir Province
