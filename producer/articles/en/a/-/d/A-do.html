Do Cheng Yi (Simplified Chinese: 杜成义; Traditional Chinese: 杜成義; pinyin: Dù Chéngyì), popularly known as A-Do (阿杜), is a singer from Singapore. Famed for his husky voice, he has been coined as the Rod Stewart of the Chinese music scene.

The ex-construction worker was spotted by accomplished Singaporean producer Billy Koh in his company's talent search. He has since been signed to Ocean Butterflies Music.

A-Do propelled to phenomenal fame across Asia with the release of his first album in 2002. He is loved for his modesty, shy demeanour and particularly loved by Chinese fans.
