A-1 Pictures Inc. (株式会社エー・ワン・ピクチャーズ, Kabushiki-gaisha Ē Wan Pikuchāzu?) is a Japanese animation studio, a subsidiary of Aniplex. The studio was established by Aniplex on May 9, 2005, to animate its anime series and productions.[3][4]
