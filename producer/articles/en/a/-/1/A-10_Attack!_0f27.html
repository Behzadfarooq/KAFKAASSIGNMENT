A-10 Attack! is a Flight simulation computer game for the Apple Macintosh computer. The game features an A-10 Thunderbolt II aircraft, and has a sequel named A-10 Cuba! This game boasted the most comprehensive and realistic flight model in its day. A-10 Attack! featured an impressive map overview where the player could command a small squadron of aircraft including F-16's, F-15's, and C-5 Galaxy transport aircraft. A-10 Cuba! was later produced for Windows based computers. The Mac OS versions were produced by Parsoft, and the PC version of A-10 Cuba! was a joint production of Activision and Parsoft.
