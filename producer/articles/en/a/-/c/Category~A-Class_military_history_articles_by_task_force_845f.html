This category classifies articles that have been rated as "A-Class" by the Military history WikiProject according to the related task forces. Articles are automatically placed in the appropriate sub-category when the corresponding rating is given and the appropriate parameter is added to the project banner; please see the assessment department and the project banner instructions for more information.
