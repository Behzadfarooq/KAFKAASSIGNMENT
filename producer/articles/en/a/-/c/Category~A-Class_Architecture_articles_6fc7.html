This category contains articles of specific interest to WikiProject Architecture which have been assigned an assessed value of A-Class for quality rating, following the overall WikiProject article assessment and rating scheme. The contents of this category form a subset of the equivalent Architecture rating and assessment category.

Articles are automatically placed in this category according to the particular class rating which has been assigned after an assessment by a Project team member. This should reflect the member's assessment of the article's quality at that point in time- if the quality of an article has subsequently changed, or the assessment could otherwise be re-evaluated, then the article's class rating may (at any time) be revised.

Revisions of assessment ratings are done by assigning an appropriate value via the class parameter in the WikiProject {{Architecture}} project banner. This banner is placed at the top of these articles' talk pages.

Please see the Project's article rating and assessment scheme for more information and the details and criteria for each rating value, or enquire at the Project's main discussion board for assistance


 This article covers subjects of relevance to Architecture. To participate, visit the Wikipedia:WikiProject Architecture for more information. The current monthly improvement drive is Castle.
