A-ca-oo-mah-ca-ye

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

A-ca-oo-mah-ca-ye, also known as Feathers or Old Swan (d. 1859 or 1860) was a chief of the Blackfoot First Nation with a reputation as a peacemaker. In July 1859, John Palliser and other members of the Palliser Expedition were his guests at his encampment on the Red Deer River in Alberta.
