Korea-related pages: The Ascent to Paradise edit
A-Class The article provides a well-written and complete description of the topic, as described in How to write a Great Article. It includes a full Korean name table, if appropriate, and an appropriate series of headings to break up the article. It should have sufficient external literature references, preferably from the academic literature. Most Good Articles are at this level.

Pages in this category are not sorted by the usual alphabetical manner. Instead, they are sorted by priority. If the priority of the page has been assigned, then it is sorted under the following numbers:

  • 1 — Top-importance Korea-related articles
  • 2 — High-importance Korea-related articles
  • 3 — Mid-importance Korea-related articles
  • 4 — Low-importance Korea-related articles
  • 5 — 5th-class importance Korea-related articles

Pages under each letter are sorted alphabetically. Pages where the priority have not been assigned are sorted in the usual alphabetical order, and come after the prioritized pages if they start with one of the numbers above.
