Description

An US Navy VA-75 (Attack Squadron Seven Five, the "Sunday Punchers") A-6E Intruder prepares to catch the hook as it lands onboard the USS ENTERPRISE (CVN-65). The USS ENTERPRISE is attached to the Sixth Fleet and conducting flight operations in the Adriatic Sea in support of Operation Joint Endeavor. Operation Joint Endeavor is a peacekeeping effort by a multinational Implementation Force (IFOR), comprised of NATO and non-NATO military forces, deployed to Bosnia in support of the Dayton Peace Accords.
