A9 or A-9 can refer to:

  • A9.com, a website and search engine by Amazon.com
  • A9 ISO 216, international paper size standard (37×52 mm)
  • Airzena Georgian Airlines IATA code
  • British NVC community A9, the Potamogeton natans community, one of the aquatic communities of the British National Vegetation Classification

A-9 also refers to various aerospace and defense hardware:

  • A-9, a ground attack aircraft based on the Lockheed YP-24 fighter prototype
  • A9 Cruiser Mk I, a British tank
  • A-9 Quail, an agricultural aircraft design by CallAir
  • A9 rocket, a German rocket design in World War II
  • A-9 Vigilance, a fictional starfighter from the Star Wars universe
  • HMS A9, an A-class submarine of the Royal Navy
  • Grumman OA-9 "Goose" amphibious aircraft in USAAC service (redesignation of the G-21)
  • Northrop YA-9, a prototype attack aircraft developed for the United States Air Force

  • Autopista A9, is a Spanish motorway.
