The Motorway A9 (Croatian: Autocesta A9) is a motorway in western Croatia, in the Istria region. It is part of the Istrian Y. The sections south of Kanfanar are built as motorway, sections north of it are built as expressway. The entire motorway/expressway is toll-free, with the exception of bridge over the Mirna River.
