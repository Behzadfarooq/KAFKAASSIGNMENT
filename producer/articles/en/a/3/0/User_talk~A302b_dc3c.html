Well, this is my first wikipedia page, and I am not sure what all of this means. I came across the Get Medieval webcomic, and really liked it, so I looked on Wikipedia and found nothing. I am not sure what "notable" means, but there seems to be quite a community that regularly reads the comic, and it has over 700 comics, going regularly and daily since 2004.
