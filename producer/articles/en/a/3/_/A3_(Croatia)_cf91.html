The Motorway A3 (Croatian: Autocesta A3) is a motorway in northern Croatia. It is part of the Highway "Brotherhood and Unity". The first section of the motorway was open in 1979, near Zagreb, and the last section between Županja and the Serbian border was open in 2006, what means, that the Croatian part of the highway is completed. The entire motorway, except the Zagreb bypass, is a toll road.
