A320 Airbus is a flight simulator computer game, in which the player pilots an Airbus A320. It was released in 1991 by Thalion Software. The game was developed over three years with cooperation with the Lufthansa and Airbus airline companies. The game is notable for its attention to detail, since it came with comprehensive approach charts for over 80 airports. The game was written by Rainer Bopf and converted to the Atari ST by Christian Jungen.
