A35 or A-35 is a three-letter acronym that may refer to:

  • A35 road, a major road in England.
  • The A-35 anti-ballistic missile system deployed around Moscow, currently upgraded to the A-135 system.
  • One of the Encyclopaedia of Chess Openings codes for the English Opening in chess.
  • A-35, an improved version of the Vultee A-31 Vengeance aircraft.
  • Aero A.35, a Czech airliner of the 1930s.
  • Junkers A 35, a German aircraft of the 1920s.
