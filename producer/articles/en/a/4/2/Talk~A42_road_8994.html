[edit] A42 Terminus

The A42 terminates at a roundabout with the A453. It is separated from the A50 by the A453 to J24 of the M1. The terminus is joined by the northbound exit of the M1 at J23A.

[edit] Corrections to roundabout at junction 23a

I drive on this junction every day and have done for the past five years. It has FIVE roads coming off it. Working clockwise in order they are: a non-numbered slip road to Donington Park services, the A453 south to the airport, the A453 north to Junction 24 of the M1, an exit offering southbound access ONLY to the A42 and M1, and a no entry terminus coming from the A42 and M1 north. Junction 24 at the end of the A453 offers south and north access to the M1 and not just south as a previous edit of this article had indicated. Robdav69 July 30 2006.

The point of my mentioning the J24 southbound access to the M1 is that you cannot do this from 23a. I didn't feel it was particularly relevant to list the other routes from the A453/M1 J24 roundabout (M1 north, A50, A453 and A6) since they are neither part of, nor relevant to the A42. jimfbleak 15:03, 30 July 2006 (UTC)
