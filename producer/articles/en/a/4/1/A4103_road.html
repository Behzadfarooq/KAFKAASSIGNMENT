The A4103

The A4103 is a major road in England, which runs west-south-west through rural surroundings from Worcester to just east of Hereford where it joins the A465. The A4103 crosses the A417 at Newtown a few miles east of Hereford, and is susceptible to flooding at Bransford, where it crosses the River Teme.
