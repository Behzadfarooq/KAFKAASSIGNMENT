Length: 97 km (60 mi)
From: Zagreb, A3 motorway
To: Hungarian border, M7 motorway
Countries: Croatia
Regions: Zagreb, Zagreb County, Varaždin County, Međimurje County
Major cities: Zagreb, Varaždin, Čakovec


The Motorway A4 (Croatian: Autocesta A4) is a motorway in northern Croatia northeast from Zagreb. The motorway is a toll road.
