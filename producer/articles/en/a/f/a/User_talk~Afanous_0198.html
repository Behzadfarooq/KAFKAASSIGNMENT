[edit] Coptic saints

The articles' naming conventions do not endorse titles with regards to saints (or any other title, actually). For example, the article about Saint Mina should be titled "Mina". A redirection will be aceptable, though. The same should go for popes although this isn't currently the case with Roman Catholic Popes! --Alif 16:00, 31 Jan 2005 (UTC)

[edit] Capitalising second words in titles

It is not in accordance with the naming conventions to capitalise second words in articles' titles, for example "Coptic language" and "Coptic Alphabet". It sure does not imply any greater emphasis or importance. AFanous, take your time browsing and reading in the encylopedia, as well as the editing guides. --Alif 16:12, 31 Jan 2005 (UTC)
