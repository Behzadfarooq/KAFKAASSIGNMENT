Born in 1988,His full name is Afandi Oh Bin Omar oh.He studied in the MBF kindergarden during his early days before entering Sekolah Kebangsaan Bukit Bandaraya(S.K.B.B) as his first primary school.He left the school when he was std 2 and joined Sekolah Sri Cempaka when he was std 3.Afandi oh has since then finish his entire primary and secondary life in Sekolah Sri Cempaka.Afandi grew up becoming a fan of the rugby and football sports.Afandi oh played rugby for the KL barbarians club for quite some time before quitting to concentrate on his studies.Afandi oh is also a collector of foreign currency and old coins,he is known to take this hobby very seriously.He also loves to play sports during his free time

Afandi then joined the HELP University college to pursue his education in the higher level.He has joined the Arts Foundation in which he plans to take the Entrepreneurship degree after the pre-university programme finishes.Afandi oh has always shown an interest in business ever since at the of 9

Afandi being the Inky leader has also stated that he would love to open Inky industries when he finishes his education.He has allocated a lot in an industrial area to do so for his future business.

In an interview,it was reported that Afandi oh once said "Building up the Inky industries with the Inky group is a small boy's dream come true.I have my visions set on this,I can tell you that Inshaallah(If god allows) the Inky group will be doing many kinds of businesses including building malls and of course,building our main dream.The Menara Inky and the Inky Islands."

Afandi oh is a man that puts religion as a major part of his life.His grandfather has influence him about the importance of religion ever since he was young.Afandi oh respects him and became the only grandson to follow him every week for Friday prayers.Afandi oh has also followed his grandfather's footsteps on charity.

"I think that believing and following a religion is very important.It brings peace into this world and it helps us alot in life,To whom will we hope for when everything is taken away from us?We must also learn to give to charity if we have enough.If god gives us extra we should be thankful and help the other poor people so everyone wins"Quoted Afandi oh in an interview.

Afandi oh doesnt belive in relying on people alot.He has stated that everybody should be independent on their own.

Afandi oh likes to blog during his spare time,His blog address is www.afandioh.blogspot.com
