Afghanada is a Canadian radio drama series, currently airing on CBC Radio One.

Written by Andrew Moodie, Greg Nelson, Adam Pettle and Jason Sherman, Afghanada is a series dramatizing the experiences of Canadian military serving in Afghanistan. Initially, twelve episdodes were ordered, with a scheduled run from November 3, 2006 until January 18, 2007. CBC has since ordered an additional ten episodes, which are scheduled to air from January 26 through March 30, 2007.[1]

[edit] Cast

The cast includes Billy MacLellan as Lucas, Jenny Young as Patricia "Coach" Kinsella, Paul Fauteux as Dean Donaldson, Billy Abrams as Stuart Darcy, and Sam Kalilieh. These actors portray the young Canadian soldiers at the core of the story.

[edit] Merchandising

The series to date is being collected and sold in CD format by the CBC's merchandising arm, CBCShop/boutique SRC.
