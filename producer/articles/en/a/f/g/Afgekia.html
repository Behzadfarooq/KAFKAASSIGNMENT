Afgekia is a small genus of large perennial climbing shrubs native to Asia, reminiscent of the related genus Wisteria. The genus was named from the initials of Arthur Francis George Kerr (1877â€“1942), an Irish-born plant collector who worked in then-Siam in the early twentieth century.
