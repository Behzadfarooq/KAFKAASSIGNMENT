Afghan Christians are a small group of Christians with Afghan background. Until recently they did not receive much international attention until the arrest of Abdul Rahman for apostasy brought to light this small minority of the Afghanistan population. The majority of Afghan Christians reside in foreign countries, mainly in United States, where they converted to Christianity. However, there is no report of Christian converted Afghans in Afghanistan.

They are an organized community of Afghan Christians who have multiple Radio programs such as Radio Afghan and a weekly television program, "Voice of Christ" (آواز مسيح) and many other websites in English and Dari languages. Hussain Andaryas, an Afghan Christian, was the first to launch a website in the Dari language in 1996.
