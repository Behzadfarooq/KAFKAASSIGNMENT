May I suggest you put something at your userpage so that it is not redlinked. For istance, many users choose to put a WP:Babel template. You could put something like this for example: {{Babel-2|en-3|ru}} or at least "user:Afganets" if you haven't decided yet what to put there. If you prefer the red link, it is just fine of course.
