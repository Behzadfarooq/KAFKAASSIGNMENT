Rulers of Afghanistan

Monarchs:

  • Ahmed Shah Abdali, Padshah of Afghanistan 1747-1772
  • Timur Shah, Shah of Afghanistan 1772-1793
  • Zaman Shah, Shah of Afghanistan 1793-1801
  • Padshah Dur-i-Durran Mahmud Shah, Shah of Afghanistan 1801-1803,1809-1818
  • Shuja ul-Mulk Muhammad Shah, Shah of Afghanistan 1803-1809,1839-1842
  • Sultan Ali Shah, Shah of Afghanistan 1818-1819
  • Ayub Shah, Shah of Afghanistan 1819-1823
  • Abdul Wakil Panah Khan ,Emir of northern Afghanistan 1822-1839
  • Akbar Panah Khan , Emir of northern Afghanistan 1839-1842

1823 - 1835 Interregnum, the country divided between various Barakzai Sardars

  • Dost Mahommed Khan, Amir of Afghanistan 1835-1839,1842-1863.
  • Sher Ali Khan, Amir of Afghanistan 1863-1866,1868-1879.
  • Muhammad Afzal Khan, Amir of Afghanistan 1866-1867.
  • Muhammad Azam Khan II, Amir of Afghanistan 1867-1868
  • Muhammad Yaqub Khan, Amir of Afghanistan 1879

1879-1880 interregnum

  • Abdur Rahman Khan, Amir of the God granted Kingdom of Afghanistan 1880-1901
  • Habibullah Khan, Amir and King of Afghanistan 1901-1919
  • Nasrullah Khan, Amir of Afghanistan 1919
  • Amanullah Khan, King of Afghanistan 1919-1929
  • Inayatullah Khan, King of Afghanistan 1929
  • Habibullah Kalakani, King of Afghanistan 1929
  • Muhammad Nadir Shah, King of Afghanistan 1929-1933
  • Muhamad Zahir Shah, King of Afghanistan 1933-1973

Presidents:

  • Sardar Mohammad Daud Khan, President 1973-1978

chairman of the Revolutionary Council of the Armed Forces:

  • General Abdul Qadir 27th-30th April 1978

Presidents of the Republic/Chairmen of the Revolutionary Council:

  • Nur Mohammad Taraki 1978-1979
  • Hafizullah Amin 1979
  • Babrak Karmal 1979-1986
  • Mohammad Chamkani 1986-1987
  • Mohammad Najibullah 1987

Presidents of the Republic:

  • Mohammad Najibullah 1987-1992
  • Abdul Rahim Hatef 1992

Chairman of the Jihad Council (interim):

  • Sibghatullah Mojaddedi 1992

President of the Islamic State:

  • Burhanuddin Rabbani 1992-1996

Heads of the Taliban

  • Mullah Mohammed Omar (spiritual and paramount leader) 1996-2001
  • Mullah Mohammad Rabbani (Head of Taliban Supreme Council) 1996-2001

President of the Islamic State:

  • Burhanuddin Rabbani (again) 2001

Chairman of the Interim Administration:

  • Hamid Karzai 2001-2002

Head of State for the Transitional Administration:

  • Hamid Karzai 2002-2004

President of the Islamic Republic of Afghanistan

  • Hamid Karzai 2004-
