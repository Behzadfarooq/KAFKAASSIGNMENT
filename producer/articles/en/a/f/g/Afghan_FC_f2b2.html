Afghan FC are a football (soccer) club playing in the Pakistan Premier League. The club is based in Chaman, Balochistan and was one of the only two non departmental teams to have competed in this years PPL.

Afghan FC barely avoided relegation this season finishing in 9th spot 2 points above Wohaib FC who edged out HBL in the drop zone to survive as well.

The club has a small but loyal fan base with locals turning up in huge numbers to support their team at their home matches held in a makeshift pitch at the Chaman High School Ground.

Without play maker Mohammad Essa, who left for WAPDA at the end of last season, they struggled against the much stronger opposition in the league this year.
