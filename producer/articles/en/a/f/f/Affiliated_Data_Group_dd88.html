Affiliated Data Group is a California based company that specializes in IT BPO services. Founded in 2004 ADG has gained significant market share focusing on delivering value added software development services to small to medium size US companies. This market sector had been previously overlook by the majority of the outsourcing community but by pioneering an innovative business model and leveraging a blend of on and off shore resources ADG was able to prove that if targeted correctly this market share was profitable. Successfully identifying and capturing this niche allowed ADG to gain the foothold that it need to emerged as one of the major players in the high competitive outsourcing game. In 2006 it was rumored that there New Delhi data center was being bought out by a publicly traded UK based company but those allegations where later proven to be false.


[edit] Origins Of The Name

The name "Affiliated Data Group" arrived because of a series of strategic alliances that were formed between small to medium size web development companies in the US, UK, Canada and itself. Unlike most outsourcing companies who where more interested in just filling desks with qualified candidates, ADG took a more proactive approach having the architecture and management of there clients projects done in house by US programmers and account managers but still leveraging their international labor pool to save costs. This model ensured companies working with ADG did not get there project scope lost in translation and that they always had easy access to there account manager, which were two major problem many companies faced when dealing with BPO companies before the foundation of ADG. This partnership environment proved to be the cohesive gel small businesses where looking for to make the successfully transition to an oversea work force. As time went on the name was shortened to the acronym "ADG".
