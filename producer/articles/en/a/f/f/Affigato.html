Affigato

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

You have new messages (last change).

An affigato is a coffee-based beverage or dessert. It usually consists of vanilla ice cream topped with a shot of hot espresso. "Affigato style", which refers to the act of topping a drink or dessert with espresso, may also incorporate caramel sauce or chocolate sauce.

A "cafe affigato" is sometimes referred to as a "Cold Shower".[1]

An "affigato di gelato" is usually vanilla bean gelato topped with espresso.
