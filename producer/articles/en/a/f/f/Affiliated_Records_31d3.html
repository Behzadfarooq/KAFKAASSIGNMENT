Affiliated Records is a record label that was founded in 1997 by Richard Hentosh. Known then as SHORTY OUNCE, Hentosh tried to get a major record deal but with no prior sales or connections he was turned down numerous times. Fed up with all the rejection he picked up a book and started reading. From one book to another in one month Rich had read 10 different books on the music industry and it gave him an idea. "I was learning all this stuff within these different books it almost seemed too easy for someone like me to do this without a major record company".

So Rich called up a few partners and Affiliated Records was born. But please don't think this was an overnight success because it wasn't. From 1998 until 2001 Rich moved and negotiated his way into many of the biggest offices in the game and finally scored nationwide distribution for his 1st release "Living My Life". After a sour deal with 101 Distribution Rich went back to the lab and started building an impressive catalog with 3 of his artists to present to the next distributor that would be interested in his label.

Fortunately a few were biting it was only a matter of time. Now in 2007 with 4 releases under the company's belt which have sold over 100,000 units and 3 more prepared for retail Rich has no choice but to be on top.

[edit] Current Roster

  • Shortyo - Founder of Affiliated Records and member of the hip-hop era, Shortyo released his debut album in 2002 titled "Living My Life" featuring Daz, Snoop Dogg, and more. His new album Currently untitled is in the works set for rrelease 2007-2008.
  • Shoshyn - R&B Sensation Tony "Shoshyn" Gilmore released his long awaited debut album "Sincerely Yours" in 2006 leading Affiliated Records into indie stardom.
  • Cyssero - The youngest member of the Affiliated camp born in Philadelphia, has 2 retail albums under his belt, Phillys Bad Guy (Featuring Kurupt, and The Game and Protege Of The Game (Featuring Akon and The Game.
