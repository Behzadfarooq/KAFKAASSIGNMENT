Affiliate Summit

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Company logo

The Affiliate Summit conference was founded by Affiliate marketing industry veterans Shawn Collins and Missy Ward and serves the affiliate marketing professional community. The mission of Affiliate Summit is to be the leading global source of learning and networking opportunities surrounding the affiliate marketing industry.

[edit] History of Affiliate Summit

Affiliate Summit started in November 2003 as a small, one day seminar at Baruch College in New York City. The conference has grown significantly over the first six events.

Affiliate Summit 2003 had 200 registered attendees and Affiliate Summit 2007 West has over 1,600 registered to attend.

Chronology of Affiliate Summit Events:

• November 3, 2003 (New York, NY): Affiliate Summit 2003
• June 24-28, 2004 (Cruise ship from New York, NY to Halifax, Nova Scotia): Affiliate Summit 2004
• June 13-14, 2005 (Las Vegas, NV): Affiliate Summit 2005
• January 8-10, 2006 (Las Vegas, NV): Affiliate Summit 2006 West
• July 9-11, 2006 (Orlando, FL): Affiliate Summit 2006 East
• January 21-23, 2007 (Las Vegas, NV): Affiliate Summit 2007 West
• July 8-10, 2007 (Miami, FL): Affiliate Summit 2007 East

[edit] Future of Affiliate Summit

Affiliate Summit currently takes place twice yearly in the United States. There are tentative plans to expand the conference to London in the second half of 2007.
