Affinia Hotels

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The "top hat" is the official logo for Affinia hotels.

Affinia Hospitality is a privately owned upscale hotel chain in the New York City and Chicago area. The hotel chain has nine hotels in Manhattan and one in Chicago. Four of the Manhattan hotels are under the Affinia name, another Four have private names but are still operated by Affinia Hotels. One of the hotels is named 'The Benjamin hotel' and was the first hotel in the Chain. It is named after Benjamin Denihan who founded the Affinia Hospitality group. Affinia Hotels are in partnership with Thistle Hotels, First Hotels, and Rotana Hotels.

[edit] History

  • The first Affinia Hotel was established in 1962 by founder Benjamin J. “Bud” Denihan.
  • The Affinia Dumont located in Manhattan, became the first hotel in North America to offer fitness facilities in the suites themselves.
