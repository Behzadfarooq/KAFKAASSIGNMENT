[edit] Album details

Affirmation is title of the fourth album from UK Soul Queen Beverley Knight, released by Parlophone records.

The album was released on 28 June 2004, to much critical acclaim and is her most personal album to date. The album features three hit singles, "Come As You Are", "Not Too Late For Love" and "Keep This Fire Burning".

Affirmation was certified Silver by the BPI on Friday 2 July 2004, less than a week after the album was released [1]. By December 2004, the album had received a Gold certification [2].

"Come As You Are"
Released 20 June 2004
Recorded Town House and Orgasmatron Studio, London
Writer(s) Beverley Knight and Guy Chambers
Producer(s) Guy Chambers and Richard Flack
Come As You Are was the first single from UK soul queen Beverley Knight's fourth album, Affirmation. The single became her highest charting single to date, becoming her second UK top 10 hit and remained in the UK Top 75 for 10 weeks.

"Not Too Late For Love"
Released 27 September 2004
Recorded Area 21, London
Writer(s) Beverley Knight and Chris Braide
Producer(s) Chris Braide
Not Too Late For Love was the second single from the Affirmation album from Beverley Knight. The single was not nearly as successful as Come As You Are. Despite peaking at #11 on the UK Airplay chart, it only managed to peak at #31 in the UK singles chart, becoming her smallest hit since Sista Sista in 1999.

"Keep This Fire Burning"
Released 14 March 2005
Recorded Area 21, London
Writer(s) Remee, Robyn, Lindastrom and Ekhe
Producer(s) GHOST
Keep This Fire Burning was the third and final single from Beverley Knight's, Affirmation album. The single was a cover of Robyn's European hit single from her album "Don't Stop The Music". Robyn's version of Keep This Fire Burning was not, however, released in the UK The single was a massive airplay hit, peaking inside the UK airplay top 5. Keep This Fire Burning became Beverley's 6th top 20 and 12th top 40 solo single in the U.K, managing to chart at a very respectable #16.
