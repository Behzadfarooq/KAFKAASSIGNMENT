Affine Hecke algebra

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

You have new messages (last change).

An affine Hecke algebra is the Hecke algebra of an affine Weyl group.

Affine Hecke algebras can be used to prove Macdonald's constant term conjecture for Macdonald polynomials.
