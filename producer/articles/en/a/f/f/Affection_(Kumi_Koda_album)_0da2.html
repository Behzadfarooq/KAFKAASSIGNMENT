affection was the first studio album released by Japanese Pop singer Kumi Koda. This first album of Kumi contains single like "Take Back" and "Trust Your Love", songs that were even promoted in the U.S. It failed to achieve much success in Japan, despite peaking at #12 on the Oricon album chart.
