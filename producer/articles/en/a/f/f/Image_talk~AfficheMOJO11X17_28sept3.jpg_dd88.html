[edit] THE STUFF

Mojo is a RTD alternative alcoholic beverage with guarana. At 7% ABV + Guarana, Mojo is not your mother's cooler.




[edit] THE FLAVOR

This energy alcoholic beverage comes in 3 flavours: Liquid (tropical fruits), Vibe (green apple) and Rhythm (strawberry kiwi).




[edit] THE ENERGY

Guarana is a nut-like seed that grows in the Amazon Forest in Brazil. Guarana has been used for centuries by the indigenous people of this area as natural energy supplement. Its main ingredient contains 2.5 more caffeine than the coffee bean itself!



[edit] THE TEMPERATURE

2 words. Ice cold. Enuff'said!




[edit] THE PACKAGE

Mojo comes in 330ml plastic bottles that are easy to hold with no breakage when you're out tearing it up on the dance floor. You can reseal the bottle for minimum spillage.

It can be bought individually or in 4-packs...it's up to you.




[edit] THE PLACE

Mojo is now available only in Quebec and Alberta. In Quebec, you can find it in c-stores (Couche-Tard and independent ones), gas stations and grocery stores across the province. As for Alberta, it is available in liquor stores. It will be soon available in other canadian provinces and in the North-East states. Stay tune!



[edit] THE STORY

Mojo was developed in 2005 by 2 college buddies in Montreal. The brand owner is a company called Blue Spike Beverages Inc.. [1]

Get your mojo going!
