Af Upplendinga konunum or Of the Kings of the Uplands, found in the Hauksbók, is a short presentation of the House of Yngling from Olof Trätälja to Ragnvald the Mountain-High. It tells the same story as the last part of the Ynglinga saga but in summarized form.
