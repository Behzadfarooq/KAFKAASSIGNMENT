African Championships in Athletics

The African Championships in Athletics is a continental athletics event organized by the Confederation of African Athletics. It is generally organised biennually, every other event is held same year as the Summer Olympics.

The 2008 competition is set to be held in Ethiopia.

[edit] Summary of Championships

  • 1979 African Championships in Athletics - Dakar (  Senegal)
  • 1982 African Championships in Athletics - Cairo (  Egypt)
  • 1984 African Championships in Athletics - Rabat (  Morocco)
  • 1985 African Championships in Athletics - Cairo (  Egypt)
  • 1988 African Championships in Athletics - Annaba (  Algeria)
  • 1989 African Championships in Athletics - Lagos (  Nigeria)
  • 1990 African Championships in Athletics - Cairo (  Egypt)
  • 1992 African Championships in Athletics - Belle Vue Mauricia (  Mauritius)
  • 1993 African Championships in Athletics - Durban (  South Africa)
  • 1996 African Championships in Athletics - Yaoundé (  Cameroon)
  • 1998 African Championships in Athletics - Dakar (  Senegal)
  • 2000 African Championships in Athletics - Algiers (  Algeria)
  • 2002 African Championships in Athletics - Tunis / Radès (  Tunisia)
  • 2004 African Championships in Athletics - Brazzaville (  Republic of the Congo)
  • 2006 African Championships in Athletics - Bambous (  Mauritius)
  • 2008 African Championships in Athletics - Addis Ababa (  Ethiopia)
