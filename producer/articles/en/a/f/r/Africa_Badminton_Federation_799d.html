Africa Badminton Federation

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Africa Badminton Federation (ABF) is the governing body of badminton in Africa. It is one of the 5 continental bodies under the flag of the Badminton World Federation (BWF). It now has 31 member countries.[1]
