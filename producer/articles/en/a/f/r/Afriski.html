Afri-Ski is currently the only skiing resort in Lesotho , on the "Roof of Africa" (3322 m above sea-level) and in the Heart of Lesotho Maluti mountains , at 28.8200° S 28.7300° E. It's 4½ hours' drive from Johannesburg South Africa via the steep tarred Moteng pass and the Mahlasela pass. The resort is one of only two skiing resorts in Southern Africa.
