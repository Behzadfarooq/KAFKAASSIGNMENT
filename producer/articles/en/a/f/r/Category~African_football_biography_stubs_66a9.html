This category is for biographical stub articles relating to African football (soccer). This covers all countries in the CAF region, and includes football players, managers, coaches, etc. You can help Wikipedia by expanding them.

To add an article to this category, use {{Africa-footy-bio-stub}} instead of {{footy-bio-stub}}. Due to the large number of stubs in this category, some countries have their own stub types; in these cases, use the stub tags listed below:

  • For people from Côte d'Ivoire, use {{Coted'Ivoire-footy-bio-stub}}
  • For people from Democratic Republic of Congo, use {{DRCongo-footy-bio-stub}}
  • For Egyptian people, use {{Egypt-footy-bio-stub}}
  • For Ghanaian people, use {{Ghana-footy-bio-stub}}
  • For Moroccan people, use {{Morocco-footy-bio-stub}}

  • For South African people, use {{SouthAfrica-footy-bio-stub}}
