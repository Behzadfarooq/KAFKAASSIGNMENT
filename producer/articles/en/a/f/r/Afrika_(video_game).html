Afrika is the working title of a PlayStation 3 game first announced briefly in a promotional video during the Sony press conference at E3 in 2006. Since then little information has leaked about the game, but because of its seemingly unique design it has generated a cult following. The game is being developed by Rhino Studios and published by Sony Computer Entertainment, it is still unknown what the game mechanics or even what the objectives of the game will be.
