Afro-Seminole Creole

Afro-Seminole Creole is an English-based creole spoken in Brackettville, Texas, in the United States and in Nacimiento de los Negros, Coahuila, in Mexico. The number of speakers of Afro-Seminole Creole is around 200. Afro-Seminole Creole has no official status. It is related to the Gullah language, a creole spoken on the Sea Islands off the coast of the U.S. states of Georgia and South Carolina.
