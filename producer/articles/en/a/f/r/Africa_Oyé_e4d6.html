Africa Oyé logo, as seen on the stage during Oyé 2006

Africa Oyé is an annual music festival in Liverpool, England. It is held in Sefton Park and showcases up-and-coming African and Caribbean artists, as well as presenting already famous artists, such as Bonga and Luciano.
