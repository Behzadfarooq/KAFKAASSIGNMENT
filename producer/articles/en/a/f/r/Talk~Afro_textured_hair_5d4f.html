[edit] Semi-nappyy?

Would mixed-race persons get genetically "half-nappy" hair (between nappy/straight) or would the result be one or the other? 惑乱 分からん 17:35, 9 November 2006 (UTC)

It would be wavy, which is a different texture altogether. It can vary from being very curly, but distinct from nappy, to being almost straight, but in most cases it will be jet black (though it can sometimes be other colors). Think of the hair of Arabs, for instance, on one extreme, and Horn of Africans as the other for hair types between Straight and Nappy. — ዮም | (Yom) | Talk • contribs • Ethiopia 11:55, 19 January 2007 (UTC)
