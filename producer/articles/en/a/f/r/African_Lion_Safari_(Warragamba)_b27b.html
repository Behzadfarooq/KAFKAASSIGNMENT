African Lion Safari (Warragamba)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The African Lion Safari operated near Warragamba on the outskirts of Sydney.

Originally opened by Stafford Bullen in 1968, the park attracted hundreds of thousands of visitors each year. With Sydney's increased urban sprawl bringing the suburbs ever closer to the park, it eventually closed in 1991.
