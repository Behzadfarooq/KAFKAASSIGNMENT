This category is for stub articles relating to Geography of Africa. You can help Wikipedia by expanding them.

To add an article to this category, use {{Africa-geo-stub}} instead of {{stub}}.

If possible, please assign stubs to one of the five regional African geography stub categories (for Central, East, North, Southern, and West Africa) using the respective stub templates {{AfricaC-geo-stub}}, {{AfricaE-geo-stub}}, {{AfricaN-geo-stub}}, {{AfricaS-geo-stub}}, and {{AfricaW-geo-stub}}.
