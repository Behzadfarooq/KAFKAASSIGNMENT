Africa-Israel Investments Ltd. (Tel-Aviv Stock Exchange (TASE):AFIL) is an international holding and investment company that has an extensive range of investments in Israel and abroad.

[edit] Divisions

  • Residential and Land Development in Israel, the famous "Savyonim" brand neighborhoods with thousands of residential units around Israel.
  • Income-Yielding Properties
  • Shopping Malls
  • Real Estate Investments and property developers in North America, Europe.
  • Contracting and Constructing - The company operates in this field through its subsidiary Danya Cebus Ltd who are listed on the TASE:DNYA.
  • Infrastructure and B.O.T. Projects
  • Tourism and Leisure - the company's activities in tourism and leisure are carried out through its subsidiary Africa Israel Hotels Ltd. who are listed on the TASE:AFHO.
  • Industry, Telecommunications and Media - holds shares in industrial companies and other enterprises in a variety of fields including 34% of Packer Plada (steel industry) and 69% of Negev Ceramics (ceramic industry) both of which are listed on the TASE. The company also holds 51% in Anglo-Saxon Realty, 20% of Mapal Communications Ltd. and Tadiran Telecom.
  • Energy
  • Fashion and Beach Wear. Owner of the Gottex brand.
