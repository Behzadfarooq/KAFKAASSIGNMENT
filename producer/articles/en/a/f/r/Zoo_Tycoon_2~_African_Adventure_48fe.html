Zoo Tycoon 2: African Adventure is the second Zoo Tycoon 2 expansion pack (The others are Zoo Tycoon 2: Endangered Species, Zoo Tycoon 2: Marine Mania and the upcoming Zoo Tycoon 2: Extinct Animals.). It gives the player 20 new animals such as the meerkat and the pygmy hippo. It also adds several maps based on real African Locations, such as The Sahara Desert, Mount Kilimanjaro, the Drakensberg, and the Serengeti Plains. Zoo Tycoon 2: African Adventure also gives the player the Jeep Liberty (others available in Zoo Tycoon 2: Endangered Species) and a new campaign. Another new feature is more realistic herding and predator/prey behaviors.

[edit] Campaign

Spoiler warning: Plot and/or ending details follow.

The first scenario in the campaign is called "Mob of Meerkats". The player must care for a group of meerkats. If they meet the need of your first set of meerkats they are given more. The meerkats can dig under the tundra fence, so care must be taken. The second is "The Secretary Bird". The player must take pictures of a secretary bird doing various activities, and then of a baby secretary bird (The player may only have 1 secretary bird at the start). In the third scenario the player must make a zoo out of 14 African animals but only a 4 can be savannah. Animals from other continents can be included. $10,000 are received if the player includes two animals from the scrub biome and $20,000 after the fourth species of African animal is added and $40,000 after the eighth species of African animal is added. The player unlocks the Desert-Theme for his/her buildings and objects if he/she completes the campaign.

Spoilers end here.

[edit] Trivia

  • The new animal behavior includes hunting as a pack and moving as part of a herd.
  • Blue Fang Games were working on the expansion from early 2005.
  • The Masai giraffe can breed with the reticulated giraffe from the original Zoo Tycoon 2.
  • The average African Adventure animal has around 150 different tasks it can do.
  • African Adventure is the first expansion to have live food.
  • The ratel was the last animal announced.
