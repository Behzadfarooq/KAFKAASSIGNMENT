[edit] Bugbear

My favourite Bugbear is actually about the special German letter ß. It's a character that - following a long vowel - substitutes what would otherwise be spelled as 'ss'. As in 'Fußball' (football). However, there is no capital version of that character, which means that the ß needs to be replaced by 'SS' when writing a word in all capital letters. So 'Fußball' becomes 'FUSSBALL', not 'FUßBALL'. Mind you, I'm not just being pedantic about how to and how not to spell stuff. It's just that DREHVERSCHLUß, FUßBALL etc. look awful.
