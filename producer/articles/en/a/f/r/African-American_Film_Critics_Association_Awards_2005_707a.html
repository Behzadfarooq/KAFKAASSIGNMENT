African-American Film Critics Association Awards 2005

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

3rd African-American Film Critics Association Awards

December 30, 2005

Best Picture
Crash

The 3rd African-American Film Critics Association Awards, honoring the best in filmmaking of 2005, were given on 30 December 2005.

[edit] Top 10 Films

  1. Crash
  3. The Constant Gardener
  4. Good Night, and Good Luck.
  5. Brokeback Mountain
  6. Syriana
  7. Walk the Line
  8. Hustle & Flow
  9. Capote
  10. Batman Begins
  11. North Country

[edit] Winners

  • Achievement Honor:
    • John Singleton; producer of Hustle & Flow
  • Best Actor:
    • Terrence Howard - Hustle & Flow
  • Best Actress:
    • Felicity Huffman - Transamerica
