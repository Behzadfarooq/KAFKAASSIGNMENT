[edit] Geographical

There are also African geography-related stub messages. If you include the {{Africa-geo-stub}} tag in the body of an article the article becomes listed in Category:Africa geography stubs

Each of the five main geographical regions of Africa (West, North, East, Central, and Southern) also has a separate geography stub associated with it. The templates being, respectively,

  • {{AfricaW-geo-stub}}
  • {{AfricaN-geo-stub}}
  • {{AfricaE-geo-stub}}
  • {{AfricaC-geo-stub}}
  • {{AfricaS-geo-stub}}

Each of these assigns articles to a separate subcategory of Category:Africa geography stubs.
