Afro-Arabs are people who were born in Arab countries (such as those found in the Middle East or North Africa), and who have sub-Saharan African ancestry. They also include those who were born in nations outside of the Arab world, but who can ethnically identify themselves Arabs, sub-Sarahans, and racially be classified as Black.
