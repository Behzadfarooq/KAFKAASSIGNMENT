[edit] States-general at Lamego

This sentence is innacurate: "Next, he assembled the first assembly of the states-general at Lamego, where he was given the crown from the bishop of Bragança, to confirm the independence."

It is widely accepted that no States-general took place at Lamego in that context.

[edit] Afonso I didn't rescue his son in Santarém

The article says Afonso I came to the rescue of his son in Santarém, but this is a legend because at the time Afonso I was already crippled!!
