Afternoons in Utopia was the second album released by Alphaville in 1986, by Warner Music.

The album produced five singles:

  • "Dance with Me" (1986)
  • "Universal Daddy" (1986)
  • "Jerusalem" (1986)
  • "Sensations" (1986, released only in Austria, France, Netherlands, and Switzerland)
  • "Red Rose" (1986)
