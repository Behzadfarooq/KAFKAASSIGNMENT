This, the second full-length album from the Springbok Nude Girls was released in 1997. An anarchic, industrial sound is relieved by reflective moments (“Horizontal Landscape”), ska-inspired anthemic love songs (“I Love You”), pop hits (“Genie” and “Baby Murdered Me”), and the album even sports an accordion played by Nico Carstens (“Beautiful Girl”) in a tongue-in-cheek callback to Afrikaans music.
