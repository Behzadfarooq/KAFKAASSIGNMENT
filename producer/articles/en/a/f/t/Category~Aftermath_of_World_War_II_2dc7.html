This category is for articles concerning World War II that deal primarily with the immediate aftermath of the war. Only articles eligible for Category:World War II should be placed in this subcategory. Examples include peace treaties, war reparations, post-war diplomatic negotiations. The articles should be primarily concerned with World War II and its aftermath, and not a consequence that arose some years later.
