"Afternoons & Coffeespoons" was a song of the Canadian pop band Crash Test Dummies and was the third single 1993 album God Shuffled His Feet. Afternoons & Coffeespoons is arguably the band's most popular song [1].

The title of the song is based on the T. S. Eliot poem The Love Song of J. Alfred Prufrock.

[edit] Music Video

The music video features Brad Roberts as a patient in a hospital, with the rest of the band playing the doctors operating on him.
