After School Nightmare (放課後保健室, Hōkago Hokenshitsu?, lit. After School Infirmary) is a shōjo manga by Setona Mizushiro, published in the U.S. by Go! Comi.

[edit] Story

Mashiro Ichijo is the girls' idol, handsome and kind, but he has been hiding a secret for all his life: he's not a male, and not a female. When a mysterious school nurse introduces him to a new class, he finds that in order to graduate he has to go to a world of dreams, and his classmates can come into his dreams; will he be able to keep his secret hidden?
