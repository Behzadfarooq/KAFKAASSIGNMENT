After Nine

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

After Nine was a programme on the former UK Breakfast Television Station TV-am. It ran from 09.00 until 09.25 finishing the days broadcasting for TV-am. It concentrated on lifestyle issues such as fashion and health and was presented by Jayne Irving and latterly Kathy Tayler.It generally finished with a workout by Lizzie Webb Mad Lizzie the stations fitness expert.

The final broadcast of TV-AM ended with After Nine and then the final closing announcement, and the last words: "We've enjoyed your viewing,but from TV-AM sadly,we have to say this finally, Thank You and Goodbye."
