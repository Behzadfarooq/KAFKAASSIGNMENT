[edit] Guy Montag

I guess I will have to correct the Zionist terrorism page to fit your definition. I am watching all of these articles very closely. Guy Montag 18:52, 6 September 2005 (UTC)

I'm not sure what you mean by 'my definition' but I am reassured by the statement on your user page that you 'play by the rules'. WP's NPOV rule means that we musn't endorse one country's/faith's perspective on who is 'terrorist' and who is not - rather discuss how different definitions of terrorism might apply. For example, I notice this courtesy has been (rightly) extended to Irgun, which is introduced in the first few paragraphs as a militant organisation classified as terrorist by the UK.
I continue to assume good faith when it comes to moving/renaming/merging articles. In fact I think a lot more of this needs to be done to make wikipedia's information on terrorism in Israel more comprehensive in terms of scope and organisation. And then we need to cite our sources and edit out the bias and inaccuracies... it is a formidable project for a formidable mess.--User:afterword 20:02, 6 September 2005 (UTC)
