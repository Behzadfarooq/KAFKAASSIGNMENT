Afterglow is an episode from That 70s Show.

Kitty is surprised when Red buys her a motorcycle, and Eric is embarrassed when the gang finds out how Donna really feels about his performance in bed.

[edit] Plot summary

Spoiler warning: Plot and/or ending details follow.

After Eric and Donna do it for the first time, Donna tells Eric it was the best she's ever had. Coming back Donna has a look on her face that leads Jackie to suspect she had sex, so they talk about it. She told her it was awkward and weird. Afterwards Jackie told Kelso after Donna told her not to tell anyone. Kelso decided to burn Eric by telling him in front of Hyde and Fez that Eric didn't perform in bed. Eric then gets mad at Donna for telling Jackie about how he performed and not telling him. Donna gets upset regretting that she might have ruined what they had. Jackie then gets mad at Kelso for burning Eric instead of helping him. Kelso thought the first time he was great, but Jackie tells him she said it sarcastically and he was bad too which helps Eric feel a little better. Donna goes around asking people hypothetically what their first time was like and Midge said it was very disappointing and she dumped him, Laurie can't remember who, and Kitty says that her and Red's first time was 5 seconds after their wedding. Eric then tells Donna that they are both overreacting and tells her that everything they seem to do is wrong at first, but it gets better like their first kiss (That '70s Pilot), date (First Date), and first time getting to second (Punk Chick). Donna asks him if they practice then so they can talk about it and make it better like the other things and Eric tells her lots of it, so they do it again and afterwards Donna tells him it was a little better.

Red gets his first paycheck from Price Mart and tells Kitty that he wants to treat her to something nice, so he gets her a motorcycle. Kitty at first is upset that a motorcycle is only good for him, instead of a necklace which would be good for her. Red tells Kitty how great he looks on it and mentions that they could get matching jackets, but Kitty tells him they need to get out of debt first and have money to pay for Eric's college and Laurie's wedding someday. Red tells her to try it one time before he takes it back and she agrees. After the ride Kitty tells Red she wants to keep it and after working those double shifts she deserves it.

This is the only episode where the cast is seen as animated characters or cartoons. They are seen as such after Fez says “You know guys, sometimes I wish we could be cartoon teenagers”; he says this after talking at length about “Scooby-Doo”.
