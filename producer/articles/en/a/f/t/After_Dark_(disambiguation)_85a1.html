After Dark (disambiguation)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

After Dark may refer to:

  • After Dark, a series of successful screensaver software products.
  • After Dark, a Swedish drag act.
  • After Dark, a novel by Haruki Murakami.
  • After Dark, a magazine that was published from 1968 to about 1983 covering all aspects of the Theatre (cinema, stage plays, ballet, performance art, etc.).
  • "After Dark," a Tito & Tarantula song from the From Dusk Till Dawn soundtrack, and their debut album, Tarantism.
  • After Dark, a 1998 DVD released by Type O Negative.
  • After Dark, a 1997 album by The Make-Up
  • After Dark, a 1987 album by Los Cruzados
