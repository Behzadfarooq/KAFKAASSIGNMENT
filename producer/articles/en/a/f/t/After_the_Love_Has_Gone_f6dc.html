"After The Love Has Gone" was the seventh single from British pop group Steps. The video was shot in America, and has an Oriental influence. The group wear jade-green outfits for the dance sequences, in a setting of Chinese lanterns and a dancing dragon.

There is an unrelated song by the same name on the Earth, Wind & Fire album I Am.

"My Best Friend's Girl" served as the single's B-side. It also appeared on the group's second album Steptacular.

[edit] Chart success

The song spent eleven weeks in the UK singles charts. It fell out of the top 75 after nine weeks, but rose and spent two more weeks inside.
