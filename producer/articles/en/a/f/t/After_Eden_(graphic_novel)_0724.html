After Eden by Arnold Arre (2002)

After Eden is a graphic novel written and illustrated by Filipino comic creator Arnold Arre. It was published by Adarna House in September of 2002.

[edit] Synopsis

Can man and woman still find Paradise in the world outside the Garden of Eden?

After Eden tells the story of Celine and Jon, estranged childhood friends who bonded over nasty pranks and seesaws. A mall brawl and an automobile mishap bring them together, sparking the genesis of a romance that changes six lives.

The novel was a finalist at the Manila Critics Circle National Book Awards in September 2003.
