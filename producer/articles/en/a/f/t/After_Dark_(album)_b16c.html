After Dark is the second full-length album and first studio album by The Make-Up. Although After Dark was recorded within a studio setting, it was done so with an eye towards spontaneity and improvisation. "Live" sounds were later mixed into the studio recording to reflect this. [1]

"Warning" on the back of the LP version of After Dark.
