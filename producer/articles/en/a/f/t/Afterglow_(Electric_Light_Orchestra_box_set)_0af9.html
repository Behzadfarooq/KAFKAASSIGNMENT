Afterglow was a box set compilation by Electric Light Orchestra released in 1990. See also: Flashback.

It is one of the most Eclectic selections ever on an ELO compilation, mixing the usual hit singles with lesser known album tracks, including most of the Secret Messages album tracks that did not survive the hatchet job in 1983, surfacing for the first time on disc three.
