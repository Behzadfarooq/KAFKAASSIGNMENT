After the Lights
Studio album by Jade Villalon
Released 2004

After the Lights is the fourth album by singer Jade Villalon of Sweetbox. It was released in 2004 in Japan. On various online stores the CD is listed as Sweetbox Presents a Very Merry X-mas because it was released late 2004 and was originally intended to be marketed as a Christmas album. In 2005 two different versions of the album were released in Korea, with one version being a special two disc Christmas edition.
