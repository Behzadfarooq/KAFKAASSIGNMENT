Aftab Ahmed Vohra (Aftab Mughal) is a Pakistani business figure based in Lahore. He is the Chief Executive of Vohra Group which consists of Mughal Agencies, Vohra International, Sheikh Sons, and Vohra Chemical Corporation. He belongs to reputed family of Chinioti Sheikhs. He started his business in early 1970s and the business quickly flourished under his guidance. Most of his business deals with the Chemical industry.

Mr. Vohra is the Vice President of the Lahore Chamber of Commerce & Industry (LCCI).[1][2][3] He previously served as the Executive Committee Member in the LCCI from 1997â€“2000, and he remains the Convener of the Sub Committee on Imports, Customs & Dry Ports. He has also represented LCCI at various forums and remained a member of several Advisory Committees such as the Railways Advisory Committee and Airport Advisory Committee.
