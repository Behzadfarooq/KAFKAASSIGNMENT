AFI's 100 Years...100 Heroes and Villains is a list of the 50 greatest movie heroes and villains chosen by American Film Institute in June of 2003. It is part of the AFI 100 Years... series. There was an accompanying CBS special that discussed the list presented by Arnold Schwarzenegger.

In several cases, the characters on the lists are seen in two or more films. The primary or original (if possible) film that character(s) was in is listed, or that film that was given particular attention during the program.
