Afili Yalnızlık
Studio album by Emre Aydın
Released 2006
Recorded GRGDN
Genre Rock
Label Sony Music/GRGDN
Producer(s) Haluk Kurosman

Afili Yalnızlık is the first solo album of Turkish alternative rock musician Emre Aydın, released by Sony Music/GRGDN. The album has received wide attention and several awards[1]in Turkey. The first video, having the same title as the album, was shot by renowned director Yon Thomas.
