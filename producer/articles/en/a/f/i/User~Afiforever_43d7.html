Strictly Platonic, an underground punk band from Brooklyn, New York is one of the bands to honestly take control of the genre and take over where the bands of yesteryear left off. The band which was founded by Bettyracecar, real name Linda Mercer, in early 2005 as a side project, but what was started has now become something more than anyone would have ever expected. The band can also boost about their debut show being held at CBGB in Februray of 2006. The band has also played The Mercury Lounge, The East River Bar, The Lucky Cat, The Trash bar, Fontana's, The Strand Bookstore and The Tank to name a few.

[edit] Members

The band which consist of Imogen Explosion, Bettyracecar, Johnny Firehouse and Romeo Platonic will bring New York punk to another level. The band is also graced by a great guitarist and bassist who are both pushing the envelope of what punk is considered and how it will sound in years to come.

[edit] Music

The energy of the band with its dark lyrics and esthetics is credited to front man Romeo Platonic, real name Romeo Lopez, who embrasses the goth subculture and its dark side. "Being the face of a band is very hard. The way people accept any band and its music these days is though the lead singer and if they don't like you, they won't like the band" Romeo said during an online interview with Gothpunk.com [1]. He also went to say "the energy we bring on stage is real and not produced. Our fans enjoy it when we jump and when we scream in their faces. We have very dedicated fans who travel from everywhere in the country to come and see us here in New York. They understand our music and us a individuals which is something very special".

Image:Bettyracecar.jpg
Bettyracecar (taken by GreenAndy and used with permission)

During a casual chat which drummer bettyracecar during one of their shows she was asked what bands influenced them and she responded by saying "we have different people in this band with different taste in music; we're influnced by bands such as Iggy and The Stooges, MC5, MxPx, AFI, The Bouncing Souls, The Ramones, Rancid, The Brides, DNA (band), Dark Day and Hickey (band) ". Romeo added "We're not trying to sound like any band we like and we're also not trying to own the genre 'punk'. What we play is a style of music which is our own and in the end we're all very happy with what we've created from nothing".

During their first years together they have developed a reputation for being a very energetic band on stage. The charisma Romeo displays just overtakes the crowd and he has them eating form the palm of his hands. A fan of S.Plat said to us "I like their live shows. I know that when I come see them tonight and then next week that their set won't be the same. They're always changing and I like that".

The band who will release their first album "Too Drunk To Cut" has gathered a huge following the old fashion way...word of mouth!

In this new age of wannabe's and teeny rock, Strictly Platonic is bringing back the raw sound we old timers used to love.
