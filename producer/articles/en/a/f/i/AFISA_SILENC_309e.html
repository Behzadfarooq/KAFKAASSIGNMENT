AFISA SILENC

Athletics club of Sils (Girona, Spain). Among its athletes stand out Xavi Sánchez, world champion in 3.000 m obstacles (master M-35) in Master World Championship of San Sebastián (2005); Gabriel Abraham Rojas, 10th in cross (master M-35) in Master World Championship of San Sebastián (2005); Xevi Masoliver, outstanding Catalan long-distance runner, etc.

In 2006, the team won the Provincial Cross Championship of Girona (Spain). As individual way, they have obtained championships of Catalonia, international meetings, etc.
