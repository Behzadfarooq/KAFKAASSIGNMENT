AFL Live 2004 is the 2004 edition of the Australian rules football game for the PlayStation 2, PC and Xbox. You play as a player in the Australian Football League. This is the next edition after many other editions such as AFL 99 (EA Sports) and AFL 2003. The game was published by Acclaim.

[edit] Game Features

AFL Premiership 2004 had many new features such as:

  • new kicks (torpedo, snap), handballs, tackles, marks and bumps.
  • Commentary by Dennis Cometti, Gerard Healy and boundary rider Christi Malthouse
  • Wizard Cup preseason competition with nine point goal and three point rushed behind rules
  • 4 mini games (Handball (football) challenge, Superkick, Around the World and Elimination)
  • Instant Replay
  • Play current team in classical historical match
  • Brownlow, Coleman, Norm Smith Medals and club Best and Fairest awards
  • Statistics on season marks, kicks and handballs
  • Training mode
  • Three new grounds including training ground
