Alan Muther (born May 18, 1988), is a Spanish rapper known by his stage name Aflie. He was born in Madrid, Spain. To date, he is unsigned and has no studio albums but has released two mixtapes credited to his name online. Aflie, originally well known for his freestyle and battle rap technique, is now working to be considered a skilled lyricists in hip-hop.

[edit] Today

Aflie attends college full-time, but is currently working on a collaboration with other budding rappers to be released sometime in 2007.

[edit] Technique

Aflie is known for his steady flow to notoriously popular beats.

[edit] Affiliations with Other Rappers

Aflie has worked with Young Knight and Nick Casanova among other during his brief career.

[edit] Nicknames

Aflie has gone by another nickname other than his stage name. He briefly used the name "DaKnockOut" before switching back to Aflie.
