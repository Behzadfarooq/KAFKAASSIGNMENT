AFL Premiership 2006 is a sports simulation game for the Playstation 2 based on the Australian Football League. The game was developed by Australian games company IR Gurus and will feature all sixteen teams playing in the 2006 AFL season.

The game is a follow-up to AFL Live 2004 and AFL Premiership 2005. The game, unlike its predecessor, was less criticized by game reviewers, and was felt to be one the best AFL games made. Its main features included the addition of training mode, revamped career mode as well as more attention paid to gameplay, rather than graphics, with the whole gameplay system being started from scratch.

You can select to play as one of all 16 teams.
