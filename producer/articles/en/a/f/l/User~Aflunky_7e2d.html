[edit] FLUNKY

[edit] HISTORY

Flunky (Born Arturo Roberto Fusco on April 20 1983 in Balboa, Panama) is one of the many unique underground triple threats of today. He curently lives in Chula Vista, CA. in the San Diego County.

Artie, as most of his friends call him, had a regular childhood. The son of a Sailor, he moved to San Diego at the age of 1. During his youth, he was always one to imitate his favorate movies and cartoon characters. He went to Loma Verde Elementry from 1st to 6th grade, went to Castle Park Middle School from 7th to 8th grade, his parents divorced at this time. Then went to Castle Park High from 9th to 12th grade.

Flunky got his name in high school from the first play that he was in called "John Lennon and Me" about a girl who had cystic fibrosis, He played one of her imaginary friends named the Flunkies. Since then the name stuck. Flunky went on to do other plays such as "You Can't Take It With You", "Annie", and "Macbeth". In 2000 he opened the new year with his first album "Mutha Flunka" pretty much an experimental album. Though in 2001, four months before graduation, he was kicked out of his high school because of his lyrical content. He went on to graduate high school and went off to college.

During college he got into the Telemedia field he, along with his best friend Mike Robledo Created Flunky/Caveman Productions. Three films were made before, Flunky droped out of an unsuccessful second semester in college. During this time Flunky started working at a local 7-11 and hanging out at a local gas station during the night shift where some of his other buddies worked. During this time, he started creating comics based on him and his friends. The scratchy stick figure art was called "Flunky/Caveman Comics" which later on became a book. Also during this time, his long time girlfriend, Heather Baxter, became pregnent. This prompted Flunky to marry Heather and join the US Navy. Before he left for bootcamp though, he made his second album, "Music To Kill By"

After being gone for 6 moths, Flunky gets stationed back in San Diego. In less then 3 years he's come out with numorous albums and films including "Split", "B-Sides", and a poem book called "Know Me". He's opened his own website, which he's been able to maintain for the last 3 years, and his own online store. All the while keeping his duties to the Navy. Last September, after Hurricaine Katrina hit, Flunky was sent to New Orleans to help out over there. Flunky described New Orleans by compairing it to the set of "Dawn of the Dead". Flunky now lives in Chula Vista, CA. with his wife Heather, is daughter Chardonnay, and another baby on the way.
