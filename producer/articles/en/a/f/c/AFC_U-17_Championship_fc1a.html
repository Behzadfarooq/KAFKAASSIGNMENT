AFC U-17 Championship

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The AFC U-17 Championship is a meeting held once in two years for Asian under-17 teams and serves as a qualification tournament for the FIFA U-17 World Cup. The top 4 countries will participate in the World Cup.
