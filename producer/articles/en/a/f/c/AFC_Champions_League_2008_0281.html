AFC Champions League 2008

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

This article or section contains information about a future sporting event or team.
It is likely to contain information of a speculative nature and the content may change dramatically as the event approaches and more information becomes available.

AFC Champions League 2008 will be the 27th AFC Champions League. It will be played between 28 clubs from nations who are members of the Asian Football Confederation.

Clubs from the following countries will be competing:

  • Kuwait (2 clubs)
  • Uzbekistan (2 clubs)
  • Syria (2 clubs)
  • Iran (2 clubs)
  • United Arab Emirates (2 clubs)
  • Saudi Arabia (2 clubs)
  • Iraq (2 clubs)
  • Qatar (2 clubs - Al Sadd - )
  • South Korea (2 clubs)
  • China (2 clubs)
  • Japan (2 clubs)
  • Vietnam (1 club)
  • Indonesia (2 clubs)
  • Thailand (1 club)
  • Australia (2 clubs - Melbourne Victory and Adelaide United)
