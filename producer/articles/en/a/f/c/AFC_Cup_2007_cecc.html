AFC Cup 2007 is the 4th edition of the AFC Cup. The draw has taken place in Kuala Lumpur on December 22, 2006. [1]

[edit] Knockout stage

[edit] Quarter finals

The first leg will be held on September 18, 2007, while the second leg will be held on September 25, 2007.

[edit] Semi finals

The first leg will be held on October 2, 2007, while the second leg will be held on October 23, 2007.

[edit] Finals

The first leg will be held on November 2, 2007, while the second leg will be held on November 9, 2007.
