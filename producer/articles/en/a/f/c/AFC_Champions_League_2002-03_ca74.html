AFC Champions League 2002-03

You have new messages (last change).

AFC Champions League 2002-03 was the 22nd AFC Champions League. Al Ain of the United Arab Emirates beat BEC Tero Sasana of Thailand 2-1 on aggregate.

[edit] Qualifying Zone 2 (East)

[edit] Round 1

Saunders 1-7 (0-2, 1-5) Mohun Bagan Sugathadasa Stadium/Salt Lake Stadium
Sri Lanka AF 2-3 (AET) (1-1, 1-2) New Radiant Sugathadasa Stadium/National Stadium

[edit] Round 2

Geylang 7-0 (3-0, 4-0) DPMM Bedok Stadium/Stadium Negara Hassanal Bolkiah
Petrokimia Putra 4-6 (3-1, 1-5) Shanghai Shenhua Petrokimia Stadium/Hongkou Stadium
South China 3-2 (2-1, 1-1) Home United Hong Kong Stadium/Bishan Stadium
Persita Tangerang 0-1 (0-1, 0-0) Osotspa Krakatau Stadium/Thai-Japan Stadium
Monte Carlo 1-8 (1-5, 0-3) Taejeon Citizen Macau Stadium/Daejeon World Cup Stadium
Shimizu S-Pulse 7-0 (7-0, 0-0) New Radiant Nihondaira Stadium/National Stadium
Mohun Bagan 5-2 (2-2, 3-0) Valencia Salt Lake Stadium/National Stadium
Churchill Brothers 2-1 (2-0, 0-1) Saigon Port Nehru Stadium/Thong Nhat Stadium
