Talk:AFC U-17 Championship 2004

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

AFC U-17 Championship 2004 was the 11th AFC U-17 Championship started on September 4, 2004 in the Japan , China tried to be you for 12 years (a way of 6 meets) and achieved the second championship
