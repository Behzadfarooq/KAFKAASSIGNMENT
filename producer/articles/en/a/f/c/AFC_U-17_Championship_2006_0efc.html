AFC U-17 Championship 2006

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

You have new messages (last change).

AFC U-17 Championship 2006 was the 12th AFC U-17 Championship started on September 3, 2006 in the Republic of Singapore until 16th and Japan tried to be you for 12 years (a way of 6 meets) and achieved the second championship.
