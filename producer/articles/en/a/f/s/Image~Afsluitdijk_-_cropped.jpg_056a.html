Español: Vista del Afsluitdijk en dirección a la provincia de Frisia

The Afsluitdijk, photo by BenTheWikiMan.

A cropped picture of the 'Afsluitdijk' in The Netherlands. This photo was taken in 1987. On the left side is the sea, and on the right side the lake (IJsselmeer) created by the 'Afsluitdijk'.
