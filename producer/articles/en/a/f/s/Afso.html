[edit] AFSO - Airforce Special Ops,

A Marketing Campaign run by the Royal New Zealand Airforce

This Marketing Campaign is made up of a competition aimed at People aged 13-18 with a series of different tasks, games, etc.

There are 3 Main Parts to this Competition

Career Challenges

Spot Challenges

Parachutist Location

[edit] Career Challenges

There are 28 Career Challenges that are released in lots of four fortnightly on Wednesday, each challenge earns you a certain amount of points towards the leaderboard, while also giving you the clues, and skills for the final Challenge.

There has been much debate on the AFSO forums regarding the use of the flash games, especialy the pilot challenge, at one point someone managed to get 11,000 points on this challenge, where you would have to be superman to be able to achive this number of points without cheating.


[edit] Spot Challenges

Spot Challenges are a Challenge with a difference, there are a total of 30 spot challenges each worth 100 points each. Spot challenges are released on alternate weeks to the Career Challenges on a Wednesday.

Some of the spot challenges include answering questions on the Airforce, creating a video, making paper planes, and taking interesting photographs etc.

[edit] Parachutist Location

On the Friday following the release of the Career challenges a Clue will be released to the most recent whereabouts of the parachutist. There are up to 5 Clues for the Location, and the sooner you solve the clue, the more points you get. One clue is released every day for 5 days, solve it on the first day and you get 500 points, second location 400, and so on. The day after the 5th clue is released the Location is released to everyone.

There is also 100 Bonus points for the first person to find the location, and the first person to get to the actual physical location where there is a "Cache"
