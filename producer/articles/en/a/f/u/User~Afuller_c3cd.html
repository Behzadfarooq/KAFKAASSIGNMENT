Who is Andrew Fuller?

I developed a love of reggae in the cellars in west of london before we learned to distrust one another for far too trivial reasons and have never grown out of it, some might say up....and the rest is history...but a living history.....

What does Andrew Fuller enjoy?

.....if you insist on a list: bowie...the original...because he is different, orwell..."even the chocolate did not taste the same anymore", black and white, huxley, mark twain, port for winter evenings, gin with my friends, tamara de lempicka because of the boundaries she pushed, khalte chhole, blue monday, the grandmaster F, bo diddley, etta james, rohinton mistry, art deco, cheese, reggae, the clash, massive attack, fine assam tea (always warm the pot), primal scream, voltaire, the warmth of charles rennie mackintosh, the vision of Frank Lloyd Wright, my grandma, travelling by train, science...try asking me about multiple parallel universes, ginger and corriander, "warm soapy" beer...if you've drunk it you know what i mean, dub reggae, any depp or jarmusch movie...better still together, million dollar baby, billy holliday, mod style, e-types, Dr No, green onions, the photos of bailey, donnovan, horst, pennie smith and man ray, the works of picaso, turner, and caravaggio....dirty funky 70's NY soul...

What makes Andrew Fuller Andrew Fuller?

passions for music keeps me young, books make me think, people make me alive, travel makes me wise (reputedly) and food and drink just make me fat (and happy).
