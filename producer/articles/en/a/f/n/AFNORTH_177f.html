  • AFNORTH International School(AIS) is an international school (for students from kindergarten through to grade twelve) that is located just outside the current JFC HQ in Brunssum, the Netherlands. It provides education for the children of NATO participating nations. There are also a limited number of places available for non-entitled families who may register their children as fee-paying students. The quality of education is high. From the earliest years, there is an emphasis on the learning of partner languages, with superb results in evidence. In the primary years, international classes produce an outstanding degree of tolerance and co-operation among the students. U.K., U.S. Canadian and German administrators collaborate effectively to meet the requirements of both their own sections and the broader mandates of the school. The Director of the school is responsible for the application of the international mandates. Recent NCA and OFSTED inspections have been good. Parent and student feedback on the quality of education provided at AFNORTH International School is overwhelmingly favorable. Direct feedback from individual former students is very complimentary and indirect commentaries through weblogs and internet sites convey a general impression of affection, respect and gratitude. In 2007, AIS celebrates its 40th anniversary. It is a unique school which continues to offer quality education.
