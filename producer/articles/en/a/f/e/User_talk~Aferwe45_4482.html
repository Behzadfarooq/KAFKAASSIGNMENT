Make sure to use '''Bold text''' for bold text instead of <b>Bold text</b>. Try to find something you like by experimenting at the sandbox. So, you might start from something like [[User:Aferwe45|Afer]]''[[User:Aferwe45|we]]''<font color=orange>[[User:Aferwe45|45]]</font> ''[[User talk:Aferwe45|talk!]]'' which looks like: Aferwe45 talk!. Note, however, that the link to your user page will remain red until you create that page; most editors provide a brief bio of themselves -- you can take a look at mine. (Also the link to your talk page isn't a link right now, since this is your talk page, and if you link to the page that you are on, Wikipedia changes the link to bold text. See: User talk:Aferwe45.) When you find something you like, go to "my preferences" at the top of any page, copy-and-paste your signature into the "signature box," and make sure that the option for "raw signature" is checked. Then save, and you should be good to go! I hope that wasn't too confusing -- I know I rambled on a bit about the technicalities, but basically, once you find a design you like, head to "my preferences" and then you'll be done! --Fbv65edel / ☑t / ☛c || 15:32, 4 March 2007 (UTC)
