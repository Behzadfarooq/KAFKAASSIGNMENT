[edit] AfD nomination of [[{{{1}}}]]

I've nominated [[{{{1}}}]], an article you created, for deletion. We appreciate your contributions, but in this particular case I do not feel that [[{{{1}}}]] satisfies Wikipedia's criteria for inclusion; I have explained why in the nomination space (see also "What Wikipedia is not" and the Wikipedia deletion policy). Your opinions on the matter are welcome; please participate in the discussion by adding your comments at [[Wikipedia:Articles for deletion/{{{1}}}]] and please be sure to sign your comments with four tildes (~~~~). You are free to edit the content of [[{{{1}}}]] during the discussion but should not remove the articles for deletion template from the top of the article; such removal will not end the deletion discussion. Thank you.

This template is intended to be placed on a user talk page to warn the creator of an article that you have nominated such article for deletion.

To use, append {{subst:AFDWarning|Name of nominated page(s)}} to a user talk page; a section heading will be automatically created. The template should always be substituted lest attempted replies should modify the template page; editors are encouraged to modify substituted templates to suit unique circumstances.

[edit] See also

  • Template:AFDNote, to be used by an editor to notify the creator of or principal contributors to an article of its being nominated for deletion by another editor
