[edit] AfD categories

[edit] Proposal

When Articles for deletion can also be viewed through a few basic categories, it becomes much more accessible to the community at large.

  • If a nominator is able to list an AfD correctly, they're probably able to tell whether it's a biography, media, science, and so on -related topic.
  • With a categorizarion option in place, editors are less likely to miss AfDs of interest, and save the time from browsing the entire list.

[edit] Result

  • Categorized AfD discussions
