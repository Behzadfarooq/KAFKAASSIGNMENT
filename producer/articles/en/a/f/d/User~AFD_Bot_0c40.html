This user account is a bot operated by AllyUnion (talk).

It is not a sock puppet, but rather an automated or semi-automated account for making repetitive edits that would be extremely tedious to do manually.

I am the new Wikipedia:Articles for deletion bot. I am maintained and run by AllyUnion.

[edit] Bot Work

  1. Maintain and update User:AllyUnion/AFD List every 30 minutes at 15 and 45 minutes on the hour.
  2. Add the section on the new UTC AFD subpage day one hour before
  3. Add the six days ago link
  4. Close the AFD discussion, 5 minutes after a new UTC day.
  5. Update Wikipedia:Articles for deletion/Log/Yesterday every new UTC day
  6. Update Wikipedia:Articles for deletion
