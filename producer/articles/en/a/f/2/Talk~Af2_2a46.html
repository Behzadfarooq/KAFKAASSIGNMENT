[edit] Cincinnati

I removed the Cincinnati Marshalls from the listings. This is not the new Cincinnati af2 franchise; they are a part of the NIFL.

[edit] Baker's Vision needs revision

The entire Baker's Vision section is out of date, and the majority of it is unnecessary. We don't need to list each year's expansion teams. At most, showing a team count by year would suffice. Also, numbers over ten should be written numerically, not alphabetically.

[edit] Possible Expansion

We need to get this section cleaned up. It should only contain cities that are verifiable as having been mentioned for af2 teams either through press conferences or through newspaper articles. Nolesrule 17:36, 2 November 2006 (UTC)

Agreed. If it is not verifiable, it should not be on the list. As it is, I could say that Smalltown population 3 in Montana is a possible expansion. I recommend that we delete the entire section except those that can be shown to be looking into acquiring a team. WeatherManNX01 18:17, 2 November 2006 (UTC)
I have removed a number of the speculated cities from the list in the process of correcting team and city listings by an anonymous user who was changing things for no apparent reason, including adding teams to expansion that were speculation or have been withdrawn (Alaska Wild). Nolesrule 06:58, 10 November 2006 (UTC)
I moved Fort Wayne back to "Rumored to Play" because they have not been formally announced as a 2007 expansion team. They were also listed as the Fusion, which has also not been announced. WeatherManNX01 21:03, 15 November 2006 (UTC)

Please do not add cities to the possible expansion list without providing at least a link to a newspaper reference. Any addtional cities added to the list without reference will be removed. I follow this subject closely. Nolesrule 20:02, 22 November 2006 (UTC)

Okay, who died and made you admin? If you wanted to have total power, you should have accepted my nomination. As it is, there are several unsourced cities as it is. So mind your own business, please. Tom Danson 22:19, 22 January 2007 (UTC)
I have no desire to be an admin, but if you look, the date on my most recent comment on this subject in the talk area was 2 months ago. There needs to be some control over the possible expansion list, otherwise what's the point? You could add every city that has an arena that can seat 6,000 or more. And as you can see, at least one other person agreed with me. Nolesrule 01:25, 23 January 2007 (UTC)
