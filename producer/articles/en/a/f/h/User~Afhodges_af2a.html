I am a Ph.D. candidate in Comparative Literature specializing in critical theory, continental philosophy, and 20th century fiction and poetry in English, French, and German.

Currently, and given time, I plan to contribute to the following articles:

  • Martin Heidegger
  • Michael Hardt
  • Fredric Jameson
  • Ernesto Laclau
  • Niklas Luhmann


In general, I have further interest in discussing articles pertaining to:

  • Marxism and post-Marxist political theory
  • 20th century continental thought
  • German Idealism, especially Kant and Hegel
  • Modernism
  • Romanticism



My non-scholarly interests include rock music, wine, vegetarianism, college basketball, and the history and theory of chess.
