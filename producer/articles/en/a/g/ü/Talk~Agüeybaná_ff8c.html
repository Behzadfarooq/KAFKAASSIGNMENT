[edit] Arrival of the conquistadors error

In the Arrival of the conquistadors section, it says that guaitiao means friends, and that Juan Ponce de León and Agüeybaná's wives exchanged names. Guaitiao is actually a Taíno ritual in which Juan Ponce de León and Agüeybaná exchanged their names, not their wives'. I will be correcting the error as soon as possible.

Boricuaeddie 03:01, 23 March 2007 (UTC)
