Agim Shabani (born 1988) is a Norwegian football player. He is currently playing for Fredrikstad in the Norwegian Premier League. He is a local player from the club Trosvik I.F. in Fredrikstad in which many Fredrikstad youngsters have their roots, for instance Tarik Elyounoussi and Lasse Staw.

A central defender

Shabani played his first official match for Fredrikstad in the Norwegian Cup final 2006 , which Fredrikstad did win 3-0 against Sandefjord.
