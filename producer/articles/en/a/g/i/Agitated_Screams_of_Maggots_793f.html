"Agitated Screams of Maggots" is a single released by Dir en grey on November 15, 2006 in Japan and on January 9, 2007 int the United States. The title track is followed by three live songs, recorded during the It Withers and Withers and Inward Scream tours at Nippon Budokan, Tokyo.

[edit] Track listing

  1. "Agitated Screams of Maggots" – 2:59
  2. "Kodoku ni Shisu, Yueni Kodoku." [Live] – 3:52
  3. "Spilled Milk" [Live] – 3:57
  4. "Obscure" [Live] – 3:55
All lyrics by Kyo, all music by Dir en grey.

[edit] Notes

  • The animated video for the song was created by Keita Kurosaka and will be shown at the 2007 International Film Festival Rotterdam.[1]
  • In the United States the single was advertised as an EP and sold exclusively through For Your Entertainment.[2]
  • The promotional video for this song can be seen on Comcast digital cable's Music Choice.
