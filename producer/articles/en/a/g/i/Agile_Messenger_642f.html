Agile Messenger

Screenshots of Agile Messenger 3.70 on Series 60
Developer: Agilemobile.com Limited
Latest release: 3.70 / March 16, 2006
OS: Symbian Windows Mobile
Use: Instant messaging client
License: Proprietary
Website: http://www.agilemobile.com

Agile Messenger is a multiprotocol instant messaging application for mobile devices which allows users to be connected to multiple instant messaging networks while away from the PC. Agile Messenger can connect to the AIM, ICQ, Yahoo! Messenger, MSN Messenger, and Jabber/XMPP networks.

Versions are available for Windows Mobile/Windows CE, Symbian OS, Palm OS, and J2ME enabled mobile phones.
