Aglie Property Holdings Limited (雅居樂地產控股有限公司) SEHK: 3383 is a land developers of Guangdong Province, China. It was established in 1985 as a furniture maker in Zhongshan City, and entered the property business in 1992. On December 15, 2005, Agile Property was listed on the Hong Kong Stock Exchange.
