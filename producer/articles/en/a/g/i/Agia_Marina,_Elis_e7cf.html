Agia Marina (Greek: Άγια Μαρίνα ) or Ayia Marina is a village in Greece, part of the municipality of Vouprasia, Elis Prefecture. It is located ENE of Borsi and NE of the barrier of the Pineios River dam. Distance from the GR-9/E55 is about 20 km NE from the northbound lanes and about 15 km S from the southbound lands.

[edit] Nearest places

  • Nisi (northwest)
  • Aetorrachi (west)
  • Borsi (southwest)
  • Portes (east), but no connecting paved roads

The community has a church named Agia Marina, and a square(plateia).

The hills are located in the south, while the mountains are founded to the northeast and east with grasses. Mount Skollis is east of the mountains..
