Agiel (band)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

You have new messages (last change).

Agiel was a brutal death metal band hailing from Rochester, New York. They officially formed in 1997, and have since disbanded. They were signed to Unique Leader Records for their Dark Pantheons Again Will Reign album, but left shortly after. The only consistent members of Agiel have been James Taylor and Mike Dillio; in the last few years they added bassist Rich Buzzell.
