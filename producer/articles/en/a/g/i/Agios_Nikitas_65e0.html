Agios Nikitas (Greek: Άγιος Νικήτας) is a town and a municipal district of the Lefkada Prefecture on Lefkada island as well as the municipality of the island's name in Greece, not far from the prefectural capital.

[edit] Information

The village is situated in a valley area surrounded by rocks and ledges. Forests and grasslands covers the area around the village. The village is famous for tourism as well as a beach located in the village centre in which it features several shops, bars and taverns. It is linked with a road linking to the rest of the island.
