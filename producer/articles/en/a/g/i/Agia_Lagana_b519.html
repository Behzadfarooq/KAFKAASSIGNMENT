Agia Lagana, Agia Lagana, a Place of Interest in Greece on the Peloponnese near to Olympia with many beautiful Palmtrees. It lies at a lonely sandy beach between Zacharo in the north and Kyparissia in the south. The sea is 90m far and named Gulf of Kyparissia which is part of the Ionian Sea. The place was cultivated and planted by greek farmers before 1850. NM=浬
