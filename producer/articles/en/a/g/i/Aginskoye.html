Aginskoye (Russian: Агинское) is an urban-type settlement and administrative center of Agin-Buryat Autonomous Okrug in Chita Oblast, Russia. It is located in the valley of the Aga River (the Amur basin). Population: 11,717 (2002 Census); 9,286 (1989 Census); 7,200 (1967).

Aginskoye was founded in 1811.
