Aggressor (2003) is an album by the industrial and synthpop band, And One. The entire album is in German. This album produced one single for Krieger.

[edit] Track listing

  1. "Kein Anfang" – 2:01
  2. "Schwarz" – 4:48
  3. "Krieger" – 5:35
  4. "Sternradio" – 4:39
  5. "Speicherbar" – 3:49
  6. "Fehlschlag" – 5:34
  7. "Für Immer" – 4:09
  8. "Einstieg" – 2:05
  9. "Strafbomber" – 4:22
  10. "Fernsehapparat" – 3:11
  11. "Tote Tulpen" – 3:09
  12. "Kein Ende" – 1:31

[edit] Personnel

  • Steve Naghavi: Vocals & Machines.
  • Chris Ruiz: Backing Vocals & Programming, leadvocal on "Fernsehapparat."
  • Gio van Oli: Backing Vocals & keyboards.
  • Oliver Warzecha: Additional programming on "Strafbomber."
  • Micensen at Forward music: FX-overdub
  • Arne T.: chorus on "Krieger."
  • Kira: chorus on "Krieger."
  • Ato Yankah: chorus on "Krieger."
  • Dana: female voice on "Tote Tulpen."
  • Artwork by Chris Ruiz.

[edit] Notes

  • The Virgin Records version of the CD is copy protected.
  • In 2004 Metropolis Records rereleased this album without copy protection.
