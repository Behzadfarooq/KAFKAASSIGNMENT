While I've never heard the term 'Aggrotech' in my life, other names of the genre: 'Hellectro', 'Technine' or 'Terror EBM' seem to be the most common, with 'Terror EBM' making the most sense. However, where I live, and in my informal communications on line, the term 'Harsh EBM' seems to be the most common, best describes the sub-genre, and doesn't contain auxiliary references to other aspects of Industrial Culture.

[edit] Find 'Aggrotech'?

After doing some simple research through Google, it seems that Aggrotech typically refers to Coldwave styled heavy electronic music with guitars. Where as the terms Terror EBM or Harsh EBM are much more commonly used in reference to bands like Terrorfakt, Hocico, CombiChrist, Feindflug, Tactical Sekt, etc. by the artist, labels and critical media, not to mention clubs like Das Bunker and Albion Batcave, which are major showcases for the genre in the US.

  • Search for Harsh EBM on industrial-music.com: 10 Results, including wumpscut:, Implant, Aiboforcen and Tactical Sekt
  • Search for Aggrotech on industrial-music.com: 0 Results


  • Search for Harsh EBM on rec.music.industrial archive: 57 References, including wumpscut: and DSBP
  • Search for Aggrotech on rec.music.industrial archive: 30 References, all from one source, the Aggrotech '96 Tour featuring Cubanate and Acumen Nation which are two Coldwave acts.


  • Search for Harsh EBM on Last.fm: 112 Tags
  • Search for Aggrotech on Last.fm: 40 Tags, for artists ranging from Imminent Starvation to Kevorkian Death Cycle


  • Search for Harsh EBM on Google.com: 28,700 hits
  • Search for Aggrotech on [1]: 10,800 hits, the first of which is the wikipedia entry.


My suggestion would be to rename this entry Harsh EBM due to its more common usage by labels, artists and fans, not to mention the fact that I've never heard the term aggrotech until today. Aside from that aggrotech seems to be used to describe older guitar industrial. Not suprizing, since another term for coldwave/guitar-industrial is aggro-industrial. Harsh EBM would disambiguate this subgenre into a simple, descriptive term which clarifies both the style and origin of this type of music.
