Aggie MacKenzie

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Aggie MacKenzie (1955 Rothiemurchus, Scotland) is a television presenter. She appeared on How Clean Is Your House?, Too Posh To Wash with Kim Woodburn.

Before being a television presenter Aggie worked for DC Thomson, Good Housekeeping and MI6 (but not all at the same time!).

Despite publicly condemning Kim Woodburn's decision to bury her still-born child in a park and confirming in a newspaper column that she and Kim do not like each other[citation needed], Aggie continues to work with her 'How Clean Is Your House?' co-star. The pair are both expected to renew their contract for the show when it runs out next year.
