Aggeliki Tsiolakoudi

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Aggeliki Tsiolakoudi (born 10 May 1976) is a Greek javelin thrower.

Her personal best throw is 63.14 metres, achieved at the 2002 European Championships in Munich.

Year Tournament Venue Result Extra
1995 European Junior Championships Nyíregyháza, Hungary 3rd
