The Aggressor-Class Destroyer is a very unusual destroyer. It first uses its ion-plasma cannon to disable the target's shields, then uses the plasma cannon to severely damage the ship. To make sure such a powerful weapon doesn't fall into the wrong hands, the destroyer can create a chain reaction in its main cannons, thus self-destructing. A Consortium capitol ship of unique design, the Aggressor-class Destroyer is built around a pair of cannons that fire two shots in succession inflicting massive damage on a target.
