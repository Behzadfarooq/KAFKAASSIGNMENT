Aggie 100

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Texas Aggie

Aggie 100 is a list of the fastest growing businesses managed or owned by former students of Texas A&M University. The list was launched by the university's Center for New Ventures and Entrepreneurship in cooperation with The Association of Former Students launched in 2005. Aggie 100 is published as part of Texas Aggie at the end of each year. Nominated companies are ranked by percentage of growth in revenue.[1]
