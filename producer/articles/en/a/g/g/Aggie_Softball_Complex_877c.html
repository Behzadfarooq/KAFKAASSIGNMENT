The Aggie Softball Complex is the home to the Texas A&M University softball program. The stadium was dedicated on March 30, 1994. The record attendance for the complex is 2,341, set on April 25, 2005 versus the Texas Longhorns. Nineteen of the 20 highest attendance numbers were set during the 2005 and 2006 seasons.
