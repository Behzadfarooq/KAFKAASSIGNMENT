Categorymagic, adding arrows to lists of years in categories, typically lists of births and deaths by year.

For an example see 1999 tests. (change tests with births in a real implementation)

The closest in use now are 1999 births.

Involved templates

  • Template:Birthyr2
  • Template:BDYearsInDecade1
  • Template:Before year (contains a list of all years with a previous year relation)
  • Template:After year (contains a list of all years with a next year relation)

For every year there must be a template Y<year> containing {{{<year>}}}

Existing templates

  • Template:CategoryTOC

It is possible to do a simplification and only add relations for two of the years in a decade, typically 1990 and 1999. This saves some memory but dosn't generalize so well.
