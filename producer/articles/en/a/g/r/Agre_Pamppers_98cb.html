Agre Pamppers is a demo by Sonata Arctica which was released under the band name Tricky Beans in 1996. The song Shy later reappeared on the EP Successor. Also, the song Addict has been partially renewed as Sing In Silence, that appeared in the full-length studio album Silence.

[edit] Track listing

  1. I'm Haunted
  2. Addict
  3. Shy
  4. Apartment 54
