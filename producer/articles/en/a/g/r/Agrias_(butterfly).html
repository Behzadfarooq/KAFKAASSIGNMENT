Agrias is a genus of Nymphalid butterflies found in South America and Central America.

There are five species

  • Agrias aedon
  • Agrias amydon
  • Agrias claudina
  • Agrias hewitsonius
  • Agrias narcissus

Prized by collectors, these large, showy butterflies have had hundreds of names applied to polymorphic variants

Agrias amydon phalcidon upperside
