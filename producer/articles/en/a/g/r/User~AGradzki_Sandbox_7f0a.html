I am an upperclassman student at Walt Whitman High School I take part in sport rowing, biking and am also interested in Linux. I have a 2005 Fuji Roubaix Pro that I ride a lot around Washington D.C. I have also have used Linux since 1999 when my friend introduced it to me after my old Windows 95 machine died. Since then I have enjoyed the speed and usability of many distributions until I settled on Ubuntu

[edit] Wikipedia

I love reading numerous articles on wikipedia and recommend it over any other general encyclopedia. My obsession with wikipedia began early in 2003 when I discovered that wikipedia was more concise and larger than Encyclopedia Britannica, which costed $50 for me at the time.
