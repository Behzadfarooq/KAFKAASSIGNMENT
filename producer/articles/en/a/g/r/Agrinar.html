Agrinar

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Agrinar SA is a tractor manufacturer located in Santa Fe, Argentina. It was founded in 2002, utilizing former a Massey Ferguson factory and designs. A lawsuit from AGCO, Massey Ferguson's parent company now, brought about design changes.

The company also produces backhoe loaders under the Agrinar name, and combine harvesters under the Agrinar, Marani and Marani Agrinar names.
