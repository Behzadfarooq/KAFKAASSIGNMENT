Providing employment for 70% of the population, agriculture is a major source of economic activity in Nigeria[citation needed].

[edit] Major agricultural products

[edit] Crops

The following are major crops grown in Nigeria:

  • beans
  • benne seed (sesame)
  • cashew nuts
  • cassava
  • cocoa
  • groundnuts
  • gum arabic
  • kolanut
  • maize (corn)
  • melon
  • millet
  • palm kernels
  • palm oil
  • plantains
  • rice
  • rubber
  • sorghum
  • soybeans
  • yams
  • carrots

[edit] Livestock

The following are often reared as livestock:

  • cattle
  • donkey
  • ducks
  • geese
  • goats
  • chicken
  • guinea fowls
  • pigeons
  • pigs
  • sheep
  • turkey
