AGR is a three-letter abbreviation with multiple meanings, as described below:

  • Active Guard Reserve of the United States Army National Guard
  • Adjusted Gross Revenue, an accounting term
  • Advanced gas-cooled reactor, a type of nuclear reactor
  • Agere Systems, a ticker symbol on the New York Stock Exchange
  • AgriTec Systems, a ticker symbol on the TSX Venture Exchange
  • AGR (automobile)
  • Alabama and Gulf Coast Railway
  • Alpha Gamma Rho, the largest social-professional fraternity in the United States
  • Amtrak Guest Rewards, the frequent traveler program of U.S. passenger rail operator Amtrak
  • The National Rail code for Angel Road railway station, United Kingdom
  • The Asheville Global Report, an independent weekly newspaper
  • L'Association de la Gauche Républicaine, a French political party
  • Andretti Green Racing of the IRL
  • Advanced Graphics Riser, a type of slot found on some computer motherboards
