[edit] Fair use problem with Image:Paolo.gif

I notice you uploaded the image Image:Paolo.gif with the {{logo}} fair use image tag. However, it appears that this tag is totally unrelated to the content of the image, and as a result it may qualify for speedy deletion. Image:Paolo.gif has been tagged for deletion, and may have been deleted by the time you see this message.

If you can find a valid tag that expresses why Image:Paolo.gif can be used under the fair use guidelines, please replace the {{logo}} tag that you have placed on it with that tag. If no such tag exists, please add the {{fairusein|article name that the image is used in}} tag, along with a brief explanation of why this constitutes fair use of the image. If the image has been deleted, you can reupload it but please ensure you place the correct tag on it. However, you must not remove the speedy deletion notice. Thank you, and please feel free to continue contributing to Wikipedia. Hagerman(talk) 08:36, 17 December 2006 (UTC)

[edit] License tagging for Image:Rozzotto.gif

Thanks for uploading Image:Rozzotto.gif. Wikipedia gets thousands of images uploaded every day, and in order to verify that the images can be legally used on Wikipedia, the source and copyright status must be indicated. Images need to have an image tag applied to the image description page indicating the copyright status of the image. This uniform and easy-to-understand method of indicating the license status allows potential re-users of the images to know what they are allowed to do with the images.

For more information on using images, see the following pages:

  • Wikipedia:Image use policy
  • Wikipedia:Image copyright tags

This is an automated notice by OrphanBot. If you need help on selecting a tag to use, or in adding the tag to the image description, feel free to post a message at Wikipedia:Media copyright questions. 20:09, 26 December 2006 (UTC)

[edit] Re: Explain

Regarding your message: this article was deleted via consensus at Wikipedia:Articles for deletion/Paolo Rozzotto. The reasoning is that the claim to notability (per WP:BIO) that he is the "fastest guitarist in the world" cannot be verified from reliable sources. A cursory Google search finds nothing to support that claim, nor can I find anything using Factiva or Lexis-Nexis. The proper way to contest deletion would be to find reliable sources and make your case at deletion review. Simply recreating the article is considered a breach of process and may be construed as vandalism, especially if the articles as recreated do not contain any additional useful information (i.e., reliable sources). --Kinu t/c (éŕ) 18:09, 29 December 2006 (UTC)
