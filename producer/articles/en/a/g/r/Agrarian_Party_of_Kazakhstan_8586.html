The Agrarian Party of Kazakhstan (Qazaqstan Agrarlyk Partiyasi) is a political party in Kazakhstan. At the last legislative elections, 19 September and 3 October 2004, the party was part of the Agrarian and Industrial Union of Workers Block, that won 7.1 % of the popular vote and 11 out of 77 seats.
