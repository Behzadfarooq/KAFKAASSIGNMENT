Agri South Africa (Agri SA) is an agricultural trade association in South Africa. It is a federal organisation, which promotes on behalf of its members, the sustainable profitability and stability of commercial agricultural producers and agribusinesses through its involvement and input on national and international level.<ref

It represents more than 70 000 small and large scale commercial farmers. The union has the following focus areas:
