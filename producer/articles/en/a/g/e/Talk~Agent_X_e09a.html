[edit] Royalties

It should be noted that the reason Cable and Deadpool changed title names was because the creator of the two (Rob Liefield)was still getting paid royalties and Marvel decided to change the names of the two to avoid paying him still. —The preceding unsigned comment was added by 153.2.246.33 (talk • contribs) .

Actually, he wasn't - none of the titles were selling enough for him to get royalties. It was (an attempt at) a sales-grab, pure and simple - SoM 13:13, 9 March 2006 (UTC)

[edit] Who Is Agent X?

The article says almost nothing about the actual character of Agent X. I notice that it mentions in passing that his name is Nijo, and his powers are listed in the superhero box. That's IT. I know there's more to this guy than that, or there wouldn't be a comic book about him. The Agent X series isn't the only thing with "ambiguous hints". Info, please...
