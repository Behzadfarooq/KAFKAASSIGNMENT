Agent Sparks is a band, formed in early 2005 when two former band mates from Audiovent decided they wanted to continue making music. Members include Benjamin Einziger (lead vocals, guitar), Stephanie Eitel (lead vocals, keyboard, percussion), Paul Fried (bass, vocals), and George Purviance (drums, vocals). They released their first EP entitled Not So Merry in September 2005. This EP includes song like, "It's Not My Time," "Choke," and "So Long Darlings." On June 20th 2006 they released their first full length album "Red Rover." Both their EP and full length were produced by Ben's brother and Paul's step-brother, Incubus guitarist Michael Einziger. The band later went on to tour with bands like 30 Seconds to Mars and Veruca Salt. They are currently on tour to support their full length album "Red Rover" with Hoobastank.

  • Their first EP Not So Merry was released on September 20th 2005.
  • Their debut album Red Rover was released on June 20th 2006.
