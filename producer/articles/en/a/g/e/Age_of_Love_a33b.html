Age of Love is the fourth studio album by German techno band Scooter, released in 1997. Age of Love was released conatining two singles, "The Age of Love", and "Fire".

[edit] Track listing

  1. Introduction - 1:00
  2. The Age of Love - 3:48
  3. She Said - 5:18
  4. Fire - 3:32
  5. Dancing in the Moonlight - 4:33
  6. Forever (Keep Me Running) - 4:46
  7. Hit The Drum - 4:37
  8. Don't Waste No Time - 4:14
  9. Tonight - 4:58
  10. Return of the Future - 4:57
  11. Leave in Silence - 3:34

[edit] Notes

  • "The Age of Love" is based on the theme to Terminator 2: Judgement Day.
  • "Leave in Silence" was originally written by Depeche Mode.
