Ageo (上尾市, -shi?) is a city located in Saitama, Japan.

As of 2003, the city has an estimated population of 216,870 and the density of 4,761.14 persons per km². The total area is 45.55 km².

The city was founded on July 15, 1958.

[edit] Traffic

  • Ageo Station on the Takasaki Line. It takes about 40 minutes from Ueno Station (Tokyo) to Ageo station by train.
  • The National Road Route 17 runs through Ageo.
