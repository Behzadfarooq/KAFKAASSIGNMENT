Age of Reason (album)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Age of Reason
Studio album by John Farnham
Released July 25, 1988 (AUS)
Recorded 1988
Genre Pop
Rock
Adult Contemporary
Length 77:50
Label BMG

Released 25th July 1988. The follow up to John Farnham's Age of Reason attained 11 x Platinum sales.
The first two singles from the album "Age Of Reason" and "Two Strong Hearts" are Australian classics.
