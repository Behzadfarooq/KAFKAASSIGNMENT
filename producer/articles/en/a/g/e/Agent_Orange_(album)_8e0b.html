Agent Orange is an album by German thrash metal band Sodom, their last with guitarist Frank Blackfire. It was the first thrash metal album to enter the German album charts. The song "Ausgebombt" was released on the EP Ausgebombt with German lyrics.

  2. "Tired and Red" – 5:25
  3. "Incest" – 4:38
  4. "Remember the Fallen" – 4:20
  5. "Magic Dragon" – 5:59
  6. "Exhibition Bout" – 3:35
  7. "Ausgebombt" – 3:04
  8. "Baptism of Fire" – 4:03
  9. "Don't Walk Away" – 2:56 (Cover Tank)


This album managed to sell 100,000 copies in Germany alone. (http://www.myspace.com/sodomisthy)
