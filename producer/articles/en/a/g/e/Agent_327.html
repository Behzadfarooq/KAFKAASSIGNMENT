Agent 327 is a playful Dutch action/comedy comic series by artist Martin Lodewijk. It features the eponymous Agent 327 as a James Bond/Maxwell Smart Dutch secret agent who fights for "Righteousness and World Peace". Often partnered with the junoesque Olga Lawina (an agent of the Swiss secret service), his adventures take him around Europe and the rest of the world as he battles numerous parodies of real and fictional villains.

[edit] Main Characters

Agent 327

Initiated into the world of espionage in the Dutch resistance, often employs creative and elaborate disguises just to get to work in the morning.

Olga Lawina

A Swiss secret agent partnered with Agent 327 in a wide variety of adventures. Her good looks often present both an advantage and a liability.

Doctor Papa

Villainous dictator of a Caribbean island, parody of Papa Doc.
