[edit] Summary

This is a map regarding age of consent laws for sexual activity worldwide. In countries where there are different ages for different genders, I have used the older age (I think that it would be too much to try to map both). Also, the laws vary from state to state in the US, but I have yet to see a version of the original world map that was used for this that included US states.

I made this file. Most of the information comes from http://www.ageofconsent.com/ageofconsent.htm and the original world¹ map is from http://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/c/c3/BlankMap-World.png

The information is now based on the verified data in the Age of consent pages, hence the large amount of grey areas. It will be updated as new information comes to light. --Monotonehell 05:34, 10 May 2006 (UTC)
