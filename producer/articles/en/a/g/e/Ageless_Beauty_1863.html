Ageless Beauty was the fifth single put out by the Canadian indie pop group Stars.It was the second single from the critically acclaimed album Set Yourself On Fire. It was featured in a promotional trailer for VH1's show So NoTORIous as well as the pop culture review Best Year Ever

[edit] Music Video

A music video for the single was released in which a girl walks into a party while the house band (Stars) begins to play Ageless Beauty. As she tries to make her way to the back of the house it seems that the other people at the party are mesmerized by her presence. The girl walks into a bathroom, checks her hair, then begins to make her way out of the house. Before she is able to reach the front door the party goers stop and crowd around her. Her jacket, which has been tightly closed for the entire video, opens up to reveal a seemingly endless light coming out of her chest.
