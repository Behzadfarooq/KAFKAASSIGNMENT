About Me

Hello, I'm Scott. I've had a Wikipedia account since January 16, 2006. I started actively editing around late April 2006, and since then have been regularly making small edits to the RuneScape series, often checking through Special:Recentchanges, and monitoring edits on pages which I've got on my watchlist. A large amount of my time is spent on my computer, either fiddling around in Photoshop, messing with website designs in notepad, talking on IRC, or editing Wikipedia. I also love reading books, and if you know any good ones, feel free to tell me about them, I read fairly quickly and need things to keep my attention span going. Sadly, however, I was denied a job at the local public library. The quickest way to contact me is by emailing me, either through the link in the toolbox or at the top of this page.
