Agent M (real name Emily Whitehurst, born July 1979) who grew up in Los Banos was the lead singer of the California based punk band Tsunami Bomb until their breakup in September 2005. She is the sister of the late Logan Whitehurst, a musician with numerous solo and album credits.

She and former Tsunami Bomb bassist Matt Mckenzie have recently formed the band, The Action Design, along with drummer Brett Friesen from Ariel Mile.

Favourite bands include Green Day and The Smiths, Emily says her dream duet would be with Billie Joe Armstrong. Is also heavily in to Glassjaw and Daryl Palumbo's subsequent band, Head Automatica.

Her favourite Tsunami Bomb songs are Russian Roulette, El Diablo and .....Not Forever (to perform live). Whitehurst has delared new band, The Action Design, as not Tsunami Bomb 2 and appears to have adopted a more experimental approach with her new band.

Appears on the Guttermouth record Gusto, featuring on My Town and Twins. When Tsunami Bomb supported The Bouncing Souls on tour, Agent M performed a song with them.

She also performed guest vocal on her brother Logan's album, Goodbye, My 4-Track.
