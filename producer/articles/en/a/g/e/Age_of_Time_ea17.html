Age of Time is an action computer role-playing game that attempts to combine elements from regular role playing games with that of a first person shooter. It uses the Torque Game Engine, but it is unknown whether the game will be ported to the Torque Shader Engine.

[edit] Background

The game is set in a fantasy realm that combines elements from both the modern and mythological worlds. The user plays as an unknown female character and attempts to rise through the ranks of the social ladder by fighting other players, collecting dyes, gathering gold, completing quests, and other such activities.

[edit] Monsters

Monsters are enemy entities in the game world.

  • Orc: An Orc that deals normal physical damage through collision.
  • Dynamite Orc: A red orc that throws lit dynamite. Although it cannot damage through collision like a normal orc, it's dynamite can be very dangerous.
  • Zombie: The restless souls of the Orcs you have killed, that spawn in the swamp to extract their revenge.
  • Knight: A fallen knight who has nothing to live for anymore. Although he is weak he fires very fast and can be dangerous.
  • Sea Monster: A blue orc spawning in deep water. This orc can outrun players and can kill in one hit.
  • Rocket Orc: An incredibly fast, orange orc that can kill players in a few hits. They spawn at Fort Bad.
  • Fire Orc: A red orc that will attack players with a spell similar to Burn.
  • Imps: A small green orc that fires bolts at players. They spawn in The Woods, and drop bolts when they die.
  • Meat Cube: A red cube of meat that spawns in the wilderness. Bigger cubes will split into 4 smaller cubes once defeated.
  • Blue Slime: Similar to a small Meat Cube but blue in color and much faster. Found near the Beach House.
  • Bunnies: Small, weak Orcs that spawn in the wilderness.

[edit] Basic items

  • Crossbow: A weapon that shoots bolts at enemies. It can be created at the blacksmith shop or purchased.
  • Broadsword: A weapon that you hit players or monsters with. It can be created at the blacksmith shop or purchased.
  • Blue Vial: A small container that holds a blue liquid and restores a small amount of health.
  • Insta-Horse: A crate that makes your own personal horse appear when you use it. They become a 'community horse' when you log off, and you are almost invulnerable on it save ranged attacks, player attacks and dynamite. The horse has a health bar, and disappears when it's health reaches 0.
  • Dye: Comes in 30 different colors with each color being mixable with other colors. Changes the colors of almost any item. It can be found in various locations, such as Red Crater and The Woods.
  • Bolts: Comes in exploding and steel. It is used as ammo for the crossbow.
