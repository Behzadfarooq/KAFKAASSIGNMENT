[edit] The Agents

The Agents in the Heroscape Universe is part of the large and diverse Human race. The Agents are cunning warriors in the service of Vydar and are from an apocalyptic future in Earth's timeline. They are masters of street fighting and defensive strategy.

Agent technology is highly advanced, giving the Agents much leverage in the battlefield. Some Agents have even been given magical weaponry created by the Kyrie.

[edit] Trivia

  • Agent Carr's appearance and weaponry is inspired by Guardians from the 2003 Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles TV Series.
  • The Krav Maga Agents' appearance and special power is inspired by the Agents from The Matrix movies.
  • The Nakita Agents are inspired by She Spies and Charlie’s Angels.
  • The Gorillinators are also described as “Agents”
