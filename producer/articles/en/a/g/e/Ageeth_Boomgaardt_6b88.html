Ageeth Boomgaardt (born on November 16, 1972 in Tilburg, Noord-Brabant) is a former Dutch field hockey defender, who played 192 international matches for The Netherlands, in which she scored 86 goals. She made her debut on January 27, 1996 in a friendly match against the United States. A ferocious penalty corner specialist, Boomgaardt ended her international career at the 2004 Summer Olympics in Athens, where she won the silver medal with the Dutch national squad. In the final of the women's tournament in Greece Germany proved to be to strong: 2-1. She played for Dutch clubs HOCO, MOP en HC Den Bosch.
