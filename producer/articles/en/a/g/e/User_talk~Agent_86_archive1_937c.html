Speedy delete

I notice that you've asked for a speedy delete on several AFD discussions. I'm not sure if you're fully familiar with the exact meaning of a speedy delete - it refers to articles that meet the narrow speedy deletion criteria. If you request a speedy delete on articles in the future, please mention which criterion the article meets; if you feel strongly that the article should be deleted, please use "strong delete" instead.
