[edit] History

After Audie Pitre died, Dax Riggs decided to form a slower band. That was Daisyhead & The Mooncrickets. However it only laid out the basics to what was to come. Soon after Agents Of Oblivion was born. It was slower and more poetic then Dax's previous band.

The band released one album and then broke up.
