[edit] Where I came from

I was born in Manhattan, New York, and spent my first year in Brooklyn, NY. Then my folks moved to the quaint little town of Westport, CT. This remained the status quo for the next 17 years, until I made a break for it, spending 6 months in Australia with various cousins and relations.

I went to Southern Connecticut State University in New Haven, CT, where I learned a lot and fell in love.
