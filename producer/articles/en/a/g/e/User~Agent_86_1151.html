Agent 86

I like to edit articles to improve them as articles. In that regard, I will often try to clean-up articles listed as part of the wikification drive, list of dead-end pages and Category:Category needed, among others. I also like to look at new pages with the same goal in mind. Unfortunately (or perhaps fortunately from other people's perspective), I often end up finding things that pose bigger problems and I spend more time putting in templates or nominating them for the deletion process.
