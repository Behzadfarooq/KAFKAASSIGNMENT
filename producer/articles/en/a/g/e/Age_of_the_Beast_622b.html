Age of the Beast
Developer(s) Senile Team
Release date(s) TBA
Genre(s) Beat 'em up
Mode(s) Single player, Multiplayer
Platform(s) MS-DOS, Dreamcast, PlayStation 2, GP2X, Xbox

Age of the Beast is a beat 'em up video game with a sword and sorcery theme, that is under developement by Senile Team.

[edit] Features

  • Fully original graphics and soundtrack
  • Animated backgrounds
  • 4 players simultaneously
  • Moddable, freeware engine
  • True-color (max 256 colors per character)
  • Support for widescreen TV sets
  • Alpha blending and other graphical effects
  • 16-bit, 44100 KHz stereo sound

[edit] Status

Developement is currently on hold; the developers have stated that it will resume as soon as Rush Rush Rally Racing is completed.
