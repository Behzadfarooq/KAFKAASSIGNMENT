This is an incomplete list of the average ages of people when they first marry in various countries. This list is current, and does not treat the topic in history.

[edit] World (selected countries averaged)

  • 28.7 years for men, 26.8 years for women, 27.8 final average.

[edit] Africa

  •  Botswana: 30.8 for men, 11.9 for women.
  •  Cameroon: 26.7 for men, 20.2 for women.
  •  Chad: 24.1 for men, 18.0 for women.
  •  Egypt: 27.9 for men, 22.2 for women.
  •  Kenya: 26.3 for men, 21.7 for women.
  •  Libya: 32.0 for men, 29.2 for women.
  •  Mozambique: 22.6 for men, 18.0 for women.
  •  Niger: 23.9 for men, 17.6 for women.
  •  South Africa: 28.9 for men, 27.1 for women.
  •  Togo: 27.0 for men, 21.3 for women.

[edit] Americas

  •  Argentina: 25.6 for men, 23.3 for women, 25 final average.
  •  Brazil: 25.8 for men, 22.7 for women.
  •  Canada: 30 for men, 27.4 for women, 28.2 final average.
  •  Mexico: 24.6 for men, 22.4 for women, 23.5 final average.
  •  Peru: 25.7 for men, 23.1 for women.
  •  United States: 27 for men, 25 for women, 26 final average.

[edit] Asia

  •  China: 23.8 for men, 22.1 for women.
  •  Hong Kong :31.1 for men, 28.8 for women.
  •  India: 23.9 for men, 19.3 for women.
  •  Indonesia: 25.2 for men, 21.6 for women.
  •  Iran: 26.7 for men, 23.9 for women (2004).
  •  Israel: 25.5 for men, 24.3 for women.
  •  Japan: 30 for men, 27.3 for women.
  •  Taiwan: 31.8 for men, 27.7 for women
  •  Kuwait: 29.6 for men, 25.0 for women.
  •  Myanmar: 27.5 for men, 26.4 for women.
  •  Nepal: 22.0 for men, 18.8 for women.
  •  Thailand: 25.8 for men, 23.5 for women.
  •  South Korea: 30.1 for men, 27.3 for women (2004)
