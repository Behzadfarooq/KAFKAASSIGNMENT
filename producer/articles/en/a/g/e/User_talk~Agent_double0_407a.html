[edit] Dak: Apocolypse

  • A beautiful day in London, birds twittering, the fresh smell of air, everything seems perfect. Agent Athe and Agent Dak, hungry after a conference at Agent headquarters head into a pomfrite shop.

Athe (asking the round man at the register): What flavor of fries would u recommend?

Buzz Buzz (Dak's pager starts vibrating)

Dak: Hey Athe, I'll just have what your having. I need to attend to this pager.

Athe: Sure

(Dak rushes out of the shop and takes a look at the message on her pager. It's from the Agent headquarters. It reads, "Hurry, a disastrous plan is on the verge of becoming a disastrous reality....hurry to the Agent Jet. You'll find more details there.)

Dak: Ahhhhhhh!!!!!

(Dak faints...she is fading away...)

[edit] Athe: Flabbergast Capture

Image:Ufo.jpg

AThe: Where's Dak? What's taking her so long? I'll check it out.

(Athe goes to the place where Dak sat.)

Athe: Dak? daaaak? where are you? *gasp* Dak's pager is here, but where is Dak? Dak would never leave our top secret pager! It looks like from the popcorn and the drinks pushed over, it seems that somebody-dak was in a struggle to..to...escape!! but from what?

(Athe grabed Dak's pager and was surprised to see text that seems that dak wrote quickly before she was captured)

The message read: Athe...help me...i tried to get to the Agent jet but they have captured me...help...ow...help...come quickly..before it's too late...

Athe: Oh no!!! who captured you?! where?! what happened?! Too late for wat? Oh no! Let me see if there is a map around here. Oh there it is---aaaaaah!!! what are you doing, ow! stop it!

(Athe feels herself captured by a net and carried off. Seems to be a spaceship and then dropped into a long sealed vertical tube.)

Athe: where..where am i? ow...where is this...it is dark in here

Deep voice with green evil eyes: Not anymore! (switched on the light)...woohahaha (evil laugh)

Athe: wat who are you. Get me out of--*Gasp* wait...dak?

Voice of dak: wooohaha! dak is not here anymore. I have taken over. (evil laugh) wooohahahahahahahaa
