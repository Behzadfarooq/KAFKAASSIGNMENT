Agent Aika (known in Japan as simply "AIKa" [アイカ]) is an anime OVA series which stars a Japanese woman named Aika Sumeragi (voiced by Rei Sakuma) who works as a salvager for companies that hire her, but gets caught up in a plot for world domination. This OVA series is available in North America from Central Park Media.

[edit] Plot

Tokyo has sunk into the ocean, along with most of the world, during a land subsidence. Aika Sumeragi is a salvager who works for a small company run by a father and daughter tandem. Alas, the company isn't exactly successful, and money is needed desperately. Thus, Aika accepts the very dangerous job of acquiring an artifact called Lagu for a hefty sum. The boss' daughter Aida Rion joins Aika on this particular mission, and the two set off. Later on, Aika and Rion are captured by Rudolf Hagen -- an effeminate but over-sexed man who wants Lagu and Aika (and just about every pretty girl) for himself. Hargen commands a huge battleship manned exclusively by droves of cute girls in mini-skirts (the source for what is often called "fan service"). Alongside Hargen is his obsessive sister Nena, who is jealous of Aika, as well as another salvager named Gusto, who's also after Lagu and has a crush on Aika as well. When Aika's special bustier is activated, she becomes a " superwoman" who can take on whole armies by herself.

[edit] Characters

  • Aika Sumeragi – A Salvager with a mysterious past. Is considered to be one of the best in the business. Constantly fights with Gusto who either tries to steal her bounty or hits on her. Will do anything to get the job done, even if it means sacrificing her morals. Possesses a unique battle suit made out of 'ultranate' (or more accurately just "alternate metal" from the katakana オルタネートメタル), shaped into a golden bustier that transforms her into a dark-skinned and blue-haired woman with supernatural strength and resiliance. Voiced by: Rei Sakuma
  • Aida Rion – Aika's salvaging partner and her Boss' daughter. Wants to run the business better, but her father keeps to his policies. Does not like being left out of transactions nor having Aika do the work herself. Voiced by: Hiroko Konishi
  • Aida Gouzo – Owner of the K2 corporation, a Salvaging operation, and Rion's dad. Prefers to hold onto the old ways (cheapest price for the best service) and principles of doing business. His past with Aika goes far back. Voiced by: Akio Otsuka
  • Gust Turbulence – Another Salvager who has made it big in the business. Would like Aika to go out with him, but she refuses. Seems to end up in the right place to get the bounty. Spends most of the time showing off, but he does get the job done with enough skill. Voiced by: Juurouta Kosugi
  • Alexymetalia Maypia – A 'Class A' spy (complete with business card!) employed by the government to retrieve information on Lagu by infilitrating Hargen's army dressed as a black Delmo. Runs into Rion and ends up following her. Voiced by: Sakiko Tamagawa
  • B.A. Bandora – Gust's cyberpunk-styled partner. May not always agree with him but helps him out anyway. Voiced by: Hisako Kyoda
  • Ludwig Hagen – The evil mastermind who, even though contracted by the military to find the Lagu, wants it for his own ends. A scientist who can see the Lagu is more than just an energy source. Enjoys commanding his army, especially the bridge (ever see a captain's chair that was lower than the rest of the bridge?). Passive but calculating. Plans to repopulate the Earth with his own progeny conceived with his Delmo staff.
  • Nina Hagen – Another Salvager who shows up when Aika accepts the job to look for the Lagu. Cruel and sadistic, she walks around in her bare essentials and makes sure the black Delmos are punished. Loves her brother Hargen and is extremely jealous when he looks at another woman, namely Aika. Likes to drink until drunk and smash up her room when mad. Voiced by: Atsuko Tanaka
  • Michikusa Shuntaro – a young worker in the K2 corporation who joins the cast for episodes 5-7. Cute-looking but totally air-headed, prone to dressing cutely as well (including donning Delmo staff uniforms on several occasions) and using a kappa doll and squeaky voice to try and cheer up Rion when her mood is down, which earns him frequent poundings by Rion. Voiced by: Masaya Onosaka
  • White Delmo (Captain) – The commanders and Hargen's personal bodyguards. They are responsible for the smooth operations of the ship. Their (unnamed) Supreme Commander is distinguished by two green patches with a white H on her collar.
  • Golden Delmo - These gold-clad members of the Delmo force are its intelligence agents, assigned for special operations.
  • Blue Delmo – The middle-ranking officers. They carry weapons and perform duties such as piloting or defending the ship against intruders.
  • Black Delmo – The lowest ranking of the army, in charge of carrying out simple missions, mass attacking using hand-to-hand combat and the administration of the ship (the lowest jobs).
  • Pink Delmo - These specialized Delmo troopers operate the organisation's heavy weapons, from tanks to battleship guns.
  • Rudolf Hagen – Voiced by: Kaneto Shiozawa

[edit] Trivia

  • The automatic rifles shown and used throughout the series are German StG45(M), a weapon which historically never went past the prototype stage.
  • The planes Aika flies resemble Russian MiG-21 (episodes 1-4) and MiG-25 (episode 6).
  • There is about one panty shot every 20 secs in the Agent Aika anime (the first episode alone has about 122 panties in more or less plain view), even more, if you count group-scenes as more than one.
