[edit] Summary

Age of Sail is a real-time naval combat PC game. The time period of the game is 1775-1820. There is a campaign to play as well as over 100 scenarios of great battles such as Trafalgar and Camperdown. Also included in the game is a Scenario Editor with over 2,000 historically accurate sailing ships.
