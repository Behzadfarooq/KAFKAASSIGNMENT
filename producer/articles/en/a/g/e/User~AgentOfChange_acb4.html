AgentOfChange is a 16-year-old male based in the suburbs of Chicago, Illinois named Sam. He enjoys music, politics, and good books. He has been told he bears a strong resemblance to John Lennon or Jesus Christ. He has noticed that this tends to depend on how recently he has shaved. He is extremely sarcastic, and will taunt you as much as he feels is necessary.


[edit] Philosophy

AgentOfChange considers himself Libertarian on civil rights issues, and borderline Socialist in economic matters. He is an avowed atheist, but when asked he will tell you he follows the Flying Spaghetti Monster. He does not mind alternate points of view, but despises those who are willfully ignorant of the world around them.
