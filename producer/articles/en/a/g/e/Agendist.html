Agendist a term commonly used to refer to someone who is promoting a political, economic or social "agenda." [1] In contermporary internet use this has come to mean a person, or group, who supports a lexicon of political or social beliefs, addressed as an "agenda" It is also meant to describe one who is a follower of agendism.

This term in contermporary internet use has come to mean persons who support a lexicon of political or social beliefs, addressed as an "agenda." It is similar in use to the term Kool-Aid drinker, originally popularized by talkshow host Bill O'Reilly.[2]

The term agendist is used primarily by blogs to describe biased sources in the attempt to promote a given agenda.[3] A search of the term agendist demonstrates its growing usage by bloggers. [4]
