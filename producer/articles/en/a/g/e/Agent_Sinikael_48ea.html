Agent Sinikael (Estonian language:Agent Wild Duck) is a 2002 Estonian action-drama film directed and written by Marko Raat with Andres Maimik. The film premiered on 19 September 2002 in Talinn and starred Mait Malmsten.

[edit] Plot

Spoiler warning: Plot and/or ending details follow.

Hans Wild Duck Gens, an old school industrial spy becomes suicidally jealous when the firm brings in a German performance artist who becomes not only the top agent, but also replaces him in his girlfriend Monika's affections.
