[edit] Scenario Editor

I was slightly annoyed that no mention of the scenario editor was made, so I inserted a text myself. There is thriving online designer's page based in Age of King's Heaven. Though the game is difficult to modify and thus given a back seat to other mods and games, it makes up for this with its unique editor. This accessed in game, is fairly simple and user-friendly, and can be used to create a vast variety of scenartios. AoK custom campaigns are unique in gaming in that they can feature whole stories and complex plots. On their highest level they can even compete with real literature, which is something most official games can't even say. Those are few, however, and fewer than ten campaigns out of roughly 3000 have received perfect scores in the Age of Kings Heaven. I believe that the designing community outnumbers the gaming community nowadays.

EDIT - Let me just add to my fellow designers words (Oliver? Ana? Zyx?), the community is tight-knit and willing to accept everyone, so long as they keep their behavior respectable. ES has recognized many of our designs, and they seem to enjoy it some. We love our craft, and I'm sure that anyone who tries it and gets used to it will love it too. -SS

[edit] Artificial Intelligence scripting

This game offers all its Artificial Intelligence scripting documentation via a file in the Docs folder. Also complete documentation regarding map scripting is available in the same folder. These things are completely different from the scenario editor. There are AI scripts available at heavengames and at http://www.aiscripters.com. Also AllianceThundaEmpire, an AI script, appears as a well known bot in http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Computer_game_bot .
