These tags go at the top of an article that clearly doesnt belong here (not on the talk page) and you choose the tag based on what is wrong with the article, for example {{db-bio}} if the article is about a person, club or band etc. that isn't notable enough for wikipedia (see Wikipedia:Notability). You can also use prods, which are also for articles which clearly don't belong, but they give a five day notice to allow the article to be improved. If you are going to start newpage and recent changes patrolling I'd suggest reading Wikipedia:Deletion and Wikipedia:What wikipedia is not to get the jist of all the ways to delete and when you should use them.
