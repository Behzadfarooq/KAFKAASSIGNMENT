Ago of Friuli

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

You have new messages (last change).

Ago was the Duke of Friuli from between 641 and 661 until about 663 (some sources put it as early as 660).[1] He succeeded Grasulf.

According to Paul, there was a house in Cividale named "Ago's House" after this duke. Ago died and was succeeded by Lupus.

[edit] Notes

  1. ^ Hodgkin, vol. vi, p 285, gives 645 to 660.
