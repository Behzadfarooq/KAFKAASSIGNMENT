Agostino Falivene

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Agostino Falivene born from Giffoni in the province of Salerno, of the Order of the Servants of Maria, was bishop of the island of Capri from the 25 of September of 1528 to the 24 of April of 1534 in that Pope Paul III transfers it to the island of Ischia where he died in 1548. His tomb is in the castle of Ischia.
