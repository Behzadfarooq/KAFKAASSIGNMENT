[edit] 2005

  • Tan Sri Datuk Seri Ahmad Fairuz Datuk Sheikh Abdul Halim and Tan Sri Datuk Seri (Dr) Muhammad Ghazali Shafie are the 2 recipients of Seri Setia Mahkota (SSM), which carries the title Tun.
  • 2 recipients of Panglima Mangku Negara (PMN), which carries the title Tan Sri.
  • 25 recipients of Panglima Setia Mahkota (PSM), which also carries the title Tan Sri.
  • 39 recipients of Panglima Jasa Negara (PJN), which carries the title Datuk.
  • No recipient of Panglima Setia Diraja (PSD), which also carries the title Datuk.

[edit] 2004

  • Datuk Seri Dr Ling Liong Sik is sole recipient of Seri Setia Mahkota (SSM), which carries the title Tun.
  • 1 recipient of Panglima Mangku Negara (PMN), which carries the title Tan Sri.
  • 21 recipients of Panglima Setia Mahkota (PSM), which also carries the title Tan Sri.
  • 47 recipients of Panglima Jasa Negara (PJN), which carries the title Datuk.
  • 5 recipients of Panglima Setia Diraja (PSD), which also carries the title Datuk.

[edit] 2003

  • Tan Sri Abdullah Salleh and Tan Sri Fatimah Hashim are the 2 recipients of Seri Setia Mahkota (SSM), which carries the title Tun.
  • 21 recipients of Panglima Setia Mahkota (PSM), which also carries the title Tan Sri.
  • 54 recipients of Panglima Jasa Negara (PJN), which carries the title Datuk.
  • 3 recipients of Panglima Setia Diraja (PSD), which also carries the title Datuk.

[edit] 2002

  • Tan Sri Mohamed Dzaiddin Abdullah is the sole recipient of Seri Setia Mahkota (SSM), which carries the title Tun.
  • 17 recipients of Panglima Setia Mahkota (PSM), which also carries the title Tan Sri.
  • 49 recipients of Panglima Jasa Negara (PJN), which carries the title Datuk.
  • 5 recipients of Panglima Setia Diraja (PSD), which also carries the title Datuk.

[edit] 2001

  • Tan Sri Dato' Sulaiman Ninam Shah is the sole recipient of Seri Setia Mahkota (SSM), which carries the title Tun.
  • 3 recipient of Panglima Mangku Negara (PMN), which carries the title Tan Sri.
  • 19 recipients of Panglima Setia Mahkota (PSM), which also carries the title Tan Sri.
  • 41 recipients of Panglima Jasa Negara (PJN), which carries the title Datuk.
  • 3 recipients of Panglima Setia Diraja (PSD), which also carries the title Datuk.

[edit] 2000

  • Tan Sri Ibrahim Ismail is the sole recipient of Seri Setia Mahkota (SSM), which carries the title Tun.
  • 35 recipients of Panglima Setia Mahkota (PSM), which also carries the title Tan Sri.
  • 43 recipients of Panglima Jasa Negara (PJN), which carries the title Datuk.
  • 7 recipients of Panglima Setia Diraja (PSD), which also carries the title Datuk.
