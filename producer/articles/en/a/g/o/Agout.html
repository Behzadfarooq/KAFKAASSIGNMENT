The Agout (Occitan: Agot) is a 180 km long river in south-western France, left tributary of the Tarn River. Its source is in the southern Massif Central, in the Parc naturel régional du Haut-Languedoc. It flows generally west through the following départements and towns:

  • Hérault: La Salvetat-sur-Agout
  • Tarn: Brassac, Castres, Lavaur, Saint-Sulpice

The Agout flows into the Tarn River in Saint-Sulpice.
