Agos (Armenian: Ակօս, "Furrow") is an Armenian weekly newspaper published in Turkey, established 5 April 1996. It is published in Istanbul and has a circulation of around 5,000. It has both Armenian and Turkish pages. Hrant Dink was its editor from the newspaper's start until his assassination outside of the newspaper's offices in Istanbul in January 2007.
