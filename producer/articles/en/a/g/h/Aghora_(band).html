Aghora is a progressive metal band formed in 1995 by Guitarist Santiago Dobles. In 2000 they released their first album, Aghora, recorded & Produced by Santiago Dobles and Dan Escauriza in 1999 in Miami Florida. The album featured Sean Malone and Sean Reinert[1], both former members of Cynic. The band has recently released its second album, Formless, which was produced by Santiago Dobles and Mixed by Neil Kernon.
