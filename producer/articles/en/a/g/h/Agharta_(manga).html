Agharta (アガルタ, Agaruta?) is a manga by mangaka Matsumoto Takaharu serialized in Young Jump. So far 9 collected volumes have been published in Japan by Shūeisha.

[edit] History

After the earth becomes an immense desert following a cataclysm and life has become a struggle of the survival of the fittest. The story centers around Juju, a teenager who belongs to the one of the gangs which prevail on behalf of the new organizations Rael, teenager who will be able to perhaps save humanity.
