Aguila

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Águila or Aguila is an spanish name that means eagle. The name refers to:

[edit] Persons

  • Chris Aguila (b. 1979), American major league baseball player
  • Mr. Águila (b. 1978), Mexican professional wrestler
  • Pancho Aguila, nom de plume of Roberto Solis (contemporary), American fugitive criminal and poet
  • Aguila LIV, (b.1985)

[edit] Places

  • Aguila, Arizona, USA

[edit] Other

  • Águila o sol, (lit., eagle or sun; or more loosely heads or tails), 1937 Mexican film
  • C.D. Águila (Club Deportivo Águila), Salvadoran football club
  • El Aguila, character in the Marvel Comics universe
  • Mall de las Aguilas, shopping mall in Eagle Pass, Texas, USA
  • Order of the Aztec Eagle (Orden del Águila Azteca), highest decoration awarded to foreigners in Mexico
  • Patrulla Águila (eagle patrol), aerobatic demonstration team of the Spanish Air Force
  • Piedra del Águila Dam, on the Limay River, Patagonia, Argentina
  • Pico del Águila, Mexican mountain peak near Mexico City
  • .17 PMC/Aguila, type of bullet
