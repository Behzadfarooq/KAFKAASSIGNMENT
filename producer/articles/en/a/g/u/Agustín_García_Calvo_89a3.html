Agustín García Calvo is a Spanish philologist, thinker, poet and dramaturge. He was born in Zamora on October 15, 1926.

[edit] Biography

Agustin Garcia Calvo formed Acratas [1], an anarchist movement, in 1967 while studying at the Complutense University of Madrid. The movement was influenced by new protest movements amongst students abroad.
