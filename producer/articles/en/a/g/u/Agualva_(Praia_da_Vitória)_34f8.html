Agualva (Praia da Vitória)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

You have new messages (last change).

Agualva is a parish in the district of Angra do Heroísmo in the Azores. The population in 2001 is 1,573, its density is 44.1/km² and the area is 35.7 km².

  • Location:
    • Latitude: 38.7667 (38°46') N
    • Longitude: 27.183 (27°11') W
  • Altitude: 186 m
