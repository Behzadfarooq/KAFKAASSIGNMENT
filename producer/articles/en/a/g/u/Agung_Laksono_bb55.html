Agung Laksono (born Semarang, Central Java; March 23, 1949) is the head of Indonesia's People's Representative Council (DPR) from 2004 to 2009. He is a member of the ruling Golkar Party. In December 2004, during the Golkar convention, he was also elected as vice-chairman of the party.

Laksono is a supporter of Iran's nuclear program.[1] Laksono is the founder of Adam Air, a commercial airline.[citation needed]
