Aguçadoura Beach

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Aguçadoura Beach (Praia da Aguçadoura in Portuguese) is a very wide and extensive white sand beach of Póvoa de Varzim, Portugal. It is located in the parish of Aguçadoura. The beach is very hassle-free and notable for its sand dunes, a reminder of the beaches of the city before the urbanization and farming. The remaing dunes, that are still several, are protected by the city council and Municipal Director Plan (PDM) from further built-up.
