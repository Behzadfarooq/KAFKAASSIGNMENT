Agustín Aranzábal Alkorta (born March 15, 1973 in Bergara) is a Spanish/Basque football player.

He played for Real Sociedad until 2004, when he moved to Real Zaragoza.

He played for Spain national football team and was a participant at the 1996 Summer Olympics, the 1998 FIFA World Cup and Euro 2000.
