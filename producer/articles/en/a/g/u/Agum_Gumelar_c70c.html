Agum Gumelar

Agum Gumelar (born in Tasikmalaya, Jawa Barat, December 17, 1945) was Indonesian Minister for Transport in Gotong Royong Cabinet. He was 1969 alumni from National Military Academy, Magelang. In 1998, he got master degree from American World University, an organization forbidden to operate in 2005 because of its diploma mill action.

[edit] Career

  • 2003-2007: General Head of Central KONI
  • 2001-2004: Minister for Transport (Gotong Royong Cabinet)
  • 2001: Coordination Minister for Politics, Social, and Security (Nasional Unity Cabinet)
  • 1999-2000: Minister for Transport and Telecommunication (National Unity Cabinet)
  • 1999-2000: Minister for Transport (National Unity Cabinet)
  • 1999-2003: PSSI General Head
