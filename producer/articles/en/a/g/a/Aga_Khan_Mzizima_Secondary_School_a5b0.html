The Aga Khan Mzizima Secondary School, Dar es Salaam is a co-educational non-government school in Dar es Salaam, Tanzania. It was founded in 1967, and is operated by the Aga Khan Education Services, an agency of the Aga Khan Development Network. The school's motto is "Better Always Better". It adopted the International Baccalaureate system before the 2005/2006 academic year began.
