Again and Again and Again and Again is an EP by American indie/pop duo The Bird and the Bee, released in October 2006. It was issued three months prior to the band's debut album The Bird and the Bee and contains three of the same tracks. Exclusive to this EP is a remix of the band's first single "Fucking Boyfriend" by electronic musician Peaches.

[edit] Track listing

  1. "Again & Again"
  2. "I'm a Broken Heart"
  3. "Fucking Boyfriend"
  4. "Fucking Boyfriend" (Peaches remix)
