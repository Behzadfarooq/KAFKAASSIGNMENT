Against the Wind was a 1978 Australian television mini-series.

Set in 1798, it is a historical drama portraying both the British occupation of Ireland, and the development of New South Wales and Australia.

This series is now available on DVD in Australia in PAL format.

[edit] Plot

Spoiler warning: Plot and/or ending details follow.

Set in Australia's colonial era, the series is about Englishman, Jonathan Garratt, and his life as a transported convict, in the colony of New South Wales. Mary Mulvane, an 18 year old Irish girl, is transported to New South Wales for a term of seven years after attempting to take back her family's milk cow which had been seized by the British "in lieu of tithes" to the local proctor. She endures the trial of a convict sea journey to New South Wales and years of service as a convict before her emancipation and life as a free citizen.
