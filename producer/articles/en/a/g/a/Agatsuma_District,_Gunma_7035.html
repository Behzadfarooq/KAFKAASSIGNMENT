Agatsuma District, Gunma

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Agatsuma (吾妻郡; -gun) is a district located in Gunma, Japan.

As of 2003, the district had an estimated population of 66,192 and a density of 51.78 persons per km². The total area is 1,278.27 km².
