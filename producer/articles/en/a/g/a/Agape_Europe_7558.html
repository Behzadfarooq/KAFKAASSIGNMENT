Agape Europe is the Western European branch of Campus Crusade for Christ, an interdenominational movement that was founded in 1951 in the United States of America. "Agape" is the Greek word used in the Bible for God's love. Worldwide as of 2004, around 20,000 people have joined Agape Europe. [1]

Agape Europe has ministries for students in universities and secondary schools [2]), sports [3], media, families, communities, churches, musicians [4], leaders [5] and much more.

[edit] Leadership

The ministry has been led as follows:

  • Kalevi Lehtinen
  • Leo Habets
  • Markku Happonen ? - 2005
  • Javier Garcia - 2005 - present
