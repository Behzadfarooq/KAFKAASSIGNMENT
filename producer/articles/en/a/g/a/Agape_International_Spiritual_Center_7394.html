Agape International Spiritual Center

Agape International Spiritual Center is a New Thought church with a congregation often estimated in excess of 8,000 members[1][2][3] in Culver City, California. The church was founded by Rev. Michael Beckwith, who is a leader in the New Thought Movement and has been prominently featured in the documentaries The Secret and Dalai Lama Renaissance, and has also appeared on Larry King Live[4] and The Oprah Winfrey Show.[5] The Center is sometimes referred to as a mega church because it is one of the biggest New Thought churches in the world and one of the biggest churches in the Greater Los Angeles area.[citation needed]
