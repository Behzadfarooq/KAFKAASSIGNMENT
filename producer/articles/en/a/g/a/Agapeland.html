Agapeland

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Agapeland, or "Land Called Love," is a fictional place created for a series of children's Christian musical albums by Candle, Birdwing Kid's Korus, Barry McGuire, Tony Salerno and Agape Force in the 1970's and 1980's. Many of these have been recently re-released on CD including the multi Dove Award winning Music Machine & Bullfrogs and Butterflies series.
