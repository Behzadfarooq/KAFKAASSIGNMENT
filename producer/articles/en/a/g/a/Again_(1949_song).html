Again (1949 song)

"Again" is a popular song, recorded by many singers.

The music was written by Lionel Newman, the words by Dorcas Cochran.

It was in the movie Road House (1948).

In 1949, versions by Vic Damone, Doris Day, Tommy Dorsey, Gordon Jenkins, Vera Lynn, Art Mooney, and Mel Tormé all made the Billboard charts.

The biggest version was by Doris Day, reaching #2. The recording was released by Columbia Records as catalog number 38467. The flip side was "Everywhere You Go."

  • Vic Damone (1949) (#6 on Billboard)
  • Doris Day (1949) (#2 on Billboard}
  • Tommy Dorsey and his Orchestra (1949) (#6 on Billboard)
  • Gordon Jenkins and his Orchestra (vocal: Joe Graydon) (1949) (#2 on Billboard}
  • Ida Lupino (1948)
  • Vera Lynn (1949) (#23 on Billboard)
  • Art Mooney and his Orchestra (vocals: Johnny Martin, Madelyn Russell) (1949) (#7 on Billboard)
  • Mel Tormé (1949) (#3 on Billboard)
  • The Lettermen (1962)
