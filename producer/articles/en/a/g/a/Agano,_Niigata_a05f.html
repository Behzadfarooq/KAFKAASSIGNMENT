Agano (阿賀野市, -shi) is a city located in Niigata Prefecture, Japan. It has an area of 192.72 km² and a population of 48,779 (as of November 1, 2004).

[edit] History

The city was established on April 1, 2004 in a merger of Yasuda, Suibara, Kyogase, and Sasakami.

Agano was the namesake for the Agano and Agano class, a light cruiser and its class of the Imperial Japanese Navy during World War II.
