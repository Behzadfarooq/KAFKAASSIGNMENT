Against the Grain

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

There are several books with the title Against the Grain:

  • Against the Grain, one English title of À rebours, a Symbolist novel by Joris-Karl Huysmans (Also known as Against Nature, the second English title)
  • Against the Grain: Mad Artist Wallace Wood, edited by Bhob Stewart
  • Against the Grain: Biotechnology and the Corporate Takeover of Your Food, by Marc Lappe
  • Against the Grain: The New Criterion on Art and Intellect at the End of the Twentieth Century, edited by Hilton Kramer
  • Against the Grain: Southern Radicals and Prophets, 1929-1959, by Anthony P. Dunbar
  • Against the Grain: Agri-Environmental Reform in the United States and the European Union, by Clive Potter
  • Against the Grain: Parody, Satire, and Intertextuality in Russian Literature, edited by Janet G. Tucker
  • Against the Grain: New Anthology of Contemporary Austrian Prose, edited by Adolf Opel
  • Against the Grain: An Autobiography, by Boris Yeltsin
  • Against the Grain: An Irreverent View of Alberta, by Catherine Ford

Against the Grain is also the title of the following albums:

  • Against the Grain (Snakefinger album), a 1983 album by the musician Snakefinger
  • Against the Grain (Bad Religion album), a 1990 album by the band Bad Religion
  • Against The Grain (Acoustic Alchemy album), a 1994 album by the band Acoustic Alchemy
  • Against The Grain (Rory Gallagher album), a album by Rory Gallagher.

Other Uses

  • Against the Grain (record label) is a British breakbeat record label
  • Against The Grain is the 1990's New York Hardcore band
  • Against the Grain is the title of a song by country-western singer Garth Brooks on his 1991 album Ropin' the Wind
