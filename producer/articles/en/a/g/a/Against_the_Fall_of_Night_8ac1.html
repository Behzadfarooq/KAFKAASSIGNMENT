Against the Fall of Night is an early (1953) work by Arthur C. Clarke. It was later expanded and revised as The City and the Stars. The original also appeared in a more recent edition along with another unconnected early work: The Lion of Comarre & Against the Fall of Night. A sequel called Beyond the Fall of Night was written in collaboration with Gregory Benford.
