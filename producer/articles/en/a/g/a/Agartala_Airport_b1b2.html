Agartala Airport or Singerbhil Airport (IATA: IXA, ICAO: VEAT) is located 12 km (6.5 nautical miles) north of Agartala, Tripura, India. It connects Agartala to Kolkata and Guwahati. Several flights in Agartala-Kolkata and Agartala-Guwahati routes operate every day. The airport is administered by the Airports Authority of India (AAI).

[edit] Airlines and destinations

  • Air Deccan (Guwahati, Kolkata, Chennai) [1]
  • Indian (Airlines) (Guwahati, Kolkata, Silchar) [2]
  • Jet Airways (Guwahati, Kolkata) [3]
  • Kingfisher Airlines (Guwahati) [4]
