Agarn

Agarn (Walliser German: Agaru) is a municipality in the district of Leukin the German-speaking part of the canton of Valais in Switzerland.

The church of Agarn is a Marian church. The pastor is H.H. Anderas Werlen. Agarn was twice nearly totally burned down, in 1799 and 1899.
