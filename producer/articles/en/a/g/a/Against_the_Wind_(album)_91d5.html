Against the Wind is an album by American rock band Bob Seger & The Silver Bullet Band, released in 1980 (see 1980 in music). It is Seger's only Number 1 album to date, spending 6 weeks atop the Billboard 200. It was the album that knocked Pink Floyd's massive-selling The Wall from the top spot.
