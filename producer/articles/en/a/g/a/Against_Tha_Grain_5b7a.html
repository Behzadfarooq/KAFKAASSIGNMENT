Against the Grain is Kurupt's 4th solo album and his first on Death Row as a solo artist. Kurupt signed back on to Death Row, except as a solo artist in 2002. The album was supposed to be released in 2004, but was delayed. It was finally released in August 2005. It was critical because it was Death Row's first freshly recorded album in over 4 years. Unfortunately, the album went almost unnoticed due to the lack of promotion by Koch Records, which currently distributes all of Tha Row's albums. It did peak at number 60 on the Billboard 200 on September 2005.
