Category:Aga Khan Schools

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Listing of schools administered under the Aga Khan Education Services (AKES) unit of the Aga Khan Development Network (AKDN). AKES operates more than 300 schools internationally, in Pakistan, India, Bangladesh, Kenya, Kyrgyz Republic, Uganda, Tanzania and Tajikistan. Schools are also envisaged, or under development, in Afghanistan, the Democratic Republic of Congo, Madagascar, Mali, Mozambique and Syria.
