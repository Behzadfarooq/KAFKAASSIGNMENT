AGA AB, previously AB Gas-accumulator and AB Svenska Gasaccumulator, was a Swedish industrial gas company founded in 1904. Nobel Prize laureate Gustaf Dalén was instrumental in the success of the company. Important inventions included the AGA cooker and the AGA Lighthouse. In 2000, AGA was integrated into Linde AG.
