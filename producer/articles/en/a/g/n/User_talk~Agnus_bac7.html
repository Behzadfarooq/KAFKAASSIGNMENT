[edit] Fallout 3

Thank you for bringing up my edits/reverts on this subject. I have actually looked up this matter myself awhile back. The only concrete evidence I was able to find was the interview with the lead designer, which stated that Fallout 3 was in "pre-production". The Bethesda press release from 2004 only state they "will develop". I was not able to find any additional press release from Bethesda that indicate it is currently in production, as such, Fallout 3 article is currently worded such way. If you find additional evidence from credible source, feel free to cite it.--Voidvector 18:31, 30 October 2006 (UTC)

I have amended Fallout 3 page accordingly. Thank you for bringing it to my attention. --Voidvector 22:33, 30 October 2006 (UTC)
