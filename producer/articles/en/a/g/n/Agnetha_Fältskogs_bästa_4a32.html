Agnetha Fältskogs bästa is a compilation album by Swedish pop singer Agnetha Fältskog, released in 1973 to fill the gap between her last album När en vacker tanke blir en sång and the next album eventually called Elva kvinnor i ett hus. Because of her pregnancy and the upcoming fame of ABBA the release of the album "Elva kvinnor i ett hus" had to wait until late 1975.

It included many of her big hits from her first recording "Jag var så kär" until her recent single "Så glad som dina ögon".
