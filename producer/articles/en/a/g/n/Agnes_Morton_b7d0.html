Agnes Morton

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Agnes Morton (born March 6, 1872, Halstead, Essex, England - died April 5, Kensington, London, England) was a former British female tennis player. She is best remembered for reaching the finals at the 1908 and 1909 Wimbledon Championships.
