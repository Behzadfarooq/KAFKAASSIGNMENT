Agnetha Fältskog vol. 2 was the second solo-album by Swedish pop singer Agnetha Fältskog, recorded and released in 1969.

The album featured only two compositions by Agnetha Fältskog herself, "Zigernervän", was maybe the most well know track that caused some controversial discussions.

Most of the songs were written by Agnetha's boyfriend Dieter Zimmermann who also wanted Agnetha to record some songs in German to gain fans in German speaking countries. Agnetha was never successful there but still some songs were re-recorded in Swedish and included on this album like "Señor Gonzales" or "Wie der Wind" (Som en vind kom du till mej).
