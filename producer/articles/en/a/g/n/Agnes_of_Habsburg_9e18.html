Agnes of Habsburg

Agnes of Habsburg (ca. 1257 – 11 October 1322) was a daughter of Emperor Rudolph I of Germany and his first wife Countess Gertrude of Hohenberg.

In 1273 she was married to Elector Albert II of Saxony. They had six children:

  1. Duke Rudolf I of Saxe-Wittenberg (d. 11 March 1356) he was an Elector of Saxony
  2. Otto (d. after 29 August 1311)
  3. Albrecht (d. 19 May 1342), Bishop of Passau in 1322-1342
  4. Wenzel (d. 17 March 1327), a canon in Halberstadt
  5. Anna (d. 22 November 1327), married:
    1. on 8 August 1308 to Margrave Friedrich der Lahme, son of Frederick I, Margrave of Meissen;
    2. on 6 Jule 1315 Duke Henry II of Mecklenburg
  6. Elisabeth (d. 1341)
