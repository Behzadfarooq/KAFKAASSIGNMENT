Agnes of Burgundy

Agnes of Burgundy (1407- December 1, 1476) was the daughter of John I, Duke of Burgundy (1371-1419) and Margaret of Bavaria. Her maternal grandparents were Albert I, Duke of Bavaria and Margarete of Brieg.

[edit] Marriage and Issue

Agnes was married to Charles I, Duke of Bourbon (1401-1456), and together they had eleven children:

  • John II, Duke of Bourbon (1426–1488).
  • Mary of Bourbon (1428–1448).
  • Philip of Bourbon (1430–1440).
  • Charles II, Duke of Bourbon (1434–1488).
  • Isabella of Bourbon (1436–1465).
  • Peter II, Duke of Bourbon (1438–1503).
  • Louis of Bourbon (1438-1482).
  • Margaret of Bourbon (1439–1483).
  • Catherine of Bourbon (1440–1469).
  • Joanna of Bourbon (1442–1493).
  • Jacques of Bourbon (1445–1468).
