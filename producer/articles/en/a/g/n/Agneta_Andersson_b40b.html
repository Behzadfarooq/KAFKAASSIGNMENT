Agneta Andersson (born April 25, 1961) is a Swedish canoeist. Andersson was awarded the Svenska Dagbladet Gold Medal in 1996, jointly with Susanne Gunnarsson.

[edit] Medals

  • K1 - Olympic gold 1984
  • K2 - Olympic gold 1984 (with Anna Olsson)
  • K4 - Olympic silver 1984
  • K2 - Olympic silver 1992 (with Susanne Gunnarsson)
  • K4 - Olympic bronze 1992
  • K2 - World championships gold in Copenhagen 1993 (with Anna Olsson - Sweden's first female world championships gold in K-2 class)
  • K2 - Olympic gold 1996 (with Susanne Gunnarsson)
