Agnes (name)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Agnes
Gender: Female
Origin: Greek
Meaning: "Chaste"
"Sacred"


For other uses, see Agnes (disambiguation).

Agnes is a woman's given name, which derives from the Greek word hagnē, the feminine form of hagnos, meaning "chaste" or "sacred".
