Agnes von Krusenstjerna

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Agnes von Krusenstjerna (October 9, 1894 - March 10, 1940) was a Swedish writer.

Krusenstjerna was born in Växjö.

Krusenstjerna died in Stockholm.

[edit] Works

  • Fröknarna von Pahlen ("Miss von Pahlen", 1930-1935)
