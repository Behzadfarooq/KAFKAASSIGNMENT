Agnieszka Kotlarska

Agnieszka Kotlarska (c. 1970-August 27, 1996) is the first Polish winner of the Miss International beauty pageant in 1991.

She went on to pursue a modeling career in New York for the likes of Ralph Lauren and Calvin Klein, before returning to Poland to settle in Maślice.

Agnieszka was stabbed to death by a stalker in August 27, 1996. The attack also injured her husband, Jarosław Świątek.
