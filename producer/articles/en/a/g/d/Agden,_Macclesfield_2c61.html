Agden, Macclesfield

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Agden is a civil parish in the Borough of Macclesfield, Cheshire, England. It is near High Legh, and about fifteen miles south-west from Manchester City Centre. It is the site of Agden Hall.
