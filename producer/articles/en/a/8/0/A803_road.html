The A803 is a road in central Scotland. It runs from from Glasgow to Linlithgow.

[edit] Route

Beginning at Springburn in Glasgow, the A803 passes through the towns of Bishopbriggs, Kirkintilloch, Kilsyth, Banknock, Haggs, Bonnybridge, Falkirk, Laurieston, ending at Linlithgow.

It connects to the A80/M80 at Haggs.
