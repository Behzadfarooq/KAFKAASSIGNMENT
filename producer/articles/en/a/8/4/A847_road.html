The A847 road is one of the two principal roads of Islay in the Inner Hebrides off the west coast of mainland Scotland.

It connects Bridgend, at a junction with the A846 road, with Portnahaven at the southern end of the Rinns of Islay peninsula. It is some 14½ miles long.

[edit] Settlements on or near the A847

North to South

  • Bridgend
  • Bruichladdich
  • Port Charlotte
  • Nereabolls
  • Easter Ellister
  • Portnahaven
