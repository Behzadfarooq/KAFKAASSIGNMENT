[edit] Please watch your tone

I speak from experience when I tell you that you will end in ruin if you persist in "jabbering" in a hostile manner at other editors. I recommend you approach things this way:

  1. Smile.
  2. Bite yout tongue.
  3. Focus on your own editing.
  4. Document those issues which bother you on a notes page such as /problems.
  5. Do not exceed 3 reverts on any page in one 24 hour period.
  6. Don't try to "convince" editors who don't listen - instead, respond only to the issues they raise - not them personally.
  7. If you keep a list do not taunt editors by saying "you are on my list" or anything like that. Better to not even discuss your list.
  8. Keeping a list of "problems" will help you see the big picture and more easily discern between modest, ordinary SNAFUs and those editors/issues where closer inquiries would be in order.
  9. If you keeep a list, do not put threats or vulgarities on it ( ! ).
  10. Delete items from your list when they no longer bother you.

Rex071404 216.153.214.94 05:23, 23 October 2005 (UTC)
