The A1 Team France is the French team of A1 Grand Prix, an international racing series, and winner of the 2005/06 season of A1 Grand Prix of Nations.


[edit] Owners

A1 Team France owner is Jean Paul Droit.


[edit] Drivers

The main driver for the French team is Nicolas Lapierre, with Loïc Duval as his reserve.
