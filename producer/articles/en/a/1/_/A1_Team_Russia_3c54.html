The A1 Team Russia is the defunct Russian team of A1 Grand Prix, an international racing series.


[edit] Owners

A1 Team Russia owner was Svetlana Strelnikova.


[edit] Drivers

The drivers for the Russian team were Nikolai Fomenko, Alexey Vasiliev, and Mikhail Aleshin.
