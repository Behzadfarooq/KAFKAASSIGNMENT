A1 is an comics anthology published by Atomeka Press. It has recently started being published again with new and old material.

The first series (from the late 80's) comprised 6 issues, plus a 'Bikini Special'. Page count varied around the 128-192 range. Most stories were one-off showcases, sometimes featuring characters that had publishing history elsewhere (eg Concrete, Mr Monster). 'Bricktop' was the one ongoing serialised story, though the The Bojeffries Saga by Alan Moore and Steve Parkhouse appeared as self contained stories in every issue.

In 2004 Atomeka started publishing A1 again starting with A1 Big Issue Zero.
