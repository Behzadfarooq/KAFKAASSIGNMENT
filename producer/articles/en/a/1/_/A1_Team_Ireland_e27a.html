The A1 Team Ireland is the Irish team of A1 Grand Prix, an international racing series. Their franchise covers the island of Ireland, however, and not just the state.

[edit] 2005/06 Season

The team's 2005/06 car was presented to the public on 15 September 2005 [1]. They finished eight overall, on 68 points, competing in all races and scoring points in all but one.

[edit] 2006/07 Season

On the 8th September 2006 A1 Team Ireland unveiled their new livery & driver line-up for the 2006/07 season, as well as new sponsors for the team, eagarly awaiting the start of the new season. [2]

[edit] Drivers

The main driver for the Irish team for the 2006/07 season is Michael Devaney, with John O'Hara as Team Ireland's reserve driver.
