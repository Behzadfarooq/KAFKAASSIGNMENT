The A1 Team Czech Republic is the Czech team of A1 Grand Prix, an international racing series.

[edit] Owners

The A1 Team Czech Republic seat holder is Antonin Charouz.

[edit] Drivers

The drivers for the Czech Republic team are Tomas Enge, Jaroslav Janiš and Jan Charouz.

[edit] Race Performances

  • 20 November 2005, Malaysia: Third place in sprint race.
  • 2 April 2006, China: Won the feature race.
