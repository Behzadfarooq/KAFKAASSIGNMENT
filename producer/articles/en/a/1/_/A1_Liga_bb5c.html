A1 Liga

The A1 Liga is the highest level basketball league in Croatia. It is operated by the Croatian Basketball Federation.

[edit] Teams in the league for the 2006/2007 season

  • KK Alkar
  • KK Borik-Puntamika
  • KK Svjetlost
  • KK Cedevita
  • KK Cibona
  • KK Dubrava
  • KK Dubrovnik
  • KK Kvarner Novi Resort
  • KK Osijek 2006
  • KK Split C.O.
  • KK Šibenik
  • KK Zadar
  • KK Zagreb

Note: The letters 'KK' are similar to the 'FC' found in many football clubs' names. It stands for Košarkaški Klub (Basketball Club).
