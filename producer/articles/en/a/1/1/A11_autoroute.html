A11 autoroute

The A11 autoroute is a motorway which connects Paris with Nantes via Le Mans and Angers. It is called L'Océane .

[edit] Construction

The A11 is as yet unfinished. The by-pass of Angers, proposed for thirty years, is currently under construction. Its opening is proposed for August 31, 2008.

[edit] Regions crossed

The following list indexes the sites worth visiting within 14km of the road.

  • Paris
  • Chartres
  • Mans Parc de l'Image.
  • Laval
  • Angers
  • Nantes

[edit] History

  • 1972: Opening of the first section, La Folie Bessin - Thivars, is 68 km (A10 and A11).
  • 1975: Opening of the section Chartres to La Ferté Bernard.
  • 1978: Opening of the section La Ferté Bernard to Le Mans.
  • 1981: Opening of the section Angers-Nantes.
