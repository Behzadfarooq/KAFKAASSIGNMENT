A12 is a three-letter acronym that may refer to:

  • Highway A12, also known as Autoroute A12, a major highway in France
  • A12 road (England), a trunk road connecting London to Great Yarmouth
  • HMS A12, a British A class submarine of the Royal Navy
  • A12, a German rocket design in World War II that was never constructed: see Aggregate series#A12
  • One of the Encyclopaedia of Chess Openings codes for the English Opening in chess.

A-12 may refer to:

  • A-12 Shrike, a World War II-era American attack aircraft
  • A-12 Avenger II, a Cold War-era American aircraft project that was cancelled prior to production
  • A-12 OXCART, a high-altitude, high-speed reconnaissance aircraft manufactured by Lockheed Corporation for the CIA
  • Abrial A-12 Bagoas, a French experimental glider of the 1930s

A.12 may refer to:

  • Aero A.12, a Czechoslovak light bomber built after World War I
