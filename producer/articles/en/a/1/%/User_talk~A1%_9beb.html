[edit] Gold Coast (New Jersey)

I acknowledge your efforts on the Gold Coast (New Jersey) article, but some of the sources you have provided don't support the claims being made. Neither source on the most densely populated city or town backs up the claim. For Union City, the link lists New Jersey municipalities, not a ranking of all cities in the United States. The Guttenberg source uses a Wikipedia mirror, and it only says that it includes selected places, not all, nor is any independent source provided. I have searched in vain for months to find the sources to prove these claims. I will be more than happy to work with you to find appropriate sources, but I must remove them at this point. Alansohn 18:29, 25 February 2007 (UTC)
