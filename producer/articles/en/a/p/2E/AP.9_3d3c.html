AP. 9
Background information
Origin Oakland, California
Genre(s) Gangsta Rap
Label(s) Mob Shop Entertainment
Website http://www.ap9online.com/

Born in Oakland, California, AP.9 has been through it all in his life, from the murder of both his mother and little brother, to being shot 12 times and his numerous brushes with the law. But AP.9 has jumped every hurdle and obstacle thrown in his way and is a firm believer that whatever doesn't kill you only makes you stronger.

He has released albums in the group Mob Figaz, albums with Scoob Nitty and Gonzoe and several solo albums. He has a house in Las Vegas, Nevada where he currently resides. He can also be seen on an episode of A&E's Inked getting his famous "Mob Figaz" tattoo. [1] He is currently serving 16 months in jail for violating his probation. He also had 2 more felony charges. AP.9,who was close to Mac Dre offered a 10,000 USD reward out of his own pocket for anyone who had any information on the location of the tombstone. AP9 stated that he believes the theft was an isolated incident, and nobody involved with Hicks' murder was responsible.
