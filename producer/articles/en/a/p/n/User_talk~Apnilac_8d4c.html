APNILAC OWERRI 2006


Association for Promoting Nigerian Languages and Culture (APNILAC)11th Annual National Conference, 19th-23rd November, 2006.Conference Announcement and call for papers.

I N V I T A T I O N :

The Association for Promoting Nigerian Languages and Culture invites you to the 2006 Annual Conference.

Date: Sunday 19th Novemeber, 2006.....Arrival Monday 20th Novemeber 2006.........Opening Ceremony Thursday 23rd November, 2006.........Departure. Venue: Imo State University-Owerri. Theme: language, culture and endangerment.

Theme: language, culture and Endangerment.

SUB-THEMES

Issues: Languages Philosophy Education Democracy Gender Economic Development Literature Government/Public Policies Religion Int’l relations History Science and technology Music Resource Control Law Culture Computer/ICTS Foreign Languages Nigerian Languages Environment Sciences Mass Media Banking and finance Video Films Marketing and Advertising NGOs

CALL FOR PAPERS:

Intending participants at this conference are required to submit abstract(s) of not more than 200 words on any of the conference sub-themes or any issue on Language. Culture and endangerment. Please note that only papers on the suggested sub-themes and those closely related to the theme will be accepted for presentation. The abstract (s) should reach any of the underlisted persons not later than 31th October, 2006.

Prof. Jerome Ikechukwu Okonkwo Dean faculty of Humanities Imo State University, Owerri (Co-ordinator. APNILAC)

Calistus Anopue-Cussons C/o The dean’s Office, Faculty of Humanities Imo State University, owerri (National Secretary, APNILAC) +234-(0)8037237470.

OUR E-MAIL CONTACT ADDRESSES FOR THIS CONFERENCE: callycussons@Yahoo.com, apnilac4conference@yahoo.com. (Abstracts could be sent through these E-mail Addresses)

For any information on the conference you can call Calistus on +234-(0) 8037237470

Participants are expected to submit on arrival 30 copies of their papers(s) including a High density diskette or CD containing the article(s) written in (at least) “Windows 98’ programme. Only the (APA) referencing format should be used. All papers found publishable will be on the 9th volume of JONLAC (journal of Nigerian Languages and Culture).

N/B: JONLAC Volume 8, 2006 edition will be available for collection at the conference.

FEES PAYABLE: Conference Fee - N2,000. Vetting Fee - N1000. Membership Fees - N500. Annual Dues - N1,000.
