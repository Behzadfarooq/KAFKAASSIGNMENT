Many times a low-budget and low-interest serial TV show ends in tragedy. The bad guy gets the desired item, a character is left on a cliff, or everything just blows up.

[edit] What to Look For

[edit] No announcement

When these episodes come, the next one is not advertised.

[edit] Bad effects

If the special effects are especially horrible, but not on purpose, expect the show to be cancelled.

[edit] Bad plotline

If a plotline makes no sense, but not to the point where it is supposed to be funny, expect the show to end shortly.

[edit] Cheese

Cheese is a Chao that belongs to a tan-colored rabbit in an orange bell dress. The dubbed ending of Sonic X is very poorly put together, but provides a somewhat viable ending.

[edit] Parodies

This was even parodied on The Grim Adventures of Billy and Mandy and Futurama.
