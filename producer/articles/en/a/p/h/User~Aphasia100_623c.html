Hi, my name is Rich.

I have my own Wiki set up at: http://www.industrialdesignwiki.com - The Industrial Design Wiki I have created is aimed at Industrial Designers and Product Designers, to create a resource which might help the community to find links appropriate to research into specific ideas, materials, books, images.. well, pretty much anything that could help out the general designer. The idea is that people add links to their favourite resources giving a description when ever they find a useful link within the site.

[edit] Interests

I am a designer by trade, albeit a young one. I dream of creating product which function well and fulfil their place within souciety peacefully! I have an interest in Cradle to cradle design and materials technology as well as Sustainability, Photography, Psycology, New Technology, Industrial Design, The Bauhaus movement.
