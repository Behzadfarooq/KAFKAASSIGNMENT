Apsolutno Romantično is a Serbian rock/folk rock band found in Novi Sad in 1997. They are well known for their combination of traditional music of Vojvodina with rock music. They are known for corporation with Djordje Balasevic. Their most famous songs include: "Kao topli maj", "Ivana", "Djoletova pesma" (with Djordje Balasević), "Daj, Daj, Daj", "Noćas ću da verujem vinu", "Voli me ili me ostavi", "Dunave"...

[edit] Band members

Alvirović Petar - vocals, guitar
Alvirović Zoran Kina - guitar, vocals
Tasić Dragan - drums
Arapović Kemal Moki - bass guitar
