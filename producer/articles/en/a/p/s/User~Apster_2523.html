Apster would be me, also known as Alex (and a variety of other nicknames). I finally decided to join the ranks of Wikipedia users in July 2005 after deciding to rebel against my school's wishes that we not use Wikipedia for schoolwork.

I am a Taiwanese American (though commonly mistaken as Chinese), Italian American on my mother's side, born and raised in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania but now residing in Ambler. I am currently entering my sophomore year of high school at Chestnut Hill Academy.

I have a somewhat pathetic list of obsessions, including Wicked and Asian people.
