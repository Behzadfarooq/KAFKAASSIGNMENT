From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

I am Brazilian, and currently, I am living in Toronto, Canada. I very much like Geography, History and Brazilian music themes, and my contributions in Wikipedia are almost all Geo/Hist/Culture based. I have a special interest in education, urbanism and public transport. I am also starting to have some interest for politics.

I used to live in Foz do Iguaçu, where people of many different ethnies and cultures can be found - that is why, perhaps, that I like to learn new things about different cultures and different countries. I can speak, write and read English, French, Portuguese, Spanish and Italian.

I like soccer and multiplayer games, especially Counter-Strike and Starcraft; also, Sim City is very good too.

On the English Wikipedia, I work mainly in Brazilian articles, when I can - For now, I use the Portuguese Wikipedia more :)
