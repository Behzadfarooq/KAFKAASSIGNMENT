APS Netherlands

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

APS Netherlands, also known as the National Center for School Improvement, is a non-governmental, not-for-profit educational organization in Utrecht, Netherlands. Founded in 1969, it is comprised of approximately 130 educators from various professional backgrounds. APS International, founded in 2003, is their international group.
