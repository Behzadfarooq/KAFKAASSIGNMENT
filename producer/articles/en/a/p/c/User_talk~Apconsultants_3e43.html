Image:Ap.eartists.gif Anne Palumbo, owner of AP Consultants, has been designing & programming websites for small to mid-sized companies from dogs to nannies since 1995. She received her masters degree in Psychology in 1992 from Lincoln University, co-hosted a thirteen week radio talk show series: "Alcohol/Drugs and Society." WHRF/1520.am in 1993 and Appeared on Philadelphia Cable Network Television: "Personal Choices" to advocate personal growth methods in July of 1991. In addition, she has conducted seminars, taught courses, developed training materials and marketing proposals for a wide variety of companies throughout her career in psychology and as a web developer.

Anne Palumbo also owns AP Consultants Hosting, (APWH), offering animal lovers and small businesses affordable website hosting and The Animal News Network, (ANN). The ANN is a digital newsletter covering animal behavior, ethology, and articles on business growth, call for papers and other areas of interest for dog trainers, animal behaviorists, and all other animal lovers. In her spare time, she is revamping the Digital Stacks Library which should reopen soon.
Anne Palumbo is well known and liked in her community and valued for her good sense of humor, business acumen and clairty of vision regarding her work, love of animals and dedication to family and friends.
