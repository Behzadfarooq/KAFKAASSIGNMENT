APC-Talha has been designed and manufactured by Heavy Industries Taxila (HIT) using Ukrainian made engines. It is a modernised American M113-A2-Mk1.[1]

There are several provisions for firing ports and periscopes. The army of Pakistan plans to include 2,000 of these by year 2010.


[edit] Iraqi Interest

In November 2004 Iraqi defense Delegation paid visit to Pakistan and showed interest.They have ordered 44 of these.[2]
