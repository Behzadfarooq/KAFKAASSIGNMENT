The Asia-Pacific Council of American Chambers of Commerce (APCAC) was formed in 1968. APCAC represents 50,000 business executives, 20,000 organizations in 20 countries within the Asia-Pacific region.

Through 25 member chambers, APCAC represents the growing interests of over 50,000 business executives and over 10,000 business entities in 20 countries of the Asia-Pacific region. The APCAC membership manages trade volumes in excess of US$400 billion and direct investments (FDI) of over US$200 billion.
