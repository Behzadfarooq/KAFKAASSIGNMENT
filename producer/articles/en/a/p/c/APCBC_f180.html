APCBC

Main article: Shell (projectile)

APCBC is a kind of armour piercing shell.[1] During World War II Armour-Piercing Capped projectiles were found to be unstable in flight. So rounds were developed with a ballistic cap, called Armour-Piercing Capped Ballistic Capped.
