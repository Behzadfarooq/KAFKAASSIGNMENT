This course is for students interested in college-level work in economics. Study begins with fundamental economic concepts such as scarcity, opportunity costs, production possibilities, specialization, comparative advantage, demand, supply, and price determination. Major topics include measurement of economic performance, national income and price determination, and international economics and growth.

[edit] The exam

  • Section I: Multiple Choice [70 minutes - worth 66.7% of the exam grade]
  • Section II: Free-Response [60 minutes - worth 33.3% of the exam grade]
