Ap Lei Chau Bridge

Ap Lei Chau Bridge (鴨脷洲大橋) is a highway bridge in Hong Kong connecting the island of Ap Lei Chau and Aberdeen of the Hong Kong Island acrossing the .

This bridge was made up of two lanes for each direction across and later expanded to four lanes in 1994.
