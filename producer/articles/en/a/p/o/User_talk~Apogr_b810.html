Hi Apogr, I just noticed your comment on Talk:Balatonfüred. You don't necessarily need to put anything on the talk page for an article, unless you feel like it needs some justification or there is a debate—or, as in this case, you weren't logged in when you posted it. If you logged in, the article history will show that you were the one who wrote it or made the changes. If you accidentally post anonymously and it's a significant edit (or new article) mentioning it on the talk page is a good idea. —Tkinias
