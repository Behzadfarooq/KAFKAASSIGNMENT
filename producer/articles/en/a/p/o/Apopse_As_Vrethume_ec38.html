Apopse As Vrethume

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Apopse As Vrethume (Greek script: Απόψε ας βρεθούμε, English translation: "Let's Meet Tonight") was the Cypriot entry in the Eurovision Song Contest 1989, performed in Greek by Fani Polymeri and Yiannis Savvidakis.
The song was performed seventeenth on the night (following Spain's Nina with Nacida Para Amar and preceding Siwtzerland's Furbaz with Viver Senza Tei). At the close of voting, it had received 51 points, placing 11th in a field of 22.

The song is a love duet, with the singers pledging to "meet tonight" and describing the world they intend to create for themselves as a result.

It was succeeded as Cypriot representative in the 1990 Contest by Haris Anastasiou with Milas Poli.
