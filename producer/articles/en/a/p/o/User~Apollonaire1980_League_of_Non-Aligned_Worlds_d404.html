[edit] Interstellar Alliance

Upon conclusion of the Earth Alliance Civil War the League of Non-Aligned Worlds was dissolved and its member races join the new Interstellar Alliance. The political structure of the Interstellar Alliance is based upon the old League of Non-Aligned Worlds in some respects.
