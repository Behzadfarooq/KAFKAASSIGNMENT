Apocalypse can be:

  • Apocalypse, a Biblical event.
  • The Apocalypse tank, a fictional tank featured in the Westwood PC game Red Alert 2.
  • The New Testament Apocalypse, or Book of Revelation.
  • Apocalyptic literature, including:
    • Apocalypse of Peter.
  • Apocalypticism, a worldview found in both religious and secular settings.
  • Apocalypse (comics), a Marvel comic book supervillain.
  • Apocalypse, a heavy metal band active from the early 1980s to the early 1990s.
  • Apocalypse, a 2004 YA book written by Tim Bowler.
  • Apocalypse Please, a song by the British band Muse off their album Absolution.
  • Apocalypse Now, a film by Francis Ford Coppola.
  • Apocalypse Rising (DS9 episode), the fifth season premiere of Star Trek: Deep Space Nine.
  • Apocalypse, a film by Cloud Ten Pictures
  • Resident Evil: Apocalypse, a film by Alexander Witt.
  • Apocalyptic and post-apocalyptic science fiction.
  • Apocalypse, an expansion to the Magic: The Gathering collectible card game.
  • Apocalypse, a PlayStation video game by Neversoft starring Bruce Willis
  • Apocalypse, a 1974 Mahavishnu Orchestra album
  • X-COM: Apocalypse, a PC strategy game (Microprose, 1997)
  • ApocNation, a Web Based Strategy Game (GNU, 2001)
  • Apocalypse, a tower drop ride at Drayton Manor Theme Park, England.
  • 10.5: Apocalypse, a 2006 NBC miniseries depicting a series of catastrophic seismic disasters striking the United States.
  • Apokolips, the homeworld of the villain Darkseid in DC Comics.
  • Werewolf: The Apocalypse, a role-playing game from White Wolf
  • Apocalypto, Mel Gibson's 2006 film that depicts the decline of the Maya civilization.
