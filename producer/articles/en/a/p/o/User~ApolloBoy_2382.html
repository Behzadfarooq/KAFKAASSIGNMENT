Hello everyone, I'm ApolloBoy. I'm 17 years old and I live in San Jose, California. Don't let my age fool you though, I'm quite knowledgable about these subjects...

  • Analog synthesizers (I have three, an Akai AX-60, a Roland Jupiter-4 and a Roland JX-10)
  • Automobiles (especially Chrysler and Saab models)
  • Music
  • Video games

I also help edit articles dealing with these subjects. If you've got a question dealing with these subjects, please don't hesitate to ask me. Thanks!

Oh, and if I decide to take a Wikibreak "until further notice", don't be alarmed; I just get stressed out easily (due to my Asperger's Syndrome) and need some time to think things through. I'm not thin-skinned or anything, just stressed. And besides, it's always better to think responses through than just impulsively saying something, right?
