Apollo Papathanasio

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Apollo Papathanasio is a Greek-Swedish heavy metal vocalist, currently with the bands Firewind and Evil Masquerade. He has also sung for Meduza, Gardenian, and Richard Andersson's projects Majestic and Time Requiem.
