Apocalypse Nerd

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Cover of Apocalypse Nerd 1 ©Peter Bagge used with permission

Apocalypse Nerd is a six part comic book series created by Peter Bagge and published by Dark Horse Comics. It revolves around two men -Perry and Gordo, attempting to survive in the wilds of the Pacific Northwest after Seattle is destroyed in a North Korean nuclear attack.

The main character Perry is a somewhat introverted computer programmer whilst his longtime friend Gordo works in various "self-employed" fields and is also a drug dealer. The story is a masculine character study as the two evolve and adapt to the changes in the post-apocalyptic world. Gordo becomes more amoralistic while Perry learns survival skills. Both have unique personality traits which help them adapt.

The end of every main chapter is followed by a story on the American Founding Fathers. These are somewhat humorous and based on actual events.1
