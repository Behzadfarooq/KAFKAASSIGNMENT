Apocalyptica is the sixth album from cello rock group Apocalyptica, it was released in 2005.

[edit] Track listing

  1. "Life Burns!" (sung by Lauri Ylönen) – 3.06
  2. "Quutamo" – 3.28
  3. "Distraction" – 3.56
  4. "Bittersweet" (sung by Lauri Ylönen and Ville Valo) – 3.23
  5. "Misconstruction" – 3.56
  6. "Fisheye" – 4.09
  7. "Farewell" – 5.33
  8. "Fatal Error" – 2.59
  9. "Betrayal/Forgiveness" – 5.13
  10. "Ruska" – 4.39
  11. "Deathzone" – 10.15
  12. "En Vie" (sung by Manu)

[edit] Special edition tracks

  1. "How Far"
  2. "Wie Weit"

[edit] Notes

  • Track No. 1, "Life Burns!", was featured on the soundtrack of the video game Burnout Revenge.
  • The special version sold on the iTunes Music Store has three extra songs:
    • My Friend of Misery (Metallica cover)
    • South of Heaven (Slayer cover)
    • Mandatory Suicide (Slayer cover)
