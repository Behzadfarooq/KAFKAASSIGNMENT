[edit] Andrew J Porwitzky

I'm a PhD student in the University of Michigan department of Aerospace Engineering. My current research involves computer modeling of the plasma-propellant interaction encountered in ETC guns.

Apart from a general interest in all the Hard sciences, among my interests are Science Fiction television, books, and film.
