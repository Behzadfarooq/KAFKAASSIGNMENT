Apollon (Formula One)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Apollon was a Formula One team from Switzerland. They participated in one Grand Prix. In 1977, the Jolly Club of Switzerland bought a 1973 John Clarke-designed Frank Williams Racing Cars FW03. The car was renamed the Apollon Fly and was driven by Loris Kessel at the Italian Grand Prix. The car was hopelessly off the pace and crashed while qualifying. Loris Kessel was also the owner of Apollon. Before the Italian GP, Kessel tried to race in Belgium, France, Austria and the Netherlands. However, in Belgium and France, Kessel had transport problems, and for the other two races, the car could not be transported. The Ford Cosworth engine of the FW03 was kept, as were the Goodyear tires.
