[edit] Accomplishments and Size.

Concerning the orchestra: in almost all Texas competitions, a 4 rating is very bad: a 1 is almost universally the best, and 5 is the worst. I've never heard of a competition in north Texas that used a different system. If they were using a different system, maybe we should state "straight 4's: the best rating", or something like that. Concerning the size: The schools size in 2005-06 was about 800.
