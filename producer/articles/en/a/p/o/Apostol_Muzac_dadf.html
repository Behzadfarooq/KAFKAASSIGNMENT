Apostol Muzac, (born July 6, 1987 in Bucharest), is a Romanian football player, currently under contract with Steaua Bucharest.

He started his career playing for the youth teams of Dinamo Bucharest between 1995 and 2003 before joining Steauas Youth Academy.

During the 2005/06 season he was on loan to Apulum Alba Iulia where he played 14 games in the Liga II.
