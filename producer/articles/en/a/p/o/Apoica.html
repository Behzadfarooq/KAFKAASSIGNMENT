Apoica to a genus of wasps found in the Llanos region of Venezuela and other regions of South American tropical jungle. This wasp is truly nocturnal (one of only two species), carrying out its foraging activities after the setting of the sun. It prefers shelter under large leaves, shrubs, or hollow trees. The sting of the apoica is very intense, one of the most painful of any wasp sting. During the day, the hive fans tits collective group of wings to cool the overall temperature of the hiuve, keeping it at a suitable temperature for larvae development.

When attacking prey, the Apoica release a drop of venom from there stings, which in turn attracts the rest of the swarm to attck.
