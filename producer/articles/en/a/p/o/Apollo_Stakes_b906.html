Apollo Stakes

The Apollo Stakes is a Group 2 Australian thoroughbred horse race at Weight for Age run over a distance of 1400 metres at Rosehill Gardens Racecourse, Sydney in February. It was originally held at Randwick Racecourse until 2005.[1]
