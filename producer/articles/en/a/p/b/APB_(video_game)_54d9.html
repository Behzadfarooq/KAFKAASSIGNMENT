APB (All Points Bulletin) is an unreleased open-ended MMORPG based in urban sprawls and featuring two sides, the Squads and the Gangs. Players may join either the 'cops' or the 'robbers' side, and form sub-groups in these.

The concept was created by David Jones, creator of the original Grand Theft Auto franchise. Interestingly, A.P.B. is also the name of an earlier game that may have inspired Grand Theft Auto.
