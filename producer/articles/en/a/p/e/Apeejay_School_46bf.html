Apeejay schools is a group of school under the management of The Apeejay Education Society (AES).The schools are sponsored by industrial House of Apeejay Stya

All the Apeejay schools are members of the "National Progressive Schools Conference" (NPSC) and are affiliated to the Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE). They have been accredited to the British Council for London Examination Qualifications (for both GCE 'O & 'A' level courses).

Dr. Stya Paul is the Founder Chairman of the Apeejay Education Society.

Apeejay Education Society (AES) runs 13 schools and 14 institutions of higher learning across the country.
