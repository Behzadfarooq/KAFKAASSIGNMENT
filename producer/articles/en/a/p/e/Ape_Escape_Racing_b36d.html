Ape Escape Racing (Saru Get You: Pipo Saru Racer in Japan) is a racing game featuring the franctic monkeys from the Ape Escape series.

In Ape Escape Racing you will 'build' your monkey into a racing machine, by giving him an engine and tires. The game will feature 47 'vehicles', each in two forms: one specialized in drifting, one specialized in acceleration.
