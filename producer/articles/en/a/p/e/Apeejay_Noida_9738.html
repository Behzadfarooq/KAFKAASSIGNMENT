Apeejay School Noida' is a higher secondary school located in NOIDA.

[edit] History and Traditions

Apeejay Noida started functioning in 1981 in Chilla Village in East Delhi. Later, it shifted to its current campus in Sector 16-A, NOIDA. It has celebrated its Silver Jubilee, marking 25 years of its inception. It has been acknowledged as one of the most reputed schools of Noida and NCR. It has grown from strength to strength under three principals : Mr. Arora, Mr. Prashar and Mr. Hassija.

[edit] Co-curricular Activities

Apeejay has a long tradition of organising weekly activity classes in various fields. It has gained recognition for its efforts in nurturing the talents of its students ever since its inception as a fledgling institution in 1981. The following is the list of activity classes held in Apeejay Noida:

  • Swimming
  • Music
  • Dance
  • Instrumental Music
  • Vocal Music
  • Theatre
  • Extempore
  • Speeches
  • Home Science
  • English Debating
  • Hindi Debating

[edit] Important Events
