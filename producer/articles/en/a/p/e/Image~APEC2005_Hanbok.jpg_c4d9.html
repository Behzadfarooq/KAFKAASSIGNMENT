Description U.S. President Bush and Canada's Prime Minister Paul Joseph Martin break out in laughter Saturday, Nov. 19, 2005, as they stand in on the back riser for the official APEC photograph.

From left to right: President Hu Jintao of the People's Republic of China, President Alejandro Toledo of Peru, President Tran Duc Luong of Vietnam, Prime Minister Paul Martin of Canada, President George W. Bush of the United States, President Roh Moo-hyun of South Korea, President Ricardo Lagos of Chile, and Prime Minister Thaksin Shinawatra of Thailand.
