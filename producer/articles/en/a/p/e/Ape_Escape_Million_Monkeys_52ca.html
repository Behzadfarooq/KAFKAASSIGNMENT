Ape Escape Million Monkeys is a video game for the PlayStation 2. It is the fourth in the Ape Escape series.

To save the day, and the planet, players need to take on a mighty army of angry apes, hell-bent on world domination.

[edit] Characters

The story of the game features almost all preexisting characters from the Ape Escape games.

[edit] Playable

  • Kakeru/Spike
  • Natsumi/Natalie
  • Haruka/Helga
  • Charu/Casi
  • Hakese/The Professor
  • Specter
  • A team of SX Pipo-Monkeys, consisting of 6 different monkeys who help Specter on his levels. Their pants are yellow, green, black, red, blue, and aqua blue, respectively.
  • The Pipotron Monkeys, in a team of Pipotron Red, Pipotron Blue, and Pipotron Yellow
  • Pipotron G

[edit] Bosses

  • Mission 1: A giant robot controlled by Pipo Monkeys.
  • Mission 3: A giant trailer holding a laser beam cannon.
  • Mission 5: A Giant Robot with 4 jets and 2 chainguns.
  • Mission 10: A propelled plane that attacks you.
  • Mission 13: The same robot as Mission 1.
  • Mission 15: Specter (Team Kakeru only)
  • Mission 15: Pipotron Meta (Team Specter only)
  • Mission 16: Kakeru/Spike (Team Specter only)
  • Mission 16: Pipotron G (Team Kakeru only)
  • Mission 17: Virus Charu/Casi
  • Mission 20: Grid Core, a lot smaller than the one in Pumped and Primed.
  • Mission 21: A giant mutated robot that attacks you but gets away when defeated.
  • Mission 24: The same robot and fires a giant laser beam from its cannon at you.
  • Mission 29: The same previous robot from Mission 1, except covered and is mutated.
  • Mission 31 (Last Boss): A giant mutated half monster half robot. He can heal whenever his health is low.

[edit] Pipotron J's Gang

Pipotron J and the rest of them are a new species of Pipotrons in Ape Escape Million Monkeys. They star in the first season of Ape Escape anime.

  • Pipotron J: He is the leader of the new species of Pipotrons. He turned against his creator and killed him. He survived when Specter's battleship crashed into the sea. Nothing was left mentioning about later on in the game.
  • Pipotron G: He was a gorillia before he was turned into a Pipotron. Pipotron G saved Natalie and Casi when a giant chunk of the ceiling falls down. He didn't survived it. He is a playable character in Versus or Coliesum Mode.
  • Pipotron Kuratsuku: He is the genius of the gang. A true hacker. He created Virus Casi to stop Specter and Kakeru in Virtual Space. He Can Make Illusions In The Anime.
  • Pipotron Meta: He is the only Pipotron that can morph into anyone. He is one of the boss battles in Team Specter's story mode.
  • The man behind the puzzle:He created a new species of Pipotrons, after he fails to get the hands of Haruka/Helga's father's disk. He kills two of the new Pipotrons (excluding Pipotron G who got crushed by the falling ceiling) for failing to carry out his orders. He was strangled to the death by Pipotron J and went down with one of the battleships, that had been lit flame accidentally (he had been aboard it when he got strangled).

Virus Casi, Dark Specter, Dark Jake, Legend and Volcano requires a code to unlock them.

[edit] Bosses in Coliseum Mode

  • Volcano: A monkey that uses fire elemental weapons (World Tournament).
  • Legend: A monkey that attacks mainly by magic sword arts (Legend Tournament).
  • Dark Specter: The even more evil counterpart of Specter (Devils Tournament).

Note: You will get a special gadget if you beat Dark Specter.
