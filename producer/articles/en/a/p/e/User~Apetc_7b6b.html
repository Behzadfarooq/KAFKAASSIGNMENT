[edit] Residence

I currently live in Mesa, Arizona and have for just over 11 years.

[edit] Interest in Wikipedia

This is a fantastic resource for most anything I've needed to look up. Whenever I find that something I'm interested isn't available or if further information is available on something that does exist, I update it.

[edit] Interests

As far as school goes, I'm currently a college student, going towards a BS in Computer Systems Engineering. Somewhat tentative choice of major, but the frontrunner so far.

For work I do tech support and in-house projects for a large tech company. This includes Windows, Linux, Mac OS X and any of a variety of Unix-based systems on a regular basis.

Computers have been a long-time hobby of mine. For the most part, all my computers are home-built. The one major exception being my laptop, a Dell Latitude D810.

Between everything else, I try to occasionally play video games, particularly my PlayStation 2. I'm a big fan of rhythm games, such as Konami's Bemani series and play Dance Dance Revolution, Stepmania (a DDR clone) and BeatMania IIDX at home regularly. DDR and IIDX on the PS2, SM on a PC. I also frequent a local arcade which has a large selection of rhythm games available.

I am also a big anime fan. Current series include Naruto, Witch Hunter Robin, Bleach, Yakitate!! Japan and Ghost in the Shell: Stand-Alone Complex.

For music, I am a longtime industrial fan, with some of my favorite bands being VNV Nation, Assemblage 23, and Imperative Reaction.
