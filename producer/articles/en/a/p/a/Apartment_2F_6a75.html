Apartment 2F, often written Apt. 2F, was a 1997 MTV sitcom, sketch comedy, and stand-up television show based around Randy and Jason Sklar's characters living in New York City. The show aired from July 13, 1997 through November of 1997 with reviews including The Hollywood Reporter declaring that the Sklars' could be the Seinfeld of the late-1990s.

The show included appearances by a plethora of comedic actors, many of whom are well-known today, including Zach Galifianakis, Matt Price, Alanna Ubach, Michael Showalter, Thomas Lennon, Matt Walsh, Amy Poehler, Ian Roberts. Stephen Colbert, Paul Dinello, Jeffrey Ross, Lewis Black, Mary Birdsong, David Wain, Sam Seder, Patrice O'Neal, Patton Oswalt, Jim Norton, Tim Young, Ted Alexandro, Greg Behrendt, Godfrey, Marta Ravin, Mike Lee, and Jon Benjamin.

The writers included both Sklar brothers, Vito Viscomi, Mike Royce, Michael Lee, Brian Frazer, Eric Friedman, and Katty Biscone.
