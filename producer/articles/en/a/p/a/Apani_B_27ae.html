Apani, also known as Apani B. Fly Emcee, is a female MC from Hollis, Queens, New York. She is a founding member of the hip hop super-group Polyrhythm Addicts, along with fellow members DJ Spinna, Mr. Complex, and Shabaam Sahdeeq.

Apani emerged from New York City's underground hip hop scene in the 90's. Over the years, she has gained enough fame and status to work with some of the most revered names in hip hop music, including Da Beatminerz, DJ Spinna, Anti-Pop Consortium, Talib Kweli, and Pharoahe Monche. She has made a reputation for herself as a lyrical powerhouse and one of the best emcees to ever grace the mic - male or female.
