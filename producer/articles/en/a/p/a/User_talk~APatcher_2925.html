[edit] From Atomaton

Hi, and welcome to the Sexuality and Sexology project. I look forward to working with you on the variety of articles in our area. We are a small group, but slowly seem to get things done.

I saw that you did an undo of my removal of external links in the ejaculation article. Also, from reading your earlier comments I am aware that you are relatively new to this, and not familiar with wikipedia policies yet. The WP:EL it always an area of hot debate. In general most people feel that the use of external links should rarely be done, and that the important content from such links should be incorporated into the articles themselves. The specific links that I removed were pretty clearly defined in WP:EL as the kind of links we do not want in Wikipedia. ejacu.com has advertising for a variety of porn sites, and offers little to no value to the article. www.embryology.ch/anglais/dbefruchtung/bereitstell03.html looks interesting, but this is the kind of information that should be in the article, not referenced externally. Pretty much the prevailing thought is that articles should almost never have external links.

The same applies in the Exhibitionism and metrosexual articles. You added an external link there, instead of adding the content into the article. Generally we work to remove and prevent external links in articles, not to add them. Atom 13:03, 19 February 2007 (UTC)
