The comics about the little monkey named Apan Pongo were probably born in 2002. Apan Pongo has been included in the well known Swedish Comicbook Serieblandat. The comic is available for free on Apan Pongo's official website Apanpongo.inc.se.

[edit] Characters

[edit] Apan Pongo

The monkey Pongo is a nice little orangutang that is drawn by [Per Larsson]. You can recognize him by his big nose. Unfortunately he often gets into trouble, often caused by Wealthy Sven (Rike Sven in Swedish).

[edit] Wealthy Sven

Wealthy Sven is another character in the epic story about Pongo the Monkey. He's a small bird that always wears a partyhat. He doesn't speak often but when he does, he often screams "NOOO"

[edit] Elefanten (The Elephant)

The Elephant is Pongo's closest and only friend. We don't get to know much about him..

[edit] Hund (Dog)

The dog Dog is rather unknown but he's very suspicious.

[edit] The Game

There is a game made by the Swedish game developers Cinema Productions. You can download a demo free at the official Apan Pongo site.
