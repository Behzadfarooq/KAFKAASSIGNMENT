[edit] Starting the article

The Isreali Aparteid article made me want to think about what aparteid really was. And how appropriate quantitative comparisons can be made around the world.
Anything too long should be a separate article like the Israeli one.

[edit] Comparisons

Of course we can never agree on anything because as much as people like to say NPOV, most of the referenced articles are biased and POV... just that they are published.
Lets debate the merits if you will on comparison equally across the board though.

[edit] Included Sex, and Religion

Anytime blanket rights violations occur it appears to be in the three areas, tribal or cultural issues are race, and not a separate catagory.

[edit] Not sexual orientation

A case could be made for sexual orientation, but not since Nazi Germany has there been a forced rounding up of homosexuals en masse. Homosexuality is punishable by death in 9 countries around the world, and I suspect that they have problems not just with homosexuals for comparison. Please no one mention Gay or Lesbian marriage. It is a relationship right that is violated or allowed depending on your point of view (evil phrase) and again - the points of aparteid wouldnt apply to this article.

[edit] Apartness

This is the underlying thought of Aparteid, my people here, your people there. We can see it around the world, but is it really Aparteid. Is it wide spread, government sanctioned, how many of the six points does it meet? Should it be 7 points? or 3?

[edit] Israel

I read the anti-semetism discussion, and the Israel-Aparteid discussion, and all I could do is look at the facts. Normally an area like this would be forceable cleansed (like the Kurds in Iraq), or there would be a genocide (armenians in Turkey), or maybe just killed in general (Tutsi's in Rwanda). But it didnt happen. The occupied territories (or as the Israelis prefer... the disputed territories) were never a nation to be conquered, people were offered citizenship and they refused, there is a political party in power that is for wiping Israel out... granting them freedom of movement would make it a better place?
In comparison to most countries in the world, Israel is a better place than most for its citizens, and that is one of the Aparteid situations.... against its own people. The Palestinians dont view themselves as Israelis, nor do the Israelis. The comparisons to Aparteid are stretched and weak. That it is a terrible situation to be living in, I dont believe is in dispute. Who is right who is wrong.... but it's not aparteid.
