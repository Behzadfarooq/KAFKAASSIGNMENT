Apalachee Parkway is a major east-west thoroughfare in Tallahassee, Florida, USA.

Designated as part of US 27, the route starts at Monroe Street in front of the Florida State Capitol building. It has a short "semi-freeway" section just east of the capitol, then is a busy four-lane surface boulevard with service roads for the next few miles, passing the Governor's Square Mall and many state office buildings.
