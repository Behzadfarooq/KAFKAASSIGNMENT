Apistidae is a family of fish that includes the waspfishes. They are 19 - 20 cm in length.

[edit] Species

  • Genus Apistops
    • Shot-Armed Waspfish, Apistops caloundra (De Vis, 1886)
  • Genus Apistus
    • Ocellated Waspfish, Apistus carinatus (Bloch & Schneider, 1801)
  • Genus Cheroscorpaena
    • Cheroscorpaena tridactyla (Mees, 1964)
