Application is a sketch written by Harold Pinter. It was originally written in 1961 as a radio sketch and was first broadcast on BBC Radio 3 in 1964.

[edit] Synopsis

Spoiler warning: Plot and/or ending details follow.

Applying for a job, a young man is given a bizarre psychological test which leaves him gasping, blushing and rolling on the floor in shock.
