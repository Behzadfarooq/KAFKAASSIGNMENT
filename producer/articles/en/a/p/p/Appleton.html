Appleton

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Appleton can refer to:

  • Appleton (music duo), a UK-based musical group with Canadian-born members.
  • Appleton Estate, a Jamaican producer of rum.

Appleton is the surname of the following people:

  • Alistair Appleton, British television presenter
  • Edward Victor Appleton, English physicist, known for his research on the ionosphere.
  • Natalie Appleton, British/Canadian singer
  • Nathan Appleton, American merchant and politician
  • Nicole Appleton, British/Canadian singer
  • Richard Appleton, English Engineer, involved in Telecommunications.
  • Norman Appleton, British/Australian Industrialist

Appleton is also the name of these places:

  • Appleton, Wisconsin, United States
  • Appleton, Maine, United States
  • Appleton, Minnesota, United States
  • Appleton, Newfoundland and Labrador, Canada
  • Appleton Thorn, Warrington, Cheshire, England
  • Appleton Wiske, N Yorks, England
  • Appleton,Oxfordshire, England
  • Appleton le Moors, Yorkshire, England
  • It is also the fictional city where most of the characters (most notably Cliff Yablonski) of the Something Awful website live.
