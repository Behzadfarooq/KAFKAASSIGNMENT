Apparition

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Wiktionary, the free dictionary.

Generally, an apparition is the act or instance of appearing.

Apparition may also refer to:

  • An apparitional experience of a supernatural being such as:
    • a ghost
    • God
    • Satan
  • an appearance of a living person not physically present by supernatural means, such as:
    • a doppelgänger
    • someone experiencing bilocation
  • Marian apparitions, appearances of the Virgin Mary, celebrated by the Roman Catholic Church
  • Apparition and Disapparition in Harry Potter, a form of teleportation used in the Harry Potter novels
