Apple Box is a boxed set by XTC compiling Apple Venus Volume 1 and Wasp Star (Apple Venus Volume 2) along with their respective demo albums Homespun and Homegrown. It is the third boxed set of their career following Transistor Blast: The Best of the BBC Sessions and Coat of Many Cupboards.
