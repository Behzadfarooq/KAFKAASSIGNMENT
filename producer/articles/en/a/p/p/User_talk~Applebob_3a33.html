Vandal reporting

Thank you for you efforts in reporting vandalism atWikipedia:Administrator intervention against vandalism. It would be helpful if you would include a short description of the vandal's activities (see the instructions on that page for more information). Happy editing. No Guru 20:17, 25 April 2006 (UTC)

Also, don't forget to substitute vandalism warnings, use the format {{subst:test}}, for example. Thanks for your help! Prodego talk 20:19, 25 April 2006 (UTC)

Reporting users to AIV

Hi, I am glad you are helping fight vandalism! Just remember to only report vandals if they have recently vandalized and have vandalized more than four times (of course that can be bent a little if they are doing something very serious). subst warning messages

Hi, remember to add subst: before your warning messages. This allows the text to be substituted so it doesn't have to look for the template each time talk pages are displayed>>> {{test}} should be written as {{subst:test}}. Thanks and happy editing!--Kungfu Adam (talk) 02:23, 26 April 2006 (UTC)
