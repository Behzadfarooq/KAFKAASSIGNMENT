Apple Blossom Handicap

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Apple Blossom Handicap is an American Thoroughbred horse race for fillies and mares age three and up run each year during the Racing Festival of the South at Oaklawn Park in Hot Springs, Arkansas.

The race is run over a distance of one and one-sixteenth miles on the dirt. With a purse of $500,000 and Grade I status, the race is a premier event for distaffers in the Spring.
