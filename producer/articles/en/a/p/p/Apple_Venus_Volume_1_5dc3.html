Apple Venus Volume 1 is an XTC album released in 1999. It was the first on the band's own Idea Records. It reached #42 in the UK and spawned two singles: "I'd Like That" and "Easter Theatre." "I'd Like That" appears on the soundtrack for the Jim Carrey film Me, Myself and Irene.

Dave Gregory left the band before the release.
