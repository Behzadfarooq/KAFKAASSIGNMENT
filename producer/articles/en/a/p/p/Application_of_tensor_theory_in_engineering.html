Application of tensor theory in engineering

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Tensors are frequently used in engineering to describe measured quantities.

[edit] Common applications

  • Measuring deformations (finite deformation tensors) and strain (strain tensor) in continuum mechanics
  • Representing diffusion as a tensor in Diffusion tensor imaging
