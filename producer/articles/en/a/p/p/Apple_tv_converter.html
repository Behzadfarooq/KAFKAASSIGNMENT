Apple TV Converter Suite
Developer: Wondershare Software, Ltd
Latest release: 1.0.0 / February 15, 2007
OS: Windows
Use: Video Converter
License: Shareware
Website: Apple TV Converter Suite Home Page

Apple TV Converter Suite is a Windows application that copies DVDs and convert Video to MPEG-4 or H.264 format for playback on Apple Apple TV. It is produced by Wondershare Software, Ltd, the direct vendor of multimedia video and audio solutions. Due to ease of use, depth of useful options and stable functions, Apple TV Converter Suite has become popular among users worldwide. With this Apple TV Converter, a user can extract audio tracks from DVD or Video and save as MP3, AAC, M4A format which can be loaded into an MP3 player. You also can trim or crop video, watch a movie on Apple TV in full screen.

[edit] Features

  • Auto rips DVDs from DVD-Rom or hard disk to Apple TV support video format MPEG-4 or H.264.
  • Convert video from Windows Media Player (WMV), Realplayer (RM, RMVB), AVI (DivX, XviD), QuickTime (MOV), Youtube (FLV), VCD (DAT), MPG, ASF to MPEG-4 or H.264 for Apple TV.
  • Extract audio signal from DVD or Video and save as MP3, WMA, or M4A format.
  • Support output resolution from 640*480 to 1280* 720 for palyback on Apple TV.
  • Video Trimming: cut black margin of DVD or Video, view with full screen.
  • Video effect adjusting: modify the movies brightness, saturation and contrast.
  • Partial conversion supported: get your desired segment to encode.
  • Support converted by selecting chapters you want.
  • Support photo capturing of the playing video,and save as jpeg or bmp format.
  • Support display ratio converted in widescreen(16:9)/4:3/full screen/keep original mode.
  • Estimate output files size, allow you to select target subtitle and audio track.

Apple TV Converter Suite runs under Windows 98/2000/XP/2003
