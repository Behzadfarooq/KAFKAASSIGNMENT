Appius Claudius Pulcher was the name of several members of the Claudii during the Roman Republic:

  • Appius Claudius Pulcher, consul of 212 BC
  • Appius Claudius Pulcher, consul of 185 BC
  • Appius Claudius Pulcher, politician of the 2nd century BC and consul 143 BC
  • Appius Claudius Pulcher, born by this name, but changed it later after his adoption by Marcus Livius Drusus
  • Appius Claudius Pulcher, consul of 79 BC and praetor of 88 BC.
  • Appius Claudius Pulcher, son of previous, praetor of 57 BC and consul of 54 BC
  • Appius Claudius Pulcher, adoptive nephew of previous and consul in 38 BC
