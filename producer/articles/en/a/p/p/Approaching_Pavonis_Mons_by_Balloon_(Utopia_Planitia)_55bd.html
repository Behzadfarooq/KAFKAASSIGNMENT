Approaching Pavonis Mons by Balloon (Utopia Planitia)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

For the Martian location, see Utopia Planitia.

"Approaching Pavonis Mons by Balloon (Utopia Planitia)" is the eleventh and final track on The Flaming Lips' 2002 album Yoshimi Battles the Pink Robots. It won the 2003 Grammy for Best Rock Instrumental Performance. At 3 minutes and 9 seconds long, it features, according to the CD insert, "strange talking and screaming."
