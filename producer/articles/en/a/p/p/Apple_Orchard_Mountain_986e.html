Apple Orchard Mountain is the highest point along the Blue Ridge Parkway in Virginia, United States. Located in Jefferson National Forest, Apple Orchard Mountain is the county highpoint for both Bedford County and Botetourt County, Virginia. It is also the most topographically prominent mountain in the state. The summit is open, and an FAA radar stands nearby. This radar makes the mountain recognizable from miles away.
