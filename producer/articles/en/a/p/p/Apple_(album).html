Apple is the one and only full-length album by Mother Love Bone. It was originally released posthumously in 1990 following the death of frontman Andrew Wood. The entire album later appeared on the 1992 compilation Mother Love Bone (also known as Stardog Champion). It was also remastered and reissued in 2003 by the UK-based Lemon Recordings.
