[edit] Proposed Merger

The title here is self-explanatory: Should Appa have his own article? I mean, he had an entire episode. User:Kochdude388 01:58, 2 November 2006 (UTC)

Err, that one is a tough one. Technically he could, but it would mostly be plot summary. Because he doesn't actually talk there isn't much to say about his personality or anything similar. H2P (Yell at me for what I've done) 02:43, 2 November 2006 (UTC)

I also think that the Long Feng should have it's own page I mean he is one of the bad guys in the last episodes.--Coallen 13:31, 15 November 2006 (UTC)

I think yes --Coallen 13:28, 15 November 2006 (UTC)

  • I don't see any reason why he shouldn't...Azula's got her own page, and Appa's probably had just as much of an impact on the series as she has. Although Appa doesn't say anything, there's a lot that can be drawn from his relations with the other characters, and there have easily been four episodes (The Chase, The Desert, Appa's Lost Days, and Lake Laogai) where a large portion of the storyline centers on him. If you look on Appa's section in the major secondary characters page, you'll see that there's a lot of stuff to be added anyways. If someone's willing to make the transfer, I say go for it. Y BCZ 04:12, 2 November 2006 (UTC)
I'm on it. User:Kochdude388 22:08, 2 November 2006 (UTC)
I really don't think its necessary...our character pages are mostly plot summaries as it is. If anything, they need to be pruned, not have more made.--Fyre2387 (talk • contribs) 22:20, 2 November 2006 (UTC)
Agreed. Appa may have had a short story of his own recently, but I do not believe that's enough to boost him into "main character" status. H2P brings up a great point in that there really isn't much else to go off of other than the events in "Appa's Last Days" because he doesn't have much to talk about in the way of a personality, relationships, etc. Having an entire episode isn't much when put in perspective of the entire series, where the main characters have had almost 30 episodes to actively develop and grow (as opposed to serve mainly as a taxi service). I just don't see it as necessary to split off this page. –Prototime (talk • contribs) 07:11, 4 November 2006 (UTC)

I agree, Appa has appered in more episodes than most secondary characters, and it would be benificial to explain the relationships he has with other characters. Momo, on the other hand, could stay without his own article.--Piemanmoo 18:47, 4 November 2006 (UTC)

I recently added to the article. I mention it because it was a drastic one. Perhaps with this description, it will allow Appa's role to be further shown, yet still keep him on this page. (61.91.191.2 13:35, 9 November 2006 (UTC))

I have created an article on him, using the standard for most characters. Its just a prototype so delete it if you see fit. I really won't mind. It does need serious editing though if its going to stay. Scaleylizardman 20:15, November 15 2006 (UTC)
