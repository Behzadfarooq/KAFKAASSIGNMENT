Apple Town Story (アップルタウン物語, Apple Town Story?) is a port of the 1985 Activision computer game Little Computer People to the Famicom Disk System. The port was release by Squaresoft of Final Fantasy fame in 1987. The game consists of watching an animated little girl wander around her house and play with her cat. The game offers little interactivity, and there is little plot or actual gameplay. The game is seen as a precursor to Tamagotchi, Pokémon, and dating sims.
