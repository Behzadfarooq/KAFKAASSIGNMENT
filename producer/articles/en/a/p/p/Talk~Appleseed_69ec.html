[edit] Deunan's Name

According to the first paragraph, Deunan's name wasn't spelled "Deunan" until the 2004 movie. This is incorrect. The movie name matches with the much older translation of the manga by Dark Horse Comics. I've removed the reference to "Dunan Nuts" as a result. I'm not sure where the latter name came from, though I'm guessing it was either the official release of the Appleseed OVA, or a fansub of the same. --Junior612 18:33, 26 March 2007 (UTC)
