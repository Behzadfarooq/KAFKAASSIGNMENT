Applix Inc. is a software company that publishes TM1, a MOLAP database server, and related presentation tools, including Applix Executive Viewer.

Many of the applications of TM1 are in the fields of business intelligence and business performance management. Typically, TM1 helps businesses undertake in-depth analysis on large sets of financial or operational data, in order to discover trends and make better business decisions.

In addition to analysis and reporting applications, many collaborative planning, budgeting and forecasting solutions are based on TM1 because of its unique real-time write-back and calculation capabilities, as well as its seamless integration with Microsoft Excel.
