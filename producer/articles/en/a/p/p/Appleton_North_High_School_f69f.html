Appleton North High School

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Appleton North High School is a comprehensive secondary school in Appleton, Wisconsin. It is part of the Appleton Area School District. Opened in August 1995, it is the most recently built of the three four-year public schools in the district. The school has roughly 1800 enrolled students. The school colors are gold, silver, and navy blue. The school's nickname is the Appleton North Lightning. The theatre department is well-known in the Fox Valley for the musicals that they produce. Some of the musicals the department has done are Aida, Les Misérables , and most recently The Secret Garden. The One Act Team has won the prestigious Critics Choice Award eight years in a row at the Wisconsin High School Theater Festival. For the 2006 Festival, the school did a One Act of Hamlet. The spring drama for the 2006-2007 school year will be The Arabian Nights. The head of the department is Ron Parker.
