Apple (Suikoden)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Official artwork of Apple by Konami from Suikoden III

Apple is a recurring character in the Suikoden video game series. She appeared in Suikoden I, Suikoden II, and Suikoden III. A student of strategy, Apple studied under Mathiu Silverberg along with Shu. She showed a true knack for strategy and by the time of the Second Fire Bringer War had taken on a pupil in Caesar Silverberg.
