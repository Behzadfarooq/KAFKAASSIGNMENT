[edit] Fair use image

I removed the image of the iTunes icon, as it was a violation of Wikipedia's fair use policy. Please remember that fair use images can only be used in the article main spaces (the main page though is an exception)--☆TBC☆ (aka Tree Biting Conspiracy) 12:18, 10 June 2006 (UTC)

Really? I thought that because Portals are reader-facing they are okay. Let me check that...--HereToHelp 13:20, 10 June 2006 (UTC)
The word "portal" appears nowhere in the fair use or logo guidelines.--HereToHelp 13:24, 10 June 2006 (UTC)
The policy section states that "fair use images should only be used in the article namespace". Portals are not considered part of the article namespace thus should not include fair use images.--☆TBC☆ (aka Tree Biting Conspiracy) 09:22, 11 June 2006 (UTC)
That's very counterproductive, how are we supposed to highlight something if the appropriate image cannot be used? Dread Lord CyberSkull ✎☠ 12:13, 13 June 2006 (UTC)

[edit] Move to "Apple Inc."

I felt that since the topics covered by this portal dealt more with the company than the Macintosh line of computers, the name of the portal should be changed. Ordinarily, I would have suggested the move on the talk page and the related Wikiproject, but both appeared to be inactive. I think that I have properly moved all of the appropriate pages. This portal will be one of my main focuses for a while, so if you disagree with my rational, please let me know. I appreciate any feedback. Thanks a lot, —W.
