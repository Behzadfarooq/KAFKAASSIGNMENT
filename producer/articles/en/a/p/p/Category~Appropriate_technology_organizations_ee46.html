Category:Appropriate technology organizations

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

This category includes organizations and educational departments which have a major focus on Appropriate technology. It does not necessarily mean that they all hold similar views on this topic.

Many other organizations also use or advocate such technologies, although they may not be a major focus. In particular, see Category:Development charities.

For individuals and publications, see Category:Appropriate technology advocates.

For information on the technology itself, see Appropriate technology and Category:Appropriate technology.
