The PenLite was an early tablet PC prototyped by Apple Computer in 1993 around the same time as the Apple Newton. It was not a PDA but rather a full-blown computer. The project was canceled in 1994 due to the similarity to the Newton.[1]

[edit] Design

The Penlite was based off of the PowerBook Duo and was meant to be a tablet-style addition. It was designed to be compatible with PowerBook Duo docks and accessories.[2] It used a stylus and had a built-in floppy drive. It ran the normal Mac OS.
