Appalachian Airlines was a commuter airline that operated from 1977 to 1980.

[edit] Former Destinations

  • Tennessee
    • Johnson City/Bristol/Kingsport (Tri-Cities Regional Airport)
  • Virginia
    • Wise (Lonesome Pine Airport)*
  • West Virginia
    • Beckley (Beckley Raleigh County Memorial Airport)
    • Bluefield (Mercer County Airport)
    • Charleston (Yeager Airport)

Those airports marked with an asterisk (*) are no longer served by scheduled airline service.
