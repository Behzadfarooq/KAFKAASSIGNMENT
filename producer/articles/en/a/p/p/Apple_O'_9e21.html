Apple O' is Deerhoof's fifth LP, and is considered by many to be one of the best indie pop records of 2003. It was released on Kill Rock Stars and 5 Rue Christine (an offshoot of Kill Rock Stars), and is also Deerhoof's first album with guitarist Chris Cohen.
