Appearances and Other Deceits is an episode from the first season of the television series Crusade.

[edit] Plot synopsis

Spoiler warning: Plot and/or ending details follow.

Two Earthgov representatives arrive to imrpove the ship's image when an alien being spread through people by touch arrives on the ship.

[edit] Arc Significance

  • The 'new' crew uniforms are introduced.

[edit] Trivia

  • The uniforms introduced as being 'new' in this episode were in fact old, the black uniforms seen in the previous episodes having been designed in real life after episode 105 to replace them.
  • Stephen Furst, who directed this episode was a regular actor in Babylon 5.
  • The character of Mr. Welles was a member of President Clarke's EarthGov and head of the Night-Watch Division. Hence his comment that "if anyone can cope with political change. It's me"
