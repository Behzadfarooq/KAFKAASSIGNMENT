Appoooh is a wrestling arcade game released by Sega in 1984 only in Japan.

Each wrestler is a parody from a real-life wrestler:

  • G.Babu - Giant Baba
  • H.Hogen - Hulk Hogan
  • A.Inoke - Antonio Inoki
  • S.Hanson - Stan Hansen
  • Tigerman - Tiger Mask
  • A.Giants - Andre the Giant
  • U.Uma - Ueda Umanosuke
  • A.Buchie - Abdullah the Butcher
