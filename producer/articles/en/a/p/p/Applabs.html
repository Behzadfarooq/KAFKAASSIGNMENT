AppLabs Technologies is a global IT services company headquartered in Philadelphia, PA, with its engineering facility located in the South Indian city of Hyderabad. Applabs specializes in Quality Management, Software Testing, and Certification Solutions. In 2005, AppLabs acquired Keylabs and IS Integration in 2006. With these acquisitions, it has got more than a decade of experience behind it. It has partnered with more than 600 companies, providing both quality assurance and third-party validation also known as IV&V (Independent Verification & Validation). AppLabs is a typical Indian outsourcing company starting off with a small team and cashing on the outsourcing trend in the US. Now with Keylabs and IS Integration under its wings, it has got the technical expertise as well to go along with cost advantage when it comes to outsourced IT (Information Technology) services. It offers its customers risk mitigation processes and capability to deliver its services globally. The company maintains testing facilities in the US, India and the UK.

[edit] History

Sashi Reddi

AppLabs was founded in April 1998 by Sashi Reddi, Chairman & CEO, AppLabs Technologies. A serial entrepreneur, Sashi has started two other companies prior to AppLabs. Sashi received his BTech (B.S.) degree in computer science from Indian Institute of Technology in Delhi, and later, an M.S. in computer science from New York University and a Ph.D from The Wharton School, University of Pennsylvania.

According to the company’s official website, his first company was EZPower Systems, a developer of products for building and maintaining large-scale web applications. EZPower was acquired by DocuCorp International (NASDAQ: DOCC), a safeguard scientifics company, and Sashi went on to serve as senior VP. Following DocuCorp, he founded iCoop to provide group purchasing software to serve the specific needs of co-operative businesses such as Dunkin Donuts. He began his career consulting on technology and strategy to Fortune 500 companies in the travel, financial services, automotive and consumer packaged goods industries.

[edit] Services

  • Software QA
  • Hardware Certification Services

AppLabs offers Software Testing services like Functional, White box testing, Compatibility, Performance Testing, Load Testing, Penetration Testing, Security Testing, Test Automation and test engineering for both hardware and software. Development services are aimed at companies’ software product development, application customization and the need for support of ongoing maintenance. The Hardware Certification services started with the recent acquisition of Keylabs. Applabs runs certification programs for Cisco, Novell, Sun Microsystems and RealNetworks and specialized certification for Linux and Microsoft Pre-Testing.
