Appleby Matthews

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

T. Appleby Matthews (born 1881 or 1882, died Moseley, Birmingham, June 22 1948)[1] was an English conductor and organist. He served as organist of Birmingham Cathedral.[1] In 1920, he became the first conductor of the City of Birmingham Orchestra (today the City of Birmingham Symphony Orchestra). He gave the first complete public performance of Holst's The Planets suite in Birmingham that same year.
