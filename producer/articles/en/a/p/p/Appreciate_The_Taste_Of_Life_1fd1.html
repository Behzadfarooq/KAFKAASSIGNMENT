Appreciate The Taste Of Life (賞味人間) Seung2 Mei6 Yan4 Gaan1 is Candy Lo's (盧巧音) 6th studio album. It was released on July 7, 2002. For this album Candy Lo worked together withwith Hong Kong producer Kubert Leung (梁翹柏) with whom she worked on previous albums, such as Fantasy, as well.

There are two editions of this album. The first has only one disc and a VCD with the music videos for 3 of the album's songs. The second version (pictured) contains an extra disc with four different versions of Break-Up With Good Intentions (好心分手) of which the the first track is a a duet version featuring Lee Hom Wang (王力宏). The other three are remixes of the original solo version. The second version also comes with a VCD. This one is different than the one with the first version because it has an extra music video, namely the version of Break-Up With Good Intentions featuring Lee Hom Wang.
