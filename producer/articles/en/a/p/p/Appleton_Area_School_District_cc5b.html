Appleton Area School District

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Appleton Area School District is a school district which serves Appleton, Wisconsin. As of 2004, 15,275 pupils were enrolled at member schools.[1] As of 2006, the Superintendent of Schools is Dr. Thomas G. Scullen.[2]
