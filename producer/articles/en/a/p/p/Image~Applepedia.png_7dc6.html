Proposed logo for the Wikipedia:WikiProject Macintosh. Created by User:grm_wnr and based on Image:Wikipedia-logo.png This image is tagged as fair use because I'm not sure if there's a better tag available. It is the Wikipedia logo modified to resembly the Apple Computer logo - both are copyrighted and trademarked. However, this should be an acceptable fair use in any context (not just articles), since it's a parody (both ways), and it's even not just a straight copy of a copyrighted work used in a parodistical context. Its use does not detract from the involved parties' ability to make profit from their copyright either. Additionally, it is intended to replace a fair use logo. If someone knows of a more applicable tag, please add it (Tags considered and not used because of one reason or another include {{logo}}, {{copyright by Wikimedia}} and any free licenses that the creator may be able to grant).
