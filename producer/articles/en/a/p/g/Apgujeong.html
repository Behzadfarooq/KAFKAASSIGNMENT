Apgujeong

Apgujeong (Hangul: 압구정 Hanja: 狎鷗亭), also spelled Apkujong is an important fashion and shopping district in Gangnam-gu of Seoul serviced by Apgujeong Station. It contains many upscale department stores, shops, and boutiques, such as Prada, Gucci, and Giorgio Armani.

Apgujeong is well-known to be one of the centers of social gatherings among college students as well as foreign students in Seoul.
