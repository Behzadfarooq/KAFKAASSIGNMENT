Apr. 14 - Eastern Orthodox Church calendar - Apr.16

All fixed commemorations below celebrated on Apr. 28 by Old Calendarists

[edit] Saints

  • Martyr Sabbas the Goth of Walachia
  • Martyrs Basilissa and Anastasia of Rome, disciples of Apostles Peter and Paul
  • Saint Ruadhan, abbot and bishop in Ireland
  • Saint Mscislav-Theodore, Prince of Kiev
  • Martyr Suchias and those with him in Armenia
  • Saint Leonidas of Athens, bishop
  • Martyrs Presbyter Theodore and Pausilippus
  • Martyr Crescens

[edit] Other Commemorations

  • Repose of Blessed Daniel of Siberia (1843)
  • Repose of Hiero-schemamonk Michael, the last Elder of Valaam (1962)
