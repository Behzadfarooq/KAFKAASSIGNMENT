April Twenty-Five F.C. is perhaps the most successful football (soccer) team in North Korea with over 10 wins the DPR Korea League Cup. They play in Namp'o and their stadium is Nampo Stadium. [1]

[edit] Achievements

  • DPR Korea League: 10
