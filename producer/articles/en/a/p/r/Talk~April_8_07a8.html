April 8: Easter in Christianity (2007); Hanamatsuri in Japan.

  • 1767 - The Ayutthaya kingdom fell to Burmese invaders.
  • 1904 - France and the United Kingdom signed the Entente Cordiale.
  • 1904 - Longacre Square in Midtown Manhattan was renamed Times Square (pictured).
  • 1953 - Jomo Kenyatta was convicted of being a Mau Mau leader.

[edit] Grace Park (BSG Actress)

Grace Park the actress on Battlestar Galactica was born the same day (April 8, 1980) as Katee Sackhoff. This is from an interview with Katee Sackhoff on SciFi Talk. There is another Grace Park (the golfer) born March 6, 1979 and many web sites confuse the two (obviously doing a quick Google).
