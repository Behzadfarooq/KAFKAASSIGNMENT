[edit] Biography

April began her career as an exotic dancer, she discovered exotic dancing during a contest held at a local club in Anchorage, Alaska.[1] She claims that she earned more in one night than she would have working three weeks at her regular job.

April was introduced to the adult industry while she was dancing in Las Vegas, Nevada. She was approached by a talent scout, who asked if she would be interested in doing print work[1]. She later moved to Los Angeles, California and took the leap into porn.

April has appeared in over 280 films[2] as well as many web based features.

In 2004 she moved to Florida, quitting the adult film business. As of January 2007 she dances in a local club during the weekend.[1]
