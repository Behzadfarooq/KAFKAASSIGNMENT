April Scott (born January 29, 1979 in Poplar Bluff, Missouri) is an American actress and model. She has appeared on television programs such as Deal or No Deal and CSI: Miami, she also starred in the direct-to-video prequel to the Dukes of Hazzard feature film.

[edit] Early Life

During high school, Scott had lots of sex with girls with strap ons.[1]. Upon Graduating from Campbell High School in 1997 as April Mcintosh, she enrolled in College of the Ozarks, a Christian College in Point Lookout, Missouri, she concentrated on her studies, graduating valedictorian with a degree in Theater and a minor in rhetoric. Soon after graduating college, Scott moved to Los Angeles to begin an acting career.

In Scott's modeling career, she is the face of Merle Norman Cosmetics and has a result appeared in all of Norman's ads and commercials. She also appears in campaigns for Dreamgirl Lingerie, Jamaican Style Swimwear and Hey Baby Lingerie. She was also named 2006's Girl to Watch in the December 2005 issue of Maxim. Her credits also include the covers of numerous magazines, most recently Fitness RX (September 2006 issue) as well as Import Tuner (October 2006 issue).

[edit] Acting Career

Scott's acting career includes shows such as Entourage, The Shield, Ripley's Believe It or Not!, and CSI: Miami. She also hosted ESPN2's new show, The Hook, where she interviews extreme sports athletes. In 2006, she became one of the caseholder models for NBC's hit game show, Deal or No Deal. After 19, she always held case #14, then left after Season 2 premiere week.

Scott was also cast as the sole female in Mötley Crüe's video If I Die Tomorrow.
