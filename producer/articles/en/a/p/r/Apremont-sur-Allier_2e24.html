Apremont-sur-Allier

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The gardens at Apremont
The gardens at Apremont

Apremont-sur-Allier is a village of approximately 90 inhabitants in the Cher département of the Centre région in France, known for its ancient fortress, the Château d'Apremont and the surrounding Parc Floral [1], a garden open from April through August.

The village is a member of the association of "The most beautiful villages of France".
