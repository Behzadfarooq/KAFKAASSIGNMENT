Armée Patriotique Rwandaise FC is a football club from Kigali in Rwanda. Armée Patriotique Rwandaise FC, which is their full name, play their home games at Amahoro Stadium. APR FC were founded only in June 1993, at the end of the civil war in Rwanda.

Despite their short history, they are Rwanda's leading football club and have won seven Rwandan league titles and the CECAFA Clubs Cup in 2004, after ending up as losing finalists in that competition in 1996 and 2000.
