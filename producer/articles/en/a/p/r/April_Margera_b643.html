April Margera (born March 28, 1956 in Glen Mills, Pennsylvania, United States) aka "Ape" to fans of MTV's Viva La Bam, Jackass, and the CKY videos. April is the mother of Viva La Bam host Bam Margera, CKY drummer Jess Margera, and is married to Phil Margera.[1] April recently published a cookbook called "April Cooks: There's An Alligator In My Kitchen" which is a play on the fact that her son, Bam, put an alligator in her kitchen as part of a skit on Jackass:The Movie.[2]
