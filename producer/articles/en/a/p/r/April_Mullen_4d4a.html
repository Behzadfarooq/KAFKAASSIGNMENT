April Mullen was brought up in the city of Niagara Falls, Ontario. She studied at the prestigious Ryerson Theatre School in Toronto where she received a B.F.A. Honors. She skipped over to the United Kingdom to further her training at the Royal Welsh College of Drama. Mullen stood out in the Original Showtime feature Cavedweller where she was nominated for Best Actress by the Young Artist Award. This young spirit had had the opportunity to work with great director such as Lisa Cholodenko, Robert Dorhelm, and David Cronenberg. Mullen hits the creative field as a strong passionate individual with the energy of a child and great humility. She enjoys oil painting, writing, editing, directing, risk taking and filming. (Adapted from IMDb and northernstars.ca)
