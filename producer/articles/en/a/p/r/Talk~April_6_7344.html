April 6: Holy and Great Friday in Eastern Christianity and Good Friday in Western Christianity (2007); Tartan Day

  • 1782 - Rama I succeeded King Taksin of Thailand, founding the Chakri Dynasty.
  • 1830 - Joseph Smith, Jr., Oliver Cowdery, David Whitmer and others formally organized the Church of Christ, starting the Latter Day Saint movement.
  • 1895 - Playwright Oscar Wilde (pictured) was arrested in London for "acts of gross indecency".
  • 1896 - The first modern Olympic Games opened in Athens.
  • 1994 - The assassination of the presidents of both Rwanda and Burundi marked the beginning of the Rwandan Genocide.
