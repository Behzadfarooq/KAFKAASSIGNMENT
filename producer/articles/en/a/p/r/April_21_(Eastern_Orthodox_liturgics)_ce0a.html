Apr. 20 - Eastern Orthodox Church calendar - Apr. 22

All fixed commemorations below celebrated on May 4 by Old Calendarists

[edit] Saints

  • Hieromartyr Januarius of Benevento, bishop, and his companions Festus, Proculus, and Sosius, deacons; Desiderius, reader; and Eutychius and Acutius, laymen; at Pozzuoli. See Saint Proculus of Pozzuoli.
  • Hieromartyr Theodore of Perge in Pamphylia, his mother Philippa, and martyrs Dioscorus, Socrates, and Dionysius
  • Martyr Alexandra the Empress (same as April 23)
  • Martyrs Isaacius, Apollo, and Codratus of Nicomedia
  • Archbishop Maximianus of Constantinople
  • Saint Niphont of Novgorod, bishop from the Kiev Caves

[edit] Other Commemorations

  • Repose of Schemamonk Nicetas of Valaam (1907)
