April Grace

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

April Grace (born May 12, 1962 in Lakeland, Florida, USA) is an American actor. She has recently appeared in the television series Lost.

April Grace, as Ms. Klugh in Lost

She is also well known for playing Maggie Hubbell in five episodes of Star Trek: The Next Generation and in Star Trek: Deep Space Nine's pilot episode "Emissary".
