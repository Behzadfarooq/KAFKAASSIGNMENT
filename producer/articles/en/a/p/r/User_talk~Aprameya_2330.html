[edit] Cisse

Hi there,

I notice that more than once, you've added information to the squad/transfers section of Liverpool F.C. suggesting that Djibril Cisse has gone on loan to Marseille. I wonder if I could ask you to refrain from adding this in future. It's a pain to have to keep reverting the transfer speculation that gets added into the article, and Wikipedia is categorically not a place for speculation. At the moment, there has been absolutely no news from Liverpool about whether or not Cisse is going out on loan, and in all likelihood, no deal will happen until he recovers from his injury. In the interests of keeping the encyclopaedia verifiably accurate, we prefer not to add any information about transfers until an official press release or statement is made by the club. There is a note to this effect within the markup of the article, asking editors to refrain from adding such speculation, but since you may have missed it, I thought I'd drop you a friendly note. Thanks, Seb Patrick 15:23, 6 July 2006 (UTC)
