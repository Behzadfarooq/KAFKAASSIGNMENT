APRA, or Apra, can refer to:

  • American Popular Revolutionary Alliance, a Peruvian political party
  • American Poker Runs Association, a sanctioning body for performance-boat poker runs
  • Australian Prudential Regulation Authority
  • Australasian Performing Right Association, a copyright collection society
  • Austrian Personell Reorganisation Association
  • Apra Harbor, the main port of Guam
  • Automotive Parts Remanufacturers Association
  • Angkatan Perang Ratu Adil (Just King Armed Forces), a rebel military group active around Bandung, Indonesia in the early 1950s.
  • Association of Professional Researchers for Advancement, an international organization of professionals who perform research and information management roles within nonprofit organizations. This job is often known as prospect research. [1]
