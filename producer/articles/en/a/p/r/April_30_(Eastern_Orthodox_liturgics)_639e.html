April 30 (Eastern Orthodox liturgics)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Apr. 29 - Eastern Orthodox Church calendar - May 1

All fixed commemorations below celebrated on May 13 by Old Calendarists

[edit] Saints

  • Saint Donatus, Bishop of Euroea in Epirus
  • Martyr Maximus of Ephesus
  • Saint Clement the Hymnographer, abbot of the Studion
  • New martyr Argyra of Prussa
  • Saint Ignatius Brianchaninov (1867)
  • Saint Quintian and Saint Atticus

[edit] Other Commemorations

  • Uncovering of the relics of Saint Basil of Amasea, bishop
  • Uncovering of the relics of Saint Nicetas of Novgorod, bishop
  • Translation of the relics of Saint Sabbas of Zvenigorod Monastery
  • Icon of the Most Holy Theotokos "Of the Passion"
  • Repose of Schema-abbess Martha Protasieva, disciple of Saint Paisius Velichkovsky (1813)
