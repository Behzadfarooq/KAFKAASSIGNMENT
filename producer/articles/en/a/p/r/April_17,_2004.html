April 17, 2004

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

You have new messages (last change).

See also: April 16, 2004 - April 2004 - April 18, 2004

  • His Holiness the Fourteenth Dalai Lama begins a visit to Canada. (Toronto Star)
  • Burma's National League for Democracy reopens its Rangoon headquarters nearly a year after the government closed it down. (VOA)
  • Israeli-Palestinian conflict:
    • Abdel Aziz al-Rantissi, leader of Hamas, is killed by an Israeli missile attack. (CNN) (BBC)
    • An Israeli border police officer is killed in a suicide bombing attack at the Erez Crossing in northern Gaza. Hamas and Fatah claim responsibility. (INN)
  • Spanish Socialist Workers Party leader José Luis Rodríguez Zapatero is sworn in as Spain's prime minister. (CNN)
