April Fool's Day (Hey Arnold Episode)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

"April Fools Day" is the 91st episode in the Hey Arnold series. It aired during Hey Arnold's fifth season, on April 1, 2002.

[edit] Synopsis

It's April Fools' Day and Grandpa is in the pranking spirit and gives him a prank toy that looks like a present, but when opened gives off a blinding light, he farts. Arnold doesn't think anyone pranked on April Fool's, but as he arrives at school he gets repeatedly pranked by Helga using a machine that makes a farting sound. Meanwhile Stinky and Sid decide to prank somebody at the "April Fool's Dance" that night. While eating lunch, Arnold is pranked again, which makes him very angry. Arnold calls Grandpa to bring him the box. Arnold uses the box as a "late birthday present". Helga believes and becomes blind after opening the box. After visiting the nurse, Helga regains her vision, but she has a plan to pretend to be permanently blind. The kids believe her, and Arnold does whatever she says.

When Arnold returns home, he asks Grandpa why he didn't tell him the prank blinds people. After explaining Grandpa suggests that he takes her to the dance.

At the dance the kids are still amazed that Helga is still blind. Eugene, who is running for Fool's King, is going around asking people for their vote. Arnold is treated like dirt until the two dance (or drag) each other. Sid and Stinky put their turtle in the girl's room toilet. Helga then goes to the bathroom, where Pheobe finds out that she is not blind. Helga tells Pheobe all about her plan, and over at the boy's room Gerald can hear everything. Helga (not realizing it) flushes the turtle down the toilet. Back on the dance floor, Gerald and Arnold come up with a plan to get back at Helga. During the tango, Gerald opens the pool (which the turtle finds his way into) and while the King of fools is being announced Helga admits that she is not blind. Arnold says that she herself has been pranked and pushes her into the pool. Eugene then also falls into the pool dropping his hat onto Helga. Helga pulls Arnold into the pool, causing everyone to jump in. While everyone is enjoying themselves, Helga is bitten by the turtle.This makes Sid and Stinky very happy, but then seeing as Snappy (the turtle) was flushed and ended up in the pool, they realized that they were swimming in sewer water.

[edit] Trivia

  • This is the first and one of two episodes that take place after the movie.
  • Alex Linz does the voice of Arnold (instead of Spencer Klein) in this episode.
  • It is almost impossible for a small turtle to go up a tube.
