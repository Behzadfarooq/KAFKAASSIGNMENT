April Stewart (aka Gracie Lazar) is an American voiceover artist. She can currently be heard on the animated cartoon TV series South Park.

Born and raised in Truckee, California, Stewart started acting at the age of four. Her father, Freddie Stewart, was a singer with the Tommy Dorsey Orchestra.

April is the voice of Liane Cartman, Sharon Marsh, Mrs. McCormick, Shelley Marsh, The Mayor, Principal Victoria, Wendy Testaburger and numerous other characters starring in South Park. She is also the voice of Maria Rivera on Nickelodeon's El Tigre: The Adventures of Manny Rivera. Recently she obtained a job for the Fox show American Dad, cast in the roles of Salima and Bahir. She was also recently the voice of Lucrecia Crescent in the video game Dirge of Cerberus: Final Fantasy VII, Ms. Marvel in Marvel: Ultimate Alliance, and Mrjn in Final Fantasy XII. She has also voiced the English voice of Christie in Dead or Alive Xtreme 2. She also voices Queen Isabella in Age of Empires III. April also voiced Pandora in the Danny Phantom episode Boxed Up Fury.
