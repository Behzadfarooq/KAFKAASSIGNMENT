April (Retail Version) is the "finished" version of the latest VAST album, April, and is set to be released in early 2007. According to a post at the official VAST message board[1], the "finished" version will contain six new songs, and members of the VAST Fan Club will have the opportunity to have their names printed in a "special thanks" section of the album sleeve.

On November 11, 2006, Jon Crosby (at the official VAST message board) indicated that the new album April has "a very human feel to it". Because of this, it was decided that the fans will have the opportunity to submit both front- and back-cover artwork for the new record. The first place winner will have their artwork implemented on the new album April. Second place will have their artwork placed upon a "limited edition" version of the record that will be available online only, prior to the retail release of April. Third place will have their artwork used on a tour poster/s; and, finally, the remaining four-through-ten are set to receive a VIP pass to a VAST show in their area.

A text document made available to VAST Fan Club members recently said the following:

A limited edition version of April will be available early 2007; the wide release is scheduled for sometime in April.

In the Visual Audio Sensory Theater 2007 "Q & A" audio commentary, Jon Crosby confirmed that "Frog" and "Everything Passing By" (both unreleased VAST songs) will appear on April.

In early February, 2007, a posting at the official VAST website said:

The top three winners in the VAST "April" art contest are Angel Royster, Melissa Grayson and Joanie Boucher. The limited, collectors edition and wide release art will be a combination of the three artist's work.
