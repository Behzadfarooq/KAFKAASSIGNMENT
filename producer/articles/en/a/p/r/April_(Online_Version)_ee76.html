April (Online Version) is a version of the latest album by the band VAST and was released in 2006. It was recorded all in one take, and made available as an online download. The "finished" version of April (see: April (Retail Version)) is due out in early 2007, and is set to include six new tracks.

The versions of "Be With Me" and "I Can't Say No (To You)" on this release are identical to those found on Nude and Turquoise & Crimson (Retail Version), only performed in a more acoustic fashion.

Further information, including lyrics, can be found at the following URL: http://www.2blossoms.com/april/
