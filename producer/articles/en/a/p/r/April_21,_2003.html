April 21, 2003

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

You have new messages (last change).

See also April 20, 2003 - April 2003 - April 22, 2003

  • Boston Marathon: Robert Cheruiyot wins in 2:10:11, Svetlana Zakharova in 2:25:20 (legally blind American Marla Runyan finishes 5th), Ernst Van Dyk and Christina Ripp win the wheelchair races in 1:28:32 and 1:54:57 respectively.
  • Vote counting in Nigeria heavily favors Olusegun Obasanjo; opposition makes accusations of electoral fraud. [1]
  • The International Criminal Court elects its first prosecutor, Moreno Ocampo. [2]
