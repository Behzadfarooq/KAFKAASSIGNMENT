April 1, 2000 is a 1952 Science fiction film directed by Wolfgang Liebeneiner and starring Hilde Krahl. The script was reportedly commissioned at the request of the Austrian government and depicts a future harmonious Austria. The film is marked by a lack of action and according to commentators such as science fiction author David Wingrove, serves largely as a piece of propaganda aimed at getting Allied troops out of Austrian territory.
