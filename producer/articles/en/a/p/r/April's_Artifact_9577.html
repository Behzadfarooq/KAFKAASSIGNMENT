"April's Artifact" is the forty-seventh episode of the animated series Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles (2003), which originally aired on May 1, 2004.

[edit] Miscellanea

  • In the alternate dimension, the Turtles and April decide to head to the large temple just downriver from the treehouse that once served as Uncle Auggie's HQ. In order to disguise their scent, Donatello jumps into the muddy, putrid river and encourages the others to do the same. They do (if somewhat reluctantly) and April resurfaces very dramatically, only allowing the top of her head down to her nose to come above the water.

Leonardo: Uh, April? Don't you think you're taking this Jungle Girl thing a little too seriously? *he promptly gets a face-full of mud*

  • First appearance of Uncle Augie.
  • April's new shop makes its first appearance.
