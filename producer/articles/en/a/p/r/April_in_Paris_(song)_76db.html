"April in Paris" is a song composed by Vernon Duke and written by E. Y. Harburg in 1932. It has been performed by many artists, including Louis Armstrong, Count Basie, Charlie Parker, Coleman Hawkins, Frank Sinatra, Billy Holliday, Thelonious Monk, Ella Fitzgerald, Dinah Shore, Glenn Miller, Doris Day, and Tommy Dorsey.
