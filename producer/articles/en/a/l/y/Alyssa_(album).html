Alyssa
Studio album by Alyssa Milano
Released October 25, 1989
Recorded 1989
Genre Pop, Dance Club
Length 42:26
Label Pony Canyon Inc./Canyon International/Marubeni Corporation
Producer(s) Joey Carbone, Katz Nagasawa, Tom Milano

Alyssa is Alyssa Milano's self-titled second album, released October 25, 1989. On this album she worked with most of the same producers she had worked with on the first album. It wasn't until her third studio album that she went for a different sound with different producers. Also released as a Picture Disc Edition.
