Alyce Platt

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Alyce Platt (born 19 December 1963) is an Australian actress known for her roles in television soap operas, and as a television presenter.

Her acting roles include Amanda Morrell in Sons and Daughters from 1983 to 1985, a guest role in the short-lived serial Family and Friends in 1990, and the on-going part of Jen Handley in Neighbours in the early 1990s.

From 1986 until 1991, Platt was the co-host of the then top-rated quiz show Sale of the Century, a job that probably brought her her widest audiences and recognition. She since returned to this role for one special episode of Temptation on March 14, 2007, during the show's Battle of the Network Shows series, in which Temptation's hosts Ed Phillips and Livinia Nixon were contestants themselves.

Today, she can be seen alongside former Sale co-host Tony Barber on TV1's Cash Trivia Challenge on Pay TV.
