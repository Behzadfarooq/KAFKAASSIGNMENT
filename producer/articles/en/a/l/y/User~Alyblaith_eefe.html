[edit] Why I'm Here

I'm not really that big of a contributer; I just make minor edits to stuff like spelling and grammar. I'm still trying to get the hang of using the wiki, so expect my contributions to grow. Wikipedia is my #1 resource for all my papers, so I try to help prevent/revert vandalism because I know many other people use it too. I also try to stop my classmates from vandalizing, though I haven't succeeded completely yet. This is my personal sandbox, and I'm going to make my userboxes say everything you never knew about me and didn't want to ask.

[edit] More About Me

The userboxes say it all, or nearly all of it. I will read pretty much anything once it seems interesting. I'm a packrat, and a sentimentalist.I pride myself on not making any prejudices I have really, really obvious. I write most of my papers casually, like I’m talking. I like most kinds of music, and I’ll tolerate the others. I’m trying to perfect NPOV-talk. I want to actually fill up this page to the bottom of the userbox column.
