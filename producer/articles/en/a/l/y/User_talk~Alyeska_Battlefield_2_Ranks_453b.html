[edit] Clarifications on Sergeants, please

"Note: A Rank of Sergant Major of the Corps is given monthly to the best performing Master Sergant. It is only held by one player at a time. Please note the game follows the Marine Corp's ranking system, where at the paygrade E-7 there are two ranks. They are considered to be equal in rank, but perform different tasks."

According to the website http://www2.powercom.net/~rokats/armedforces.html

E7 is for Gunnery Sergeant. E8 is where you have First and Master Sergeants.

Also, I'd like more clarifications on the first sentence. Master Sergeant is one level below Sergeant Major of the Marine Corps. It'd make sense if one gets promoted each month from E8 to the single Sergeant Major of the Marine Corps in E9.

OR, is one of the existing E9 sergeants (Master Gunnery Sergeant/Sergeant Major) selected as the greatest E9 as Sergeant Major of the Marine Corps?

So to simplify things, I wanted to ask: In BF2, is Sergeant Major of the Marine Corps selected from existing E9 sergeants or promoted from one level below?

- Selected from exsisting, i think. The reason only one person a month gets the position is because there is only one at a time in real life. --Herzog 01:34, 26 January 2006 (UTC)
