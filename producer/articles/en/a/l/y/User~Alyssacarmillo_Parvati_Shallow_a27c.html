Parvati Piper Shallow is a female boxer that resides in Los Angeles,California.She is 24 years old.

[edit] Career

Went to a university in California near her Los Angeles home.She went to a boxing school for which she attended for 2 years.

Was a teenage model for her city.She appeared in advertising billboards for products such as BeBe,Lancome,and several sweet products.She was known as "Miss Parvati" back then.

Was on Survivor:Cook Islands and made it into the final six,where she then was voted out by the contestants,Sundra,Ozzy,Yul,and Becky.She and her voting partner,Adam,voted against Sundra,hoping that Ozzy would flip to their side,but it didn't work out.

Attended a Mandarin-Chinese weekend workshop when she was 10,hoping to bring up her bilingual skills.She gave up when she was 12,but can still speak a little Mandarin,though not completely fluent.

[edit] Music Appearances

Often at times when she is offered in a music video,she is mosstly in hte crowd,not playing quite a major role.Commonly,she was a cheerleader (alongside Alyssa),a party girl,and a gymnast.

She has been working and recording a CD since May 2005,which she confirmed was going to be name "Miss Parvati" from back in the days.It was going to feature 24 songs (her age) and 2 exclusive music videos (Wal-Mart stores only).But recently,she has send out a 3-song singles CD with the title "Miss Parvati".Her first debut album will be name "Shallow Like Me" and will feature between 10 and 30 songs (unconfirmed).

[edit] Personal Life

As a teenage child,Shallow was known as the "Flirty Girl" of the crowd. She states,"I even went out with my best friend's boyfriend. What could be more worst than that!".

At the age of 6,she thought she could sing.She adored Mariah Carey as a singer,although not her voice.She states that,in her opinion,that Mariah has a "too deep" voice and would rather sing with a voice as of one of the Aly & Aly girls.

After the filming of Survivor:Cook Islands,her partner Adam Gentry and Shallow tried dating.They broke up after 3 months,when Gentry started talking about him and other former contestant,Candice Woodcock,who was also part of their trio before being voted out.Shallow grew jealous and decided to end the relationship.

Mother,Melissa Shallow,raised Parvati by her self as a single mother when dad Michael left Parvati and Melissa when Parvati was 3.

Got somewhat bad grades as a junior high student.Once,her report card said :

  • Mathematics : C-
  • Physical Education : B+
  • Science (Chemistry) : D
  • Spanish : B-
  • History : A-
  • Language Arts : A
  • Reading Enrichment : B+

Shallow also wanted to star in the drama series,Charmed as the youngest sister,which was then,Phoebe Halliwell.She had no possibility to star in the Phoebe role as she never went to actually audition.Says,"I was probably going to be underestimated by the big star celebrities that were there.I actually got to see Holly Marie Combs in person,thought we never 'talked'.All she said was a little hi and I said hi back.She was also with this other girl,which I later found out was Shannen Doherty.I was just still a little teen back then.I had high hopes.But I got to guest star once when I was about 20.So yeah,it's all good."
