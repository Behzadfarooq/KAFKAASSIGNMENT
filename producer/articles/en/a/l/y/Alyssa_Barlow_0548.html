Alyssa Barlow

Alyssa Katherine Nicole Barlow (born January 4, 1982) is the lead singer for BarlowGirl, a Christian pop and rock band. Barlow also plays bass and keyboard for the band. The other members of the band include her sisters, Becca Barlow and Lauren Barlow.
