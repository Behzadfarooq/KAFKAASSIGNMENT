Alyas Batman en Robin is an unauthorized Filipino Batman comedy film produced by Regal Films spoofing the 1960s Batman television series. It stars comedian Joey de Leon as Batman and his son Keempee as Robin. Several villains are also featured such as the Joker and the Penguin (here "Chu-pa-eng-gwin"), portrayed respectively by the late comedians Rene Requiestas and Panchito.

[edit] Quote

  • Batman: "Would you like Bat-tea? Or Bat-coffee? Or Bat-milk? Bat-juice? Salabat?" (Salabat is Filipino for ginger tea.)
