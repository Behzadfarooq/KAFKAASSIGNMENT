Alyson is a California based singer/songwriter/producer who started in the music industry behind the scenes. This former Fitness Instructor has scored phenomenal success in North America and abroad. All of her singles to date have made the Top 5 or higher on the various music charts. Alyson is a multi style recording artist who feels at home with Dance-Pop and House music as she does while doing Urban contemporary and Adult Contemporary fare. Her songs have charted on all the charts for the aforementioned styles. Her debut album "Take A Good Look" released on her own label, PM Media, has become a strong seller in the States and overseas since its release on May 17, 2005. The album features the hits "Baby Come Back", "What're Your Gonna Do", "Nothin' More To Say", "Feel You", "Take A Gook Look" and the new smash, "Forever." She is currently working on her sophomore album due in early-mid 2007.
