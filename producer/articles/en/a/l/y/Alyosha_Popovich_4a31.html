Alyosha Popovich (Russian: Алёша Попо́вич, literally Alexey, son of the priest), alongside Dobrynya Nikitich and Ilya Muromets, is a bogatyr (i.e., a medieval Russian knight-errant).

The three of them are represented together at Vasnetsov's famous painting Bogatyrs.

In Byliny (oral stories) he is described as a crafty priest's son who wins by tricking and outsmarting his foes. He defeated the dragon Tugarin Zmeyevich by trickery. In later versions the dragon was transformed into the figure of a Mongol Khan.
