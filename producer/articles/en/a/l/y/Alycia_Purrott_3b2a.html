Alycia Purrott

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Alycia Purrott as Sydney Drew in Power Rangers: S.P.D.

Alycia Purrott (born October 24, 1983) is a Canadian television and movie actress best known for playing the pink-clad Syd Drew on TV's Power Rangers: S.P.D.

[edit] Biography and career

Alycia, who stands just under 5' 1", was born in London, Ontario and has been a model and actress since the age of 14, starting with commercials and bit parts. In 1998, less than a year after becoming a performer, she won the best actress award at Ontario's Sears Drama Festival for playing Rudy in Asleep on the Wind. She subsequently had major roles in two 2000 made-for-TV films and briefly settled in Southern California where she went on to study broadcast journalism.

In 2005, Alycia gained high recognition and intense fan following for her role in the New Zealand-filmed TV series Power Rangers: S.P.D.. She had maintained a residence in the Greater Vancouver area.
