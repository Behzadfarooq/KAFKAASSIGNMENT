My username, Alyx Bradford, is also the pen name I use for writing.

I am twenty years old, a senior at the College of William and Mary, born in Harrisonburg, Virginia, though I have lived most of my life in Richmond, Virginia. This summer, I will be studying Shakespeare at Christ's College in Cambridge; I'll be seeing Titus Andronicus at the Globe Theatre in London, and Romeo and Juliet in Stratford-upon-Avon, as well as visiting York to watch the York Mystery Plays Cycle. I'm hoping to be able to take side trips to Glastonbury, Stonehenge, and Tintagel, to feed my obsession with Arthurian legend.

On Wikipedia, I have been largely responsible for the contributions regarding Jacqueline Carey's Kushiel's Legacy series. My favorite character in that series, and the one I feel the greatest connection with, is Ysandre de la Courcel.

My other interests include writing, reincarnation, and Livejournal.
