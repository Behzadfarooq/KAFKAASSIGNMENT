Alyssa Campanella (born March 21, 1990) is a beauty queen from Manalapan, New Jersey who will compete in the Miss Teen USA pageant in 2007.

Campanella won the Miss New Jersey Teen USA 2007 title on October 15, 2006 in Parsippany, New Jersey. It was her second attempt at the title, as she was a semi-finalist in 2006. She was crowned by Julianna White, Miss New Jersey Teen USA 2006.

Campanella will now represent the state of New Jersey and compete in the Miss Teen USA 2007 pageant broadcast live from an as-yet un-announced location in August 2007 on NBC where she will attempt to become the seventh delegate from New Jersey to place in the nationally televised pageant, following Julianna White's top ten placement in 2006.

In 2005, she organized a fundraiser within her community for the United Nations' organization UNICEF for the victims of Hurricane Katrina and was named an Honorary UNICEF Ambassador for the U.S. Fund in 2006. She hopes to one day work for the United Nations as a goodwill ambassador for the UNHCR.[1]

Her "sister titleholder" was Ashley Harder, but after Harder resigned in January 2007 due to her pregnancy, Erin Abrahamson became her new sister titleholder, Miss New Jersey USA 2007.

She is currently a Junior at Freehold Township High School.
