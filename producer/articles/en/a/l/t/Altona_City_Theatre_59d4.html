Altona City Theatre

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Altona City Theatre, or ACT, is a community based production company residing at the Altona Theatre in Altona, Victoria, Australia. The company is over 30 years old and has been at its home in the Altona Theatre for over half that time. The group are accessible to people of all ages and experience levels with even a resident youth class called ACTion Youth Theatre for school aged students.
