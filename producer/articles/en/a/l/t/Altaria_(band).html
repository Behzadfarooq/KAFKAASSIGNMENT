[edit] Members

[edit] Current

  • Marco Luponero - Vocals
  • J-P Alanen – Guitar (2005–present) (formerly Celesty)
  • Petri Aho – Guitar (2004 (live), 2006–present)
  • Marko Pukkila – Bass (2000–present)
  • Tony Smedjebacka – Drums (2000–present)

[edit] Former

  • Johan Mattjus – Vocals (2000–2001)
  • Jouni Nikula – Vocals (2002–2003) (also Requiem)
  • Erno "Emppu" Vuorinen – Guitars (2002–2004) (also Nightwish)
  • Jani Liimatainen – Guitars, keyboards (2000–2005) (also Sonata Arctica)
  • Taage Laiho (b. 16 February 1966) – Vocals (2003–2006) (also Kilpi)
