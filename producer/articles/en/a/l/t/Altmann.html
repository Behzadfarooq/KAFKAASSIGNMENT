Altmann

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Altmann or Altman may refer to:

  • Altmann of Passau (bishop) (1020-1091)
  • Altman (automobile), Ohio-based manufacturer
  • Republic of Austria v. Altmann, a notable decision by the United States Supreme Court
  • Altman Stage Lighting, a manufacturer of stage lighting instruments and accesories.
