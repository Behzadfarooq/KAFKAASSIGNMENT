Alternative natural history, also known as speculative biology, is the extrapolation of the laws of evolution into the future to hypothesize about future species. Also, it explores question such as how would life have evloved if dinosaurs never died out.

The well known discovery mini-series The future is wild is a good example of speculative biology.
