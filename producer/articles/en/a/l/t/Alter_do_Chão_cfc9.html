Alter do Chão (pron. IPA: [aɫ'tɛɾ du ʃɐ̃ũ]) is a municipality in Portugal with a total area of 362.0 km² and a total population of 3,666 inhabitants.

The municipality is composed of 4 parishes, and is located in the District of Portalegre.

The present Mayor is Joviano Martins Vitorino, elected by the Social Democratic Party.

The municipal holiday is Ascension Day.
