[edit] Suggested Article of the month candidates

[edit] Guidelines

Please nominate an article which you think would make a suitable candidate for article of the month.

  1. Preference will be given to good articles and (moreso) featured articles, although this does not prohibit other articles from being nominated.
  2. The article must have a adequate lead section.
  3. The article should contain at least one free image if possible (exceptions can be made for albums and songs).

[edit] Candidates

[edit] January 2007

  • In lieu of the creation of this portal, Teemu08 has chosen featured article the Pixies as the article of the month for January.

[edit] February 2007

  • Since no article was nominated, Elliott Smith was chosen as the article of the month.

[edit] March 2007

  • The Smashing Pumpkins - Highly-developed GA article. Band has reformed and is currently recording; will release a new album and tour this year. Nominating for March in particular because this month marks frontman Billy Corgan's 40th birthday and would be timely. WesleyDodds 08:55, 7 January 2007 (UTC)
