Altovis

Altovis is a herbal drug that was marketed as a pharmaceutical drug in major television advertising campaigns in 2004 and 2005, targeted primarily at individuals who find themselves low on energy[citation needed]. Its main active ingredients are caffeine and ginseng. Altovis is made from green tea leaf extract (provides caffeine), cordycepts extract mycelium, Eleutherococcus (ginseng), vocanga tree seeds, and octacosanol. It also contains the following other ingredients: Dicalcium phosphate, microcrystalline cellulose, croscarmellose sodium, stearic acid, silica, magnesium stearate, and film coat (hypromellose, hydroxypropyl cellulose, polyethylene glycol, propylene glycol, titanium dioxide, FD&C yellow #6 lake, riboflavin, FD&C blue #2 lake).
