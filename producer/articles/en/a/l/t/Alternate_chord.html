Alternate chord

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The altered chord, derived from altered scale is an extended chord found in jazz music.

The tensions in an alternate chord are:

  • flat seventh (b7)
  • flat ninth (b9)
  • sharp ninth (#9)
  • flat fifth (b5)
  • sharp fifth (#5)
