Alternative Television was a Trinidad and Tobago Television station, better known as TTT Channel 9 vhf and 14 uhf. It was launched in late 1983, and ran from 6.00-10.00 pm. It showed programs like Crown Court, Rituals, Prisoner, and Butterflies. There was a newscast at 9.00 pm. In 1987 it was broadcast only on weekends due to cutbacks.It was finally closed on July 27, 1990, the day of the Jamaat al Muslimeen coup attempt.
