Alternately Deep is the fifth studio album of original material by Roots Manuva. It was released on 13th March 2006 on the Big Dada label.

The album contains 12 tracks recorded during the sessions for his last studio album, Awfully Deep, including a remix of "Colossal Insight" and the track "Double Drat" which was previously only available via Roots Manuva's website.

The album features guest appearances from Colossus, Easy Access Orchestra, Lotek and Ricky Rankin.

  2. "Seat Yourself" (Miami mix)
  3. "Double Drat"
  4. "Nobody's Dancing"
  5. "Things We Do"
  6. "Check It"
  7. "Get U High"
  8. "Colossal Insight" (Jammer Remix and Revox)
  9. "Mean Street"
  10. "Pep My Game"
  11. "This World Is Mine" (Plan B Studio Version)
  12. "Grown Man"
