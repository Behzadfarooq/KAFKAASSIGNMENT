Alternative Freedom is an independent documentary film produced and directed by Twila Raftu and Shaun Cronin. The film is comprised of footage from six interviews, stock-footage gathered from the Internet Archive, and an original soundtrack.

[edit] Interviews

  • Danger Mouse
  • Lawrence Lessig
  • Andrew "bunnie" Huang
  • Richard Stallman
  • Jason Schultz
  • Doseone
