Category:Alternative high schools

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

An alternative high school is any public or private secondary school having a special curriculum offering a more flexible program of study than a traditional school.

A school should only be included in here if it actually operates as a substantially different alternative to other schools in the same area (e.g. same school district). Generally, only government run public schools (also known as state schools in some places) should be included here. Articles included in a Private schools category or the Charter schools category should not be included here.
