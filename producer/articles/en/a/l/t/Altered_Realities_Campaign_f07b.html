ARC or Altered Realities Campaign is a LARP organized by Dirk Parham, in 2003. The game is heavily based on Call of Cthulhu material, and embodies connections to an earlier Call of Cthulhu LARP organized by Xanodria Productions, Inc. (XPI) constituting an effective sequel to that series of events. The event occupies a significant place in the evolution of Adventure Style LARP in the northeastern US, being transitory between Live Combat and Theatre Style LARP.
