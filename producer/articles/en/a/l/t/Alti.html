[edit] Overview

Alti is an Amazon shaman driven out of her tribe by queen Cyane, because of her hunger for power. She is one of the people who taught Xena in her dark days. Alti possess various and vast powers.

[edit] Character development

Alti commands incredible spiritual powers which have been fuelled by her evil rituals performed on the slain Amazon tribe leaders.[1] While she is strong as a warrior, Alti's true strength lies in her psychic/spiritual abilities. The curses she place on targets never fail, although only one instance of her curses is actually shown, as Alti curses the unborn child of Xena and Borias. She is very prophetic and every prophecy she mentions in the show eventually happens (such as Solan never being able to experience the love of either Xena or Borias). She can possess living beings in the physical world, Alti possesses the Berserker's horse[1] and attacks Xena through the horse's spirit and also possesses a spider to bite Xena and extract information about Gabrielle.[2]

Alti is probably best known for her trademark stare, which brings up pain and suffering from the target's life and unleashes the torment once again. Whenever Alti stares at Xena, Xena often feels the pain of when her legs were broken, her back snapped by her Chakram,[3] and multiple barrages of attacks from some of her mortal enemies. However, as Alti grows in power in the series, she is able to conjure up pain and suffering from a person's future as well. This is what causes Xena to realize that Gabrielle was alive in Season 4, after Alti shows Xena a vision of her and Gabrielle being crucified together on Mount Amarro.[2]

Xena isn't the only one who has felt Alti's powers. When Gabrielle is shown the vision of her and Xena dying she feels like she is being crucified.[4] Alti also forces two Chinese Emissaries to envision the suffering of war.[5]

In the few dealings with Alti, Alti claims that her powers are always evolving. Alti discovers the rich abundance of life force that can be drained from the Karmic Cycle, and says that "Here, there are generations of power, just waiting for me to take".[4] In an alternate reality Alti realizes who she really was in the original world, and is able to amplify her power from both worlds. In doing so, she is able to fly, and dart across fields at an inhumanly fast speed.[5] Later, although deceased Alti is able to wreck havoc on Xena, Xena's unborn child Eve, and Gabrielle from the Spiritual Realm.[6] Also, the Mother of Peace (which is Xena's next life) tells the hero (which is Gabrielle's next life) that "Alti's evil just moved on to the next life," which credits that Alti's powers do extend beyond just one lifetime.[4]

A spiritual guide from India told Xena that no matter what happens in the Karmic Cycle, Xena would always stop Alti in every lifetime as soon as Alti discovers her powers.[4] This has been true; in each episode that featured Alti, Xena stops her before she could do anything else.

Spoiler warning: Plot and/or ending details follow.

Alti trains Xena as a shaman and seduces her with the promise she will become the Destroyer of Nations.[2] This finally leads to Xena murdering Cyane and several other leaders of the Northern Amazons.[2] Xena escapes from her when she becomes pregnant with Solan, but not before her unborn child is cursed by Alti.[2]

In Season 4, after Gabrielle falls in Dahak's lava pit with Hope,[7] Xena travels to the Northern Amazon territory and defeats Alti.[1]

Alti appears again when she tries to take over the spirit of Xena's unborn child in Season 5[6] and next in Season 6 when she clones Xena and Gabrielle into the present time.[8] Her final appearance is in Season 6, as the High Priestess of Rome in an alternate reality created by Julius Caesar after he tampers with The Fates and their loom and changed the course of history. This reality is ultimately destroyed as Gabrielle destroys the loom in this reality, marking the final appearance of Alti.[5]
