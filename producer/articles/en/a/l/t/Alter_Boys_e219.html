Alter Boys is the sixth episode in the second series of CSI: Crime Scene Investigation, an American crime drama set in Las Vegas, Nevada.

In this episode, a man is found burying a body in the desert and although at first it seems to Grissom to be a simple case, he soons finds out all is not as it seems. Elsewhere, Catherine and Warrick investigate a woman who was found dead in a hotel spa.
