Alternatywy 4

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Alternatywy 4 (pol. Disjunction St. 4, meaning also Four disjunctions) was a Polish TV series completed in 1983. Many famous Polish actors (not only comedians) appear in Alternatywy 4.
