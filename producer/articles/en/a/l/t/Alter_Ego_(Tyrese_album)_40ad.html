Alter Ego is a double album by Tyrese. One CD is only with R&B and the other is only with rap. The rap part is sung by his alter ego "Black-Ty".

Guest features include: The Game, Method Man, Snoop Dogg, Lil Scrappy, Kurupt, Too $hort, R. Kelly, Lil Jon, Mannie Fresh.

The first single is "One" & the second single is "Turn Ya Out" which is produced by Lil Jon and features Lil Jon.

The album was released in stores on December 12th, 2006. It sold 115,957 copies during its first week and landed on #23 on Billboardâ€™s Top 200 Album chart, #4 on the R&B Album charts and #3 on the Rap Album chart, according to Nielsen SoundScan.
