The Legion Wars, more formally known as the War of Legions, was a small galactic conflict between the Legion Alliance and The Forces of Marudubshinki.



NOTE: This event did not happen canonically.


[edit] Causes

The main cause of the Legion Wars was of one of the administrators of Wikicity, Marudubshinki, banned certain civilians from visiting Wikicity and numerous other places under Wikicity rule. These civilians, now with Marudubshinki as their main enemy, were devastated when Marudubshinki sent his forces to blow up the civilians houses. These civilians then watched their homes burn on Coruscant, and then banded together, formed an alliance and personally declared war on Marudubshinki. The other administrators of Wikicity allowed Mardubshinki to do these actions because they were afraid of his military power.

[edit] Key Battles

There were only a few battles in the Legion Wars as the war itself was small. The Commanders of the Legion Alliance, Mil Falcon, Alt Gr, B-Wing and Commander Naayu, sent an attack group to Marudubshinki's private plaza on Dantooine. This resulted in The Mini Battle of Dantooine, in which Marudubshinki's forces suffered medium losses, The Legion Alliance suffered minor losses, and Maru's private plaza was blown up. News of this had reached Maru's ears, and reacted with anger, by sending a third of his army to Tatooine, where he had heard that was where the entire alliance was. This resulted in medium losses for both sides, and 24 civilians were killed in the conflict, either accidentally or because they were helping one of the factions. Maru's forces won this battle. 5 months later, after many near-battles, the entire Legion Alliance went to Wikicity to kill Marudubshinki. After a long and fierce battle, in which both sides suffered heavy losses, the Legion Alliance provailed and confronted Maru. Maru, in his cowardice, tried to escape in his transport, but Mil Falcon shot him in the back as Maru ran. Marudubshinki was finally dead, and the remaining forces of his army were imprisoned. The Legion Alliance's bans were lifted, and the alliance then disbanded two days after the war. But all of the soldiers that fought with the legion joined the Republic's forces.

[edit] Losses

Forces of Marudubshinki: Marudubshinki, 2000 soldiers, 10 Wikicity tanks, 23 Wikicity fighters, 5 civilians.

Legion Alliance: 900 Soldiers, 2 civilian fighters, 1 prototype Tie Mauler, 4 Naboo Starfighters, 10 civilians.
