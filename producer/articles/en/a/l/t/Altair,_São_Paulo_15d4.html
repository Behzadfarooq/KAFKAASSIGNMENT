Coordinates: 20°31′27″S, 49°03′34″W Altair is a city in the state of São Paulo in Brazil. The latitude is 20.37 South and to one 49.03 longitude West. The population in 2004 is 3,679. The area is 317.832 km². The neighboring municipalities are Icém to the north and Olímpia to the east.

[edit] Other

Altair has a school or a collegiate, high schools, churches, banks and a few parks.
