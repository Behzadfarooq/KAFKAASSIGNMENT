Location of Altrei / Anterivo in Italy

Altrei (German) or Anterivo (Italian) is a comune (municipality) in the South Tyrol in the Italian region Trentino-Alto Adige/Südtirol, located about 30 km northeast of Trento and about 25 km south of Bolzano. As of 31 December 2004, it had a population of 383 and an area of 11.1 km².[1]

The municipality of Altrei contains the frazioni (subdivisions, mainly villages and hamlets) Pramarino and Guggal.

Altrei borders the following municipalities: Capriana, Carano, Castello-Molina di Fiemme, Truden, and Valfloriana.
