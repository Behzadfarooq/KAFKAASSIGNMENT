The list of people who have said they are a god is divided into two parts. The first contains those who have made unequivocal statements about being a god, while the second contains those whose statements are considered ambiguous and debateable, or where the person's historicity is uncertain.

An asterisk (*) indicates they have a significant amount of followers, while a double dagger (‡) marks those who became the focus of a religion.
