Gate building of Altshausen Palace

Altshausen is a small Swabian town (c. 4,600 inhabitants) near Ravensburg, Baden-Württemberg, southern Germany. It is notable for its Teutonic Order castle and as the birthplace of Hermann of Reichenau. Sister city of Altshausen in Bicske, Hungary.
