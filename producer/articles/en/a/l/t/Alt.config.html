Alt.config

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The correct title of this article is alt.config. The initial letter is shown capitalized due to technical restrictions.

alt.config is a newsgroup used to discuss the creation of, as well as create, new groups in the alt hierarchy of usenet.
