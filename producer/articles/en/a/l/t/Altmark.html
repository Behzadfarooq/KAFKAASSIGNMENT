The Altmark is a region in Germany, between Hamburg and Magdeburg, the northern third of Saxony-Anhalt (the districts of Altmarkkreis Salzwedel and Stendal).

[edit] Geography

The region is rural and covered with forests. The most important towns are Salzwedel and Stendal.
