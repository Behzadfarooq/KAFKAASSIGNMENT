Alter ego (disambiguation)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Look up alter ego in Wiktionary, the free dictionary.

An alter ego is an alternate personality or persona.

Alter ego may also refer to:

In gaming:

  • Alter Ego (video game), personality computer game released by Activision in 1986

In law:

  • Alter Ego (judicial doctrine), a doctrine by which a court of law holds individual shareholders liable for a corporation's debts if the corporation is deemed to be nothing more than an "alter ego" of the corporation's owners.

In literature:

  • Alter Ego (fanzine), comics fanzine published in the 1960's
  • Alter Ego (magazine), slick magazine concerning comics

In music:

  • Alter Ego (album), the third album of the Finnish pop-group Egotrippi
  • Alter Ego (Tyrese album), a 2006 album by Tyrese Gibson which features his alter ego Black Ty
  • Alter Ego (band), industrial-rave techno music group
  • Alter Ego (Peruvian band), Peruvian rock band playing mainly neo-progressive rock music of the 1990's
  • Alter Ego (cover band), popular San Antonio, Texas rock cover group playing mostly hard rock and alternative covers of bands such as Godsmack, Staind, Bush, the Cult, and many others
  • "Alter Ego" (instruments), electric acoustic string instruments

In other fields:

  • "Alter Ego" (Voyager episode), Star Trek: Voyager episode
  • Altar Egos, an episode of Arrested Development
