Altona Gate Shopping Centre is a shopping centre located in Altona North, Victoria, a suburb of Melbourne, Australia. It fronts onto Millers Road just south of the West Gate Freeway exit. It is serviced by bus via route 232 from Melbourne city centre and route 471 from Sunshine or Williamstown. It was featured many times on the Australian comedy series [The Wedge.]
