Alternative display facility (ADF) is an equity trading facilitycreated in the United States by NASD, a self-regulatory organization (SRO). The ADF is an alternative to the exchange for publishing quotations and for comparing and reporting trades. This differs from a trading facility with execution capabilities (stock exchange) in that the exchange would simply send back to the owner of the displayed order a notice of execution. The National Association of Securities Dealers NASD has operated an ADF since July 29, 2002.[1][2]
