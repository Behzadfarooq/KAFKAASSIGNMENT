Alton "Big Al" Carson

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Big Al Carson (right), with pianist Lars Edegran (left)

Alton "Big Al" Carson (Born October 2, 1953) is a United States blues and jazz singer from New Orleans, Louisiana.

As of 2006, Big Al sings with his band "The Bluemasters" at the Funky Pirate on Bourbon Street in the French Quarter of New Orleans several nights a week.

He also performs irregularly with other bands.

Carson formerly played tuba in addition to singing.

Carson has performed and recorded with a number of brass bands and jazz band in the city.
