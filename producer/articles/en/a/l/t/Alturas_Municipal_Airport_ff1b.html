Alturas Municipal Airport (ICAO: KAAT, FAA LID: AAT), formerly O00, is a general aviation airport located one mile (2 km) west of the central business district (CBD) of Alturas, in Modoc County, California, USA.

Although most U.S. airports and airbases use the same three-letter location identifier for the FAA and IATA, Alturas Municipal Airport is assigned AAT[1] by the FAA but has no designation from the IATA (which assigned AAT to Altay, Xinjiang, China[2]). The airport's ICAO identifier is KAAT.[3]

[edit] Facilities

Alturas Municipal Airport covers 160 acres and has two runways:

  • Runway 13/31: 4,301 x 50 ft. (1,311 x 15 m), Surface: Asphalt
  • Runway 3/21: 2,935 x 60 ft. (895 x 18 m), Surface: Asphalt
