Altstadt Spandau (Berlin U-Bahn)

Altstadt Spandau is a station on the Berlin U-Bahn line U7. It was opened on October 1, 1984 with the line's extension from Rohrdamm to Rathaus Spandau. Its name, which means "Old Town of Spandau" in English, comes from being located in one of Spandau's oldest quarters.

It lies between the stations Rathaus Spandau and Zitadelle.
