Al Shaab Stadium

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Al Shaab Stadium is a multi-use stadium in Baghdad, Iraq. It is currently used mostly for football matches. The stadium holds 45,000. It was built in 1966. The opening match featured Iraq and Benfica, for whom the legendary Eusébio was playing.
