Albert C. Buckenberger (January 31, 1862 - July 1, 1917) was a manager in Major League Baseball for the Columbus Solons, Pittsburgh Pirates, St. Louis Cardinals and Boston Beaneaters from 1889 to 1904. In 1896, he managed the Toronto team in the Eastern League. Born in Detroit, Michigan, he died at age 55 in Syracuse, New York.
