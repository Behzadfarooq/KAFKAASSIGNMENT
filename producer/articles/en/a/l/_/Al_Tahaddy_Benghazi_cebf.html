Al Tahaddy Benghazi is an Libyan football club based in Benghazi. They are a member of the top division in Libyan football. Their home stadium is March 28 Stadium.

[edit] Honors

  • Libyan Premier League
    • Champion : 1967, 1977, 1997
  • Libyan Cup
    • Finalist : 1999
  • Libyan SuperCup
    • Champion : 1997
