Al Da Flaga (アル・ダ・フラガ, Aru Da Furaga?) is a fictional character appearing in the fictional Cosmic Era timeline of the Gundam anime series. He is voiced by Kouzou Mito in the original Japanese version.

Spoiler warning: Plot and/or ending details follow.

[edit] History

He was Mu La Flaga's father, a Natural, and an influential and affluent Atlantic Federation politician. His relation with his son Mu La Flaga, however, was strained at best. He did not want his fortune to be inherited by Mu, whom he believed to be an impure son, "tainted" by the inferior DNA of a mother. He made a deal with Kira Yamato's father, Professor Ulen Hibiki, to make a clone of him in exchange for research grants. This way his fortune would go to someone he approves of—a son who is an exact copy of himself. However, the process was not completely successful and several clones were created after the instability of Al Da Flaga, Jr. (who would later become known as Rau Le Creuset) became apparent: including Rey Za Burrel and Prayer Reverie. These cloning experiments were the precursor to Dr. Hibiki's Ultimate Coordinator project which created Kira Yamato, as Hibiki used the funding he received from Al Da Flaga, Sr. in creating Kira.

Al, Sr. had Al, Jr. him educated in the ways of the social elite but as time passed Al, Jr. experienced a mental breakdown. Al, Jr. set fire to the mansion and escaped. Al Da Flaga, Sr. died in that fire; Mu La Flaga survived. Al, Jr. would then assume the name Rau Le Creuset, move to PLANT, and join the ZAFT military. The other clone, Prayer Reverie would join the Junk Guild and die due to unknown causes. But he actually faked his own death, to let his nemesis, Canard Pars change his life for the better.
