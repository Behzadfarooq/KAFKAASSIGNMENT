Al Garhoud bridge

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Al Garhoud bridge is one of two road bridges over the Dubai creek.

Nearly 9,000 vehicles cross the Al Garhoud Bridge every hour at peak flow. It has been the cause of huge traffic snarls and is currently undergoing expansion. The bridge has 6 lanes, 3 lanes in each direction.
