Alshaheed Fahed Al-Ahmed League (Arabic: دوري الشهيد فهد الأحمد) is a football competition in Kuwait that started in 1961/1962. Alarabi club was the first club to win the league.

[edit] Overview of seasons

[edit] 1961/1962 season

  • Champion: Alaradi club
  • 12 matches played
    • 12 wins
  • 42 goals scored

[edit] 1962/1963 season

  • Champion: Alarabi club
  • 10 matches played
    • 8 wins
    • 2 draws
  • 45 goals scored

[edit] 1963/1964 season

  • Champion: Alarabi club
  • 10 matches played
    • 9 wins
    • 1 loss (to Alqadsia club)
  • 40 goals scored

[edit] 1964/1965 season

  • Champion: Alkuwait club (first championship in their history)
  • 10 matches played
    • 7 wins
    • 3 draws
  • 44 goals scored

[edit] 1965/1966 season

  • Champion: Alarabi club
  • 10 matches played
    • 9 wins
    • 1 draw
  • 38 goals scored

[edit] 1966/1967 season

  • Champion: Alarabi club (fifth championship in their history)
  • 10 matches played
    • 7 wins
    • 2 draws
    • 1 loss
  • 22 goals scored

[edit] 1967/1968 season

  • Champion: Alkuwait club
  • 10 matches played
    • 7 wins
    • 2 draws
    • 1 loss
  • 15 goals scored

[edit] 1968/1969 season

  • Champion: Alqadsia club (first championship in their history)
  • 10 matches played
    • 5 wins
    • 5 draws
  • 20 goals scored

[edit] 1969/1970 season

  • Champion: Alarabi club
  • 10 matches played
    • 7 wins
    • 1 draw
    • 2 losses
  • 21 goals scored

[edit] 1970/1971 season

  • Champion: Alqadsia club
  • 10 matches played
    • 8 wins
    • 1 draw
    • 1 loss
  • 24 goals scored
