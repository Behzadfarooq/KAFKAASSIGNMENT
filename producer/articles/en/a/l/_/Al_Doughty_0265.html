Alan Doughty (born Alan Jaworski, 31 January 1966, in Plymouth, England) is a musician and bassist who is currently based in Chicago.

Doughty attended Beechen Cliff School in Bath, England between 1977 and 1982.

Originally the bassist for the London based rock band, Jesus Jones, Doughty later became the bassist for the Waco Brothers and The Dollar Store.
