Al Khalis

Al Khalis or Khalis whose population center is the city by the same name, is one of the six districts of the Diyala Governorate. The city of Al Khalis is roughly 15 kilometers (9 mi) North of Baqubah.

The Khalis district houses the Iranian terrorist group, the People's Mujahedin of Iran (PMOI) in Camp Ashraf. They are currently being protected by the U.S. military and Bulgarian Army deployed within the camp, on Forward Operating Base Grizzly.
