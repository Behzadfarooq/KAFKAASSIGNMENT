Al Massira Airport (Arabic: مطار المسيرة; transliterated: Matar al-Maseera) (IATA: AGA, ICAO: GMAD) is an airport in Agadir, Morocco.

In 2004, the airport served 1,160,127 passengers (+19% vs. '03).
