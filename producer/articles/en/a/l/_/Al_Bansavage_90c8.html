Albert A. Bansavage (born January 9, 1938) was an american football linebacker.

[edit] Professional Career

Bansavage played professionally in the American Football League for the Los Angeles Chargers in 1960 and the AFL's Oakland Raiders in 1961 .

[edit] College Career

Bansavage played college football at The Citadel and at Southern California.

[edit] High School Career

Bansavage prepped at Union City High School in Union City, NJ.
