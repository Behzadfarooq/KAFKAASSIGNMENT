Victor "Al" Pease (born 15 October 1921, Darlington, England) is a former motor racing driver from Canada. He participated in 3 Formula One Grands Prix, debuting on August 27, 1967. He holds the unfortunate distinction of being the only competitor ever to be disqualified from a Formula One race, the 1969 Canadian Grand Prix, for being too slow. Despite the brief duration of his Formula One career, Pease was highly successful in domestic Canadian motor sport competitions, and was inducted as a member of the Canadian Motorsport Hall of Fame in 1998.
