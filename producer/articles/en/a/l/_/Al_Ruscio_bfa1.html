Al Ruscio was born June 2, 1924 in Salem, Massachusetts. He has three children with wife Kate Williamson: Elizabeth Ruscio, Nina Ruscio and Michael Ruscio. All are involved in showbusiness.

[edit] Filmography

Al Ruscio has spent most of his acting career playing minor roles. Sometimes these characters are in movies or reaccuring roles in various television series. He did, however, star in three short-lived series: Shannon (1982), Steambath (1983), and Joe's Life (1993).

His first job as an actor was Gunsmoke in 1958. Early on in his career, he appeared in Al Capone (1959), 77 Sunset Strip (1959), and Bonanza (1960-1961). He has also acted in soap operas including Port Charles and Days of Our Lives. In more recent years, he has guest starred on 7th Heaven.
