Al Forat Network (Arabic: قناة الفرات الفضائية ) is a satellite television network in Iraq. The Arabic language network is owned by SCIRI the largest Shi'a political party in Iraq. Al-Forat has 300 employees with offices located in the city of Karrada in Baghdad.[1]
