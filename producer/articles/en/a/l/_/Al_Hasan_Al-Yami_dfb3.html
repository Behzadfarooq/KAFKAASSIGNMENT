Al Hasan Al-Yami

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Al Hasan Al-Yami (Arabic: الحسن اليامي) is a Saudi Arabian football player who is loved in his home country most notably for having a jet black handlebar moustache. He featured in the Football World Cup 2002 and was part of the infamous team that was beaten 8-0 in a group match against Germany. Most of his career has been played at the Saudi Arabian football club Al Ittihad.
