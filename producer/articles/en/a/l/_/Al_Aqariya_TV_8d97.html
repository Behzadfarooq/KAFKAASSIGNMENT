Al Aqariya TV (Arabic: تلفزيون العقارية‎), the first real estate channel in the gulf region, is a 24-hour Arabic language real estate Channel, presenting in-depth and up-to-the-minute coverage of regional real estate affairs, including expert reviews on the Gulf region real estate markets, live real estate index on their channel 2, Updated News, Informative Programs and talk shows between real estate giants in the region.
