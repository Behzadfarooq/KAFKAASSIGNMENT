Adalbert Weill (born December 28, 1893 in Guebwiller, France; died October 20, 1969 in Florida) was the manager of Rocky Marciano, Marty Servo, Lou Ambers, Joey Archibald and several other boxers. He moved with his father to the United States from his birthplace of France at the age of 13, where he eventually began his career. He died broke, in a nursing home.
