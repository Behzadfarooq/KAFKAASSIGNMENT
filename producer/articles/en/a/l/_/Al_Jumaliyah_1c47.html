Al Jumaliyah (Arabic: الجميلية‎) is a municipality of Qatar.

Al Jumaliyah's western edge is the shores of the Persian Gulf. On land, it borders the following municipalities:

  • Al Ghuwariyah - north
  • Al Khawr - northeast
  • Umm Salal - east
  • Ar Rayyan - southeast
  • Jariyan al Batnah - south
