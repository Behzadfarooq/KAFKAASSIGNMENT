[edit] Beth

I changed the information about Beth. I hope the previous author doesn't get angry, but the way this article was written was very misleading. Beth did not go out of her way to get the Navy to declare Al MIA so that she could escape from an unhappy marriage; she waited faithfully for a long period of time even after Al had been declared MIA (a declaration made because of Al's capture, not because of any actions on Beth's part).—The preceding unsigned comment was added by Minaker (talk • contribs) 17:56, 6 September 2006 (UTC)
