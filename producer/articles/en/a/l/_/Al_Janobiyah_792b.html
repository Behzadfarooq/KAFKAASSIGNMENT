al-Janubiyah (Arabic: الجنوبية‎ the southern) may refer to:

  • Southern Governorate, Bahrein
  • South Governorate, Lebanon
  • Janub Darfur — South Darfur, Sudan
  • Janub Kurdufan — South Kurdufan, Sudan
  • Janub Sina', Egypt
