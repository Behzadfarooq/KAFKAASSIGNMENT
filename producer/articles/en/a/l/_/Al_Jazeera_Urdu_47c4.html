Al Jazeera Urdu

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Al Jazeera Urdu is a forthcoming channel from Al Jazeera. An Urdu language version catering mainly to South Asians. It is offered as part of the ARY Digital Network.

Other products from Al Jazeera also include an English-language channel, Al Jazeera English and a channel specializing in documentaries. Al Jazeera is said to be considering launching music television channels[citation needed] or an international newspaper.[1]
