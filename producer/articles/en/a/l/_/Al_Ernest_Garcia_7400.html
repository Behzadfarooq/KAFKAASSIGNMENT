Al Ernest Garcia

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Al Ernest Garcia (11 March 1887, San Francisco, California - 4 September 1938, Los Angeles, California) was an American silent film actor.

Al starred with Charlie Chaplin in films such as The Circus.
