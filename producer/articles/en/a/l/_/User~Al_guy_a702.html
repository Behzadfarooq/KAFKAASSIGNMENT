My interest is mostly on British Columbia history and especially on Canadian Pacific Railway history. My major contributions have been the following articles:

  • explorer Walter Moberly
  • construction manager Andrew Onderdonk
  • Federal Court of Canada
  • Thompson River
  • major re-write of Rogers Pass
  • BC railways: Nelson and Fort Sheppard Railway, Columbia and Kootenay Railway, Kaslo and Slocan Railway, Nakusp and Slocan Railway

I contributed a photo for The Ocean.

I have also contributed the following maps:

  • map for Craigellachie, British Columbia
  • map for Thompson River
  • map for Revelstoke, British Columbia
  • map for Fraser River
  • map for Rocky Mountains
  • railway network maps for the Canadian Pacific Railway , Canadian National Railway, Via Rail and Nelson & Fort Sheppard Railway.
  • railway route maps for Super Continental and The Canadian.

I put together a map how-to page on making maps suitable for locator maps. I used variations on this procedure for making the above maps.
