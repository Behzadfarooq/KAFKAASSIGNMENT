Al Hidd

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Al Hidd (Arabic: الحد) was a small municipality of Bahrain in the northeastern part of the country. Its territory is now in the Muharraq Governorate.

The Hidd area is close to the Bahrain International Airport. There is also a large power plant and water desalination project in Hidd.

The Hidd Industrial Area consists of a large number of factories. The Arab Shipbuilding and Repair Yard (ASRY) is also located in Hidd.
