Al Sapienza (born July 31, 1962 in New York City, New York) is an Italian American actor, who has had numerous roles in television and film productions. He is most well known for his role as Mikey Palmice on the HBO series, The Sopranos.

[edit] Early life

Born on July 31, 1962 in the City of New York, Sapienza is of Italian descent. His four grandparents all originated from different regions of Italy.[1] He is the youngest among his four siblings. He is also brother-in-law to the infamous Bruce Rupp.
