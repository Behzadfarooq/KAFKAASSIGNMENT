Al Furqan Centre is a Salafist propaganda & educational organization, which operates in the Kingdom of Bahrain and is associated with the Islahi Movement in Kerala. The activities of other Islahi Centres in the Gulf are presently carried out in Bahrain by "Al Furqan Centre, Malayalam Wing" which is under the patronage of Jam'eyyatul Tarbiyyatul Islamiyya (Muslim Educational Society) which acts as an altar for the religious missionaries for the natives.
