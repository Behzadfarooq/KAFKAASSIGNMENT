Al Wusta is dominated by a flat, featureless, barren rock desert

Al Wusta (Arabic: الوسطى) is one of the regions (manatiq) of Oman. Its capital is Haima (Hayma).

Al Wusta Region consists of four wilayat: Haima, Duqm, Mahout and Al Jazer.
