Al Lee, born in Boston, Massachusetts[1] was an actor in the vaudeville and silent film era. He married actress Lilyan Tashman in 1914, and they were later divorced in 1921. Tashman had met Lee while working on a double act with Eddie Cantor. Lee later went on to become a manager for George White's Scandals.
