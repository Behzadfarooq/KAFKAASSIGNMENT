Al Rodrigo

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Al Rodrigo (right) with Dick Miller in the Star Trek: Deep Space Nine episode "Past Tense".

Al Rodrigo is an American character actor who has starred in films and on television. Al's film credits range from The Glass Shield, The Birdcage, and House of Sand and Fog.

Al has made guest appearances on tv shows,some of those appearances range from SeaQuest DSV, Beverly Hills, 90210, The West Wing and CSI: Miami. He also guest starred in Star Trek: Deep Space Nine in the popular season three two-part episode "Past Tense" as Bernardo Calvera.
