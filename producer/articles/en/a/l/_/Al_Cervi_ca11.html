Al Cervi

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Alfred Nicholas Cervi (February 12, 1917 in Buffalo, New York) is a former professional basketball player and coach. He played in the National Basketball League and is mostly known for his time with the Syracuse Nationals.
