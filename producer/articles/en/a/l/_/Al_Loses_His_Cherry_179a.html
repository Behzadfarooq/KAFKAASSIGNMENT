"Al Loses His Cherry" is the tenth episode of season one of the television sitcom Married... with Children.

  • First Aired: Sunday June 7, 1987 on FOX.
Spoiler warning: Plot and/or ending details follow.

[edit] Plot

Al spends some time at Luke Ventura's apartment after running out from a fight with Peg.

[edit] Guest stars

Delilah – Estelle Harris
Luke Ventura – Ritch Shydner
Sherry Cherry – Pamela Bowman
Terry Cherry – Jerry Hall

  • Writer: Sandy Sprung, Marcy Vosburgh
  • Director: Arlando Smith
