[edit] Filmmaking

I wish to be a filmmaker, and I'm trying to learn as much as I can before my next short is made.

I've recently discovered that the "Dialectic" in cinema is the process by which meaning is created when shots are put together. In that context, you can create thousands of different of meanings by how you position your shots. And since features contain hundreds, if not thousands, of shots, then the possible meanings are infinite.
