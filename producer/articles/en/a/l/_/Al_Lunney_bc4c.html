Al Lunney

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

James Alphonsus Lunney (born January 2, 1941) is a Canadian politician who lives in Mississippi Mills, a town in eastern Ontario, Canada. He is the current mayor of Mississippi Mills, having been elected to the position in 2003 and re-elected in 2006 for a four-year term.

[edit] Background

Lunney was born in Almonte, Ontario and spent his earliest years on the family farm in Pakenham, Ontario, Canada. He worked at Ottawa Hydro for 32 years and retired as Director of Human Resources in 1996.
