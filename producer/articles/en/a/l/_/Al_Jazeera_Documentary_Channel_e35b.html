Al Jazeera Documentary Channel is an Arabic language documentary channel and a branch of the Al Jazeera network. It was launched on 12:00 GMT on 1 January 2007. It aims to provide viewers an immense amount of high quality documentary films. These films are expected to cover and analyze a breadth of topics including historical, scientific, political, artistic, and travel issues.

The channel claims that it will produce 15% of broadcast material in-house, while obtaining the rest from Arab and global producers.

The supervisor of Al Jazeera Documentary Channel is Taoufik Founi.

[edit] Programming

Al Jazeera Documentary Channel concentrates on presenting documentaries from the following five fields:[1]

  • History/archaeology
  • News/current affairs/politics
  • Discovery/exploration/ethnology
  • Science/technology
  • Health/wellness
