There are a number of notable individuals named Al Qahtani, or some variant thereof:

  • Abdullah Hamid Al Qahtani -- Guantanamo detainee ID 652, born in 1979 in Mecca, Saudi Arabia.
  • Jabir Hasan Muhamed Al Qahtani -- Guantanamo detainee ID 650, born in 1978 in Mecca, Saudi Arabia.
  • Jabran Said Wazar Al Qahtani -- Guantanamo detainee ID 696, born in 1977 in Tabuk, Saudi Arabia. Alleged to have been a member of Abu Zubaydah's entourage. One of the ten detainees who faces charges before a Guantanamo military commission.
  • Khalid Malu Shia Al Ghatani aka Khalid Malluh Shayi Al Jilba Al Qahtani -- Guantanamo detainee ID 439, born in 1983 in Al Arib, Saudi Arabia.
  • Muhammad Mani Ahmded Al Shal Lan Al Qahtani aka Mohamed al-Kahtani -- Guantanamo detainee 63 One of the "20th hijackers". DoD acknowledged using controversial "extended interrogation techniques" while interrogating him.
  • Said Muhammad Husayn Qahtani -- Guantanamo detainee 200, born in 1978 in Khamees Mushail, Saudi Arabia.
  • Yasser Al Qahtani -- A Saudi Arabian football player.
