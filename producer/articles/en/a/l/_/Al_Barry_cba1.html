Al Barry is a retired american football player.

[edit] Professional Career

Barry played professionally in the National Football League for the Green Bay Packers and the New York Giants and in the American Football League for the Los Angeles Chargers in 1960.

[edit] College Career

He played college football at the University of Southern California, where he was an offensive guard.
