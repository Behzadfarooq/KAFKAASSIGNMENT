Al Jawf Province

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

For other uses, see Al Jawf.

Al Jawf IPA: [ælˈʤɑu̯f] (Arabic: الجوف) is a province of Saudi Arabia, located in the north of the country, bordering Jordan. It has an area of 139,000 km² and a population of 332,400 (1999). Its capital is Al Jawf.
