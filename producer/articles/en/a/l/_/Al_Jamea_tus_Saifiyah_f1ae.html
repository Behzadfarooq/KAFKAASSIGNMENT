Al Jamea Tus Saifiyah is an Islamic Arabic Academy situated in the heart of Surat city, India which is a leading theological University for Dawoodi Bohras. It was founded in 1814 by the 43rd Dai Syedna Abdeali Saifuddin [R.A].

A connected academy of the same name started in Karachi, Pakistan in 1983.[1]
