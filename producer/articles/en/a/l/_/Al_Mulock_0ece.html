Al Mulock (June 30, 1925 - 1968) was born in Toronto, Canada. He is best known for his roles in the Spaghetti Western movies:

[edit] Selected filmography

  • Once Upon a Time in the West (1968)
  • Day of Anger (1967)
  • Hellbenders (1967)
  • The Good, the Bad and the Ugly (1966)

Mulock committed suicide May 1968, in Guadix, Spain during shooting of Once Upon a Time in the West. He jumped from a hotel window wearing his movie costume.
