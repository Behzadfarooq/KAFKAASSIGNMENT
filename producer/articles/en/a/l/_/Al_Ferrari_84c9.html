Albert (Al) R. Ferrari (born July 6, 1933 in New York, New York) is an American former National Basketball Association player. At 6'4", and weighing 190 lbs, he played both at guard and forward. Before his basketball career he went to high school at Brooklyn Tech, in Brooklyn, New York and after he attended college at Michigan State University. He was drafted by the St. Louis Hawks in the 3rd round (1st pick) of the 1955 NBA Draft.

For the 1957-58 NBA season he was not on the team's roster due to a commitment to military service.[1]
