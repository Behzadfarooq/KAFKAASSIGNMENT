Algebra is a branch of mathematics.

Algebra may also mean:

  • elementary algebra
  • abstract algebra
  • linear algebra
  • universal algebra
  • computer algebra

In addition, many mathematical objects are known as algebras.

  • In logic:
    • Boolean algebra
    • Heyting algebra
  • In set theory:
    • algebra over a set
    • sigma algebra
  • In linear algebra, and the study of vector spaces:
    • algebra over a field
    • associative algebra
    • commutative, anticommutative, and super- algebras
    • Lie algebra
  • In ring theory:
    • algebra over a commutative ring (or R-algebra)
  • In category theory:
    • F-algebra
    • F-coalgebra
  • In the relational model:
    • Relational algebra
