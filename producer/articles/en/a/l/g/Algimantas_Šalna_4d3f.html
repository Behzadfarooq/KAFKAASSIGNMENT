Algimantas Šalna (born September 12, 1959) is a former Soviet/Lithuanian biathlete.

[edit] Career

  • World Championships
  • 1983 Anterselva: Gold medal on the relay
  • 1985 Ruhpoldinh: Gold medal on the relay
  • Winter Olympics
  • 1984 Sarajevo: Gold medal on the relay
