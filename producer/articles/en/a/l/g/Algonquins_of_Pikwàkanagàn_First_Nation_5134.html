The Algonquins of Pikwàkanagàn First Nation are an Algonquin First Nation in Ontario, Canada. Their territory is located in the Ottawa Valley on Golden Lake. Their reserve of 688.8 hectares is located near Killaloe Station, Ontario, not far from Pembroke. As of June 2005, the registered population of the First Nation was 1,899 people, the great majority of whom lived off reserve.
