Algernon Percy can refer to:

  • Algernon Percy, 1st Earl of Beverley, FSA (21 January 1750 - 21 October 1830), British peer, known as Lord Algernon Percy from 1766-86.
  • Algernon Percy, 4th Duke of Northumberland (15 December 1792 - 12 February 1865), British aristocrat and Conservative politician
  • Algernon George Percy, 6th Duke of Northumberland, KG, PC (20 May 1810 - 2 January 1899), British Conservative politician

  • Henry Algernon Percy, 5th Earl of Northumberland KG, (13 January 1477 - 19 May 1527), eldest son of Henry Percy, 4th Earl of Northumberland and Maud Herbert.
  • Hugh Algernon Percy, 10th Duke of Northumberland KG, GCVO, KStJ ,PC, TD, FRS (April 6, 1914 - October 11, 1988), son of Alan Percy, 8th Duke of Northumberland and Lady Helen Gordon-Lennox.
