Algeria national basketball team

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

You have new messages (last change).

The Algeria national basketball team are the men's basketball side that represent Algeria in international competition and have played in the 2002 FIBA World Championship in United States where they finished 15th in the tourament.
