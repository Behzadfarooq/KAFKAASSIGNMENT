Algerian national ice hockey team

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Algerian national ice hockey team is the national men's ice hockey team of Algeria. Formed in 2006, Algeria currently is not a member of the IIHF and does not currently have its own ice rink, however the national team was formed with the increasing number of Algerians who are playing ice hockey around the world.

The most high profile player of Algerian origin is Josef Boumedienne who played for Tampa Bay Lightning, New Jersey Devils and Washington Capitals in the NHL.
