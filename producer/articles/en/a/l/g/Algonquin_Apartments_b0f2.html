Algonquin Apartments
(U.S. National Register of Historic Places)
Location: Miami, Florida
Added to NRHP: January 4, 1989

The Algonquin Apartments is a historic site in Miami, Florida. It is located at 1819-1825 Biscayne Boulevard. On January 4, 1989, it was added to the U.S. National Register of Historic Places.
