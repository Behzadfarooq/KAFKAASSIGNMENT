Algeria competed at the 1980 Summer Olympics in Moscow, USSR. The nation returned to the Olympic Games after boycotting the 1976 Summer Olympics.

[edit] Boxing

For more details on this topic, see Boxing at the 1980 Summer Olympics.

Men's Light Flyweight (– 48 kg)

  • Ahmed Siad
    1. First Round — Bye
    2. Second Round — Defeated Lincoln Salcedo (Ecuador) on points (5-0)
    3. Quarter Finals — Lost to Ismail Mustafov (Bulgaria) on points (0-5)


Men's Light-Welterweight (– 63,5 kg)

  • Boualem Belaouane
    1. First Round — Defeated Barrington Cambridge (Guyana) on points (5-0)
    2. Second Round — Lost to Ace Rusevski (Yugoslavia) on points (0-5)
