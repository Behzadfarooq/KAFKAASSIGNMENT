Algernon Kingscote

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Algernon R. F. Kingscote (born December 3, 1888 in Bangalore, India; died 1964, Surrey, England) was a male tennis player from Great Britain.

He is best remembered for winning the men's singles event at the Australian Championships in 1919.
