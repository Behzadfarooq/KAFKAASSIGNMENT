Algernon Seymour, 15th Duke of Somerset (1846 – October 29, 1923) was the son of Algernon St. Maur, 14th Duke of Somerset and Horatia Morler.

On September 5, 1877, he married Susan Margaret Richards Mackinnon. Because Seymour did not have any children, his titles passed to his nearest relative, Edward Seymour.
