ALGOL Bulletin

A publication of Association for Computing Machinery regarding the ALGOL 60 and ALGOL 68 programming languages. Published for almost 30 years.

[edit] Time-line of ALGOL Bulletin

Year Event Contributor
Mar 1959 ALGOL Bulletin Issue 1 (First) Peter Naur / ACM
Dec 1968 Algol 68 Final Report Presented at Munich Meeting IFIP Working Group 2.1
Jun 1977 Strathclyde ALGOL 68 conference, Scotland ACM
Aug 1988 ALGOL Bulletin Issue 52 (last) Ed. C. H. Lindsey / ACM
