Algonquin Students' Association

The Algonquin Students' Association (SA) is a not-for-profit corporation with over 30 years of service. They represent students at Algonquin College in Ottawa, Canada. Algonquin College has campuses in Ottawa, Perth, and Pembroke though the SA represents students at only Ottawa and Pembroke.

It is their mission to create an environment that inspires a passion for student success. A Board of Directors governs the Students' Association and they make all the key decisions that affect students.

Jack Doyle, MBA, serves as General Manager and Chief Executive Officer of the SA.

The SA had been affiliated with the College Student Alliance (CSA) and SA President, Michael G. Barrett, served as President of the CSA in 2005. Barrett resigned from the CSA Board when the SA withdrew its membership that year.

[edit] The Board

The Board consists of nine directors who make all the key decisions that affect students. They govern the management of the corporation.
