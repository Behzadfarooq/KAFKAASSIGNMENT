Algeciras Club de Fútbol is a football team based in Algeciras in the autonomous community of Andalusia. Founded in 1912, its plays in Tercera División - Group 10. Her stadium is Estadio Nuevo Mirador with capacity of 7,100 seaters.

[edit] Seasons

  • 2004/2005: Segunda División B 6th
  • 2005/2006: Segunda División B 19th - Relegated
  • 2006/2007: Tercera División
  • 9 seasons in Segunda División
  • 13 seasons in Segunda División B
  • 34 seasons in Tercera División
