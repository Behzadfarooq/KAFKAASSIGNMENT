Colonel Almerindo Jaka Jamba (born 21 March 1949) is an Angolan politician and former rebel leader in UNITA. He studied in Portugal where he had a degree in philosophic sciences. He is a member of Angolan National Assembly with UNITA and Angolan ambassador to UNESCO. He was previously the deputy speaker of Angolan parliament from 2002-2004. In 1975, Jamba was named Secretary of State by Jonas Savimbi under UNITA in the so called "transition govern" that was set from January of 1975 to July of the same year in accordance of the "Alvor agreements". He is currently the oldest member of UNITA.
