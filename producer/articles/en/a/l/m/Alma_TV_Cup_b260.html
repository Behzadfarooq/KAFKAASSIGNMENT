Alma TV Cup

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Alma TV Cup is an international football competition for national teams held in Kazakhstan won this year by Kazakhstan. It is sponsored by the cable TV company Alma TV. 2007 is its inaugural year.

These teams are invited to the first cup: Kazakhstan, Uzbekistan, Kyrgyzstan, and Azerbaijan, in 7-13 March 2007 in Shymkent at Munaytpasova Stadium.[1]

Kyrgyzstan replaced Turkmenistan who withdrew[2]
