Alma Hodge was a fictional character on the ABC television series Desperate Housewives. The character was played by Valerie Mahaffey.

[edit] History

“ I want what I've always wanted, Orson. You. ”
 
— Alma Hodge
Spoiler warning: Plot and/or ending details follow.


Alma was the first wife of Orson Hodge. In the season 3 premiere, it is revealed that she was planning to leave Orson, but Orson found out about this at the last moment. Bree became suspicious that Orson killed her. After several months, Alma returned to Wisteria Lane to reveal she is alive and well, having lived in Winnipeg with an aunt since she left Orson, and wanting to reconcile with him. After a welcome dinner which was held by Orson's new wife Bree, Alma moved into the neighborhood permanently and purchased the Applewhite home. She also knew about Orson running over Mike Delfino.

Alma wanted a child so desperately that she faked her own suicide. Gloria Hodge, Orson's mother, then drugged him while Alma raped him in order to get pregnant. When Bree learned of Orson's whereabouts, she rushed over and, after she realized that he was knocked out cold, she put together that Alma indeed raped Orson. She punched Alma, knocking her out, and then attempted to get Orson home. After Alma learned that Orson told Bree everything and that she has forgiven him to an extent, Gloria locked her in a closet. While trying to escape and to try and get Danielle Van De Kamp to notice her Alma loses her grip on the roof and falls to her death.
