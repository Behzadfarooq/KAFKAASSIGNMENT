Almamy Schuman Bah (born August 24, 1974) is a Guinean football player.

He was part of the Guinean 2004 African Nations Cup team, who finished second in their group in the first round of competition, before losing in the quarter finals to Mali. In 2004, he went from FC Metz to Turkish Super League team Malatyaspor. However, because of injuries, he could play only 15 matches for the Turkish side and his contract was cancelled at the end of season and he moved to Clermont Foot.

Other teams he has played for are Le Mans UC72, Berrichonne de Châteauroux, Neuchâtel Xamax and Ipswich Town.
