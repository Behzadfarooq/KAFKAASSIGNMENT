ALMA is a Spanish lifestyle magazine published in the United States, intended to provide high quality content for all the Hispanic community residing in that country.

The magazine focuses on culture, fashion and politics mainly. However, it also features articles on Latin America, the world, travel, economy, music, real estate, technology, movies, and books.
