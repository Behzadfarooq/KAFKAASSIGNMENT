Alma Swanley F.C.

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Alma Swanley F.C was an English football (soccer) club based in Swanley. They were founding members of the London Spartan League in 1975 and joined the Kent League in 1982. They won the title in 1986 but resigned from the league in 1994 and appear to have subsequently folded. [1]
