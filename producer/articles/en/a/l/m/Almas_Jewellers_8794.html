Almas Jewellers

Almas Jewellers is a company in the jewellery business. Its products include 22 carat gold and diamond jewellery. It was founded in 1967 in Liaquatabad Town Karachi, Sindh, in Pakistan by Ishtiaque Ahmed, and is owned by Almas Ishtiaque group of industries.

Almas Jewellers has pending cases with companies that have broken copyright rules by using their name in other countries. Almas Jewellers has the following branches:

  • Almas Jewellers, Liaqatabad, Karachi-Pakistan
  • Almas Collection Jewellers, Saddar, Karachi-Pakistan
  • Almas Jewellers Inc., Edmond, Oklahoma, USA

Almas Jewellers is currently introducing a new line of diamond jewellery with the support of Adnan Ishtiaque, a certified GIA gemologist and is also the son of the founder Ishtiaque Ahmed.
