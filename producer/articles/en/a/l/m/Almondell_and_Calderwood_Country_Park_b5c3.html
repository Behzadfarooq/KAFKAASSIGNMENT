Almondell and Calderwood Country Park

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

River Almond and Naismith Bridge

Almondell and Calderwood Country Park is a 220-acre Country Park in Mid Calder and East Calder in West Lothian, Scotland. The Almondell estate originally belonged to the Erskine family of Broxburn whilst the Calderwood estate once belonged to the Barons of Torphichen.

A Visitor Centre is housed in the former stable block of the Almondell Estate, with a coffee shop, aquarium, and classroom and function room facilities. There are many woodland and riverside walks, BBQ sites, toilet facilities and children's play areas. The Ranger Service arranges talks and school visits.
