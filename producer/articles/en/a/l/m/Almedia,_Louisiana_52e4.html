Almedia, Louisiana

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Almedia is a small community in St. Charles Parish, Louisiana, on the East Bank of the Mississippi River, just up from Kenner, Louisiana, adjacent to Frellsen.

Almedia is considered part of the Greater New Orleans Metropolitan area.
