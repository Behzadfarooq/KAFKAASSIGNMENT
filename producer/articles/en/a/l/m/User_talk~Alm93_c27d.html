[edit] French Canadians category

Please note that the provinces of Manitoba, Ontario and Quebec already have their own dedicated subcategories (Category:Franco-Manitobans, Category:Franco-Ontarians, Category:French Quebecers), as do Category:Acadians. People from those provinces are to be filed in those categories, not directly in Category:French Canadians; the French Canadians category is to be directly applied only to people from the other provinces that don't have their own separate subcategories. Bearcat 17:55, 10 October 2006 (UTC)

Also, place such categories with the other category links, not below the interlanguage (i.e. "[[fr:Paul Piché]]") links. Bearcat 17:57, 10 October 2006 (UTC)
