Almir Tolja

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Almir Tolja (Persian: المیر تولیا‎ , born October 25, 1974 in Travnik, Bosnia and Herzegovina) is a Bosnian football goalkeeper.

[edit] Club career

  • FK Sarajevo (2000-01)
  • NK Čelik Zenica (2001-02)
  • SC Bregenz (2002-05)
  • Saba Battery FC (2005-)

[edit] International career

Almir Tolja is part of the Bosnia and Herzegovina national football team.
