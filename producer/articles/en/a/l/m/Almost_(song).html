Almost is a song by the band, Bowling for Soup. It was released as the second single of their album, A Hangover You Don't Deserve in 2005. It tells the story of a young adult who goes through many 'almost' experiences during his life, from almost making out with the homecoming queen to almost getting addicted to drugs. He shows remorse for not 'making his move' earlier, and he almost wishes she loved him too.

There is a version of this song with cleaner lyrics that plays on Radio Disney. The chorus remains the same, but the first two verses are changed to remove references to sex, guns and drugs.
