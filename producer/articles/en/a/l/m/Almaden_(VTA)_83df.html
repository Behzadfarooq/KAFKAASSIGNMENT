Almaden (VTA) is a light rail station operated by Santa Clara Valley Transportation Authority (VTA). This station is the southern Terminus of VTA's Ohlone/Chynoweth - Almaden line, popularly known as the Almaden Shuttle.

[edit] Location

Almaden station is located near Winfield Blvd and Coleman south of Blossom Hill in southern San Jose, California.

[edit] Station amenities

  • Bicycle parking
  • Payphone
  • Park and Ride Lot
  • Wheelchair accessible
