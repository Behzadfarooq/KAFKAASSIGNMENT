Almost Here is the first full-length album by The Academy Is..., released on February 8, 2005 by Fueled by Ramen. It debuted at #20 of the Billboard Heatseakers chart, as well as #24 of the Billboard Independent Albums chart.[1] The album spawned three singles: "Checkmarks"; "Slow Down", which was chosen through an online poll on the band's website; and "The Phrase that Pays."
