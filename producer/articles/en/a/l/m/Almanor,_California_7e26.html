Almanor is a census-designated place located in Plumas County, California. As of the 2000 census, the town had a total population of 0.

[edit] Geography

Almanor is located at 40°13'1" North, 121°10'38" West (40.216857, -121.177325)GR1.

According to the United States Census Bureau, the town has a total area of 2.3 km² (0.9 mi²). 2.3 km² (0.9 mi²) of it is land and none of it is covered by water.

[edit] Demographics

As of the censusGR2 of 2000, there are no people living in the town.
