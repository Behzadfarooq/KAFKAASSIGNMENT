Almost Human (2000) is an album by the Dark Cabaret/Darkwave artist Voltaire. It was released in 2000 by Projekt Records.

The tracks "Dead Girls" and "The Headless Waltz" also appear on Voltaire's greatest hits album, Deady Sings!. A live version of the song "God Thinks" appears on Voltaire's live album, Live!.
