[edit] Kung fu cult cinema

A tag has been placed on Kung fu cult cinema, requesting that it be speedily deleted from Wikipedia. This has been done because it is an article about a certain website, blog, forum, or other web content that does not assert the importance or significance of that web location. Please read our criteria for speedy deletion, particularly item 7 under Articles, as well as notability guidelines for websites. Please note that articles must be on notable subjects and should provide references to reliable sources which verify their content.

Please do not remove the speedy deletion tag yourself. To contest the tagging and request that administrators wait a while for you to add contextual material, please affix the template {{hangon}} to the page, and put a note on Talk:Kung fu cult cinema. Feel free to leave a note on my talk page if you have any questions about this. --Nenyedi TalkContribs@ 23:29, 21 March 2007 (UTC)

Hello! Sorry I couldn't get back to you quicker. In the meantime, the page was deleted. I am not quite sure what was on the page, but it was deleted under CSD A7 Web. If you would like to re-create the article, you must provide information as to the notability of the website. You can read the notability guidelines here: WP:WEB.

About the {{hangon}} tags- simply add {{hangon}} to the top of the article by clicking edit page, just like you would do to add content. You will also see the speedy deletion tag, BUT DO NOT REMOVE THE SPEEDY TAG. Both must be intact for the process to go smooth.

If you have any more questions about contributing here, please leave me a message on my talk page. I am a high school student, so I may not be able to get back immediately. You can also post a message on the help desk.
