Almond Valley Light Railway
Locale Scotland
Dates of operation 1993 – Present
Track gauge 2 ft 6 in (762 mm)
Length ¼ mile
Headquarters Livingston

The Almond Valley Light Railway is a narrow gauge heritage railway running at the Almond Valley Heritage Trust site at Livingston, Scotland.
