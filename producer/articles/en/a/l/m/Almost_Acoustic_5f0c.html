Almost Acoustic is a 1988 live acoustic album by the Jerry Garcia Acoustic Band which was the band's one and only release. Held in high esteem by fans for superb sound quality and fine musical selection, it was culled from 18 performances at the Lunt-Fontanne Theatre during the fall of 1987.
