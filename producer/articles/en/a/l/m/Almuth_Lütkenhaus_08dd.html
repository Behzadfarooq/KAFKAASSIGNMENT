Almuth Lütkenhaus

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Almuth Lütkenhaus (née Wirsing; born 8 March 1930 in Hamm; died 1996 in Hamilton, Ontario)[citation needed] was a sculptor. From 1948 until 1952 she studied art at schools in Dortmund and Münster. She married Erich Lütkenhaus, an artist, in 1952, from whom she later separated. She lived in Soest from 1962 until 1966, when she moved to Canada, where she died in 1996.
