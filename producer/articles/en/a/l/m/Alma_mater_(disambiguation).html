Alma mater is Latin for "nourishing mother". In many modern languages it is principally heard as a term of academia.

Alma mater may also refer to:

[edit] Academia

  • Alma Mater Studiorum ("Nourishing mother of studies"), the name of the University of Bologna
  • Alma Mater, a sculpture on Green Street and Wright Street in University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign
  • Alma Mater, a sculpture in the center of the campus of Columbia University
  • Alma Mater Society, the central undergraduate student government of Queen's University in Kingston, Ontario
  • Alma Mater, the title of a Polish academic publication, produced by Jagiellonian University

[edit] Culture and entertainment

  • "Alma Mater", the title of various songs, by such artists as Chicago, Moonspell, Alice Cooper, and Viktor Berkovsky and Dmitri Sukharev
  • "Alma Matters", the title of a 1997 single by Morrissey
