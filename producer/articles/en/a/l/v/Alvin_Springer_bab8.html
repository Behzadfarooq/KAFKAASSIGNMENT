Alvin Springer

Alvin Springer was one of 3 founding partners in Andial (Arnold Wagner and Dieter Inzenhofer were the other two), a world renowned builder and tuner of Porsche street cars and race cars.

Alvin, Dieter, and Arnold had a long relationship with Porsche AG and Porsche cars so much so that Alvin was recruited by Porsche AG to head Porsche Motorsports North America (PMNA). The racing side of the Andial operation was basically converted to PMNA's facility.

Before Alvin moved to PMNA the 3 person Andial team was responsible for many sports car wins on the track including many 962's, 935's, 911's, and many others!
