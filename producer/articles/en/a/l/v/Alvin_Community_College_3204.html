Alvin Community College is a public two-year college in Alvin, Texas.

[edit] Curriculum

Alvin Community College has a wide variety of university transfer and career programs including, but not limited to:

  • Business Administration
  • English
  • Mathematics
  • Speech
  • Biological Science
  • General Liberal Arts
  • Music - Instrumental/Voice/Musical Theater
  • Psychology
  • Court Reporting
  • Nursing
  • Broadcast Communications - Radio/TV
  • Law Enforcement Academy
  • Emergency Medical Technician

[edit] Cities and communities within the Alvin Community College district

All of the following cities:

  • Alvin
  • Brookside Village
  • Iowa Colony
  • Liverpool
  • Manvel

Portions of the following cities:

  • Pearland (portions in Pearland ISD and Alvin ISD)

Portions of the following communities:

  • Rosharon (portion in Alvin ISD)

All residents of Pearland ISD and Alvin ISD are within the college's tax district.
