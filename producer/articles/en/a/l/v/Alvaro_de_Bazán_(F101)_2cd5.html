The Alvaro de Bazán is the lead ship of a new class of air defence frigates entering service with the Spanish Navy.

In February 2007 The Alvaro de Bazan became the first Spanish Naval ship to visit Australia since the Bilbao in 1859. The main purpose of the warship's visit was to promote the Spanish bid to build the Royal Australian Navy's new Air Warfare Destroyer.
