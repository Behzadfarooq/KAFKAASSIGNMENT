Alvito (pron. IPA: [aɫ'vitu]) is a municipality in Portugal with a total area of 264.8 km² and a total population of 2,708 inhabitants.

The municipality is composed of 2 parishes, and is located in the District of Beja.

The present Mayor is João Lança Trindade.

The municipal holiday is Ascension Day.
