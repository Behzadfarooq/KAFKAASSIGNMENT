Alvin Purple Rides Again is a 1974 Australian sex-comedy film sequel to Alvin Purple. It was directed by David Bilcock and Robin Copping.

[edit] Plot summary

Spoiler warning: Plot and/or ending details follow.

The irresistible Alvin Purple discovers he is identical in appearance to gangster Balls McGee. When Balls is killed, Alvin is forced to take his place.
