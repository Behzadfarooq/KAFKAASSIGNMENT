This article is about the ski resort in Iran. For the common Portuguese surname, meaning Son of Álvaro, see Álvares. The Spanish variant is Alvarez.

Alvares is also a ski resort situated in Iranian province of Ardabil. It is the second standard ski resort in Iran and is situated in the hamlet of Alvares which is located 24 kilometers away from the city of Sarein and at an altitude of 3,200 meters above sea level.
