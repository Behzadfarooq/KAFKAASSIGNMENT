Alveston F.C.

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The club badge

Alveston F.C. is a football club based in Alveston, near Thornbury, Warwickshire, England. They were established in 1927. In 1993, they joined the Midland Football Combination Division Three. Currently they are members of the Midland Football Combination Premier Division.
