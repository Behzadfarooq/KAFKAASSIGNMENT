Alí Manuel Manouchehri Moghadam Kashan Lobos (more commonly know as Alí Manouchehri, born August 2, 1986 in Vienna) is an Iranian-Chilean football player born to an Iranian father and Chilean mother, who currently plays for Levante UD in the La Liga.

[edit] Club career

Manouchehri played for Coquimbo Unido before transferring to Levante UD on January 30, 2007 on a deal that will keep him at the club until 2009. [1]

[edit] International career

He is also a member of the Chile national under-23 football team.
