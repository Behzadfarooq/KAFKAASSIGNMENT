Alíz Derekas (born August 3, 1977) is a Hungarian astronomer who co-discovered (82092) 2001 DV86 whilst working at Piszkéstető Station, Konkoly Obszervatórium, Hungary.

She was born in Kalocsa, Hungary and currently works at the University of Sydney in Sydney, Australia.
