Al-Qadisiya is a Saudi Arabian football team playing at the top level. It is based in Khobar. Their home stadium is Prince Saud bin Jalawi Stadium.

[edit] Achievements

  • Crown Prince Cup: 1
    • Champion : 1992.
  • Saudi Federation cup: 1
    • Champion : 1994.
  • Asian Cup Winners Cup: 1
    • Champion : 1993
