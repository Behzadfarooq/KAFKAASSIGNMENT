Al Ittihad Tripoli (Arabic: الاتحاد‎) is a Libyan football club based in Bab Ben Ghesher -Tripoli, Libya. They are Libya's most successful club having won the Libyan Championship twelve times and the Libyan Cup four times. They are the current league champions (Libyan Championship) as of the end of the 2005/06 season.

[edit] History

Al-Ittihad Club was founded on Saturday July 29, 1944 after a merger between two clubs Al Nahda & Al Shabab.ok :)

[edit] Honors

  • Libyan Premier League
    • Champion : 1965, 1966, 1969, 1986, 1988, 1989, 1990, 1991, 2002, 2003, 2005, 2006
  • Libyan Cup
    • Winner : 1992, 1999, 2004, 2005
  • Libyan SuperCup
    • Winner : 1999, 2002, 2003, 2004, 2005, 2006

[edit] African Championships

  • African Cup Winners' Cup
    • Semi-Final: 2000

[edit] Stadium

[edit] 11 June Stadium

  • Capacity: 65,000
  • Place: Tripoli, Libya - 6km from center of Tripoli-
  • Inauguration: 1970

11 June Stadium is the formal ground for Al-Ittihad since 1970's.


[edit] 7 October Stadium

  • Capacity: 10,000
  • Place: Center Of Tripoli, Libya
  • Inauguration: 1950

7 October Stadium is Al-Ittihad’s training ground, used currently as a training ground.


[edit] Other Sports

Al Ittihad got teams competing in different other sports. Al Ittihad's Handball , Volleyball and Basketball teams have won many national competitions.
