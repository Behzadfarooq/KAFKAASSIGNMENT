Al-Arqam was a former religious movement in Malaysia. The movement was banned by the federal government on June 1993. More then 5 Al-Arqam members including Ashaari Mohammad (leader of movement) were arrest under Internal Security Act (ISA) in the main camp at Kampung Sungai Penchala, Kuala Lumpur.
