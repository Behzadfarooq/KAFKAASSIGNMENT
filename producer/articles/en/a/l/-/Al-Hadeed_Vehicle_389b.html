Al-Hadeed Vehicle

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Al Hadeed is a recovery vehicle manufactured by Heavy Industries Taxilla HIT. It is based on APC-Talha with a crane, 15-tonne hydraulic winch, mill, lathe and other tools to fix and recover vehicles in the field. It weighs 12,521 kg and has armour similar to that of the United States M113P. [1]

Pakistan has so far no interest to export this vehicle.
