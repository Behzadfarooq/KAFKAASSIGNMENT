Al-Merrikh (Arabic: نادي المريخ السوداني ) is a Sudanese football club based in Omdurman, Sudan. Along with AlHilal and Al-Mourada, it forms the triplet of Sudanese football. Their home stadium is Stade de Al-Merrikh.

[edit] Name and Meaning

Al-Merrikh is the Arabic translation for planet Mars.

[edit] Leagues

Primarily, Al-Merrikh competes in the Sudanese Premier league, Sudan's top football league. Based on the club's end-of-season standing, it competes in the Arabic and African CAF tournaments including Arab Champions League, African Champions League or African Confederation Cup.
