Al-Wakra (Arabic: الوكرة) is a Qatari professional football (soccer) club. They are based in Al Wakrah and play at the Al-Wakrah Stadium.

[edit] Achievements

  • Qatari League: 2

1999, 2001

  • Qatar Crown Prince Cup: 1

1999

  • Qatar Sheikh Jassem Cup: 2

1999 , 2005
