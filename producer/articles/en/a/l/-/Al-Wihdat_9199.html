Al-Wihdat (Arabic: الوحدات) is a sports club founded in 1956 and located in the Amman New Camp. This local club is considered to be one of the strongest in Jordan. In football, it has a fierce rivalry with long-time local rivals Al-Faisaly. The club has won the Jordanian league eight times and the Jordanian cup six times. There are several other sports offered in this club, such as volleyball, basketball, and table tennis.
