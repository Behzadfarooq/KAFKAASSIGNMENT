The name ابوالحسن عامرى or" حسن عامرى" ( Persian )

  • Al-Amir Bi-Ahkamillah, the tenth Fatimid Caliph.
  • Al Amiri العميري, was an Iranian philosopher, who spent most of his life in Eastern provinces of Iran & died in Neyshaboor 992/381, was the most prominent muslim philosopher following the tradition of Kindi in Islamic Philosophy.
  • Saheb al-Amiri was Muqtada al-Sadr's top aide, was killed 27 Dec 2006 in a raid by U.S. troops in the Shiite holy city of Najaf
  • Hadi Al-Amiri is the head of the Badr Organization, which was a military wing of the Supreme Council for the Islamic Revolution in Iraq (SCIRI). Moreover, he is a member of the Iraqi parliament[1] under the United Iraqi Alliance list, which mainly represents religious Shiite parties, some of which are strongly pro-Iranian.
