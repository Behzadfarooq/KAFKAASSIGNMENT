Al-Ittihad (Arabic: الاتحاد‎) is a Syrian football club based in Aleppo. It was founded in 1953. They play at the Hamadaniah Stadium.

[edit] Achievements

  • Syrian League: 6

1967, 1968, 1977, 1993, 1995, 2005

  • Syrian Cup: 8

1966, 1973, 1982, 1984, 1985, 1994, 2005, 2006
