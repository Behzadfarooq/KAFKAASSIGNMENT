Hakim ‘Ali ibn Kamal al-Din Muhammad al-Jilani (Arabic: حكيم علي بن كمال الدين محمد الجيلاني) was a 16th century Persian royal physician from Gilan, Iran.

He came from Persia to the Mughal court of Akbar and served under several Mughal rulers in northwest India. He is particularly known for his commentary on The Canon of Medicine by Avicenna.

Hakim ‘Ali al-Jilani died on 14 Dhu al-Hijjah 1017AH, or 22 March 1609.
