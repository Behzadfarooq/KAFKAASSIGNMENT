Al-Arabi (Arabic: العربي) Sports Club, also known as Al-Arabi Doha, is a Qatari professional football (soccer) club. They are based in Doha. Al Arabi has dominated the Qatari league for decades, they are known as "fareeg al ahlam" which is translated to "the dream team." Their stadium is called Grand Hamad Stadium.
