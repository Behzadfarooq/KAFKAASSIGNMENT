Al-Gharrafa (Arabic: الغرافة) is a Qatari football club from Doha. It was founded in 1979 as Al Ittihad Doha and renamed in 2004. Al Gharrafa Currently stands 2nd in the Qatari top division league with 34 pts. behind Al Sadd 48 pts.
