Al-Thani

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Al-Thani family is the ruling family of Qatar. They belong to the Al-Maadeed of the Bani Tameem tribe. They include:

  • Muhammad bin Thani Ruler of Qatar (1868-1878) (first ruler)
  • Ahmad ibn 'Ali Al Thani, Emir 1960- 1972
  • Khalifa bin Hamad Al Thani, Emir 1972-1995
  • Hamad bin Khalifa, present Emir
  • Sheikha Mozah Bint Nasser Al-Missned, wife of present Emir
  • Sheikh Tamim Bin Hamad Al-Thani, Crown Prince
  • Abdullah bin Khalifah Al Thani, present PM, younger brother of the Emir
  • Hamad ibn Jaber al Thani, present Foreign Minister, cousin of the Emir
