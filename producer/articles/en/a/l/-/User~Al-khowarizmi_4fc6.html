[edit] Contributions I may make

This will probably change before much is actually written. There is a large number of articles on related topics - lasers etc, much of which I have yet to read. Initial impression is that there could be major improvement with collation, redistribution, and better cross-linking within the existing content.

Also suggestions regarding appropriate article titles & disambiguation (e.g. correlation, for which an article exists on the more specific topic of statistical correlation.
