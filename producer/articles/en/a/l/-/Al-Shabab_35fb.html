This article is about a football team from Saudi Arabia. For a football team from the United Arab Emirates, see Al-Shabab (UAE).

Al-Shabab (Arabic: الشباب) is a Saudi Arabian professional football club based in Riyadh. It was founded in 1947.

[edit] Achievements

  • Saudi Premier League: 5
    • Champion : 1991, 1992, 1993, 2004, 2006
  • Crown Prince Cup: 3
    • Champion : 1993, 1996, 1999
  • Saudi Federation cup: 2
    • Champion : 1988, 1989
  • Asian Cup Winners Cup: 1
    • Champion : 2001
  • Arab Champions League: 2
    • Champion :1992, 1999
  • Arab Cup Winners Cup: 2
    • Champion : 1994, 1997
  • Arab Super Cup: 2
    • Champion : 1996 , 2001
  • Gulf Club Champions Cup: 3
    • Champion : 1993 , 1994
