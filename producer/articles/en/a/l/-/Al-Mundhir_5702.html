Al-Mundhir (Arabic: المنذر ), (c. 842 - 888) was Emir of Cordoba from 886 to 888. He was a member of the Umayyad dynasty of Al-Andalus (Moorish Iberia), the son of Muhamad bin Abd al-Rahman, amir al-Qurtubi.
