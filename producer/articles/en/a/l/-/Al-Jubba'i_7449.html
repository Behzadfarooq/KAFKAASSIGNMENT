Al-Jubba'i

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Abu 'Ali Muhammad al-Jubba'i (Arabic: أبو على محمد الجوبعي) was an Arab Mu'tazili philosopher of the 10th century. He was born in Khuzistan and died in 915-6.
