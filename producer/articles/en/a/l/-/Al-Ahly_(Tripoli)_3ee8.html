Al Ahly Tripoli (Arabic: الأهلي طرابلس) is a Libyan football club based in Tripoli, Libya.


[edit] Achievements

  • Libyan Premier League: 10
1964, 1971, 1973, 1974, 1978, 1984, 1993, 1994, 1995, 2000
  • Libyan Cup: 5
1975, 1995, 2000, 2001, 2006
  • Libyan SuperCup: 1
2000
