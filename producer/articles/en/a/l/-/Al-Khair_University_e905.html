Al-Khair University

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Al-Khair University has several campuses in Pakistan. The University has established Faculty of Engineering and Faculty of Management Science at Abbottabad, which will gradually be followed by other disciplines. It has established faculties at Mirpur in Azad Kashmir, Lahore, Islamabad and Peshawar. Now the Faculty of Business Administration & Computer Science has been started at Karachi, Sukkur, and Larkana.

Al-University is recognised by the Higher Education Commission, Islamabad.
