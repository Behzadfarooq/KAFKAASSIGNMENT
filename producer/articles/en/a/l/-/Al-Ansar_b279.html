Al-Ansar is a Lebanese football club. They won the Lebanese national league every year between 1990 and 1999 and won the title back in 2006.


[edit] Achievements

  • Lebanese Premier League: 12
1988, 1990, 1991, 1992, 1993, 1994, 1995, 1996, 1997, 1998, 1999, 2006
  • Lebanese FA Cup: 9
1998, 1990, 1991, 1992, 1994, 1995, 1996, 1999, 2002, 2006
  • Lebanese Super Cup: 4
1996, 1997, 1998, 1999
  • Lebanese Federation Cup: 2
1999, 2000
  • Lebanese Elite Cup: 2
1997, 2000
