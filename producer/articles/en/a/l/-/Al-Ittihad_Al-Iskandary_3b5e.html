Al-Itthad Al-Iskandary is one of the biggest teams soccer teams in Egypt. The team, who play in the Egyptian Premier League, have won the Egyptian soccer cup 6 times.

The basketball team in Itthad is the biggest team in Egypt. It has won the Egyptian Basketball League 14 times and the cup 11 times. They have been Arab champs 4 times and African champs once.
