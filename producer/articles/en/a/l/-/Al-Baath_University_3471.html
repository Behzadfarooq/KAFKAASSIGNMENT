Website http://www.albaath-univ.edu.sy/

Al-Baath University (Arabic: جامعة البعث‎), founded in 1978, is Syria's 4th largest university and is a public university located in the city of Homs, Syria, 180 km North of Damascus. It has more than 18 faculties.
