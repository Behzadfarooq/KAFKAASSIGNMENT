Abu-Abd-Allah al-Zurqani (851/January 28, 1448)[1] was an Islamic scholar.

[edit] Name

His full name was Imam Shihab al-Din Abu al-Abbas Ahmad ibn Muhammad ibn Abu Bakr ibn Abd al-Malik ibn Ahmad al-Qastallani al-Qutaybi al-Shafi'i [4], also shortened as Qutb al-din al-Qastallani.

[edit] Biography

He was married to ‘Aishah al-Ba’uniyyah [1]

[edit] Legacy

[edit] Works

  • Al-Muwahib al-Ladunniyyah bi al-Minah al-Muhammadiyya [4]
  • Irshad al-Sari, 10 vol [5], one of the best commentaries on Sahih Bukhari [3]
  • Masalik al-Hunafa ila Mashari’ al-Salat ‘ala al-Nabiy al-Mustafa [1]
  • Minhaj al-Ibtihaj: Sharh Muslim b. al-Hajjaj, a commentary on Sahih Muslim [1]
  • al-Jana al-Dani fi Hall Hirz al-Amani, a commentary on the Shatibiyyah [1]
  • al-Fath al-Mawahibi, a biography of the author of Shatibiyyah [1]
