Al-Adudi Hospital

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Al-Adudi Hospital was a hospital in Baghdad named after Emir 'Adud al-Daula. The hospital was built in AD 981, but destroyed in 1258 by the Mongol invasion.
