Al-Ahli (Arabic: الأهلي السعودي‎) (formerly known as Al-Thaghar) is a Saudi Arabian professional sports club based in Jeddah.

The club is one of the largest in Saudi Arabia and it has had many Saudi internationals on the club roster. Exactly like the Saudi national team, Al-Ahli play in a green and white jersey.

Al-Ahli won the Saudi Premier League twice in 1978 and 1984.
