PBS Documentary ALQaeda's New Front

Al-Qaeda's New Front is PBS documentary on Islamic terrorist network in Europe and its relationship to Islam and Al-Qaeda.

[edit] Summary

Muslim Immigrant population in Europe has reached 18 million Muslims and are expected to grow to 20 percent of Europe's population by 2020. Many of these immigrants who share religious and ethnic bonds have failed to integrate into European societies and have become alienated partly through rejection of Western values and throught the constant Islamist propaganda from local radical imams and jihadist Web sites and anti-western media.
