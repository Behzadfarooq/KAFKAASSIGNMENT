Al Ahli Football Club (Arabic: الأهلي) is a United Arab Emirates professional football (soccer) club. They are based in Dubai.

[edit] Achievements

  • UAE League: 4
1975, 1976, 1980 , 2006
  • UAE Emir Cup: 6
1975, 1977, 1988, 1996, 2002, 2004
