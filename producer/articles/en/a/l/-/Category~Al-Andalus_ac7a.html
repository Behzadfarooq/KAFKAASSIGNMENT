Category:Al-Andalus

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The main article for this category is Al-Andalus.

This category deals with both the historical period as well as the geographical descriptor of Al-Andalus. It refers to time of the Muslim entry in 711 through Reconquista period till the end of the last Moorish kingdom in the Iberian Peninsle, the Kingdom of Granada, in 1492.
