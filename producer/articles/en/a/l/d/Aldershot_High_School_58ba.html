Aldershot High School, also known as Aldershot School, is a high school located in the Aldershot community of Burlington, Ontario. It has grades 7-12. The enrollment of the school is about 600 high schools students and 300 elementary.

  • Principal: Mrs.Conn Smith
  • Vice Principal: Mr.Tadman
  • Vice Principal: Mrs.Parsons
