Alderman House
(U.S. National Register of Historic Places)
Location: Fort Myers, Florida  United States
Built/Founded: 1925
Architectural style(s): Spanish Colonial Revival
Added to NRHP: December 1, 1988
Reference #: 88002690

The Alderman House (also known as the Royal Palm Antiques) is a historic home in Fort Myers, Florida, United States. It is located at 2572 1st Street. On December 1, 1988, it was added to the U.S. National Register of Historic Places.
