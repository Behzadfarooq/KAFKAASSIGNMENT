Aldrin Chlordane is a fictional character and a villain from Chip 'n Dale Rescue Rangers television show. He is a criminal mastermind and the owner of the Rangers' arch-enemy, Fat Cat. His name comes from the pesticides aldrin and chlordane. His surname is often mistakenly written as Klordane, Claudaine or Clawdane or something similar.

Spoiler warning: Plot and/or ending details follow.

Chlordane appears in To The Rescue episodes, wherein he hired Professor Norton Nimnul to construct a laser cannon to turn steel into a gelatin mold so that they can cause an earthquake at the United States Gold Reserve. To power the laser, Nimnul required a jewel which Chlordane stole for him. In the process, he framed Detective Drake with whom he was old enemies. He and Drake, who was retiring just as Chlordane was apprehended, seem to have been enemies for a long time. Before this, it was said he drowned in a river a year ago and was thought dead.
