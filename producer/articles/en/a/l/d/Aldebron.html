Aldebron

Publisher Marvel Comics
First appearance Star Masters #1 (December 1995)
Created by Mark Gruenwald, Scot Easton and Bob Almond
Characteristics
Alter ego Aldebron

Aldebron is a fictional extra-terrestrial supervillain in the Marvel Universe of the Axi-Tun race. The character first appeared in Star Masters #1 in December 1995. She participated in Votan's attack on Charter and survived the destruction of her ship. She frequently is at odds with Quasar and has vowed to one day take over the universe.

[edit] Powers

Aldebron has not demonstrated any superhuman abilities.
