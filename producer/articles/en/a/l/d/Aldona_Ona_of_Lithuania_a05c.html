Aldona Ona of Lithuania

Aldona Ona or Anna (after 1309 – 26 May 1339) was the Queen of Poland 1333 – 1339, and the Princess of Lithuania. She was the daughter of Grand Duke Gediminas of Lithuania.

She married Casimir III the Great. Their daughter was Cunigunde, who married the son of Louis IV, Holy Roman Emperor, Louis VI the Roman, on January 1, 1345, possibly at Kraków.
