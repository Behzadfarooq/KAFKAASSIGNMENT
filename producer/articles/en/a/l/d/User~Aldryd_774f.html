I've been a registered member of Wikipedia since around April 2005. After over a year of inactivity, I started contributing more in September 2006.

I'm a casual editor. I try to just stay out of the way while I clean up pages here and there. My sort of ongoing project is cleaning up The Tick article to make it look nicer.
