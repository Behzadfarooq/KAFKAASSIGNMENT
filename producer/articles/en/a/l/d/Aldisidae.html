Aldisidae is a family of nudibranchs of the Order Opisthobranchia.

[edit] Genera and species

  • Genus Aldisa Bergh, 1878
    • Aldisa albomarginata Millen, 1984
    • Aldisa cooperi Robilliard and Baba, 1972
    • Aldisa sanguinea (J. G. Cooper, 1863) - Blood-spot doris
    • Aldisa tara Millen, 1984
    • Aldisa zetlandica (Alder and Hancock, 1854)
