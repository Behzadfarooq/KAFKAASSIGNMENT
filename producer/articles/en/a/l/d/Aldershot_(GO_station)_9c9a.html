Aldershot GO Station is a train and bus station in used by VIA Rail and GO Transit, located at Highway 403 and Waterdown Road in the Aldershot community of Burlington, Ontario, Canada.

It is a peak-hour stop on GO's Lakeshore West line train service, and at other times, the bus connecting Hamilton travels into the station.

Aldershot serves Burlington and Hamilton on VIA Rail's Quebec City-Windsor Corridor|Windsor-Québec corridor routes. It is served by trains to Toronto, Ottawa, Montréal, Windsor, and Niagara Falls, and by the joint VIA-Amtrak Maple Leaf train, connecting Toronto and New York City through Niagara Falls.

Burlington Transit does not presently connect with this station, but there is a route on nearby Plains Road West, a five-to-ten-minute walk away.
