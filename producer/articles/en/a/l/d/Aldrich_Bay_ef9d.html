Aldrich Bay (Chinese: 愛秩序灣) was formerly a bay in the north shore on the Hong Kong Island, Hong Kong. It is now reclaimed and is a housing area outside the area of Shau Kei Wan, neighbouring A Kung Ngam and Lei King Wan. Outside Aldrich Bay, it is the Shau Kei Wan Typhoon Shelter. It is not a small community. It contains Oi Tung Estate and some HOS and private housings, with several primary school and secondary school. It is administratively part of the Eastern District.

The Chinese name for Aldrich Bay roughly translates to "The Bay that Loves Discipline"

[edit] Transport

A New World First Bus on route 2X at Aldrich Bay.

By bus:

  • New World First Bus: 2, 2X, 14, 720, 720A
  • Kowloon Motor Bus: 102, 102P, N102

By MTR:

  • The Nearest MTR stations: Sai Wan Ho and Shau Kei Wan
  • Using Sai Wan Ho station: All exits are suitable
  • Using Shau Kei Wan station: Exit B3 or D2

By minibus:

  • Route No. 20 from Chai Wan
  • Route No. 66 from Chai Wan
  • Route No. 66A from Chai Wan
  • Route No. 68 from Quarry Bay

By Tram:

  • All Stops on Shau Kei Wan Road, Sai Wan Ho Depot and the Shau Kei Wan Terminus.are suitable
