Aloha Elementary School is located in the city of Lakewood, California. Aloha is a primary school serving students in grades Kindergarten through 6. Aloha Elementary is a part of the ABC Unified School District.

The address for the school is 11737 East. 214th Street, Lakewood, CA.

As of 2003, Aloha had 23 full credentialed teachers and 21 classrooms serving 461 students. Average class size is 25.0 students.
