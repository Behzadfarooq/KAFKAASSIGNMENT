Alois Švehlík (born 1939, Pardubice, Czechoslovakia) is a Czech actor.

After graduation from a technical secondary school he joined theatre in Kladno. Later he moved to Most, Olomouc and Liberec. Currently he is member of National Theatre in Prague (ND). He is father of actor David Švehlík.

[edit] Notable performances

  • 1993 – Willy Loman in Death of a Salesman, ND
  • 1993 – John Gabriel Borkman in John Gabriel Borkman by Henrik Ibsen, ND
  • 2000 – Jack in The Weir by Conor McPherson, ND
  • Sir in The Dresser by Ronald Harwood, Divadlo F.X.Šaldy Liberec
  • 2004 – He in From the Unknown (Aus der Fremde) by Ernst Jandl, Theatre on the Balustrade (nominated for Alfréd Radok Award)
  • 2005 – Malvolio in Twelfth Night, or What You Will, Summer Shakespeare Festival at Prague Castle
