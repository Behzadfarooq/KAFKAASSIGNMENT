Alona is a fictional character in the GMA's Atlantika. Portrayed by Isabel Oli

[edit] Character information

Alona is one of the princesses of Atlntika. She is the daughter of Celeste whom she reawakened from coma. She is the only person whom can also defeat her mother. Her blood degenerates Celeste's energy and power absorbing it. When the right times she was able to kill her mother using her blood.
