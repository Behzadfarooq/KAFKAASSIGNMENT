Alona Alegre

Alona Alegre (born 1948) is a Filipino actress who is the daughter of comedian actor Lou Salvador and LVN actress Inday Jalandoni.

She made her film debut as a 7 year old poor girl in 1955 movie of Tagapagmana under Lvn Pictures.
