[edit] Could alopecia areata be the result of Chlamydia Pneumoniae infection?

I have been following a rather difficult antibiotic treatment for Chlamydia Pneumoniae but have noticed that my Alopecia is improving. I am a woman in my 60's and have had alopecia since I was 30. It comes and goes as the name suggests, but in the past 10 years the hair has been mostly gone and now its coming back. Any comments? Michele
