Alouette

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Alouette may refer to:

  • Alouette 1 and Alouette 2, two Canadian satellites
  • Alouette also means skylark in French, a type of bird
  • Alouette (song), children's song about the bird
  • Alouette, Alouette II and Alouette III are light utility helicopters built in France
  • Montreal Alouettes, a team in the Canadian Football League
  • Aluminerie Alouette, a major primary Aluminum smelter located at Sept-Îles, Quebec
  • Alouette (Mega Man Zero), a character in the Mega Man Zero series
  • Alouette, a character in the anime and manga universe encompassing The King of Braves GaoGaiGar and The King of Braves GaoGaiGar FINAL
  • Alouette (B&M), a passenger train operated by the Boston & Maine Railroad between Boston, MA and Montreal, QC.
