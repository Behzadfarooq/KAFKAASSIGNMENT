[edit] Working

I've noticed that someone keeps taking out parts about what it is to work at one of these places; I've been here well over a year, the pay for the California Center Sucks, simply put. Magna makes more then us an hour, and we do the same work!?

"Someone" keeps editing the information because it's simply not true. Perhaps the money made in California is lower than Magna, but those things are determined by local factors. Encyclopedia articles should be neutral and factual, not bias from a disgruntled employee. If you feel you are being treated unfairly or not paid your worth, seek employment elsewhere. And no, I'm not an Alorica Exec, just a lowly agent like you.

You guys need to remember that everyone does the base wage thing, most increases on top of that come from some slack given by the account you work on. If the account generates a lot of profit, it's easier to put things into place that make you more money. Such as the diffrence between working on an account that is in 1 center with 50 front-line agents, or 3 centers with 1000 front line agents
