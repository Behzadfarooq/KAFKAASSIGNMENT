Aloys Thomas Raimund Count Harrach (* 7 March 1669; â€  7 November 1742 in Vienna) was an Austrian politician and diplomat. His father was Ferdinand Bonaventura I. Count Harrach. He belonged to the Austrian nobility.

Aloys von Harrach was the envoy of the Austrian Emperor in Dresden in 1694, served in the same function in Spain from 1697 to 1700 and was in Dresden again in 1711, and thereafter in Berlin and Hannover on diplomatic missions. From 1715 to 1742 he acted as 'Landmarschall' in Lower Austria, and from 1728 to 1733 as vice-king of Naples, from where he gathered numerous art pieces (Gallery in Rohrau). From 1734 to his death Count Harrach was a member of the Secret State Conference (Geheime Staatskonferenz) in Vienna.

The Count was married to Marie Barbara von Sternberg.
