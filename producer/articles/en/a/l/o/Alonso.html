Alonso is the name of:

  • Alonso Fernández de Lugo, Spanish military man
  • Alonso Mudarra, Spanish composer and vihuelist
  • Alonso Álvarez de Pineda, Spanish explorer and cartographer
  • Alonso Solís, Costa Rican football (soccer) player
  • Alonso de Ercilla y Zúñiga, Basque nobleman, soldier and poet

Alonso is the surname of:

  • Alicia Alonso, Cuban ballerina and choreographer
  • Dámaso Alonso, Spanish poet, philologist and literary critic
  • Fernando Alonso, Spanish Formula One Champion driver
  • Juan Alonso, Spanish football (soccer) player
  • Manuel A. Alonso, Puerto Rican writer, poet and journalist
  • Maria Conchita Alonso, Cuban singer and actress
  • Norberto Alonso, Argentine football (soccer) player
  • Xabi Alonso, Spanish football (soccer) player
  • Mikel Alonso, Spanish football (soccer) player
