Alone is the sixth album by the US doom metal band Solitude Aeturnus. It was released in 2006. The album cover is done by Travis Smith .

[edit] Track listing

All music by Solitude Aeturnus. All lyrics by Robert Low, except "Lucid Destitution" by Heather Hunt.
  1. "Scent of Death" – 9:42
  2. "Waiting for the Light" – 4:42
  3. "Blessed Be the Dead" – 5:03
  4. "Sightless" – 4:25
  5. "Upon Within" – 7:57
  6. "Burning" – 8:43
  7. "Is There" – 8:02
  8. "Tomorrows Dead" – 6:26
  9. "Essence of Black" – 5:34
  10. "Lucid Destitution" – 10:10 (Bonustrack on digipak)
The track "Lucid Destitution" was originally titled "Embraced" but changed on the last minute.
