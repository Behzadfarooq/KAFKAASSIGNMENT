Alok Kapali (Bengali: অলক কাপালী ) (born January 1, 1984 in Sylhet) is a Bangladeshi cricketer. He is a batsman that can bowl leg spin with the run-up (walk up) of Shane Warne. He took a hat-trick against Pakistan in 2003. He is slowly emerging as a promising cricketer.
