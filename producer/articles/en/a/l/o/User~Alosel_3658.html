[edit] About Alosel

I am Alosel, also known in the mundane world as Paul Rousselle [1][2]. Currently a student at Coleg Sir Gâr studying Psychology, Religious Studies and French to A Level standard, I enjoy thouroughly my studies and hope do to well. I'll try not to inflate my ego to much here...

[edit] Extra-Curricular Activities

I aid regularly with Peace Mala having been involved with the group for many years (since I was 13 in fact)[2]. I help to make some of the actual Peace Malas, and attend various ceremonies such as the 2006 Youth Awards. Also, I attend monthly moots with the Carmarthenshite Pagan Community Network, and enjoy an everning of much merriment and revelry.

[edit] Interests

A large passion of mine includes reading, some of my favourites being His Dark Materials, Harry Potter, Lord of the Rings, the Goblin Trilogy, Digital Fortress, Deception Point (I rather prefer those two than the Da Vinci Code and Angels and Demons). Musically, my tastes range from Wagner to Therion, Nightwish to Jethro Tull. Lately my tastes lean towards symphonic rock, symphonic metal or various bands such as Nightwish, Therion, Rhapsody of Fire, Epica etc.
