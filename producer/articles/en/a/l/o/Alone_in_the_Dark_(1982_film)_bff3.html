Alone in the Dark is a 1982 horror film directed by Jack Sholder.

[edit] Plot

Spoiler warning: Plot and/or ending details follow.

Thanks to a major power cut, a gang of psychopaths breaks out of the Haven maximum security mental institute in order to lay siege to the psychiatrists who have tormented them over the years with their bizarre theories, that evantually led to their undoing in the sense that they feel they need to break free psychologically.
