Alois Stadlober (born April 11, 1962) was an Austrian cross country skier who competed from 1988 to 2000. He earned two medals at the 1999 FIS Nordic World Ski Championships with a gold in the 4 x 10 km and a silver in the 10 km.

Stadlober's best individual finish at the Winter Olympics was an 8th in the 10 km event at Albertville in 1992. He won four races in his career, all in 10 km and in Austria, from 1995 to 1997.
