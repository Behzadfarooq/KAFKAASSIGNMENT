[edit] Who Am I?

Mostly, I'm just an ordinary first year university student with a major in computer science and a minor in the Japanese Language. I finally decided to join up and start editting things when I found a lot of articles on my favourite video game series, Suikoden, were in terrible shape and in need of being corrected. With a highly intimate knowledge of the games, I set out to start overhauling the articles and bringing them up to snuff. Since then, I'm just trying to help whereever I can.

Aside from Suikoden, which seems to be my prime interest these days, I have a keen interest in Gundam and anime and manga in general, as well as most role playing games. These interests have heavily influenced my program choices here in university, including the selection of Japanese as a minor. My biggest love is, and probably always will be, computers and, specifically, programming.

I'm also a huge metal nut, listening to pretty much everything that falls into the genre. Everything from the classics of Ozzy right up to modern power and progressive bands, right through to death and black styles - chances are I listen to it in my regular routine. My favourite bands, more than any, though are Nightwish and Kamelot, which pretty much gives my stylings away.

[edit] My Goals Here

Essentially, I want to get all the Suikoden articles cleaned up and containing accurate information. Aside from that, I have no clue exactly what I wish to accomplish here, amongst the editors of Wikipedia, so only time will tell. Hopefully I'll do something that'll make it worth my time to have come here.
