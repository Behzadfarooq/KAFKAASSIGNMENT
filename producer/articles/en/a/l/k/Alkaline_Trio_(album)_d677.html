Alkaline Trio is a CD by Alkaline Trio released in 2000 on Asian Man Records. Alkaline Trio is a self-titled compilation of earlier out-of-print EP's For Your Lungs Only, I Lied My Face Off, the Sundials 7-inch record, and three songs from various compilation CDs.

  2. "This Is Getting Over You"
  3. "Bleeder"
  4. "I Lied My Face Off"
  5. "My Friend Peter"
  6. "Snake Oil Tanker"
  7. "Southern Rock"
  8. "Cooking Wine"
  9. "For Your Lungs Only"
  10. "Exploding Boy" (The Cure cover)
  11. "Sun Dials"
  12. "Nose Over Tail"
  13. "97"
