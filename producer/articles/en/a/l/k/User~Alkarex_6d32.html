I am French, but currently, I live in Denmark.

I was born on November 24, 1980 in Avignon, I was a student in Montpellier and for a short semester in Toulouse. I am an informatics engineer, and I am doing a PhD in speech recognition at Risø National Laboratory and Roskilde University.

[edit] Wikipedia & others

  • I have started to contribute anonymously on the French Wikipedia from May 26, 2003.
    • My first contributions were the creation of fr:Longitude then fr:Latitude articles, to put this information in fr:Montpellier article.
  • I have created my French Wikipedia account on March 31, 2004.
    • First contribution on fr:Pâques article.
  • I have created my English Wikipedia account on December 20, 2004.
    • First contribution on Matford article.
  • I have created my Wikimedia Commons account on April 3, 2005.
    • First contribution on commons:Avignon article.
  • I have created my Danish Wikipedia account on April 10, 2005.
    • First contribution on da:Kategori:Rovdyr category.
  • I have created my French Alkarex wiktionary account on May 30, 2006.
  • I have created my English Alkarex wiktionary account on June 10, 2006.
