Alki Larnaca(Greek: Αλκή Λάρνακα) is a cypriot football club based in the town of Larnaca. The club was founded in 1948 and has as jersey colours blue and red. They reached the Cyprus Cup final on 5 occasions without a win. In 1978/1979, they achieved their greatest position in the ranking by gaining the third position in the Cypriot Championship and since the APOEL Nicosia, were both Runners up and Cup Winners, it gave them the right to play in the UEFA Cup, where they were eliminated in the first round by Dinamo Bucharest. The team currently plays in Cypriot Second Division.

[edit] Honours

  • Cyprus Cup:
    • Runner-up: 1967, 1970, 1976, 1977, 1980
  • Cypriot Second Division
    • Champions (2): 1982, 2001
