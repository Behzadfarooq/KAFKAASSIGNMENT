Alkaid, full title Alkaid, Former Emperor of the Demon Palace, is a fictional character appearing in the .hack franchise. Like most characters in the franchise, she is a player of the fictional MMORPG The World.

[edit] Offline

Alkaid is played by Chika Kuramoto who a sixteen-year old Japanese girl. She lives in Sapporo, Hokkaido and is on the library council for her high school. She values reading and reads both Chinese classics and Western contemporary fiction.

[edit] The World

In .hack//G.U., Alkaid was first introduced when the protagonist and main character Haseo was called by another character, Antares, to witness the state of Icolo, a guild he created for the Champions of the "Battle Arena". Although Alkaid is no longer a member of Icolo since she has lost to the powerful Endrance, she still had access to Icolo with the guild's key card. Alkaid's first impression about Haseo was that he didn't look like much. Further into the series, Alkaid reveals a grudge against Endrance for defeating her. Even though she cannot see Avatars, which Endrance had been using to win, Alkaid has figured out that something went wrong and Endrance was not a normal PC. Soon afterwards, Haseo faces off with Alkaid in the Demon Palace. No matter how strong Haseo was, Alkaid proved herself to be stronger, and Haseo would have lost if not for summoning his Avatar Skeith and destroying Alkaid and her party members in an instant death attack. Alkaid also suspected and accused Haseo of cheating at an optional quest near the end of the game. In the upcoming volumes, it is portrayed that she joins the party.

In the second volume, Alkaid gains a romantic interest for Haseo, then falls into a coma by being PKed by the PKer Bordeaux, whom at the time was infected by an AIDA.
