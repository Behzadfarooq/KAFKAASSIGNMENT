Welcome to my user page. I started writing for Wikipedia back in 2001. I was a student then, majoring in Business Information Systems. I was trying to write an essay for types of information systems, so while I was searching for some matterial for my essay I got into Wikipedia. I didn't make much use of the article of Wikipedia (because it was poor then) but as soon as I finished the essay I got back in and started reading and after a while editing. The following year (I think) I started writing for the Greek Wikipedia which was not active at the time. For a period of two years I couldn't contribute for various reasons, but I hope now to be able to help to the development of this amazing project.
