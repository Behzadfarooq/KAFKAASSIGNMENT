"Alkoholen delirium" (Alcoholic Delirium) is the debut album of Bulgarian rock band Hipodil, released 1993. It includes 22 tracks recorded on audio tape. The music is varied and a little bit aggressive. Many of the song lyrics are on sexual or alcoholic topics, but with a good sense of humor. The mood of the album is funny and party-orientated at all. This is the very first album of this kind (explicit and nonchalant lyrics in the wide public space) in Bulgaria after the end of the communistic order in 1990. The release of this album started the existing of many other bands, making such kind of music.

The track "Novata" contains a lot of traditional Bulgarian folk motives, and the whole album is full of wind instruments.
