The simple station Alquería is part of the TransMilenio mass-transit system of Bogotá, Colombia, opened in the year 2000.

[edit] Location

The station is located in southern Bogotá, specifically on Autopista Sur with Carrera 49.

[edit] History

This station was opened April 15, 2006 as part of the section between the stations General Santander and Portal del Sur of the NQS line.

[edit] Feeder routes

This station does not have connections to feeder routes.

[edit] Inter-city service

This station does not have inter-city service.
