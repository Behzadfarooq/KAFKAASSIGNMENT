[edit] Usage

Add {{Alberta census divisions map|map=[[...map image...]}} at in articles pertaining Census divisions of Alberta.

A census division map with linked labels will be rendered.

The map parameters can be changed with equal map images, if omitted, the default Census divisions AB.png will be used.
