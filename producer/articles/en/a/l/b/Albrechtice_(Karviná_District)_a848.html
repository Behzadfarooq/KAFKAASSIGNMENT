Albrechtice (Polish: Olbrachcice, German: Albersdorf) is a large village in Karviná District, Moravian-Silesian Region of the Czech Republic. It is located 8 km south of Karviná, on the Stonávka River and has a population of 4,071 (2001 Census). 23,5% of the population are the Poles.[1]
