Albirex Cheerleaders (アルビレックスチアリーダーズ, Arubirekkusu tiaridazu?) is an official cheerleading team of J League Albirex Niigata and Bj league Niigata Albirex BB formed in 2001.
Tomoko Mita who is the authority of cheerleading in Japan serves as the chief director.
