The Albany Conquest are a professional arena football team based in Albany, New York. They are a 2002 expansion member of the af2 and play their home games at Times Union Center. This is Albany's second team, as the town originally had the Albany Firebirds of the Arena Football League from 1990-2000 before moving to Indianapolis, Indiana and playing as the Indiana Firebirds from 2001-2004 before disbaning.
