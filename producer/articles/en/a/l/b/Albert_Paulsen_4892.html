From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Albert Paulsen (Guayaquil, Ecuador, 13 December 1925â€”Los Angeles, California, 25 April 2004) was an Ecuadorian actor who appeared in many United States television series beginning in the 1960s. He changed his last name from Paulson in the 1940s. He died from natural causes in his sleep in 2004.
