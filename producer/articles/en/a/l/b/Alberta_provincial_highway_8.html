8

Alberta Highway 8 is a provincial highway within the Canadian province of Alberta, which connects Highway 22 just north of Bragg Creek to its terminus at Highway 2 in Calgary. While in Calgary, Highway 8 is known as Glenmore Trail, referring to the reservoir it crosses. Glenmore Trail is a major east–west expressway in Calgary, and construction of interchanges at 5th Street SW and Elbow Drive SW, now under way and scheduled for completion in 2007, will make Glenmore Trail a full freeway from Ogden Road to west of Crowchild Trail.
