Albany Stakes (UK)

The Albany Stakes is a Group 3 flat horse race in the United Kingdom for two-year-old thoroughbred fillies run over a distance of 6 furlongs (1,207 metres) at Ascot Racecourse at the Royal meeting in June.

It was first run in 2002 and was initially classed as a Listed race, upgrading to Group 3 status in 2005.
