[edit] Summary

ALB Consultancy, www.albconsultancy.com


ALB…And Life Begins. Welcome to the world of ALB, where our Focus is our student, Trust – our primary goal and Career Building – our job. We believe in celebrating every single student under the sun. Our prime motto is to build a Concrete Foundation through Personalized Counseling and helping students reach their dream career. In short, we cater to all the Student’s requirements and career needs. At ALB we are dedicated to building a better society by helping students join a great workplace. Our mission is to help them find one.

                             In other words we help companies in building trust
and becoming more effective and efficient by hiring manpower trained by ALB.

What is ALB?

A CONSULTANCY...NOT EXACTLY!!! TRAINING INSTITUTE...NO WAY!!! HR SOLUTION PROVIDER…TO SOME EXTENT!!!

Then what is ALB?

“PERFECT REQUIREMENT MATCHING” …is what we aim at. Acting as an interface between Institutes and Industries, we are not bound by any rules when it comes to serving Industry’s Requirement and Student Expectations.

Who we are

ALB, the hobby-horse of four young IT entrepreneurs, was founded with a mission to be one of the top three leading providers of Targeted Education and Job related Consultancy services in India in 2006.

Driven by the core value of Every Student Is Special, we inspire and involve the young talents and aspirants looking out for their dream job through advocacy, consultancy, projects, targeted education and overall personality development.

ALB makes Industries and Institutes to be more valuable, beating their competitors and generating substantial and lasting impact. The mission of ALB is to be one of the leading providers of Targeted Education and Job related Consultancy services in India.


Who we serve

If you happen to recognize words like… Recruitment, jobs, opportunity, placement, career, knowledge, achievement, performance, success and alike; be sure we can serve you.

With outstanding talent, innovative processes, powerful creativity, and state-of-the-art technology, we produce exceptional results for a wide variety of clients. Whether you choose us as a complete recruitment service provider, or let us enhance your recruitment services, you’re assured of customized, quality service based on best practices.

Services we provide

Choose from ALB’s array of services, customized and tailored to suit your needs whether you are a student, an educational institute or a leading company…

• RECRUITMENT SERVICE ENHANCEMENT • DIRECT INDUSTRY INTERACTION • EXPERT COUNSELING • PROJECTS • SUMMER TRAINING • OFF-CAMPUS DRIVES • PERSONALITY GROOMING

Why Placement Process is somewhat slow at ALB?

Hmmm…an intelligent question.

Sorry, we are not here to do business or make a fool of all you guys possessing hidden talent. And above all we are not UNETHICAL

We are not into providing an IT job to an electrical engineer, a BPO job to an IT Graduate or a Data Entry Job to a Mechanical Engineering student. We respect the CORE KNOWLEDGE you have gained and we look forward to get you a job in your domain only unless you are interested in trying your hands in other domains also.

Remember, People behind ALB are not some businessmen or brokers (in short running for money). There is a workforce of Engineers and Management Gurus with a vision and foresight. And they are here for you.

Vision

We look forward to be a leader in education, attracting superior students, making them ready to succeed, and providing them excellent lifelong career opportunities.

Eminent industry persons train our students as per the current demands making them industry-tailored. ALB students also have an opportunity to boast of live projects in industry after which the Industries can directly appoint and put our students to projects.


Guiding Principles

PASSION FOR WINNING:

We understand our responsibility with a deep commitment. We are determined to be the best. CAREER FOCUS: We have superior understanding of students need and we are here to match them up with the industry standards and demands. INNOVATION: Our success rests on the continuous innovation in teaching techniques based on student’s caliber. We also maintain pace with the current industry trends. PERSONALITY DEVELOPMENT:

Students are our most important assets. We add value to them along with knowledge, grooming their personality towards excellence.

BUILDING RELATIONSHIPS:

We believe success in any work is on built on relationships that are founded on Trust. It works because of clear communication, action and proper co-ordination supported by technology.
