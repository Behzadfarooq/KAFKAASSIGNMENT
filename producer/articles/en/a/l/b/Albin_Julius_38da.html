Albin Julius

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Albin Julius (b. October 16, 1967) is an Austrian martial music and industrial artist; his primary musical project is called Der Blutharsch.

Julius founded the history-themed, folk-based ensemble, The Moon Lay Hidden Beneath a Cloud, and has worked with Douglas P. of Death In June on the albums, Operation Hummingbird and Take Care & Control. His first release as Der Blutharsch was a self-titled picture disc, limited to 250 copies. Julius also collaborated with Douglas P. and Boyd Rice to create the Boyd Rice and Fiends Wolfpact album.

Julius releases all Der Blutharsch music on his own record label, Wir Kapitulieren Niemals (WKN, which translates: We Will Never Surrender). Julius founded the HauRuck! music label, which releases albums by several Neofolk and industrial groups.
