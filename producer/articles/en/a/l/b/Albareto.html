Albareto is a comune (municipality) in the Province of Parma in the Italian region Emilia-Romagna, located about 130 km west of Bologna and about 60 km southwest of Parma. As of 31 December 2004, it had a population of 2,265 and an area of 103.9 km².[1]

The municipality of Albareto contains the frazioni (subdivisions, mainly villages and hamlets) Bertorella, Boschetto, Buzzò, Cacciarasca, Campi, Case Bozzini, Case Mazzetta, Case Mirani, Caselle, Codogno, Folta, Gotra, Il Costello, Lazzarè, Le Moie, Montegroppo, Pieve di Campi, Pistoi, Ponte Scodellino, Roncole, San Quirico, Spallavera, Squarci, and Torre.

Albareto borders the following municipalities: Borgo Val di Taro, Compiano, Pontremoli, Sesta Godano, Tornolo, Varese Ligure, Zeri.
