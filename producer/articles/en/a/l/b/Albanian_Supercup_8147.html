Albanian Supercup

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

You have new messages (last change).

The Albanian Supercup of football has been played since 1989. It is a single match at the beginning of the season between the winners of theAlbanian First Division and Albanian Cup. Every match has been played in the National Stadium of Tirana.

[edit] Winners of Albanian Supercup

  • 1989: Dinamo Tiranë (final match, January 11, 1990: 2-0 vs. 17 Nëntori Tiranë)
  • 1990: Flamurtari Vlorë (final match, January 11, 1991: 3-3 EX, 5-4 PEN. vs. Dinamo Tiranë)
  • 1991: Flamurtari Vlorë (final match, January 11, 1992: 1-0 vs. Partizani Tiranë)
  • 1992: SK Elbasan (final match, January 10, 1993: 3-2 EX. vs. Vllaznia Shkodër)
  • 1993: Partizani Tiranë (final match undisputed: Partizani double winner first division and cup, awarded trophy without playing any opponent)
  • 1994: SK Tiranë (final match, January 8, 1995: 1-0 vs. Teuta Durrës)
  • 1995: SK Tiranë (final match vs. Teuta Durrës, not played)
  • 1996: Not Awarded trophy because same opponent in Championship and Cup winners (SK Tiranë)
  • 1997: SK Tiranë (final match vs. Partizani Tiranë, not played)
  • 1998: Vllaznia Shkodër (final match, January 9, 1999: 1-1 EX, 5-4 PEN. vs. Apolonia Fier)
  • 1999: Not Awarded trophy because same opponent in Championship and Cup winners (SK Tiranë)
  • 2000: SK Tiranë (final match, January 13, 2001: 1-0 vs. Teuta Durrës)
  • 2001: Vllaznia Shkodër (final match, September 15, 2001: 2-1 vs. SK Tiranë)
  • 2002: SK Tiranë (final match, September 14, 2002: 6-0 vs. Dinamo Tiranë)
  • 2003: SK Tiranë (final match, August 16, 2003: 3-0 vs. Dinamo Tiranë)
  • 2004: Partizani Tiranë (final match, August 17, 2004: 2-0 vs. SK Tiranë)
  • 2005: SK Tiranë (final match, August 21, 2005: 0-0 EX, 5-4 PEN. vs. Teuta Durrës)

[edit] Most winners

  • SK Tiranë (7 Supercup)
  • Flamurtari Vlorë (2 Supercup)
  • Vllaznia Shkodër (2 Supercup)
  • Partizani Tiranë (2 Supercup)
  • Dinamo Tiranë (1 Supercup)
  • SK Elbasan (1 Supercup)
