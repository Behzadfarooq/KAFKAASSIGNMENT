Albert Meyong Zé, born October 19, 1980, is a Cameroonian football player who currently plays for Levante UD.

He has previously played for Italian club Ravenna Calcio and Portuguese clubs Vitória FC "de Setúbal" and CF Os Belenenses. With Vitória, he won a Portuguese Cup, having scored the winning goal in the final against SL Benfica, and was the Liga's top goalscorer with Belenenses, despite the club's bottom 4 finish.
