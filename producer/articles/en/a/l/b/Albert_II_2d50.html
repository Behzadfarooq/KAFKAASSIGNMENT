From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Albert II is the name of several monarchs:

  • Albert II of Gorizia (d. 1304)
  • Albert II of Austria (1298–1358)
  • Albert II of Germany (1397–1439)
  • Albert II of Belgium (1934–)
