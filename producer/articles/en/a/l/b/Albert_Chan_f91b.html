Albert Chan Wai Yip (陳偉業, born 3 March 1955 in Hong Kong with family root in Shunde, Guangdong) is member of the Legislative Council of Hong Kong since 1991 except between 1997 and 2000. Chan is also a member of the Tsuen Wan District Council.

He and Lee Wing Tat were co-founders of the Hong Kong Association for Democracy and People's Livelihood in 1986. Chan served as Chairman of the party after Lee left in 1990. He joined the Democratic Party (Hong Kong) in 1994 and left the party in 2002. He is one of the founder of the newly-formed League of Social Democrats.
