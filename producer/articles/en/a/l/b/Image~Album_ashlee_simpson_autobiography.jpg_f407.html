[edit] Rationale of fair use for Ashlee Simpson, Autobiography (Ashlee Simpson album)

The image Image:Album ashlee simpson autobiography.jpg is being linked here; though the picture is originally copyright, I (Ryan Norton T | @ | C 18:28, 2 September 2005 (UTC)) feel it is covered by fair use because:

  1. The image is a picture of an album of Ashlee Simpson originally created by Geffen Records, and intended for wide distribution.
  2. It is needed to show what albums Ashlee Simpson made
  3. No free or public domain images have been located for this person.
  4. The image does not limit the copyright owners' rights to distribute the album in any way.
  5. The image is intended to represent the person, and only the person. Its inclusion in the article is important because it represents the person's "image". The image accompanies a section in the article that discuss the person's music and film career.
  6. The image is being used for informational purposes only, and its use is not believed to detract from the original album in any way.
