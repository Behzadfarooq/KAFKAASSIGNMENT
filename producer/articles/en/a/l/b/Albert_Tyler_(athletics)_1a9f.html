Albert Clinton Tyler (1872-01-04 – 1945-07-25) was an American athlete. He competed at the 1896 Summer Olympics in Athens.

Tyler competed in the pole vault. He placed second in the event with a height of 3.20 metres, behind fellow American William Welles Hoyt's 3.30 metres.
