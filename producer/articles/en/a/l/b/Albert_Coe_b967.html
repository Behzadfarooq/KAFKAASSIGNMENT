Albert Coe was an American contactee who supposedly made contact with a young Space Brother-type alien, who needed assistance from Coe, who was on a fishing trip in the wild hinterlands of Ontario, Canada. His book The Shocking Truth [1], published in 1969 claimed the contact occurred back in the 1920s, which put him thirty years before other contactees such as George Adamski, Truman Bethurum, George Van Tassel, Daniel Fry and Orfeo Angelucci. Coe's story was closely similar to those told by the other contactees concerning their experiences with wise, friendly human-appearing extraterrestrials.
