Albert Pintat Santolària (born 23 June 1943) is the head of government of Andorra.

Pintat graduated from the University of Fribourg in Switzerland in 1967, majoring in economics.

He is a member of the Liberal Party of Andorra and was the foreign minister of Andorra from 1997 to 2001, following a series of ambassadorships,including the United Nations.

He helds the position of head of government since being appointed by the General Council on May 30, 2005.
