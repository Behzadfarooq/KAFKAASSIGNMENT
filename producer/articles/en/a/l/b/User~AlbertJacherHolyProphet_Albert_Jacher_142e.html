Albert Jacher was born on 1968.01.03 (Gregorian Calendar)= -0034.7.13 (Albertan Calendar) in Tarnow, Poland.

In 1994 he graduated from Medical Academy of Breslau as physician.

In 2000 he published Theory of wisdom.

In 2001 he started a new religion - Albertanism or Natural Universal Wisdom Religion. He proclaimed himself as prophet chosen by god Wakan Alo and published the proclamation in the Internet (Albertanism) and on Polish TV (TVN)

Albert Jacher created ideas of

  • Theory of Wisdom - a theory about how wisdom and stupidity corelate and are met in objects like human populations.
  • Jacher Uncertainty Principle - One cannot determine whether an act is stupid or wise in the moment of action.
  • Justified Stupidity Principle - Any stupid opinion can be justified by someone, somehow.
  • Albertanism - a modern religion based on god Wakan Alo, revelations of its messenger - Albert Jacher and the priority of wisdom
  • Albertan Calendar - a calendar based on Earth movements around Sun
  • Interlanguage Smart Script - a script that enables writing and reading of words, names of many languages using one system, code of representing pronounciation.
  • Political Psychiatry - about how mental disorders influence social and political affairs and how politicians use psychiatry for political purposes.
