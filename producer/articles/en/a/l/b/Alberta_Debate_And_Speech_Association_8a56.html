The Alberta Debate And Speech Association (ADSA) is the association in charge of debate in Alberta.

[edit] History

The ADSA was formed in 1974 to promote and organise debate events in the province of Alberta.

[edit] Purpose

The association is resposible for administrating debate in alberta. In practice, this includes such things as: organising tournaments, regional and provincial tournaments, and other events, including law day. Also, the ADSA is responsible for organising the Alberta entrants for the Canadian national debate tounament.

[edit] Language

For the most part, ADSA tounaments are conducted in english. However, regional, provincial and national competition can be done either in the English or bilingual (English and French) categories.
