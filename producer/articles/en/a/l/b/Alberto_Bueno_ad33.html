Alberto Bueno Calvo (born 20 March 1988 in Madrid) is a Spanish football player, as of 2006 plays striker role for Real Madrid Castilla in Segunda División.

[edit] Teams and clubs

  • 200?-2006: Real Madrid Juvenil A
  • 2006-present: Real Madrid Castilla

[edit] International career

Bueno is Spain U19s internationals, which won 2006 UEFA European Under-19 Football Championship. He was the topscorer of the competition.

[edit] Trivia

  • He wears no.29 for Real Madrid Castilla, but he also included in first team squad list submited to UEFA for Champions League, which he will be no.30 if he made his debut on first team.
