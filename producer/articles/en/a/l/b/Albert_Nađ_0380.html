Albert Nađ (Cyrillic: Алберт Нађ, Hungarian: Nagy Albert) (born October 29, 1974 in Zemun, Serbia, SFR Yugoslavia) is a Serbian football midfielder of Hungarian decent.

He is currently playing for Partizan Belgrade. He played 32 games for the Serbia and Montenegro national football team and scored 3 goals. He has been named as a member of the Serbia and Montenegro national football team for the 2006 FIFA World Cup, and was also in the squad for the 2000 European Football Championship. He has also played in Spain for Real Betis and Real Oviedo.
