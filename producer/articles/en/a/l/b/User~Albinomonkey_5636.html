About Me

Albinomonkey (born 18 April 1986 – Canberra, Australia) is a Wikipedian from Sydney, Australia. I think I joined on September 27, 2004. I am a student at the University of Sydney, and strongly believe in the Grand Unification Theory of Delivered Pizza. I am interested in and will mainly contribute in the following areas:

  • Football (soccer)
    • Sydney FC
    • A-League
  • Formula One
    • Mark Webber
    • Felipe Massa
    • Nick Heidfeld
  • Rugby league
    • St George Illawarra Dragons
  • Cricket
  • A1 Grand Prix
