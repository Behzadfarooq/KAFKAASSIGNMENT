Albuquerque School for Circus Arts

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Albuquerque School for Circus Arts founded by Rosalinda Rojas offers classes, workshops and summer circus camps. The school gives instruction in trapeze, Spanish web, aerial dance, acrobatics, aerial silk, juggling and more.
