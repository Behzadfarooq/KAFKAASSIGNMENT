Alberto Cossentino (born 10 September 1988 in Palermo) is an Italian footballer presently playing with U.S. Città di Palermo in the role of defender. Cossentino has yet to make his Serie A debut for the club, but he already played as starter in this year's Coppa Italia (against Sampdoria) and UEFA Cup (against Fenerbahçe).
