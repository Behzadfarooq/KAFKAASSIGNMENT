The Alberta College & Technical Institute Student Executive Council (ACTISEC) is a coalition of college and technical institute students' associations from across Alberta. Our group is best known throughout Canada as a strong and professional student lobby organization.

ACTISEC has developed a strong rapport and profile with decision-makers and policy-developers through constant and consistent dialogue. This communication has developed within the ranks of the provincial Government, provincial opposition parties, and the post-secondary education policy realm. ACTISEC also advances member interests to the federal Government.

Our elected student representatives work together to set ACTISEC's policies and direction. Currently, ACTISEC represents 100,000 Alberta college and technical institute students at 14 institutions.
