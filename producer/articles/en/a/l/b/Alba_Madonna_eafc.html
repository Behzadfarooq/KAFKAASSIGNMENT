The Alba Madonna is a painting by the Italian High Renaissance artist Raphael. It is part of the Andrew W. Mellon Collection at the National Gallery of Art in Washington, D.C. It was commissioned by Paolo Giovio who planned to send it to the church of the Olivetani in Nocera dei Pagani.
