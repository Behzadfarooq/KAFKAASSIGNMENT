Alberto de Mendoza

Alberto de Mendoza (21 January 1923 -) is a major star of Argentine cinema, an actor who has appeared in some 114 films between 1930 and 2005 spanning 8 decades. The actor is known in Argentina cinema for his suave handsome looks.

A life long figure in Argentina film he began acting aged 7 in 1930 and has appeared in film such as Adán y la serpiente in 1946 and A hierro muere in 1962 often working alongside Olga Zubarry.

In the late 1960s and 1970 he appeared in a number of Spaghetti Western's.
