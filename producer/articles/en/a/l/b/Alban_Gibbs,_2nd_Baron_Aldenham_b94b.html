Alban George Henry Gibbs, MA, FSA, MP, 2nd Baron Aldenham (23 April 1846-9 May 1936) was a British peer, the son of Henry Hucks Gibbs, 1st Baron Aldenham. He succeeded to the title Baron Aldenham on 13 September 1907.

He married Bridget Beresford-Hope, on 18 February 1873. They had three children.

  • Hon. Catherine Louisa Gibbs (1875-1967)
  • Hon. Mildred Dorothea Gibbs (1876-1961)
  • Gerald Henry Beresford Gibbs, 3rd Baron Aldenham (1879-1939)
