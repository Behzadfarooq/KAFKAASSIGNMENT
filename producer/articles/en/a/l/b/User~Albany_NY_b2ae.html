[edit] Photographs

I have taken the following pictures and uploaded them to Wikipedia. They were shot with a Canon PowerShot S50 digital camera. Some images were edited with Adobe Photoshop. I have released them under the terms of the GNU Free Documentation License.

The Egg, a performing arts center located in the Empire State Plaza, is an Albany landmark. The Erastus Corning Tower is visible on the right.

The Cultural Education Center is also located in the Empire State Plaza. The ten story building houses the New York State Museum, Library, and Archives.

The Home Savings Bank Building is a 1927 office tower in downtown Albany. It is the city's tenth tallest structure.

The New York State Court of Appeals in Albany is the state's highest judicial body.

The Times Union Center is a major sports and entertainment venue in downtown Albany. When this photo was taken in 2006, it was still known as the Pepsi Arena.

A tour boat passes though the Livingston Avenue Bridge, a railroad swing bridge over the Hudson River.

The New York State Education Building is an imposing Neoclassical structure across the street from the Capitol. It formerly housed the New York State Museum.

One Commerce Plaza is Albany's eleventh tallest building. Completed in 1971, it has 20 floors and is 270 feet tall.
