Location of Albrechtičky in the Czech Republic

Albrechtičky is a village in the Moravian-Silesian Region of the Czech Republic. It is located 12 km northeast of Nový Jičín in vicinity of Ostrava-Mošnov International Airport and haa a population of 682 (2006).
