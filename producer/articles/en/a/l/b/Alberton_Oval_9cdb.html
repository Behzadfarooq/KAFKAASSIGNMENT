Alberton Oval

Alberton Oval is located on Queen St, Alberton. The ground is primarily used for Australian rules football and has a capacity of 15,000 people with seated grandstands holding 2,000 (the RB Quinn and the Fos Williams Stands). The record crowd for Alberton is 22,738 for a 1977 SANFL match between Port Adelaide and Norwood.

Alberton Oval is the training and administration base for the Port Adelaide Football Club. The oval also hosts matches for the Port Adelaide Magpies Football Club in the local SANFL competition.
