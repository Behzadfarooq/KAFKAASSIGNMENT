Alberto Górriz

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Alberto Górriz Echarte (born 16 February 1958 at Irun) is a former Spanish footballer. He played for Real Sociedad between 1979 and 1993, winning the 1981 and 1982 La Liga titles and the 1987 Copa del Rey. He played 12 times for the Spain national football team, scoring one goal in the 1990 FIFA World Cup match against Belgium.
