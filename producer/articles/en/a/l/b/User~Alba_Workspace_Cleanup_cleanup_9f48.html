I'm rather familiar with cleanup -- heck, I wrote WP:CSORT -- but even I find our cleanup pages confusing. This must stop.

Pages that need to be worked on:

User:Alba/Workspace/Cleanup cleanup/Wikipedia:Cleanup The soi-disant head page for cleanup also contains a handhacked list of pages. Perhaps should be pages needing special or urgent attention?

User:Alba/Workspace/Cleanup cleanup/Wikipedia:Cleanup resources Kitchen-sink list of tools for cleanup.

User:Alba/Workspace/Cleanup cleanup/Wikipedia:Pages needing attention The bot-maintained listing of article cleanup needs by topic.

User:Alba/Workspace/Cleanup cleanup/Wikipedia:Template messages/Cleanup Kitchen-sink list of templates devoted to cleanup.
