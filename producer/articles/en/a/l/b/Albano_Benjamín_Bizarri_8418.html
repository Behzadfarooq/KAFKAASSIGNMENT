Albano Bizarri is an Argentine football goalkeeper. He was born on November 9, 1977, in the city of Villa María in the Córdoba Province of Argentina. He is currently playing for Gimnàstic in La Liga in Spain.

Bizarri began his career Racing Club in the Argentine Primera. In 1999 he won a transfer to European giants Real Madrid, he was a reserve goalkeeper with the club when they won the UEFA Champions League in the year 2000.

During the summer of 2000 Bizarri was transferred to Real Valladolid where he played for six seasons before moving to his current club.
