The Albertsons Boise Open Presented by First Health is a golf tournament on the Nationwide Tour. It is played annually at the Hillcrest Country Club in Boise, Idaho, USA.

The title sponsor since 2002 is Albertsons, a supermarket retailer, headquartered in Minnesota, USA.

The 2006 purse was $650,000, with $108,000 going to the winner.
