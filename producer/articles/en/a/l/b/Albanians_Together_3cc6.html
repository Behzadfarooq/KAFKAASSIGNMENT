Albanians Together (Albanci zajedno) is a political alliance of Albanians in Montenegro. At the last legislative elections in Montenegro, 21 October 2002, the alliance won 2 out of 75 seats. The party is also represented in the Serbian and Montenegran parliament with 1 seat.
