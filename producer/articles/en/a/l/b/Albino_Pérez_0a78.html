Albino Pérez

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Albino Pérez (d. August 8, 1837) was a Mexican soldier and politician.

Pérez was appointed Governor of New Mexico in 1835. He was assassinated near Santa Fe, New Mexico in 1837 during the Revolt of 1837 after he tried to impose taxes ordered by President Antonio Lopez de Santa Anna.
