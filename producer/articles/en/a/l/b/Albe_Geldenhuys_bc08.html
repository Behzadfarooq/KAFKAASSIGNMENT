Albe Geldenhuys

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Albe Geldenhuys (born c. 1973) is the second winner of the Manhunt International competition. He is from South Africa.

A native of Durban, the 1.84 m model won the competition in 1994, when it was held in Singapore. He also won the Sun Smart and Vitality Awards.
