Albert Celades López (born September 29, 1975 in Andorra la Vella, Andorra) is a Spanish football player.

He played mostly for FC Barcelona and later for Real Madrid (becoming one of the few Catalans to do so). He now plays for Real Zaragoza.

He played for Spain national football team and was a participant at the 1998 FIFA World Cup.

[edit] Teams and clubs

  • 1993-95: FC Barcelona B
  • 1995-99: FC Barcelona
  • 1999-00: Celta de Vigo
  • 2000-03: Real Madrid
  • 2003-04: Bordeaux on loan from Real Madrid
  • 2004-05: Real Madrid
  • 2005-present: Real Zaragoza

[edit] Honours

  • 2 Spanish La Liga
  • 2 Copa del Rey
  • 1 Intercontinental Cup
  • 1 UEFA Champions League
  • 1 UEFA Super Cup
  • 1 UEFA Cup Winners' Cup
  • 1 Spanish Super Cup
