Album is the third studio album by Joan Jett and the second to feature her backing band The Blackhearts. The album was originally released in 1982. It was re-released in 1992 with six bonus tracks.

[edit] Track listing

All tracks written by Joan Jett and Kenny Laguna except where noted.

  1. "Fake Friends" – 3:23
  2. "Handyman" – 3:23
  3. "Everyday People" (S. Stewart) – 2:40
  4. "A Hundred Feet Away" (J. Jett, K. Laguna, P. Anders) – 2:33
  5. "Secret Love" – 4:03
  6. "Star Star" (M. Jagger, K. Richards) – 4:00
  7. "The French Song" (J. Jett, K. Laguna, R. Byrd, M. Winter Jr.) – 3:35
  8. "Tossin' & Turnin'" (R. Adams, M. Rene) – 2:25
  9. "Why Can't We Be Happy" – 3:53
  10. "I Love Playing With Fire" (J. Jett) – 3:03
  11. "Coney Island Whitefish" – 3:35
  12. "Had Enough" (J. Jett, K. Laguna, R. Byrd) – 2:26
  13. "Nitetime" – 4:52
  14. "Everyday People (Dance Mix)" (S. Stewart) – 4:19
  15. "Wait For Me" (J. Jett) – 3:45
  16. "Who Can You Trust" (J. Jett/K. Laguna/S. Lunt) – 2:51
  17. "Scratch My Back" (J. Jett/K. Laguna/S. Lunt) – 4:13
  18. "Locked Groove" – 6:17

Tracks 13 through 18 are bonus tracks on the 1992 re-release.
