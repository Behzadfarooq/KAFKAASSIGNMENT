Albania (Albanian: Shqipëria) is a Mediterranean country in southeastern Europe. It is bordered by Montenegro and Serbia in the north, the Republic of Macedonia in the east, and Greece in the south; it has a coast on the Adriatic Sea in the west, and a coast on the Ionian Sea in the southwest. The country is an emerging democracy and is formally named the Republic of Albania (Albanian: Republika e Shqipërisë).
