Albin Cardinal Dunajewski

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Cardinal Albin Dunajewski (b. March 1, 1817 in Stanisławów - June 19, 1894 in Kraków) was a political activist and Bishop of Kraków.

In 1882, he ordained Brother Raphael Kalinowski as a Priest of the Discalced Carmelite Order. Father Raphael was later canonized in 1991 by Pope John Paul II.
