Albeşti is a commune in Constanţa County, Romania.

The commune includes five villages:

  • Albeşti (historical name: Turkish: Akbaş)
  • Arsa
  • Coroana (historical names: Cadichioi, Turkish: Kadıköy)
  • Cotu Văii (historical names: Ciragi, Turkish: Kiracı)
  • Vârtop
