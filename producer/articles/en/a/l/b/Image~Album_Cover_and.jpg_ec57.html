[edit] Summary

With her angelic voice and in-depth lyrics, Jessica Cassidy breathes fresh air into the world of R&B and Urban Hip-Hop. She is a multi-talented artist who writes, produces and arranges her own songs. Her latest single "Dangerous Game" and first hit single "Where Are You ", which debuted in late 2002, showcase her undeniable talent as a singer.

Her life began in Jasper, Indiana on September 19, 1982, where she was born to Kevin and Jasminia Cassidy. When Jessica was four years old, Jessica and her parents moved to Houston, Tx to begin a new life. This is when her love of singing developed. As a young girl; Jessica would take her father's tape recorder in the closet and record herself singing the latest commercials such as the Mattress Giant commercials. She would do this for hours her parents recalled.

Her love for singing fostered as she grew older. She continued to sing throughout grade school and even performed at her own birthday parties. As she entered high school, she began to write and arrange her own songs. She used her writing as a release to deal with situations in her life, which is why so many of her fans can relate to her songs.

In mid 2003, Jessica met two other females, who were interested in music as well, and they started a group together. Jessica took lead of the group by directing them in the right direction toward their goal of becoming professional singers. In addition to being the lead singer; Jessica wrote, produced and arranged all of J3's songs. Together they performed at local clubs and bars in the Houston area including the Dacari Bar and grill, Co-Co loco club, Metropolis club, LaRaza club, Woodrow Bar, and T-Town club. The first member left the group around this time due to different priorities in her life, which did not include commitment to the group. The two remaining members including Jessica and another girl called themselves J2. Success continued to happen to the group as they began to perform at larger venues outside of the Houston area. J2 performed to a sold out crowd at Madison Square Garden and Le Bar Bat in New York City. As their success grew, tension between the group grew causing them to break-up and leaving Jessica Cassidy a solo artist.

She's proven that she has the drive and tenacity to make it in the industry. She single handedly became the first ever R&B artist to receive an on air interview at the world famous radio station 97.9 the Boxx (in Houston, TX) Sunday night program "Strait from the streets". Subsequent success was achieved when her next single "I'm Feeling You" was featured on local radio station Party 104.9 earlier this year. Many listeners called the station to request her songs, which gave her a powerful fan base in Houston. Her latest single "Dangerous Game" is receiving heavy rotation on KOZZ 103.7 in Opelousas, LA.

Jessica comes form a diverse background. She is an Irish Nicaraguan American and is not your average latin R&B singer. In addition to being biracial she is also bilingual. With her natural talent and strong will she is a force to be reckoned with in the music business and is ready to hit the mainstream scene, so be checking for her upcoming album.
