Albert Demtschenko (Russian: Альберт Михайлович Демченко; born November 27, 1971 in Chusovoy) is a Russian luger.

At the 1994 Winter Olympics he placed ninth in the men's singles event. At the 1998 Winter Olympics in Nagano he was disqualified because his runners were above the allowable temperature. He also competed at the 2002 Winter Olympics in Salt Lake City, where he placed fifth, and the 2006 Winter Olympics in Turin, Italy, winning silver in the men's singles event.
