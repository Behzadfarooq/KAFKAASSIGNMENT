Albuquerque Asylum are an American soccer team, founded in 2004. The team is a member of the National Premier Soccer League (NPSL), the fourth tier of the American Soccer Pyramid, and play in the Southwest Conference against teams from Denver, La Mesa, Las Vegas, Phoenix and San Diego.

The Asylum play their home matches at the stadium on the grounds of Menaul School in the city of Albuquerque, New Mexico. The team's colors are blue and white.
