Albino (disambiguation)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Look up Albino, albino in Wiktionary, the free dictionary.

Albino is an often derogatorily used term for a person (or animal) with oculocutaneus albinism — the lack of pigmentation in the eyes, skin and hair (or fur and feathers respectively).

Albino may also refer to:

  • Any of a number of characters in literature, movies, TV shows, etc., referred to as "the albino"
  • A problem in the game of chess
  • Albino, Italy, a location
  • Albino Luciani (1912-1978), better known as Pope John Paul I
  • Albino Blacksheep, an entertainment website
