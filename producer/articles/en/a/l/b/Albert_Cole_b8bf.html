Albert Cole (born July 7, 1981 in Freetown, Sierra Leone) is a Sierra Leonean international footballer. He currently plays as a midfielder for Raufoss I.L. in the Norwegian first division. He began his career with local club Mighty Blackpool in the Sierra Leonean Premier League.
