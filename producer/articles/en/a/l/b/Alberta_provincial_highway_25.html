Alberta provincial highway 25 is a short highway located in southern Alberta north of the city of Lethbridge.

25

It begins along Highway 3 on the northwestern outskirts of Lethbridge where it meets with University Drive West and interchanges with Highway 3.

It proceeds north passing by the hamlets of Diamond City and Shaughnessy until it comes to Highway 519 where it turns east and then goes north just before the town of Picture Butte.

It continues north until it reaches Highway 843 where it turns east again.

It passes by the hamlet of Iron Springs; crosses Highway 845; and reaches the hamlet of Turin.

Just north of Turin, Highway 521 branches off to the east as Highway 25 moves north and ends when it meets Highway 526 just a few km west of the hamlet of Enchant.
