Albert Achard (March 26, 1894 - August 21, 1972) was a World War I flying ace. Born in Briancon in France, Achard scored 1 victory as an observer and 4 victories as a pilot before being wounded in action on July 24, 1918.[1]

[edit] Medals

  • Croix de Guerre
  • Legion d'Honneur
