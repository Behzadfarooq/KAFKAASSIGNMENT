[edit] Deletions

Hi Alba. If you want to delete an article, put the {{del}} tag at the beginning of it, rather than removing all the content. If you remove the content, then someone could mistakenly think that you were vandalizing the page and undo your change. Also, blanking the page does not delete it. Thanks for contributing. Bart133 (t) 03:56, 15 Feb 2005 (UTC)

  • I think that obvious vanity can be marked with a {{db|Obvious vanity}} tag, unless you think there would be someone who didn't create the page who thinks it should be kept. Bart133 (t) 04:05, 15 Feb 2005 (UTC)
    • Even obvious vanity cannot be subjected to speedy delete. It must be listed on the VfD. If you list it with {{del}} and a sysop deletes it, they will be in violation of policy.
      See Wikipedia:Criteria for speedy deletion and Wikipedia:Proposal to expand WP:CSD/Proposal III (Vanity articles) for more details. →Iñgōlemo← talk donate 08:20, 2005 Feb 15 (UTC)
