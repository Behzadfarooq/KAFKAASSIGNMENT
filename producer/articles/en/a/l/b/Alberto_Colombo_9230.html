Alberto Colombo (born in Varedo, Milan, February 23, 1946) is a former Formula One driver from Italy. He unsuccessfully entered 3 Formula One Grands Prix in 1978 with ATS (2 failures to qualify) and Merzario (1 failure to pre-qualify).
