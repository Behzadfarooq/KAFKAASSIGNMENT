The Type 143 Albatros class was a German class of missile bearing Fast Attack Craft.

[edit] Specifications

  • Length: 57.8m
  • Beam: 7.8m
  • Draft: 2.6m
  • Displacement: 398t
  • Propulsion: 13.235kW
  • Speed: 40kts
  • Armament:
    • 2 OTO-Melara 76mm gun
    • 4 MM38 Exocet anti-ship missiles
    • 2 torpedo tubes (not used)
  • Countermeasures:
    • Decoy launcher HOT DOG
    • Chaff launcher DAG 2200 Wolke
  • Complement: 40

The boats are named after the birds of prey.

S61—S65 were part of the 2. Schnellbootgeschwader (Fast attack craft squadron), and S66—S70 belonged to the 7. Schnellbootgeschwader. Both squadrons were based in Warnemünde.
