Alberto Iglesias

Alberto Iglesias Fernández-Berridi (born San Sebastián, 1955) is a Spanish composer. He wrote the music for several spanish films, mostly from Pedro Almodóvar. He was Oscar nominated with his soundtrack for the film The Constant Gardener, directed by Fernando Meirelles.

[edit] Filmography (partial)

  • 2007 - The Kite Runner, directed by Marc Forster
  • 2006 - Volver, directed by Pedro Almodóvar
  • 2005 - The Constant Gardener, directed by Fernando Meirelles
  • 2004 - La mala educación, directed by Pedro Almodóvar
  • 2002 - Hable con Ella, directed by Pedro Almodóvar
  • 2001 - Lucía y el sexo, directed by Julio Medem
  • 1999 - Todo sobre mi madre, directed by Pedro Almodóvar
  • 1998 - Los Amantes del Círculo Polar, directed by Julio Medem
  • 1997 - La Femme de chambre du Titanic, directed by Bigas Luna
  • 1997 - Carne trémula, directed by Pedro Almodóvar
  • 1995 - La Flor de mi secreto, directed by Pedro Almodóvar
