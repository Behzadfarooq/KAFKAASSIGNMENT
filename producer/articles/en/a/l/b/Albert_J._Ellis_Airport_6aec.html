Albert J. Ellis Airport (IATA: OAJ, ICAO: KOAJ) is a public airport located 10 miles (16 km) northwest of the city of Jacksonville in Onslow County, North Carolina, USA. The airport has a single runway and three gates. It is mostly used for general aviation but is also served by two commercial airlines. On August 30, 2006, Delta Air Lines announced new service from the airport to Atlanta, operated by Delta Connection carrier Atlantic Southeast Airlines.

[edit] Airlines

  • Delta Air Lines
    • Delta Connection operated by Atlantic Southeast Airlines (Atlanta)
  • US Airways
    • US Airways Express operated by Air Wisconsin (Philadelphia)
    • US Airways Express operated by Piedmont Airlines (Charlotte)
