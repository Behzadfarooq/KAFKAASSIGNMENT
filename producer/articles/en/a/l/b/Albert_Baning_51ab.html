Albert Baning (born 17 March 1985) is a Cameroonian football midfielder currently playing club football for Paris St. Germain. Baning played for Swiss side FC Aarau before joining PSG in July of 2006 for an undisclosed transfer amount. [1]

His transfer was largely engineered by new team president Alain Cayzac. PSG's coach, Guy Lacombe, has admitted to never actually having seen him play. [2]
