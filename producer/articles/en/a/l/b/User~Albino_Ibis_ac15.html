Albino Ibis is the creator and main filmmaker of the independent Australian film company Timto Enterprises, who have created and produced such films as The Cat? (2006) and others. He co-produced The Cat? with HIM357. Both are active members of the popular forum ShadowThief. Albino Ibis is a super moderator of the forum ShadowThief.

Albino Ibis is also a DJ, known as Wild Child. He has already produced two albums: Airborne (2004) and The Pleonastic Chronicles (2006).
