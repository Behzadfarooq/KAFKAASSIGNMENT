Albinus Nance (1848 – 1911) is best known as the 5th Governor of Nebraska. A Republican, Nance also served as a member of Nebraska state house of representatives (1875-78), and was the speaker from 1877 to 1878 when he was elected governor. He was also a delegate to Republican National Convention in 1876, served in the union army during the Civil War and was a lawyer.
