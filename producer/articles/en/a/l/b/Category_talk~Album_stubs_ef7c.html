[edit] Subcategories

I think that we probably could use some more subcategories, as those four leave a lot of albums uncovered. Thebogusman 01:58, 14 December 2005 (UTC)

Well, Category:Rock-album stubs is virtually unused. But maybe it's unclear as to what exactly goes in there, since there are so many sub-variations of that? In either case, yeah, albums are currently the most overfilled category of any stub, and suggestions should probably be discussed or suggested at the Stub Sorting project asap. --Interiot 02:11, 14 December 2005 (UTC)
Thanks, I just found that project page. I definitely think it would be helpful to have subcategories for country, rap, classical, R&B and other genres, but it seems some people have already recommended it. Anyway, I'll try to wade through some of these when I have free time. Thebogusman 02:20, 14 December 2005 (UTC)

[edit] Suggested subcategories

Some categories that don't have yet, but seem to be somewhat common include:

  • {{metal-album-stub}}
  • {{folk-album-stub}}
  • {{punk-rock-album-stub}}
  • Less well-defined genres, probably best to put in other stub categories, but could be created if need be:
    • {{indie-album-stub}}
    • {{pop-album-stub}}

[edit] Bot for tagging?

I recently discovered WikiProject Albums, and thought that since I'd often put album-stub on a page I came across in NewPages, that I'd go through Category:Album stubs and add Template:Album to the articles in this category. I got through all the As and wondered why I was bothering with this when a bot could do it much faster and easier. So, is there a bot for doing that? Does anyone think it's a good idea? Maybe one could be requested at WP:BOTREQ. ~ ONUnicorn (Talk / Contribs) 20:03, 13 October 2006 (UTC)

[edit] Question about DVDs

I've noticed that there are a few things tagged as "album stubs" that aren't really albums at all, but are articles about concert DVDs that don't seem to have a live album to go with them. Is this really the best category for these? I guess I can't think of anywhere else to put them, but I'm rather reluctant to add an "album" tag to something that isn't an truly an album. Maybe I'm just being picky. Ministry of Silly Walks 02:15, 20 December 2006 (UTC)
