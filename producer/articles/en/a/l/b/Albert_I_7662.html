Albert I

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Albert I is the name of several historical people:

  • Albert I of Belgium (1875-1934), third king of Belgium
  • Albert I of Brandenburg (c. 1100-1170), Margrave of Brandenburg
  • Albert I, Duke of Brunswick-Lüneburg (1236-1279)
  • Albert I of Germany (c. 1250-1308), German king
  • Albert I, Prince of Monaco (1848-1922), Prince of Monaco
  • Albert I, Count of Namur (c. 950-1011), a Belgian count
  • Albert I, Duke of Prussia (1525–1568), first Duke of Prussia.
  • Albert I, Count Vermandois (917 - 8 September 987), Count of Vermandois in Normandy
  • Albert I of Käfernburg (d. 1232), Archbishop of Magdeburg
