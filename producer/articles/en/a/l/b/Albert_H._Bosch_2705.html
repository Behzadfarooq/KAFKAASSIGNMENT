Albert H. Bosch

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Albert Henry Bosch (October 30, 1908 - November 21, 2005) was a Republican member of the United States House of Representatives from New York.

Bosch was born in New York City. He graduated from St. John's University School of Law in 1933. He was elected to Congress in 1952 and served from January 3, 1953 until his resignation on December 31, 1960. He was elected judge of Queens County court and served from December 31, 1960 until September 1, 1962. He was a justice of the New York Supreme Court from 1962 until his retirement on December 31, 1974. He died in Amityville, New York.
