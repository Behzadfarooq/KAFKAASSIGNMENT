Albert Ezekiel Dingle was a fictional character on the ITV soap opera Emmerdale. He was played by Bobby Knutt.

Albert is the brother of Zak Dingle, a long-running character in Emmerdale, and father to Marlon and Eli. He is regularly in prison.
