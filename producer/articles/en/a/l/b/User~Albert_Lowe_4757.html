[edit] Summary

This list could go on, but I think I'll stop here for the moment. I put up the first website that tried to address the controversy surrounding WWI fighter ace, Billy Bishop. [1]

It was as an additional page to the Billy Bishop site, that I put up the first (as far as I know) web page about WWII American ace, Lance C. Wade. Upon finding Wikipedia, I have since created the Lance C. Wade article. So far, several people have contributed to it, making it better along the way.

Currently I am engaged in an eternal struggle to see to it that the Wiki article on John Lott is at least fair, and attempt to revert it to NPOV status. And no, I do NOT believe that the article on John Lott fits the Wikipedia Ideal of NPOV. And it would have the {{NPOV}} Except, every time I, or anyone else attempts to put it there, the nay-sayers and critics of Dr. Lott, take it off. I've tried objecting, but currently I am one against many.

In Michigan, I am the current coordinator for the Michigan Pink Pistols. [2]
