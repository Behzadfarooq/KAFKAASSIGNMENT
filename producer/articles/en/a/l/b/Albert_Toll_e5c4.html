Albert Toll

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

You have new messages (last change).

Albert Toll (1865-1960) is the founder of Australia and New Zealand's largest transport company, Toll Holdings and Toll NZ. He formed the company in 1888, when he hauled coal with a horse and a cart. The Toll Group is now Australia's largest transport and logistics provider.
