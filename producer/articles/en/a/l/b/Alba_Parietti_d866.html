Alba Parietti (born in Turin, Italy, on July 2, 1961) is an Italian film actress, television host, and showgirl.

Her career began in 1977 with an appearance in the play, The Importance of Being Ernest, by Oscar Wilde.

[edit] Filmography

  • Sapore di Mare (U.S. version entitled Time for Loving), 1983
  • Bye Bye Baby, 1988
  • Abbronzatissimi, 1991
  • Saint Tropez, Saint Tropez, 1992
  • Il Macellaio (U.S. version entitled The Butcher), 1998
  • Paparazzi, 1998
