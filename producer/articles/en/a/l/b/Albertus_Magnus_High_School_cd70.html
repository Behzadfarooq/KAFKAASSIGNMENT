Albertus Magnus High School

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Albertus Magnus High School is the only Catholic high school in Rockland, New York. It describes itself as a "Catholic, co-educational community of faith in which our future leaders strive for academic excellence, seek truth and show respect for the dignity of each person." [1]
