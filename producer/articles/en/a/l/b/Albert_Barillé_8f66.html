Albert Barillé is a French television producer, creator, screenwriter, cartoonist, and founder of Procidis. He is the creator of the puppet animated series Colargol, and the series Once Upon a Time... He is also an author of medical documentaries, theatre pieces, and vulgarised philosophy.

“ Make our children want to know, arouse their curiosity. Also treat them as people in their own right, who understand much more than adults would have us believe.They will be stronger for it and be grateful to you. ”
