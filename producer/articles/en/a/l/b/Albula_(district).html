Albula is an administrative district in the canton of Graubünden in Switzerland. It has an area of 723.13 km² and a population of 8,720 (as of December 2004).

Albula consists of four Kreise (sub-districts) Alvaschein, Belfort, Bergün and Surses, which are formed from a total of 23 municipalities
