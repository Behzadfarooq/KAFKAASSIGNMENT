Albert E. Cowdrey

Albert E. Cowdrey is an American fantasy author. He has published the science fiction novel Crux, but most of his short fiction published in Fantasy and Science Fiction centers on his love for New Orleans. He was Chief of the Special History Branch in the U.S. Army, and wrote a number of nonfiction books about the history of the medical branches of the army.
