Albarracín is a town of Spain, in the province of Teruel, part of the autonomous community of Aragon.

The Albarracin mountains can be found in Spain. The river Tagus flows from here through Spain to Portugal.

A Cottage The Town of Albarracín

Albarracin is known for its beauty and tourism.
