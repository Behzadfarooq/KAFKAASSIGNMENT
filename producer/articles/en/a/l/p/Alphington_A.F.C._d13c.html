Alphington A.F.C. is an Exeter based football club from the Exeter suburb of Alphington, Devon in England. They were established in 1946. They were the founder members of the Devon County League in 1992. Fifteen years on, for the 2006-07 season, they still members of the Devon County League.

[edit] Honours

  • Devon League Runners-up: 1
    • 1994-95
  • Throgmorton Cup Winners: 1
    • 1993-94
  • Devon & Exeter League Premier Division Champions: 2
    • 1977-78 & 1978-79
  • Devon & Exeter League Premier Division Runners-up: 2
    • 1979-80 & 1985-86
  • Devon FA Intermediate Cup Winners: 1
    • 1956-57
  • East Devon Senior Cup Winners: 1
    • 1979-80
