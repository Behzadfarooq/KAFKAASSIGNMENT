At the 1976 Winter Olympics, six Alpine Skiing events were contested.

[edit] Men's events

[edit] Downhill

Medal Athlete Time
Gold Franz Klammer (AUT) 1:45.73
Silver Bernhard Russi (SUI) 1:46.06
Bronze Herbert Plank (ITA) 1:46.59

[edit] Giant Slalom

Medal Athlete Time
Gold Heini Hemmi (SUI) 3:26.97
Silver Ernst Good (SUI) 3:27.17
Bronze Ingemar Stenmark (SWE) 3:27.41

[edit] Slalom

Medal Athlete Time
Gold Piero Gros (ITA) 2:03.29
Silver Gustav Thöni (ITA) 2:03.73
Bronze Willi Frommelt (LIE) 2:04.28

[edit] Women's events

[edit] Downhill

Medal Athlete Time

Silver Brigitte Totschnig (AUT) 1:46.68

Medal Athlete Time

[edit] Slalom

Medal Athlete Time
