Alphonse D'Arco

Alphonse "Little Al" D'Arco, born July 28, 1932 in Brooklyn, New York, is a member of the Lucchese crime family and former interim Mafia boss who became a "devastating informant"[1] to the police following a loss of faith in Mafia bosses Vittorio "Vic" Amuso and Anthony "Gaspipe" Casso.
