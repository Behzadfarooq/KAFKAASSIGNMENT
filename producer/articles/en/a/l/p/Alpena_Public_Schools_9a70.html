Alpena Public Schools

Alpena Public Schools is a Michigan school district encompassing all of Alpena County and, in Presque Isle County, all of Presque Isle Township and part of Krakow Township. APS covers more than 620 square miles and was officially established as Michigan's first county-wide school district in 1963.
