Alphons Egli (born October 8, 1924) is a Swiss politician and member of the Swiss Federal Council (1983-1986).

He was elected to the Federal Council of Switzerland on December 8, 1982 and handed over office on December 31, 1986. He is affiliated to the Christian Democratic People's Party of Switzerland.

During his office time he held the Federal Department of Home Affairs and was President of the Confederation in 1986.

Egli is a citizen of Entlebuch and Lucerne.
