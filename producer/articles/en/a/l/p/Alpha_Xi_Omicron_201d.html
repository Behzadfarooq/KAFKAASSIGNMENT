[edit] History

Alpha Xi Omicron was founded on March 9, 1927, by seven students at the University of Redlands. The women met on the principles of promoting diversity on campus as well as the local community. During this period the official colors, flowers, crest and symbol were selected. Alpha Xi became inactive in the 1970's. On September 18, 1998 six women re-founded Alpha Xi along with one honorary member. During this period the Sylvan house was awarded to the Alpha Xi's by the University.

[edit] Pledge Process

Alpha Xi Omicron's pledging process is secretive and takes place within a five week period of time in the Fall and Spring semesters according to the University of Redlands regulations.[2]
