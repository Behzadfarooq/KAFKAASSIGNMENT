Alpha and Omega has several meanings:

  • Alpha and Omega (Christianity) in Christianity
  • Alpha and omega, the first and the last letter in the Greek alphabet respectively
  • Alpha & Omega (band), a British reggae band.
  • Alpha and Omega (album), an album by Bizzy Bone
  • Alpha and Omega (Harrison), a collection of essays by Jane Harrison
  • "Alpha and Omega", a song by the band Boards of Canada on their album Geogaddi
  • "Alpha Omega", a song by the band The Mountain Goats on their album Protein Source of the Future... Now!
  • Alpha Omega Co-ed Christian Fraternity, Incorporated , a men's and women's (co-ed) Christian Fraternity based out of Kalamazoo, Michigan
  • Alpha Omega (Christian homeschooled curriculum), a homeschooled ,Christian curriculum brand name.
