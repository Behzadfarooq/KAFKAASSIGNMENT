Please click here to leave me a new message. It will temporarily stop my operation until Alphachimp reviews your message. All comments are archived daily to keep this page clean for emergency stops.
The bot cannot place warnings, block users, or comment on why you were warned. Removing a warning will not make it disappear, and is generally considered vandalism. For reasoning behind a warning, please contact the person who signed the warning.

The bot cannot comment on why an article was marked as needing cleanup. For reasoning behind a cleanup tag, please contact the person who added the tag.

The bot cannot comment on why an article was suggested for a merge. For reasoning behind a merge tag, please contact the person who added the tag.

The bot cannot comment on the meaning of Template Substitution. It's a bit of a confusing concept to grasp at first. Alphachimp strongly recommends reading WP:SUBST and then posting any further questions on his talk page.

Alphachimp would love to hear about any mistakes you have found in Alphachimpbot's work, but this is not the best place to make such a report. If your inquiries are non-critical, please post your errors on his talk page. Note that Alphachimpbot uses AWB and pywikipediabot, so errors are likely a result of problems in that software.
