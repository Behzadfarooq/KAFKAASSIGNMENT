Alphaville

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Alphaville is:

  • Alphaville (band), a German music trio whose members are Marian Gold, Rainer Bloss and Briton Martin Lister.
  • Alphaville (film), a French science fiction film directed by Jean-Luc Godard.
  • Alphaville, São Paulo, a municipality on the western edges of São Paulo, Brazil.
  • A fictional city in The Sims Online.
  • An American film production company founded in the early 1990s, notable films include The Mummy series.
