Alpha Wave Movement (established 1992) is the electronic music project of Gregory T. Kyryluk. AWM's style can be considered an amalgam of classic 1970s period German style electronic music otherwise known as "Berlin-school" and the ambient aesthetics of Brian Eno, John Serie and Steve Roach. AWM's music is all electronic-based, utilizing digital synthesizers and MIDI to create its sonic explorations. Aside from the music influences nature and natural landscapes are a continuous source of inspiration for compositions. AWM has performed at the Ambient Ping Canada and at the Gathering Room USA. AWM has released music on the Dutch label Groove Unlimited, Silent Records (USA), Waveform Records (USA), Spiralight (USA) and on the private label Harmonic Resonance Recordings. AWM's other projects include Thought Guild, Open Canvas and Kyryluk's solo releases.
