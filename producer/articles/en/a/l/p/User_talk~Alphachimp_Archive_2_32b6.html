Your 26 May 2006 Reverting of Wright Amendment

I had modified the sentence about the proposed Nebraska Wright Amendment exemption bill because (a) I think that having that sentence end simply with "will open up Nebraska" assumes that the bill's enactment is inevitable and (b) I think that the use of the term "recently" is not necessary, given that the sentence comes right after descriptions of the 1997 Shelby Amendment and the 2005 exemption of Missouri from Wright. If this is indeed vandalism, I am very sorry to have committed it. -- 68.85.203.233 08:24, 26 May 2006 (UTC)
