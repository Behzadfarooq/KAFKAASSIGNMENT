[edit] Clone Wars

Alpha 17, or just Alpha was an ARC Trooper from the planet Kamino. He was part of the first batch of ARC Troopers. He fought in the First Battle of Kamino with Obi-Wan Kenobi. There he defended the cloneing complex. He contenued to serve with Kenobi through out the Clone Wars. After the Battle of Jabiim he was thought dead, along with Kenobi. But he was actualy captured by Asajj Ventress. He was taken to Ventress's castle on the planet Rattatak. There he was tortured. He escaped with Kenobi. After that he returned to Kamino to train the new batch of Clone Commanders. Around 19 BBY he returned to fight in the Battle of Boz Pitty with Kenobi. There he was stabbed in the stomach with a Lightsaber by General Grevious. He survived and was borded onto the medical transport Bright Flight. That was the same ship that the thought dead Asajj Ventress. While in Hyperspace Ventress awoke and told the pilots to take her far away from the core. Ventress and Alpha were never heard from again.
