Alppila

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Location of Alppila within Helsinki

Alppila (Swedish Alphyddan) is a district of Helsinki, Finland. It is located north of the city centre, between the districts of Kallio and Pasila. Alppila has a population of 4244 (as of 2005) and an area of 0.60 km². The Linnanmäki amusement park is located in Alppila.
