The ΑΜΩ Fraternity

Alpha Mu Omega, or the ΑΜΩ fraternity, is a coeducational fraternity and was officially formed in secret at midnight on Wednesday, January 31, 2007 by seven academic students from inside the campus of Redeemer University College in Ancaster of greater Hamilton, Ontario, Canada. Alpha Mu Omega has a open-closed moral code for pledging, rushing or just plain old joining up, with bonds starting to grow and members transferring from within the student bodies of fellow institutions of higher education McMaster University of Westdale and Mohawk College of Hamilton Mountain, who are both also part of the greater City of Hamilton. Alpha Mu Omega and its adherents are absolutely committed to excellence in each and every academic, political, athletic and personal life matter. Alpha Mu Omega's maroon-red and grey-white logo ΑΜΩ includes MRM in the background to signify its brotherly relations between Hamilton's trio of higher education institutions and distinct chapters within this particular fraternity. Alpha Mu Omega's motto in Latin "Usquequaque memor efficio ad ut alius ut vos spero fui lemma operor ad vos!" literally means "Always remember to do unto others as you would have them do unto you!" in English. Alpha Mu Omega's handshake is distinctive, with its extra snap after the smack of the palm, which had been invented by one of the three co-founding brothers. Alpha Mu Omega's international constitution, regulations or bylaws essentially consist of three simple rules, those selected individuals who successfully complete ritualistic initiation to join and who can follow those simple rules of the collective, are official ΑΜΩ frat bros.


Three Rules of Alpha Mu Omega:


  • 1. Do not confirm, deny or admit the existence of the secret Alpha Mu Omega fraternity.
  • 2. There are no more rules after the first rule.
  • 3. Neither rules two nor three are rules or regulations but rather guide lines or conventions.


Signed by Joshua E. Eriksen, Nathan Bloomfield and Macieo Justice - original co-founding brothers of Alpha Mu Omega
