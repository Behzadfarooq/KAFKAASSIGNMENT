I'm a student at the University of Waterloo, Ontario, Canada in Fourth Year Computer Science. I'm sort of new to Wikipedia, but I've been working at Encyclopedia Gamia, currently the Web's largest dedicated Gaming Wiki for the last 8 months as part of the core contributors. I was the founder of the UWGamers, a University of Waterloo club, which promotes video games as entertainment and an emerging industry.
