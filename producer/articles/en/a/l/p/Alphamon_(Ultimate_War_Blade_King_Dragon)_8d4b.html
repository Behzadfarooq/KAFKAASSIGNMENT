Alphamon (Ultimate War Blade King Dragon) is a fictional character from the Digimon franchise, a Mega Level Holy Knight Digimon, the powered up version of Alphamon. He is not just one of the 13 Royal Knights but also their leader of legend. He is a digimon of great power due to his possession of the "Alpha-Gain-Force". However, this very same factor has deemed him "Lord of the Empty Seat"; as he is destined to rule the Royal Knights but to be forever absent from their ranks. Thus his fate leaves him to only make temporary appearances wherever his aid is needed.

[edit] Description

The powered-up version of Alphamon, Alphamon (Ultimate War Blade King Dragon) features a pair of wings on his back, as well as a giant sword.

[edit] Attacks

  • Ultimate War Blade King Dragon Sword (Great Dragon's Ultimate Vanguard Sword/Kyuukyoku Senjin Ou Ryuu Ken): Uses his giant sword to attack his enemies. His most powerful attack.
  • Digitalize of Soul: Fires several green energy beams from his hands. His signature move.

[edit] Appearances

[edit] Digital Monster X-Evolution

Alphamon summoning Ultimate War Blade King Dragon Sword.
Spoiler warning: Plot and/or ending details follow.

Alphamon (Ultimate War Blade King Dragon) appears at the end of the Digital Monster X-Evolution movie, though he is still named Alphamon and there is no evolution scene or any mentioning why he changed. In this form he fights against Dexmon and when he realizes that the evil Digimon is his shadow, he uses his sword to impale himself on Dexmon. Before he vanishes he gives his X-Antibody to Omnimon, X-modifying him into Omnimon X. Afterwards he merges with Dexmon and becomes Dorumon again.
