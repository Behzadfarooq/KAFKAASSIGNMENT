The AKO Fraternity

Alpha Kai Omega, or the AKO Fraternity, was formed in 1929 by seven students from the Walkerville-Windsor Technical School in Windsor, Ontario, Canada. The Fraternity serves the community in many ways, mostly with fundraisers and sponsorships. AKO takes great pride in sponsoring athletic teams at all levels of play in the City of Windsor. Two of the more successful teams have been the Windsor Fratmen Football Team of the Canadian Junior Football League and the Jr. "B" Fratmen Lacrosse Team of the Ontario Lacrosse Association.
