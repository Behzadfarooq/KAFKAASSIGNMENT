Welcome to Wikipedia, Alphaphoenix!

I'm glad you found WIkipedia and I hope you decide to stick around. Be sure to post your name on the Wikipedia new user log and say hello. Here is a list of tools and tips to get you started.

  • Five pillars of Wikipedia — learn about Wikipedia's basic guidelines and policies
  • Wikipedia:Tutorial runs through all the basics in a quick and easy way
    • Editing tutorial — learn how to edit articles
    • Picture tutorial — learn how to upload pictures
  • Manual of Style — learn about Wikipedia style
  • Sandbox — practice using Wikistyle
  • User_page — set up your user page and tell us a little about yourself
  • The Department of Fun, The Newcomer's Help Page and The Open Tasks List are good places to check out.
You can sign your comments on talk pages with four tildes: ~~~~ This adds your name and current time to your comments. If you need any more help, feel free to come to Bootcamp, add {{helpme}} to your talk page, or contact me on my talk page and I'll do what I can to assist you. Be bold and have fun. Thanks for helping out. Jessamyn 18:23, 6 March 2006 (UTC)
