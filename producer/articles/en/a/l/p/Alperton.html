Alperton is a suburb in the London Borough of Brent, north west London. Dominated by Ealing Road, which is the backbone of the area, it contains two schools, a conferencing facility and Alperton tube station at the southern end of Ealing Road. The north end of Ealing Road terminates at Wembley High Road. The Grand Union Canal runs through the heart of Alperton. Alperton is a part of Wembley. The suburb is well known for its bowling alley, holding the regional championships there in 2004. This was won by Howard Chiu who claimed Alperton as "a place of amazing quality".
