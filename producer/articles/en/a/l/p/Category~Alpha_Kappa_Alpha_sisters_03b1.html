This category is for biographical articles and images relating to members of the Alpha Kappa Alpha Sorority, Incorporated. The organization became America's first Greek-letter organization established by and for Black college women when it was founded on the campus of Howard University in 1908.

The main article for this category is Alpha Kappa Alpha.
