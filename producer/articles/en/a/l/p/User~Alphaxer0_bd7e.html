Hi, I am alphaxer0. Alphaxer0 is the handle of a Maryland Resident. I am currently a history major at UMBC. My website is at http://history-guy.blogspot.com

I have done some contributing to wikipedia since the end of 2003. My main interests are in the social science categories, mainly history, but military and current events related things get some attention from me.


Articles I have started

Here are some articles, I have started

Kenneth Pollack- Pollack is an American author and expert on the Middle East. I have read both of his books, in particular Arabs At War. Arabs at War is an excellent book, which debunks many myths about the Arab military experiences of the Post-WWII era.

Imad Mugniyah is the founder/intelligence chief/security officer of Hizbullah. I have been interested in him, since I was assigned to do a paper on him in an English class.

Point of Interest

Since, I have starting editing wikipedia, I have found it is fun to go to the requested articles section and randomly pick a requested article in category you're somewhat familar with, research it, and then write up an entry.

Contacting me-- The best way to reach me is by email. I would really prefer that, given, I am semi-compulsive in regards to checking it. So, given that, it is nice to occassionally receive some. Otherwise, just post on my talk page.
