[edit] Jan Akkerman

Re Jan Akkerman's bio: this was probably translated from the Dutch original - there's nothing wrong with that, but if somebody takes the trouble to correct or revise some of the Dinglish (is active instead of has been active)or misused musical terms (note instead of tone, for a note can only be described in terms of pitch and duration) or adds a pun on metal that made Jan the Man himself laugh out loud at a Vondelpark gig, I feel it's a little unreasonable to just delete these additions. The reason why Akkerman is not included among Rolling Stone's 100 Greatest Guitarists is, as I wrote, unfathomable. I asked a lifelong (British)fan of Akkerman's to clarify this matter, and he came up with the xenophobia mentioned. Jan himself commented on Neil Young's technical ability in a documentary by quipping that the latter sounds like a "bromvlieg in een puntzak", so much for the Canadian wailer that sure enough does appear on the RS list! It may get lost in translation, but the reference is to a buzzing insect in a wrapper from which the Dutch tend to eat either liquorice or French fries. Happy New year, anyway and please let me know what you think. Btw the number covers a server that apparently includes various contributors, so I'm not responsible for all of the stuff listed under this code - it's mainly music, AmLit and Meher Baba. 202.150.34.8 02:01, 5 January 2006 (UTC)
