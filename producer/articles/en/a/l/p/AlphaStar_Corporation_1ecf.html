AlphaSTAR Corporation (ASC) is a provider of advanced engineering analysis services and of specialized software for the analysis of complex engineering material systems. ASC supports a wide range of customers from the aerospace, automotive and civil structures industries.

ASC specializes in the analysis of complex (linear and non-linear) structural systems of composites, ceramics, and other advanced materials under environmental, cryogenic and elevated temperatures subject to static, fatigue, dynamic and impact loads.

The company has teamed with government centers, select business entities and universities to develop and enhance advanced methods required for solving complex engineering problems. ASC applies advanced probabilistic and optimization assessments for increasing design robustness.

ASCâ€˜s software flagship product, GENOA, couples finite element analysis and micro-mechanics to track damage and crack growth progressively. GENOA was developed in collaboration with NASA and extensively verified by aerospace, automobile, and construction industries.

ASC was incorporated in 1989 and is a privately owned business with its headquarters office in Long Beach, California USA.
