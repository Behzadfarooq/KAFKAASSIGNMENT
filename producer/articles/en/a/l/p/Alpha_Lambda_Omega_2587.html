Alpha Lambda Omega Sorority (AΛΩ)-is a national Christian sorority founded April 9, 1990 by four founders at the the University of Texas at Austin. The sorority consists of 14 chapters in the states of Texas, Pennslyvania, Michigan, and Oklahoma and is governed by a national executive board in the headquarters in Texas.

[edit] History

In the Spring of 1990, four newly converted Christian ladies named Tyesha Elam, Amanda Tezeno, Dawn Walton, and Terra Delaney founded Alpha Lambda Omega Christian Sorority, Inc. after much debate on trying to pursue a better Christian walk. So on April 9, 1990 Alpha Lambda Omega Christian Sorority was born.


[edit] Brothers

The official brothers of Alpha Lambda Omega Christian Sorority are the brothers of Gamma Phi Delta Christian Fraternity, Inc. founded in 1988.
