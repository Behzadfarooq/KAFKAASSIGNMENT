Alpine skiing at the 2007 Asian Winter Games

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

You have new messages (last change).

Alpine skiing at the 2007 Asian Winter Games was held at Beida Lake Skiing Resort in Changchun, China. Events were held from 31 January to 3 February 2007.[1]
