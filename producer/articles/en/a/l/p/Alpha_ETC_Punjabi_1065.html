Alpha ETC Punjabi

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Alpha ETC Punjabi is a pay television channel that is available in the United States, Canada, United Kingdom. It is a popular Punjabi language television channel from Noida, India. Programming comes in the form of movies, news, dramas, comedies, talk shows and religious programming from Harmandir Sahib.
