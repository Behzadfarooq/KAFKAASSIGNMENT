This category is for biographical articles and images relating to the founders (commonly referred to as The Seven Jewels, but also as Cornell Seven) of Alpha Phi Alpha, the first intercollegiate Greek-letter fraternity established for African Americans. The organization was founded on the campus of Cornell University in 1906.[1]

The main article for this category is Alpha Phi Alpha.
