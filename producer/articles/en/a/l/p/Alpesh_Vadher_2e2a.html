Alpesh Vallabhdas Vadher (born September 7, 1974 in Nairobi) is a Kenyan cricketer. He is a right-handed batsman. Having made a commendable 42 on his debut against Bangladesh in 1997, he did little more until the 1999 Cricket World Cup, in which he made two fifties, including an unbeaten 73 against Sri Lanka.

He retired in the year 2000 and was called back by the team in time for the Cricket World Cup along with Asif Karim, former captain, but did not represent Kenya in any more matches.

His father's name is Vallabdhas Vadher.
