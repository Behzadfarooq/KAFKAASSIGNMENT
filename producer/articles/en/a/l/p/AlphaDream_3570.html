AlphaDream Corporation, Ltd. (株式会社アルファドリーム, Kabushikigaisha ArufaDorīmu?) is a Tokyo-based videogame development team, founded in 2001 by Tetsuo Mizuno. As a second-party developer for Nintendo, it has produced software for the Game Boy Color, the Game Boy Advance and the Nintendo DS.
