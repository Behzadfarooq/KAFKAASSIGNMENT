Alpine School District is the primary school district in northern Utah County in Utah, including the cities of Orem, Lindon, Pleasant Grove, American Fork, Alpine, Highland, Lehi, Saratoga Springs, and Eagle Mountain. It includes all grades from kindergarten all through high school (K-12). As of January 2007, there were 46 elementary schools, 10 junior high schools, 7 high schools, and 3 alternative schools. The district offices are located in American Fork, Utah.
