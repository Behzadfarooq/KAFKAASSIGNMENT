Alpena County Regional Airport (IATA: APN, ICAO: KAPN, FAA LID: APN) is a public-suse airport located six miles (10 km) west of the central business district of Alpena, a city in Alpena County, Michigan, United States. It is mostly used for general aviation, but is also served by one commercial airline.

Several scenes of the film Die Hard 2 were shot there.

[edit] Facilities

Alpena County Regional Airport covers 1,284 acres and has two runways:

  • Runway 1/19: 9001 x 150 ft. (2744 x 46 m), Surface: Concrete
  • Runway 7/25: 5031 x 100 ft. (1533 x 30 m), Surface: Concrete

[edit] Airline

  • Northwest Airlines
    • Northwest Airlink operated by Mesaba Airlines (Detroit, Sault Ste. Marie)
