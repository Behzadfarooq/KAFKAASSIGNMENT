[edit] Boundary of Aljunied

Aljunied is bounded by PIE in the north, and Paya Lebar Rd in the East. What should be the boundary in the South:

  • Sims Ave,
  • Geylang Rd,
  • Guillemard Rd, or
  • Kallang basin river?
