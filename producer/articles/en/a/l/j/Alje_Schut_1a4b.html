Alje Schut

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Alje Schut (born February 18, 1981 in Utrecht) is a football defender from the Netherlands, who is playing for FC Utrecht since the 1999-2000 season in the Dutch highest league, Eredivisie.
