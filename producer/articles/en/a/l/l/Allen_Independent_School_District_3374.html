Allen Independent School District is a public school district based in Allen, Texas (USA).

Allen ISD covers most of the city of Allen and a portion of the city of Parker.

[edit] History

Until fall 2006, Allen ISD took high school students zoned to Lovejoy Independent School District, which includes city of Lucas, most of Fairview, and a small portion of Allen. In fall 2006, Lovejoy High School opened. During the same year, Allen ISD opened Chandler Elementary School [1].
