[edit] Alligators attacking people

In the week leading up to 2006-05-15, alligators in Florida attacked and killed three women[1].

A 54-year-old woman was killed by an alligator on 2004-07-27 while she was landscaping[2]. Florida police officers shot and killed the 457-pound alligator.The alligator was 12 feet long, and the only eyewitness said she was 15 feet away from the waters edge. This may be the only physical proof alligators are seeing humans as food. This was the 14th death by alligator in Florida since record-keeping began.Many people think these alligator attacks are due to their loss of marshy habitat because of development in alligator's habitat areas.

Florida fatal alligator attacks have occurred sporadically since 1973 when recordkeeping first started. From 1973 to June 2006, there have been 17 deaths from alligators in Florida[3].

[edit] People attacking with alligators

On 2004-07-19, a man in Florida was charged with assaulting his girlfriend with a three-foot alligator[4].
