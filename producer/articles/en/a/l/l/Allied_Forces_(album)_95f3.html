Allied Forces is the fifth studio album by Canadian rock band Triumph, released in 1981 (see 1981 in music). The album reached #23 on the Billboard Pop Albums chart assisted by the singles "Magic Power" and "Fight The Good Fight" which hit #8 and #18, respectively, on the Mainstream Rock chart of 1981. A remastered CD was first released in 1995 then again in 2004.
