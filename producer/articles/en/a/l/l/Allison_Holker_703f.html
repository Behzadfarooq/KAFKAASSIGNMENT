Allison Renae Holker (b. February 6, 1988, Minnesota), is a contemporary jazz dancer from Orem, Utah, a top eight finalist on season two of So You Think You Can Dance. Holker was eliminated on the July 27, 2006 episode.

Holker grew up in Orem, Utah. In 2006, she graduated from Timpanogos High School in June 2006. She also attended kampNYC, a Broadway camp. She is a modern lyrical specialist. Allison has been teaching at "The Dance Club" in her hometown since she was 14 years old. She has trained at that same studio since she was 4 years old. According to her biography, she has been dancing since she was three years old. Holker is teaching at the New York City Dance Alliance.

Allison also appeared in the Disney Channel hit movie "High School Musical" as a cheerleader for the East High Wildcats.

Allison's parents' names are David and Nikki Holker. She has two older brothers, David and Aaron, and two older sisters, Jessica and Becky, and one younger sister Abbygail, who is 2 years old. Her sister, Jessica Nicole Holker, who is 5 years older than Allison, is an award-winning dancer currently working for Celine Dion [1].

She was in So You Think You Can Dance Season 2 Top 10 and Top 20. According to Mary Murphy, her elimination was the most shocking the entire season.
