Allgäu as seen from a hot air balloon

Allgäu is an area in south-west of Swabia (Bavaria) and contains also a small part of south-east Baden-Württemberg.

It's sub-divided in following regions:

  • Oberallgäu
  • Unterallgäu
  • Ostallgäu
  • Westallgäu
