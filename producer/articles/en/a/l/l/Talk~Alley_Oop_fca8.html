[edit] Wonmug

Perhaps this is a quibble, but it is a misconception that "Ein stein" means "one mug" in German. It actually translates as "one stone" or "a stone." I know that Americans commonly call large beer mug "steins" but this is not what native German speakers call them (Becher). Ozarkbunnyboy 14:40, 21 February 2007 (UTC)
