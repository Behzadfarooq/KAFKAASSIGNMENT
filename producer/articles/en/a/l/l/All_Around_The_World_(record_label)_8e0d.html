All Around The World (record label)

All Around The World (also known simply by AATW) is a record label from Blackburn, England, and based on the principles of Italian label Media Records, with a number of studio based projects supplying a flow of hits. Starting off in the early 1990s as an independent label specialising in house music, the label's musical spectrum has widened to encompass pure pop hits such as Liz McClarnon's covers of "...Sweetest Felling" and "Woman In Love". The label is also very active in the compilations and specialist-marketing area of the music business with its main compilation brands being the Floorfillers and Clubland brands in association with UMTV

[edit] Chart Success

AATW have had three Number 1 singles in the UK Charts.

  • LMC Vs U2 - Take Me To The Clouds Above (Feb 2004)
  • DJ Casper - Cha Cha Slide (Mar 2004)
  • Frankee - F.U.R.B. (F U Right Back (May 2004)

[edit] Promos

All Around The World regularly release promotional/promo vinyls to promote their new music before general release. The large bulk of promos never make it to an official release and most of the promos are featured on the Clubland and Floorfiller compilation brands.
