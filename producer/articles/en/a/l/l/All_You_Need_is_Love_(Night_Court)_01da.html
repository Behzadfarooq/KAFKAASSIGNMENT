All You Need is Love is the Pilot episode of the television situation comedy Night Court.

[edit] Plot

All You Need is Love introduces Judge Harold T. Stone on his first night presiding over Criminal Court part 2 of the city of New York. The Court Clerk, Lana Wagner, is introduced along with Dan Fielding and Bull the Court Bailiff. His first cases are with his friend Leonard G. "Hacksaw" Hammond and Louise Kerr who is charged with attempted murder after she takes a couple of shots at her husband.

[edit] Cast

  • Harry Anderson as Judge Harold T. "Harry" Stone
  • Karen Austin as Court Clerk Lana Wagner
  • Selma Diamond as Bailiff Selma Hacker
  • Richard Moll as Bailiff Nostradamus 'Bull' Shannon
  • John Larroquette as Assistant D.A. Dan Fielding
  • Gail Strickland as Sheila Gardner
  • Peggy McKay as Louise Kerr
