The All-Ireland Senior Camogie Championship (known for sponsorship reasons as the TG4 Camogie Championship) is the premier competition in the game of camogie played in Ireland. The series of games are organised by Cumann Camogaíochta na nGael and are played during the summer months with the All-Ireland Camogie Final being played on the second or third Sunday in September in Croke Park, Dublin. The prize for the winning team is the O'Duffy Cup.
