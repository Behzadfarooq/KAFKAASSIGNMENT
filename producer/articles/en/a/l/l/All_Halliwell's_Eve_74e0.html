[edit] Synopsis

Spoiler warning: Plot and/or ending details follow.

It's Halloween at the Manor and the Grimlocks, which the Charmed Ones vanquished in Season 1, return. But before the Charmed Ones can vanquish them again, they are transported back into the 1600s. They don't have their powers and they are supposed to save a woman who is going to have a very powerful magical child. Through all of this, the Charmed ones have to learn more about the magic that was used back then to protect the baby. Unknown to them, Cole is the demon behind their time travel in an attempt to destroy the Charmed Ones' family line. He is surprised to discover that Phoebe's true love's name begins with a C. Both believe that he may be her destined true love. Before the Charmed Ones leave, they figure out that the baby is destined to be named Melinda Warren.

[edit] Book of Shadows

[edit] Enemies

  • Grimlocks: Grimlocks are white demons with whirling red eyes. Underground demons who roam from city to city killing powerful forces of Good by seeing the unique auras that surround them, which they are able to do by stealing the sight of innocent children. The stolen eyesight will last twenty-four hours, but only if the children are alive. After the children's use has waned, the Grimlocks consume them.
  • Ruth: Past witch that kidnapped Charlotte (Melinda Warren's mother), worked with Cole.

[edit] Spells

  • To Vanquish a Grimlock: Gather a Shisandra root and boil it in water with thyme; this is used to blind the Grimlocks, thus killing them.
  • To Cast a Protective Circle: You need: Lavender, Rosemary, 4 half apples. Make a circle around the area you’d like to protect and place one apple in each direction of the wind. Then repeat the words: Knowledge and reverence. Knowledge and reverence. Knowledge and reverence.

[edit] Trivia

  • The title of this episode is a play on words for the term All Hallow's Eve, meaning Halloween.
  • For Halloween, Phoebe dresses as Elvira, Piper dresses as Glinda from The Wizard of Oz, and Prue dresses as a witch of nature.
  • The WB used the alternate title A Charmed Halloween in promotional commercials.
  • Until the season eight episode Kill Billie Vol. 1, this had been the only time the Charmed Ones were shown to celebrate Halloween on the series.
  • Grimlocks were said to have whirling red eyes in two separate episodes. They are whirling until they absorb a child's sight, then they turn black.
  • This episode shows the mother of Melinda Warren, Charlotte Warren.
  • Prue asked, "Who was that masked man?" to her sisters. Phoebe asked the same question in Season 5 episode Witches in Tights.
  • This episode shows that Phoebe's true love's name starts with a 'C'. Naturally you would think it was Cole Turner. Instead in the series finale, Phoebe married a Cupid nicknamed Coop. Starts with a 'C'. It is unknown whether the Coop character was named with this in mind, or whether it was just a happy accident. Many say that Cole was Phoebe's true love whilst Coop is her soulmate. Phoebe also dated a man named Clay while she was in New York who reappeared in her life in the episode Feats of Clay. This also plays into the 'C' prophecy.
