All Things Are Possible is the sixth album in the live praise and worship series of Christian Contemporary music by Hillsong.

[edit] Making of the album

All Things Are Possible was recorded live at the Hills Entertainment Centre by Darlene Zschech, Russell Fragar and the Hillsong team.
