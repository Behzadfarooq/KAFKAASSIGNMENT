All That Remains is the third album from heavy metal group Fozzy. This album, unlike the two before it, contains entirely original material. The album, despite selling little over 100,000 copies, was praised by rock magazines like Guitar One. This is the first album produced under Fozzy's own label, Ash Records, named after lead singer Chris Jericho's son.

[edit] Miscellanea

  • "Enemy" was the theme song for WWE's No Way Out 2005.
  • "Enemy" was also used in a video package to hype TNA Wrestling and the TNA Pay-Per-View event "Bound for Glory 2006."
  • Another version of All That Remains was released in 2006. This album has a different sound & is labeled alternative in iTunes.
