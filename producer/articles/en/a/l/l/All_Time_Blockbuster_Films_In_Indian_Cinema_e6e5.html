There has been a total of fourteen All Time Blockbuster films in the Hindi/Urdu film industry of Indian Cinema (popularly known as Bollywood) as of October 19, 2006.


[edit] List of fourteen all time blockbuster films

  • Sholay- 1975
  • Mughal-E-Aazam- 1960
  • Mother India- 1957
  • Hum Aapke Hain Koun- 1994
  • Dilwale Dulhaniya Le Jayenge- 1995
  • Gadar Ek Prem Katha- 2001
  • Raja Hindustani- 1996
  • Kuch Kuch Hota Hai- 1998
  • Maine Pyar Kiya- 1989
  • Kismet- 1943
  • Jai Santoshi Maa- 1975
  • Rattan- 1944
  • Ram Teri Ganga Maili- 1985


This list was compiled on 19th October, 2006 based on data from BoxOfficeIndia.Com [1]
