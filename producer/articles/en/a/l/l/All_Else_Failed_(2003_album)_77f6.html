All Else Failed is a 2003 album by the metalcore band Zao, released on February 19, 2003 by Solid State/Tooth & Nail/EMI Records. It features newly recorded versions of eight of ten tracks from their original titled debut album, All Else Failed (1995). The recording was done during the same session as Parade of Chaos. The 2003 album was recorded with a different line-up and can be generically placed as metalcore, where the 1995 album was more hardcore punk.
