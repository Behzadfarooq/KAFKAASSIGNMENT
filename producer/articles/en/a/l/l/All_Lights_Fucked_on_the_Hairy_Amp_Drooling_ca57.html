All Lights Fucked on the Hairy Amp Drooling is the earliest release from Godspeed You Black Emperor!,[1] a Canadian band. It was published on cassette in December 1994 and limited to 33 copies.[1] At this point, the group consisted only of Efrim Menuck, Mauro Pezzente, and Mike Moya. This release is considered very hard, if not impossible, to find.
