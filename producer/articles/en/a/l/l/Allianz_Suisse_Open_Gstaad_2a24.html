Allianz Suisse Open Gstaad

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Allianz Suisse Open Gstaad is a tennis tournament held in Gstaad, Switzerland. Held since 1974, the tournament is played on outdoor clay courts and is a part of the ATP Tour schedule.
