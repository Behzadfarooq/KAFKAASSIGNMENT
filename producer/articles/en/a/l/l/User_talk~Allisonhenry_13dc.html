[edit] PETA

Hi Allison, regarding the use of coporate logos on the PETA page, please read WP:L and the fair use rationale therein. For them to qualify they must "illustrate the organization, item, or event in question" and "other uses of this image, on Wikipedia or elsewhere, may be copyright infringement. (i.e. the PETA logo is suitable for the PETA article and the KFC logo is suitable for the KFC article, but not vice versa.) Moreover, the guideline makes a specific point of warning against use in "contexts which are, taken as a whole, strongly negative toward the company". I'm pretty sure this qualifies. Please stop replacing these logos in the page without justification in policy. Thank you. Rockpocket 02:56, 25 June 2006 (UTC)
