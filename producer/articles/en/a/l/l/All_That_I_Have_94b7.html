All That I Have
Studio album by Chi Rho Wake Forest Christian Male A Cappella Ensemble
Released 1996
Recorded 1997
Genre A Cappella
Length 43:48
Producer(s) Chi Rho Wake Forest Christian Male A Cappella Ensemble

All That I Have is an album recorded in 1997 by Chi Rho, and features songs with solos and complimentary harmonies. This CD features more traditional Christian Music like Holy, Holy, Holy, and Salvation Belongeth To Our God.
