All I Really Want to Do was Cher's 1965 debut album. The album, released soon after Sonny and Cher stormed into the music charts with "I Got You Babe", peaked at #16 in the U.S. and #6 in the UK. It was released by Imperial Records, an imprint of Liberty Records.
