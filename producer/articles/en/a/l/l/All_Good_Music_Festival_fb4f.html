All Good Music Festival

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The All Good Music Festival and Camp Out is an annual weekend-long event in Preston County, West Virginia full of renowned jam bands, many tents, fun activities, and good people. The 10th Annual festival was held July 14-16, 2006 on Marvin's Mountaintop in Masontown, West Virginia. The lineup included artists such as The Black Crowes, Trey Anastasio & Mike Gordon with The Benevento/Russo Duo, John Medeski & The Itch, Robert Randolph & the Family Band, Ween, Grace Potter, Les Claypool, The Greyboy Allstars, The Disco Biscuits, Aquarium Rescue Unit, Galactic, Donavon Frankenreiter, Railroad Earth, Umphrey's McGee, and Xavier Rudd.

All Good has always provided jam band fans a great time. Previous lineups have included Medeski, Martin & Wood, Keller Williams, The Flaming Lips, and Michael Franti with Spearhead.
