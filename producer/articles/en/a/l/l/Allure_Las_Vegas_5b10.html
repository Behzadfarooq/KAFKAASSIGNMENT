Allure Las Vegas

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Allure is a condo project located in Las Vegas, Nevada on the north side of Sahara Ave just east of the Las Vegas Strip. Allure Condominiums will have 428 units and will offer 15 floor plans, with studios, suites and penthouse apartments. One-, two- and three-bedroom layouts are available.

The condo project will have two towers, with the first one , a 41 story tower, is slated to open in early 2007. It is rumored to include a small casino in the second tower, which will be taller than the first tower.

[edit] Features

  • 15 Floor Plans from 664 to 4,218 Square-Feet
  • 24-Hour Doorman
  • Valet and Concierge
  • Private Outdoor Pool Area Surrounded by Private Cabanas
  • Private Wine Cellar
  • Club Room with Catering Kitchen
  • State-of-the-Art Fitness Facility
  • Business Center with Wi-Fi
  • Private Library
  • Media Room with Conference Facilities
