All About Us was a comedy-drama series that on TNBC, it focused on the development of four teenage girls who live and attend high school in Chicago. In the show, the girls' divergent talents, perspectives, and family experiences become the platform for illustrating alternative approaches to understanding and solving problems. The show was cancelled after one season. It was produced by Peter Engel Productions.

[edit] Trivia

  • Alecia Elliott was in fact an up and coming country music star (at the time, she had released one album "I'm Diggin It"). She put her country music career aside, to star in All About Us.
