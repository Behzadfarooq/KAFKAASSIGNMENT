All The Stuff (And More)-Vol 1
Studio album by The Huntingtons
Released 1998

All The Stuff (And More)-Vol. 1 is an album by the Huntingtons released in 1998 on the Burnt Toast Vinyl label.

[edit] Album Info

Re-release of the band's debut disc, Sweet Sixteen, plus 10 bonus tracks.

Executive Producers: Scott Hatch and the Huntingtons
Mastered by Kraynight Productions, Inc.

[edit] Band Lineup

Chris Turver: Vocals/Bass
Cliffy Huntington: Guitar/Vocals
Mikee Huntington: Drums
