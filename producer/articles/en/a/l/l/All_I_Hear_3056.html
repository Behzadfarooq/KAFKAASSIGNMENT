"All I Hear" was the third single from Kate Alexa, released in March 2006. This single is from her debut album, Broken & Beautiful. There are two different versions of the single, one of them is the original and the other is the enhanced limited edition which has two extra tracks. This is her most successful single to date.

[edit] Original Single Track Listing

  1. All I Hear

[edit] Enhanced Limited Edition Track Listing

  1. All I Hear
  2. My Day Will Come (Remix)
  3. On Tour With Jesse McCartney (Computer Video)
