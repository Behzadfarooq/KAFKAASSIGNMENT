"All Ablaze" is an original single from Ian Brown's compilation album, The Greatest. Released on September 5, 2005, it placed #20 on the UK Singles Chart. The song dabbles with Middle Eastern beats and low-fi electronica. Another version of the song appears on Brown's compilation album, The Greatest with some changes in the instrumental arrangement.
