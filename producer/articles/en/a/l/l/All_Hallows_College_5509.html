All Hallows College is a Roman Catholic college located in Drumcondra, Dublin, Ireland. All Hallows is one of six linked colleges of Dublin City University, meaning that the college's degrees are validated and accredited by the university. The college was in founded in 1842 and since 1892 has been under the direction of Vincentians. It offers courses mainly in subjects aligned to religion including theology, and enrolls approximately 700 students. Its current president is Mark Noonan.
