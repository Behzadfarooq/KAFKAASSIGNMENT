All Eyes on The Shins is a video project by Current TV documenting The Shins live performance at the Austin City Limits Festival on September 16, 2006 using video captured by thousands of fans using their own digital cameras and cell phones. The band and production crew compiled hundreds of user generated video clips and then used them to produce an audience shot concert film for air on television. Nearly 200 fan clips were uploaded and used in the production, making it the largest known concert film shot on mobile video.
