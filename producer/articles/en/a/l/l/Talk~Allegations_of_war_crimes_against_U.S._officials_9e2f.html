[edit] how does one deal with non mainstream information

many posters have objected on the grounds that only biased ("left wing") sources are cited. But one can easily imagine circumstances where only marginal sources provide the truth, eg samizdat in the USSR. It is not that hard to imagine that (a) US officials are guilty of war crimes; (b) a majority consensus refuses to recognize that fact; (c) therefore, only "marginal" sources are available. Rather then getting on a jingoistic high horse about this, it is better to realize that all govt's by their very nature, frequently are responsible for acts that could be called war crimes; what distinguishes good and bad govts is the effort they make to atone for thier sins.
