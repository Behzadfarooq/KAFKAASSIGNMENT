"All Eyes on Me" is the 2005 promo single from the American R&B singer LeToya Luckett.

This is seen as LeToya's "Original" first single, as this is the first track that showcased her as a solo artist. There are now currently 4 versions of this song. The first, original version, is a demo recording that leaked very early after recording. The second is a mastered version of the original demo recording. The third version features Paul Wall & Slim Thug on guest rapping vocals, and the final Album version features just Paul Wall on guest rapping vocals. This version has also been slightly shorted from the original.
