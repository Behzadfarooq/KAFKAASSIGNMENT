Allison Keith is an actor and voice actor who is best-known for her English-dubbing work with ADV Films on such anime movies and television series as Neon Genesis Evangelion, in which she voiced the character Misato Katsuragi. Keith was introduced to voice acting by Amanda Winn Lee. She has a Bachelor's degree in musical theatre from the University of Houston, and a teacher's certification. She currently teaches 4th grade at Mission West Elementary School in the Fort Bend Independent School District, in addition to her voice acting roles.

As a student at Clear Brook High School in Friendswood, Texas, Keith was known as a defensive standout, but later gave up basketball for acting.
