Allyn Condon

Allyn Condon (born August 24, 1974) is an English sprinter, the 1998 European champion in 4 x 100 metres relay.

[edit] Personal bests

  • 100 metres - 10.21 (1999)
  • 200 metres - 20.63 (1997)

[edit] Achievements

Year Tournament Venue Result Extra
1998 European Indoor Championships Valencia, Spain 3rd 200 metres
European Championships Budapest, Hungary 1st 4x100m relay
1999 World Indoor Championships Maebashi, Japan 3rd 4x100m relay
