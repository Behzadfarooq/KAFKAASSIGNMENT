Allandale is a former railway station in the Maitland local government area. It is part of CityRail's outer suburban network, on the Main North Line branch of the Hunter Line. It was 'Temporarily' closed in 2002 due to the nearby bridge construction, but permanently closed on 9 September, 2005, after railway works by the Australian Rail Track Corporation.
