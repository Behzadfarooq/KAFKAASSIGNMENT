The Alliance for Progress and Justice/Jëf-Jël (Alliance pour le Progrès et la Justice/Jëf-Jël) is a political party in Senegal. At the last legislative elections on 29 April 2001, the party won 0.8% of the popular vote and 1 out of 120 seats.
