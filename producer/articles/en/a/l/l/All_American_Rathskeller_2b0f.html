The All American Rathskeller[1] is a bar in State College, PA. The "Rathskeller" as its called for short. Opened its doors in 1933. It is famous for its Case Race, which started in 1982, and for a very special patron Timothy Leary who purchased a tabletop there in 1991.

[edit] Pony Case Race

Each year, the 'Skeller holds an annual case race. Participants buy cases of Rolling Rock beer with the goal of consuming the most beer in one day. This event has earned its way into the The Guinness Book of World Records.[2]

[edit] Timothy Leary

In 1991, American writer and psychologist Timothy Leary purchased a table top from the 'Skellar for one dollar. He is quoted as saying, "I believe that the tables at "the Rathskeller" are wonderful pop-art iconic wood-sculpture which express the consciousness for the Pennsylvanians who have celebrated the ancient ceremonies in your temple."
