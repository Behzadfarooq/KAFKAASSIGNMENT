All Your Life is the second independent cassette from American Christian rock band Resurrection Band, released in late 1974.

[edit] Recording history

Following on the heels of Music to Raise the Dead, All Your Life is an acoustic collection of folk-influenced music that Resurrection Band would play in more conservative venues like nursing homes and churches, who would otherwise be unreceptive to the borderlline heavy metal that Resurrection Band otherwise specialized in.

The cassette has never been re-released, which makes it a highly prized and extremely hard to find collector's item.
