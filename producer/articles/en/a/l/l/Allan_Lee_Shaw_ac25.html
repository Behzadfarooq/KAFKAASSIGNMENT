Allan Lee Shaw

Allan Lee Shaw is a long time friend and collaborator of Rat Scabies (The Damned and Nosferatu) and Brian James (The Lords of the New Church, The Damned, and solo work). His writing is best known on The Damned album Not of This Earth. Allan Lee Shaw is to some an instigator who pulled The Damned and Rat Scabies apart, and to some a great songwriter who put The Damned on the map of pop culture in the 1990s.
