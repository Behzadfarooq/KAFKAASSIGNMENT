"All the Way from Memphis" is a single released by Mott the Hoople. The song tells a story about a rock n' roller who's guitar is shipped to "the Orioles" instead of Memphis. The musician gets half-way there before he realizes his instrument is missing and takes a month to track it down. When he gets the guitar back, he is scolded by a stranger for being neglectful and self-centered.

It was covered by Brian May on his 1998 album Another World
