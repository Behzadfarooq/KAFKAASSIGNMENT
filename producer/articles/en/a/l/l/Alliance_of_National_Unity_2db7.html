Alliance of National Unity(Ukrainian: Коаліції національної єдності) is the name of the governing coalition of the Party of Regions, the Communist Party and the Socialist Party in Ukraine after the Ukrainian parliamentary election, 2006. Until 24 March 2007, it was known as Anti-Crisis Alliance (Ukrainian: Антикризова коаліція).[1]

  • Party of Regons (186 - all)
  • Commmunist Party of Ukraine (21 - all)
  • Socialist Party of Ukraine 31
  • Yulia Tymoshenko Electoral Bloc 6
  • Our Ukraine 5

On April 6, 2007 coalition members count has reduced to 238 members.[2]
