Allison Leigh Alderson DeMarcus is a beauty queen from Nashville, Tennessee who has competed in the Miss Teen USA, Miss USA and Miss America pageants. She is considered a Triple Crown winner because she has held state titles in each system. Alderson is married to Jay DeMarcus, a bassist in the band Rascal Flatts.[1]

[edit] Pageantry

Alderson won her first major pageant title in 1994 when she was crowned Miss Tennessee Teen USA. She went on to compete in the Miss Teen USA 1994 pageant held in Biloxi, Mississippi in August 1994. Alderson placed as the highest of the top twelve after the preliminary competition, second during the finals interview competition, third in finals swimsuit and seventh in evening gown which ranked her fourth going in to the final interview round, eventually finishing among the top six. Later that year she crowned Lynnette Cole as the new Miss Tennessee Teen USA; Cole would later win the Miss Tennessee USA and Miss USA 2000 titles.

In 1999, Alderson won the Miss Memphis local title and then the Miss Tennessee state title for the Miss America pageant.[2][3] She represented Tennessee in the Miss America 2000 pageant held in Atlantic City, New Jersey in September 1999. Alderson did not place in the pageant, but won the Waterford Crystal Award for Business Management and Marketing.

In 2001, Alderson won the Miss Tennessee USA title on her first attempt, becoming one of the first women to win state titles in all three systems. She was the second former Miss Tennessee Teen USA and the third Miss Tennessee to win the Miss Tennessee USA title. Her sister titleholder, Miss Tennessee Teen USA 2002, was Rachel Smith, who would later hold the Miss Tennessee USA 2007 title. Alderson competed in the Miss USA 2002 pageant broadcast live from Gary, Indiana in March 2002, but did not place. The pageant was won by Shauntay Hinton of the District of Columbia. She passed on her title in late 2002, to Beth Hood, who had also succeeded her as Miss Tennessee. The following year Hood crowned Stephanie Culberson as Miss Tennessee USA 2004; she had crowned Culberson Miss Tennessee. This is the first time that consecutive three Miss America state titleholders have held the same Miss USA state title in succession. Alderson was one of two women who earned Triple Crown winner status in 2002, for holding state titles in the Miss Teen USA, Miss USA and Miss America systems. The other was Kelly Lloyd of Indiana, who competed against Alderson at both Miss America and Miss USA.

[edit] Biography

Alderson graduated cum laude with a degree in business administration. from Rhodes College in 1999, where she served as president of the Chi Omega sorority. As part of the Bryce Harlow Institute of Business and Government Affairs she has also studied at Georgetown University in Washington, DC.[4] Alderson's sister Amy held the Miss District of Columbia USA 1999 title, at the same time that Alderson was Miss Tennessee.

On May 15, 2004, Alderson married Jay DeMarcus, drummer in the band Rascal Flatts. She is a regular host and co-host of the Miss Tennessee pageant. In January, Allison served as the Roving Reporter for the 2007 Miss America Pageant. Mario Lopez served as host. She was CMT's pageant correspondent for the Miss America pageant and filmed a behind-the-scenes look for CMT Insider. Allison has also served as guest host of CMT Insider, as well as serving as "Style Judge" for CMT's Karaoke Dokey. She will be hosting the Red Carpet Pre-Show for the CMT Music Awards live from Nashville, Tennessee on April 16th.
