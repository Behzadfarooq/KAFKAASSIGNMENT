The Tenth Question: All or Nothing (Japanese: 出たとこ勝負！踏ん張りどころの10問目, - Deta-toko shōbu! funbari dokoro no jū monme) is episode 25 of the anime series Naruto. The episode's storyline is from the Chūnin Exam manga arc.

[edit] Plot

However, the 10th Question is not as simple as it appears. Ibiki begins to explain the specific rules that apply to this question, and does so despite all of the interruptions. Firstly, the entrants must choose whether or not they will take the exam, if they choose not to, their points will be changed to 0, and they will fail along with their two teammates. Secondly, if they do decide to take it, and answer wrongly, they will lose their right to ever take the Chuunin exam again. Kiba interrupts and shouts that there are people who have taken the exam before, Ibiki laughs and tells them they were unlucky because this year it is his rules. Alternatively, they can choose not to take the tenth question, and, along with their team, try and take the exam again next year.

Everyone begins to panic, thinking both of the options are bad because they don't want to risk disappointing their teammates or being a Genin forever. Naruto contemplates quitting but does not decide, while around him people raise their hands and quit... their numbers are called out and they leave with their team. Sakura makes a decision: although she is confident she can answer it, she thinks about Naruto and decides she doesn't want to see his dream crushed. She begins to raise her hand with a subtle smile on her face. However, she stops and stutters as she sees Naruto's hand being raised. Sasuke, Hinata and Sakura are all shocked, but as they think he is quitting, he slams his hand down onto the table and shouts out that he will not run.

Ibiki, who knows the human mind completely, realizes that Naruto has blasted away everyone's worries. Everyone smiles at Naruto who is determined to take the 10th Question, even if he has to stay a Genin forever. After this, no one else quits. Ibiki says that there is no point waiting any longer.

Ibiki congratulates them on completing the 1st test! The other true purpose of the exam was to test their will. The exam ends with 78 participants remaining... more than Ibiki expected.

Then at that moment the next test proctor Anko Mitarashi jumps through the window and inroduces herself as the next test proctor(interupting Ibiki) She then makes a remark about the large amount of candidates and that Ibiki was getting soft. She then says that by the time she through with them more then half will be eliminated which cause Sakura to worry.

Later, after everyone has left, Ibiki gathers up the test papers. He sees that Naruto's is completely blank. He chuckles to himself, thinking that Naruto is an "interesting guy". The next morning, the genin gather outside the gates to the 44th Battle Training Zone, a.k.a. "The Forest of Death". The episode ends.


Note: It is unknown if this was intentional or not, but a genin shown in a few shots looks like Gepetto from Pinnochio.
