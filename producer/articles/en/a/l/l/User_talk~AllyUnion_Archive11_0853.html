[edit] Yet another bot thing

Hi there! I had some thoughts about clarifying the Wikipedia namespace, that might be done by bot. I would like your opinion on whether it's a good idea... first, it may be useful to move all pages in Category:WikiProjects into Category:Inactive WikiProjects if they haven't been edited for a set period of time, say two months. Second, it may be useful to put something like Template:inactive or Template:historical on any Wikipedia namespace page that hasn't been edited for a lengthy period of time, say half a year. Either can of course easily be undone if a page once more becomes active. What do you think? Good idea? Bad idea? Needs more discussion? Yours, Radiant_* 09:04, Apr 25, 2005 (UTC)

The pywikipedia framework doesn't have anything encoded for moving stuff at the moment. -- AllyUnion (talk) 03:55, 27 Apr 2005 (UTC)
