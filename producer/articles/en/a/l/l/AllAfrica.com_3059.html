AllAfrica.com is a website that aggregates news produced primarily on the African continent on all areas of African life, politics, issues and culture. It is available in both English and French and produced by AllAfrica Global Media, which has offices in Cape Town, Lagos, Monrovia, and Washington, DC. AllAfrica is the successor to the African News Service.
