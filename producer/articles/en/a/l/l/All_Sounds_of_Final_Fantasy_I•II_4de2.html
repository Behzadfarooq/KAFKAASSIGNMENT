All Sounds of Final Fantasy I•II is a soundtrack album of video game music from the computer role-playing game Final Fantasy I and Final Fantasy II, produced by Square Co., Ltd. for the Famicom (known internationally as the Nintendo Entertainment System). The album contains the musical tracks from the game, composed, arranged, produced and performed by Nobuo Uematsu. It was released on February 28, 1989. Tracks 1 and 49 are arranged versions of Final Fantasy themes from Final Fantasy I and Final Fantasy II, tracks 2-21 are from Final Fantasy I and 22-48 are from Final Fantasy II.
