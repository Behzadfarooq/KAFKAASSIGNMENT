Allison L. Sumrall-Hunt is an American voice actress working with ADV Films, known for her roles in the English-language dubs of anime series. She is generally credited simply as Allison Sumrall.

She is a veteran of the Masquerade Theatre in Houston, Texas, and received her education in Theatre from the University of Houston 1.

[edit] Notable roles

  • Hiromi Fujimori - Angelic Layer
  • Kagura - Azumanga Daioh
  • Yuri - Dirty Pair (replacing Jessica Calvello when she lost her voice)
  • Shoko Saibara - Kita e ~Diamond Dust Drops~
  • Rinna Charat - Panyo Panyo Di Gi Charat
  • Saori - Saint Seiya
  • Saito - Elfen Lied
  • Mami Honda - Super GALS! Kotobuki Ran
  • Asako Nakamura - Ushio & Tora
  • Quanzitta Marison - Madlax
  • Eri Kagurazaka, Shiroi Kudo - Full Metal Panic!
  • Eri Kagurazaka, Kozue Nishino - Full Metal Panic? Fumoffu
  • Ayako Asahina - Magical Shopping Arcade Abenobashi
  • Serina - Najica Blitz Tactics
  • Sara - suspiria
