All Nations University
Motto Equipped for every good work
Established October 2005
Type Private
President Rev. Dr. Samuel Donkor
Location Koforidua, Eastern Region, Ghana
Website www.allnationsuniversity.anida.org


The All Nations University was founded by Rev. Dr. Samuel Donkor in Ghana. It began with 37 students in October 2002 and expanded to over 500 students in January 2005. [1] It became an accredited university in Ghana in October 2005.[1][2] The university is affiliated to the Karunya Institute of Technology and Sciences[3]
