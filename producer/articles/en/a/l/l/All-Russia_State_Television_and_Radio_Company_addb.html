The All-Russia State Television and Radio Company, or VGTRK (Russian: Всероссийская государственная телевизионная и радиовещательная компания, ВГТРК is a state-owned Russian television and radio company founded in 1990 which owns the Russia TV Channel. In 1998 it was transformed into a holding including the Russia TV Channel, RIA Novosti, Mayak Radio, RTR Planet TV Channel, Radio of Russia Broadcasting Company, Culture TV Channel, Culture Radio, Sport TV Channel and Russia State Intenet Channel.

[edit] Chairpersons

  • Oleg Poptsov (1990—1996)
  • Eduard Sagalayev (1996—1997)
  • Nikolay Svanidze (1997—1998)
  • Mikhail Shvydkoi (1998—2000)
  • Oleg Dobrodeev (since 2000)
