"All for You" is Namie Amuro's 26th solo single under the Avex Trax label. Released in July 2004, the song was a smash hit becoming the first of three consecutive singles to sell over 100,000 copies. "All for You" has been certified gold for shipments of over 100,000 by the Recording Industry Association of Japan.[1]
