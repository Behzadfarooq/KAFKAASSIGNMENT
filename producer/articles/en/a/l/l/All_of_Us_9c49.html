All of Us is a situation comedy (sitcom) airing on The CW. The show involves a blended family.

Robert James (Duane Martin) is a California-based TV entertainment reporter with a young son, Bobby Jr. (Khamani Griffin). He is divorced from his ex-wife Neesee James (LisaRaye McCoy) and now dealing with Neesee having to move back in after her apartment burned down. This only compounds the pain of dealing with being single again after being left by his former fiancée Tia Jewel (Elise Neal). Robert and Neesee receive support from Dirk Black (Tony Rock), Robert's best friend and co-host of his new show.

All of Us was created and executive produced by Jada Pinkett Smith, Betsy Borns, and Will Smith and is produced by Warner Bros. Television and Overbrook Productions (owned and operated by Will Smith and Jada Pinkett Smith). It premiered on UPN September 19, 2003. It premiered on the CW network, the successor to UPN and the WB, October 1 at 7:30PM/6:30C. All of Us is now moved from Sundays to its original Monday nights at 8:30PM/7:30C on the new CW.

[edit] Tia/Elise Neal's departure

The real reason why Tia's character played by the actress Elise Neal, left the show "All of Us" is because, despite the fact that the show was suffering financially, the actress Lisa Raye who plays the character "Neesee" was getting paid about $20,000 per week. ( Lisa Raye stated this during an interview that she had with Sister 2 Sister magazine in the December 2003 issue) Elise was barely getting paid over 5,000 per week. Elise expressed her concern with the producers of the show about the large pay scale difference between her and Lisa Raye. Elise stated that she has more acting experience than Lisa Raye through countless movies, plays, and T.V. shows. She couldn't understand why Lisa was still getting paid that amount of money, despite the financial struggles of the show.The producers told Elise that because Lisa Raye was under contract to receive that amount of money per week on the show, that they simply couldn't pay Elise the same amount of money to match the amount that Lisa is getting. No compromise was reached, and Elise left the show.

[edit] Performance

All of Us is performing poorly, getting 2.74 million viewers average per week.[citation needed] It is the fourth most watched sitcom (out of five excluding the prime time encores of Reba) on The CW. All of Us is #126 in the ratings, surpassing The Game, America's Next Top Model (encore presentation) and the cancelled Runaway. The show is beaten by the CW sitcoms Girlfriends, Everybody Hates Chris and Reba and all of the CW drama and reality series excluding encores.

  • The show was cancelled in March 2006, but thanks to fans and the star power of the shows executive producers Will Smith and Jada Pinkett Smith and co-star Tony Rock's brother Chris Rock, the show was "uncancelled" and given the coveted timeslot behind the critic darling Everybody Hates Chris on the CW's inaugural first schedule. Similarly, 7th Heaven was brought back. [1]
