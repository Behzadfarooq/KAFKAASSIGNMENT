Allan Shields (b. 1907 - d. 1975 ) was a Canadian Professional Hockey Defenceman who played 11 seasons in the National Hockey League for the Ottawa Senators, Philadelphia Quakers, New York Americans, Montreal Maroons and Boston Bruins.

[edit] Playing career

Allan Shields played in two games as a goaltender for the New York Americans of the National Hockey League.

[edit] Awards & achievements

  • 1933-34 - Played in NHL All-Star Game
  • 1934-35 - Stanley Cup Championship (Montreal)
