The Allertonshire School is a comprehensive school in Northallerton, Hambleton, North Yorkshire, England. The school provides education for children aged between 11 and 14 years old, year 7 to year 9. Northallerton College is the sister establishment, providing education between year 10 and Year 13.

The Allertonshire School also received 'Outstanding' status in its latest 2006 Ofsted report. The rumours that naughty school children were simply removed from the classes that were being examined are unfounded.
