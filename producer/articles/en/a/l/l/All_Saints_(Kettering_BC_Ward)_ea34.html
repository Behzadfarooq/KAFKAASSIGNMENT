Kettering Borough within Northamptonshire
Northamptonshire within England

All Saints Ward is a 2-member ward within Kettering Borough Council and is commonly regarded as a marginal ward between Labour and the Conservatives. The ward was last fought at Borough Council level in the 2003 local council elections, in which both seats were won by the Conservatives.

The current councillors are Cllr. Greg Titcombe and Cllr. Chris Smith-Haynes.
