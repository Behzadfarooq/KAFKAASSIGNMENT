"All This Time" was the first single off of Tiffany's second album, "Hold an Old Friend's Hand". In the UK it served as the second single to "Radio Romance".

[edit] Song information

In 1988 Tiffany was unstoppable. Her chart reign continued by the end of the year. The first single to her sophomore album, "All This Time" was no exception. It shot up on the U.S. charts to #6. This was Tiffany's fourth top-ten hit, it was also her last. The single was also a top-ten hit on the Adult Contemporary Billboard chart. In the UK the single wasn't as successful it barely made a dent in the top 50. The lack of success with the single in the UK is possible since it was the second single off the album and not the first over in the UK.

The music video also aired heavily on MTV like her previous videos the accompanied the releases of her singles.

The b-side was "Can't Stop A Heartbeat" without a doubt her most popular b-side among fans. The song has been released later on the Japanese remix EP, "I Saw Him Standing There". It was also released on one of her greatest hits compilations.
