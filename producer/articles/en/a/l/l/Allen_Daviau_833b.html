Allen Daviau

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Allen Daviau (born June 14, 1942 in New Orleans) is a five-time Academy Award nominated American cinematographer.

[edit] Selected Filmography

  • Amblin' (1968)
  • Close Encounters of the Third Kind (1977) (Gobi desert sequence)
  • E.T. the Extra-Terrestrial (1982)
  • Indiana Jones and the Temple of Doom (1984) (second unit cinematographer)
  • The Color Purple (1985)
  • Empire of the Sun (1987)
  • Avalon (1990)
  • Bugsy (1991)
  • Fearless (1993)
  • The Astronaut's Wife (1998)
  • Hearts in Atlantis (1998)
  • Van Helsing (2003)
