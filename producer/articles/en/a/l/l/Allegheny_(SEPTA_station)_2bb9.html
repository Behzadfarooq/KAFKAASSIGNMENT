Allegheny (SEPTA station)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Allegheny is a SEPTA Regional Rail station located along the R6 Norristown line located at 22nd Street and Allegheny Avenue in the Swampoodle neighborhood of North Philadelphia. [1] Allegheny Station is the first station along SEPTA's R6 Norristown line not to be shared with any other line. In FY 2005, Allegheny Station had an average of 66 average weekday boardings and 97 average weekday alightings. [2] In addition to the R6 Regional Rail line, Allegheny Station is also served by SEPTA bus routes 33[3] and 60. [4].
