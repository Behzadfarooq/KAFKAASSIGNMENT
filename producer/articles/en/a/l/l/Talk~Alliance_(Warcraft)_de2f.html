[edit] Major Revamp

I changed this article to show that the Allience is no longer "good" perse. As the World of Warcraft manual says "At one time in Azeroth's past, the Horde was a force of evil, and the Alliance was a bastion of good. However, in today's war-torn Azeroth, such black and white distinctions are gone. Both factions are simply fighting to preserve their way of life in the wake of the Chaos War." (p.14). Also added races that are also included in the alliance but left out here. I added this page to Category:Warcraft additionally.
