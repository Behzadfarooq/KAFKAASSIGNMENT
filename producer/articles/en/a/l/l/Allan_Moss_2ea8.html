Allan Moss AO is currently the Managing Director/CEO of Macquarie Bank Ltd. Prior to this Mr Moss was a director of Hill Samuel Australia and led the team responsible for preparing the submission to the Australian Government to form Macquarie Bank in 1983. He held various positions within Macquarie Bank before becoming Managing Director/CEO in 1993. He has been a director of the company since June 1989.

He has a Bachelor of Arts with the Bachelor of Laws (Hons) from the University of Sydney and an MBA from Harvard.
