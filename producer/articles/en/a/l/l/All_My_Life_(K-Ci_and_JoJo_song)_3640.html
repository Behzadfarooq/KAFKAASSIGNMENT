"All My Life" is a R&B love ballad recorded by Jodeci members (and brothers) K-Ci and JoJo. It was released as a single from the duo's album Love Always.

This was K-Ci and JoJo's second number-one song, following 2Pac's "How Do U Want It", on which they were credited as featured vocalists. "All My Life" had earned enough airplay to debut on the U.S. Billboard Hot 100 at number fifteen, once the single was released to retail stores in March 1998. The following week it jumped to number one and stayed there for three weeks.

Fantasia Barrino performed her rendition of this song in the American Idol finale, receiving mixed reviews from the judges.

Panic! at the Disco sometimes perform the song in concert.
