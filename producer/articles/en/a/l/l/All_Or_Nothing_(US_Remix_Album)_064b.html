Due to the significant differences between the original Milli Vanilli debut album, All or Nothing and the US-only Girl You Know It's True release, the producer, Frank Farian, decided to repackage these previously unreleased songs in a remix album.

The unreleased in Europe songs are "Blame It On The Rain", "More Than You'll Ever Know", "Take It As It Comes", "It's Your Thing". It also includes the US short / single version of "Girl You Know It's True". Most of the songs on this album are completely remixed from the version included in the US debut album.

This album is not to be confused with the US-only "Remix Album", released one year later in 1990.
