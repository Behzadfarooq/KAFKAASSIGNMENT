Allan Goes born (April 20, 1971 in Rio de Janeiro, Brazil) is a professional mixed martial arts (MMA) fighter. He is currently fighting for the Seattle Tiger Sharks of the International Fight League.

[edit] Bio

He began training in mixed martial arts more than 12 years ago, to show the world that jiu-jitsu was the best fighting method. Weighing around 200 pounds, Goes has utilized those techniques, many of which he developed himself after 13 years of jiu-jitsu training, to bring down larger opponents.

Goes, a fifth-degree jiu-jitsu Black Belt and eight-time Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu champion, has won over 300 jiu-jitsu bouts in his career by his count, and lost just once.

Allan also runs the Allan Goes Jiu-Jitsu Systems in Aliso Viejo, California.
