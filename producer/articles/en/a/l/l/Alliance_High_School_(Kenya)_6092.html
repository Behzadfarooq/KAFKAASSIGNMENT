Alliance High School established in March 1926, is a school in Kenya. It was the first school in Kenya to offer secondary school education to Africans.

The school is situated in Kikuyu, about 20 kilometres (12.42742 miles) from Nairobi's central business district. As of 2005 the school top ranked in the country based on the results of the Kenya Certificate of Secondary Education [1]. In 2006 its rank dropped slightly to became third best high school in Kenya [2].

The school is also known as "BUSH" because of the dense vegetation that used to surround the school.
