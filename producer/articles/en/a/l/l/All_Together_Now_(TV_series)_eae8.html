All Together Now was an Australian television situation comedy programme that was broadcast on the Nine Network from 1991 to 1993.

[edit] Cast

  • Jon English as "Bobby Rivers"
  • Rebecca Gibney as "Tracy Lawson" (Eps 1-86)
  • Steven Jacobs as "Thomas Summers"
  • Jane Hall as "Anna Summers"
  • Garry Who as "Doug Stevens" (Eps 1-76)
  • Bruno Lucia as "Wayne Lovett"
  • Kerry Armstrong as "Beth Summers" (Eps 85-101)

One-time Rock'n'Roll star Bobby Rivers discovers he has 15-year-old twins when his former lover dies in a plane crash. They and Tracy, the woman who looked after them when their mother was traveling the world, move in with him, but have problems with his way of life; it's the 70s against the 90s!
