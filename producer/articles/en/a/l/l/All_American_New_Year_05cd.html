All American New Year is a annual live New Year's television program on the Fox News Channel.

Airing on New Year's Eve, though the early morning of New Year's Day, the program features a number of different elements, including banter between the show's hosts, on the street reports with people surrounded by people celebrating the New Year, in addition to special celebrity guests.

Each year, the program has had a different group hosting, starting with Steve Doocy and Alisyn Camerota for 2005, Mike Jerrick and Juliet Huddy for 2006, and Bill Hemmer and Julie Banderas for 2007.
