Allen West (born October 17, 1967, Brandon, Florida) is an American guitarist who has been in such bands as Obituary, Six Feet Under and Lowbrow. He is considered to have been one of the first pioneers of death metal in the 1980s[citation needed].

In high school, West formed the band Massacre.West's second band was Xecutioner, which laid down the foundation of Obituary. He is currently back in Obituary after the band's hiatus. He has been interviewed lately on MTV2.

West has one son and resides in Florida.
