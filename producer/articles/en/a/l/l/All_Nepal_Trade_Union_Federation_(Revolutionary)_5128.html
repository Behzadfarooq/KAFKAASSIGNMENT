All Nepal Trade Union Federation (Revolutionary)
Country Nepal
Key people Shalikram Jamarkattel, president

All Nepal Trade Union Federation (Revolutionary) is the trade union wing of the Communist Party of Nepal (Maoist). ANTUF(R) is an underground organisation. CPN(M) often calls for general strikes in the name of ANTUF(R).

Shalik Ram Jamarkattel is the president of ANTUF(R).[1]
