All Out War Volume 1 is the first album from The Regime. Originally Regime group started in 1997 but first release came in 2005. All Out War Volume 1 is collection of US rappers. Members are from everywhere: south side (Houston, TX; Miami, FL), west side (Los Angeles, CA; San Diego, CA; San Francisco Bay Area, CA), east side (Queens, NY), mid-west (Kansas City, MO; Chicago, IL). All instrumentals are made by another rappers and the album actually consists of diss tracks, made for enemies of The Regime members. The Regime is not an actual group like N.W.A., Wu Tang Clan or Three 6 Mafia (all or these three bands have had alots of members during their days), it's more like collection of rappers coming from different states and different music.
