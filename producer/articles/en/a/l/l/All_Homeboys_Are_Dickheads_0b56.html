All Homeboys Are Dickheads was a single from the Machiavelli and the Four Seasons 1995 album that was never officially released and that reason actually shares the Genre B. Goode catalogue number GOO8 with Shut Up - The Footy's On The Radio.

It was also released as a promo with Machines Against the Rage.

[edit] Track list

  1. "All Homeboys Are Dickheads (Remix)"
  2. "All Homeboys Are Dickheads (Extended Remix)"
  3. "All Homeboys Are Dickheads (Album Version)"
  4. "What Nationality Is Les Murray? (Live)" [Collingwood Town Hall, 21/10/95]

[edit] Trivia

Remixes by Josh Abrahams. Cover art by Cath Lindsey.
