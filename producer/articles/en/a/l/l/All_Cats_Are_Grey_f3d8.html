All Cats Are Grey is a song by The Cure.

The song is one of the few with lyrics apparently not written by Robert Smith, with Laurence Tolhurst claiming the credit for the lyrics in 2006.[citation needed] This song is one of the two in the entire history of the band with lyrics not written by Smith, with the song Doubt (also off the Faith album) apparently having lyrics by Simon Gallup.[citation needed]

All Cats Are Grey was first played in late 1980 on the tour for the single Primary. It was played on the Picture tour in 81, and then occasionally on the tours for Disintegration in 1989, and Bloodflowers in 2000. The song is featured during the end credits of Sophia Coppola's 2006 film Marie Antoinette.
