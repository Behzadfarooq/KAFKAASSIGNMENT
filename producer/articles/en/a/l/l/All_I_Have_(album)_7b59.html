All I Have is the debut album by American R&B singer Amerie. It reached number nine on the U.S. Billboard 200, remained in the top twenty for four weeks and on the chart for seventeen weeks. It also earned Amerie a Soul Train Music Award for "Best R&B/Soul or Rap New Artist."
