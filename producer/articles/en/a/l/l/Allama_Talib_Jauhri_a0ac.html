Allama Talib Jauhri

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Allama Talib Jauhari ( Talib Johri or Talib Jauhry) is a prominent and distinguished Pakistani scholar, religious leader, public speaker, poet and philosopher from the Shi'ite sect of Islam who holds the prestigious position of Zakir-e-Sham-e-Ghariban (speaker of the evening of sorrow & ruined) on Pakistan Television for several years and now on GEO TV.

Allama Talib Jauhari.

He has been an outstanding speaker on different themes of Islam for many decades. He has delivered speeches all over the world on Islamic themes particularly with the reference of the Ahl al-Bayt(Household) of Muhammed (May Peace be Upon them). Along with being an extra ordinary sepaker, Allama Talib Jauhri is a prolific writer, philosopher and a poet as well. He successfully relates Islamic ideas with modern political and philosophical thought. His recent book on philosophy Aqliyat-e-Ma'asir is an outstandng work on the history and major themes of philosophy in Urdu.

Allama Talib Jauhari is the son of a Shia scholar Moulana Mohammad Mustafa Jauhar Sahib Qibla, he studied Islamic theology in Najaf, Iraq under some of the biggest Scholars of the time such as Ayatullah al Uzma Sayyid Abul Qasim al Khoei and Shaheed Sayyid Muhammad Baqir as-Sadr and his class Peers include Allamah Sayyid Zeeshan Haider Jawadi Sahib Qibla and Maulana Hameed-ul-Hasan. Apart from giving lectures in Karachi, he also Orates in Dubai and the USA.

He is not only respected among the Shia community in the Indo-Pak Subcontinent but Sunnis also like to attend his lectures due to his Tafseer-e-Quran in a logical way. He is also a promoter of Shia Sunni unity in Pakistan.
