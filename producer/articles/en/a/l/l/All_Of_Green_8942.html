All Of Green are a Canadian reggae rock band from Fredericton, New Brunswick. At this point in their careers, they are thought of as a "live band," as they have not yet released a studio album, and their shows are a huge draw, largely due to the amiable onstage antics of frontman Caleb Crandall. Bottlegged live shows and leaked demo cuts appear frequently on the internet. Topics covered in their lyrics thus far include zombies, Saturday morning cartoons, graphic novelist Alan Moore, and the life of Christ.
