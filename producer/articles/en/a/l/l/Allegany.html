From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Allegany (see also the variants Allegheny and Alleghany) is the name of several places in the United States:

  • Allegany County, New York
  • Allegany (town), Cattaraugus County, New York
  • Allegany (village), Cattaraugus County, New York
  • Allegany Reservation, Cattaraugus County, New York


  • Allegany Township, Pennsylvania


  • Allegany County, Maryland
