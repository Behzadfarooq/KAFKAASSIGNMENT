Alfred Aloysius "Allie" Clark (June 16, 1923 in South Amboy, New Jersey) was a professional Baseball player playing most notably for the New York Yankees. He made his pro debut on August 5, 1947 with the Yankees and played his final game on June 5, 1953 with the Philadelphia Athletics.
