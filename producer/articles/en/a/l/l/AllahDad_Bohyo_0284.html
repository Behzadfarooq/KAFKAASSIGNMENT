[edit] Dr. Allahdad Bohyo

(1934- 1994) born in Ghoghari village of Taluka Kambar, Larkana, Sind, Pakistan. Son of a farmer, Dr. Bohyo was a known writer, educationist, scholar, linguist, poet. Bohyo's family afterward shifted to Pechuha village in Taluka Warah, where he received his Primary Education. He passed his Matriculation from Government High School Larkana, and Intermediate Arts from S.I.C. Hala. He Joined Government service on te 1st of October 1957 after his graduation. He has worked at various posts in the Department of Education. He was working as Director of School Education Mirpurkhas when he died in 1994.

[edit] Education

He had a degree in Agro Technical Education and a degree in Teaching from Sind University. He had a degree in Law also. He had a Master's Degree in English and a Doctorate in Socio-linguistics--a subject of his special study.

[edit] Famous for

He was the first scholar in Pakistan who has studied socio-linguistics and studied Sindhi Language under this discipline. He was know as a writer, during even his school age. He contributed to the college magazine of Sarwary College hala, as a student and then to "Chandea Magazine of Government College Larkana" as a Lecturer in English. He contributed sufficient to "Naeen Zindagi" Sindhi and to "Mehran" of Sindhi Adabi Board. During the dictatorial regime of General Zia-al-haq he pseudonymously contributed articles on free speech and democracy in various Sindhi Magazines.

[edit] Writings

He wrote on language, criticism, art, history, and literature and he is know for his interest in socio-linguistics and criticism. His writings in sociology have also been studies of interest. During his school years he was known as good speaker and a debater.

[edit] Books

Following of his books have been published:

1. Sindhi Boli-e-jo-Samaji Karij (Sindhology). (Doctoral Thesis) 2. Tanqeedoon (Sindhology) (Criticism). 3. Adab-a-jo Fiqri-Muharrik (Sindhi Adeeban Jee Co-operative Society). (Ideas and Literature) 4. The Tales Retold (English short stories) 5. Science of Research (illim-e-Tehqeeq)


[edit] Un-Published Books

6. Education: Issues and Ideas (English) 7. Sindh in the context of Southeast Asia (Sindhi) 8. Haalaat-o-khayalaat (Sindhi, Poetry)

He has written some prefaces to books, but a good number of his articles and writings are yet to be published.

[edit] Why he is in Wikipedia?

Mr. Bohyo devoted his life to Sindhi language and Sindhi education. His contributions also include children's poetry and lessons in the primary classes. His children's poetry is known to all Sindhi children and adults at Sindh, due to its inclusion in textbooks and its popularity. He was perhaps the first person in Asia during his times to do Ph.D work in socio-linguistics with reference to sindhi language. No reference is currently available on the Internet because of the lack of Sindhi literature on the Internet. The purpose of this article is to provide researchers of linguistics references to his writings. To some he may be less notable but it is just because they do not know of him.
