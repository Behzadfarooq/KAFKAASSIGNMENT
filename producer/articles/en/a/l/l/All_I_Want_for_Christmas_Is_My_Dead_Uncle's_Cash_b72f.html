All I Want for Christmas Is My Dead Uncle's Cash is an episode of the U.S. television sitcom Yes, Dear.

[edit] Plot

Spoiler warning: Plot and/or ending details follow.

Jimmy's uncle dies and leaves him $5000. He promises Greg half the money and he buys Christmas presents with the other half. When he goes to collect the money he is told by the lawyer that he must pay taxes on it. Now he doesn't have enough money to pay Greg. Jimmy gets another job at a toy store where Greg bumps into him. Greg talks Jimmy into taking his money back when he tells him how much he helps around the house.
