The Allen & Company Sun Valley Conference is an annual conference hosted and wholly independently funded by private investment firm Allen & Company. The conference has taken place in Sun Valley, Idaho for one week each July since 1983. The conference typically features business leaders, political figures, and major figures in the philanthropic and cultural spheres. Previous conference guests have included Bill and Melinda Gates, Warren and Susan Buffet, BET founder Robert Johnson, Time Warner Chairman Richard Parsons, Google founders Larry Page and Sergey Brin, Katherine Graham of The Washington Post, Diane Sawyer, InterActiveCorp Chairman Barry Diller, and eBay CEO Meg Whitman.

Allen & Company is a private investment firm formed in 1922, located in New York. Allen & Company has advised, helped found and/or invested in numerous companies including BET, InterActiveCorp, Oxygen Media, Discovery Communications, News Corporation, The Coca-Cola Corporation and Google.
