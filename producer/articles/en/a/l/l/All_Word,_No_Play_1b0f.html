All Word, No Play is a 12" vinyl single released by Non-Prophets on Emerge Records in 200. Non-Prophets are Joe Beats and Sage Francis

[edit] Track listing

Side A

  1. "All Word No Play"
  2. "Majority Rule (Take It Back)"
  3. "Whore Monger Sing-Along"

Side B

  1. "All Word No Play" (Instrumental)
  2. "Majority Rule (Take It Back)" (Instrumental)
  3. "Whore Monger Sing-Along" (Instrumental)
Sage Francis

Live albums" Dead Poet Live Album · Road Tested (2003-2005)
