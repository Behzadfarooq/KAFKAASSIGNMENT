Allison Hubert

Allison Hubert was a college football coach at Southe Miss, and VMI. From 1931 to 1936, he coached at Southern Miss, where he compiled a 26-24-5 record. From 1937 to 1946, he coached at VMI, where he compiled a 43-45-8 record. His 1938 squad set a school record with 4 ties. His best season came in 1940, when he went 7-2-1.
