Allensville, West Virginia

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Allensville is an unincorporated hamlet in Berkeley County, West Virginia, USA. It is located on Allensville Road (West Virginia Secondary Route 3/2) to the east of Back Creek shortly before it enters the Potomac River. The claypits in North Mountain along the old Baltimore and Ohio Railroad mainline lie to the southeast of Allensville.
