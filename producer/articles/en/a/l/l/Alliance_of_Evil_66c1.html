In the Marvel Comics Universe, the Alliance of Evil is a villain group composed of young and reckless mutants with a radical way of going about things. Employed by the evil Apocalypse, and later by Harness, the Alliance's motivation was strictly in getting more power.

[edit] History

The Alliance of Evil was Apocalypse's personal strike force in the very beginning, and carried out his bidding. Rounding out the team were the super-strong Frenzy, the growing Tower, the electrifying Stinger, and the time-phasing Timeshadow. Their first mission was to find and capture Michael Nowlan, a mutant with the ability to heighten other mutants' powers. The Alliance eventually grew addicted to Nowlan's powers, but when he tried to leave them, they lashed out and kidnapped his girlfriend, Suzy, to use as bait to get him back. After Nowlan was found by X-Factor, who vowed to protect him, Suzy spilled the whereabouts of Nowlan's location to the Alliance, who promptly traced him down.

The Alliance didn't realize X-Factor would be there, as both Tower and Frenzy had faced the group before. They threatened to snap Suzy's neck if Nowlan didn't comply with their plans, which he eventually agrees to. He uses his power on the Alliance, and they immediately attack X-Factor. Frenzy takes out Beast, while Tower battles Angel and Stinger blasts away at Iceman. Cyclops attempts to get Nowlan to power X-Factor up, but he refuses and runs away to find Suzy. Timeshadow makes quick work of Marvel Girl, and Frenzy knocks out Cyclops. They find Nowlan and he agrees to go with them. They take him back to their master, Apocalypse.

The Alliance of Evil give their input on the Mutant Registration Act.

Later, the Alliance tries to find out who X-Factor really are from Nowlan, who knows nothing about the group. Frenzy and Tower beat on Nowlan a little bit, while he begs Timeshadow to give him his drugs. Stinger suggests hurting Suzy in order to get Nowlan to talk, which caused Nowlan to freak out and zap Stinger with more power than she could handle, and causes an explosion. Apocalypse appeared, angry at the Alliance, but at the same time, pleased with them as they are carrying out his Survival of the Fittest motif.

X-Factor eventually finds the Alliance's hideout, where they are ambushed. Apocalypse orders Nowlan to power up the Alliance, which he does. Suzy tries to escape, but Stinger's bolts accidentally kill her. Nowlan flips out again, causing his own death. Apocalypse abandons the Alliance of Evil and escapes. The Alliance attempt to flee, but are caught by the police and taken into custody.

Much later, the Alliance of Evil caused a commotion over the Mutant Registration Act. They started tearing the streets apart, challenging X-Factor to come stop them. X-Factor saw them on the news and immediately attacked the Alliance. Iceman was able to contain Tower, and the young mutant Rictor took out Stinger. The Alliance recuperated and got the upper hand, until Frenzy recognized Rusty Collins and taunted him, offering him a position with the Alliance. He refused, which prompted the Alliance to attack, only to be pummelled by the Beast. They were taken out by the rest of X-Factor and taken into custody by Freedom Force.

The group later encountered the New Mutants, along with Harness and Piecemeal, but without Timeshadow. The mutant strike force known as X-Force saught them out and attacked. Cannonball took out Stinger, Shatterstar and Feral beat Tower, and Warpath finished Frenzy. When questioned later, Frenzy admitted to being hired by A.I.M. to bodyguard Harness and Piecemeal. She was then dropped out of a helicopter. The group eventually disbanded. Frenzy went on to join the ranks of Magneto's Acolytes, Tower was killed by the X-Cutioner, and Stinger and Timeshadow have not been seen since.

[edit] Members

  • Frenzy, field leader of the Alliance, a super-strong mutant with steel-hard skin and resistance to temperature extremes. At first glance, she seems like your typical strongwoman, but she is actually quite intelligent. She later went on to join Magneto and his Acolytes, and eventually mind-controlled to join the X-Men.
  • Tower, a mutant with the ability to alter his molecular structure, increasing his size and strength. He is probably the most reckless out of the group, and isn't very bright. Tower was killed by the mutant-hunting X-Cutioner.
  • Stinger, a young valley girl with electricity control. She was apparently seen in the Age of Apocalypse, as a refugee in Avalon that is mind-controlled by the Shadow King and was eventually killed by Mystique. She hasn't been seen in 616 since New Mutants Annual #7.
  • Timeshadow, who had the mutant power to phase into time, allowing himself to teleport in a way, or appear to be in several places at once. He hasn't been seen since X-Factor #33.
  • Michael Nowlan, who was captured and forced to "join". He died in X-Factor #6.

[edit] Members/Employers

  • Harness, with her son Piecemeal.
