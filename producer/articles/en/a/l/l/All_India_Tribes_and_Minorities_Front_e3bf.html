All India Tribes and Minorities Front is a political party in the Indian state of Himachal Pradesh. The party works for the issues affecting the adivasi population. The party president is Mangal Singh Negi.

AITMF advocates United Nations involvement as mediators in the Kashmir issue. The party also advocates a trifurcation of the state of Jammu and Kashmir in Kashmir, Jammu and Ladakh.
