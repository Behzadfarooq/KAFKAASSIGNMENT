"All Sparks" is a song by British Indie rock band Editors and is featured on their 2005 debut album, The Back Room. It was released 27 March 2006 as the fourth single from the album (see 2006 in British music).

It may be named after the band, Sparks, whose website is called All Sparks.[citation needed] The song is also used as the closing theme to the ITV1 show Primeval.

An acoustic version of the song features on the Radio 1's Live Lounge album.
