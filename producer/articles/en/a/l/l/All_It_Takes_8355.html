"All It Takes" is New Zealand band Stellar*'s seventh, and their first single from their sophomore album Magic Line. The single would spend two weeks at #9 before dropping to #14, then ascending to #7.[1] The single features two B-sides, a differently arranged version of "You" from Stellar*'s Mix album [officially designated as "You (All It Takes Version)" on the band's website) as well as a live version of Violent, recorded live in Hamilton on March 4, 2001.
