  1. a b  Susie Meister replaced Ayanna Mackins when the teams arrived in Boston. Ayanna physically attacked Christian in the Road Rules bus after Christian made a joke to Laterrian that Ayanna perceived to be racist. Although the cameras didn't capture the incident and Christian didn't want to send Ayanna home, producers asked Ayanna to leave. Susie replaced Ayanna when their casts received their next clue at the Boston firehouse where the sixth season of the Real World took place. The Road Rules team later admitted on the reunion that Ayanna was a more valuable physical asset than Susie.
  • AS: Road Rules: All Stars
    • The entire cast worked as a single team.
  • C: Challenge
  • C2K: Challenge 2000
  • XC: Extreme Challenge
    • The "Challenges" each had a team of six Real World cast members against a team of six Road Rules cast members.
  • BSea: Battle of the Seasons
    • Cast members were in sixteen teams of two, one male and one female on each team, assigned by the Real World or Road Rules season on which the cast member was originally.
  • BS: Battle of the Sexes (2)
    • Two teams, males against females.
  • G: The Gauntlet (2)
    • Gauntlet 1 had a Real World team against a Road Rules team. Gauntlet 2 had a team of players with at least two prior seasons of experience, the Veterans, against a team of players with at most two prior seasons of experience, the Rookies.
  • I: The Inferno (2)
    • Inferno 1 had a Real World team against a Road Rules team. Inferno 2 had two arbitrarily selected teams called the Good Guys and the Bad Asses.
  • FM: Fresh Meat
    • Each Fresh Meat team had two members, an "alumnus" from a Real World or a Road Rules season as is usually the case for RW/RR Challenges paired with an opposite-sex partner who had not appeared on either Real World or Road Rules, referred to as "Fresh Meat."
  • D: The Duel
    • There are no teams except temporarily during missions; each player is competing individually against the other same-sex players.
