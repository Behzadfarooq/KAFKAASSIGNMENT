Allsång på Skansen

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Allsång på Skansen in 2004.

Allsång på Skansen, is a Swedish show held at Skansen in Stockholm, every summer. The audience is supposed to sing along with musical guests, to well-known Swedish songs.

The show started in 1935 on a small scale - about fifty people in the audience. Today, about 10 000-15 000 people come to each performance.

Since 1979, the show has been broadcast by Swedish Television. Initially the show had about 300,000 viewers. When Lasse Berghagen took over as host, the ratings increased to about two million viewers.

[edit] Hosts

  • 1935-1951. Sven Lilja (deceased 1951)
  • 1956-1966. Egon Kjerrman (on Swedish radio, as Siste Man På Skansen
  • 1974-1993. Bosse Larsson
  • 1993-2003. Lasse Berghagen
  • 2003-Today. Anders Lundin

The guest who has visited the show the most is Lill-Babs, who has appeared nine times.
