Ally Blake is an Australian writer of romance novels. After marrying her husband in Las Vegas in 2000, Ally moved to Melbourne with their miniature fox terrier, Squiffy, their two black-headed pythons, Mary and Frederick, and white silky chickens, Laverne and Shirley. When not writing she enjoys eating M&Ms, watching The West Wing reruns, and barracking at as many Collingwood football games as humanly possible.

[edit] Biography

Ally was born in a small town in Outback Queensland, Australia. She graduated from St Peters Secondary College in Brisbane, duxing Theatre. She earned her bachelor's degree from the University of Queensland, with a double English major.

Ally has tutored in english and mathematics, worked in fashion retail, and was a cheerleader for both the Brisbane Broncos football team and the Gold Coast Rollers basketball teams. She was a contracted dancer on The Footy Show for Channel Nine in Brisbane. For two years she was the secretary of the Queensland branch of the Australian Cinematographers Society.

In 2003, her first romance novel, THE WEDDING WISH, was published by Harlequin Mills & Boon. This book was nominated by industry magazine Romantic Times as Best First Series Romance of 2004. Since that time she has sold 13 novels, both for the Harlequin Romance and Modern Extra Sensual imprints.
