Allen Pitts (born June 28, 1964 in Tucson, Arizona) was a receiver for the Calgary Stampeders in the Canadian Football League. He attended Cal-State Fullerton and played his entire professional career as a receiver for the Calgary Stampeders. He holds many Stampeder records including most career touchdowns with 117, and the CFL All-Time receiving yards with 14,891. He played in 5 Grey Cup games, and was instrumental in two Grey Cup victories in 1992 and 1998. He was elected into the Canadian Football Hall of Fame in 2006. He was enshrined on the Stampeders Wall of Fame in 2005, with the retirement of his number 18.
