The Alliance of Primorje-Gorski Kotar (Croatian: Primorsko goranski savez) is a Croatian regional political party of Primorje-Gorski Kotar County.

Currently they have 1 representative in the Croatian Parliament in an alliance with the Croatian People's Party.

The party originated in city of Rijeka under the name of Rijeka Democratic Alliance (Croatian: Riječki demokratski savez, RiDS). After winning a seat in 1992 elections, the party expanded its activities to the rest of Primorsko-goranska County and changed the name accordingly.
