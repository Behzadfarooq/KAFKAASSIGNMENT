Alley Kat Brewing Company is one of several local craft brewers in Edmonton, Alberta, Canada (see Maverick Brewing Company). Alley Kat is locally owned and operated by Neil and Lavonne Herbst. Neil is Alley Kat's brewmaster.

[edit] Products

As of 2005, Alley Kat has 6 flagship brews as listed to the right. Alley Kat also brews seasonal batchs, which are brewed only for a limited time. Some examples include:

  • Ein Prosit!
  • Razzykat
  • St. Paddy's
  • Weihnachtskatze
  • Smoked Porter
  • Kiltlifter Scotch Ale

The company also released a special "Captain Tractor Yellowhead Wheat Ale" for the tenth anniversary of Edmonton folk band, Captain Tractor.
