All Time Low is a pop punk band from Lutherville-Timonium, Maryland, recently named by Alternative Press magazine's annual survey as one of the "Bands You Need to Know in '07". After releasing two records on Emerald Moon Records and performing with popular bands such as, Motion City Soundtrack, The Early November, Acceptance, Gym Class Heroes, Cartel, and The Academy Is..., the four-piece of high school seniors was signed by Hopeless Records, who released their latest EP Put Up or Shut Up in 2006. They were on the 2006 Warped Tour and opened the 2006 HFStival. In 2007, they are on national tour with Cute Is What We Aim For and This Providence. Shortly after that tour, they will be on tour with Hit The Lights and Valencia.

Starting out as a pop punk band from the suburbs of Baltimore in 2003, All Time Low had influences like Green Day and blink-182, who really brought out who they are during a live show: energetic and rough. During school they tried hard to practice and when they did not have school they would tour the east coast. All Time Low then made their first four tracks EP, The Three Words to Remember in Dealing with the End, with regional record label Emerald Moon Records. In July 2005, the band released their first full length The Party Scene on Emerald Moon Records. In 2006, just months before Gaskarth, Barakat, and Dawson graduated from Dulaney High School and Merrick graduated from Towson High School, All Time Low signed with Hopeless Records. Several months later the band released a new EP entitled Put Up or Shut Up on Hopeless Records, containing newly recorded versions of songs from The Party Scene along with two new tracks. The band is going into the studio with Matt Squire in April to record their yet to be named new album.
