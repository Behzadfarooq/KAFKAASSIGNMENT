All Is Well, released November 28, 2006 is a studio EP recorded by Clay Aiken exclusively for Walmart.[1] The four tracks are all Christmas songs. It is unusual for an extended play (EP) disc to chart or have its sales tracked; however, All Is Well debuted at #54 on the Billboard Comprehensive Albums chart.
