[edit] Alligator or Adelaide?

The article states that the three rivers are called the East (West, South) Alligator Rivers then goes on to describe the East (West, South) Adelaide Rivers. Some confusion here? Tonywalton  | Talk 14:35, 11 October 2005 (UTC)

We've let the typos live for too long here! These three rivers are Alligator. The Adelaide River headwaters are nearby too. Thankyou for noticing, sorry nobody responded promptly. --Scott Davis Talk 11:21, 17 January 2007 (UTC)
