[edit] Gorilla rhymes

Did Koko pick the name 'All Ball' for her cat because it rhymes? Sign language doesn't 'rhyme', per se, but when the good Dr. talked to Koko it rhymed. Did Koko recognise this? (13 March 2005)

  • This is not really something that could be answered by anyone other than Koko, and - the question of how adept her language skills really are aside - I don't think she's ever been taught the concept of "words that rhyme". Furthermore, you'll note that her later kittens did not have rhyming names. DS 13:18, 19 November 2005 (UTC)
  • I speculate that the term may have been Koko's reaction to the kitten's lack of a tail and thus having a more rounded overall shape; or perhaps her attempt to state that the curled-up kitten was all balled up. Or it could have been mere babbling. LeoO3 20:50, 22 May 2006 (UTC)
