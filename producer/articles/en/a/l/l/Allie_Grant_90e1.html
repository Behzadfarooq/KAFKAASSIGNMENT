Allie Grant (born on February 14, 1994 in Tupelo, Mississippi) is an American actress who currently has recurring roles in the Showtime original series Weeds and the Disney Channel Original Series The Suite Life of Zack and Cody. She has also guest starred in That's So Raven. Her parents are Bob and Angie Grant.
