"All the Small Things" is a song by Blink-182 from their 1999 album Enema of the State.

  4. "All the Small Things" (video)
  • All live tracks were recorded at the Electric Ballroom, London, England, on November 30, 1999.

[edit] Music video

Directed by Marcos Siega, the video parodies various pop videos produced by a number of artists, including The Backstreet Boys, *NSYNC, 98 Degrees, Sugar Ray, Britney Spears, Westlife, Christina Aguilera, and even themselves.

[edit] Success

"All The Small Things" was Blink-182's first number 1 single and their biggest hit, the second being I Miss You. All The Small Things spent several weeks at #1 on the Billboard Modern Rock Tracks chart.

[edit] Trivia

Ottawa's Radio Station The Team 1200 and some of its anchors made a remake version of this song called "All the Leafs Stink" during the on-going feud of the Battle of Ontario between the Ottawa Senators and Toronto Maple Leafs.
