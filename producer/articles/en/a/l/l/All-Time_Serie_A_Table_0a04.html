The All-Time Serie A Table is a cumulative record of all match results, points, and goals of every team that has played in the Serie A since its inception in 1929. The table that follows is accurate as of the end of the 2005-06 season; the 1945-46 championship is not included because played by both Serie A and B teams. Every victory is worth 2 points and no point is deducted.
