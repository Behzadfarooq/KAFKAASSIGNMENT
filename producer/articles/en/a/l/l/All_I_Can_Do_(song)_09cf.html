"All I Can Do" was a 1976 song, written and performed by Dolly Parton. The song, the title song of Parton's All I Can Do album, was released as a single in May 1976, and reached number 3 on the U.S. country singles charts. An uptempo singalong number, it also became a concert favorite at Parton's live shows.
