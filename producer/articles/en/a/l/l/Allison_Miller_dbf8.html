Allison Miller

Allison Miller is a character actor, born in Rome, Italy, who grew up in Lexington, Kentucky; State College, Pennsylvania and Tallahassee, Florida where she attended Leon High School and graduated from The Maclay School and attended the University of Florida in Gainesville. Allison got her acting start at Tallahassee's Young Actors Theatre.
