"All This Time" is a popular song written for the winner of the second series of Pop Idol in the UK. the final two acts in the show, Michelle McManus and Mark Rhodes both performed the song in the final of the show. McManus went on to win, and released "All This Time" as her debut single. It went straight to number 1 in the UK Singles Chart on 11 January 2004 where it stayed for three weeks.
