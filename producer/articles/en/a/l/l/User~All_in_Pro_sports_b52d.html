[edit] Conferences and divisions

  • Note: not the actual conferences and divisions used in each league.
  • Suggested realignments for the four major North American sports leagues for the following cases:
    • 2 non-geog. conferences, 3 divisions in each (current MLB, past NFL)
    • 2 non-geog. conferences, 2/4 divisions in each (past MLB/NFL, current NFL
    • 2 conferences, 3 divisions in each (current NBA/NHL)
    • 2 conferences, 2/4 divisions in each (past NBA/NHL)
  • Aligned such that:
    • Cities (metro areas) rather than teams are placed in divisions: any rivalry beginning with one sport can then be carried on to another
    • With non-geographic conferences, metro areas with multiple teams in the same league have those teams in different conferences to allow such things as one NL and one AL team, or two teams from the same metro area in the championship
