Alliance Marxist-Leninist

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Alliance Marxist-Leninist was founded in 1989 by former members of the Albania-USA Friendship Society. Its members then are Hoxhaists, meaning they follow in the legacy of Enver Hoxha, former leader of Albania - of course, they describe themselves as orthodox Marxist-Leninists, since they uphold Hoxha as the true bearer of the tradition of Marx and Lenin, as once upheld by Stalin. They stopped being friendly to Albania once Hoxha's hardline rule had been revoked following his death. As an anti-revisionist group, they are highly supportive of Stalin, as well as Hoxha.

They are linked to the Communist League of Great Britain.
