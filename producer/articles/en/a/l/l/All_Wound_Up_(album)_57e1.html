All Wound Up, the first official album by Godsmack, was released independently by EK Records on July 28, 1997. It was eventually edited, remastered and re-released as the self-titled Godsmack with the song "Goin' Down" being removed and a new song, "Someone in London" taking its place. "Goin' Down" later appeared on the soundtrack to Mission Impossible 2 as well as Godsmack's follow up album Awake.
