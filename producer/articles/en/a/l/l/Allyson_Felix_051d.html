Allyson Felix (born November 18, 1985 in Los Angeles, California) is a track and field sprint athlete, competing internationally for the United States in the 200 meters.

Felix, sometimes nicknamed Chicken Legs, was a silver medalist in the 200 metres at the 2004 Summer Olympics, behind Veronica Campbell of Jamaica. She also won a gold medal in the 200 metres at the 2005 World Championships in Athletics in Helsinki. She is also the World Junior record holder over 200 meters with her time of 22.18, set in the 2004 Olympic final.

Felix made headlines in 2003 by foregoing college eligibility to sign a professional contract as a track athlete. She is currently under contract with Adidas.

Felix is coached by Pat Connolly, coach of Olympic champion and former world-record holder Evelyn Ashford.
