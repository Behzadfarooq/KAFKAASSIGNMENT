"All Time Love" is a popular song, written by Jamie Hartman and performed by Will Young. It was released as the second single from Young's third album Keep On and his ninth single overall. The single went to number three in the UK Singles Chart.

"All Time Love" was also made into a mini movie which served as a music video. A second video for the song appears on CD2 of the single release, as well as the DualDisc version of Keep On. In this video, Young portrays a skywriter who has written the words "All Time Love" in the sky and then parachuted from his aircraft, which is seen crashing in the distance at the end of the video.

The song was voted Song of the Year in the heat readers poll 2006 and. It is also currently shortlisted for the British Single award at the 2007 Brit Awards, where it has reached the final round of voting.
