Allan Saxe

Dr. Allan Saxe is a renowned political scientist, author, lecturer, radio commentator, and philanthropist. He was born in Oklahoma City, Oklahoma and earned his Ph.D in political science at the University of Oklahoma.

Dr. Saxe is an Associate Professor of Political Science at the University of Texas at Arlington, where he has been a faculty member since 1965. He was named Best Liberal Arts Teacher and received a Chancellor's Award for Teaching, and he won the statewide Piper Award for Excellence in Teaching. Dr. Saxe was also awarded the State of Texas Humanitarian Volunteer Award in 1992.

UTA's baseball field was named after him after he donated a large part of the funds necessary for its renovation.

Dr. Saxe was, for a time, a member of the Dallas-based and now-defunct Global Wrestling Federation as a "psychiatrist/valet".
