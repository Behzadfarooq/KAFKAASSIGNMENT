Allsorts (TV series)

Allsorts, later Gigglish Allsorts was a television programme for young children produced by Granada Television and was broadcast on Children's ITV from 1987 until the early 1990s. The show comprised of five main characters.

  • Jiffy was a weird man who was always making mistakes.
  • Jay-Jay was a short women and friend of Jiffy. Played by Jane Cox.
  • Bonzo was another man, a little more sensible than Jiffy. However, in the first series Bonzo was a dog played by a female human!!! Bonzo the human, was played by Wayne Jackman
  • Box was a talking box who always seemed determined to get her own way.
  • In the later years, there was also a mole called Mouldy, a friend of Box; he lived in the garden and wore a miner's helmet.

The show was situated in a house and garden and usually involved the other characters having to put up with Box and her problems. There was often a song within the programme.
