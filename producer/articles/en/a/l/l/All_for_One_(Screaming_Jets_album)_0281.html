All for One is the debut album by the Australian band The Screaming Jets. Singles that were taken of this album were C'mon, Better (AUS#4-May 1991), Stop The World (AUS#33-July 1991), Shine On (AUS#36-Sep 1991). The song Better is their most well known song.
