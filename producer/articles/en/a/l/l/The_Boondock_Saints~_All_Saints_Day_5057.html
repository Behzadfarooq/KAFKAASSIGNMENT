The Boondock Saints: All Saints Day, also known as The Boondock Saints II is a planned sequel to The Boondock Saints. The film has been in the works since spring of 2002, and has just recently made major progress.

[edit] Details

The rights to a sequel were wrapped up in a lawsuit until late 2006. It was announced in June that 20th Century Fox would finance the sequel. It has also been announced that the budget for this film is double that of the original film, partially due to plans for a theatrical release. In Troy Duffy's most recent web video, he announces that the next movie he does will either be The Good King or All Saints Day, and that currently it appeared that All Saints Day was going to be ready first because it was farther along. Duffy has also announced that all of the cast plans to return except for Willem Dafoe.
