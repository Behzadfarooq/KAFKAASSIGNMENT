Allister Brimble

Allister Brimble (born in 1970) is a video game music composer. He wrote music during the 1990s for games, such as Superfrog, Alien Breed, Colonization, Descent II, and the Dizzy series, on the Amiga and other home computers. More recently, he has composed music for the games RollerCoaster Tycoon 1 & 2. He also produced various audio tracks, as "Brimble's Beats", that were distributed on coverdisks of magazines including CU Amiga and Amiga Format.

He now works as a freelance musician and sound designer for the games and console industry with Anthony Putson as "Orchestral Media Developments".
