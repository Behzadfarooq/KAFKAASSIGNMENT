Category:All-star recordings

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Articles about songs that have been recorded by groups of 10 or more celebrity participants.

[edit] All-star recordings that need articles

  • "What's Going On", a charity single organized by Jermaine Dupri in the struggle against Aids, released in the wake of the September 11, 2001 attacks.
  • "U Will Know", a 1994 song from the Jason's Lyric soundtrack recorded by a collective called Black Men United which included R. Kelly, LL Cool J, Silk, Usher, Joe, Tevin Campbell, Boyz II Men, Keith Sweat, Maxwell, and many others.
  • "We're All In The Same Gang", an anti-violence single recorded by a collaboration of hip-hop artists who called themselves The West Coast All-Stars including Def Jef, Michel'le, Tone-Loc, Ice-T, JJ Fad, Young MC, Digital Underground, and others.
  • "Self Destruction", an anti-violence awareness single organized by KRS-One's Stop The Violence Movement which raised half a million dollars for the National Urban League in 1989.
