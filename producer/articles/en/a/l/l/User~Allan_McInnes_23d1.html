I'm currently working as an embedded systems engineer for a small contract product-development company. I'm also finishing up a Ph.D. in Electrical & Computer Engineering, in which my primary focus has been concurrency theory (specifically Communicating Sequential Processes) and distributed systems. Prior to creating an account I made a few minor contributions to the Wikipedia as 67.161.208.110.

[edit] Interests

I'm a participant in WikiProject Computer science, but also have interests outside that project.

These are the areas that I'm most interested in (at present), and most likely to try to contribute to:

  • Improving the Wikipedia articles on concurrency
  • Improving Wikipedia coverage of Formal specification and Formal methods, especially
    • Process calculi
      • Communicating Sequential Processes (CSP)
      • Algebra of Communicating Processes (ACP)
      • Ambient calculus
      • Calculus of Communicating Systems
    • Alloy language
  • Improving Wikipedia coverage of Engineering disciplines such as Systems engineering and Software Engineering
