The Alliance of Vojvodina Hungarians (Hungarian: Vajdasági Magyar Szövetség (VMSZ); Serbian: Савез војвођанских Мађара or Savez vojvođanskih Mađara) is an ethnic Hungarian political party in the Serbian province of Vojvodina. Its chairman is József Kasza.

[edit] Participation in elections

[edit] State elections

At the parliamentary elections on January 21, 2007, the party got enough votes to send 3 of its members to the parliament. The party could be a coalition partner of the pro-European democratic parties.

At the previous legislative elections in Serbia on December 28, 2003, the party was part of the Together for Tolerance alliance, that won no seats.

[edit] Provincial elections

On the provincial elections in Vojvodina in 2004, the party won 8.50% of votes in the one-round voting system, and currently, it is part of the ruling coalition in the Vojvodina provincial parliament.

[edit] Local elections

In the last local elections in Serbia in 2004, the party won largest number of seats in the municipal parliaments of Subotica, Senta, Bačka Topola, Mali Iđoš, Kanjiža (where Reformists of Vojvodina won same number of seats) and Čoka.
