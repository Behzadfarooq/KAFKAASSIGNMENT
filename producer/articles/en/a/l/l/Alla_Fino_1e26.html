Alla Fino

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Alla Fino is a painter who won numerous awards for her paintings while living in Belarus and Russia. She now lives in the United States. She is married to Ronald Fino who is known for his work for the FBI and CIA on the subject of the Mafia.
