Alley Broussard (born September 6, 1983) is a college football running back for the Louisiana State Tigers football team.


[edit] College Career

He has been a backup for most of his college career, to Joseph Addai and Justin Vincent. He has amassed a good amount of yardage however, and splits time with Justin Vincent in the 2006 season.
