Allapattah Station is a Metrorail station in Miami, Florida, USA, in the neighborhood of Allapattah.

This station is located near the intersection of Northwest 12th Avenue (State Road 933) and 36th Street (US 27/SR 25). It was opened to service December 17, 1984.
