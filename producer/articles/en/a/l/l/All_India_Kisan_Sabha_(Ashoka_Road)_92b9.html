All India Kisan Sabha (Ashoka Road)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

AIKS demonstrator in Delhi
Kerala Karshaka Sangam, the Kerala regional body of AIKS, mural in Allepey

All India Kisan Sabha, the peasants front of Communist Party of India (Marxist). It is sometimes referred to as All India Kisan Sabha (Ashoka Road), to distinguish it from the AIKS of Communist Party of India. The AIKS of CPI is sometimes called AIKS (Ajoy Bhavan).


The address of AIKS (Ashoka Road) is 4, Ashoka Road in New Delhi. The president of AIKS is S. Ramachandran Pillai and the general secretary K. Varadharajan.
