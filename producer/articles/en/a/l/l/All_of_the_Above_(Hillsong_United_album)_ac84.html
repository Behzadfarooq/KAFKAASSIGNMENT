All of the Above is the eighth praise and worship album by Hillsong United, their first studio recorded project and the first of a three-part global project. The album includes a DVD containing 3 live worship tracks recorded at the 2006 Encounterfest youth conference, a sermon from Phil Dooley and a bonus features section. It debuted at #6 on the Australian Recording Industry Association album chart on 26 March 2007. [1]

[edit] Notes

  • The album chronology lists only the albums in the United series.
  • Previews of each song are on Hillsong's website. [1]
  • This album is the first of a three-part project announced on Joel Houston's myspace "... we've spent the last week recording the first installment in what I'm considering a trilogy of very different projects we have in store..." The others are theiheartrevolution [2] and the as-yet unannounced under-21s project [3], a live EP of the songs from this album played by Hillsong United members who are under 21 years old.
