Alley Cats Strike is a Disney Channel Original Movie.


[edit] Plot Summary

A high school has a sports hero who's good in every sport. But when the town tries to settle a long standing sports rivalry, and the only sport left to compete in is bowling. So he and the high schools regular bowlers ( Alex, Ken, Elisa, and Delia) are suddenly made team-mates. A sort of class conflict erupts, as all of them try to deal with entering the others world, and dealing with the difference between winning and having fun.
