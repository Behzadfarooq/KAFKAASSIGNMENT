"All I Want to Do" is a song written by Dennis Wilson and Stephen Kalinich for the American pop band The Beach Boys. It was released on their 1969 album 20/20. The song was recorded on November 21, 1968. In the fade out at the end of the song, two people can be heard having sex, which is confirmed by Snopes.com. It is rumored that these two unidentified people actually had sex in the studio while the song was being recorded. It is also rumoured that one of those people is Dennis Wilson himself.
