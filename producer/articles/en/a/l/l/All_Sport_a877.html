All Sport

You have new messages (last change).

All Sport was a sports drink similar to Gatorade and POWERade. It was produced by PepsiCo, as their response to the Coca-Cola Company's POWERade. All Sport originally was carbonated, but many athletes and consumers objected to this, as it was not consistent with the healthful nature of sports drinks. It also had more sugar than its competitors, making it too sweet for some tastes. With PepsiCo's acquisition of the Quaker Oats Company, the makers of Gatorade, in 2001, All Sport was sold off to the Monarch Beverage Company, of Atlanta, Georgia.
