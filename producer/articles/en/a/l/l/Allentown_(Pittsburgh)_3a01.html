Allentown (Pittsburgh)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

For the city in the Lehigh Valley region of eastern Pennsylvania, see Allentown, Pennsylvania

Allentown is a neighborhood on the southside of Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania, in the United States. It has two zip codes, 15203 and 15210, and has representation on the Pittsburgh City Council by the council member for District 3 (Central South Neighborhoods).

[edit] History

Beltzhoover Avenue is a north-south thorofare that separates Allentown and Knoxville (to the East) from Beltzhoover and Mt. Washington, (to the West). The city of Pittsburgh expanded and absorbed these areas lying southward of the original city of Pittsburgh.
