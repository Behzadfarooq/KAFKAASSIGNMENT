All's Well That Ends Well is a Chiodos album. They have done two videos for this album, "One Day Women Will All Become Monsters" and "Baby, You Wouldn't Last a Minute on the Creek" which was released on the re-release of ""All's Well That End's Well"". They also previously filmed a home movie for "All Nereids Beware", but they re-recorded the song for the album. This is their first CD on their new label, Equal Vision Records. A 2006 reissue contains three bonus acoustic tracks. Chiodos also recently filmed a video for "The Words 'Best Friend' Become Redefined."
