The All Japan Road Race Championship (全日本ロードレース選手権, Zen Nihon Rōdorēsu Senshuken?) is a national motorcycle racing championship held in Japan and is run by the Motorcycle Federation of Japan (日本モーターサイクルスポーツ協会) and approved by the FIM.

[edit] History

The MFJ was formed in 1961 and held its first motorcycle road race
