"All For Love"
Single by Bryan Adams, Rod Stewart and Sting
Released 1993
Genre Pop
Label A&M Records
Producer(s) Chris Thomas, Bryan Adams and David Nicholas

"All For Love" is a hit song performed by Bryan Adams, Rod Stewart and Sting. It was written for the soundtrack of the 1993 film The Three Musketeers by Adams, Mutt Lange and Michael Kamen, who were on a roll after writing the massive 1991 hit "(Everything I Do) I Do It for You". The title was inspired by the Musketeers' motto: "All for one, and one for all".

The single reached number one on the Billboard Hot 100 on January 22, 1994, and remained there for three weeks.

"All For Love" is only available on two Bryan Adams albums; The Best of Me and Anthology 1980-2005 (disc 2).
