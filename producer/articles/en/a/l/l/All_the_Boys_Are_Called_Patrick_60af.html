All Boys Are Called Patrick (Orig. Tous les garçons s'appellent Patrick ) is a 1957 short film written by Éric Rohmer and directed by Jean-Luc Godard and made before both filmmakers achieved fame as French New Wave filmmakers. It tells the story of a playboy named Patrick who discovers that the two young women he's just asked out are actually roommates.

The film can be found on the Criterion Collection DVD for Godard's Une femme est une femme.
