All We Want is the title of an EP released by H2O. It was released on October 29, 2002, and is the most recent CD released by the band. It includes three previously unreleased tracks, 2 live versions of songs from the album Go and the music video for the song "Role Model".

[edit] Track listing

All songs by Todd Morse unless otherwise noted.

  1. "All We Want"
  2. "Static"
  3. "Wrong"
  4. "Role Model (Live at CBGB's)"
  5. "Memory Lane (Live at CBGB's)" (Rusty Pistachio)

[edit] Personnel

  • Toby Morse – vocals
  • Todd Morse – guitar, vocals
  • Rusty Pistachio – guitar, vocals
  • Adam Blake – bass
  • Todd Friend – drums, vocals

Recorded August 2002 & mixed September 2002 in Suffern, NY.
