All Evil is a demo tape by the Norwegian black metal band Satyricon. It was recorded on June 21st and 22nd, 1992. This demo is actually untitled, but it ended up being know as the "All Evil" demo. It was reissued by Black Hearts Records in 1993.

[edit] Track listing

  1. "All Evil" – 5:03
  2. "This Red Sky" – 1:45
  3. "Dreams Of A Satyr" – 4:38
  4. "All Evil (shorter version)" – 0:54
