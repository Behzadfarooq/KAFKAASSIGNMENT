The Alliance Football League (AFL) is a semi-pro football league. The Alliance Football League was originally founded as the Appalachian Football League.

[edit] MCFL vs AFL All-Star Game

This section is a stub. You can help by expanding it.

In 2006, the AFL agreed to send their best players to compete in an annual all-star game against the MCFL.

On Saturday May 27th, 2006. The MCFL played the AFL all-stars in Bluefield, West Virginia. (Home of the Bluefield Barons.)
