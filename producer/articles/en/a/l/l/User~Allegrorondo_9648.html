[edit] RPGs

I started with D&D in the early 80's and have quite the collection of old, out of date, and unusual RPGs. Finally I can put them to use by making articles about them. My first major contributions: Timelords (role-playing game), EABA, CORPS, Blacksburg Tactical Research Center, Hollyworld.

[edit] Beer

Im currently a memeber of WikiProject Beer. As an avid homebrewer, I'd like to help out with the cataloging and description of the world's beer styles. My articles: Saint Louis Brewery, Boulevard Brewing Company, Oud bruin, Russian Imperial stout, Mild ale
