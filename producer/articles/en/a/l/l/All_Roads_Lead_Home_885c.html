All Roads Lead Home is a feature film scheduled for release in 2007, directed by Dennis Fallon. It stars Peter Boyle, Patton Oswalt, Jason London, Vivien Cardone, Vanessa Branch, Peter Coyote and Allan Kayser.

It is the sequel to the 2000 movie More Than Puppy Love. The story is about a 10-year old girl (Belle) who loses her mother in an automobile accident. Her father (Cody) struggles with the loss of his wife and trying to raise a young girl who believes her father is partly responsible for her mother's death. Hock, Belle's grandfather since the accident, has trouble communicating with Belle, because she reminds him too much of his daughter. It is Atticus the new puppy that will change Belle's life and everyone else's around her.

The movie was filmed in and around Kansas City, Missouri.

Peter Boyle passed away before the movie's release.It was the last movie he appeared in.
