All Areas - Worldwide
Live album by Accept
Released 1997
Genre Heavy metal
Length 1:53:05
Label Gun
Producer(s) Stefan Kaufmann

All Areas - Worldwide is a double live album by Accept. It was released in Japan and U.S.A. as The Final Chapter in 1998.
