All Grown Up! (also known as AGU) is an animated television series based on characters from Rugrats, as older characters facing pre-teen and teen issues. It premiered in April 2003 as a preview before starting its regular run in late November (although in October, it began its regular run in the UK, on Nickelodeon UK and CITV, and in Canada on YTV). The show is based on the Rugrats' 10th anniversary episode and television movie All Growed Up (aired in 2001), which proved popular enough that Nickelodeon decided to commission a whole series. Production began in September 2002. It aired on Nick on CBS on March 14, 2004 and ended on September 9, 2004.

When the series premiered its regular run, more than 3.2 million viewers, and over a third of all kids watching cable in the United States, tuned in, enough to put it into the 2nd place slot for the week (behind an NFL game on ESPN) and making it the highest-rated premiere in Nick's history. (The April sneak peek didn't make the top 15 cable programs for that week, due to the ongoing war in Iraq.) The original titles for this series were All Growed Up and Rugrats: All Growed Up!.

On April 21, 2007, All Grown Up will return to the US Nickelodeon schedule @ 10:00am Eastern Time and will also premiere on Nicktoons Network on an unknown date.

[edit] Characters

The principal characters and their traits (as well as their voice performers) have been carried over from the original Rugrats series, with a handful of changes and additional character traits:

  • Tommy (E.G. Daily)
  • Chuckie (Nancy Cartwright)
  • Phil and Lil (Kath Soucie)
  • Angelica (Cheryl Chase)
  • Dil (Tara Strong)
  • Susie (Cree Summer)
  • Kimi (Dionne Quan)

A handful of new characters have been added, for example:

  • Harold is Susie's and Angelica's best friend. He is secretly in love with Angelica
  • Savannah (Shayna Fox) is Angelica's "rival" who thrives in popularity.
  • Nicole Boscarelli (Lizzie Murray) is a good friend of Chuckie's.
  • Estes "Slambang" Pangborn (Clancy Brown), former wrestler and Principal to the school the gang go to.

[edit] Airings

  • It is believed that All Grown Up has apparently been "cancelled" as of November 25, 2006 (though no official announcement has been made). Klasky-Csupo and Viacom have not announced any plans to release the unaired episodes to regular daytime programming after the show went out of production. The last episode to air was titled "Rachel, Rachel" and had been one of the previously unaired episodes.
  • Several unaired episodes were leaked onto the Google-owned "video-sharing" site YouTube in late 2006. In February 2007, Viacom ordered the site to remove over 100,000 copyrighted clips, citing no compensation for the copyrighted material after neogotiations failed to produce a deal. The site followed these orders as to avert legal tensions. YouTube is currently being sued by Viacom over these copyrighted clip leaks for an estimated $1B.
