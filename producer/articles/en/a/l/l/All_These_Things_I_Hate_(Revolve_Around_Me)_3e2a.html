All These Things I Hate (Revolve Around Me) is an EP from Welsh metalcore quartet Bullet for My Valentine. It is a German only release, but could be imported into the UK. It was released through Sony BMG Recordings on 3rd February, 2006. The EP includes two songs from the band's full-length debut The Poison (one studio; one live), and two previously unreleased b-sides from The Poison sessions.

[edit] German EP Tracklisting

  1. All These Things I Hate (Revolve Around Me)
  2. Room 409' (Live)
  3. Seven Days'
  4. My Fist, Your Mouth, Her Scars (Includes a sample of the song Breathe Life by Killswitch Engage)

[edit] UK Singles Release

All These Things I Hate (Revolve Around Me) was released in two formats in the UK. These were in the form of two CD singles. They were both released through Visible Noise Records on 6th February, 2006 in the UK. Here are the tracklistings:

All These Things I Hate (Revolve Around Me) CD1

  1. All These Things I Hate (Revolve Around Me)
  2. Seven Days

All These Things I Hate (Revolve Around Me) CD2

  1. All These Things I Hate (Revolve Around Me)
  2. My Fist, Your Mouth, Her Scars Includes a sample of the song Breathe Life by Killswitch Engage
  3. Enhanced Content including the music video for All These Things I Hate (Revolve Around Me) and two desktop wallpapers

[edit] Misellaneous Information

  • The two b-sides; Seven Days and My Fist, Your Mouth, Her Scars were later included on a special edition re-release of The Poison in the UK as bonus tracks.
