All Saints Catholic High School, opened September 2002, is a secondary school in Kanata, Ontario, near Ottawa. The school is located in the Kanata North suburb of Kanata Lakes and serves the communities of Kanata and West Carleton. The school motto is Dei Gratia, Latin for 'The Grace of God'.

The school's first year of operation only saw classes from grade 7 up to and including grade 11. In order to prevent over-crowding, the school capped the maximum number of students permitted to attend in the first year. All Saints' second year of operation saw classes ranging all the way to grade 12, with population limits beginning to max out.

Now in its fourth year of operation, All Saints has installed multiple portables in order to increase the maximum number of students that can be educated at the school. The idea of an expansion to the school has been put forth, and is currently under construction. The expansion should be completed by September of 2007.

[edit] School Layout

The school is very large. It has three floors, two gymnasiums (four, if you count the dividers), 12 washrooms (male and female), four changerooms, and about 1500 lockers.

[edit] Expansion

In mid-September 2006 a final meeting by the school council determined that the school was getting an expansion. This expansion is currently under construction. This expansion will have approximately three floors (four with maintenance areas), and is being designed to hold all grade 7/8 students at All Saints. This is also providing all 7/8 students with lockers in the expansion. When the expansion is completed (deadline September 2007), all 19 portables will be removed from the football field, clearing up space for outdoor sports. This expansion is expected to have its own P.A system, as the 7/8 schedule will run quite differently starting in the 1st semester of the 07/08 year. (Its contract has been awarded to Taplen Construction Inc.)

[edit] Teachers

There are many teachers at All Saints. Many of the teachers came from All Saints' neighbouring school, Holy Trinity. There are currently around 140 plus teachers and staff working at All Saints.
