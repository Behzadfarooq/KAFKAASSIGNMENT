To allure is to attract, entice, or seduce.

Allure has several other meanings:

  • Allure (group), an R&B music group, known for their 1998 hit "All Cried Out".
  • Allure is a pseudonym of Tijs Verwest, a trance music DJ and producer best known as DJ Tiësto.
  • Allure Las Vegas is a condominium project in Las Vegas.
  • Allure (magazine)
  • Allure (song) is a song produced by the Neptunes and peformed by Jay-Z, from his album "The Black Album"
