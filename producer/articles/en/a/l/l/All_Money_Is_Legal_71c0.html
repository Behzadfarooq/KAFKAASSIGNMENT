A. M. I. L. is Amil's first album. Lyrically, Amil impressed many of her fans on cuts like "Smile For Me," where she flips her personal struggles into a heartfelt rhyme. On "I Got That," featuring Beyoncé, Amil raps for all independent women who don't need a man to pay their bills. Labelmate Jay-Z adds his lyrical parts on a number of cuts including the male vs. female joint: "Heard It All Before". The album principally was recorded at The Cutting Room, Hit Factory and Quad Studios, New York, New York.
