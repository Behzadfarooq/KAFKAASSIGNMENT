All I Want, also known as Try Seventeen, is a dramedy starring Elijah Wood, Franka Potente, and Mandy Moore.

[edit] Plot

Wood plays a 17-year-old dreamer, Jones Dillon, who drops out of college after the first day and moves into an old apartement building. That is where he meets a temperamental photographer (Potente) and a self-absorbed aspiring actress (Moore), both who attract him.

[edit] Cast

  • Elijah Wood - Jones Dillon
  • Franka Potente - Jane
  • Mandy Moore - Lisa
  • Elizabeth Perkins - Blanche
  • Deborah Harry - Ma
  • Chris William Martin - Steve
  • Aaron Pearl - Brad

[edit] Trivia

  • This is the second film which Elizabeth Perkins plays Elijah's mother, the first film was Avalon, directed by Barry Levinson.
