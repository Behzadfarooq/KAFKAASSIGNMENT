All I Need to Know is the second and final single to be taken from Emma Bunton's third solo album Life In Mono. The song was co-written with Jamie Hartman, who is notable for previously writing the hit All Time Love for Will Young. First announced by her official site,[1] the single was released physically on February 12, 2007 in the UK & Ireland. It was also available to download from February 5, 2007. The physical release of this single is a two-track CD, with a remix being available only as an iTunes exclusive.

[edit] Music video

Emma Bunton as she appears in the music video for "All I Need to Know".

Directed by Max & Dania, the video was shot shortly before Christmas 2006 in the East End of London.[2] The video features Emma as an angel watching over various lonely people, none of whom can see her.

The only trace of her presence are floating feathers that come from her wings, which are only observable as reflections or from a distance.

The video was originally inspired by the photography of Peter Lindbergh, who is one of the world's preeminent fashion photographers.[3]

[edit] Charts

The single entered to the UK Singles Chart in a disappointing #200 on only downloads. On the week of 25 March, she has currently sold around 4,000 copies of the single, giving problems to rise up the chart, which only charted eventully at 60 (the lowest peaking single for an former Spice Girls member), falling on the following week down the UK Top 75 at 134 and then folling straight out of the top 200.
