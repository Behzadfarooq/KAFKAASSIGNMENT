All Saints Greek Orthodox Grammar School or simply 'All Saints Grammar' is a "Greek Orthodox Christian School for Boys and Girls" comprised of two schooling campuses, being a Junior (Kindergarten to Grade 6 - Primary education) and "Towers Campus"
(Grade 7 to Grade 12 - High school) located in Lakemba and Belmore, Sydney, NSW, Australia respectively.
