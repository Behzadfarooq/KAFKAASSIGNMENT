Alleghany High School is a public secondary school in Covington, Virginia, United States. It is part of Alleghany County Public Schools and is located on 210 Mountaineer Drive. Though the school has a Covington mailing address, it is actually about 10 miles east of Covington City, and about two miles west of Low Moor.

[edit] History

Alleghany High School's current building opened in 1964 as Alleghany County High School. The school's colors were red, white, and columbia blue, but the mascot was known as the Colts.

In 1983, Clifton Forge High School, located in nearby Clifton Forge City closed down, and students from there were consolidated with ACHS forming the current Alleghany High School. Also as a result of the consolidation, Alleghany's mascot changed to the Mountaineers as it currently is today, as it was the mascot of the former Clifton Forge High School.

In 2005, the Alleghany County School Board began holding meetings with the public and also started negotiations with contractors about the possibility of building a new Alleghany High School building. The current high school is located on top of a floodplain which has flooded two times since it has been open, the most recent account being in the year 1989. The school itself is a safety hazard, students have been accustomed to seeing cockroaches in the hall, asbestos fills the air, and mold creeps through the walls. The electrical system is faulty, and not safe in the least bit. All in all this school is not safe to be in. There has also been an increased interest in the consolidation of Alleghany County and Covington City's school districts due to the economic hardships that the Alleghany Highlands have faced recently, as well as declining enrollment in both school districts.

[edit] Demographics

Alleghany High School's student body is 91% White; 8% Black; and 1% are of other ethnic backgrounds.

[edit] Test Scores

Alleghany High School is a fully accredited high school based on the Standards of Learning tests in Virginia. The average SAT score in 2004 for Alleghany was a 986 (496 in Verbal; 490 in Math).

[edit] Athletics

The mascot is a mountaineer and the sports teams currently play in the AA Blue Ridge District and Region III. The Mountaineers have recently been best known for their boys SOCCER, boys basketball, and baseball teams, which have advanced in Region III competition in the last few years, rather than their oh so not great football team. In the 2005-2006 season, the Mountaineer football team had a discouraging (1-11) record.

[edit] Destination Imagination

In 2006 The Alleghany Destination Imagination team "The Black Hats: Masters of Piracy", consisting of Chris Rybitski, Sarah Wilson, and Zach Hunter, placed 7th in the global finals.
