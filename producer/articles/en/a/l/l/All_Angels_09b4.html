All Angels are a British classical/pop group consisting of Melanie Nakhla, Charlotte Ritchie, Laura Wright, and Daisy Chute.

Their self-titled debut album was released in 2006.

[edit] Trivia

Bookmakers have put odds of 7-1 on them getting a U.K. Christmas number-one single with their cover of the Robbie Williams song "Angels".[1]

Daisy Chute went to an independent school in Scotland called Loretto. Charlotte Ritchie currently attends JAGS in Dulwich, south east London.

Laura Wright is currently studying AS-Levels at Framlingham College in Suffolk. She was the Radio 2 Young Chorister of the Year for 2005/06.
