Alloran-Semitur-Corrass is a fictional character in the Animorphs series by K. A. Applegate.

Spoiler warning: Plot and/or ending details follow.

Alloran-Semitur Corass was once a venerable Andalite war prince until the battle on the Hork-Bajir world. At the end of the battle, realising that the war was lost, he orders the release of the quantum virus, a space/time disease that breaks down the affected organism's individual cells in order to deprive the Yeerk invaders of Hork-Bajir hosts. This action decimates the planet's Hork-Bajir population. Alloran is court martialled discreetly by the Andalite military authorities, and is effectively stripped of his rank - except in title.

As the war against the Yeerks drags on, Alloran was assigned to the Dome ship StarSword that is pursuing a Yeerk task force in the uncharted Graysha Nebula. The ship's Captain Feyorn orders Alloran to take command of two arisths, Elfangor-Sirinial-Shamtul and Arbron, and take two humans, Loren and Hedrick Chapman, back to Earth. However, during the trip through Zero space, Arbron discovers the existence of the Time Matrix in a Skrit Na computer file. Alloran urgently travels with the two arisths and the two humans in his ship the Jahar (named after his wife) to the Taxxon home world, where the Skrit Na had taken the Time Matrix. Alloran orders Arbron and Elfangor to board a Yeerk transport ship. They find out that there are tens of thousands of slugs awaiting Taxxon hosts. Alloran plans to flush them out into space, but Elfangor objects on moral grounds. Alloran, now contemptuous of the young warrior, decides to retrieve the Time Matrix and then flush the Yeerks out when they leave the system. Elfangor frustrates his plans again, however, and allows Chapman to knock Alloran out. Elfangor, occupied by the events around him, is unaware that Alloran is already being infested by Esplin 9466, who eventually rises to the rank of Visser Thirty-two. Elfangor realises it too late, and wastes his opportunities to free or finish Alloran. Elfangor later blames himself for Alloran's infestation, which has made Alloran the Yeerks' first Andalite host.

Alloran and Esplin eventually become Visser Three, in command of the invasion of Earth. Despite his long-standing infestation (Alloran remains infested for nearly two decades), he retains hope that one day he will be free. Even he is shaken by the horrors he sees under Yeerk control. When he is poisoned, forcing Esplin to flee his host, Alloran tries to enlist the help of Aximili-Esgarrouth-Isthill to kill himself, but is too weak to do so.

Eventually, Alloran is freed in the final Animorph books, and Esplin is imprisoned. What happened to Alloran is unknown, although it is assumed he returned to the Andalite home world. He reached near taboo status during his infestation, and every Andalite came to know Esplin/Alloran as 'The Abomination'.
