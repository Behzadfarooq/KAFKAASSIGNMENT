[edit] Summary

Rob Rohde's palaeotemperature graphs pasted together on one page, with Royer et al's CO2-corrected Paleozoic - Mesozoic record substituted for Veizer et al's uncorrected record.

The Holocene temperatures are not polar temperatures, so the range has been expanded a little to compensate - by about the usual times two. The Royer et al record is similarly expanded. The relativities are very approximate.
