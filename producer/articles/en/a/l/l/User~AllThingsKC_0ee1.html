[edit] Born and Raised

I was born in Kansas City, Missouri on January 17, 1983. When I was 12-years-old, my family moved to Grandview, Missouri, where I graduated from high school in 2001. In May 2005, I moved back to Kansas City and I now live in the Downtown Kansas City neighborhood of Quality Hill.

[edit] Current Plans

  • I am attending Calvary Bible College in Kansas City. I am majoring in Broadcasting/Mass Communications. I am often on the air at Calvary 88.5 FM.
  • I am currently attending an Assembly of God church in a Kansas City suburb.
  • I also manage 3 different Quiznos Subs stores in the Kansas City area.

[edit] My Passions

  • If you couldn't tell from reading above, I take no shame in saying I'm a Christian. Jesus Christ and anything related to the church and Israel is my first passion.


  • A very close second passion is my love for Kansas City, Missouri, and anything having to do with it. Because of this, I am a big KC Sports fan. I like:
  • Kansas City Chiefs
  • Kansas City Royals (not scared to admit I root for the Royals!!!)
  • Kansas City Wizards
  • Kansas City Brigade
  • Kansas City Knights
  • Missouri Tigers


  • I also have a passion for Media and Broadcasting. I can't get enough news!
