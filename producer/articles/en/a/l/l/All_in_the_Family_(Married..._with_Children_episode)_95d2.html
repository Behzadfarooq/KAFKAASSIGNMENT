All in the Family (Married... with Children episode)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

This article is about the second season finale of Married... with Children. For other uses, see All in the Family (disambiguation).

"All in the Family" is the 22nd (and last) episode of season two of the television sitcom Married... with Children.

  • First Aired: Sunday May 1, 1988 on FOX.

[edit] Plot

Peg's hillbilly relatives from Wanker county pay a visit. Fighting uncles, singing triplet aunts, whose showbiz career is threatened by Al's imputs, and Al is trying all he can to watch the John Wayne film Hondo."
