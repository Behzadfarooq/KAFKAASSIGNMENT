All Time Greatest Movie Songs
Studio album by Various Artists
Released March 16, 1999
Genre Soundtrack
Label Sony

All Time Greatest Movie Songs is a joint effort with Columbia Records and is the first of 2 Sony Music movie compilations released in 1999.
