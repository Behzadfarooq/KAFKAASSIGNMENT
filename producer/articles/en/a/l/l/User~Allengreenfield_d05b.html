{information on publishers - none of the books listed are published by vanity publishing houses. IllumiNet Press published the original best-selling edition of "Secret Cipher of the UFOnauts". Dr. Greenfield has no connection with IllumiNet Press other than his contract between publisher and writer. "The Book of Lies" was published by Disinformation, Ltd. with the same relationship. Luxor Press is a publishing corporation in which Allen Greenfield once held stock, but no longer has any connection with other than former publisher- writer relations. Greenfield is not involved with Manutius Press, his current publisher, except as author in relationship to publisher. As part of his agreement with his currewnt publisher, he also serves as an advisor to the publishing house. The closest thing to a "vanity" publisher would be the 1975 "Saucers & Saucerers" which was self-published. }
