[edit] About Me

My name is Jason Lee. Hello. Ni hao. 你好. Douzo yoroshiku. (Please to meet you.) どうぞ宜しく.
I am a former Computer Science student that was studying at the University of California, Riverside.
My hometown in California is Cerritos, California. I keep an eye on stubs, and try to offer useful suggestions on articles here and there.
I am primarily a helper in organizing things; I am currently fixing and programming various bots that help maintain the Wikipedia.

Depending on the complexity of what I'm doing for a bot, it may take several hours, or it may take several weeks. It really depends on the mood I feel like devoting myself to programming.
I joined the Wikipedia on 19 August 2004, and have been an admin since 10 December 2004.
