All I Can Do (Chantal Kreviazuk)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

All I Can Do is the first single off of Chantal Kreviazuk's fourth album, Ghost Stories.

It can be heard on Kreviazuk's official website as well as on her myspace page, and it is currently being played on Canadian radio stations. Kreviazuk has claimed that she has received the most positive feedback from fans and radio in her career for this single.

The music video for "All I Can Do" premiered on Canada's music channel MuchMoreMusic on July 24, 2006 in a half-hour segment called "Inside The Video: Chantal Kreviazuk" which featured on-set interviews with the singer/songwriter as well as a behind-the-scenes look at the making of her new video.
