Alley Cats is the twenty-first episode of season one of the television situation comedy Will & Grace.

Spoiler warning: Plot and/or ending details follow.

[edit] Plot

After a particularly competitive evening of charades, Rob and Ellen confess that Grace's competitive nature has turned them off playing games. While Grace sets about trying to ease off of it, Will begins to realise that he likes winning, and wants Grace to be the same. Jack teaches Karen CPR, which comes in handy sooner rather than later.
