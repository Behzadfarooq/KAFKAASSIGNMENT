Allegheny West (Pittsburgh)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Allegheny West is a neighborhood in on Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania's northside. It has two zip codes of both 15233 and 15212, and has representation on Pittsburgh City Council by the council member for District 6 (Downtown, northshore).[1]
