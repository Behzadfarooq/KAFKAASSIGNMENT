"All Growed Up" is a 2001 telefilm and a special one-hour episode of Rugrats that aired during its 8th season on August 11, 2001 to celebrate the series' 10th anniversary. Its premise was to focus on what the babies' lives would be like if they were 10 years older.

[edit] Plot

Spoiler warning: Plot and/or ending details follow.

When the babies were watching a sci-fi oriented movie, Angelica shows them her new "tapiyokie" (karaoke) machine. She says the babies can't play with it, but they do so. Angelica is angry and chases the babies into a closet, with Angelica on the outside. Angelica starts counting to ten, and Tommy suggests they go to the "foocher" (future). And at the exact moment Angelica reaches ten, there is a swirling orange vortex and the babies see themselves in the future. Coincidentally, they are now ten years older.

Teenage Angelica demands her Emica CD back when the group comes out of the closet. In the beginning of this part of the episode, ten-year-old Dil is shown having small problems, and Angelica states that Phil and Lil dropped him on his head when he was a baby. Stu says that he has a disco dance on the same day that the group has their concert. Stu also states that he will wear his special zodiac necklace. Emica wears the same necklace.

When the group leaves for school, Tommy's grandfather is now a bus driver. Angelica's friend, Samantha Shane (whom Chuckie has a crush on), tells Angelica that she is going to the Emica concert. Angelica lies that she is going to wear the zodiac necklace that Emica (and Stu) wears. She needs Tommy to steal his father's necklace so she will look like she told the truth to her friend. Tommy makes a fake necklace (a round dog biscuit wrapped in gold foil with the zodiac sign drawing). Unexpectedly, Spike eats the decoy overnight and Stu finds out.

Tommy is grounded and he is going to miss the concert. The group is sitting in their old sandbox, feeling glum that their friend is going to miss the concert. Stu and Didi hire Susie to babysit Tommy while they are at the dance. Lil finds the necklace in the sandbox, and they need to return the necklace to Stu. Tommy is reluctant to do so, due to the fact that he mustn't leave the house. Dil encourages Tommy to do otherwise. They return Stu's necklace in the middle of the dance, and Stu can dance. They are now at the concert and the singer sings a song, with previous clips from the original Rugrats series playing. They are now babies again and they are glad that Angelica will be nicer to them in ten more years. Although, it seems short-lived after Angelica finds Dil's drool over her player and chases the babies for the rest of the episode.
