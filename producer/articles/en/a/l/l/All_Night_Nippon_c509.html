All Night Nippon is a Japanese radio program broadcast by Nippon Broadcasting System and other radio stations from 1-5 am (JST). It preempts broadcasts from TBS Radio's programming.

The highest ratings were achieved on the night of October 1, 1967.

[edit] DJs

The day of the week below are for the evening preceding in Japan, i.e. day in UTC.

  • Mondays – Saturdays, 16:00 UTC – 18:00 UTC with "All Night Nippon (オールナイトニッポン)" (aired on 36 radio stations in Japan)
    • Mondays: Takanori Nishikawa → Shumputei Shota → Toru Hidaka
    • Tuesdays: Cream Stew
    • Wednesdays: Aya Matsuura → Shun Oguri
    • Thursdays: Ninety-nine
    • Fridays: Masuda Okada
    • Saturdays: SHOGO (175R (Inago Rider))( - April 1, 2006) → Under Graph (April 8, 2006 - March 31, 2007)
  • Mondays – Thursdays, 18:00 UTC – 20:00 UTC with "All Night Nippon Ever Green (オールナイトニッポンエバーグリーン)" (aired on 9 radio stations in Japan)
    • Yasuhiro Saito (Anko)
  • Fridays, 18:00 UTC – 20:00 UTC with "All Night Nippon R (オリエンタルラジオのオールナイトニッポンR)" (aired on 9 radio stations in Japan)
    • Oriental Radio (Owarai)
  • Saturdays, 14:30 UTC – 16:00 UTC with "All Night Nippon Saturday Special Tamashii no Radio (福山雅治のオールナイトニッポンサタデースペシャル 魂のラジオ)" (aired on 33 radio stations in Japan)
    • Masaharu Fukuyama
  • Saturdays, 18:00 UTC – 19:30 UTC with "All Night Nippon R by Yamasato (南海キャンディーズ山里亮太のオールナイトニッポンR)" (aired on 21 radio stations in Japan)
    • Ryota Yamasato (Yama-chan, Nankai Candies)
  • Saturdays, 19:30 UTC – 20:00 UTC with "All Night Nippon Record (オールナイトニッポンレコード)"
    • Aired on 5 radio stations in Japan except JOLF (on STV Radio, Ibaraki Broadcast System, CRT Tochigi, KBS Kyoto, and KBC Radio)
    • Noriko Tomita (an announcer at JOLF)
  • The first Sunday every month, 19:00 UTC – 20:00 UTC with "All Night Nippon Ever Green Additional Broadcasting (オールナイトニッポンエバーグリーン 増刊号)" (aired only on JOLF, sometimes not aired because of the checking)
    • Yasuhiro Saito (Anko)
  • The second Sunday (in February, April, June, August, October, and December), 16:30 UTC – 20:00 UTC with "All Night Nippon Ever Green Special Additional Broadcasting (オールナイトニッポンエバーグリーン 特別増刊号)" (aired only on JOLF)
    • Yasuhiro Saito (Anko)
