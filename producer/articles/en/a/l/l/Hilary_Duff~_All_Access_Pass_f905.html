Hilary Duff: All Acess Pass is the first music DVD to be released by Hilary Duff. The 60-minute DVD was released on November 4, 2003 and features:

  • Music videos for "I Can't Wait", "Why Not" and "So Yesterday".
  • Making the music videos for "Why Not" and "So Yesterday".
  • The making of the Metamorphosis album.
  • Acoustic song performances as seen on Sessions@AOL.
  • Photo gallery, home movies and biography.
