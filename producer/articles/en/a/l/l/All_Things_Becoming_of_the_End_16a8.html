Studio album by Submersed
Genre Alternative Metal

Submersed chronology
Demo CD All Things Becoming of the End In Due Time


Submersed's debut album, tentatively titled "All Things Becoming of the End". They brought with them a whole host of songs, mostly old and some new. The album was recorded and mixed, but eventually scrapped as the band began writing new material that they felt was far better than anything on All Things. This is a list of songs recorded in the ATBOTE sessions. MP3's of these recordings can be found if one looks hard enough
