The Alliance of the New Citizen (Slovak: Aliancia Nového Občana, ANO) is a liberal party in Slovakia. The abbreviation ANO also means YES in Slovak. It was a member of the government coalition from October 2002 to September 2005. Its leader Pavol Rusko is the founder and former owner of Markíza (the biggest commercial TV channel in Slovakia) and he was the Minister of Economics from September 24, 2003 to August 24, 2005. In the parliamentary election of 17 june 2006, the party won only 1.42% of the popular vote and lost parliamentary representation.

It is a liberal party and connected with the European Liberal, Democrat and Reform Party.
