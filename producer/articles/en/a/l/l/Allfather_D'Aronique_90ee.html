'Allfather D'Aronique' is a character from the comic book series "Preacher," created by Garth Ennis and Steve Dillon. He is French, and his first name is never given.

He was the leader of the Grail organization, one occasion Allfather stating "I am the hundred and twelfth Allfather of the Grail". Extremely obese, he was barely mobile and at one point his weight caused a small plane's wheel to fall off during landing (although Herr Starr replies to it "Every Fucking time.", so it seems to be regular) D'Aronique was also bulimic, retaining a stick with the shape of two human fingers that he would put in his mouth upon every meal and vomit (though the Allfather's bulimia seems to be closer to the Roman practice of vomiting so as to enable more eating than it was a weight-control method). He is depicted as a ruthless and sadistic murderer, albeit very pious and fully believing in the cause of The Grail and the potential of the messiah. The messiah being the retarded, inbred alleged descendent of Jesus Christ, who was under the full control of the Grail. He is also fond of manipulation and is somewhat egomaniacal; he required multiple world leaders (including the American President) to call him daily and thank him for preserving their power.

He has a habit of speaking in oddly-constructed sentences, often using poetic reduplication (ie: "You wonder, you wonder, why do you wonder"). He is related to Jesse Custer and the L'Angelles, being Jesse's distant uncle.

Unfortunately, for Jesse, he had killed his own evil grandmother, who was a favored relative of D'Aronique.

Allfather D'Aronique died in Herr Starr's coup of the Grail. While attempting to escape the collapsing Grail compound in a helicopter Herr Starr kicked him out while in mid-air. D'Aronique landed on the Grail's messiah and a nearbly soldier, killing all three instantly. Starr subsequently inherited the title of Allfather (assassination being an acceptable form of self-promotion), becoming the Grail's 113th. He was also the last, as deepening insanity soon doomed the group.
