All Guns Poolside is an album by the now defunct hardcore punk band, Redemption 87.

It was released in 1998 and subsequently re-released in 2002 by Blackout Records – with added bonus tracks which were the bands original demo from 1995.

It features vocalist, Eric Ozenne, who went on to form The Nerve Agents and Jade Puget who is now guitarist with another hardcore punk band, AFI.
