Category:All-Australians

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

This is a listing of all Australian rules football players to have been selected in an All-Australian team. From 1953 to 1988 selection of the official All-Australian team was by representatives of the various state and territorial teams at national carnivals. Since 1991, following the VFL's conversion to a national competition and its renaming as the Australian Football League (AFL), the team has been chosen exclusively from AFL players each season.
