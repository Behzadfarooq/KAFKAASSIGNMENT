All or Nothing is a 2002 drama film written and directed by Mike Leigh. It is typical of Leigh's work. Set in present-day London, the film revolves around three working class families and the depiction of their everyday lives. It was well-received by critics and audiences alike receiveing an 82% fresh rating on rottentomatoes.com and an average B+ grade on Yahoo! Movies. It also won the London Film Critics Cricle Award for best British film of the year and Mike Leigh was nominated for best Director at the European Film Awards.

[edit] Plot

Spoiler warning: Plot and/or ending details follow.

The film begins with a day nearing end. Rachel, the daughter in the first family is shown working in a nursing home. Phil, the father in the first family is shown driving people around in his taxi. Penny, the mother in the first family is shown working as a cashier at Safeway alongside Maureen, the mother in the second family. When Penny gets off of work, she bikes home to find her eighteen year-old son, Rory in a fight with a chav for taking his football. Rory is a lazy, obese, ill-mannered, teenager that stays at home all day and doesn't work. Complications with Rory's obesity arise when a gang of chavs playing "Keep Away" with Rory, he ultimatley runs out of breath, begins to hyperventilate and must be hospitalised because of a heart attack.

The second family consisted of Maureen, a cashier at Safeway and her daughter Donna, a cashier at a delicatessen. A brief storyline involves a dispute with Donna's boyfriend, Jason. Donna didn't take her birth control pills as Jason expected her to which resulted in Donna getting pregnant. this was followed by a large argument between Maureen, Jason and Donna.

The third family consisted of Ron who is a taxi driver along with Phil. He is the father of Samantha, an unemployed teenage girl and the husband of Carol, an unemployed alcoholic woman who is always shown intoxicated.

The film doesn't have one single storyline but several. All are leading up to the climax, when Rory has a heart attack and the rest of the film involves only the first family and their crisis of faith.
