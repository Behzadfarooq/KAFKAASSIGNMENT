All is Violent, All is Bright
Studio album by God is an Astronaut
Released 2005
Genre Post-rock
Label Revive Records
God is an Astronaut chronology
The End of the Beginning (2002) All is Violent, All is Bright (2005) A Moment of Stillness (EP) (2006)


All is Violent, All is Bright is the second studio album by Irish post-rock band God is an Astronaut.

[edit] Track listing

  1. "Fragile" – 4:34
  2. "All Is Violent, All Is Bright" – 4:14
  3. "Forever Lost" – 6:22
  4. "Fire Flies and Empty Skies" – 3:55
  5. "A Deafening Distance" – 3:49
  6. "Infinite Horizons" – 2:28
  7. "Suicide by Star" – 4:38
  8. "Remembrance Day" – 4:16
  9. "Dust and Echoes" – 4:13
  10. "When Everything Dies" – 10:00
    • The song "Halo of Flies" is contained on the last track after a minute or so of silence.

The enhanced CD also contains an mp3 of the track "Disturbance" 3:43.
