All Blacks at the Rugby World Cup

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

You have new messages (last change).

The All Blacks have played a total of 31 matches in the five Rugby World Cup tournaments. They are the only team to have played a semi-final in all five World Cups.

[edit] By position

  • 1987 First
  • 1991 Third
  • 1995 Second
  • 1999 Fourth
  • 2003 Third
