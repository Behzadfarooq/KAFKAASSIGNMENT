Allenport is an unincorporated village located in Huntingdon County, Pennsylvania, South East of Mount Union on Juniata River in Shirley Township.

[edit] Geography

Allenport is located at 40°22′0″N, 77°52′0″W (40.37361, -77.87028)GR1.
