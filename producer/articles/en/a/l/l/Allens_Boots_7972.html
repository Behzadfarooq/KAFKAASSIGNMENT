Allens Boots is a retail store in Austin, Texas that specializes in western wear. The store offers items such as boots, hats, jeans and shirts. Allens Boots opened its doors in 1977; the big, red boot above the entrance has since made the store easy to spot among the many other boutiques and shops along South Congress Avenue.

[edit] Allens Boots trivia

Allens Boots is a popular shopping destination for famous musicians, movie stars and celebrities.

Allens Boots doesn't have an apostrophe in its name.
