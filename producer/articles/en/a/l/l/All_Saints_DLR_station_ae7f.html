All Saints DLR station is a station on the Docklands Light Railway (DLR) in East London. It is named after the neaby All Saints, Poplar, a church dating from 1821-3. The station entrance is on the East India Dock Road.

There was a previous station on the same site, called Poplar station, which was served by the North London Railway. The location of this station can be seen in the bottom-right hand corner of the Map of Poplar, 1885, referred to under External links.

The station is located on the DLR's Stratford branch, between Poplar and Devons Road stations, and is in Travelcard Zone 2.

About 1/4 of a mile north of this station is the proposed Langdon Park DLR station.
