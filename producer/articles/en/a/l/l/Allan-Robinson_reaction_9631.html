Allan-Robinson reaction

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

You have new messages (last change).

The Allan-Robinson reaction is the chemical reaction of o-hydroxyaryl ketones with aromatic anhydrides to form flavones (or isoflavones).[1][2][3]
