Alluda Majaka

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

DVD Cover.

Alluda Majaka (Telugu: అల్లుడా మజాకా) is a popular telugu movie produced by Devi Vara Prasad on Devi Films banner with Chiranjeevi in the lead role. The movie was directed by E.V.V. Satyanarayana and was released in 1995, at a time when Chiranjeevi was going through a lean phase. Eventhough it gave him a much needed hit, the movie had a lot of double entendres. And eventhough there were other movies with much worse dialogues, this movie drew a lot of flak since Chiranjeevi has a huge following among family audiences as well.
