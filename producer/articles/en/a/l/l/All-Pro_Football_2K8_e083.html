All-Pro Football 2K8 (abbreviated APF 2K8) is a football game for seventh generation consoles, and the first football game to be published by 2K Sports since EA Sports purchased exclusive licenses to the intellectual properties of the NFL and NFLPA; the last football game published by 2K Sports was NFL 2K5.

[edit] Speculations

There was a rumor that 2K Sports would circumvent the NFL license with Legends Football 2K(x) [1], which would feature all-time NFL greats; however, it seems that APF 2K8 may feature fictionalized teams and players, presumably with a franchise mode. Visual Concepts President Greg Thomas hinted in an interview that it would be a cross between Madden and Blitz: The League. It is rumored that nearly everything in the game will be customizable, which would allow players to make the game as close to a licensed NFL title as possible if they chose to do so.
