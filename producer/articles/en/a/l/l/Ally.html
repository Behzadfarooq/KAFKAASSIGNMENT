[edit] General

Ally may refer to:

  • A member of an alliance
    • Allies of World War I
    • Allies of World War II
    • Major non-NATO ally, a U.S. designation for close allies having strong working relationships with U.S. forces, not members NATO
    • The Neutral Ally, a reference to Norway during World War I
  • Ally, nickname for Allison, Alexandra, et al.
  • Ally (shamanism), a spiritual entity that assists a Shaman or sorcerer
  • Ally & Gargano, former American advertising agency (1962-1991), see Carl Ally
  • Ally Week, a national youth-led effort empowering students to be allies against anti-LGBT
  • Straight ally, a colloquial term that describes a heterosexual person who conscientiously supports equal civil rights, gender equality, and LGBT social movements
  • A slang term for someone who plays on the Alliance faction in World of Warcraft

[edit] Science and technology

  • Fern ally, a general term covering a somewhat diverse group of vascular plants that are not flowering plants and not true ferns
  • Plant ally, plants grown near crop plants for the purpose of providing some benefit to the crop
