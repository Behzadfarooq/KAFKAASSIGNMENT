All of Me is a 7" single by NOFX. The title track is a cover of an old Tin Pan Alley song most associated with Billie Holiday and Louis Armstrong. The title song was also featured on the compilation 45 or 46 Songs That Weren't Good Enough to Go on Our Other Records and on the [[Fat Wreck Chords] Sampler Physical Fatness - Fat Music Vol. III under the title "Olive Me".

The record was limited to 8,000 copies on aqua-colored vinyl (or, as the band referred to it, "ice cream colored").
