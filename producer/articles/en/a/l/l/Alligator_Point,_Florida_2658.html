Alligator Point, Florida

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Alligator Point is a town in Franklin County, Florida, United States. It is located along US 98, on the western shore of Dickerson Bay, near the Gulf of Mexico.

[edit] Geography

Alligator Point is located at 29°48′28″N, 84°51′34″W (29.8078, -84.8594).GR1
