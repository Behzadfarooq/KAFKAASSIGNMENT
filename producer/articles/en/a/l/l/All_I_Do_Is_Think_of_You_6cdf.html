"All I Do Is Think Of You" was a 1975 soul single released by The Jackson 5. The song is about a young man who tries to tell a young woman he met in school his real feelings about her.

The classic soul ballad was written by Edward Holland, Jr. and Michael Lovesmith and was released as a b-side to the group's single, "Forever Came Today". The song was so memorable that it would be covered by a lot of R&B artists. The most famous cover was by new-jack swing group Troop, released in 1990, which went to #1 on the Billboard R&B chart and recently released by teen group B5 in 2005. The original reached #50 on the R&B chart.
