Allisyn Ashley Arm (born April 25, 1996) is an American child actress. Allisyn made her acting debut in 2002 on the television drama series Strong Medicine; she made her movie debut 2 years later in Eulogy. She has also appeared in over 40 commercials.

[edit] Filmography

  • Eulogy (2004) playing Collins Daughter
  • Man of the House (2005) playing Charlotte Tulaire
  • Mr. Woodcock (2006) (post-production) playing Little Girl Scout Zoe

[edit] TV guest appearances

  • Strong Medicine playing Wendy in episode Outcomes (season 3 episode 2) July 28, 2002
  • Miracles playing Little Girl/Amelia Wye in episode Little Miss Lost (season 1 episode 4) March 3, 2003
  • Friends playing Leslie Buffay in episode The One Where Ross Is Fine (season 10 episode 2) October 2 2003
  • Judging Amy playing Molly Maddox in episode Ex Parte of Five (season 5 episode 3) October 7 2003
  • 10-8: Officers on Duty playing Little Wailing Girl in epiosde Late for School (season 1 episode 7) November 9 2003
  • Still Standing playing Ella in episode Still Holding (season 3 episode 20) April 18 2005
  • Inconceivable playing Lexie in episode To Surrogate, with Love (season 1 episode 3) October 7 2005
