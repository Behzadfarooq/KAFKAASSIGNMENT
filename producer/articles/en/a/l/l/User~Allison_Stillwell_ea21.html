I'm a CNA at a nursing home in Middle Tennessee, and am starting nursing school this fall after spending my entire summer in Ghana. If all goes as planned, I'll get my NP within the next four years and hope to work with Médecins Sans Frontières in Africa.

I live in Brentwood, Tennessee with my parents, younger sister, seven domestic cats, a Golden Retriever, and a Llewellin Setter. My Golden Retriever, Bellamy, is a therapy dog in training.

Most articles that I edit have to do with geriatrics, nursing, or domestic animals. I've started two articles, Cytauxzoonosis and Carte Jaune, and have majorly edited others such as Bedsores.
