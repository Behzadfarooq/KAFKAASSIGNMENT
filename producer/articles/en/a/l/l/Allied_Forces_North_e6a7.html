Allied Forces North

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Allied Forces North (AFNORTH) was the most northern NATO headquarter located at Kolsås outside Oslo. The base is now used by the high command of the Royal Norwegian Defense Forces.

AFCENT, located in Brunssum the Netherlands, was renamed AFNORTH. In the year 2004, it was again renamed JFC.

AFNORTH is also a school that is just outside the current JFC base. It kept the name AFNORTH because of the hassle and money required to change all of the legal documents.
