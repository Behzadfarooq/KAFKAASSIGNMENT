Allann Petitjean

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Allann Petitjean (born July 31, 1974 in Luxeuil-les-Bains) is a French football midfielder currently playing for French Championnat National side Nîmes Olympique.

His previous clubs include FC Sochaux, Amiens SC, CS Sedan and Stade Reims.
