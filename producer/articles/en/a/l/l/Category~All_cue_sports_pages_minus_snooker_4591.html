This is a maintenance category for WikiProject Cue sports. All talk pages tagged with the {{Cue sports project}} or {{Cue sports project2}} templates will appear here, including articles, categories, templates, etc. It is a god's-eye view of the entire projectspace.

When creating a new article (or stub-tagging an existing article, etc.), or creating a category or template, that is under the purview of this WikiProject, please immediately add one of the above-mentioned templates to its talk page (creating that page in the process if necessary) so that the page appears here.

[edit] See also

  • Category:All cue sports pages including snooker for an even more complete view of cue sports pages, including those maintained by WikiProject Snooker
  • Category:All snooker pages for only those maintained by WikiProject Snooker (other than where the subjects overlap and have been flagged as both cue sports and snooker).
