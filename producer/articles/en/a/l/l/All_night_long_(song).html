all night long was the third single released by Japanese J-Pop singer Ami Suzuki in November, 1998.

[edit] Information

Once again the full single was produced by Globe's member Tetsuya Komuro, even the remixes were made by him. The song was pretty different from their previous releases, being a House-like dance song, a really catching pop-house tune. The song was used in TV commercials of Japanese products called "Kissmark".

When Ami went to perform to ASAYAN (where she won the deal with Sony) for the first time after she started her music career, she performed this song.

The song peaked at #2 in the Oricon charts, and Ami Suzuki won one of the golden prize at the Japan Record Awards later in 1998 for this song.
