"All We Know" is the third and final single from Paramore's debut album All We Know Is Falling. Released in 2006, the single was released in the UK on Monday 11th of December, the video got air time on Kerrang! TV and MTV2.

[edit] Music Video

A live video is present, filmed at a concert on their Paramore Tour.

[edit] Trivia

  • Many people confuse the Dandobi Live Video with their music video, this was only for promotion.
  • The cover art for the 2nd edition of the promo was shot in London.
