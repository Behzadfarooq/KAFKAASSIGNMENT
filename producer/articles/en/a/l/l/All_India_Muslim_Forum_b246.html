All India Muslim Forum is a Muslim political party in India. The president is Nihaluddin and the general secretary is Dr. M. K. Sherwani. The Forum is staunchly opposed to the Hindutva nationalism of BJP.

The Forum oppose U.S. occupation of Iraq.

The Forum demands that 4% of the parliamentary seats should be reserved for Muslim women.

The Forum has collaboration with Communist Party of India (Marxist-Leninist) Liberation.

In the 1999 Lok Sabha elections AIMF had launched three candidates, who together mustered 10 010 votes.
