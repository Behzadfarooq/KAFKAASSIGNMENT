All Nippon Airways destinations

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

This is a list of airports that All Nippon Airways flies to, correct as at January 2007.

The new CEO of ANA has laid out a future expansion plan, including resuming services to India, Malaysia, Indonesia, and new service to the Russian Far East.
