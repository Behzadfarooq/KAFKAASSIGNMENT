[edit] Making Of Apmso

Image:Altaf Hussain 2003-apmso25-4.jpg
altaf hussain and others present on the foundation of apmso.

(APMSO) was a student political organization founded by Altaf Hussain in Karachi, Sindh, Pakistan. All Pakistan Muhajir Students Organisation was founded on in June 11, 1978. APMSO started gaining support of Urdu speaking students Muhajirs but were not able to make their position in the hot students political tug between pro-Communism and pro-Islam students organisations with massive support of students. It was only after the ban on students union and stoppage of ideoligical discussion in institution by the then military regime lead by General Zia ul Haque . The ban brought a prosperous period for regeonal, ethnic and sectarian groups. The military regime very strategically replaced the voice of people by the voices of groups which was their great success. However, APMSO faced a strong opposition from Islami Jamiat-e-Talaba, main opponent and student wing of Jamaat-e-Islami. APMSO was converted to Mttahida students (replacing mohajir to muttahida) in June 2006 by its leader Altaf Hussain when he announced to become a national political group instead of a regional and linguistic group. APMSO is blamed for killing dozens of students belonging to opponent groups Islami Jamiat-e-Talaba and Peoples Students Federation.
