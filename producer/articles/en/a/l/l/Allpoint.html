Allpoint is an interbank network in the United States connecting ATMs in all 50 states. It advertises itself as being "America's Largest Surcharge-Free ATM Network", with "1 in every 12 ATMs" being part of its network. It is the youngest of the American interbank networks, having been founded only in 2003 and also being the first network to be founded in over 20 years.[1]
