Allatou
Publisher Marvel Comics
First appearance Marvel Spotlight #18 (Oct 1974)

Characteristics
Species Demon

Allatou is a fictional character appearing in the Marvel Comics universe. She is a demon who has clashed with the West Coast Avengers.
