All the Way (song)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

"All the Way" is a popular song.

The music was written by James Van Heusen, the lyrics by Sammy Cahn. The song was published in 1957 by Maraville Music Corp.

It was introduced by Frank Sinatra in the movie The Joker Is Wild. Sinatra also had the best-selling recorded version of the song. Aside from this song,he also sang Chicago (That Toddlin' Town) for the movie.It wound up as the flipside of All The Way when Capitol Records released the song as a single.

The song received the 1957 Academy Award for Best Original Song.

Tierney Sutton also did a cover of this single on her album, "Dancing in the Dark," a compilation of Sinatra tunes.
