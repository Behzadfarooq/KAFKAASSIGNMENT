Allen Jenkins (April 9, 1900 – July 20, 1974), originally Alfred McGonegal, was a character actor born on Staten Island, New York.

He was known for playing mob henchmen, policemen, and other "tough guy" roles in comedy films of the 1930s and 1940s. He also is remembered as the voice of Officer Dibble on the TV cartoon Top Cat. He was a regular on the television show "Hey, Jeannie!" in 1956.
