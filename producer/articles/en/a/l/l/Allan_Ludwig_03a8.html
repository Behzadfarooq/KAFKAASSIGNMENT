Allan Ludwig

Allan Ludwig (b. April 1977) is an American artist who uses the Golden Ratio to create dynamic foregrounds and visual proportions.

Ludwig was born in Orlando, Florida. He received a Bachelor of Arts from Brigham Young University and a Masters of Arts from Claremont Graduate University. He has a significant following in Southern California, and has exhibited paintings nationwide.[1]

His best-known paintings are in a collection called "Into the Wilderness".[citation needed]
