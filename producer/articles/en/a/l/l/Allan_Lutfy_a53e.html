Allan Lutfy

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Allan Lutfy QC (born November 21, 1943 in Montreal, Quebec) is the current Chief Justice of the Federal Court.

He studied at Loyola College and McGill University before being called to the Quebec Bar in 1968. He was appointed Queen's Counsel in 1983. He was appointed as a judge on the Federal Court in 1996, made Associate Chief Justice in 1999 and was appointed Chief Justice in 2003 after the passing of the Courts Administration Service Act.
