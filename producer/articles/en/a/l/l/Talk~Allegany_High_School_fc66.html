[edit] Vandalism

This page was mostly vandalism. I've removed a whole bunch of non-notable groups and clubs, and some photos that, besides being non-notable, appear to be school photos which are copyrighted by the photographer. I'm adding this page to my watchlist, but will be happy for help in keeping it free of vandalism. -FisherQueen 23:05, 27 November 2006 (UTC)

[edit] On the cheerleading photos

The copyright information on the cheerleading photos identifies them as a 'sports poster of low resolution.' But they are clearly not a sports poster, nor are they of low resolution. They appear to be the traditional school group picture, which would be copyrighted by the photographer. If there's someone out there who knows more about Wikipedia image use policy and can take a second look at them, it would be helpful. For now, I've removed them, because I really, really don't think they are fair use as described by the information on Wiki's image help pages. -FisherQueen 12:48, 28 November 2006 (UTC)
