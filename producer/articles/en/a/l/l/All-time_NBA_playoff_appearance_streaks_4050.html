The following is an all-time list of National Basketball Association teams with more than ten consecutive seasons appearing in the playoffs:

21 seasons

  • Portland Trail Blazers (1982-83—2002-03)

20 seasons

  • Utah Jazz (1983-84—2002-03)

19 seasons

  • Boston Celtics (1950-51—1968-69)

17 seasons

  • Los Angeles Lakers (1976-77—1992-93)

16 seasons

  • Minneapolis/Los Angeles Lakers (1958-59—1973-74)

14 seasons

  • Boston Celtics (1979-80—1992-93)
  • Chicago Bulls (1984-85—1997-98)
  • Ft. Wayne/Detroit Pistons (1945-50 BBA/NBA season—1962-63)
  • New York Knicks (1987-88—2000-01)

13 seasons

  • Phoenix Suns (1988-89—2000-01)

12 seasons

  • Baltimore/Capital/Washington Bullets (1968-69—1979-80)
  • Denver Rockets/Nuggets (1967-68 ABA season—1978-79)
  • Milwaukee Bucks (1978-79—1990-91)
  • Philadelphia 76ers (1975-76—1986-87)

11 seasons

  • St. Louis/Atlanta Hawks (1962-63—1972-73)
