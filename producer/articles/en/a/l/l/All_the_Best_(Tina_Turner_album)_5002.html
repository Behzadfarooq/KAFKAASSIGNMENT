All The Best is a greatest hits album by Tina Turner, released in Europe in November 2004, and in the United States in February 2005.

The tracklist concentrates on Turner's solo hits, but also features a few highlights from her partnership with Ike Turner and some new recordings.

The album peaked at #6 in the UK Albums Chart and at #2 on the Billboard 200 albums chart making All the Best Tina Turner's highest debut in the United States. In March 2007 the Album re-entered the UK Charts and peaked at #18.
