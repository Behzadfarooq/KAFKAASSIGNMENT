Allentown

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Allentown is the name of some places in the United States of America:

  • Allentown, Georgia, a town in Wilkinson County
  • Allentown, New Jersey, a borough in Monmouth County
  • Allentown, Pennsylvania, a city in Lehigh County
    • Allentown-Bethlehem-Easton metropolitan area, the area of which Allentown, Pennsylvania is part of
    • Allentown, a neighborhood on the southside of Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania
  • Allentown, Washington, a neighborhood in Tukwila
  • Allentown, New York, the name of more than one location in New York, including:
    • Allentown, New York, a hamlet in the Town of Alma in Allegany County
    • Allentown, New York, a hamlet in the Town of Hadley in Saratoga County
    • Allentown, a neighborhood in Buffalo, New York
  • Allenstown, New Hampshire, with an added "s"
  • See also Allen
  • "Allentown" (song), a song penned by singer Billy Joel about Bethlehem, Pennsylvania
