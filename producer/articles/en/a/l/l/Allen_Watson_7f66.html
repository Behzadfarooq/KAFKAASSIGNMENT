Allen Kenneth Watson (born November 18, 1970 in Jamaica, New York) is a former Left handed Major League starting pitcher who spent his career with the St. Louis Cardinals, San Francisco Giants, Anaheim Angels, New York Mets, Seattle Mariners and New York Yankees. His trade from the Giants to the Angels was the trade that brought long-time Giants first baseman J.T. Snow to San Francisco.
