< User:Allen3 | Templates

"Cadet Grey"

Design philosophy was based on grouping the templates into three categories:

  1. Templates requesting action ({{peerreview}}, {{fac}}, {{farc}}). A splash of color has been added to these templates to help draw attention.
  2. Highlighted informational templates ({{featured}}, {{press}}, {{chemistry}}). Use of a lighter color used to help highlight these items.
  3. Informational templates ({{comment}}, {{oldpeerreview}}, {{oldvfd}}, {{facfailed}}). Base color combination.

Other major considerations were minimizing blank screen space and keeping a unified feel to all the templates.

This design based its wording on Violet by User:Violetriga.
