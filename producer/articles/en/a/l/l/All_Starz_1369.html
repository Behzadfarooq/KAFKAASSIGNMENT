The All Starz are a fictional team of Beybladers in the anime series Beyblade. The team includes Michael Parker (the team leader), Emily York, Eddy and Steven. Note that the All Starz also got a lot of support from Max's mom Judy and a group of Beyblade scientists. The whole team is sport-themed, as every member is a professional player in a different sport, and blade in the style of their sport. In G-Revolution, the team is renamed Project Power Beyblade All Starz.

The team was America's representative in the Beyblade world championships in the first season. By being the top American team, they had a huge success until they lost against the Bladebreakers in the American finals after Michael lost a match against Max, eliminating them from the tournament. The All Starz were using digital methods to improve their blading as seen in the first season, and some consider this as being a type of cheating; however the matter was not discussed whatsoever in the anime itself.

The All Starz reappear in the third season, Beyblade G-Revolution, with a modified player line-up, excluding Steven and adding Max Tate and Rick Anderson. On the first chapters, Rick win agains Michael, this victory bring him the main place in the team, along with Max, who win against Emily. At first, Rick doesn't want to cooperate with Max, and only want to win by himself, but at the end of the series, he learn a few things about teamwork.

BBA Revolutions eventually eliminates the All Starz from the championship. In the BEGA saga, Max wins a place on the new team G-Revolutions, and Michael and Emily brings them the pieces for new beyblades.
