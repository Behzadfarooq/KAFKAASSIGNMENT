Allison Lozano (born Allison Marian Lozano Núñez on August 11, 1992 in Chihuahua, Chihuahua) is a well-known Mexican actress and singer. She is best known for her role in the Mexican telenovela Mision S.O.S as Diana Lozano and her role in Rebelde as Bianca Delight.
