Allsvenskan (literally, "The All Swedish") is since the 2007-08 the second highest level of bandy in Sweden and comprises 30 teams in three groups. Allsvenskan succeeded Allsvenskan and Elitserien.

[edit] Season structure

The 30 teams in the league are divided into three groups, set up by geographical belonging. The first two teams in each group qualifies for a play-off round robin tournament against the eleventh and twelfth team of Elitserien. The ninth team plays a relegation play-off against teams from the Division 1 and the last team are relegated.
