All Hallows is an EP by AFI released on 5th October 1999 on Nitro Records. It contained three original songs and a cover of the Misfits song Halloween. A music video was later made for the song Totalimmortal.

[edit] Track listing

  1. "Fall Children" – 3:12
  2. "Halloween" (Misfits Cover) – 1:42
  3. "The Boy Who Destroyed the World" – 3:05
  4. "Totalimmortal" – 2:44

[edit] Miscellanea

  • On the inner-edge of the CD case is a hidden message:
Boo!
  • Such hidden messages are present on many of AFI's releases on Nitro Records.
  • "The Boy Who Destroyed The World" was included in the videogame "Tony Hawk's Pro Skater 3".
  • Totalimmortal was covered by The Offspring for the soundtrack to the film Me, Myself and Irene (2000).
