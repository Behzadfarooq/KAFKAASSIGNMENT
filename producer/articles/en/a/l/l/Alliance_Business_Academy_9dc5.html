The Alliance Business Academy (ABA) is a business school located in Bangalore, Karnataka, India. It was founded in 1996 and offers programs in management and information technology.

Currently ranked by various ranking agencies among the Top 15 B-schools in INDIA, it is an efficiently run organisation providing quality education and nurturing the future managers of the country
