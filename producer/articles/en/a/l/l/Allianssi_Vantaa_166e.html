Allianssi Vantaa

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Allianssi Vantaa is a football club situated in the city of Vantaa in Finland. Allianssi Vantaa is not the same club as the former Finnish football club, AC Allianssi.

Allianssi Vantaa was founded in 2006 and they currently play in the fourth highest league in Finland, Kolmonen (division III). The club has a total of almost 4000 youth players at clubs that are affiliated with Allianssi in the city of Vantaa, such as KOPSE, KoiPS, TiPS and Vantaan Jalkapalloseura. Timo Askolin is the headcoach of Allianssi Vantaa.

Allianssi Vantaa's homeground is called Pohjola Stadion. The stadium is situated in Myyrmäki and its capacity is 4,700. Pohjola Stadion was built in 2000. The stadium was also the homeground of AC Allianssi.
