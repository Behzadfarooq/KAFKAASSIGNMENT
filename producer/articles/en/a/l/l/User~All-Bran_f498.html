[edit] Apology

I apologize if you felt attacked by my comments. On the one hand, I think that you and I as everyone use sentences that could be considered attacks. The comment to your personal page was a joke, and I regret that you felt offended by it. Please accept my sincere apologies. On the other hand, I will ask you to be open about the contributions that others make, I love this encyclopedia because it gives us the unique and wonderful opportunity to make it a melting pot where everyone can give an opinion and every voice and point of view has a space. Regretfully, however, sometimes there are chauvinists that are unwilling and are unable to accept criticism or the points of view that others have, and do not even consider the other's ideas; the aftermath of that is a loss for everyone. I feel sad that some of the points that I wanted to expose with my contribution, were disregarded for purely personal reasons, without much consideration. For example; Hari is really a good sport, and very well intentioned, but many times he gives opinions that he considers to be facts and that is just not appropriate. (Believe it or not, I like him very much, and I admire that even after a heated argument he is willing to consider my opinion, and sometimes even embrace it). I try to participate as much as I can, but I can assure you that I never jump to talk or article to write about something I do not know (or that I have not researched). Noam Chomsky said, "freedom of speech is not about defending your points of view, but those of the ones that disagree with you". I will try to "keep it real" Andy Rosenthal 03:44, 20 December 2006 (UTC)
