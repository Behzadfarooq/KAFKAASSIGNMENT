"All In A Family" (四海一家) was an immensely popular Hong Kong drama that first screened in 1994. It was based on the British television series 'Til Death Us Do Part.

[edit] All In A Family - cast and roles

  • Chan Chun Wah - Father/Mr Tang
  • Siu-ha Chan - Autumn
  • Angela Chow - Jackie
  • Andrew Johnston - Cameron
  • Priscilla Koo - Spring
  • Wilson Lam - Summer
  • Scudder Smith - Scudder
  • Pierre Tremblay - John
  • May Tse - Mother/Mrs Tang
