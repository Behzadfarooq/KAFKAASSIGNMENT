Allen Jeardeau

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Allen W. Jeardeau was the head football coach at Louisiana State University from 1896 to 1897. In 1896, his first season with the Tigers, he led the team to a 6-0-0 record and a Southern Intercollegiate Athletic Association conference championship. He finished his coaching career with an overall record of 7-1-0. Jeardeau was a graduate of Harvard University.
