All Souls (TV series)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

All Souls was an American television program broadcast on UPN, first aired in 2001. The program was then cancelled in late August 2001 due to low ratings. The series will begin airing on The Sci Fi Channel in late 2006. [1]
