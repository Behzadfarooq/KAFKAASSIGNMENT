Allen Danzinger (born July 12, 1957), is an actor, known for his only movie role, as Jerry, in The Texas Chainsaw Massacre (1974).

Danzinger was born in Boston, Massachusetts. He currently owns a company that rents out strippers. He recently participated in a documentry on The Texas Chainsaw Massacre, for the movie's DVD release.

Danzinger's character in The Texas Chainsaw Massacre, Jerry, is pretty average, with nothing really setting his character apart from anyone else, and is perhaps the most levelheaded in the van. Jerry is killed by Leatherface, when he goes to look for his already dead friends Pam, and Kirk. Jerry is the protagonist Sally's boyfriend.
