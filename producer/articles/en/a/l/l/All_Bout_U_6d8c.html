"All Bout U" is a single by the prolific rapper Tupac Shakur. It was released off his legendary rap album All Eyez on Me. It was made into 2 videos. The first one was a solo by Shakur. The second one contained all the guest artists. The song samples "Candy" by Cameo and features Nate Dogg singing the chorus.

The music video was released in 1996, as well as the album that featured All Bout U, Tupac Shakur's All Eyez On Me. How ever it was re-released on vinyl, on October 14th, 2005. The vinyl also contained Tupac's Thug Passion.

In 1998 the song was included on 2Pac's Greatest Hits. This version was listed as "All About U" and features a verse by Top Dogg which the All Eyez on Me version didn't.

The Game pays a homage in his single Wouldn't Get Far after the final verse, he can be heard talking in a similar style as Snoop Dogg.
