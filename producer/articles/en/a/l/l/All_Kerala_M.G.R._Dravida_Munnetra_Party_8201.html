All Kerala M.G.R. Dravida Munnetra Party, a political party working amongst the Tamil minority in the Indian state of Kerala. AKMDMP was formed as a splint of All India Anna Dravida Munnetra Kazhagam. AKMDMP supported the widow of M.G. Ramachandran, Janaki Ramachandran.
