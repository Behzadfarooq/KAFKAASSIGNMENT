All Ages is Bad Religion's compilation album, released on November 7, 1995. It contains songs from 1988 to 1992 plus one song from their first album How Could Hell Be Any Worse? and two live tracks recorded live during their 1994 European tour. It does not have any songs from later albums – Recipe For Hate and Stranger Than Fiction – because they were released for Atlantic Records.
