Allieu Kondewa

Allieu Kondewa is a former traditional doctor in the Kamajors tribe and military commander of the Civil Defence Forces in Sierra Leone. He was born in the Bo District and lived served until his arrest and subsequent trial for war crimes under the Special Court for Sierra Leone. He has been on trial since 3 June 2004. He has been tried alongside his fellow ranking members of the CDF, Moinina Fofana and Samuel Hinga Norman.

[edit] Trial

If found guilty, Kondewa may be sentenced to prison or have his property confiscated. The Court does not have the power to impose the death penalty.
