The Allied Force Lords (AFL) is a multiplayer gaming clan within the online game, Jedi Knight: Jedi Academy. It started within Jedi Academy's predecessor, Jedi Outcast. This clan offers three distinct paths for a player to gain mastery of the Force: Jedi, Sith or Rogue Jedi. This clan was founded by Masters Reign and Hunter, and is currently ran by Clan Master Yoset, a former Sith Lord and Councilman.

[edit] History

The clan of the Allied Force Lords (AFL) has been a part of the Jedi Knight community since the turn of the millenium. Jedi Knight II: Jedi Outcast was the spawning ground for a myriad of saberist groups, clans and bands of mercernaries. The -=AFL=- blossomed into a clan of more than twenty members months after it's creation, and was headed by its co-founder and Clan Master (CM): Master Reign. Upon its conception, the founders - Master Reign and Master Hunter - recruited members from both sides of the Force.

Since that time, the AFL has evolved into a clan where disciples of the Jedi, Sith and even the Rogue Jedi coexist in an environment of mutual respect.

The AFL has been a clan that has strived in the presence of both foreign and domestic struggles. It has prevailed through such times coming out with more vigor and a deeper loyalty of its members to the concept AFL was founded on. That concept is as true now as it was when Master Reign and Hunter first brought the AFL to prominence back in the day of Jedi Outcast. It can be summed up in one word: Family. The AFL protects and fights for it's own in and out of the server as a family protects each of its members. The clan server has brought forth and forged friendships that have lasted (for some) almost five years.

[edit] Alliances

The AFL has made allies with half a dozen other similar clans including the Imperial Knights Clan (IKC), Dark Tide Rising (DtR), the Shadows of the Sith (!SoS!) and more recently, COF (Concordance of Fealty). Strong and organized enemy clans have also been apart of the AFL's history and have included such names as the Viking (V) clan.

[edit] Organization

The AFL is led by Clan Master and Sith Lord Yoset and Vice Clan Master, Lord Sixx. A ruling Council with representatives from each faction are answerable only to the CM and VCM and carry out the day-to-day leadership duties of the AFL, including moderating the clan forum and the game server in addition to liaising with Jedi Masters, Sith Lords and Rogue Jedi Masters which form the lowest tier in the leadership structure.

[edit] Factions

AFL consists of three factions: Jedi, Sith and Rogue Jedi. After someone is recruited by an AFL Master he/she must choose one of these three factions. While all followers of each faction are expected to eventually gain mastery over saber techniques, basic and advanced, the Force power usage differs in each case.

  • All factions share certain neutral Force powers: jump, push, pull, speed and sense.
  • The Jedi have a further set of four light side powers: absorb, heal, protect and mind-trick.
  • The Sith have the four dark side powers: grip, drain, lightning and dark rage.
  • The Rogue Jedi are allowed the use of any Force power, light or dark.

All factions are encouraged to learn the use of the Force through appropriate testing protocols although the Sith and Jedi focus more on saber techniques. The Rogue Jedi seek a balance between saber techniques and Force usage and their trials are designed to lend focus to this philosophy.
