All Blacks vs Springboks

The All Blacks (New Zealand) and the Springboks (South Africa) have been playing test match rugby union since 1921 when the All Blacks beat the Springboks in Dunedin 14-6. There is considerable history behind the All Blacks-Springbok matches, much of it off the field. In 1981, there were protests in New Zealand over the Springboks coming to New Zealand with a basically white-only team. Up until the start of the professional era, the Springboks had won 21 tests to the All Blacks 18. In the 29 matches since the start of the professional era, the All Blacks have won 20 tests to the Springboks' 8.
