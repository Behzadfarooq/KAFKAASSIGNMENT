Allotopic expression

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Expression from the nuclear genome of genes that normally are expressed only from the mitochondrial genome is referred to as allotopic expression (AE). Biomedically engineered AE has been suggested as a possible future tool in gene therapy of certain mitochondria-related diseases (de Grey 2000), however this view is controversial (Oca-Cossio et al 2003).

  • Oca-Cossio et al, Limitations of allotopic expression of mitochondrial genes in mammalian cells. Genetics. 2003 Oct;165(2):707-20[1] (abstract & full article)
