Allan Picken (born September 17, 1981 in Sydney, New South Wales, Australia) is an Australian football (soccer) player. He currently plays as a left full back and signed for Walsall on 31st July 2006, for $AU50,000 (Â£20,390) subject to international clearance. He formerly played for A-League club Newcastle United Jets and was a concreter by trade.
