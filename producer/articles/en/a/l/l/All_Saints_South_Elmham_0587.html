All Saints South Elmham

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

All Saints South Elmham belongs to The Saints, Suffolk, a group of villages near the border with Norfolk.

[edit] Church

All Saints South Elmham

All Saints South Elmham is one of 38 existing round-tower churches in Suffolk.
