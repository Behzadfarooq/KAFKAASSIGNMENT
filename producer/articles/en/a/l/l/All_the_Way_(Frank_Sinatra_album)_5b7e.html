All the Way is an album by American singer Frank Sinatra, released in 1961.

The fourth Capitol compilation album of singles and B-sides from 1957 to 1960. Although not available in this form on compact disc, all tracks are available on The Complete Capitol Singles Collection box set.

  6. "River, Stay 'Way from My Door" (Matt Dixon, Harry Woods)
