The Alliance of Free Stars is a fictional alliance of alien species in the universe of the computer game Star Control. They were destroyed by the Ur-Quan Kzer-Za and their Hierarchy of Battle Thralls as seen in Star Control I, but rebuilt as the New Alliance of Free Stars although, actually, the player (in Star Control II) can choose the name from a list of possibilities. Its members were the following species:

  • Chenjesu
  • Yehat
  • Syreen
  • Human
  • Shofixti
  • Mmrnmhrm
  • Ariloulaleelay.
