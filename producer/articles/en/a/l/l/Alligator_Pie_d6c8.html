Alligator Pie

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Book cover, featuring characters from the poems

Alligator Pie is a popular book of children's poetry written by Dennis Lee and illustrated by Frank Newfeld. The book was first published in 1974. It won the Book of the Year for Children award from the Canadian Library Association.[1]
