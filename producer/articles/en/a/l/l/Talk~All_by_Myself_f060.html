You do have a point. But I'm thinking: if we delete it, we just leave the way open for a future recreation (for the Green Day song again, or for another track of the same name), and we'd be back to square one. By leaving it, we provide a more neutral entry that will not allow the creation of an unsuited page (which is the present article). Furthermore, we can never completely exclude, I believe, that a reader might very well type "All by Myself" in the search box, thinking of "some song". The article listing the variants would be useful to help the reader locate which song/band (s)he is looking for. I'm not thinking of a blunt list though.
