Allen Coulter

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Allen Coulter is the director of a number of successful television programs. Thus far, he has directed one feature film entitled Hollywoodland, a film regarding the questionable death of George Reeves starring Adrien Brody, Diane Lane, and Ben Affleck. It was released September 2006.
