All My Loving is an EP released by The Beatles in the United Kingdom on 1964-02-07. The EP features four tracks from their albums Please Please Me and With the Beatles, and was only released in mono.

[edit] Track listing

  • All songs written by John Lennon and Paul McCartney, except where noted (see also: Lennon/McCartney).
  1. "All My Loving" - 2:10
    • Originally released as track three (side one) on the album With the Beatles.
  2. "Ask Me Why" - 2:28
    • Originally released on 1963-01-11 as the B-side to "Please Please Me", and later released as track six (side one) on the album Please Please Me.
  3. "Money (That's What I Want)" (Janie Bradford, Berry Gordy) - 2:52
    • Originally released as the closing track (side 2, track 7) on the album With the Beatles.
  4. "P.S. I Love You" - 2:02
    • Originally released on 1962-10-05 as the B-side to "Love Me Do", and later released as track two (side two) on the album Please Please Me.
