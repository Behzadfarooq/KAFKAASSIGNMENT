Allston-Brighton

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Allston-Brighton is a Boston neighborhood accessible by the Boston MBTA subway Green Line. The "B Branch" runs along Commonwealth Avenue and ends at Boston College. The "C Branch" ends at Cleveland Circle after passing through Brookline. Several bus lines run to and through the neighborhood. It was recently picked as part of a top ten worst places in the country to live by Dave Gilmartin author of "The Absolutely Worst Places to Live in America". The general local perception is that it is populated by rowdy overprivilaged white college students, hipsters, homeless people with mental issues, gangs of angry ethnic youth, and relics of the fading working class population. Harvard Ave is a popular street running through Allston, acting as a main street esq road, lined with shops and restaurants. Also see Allston and Brighton.
