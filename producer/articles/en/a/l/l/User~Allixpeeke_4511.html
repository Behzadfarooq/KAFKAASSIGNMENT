allixpeeke (born 22 March 1985 in New York, New York) is an American writer, poet, musician, filmmaker, and Pantheistic-Christian-Existentialist-who-would-rather-be-an-Atheistic-Romantic-Nihilist. It is generally agreed by all persons that he is super-sexy.

[edit] Philosophy and Religion

allixpeeke accept both biological determinism and individual free will. He accepts determinism as it seems to him to be the materialist truth to our physical existence, but he rejects the assumption that this dictates a necessity for fate, and therefore he celebrates free will insofar as we have choice, regardless of how it may be determined by circumstance and environment.

He is a Christian in that he believes in Christ, the Saviour promised by the Jews. But his impression of God differs greatly from classical Christianity in that he views God as a pantheistic manner. Further, he's not convinced that God is omniscient. "But what do I know?" he says.

He is an existentialist in that it seems reality is fundamentally empty. Life has no [inherent] meaning, according to allixpeeke, and existence has no [inherent] purpose. Life only has so much purpose as the individual chooses for it, but no inherent meaning, as each individual is an agent in and of himself, sovereign from the mores and values of his peers. He would like greatly to be a nihilist, as it seems a purer escape from the insanity, or more appropriately, absurdity of life. "Why believe at all?" he asks. "Unfortunately, I cannot seem to escape belief; it is deeply rooted in me."

As is mentioned earlier, he would like rather to be an atheist. This topic had come up in a discussion at the Towson Philosophy Forum, where it was stated by one or more members that people construct God because such a concept as God gives them a sense, perhaps a false sense, of comfort, of internal peace. allixpeeke cannot personally concur, as he personally would gain far greater comfort from knowing positively that there is no God, that there is no afterlife, that death is just that: the end of everything. That would provide him with "so much comfort, [he]'d be set for life (whatever that is)."

He would also rather be purely a romantic, rather than an existentialist, as there seems to be an obscene beauty in such an abstract perfection that romanticism establishes. "Perhaps this alone already determines me as a romantic," says he, "but as I don't perceive the world as an overtly romantic establishment, and instead realize it to be more akin to a pure grittiness, I tend to view myself as an existentialist in that context. Although, one could easily see this desire for romanticism-just-out-of-reach as a reflection of of the term Hopeless Romantic."

[edit] Political Philosophy

He would say that his political view, which he describes as minarchist (libertarian), has tendencies towards romanticism, despite being a product of the Age of Enlightenment, which proceeded the Romantic Era, and of which, artistically, the Romantic Era was a rejection thereof.

"More specifically, I'm a classical liberal, or more commonly a libertarian, who happens to adhere strongly to the conception of Natural, Inalienable, Innate, Individual, Human Rights."

Henry David Thoreau

He would also be willing to describe myself as a humanist, a feminist, an individualist, a revolutionary, and a free-marketeer, and a small-R republican (in the sense that Proudhon used the term).

He is "attracted to" other political philosophies, including anarcho-capitalism (Murray N. Rothbard), individualist anarchism (Lysander Spooner), and even libertarian socialism (Pierre-Joseph Proudhon). He is intrigued, also, by the fictional philosophy English Socialism, however he "obviously rejects [this] one outright," he says.

[edit] Philosophical Influences

His favourite philosophers are Jesus, Descartes, Locke, Thoreau, Rothbard, Paine, Nozick, Spooner, and to a lesser extent himself, if for no other reason than that "all my good ideas, so it seems, have already been taken by these and other philosophers."

allixpeeke is also a musician, a poet, a writer, a filmmaker, and a painter, as well as one who delves into other forms of art, such as digital art. Philosophically, he looks at everything as "art." Epistemologically, he also looks at absolute knowledge as unattainable.

[edit] Goals in Life

He has three general goals in life. Firstly, to enjoy his life. Secondly, to change the world, "or at least make a dent." And thirdly, to gain as much knowledge as he can, and then to die.

[edit] Childhood and Education

Although allixpeeke was born in New York, he spent most of his life in Baltimore, Maryland. For K-5, he attended Fullerton Elementary School. For 6-8, he attended Parkville Middle School and Center of Technology. And, for 9-12, he attended Eastern Technical High School, where he graduated in 2003 in the top five percent of his class.

allixpeeke only applied to one college, Towson University, at which he was accepted. He was previously majoring in Electronic Media and Film, but is currently majoring in Political Science. He also served as President of the College Libertarians of Towson from the Spring semester of 2004 through the Spring semester of 2006.
