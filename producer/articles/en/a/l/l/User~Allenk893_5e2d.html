Allenk893 is a 2005 user of The English Wikipedia. He is known on the web for webmaster of Hilary-Palooza,Topsecret-Online, And Chucky-Unlimited. Starting on the web back in circa 2002, his web building skills has grown professionally and masterfully. Still learning the few techniques of graphic making etc... He is a known user on The Madtv Forum,The Kidactors Chat, and an anonymous yahoo!,group. Back in 2003, he joined TV.COM (tvtome) and is currently a level 28 user for his countless contributions. You may view his sensational profile at he bottom of this page.

Where is Allenk893 now? Well, no one knows. You can say 2006 marked the big time for him. At the beginning of the year, Allenk donated three graphic tv.com banners to users he believed needed them. Also having some for his own profile. The users thanked him incredibly and said fairwell. After that, Allenk made his journey unto the forum scene. He has donated music, photos, videos, graphic layouts, and tons of other things to celebrity-related forums. He has also made and donated up to 12 myspace layouts so far this year. 5 of them include himself in them. As for the wiki scene, Allenk is currently keeping a low profile mainly because of buisness elsewhere. But on occasion he donates some things to pages. He has recently just opened another brand new website entitled "Sincerely Raven" expected to be out by spring of 2007. He has made recent updates to his old website Chucky-Unlimited stating he his going to have major updates next year. Fans can definetly expect good things to come for Allenk in 2007.
