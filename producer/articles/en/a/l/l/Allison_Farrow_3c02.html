Allison Farrow (born August 9, 1989) is a beauty queen from Earleville, Maryland who will compete in the Miss Teen USA pageant in 2007.

Farrow won the Miss Maryland Teen USA 2007 title on November 5, 2006.[1] This was her third attempt at the title, as she placed third runner-up in the 2006 event and was a non-finalist in 2005. She also won a non-finalist evening gown award in 2005. Her sister titleholder is Michae' Holloman, Miss Maryland USA 2007, who was also a runner-up in 2006 and 2005. Farrow will now compete in the Miss Teen USA 2007 pageant, broadcast live from an as-yet unknown location in August 2007.

Farrow is a student at Bohemian Manor High School.[2] She plans to pursue a degree in elementary education in the future.[3]
