Allan Olesen (born January 25, 1974) is a Danish professional football (soccer) defender who currently plays for Danish Superliga side Aalborg Boldspilklub (AaB). He started his career at Danish clubs Albertslund IF, Brønshøj BK and Akademisk Boldklub before he moved abroad to play for French club AS Saint-Etienne in 2000. After three years at the club, he moved back to Denmark to play for AaB in 2003.
