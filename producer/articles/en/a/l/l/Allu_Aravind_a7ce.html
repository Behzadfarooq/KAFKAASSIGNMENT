Allu Aravind (Telugu:అల్లు ఆరవింద్), born January 10, is a film producer and distributor in Tollywood. He produces films under Geetha Arts. He is the son of the late sri Padmasri Allu Rama Lingaiah. He is the brother-in-law of Chiranjeevi and Pawan Kalyan and father of Allu Arjun, all of whom are actors.
