AllHipHop.com

AllHipHop.com is a news website with focus on hip hop music and related topics, founded by Chuck "Jigsaw" Creekmur and "Grouchy" Greg Watkins in 1998. Since its inception, it has grown into a frequently visited news source, delivering more than 27 million page views per month to over 2 million visitors.

Since 2002 the company also hosts a week of offline programming in New York City, a seven day event called "AllHipHop Week", featuring a number of events ranging from fashion shows to gaming tournaments to events dealing with social issues, and has featured celebrities such as Remy Martin, Jim Jones, Gloria Velez, and Russell Simmons.

AllHipHop.com maintains offices in Newark, Delaware and the New York City.
