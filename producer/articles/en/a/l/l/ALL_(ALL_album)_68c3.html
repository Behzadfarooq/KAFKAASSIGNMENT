ALL is a best of album by ALL. It was released on February 23, 1999 on ALL's own label, Owned & Operated. The songs were picked by the fans and were remastered for this release.

[edit] Song origins

  • Tracks 12 and 21 originally appeared on Allroy Sez (1988)
  • Tracks 3 and 7 originally appeared on the Allroy for Prez EP (1988)
  • Tracks 4, 6 and 19 originally appeared on Allroy's Revenge (1989)
  • Track 1 originally appeared on "She's My Ex" single (1989)
  • Tracks 9, 11, 16 and 22 originally appeared on Allroy Saves (1990)
  • Tracks 13, 15 and 17 Percolater (1992)
  • Tracks 5, 8 and 10 originally appeared on Breaking Things (1993)
  • Tracks 2, 14 and 20 from Pummel (1995)
