ALL/2 is the second mini album by the Japanese enterainers AAA. It was released on avex trax on 13 September 2006 in two different editions: CD and CD accompanied by a DVD.

[edit] CD Track listing

  1. "ハリケーン・リリ，ボストン・マリ (Original Long Version)" (Hurricane Riri, Boston Mari)
  2. "Let it Beat!"
  3. "ハレルヤ" (Hallelujah)
  4. "Q"
  5. "Shalala キボウの歌" (Shalala Kibō no Uta)
  6. "夢ノカケラ" (Yume no Kakera)

[edit] DVD Track listing

  1. "夢ノカケラ" (Yume no Kakera)
  2. "AAA 1 Year Anniversarry Clip?"
  3. "Making of 夢ノカケラ"
