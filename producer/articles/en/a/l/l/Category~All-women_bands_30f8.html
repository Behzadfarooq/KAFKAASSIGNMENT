Category:All-women bands

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

This is a category for all-women bands. An all-women musical group is one where the members are women who sing and who play their own instruments. Vocalists and vocal groups are not included here. This category also includes individual artists who are in all-women bands and articles pertaining to them.
