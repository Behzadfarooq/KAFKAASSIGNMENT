All About the Benjamins (2002) is an action movie/comedy movie directed by Kevin Bray. The film stars Ice Cube, Mike Epps, and Tommy Flanagan.

[edit] Plot

Spoiler warning: Plot and/or ending details follow.

Bookum, (Ice Cube) a bounty hunter is trying to capture Reggie Wright (Mike Epps), whom he has already arrested multiple times. While chasing Wright, he discovers a diamond heist. Reggie's girlfriend, Gina (played by Eva Mendes) and Bookum's friend Pam, join the chase for the thieves, because of a winning lottery ticket which Reggie lost in the thieves' van.
