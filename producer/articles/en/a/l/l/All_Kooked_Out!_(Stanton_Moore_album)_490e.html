All Kooked Out! is the first studio album by New Orleans, Louisiana based drummer Stanton Moore.

It was recorded entirely live in the studio with no overdubs and no headphone, in a week-long session that also yielded Mysteryfunk, the debut album for Garage A Trois. Prior to this session, producer Dan Prothero worked with Moore on Galactic's first two albums.

This Enhanced CD contains bonus audio and in-studio video.
