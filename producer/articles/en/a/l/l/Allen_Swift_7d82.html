Allen Swift

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Ira Stadlen, known professionally as Allen Swift (January 16, 1924-) is a voice actor, known for playing characters like Simon Bar Sinister on the Underdog cartoon show. He also provided the voices of many of the characters in The Bluffers. He was also a children's television show host on WPIX in New York City as "Captain Allen". He took his professional name from radio comedian Fred Allen and satirist Jonathan Swift.

Swift was an early television star, as he'd hosted the Popeye Show from September 10, 1956 to September 23, 1960 before getting his own program.

Swift is the father of character actor, mimic and singer Lewis J. Stadlen.
