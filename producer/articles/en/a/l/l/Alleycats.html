  • Loganathan Arumugam and David Arumugam - vocals

  • Shunmugam Arumugam - lead guitar
  • Chester Anthony Passerella - saxophone
  • Khoo Fook Sin and Greenville - keyboards
  • Frank Ong - bass

[edit] History

The existence of this band is vital to the Malaysian music industry. They have contributed to the development of the Malaysian pop music and are till today regarded as one of the classic Malaysian pop music bands.

Malaysia is a multi racial country that achieved independence from England in 1957 and the development of pop music was already on its way but during the 1970s and 80's it was the major thrust from a new generation of Malaysians questing for new directions and development in the pop culture especially music.

There were earlier generations of the Band but this line up mentioned aboved were the ones that were granted the opportunities for a higher level of achievement in music. The Alleycats were always on top of the musical stage in Malaysia even before they were signed up by Philips and later on Polygram as recording artistes.

This line up secured a performance contract in Mocambo club situated in Central, a business sector on Hong kong island in 1976. It was supposed to be a three month contract but their success in wooing the crowd earn them three yearly contracts bundled with TV and concert apprearences as promotion. They became a hit with the locals in Hong Kong.

It was not until they left Hong Kong to start a new performance contract in Singapore that they were spotted by major recording companies.

Even with changes in the line up till today in 2007, they are still active in performing and recording
