All Things New is an album released by Steven Curtis Chapman in 2004 on Sparrow Records. In 2005, it won the Grammy Award for Best Pop/Contemporary Gospel Album; unusually for Chapman, however, the album did not win the corresponding Dove Award that year, although it had been nominated.[1]
