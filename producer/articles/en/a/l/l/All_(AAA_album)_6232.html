ALL is the third album released by the Japanese group AAA. It was released on January 1, 2007 through avex trax. Only three songs on this album was not released as a single.

[edit] Track listing

  1. ハレルヤ (Hallelujah)
  2. ハリケーン・リリ，ボストン・マリ (Original Long version) (Hurricane Lili, Boston Mari)
  3. Let it beat!
  4. Samurai heart -侍魂- (samurai tamashii)
  5. チューインガム (Chewing Gum)
  6. ソウルエッジボーイ (Soul Edge Boy)
  7. キモノジェットガール (Kimono Jet Girl)
  8. "Q"
  9. Champagne Gold
  10. Winter lander!!
  11. Shalala キボウの歌 (kibou no uta)
  12. Us
  13. ミカンセイ (mikansei)

The mini-album ALL/2 contains tracks 1, 2, 3, 8, and 11.
