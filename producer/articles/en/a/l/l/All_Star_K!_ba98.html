All Star K! is a videoke game show in the Philippines. There are seven contestants who sing videoke pieces, filling in the blanks as they go along. After each round, one contestant is eliminated until two contestants remain. These final two battle it out in the “1 on 1 Sa Katayan” round, where the contestants attempt to guess the missing word in a song. The winner then gets a chance to win a million pesos by completely filling in 10 blanks plus the jackpot line of one song.

All Star K! started as K! The 1 Million Peso Videoke Challenge with Arnell Ignacio as host. But when Arnel Ignacio transferred to the rival station ABS-CBN, the show was reformatted into a celebrity game show with Jaya and comedian Allan K as hosts

[edit] Celebrity winners

There are already three celebrities who have one the 1-million peso jackpot at present. Regine Velasquez won twice, Mark Tupaz of Shamrock and, the StarStruck Kids avenger Sandy Talag won as she bravely sang the song "Through The Fire" of Chaka Khan. Iwa Moto and Sheena Halili has also won twice but didn't got the Jackpot.The latest millionaire is EB Babe Belle won 1 million this March 4, 2007.
