Steven Arthur Miles was born April 18, 1988 in Richmond, Virginia. He has lived in Chesterfield County, Virginia for all of his life. He doesn't practice religion, and he seldom eats wheat or oats.

Known since early years by the name "Stevie," he's used that name early on, and he switched to Steven Miles, Jr. in 1995, influenced partially by Ken Griffey, Jr., but started just using Steven Miles later. Recently, he's been using Steven A. Miles alongside Steven Miles, and the former became most commonly used.

Steven is admired by many friends and relatives for his intellect and nice disposition, although his social skills could use work. His interests include game shows, video games, 1980s media including television and music, anime, old-school British television, sport (including baseball), and furry fandom.

Miles has contributed to Wikipedia before creating his account. Topics he created include Lingo (game show), Play Your Cards Right, and Telemundo. He has also been a contributor to TechTV, GSN, and Takeshi's Castle, among other things.

On August 21, 2004, Miles created a Wikipedia account under the name All_SMiles. Since then, he has been a frequent contributor.

Miles took four years of French and has finished one year of Visual BASIC programming. Lately, he's been advised many times to drink many bottles of water.
