Allan Evans, (born 12 October 1956 in Dunfermline, Scotland) is a former Scottish footballer who played for Dunfermline Athletic (1973-77), Aston Villa (1977-89), Leicester City (1989-90) and Darlington (1990-91). A central defender, Evans was part of Villa's 1982 European Cup winning team. He was later club captain and helped them win promotion back to the First Division in 1988.

Evans also won four caps for Scotland, and was a member of the country's 1982 World Cup squad.
