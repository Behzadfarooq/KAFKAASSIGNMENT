Allen and Ginter was the Richmond, Virginia tobacco manufacturing firm formed by John Allen and Lewis Ginter in 1875 that created and marketed the first Cigarette cards for collecting and trading. The company also offered a prize for the invention of a machine able to roll cigarettes (which had until then be hand-rolled). James Albert Bonsack won this prize with his 1880/81 invention. The cigarette brands of Allen & Ginter included Richmond Gems, Virginia Brights, Perfection, Dandies and Little Beauties.
