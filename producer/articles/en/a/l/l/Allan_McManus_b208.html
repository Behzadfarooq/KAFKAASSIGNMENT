Allan William McManus (born November 17, 1974 in Paisley, Renfrewshire) is a Scottish professional footballer, currently playing for St. Johnstone. He signed from Airdrie United in the summer of 2006.

McManus scored his first Saints goal on January 2, 2007, a 90th-minute winner against Ross County at McDiarmid Park.[1]
