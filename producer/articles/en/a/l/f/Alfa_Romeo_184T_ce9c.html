Alfa Romeo 184T was Benetton sponsored F1 car used during 1984 Formula One season and the beginning of 1985 season (184TB). Car was Mario Tollentino`s first F1 design.[1] Team achieved only 11 points. 1.5 L V8 turbo engine had around 670 bhp. Best result was from Italian GP where Riccardo Patrese came 3rd. Most cases reason for retirement was unreliable engine or out of fuel.
