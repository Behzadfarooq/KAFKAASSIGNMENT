Alfa Romeo 185T was the car that Benetton Team Alfa Romeo used during 1985 Formula One season. Car was entered to 8 starts but without any success and bad reliability the team returned to earlier version 184T. 1985 season was last one for Alfa Romeo team.

This car had 1.5 L turbocharged V8 engine which produced around 640bhp.
