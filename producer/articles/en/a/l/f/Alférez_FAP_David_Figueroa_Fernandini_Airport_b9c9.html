Alférez FAP David Figueroa Fernandini Airport (IATA: HUU, ICAO: SPNC) is an airport serving Huanuco, Peru. It is the most important airport in the Huánuco Region. It is operated by the civil government. It is currently served by one airline: LC Busre. It has a flight to Lima which connects the city to the rest of the country. It currently does not at any scheduled services to any Andean cities. It does, however, offer many charter and private flights.
