Alfonso Castaneda, Nueva Vizcaya

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Alfonso Castaneda is a 5th class municipality in the province of Nueva Vizcaya, Philippines. According to the 2000 census, it has a population of 4,808 people in 873 households.

[edit] Barangays

Alfonso Castaneda is politically subdivided into 6 barangays.

  • Abuyo
  • Galintuja
  • Cauayan
  • Lipuga
  • Lublub (Pob.)
  • Pelaway
