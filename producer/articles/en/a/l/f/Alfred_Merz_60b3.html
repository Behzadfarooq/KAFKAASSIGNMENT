Alfred Merz (born January 24, 1880, Perchtoldsdorf, Niederösterreich - August 16, 1925, Buenos Aires), Austrian geographer, oceanographer.

Merz Peninsula is named after him.

[edit] Literary works

  • Hydrographische Untersuchungen im Golf von Triest (), 1911
  • Die Oberflächentemperatur der Gewässer (), 1920
  • Die atlantische Vertikalzirkulation (), 1922-1933 (with Georg Wüst)

[edit] Other readings

  • writings about exploration with Meteor, completed by Albert Josef Maria Defant
