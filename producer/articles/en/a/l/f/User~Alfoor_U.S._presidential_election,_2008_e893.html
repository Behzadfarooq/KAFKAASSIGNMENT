Presidential electoral votes by state

The U.S. presidential election of 2008 is scheduled to occur on November 4, 2008. The election will determine the next President of the United States. The allocation of electoral votes to each state will remain the same for this election as it was for the election in 2004, relying on the 2000 Census. The winner will be sworn in on January 20, 2009.
