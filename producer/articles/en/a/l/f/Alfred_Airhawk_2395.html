Alfred Airhawk (アルフレッド, Arufureddo?) is a fictional character from SNK's Fatal Fury series. He is the hero of the new Southtown story that began in Real Bout Special: Dominated Mind. He also appeared in Real Bout 2: The Newcomers, Fatal Fury: First Contact and Card Fighters Clash. He makes friendship with the Bogard brothers (Terry and Andy).

[edit] Story

Alfred lived in a small village with his grandfather, who always visited and flied with him in his old airplane. But that village was invaded by a criminal organization, leaded by White, who wants to installed a base there. Alfred attemps to escape when those criminals invaded the house, but his grandfather doesn't.

Soon, Alfred began a journey around the world in his trusty airplane (who was of his grandfather), where he meets many people and created his own fighting style based in flying. But, in middle of his travel, his airplane suffered a slight mechanical failure, so Alfred had to make a forced landing in the forests just outside Southtown. Going to that city for some spare parts for his plane, he heard again the name of White. Now, Alfred goes after the man who took the life of his grandfather and the control of his village, making the justice by his own hands.

[edit] Personality

A joyful young man who has high spirits and filled with joy and friendship, Alfred is a pretty mature person for his age. Always filled with dreams, and a huge lover of the sky, Alfred has never let anything stand in the way of his goals. His strong will and determination has allowed him to soar over every hardship, and leads him further into the heavens.

[edit] Trivia

  • Alfred doesn't appear on the original Real Bout Special. He has been added for a special version called Real Bout Special: Dominated Mind (only for PlayStation) along with his enemy White.
  • Even though he is the new hero of the Dominated Mind, he just appeared in other 4 games:
    • Real Bout 2 (arcade): as a hidden boss and a non-playable character.
    • Fatal Fury First Contact (NGPC): Same as above (since this game is a port of RB2 for Neo-Geo Pocket Color).
    • Card Fighters Clash (NGPC): as a SNK character card (he is one of the weakest cards in the game, even though he is the only one who can attack in the same round that he is put in play)
    • The King of Fighters '99 Evolution (Dreamcast): as a striker.
  • Probably Alfred's grandfather was one of the pilots of the Prehistoric Isle series, like Billy Kane's father.
