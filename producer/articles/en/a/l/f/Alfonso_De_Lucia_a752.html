Alfonso De Lucia

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Alfonso De Lucia (born 12 November 1983 in Nola, Naples) is an Italian football (soccer) player.

He plays goalkeeper for Parma F.C. in Serie A, he was Italian U20s in 2003.

[edit] Trivia

He wore No.31 in the 2004/05 season and changed to No.1 in 2005/06 (after the departure of Gianluca Berti). But he has yet to give a world-class performance and needs to earn more experience. He changed numbers again, this time to No.23 after the departure of Mark Bresciano in 2006/07 season instead of the No.1 shirt number.
