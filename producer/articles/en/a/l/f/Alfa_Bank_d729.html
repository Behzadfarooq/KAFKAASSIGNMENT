Alfa Bank, the corporate treasury of the Alfa Group, is the largest private commercial bank in Russia. Its headquarters are in Moscow, Kalanchevskaya street. Today it is a high technological universal financial institution, providing service for over than 1.5 million citizens and 40 000 organizations.

Since October 1994 Pyotr Aven has been its President.
