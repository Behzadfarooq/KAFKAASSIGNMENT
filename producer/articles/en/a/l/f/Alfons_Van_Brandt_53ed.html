Alfons Van Brandt

Alfons Van Brandt (born June 24, 1927) nicknamed Fons, was a Belgian football player who won the Belgian Golden Shoe in 1955 while at Lierse. He played 38 times for the national team between 1950 and 1957, starting in a 7-2 friendly win against the Netherlands on November 12, 1950. Van Brandt played in the 1954 World Cup.
