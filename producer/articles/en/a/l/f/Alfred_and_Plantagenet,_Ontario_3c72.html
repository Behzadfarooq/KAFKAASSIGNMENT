Alfred and Plantagenet (2006 population 8,654) is a township in eastern Ontario, Canada, in the United Counties of Prescott and Russell at the confluence of the Ottawa River and the South Nation River.

According to the 2006 Statistics Canada Census:

  • Population: 8,654
  • % Change (2001-2006): 0.7
  • Dwellings: 3,581
  • Area (km²): 391.7
  • Density (persons per km²): 22.1
