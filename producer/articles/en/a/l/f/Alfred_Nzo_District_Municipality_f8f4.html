Alfred Nzo is one of the 7 districts of Eastern Cape province of South Africa. The seat of Alfred Nzo is Mount Ayliff. The majority of its 550 392 people speak IsiXhosa (2001 Census). The district code is DC44.

[edit] Geography

[edit] Neighbours

Alfred Nzo is surrounded by:

  • the kingdom of Lesotho to the north
  • Sisonke (DC43) to the north-east
  • Ugu (DC21) to the east
  • OR Tambo (DC15) to the south
  • Ukhahlamba (DC14) to the west
