Alfred Valenzuela

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

MG Alfred A. Valenzuela

Alfred A. Valenzuela is a United States Army major general who commands U.S. Army South (USARSO) at Fort Buchanan, Puerto Rico. He comes from a Mexican-American family from San Antonio, Texas. He frequently discusses how he overcame his childhood as a gang member.

Valenzuela is married to Esther Valenzuela and they have several children.
