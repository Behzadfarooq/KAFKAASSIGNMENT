Alfred Malone ("Big Pooh") (born in Frisco City, Alabama) is a professional American Football player. He went to Frisco City High School, Frisco City, Alabama and played on the Frisco city's Football team as a (WHIPPET). After his graduation from Troy State University, (Troy, AL) he became a professional football player for the Houston Texans in 2005.
