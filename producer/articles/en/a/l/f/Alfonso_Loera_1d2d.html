Alfonso Loera Pico (born December 4, 1978 in Ensenada, Baja California) is a Mexican soccer defender.

Loera spent part of the 2005 Major League Soccer season with C.D. Chivas USA. Loera came to Chivas USA as one of imports from parent club Chivas de Guadalajara, and also played for their other child club, La Piedad. He debuted with Guadalajara in 2000 after joining the team through open tryouts. Loera was released by Chivas USA during the 2005 season.
