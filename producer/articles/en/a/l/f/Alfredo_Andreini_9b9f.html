Alfredo Andreini

Alfredo Andreini was an Italian doctor and entomologist, born on July 27, 1870 in Florence and died on December 11, 1943 in Lippiano.

He carried out a large collection of insects collected in particular from Cape Verde (1908) and in Libya (1913) and Érythrée. He collaborated with the zoological museum La Specola.

In geometry, he enumerated and published a list of 25 convex uniform honeycombs in 1905 (the space-filling tessellations of regular and semiregular polyhedra). This was the most complete list published until approximately 1991 when Norman Johnson completed the full list of 28 forms.
