Alfonso Thiele (April 5, 1922 - c. 1986) was a Formula One driver from the United States. He held dual citizenship of the United States and Italy. Thiele spent most of his life in Italy. He participated in one World Championship Grand Prix, on September 4, 1960. He scored no championship points.

His year of birth is also given as 1920.
