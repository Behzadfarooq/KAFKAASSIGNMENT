Alfred Leete

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Alfred Leete's Lord Kitchener poster

British illustrator (1882- 1933). Born in Northampton, he studied at Weston-super-Mare Grammar School before becoming a printer. In 1905 Punch magazine used one of his drawings. He then became a full time ilustrator. He is famous for the Lord Kitchener poster design which first appeared on the cover of the weekly magazine London Opinion in 1914.
