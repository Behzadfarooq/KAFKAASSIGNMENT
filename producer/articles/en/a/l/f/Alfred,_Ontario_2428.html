Alfred, Ontario

Alfred is a small community located in the township of Alfred and Plantagenet which is in turn located in Prescott and Russell United Counties.

[edit] Population

  • 2006 Census: 1,340 [1]
  • 2001 Census: 1,301[1]
  • 1996 Census: 1,228[1]
  • 1991 Census: 1,130[2]
