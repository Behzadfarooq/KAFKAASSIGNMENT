Alfred Kohlberg

Alfred Kohlberg (1887-1960) was a American entrepreneur and staunch anti-communist -- being the head of the so called "China lobby", a close ally of Joe McCarthy, a guide to Robert Welch and founding director of the John Birch Society.[1]
