Alfonso Brescia

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Alfonso Brescia (6 January 1930 - 6 June 2001) was an Italian film director of low budget films, sometimes credited as Al Bradley.

[edit] Selected filmography

  • Iron Warrior (1987)
  • War of the Robots (1978)
  • Cosmos: War of the Planets (1977)
  • Battle of the Amazons (1973)
