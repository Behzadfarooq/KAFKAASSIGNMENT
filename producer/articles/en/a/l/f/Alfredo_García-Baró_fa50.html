Alfredo García-Baró (born 5 February 1972) is a Cuban sprinter specializing in the 100 metres.

He finished fourth with the Cuban 4 x 100 metres relay team at the 1997 and 1999 World Championships. 2000 Olympic relay bronze medalists Iván García and Luis Alberto Pérez-Rionda were part of the team on both occasions.
