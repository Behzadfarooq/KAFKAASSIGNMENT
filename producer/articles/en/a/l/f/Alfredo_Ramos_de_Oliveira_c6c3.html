Alfredo Ramos, full name Alfredo Ramos de Oliveira and nicknamed Alfredo or Polvo by fans (born in Jacareí, October 27, 1924) was a brazilian footballer in defense role, brother of Mauro Ramos de Oliveira.

In career (1950–1957) for Santos and Corinthians, played 285 games, scoring 3 goals and won one São Paulo State Championship in 1953. With Brazilian team was in roster for 1954 FIFA World Cup, without playing any game. In 1970s he was coach for São Paulo squad.
