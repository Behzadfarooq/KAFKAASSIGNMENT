Alfred Wentworth was a fictional character on the pilot episode for the TV drama series Law & Order. His character was replaced by Adam Schiff (Steven Hill) for the remainder of the first season which first aired in 1990. The pilot episode was produced for CBS in 1988, but never aired on that network. Instead, it aired as episode number 6 on NBC in 1990. [1]
