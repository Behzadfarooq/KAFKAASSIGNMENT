Alfred Santell

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

You have new messages (last change).

Alfred Santell was an American director born September 14, 1895 in San Francisco, California. He directed over 60 films, including The Patent Leather Kid (1927), Body and Soul (1931), and Beyond the Blue Horizon (1942). He died on June 19, 1981 in Salinas, California.
