Alfredo Tomassini

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Alfredo Tomassini (29 June 1964–8 December 1987) was a notable football player, the midfielder of the club Alianza Lima, until 1987 when he died in an airplane crash known as the 1987 Alianza Lima air disaster. He survived the inital crash but due to a broken leg was not able to stay afloat.[1] Tomassini was also an expert swimmer graduated from the British Markham College.
