Alfonso Quiñónez Molina

Alfonso Quiñónez Molina (January 11, 1874 – May 22, 1950) was President of El Salvador 21 December 1918 - 1 March 1919 and 1 March 1923 - 1 March 1927.

He was born in Suchitoto, El Salvador.
