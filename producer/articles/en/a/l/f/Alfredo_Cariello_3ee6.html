Alfredo Cariello (born 10 September 1979 in Naples) is an Italian football (soccer) midfielder. He currently plays for F.C. Crotone, on loan from A.C. ChievoVerona.

Cariello started his career at Salernitana Calcio 1919, one of the largest club in Campania, just behind S.S.C. Napoli. He was loaned to Nocerina and Savoia, also in Campania. He then joined Taranto and Giulianova.

He was spotted by Chievo in 2004, which the club keen on signing players in lower league and loaned out to ensure there was good players in squad when regelated.

He played for Frosinone Calcio in 2004-05, and played his first Serie A season at Ascoli in 2005-06.

In summer 2006, he was loaned to F.C. Crotone.

As of the end of 2005-06 season, he played 22 Serie A games, scored 2 goals, 1 goalless Serie B games, 113 Serie C1 games, scored 14 goals.
