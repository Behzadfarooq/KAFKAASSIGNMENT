Alfrick is a village and civil parish in the Malvern Hills district of Worcestershire, England, about seven miles west of Worcester. The 2001 census counted 511 people. Once a farming village, Alfrick is now mainly a dormitory village for nearby Worcester and Malvern. Alfrick is probably currently best known for the annual Alfrick and Lulsley Village show which attracts visitors from across the West Midlands.
