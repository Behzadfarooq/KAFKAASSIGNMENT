Alfhild Agrell

Alfhild Agrell (born Alfhild Martin in Härnösand; 13 January 1849 — 8 November 1923) was a Swedish writer. She was born to Erik Johan Martin and Karolina Margareta Adolphson, who worked as confectioners. From 1868 until 1995 she was married to the Stockholmer merchant A. Agrell.

She also temporarily used the pseudonyms Thyra, Lovisa Petterqvist and Stig Stigson.
