Alfredo Oscar Saint-Jean (b. 1926) is the former army brigade general who briefly served as interim de facto President of Argentina from June 18 to July 1, 1982, during a period of military rule, after his predecessor Leopoldo Galtieri was ousted from office due to the country's defeat in the Falklands (Malvinas) War. He was succeeded by General Reynaldo Bignone.
