Alfons Karpiński (February 20, 1875 in Rozwadów near Tarnobrzeg - June 6, 1961 in Kraków, Poland) was a Polish painter.

He painted mainly portraits of women.

"The Parisian Street", 1911, National Museum in Kraków.
