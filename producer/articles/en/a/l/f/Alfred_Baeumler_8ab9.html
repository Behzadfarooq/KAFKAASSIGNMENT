Alfred Baeumler (November 19, 1887 - March 19, 1968), was a German philosopher and pedagogue.

Baeumler was born at Neustadt an der Tafelfichte, Northern Bohemia. He was one of the most important philosophers in Nazi Germany. He died at Eningen unter Achalm, near Reutlingen.
