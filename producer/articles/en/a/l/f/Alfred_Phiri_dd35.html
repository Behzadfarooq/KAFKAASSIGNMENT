Alfred Phiri (born June 22, 1974 in Alexandra) is a South African football player.

He played mostly for Genclerbirligi (Turkey), Jomo Cosmos and Ajax Cape Town.

He played for South Africa national football team and was a participant at the 1998 FIFA World Cup.
