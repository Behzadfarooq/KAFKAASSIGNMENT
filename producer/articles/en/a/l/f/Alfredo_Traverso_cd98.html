Alfredo Traverso (c.1910-1980) was a prolific Argentine cinematographer who worked on over 70 films in the Cinema of Argentina between 1940 and 1970.

He delt with the cinematography on films such as El Asalto 1960 in which he worked with Austrian director Kurt Land and Asalto a la ciudad 1968 was one of his last films in which he worked with Carlos Cores.
