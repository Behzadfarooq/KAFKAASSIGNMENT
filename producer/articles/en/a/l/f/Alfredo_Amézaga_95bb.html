Alfredo Amézaga Delgado (born January 16, 1978, in Ciudad Obregon, Sonora, Mexico) is a Major League Baseball utility player for the Florida Marlins.

Amézaga made his Major League Baseball debut with the Anaheim Angels on May 24, 2002.

[edit] Teams

  • Anaheim Angels: 2002-2004
  • Colorado Rockies: 2005
  • Pittsburgh Pirates: 2005
  • Florida Marlins: 2006-present
