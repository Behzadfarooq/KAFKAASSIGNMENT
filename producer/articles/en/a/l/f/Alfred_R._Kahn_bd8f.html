Alfred R. Kahn, chairman and CEO of 4Kids Entertainment

Alfred R. Kahn is Chairman and CEO of 4Kids Entertainment, a New York Stock Exchange-listed company that is a global provider of children's entertainment and merchandise licensing.

[edit] Awards and recognition

In June 22, 2005, LIMA, the International Licensing Industry Merchandisers' Association, inducted Kahn into the Murray Altchuler Licensing Hall of Fame for his "continual and outstanding" contributions to the licensing industry for over 10 years.[1] Over those 10 years, Kahn and 4Kids had licensed such huge and popular properties such as Pokémon, Yu-Gi-Oh!, Cabbage Patch Kids, and Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles.
