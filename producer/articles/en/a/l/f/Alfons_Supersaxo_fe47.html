Alfons Supersaxo

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Alfons Supersaxo was a Swiss nordic skier who competed in the late 1940s and early 1950s. At the 1952 Winter Olympics, he finished tenth in the nordic combined event and 26th in the 18 km cross country skiing event. Supersaxo also competed in the 1948 Winter Olympics in St. Moritz, finishing 34th in the 18 km event.
