Alferez FAP Alfredo Vladimir Sara Bauer Airport (IATA: N/A, ICAO: SPAS) is a small regional airport serving Andoas, in the northern Loreto Region, of Peru. It is currently not served by any scheduled airline but it is by private and charter airlines. The airport is situated in the jungle and is near the border between Peru and Ecuador.
