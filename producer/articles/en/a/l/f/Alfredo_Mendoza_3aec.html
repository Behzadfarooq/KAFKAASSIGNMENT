Alfredo Damian Mendoza (born December 12, 1963 in Encarnación) is a retired football striker from Paraguay. A player of Cerro Porteño he was a member of the national team that competed at the 1986 FIFA World Cup in Mexico. At the club level he also played for Olimpia Asunción, Newell's Old Boys of Argentina and CF Atlas of Mexico.
