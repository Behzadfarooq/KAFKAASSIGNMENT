The Alfarista Radical Front (Spanish: Frente Radical Alfarista) is a centrist liberal party in Ecuador, founded in 1972. One of its best known figures is former acting president Fabián Alarcón.

The party is named after the most prominent liberal politician in Ecuadorian history, Eloy Alfaro, who founded the country's first institutionalized Liberal Party in 1884.

The present state of the party is unclear.
