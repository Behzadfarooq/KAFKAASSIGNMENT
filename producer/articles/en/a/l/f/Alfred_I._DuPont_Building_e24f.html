Alfred I. DuPont Building
(U.S. National Register of Historic Places)
Location: Miami, Florida
Added to NRHP: January 4, 1989

The Alfred I. DuPont Building is a historic building in Miami, Florida. It is located at 169 East Flagler Street. On January 4, 1989, it was added to the U.S. National Register of Historic Places. The building was built in 1939 and is 260 feet (87 meters) tall with 21 floors. It is one of Miami's only Art Deco buildings that is over 20 floors.
