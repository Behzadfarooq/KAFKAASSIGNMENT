A View For Freedom: Alfie Roberts Speaks on the Caribbean, Cricket, Montreal, and C.L.R. James first published in June 2005 as the very first publication of The Alfie Roberts Institute.

A View For Freedom: Alfie Roberts Speaks is based on an extensive interview with Alfie Roberts, conducted by David Austin in January 1995, over a year before Roberts died.
