Alfred George Ernest Tasker (born 16 June 1934 in Southwark, London) was an English cricketer who played a single first-class match, keeping wicket for Worcestershire against Cambridge University in 1956. He took a single catch, to dismiss future Test player Bob Barber, but Worcestershire declared in both their innings before he had a chance to bat; he never did get an opportunity.
