Alf Svensson (guitarist)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

This article is about the guitarist. For the politician, see Alf Svensson.

Alf Svensson is a Swedish heavy metal guitarist. He initially played in Oral from 1984 to 1989. He temporarily entered Grotesque and Liers in Wait before joining At the Gates in 1990. He was their guitarist until 1993. In 1994, he reformed Oral to record an EP. He then formed Oxiplegatz, and he released three albums with them.
