Alfie is a 1966 film starring Michael Caine.

Alfie may also refer to:

  • Alfie (2004 film), a 2004 re-make of the film mentioned above
  • Alfie (soundtrack), the soundtrack to the 2004 re-make.
  • Alfie (radio play), a radio play written by Bill Naughton that the above film was based on.
  • Alfie (band), a Manchester-based indie rock band
  • Alfie (song), a Burt Bacharach and Hal David song, first sung by Cilia Black for the 1966 film Alfie
  • Alfie (Lily Allen song), a Lily Allen song
  • The Alfee, a Japanese rock band formerly named Alfie
  • Allan Langer, an Australian Rugby League player
  • Alfie Kohn, an American lecturer and author in the fields of education, psychology and parenting
  • Alfie Moon, a fictional character in the BBC Soap opera EastEnders.
  • Gareth Thomas (rugby player), former Welsh rugby union team captain
  • Daniel Alfredsson, an ice hockey player for the Ottawa Senators in the NHL.
  • "Alfie" is the nickname of Stockport County striker Adam le Fondre (born Glenville le Fondre)
