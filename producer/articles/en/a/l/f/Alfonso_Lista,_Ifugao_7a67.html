Alfonso Lista, Ifugao

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Alfonso Lista is a 4th class municipality in the province of Ifugao, Philippines. According to the 2000 census, it has a population of 21,167 people in 4,275 households.

[edit] Barangays

Alfonso Lista is politically subdivided into 20 barangays.

  • Bangar
  • Busilac
  • Calimag
  • Calupaan
  • Caragasan
  • Dolowog
  • Kiling
  • Namnama
  • Namillangan
  • Pinto
  • Poblacion
  • San Jose
  • San Juan
  • San Marcos
  • San Quintin
  • Santa Maria
  • Santo Domingo (Cabicalan)
  • Little Tadian
  • Ngileb
  • Laya
