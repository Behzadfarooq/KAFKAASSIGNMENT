Alfred Phillips House
(U.S. National Register of Historic Places)
Location: Gibson City, Ford County, Illinois, USA
Coordinates: 40°27′56″N, 88°22′29″W
Added to NRHP: February 5, 1999[1]

The Alfred Phillips House, a Registered Historic Place, is located in the Ford County, Illinois city of Gibson City. It has been listed on the National Register since February 5, 1999. The Phillips House is one of Ford County's four Registered Historic Places. All three of the other sites, the Paxton Carnegie Public Library, Paxton Water Tower and Pump House and the Paxton First Schoolhouse are located in the county seat of Paxton, Illinois.
