Class Concept car
Body style Coupe
Engine Twin-Turbo V6
Transmission 6 speed manual
Length 428.6 cm (169 in)
Width 185.9 cm (73 in)
Designer Walter de Silva

The Alfa Romeo Nuvola concept car was released at the Paris International Auto Show in 1996. It is a 2-door model featuring a 2.5 liter, twin-turbo V6 coupled with a 6-speed manual transmission which puts out 300bhp and can power the car to 166 mph. The Nuvola was named after the legendary Italian race driver Tazio Nuvolari.

The chassis of the Nuvola was designed to handle virtually any body style, from coupe to station wagon.
