ALF, A.L.F. or Alf may refer to:

  • Afar Liberation Front, a political party in Ethiopia.
  • Africa Leadership Forum
  • Alternative Life Form
  • American Leadership Forum
  • American Liver Foundation
  • Animal Liberation Front, a name used by some animal rights activists.
  • Arab Liberation Front, a Palestinian group
  • Artists Liberation Front, 1960s-era guerrilla street fair production company run by the San Francisco Mime Troupe
  • Assisted Living Facility, a facility providing assistance to adults who are aged, infirm or disabled
  • Australian Labour Federation, now the Australian Labor Party
  • Australian Lebanese Foundation

  • ALF (TV series), an American television sitcom and the nickname of that show's title character, also

    • Alf's Hit Talk Show
  • Alfie Alfredo Turnweasal, a character on the children's television programme, The Ziggurat
  • Alf (album), an album by Alison Moyet
  • Alf Mushpie, a character from the comic strip Bloom County

[edit] Places

  • Alf, Germany, a village in Rheinland-Pfalz, Germany
  • Alfreton railway station, United Kingdom, from its National Rail code
  • Alta Airport, Norway, from its IATA airport code

[edit] Science and Technology

  • ALF dynamical entropy, a concept introduced by Alicki, Lindblad and Fannes
  • ALF programming language
  • Activated lactoferrin
  • Atomic line filter, an advanced optical filter

[edit] Personal Name

Alf is short for the names Alfonso and Alfred. In Australia, it is a nickname for Alan/Allan.

Alf is the name of:

  • Alf (mythology), a king in Norse mythology
  • Alf, a Swedish king of the House of Yngling; see Yngvi and Alf
  • Alf Bicknell, a driver and roadie for The Beatles
