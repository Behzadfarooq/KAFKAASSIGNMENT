Alfred Henry Scott

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Alfred Henry Scott (c. 1840 – 28 May 1872) was a bartender and clerk at the Red River Settlement community of Saint Boniface, Manitoba, and was noted for having been selected as a delegate to represent the provisional government of Louis Riel in negotiations with the Government of Canada during the Red River Rebellion of 1869 – 1870.
