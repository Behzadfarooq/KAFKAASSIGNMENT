[edit] Ship classes category

Could you let me know why you removed the Farragut-class category from the Category:Ship classes? The Farragut class is a ship class, so appears that it had been properly categorized. Thanks. Jinian 11:32, 9 December 2005 (UTC)

Yes, so put the article Faragut class in category ship classes (if it's not already there) since it's about the Farragut ship class, but not the category Farragut class, since it's a collection of individual ships of the Farragut class. Actually, it's more about consitency. We have dozens of such ships of a class cats, but only the two farragat cats were put in cat ship classes. Alureiter 12:14, 9 December 2005 (UTC)
Pardon my interuption here, but I am the editor that recently added the ship class to those categories, so I was curious when you changed it.
