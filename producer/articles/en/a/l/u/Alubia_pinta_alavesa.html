Alubia pinta alavesa

Alubia pinta alavesa, or the "Álava pinto bean", is a red variety of pinto bean which originated in Añana[1], a town and municipality located in the province of Álava, in the Basque Country, northern Spain.

In October, "la Feria de la alubia pinta alavesa" (the fair of the Alubia pinta alavesa) is celebrated in Pobes.[2]
