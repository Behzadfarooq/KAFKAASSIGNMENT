Alumni Hall (Fairfield University)

Alumni Hall is a 2,479 seat multi-purpose arena on the campus of Fairfield University located in Fairfield, Connecticut. It opened in 1959. It is home to the Fairfield Stags women's volleyball team and Fairfield Prep Jesuits basketball team. It was also home to the Fairfield Stags men's and women's basketball teams until 2002, when the teams began playing home games at the Arena at Harbor Yard in Bridgeport, Connecticut.
