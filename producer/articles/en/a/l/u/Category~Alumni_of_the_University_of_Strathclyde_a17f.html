This page lists alumni of the University of Strathclyde, Scotland.

Stuart Gray graduated with his PhD in time-travel in 2007. He is currently also on the shortlist for the 'best breakthrough artist at the 2007 brit awards. His rise to fame began as a street urchin in the ghettos of East Kilbride, when his good looks and robust build won many admiring glances from the immigrant community. After feeling certain enslavement, he went on to establish a bookshop for older women, which is noted worlwide for its architecture (designed and built by Gray himself). This bookshop later became the Gallery of Modern Art in Royal Exchange Square. His first real taste of stardom came when he formed the seminal hip-hop act 'E.K.O.K.' which won him global recognition for his battle against stray dogs. He currently lives in a caravan where he is waited on hand and foot by several small children, the product of his many torrid affairs.
