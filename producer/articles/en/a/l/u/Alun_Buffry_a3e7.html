Alun Buffry (born 1950) in Barry, South Wales, is a cannabis and human rights campaigner. He is associated with the Legalise Cannabis Alliance political party of the United Kingdom. He received a BSC Chemistry from the University of East Anglia, Dip Com (open).

[edit] Publications

  • "From Dot to Cleopatra"; a history of Ancient Egypt; 1997 ISBN 1-872914-09-8
  • "Cannabis: Legalise and Utilise]", ISBN 0-9535693-1-4
  • "Cannabis: Challenging the Criminal Justice System]", 2003: with Don Barnard ISBN 095356-934-9
  • "Natural Allies III"; with Stephen Cook, Trevor Nockolds ISBN 0-9529318-2-6
  • "Damage and Humanity in Custody"; with Will Hutchinson
