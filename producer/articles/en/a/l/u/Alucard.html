"Alucard" is "Dracula" spelled backwards, and as such is often in Dracula or vampire related fiction.

Alucard may also refer to:

  • Alucard, a character in the ongoing series of video games, Castlevania
  • Alucard, character of the manga and anime series, Hellsing
  • Alucard von Mosquiton, the title character of the anime Master Mosquiton '99.
  • The name of several characters in several other vampire-related movies/series and computer games:
    • Son of Dracula
    • Dracula A.D. 1972
    • The Batman Vs. Dracula
    • A monster in the game .hack//QUARANTINE
    • "A(lexander) Lucard in Dracula: The Series

  • Alucard is also a Belgian alternative rock band known for creating a style called "Dark Romantism".
