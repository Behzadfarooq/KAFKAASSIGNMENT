Master thesis: "Implementing cryptographic algorithms in a micro-controller"
The purpose of the thesis is to secure the embedded applications of NEC’s 32-bit V850 micro-controller; which uses flash memory. Applications; which can be updated in the field (software upgrades, bug fixes ...etc) are encrypted on the PC side, sent to the client, decrypted by the V850 and rewritten to the flash memory for the appropriate use. Two cryptographic schemes were used; namely AES and RSA.
