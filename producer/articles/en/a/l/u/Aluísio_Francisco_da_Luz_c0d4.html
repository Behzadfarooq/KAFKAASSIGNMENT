Aluísio Francisco da Luz

Aluísio Francisco da Luz, simply known as Índio (born March 1, 1931 in Cabedelo, PB) is a former Brazilian football player.

A very good striker, Indio was an important player in Flamengo's 1953-54-55 Rio State Championships titles.

[edit] Clubs

  • 1948-1948 - Bangu
  • 1949-1957 - Flamengo
  • 1957-1958 - Corinthians
  • 1959-1965 - RCD Espanyol
  • 1965-1965 - América-RJ

[edit] Honours

  • Rio State Championship 1953, 1954, 1955
  • 1954 FIFA World Cup - Quarter-finals
