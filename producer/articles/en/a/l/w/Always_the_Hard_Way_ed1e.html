Always the Hard Way
Studio album by Terror
Released 2006

Always the Hard Way is an album by hardcore band Terror, released on the Roadrunner label in 2006 (see 2006 in music). The album also features a guest appearance by Mr. Dibbs and MURS (credited as "Mr. Murs") as a rap interlude and on "So Close to Defeat" a special vocal performance is given by Eddie Sutton of Leeway.
