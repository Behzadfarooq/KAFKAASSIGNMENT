Alwar (Lok Sabha constituency)

Alwar is a Lok Sabha parliamentary constituency of Rajasthan.

[edit] Members of Parliament

  • 1952: Shobha Ram, Indian National Congress
  • 1957: Shobha Ram, Indian National Congress
  • 1962: Mukat Behari Lal Bhargava, Indian National Congress
  • 1967: Bholanath Master, Indian National Congress
  • 1971: H P Sharma, Indian National Congress
  • 1977: Ramjilal Yadav, Janata Party
  • 1980: Ram Singh Yadav, Indian National Congress
  • 1984: Ram Singh Yadav, Indian National Congress
  • 1989: Ramjilal Yadav, Janata Party
  • 1991: Mahendra Kumari, Bharatiya Janata Party
  • 1996: Nawal Kishore Sharma, Indian National Congress
  • 1998: -
  • 1999: Jaswant Singh Yadav, Bharatiya Janata Party
  • 2004: Dr. Karan Singh Yadav, Indian National Congress
