Always Forever Now is After The Fall's sophomore album, named after the song off Original Soundtracks 1. It was released on August 21, 2005 by Festival Mushroom Records. The album was produced by Melbourne's Richard Stolz and mixed by American Kevin Shirley.

The album debuted at Number 36 on the Australian ARIA charts and stayed in the Top 40 for 8 weeks. The album achieved Album Of The Week on Triple J Radio with the first single from the album, "Concrete Boots" added to Triple J Radio, MTV, Channel V and Nova FM. The single also made Triple J's Hottest 100 coming in at No. 30. Subsequently, it was added to Triple J's Hottest 100 CD.

The band released its second single from the album in early September 2005, "The Fighter". It also received great rotation on Triple J Radio, MTV, Channel V and Nova FM. The latest, and third, single from the album is "Outta Mind".
