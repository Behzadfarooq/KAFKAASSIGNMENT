Alwyn Morris, C.M. (born 22 November 1957) is a Canadian Olympic medalist canoeist.

A member of the Mohawk nation in Kahnawake, he won a gold medal with Hugh Fisher in the 1000-metre kayak pairs and a bronze in the 500-metre pairs at the 1984 Summer Olympics.

In 1985, he was made a Member of the Order of Canada.
