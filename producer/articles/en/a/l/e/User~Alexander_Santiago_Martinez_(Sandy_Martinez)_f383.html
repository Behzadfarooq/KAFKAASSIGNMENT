User:Alexander Santiago Martinez (Sandy Martinez)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

My Name is Alexander S. Martinez. My childhood friends call me Sandy. I was born in Gaspar Hernandez, Dominican Republic and came to the United States in 1985.

My education background includes:

High School Diploma from Liceo Gregorio Luperon in 1982.

Associate in Applied Science Degree in Human Services from New York City Technical College in 1989.

Bachelor of Business Administration Degree from Baruch College of the City University of New York in 1993.

Masters Degree in Public Administration from Columbia University in 2004

My Work History includes:

Weekend and Summer College Coordinator, New York City Technical College 1987-1992

Assistant Program Director, HIRE, Inc. 1993-2000

Assistant Director, Office of the University Chaplain, Columbia University 2001-2005

Clinical Supervisor, Camden County Partnership for Children, New Jersey 2005 - Present
