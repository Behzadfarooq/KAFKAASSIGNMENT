Alex Taylor (born Adriana Molinari on November 26, 1970 in Buenos Aires, Argentina) was a pornographic actress who mainly worked for Vivid Entertainment from 1998 to 2004. She resides in Orlando, Florida and is no longer active in the adult industry.

[edit] Biography

Taylor, originally Argentine, moved to the United States in 1982. She won the Miss New Hampshire USA 1991 pageant in late 1990 and competed in the Miss USA 1991 pageant held in Wichita, Kansas in February 1991.

Alex Taylor was the August 1994 Penthouse Pet and appeared in the adult magazine over 30 times. She was featured in pictorials of a bisexual nature.

She had a web presence at the URL of www.alex-taylor.com, which is no longer active.
