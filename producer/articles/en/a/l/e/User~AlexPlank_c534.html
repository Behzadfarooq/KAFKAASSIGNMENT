[edit] User Summary

Alexandros(aplank) My name is Alexander Plank and I live in Charlottesville, Virginia. I have Asperger's syndrome, which means I have difficulty working out what other people are thinking. If you tell me that i shouldnt do something, i might get insulted, or I might think you are just kidding. Dont leave me the responsibility of reading between the social lines. The public computer that I use at school is User:208.34.32.252, in case I accidently log out.

In my free time, I enjoy contributing to Wikipedia, playing with Gentoo Linux, and watching Seinfeld, Smallville, and The West Wing. I also contribute to fr.wikipedia.org.
