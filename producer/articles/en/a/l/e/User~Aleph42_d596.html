[edit] wikipedia phylosophy

I'm probably an inclusionist , in particular I think it doesn't hurt wikipedia to have thorough pop-culture and video-games related articles; for these articles I would be an immediatist (honnestly, who would need any battlefield 2 related article in 100 years ?), while being quite an eventualist for politic and historic articles. I think wikipedia is not paper, not an encyclopedia either but a website. What count is that it still exist and be popular in 100 years. So it needs pop culture video games and immediatly useful article to lure people now, so it keep growing. That's what happened to me for exemple.
