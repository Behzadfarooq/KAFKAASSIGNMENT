Aleksei Vladimirovich Berezoutski (Russian: Алексей Владимирович Березуцкий) (born March 20, 1982 in Moscow) is a Russian football defender. He began his professional career in 1999 at the age of 17 in Torpedo-ZIL. He is now playing for CSKA Moscow. He is a Russia national football team regular. He tends to play as a central defender but he can play as fullback, wingback or even as a winger. He is a great defender who can join attacks from the wing.

Alexey started to play football in sport school "Smena" in Moscow. He is married and has a daughter named Alyona. His twin brother, Vassili, is also a CSKA defender.

Berezoutski's current contract with CSKA Moscow runs until 2011.
