    Alexander Gnilitskiy

Alexander Gnilitskiy (* 1961 in Kharkiv). He is the leading artist of Ukrainian Transavantgarde and works as painter, Video-Concept-Artist and graphic. He currently lives and works in Kiev, Ukraine and Munich, Germany.

[edit] Education

  • 1981 - 1987: Ukraine Stat Academy of Fine Art
  • 1976 - 1980: Kharkiv Fine Art College

[edit] Works and Reception

Alexander Gnilitskiy is one of the pioneers of Southern Wave in painting. He is the most predictable artist in the Ukrainian artistic prrocess, where uncertainty as a way of life provokes the fear of distinctness. Consequently the strategy of his work is escape and permanent mimicry, multiplied to absurdity of a dad-kind. Recently the artist again turned to the quasi-irrational painting, re-coding the mythos and semantics of the heroes of cult animation films, fairy-tales and legends into a wave of personal, individualized myth.

[edit] Exhibitions

  • 2007: Venice Biennale, Ukrainian Pavillon
  • 2006: Bereznitsky-Gallery (L-art), Kiev, Ukraine, Art Moscow
  • 1998: Gallery of the Center for Contemporary Art at NaKUMA, Kiev, Ukraine, The Thing within inside
  • 1994: Szuper Gallery, Munich, Germany, Dairy Maid
  • 1991: 1.0 Gallery, Moscow, Russia, According to the Plan
