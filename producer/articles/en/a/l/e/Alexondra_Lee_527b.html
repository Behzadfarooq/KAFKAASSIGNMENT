Alexondra Lee born 8 February 1975, is an American actress. She is originally from Pennsylvania.[citation needed] Her parents are Mae and Harry Lee. Lee studied ballet since the age of four and has danced in The Nutcracker Suite with the New York City Ballet Company.[1] She began dancing with the New York City Ballet at the age of seven. She starred in the short lived Special Unit 2 as Detective Kate Benson.

[edit] Trivia

  • Has been voted as one of Stuff magazine's 101 "Most Beautiful Women in The World" in 2001. She was number 81.
