Aleksander Malota is a fictional character in the British television soap opera Hollyoaks. He is played by actor Jon Lolis.

[edit] Character history

Aleksander, an Albanian immigrant, first appeared in Hollyoaks in 2007. Davey Thomas arranged for him to marry Jacqui McQueen so that he could stay in the United Kingdom. Despite Jacqui's reluctance he is determined to see it through for the sake of his family in Albania. He is living in the McQueen household.

There is an unspoken attraction between Aleksander and Jacqui's sister Carmel.
