Alec Powers (born July 17, 1975) is a bisexual American pornographic actor (porn star) who has appeared in gay and bisexual pornographic films. Born in El Paso, Texas, Powers grew up in France and spent most of his childhood there. His father is Scottish and Swedish, and his mother is French Hungarian. He reports enjoying extreme sports such as bungee jumping and sky diving as well as snowboarding and scuba diving. In his spare time he enjoys traveling and owning cars (reportedly he’s owned more than 35 cars since he was 16 years old). Currently he lives in Las Vegas but travels to Los Angeles to visit family and friends. He enjoys working out and maintaining a healthy lifestyle.

He reports himself as being versatile but prefers to be a top and his personal philosophy is to only have safe sex. He has an athletically muscular build with a naturally hairy chest which he does shave on occasion. He was discovered when he was 18 and has done many films since then.
