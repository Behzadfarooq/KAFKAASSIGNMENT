Here, you can claim a color in the RGB spectrum as your very own! I got the idea on IRC while Denelson83 was asking for color name suggestions for a 9 × 9 × 9 RGB table. The goal is to eventually name at least 256 colors after a Wikipedian. One color per user and one user per color, please. To add yourself to the list, use {{subst:User:Alerante/CAC|color|username}}, where color is a hex triplet or CSS color name.
