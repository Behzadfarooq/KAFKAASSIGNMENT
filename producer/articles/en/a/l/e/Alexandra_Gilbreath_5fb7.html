Alexandra Gilbreath (born 28 March 1969) is a British actress.

Gilbreath is widely known for her work both on stage and onscreen (both film and television). She gained popularity with the Royal Shakespeare Company on such works as The Taming of the Shrew, Romeo and Juliet, The Tamer Tamed, and The Winter's Tale. She is currently appearing in their production of Merry Wives: the Musical.

Her work on the British television series Monarch of the Glen as Stella Moon, brought her worldwide attention. Gilbreath also played serial killer Pat Kitson in The Bill.

She was born in Chalfont St Giles and trained at the London Academy of Music and Drama.
