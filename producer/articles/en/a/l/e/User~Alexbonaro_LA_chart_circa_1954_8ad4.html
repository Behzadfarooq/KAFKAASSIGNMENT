More Notes

  • "Great chart. Good work as usual. Cool

Although on Nick Licata, I don't think he was a Capo, at least not under Dragna. I remember Frattiano writing in his book on the making ceremony when he was promoted to Capo. He said it was rumored that a new Capo would be named and everyone was expecting Licata to get the job he got it instead which inraged Licata and probably started the beef between them. As a way to placate him, Dragna allowed Licata to report directly to him and not be under any Capo. Pogo"

  • "From what I understand was that Brooklier was briefly Licata's UnderBoss when he died. That plus the fact that LA was decimated by generational attrition and fading fast by that point likely contributed to Brooklier getting elected as Boss. There was really no one left to take it (who wanted it) by that point. Pogo"
