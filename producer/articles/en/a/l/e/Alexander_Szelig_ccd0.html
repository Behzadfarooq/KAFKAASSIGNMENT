Alexander Szelig

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Olympic medal record
Men's bobsleigh
Gold 1994 Lillehammer Four-man

Alexander Szelig (born 6 February 1966) is a retired German bobsledder who competed in the bobsleigh events at the 1994 Winter Olympics in Lillehammer, Norway, winning a gold medal in the four-person bobsleigh event with teammates Harald Czudaj, Karsten Brannasch and Olaf Hampel. He was also on the Germany II team that finished sixth at the 1992 Winter Olympics.
