Alesia Turava

Alesia Turava (born 6 December 1979) is a Belarusian middle distance runner. She is a former world record holder in 3000 metre steeplechase with 9:16.51 minutes, achieved in Gdansk on 27 July 2002. She is still third on the all-time list, behind Gulnara Samitova and Docus Inzikuru. Still, Turava mostly competes over 1500 metres.

She won the gold medal in the inaugural 3000 m steeplechase for women held at the 2006 European Athletics Championships in Gothenburg.

Her sister Ryta Turava is a successful race walker.
