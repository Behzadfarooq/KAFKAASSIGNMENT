Aleksin (Russian: Але́ксин) is a town in Tula Oblast, Russia, located 71 km northwest of Tula. It is the administrative center of Aleksinsky District. Population: 68,156 (2002 Census); 74,274 (1989 Census).

[edit] History

The town was first mentioned in 1348 in the Nikon Chronicle. Because of its situation on the Oka River it was, for a while, an important inland port.
