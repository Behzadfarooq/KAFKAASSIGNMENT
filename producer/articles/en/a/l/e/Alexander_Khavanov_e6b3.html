Alexander Khavanov (born January 30, 1972 in Moscow, Russia) is a professional ice hockey defenceman who last played in the NHL for the Toronto Maple Leafs. He also goes by the nickname Sasha.

Khavanov was drafted in the 8th round (232nd overall) by the St. Louis Blues in the 1999 NHL Entry Draft.

Khavanov has now left the NHL to play 2006 / 2007 season with HCD Davos in Switzerland.
