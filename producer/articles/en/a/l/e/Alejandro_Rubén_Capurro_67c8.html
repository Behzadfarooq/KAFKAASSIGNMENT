Alejandro Rubén Capurro is an Argentine football midfielder. He was born on October 31, 1980, in the city of San Lorenzo in the Santa Fe Province of Argentina. He currently plays for Sakaryaspor of the Turkcell Super League in Turkey.

Capurro started his career at Colón de Santa Fe in the Primera Division Argentina in 1999. He played for them until 2006 when he and his Colón teammate Franco Cángele were signed by Sakaryaspor in 2006.
