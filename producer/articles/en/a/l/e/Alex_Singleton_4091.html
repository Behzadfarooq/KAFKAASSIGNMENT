Alex Singleton

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Alex Singleton (born 25 October 1979 is Director-General of the Globalisation Institute, a trade and international development think tank in London, the United Kingdom. He was educated at Dulwich College and the University of St. Andrews.

Described as "the high priest of globalisation" by Mark Malloch Brown (former head of the UN Development Programme, now Kofi Annan's Chef de Cabinet), he is a regular commentator on television and radio programmes such as Newsnight, The Today Programme, Channel Four News, CNBC Europe and Sky News.

He was formerly Research Director of the Adam Smith Institute and an Institute for Humane Studies Charles G. Koch Fellow in Washington DC.
