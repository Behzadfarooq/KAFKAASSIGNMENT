Alexander Alexandrovich Bryantsev (Russian: Александр Александрович Брянцев;1883 - 1961) was a Soviet Theater director, creator (1921) and the Chief Director (1921-1961) of the Leningrad Youth Theatre (since 1980 Briantsev Youth Theatre).

Alexander Bryantsev won the Stalin Prize in 1950, and was awarded People's Artist of the USSR in 1956.

Alexander's son, Dmitry Alexandrovich Bryantsev is a renowned ballet master.
