Alexei Volkov (ice hockey)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

You have new messages (last change).

Alexei Volkov, (born March 15, 1980), is a Russian-born hockey player who was drafted in the third round, 76th overall, in the 1998 NHL Entry Draft by the Los Angeles Kings. He is currently a goalie for Vityaz Chekhov in the Russian Hockey Super League.
