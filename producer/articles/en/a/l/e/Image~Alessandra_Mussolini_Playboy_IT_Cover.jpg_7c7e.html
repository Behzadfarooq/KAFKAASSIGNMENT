[edit] Fair use for Alessandra Mussolini

The image linked here is claimed to be used under fair use as:

  1. it is a historically significant photo of a famous individual;
  2. Mussolini is notable as the descendant of an infamous politician, and leader of a political party; that she was also a famous enough topless model to gain the cover of Playboy is notable.
  3. Its inclusion in the article adds significantly to the article because it shows the subject of this article and how the event depicted was very historically significant to the general public. AnonEMouse 15:16, 20 April 2006 (UTC)

To reframe the issue within a fair use legal analysis:

  1. The image is the low resolution scan of only the cover of a magazine, and so does not constitute a substantial copy of the original creative work
  2. The image is used in the transformative context of an informative article about the subject featured on the cover and in the issue, and that article discusses this magazine and its significance for the subject
  3. The image has no independent commercial value that can compete with the original magazine
