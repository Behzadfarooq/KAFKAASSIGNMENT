Alex Ebert

Alex Ebert is the lead singer for the Los Angeles based band, Ima Robot. Before becoming a singer, he wanted to become a rapper. He is recognizable for his frentic onstage performances, unique fashion sense, and peculiar haircuts.

He appeared as the delivery man in the VHS or Beta video for You Got Me.

The Ima Robot video for Lovers in Captivity was produced independently of their Virgin record label and was featured in an Out Magazine[1] article. The video itself features lead singer Alex Ebert and Brady Corbet. (24, Thunderbirds, and Mysterious Skin)
