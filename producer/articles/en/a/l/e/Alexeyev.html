Alexeyev

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Alexeyev, Alekseyev, Alexeev or Alekseev (Russian: Алексе́ев) is a common Russian last name and may refer to several people. Often the same name appears in English in several different transliterations. Similarly, Alexeyeva, Alekseyeva, Alexeeva and Alekseeva are female versions of the same last name.
