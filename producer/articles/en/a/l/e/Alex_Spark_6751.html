Alex Sparks was a Scottish football player who Played for Preston North End Football club.


Alex Sparks
Personal information
Date of birth October 16, 1949
Place of birth    Stenhousemuir, Scotland
Date of death    1993


* Appearances (Goals)

Footballer - Preston North End (1967-1976) Bradford City (1976-1978)

Joined Preston North End as a Junior from his native Stenhousemuir. Made his debut coming on as substitute against Rotherham United on 30th December 1967.

Made 225 appearances for Preston (18 as substitute), scoring 6 goals between 1967 amd 1976 and winning a Third Division championship medal in 1970/71 season.

Joined Motherwell briefly in 1976 but left to join Bradford City without making a first team appearance. Made 34 appearances for The Bantams between 1976 and 1978.
