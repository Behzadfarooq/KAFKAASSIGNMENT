Alexa Rae (born December 10, 1980 in Atlanta, Georgia) is an American pornographic actress.

During her youth she was an avid horse rider for fourteen years, even competing in events at a national level. One of her most memorable experience is having sex with her ex-boyfriend on top of her horse "Chance."

She attended Catholic high school at St. Pius X Catholic High School in Atlanta but left between her Junior and Senior year.

She broke into the adult film industry in 1998, at age 18, shortly after she began dancing in Atlanta--under the name Fantasy--at The Gold Club.

She created her porn stage name by using the name of Billy Joel's daughter Alexa Ray Joel.

She appeared in a music video clip for "Complicated" by the all-girl punk band Lo-Ball. Alexa was also featured in a Time magazine article about Cyber sex.

Alexa continues to be active in the adult film industry.
