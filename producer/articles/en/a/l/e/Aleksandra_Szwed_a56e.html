Aleksandra Szwed

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Aleksandra Szwed called Ola was born on August 8, 1990 in Warsaw, Poland from a Polish mother and Nigerian father. She is well-known in Poland as a child actress starring regularly since 1999 in the Rodzina Zastępcza television series, which, in 2004, changed its formula, and also changed its title to Rodzina Zastępcza Plus. Before getting into television, Ola was singing, and dancing, with a group of children backing the popular Polish singer for children, Majka Jeżowska.
