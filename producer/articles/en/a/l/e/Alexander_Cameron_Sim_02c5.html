Alexander Cameron Sim

Alexander Cameron Sim (1840-1900) was born in Aberlour, Scotland on August 28, 1840. He died on November 28, 1900 having lived most of his adult life in Japan.

Sim founded the Kobe Regatta & Athletic Club at Kobe on September 23, 1870. He was a pharmacist and also introduced lemonade (locally called ramune) to Japan. He is buried in the Kobe Foreign cemetery on Mount Futatabi.
