Alexa

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Alexa may refer to:

  • Alexa (name), including that of Canadian politician Alexa McDonough and Popworld presenter Alexa Chung
  • Alexa (typeface)
  • Alexa Internet, a subsidiary of Amazon.com that is best known for operating alexa.com that provides information on the web traffic to other websites
  • Alexa Vega (1988-), a child actress, known for portraying Carmen Cortez in the Spy Kids trilogy
  • Billy Joel owned a boat named The Alexa, which he wrote his song The Downeaster Alexa about. He also named his daughter Alexa.
