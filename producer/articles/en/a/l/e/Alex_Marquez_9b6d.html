Alex Marquez

Alex Marquez, born in Miami, Florida, is a major contributor to the Florida death metal scene. He has played drums for the bands Anger, Demolition Hammer, Disincarnate, Divine Empire, Malevolent Creation, Resurrection, and Solstice. His latest role involves performing vocals for the newly formed bands Apocalypse Rising and Maximum Reign.
