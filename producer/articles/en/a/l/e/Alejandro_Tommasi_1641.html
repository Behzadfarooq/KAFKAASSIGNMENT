Alejandro Tommasi

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Alejandro Tommasi (born August 14, 1957 in Mexico City, Distrito Federal) is a Mexican television, stage and film actor. He enrolled in Televisa's Centro de Educacion Artistica (CEA), where he studied acting, dancing and singing. Through out his successful acting career, Tommasi has participated in numerous telenovelas, plays and in some films.
