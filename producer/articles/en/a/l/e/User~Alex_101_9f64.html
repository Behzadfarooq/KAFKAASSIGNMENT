Hi, everyone! My name is Alex and I'm a big fan of punk rock! I had to name my account as "Alex 101" since "Alex101" was already taken. Oh, well!

I discovered Wikipedia and had some anon edits in 2004/2005. I finally registered on Sunday, November 20, 2005.

I am also the Alex101 who contributes on Ultimate-Guitar.com, a website where you post news, columns, tabs, etc. I also have an account on Lyriki where I post lyrics by any one of my favorite bands. I have also been recently active at other websites like Musician Dictionary and Sputnikmusic.

If anyone was wondering where I've been, I've been banned since March 2006 up to six months, due to an edit war on the Angels and Airwaves. Although, I've been editing anonymously ever since I was banned. However, I came back in September 2006 and have continued editing ever since.

Recently, I moved my all userboxes to a new page since I had too many on my user page.
