Alexandros Tzorvas (born August 12, 1982 in Athens) is a Greek goalkeeper currently playing for Panathinaikos FC. Be began his career in Panathinaikos FC Youth Academy and played mainly for not very known teams for about 4 years. In 2005 Panathinaikos recalled him from loan and now he is the third goalkeeper behind Mario Galinovic and Pierre Ebede.
