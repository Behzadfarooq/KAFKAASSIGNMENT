Alexander Horváth (born 28 December 1938) is a former Slovak football player. He played for Czechoslovakia, for which he played 26 matches and scored 3 goals.

He was a participant at the 1970 FIFA World Cup.

He played mostly for Slovan Bratislava and also briefly for RWD Molenbeek in Belgium, where he emigrated to.
