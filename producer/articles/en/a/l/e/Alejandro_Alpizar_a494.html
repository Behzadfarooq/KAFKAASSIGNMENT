Alejandro Alpízar Delgado (born June 14, 1979) is a Costa Rican soccer player, who plays for Deportivo Saprissa.

Before moving to Saprissa, he played for Municipal Pérez Zeledón and Saprissa's archrivals L.D. Alajuelense.

He is considered a very competent striker, with natural skills for scoring, quickness, great shooting and excellent view of the game, making him a very complete player.
