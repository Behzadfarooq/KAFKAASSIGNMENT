Alex Comfort

Alexander Comfort (February 10, 1920 - March 26, 2000) was a medical professional, gerontologist, anarchist, pacifist and writer, best known for The Joy of Sex, which played a part in what is often called the sexual revolution. He was also the author of many other books on a variety of topics.
