Alexeyevka, Belgorod Oblast

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

For other uses, see Alexeyevka.

Alexeyevka (Russian: Алексе́евка) is a town in Belgorod Oblast, Russia, located on the Tikhaya Sosna River (Don's basin) 306 km east of Belgorod at 50.6389° N 38.7011° E. Population: 39,312 (2002 Census); 36,641 (1989 Census).

Founded in 1685 as Alexeyevka (or Alexeyevskaya) sloboda after Alexey Cherkassky, its first owner. Town status since 1954.
