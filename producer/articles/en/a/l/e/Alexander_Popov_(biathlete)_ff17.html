Alexandr Vladimirovich Popov (Russian: Александр Владимирович Попов) (born February 22, 1963 in Tobolsk) is a Russian biathlete who competed for the USSR, the Unified Team and Belarus. Since 1999 he is the Head Coach of Belarus National Biathlon Team.

[edit] Career

  • Winter Olympic Games
  • 1988 Calgary - Gold medal on the relay
  • 1992 Albertville - Silver medal on the relay
  • World Championships'
  • 1987 Lake Placid - Silver medal on the relay
  • 1989 Feistritz - Silver medal on the relay
  • 1991 Minsk - Silver medal on the relay and on the 20 km individual
  • 1995 Anterselva - Bronze medal on the relay
  • 1996 Ruhpolding - Bronze medal on the relay
