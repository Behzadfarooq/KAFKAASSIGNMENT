Alexander Edgar Lascelles (born 13 May 1980), styled The Hon. Alexander Lascelles, is the third child and second son of David Lascelles, Viscount Lascelles and his first wife Margaret, Viscountess Lascelles. His elder siblings were born out of wedlock and therefore have no claims to the Harewood titles: Emily Lascelles (born 1976) and Benjamin Lascelles (born 1978). He also has one younger brother, Edward Lascelles (born 1982). Currently he is 41st in line for the British Throne. He is heir, after his father, to the Earldom of Harewood.
