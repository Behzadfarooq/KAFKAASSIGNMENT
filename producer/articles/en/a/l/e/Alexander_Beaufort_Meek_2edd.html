Alexander Beaufort Meek

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Alexander Beaufort Meek (July 17, 1814 (Columbia, South Carolina) - November 30, 1865 (Columbus, Mississippi)) was an American politician, lawyer, writer and poet. He served as Alabama's Attorney General in 1836.
