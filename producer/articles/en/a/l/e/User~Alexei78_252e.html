[edit] Weigmanism

Weigmanism was created by Alex Weigman (1992-) and is a form of government that is a combination of democracy, militarism, and dictatorship. Although it does not have complete democracy, it still has some assets of it. The country with Weigmanism is run by one ruler and a commitee of people from the military and somewhat politics. ____________________________________________________________________ Rights

Though weigmanism does have a form of democracy, it is not based on the United States constitution. You do not have the freedom of speech, although you do have the freedom of religon. The military primarily is the base of the government as economy and other parts of government come second.

Eye for an Eye, Life for a life is the main judgement. But a judge and jury will be there to decide if there is a better punishment or to make the punishment less. ____________________________________________________________________ Ruler

The ruler of the country is the ruler for life unless the commitee (military and people) vote him/her out. The ruler cannot modify rights (create or delete) without the vote of the commitee. ____________________________________________________________________ The People

The people own somethings while the government owns the rest. The people are responsible for: -Their family -Their land -Their house -Their businesses

Everything else is run by the government. ____________________________________________________________________ Economy

A strong government backed up by a strong military is the prime point torwards Weigmanism. The economy is judged by what you have and not what you can make. If you have the material to build something and export it do so, but if you plan on exporting something that you cannot make, it will not happen. ____________________________________________________________________ Military

The military is the backbone to Weigmanism. It's responsible for: -Protecting the people -Protecting the country

Any rebellions, fights, etc. will be put down. Even small street fights will be put down by military force. The military is in control of the ruler and no one else. ____________________________________________________________________ Analasis

Though this form of government does not seem very popular, it would be used for nations not under good government right now. ____________________________________________________________________ (more to come)
