Alexander Shubin (born July 14, 1983 in Moscow, Russia) is a Russian figure skater.

He started to skate with the age of 5. His coach is Viktor Kudriavtsev since autumn 2004. Alexander Shubin represents the club IP Moskwich and therefore Russia. 2003 he became World Junior Champion, but suffered since from injuries.
