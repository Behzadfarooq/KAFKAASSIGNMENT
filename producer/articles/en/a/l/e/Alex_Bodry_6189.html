Alex Bodry

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Alex Bodry (born October 3, 1958 in Dudelange) is a politician from Luxembourg.

After attending a grammar school in Esch-sur-Alzette, Alex Bodry studied law at the University of Paris. He later became an advocate. From 1982 to 1989, he was a member of Dudelange city council. From 1989 to 1999 he was a minister. Since 2004, he has been president of the Luxembourg Socialist Workers' Party.
