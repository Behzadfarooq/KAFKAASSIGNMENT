Alexander Charles David Drogo Montagu, 13th Duke of Manchester (born 11 December 1962) is a British hereditary peer, the son of the 12th Duke of Manchester. He married Wendy Dawn Buford, and has two children:

  • Alexander Michael Charles Drogo Montagu, Viscount Mandeville, (born 13 May 1993) (styled Lord Kimbolton until 2002)
  • The Lady Ashley Faith Maxine Nell Montagu (born 16 June 1999)

The Duke lives in California.
