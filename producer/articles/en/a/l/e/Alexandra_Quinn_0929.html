Alexandria Quinn (born Diane Purdie Steward)[1] is a Canadian porn star who has been active in the industry since the late 1980s. Quinn was born in Toronto, Ontario.

[edit] Adult film career

Quinn started in the adult industry working as a table dancer at a strip club. Erica Boyer, worked at the club one night as a feature dancer, Quinn asked Boyer about how she could become an actress in pornographic movies. Boyer told Quinn that she could stay with her until she found a place of her own, and took Quinn to a casting agent.

Quinn's first year in the industry took place prior to her 18th birthday. Age 18 is the legal minimum in which United States citizens may appear in pornography. Like the case of Traci Lords which came several years earlier, it was discovered in the early 1990s that Quinn's earlier films were made before she was 18. According to a 2000 interview with Quinn, the discovery occurred when she forgot to bring her fake ID to a session and used her real ID, thinking they would not notice the difference.[2]

The videos were removed from the market. Many were edited to remove all footage of Quinn and re-released. Determining which films were illegal was simplified as Quinn obtained breast augmentation for her 18th birthday. Amid the controversy, Quinn left the porn industry for a time, appearing on news programs such as Hard Copy and blasting the porn industry. She resumed her pornographic career in the mid-1990s. She was still an active porn star as of 2005, usually playing the role of the older woman.
