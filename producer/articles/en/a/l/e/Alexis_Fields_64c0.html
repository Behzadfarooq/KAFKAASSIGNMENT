Alexis Fields

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Alexis Fields (born in 1979 in California) is an American actress. She has guest starred on sitcoms including Roc, Moesha, The Secret World Of Alex Mack, Sister, Sister, and Kenan & Kel during the '90s. In 2000, she also played in House Party 4.
