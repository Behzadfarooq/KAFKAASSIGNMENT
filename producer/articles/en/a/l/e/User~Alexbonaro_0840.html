[edit] Crime Family charts

Every chart posted below is from the Real Deal Forum (connected to Kenny Torro's Mafia-International.com website). Each chart is here for cross referencing other articles; therefore they must not be treated as articles in themselves and must not be edited. Each chart has a section for comments below them, you may post conflicting views, mistakes, comments etc. down there but the charts should stay in their original format. I may edit charts to make them more readable for Wikipedia however no one else is to do this. Any questions about the charts can be answered on the "Comments" section of each chart, my talk page, or the Real Deal Forum itself. Thankyou for your consideration; each of these charts took countless hours to create and are here as a resource. Alexbonaro 06:05, 1 December 2006 (UTC)
