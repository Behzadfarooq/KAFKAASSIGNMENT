Alexander Potresov

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Alexander Nikolayevich Potresov (Russian: Александр Николаевич Потресов) (September 1(13), 1869, Moscow - July 11, 1934, Paris) was a Russian social democrat and one of the leaders of Menshevism.
