Alexandrovsk (Russian: Алекса́ндровск) is a town in Perm Krai, Russia, situated some 185 km north-east of Perm on the Lytva River (Kama's tributary). Founded in 1783; urban-type settlement status granted in 1929; town status since 1951. Population: 16,231 (2002 Census); 20,073 (1989 Census).
