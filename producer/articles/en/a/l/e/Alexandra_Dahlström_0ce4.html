Alexandra Dahlström (born February 12, 1984) is a Swedish actress.

She was born in Gävle, Sweden, and grew up in Stockholm. Her mother is Russian, and her father Swedish. She speaks both Swedish and Russian.

Dahlström gained international attention in 1998, after starring as "Elin" in the film Fucking Åmål (Show Me Love, English title). For this, she won the Guldbagge Award for Best Actress, together with Rebecka Liljeberg. Despite receiving numerous offers to act in other films, she declined, stating that she wanted to concentrate on finishing school.

When once asked when she was younger what she wanted to be when she grew up, Dahlström stated: "Serial killer, or rockstar."[1]

In 2002, Dahlström acted as assistant director and Russian translator, alongside Fucking Åmål director Lukas Moodysson, with his film Lilya 4-ever.

Dahlström lived alone in Stockholm after graduating high school. She continued to act in theatre. After a several-year break, Dahlström again starred in the film Fröken Sverige (Miss Sweden), for which she received favorable reviews.

During the autumn of 2004 she was the DJ for the biggest late night talk show in Sweden, called 'Late Night with Luuk', which she had been originally interviewed on in May 1998 with Rebecka Liljeberg for her acting in Fucking Åmål [1]. On her debut as DJ for the show, the guests were R.E.M. and British actor Paul Bettany.

After completing her studies, she now lives in Rome, Italy and is again pursuing a serious acting career.

From the 5th of March 2007 Alexandra wil be participating in the Dutch soap opera Goede Tijden, Slechte Tijden on RTL 4. She will play a Swedish exchanges student called Skylar Nilsson.
