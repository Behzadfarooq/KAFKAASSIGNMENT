Alexander the Greatest was a British sitcom that starred Gary Warren that lasted for two series from 1971 to 1972. It was written by Bernard Kops. It was made for the ITV network by ATV. It is not currently available on DVD.

[edit] Plot

Alexander Green was a sixteen-year-old boy, who lives in Golders Green in London, who wants to leave his middle-class Jewish home. He was based on the writer's fourteen-years-old Adam. Alexander's parents, Joe and Fay Green, tried to understand him and he had a sister Renata.
