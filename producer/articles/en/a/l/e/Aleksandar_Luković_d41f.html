Aleksandar Luković (born 23 October 1982 in Kraljevo) is a Serbian international football (soccer) player. He plays the defender position for the team, Udinese Calcio, on loan from Ascoli Calcio 1898.

On January 27, 2007 he played his first Serie A match for Udinese against Torino [1]

He made his national team debut against Poland in 15 August 2005
