[edit] Summary

Alexander H. Rice - Mayor, Boston, Massachusetts (1856); and later Governor of Massachusetts. This image is contrast-enhanced from the onlined Library of Congress collection; see catalog information below. It is now in the public domain as copyright has expired.

TITLE: Alexander H. Rice, Esq., Mayor of Boston

CALL NUMBER: Illus. in AP2.B227 (Case Y) [P&P]

REPRODUCTION NUMBER: LC-USZ62-71040 (b&w film copy neg.) No known restrictions on publication.

SUMMARY: Head and shoulders, facing right; with biogr. text and full masthead illus. of crowded Boston Harbor.

MEDIUM: 1 print : wood engraving.

CREATED/PUBLISHED: 1856.

NOTES:

Illus. in: Ballou's Pictorial, 1856 January 12.

This record contains unverified, old data from caption card.

Caption card tracings: Newspapers...; BI; Massachusetts--Boston--Harbor; Massachusetts--Boston--1856; Shelf.

REPOSITORY: Library of Congress Prints and Photographs Division Washington, D.C. 20540 USA

DIGITAL ID: (b&w film copy neg.) cph 3b18427 http://hdl.loc.gov/loc.pnp/cph.3b18427
