Alex Reynolds

I am a former writer, photographer and editor for the electronic music magazine Grooves, currently completing studies in a bioinformatics graduate program at the University of Pennsylvania. I also produce and release electronic music locally within the Philadelphia, Pennsylvania area for art installations and one-off performances.
