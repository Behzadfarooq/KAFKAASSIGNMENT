Alexander Nevsky (disambiguation)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Alexander Nevsky was a Russian statesman and military hero.

A number of things are named after him:

  • Life of Alexander Nevsky, literary monument
  • Alexander Nevsky Lavra
  • Order of Alexander Nevsky
  • Alexander Nevsky Cathedral is the name of various cathedrals
  • Nevsky Prospekt, the main street in the city of St. Petersburg, Russia
  • Icebreaker Saint Alexander Nevsky
  • Alexander Nevsky, a Russian bomber
  • Alexander Nevsky, a film by Sergei Eisenstein, and its score by Sergei Prokofiev (later reworked into a cantata)
  • Alexander Nevsky a naval frigate, the Alexander Nevsky was lost at sea of the Danish coast in 1868
