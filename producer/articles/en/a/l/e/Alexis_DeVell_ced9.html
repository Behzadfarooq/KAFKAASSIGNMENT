Alexis DeVell (born October 8, 1971 in Loveland, Colorado) is a former porn star and writer known for her petite frame and (enhanced) large breasts. She grew up in Colorado and worked as an exotic dancer and stripper before entering the world of adult film.

DeVell had a long friendship and love affair with fellow actress Shayla LaVeaux, whom Alexis introduced into the adult film industry. She stopped performing in hardcore in 1994, but appeared in non-sexual roles in adult films for the next few years. DeVell currently works as a feature dancer in clubs on the West Coast.

Her website gives her height as 5 ft 4 in, but other sources put her at a more credible 5 ft 0 in.

In addition to dozens of acting credits in videos between 1991 and 1998, DeVell is credited as the writer on two productions, and the production manager on another. She appeared at the Adult Video News Awards in 1994.[1]
