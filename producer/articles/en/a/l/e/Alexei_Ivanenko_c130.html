Alexei Ivanenko (Russian: Алексей Игоревич Иваненко) (1977) is a Russian self-proclaimed postmodernist philosopher & pro-islamist publisher from Saint-Petersburg.[citation needed] He also collaborates with rebel information agency Kavkaz Center.[1]
