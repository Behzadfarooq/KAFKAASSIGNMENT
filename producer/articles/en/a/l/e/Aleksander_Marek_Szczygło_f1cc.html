Aleksander Marek Szczygło
born October 27, 1963 in Jeziorany, Poland
Function: Ministry of National Defence
Party: Prawo i Sprawiedliwość

Aleksander Marek Szczygło (born October 27, 1963 in Jeziorany) is a Polish politician. He was elected to Sejm on September 25, 2005 getting 19006 votes in 35 Olsztyn district, candidating from Prawo i Sprawiedliwość list.

Since February 7, 2007 Minister of National Defence

He was also a member of Sejm 2001-2005.
