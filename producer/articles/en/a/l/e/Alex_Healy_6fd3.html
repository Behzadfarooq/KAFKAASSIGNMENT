The Reverend Alexander "Alex" Healy was a fictional character in the popular BBC soap opera EastEnders. He was played by Richard Driscoll.

[edit] History

Alex arrived in Walford as the local vicar and presided over many services during his tenure- including the near-bigamous marriage of Terry Raymond and the funeral of one of his best friends, Tiffany Mitchell . Alex was briefly involved with Kathy Mitchell while her marriage to Phil had began to crumble. Alex and Kathy later broke up when she left for South Africa. A while later, Alex left the Square for Somalia.
