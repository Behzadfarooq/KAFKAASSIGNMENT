Alexander Walke (born June 6, 1983 in Oranienburg, Brandenburg) is a professional football goalkeeper currently playing for German club SC Freiburg.

Walke moved to Baden-Württemberg in 2005 from Werder Bremen, where he had spent six years. He began his career at Energie Cottbus as a 14-year-old. He competed for Germany at the FIFA World Youth Championship 2003, but tested positive for tetrahydrocannabinol.[1]
