Alexandra Adi (born April 13, 1971 in New York) is an American actress who most recently starred in the horror film, Mortuary. directed by Tobe Hooper. Raised in upstate New York, Adi resides in Los Angeles. She is married to former Sapient chief executive officer Jerry Greenberg.

[edit] Trivia

  • In an interview about the film, Slap Her... She’s French, Adi was quoted as saying "There’s a big lesbian undertone between Nicki Lynn Aycox’s character and myself. I say ‘undertone’ because there was a full-blown kiss at the end, but they cut it out".[1]
  • Adi plays the oboe.
  • She has said on her views on joining the practice of Scientology, "God, no. I once sat in on an acting class that turned out to be some kind of Scientologist haven. They told us to put $1,000 down and buy this set of books. I just walked. I guess it works for some people, though. Look at Tom Cruise".
