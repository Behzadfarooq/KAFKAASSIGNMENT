Aleksandar Atanacković

Aleksandar Atanacković( born on November 29, 1980) is a Serbian footballer (midfielder) playing lately for Unia Janikowo. His former clubs were SV Wacker Burghausen(Germany) and FK Partizan (Serbia). In the winter 2006 he has been relased free from Unia
