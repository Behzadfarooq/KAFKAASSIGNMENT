Alexander Páez (born January 4, 1971 in Bogotá, Colombia) is a dubbing actor (Spanish) who works for distributing companies in Colombia and Latin America.


Voice "Ages"

Child, Teen, Young Adult and Middle Age.


Experience

14 years


Companies in Colombia: Provideo S.A./Centauro Comunicaciones/Grabando Estudios/
City TV/JN Global Productions-Voces&Imagen.

Companies in Latin America: National Geographic/Nickelodeon (TV Channel)/
Cartoon Network/Disney Channel/Animax/Jetix.
