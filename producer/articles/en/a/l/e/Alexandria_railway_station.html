Alexandria railway station is a railway station serving the town of Alexandria, Scotland. The station is managed by First ScotRail and is served by trains on the North Clyde Line.

[edit] Service

There is a half-hourly service from Alexandria to Balloch railway station northbound and Glasgow Queen Street southbound. Services continue from Glasgow to Airdrie Monday to Saturday daytimes and Springburn railway station. Evenings and Sundays to Motherwell.
