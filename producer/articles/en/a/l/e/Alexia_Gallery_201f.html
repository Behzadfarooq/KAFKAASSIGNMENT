Alexia Gallery is a contemporary art gallery based in Decatur, Georgia, in the Atlanta Metropolitan Area. The gallery displays and sells Handcrafted Jewelry, Glass, Pottery and Wood. It is also a full service jewelery store with a gemologist on staff.

Alexia Gallery is a Silver Level dealer of Pandora Jewelry.

The gallery is located at: 335 West Ponce de Leon Ave. Suite B Decatur, GA 30030 Hours are are Tuesday - Saturday 11am - 7pm or by appointment.

[edit] Artists

The gallery has 126 artists from around the world. Current featured artists are:

  • Corinne Herbst
  • Link Wachler
  • Laura Gibson
  • Marise Fransolino
  • Pat Scull
  • Leon Milmeyster
  • Lisa Mote
