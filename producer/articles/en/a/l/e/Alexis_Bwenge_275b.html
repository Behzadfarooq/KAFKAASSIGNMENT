Alexis Bwenge

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Alexis Bwenge (born October 19, 1981 in St. Apollonaire, Quebec) is a fullback for the CFL's British Columbia Lions. He went to the University of Kentucky, wears number 37, weighs 215 lbs and is 6'1" tall. Bwenge was drafted by the Lions (1st round, 8th overall) in the 2005 CFL Draft. In the 2006 CFL season, he had one four-yard touchdown reception.
