[edit] Life

Alexander Besher was born in China in 1951 to Russian parents, raised and educated in Japan. Alexander Besher is a San Francisco-based author, journalist and novelist. He was a contributing editor to Infoworld magazine.

[edit] Bibliography

He is the author of The Pacific Rim Almanac (HarperCollins, '91), as well as the Rim trilogy of futuristic thrillers. He has just completed The Hanging Butoh, the first title in his new Hanging Butoh trilogy of alternative noir fantasy novels. His novels have been translated into over a dozen languages.

[edit] Rim Trilogy

(Including Rim, Mir, and Chi)

Rim (HarperCollins '94; HarperPrism, '95, Nominated for the Philip K. Dick Award) is set in the wake of a mega-earthquake in 2027 Japan, the virtual-reality entertainment empire Satori Corporation attempts to rescue thousands of people whose minds are trapped in virtual worlds, including the ten-year-old son of professor and virtual reality investigator Frank Gobi.

Mir picks up nine years later, as Frank's son Trevor Gobi is tracking Mir, a new breed of virus lethal to both technology and humankind, as it travels from person to person through sentient cybernetic tattoos.

In Chi (Simon & Schuster, '98 and '99), it is 2038, and the world of cyberspace is again being manipulated for immoral purposes. The head of an Asian biotechnology drug cartel is siphoning off vital chi essence from enslaved humans to produce bootleg high-demand "chi products", which cause temporary mental, physical, digital, and vital enhancements of every sort. Frank and Trevor Gobi return to set the world right in this thrilling sci-fi adventure.

[edit] Hanging Butoh Trilogy

(Including The Hanging Butoh, The Tower of Ra, and Point Sublime)

It's a world in which things, already gone awry, get further convoluted. Now the International Planetary Union must deal with the latest threat to global security, the newly remilitarized state of Imperial New Nippon which is determined to release a psi-virus codenamed "Fingermoon,"in a bid to conquer Mediaspace.
