I am Alexander Michaelovich Gerashchenko (Александр Михайлович Геращенко) from Biysk, Russia.

I graduated from the Faculty of Foreign Languages of Biysk Pedagogical State University. Now I work as an assistant-professor of this faculty, being also a post-graduate of Barnaul State Pedagogical University.

Learning foreign languages is the thing I am especially fond of. Other spheres of my interest include History, Philosophy (especially that of Epicurus), Religion (despite my being an atheist), etc.

My main contribution to Wikipedia is adding information about my native country to the versions of this encyclopedia that lack it (mostly to the Latin one).
