Alex Johnstone

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Alex Johnstone (born 31 July 1961, Kincardineshire) is a Scottish Conservative & Unionist politician, and Member of the Scottish Parliament for the North East Scotland Region since 1999. He fought the West Aberdeenshire and Kincardine constituency in the 2005 General Election, finishing second.

He has served as Chief Whip for the Scottish Conservatives and is currently their spokesman for rural affairs.
