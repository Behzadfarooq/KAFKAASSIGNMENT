Alex Abasto

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Alexander "Alex" Abasto is an assistant equipment manager who currently works for the New Jersey Devils during the NHL.

During Alex Abasto's only year as head equipment manager in the NHL in 2003, he won the Stanley Cup, and made great friends with All-Star Scott Niedermayer. During his 24 hours with the Cup, he brought it to his house and had a Thanksgiving dinner with his family, and his good friend, Scott Niedermayer. During his career with the New Jersey Devils He has won 3 Stanley Cup championships in 1995, 2000, 2003.
