Takashi Otsuka (大塚 崇, Ōtsuka Takashi?) (born July 17, 1971 in Tokushima, Tokushima, Japan, better known as Alexander Otsuka (アレクサンダー大塚, Alexander Otsuka?) is a professional wrestler and a mixed martial arts fighter. His nickname of Diet Butcher comes from one of his fight sponsors. He currently wrestles using the name Otoko Sakari.

Otsuka primarily wrestled for the BattlARTS organization. As a freelancer, he has also competed in ZERO-ONE, Michinoku Pro, New Japan Pro Wrestling, and U-STYLE.


Otsuka fought for PRIDE with a record of 4 wins and 12 losses.
