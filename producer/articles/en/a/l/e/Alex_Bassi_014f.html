Alex Bassi (b. Paris, France) is a race car driver who raced Shifter Karts in California from 1991 to 1995. Then he raced the West Coast Skip Barber Formula Dodge Series from 1996 to 2000. He won the P1 class at the 2001 24 Hours of Moroso in a Porsche 944. He has podium finishes in other SCCA and NASA Endurance Sports Car Racing, such as the 12 Hours of Summit Point, 6 Hours of Buttonwillow and 25 Hours of Thunderhill.

Next up is the 24 Hours Nürburgring.
