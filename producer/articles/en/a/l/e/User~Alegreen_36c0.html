Hello, my username is alegreen. I am a musician who has graduated in both piano and electronic music. I've been using computer since 1988 and internet since 1998 especially for classical music purposes and researches.

I joined Wikipedia to work on topics concerning classical music, however I have various other interests such as cinema, photography, drawing, nature, electronics (computer music) and the internet.
