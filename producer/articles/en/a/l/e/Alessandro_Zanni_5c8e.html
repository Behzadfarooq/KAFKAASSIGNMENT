Alessandro Zanni

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Alessandro Zanni (born 31 January 1984 in Udine) is an Italian rugby union footballer. He currently plays for Calvisano in the Super 10 in Italy. His usual position is as a number 8.

Zanni made his debut for the Italian national team in November of 2005 in a Test against Tonga. He also played for Italy during the 2006 2007 Six Nations Championships, and the 2006 Autumn internationals.
