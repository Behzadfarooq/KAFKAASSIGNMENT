Attempting to create a list of articles and contributors from "the undergraduate student authors of this collaborative classroom project" who seem to be either from an art history class dealing with gender and the Renaissance, or from a gender studies class messing around with the Renaissance.

All of those articles are badly formated and wikified, and most are essay style instead of articles, hence a few already are on VfD. The rest needs to be wikified and often heavily edited, and particularly, through linking from and to them, brought into the rest of Wikipedia. Also, many probably need to be moved to more appropriate titles, or titles that have at least a remote chance of ever being linked. Quite a few of them might be merged with existing articles as well. Oh, yes, and there is lots of duplicated content in some, whole paragraphs appearing in more than one article.

Please note that I did not make any more VfDs or inserted cleanup or POV notices or anything - that too needs to be done.

If you find anything I missed, please feel free to add things.
