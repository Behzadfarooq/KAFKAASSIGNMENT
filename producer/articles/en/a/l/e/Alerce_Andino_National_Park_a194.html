Alerce Andino National Park
IUCN Category II (National Park)
Location: Los Lagos Region, Chile
Nearest city: Puerto Montt
Area: 393 km²
Established: 1982
Governing body: Corporación Nacional Forestal

Alerce Andino National Park is located in the Andes, in Los Lagos Region of Chile. This national park covers about 393 square kilometres. The Fitzroya cupressoides (locally known as Alerce) forests are the centerpiece of the park. Besides, it provides important shelter for species such as Pudú, Monito del Monte and Geoffroy's Cat.
