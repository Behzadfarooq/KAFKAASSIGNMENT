Alexander Michael Wolff is an American child actor, musician, and drummer, known for his work with The Naked Brothers Band. He started The Naked Brothers Band with older brother,Nat Wolff.

Alex is the son of actress/director Polly Draper (who directed The Naked Brothers Band). His family is Jewish.[1] He plays the drums in the band. He was 6 1/2 in the movie, in the show he's 8 1/2, and now he is 9 years old. He learned how to play the drums from Ringo Starr
