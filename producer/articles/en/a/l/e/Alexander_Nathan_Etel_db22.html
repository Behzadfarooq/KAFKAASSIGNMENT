Alexander Nathan Etel

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Etel in Millions, 2005

Alexander Nathan Etel (born 19 September 1994 in Manchester) is an English child actor. His film debut and so far only role was the starring role of Damian Cunningham in 2005's Millions, a family film directed by Danny Boyle. He is currently filming a role in the film, The Waterhorse, which will be released in 2007.

Etel currently attends school and enjoys football, swimming, listening to music, PlayStation, playing the trampoline, go-carting, and playing cricket. He hopes to one day play for Manchester United, his favourite team.
