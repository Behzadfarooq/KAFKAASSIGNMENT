Alex Bailey (born September 21, 1983 in Newham, London) is a professional footballer with Chesterfield.

A right-back, Bailey began as an Arsenal trainee, winning the FA Youth Cup in 2001, but was released in 2004.

He has been a regular in Chesterfield's first team but is currently out with a groin strain.
