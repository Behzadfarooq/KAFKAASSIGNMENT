Alexander Anthony Barnett (born Málaga, Spain 11 September 1970) is an English cricketer.

He represented Middlesex, Lancashire and Surrey as an left-arm orthodox bowler and a right-handed batsman. He has also played club cricket for Hampstead.

His great-uncle Charles Barnett opened the batting for Gloucestershire and England.

He works as the Community Program Manager for Data Programmability for Microsoft in Redmond, WA, USA. Before working for Microsoft in the US he managed Microsoft.com/uk for Microsoft's United Kingdom subsidiary.
