'Alex Robinson is a comic book writer and artist born on August 8, 1969. In 2001 he won an Eisner Award for "Talent Deserving of Wider Recognition" for his graphic novel Box Office Poison which was published by Top Shelf Productions. The French translation of this book called "De Mal En Pis" won the "Prix Du Premier Album" at Angoulême International Comics Festival in 2005.

Alex's second graphic novel, Tricked, also published by Top Shelf, was released in August 2005, and was nominated for an Eisner Award and won both the 2006 Harvey Award for Best Original Graphic Novel and the 2006 Ignatz Award for Outstanding Graphic Novel. Alex was also nominated in the Best Writer category for the Harveys.

Alex's favorite games are Dungons & Dragons Miniatures, Heroclix, and Zoo Tycoon. He is often associated with the cartonist Tony Consiglio
