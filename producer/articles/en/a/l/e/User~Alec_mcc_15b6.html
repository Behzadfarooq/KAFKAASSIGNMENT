From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

A wikipedia member, based in Townsville, Queensland, Australia.

I have been editing on Wikipedia since September 2006 and mostly focus on Townsville and Palm Island related pages, and sometimes political articles. I do a little bit of monitoring for vandalism but try to focus more on bringing new content to wikipedia.


This user is a member of WikiProject Townsville.
