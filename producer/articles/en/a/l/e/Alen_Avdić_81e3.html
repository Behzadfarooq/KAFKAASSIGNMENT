Alen Avdić (Persian: آلن آودیچ‎ , born April 3, 1977 in Sarajevo, Bosnia and Herzegovina) is a Bosnian football player who currently plays for Saba Battery FC in Iran's Premier Football League and the Bosnia and Herzegovina national football team. He usually plays the striker position.

[edit] Club career

Currently, Avdić plays for Saba Battery FC in Iran's Premier Football League. Previously, he has been active for FK Sarajevo in the Bosnian Premier League, Cercle Brugge and FC Denderleeuw in Belgian second division, Avispa Fukuoka of J-League and Suwon Samsung Bluewings of K-league.

[edit] International career

Avdić has 15 caps for the Bosnia and Herzegovina national football team.
