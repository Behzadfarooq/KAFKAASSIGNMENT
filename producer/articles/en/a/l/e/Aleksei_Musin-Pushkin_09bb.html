Aleksei Musin-Pushkin

Aleksei Ivanovich Musin-Pushkin (1744 â€” 1817), (also spelled Aleksei Ivanovich Mussin-Pushkin) count since 1797, statesman, historian and art colector. Musin-Pushkin is credited with discovering in Yaroslavl the manuscript The Tale of Igor's Campaign.
