Alex Kelly, played by Olivia Wilde, is a fictional character from the second season of the FOX television series The O.C.. She was bisexual and dated both Seth Cohen and Marissa Cooper.

Spoiler warning: Plot and/or ending details follow.

Alex appears in the series at the Bait Shop, when Seth needs tickets to The Walkmen show for Summer and Zach, in an attempt at friendship. Alex was kicked out of three high schools, and works at the Bait Shop as a bartender. Seth ends up working under Alex and the two briefly have a relationship. Seth later learns of her bisexuality and ends the relationship. She soon afterword dates Marissa. Marissa moves out shortly after delivering the news that Alex is her girlfriend, and moves in with her. Marissa falls back in love with Ryan Atwood, though, so she and Alex break up, leaving Alex devastated.

After the two girls break up, Marissa moves back to live with her mother and Alex decides to go back home to live with her parents and go back to school.
