Alexander Peya (born June 27, 1980), is an Austrian male tennis player. He reached a career high singles ranking of 96 in the world on March 5, 2007. His career high doubles ranking was 48 on January 15, 2007. He was born in Vienna, Austria, and currently resides there.

  • 2006: Munich (w/ Björn Phau) (lost to Andrei Pavel / Alexander Waske)
