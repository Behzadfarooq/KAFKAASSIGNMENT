Ale_Jrb Talk :: Read Me!

Welcome to my talk page! Please read the following guidelines and tips before posting a message.

  • SIGN YOUR EDITS! This is the most important point. If you don't sign your comment, I won't know who said what. To sign your comment, use four tildes (~~~~).
  • Create a new section for a comment that is unrelated to all other comments. If it is related, however, please use the section it is related to - this saves space and makes reading easier.
  • Use the "Write a Message" link to start a new discussion, or to make a comment to me. Don't use this link if you are replying to someone else's comment.
  • Be nice! Don't insult anyone, including me of course! This can hurt people's feelings and make them upset.
  • If you have pop-ups enabled and are logged in, the "Report Incorrect Vandalism" link will fill out the necessary headings. If you do not have pop-ups, or are not logged in, please put "Incorrect Vandalism Reversion" as your subject.

Many thanks! Ale_Jrb
