Alexander Marse was born on March 26, 1989 in Reston, VA. His parents are Michael Marse and Allison Marse. Born at 8 lbs and 0 oz, Alex was one month pre-mature. When he was born he was a jondis baby, which means that his skin turned yellow until it was perfectly pigmented. Alex later moved to Washington DC where he lived with his mother and father until they both got diveorced. At the time of the divorce, Alex was 2 years old. Soon after, his father and mother moved to Atlanta, GA where Alex was "shared" between the parents. Michael, the father, gained full custody of Alex Marse with Allison, the mom, aloud visitation rights.

Alex has lived in about 10 states all in the South Atlantic United States. Washinton DC, Virginia, North Carolina, South Carolina, Georgia, Florida, and Alabama. You say, "Military?," No. They just moved alot so Alex had no trouble ever "fitting in" or finding new friends. Right now, he resides in Newport News, VA attending Woodside High School.

Mom: For about 10 years, Alex never heard from or had any reaction or interaction with his mother. From about the age of 3-13, they didnt talk whatsoever. What happened? No one knows. Suddenly, Alex is living in South Carolina and recieves a card and a $50 gift card to Target from her; this sparks the beginning of a new life. Since then, Alex visits his mom in Florida on vacation, holidays, and other times.

Dad:Alex has always lived with his father. From day one, he has always been the person that was there for him. Moving to almost 20 different cities with him was a bear sometimes, but it happens. Alex and his father have been through thick and thin, but in the end they are always happy.
