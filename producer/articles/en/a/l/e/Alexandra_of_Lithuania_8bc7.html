Alexandra of Lithuania

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Alexandra (after 1350 – 19 June 1434) was the daughter of Algirdas and Uliana of Tver, wife (from 1387) of Siemowit IV, and Princess of Czersk, Rawa, Sochaczew, Płock, Gostynin, Płońsk, and Kujawy.
