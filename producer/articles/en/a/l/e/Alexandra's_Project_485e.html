Alexandra's Project is a 2003 Drama/Thriller Australian movie. It depicts a nuclear family, where the protaginist, Steve, comes to find the life that he has been living is a lie. After years of dominance as the patriachial head of his family, he's wife Alexandra confronts him in the most powerful way.
