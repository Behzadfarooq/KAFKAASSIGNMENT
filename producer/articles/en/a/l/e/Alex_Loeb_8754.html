Alex Loeb A Mississippi painter. Alex is pictured here with wife, Jean Loeb. He lives in Meridian, Mississippi. Under Alex Loeb, the Mississippi Art Colony was incorporated as a non-profit entity, making it eligible for funding from the Mississippi Arts Commission. Grants from the Commission gave much-needed help over several years. Attendance began to dwindle, with only 21 painters signing on in the Fall of 1971, and these monies were to Colony's survival.
