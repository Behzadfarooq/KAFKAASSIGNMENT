Aleksei Bugayev (Russian: Алексей Иванович Бугаев) (born 25 August 1981 in Moscow) is a Russian football defender. He plays for the Russian Premier League team Tom' Tomsk.

Bugayev has previously played for Torpedo Moscow and Lokomotiv Moscow. In 2004 he was included in the Russia national team and played at Euro 2004.

[edit] Achievements

  • 2005 - Russian Super Cup, winner
  • 2005 - Russian Premier League, 3rd position
