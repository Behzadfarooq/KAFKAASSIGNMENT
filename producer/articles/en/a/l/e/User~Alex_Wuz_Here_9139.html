[edit] Alex

Alex is a student at Longfellow Middle School in Wisconsin. He is the most awesome kid in the world. His hobbies are reading, writing, plying basketball, watching movies, and surfing the web. He uses Wikipedia for school research and for information on subjects he is in the dark about. Recently he has used Wikipedia to search for information on Saw. He has recently seen Saw and loved it.

[edit] Saw

Alex's obsession with the Saw film has encouraged him to start writing 'games' of his own. He is currently writing his own version of a Saw film and if possible would like to send it to the writers of the Saw films for critiquing. He has seen all three Saw films, read the Saw script, seen the original Saw short, and has two songs composed by Charlie Clouser from the film, including the famous Hello Zepp. He would also like to note that Zep from Saw I is spelled with one p unlike the score.

[edit] RHCP

The Red Hot Chili Peppers (RHCP) are Alex's next obsession. He currently has 23 of their songs on Itunes and would like to see them in concert one day. He loves to listen to the Red Hot Chili Peppers whenever he gets the chance.

[edit] Other Music

Alex also has a variety of other music on his ipod including rock, alternative, rap, some pop, and stand up comedy. Some of the stand up he has is by Kyle Cease and Dane Cook. He has Red Hot Chili Peppers, and some Killers, AcDc, The Beatles, Eminem, Akon, and Gym Class Heroes. Those are probably the biggest names on my ipod. Also don't forget "Weird Al" Yankovic, the comedy genius is on there too.

[edit] School

School life is no challenge for Alex. He has many friends, a solid A and B+ average, and is climbing the ranks to high school. He is in 6th Grade and in a few years he will be in his sisters school at East. East High School is a lot to look forward to.

Wikipedia is Alex's main resource for homework and personal interest. He personally thinks that it is better than regular search engines like google that give u things that u don't want.
