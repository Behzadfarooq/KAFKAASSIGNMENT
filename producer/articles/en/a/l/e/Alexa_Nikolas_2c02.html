Alexa Nikolas (born April 4, 1992 in Chicago, Illinois) is an American actress of Greek descent best known for her good looks and for her role as Nicole Bristow in the Nickelodeon series Zoey 101 which she co-starred in along with Jamie Lynn Spears, Kristin Herrera (season 1), and Victoria Justice (season 2) during the show's first two seasons. She also appeared in Vanessa Anne Hudgens' music video "Come Back to Me" and guest starred in The Suite Life of Zack and Cody, among various other television series.
