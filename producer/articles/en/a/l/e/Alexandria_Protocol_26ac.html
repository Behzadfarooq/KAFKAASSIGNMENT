Alexandria Protocol

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

In 22 March 1945, in Alexandria, seven Arab Leaders agreed of the foundation of a joint Arab Organization, called the League of Arab States.

It included 7 countries in the Middle East.

[edit] Founding Members

  • Egypt with King Farouk
  • Iraq with King Faisal II
  • Lebanon with the president Bechara El Khoury
  • Saudi Arabia with King Ibn Saud
  • Syria with President Shukri al-Kuwatli
  • Jordan TransJordan then, with King Abdullah I
  • Yemen withKing Yahya Muhammad Hamid ed-Din
