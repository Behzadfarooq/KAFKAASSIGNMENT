Alexandru Suţu 1758 - January 18/19, 1821, Bucharest) was a Prince of Moldavia (July 10, 1801 - October 1, 1802 and Prince of Wallachia (July 2, 1802 - August 30, 1802; August 24, 1806 - October 15, 1806; December 1806; November 17, 1818 - January 19, 1821. Born in Istanbul, he had earlier been a Dragoman of the Ottoman Empire.
