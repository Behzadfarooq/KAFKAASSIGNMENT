Alexander Heidel

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Alexander Heidel (1907-1955) was a Member of the Research Staff of the Oriental Institute, University of Chicago.

[edit] Major publications:

Alexander Heidel, The Babylonian Genesis of our time, The University of Chicago Press, Chicago 1942, 1951; reprint 1993.

Alexander Heidel, The Gilgamesh Epic and Old Testament Parallels, The University of Chicago Press, Chicago, 1949; paperback edition, 1963.
