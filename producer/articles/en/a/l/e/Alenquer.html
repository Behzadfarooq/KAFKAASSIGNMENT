Alenquer (pron. IPA: [ɐlẽ'kɛɾ]) is a municipality in Portugal with a total area of 304.2 km² and a total population of 42,932 inhabitants.

The municipality is composed of 16 parishes, and is located in the District of Lisbon.

The present Mayor is Álvaro Joaquim Gomes Pedro, elected by the Socialist Party.
