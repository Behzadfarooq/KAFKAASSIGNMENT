Alex Pourteau is a professional wrestler, best known for his appearances in the World Wrestling Entertainment as Alex "The Pug" Pourteau.

[edit] Career

Alex Pourteau also wrestled in the WCCW, WWC, WCW, NOW (Japan). Alex was trained in 1987 by Jim Starr owner of Starr's Gym in Bossier City, Louisiana. His first break was in WCCW Dallas under the guidance of Skandar Akbar. Porteau was trained by several Texas wrestling legends, including Chris Adams, Gary Hart and Akbar himself.

[edit] Profile

  • Hometown: Bossier City, Louisiana
  • Height & Weight: 5'11 - 226lbs
  • Debut: December 17, 1987
  • Previous Gimmicks: Custom Made Crippler, "The Dirty White Boy's" w/ Tony Anthony "Beach Bully", Heartbreaker Apollo, "The Pug"
  • Finishing Move: Victory Roll, Abdominal Stretch Inside Cradle, Power Bomb

[edit] Active years

  • WCCW Dallas TX. as Alex Pourtaeu 1987-90
  • WCW Atlanta, GA. as Alex Pourteau 1989-90
  • USWA Nashville, TN. with the "Dirty White Boy's" 1990-91
  • WWC San Juan, P.R. as Alex Pourteau 1991-92
  • GWF Dallas TX. as the "Beach Bully" 1991-94
  • NOW Tokyo, JP. as Alex Pourteau 1993-94
  • WWE Stanford, CT. as "The Pug" 1996-97

[edit] Achievements / championships

  • 4-time GWF Light Heavyweight Champion
  • 1-time SECW Tag Team Champion (with Frankie Lancaster)
  • 1-time TAPC Heavyweight Champion
  • 1-time WWC Junior Heavyweight Champion
  • 2-time WWC Television Champion
