Alex Campana (born Alessandro Campana 11 October 1988) is an English footballer who currently plays as a right midfielder for Watford F.C..

His early years were spent in Harrow, until he moved to Oxhey, Watford. He attended St. Joan of Arc secondary school in Rickmansworth and left in 2005 after achieving outstanding GCSE grades.

He was scouted by Watford F.C. from an early age and has been playing in the academy since. He has recently (as of 2006) been a regular in the Watford F.C. reserve team.
