Aleksandr Goremykin

Aleksandr Goremykin (born 3 March 1971) is a Russian sprinter who specialized in the 200 metres. He is one of the current national record holders in 4 x 100 m relay with 38,46 seconds, achieved at the 1990 European Championships in Split.

[edit] Achievements

Year Tournament Venue Result Event Record
1990 European Championships Split, Yugoslavia 4th 4x100m relay 38.46 s NR
World Junior Championships Plovdiv, Bulgaria 1st 200 m 20.47 s CR
1992 European Indoor Championships Genoa, Italy 3rd 200 m
