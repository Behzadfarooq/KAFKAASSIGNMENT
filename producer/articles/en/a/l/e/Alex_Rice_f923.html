Alex Rice (sometimes credited as Alex Kawisenhawe Rice[1]), born on the Kahnawake Mohawk reserve in Quebec, Canada, is a Native American actress who spent her childhood in Brooklyn, where she began acting. She is perhaps best known for her recurring role as Janet Pete in the films Skinwalkers[2] (2002), Coyote Waits (2003), and A Thief of Time (2004). She has appeared in other films including Lewis and Clark: Great Journey West[3], On The Corner[4], A Thousand Roads[5], the IMAX release of Sacajawea, and The New World[6]. She has also appeared in various television roles on such series as Spin City, CSI, Strong Medicine, and The Sopranos.
