Aleksei Yuryevich German (in Russian: Алексей Юрьевич Герман, born on June 20, 1938, in Leningrad, Soviet Union) is a Russian film director.

[edit] Filmography

[edit] Film director

  • 1967 - Sedmoy sputnik (The Seventh Companion)
  • 1971 - Proverka na dorogakh (Checkpoint)
  • 1976 - Dvadtsat dney bez voyny (Twenty Days Without War)
  • 1984 - Moy drug Ivan Lapshin (My Friend Ivan Lapshin)
  • 1998 - Khrustalyov, mashinu! (Khrustalyov, My Car!)
  • 2007 - Hard to Be a God, currently in production
