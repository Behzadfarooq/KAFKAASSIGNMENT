Alejandro Cañizares (born 9 January 1983) is a Spanish golfer.

Cañizares won the 2003 NCAA men's golf championship while playing at Arizona State University. In August 2006, he won his third event as a European Tour golfer, the Imperial Collection Russian Open. He is the son of five time European Tour winner and four time Ryder Cup player José Maria Cañizares.
