Alexis Cruz

The actor Alexis Cruz is best known for his performances as Rafael in Touched by an Angel and as Skaara in Stargate and Stargate SG-1. He was born September 29, 1974 in New York and currently resides in Los Angeles. He went to Boston University majoring in Independent Technical Theatre. Cruz appeared as assistant D.A. Martin Allende in the legal drama Shark, with costars James Woods and Jeri Ryan. His character was killed off on the show of January 4, 2007.

[edit] Trivia

Along with Erick Avari, he is one of only two actors to appear in both the original Stargate movie and the spin-off series Stargate SG-1.

He is an ordained minister.

He is a certified Emergency Medical Technician.

His first work was a Spanish-language commercial for boxed macaroni and cheese.

Alexis is a longtime fan of the World of Darkness roleplaying setting by White Wolf Publishing and regularly gets together with his friends to play.
