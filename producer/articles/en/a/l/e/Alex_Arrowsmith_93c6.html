Alex Arrowsmith (born July 10, 1982) is a rock/pop musician from Portland, Oregon, who makes eclectic and idiosyncratic pop/rock music, often with a satirical or humorous edge. He has self-released 9 albums on his own independent label, Ectoplasmic Records. All of his albums are self-produced, and many of them feature his trademark clean and layered production. Arrowsmith's music has been compared to They Might Be Giants, Ben Folds, Ween, XTC, The Beatles and The Cure.
