Aleksandr Parkhomenko

Aleksandr Parkhomenko (born January 3, 1986) is a Belarusian decathlete.

He finished 6th in the decathlon at the 2006 European Athletics Championships in Gothenburg.

He also competed in the 2004 Olympics.
