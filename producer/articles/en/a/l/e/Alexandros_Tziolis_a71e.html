Alexandros Tziolis (Greek: Αλέξανδρος Τζιώλης) (born February 13, 1985 in Katerini) is a Greek footballer.

Alexandros begun his playing career as a teenager in a local team called Apollon Litochoriou. He played there for about 7 years and in 2002 Panionios scouter found his talent and brought him to Athens. He was an integral part of Panionios team for about 4 season, playing 61 matches and scored 3 goals. As a result, Panathinaikos FC offered Panionios 650.000€ and bought him. He is considered a very good youngster and some people believe he can be Angelos Basinas' successor in Greece national team.
