Alexander Lund Hansen (born October 6, 1982) is a 197 cm tall Norwegian football player, playing as a goalkeeper at the Norwegian club Rosenborg.

He came to Rosenborg from Nardo F.K. (Trondheim) in 2001. In parts of 2004 and 2005 he was on loan to Fredrikstad F.K., where he played 19 matches.

He has not played any league matches for Rosenborg yet (1. January, 2007), but he played in six matches in the Royal League in November/December 2006, where Rosenborg only got one point.
