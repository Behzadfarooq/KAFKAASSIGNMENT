Alexander Hamilton High School Academy of Music is a magnet school on the Westside of Los Angeles, California. The Music Academy gained national attention in June 2002 when the Disney Channel premiered the reality TV show Totally in Tune which chronicled members of the Academy's Symphony Orchestra as they struggled to cope with the demands of being a student, a musician, and a teenager in and out of the concert hall.

What makes the Academy of Music unique from other high schools is that it offers a large array of classes and programs in music and other performing arts. Hamilton Academy of Music students take traditional high school classes, but are also enrolled in classes in the Academy's elective departments.
