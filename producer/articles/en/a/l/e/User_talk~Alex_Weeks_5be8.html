[edit] Mbira players and keyboardist-stubs

In my opinion there isn't a more appropriate stub available at this time for mbira players. It does use a keyboard, even if it isn't the 88 keys of a piano. If we had a percussionist-stub (and we may have one soon, as it has been proposed) there would be a strong argument to use that stub instead, but it doesn't exist yet. Since you seem to be the primary person working on articles about mbira players right now, I'll defer to you as to which stub would be preferable if you have a preference. Caerwine 04:08, 14 November 2005 (UTC)

[edit] Mbira & religious belief
