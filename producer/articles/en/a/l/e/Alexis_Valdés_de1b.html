Alexis Valdés

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Alexis Valdés is a Cuban actor, comedian, monologist, film producer and scriptwriter born in Havana.

After receiving an engineering degree and working in the stages in Cuba for 10 years, he went to Spain in the 1990s, where he became very popular due to his performances in programs like El club de la comedia. In 2005, he was actor, director and producer of his first film, Un rey en La Habana. His most recent venture has taken him to Miami where he broadcasts his comedic show Seguro Que Yes on America teve.
