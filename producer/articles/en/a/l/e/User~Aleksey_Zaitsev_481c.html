[edit] Making an International Long Distance Call from Your Cell Phone

International phone calls placed using a cellular telephone can be quite expensive. Callers who wish to avail themselves of the best savings tend to use a prepaid calling service. When you purchase a prepaid calling plan online from a company such as LDPOST.COM, you are prepaying for your telephone time. Why this kind of service is becoming more and more popular is because of the ease of use, great connection quality and the ability to keep track of the calls made.

As with most prepaid calling services, including prepaid calling cards, a caller needs to sign up and pay for the calling service on the Internet. One of the most popular prepaid services is Peanuts-LD Service. The new subscriber receives an individual PIN number and is ready to start making the lowest-cost telephone calls anywhere in the world. It is advisable to enter all the telephone numbers from which the calls will be made in the system speed dial menu for quick access. In this way, whenever a call has to be made while driving, there are only a few keys to press.

The prepaid service subscriber who uses a mobile phone benefits from additional savings when dialing a special access number, since most cellular telephone operators now offer free nationwide long distance. Furthermore, the one-touch feature can be used to program and dial the access number from the cellular telephone. Once the call is received by the prepaid service network, the caller's telephone number is recognized and the balance is announced. There is no need to key in the authorization code. The telephone call is then connected after two more presses of the buttons corresponding to the presaved destination.

Long Distance Post is one of the premier prepaid calling service companies based in the United States. Founded in 1994 and specializing in prepaid services, Long Distance Post is committed to providing its customers with the best low-cost, high-quality residential and commercial telecommunication services. Long Distance Post runs a fully equipped telecommunication webstore where prepaid calling service subscribers can refill their accounts online or choose from a variety of other convenient products such as prepaid calling cards, prepaid mobile phones, domestic long distance, toll-free 800 numbers, residential and commercial VoIP systems.
