Alex (アレックス, Arekkusu?) is the main character of the Street Fighter III series.

[edit] Appearance

Alex is loosely based on professional wrestler Hulk Hogan. His pre-fight intro is similar to Hogan's trademark entrance. He wears green pants and combat boots and green fighting gloves. He wears a red headband like that of Ryu.

[edit] Story

As a Street Fighter III character, Alex has no official biography. All that is conclusively known about him is that he is an American from New York (although given his accent, many assume Alex is from Brooklyn).

Alex entered the third World Warrior tournament because its sponsor, Gill, had seriously injured his best friend and father figure Tom, even though Tom had told him Gill had won fairly. Tom allowed him to go, letting him make his own decision. Alex won every match and then faced Gill. Although he defeated Gill, Alex did not have a chance to kill him, and he went home, to find Tom fully recovered.

Thoughts of revenge no longer clouding his mind, Alex encountered Ryu, who offered a match with him. When Ryu won, he encouraged Alex to hone his skills and seek out worthy opponents. Alex is Gill's final boss in 3rd Strike. In his ending, Gill turns Alex's skin with the same features as Gill, with half of his body red and half blue.
