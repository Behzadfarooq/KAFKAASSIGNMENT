Since we are on the topic of charts I figure I'd post a Philly Circa 1986 one I posted on the VIP section a few weeks ago. This is in 1986 before it all fell apart. I updated it a little bit.

Administration: 1. Boss: Nicodemo "Little Nicky" Scarfo 2. UnderBoss: Philip "Crazy Phil" Leonetti (Nephew of Nicky Scarfo) 3. Consiglieir: Nicholas "Nicky Buck" Piccolo (Inactive due to age and declining health)(Uncle of Nicky Scarfo) -Acting Consiglieri: Anthony "Tony Buck" Piccolo (Cousin of both Nicky Scarfo and Philip Leonetti)

Capos: 1. Francis "Faffy" Iannarella Jr. (Son of deacesed Soldier Francis Iannarella Sr.) 2. Joseph "Chickie" Ciangalini Sr. (IP) (His Crew reported to Faffy Iannarella) 3. Santo "Big Santo" Idone 4. Alfred "Freddie" Iezzi 5. Pasquale "Patty Specs" Martorano 6. Leonetti Crew (Even though he was the UnderBoss, Leonetti still ran his old Crew)


Soldiers:

Ciangalini Crew/Iannarella: (Philly) 1. Thomas "Tommy Del" DelGiorno 2. Charles "Charlie White" Iannece 3. Nicholas "Nicky the Crow" Caramandi 4. Frank Narducci Jr. (Son of decaesed Capo Frank Narducci Sr.) 5. Ralph "Junior" Stiano 6. Felix "Little Felix" Bocchino 7. Eugene "Gino" Milano 8. Nicholas "Nicky Whip" Milano (Brother of Gino Milano) 9. Anthony "Martino/Bonde Babe" Pungitore Sr. 10. Joseph "Joey Pung" Pungitore (Son of Anthony Pungitore Sr.) 11. Anothny "Tony" Pungitore Jr. (Son of Anthony Pungitore Sr.) 12. John "Coo Coo" Grande 13. Salvatore "Wayne" Grande (Son of John Grande) 14. Alphonse "Funzie" Marconi 15. Guerino "Mark" Marconi (Brother of Funzie Marconi) 16. Joseph “Joe Buck” Piccolo (Brother of Nicky Buck) 17. Michael “Mike Buck” Piccolo (Brother of Nicky Buck) 18. Joseph Lanciano

Idone Crew: (Chester, PA/South Florida) 1. Joseph "Joe" Scalleat (Brother of Buffalino Soldier Albert Scalleat) 2. Nino Sciglitano 3. Santo Romeo 4. Dominic Rugnetta (Son of former Consiglieri Guiseppe "Joe the Boss" Rugnetta ) 5. Jack Parisi (I belive a cousin of Nicky Scarfo)

Leonetti Crew: (Reported directly to UnderBoss Philip “Carzy Phil” Leonetti)(Philly, Trenton, NJ and Atlantic City) 1. Salvatore "Shotsie" Sparacio 2. Samuel Scafidi (Son of former Capo Joseph Scafidi and brother of deceased Soldier Rocco Scafidi) 3. Salvatore "Tori" Scafidi (Nephew of Samuel Scafidi) 4. Philip Narducci (Son of decaesed Capo Frank Narducci Sr./ Brother of Frank Jr.) 5. Joseph "Joe" Grande (Son of John Grande. Brother of Salvatore "Wayne") 6. Nicholas "Nick the Blade" Virgilio 7. Albert "Reds" Potani

Iezzi Crew: (Philly and South Jersey) 1. John Johnny” Cappello (Former Capo)(Brother in law of Shelved Soldier Anthony Casella and deceased former UnderBoss Peter Caella) 2. Lawrence "Yogi" Merlino (Former Capo)(Shelved)(Brother of demoted UnderBoss Salvatore "Chucky" Merlino) 3. Joseph Ligambi 4. Gennaro "Joseph Costello" Boccia 5. Frank Nicoletti

Martorano Crew: (North Jersey and New York City) 1. Ralph "Blackie" Napoli (Former Capo) 2. Joseph "The Nodder" Sodano 3. Mickey "The Walker" Ricciardi 4. Anthony "Slicker/Duke" Anttanasio 5. Joseph "Joe Scoops" Licata 6. Peter "Pete the Crumb" Caprio 7. Anthony "Tony" Capozzi (Cousin of Pete The Crumb) 8. Gerardo "Jerry" Fusella 9. Guiseppe "Joe Happy" Bollina 10. Dominick “Tommy Adams” DiNorscio (Former Acting Capo)

Other Made Members: 1. Salvatore "Chucky" Merlino (Former Under Boss/Demoted. In Prison) 2. Harry “The Hunch Back” Riccobene (In Prison) 3. Raymond “Logn John” Martirano (In Prison) 4. John Stanfa (In Prison) 5. Anthony Casella (Shelved/May have been Dead)
