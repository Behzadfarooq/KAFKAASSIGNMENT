Aleksandrs Isakovs (born September 16, 1973 in Daugavpils) is a football defender from Latvia. As of August 2006 he has played 58 international matches for the Latvia national team. He debuted in 1997, and played at the Euro 2004. He started his career in Dinaburg FK, and played for Lokomotiv Nizhniy Novgorod before joining Latvian champion team Skonto FC.
