Alex Easton MLA is a Unionist politician in Northern Ireland.

He was educated at Bangor Technical College and since 1989 he has worked in the Health Service in the accident and emergency department in Newtownards Hospital. He is currently employed at the Ulster Hospital.

He is a Democratic Unionist Party politician first elected to North Down Borough Council in 2001.

Ennis was elected to the Northern Ireland Assembly in 2003 for the constituency of North Down and returned again in 2007.
