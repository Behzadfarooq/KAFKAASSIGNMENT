Aleksander Baath (born 1972) is a norwegian composer of swedish decent. He got his master degree from Stockholm University School of Journalism in 1996, but has no formal musical education. Baath's earliest works are often categorised within the genre of postminimalism. Many of his later works frequently contain elements of just intonation, and some critics have adopted the term harmonism to discribe his more recent works.
