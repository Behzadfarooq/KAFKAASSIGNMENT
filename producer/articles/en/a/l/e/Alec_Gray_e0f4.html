Alec J. M. Gray was a British socialist.

A railway clerk, Gray is believed to have been a member of the Independent Labour Party[1] before helping found the Socialist Party of Great Britain in June 1904. Gray was a writer of some note for the Socialist Standard until 1908, served on the Executive Committee (1904–1905, 1907–1911) and was the Party’s second Treasurer (1905–1907).
