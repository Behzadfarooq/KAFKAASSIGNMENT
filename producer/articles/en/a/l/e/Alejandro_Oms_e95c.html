Alejandro Oms

Alejandro Oms (March 13, 1895 - November 5, 1946) was a Cuban center fielder in Negro league baseball and Latin American baseball, most notably with the Cuban Stars. Born in Santa Clara, Las Villas, he died at age 51 in Havana.
