Alexander Stewart, 2nd Earl of Buchan

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Alexander Stewart, Earl of Buchan was the son of James Stewart, 1st Earl of Buchan and Margaret Ogilvie. He became earl in either late 1499 or early 1500 when his father died. He was earl for only 5 years, dying in 1505.
