[edit] Internet slang

Well, I looked at the page and watched it for several days. And I concluded that every entry on there had been added just because someone had used it themselves, noticed it once in a blog, heard of it on AOL/MSN/IRC etc and that not a single one of the terms had a reliable sourcing attached to it. Not a single one! Since I reckoned that a good portion of the article was simply made up, and that WP:V says that the burden of proof is on the people adding the info to the page, I decided to just reset the page. I addded that {verify} tag to the page in a (probably futile) attempt to have people give a citation for each word they add to it. A less-terrible example of how this might turn out is List of sexual slang (don't read it easily offended). Whilst pretty silly, and WP:NOT a dictionary, at least entry has a reference to go with it. -Splash - tk 00:40, 11 June 2006 (UTC)
