Alexandra Rosenfeld

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Alexandra Rosenfeld (born November 23, 1986, Béziers, Hérault) was elected Miss France in 2006. Representing the region of Languedoc, she succeeded Cindy Fabre as the 52nd Miss France on December 3, 2005.

Rosenfeld has blonde hair and brown eyes [1]. She is 1.73 meters tall.

She lives in Saint-Thibéry and studies tourism in Pézenas.

On February 2006, Rosenfeld, who is Jewish,[2] took part in a French demonstration against anti-Semitism and racism following the brutal murder of a young Jewish phone salesman Ilan Halimi in Paris.[3]

She was elected Miss Europe 2006 on October 27 in Kiev, Ukraine. [4]
