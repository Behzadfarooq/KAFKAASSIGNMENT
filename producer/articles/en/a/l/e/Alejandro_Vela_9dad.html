Alejandro Vela

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Manuel Alejandro Vela Garrido (born March 28, 1984 in Cancún, Quintana Roo) is a Mexican football striker who currently plays for Jaguares de Chiapas.

Brother of Carlos Vela, who is seen to be a much better footballer. Alejandro is seen to be what is known in the Midlands, UK as a 'WG1' to the rest of the Vela family, when it comes to footballing ability.
