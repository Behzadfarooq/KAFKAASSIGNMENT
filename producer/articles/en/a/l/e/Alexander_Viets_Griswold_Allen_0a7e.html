Alexander Viets Griswold Allen

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Alexander Viets Griswold Allen, D.D. (1841â€”1908) was a Episcopal theologian, born at Otis, Massachusetts, United States of America. He graduated at Kenyon College in 1862 and at Andover Theological Seminary in 1865.

His publications include:

  • Continuity of Christian Thought (Boston, 1884; eleventh edition, 1895)
  • Life of Jonathan Edwards (1889)
  • Religious Progress (1894)
  • Christian Institutions (New York, 1897)
  • Life and Letters of Phillips Brooks (two volumes, 1901)
