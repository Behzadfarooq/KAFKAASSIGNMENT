Alessio di Mauro

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Alessio di Mauro (born August 9, 1977 in Syracuse) is an Italian tennis player. In Buenos Aires, he reached his first final of his career, where Argentine Juan Monaco defeated di Mauro to win his first ATP singles title 6-1, 6-2.
