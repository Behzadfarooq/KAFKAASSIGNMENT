[edit] My Goals

Born and raised in Saint John, I grew up listening to my family regaling me with tales of Saint John's history.

I want to provide a visual history of Saint John and New Brunswick, especially the smaller communities which are slowly disappearing.

[edit] My Camera

Digital: Canon Powershot G1, in continual use since 2001. I have used this camera in England, France, and all across North America. Unfortunately, it is on it's last legs and being held together by duct tape after being dropped multiple times, the most seriously from the top of a car moving at 90 km/h.

Film: A Nikon FE that I have inherited from my grandfather.
