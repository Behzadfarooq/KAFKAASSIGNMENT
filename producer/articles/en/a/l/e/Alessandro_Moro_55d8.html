Alessandro Moro (born 2 October 1984) is an Italian football midfielder.

[edit] Teams and clubs

  • 2003: U.S. Foggia (Serie C1)
  • 2003-2004: Isernia (Serie C2) on loan from Foggia
  • 2004-2005: U.S. Foggia (Serie C1)
  • 2005-2006: Ascoli Calcio 1898 (Serie A) on loan from Foggia
  • 2006-present: Treviso (Serie B) co-ownership from Udinese
