Alex Kew

Born October 21, 1986
Notable roles Josh Barker in My Parents Are Aliens

Alex Kew (born October 21, 1986) is a British actor who is most well known for playing Josh Barker in children's sitcom My Parents are Aliens on CITV. He has also played other small parts, most notably Declan in the final series of 2point4 children. He has also appeared in an episode of ITV1's The Bill and had a small role in film About a Boy.
