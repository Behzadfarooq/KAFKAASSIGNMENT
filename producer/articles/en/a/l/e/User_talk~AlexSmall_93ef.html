[edit] Sources

What sources do you use to create articles like Liu Yan (Three Kingdoms) or Pan Zheng? Sarazyn • TALK • DE 13:20, 15 August 2006 (UTC)

I get it all from Japanese Wikipedia, and translate it. Unfortunately, the folks who write the articles in Japanese never really cite their sources unless a direct quote is made. I assume, however, that the primary source they use is Records of Three Kingdoms. I don't have a copy of that text in any language, so I'm forced to rely on the Japanese articles and HOPE that they're accurate.--Alex Small 23:28, 18 August 2006 (UTC)
