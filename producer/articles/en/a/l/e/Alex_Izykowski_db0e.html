Alex Izykowski

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Olympic medal record
Short track speed skating
Bronze 2006 Turin 5000m Relay

Alex Izykowski (born January 26, 1984 in Bay City, Michigan) is an American short track speed skater. He competed in the 2006 Winter Olympics of Turin in the 1500m, and was also part of the bronze medal winning 5000m relay.
