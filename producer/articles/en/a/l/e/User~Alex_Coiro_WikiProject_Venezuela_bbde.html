The present is a proposition for a WikiProject, or Pre-Project, focusing in Venezuela and related articles. This Project's orjectives are to maintain and expand articles with complete information, better organization, and achieve a neutral point o view in cases required so. List yourselves in Wikipedia:Wikiproject/List of proposed projects on in this page. Everyone is invited to join, including spanish translators.

[edit] Goals

  • To improve the articles, lists, and tables related to Venezuela, in order to achieve a better quality standard of the articles.
  • To create articles necessary and/or related to a general subject (i.e., History, Geography, Culture, etc.)
  • To maintain a neutral point of view in parcialized articles.
  • To encourage participation and general participation of users. Including those that are not members of this project.
