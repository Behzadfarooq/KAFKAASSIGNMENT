Alexander Tettey

Alexander Banor Tettey (born April 4, 1986 in Accra, Ghana) is a football midfielder who currently plays for Rosenborg in Norway. He is now a naturalized Norwegian, and has been capped for the Norwegian under-18, 19 and 21 national teams.
