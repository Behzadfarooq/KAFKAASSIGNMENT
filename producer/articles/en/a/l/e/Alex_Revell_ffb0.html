Alex Revell (born July 7, 1983) is a professional footballer playing for Brighton & Hove Albion. He plays as a forward.

A graduate of the Cambridge youth team, Revell struggled to establish himself at the Abbey Stadium and in 2004 left the club, joining non-league side Braintree Town, for whom he netted 35 times for and helped gain promotion to the Conference South in the 2005/06 season. Revell joined Brighton for Â£8000 in the close season and soon became a popular figure with fans thanks largely to his strong work ethic and whole hearted displays.

During the January transfer window of 2007, he has been strongly linked with a move to Football League Championship side Coventry.
