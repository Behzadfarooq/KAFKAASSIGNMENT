Alexander Cambias, Sr. is a fictional character on ABC's daytime drama, All My Children. He is being portrayed by Ronald Guttman from July 23, 2003 until August 2003 and return on February 23, 2007. He faked his death in 2003. Alexander has turned out to be alive and also the Satin Slayer.
