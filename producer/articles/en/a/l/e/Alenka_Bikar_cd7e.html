Alenka Bikar

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Alenka Bikar (born 7 January 1974) is a Slovenian sprinter who specialized in the 200 metres.

[edit] Achievements

Year Tournament Venue Result Extra
1997 Mediterranean Games Bari, Italy 3rd 200 m
2000 European Indoor Championships Ghent, Belgium 2nd 200 m
2001 World Indoor Championships Lisbon, Portugal 6th 200 m
World Championships Edmonton, Canada 6th 200 m
2002 European Championships Munich, Germany 8th 100 m
8th 200 m
2005 Mediterranean Games Almería, Spain 1st 200 m

[edit] Personal bests

  • 100 metres - 11.21 s (2001)
  • 200 metres - 22.76 s (2001)
