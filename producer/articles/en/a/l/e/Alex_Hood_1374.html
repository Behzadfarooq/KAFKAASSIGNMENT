Alex Hood (born 20 October 1989) is a Canadian actor who is known as the voice of Kenny in Beyblade. Hood has featured in other cartoons such as Arthur (as "the Brain") and Peep and the Big Wide World (as "Tom"). Hood has also had roles in TV shows/movies such as Zixx: Level One (as "Griff Chalanchuk"), The Man Who Saved Christmas, Khaled, In a Heartbeat, a recurring role in Naturally, Sadie as Ron Yuma and was recently in the Disney Channel Original Movie Cow Belles. He currently is in his final year at Upper Canada College.
