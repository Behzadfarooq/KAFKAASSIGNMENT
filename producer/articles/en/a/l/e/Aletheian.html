Aletheian are a Christian progressive death metal band that was formed in Lebanon, Pennsylvania in 1997 under the name "Crutch". They changed their name to "Aletheian" prior to the 2003 album Apolutrosis. Guitarist Alex Kenis is also a member of the band Becoming the Archetype
