Alex Chiu (Canadian politician)

Alex Chiu is a Filipino-Chinese-Canadian local politician and businessman.

Born in the Philippines, Chiu has been a town councillor for Ward 8 (and before Ward 7) in Markham since the 1990s. He also maintains a small business at Market Village Mall.
