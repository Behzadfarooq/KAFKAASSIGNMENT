Alex Von Schwedler

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Alex von Schwedler (born February 17, 1980 in Santiago, Chile) is a Chilean football (soccer) defender of German Descent, who currently plays for CS Marítimo in Superliga.

He debuted with Universidad de Chile in 1999, also has played for Chile national football team in 4 times debuting in 2000 and also for Red Bull Salzburg, SV Pasching, and Bari.
