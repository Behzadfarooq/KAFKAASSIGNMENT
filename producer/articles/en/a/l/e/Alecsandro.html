Alecsandro

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Alecsandro Barbosa Felisbino or just Alecsandro (born 1981-02-04 in Salvador) is a Brazilian footballer. He is the new on-loan striker for the Portuguese giants Sporting Clube de Portugal. Most notably he is the brother-in-law of Portugal ace Deco. Alecsandro joins Sporting with a promising record with his ex-club Cruzeiro, 5 goals in 8 games. Alecsandro recorded his first Sporting goal against Desportivo Aves in a league match.

[edit] Former clubs

  • Esporte Clube Vitória
  • Associação Atlética Ponte Preta
  • Cruzeiro Esporte Clube
