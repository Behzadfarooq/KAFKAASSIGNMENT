Hello! I am Alex from Germany. I learned English language for 9 years at school, then for few years at the university, and further through Internet articles, internet forums, communication with people all around the world, etc. I am still improving my knowledge of English and German languages, so minor errors can occur.

I contribute to articles in all of the languages that I speak, and I try to be neutral while writing and editing articles. If you have any notes on my spelling and grammar - please don't hesitate to correct them and to draw my attention to them. Any general disagreement with the content contributed by me or concerns about its NPOV, etc - please feel free to contact me using my discussion page: User talk:Alex ex. I am generally open to criticism.

Actually, I understand a little bit of various European languages, including french, italian, dutch, different germanic, slavic and romance languages. Not enough to understand most of the written text in their Wikipedias, but enough to understand, for example - that the interwiki link to the article was wrong.

By the way, I was born in Russia (at that time USSR) and live in Germany permanently since 1998. I am of Russian Germans / Volga German origin, so I am ethnical German, but a native Russian speaker. I speak german not absolutely perfect, but pretty good.
