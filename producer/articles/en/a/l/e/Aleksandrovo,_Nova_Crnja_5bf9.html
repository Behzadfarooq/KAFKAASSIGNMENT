Aleksandrovo, Nova Crnja

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Map of the Nova Crnja municipality, showing the location of Aleksandrovo
The Orthodox Church

Aleksandrovo (Александрово), formerly known as Velike Livade (Велике Ливаде), is a village in Serbia. It is situated in the Nova Crnja municipality, Central Banat District, Vojvodina province. The population of the village numbering 2,665 people (2002 census), of whom 2,435 are ethnic Serbs.

[edit] Name

In Serbian, the village is known as Aleksandrovo or Александрово, in Hungarian as Bozítópuszta, and in Croatian as Aleksandrovo.

[edit] Historical population

  • 1961: 4,034
  • 1971: 3,406
  • 1981: 3,061
  • 1991: 2,902
