User:Alexbonaro/Succession chart of Chicago Outfit crews

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

< User:Alexbonaro

Here is what I have for them.

North Side/North Suburbs Crew:

  • Ross Prio (1930s-1972) Died.
  • Dominick DiBella (1972-1976) Died.
  • Vincent Solano (1976-1992) Died.
  • Joseph "Joe the Builer" Andriacchi (1992-Present?) Also served as UnderBoss and Acting Boss/Became Boss.


Elmwood Park Crew: (Really part of the North side Crew)

  • John "Jackie" Cerone (1960s-1969) Became Boss.
  • Ernest "Rocky" Infelice (1969-1986) Became UnderBoss.
  • John "No Nose" DiFronzo (1986-1990) Became UnderBoss.
  • Marco D'Amico (1990-1994) Imprisoned.
    • -In 1994 this Crew was dispanded and merged with the North Side Crew.


Grand Ave. Crew:

  • Joseph "Joey the Clown" Lombardo (1970s-1986) Imprisoned.
  • Louis "The Mooch" Eboli (1986-1987) Died.
  • James Cozzo (1987-1994)
    • In 1994 this Crew was disbanded and merged with the North Side Crew.


West Side/Dupage County Crew:

  • William "Willie Potatoes" Daddano (1950s-1967) Imprisoned.
  • Fiori "Fi Fi" Buccieri (1967-1973) Died.
  • James "The Turk" Torrello (1973-1979) Died.
  • Joseph "Joe" Ferriola (1979-1986) Became Boss.
  • Dominic "Large" Cortina (1986-1990) Imprisoned.
    • Anthony Centracchio (1989-1990) Became Official Crew Boss.
  • Anthony Centracchio (1990-2001) Died.


Chicago Heights Crew:

  • Dominic Roberto (1920s-1928) Stepped Down.
  • James "Vincenzo Ammeratto " Emery (1928-1957) Died.
  • Francisco "Frankie Laporte" LiParota (1957-1972) Died.
  • Alfred "Al" Pilotto (1972-1982) Imprisoned.
  • Albert "Al the Fox" Tocco (1982-1989) Imprisoned.
    • Dominick "Tootsie" Palermo (1988-1989) Became Official Crew Boss.
  • Dominick "Tootsie" Palermo (1989-1994) Imprisoned.
    • Crew Boss-Vacant- (1991-1994)
    • In 1994 this Crew was disbanded and merged with the Chinatown Crew to form the new South Side Crew.


Chinatown Crew:

  • Ralph Pierce (1930s-1960s) Died.
  • Frank "Skids" Caruso (1960s-1970s) Died.
  • James "The Bomber" Cantura (1970s-1978) Killed.
  • Angelo "The Hook" LaPietra (1978-1986) Imprisoned.
  • James "Jimmy" LaPietra (1986-1993) Died.
  • Michael Talarico (1993-1994) Demoted.
    • In 1994 this Crew was disbanded and merged with the Chicago Heights Crew to form the new South Side Crew.


South Side Crew:

  • John "Johnny Apes" Monteleone (1994-2001) Died.
  • Anthony "Tony the Hatch" Chiaramonti (2001) Killed.
  • Anthony "Little Tony" Zizzo (2001-2006) Killed?. Also served as UnderBoss.


Cicero Crew:

  • Edward "Eddie" Vogel (1924-1960s) Died.
  • Joseph "Joey Doves" Aiuppa (1960s-1969) Became UnderBoss.
  • Sam "Wings" Carlisi (1969-1988) Became Boss.
  • Michael Spano (1988-2003) Imprisoned.
  • Michael “Mickey” Marcello (2003-2005) Imprisoned.
