Alex Stieda

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Alex Stieda, (Born April 13, 1961, Belville,Ontario) is a former professional road bicycle racer from Canada. Stieda captured the the yellow jersey on the second day of the 1986 Tour de France, becoming the first North American to lead the Tour de France. He finished in 120th place, in his only Tour de France, riding on the 7-Eleven Cycling Team. He also placed bronze in the 1983 Australian Commonwealth Games.
