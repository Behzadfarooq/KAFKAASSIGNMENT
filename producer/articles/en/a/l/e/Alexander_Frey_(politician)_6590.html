Alexander Frey (politician)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

This article is about a Finnish politician. For a conductor, see Alexander Frey.

Alexander Frey (7 June 1877 Vehkalahti - 28 November 1945 Helsinki) was a Finnish senator, bankman and was in the Finnish delegation of Treaty of Tartu between Finland and Russia. He was in Finnish parliament in 1917. Frey was a Chairman of the Board in Pohjoismaiden Yhdyspankki (nowadays part of Nordea) in 1928 - 1945.
