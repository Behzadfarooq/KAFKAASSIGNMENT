Alexa Havins

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Alexa Bruening, in a still from the opening sequence of All My Children

Alexa Carol Havins (born November 16, 1980 in Artesia, New Mexico) is an American actress.

An alumna of Chandler High School in Chandler, Arizona, her first major break was being cast in the film The Crow: Wicked Prayer. Later she was cast on All My Children as Babe Carey in 2003. Scheduling Conflicts between All My Children and Wicked Prayer forced her out of the film. She recently made a guest appearance on FX's Rescue Me, 2006.

Babe soon fell in love with the character of Jamie Martin, who is played by Justin Bruening. Off-screen the actors fell in love, and Bruening proposed to Havins following taping a scene where the character of Jamie proposes to Babe.

In 2005 she was nominated for a Soap Opera Digest Award and an Daytime Emmy Award, losing both to co-star Eden Riegel.

Since June 5, 2005 Havins is married to her All My Children co-star Justin Bruening.

It was said in an interview with Jeffrey Carlson that she was giving him tips about soap acting.
