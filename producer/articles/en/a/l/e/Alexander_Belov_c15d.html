Alexander Alexandrovich Belov (Russian: Александр Александрович Белов) (November 11, 1951 in Leningrad - October 3, 1978) was a Russian basketball player who won gold with the Soviet basketball team in Basketball at the 1972 Summer Olympics, scoring the winning basket in the gold medal game with no time left. He trained at Spartak in Leningrad. Belov died from a very rare decease - heart sarcoma.
