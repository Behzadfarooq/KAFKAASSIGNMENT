Alexandre Licata (born January 2, 1984 in Grenoble) is a French soccer player who currently plays for FC Gueugnon in Ligue 2 on loan from AS Monaco. He started his professional career at CS Louhans-Cuiseaux. Licata joined CS Louhans-Cuiseaux when they were in the Championnat National, scoring 20 goals in his first two season.
