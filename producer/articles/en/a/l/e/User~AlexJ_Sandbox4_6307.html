WikiProject: Motorsport
Shortcut:
WP:MOTOR

The purpose of the WikiProject:Motorsport is to maintain its child Wikiprojects as well as recognising, organising and improving the common areas between different categories of motorsport.

If you are interested, please feel free to help.


[edit] Child WikiProjects

Listed below are the child WikiProjects of WikiProject: Motorsport

[edit] Formula One


WikiProject Formula One aims to create, expand, update and standardize Wikipedia articles related to Formula One.

[edit] America Open Wheel Racing


WikiProject American Open Wheel Racing is a project to better organize information in articles related to Open Wheel Racing based in the United States, with an emphasis on the Indy Racing League, Champ Car, and Indy/Championship racing under the AAA and USAC banner dating to approximately 1909.

[edit] A1 Grand Prix


WikiProject A1 Grand Prix is aimed at keeping up-to-date, accurate and consistent information about the new motorsport series, A1 Grand Prix.

[edit] NASCAR


WikiProject NASCAR is a project to better organize information in articles related to NASCAR.

[edit] WRC


WikiProject World Rally aims to create and improve articles related to the World Rally Championship.

[edit] IROC


WikiProject IROC is a project to better organize information in articles related to the International Race of Champions invitation only racing series.
