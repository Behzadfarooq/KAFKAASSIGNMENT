Alex Rizzo (January 12, 1968 - May 16, 2002) was a professional wrestler, best known for his work in Extreme Championship Wrestling as Big Dick Dudley, part of the Dudley Family. He died on May 16, 2002 due to a kidney failure. He was 34 years old.

Trivia

  • Of the original Dudley Boys, Rizzo was in ECW the longest.
  • Rizzo was one of the founders of Xtreme Pro Wrestling.
  • Outside of the ring, Rizzo was good friends with Buh Buh Ray Dudley and Nicole Bass.
  • Nova gave Rizzo the idea for his Total Penetration finishing move.
  • At both November to Remember 1996 and Wrestlepalooza 1998, Rizzo did a Moonsault off of the top turnbuckle.
  • At ECW Barely Legal 1997, Rizzo was thrown off the announcer's stage, known as the 'eagle's nest', through a huge stack of tables by Tommy Dreamer.
  • At mid-2000, Rizzo wrestled in Puerto Rico at the World Wrestling Council against Abdullah the Butcher in the Anniversary 2000 super event, which received a losing effort.

In Wrestling

Championships

  • 1-time XPW Heavyweight Champion
  • 2-time USPW Heavyweight Champion
  • 1-time NWA New Jersey Heavyweight Champion
  • 1-time NWA New Jersey Hardcore Champion
  • 1-time USPW Tag-Team Champion with Psycho Sam Dudley

Finishing and signature moves

  • Big Dick Driver (Argentine Neckbreaker)
  • Total Penetration (Chokebomb)
  • Chokeslam
  • Lariat
  • Dudleysault (Moonsault)
