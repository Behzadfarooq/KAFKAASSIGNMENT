Alessandra is an Italian given name for a female. It is the female form of the male given name Alessandro.

Alessandra is the name of:

  • Alessandra Acciai, an Italian actress
  • Alessandra Ambrosio, a Brazilian supermodel who works for Victoria's Secret
  • Alessandra Ferri, an Italian-born ballet dancer
  • Alessandra Mussolini, an Italian politician, granddaughter of Benito Mussolini
  • Alessandra de Rossi, an Italian-Filipino actress
  • Alessandra Silvestri-Levy, a Brazilian curator and writer
  • Alessandra Stanley, an American journalist
  • Alessandra Toreson, an American actress
  • Alessandra Rubi Streignard Villarreal, a Spanish singer and model
