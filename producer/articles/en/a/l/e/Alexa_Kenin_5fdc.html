Alexa Kenin

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Alexa Kenin (February 16, 1962 â€“ September 10, 1985), was an American actress. She played Jena in the movie Pretty in Pink, Dana in Little Darlings and Marlene in Honkytonk Man. Kenin was murdered in New York soon after making her last movie, which was filmed in 1985 but not released until 1989. The murder was not solved. Pretty in Pink is dedicated to her and to the film's set decorator Bruce Weintraub, who died three months after her.
