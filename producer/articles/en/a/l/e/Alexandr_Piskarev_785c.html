Alexandr Piskaryov (Russian: Александр Михайлович Пискарёв) (born November 18, 1949) is a Russian football player and manager.

[edit] Work history:

  • Krasnaya Presnya Moscow (1980-83)
  • Kareda (1998-99)
  • FC Khimki Moscow (2000)
  • FC Dinamo Minsk (2000-01)
  • Mostransgaz Gazoprovod (2000)
  • FC Vostok (2004)
  • FC Anzhi Makhachkala (2003)
  • FC MTZ-RIPO Minsk (2004)

[edit] Played for:

  • Spartak Moscow (1971-75)
  • FC Rostov (1976-77)
  • Lokomotiv Kaluga (1978)
  • Spartak Ryazan (1979)
  • Krasnaya Presnya Moscow (1980)
