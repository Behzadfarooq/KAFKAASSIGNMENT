Alexander George Gordon, 7th Marquess of Aberdeen and Temair DL (b. March 31, 1955) is a British peer and the son of Alastair Gordon, 6th Marquess of Aberdeen and Temair.

Gordon was educated at Harrow School and married Joanna Clodagh Houldsworth, in 1981. They have four children:

  • George Ian Alastair Gordon, Earl of Haddo (b. 4 May 1983)
  • Lord Sam Dudley Gordon (b. 25 October 1985)
  • Lady Anna Katherine Gordon (b. 2 September 1988)
  • Lord Charles David Gordon (b. 4 June 1990)

He held the office of Deputy Lieutenant of Aberdeenshire in 1998.
