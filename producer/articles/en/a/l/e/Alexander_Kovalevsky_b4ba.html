Alexander Kovalevsky

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Alexander Onufrievich Kovalevsky (7 November 1840 Dvinsk, Russia - 1901) was a Russian embryologist who studied medicine at the University of Heidelberg and became professor at St Petersburg. He showed that all animals go through a period of gastrulation.
