I fixed the grammar error, added some context to the top, did some formatting and copyedit, added a link to the page that explains what White Lighters are, and added some to the plot, such as which central Charmed characters he has the most interaction with, which makes it a much beefier description of him than the dozens of other fan sites with articles for the character have (many of the others have exactly [other than the grammar error] what this started with). I don't watch the series, so there's not much more I can add. FWIW, White Lighters and Dark Lighters seem to be running themes in the series--terms for key groups of "good" characters and "evil" ones--central character Leo Wyatt is a White Lighter. I don't think Dark Lighters stay around for many episodes--the point of the show is to kill them. Niteowlneils 01:35, 24 Apr 2004 (UTC)
