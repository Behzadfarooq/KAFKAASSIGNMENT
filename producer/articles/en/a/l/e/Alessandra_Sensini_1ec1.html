Alessandra Sensini is an Italian sailor. She is a 4-time Olympian, winning a gold medal and two additional bronze medals. She also won 3 gold and 2 silver medals at World Championships, as well as 3 gold, 2 silver and 2 bronze medals at European Championships. She has sailed in three different boat types: Lechner, Mistral and RS:X.
