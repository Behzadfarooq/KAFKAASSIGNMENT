[edit] Biography

A native of Craigantlet in Northern Ireland's County Down, Alec Bennett immigrated with his parents to Canada at a very young age. He did not return to the United Kingdom until active duty with the Canadian Expeditionary Force during the First World War when he served as a dispatch rider and later as fighter pilot[1]. After racing with the works Norton team and also with the factory Sunbeam and Velocette teams, Alec Bennett retired from motor-cycle racing in the 1930s to concentrate on his motor-trade business in Southampton, Hampshire.[2]
