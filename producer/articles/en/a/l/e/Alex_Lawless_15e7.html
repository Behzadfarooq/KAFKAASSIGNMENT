Alexander Graham Lawless (born March 26, 1985) is a Welsh professional footballer currently playing in defence for Conference National side Forest Green Rovers. He has represented Wales at Under-21 level.

Lawless was born in the Rhondda Valley and began his career as a junior with Cardiff City, but signed as a trainee with Fulham, turning professional in July 2003. He spent two years at Craven Cottage, earning his only Under-21 cap in February 2006 against Northern Ireland, but never made the Fulham first team and was released at the end of the 2004-05 season.

In July 2005, Lawless joined Torquay United on a free transfer,[1] making his league debut on August 6, 2005 in a 0-0 draw at home to Notts County, Lawless only lasting 26 minutes before being replaced by substitute Tony Bedeau. He struggled to establish himself in the side and was released at the end of the season.

In August 2006 he joined Conference National side Forest Green Rovers.[2]
