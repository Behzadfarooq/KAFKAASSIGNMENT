Count Aleksander Stanisław Potocki (1778-1845) was a Polish noble, landowner, politician.

Senator castellan of the Polish Kingdom in 1824, chambellan of Napoleon Bonaparte. Knight of the Order of the White Eagle awarded on May 24, 1829
