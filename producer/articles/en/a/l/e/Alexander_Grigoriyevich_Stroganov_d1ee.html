Alexander Grigoriyevich Stroganov

Alexander Grigoriyevich Stroganov (Russian: Александр Григорьевич Строганов) was a member of the Stroganovs family.

He was the Russian minister of the interior from 1839-1841 and then a member of the State Council, beginning in 1849.

He also served as the Governor General of Novorossiia and Bessarabia from 1855 to 1863.[1]
