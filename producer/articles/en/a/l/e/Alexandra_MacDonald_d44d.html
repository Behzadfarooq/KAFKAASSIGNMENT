Alexandra MacDonald (born 1989) is a beauty queen from New Hampshire who will compete in the Miss Teen USA pageant in 2007.

MacDonald won the Miss New Hampshire Teen USA 2007 title in a state pageant held in Bedford, New Hampshire on September 9 2006. Her "sister" 2007 titleholder is Laura Silva of Londonderry, Miss New Hampshire USA 2007. This was her first attempt at the title.

MacDonald was crowned by outgoing titleholder Camille Westbrooks. She will competed in the Miss Teen USA 2007 pageant which will be broadcast live from an as-yet un-announced location in August 2007, where she will attempt to become the first delegate from New Hampshire to place in the nationally televised pageant since Janel Bishop won the Miss Teen USA title in 1991.
