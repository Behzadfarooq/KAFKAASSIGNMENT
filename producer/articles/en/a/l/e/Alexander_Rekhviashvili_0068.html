Alexander Rekhviashvili

Alexander Rekhviashvili (born August 6, 1974) is a football midfielder from Georgia. He plays on the national team and has been capped 21 times since his debut in 1999.

His club career started in Dinamo Tbilisi in 1991. He was fairly regular during the 1992/93 season, but was transferred to Gorda Rustavi where he spent four seasons. In 1997 he went to Latvian champion team Skonto FC, where he gradually became one of the better midfielders in the Latvian league, being voted the best league player at the end of 2000/01. In 2003 he was bought by Russian team Torpedo-Metallurg, but after one season he returned to Latvia and FK Ventspils where he plays today.
