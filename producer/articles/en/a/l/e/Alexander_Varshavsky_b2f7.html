Alexander Varshavsky

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Alexander Varshavsky is a Russian American biochemist and recipient of the Albert Lasker Award for Basic Medical Research, the Wolf Prize in Medicine and the Louisa Gross Horwitz Prize for his research on ubiquitination. He is currently researching at Caltech.
