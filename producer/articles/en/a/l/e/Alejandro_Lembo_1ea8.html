Alejandro Lembo (born 15 February 1978 in Montevideo) is a Uruguayan footballer. Lembo can play as a central or right-sided defender.

[edit] Career

[edit] Bella Vista

  • Lembo played for Bella Vista between year 1997 and 2000. He made 56 appearances, scoring 5 goals.

[edit] Parma

  • In 2000, Lembo signed for Parma. He left without playing a single game.

[edit] Nacional

  • Lembo played 109 games for Nacional, scoring 10 goals.

[edit] Real Betis

  • Lembo signed for Real Betis in 2003. He has played 44 La Liga matches and scored 1 goal. Although playing a major role in Betis's 2005 Copa del Rey run, he was dropped for the final.

[edit] International success

  • Lembo has made 44 international appearances for Uruguay, scoring 4 goals. He made his debut on 17 June 1999 against Paraguay.
