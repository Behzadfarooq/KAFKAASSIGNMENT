Alexis Michelle Gray (born January 20, 1992) is an up and comming professional wrestler, and will be training at OVW when she turns eighteen. Her wrestling gimmick is a follower of the Straight Edge culture, which she does follow in real life.

[edit] Personal Life

Born in Lexington, Kentucky, Alexis and her family traveled a lot when she was young, she's lived in Philadelphia, Lexington, & Columbus. Alexis has a younger sister, and a friend who she refers to as her "Big Brudder" because of the infulence he has had on her, kind of like a older brother in a way.


[edit] In wrestling

  • Finishing and signature moves
    • Devil Hearted Drop (630 splash)

  • Theme music
    • She is very vocal about what theme songs she wants to have, she is torn between three songs at the moment "The Runaways" by Anberlin, "Bitches" by Mindless Self Indulgence, & "7861" by Beneath the Sky.
