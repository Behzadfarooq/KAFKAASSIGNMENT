Alesana "Alex" Tuilagi is a Samoan rugby union footballer. He currently plays for the Leicester Tigers in the English club competition, the Guinness Premiership, and has appeared numerous times for his national team Samoa. He previously played for Parma in Italy.

Whilst at Parma, whom he played with for two seasons, he was equal second top try scorer of the league for the 2003-04 season, with 12 tries in total for the season. He helped them get to the semi-finals of both the domestic competition, and the ParkerPen Shield. He made his debut for Samoa in a 2003 Rugby World Cup qualifer against Fiji in June of 2002.

The Tuilagi family has strong connections with Leicester Tigers; Alex's brothers Henry and Anitelia (Andy) also play for Leicester, and his other brother Fereti (Freddie) is a former player.

He was recently included in the Pacific Islanders rugby union team tour to Europe in late 2006.
