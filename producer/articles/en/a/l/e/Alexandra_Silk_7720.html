Alexandra Silk (born September 19, 1963 in Long Island, New York) is an American pornographic actress, pornographic director and adult model.

[edit] Biography

A graduate of State University of New York in Albany, New York, she started off in the pornographic film industry as a stripper in Las Vegas. During her tenure as a stripper, she encountered porn star Jenna Jameson who gave her some tips to enter the pornographic industry. Upon following up on this, she made way to Hollywood, where she met up with Ron Jeremy, who introduced her to performers, directors and producers.

During the course of her career she married porn star Luc Wylder on December 31, 1999. Amongst performances in over 350 pornographic films, she also appeared in music videos, television spots and movies.

In print, she has appeared in pictorials for magazines, notably Playboy, Vogue, and Maxim.

She can still be seen in reruns as Bailiff Silk in the PlayboyTV's sitcom, Sex Court and its radio show on Sirius Satellite Radio called Sex Court.
