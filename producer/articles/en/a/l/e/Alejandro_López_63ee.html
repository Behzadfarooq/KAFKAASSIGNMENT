Alejandro López

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Alejandro López (born February 9, 1975) is a Mexican race walker.

[edit] Achievements

Year Tournament Venue Result Event
1997 Universiade Catania, Italy 2nd 20 km
1998 Central American and Caribbean Games San Juan, Puerto Rico 3rd 20 km
1999 World Championships Seville, Spain 12th 20 km
Universiade Palma de Mallorca, Spain 1st 20 km
2001 World Championships Edmonton, Canada 11th 20 km
Pan American Race Walking Cup Cuenca, Ecuador 1st 20 km
2002 World Race Walking Cup Turin, Italy 2nd 20 km
Central American and Caribbean Games San Salvador, El Salvador 1st 20 km
2003 World Championships Paris, France 9th 10 km
World Championships Paris, France 9th 20 km
Pan American Games Santo Domingo, Dominican Republic 3rd 20 km
