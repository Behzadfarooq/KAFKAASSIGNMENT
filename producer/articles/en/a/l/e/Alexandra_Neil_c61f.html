Alexandra Neil (born April 7, 1955 in Boston, Massachusetts) is an American actress.

Neil has portrayed a number of characters on numerous soap operas such as Texas, Ryan's Hope, Another World, Guiding Light, and As the World Turns.

Previously portraying the role of Jeannie Johnson in the 1980s, Neil returned to the ABC soap opera, One Life to Live in the role of Dr. Paige Miller #3 since January 2006. Paige is the love interest of Bo Buchanan (Robert S. Woods.
