Aleisha Allen (born in the Bronx, NY on April 28, 1991, also known as LaNa√© Allen) is an American child actress.

Since the age of four, she has been active in television and print as a model. At the age of six, she made an appearance on the children's television series Blue's Clues. She was also a regular on Disney's television show Out of the Box. In 2001 she was in The Adventures of Joan Lee. Recently she has appeared along side Jack Black in School of Rock (2003). She played Alicia, one of the children in the class. In 2005, she played Lindsey in Are We There Yet? starring Ice Cube. She is planning to appear in the sequel Are We Done Yet? scheduled for release in 2007. She is a singer and ballerina. She is currently working in Australia for the cameo role in the movie "Fools Gold" starring A grade celebrities Kate Hudson and Matthew McConaughey.

She currently attends the Professional Performing Arts School in Manhattan, NY.
