Alejandro Brodersohn

Alejandro Brodersohn (born in Buenos Aires) is an Argentine film editor.[1]

Some of the films he's edited have been critically well received: Buenos Aires Vice Versa, (1996) Whisky Romeo Zulu (2004), 18-j (2004), and El Abrazo partido (2004).
