Alejandro Corichi

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Alejandro Corichi is a theoretical physicist working at the Department of Gravitation and Field Theory of the National Autonomous University of Mexico (UNAM). He obtained his bachelor degree at UNAM and his PhD at Pennsylvania State University (1997). His field of study is General Relativity and Quantum Gravity, where he has contributed to the understanding of classical aspects of black holes and to the non-commutativity and black holes within the approach known as Loop quantum gravity.
