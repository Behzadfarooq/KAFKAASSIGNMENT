Alexandre Rodrigues

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Alexandre Rodrigues as Busca-pé/Rocket

Alexandre Rodrigues (born 1983 in Rio de Janeiro, Brazil) is a Brazilian actor. He is most famous for playing the part of Busca-pé (Rocket in the English subtitled version), the narrator and main protagonist in the 2002 film Cidade De Deus (City Of God). He has most recently appeared in a cameo of his character in City of God in American singer John Legend's music video "PDA" released in 2007
