Alex Wetten

Alex Charles Wetten is a film star who has appeared in the film Wondrous Oblivion, as Baxter. More recently he starred in the popular Channel 5 children's programme, The Secret of Eel Island as Grasse, the bully's sidekick.

London born Alex now resides in Norfolk and is currently in education at the independent Norwich School. He is taking an active part in local theatre projects but has put television appearances to one side.

Alex was recently in the Oddessy at the Maddermarket theatre, Norwich. He is my hero!
