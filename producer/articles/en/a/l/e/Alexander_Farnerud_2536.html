Alexander Farnerud (born May 1, 1984) is a Swedish football player.

He currently plays for VfB Stuttgart in Germany. He played for Landskrona BoIS in Sweden and Racing Strasbourg in France. He's the brother of Pontus Farnerud. He has played for the Sweden national football team.
