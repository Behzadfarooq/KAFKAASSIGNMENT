Alexandros Khalkokondilis was a Greek athlete. He competed at the 1896 Summer Olympics in Athens.

He competed in the long jump, placing fourth of the nine jumpers. His best jump was 5.74 metres.

In the 100 meters competition, Khalkondilis placed second in his initial heat with a time of 12.75 seconds. In the final, he came in at 12.6 seconds, just barely behind the joint bronze medal lists Francis Lane and Alojz Sokol to finish in fifth place.
