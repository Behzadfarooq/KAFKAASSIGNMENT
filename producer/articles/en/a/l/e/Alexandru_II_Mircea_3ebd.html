Alexandru II Mircea

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

You have new messages (last change).

Alexandru II Mircea was Hospodar of Wallachia from 1574 to 1577. He was the father of Mihnea Turcitul. His parents were Mircea III Dracul and Despina Kantakouzenos.
