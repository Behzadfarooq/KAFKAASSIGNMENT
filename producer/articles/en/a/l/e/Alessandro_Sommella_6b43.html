Alessandro Sommella

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Alessandro Sommella (Napoli, Italy, 1956) is an Italian musician.
He studied electric bass, guitar, violin and double bass.
He works in London with Souled out (1980) and with Planet Funk, and in Italy with Marcella Boccia, an Italian poet.
