Alessandro Lambruschini

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Olympic medal record
Men's athletics
Bronze 1996 Atlanta 3000 m steeplechase

Alessandro Lambruschini (born 7 January 1965 in Fucecchio) is an Italian long-distance runner who specializes in the 3000 metres steeplechase.

[edit] Achievements

Year Tournament Venue Result Extra
1987 Mediterranean Games Latakia, Syria 1st
1988 Olympic Games Seoul, South Korea 4th
1990 European Championships Split, Yugoslavia 3rd
1991 Mediterranean Games Athens, Greece 2nd
1992 Olympic Games Barcelona, Spain 4th
1993 World Championships Stuttgart, Germany 3rd
1994 European Championships Helsinki, Finland 1st
1996 Olympic Games Atlanta, United States 3rd
1998 European Championships Budapest, Hungary 2nd

[edit] Personal bests

  • 1500 metres - 3:35.27 min (1987)
  • 3000 metres - 7:44.7 min (1997)
  • 3000 metres steeplechase - 8:08.78 min (1993)
