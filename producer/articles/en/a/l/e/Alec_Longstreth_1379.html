Alec Longstreth

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Alec Longstreth is a New York City-based comics creator whose works include Phase 7 and the Dvorak Zine.

He is a graduate of Oberlin College.

[edit] Awards

2005 Ignatz Award for Outstanding Minicomic
