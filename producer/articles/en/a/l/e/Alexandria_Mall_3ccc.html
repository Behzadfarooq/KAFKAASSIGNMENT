Alexandria Mall

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Alexandria Mall is a shopping mall located in Alexandria, Louisiana. It is anchored by Dillard's, JCPenney, Sears, Burlington Coat Factory, Stage and Weiss-Goldring. There was also a Stein Mart at the mall which closed its 38,019 sq. ft. store on April 30, 2006 due to underperformance. Service Merchandise had a freestanding 59,400 sq. ft. store that closed in 2001 when the company went out of business. The mall is 869,000 sq. ft. total with 80 stores and 4,800 parking spaces. The mall opened in 1973 with renovations in 1986.

[edit] Anchors & Majors

  • Dillard's (102,000 sq. ft.)
  • JCPenney (145,481 sq. ft.)
  • Rite Aid (12,734 sq. ft.)
  • Sears (109,000 sq. ft.)
  • Burlington Coat Factory (63,799 sq. ft.)
  • Stage (29,099 sq. ft.)
  • Weiss & Goldring (30,040 sq. ft.)

[edit] Former anchors

  • K&B (now Rite Aid)
  • Mervyns (closed 2006, now Burlington Coat Factory)
  • Stein Mart (38,019 sq.ft.) (closed 2006, vacant)
