Alexandre Geijo Pazos (born 5 September 1982 in Geneva) is a Spanish/Swiss football (soccer) striker. Currently plays for Xerez CD in Segunda División.

He also played for Neuchâtel Xamax, Málaga CF B and Málaga CF.
