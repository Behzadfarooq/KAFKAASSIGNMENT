[edit] History

[edit] Tekken 2

Alex is a genetically-created velociraptor made from a fossil by Doctor Boskonovitch during Kazuya Mishima's tenure as the head of the Mishima Zaibatsu. Alex and Roger appear to have been trained in their fighting art by Armor King.

[edit] Tekken Tag Tournament

Alex is a playable character in the non-canonical Tekken Tag Tournament game.

[edit] Tekken Tag Tournament Ending

Alex is seen playing "Rock, Paper, Scissors" with Yoshimitsu. His boxing glove forces him to choose "Rock", while Yoshimitsu picks "Paper". Yoshimitsu wins three times.

[edit] Other Tekken games

After they appear in Tekken Tag Tournament, Alex and Roger do not make any more appearances in the Tekken series. Roger's wife and son are playable in Tekken 5, though. They fight as one, using the screen name Roger Jr., the name of Roger's son, although Roger's wife actually fights, and they replace both Roger and Alex in Tekken 5. Roger does appear in Tekken 5, but he is not playable, and he only appears in Roger Jr.'s Tekken 5 prologue and epilogue.

[edit] Trivia

  • His 'sub-bosses' could include Roger and Yoshimitsu.
  • He shows up as another fighting animal and 'alternate costume' to the boxing kangaroo Roger in Tekken 2 and Tekken Tag Tournament.
  • A pack of Alexes appears in Tekken the Animated Movie. They were torn apart by Kazuya Mishima with Jun Kazama, Lei Wulong, and Jane.
  • Alex's original name was mentioned by Lee Chaolan as Rex. But due to ambiguous use of the term 'Rex' to describe a raptor, Namco changed it to Alex.
