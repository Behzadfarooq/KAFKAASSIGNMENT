Here is a followup chart I made for the Luccheses in 1998 just before Joe Defede was indicted. I elimninated the guy's that were imprisoned for long stretches in the early/mid 90s, plus the guy's who flipped or died. I've also added the guy's that were made after that. I also made the necessary leadership changes. If you eliminate the guy's that were imprisoned and the guy's who may have been inactive/dead, you get the 75 Active Made Member figure that the Feds had for the family in 1998. It gives a much more clear picture of how the family looks today.
