Alexander Nikolaevich Kurlovitch (Russian: Александр Николаевич Курлович) (English Alternate: Alexandre Kurlovich[1]) (born July 28, 1961 in Grodno) was an Olympic weightlifter for the USSR. He trained at Armed Forces sports society in Grodno.

[edit] Weightlifting achievements

  • Senior World Champion (1987, 1989, 1991, and 1994)
  • Set twelve world records during career
