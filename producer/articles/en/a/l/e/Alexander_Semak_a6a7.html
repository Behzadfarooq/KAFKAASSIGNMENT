Alexander Semak (born February 11, 1966 in Ufa, U.S.S.R.) is a retired professional ice hockey centre who played in the NHL for six seasons from 1991-92 until 1996-97. He played for the New Jersey Devils, New York Islanders, Tampa Bay Lightning and Vancouver Canucks.

Semak was drafted 207th overall by the New Jersey Devils in the 1988 NHL Entry Draft. He played 289 career NHL games, scoring 83 goals and 91 assists for 174 points. His best offensive season was the 1992-93 NHL season when he scored 37 goals and 42 assists for 79 points, all career-highs.

Currently, he is coaching hockey in Moscow. Married to his wife, Julia and has a daughter named Kristina.
