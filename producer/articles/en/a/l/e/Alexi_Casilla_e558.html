Alexi Casilla (born July 20, 1984 in San Cristobal, Dominican Republic) is a Major League Baseball infielder. On December 9, 2005, the Anaheim Angels traded Casilla to the Minnesota Twins for pitcher J.C. Romero. Casilla made his major league debut with the Twins in September of 2006.

[edit] Trivia

  • Casilla was playing for the New Britain Rock Cats when he was called up to the Twins.
