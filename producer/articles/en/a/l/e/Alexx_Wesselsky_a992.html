Alexx Wesselsky is the lead singer of the German band Eisbrecher. He is also the former lead singer of the band Megaherz.

Alex joined in Megaherz in 1993 as one of the founding members, writing lyrics and composing as well as singing. He left the band in 2002, and was replaced by Mathias "Jablonski" Elsholz. In addition to Megaherz, since 1999 he has been working as a studio singer and lyric writer for several independent projects (among others, a platinum production).

The desire for something new and for hard music beyond current metal-clichés led him to join Noel Pix in the Fall of 2002 to form Eisbrecher.
