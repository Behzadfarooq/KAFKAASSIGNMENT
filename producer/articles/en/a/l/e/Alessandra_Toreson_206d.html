Alessandra Olivia Toreson (born May 29, 1987 in Palo Alto, California), an American actress whose most notable role to date is as "Holly Pryor" in the TV show Newton. She also co-starred in the Disney Channel Original Movie Going to the Mat. Before being an actress she studied dancing and singing from the age of two. Her television debut was at age nine when she hosted the "Kids WB Club" in San Francisco. Her other television credits include Even Stevens, JAG, ER, Arrested Development, and Malcolm In The Middle among others.
