The Alexander Graham Bell Association for the Deaf and Hard of Hearing, also known as the A.G. Bell Association is a resource, support network and advocate for listening, learning, talking and living independently with hearing loss. Through publications, advocacy, training, scholarships and financial aid, The A.G. Bell Association promotes the use of oralism as a means of language acquisition as well as hearing technology and cochlear implants. It is headquartered in Washington, D.C., with chapters located in the United States with a network of international affiliates.
