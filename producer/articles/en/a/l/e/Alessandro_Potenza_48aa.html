Alessandro Potenza (born 8 March 1984 in San Severo) is an Italian football central defender.

Potenza only appearance for Inter's first team was Coppa Italia match against A.S. Bari, 4 December 2002.

[edit] Teams and clubs

  • 2002-2003: Internazionale
  • 2003-2004: Ancona & Parma on loan from Inter
  • 2004-2005: Parma & Chievo on loan from Inter
  • 2005-2006: Mallorca on loan from Inter
  • 2006-present: Fiorentina

[edit] Awards

  • 2003 UEFA U-19 Championship
  • 2004 UEFA U-21 Championship
