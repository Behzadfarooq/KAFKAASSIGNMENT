Alex Raphael Meschini or simply Alex (born March 25, 1982 in Cornélio Procópio) is a attacking-midfielder player from Brazil. He currently plays at Internacional. Alex joined from Guarani on March 11 2004 for a fee of £325,000, signing a 4 year deal with a buy-out clause of £13.25 million.
