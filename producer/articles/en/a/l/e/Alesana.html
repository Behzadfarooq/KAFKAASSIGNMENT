Alesana is an American post-hardcore band formed in October 2004, in Raleigh, North Carolina. Alesana first joined Tragic Hero Records in 2005. Alesana was Tragic Hero Records first band to be signed on. In late 2006 they signed on with Fearless Records.

Alesana has toured the United States and Mexico during 2006 & 2007 with various bands such as Haste The Day and blessthefall.
