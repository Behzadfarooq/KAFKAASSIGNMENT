Alessandro Pesenti-Rossi (born August 31, 1942) is a former Formula One driver from Italy, born in Bergamo. He participated in four Grands Prix with a privately entered Tyrrell, debuting on August 1, 1976. He scored no championship points, but managed to finish every race that he started.

Prior to his brief time in Formula One, Pesenti-Rossi raced in Italian Formula 3 and Formula 2 with moderate success, and it was to F2 that he returned in 1977.
