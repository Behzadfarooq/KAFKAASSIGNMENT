[edit] Admins

Admins, or administrators, or sysops (system operators), are certain Wikipedians who are selected by the community to gain certain extra privileges. Those privileges, including the ability to delete pages, to protect pages from editing and to block and ban abusive users, are used to maintain the Wikipedia project. For more information, see WP:ADMIN. —Cuiviénen, Thursday, 23 March 2006 @ 20:41 (UTC)
