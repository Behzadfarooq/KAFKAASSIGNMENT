Alessandra Marc

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Alessandra Marc, born 29 July 1957 in Berlin, Germany is an American opera singer.

Born Judith Borden, Marc studied at the University of Maryland and privately with soprano Marilyn Cotlow. She first came to public attention at the Wexford Festival in 1987 in Giordano's La cena delle beffe. She made her debut in Richard Strauss' Ariadne auf Naxos at the Vienna State Opera in 1992 and as Aida at the Rome Opera. She also made her debuts at the Metropolitan Opera, the San Francisco Opera and the Lyric Opera of Chicago in that role. Her other signature role, Turandot, is one she has sung at the Royal Opera House, Covent Garden, Opéra Bastille in Paris and the Teatro alla Scala in Milan, among others.
