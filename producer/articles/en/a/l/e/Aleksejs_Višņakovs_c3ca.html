Aleksejs Višņakovs (born February 3, 1984 in Riga) is a football midfielder from Latvia.

As of April 2007 he has played 20 international matches and scored 3 goals for the Latvia national team. He debuted in 2004 against Oman. Since 2001 he is playing for Skonto FC.
