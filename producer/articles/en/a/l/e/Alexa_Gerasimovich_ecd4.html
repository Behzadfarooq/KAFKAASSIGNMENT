Alexa Veronica Gerasimovich (born April 11, 2002 in New York City, New York) is an American child actress.

[edit] Alexa Early life

Alexa started her modeling career at 5 months old and by the time she was 6 months old, she booked her first commercial (GAP) and appeared on the cover of Parents magazine. She appeared on the cover of American Baby the following month.

Alexa has two younger sisters, Ashley and Erin, who are also models.

[edit] Television roles

Alexa briefly protrayed Natalie Snyder on As the World Turns in mid-2006. She is currently protraying Kathy on All My Children, who will turn out to be Tad and Dixie's daughter that was given up at birth named Kate.

[edit] Film roles

Alexa appeared in 2006's film the World Trade Center as Erin McLoughlin. But before landing the role on film World Trade Center, she landed her first role in the movie "Bella" playing the role of Lucinda (2006).
