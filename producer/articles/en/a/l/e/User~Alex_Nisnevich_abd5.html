[edit] An explanation of sorts

When I started editing Wikipedia, about a year ago, there were only about 200 WikiProjects, so I joined those that interested me. However, now we have several thousand. There are dozens that interest me, and that I can join, but quite frankly I don't have the time to work in so many WikiProjects at once. For this reason, other than the Cleanup Taskforce, perhaps the 0.5/1.0 crew, and my own WikiProject (Shannara), I do not think I'll participate in Projects or Organizations at this point. -- Alex Nisnevich 05:18, 26 October 2006 (UTC)
