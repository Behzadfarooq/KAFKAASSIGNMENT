Alex Ramírez (born October 3, 1974 in Caracas, Venezuela) is a former Major League Baseball right fielder who played for the Cleveland Indians (1998-2000) and Pittsburgh Pirates (2000). He currently plays for the Tokyo Yakult Swallows as a cleanup batter. He bats and throws right-handed.

In three seasons, Ramírez batted .259 with 12 home runs, 48 RBI, 38 runs, 17 doubles, three triples, and three stolen bases in 135 games.
