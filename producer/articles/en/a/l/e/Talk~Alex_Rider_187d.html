[edit] When were these books published?

Why isn't this information included? Brother William has a very important point, when he says that books can be seen as conversations. Not just scholarly works, but also fiction. One author writes something. Five, ten or fifteen years later, another author writes a book in reply (or a series of books), and later a reply to that is written by a third person. That's why first publication date is such a vital bit of information. Are these books from the 1960's, 1980's, very recent, or what? --Peter Knutsen 02:16, 11 March 2006 (UTC)
