Alejandro Silva

Alejandro Silva is a Chilean rock guitarist. His albums generally consist of instrumental work only, without the vocals that are common to most rock and heavy metal music.

Silva started guitar when he was fourteen years old. Other musicians he has played with include Marty Friedman, Joe Satriani, Steve Vai, and Billy Sheehan.

He is also a guitar teacher, sound engineer, and has a line of guitar amplifier with his name.

[edit] Discography

  • Alejandro Silva I (1999)
  • Dios Eol (2002)
  • ASPC Live (2003) (ASPC stands for Alejandro Silva Power Cuarteto, Spanish for Alejandro Silva Power Quartet)
