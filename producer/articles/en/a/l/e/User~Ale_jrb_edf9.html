Articles and Writing

I spend a reasonable amount of time on Wikipedia, but am generally poor at writing articles and would have to classify myself as a Wikignome. I spend a lot of time patrolling recent changes and fighting vandalism, but I also will correct spelling errors and help Wikify articles.

Reversion Mistake?

I have been known to make mistakes when it comes to reverting vandalism. If I have reverted your changes, and warned you, for vandalism and you think I am mistaken, please contact me by clicking the 'Report Incorrect Vandalism Reversion' button to the right and letting me know.
