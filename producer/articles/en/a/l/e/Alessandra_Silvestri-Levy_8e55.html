Alessandra Silvestri-Levy (born 01 November in São Paulo) is a Brazilian curator and writer. Researcher and art curator, produced several exhibitions about photographie, contemporary painting, literature, music and film festivals, mainly in Paris, Rome, São Paulo and Havana. Co-writer of Cuba by Korda International Executive Director for the Brazilian Cinema Foundation - Cinemateca Brasileira. Lives between Paris and São Paulo. Humanitarian activist.

Born to Italian parents and educated between Italy, Brazil, the USA and France.
