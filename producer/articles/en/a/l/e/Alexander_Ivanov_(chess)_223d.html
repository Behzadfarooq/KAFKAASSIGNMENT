Alexander Ivanov (born May 1, 1956) is a chess Grandmaster who lives in and represents the United States of America. He was joint US Champion in 1995 (with Nick de Firmian and Patrick Wolff). In the April 2004 FIDE list, Ivanov had an Elo rating of 2544, making him joint nineteenth in the United States.
