Alex McSweeney

Alex McSweeney is a British actor, best known for playing Graham Foster in EastEnders from 2003 to 2004.

He also played David Radford in The Bill in 2004.

Amongst others, programmes he has appeared in include Silent Witness and Holby City.

He will also appear in Lewis in 2007.
