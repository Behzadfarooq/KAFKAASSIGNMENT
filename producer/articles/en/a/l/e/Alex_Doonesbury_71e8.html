Alex Doonesbury is a character in the comic strip Doonesbury.

She is the daughter of Mike Doonesbury and J.J. Caucus. From her early years Alex has shown herself to be highly intelligent, and an excellent student. She is brilliant at all things related to computers, and is an exceptional hacker.

Alex Doonesbury

Although she gets along fairly well with her father, she harbors some resentment for her mother, whom she blames for the ruination of her and Mike's marriage. This was made worse when Mike remarried to Kim Rosenthal, whom Alex adores, and J.J. remarried to "Uncle Stupidhead", whom Alex loathes. Alex has a good relationship with her grandmother Joanie, they have bonded over the strangeness of J.J.

Alex has some differences with her father as well, whom she considers a sellout for voting Republican (he was formerly a liberal) and for driving an SUV. She is politically very liberal and was a supporter of Howard Dean in the 2004 election. She also supports file sharing, another issue on which she and Mike clash.

Alex runs "Alex-Cam", a webcam show devoted entirely to herself. She has a fanbase of over 700,000, and even makes money from product placement. She also runs Myvulture.com, the overstock selling website that Mike and Kim own.

She recently graduated from high school, and was accepted into her father's alma mater, the fictional Walden College, along with Cornell[1], Georgetown, MIT, RIT, Rensselaer, and Harvard, among others. In an online poll, it was decided that she would attend MIT[2], although Alex pointed out that MIT "hacked the poll."

Alex has begun at MIT but continues to call home at the slightest difficulty, such as a dorm dispute over one student's collection of mold. She's also started a Facebook account.
