Alessandra Rosaldo (born Alejandra Sánchez Barredo on September 11, 1971) is a Mexican actress and singer, earning her acting fame in the the Spanish soap-opera DKDA. She was recognized as the best debut actress with the TVyNovels Award. She was also the lead singer for Sentidos Opuestos, a famous group in Mexico during the 90's decade.

[edit] Biography

Alessandra has always been surrounded by music and acting, her father Jaime S. Rosaldo is a great producer. At age 12, she started participating in choirs and she even started in a children's movie. At age 19 she met with Chacho Gaytan at an airport, since then they've becom great friends, years later Miguel Blasco proposed that they should form a trio of which would be formed by Chacho, Alessandra and another member. Later they would be know as Sentidos Opuestos.
