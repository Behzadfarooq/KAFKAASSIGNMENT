Alex Leigh is an English fashion model. She was born in Manchester on 3 January in 1978 and was formerly a waitress for William's Tavern.

Leigh started modelling at the age of 17. She has claimed that she was attracted by the hectic and unpredictable lifestyle rather than fame and fortune[citation needed]. Many catwalk shows for well-known designers such as Betty Jackson, Tristan Webber, Jasper Conran, Dai Rees and Boudica have featured her[citation needed]. Alex has graced the pages of class leading magazines, including Cosmopolitan, Harpers & Queen, ELLE, Dazed & Confused and Australian Vogue[citation needed]. She has appeared as the cover model for Later, Shine and Condé Nast Traveler and has been photographed by prestigious photographers like Rankin, Tony McGee and John Swannell[citation needed]. She stars in the Lynx TV fragrance adverts[citation needed].
