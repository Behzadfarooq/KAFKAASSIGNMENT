[edit] Movies

Hi there. I and other editors have removed your edits from Luis Bunuel and Mamma Roma because they looked like essays copied and pasted from other parts of the internet. We don't do that in articles even if you have permission from the author because its preferable just to link to the essay (Wikipedia: External links). If however, that is your own work, you've got to make more of an effort to integrate it into the existing frame of the article, otherwise it sticks out like a sore thumb when someone is reading the article. Also be aware that conclusions that you have drawn by yourself can't be used (Wikipedia: NOR).

Don't let that discourage you from editing; this is just advice on how articles are generally written. Its obvious that you have a good enthusiam for movies and we always need more people like that. Gershwinrb 19:18, 3 May 2006 (UTC)
