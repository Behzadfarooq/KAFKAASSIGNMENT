Aleppo International Airport (IATA: ALP, ICAO: OSAP) is an international airport serving Aleppo, Syria. The airport serves as a secondary hub for Syrian Arab Airlines

[edit] Terminal

Aleppo International Airport has a modern terminal which combines a modern and Islamic architecture. The total area is 28,000 square meters over four floors. The airport capacity is 1.5 million passengers a year. There is a bank, coffee shop, duty free shop, rent-a-car office, and a syriaTel office.
