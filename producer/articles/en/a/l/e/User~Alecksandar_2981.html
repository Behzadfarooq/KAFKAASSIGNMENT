[edit] Alexander Roussel (1988-)

[edit] Beginnings

Alexander James Roussel was born in Lafayette, Louisiana on May 27, 1988 to Tim Morgan and Donna Orgeron. Witnessing his birth was Paige Guarisco Roussel, his adoptive mother. He was part of an open adoption. Full visitation between both parties has existed throughout his life.

Alexander, or Alex, was raised in Berwick, Louisiana by Paige and her husband, Gregory Roussel. He enjoyed a life free of want in the small town. After only a year of bliss the perfect life seemed to be shattered when Alex began having seizures. He has lived his entire life with epilepsy and also with three other adopted children: Peytyn in 1989, Cooper in 1998, and Shanna in 2003.

[edit] Art

Alex has always loved to paint and from the age of five until he started college. Everything from animals to Greek gods, flowers to his family members all created in his own special way.

[edit] Music

Early on Alex was discovered to have a gorgeous voice and he on many occations has been asked to perform and participate in varioius venues both religious and secular. The song that made everyone in his area's relegious circles take notice was O Holy Night, which he perform in a musical done by a Baptist church near his home town. In the secular world Memory, from Cats, perked the ears of music lovers.

His performances continued with the Prayer in both Italian at his high school and in english as a cameo appearance at a local Pentacostal church. His was asked by his high school's drama club if he would perform the part of Beast in Disney's Beauty & the Beast. He had never performed with the drama club before.

[edit] New Beginnings

Alexander is currently studying to be a History Professor at the University of Louisisana at Lafayette. He is back in his home town and has just moved into a new home. He still maintains a wonderful relationship with both his birth parents (who are now married) and his adoptive parents and all of his many siblings.

In January is chaperoning a group of high school students, from his Alma Mater (Berwick High) and the near by Morgan City High, on a trip to New York City. There he hopes to help immerse them all in the art, music, and culture of the Big Apple.
