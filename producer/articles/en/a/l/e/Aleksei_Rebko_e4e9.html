Aleksei Rebko (born 23 April 1986) is a Russian football (soccer) midfielder. He currently plays for FC Spartak Moscow.

He broke the record of youngest players record for Spartak in 2002, previously hold by current team mate Aleksandr Pavlenko.

He made two appearances in UEFA Champions League 2006-07, but in total just played 13 mins.
