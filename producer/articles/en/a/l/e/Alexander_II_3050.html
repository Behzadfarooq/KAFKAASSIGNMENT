Alexander II

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

A number of historical people were named Alexander II:

  • Alexander II of Macedon, King of Macedon from 370 to 368 B.C.
  • Alexander II of Epirus, King of Epirus in 272 B.C.
  • Pope Alexander II, Pope from 1061 to 1073
  • Alexander II of Scotland (1198–1249), the King of Scotland
  • Alexander II of Russia (1818–1881), the Emperor of Russia
