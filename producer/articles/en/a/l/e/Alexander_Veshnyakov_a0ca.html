Alexander Veshnyakov

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Alexander Albertovich Veshnyakov (Russian: Александр Альбертович Вешняков; b. November 24, 1952, in Arkhangelsk Oblast, Soviet Union) is a Russian official. From March 21, 1995, till March 24, 1999, he was the Secretary of the Central Election Commission of Russia. He was the Chairman of the Central Election Commission from March 24, 1999, till March 26, 2007.
