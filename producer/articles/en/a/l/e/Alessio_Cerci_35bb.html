Alessio Cerci (born July 23, 1987 in Velletri, Rome) is an Italian footballer who plays for Brescia Calcio, on loan from A.S. Roma.

He was a regular for the Roma youth team, but has already played three times for AS Roma's first team (one in 2003/04, two in 2004/05).
