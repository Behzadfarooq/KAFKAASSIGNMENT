[edit] Born and raised

I was under the impression that Alex was born and raised in Vienna, Virginia and moved to LA later in life. He has mentioned his family in Virginia on Diggnation many times, and has said his dad is involved in the Federal Government.


I agree that he has mentioned many times about a portion of his youth in Virginia. Francis Burdett 22:37, 10 February 2006 (UTC)

His dad is actually Dr. Mark Albrecht, the president of the ILS space association. He mentions that near the end in Diggnation episode #57 from 2006-08-03. I would add that to the article, but I have no clue about mother/[brothers|sisters] so I thought this is a good place to stick that. 21:40, 16 August 2006 (IST)

[edit] Alefox

I think the Alefox story does not deserve to take up so much space of the article and the weblink section as it currently does. It was a funny story for the two episodes in which it was mentioned. But since this "skin" for Firefox was not developed by Alex Albrecht himself, this is definitely not a major thing in Alex's life (probably more so in the life of the person who created it). —The preceding unsigned comment was added by 87.174.82.170 (talk) 17:23, 7 April 2007 (UTC).
