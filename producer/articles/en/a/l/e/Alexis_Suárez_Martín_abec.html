José Alejandro Suárez Martín, also known as Alexis (born March 6, 1974 in Las Palmas de Gran Canaria, Canary Islands, Spain) is a Spanish footballer who plays in the centre back position for Levante UD. He has also played for UD Las Palmas and CD Tenerife.
