Silver 2006 Torino Bobsleigh Four man event

Alexandre Zoubkov of Russia is a bobsledder who competed in the bobsleigh events at the 2006 Winter Olympics in Turin, Italy. Zoubkov won silver in the four-person bobsleigh event with teammates Philippe Egorov, Alexei Seliverstov, and Alexey Voevoda.
