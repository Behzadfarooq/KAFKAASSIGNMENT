Alexander Gazsi

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Nailya Zhiganshina and Alexander Gaszi at the free program of the German Championships 2007 in Oberstdorf.

Alexander Gazsi (born October 06, 1984 in Chemnitz, Germany) is a German ice dancer. His father is Hungarian and his mother German. He previously competed with Mandy Kästner, Julia Novikov, and Sandra Gissmann. He teamed up with Nelli Zhiganshina in 2005 and the two are the 2007 German national champions. They train in Moscow and Chemnitz. Their coach is Elena Kustarova. Alexander Gaszi is representing the club SC Berlin.
