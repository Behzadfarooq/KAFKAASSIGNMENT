Alessio Boggiatto (born 18 December 1981 in Moncalieri, Torino) is an Italian Medley swimmer.

Boggiatto is specialized in 400 m medley, he won several European Championship and one World Championship medals. He participated for Italy in the Summer Olympic of Sydney 2000 and Athens 2004 achieving the 4th place twice.

He is brother of Chiara Boggiatto, a world class breaststroke swimmer.
