Alexander Denk

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Alexander Denk is an Austrian American film actor and was the personal bodyguard of Anna Nicole Smith until she died 8 February 2007. Denk also had reported to tabloids that he could be the father of Anna's baby, Dannielynn. He has come forward to claim paternity of Anna Nicole Smith's child.[1].
