[edit] Career(2002-)

Montalvo debuted in the WWC in 2002 and won their Jr. Heavyweight title for the first time over Tommy Diablo on January 18, 2003. Spent the fist few years of his career feuding with Diabolico, Maniac, Brent Dail and others over the Junior Heavyweight title. Left WWC for the IWA in 2005 and won the Tag Team titles with Golden Boy. Returned to WWC and won the Tag Team titles with Chris Joel 2 times and turned heel and feuded with Chris Joel & Fire Blaze. Won the Puerto Rico Heavyweight title from Bronco #1 on June 17, 2006 in Bayamon ,Puerto Rico. Lost the title to Fire Blaze at WWC Anniversary on August 13, 2006 in Bayamon, PR briefly turned babyface helping Fire Blaze in his feud vs Barabaras Jr. & Rico Suave. Recently returned being a heel after he turned on Fire Blaze in a match vs Rico Suave & Barabaras Jr. at WWC Lockout in Bayamon, Puerto Rico on December 16, 2006.
