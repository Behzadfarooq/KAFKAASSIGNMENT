[edit] Welcome

Hello Alex, welcome to Wikipedia.

To answer your question about ToothyWiki, there's no hard and fast rule about what does and doesn't belong on Wikipedia. However, looking at the ToothyWiki Recent Changes, I suspect that most Wikipedians would feel that a wiki with only a couple of dozen changes a day is probably not popular enough for inclusion, unless there is some reason why it's particularly noteworthy (e.g. it's won an award, been in the news, etc.)

That's only my opinion - you could always ask your question on the village pump - you'll find a lot of people there with a better grasp of our relevant policies than me.

I hope you enjoy editing here and being a Wikipedian! -- ALargeElk | Talk 16:25, 15 Jun 2004 (UTC)
