Alejandro Abellan

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Alejandro Abellan (born May 13, 1965, Ottawa) is a Canadian film and television actor.

The son of Spanish immigrants, his first role in the film industry was as Antonio Banderas's stand-in for the film Body Double. Alejandro's first big acting role was as Tito in "Phenomenon", based on the John Travolta movie. Alejandro then was cast as Coach Quintero on the Nickelodeon show Romeo!. Alejandro has also been in a few featured films most recent of which is Gray Matters.
