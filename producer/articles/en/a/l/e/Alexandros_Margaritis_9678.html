Alexandros (Alex) Margaritis (born September 20, 1984 in Bonn, Germany) is a Greek racing driver. He is currently driving with the 'Easy Rent' AMG-Mercedes team in the Deutsche Tourenwagen Masters (DTM) touring car championship. He has dual German and Greek nationality because he was born in Germany to Greek parents.

In 1997, he began his racing career in the Netherlands. He finished second overall in the FSA Cart Championship. In 1999, Margaritis took 4th place in the German Cart Junior Cup and 3 years after that, he participated in the ADAC Formula BMW championship, finishing 14th overall. 2 years later, he finished in 13th place overall in the Formula 3 Euroseries.
