Alexis Bertin

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Alexis Bertin (born May 13, 1980) is a professional footballer who currently plays for League 1 side Brighton & Hove Albion. Bertin joined the Seagulls on January 31, 2007, the last day of the transfer window from French side Le Havre AC.

Bertin plays usually as a central midfielder.
