Alexander Niver Polinsky (born October 7, 1974) is an American actor, voice actor, and singer who is also known as Alexander Niver, Alexander Niver Polinsky, and Alex Polinsky. Since debuting as a teenager on Charles in Charge, he has made TV, voice, and movie appearances, as well as working as a mold-maker at a movie prop design studio. He has also been linked to Scientology. He currently works with the Walt Disney Company.

Among the songs he recorded while playing Vance Michael Justin on W.I.T.C.H. are Feel the Beat, Fantasy Love, and Don't Wanna Say Goodbye.
