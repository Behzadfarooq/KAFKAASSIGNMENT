Alex Meechan (born January 29, 1980 in Plymouth) is an English footballer with Chester City.

He was previously in his third spell with Forest Green Rovers, having rejoined from Conference rivals Halifax Town in July 2005. His other previous clubs include Yeovil, Bristol City, Leigh RMI, Swindon and Dagenham & Redbridge. He is 5 foot 8 inches tall.

He left Forest Green to join Chester City in January 2007 until the end of the season[1].
