Alexis LeVan (born August 18, 1986) is a beauty queen from Sioux Falls, South Dakota who has competed in the Miss USA pageant.

LeVan won the Miss South Dakota USA 2006 title in a state pageant held in Watertown, South Carolina in late 2005. This was her first attempt at the title, and she was crowned by outgoing titleholder Jessica Fjerstad of Madison.

LeVan represented South Dakota in the Miss USA 2006 pageant broadcast live from Baltimore, Maryland on April 21, 2006. She did not place in the nationally televised pageant, which was won by Tara Conner of Kentucky.

LeVan graduated from Roosevelt High School in 2004 and is currently attending the University of South Dakota. She was on the Dean's List in 2004 and 2005 and is a member of the National Forensic League.

Her other hobbies and interests include jogging, bike riding, snowshoeing, hiking, decorating and shopping.
