I am a Sr. Software Developer for a major US Corporation. I have been awarded as Microsoft MVP - Visual FoxPro every year continuously since 2001 for contributions to the community in English, Spanish and Portuguese language newsgroups.

I am a Pastafarian, or more seriously an Atheist Jew. This is not a contradiction in terms as you are born from a Jewish mother, therefore always a Jew. Being Jewish is not only a religious choice but a being descendant from a common people, with a common history. I very much idenify myself with that history and ancestry, not with a choice of one magical deity over another.

I am a Skeptic a Secular Humanist and a FreeThinker. I am for Freedom of Expression and Freedom of the Press. I am against any type of censorship or coercion. I am for Separation of Church and State.
