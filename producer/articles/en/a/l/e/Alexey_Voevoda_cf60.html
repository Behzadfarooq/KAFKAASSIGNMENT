Alexey Voevoda (born May 9, 1980, Sochi) of Russia is a bobsledder who competed in the bobsleigh events at the 2006 Winter Olympics in Turin, Italy. Voevoda won silver in the four-person bobsleigh event with teammates Philippe Egorov, Alexei Seliverstov, and Alexandre Zoubkov. He is also a world champion in armwrestling.
