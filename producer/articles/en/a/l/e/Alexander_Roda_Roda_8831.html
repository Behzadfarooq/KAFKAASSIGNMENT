Alexander Roda Roda

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Alexander Roda Roda (April 13, 1872 - August 20, 1945) was the pen name of Alexander/Sándor Friedrich Ladislaus Rosenfeld, an Austrian Jewish writer.

Rosenfeld was born either in Drnowitz/Drnovice in Moravia or in Zdenci, Slavonia (near Virovitica). He died in New York.

[edit] Works

  • 1892 - "Der Gutsherr von Ljublin"
  • 1906 - "Eines Esels Kinnbacken"
  • 1913 - "500(Fünfhundert) Schwänke"
  • 1925 - "Roda Rodas Roman"
