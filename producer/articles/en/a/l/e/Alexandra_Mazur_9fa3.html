Alexandra Mazur

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Alexandra Mazur (born in 1986 in Moscow) was elected Miss Russian Beauty in July 2006, while competing with 44 other contestants from various regions of Russia. During the pageant, she represented her home city of Moscow. [1]

Mazur is one of the ten children in her family. She lives in Moscow and studies law at Moscow State Law Academy. She competed for Russia at the Miss World 2006 pageant in Warsaw, Poland on September 30.
