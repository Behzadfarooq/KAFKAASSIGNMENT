Alec Medlock

Alec Hamilton Medlock (born March 13, 1990 in Torrance, California) is an American child actor. He has numerously guest starred on quite a few shows and has a recurring role as Craig on Drake & Josh.

[edit] Filmography

  • Dinotopia: Quest for the Ruby Sunstone (2005) (voice)
  • Arnold Schwarzenegger: A VH1 Popumentary (2004) (TV)
  • The Land Before Time X: The Great Longneck Migration (2003) (V)
  • The Long Ride Home (2003)
  • The Core (2003) (voice) (uncredited)
  • The United States of Leland (2003)
  • Hilfe, ich bin ein Junge (2002) (voice)
  • Dennis (2001)
  • Les Soldats (2000)

[edit] Recurring role

  • Drake & Josh (2004-) as Craig (TV)
