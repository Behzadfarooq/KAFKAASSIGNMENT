[edit] Race Reports

  • 1969 Italian Grand Prix - 1st to 4th seperated by 0.19 seconds.
  • 1971 Italian Grand Prix - Fastest Grand Prix ever and closest finish...ever.
  • 1973 British Grand Prix - Big accident at the start of the race
  • 1976 German Grand Prix - Lauda's near fatal accident.
  • 1976 Japanese Grand Prix - James Hunt wins the world title...while title rival Lauda retires due to the conditions. Also I think the first GP televised live by the BBC.
  • 1977 South African Grand Prix - Niki Lauda's first win since his near fatal accident at the Nurburgring, the race was marred by the horrific death of Brit Tom Pryce.
  • 1983 United States Grand Prix West - John Watson won the race...from 22nd on the grid.
  • 1984 Monaco Grand Prix - Contraversial finish, with great drivers by Senna and Bellof
  • 1985 Portuguese Grand Prix - Senna's first win in Wet Weather of course.
  • 1991 Canadian Grand Prix - Mansell was on course to win the Grand Prix and after exiting the Pit Lane hairpin on the last lap, he started waving to the crowd and then...his engine stalled. Leaving Nelson Piquet's Benetton to win the race.
  • 1993 European Grand Prix - Senna's fantastic drive around Donington Park
  • 1994 San Marino Grand Prix - F1's blackest weekend
  • 1994 Australian Grand Prix - Controversial collision between Schumacher and Hill
  • 1996 Monaco Grand Prix - Fewest cars to finish an F1 race
  • 1997 European Grand Prix - Controversial collision between Schumacher and Villeneuve
  • 1998 British Grand Prix - Race won in the pits by Schumacher
  • 1998 Belgian Grand Prix - That crash, Schumacher/Coulthard Collision, other incidents
  • 2002 Austrian Grand Prix - Schumacher/Barrichello switch on the finish line, Sato/Heidfeld collision
  • 2003 Brazilian Grand Prix - All the crashes, and the incorrect driver being crowned winner initially.
  • 2005 San Marino Grand Prix - BAR fuel tank controversy
  • 2005 United States Grand Prix - 6 Car Race
  • 2006 Monaco Grand Prix - Schumacher's Parking incident during qualifying,
  • 2006 Brazilian Grand Prix - Schumacher's last race and overtakes, Alonso WDC, Massa's win (and flag recieve on slowdown lap)
