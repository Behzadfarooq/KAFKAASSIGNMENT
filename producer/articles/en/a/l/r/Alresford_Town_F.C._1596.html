Alresford Town F.C.

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Alresford Town F.C. is a football club based in New Alresford, Hampshire, England. They joined the Hampshire League Division Two in 1990. For the 2006-07 season, they are members of the Wessex League Division One.
