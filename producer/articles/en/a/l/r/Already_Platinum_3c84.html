Already Platinum is an album by American rapper Slim Thug released in June 2005. It was released on Pharrell Williams and Chad Hugo's Star Trak label. The original release date was slated for February 2005. But Heavy bootlegging and numerous delays held the album back, forcing a vast majority of its original track listing to be replaced. The original track listing included the original "I Ain't Heard Of That" track which featured Jay-Z.
