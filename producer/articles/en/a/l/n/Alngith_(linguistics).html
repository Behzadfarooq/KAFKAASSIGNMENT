Alngith is a moribund extinct Australian Aboriginal dialect formerly spoken on Cape York Peninsula, Queensland, Australia.

[edit] Classification

Ethnologue (2006) treats Alngith as a distinct language. R. M. W. Dixon (2002) regards it as a dialect of Anguthimri.
