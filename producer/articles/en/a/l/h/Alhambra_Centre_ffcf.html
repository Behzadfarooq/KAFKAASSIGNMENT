The The Mall Barnsley is Barnsley's main shopping complex, housing 41 shops and adjacent to Barnsley Market. At various points during the year, it plays host to the visiting German Market which also runs through the main shopping streets and is very popular in the run up to the festive season.

It is owned and operated by The Mall Company, a large company that operates shopping centres throughout the UK.

Most popular community activity is the Mall Monster - the pink monster usually inhabited by 'Taz', the bubbly security officer/CCO, who frequents the children's ward of Barnsley General Hospital each year to hand out Easter Eggs and Christmas presents to sick children.
