Alhassane Keita (born June 26, 1983) is a Guinean football player who, as of 2004 was playing for FC Zurich. Currently, Keita is playing for the Saudi Arabian club Al-Ittihad. He left as the Axpo Superleague's top scorer for the 2005-2006 season with 20 Goals. He was part of the team 2004 African Nations Cup team, who finished second in their group in the first round of competition, before losing in the quarter finals to Mali. Keita is also known for his fast sprint speed, because of that he scores many extreme goals.
