Alan West (musician)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Alan West is a former British singer. He was a friend of Barry Thompson who asked him to join Bolt Thrower in 1986. He recorded two demos with the band and a session with John Peel. He left in 1988 prior to the recording of the debut album due to personal reasons. He is now working as an engineer.
