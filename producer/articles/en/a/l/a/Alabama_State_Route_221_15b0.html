State Route 221 or S.R. 221 is a 9 mile long route that serves as a western bypass of Camden in Wilcox County.

[edit] Route description

The southern terminus of SR 221 is located at its intersection with State Route 41 to the southwest of Camden. From this point, the route travels in a northward direction before terminating at State Route 28 northwest of Camden at Canton Bend. Of note along the route is the Camden Municipal Airport located along the route at its junction with State Route 10.
