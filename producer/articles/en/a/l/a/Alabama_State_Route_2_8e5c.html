State Route 2, a state highway in Alabama, is the unsigned partner of U.S. Route 72 throughout the state. The eastern terminus of SR 2 is at the Mississippi state line west of Cherokee. The northwestern terminus of SR 2 is at the Tennessee state line north of Bridgeport.

[edit] Cities and Towns

SR 2 and US 72 pass through the following cities and towns in Alabama:

  • Cherokee
  • Barton
  • Tuscumbia
  • Muscle Shoals
  • Sheffield
  • Florence
  • Killen
  • Center Star
  • Elgin
  • Rogersville
  • Madison
  • Huntsville
  • Ryland
  • Gurley
  • Paint Rock
  • Scottsboro

[edit] Junctions

SR 2 and US 72 intersect with the following major junctions in Alabama:

  • Natchez Trace Parkway west of Cherokee
  • U.S. Route 43, State Route 13, and State Route 17 at Muscle Shoals. These routes overlap until they split west of Killen, except for State Route 17 which splits at Florence.
  • U.S. Route 72 Alternate and State Route 20 at Muscle Shoals. State Route 20 overlaps until it splits at Florence.
  • State Route 101 at Elgin
  • U.S. Route 31 and unsigned State Route 3 south of Athens
  • Interstate 65 south of Athens
  • State Route 255 at Huntsville
  • State Route 53 at Huntsville
  • U.S. Route 231, U.S. Route 431 and unsigned State Route 1 at Huntsville. These routes overlap from University Drive to Sparkman Drive.
  • Interstate 565 and U.S. Route 72 Alternate at Huntsville
  • State Route 79 southwest from Scottsboro
  • State Route 35 at Scottsboro, Alabama
