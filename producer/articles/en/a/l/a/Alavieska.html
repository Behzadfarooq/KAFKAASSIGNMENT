Alavieska is a municipality of Finland.

It is located in the province of Oulu and is part of the Northern Ostrobothnia region. The municipality has a population of 2,902 and covers an area of 253.01 km² of which 1.62 km² is water. The population density is 11.5 inhabitants per km².

The municipality is unilingually Finnish.
