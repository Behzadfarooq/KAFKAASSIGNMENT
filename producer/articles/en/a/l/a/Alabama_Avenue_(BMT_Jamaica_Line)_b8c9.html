Alabama Avenue is a rehabilitated skip-stop station on the New York City Subway's BMT Jamaica Line. The J serves this station at all times while the Z bypasses the station, although it stops here sometimes for access to the East New York Yard, located just to the south. The mezzanine under the platform is metal and has a wooden floor. The roof canopy is flat, suggesting that there was planned express track as the flat top would be used for support.. All that remains or was built is a trackway which rises east (north on the line) of Broadway Junction and ends at the west (south on the line) end of this station. The station was renovated in early 2006.
