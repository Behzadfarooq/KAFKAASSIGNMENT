Alain Rochat (born on February 1, 1983 in St.-Jean-sur-Richelieu, Quebec, Canada) is a Swiss footballer. He plays in defence and is currently on loan to FC Zürich from French club Stade Rennais FC. Before he played for BSC Young Boys and Yverdon Sport FC. In 2005 he made his only appearance so far for the Swiss national team.
