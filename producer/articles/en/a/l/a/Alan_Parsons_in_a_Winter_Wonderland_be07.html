Cover of Alan Parsons In A Winter Wonderland

A promotional single released by the band Grandaddy in 2000. Though not released on any official Grandaddy albums it can be found on The Windfall Varietal bootleg and on the XFM benefit compilation It's a Cool, Cool Christmas. This track proved to be a legal problem on the former of the two, since the band did not have the rights to distribute it.[citation needed]
