Alarming Echo Beats

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Alarming Echo Beats is a sub-division of Fecal-Matter Discorporated run by the self described satanist Reverend Samekh. It caters more to black metal, noise music, industrial music, power electronics noise , and dark ambient types of music dealing with Satanism and the Occult.
