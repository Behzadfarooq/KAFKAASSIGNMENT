Alan Reeves (born 19 November 1967 in Birkenhead) is a former English soccer player. He is currently assistant manager of Brentford F.C..

He was known primarily as a reliable lower division centre half, although he did have premier league experience with Wimbledon. He played league football for Gillingham, Chester City, Rochdale, Wimbledon and Swindon Town, where he played out his career before moving into dual role as player coach before becoming assistant manager.

He is currently helping out former Wimbledon central defensive team mate Scott Fitzgerald in coaching at Brentford.
