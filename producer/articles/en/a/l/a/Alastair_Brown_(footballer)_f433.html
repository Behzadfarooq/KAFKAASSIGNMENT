Alastair Hugh Brown (born 12 December 1985 in Irvine, North Ayrshire) is a goalkeeper on the books of Hibernian F.C. He spent the 2005-2006 season out on loan to Raith Rovers in order to gain experience from playing in first team games. Ally has represented Scotland at youth levels.
