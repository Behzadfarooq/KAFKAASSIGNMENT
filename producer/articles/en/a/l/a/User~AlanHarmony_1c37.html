Alan Harmony is a published author of 7 non-fiction works and instigator of Epiphanies Magazine. The subjects include peace, fascism and democracy; the practical uses of dreams and remote viewing; an annotation of Orwell's dream based "1984" from the standpoint of prophecies within it which include serious threats about an atomic war (84 suitcase sized tactical nukes went missing during the collapse of the Soviet Union); an annotation of the Book of Enoch as its prophecies relate to the End Time; plus another about prophecies of the 'End Time' from a spiritual but non-religious approach. His areas of specific knowledge include medical and health related subjects as well as some areas of technology.

He was born in Vancouver BC, November 8, 1943. He resides in the Greater Vancouver area.
