Alabama State Route 195 is a 42.4 mile long highway running mainly in a north and south direction through two counties, Winston and Walker.

[edit] Route description

The northern terminus is on State Highway 13 in Haleyville The southern terminus is on Alabama State Route 5 in Jasper.

The first leg of SR-195 (17.6 miles long) runs concurrently with U.S. Highway 278 in Double Springs for approximately two-tenths of a mile before coming to the second leg of SR-195 (24.8 miles long).

[edit] Communities along the route

  • Haleyville (Winston County)
  • Ashridge, Alabama (Winston County)
  • Double Springs (Winston County)
  • Poplar Springs (Winston County)
  • Manchester, Alabama (Walker County)
  • Jasper (Walker County)
