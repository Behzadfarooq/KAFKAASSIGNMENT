Alaska Avalanche

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Alaska Avalanche are a Junior A hockey team in the North American Hockey League's south division, and play out of Wasilla, Alaska. The franchise was originally called the Central Texas Blackhawks, and in 2004 moved to Springfield, Missouri as the Springfield Spirit. In 2005, they made their most recent move to Alaska as the Wasilla Spirit. The Avalanche play out of the Wasilla Multi-Use Sports Complex.
