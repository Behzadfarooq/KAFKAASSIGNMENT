Alabama State Route 144 is an east-west route in east Alabama that runs from U.S. Route 231 in St. Clair County near Pell City to U.S. Route 431 at Alexandria in Calhoun County. The highway is two lanes for its entire length.

[edit] Major Junctions

  • Alabama State Route 77 in Ohatchee, Alabama
  • U.S. Route 431 in Alexandria, Alabama

[edit] Other Information

Until 1997, the section of S.R. 144 between Alabama State Route 77 and U.S. Route 431, was designated as Alabama State Route 62. State Route 62 is now assigned to a short route in Marshall County.
