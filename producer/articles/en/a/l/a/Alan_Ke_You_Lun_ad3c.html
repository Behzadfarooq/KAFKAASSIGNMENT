Alan Ke You Lun (Traditional Chinese: 柯有倫; pinyin: Kē Yǒulún), also known as Alan Kuo or Kuo You Lun, is a Taiwanese singer and actor. Ke is the son of the late Taiwanese stuntman/actor, Blackie Ke Shou Liang.
