Alan Moore's Yuggoth Cultures and Other Growths

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Alan Moore's Yuggoth Cultures and Other Growths is a collection of some of Alan Moore's previously unpublished work, as well as adaptations of his performance work by Antony Johnston. It was released in three parts by Avatar Press.

[edit] Publication

It was published in 3 issues and collected as a trade paperback.

[edit] Comics

It was originally published as a 3 part black and white monthly comic:

  • Issue #1. Published September 2003, containing:
    • Nightjar (with Bryan Talbot, scheduled to appear in Warrior)
    • Zaman's Hill (with Juan Jose Ryp, an adaptation of part of a lost novel, Yugguth Cultures, which gave this series its name)
  • Issue #2. Published October 2003, containing:
    • Itchy Peterson (with Val Semieks)
    • A short story drawn by Mike Wolfer
    • Another adaptation from Yugguth Cultures drawn by Jacen Burrows
  • Issue #3. Published November 2003, containing:
    • I Keep Coming Back (with Oscar Zarate, described as a coda to From Hell)
    • An adaptation by Antony Johnston and drawn by Marat Mychaels
    • An interview with Alan Moore about the series.

[edit] Trade paperback

The issues were collected into a trade paperback:

Yuggoth Cultures (Avatar, 196 pages, 2006 paperback ISBN 1-59291-026-2, hardback ISBN 1-59291-027-0)

[edit] Novel

The unpublished novel appears on a number of listings:

Yuggoth Cultures (Creation Books, 256 pages, 1997 ISBN 1-871592-31-3)

[edit] Trivia

The pages of the lost novel were left on the seat of a cab as Moore says "Unfortunately, when the texts were about half completed, I left the only copy of three or four of them in a London taxi cab. Since it was impossible to reconstruct, the work went on hold, remaining incomplete." [1]

Alan Moore's The Courtyard was originally scheduled for appearances in this collection but was turned into its own separate series.

Antony Johnston would later produce more work in the Cthulhu Mythos, for Avatar, under the title Yuggoth Creatures [2]
