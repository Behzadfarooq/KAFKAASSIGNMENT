Alaras Sharpthorn-Stormrage was born almost right after the World Tree was destroyed by Archimonde. Malfurion Stormrage and Tyrande Whisperwind are the mother and father of Alaras. When he was about three years old, Alaras was taken from his birth place of Darnassus by Satyrs to Felwood in Kalimdor, for revenge on Malfurion. About when the Satyrs were going to kill Alaras, a very wise Moonkin Gar'thin Sharpthorn, rescued the baby Night Elf from the corrupt Satyr Hovel. The wise Moonkin took the child to the Druid town of Moonglade, where he trained and raised Alaras to be a Druid. Alaras lived there for 56 years, after until he left to go find his true parents. He went back to the town of Darnassus and found his true mother. After that, Alaras went to go help cleanse The World of Warcraft of evil that still lurks in it. Alaras takes no sides, he is at peace with Horde and Alliance.

Alaras killed Van'Cleef of the deadmines. He helped Rescue surviving Gnomes in Gnomergan, he helped with the cleansing of Zul'Farak and Stratholme, and helped protect Warsong Gulch.

Alaras is bent on finding the staff of Aetish, the Staff known to be used by medivh, barley yet close to discovereing the staff, Alaras still lives on to finding clues.
