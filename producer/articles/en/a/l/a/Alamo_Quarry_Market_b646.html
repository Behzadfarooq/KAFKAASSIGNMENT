The Alamo Quarry Market is an open-air shopping mall located in the Alamo Heights and Olmos Park areas. It used to be a cement factory until it was abandoned. Since then it has turned into a thriving market with a Regal Cinema theater dominating the oldest building and is surrounded by other businesses including bookstores and many restaurants. The Quarry still maintains the facade of the cement factory and one of it's notable features are the several smokestacks that still exist. A popular feature of the Quarry are the life sized, brightly colored metal cows that are scattered throughout the market place.
