Alan Comer is a professional Magic: The Gathering player. He was elected to the Magic: The Gathering Pro Tour Hall of Fame as part of the inaugural class in 2005.[5]

[edit] Accomplishments

  • Magic: The Gathering Hall of Famer
  • Top 8 at Worlds (1998)
  • Top 8 at Pro Tour-Los Angeles (1997)
  • Top 8 at Pro Tour-Washington D.C. (1999)
  • Top 8 at Pro Tour-Chicago (1999)
  • Top 8 at Grand Prix-San Francisco (1999)
  • Top 8 at Pro Tour-Barcelona (2001)
  • Top 8 at Grand Prix-Boston (2001)
  • 9th at Grand Prix-Las Vegas (2001)[5]

[edit] Trivia

  • Alan was the only 2005 Hall of Famer to be inducted without a Pro Tour victory.
  • He worked for Wizards of the Coast in its Magic Online division.
  • He was also the only 2005 Hall of Famer who was not eligible to play Magic professionally at the time of voting. This was due to his position at Wizards of the Coast.
