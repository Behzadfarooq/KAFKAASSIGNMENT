Alabama State Route 57 is the unsigned partner route assigned to U.S. Route 45 in rural Washington County. The southern terminus of the route is at the junction where State Route 17 diverts from U.S. 45 near Deer Park, a small community in southern Washington County. The northern terminus of the route is at the Mississippi state line near Yellow Pine, another small community in Washington County. In Mississippi, the route continues as U.S. 45.
