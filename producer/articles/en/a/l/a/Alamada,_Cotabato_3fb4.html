Alamada, Cotabato

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

You have new messages (last change).

Alamada is a 2nd class municipality in the province of Cotabato, Philippines. According to the 2000 census, it has a population of 44,303 people in 8,412 households.

The town is named after the local chieftain, Datu Alamada.

[edit] Barangays

Alamada is politically subdivided into 17 barangays.

  • Bao
  • Barangiran
  • Camansi
  • Dado
  • Guiling
  • Kitacubong (Pob.)
  • Macabasa
  • Malitubog
  • Mapurok
  • Pacao
  • Paruayan
  • Pigcawaran
  • Rangayen
  • Lower Dado
  • Mirasol
  • Raradangan
