Alan Comrie (born November 5, 1987) is a Scottish footballer. He is a midfielder who has played for Alloa Athletic since 2003.

Comrie, who was a part of the youth system at Falkirk, moved to Alloa in August 2003, playing for three years as a member of the youth team. He was awarded a first-team contract at the beginning of the 2006-07 season.

Comrie's debut performance came in the League Cup victory against Forfar Athletic. A regular starter in the first half of the season, his lack of firepower in front of goal has not seen him appear for the team so far this year, while most of his league appearances have come as second-half substitutes for tired or resting players.

Comrie is 5'9" tall and weighs 11 stone.
