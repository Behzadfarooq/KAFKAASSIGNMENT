[edit] Episode I: Rebel Yell

[edit] Introduction

In the decade since the end of the Guild Wars, the Terran Confederacy has stood unchallenged in its supremacy over colonized space.

All of that changed four days ago when a large alien fleet emerged from warp-space and lade waste to the unsuspecting Confederate colony of Chau Sara.

The Confederacy, fearing that the alien fleet's next target will be the colony of Mar Sara, has sent you to intervene. Acting as Magistrate, you are to safeguard the colonists and keep their state of panic from escalating any further.

[edit] Wasteland:

Office of the Colonial Magistrate, Mar Sara Colony

[edit] Briefing:

You are informed that your colony will be put under lockdown, and you must relocate your core colonists. The local Marshal, James Raynor, has agreed to meet you en route and escort you.

[edit] Objectives:

  • Build a Barracks.
  • Train 10 Marines.
  • Raynor must survive.

[edit] In-game story:

None.

[edit] Backwater Station:

Office of the Colonial Magistrate, Mar Sara Colony

[edit] Briefing:

You are informed by Raynor that the refugees are doing fine. The Adjucant then alerts you and Confederate high command about unknown alien orginisims attacking Backwater Station. General Duke tells you that he knows of and will take car of the problem. Raynor says the Confederates will never get there on time, to trust him and get milita to rescue them now.

[edit] Objectives:

  • Eradicate the alien infestation.
  • Raynor must survive.

[edit] In-game story:

Raynor destroies an infested command center. This prompts Duke to tell Raynor he has violated law and is under arrest. Duke then tells Raynor to put down his weapons and surrender. Raynor replies that the colony would have been overrun if they hadn't destroyed it. Duke claims that does not matter, and tells Raynor to surrender again.
