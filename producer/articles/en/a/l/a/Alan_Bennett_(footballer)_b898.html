Alan Bennett (born 4 October 1981 in Cork, Republic of Ireland) is an Irish footballer who currently plays in central defence for English Premier League side Reading.

Bennett joined Cork City from local club Richmond. He initially played in midfield, but during the 2004 season changed to centre-half. He made more than 100 appearances for the Rebel Army, scoring nine goals. Following several bids,[1] Bennett joined Reading on a two and a half year contract for an undisclosed fee on 30 January 2007.[2]
