Alan Ladd, Jr. (born October 22, 1937 in Los Angeles, California) is an American film industry executive and producer. He is famous for giving George Lucas the go-ahead to make Star Wars. He also produced Blade Runner. He is the son of late actor Alan Ladd. He was also the chairman of The Ladd Company.
