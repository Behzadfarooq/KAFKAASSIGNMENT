Alain Caron (ice hockey)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

You have new messages (last change).

Alain Caron ( born April 27, 1938 - died December 19, 1986) was a professional ice hockey player who played in various leagues from 1956 to 1976. Born in Dolbeau, Quebec, he played in the NHL for 60 games while playing for the Oakland Seals and Montreal Canadiens.
