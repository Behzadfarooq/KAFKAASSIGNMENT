[edit] Ballantine, Montana

A basic community page done with a program or script called MapIt.

I added a History section, which led me to work on Worden, Montana and Huntley, Montana. I also started a Huntley Project article, only the second Wikipedia article about a United States Bureau of Reclamation irrigation project. Also one on the community of Pompeys Pillar, Montana.

[edit] Chebyshev's inequality

A concept in probability theory, to be distinguished from Chebyshev's sum inequality, which is not the same thing at all. The article started out as Tchebysheff's theorem.
