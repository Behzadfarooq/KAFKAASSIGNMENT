[edit] Not yet in use

This image is not yet in use. My initial research into Wikipedia's guidelines lead me to believe that this may be fair use as a publicity photograph. Unfortunately having actually uploaded the image and gotten linked to guidelines I was previously unfamiliar with, I am no longer certain that this is the case. In the interest of eliminating any possibility of uncertainty, I have asked Baker if he is willing to license the photograph under one of the Wikipedia acceptable licenses (GFDL or CC-BY-SA-2.5) and am awaiting a response. — Alan De Smet | Talk 04:05, 28 March 2007 (UTC)

[edit] Summary

Photograph of Keith Baker, taken by his wife. Provided to me by Baker himself upon my request. Baker specifically granted Wikipedia the ability to use the image, however it's also fair use as a publicity photo for identification (See also Wikipedia:Publicity photos).
