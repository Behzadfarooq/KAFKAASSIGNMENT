Admiral Alain Oudot de Dainville (15 March 1947 in Marsat (Puy de Dôme)) is a French Navy officer. As of 2006, he is head of the general staff of the French Navy.

[edit] Career

A former naval pilot, Oudot de Dainville was the commander of the Clemenceau from 1993 to 1995.

Between 1995 and 1997, he directed the nuclear powered Charles De Gaulle aircraft carrier project. Since 1999, he held general staff position. He was appointed head of the general staff in June 2005.

[edit] Honours

  • Commander of the Légion d'Honneur
  • Commander of the Ordre National du Mérite
