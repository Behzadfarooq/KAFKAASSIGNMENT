Alah Valley

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Alah Valley is located at the coordinates 6°27′40″N, 124°40′8″E in the province of South Cotabato on the island of Mindanao, Philippines.

[edit] Economy

Large fish cages similar to these are constructed in Lake Sebu

The economy is based on agriculture producing mostly copra from vast coconut plantations. Another major source of income is aquaculture of Tilapia in the lake of Lake Sebu.

[edit] People

It is home to the indigenous T'bolis, Tirurays, Ubos and Manobos.

[edit] Geography

It is a well populated valley with fertile soil. The placid lake of Lake Sebu can be found in Alah Valley.
