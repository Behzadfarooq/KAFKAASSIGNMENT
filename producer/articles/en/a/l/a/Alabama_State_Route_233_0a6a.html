State Route 233 or SR 233 is a 17 mile long route that serves as a connection between State Route 129 at Glen Allen in Fayette County with U.S. Route 278 west of Natural Bridge in Marion County.

[edit] Route description

The southern terminus of SR 233 is located at its intersection with State Route 129 on the Fayette County side of Glen Allen. From the terminus, it takes a winding northeasterly route to U.S. Route 78. After crossing U.S. Highway 78, it takes a northerly track to its northern terminus at U.S. Route 278.
