Alain Sarde

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Alain Sarde is a French film producer and actor who was born on the 28 March 1952 in Boulogne-Billancourt. One of his films, Mulholland Dr. received the Online Film Critics Society Award for Best Picture. The Pianist was nominated for the Academy Award for Best Picture and the BAFTA Award for Best Film. Sarde's credits include:

  • Speak to Me of Love
  • Les roseaux sauvages
  • Belphégor - Le fantôme du Louvre
  • The Pianist
  • Oliver Twist
  • Sole Sisters
  • Prénom Carmen
  • Ponette
  • Life Is a Miracle
  • L'Appât
  • Nelly et Monsieur Arnaud
  • Water Drops on Burning Rocks
  • Place Vendôme
  • Barocco
  • Alice and Martin
  • Les Enfants du Siècle
  • Décalage Horaire
  • Notre musique
  • Chaos
