State Route 285 or SR 285 is a .266 mile long route that serves as a connection between U.S. Route 431 at Lakepoint Resort State Park to State Route 165 north of Wylaunee in Barbour County.

The northern terminus of S.R. 285 is located at State Route 165 north of Wylaunee. The route then takes a generally southwesterly track to its southern terminus at U.S. Route 431 at the Lakepoint Resort State Park. Its entire routing is also signed as 1st Avenue.
