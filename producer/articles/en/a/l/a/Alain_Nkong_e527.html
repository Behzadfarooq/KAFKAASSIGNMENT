Alain Nkong

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Alain Nkong (born April 6, 1979 in Yaounde) is an Cameroonian soccer midfielder who last played for the Colorado Rapids of Major League Soccer

Prior to signing with the Rapids in 2005, Nkong played all over the world, including Las Palmas (Spain), Nacional (Uruguay), and FC Paços de Ferreira (Portugal). He has represented Cameroon on various youth levels, and had appeared with the full national team in qualifiers for the 2002 FIFA World Cup.

Nkong signed with the Rapids prior to the 2005 MLS season. Despite having a successful season he was released in November 2005. He returned to Colorado in August 2006, but was again waived at the end of the season.
