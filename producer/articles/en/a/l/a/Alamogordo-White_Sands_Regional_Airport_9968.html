Alamogordo-White Sands Regional Airport (IATA: ALM, ICAO: KALM), formerly known as Alamogordo Municipal Airport, is a public airport located four miles (6 km) southwest of the central business district (CBD) of Alamogordo, a city in Otero County, New Mexico, USA. The airport covers 1,450 acres and has two runways. It is served by one commercial airline, with service subsidized by the Essential Air Service program.

Air Midwest, operating as Mesa Airlines, commenced service on September 24, 2006, with flights to Albuquerque International Sunport. One of the flights includes a stop at Roswell International Air Center.[1]
