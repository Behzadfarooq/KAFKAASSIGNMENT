Actual Alan Myers

Alan Myers was the third drummer of the band Devo from 1976 until sometime after the album Shout (Devo album). He joined to replace Jim Mothersbaugh and left at some point between 1986 and 1987, unwilling to become obsolete in the shadow of a drum machine. He was replaced by David Kendrick of Sparks. Among all of Devo's drummers, he is perhaps the one most associated with the band and probably the most popular among Devo fans. After he left Devo he recorded a demo with Babooshka, a band that was his wife's brain child. However, even this demo uses a drum machine. Alan is supposedly living in California somewhere with his family and working as an electrician.
