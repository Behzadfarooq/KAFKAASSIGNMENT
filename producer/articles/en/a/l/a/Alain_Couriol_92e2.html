Alain Couriol (born 24 October 1958 in Paris) is a former football striker from France, who earned 12 caps and scored 2 goals for the France national football team, including playing in the 1982 FIFA World Cup where France finished fourth. During his career, he played for Inf Vichy (1978-1979), AS Monaco (1979-1983), Paris Saint-Germain (1983-1989), Toulon FC (1989-1990), Saint Denis Saint Leu (1990-1991).
