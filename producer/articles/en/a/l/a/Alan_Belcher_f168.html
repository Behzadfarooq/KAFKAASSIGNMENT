Alan "The Talent" Belcher is an American Mixed Martial Arts fighter from Biloxi, Mississippi by way of Jonesboro, Arkansas. He currently fights at middleweight for the Ultimate Fighting Championship.

[edit] Career

Making his pro debut in July of 2004 against Tim Ellis in Freestyle Fighting Championship and winning by TKO, Belcher really didn't get started in his MMA career until early 2006. Belcher amassed 9 fights, going 8-1. His only loss in 2006 came at the hands of Yushin Okami, a skilled Japanese fighter. The Talent took the fight on 2 weeks notice after David Terrell pulled out citing a sinus infection.

The young upstart wouldn't stay down for long, making his second UFC appearance against Jorge Santiago just 3 months later. The fight was competitive for the most part, but The Talent pulled away in the 3rd round when he started landing devestating leg kicks, which eventually set up the knock out via right high kick. Belcher followed up with 2 punches to his fallen foe for good measure.

On April 7th, 2006, Belcher will be taking on The Ultimate Fighter 3 middleweight winner Kendall Grove.
