Alastair John Lyndhurst Bruce, 5th Baron Aberdare (b. May 2, 1947) is the son of Morys George Lyndhurst Bruce, 4th Baron Aberdare and Maud Helen Sarah Dashwood. The 5th Baron succeeded his father upon the latter's death in 2005.

Lord Aberdare was educated at Eton and Christ Church, Oxford. He married Elizabeth Mary Culbert in 1971 and has two children:

  • The Honourable Hector Morys Napier Bruce b. 1974
  • The Honourable Sarah Katherine Mary Bruce b. 1976
