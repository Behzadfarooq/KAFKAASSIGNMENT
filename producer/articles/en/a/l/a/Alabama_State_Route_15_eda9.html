State Route 15 is the unsigned partner route assigned to U.S. Highway 29 throughout Alabama. S.R. 15 begins at the Florida state line near Flomaton in southern Escambia County. The northern terminus of S.R. 15 is at the Georgia state line at Lanett in Chambers County.

U.S. Highway 29 and S.R. 15 traverse Alabama in a general northeast/southwest slope. It has never been a major route in the state; its significance was completely overshadowed with the completion of Interstate 65 and Interstate 85 during the 1970’s. Today, U.S. 29 and S.R. 15 serve primarily to connect numerous smaller towns and cities in the southwest, south-central, and eastern parts of Alabama.

S.R. 15 and U.S. 29 pass through the following Alabama counties:

  • Escambia County
  • Covington County
  • Crenshaw County
  • Pike County
  • Bullock County
  • Macon County
  • Lee County
  • Chambers County

Cities and towns along the route of S.R. 15 and U.S. 29 in Alabama include:

  • Flomaton
  • Brewton
  • Andalusia
  • Brantley
  • Troy
  • Union Springs
  • Tuskegee
  • Auburn
  • Opelika
  • Valley
  • Lanett

U.S. 29 and S.R. 15 intersect the following Interstate highways, U.S. highways, and major state routes in Alabama:

  • U.S. Highway 31 and unsigned State Route 3 at Flomaton. U.S. 29, U.S. 31, S.R. 3 and S.R. 15 are co-routed until they split at Brewton
  • U.S. Highway 84 and unsigned State Route 12 at Andalusia. U.S. 29, U.S. 84, S.R. 12 and S.R. 15 are co-routed as they pass through the city.
  • U.S. Highway 331 and unsigned State Route 9 at Brantley. U.S. 29, U.S 331, S.R. 9 and S.R. 15 are co-routed until they split at Luverne.
  • U.S. Highway 231 and unsigned State Route 53 at Troy
  • U.S. Highway 82 and unsigned State Route 6 in Union Springs.
  • U.S. Highway 80 and unsigned State Route 8 in Tuskegee. U.S. 29, U.S. 80, S.R. 8 and S.R. 15 are co-routed until they split west of Auburn in Lee County.
  • Interstate 85 at Auburn. U.S. 29 is routed along I-85 from Auburn until east of Opelika. I-85 parallels U.S. 29 from Auburn to near Winston-Salem, North Carolina.
  • U.S. Highway 280 and unsigned State Route 38 in Opelika. U.S. 29, U.S. 280, S.R. 15 and S.R. 38 are co-routed with I-85 between Exits 58 and 62.
  • U.S. Highway 431 and unsigned State Route 1 in Opelika. It is at this junction where U.S. 280 and S.R. 38 split from I-85, U.S. 29 and S.R. 15.
