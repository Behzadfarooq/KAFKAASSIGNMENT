Alan Morgan (born 27 November 1983, in Edinburgh) is a Scottish professional footballer currently playing for Scottish Football League club Ross County, on loan from Scottish Premier League club Inverness CT in the Scottish Highlands. Morgan joined Inverness after spells with Blackburn Rovers and Darlington. To date, Morgan has played in 28 games for ICT, scoring 3 goals. Morgan wears the number 11 shirt.
