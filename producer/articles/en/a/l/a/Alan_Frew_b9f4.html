Alan Frew (born 8 November 1956 in Coatbridge, Scotland) is the lead singer for the Canadian band, Glass Tiger, and has also released two solo albums. He has appeared on many television specials, raises money for various causes and continues to perform around Canada as a solo artist and the lead singer for Glass Tiger.

Alan Frew has 5 Canadian Juno Awards, 4 Canadian Classic Awards and was nominated for a Grammy Award. In addition to being a singer/songwriter he is an actor, public speaker and the celebrity face working on behalf of the Canadian Crime Victim's Foundation.

Currently he is working with Terry Moshenberg in a mentorship project called League of Rock, lending his musical and vocal talents to everyday musicians. Alan's inspirational and motivational book "Lessons From a Tour Bus" is due to be released the summer of 2007.
