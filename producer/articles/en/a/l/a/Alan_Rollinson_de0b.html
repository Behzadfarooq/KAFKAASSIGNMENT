Alan Rollinson (born May 15, 1943 in Walsall, Staffordshire) is a British former racing driver from England. He entered one Formula One Grand Prix, the 1965 British Grand Prix, with a Cooper T71/73 run by Gerard Racing, but he failed to qualify. He competed more successfully in various other formulae, including Formula 5000.
