The Alamgir Tower is a high rise building currently under construction in Lahore, Pakistan. It will have 31 floors with 5 basement floors. The project was proposed by the Alamgir Developers was approved by the government. The construction started during the start of 2006 and is expected to be completed in the next two years in 2008.

The location of the building is on Main Boulevard, Gulberg III in the city of Lahore. It will include shops, shopping malls, luxury apartments and business offices to suit all ypes of stakeholders.
