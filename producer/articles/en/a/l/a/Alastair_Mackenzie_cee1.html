Alastair Mackenzie

Alastair Mackenzie is a Scottish actor. He was born in 1970 in Trinafour, near Perth.

Mackenzie left home at the age of 18 and moved to London. Though best known as a television and film actor, he also has some theatre credits to his name.

He lives with his wife, Scottish actress Susan Vidler, with whom he has one daughter, Martha, born in February 2000. His brother is director David Mackenzie.
