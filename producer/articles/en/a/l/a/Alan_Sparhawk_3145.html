Alan Sparhawk

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Alan Sparhawk is the guitarist and vocalist for American band Low. He is a native of Duluth, Minnesota. He is married to fellow bandmember Mimi Parker, and is a member of the Mormon faith.

His side projects include the blues-roots band The Black Eyed Snakes and more recently the Retribution Gospel Choir. He also released a track on a various artists compilation Songs for the End of the World as Hollis M. Sparhawk & Her Father.

Beyond this he occasionally plays solo shows in his hometown. He released a the solo track Be Nice To People With Lice on the various artists compilation album See You on the Moon!. During August, 2006 he released his debut solo album titled Solo Guitar.
