Alan Osório da Costa Silva, best known as Alan (born 19 September 1979 in Salvador, Brazil is a Brazilian football (soccer) player, who currently plays in Portugal for FC Porto. He joined FC Porto from Portuguese outfit CS Marítimo in 2005. Alan is a player with good techique skills and explosive speed.

[edit] Honours

  • Portuguese Championship: 2005/06
  • Cup of Portugal: 2005/06
  • SuperCup Cândido de Oliveira: 2006/07
