State Route 3, a state highway in Alabama, is the unsigned partner route assigned to U.S. Route 31 throughout the state. SR 3 and US 31 begin in Spanish Fort at the junction of U.S. Route 90 and U.S. Route 98 and their unsigned partner routes, State Route 16 and State Route 42. The northern terminus of SR 3 is at the Tennessee state line 13 miles north of Athens. US 31 and SR 3 roughly parallel Interstate 65 throughout the state and serve as a route to connect rural towns throughout the state.

In Alabama, U.S. Route 31 and State Route 3 pass through the following cities and towns:

[edit] Highway junctions

In addition to the junction with U.S. 90 and U.S. 98, U.S. 31 and S.R. 3 intersect the following Interstate highways, U.S. highways, and major state routes in Alabama:

  • U.S. Route 29 and unsigned State Route 15 at Flomaton. These routes overlap until they split at Brewton
  • U.S. Route 84 and unsigned State Route 12 at Evergreen
  • Interstate 65 at Hope Hull
  • U.S. Route 80 and unsigned State Route 8 at Montgomery. These routes briefly overlap in the city.
  • U.S. Route 82 and unsigned State Route 6 in Prattville
  • State Route 14 in Prattville
  • Interstate 65 north of Prattville
  • State Route 22 in Clanton
  • Interstate 65 at Clanton
  • Interstate 65 at Calera
  • Interstate 65 at Alabaster
  • Interstate 459 at Hoover
  • Interstate 65 at Vestavia Hills
  • U.S. Route 280 and unsigned State Route 38 at Homewood. These routes overlap until they junction I-20/I-59 in downtown Birmingham
  • U.S. Route 78 and unsigned State Route 4 in downtown Birmingham
  • U.S. Route 11 and unsigned State Route 7 in downtown Birmingham
  • Interstate 20/Interstate 59 in downtown Birmingham. This is the western terminus of U.S. 280 and S.R. 38
  • Interstate 65 at Fultondale
  • Interstate 65 at Warrior
  • Interstate 65 at Hayden. S.R. 3 and U.S. 31 share the same route as I-65 between Exit 284 and Exit 287 Blount Springs
  • U.S. Route 278 and unsigned State Route 74 at Cullman
  • Interstate 65 near the border of Cullman County and Morgan County
  • State Route 67 in Decatur, Alabama.
  • U.S. Route 72 Alternate and State Route 20 in Decatur.
  • U.S. Route 72 and unsigned State Route 2 at Athens
  • Interstate 65 at Athens. SR 3 and US 31 are share the same route as I-65 from Athens to Ardmore, Tennessee
