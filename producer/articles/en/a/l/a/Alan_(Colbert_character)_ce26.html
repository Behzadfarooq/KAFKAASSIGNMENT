Alan is a character on Comedy Central’s The Colbert Report , played by stand up comedian Jordan Carlos. He was first introduced as Stephen Colbert’s "Black Friend" on the Dr. Martin Luther King Jr. Day show when Stephen asked him if he too was upset with having to take the day off. However Stephen reduced his status from friend to "acquaintance" after spotting Alan at a protest rally. Since then Stephen has been looking for a new Black Friend. He has insisted that those who apply must be black, because he doesn’t see color. On one episode he had an awkward encounter with Alan who was sitting in the audience with a new "friend." Stephen has yet to find his new Black Friend.
