Alabama State Route 53 is a 368-mile state route that stretches from the Florida state line in southeastern Houston County south of Dothan to near the Tennessee state line at Ardmore in Limestone County. Between Dothan and Huntsville, S.R. 53 is the unsigned partner route assigned to U.S. Route 231.

The length of S.R. 53 is due in part to its irregular orientation. Beginning at its southern terminus, S.R. 53 assumes a slight northwestwardly trajectory as it passes through Dothan heading towards Montgomery. North of Montgomery, S.R. 53 and U.S. 231 bear northeastwardly towards Sylacauga, where the routes begin a multiplex with U.S. Route 280 and its unsigned partner route, S.R. 38. From Sylacauga, the routes head in a northwestwardly direction until they reach Harpersville where S.R. 53 and U.S. 231 divert from U.S. 280 and S.R. 38. North of Harpersville, S.R. 53/U.S. 231 assumes a general north-south orientation until the routes reach Huntsville. At Huntsville, S.R. 53 diverts from U.S. 231 and once again becomes a signed route until it reaches its terminus in Ardmore.
