State Route 5 is a 201 mile long route that extends from Thomasville in Clarke County to Natural Bridge in Winston County. Other cities and towns along the route include Marion, Brent, Bessemer, Birmingham, Graysville, Sumiton and Jasper. Prior to the completion of Interstate 65, S.R. 5 was part of one of the main routes between Birmingham and Mobile.

[edit] Route Description

In essence, S.R. 5 is two separate routes. The first leg of the route begins at the southern terminus of the route at the junction with U.S. Highway 43 at Thomasville. For the next 135 miles, the route heads to the northeast towards Birmingham, passing through the rural areas of Alabamaâ€™s Black Belt. In northern Bibb County, S.R. 5 junctions with U.S. Highway 11, then Interstate 20/Interstate 59 and continues its northeastward trajectory. S.R. 5 is co-routed with U.S. 11 for 30 miles between Woodstock and Birmingham.

The second section of the route begins near historic Legion Field in Birmingham, where U.S. 11 and S.R. 5 junction U.S. Highway 78. There, the overlap of S.R. 5 and U.S. Highway 11 end, and the 40 mile long overlap of S.R. 5 and U.S. 78 begins. U.S. 78 and S.R. 5 head northwestwardly towards Jasper, passing through the suburbs of Forestdale, Adamsville and Graysville. At Jasper, S.R. 5 splits from U.S. 78, continuing through rural areas of Walker County and Winston County. The northern terminus of S.R. 5 is at its junction with U.S. Highway 278 and State Route 13 at Natural Bridge.

[edit] Junctions with Major Routes

  • The southern terminus at U.S. Highway 43 in Thomasville
  • U.S. Highway 80 near Marion Junction in western Dallas County.
  • U.S. Highway 82 at Brent
  • U.S. Highway 11 at Woodstock. S.R. 5 is co-routed with U.S. 11 until they split in Birmingham
  • Interstate 20/Interstate 59 near Woodstock. S.R. 5 and U.S. 11 are routed on I-20/I-59 between Exits 97 and 108. S.R. 5 is one of only two signed Alabama state routes to be co-routed on an Interstate highway. (The other is State Route 69, routed along Interstate 65 in Cullman County}.
  • Interstate 459 near Bessemer
  • State Route 150 at Bessemer
  • U.S. Highway 78 in Birmingham. S.R. 5 is co-routed with U.S. 78 until they split near Jasper
  • Interstate 20/Interstate 59 near Birmingham-Southern College
  • State Route 69 at Jasper. S.R. 5 and S.R. 69 are co-routed briefly in Jasper
  • The northern terminus at the junction with U.S. Highway 278 and State Route 13 at Natural Bridge
