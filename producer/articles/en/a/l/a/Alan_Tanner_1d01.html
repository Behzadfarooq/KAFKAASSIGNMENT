Alan Tanner (born October 16, 1975) is a film director living in Omaha, Nebraska. Tanner is originally Orange Park, Florida(suburb of Jacksonville, Florida.) He directs the humorous "weekly movies" for the Saddle Creek Records website, and has made several short films.

Tanner also used to be in the band Your American Math and has a solo project Knitting Club

He also plays guitar for Saddle Creek recording artist Maria Taylor. In addition, he is a barista at an Omaha coffee shop.

Tanner dates Tilly and the Wall singer Neely Jenkins.

Tanner has 3 sisters.
