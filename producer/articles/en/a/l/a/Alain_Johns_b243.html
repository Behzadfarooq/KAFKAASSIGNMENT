Alain Johns is a character in Stephen King's epic series of Dark Tower novels. A childhood friend and classmate of Roland Deschain, Alain accompanied Roland when he was sent to the Barony of Mejis following his test of manhood. During this time, Roland, Alain, and Cuthbert Allgood rode against and defeated the forces of John Farson, preventing them from absconding with the crude oil needed to power Farson's machines of war.

Alain was later accidentally killed by Roland and Cuthbert the night before the battle of Jericho Hill. After riding through the night to bring his allies word of the approach of Farson's troops, Alain was mistaken for an ambushing enemy and gunned down.

In addition to his skill as a gunslinger, Alain was proficient in "The Touch," a form of ESP.

Alain has appeared in every issue of the Gunslinger comics, where he was drawn having sandy hair and soft features.
