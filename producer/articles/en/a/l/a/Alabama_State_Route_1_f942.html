State Route 1 in Alabama, also known as SR 1, is a hidden route overlayed by U.S. Route 231 from the Florida state line to Dothan, U.S. Route 431 from Dothan to Huntsville, and then the multiplex of US 231/431 from Huntsville to the Tennessee state line.

SR-1 passes through these cities and towns in Alabama:

  • Dothan
  • Abbeville
  • Eufaula
  • Phenix City
  • Opelika
  • Lafayette
  • Oxford
  • Anniston
  • Gadsden
  • Attalla
  • Boaz
  • Albertville
  • Guntersville
  • Huntsville
  • Hazel Green

State Route 1 and U.S. Route 431 junction the following major routes:

  • Unsigned State Route 53 at Dothan. SR-53 is a hidden state route assigned to U.S. Route 231 between Dothan and Huntsville.
  • U.S. Route 84 and unsigned State Route 12 at Dothan
  • U.S. Route 82 and unsigned State Route 6 at Eufaula
  • U.S. Route 280 and unsigned State Route 38 at Phenix City. The four routes overlap until they split at Opelika
  • U.S. Route 80 and unsigned State Route 8 at Phenix City
  • U.S. Route 29 and unsigned State Route 15 at Opelika
  • Interstate 85 at Opelika
  • State Route 9 in rural Cleburne County
  • Interstate 20 east of Oxford
  • U.S. Route 78 and unsigned State Route 4 east of Oxford. The four routes overlap until they split in Oxford
  • U.S. Route 278 and unsigned State Route 74 at Gadsden. The four routes overlap until they split in Attalla
  • U.S. Route 411 and unsigned State Route 25 at Gadsden
  • Interstate 59 at Gadsden
  • U.S. Route 11 and unsigned State Route 7 at Attalla
  • State Route 75 at Albertville
  • State Route 79 at Guntersville. SR-79 is routed with SR-1 and US 431 through the city.
  • U.S. Route 231 and unsigned State Route 53 at Huntsville. Routed with US 231 to the Tennessee State line.
  • Interstate 565 at Huntsville
  • U.S. Route 72 and unsigned State Route 2 at Huntsville. Routed with US-72 from University Drive to Sparkman Drive.
