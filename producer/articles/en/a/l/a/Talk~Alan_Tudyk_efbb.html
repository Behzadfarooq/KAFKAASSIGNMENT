[edit] Popularity...

I'm pretty sure that Alan is more popular for his role in Firefly/Serenity than in Dodgeball (to be honest, I didn't even notice it was him until I went back and rewatched Serenity...). Firefly/Serenity, if I'm not mistaken, have both taken in more money in DVD sales than Dodgeball, and I'm fairly certain Serenity took in more money at the box office. Kind of seems silly to say he's best known as Steve the Pirate then to mention the CULT following of Firefly...

Also think a different picture should be used, as in pretty much all his roles he's had blonde hair... doesn't make too much of a difference though, but I think the above point is very important. Jvincep 12:28, 18 December 2006 (UTC)
