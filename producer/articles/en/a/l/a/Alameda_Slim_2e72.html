Alameda Slim (b. January 1, 1830) is the villain of the Disney's Home on the Range. He is a big, fat rustler with a red mustache and goatee. He also goes by the alias Y.O'Del. His nephews, the Willie Brothers, never catch on that Y.O'Del is actually Slim, even when he puts the Y.O'Del disguise on in front of them.

Angry at the world for not appreciating his yodeling, Slim takes his revenge by using his vocal talents to hypnotise and steal cows. He then sells them, using the proceeds to buy all the local farms under his alias. One of the cows, Maggie, has her life ruined when Slim robs the farm she lives on, and sets out to stop him.

[edit] Trivia

  • In the original script Alameda Slim was a ghost.[citation needed]
  • In the early script Alameda Slim was going to use the cows he stole to attack the White House and become president.[citation needed]
