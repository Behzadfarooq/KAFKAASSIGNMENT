Alan Reid (Royal Household)

Sir Alan Reid has been Keeper of the Privy Purse, Treasurer to the Queen and Receiver General to the Duchy of Lancaster in the Royal Household of the Sovereign of the United Kingdom since 2002. As Keeper of the Privy Purse, he is responsible for the expenditure of public funds voted by the Parliament to the Sovereign, usually called the Civil List. As Treasurer to The Queen, Sir Alan is also responsible for the Sovereign's private finances. The Sovereign also holds the title of Duke of Lancaster (regardless of gender), which brings with it responsible for the Duchy of Lancaster estates.

Like his predecessor, Sir Alan was formerly a senior partner with International Big 4 accountancy firm KPMG. He was appointed a Knight Commander of the Royal Victorian Order (KCVO) in 2007. This is a personal honour from Queen Elizabeth II.
