State Route 216, also known as SR 216, is a numbered state highway in Alabama. SR 216 follows the former route of U.S. Route 11 through eastern Tuscaloosa County.

[edit] Route description

The route starts in eastern Tuscaloosa at University Boulevard (State Route 215) and Veterans' Memorial Drive and ends just as it crosses over Interstate 20/Interstate 59 in Bucksville, just short of the Tuscaloosa-Jefferson county line.

The highway passes through the coal-mining country of eastern Tuscaloosa County. The largest town on the highway are Brookwood and Lakeview. The highway is mostly narrow and windy and is known for the high number of fatal accidents on the Brookwood-to-Tuscaloosa stretch. [2]
