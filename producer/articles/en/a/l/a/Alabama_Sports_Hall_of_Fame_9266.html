Alabama Sports Hall of Fame

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Alabama Sports Hall of Fame (ASHOF) is a state museum dedicated to the celebration and preservation of Alabama’s sports heritage. ASHOF has over 5,000 sports artifacts elegantly displayed in the 33,000 square foot building in downtown Birmingham. ASHOF features 5 out of the top 15 on ESPN’s list of the top 100 athletes of the century including Jesse Owens, Hank Aaron, Joe Louis, Willie Mays, and Carl Lewis.
