Alan Cowell

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Alan S. Cowell (born March 16, 1947) is a British journalist who is the London bureau chief of The New York Times.

Cowell began his journalism career as a reporter for Reuters[1].

In 1985, Cowell won the George Polk Award for foreign reporting[2].

[edit] Personal

Cowell is married and lives in London.
