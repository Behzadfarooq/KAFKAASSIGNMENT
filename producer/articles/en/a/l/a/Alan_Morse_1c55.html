Alan Morse is a guitarist for the progressive rock band Spock's Beard. He is the brother of Neal Morse, who left the band in 2002. He has recorded with many artists including Chad & Jeremy, Spencer Davis, Chuck Norris, Neal Morse, and (Spock's Beard keyboardist) Ryo Okumoto. Along with the guitar, he sings and plays the theremin, the cello, musical saw, keyboards, drums, bass & bouzouki. Morse has a degree in electrical engineering and own an electronics manufacturing company, DynaMetric, Inc. Unusually for a rock guitar player, Alan Morse does not use a plectrum.
