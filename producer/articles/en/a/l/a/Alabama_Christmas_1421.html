Alabama Christmas is a Christmas album by country band Alabama. The album was released on RCA Records in 1985, and has since been certified platinum for sales of 2 million units by the Recording Industry Association of America [1].

The album represents Alabama's first Christmas album, and includes their best-known holiday song, 1982's "Christmas in Dixie".
