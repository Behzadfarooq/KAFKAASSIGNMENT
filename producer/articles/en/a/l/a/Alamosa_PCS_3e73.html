Alamosa PCS was one of several affiliates of Sprint PCS. Alamosa PCS is no longer an affiliate of the Sprint Nextel Corp. Alamosa Holdings has held a shareholder meeting and agreed to Sprint Nextel's offer of $4.6 billion for the acquisition of their 19-state affiliate market.[citation needed]
