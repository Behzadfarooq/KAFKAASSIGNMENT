Alabama Governor's Mansion

The Alabama Governor's Mansion is the official residence of the Governor of Alabama and his family. The mansion has been occupied by Governor Bob Riley and First Lady Patsy Riley since Riley's first inauguration on January 20, 2003.

The 96-year-old home has served Alabama's Governors since 1911, when Governor Emmitt O'Neal moved in. The mansion has held two weddings in its history, that of First Daughter Emily Ligon in 1919 and that of First Daughter Peggy Wallace in 1973.
