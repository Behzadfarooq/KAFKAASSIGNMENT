Alariaceae are a family of kelp.

[edit] Genera

  • Alaria
  • Ecklonia
  • Eckloniopsis
  • Egregia
  • Eisenia
  • Pterygophora
  • Undaria (e.g. wakame)

[edit] Distribution

The distribution of Alariaceae includes the northeastern Pacific Ocean, South Africa, southern Australia, and New Zealand coasts.
