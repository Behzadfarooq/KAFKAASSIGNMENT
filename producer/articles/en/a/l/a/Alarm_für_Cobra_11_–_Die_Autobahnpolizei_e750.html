Alarm für Cobra 11 - Die Autobahnpolizei is a very popular German television series about a 2 man team of Autobahnpolizei (Highway Police) first set in Berlin then later in North Rhine-Westphalia.

[edit] Cast

The original cast included Johannes Brandrup as Frank Stolte and Rainer Strecker as Ingo Fischer. Fischer left the series just two episodes later, his replacement became Erdogan Atalay as Semir Gerkhan.

Brandrup quit the series at the end of Season 1. RTL contracted Mark Keller as André Fux in his place, who left the series at the end of Season 3. Mark Keller was replaced by Rene Steinke as Tom Kranich. He played the role between the seasons 4-6, then decided to quit to try himself in other roles.

Atalay's new partner became Christian Oliver as Jan Richter in Season 7&8. When he left the series in order to build his career in the US, RTL turned to Steinke, who decided to return at the beginning of Season 9.

However, Steinke decided not to renew his contract, so he will leave the series again at the end at Season 20. His replacement will be actor Gedeon Burkhard, known for his role in another police show, Kommissar Rex.

The opening titles have a preamble that says:

Ihr Revier ist die Autobahn (Their turf is the highway)
Ihr Tempo ist mörderisch (Their speeds are murderous)
Ihre Gegner: Autoschieber, Mörder und Erpresser (Their opponents: Carjackers, murderers and blackmailers)
Einsatz rund um die Uhr für die Männer von Cobra 11 (Always ready for 24 hours around the clock are the team of Cobra 11)
Unsere Sicherheit ist ihr Job (Our security is their job)

[edit] Stuntwork

The series is best known for its extremley well done stuntwork, which would always feature the destruction of various vehicles in every episode. Currently its 20th series, almost every episode has a very distinct structure with at least one daring action sequence, with the pretitles sequence usually consisting of the main event (i.e. a devastating crash on the autobahn). The stuntwork used is often so spectacular to watch that it would never look out of place in a full fledged movie, with the sight of a car flying into implausible heights a common occurrence.

[edit] Parodies

Alarm für Cobra 11 -Die Autobahnpolizei is regularly parodied as "Alarm für Kebap 11 – Die Dönerpölizei" by Freitag Nacht News, a German comedy sketch program.
