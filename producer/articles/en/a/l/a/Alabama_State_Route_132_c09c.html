Alabama State Route 132 is an 18-mile long route in Blount County and Etowah County. The western terminus of the route is at its junction with State Route 75 north of downtown Oneonta. The eastern terminus of the route is at its junction with U.S. Route 278 east of Walnut Grove.

[edit] Route Description

S.R. 132 is a two-lane route in its entirety. Its main purpose is to lead motorists from Oneonta to Attalla and Gadsden without travelling via State Route 75 or U.S. Route 231 and Interstate 59. It travels in a general northeastward trajectory as it leads from Oneonta as it leads through rural Blount County. The only town the route passes through is the farming community of Altoona.

[edit] Counties along the route

  • Blount County
  • Etowah County

[edit] Cities and towns along the route

  • Oneonta
  • Altoona
  • Walnut Grove

[edit] Junctions with other routes

S.R. 132 junctions only the routes at its western and eastern termini. However, S.R. 75 junctions U.S. Route 231 less than one mile south of its junction with S.R. 132, and there are trailblazer signs on U.S. 231 leading motorists to S.R. 132.
