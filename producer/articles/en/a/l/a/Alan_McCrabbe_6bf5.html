Alan McCrabbe is a Hurling player for Dublin and Craobh Chiaráin. Alan won a Dublin Senior Hurling Championship medal with Craobh Chiaráin in 2006. McCrabbe scored a 1-6 (1-6f) in the final against Ballyboden in a game that finished with the score 2-10 to 2-8.[1] McCrabbe was named on the Dublin Blue Stars team for 2006 at right half forward.
