Alan Pereira Costa or simply Alan Bahia (born January 14, 1983 in Itabuna, is a Brazilian defensive midfielder. He currently plays for Atlético-PR.

Professional debut in 0-0 draw away to Goiás on October 5, 2002. [[1]].

[edit] Honours

  • Paraná State League: 2005

[edit] Contract

1st January 2006 to 31st December 2009
