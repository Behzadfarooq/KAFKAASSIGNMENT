Alan Hoyle (born December 13, 1983 in Ashington UK), is a UK basketball player who currently plays for the ETS Renegades.

[edit] Player profile

Hoyle plays the point guard position, however, he is known to play the combo guard, a mix of both point and shooting guard. Hoyle is the only UK player to have scored over 100 points in a game, a feat which he has repeated in another two contests (scoring 107 and 113).

Hoyle also coaches basketball for Esteem Through Sports, working along side England Basketball player Carl Miller.

[edit] Statistics

  • Points: 38.9
  • Rebounds: 5.4
  • Assists: 7.1

[edit] Achievements and records

  • Hoyle holds the record for most points scored in a single game (UK) with 121 points.
  • Hoyle holds the record for most assists in a single game (UK) with 28 assists.
  • Hoyle is a four time MVP and a five time Defensive player of the year.

[edit] Trivia

  • Wears a sweat band on his left elbow as a tribute to Michael Jordan.
  • Has worn two numbers during his career, number 9 and number 23.
