State Route 208 or SR 208 is a short, west-east route that serves as a connector between State Route 165 and the bridge across the Chattahoochee River at Cottonton, the only bridge across the river between Phenix City and Eufaula.

From its western terminus, the route continues east into Georgia as Georgia State Route 39 Spur.
