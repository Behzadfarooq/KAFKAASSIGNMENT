[edit] Summary

This is an image of the final two frames of the Dean Alston editorial cartoon Alas Poor Yagan. The cartoon was published in The West Australian on Saturday 6 September 1997. This digital image of the cartoon was cropped from the full image which is available at Image:Alas Poor Yagan.jpg.
