Alabama Public Service Commission

The Alabama Public Service Commission is a body of three elected members, a President and two Commissioners, who each serve 4-year terms. The President is elected during Presidential elections and the Commissioners are elected during midterm elections. The Commission is primarily charged with setting the rates that private utility companies are allowed to charge customers and the profits that the companies are allowed to collect.
