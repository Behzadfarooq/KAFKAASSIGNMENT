Miss Wormwood is a fictional character in the comic strip Calvin and Hobbes created by Bill Watterson. She is Calvin's main teacher at his elementary school who teaches first grade level. Miss Wormwood is also described as an old, depressed, and fat lady who continously wishes to retire her job.

[edit] History

Miss Wormwood first debuted during the strip's first weeks as a school teacher who wore glasses and had a temptation of trying to make Calvin learn something in school. While trying to coax him to do something, she initally gave up in the later years and would simply send him over to the principal's or tell him to stay after class. She usually teaches varoius subjects in class, give out "show-and-tells" on speific days, and give out numberous pop quizzes and tests.

At times, she is viewed as a slimy, bug-eyed alien in Calvin's "Spaceman Spiff" world. She would usually be seen as a queen of a hostile colony of aliens who usually sentences him to death; but in the real world, it would turn out that he is usually called out to do a math problem on the board or sending Calvin to the principal's office by herself. She does her career with little effort and with tremendous stress and has a big wish that she would one day retire her job.

[edit] Characteristics

While at most of the time ahe is dull and very depressed, she is known to usually get stressed a lot from continous labor and trying to keep the class in order. But Calvin is usually her main obstacle she usually confers. Whenever Calvin would fail a test, she'd usually give him a D- grade or a F. But when Calvin begins to test her limits, she rarely begin special parent conferences with one of Calvin's parents disscusing about his grades, behavior, and social relationships with other students (mainly focusing on Susie).

In a single strip, it was also revealed that Miss Wormowood herself, is a heavy smoker and consumes many differnet medications.
