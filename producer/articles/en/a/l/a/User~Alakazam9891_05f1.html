[edit] History

[edit] Real Life

I was born in Tiffin, OH, on September 8th, 1991. I first got into video games at the early age of 6 with the Nintendo 64, my first video game ever being Diddy Kong Racing. I then soon after got a Sega Genesis about one year later, finally experiencing older-styled gaming, falling in love with Sonic 2 in the process. During Christmas of 1998, thanks to the fad that was sweeping the world, I got my very own Game Boy Color and Pokemon Red, Pokemon Blue, and Pokemon Yellow, in addition to Mario Golf (Game Boy Color) . After my parents sold my Sega Genesis in a garage sale (for $25, along with 16 games (all of which were great, which really sucked)), I began to turn more towards Nintendo. Christmas of 2001 was a great holiday season for me: although I didn't get a Nintendo Gamecube at the time, I recieved a Game Boy Advance and Pokemon Gold and Pokemon Silver. Getting a Gamecube during early 2002, I fell in love with Sonic Adventure 2: Battle and Super Smash Bros. Melee, both being my first Gamecube games. Now an obsessee of the Nintendo DS Lite, I continue my Nintendo fanboy ways through space and time.

[edit] Sports Fandoms

[edit] Seattle Seahawks

I am a die-hard Seattle Seahawks fan, and have been since birth. The reason of this is unknown, as both of my parents are Minnesota Vikings fans, and I have lived in Ohio my entire life. It is said by critics that it was "a message from God", while others claim that it was because "the blue and green of the original helmets contrasted in a way that would entrance a child who has not been influenced by one certain group of people as to which team they would like". My favorite players on the team include Mack Strong, Lofa Tatupu, Ryan Plackemeier, Shaun Alexander, and Matt Hasselbeck, although I also like Deion Branch now that he's on the Seahawks. However, I would have to say my overall favorite player on the team, as of December 4th, is Josh Brown. The tackle that caused a fumble he made was uncharacteristic for a kicker, and might be the best Defense and Special Teams play I've seen all year (oh, not to mention he kicked the game-winning field goal for the fourth time this season).

Being friends with a lot of Pittsburgh Steelers fans, Super Bowl XL was a very depressing time of my life. The Green and Blue Screw, as I call it, was the worst officiated game in Super Bowl history, and the Seahawks should have had a much better overall outcome in the game.

But hey, they're doing well this season. Maybe a Super Bowl XLI is possible...?

[edit] Ohio State Buckeyes

I also am a huge fan of Ohio State. It's a possibility that their win over Miami in the Fiesta Bowl in 2003 was the greatest moment of my life. The game against Michigan was one great game, and even though I hate Michigan, they still played their Harts out (lol pun) and I have to give them credit.

Turns out the game is gonna be against Florida. How epic, considering Flordia is my second-favorite college team (Well, maybe third behind Middle Tennessee).
