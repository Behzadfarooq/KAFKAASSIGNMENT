Alauddin Al-Azad(Bengali: আলাউদ্দিন আল আজাদ), (born 1932) is a renowned modern Bangladeshi author, famous novelist, poet.

[edit] Life

Alauddin Al-Azad was born in Dhaka. Was a very good student of Bangla Literature and also a professor. Was a first secretary of Bangladesh High Commission in Moskow.

[edit] Works

His literary works were included in the curriculum of school level, secondary, higher secondary and graduation level Bengali Literature in Bangladesh.
