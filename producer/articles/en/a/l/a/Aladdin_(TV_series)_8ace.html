Aladdin is an animated television series made by Walt Disney Television which aired from 1994 to 1996, based on the original 1992 feature. Coming on the heels of the direct-to-video sequel The Return of Jafar, the series picked up where that installment left off, with Aladdin still living on the streets of Agrabah, still unwed to beautiful and spunky Princess Jasmine.

Disney's Aladdin continued the fantasy/magic adventures of the feature films, carrying "Al" and Jasmine into peril among sorcerers, monsters, thieves, and more. Monkey sidekick Abu and the fast-talking, shape-shifting Genie came along to help, as did sassy, complaining parrot Iago, formerly Jafar’s pet but now a grudgingly good guy. In place of movie villain Jafar, the series introduced evil sorcerer Mozenrath, megalomaniacal inventor Mechanicles and dark evil incarnate Mirage, among others. The show never featured Jafar (due to plot reasons in The Return of Jafar) but featured the thief who released him in the movie, Abis Mal.

Debuting simultaneously on Saturday morning and as part of the syndicated weekday Disney Afternoon, the show set a pattern for several future Disney series. Many of the films' stars provided the voices of their TV counterparts with Dan Castellaneta filling in for Robin Williams in the Genie role.

The show is now shown in reruns on Toon Disney but due to the addition of JETIX, it is now only on weekends, but since the show airs during the weekday mornings and the Movie Stars on Toon Disney blocks, only the episodes featuring Jasmine are shown. The Disney Channel reran the series in the late-1990s until their pre-teen lineup took over.

The show became popular with Arabic speaking viewers when it aired on MBC 3 dubbed in Arabic. Most, if not all of the characters, were voiced by Egyptian actors, and as a result, Egyptian Arabic was used.

[edit] Characters

[edit] Good

  1. Aladdin (voiced by Scott Weinger)
  2. Princess Jasmine (voiced by Linda Larkin)
  3. Genie (voiced by Dan Castellaneta)
  4. Abu (voiced by Frank Welker. Monkey friend of Aladdin)
  5. Iago (voiced by Gilbert Gottfried)
  6. Razoul (voiced by Jim Cummings)
  7. Sultan (voiced by Val Bettin)
  8. Rajah (voiced by Frank Welker. Tiger friend of Princess Jasmine)
  9. Aladdin's Magic Carpet (not voiced)
  10. Eden (Voice actress unknown: Eden is a genie who became the girlfriend of Aladdin's Genie. She appeared in two episodes: "Some Enchanted Genie" and "The Book of Khartoum.")
  11. Prince Uncouthma (Voiced by Tino Insana): Uncouthma is the ruler of the barbarian land of Odiferous. He first appeared in the episode "Smells Like Trouble" as a suitor for Princess Jasmine. In later episodes, Aladdin and friends visited Odiferous and met Uncouthma's wife, Brunhilda, his son, Bud, his right-hand man, General Gouda, and other characters)
  12. Thundra (Voiced by Candi Milo): Thundra, the Rainbird, oversees all the world's weather. She is Iago's romantic counterpart
  13. Fasir (Voiced by Ed Gilbert): Fasir is a seer who hides his cycloptic nature by wearing a bandage over his eye, appearing as a blind beggar. He is the brother of the giant Fashoom, and was once in love with Mirage. He helped Aladdin and his friends in a number of episodes, often thwarting Mirage's evil plans.

[edit] Evil

  1. Abis Mal (voiced by Jason Alexander)
    1. Harud Hazi Bin (voiced by James L. Avery, Sr.) : Harud is Abis Mal's right-hand man, who often considered his boss's schemes ill-conceived.
  2. Mechanicles (voiced by Charlie Adler)
  3. Mozenrath (voiced by Jonathan Brandis)
    1. Xerxes (voiced by Frank Welker)
  4. Mirage (voiced by Bebe Neuwirth)
  5. Saleen (voiced by Julie Brown)
    1. Armand (not voiced) : Armand is Saleen's assistant, an octopus.
  6. Ayam Aghoul (voiced by Hamilton Camp)
  7. Amin Damoola (voiced by Jeff Bennett)
  8. The Al Muddy : The Almuddy is a race of subterranean mud-monsters.
  9. Malcho (voiced by Hector Elizondo) : Malcho is a flying serpent who is also Thundra's rival for control of the world's weather. He appeared in two episodes: "Rain of Terror" and "The Return of Malcho."
  10. Aziz (voiced by Michael Bell) : Aziz was an old acquaintance of Aladdin's who, upon finding the Destiny Stone (in "Seems Like Old Crimes"), turned into an evil creature who could use his breath to do just about anything he wanted. He also appeared in "Destiny of Fire."
  11. Nefir Hasenuf (voiced by Rene Auberjonois) : Nefir is an imp who is always looking to make money, at one point going so far as to start a war between Odiferous and Agrabah ("The Way We War") by stealing the Odiferans' beloved artifact, the Most Sacred Crock of Cheese, and blaming it on Aladdin.
  12. Frajeed (Voiced by Dan Castellaneta) : Frajeed an Ice Efreet is a being with the ability to create things from ice and snow who lives in the Frozen North. Frajeed appeared in just one episode, "Of Ice and Men."

[edit] Other

  1. Sadira (voiced by Kellie Martin) : A female streetrat who was saved by Aladdin and fell in love. Jealous of the princess, she learns the art of "Sand Magic" and used it to try and make Aladdin love her. Sadira eventually turned over a new leaf by helping defeat the Witches of the Sand in the episode "Witch Way Did She Go?"
  2. Merc (voiced by Dorian Harewood) : Merc is the captain of a flying ship who appeared in two episodes: "Raiders of the Lost Shark" and "Beast or Famine."
  3. The Mukhtar (voiced by John Kassir) : The Mukhtar is perhaps the last surviving member of the race of Mukhtars, renowned as the sworn enemies of the genies. Originally hired by one of Genie's old masters, Aged Al Gebraic, to capture Genie in "Genie Hunt," he eventually became Genie's friend following the events of "The Hunted."
  4. Sultan Pasta Al'Dente (voiced by Stuart Pankin : Pasta is the ruler of the land of Getsistan, known throughout the Seven Deserts for their casinos.
  5. Queen Hippsodeth (voiced by Kate Mulgrew) : Hippsodeth is the ruler of the Isle of Galifem, home of the Galifem warriors. After being defeated by the Sultan in battle, she fell in love with him.
    1. Scara (voiced by Susan Tolsky) : One of the Galifem warriors, who had a crush on King Pector, a rival of the Sultan's for Queen Hippsodeth's affections.
