Alaska Power and Telephone Company is a communications and utilities firm operating in Alaska. It currently provides service above the Arctic Circle, in The Wrangell Mountains, and throughout southeastern Alaskan Islands. It's business units are named Power, Telephone, Hydro-Power,Wireless,and Internet.
