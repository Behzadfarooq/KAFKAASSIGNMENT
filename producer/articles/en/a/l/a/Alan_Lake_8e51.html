Alan Lake (24 November 1940 - 10 October 1984) was a British actor.

Lake was born in Stoke-on-Trent. He became a master swordsman with epee and foil. He was also a keen horseman and he broke his back in a riding accident.

He is best known as the third husband of the actress Diana Dors. They were married on 23 November 1968 and they had a son Jason together. He committed suicide at their Sunningdale home, five months after her death from cancer. It was revealed that he was terminally ill with a brain tumour and was grief stricken over his wife's death.

His roles included Herrick in the Doctor Who story Underworld; and parts in The Avengers, Blake's 7, Juliet Bravo, and Hart To Hart.
