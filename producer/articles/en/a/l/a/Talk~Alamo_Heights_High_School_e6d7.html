[edit] Removed content

For the reasons mentioned above, I've removed the Notable Teachers section, condensed the athletics section, and removed the all-state band students' names. As Jersyko mentioned above (make sure to check the link he provided), Wikipedia is not for listing any and all information pertaining to your school. All-state band is a nice achievement, but it isn't noteworthy in an encyclopediac sense. Similarly, there are thousands upon thousands of AP teachers in the country. That doesn't mean that they are not good people, but it does mean that they must have something of exceeding interest to merit their listing here — for example, a significant achievement in that teacher's field, such as some sort of national award. Tijuana Brass 05:10, 6 April 2006 (UTC)
