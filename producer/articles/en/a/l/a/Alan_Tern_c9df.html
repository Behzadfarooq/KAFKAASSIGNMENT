Alan Tern (Simplified Chinese: 唐育书; pinyin: Táng Yù Shū) is a Singaporean actor. Tern joined MediaCorp after winning the Champion title in Star Search Singapore 2001. Tern's acting in Double Happiness earned him a Star Awards 2005 Best Supporting Actor Award Nomination in 2005.

Besides being an actor, Tern is also an entrepreneur who deals with spectacles.
