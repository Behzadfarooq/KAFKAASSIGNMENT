State Route 257 or S.R. 257 is a 10 mile route serving as a connection between State Route 195 at Jasper to Duncan Bridge in Walker County. Once entering Winston County the route becomes Winston County Road 41. The road is also signed as the Curry Highway.

[edit] Route description

The southern terminus of the route is at its intersection with State Route 195 northeast of Jasper. From this point, the route takes a northeasterly course to Curry. From Curry, it travels in a northerly direction to its northern terminus at the Winston County line.
