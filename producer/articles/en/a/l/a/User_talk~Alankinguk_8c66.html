[edit] Crown copyright on images?

Howdy! Whilst editing, I came across Image:In-the-valley-all-was-still.jpg and Image:In-the-valley-all-was-still-small.jpg, which you uploaded under a Crown Copyright licence. I'd just like to confirm that this is the case, since you also appear to be the author of these images. Were these images were commissioned by a government department, and did you forfeit copyright to them? If not, then the copyright still belongs to you, and the images aren't covered by Crown Copyright. If this is the case, can you provide the name of the department or agency that commissioned the work, so that the terms of use can be reviewed? Happy editing! Per your response on my talk page, I figured I'd drop a note here. There's no problem with the error, I just wanted to clarify the copyright situation to avoid stepping on anyone's toes :)

Of course, if you want to release the image I'd advise a look at the terms of the Creative Commons licenses. If you've any other questions, feel free to drop me a line on my talk page.
