Alatau

Alatau or Ala Too (from Turkic languages: "motley mountain") is a generic name of a number of mountain ranges in Central Asia, characterized by interleaving areas of vegetation, scattered rocks and snows.

  • Djungarian Alatau (also known as Dzhungarsky Alatau, Dzungarian Alatau, Semirechensk Alatau) in Dzungaria
  • Kuznetsk Alatau
  • Talas Alatoo (de:Talas-Alatau )
  • Terskey-Alatau (de:Terskej-Alatau)
  • Kungey Alatoo (de:Kungei-Alatau)
  • Trans-Ili Alatau
