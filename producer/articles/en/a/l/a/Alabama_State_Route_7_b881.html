Alabama State Routes
< SR 6 SR 8 >


State Route 7 is the unsigned partner route assigned to U.S. Highway 11 throughout the state. S.R. 7, as well as State Route 8, the unsigned partner of U.S. Highway 80, begin at the Mississippi state line near Cuba. The northern terminus of S.R. 7 is at the Georgia state line near Sulphur Springs, Alabama. U.S. 11 and S.R. 7 roughly parallel Interstate 20/Interstate 59 from the Mississippi state line until Birmingham, and I-59 from Birmingham to the Georgia state line.

In Alabama, U.S. 11 and S.R. 7 pass through the following cities and towns:

  • Livingston
  • York
  • Eutaw
  • Tuscaloosa
  • Bessemer
  • Birmingham
  • Trussville
  • Springville
  • Attalla
  • Collinsville
  • Fort Payne

U.S. 11 and S.R. 7 enter Alabama on the same route as U.S. Highway 80 and State Route 8 east of Meridian, Mississippi, and intersect the following Interstate highways, U.S. highways, and major state routes in Alabama:

  • State Route 17 at York
  • State Route 14 at Eutaw
  • U.S. Highway 43 and unsigned State Route 13 at Eutaw. These routes overlap until they split in Tuscaloosa.
  • Interstate 20/Interstate 59 in northern Greene County near Knoxville
  • Interstate 359 and State Route 69 in Tuscaloosa. Northbound U.S. 11, S.R. 7 and S.R. 69 are routed on southbound I-359 for the entirety of the interstate highway's route.
  • U.S. Highway 82 and unsigned State Route 6 in Tuscaloosa
  • Interstate 20/Interstate 59 at Cottondale
  • Interstate 20/Interstate 59 at Coaling
  • State Route 5 near West Blocton. S.R. 5 is paired with U.S. 11 and S.R. 7 for 28 miles until these routes intersect with U.S. Highway 78 in Birmingham near historic Legion Field.
  • Interstate 20/Interstate 59 near West Blocton. U.S. 11, S.R. 5 and S.R. 7 are routed on I-20/I-59 between Exits 97 and 108.
  • Interstate 459 near Bessemer
  • U.S. Highway 78 and unsigned State Route 4 in Birmingham. U.S. 11 and 78 and S.R. 4 and 7 are co-routed as they enter downtown from the west. Routes 78 and 4 spilt from Routes 11 and 7 at the intersection of 1st Avenue North and 24th Street.
  • Interstate 65 in downtown Birmingham
  • U.S. Highway 31 and U.S. Highway 280 and their unsigned partners State Route 3 and State Route 38 in downtown Birmingham
  • State Route 75 in the eastern area of Birmingham
  • Interstate 459 at Trussville
  • U.S. Highway 231 and unsigned State Route 53 near Ashville
  • U.S. Highway 278 and U.S. Highway 431 and their unsigned partners State Route 74 and State Route 1 at Attalla
