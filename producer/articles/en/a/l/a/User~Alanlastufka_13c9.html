Alan J. Lastufka (born May 24, 1983) is a zinester and columnist best known for his project, the Fall of Autumn, launched October 1, 2005. Lastufka started the Zinester Podcasts in 2005, began work on the compbook Pride & Photocopies and created ZineWiki in 2006.

In October 2006, Lastufka pubilshed his first short film online. You can watch the film, "She Handed Me Her Diary", an adaptation from Pressed Between the Pages zine, on YouTube.
