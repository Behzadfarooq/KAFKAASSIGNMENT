Alan Geoffrion

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Alan Geoffrion is the author and screenwriter of the novel and AMC mini-series Broken Trail (2006 - Fulcrum Publishing).

Geoffrion has been involved in the horse business for over forty years and owns and operates a stable outside of Warrenton, Virginia with his wife Danielle. Broken Trail is Geoffrion's first novel and first screenplay.
