Alan I. Bigel

Alan I. Bigel has been teaching at the University of Wisconsin-La Crosse since approximately 1981. He is a native of Bronx, New York. He received his teaching degree from Columbia University in New York City in the mid-1960's. Classes presently taught include five separate Constitutional Law classes, Public Administration, and general introduction to Political Science. He also teaches a statesman seminar at Yale University during the summer months. Publicans include numerous books and law review articles on the United States Supreme Court and its Justices, particularly Justice Marshall and Justice Scalia. He is an adamant supporter of the Libertarian Party and their viewpoints.
