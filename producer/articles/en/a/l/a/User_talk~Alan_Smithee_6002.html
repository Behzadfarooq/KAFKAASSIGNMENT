[edit] A few points

Hi, and great you are ever-present in film gnoming. Just saw your Looker alt. poster. It won't show in the image page because it's only an ":Image" link. Also fair use should not be high resolution (and 462x676 is Hi), or it may get deleted soon. Best is if you could resize it down to 200px width. I could do this for you in a way that doesn't reduce its quality. Also best is to display the 2nd poster in the article. Let me know. Another point is that most films you tag as {{Film}} are actually {{Film|class=stub}}, so you could go ahead and give class stub, unless of course they look like class start. Cheers. Hoverfish Talk 16:16, 6 February 2007 (UTC)

It's for any film member to assess or re-assess classes stub, start and B. Only when it comes to upgrading to GA and on a peer reviewer is needed. In my first months of film-gnoming I didn't trust myself with assessing, but soon I got the points. There are some tricky points in spite of the stub upgrading instructions. One is that a short or simple documentary may have exhausted all that can be said about it without becoming as big as a class start. So sometimes I give a class start to an article that is no bigger than a (full) stub. But then anyone who disagrees is free to change it. Hoverfish Talk 07:18, 7 February 2007 (UTC)
