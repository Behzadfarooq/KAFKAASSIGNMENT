Alan Chin is a photographer who has worked in Kosovo[1], Iraq[2], New Orleans and many other places. He is a freelance photographer for The New York Times, and also publishes with Esquire, COLORS, Newsweek, and US News & World Report.

Chin's work in Kosovo earned him a nomination for the Pulitzer Prize in 1999 and 2000[3].
