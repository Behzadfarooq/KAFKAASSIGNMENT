Alaska 2 Tha Bay is a collaborative album from the whole Thizz Nation, presented by the late Mac Dre. The timberwolf coat with white fur you see the late great Thizz King Mac Dre wearing in his classic "Romp In Peace" picture was bought all the way up in Anchorage, Alaska while he was visiting there. Now here comes the music inspired by that far away place - "Alaska 2 Tha Bay". Meezy Montana of the great Northwest was a protege of Mac Dre's and they recorded a heapin' log pile of tracks together, all featured on this CD. Includes appearances by Mac Mall, Miami The Most, Mistah F.A.B., Bavgate, Money Gang, J-Diggs, Joker Tha Bailsbondsman, Young Zee, Dubee, 3rd Wall, Major Factors, Mob Figaz, Messy Marv, Rydah J. Klyde, Fed-X, Meez and the rest of the Thizz Nation. It also introduces a few rappers from Anchorage, Alaska, as new members of the Thizz Camp, including Meezy Montana.
