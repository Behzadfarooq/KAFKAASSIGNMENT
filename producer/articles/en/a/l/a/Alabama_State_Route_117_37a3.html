State Route 117 is a state highway in Alabama that runs 50 miles southeastward from the Tennessee state line 4 miles north of Pleasant Groves, Alabama to the Georgia state line 8 miles southeast of Mentone, Alabama. [2] Towns along the route include Pleasant Groves, Stevenson, Flat Rock, Ider, Hammonville, Valley Head, and Mentone.
