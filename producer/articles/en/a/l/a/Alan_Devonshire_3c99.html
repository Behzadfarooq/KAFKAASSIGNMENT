Alan Devonshire (born April 13, 1956 in Park Royal, Wembley) was an English footballer. He was a wide midfielder who made his name in the late 1970s playing for West Ham United (1976-1990). He won 8 caps for England. He finished his career at Watford.

Devonshire's debut occurred on October 30, 1976 against West Bromwich Albion, where he played in a 3-0 defeat. He scored 29 goals in 345 full (and 13 substitute) appearances.

Alan Devonshire is currently the manager of Hampton & Richmond Borough in the Premier Division of the Isthmian League. He was previously manager of Maidenhead United.
