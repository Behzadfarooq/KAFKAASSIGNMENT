Alan Brinkley

Alan Brinkley is the Allan Nevins Professor of History at Columbia University, where he is also provost. He is a progressive historian of the New Deal. Brinkley writes regularly in magazines such as Newsweek and The New Republic and is a strong advocate for progressive issues. As a professor and provost at Columbia, he is affectionately known as the "Brinkiester". He is son of television journalist David Brinkley, recipient of the Presidential Medal of Freedom. He is married and lives with his wife and daughter in New York City.

[edit] Textbook

  • American History: A Survey an AP U.S. History Textbook used in high schools and college.

Brinkley has also written several textbooks which are used by college and high school U.S. history classes.
