Alain Ferté

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Alain Ferté (born October 8, 1955 in Falaise) is a professional racing driver. He is the elder brother of Michel Ferté, who is also a professional racing driver.

Alain Ferté competed five seasons in Formula 3000 1985-1989.
