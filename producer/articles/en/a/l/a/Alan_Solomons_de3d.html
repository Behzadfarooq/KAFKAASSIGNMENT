Alan Solomans is a 55-year-old rugby union coach, he has previously been Director of Rugby for Ulster, and was assistant coach for South African Rugby team, Springboks. he is currently assistant coach at Belfast Harlequins. Alan Solomans is perhaps most famous for having never played a game of Rugby in his life, despite being one of the most highly reguarded and professional coaches in the business.

Sources: Belfast Newsletter, Belfast Telegraph, Belfast Harlequins
