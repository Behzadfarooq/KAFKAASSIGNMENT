[edit] Geography

I've changed the lat/long location to a location with many fewer significant figures. It's ridiculous to locate to tenths of a second of arc a city whose land area spans more than four minutes north-south and more than six minutes east-west. Unless someone can tell me that the location given was the geographic center of Alameda, or comes up with coordinates for the center or City Hall, they should stay more approximate, and only use minutes-of-arc precision. Argyriou 20:03, 27 May 2006 (UTC)

[edit] "Alemeda"

Google shows the (somewhat phonetic) misspelling "Alemeda" represents about 0.2% of the number of hits on Google that "Alameda" gets (50.6K/24.5M). It's a non-notable mistake, not an 'alternate name' that was suggested by User:Maxman280. I live and work in the SF bay area (SF/Oakland/Berkeley), and have never seen this usage.  Erielhonan  talk | contribs   仕方が無い   23:45, 6 November 2006 (UTC)
