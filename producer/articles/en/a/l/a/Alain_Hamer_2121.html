Alain Hamer (born December 10, 1965). Is a Luxembourgan football referee. He has refereed many matches in the UEFA Champions League, the first in 2000/01. He has refereed a match in the African Cup of Nations, close to 30 matches in the french Ligue 1. He refereed for Norway in the World Cup qualifying match against Scotland in 2005. He has been a FIFA-referee since 1993.
