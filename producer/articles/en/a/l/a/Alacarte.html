Alacarte Menu Editor
Developer: Travis Watkins
Latest release: 0.10.1 / 3 October 2006
OS: Linux, Solaris, BSD, other Unix-like
Use: Menu Editor
License: LGPL
Website: realistanew.com/category/projects/alacarte/

Alacarte (previously the Simple Menu Editor for GNOME or SMEG) is a menu editor for the GNOME desktop. It's part of the GNOME 2.16 release.
