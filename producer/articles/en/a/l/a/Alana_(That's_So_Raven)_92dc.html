Alana is a fictional character played by Adrienne Bailon on the comedy television series That's So Raven.

[edit] Character information

Alana is the beautiful school bully, who can't stand Raven because she won a part in a play over her in 4th grade. Alana is really petty but sometimes her & her crew (her assistant, Muffy and her bodyguard Loca) end the beef with Raven & her friends until Raven creeps her out by always knowing what's gonna happen before it happens.

Alana and Raven were once friends back in elementary school but a fourth grade play that had them both competing for the role of the tooth fairy (which Raven got while Alana was tooth decay) would ruin their friendship. [1]

She often sneaks sentences of Spanish into her everyday talking.

Alana was sent to military school for her bad behavior, and her posse was taken over by Bianca. [2]
