Alain Carpentier de Changy (born in Brussels, February 5, 1922 - died in Etterbeek, August 5, 1994) was a racing driver from Belgium. His single World Championship Formula One Grand Prix attempt was the 1959 Monaco Grand Prix with a Cooper run by Ecurie Nationale Belge, but he failed to qualify. He was more successful in sports car racing.
