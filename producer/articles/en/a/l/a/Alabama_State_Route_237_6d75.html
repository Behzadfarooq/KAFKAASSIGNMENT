State Route 237 or SR 237 is a 5 mile long route that serves as a connection between State Route 172 east of Hackleburg with State Route 5 and State Route 13 in Phil Campbell.

[edit] Route description

The southern terminus of SR 237 is located at its intersection with State Route 172 east of Hackleburg. From this point, the route travels in a northeasterly direction to its northern terminus at its intersection with State Route 5 and State Route 13 in Phil Campbell.

[edit] Counties along the route

  • Marion County, Alabama
  • Franklin County, Alabama

[edit] Communities along the route

  • Phil Campbell
  • Shady Grove

[edit] Junctions with other routes

  • State Route 5 in Phil Campbell
  • State Route 13 in Phil Campbell
  • State Route 241 north of Shady Grove
  • State Route 172 east of Hackleburg
