State Route 227 or SR 227 is a 15 mile long route that serves as a connection between the DeKalb/Etowah county line south of Crossville with U.S. Route 431 at Guntersville in Marshall County.

[edit] Route description

The southern terminus of SR 227 is located at the DeKalb/Etowah county line to the southwest of Hendrixville. From Hendrixville, the route travels in a northwesterly direction in route to Crossville. From Crossville, it travels in a northerly direction to Geraldine where it again resumes its northwesterly track.

Upon entering Marshall County, it takes a more westerly route before turning towards the south in route to its northern terminus at U.S. Route 431 in downtown Guntersville.
