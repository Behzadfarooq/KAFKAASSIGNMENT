Alabama locations by per capita income

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Alabama has the thirty-eighth highest per capita income in the United States of America, at $18,189 (2000). Its personal per capita income is $26,338 (2003), the fortieth highest in the country. Its median household income is $34,135 (2000), ranked forty-second in the country, and its median family income is $41,657 (2000), the forty-second highest in the country. The median value of an owner-occupied housing unit is $85,100 (2000), ranked fourtieth in the country.
