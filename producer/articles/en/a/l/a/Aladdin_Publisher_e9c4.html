Aladdin Paperbacks is a book publisher, a division of Simon & Schuster. This paperback imprint is aimed at readers aged 4 to 12 years old. New titles include the Henry and Mudge series by Cynthia Rylant and Sucie Stevenson. Aladdin also reprints hardcover books from Simon & Schuster's catalog in paperback editions. These include the picture books Strega Nona by Tomie dePaola and Chicka Chicka Boom Boom by Bill Martin Jr., John Archambault, and Lois Ehlert. Other titles include From the Mixed-Up Files of Mrs. Basil E. Frankweiler by E. L. Konigsburg; Hatchet by Gary Paulsen; Frindle by Andrew Clements; the Shiloh trilogy by Phyllis Reynolds Naylor; part of the Hardy Boys series, written under the pen name Franklin W. Dixon; and the Bunnicula books by James Howe.
