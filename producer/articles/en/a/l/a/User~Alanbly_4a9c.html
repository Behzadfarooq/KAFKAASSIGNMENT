[edit] Biographical Information

I was born at Poudre Valley Hospital in Fort Collins Colorado, where my mother works. I lived near Loveland in Larimer County until college, when I moved to Golden in Jefferson County. I attended Carrie Martin Elemantary School (The Comanches) then Bill Reed Middle School (The Redskins then Warriors for Political Correctness) then Loveland High School (The Indians). This was all in the Thompson R2-J School District, where my father works. I moved on to study Computer Science at the Colorado School of Mines where I currently attent as an Undergraduate and a Graduate student. My Bachelor of Science is in Mathematics and Computer Science with an area of special interest in Medieval Weaponry and Blacksmithing from the Metallurgy and Materials Engineering Department, and my Master of Science is in Applied Algorithms and Computer graphics. I will graduate with the BS in May 2007 and the MS in December 2007.
