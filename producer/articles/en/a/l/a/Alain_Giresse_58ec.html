Alain Giresse

Alain Giresse (born August 2, 1952 in Langoiran, Gironde) was a French professional football (soccer) midfielder. He played for the France national football team in the 1982 FIFA World Cup (fourth place) and the 1986 FIFA World Cup (third place). He also was a member of the Euro 84 winning team.

[edit] Playing career

  • 1970-1986 : Girondins de Bordeaux
  • 1986-1988 : Olympique de Marseille
  • 582 matches in Ligue 1, 164 goals
  • 47 caps, 6 goals

[edit] Coaching career

  • 1995 - 1998 : Toulouse FC
  • 1998 : Paris Saint-Germain
  • 1999 - 2000 : Toulouse FC
  • 2001 - 2003 : FAR Rabat (Morocco)
  • 2004 - 2005 : Georgia national football team
  • 2006 - : Gabon national football team
