[edit] Fair use for Avril Lavigne

The image Image:Alavigneheather.PNG is being linked here; though the picture is originally copyright, I CyberGhostface believe this image is covered by the U.S. fair use laws because:

  1. It is a copy of a photograph released and intended by Dreamworks for promotional distribution;
  2. No free or public domain images have been located for this purpose. While public domain images may exist for the subject of the article, none has been located that is recent, nor do they adequately illustrate the points in the subject's career that are discussed in the accompanying text;
  3. The image does not limit the copyright owners' rights to distribute the film; and
  4. The image is being used for informational purposes only, and its use is not believed to detract from the subject or her work in any way.
