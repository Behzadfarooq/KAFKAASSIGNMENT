Alan Walker (anthropologist)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Professor Alan Walker, is the Distinguished Professor of Anthropology and Biology at Pennsylvania State University. He received a MacArthur Fellowship in 1988.[1] He is chairman of Community Health Sheffield NHS Trust and is a patron of the National Pensioners Convention.[citation needed]
