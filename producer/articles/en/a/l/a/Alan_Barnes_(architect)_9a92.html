Alan Barnes was a Northern Ireland architect. In 1977-79 he served as President of the Royal Society of Ulster Architects (RIBA Northern Ireland).

[edit] Travelling Scholarship

After his death in 1987, his family, together with the RSUA and Queen's University Belfast, instituted a travelling scholarship in his memory.
