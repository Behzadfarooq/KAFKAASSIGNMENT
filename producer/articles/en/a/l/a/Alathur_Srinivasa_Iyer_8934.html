Alathur Srinivasa Iyer

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Alathur Srinivasa Iyer (1911 - 1980) was an Indian vocalist from Karnataka. Together with Sivasubramania Iyer he formed the successful duo known as the Alathur brothers, though the two were not in fact brothers. A student of Alathur Venkartesa Iyer, Srinivasa gave his first performances when he was ten years old. From 1944 to 1968 he was a court musician for the Maharajah of Travancore.
