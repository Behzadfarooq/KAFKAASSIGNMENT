Alan Fitzgerald, U.S. musician (both a bassist and a keyboardist), was a member of several U.S. bands.

In the mid 1970s, he appeared on Montrose albums as a bassist, and later on Sammy Hagar solo albums as a keyboardist.

In 1979 he teamed up again with Ronnie Montrose, appearing on Gamma's first album.

In the 1980s, he was the keyboardist for Night Ranger.
