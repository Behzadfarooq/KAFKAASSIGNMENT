State Route 275, also known as SR 275, is a numbered state highway in Alabama. Present-day SR 275 is a western bypass route of Talladega. The southern terminus of the route is at the intersection with Alabama State Route 21 southwest of the city, and the northern terminus is at the intersection with Alabama State Route 77. Commissioned in 1990, State Route 275 is the second incarnation of what was originally the designation for the entire Little River Canyon Rim Parkway.
