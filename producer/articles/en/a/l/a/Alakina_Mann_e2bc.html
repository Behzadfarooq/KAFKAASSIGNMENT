Alakina Mann

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Alakina Mann (born August 1, 1990 in Surrey, UK) is an English actress. She is notable for playing Anne in The Others and also appeared in the 2003 movie Girl with a Pearl Earring. She also appeared in the television interpretation of Fungus the Bogeyman.

[edit] Selected Filmography

  • Girl with a Pearl Earring (2003)
  • The Others (2001)

[edit] Trivia

  • Is vegetarian
  • Has an interest in art and enjoys drawing
  • Is a fan of British band The Kooks
  • Has one cat, Simba, and one dog, Kipling
  • Likes to go by the name "Ali" or "Alisaurus Rex"
  • Has very pale hair on her eyelashes and legs.
