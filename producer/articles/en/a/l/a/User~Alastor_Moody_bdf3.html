    Examples: Tropical Depression Two-E (2006; E. Pacific), Tropical Depression 19 (2005; Atlantic)
  • Tropical Storm status
    Examples: Tropical Storm Bret (2005; Atlantic), Tropical Storm Aletta (2006; E. Pacific), Tropical Storm Jelawet (2006; W. Pacific)
  • Severe Tropical Storm status
    Examples: Tropical Storm Delta (2005; Atlantic), Tropical Storm Chris (2006; Atlantic), Tropical Storm Emilia (2006; E. Pacific)

    Examples: Hurricane Ophelia (2005; Atlantic), Hurricane Epilson (2005; Atlantic), Hurricane Carlotta (2006; E. Pacific)

    Examples: Hurricane Irene (2005; Atlantic), Typhoon Kaemi (2006; W. Pacific)
  • Major Hurricane (Category 3) status
    Examples: Hurricane Jeanne (2004; Atlantic), Hurricane Beta (2005; Atlantic), Hurricane Bud (2006; E. Pacific)
  • Major Hurricane (Category 4) status
    Examples: Hurricane Frances (2004; Atlantic), Hurricane Dennis (2005; Atlantic), Hurricane Daniel (2006 E. Pacifc)
  • Super Hurricane (Category 5) status
    Examples: Hurricane Ivan (2004; Atlantic), Hurricane Emily (2005; Atlantic), Super Typhoon Saomai (2006; W. Pacific)
  • Super Hurricane (Category 6) status
    Examples: Huricane Rita (2005; Atlantic), Hurricane Wilma (2005; Atlantic)
