Alan Murphy (October 3, 1981-) is an Irish footballer playing in his second spell for Galway United in the League of Ireland

He plays in the forward position and joined Galway from Derry City FC before the 2006 season began. He had been at Derry for two seasons since 2004 making 59 appearances and scoring 11 goals. Prior to this, he had his first period at Galway where he was a prolific goal scorer and a crowd favourite. Murphy was top goal scorer for three seasons running before he joined Derry in 2004.
