Alan Kelly (drummer)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Alan Kelly is a Liverpool-based drummer, who played with Ooberman from 1997 until 2000. He played on all Ooberman releases up to and including the "The Magic Treehouse" album, and is also featured on some tracks released thereafter.
