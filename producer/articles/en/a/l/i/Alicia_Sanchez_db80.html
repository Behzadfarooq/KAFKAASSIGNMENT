Alicia Sanchez is a model who worked with Playboy in the early to mid-1990s.

[edit] Appearances in Playboy special editions

  • Playboy's Book of Lingerie Vol. 12 March 1990.
  • Playboy's Book of Lingerie Vol. 15 September 1990.
  • Playboy's Book of Lingerie Vol. 17 January 1991 - cover.
  • Playboy's Sisters March 1992 (posing with her sister Olga).
  • Playboy's Book of Lingerie Vol. 31 May 1993.
  • Playboy's Girls of Summer '93 June 1993 - pages 53, 93.
  • Playboy's Nudes December 1993 - pages 35, 84.
  • Playboy's Book of Lingerie Vol. 35 January 1994.
  • Playboy's Book of Lingerie Vol. 36 March 1994.
  • Playboy's Book of Lingerie Vol. 39 September 1994 - pages 92-93.
