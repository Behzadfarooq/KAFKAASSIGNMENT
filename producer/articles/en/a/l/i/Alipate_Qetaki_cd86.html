Alipate Qetaki

Alipate Qetaki is a Fijian lawyer and politician, who served as Attorney-General and Minister for Justice in the interim Cabinet formed by Laisenia Qarase in the wake of the Fiji coup of 2000. He held office till an elected government took power in September 2001.

He is currently the executive chairman of both the Fiji Law Reform Commission (since 2003) and the Fiji Law Revision Commission.
