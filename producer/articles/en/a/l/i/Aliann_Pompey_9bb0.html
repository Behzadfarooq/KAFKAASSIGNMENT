Aliann Pompey (born 9 March 1978) is a Guyanese sprinter who specializes in short track distance.

Born : 09/03/1978 in Height : 1m65 cm Weight : 55 kg Nationality : Guyanese Speciality : 400m

She participated at the World Championships in Athletics in 2001, 2003 and 2005 as well as the IAAF World Indoor Championships in 2001, 2003 and 2006 and the Summer Olympics in 2000 and 2004, without reaching the final.

Her personal best time is 50.93 seconds, achieved in July 2004 in Madrid.
