Ali Ansarian (Persian: علی انصاریان ‎ , born July 5, 1977) is an Iranian football defender. He is currently playing for Iran Professional League champions, Esteghlal F.C..

[edit] Club career

After playing for Persepolis F.C. for several seasons, in July 2006 he joined the club's fierce rival, Esteghlal.

[edit] International career

Ali Ansarian has had a few short call-ups to the national team. He made his debut for Iran in October 1998 against Kuwait. He was recently called up to join Team Melli again for the 2007 Asian Cup qualifications.
