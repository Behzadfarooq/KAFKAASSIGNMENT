Ali ibn al-Madini (161 AH [1] - 234 AH [2]) was a well known Sunni Islamic scholar that contributed to the Science of hadith.

[edit] Name

(Arabic: أبو الحسن علي بن عبد الله المديني)

[edit] Works

  • The Book of Knowledge about the Companions (Arabic: Kitab al-Ma'rifat al-Sahaba) [4]
