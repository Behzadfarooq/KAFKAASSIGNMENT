User:Aliman

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

My name is Aliman Sears. My wife Mirnah Sears and I live in Honolulu, Hawaii, where I am a psychiatric social worker and teacher. Mirnah is an artist, working mainly in Jewelry. I recently left a position where I ran a statewide Supported Employment Program for Steadfast Housing Development Corporation, which is a private non-profit that serves the mentally ill (those challenged by Schizophrenia, bipolar disorder, etc.). I have worked with the Mentally ill and Developmentally disabled populations for about 18 years.

Currently, I am Chief Operating Officer of our newly formed social work company, Community Empowerment Services, a private company which will contract with the State of HI Dept. of Health. We're slated to begin providing case managment services to the mentally ill in July 2007.

I am a CPRP (Certified Psychiatric Rehabilitation Practitioner) and I hold a Master's degree in Philosophy from Katholieke Universiteit Leuven, Belgium. I teach philosophy, ethics, and logic/critical thinking at Chaminade University and TransPacific Hawaii College in Honolulu. My area of concentration in grad school, and my area of interest, is called Process philosophy. I'm also an 21-year member of Subud.
