Alive
Live DVD by Bury Your Dead
Released July 12, 2005
Recorded Chain Reaction on 5/5/05
Genre Metalcore
Label Victory Records
Bury Your Dead video chronology
Cover Your Tracks (2004) Alive (2005) Beauty And The Breakdown (2006)

Alive is a concert DVD release by metalcore band Bury Your Dead. It was filmed at Chain Reaction in Anaheim, CA on May 5, 2005.

[edit] Track listing

  1. "Top Gun"
  2. "Vanilla Sky"
  3. "Mission: Impossible"
  4. "Outsiders"
  5. "Sunday's Best"
  6. "Legend"
  7. "Color of Money"
  8. "33 RPM"
  9. "Eyes Wide Shut"
  10. "Camo Is My Favorite Color"
  11. "Magnolia"
  12. "Losin' It"
