Ali Akbar Nategh-Nouri

Hodjatoleslam Ali Akbar Nateq-Nouri was the speaker of the Majles of Iran. Before that, he was the interior minister of the Islamic Republic[1].

He ran to become the president of Iran[2] for the Iranian presidential election in 1997.
