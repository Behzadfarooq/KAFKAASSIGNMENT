Alin Ilie Minteuan (born 12 November 1976) is a Romanian footballer who currently plays for Romanian first league side CFR 1907 Cluj. Minteuan's first team was CFR Cluj, where he went on to play for Universitatea Cluj, Rapid Bucharest, and Gloria Bistrita. He is a right midfielder or a central defensive midfielder.
