Alien Crush

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

You have new messages (last change).

Alien Crush was a pinball videogame released for the TurboGrafx-16. It was followed by a sequel, Devil's Crush. It was re-released on December 11, 2006 in America and December 15, 2006 in Europe for the Wii's Virtual Console.
