Alien Baby is an internet guide and search engine that provides free search services and articles. Alien Baby went online June 10 1997 and was the creation of a professional Hollywood Art Director, Brad Hartmaier. Mr. Hartmaier was working in Hollywood as an art director for television commercials, film, print and music videos. He was walking down Hollywood boulevard one afternoon when he thought of the idea for alien baby. He admits it was like a strange awakening and life direction change. The art direction world had changed and everything was going digital and thats when the internet hit. Mr. Hartmaier quickly realized the potential of the web and directed all his talents in computers, design and graphics with no turning back.

The road was very difficult at that time due to the newness of the web and it's lack of documentaion and or training. Mr. Hartmaier went to UCLA and inquired about classes in web design but none were currently available. After scouring bookstores and computer stores he started learning html. He has said many times that if he had to do it all over again he would have become a doctor because of the internets steep learning curve. After 12 years working on the web he says he finally feels like he's got a little bit of a handle on whats going on. Ah, but things change...

Back in June of 2007 Mr. Hartmaier decided to create his first website www.alienbaby.com and the rest is history. Brad is now a sucessful web developer and designer working on variuos multi media projects, database driven websites ad business web development pprojects and applications.

When Mr. Hartmaier is asked what is an alienbaby, he replies "It's us, it's everything, it's everywhere, but there seem to be a few amoung us who fit the profile a little more than others. I believe it signifies people who are unique and special or stand out in the crowd. Achievers, doers, thinkers, it's whatever you want it to be, it's a beginning"
