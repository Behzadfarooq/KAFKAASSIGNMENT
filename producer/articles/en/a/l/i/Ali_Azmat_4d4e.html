Ali Azmat (Urdu: علی عظمت) born in 1970 in Abbottabad, Pakistan is the lead vocalist of the band Junoon and TV actor. Currently, he's pursuing a solo career, and has released a solo album called Social Circus. Ali Azmat is a BBA from Sydney, Australia.

Ali Azmat is one of the most famous rock vocalists in Pakistan. Azmat's major popularity comes from his band, Junoon. He started his music career with the band Jupiters. After the Jupiters, Azmat joined Junoon at age 21. After Junoon's 7th studio album "Dewaar", Azmat released a solo album called Social Circus in late 2005. It was considered a success and has won some major music awards in Pakistan. Ali Azmat's song Na Re Na was a big hit , its video is considered as the most creative and mind blowing work done in Pakistan.Ali Azmat remains on tour almost all the year. He is a good friend of Ali Noor , frontman of the band "Noori". Ali Azmat hosts a show "Pappu Yar Tang Na Kar" for Agg television. Ali Azmat also starred in a telefilm "ChaalBaz" in 2006 which was aired on ARY Digital.

Ali Azmat owns a Harley Davidson and Bmw and lives in a flat in clifton , karachi. He loves to collect masks and has a collection of masks . Ali Azmat was rumoured to be in relationship with Pakistani Top Model Vaneeza Ahmad.

Today, Azmat is one of biggest figures in the Pakistani music industry. Ali Azmat has started working on his second solo album, which is due in 2007.
