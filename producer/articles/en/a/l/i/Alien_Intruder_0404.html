Alien Intruder

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Alien Intruder is a 1993 action/science fiction film by Nick Stone and Ricardo Jacques Gale. It starred Maxwell Caulfield, Tracy Scoggins, Billy Dee Williams, Gary Roberts, Richard Cody, and Stephen Davies. This film relied heavily on Billy Dee Williams' sci-fi reputation from the Star Wars series in its marketing despite his minor role in Alien Intruder.

[edit] Plot synopsis

Spoiler warning: Plot and/or ending details follow.

A group of convicts are led on a mission into uncharted deep space by Williams to salvage a lost ship. As incentive to go on this dangerous mission, the convicts are given the opportunity to spend their weekends in a virtual reality world where they could live their sexual fantasies with any woman they chose. However, a beautiful vixen played by Scoggins appears during the convicts' weekends unexpectedly, driving them all murderously insane in fighting over her.
