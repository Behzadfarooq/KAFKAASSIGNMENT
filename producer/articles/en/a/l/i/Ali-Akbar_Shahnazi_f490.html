Ali Akbar Shahnazi

Ali Akbar Shahnazi, (1897 – 1986 Tehran) was a Persian musician and tar player.

He was son and best student of Mirza Hossein Gholi. He collected and performed his father's radif.

He also composed his own Radife Aali. He had a unique style of playing and taught some of the best musicias in Iran.
