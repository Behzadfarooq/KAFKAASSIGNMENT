Ali Clewer is the drummer of British band Morning Runner. He is known for his individual, aggressive drumming style and was a founding member of the band. He is the only unmarried member, often prompting rumours concerning his sexuality.[citation needed] He is currently working on a second album with the rest of Morning Runner.
