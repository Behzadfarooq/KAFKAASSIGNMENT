Aliya Wolf (born January 17, 1975 in Stephenville, Texas) is an American model.

In 1994, she was crowned Miss Houston USA. The same year she worked as a spokesmodel for Star Search. Soon after that, she got married. She gave birth to her daughter Zahra in 2000. After getting divorced, Wolf tried out for Playboy scouts when they were in Texas searching for models for The 50th Anniversary Playmate Hunt. She appeared in the accompanying pictorial in the December 2003 issue and was chosen to be Playmate of the Month for February 2004.
