Alicja Bachleda

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Alicja Bachleda Aka Alicja Bachleda-Curuś (born May 12, 1983 in Tampico, Tamaulipas, Mexico) Polish actor and singer.

Played lead parts in such major movies as: "Trade" (USA - 2006), "Das Blut der Templer"(Has an English version - "Blood of the Templars") and "Sommersturm" (Germany- 2004), "Herz über Kopf" (Germany - 2001), for which Hans-Christian Schmid and Michael Gutmann wrote the screenplay, and "Pan Tadeusz" (Poland - 1999) directed by Andrzej Wajda.
