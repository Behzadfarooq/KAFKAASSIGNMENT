Ali Sabieh is a region in Djibouti. Its capital is Ali Sabieh. Other major cities and towns include Holhol. Ali Sabieh borders Arta Region to the north, Somaliland (Somalia) to the east, Ethiopia to the south and the Dikhil Region to the west.
