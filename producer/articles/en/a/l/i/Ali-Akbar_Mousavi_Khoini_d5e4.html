Ali Akbar Mousavi Khoini (Persian: علی اکبر موسوی خویینی‎ ) was one of the Reformist members of 6th Session of Iranian Majlis. Before becaming Majlis representative in 1999, he was one of leaders of Iranian Student Movemenet.

On June 12, 2006, during the Iranian women protest in 7tir Sqaure of Tehran, he was arrested and imprisoned by Iranian Police Forces.
