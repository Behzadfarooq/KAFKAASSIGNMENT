[edit] Main albums

  1. Pretties for You (1969) (US #193)
  2. Easy Action (1970)
  3. Love It to Death (1971) (US #35, UK #28)

  5. School's Out (1972) (US #2, UK #4)
  6. Billion Dollar Babies (1973) (US #1, UK #1)
  7. Muscle of Love (1973) (US #10, UK #34)
  8. Welcome to My Nightmare (1975) (US #5, UK #19)

  10. Lace and Whiskey (1977) (US #43, UK #33)
  11. From the Inside (1978) (US #60, UK #61)

  14. Zipper Catches Skin (1982) (did not chart)
  15. DaDa (1983) (UK #93; did not chart in the US)
  16. Constrictor (1986) (US # 59, UK # 41)
  17. Raise Your Fist and Yell (1987) (US # 73, UK # 48)
  18. Trash (1989) (US #20, UK #2)
  19. Hey Stoopid (1991) (US #47, UK # 4)
  20. The Last Temptation (1994) (US #68, UK # 6)
  21. Brutal Planet (2000) (UK #38)
  22. Dragontown (2001)
  23. The Eyes of Alice Cooper (2003)
  24. Dirty Diamonds (2005)
  25. Along Came A Spider (2007)
