Alisa Southerncross is a fictional character from the anime/manga series Sgt. Frog.

[edit] Overview

Alisa is an undead being who feasts on aliens to regain her humanity. Her adoptive father is the black alien who stole her humanity. She befriends the Keroro Platoon after Fuyuki protects her despite what she had done.

[edit] Appearance

Alisa has scarlet eyes and looks to be around 12-14 years old physically--like Fuyuki--yet has lived since the "olden days" due to her lost humanity. Her father takes on the form of her bow.

[edit] Relationships

[edit] Fuyuki Hinata

When she appears, Alisa always associates herself with Fuyuki, then begins to have feelings for him Much to the anger of Momoka Nishizawa.

[edit] Nevula ("Daddy")

Her living alien hairband. He is not really her father, but took her in as his daughter after he took her humanity.

[edit] References

Alisa's surname, "Southerncross," may be a reference to the anime series The Super Dimension Cavalry Southern Cross. It is also the common name for the constellation Crux.
