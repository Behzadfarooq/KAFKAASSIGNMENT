Ali Ezzine

Bronze 2000 Sydney 3000 m s'chase

Ali Ezzine (born 3 September 1978 in Beni M'Tir Nord) is a Morroccan athlete who specializes in the 3000 metres steeplechase.

He won the bronze medal in this event at the 2000 Summer Olympics held in Sydney, Australia.
