HRH Princess Alix Marie Anna Antoinette Charlotte Gabriell, The Princess of Ligne, Princess of Luxembourg, Nassau and Parma (born August 24, 1929) is the daughter of Charlotte, Grand Duchess of Luxembourg and Felix of Bourbon-Parma

On August 17, 1950 Princess Alix married Antoine Maria Joachim Lamoral, 13th Prince de Ligne, (March 8, 1925 - August 21, 2005). They resided at Château de Beloeil, Belgium where their children were born:

  1. Michel de Ligne, 14th Prince de Ligne (b. May 26, 1951) - In 1981 he married Dona Eleanora de Orléans-Bragança e Wittelsbach
  2. Wauthier, Prince de Ligne (b. July 10, 1952) - In 1976 he married Regine Gräfin von Renesse
  3. Anne-Marie, Princesse de Ligne (b. April 3, 1954) - In 1981 she married Olivier Mortgat
  4. Christine, Princesse de Ligne (b. August 11, 1955) - In 1981 she married Dom António de Orléans-Bragança e Wittelsbach
  5. Sophie, Princesse de Ligne (b. April 23, 1957) - In 1982 she married Comte Philippe de Nicolay
  6. Antoine, Prince de Ligne (b. December 28, 1959)
  7. Yolande, Princesse de Ligne (b. June 16, 1964) - In 1994 she married Hugo Townsend, son of G. Capt. Peter Townsend
