Ali Mosaffa

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Ali Mosaffa (Persian: علی مصفا ‎ , born 1 December 1966 in Tehran) is an Iranian actor.

[edit] Career

He has played in movies such as Pari (1995), Leila(1996), and Borj-e Minoo (1996). In 2005, he directed his first film called Sima-ye zani dar doordast (Portrait of a Lady Far Away).

[edit] Personal life

He is married to Iranian actress Leila Hatami.
