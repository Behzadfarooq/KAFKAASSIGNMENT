Alien Express (aka Dead Rail) is a 2005 made-for-TV horror movie starring Lou Diamond Phillips, Amy Locane, Barry Corbin, and Todd Bridges. It was directed by Turi Meyer and shown for the first time on August 13, 2005 on the Sci Fi Channel.

[edit] Plot

Spoiler warning: Plot and/or ending details follow.

A new bullet train is speeding passengers to Las Vegas, along with a US Senator from Texas running for president, when a meteorite crashes into a near by car releasing a tiny creature. The train stops to survery the damage, and the police are called. This stop allows the creature to board the train before it once again speeds off. Once it kills and consumes everyone on board the train, it begins to grow and multiply into many differing creatures.
