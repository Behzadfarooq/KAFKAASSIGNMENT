Alice SOS(アリスＳＯＳ) is anime series directed by Shingo Kaneko. It is aired on 6 April 1998 - 28 January 1999.

Spoiler warning: Plot and/or ending details follow.

[edit] Story

Takashi loves reading books and especially loves "Alice's Adventures in Wonderland". One day, he buy used books, and he find a book thrown in "God" in those books.

When Takashi open the book, "the God of Math" M-1 appeared. Takashi is told that Alice is kidnapped, and asked to rescue her by M-1.

And Takashi goes adventure in wonderland to rescue Alice.

[edit] Characters

  • Takashi Sagano(嵯峨野タカシ) - Takashi is a junior high school student, and a big fan of "Alice in Wonderland". He is asked to rescue Alice by M-1, he goes adventure to do it. His ability is average, but his brave heart is grater than other member.
  • Hiromi Sugita(杉田ひろみ) - Hiromi is a neighbor of Takashi, and classmate of him. She heard Takashi goes adventure, she decides following him. She is a rowdy. But she is worried about Takashi, because he often do dangerous things (and she loved Takashi).
  • Toshio Kazumi(和美トシオ) - Toshio is a friend of Takashi. He loves girls. To start adventure, adventurer must solve a problem in Math, so Takashi asks to help him. But his ability is worth than Takashi, he is no use. He follows Takashi too.
  • Yukari Ashikawa(芦川ゆかり) - Yukari is brought by Toshio. Her behavior is very well. But when she is excited, she uses rough language. She is very clever, and solves almost all questions. She follows Takashi too.
  • M-1 - The God of Math. He asks Takashi to rescue Alice. During Takashi's adventure, he helps them with magic 3 times per world. But he sometimes fails the magic.
  • Namekuji-neko(なめくじネコ) - Namekuji-neko is a mysterious life. Its figure seems like a cat, but only its body seems like slug. And it always floats. It follows Takashi as a guide of wonderland. But it doesn't know essential thing, and it often escapes when Takashi's pinch, it is no use.
  • Nisimura-kun(西村くん) - Nishimura-kun is mysterious character. He speaks like women. Takashi often meets disguised Nishimura-kun, we cannot know what he do. And we cannot know he is friend of Takashi or not, because his advise sometimes rescues Takashi, but sometimes entraps.
  • Eiichiro Shono(翔野鋭一朗) - Shono is a classmate of Takashi. He doesn't have friend. He is possessed by devil, and he kidnaps Alice. He is fat, but he is handsome boy during possessed by devil.
  • Alice(アリス) - The heroine of "Alice in Wonderland". She is kidnapped, and transefered several worlds.
