Alison Folland (born August 10, 1978 in Boston, Massachusetts) is an actress. She tried out for the role in 1995's To Die For as a joke, having never acted previously, but she landed the part.[citation needed]

She was nominated in 1997 for the Independent Spirit Award for Best Female Lead in All Over Me.

She's also had supporting roles in films such as Good Will Hunting and Boys Don't Cry.
