Ali Amiri Khodamorad (Persian: علی امیری‎ , born March 21, 1988 in ?, Iran) is an Iranian football player who plays in the Midfield position. He is currently a member of the IPL club, Foolad FC.

[edit] Club career

Amiri as of 2006 plays for Foolad FC, although he is yet to make a league appearance he made an appearance as a substitute in the Asian Champions League against Al-Ittihad of Syria.

[edit] International career

Amiri is currently part of the Iran national under-19 football team and Iran national under-23 football team.
