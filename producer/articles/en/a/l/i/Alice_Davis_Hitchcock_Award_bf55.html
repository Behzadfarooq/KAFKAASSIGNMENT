Alice Davis Hitchcock Award

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

You have new messages (last change).

The Alice Davis Hitchcock Award, established in 1949, annually recognizes "the most distinguished work of scholarship in the history of architecture published by a North American scholar." [1]
