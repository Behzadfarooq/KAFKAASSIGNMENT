Alice et June is the tenth studio album by French band Indochine. It was released in France on December 19, 2005 in both single-disc and limited edition two disc editions.

  5. "Ladyboy" - 4:47

  8. "Adora" - 4:05
  9. "Un Homme Dans La Bouche" - 4:36
  10. "June" - 5:04
  11. "Sweet Dreams" - 4:59

  13. "Talulla" - 2:53
