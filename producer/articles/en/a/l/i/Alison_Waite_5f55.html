Alison Waite (b. November 10, 1981 in Los Altos, California) is an American model and student. Waite appeared on the cover of May 2006 issue of Playboy which focused on Top 10 Party Schools in the U.S. After being considered among the 63 college students who were photographed for the issue as "a subject worthy of advanced study", Waite was offered the Playmate of the Month pictorial in the same issue.

Prior to posing for Playboy, Waite was a graduate student from San Diego State University (SDSU) with a specialization in gerontology. She attended Inglemoor High School in Kenmore, Washington. After the pictorial, she decided to put her studies on hold for a year in order to try her hand at being a Playmate.[citation needed]

On May 26, 2006 she was actiontrip.com's "babe of the day".

She was named one of the two finalists for 2007 Playmate of the Year.[1]
