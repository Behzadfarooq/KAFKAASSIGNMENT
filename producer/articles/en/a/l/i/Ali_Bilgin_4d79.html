Ali Bilgin (born December 17, 1981 in Essen) is a German-Turkish professional football player. He currently plays for Turkish side Antalyaspor. His former club is Rot-Weiss Essen. Ali Bilgin is noticable for his speed and unexpected goals.

Bilgin has made his debut for Antalyaspor in Sakaryaspor match on 13 August 2006 as a 66th min substitute.
