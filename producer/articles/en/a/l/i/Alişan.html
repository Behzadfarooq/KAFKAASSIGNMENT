Alisan (Alişan) (b. June 19, 1976 / Istanbul) is a Turkish singer.

His real name is Serkan. His ability of singing was discovered by his parents at the earliest stages of his life. At the age of two, he could sing long and hard traditional Turkish songs by heart.

At high school he recorded his first album. In 1997 his first hit album "Sana Birsey Olmasın" was released. The song entitled "Krali Gelse" from this album became No:1 at Turkish Bilboards.
