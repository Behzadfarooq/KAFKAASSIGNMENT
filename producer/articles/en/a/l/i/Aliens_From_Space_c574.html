Aliens from Space: The Real Story of Unidentified Flying Objects is a (1973) book by Donald Keyhoe.

In the book, Keyhoe presents evidence which he argues supports the notion that the United States Air Force had secretly engaged in over 3,000 aerial sorties against alien spacecraft.
