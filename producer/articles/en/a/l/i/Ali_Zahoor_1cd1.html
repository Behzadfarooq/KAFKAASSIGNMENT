Ali Zahoor

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Ali Zahoor is a child actor, most famous for his role as Anish Mistry, in the BBC soap opera Eastenders.

More recently, he was the child chosen to 'act' as a caller in the infamous Blue Peter phone-in scandal.[1]
