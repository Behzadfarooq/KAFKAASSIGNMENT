Alice's Inferno is the debut album by the Spanish band Forever Slave, released on September 26, 2005. It followed three demos; Hate (2000), Schwarzer Engel (2001) and Resurrection (2004). It is a concept album, dealing with the life of Alice, after her parents have deceased. Like a lot of gothic metal, death vocals and Gregorian chanting is used, as well as a very high proportion of symphonic orchestral elements. Lyrics are akin to gothic imagery, linking to Alice's story: death, despair, darkness, spirits, temptation and hell.
