Alison Elizabeth MacInnis (born March 24, 1980) is an American actress best known for her role as Dana Mitchell, the Pink Lightspeed Ranger, on the television series Power Rangers: Lightspeed Rescue.

Besides her role in Power Rangers: Lightspeed Rescue, she has also worked in other television series, like Veronica Mars and Tremors.
