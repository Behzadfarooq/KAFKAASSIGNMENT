Alioune Touré

Alioune Touré (born 9 September 1978 in Saint-Denis) is a French-born Malian football striker who currently, as at June 2006, plays for União Leiria.

[edit] Clubs

  • 1996-2001: FC Nantes Atlantique
  • 2001-2002: Manchester City F.C.
  • 2002-2004: Paris Saint-Germain
  • 2004-2005: EA Guingamp
  • 2005-present: UD Leiria
