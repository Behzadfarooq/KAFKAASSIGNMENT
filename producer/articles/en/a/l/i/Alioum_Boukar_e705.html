Alioum Boukar (born January 3, 1972) is a Cameroonian football goalkeeper, who played for several clubs, including Canon Yaoundé, Samsunspor and Istanbulspor, both in Turkey. Currently he plays for Altay in Turkish 2nd level league.

He played for Cameroon national football team and was a participant at the 1998 FIFA World Cup and the 2002 FIFA World Cup.
