Allison Marie Courtney (born November 27, 1985) is a Canadian film, television and theatre actress. In 2006, Pill starred as Grace Webster in the short-lived NBC drama The Book of Daniel, and was nominated for a Tony Award for her performance in The Lieutenant of Inishmore.

She was born in Toronto, Ontario, Canada and attended Vaughan Road Academy (high school) in Toronto.
