"Alias Mr. Hackenbacker" is the 3rd episode of the second series of the Supermarionation television series Thunderbirds. It was originally broadcast on ATV Midlands in the United Kingdom on 16 October 1966. It was written by Alan Pattillo and directed by Desmond Saunders.
