Ali Akyuz is a documentary and corporate film director. [[1]] Ali Akyüz was born in 1970 in Turkey. He graduated from Istanbul University, Faculty of Communications, Department of Radio-Television and Cinema.

Ali joined the industry in 1990, is a trained and highly experienced Director and has been Directing since 1993. He worked at STUDIO GAMA -FILMA - FIRST DIGITAL ATV and BIRTV for seven years before deciding to become fully freelance. He has shot on nearly every format available-Commercials, Corporate Videos, Documentaries, Pop Promos, Programmes, Live TV,Programme Trailers and Infomercials- for both Production Companies and TV Channels.

Ali's freelance work has taken him to international experiences Argentina, Brasil, Russia, USA, Yugoslavia, Greece, Georgia, Azerbaijan, Bulgaria, France, Belgium, UK and Germany.
