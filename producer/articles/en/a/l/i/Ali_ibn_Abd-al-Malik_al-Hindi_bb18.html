Nur al-Din Ali ibn Abd-al-Malik Husam al-Din al-Muttaqi al-Hindi (The light of faith, Ali son of servant of The King, from India) (died 1590 CE/975 AH) was a Sunni Muslim Islamic scholar who is know for writing [[Kanz al-Ummal][1]].
