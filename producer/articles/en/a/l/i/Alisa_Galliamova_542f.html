Alisa Galliamova

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Alisa Galliamova (born 18 January 1972) is a Russian chess player, who is of Tatar background. She holds the FIDE titles Woman Grandmaster and International Master. In 1988 she won the World Junior Girls Chess Championship.

In March 2006, she reached the finals of the FIDE Women's World Chess Championship, competing against Xu Yuhua. [1]
