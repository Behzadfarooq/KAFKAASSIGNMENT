Alisha Rules The World is the debut album of British duo Alisha's Attic, released on November 11, 1996. The album received positive reviews, and went platinum in the following year. It sold 400,000 copies in the UK (with another 100,000 in Japan), and the four singles all went into the top twenty in the British charts.
