[edit] PPE...

Hi Aliwalla. Regarding these edits: I don't think UCT's program is notable enough for detailed inclusion in the article. UCT is not all that well known internationally and thus the level of detail you've added is a bit too much. Nobody, for instance, cares much about 325, cool as that course is. (Note: I did my PPE honours @ UCT - indeed if you're in third or fourth year I most likely tutored you - so I'm not biased here...). Mikker (...) 02:28, 15 September 2006 (UTC)

BTW... you might not want to provide your email as is on Wikipedia because spammers have automated programs that trawl the web for email addresses, so you'll get spammed very seriously if you keep it the way you have it. Make it myname AT gmail DOT com or something similar. Cheers, Mikker (...) 19:31, 17 September 2006 (UTC)
