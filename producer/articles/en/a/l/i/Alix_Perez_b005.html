Alix Perez is an up and coming Drum n Bass DJ and Producer originally from Belgium but currently based in North London. At just 21 years of age, Alix is shaking the foundations of the Drum and Bass scene with his fresh and soulful liquid funk sound. Alix already has forthcoming and current releases on established labels such as Liquid V, Bassbin, Creative Source, Horizons, Progress, Brigand, Shogun Ltd and Fokuz.

Alix started to DJ at the age of 14 and progressed into production at 17, collaborating with other hotly tipped artists such as Jenna G, Redeyes, Sabre and Specific. His inspirations include artists such as Calibre, Marcus Intalex, Ill logic and Raf, Artificial Intelligence and D Bridge.

Perez has a residency at the legendary "Movement" Drum and Bass night, is touring all around the UK and Europe and plays out regularly during the week. He describes his DJing style as "Eclectic. I play generally deep but dip into various aspects of drum & bass. I like to read the crowd and let the music breathe. I find mixing in the right musical keys essential. I basically see it as a journey."

Alix Perez's tunes are played regularly on radio shows such as BBC 1Xtra and he has been doing many mixes for radio and podcast. He is becoming more popular each day with strong support from long time respected DJs and producers such as Bailey, Fabio and Bryan Gee. Alix has been repped as "the one to watch" and "representing the future of Drum and Bass"

Alix has also been featured in popular Drum and Bass magazines- Knowledge and ATM magazine and is gaining strong exposure within the worldwide Drum and Bass community.
