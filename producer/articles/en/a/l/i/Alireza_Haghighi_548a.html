Alireza Haghighi (Persian: علیرضا حقیقی‎ , May 2, 1988 in Iran) is an Iranian footballer who plays for the Persepolis F.C. in Iran as a goalkeeper. On October 17, 2006 in a match against Saipa F.C. Haghighi saved a penalty against the legendary Ali Daei.

Haghighi is also part of Iran national under-23 football team.
