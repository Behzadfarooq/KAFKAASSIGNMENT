Alianza Futbol Club or as they are popularly known as Alianza, is a Salvadoran football team, currently one of the most popular and successful clubs in the nation. The team currently plays in the country's Primera División de Fútbol Profesional, which is the highest tier league in the country.

Alianza plays its home games at Estadio Cuscatlán in San Salvador, El Salvador.

Founded in 1960, Alianza was almost immediately successful, winning its first championships in the 1966 and 1967 Salvadoran seasons. Even more significantly, Alianza was the first Central American and Salvadoran club to win the CONCACAF Champions' Cup in 1967, and is currently one of only three Salvadoran teams to have done so.
