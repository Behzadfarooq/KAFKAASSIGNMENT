Ali Saleem (Urdu: علی سلیم) is a television host for current affairs television program in Pakistan on the Aaj TV network. Ali cross dresses as a woman and asks his prominent guests provocative questions. Ali wears a silk sari, women's dress, for Late Night Show With Begum Nawazish Ali. Saleem Ali, 27 years old, is bisexual, his father is retired colonel in Pakistan Army and his mother, a former government official.
