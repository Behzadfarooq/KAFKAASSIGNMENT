Alien vs. Predator (mobile game)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Alien vs. Predator
Developer(s) Elite Systems
Publisher(s) Elite Systems
Release date(s) 2005
Genre(s) Beat 'em up
Mode(s) Single player
Platform(s) Mobile phone

Alien vs. Predator is a side-view 2D beat 'em up game made by Elite Systems for a range of mobile phones in 2005.
