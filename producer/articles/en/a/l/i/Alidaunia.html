Alidaunia

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Alidaunia Servizio Helibus is an aviation company based in Foggia, Italy. It operates scheduled passenger services, air ambulance, air taxi/charter, offshore, aircraft maintenance and aerial work services. Its main base is Gino Lisa Airport, Foggia[1].

[edit] Code Data

  • IATA Code: D4
  • ICAO Code: LID
  • Callsign: Alida[2].

[edit] History

Alidaunia was established in March 1976 and in 1978 was licensed for film and photographic work. In 1984 it received a licence for public transport. With government support in 1985 it started scheduled services from Foggia to the Tremiti Islands[1]. In 1992 and 1993 it operated on the Foggia to Milan (Malpensa) route and from 1993 to 1995 on the Foggia to Parma route using a Mitsubishi MU 300 aircraft on both routes.

The airline is owned by Roberto Pucillo (general Manager)(55%), Roberto Manzo Rocco (20%), Paulo Giangrossi (15%), Michele Perrieone (5%)[1].

[edit] Destinations

Alidaunia operates the following services (at December 2006):[citation needed]

  • 1 Sikorsky S-76A
  • 1 Eurocopter BK 117 C1
  • On order: 2 AgustaWestland AW139
