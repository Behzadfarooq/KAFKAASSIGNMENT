[edit] WikiBio

I joined Wikipedia in May 2006 or before it (I forgot exactly when) but I made my first edit in 5 May 2006. (I created a link for a university. That time it was for me just a testing and when I made it I told myself WOW, it works!)

Then I forgot about Wikipedia, but I was using it for my project that was about Computer and Astronomy.

Later I became interested in horror films and by searching the internet I was always leading to Wikipedia.

And when I was founding films in Wikipedia I was wondering that there is a lot more in here than IMDB or any other place!

Science then I was using Wikipedia for movies that I like until I found a film, without any picture! I get angry and told myself why there is not a picture here? Why the info for the movie is so little?
