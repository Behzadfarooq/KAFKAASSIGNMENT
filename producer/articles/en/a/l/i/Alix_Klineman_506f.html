Alix Klineman is a volleyball player from Manhattan Beach, California.

A current high school senior, as a 6-4 Outside Hitter she is the #1 volleyball recruit for the class of 2007 as selected by PrepVolleyball.com.[citation needed]

[edit] Beach play

While maintaining her amateur status, Klineman has also participated in several AVP volleyball tour events[1] where her overall record is 2-3, though her best placement so far is 33rd.

[edit] Indoor play

In July of 2005, USA Girls Youth National Team coach Erikka Gulbranson said "The best player was clearly Alexandra Klineman. She is our youngest team player with the most consistent game" after a win over Tunisia in the 2005 FIVB Girls Under-18 World Championship. [2]

She currently plays club volleyball for the Mizuno Long Beach Volleyball Club[3] and high school volleyball at Mira Costa, California.

[edit] College

Hawaii, Stanford, Texas, UCLA, and Washington, all of which are currently ranked in the top-10 AVCA and Rich Kern volleyball polls, were among the candidates Klineman considered attending. On November 15, 2006, she officially signed with Stanford and is expected to star for the Cardinal in 2007.[4]
