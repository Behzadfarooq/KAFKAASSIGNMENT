Alice Practice is the debut release from Crystal Castles. It was released on Merok Records on 9th July 2006. The track "Air War" even garnered attention from prominent indie website Pitchfork media. [1]

[edit] Track listing

  1. "Alice Practice" – 2:43
  2. "Dolls" – 1:29
  3. "Air War" – 3:44
  4. "Love and Caring" – 2:18
