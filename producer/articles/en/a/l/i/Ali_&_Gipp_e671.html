Ali & Gipp are a rap duo which consists of Ali (born Ali Vaskabi-Waski-Jones in St. Louis, Missouri) of the St. Lunatics, and Gipp (born Cameron Gipp in Atlanta, Georgia) of Goodie Mob. They first appeared together on the single Grillz, by Nelly and Paul Wall. They are currently completing their new album Kinfolk which was supposed to be released on August 15th, but was pushed back to be released in 2007. There is also another group going by the name of "Kinfolk", it its not know whether or not they will keep the name or change it.
