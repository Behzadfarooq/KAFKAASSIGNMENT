Alive on Planet Earth is the first live album by progressive rock artists The Flower Kings. It was released in double-CD format in 2000.

  2. "Church of Your Heart" – 9:41
  3. "The Judas Kiss" – 15:43
  4. "Nothing New Under the Sun" – 4:13
  5. "The Lamb Lies Down on Broadway" (Genesis Cover) – 9:17

  2. "Sounds of Violence" – 6:38
  3. "Three Stories" – 6:05
