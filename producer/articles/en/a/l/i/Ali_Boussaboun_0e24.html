Ali Boussaboun (Arabic: علي بوسابون‎) (June 18, 1979 in Tangier, Morocco) is a Dutch-Moroccan football player, playing currently with Feyenoord Rotterdam and the Morocco national football team. Previously he served ADO Den Haag, FC Groningen and NAC Breda.
