Ali Pur(Punjnad)

Ali Pur is Subdivision of Muzaffargarh District , lies in the west of the Pakistani province of Punjab . In east of Ali Pur lies Punjnad Head Works at a distace of 10 Km where five rivers namely Satluj , Ravi , Chanab , Bias , and Indus (Sindhu river) are combined . Indus River is located in east of Ali Pur at a distance of about 12 Km, on the opposite bank of Sindhu River lies District Rajan Pur . In North, District Muzaffargarh at a distance of about 90 Km and District Bahawalpur in East at a distance of about 90 Km . Ali Pur comprises seven Union councel , Ghalwan-1 , Ghalwan-2 , Fateh Pur(Southern) , Chauki Gabol , Ali Wali , Khan Gharh ( Doma ) and Khairpur . There are four Police station in Sub Division Ali Pur .
