Ali Gibb

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Alistair Gibb (born February 17, 1976) is a footballer, currently playing for Hartlepool United. The former Norwich City player was signed by Hartlepool on transfer deadline day in August. The winger has been a long-standing target for Danny Wilson and was delighted to sign the player.

Gibb likes to get down the right flank and deliver crosses in to the penalty with boss Danny Wilson obviously confident he can supply the ammunition for the Club's strikers. Most of his appearances have been as substitute appearances because of the outstanding form of the first teams performances.

The talented wide man began his career at Norwich City before moving to Northampton. Then he played over 150 games for Stockport County before joining Bristol Rovers in 2004.
