Alicetown is located just to the north of Ava (Petone) and just to the west of Woburn/Lower Hutt.

Its boundrys are the Ewen Bridge that crosses the Hutt River, New Zealand to the East, the Western Hutt Rd/Melling Railway track to the South and Wakefield St/Hutt Railway track to the South.
