"Alison Lapper Pregnant" by Marc Quinn. Sculpture in white marble, Trafalgar Square, London, UK.

The Fourth Plinth in Trafalgar Square: a pigeon is on the leg of the sculpture

The Fourth Plinth Commissioning Group has recommended two sculptures for installation on Trafalgar Square's fourth plinth: Marc Quinn’s Alison Lapper Pregnant and Thomas Schütte’s Hotel for the Birds.

This sculpture was unveiled on 15th September 2005.

Made of Carrara marble, it shows a naked, heavily pregnant artist, with no arms and shortened legs (due to a congenital disorder).
