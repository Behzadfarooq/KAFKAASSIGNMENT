Alien Sun (born September 11, 1974; sometimes Paulyn Sun or Pauline Suen) is a Singapore-born actress and Miss Singapore of 1994 and represented her country at Miss Universe.

[edit] Trivia

  • She has said that the name Alien started as a nickname given to her by a friend due to a perception of her head as being disproportionately large in relation to her body.
