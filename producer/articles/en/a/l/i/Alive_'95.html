Alive '95
Live album by Gamma Ray
Released 1996
Recorded Live at Milano, Paris, Pamplona and Erlangen
Genre Power Metal
Length 64:37
Label Sanctuary Records

Alive '95 was the first live album by Kai Hansen's Gamma Ray. It was released on May 1996, following the live concerts in Milano, Paris, Pamplona and Erlangen, during the band's "Men on a Tour" European tour.
