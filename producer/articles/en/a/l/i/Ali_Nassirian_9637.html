'Ali Nasirian'(in persian:علی نصیریان), actor, born 1934, Tehran, Iran. A respected iranian actor,in both character and leading roles.he appeared in a supporting role in his first film ,Dariush Mehrjuyi's "Gav"(The Cow,1969),also first film of Ezatollah Entezami,another great Iranian actor.Nasirian then shined in the title role of "Aghaye Halu"(Mr. Stupid,1970)also by Mehrjuyi.His other memorable films include: "Postchi"(The Postman,1971),"dayereye Mina"(Circle of Mina,1974),"Kamalolmolk"(1983),"Kafshhaye mirza Norouz"(Mirza Norouz's Shoes,1985),"Shire sangi"(Stone Lion,1986),"Nakhoda Khorshid"(Captain Khorshid,1986),"Booye pirahane yusof"(Scent of Joseph's Shirt,1995).
