[edit] Another addition

I added the xenomorph to this page (to the Alien movie section) for convenience purposes. Xenomorph is not an official title for the alien species, and thus many people (including me) searching for that page had to go through the Alien movie page first (as I suspect many of the people clicking that link are searching for), and even there the single link that resides amidst the text is easily overlooked (which is why I added one to see also). Seriously, I really thought those creatures were officially called Aliens, plain and simple. So I hope this helps. (DCH) 1/4/05

Good addition. In line with keeping DAB pages as transparant as possible as the manual requests, I moved the actual xenomorph link out of your "titular alien" description, so the pipe doesn't hide the actual name anymore. Xenomorph is as official a name as you can get, so I think it's better to show it for that reason as well. Retodon8 23:25, 4 January 2006 (UTC)
