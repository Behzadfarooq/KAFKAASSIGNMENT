[edit] History

[edit] Bloody Roar

Alice was kidnapped as a small child by the company Tylon and converted into a Zoanthrope, a person with the ability to turn into a half human, half animal fighting beast. Alice managed to escape the organization before she could be brainwashed, but another girl wasn't so lucky. Her name was Uriko. Alice decided to fight back against Tylon and rescue the little girl who'd helped her escape.

[edit] Bloody Roar 2

After the fall of Tylon, Alice was all alone in the world. So it was Mitsuko, Uriko's mother, who adopted her into the Nonomura family so she could live out a peaceful life as a part of their family. After graduating high school, she became a nurse. One day, her friend Yugo was brought into the hospital where she worked. But as she tried to talk to him, he left the hospital. Concerned for his welfare, she chased after him to drag him back to the hospital for treatment.

[edit] Bloody Roar 3

Alice, a gentle soul who shows great love and compassion to those who are sick and injured. Being a close friend with Yugo, she decides to follow him to participate in the activities of the Non-Government Organisation (NGO), the World of Co-Existence (W.O.C.). In doing so, she leaves her job as a professional nurse. She becomes anxious when Yugo goes out alone to discover the origins of the 'XGC', a mark that appears on the bodies of Zoanthropes and bringing great power but at the cost of their life. Being marked by the XGC herself, Alice does not hesitate to follow after Yugo...

[edit] Bloody Roar: Primal Fury/Extreme

The W.O.C. once a small volunteer group has now grown into a commercial enterprise. Alice finds herself confused amongst the big changes occurring in the company. Aside, she concerns herself over Yugo, who has recently taken up the invitation to participate in the tournament in the Zoanthrope Kingdom. She agrees with his reasons to go, having heard the rumours of suspicious activities occurring behind the scenes, though she feels saddened she wasn't invited and that Kenji knows more information then her in the circumstances. Though, she feels the young ninja is very mature and he wouldn't let his emotions overcome him like she would over Yugo, and supports his good work as the vice-president of the W.O.C. It's as she is considering these thoughts that she receives a letter from Long, inviting her to the tournament. She takes up the invitation and heads for the tournament.

[edit] Bloody Roar 4

Alice whose been living the life of a volunteer in the W.O.C, has recently been aiding those who have been injured in the earthquakes in the area. A young girl, dress in the clothes of a shinto priestess appeared in the disaster area. Alice found this very strange under the circumstances and called to the girl. She requested that Alice tend to her friend, Ryoho. Not knowing the situation, she tried to refuse, but when she heard the girl mention that the earthquakes were their fault, Alice became intrigued and decided to follow her to find out more.

[edit] Personality

Alice is a rather tender young woman. She dedicates herself to other people who are in need of help and usually likes to get involved in dangerous situations as she doesn't like to see others struggle alone and be hurt. She values her friend and will put herself in harms way to bring a peaceful resolution. When around Yugo, she seems to dote more and concern herself too much over what he does. Jenny jokes about Alice concerning her pursuit of Yugo in Bloody Roar 2 that a woman in love is the most beautiful, making a reference to feelings that the young woman won't admit to herself. In her ending sequence of Bloody Roar: Primal Fury, it is suggested that Yugo returns her feelings.

[edit] Beast Drives

[edit] Bunny Love

When Alice (In beast form) is facing the opponent directly in front of them and the attack is initiated, the result is a barrage of extremely fast punches, kicks, and hearts. This is then followed by an upward spiral into the air, carrying the opponent with Alice, and finishing off with Alice tapping the opponent with her hips, encasing them in a big heart. This attack generally takes a little less than half of the opponents life gauge from them. Included in Bloody Roar 3 onwards.

[edit] Lifting Star Lane

When Alice is in beast form and this attack connects to the opponent, Alice curls the opponent into a ball (doing minimal damage), proceeds to kick the ball (opponent) in the air three times with her knee, and then finishes off the attack by back-kicking the opponent into either the ground (if no walls are near), a wall, or if no walls are present and Alice is near the boundary, will ring the opponent out. This attack also takes a little less than half of the opponents life gauge away from them. Included in Bloody Roar 2.

[edit] Bloody Roar Primal Fury/Extreme Ability Plus'

- Kick Attack Power Up: All of Alice's kick attacks become unblockable, and also increase in strength.

- Consecutive Hits (Air Combo): When an opponent takes a hit, and stumbles backwards, Alice is able to land air combos easier and more successfully.

[edit] Endings

[edit] Bloody Roar

Alice is half asleep in bed, mumbling about wanting an extra five minutes. When she checks her clock, she realises that she is going to be late for school. She hurries to get dressed as Uriko comes to stand in her doorway, asking what's taking so long because her friends are waiting. Alice whines that her 'sister' didn't wake her up, and the two have to rush out the house late. Alice has a piece of toast hanging from her mouth as she calls goodbye to Mitsuko and calls out to Uriko to slow down.

[edit] Bloody Roar 2

Alice is annoyed with having to fight against Gado, who is praising her skill and says she would be competent leader of a Zoanthrope group that could bring peace between their species and the humans. Alice declines, saying she has her patients to attend to and returns to the hospital. Shortly after, she is tending to Yugo who is less than pleased with her fussing over him. Aside what he thinks, she continues to nurse his wounds.

[edit] Bloody Roar 3

Xion awakens in Alice's arms free from the influence he was under. She tells him his actions weren't his fault and supports him while taking him back to civilization. He asks her to take him to a place where he can be around people and make up for what he'd done.

Alice Ending Screenshot: Bloody Roar: Extreme

[edit] Bloody Roar: Primal Fury/Extreme

Long commends her for her skills in their fight. Just behind them Yugo appears supporting what appears to to be an unconscious Cronos. Alice is surprised, but looks greatly relieved that he's alright. She walks over to them and suddenly slaps Yugo across the face. He is completely stunned by this. She shouts at him for getting them involved in a situation that could have got them killed. He tries to comfort her, but she snaps away from his touch, crying silently.

[edit] Bloody Roar 4

Alice, having defeated Ryoho, demands to know what she was fighting against. Mana explains that Ryoho is a Dragon vessel and this his existence is so dangerous that he had to be sealed so the dragon wouldn't wake. But the seal had come loose recently. Alice puts two and two together and realises the dragon's power had caused the Zoanthrope violence and earthquakes. Mana is glad that she understands and that it was strong Zoanthrope's such as her that were needed to reseal the dragon. She apologises to Alice for getting her involved in such a dangerous situation. Alice asks if the earthquakes will stop now and Mana assures her they will. Alice comments that it must be difficult just the two of them living alone in the temple, and since the place is now under the watch of the W.O.C. that she will have to check up on things. Jokily, she asks if Mana would like her to bring cake next time. Mana gladly says yes.

[edit] Trivia

  • Alice's hair colour has changed quite frequently throughout the series. In the first game she sported blue hair. Then in the second game it was light purple. And in the third game her hair was dark purple. And in Primal Fury/Extreme and the fourth game she ended up with brown hair. Also she wore her hair in pigtails for the first three games but left it down thereafter.
  • She was the only character to have a secret outfit in the first game. It was a red school uniform with a blue necktie. Her hair changed from blue to light brown and she only wore her hair up in one pigtail.
  • The last name Nonomura is taken when she becomes Mitsuko's adopted daughter, though the last names switch depending on the games. Alice's real last name Tsukagami is used in the first and third games, though Nonomura is listed in every other.
  • Alice's name means 'noble'.
  • Her name was inspired by the character Alice from 'Alice in Wonderland.' This also maybe an inspiration for her beast form as a 'white rabbit', like the character from the story.
