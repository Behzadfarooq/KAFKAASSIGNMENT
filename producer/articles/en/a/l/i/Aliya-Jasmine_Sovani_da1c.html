Aliya-Jasmine Sovani (born November, 1981 in Ottawa, Ontario) is one of seven hosts of MTV Live on the re-launched MTV Canada. She previously served as producer of MuchOnDemand, which airs on MuchMusic & was a host/producer for "Back @ Ya" Program on bpm:tv in Canada.

Sovani graduated from John McCrae Secondary School in the Ottawa suburb of Barrhaven, and studied Broadcast Communications at the University of Ottawa.

[edit] Previous Career

Was a writer for UMM Magazine in Ottawa before embarking on her broadcasting career.
