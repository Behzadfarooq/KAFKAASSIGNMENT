Alice's Curious Labyrinth is a hedge maze attraction at the Disneyland Park of Disneyland Resort Paris.

The maze is based on scenes and characters from Disney's 1951 feature Alice in Wonderland, particularly Alice's encounters with the Queen of Hearts, who had a similar labyrinth in the film. The maze is split into two sections, with some of Alice's adventures prior to meeting the Queen forming the initial part and a second area — called The Queen of Heart's Maze — based around the final scenes of the film.

The goal of the maze is to reach the castle of the Queen of Hearts, where one can obtain an aerial view of the Fantasyland section of the park. One can finish the maze prior to entering the second area, and the maze's route is similar in shape to the body of the Cheshire Cat, with a semi-floral recreation of the character's face adjacent to the attraction.

A slide used to be featured on the top floor of castle, but was in its first year closed off due to safety reasons.
