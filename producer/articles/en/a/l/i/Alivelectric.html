alivelectric is a live album by Boston rock/jazz band The Slip. It was released in 2003 along with its companion album, aliveacoustic, on the band's own label, 216 Records. It was recorded at various shows during late 2002 and early 2003. The album is almost entirely instrumental. "If One Of Us Should Fall" (later recorded in a different arrangement for the 2006 studio album Eisenhower) is the only song with singing, although "Introductions" does include singer-guitarist Brad Barr speaking as he introduces the band members.
