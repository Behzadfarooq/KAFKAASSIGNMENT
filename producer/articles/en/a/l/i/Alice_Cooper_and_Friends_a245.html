Alice Cooper and Friends is a TV Special starring Alice Cooper, broadcast September 1977 on US television and released on video in 1978. It is one of only Alice Cooper recordings with an opening band.

The video has a running time of 50 minutes and features about 25 minutes of Alice and also includes footage of The Tubes, Nazareth and Sha Na Na. It was recorded at the first night, 19th June 1977, of the Lace and Whiskey tour at the Anaheim Stadium, California. Alice was in pretty bad shape physically on this show/tour and at the end of the tour he checked into hospital.

This footage is the only commercially available film of this tour and features the infamous "Chickens with Machine Guns" act during Lace And Whiskey.

It has become very rare over the years, and has not been re-released commerically on DVD. Amazon prices start at USD88.
