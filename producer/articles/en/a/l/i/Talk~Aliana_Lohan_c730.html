[edit] Birthday

Aliana's birthday is December 21st. If you go to Aliana-Lohan.com, in a People magazine interview, they state her birthday to be December 21. The article is dated December 30, 2005, so just scroll down on the home page to see it.
People : Any holiday plans?
Ali : I love Christmas because my birthday [Dec. 21] is near Christmas and I get twice as many presents.
I know her official site says her birthday is December 22, but Lindsay's official site also had incorrect information on it for many years. They had her birth name as Lindsay Morgan Lohan and her eye color as green. Now, after finding her drivers license, they have her birth name as Lindsay Dee Lohan and her eye color as hazel. 64.30.110.9 22:49, 6 July 2006 (UTC)

It's not clear if the date was inserted by People magazine or by the site. If the former, it's a reasonable citation. If the latter, it is not a reliable source and so cannot be used as a citation. --Yamla 22:57, 6 July 2006 (UTC)
[1] According to a post here, the interview can be found in the latest People magazine as of Dec. 17, 2005. A person posted the interview on December 17th, which was also posted by the webmaster/webmistress of Aliana-Lohan.com on December 30th. The issue of People must be dated either December 11th or December 18th, though I think it's probably the 18th. Stephe1987 07:27, 10 July 2006 (UTC)
I found the article again here. I'd say that I am 99% sure that Aliana's birthday is December 21. I agree with 64.30.110.9 in that official sites are not always correct, seeing as Ali's sister Lindsay's official site had her middle name and eye color wrong for about four years before they finally fixed it. The article can be found in the issue of People magazine that hit newstands on December 17, 2005. Stephe1987 22:08, 10 July 2006 (UTC)
The people who run Ali's site supposedly get the information from her mother, but I've also been told that not all of it came from that source. As for the info at llrocks, I don't know when or why they went from green to Hazel, but the middle-name information was only confirmed on TEENick (and, later, on the site) after her Driver's License information was leaked to the Internet, perhaps leaving her no choice but to give an honest answer. RadioKirk (u|t|c) 22:25, 10 July 2006 (UTC)
