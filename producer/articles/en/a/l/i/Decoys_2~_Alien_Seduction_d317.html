Decoys 2: Alien Seduction is the sequel to the film Decoys. It was retitled as Decoys 2: Rebirth but is now called Decoys 2: Alien Seduction. It's USA/Canada DVD title is Decoys: The Second Seduction.

Back from the original film is actress Kim Poirier and actor Corey Sevier. It also stars Tobin Bell and Dina Meyer.

It was originally due to air on TV but was given a straight-to-DVD release.

[edit] Story

Sam, a college student in a small Northwestern town, reluctantly joins his roommates in a contest to see who can hook up with the most gorgeous co-eds by the end of the semester. But when men slowly start disappearing around town, he and his friends learn that when it comes to beautiful women, it’s what’s inside that really matters.
