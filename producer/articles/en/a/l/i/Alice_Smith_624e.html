Alice Smith (b. 1978) is an New York City-based singer, her style anchored in black music (R&B, blues, jazz and soul. Raised between Washington D.C. and a farm in Georgia, she sang in Moomtez and Black Rock Coalition while attending college. she released her first LP in 2006. Possessing a powerful voice, Rolling Stone said of her, "Smith could easily be lumped in with expressive chanteuses like Norah Jones and Alicia Keys, but she has a broader palette than either." The magazine listed her as one of the 10 Artists to Watch in 2006.[1]
