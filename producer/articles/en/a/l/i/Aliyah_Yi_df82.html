Aliyah Yi (also known as Aaliyah Yi, Alliyah Yi) is a pornographic actress, exotic dancer, and nude model of Asian descent. She was born 24 August 1977 in Riverside, California. She moved to New York with her family and spent most of her time growing up there. Prior to entering porn she worked as a commercial and corporate real estate agent for 2 1/2 years.

In 2000 she moved back to Southern California where she began her career in the adult industry by posing for men's magazines (such as Hustler) and adult website pictorials, mostly asian fetish and hardcore layouts. Her first appearance in an adult film was in Fuckumentary, where she is credited as Aaliyah Yi. She has appeared in only about 15 video-tape distributed adult films, but the list of websites for which she has performed and/or posed is quite long. She has appeared on Playboy TV's call-in show Nightcalls 411.

One of Aliyah's many appeals is her tattooed backside. She has chinese characters on her back which read "Snake gives love to Tigers".

Aliyah has also worked as an exotic dancer in the Southern California area. She was at one time a part of the now-defunct Exotica-2000.com, a website featuring porn stars and dancers who were also available as escorts, but it is not certain whether or not she continues in this area of the sex industry.
