Written: By Lee Rogers

Ali Rhodes, a 17 year old boy, born in Atlanta, Georgia was the first person to successfully hack the windows firewall in Spencer High school. By doing so, he inavertedly allowed Spencer to update their firewalls.

Ali Rhodes was born on December 12, 1989 in Atlanta, GA. He attended Sister Clara Muhammad School in such he attended one year. Afterwards he soon became well none for his ingenuity and problem solving technique. After he left his first year, he went to public school in which he despised for the fact not many people valued their education here, and that people always seemed to have a chip on their shoulder. He hated it, but continued public school. In his spare time at home, he would go outside where in his spare time, would shoplift from the local target in Conyers, GA. In his first years Ali was the best, swiping any and everything, in the long run though, his skills slipped, landing him in RYDC and in the S.T.A.R program. In the S.T.A.R program or Star for short, it was boot camp meant to teach discipline and honesty among other of your age. In proper conduct a recruit could possibly get out in 1 year but could be held for a maximum of 5 years. While in Star, Ali violated his probation, and ended up in RYDC 3 times before he decided to go straight. In finally straightening out, Ali moved to Columbus, GA where he soon went to Northside, soon transferring to Spencer. In arriving in Spencer, Ali adapted to his surroundings and soon carved a niche out in his new school. When he got a class in Typing, all the trouble would begin. When he joined this typing class, he had had previous experience with a P2P program called Kazaa. In his experience he had learned it was search engine/download engine that could possibly backdoor the system. Ali had been searching for a way to get free music without spending $10.95 at Wal-Mart every Saturday. Soon though he had not realized, someone was watching him, and it was not a teacher. Not known to Ali, a to this day unknown student was watching his every move. In the end it was Ali’s ingenious but naïve nature that cost him, he almost got expelled, due to the fact of this student downloaded porn and in the process revealed Ali, the downloader of the engine and music, not the porn, as culprit for all. Ali soon was suspended five days, and then was on school probation for a year.
