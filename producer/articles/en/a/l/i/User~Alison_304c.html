:: What I do at Wikipedia


I do a lot of recent changes patrolling and spend a bit of time reverting vandalism, unfortunately. When I'm not doing that, I edit Irish articles, especially geographical or maritime.

I've also done a bunchload of medical articles, especially relating to endocrine, psychiatric and surgical matters. Oh, and some biographical articles! My current crusade centres around updating references and journal citations on pharmacological and other medical articles.

I'm also an administrator so if you need help with anything, just ask!
