Alien Apocalypse is a Sci-Fi channel original movie based on Human in Chains, a Josh Becker and Robert Tapert written work. In the movie, astronaut Dr. Ivan Hood (Bruce Campbell) and his companion Kelly (Renee O'Connor) return from their 40-year-long mission in space to find the planet taken over by locust-like aliens. Now, Dr. Ivan must find the President who allegedly has an army of escaped slaves who will cause a rebellion. Unfortunately, this doesn't happen and Dr. Ivan sparks the rebellion, to later be joined by the president.

[edit] Trivia

  • Most of the shooting for this film was done in Bulgaria.
  • According to the DVD commentary, many of the actors in the film were Bulgarian and spoke little English, therefore much of the dialog was dubbed or "looped" in post-production.
