[edit] About myself

So just why am I on Wikipedia? It's a fun site: my love of films allows me fun to act as an amateur historian without the heated discussions or trolling of most message boards. Certainly it allows me to dip in and out of my favourite fictional universes if I feel bored, and because I have Asperger's Syndrome I'm certainly less interested than most teenagers in a social life. I came here for help regarding Doctor Who's mythology, above the BBC's childish regurgitation of dialogue from the new series (not anything wrong with the series, I love it, just the Beeb takes the drama out of the intention of the dialogue).

I first registered as Wiki-newbie as I didn't think much of editing here, and it was a plea for help. Now I'm fully versed in WP:ATT, WP:NPOV and WP:AVTRIVIA.
