Aliens is a run and gun shoot 'em up arcade game by Konami released in 1990, based on the Aliens movie. It picks up mid-movie with Lt. Ripley and Cpl. Hicks attempting to leave the alien-infested colony by making their way towards the drop ship, and notably takes some liberties with the film's storyline and the Aliens' biology.

Levels in the game are:

  • Level 1 - Living Quarters
  • Level 2 - Factory
  • Level 3 - Factory2
  • Level 4 - Queen Alien's DEN
  • Level 5 - Escape Route
  • Level 6 - Battle Ship SULACO
