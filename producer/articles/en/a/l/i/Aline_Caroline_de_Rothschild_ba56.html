Aline Caroline de Rothschild, Lady Sassoon (February 21, 1865 – 1909) was a French socialite and daughter of Cécile Anspach and Baron Gustave de Rothschild of the prominent Rothschild family.

In 1887 she married Edward Sassoon (1856-1912). They had the following children:

  • Philip Albert Gustave David (1888-1939)
  • Sybil Rachel Bettie Cécile, Marchioness of Cholmondeley (1894-1989)

She died in 1909 at the age of forty-two.
