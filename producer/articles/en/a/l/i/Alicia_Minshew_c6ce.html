Alicia Minshew (born May 28, 1974 in Plantation, Florida) is an American actress. Her mother is of Italian descent.

She is best known for portraying Kendall Hart on the soap opera All My Children. She first stepped into the role in January 2002, after the character's seven-year absence from the soap following the exit of Sarah Michelle Gellar, who had previously played the character.

In 2004 and 2005 she played Kendall Hart in brief appearances on the soap opera One Life to Live as part of the infamous baby-swap storyline.

She has received two Soap Opera Digest Award nominations, in 2003 and 2005. She graduated from South Fork High School in Stuart, FL.
