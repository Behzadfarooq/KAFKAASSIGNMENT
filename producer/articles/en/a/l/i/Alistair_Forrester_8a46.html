Alistair Forrester

Alistair Forrester is a retired Scottish professional darts player who competed in the 1980s. He qualified twice to play in the World Professional Darts Championship, but lost in the first round on both occasions.

He was beaten 0-2 by Bobby George in the 1981 Championship and 1-2 by the Belgian player Luc Mureel in the 1982 Championship.

His daughter is TV Presenter Philippa Forrester.
