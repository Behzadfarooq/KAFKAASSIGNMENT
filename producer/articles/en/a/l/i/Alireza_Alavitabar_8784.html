Alireza Alavitabar

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Alireza Alavitabar is an outstanding Persian political scientist and a leading Iranian reformist intellectual and writer

Alireza Alavitabar is a member of Islamic Iran Participation Front and the editor of the Sobh-e-Emrooz newspaper.

He was of the main thinkers in the Iranian reform movement during Khatami's presidency.
