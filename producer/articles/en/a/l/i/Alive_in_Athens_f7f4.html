Alive in Athens is a live metal album by the band Iced Earth. It was recorded on the nights of January 23rd and January 24th 1999 in front of sold out crowds of approximately 2000 people (on both nights) at the Rodon Club in Athens, Greece. Iced Earth was supporting the "Something Wicked This Way Comes" album and were on tour. It was turned to a single DVD, and released in October 2006. It is widely regarded as one of the best live albums ever made.
