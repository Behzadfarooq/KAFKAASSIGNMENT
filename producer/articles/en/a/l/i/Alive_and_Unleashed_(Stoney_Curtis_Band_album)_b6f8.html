Alive and Unleashed is the two disc sophomore album of the Stoney Curtis Band in 2002 off of Shrapnel records. On a sunday July 21, 2002, Alive and Unleashed was featured on KLOS 95.5 Local Licks program. Most of the show was dedicated to them and the album.[1] For the album, Stoney Curtis was the lead guitar and vocalist. Colby Smith took bass. Charles Grover was drums and the background Vocals. It was produced by Stoney Curtis himself. And it was Engineered by R. Kyle Frost. It was released in 2002 due to the band recording an album every two years.[2]
