[edit] 1970

  • Diary of a Mad Housewife feature film starring Carrie Snodgrass (see Neil Young) and Richard Benjamin with the original Alice Cooper Band in a cameo appearance at a "loft party"

[edit] 1974

  • Episode of The Snoop Sisters TV show.

[edit] 1975

  • Alice Cooper:The Nightmare TV special.

[edit] 1977

  • Alice Cooper and Friends TV special.

[edit] 1978

  • Sgt. Pepper's Lonely Hearts Club Band (film) feature film.
  • Episode of The Muppet Show, guest star.

[edit] 1979

  • The Strange Case of Alice Cooper concert film.

[edit] 1980

  • Roadie (film) feature film .

[edit] 1982

  • Alice in Paris concert film.

[edit] 1984

  • Monster Dog feature film.

[edit] 1987

  • Prince of Darkness feature film.

[edit] 1990

  • Alice Cooper: Trashes The World concert film.

[edit] 1991

  • Freddy's Dead: The Final Nightmare feature film.

[edit] 1992

  • Wayne's World feature film.

  • Mr. Monk and the Garbage Strike episode of Monk TV show.
