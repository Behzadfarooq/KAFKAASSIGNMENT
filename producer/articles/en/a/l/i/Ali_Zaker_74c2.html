Ali Zaker (Bangla: আলি জাকের) (also written as Aly Zaker) is a famous actor in Bangladeshi television drama and theatre. He is also a leading personality in the domestic advertising industry.

His wife Sara Zaker is a renowned actress.

  • Bohubrihi
  • Aaj Rabibar
