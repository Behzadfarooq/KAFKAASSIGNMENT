Ali Mukaddam (born on April 6, 1985 in Toronto, Ontario, Canada) is a Canadian actor. He graduated from Humberside Collegiate Institute in Toronto, Ontario in 2003 and is currently a university film student. His acting roles have included Angela Anaconda, Franklin, Radio Free Roscoe and guest appearances on Degrassi: The Next Generation. He also appeared in "Tagged: The Jonathan Wamback Story" and the Disney Channel Original Movie, Get a Clue. Mukaddam is of Pakistani decent
