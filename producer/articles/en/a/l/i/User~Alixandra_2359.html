Alixandra (born October 14 in Ontario, Canada) is a Wikipedia user. She lives with her many pets, which include 1 dog, 3 cats, 1 rabbit, 2 hamsters, 1 fish, and a sister named Frau-Frau.

Alixandra has black hair and very dark brown eyes. She's 5'7, but wishes she was taller. She currently has two tattoos, and no piercings.

Though British-Irish-Danish-Canadian, Alix is fascinated by Germany and their culture.
