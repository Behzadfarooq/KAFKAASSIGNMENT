Ali Reza Pahlavi I

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

This article is about the son of Reza Shah. For his nephew of the same name, see Ali Reza Pahlavi II.

Ali Reza Pahlavi (1 March 1922 - 17 October 1954) was Reza Shah Pahlavi's second son, and the brother of Shah Mohammad Reza Pahlavi. Following Reza Shah's deposition and exile Ali Reza accompanied his father into exile in Mauritius and then into Johannesburg, South Africa. He was married to Christiane Cholewski in Paris in 1946, they had one son Ali Patrick Pahlavi (AKA, "Prince Ali", born 1 September 1947). Ali Reza was killed on the 17 October 1954 in a flying accident in the Alborz Mountains.
