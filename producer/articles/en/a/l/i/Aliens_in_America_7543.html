Aliens in America is a sitcom series for The CW, being developed by NBC Universal Television Studio, for the 2007-2008 television season. It tells the story of a Wisconsin mom who arranges to host a foreign exchange student, believing the visitor will help her shy son become more popular. When the student turns out to be a Muslim teenager from Pakistan, her plans go awry.
