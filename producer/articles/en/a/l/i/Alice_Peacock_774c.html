Alice Peacock (born November 19, 1971) is an American folk singer and has recorded three independent albums. A native of White Bear Lake, Minnesota, she currently resides in Chicago, Illinois, where she sang "The Star-Spangled Banner" at U.S. Cellular Field during the 2005 playoff run of the Chicago White Sox and on April 4, 2006, at their World Series ring ceremony[1]. Her song "Leading With My Heart" was featured on the soundtrack for the film Win a Date with Tad Hamilton!.
