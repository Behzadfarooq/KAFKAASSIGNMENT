Alicia and Annie Sorell

The Sorell twins in their infamous scene.

Alicia and Annie Sorell are identical twin actresses, most famous for their shower scene in the 2000 direct to video film Cruel Intentions 2.

Since 2002, Alicia has used the name Alicia Lorén.
