Alison Matthews is a Canadian voice actress who works for Ocean Studios in Vancouver, British Columbia, Canada. She has played several roles in anime, most notably Ezalia Joule in Gundam Seed as well as the narrator in Gundam Seed and Gundam Seed Destiny. She also co-starred in NBC Saturday morning series Just Deal.

[edit] Anime Roles

  • Narrator - Gundam Seed Destiny
  • Narrator, Ezalia Joule - Gundam Seed
  • Toran - Inu Yasha
