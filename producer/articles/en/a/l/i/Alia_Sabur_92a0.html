Alia Sabur

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Alia Sabur (born February 22, 1989) is an American child prodigy. She is best known for being accepted into graduate school at the age of 14. She left public school in 4th grade, was admitted to State University of New York at Stony Brook at the age of 10, graduating summa cum laude at 14. She also received a black belt in Tae Kwon Do at the age of 9. She attends Drexel University.
