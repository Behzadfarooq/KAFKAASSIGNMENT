Ali Muddat ibn al-Husayn

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Ali Muddat ibn al-Husayn (1817 - 1902) (Arabic: علي باي بن حسين) was the ruler of Tunisia from 1882 until 1902. He was the first ruler under the French protectorate.
