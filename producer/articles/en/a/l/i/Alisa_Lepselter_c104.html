Alisa Lepselter

Alisa Lepselter is a movie editor. Since 1999, she's exclusively edited films directed by Woody Allen, including The Curse of the Jade Scorpion, Hollywood Ending, Match Point, Melinda and Melinda and Small Time Crooks.
