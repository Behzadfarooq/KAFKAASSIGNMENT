Ali Asgher Razwy (Arabic: علي أصغر رزوي‎) (d. September 15, 1996 [1]) is a Shi'a Muslim scholar known for authoring Restatement of History of Islam.

He was born in Simla, India in 1925, his father Aqa Seyyid Muhammad Shah had migrated there from Baluchistan [1].
