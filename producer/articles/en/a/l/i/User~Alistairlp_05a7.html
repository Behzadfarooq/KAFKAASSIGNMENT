I am Alistair Paterson. I am a Sixth Form student in London. I am applying to do computer science at five universities, all based in and around London.

I take an active interest in football and rugby union supporting Chelsea and Glasgow Warriors respectively. I also take great pride in my heritage, doing my best to support Scotland on every occasion possible.
