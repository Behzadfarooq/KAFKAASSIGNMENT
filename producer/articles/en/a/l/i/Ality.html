Ality Limited is a Hong Kong-based technology company. Ality manufactures, trades, and markets quality technology products for the modern home. Their line of products include digital photo frames and high-tech lifestyle gadgets.

Ality makes some of the most attractive frames available. The Pixxa model features a clock and calendar and a mirrored surface when photos are not being displayed. The model also features a unique touch-screen user interface to change settings.[1]
