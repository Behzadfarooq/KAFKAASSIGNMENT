Alice Chelangat (born 27 December 1976) is a Kenyan long-distance runner who specializes in the marathon race.

[edit] Achievements

Year Tournament Venue Result Event
2002 Rock 'n' Roll Marathon San Diego, California, USA 1st Marathon
2003 Nairobi Marathon Nairobi, Kenya 1st Marathon
2004 Olympic Games Athens, Greece 11th Marathon
2006 World Cross Country Championships Fukuoka, Japan 10th Long Race
World Cross Country Championships Fukuoka, Japan 2nd Team Competition
Rock 'n' Roll Marathon San Diego, California, USA 1st Marathon

[edit] Personal bests

  • 10,000 metres - 32:49.6 min (2001)
  • Half marathon - 1:09:10 hrs (2002)
  • Marathon - 2:26:36 hrs (2001)
