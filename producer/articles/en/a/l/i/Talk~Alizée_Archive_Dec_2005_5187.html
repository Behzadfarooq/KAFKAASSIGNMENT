Inappropriate image

As far as I am aware, Chameleon, posting screenshots from televised broadcasts falls under fair use, so there is wide range of source materials available from which you could potentially get pictures of this girl. It is not necessary that you use screenshots from amateur videos. This has bothered me too. I try to comment parts separately below. --Easyas12c 01:16, 26 November 2005 (UTC)
  • Astrological sign: Leo
    • Should be removed. There are a billion different astologic sign systems, and the signs can be calculated from date of birth. So no need for explicitly naming it in the article. As it has not been scientifically proven to have any use.

  • Weight: about 45 kg

  • Prizes: M6 Music Award, NRJ Music Award and World Music Award

  • Likes: the beach, shopping, dolphins, her black labrador retriever, Patrick Bruel, the colour purple
