Alice's Birthday (Russian: Де́нь рожде́ния Али́сы, Den' rozhdeniya Alisy), is an upcoming 2007 Russian flash-animated feature film directed by Sergey Seryogin and being made by the studio "MASTER-FILM". It is based on one of a series of stories by well-known writer Kir Bulychyov about Alisa (Alice) Seleznyova, a young girl living in the second half of the 21st century. It is the second animated feature film to be based on one of the Alice stories, with the first having been The Mystery of the Third Planet (1981).
