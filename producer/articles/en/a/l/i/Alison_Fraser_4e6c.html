Alison Fraser

Alison Fraser is an American singer and actress.

Fraser has appeared in concert at such venues as Carnegie Hall, The White House, and Joe's Pub.

Fraser is a two-time Tony Award nominee for The Secret Garden and Romance/Romance, a Drama Desk Award nominee for The Secret Garden, and a Carbonell Awards winner for Romance/Romance. She is the first ever recipient of Philadelphia's Barrymore Award for Best Actress for her portrayal of The Blonde in Gunmetal Blues.

Fraser's television credits include Law and Order: Special Victims Unit, Third Watch, The L Word, and the PBS children's series Between the Lions.

Fraser's two solo CDs are New York Romance (a Grammy Award nominee) and Men in My Life.

Fraser is the widow of Rusty Magee and the mother of one son, Nathaniel.
