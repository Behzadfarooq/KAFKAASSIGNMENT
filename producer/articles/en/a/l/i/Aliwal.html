Aliwal

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

This article is about the village in India. For the town in South Africa see Aliwal North.

Aliwal is a village in India, located in the Punjab, on the Sutlej, where, during the First Anglo-Sikh War, Sir Harry Smith gained a brilliant victory over the Sikhs, whose forces had superior numbers, in 1846.
