Alicia Alighatti (born Ellie Murdoch on 21 June 1984) is an American pornographic actress.

Alighatti was born in Easton, Pennsylvania. She is of Scottish descent. Alighatti grew up in the smaller town of Wind Gap, Pennsylvania. At age 13, Alighatti began working at the Bit By Bit Equestrian Center, an equestrian facility, eventually becoming an assistant manager. She left in 2002.

After high school, Alighatti enrolled at Lynchburg College in Lynchburg, Virginia, a small liberal arts college, related to the Disciples of Christ church. Alighatti studied French at Lynchburg, and became a fellow of the Westover Honors Program, headed by Dr. Edward DeClair. Two years into her studies, Alighatti dropped out in 2004, and moved to Los Angeles, California, where she began making pornographic films.

Alighatti has appeared in over 60 pornographic films, and is featured on many websites. In 2006, Alighatti won two Adult Video News Awards; for Best Group Sex Scene and Best Oral Sex Scene in the film titled Darkside.
