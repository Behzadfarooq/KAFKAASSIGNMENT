Ali Shakouri-Rad

Ali Shakouri-Rad (علی شکوری راد) is an Iranian reformist politician and a member of Islamic Iran Participation Front's Central Council. He was the campaign manager of Mostafa Moin for the Iranian presidential election of 2005.

Shakouri-Rad was a Majlis representative from 2000 to 2004, but was forbidden by the Guardian Council to run again in the Iranian Majlis election of 2004.
