[edit] Ali muslim

Please do not remove speedy deletion tags from articles. If you do not believe the article deserves to be deleted, then please place {{hangon}} on the page and make your case on the article's talk page. Administrators will look at your reasoning before deciding what to do with the article. Thank you.

Just to let you know, adding {{sprotected}} or {{protected}} to a page is only a means for an admin to notify users that a page is protected. This does not actually confer protection to the article.

--Signed and Sealed, JJJJust (T C) 11:01, 21 July 2006 (UTC)

[edit] Vandalism Warning (Level 3)

Welcome to Wikipedia. We invite everyone to contribute constructively to our encyclopedia. Take a look at the welcome page if you would like to learn more about contributing. However, unconstructive edits, such as those you made to Datafocal, are considered vandalism. If you continue in this manner you may be blocked from editing without further warning.
