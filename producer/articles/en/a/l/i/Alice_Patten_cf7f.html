Alice Patten

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Alice Patten in a screenshot of the film Rang De Basanti

Alice Patten (彭雅思; born 1980) is an English actress, and the daughter of Chris Patten, a prominent British conservative politician and the last governor of Hong Kong. She had a key role in the hit Bollywood film Rang De Basanti (2006) and some TV series.

As of early 2006, she is also scheduled to act in Hamlet, a West End production, where Patten plays Ophelia opposite Ed Stoppard's Prince of Denmark.

Patten was educated at Island School, Hong Kong and Queens' College Cambridge.
