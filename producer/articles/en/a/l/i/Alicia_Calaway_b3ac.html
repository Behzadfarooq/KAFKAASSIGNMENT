Alicia Calaway (born May 1, 1968) was a contestant on two US reality television shows, Survivor: The Australian Outback and again on Survivor: All-Stars.

[edit] Personal

She was born in Bridgeport, Connecticut. She won the Amateur Athletic Union "Ms. Connecticut Amateur Natural Bodybuilding Competition" in 1994. She graduated from the Fashion Institute of Technology in New York City with two Associates in Applied Science Degrees and she is certified through the National Academy of Sports Medicine (NASM).

[edit] Survivor: Australian Outback

Alicia was a contestant on Survivor's second season Survivor: The Australian Outback in 2001, where she was a member of the Kucha tribe. She allied herself with tribesmate Jeff while frequently butting heads with her tribesmate Kimmi. A strong physical competitor, she survived to the merger of the new tribe Barramundi. However, after making it to the final nine, she was voted out in a narrow 5-4 vote to Jerri.

[edit] Survivor: All-Stars

Alicia was chosen again to compete in Survivor: All-Stars, the eighth edition which aired in 2004. She was a member of the Chapera tribe along with Rob Mariano, Amber Brkich, Sue Hawk, Tom Buchanan and Rob Cesternino. She was later moved to the Mogo Mogo tribe during the tribal swap of episode 9. Although physically strong, she was not able to make a strong alliance and finished seventh in a 6-1 vote.

[edit] Other TV appearances

  • She co-hosted World's Strongest Man and World's Strongest Woman competitions on ESPN in 2002.
  • She hosted a special on Antarctica for Outdoor Life Network.
  • She guest starred in an episode of the UPN sitcom Half & Half in 2004.

[edit] Memorable Quotes

  • She is perhaps best known for her on-air fight with fellow tribesmate Kimmi where she uttered the memorable threat "I will always wave my finger in your face!"
