Alive is the title of Kate Ryan's third studio album. It was released in 2006, by EMI.

The tracklist for the album is as follows. Translations of song names are given in italicised parentheses:

  3. Alive (Radio Edit)

  5. Spinning Around
  6. How Many Times
  7. Nothing
  8. Why Imagine

  11. Wonderland
  12. Stepping Out
  13. That Kiss I Miss
  14. Je T'Adore (Radio Edit)
  15. Je Donnerais Tout (All For You)
  16. Alive (French version)
