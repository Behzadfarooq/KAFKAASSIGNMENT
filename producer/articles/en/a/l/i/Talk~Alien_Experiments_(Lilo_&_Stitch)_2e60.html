[edit] Many unrevealed experiments

If you'll notice, I added or changed a lot of experiments whose names and/or functions had not been previously revealed. I frequent a forum where Jess Winfield, the associate director of the series, is a regular and has clarified many of these. (I suppose I can't prove that it really was Jess Winfield, but he has provided information on the episodes that turned out to be true later, so the entire forum accepts that he is who he says.) Some of the experiments mentioned have not actually been revealed at all, while some others have been on that forum. For reference, the forum is this one. I think someone else from that forum must have been here before, though, since a few (like 611, Doomsday) were given their proper names and functions. --Jaycrono (64.251.148.161 07:55, 10 Mar 2005 (UTC))

Oh, and by the way, it's 033: Hammerface (though Pleakly DOES call him "Hammerhead" in The Asteroid.) Strangely enough, The Asteroid first aired BEFORE Dupe, so you can't use the "most recent episode" as an excuse for calling him Hammerhead (although The Asteroid must have taken place later chronologically.) --64.251.148.161 07:56, 10 Mar 2005 (UTC)

[edit] Cleanup update

I appreciate everyone's valid contributions to this article thus far, but this article needed to be cleaned up. I couldn't stand looking at this thing, so I cleaned the article up a bit. I organized the experiments in their appropriate series, removed story information that had little to do with the experiments themselves and made some grammar corrections.

This thing still has a ways to go before it's cleaned up to Wikipedia standards of quality. Readability needs to be improved and there's still some minor grammar problems. Please make all future edits to this article in line with the current style - briefly describe the experiment's power, the major thing it did in the episode and what happened to it by the end of the episode. The point of this is to make a nice, tidy, encyclopedic article about these experiments. Thanks! :) --Jtalledo (talk) 01:31, 22 Mar 2005 (UTC)
