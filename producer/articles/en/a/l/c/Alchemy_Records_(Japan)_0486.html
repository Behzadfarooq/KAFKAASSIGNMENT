Alchemy Records (Japan)

Alchemy Records is a record label based in Osaka, Japan, specializing in noise / experimental music and psychedelic rock. It is run by Jojo Hiroshige of the noise group Hijokaidan. Alchemy has released albums by Hijokaidan, Masonna, Incapacitants, Borbetomagus, and Merzbow, among many others. In addition to the label, Alchemy also has a record store based in Osaka.
