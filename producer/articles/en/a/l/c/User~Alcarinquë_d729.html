[edit] Genealogy

One of my accomplishments that I am most proud of is how I traced my ancestry back all the way back to 1390, by myself (with the help of my grandmother's knowledge of her family, etc.).

On my father's side I am Swiss, English, Irish, Austrian, and German.

On my mother's side I am Dutch and German with a pinch of French and Belgian.

Put that all together and I'm American.

Some notable (distant) relations to me are Henry Livingston Jr., author of "The Night Before Christmas".
