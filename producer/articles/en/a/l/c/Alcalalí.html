Alcalalí is a Valencian municipality in the comarca of Marina Alta, province of Alcante, Spain.

It is situated in the Pop Valley, between the Sierra del Ferrer and the Montes de Segili. It is bordered by Pedreguer and Benidoleig to the north; Jalón and Llíber to the east; Orba, Murla and Parcent to the west; and Tárbena to the south.
