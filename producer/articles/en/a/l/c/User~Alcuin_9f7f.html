I mostly mess around with album pages, including those by:

  • Eric Clapton discography
  • Moody Blues
  • John Mayall
  • Paul Butterfield
  • Canned Heat
  • The Kinks discography
  • Neil Young discography
  • Traffic
  • Johnny Cash discography, Sun discography
  • Kris Kristofferson
  • Waylon Jennings

That list is kind of a mix. I usually stick to adding tracklists or infoboxes and creating new articles with those 2 things. Most rock groups' albums are already well covered, so I end up spending my time on albums with which I might be less familiar, but need more work. I also sometimes work on discography or song pages as the need exists or if it suits my mood. If you have any special album or music projects in mind, or if you need help or have a question, drop me a line. I have learned from User:Cromag that editing is much more fun in collaboration!  :Alcuin 04:26, 10 August 2006 (UTC), last updated 16:55, 22 August 2006 (UTC)
