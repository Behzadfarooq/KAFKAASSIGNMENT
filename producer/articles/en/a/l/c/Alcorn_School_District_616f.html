The Alcorn School District is a public school district based in Alcorn County, Mississippi (USA).

The district serves the towns of Farmington, Kossuth, Glen, and Rienzi as well as unincorporated areas of Alcorn County.

[edit] Demographics

There were a total of 3,815 students enrolled in the Alcorn School District during the 2005-2006 school year. The gender makeup of the district was 47% female and 53% male. The racial makeup of the district was 4% African American, 95% White, 1% Hispanic, and less than 1% Asian and Native American.[1]
