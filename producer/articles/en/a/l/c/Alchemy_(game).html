Alchemy is a computer puzzle game from PopCap Games. This title can be played for free online at various websites, or a full version can be downloaded and unlocked for a nominal fee.

[edit] Gameplay

The object of the game is to turn a board of squares from grey to gold by placing randomly selected runes on every square. Each of these runes must be placed directly adjacent another on the board that shares either its colour or shape (or both). Completing an entire row either horizontally or vertically makes the runes disappear and leaves the square gold. If there is no available square for the rune currently in hand, it can be discarded into the forge on the side of the board. Runes can be discarded up to three times in a row before doing so causes the game to end.

The strategy of the game revolves around choosing exactly where to place runes in order to maximize future move possibilities. If poor rune placements are made, future moves become more difficult (or require more luck in subsequent draws).

[edit] Trivia

The symbols used in this game consists of astrological glyphs (zodiac symbols): Aries, Taurus, Gemini, Cancer, Leo, Virgo, Libra, Scorpio, Saggittarius, Capricorn, Aquarius and Pisces. A few of these glyphs will only appear in higher levels.
