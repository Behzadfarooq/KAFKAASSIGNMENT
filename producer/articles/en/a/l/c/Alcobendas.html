Alcobendas is a city inscribed in the Comunidad de Madrid (Spain). It is located roughly 13 km north of Madrid and 7 km from the Barajas international airport of Madrid. It includes a central urban zone, La Moraleja residential zone, the Valdelatas natural reserve and a light industrial zone. Home for the Cosmocaixa sciences museum and the Diversia entertainment center.
