Alcanzar Una Estrella II ("Reach for a Star II") is a telenovela produced by Televisa in 1991. It is the sequel to Alcanzar Una Estrella.

[edit] Plot

Spoiler warning: Plot and/or ending details follow.

Eduardo puts out a call to all of Mexico's youth to find members for his new musical group, Muñecos De Papel ("Paper Mache"), but at the last minute he cancels everything to go on tour with his beloved Lorena. The group decides to forge ahead without Eduardo, and thus six young people get to live their dreams of achieving fame and fortune. The series addresses many problems facing today's youth in the anecdotes about the lives of the members of the band.
