Alcithoe is a genus of gastropod molluscs in the family Volutidae. Mostly large, solid, fusiform shells, characterized, with a few exceptions, by having 4 or 5 strong plaits on the columella. They are found in most seas from shallow water to considerable depths, and are active predators, feeding mostly on bivalves. Intertidal species are found burrowing or half buried in sand or mud.
