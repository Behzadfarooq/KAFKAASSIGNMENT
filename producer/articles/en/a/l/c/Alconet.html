ALCONET s.r.o. is a small local internet service provider in Liptovsky Mikulas, Slovakia. It has connected the city of Liptovsky Mikulas and some villages (Zavazna Poruba, Liptovsky Trnovec, Bobrovec, Trstene, Jalovec, Veterna Poruba, Uhorska Ves, Paludzka, Liptovska Ondrasova, Podturen). ALCONET is using wireless (802.11b+) technology to connect their clients.

[edit] Etymology

The name ALCONET was created from the first letters of their slogan ALL CONNECTING NETWORK.
