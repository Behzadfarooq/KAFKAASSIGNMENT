Alchemy (Richard Lloyd album)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Alchemy
Studio album by Richard Lloyd
Released 1979
Recorded 1979
Genre Rock and Roll
Length 36:02
Label Elektra
Producer(s) Michael Young

Alchemy is the solo album of ex-Television guitarist, Richard Lloyd. It was released in 1979, one year after the break up of Television and their second album, Adventure.
