Alcocer de Planes (Valencian: Alcosser de Planes) is a municipality in the Valencian comarca of Comtat, in the province of Alicante, Spain.

It is situated on the shore of the Serpis River, south of the Sierra de Benicadell, in the central part of the Hoya de Cocentaina. It is bordered on the north by Gayanes, on the east by Planes, on the west by Muro de Alcoy, and on the south by Benimarfull.
