Alchemic Dream Inc. is an online gaming company founded in 2001 and whose headquarters are located in Shawinigan, Canada. The company's aim is to offer MMOG (massively multiplayer online games) management solutions to its clients.

[edit] Introduction to the company

Alchemic Dream offers a catalogue of MMO Games (massively multiplayer online games). The different AD Teams handle localization, technical support, customer service, community management and/or ingame events management (campaigns, scenarios and scripts, contests, etc.) for the games they are assigned to, corresponding to the wishes of their respective clients. Those clients include, but aren't limited to, Linden Lab and Codemasters[1] .

The company owns a community portal (PlayMMO) which gathers corporate information and the latest news related to all of AD's products.

Alchemic Dream has its own corporate blog, Dreaming Alchemists, where game masters, CSRs, translators and others talk about their own experiences in and out of the company.

AD has recently launched a subsidiary company, located in Mysore, India (AD India)[2].

The Alchemic Dream Teams are or have been involved in the following MMOG titles:

  • Call of the Warlords II - MMORTS (strategy-based MMO)

  • The Lord of the Rings Online: Shadows of Angmar - MMORPG taking place in Tolkien's Middle-Earth

since 2006

  • Dungeons & Dragons Online - MMORPG based on the ever-popular pen&paper series
  • Archlord - Medieval-fantasy MMORPG

since 2005

  • Dark and Light - Medieval-fantasy MMORPG

since 2003

  • A Tale in the Desert - Social MMO taking place in Ancient Egypt
  • Second Life - Social MMO with a fully customizable universe
  • Entropia Universe - MMORPG with a real economy

since 2002

  • Neocron - Cyberpunk MMORPG (Alchemic Dream's involvement ceased in September 2005)

since 2001

  • Lord of the Rings Trading Card Game
