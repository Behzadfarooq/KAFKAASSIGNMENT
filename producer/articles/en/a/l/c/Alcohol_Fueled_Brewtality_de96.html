Alcohol Fueled Brewtality is a 2 disc, live heavy metal album by the band Black Label Society. Disc 2 consists of studio tracks, of which 'Heart of Gold' is a cover of Neil Young's 1972 hit, and 'Snowblind' is a Black Sabbath cover originally found on Volume 4.
