Alcides Eduardo Mendes de Araújo Alves, best known as Alcides (born in São José do Rio Preto, São Paulo State, March 13, 1985) is a Brazilian footballer who currently plays for PSV Eindhoven on loan from Chelsea F.C and has played with many clubs in Brazil. He also played with Chelsea F.C. and SL Benfica .

Alcides was kidnapped in Brazil, but was released after he convinced his kidnappers he was not a football player.

According to UEFA's official site Alcides is currently owned by Chelsea FC and is being loaned per season similar to the Alex PSV deal.

On February 3, 2007 he played his first Eredivisie match for PSV against AZ Alkmaar [1]
