Antônio Castilho de Alcântara Machado de Oliveira (May 25, 1901 - April 14, 1935) was a Brazilian writer.

  • Outras Terras
  • Pathé-Baby, chronicle, 1926
  • Brás, Bexiga e Barra Funda, short story, 1927
  • Laranja da China, short story, 1928
  • Mana Maria, novela, 1936
  • Cavaquinho e Saxofone, essay, 1940
