Alcalá

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Alcalá is a Spanish placename originally from Arabic al-qalat, "the castle".

  • Alcalá, Valle del Cauca, Colombia
  • Alcalá de Guadaira, a town in Andalucía, Spain
  • Alcalá de Henares, a city in Madrid
  • Universidad de Alcalá de Henares, a university located in Alcalá de Henares
  • Puerta de Alcalá, a monument in Madrid
  • Calle de Alcalá, one of the main streets of Madrid
  • Alcala, Cagayan, a municipality in the Philippines
  • Alcala, Pangasinan, a municipality in the Philippines
  • Niceto Alcalá-Zamora, president of Spain in 1931 and 1936
