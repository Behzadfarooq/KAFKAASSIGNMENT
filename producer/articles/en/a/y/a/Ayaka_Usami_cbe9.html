Ayaka Usami (宇佐美 綾加 Usami Ayaka) is a main character from popular anime and manga series, Gravitation.

[edit] Basic information

She was betrothed to Eiri and likes him very much, but wants what's best for him, so she lets Shuichi stay with Eiri and tries to give up on him. Hiro has a crush on her and later they go on a date together. She's sweet and polite, like the perfect wife, but more caring for Eiri than most would assume.

[edit] Personality

She was Eiri's fiancee, now she's gorgeous Hiroshi Nakano's girlfriend. Charming men into love is her forte, "After you sell a million records", "Take the final leap" and other such unreasonable demands to go out with him. Because this is Hiro we're talking about, he had no problem accomplishing her demands. It appears that Nittle Grasper is one of her favorite bands.
