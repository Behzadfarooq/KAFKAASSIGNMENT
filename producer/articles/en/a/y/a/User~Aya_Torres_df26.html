[edit] Aya Torres


Aya Torres, aka Vaness, is a mixblood artist,

singer and digital photographer.

She was the second lead vocal of 80's band

Sadistic Gypsy, a metal band active from 1980 to 1989.

[edit] Biography

Aya Torres was born in Santiago de Compostela, Spain, on April 11 (year unknown, speculation says was born in the year 1971 or 1972). She has 5 younger sisters and 4 younger brother. Her mother's vocation is unknown and her father was a musician and he is a Doctor.

Aya has a hard childhood and harder as a teen. She left home at very young age and began her singing career. She played drums in a band called Black Snow White and was vocalist on Orion rock band, but was her years on Sadistic Gypsy (metal band) what gave her singing a new direction.


After leaving S G, she started her model work. Aya also starred in little theather characters in College and appeared in several photobooks during this period. It was during this time that Aya built up love for photography.

In actual day she still doing some modeling and phothography work, living a more quiet and relax life somewhere in the caribbean.
