Ayase Station (綾瀬駅, Ayase-eki?) is a JR East and Tokyo Metro station, located in Adachi, Tokyo, Japan. Tokyo Metro maneges this station.

■Joban Line		 (Terminus ・ Forwarding trains)
3・4 ■Joban Line Matsudo・Abiko・Toride

[edit] History

April 1, 1943 : This station opened as a station of Jōban Line.
April 20, 1971 : Chiyoda Line has been Started.
December 20, 1979 : Kita-ayase Branch Line has been Started. This station became the jurisdiction of Teito Rapid Transit Authority.
April 1, 2004 : Teito Rapid Transit Authority was privatized, and Tokyo Metro started.
