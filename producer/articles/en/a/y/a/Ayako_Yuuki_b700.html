SOTO is the hottest, most modern techno-rave dance club / bar&grille in town, and one of the biggest attractions there is their star Full-Contact Waitress--Ayako. Ayako's hobby--when she's not dancing or fighting--is beating up on would-be purse snatchers, rapists and other such thugs who abuse women. She loves dancing and standing out in the crowd. A "playgirl" that's always going in and out of the club. She normally looks to be absent minded, but uses her speedy techniques in battle to make fun of her opponent. She says that it's just like her own style of dance, but...

[edit] Special Moves

  • Mach Spin - Ayako hits the ground and performs a breakdance "windmill" with both legs outstretched, then soars upward, kicking both legs into the air for the final blow.
  • Rave Storm - Ayako cartwheels forward, bringing her heel (which is awash in pink-hued energy) down upon every turn.
  • Tornado Attack - Ayako flies forward and performs a mid-air somersault, ending on the ground with a low kick.
    • Tornado Franken - During the somersault portion of the Tornado Attack, Ayako kicks her legs out, wraps them around her opponent's head and flips them up and over with a pro-wrestling style "Franken-steiner" throw.
      • Tornado Fan Chop - While attempting the Tornado Franken, if Ayako is out of range, she will bring down her fan instead to strike the opponent.
  • Final Tornado Illusion Crisis - Ayako's super. Ayako performs a handstand and lifts her opponent into the whirlwind created by the swirling of her legs above.
  • Spiral Dive - Her ultimate super. Essentially another "power up" of the Mach Spin, Ayako drags her opponent with her into the air, and then after coming down, spins upon her prone body, grinding her heels into the opponent's back.

[edit] Anime

Her appearance is short in the OVAs, also maintaining a similar story, like the rest of the characters. Satomi Yajima, who recently makes the contract with Reimi Jyahana, goes to SOTO to fight against Ayako. Inmediately Ayako likes Satomi and tries to flirt with her during the fight, In the end, Ayako is defeated by Satomi, who performs one of her flamming attacks and engulfing Ayako in flames, finish her naked on the floor of the nightclub.

[edit] Trivia

  • Ayako is the first lesbian character in video games, having especially a love interest in Satomi Yajima.
  • Ayako was the joke character of the series, before Tamao Mitsurugi, because her easygoing attitude. After the joinning of Tamao, together makes various funny scenes in the last games.
  • As seen in various of the games and in the anime, Ayako works for Reimi Jyahana.
  • Her voice actress in the series, Yuri Amano, is being known for making the voices of Kiyone Makibi in Tenchi Muyo! and Ibuki in Street Fighter III, as other roles. Also, like Ayako, Yuri is from Kyoto.
  • Ayako always keep close her eyes and never has been seen with her eyes opened.
