Ayame Sohma (草摩 綾女 Sōma Ayame) is a fictional character featured in the Japanese manga and anime series Fruits Basket. The older brother (and antithesis) of Yuki Sohma, he is flamboyant, expressive, and anything but soft-spoken. According to Takaya, he usually wears long-sleeved shirts because he is prone to sunburn (possibly due to his zodiac animal, the snake) He often realizes too late that he has hurt other people emotionally from being self-centered.

Ayame's name is derived from the fifth month of the Japanese lunar calender, Ayamezuki, or Month of falling irises. His name could also just be the common feminine name 'Ayame', meaning iris, though this is speculated.

Ayame transforms into the snake of the Chinese Zodiac whenever he is hugged by the opposite gender, when his body comes under a great deal of stress, or when he gets too cold or too hot.

He is one of the members of the Mabudachi Trio, consisting of the three oldest members of the zodiac. The other two are Shigure Sohma and Hatori Sohma, who are also his best friends. Ayame is also one of the few members of the Jyūnishi who has never been hurt by Akito Sohma directly or indirectly (Kagura Sohma and Ritsu Sohma appear to be the only other ones).

[edit] Relationship with Yuki

Ayame is one of the oldest cursed members of the Sohma clan. Since Yuki was locked up for most of his childhood, Ayame hardly knew his brother at all to the point of even forgetting Yuki's name. His most vivid childhood memory he had of the two of them was when Yuki reached out to Ayame for help, presumably asking for rescue from Akito; however, Ayame responded by slapping his hand away.

Ayame and Yuki do not get along very well until Ayame arrived at Yuki's house, and offered him peaches. Yuki leaves, with Ayame soon following. Shigure notes that peaches and the crab that Ayame brought last time were probably foods that Yuki enjoyed when he was a child, showing that Ayame is trying to make up for when he abandoned Yuki. While Yuki still disapproves of Ayame's outrageous attitude and overly "royal" behavior, he admits that Ayame is somewhat reliable, which Ayame feels he must call and tell Hatori immediately about the news.

Ayame in animal form

The fact that both Ayame and Yuki are members of the Jyūnishi is quite unusual since it is very rare that any of the Jyūnishi are siblings at any given time; this may, however, be caused by the fact that the two are ten years apart. Because their mother was unable to force Ayame to bend to her will, she turned to Yuki and controlled every aspect of his life. Yuki became emotionally both fragile and detached in her attempts to gain status and wealth within the family while she bluntly and tactlessly shot down any attempts he made to refuse her wishes. The unbalanced relationship between mother and son is broken completely and Yuki is finally able to stand up to her when Ayame appears at the parent-teacher conference, interrupting his mother as she dictates Yuki's future to his teacher. The two have been on considerably better terms since.

[edit] Ayame's business

Ayame owns a men's cosplay shop which he named after himself, specializing in "men's fantasies". To most people, the place is quite strange, as Ayame has gotten orders for some very strange things. Ayame and his assistant, Mine Kuramae (pronounced, "Mee-nay"), design and sew the clothing themselves. Mine often wears some of these clothing.

In the manga and anime, Mine is often seen a maid's outfit, and she likes to grab any particularly cute girls who come into the shop and make them try on clothes. She also likes to alter her fashion style depending on her mood and the situation surrounding her. According to her, "fashion possibilities are infinite!"

[edit] In the manga

It is later revealed that Mine is Ayame's girlfriend and the seamstress of the store, wheareas Ayame designs the dresses, erotic as some or all of them may be. In chapter 130, he is freed from the curse right after the moment he confesses his love to her, stating that "I was born to love you." With that, he holds her in a warm embrace, and does not transform.

Ayame has always had a fear of being Alone. There is a sweet moment between Mine and Ayame when he confides this to her. Mine promises him that she will never leave him.
