Aya Kanno (菅野文 KANNO Aya, born 30 January 1980 in Tokyo, Japan; bloodtype: AB) is a Japanese shōjo mangaka. She debuted in the January 2001 issue of Hana to Yume with Soul Rescue. Her work has, so far, only been serialized in Hakusensha shōjo manga anthologies–Hana to Yume and now, primarily, in Bessatsu Hana to Yume (aka. BetsuHana). Her latest work, Otomen, is currently being published serially in BetsuHana.
