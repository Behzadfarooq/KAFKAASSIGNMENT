Ayaka Hibiki

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Ayaka Hibiki (響　綾香, Hibiki Ayaka?), born June 8, 1974 in Tokyo, is a Japanese stage actress and seiyū. She was formerly credited as Hisoka Moriya (森谷　密, Moriya Hisoka?), which is also her real name (森谷　静香, Moriya Hisoka?). She played the role of Miki Kaoru in the musical adaptation of Revolutionary Girl Utena.

[edit] Notable voice roles

  • Hiromi Ohara in Kochikame
  • Eimi Date in Magical Girl Pretty Sammy (TV)
  • Matsuno in Tenchi in Tokyo
