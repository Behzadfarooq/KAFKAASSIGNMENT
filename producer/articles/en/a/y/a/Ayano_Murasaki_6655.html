Ayano Murasaki (紫彩乃 - Murasaki Ayano) (born March 29, 1969) is a feminist Japanese pornographic actress. Murasaki is one of the first superstars of erotica, with a career in Japan.

Murasaki's first film was in the end of 2003. In 2005, Murasaki received an adult film best actress award for Million Madam.
