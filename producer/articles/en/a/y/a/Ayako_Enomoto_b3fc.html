Ayako Enomoto (榎本 亜弥子, Enomoto Ayako?, born April 29, 1985) is a Japanese model and actress.

Born in Kodaira, Tokyo, Ayako won teen magazine nicola's reader model audition in 1999, which led to an exclusive contract with the magazine and jump-started her career as an idol. She is particularly popular with teenage girls.

Ayako has also appeared in glamour photo shoots, movies, and several TV ads.
