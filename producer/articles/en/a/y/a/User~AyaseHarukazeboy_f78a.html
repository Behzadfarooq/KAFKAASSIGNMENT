[edit] Chinese cities

One day I transcribed some data into my notebook from the UNSTAT site. The next day I went to a library bringing the notebook, and check up with a book titled 《中国1990年人口普查资料 第一册》Tabulation on the 1990 population census of The People's Republic of China (ISBN 7-5037-1093-4). I transcribed many Chinese characters of some cities' names from the table number 2-4 of the census-book. And I spent about one week to compare them with en:Wikipedia.
