Ayame

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Ayame may refer to:

  • Ayame, Cote d'Ivoire, a town in Côte d'Ivoire
  • Ayame (train), a limited express service which connects Tokyo, Sawara, Chōshi, and Kashima-Jingū in Japan.

Ayame is also a Japanese given name.

  • Ayame Takahashi (manga artist)
  • Yoshizawa Ayame (1673-1719) - famous onnagata Kabuki actor

The name can refer to the following fictional characters:

  • Ayame (Inuyasha), a wolf girl in InuYasha
  • Ayame of Rurouni Kenshin
  • Ayame Sohma of Fruits Basket
  • Ayame (Tenchu), a character in the Tenchu video game series.
  • Ayane (Dead or Alive) a character in the Dead or Alive video game series.
  • Ayame, the "ramen girl" at Ichiraku Ramen Bar in Naruto
