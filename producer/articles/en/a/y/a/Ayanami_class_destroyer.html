Ayanami class destroyer

Ayanami class destroyer of the Japanese Maritime Self-Defense Force is the successor of the Harukaze class destroyer and with improved weaponry. This class purpose is ASW.

  • Displacement: 1720t standard; 2500t full load
  • Armament
    • 6 3in main guns
    • 4 533 mm torpedo tubes,
    • 2 ASW torpedo racks
    • 2 hedgehog
    • 2 DCT
  • Complement: 220
