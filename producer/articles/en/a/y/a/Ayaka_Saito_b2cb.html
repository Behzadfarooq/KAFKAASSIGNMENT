Ayaka Saito (齋藤 彩夏, Saitō Ayaka?, born on June 2, 1988 in Tokyo) is a seiyū who voiced Momiji Sohma in Fruits Basket, Tomoka Rana Jude in Girls Bravo, and Mitsukuni Haninozuka in the recently aired Ouran High School Host Club. She has a very soft voice (even among female seiyu).

[edit] Trivia

  • She has great respect for her Ouran co-star Maaya Sakamoto, and calls the older lady "Onesama" (お姉さま, a very respectful way of saying "elder sister").
    • Coincidentally, her character in Ouran is the same age as she was when she first voiced him (18).
