Ayano Matsumoto (松本彩乃, Matsumoto Ayano?, born on February 4, 1988) is a female Japanese voice actress. Her hometown is Gunma, Japan.

[edit] Anime cast in

  • Oroshitate Musical Nerima Daikon Brothers as Mako
  • Otome wa Onee-sama ni Koi Shiteru as Yukari Kamioka
  • Ryusei Sentai Musumet as Kurenai Mishina
