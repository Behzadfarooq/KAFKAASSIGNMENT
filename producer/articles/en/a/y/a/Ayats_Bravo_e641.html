Ayats Bravo

The Ayats Bravo is a range of coaches manufactured by Ayats. There are four variants, the Bravo 1, Bravo 2, Bravo 3, and Bravo City, all featuring similar styling cues. They are manufactured in Spain to order, on a wide variety of chassis, and are heavily adapted to customer's specifications.

[edit] Bravo 1

A tri-axle double-deck coach with a full length upper deck. The lower deck extends to the rear axles. The length is dependent on the chassis ordered: 12m, 13.0m, 13.1m, 13.75m and 15m lengths are manufactured, although the 15m variant is not sold in the UK. Seating capacity is dependent on specification, with 89 a typical maximum for the 13.75m vehicle.

[edit] Bravo 2

A single deck coach with a large under-floor storage. Available in 2 or 3 axle variants, as per the Bravo 1 the length is dependent on the chassis ordered: 12m, 13.0m, 13.1m, 13.75m are available. Seating capacity is dependent on specification, with 65 a typical maximum for the 13.75m vehicle.

[edit] Bravo 3

The Bravo 3 is identical to the 1 in construction and design, differing only in terms of the lower deck. The Bravo 3 features extensive luggage compartments to the rear, with the lower deck being significantly shorter, resulting in a lower maximum capacity of 75 for the 13.75m model.

[edit] Bravo City

This double deck bus borrows heavily from the designs of the coaches. It is a purpose-built open-top tour bus, popular with City Sightseeing, several have also been purchased by Arriva Original London Sightseeing Tour for tours in London. In the UK several examples exist using Volvo B7L chassis. Two variants are available, one with a completely open upper deck, and another with a small part of the front upper deck enclosed. Both designs feature a large, curved front window. The Bravo City is notable for being the only purpose-built tour bus with full disabled access, by means of a ramp fitted to the wide, low floor front entrance. However, from August 2006, the East Lancs Visionaire was announced, which also has these features.
