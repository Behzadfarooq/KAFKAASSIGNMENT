Ayane Sakurano is the current musumeyaku top star off the Takarazuka Revue's Flower Troupe. She is from Tsuyama, she is 163cm tall, her birthday is the 11th of July and her blood type is B. She made her debut in the revue in 2002, and, in 2006, became the first of her class to attain the status of top star.

[edit] Trivia

Her favourite foods are strawberries and green asparagus. She loves the colours white and pale pink and wears them a great deal, collects earrings and DVDs, and is a talented pianist.

Well before it was announced that she would be Sumire Haruno's new top partner, she appeared as a minor performer in the latter's 2005 concert I Got Music. The first act showed the musical journey of a percussionist, Osada-kun, played by Sumire Haruno, who was secretly in love with the pianist in his orchestra... Ayane Sakurano.

Some of the public opinions stated that she was too young and inexperienced to play opposite a star of Sumire Haruno's stature, but it is worth noting that many of Takarazuka's legendary musumeyaku, such as Mari Hanafusa and Kurara Haruka became top after an even shorter time in the revue. She has been quoted as saying that she particularly wished one day to play Marie in Utakata no Koi, a role which was created by Kurara Haruka, and during Flower Troupe's National Tour in late 2006, her wish will be granted.
