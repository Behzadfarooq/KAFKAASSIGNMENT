[edit] Ayame

I am a newcomer to Wikipedia. I enjoy the site very much and have used it a lot for myself in the past. I wanted to start contributing, so I signed up.

In the real world, I live in El Paso, Texas, USA. My real name is Susan and I'm a freelance artist with a BFA from the Cleveland Institute of Art. I plan to go to graduate school to attain my Library Science Degree in the near future. I am married to a sergeant in the US Army. We have a girl, who is moderately autistic and a son who is a toddler.
