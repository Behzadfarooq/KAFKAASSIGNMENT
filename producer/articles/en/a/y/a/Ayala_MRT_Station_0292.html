Ayala MRT Station is a station on the Manila MRT Blue Line (MRT-3). Ayala station is one of the many elevated stations that can be found on the line. The station is located in Makati City and is named because of its location near two places that bear the Ayala name: Ayala Center and Ayala Avenue, although the former is more associated with the station name than the latter.

The station is the third station for trains headed to North Avenue and the eleventh station for trains headed to Taft Avenue. The most recognizable landmark that the station is located at is Ayala Center, one of the most recognizable shopping malls in the Philippines. It is one of two stations that are considered within Makati's central business district, the other being Buendia.

[edit] Transportation links

Ayala MRT Station is considered a major transportation hub, with multiple bus stops lying under the station. Passengers can board or depart buses to and from destinations within Metro Manila and also to the provinces. Taxis also stop at the station, stopping near the main entrance at Ayala Center.

The station is also a short walk from Ayala Center, with the station's main entrance lying beside SM Makati.
