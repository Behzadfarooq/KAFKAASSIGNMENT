A'YANNA T. WEBSTER, is the Founder and President of WINNING WITHIN, Inc. an educational nonprofit organization that provides training, workshops, and conferences, geared toward empowering and maximizing the potential in all people

A’Yanna is an accomplished speaker, trainer, entrepreneur, and inspirational vocalist. She is known for her straightforward and high-energy style of equipping audiences with the insight, tools, and practical applications necessary to unleash their full potential. Her presentations are centered on the "Winning Within Philosophy," which asserts that there is power within each of us that must be recognized and developed so that it can manifest itself as success in our daily lives. That power is the Winner in You!


A’Yanna learned the importance of maximizing her own personal power as a result of adversities she faced as a young girl. Born to a young single mother, she grew up in a three- bedroom house with thirteen other relatives. Surrounded by violence, drug and alcohol abuse, A’Yanna was determined to make a better future for herself. Despite this and other early challenges she faced, A’Yanna persevered to better herself through education. She went on to pay her way through college by competing in the Miss America Pageant Organization, where she became Miss Kansas City and a Miss Missouri Finalist A'Yanna Webster Miss Kansas City, 1998



Currently, A’Yanna speaks for Monster.com’s Making It Count Programs and is a facilitator for Monster’s Diversity Leadership Program. A’YANNA is a Certified Abstinence Educator and speaks to both youth and young adults on this and other life skills topics. She is Member of the elite Les Brown’s Speakers Network, the National Speakers Association, and is a trainer for SkillPath Seminars.

She is an entrepreneur, who has served as a trainer, speaker, and coach for a $2 million a year MLM. In her coaching role, she partnered with more than 300 clients and business associates empowering them to achieve personal, financial, and physical success.

As a speaker and a vocalist, A’Yanna blends her passion for inspiration with her gift of singing into what she calls “Songspiration™.” She uses this distinctive combination to impact audiences with signature pieces such as, “There’s a Winner in You,” “I Dream in Color,” and “If You Believe.”

A’YANNA is the Author of the book, Winning Ways: How to Achieve Success No Matter What! This is the first installment in the Winning Ways book series. She is also the author of several articles and empowerment audio CD’s and DVD products. In addition to her work in the field of “people empowerment,” she has been successful in fostering both a media and non-profit career. A’Yanna has worked as reporter for a local NBC affiliate and hosted two radio talk shows. She has consulted and worked for both local and national non-profits in the area of management. She holds a Bachelors of Arts degree in Radio TV Communication and Masters of Public Affairs (MPA) with an emphasis in Non-Profit Management from Park University.
