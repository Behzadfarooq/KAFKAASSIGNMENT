Ayse Osman was a fictional character in the BBC soap opera EastEnders. She was played by Mine Keylan.

[edit] History

Ayse only appeared once on screen, on 20 June 1985. She was a Turkish Cypriot sister of Ali Osman, and she came to care for his wife Sue after their baby, Hassan, died from cot death. She only spoke Turkish.

[edit] Family

  • Father: Hassan Osman
  • Brothers: Mehmet Osman, Ali Osman
  • Nephews: Murat Osman, Rayif Osman, Hassan Osman (deceased), Little Ali Osman
  • Niece: Emine Osman
