[edit] Satellite-derived products

  • Wind shear (arrows = direction the tops are blown off)
    • University of Wisconsin
      • Upper-level
        For systems with deep convection
        150-300mb layer mean minus 700-925mb layer mean; has 5-day loop, colored and contour version
      • Mid-level
        For systems with shallow convection
        400-600mb layer mean minus 700-925mb layer mean; has 5-day loop, colored only
  • Sea surface temperature
    • From the Atlantic Oceanographic and Meteorological Laboratory
      SST (7-day loop), Heat content (7-day loop), 26Â°C isotherm depth, sea height anomaly (i.e. for Loop Current)
    • From the NHC Tropical Forecasting and Analysis Branch
      Daily SST, 7-day SST and anomaly
  • Dvorak technique
    • University of Wisconsin
      Advanced Objective
    • Air Force Weather Agency
