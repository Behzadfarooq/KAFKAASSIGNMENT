The phrase ayes first came apparent whilst a game of pro evolution soccer was taking place. James Cumiskey felt the need to exclaim 'aayyesss' when his opponent was nearly scoring. Ayes is the game that is currently sweeping a nation.It originated in the dining room of an elitist, William Storey.

It is played with a tennis ball or the official ayes ball(a soft miniature football) on a court approximately 2 metres by 5 metres it is played much like volleyball. 3 players on a side with 2 judges players have to try and get the ball to bounce on the other teams half of the court.

You can also score a point if the ball bounces outside the court wen touched last by the opposing side.

In the outdoor game the game is played up to 9 points however in the indoor version of the sport it is played until one team reaches 11 points. Also a team must be winning by 2 clear points to win.

Players must not play the ball down with their hands and the only way to play the ball down is using the upper half of the body.

The ball can be played up with any part of the body except players must not use their feet during the game of ayes. Ayes is a game dominated by hands. Serves must pass above knee height and when serving the server must have at least one part of his body touching the floor behind the base line. Teams serve until they lose a point but points can be won when a team is not serving.

There is no net in ayes just two sides of the court. If a team is leading by 5 points then this is known as 'plate of ayes' and if a team is leading by 10 points this is known as 'eyes soup'.

The 7 inventors also known as the elite are Andrew Lodge, James Cumiskey, James Tompkinson, William Storey, Daniel White, Nicholas Whaley and Stephen Mckenna. The current Ayes World Champions are Team Win consisting of James Tompkinson, James Cumiskey and Andrew Lodge. They will accept challenges from anyone across the globe. James Coooney is regarded as the best judge and cameraman in Ayes today.

Happy Ayesing.
