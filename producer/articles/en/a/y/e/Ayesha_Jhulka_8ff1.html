Ayesha Jhulka (Hindi: अयेशा झुल्का, Urdu: اَیّشا جھُلکا) was a famous actress in Bollywood - Hindi/Indian movies in the late 80's and early 90's. She is the daughter of Wing Commander Jhulka, an Indian Air Force Officer. Some of her best films were Khiladi with Akshay Kumar, and Jo Jeeta Wohi Sikandar with Aamir Khan which has the famous song "Pehla Nasha".She was more noted for her high profile boyfriends like Akshay Kumar and Nana Patekar than her acting abilities.She stopped acting for a long while until recently where she appeared in supporting roles in films like Socha Na Tha (2005) and Umrao Jaan (2006).
