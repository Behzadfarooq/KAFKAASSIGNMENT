Ayesha Qaddafi

Ayesha Qaddafi (Arabic: عائشة القدافي) is the daughter of Libyan leader Muammar al-Qaddafi. She is a lawyer by profession. In July 2004 she joined the legal defense team of Saddam Hussein.
