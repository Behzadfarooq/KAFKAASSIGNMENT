Aytakin (آیتکین)

[edit] .


Aytakin was born in Tehran, Iran. I am 90% of Persian blood and 10% of Azari blood (My grandfather on my dad's side is from Ashlagh, in the East Azarbaijanprovince. He lived a happy and privileged life enjoying his hobbies of astronomy, Computersand football (or soccerto you North Americans!) And I consider my self a great player, since I trained under great coaches and also my grandfather(on my mom's side) was a pro soccer player. At the age of 10, when he moved to Canada, his passion of Iranian Historystarted to kick in. And since then he has been obsessed with the subject.

[edit] Passion of Iranian History

As most people know, Iran or Persia has had a long and rich history full of great kings, power, and well over 8 Million square km of area. After Aytakin moved to Canada, he started to really miss his home land and the way he found to stary connected was to learn about his country's past. He has contributed much to wikipedia on the topics of Iran, Iranian politics, Iranian History, and anything else he had knowledge about.

[edit] Currently Working On

Currently, I am working on many projects. I am creating a website for everything Persian, which includes everything from music and live TV to a colossal history section. Also, I am working on a book which will hopefully be finished by the end of 2007. The book will focus on Iran's hisory and the Persian Empire, but mainly I will discuss Cyrus and his religious beliefes. And finally I am writing a smaller book on the relations of Iran and Russia during the 20th Century.
