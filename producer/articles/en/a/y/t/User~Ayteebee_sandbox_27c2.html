[edit] Background

My real name is Andy Blower. I live in the UK and am Derbyshire-born and bred. I have just finished school and am on a gap year before I go to university to study Mechanincal Engineering. I like many things, but I very much dislike starting many sentences in a row with the word "I"...

[edit] Interests

I have interests in the following areas:

  • Computers and computing, specifically the Linux operating system and building computers
  • Inventing
  • Fabrication, machining, manufacturing etcetera
  • Aircraft
  • Fitness and Exercise
  • Music
  • Military stuff, for example weapons, camouflage and equipment
  • Christianity

[edit] Aims and intentions in life

I am a Christian and want to do something amazing for Christ. I'm currently trying to figure out how I can invent stuff for Jesus, but haven't quite reached a conclusion yet.  :)

With regard to Wikipedia, I would like to expand and reformat the manufacturing section of Wikipedia, fill in all the missing articles etc. This would give me a good opportunity to develop my knowledge and understanding of manufacturing technology.
