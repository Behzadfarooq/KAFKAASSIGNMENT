Aylin Aslım

Aylin Aslım is a Turkish Pop-Rock singer.

Aylin was born in Lich, Hessen, Germany into a Turkish family but moved back to Turkey later on. Her first album Gelgit was more of a techno-pop album. However, she switched to a Pop-rock sound for her later releases. Aylin's current band consists of Ayşe Özgümüş on guitars and Ayça Sarıgül on bass, with Aylin on vocals, and a non-regular drummer.
