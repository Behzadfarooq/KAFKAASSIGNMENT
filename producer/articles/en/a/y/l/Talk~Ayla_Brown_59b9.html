Start here: WP:CITE. If the reliable source being cited is a webpage, the simplest way is to provide a link to that page after the relevant passage. Just place the URL inside single brackets like this: [http://www.reliablesource.com]. Additional citation methods are discussed here: WP:CITE#Citation_styles.

[edit] NPOV & Cleanup

This article needs A LOT of work!! Remember that this needs to be encyclopedic, not a site "selling" Ayla Brown. To see Wikipedia's Neutral Point of View requirement for articles please start at WP:NPOV.

Examples of NPOV in this article

  • Ayla’s debut album is packed with potential singles
  • This put her in the amazing stat of 6th place leading scorer in the US, male & female!!!

Other than NPOV, this article needs cleanup badly. Many grammatical mistakes, typos, redundancies, non-notable information, lack of references, and other errors need to be corrected. If these aren't corrected, it is possible that this article may be up for deletion, even if Ayla Brown meets the notability guidelines for wikipedia.

Please also refer to What Wikipedia is Not. Thanks! Happy editing!
