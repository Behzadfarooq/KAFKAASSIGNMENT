Aylín Mújica (b. 24 November 1974) is a Cuban-born actress, model, and ballet dancer who currently lives in Mexico City, Mexico.

She posed nude in the Spanish magazine Interviu and appeared on a sexy photoshoot at the H para Hombres magazine as well.

She has participated in several Mexican TV Shows. She conducted the TV Azteca's morning weekend TV Show "Tempranito" alongside the Mexican actress Anette Michel.

As of June 2006 she was appearing in the telenovela Amor en Custodia.

At this day, she has stopped working with the TV Azteca company.
