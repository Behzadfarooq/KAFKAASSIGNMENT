Ayukawa (鮎川 "freshwater trout river"), is a Japanese surname.

[edit] People named Ayukawa

  • Ami Ayukawa (born 1981), Japanese AV idol
  • Mami Ayukawa, Japanese singer
  • Taiyo ayukawa, (born 1991), member of the J-pop group Ya-Ya-yah
  • Yoshisuke Aikawa (1880 - 1967), Japanese businessman, who was the founder and first of the president of Nissan zaibatsu (also known as Gisuke Ayukawa)
