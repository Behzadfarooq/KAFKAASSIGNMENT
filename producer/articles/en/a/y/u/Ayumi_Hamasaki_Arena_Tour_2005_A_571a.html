Ayumi Hamasaki Arena Tour 2005 A
Live album by Ayumi Hamasaki
Released August 24, 2005
Genre J-pop

Ayumi Hamasaki's Arena Tour 2005 A ~My Story~ was released August 24, 2005. Almost all the tracks on her MY STORY album are performed on the DVD.
