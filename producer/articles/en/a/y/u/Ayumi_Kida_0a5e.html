Ayumi Kida (喜田あゆ美 Kida Ayumi; born June 7, 1966) is a Japanese-born actress. Her voice-over credits include the Japanese dub voices of Dexter from Cartoon Network's Dexter's Laboratory and Dib from Nickelodeon's Invader Zim.
