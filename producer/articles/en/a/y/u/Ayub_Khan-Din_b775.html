Ayub Khan-Din

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Ayub Khan-Din (1961 - ) is a playwright and actor, who was born in Salford, Lancashire, England.

As an actor, Khan-Din is best-known for the role of Sammy in Hanif Kureishi's Sammy and Rosie Get Laid (1987). He is also an award-winning playwright, best known for East is East which was also released as a film in 1999, starring Om Puri.
