ayu-mi-x remix album is the first remix album that Ayumi Hamasaki has released. The album was released at March 17, 1999. ayu-mi-x remix album contains 2 discs. Disc 1 is the Remix Club Side, which contains remixes. Disc 2 is the Acoustic Orchestra Side, which contains Acoustic Orchestra versions and 3 mixes.
