[edit] Timothy Eisenbeisz

Timothy Eisenbeisz is a senior at Burnsville High School in Minnesota. He plans to attend the University of Minnesota in the fall. His hobbies include PC gaming, especially World of WarCraft, playing french horn, and playing Magic: The Gathering. He is also known to the inhabitants of the island nation of Palau as the Savior of the Maze.

[edit] Savior of the Maze

In 2035, Timothy Eisenbeisz traveled back in time to 1805. There, he ventured to the island of Palau, where he met the inhabitants. They were locked in an elaborate labyrinth deep underground, guarded by a fearsome lion. He fought the lion, tamed it, and rode it around the labyrinth, bringing the natives out of it. A large statue is made in his honor.What A Loser. After fully demonstrating his ability to move in time, he then went back in time to kill his younger sister, whose name and existence were thus erased from the timestream. As if he never did it, because he erased her, creating a paradoxal slipknot, which slips, making things normal in the future timestream. Only in comparison can you observe this conjecture.
