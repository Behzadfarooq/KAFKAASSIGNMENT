Ayub Shah Durrani

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Ayub Shah
Emir
Reign Afghanistan: 1819 - 1823
Predecessor Sultan Ali Shah
Successor Dost Mohammad Khan
Dynasty Durrani dynasty
Father Timur Shah

Ayub Shah, a son of Timur Shah, ruled Afghanistan from 1819 to 1823. He was deposed and presumably killed in 1823 at the end of the Durrani dynasty.
