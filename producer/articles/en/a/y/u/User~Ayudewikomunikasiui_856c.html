A clear example of WiMax and its utility for a constant access, is in the infrastructure building process that is meant to provide distribution of internet in Aceh after the great tsunami. Due to this great natural disaster, the majority of the infrastructure telecommunication wire set was severely damaged in the west coast of Aceh. Yet the access for internet in high speed was very expected especially to coordinate within supporting teams in the recovery operation. It is impossible to re-invest for a new wire set in a short time to replace the broken ones. The most possible solution is to use Wireless infrastructure. As for theWireless, WiFi network emerges as the common technology in hand that works in a frequency of 2,4 GHz. In the case of Aceh, it may not come as the perfect solution since it has a limited reach area as well as the current of data accessible.

For the instalation that covers a vast area such as the west coast of Aceh, the most possible work done would only reach around 10 MBps, and still supported with an instalation of mesh network. To cope the need of Banda Aceh city followed by several other cities including Meulaboh and Calang, a more stable solution with greater bandwidth is necessary. Intel Corporation as one of the donators for Aceh Telco Relief project chooses to provide a set of Wireless Broadband Access (WBA) BreezeACCESS VL by Alvarion. This set works on a frequency of 5,25 – 5,35 GHz and 5,725 – 5,850.
