Ayumi Murata (村田あゆみ, Murata Ayumi?), born on January 12, 1982, is a female Japanese singer from Tokyo, Japan. She has also played the role of Minako Aino in the Sailor Moon musicals from 2001-2002.

[edit] Anime performed for

  • Happiness!
  • Kappa no Kaikata
  • Lamune
  • Memories off 3.5 - Omoide no Kanata e
