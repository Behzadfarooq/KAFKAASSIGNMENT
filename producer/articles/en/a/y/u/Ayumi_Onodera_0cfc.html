Ayumi Onodera

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Ayumi Onodera (小野寺 歩, Onodera Ayumi?) is a Japanese curler, born November 25, 1978 in Tokoro District, Abashiri Subprefecture, Hokkaido Prefecture.

[edit] History

Onodera is a member of Team Aomori which was selected to represent Japan at the 2006 Winter Olympics [1]. At the Games she threw last as skip and led her team to seventh place out of the ten teams with a 4-5 record, including a surprise win over one of the usual curling powerhouses, Canada.
