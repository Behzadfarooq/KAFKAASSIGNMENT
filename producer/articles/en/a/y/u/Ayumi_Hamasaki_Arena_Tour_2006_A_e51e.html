Ayumi Hamasaki Arena Tour 2006 A
Live album by Ayumi Hamasaki
Released November 1, 2006
Genre J-pop

Ayumi Hamasaki's Arena Tour 2006 A ~(miss)understood~ was released November 1, 2006. Almost all the tracks on her (miss)understood album are performed on the DVD. Sold copies: 90,080
