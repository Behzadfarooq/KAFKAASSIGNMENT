Ayumi Hamasaki COMPLETE CLIP BOX A, by Ayumi Hamasaki, was released on February 25, 2004.

Ayumi Hamasaki 's Complete Clip Box is a DVD box set compiling all of Hamasaki Ayumi's PVs from 1998 to 2003 on three discs. It also includes various commercials advertising the release of her singles and albums. Be aware that when they say "clips", they mean the entire PV.
