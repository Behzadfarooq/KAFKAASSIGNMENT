Ayngaran International is an overseas film distributor of Tamil language films.

[edit] Description

The company distributes Tamil films to countries other than India. They are well known for their crystal-clear DVD film copies. They are responsible for transforming the Indian regional films into North American and European format videos. Since the company started, they distributed audio in audio cassettes and compact discs, and videos in VHS. In 2001 they began to distribute DVD videos and later ceased VHS distribution. They provide subtitles in English and a variety of selections on each DVD film. Recently in 2006, they added French and German languages to the subtitle seletion for films.

[edit] Distributions

The company is responsible for about 95 per cent of Indian Tamil films being distributed around the world. They also create custom home videos and audios apart from the Tamil films such as:


  • Isai Thendral/Digital Isai Thendral - A collection of video songs from films which are released in volumes.
  • Hits of Actor's name goes here - Music videos which star a specific actor/actress. Example: Hits of Ajith or Hits of Jyothika
  • Other special audio releases have randomnized film soundtracks in a CD.
