Aydın Yılmaz (born on January 29, 1988 in Eminönü, İstanbul) is a Turkish soccer player in midfield position. He is currently playing for Galatasaray. He is 176 cm and 65 kg. Aydın played for the Turkish U-17 team that came 4th in the 2005 world youth championship in Peru. Aydın scored the winning goal of his team in his debut Turkcell Super League game. Since then, he is constantly given chance to show his skills, coming on as a substitute for either Ilic or Hasan Sas.

Formerly he played for Galatasaray youth team.
