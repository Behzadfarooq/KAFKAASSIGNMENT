Hello, Aydee/Hi, and welcome to Wikipedia! Congratulations on the creation of your account and your first successful edit(s); you seem to be off to a good start. There are a number of pages with useful information for newcomers: the very basics of how to edit a page, tutorials for both editing articles and using pictures, and advice on how to write a great article.

Other links to keep in mind are the conventions for naming pages, the guide to avoiding common mistakes, and the excellent Manual of Style. Finally, you may find it useful to know what Wikipedia is not.

I hope that will you enjoy editing here and join the community of Wikipedians! As a courtesy, you can sign your name on Talk and vote pages (not in articles!) by using three tildes, like this: ~~~. Four tildes (~~~~) will produce both your name and the current date. If you have any unanswered questions, feel free to ask me on my talk page, check out the help pages or the frequently asked questions, or add your question to the village pump.
