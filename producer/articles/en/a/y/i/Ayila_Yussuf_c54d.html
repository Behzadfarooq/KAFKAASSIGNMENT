Ayila Yussuf is a professional Nigerian football player. He is a defensive midfielder for FC Dynamo Kyiv. A former Nigerian youth international, Yussuf was acquired by Dynamo from Nigerian side Union Bank in 2003. Despite injuries, Yussuf has become a regular for both Dynamo Kyiv and Nigerian national team.
