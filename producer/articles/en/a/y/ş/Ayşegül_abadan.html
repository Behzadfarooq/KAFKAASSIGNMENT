Ayşegül Abadan (1980) Turkish pianist


[edit] Life

Ayşegül Abadan was born in Ankara in 1980. Her mucis life began when she started playing the piano during his primary school years. After completing her education at Istanbul Guzel Sanatlar Lisesi (High School) Music Department as a pianist, she went to Yıldız Teknik Ünivercity Sanat Tasarım Faculty Piano Department and finished.

She has three albums.
