Bad Blood is a British made film, directed by Hettie Macdonald, and based on the memoir of the late Lorna Sage, a story of love, ambition and failure. The film features Academy Award nominee Miranda Richardson and Academy Award nominee John Hurt.

The film was originally scheduled for release in 2005, had been intended to start pre-production, and now appears to have been shelved.
