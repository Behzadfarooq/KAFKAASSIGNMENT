[edit] Alphabetical list

[edit] #

  • 17 - appeared in the television series Dr. Shrinker, winner of the 1977 PATSY award. [1]

[edit] A

  • Amber - one of two Abyssinian cats who played the title role in The Cat from Outer Space. [2]
  • Andy - pet of Florida State Senator Ken Myer, holds the record for the longest non-lethal fall at 16 stories. [3]
  • Angus - cat from the movie Jinxed!. [4]
  • Ariel - "orange Persian" pet of Carl Van Vetchen, appeared in the book The Tiger in the House. [5]

[edit] B

  • Beerbohm, a tabby who was London's longest-serving theater mouser from the Globe Theatre. [6]
  • Beeswax - cat from the movie Her Alibi. [7]
  • Binky - pet and inspiration for author Susan Becker's book All I Need to Know I Learned From My Cat. [8]
  • Bismark - pet of the owner and namesake for the Morgel Bismark criterium. [9]
  • Bob - cat from the movie One Fine Day. [10]
  • Boche - one of Anne Frank's cats. [11]
  • Boo boo kitty - Shirley's stuffed cat from Laverne & Shirley. [12]
  • Bouhaki - long considered, incorrectly, to be the first cat known to have a name. [13]
  • Brownie - one of Dr. William Grier's cats, left $415,000.00 in the doctor's will. [14]

[edit] C

  • Catarina - Edgar Allan Poe's cat and the inspiration for his story The Black Cat. [15]
  • Childebrand, a black and tan cat that resembled a tiger from ThÃ©ophile Gautier's La Menagerie Intime. [16]
  • Cownose - overweight cat of Rick Swan from InQuest Gamer. [17]

[edit] D

  • Dalton the wonder cat - author(?) of the book My Litter Box Was Dirty... and host of several television programs. [18]
  • Don Pierrot de Navarre - pure white cat from Theophile Gautier's La Menagerie Intime. [19]
  • Dusty - holder of the world record for most kittens in one lifetime, 420. [20]

[edit] F

  • Felix - played the role of Chuck the cat in Heart Condition. [24]
  • Fred - Robert Crumb's family cat and the basis of Fritz the Cat. [25]
