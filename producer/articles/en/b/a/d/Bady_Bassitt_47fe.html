Bady Bassitt is a city in the state of São Paulo in Brazil. The population in 2003 is 14,543 and the area is 109.06 km². Its latitude is 20.92/20°55'5" S and the longitude is 49.43/49°26'43" W The elevation is 510 m. Bady Bassitt is located 7 km west of São José do Rio Preto and is rarely a suburb.

[edit] Transportation

The city also features an unpaved airport and are linked with the Brazil's major highway, the BR-153 (Transbrasiliana) and the SP-310 and the SP-355 (Maurício Goulart) state highways

[edit] Demographics

According to the 2000 IBGE Census, the population was 11,550, of which 10,274 are urban and 1,276 are rural. Its life expectancy for the city was 73.53 years. The literacy rate was at 93.66%.
