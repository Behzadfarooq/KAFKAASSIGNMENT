Bad Kreuznach-Land

The German Verbandsgemeinde of Bad Kreuznach-Land includes the villages of Volxheim, Hackenheim, Tiefenthal, Pleitersheim, Pfaffen-Schwabenheim, Biebelsheim, Neu-Bamberg, Frei-Laubersheim and Fürfeld. These villages lie south of the town of Bad Kreuznach. The entire municipality is 48 square kilometers large and has 8000 inhabitants.

Kreuznacher wine are well known wines (white wines).
