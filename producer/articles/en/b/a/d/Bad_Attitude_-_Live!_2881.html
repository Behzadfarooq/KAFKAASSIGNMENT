Bad Attitude - Live!

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Meat Loaf - Live
Starring Meat Loaf
Release date(s) 1981
Language English
IMDb profile

Bad Atitude - Live! is a Live video recording of Meat Loaf. It was recorded during massive brake-downs Meat Loaf and his family were having during the 80's. It was started to promote his newest album in 1985 at the time, Bad Attitude.

[edit] Set List

  1. "Bad Attitude"
  2. "Dead Ringer For Love"
  3. "Midnight at the lost and found"
  4. "Paradise by the Dashboard Light"
  5. "Piece of the Action"
  6. "All Revved Up with No Place to Go"
  7. "Modern Girl"
  8. "Two Out Of Three Aint Bad"
  9. "Bat Out Of Hell"
