Bad English is the self-titled debut album by American rock band supergroup Bad English, released in 1989.

The band scored two huge hits on the Billboard charts including the #1 hit "When I See You Smile" and top 5 hit "Price of Love".

A five video collection was released, containing the videos for "Best of What I Got", "Heaven is a 4 Letter Word", "Forget Me Not", "When I See You Smile", and "Price of Love".
