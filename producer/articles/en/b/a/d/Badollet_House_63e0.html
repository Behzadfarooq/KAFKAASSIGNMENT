Badollet House
(U.S. National Register of Historic Places)
Location: Salem, Marion County, Illinois, USA
Coordinates: 38°37′42″N, 88°56′53″W
Added to NRHP: December 6, 1990[1]

The Badollet House is one of three properties listed on the National Register of Historic Places in the Marion County, Illinois county seat of Salem. The William Jennings Bryan Boyhood Home and Bachmann House round out the list of Salem Historic Places. The house has been listed in the Register since December 1990.
