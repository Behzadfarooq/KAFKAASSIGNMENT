Welcome to the Radio WikiProject. We are a group dedicated to improving Wikipedia's coverage of radio.

(For more information on WikiProjects, please see Wikipedia:WikiProject and the Guide to WikiProjects).

Goals
  • The goal of this project is to create, expand, and maintain wikipedia articles related to radio, including radio propogation and reception, radio programming, radio personalities, and the business of radio. The existing project Wikipedia:WikiProject Radio Stations covers the creation and maintainance of pages for specific radio stations.
Scope
  • This project intends to include within its scope the science of radio, radio programming, radio personalities, and the business of radio, as well as any other radio-related matters not covered by any existing projects.

[edit] Open tasks

  • Create articles for existing radio networks which do not already exist
  • Create articles for significant radio programs, current and historical, which do not yet exist
  • Create articles for significant radio personalities, current and historical, which do not yet exist
  • Expand existing articles and bring them up to Good article and Feature article status.
