Bad Zurzach

Bad Zurzach is a municipality in the district of Zurzach in the canton of Aargau in Switzerland. It is the seat of the district.

Bad Zurzach was previously known as Zurzach; the population voting for the official name change in a referendum of 21 May 2006.

Bad Zurzach is located on the Rhine and has a thermal water bath. it also has an outdoor bathing facility.
