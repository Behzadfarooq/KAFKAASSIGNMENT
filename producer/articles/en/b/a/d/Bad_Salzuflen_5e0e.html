Bad Salzuflen is a city in the Lippe district of North Rhine-Westphalia, Germany. It has an area of 100.07 km² and 55,020 inhabitants (2003).

Bad Salzuflen, known for its saltwater and thermal baths, is an old city with many historical buildings. It is twinned with Bridlington, a town in the East Riding of Yorkshire, England, Millau in France and Luckenwalde in Brandenburg.

The town is the birthplace of comedian Jürgen von der Lippe.
