Badharghat is a census town in West Tripura district in the state of Tripura, India.

[edit] Demographics

As of 2001 India censusGRIndia, Badharghat had a population of 47,660. Males constitute 51% of the population and females 49%. Badharghat has an average literacy rate of 80%, higher than the national average of 59.5%; with 53% of the males and 47% of females literate. 9% of the population is under 6 years of age.
