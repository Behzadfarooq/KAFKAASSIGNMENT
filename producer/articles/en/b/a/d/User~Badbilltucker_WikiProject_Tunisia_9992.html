[edit] Welcome

Welcome to those who have visited this new WikiProject, the WikiProject Tunisia! The aim of this project is to organise and steer the production of articles relating to the country of Tunisia, its cities, administrative divisions, geography, transportation, culture, history and so on. It aims to expand Wikipedia's resources on Tunisia in a fair and accurate manner.

[edit] Scope

The aims of this project are:

  • To increase the number of articles related to Tunisia
  • To improve the quality of articles related to Tunisia
  • To create as many featured articles about Tunisia as possible

[edit] Project divisions

  • Assessment (talk)- Our assessment department.
  • Categorization (talk) – Categorization of Tunisia-related articles
  • Cities (talk – Cities, towns, villages and administrative districts
  • Physical geography (talk) – Regions, parks, climate, physical features
  • Transportation (talk) – Roads, trails and rails in and around Tunisia
  • Culture (talk) – Music, art, icons, mass communication
  • People (talk) – includes Tunisia natives and those residents who have shaped Tunisia
  • Government (talk) – Governance, as well as national, regional, and local politics in relation to Tunisia
  • History (talk) – history of Tunisia

[edit] Templates

  • {{WikiProject Tunisia}} - Template stating this article is a part of this wikiproject; placed on the top of talk pages, not on article pages under construction
  • {{Tunisia-stub}} - Stub notice for all Tunisia articles that don't fit in any of the smaller groupings below; placed at the bottom of article pages
    • {{Tunisia-geo-stub}} - Stub notice for all articles relating to the geography of Tunisia; to be placed at the bottom of the article pages.
    • {{Tunisia-bio-stub}} - Stub notice for all articles relating to Tunisian people; to be placed at the bottom of the article pages.
      • {{Tunisia-footy-bio-stub}} - Stub notice for all articles relating to the Tunisian football (soccer) players; to be placed at the bottom of the article pages.
