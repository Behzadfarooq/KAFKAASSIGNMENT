Bad Karlshafen is a town in the district of Kassel, in Hesse, Germany. It is situated at the confluence of Diemel and Weser, 15 km south of Höxter, and 37 km north of Kassel.

The city hall of Bad Karlshafen in Hessen, Germany

[edit] Leisure and health

Bad Karlshafen offers a modern health centre and in 1986 a graduation tower was established.
