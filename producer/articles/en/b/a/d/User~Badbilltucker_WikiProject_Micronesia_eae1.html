[edit] Goals

The goals of this project are to:

  • create, improve, and maintain articles relating to the countries of the Micronesia and the surrounding area, their history, people, geography, and culture. Included are the independent nations of Federated States of Micronesia, Kiribati, Marshall Islands, Nauru, Northern Mariana Islands, and Palau, and the territories of Guam and Johnston Island.

[edit] Scope

The scope of this project is all articles which fall in the Category:Micronesia. We acknowledge up front that there may be other WikiProjects, both now and in the future, which deal directly with individual nations or areas within the above. We are grateful for their efforts, and will concentrate our own on those areas which are not covered by a currently existing project.

[edit] Templates

  • {{User WikiProject Micronesia}} is a userbox which all members can add to their talk pages.
  • {{WikiProject Micronesia}} is the project banner, to be added to the talk pages of all articles which fall within the scope of the project.

[edit] Stub templates

  • {{Oceania-stub}} - Stub template for articles relating to the Oceania; to be put on the bottom of each article page.
    • {{Oceania-geo-stub}} - Stub template for articles relating to the geography of the Oceania; to be put on the bottom of each article page.
    • {{Oceania-struct-stub}} - Stub template for articles relating to Oceanian buildings and structures; to be put on the bottom of each article page.
      • {{Oceania-airport-stub}} - Stub template for articles relating to people involved in Oceanian airports; to be put on the bottom of each article page.
      • {{Oceania-sports-venue-stub}} - Stub template for articles relating to Oceanian sports stadiums or venues; to be put on the bottom of each article page.
    • {{Oceania-airline-stub}} - Stub template for articles relating to Oceanian airlines; to be put on the bottom of each article page.
    • {{au-lang-stub}} - Stub template for articles relating to Austronesian languages; to be put on the bottom of each article page.
    • {{Nauru-stub}} - Stub template for articles relating to Nauru; to be put on the bottom of each article page.
    • {{Oceania-labor-org-stub}} - Stub template for articles relating to Oceanian labor organizations; to be put on the bottom of each article page.
    • {{Oceania-footy-bio-stub}} - Stub template for articles relating to Oceanian football (soccer); to be put on the bottom of each article page.
    • {{Oceania-politician-stub}} - Stub template for articles relating to Oceanian politicians; to be put on the bottom of each article page.
