Bad Girls DVD Sets

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

All eight series of Bad Girls have been released onto Region 2 DVD in the UK. Series one through seven have been released onto Region 4 DVD in Australia. Series one of Bad Girls is currently available in the US, with the second series pending release.

[edit] Region 1 DVD Releases

The first season is the only series currently available in the US on Region 1 DVD.

[edit] Season One

DVD Cover of Bad Girls Season One

The colour scheme of the design is green and yellow stripes. A 'keyhole' shape can be seen on the character of Rachel Hicks. The design is completely different compared to the Region 2 Series One release.

The characters on the cover includes: [from L-R] Helen Stewart, Zandra Plackett, Shell Dockley, Rachel Hicks, Crystal Gordon, Denny Blood and Nikki Wade.

The DVD release includes all 10 episodes.

Product Description

BAD GIRLS is a powerful drama series set in the closed world of an English women’s prison, HMP Larkhall. BAD GIRLS has hard-hitting, heart-wrenching stories that get down and dirty with life behind bars, never shying away from the gritty side of life. On the inside they must negotiate their place in the prison’s hierarchy and sometimes make new sexual choices.

Extras

  • Slang Dictionary

Number of Discs

  • 3

[edit] Region 2 DVD Releases

The Region 2 releases. Top row; series one to four, bottom row; series five to eight

All eight series have been released on Region 2 DVD in the UK.

Series One to Four was produced by Contender and Kult TV, but 2 Entertain (formerly VCI) took over the DVD releases, this is why the series one to four designs are different to the series five to eight designs.

[edit] Series One Uncut

The front and back designs on the Series One DVD

The Series One Uncut boxset of Bad Girls was released on June 18, 2001.

A miniture picture of characters Sylvia Hollamby and Lorna Rose can be seen the bottom left hand corner on the front of the box.

Contains all 10 episodes of the first series.

Product Description

Hard-hitting, original and controversial, Bad Girls depicts the trials and triumphs of prison inmates and officers in a notorious women's prison. It's a tense and sexually charged atmosphere and it's a hidden world where anything can happen.

[edit] Series Seven

The front and back designs on the Series Seven DVD

The Series Seven boxset of Bad Girls was released on August 7, 2006.

The front of the box features the characters of Pat Kerrigan, Janine Nabeski, Natalie Buxton and Jim Fenner

Contains all 13 episodes of the seventh series.

Product Description

Bad Girls is back for a seventh series with Jim Fenner facing life on the other side of the bars. As an ex-screw, Jim's a lamb to the slaughter and soon he's begging for protection in the segregation block - along with the paedophiles and rapists. For Jim there's only one way out - death or escape. Also, just when Sylvia Hollamby thinks her life is going to be ruined by an evil `voodoo curse`, her luck changes. The dashing Dr Malcolm Nicholson seems to have fallen for her and Sylvia is over the moon. Meanwhile, the two new inmates, credit card fraudsters Janine Nebeski and Arun Parmar, arrive on G Wing. Janine hates Arun for 'fessing up' in the witness box and she`s bent on bloody revenge...

Extras

  • Photo Gallery
  • Bonus Episode - 2005 Christmas Special

[edit] Series Eight

The front and back designs on the Series Eight DVD

The Series Eight Uncut boxset of Bad Girls was released on December 26, 2006.

The front of the box features the characters of Pat Kerrigan, Doctor Rowan Dunlop, Lou Stoke, Donny Kimber and Natalie Buxton.

Contains all 11 episodes of the eighth series.

Product Description

Bad Girls is back for a hard-hitting eighth series. Life on G-Wing is shaken up irrevocably when new Wing Governor Louise 'Lou' Stoke (Amanda Donohoe) takes the helm at a time of crisis. Ex-Army Governing Governor Joy Masterton (Ellie Haddington) likes to run G Wing with a rod of iron, but Lou has some very different ideas and she`s not afraid to fight for them.

With a suspected terrorist on the wing, new Prison Officer Donny Kimber (Sid Owen) is on a secret mission. Fancying himself as a bit of a 'James Bond', he couldn't be more thrilled when he`s asked to be a secret Police Liaison Officer. He's working undercover but how long will it take until he`s found out - especially when he gets cosy with one of the inmates?

Pat Kerrigan (Liz May Brice), still regarded as top dog, is driven to the edge by Natalie Buxton (Dannielle Brent). Could this be the end for one of them?

Prison Officers and inmates alike are in for a tough ride this year.

Extras

  • Photo Gallery
  • Bonus Episode - 2006 Christmas Special
