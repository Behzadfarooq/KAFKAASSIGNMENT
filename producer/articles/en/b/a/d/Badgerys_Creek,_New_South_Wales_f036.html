Badgerys Creek is a suburb of Sydney, New South Wales, Australia. It is in the Local Government Area of the City of Liverpool and is adjacent to Kemps Creek and Austral. Its postcode is 2555.

For several years, Badgerys Creek has been the proposed site for a second international airport for Sydney, but no action has taken place. While Badgerys Creek itself is unpopulated, several small towns are nearby and suburban development in south-western Sydney has reached within 10 km of the proposed site, and residents of these areas have lobbied on an aircraft noise platform.

Sydney Airport, a subject of complaints about aircraft noise, has temporarily alleviated the problem by introducing curfew times.
