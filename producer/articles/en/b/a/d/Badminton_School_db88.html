Badminton School is an independent girls boarding school situated in Westbury-on-Trym, Bristol, United Kingdom.

[edit] Notable alumnae

  • Dame Iris Murdoch — English writer
  • Rosamund Pike — actress most famous for her role of Miranda Frost in the James Bond film, Die Another Day
  • Indira Gandhi — Indian Prime Minister
  • Polly Toynbee — UK Journalist
  • Claire Bloom — Actress
