Featured Deletion Discussions in Wikipedia

Featured deletion discussions are what we believe to be the best deletion discussions on Wikipedia. Prior to being listed here, discussions are reviewed at Wikipedia:Featured deletion discussion candidates for animosity, snarkiness, sockpuppetry and overall entertainment value according to our deletion discussion criteria. Of course, it helps a lot if the article actually gets deleted.

At present, there aren't any featured deletion discussions out of ... well, no one's ever really bothered to keep track of how many we've had. Deletion discussions that no longer meet the criteria can just be nominated for deletion (We wouldn't have it any other way).

Deletion discussion titles in boldface indicate that they have been featured on the main page. marks articles that have been recreated as an audio shouting match (They're worth listening to, believe us).
