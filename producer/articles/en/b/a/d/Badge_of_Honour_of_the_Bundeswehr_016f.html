Badge of Honour of the Bundeswehr

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Badge of Honour of the Bundeswehr (German: Ehrenzeichen der Bundeswehr) is a German decoration of the Bundeswehr.

The decoration is usually awarded for long and loyal service however it could also be awarded for valor in combat and other merits regardless of time of service, it could also be awarded to foreign soldiers.

The decoration has four grades:

  • Medal of Honour of the Bundeswehr (Ehrenmedaille der Bundeswehr) for 7 months of loyal service.
  • Cross of Honour of the Bundeswehr in Bronze (Ehrenkreuz der Bundeswehr in Bronze) for 5 years of loyal service.
  • Cross of Honour of the Bundeswehr in Silver (Ehrenkreuz der Bundeswehr in Silber) for 10 years of loyal service.
  • Cross of Honour of the Bundeswehr in Gold (Ehrenkreuz der Bundeswehr in Gold) for 20 years of loyal service.

The ribbon is black with two red strips beside it and golden strips of the edges as the colors of the German flag, the ribbon bar has a small claps of the decoration attached to it.
