Badri Lal Chitrakar is an Indian miniature art artist. He has expertise in all traditional Indian arts. He was awarded with Shilp Guru award on September 9, 2006.He hails from Bhilwara town in Rajasthan state in India.

[edit] Other awards

He has been conferred following other awards:

  • Shilp Guru Award, September 9, 2006
  • National Award of Master craftperson, Year 1986
  • Pune Festival Award

[edit] Heritage of Indian art

He and his succesors/students are keeping traditional Indian art schools alive. He has practiced and create different techniques for different schools of Indian art.
