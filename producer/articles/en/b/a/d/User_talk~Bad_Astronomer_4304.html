Explanations and welcome

Howdy Bad Astronomer. I'll just briefly mention, that the corollary of your being able to edit any page, is that anyone else can too. With over 1 million articles, and 1 million registered users, this is a fairly complex endeavour! The formal "please only link according to our guidelines" messages are templates for warning new editors that their additions are going to keep being removed, so they might want to read the relevant rules to avoid futile effort.
