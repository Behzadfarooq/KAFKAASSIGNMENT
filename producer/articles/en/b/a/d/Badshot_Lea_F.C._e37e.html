Badshot Lea F.C.

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Club crest

Badshot Lea F.C. is a football club based in Badshot Lea, near Farnham, Surrey, England. They entered the Hellenic Football League Division One East in 2003 from the Surrey County Intermediate League (Western) Premier Division. They were established in 1904 and are currently members of the Hellenic Football League Division One East.

06-07 season they are in first place in the Hellenic Football League Division One East.
