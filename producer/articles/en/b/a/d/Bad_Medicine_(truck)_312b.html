Bad Medicine was a monster truck that raced on the USHRA circuit from 1991 and 1992. It was owned and driven by Don Van Loo. The truck was the famous Carolina Crusher 2, however modified to fit the smaller Dodge Dakota body. The truck was notorious for its crash at oklahoma in its first race and it's out of control behaviour. Notably, it was involved in an incident at a show in Niagara Falls, New York which resulted in the death of one of the track crew, the sport's first fatality and one of only a few in the sport's history.

Magnum Force was the successor to Bad Medicine, and was also campagined by Van Loo. The truck was one of the first Dan Patrick trucks, and was more successful than its predecessor. The truck was sold Brian Barthel in 1996, renamed Midnight Maniac with co-owner Don Williams and later became Little Tiger
