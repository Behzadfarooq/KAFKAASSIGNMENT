[edit] About

I'm sixteen years-old, work at Meijer, and have an obsession with hand bags. I love Urban Outfitters and BoA. Diet Coke and pizza are what I live on. After high school I plan to attend the Cleveland Institute of Art or the Columbus College of Art and Design, majoring in Illustration or Fine Arts (I am also interested in Graphic Design, Fashion design, and Photography). Another college I'm considering is Parsons The New School for Design, though it is quite expensive. I am an all-around arty kid.

I am very interested in the asian music industry and try to keep up with it as much as possible, so you'll most likely see me contributing to asian music pages. I have contributed a considerable amount to BoA's page (as she is my favorite singer ever ♥) and plan to expand other artists' pages that are stubs.

[edit] Languages

I am a native speaker of American English seeing as I live in the United States. I know a pretty good amount of Japanese vocabulary, know basic sentence structure and at one time had memorized hiragana, but I've since forgotton nearly all of it. Currently I am enrolled in French classes and plan on taking four years of it. I can speak really basic French, but my accent isn't that good so I prefer writing/typing French. XD I hope to visit Paris next summer with my French class. Never ask me to pronounce Korean. Ever. I can't pronounce it to save my life. I can, however, say "saranghae" and some other random words!
