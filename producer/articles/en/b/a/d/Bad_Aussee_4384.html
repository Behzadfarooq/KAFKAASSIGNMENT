Bad Aussee

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Bad Aussee is a town in Styria, Austria in the Salzkammergut region. It claims to be the geographical mid-point of Austria, which is symbolized by a "Mercedes star"-shaped bridge.

[edit] See also

Klaus Maria Brandauer, world famous Austrian actor and director who was born in Altaussee.
