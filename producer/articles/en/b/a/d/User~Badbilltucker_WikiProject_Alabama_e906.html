Welcome to the Alabama WikiProject. We are a group dedicated to improving Wikipedia's coverage of articles related to Alabama.

Goals
  • The goal of this WikiProject is to provide complete coverage on subjects related to Alabama, by expanding articles on people, places, and things resident or native to Alabama.
Scope
  • This project proposes to work with all pages related to the state of Alabama, its history, geography, and people.

All these articles should be included in the Category:Alabama or one of its subcategories.

    [edit] New articles

    Please feel free to list your new Alabama-related articles here (newer articles at the top, please). Any new articles that have an interesting or unusual fact in them should be suggested for the Did you know? box the Main Page. Please also make sure that each new article created is put into one of the categories for Alabama, so that the articles will appear there. Also, if possible, add each new article to the page Wikipedia:WikiProject Alabama/Articles (still under construction) so that they can be viewed for recent changes.

    [edit] Stubs

    • {{Alabama-stub}} - Stub template for articles relating to Connecticut. To be placed on the bottom of the article page.
      • {{Alabama-struct-stub}} - Stub template for articles relating to buildings and structures in Alabama. To be placed on the bottom of the article page.
      • {{Alabama-road-stub}} - Stub template for articles relating to Alabama roads and road transport. To be placed on the bottom of the article page.

    NOTE: Stubs are managed by the Stub-sorting WikiProject. Before creating a new stub, you should propose it at Wikipedia:WikiProject Stub sorting/Proposals. See Wikipedia:Stub for general guidelines on proposing stub templates and categories. DO NOT simply create new stub templates, as these will be deleted.

    [edit] Other templates
