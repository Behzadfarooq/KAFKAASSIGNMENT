Studio album
Released July 29, 2003
Genre Alternative rock/Emo
Label Dying Wish Records

Bad Scene Everyone's Fault JAWBREAKER TRIBUTE is an tribute album by various artists. Various artists cover songs by the punk band Jawbreaker that broke up in 1996.

It was released July 29, 2003 on the Suburban Home record label.
