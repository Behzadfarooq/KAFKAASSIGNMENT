Baddeckenstedt is a Samtgemeinde ("collective municipality") in the district of Wolfenbüttel, in Lower Saxony, Germany. Its seat is in the village Baddeckenstedt.

The Samtgemeinde Baddeckenstedt consists of the following municipalities:

  • Baddeckenstedt
  • Burgdorf
  • Elbe
  • Haverlah
  • Heere
  • Sehlde
