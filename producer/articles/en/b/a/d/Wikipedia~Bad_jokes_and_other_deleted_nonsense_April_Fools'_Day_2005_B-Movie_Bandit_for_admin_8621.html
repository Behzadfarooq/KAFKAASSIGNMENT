[edit] B-Movie Bandit

Although not as active recently, few users have shown such dedication to Wikipedia. The man just keeps coming back, day after day. This kind of devotion to Wikipedia deserves adminship at least, if not God-King status. Meelar (talk) 05:23, Apr 1, 2005 (UTC)

Support

  1. Of course. --Slowking Man 07:08, Apr 1, 2005 (UTC)
  2. Strong Support, his devotion to dealing in vandalism has been unbelievable. As for the sheer volume of new articles he has added, outstanding. Rje 11:56, Apr 1, 2005 (UTC)
  3. Absolutely. You mean he isn't an admin already? Gamaliel 18:53, 1 Apr 2005 (UTC)
  4. Good effort to add new articles. --Lst27 (talk) 23:28, 1 Apr 2005 (UTC)

Oppose

  1. Hasn't done nearly enough work on pelican shit. Snowspinner 05:26, Apr 1, 2005 (UTC)
    He was part of WikiProject Pelican shit for a few weeks, and it looks like over 10% of the edits recorded to him were part of that project. I see no problem there. Grutness|hello? 09:51, 1 Apr 2005 (UTC)
  2. Oppose. The user has too many edits; may support when the number of edits goes down.—Ëzhiki (erinaceus europeaus) 20:02, Apr 1, 2005 (UTC)

Repose

  1. It would save time for the rest of us if B-Movie Bandit could block himself and revert his own edits. -- Infrogmation 05:52, 1 Apr 2005 (UTC)

Neutral

  1. Both Ëzhiki and Infrogmation make excellent points, so I'll stay neutral for now. May change my vote if there is a workable way to de-admin people like Jimbo Wales. Jonathunder 23:24, 2005 Apr 1 (UTC)

Comments

  1. B-Movie Bandit current has 0 edits, 0 of which are in the article namespace. There may be more edits yet to be attributed to this username.-gadfium 05:36, 1 Apr 2005 (UTC)

Questions for the candidate
A few generic questions to provide guidance for voters:

1. What sysop chores, if any, would you anticipate helping with? (Please read the page about administrators and the administrators' reading list.)
A. creature from the black lagoon was made sometime like that.
2. Of your articles or contributions to Wikipedia, are there any about which you are particularly pleased, and why?
A. julie andrwes starred in sound of music
3. Have you been in any conflicts over editing in the past or do you feel other users have caused you stress? How have you dealt with it and will deal with it in the future?
