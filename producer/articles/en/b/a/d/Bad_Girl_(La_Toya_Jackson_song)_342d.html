"Bad Girl" is a single by American singer La Toya Jackson, from her 1989 album of the same name. Extra care went into the making of the single, as it would be the lead single from the Bad Girl album, which Jackson felt was her "control album".

The B-side to the single is "Piano Man", a track which later appeared on the album. The single did not chart in any country. A music video was filmed for the track but it was given very little airplay and has never been released on video or DVD. Only small clips have surfaced.

Both songs were written by the German songwriter team Klarmann/Weber.
