[edit] Plot synopsis

Sh'Okanabo makes a deal with Darius Dun by giving him Kanabo clones of the Turtles, each with the Turtles' fighting style and exact memories. Darius leads the Turtles into an ambush and disarmed them. Cody, Splinter and Serling come to help but end up in a mess of trouble. With Splinter and Serling arrested by the Peacekeepers, Cody must find the Turtles before it's too late.

[edit] Quotes

  • Dark Michelangelo - "Clones from your tissues, but with lots and lots of issues!"

[edit] Miscellanea

  • First appearance of the Dark Turtles, whose skin colors (with the exception of Dark Mikey) match their facemask colors (i.e. Leonardo = blue).
