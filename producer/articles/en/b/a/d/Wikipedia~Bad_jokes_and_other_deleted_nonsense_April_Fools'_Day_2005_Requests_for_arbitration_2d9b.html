[edit] Everybody who isn't me

Everybody who isn't me has an annoying tendency for being wrong. This is particularly true of other admins. I respectfully request that they be put on a one year probation whereby I am empowered to tempban them for being wrong. Snowspinner 04:53, Apr 1, 2005 (UTC)

[edit] Arbitrators' opinions on hearing this matter (0/1/0/1)

  • Reject, and suggest disciplinary action against such frivolous abuse of procedure. I AM THE ONLY PERSON EMPOWERED TO SAY WHO IS RIGHT AND WRONG HERE, AND YOU ARE CLEARLY WRONG!!!! -- Grunt   ҈  04:55, 2005 Apr 1 (UTC)
  • Abstain — I'd like to see more evidence indicating whether or not your possibly erroneous definition of rightness differs from my infallible one ➥the Epopt 05:30, 1 Apr 2005 (UTC)

In light of the new evidence of sockpuppet abuse, I request a year IP ban to close off this loophole. (Excepting AOL IPs in the range, of course). I also request, considering the severity of the concern, that the arbcom suspend its usual due process and act on this within 24 hours. Snowspinner 01:51, Apr 1, 2005 (UTC)

[edit] Statement by party 1

For over four years, the Wikipedia community has displayed a willful and incorrigible disregard for every policy and guideline in the book. Its offenses include, but are not limited to:

  • Persistent POV pushing [1]
  • Insertion of inaccurate and misleading material [2], including a great deal of original research
  • Innumerable personal attacks: see [3] [4] and [5] for starters.
  • Constant revert warring, including many violations of the three-revert rule: [6] and [7] have a small sampler of the evidence
  • Misuse of VfD: [8]
  • Use of sockpuppets: [9], especially for voting ([10] lists a few incidents)
  • Constant disruption of Wikipedia to prove a point: [11]
  • Uploading of inappropriate images: [12]
  • Committing numerous violations of copyright [13] (both text and images)
  • Filing frivolous requests for arbitration: [14] and [15]
  • And of course, scads of vandalism: [16] [17]

I believe that the persistence and gravity of these offenses warrants an immediate and lengthy ban. —Charles P. (Mirv) 01:21, 1 Apr 2005 (UTC)
