Thanks for for contribution to WP:DPL, it is much appreciated! I was thinking about expanding Diurnal into an article (about daily occurences). How many links were there (in the 50 or so you went through) that would benefit from an article? --Commander Keane 04:25, 25 November 2005 (UTC)

I focused on articles about diurnal animals, redirecting them to diurnal animal. All 50 of them would benefit greatly from a diurnal animal article. I toyed around with the idea and did some reading -- turns out to be an interesting (fascinating) but complicated subject. When you're awake impacts your eyes, obviously, but in ways I didn't think of, for example how color is perceived or whether it is perceived at all. If you decide to proceed with diurnal alone or as it pertains to animals or meterology, let me know if I can do anything to help.
