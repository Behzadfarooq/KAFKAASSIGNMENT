The Bad Boys Of SNL, occasionally referred to as the Slap Pack or Happy Gilmore Gang, is a group of actors who have performed together on Saturday Night Live. This group has continued to appear together is movies, with the exception of the now deceased Chris Farley. Recently, many of these movies have been released under the Happy Madison label.

[edit] The Members

Adam Sandler, Chris Farley (deceased), Chris Rock, Rob Schneider and David Spade

The Secondary Members (Friends)

Dana Carvey, Jon Lovitz, Norm MacDonald, Christopher Walken, Sean Astin, Dan Aykroyd, Drew Barrymore, Steve Buscemi, Blake Clark, Allen Covert and Peter Dante

[edit] Movies

Wayne's World, Wayne's World 2, Coneheads, Airheads, Billy Madison, Tommy Boy, Black Sheep, Happy Gilmore, Beverly Hills Ninja, The Wedding Singer, The Waterboy, Big Daddy, Deuce Bigalow: Male Gigalo, Little Nicky, Joe Dirt, The Animal, The Hot Chick, Mr. Deeds, Eight Crazy Nights, Dickie Roberts: Former Child Star, Anger Management, 50 First Dates, The Longest Yard, Deuce Bigalow: European Gigolow, Grandma's Boy, The Benchwarmers and Click
