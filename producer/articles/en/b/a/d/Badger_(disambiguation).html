Badger (disambiguation)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Look up Badger in Wiktionary, the free dictionary.

Badger may refer to:

  • Badger: an animal, and specifically to:
    • Eurasian Badger found across Europe and Asia
    • American Badger found in North America
    • Ratel or honey badger found in Africa and Asia

Characters & People

  • Badger (Farthing Wood), a fictional character from the animated children's television series The Animals of Farthing Wood
  • Badger (band), an early 1970s rock band featuring Tony Kaye
  • Badger (comics), a comic book character whose adventures were published by three separate US companies throughout the 1980s and early 1990s
  • Badger (person), archaic English term for a dealer in food
  • Badger (Firefly), a fictional character with a Cockney accent who appears in several episodes of the currently retired television show Firefly
  • Len Badger, a footballer, who played for Sheffield United F.C. between 1962-1976
  • Ruth Badger, runner up in the final of British TV show The Apprentice series 2 and is currently starring in the Sky One TV show The Big Idea
  • Steve Badger, poker player
  • Wisconsin Badgers, the collegiate athletic teams at the University of Wisconsin-Madison
  • Bucky Badger, mascot of the University of Wisconsin

Things

  • S.S. Badger, an auto/passenger ferry operating on Lake Michigan.
  • Badger Badger Badger, an Internet animation.
  • Breezy Badger, a code name for version 5.10 of Ubuntu (Linux distribution), released in 10/2005.
  • Tupolev Tu-16, a Soviet twin-engine jet bomber (NATO codename Badger).
  • Badger Armoured Engineering Vehicle, a Canadian Combat engineering vehicle based on the Leopard tank.
  • British Rail Class 89, an electric locomotive developed by British Rail
  • Badger Ales, beers brewed by Hall & Woodhouse. See Hall & Woodhouse for more information.
  • Badger Records, a record label.
