Bad Boy Boogie is a song by Australian rock band AC/DC. This appears as the fourth track on their 1977 album, Let There Be Rock, released in Australia. The song is also the fourth track on their International album with the same name. The song is also done live by former lead sing Bon Scott on the album "If You Want Blood, You've Got It"

[edit] Personnel

  • Bon Scott- vocals
  • Angus Young- lead guitar
  • Malcolm Young- rhythm guitar
  • Mark Evans- bass guitar
  • Phil Rudd- drums
