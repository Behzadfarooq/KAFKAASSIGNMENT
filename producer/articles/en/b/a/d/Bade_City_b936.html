Bade City

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Bade City (八德市) is a city in the central part of Taoyuan County, Taiwan (Republic of China). Bade is served by No. 2 Freeway.

  • Area: 33.71 km²
  • Population: 171,539 people (Oct. 2006)

[edit] Transportation

Gueishan is served by National Highway No. 2.
