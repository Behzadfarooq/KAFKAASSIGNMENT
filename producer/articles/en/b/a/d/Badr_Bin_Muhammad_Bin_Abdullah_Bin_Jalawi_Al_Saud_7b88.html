Prince Badr bin Muhammad bin Abdullah bin Jalawi Al Saud (Arabic: بدر بن محمد بن عبدالله بن جلوي آل سعود also بدر بن جلوي) has been the governor of Al-Ahsa[1] in Saudi Arabia since 13 December, 1997.
