Questions for the candidate on wheels
A few generic questions to provide guidance for voters:

1. What sysop chores, if any, would you anticipate helping with? (Please read the page about administrators and the administrators' reading list.)
A. Ideally, I'd like to mobilize all of Wikipedia, and other wikiprojects. Would you believe that, despite all my efforts, there are still some wikipedia articles that are not on wheels?! This must be recitified post haste.
2. Of your articles or contributions to Wikipedia, are there any about which you are particularly pleased, and why?
A. Well, I gotta say, moving All your base are belong to us to All your base are belong to us ON WHEELS! may have been my piece de resistance.
3. Have you been in any conflicts over editing in the past or do you feel other users have caused you stress? How have you dealt with it and will deal with it in the future?
