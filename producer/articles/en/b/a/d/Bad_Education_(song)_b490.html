Bad Education (song)

Bad Education is the third song on the album Bottoms of Barrels, by Tilly and the Wall. It was released as a bonus 7" with the purchase of Bottoms of Barrels.

"Bad Education" is said to be based off the 2004 film, Bad Education about a transvestite, but the band has not confirmed it. They refer to the song as being about a person with a split personality. The lyrics tell of a boy who dresses up in women's clothes. "Forget the Feeling" and "Patience, Babe" are the two non-album tracks.
