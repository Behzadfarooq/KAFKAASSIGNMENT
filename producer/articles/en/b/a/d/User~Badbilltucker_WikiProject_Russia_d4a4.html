[edit] Scope

This project covers the creation and management of articles related to the nation of Russia, its cities, counties, geography, transportation, culture, history and so on. It aims to expand Wikipedia's resources on Russia in a fair and accurate manner.

[edit] Project category

The category of this project is Category:Russia.

[edit] Project divisions

  • Categorization (talk) – Categorization of Russia-related articles
  • Cities (talk) – Cities, towns, villages and districts
  • Physical geography (talk) – Regions, parks, climate, physical features
  • Transportation (talk) – Trails, roads, rails, airports and maritime ports in and around Russia
  • Culture (talk) – Music, art, icons, mass communication
  • People (talk) – includes residents of the current nation of Russia, all its predecessors, and all those people who have resided in any of Russia's predecessor states who have shaped Russia
  • Government (talk) – Governance, as well as federal, state and local politics in relation to Russia and its predecessors
  • Religion (talk) – Religions and religious beliefs, rites and practices associated with the people of the nation of Russia, and its predecessors
  • History (talk) – history and foreign relations of Russia and its predecessors
  • Science, technology and engineering (talk) – Science, technology and engineering aspects, components and infrastructure of Russia and its predecessors
  • Photo requests (talk) – List of requested photos.
