"Badd"
Single by Ying Yang Twins
Released 2006

"Badd" is the second single from the Album "U.S.A. (United State of Atlanta)" featuring Mike Jones and Mr. Collipark. In 2006 it reached #6 on the US Rap chart. The song was also produced by Mr. Collipark. The music video is set in Las Vegas, inside various casinos and hotels.
