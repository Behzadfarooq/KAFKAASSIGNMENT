Bad Acid Trip

Bad Acid Trip is a metal band that is currently signed with System of a Down lead singer Serj Tankian's record company, Serjical Strike. They released an album, Lynch the Weirdo, produced by Daron Malakian (also from System of a Down), in 2004. They just finished touring with System of a Down on the Ozzfest 2006 Tour. They have toured with System of a Down before along with The Mars Volta. Bad Acid Trip combines Thrash Metal with High-Pitched noises, spoken-word-esque singing, and political lyrics to create a sound that is unique to the band. It was announced while on Ozzfest that a new cd will be released in 2007.
