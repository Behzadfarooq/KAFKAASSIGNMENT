Bad Blood is the fifth episode of the SciFi Channel science-fiction series the Dresden Files.

[edit] Plot synopsis

Spoiler warning: Plot and/or ending details follow.

Bianca, one of the most beautiful and dangerous vampires in Chicago, has history with Harry Dresden — she was the one who protected him five years ago when the High Council came after him for the death of Justin Morningway. Now, Bianca hires Harry after a failed attempt to kill her, and Harry tracks the attack back to a retired High Council vampire hunter. When the operative is attacked as well, Harry discovers links to the psychotropic vampire-drug Third Eye, and realizes that someone close to Bianca is making a powerplay.

[edit] Trivia

  • This episode introduces two vampire clans: the Red Court and the Black Court.
  • Natalie mentions the 1950s-1980s science-education series Mister Wizard.
