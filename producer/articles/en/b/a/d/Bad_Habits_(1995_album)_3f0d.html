Bad Habits is a blues album by Canadian musician Colin James, released in 1995 (see 1995 in music). For the USA, the album was released on Elektra Records. The album was produced, engineered and mixed at Compass Point in Nassau and mastered at MasterDisk in New York City.

The album earned James the 1996 Juno Award for "Male Vocalist of the Year".
