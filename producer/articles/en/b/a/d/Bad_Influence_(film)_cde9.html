Bad Influence is a 1990 American film starring Rob Lowe and James Spader. In this noirish film, Spader plays a yuppie who meets a mysterious stranger (Lowe) who encourages him to explore his darker side.

[edit] Cast

  • Rob Lowe as Alex
  • James Spader as Michael Boll
  • Lisa Zane as Claire
  • Christian Clemenson as Pismo Boll
  • Marcia Cross as Ruth Fielding

[edit] Crew

  • Written by David Koepp
