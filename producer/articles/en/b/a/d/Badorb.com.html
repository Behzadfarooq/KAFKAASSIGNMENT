Badorb.com was a record label label created by Alex Paterson of The Orb in 2002. Though it was an internet label, its releases were vinyl only, which Paterson believes was the reason the label failed and closed in 2003.[1] It had featured fourteen releases from artists including The Orb, S.E. Berlin, and Ayumi Hamasaki.
