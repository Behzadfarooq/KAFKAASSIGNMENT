[edit] Regarding edits made during September 28, 2006

It might not have been your intention, but your recent edit removed content from an article. Please be careful not to remove content from Wikipedia without a valid reason, which you should specify in the edit summary or on the article's talk page. Thank you. Luna Santin 01:32, 28 September 2006 (UTC)

Please see Wikipedia's no personal attacks policy. Comment on content, not on the contributor; personal attacks damage the community and deter users. Note that continued personal attacks may lead to blocks for disruption. Please stay cool and keep this in mind while editing. Thank you. Luna Santin 01:36, 28 September 2006 (UTC)

[edit] Bad Faith Nomination

Hi. I peruse the AfD boards most week days to help make wikipedia a better place by removing unecassary articles and ensuring that notable articles do not get sent to the digital garbage can. I was a little bit troubled on October 30, 2006 to see your nomination for a notable hacker which you justified by saying "...article made by m8v2 who hates battlefield 2". Petty revenge is never a reason to delete an article, and the act of nominating a page to get back at another editor severely hampers the efforts of editors such as myself who wish to make Wikipedia better.

In order to help you try and get into the spirit of Wikipedia, I encourage you to read this page and put the following on your user page to let others know that you are dedicated to approaching disagreements with good faith and a polite tone.

This user maintains a strict policy advising against all personal attacks.

Cheers, and I look forward to your continued contributions. -bobby
