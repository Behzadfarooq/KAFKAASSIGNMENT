"Bait" is the tenth episode of the second season of the CBS television series The Unit. The episode aired on November 28, 2006.

[edit] Summary

Spoiler warning: Plot and/or ending details follow.

The team is sent to rescue Jonas, after Colonel Ryan is shut down by his contacts in Washington D.C. to negotiate a prisoner exchange to free him. On base, Tiffy faces legal repercussions for her traffic accident and receives legal support from Charlotte.
