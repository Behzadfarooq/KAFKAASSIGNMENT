BAIF Development Research Foundation

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The BAIF Development Research Foundation is an award-winning charitable organisation based in Maharashtra, India that pioneers agricultural development. It was founded in 1967 by Manibhai Desai as the Bharatiya Agro Industries Foundation.

In 1997 it received the Adivasi Seva Sanstha award from the State Government of Maharashtra.
