Bairros Unidos is a football club that plays in the São Tomé Island League in Santana in São Tomé and Principe. The team is based in the island of São Tomé. The team has won three titles and is the ninth and last team ever to win their first title in 2001 and is the team that won only one title. In the same year, they won the insular title as well.
