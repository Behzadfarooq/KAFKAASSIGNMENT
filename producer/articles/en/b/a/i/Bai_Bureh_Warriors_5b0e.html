Bai Bureh Warriors FC is a Sierra Leonean football (soccer)club based in Port Loko, Sierra Leone, currently a member of the Sierra Leonean Premier League .

The club is named after Bai Bureh, a great Sierra Leonean warrior and military strategist who led the Temne Uprising against the British in 1878.

Bai Bureh Warriors won the Sierra Leonean FA Cup in 1978 and 1982 and their best performance in any Caf competitions came in 1979 when they reached the second round of the CAF Cup Winners' Cup.
