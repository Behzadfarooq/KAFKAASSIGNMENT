Baitul Futuh

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Baitul Futuh Mosque (House of Conquests) is the largest mosque in Western Europe as of its construction in 2003. It is situated in the London town of Morden and is considered among the finest examples of contemporary architecture.

The mosque is built and run by the Ahmadiyya muslim community.

[edit] Facts

  • Area: Entire site is situated on 5.2 acres
  • Capacity: Can accommodate up to 10,000 worshippers in three prayer halls
  • Facilities: Includes gymnasium, offices, library and television studios.

  • Location: Conveniently located next to Morden South Railway Station and 150 yards from Morden Tube Station.
