Scott Baietti in all his gloriousness. Note the flannel.

Scott is a charismatic individual, and a student leader on his campus. He is, at times, an avid wikipedia editor, and also a strong believer in the wikiality principle proposed by Stephen Colbert. One of his favorite past times is the sand box game "Word Association" (see box to the right). He is also a huge fan of new music, and music videos. He can often be found investigating new musical acts using the music channels FUSE, MTV2, and MTVU, and the websites purevolume, and myspace, and is always looking for suggestions from friends and strangers.
