Baillie & the Boys

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Kathie Baillie and Michael Bonagura from The Best of Baillie & the Boys. Original photograph by Randee St. Nicholas.

Baillie & the Boys is a country music group which gained prominence in the late 1980s and early 1990s was originally comprised of lead singer Kathie Baillie, her husband guitarist Michael Bonagura, and bassist Alan LeBoeuf. They performed in live shows in Baillie's home state of New Jersey before signing their first recording contract with RCA Victor in 1987. The group's music combines elements of country, rock, and sometimes even pop jazz.
