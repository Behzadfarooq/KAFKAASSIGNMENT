[edit] Baillie of Lochend, Haddington

Created in the Baronetage of Nova Scotia 21 November 1636.
  • Sir Gideon Baillie, 1st Baronet.
  • Sir James Baillie, 2nd Baronet. On his death the baronetcy became extinct.

[edit] Baillie of Portman Square, London

Created in the Baronetage of the United Kingdom 11 December 1812.
2nd patent dated 26 May 1819.
  • Sir Ewen Baillie, 1st Baronet.
  • On his death the creation of 1812 became extinct, but the creation of 1819 survived - see MacKenzie of Berkeley Square, London.

[edit] Baillie of Polkemmet, West Lothian

Created in the Baronetage of the United Kingdom 12 February 1919.
  • Sir William Baillie, 1st Baronet.
  • Sir William Baillie, 2nd Baronet.
  • Sir George Baillie, 3rd Baronet.
  • Sir Robert Alexander Baillie, 4th Baronet.
  • Sir Gawaine George Stuart Baillie, 5th Baronet.
  • Sir Adrian William Maxwell Baillie, 6th Baronet.
  • Sir Gawaine George Hope Baillie, 7th Baronet.
  • Sir Adrian Louis Baillie, 8th Baronet. Born 26 March 1973.
Baronetcy extant
