Hello, thank you for your input. For your information the Wikipedia:WikiProject Baiting link is the main page for this template. Please note I was the creator of the template. Cordially SirIsaacBrock 16:38, 7 April 2006 (UTC)

You can't link to non-encyclopedic content in templates meant to be used in articles. Wikipedia:WikiProject Baiting is not meant to be an article, but something to assist editors in writing articles on baiting. A template meant to link the different topics of baiting together should have baiting as the main link, not the non-encyclopedic WikiProject. Please read through Wikipedia:Avoid_self-references. —Nightstallion (?) Seen this already? 18:38, 7 April 2006 (UTC)
Nightstallion is correct. Article space should not link to Wikipedia space, unless it is absolutely necessary to explain what the template means, e.g. on Wikipedia maintenance templates like {{POV}}.
