[edit] My online existence, a potted history

I first accessed an online service in 1993, subscribing to CompuServe, and using the service (amongst other things) to buy the album Changesbowie.

Soon after (October 1994), I commenced studying at Grey College, Durham, where I first accessed the internet at large. Initially it was using Gopher, but I soon graduated to the WWW, using Mosaic.
