Bailee Madison (born October 15, 1999 in Fort Lauderdale, Florida) is an American actor. She made her debut on an episode of Cory in the House and also will appear in the upcoming film Lonely Hearts. She is most famous, though, for her role in the 2007 film adaption of Katherine Paterson's Newbery Medal-winning novel Bridge to Terabithia in which she plays the spunky younger sister of Jesse Aarons, May Belle. Madison has also appeared on several TV commercials. Her career started when she was just 2 months old appearing in an Office Depot commercial.
