Baker Street is a comic book series created by Gary Reed and Guy Davis and published by Caliber Comics.

[edit] Plot

It features an alternative Sherlock Holmes mythos where Victorian era England is populated with punks.

[edit] Publication

It was published as a ten-issue limited series and collected in 2 trade paperbacks by Caliber and then as a single volume:

  • Honour Among Punks : The Complete Baker Street Graphic Novel (iBooks, 352 pages, ISBN 978-0743459044, 2003)
