[edit] Editing on Matthew Bellamy

I see you have added back some content that I removed from the article, without commenting on it in the edit summary or in any of my, your or the article's talk pages. I've reverted it back, and I'll explain why here:

  • I know that Matt is the most talented musician in the world, but you can't say that in a Wikipedia article. It is not NPOV; not everyone would agree with that and the article itself suggests why not everyone likes his style.
  • His GCSE results aren't particularly relevant. If his dislike for school is notable, then please Cite Sources.
  • The comments on his musical tastes were rather particular and unverifiable. If he has discussed in detail his musical influences in interview then this could be referred to.
  • I'm concerned about the copyrights to your images. The microcuts gallery is an open gallery and has no guarantee on the copyright owner or license. In particular, Image:PF1005 MUSE P.jpg cannot possibly be used. It still has a watermark identifying the source and stating NOT FOR REPRODUCTION.

You seem to know a fair bit about Bellamy, so I hope that you won't take this personally and contribute to this article in a positive way. Just remember that Wikipedia is an encyclopedia, not a fansite, and articles must be encyclopedic.

--BigBlueFish 11:45, 21 December 2005 (UTC)

[edit] Image copyright problem with Image:Matt.jpg

Thanks for uploading Image:Matt.jpg. However, the image may soon be deleted unless we can determine the copyright holder and copyright status. The Wikimedia Foundation is very careful about the images included in Wikipedia because of copyright law (see Wikipedia's Copyright policy).

The copyright holder is usually the creator, the creator's employer, or the last person who was transferred ownership rights. Copyright information on images is signified using copyright templates. The three basic license types on Wikipedia are open content, public domain, and fair use. Find the appropriate template in Wikipedia:Image copyright tags and place it on the image page like this: {{TemplateName}}.

Please signify the copyright information on any other images you have uploaded or will upload. Remember that images without this important information can be deleted by an administrator. If you have any questions, feel free to contact me. Thank you. BRossow 03:44, 18 February 2006 (UTC)
