Bakori

Bakori is a town and Local Government Area (LGA) in Katsina State, Nigeria.[1] The population of the LGA is approximately 133,000 people and the area is 679 km²;[1] the town, meanwhile, has a population of approximately 13,000.[2] Tukur Idris Nadabo, a People's Democratic Party member, is the representative to the House of Representatives for the Bakori/Danja consituency.[3]
