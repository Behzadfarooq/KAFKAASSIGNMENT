Bacari Sagna (born February 14, 1983 in Sens, Bourgogne) is a French soccer right-sided defender who has been playing for AJ Auxerre in the top division of French soccer since the 2004-5 season. Sagna has played for the French under-21 team during the 2006 UEFA European Under-21 Football Championship.

[edit] Honours

  • Winner of the Coupe de France in 2005 with AJ Auxerre
  • Runner-up of the Trophée des Champions in 2005.
