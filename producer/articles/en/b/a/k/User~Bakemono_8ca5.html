what i would like to do i to argue less and do more and not fall into musical disputes. if we can work out a deal then its fine for me. we both have to compromise because apparently, you dont believe in what i wrote but too many people do while i believe that too many people dont either. its difficult to be 100% accurate and please ALL heads but at the end, i was THE ONLY ONE who actually wrote something because all i got at the end, was people adding BC tunes to signature recordings. i believe in what i wrote and i got an ok from some people out there in forums - check lifted forum and the opinion of some people in relation to this definition - but i think that it can be more accurate.

so, we start from here. tell me what you want to do, and how we can do it together. one thing which will be an obstacle for us is to be taking our personal tastes into this. we dont have a community struggling to correct what we write because neurofunk is small and uninteresting in comparision to themes such as israel for instance. so whatever we believe is right, we might get away with it, both, you and i. if you think that masheen does neuro, and theres no one to dispute this with you, it will be an actual fact that masheen does, indeed, neurofunk. one thing i do agree with you: glimpse of truth is neuro indeed due to the wah-wah pedal sound effect over the bassline and thats how early virus defined it back then. its not enough to have this wah-wah effect to be qualified as neuro. i also believe that the techstep article should be re-written because its really bad but i have no interest in doing this at all. well, theres a very thin line between both styles, techstep and neurofunk. this will be difficult but lets try: with compromise, otherwise it will be a reverting war forever. im willing to compromise from my end here.

personally, i dont like noisia. i have their facade tune and thats about it. but i cant deny that they actually are running this style now. i dont like phace and wont bag his records but he is running this style as well. im now beginning to work with teebee, perhaps, on some rmx project. i can ask him to make a contribution - which i believe he wont - but i can send him links to this article and check his opinion and send you a mail with whatever he writes.
