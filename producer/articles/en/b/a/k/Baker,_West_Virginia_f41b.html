Baker, West Virginia

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Baker is an unincorporated community on the Lost River in Hardy County, West Virginia, USA. Baker is located at the intersection of West Virginia Route 259 and West Virginia Route 55. According to the 2000 census, the Baker community has a population of 1,262 [1].
