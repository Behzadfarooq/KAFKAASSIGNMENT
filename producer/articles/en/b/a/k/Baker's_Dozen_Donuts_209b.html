Baker's Dozen Donuts is a donut store operator in the Canadian province of Ontario.

[edit] History

Founded in 1978 by Peter Paraskaksis as Baker's Dozen Donut Corporation and based in Mississauga, Ontario.

[edit] Organization

The chain operates 58 stores, mostly in the Toronto, Ontario market area. Expansion came in the late 1980s, but it remains a small scale operation. Stores are chain owned and operated by hired staff. Most are located away from larger rivals, like Tim Hortons.
