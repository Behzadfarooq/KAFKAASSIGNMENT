The Bakersfield Jam are an expansion franchise of the National Basketball Association Development League that began play in 2006-07. They play their home games at Rabobank Arena in Bakersfield, California, United States.

Gerry McNamara, former Syracuse University standout, joined the team on December 12, 2006.
