[edit] SO3

Thanks for the, er, thanks. Nice to see that some people do notice... I'll just add that ironically enough, I was previously quite opposed to the idea of list articles. However, I noticed recently by chance that redirects to an anchor now work, so instead of having to link to other characters in a really horrible piped link if a list is used, they can now be linked to semi-rationally. I still don't see much inherent benefit of lists over individual articles, but that seems to be the new Wikipedia style, and the articles did need to be cleaned up, which I will accept any thanks over. ;-) (as I noted in the SO3 main talk page, I ran across these articles quite by chance when looking at WP:PRODSUM, and since I removed the prod, I figured I had to back that up.)
