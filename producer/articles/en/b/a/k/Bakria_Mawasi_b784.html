Bakria Mawasi is a published Israeli Arab artist and writer. Born on December 8, 1987 in Baqa Al-Gharbeyya, she has made claim to renown through her memorable participation in "Crossing Borders" - a magazine supervised by Garba Diallo, International People's College, Elsinore, Denmark - which aims to bring young Israeli and Arab people together, and to form a dialogue.

Mawasi currently resides in Jerusalem, and having been schooled in The Orthodox Arab College in Haifa for 4 years, she now is pursuing a higher education at Hebrew University in communication studies.
