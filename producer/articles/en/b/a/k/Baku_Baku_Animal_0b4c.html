Baku Baku Animal is a Tetris-type arcade game released by Sega in 1995. You attempt to line up blocks featuring an animal's favorite food. When this happens, the animal eats the food causing the other player to receive more blocks.

[edit] Trivia

  • The title of this game translates from Japanese as 'Gobble Gobble Animal'.
