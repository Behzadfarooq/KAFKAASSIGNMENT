[edit] NPOV dispute

This article appears to be a pro-windpower advertisement combined with a family history. The majority of this information is not NPOV, and is not notable.

The user who wrote the article is User:Tim.bakke, who, based on the username, may be a Bakke family member. Sections of this article read like a Bakke family history, such as this:

During World War II, five brothers served in the United States Armed Forces. The brothers' parents emigrated from Bergen, Norway in 1925 with their daughter, Borgny (affectionately nicknamed Bunny), and their sons Oscar, Erving, and Hans. The births of Roald (Roy) and Harold would soon follow in Brooklyn, New York.

What does that have to do with an encyclopedia article about a small mountain in Florida, Massachusetts? Econrad 05:01, 29 December 2005 (UTC)
