Baal (disambiguation)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Baal is a Hebrew word meaning "lord" or "dominator".

Baal may also refer to:

  • Baal (demon), a Christian demon, loosely identified with the Canaanite god
  • Baal (play), a play by Bertolt Brecht
  • David Bowie in Bertolt Brecht's BAAL, an EP of the songs from Brecht's play recorded by David Bowie
  • Ba'al (Stargate), a fictional character on Stargate SG-1
  • Baal, the Lord of Destruction – one of the three Prime Evils in the Diablo games and the final boss in Diablo II: Lord of Destruction
  • Baal (computer game), a computer game for the Amiga
  • Baal I, an ancient king of Tyre
  • Baal Peor, a Canaanite deity
  • Hadad, a Canaanite deity commonly known as Baal or Ba'lu
  • A fictional planet in the Warhammer 40,000 universe
  • Baal, Belgium, a place in Flemish Brabant
  • Baal (band) a Danish rock band

[edit] See also

  • Bhaal, Lord of Murder, fictional deity of the Forgotten Realms Dungeons & Dragons campaign setting
  • Baal, The one who rescued Apocalypse from execution by the Egyptians in the X-Men series.
