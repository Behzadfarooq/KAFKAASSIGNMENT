Baard Owe

Baard Owe in the 1964 film Gertrud
Born July 3, 1936
Mosjøen, Norway

Baard Owe (born 1936) is a Norwegian-born actor who has acted in many Scandinavian films and TV-series. He moved to Denmark in 1956, where he has lived and worked since.

Owe is mostly known for his role as pathologist Dr. Bondo in the TV series Riget ('The Kingdom'), directed by Lars von Trier.
