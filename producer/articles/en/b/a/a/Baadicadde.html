Baadicadde

Main article: Somali clan

The Baadicadde are a Somali clan, that belong to the larger Hawiye clan. They live in the Hiiraan, Middle Shabelle, Lower Shabelle, Banaadir.The Baydiisle subclan of Baadicadde live in the Somali Region of Ethiopia.
