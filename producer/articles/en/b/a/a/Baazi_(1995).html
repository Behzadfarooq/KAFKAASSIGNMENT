Baazi is a 1995 Bollywood film which starred Aamir Khan and Mamta Kulkarni and directed by Ashutosh Gowarikar.

[edit] Plot Summary

Inspector Amar Damjee finds a terrifying crime in the street when he saves the lives of certain bus passengers. Now his job as a police inspector is to identify the terrorists. Suddenly, a police commissioner's daughter is killed in a homicide but when Amar reaches there police comes and is framed for it and goes to jail for the crime. When he escapes along with the other prisoners accidentally, he goes out to prove his innocence and searches for the man who is behind all this, by which Amar also discovers that he is the same man who killed his paretns too.
