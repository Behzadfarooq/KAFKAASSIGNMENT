Baanah

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Baanah (ba'-a-na ba'anah) is the name of two men in the Bible.

  1. Baanah, son of Rimmon
  2. Baanah the Netophathite
  • Meaning: "son of affliction"

[edit] Baanah â€” son of Rimmon

Baanah, one of the two sons of Rimmon the Beerothite, was a captain in Saul's army. He, along with his brother Rechab, assassinated Saul's son Ishosheth, while he was asleep in bed. They beheaded him and brought his head to David. In punishment for this murder, David had Baanah and Rechab put to death and their bodies suspended over the pool at Hebron.

  • References: 2 Samuel 4:2, 2 Samuel 4:5,6,12

[edit] Baanah the Netophathite

Baanah the Netophathite was the father of Heled (one of David's thirty heroes).

  • References: 2 Samuel 23:29, 1 Chronicles 11:30
