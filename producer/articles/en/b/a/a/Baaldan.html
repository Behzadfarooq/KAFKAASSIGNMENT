Baal Dan is a charity that provides aid in Kolkata (Calcutta),India. Its name stems from the Hindi words for child (baal) and donation (dan). Baal Dan helps homeless/street children and orphans that are in need of food, clothing, shelter and education. The founder, Tanya Pinto, lives in Texas and established Baal Dan after working in Mother Teresa's orphanage in Kolkata in 2005. Baal Dan plans to expand aid to other parts of India and to Africa in 2007.
