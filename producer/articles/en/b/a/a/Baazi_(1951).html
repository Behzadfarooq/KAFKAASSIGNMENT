Baazi is a 1951 Indian Hindi-language film. Dev Anand's Navketan production house produced the movie, and as per a commitment given by Dev Anand to Guru Dutt in their days of struggle, the movie was directed by Guru Dutt. The movie stars Dev Anand with Geeta Bali and Kalpana Kartik. It is a crime thriller and had very popular music composed by S.D. Burman.

Trivia: Guru Dutt kept only half of his commitment to Dev Anand, he was to direct Dev Anand in his production, though he cast Dev in C.I.D. , Raj Khosla directed it.
