Baatin is a rapper and soul singer. Born in Detroit, Michigan, he was a member of the legendary group called Slum Village. He left the group in 2003 when he was diagnostic skizophrenia. Baatin recovered from this illness in 2005 but unfortunately he had no money to pay the hospital so he had to sell his house and his car. But he truly believed that he could make new music so he started working on a new album. The project, called "Titus chapter 10 verse 13", was released at the end of 2006. During the same year he even made a record with noel zancanella. Now Baatin is good and maybe he is going to be back in the slum village crew. In fact they re going on world tour on 2007 togheter and in Black Milk new album there's a song featuring Slum Village and Baatin sings in that song too.
