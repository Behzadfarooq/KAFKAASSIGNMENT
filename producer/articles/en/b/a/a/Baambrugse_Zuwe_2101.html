Baambrugse Zuwe (52°14′N 4°57′E) is a hamlet in the Dutch province of Utrecht. It is located in the municipality of De Ronde Venen, northeast of Vinkeveen. The hamlet consists of one road, close to 4 km long, going straight through the lake area the Vinkeveensche Plassen.

In 2004, Baambrugse Zuwe has about 500 inhabitants.[1]
