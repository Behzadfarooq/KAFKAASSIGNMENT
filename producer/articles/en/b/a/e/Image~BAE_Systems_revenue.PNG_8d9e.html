[edit] Notes

  • Eletronics, Intelligence & Support (28%): Includes majority of US business (BAE Systems Inc.).
  • Land & Armaments (15%): Includes BAE Systems Land Systems and the former United Defense. Main business in the UK, US, Sweden and South Africa.
  • Programmes (21%): BAE Systems Air Systems, BAE Systems Surface Fleet Solutions and BAE Systems Submarine Solutions.
  • Customer Solutions & Support (22%): Includes support of militaries, BAE Systems Australia and the Al Yamamah businesses.
  • Integrated Systems & Partnerships (12%): Includes BAE Systems Insyte, Underwater Systems business and the shares in MBDA (37.5%), SAAB (20%) and Gripen International (50%).
  • HQ and other businesses (2%): Includes BAE Systems Regional Aircraft and support activities as well as costs of administration, "UK shared services activity", including research centres, property management and corporate air travel.
