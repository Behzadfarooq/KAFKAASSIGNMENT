Bae, also often spelled Pae or Pai, is a unique Korean family name. The South Korean census of 2000 found 372,064 people by this surname, or slightly less than 1% of the population.

[edit] List of famous Baes

  • Bae Doona (배두나), b. 1979, South Korean model and actress.
  • Bae Eun-sik (배은식), South Korean actor who often portrays Kim Jong-il.
  • Pae Gil-Su (배길수), b. 1972, North Korean gymnast.
  • Hyung-il Pai (배형일), scholar of Korean studies.
  • Bae Jeong Min (배정민), South Korean voice actor.
  • Bae Seul Ki (배슬기), b. 1986, South Korean pop singer.
  • Pae Tal-jun (배달준), b. 1936, high-ranking North Korean politician and bureaucrat.
  • Bae Yong Joon (배용준), b. 1972, South Korean actor.
  • Bae Yong-Kyun (배용균), b. 1951, South Korean film director.
