Hello, I'm Bavaria. All I can really say about myself is that I've been a car fanatic my whole life. Since joining in January of 2006, I've edited thousands of times, but took a much needed break from editing in August. As of December 5, 2006 I have returned to editing.

Most of the articles I edit are Chrysler related(mainly Plymouth). I beleive that the Plymouth-marque never should have been discontinued and Wikipedia-wise, I belive that when Plymouth & Dodge cars sharing an article, it shouldn't automatically be named whatever the Dodge car was.
