[edit] Me

Heya, my name is Jon F. Danilowicz Jr. I was born on December 18th, 1991, in Worcester, a city in Central Massachusetts. I am currently a freshman at North Kingstown High School, in North Kingstown, Rhode Island

[edit] Interests

I am interesting in many things, some of which are odd for a 14 year old to be interested in. I am interested in debate, history, geography, politics, literature, computer games, computers, sports, travel, languages, international relations, and military history.

My favorite sports teams are the New England Patriots, the Boston Red Sox, the Boston Bruins, the Boston Celtics, and any other team from Boston, Massachusetts. I also like the Pakistan Cricket Team, although Cricket still manages to confuse me.

[edit] Places I have lived

In my relatively short life, I have lived in plenty of places. I have lived in:

  • Islamabad, Pakistan
  • Springfield, Virginia
  • Auburn, Massachusetts
  • Worcester, Massachusetts
  • Harare, Zimbabwe
  • Maputo, Mozambique
  • Dhaka, Bangladesh
  • Panama City, Panama

I have also visited many more countries, and listing them would take a while.
