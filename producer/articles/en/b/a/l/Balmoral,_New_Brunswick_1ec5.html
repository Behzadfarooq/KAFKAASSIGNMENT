Balmoral (2006 Population 1,706) is a village in Restigouche County, New Brunswick, Canada; approximately 10 kilometres south of Dalhousie.

Balmoral was settled in the 1850s by Joseph Drapeau. When a contingent of visitors from Scotland came to the area, they gave the village its present name after Balmoral Castle.

Forestry is the most important industry in the village.
