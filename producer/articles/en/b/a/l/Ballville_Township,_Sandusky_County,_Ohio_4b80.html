Location of Ballville Twp, Sandusky Co, Ohio.

Ballville Township is one of the 12 townships that make up Sandusky County, Ohio. It is located in the south-central part of the county, and makes up part of the border with Seneca County. Ballville Township borders the following townships and municipalities:

  • Adams Township, Seneca Co. (southeast corner)
  • Green Creek Township (east)
  • Jackson Township (west)
  • Liberty Township, Seneca Co. (southwest corner)
  • Pleasant Township, Seneca Co. (south)
  • Riley Township (northeast corner)
  • Sandusky Township (north)
  • Washington Township (northwest corner)
  • The city of Fremont (north)

The Sandusky River runs through Ballville Township prior to Fremont and before emptying into Lake Erie to the north.
