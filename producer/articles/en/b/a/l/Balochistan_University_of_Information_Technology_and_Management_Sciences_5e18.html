Balochistan University of Information Technology and Management Sciences (BUITMS) is located in Quetta, Balochistan, Pakistan. It offers degree programs in Engineering, Computers, Business and Sciences.

Balochistan University of Information Technology and Management Sciences (BUITMS) has been planned and is being developed as a premier seat of learning and is expected to attract the students from all over Pakistan and neighboring countries in the region. Balochistan University of Information Technology and Management Sciences is an institution that is provincial in implementation but national, regional and global in its outlook.

Balochistan University of Information Technology and Management Sciences (BUITMS) is an equal opportunity institution open to all persons of either sex without any discrimination of the religion, race, creed, color and geographical origin or physical impairment who are qualified and meet the criteria to pursue the program offerings by the University. The university has a policy in place that top students study free and that the university staff and faculty who want to improve their qualification admitted in any program also study free.

The university offers admission in following programs:

  • BS in Petroleum and Gas Engineering
  • BS in Mining Engineering
  • BS in Geological Engineering
  • BS in Chemical Engineering
  • BS in Industrial Engineering
  • BS in Architecture and Urban Planning

Faculty of (Basic) Sciences

  • BS in Chemistry
  • BS in Biological Sciences
  • BS in Physics
  • BS in Mathematics
  • MS in Mathematics
  • PhD in Mathematics
  • BS in Food Science and Technology

Faculty of Computer and Emerging Sciences

  • BS in Computer Science
  • MS in Computer Science
  • BS in Computer Engineering
  • MS in Computer Engineering
  • BS in Information Technology
  • BS in Electronic Engineering
  • MS in Electronic Engineering
  • PhD in Electronic Engineering
  • BS in Telecommunication Engineering
