Balance Toi

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Balance Toi is the first single from the forthcoming album off NBA player Tony Parker. The song is produced by Skalp, known from the famous french producer duo Kore & Skalp. It was realeased 26th March and have heavy rotation on french radio stations like NRJ and Skyrock

Tony Parker is also the first artist to sign with new established record label Music One, the new record label from french TV station TF1.

In the video Tony Parkers fiancee Eva Longoria can be seen.
