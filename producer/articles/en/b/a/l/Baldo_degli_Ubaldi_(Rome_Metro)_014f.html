Baldo degli Ubaldi is a station on Line A of the Rome Metro, situated on via Baldo degli Ubaldi, near the crossroads with Via Francesco Scaduto. The station was inaugurated, along with the others between Valle Aurelia and Battistini, on January 1st 2000.

[edit] Services

This station has:

  • Access for the disabled.
  • Escalators
