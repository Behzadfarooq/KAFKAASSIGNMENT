Ballylooby

Ballylooby is a town and parish located in South Tipperary, Ireland, between Caher and Clogheen. It is located at latitude 52° 19' 37N and longitude 7° 58' 44W.[1]

[edit] Local places of interest

Ballylooby is of local notability primarily for the Ballylooby-Castlegrace Gaelic Athletic Association Club.[2] The village has few local services other than a Roman Catholic church.
