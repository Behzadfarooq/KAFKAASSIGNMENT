Balsas is a city in the state of state of Maranhão, Brazil. It gives its name to the Balsas Region.

GEOGRAPHICAL AND DEMOGRAPHIC SITUATION The municipal district of Balsas is located in the microrregion of the General of Balsas to the south of the State of Maranhão. It has a distance of 800 Km of the Capital of the State, São Luís. The geographical coordinates of the healthy municipal district: Area of 13.141,64 Km² with altitude of 247 m. It is the 3rd municipal district in territorial greatness of the State. In agreement with the Census of 2001, the population of Rafts totaled 60.613 inhabitants, distributed like this: 50.144 inhabitants (82,73% of the population) they are in the Urban Zone and 10.019 inhabitants (17,27% of the population) in the Rural Zone. With annual medium growth of the population of 4,21%. Today in 2007 the population is superior than 78.000 inhabitants. The infant mortality rate is from 35,1 to thousand and the life espectativa it is of 64,1 years. The demographic density is of 4,79 Km² and the growth rate is of 5,62%. The limits of the municipal district are determined like this: To the North: with the municipal district of Fortaleza of the Walnuts and São Raimundo das Mangabeiras. To the South: with the municipal district of Alto Parnaíba, Sambaíba and the State of Tocantins. To the East: with the municipal districts of Alto Parnaíba, Sambaíba and Tasso Fragoso. To the West: with the municipal district of Riachão and the State of Tocantins.

CLIMATE Of the tropical and humid type, the climate is hot in the months of April to Octuber. The months of November to April the rainiest of the year are considered. RELIEF It possesses areas plans with topography it glides, offering good conditions for the automated agricultural activity. VEGETATION It presents typical vegetation of savannah. The trees possess larger amount of foliages that the trees of the savanna, by virtue of the area to have larger index pluviometric. But, according to the World Fund for the Nature, that vegetation is being devastated. The occupation of lands for the agricultural activities and the use of the wood for the production of the coal vegetable is the responsible principal for this devastation. HIDROGRAPHY The hidrografy of the municipal district is characterized by the existence of the rivers Balsas and it Maravilha. Rio Balsas stands out for your beauty and extension, he divides the city to the middle, taking a bath her and serving as tourist attraction for the children of the earth and for those that visit it, it still serves there as feeding source through the fishing accomplished. Rio Maravilha already stands out for the limpid waters, great for the bath. ECONOMY Balsas have an emergent economy and it is for that that offers singular opportunities for daring people and with managerial vision. Balsas have in the agriculture one of your more important economical activities. It especially produces in the agricultural field, with the automated farming, the technology used in the exploration of the savannahs, today transformed in immense and rich soy fields, rice, corn, sugar-cane and cotton. The economical activities involve the production, the distribution and the consumption of the necessary goods to the survival, well-being and the man's coexistence. The trade is quite expressive, because the agricultural production happens in great scale, standing out the soy grain that is exported to Europe through the North Corridor of Export.
