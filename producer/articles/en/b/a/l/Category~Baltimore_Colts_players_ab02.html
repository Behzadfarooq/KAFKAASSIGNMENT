Category:Baltimore Colts players

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

This category is for players of the Baltimore Colts of the NFL that existed from 1953 to 1983, when the franchise moved to Indianapolis. Players for the Indianapolis Colts have their own category, Category:Indianapolis Colts players. Some players are members of both categories.

There is also a separate category for the AAFC and later NFL franchise of the same name that existed from 1946 to 1950, Category:Baltimore Colts (1940s) players.
