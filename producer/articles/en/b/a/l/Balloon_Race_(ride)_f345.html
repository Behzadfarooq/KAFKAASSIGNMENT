Balloon Race (ride)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

You have new messages (last change).

The Zamperla Balloon Race is a circular motion ride that tilts. The ride will make it's way up the structure, and once it gets to a certain height, it will start tilting. This ride is very similar to swings. The Balloon Race can be seen as a relaxing ride.

[edit] Type of Seats

There are twelve balloons that can hold up to 4 passengers per car. The ride comes in 8 balloons and 12 balloons. There is a seatbelt on each side of the balloon. There is also a locking door. At some locations, a person has the power to get out of there balloon when the ride has finished. However, at Six Flags Over Georgia, the operator has a key to open them, and thus they are the only ones that can open them.

[edit] Another Manufacturer

Another manufacturer of the Balloon Race ride is Morgan. This ride is very similar except for the fact that the balloons are much bigger.

[edit] Balloon Race Locations

Here is a list of the Balloon Race rides. The number of Balloons / Pirate Ships that the ride has are in parenthesis:

  • Big Easy Balloons(12)--Six Flags Great America
  • The Pirate's Flight(12)--Geauga Lake in Ohio (themed to pirate ships instead of balloons)
  • Up, Up, and Away(8)--Six Flags Over Georgia

[edit] The Kiddie Version

Zamperla has made a smaller version of this ride. The ride does a similar motion, but the people riding the ride have an option of spinning the balloon. The ride still has balloons, but it's manufacturing name is Samba Balloons]. You should be able to find this ride in the kids area of amusement parks, and at carnivals.
