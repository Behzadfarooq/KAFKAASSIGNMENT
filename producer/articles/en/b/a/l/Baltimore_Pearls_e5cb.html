The Baltimore Pearls are a former team in the American Basketball Association playing in Baltimore, Maryland. The Pearls played inaugural season home games first at a local boys and girls club, before moving up to a local high school. The team finished a dismal 2-23 and missed the playoffs. The team played at the Coppin Center at Coppin State University in Baltimore. The team folded shortly after beginning the 2006 season [1]
