[edit] Will Be Krishna Devotee strictly ruled not to carry non vegetarian food?

Hi aLl,

Would like to query whether a krishna devotee is strictly ruled not to carry non vegetarian food or serving it to another people?Please reply me on this issue ASAP.thanks

Balan —The preceding unsigned comment was added by Balan4 (talk • contribs) 01:03, 24 January 2007 (UTC).

Dear Balan, I hope you don't mind me moving your question to here as it doesn't directly relate to any content in the Hare Krishna article. Hare Krishna devotees follow a Lacto vegetarian diet and would thus avoid carrying and especially serving food containing meat to other people. There may be some rare instances where if it was for the greater good to serve or carry non-vegetarian food then according to the discresion of a particular devotee they may end up doing so, but as a general rule it would be something which devotees would avoid where possible. You may find this linked conversation of interest: Can God Directly Talk to Men? Ys, Gouranga(UK) 21:16, 24 January 2007 (UTC)
