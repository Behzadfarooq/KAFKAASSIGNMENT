Balabac, Palawan

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Balabac is a 3rd class municipality in the province of Palawan, Philippines. According to the 2000 census, it has a population of 25,257 people in 4,723 households.

[edit] Barangays

Balabac is politically subdivided into 20 barangays.

  • Agutayan
  • Bugsuk (New Cagayancillo)
  • Bancalaan
  • Indalawan
