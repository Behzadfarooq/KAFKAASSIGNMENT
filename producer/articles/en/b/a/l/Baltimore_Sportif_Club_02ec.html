Baltimore Sportif Club is a Haitian football club, located in Saint-Marc, Haiti.

The club was founded August 1, 1974, and apparently named after the NFL club the Baltimore Colts. Although Baltimore has never won the Haitian league, they have consistently been one of the best clubs in the league.
