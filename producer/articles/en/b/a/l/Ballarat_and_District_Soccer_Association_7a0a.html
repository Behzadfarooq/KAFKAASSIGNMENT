The Ballarat and District Soccer Association is league that operates in the Ballarat area.

[edit] Age Groups

There are under 8's, 9's, 11's, 13's, 15's, 17's, reserves and seniors.

[edit] Clubs

There are 9 clubs, they are: North United, Ballarat, Victoria Park, Forest Rangers, Sebastapol Vikings, Creswick, Daylesford, Maryborough, Warnambool,

[edit] 2006 Cup Champions

The 2006 champions were Under 13's Warnambool, Under 15's North United, Under 17's Warnambool, Reserves Victoria Park, Seniors Sebastapol Vikings.

[edit] 2006 League Champions

Under 13's Warnambool, Under 15's Warnambool, Under 17's Warnambool, Reserves Victoria Park, Seniors Sebastapol Vikings.
