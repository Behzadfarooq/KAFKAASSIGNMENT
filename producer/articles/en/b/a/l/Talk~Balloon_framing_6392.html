One other positive for balloon framing is that there is minimal vertical shrinkage. Thus was a great way to attach siding an not have it buckle or buttcrack.


It is my understanding that a fourth Disadvantage of Baloon Framing is that it can easily 'balloon' out ward in the middle as the home settles. I live in michigan and have seen numerous older homes were a Buldging middle is evident I was told by my uncle a Cabinet maker who spent 15 years working as a rough framer that over time homes settle causing either a buldge outward or inward, the outward buldge causes the bubble shape the inward causes the floor in the middle to bump upward.

This is not a problem with steel balloon frameing only with wood.
