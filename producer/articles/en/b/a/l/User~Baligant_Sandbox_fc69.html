Regional scenes

Cybergrind (also known as digital grindcore, digigrind, and midicore) is a form of grindcore that, aside from the instruments used by ordinary grind, uses computer generated sounds, drum machines, and other synthetic instruments. Typical bands in the genre are often inhumanly fast, with songs usually under one minute in length, and relying heavily on samples.
