Balurghat (বালুরঘাট) is the headquarters of the Dakshin Dinajpur district of West Bengal in India.

[edit] Geography

Balurghat is located at 25.22° N 88.77° E[1]. It has an average elevation of 25 metres (82 feet).

[edit] Demographics

As of 2001 India census[2], Balurghat had a population of 135,516. Males constitute 51% of the population and females 49%. Balurghat has an average literacy rate of 79.2%, higher than the national average of 59.5%; with 82.5% of the males and 75.7% of females literate. 9% of the population is under 6 years of age.

[edit] Education

  • Balurghat College is located in Balurghat.
  • Balurghat High school is now 100 years old established in 1907 AD
  • The Atreyee English Medium School, Mongalpur is an english medium school affiliated to CBSE.
