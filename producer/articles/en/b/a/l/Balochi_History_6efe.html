[edit] Baloch

Different Researchers Analysed the Baloch ( Balochi : بلوچ ) or Baluch in different ways. Some says it means "Plume of Cock". The claimer refences from Shahnama by Firdausi in which he stated the balochies with their cultural sketch, who fought in Ventures of Kai Khosrow , they had worn Cloak with linings and a Plumy Turban . Others say that it means: Mobile . A third Group says it is modified form of "Baloot" a Persian word,means "Deserts Resider". An English researcher says that flair word Baloch is a modified form of "Barlooch" which means "Plunderer" or "Desert Fighter".

[edit] Balochistan

The word Balochistan is the whole of "Baloch"(a tribe) and "Satan"(living place) so Balochistan means Homeland of Balochis. All baloch tribes migrated from Sistan to Makran then to Balochistan(also known by east balochistan) then on the basis of different reasons they migrated, and spread throughout the Sub-continent .

[edit] Baloch Ancestory

Baloch as a Nation has fame in this area since long, there are different viewpoints about there origin, which are followings:

1. Arabs 2. Turks 3. Iranis 4. Rajputs

L.M.Dames says that Balochs are ancestral Irani, they migrated from southern Coastline of Caspian Sea . A well renowned Anthropalogist of Soviet Union, L.W.Oshanen has coincidence in Dames theory . All the northern and Southern baloch consider themself Arab generation, and Aleppo their first Homeland . So, Aleppo is the first homeland of balochis is based upon Baloch Anecdote . There is no dought in it that Baloch tribes particularly Rind Joined the Balochis during wars in baloch regions, So there were many Arabs in that area that time . To this viewpoint that Balochis are Arabs, "Rai Bahadur Hetoraam" also agrees and decides them Filiation of Hazrat Ameer Hamza, uncle of Hazrat Muhammad. Now have an overview on those anecdote that are based upon Balochi Ancient poetry, reasoning that the balochis are Qureshi Arabs( Generation of Hazrat Ibrahim). All balochis are well-knowned due to Hospitality like Arabs, no dought whom ancestor is Hazrat Ibrahim. On the other hand balochi curls are Imitation of Spiritual Hierarchical . Prayers of al-Aqsa Mosque never shaved there poll hair . Baloch also follow the Practice of Hazrat Abraham to judge the Truth and Falsehood by moving the suspicious person on the live coal (Baloch use Specially wood of "Kaheer" (because its coal does not turns into ash within about 24 hours) .

Briefly describing, first of all a channel (about 10ft in length, 4ft in width and 3ft depth) is digged and wood of Kaheer (commonly) is burnt to prepare coal during this time a reciter recites some verses of the Qu'ran, after that the suspicious person is asked to walk on the blazing coal in the presence of Above All (Muqadams, white beard men). If he is true then coal never burns his feet, a blameless baloch has faith in this fact to that day . It is known by "Patt" in balochi .

[edit] Ancient Baloch History

According to The balochi Narration, they are Quraishi Arabs, generation of Hazrat Ameer Hamza. When Yazeed, 2nd caliph of Umvi caliphate, Ejected Hazrat Imam Hussain, at that time balochs living in Aleppo, a region in Syria, were confederates[disambiguation needed] of Imam Hussain (A.S) against Yazeed . Yazeed Oppressed Balochis ( Then known by Balushi or Bailoos ) so they left Aleppo .


شکر الحمد گزاران ملک تھيں

يگ وت کھوشتی مہ ملکا ، اے جھان خان گل ايں

ما مريدوں يا علی ، اے دين ايمان پشتيں

کل بلا بھمپور نياما ، شہر سيستان مريض

( Domki Epics )

In history " Koosh and Baloosh " have been described together an the same way, wrote the Arabic writers that they are same . Firdausi also narrates their same culture, and same Commander in the following epics of Shahnama:

سپاہی زگردان کوچ و بلوچ

سگاليدہ جنگيدہ مانند غوچ

کے در جہان ديد ايشان نہ ديد

نہ آہن يک انگشت نامد پديد

سپہدار شان اشکش تيز ہش

کہ باراۓ دل بود دبا مغز ہوش

در فش بياوردہ پيکر پلنگ

ہمی از در فشش بيا رند چنگ

( By: Firdausi )

It is obvious from above epics that baluch and Kooch(Kurds), used to fight in the same way, under same commander and under the same Flag, showing a Leopard image (Commonly sign of avenge), according to Baluchi proverb " if stone can liquify then Baluchi is not Avenger " because avenging is their instinct .

According to Arabic writers:

1.Kuchies and Baluchies are same by ancesstory. 2.They speak their Languages,different from Persian . 3.During the age of Arabic Conquerors , Kushies and Balushies lived together and belonged to same Genealogy however, they spoke different languages . Similarly Historians claim, Language can not be the identifier of Genealogy .

In about 680 A.D, they migrated towards Karman in Iran and stayed there for a while with peace .

It is found from the ancient baluchi epics that there were 44 branches of Baluchies at that time . Based upon the ancient Baluchi poetry, they migrated from Karman when Emperor of Karman Pressurized them to give him 44 girls from each Tribe.

So, the baluchies sent 44 boys in Girls's dress and before the secret opened, they decamped towards Kech and Makran in South-East , and camped near Arabian Sea . After dwelling for a short while , they inhabited the area now called Baluchistan (Eastern Baluchistan) . The Emperor cameback the boys and chased the Baluchies towards Kech and Makran, where he was retreated by Baluchies .

Baluchi epics relating to baluch Ancesstory, are of about 11th or 12th cent (A.D) that is the time of immigration from Sistan to Makran . In these epics 44 elementary branches (In some Baluchi [[epics 26) of Baluchies have been adverted . Some of those retained their Tribal names such as:

Magsi , Gorgage , Kalmati , Nutkani , Gashkori , Korai , Rakhshani , Kulachi , Rind , Dareshak, Gabol , Domki , Lashari , Khosa , Jatoi , Gopang , Kurd , Dashti , Jiskani , Mirali and Chandio etc . These baloch tribes adopted their names either from an "Abode" from where they immigrated or characterised by neighborhood as a denotation .

For example, Magsi after Magas , a valley in Makran, Dashti after Dasht a region in Makran, Buledi after Buleda, a valley in Iran , Domki after Domak, a river in Iran where they used to live . Gashkori after Gash river in Iran which irrigates Bulaida Valley) . While Doodai after ( Doda Khan) , Jatoi ( Jatto, daughter of Mir Jalal Khan ), Mirali (Mir Khan) , Ahmadani (Ahmad Khan) , Hasni (Hassan Khan), Umrani (Umer Khan) . Similarly , Mazari is a balochi word meaning Lion , Leghari ( Balochi ) Dungy or Nasty , Khosa( Balouchi)"The warrior", Hoot ( Balouchi) Brave, Gabol ( Persian ) Aggressor or Combatant , Bozdar ( Persian ) Goat Keepers , also claimed to be derived from " Bordar " which means " Horse Bestrider " Dareshak ( Persian ) Brawn .
