Balingasag, Misamis Oriental

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

You have new messages (last change).

Balingasag is a 3rd class municipality in the province of Misamis Oriental, Philippines. According to the 2000 census, it has a population of 51,782 people in 9,618 households.

[edit] Highlights

  • Vega Ancestral House located in the Poblacion district, is a preserved Philippine colonial house which has been visited by national leaders like General Emilio Aguinaldo and President Sergio Osmeña.
