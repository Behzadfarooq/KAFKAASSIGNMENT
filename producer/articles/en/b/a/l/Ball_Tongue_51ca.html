Ball Tongue is a song by the Nu Metal band Korn. The track is listed as track #2 off their debut self-titled album, Korn. This song is about a man who worked with the band on T-Shirt designs. He had a pierced tongue or something else on his tongue (Hence-"Ball Tongue") and he gave the band a really hard time. This song is the first of Korn's works that includes Davis' unique scat vocals, although in this case he is singing real words, but not pronouncing them fully.
