Balfour

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Balfour may refer to:

  • Arthur Balfour, Prime Minister of the United Kingdom from 1902 to 1905, 1st Earl of Balfour and author of the Balfour Declaration.
  • Gerald Balfour, 2nd Earl of Balfour, his brother and also a Conservative politician.
  • Balfour_v._Balfour, Legal Case sited in Contract Law.
  • Henry Balfour Gardiner, British composer.
  • One of several people named James Balfour.
  • Eric Balfour, an American actor.
