Baladiyah

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Baladiyah (Arabic: بلدية‎) is an Arabic country subdivision term that can be translated as municipality. The plural is baladiyat (Arabic: بلديات‎).

See municipalities of Qatar

The current municipalities of Libya are called shabiyat, but the former ones were called baladiyat, sometimes translated as governorates.
