Balqa

Nahias of Balqa

Balqa' (Arabic: البلقاء‎ Al Balqā) is one of the governorates of Jordan. It is located northwest of Amman, Jordan's capital.

The capital of Balqa' Governorate is Salt.Balqa includes other Jordanian cities such as Mahis,Fuhais and , Ain el Basha

Balqa' is divided into 5 nahias:

  • Al-Balqa
  • Ardhah
  • As-Salt
  • Dair Alla
  • Shuna al-Janibiyya
