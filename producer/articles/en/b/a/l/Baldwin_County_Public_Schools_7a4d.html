Baldwin County Public Schools is a school district based in Bay Minette, Alabama, United States.

At the moment this article is only a list. This list itself does not provide the information that this article should include. Please help Wikipedia by expanding this article, and once it is a sufficient article please remove this message. Thank you!


BCPS serves all of Baldwin County, Alabama. It serves suburban areas and rural areas.
