Ballygunner GAA is a hurling club based in Ballygunner Village, on the outskirts of Waterford City.

The club holds ten Waterford Senior Hurling Titles: 1966–1968, 1992, 1995–1997, 1999, 2001, and 2005. It recently beat De La Salle GAA to win the 2005 Waterford Hurling Championship, although it later lost to Newtownshandrum of Cork GAA in the Munster Club Championship.

The club's players include Paul Flynn,Fergal "Bird" Hartley and Gearoid "Drohan" o Connor, Niall "Spill" Mullane and Paul "Doog" Hogan.
