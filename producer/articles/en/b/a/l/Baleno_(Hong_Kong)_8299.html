Baleno (Hong Kong)

Baleno (班尼路) is a clothing store chain prominent in East Asia, especially in Chinese-speaking regions.

Texwinca acquired the Baleno trademark and formed Baleno Holdings Limited in 1996.

Texwinca currently operates approximately 5000 stores, with key markets in mainland China, Hong Kong, Taiwan, Singapore and the Middle East.

Andy Lau has been a model for the Baleno label since at least 2003, and is scheduled to celebrate the Voice of Baleno 10th Anniversary with a concert at the Hong Kong Convention and Exhibition Centre on 5 November 2006.

Faye Wong has also been a model for the brand, and Baleno was Title Sponsor for her December 2003 concert series at the Hong Kong Coliseum.
