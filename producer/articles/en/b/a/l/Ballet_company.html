[edit] Members

A ballet company usually consists of:

  • Artistic director
  • Ballet mistress or Ballet master
  • Choreographer
  • Prima ballerinas
  • Ballerinas
  • Principal dancers
  • Soloists
  • Corps de ballet
  • Regisseur
  • Character dancers
  • Répétiteur or Dance notator
  • Music director
  • Orchestra
  • Executive director
