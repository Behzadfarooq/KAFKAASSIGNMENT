Balitanghali is the noontime newscast of Q (television network) in the Philippines. It is produced by GMA Network's News and Public Affairs division. The newscast is anchored by Pia Arcangel and Raffy Tima. It is currently the highest-rated noontime newscast in the Philippines. It is the only 45-minute news program produced by GMA Network to air on during Saturdays. The newscast was pivotal in the network's coverage of the PhilSports Arena stampede, in which the network was praised for, due in part of the anchors and the reporters on the scene.
