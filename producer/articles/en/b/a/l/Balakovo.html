Balakovo (Russian: Балако́во) is a town in Saratov Oblast, Russia.

Founded in 1762, town status since 1913. Balakovo is located at 52°2′N 47°47′E.

Population: 200,470 (2002 Census).

Balakovo is the location of the Saratov Hydroelectric Station on the Volga River. It is served by Balakovo Airport.
