Baloot

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Baloot (Arabic: بلوت‎, is a popular trick card game played in Saudi Arabia and some nearby countries.

[edit] Seating

Baloot seating arrangement.

The two members of each team are seated across each other, often on a table in such a way that no player can see the cards of other players .
