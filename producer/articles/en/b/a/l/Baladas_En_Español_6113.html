Baladas En Español is a Spanish language album by Roxette, released in 1996. The album was criticized for being badly translated from English, leading to nonsense lyrics. It has been suggested that the translator, Luis Gómez Escolar, apparently did not take the project seriously.

"Un Día Sin Ti" and "No Se Si Es Amor" were released as singles.
