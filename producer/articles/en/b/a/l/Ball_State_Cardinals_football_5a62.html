Ball State Cardinals football

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Ball State Cardinals are a mid-major college football program. They compete in Division I-A and the Mid-American Conference

[edit] Bowl Games

The Cardinals have gone to three Division I bowl games. They have an 0-3 record in these games.

Date Played Bowl Game Winning Team Losing Team
December 9, 1989 California Bowl Fresno State 27 Ball State 6
December 17, 1993 Las Vegas Bowl Utah State 42 Ball State 33
December 18, 1996 Las Vegas Bowl Nevada 18 Ball State 15
