[edit] About Me

My name is Nicholas Bering. Balrog30 is an internet alias that I've been using for about 7 or 8 years. I've lived in St. Catharines, Ontario (a stone's throw from Niagara Falls), all my life. I have many diverse interests, and a wealth of knowledge pertaining to a wide variety of subject matter. I was raised on a farm, but my passion is in computers. I worked for EB Games Canada for about 2 years as a retail sales associate, and I think it says a lot about me that I think it was a big mistake to quit and move onto a better paying job. I loved my work there. I loved sharing what I knew about video games with my customers, and helping them pick the best product for them. I have a passion for programming, and would like to attend university for computer science, and possibly move on to teach programming at the high school level.

As a great deal of my use of wikipedia is related to computer science, computer engineering, and other technology-related topics, I feel it would be helpful to mention some of my background in the field.

I am by no means an expert in the field, but I do have strong research skills, and am familiar with a range of topics. I started programming in grade seven, when my brother came home from high school and showed my family the program he had made in QBasic. I looked at his code, and figured out how it worked, and then changed it to make my own program. I found others in my grade who were as interested in computers and programming as I was, and we formed a book exchange. Technical reference books are very expensive, especially to young students who have yet to get jobs beyond paper routes and cutting lawns, so we each bought a book, and when we were done learning from it, we'd trade with someone else, until everyone had taken a turn with every book.

Anyway, over the years since then, I've learned quite a bit in a wide variety of fields.
