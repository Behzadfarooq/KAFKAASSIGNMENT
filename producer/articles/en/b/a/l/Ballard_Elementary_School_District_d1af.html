Ballard Elementary School District is located in the rural town of Ballard, California serving residents of the Santa Ynez Valley.

The district was founded in 1882 and was the first school in the valley. There is one school in this district, Ballard Elementary. Ballard Elementary is a Kindergarten through 6th grade school originally built in 1886 as a one-room schoolhouse. The "little red schoochlhouse" is still used today by the Kindergarten and 1st grades. 128 students attend this school.[1]
