Balboa

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Balboa may refer to:

  • Vasco Núñez de Balboa, Spanish explorer, for whom are named:
    • Balboa, Panama, a port city in Panama
    • Balboa, California, a subsection of Newport Beach, California, and also called Balboa Peninsula, Newport Beach, California
    • Balboa Island, Newport Beach, California
    • Balboa (dance), a close embrace style of swing dancing
    • Panamanian balboa, official currency of Panama
    • Balboa, the merged tribe name in Survivor: Pearl Islands
    • Balboa High School, a public high school in San Francisco, California.
    • Balboa, a probe featured in Alien Planet
  • Balboa, a municipality (pop. 440) in the province of León, Castile-Leon, Spain
  • Balboa, synonym of the plant genus Chrysochlamys
  • Rocky Balboa, protagonist of the Rocky film series, played by Sylvester Stallone.
