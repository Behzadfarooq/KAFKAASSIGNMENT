Ballasalla railway station is located in the village of Ballasalla in the south of the Isle of Man. It is a station for the Isle of Man Steam Railway.

The original wooden railway station was demolished in the mid 1980s and replaced with a brick building to house a small ticket office and waiting area.

Car parking is available at the station and it is also served by regular buses travelling between Douglas and Port Erin or Port St Mary on the A5 road.
