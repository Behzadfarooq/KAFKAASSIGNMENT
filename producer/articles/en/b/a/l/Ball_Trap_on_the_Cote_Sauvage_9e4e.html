Ball Trap on the Cote Sauvage

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Ball Trap on the Cote Sauvage is a 1989 British television comedy drama (transmitted on BBC1), written by celebrated screenwriter Andrew Davies, and set in France. The one-off drama starred Michael Kitchen, Miranda Richardson, Erika Hoffman, and Zoe Wanamaker.
