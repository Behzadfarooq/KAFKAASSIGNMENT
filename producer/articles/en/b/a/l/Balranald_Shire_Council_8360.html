Balranald Shire is local government area in the Riverina area of western New South Wales, Australia on the Sturt Highway. It is the location of World Heritage listed Mungo National Park. It includes the towns of Balranald and Euston. Other localities in the Shire include, Kyalite, Hatfield, Penarie, Clare and Oxley.
