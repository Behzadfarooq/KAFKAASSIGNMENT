Baldichieri d'Asti is a comune (municipality) in the Province of Asti in the Italian region Piedmont, located about 35 km southeast of Turin and about 9 km west of Asti. As of 31 December 2004, it had a population of 1,000 and an area of 5.2 km².[1]

Baldichieri d'Asti borders the following municipalities: Asti, Castellero, Monale, Tigliole, and Villafranca d'Asti.
