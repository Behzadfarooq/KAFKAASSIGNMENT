Title The Affinity of Stadérrouté
Description The purpose of laying before the reader, in words of the adventures itself, which we must otherwise have told of on our own, may well be too practical than for the pleasure of our eyes. It was thus a joy for one to talk of the tales, than told of the days; to forsake the format and to induce a form of counter-sophistication of words, to construct the experience never before illustrated in mind, by that of a pen.
