Balls & My Word is the eighth solo album from Houston Hip Hop artist Scarface. This was his least successful album commercially, also receiving mediocre reviews. The single "Recognize" got moderate radio and video airplay. Its a compilation album of old & lost tracks created by J Prince. Scarface has stated he had nothing to do with this album & that The Fix was his last official album.
