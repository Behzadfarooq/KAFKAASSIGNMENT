[edit] Warning another user about vandalism

I noticed you were trying to warn another user about vandalizing an article. Wikipedia has an automatic mechanism for doing just that. As an example you would add {{subst:test2-n|Anthony Lee}}(for the Anthony Lee article) to the talk page of a user who is quite obviously engaged in acts of vandalism. If you want more information on warning templates please feel free to ask me on my talk page. Happy editing, i kan reed 08:45, 4 February 2007 (UTC)

I wasn't trying to chastise you here, just provide you with useful information about how to handle certain situations. Forgive me if I've bitten you. i kan reed 08:50, 4 February 2007 (UTC)
just as added information a list of kinds of warnings can be found here: Wikipedia:Warnings. i kan reed 08:52, 4 February 2007 (UTC)

[edit] Adoption

I don't think I'd be the best possible adopter for you. You seem to be interested in fighting vandalism, which is a noble effort, so you should seek out someone who is more familiar at that task. I looked at Wikipedia:Adopt-a-User/Adoptee's Area/Adopters and it seems that User:Tellyaddict, User:CattleGirl, User:Orderinchaos78, and User:SGGH both claim to fight vandalism and are currently adopting! You should ask those four to adopt you and be sure to state that your interest is to fight vandalism (and any other interests!). You should put the userbox {{adoptme}} on your userpage as well.
