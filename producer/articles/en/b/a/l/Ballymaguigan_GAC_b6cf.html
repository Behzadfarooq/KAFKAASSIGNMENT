St. Trea's Ballymaguigan GAC, in is a club in the South Derry GAA league of the Gaelic Athletic Association. The club was originally founded in the 1944 in Ballymaguigan, County Londonderry (five miles from Magherafelt, and two from Ballyronan. Originally Ballymaguigan shared a team with Newbridge, County Londonderry, although during World War II the areas were split up by an airfield, and separate teams were formed. The team consists of a few different age groups of Gaelic football teams.
