Balangir, also known as Bolangir, is a city and municipality in Balangir district, in the state of Orissa, India.

[edit] Geography

Balangir is located at 20.72° N 83.48° E[1]. It has an average elevation of 183 metres (600 feet). Bolangir has a rich cultural heritage.Though economically not very well off, it has a great potential for development

[edit] Demographics

As of 2001 India censusGRIndia, Balangir had a population of 85,203. Males constitute 52% of the population and females 48%. Balangir has an average literacy rate of 74%, higher than the national average of 59.5%; with 57% of the males and 43% of females literate. 11% of the population is under 6 years of age.
