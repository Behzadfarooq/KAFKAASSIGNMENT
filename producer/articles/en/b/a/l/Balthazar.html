Balthazar (also spelled Balthasar or Balthassar), is a traditional name for one of the anonymous Three Wise Men in the Gospel of Matthew. The others below are named for him. It is also an alternate form of the Babylonian king Belshazzar, mentioned in the Book of Daniel.

Balthazar may also be:

  • Balthazar (novel), a 1958 novel by Lawrence Durrell
  • Balthazar, a 1909 book by Anatole France
  • Balthazar (film), a 1966 film by Robert Bresson
  • Professor Balthazar, a Croatian cartoon series for children about an old inventor.
