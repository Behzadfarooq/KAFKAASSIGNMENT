Bali (Chinese: 八里; pinyin: Bālǐ; Wade-Giles: Pa1-li3; Taiwanese POJ: Pat-lí) is a rural township in the northwestern part of Taipei County, Taiwan Province of the Republic of China. In Taiwanese, it was known as Pat-lí-hun (八里坌) during the rule of the Qing Dynasty. The new Port of Taipei is under construction in Bali.

  • Area: 39.49 km²
  • Population: 31,453 people (2004)
