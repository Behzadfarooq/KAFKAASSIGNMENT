Bala Sai Baba

Bala Sai Baba is an Indian guru. People claim that he copies everything- way of dressing, way of addressing, walking and talking- from Bhagawan Sri Sathya Sai Baba at Puttaparthi. Even the projects that he takes up are exactly along the same lines. He has his ashram at Kurnool in Andhra Pradesh.

This often fools many new devotees arriving to have the darshan of Sri Satya Sai Baba. They go to Bala Sai as " He is more accessible."
