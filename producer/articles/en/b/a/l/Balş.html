Balş is a town in Romania, in the Olt county.

There are 3 hypotheses about its name:

  1. The locality was named after the Balşiţa brook.
  2. The name comes from the Turkish word "Baliş" (honey) as there is a large apiculture area.
  3. A boyar named Balş settled here in 5th or 6th century.
