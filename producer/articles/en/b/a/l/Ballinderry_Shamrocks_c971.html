Ballinderry Shamrocks (Irish:Baile an Doire na Seamróg ) is a Gaelic Athletic Association club based in Ballinderry. They currently cater for both Gaelic football and Camogie. Ballinderry teams play all their GAA in Derry championships, however about a third of the parish is located in County Tyrone as well.

Ballinderry fields Gaelic football teams at U8, U10, U12, U14, U16, Minor, Reserve, Thirds and Senior levels. There are also Minor and Senior Camogie teams.
