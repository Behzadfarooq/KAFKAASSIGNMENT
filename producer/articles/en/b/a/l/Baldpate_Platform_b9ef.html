Baldpate Platform

Baldpate Platform is a 579.7-metre-tall offshore oil platform near the coast of Lousiana. Baldpate Platform was designed by Hudson Engineering (now J. Ray McDermott Engineering) in Houston, Texas, and installed by Hudson's parent company, J. Ray McDermott, through its Marine Division.
