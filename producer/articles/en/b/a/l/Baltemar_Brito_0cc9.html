Baltemar Brito

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Baltemar Brito is the 52 year old Brazilian football assistant manager to Chelsea F.C.'s manager José Mourinho. He also specialises in audio-visual work to prepare the team for matches. He also used to play as a defender in his playing days.[1]
