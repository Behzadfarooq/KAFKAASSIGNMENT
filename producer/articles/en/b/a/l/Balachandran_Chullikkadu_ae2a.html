Balachandran Chullikkadu

Balachandran Chullikkadu is a renowned Malayalam poet from Kerala, India. He was born on July 30, 1957 in Paravur. He completed his graduation in English literature from Maharajas college, Ernakulam. He works in the District Treasury Office.

His collection of poems published are "Pathinettu kavithakal", "Amaavaasi", "Ghazal", "Maanasaantharam", "Dracula" etc. A collection of his complete poems, Balachandran Chullikkadinte Kavithakal(The Poems of Balachandran Chullikkad)(2000) was published by D.C Books,Kerala. They have also published the book of his memmoirs, Chidambarasmarana(2001).

Most of his poems are in the Malayalam language. His wife is the poetess Vijayalakshmi.

[edit] Some verses from Chullikkadu

Choodathe poyi nee, ninakkayi naan
 Ente chora chaari chuvappicha
 Panineer pookkal

Kaanathe poyi nee, ninakkayi naan
 Ente praanante pinnil
 Kurichitta vaakkukal

English translation:

You left without gathering
 The Rose petals
 That I reddened with my blood
 Just For You

You left without seeing
 The words I had scribbled
 On my soul, for you
 ... Dukhamaanenkilum ninnekkurichulla dukham enthaandam anenikkomane

Even if its grief, the grief for you - Oh, how joyful it is, sweetheart.


Amme pinvili vilikkathe
 Mutinarukontente kazhalu kettathe

English translation: Mother, don't call me back
 Dont tie my legs with hair
