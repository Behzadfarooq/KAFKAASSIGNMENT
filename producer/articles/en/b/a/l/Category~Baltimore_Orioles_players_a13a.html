  • This category includes players for the franchise that became the present Baltimore Orioles in 1954. It does not include players for earlier Baltimore Oriole teams: the American Association and National League team of the 19th Century, the American League team of 1901-02 (now the New York Yankees), the St. Louis Browns (1902-1953), or the minor league Orioles of the 20th Century.
