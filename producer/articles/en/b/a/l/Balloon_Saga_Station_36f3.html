Balloon Saga Station

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

A train at Balloon Saga station during the Balloon Fiesta

Balloon Saga Station (バルーンさが駅, Baruun saga eki?) is a train station beside the Kase river, Saga City, Saga Prefecture, Japan. It is run by JR Kyushu and is on the Nagasaki Main Line. It is a temporary station which is only operational during the Saga International Balloon Fiesta (around the end of October/beginning of November).

[edit] Layout

There is only one platform, which one must ascend to or descend from. Limited express trains with eight cars or under stop here.

[edit] Environs

Built at the dry Kase riverbed, there are mainly fields surrounding the station and not many homes or businesses. During the Balloon Festival the area is full of tourists.
