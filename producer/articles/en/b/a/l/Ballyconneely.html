Ballyconneely (Irish: Baile Conaola) is a small village in the west of the Connemara region, County Galway, Republic of Ireland.

It is not a village as such, as settlements are spread out north on the road to Clifden and south on the road to Roundstone. Every July, the Ballyconneely Pony Show attracts people from all around County Galway to exhibit livestock.
