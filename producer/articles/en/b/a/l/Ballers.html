Ballers is a 2007 comedy film that has been announced and is written by Robinson Young and will be produced by Jerry Bruckheimer.

[edit] Plot

Ballers is a comedy set in the world of pro basketball. Due to a lack of production effort and time the films release date or release in general is unknown. Lil Wayne will not have a major role in Ballers when/if the movie comes out. The film will star Dave Chappelle and Tip Harris (T.I.)
