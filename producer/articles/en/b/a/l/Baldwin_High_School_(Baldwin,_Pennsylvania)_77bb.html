Baldwin High School is in suburban Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania. It is the 3rd largest public school in Pittsburgh and is currently under renovation. It is scheldued to be finished in January of 2009. Long time NFL coach for the Dallas Cowboys, Chicago Bears and Miami Dolphins and the current head coach for the University of Pittsburgh football team, Dave Wannstedt (Graduated in 1970) and Utah senator Orrin Hatch are alumni of Baldwin High School.

[edit] Communities Served by Baldwin High School

  • Baldwin, Allegheny County, PA
  • Whitehall, Allegheny County, PA
  • Baldwin Township, PA
