Bali FC (registered as Bali Persegi FC) is an Indonesian football club based in Bali. They play in the top division in Indonesian football, the Liga Indonesia.

[edit] History

Bali FC, formed in 2006 is a merger of football clubs from Bali, such as Persegi Gianyar, Perseden Denpasar, Persekaba Kabupaten Badung and Perst Tabanan. The club joined the Liga Indonesia using Persegi Gianyar's license as Persegi Gianyar could not continue it's participation in the Liga Indonesia due to financial difficulties.
