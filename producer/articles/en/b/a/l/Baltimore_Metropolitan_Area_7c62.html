Baltimore Metropolitan Area

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Location on the US east coast

The Baltimore Metropolitan Area, also known as Baltimore-Towson, is a Metropolitan Statistical Area (MSA) encompassing northeastern Maryland and a small section of southern Pennsylvania. The MSA is focused on Baltimore City, Baltimore County, Anne Arundel County, Harford County, and portions of Howard County, and York County, Pennsylvania. It has an estimated population of 2,655,675, making it the 19th-largest metropolitan area in the United States.
