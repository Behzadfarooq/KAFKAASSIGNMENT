The Baltimore Bandits were an ice hockey team in the American Hockey League in the 1995-96 and 1996-97 seasons. They played in Baltimore, Maryland at the Baltimore Arena. After two years of minimal success, the franchise was moved to Cincinnati, Ohio as the Cincinnati Mighty Ducks.


[edit] History

The franchise became known as:
  • Cincinnati Mighty Ducks (1997-2005)
  • Rockford IceHogs (2007- )
The market was previously served by:
  • Baltimore Clippers (1962-76)
  • Baltimore Skipjacks (1982-93)
