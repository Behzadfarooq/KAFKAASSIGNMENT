Balter, Guth, Aloni & Co. (a.k.a. "BGA"), founded in 1974, is the second largest law firm in Israel. It has offices in Tel Aviv, Jerusalem, Haifa, Tiberias and Beer Sheva.

BGA is a member of Consulegis, an international network of law firms. It holds a cooperation agreement with the U.S law firm of Herzfeld & Rubin.
