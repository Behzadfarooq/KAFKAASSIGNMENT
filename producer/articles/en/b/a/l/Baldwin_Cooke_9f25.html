Baldwin Cooke also known as Baldy Cooke (10 March 1888 - 31 December 1953) was an American actor.

New York-born Cooke, and his wife Alice, toured in vaudeville with Stan Laurel, remaining close friends over the years. He appeared in some thirty Laurel and Hardy comedies. Cooke also appeared in the Our Gang series, and supported Charley Chase in 1931's La Señorita de Chicago.
