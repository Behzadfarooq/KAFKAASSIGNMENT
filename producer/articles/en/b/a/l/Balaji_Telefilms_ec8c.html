Balaji Telefilms

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

You have new messages (last change).

Balaji Telefilms is a production house in India and is a company that has been promoted by Mr. Jeetendra, Mrs. Shobha Kapoor and Ms. Ekta Kapoor. It has produced a number of famous serials in India. Its headquarters are in Mumbai.
