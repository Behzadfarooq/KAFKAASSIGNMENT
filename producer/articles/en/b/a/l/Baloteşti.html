Baloteşti

Baloteşti is a commune in the northwestern part of Ilfov county, Romania. Two small rivers flow through this location: Cociovaliştea and Vlăsia.

The commune is named after an 18th century boyar, Balotă, who owned local lands.[citation needed]

[edit] Trivia

  • Elena Ceauşescu was born in the village of Petreşti, Baloteşti Commune.
  • On March 31, 1995, a TAROM Romanian Airbus A310 crashed near Baloteşti, leaving 60 dead (see Tarom Flight 371).
