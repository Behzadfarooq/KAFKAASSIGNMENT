Baltimore Oriole may refer to:

  • Baltimore Oriole (Icterus galbula), a small blackbird found mostly in the Americas. They are New World orioles, not to be confused with orioles.
  • The 1942 Jazz song "Baltimore Oriole", originally by Hoagy Carmichael and Paul Francis Webster, appeared in the Warner movie "To Have and Have Not" (1944).

[edit] Baseball

  • The Baltimore Orioles, an American Major League Baseball team from Baltimore, Maryland in the USA.
  • Baltimore Orioles (19th century), a 19th-century US National League baseball team.
  • Baltimore Orioles (minor league), a 20th-century US minor league baseball team.
  • For the 1901-1902 American League team known as the Baltimore Orioles, see New York Yankees.
