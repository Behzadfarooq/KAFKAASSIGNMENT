Ballistic Weapons

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Ballistic Weapons is a mod for Unreal Tournament 2004, designed to replace elements, especially the weapon system, of the original game to provide a more tactical kind of gameplay. Ballistic replaces all original weapons with 31 new ones based on a new, greatly enhanced weapon system hosting a wide array of features. In addition, items are replaced with the new Ballistic ones, such as the health, armor, ammo, and power-ups.

The design, style and theme for the elements of this mod are taken from RuneStorm's "Project Archon"Â© universe, a setting where sinister alien races and vast Terran armies battle for survival across a diversity of unchartered planets, war-torn worlds and battle-ravaged colonies. All of the content in this mod is 100% original with new meshes, textures and sounds for all weapons and pickups. Completely new aiming, damage and reloading systems allow for a much more realistic gameplay environment.

To play UT2004 with Ballistic Weapons use the "Ballistic" mutator. The entire mod can be implemented through a single, simple mutator which allows for many possibilities. You can use Ballistic weapons in any gametype such as Capture the Flag, Deathmatch, Assault, Onslaught, etc. Ballistic Weapons can also be used in conjunction with another mod or gametype which may use new levels or all kinds of different gameplay modifcations.

The new Ballistic Gametypes, are simply the standard gametypes, with Ballistic's options. The main reason for this, is to add several options and tweaks, that mutators cannot do, such as Dark Players.

This release also includes a special "Ballistic Melee" mutator. This allows you to play Ballistic using only the melee weapons. Also included mutators are; Ballistic Gore, Ballistic Loadout, Ballistic Arena, Ballistic Sprint, and Itemizer. More info on all these mutators, can be found under the "Ballistic Mutators" section.

[edit] Cameras

The M50 can be used to place a special camera on the wall. It can also be used to check the view of the camera and adjust its rotation and zoom. Cameras are difficult to see, but can be disabled by enemies. To place, and switch view, use the "Weapon Special" key when you have the M50 out. To pick up the camera, simply press the "Use" key on the camera.

The G5 RPG, also has a rocket's eye camera view, which can be viewed when the Weapon Special key is pressed when a rocket is launched from the secondary scope mode.
