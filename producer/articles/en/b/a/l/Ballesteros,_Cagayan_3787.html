Ballesteros, Cagayan

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

You have new messages (last change).

Ballesteros is a 4th class municipality in the province of Cagayan, Philippines. According to the 2000 census, it has a population of 27,534 people in 2,270 households.

[edit] Barangays

Ballesteros is politically subdivided into 19 barangays.

  • Ammubuan
  • Baran
  • Cabaritan East
  • Cabaritan West
  • Cabayu
  • Cabuluan East
  • Cabuluan West
  • Centro East (Pob.)
  • Centro West (Pob.)
  • Fugu
  • Mabuttal East
  • Mabuttal West
  • Nararagan
  • Palloc
  • Payagan East
  • Payagan West
  • San Juan
  • Santa Cruz
  • Zitanga
