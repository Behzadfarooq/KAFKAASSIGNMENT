Balisto

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

German Balisto bar with Yoghurt and berries

Balisto is a muesli-based confectionery covered with chocolate and is manufactured by Mars Incorporated.

Normally, there are two fingers in a package, though one-finger packages also exist. They are available in Austria, Germany, Switzerland, Ireland, The Netherlands, France, and Belgium.

[edit] Varieties

Balisto is available in several different flavours, each with their own colour. Not all flavours are available everywhere. A partial list:

  • Muesli-mix (green wrapper)
  • Grain-mix (red wrapper)
  • Yoghurt and berries-mix (purple wrapper)
  • Almond and honey-mix (yellow wrapper)
  • Cocos-mix
