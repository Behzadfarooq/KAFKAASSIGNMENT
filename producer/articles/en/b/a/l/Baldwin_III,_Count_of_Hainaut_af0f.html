Baldwin III, Count of Hainaut

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Coats of Arms of the Counts of Hainaut.

Baldwin III (1088–1120) was count of Hainaut from 1098 to his death. He was son of Baldwin II, Count of Hainaut and Ida of Louvain.

[edit] Family and children

He was married with Yolande de Wassenberg, daughter of Gerald I (Gerhard)Flaminius de Wassenberg, Count of Geldern. Their children were:

  1. Baldwin IV, Count of Hainaut, married Alix (Adelaide) de Namur.
  2. Gerhard of Hainault, ancestor of Counts of Dale.
  3. Gertrude/Ida of Hainault, married before 9 August 1138 Roger de Toeni of Conches and Flamsted.
  4. Richildis of Hainault, married:
    1. Thierry d'Avesnes;
    2. Everard II Radulf, Burgrave of Doornick.
