Icelandic film director Baltasar Kormákur

Baltasar Kormákur (born 27 February 1966) is an Icelandic actor and theater and film director and film producer. Nowadays he is best known for his work as a director of films like 101 Reykjavík, Hafið, A Little Trip to Heaven (starring Julia Stiles and Forest Whitaker) and a film based on the book Jar City by Arnaldur Indriðason.
