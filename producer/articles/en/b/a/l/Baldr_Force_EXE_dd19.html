Baldr Force EXE is a 2D action-shooter game, the upgraded edition to 2001's Baldr Force, boasting fast action and detailed sprite characters. The game feature a world in not so distant future, where human able to dive in the network, and fight using a humanoid tools called "Simulacrum".

[edit] Plots

As a hacker of renouned hacking group "Steppenwolf", Tōru Sōma (相馬 透, Sōma Tōru?) and his partners earn their living by hacking into servers and sell the infomations they stole.

As the last hacking before disbanding their group, they hacked into a military server, but soon they find this is a setup by the military to lure terrorists, and they were drew into battle between two factions. At the end, one of Tōru Sōma's friend was killed by an unknown Simulacrum, while most of other group members have been arrested. To investigate and avenge his friend's death, Tōru Sōma accepted the offer to join the military.

[edit] Difference between versions

  • DC and PS2 version removed hentai scenes.
  • PS2 version has new voice casting, a new theme song, a new opening movie, and a new cutscene.
