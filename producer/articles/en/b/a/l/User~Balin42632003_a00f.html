A little bit about me...

My name is Sean Storm, 43 years old and originally from New England. I am living presently in Southwest Georgia with my fiancee and her son. I have been on Wikipedia now since August of 2005, and have contributed in a small way on more then 3,400 articles, and have created about 50. I hope to be of more service in the future. My nickname I took from two sources, Sir Balin of Arthurian legend, and Balin a famous Dwarf from LOTR.

I am an orthodox Anglo-Catholic Anglican, rather old-fashioned and conservative...lol. I try to stay out of politics, and but I haven't destroyed my soapbox yet. I am a member of the Anglican Communion Network.

I am interested primarily in Anglican, Anglo-Catholic, classic Monster movies, cooking, baking, British comedys, Science fiction, Shipwrecks, and topics of military history.

My Motto: When something is a long-standing tradition, it is dangerous to assume it is pointless!
