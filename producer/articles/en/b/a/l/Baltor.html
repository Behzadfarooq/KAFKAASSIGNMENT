Valtor is the new antagonist in Season 3 of the TV series, Winx Club. He is named Baltor in the 4kids version. He originated from Planet Domino (Sparx in the 4kids version) and has fire-like powers.

Valtor was created from the embers of the Dragon's Flame by the Three Ancient Witches that invaded Domino. He was banished to the Omega Dimension for 17 years, until The Trix found him and wanted to sacrifice him to some giant snakes in order for them to escape. But Valtor manages to destroy the snakes and joins the Trix. It's been that way since. He first invaded Andros and turned all the mermaids into monsters.

Valtor is the third main villain after Lord Darkar. He is a powerful wizard who would love nothing more than to be the greatest wizard of them all, and to do so, he turns people into monsters that willingly serve him, and places a mark that looks like a "V" on other people so they too will serve him, in order to conquer each planet or realm. He, like Bloom, was born from the Dragon's Flame, but unlike Bloom, he was born from its dark side. He was with the Three Ancient Witches when they destroyed Domino, but was sent to the Omega Dimension. Not long after Darkar's defeat, the Trix were sent there too where they found and broke Valtor out of his prison. Valtor escaped from Omega with the Trix and began conquering Andros. Valtor then moved on to go to Solaria where he took some power from the second sun of Solaria, which was enough to give him back the power that Omega took from him. Valtor is often very interested in Bloom, possibly because of their mysterious connection in the past, which makes the Trix wonder if he has a crush on her.
