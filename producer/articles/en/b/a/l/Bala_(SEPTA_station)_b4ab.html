Bala (SEPTA station)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

You have new messages (last change).

Bala is a station along the SEPTA Cynwyd Line. The station, located at the intersection of Bala Avenue and City Avenue (US-1), includes a 76-space parking lot.
