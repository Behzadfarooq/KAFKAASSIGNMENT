Baxter Dury is a British indie musician. Baxter, son of Ian Dury, has been signed to Rough Trade Records since his debut single 'The Oscar Brown EP' in 2001. He has yet to achieve anywhere near the success of his father and is still supporting more popular indie bands such as The Long Blondes and the Magic Numbers. However he is well regarded by many in the music press, for instance having a single of the week in NME.
