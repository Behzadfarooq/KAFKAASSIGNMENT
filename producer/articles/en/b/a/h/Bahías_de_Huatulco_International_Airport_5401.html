Bahías de Huatulco International Airport (IATA: HUX, ICAO: MMBT) is an international airport located at Huatulco, Oaxaca, Mexico on the Pacific coast. It handles national and international air traffic for the pacific coast south of the city of Oaxaca.

[edit] Airlines and destinations

  • Continental Airlines
    • Continental Express operated by ExpressJet Airlines (Houston-Intercontinental)
  • Frontier Airlines
    • Apple Vacations operated by Frontier Airlines (Denver) [weekend charter]
  • Magnicharters (Mexico City)
  • Mexicana
    • Click Mexicana (Mexico City)
  • Skyservice (Calgary, Edmonton, Montreal, Toronto-Pearson, Vancouver, Winnipeg)[1]
  • Sunwing Airlines (Toronto)
