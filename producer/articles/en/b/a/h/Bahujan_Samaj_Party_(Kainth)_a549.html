Bahujan Samaj Party (Kainth), a splinter group of Bahujan Samaj Party in Punjab. BSP(K) was founded on October 30, 2004. BSP(K) is led by Satnam Singh Kainth (ex MP and ex-president of Democratic Bahujan Samaj Morcha). Kainth had re-joined BSP ahead of the 2004 Lok Sabha elections, but was later expelled.
