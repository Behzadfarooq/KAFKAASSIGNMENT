Bahlingen am Kaiserstuhl is a municipality in the district of Emmendingen in Baden-Württemberg, Germany. It is located on the edge of the volcanic region Kaiserstuhl and is inhabited by 3,823 people. The town is home to the Oberliga football team Bahlinger SC.

Bahlingen should not be confused with the town Balingen in Zollernalbkreis.
