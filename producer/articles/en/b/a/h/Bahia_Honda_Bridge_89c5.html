Bahia Honda Bridge

Bahia Honda Bridge is a bridge in the lower Florida Keys connecting Bahia Honda Key with the Spanish Harbor Keys. It conveys motorists along US 1 to Key West.

[edit] History

Built as a replacement for the original railroad bridge, which had been converted for highway use in 1938.
