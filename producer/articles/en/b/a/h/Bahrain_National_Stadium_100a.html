The Bahrain National Stadium (Arabic: إستاد البحرين الوطني; transliterated: Stād al-Bahrayn al-Watanī) is the national stadium of Bahrain, located in Isa Town. It can accommodate 30,000 people and is used mostly for football matches. It was built in 1982.
