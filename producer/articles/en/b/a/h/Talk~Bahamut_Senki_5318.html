[edit] Is this actually a Square-produced game?

I downloaded the game and I see no indication that this game was produced by Square. Neither does Square-Enix's Japanese site include any record of having produced a game for any Sega system. Where's the proof that this is a Square game?

- Tristam 10:48 p.m., 30 Jan 2006 (CST)

I have no hard evidence for it actually. I based it on all the websites that state it, including GameFAQs. Who knows, maybe one site made an assumption and several others copied it. ~ Hibana 17:13, 1 February 2006 (UTC)
Fair enough. I suppose it'd be best to leave it under Square's name for the time being. Hopefully someone can come along and clear this up. But I do believe you're right in saying that GameFAQs listed it as a Square game, and a bunch of other sites just got their info from GameFAQs. - Tristam 12:14 p.m., 2 April 2006 (CST)
"バハムート戦記 株式会社スクウェア" returns no google hits. Browsing Japanese fansites (with machine translation, so maybe I missed something) shows no evidence that Square was in any way involved.4hodmt 23:23, 13 July 2006 (UTC)
Removed mention of Square due to lack of evidence.4hodmt 11:22, 16 July 2006 (UTC)
