Bahama Village

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Bahama Village market in Key West, Florida

Bahama Village is a section of Key West, Florida southwest of downtown, in Old Town.

It covers over a 16 block area that lies southwest of Whitehead Street and northeast of Truman Annex, bordered by Whitehead, Southard, Fort and Louisa Streets.

It is a primarily black neighborhood, and is named for its many original residents who were of Bahamian ancestery.

The entrance to the village on Petronia Street has an open air flea market, shops and restaurants, and has recently become a small tourist attraction.

It contains the city of Key West's public swimming pool.
