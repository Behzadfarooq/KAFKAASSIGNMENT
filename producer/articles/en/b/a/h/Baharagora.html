Baharagora is a small town in East Singhbhum district & situated on the south-east corner of Jharkhand, India. It is located on the south-east bank of river Subarnarekha. It is 95 km away from Jamshedpur. Kolkata-Mumbai National Highway 6 passes through this place. National Highway 33 connects NH-6 at Baharagora and NH-2 at Barhi, Bihar.

Baharagora became a block in the year 1956.

Since it is located at the boarder, Orissa is 10 km and West Bengal is 15 km away from this place. Although the official language of the state is Hindi, the local language in this area is a blend of Bengali and Oriya.
