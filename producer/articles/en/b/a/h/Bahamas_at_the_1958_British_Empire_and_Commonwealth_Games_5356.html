Bahamas at the 1958 British Empire and Commonwealth Games

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

You have new messages (last change).

The Bahamas was represented at the 1958 British Empire and Commonwealth Games in Cardiff by one competitor, Tommy Robinson, who won gold in the 200 yards dash and silver in the 100 yards dash.
