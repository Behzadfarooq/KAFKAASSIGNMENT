Bahry TV was established in Kuwait. It is the only marine-thematic broadcasting channel that caters to the diverse tastes of Arabic TV viewers, as it provides exclusive marine programming of high quality.

[edit] Distribution Platform

Bahry TV broadcasts free-to-air over digital satellite transmission, able to reach the TV sets of a wide spectrum of viewers in the Middle East and North Africa.

[edit] Programming Content

Bahry TV has networked and initiated dialogues with specialized content providers from different parts of the world to provide rich quality of programming, which will help the channel in creating a competitive edge.

[edit] Bahry International Magazine

Founded in 2001 as an initiative of the Al-Nafisi brother's, the Bahry International Magazine(BIM) is the Middle East's premier Marine Arabic Magazine that covers water activities such as water sports, yachting, boating, fishing, diving and sailing, in addition to the new technology of boat engines, GPS, radars, etc.

[edit] Bahry Online

bahry.comlink title occupies a prominent position around the world, to complete the formula through its developed information services pertaining to all marine activities and from which all marine sectors can benefit. The importance of this site lies in being the single resource point for all categories of sea lovers meet and interact with each other through its forum round the clock.

[edit] The Essence of Bahry TV

The only marine TV station to be launched in MENA. Created and Catered to the passionate marine lovers.
