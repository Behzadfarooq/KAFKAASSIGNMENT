Bahrain Basketball Association

Basketball is a very popular sport in Bahrain and the Bahrain Basketball Association has the following teams:

  • Manama
  • Ahli
  • Muharraq
  • Hala
  • Najma
  • Isa Town
  • Nawaydrat
  • Bahrain
  • Sitra
