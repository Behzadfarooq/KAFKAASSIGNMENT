Bahodirjon Sooltonov

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Olympic medal record
Men's Boxing
Bronze 2004 Athens Bantamweight

Bahodirjon Sooltonov (born January 15, 1985) is an Uzbek boxer who competed in the bantamweight (54kg) division at the 2004 Summer Olympics and won the bronze medal.
