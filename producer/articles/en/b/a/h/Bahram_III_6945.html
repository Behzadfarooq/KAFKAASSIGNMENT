Bahram III

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Bahram III (d. 293) was King of Persia in 293.

He was the son of Bahram II of Persia (276–293), under whose rule he had been governing Sistan (therefore called Saganshah, Agathias iv. 24, Tabari).

Bahram III reigned only four months (in 293), and was succeeded by the pretender Narseh of Persia (293–302).
