"Bahavian Idol" is the ninth episode of the first series of Cory in the House.

[edit] Plot

Cory, Meena, and Newt get a gig at The Liberry Club, but their only reward is a small cup of juice. Cory presents Meena with a gift he believes to be from Bahavia, which turns out to be a cursed charm. As a result, Meena suffers a spate of bad luck and is frightened of performing for fear that something bad will happen to her on stage.

[edit] Trivia

  • This episode was shown on Disney Channel Video Player before it aired on television.
  • "Bahavian Idol" is a parody of American Idol.
  • It is revealed that Victor collects glowsticks.
  • The name of Cory's band is finally revealed: D.C.3
  • This is the first episode to focus solely on Cory, Newt, and Meena.
