[edit] Short autobiography

14 This Wikipedian is 14 years, 2 months, and 25 days old,
on: April 10, 2007.

I was born on the 16th of January 1993 in Columbus, Ohio to an Istanbulian family as their first daughter and second child. When we came back to Turkey, I had to learn Turkish, my mother tongue that I could not speak before. Playing the flute, athletics, dancing, reading newspapers and books are amongst my hobbies. I recently became an 8th grader. I would be delighted if I could contribute to Wikipedia more. Greetings to everybody.

Bahar (means spring in Turkish)

Please don’t hesitate to write on my talk page. I would love to respond to your comments and questions about anything I can help and would teach Turkish at any level to those who are interested.
