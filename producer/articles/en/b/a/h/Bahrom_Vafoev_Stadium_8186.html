Bahrom Vafoev Stadium

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Bahrom Vafoev Stadium is a multi-use stadium in Mubarek, Uzbekistan. It is currently used mostly for football matches and serves as the home for Mash'al Mubarek, an Uzbeki football club. The stadium holds 10,000 people.
