[edit] Uneeded Trivia

  • The part about a skunk's signature smell is irrelevant and not even really trivia. So Flower uses his skunk smell. Big whoop.
  • The "trivia" about Faline asking if Bambi remembered her is also not needed. In the original movie, Faline, Thumper, and Flower all ask Bambi if he remembers them. It's obvious that this was used in order to introduce the adult versions of the characters to the audience. If you want to take it literally, then it suggests that Bambi and all his friends have been separated for awhile. The fact is that it's not a very interesting point and shouldn't be considered actual trivia. I doubt that Walt was thinking about the 1 year gap between Faline and Bambi when he decided to introduce them that way.--Linklewtt 07:23, 12 August 2006 (UTC)

Should we mention the fictional video nasty mentioned in The Young Ones called "Bambi Goes Crazy Ape Bonkers With His Drill and Sets"? (AndrewAnorak 19:33, 8 February 2007 (UTC))

[edit] II versus 2

Any idea why sometimes this film is called Bambi II and sometimes Bambi 2? --WhiteDragon 19:35, 19 September 2006 (UTC)

It's because the two are often seen as interchangeable. Since "II" means the same as "2," a lot people use them interchangeably.--Ben 10 17:33, 24 October 2006 (UTC)
