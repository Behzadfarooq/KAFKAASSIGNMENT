[edit] Plotlines included here

Is there any good reason why the entire plots of the 'Bambi' and 'Bambi II' movies are included here in the Bambi (character) article? To avoid duplication, these should be placed in the articles for those movies, then links provided here. This article should describe the character only. I see a lot of work went into this, any comments before I start moving stuff around? --Bridgecross 17:11, 29 December 2006 (UTC)

  • Addendum; after reviewing the plotlines in the articles for the specific films 'Bambi' and 'Bambi II' I don't see any need to merge the text. The plotlines in those articles are more concise and encyclopedic, while these seem to be nearly scene-for-scene descriptions. Again, I'm going to wait for comments before reverting. --Bridgecross 17:15, 29 December 2006 (UTC)
It seems to be basic fare for character articles to include the plots of the media they were in, but in a wording that focuses on the character. Although the plot summaries in this article could use shortening. Blue Phoenix 21:11, 7 January 2007 (UTC)
It may be basic fare but it's not good Wikipedia style Wikipedia:Manual_of_Style_(writing_about_fiction). A movie page should not just reiterate the plot (Wikipedia is not an indiscriminate collection of information), and a page for just one character should especially not recap the entire plot. --Bridgecross 20:31, 9 January 2007 (UTC)
