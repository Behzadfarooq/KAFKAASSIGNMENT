Bam is one of the 45 provinces of Burkina Faso.

The capital of Bam is Kongoussi.

Departments of Bam

Bam is divided into 6 departments:

  • Bourzanga
  • Guibare
  • Kongoussi
  • Rollo
  • Sabce
  • Tikare
