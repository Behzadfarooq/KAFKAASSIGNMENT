Bamberton, British Columbia

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Cities of Vancouver Island

Bamberton is located on the Saanich Inlet, just south of Mill Bay, 45 kilometers north of Victoria on Vancouver Island. It was abandoned when the British Columbia Cement Company closed its doors in 1980. It shares its name with the nearby Bamberton Provincial Park and is named in honour of H K Bamber.
