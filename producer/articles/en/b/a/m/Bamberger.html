Bamberger as a surname may refer to:

  • George Bamberger (1923-2004), U.S. Major League Baseball pitcher
  • Louis Bamberger, founder of the Institute for Advanced Study
  • Ludwig Bamberger (1823-1899), German deputy and political economist
  • Simon Bamberger (1846–1926), U.S. politician, governor of Utah (1917–1921)
