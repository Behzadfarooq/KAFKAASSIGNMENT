[edit] The Fake Snake

At the end of episode 5: Flipping the Christmas Bird of Bam's Unholy Union, there is a package from Ryan Dunn that sparks a reaction from Bam. In the trivia section it mentions that Bam is afraid of snakes. It also mentioned that Ryan Dunn sent him a live snake. This is not true. The snake was set up in the package so when Bam opened the flap, the snake would appear to "jump out at him." The final shot clearly shows that this is a rubber toy snake. I've had to change this section twice. Kjhstuph 08:37, 25 March 2007 (UTC)
