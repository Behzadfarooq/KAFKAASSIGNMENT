Bambinos Italian Cafe

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Bambino's Patio

Bambino's Italian Cafe is a restaurant and bar at 203 Hitt Street in downtown Columbia, Missouri, USA. Bambino's opened in 1994[1] and features house pasta specialties, "Create Your Own" custom pastas, fresh salads, sub sandwiches, "Build Your Own" gourmet pizzas, homemade soups and daily lunch and dinner specials.[2]

Located near the University of Missouri campus, Bambino's is popular both as a college hangout and as a family-friendly atmosphere, famous for its big plates of pasta, ice cold schooners of beer and large outdoor patio.

Bambino's is known for the saying "Use your noodle...Eat some pasta," and ubiquitous television ads that feature no other dialogue than a repetition of young people shouting "BAM," "BINOS" and "Italian Cafe."

Bambino's offers dine-in, carry out, free delivery and catering.

Bambino's is a member of the COLORS (Columbia Locally Owned Retail & Services) organization, a business alliance promoting local independently owned businesses within the city.
