[edit] Babe Danzig

Harold Paul "Babe" Danzig was born on April 30, 1887, in Binghamton, New York. Danzig was 21 years old when he started in the major leagues. He debueted on April 12, 1909, with the Boston Red Sox. [1] Danzig only played 1-year as a first baseman. He appeared in only 6 games, all for the Red Sox. In 13 at bats he only got 2 hits; he was hit by the pitcher once and was walked twice. [2]

It is unknown why Harold quit playing baseball.
