Baby 81 is the forthcoming album by Black Rebel Motorcycle Club. It is their fourth studio album and is scheduled to be released on May 1, 2007 in the US and on April 30, 2007 in Europe. The new album will feature a more hard and raw sound juxtaposed to their previous record Howl. It is also a studio comeback for drummer Nick Jago who was unable to participate during the recordings of Howl.

The first single from the album will be Weapon of Choice.

The album title refers to Abhilasha Jeyarajah.
