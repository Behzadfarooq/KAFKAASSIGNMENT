Baba Sehgal

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Baba Sehgal AKA Harjit Singh Sehgal is an iconic Indian rapper who was famous in the mid 90's. He is credited as the first Indian rapper to jump on the indipop bandwagon, and released an album which got frequent airplay on MTV India. His famous hits include Thanda Thanda Paani (a remake of "Ice Ice Baby" by Vanilla Ice), Manjula and Dil Dhadke.

He was an engineer, but did not want to work as one and started doing what he wanted to, i.e. singing. Dil Dhadke had the distinction of being the first Indian music video ever to be shown on MTV Asia (which was broadcasting out of Hong Kong at the time).

He has released 22 albums till now, though they haven't all met with the same level of commercial success. He released 'Main bhi Madonna', Thandaa thandaa paani (1992), Dilruba(1990), Alibaba(1991) and many more albums. He almost always writes, composes and sings the songs in his albms himself. He was the music director of he movie 'Dance Party' which had a popular song "kapurthala se aaya hoon, tere liye laaya hoon, Orange kurta peela pajama.." (citation = his website (see external links)).

He made a one time acting appearance as the main lead (hero) of the Bollywood film 'Miss 420'. Before this he hosted the TV show 'superhit muqabla' which was aired on DD2 at primetime. He has also worked as a stage performer and a model

He was in New York for 4 to 5 years and when he came home to Mumbai, he released his album 'Welcome to Mumbai', which was his 22nd and latest music album

He recently directed the music for the bollywood film 'bhoot uncle'. On Hindi T.V. Serial Bigg Boss Days:Mon-Fri Time:10-11 Channel:Sony Network
