Baboucarr-Blaise Jagne

Baboucarr-Blaise Ismaila Jagne (born February 11, 1955) was the foreign minister of The Gambia from 1995 until 1997 and from August 30, 2001 until October 14, 2004. He was The Gambia's Permanent Representative to the United Nations from 1998 to 2001, and was President of the Security Council in June 1999.
