[edit] Muncie Island

Do you have a reference for the name? And since Oak Beach is on the main barrier beach (often called Jones Island) does Muncie Island only refer to the area around Oak Beach or to the entire barrier beach? I know that's a lot of questions but I can find no reference to "Muncie Island." -- Cecropia 15:06, 28 April 2006 (UTC)

Originally I believed Muncie Island to be what is presently Oak Beach on Jones Beach Island; this was after I was looking at an old map. Then when I took a second look I realized it was its own island, near Oak Island and Oak Beach (Jones Beach Island). I read three different stories but both with the similar endings, a demise to the island. One is that the island was lost after 1938 Hurricane, second was during dredging for the NY State Boat Channel, (Captree Channel) the island was buried under mud/sand 1, and lastly this island was destroyed so that its sand (resources) could be used to widen Jones Beach Island during the construction of Ocean Parkway.
