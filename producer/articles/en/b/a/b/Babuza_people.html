Babuza people

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Babuza (Chinese: 貓霧捒族; pinyin: Māowùzú; POJ: bâ-bū-cho̍k) are a Taiwanese aboriginal people, living primarily in Changhua County and around the western part of Taiwan's Central Basin.
