Babu (red panda)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

You have new messages (last change).

Babu is a red panda who disappeared from a nature centre in Moseley, Birmingham in November 2005 and spent four days "on the loose" before being discovered. Nature centre staff believe he was blown out of a tree and found himself outside his enclosure. His disappearance and the city-wide panda hunt that ensued made national headlines. After being discovered by a dog-walker in Moseley, Babu was dramatically reunited with his brother Tensing live on Midlands Today. He was voted Brummie of the Year 2005 in an unofficial poll.[citation needed]
