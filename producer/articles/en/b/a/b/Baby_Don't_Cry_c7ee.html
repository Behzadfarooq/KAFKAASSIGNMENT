"Baby Don't Cry" was the second single from the 1992 album Welcome to Wherever You Are by INXS. It was written by Andrew Farriss and Michael Hutchence

Incorporating a 60 piece orchestra, the song hit #20 on the UK charts but flopped on the U.S. charts.
