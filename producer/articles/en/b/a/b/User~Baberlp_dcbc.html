[edit] Statistics

I am 16 years old. I live in England. I have dark blonde hair and blue eyes. I am female. I am 5'10 tall. I am currently in year eleven at school and have already achieved two GCSEs (Statistics and ICT, both at grade A) from the exam season at the end of year ten, and I will be sitting exams in the remaining 11 subjects I take next May/June. I attend a non-selective girls comprehensive school. I consider myself intelligent. I am shy and introverted, but do enjoy conversing with people. I am very funny, but generally only once I get to know people. I make people and myself laugh. I have suffered from bouts of depression before. Possible future career options are journalism, author-ism, some other form of creative writing, research/statistics or writing for television. I like to write :) I am currently undecided on my A-level subjects and would appreciate help.
