Babak Payami (Persian: بابک پیامی ‎ , born 1966 in Tehran) is an Iranian film director.

[edit] Biography

Born in Iran, Payami spent nine years in Afghanistan, where his father worked for an oil company. He later moved to Canada, where he took cinema studies classes at the University of Toronto and became a citizen. In 1998 her returned to Iran to create his debut film One More Day. His best known film is Secret Ballot.

[edit] Filmography

  • Silence between Two thoughts (2003)
  • Secret Ballot (2001)
  • One More Day (1999)

[edit] Awards and Honors

  • FIPRESCI Prize, Tromso Film Festival, 2003
  • Best Feature (Secret Ballot), Newport Film Festival, 2002
  • Best Director, Venice Film Festival, 2001
  • OCIC Award (Secret Ballot), Venice, 2001
  • Pasinetti Award (Secret Ballot), Venice, 2001
  • UNICEF Award (Secret Ballot), Venice, 2001
  • NETPAC Award (Secret Ballot), Venice, 2001
  • Best Director, Valladolid Film Festival, 2001
  • FIPRESCI Prize, Special Mention, London Film Festival, 2001.
  • Best Artistic Contribution, Tokyo Film Festival, 2000
  • Special Jury Award (One More Day), Torino Film Festival, 2000
