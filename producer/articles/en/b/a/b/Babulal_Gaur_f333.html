Babulal Gaur

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Babulal Gaur (born June 2, 1930) was a Chief Minister of Madhya Pradesh, India. He was born in a yadava family in a village in Uttar Pradesh. He served as chief minister from August 23, 2004 following the resignation of Uma Bharti. He stepped down in November 2005 after Shivraj Singh Chauhan was elected the Chief Ministers by the MLAs. Gaur is a member of the BJP.
