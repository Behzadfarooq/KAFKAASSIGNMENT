Zvolen District in the Banská Bystrica region

Babiná (German: Frauenstuhl; Hungarian: Bábaszék) is a village and municipality of the Zvolen District in the Banská Bystrica Region of Slovakia

[edit] History

In historical records, the village was first mentioned in 1254. In 1270 German colonists settled here. It belonged to Dobrá NIva. in the 16th century it had to pay tributes to Turks. In the 17th century, it belonged to Esterházy family.
