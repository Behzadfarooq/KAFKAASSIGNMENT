BabyFirstTV

BabyFirstTV is the first cable and satellite channel in the US dedicated to providing innovative programming designed to inspire babies' learning in a delightful and engaging way.

BabyFirst TV is available on Dish Network Channel 126 and DirecTV Channel 293. the standard subscription price is $9.99 a month for the channel. It launched in Summer 2006.

This channel has received criticism from some parents, who believe that babies should not be exposed to television, and should instead learn through social interaction.[citation needed]

In early 2007 the channel launched in the UK on Sky channel 627 (along with Baby TV on EPG 626)and was free in the first few weeks but is now a subscription channel.
