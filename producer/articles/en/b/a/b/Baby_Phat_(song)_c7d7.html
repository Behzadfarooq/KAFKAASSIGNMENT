"Baby Phat" is the first and only official single from De La Soul's sixth studio album, AOI: Bionix, released in late 2001. The song was produced by Dave West and featured vocals from Devin the Dude and Yummy Bingham. This single was the introduction for Yummy Bingham, who was simultaneously establishing herself as De La Soul's appointed vocalist and a member of the short lived R&B group Tha Rayne.

The song is an ode to "plus size" women, as Devin sings "Don't get stuck on the things they say, now you know it's a nasty world / Tryin' to get with ya anyway cause I know you're a nasty girl / We ain't never gon' discriminate so let me compliment your size"

The B-Side of the 12" single is "Watch Out", a lyrical trunk rattler, also produced by Dave west and featuring samples from "Mood for Milt", and "Cubano Chant" by Latin musician Cal Tjader.

The single was released as a 12" vinyl single in the United States on November 6th, 2001. The CD single is a UK edition, released on February 18, 2002 and reached number 55 in UK Singles chart.
