Elbert "Babe" Stapp (February 26, 1904 San Antonio, Texas - September 17, 1980 Indianapolis, Indiana) was an American racecar driver active in the 1920's and 1930's.

[edit] Career award

  • He was inducted in the National Sprint Car Hall of Fame in 1994.
