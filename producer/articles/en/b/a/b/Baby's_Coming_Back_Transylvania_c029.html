"Baby's Coming Back" is the first single from McFly's fourth studio album (as yet untitled and due to be released in August[citation needed]) and is due to be released in May 2007. The band had indicated previously that they were going to release "Transylvania" as the fourth single from their third studio album, Motion in the Ocean, but on March 20, 2007, they announced that the new single would be "Baby's Coming Back".[1] The single will include "Transylvania". "Transylvania" was due to be released on February 26, 2007, but the release date was moved, first to April and then to May, because McFly had been asked to return to Africa with Comic Relief.[2]


On Saturday, January 27, 2007, McFly performed a world exclusive premiere of "Transylvania" on television, live on the BBC show TMi.

[edit] Transylvania music video

Their official site reported in January that the video "will see all the boys dressed up as girls in a gothic castle, with nasty villains, damsels in distress, and over the top shenanigans". The silent-movie-style black and white video, shot in late January 2007 and viewable on YouTube as of February 3, features the boys dressed up in costumes from a variety of mismatched time periods.
