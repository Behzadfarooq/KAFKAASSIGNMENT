Babylon is a Long Island Rail Road train station in the village of Babylon, New York at Railroad Avenue west of Deer Park Avenue. It lies along the Montauk Branch, and is the eastern terminus of the Babylon Branch service. To the west is the junction with the Central Branch, which heads northwest to the Main Line at Bethpage. The station is elevated with two island platforms and is wheelchair accessible through elevator access. The electrified portion of the Montauk Branch ends east of the station.

The station is also an intermodal transportation hub with connections to several bus lines. Suffolk County Transit buses S20, S25, S27, S40, S42, and S47, and MTA Long Island Bus buses N19 and N72 terminate at the station. Suffolk County Transit bus S29 also serves the station but does not terminate there.
