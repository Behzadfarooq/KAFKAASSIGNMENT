"Baby Sister" is a single by American singer La Toya Jackson, taken from her fourth album Imagination. The single was released in a 12" format in Japan, where it failed to chart. The other singles from the Imagination album also failed to make much of an impact on the charts. The single includes an instrumental mix of "Baby Sister".
