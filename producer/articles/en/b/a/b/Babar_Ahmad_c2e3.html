Babar Ahmad (b. 1974) is a prisoner currently held in the United Kingdom pending an appeal against extradition to the United States where he faces terrorism-related charges.

Ahmad was born in London, the son of a British Civil Servant who immigrated from Pakistan in 1962. He studied for a degree in engineering at Imperial College London and after graduation got a job in IT Support at the College.
