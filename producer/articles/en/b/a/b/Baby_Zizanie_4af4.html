Baby Zizanie

Baby Zizanie is musical collaboration between J. G. Thirlwell (Foetus) and Jim Coleman (ex-Cop Shoot Cop) featuring both performers creating music on laptop computers. Primarily a live project, the duo's music is loosely composed and allows for improvisation when performed.

Baby Zizanie has released one recording to date, 2003's Thalassaphobia.
