BABair is the wikipedia login for Bettina Bair, a senior lecturer on the faculty of the Computer Science and Engineering Department at the Ohio State University.

[edit] Affiliations

• Association for Computing Machinery Committee on Women (ACM-W) - Working Committee 2003-present

• ACM Special Interest Group Computer Science Education (SIGCSE), 2001-present

• The Women In Computer Engineering (TWICE), Director, 2003-present

• Department of Computer Science & Engineering Undergraduate Diversity co-Coordinator, 2002-present

• SIGCSE Committee – Expanding the Women in Computing Community, 2004-present

• ACM-W Student Chapter Faculty sponsor, 2003-present

• Training and Leadership Council (TLC), Board of Directors, 2004-present

• Ohio Celebration of Women In Computing Conference, General Chair, 2005

• Journal of Educational Resources in Computing (JERIC), Special Issue co-editor, April 2005
