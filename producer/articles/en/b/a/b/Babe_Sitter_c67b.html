"Babe Sitter" is an episode of Dexter's Laboratory. It aired as part of a special trilogy of episodes devoted to the character of Dexter's arch enemy Mandark (the other two were Mountain Mandark and 2Geniuses 2Gether 4Ever), though it has also aired separately in reruns

Spoiler warning: Plot and/or ending details follow.

Mandark's parents go out for the night, leaving him to the mercy of his baby sitter, Dee-Dee. Mandark sees this as an opportunity to impress her, but Dee-Dee is eager to stick to her responsibilities and subjects Mandark to a stressful night, with one problem emerging after another, from lab compromising, to rigorous bath time, will Mandark ever get his kiss from Dee-Dee?

[edit] Notes

  • Mandark reenacts scenes from the Star Wars movies with dolls, playing the role of hero rescuing the fair "Princess Leia", represented by Dee-Dee
  • Dee-Dee does little damage to Mandark's lab this time, a far different outcome occurred in the conclusion of "Dexter's Rival"
  • This marks the first time Dee-Dee has ever kissed Mandark willingly, even if the kiss is a requirement on her "to do" list of babysitting duties. The kiss is surprisingly straight forward, indicating Dee-Dee may have feelings towards Mandark herself.
  • this Episode's Theme song is titled "Mandark's Laboratory" as all 3 segments are about mandark. dexter gets his own back in "2geniuses 2gether 4ever"
