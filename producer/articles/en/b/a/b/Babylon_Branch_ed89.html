Babylon Branch

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Babylon Branch is a fully-electrified rail service operated by the Long Island Rail Road in the U.S. state of New York. LIRR maps and schedules indicate that the Babylon Branch is the portion of the Montauk Branch from Valley Stream east to Babylon. Its west end is at the junction between the West Hempstead Branch and Montauk Branch (Valley Interlocking); the Montauk Branch from Valley Stream west to Jamaica is part of the West Hempstead Branch service. At the east end of the Babylon Branch, trains that continue are known as Montauk Branch trains; several of these use the Central Branch between a junction west of Babylon station and the Main Line at Bethpage, running to New York via the Main Line.[1][2]

The Babylon Branch portion of the Montauk Branch is fully grade separated with bridges over almost all intersecting roadways.
