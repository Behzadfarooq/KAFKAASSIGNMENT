Babicz Guitars is a manufacturer of high-end acoustic and electric guitars and a producer of innovative and licensable guitar technology. Babicz guitars is most well known for itâ€™s first 3 patented technologies, the Continually Adjustable Neck TM, Lateral String Compression Soundboard TM, and the Torque Reducing Split Bridge TM. In addition to selling guitars, Babicz Guitars licenses these technologies to other guitar manufacturers.

The Babicz Guitars offices are in Poughkeepsie, NY, ninety miles north of Manhattan. The company is a member of the Poughkeepsie Area Chamber of Commerce, and is also an EDZ (Economic Development Zone) Certified business for the Dutchess County Economic Development Corporation. In addition, Babicz Guitars is a member of NAMM (National Association of Music Merchants).
