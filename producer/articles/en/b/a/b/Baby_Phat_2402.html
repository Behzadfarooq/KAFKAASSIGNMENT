Baby Phat is an urban fashion line of clothing and other items for women and girls designed by Kimora Lee Simmons. Besides clothing, the brand also includes accessories for phones, shoes and clothes. It is the corporate "sibling" of the clothing brand Phat Farm. Baby Phat uses a sleek cat as the brand logo.


Since baby Phat first stepped on the scene 9 years ago, it has made an impact on young women around the world. The Baby Phat feline logo has become internationally recognizable; the symbol is synonymous with the brand.

[edit] Controversy

Baby Phat has come under fire many times for its use of fur and fur trim. Creative Director Kimora Lee Simmons has refused to cease using fur in the line.
