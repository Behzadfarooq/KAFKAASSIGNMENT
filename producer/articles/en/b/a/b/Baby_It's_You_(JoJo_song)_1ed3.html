"Baby It's You" is the second single from JoJo's debut album, JoJo. Released in 2004, the single (which features rapper Bow Wow) reached number twenty-two on the Billboard Hot 100. It also peaked at number eight in the UK.

[edit] Song information

The song was co-written by Antonio Dixon, Damon Thomas, Eric Dawkins, and Harvey Mason and produced by The Underdogs. This version of the song is the one released to radio airplay and as a commercial single (which can also be found on the UK edition of her album), but the original version, called "album version", does not feature Bow Wow.
