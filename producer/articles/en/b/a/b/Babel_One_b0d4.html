"Babel One" is the title of a Star Trek: Enterprise television episode from season four.

Tellarite ambassador Gral in a heated exchange with Archer.
Spoiler warning: Plot and/or ending details follow.

Plot Details: The Romulans are using a telepathically controlled ship capable of taking on the image of any other ship to attack assets of different worlds in an attempt to destabilize the sector.

[edit] Trivia

The title is a reference to the original series episode Journey To Babel.
