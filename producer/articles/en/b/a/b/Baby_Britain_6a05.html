"Baby Britain" is a song written by Elliott Smith for his fourth studio album XO, released in 1998. The song was also released as a single in 1999.

A remix version of "Baby Britain" was released on the Baby Britain promo CD.

A short instrumental version of the song appears in the short film Lucky 3, which was directed by Jem Cohen.

[edit] Music video

A music video, directed by Steve Hanft, was made for the song. In the video Smith can be seen in a recording studio playing various instruments, including a guitar, piano and drums. There is also footage of Smith playing live.
