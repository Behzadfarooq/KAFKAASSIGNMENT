Babygrande Records is an independent hip-hop record label based in New York City. Some of the artists included on this label are Purple City, Jedi Mind Tricks, Army of the Pharaohs, 7L & Esoteric, and Jean Grae. Other artists, such as Canibus, Immortal Technique, and GZA have done or are planning to do joint projects with Babygrande.

Babygrande Records is distributed by Koch Entertainment, the leading indie distributor in North America.

Babygrande is represented by the Creative Artists Agency in Hollywood. Its founder is none other than Babygrande Guy Chuck Wilson, who also happened to co-write the screenplay for Soul Plane. Jesse Stone, formerly of Interscope Records, is Babygrande's vice president and general manager.
