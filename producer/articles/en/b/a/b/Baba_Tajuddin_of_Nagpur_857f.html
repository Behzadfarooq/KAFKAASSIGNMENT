Baba Tajuddin of Nagpur

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

You have new messages (last change).

Hazrat Tajjudinn Baba, of Nagpur, India was a Sufi Muslim. He studied under another Sufi master who is said to have given the Sufi movement its name. He had a habit of calling everyone Allah Saheb, which means Allah is present in every thing.
