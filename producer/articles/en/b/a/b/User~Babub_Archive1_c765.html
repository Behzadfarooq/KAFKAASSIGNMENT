I am Babub. I am a Smārta Brāhmaṇa. I will be mostly concentrating on the topics:

  • The tradition of Advaita Vedanta, including its teachers
  • Karma-kanda of the Vedas (popularly called rituals)
  • History of Hinduism
  • Topics on India

[edit] Favourite quotes

  • Anṛtāt satyamupaimi mānuṣāddaivyamupaimi daivīm vācaṃ yacchāmi —Taittirīya Brāhmaṇaṃ 1.2
  • Karmaṇya eva adhikāraste mā phaleṣu kadācana| Mā karmaphala heturbhuḥ ma te sango astv'karmaṇi|| —Śrīkṛṣṇa, Bhagavadgīta
  • Durlabhaṃ evaitaddevānugrahahetukam| Manuṣyatvaṃ mumukṣutvaṃ mahāpuruṣasaṃśrayaḥ|| —Ādi Śaṅkarācārya, Vivekachūḍāmaṇi
  • Bhagavadgīta kiñcitadhīta gaṅgājala lavakaṇikā pīta| Sakṛdapi ena Murāri samarca kriyate tena yamena na carcā||—Ādi Śaṅkarācārya, Mohamudgara (Bhajagovindaṃ)
