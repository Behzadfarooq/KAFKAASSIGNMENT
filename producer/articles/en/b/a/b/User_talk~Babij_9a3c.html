[edit] Howdy

Hi Babij. I noticed you were from South Australia, so I thought I'd let you know about some resources on Wikipedia. Be sure to check out Australian resources, like The Australia Portal, Australian Wikipedians' Notice Board, Australian Collaboration of the Fortnight, New Australian Articles and Australian stub articles.
