This is category is part of the test project Babylon (see User:Jmfayard) related to Wikipedia:Babel.

This is a list of wikipedians who are willing to translate articles from Spanish.

You can see their language skills either in their home page or in Category:User es or its subcategorys es-1,es-2,es-3,es-4,es-N

If you want to request a translation from Spanish, just add your request at the top of User:Jmfayard/Babylon/Request/es
