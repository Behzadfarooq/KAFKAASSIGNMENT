Babes in the Wood was a British sitcom starring Karl Howman and Denise van Outen. It aired for two series from 1998 to 1999. Babes in the Wood was written by Geoff Deane, Paul Alexander, Simon Braithwaite, Ian Searle and Fleur Costello. It is not currently available on DVD.

[edit] Plot

Babes in the Wood revolved around three women in their 20s who share a flat in St John's Wood in London. Leigh is streetwise and tough, while Ruth is bossy and Caralyn is bossy. In the flat next door lives Charlie Lovall, the former owner of a video shop whose divorce has cost him dear, although he still has his Porsche. Leigh works as a waitress for public school-eductaed Benito at the nearby Bar Coda. Ruth married an old millionaire and left after the first series and was replaced by would be model Frankie.
