A bit about me...

I'm a qualified Fellow of the Institute of Legal Executives (F.ILEX) with experience in Conveyancing and Product liability. I have worked at Young & Pearce Solicitors and at Freeth Cartwright LLP, both of which are in Nottingham. I have also lectured for the Open University on their LL.B courses.

I trained with the OU and the Institute of Legal Executives. I hold a first in law (LLB) and several single subjects enabling me to practice in civil litigation, company law and property. I also hold a Diploma in English Law. I have qualifications in philosophy and criminology.

I have three children - Holly Amber, Jodie Ann and Courtney Jade. I live in the East Midlands in Nottingham near Newstead Abbey.

I'm currently working on an awesome project called STUDENT JAM - which is probably going to be the hottest social networking site in the UK.
