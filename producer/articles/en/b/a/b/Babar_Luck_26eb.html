Babar Luck

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Babar Luck is the former bassist of UK skacore band King Prawn. However he has also had notable success as a solo artist and with the collection of UK musicians Suicide Bid. Babar is notable for being one of the most colourful and eccentric members of the UK underground ska punk movement, instantly recognisable with his trademark skin head and beard.

His music often includes anecdotes about people he has known and his own experiences, strong political messages and 'pro-love' spiritual themes.
