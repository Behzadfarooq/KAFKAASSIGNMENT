Baby Cakes

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

"Baby Cakes"
Single by 3 Of A Kind
Released 2004
Format CD Single
Genre UK Garage
Label Relentless

"Baby Cakes" was the only hit single for the garage trio 3 Of A Kind. It reached number one in the UK singles chart. A follow up "Wink One Eye" was recorded but was never released.
