Hello, My name is Ryan Babij and I live in South Australia. I'm 16 years old, and I enjoy contributing to Wikipedia because... Well I suppose I enjoy helping other people in any way I can, and helping to produce an encyclopedia which anyone with an internet connection can access is a pretty good way of helping out in this world.

I spend about 75% of my life on the computer and like hang around at GameFAQs. But I enjoy it. As long as I enjoy it, it's all good right? Right!?

Anyway, I've been studying Chinese for four years, but I'm still kinda crap at it:

我爱中国因为美丽

Wo ai Zhongguo yinwei mei li.

^ Yep, that's Chinese. It means something like 'I like China because it is beautiful'. I haven't been there, but the photos look nice...

BTW, my name in Chinese is apparently 里安 or lian. 'An' means calm or peaceful... I suppose that is kinda true at school, but I guess I don't live up to my name at home, hehe...

- Did a spoken article on Australia (I just know my friends are gonna make fun of me for this) :-)
