Baby Mammoth is an English downtempo duo consisting of Mark Blissenden and Andrew Burdall.

Formed roughly in 1994 in Hull, England. They signed with Pork Recordings in 1995. They recorded 8 albums with Pork Recordings before signing on to Ecco. Chamber in 2002
