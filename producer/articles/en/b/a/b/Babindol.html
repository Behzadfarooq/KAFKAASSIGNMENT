Babindol is a village and municipality in the Nitra District in western centralSlovakia, in the Nitra Region.

[edit] History

In historical records the village was first mentioned in 1271.

[edit] Geography

The village lies at an altitude of 175 metres and covers an area of 5.414 km². It has a population of about 650 people.

[edit] Ethnicity

The village is approximately 69% Slovak and 31% Magyar.

[edit] Facilities

The village has a public library and football pitch.
