[edit] Obafemi Martins

You have replaced: "Martins is known for his pace, powerful shots and spectacular celebrations.

Although relatively short, Martins has a good leap and the Nigerian based newspaper 'The Daily Sun' reported that he used to be very good at the long jump and high jump.[1]"

With:

"Martins is known for his powerful shots, acrobatic celebrations and also his speed. He can reportedly run 100 meters in less than 10.5 seconds (Olympic quality). His speed usually enables him to get behind defenders and create one-on-one chances with the goalkeeper. He is also very good at the long jump and high jump"

Twice now, I just want to tell you why I have reverted it back. The main problem is that this part of the article (as I put on the talk page) pre-dates the Daily-Sun's article and if you compare the two complete articles you'll find that huge parts have been lifted, presumably from the wiki text as it pre-dates the other. So using the newspaper for a source is a little problematic and I can't find anything else that confirms it, fair enough if you can. The other problem is that the newspaper only says it is rumoured that he used to be able to run 100m in under 11 seconds. Not that he can run it now in under 10.5 seconds. We can't put everything in that has just been rumoured in a Nigerian tabloid paper otherwise wiki would fill up with nonsense. Same thing goes for the long and high jump which he used to be good at.Stev17 14:53, 14 February 2007 (UTC)
