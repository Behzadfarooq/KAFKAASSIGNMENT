Babel is the name used in the Hebrew Bible for the city of Babylon.

Babel may refer to:

In literature:

  • Babel (newspaper), Iraqi newspaper
  • Babel-17, science fiction novel by Samuel R. Delany in which the Sapir-Whorf Hypothesis is strongly influential
  • Isaac Babel, a Soviet journalist, playwright, and short story writer.

In Star Trek:

  • "Babel" (DS9 episode), first season episode of Star Trek: Deep Space Nine
  • Babel (planet), neutrally aligned planet in the fictional Star Trek universe

In other fields:

  • Babel (film), a film directed by Alejandro González Iñárritu, starring Brad Pitt.
  • Babel Label, record label
  • Babel, a Turbo CD RPG released by Nippon Telenet
