Babol University of Medical Sciences (BUMS), is a medical sciences university in the city of Babol, Mazandaran province, Persia (Iran).

It was founded originally as a school for laboratory technicians in 1962, and was elevated to university status in 1985.

Currently there are more than 2300 students in all faculties. The university operates 6 hospitals.

[edit] Current schools

  • medical school (founded in 1992),
  • nursing school
  • dental school (founded in 1993),
  • midwifery school
  • paramedical school (founded in 1962)

The Dental Faculty is one of only two schools in Iran that trains students for Dental Nursing Associate degrees.
