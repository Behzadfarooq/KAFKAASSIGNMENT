Hazarath Syed Shah Baba Hyder Auliya Hussaini Saharwardy (Rehmatullah Aliah) is a 12th century Sufi saint of Saharwardy order.He was the disciple of Hazarath Tabr-e-Aalam Baadshah Nathar Vali (Rehmatullah Alaih), of Tirchy. His mausoleum is located in Mulbagal, Kolar District, Karnataka.

Hazarath Baba Hyder Safdar vali, was originally King Syed Simali, King of Sabhunat, Iran. After renouncing the world, he was inducted into Sarwardy Sufi order by his spiritual master, Hazarth Nathar Wali of Tirchy.

When Baba Hyder Safdar came to India it was the regime of Gayasuddin Balaban in Delhi and Halebeedu Hoysala in Southern India. He settled in Mulbagal along with 300 qalandars and his companions, Hazrath Haji Makki and Hazrath Syed Baba Fatah Allah, and began to spread the word of the Saharwardy Order.

Baba died in 1269 (11th of Rajab, 668 Hijri).
