Babsk is a village in Central Poland, located between Rawa Mazowiecka and Mszczonów, in Gmina Biała Rawska within Rawa Mazowiecka County and the Łódź Voivodeship.

Population: 700 inhabitants (as of 2004).

The village was founded probably in the XIV century
