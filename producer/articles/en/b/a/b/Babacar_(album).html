Babacar is the first album released by the British rock band Babacar in 1998. The album only received mediocre reviews and was a commercial failure. The band dissolved when Shelleyan Orphan reunited shortly after the release of Babacar's next album Bartolo.

[edit] Personnel

  • Caroline Crawley - vocals
  • Bruno Ellingham - violin
  • Tristan Powell - e-bow
  • Roberto Soave - bass guitar
  • Rob Steen - guitar
  • Porl Thompson - guitar, banjo
  • Boris Williams - drums
