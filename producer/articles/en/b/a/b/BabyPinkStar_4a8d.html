BabyPinkStar are an Electro Glam Punk band from Sheffield, UK formed in 2004.

  • Bleepy (Synths)

[edit] History

BabyPinkStar originally formed in September 2004 with members Tom, Ben, Alice, and Bleepy.

Breezie, founder member of The Dodgems, joined the band in January 2006 to replace Bleepy the drum machine on drums.
