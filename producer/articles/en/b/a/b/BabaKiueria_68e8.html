BabaKiueria is a 1986 Australian satirical film on relations between Indigenous Australians and Australians of European descent. The plot revolves around a role-reversal, whereby it is the Indigenous Australians who have invaded the land of stereotypical European Australians - the fictitious country of BabaKiueria (a transliteration of "Barbaque Area").
