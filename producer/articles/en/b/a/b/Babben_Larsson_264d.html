Barbro 'Babben' Larsson (b. October 29, 1956) is a Swedish actress and comedian.

Larsson was born in Gotland and is recognised by her pronounced dialect. Now living in Sundbyberg with her husband and daughter, she attended the National Academy of Dramatic Art in Stockholm in 1980. She has been active in the S.U.C.K. (Stand Up Comedy Club) since 1988 and comedian group R.E.A. (Roligt Elakt Aktuellt). She has also appeared in the TV show Parlamentet.

In 2006 she was voted the most popular female comedian in Sweden in the popular TV-programme Folktoppen.
