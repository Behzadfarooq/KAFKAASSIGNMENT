Babase Island

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Feni Islands seen from space. Ambitle (larger) and Babase (smaller).

Babase Island is an island of the Feni Islands in Papua New Guinea, located east of New Ireland, at 4.0166667° S 153.7° ECoordinates: 4.0166667° S 153.7° E. It consists of a stratovolcano and a lava dome, joined by an isthmus.
