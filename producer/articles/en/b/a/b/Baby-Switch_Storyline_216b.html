The baby-switch storyline was a story arc on the soap operas All My Children and One Life to Live. It involved the fictional characters Babe Carey, Bianca Montgomery, JR Chandler, Paul Cramer, Kelly Cramer, Kevin Buchanan, and the rest of the characters on All My Children and One Life to Live.
