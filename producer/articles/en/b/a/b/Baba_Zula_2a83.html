Baba Zula is a Turkish musical group based in Istanbul.

[edit] Musical style

Baba Zula creates music through an amalgamation of natural field recordings coupled with traditional and modern acoustic and electronic instruments.

Mixed and mastered by British Guyanan dub producer Mad Professor, the "oriental–dub" album, Psychebelly Dance Music, incorporates folk tunes, gazel, taksim and bozlak with a contemporary approach and fuses them with universal concepts such as sample, dub and groove.

With their third album, Duble Oryantal again mixed and mastered by Mad Professor, Baba Zula have further developed the genre they have named oriental dub.
