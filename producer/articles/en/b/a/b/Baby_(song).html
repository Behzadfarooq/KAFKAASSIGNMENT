Baby is a song performed by Fabolous and Mike Shorey, taken from Fabolous' 2004 album Real Talk. The song reached #17 in the Hot Rap Tracks chart and #22 in the Hot R&B/Hip-Hop Singles & Tracks chart, although only managed #71 in the Billboard 100. The song failed to make a big impact on the UK Chart, where it missed out on the top 40 following its release.
