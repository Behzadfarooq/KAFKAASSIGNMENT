Charalambos "Babis" Stefanidis (born 8 March 1981) is a Swedish football player of Greek heritage, who is currently representing Helsingborgs IF. Prior to joining the Scanians, he represented Danish giants Brøndby IF and he has twice won the Allsvenskan championship with Djurgårdens IF. He started his career at IF Brommapojkarna and has also represented the Greek team Iraklis. Babis is an offensive midfielder, who normally plays on the right wing.
