Baby, Don't Do It is the 24th episode of Popular, and was written by Adam Horowitz and Edward Kitsis.

In this episode, Ms.Glass makes the class have a project to treat baby dolls like their are the students real children. Lily and Carmen are the only ones who don't have a doll and have to share one. They decide to name the baby Hilaryswank. Brooke doesn't have time with her baby after she juggles with her mother, Josh, and father. Mary Cherry and Nicole become role models to Emroy Dick's twin sisters Em and Em and leave their babies to a British nanny who they discover abuses their children.

Sam helps her mother and Mike's relationship and Brooke decides on not moving in with her mother.
