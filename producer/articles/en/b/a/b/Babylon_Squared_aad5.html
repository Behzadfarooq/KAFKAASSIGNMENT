"Babylon Squared" is an episode from the first season of the science-fiction television series Babylon 5.

[edit] Plot synopsis

Spoiler warning: Plot and/or ending details follow.

Babylon 4 reappears four years after it first vanished, and is calling for help.

[edit] Arc significance

Another perspective behind the events of "Babylon Squared" is seen in the third season episode "War Without End."

  • Babylon 4 was stolen by people from the past. It seems Sinclair was somehow behind this. It was stolen to act as a base of operations in a past war being fought between forces of light and darkness.
  • Introduces the character of Zathras.
  • Introduces the concept of "the One".

[edit] Trivia

  • The Babylon 4 station is larger than Babylon 5. B4 also has engines which are capable of moving it through space if necessary.
