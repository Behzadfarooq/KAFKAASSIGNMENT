Baby Come On, is the second US single released by the band (+44), as confirmed by Mark on his website. On October 31st, the acoustic version of the song was released on iTunes. So far. it has gotten very little airplay.

[edit] Meaning of the Song

In the December issue of Alternative Press, Mark Hoppus described the song as "A good indication of what (+44) is about: It's got rock guitar, amazing drum stuff and some electronic elements. I love the line that says, 'The past is only the future with the lights on.'"
