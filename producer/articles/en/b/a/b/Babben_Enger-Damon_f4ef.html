Babben Enger-Damon

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Olympic medal record
Women's cross-country skiing
Gold 1968 Grenoble 3 x 5 km

Babben Enger-Damon (born September 19, 1939) is a former Norwegian cross-country skier who competed during the 1960's. She won a gold medal in the 3 x 5 km relay at the 1968 Winter Olympics in Grenoble.
