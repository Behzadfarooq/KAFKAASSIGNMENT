Baby Cow Productions

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Baby Cow Productions Ltd is a UK comedy television production company established in 1999 by Steve Coogan and Henry Normal. It has since diversified into radio, animation and film.

The company's name is derived from Coogan's characters Paul and Pauline Calf.
