Babek (Babək) is a rayon of Azerbaijan in the Nakhichevan Autonomous Republic. Formally known as Nakhichevan, it is named for Babak Khorramdin who led an 20 year long uprising against the Arabs in present-day Iran, Nakhichevan and Azerbaijan.
