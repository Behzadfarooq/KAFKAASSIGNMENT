[edit] Alignment in the Sonic universe

I'm looking at updating the Sonic template - should the Babylon Rogues be categorized neutral or villains? I'm leaning towards keeping them neutral. Alex 21:52, 24 February 2006 (UTC)

Neutral, AKA, anti-heroes. That's where they should be put. The rogues are thieves, yes, but they still have common enemies with some heroes. As stated in both Jet's theme song, and by Jet himself in the game, they don't want advanced technology or world domination--just treasure. Rouge is kind of like this, but she wants JEWELS. And she's also a government spy. Also, she more treasure 'hunter' than jewel thief. The Rogues are straight-out thieves, and will take any real treasure--namely, jewels, gems, gold, silver, money, etc. --Ultimate 21:58, 4 March 2006 (UTC)

It confused me at first, also, as I thought it meant that something was 'stolen,' but it's confirmed that Stolen was the 13th leader of the Babylon Rogues. He's probably also the one who developed the first Extreme Gear. The Extreme Gear allowed them to 'fly' without having to struggle to do so. My guess is that Wave is younger, thinner, and smaller, and she can fly a lot easier than Jet and Storm. These are unusual birds, you know. They are much larger than ordinary ones. Oh, and it's found in the note that Eggman reads after they beat the Babylon Guardian, and claim the treasure of Babylon. --Ultimate 21:50, 4 March 2006 (UTC)

No, Storm's voice actor is NOT Ted Elliot. IMDB is not a very reliable source as once in Shadow the Hedgehog, they said David Humphrey and Ryan Drummond would be returning to play Sonic and Shadow, even though they ended up being played by Jason Griffith instead. Plus, do you see Elliot's name in the credits? It's pretty obvious he's played by Madeleine Blaustein.Shadoman

And yet, people still keep on posting in the article that Storm is voiced by Dan Green. I'm changing it. --Ultima 20:16, 22 August 2006 (UTC)
He IS played by Dan Green. Notice that Maddie Blaustien's name is at the bottom of the credits with the likes of Rouge (Kathleen Delaney) and Cream (Rebecca Honig)? Blaustien plays the two Egg-Robots, as well as the final boss (the giant Babylonian Genie). I DO know that Maddie Blaustien can play deep-sounding characters, but if that was the case for Storm, the character would sound more like Lt. Surge or Solomon Mutou. I think the point was that Jet has the same VA as Sonic, and Storm has the same VA as Knuckles. Amy Palant played Omochao in addition to Tails, so that was her second job as opposed to playing Wave. If Wikipedia's policy for confirming voice actors for characters in 4Kids related production is just 'guessing' (which it seems to be, check out some of the One Piece character pages), I think that's enough confirmation.

[edit] Sonic Riders 2

Aside from a mention in EuroGamer, there is no evidence Sonic Riders 2 is even in development; it's quite possible that plans for such a game have been shelved, and we havn't heard anything about the game since that interview (which took place before Sonic Riders itself was released at the beginning of this year). Although it would be likely that the Rogues would appear in such a game, there is nothing official that says so, and adding the rumours about the Rogues appearing in it is pure fan speculation. As such, I don't think the article should even mention SR2, until there is more info. -- VederJuda 23:07, 16 November 2006 (UTC)
