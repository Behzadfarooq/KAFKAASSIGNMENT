Babs Kramer, Mother of Cosmo Kramer.

Babs Kramer is a fictional character on the US television sitcom Seinfeld (1989–1998).

Babs Kramer is the mother of Cosmo Kramer.

In the past, Babs was a matron in the women's restroom at a restaurant, but was convinced by Kramer to resign from that job and pursue an undefined venture with him. She was featured in a sixth-season episode ("The Switch") where she also revealed Kramer's first name to be Cosmo and was seen to have relations with Newman.

In "The Switch," Babs commented "I've been clean for two years!", implying that she once had a drug addiction problem.

Babs Kramer was played by actress Sheree North (1932-2005).
