Baby Calendar

Baby Calendar is a three- piece indie pop band from Miami, FL on Happy Happy Birthday To Me Records, an indie label based in Athens, GA known for releasing mostly indie_pop and twee-pop bands and for working with bands such as Of Montreal, Casper And The Cookies, and Bunnygrunt. The band formed in 2004 in Miami and are currently active and performing frequently in and around the United States.

The band has scheduled and performed hundreds of tour dates all across USA, including spots at Plan-It-X Fest (2005, 2006), the HHBTM Athens Popfest (2006), and the CMJ Music Marathon in NYC (2006). Festival appearances and tour dates are a regular staple for the band.

[edit] Members

Tom Gorrio - Vocals, Guitar, Piano, Bass, Drums

Jaquelinne Biver - Vocals, Bass guitar, Keyboard

Arik Dayan - Drums, Xylophone
