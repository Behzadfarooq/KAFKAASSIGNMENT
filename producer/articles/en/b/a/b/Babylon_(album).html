Babylon is the 2002 debut album by Welsh Ragga-Metal act Skindred. It has since been re-issued several times.

[edit] RCA / BMG Release (2002)

The original 2002 release of Babylon hit the shops on 2002/07/03. Reportedly selling around 50,000 copies before BMG dropped Skindred. [1]

[edit] Track listing

  1. "Set It Off"
  2. "Kiss And Make Up"
  3. "Pressure"
  4. "Sicker"
  5. "Selection"
  6. "Babylon"
  7. "The Fear"
  8. "Bruises"
  9. "Together"
  10. "World Domination"
  11. "Nobody"

[edit] Limited Edition

A limited edition version with two extra tracks was also released by RCA / BMG. It contained the same listing as above to the following tracks added.

    12. "Firing The Love"
    13. "Falling Down"

[edit] Bieler Bros. Re-Issue (2003)

Skindred were picked up by Bieler Bros. They re-issued Babylon (with a slightly different tracklisting) on 2003/04/22.

[edit] Track listing

  1. "Set It Off"
  2. "Nobody"
  3. "Babylon"
  4. "Selector"
  5. "Firing The Love"
  6. "Pressure"
  7. "Bruises"
  8. "The Fear"
  9. "Falling Down"
  10. "Kiss And Make Up"
  11. "Domination"
  12. "Together"

[edit] Lava Re-Issue (2004)

When Skindred signed with Lava Records Babylon was yet again re-issued. However, this time there was new artwork & some newly recorded tracks.

  10. "Firing The Love"
  11. "Tears"
  12. "World Domination"
  13. "The Fear"
  14. "Interlude 3"
  15. "Babylon"
  16. "The Beginning Of Sorrows"

On 2005/10/10 the re-packaged Babylon came to the UK. Although the tracklistings were virtually the same, this version came with one more track "We Want" & a bonus CD.
