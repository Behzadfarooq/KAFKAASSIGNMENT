Babes of Broadway

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Babes of Broadway #1 (1996)

Babes of Broadway was a comic book published by short-lived Broadway Comics in 1996 featuring the character Fatale and pin ups of the other Broadway characters. It was written by Jim Shooter with art work by J. G. Jones and many others. The only issue published was issue #1.
