[edit] Slowly I Turned routine by Abbott and Costello

Regarding copyright on this article - the temp page still seems problematic. The issue is not attributing the copyright (it probably shouldn't be designated Tom Raymond - he is author of Clown Ministry website, not the Slowly I Turned routine). The concern is whether the sketch can be copied on Wikipedia. Please read WP:COPY for details of the relevant Wikipedia policies.

It's possible the script now falls into public domain, but that situation would likely need to be determined by a Wikipedia copyright examination. Even so, an article on the sketch should be more than a copy of the script - it should dwell on the background material and history. A good example of how this is done is the Who's on First? article. Your efforts on Wikipedia are appreciated and hopefully this matter can be worked out. Dl2000 03:34, 3 October 2006 (UTC)
