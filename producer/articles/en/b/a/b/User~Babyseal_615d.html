[edit] About me

Hi, I'm Babyseal! I was born in Vryheid, South Africa. I have lived in America, in the states of Nevada and Colorado, but now I live in the green and pleasent land of Oxfordshire, England. My favourite subjects are Science, History, Art, Drama, Food Tech, Resistant Materials, and Religious Studies.

I have many friends both in the UK and over the puddle we call the Atlantic Ocean, in the USA. We are all very eccentric in one way or another and all enjoy having a laugh. Our conversations mainly consist of rather random topics.

My father is a geologist and my mother is a teaching assisstant. I have a younger brother, too and he is a fan of Formula One racing!

[edit] Pets

I have one cat by the name of Penny. Her name was originally Penelope and she was rescued by the Blue Cross. She is a tabby cat and loves playing outside and dozing near the window sill. She also has a rather keen interest in decapitating mice...Yuck!

I've once owned a Campbell's dwarf hamster called Kiwi. He was a chubby hamster and enjoyed cucumber, sunflower seeds, and tomatoes. He died on the 23rd Decemeber 2004.

I have also owned a couple of Siamese fighting fish.
