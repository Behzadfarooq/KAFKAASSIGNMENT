"Baby Come On Home" is a song by English rock band Led Zeppelin. It was recorded during sessions for the band's debut album but sat unreleased before appearing on the 1993 compilation Boxed Set 2.

The song was recorded under the title "Tribute To Bert Berns", in honor of the American songwriter & producer who had died in December 1967. It is loosely based on two slightly different songs Berns had written and produced for Hoagy Lands (1964) and Solomon Burke (1965), although the lyrics were completely re-written for Led Zeppelin's version.

The song was never performed live by Led Zeppelin or any of its members.
