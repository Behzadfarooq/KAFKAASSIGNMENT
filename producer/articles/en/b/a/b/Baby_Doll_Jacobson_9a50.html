William Chester "Baby Doll" Jacobson (August 16, 1890 - January 16, 1977) was a Major League baseball outfielder. Jacobson was born in Cable, IL.

His best season was 1920, when he hit for a .355 batting average and amassed 122 RBI's.

Jacobson died in Orion, IL at the age of 86. Jacobson was laid to rest at Dayton Corners Cemetery in Colona, IL.[1]
