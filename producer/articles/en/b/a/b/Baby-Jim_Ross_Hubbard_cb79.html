Baby-Jim Ross Hubbard (better known as Baby Tubfat Maloy) is a fictional character in the TV comedy series 'Mummy and Baby'.

The series is very popular across some parts of the world and focouses around 'Baby', who is one years old and can speak full english, but still does not understand the concept of adult life.

His real name is actually 'Baby' as he was named this when he was born. He is part of the Maloy family, which consists of the family members; 'Mummy', 'Grandmama' and 'Grandpapa', all characters in the show.

Despite the show not being well known in the United Kingdom, some countries find the show extremely entertaining and enjoyable, with average viewing figures reaching above the expectations almost every week.

The show focouses on the every day life on single parent 'Mummy', who looks after 'Baby' every day and usually finds herself in many dilemmas involving her sons opionins and desires.

Some of these controversial desires, include 'Baby' expressing that he would like to actually be a female and his role model being WWE diva Candice Michelle. He also occasionally makes references to his pre school teacher, who 'Mummy' seems to be slightly envious of, over her relationship with 'Baby'.

Babys trademark includes of a facial smile known as 'the tubby face'. This is continually referenced in the show and has proved a hit with viewers.

'Mummy and Baby' continues to be a hit show, with 'Baby' now going by the name of 'Baby-Jim Ross Hubbard', due to his interests at the moment.
