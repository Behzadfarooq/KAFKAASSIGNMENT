Ba game

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Medieval ball game played in Scotland, perhaps most notably in Orkney, around Christmas and New Year.

Ba games are played in...

  • Duns
  • Jedburgh
  • Roxburgh
  • Kirkwall
  • Scone
  • Stromness
  • Melrose
