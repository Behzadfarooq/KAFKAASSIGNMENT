Ba Ba Ti Ki Di Do is an EP by Sigur Rós, released in 2004 by Geffen Records (see 2004 in music). The EP consists of the songs the group composed for Merce Cunningham's dance piece Split Sides, which Radiohead were also involved in.

The title refers to the only spoken words throughout the whole piece. Merce Cunningham was recorded saying "ba ba, ti ki, ba ba, di do" and this can be heard in the last track, "Di Do".

  2. "Ti Ki" – 8:49
  3. "Di Do" – 5:42
