Baf Ülkü Yurdu S.K.

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Baf Ülkü Yurdu Spor Kulübü is a Turkish Cypriot sports club established in Baf on 1947, however they moved to Güzelyurt on 1974.

The club currently plays at İkinci Lig ever since their relegation from Birinci Lig at the end of the 2004-05 campaign.

[edit] Colors

The club colors are green and red.

[edit] Stadium

The club's home stadium is Zafer Stadı.
