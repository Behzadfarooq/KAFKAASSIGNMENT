Bafatá is a region in north-central Guinea-Bissau. Its capital is Bafatá.

Sectors of Bafata

Bafata is divided into 7 sectors:

  • Bafata
  • Bambadinca
  • Contuboel
  • Sonaco
  • Galomaro
  • Gamamundo
  • Xitole
