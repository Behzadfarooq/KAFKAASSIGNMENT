Baffin, Unorganized

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Baffin, Unorganized is part of the Baffin Region census division in Nunavut, Canada. This area covers the whole Qikiqtaaluk Region outside of the 13 communities such as Iqaluit, Resolute or Grise Fiord.

Included in the area are the weather station at Eureka and the Canadian Forces base at Alert, which houses the entire populaton.
