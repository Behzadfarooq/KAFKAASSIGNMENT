Backyard Baseball 2007 is the sixth Backyard Baseball game. The backyard kids change looks and there even three new backyard kids (Joey MacAdoo, Arthur Chen, and Samantha Pearce). There are 17 pros as kids. It is part of the Backyard Sports series. It is available on the PlayStation 2, Game Boy Advance, PC, and Nintendo GameCube.

Major league players featured in the game are:

  • Alex Rodriguez (Chicao Cubs)
  • Derek Jeter (New York Yankees)
  • David Ortiz (Boston Red Sox)
  • Miguel Tejada (Baltimore Orioles)
  • Paul Konerko (Chicago White Sox)
  • Johan Santana (Minnesota Twins)
  • Eric Chavez (Oakland Athletics)
  • Vladimir Guerrero (Los Angeles Angels of Anaheim)
  • Ichiro Suzuki (Seattle Mariners)
  • Andruw Jones (Atlanta Braves)
  • Bobby Abreu (New York Yankees)
  • Dontrelle Willis (Florida Marlins)
  • Alfonso Soriano (Chicago Cubs)
  • Albert Pujols (St. Louis Cardinals)
  • Lance Berkman (Houston Astros)
  • Derrek Lee (Chicago Cubs)
  • Ken Griffey, Jr. (Cincinnati Reds)

[edit] Trivia

  • One of the fields in the game is "Tokyo Field" an obvious spoof of the Tokyo Dome in Tokyo, Japan.
