Back in the Circus (2004) is the fourth album released by Jonatha Brooke.

The track "Less Than Love is Nothing" is the first track Jonatha has ever co-written with someone, having written it with Eric Bazilian. Additionally, this is her first album to feature cover versions of other bands' songs: Fire and Rain, God Only Knows, and Eye in the Sky.
