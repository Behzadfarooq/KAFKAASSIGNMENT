Bacău International Airport (IATA: BCM, ICAO: LRBC) is the second important airport (it was surpassed in 2006 by Iasi International Airport in terms of passenger traffic ) in the north-eastern part of Romania (historical region of Moldavia). The airport serves both Bacău and Neamţ counties. The airport region is the site of Aerostar SA, an aircraft manufacturing and maintenance plant.

[edit] City access

The airport is located 10 km southwest of the Bacău city center, accessible by bus, airport shuttle and taxi.

[edit] Airlines and destinations

  • Blue Air (Rome-Ciampino, Turin-Cuneo)
  • Carpatair (Timişoara)
  • TAROM (Bucharest-Otopeni, Suceava)

[edit] Military uses

The airport is currently the home of the Romanian Air Force 95th Air Base with one fighter regiment (operating MiG-21 LanceR) and a helicopter regiment (operating IAR-330L). The base is also the home of the 250th training regiment.
