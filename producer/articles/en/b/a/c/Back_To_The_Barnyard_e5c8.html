Back To The Barnyard is a new Nickelodeon show that is a spinoff of the 2006 film Barnyard. According to the Nick.com message board for this show it is set to air September 1, 2007.

[edit] Notes

According to the DVD commentary on Barnyard, the underground world of gophers will be revealed. On the preview, it indicates that Otis may still play pranks on the humans. This will be Steve Oedekerk's next animated series for Nickelodeon. This will also be the first time he hasn't collaberated with DNA Productions and John A. Davis.
