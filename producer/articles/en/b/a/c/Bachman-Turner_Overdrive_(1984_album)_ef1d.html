Bachman-Turner Overdrive is an album by Canadian rock band Bachman-Turner Overdrive, released in 1984 (see 1984 in music).

[edit] Track listing

  1. "For the Weekend" (Bachman) – 4:20
  2. "Just Look at Me Now" (Bachman, Turner) – 4:38
  3. "My Sugaree" (Bachman) – 3:47
  4. "City's Still Growin'" (Turner) – 5:44
  5. "Another Fool" (Bachman) – 5:29
  6. "Lost in a Fantasy" (Bachman) – 4:11
  7. "Toledo" (Turner) – 4:12
  8. "Service With a Smile" (Bachman) – 4:21
