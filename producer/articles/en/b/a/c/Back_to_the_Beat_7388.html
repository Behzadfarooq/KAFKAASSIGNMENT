Back to the Beat was the first major E.P. release from Minneapolis based band Motion City Soundtrack. This release featured tracks that were later re-released. "Throw Down", "Back To The Beat", and "Capital H" were all re-released on a split E.P. done with Shatzi. "Capital H" was also re-released on Motion City Soundtrack's break-out album I Am the Movie.

[edit] Band Members

The band members on this E.P. are different from the current Motion City Soundtrack lineup[1] and include:

  • Joshua Cain - Guitar
  • Austin Lindstrom - Bass Guitar
  • Joel Habedank - Drums
  • Justin Pierre - Vocals, Guitar
