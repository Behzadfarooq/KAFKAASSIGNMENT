Back to Me is the second studio album by Canadian singer-songwriter Kathleen Edwards. It was released on March 1, 2005 on the independent labels MapleMusic in Canada and Zoe Records in the United States.

The album art is reminiscent of Christina's World, a painting by Andrew Wyeth.
