Back To Jerusalem movement

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Back To Jerusalem movement (Chinese:传回耶路撒冷运动) is a vision of the Chinese house churches to evangelise all the Buddhist, Hindu, and Muslim countries of the world. It aims to evangelize all Muslim, Buddhist, and Hindu countries by sending a minimum of 100,000 missionaries along the Silk Road, an ancient trade route that winds from China to the Mediterranean Sea.
