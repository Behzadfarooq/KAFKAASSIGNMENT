Bacdafucup (pronounced "Back 'da fuck up") is the first album from hardcore rap group Onyx. The album featured their breakout single, "Slam", which received heavy airplay on both radio and television (MTV and BET). This album is similar to other hardcore rap albums of the time, such as Mobb Deep's The Infamous in the way the lyrics are delivered and the gritty, haunting and atmospheric instrumentation used, including Piano's, Horn's and menacing bass lines.

In 1998, the album was selected as one of The Source Magazine's 100 Best Rap Albums.
