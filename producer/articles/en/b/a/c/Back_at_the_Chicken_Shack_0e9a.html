Back at the Chicken Shack is an album by Jimmy Smith. It was released in 1960 on the Blue Note label.

[edit] Track listing

  1. "Back at the Chicken Shack" (Jimmy Smith) — 8:01
  2. "When I Grow Too Old to Dream" (Oscar Hammerstein II, Sigmund Romberg) — 9:54
  3. "Minor Chant" (Stanley Turrentine) — 7:30
  4. "Messy Bessie" (Smith) — 12:25
  5. "On the Sunny Side of the Street" (Dorothy Fields, Jimmy McHugh) — 8:01 (Appeared as a CD-only bonus track, not part of the original LP configuration).
