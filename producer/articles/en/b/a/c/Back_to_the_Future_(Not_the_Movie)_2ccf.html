"Back to The Future (Not the Movie)" is episode #2.43 of the television show Phil of the Future. It is also the series finale of Phil of the Future.

Phil and Keely are voted "Cutest Couple" by the entire school. After talking it over Phil and Keely decide to start dating. Back at home, Pim discovers Lloyd has known how to fix the time machine for a while, yet he never fixed it because "everybody was happy here", Pim fixes the time machine and Lloyd takes it for a test drive. When he returns we find out that once they return home, they can never come back because of a law created called the "Thanks to the Diffys Law". Everybody sneaks downstairs that night to destroy the time machine so that Phil and Keely can stay together. Phil comes down and stops them saying he can't ask the others to sacrifice their lives for him. The family leaves the next day for the future just as Keely is running to say good bye. However, Phil convinces Lloyd to turn the time machine around so that he can say Good-Bye to Keely. They kiss while she's doing the school news and he then returns to the time machine. After a while, the family realizes that they have left Curtis back in 2006, and Lloyd reluctantly throws the time machine into reverse to pick him up.

[edit] Trivia

  • When the Diffy's are getting into the time machine the first time, look closley in Pim's hand. There is a painting. The painting is The Mona Lisa by Leonardo Da Vinci.
  • This episode is also the same name of the Back to the Future Trilogy starring Michael J. Fox and Christopher Lloyd, which is why (Not The Movie) is added after the episode's title.
  • In the end, the Diffy Family realize Curtis was left behind. It is unknown if when they went back for Curtis if the Diffys decided to stay in the 21st Century or go back to 2121.
