[edit] WP:NOT a crystal ball

Recent edits to this page may or may not be true, including the addition of several engines I can find no confirmation of the existance of - also, please do not list forward releases (namely 2007 releases) in the article, since Wikipedia is not a crystal ball. Molly the Yellow Engine, Spencer the Silver Engine - show no existance on the net. I will return to this article in 72 hours, and if sources for the existance of those engines I listed have not been added, I will see fit to go ahead with deletion. Thor Malmjursson 13:28, 22 February 2006 (UTC)

As per the above note, I have now completed deletion of everything which should not have been listed on this page. This includes all the unverifiable information I found, and references to characters who did not appear in Thomas the Tank Engine or any of the books or related stories. Thor Malmjursson 12:39, 28 February 2006 (UTC)

Whoever is flouting what I listed above, please don't. I am removing everything from this page which does not conform to the Wikipedia is not a crystal ball rules. If I see this information put back again, I am going to request administrators to lock the page. Thor Malmjursson 10:54, 15 March 2006 (UTC) Thor's Pet Yack
