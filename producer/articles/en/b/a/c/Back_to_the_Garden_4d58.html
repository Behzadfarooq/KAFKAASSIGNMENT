Back to the Garden is a 1992 tribute album to Joni Mitchell (see 1992 in music).

It was released on Intrepid Records, after the Canadian chart success of their 1991 Bruce Cockburn tribute, Kick At the Darkness.

The album's most successful single was Sloan's rendition of "A Case of You", from Mitchell's 1971 album Blue.
