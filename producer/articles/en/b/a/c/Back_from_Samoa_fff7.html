Back from Samoa is the second album by the American punk rock band Angry Samoans. It was released in 1982 (see 1982 in music).

The song "Homo-Sexual" was covered by The Soft Pink Truth on the album Do You Want New Wave or Do You Want the Soft Pink Truth? in 2004. Also the song "Lights Out" was covered by German punk band The Spermbirds on their album Nothing Is Easy, released in 1987 and by The Mighty Mighty Bosstones on the EP Ska-Core, the Devil, and More, released in 1993. "The Todd Killings" was covered by The Briefs on their Joy of Killing 7" and regularly perform the song live.
