[edit] 3 Foot Ninja 2

[edit] About Game

3 Foot Ninja 2 is a game in which you have to fight enemies. There are 5 chapters: Chapter 1, Chapter 2, Chapter 3, Chapter 4, and Chapter 5.

[edit] Chapters

Chapter 1: You have to fight fight 3 green monsters. 2 are on the ground, and one is on a branch. Chapter 2: Their are 3 parts of Chapter 2. In the first part, you need to fight 2 chicken pox monsters. They can be pretty stupid and silly. In the second part, their are again 2 chicken pox monsters. In the third part, their is a mushroom monster. They also can be silly, but powerful. Chapter 3: Their are 3 parts of Chapter 3. In the first part, you need to fight 2 black knights. Their is a barrel you have to break. In the second part, their are also 2 black knights. One is on the top. When you destroy it, you have to duck into the house to fight the second black knight. In the third part, their is 1 black knight and 1 chicken pox monster. The playing course looks like a skateboarding place.
