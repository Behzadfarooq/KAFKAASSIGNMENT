"Back Down" is a song from 50 Cent's 2003 commercial debut album Get Rich or Die Tryin'. It is track 10 on the album. It is produced by Dr.Dre.

Most of the song is intended as a 'diss' towards 50's rival Ja Rule. One excerpt from the song is:

"The rap game is all fucked up now,
What we gon' do now?
How we gon' eat, man? 50 back around!"
That's Ja, lil' punk-ass, thinkin' out loud...
