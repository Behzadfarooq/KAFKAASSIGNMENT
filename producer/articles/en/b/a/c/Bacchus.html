Bacchus is the name of:

  • The Greek god of wine and fertility, Dionysus, known also as Eleutherios (a.k.a "the Liberator"). Bacchus was also known to Romans.
    • Artworks depicting the god, including:
      • Bacchus (Leonardo) by Leonardo da Vinci
      • Bacchus (Michelangelo) by Michelangelo
      • Bacchus and Ariadne by Titian
      • Bacchus and The Sick Bacchus by Caravaggio
  • The Christian martyr Saint Bacchus, companion to Saint Sergius; see: Saints Sergius and Bacchus.
  • The asteroid 2063 Bacchus.
  • The Bacchus grape variety, grown predominantly in Germany.
  • The comic book Bacchus by Eddie Campbell.
  • Bacchus (Marvel Comics), a group of Amazon villains in the Marvel Comics comic book X-Men.
  • The energy drink Bacchus-F from South Korea.
  • The Krewe of Bacchus, a large New Orleans Mardi Gras parade.
  • A former night club, which was active in 1960's Jerusalem .
  • A song, In Praise of Bacchus, from the album October Rust by the Gothic doom band Type O Negative.
  • Bacchus Marshe a large town in central Victoria, Australia
