The Bacchiads were a clan of petty despots in Archaic Corinth. For some reason I chose it as my screen name. Anyway, my first edits focused mostly on Ancient mythology, poetics and cult practice. Lately I've been branching out into psychoanalytic theory and anarchist politics.

[edit] Stuff I've worked on

Some articles I've started: Urizen, Orc (Blake), Albion (William Blake), aulos, Georges Dumezil, Anahita, Hargrave Jennings, Emerald Tablet, Bakis, Aristeas, Lasus of Hermione, dithyramb, Late Antiquity, Erwin Rohde, William Blake's mythology, demand (psychoanalysis), desire (psychoanalysis), Name of the Father, desiring-production, freudo-marxism.

Some articles I've significantly re-worked: Orpheus, Pythagoras, Nyx, Mitra (and the late Mithra), Trophonius, Life-death-rebirth deity, Homer, Titan (mythology), Hermetica, Abaris, Epimenides, Onomacritus, Jupiter (god), Zeus, artificial mythology, Greek mythology, subject (philosophy), left-libertarianism, libertarian socialism, Anarchism

Other articles I've contributed to: Mithras, asura, Roman mythology, Hesiod, Theogony, Pindar, Gymnopaedia, Korybantes
