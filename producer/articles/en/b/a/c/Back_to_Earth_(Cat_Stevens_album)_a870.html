Back to Earth is a 1978 album released by the British singer/songwriter Cat Stevens, it was his last album before converting to Islam and leaving the music business.

[edit] Track listing

All songs by Cat Stevens, except where noted:

  1. "Just Another Night"
  2. "Daytime"
  3. "Bad Brakes"
  4. "Randy"
  5. "The Artist"
  6. "Last Love Song"
  7. "Nascimento"
  8. "Father"
  9. "New York Times"
  10. "Never"
