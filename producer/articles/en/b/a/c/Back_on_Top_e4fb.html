Back on Top is an album by Irish singer-songwriter Van Morrison released in 1999.

This album marks a slight return to the forms of music he is most known for: blues and R&B.

Recorded at The Wool Hall, Bath, England and Windmill Lane Studios, Dublin, Ireland.
