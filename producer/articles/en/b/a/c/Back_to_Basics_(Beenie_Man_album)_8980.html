Back to Basics (Beenie Man album)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Back to Basics
Studio album by Beenie Man
Released 2004


Back to Basics is an album by the reggae artist Beenie Man released in 2004. He comes back from Tropical Storm, a hip hop album. His hit single "Dude" reached #26 on the Billboard charts.

  10. "Eloh"

  13. "D-g Or G-g"
  14. "If Neva A God"
  15. "Back Against The Wall"
