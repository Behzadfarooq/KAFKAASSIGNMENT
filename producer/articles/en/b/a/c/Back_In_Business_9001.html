Back in Business is the return album from legendary Hip Hop duo EPMD, who broke up due to personal problems in 1992. After releasing four successful albums between 1988 and 1992 (three of them being considered classics), the duo returned with another successful effort. The single "Da Joint" became their second Billboard Hot 100 hit in 1997.

The album was certified Gold by the RIAA on November 17, 1997.
