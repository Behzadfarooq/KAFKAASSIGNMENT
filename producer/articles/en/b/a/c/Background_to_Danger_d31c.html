Background to Danger

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Background to Danger is a 1943 film starring George Raft, Brenda Marshall, Sydney Greenstreet, and Peter Lorre. Based on the novel of the same title by Eric Ambler and set in Turkey (an atmospheric studio version), the screenplay was credited to W.R. Burnett, although William Faulkner also contributed, and the movie was directed by Raoul Walsh.

[edit] Cast

George Raft as Joe Barton
Brenda Marshall as Tamara Zaleshoff
Sydney Greenstreet as Col. Robinson
Peter Lorre as Nikolai Zaleshoff
Osa Massen as Ana Remzi
Turhan Bey as Hassan
