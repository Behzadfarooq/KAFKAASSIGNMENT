Back in The D.H.S.S is the first album released by UK rock band Half Man Half Biscuit in 1985.

The title is a pun on the songBack in the U.S.S.R. by The Beatles

  1. "God Gave Us Life"
  2. "Fuckin' 'Ell It's Fred Titmus"
  3. "Sealclubbing"
  4. "99% Of Gargoyles Look Like Bob Todd"
  5. "Time Flies By (When You're The Driver Of A Train)"
  6. "I Hate Nerys Hughes"
  7. "The Len Ganley Stance"
  8. "Venus In Flares"
  9. "I Love You Because (You Look Like Jim Reeves)"
  10. "Reflections In A Flat"
