Bacolet

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

This article is on the Tobago town. For other uses see Bacolet (disambiguation).

Bacolet is a town on the island of Tobago, Republic of Trinidad and Tobago. It lies beneeth of Fort King George at the Bacelot Bay near Scarborough, Tobago to the south-east of the island-capital. This town is one of the most developed parts of tobago where the high society of the island lives. There are also many villas from tourists. [1] The Dwight Yorke Stadium is located in Bacolet.
