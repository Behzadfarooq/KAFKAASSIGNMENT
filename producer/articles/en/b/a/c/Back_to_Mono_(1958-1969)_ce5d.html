Back to Mono (1958-1969) is a four-CD box set of the work of producer Phil Spector. Three of the CDs are singles produced by Spector in the 1950s and 1960s by groups such as The Crystals, The Ronettes, and The Righteous Brothers. The fourth CD contains the Christmas album produced by Spector, A Christmas Gift for You from Phil Spector (1963).

In 2003, the album was ranked number 64 on Rolling Stone magazine's list of the 500 greatest albums of all time.
