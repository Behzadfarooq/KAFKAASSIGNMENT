Bacco Baccanels (born March 1, 1964 in Bollate (Milan), Italy) is an Italian songwriter. Invetor of the so called "musicucina" ("musikitchen" or "kitchen music" or even "musicooking").

He released his first album - "Lavori di Bocca" - in 1999. It was released on cassette and distributed by hand and word of mouth. It's no more available and will be re-recorded from scratch.
