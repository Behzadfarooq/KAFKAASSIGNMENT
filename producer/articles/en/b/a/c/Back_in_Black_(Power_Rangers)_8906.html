Back in Black (Power Rangers)

Back in Black is the fifth episode of Power Rangers: Dino Thunder in which Tommy Oliver gets new powers.

[edit] Plot

Utilizing Hayley's help, and their new Raptor Cycles, the Dino Rangers rescue Tommy from Mesogog's clutches. He gains the next Dino Gem, and with the help of a prepared Brachio Morpher, comes back to action as a Power Ranger.

[edit] Cast

  • Emma Lahana as Kira Ford, Yellow Dino Ranger
  • James Napier as Conner McKnight, Red Dino Ranger
  • Jason David Frank as Tommy Oliver, Black Dino Ranger
  • Kevin Duhaney as Ethan James, Blue Dino Ranger
  • Latham Gaines as Dr. Anton Mercer, Mesogog
  • Miriama Smith as Elsa, Principal Randall
  • James Gaylyn as Zeltrax
  • Ismay Johnston as Hayley

Guest Starring

  • Alistair Browning as Pollinator

[edit] Crew

  • Written by Ann Knapp Austen
  • Directed by Andrew Merrifield

[edit] Trivia

  • Available on the "Power Rangers Dino Thunder: Legacy of Power" DVD and VHS.
  • First appearances of the Brachio Morpher; Brachio Staff; Red, Yellow & Blue Raptor Cycles; Brachiozord; Cephalozord; and only appearance of the Thundersaurus Megazord's Cephalo Mode.
  • Tommy openly makes the first in-show reference to the tendency of Rangers to wear their power-color constantly.
  • Tommy gains the power of invisibility by bonding his DNA with the Black Dino Gem.
  • Tommy becomes the Black Dino Ranger, his fourth Power Ranger color and sixth power variation overall (Green Power Ranger, White Power/Ninja Ranger, Zeo Ranger V, Red Turbo Ranger and Black Dino Ranger).
  • The 501st episode
