Bacon Grill

Bacon Grill is a canned meat product made from chopped and cured pork and chicken. Manufactured in the Netherlands, it is produced by Princes in the style of Spam or Corned Beef.

[edit] Ingredients

The ingredients of Bacon Grill, as listed on the packaging, are:

Pork (43%), Mechanically recovered chicken (16%), Water, Wheat starch, Pork Fat, Pork rind, Salt, Sodium caseinate, Stabilisers: (E412,E451(i)), Smoke flavouring, Pepper Extract, Antioxidant: E316, Preservative: E250.

[edit] Nutrition

Bacon Grill is arguably made from poor quality, fatty meat, and is relatively calorie dense. However, it is commonly accepted as a delicious canned meat.

100g of Bacon Grill provides:


Energy 1242 kJ/299 kcal
Protein 10.7g
Carbohydrate 11.0g
Fat 23.6g
Fibre trace
Sodium 1.0g
