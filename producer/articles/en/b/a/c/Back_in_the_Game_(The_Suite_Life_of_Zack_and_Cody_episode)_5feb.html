Back in the Game is an episode of The Suite Life of Zack & Cody.

Zack and Cody's basketball team plays an exhibition game against a wheelchair basketball team visiting The Tipton. Maddie is making a movie with the help of London, Mr. Moseby and Lance the lifeguard.

[edit] Trivia

  • This was originally titled Wheelchair Basketball.
  • When Mr. Moseby warms up for his acting role, it is a reference to Ashley Tisdale's role as Sharpay in High School Musical when she warms up with Ryan before they perform.
  • This episode has already aired on the Family Channel in Canada.
  • Despite being on the team in season 1, Max and Tapeworm are replaced by Bob and another kid which probably means that their characters are canceled.
