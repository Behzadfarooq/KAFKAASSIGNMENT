Bachman Academy

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Bachman Academy is a private Christian Prepratory school located in McDonald Tennessee which serves grades 6-12. It became a Co-ed christian bording school in 1999, mainly for students with special needs.


The School Mascot is the Warriors, and the school colors are Maroon and Gold. The estimation of students enrolled is 54. It's a small school so there are less students then ussual. The classes are small and easy so it can help stutents earn credits in time for the future.
