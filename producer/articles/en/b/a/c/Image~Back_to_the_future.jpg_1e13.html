  1. This image illustrates the text next to which it appears, which describes the film in question for which it promotes

[edit] Fair use for Science fiction film

The use of this image in the article named / linked above is claimed as Fair use of copyrighted material because:

  1. it is a scaled-down, low resolution image of a movie poster;
  2. the article named above concerns the film in question;
  3. the inclusion of the image in the named article makes a significant contribution to the article because it:
    1. identifies the film in question;
    2. illustrates a section of the article;
  4. no free version of the image is available that would serve the equivalent purpose;
  5. the inclusion of the image in the named article does not limit the copyright owner's rights to market the film, or to market the movie poster itself, in any way;
  6. the image cannot be used to replace the original market role of the film in question or of the movie poster itself.
