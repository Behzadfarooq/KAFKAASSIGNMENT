[edit] Duck King

This shouldn't be merged until there's a Fatal Fury list to merge it to. For the time being, it will remain unmerged. In the meantime, it needs a lot of work.

First things first, things that happen in a fictional work are eternally happening in the present. For example, "Duck King jumps at a chance to enter the King of Fighters tournament," not "Duck King jumped for a chance to enter the King of Fighters tournament." Events that happened in expository backstory (e.g. his upbringing) get handled in past tense, though.

You need to mention what happens in what game. I'm having a little trouble telling where all of the story before "During the events of Fatal Fury 3 and Real Bout Fatal Fury..." came from.

Most importantly, there's a lack of a distinction between reality and fiction. Read User:BrianSmithson/Writing about fiction, and take a look at List of Green Lanterns#Alien Green Lanterns for an example of how to do this. Make sure you're writing about DK as a character in a fictional work, not as a historical figure. - A Man In Blâ™Ÿck (conspire | past ops) 05:44, 21 April 2006 (UTC)
