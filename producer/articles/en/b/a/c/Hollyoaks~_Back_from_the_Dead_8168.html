Hollyoaks: Back from the Dead

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Hollyoaks: Back from the Dead was a TV spin-off show from the long-running Channel 4 British television soap opera Hollyoaks.

[edit] Summery

The series followed Andy Holt’s return to seek revenge on Sam Owen and Russ Owen for nearly killing him.

He kidnaps their sister, Nicole Owen-, and waits for the brothers to fall into his trap. When they do he beats them up with a metal pole. He tells Russ and Nicole of Sam's part in the rapes.

Meanwhile Sam "O.B." O'Brien brings Mel Burton to Southport where she sees Andy, but O.B doesn't believe her. She tracks Andy down and finds him just as he's about to rape Nicole. She drugs him and ties him up. Andy tries to escape, but runs into a spike and dies.

Also Clare, Mandy and Louise go on a country holiday where they meet Sean, Louise's husband.
