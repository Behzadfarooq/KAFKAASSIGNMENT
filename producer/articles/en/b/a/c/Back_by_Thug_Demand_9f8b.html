Back by Thug Demand is american rapper Trick Daddy's 7th album. It was released on December 19, under Atlantic/Slip-N-Slide Records. The album features Chamillionaire, Trey Songz, 8 Ball,Young Buck, Webbie, Jaheim, Gold Ru$h, Trina, Baby (Birdman) and others. Book of Thugs: Part II was the tentative title name for the album before he changed it. The first single for the album is Bet That featuring Chamillionaire and Gold Rush, with production from The Runners. The album leaked on 14 December 2006. The second single was going to be "Straight Up" featuring Young Buck, but it was changed to "Tuck Ya Ice" featuring Baby. The album debuted on #48 on the Billboard 200 charts with 87,000 copies sold. So far the album has been a disappointment compared to the sales of his other albums.
