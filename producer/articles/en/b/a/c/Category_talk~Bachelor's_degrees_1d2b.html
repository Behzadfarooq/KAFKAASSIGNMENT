[edit] Needs help!

I'm not sure if anyone over at Wikipedia:WikiProject_Categories has noticed yet, but this category needs serious attention. I think most of the articles here could never be more than stub-length because of the intense amount of overlap between, say, Bachelor of Science, Bachelor of Mathematics, Bachelor of Engineering, Bachelor of Applied Science, Bachelor of Technology, Bachelor of Computer Science, and Bachelor of Computing, many of which are described in detail in Bachelor's degree anyway. My suggestion would be to either dewikify the Bachelor's degree article to eliminate the urge to make more stubs for each possible degree name, or to fold all the spin-off articles back into the main one. --bmills 03:45, 14 February 2006 (UTC)

Probably wise to fold them all back into one. Goldfritha 04:17, 13 August 2006 (UTC)
