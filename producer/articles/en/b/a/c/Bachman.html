People named Bachman include:

  • Bachman Khan, a Kimak khan (13th century)
  • Charles Bachman, computer scientist
  • Charlie Bachman (1892-1985) Hall of Fame college football coach
  • John Bachman (1790-1874) American naturalist
  • Randy Bachman (1943-) Canadian musician, member of The Guess Who and Bachman-Turner Overdrive
  • Richard Bachman, a pseudonym used by Stephen King
  • Tal Bachman (1970-) Canadian musician, son of Randy Bachman

The Bachman knot is often used in mountaineering for ascending on ropes. Bachman was also a the name of a sequel of sorts to the Pac-Man variant Macman (currently neither Bachman nor Macman has a page.)
