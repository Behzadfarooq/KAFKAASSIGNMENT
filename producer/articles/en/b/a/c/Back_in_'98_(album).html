Studio album by Bob & Tom
Released 1998
Genre Comedy
Length Disc One: 66:00
Disc Two: 70:37
Label Friggemall Industries
Producer(s) Bob Kevoian, Tom Griswold, Dean Metcalf, Steve Allee


Back in '98 is a comedy album by The Bob and Tom Show, which was first released in November 1998. It is a two-disc set which represents original material recorded during their syndicated, daily radio show and other studio numbers which had not been previously presented on air.
