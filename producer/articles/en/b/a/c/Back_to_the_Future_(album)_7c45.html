Back to the Future is the title of New Orleans rapper, B.G.. His debut album off of Atlantic Records should be released in May of 2007. The executive producer will be T.I. and guest appearances may include Young Buck, 50 Cent, Young Dro, Young Jeezy, T.I., R. Kelly, Swizz Beatz, Snoop Dogg, Chopper City Boyz, Mannie Fresh, David Banner, Yung Joc, and Juvenile.

[edit] Speculated Tracks

  • "I'm Out Here" (Produced by Keith Mack)
  • "Kings Amongst Kings" (featuring T.I.
  • "Back To The Future" (featuring Juvenile, Mannie Fresh, Turk & Chopper City Boyz) (Produced by Mannie Fresh)
  • "Hot Boys Reunion (Skit)"
  • "Came Back" (featuring C-Murder & Pimp C)
  • "No Beefin'" (Produced by Carnival Beats)
  • "Sellin' Everythang" (featuring Young Buck)
