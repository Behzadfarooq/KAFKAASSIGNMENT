Back to the Bus is a compilation of Funeral for a Friend's favourite songs released on March 26, 2007.

  2. Dub War - Enemy Maker

  4. Faith No More - Midlife Crisis
  5. Filter - Take a Picture
  6. Boston - More Than a Feeling
  7. Van Halen - Ain't Talking About Love
  8. Far - Water and Solutions
  9. Megadeth - Holy Wars
  10. The Haunted - Bury Your Dead
  11. Pantera - Mouth for War
  12. Black Sabbath - Black Sabbath
  13. Longview - Further
  14. Johnny Cash - Folsom Prison Blues
  15. Funeral for a Friend - Roses for the Dead (exclusive live version)
  16. Tourbus Tales - an interview with the band
