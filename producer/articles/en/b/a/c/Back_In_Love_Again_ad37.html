"Back In Love Again" is a song by Donna Summer from her I Remember Yesterday album. The "queen of disco" combines her trademark disco beats with a 1960s sound on this track. The song is actually a re-working of a track called "Something's In The Wind," which was a B-side for Summer in 1974.
