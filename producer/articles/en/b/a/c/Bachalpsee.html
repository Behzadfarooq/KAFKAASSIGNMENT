Bachalpsee or Bachsee is a lake with an area of 8.03 ha on the mountain First above Grindelwald in the Bernese Oberland, Switzerland. The lake, located at an elevation of 2265 m, is split by a natural dam, the smaller part of the lake being 6 m lower.
