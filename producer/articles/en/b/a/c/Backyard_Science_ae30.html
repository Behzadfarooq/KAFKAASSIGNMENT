Backyard Science is a successful Australian children's television show. It features kids experimenting with everyday items to make something fun and practical, but also scientific.

The show is hosted by two adults named Tarun and Dana. The show has catapulted many of the kids into stardom - one of the kids, Kerry-Ann Thoo,has even started a movie career.

[edit] Syndication

The show, which is produced by Beyond Television Productions, is broadcast in

  • Australia, (Seven Network and ABC)
  • Czech Republik, (Čt)
  • Germany
  • UK, Toonami As of March 2006

Backyard Science has also been sold to the following countries: Russia, the US, Latin America, Colombia, Canada, France, Italy, Malta, Finland, Czech Republic, Bosnia-Herzegovina, Hong Kong, India, Korea, Tawain, Indonesia, Vietnam, Brunei, Japan, Thailand, Singapore, New Zealand, South Africa, the Middle East and Israel.
