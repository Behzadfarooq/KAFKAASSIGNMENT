Backstage Pass is episode 324 of the FOX sitcom That '70s Show.

[edit] Synopsis

Spoiler warning: Plot and/or ending details follow.

Eric wants to spend more time with Donna. When he goes down to the radio station to confront her, Donna's boss gives her tickets to a Ted Nugent concert. Jackie wants her and Kelso's "second first time" to be magical, but Kelso just wants to do it. They agree to have a magical romance week first. On their 18th wedding anniversary, Bob and Midge relate how they first met, but Red and Kitty have trouble doing the same. Hyde and Fez decide to sell their own concert T-shirts but Fez ruins the plan after spelling Ted Nugent's name wrong. Donna's boss gives her a backstage pass after the concert, leaving Eric alone to wait for her. Hyde and Fez try to sell the misprinted shirts anyway and get arrested. Ted Nugent shows off to Donna backstage and she stays longer. Kelso and Jackie decide to do it in Eric's car after only one night of the magical romance week.

Later, Donna and Eric come to terms over their disagreements in the parking lot. Because the shirts read "Tad Nugent", they weren't really counterfeit merchandise, and Fez and Hyde are let go. Red and Kitty remember their first meeting after sharing Manhattans together.
