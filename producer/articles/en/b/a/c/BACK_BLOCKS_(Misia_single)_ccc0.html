"BACK BLOCKS" is MISIA's 11th single. It was released on November 20, 2002. It peaked at #7 selling 30,846 copies on its first week. The 3rd coupling, "Tobikata wo Wasureta Chiisana Tori" served as theme song for the PlayStation 2 Game "STAR OCEAN: Till the End of Time".

[edit] Track list

  1. BACK BLOCKS
  2. めくばせのブルース (Mekubase no Blues; Winking blues)
  3. LAILA (So So Def Remix)
  4. 飛び方を忘れた小さな鳥 (STAR OCEAN version) (Tobikata wo Wasureta Chiisana Tori; Small bird that has forgotten how to fly)
