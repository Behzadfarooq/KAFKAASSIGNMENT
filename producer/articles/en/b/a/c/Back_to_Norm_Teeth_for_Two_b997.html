"Back to Norm/Teeth for Two" is episode #5.4 of the Nickelodeon animated television series The Fairly OddParents. It aired on February 17, 2005.

[edit] Plot

Spoiler warning: Plot and/or ending details follow.

[edit] "Back to Norm"

Crocker has just received a lavalamp, and out pops Norm the Genie. Teaming up with Norm, the two try to get revenge on Timmy by trying to kill him, while Norm always suggests that Timmy be sent to Mars. Eventually Norm gets tired of Crocker's nervous breakdowns in his plans constantly failing and sends him to Mars.

[edit] "Teeth for Two"

Jorgen Von Strangle has fallen in love, and tries to pull out Timmy Turner's teeth as a wedding gift for the Tooth Fairy.

[edit] Trivia

  • Norm coming out of the lava lamp is an obvious parody of Aladdin.
  • In "Teeth for Two" it shows Dr. Bender showing off a pair of molar cufflinks. This might be a parody on The Great Gatsby as Meyer Wolfshiem also sported a pair of the same kind.
  • "Teeth for Two", is loosely based on the Oh Yeah! Cartoons segment "The Zappys".
  • In "Back to Norm", by looking at the blueprints for Operation Smashing Failure, you can see the words: Age 31. This may be Crocker's age, or his age when he thought of the plan.
  • The title screen for "Back to Norm" has the blueprints for the Timmy Tuck from "Hex Games".
  • In Teeth for Two, a picture of Butch Hartman as a fairy can be seen in the Pointy Crown.
  • In Teeth For Two, at The Pointy Crown, when the camera zooms to Timmy and Wanda, you can see a fairy version of Elvis Presley.
