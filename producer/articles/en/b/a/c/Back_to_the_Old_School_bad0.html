Back to the Old School is the debut album by old school/hardcore rapper Just-Ice, it was released in 1986, and was produced by Kurtis Mantronik. The album has been described as a "classic early hip-hop album" and "revolutionary" for its time by the All Music Guide, and is an AMG Album Pick.[1] It was the first full-length album released by Fresh Records, and its catalog number is LPRE-1.
