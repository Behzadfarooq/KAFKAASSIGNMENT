Bachelor of Resource and Environmental Planning

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Bachelor of Resource and Environmental Planning is a professional degree that seeks to integrate the disciplines of Urban planning, Environmental planning and Natural resource management. It is similar to the Bachelor of Environmental Studies offered by various schools in Canada, but is designed to give effect to the Resource Management Act 1991.

[edit] Institutions awarding Bachelor of Resource and Environmental Planning degrees

  • Massey University, Palmerston North[1]
  • University of Waikato, Hamilton[2]
