Brap: Back and Forth Series 3 & 4 is a compilation album by industrial band Skinny Puppy. It was released as a double-CD in 1996. This album reached to #9 in Billboard chart's Top Heatseekers [1].

[edit] Notes

  • "Brap" – Live 21 October 1986
  • "Dead Doll" – Demo Version
  • "Dead Lines" – Demo Version
  • "Last Call" – Live in CBC Radio studios, 29 September 1986
  • "Uranus Cancelled" – Alternate version of "Knowhere?" from the 'Last Rights' album
  • "All Eyes" – Live improvisation from the tour for the 'Too Dark Park' album
  • "Reclamation" – Live 14 December 1990
  • "Spasmolytic" – Raw "live" recording of them recording the song for the 'Too Dark Park' album
  • "Grave Wisdom" – Alternate mix
  • "Tin Omen" – Beginning minutes live 3 November 1990, the rest of the song is live 14 December 1990
  • "Gods Gift" – Live 14 December 1990
  • "Convulsion" – Live 14 December 1990
  • "Nature's Revenge" – Raw "live" recording of them recording the song for the 'Too Dark Park' album
  • "Love In Vein" – Studio Remix. Rumored to have been a track on the deleted single for the song.
  • "TFWO" – Live 14 December 1990
  • "Choralone" – Live 14 December 1990. Different lyrics from the studio version.
