Backstreet's Back is an album by the Backstreet Boys released internationally in 1997, but not in the United States. It was a follow-up to their successful self-titled international debut. Some songs from this album, and the prior release were compiled into a second self-titled album whose release coincided with this album. There were a few differences between the versions of songs released on this album and the American album (see the US album for details). It was also an "enhanced CD".

[edit] Track listing

This album, as has been common with Backstreet Boys albums, had several different tracklistings in various markets. The Dutch (European) edition is generally considered to be the "standard" tracklist for this album. The bonus tracks on this album, unlike the first international album, were scattered into the tracklist instead of being added to the end of the album. (eg: a bonus track as the fourth track would push "That's The Way I Like It" to fifth, and so on down the line.

  2. "As Long As You Love Me" – 3:41

  11. "If I Don't Have You" – 4:34

[edit] Canadian version

In Canada, "Anywhere For You" was omitted from the first album, and was put on this album as the tenth track.

[edit] Japanese version

In Japan, "Missing You" was added as the fourth track, and "Everybody (Matty's Club Mix)" was added as the last track.

[edit] English/Asian version

In England and Asia, "Missing You" was added as the fourth track, and "All I Have To Give (Part 2 - The Conversation Mix)" was added as the twelfth track. Additionally, the album was not enhanced like in other markets, but the interview from the enhanced portion was added as an audio track at the end of the album.

[edit] Boxed set version

A boxed set version based on the English/Asian version was made, featuring the same disc as in that version, but with a bonus disc. This edition was also released as a Christmas edition in Australia with alternate art. The tracks on the bonus CD are:

  1. Christmas Time – 4:07
  2. As Long As You Love Me (Unplugged Version) – 3:32
  3. Quit Playing Games (With My Heart) (e-Smoove Vocal Mix) – 6:47
