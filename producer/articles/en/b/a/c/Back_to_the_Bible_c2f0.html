Back to the Bible

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Back to the Bible is an international Christian ministry based in Lincoln, Nebraska, USA.

Founded in 1939 by Theodore H. Epp, Back to the Bible started as a radio broadcast in Nebraska, but expanded by supporting missionaries and broadcasting via shortwave radio to other countries. In 1954, their first international Bible teaching ministry office opened in Canada. Currently the ministry has offices in seventeen different countries and broadcasts in twenty-five different languages.

Epp served as General Director until 1984 and was succeeded by Warren Wiersbe. In 1990 Dr. Woodrow Kroll became the President and speaker for Back to the Bible
