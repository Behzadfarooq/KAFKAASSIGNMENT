Bacon's

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

You have new messages (last change).

Bacon's was a local chain of department stores in Louisville, Kentucky. It was a part of the Mercantile Stores. Mercantile Stores was acquired by Dillard's in 1998 and the Bacon's name was retired.
