Bacardi Breezer, Breezer for short, is a rum-based alcoholic beverage with 4% alcohol (5% in Australasia). It comes in a variety of lemonade-like flavours: lemon, pineapple, apple, rubygrapefruit, lime, orange, watermelon, cranberry, orange light and lemon light, all with a generally sweet taste.[citation needed]

The drink is not particularly popular in the United States, however in Europe, Canada and Australia, where the legal drinking age is generally lower than in the US, Bacardi Breezer is relatively popular particularly amongst the teenage market.

In India, Bacardi Breezer was the first entrant in the alcopop category and is currently the market leader in its segment.[citation needed]
