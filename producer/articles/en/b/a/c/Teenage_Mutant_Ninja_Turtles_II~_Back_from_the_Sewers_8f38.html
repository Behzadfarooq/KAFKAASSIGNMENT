Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles II: Back from the Sewers

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The title screen of TMNT II.

Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles II: Back from the Sewers is a 1991 Game Boy game by Konami. The game is based around the Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles series.

[edit] Gameplay

Similar to the gameplay of the original NES Turtles game, you can switch between the turtles between stages. Each turtle has their own strengths and weaknesses. Donatello has a big attack but is slow, Raphael is fast but his attack is very small, and Leonardo and Michelangelo are balanced. When a turtle is defeated, they are captured. At the end of the stage, you get a chance to rescue a turtle. If no turtles are captured, you get to play a bonus stage.
