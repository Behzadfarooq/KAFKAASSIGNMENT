Back For More is the second album of Natalia, a Belgian singer. It was released in Belgium 30 August 2004.

The album reached #1 in Belgium charts immediately. In October 2005 she received a TMF-award for Best Album national. In January 2006 after the first show of Natalia meets The Pointer Sisters she received 2X platinum for the sales of her album.

In June 2006, Back For More was re-released and included two bonus tracks: You Are & Heart Of Gold.

[edit] Tour

Back For More live sold out over more than 90 times in Belgium. In February 2005 she released her first live-DVD: Back For More live. For 15 weeks, the DVD was the best-sold music-DVD of Belgium and it was the best-sold DVD of 2005.
