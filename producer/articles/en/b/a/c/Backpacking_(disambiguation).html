Backpacking (disambiguation)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Backpacking is hiking and camping overnight in backcountry with one's gear carried in a backpack.

Look up backpacking in Wiktionary, the free dictionary.

Backpacking may also refer to:

  • Backpacking (wilderness): hiking or camping overnight in the wilderness.
  • Backpacking (travel): low-cost, generally urban, travel with minimal luggage and frugal accommodations
  • Backpacking (Canada): travel backpacking in Canada
  • Backpacking (ultralight): Backpacking while carrying very few or very light supplies
