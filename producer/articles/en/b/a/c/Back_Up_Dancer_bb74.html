Back Up Dancer is the second episode of season seven of the television situation comedy Will & Grace.

Spoiler warning: Plot and/or ending details follow.

[edit] Plot

After Jack took off as a backup dancer with iconic pop star Jennifer Lopez in the sixth season's finale, he now reappears in the same role, but as part of Janet Jackson's performance tour. Elsewhere, Will and Grace try to get back to business as usual and mend Grace's broken heart.
