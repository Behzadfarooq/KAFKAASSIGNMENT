Bacon Level, Alabama is a small community located 3-4 miles southeast of Roanoke, Alabama, in Randolph County, Alabama. Nearby churches include Bacon Level Baptist Church.

The surnames of some early settlers were Prather, Meachum, Ussery, Vinson and Gladnys/Gladnys Mill.
