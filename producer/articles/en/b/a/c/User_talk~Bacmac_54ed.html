[edit] Jung Sin Yuk-Do/Deletion

Hi,

When you nominated Jung Sin Yuk-Do for deletion you did it through the {{db}} tag. This is termed Speedy Deletion and actually covers a quite narrow number of cases, as covered in the criteria for speedy deletion. The reason you gave didn't match any of these. If you want to nominate the article for deletion, then you should do it through Wikipedia:Articles for deletion or Wikipedia:Proposed deletion (which might not be working at the moment). These allow a period of a week or so for interested parties to have their say. Hope that helps and leave me a message if you have any questions. Leithp 13:08, 7 May 2006 (UTC)
