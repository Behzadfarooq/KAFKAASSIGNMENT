Bob Rice of the Backyard Galaxy performing in Greensburg, Pennsylvania

Backyard Galaxy is a popular Roman Catholic musical group of the 2000s. Bob Rice and Bob Lesnefsky (Righteous B) perform at Steubenville and other youth conferences. Rice teaches at Franciscan University of Steubenville in Ohio.
