Should there not also be a definition for "backpacker" as a derogatory term for a certain type of hip-hop fan?

[edit] Disambig?

This disambig is not really disambiguating anything. Backpacking (wilderness) and Backpacking (travel) are not nearly different enough to have separate articles. Additionally, someone looking for backpacker (hip hop) will never even hit this page, mostly because they will no doubt search "backpackER". Look at what links here and there isn't a single hip hop article. The two top articles need to be merged into one article, and replace this disambig page.

As you can see in prior discussion on this page, the two backpacking articles were together before, but then I proposed that they be split, and someone else agreed. I will maintain that urban and wilderness backpacking are related but nonetheless different activities. --Smack (talk) 03:15, 14 December 2005 (UTC)

[edit] Styles of travel

Like martial arts, there are many different styles and methods to exploring the planet. The Backpacking (disambiguation) page has been edited to reflect the diversity among the planet's best travellers - generally those that are familiar with the outdoor and survival skills needed to traverse the wilds of a united Western hemisphere. Rightofcenter 18:31, 3 May 2006 (UTC)
