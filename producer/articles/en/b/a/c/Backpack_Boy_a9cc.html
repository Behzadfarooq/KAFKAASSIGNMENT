Backpack Boy (played by Kendre Berry) is a fictional character on the Nickelodeon kid's sitcom Ned's Declassified School Survival Guide who has a crush on Claire Sawyer and carries around a backpack that's so heavy, it usually causes him to fall to the ground. He has two lockers, both on top of each other, both of which lead to a room full of backpacks that no one but he or Ned probably know about (the school staff doesn't). In the short Guide to: School Photos a kid that looked like Backpack Boy got his picture taken in a SpongeBob costume after Ned said, "Look like yourself, not someone you saw on TV.". The rumors about Backpack Boy being a superhero are actually true as shown in the Guide to: Rumors short. In the Guide to: Talent Shows, Backpack Boy fits Claire Sawyer into his backpack as a magic trick, but forgets to take Claire out and she gets very mad. In the Guide to: Class Clown, Backpack Boy wins the tryout for the next class clown, but it caught by Vice Principal Crubbs and given a detention.
