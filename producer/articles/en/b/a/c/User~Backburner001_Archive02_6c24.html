[edit] Importance: Kids on the Street

The Kids on the Street article you flagged with the importance tag is part of a larger body of works known as the Wikipedia:WikiProject Albums. Many albums lack importance on their own, but are significant in the bigger picture. The article in question is a studio album by a notable artist, which qualifies it for existance under this project. Any discussion to the importance of this type of article in general should be directed to the project talk page. UnhandledException 20:36, 7 June 2006 (UTC)

Okay, thank you for directing me to the proper WikiProject. I'll post any future comments I may have there. -- backburner001 15:13, 8 June 2006 (UTC)
