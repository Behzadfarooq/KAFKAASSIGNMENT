[edit] Bachrodi

Bachrodi is the alias used by Kris Hartman, either for his musician name or his online identity.

Born October 6, 1982 near Chicago, Illinois. He was raised by his grandparents in Stuart, Florida. He currently resides in Miami, Florida.

[edit] Technology
