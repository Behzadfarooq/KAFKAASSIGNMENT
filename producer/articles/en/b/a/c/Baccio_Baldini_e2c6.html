Baccio Baldini (1436-1490?, fl. 1460-1490) was an Italian engraver.

[edit] Biography

Little is known of Baldini's life, apart his Florentine origin. The biographer Vasari noted that Baldini was a goldsmith who based all of his works on Botticelli.

Engravings by Baldini were published in 1477 in a work by Antonio Bettini and also in a 1481 edition of Dante's Inferno.
