Backstage is the Fifth studio album, released by singer Cher. The album was produced by Sonny Bono/Denis Pregnolato & Harold R. Battiste Jr. and was Issued under The Liberty Records Subsidiary Imperial Records. The album was a flop and it failed to chart. only one single "The Click Song" Was Issued & Failed To Chart! At Present Time Several Of The Tracks Are Still Not Issued To Cd! Including The Standout Cut Of Cher's Cover Of Bob Dylans "MASTERS OF WAR"!
