Bacup Cricket Club, based at Lanehead in Bacup, Lancashire, are a cricket club in the Lancashire League, who will play in the senior league in the 2006 season. Their captain is wicketkeeper John Chapman.

The club started in 1892 when the Lancashire League was formed. Their professional for the 2006 season is Chris Cairns. In their history, they have won the league championship 10 times and the cup 6 times.
