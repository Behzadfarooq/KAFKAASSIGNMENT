Bash can have different meanings; some usage cleverly combines different meanings.

Look up bash in Wiktionary, the free dictionary.

Computing:

  • bash, a Unix command shell written for the GNU project
  • Bash.org, a web site that hosts a popular database of quotes
  • Monster Bash is a video game created by Apogee Software

Violent connotations:

  • Killer Bash, a homoerotic Horror movie released in 2005, directed by David DeCoteau
  • The Bash Street Kids, a comic strip in the UK comic The Beano, with the annual book The Bash Street Kids Annual, situated in Bash Street School
  • Franco Bash, a video game character in the Fatal Fury series of fighting games
  • Crash Bash, a party game for the PlayStation
  • Bird strike is also called BASH (bird aircraft strike hazard)
  • Bash: Latter-Day Plays are three one act plays by Neil LaBute (1999)
  • Kit-bash, a scale modeler's term for modifying a commercial model kit into a unique or unavailable model

A party or gala event:

  • Pokémon Christmas Bash, Christmas songs performed by characters of the Pokémon television series
  • Plot 'n' bash, a term used to describe a style of navigational information used on road rallies
  • Bash At The Beach, a yearly professional wrestling event
  • The Great American Bash, an annual summer pay-per-view event held by World Wrestling Entertainment
  • Hash Bash, an annual event in Ann Arbor, Michigan in favour of marijuana
  • Bash & Pop, the band that Tommy Stinson formed after the dissolution of The Replacements

A person's name:

  • Baby Bash, also known as Baby Beesh, (born Ronnie Ray Bryant on October 18, 1975 in Vallejo, California, USA) is a Latin American rap/hip-hop artist
  • Dana Bash, a CNN reporter and anchorwoman.
  • Todd Bash (born 1965), an avant-garde playwright from Los Angeles, California

Other:

  • Kizilbash, or Qizilbash, a Muslim religious brotherhood
  • Bash Chelik, ("Man of Steel"), a famous Serbian folk tale
  • Battle of Bash Abaran, Caucasus, 1918
  • Bash Bish Falls, a waterfall in southwestern Massachusetts in the Bash Bish Falls State Park
