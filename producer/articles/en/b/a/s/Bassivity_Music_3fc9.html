Bassivity Music

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Bassivity Music is a music label which specialises in hip hop, based in Belgrade, Serbia. Artists who have released albums with the label include V.I.P., Marčelo, Suid, Struka, Oneya, Shorty and Edo Maajka.
