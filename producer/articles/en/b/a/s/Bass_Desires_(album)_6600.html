Bass Desires is a 1985 studio album by jazz bassist Marc Johnson.

[edit] Musicians

Marc Johnson (bass); Bill Frisell (synthesizer, guitar); John Scofield (guitar); Peter Erskine (drums).

[edit] Track listing

  1. Samurai Hee-Haw
  2. Resolution
  3. Black Is The Color Of My True Love's Hair
  4. Bass Desires
  5. A Wishing Doll
  6. Mojo Highway
  7. Thanks Again
