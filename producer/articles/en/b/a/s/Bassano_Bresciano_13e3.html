Bassano Bresciano is a commune in the province of Brescia, in Lombardy. As of 2007 Bassano Bresciano had an estimated population of 2,176. [1]

The Osservatorio Astronomico di Bassano Bresciano (Astronomical Observatory of Bassano Bresciano) is located within its territory.
