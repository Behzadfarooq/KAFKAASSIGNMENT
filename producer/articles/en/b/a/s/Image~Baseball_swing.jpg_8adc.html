GREELEY, Colo. (AFPN) — Outfielder Dale Kasel had his finest baseball game as a Falcon, going 3-

for-4 with three runs and three RBIs. The Air Force Academy baseball team (5-14) split a midweek 
doubleheader with the University of Northern Colorado (13-8) on March 16 at Jackson Field here. 
Photo by Danny Meyer.
