Bastard Noise

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Bastard Noise is a noise music band founded in 1991 by Eric Wood, Henry Barnes, and Bill Nelson. The band was initially a side project of Wood's and Barnes's hardcore band Man Is The Bastard.

Bastard Noise often collaborates, and has had a number of members or almost-members during the years. One of the more prolific ex-members is John Wiese, who joined the Bastard Noise lineup in 1998 and contributed in updating the sound of the band. Weise is however no longer a member of Bastard Noise. The band toured for the first time in the United States and Canada in April of 2007. The touring line up consisted of Eric Wood and Bill Nelson.

The band have numerous releases on prominent noise labels such as Release, Ground Fault Recordings and Alien8.
