Baseball players

This is a category for baseball players. Almost no biographical articles should be "Pages" in this main category (an exception may be Aaron Durley, a 12-year-old in Little League). Instead, players should be put in one or more "Subcategories". Major league players should be categorized at least by national origin and team.
