Hello. I currently live in Montreal, Quebec, Canada. I was born in Montreal but was raised in Kingston, Ontario, Canada. My interests include politics, history, geography, music, and languages. I've been trying to improve my French lately and I am also learning Mandarin Chinese. I attend McGill University in Montreal.

Salut. J'habite au Montréal, Québec, Canada. Je suis né au Montréal, mais j'habitais au Kingston, Ontario, Canada pour la plupart de mon vie. J'aime les politiques, l'histoire, la géographie, la musique et les langues. J'apprend français, mais c'est difficile parce que j'apprend le chinois à le même temps.
