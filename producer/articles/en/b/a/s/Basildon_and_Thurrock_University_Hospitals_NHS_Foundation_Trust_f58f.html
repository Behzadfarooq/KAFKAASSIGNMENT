Basildon and Thurrock University Hospitals NHS Foundation Trust is one of the United Kingdom's thirty-four NHS Foundation Trusts. It provides healthcare for people in the south west Essex, in the East of England. There are two hospitals in the trust and one clinical centre: Basildon Hospital and Orsett Hospital and Billericay St. Andrew's Centre.
