Bass in the Grass
Location(s) Darwin, Australia
Years active 3 Years
Date(s) May 26
Genre(s) Rock, Hip hop, Electronic
Website http://www.bassinthegrass.com.au/


Bass in the Grass, (Base), is an annual music festival in Darwin, Australia. Australian bands come together on May 26 and play for the Darwin community. The event has been running for 3 successful years now, being the annual big event for youth in the area.

[edit] When is it?

Bass in the Grass is on May 26, at the Darwin Amphitheatre.
