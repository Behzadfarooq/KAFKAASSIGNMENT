Baskholm is my nom de guerre, because my given name is unpronounceable in other languages than Danish.

I began as a cabinet maker, later I became a teacher, and now I'm retired. My interrests differs from tools, to pictures (specially photography), to history, genealogy and literature.

I've been a Wikipedian since the beginning of 2005.
