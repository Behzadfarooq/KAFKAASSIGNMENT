[edit] Criteria

The only official criteria is for a Selected picture is that it must be a free image. Fair use or copyrighted images cannot be used in Portal space.

[edit] Instructions

[edit] Nominate

To nominate an image as a Selected picture, paste the following markup below the Nominations header:


===ImageSubject===
[[ImageName|right|200px]]
'''Nominate:''' --~~~~


After the word Nominate:, please add a brief explanation of why the article should be featured.

[edit] Debate

Post comments or votes below each image, and sign the message ~~~~

  • Comment / Support / Oppose

[edit] Close

All debates should remain open for five days, except in cases where the nomination was obviously made in bad faith. An article should never be passed before five days, regardless of how decisive the vote is.

For Passed nominations:

  • Cut and paste the debate source code, including the header, directly under the Passed header on Portal:Baseball/Selected picture/Nominations/2007.
  • Create a new page by clicking on the first available red link under selected articles.
  • Center the image by enclosing it with the following tags:
<center> </center>
  • Use your own discretion when deciding what text to include, keeping in mind any suggestions that were made during the debate. Other editors can always make changes later.
  • At the end of the text, include a link to the applicable article in the following format:
'''([[SOURCE ARTICLE|more...]])'''
  • On the next line, insert the following code:
<center> '''- [[Portal:Baseball/Selected picture/Archive|Archive]] - 
[[Portal:Baseball/Selected article/Nominations|Nominations]] -''' </center>

For Failed nominations:

  • Cut and paste the debate source code, including the header, directly under the Failed header on Portal:Baseball/Selected picture/Nominations/2007.
