[edit] Category merge

A merge notice has been placed on Category:History of Basque Country as this category already existed and was more complete. As the Basque Country is an ambiguous term (see Basque Country), this is a more neutral title that can refer to all the 'Basque lands' and the Basque people and culture. In any case, 'Basque Country' requires a 'the'. Mtiedemann 15:43, 17 July 2006 (UTC)

The key issue is that there is already a category for these articles. If you want to change the name of the category, you should not just create a new one, you need to follow the proper procedures as at WP:CFD so that you give proper reasons and so that others have a chance to comment.
Incidentally, there is constant controversy on Wikipedia about the term 'Basque Country' with those whom I assume to be pushing a Spanish-centred view of the matter claiming it only extends to the autonomous community. I disagree and feel the best way forward is to be clear on every occasion when naming articles and categories. Mtiedemann 17:56, 17 July 2006 (UTC)
