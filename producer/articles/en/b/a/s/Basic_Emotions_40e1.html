Told in three separate but intertwined short films, Basic Emotions is a story of how four young Manhattan roommates deal with insecurities, create facades, express compassion and struggle to believe in themselves. In a creative approach, three different teams wrote, directed and shot each of these poignant stories, using the same actors and crew.
