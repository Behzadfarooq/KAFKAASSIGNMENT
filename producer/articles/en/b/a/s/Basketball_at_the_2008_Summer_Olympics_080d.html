Basketball at the 2008 Summer Olympics will be held from August 9 to August 24, 2008. Competitions will be held at the Wukesong Indoor Stadium[1] in Beijing, China.

[edit] Events

2 sets of medals will be awarded in the following events:

  • Basketball Men (12 teams)
  • Basketball Women (12 teams)

[edit] Qualification

An NOC may enter up to 1 men's team with 12 players and up to 1 women's team with 12 players.
