Basketball TV or BTV is a 24-hour all-basketball cable channel in the Philippines owned by the Solar Entertainment Corporation. It was launched in October, 2006, rebranding the Sports Plus channel.

The channel focuses more on showing NBA TV related programs and also regular NBA games once the season begins. In its initial broadcasts, it has been showing re-runs of the 2006 NCAA Men's Division I Basketball Tournament and the 2006 FIBA World Championship.

For the 2006 edition, the Philippine Collegiate Champions League signed a deal with the channel to cover all games on its channel on a taped-delay basis.

On their recent schedule grid available on their official webpage, the cable channel is slated to broadcast a few Euroleague, National Basketball League and the local National Capital Region Athletic Association basketball games on a delayed basis.
