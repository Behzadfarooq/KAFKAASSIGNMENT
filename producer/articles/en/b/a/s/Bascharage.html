Bascharage (Luxembourgish: Nidderkäerjeng, German: Niederkerschen) is a commune and a town in south-western Luxembourg. It is part of the canton of Capellen, which is part of the district of Luxembourg.

As of 2005, the town of Bascharage, which lies in the centre of the commune, has a population of 4,550. Other towns within the commune include Hautcharage and Linger.

It is also the location of Luxembourg's famous Bofferding brewery.
