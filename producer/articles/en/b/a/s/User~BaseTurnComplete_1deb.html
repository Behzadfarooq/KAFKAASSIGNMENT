[edit] About me

I'm an Air traffic controller and a licensed Commercial pilot, although I don't fly professionally. In a former life I trained as a Chemical Engineer and worked in the IT industry. My main interest is aviation, but I am also interested in (in no particular order) rugby, popular music, computing, finance and investment, law, and collecting 19th Century pottery and porcelain. I'll also admit to having a latent interest in the history of railways, probably because I'm from a railway family, but you'll need to get me drunk to admit that in person.
