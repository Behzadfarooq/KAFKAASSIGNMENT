Basket Napoli is an Italian basketball club based in Naples.

Before the 1999/2000 season the team played in Serie A2, under the name Serapide Pozzuoli;[1] later the team headquarter was moved to Naples by its owner Lubrano who then sold the society to Mario Maione. In the 2000/2001 league Basket Napoli was promoted to Serie A. Basket Napoli took part in the ULEB Cup in 2004/2005 and won the Coppa Italia in 2006.
