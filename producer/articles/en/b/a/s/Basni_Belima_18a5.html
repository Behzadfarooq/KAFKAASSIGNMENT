Basni Belima is a census town in Nagaur district in the state of Rajasthan, India.

[edit] Demographics

As of 2001 India censusGRIndia, Basni Belima had a population of 21,557. Males constitute 50% of the population and females 50%. Basni Belima has an average literacy rate of 54%, lower than the national average of 59.5%; with 63% of the males and 37% of females literate. 20% of the population is under 6 years of age.
