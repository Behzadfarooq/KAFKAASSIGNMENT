Basilisk (disambiguation)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Look up basilisk in Wiktionary, the free dictionary.

The basilisk is a legendary death-dealing creature. Derived meanings include:

  • Basiliscus basiliscus, a species of lizards in the Basiliscus genus
  • Basiliscus plumifrons, the plumed basilisk lizard
  • Basilisk (comics), a fictional supervillain in the Marvel Universe
    • Basilisk (Mutant), a fictional mutant character in the Marvel Universe.
  • Basilisk: The Kouga Ninja Scrolls, a Japanese anime
  • Basilisk II, an emulator
  • Basilisk Linux, a live CD distribution of Linux based on Fedora Core
  • On Basilisk Station, a novel by David Weber set in the Basilisk star system
  • Basilisk, a concept by author David Langford
  • In military history, a basilisk is a large brass cannon, said to have been named for its resemblance to the large-variety legendary basilisk in size and potential. Fictional Basilisk cannon of the Warhammer 40,000 is based on it.
  • Basilisk, a starship in the video game Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic
