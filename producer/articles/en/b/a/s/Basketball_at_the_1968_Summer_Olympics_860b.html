Basketball at the 1968 Summer Olympics

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

You have new messages (last change).

The United States, coached by Henry Iba of Oklahoma State University, went 9-0. Their gold medal-winning game was against Yugoslavia, 65-50. The bronze medal winner was the Soviet Union.
