Bassett High School is a public secondary school located in Bassett, Virginia, and is one of two high schools in the Henry County, Virginia public school system.

Bassett High School was consolidated with its long-time rival, Fieldale-Collinsville High School, after the 2003-2004 academic year due to declining enrollment in the Henry County public schools.
