Basque Nationalist Republican Party (in Spanish: Partido Republicano Nacionalista Vasco) was a political party in Euskadi, Spain. PRNV was founded in 1911, as a progressive alternative to the conservative Basque Nationalist Party (PNV). PRNV was modelled after the Republican Nationalist Federal Union (UFNR) in Catalonia.
