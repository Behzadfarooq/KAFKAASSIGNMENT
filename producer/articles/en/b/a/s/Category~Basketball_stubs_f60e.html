This category is for stub articles relating to basketball. You can help Wikipedia by expanding them.

To add an article to this category, use {{Basketball-stub}} instead of {{stub}}.

This category was inspired by the recent addition of the "footy-stub" for football (soccer)-related stubs. This will help out basketball fans who would be interested in finding articles to work on, but who do not want to wade through the huge list of regular stubs and bio stubs.

A new stub category specifically for biographies of basketball figures has been created. To add an article to this category, use {{Hoops-bio-stub}}.
