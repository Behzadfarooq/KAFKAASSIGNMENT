[edit] Club Atlético Basáñez

Club Atlético Basáñez are a football club from Montevideo in Uruguay founded in 1920. They currently play in the Uruguayan 2nd Division.

The club is named after Tomas Basáñez, who was a major landowner in the locality of the club. The club colors are inspired on the anarchist flag.

[edit] First Division

After being champions of the Uruguay Second Division in 1993, they were promoted to play in first division where they spent two seasons 1994 and 1995. They had never played in First Division before.
