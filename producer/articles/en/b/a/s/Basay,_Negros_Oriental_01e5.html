Basay, Negros Oriental

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

You have new messages (last change).

Basay is a 4th class municipality in the province of Negros Oriental, Philippines. According to the 2000 census, it has a population of 21,366 people in 4,220 households.

[edit] Barangays

Basay is politically subdivided into 10 barangays.

  • Actin
  • Bal-os
  • Bongalonan
  • Cabalayongan
  • Cabatuanan
  • Linantayan
  • Maglinao
  • Nagbo-alao
  • Olandao
  • Poblacion
