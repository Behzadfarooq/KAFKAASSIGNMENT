Bass Culture is an album by dub poet Linton Kwesi Johnson, released on 9 May 1980 on the Island Records label. It was produced by Linton Kwesi Johnson and Dennis Bovell (credited as Blackbeard).

Reggae Fi Peach laments the death of Blair Peach, an Anti-Nazi protestor who was killed in London during a clash with police officers during a protest by the Anti-Nazi League against a British National Front meeting in 1979.
