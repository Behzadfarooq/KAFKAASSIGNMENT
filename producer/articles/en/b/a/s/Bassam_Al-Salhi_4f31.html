Bassam Al-Salhi

Bassam Al-Salhi (b. 1960) is a General Secretary of the Palestinian People's Party and elected member of the Palestinian Legislative Council.

He was the party's candidate for President of the Palestinian Authority in 2005. He received 2.67% of the vote.

He was one of the two deputies elected from The Alternative at the Palestinian legislative election, 2006.

In the 2007 so called "Unity Government" he is appointed Minister for Culture.
