Bas Ek Pal (Hindi: बस एक पल, Urdu: بس ایک پل) is a 2006 Bollywood film, directed by Onira. The movie stars Juhi Chawla, Urmila Matondkar, Jimmy Shergill, Sanjay Suri, Rehaan Engineer and Yashpal Sharma.

[edit] Music

The Bas Ek Pal album contains nine songs, three of them being remixes. The soundtrack is notable for its Western flavor and was praised from all critics. The music directors were Vivek Phillip, Mithoon and Pritam. Lyrics were written by Amitabh Verma and Sayeed Quadri for Tere Bin which was one of the best songs of 2006. [1]

Soundtrack scored by Vivek Phillips otherwise stated.

  • Hai Ishq Ye Kya Ek Khata Singers: K.K. & Sunidhi Chahun Music: Pritam
  • Tere Bin Singer: Atif Aslam Music: Mithoon
  • Zindagi Hosh Mein Singers: K.K. & Zubin
  • Bas Ek Pal Singers: K.K. & Dominque
  • Ashq Bhi Singers: K.K. & Sunidhi Chahun
  • Dheemey DheemeySingers: K.K. & Sunidhi Chahun
  • Bas Ek Pal (Remix)
  • Tere Bin (Remix)
  • Zindagi Hosh Mein (Remix)

The soundtrack was released on 2-channel compact disc and cassette tape[2]
