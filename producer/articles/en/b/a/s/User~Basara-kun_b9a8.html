[edit] A little biography

Since I was a child, always I've fan of video games of the time (like platforms,), but especially fighting genre, the VS and beat'em up games. Later I found manga and anime and I converted in otaku (in the occidental meaning), but the fighting games still passionates me like when I was a child, until today.

[edit] My contribuitions

Mainly my contribuitions are in video game, aporting with various sections as a freelance, creating character biographies, modifing existing ones and creating new templates for those sections. These are the games I worked in certain grade, adding minor things as creating/modificating profiles or re-making info from scratch:

  • Aggressors of Dark Kombat
  • Fu'un series (Savage Reign and Kizuna Encounter)
  • Power Instinct series
  • Neo Geo Battle Coliseum (creating Yuki's profile and the NGBC template)
  • Fatal Fury series (mainly in Alfred Airhawk and Rick Strowd's profiles)
  • The King of Fighters series, with minor fixes (especially Striker (KOF))
  • Samurai Shodown series (mainly in Basara Kubikiri and creating Andrew and Iroha profiles)
  • Buriki One (creating some character profiles)
  • Purikura Daisakusen
  • Waku Waku 7
  • Galaxy Fight (minor fixes)
  • Variable Geo and Variable Geo (anime) (both made from scratch)
  • Fighter's History
  • Art of Fighting (mainly in non-KOF characters)

Also these are some non-games I created or modified:

  • Alpha Denshi Corp. (ADK) (re-made from scratch)
  • Otaku (adding Groove on Fight quoting)
