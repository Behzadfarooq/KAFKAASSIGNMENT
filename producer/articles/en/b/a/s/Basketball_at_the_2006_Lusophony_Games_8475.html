Basketball at the 2006 Lusophony Games

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

You have new messages (last change).

The Basketball tournament of the 2006 Lusophony Games was played in Macau, People's Republic of China. The venue was the Tac Seac Multi-sports Pavillion. The tournament was played from 8 to 14 October 2006, and there was a men's and women's competition. The male and female teams of Portugal and Mozambique, respectively, were the winners of both tournaments, beating in the final, Angola and Portugal. The bronze medals went to both the the Cape Verdian teams.
