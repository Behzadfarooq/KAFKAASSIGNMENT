Bastard (song)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

"Bastard" is a song by the rock band Mötley Crüe, from the album Shout At The Devil released in 1983. This song was rated #5 in the Parents Music Resource Center Filthy Fifteen list in 1985.
