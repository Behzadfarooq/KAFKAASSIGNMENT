Mother Basilea, born Klara Schlink (21 October 1904, Darmstadt, Germany - 21 March 2001, Darmstadt) was a German religious leader and writer. She was leader of the Evangelical Sisterhood of Mary, which she cofounded, from 1947 to 2001.
