Basem Mohamad

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

You have new messages (last change).

Basem Mohamad was an early 18th century muslim musician of Palestinian origin. He revolutionized early styles of Palestinian music in the Classical period (1700AD - 1850AD). His influence is still profound today.
