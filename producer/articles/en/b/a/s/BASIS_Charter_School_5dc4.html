BASIS Charter School is a school in Tucson, Arizona. In 2006, its High School was named # 3 on Newsweek's Top 1200 High Schools list. It has also received numerous local accolades for its high scores, as well for its embracing of the AP program. In fact, it is one of the only schools in the country to require students to take at least 5 AP exams in order to graduate.

Also, the school has received local recognition for its drama program, which recently put on the children's opera Brundibar: Hear My Voice (the new half of which was written by Colin Killick, a student at the school) in cooperation with Arizona Onstage Productions
