[edit] Basketball Northern Ireland

Wikipedia Encyclopedia


Basketball Northern Ireland (BNI) the National Governing Body for Basketball in Northern Ireland. The Association is affiliated to Basketball Ireland has responsibility for the promotion, development and administration of all basketball activities in Northern Ireland.
