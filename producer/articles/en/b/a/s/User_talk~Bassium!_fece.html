[edit] Your questions

I noticed that on your user page you were confused as to just what Wikipedia is. No, Wikipedia is not focused on anything but all around encyclopedic topics. What we're trying to do here is create an online encyclopedia - an information repository of notable things.

You can put anything you want here, provided it's notable (something that's part of popular culture, an invention, etc.) - you can't put stuff about a garage band with three listeners, for instance, but you could put stuff about a popular band with thousands of listeners; see Wikipedia:notability - and that it is written from a neutral point of view. If you have any questions, please ask me on my talk page and I will get back to you. If you are still confused as to what Wikipedia is, see Wikipedia:about and Wikipedia:what wikipedia is not. If you are learning english, you might want to check out the Simple English Wikipedia. Hope this helps. -Jetman123 23:53, 14 March 2006 (UTC)
