Bastia - Poretta Airport (French: Aéroport de Bastia-Poretta) (IATA: BIA, ICAO: LFKB) is an airport situated 17 km southeast of Bastia, a commune of the Haute-Corse département in France.

[edit] Airlines and destinations

  • Air France (Paris)
  • CCM Airlines (Lyon, Marseille, Nice)
  • Germanwings (Cologne/Bonn, Stuttgart)
