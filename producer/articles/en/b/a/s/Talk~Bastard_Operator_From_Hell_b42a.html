[edit] Mantra

I removed the "Mantra" section. "Down, not across" is the motto of alt.sysadmin.recovery (see ftp://rtfm.mit.edu/pub/faqs/sysadmin-recovery section 1.6). "Non ex transverso sed deorsum", which appears to be a Latin translation, doesn't seem to be the mantra or motto of anything (though it does seem to be a somewhat popular sig).
