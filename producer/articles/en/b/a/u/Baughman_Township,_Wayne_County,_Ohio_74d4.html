Municipalities and townships of Wayne County.

Baughman Township is one of 16 townships in Wayne County, Ohio, United States.

It borders (or touches the tip of) the following Wayne County townships:

  • Green (west)
  • Milton (northwest tip)
  • Chippewa (north)
  • Sugar Creek (south)
  • East Union (southwest tip)
