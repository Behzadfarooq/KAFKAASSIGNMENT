The Bausch & Lomb Championships is a tennis tournament held in Amelia Island, Florida, United States. Held since 1980, this WTA Tour event is a Tier II-tournament and is played on outdoor green claycourts.

  • 1980-'83: Murjani WTA Championships
  • 1984: Lipton WTA Championships
  • 1984: NutraSweet WTA Championships
  • 1985-'86: Sunkist WTA Championships
  • 1987- : Bausch & Lomb Championships
