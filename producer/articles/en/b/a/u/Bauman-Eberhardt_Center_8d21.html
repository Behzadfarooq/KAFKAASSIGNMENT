Bauman-Eberhardt Center is a 2,200-seat multi-purpose arena in St. Louis, Missouri, in the United States. The arena opened in the 1920. It is home to the Saint Louis University Billikens women's basketball team, until the program moves into the new Chaifetz Arena at the start of the 2008-2009 season. It was home of the men's team until the Kiel Auditorium opened in 1934.
