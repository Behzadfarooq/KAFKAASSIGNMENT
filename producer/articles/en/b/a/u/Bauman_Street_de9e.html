Bauman Street

Bauman Street, (Tatar: Bauman uramı/Бауман урамы; Russian: улица Баумана), is a main street in Kazan, Tatarstan, Russia. The street runs from the Kazanka River near the Kazan Kremlin to the north-west, to Pushkin Street to the south-east. The street is pedestrian to the south and is a major shopping spot.
