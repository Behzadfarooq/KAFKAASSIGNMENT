Baqiyatallah Medical Sciences University (BMSU) is a non-civilian medical school in Iran.

Located in Tehran, it was founded in 1994 as the primary medical institution for the military IRGC (دانشگاه علوم پزشكي سپاه پاسداران انقلاب اسلامي). The institute is operated by the IRGC, and currently trains students up to PhD levels in three Colleges, namely: "Nursing", "Allied Health", and "Medicine", as well as the "Institute of Research for Military Medicine" (پژوهشكده طب رزمي).

The university operates three hospitals, among them the large "Baqiyatallah Hospital" near Vanak, Tehran, as well as a dental hospital.
