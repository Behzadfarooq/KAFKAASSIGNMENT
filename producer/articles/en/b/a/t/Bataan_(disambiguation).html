Bataan often refers to a province of the Philippines. Other meanings, all of which were named after the province are:

  • Bataan Peninsula - the landmass on which Bataan province lies
  • Battle of Bataan - during 1942 in the Pacific War of World War II.
  • Bataan Death March - an event during World War II
  • USS Bataan - the name of two United States ships
  • Bataan - a 1943 movie
  • Back to Bataan - a 1945 movie
  • Bataan (barangay) in San Juan, Batangas
  • Bataan 1 and Bataan 2, the call signs for two disarmed Mitsubishi G4M "Betty" bombers which carried the first Japanese surrender delegations to Ie Shima as the first leg of their journey to Manila in August, 1945. [1]
