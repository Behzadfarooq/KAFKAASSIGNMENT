The Battle of West Henan-North Hubei (Traditional Chinese: 豫西鄂北會戰; Simplified Chinese: 豫西鄂北会战; pinyin: Yù Xī È Běi Huìzhàn), was one of the 22 major engagements between the National Revolutionary Army and Imperial Japanese Army during the Second Sino-Japanese War.
