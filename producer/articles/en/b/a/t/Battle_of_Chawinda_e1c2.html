Battle of Chawinda

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Battle of Chawinda was a tank battle, fought as part of the Sialkot Campaign in the Indo-Pakistani War of 1965. The battle saw huge casualties on both sides, though badly outnumbered Pakistan managed to resist the Indian thrust. This battle was the largest tank battle since Russia and Germany in World War II
