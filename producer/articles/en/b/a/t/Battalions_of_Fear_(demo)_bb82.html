Battalions of Fear was the second demo album by Blind Guardian, then going under the name Lucifer's Heritage.

[edit] Track listing

  1. "Majesty" – 7:28
  2. "Trial by the Archon" – 1:41
  3. "Battalions of Fear" – 6:06
  4. "Gandalf's Rebirth" – 2:10
  5. "Run for the Night" – 3:33

[edit] Credits

  • Hansi Kürsch - vocals & bass
  • André Olbrich - guitar
  • Christof Theissen - guitar
  • Hans-Peter Frey - drums
