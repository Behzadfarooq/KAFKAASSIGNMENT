The first Battle of St-Dizier was fought on January 26, 1814, and resulted the victory of French under Napoleon Bonaparte against Russians under General Lanskoy.

The second battle of St-Dizier was fought on March 26, 1814 and resulted the victory of French under Napoleon Bonaparte against Russians under Field Marshal Ferdinand Winzingerode. The second battle was more important than the first battle; it was to be Napoleon's next-to-last victory.
