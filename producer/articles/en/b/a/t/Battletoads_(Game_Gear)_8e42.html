Battletoads was released for Sega Game Gear in 1993. It is a port of the game Battletoads

[edit] Game play

Battletoads is a beat 'em up game from the era when the Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles were at a height of popularity. The game, while considered a classic, is sometimes criticized for being overly difficult, with cheap losses of life.

[edit] Graphics

The graphics are fair for an 8-bit game of its era, and have lots of greens and browns.
