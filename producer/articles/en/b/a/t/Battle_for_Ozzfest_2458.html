The Battle for Ozzfest was a reality TV show aired during the fall of 2004 on MTV, where 8 bands 'battle' it out in a series of challenges to win a slot on the stage of the rock festival Ozzfest, which is a primarily heavy metal-subgenre based music festival. The series lasted 12 episodes, the winner being decided by online voters.

[edit] Contestants

  • A Dozen Furies
  • Cynder (now known as Curse Your Name)
  • Manntis
  • Final Drive
  • Beyond All Reason
  • Guilt by Association
  • Sicks Deep
  • Trauma Concept

A Dozen Furies won, and got $60,000 in cash, gear from Gibson guitars, and a record deal.
