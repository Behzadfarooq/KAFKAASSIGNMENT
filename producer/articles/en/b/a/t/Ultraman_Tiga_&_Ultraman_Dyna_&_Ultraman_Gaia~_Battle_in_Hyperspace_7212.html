Ultraman Tiga & Ultraman Dyna & Ultraman Gaia: Battle in Hyperspace is a tokusatsu kaiju eiga produced by Tsuburaya Productions in 1999. It was a sequel to the previous year's Ultraman Tiga and Ultraman Dyna and became second in a succesful Ultraman series. This film is the only theatrical spinoff of the Ultraman Gaia tv series.

[edit] Plot

In a change from previous Ultraman movies, this takes place in our own universe, in which Ultraman is just a popular kids' TV show. A young boy (and Ultraman addict) named Tsutomu finds a mysterious glowing ball that tells him it can make any wish come true. The one thing Tsutomu wants more than anything else is to meet Gamu, Ultraman's human counterpart, so he can give the boy some advice on how to stand up to the school bully, Chiba. Unfortunately, the bully finds the wishing ball and wishes for a giant monster to appear and starts destroying their suburban Tokyo neighborhood. Gamu and the Science Patrol team must then find a way to open a gateway between the two universes to stop the monster and save the world we live in.

[edit] U.S. release

This movie was released on Region 1 DVD by Image Entertainment on May 14, 2002.
