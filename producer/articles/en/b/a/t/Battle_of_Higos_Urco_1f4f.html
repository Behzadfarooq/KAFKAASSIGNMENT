The battle of Higos Urco, near Chachapoyas in the Amazonas Region of Peru was part of the war for the independence of Peru. It was fought on 6 June 1821, was fought between small pro-independence and pro-Spanish forces, was part of the campaign which led to the proclamation of Peruvian independence on 28 July 1821.
