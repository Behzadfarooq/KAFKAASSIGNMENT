Battle Arena Toshinden 2 for the PlayStation is the sequel to Battle Arena Toshinden. As with its prequel it is a weapons-based 3D fighting game.

Though the core gameplay remains unchanged the game adds a simple combo system: Light Attack, Hard Attack, Special move. Though several characters have a couple of unqiue combo's that break this rule. The 3D movement has also been altered to better balance it in battle.

A version of the game was also released in arcades by Capcom with altered music.

[edit] Story

After the Himitsu Kesha discovered that Gaia organized a Toshinden tournament behind their backs in order to start a rebellion, he was branded a traitor. Hence a new tournament is organized to lure him to his doom. Meanwhile one of Gaia's former co-workers put's the next stage of their plan into action...

[edit] Other versions of Battle Arena Toshinden 2

Like Battle Arena Toshinden, Toshinden 2 was also ported to the Sega Saturn and PC.

[edit] Toshinden URA (Sega Saturn)

Toshinden URA (full title "Ultimate Revenge Attack") is a saturn specific sequel to Toshinden Remix. Though clearly based off Toshinden 2 for the PSX it was considerably altered with all new arenas, a new story and four new characters replacing four of Toshinden 2 PSX's new characters. The gameplay also differs quite a bit (it plays somewhat like Toshinden 1 with Toshinden 2's combo system).

[edit] Toshinden 2 PC (PC)

Toshinden 2 was also ported to the PC. It is a straight windows port with the ability to play in much higher resolutions than the PSX version (as high as your computer can go). It will also run at the speed of the computer meaning a slow down program is required to run it on new computers.

[edit] Toshinden 2 Plus (PSX)

A special edition of Toshinden 2 released only in Japan. It features notably enhanced graphics (in particular it uses particles instead of sprites) and improved control. It also makes a few balancing tweaks to the chracters (examples include that Fo moves forward during his mystic sphere attack and the second hit of Dukes Knight Strike is much harder to connect with). An option to save your best times, option settings and unlocked characters to memory card was also added.
