The Baths Hall is an entertainment venue in Scunthorpe, North Lincolnshire, United Kingdom. It hosts many types of live entertainment, including live music on a regular basis. Bands to have played the venue include The Damned, The Bluetones and The Levellers. The building is a former public bath house. It was renovated in 2005.
