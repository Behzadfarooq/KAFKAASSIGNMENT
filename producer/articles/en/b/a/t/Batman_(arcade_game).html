Batman is a horizontally scrolling beat 'em up and "Run and gun" arcade game released by Atari Games in 1991, produced by Numega. Batman must save Gotham City from The Joker. Features stages with the Batmobile and the Batwing. The storyline is based on the 1989 movie of the same name. The game features realistic sounds and voices from the movie.
