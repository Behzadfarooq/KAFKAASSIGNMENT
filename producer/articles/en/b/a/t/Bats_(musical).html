[edit] Synopsis

Sleepy, timeless Transylvania! Locked away in the Transylvanian Alps and somewhat removed from the rat race of our modern times.

The remote village of Humperdink in the shire of Engelberta has survived for centuries by growing grapes, and even though they can boast about Count Dracula's castle, no-one really seems to care! But this is all about to change...

The grape harvest is ruined by disease, and the town is on the verge of bankruptcy! They need outside money and they need it fast, but what have they got to give in return? Nothing! Except a lot of things that go bump in the night!
