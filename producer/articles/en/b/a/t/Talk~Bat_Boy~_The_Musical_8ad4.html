[edit] Page Cleanup

This page is getting rather out of hand. There are various references to productions, which is all well and good, but these productions have ticket sale phone numbers. Wikipedia isn't the classified section of the internet, thanks -- I've deleted the list of productions. I'm sure it's justifiable. I wouldn't have done it if it hadn't jumped out at me.

Also, I've taken issue with the overly long plot "summary". The current problem I see right now, ignoring the shoddy writing, is the paragraph breaking (it's rather complex and tediously long at the moment). When I have time, I'll be sure to change it -- though I invite anyone else who can to change it. --Ryoji.kun 03:16, 27 October 2006 (UTC)
