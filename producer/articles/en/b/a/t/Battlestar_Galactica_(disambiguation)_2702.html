Battlestar Galactica (disambiguation)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Battlestar Galactica is an American science fiction franchise.

Battlestar Galactica may also refer to:

In cinema:

  • Battlestar Galactica (film), the original 1978 cinema film
  • Battlestar Galactica: The Second Coming, a cinema trailer created to revive interest in the franchise

In television:

  • Battlestar Galactica (1978 TV series), the original television series
  • Galactica 1980, the short-lived 1980 sequel series to the original 1978 series
  • Battlestar Galactica (re-imagining), the "re-imagined" universe based on the original, containing:
    • Battlestar Galactica (TV miniseries), the three-hour miniseries
    • Battlestar Galactica (2004 TV series), the regular series that followed the miniseries
    • Battlestar Galactica (DVD movie), the forthcoming DVD film

In other media:

  • Battlestar Galactica: The Resistance, a series of short internet episodes ("webisodes") set between the end of season 2 and the beginning of season 3 of the re-imagined series
  • Battlestar Galactica (video game), the 1978 and 2003 video games and the 2007 Xbox Live Arcade game
  • Battlestar Galactica (comic book), several comic book adaptations
  • Battlestar Galactica Collectable Card Game, a card game created by WizKids based on the 2003 reimagining

Within the fictional universe created by these works:

  • Battlestar Galactica (ship), the titular ship central to the franchise
