Baton Rouge Capitals are an American soccer team, founded in 2007. The team is a member of the United Soccer Leagues Premier Development League (PDL), the fourth tier of the American Soccer Pyramid, and will make their debut in the Mid-South Division of the Southern Conference in 2007, playing against teams from Austin, Dallas, El Paso, Jackson, Laredo and New Orleans.

They will play their home games at Olympia Stadium, 8 miles outside of downtown Baton Rouge, near Baker, Louisiana.
