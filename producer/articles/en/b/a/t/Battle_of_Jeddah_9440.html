Jeddah battle 1813 have taken place in Jeddah (Cidde) , in western Arabia. Army of Tusun Pasha reached from Medina and a new army led by Mohammed Ali Pasha has arrived from egypt. egyptians have cuptured the city immediatly and the pre-Ottoman Sharif was sent to Istanbul and jailed. and few days later these forces have returned Mecca it self.
