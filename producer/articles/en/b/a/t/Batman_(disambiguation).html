Batman is a comic book superhero in the DC Comics universe who has appeared in several media, most notably:

  • Batman (comic book), published from 1940 to the present
  • Batman (comic strip), a comic strip that ran off and on in newspapers from 1943 to 1991, at first titled Batman and Robin, later titled Batman
  • Batman (serial), 1943 movie serial
    • Batman and Robin (serial), the 1949 followup.
  • Batman (TV series), 1960s television series
  • Batman (1966 film), based on the TV series
  • Batman (1989 film), a 1989 film with several sequels:
    • Batman Returns, the first sequel to the 1989 film.
    • Batman Forever, the second sequel
    • Batman & Robin (1997 film), the third and final sequel.
  • Batman (album), official soundtrack album to the 1989 film.
  • Batman: The Animated Series, a 1990s animated series, which has spawned several related series:
    • The New Batman Adventures, a sequel to the first animated series
    • Batman Beyond, an animated series starring a new Batman of the future.
  • The Batman (TV series), a 2000s animated series
  • Batman Begins, a 2005 film
    • The Dark Knight, sequel to the 2005 film
  • Batman (video game), released on many platforms

Batman may also refer to:

  • Batman (army), a soldier assigned as the personal assistant to a commissioned officer
  • Batman (truck), a monster truck that debuted in 2006
  • "Batman", a song by John Zorn from the 1989 Naked City (album)
  • Marques Houston, nicknamed "Batman", the lead singer of the American hip-hop and R&B boy band IMx, formerly Immature.
  • Batman (mass measure), used in Khanate of Kazan
  • Batman, Turkey
    • Batman Province
    • Batman River
  • John Batman, one of the founders of Melbourne, Australia
    • Batman's Treaty
    • Division of Batman, an electoral district in the Australian House of Representatives in Victoria, named after Batman.
  • Ira Coleman Batman, an American judge and politician in Indiana in the early 20th century
  • Daniel Batman, an Australian sprinter, and descendant of John Batman
  • Tro, håb og Batman (Faith, Hope and Batman), a short Danish film
  • B.A.T.M.A.N., a mesh network routing protocol
