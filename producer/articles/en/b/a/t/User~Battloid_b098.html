[edit] What is Battloid?

Battloid is a name taken from the anime series Robotech. It refers to the humanoid robotic form of the Earth's Veritech fighters used to battle the alien invaders.

[edit] Who is Battloid?

Battloid was born 1st April 1965 in Portsmouth, United Kingdom.

[edit] Where is Battloid?

Currently living in Hampstead, London NW3 in the United Kingdom. Also buying an apartment in Bangkok, Thailand.
