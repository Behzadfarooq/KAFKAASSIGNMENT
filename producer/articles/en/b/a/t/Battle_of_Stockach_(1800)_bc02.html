Battle of Stockach (1800)
Part of War of the Second Coalition
Date May 3, 1800
Location Stockach, Germany
Result French victory.
Combatants
French Consulate Austrian Empire
Commanders
General Lecourbe Prince of Lorraine
War of the Second Coalition
1st Stockach –Cassano – 1st Zürich – Montebello – Trebbia – Novi – Bergen – 2nd Zürich – Castricum – Genoa – 2nd Stockach – Marengo – Hochstadt – Hohenlinden – Copenhagen – Algeciras – Alexandria

The Battle of Stockach was fought on May 3, 1800 and resulted the victory of French under General Lecourbe against the Austrians under the Prince of Lorraine.
