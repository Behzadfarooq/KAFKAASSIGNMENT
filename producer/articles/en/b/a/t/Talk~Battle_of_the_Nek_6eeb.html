[edit] Location of Harold Rush's grave

I think Harold Rush is buried in Walker's Ridge Cemetery, not in the The Nek Cemetery as currently stated. The Commonwealth War Graves Commission thinks so too [1]. I happened to take a photo of his grave and the ones around it when I was there, and the graves to the left [2] and right [3] of his are also listed as being in Walker's Ridge, so it is unlikely to be a mistake on the part of the CWGC. Jll 15:02, 12 August 2006 (UTC)
