Total Annihilation: Battle Tactics

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Total Annihilation: Battle Tactics is the second of two expansion packs for the popular RTS computer game Total Annihilation. It was released on June 30, 1998 in the US. Its features included 100 new missions as either the Arm or Core, four new units (the Arm Stunner and Phalanx and the Core Neutron and Copperhead) and some new keyboard shortcuts. Six new multiplayer maps were included and for the first time in the TA series, some single-player missions were set on acid and crystal worlds. [1].
