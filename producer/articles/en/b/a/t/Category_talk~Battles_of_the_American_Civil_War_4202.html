[edit] Why does this category exist?

Why does this category exist? Aren't all of the Civil War battles categorized under their campaigns? This category is used by only a tiny number of battle articles, seemingly randomly selected. Hal Jespersen 16:21, 14 August 2005 (UTC)

  • The battles go in their campaign categories, the campaigns go in their theater categories, and the theaters go in this category, I guess. Not all of the battle article have been created yet, so this category also serves as a temporary category for later sorting. For some battles, it's also not known in what campaign they belong. — BRIAN0918 • 2005-08-14 16:54

I will let this lie as a resting place for non-campaign-affiliated battles, which I suppose might be useful, but will edit the pointers from some of my campaign overview articles. Hal Jespersen 17:27, 14 August 2005 (UTC)
