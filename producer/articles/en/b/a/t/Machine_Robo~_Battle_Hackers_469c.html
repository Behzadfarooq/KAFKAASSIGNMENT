Machine Robo: Battle Hackers (マシンロボ ぶっちぎりバトルハッカーズ, Machine Robo: Butchigiri Battlehackers?) is a Japanese animated television series produced by Ashi Productions. It ran on TV Tokyo from June 3rd, 1987 through December 30th 1987. It is a sequel of sorts to Machine Robo: Revenge of Cronos, though the Stōl siblings and Triple Jim are not in it. The villainous Gurendos' names and appearances are a parody of Japanese juvenile delinquent gangs. Hero R. JeTan has a "collar" reminiscent of a Japanese student's uniform.

[edit] Story

In the Year 406AE, the electronic planet B-1 was attacked by the mechanoid space gang Gurendos. To combat the gang, a rough-and-tumble bunch of Machine Robo gather to fight them.

[edit] Battle Hackers

  • R. JeTan (voice: Shinya Ōtani)
The leader of the Battle Hackers. Like his name suggests, he transforms from robot to jet to tank. Carries the "R. Bazooka" and sub-rifle.
  • Garzack (voice: Nobuyuki Furuta)
Non-transforming sub-commander, his weapon is the "Garfire SP".
  • Mach Blaster (voice: Issei Futamata)
A triple changer that can go from robot to jet to gun.
  • Drill Crusher (voice: Koichi Yamadera)
"Muscle"-type character. Can transform from robot to drill-tank to rhinoceros.
