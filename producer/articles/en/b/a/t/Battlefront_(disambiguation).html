Battlefront (disambiguation)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Battlefront can refer to:

  • A military front in warfare (see also FEBA).
  • Star Wars: Battlefront, a video game based in the Star Wars universe.
  • Star Wars: Battlefront II, its sequel.
  • A New Zealand wargaming company, makers of the Flames of War rule set and miniatures.
  • An American computer software company that makes the Combat Mission games.
