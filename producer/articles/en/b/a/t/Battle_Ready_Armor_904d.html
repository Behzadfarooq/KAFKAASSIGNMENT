Battle Ready Armor

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

This article refers to the fictional technology. For the actual type of woman's clothing, see bra.

In the context of the American animated television series Codename: Kids Next Door, BRA is an acronym which stands for Battle Ready Armor. This technology was first showcased in Operation: S.U.P.P.O.R.T. where Numbuh One believes Cree's bra is some sort of teenage technology, which turns out to be true (although the operatives don't see her suit in action until Operation: K.I.S.S.).

Battle Ready Armor can transform into flight-capable body armor, frequently seen on Cree (resembling traditional Samurai armor). It can also create a convincing disguise which can conceal gender, age, and voice (Operation: S.L.U.M.B.E.R.).

Generally, Battle Ready Armor is utilized only by teenagers. Gender is no bar to use either, as many teenage boys like Chad wear it [1].

And as shown on K.I.S.S., M.A.U.R.I.C.E. and E.L.E.C.T.I.O.N.S., they can also be used on bicycles.
