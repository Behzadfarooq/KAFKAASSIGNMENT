Battle of Beauharnois

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Patriotes Rebellion
Saint-Denis – Saint-Charles – Saint-Eustache – Beauharnois – Baker's farm – Lacolle – Odelltown

The Battle of Beauharnois was fought on November 10, 1838, between Great Britain and Canadian rebels. The city rose up following a series of raids by rebel leaders who had escaped into the United States. François-Marie-Thomas Chevalier de Lorimier commanded the ranks of the Patriote rebels. The British were victorious.
