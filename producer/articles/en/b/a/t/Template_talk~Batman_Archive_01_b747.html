Besides the creators, I added all of the really influential writers and artists on Batman (not all the famous ones, just the ones who formed Batman). Does Jeph Loeb belong on this list? While Jim Lee has pretty much defined the look of Batman in the comics of the last three or so years, Loeb has just written a lot of really good stories.   8. We don't have a section on influential people outside of the comics. I don't know what to name it, but I'd say Adam West, Paul Dini, Bruce Timm, whoever voiced Batman in the animated series, Tim Burton, Joel Schumacher, and Christopher Nolan would belong on it at least. Definitely major directors and people who have become typecast as Batman, but not the one-off actors who played Batman in various movies.
