[edit] Someone's deleting your contributions

Hi Batesy02, and thanks for your contributions to Wikipedia this week. You may have noticed that other users have been deleting your contributions to articles, such as Wonga Park, Victoria. The reason this may be occurring is that your comments are considered POV (or Point of View). At Wikipedia, we try to maintain a balanced, neutral resource. Thus, when we spot information that may be considered to be one person's point of view or opinion, we generally remove it. You might want to read Wikipedia:Neutral point of view to see how your contributions could be improved. --Cnwb 05:24, 9 November 2005 (UTC)
