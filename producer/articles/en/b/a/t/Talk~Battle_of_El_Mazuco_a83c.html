[edit] Spelling of place names

81.9.240.159 wrote:

Real name of the place is "El Mazucu". the same for "El Biforcu" and specially "Peña Blanca".
People who live in the village call it El Mazuco, and the sign outside the village spells it that way, too. And the primary reference (see references section) spells it that way too. He also uses 'la altura del Biforco' (and current maps also spell it that way). He uses both Peña Blanca (to refer to the whole hill) and Peñas Blancas (to refer to the three summits of the hill), so it would seem both are correct. mfc 16:27, 4 October 2006 (UTC)
I've changed the wording to clarify when the three summits or whole mountain is referred to. quota 11:53, 18 October 2006 (UTC)
