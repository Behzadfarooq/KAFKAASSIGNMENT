Battle of Cassano d'Adda

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Battle of Cassano or Battle of Cassano d'Adda refers to several battle fought near the city of Cassano d'Adda in Lombardy, Italy:

  • 1158 - Battle of Cassano fought by Federico Barbarossa against the Milan citizens
  • 1259 - Battle of Cassano fought by Ezzelino III da Romano and Oberto Pallavicino of Cremona against the Guelph League
  • 1705 - Battle of Cassano during the War of the Spanish Succession;
  • 1799 - Battle of Cassano during the French Revolutionary Wars; The Russians under Suvorov won over the French under Moreau
