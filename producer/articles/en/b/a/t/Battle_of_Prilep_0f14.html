Battle of Prilep

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Battle of Prilep in the First Balkan War took place on the November 3, 1912. The Serbian army encountered Ottoman army near the town of Prilep, in today Republic of Macedonia. The clash lasted for three days, and the Ottoman army was owerhelmed and forced to resumed to retreat.
