[edit] Protections

Protect is an admin-only function, much like delete and block. Adding a tag does not do anything to the page. – Chacor 12:58, 15 October 2006 (UTC)

Just to add to this, the addition of {{sprotected}} (or, for that matter {{protected}}) does not actually protect the page. The templates are used by admins, who can impose a protection on the page, to label it as protected. To reiterate, the templates are for informational purposes, and don't actually change anything in terms of page protection on the article .
