Full Auto 2: Battlelines is the second Full Auto game and is available on the PlayStation 3 and PlayStation Portable. It is developed by Pseudo Interactive on PlayStation 3 and by Deep Fried Entertainment on Playstation Portable and published by Sega.

The game was released on December 7th, 2006.[1] A demo with single and multi-player modes was released on the PlayStation Network on February 15th, 2007.

[edit] Trivia

  • There is a billboard that has an image of Sonic the Hedgehog somewhere in the Chasm Arena along with many other billboards of vehicles in the game.
  • If you look hard in Chasm arena you can find a billboard of SAGE, The AI That helps you in Career mode. However, at the last stage, SAGE is an antagonist, and SAGE controls the Executioner vehicle.
  • Some of the returning vehicles resembles real life vhicles, for example; the Sidewinder resembles the Pontiac Trans Am, the Vulcan, resembling the Dodge Challenger, the Outlaw, resembles the NASCAR version of the Ford Taurus and the Honcho resembles the Cadillac Escalade.
  • One of the dragon decals is called "Burninator" in a reference to the character Trogdor the Burninator from www.homestarrunner.com.
