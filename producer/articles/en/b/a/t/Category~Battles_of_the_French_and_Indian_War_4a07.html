This category contains historical battles fought as part of the French and Indian War (the North American theater of the Seven Years' War) (1754–1763). Please see the category guidelines for more information.

  • For the European theater, see: Category:Battles of the Seven Years' War.
  • This was the last of a series of conflicts referred to as the French and Indian Wars.
