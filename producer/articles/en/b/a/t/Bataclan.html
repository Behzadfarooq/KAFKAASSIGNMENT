"Bataclan" is a digital single made available for sale on Prince's website on 29 March 2004. The single consists of a live performance of 2 songs: "NPG In This Funky House", which segues into "All The Critics Love U In Paris", which was an update of "All The Critics Love U In New York", from the album 1999. The tracks were performed live with the New Power Generation at the Bataclan nightclub in Paris, France.
