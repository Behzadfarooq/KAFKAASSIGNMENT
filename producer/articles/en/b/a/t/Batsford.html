Batsford is a village and civil parish in the Cotswold district of Gloucestershire, England. According to the 2001 census it had a population of 99. The village is about 1Â½ miles north-west of Moreton-in-Marsh. There is a Falconry close to the village and Batsford Arboretum is nearby, situated on the Cotswold scarp.
