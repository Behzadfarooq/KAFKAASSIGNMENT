Battle of Faventia (542)
Part of Gothic War (535-552)
Date Spring 542
Location Faenza, Italy
Result decisive Ostrogothic victory
Combatants
Ostrogoths Byzantine Empire
Commanders
Totila
Strength
5,000 12,000

In the spring of 542, at the Battle of Faventia (modern Faenza), an Ostrogothic army scattered the larger Byzantine army in Italy and temporarily reversed the Byzantine conquest of Italy.
