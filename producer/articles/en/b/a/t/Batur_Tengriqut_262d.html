Mao-Tun Baatur, Bator, Baghadur

(Batur Tengriqut, Bator Tengrikhan) the son of Tou-Man. The founder of the great asian Hun Empire.

Touman was the earliest known Xiongnu chanyu, reigning from 220 BC to 209 BC. During his reign, he united the nomadic tribes living in Mongolia and invaded Northern China.
