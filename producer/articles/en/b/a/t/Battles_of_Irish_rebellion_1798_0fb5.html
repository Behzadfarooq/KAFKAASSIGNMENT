This page lists the principal engagements of the Irish Rebellion of 1798. Some of these "battles" could be more accurately termed massacres and are denoted as such by the "+" symbol.


  • 24 May 1798 - Ballymore-Eustace, Naas, Prosperous, Kilcullen
  • 25 May 1798 - Carlow + Carnew +
  • 26 May 1798 - Tara Hill
  • 27 May 1798 - Oulart Hill
  • 28 May 1798 - Enniscorthy, Newtownmountkennedy
  • 29 May 1798 - Gibbet Rath +
  • 30 May 1798 - Three Rocks
  • 1 June 1798 - Bunclody (Newtownbarry)
  • 4 June 1798 - Tuberneering
  • 5 June 1798 - New Ross, Scullabogue +
  • 7 June 1798 - Antrim
  • 9 June 1798 - Arklow, Saintfield
  • 13 June 1798 - Ballynahinch
  • 18 June 1798 - Ovidstown
  • 20 June 1798 - Foulksmills
  • 21 June 1798 - Vinegar Hill
  • 30 June 1798 - Ballyellis
  • 27 August 1798 - Castlebar
  • 5 September 1798 - Collooney
  • 8 September 1798 - Ballinamuck
  • 23 September 1798 - Killala

1798 in County Mayo

  • 22nd August - battle of Killala; battle of Moyne Friary; battle of Rosserk Friary
  • 26th August - battle of Mount Burren (skirmish)
  • 27th August - Castlebar (The Races of Castlebar)
  • 4th September - battle of Tobercurry
  • 5th September - Collooney
  • 7th September - Ballinamuck
  • 11th September - Second Battle of Castlebar
  • 21st September - battle of Scurmore
  • 23rd September - Second Battle of Killala
