Battle of Kirk Kilisse

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

First Balkan War
Sarantaporo - Giannitsa - Kumanovo - Kirk Kilisse - Pente Pigadia - Prilep - Lule-Burgas - Vevi - Bitola - Elli - Adrianople - Lemnos - Bizani

The Battle of Kirk Kilisse was part of the First Balkan War.

It took place on 24 October 1912, when the Bulgarian army defeated an Ottoman army in Eastern Thrace.
