[edit] Reception

Maybe it's just me, but the 'reception' part of this article seems to be, if not completely fabricated, then extremely biased. A Metacritic rating of 79 (plus a user average of 9.1) and an Amazon average user rating of 4/5 for PS2 and 3.5/5 Xbox would suggest to me that the game wasn't poorly received but, instead, was actually quite warmly received by both critics and gamers. Now maybe a certain number of PC Battlefield 1942 fans were unhappy with it, but as this was changed to be a more console friendly experience I really don't see how that viewpoint is more valid than general critical reception. Just a thought. 88.104.46.27 00:37, 2 March 2007 (UTC)

[edit] About the online experience

This game's one of the best FPS for the PS2 but it is plagued with Glitches and What we call RankWhores. It is a very Fun game to play and Many Clans out there. 24 players online(12 on 12)CTF(Capture the Flag) & Conquest for those of you who are new to Conquest mode I'll explain as best as I can; You have a Set of Flags in certain Key areas in the game where all the flags start off as neutralized your team has to basically run around and get them all, But it's not as easy as capture the flag cause you have to remain in that spot as the Flag meter moves up and you finally capture it(The more teammates on the flag the Faster you'll capture it). Conquest games are decided by tickets the team with the most of the tickets at the end of the game is the Victor. In some stages if you capture every single Flag the game os over and your team wins. Now, Teamkilling is ugly in this game(The no Friendly Fire option does not exist) and as some other FPS games The team has to vote to kick the person out, TK's tend to Vote against the whole team to even the odds but it makes no difference. Glitchers Are another issue in this game, Wall Glitches are the biggest part of Glitching in this game, Enemies can shoot you through the wall but you cannot shoot at them, They're Basically invincible. Weapon Glitches(Don't ask me about those), Chopper Glitches and even flying glitches done with Vehicles(Airwalk Glitch). The Servers Suck badly, Sometimes it will Delete your Clan, Delete your Stats and Clan Servers are down most of the time so to start Clan matches Kinda sucks but besides that this game is pretty Cool. Vehicular Mayhem and Good Gameplay. The first time you play it you'll be hooked but the PC version is way better.
