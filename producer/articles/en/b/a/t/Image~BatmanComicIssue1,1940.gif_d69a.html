Batman Comic Book: No. 1 Spring Issue, 1940. Item from Record Group 21: Records of District Courts of the United States, 1685 - 1991. This comic book was an exhibit in the case titled Fox Publications Inc. v. Detective Comics Inc., Independent News Co. Inc. and Interborough News Co. From the National Archives.

This image comes from the National Archives and Records Administration, the vast majority of whose images and documents are in the public domain in the United States. See the NARA FAQ and Conditions of use.
