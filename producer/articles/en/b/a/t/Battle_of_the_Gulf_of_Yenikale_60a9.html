The Battle of the Gulf of Yenikale was a naval battle fought between the Russian Empire and the Austrian Empire during the Russo-Turkish War of 1787-1792. The Russian navy was led by admiral Admiral Fyodor Ushakov. The battle ended in the withdrawal of both fleets without any decisive result.
