The Battle of Keresztes or Battle of Mezokeresztes (Mezökeresztes) took place on October 24-26, 1596, between a combined Habsburg-Transylvanian force and the Ottoman Empire, near the village of Mezokeresztes in northern Hungary. The battle ended in an unexpected Ottoman victory.
