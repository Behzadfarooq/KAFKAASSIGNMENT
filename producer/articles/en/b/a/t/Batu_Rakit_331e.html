Batu Rakit

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Batu Rakit is a small town in Terengganu, Malaysia. It has been recognized by travellers that the women from this small town are beautiful, especially the women from the Mukhtar family and mohd nor family from kampung padang kemunting.
