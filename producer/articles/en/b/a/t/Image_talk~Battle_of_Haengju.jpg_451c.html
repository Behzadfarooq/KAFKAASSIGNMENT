I have deleted this image because there is still no information on the author (painter). Regardless of whether public domain or fair use is claimed, it must be verifiably shown who the author is. If the author died in or before 1935, or if the painting was first published in or before 1922, it's public domain. Otherwise, the painting is copyrighted and can only be used under a "fair use" claim, including rationale. If anyone can confirm who the painter is and what his dates are, leave me a note on my talk page. Until then, the image is deleted. User:Angr 10:44, 15 August 2006 (UTC)
