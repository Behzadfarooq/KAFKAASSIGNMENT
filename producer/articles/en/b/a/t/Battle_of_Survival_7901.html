Bos Wars (Formally known as Battle of Survival) is a free cross-platform real time strategy game developed by Tina Peterson Jensen and continued by François Beerten at Jenson's unfortunate passing.

Battle of Survival was also the first Original and independent game to be based on the Stratagus engine since the earlier projects had been based off of Warcraft.

[edit] History

Battle of Survival 2.3 screenshot

Originally released in 2006, Battle of Survival was the brain child of Tina Peterson Jensen. It was an original game being developed along side the Stratagus engine.

In 2006, Jensen passed away and François Beerten and team continued to develop the game in memory of the original creator.

[edit] Plot Synopsis

Spoiler warning: Plot and/or ending details follow.
Battle of Survival 2.3 screenshot

The story takes place in the distant future where Earth has become an Empire and you play the last surviving free leader of the world. The might of the Elite Empire is bearing down on the player and only his decisions will stop the extermination of the last free men, women, and children on the whole planet.

[edit] Missions

Battle of Survival 2.3 screenshot


Mission#1: Last Stand For Freedom!

The First mission opens up with you as the last leader of the Free humans and Imperial General Szarin has found you out and plans to crush you. It is a defense at all costs because if you fail, then the end of all resistance against the Elites would be over.

Mission#2: Mercenaries

Successfully stopping General Szarin has given you're army a breather, but others are interested in expanding their influence and Green Valley harbors Mercenaries who have their own supplies. The Elites are also coming after you again and so you must use the Merc camp to your advantage by seizing their supplies and holding off till you can get established.
