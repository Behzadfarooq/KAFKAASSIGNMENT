[edit] Speedy Deletion of Category:Battles of the War of 1812 (Northern Theaters)

We already have Category:Battles of the War of 1812. I think that would be adequate Battlefield 13:39, 1 February 2006 (UTC)

Delete. I'm not sure why this category was created, but the war along the U.S.-Canadian border was the main theatre of the war, and so almost all of the battles in the main category Category:Battles of the War of 1812 were in the northern theatre. This means that this category duplicates the main category, with the primary exception being the six actions of the Chesapeake campaign. The Chesapeake campaign could be put into its own sub-category, perhaps, but there's no reason for a sub-category for the northern theatre, which should be the "default" category. --Kevin Myers | (complaint dept.) 15:49, 1 February 2006 (UTC)

DELETE duplicate category not needed. SirIsaacBrock 21:13, 1 February 2006 (UTC)

Empty this category first. enochlau (talk) 23:42, 1 February 2006 (UTC)
