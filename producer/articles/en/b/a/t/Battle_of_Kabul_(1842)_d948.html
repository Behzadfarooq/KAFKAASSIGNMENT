Battle of Kabul (1842)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

You have new messages (last change).

The Battle of Kabul was fought on between August to October, 1842 between British and Afghan forces. The British re-invaded Afghanistan to avenge the shattering defeat at Gandamak. The British won the battle.
