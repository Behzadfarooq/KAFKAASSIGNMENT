Battle of Gemauerthof was a battle in the Great Nordic War. The Swedes under Adam Ludwig Lewenhaupt fought the Russians under Boris Sheremetyev. The Swedes were victorius, but the vicory was only symbolic. In August, the Russians conquered Courland.
