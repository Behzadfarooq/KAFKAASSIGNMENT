The Batalion Zośka (named after Tadeusz Zawadzki) was a Scouting battalion of the Armia Krajowa, mainly consisted by members of the Szare Szeregi, which took part in the Warsaw Uprising of 1944. It was one of the Batalions of the Radosław Regiment. Other famous Batalions was: Miotła, Czata, Pięść and Parasol.
