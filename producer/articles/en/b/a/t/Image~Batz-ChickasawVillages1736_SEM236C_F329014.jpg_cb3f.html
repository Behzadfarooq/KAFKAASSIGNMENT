[edit] Summary

Plan et Scituation des Villages Tchikachas Plan and Situation of the Chickasaw Villages, Alexandre de Batz, 1737. French copy of a map made by a visitor to the Chickasaw. A indicates 'Ogoula Tchetoka, fort where M. d'Artaguette attacked', 4 mi SSE of present day Pontotoc, Mississippi on 25 March, 1736. M indicates Ackia, at present day Tupelo, Mississippi, Bienville's point of attack on May 26, 1736. http://bd.archivescanadafrance.org/acf/navimages/FRDAFANCAOM/FRCAOM_F3290_14/DAFCAOM03_F3290001401_H.jpg

Free use and distribution according to the archival description. http://bd.archivescanadafrance.org/acf/view.xsp?qid=sdx_q0&p=1&b=acfdb&id=FRCAOM_F3290_14

This map is described by Gregory A. Waselkov in Indian Maps of the Southeast, which is published in Powhatan's Mantle: Indians in the Colonial Southeast, ISBN 08032986 pp. 483-486
