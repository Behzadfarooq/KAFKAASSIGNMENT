Real World/Road Rules Challenge: Battle of the Sexes II

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Battle of the Sexes II premiered in 2004 and took place in Santa Fe, New Mexico. Before every mission, each team would choose 3 leaders. If the team won, those leaders got to vote off anyone else; if the team lost, one of those leaders would be voted off. No life-shields were used during this season.

[edit] Final results

Dan, Eric and Theo are the final three remaining men. Arissa, Coral and Sophia are the final three remaining women. The men win the final challenge.

[edit] Memorable moments

  • After being voted off, Ayanna delivered an unintentionally humorous dramatic diatribe.
  • Mike was voted off for the first time ever.
  • Steven slapped teammate Shane during a tense confrontation during a Challenge, violating the rule against physical violence, and is only the second person to ever be sent home from a Challenge. Steven apologized to Shane immediately after the incident, but was still sent home, much to Shane's upset. Ayanna was sent home in the Extreme Challenge.
  • There was controversy on the women's team when Cynthia was voted off.
  • Tonya and Tina had a short-lived argument.
  • The men's team deliberately lost a mission, choosing to sacrifice Mark in order to guarantee Arissa, whom they considered weak a spot in the final challenge, in order to ensure victory.
  • Mark, Eric and Dan stepped up to be leaders one mission to protect their alliance and eliminate Brad.
  • There was much drama surrounding Angela during her entire time on the show.
  • Mark began a relationship with Robin and possibly Tonya. This laid the seed for the intense animosity that Robin expressed toward Tonya in the The Inferno II.
  • Kina and Randy began dating after the show.
