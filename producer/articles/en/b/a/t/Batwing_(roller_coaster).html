Batwing is a steel roller coaster at Six Flags America in Prince George's County, Maryland. Riders are positioned as if they were taking a flight with the Dark Knight. The ride has three trains with six cars per train. A total of twenty four guests can ride in each train arranged four across in each car.

[edit] Trivia

  • The Batwing is themed to Batman's personal fighter jet, the Batwing.
  • An exact copy of this ride exists at Kings Island named Firehawk.
