[edit] Power Rangers get it first?

Why is it that the Power Rangers get the battlizer first, then the Super Sentai, if they choose, adopt it? I have a hard time following the logic. Signed by BigMac1212

I have a hard time following your statements, though I'll try my best to answer. In terms of credit, the "Battle Riser" and Bandai of America (commonly appreviated as "BoA" due in part to its less than savory reputation amongst fans) gave Saban the idea to give Power Rangers the "Battlizer". The idea came from no one source and numerous ones can be mentioned as a partial insperation.

As for Super Sentai using the finished "Battlizer" from SPD, that was one of the many products of Disney being in bed, so to spoke, with Toei. So, technically, that's no one company's fault, either. It resulted in mixed feelings, to be sure, but it wasn't just a rare case of "Super Sentai copying Power Rangers" or "Disney getting their way". And, despite what many japanese fans may believe, I'm sure both sides benefit.

Anyway, I hope that answers your question(s).

Oh! And before I forget, you can type out four "~" a the end of your comments to activate the automatic signing feature. It really helps. ACS (Wikipedian) 03:04, 6 May 2006 (UTC)
