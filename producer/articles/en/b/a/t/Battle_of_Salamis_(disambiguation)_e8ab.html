Battle of Salamis (disambiguation)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Battle of Salamis may mean:

  • Battle of Salamis, naval battle between the Greek city-states and Persia, fought in September, 480 BC
  • Battle of Salamis in Cyprus (450 BC) was a simultaneous land and sea battle of the Greco-Persian Wars.
  • Battle of Salamis in Cyprus (306 BC), naval battle that took place in 306 BC near Salamis, Cyprus between the fleets of Ptolemy I of Egypt and Demetrius
