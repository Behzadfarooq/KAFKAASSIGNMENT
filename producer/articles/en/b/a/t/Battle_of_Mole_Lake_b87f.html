Battle of Mole Lake

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Battle of Mole Lake 1806 was a battle between Sioux and Chippewa warriors fought over ancient wild rice beds which exist to this day in Forest County north east Wisconsin, just south of Crandon in the community of Mole Lake, home to the Sokaogon Chippewa Community.
