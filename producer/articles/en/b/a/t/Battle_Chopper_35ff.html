Battle Chopper (Mr Heli no Daibouken in Japan} is a horizontally scrolling shoot 'em up arcade game released by Irem in 1987. You control a fighter chopper and shoot enemies, collect power-ups, and defeat bosses to advance levels.

The Mr. Heli ship used by the player makes an appearance in R-Type Final as a playable ship.
