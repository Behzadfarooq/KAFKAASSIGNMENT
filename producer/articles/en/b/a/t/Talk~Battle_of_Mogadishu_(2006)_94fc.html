[edit] Name

Whaere exactly does the name "Second Battle of Mogadishu" come from? There has been huge amounts of fighting in Mogadishu both before and after the so-called "Battle of Mogadishu"– distinguished in Western journalism because it involved Americans. I don't see any particular historical connection between the recent fighting and the "Battle of Mogadishu"– has this term been used in other sources? If we're looking for something more descriptive, we could go with something like 2006 Mogadishu violence or Islamic Courts Union capture of Mogadishu.--Pharos 08:52, 16 June 2006 (UTC)
