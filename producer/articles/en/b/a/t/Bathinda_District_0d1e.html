Bathinda District is in Punjab, India. The districts encompasses an area of 3,344 square kilometres. It is bounded by Faridkot District on the north, Mukatsar District on the west, Sangrur and Mansa districts on the east, and the state of Haryana on the south. Bathinda is cotton producing belt of Punjab.
