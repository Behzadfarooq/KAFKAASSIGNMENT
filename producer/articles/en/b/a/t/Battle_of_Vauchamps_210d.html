A memorial column at Vauchamps (Marne), France

The Battle of Vauchamps, the final major engagement of the Six Days Campaign of the Napoleonic Wars, was fought on February 14, 1814 and resulted in 18,000 French under Napoleon defeating 30,000 Prussian troops. The Prussians lost 9,000 men, including 5,000 prisoners, against 800 French casualties.
