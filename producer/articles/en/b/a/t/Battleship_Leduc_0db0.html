Albert "Battleship" Leduc (born November 22, 1902 in Valleyfield, Quebec) (Died July 31, 1990 in Montreal, Quebec) was a Canadian ice hockey defenseman.

Albert played in the National Hockey League from 1925 to 1935. During this time, he played for the New York Rangers, Ottawa Senators, and Montreal Canadiens. While with the Habs, he won two Stanley Cups.
