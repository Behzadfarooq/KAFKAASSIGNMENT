Batizovský štít from Batizovské pleso lake
Elevation 2,448 metres (8,711 feet)
Location Slovakia
Range Tatra Mountains (Carpathians)
Coordinates 49°09′55″N, 20°07′26″E
First ascent 1900 by Karol Jurzyca

Batizovský štít is a mighty double-peak 2,448 metres high mountain in the High Tatras in Slovakia. It is named after the village of Batizovce, what refers to period until 1947 when it lied within its cadastral area.

It isn't accessible for tourists by the marked footpath, which however passes around Batizovské pleso lake under the mountain.
