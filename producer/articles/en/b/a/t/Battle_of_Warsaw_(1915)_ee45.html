Poniatowski Bridge, blown up by the retreating Russian Army in 1915 only months after it's grand opening.

The Battle of Warsaw in 1915 resulted in the defeat of the Russian Army by the Germans and its withdrawal from Poland. It was the culmination of the setbacks suffered by Russia on the Eastern Front that year.


Eastern Front
Stalluponen – Gumbinnen – Tannenberg – 1st Lemberg – Krasnik – 1st Masurian Lakes – Przemyśl – Vistula River – Łódź – Bolimov – 2nd Masurian Lakes – Gorlice-Tarnów – Warsaw – Lake Naroch – Brusilov Offensive – Kerensky Offensive
