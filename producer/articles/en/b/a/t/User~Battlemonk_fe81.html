Battlemonk is a Secret Celtic Battlemonk of the Irish order. He can usually be found serving one of many kinds of indigenous alcoholic beverages, hunting demons, or staring at customers.

How came about the username? Well, it all started (as it often does) with an RPG concept: be whatever kind of character you wanted to be. So, from the origin story of a friend's character in a different game came the concept of an order of Secret Celtic Battlemonks living in North Dakota (what they were doing there I will never know.) I made the character with little direction and no purpose: he is now a fixture in the game world, tending bar at the main hangout for all characters.

I tend to be (myself) a rather strict grammarian and orthographer, so if you want an article revised for grammar, spelling, and so forth, please feel free to drop me a note.
