Batman, a fictional superhero appearing in publications by DC Comics, has appeared in numerous filmed works, with a variety of casts. The list below presents the casts of motion pictures and television series in which Batman was the eponymous starring character.

Batman (TV): Guest appearances and episodes

[edit] The Adventures of Batman and Robin the Boy Wonder (1968-1969)

Episodes from this series were originally packaged as part of The Batman/Superman Hour. Soule and Kasem later reprised their roles as Batman and Robin for The New Scooby-Doo Movies and various incarnations of Super Friends.

  • Olan Soule - Batman/Bruce Wayne, Alfred Pennyworth
  • Casey Kasem - Robin/Dick Grayson
  • Jane Webb - Batgirl/Barbara Gordon, Catwoman
  • Larry Storch - The Joker
  • Ted Knight - Narrator, Commissioner Gordon, Penguin, Riddler

[edit] The New Adventures of Batman (1977-1978)

Episodes from this series were repackaged as part of the Batman/Tarzan Adventure Hour and Batman and the Super 7.

  • Adam West - Batman/Bruce Wayne
  • Burt Ward - Robin/Dick Grayson
  • Melendy Britt - Batgirl/Barbara Gordon
  • Lou Scheimer - Bat-mite
  • Lennie Weinrib - other voices

[edit] Batman: The Animated Series (1992-1995, 1997-1999)

The series' title changed to The Adventures of Batman and Robin and later continued as The New Batman Adventures. The characters' continuity carries on in The New Batman/Superman Adventures, Justice League, and Justice League Unlimited, with Kevin Conroy and others continuing to voice their roles from Batman: The Animated Series.
