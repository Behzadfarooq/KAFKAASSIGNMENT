Batman/Aliens

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Batman/Aliens is a fictional crossover between the Batman and Aliens comic book franchises. It was published in 1998. A sequel was released in 2003.

The Cover to the first Batman/Aliens graphic novel.

[edit] Plot

[edit] Batman/Aliens

Batman and a handful of soldiers have to travel to Mayan ruins between Guatemala and Mexico to prevent an Alien outbreak.

[edit] Batman/Aliens 2

In 1927 an explorer discovered something frozen in the Alaskan ice and he brought it home with him to Gotham where he sealed himself in a lab. It remained undiscovered until a present day construction crew broke it open to find the explorer torn apart.

Dr. Fortune, an army scientist, soon gets wind of this and hatches a plan to combine the aliens with Arkham Asylum to create a xenomorph/supervillain hybrid capable of keeping the superheroes in check.
