Batool Cheema, born January 1, 1984, is a Pakistani model and the second Miss Pakistan World winner. She was crowned in April 2004 at age 19. After crowning, she has been an emcee for various events and her speaking abilities have landed her roles on Canadian TV as a VJ.

Cheema has appeared in several South Asian magazines, as well as appearing on the cover of the Miss Pakistan World 2006 magazine. She has hosted Mirchi, a South Asian television show which aired for 3 months. Batool has also modelled for Age Studio and Salon, leading photography studios in Toronto, Canada.

A mathematics student at the University, Batool has dedicated her time to charity, education, and community work. Batool is currently continuing with her studies and focusing on graduating. Batool has a passion for wild sports and motorbikes - she acquired her license in 2005.
