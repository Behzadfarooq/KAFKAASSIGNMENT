Battle of Damghan
Date 1729
Location Mehmandust near Damghan
Result Persian victory
Combatants
Persia Afghans
Commanders
Nadir Shah Ashraf Ghilzai

The Battle of Damghan was fought in 1729 between Persian and Afghan forces near city of Damghan. The Persians were led by Nadir Shah, while the Afghans were led by Ashraf Ghilzay. The battle followed by another one in Murcheh-Khort a village near Isfahan. The Nadir's force was victorious in both which led him to remove Ghalzai tribe from throne.
