Turok: Battle of the Bionosaurs is a video game for the Game Boy Color. It was originally released in 1997, and is part of the Turok video games franchise. It was intended to be a companion to the Nintendo 64 game Turok: Dinosaur Hunter.[2] It includes eight stages and a password system.
