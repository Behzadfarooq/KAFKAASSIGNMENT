Batman Chronicles

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Batman Chronicles is a series of comics published by DC Comics from 1995 to 2000, which lasted 23 quarterly issues. With a larger page count than typical comics of the time, most issues contained 3 separate stories, with Batman usually featuring in at least one of these. Other stories featured Batman's friends and foes. The additional 4 issues of a quarterly title allowed DC to, along with the 4 existent monthly titles, publish a Batman comic every week of the year.

The Batman Chronicles is also the name of a series of trade paperbacks intended to reprint all Batman stories chronologically from his earliest appearances. Two volumes have been published to date. A third volume will be available in May.

The Batman Chronicles: Volume One collects material from: Detective Comics # 27-38 and Batman #1 The Batman Chronicles: Volume Two collects material from: Detective Comics #39-45, Batman #1-3 and New York World's Fair Comics #2.
