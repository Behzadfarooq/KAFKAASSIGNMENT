The naval Battle of Elba (or Battle of Monte Cristo) was a tough battle which took place on 6 September 1652 between a Dutch fleet under Johan van Galen and an English fleet under Richard Badiley. The English were trying to reach Livorno, where a squadron under Henry Appleton was. The captured ship, Phoenix, was recaptured 2 months later during a night raid at Livorno.
