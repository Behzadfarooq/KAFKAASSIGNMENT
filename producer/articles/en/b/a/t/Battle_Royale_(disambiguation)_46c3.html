Battle Royale may refer to:

  • Battle Royale, a novel written by Koushun Takami in 1999

Adaptations of the novel:

  • Battle Royale (film), a film adaptation directed by Kinji Fukasaku in 2000
    • Battle Royale II: Requiem, the 2003 sequel to the film
  • Battle Royale (manga), a manga series adaptation written by Koushun Takami and Masayuki Taguchi in 2000
    • Battle Royale II: Blitz Royale, the sequel to the manga

Also:

  • Battle Royale, a special unserialized issue ("Sonic Super Special") of the Sonic the Hedgehog comic book series by Archie Comics
  • Battle Royale, a collection of cards from the trading card game Magic: The Gathering
  • The Battle Royale, a band
