Battleaxe (Anita Ehren) is a fictional character in the Marvel Comics Universe who first appeared in The Thing #33 (March 1986).

[edit] Fictional character biography

An unlimited class wrestler, Battleaxe is a massive woman that carries an axe as her weapon of choice. Defeating Titania in a wrestling match, she claimed the title as champion of the Grapplers. However, when Titania was slain by the Scourge of the Underworld, Battleaxe vowed to avenge her former teammate. She took out her aggression on The Thing, battling him in a wrestling match. The Thing realized Battleaxe was taking her anger out on him, and purposely lost the match. She later joined Superia's Femizons and battled Captain America. She also battled B.A.D. Girls, Inc. while in a costumed bar.

[edit] Powers and abilities

Battleaxe has superhuman strength and durability. She carries a set of two axes which are her weapons of choice.
