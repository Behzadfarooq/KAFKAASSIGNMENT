[edit] Plot summary

Jackie warns Donna that Eric will never be her boyfriend if she keeps beating him when they play one-on-one basketball.

Spoiler warning: Plot and/or ending details follow.

Since Red got transferred to a part-time job, he has been using his spare time to fix things around the house, making Kitty uncomfortable. Meanwhile at a basketball game, Donna demonstrates her abilities over Eric, who loses the game. For that reason, Jackie tells Donna that Eric will never be her boyfriend if she keeps winning over him.

Kelso continues to act like a "whipped pig" to Jackie much to Hyde and Fez's ridicule when they urge him to be a man and stand up to her overbearing nature.

While Red continues to 'fix' things around the house, Donna (determined to let Eric win) asks him to play air hockey with her. After Eric's first score Donna loses focus and beats him again.

In another attempt, Donna clearly allows Eric to win a basketball match in his driveway, causing them to fight. By the advice of Kitty, Eric and Donna play basketball one more time without keeping scores, this time having fun and wrestling at the end. Donna has to leave and when she does, Red reveals himself from the garage and 'teaches' Eric how to play the game/win against girls.
