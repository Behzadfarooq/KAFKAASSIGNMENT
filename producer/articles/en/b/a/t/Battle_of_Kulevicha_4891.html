The Battle of Kulevicha was fought during the Russo-Turkish War, 1828-1829 on June 11, 1829 between Russia and the Ottoman Empire.

Russo–Turkish War, 1828–1829
Navarino – Varna – Kulevicha – Akhalzic

The Russians were led by Hans Karl von Diebitsch, while the Ottomans were led by Reshid Pasha to the relief of Varna. The Russians were victorious.
