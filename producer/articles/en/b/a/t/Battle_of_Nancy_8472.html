Battle of Nancy

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Battle of Nancy was the final and decisive battle of the Burgundy Wars, fought outside the walls of Nancy, France on 5 January 1477 between Charles the Bold, Duke of Burgundy, and René, Duke of Lorraine. René's forces won the battle, and Charles' mutilated body was found three days later.
