Battery Check is a single released on July 3, 2003 by the Swedish punk-rock band Millencolin comprising of the title track "Battery Check" from the full length album Home from Home along with non-album tracks "E20 Norr" ("Battery Check" sung in Swedish) and "Bowmore". The single E20 Norr, contains the same songs but with tracks 1 and 2 switched.
