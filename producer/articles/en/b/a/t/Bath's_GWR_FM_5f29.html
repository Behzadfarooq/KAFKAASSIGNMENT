GWR FM is a UK radio station that serves Bath. It broadcasts on 103 FM, on DAB Digital radio and online [2]. The station plays top 40 and Hot Adult contemporary music targeting 15-35 year old adults. Is main local competitor is Bath FM.

[edit] History

In 1986 a Bath ILR licence was awarded by the IBA. GWR Radio Bath debuted on 22 May 1987. To this day the station shares much of its programming with GWR Bristol, these programs are now network across what is now The One Network with local programming is installed during peak listening hours. However for official licencing purposes GWR Bath and Bristol are listed as one station and audience figures from RAJAR are combined with GWR Swindon. All programming for GWR Bath comes from studios in Bristol.
