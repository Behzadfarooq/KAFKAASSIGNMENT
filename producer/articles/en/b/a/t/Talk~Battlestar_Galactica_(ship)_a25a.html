[edit] Crew size

I removed the reference to an "exceptionally" large crew because it does not fit with what has been seen in the series. The series has given the ship a crew compliment of about 2500, and although that seems large, it is actually quite small given the 1200+m (4000+ft) length of the ship. By comparison, a Nimitz class aircraft carries is a bit over 300m (1000ft) long, a fiftieth of the overall size, and has a crew compliment of nearly 6000. —The preceding unsigned comment was added by 61.207.104.115 (talk • contribs) 2006 February 26.

I think this page should be split between the two different series. They exist in separate universes. One series should not be attached to the other. —The preceding unsigned comment was added by Bijou88 (talk • contribs) 2006 June 30.

[edit] Ship age

The original text stated:

The Battlestar Galactica was built 500 yahren before the close of the Thousand Yahren War (and the start of the TV series).

Given that a yahren is a year, it seems suspicious. I also think that from the text below:

At the time of the Cylon Attack, Galactica was 50 years old and she was undergoing formal decommissioning from the Colonial fleet following her retirement as an operational vessel.

As such, I edited the first sentence to read that the Galactica was built 50 years before the close. —The preceding unsigned comment was added by 24.166.178.14 (talk • contribs) 2006 September 19.

Remember that they are two different series. The original numbers were correct - the 1978 Galactica was 500 yahren old, the new one is 50 years old. Denorios 22:24, 31 October 2006 (UTC)


[edit] Viper classes

I have recorrected previous information I had about a Viper hanging in Joe's Bar. I had correctly labeled the Viper as a Mark II and an editor changed it to a Mark I. It is a Mark II, and there are only Mark IIs and VIIs in the new series. It is also verifable in the RDM podcast that the hanging vessel is a Mark II. Please do not change it back to Mark I. Thanks for the consideration in this. Kerusso 18:30, 2 March 2007 (UTC)
