Battle of the Philippines

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Several battles and wars have been fought in the Philippines:

  • The battle of Manila Bay on 1898-05-01 in the Spanish-American War.
  • The Philippine-American War of 1899–1913.
  • The invasion by Japan in 1942 — see Battle of the Philippines (1941-42) — including the battles of Bataan and Corregidor.
  • The Allied re-capture of the Philippines in 1944–1945, including the battles of Leyte, Leyte Gulf, Luzon, and Mindanao.
