[edit] Opinions on Moving Article

I think the main article should be at "The Battlefield Band" — which is the full name of the group — and that the redirect page should be here. Anyone want to weigh in before I do anything? - Anna Kucsma 19:56, 22 June 2006 (UTC)

I disagree - the official name is just "Battlefield Band" without the article. All their albums (with the possible exception of some of the early ones) and their official website omit the The. A site search of said website does bring up just 15 results for The Battlefield Band, but almost all of them are from reviews. Peruse the website, meanwhile, and you'll find only references along the lines of "Named after the Glasgow suburb of Battlefield, ... Battlefield Band have been on the world's roads for just on 30 years now". -- Blisco 22:45, 22 June 2006 (UTC)
Hmmm.... Odd that their name doesn't include the article; the "Band" part of the name makes it sound like it needs a definite article. - Anna Kucsma 15:09, 23 June 2006 (UTC)
