Battlebowl was a one-time professional wrestling pay-per-view produced by World Championship Wrestling. It was made up of tag team matches in which the partners were randomly drawn in a "Lethal Lottery" and a team had to work together to advance to the Battlebowl Battle Royal. Previous versions of Battlebowl had taken place at Starrcade 1991 and 1992. It took place on November 20, 1993 from the Pensacola Civic Center in Pensacola, Florida.

[edit] Results

  • Vader and Cactus Jack (w/Harley Race) defeated Charlie Norris and Kane (7:34)
    • Vader pinned Norris.
  • Brian Knobbs and Johnny B. Badd (w/Missy Hyatt) defeated Erik Watts and Paul Roma (12:56)
    • Knobbs pinned Watts.
  • The Shockmaster and Paul Orndorff (w/The Masked Assassin) defeated Ricky Steamboat and Lord Steven Regal (w/Sir William) (12:26)
    • Shockmaster pinned Regal.
  • King Kong and Dustin Rhodes defeated The Equalizer and Awesome Kong (5:55)
    • Rhodes pinned Awesome Kong.
  • Sting and Jerry Sags (w/Missy Hyatt) defeated Ron Simmons and Keith Cole (13:14)
    • Sags pinned Cole.
  • Ric Flair and Steve Austin (w/Col. Robert Parker and Fifi) defeated 2 Cold Scorpio and Maxx Payne (14:31)
    • Flair forced Payne to submit.
  • Rick Rude and Shanghai Pierce defeated Tex Slazenger and Marcus Bagwell (14:50)
    • Rude pinned Slazenger.
  • Road Warrior Hawk and Rip Rogers defeated Davey Boy Smith and Kole (7:55)
    • Rogers pinned Kole.
  • Vader won the Battlebowl battle royal (25:33)
    • The other participants were the winners of the tag team matches: Steve Austin, Johnny B. Badd, Cactus Jack, Ric Flair, Brian Knobbs, King Kong, Paul Orndorff, Shanghai Pierce, Dustin Rhodes, Road Warrior Hawk, Rip Rogers, Rick Rude, Jerry Sags, The Shockmaster, and Sting.
