The Batman/Tarzan Adventure Hour was a Filmation series that ran on CBS during the 1977–1978 television season.

It consisted of the second seasons of Tarzan, Lord of the Jungle and The New Adventures of Batman aired together.

For the 1978–1980 seasons the series was retitled Tarzan and the Super 7 and expanded to ninety-minutes with additional content.

For the 1980–1982 seasons, reruns of the Batman and the Super 7 episodes moved to NBC and Tarzan joined The Tarzan/Lone Ranger Adventure Hour.
