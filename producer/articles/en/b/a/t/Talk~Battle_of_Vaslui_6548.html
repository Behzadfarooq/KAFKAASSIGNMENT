There is nothing that needs toning down, but you (or someone) will need to find and add more material on what the Turks and their allies were doing and thinking, on what their correspondence was, on how this fits the arc of their history.
  3. I'd be interested to know if someone else would feel that any specific statements that lack citations require them. Nothing leapt out at me, though I may take another look.
