Battlefield (video game series)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

You have new messages (last change).

The Battlefield franchise is a series of computer and console games that started with the PC/Mac game Battlefield 1942, the series is developed by Digital Illusions CE, and published by Electronic Arts. The games feature a focus on large maps and vehicle warfare over and above traditional first person shooters. The main draw of the games are their online gameplay.

Games in the series include:

  • Predecessor to Battlefield games: Codename Eagle (PC), - 1999
  • Battlefield 1942 - (PC, Mac), released September 10, 2002.
    • The Road to Rome (Expansion Pack)
    • Secret Weapons of World War II (Expansion Pack)
  • Battlefield Vietnam - (PC), released March 14, 2004 (US only), and March 19, 2004.
  • Battlefield 2 - (PC), released June 21, 2005 (US only) and June 24, 2005.
    • Special Forces (Expansion Pack), released November 22, 2005 (US only), and November 24, 2005
    • Euro Force (Booster Pack), released March 14, 2006.
    • Armored Fury (Booster Pack), released June 6, 2006.
  • Battlefield 2: Modern Combat - (PS2, Xbox, Xbox 360, PlayStation Portable), released October 24, 2005 (PS2/Xbox), May 11, 2006 (Xbox 360), and (PSP) release date TBA 2006.[1]
  • Battlefield 2142 - (PC), was released 17 October 2006.
    • Northern Strike (Booster Pack), released March 8, 2007.
  • Battlefield: Bad Company - (Xbox 360, PS3, PlayStation Portable, PC), in development, with release in 2007.
