[edit] Error in statement concerning "volume two".

Article currently states, "Volume 2 was initially published as hardcover book, these stories were latter reprinted as backup stories in Batman: Gotham Knights." I believe that this is actually the reverse of what happened. The stories included in the volume two collection were actually first released as back-ups in the Batman: Gotham Knights comic book series.

From solicitation information on DC Comics website (this specifically for the volume two softcover edition): "Collecting the first sixteen Batman backups from BATMAN: GOTHAM KNIGHTS plus 40 pages of material commissioned for the hardcover in a softcover edition!"

(A volume three softcover collection has just been solicited for May 2007. No information at this time as to how many of the Batman: Gotham Knights stories will be reprinted in this collection, nor if there will be more original material commissioned exclusively for the collections.)

YoungTrek 16:28, 16 January 2007 (UTC)
