The Battle of Ostia, fresco in one of the Raphael Rooms

The naval Battle of Ostia took place in 849 between the Muslims of southern Italy and a Christian League of Papal, Neapolitan and Gaetan ships.

The Neapolitan contingent was commanded by Caesar, son of Sergius I.
