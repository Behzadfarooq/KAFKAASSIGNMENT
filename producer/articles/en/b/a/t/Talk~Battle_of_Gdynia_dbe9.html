An entry from Battle of Gdynia appeared on Wikipedia's Main Page in the Did you know? column on 26 April 2006.

As far as I can tell most of the first part of the Before the battle section is false. Indeed, the Pomorze Army was to prevent the Germans from breaking through to East Prussia, but it was to hold out for as long as possible, not eternally. From the very beginning it was clear that the Land Defence would fight surrounded and the Pomorze Army was to defend Bydgoszcz and Toruń rather than Gdynia. It's well-described in the second part, which contradicts the first one. //Halibutt 08:10, 23 April 2006 (UTC)
