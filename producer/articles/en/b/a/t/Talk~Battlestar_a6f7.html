[edit] Viper Count

Fixed the info on the viper count, which was inconsistant with the events of the miniseries. If Galactica had 2 squadrons of 20 MKVII's on board, then what happened to the second squadron? The first squadron launched along with Roslin's ship and was then destroyed by the cylons. When Starbuck is freed from the brig she says that there are 23 pilots but no vipers on board. (It seems unlikely that all 20 of the second squadron would be out of service at this point.) Adama then orders the museum pieces pressed into active duty. Based on this it is much more likely that the 20 MKVII's destroyed in the first attack were all that Galactica had at that time.

Furthermore, there are clearly several MK VII's escorting Roslin's fleet later in the series. These MK VII's were probably still vulnerable to the virus and were thus not capable of intercepting the cylon raiders that tried to destroy the fleet. Most likely these vipers were stowed on the ships, then transferred to Galactica where they were stripped of their vulnerable computer systems. These vipers now form the squadron of 16 MKVII's we see in "Flight of the Phoneix."
