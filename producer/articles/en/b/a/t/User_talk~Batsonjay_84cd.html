[edit] Neutrality

Hi, Batsonjay, I see that since the deletion of the articles on Plum Canary and Chirp software in September (AFD/Chirp software, AFD/Plum Canary) you have been adding links to Plum Canary and Chirp software in many articles (which others have reverted), and mass-nominating competing groupware products for deletion. Please know that Wikipedia frowns on commercial advertising (see What wikipedia is not) and that we strive for a Neutral point of view. I agree that some of the articles you have nominated should be deleted but please be careful not to nominate everything out of spite. It might be useful if you first get some experience with how Wikipedia works by editing other articles where you have no bias and then come back to improve Wikipedia's coverage on groupware. —Quarl (talk) 2006-01-13 23:04Z
