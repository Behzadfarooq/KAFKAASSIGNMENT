Batty (surname)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

This article is about the surname Batty. For the videogame, see Batty (video game).

Batty is a surname and is most commonly found in Yorkshire, England.

[edit] Noun

  • Used in conversation to call somebody crazy. Crazy about something for example, batty about bats.

[edit] Adjective

  • Used in a derogatory context as slang to describe a person as being homosexual (see batty boy).
  • Also in Carribean countries, such as Guyana, Trinidad and Tobago, and Jamaica, batty means buttocks.
