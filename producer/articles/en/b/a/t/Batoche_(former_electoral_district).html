Batoche (former electoral district)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

You have new messages (last change).

Batoche was a provincial electoral district for the Legislative Assembly of Saskatchewan, Canada. It was located in north central Saskatchewan, and was one of the original 25 electoral districts formed when the province was created in 1905. The electoral district was replaced before the next general election in 1908.

A new district of the same name was created in 2002.
