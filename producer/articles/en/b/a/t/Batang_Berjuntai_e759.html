Batang Berjuntai

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Batang Berjuntai is a town in Selangor, Malaysia. It is located in Kuala Selangor district. The Universiti Industri Selangor (UNISEL) is a state university to be located here.

"Batang Berjuntai" means a dangling branch in Malay.

There used to be a railway line connecting Batang Berjuntai with Batu Arang and onwards to the mainline of Malayan Railways at Kuang. The line between the town and Batu Arang was dismantled by the Japanese during World War II and never reinstated.
