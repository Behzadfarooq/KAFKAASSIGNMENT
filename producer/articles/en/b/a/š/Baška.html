Baška
Baška (Croatia)

Baška is a small town located on the island of Krk, in the Kvarner Bay, in the Adriatic Seacoast of Croatia. The population of the municipality is 1,554 (2001), with 901 residents of Baška itself and the rest in three other villages.

The historic monument Baška tablet was found in Jurandvor near Baška.
