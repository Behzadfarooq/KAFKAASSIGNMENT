Baz Bamigboye is a British gossip columnist and arts reviewer, mainly writing for the Daily Mail group of newspapers. His reviews are often quoted in well-regarded secondary reviews (eg The Guardian [1], The Daily Telegraph, [2] and the BBC [3]). He was given the "Showbusiness Reporter of the Year" award at the British Press Awards in 2003.[4]

Numerous TV appearances include Club Mix on Channel 4, and Jack and Baz on BSkyB, and appearances on US TV.[5]

He is the son of Bamidele, the Oba of Iresi, Nigeria[citation needed]. In July 1985 he married Patricia Evans of Australia.[citation needed]
