Bazaliya

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Coat of Arms

Bazaliya (Ukrainian: Базалія) is a town in the Khmelnytskyi Oblast (province) of western Ukraine. It is located in Teofipolsky Raion (district) on the Sluch River (province) at around 49°43′N, 26°28′E.

The current estimated population is around 2,114 (as of 2001).

Bazaliya was founded in 1570, and has had city status since 1957.
