Bangkok Patana School or "BPS" is a British International School located in Bangkok, Thailand on Soi Lasalle, in the district of Bang Na.

[edit] History

Founded in 1957 by expatriate Rosamund Stuezel because of the lack of British education in Bangkok, the school quickly rose to prominence as a preparatory school[citation needed]. It is Bangkok's first largest international school in area next to ISB (International School of Bangkok), and has a population of over 2,600 students, from Nursery to Year 13. Most of their graduates have been accepted to college[citation needed].

[edit] Education

Bangkok Patana School is the British international School of Bangkok. It uses the British schooling system up to and including year 11 , which ends with the IGCSE examinations. In keystage 5 it offers the IB instead of the A Levels. Bangkok Patana School is affiliated to the CIS (Council of International Schools, and has not been awarded accredidation.
