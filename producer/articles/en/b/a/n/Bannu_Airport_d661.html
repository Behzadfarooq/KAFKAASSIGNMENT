Bannu Airport (IATA: BNP, ICAO: OPBN) is situated at 10 km away from city centre of Bannu. It is not as large as the other airports in Pakistan, as it operates to cater mainly to the population of Bannu.

[edit] Airlines and destinations

Currently, Pakistan International Airlines does not have any scheduled flights to Bannu Airport.
