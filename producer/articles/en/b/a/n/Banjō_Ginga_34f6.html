Banjō Ginga (銀河 万丈, Ginga Banjō?, born November 12, 1933), sometimes credited as Takashi Tanaka (田中 崇, Tanaka Takashi?), is a Japanese voice actor (seiyū) who was born in Kofu, Yamanashi. He is best known for his numerous narration roles. His blood type is B. Ginga is currently affiliated with Aoni Production.

He is most well-known for the roles of Gihren Zabi (Mobile Suit Gundam), Jean Paul Rocchina (Armored Trooper Votoms), Shōhei Harada (Touch), and Souther (Fist of the North Star).
