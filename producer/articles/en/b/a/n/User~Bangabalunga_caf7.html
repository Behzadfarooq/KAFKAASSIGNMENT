Hello, I am a wikipedian living in Vancouver Canada. I am a political nut, science, aviation and car enthusiast, and a big fan of all kinds of encyclopedias and chronicles. I strive to help create an accurate an article as possible. My intrests and knowledge are varied and I spend a lot of time reading articles and scientific journals. I have a BA degree in Economics working on my MA. I am a deist.

Currently I like to contribute aviation articles on all types of planes. I have written and edited articles on numerous and various articles but now I want to specialize on aviation articles. I have an extensive background of the aviation industry and for a brief time worked for Union Bank of California as an aviation industry analyst. However, I do not do military articles. There is a tendency for people to take sides and be pro-Boeing or pro-Airbus and so on. I am very neutral and if I criticize a plane or write supporting arguments about one, it is an accurate statement and not a point of view. This is an encyclopedia and we must not forget that.
