Banat-i Hill, Tagbilaran City, Bohol, Philippines

Banat-i is a hill located in Bool, Tagbilaran City. This hill commands a scenic view overlooking the vast Bohol Sea, Islands of Mindanao, Negros and Siquijor and the strait of Panglao Island. Elevation is 145 meters above sea level.
