Bangor is a place-name found in a number of countries.

[edit] Australia

  • Bangor, New South Wales
  • Bangor, South Australia
  • Bangor, Tasmania

[edit] Canada

  • Bangor, Nova Scotia
  • Bangor, Ontario
  • Bangor, Prince Edward Island
  • Bangor (city), Saskatchewan
  • Bangor (town), Saskatchewan
  • Bangor Lodge, Saskatchewan
  • Bangor Road, Prince Edward Island

[edit] France

  • Bangor, Belle-Île-en-Mer, Morbihan, Brittany

[edit] United Kingdom

  • Bangor, County Down, Northern Ireland
  • Bangor, Gwynedd, Wales
  • Bangor-on-Dee (also known as Bangor Is-Coed or Bangor-Is-y-Coed), Wrexham, Wales

All three of these places called Bangor figure in the monastic and/or ecclesiastical history of the first millennium AD.
