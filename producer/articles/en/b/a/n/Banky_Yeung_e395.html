Banky Yeung Ping-kei (Traditional Chinese: 楊秉基; Simplified Chinese: 杨秉基; pinyin: Yáng Bǐngjī; Born March 20, 1975 in Hong Kong) is a theatre playwright, director and actor in Hong Kong. Graduated from Faculty of Playwriting in Hong Kong Academy for Performing Arts, Yeung is the Artistic Director of Hong Kong theatre company FM Theatre Power.
