Bank Technology News is a technology publication written for 30,000 CIOs, CTOs and IT readers, all of whom have responsibility for recommending, purchasing and supporting the front-, middle- and back-office technology needs of financial institutions. As banks and other financial organizations focus intently on compliance and technology ROI, Bank Technology News analyzes the strong link between business strategy and technology implementation, focusing on the players, market trends and innovations that enable institutions to play by new rules and establish benchmarks by which their performance and regulatory compliance will be judged.

The technology magazine is published monthly. It also publishes a weekly newsletter.
