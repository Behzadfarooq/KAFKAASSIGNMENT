Bang Bang is a 1998 album by American jam band Dispatch. It is their 2nd Album after Silent Steeples.

[edit] Track listing

  1. "Here We Go" (Urmston)
  2. "Bats in the Belfry" (Urmston)
  3. "The General" (Urmston)
  4. "Bang Bang" (Urmston and Heimbold)
  5. "Mission" (Urmston and Heimbold)
  6. "Drive" (Heimbold)
  7. "Two Coins" (Heimbold)
  8. "Railway" (Urmston and Heimbold)
  9. "Whirlwind" (Corrigan)
  10. "Out Loud" (Urmston)

[edit] Remastered version

A later release of the album with remastered tracks included the following songs after "Out Loud":

  • "The Way It Goes" (Urmston)
  • "Bats In The Belfry (acoustic)" (Urmston)
