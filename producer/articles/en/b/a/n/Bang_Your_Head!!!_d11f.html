Bang Your Head!!!
Location(s) Balingen, Germany
Years active 1996 – present
Date(s) June
Genre(s) Heavy metal
Website www.bang-your-head.de

Bang Your Head!!! is an annual metal and hard rock festival held in Balingen, Germany. The 2007 edition will be held on June 22 and June 23.

First held in 1996, as a one-day indoor event in the Stefan-Hartmann-Halle in Tübingen, it has grown to become one of the most prominent metal festivals in central Germany, drawing an audience of thousands from all over the world.
