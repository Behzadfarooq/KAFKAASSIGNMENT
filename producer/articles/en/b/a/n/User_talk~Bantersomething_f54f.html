[edit] Thurrock Paranormal

Reasons for keeping

First of all, apologies for deleting the speedy tag! I was going to post a reply last night but got distracted and only remembered this morning.

We are a non profit non charging organisation and all we want to do is to carry out are paranormal investigations and show that there are many associations out there. Who are trying to see if there's anything on the "other side"

If we charged then I can see why it would be advertising but we carry out investigations into the area we live plus the UK with out a fee.

You have other organisations on here that could be classed as advertising such as Most Haunted which is actually advertising a FlexTech television company. We are like Most Haunted but we do not show ourselves on television.

They are a profit making company with Antix Productions who charge for their services! We do not and have no profit in mind. If this is the case then the Most Haunted entry should be removed for advertising.

Also you have an entry about Derek Acorah and all of his books he has written. I have removed any "adverts" about purchasing DVDs, all that is mentioned on there now is what we do. Virtually the same as the Most Haunted entry.

Also today this article was wikified after I had removed the ads and parts of it were changed by an administrator. Does this mean it now meets your approval?
