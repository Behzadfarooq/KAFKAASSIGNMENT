[edit] Habermann's "Prevention of System Deadlocks"?

I'm not sure how this paper diverges from the banker's algorithm. It says that it is a generalization:

Acknowledgment. The deadlock problem occurred to me for the first time when E. W. Dijkstra gave a practical solution of the problem for a special purpose [1]. Later on, I had the pleasure of discussing a general solution of the problem with him when I wrote my thesis.

(where [1] is "De Bankiers Algorithm"), but I don't see the difference between what that paper and this article describe. Anyone? -Slamb 01:15, 28 August 2006 (UTC)
