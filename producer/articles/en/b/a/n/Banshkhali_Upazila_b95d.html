Banshkhali (Bengali: বাঁশখালী) is an Upazila of Chittagong District in the Division of Chittagong, Bangladesh.

[edit] Geography

Banshkhali is located at 22.0250° N 91.9542° E . It has 55609 units of house hold and total area 376.9 km².

[edit] Demographics

As of 1991 Bangladesh censusGRBangladeh, Banshkhali has a population of 320339. Males constitute are 52.07% of the population, and females 47.93%. This Upazila's eighteen up population is 151875. Banshkhali has an average literacy rate of 22.6% (7+ years), and the national average of 32.4% literate. [1]

[edit] Administrative

Banshkhali has 15 Unions/Wards, 72 Mauzas/Mahallas, and 111 vilages.
