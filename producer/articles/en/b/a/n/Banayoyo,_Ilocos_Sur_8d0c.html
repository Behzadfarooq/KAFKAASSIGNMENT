Banayoyo, Ilocos Sur

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

You have new messages (last change).

Banayoyo is a 5th class municipality in the province of Ilocos Sur, Philippines. According to the 2000 census, it has a population of 6,728 people in 1,364 households.

[edit] Barangays

Banayoyo is politically subdivided into 14 barangays.

  • Bagbagotot
  • Banbanaal
  • Bisangol
  • Cadanglaan
  • Casilagan Norte
  • Casilagan Sur
  • Elefante
  • Guardia
  • Lintic
  • Lopez
  • Montero
  • Naguimba
  • Pila
  • Poblacion
