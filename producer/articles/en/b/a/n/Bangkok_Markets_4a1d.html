Bangkok Markets

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

There are a lot of markets in Bangkok, Thailand, the most notable are

  • Chatuchak weekend market (The Mother of All Markets)
  • Suan Lum Night Bazaar (next to Lumpini Park)
  • Pratunam Market (Bangkok's largest wholesale clothing market)
  • Patpong Night Market
  • Sampeng Lane and Pahurat Textile Market (Wholesale Market)
  • Bo-bae Market
  • Baiyoke Market
  • Pak Khlong Talat Market (A colourful market by the Chao Phraya River with fresh flowers and vegetables)

[edit] Local Markets

  • Khlong Toey Market (A meat and Seafood market opend every day from 06:00 - 02:00)
  • Or Tor Kor Market (A modern and clean market selling domestic and imported fruit opend every day 06:00 - 20:00)
  • Ying Charoen (Saphan Mai) Market (Large Market with no tourist items every day opend from 06:00-20:00)
  • Minburi Market (similar to Saphan Mai market opend every day from 09:00 - 20:00)
  • Khlong San Market (Market for youn people selling: T-shirts, stilleto's, handbags and some accessories opend every day from 07:00 - 22:00)
  • Major Ratchayothin Market (Selling many things from Clothes to Watches/sunsglasses etc. next to the Major Cineplex Ratchoyothin opend every day from 16:00 - 23:00)
