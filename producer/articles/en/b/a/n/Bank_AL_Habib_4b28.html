The Bank AL Habib Limited is one of the growing banks in Pakistan run by the Dawood Habib group of companies, and was selected as one of the top 200 Best Under A Billion companies by Forbes Asia in their annual review of 2005. The Bank's Head Office is located in the city of Multan in Punjab, but its main Principal Office is located in Karachi. Presently, the Bank has a network of 152 branches in all the major cities of Pakistan. Habib Group are pioneers in introducing banking in Pakistan. Their involvement in Banking service dates back to 1930s. They successfully launched Habib Bank. Still one of the largest bank in Pakistan. This bank was nationalized in 1971. After the privitization scheme announced in 1991 by the Government, Habib Group were the first to be awarded to start a private bank.

[edit] History

The Habib Bank has come a long way from its modest beginnings in Bombay in 1941 when it commenced operations with a fixed capital of 25,000 rupees. Impressed by its initial performance, Qaid-e-Azam Mohammed Ali Jinnah asked the Habib Bank to move its operations to Karachi after the independence of Pakistan. Habib Bank Limited established itself in the Karachi in 1943 and became a symbol of pride and progress for the people of Pakistan.

During October 1991, Bank AL Habib was incorporated as a public limited company. Ater two years of gradual improved growth in 1992 the bank started banking operations. Also during the same year in January the bank was granted membership to be part of the Clearing House. In 2005, as information technology was being the thing of the future, Bank AL Habib launched it first internet banking so accounts and records could be viewed online. In 2006, Bank AL Habib became partners with MasterCard that allowed the lauch of credit cards.

[edit] 1-Link partner

Bank AL Habib is one of the eleven partners of 1-Link system, an inter-bank ATM sharing system which allows customers to transact on ATM of any partner bank. All the branches of Bank AL Habib are currently operating their ATMs under 1-Link umbrella. This includes the newly opened branches and from day one. The shared network also facilitates other banking functions that allow customers a faster and more convenient banking experience. Bank AL Habib is the third largest ATM service provider in Pakistan, having 129 ATMs.

[edit] Lion symbol

Bank AL Habib Limited logo is diversified one adopting traditional dark green Islamic color. Mainly because Prophet Muhammad (P.B.U.H) consistently quoted in various hadith about the purity of this color. As saying that water, greenery, and a beautiful face were three universally good things. Also in Qur'an, sura Al-Insan says, followers of Allah in Jannah wear fine green silk. [1].

With green color spectrum there is Basmala calligraphy on the top of the logo. The lion represents Imam Ali, the sword represents Zulfiqar and the five stars represent Muhammad, Ali, Fatima Zahra, Hasan & Husayn ibn Ali.
