Banga is a town and a nagar panchayat[2] in Nawanshahr district in the state of Punjab, India. It is located on the Punjab Plain.

[edit] Geography

Banga is located around 310 kms from New Dehli and 120 kms from Amritsar. 1400 kms is the distance between Banga and Mumbai. It is fairly in the North-West part of India a few hundred miles before Kashmir . Banga, situated on the Jullundur City Jaijon Doaba Line of the Northern Railway, is 12KM from Nawanshahar and 46KM from Jullindur. It is also linked by road with Nawanshahar on one side and with Phagwara on the GT Road on the other side.

[edit] History

Banga is said to be have founded by Gola a.k.a Banga of the Hoshiarpur in 1963.[3]

[edit] Shopping

Shopping In Banga is interesting. Some visitors are have known to have said that Banga "contains a smell of petrol mixed with diesel". People can buy almost everything in Banga. People could buy Indian Suits and Kurtas. Some sellers even give you drinks such as Mountain Dew or Coca-Cola in the Indian Suit shops to supposedly to influence your purchases. Tourists can get almost anything Indian or Canadian to eat there. Foods such as Samosas, Pakora and many more things. Canadian things range to Pepsi and Cadbury to breakfast cereals that seem very similar in appearance to Canadian breakfast cereals.

[edit] Transportation

Getting around in Banga is hard to some while easy to natives of the city. There are taxicabs which have three wheels and are known to hold more than their limit. Tourists are known to have seen more than their fair share of traffic violations. The three wheeled taxis exceedingly hold up to 5 people, while a few more stand or sit on the top of the moving vehicles. Eye-witnesses say that at a traffic signal intersection, some of the cars burst through the intersection passing the red light nearly causing an accident. Some major roads have intersections where there is no traffic signal, so motorists basically honk their way through the mess. Some may say that the road lanes are poorly painted, Tourists may see the lane markers zig-zagging a little bit while driving down the roads.
