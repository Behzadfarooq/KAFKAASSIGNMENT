Band of Bugs (formerly known as Bugs of War) is a strategy game developed by NinjaBee. The game will include a level editor, leaderboards, and multiplayer gameplay and will be available on Xbox Live Arcade sometime in the first half of 2007.

The development of the game took only 10 months, which was partially due to the fact that the engine was based on the Outpost Kaloki engine. Band of Bugs was a finalist in the 2007 Independent Games Festival in the Technical Excellence category.
