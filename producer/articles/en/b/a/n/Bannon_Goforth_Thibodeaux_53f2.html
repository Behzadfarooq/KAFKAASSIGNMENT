Bannon Goforth Thibodeaux (December 22, 1812 – March 5, 1866) was a member of the U. S. House of Representatives representing the state of Louisiana. He served two terms as a Democrat.

Thibodeaux was born on St. Bridget Plantation near Thibodaux in Lafourche Parish. He was the son of Governor Henry S. Thibodaux. He died in Terrebonne Parish.
