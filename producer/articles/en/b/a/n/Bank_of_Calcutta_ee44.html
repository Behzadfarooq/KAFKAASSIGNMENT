Bank of Calcutta

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

You have new messages (last change).

The Bank of Calcutta was founded on June 2, 1806, mainly to fund General Wellesley's wars against Tipu Sultan and the Marathas. It was renamed Bank of Bengal on January 2, 1809.
