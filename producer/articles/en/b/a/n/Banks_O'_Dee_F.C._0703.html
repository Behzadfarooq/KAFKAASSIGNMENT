Banks O' Dee F.C. are a junior football club from the city of Aberdeen, Scotland. They currently play in the Scottish Junior Football North Premier League. They play their games at Spain Park, by the banks of the River Dee. It has been suggested that Banks O' Dee F.C. may leave junior football and apply for membership of the Highland Football League.
