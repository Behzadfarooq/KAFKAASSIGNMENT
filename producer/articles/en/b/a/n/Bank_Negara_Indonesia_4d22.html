Bank Negara Indonesia

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Bank Negara Indonesia, commonly known known as BNI, is an Indonesian bank. It has branches primarily in Indonesia, but also in Singapore. It has 1000 branches and 9 million customers.[1] It is quoted on the Jakarta Stock Exchange (symbol BBNI). Its market capitalization as of March 12, 2007 was 23.8 trillion rupiah (approximately US$2.6 billion).[2]
