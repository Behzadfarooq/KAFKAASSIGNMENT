Bank of China (Canada) Limited is part of the Bank of China and the operating arm of its Canadian operations. The BOC was founded in 1912 and is one of many banks operating in China. It is not to be mistaken as China's central bank, People's Bank of China. BOC operates in the Asia Pacific region, as well as Europe and North America.

[edit] Operations

BOC does not have a headquarters in Canada, but is part of the American operations (BOC USA) out of New York.

[edit] Services

  • Personal and commercial banking accounts
  • Remittances
  • Loans
  • Mortgages
  • Foreign Exchange
  • Funds Transfer

BOC Canada has 4 locations:

  • Scarborough ON (Midland Ave., north of Finch Ave. East)
  • Toronto ON (Chinatown)
  • Toronto ON (BCE Place) - Canada corporate offices
  • Vancouver BC

[edit] Membership

BOC Canada is a member of the Canadian Bankers Association (CBA) and registered member with the Canada Deposit Insurance Corporation (CDIC), a federal agency insuring deposits at all of Canada's chartered banks. It is also a member of:

  • Interac
