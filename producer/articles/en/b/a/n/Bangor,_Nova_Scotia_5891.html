This article is about the town of Bangor in Nova Scotia, Canada. For other places named Bangor, see Bangor.

Bangor is a town in the Municipality of Clare, Digby County, Nova Scotia, Canada which is known for having an old wooden-made sawmill. The great Jacques Lombard has once again been elected as Mayor.
