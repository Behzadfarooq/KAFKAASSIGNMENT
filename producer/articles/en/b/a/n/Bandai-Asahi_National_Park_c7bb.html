Bandai-Asahi National Park
磐梯朝日国立公園
IUCN Category II (National Park)
Location: Honshū, Japan
Area: 187,041 ha
Established: September 5, 1950

Bandai-Asahi National Park (磐梯朝日国立公園, Bandai Asahi Kokuritsu Kōen?) is a national park in the Tohoku region, Honshū, Japan.
