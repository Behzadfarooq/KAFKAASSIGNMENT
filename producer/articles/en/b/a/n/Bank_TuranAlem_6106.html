Bank TuranAlem JSC (BTA) is the system-making bank of Kazakhstan and the leader in creation of banking network among the Commonwealth of Independent States (CIS).

For the period of its activity Bank TuranAlem JSC received several awards, including "The Best Bank in CIS" and "The Best Bank in Central Asia" were awarded to BTA by Euromoney magazine in 2005. Another magazine, The Banker, conferred "Bank of the Year in Kazakhstan 2005" status on BTA.

BTA’s long-term current ratings from Moody's and Standard & Poor's rating agencies are one of the highest in CIS with positive outlook. On Kazakhstan Stock Exchange (KASE) BTA has the status of market-maker in terms of US Dollars.
