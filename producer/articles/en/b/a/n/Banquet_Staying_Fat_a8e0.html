Banquet/Staying Fat

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

You have new messages (last change).

The follow up to Bloc Party's She's Hearing Voices single was this, a double-A side release on Moshi Moshi Music. Released in May 2004, it followed the band gaining a support slot for several dates with rejuvented ex-Blur guitarist Graham Coxon. Banquet became something of a "cult" hit, the low budget video picking up numerous plays on MTV2, with the single eventually charting just outside the top 50.


[edit] Track Listing

CD single:

  1. Banquet
  2. Staying Fat

7" single:

  1. Banquet
  2. Staying Fat

[edit] Other Info

Label: Moshi Moshi Music

Cat #: CD: MOSHI10CD; 7": MOSHI10

Release Date: 03.05.2004

Pressing Info: 2000 x CD; 500 x 7" vinyl

Chart Position: 51

Misc:

  1. Both tracks were later included on the Bloc Party EP compilation CD, which was imported in vast quantities into the UK.
  2. Banquet was later re-recorded for the band's debut album Silent Alarm (2005).
