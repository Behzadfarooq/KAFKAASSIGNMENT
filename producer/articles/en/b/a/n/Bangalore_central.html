Bangalore Central situated in Bangalore, India, is a 'seamless' shopping mall. Spread over 120,000 sq.ft.[citation needed], it is situated on Residency road, off M G Road. It is a part of the Central brand, which has malls all over India. It has a food chain with 3 Amigos, Slambay, Baskin Robbins among many others and has three main restaurants like Bombay Blues and Copper Chimney. It is owned by Pantaloon Retail that also owns the Indian Big Bazaar super market chain.
