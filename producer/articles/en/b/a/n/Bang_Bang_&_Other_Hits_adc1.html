Bang Bang & Other Hits is a 1992 Cher compilation album, her first since 1974's Greatest Hits. This compilation most prominently features Cher's 1966 hit single, "Bang Bang (My Baby Shot Me Down)", as well as her 1965 hit debut single, "All I Really Want To Do". The rest of the album features Cher's overlooked and underrated hit singles from the '60s. Among those overlooked hits are: "Needles and Pins", "Alfie", "Hey Joe", and many more.
