The Bangor Auditorium is a 5,948 seat multipurpose arena located in Bangor, Maine. It opened in 1955 and is used for concerts (maximum capacity 6,000), sporting events, circus, political rallies, as well as trade shows with 16,000 square feet (1500 mÂ²) of space. It shares the same complex as the Bangor Civic Center.
