Bangabandhu Sheikh Mujib Medical University (BSMMU) is the only medical university in Bangladesh. The university offers MD, MS, M.Phil, MDS, Diploma and FCPS Courses.

[edit] History

The university started as Government-controlled postgraduate institute named Institute of Postgraduate Medicine and Research (IPGMR) (popularly called PG Hospital). IPGMR was established in December 1965. It was upgraded to a medical university and named as Bangabandhu Sheikh Mujib Medical University by the Act (No. 1, 1998) of the National Assembly of Bangladesh.[1]

[edit] Organization

The university is now autonomous governed by a 24-member syndicate. BSMMU comprises 36 Departments under 4 faculties. The university campus is located at Shahbag. It has a compact campus with a 750 bedded hospital.[2]

BSMMU is also equipped with a modern academic library known as BSMMU Central Library as well as a separate digital library.

[edit] The Campus

BSMMU Campus and hospital at the Shahbag intersection, Dhaka, Bangladesh.

BSMMU Campus has five main multistoried buildings that are identified as Block-A, B, C, D and Cabin Block.[3]

  • Block A (a 8-storied building) accommodates the library, lecture theatre, auditorium, students hostel, Dental Faculty and Blood transfusion services.
  • Block B is another 6-storied building which houses the administrative offices.
  • Block C comprises 10 storied hospital.
  • Block D, a 12-storied building under construction, houses several laboratories and emergency units.
  • The Cabin Block is dedicated to causality department, and several other out-patient departments.
