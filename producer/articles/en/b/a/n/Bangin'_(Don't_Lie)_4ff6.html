Bangin' (Don't Lie) is the underground single to give buzz to Nicole's unreleased sophomore album, Elektric Blue. The song features a rap from Prodigy of Mobb Deep. The alternate version features a rap from Missy Elliott. The song was produced by Timbaland. The single was released to mixtapes to give hype for Nicole's upcoming album during that time. Later on in 2001, her label decided to go with the ballad, I'm Lookin' as the official first single for the album. The song also features Nicole singing explicit lyrics.
