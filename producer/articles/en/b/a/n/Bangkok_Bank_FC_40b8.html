Bangkok Bank FC is a Thai football club based in Bangkok. They play in the top division in Thai football, the Thailand Premier League. Their home stadium is Thupatemee Stadium.


[edit] Achievements

  • Thailand Premier League: 12

1964, 1966, 1967, 1969, 1970, 1981, 1984, 1986, 1989, 1994, 1996, 1997
