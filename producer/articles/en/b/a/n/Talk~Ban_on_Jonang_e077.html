That section suffers from some POV issues, too, though. NPOV means that Wikipedia doesn't use words like "hopefully," because that indicates an opinion. Calling another point of view "archaic" is also an opinion. The closest thing Wikipedia comes to stating a conclusion as fact within a controversy is "the large consensus of experts is X" or that "there is no evidence for Y". Just look at Modern geocentrism for an example of NPOV. Phoenix-forgotten 20:46, 8 March 2006 (UTC)
