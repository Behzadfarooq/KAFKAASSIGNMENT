That heading looks a bit tall; v•d•e could be moved to the left or right. Especially in the case of prolific bands with huge templates that take up more than half the screen, they can instead use any of the collapsible Template:Navigational templates with v•d•e built-in. The default colour for these templates is more of a purple than this blue, but it's rare for 2 band templates to appear in 1 article anyway. Pomte 06:27, 15 January 2007 (UTC)


Is there some standard for these templates? E.g. I see the use of different colours on, most seem to use blue(-ish) and some use black, like (Death (band) Spearhead 15:23, 12 March 2007 (UTC)

Most do use blue, #69c, in the same table format with Discography and Related articles as subheadings. User:MrDolomite has reworked some templates using collapsible tables/NavFrames, and their default colour is grey (see Template:The Pussycat Dolls). I have done the same thing myself, though I am contemplating changing them back to blue for consistency. If the band is notably associated with some color, then I guess that justifies the use of a special color in their template. –Pomte 17:04, 12 March 2007 (UTC)
