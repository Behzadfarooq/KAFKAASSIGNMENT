Banda (Sudan)

Banda is an ethnic group of Southern Sudan.

The Banda language belongs to the Benue-Congo languages. The number of persons within this ethnic group exceeds 100,000.[citation needed] Many members of this group are Christians, which endangers them.[citation needed]
