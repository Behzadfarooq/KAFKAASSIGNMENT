Bangalee is a beach settlement on the south eastern tip of Wild Cattle Island.

It consists of about 16 parcels of residential land on which most have houses or shacks that are used for recreational purposes and are not principal places of residences.

Access to the settlement is via boat or 4WD vehicle for those with an interest in an estate and a permit from Calliope Shire Council across and along Wild Cattle Island National Park.
