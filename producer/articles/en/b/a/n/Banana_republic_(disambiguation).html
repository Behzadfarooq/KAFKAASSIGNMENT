Banana republic (disambiguation)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Look up banana republic in Wiktionary, the free dictionary.


Banana republic may refer to:

  • Banana Republic, a chain of clothing stores owned by Gap Inc.
  • Banana republic is a pejorative term for a country with a kleptocratic government, often with a primitive economy and sometimes a puppet state of a major power.
  • The fictional country depicted in the board game Junta by West End Games
  • "Banana Republic", a single from the album Mondo Bongo by The Boomtown Rats
