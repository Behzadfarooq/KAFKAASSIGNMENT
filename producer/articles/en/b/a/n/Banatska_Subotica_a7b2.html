Banatska Subotica

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Orthodox church.

Banatska Subotica (Банатска Суботица) is a village in Serbia. It is located in the Bela Crkva municipality, in the South Banat District, Vojvodina province. The village has a Serb ethnic majority and its population numbering 200 people (2002 census).
