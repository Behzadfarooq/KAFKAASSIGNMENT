Bang Bang is the second of three albums by country music artist Kelly Willis for MCA Records. It never got much attention from country radio, although it was the biggest-selling of the three. It includes the Billboard minor country hit "Baby Take a Piece of My Heart," which only rose to #51 on the charts.
