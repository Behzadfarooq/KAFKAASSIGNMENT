Bantamweight

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

For the mixed martial arts division of the same name, see Bantamweight (MMA).

The term bantamweight usually refers to a class in boxing or boxers who weigh between 112 and 118 pounds (between 51 and 54 kilograms). Wrestling also has similar weight classes including bantamweight.
