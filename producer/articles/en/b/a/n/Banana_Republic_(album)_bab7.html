Banana Republic (1979) is a live album of Italian singer-songwriters Francesco De Gregori amd Lucio Dalla. It was recorde in their tour of 1979 and released by RCA Italia. Ron collaborated to the arrangements, and also was present as singer in the film shot during the tour.
