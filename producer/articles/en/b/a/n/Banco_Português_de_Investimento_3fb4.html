[edit] BPI at a Glance

  • 4th major Portuguese private financial group
  • Total assets of € 36.4 th. M. [1]
  • Market share of 10% in the domestic commercial banking business
  • Integrated multi-channel distribution network
  • Stock market capitalisation of € 4.5 Bn. [2]
  • Return on Investment: 17.9% [3] since IPO (Oct. 86)

[edit] Domestic activity

The Group's commercial bank Banco BPI serves more than 1.3 million Customers - Individuals, Companies and Institutions - through its multi-channel distribution network comprising 542 retail branches, 19 investment centres, branches specialising in home loans (19), a network of external promoters (3 847), structures dedicated to the Corporate (42 centres) and Institutional Customers (6 centres), telephone banking (BPI Directo) and a homebanking service (BPI Net).

Banco Português de Investimento, the BPI Group's original matrix, is engaged in investment banking business - Equities, Corporate Finance and Private Banking - at Iberian Peninsula level.

In asset management activity, BPI is a prime player in the management of unit trust (mutual) funds, pension funds and life-capitalisation insurance, which it distributes via Banco BPI and Banco Português de Investimento.

[edit] International activity

In Angola, BPI is the leader in commercial banking activity with a market share of more than 23% through its 100%-stake in Banco de Fomento. BFA served a universe of 261 thousand Customers at the end of June 2006.
