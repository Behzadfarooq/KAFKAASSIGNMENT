The Bank of Beijing (Simplified Chinese: 北京银行; Traditional Chinese: 北京銀行; pinyin: Běijīng Yínháng) is a bank based in Beijing, People's Republic of China. Founded on January 8, 1996 as Beijing City Commercial Bank (sometimes referred to as Beijing Commercial Bank), it adopted its present name in January 2005.
