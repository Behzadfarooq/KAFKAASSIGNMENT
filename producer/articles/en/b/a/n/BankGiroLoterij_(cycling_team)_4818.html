BankGiroLoterij (cycling team)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

BankGiroLoterij was the name of a Dutch professional road bicycle racing team. The main sponsor from 2000 to 2004 was a Dutch lottery operator. No sponsor was found for 2005, so the team closed. At that time, their UCI World Ranking was #29 in division 1. A big part of the team riders joined new founded team Shimano-Memory Corp, a merge with Japanese team Shimano.
