Banský Studenec (German: Kohlbach, Goldbach; Hungarian: Tópatak) is a village and municipality in Banská Štiavnica District, in the Banská Bystrica Region of Slovakia.

[edit] History

In historical records, the village was first mentioned in 1266 (Kulpach) as a German settlement. It belonged to Banská Bystrica.
