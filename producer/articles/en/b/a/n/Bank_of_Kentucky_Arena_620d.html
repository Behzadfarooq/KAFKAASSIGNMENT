Bank of Kentucky Arena

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

This article or section is about a planned or proposed arena.
It may contain information of a speculative nature and the content may change dramatically as the construction and/or completion of the arena approaches.

The Bank of Kentucky Arena is a 9,010 seat multi-purpose arena in Highland Heights, Kentucky that is currently under construction. It is due to be completed in 2008. It will be the home of the Northern Kentucky University Norse basketball teams.
