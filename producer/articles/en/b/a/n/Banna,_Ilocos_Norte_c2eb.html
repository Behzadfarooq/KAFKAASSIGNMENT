This article is about Banna in Ilocos Norte. For other uses, see Banna.

Banna is a 4th class municipality in the province of Ilocos Norte, Philippines. According to the 2000 census, it has a population of 16,704 people in 3,390 households.

[edit] Barangays

Banna is politically subdivided into 20 barangays.
