Bantam Spectra

Bantam Spectra is the science-fiction division of Bantam Books, which is owned by Random House.

Spectra has published, among others, books by:

  • Stephen Hawking
  • Lisa Goldstein
  • Margaret Weis
  • Tracy Hickman
  • Timothy Zahn
  • Kevin J. Anderson
  • Dave Wolverton
  • Barbara Hambly
  • Michael A. Stackpole
  • Aaron Allston
  • Kathy Tyers
  • Kristine Kathryn Rusch
  • Vonda McIntyre
  • K.W. Jeter
  • A.C. Crispin
  • George R.R. Martin
  • Kelley Armstrong
  • Connie Willis
