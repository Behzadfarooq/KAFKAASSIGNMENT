Banco del Mutuo Soccorso

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Banco del Mutuo Soccorso is an Italian rock band having its origins in the spread of progressive rock from Britain to the continent in the 1970s. They first recorded in 1972 under Ricordi and they are still active as of 2006, with a recorded 25 albums.

The record Darwin! (1972) is famed in experimental/progressive rock circles for being voted one of the best records ever on the web site Gnosis, from 60,000 candidate records. It is considered an outstandingly successful combination of classical music, rock, and jazz.

Among progressive rock fans, their entire 1970s output is held in high regard, particularly Darwin! (1972), Banco del Mutuo Soccorso (1972), Io sono nato libero (1973), Come in un Ultima Cena (1975) and ...di terra (1978).

The 1980s saw their musical direction change towards lighter pop, although in the 1990s they started playing their 1970s material again.
