Bandalungwa

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Bandalungwa, Kinshasa.

Bandalungwa is a neighborhood in Kinshasa, the capital city of the Democratic Republic of the Congo. Its northern portion is occupied by the military barracks of Kokolo, whose buildings prominently display traditional Flemish architecture. The barracks is separated from residential areas such as the "Block" by Kasavubu Avenue.
