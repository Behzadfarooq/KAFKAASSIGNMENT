[edit] Re: CfR for Category:Tall Buildings in Glasgow

Hello, Banoffee Pies. Just to let you know, I have nominated Category:Tall Buildings in Glasgow, which it appears you created, for a renaming to Category:Buildings in Glasgow. I think the category would best serve the project by not including the qualification of height, something which is somewhat ambiguous to begin with. The category won't be renamed immediately - there is a discussion taking place here to decide if this will be worthwhile or not. Please help us decide by participating in the discussion - we would be more than happy to have your input as the creator. Thank you! Hersfold (talk/work) 21:25, 24 February 2007 (UTC)
