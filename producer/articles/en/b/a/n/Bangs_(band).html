Bangs (band)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Maggie Vail

Bangs (often erroneously referred to as THE Bangs) was a punk rock musical band from Olympia, Washington. It was established March 31, 1997 and broke up on September 12, 2004. The band put out all of their releases on Kill Rock Stars except for one 7". They broke up to pursue other careers. Maggie Vail's sister is Bikini Kill's Tobi Vail.
