Bango bunch is a type of fantasy basketball team found in the Wisconsin, and particularly Alex's house. It is generally perceived as lighter in effect and more harmful than other teams like the jurassics, but has the same health and policy implications as other teams. It takes around 20 - 30 minutes to realize the full potential of this team, but within an hours time the team stats may be incapacitating leaving you unable to drive motor-vehicles

The leader of the team is a hacker who plans to some day take over the software company Microsoft and force all employes to smoke Bango (cannabis)

In 2007, Alex went into hiding because he was beaten by his long time foe, the jurassics.
