[edit] Records - Tests

[edit] Team records

  • Highest team total: 488 v Zimbabwe at Chittagong (2004/05)
  • Lowest team total: 86 v Sri Lanka at Colombo (2005/06)

[edit] Individual records

  • Most matches: 42 by Habibul Bashar followed by 41 by Khaled Mashud

[edit] Batting

  • Most runs: 2838 by Habibul Bashar
  • Best average: 34.60 by Habibul Bashar
  • Highest individual score: 158* by Mohammad Ashraful v India at Chittagong (2004/05)
  • Most centuries: 3 by Habibul Bashar and Mohammad Ashraful

[edit] Bowling

  • Most wickets: 87 by Mohammad Rafique
  • Best average: 36.59 by Mohammad Rafique
  • Best innings bowling: 7-95 by Enamul Haque jnr Vs. Zimbabwe at Dhaka (2004/05)
  • Best career strike rate: 71.2 by Mashrafe Mortaza
  • Best economy rate: 2.64 by Mohammad Rafique

[edit] Fielding

  • Most dismissals: 83 (75 catches, 8 stumpings) by Khaled Mashud
  • Most dismissals in an innings: 4 (4 catches, 0 stummping) by Khaled Mashud v Sri Lanka at Colombo (2002).

[edit] Records - ODIs

[edit] Team records

  • Highest team total: 301-7 in 50 overs v Kenya at Bogra (2005/06)
  • Lowest team total: 76-10 in 30.1 overs v Sri Lanka at Colombo (2002

[edit] Individual records

  • Most matches: Khaled Mashud 126

[edit] Batting

  • Most runs: 2039 Habibul Bashar
  • Best average: 51.42 Saqibul Hasan
  • Highest individual score: 134* Saqibul Hasan vs. New Zealand at Hyderabad (1999/00)
  • Most centuries: 4 Shahriar Nafees

[edit] Bowling

  • Most wickets: 112 Mohammad Rafique
  • Best average: 21.59 Abdur Razzak
  • Best innings bowling: 6/26 Mashrafe Mortaza v Kenya at Nairobi (2006)
  • Best career strike rate: 33.7 Abdur Razzak
  • Best career economy rate: 3.63 Saqibul Hasan

[edit] Fielding

  • Most dismissals: 126 (91 catches, 35 stumpings) by Khaled Mashud
  • Most dismissals in an innings: 5 Khaled Mashud.
