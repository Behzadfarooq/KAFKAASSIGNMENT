[edit] Image tagging

I'm afraid that there is no such tags (or none that I know of) that fits the description that you want. The thing is, you want one that states that no one can reuse your image. However, uploading it to Wikipedia means that your work can be distributed to anyone, as it is liscened under the GNU Free Documentation License. The only way to keep the photo to yourself, is by deleting it. If you still want to keep it, the only available tags for such works are Template:GFDL-self or Template:No rights reserved. Additional info is found at Wikipedia:Image_copyright_tags. Hope that helps. Orane (t) (c) (e) 05:38, 1 February 2006 (UTC)
