Bank of China Tower, Shanghai

Bank of China Tower (Chinese: 上海浦东国际金融大厦 or 中银大厦), completed in 2000, is a 53-storey tower in the Pudong District of Shanghai, People's Republic of China. It was built for the Bank of China by the Japanese architectural firm Nekken Sekkei Limited.

[edit] In popular culture

It was one of the three buildings part of the filming of Mission: Impossible III starring Tom Cruise. It is the building where Tom Cruise did a bungee jump.
