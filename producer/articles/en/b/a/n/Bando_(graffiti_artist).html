Bando was the most famous graffiti artist of Paris (France) hip hop scene during the 1980s. He is recognized as the introducer of New York style graffiti in France, around 1982. He stated to paint with people like Dondi White a few years ago, in New York.

He became a funk music producer (Soul Fire Records) in the United States.
