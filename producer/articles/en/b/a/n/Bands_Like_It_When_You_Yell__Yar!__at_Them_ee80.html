Bands Like It When You Yell "Yar!" at Them is Seattle band Minus the Bear's promotional EP for Highly Refined Pirates. Tracks 1 and 2 are featured on Highly Refined Pirates, and tracks 3 and 4 are on the UK Edition of Highly Refined Pirates.

[edit] Track listing

  1. "Spritz!!! Spritz!!!" – 3:03
  2. "Women We Haven't Met Yet" – 4:04
  3. "You're Some Sort of Big, Fat, Smart-Bug, Aren't You?" – 1:08
  4. "Drop It Like It's Hot" – 2:41
  5. "I Lost All My Money at the Cockfights (Demo)" – 5:00

[edit] Writing Credits

All songs were written by Minus the Bear.
