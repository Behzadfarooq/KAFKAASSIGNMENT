The Bannan Line (Banqiao (Banciao) / Nangang Line, Traditional Chinese: 板南線; Hanyu Pinyin: Bǎn-Nán xiàn; Tongyong Pinyin: Bǎn-Nán siàn) is a combination name of two Taipei Rapid Transit System lines, consisting of:

  • Banqiao (Banciao) Line
  • Nangang Line
