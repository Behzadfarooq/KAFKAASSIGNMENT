Bansko Peak (Vrah Bansko \'vr&h 'ban-sko\) is a 280m rocky peak in the eastern extremity of the Delchev Ridge in the Tangra Mountains of Livingston Island. The peak was named after the town of Bansko in Southwestern Bulgaria.

[edit] Location

The peak is located at 62°36′51″S, 59°49′00″W, 740 m northeast of Karlovo Peak, and 480 m southwest of Lyulin Peak.
