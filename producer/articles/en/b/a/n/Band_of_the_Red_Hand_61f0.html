In the The Wheel of Time series of fantasy fiction books by Robert Jordan, the Band of the Red Hand is a military group named after a legendary band of heroes who died in the defense of Manetheren.


Historically

The story of Manetheren is relayed by Moraine during The Eye of the World. During the Trolloc Wars, Manetherens' forces had been a 'thorn in the Dark One's foot', their forces confronting the Trolloc's at every juncture. After one particular battle they learnt that the Trollocs were moving on their homeland. Marching straight from the battle they arrived just in time to confront the Horde. They were vastly outnumbered, but Aemon had sent out messengers, and aid was promised within 3 days, when facing odds that should overwhelm them in the first hour. They held for 3 days, then 5 and finally 10 days, and Aemon and Manetheren was betrayed. Retreating and burning the bridges across the river, they sent their people to flee, hide from the Trollocs. But at first in a trickle then a flood, farmers and their wives, armed with bows and whatever weapons they had joined the army, defending their country until finally they were overwhelmed, the Band dying defending Aemon.


Currently

It is reconstituted after the events in The Fires of Heaven when Matrim Cauthon, aided by the memories gifted him by the Eelfinn, takes command of the forces of Tear and Cairhien fighting the Shaido, winning enough battles to win their allegiance.

Known as Shen an Calhar in the Old Tongue, The Band's original members were from Tear and Cairhien,although their reputation has garnered recruits from Altara, Andor and Murandy. It is formed around military formations based around those used during the Trolloc Wars and Artur Hawkwing's time.

[edit] Today

As of events in Knife of Dreams the band is in two sections. One part is with Mat moving northeast out of Altara; this group was comprised of three banners of horse and four thousand mounted crossbowmen. They had lost four hundred crossbowmen and five hundred cavalry before their last battle in Altara and recruited some Altarans (amount unknown) who were fighting the Seanchan.
