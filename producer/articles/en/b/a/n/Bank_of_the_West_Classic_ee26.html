Bank of the West Classic is a tennis tournament on the WTA Tour held in Stanford, California, United States. The 2006 event is being held from July 24 till July 30. Held since 1979, the tournament is played on outdoor hard courts.

Martina Navratilova holds the record for the most singles titles: 5 (1979-80, 1988, 1991 and 1993).
