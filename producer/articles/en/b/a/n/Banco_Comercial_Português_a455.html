Banco Comercial Português ("Portuguese Commercial Bank"), also known as BCP, is a major bank of Portugal, member of the Euronext 100 stock index; current CEO is Paulo Teixeira Pinto.

BCP is based in Porto and operates a branch brand dubbed and restyled in 2004 as Millennium BCP, amalgamating the former branch networks of BPA and BCP (Nova Rede) itself.
