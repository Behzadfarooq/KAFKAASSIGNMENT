Banks Barbados Brewery

Banks Barbados Breweries is a Caribbean brewery founded in 1961 in St. Michael, Barbados. The Bank Beer brand is also the flagship product of the wider BHL Group which owns the Banks Barbados Breweries.

[edit] Brief details

The company brews filtered beer.

[edit] Beer

Banks Beer, a standard lager, is the best known brand.

[edit] Availability

The beers are available on the island of Barbados and in many other countries in the Caribbean. The brand is also sold in the United Kingdom. Shipments to the US are surpassed by more popular brands such as Budweiser and Heineken.
