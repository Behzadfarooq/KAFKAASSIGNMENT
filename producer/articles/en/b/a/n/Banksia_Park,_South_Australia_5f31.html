Banksia Park is a north-eastern suburb of Adelaide, South Australia. It is nominally located at 34°48′S, 138°44′E, within the City of Tea Tree Gully. Its postcode is 5091.


[edit] Residents

Banksia Park has a population of 3,455 with 48% being in the age group of 25 to 54 years 2001 Census QuickStats
