Banshee (disambiguation)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

A banshee, in Irish mythology, is a "fairy woman" whose mournful wails herald an imminent death.

The name is also used for super-natural undead monsters in many role-playing games, particularly Dungeons & Dragons and Fighting Fantasy, whose scream serves as a deadly weapon.

Banshee may also refer to:

  • Banshee (comics), a superhero in Marvel Comics
  • The Banshee, a musical piece by American composer Henry Cowell
  • Siouxsie & the Banshees, a British punk band
  • Banshee (Discworld), a variant on the banshee myth
  • Banshee, the initial name for the "Mantis" rollercoaster at Cedar Point Amusement Park
  • "Ba She", a snake reputed to swallow elephants in China
  • ACA Banshee, Civil Defense Siren made by Alerting Communicators of America.

A vehicle:

  • McDonnell F2H Banshee, a military fighter aircraft
  • Yamaha Banshee 350, a high-performance all-terrain vehicle
  • Pontiac Banshee, a concept car

In computer and video games:

  • Banshee (game), an Amiga shoot-em-up computer game
  • Banshee light fighter, a vehicle from the video game Wing Commander
  • Banshee, a car from the video game series Grand Theft Auto
  • Banshee, The main air assault vehicle of the Covenant from Halo (video game series)
  • Banshee (Warcraft), a unit in the armies of the undead in the Warcraft Universe
  • Banshee, a Grendel/Shee hybrid in the game Creatures

In computer technology:

  • Banshee (music player), a GTK+ based music player
  • Voodoo Banshee, a computer video card by 3Dfx
