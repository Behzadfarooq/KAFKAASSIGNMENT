The Bloc Party EP is a compilation of Bloc Party's first two, UK-only singles - She's Hearing Voices and Banquet/Staying Fat. Released in May, 2004 throughout Europe, it was Bloc Party's first EP to be released through V2 Records, their label. This EP was released later in Japan in August, and then in America in September of the same year.
