Bande à Part is French bossa nova group Nouvelle Vague's second album, released in 2006. Like their first album, it is a collection of bossa nova covers of 1980's New Wave tracks.

UK edition 14 tracks; Ltd edition 17 tracks; Label: Luaka Bop - US edition 14 tracks; US extended 17 tracks (not same 14 or 17)
