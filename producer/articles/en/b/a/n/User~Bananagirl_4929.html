Last year I bought a video camera (Panasonic NVG-S15)and am loving it! I love filming so much! I also really enjoy the editing aspect of it! I (apparently) have a flair for it so I am going to keep going and see what comes of it!

I really enjoy photography and editing pictures in Paint Shop Pro! I am currently learning HTML so I can better my work!

I am also in the process of creating a website - DevilishGraphix. This is obviously for graphics that I create. I am hoping to get heaps of PSD's on the site (PSDs are images with a transparent background)as well as wallpapers, signitures, tags etc.
