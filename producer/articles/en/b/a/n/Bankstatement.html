Bankstatement is a solo project by Genesis keyboardist Tony Banks, issued under a band name. The other "band" members were singers Alistair Gordon and Jayney Klimek.

[edit] Facts

  • Tony Banks had the idea for Bankstatement after hearing of the success that Mike and the Mechanics was having in both Europe and America.
  • The track "Queen of Darkness" includes parts of the track "Lorca" from the "Redwing Suite" (from Banks' Soundtracks album, 1986).
  • The music for "A House Needs A Roof" was originally a demo for the Quicksilver movie. It was not used, and was given lyrics and included on this album.
