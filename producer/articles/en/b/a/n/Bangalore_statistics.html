Bangalore statistics

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

It has been suggested that this article or section be merged into Bangalore. (Discuss)

The city of Bangalore, with a population of over 6 million, is the 3rd largest city in India(5th largest metropolitan area) and the 27th largest city in the world [1]. Bangalore is the Information Technology (IT) hub of India and is often referred to as the "Silicon Valley of India". Due to the rapid growth of Information Technology and Business Process Outsourcing (BPO) in the city, it has experienced a growth rate of 34.8% for the decade 1991-2001, considerably higher than the 21.54% decadal growth rate of the country.
