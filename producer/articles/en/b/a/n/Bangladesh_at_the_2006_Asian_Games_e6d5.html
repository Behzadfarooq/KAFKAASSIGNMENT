Bangladesh participated in the 2006 Asian Games held in Doha, Qatar with a total of 73 athletes (64 men, 7 women) in twelve different sports. This country won a bronze medal in Kabaddi. Ranked 36th in a three-way tie with Afghanistan and Yemen in the medal standings.
