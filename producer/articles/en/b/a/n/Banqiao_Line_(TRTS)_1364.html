Banqiao Line (TRTS)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Banqiao (Banciao) Line (Traditional Chinese: 板橋線; Hanyu Pinyin: bǎnqiáoxiàn; Tongyong Pinyin: bǎn-ciáo-siàn) is a line of the Taipei Rapid Transit System.
