Bank of America Championship

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Bank of America Championship is a golf tournament on the Champions Tour. It is played annually in June in Concord, Massachusetts at the Nashawtuc Country Club. Bank of America is the main sponsor of the tournament.

The tournament was founded in 1981 as the Marlboro Classic. The purse for the 2006 tournament was set at US$1,600,000, with $240,000 for the winner. However the event was cancelled due to rain and flooding. [1]
