Bantry-Scott is a Vivastation on York Region's Viva bus rapid transit system, north of Toronto, Ontario, Canada. It opened on September 4, 2005, at the intersection of Bantry Avenue, Scott Drive, and Yonge Street in Richmond Hill, Ontario. It is on the Viva Blue line.

York Region Transit routes connecting to Bantry-Scott:
