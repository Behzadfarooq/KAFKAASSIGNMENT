Banksville (Pittsburgh)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Banksville is a neighborhood in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania's South Hills area. It has two zip codes of both 15220 and 15216, and has representation on Pittsburgh City Council by the council member for District 2 (West Neighborhoods).
