[edit] Miscellaneous songs

The following is a list of songs that were not released as singles and do not appear on Bananarama albums.

  • 1982 The Fun Boy Three (album included 4 tracks with Bananarama :"T'ain't What You Do (It's the Way That You Do It)", "The Funrama Theme", "Sanctuary" & "Alone")
  • 1982 "No Feelings" (a cover of the Sex Pistols song) on the Party Party soundtrack
  • 1984 Band Aid "Do They Know It's Christmas?"
  • 1987 Ferry Aid (included Sarah Dallin and Keren Woodward) "Let It Be"
  • 1987 "Riskin' a Romance" (produced by Daryl Hall) on The Secret of My Success soundtrack
  • 1989 Band Aid II "Do They Know It's Christmas?"
  • 1998 "Waterloo" from A Song For Eurotrash
  • 2002 "U R My Baby" on the Disco Brothers album Disco Brothers present Stars of the '80s
  • 2002 Multitude featuring Bananarama "Love, Leave, Forget" on the Is Harry on the Boat? TV soundtrack
