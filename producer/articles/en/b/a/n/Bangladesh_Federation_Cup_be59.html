Bangladesh Federation Cup

You have new messages (last change).

The Bangladesh Federation Cup is Bangladesh's premier knockout tournament in men's football (soccer). Tournament (usually) open to the top-8 teams of the league; occasionally Indian sides were invited

[edit] Previous Winners

  • 1980 : Mohammedan SC and Brothers Union (joint winners)
  • 1981 : Mohammedan SC bt Abahani KC
  • 1982 : Mohammedan SC and Abahani KC (joint winners)
  • 1983 : Mohammedan SC bt Abahani KC
  • 1984 final (Mohammedan SC vs Abahani KC) abandoned due to riots
  • 1985 : Abahani KC 1-0 Brothers Union
  • 1986 : Abahani KC bt Brothers Union
  • 1987 : Mohammedan SC 1-0 Dhaka Wanderers
  • 1988 : Abahani KC 1-0 Mohammedan SC
  • 1989 : Mohammedan SC bt Abahani KC
  • 1990 no competition
  • 1991 : Brothers Union 0-0 Mohammedan SC [aet, 4-2 pen]
  • 1992-93 no competition
  • 1994 : Muktijoddha SKC bt Abahani Ltd.
  • 1995 : Mohammedan SC 0-0 Abahani Ltd. [aet, 6-5 pen]
  • 1996 no competition
  • 1997 : Abahani Ltd. 2-1 Arambagh KS
  • 1998 no competition
  • 1999 : Abahani Ltd. 0-0 Muktijoddha SKC [aet, 5-3 pen]
  • 2000 : Abahani Ltd. 2-1 Mohammedan SC
  • 2001 Muktijoddha SKC 3-0 Arambagh KS
  • 2002 : Mohammedan SC 1-0 Muktijoddha SKC
  • 2003 : Muktijoddha SKC 2-1 Mohammedan SC [asdet]
  • 2004 no competition
  • 2005 : Brothers Union 1-0 Muktijoddha SKC
