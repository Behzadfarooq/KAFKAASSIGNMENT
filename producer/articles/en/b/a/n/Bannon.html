Bannon

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Bannon is an Irish surname. The name refers to:

  • Ann Bannon (contemporary), American author of lesbian pulp fiction novels
  • Brian Bannon (dates unknown), Australian politician from New South Wales
  • Bruce Bannon (b. 1951), American professional football player
  • Chad Bannon (b. 1970), American television and film actor
  • David “Race” Bannon (b. 1963), American fraudster who posed as a former Interpol agent
  • Eamonn Bannon (b. 1958), Scottish football player
  • Gary Bannon (contemporary), British actor, poet, and comedian
  • J.G. Bannon (fl. late 19th c.), American college football coach
  • Jack Bannon (b. 1940), American television actor
  • Jacob Bannon (contemporary), hardcore/metalcore singer
  • James Bannon (b. 1959), Irish politician, farmer, and auctioneer
  • Jim Bannon (1911–1984), American radio and film actor
  • John Bannon (b. 1943), Australian politician and academic
