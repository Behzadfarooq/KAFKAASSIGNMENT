Band of Gold (1955 song)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

"Band of Gold" is a popular song.

The music was written by Jack Taylor, the lyrics by Bob Musel. The song was published in 1955.

The biggest hit version was recorded by Don Cherry in 1955. Another recording was done by Kit Carson.
