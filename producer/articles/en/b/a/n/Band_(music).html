In music, a band is a company of musicians, or musical ensemble, usually popular or folk, playing parts of or improvising a musical arrangement on different musical instruments.

[edit] Types of bands

  • All-women band: A band that consists only of women which both sing and play instruments.
  • Big band: A type of musical ensemble associated with playing jazz
  • Bluegrass band: A band that plays Bluegrass music.
  • Blues band: A band playing US-style Blues
  • Boy band: A type of pop group, usually consisting of three to five young male singers.
  • Celtic band: A band playing Celtic music.
  • Church band: A band providing music for churches.
  • Concert band: Also known as a wind band or symphonic band, is an ensemble composed of members of the woodwind, brass, and percussion families.
  • Cyberband
  • Dansband: A Swedish or Norwegian band playing "dansband" music.
  • Folk group: A group playing Folk music.
