Bankra is a census town in Howrah district in the state of West Bengal, India.

[edit] Geography

Bankra is located at 22.63° N 88.3° E[1]. It has an average elevation of 8 metres (26 feet).

[edit] Demographics

As of 2001 India censusGRIndia, Bankra had a population of 48,403. Males constitute 52% of the population and females 48%. Bankra has an average literacy rate of 57%, lower than the national average of 59.5%; with 56% of the males and 44% of females literate. 15% of the population is under 6 years of age.
