[edit] Summary

Description

This is an image of Banksia menziesii, taken in Jandakot Botanical Gardens. It is a slightly cropped version of Image:Banksia menziesii 1 gnangarra.jpg. It has been cropped to centre and zoom on the inflorescence, because this looks better when displayed very small on templates.
