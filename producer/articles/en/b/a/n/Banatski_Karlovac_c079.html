Banatski Karlovac (Банатски Карловац) is a town located in the Alibunar municipality, in the South Banat District of Serbia. It is situated in the Autonomous Province of Vojvodina. The town has a Serb ethnic majority and its population numbering 5,820 people (2002 census). The nearby settlement of Devojački Bunar is also officially part of Banatski Karlovac.
