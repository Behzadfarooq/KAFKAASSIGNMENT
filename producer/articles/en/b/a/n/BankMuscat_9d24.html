Bank Muscat International
Type Public
Founded Muscat
Headquarters Muscat, Oman
Industry Finance and Insurance
Products Financial Services
Revenue $6.2 billion USD
Website www.bankmuscat.com

BankMuscat is the largest bank in Oman with a network of over 90 branches in Oman and a representative office in Dubai, United Arab Emirates. The bank has a strategic stake in Centurion Bank of Punjab, a private sector bank in India, and has set up BankMuscat International B.S.C., an independent banking entity, in Bahrain.
