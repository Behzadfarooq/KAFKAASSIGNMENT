Bandung Fe Institute

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Bandung Fe Institute is an independent non-profit research organization in Bandung, Indonesia in the endeavor of social analysis by using the recent understanding on complexity theory. The institute founded in 2002 to approach the social system in the fashion of interdisciplinary studies, among computations, econophysics, sociobiology, etc.

As described by its name, the Bandung Fe Institute is highly inspired by the Santa Fe Institute as the originally first well-known research institute concerning the complexity sciences.

[edit] Research Department in Bandung Fe Institute

There are three major research department in Bandung Fe Institute recently:

  • Dept. Computational Sociology
    • Research Club on Evolutionary and Behavioral Economics
    • Research Club on Optimization and Social Robustness
    • Research Club on Memetics and Cultural Evolution
  • Dept. Cognitive Science
  • Dept. Dynamical System Modeling
    • Research Club on Econophysics and Statistical Mechanics
    • Research Club on Poverty Analysis
