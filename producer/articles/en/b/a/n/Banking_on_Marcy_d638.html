"Banking on Marcy" is the 5th episode of season eight of the television sitcom Married... with Children.

  • First Aired: October 3, 1993, on FOX.

[edit] Plot

Spoiler warning: Plot and/or ending details follow.

Marcy D'arcy is experiencing stage fright when she is chosen as the spokeswoman for her bank. To overcome this, she uses a technique to imagine that she's "fascinated" somewhere else, to the delight of the audience.

[edit] Quotes

  • Marcy: Guess what happened at the bank today?
    Al: Holdup man give you his mask to wear?
  • Al: Why are you here?
    Marcy: I wanted to see Peggy.
    Al: No, I meant here on Earth.
