The Banco Comercial do Atlântico (Portuguese meaning the "Atlantic Commercial Bank", Capeverdean Crioulo: Banku Komersial (or Kumersial) do Atlântiku, São Vicente: Bank' Komercial d' Atlântik') is a Cape Verdean banking service. The banking company is located in the largest city named Praia on Santiago Island in Cape Verde. The company is located in the downtown section of Praia.
