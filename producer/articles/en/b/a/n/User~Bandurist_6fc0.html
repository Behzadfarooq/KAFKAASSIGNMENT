User was born in Australia, raised in Australia and now lives in Toronto, Canada. He plays the bandura.

Previously known as the user Kobzar (but he lost his password).

He completed his education at the University of Sydney and the College of Advanced Education in Sydney Australia majoring in music and ethnomusicology. The User also studied at the Kyiv State Conservatory and Kyiv University. He is currently a PhD student at the Kharkiv State Academy of Culture.

User has the title of Merited Artist of Ukraine (since 1999) for his contributions to Ukrainian music, and has recorded numerous commercial CD's.

Area of expertise is Ukrainian folk music and in particular the bandura and related instruments and materials.

Currently user is porting over all the articles in his encyclopedia of the Bandura into both the Ukrainian and English verisons of Wikipedia.
