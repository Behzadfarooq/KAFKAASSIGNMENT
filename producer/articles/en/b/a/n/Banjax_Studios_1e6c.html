Banjax Studios is an animation and post-production studio, based in Belfast, Northern Ireland. Established in 2002 by Darryl Collins, Alastair McIlwain and Richard Morss, Banjax produce feature films, TV series and commercials.

Banjax's current production is Lifeboat Luke set in the magical town on Donaghadoo (not to be confused with Donaghadee!
