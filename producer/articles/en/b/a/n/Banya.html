Banya

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Banya may refer to:

  • a Hindi word, also spelled as Bania, for someone belonging to a Hindu mercantile group of castes.
  • a Korean monk. See: Banya (monk)
  • a Russian steambath, also spelled Bania.
  • Banya, a musical group featured in the arcade dance game Pump It Up.
  • three places in Bulgaria:
    • Banya, in Plovdiv Province, central Bulgaria.
    • Banya, in Burgas Province, southern Bulgaria.
    • Banya, Pazardzhik Province, in Pazardzhik Province, western Bulgaria.
