Welcome to the page of me, Bananafritter. I have used Wikipedia as a reference point for several years, and occasionally made anonmyous edits, but I figured I may as well start an account. Wikipedia saves me from daily boredom with the random article feature.

To the left and the right you will find some userboxes. Rather than boring you with a lengthy autobiography, I'm sure these userboxes will provide you with sufficient information. I am the almighty Bananafritter. It may surprise you, but I do not actually liek Banana fritters, isn't that something? I do however like cheese, in all varieties. Instead of writing a lengthy essay, I may just include some novelty userboxes.

It may surprise you to know, that I do not enjoy the taste, texture or aftertaste of Banana fritters. I like bananas on their own, as I like other fritters, however when they are combined, they form a lethal combination of foods. I do however, enjoy cheese. Cheese in all varieties provides an interesting taste. If mice like cheese, why shouldn't I?

What else should I say? Well, I believe this information should be sufficient. I should however remind you that Pineapples are people too. :)
