The downtown St Petersburg waterfront, the Bank of America tower is just left of center, with the brown top.

The Bank of America Tower is a 28-story skyscraper located at 200 Central Avenue in downtown St. Petersburg, Florida. It was designed by Jung Brannen Associates and was completed in 1990. At 118 m (386 ft) tall, it is currently the tallest building in the the city.
