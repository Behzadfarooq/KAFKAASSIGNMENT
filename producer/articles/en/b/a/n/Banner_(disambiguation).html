Banner may refer to:

  • Banner, a symbol-bearing flag.
  • Banner (country subdivision), a type of administrative country subdivision found in China.
  • Banner (military), the basic unit of the army of the Polish-Lithuanian Commonwealth.
  • Banner page, a page inserted between documents by computerized printing in order to separate them.
  • Banner Theatre, an English-based community theatre company.
  • The Banner, an American metalcore rock band.
  • Web banner (or "banner ad"), a form of online advertising.
  • Banner Records, a record label.
  • Banner (ecclesiastical), see Khorugv used by Eastern Orthodox Church.
