Banned In D.C. is a compilation of hardcore punk and reggae songs by Bad Brains.

All the tracks have been previously released, except for an instrumental version or Riot Squad. In addition, the original single version of Pay To Cum and the original mixes of several tracks from Rock For Light see their first appearance on CD here.
