Bangladeshi cricket team in Zimbabwe in 2003-04

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

You have new messages (last change).

The Bangladeshi cricket team in Zimbabwe in 2003-04 played 3 first-class matches including 2 Tests; and in 5 ODIs.

Zimbabwe won the Test series 1-0 with one match drawn:

  • 1st Test (Harare Sports Club) – Zimbabwe won by 183 runs
  • 2nd Test (Queens Sports Club, Bulawayo) – match drawn
