Hubert Howe Bancroft Middle School is a public middle school located in Los Angeles, California. It is a part of the Los Angeles Unified School District.

Bancroft serves several areas of Los Angeles, including portions of Hancock Park and portions of Outpost Estates as well as most of the city of West Hollywood.

Bancroft feeds into Fairfax High School and Hollywood High School. Some areas zoned to Bancroft are jointly zoned to both high schools.
