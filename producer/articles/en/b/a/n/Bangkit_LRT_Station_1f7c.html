Bangkit LRT station (BP9) is a LRT station on the Bukit Panjang LRT Line in Singapore. It was opened in November 1999. It is operated by SMRT.

[edit] Etymology

The station is located near Bangkit Road. Bangkit means to "evoke, bring to one's mind" in Malay.
