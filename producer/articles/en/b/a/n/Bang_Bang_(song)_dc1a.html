"Bang Bang," a song by Squeeze, was the second single released from their debut album U.K. Squeeze. Along with "853-5937," this is a single that Squeeze mainstays Glenn Tilbrook and Chris Difford are not fond of, and they have successfully fought to keep it from being included on any Squeeze compilation.
