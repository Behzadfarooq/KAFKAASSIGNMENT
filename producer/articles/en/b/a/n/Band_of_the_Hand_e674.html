Band of the Hand is a 1986 crime film, released by TriStar Pictures. It was directed by Paul Michael Glaser and stars Lauren Holly, Laurence Fishburne and Leon. Michael Mann (film director) served as its executive producer.

The film turned into a theatrical release after it failed as a television pilot [1].

The title track, "Band of the Hand", is written by Bob Dylan, and has never been released by him other than on the Band of the Hand soundtrack.
