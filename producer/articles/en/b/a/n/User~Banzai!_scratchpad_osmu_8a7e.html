[edit] Category:Old-school Mac users

This category, which pointedly does not include Johnnies-come-lately to the platform, is based on the premise that those who persisted in their patronage of the Mac during the dark Sculley-Spindler-Amelio interregnum must have something in common, be it a particular taste, lifestyle, or approach to human experience.

A more “encyclopedic” (read: uninformatively dry, but less flamebaitish) title might be Longtime Mac users.
