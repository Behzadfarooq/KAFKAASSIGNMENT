[edit] Wildly Inaccurate

More than half of the groups I recognized from the list was listed inaccurately. In some cases it refers to a group whose lineup changed before they released an album, but two or members have stayed in place for their entire significant career (Pink Floyd, Primus). In other cases it's just plain wrong, i.e. The Breeders or Black Sabbath. It really needs a cleanup.
