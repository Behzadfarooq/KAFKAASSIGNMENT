Bank of Uganda

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Image:BankOfUganda.jpg
The Bank of Uganda building on Kampala Road in Kampala

The Bank of Uganda (BOU) is the national bank of Uganda. It issues the national currency of Uganda, the Ugandan Shilling.

According to its annual accounts, the bank held assets worth UGX4,228bn (USD2.35bn) in 2004.[1]
