This is about the sub-clan of Quraish, for the tribe see Banu Tamim.

Banu Taym or Banu Taim' is a sub-clan to the Quraish tribe. It was closely affiliated with the Quraish tribe[citation needed]. They converted to Islam in 8 AH (629–630)[citation needed].

They come from Taim ibn Mura.

[edit] List

  • Abu Bakr [1]
  • Talha ibn Ubayd-Allah [2] [1]

[edit] Shi'a view

Sa'id Akhtar Rizvi, a 21st century Shi'a Twelver Islamic scholar writes:

“ ...since the day of saqifah the Banu Hashim and Banu Taym felt nothing but enmity towards each other [3]. ”
