Bar/None Records is an independent record label based out of Hoboken, New Jersey.

The label was founded by Tom Prendergast in early 1986 as a logical progression from Pier Records, an indie record store in Hoboken. The first release on the label was by Rage To Live, whose leader, Glenn Morrow, quickly became a partner in the label.
