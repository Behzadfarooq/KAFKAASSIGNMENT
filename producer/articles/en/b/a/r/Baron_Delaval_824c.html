Baron Delaval

Baron Delaval was a title created in the Peerage of Ireland on 17 October 1783 for Sir John Hussey Delaval, great nephew of Admiral George Delaval, who had previously, on 1 July 1761, been created a Baronet, of Ford in the County of Northumberland. On 21 August 1786, he was also created Baron Delavel in the Peerage of Great Britain.

On his death on 17 May 1808, both baronies and the baronetcy became extinct.
