Barry Breen (born January 7, 1948) was an Australian rules footballer in the Victorian Football League, playing with St Kilda Football Club.

He famously kicked a wobbly punt that bounced through for a behind that broke the deadlock with Collingwood late in the dying minutes of the 1966 Grand Final that became St Kilda's first, and to date only, premiership. Captain in 1979 and member of St Kilda's Team of the Century. Former club games record holder. Later became Sydney Swans general manager.
