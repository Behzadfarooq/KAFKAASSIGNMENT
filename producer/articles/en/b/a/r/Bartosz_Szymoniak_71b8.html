Bartosz Szymoniak.

Bartosz Szymoniak (born April 26, 1984 in Kalisz, Poland) is a Polish singer who rose to popularity after placing third in Idol Poland 4, the Polish version of Pop Idol, shown by Polsat. Bartek became 2nd runner-up with 14.78% of the vote in a 3-way final on June 18, 2005.

[edit] Idol Poland 4 Performances

Semi Finals: Czarne Słońca by Kult
Top 10: Black Or White by Michael Jackson
Top 9: Do Zakochania Jeden Krok by Andrzej Dąbrowski
Top 8: Could You Be Loved? by Bob Marley
Top 7: I Don't Wanna Know by Mario Winans
Top 6: Bądź Moim Natchnieniem by Andrzej Zaucha
Top 5: Bailamos by Enrique Iglesias
Top 5: Gdy Nie Ma Dzieci by Kult
Top 4: Spadam by Coma
Top 4: I Tak Warto Żyć by Raz, Dwa, Trzy
Grand Final: Ten O Tobie Film by Tadeusz Nalepa
Grand Final: Pastime Paradise by Stevie Wonder
