Variety Recordings: Barenaked Ladies (sometimes known as CBC Variety Recordings or simply Variety Recordings) is a 1991 promo release by Canadian band Barenaked Ladies.

Issued to radio stations, this CD featured early versions of some songs that would later appear on their major-label debut, Gordon. It is now a rare collector's item.
