Barira, Shariff Kabunsuan

Barira is a 5th class municipality in the province of Shariff Kabunsuan, Philippines. According to the 2000 census, it has a population of 18,296 people in 3,326 households.

[edit] Barangays

Barira is politically subdivided into 14 barangays.

  • Barira (Pob.)
  • Bualan
  • Gadung
  • Korosoyan
  • Lamin
  • Liong
  • Lipa
  • Lipawan
  • Marang
  • Nabalawag
  • Panggao
  • Rominimbang
  • Togaig
  • Minabay
