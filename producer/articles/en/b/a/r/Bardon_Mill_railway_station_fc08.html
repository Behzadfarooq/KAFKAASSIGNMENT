Bardon Mill railway station

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Bardon Mill railway station serves the village of Bardon Mill in Northumberland, England. It is located on the Tyne Valley Line which runs from Newcastle upon Tyne to Carlisle, and is managed by Northern Rail who provide all passenger train services.
