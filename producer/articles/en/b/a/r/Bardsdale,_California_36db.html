Bardsdale, California

Bardsdale is a small unincorporated community in the agricultural belt on the Santa Clara River in Ventura County, California. The closest town is Fillmore. Bardsdale is a center of citrus and avocado farming, and is home to Bardsdale Community Presbyterian Church, a Ventura County Historical Landmark.
