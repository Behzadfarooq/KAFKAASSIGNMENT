[edit] Renovations

In 2002, the village of Barnesville saw a rebirth of its school system. The high school was remodeled to expand classroom areas and improve appearances. A new middle school was built just below the high school to rectify the crowded elementary school. The elementary school was remodeled and an older section of the building was removed, because the renovations and new school left the school board in a weak financial state, it is now using fundraisers to increase its stability.

[edit] Athletics

The school's sports teams' mascot is the shamrock. Its football team for 2006 ended with an 11-1 record.
