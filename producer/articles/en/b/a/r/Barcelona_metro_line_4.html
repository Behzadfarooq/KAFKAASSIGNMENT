Line 4, currently known as Trinitat Nova - La Pau, usually called "línia groga" (yellow line), is a metro line in Barcelona operated by TMB, and part of the ATM fare-integrated transport network.

Opened to the public in the 1960s, it serves the northern half of Barcelona, covering a wide C-shaped area stretching from La Pau (in la Verneda) to Trinitat Nova, where it's linked with the recent L11. The 16.7 kilometres of the whole line are underground.
