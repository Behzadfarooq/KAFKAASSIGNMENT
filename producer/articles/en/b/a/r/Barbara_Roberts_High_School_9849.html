Roberts High School - Secondary Alternative Programs is a collection of non-traditional educational programs. It is named in honor of former Governor Barbara Roberts. Rather than a centralized campus, the "school" consists of programs conducted in various locations, including Chemeketa Community College, and internet based S-K Online. Together, enrollment in these programs make Roberts the largest high school in Salem, Oregon.[1][2]
