Bareface is the third solo album by singer/songwriter/producer Aaron Sprinkle; it was released on Silent Planet Records. "Really Something" and "The Patron" were first released on Really Something EP, and a remastered version of "Really Something" plus "Sweeter Than Me", "All You Can Give", "Colorblind", "Let Me In" and "The Boy Who Stopped The World" would later be released on Aaron's last solo project, Lackluster.
