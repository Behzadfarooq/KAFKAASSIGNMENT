Barry Wade Stokes (born December 20, 1973 in Flint, Michigan) is an American football offensive lineman who plays for the Detroit Lions of the National Football League. He attended Eastern Michigan University. Prior to playing for the Falcons, he was signed to the practice squad of several teams, but did not see NFL action until the 1998 NFL season with the Miami Dolphins. He then played for the Green Bay Packers and Cleveland Browns, and was a member of the New York Giants but did not play due to back injuries. He spent time in NFL Europe, where he played for the Scottish Claymores.

His High School Football years (Davison, Mi) were very advantagous, as he was a key leader in helping his team remain undefeated for 4 years he was there. A tradition which continued 3 years after he left.
