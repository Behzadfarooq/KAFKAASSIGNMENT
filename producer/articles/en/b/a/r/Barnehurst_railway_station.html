Barnehurst railway station serves Barnehurst, London Borough of Bexley: it is 13.9 miles (22km) from London on the Bexleyheath Line. The station stands in a cutting: the small brick-built ticket office (in a similar design to others on the Line) is above the Up platform.

Barnehurst was opened with the Line on 1 May 1895.

Trains run from the station westbound to Cannon Street or Charing Cross via London Bridge or Victoria via Denmark Hill and eastbound to Dartford.
