Barbarossa

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Barbarossa (Italian: 'Red Beard') may refer to:

  • Barbarossa, nickname of three famous people in history:
    • Frederick I (Barbarossa) (1122-1190)
    • Barbarossa I (c. 1474-1518), an Ottoman-Turkish privateer and Bey of Algiers
    • Barbarossa (Ottoman admiral) (c. 1478-1546), an Ottoman-Turkish privateer and Admiral-of-the-Fleet
  • Barbarossa (board game) (1988) by Klaus Teuber
  • Barbarossa (album) (1996) by Cubanate
  • Barbarossa (Dune), a Titan in the Dune Universe
  • Operation Barbarossa, the codename used by Nazi Germany for their invasion of the Soviet Union during World War II.
  • Barbarossa (or Barbaroux in French), an Italian red wine
  • Barbarossatown, the nickname for five cities in Germany
  • Barbarossa Rugner, the Emperor of the Scarlet Moon Empire in Konami's Suikoden
  • Barbarossa (2001), an album by Orplid
  • Barbarosa, a 1982 Movie featuring Willie Nelson and Gary Busy.
