Barney's Campfire Sing-Along

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Barney's Campfire Sing-Along is a home video featuring Barney and the Backyard Gang. Released in Spring 1990.

[edit] Plot

Barney and his friends go camping together. They get to meet the animals that live in the forest and enjoy s'mores at a campfire sing-along.
