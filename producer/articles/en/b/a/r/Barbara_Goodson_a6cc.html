Barbara D. Goodson Gustafson (born August 16, 1949 in Santa Monica, CA) is an experienced English voice actress known for her rather extensive dubbing of various anime, as well as doing the voice of Rita Repulsa in Mighty Morphin Power Rangers, Power Rangers: Zeo, and Power Rangers in Space.

Goodson has more major live-action Power Rangers voice-over credits than any voice actor/actress. In addition to Rita Repulsa, she has also voiced both Prince Sprocket and Orbus in Power Rangers: Zeo (and with her also doing the voice of Rita had at least 3 credits listed in the show's end credits), and also did the female voice of Mandilok in Power Rangers: Wild Force. She also did the voice of Ladyborg in Beetleborgs Metallix.

As for her anime dubbing, she has done voices in such shows as Dragon Ball, Ghost in the Shell: Stand Alone Complex, and FLCL, just to name a few.
