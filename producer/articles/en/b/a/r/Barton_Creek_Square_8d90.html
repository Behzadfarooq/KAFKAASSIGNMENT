Barton Creek Square, eponymously named after Barton Creek, Texas, is a shopping mall located in southwest Austin, Texas, near Westlake, at the intersection of Loop 1 (MoPac Expressway) and Loop 360.

[edit] Anchors

  • Dillard's (203,035 sq. ft.)
  • Dillard's Men's & Children (80,000 sq. ft.)
  • J.C. Penney (152,423 sq. ft.)
  • Macy's (219,773 sq. ft.) (opened 1982 as Foley's, became Macy's 2006)
  • Nordstrom (143,825 sq. ft.) (opened 2003)
  • Sears (142,367 sq. ft.)
