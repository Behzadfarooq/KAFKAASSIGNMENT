Baron Rothschild, of Tring in the County of Hertford, is a peerage title in the Peerage of the United Kingdom. It was created in 1885. The Lords Rothschild are part of the Rothschild banking family. The current baron is also a Baronet, of Tring Park, created for the uncle of the 1st Baron.

There is also a title Baron de Rothschild in the Austrian nobility.

[edit] Rothschild Baronets, of Tring Park (1847)

  • Sir Anthony Nathan de Rothschild, 1st Baronet (1810-1876)
  • Sir Nathan Mayer Rothschild, 2nd Baronet (1840-1915) (created Baron Rothschild in 1885)

[edit] Barons Rothschild (1885)

  • Nathan Mayer Rothschild, 1st Baron Rothschild (1840-1915)
  • Lionel Walter Rothschild, 2nd Baron Rothschild (1868-1937)
  • Nathaniel Mayer Victor Rothschild, 3rd Baron Rothschild (1910-1990)
  • Nathaniel Charles Jacob Rothschild, 4th Baron Rothschild (b. 1936)

The Heir Apparent is Hon. Nathaniel Philip Victor James Rothschild (b. 12 July 1971)
