The Barrymore family is an American acting family. The members have gained a reputation for both tremendous talent and tendencies for self-destructive behavior.

Herbert Arthur Chamberlayne Blyth aka Maurice Barrymore married Georgiana Emma Drew, and had:

  • Lionel Barrymore
  • Ethel Barrymore married Russell Griswold Colt, and had:
    • Samuel Peabody Colt
    • Ethel Barrymore Colt married John Romeo Miglietta, and had:
      • John Drew Miglietta (b. 10 September 1946)
    • John Drew Colt
  • John Barrymore married Blanche Oelrichs, and had:
    • Diana Blanche Barrymore
  • John Barrymore married Dolores Costello, and had:
    • John Drew Barrymore married Cara Williams, and had:
      • John Blyth Barrymore married Jaqueline Manes, and had:
        • John Blyth Barrymore IV (living)
      • John Blyth Barrymore married Rebecca Pogrow, and had:
        • Blyth Lane Barrymore (living)
        • Sabrina Brooke Barrymore (living)
    • John Drew Barrymore married Gabriella Palazzoli, and had:
      • Blyth Dolores Barrymore (living)married Antonio Gioffredi, and had:
        • Gabriella Gioffredi (living)
        • Nicole Gioffredi (living)
    • John Drew Barrymore married Nina Wayne, and had:
      • Jessica Barrymore
    • John Drew Barrymore married Ildiko Jaid, and had:
      • Drew Barrymore
    • Dolores Ethel Mae Barrymore (living)married Thomas Fairbanks, and had:
      • Hillary Klaradru Fairbanks (living)
      • Hillary Klaradru Fairbanks married Thomas Randolph, and had:
        • Isabelle Harrison Barrymore Randolph (b.7 June 1985)
      • Anthony John Barrymore Fairbanks (b. 10 July 1952)married Dianne Zaninovich, and had:
        • Samantha Mae Barrymore Fairbanks (living)
    • Dolores Ethel Mae Barrymore married Lew Bedell, and had:
      • Dore Lewis Bedell (b. 1 September 1958)
      • Stephanie Mae Bedell (b. 31 July 1966)

Georgiana's family - parents John Drew and Louisa Lane Drew; brothers John Drew Jr. and Sidney Drew, know for the Mr. & Mrs. Sidney Drew comedy act; sister Louisa Drew - were all actors. Sidney's only child, Sidney Rankin Drew, left his acting career to join the Lafayette Escadrille, and was killed in action. John, Jr.'s daughter, Louisa, married performer Jack Devereaux; their son John Drew Devereaux was a Broadway stage manager.

Ethel's children also acted, primarily on the stage.

Except for John Drew, Diana, Drew, and John Blyth, none of the other members have yet entered the entertainment industry.
