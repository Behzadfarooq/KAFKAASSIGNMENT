Barışarock

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Poster of Barışarock 2006.

Barışarock (read as Barışarak in Turkish, meaning making peace) is an annual rock festival organized in Istanbul, Turkey.

The festival started in 2003 as a reaction and counter-festival to Rock'n Coke, another large-scale rock festival of Istanbul sponsored by Coca Cola, positioning itself as an anti-capitalist alternative. The organizers and the audience of the festival maintain that, the idea of rock music is not compatible with the sponsorship of a global company like Coca Cola and it should not become a tool of advertisement and promotion of consumption.

Entrance to the festival remains free of charge.
