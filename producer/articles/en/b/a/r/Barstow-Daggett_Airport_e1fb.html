Barstow-Daggett Airport (IATA: DAG, ICAO: KDAG, FAA LID: DAG) is a public airport located near the city of Barstow and the town of Daggett in San Bernardino County, California, USA. Built in 1933, it is the oldest of the six airports operated by San Bernardino County.

[edit] Facilities

Barstow-Daggett Airport covers 1,087 acres and has two runways:

  • Runway 4/22: 5,119 x 100 ft. (1,560 x 30 m), Surface: Asphalt
  • Runway 8/26: 6,400 x 150 ft. (1,951 x 46 m), Surface: Asphalt
