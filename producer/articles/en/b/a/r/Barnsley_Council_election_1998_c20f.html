Barnsley Council election 1998

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Elections to Barnsley Council were held on 7 May 1998. One third of the council was up for election and the Labour party kept overall control of the council.

After the election, the composition of the council was

  • Labour 63
  • Independent 2
  • Conservative 1
