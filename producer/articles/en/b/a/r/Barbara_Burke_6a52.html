Barbara Hannah Anita Burke (born 13 May 1917) is a former British athlete who competed mainly in the 100 metres.

She competed for Great Britain in the 1936 Summer Olympics held in Berlin, Germany in the 4 x 100 metres where she won the silver medal with her team mates Eileen Hiscock, Violet Olney and Audrey Brown.
