Barra do Quarai is a small Brazilian town located near the border with Uruguay (on the Rio Quaraí) and Argentina on the Uruguay River. The town was separated from the municipality of Uruguaiana on December 28, 1995. It has a population of only 4,578 (2005). Its elevation is only 35 m. It has an area of 1,056.1 square kilometers. It is located 717 km west of the state capital of Porto Alegre, northeast of Montevideo, Uruguay.
