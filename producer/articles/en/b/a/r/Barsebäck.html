Barsebäck is a village belonging to Kävlinge Municipality in Skåne in southern Sweden with 400 inhabitants, about 4km east of the harbour village Barsebäckshamn. It is known for the (now closed) Barsebäck nuclear power plant, and for the internationally known golf course Barsebäck Golf & Country Club with an annual world class golf tournament.
