Baruch Kimmerling

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Baruch Kimmerling (born 1939 in Romania, emigrated to Israel in 1952) is a Professor of Sociology at the Hebrew University, Jerusalem. He is well-known as a sociologist and social-historian. He has a reputation as a vocal critic of Israeli policies and practice related to the Arab-Jewish conflict and the Israeli government.
