Bargainville was a 1993 (see 1993 in music) album by Canadian band Moxy Früvous, their first major-label release. This disc's subject matter is divided between comedy and serious personal, social, and ecological issues.

Bargainville features the band's strong songwriting, playful humor and complex harmonies. It is considered to be among their better efforts.

Bargainville was released on July 20, 1993 in Canada by Warner Music Canada and on February 8, 1994 in the US by WEA/Atlantic Records.
