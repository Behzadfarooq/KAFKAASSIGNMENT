Barry Callaghan

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

This article is about the Canadian author and poet, for the Scottish football (soccer) player, see Barry Callaghan (footballer).

Barry Callaghan (born July 5, 1937) is a Canadian author and poet. Born in Toronto, Ontario, he is the son of late Canadian novelist and short story writer Morley Callaghan. He is a graduate of the University of Toronto.
