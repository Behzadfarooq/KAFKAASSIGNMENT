Barrow Island (Queensland)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

You have new messages (last change).

Barrow Island is part of the Great Barrier Reef Marine Park in Ninian Bay, 90km South East of Cape Melville, Queensland. It is a few hundred metres from Barrow Point, where a mine is located.
