As Carl Ravna in Hammer's The Kiss of the Vampire (1963)

Barry Warren (b. 12 July 1933) is a British actor. As well as several stage and TV appearances, he played three major characters for Hammer Studios: Carl Ravna in The Kiss of the Vampire (1963); Don Manuel Rodriguez de Savilla in The Devil-Ship Pirates (1963); and Karl in Frankenstein Created Woman (1967).
