[edit] Czech?

Why is Jakub Petruzalek listed as a czech if he was born in Canada? Being born here gives him citizenship. 65.95.124.5 14:52, 3 April 2006 (UTC)

  • Jakub Petruzalek is listed in the OHL media guide as being an import draft pick chosen by the Ottawa 67's in 2004 from the hometown of Litvinov, Czech Republic, born on April 24, 1985. He is listed on eurohockey.net as born in Most, Czech Republic [1]. He is not from Cambridge, Ontario. That is most likely an entry error. It will be corrected. Thanks.
