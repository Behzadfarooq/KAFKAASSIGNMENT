The Bartercard Cup (successor of the Lion Red Cup) is the top level rugby league club competition in New Zealand. It is administered by the New Zealand Rugby League.

The Bartercard Cup is now in its eighth season and growing stronger every year. It is a feeder competition for players to move up to New Zealand Warriors and other Australiasian National Rugby League and European Super League clubs.
