Barry Meadow is a professional gambler, blackjack player and author with more than 30 years experience in the gambling community. He is best known for his books on California harness racing. He has authored multiple books on the subject including Success at the Harness Races, Money Secrets at the Race Track and Professional Harness Betting. Meadow has also written about other gambling topics including the 1999 Blackjack Autumn: A True Tale of Life, Death, and Splitting Tens in Winnemuca and in 2003 Crushing the Internet Casinos. He has also written articles for various publications including Sports Illustrated.

He is scheduled to compete in an episode of the Ultimate Blackjack Tour, a 10 week televised blackjack tournament airing on CBS.

Meadow also founded Master Win Ratings, a service that tracks and ranks race horses in California, and he has established his own publishing company called TR Publishing. He has also written outside the gambling genre including work for television sitcoms.
