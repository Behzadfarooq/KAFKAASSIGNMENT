Baron Latimer

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The title Baron Latimer was created in the Peerage of England by writ in 1299. It has not been used since 1430.

  • Elizabeth Greville, de jure 11th Baroness Latimer and 3rd Baroness Willoughby de Broke (d. 1560) (abeyance ended c. 1535)
