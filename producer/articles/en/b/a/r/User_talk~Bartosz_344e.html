Dear Bartosz,

Thank You for Your letter. I used the term “sort” in the sense as it is used in the field of “many-sorted logic”. It was a surprise also for me, that there is no Wikipedia article from many-sorted logic yet. Many-sorted logic is also for me a fresh area.
