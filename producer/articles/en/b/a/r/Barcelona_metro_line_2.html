Line 2, currently known as Paral·lel - Pep Ventura, coloured purple and sometimes simply called Línia lila, is a metro line in Barcelona operated by TMB.

Although its construction started in the 1960s, it wasn't open until 1995, being the second newest metro line in the network (after L11). All of its stations are underground.
