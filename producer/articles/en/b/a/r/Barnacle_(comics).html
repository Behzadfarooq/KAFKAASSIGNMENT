Barnacle (Mortimer Everett) is a fictional mutant from Marvel Comics who first appeared in X-Men: Magneto War #1.

[edit] Ficitional character biography

Barnacle is one of the newer Acolytes that debuted during the Magneto War. He had the power to transform the moisture on his body into a defensive armor which made him very hard to defeat. He later might have died during the Sentinel attack on Genosha, as that was where he was last seen. Though this is unclear at the moment, so Barnacle might reappear in the future.
