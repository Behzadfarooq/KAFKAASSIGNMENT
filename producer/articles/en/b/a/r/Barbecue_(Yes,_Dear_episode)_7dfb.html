[edit] Plot

Spoiler warning: Plot and/or ending details follow.

Greg's wants a barbecue but the shop is in a rough part of town. He decides to go against Jimmy's advice and when Greg is going for a few hours Jimmy sets out to find him fearing the worst. Greg is the meantime makes friends with everyone at the shop and decides to play a joke on Jimmy and his friend having the last laugh.
