Location of Baranello in Italy

Baranello is a comune (municipality) in the Province of Campobasso in the Italian region Molise, located about 10 km southwest of Campobasso. As of 31 December 2004, it had a population of 2,715 and an area of 24.8 km².[1]

Baranello borders the following municipalities: Busso, Colle d'Anchise, Spinete, Vinchiaturo.
