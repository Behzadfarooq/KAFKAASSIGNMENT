Barry Manilow Live was the fifth album by the singer and songwriter Barry Manilow. The album was released in 1977, and it scored quadruple platinum.

Manilow also was among Tony award winners Lily Tomlin, Diana Ross, National Theatre For the Deaf and Equity Library Theatre honored with a 1977 Special Award.[1] He received it for Barry Manilow on Broadway, his 12 day stay at the Uris Theatre from December 21, 1976 to January 2, 1977, where his live album and the concert parts in his first ABC special were filmed.[2]
