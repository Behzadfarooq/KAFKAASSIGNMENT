Barry Clancy (born April 20, 1985 in ??) is an Irish football player who plays with Kildare County FC in the Eircom League First Division. Clancy joined Kildare County at the start of the 2006 season from Shelbourne FC. The small statured defender makes up for his lack of height through his never ending industry on the pitch. Starting his County career as full back Clancy was an instant hit at Station Road. He is now often used as a left winger.
