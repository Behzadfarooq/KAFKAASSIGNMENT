Bardhaasht is a bollywood film released in 2004. The director of the film is E. Navis. The main cast of the movie is Bobby Deol, Lara Dutta and Rahul Dev. The genre of the film is known as drama and thriller.

[edit] Synopsis

Spoiler warning: Plot and/or ending details follow.

Aditya Shrivastiv (Bobby Deol) is a deserted army officer. He has a brother, Anuj (Ritesh Deshmukh). One day Anuj gets mad at his mother, and his brother, Anuj never came back home for a long time. Aditiya goes to the Police Station where no one helps him except for ACP Yashwant Thakur (Rahul Dev). ACP Yashwant Thakur told him they would look for him, the next day when Aditya came back he was told that his brother was killed in encounter. Aditya was confused so he told one of his officer friend Mehmoob Bhai (Veerendra Saxena) to steal the police report. Aditya reads the report, it said that Anuj was running away with drugs, and was shot and died on the spot. Aditya didn't believe it. So he started to look for the truth, he found a love letter to Anuj, by Romola (Tara Sharma) he tries to meet her but she gets scared and runs away. Her brain health wasn't great. One day she came to Aditya's hotel and told him everything, she told him that ACP Yashwant Thakur and his friend police officers, killed him and lied that he was killed in encounter. Aditya gets angry and wants revenge he goes to find a lawyer, after knowing that the lawyer he choose wasn't going to help him, he choose Payal (Lara Dutta) as his lawyer. Who used to be his girlfriend before he was a deserted Army officer. She fought for Anuj's case, and Aditya and her slowly fell in love again, after losing all their witnesses (Mehmood Bhai and Ramola) he decides to take revenge in a different way...
