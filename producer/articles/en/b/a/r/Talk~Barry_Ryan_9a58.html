[edit] Re; Barry Ryan at NPG

This is in fact the Barry Ryan of the Sixties pop music duo Paul and Barry Ryan.The Ryans recorded for Decca and then recorded a masterstroke of songs composed by Paul,sung by Barry, on 1968's "Barry Ryan Sings Paul Ryan", which spawned the genius worldwide epic single, "Eloise". It is now available on Rev-Ola. Barry has always been an accomplished photographer in addition to his singing talents.
