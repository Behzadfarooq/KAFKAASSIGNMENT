[edit] Scope

This project covers the creation and editing of articles related to the US State Wisconsin, its cities, counties, geography, transportation, culture, history and so on. It aims to expand Wikipedia's resources on Wisconsin in a fair and accurate manner.

[edit] Relations

The parents of this WikiProject are U.S. Regions and U.S. States.
