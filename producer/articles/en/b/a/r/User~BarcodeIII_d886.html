[edit] BarcodeIII

Well I need a hobby and badly, so I thought I'd head back to Wikipedia and try and take that up as a hobby, I was here a while ago and though i contributed now and again I really didn't stick around. Hopefully I will this time.

I am a 21 year old wikipedian from Melbourne, Australia. Currently I am working full time as a retail manager while I find what i want to do in life. So far in my short life i've wanted to become everything from an Astronaut to Prime Minister of Australia to an actor. I guess I am just lost and don't know where I want to be yet, I am hoping wikipedia can help me find that. I think my brain is sincerly wasted on being a retail manager, I do want to return to Uni in the next eighteen to twenty four months, I was studying Computer Science then my Bachelor of Business degree, but left both of them, I think arts is more my area, I guess I just have to wait and see what life throws my way.

I love my city of Melbourne it's history, it's present and it's future. I am also very interested in my ancestry and the histories of the countries that my ancestors hail from, (so far I know of), Ireland, Germany, England, Scotland and Wales.
