Barney In Concert

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Barney In Concert was the first Barney and the Backyard Gang concert. It features an array of traditional children's songs, nursery rhymes and original songs based on traditional tunes. In it Barney, Baby Bop and the children from Barney and the Backyard Gang perform at the Majestic Theater in Dallas, Texas in front of a live audience. The concert was performed in 1991 and the video released a year later. The video works on Actimates Barney.
