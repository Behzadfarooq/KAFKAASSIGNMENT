Barry and Batya Segal are a duo of Messianic worship singer/songwriters living in Jerusalem, Israel. They are married and are of Jewish descent.

[edit] Biography

Barry and Batya Segal are the founding directors of a humanitarian aid center in Jerusalem, known as Vision for Israel & The Joseph Storehouse. Their longing to help build and restore Israel inspired them to this work in 1994. The organization currently functions as an international humanitarian aid center and operates in the Judean Hills just outside of Jerusalem.

Vision for Israel & The Joseph Storehouse supplies essential humanitarian aid to the poor, to new immigrants, to victims of terrorism and their families, and to other organizations desperately in need of supplies. Food, clothing, toiletries, kitchen and household items, blankets, towels, linens, and toys are some of the materials distributed through relief and assistance from Vision for Israel & The Joseph Storehouse.

Barry and Batya also performed as featured artists on the album Adonai.
