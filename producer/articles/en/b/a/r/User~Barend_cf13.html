I have a university-education in English, History, Russian and International Relations, and use wikipedia when I should really be doing other things. Also contribute to Norwegian wikipedia (mainly Nynorsk).

I live in Bergen, Norway.

I wrote my MA-dissertation on the dissolution of Yugoslavia, at the University of East Anglia. On English wikipedia I have mainly written about Norwegian history, occasionally on Yugoslavia and sometimes this and that.
