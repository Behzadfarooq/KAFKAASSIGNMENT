Barbara O. Barnwell

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

 United States Marine Corps Portal

Staff Sergeant Barbara O. Barnwell, USMCR, was the first female Marine to be awarded the Navy and Marine Corps Medal on August 7, 1953 for heroism in saving fellow Marines from drowning.
