Barry Brust (born August 8, 1983 in Swan River, Manitoba) is a Canadian ice hockey goaltender for the Los Angeles Kings of the National Hockey League. He was raised in Kelowna, British Columbia.

[edit] Transactions

  • June 22, 2002 - Drafted by the Minnesota Wild in the 3rd round, 73rd overall.
  • June 9, 2004 - Signed by the Los Angeles Kings as a free agent.
