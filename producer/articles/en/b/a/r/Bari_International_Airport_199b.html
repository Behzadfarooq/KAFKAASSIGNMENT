Bari "Karol Wojtyla" International Airport (Italian: Aeroporto di Bari) (IATA: BRI, ICAO: LIBD) is an airport serving the city of Bari in Italy. It is approximately 9km (5.5 miles) northwest from the town center. The airport is also known as Palese Macchie Airport (Italian: Aeroporto di Palese Macchie) after a nearby neighborhood.
