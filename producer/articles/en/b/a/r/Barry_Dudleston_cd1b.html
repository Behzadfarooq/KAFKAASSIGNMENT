Barry Dudleston (born July 16, 1945), in Cheshire, England, was a first class cricketer. He was a right handed batsman and occasional wicketkeeper. Dudleston played cricket for Rhodesia, Gloucestershire and Leicestershire. By the end of his 295 game career he had made 14747 runs at 32.48 with 32 hundreds and 241 dismissals. He went on to become an umpire and officiated in 2 Test matches and 4 ODI games.
