Barnyard is a videogame based on the animated movie of the same name. It has been released for the Nintendo GameCube, Playstation 2, Game Boy Advance, PC, and the Wii.

[edit] Gameplay

Since it does not follow the lines of the movie, the Barnyard (film) game instead allows the player to create a cow, boy or girl, and choose different fur colors, and patterns. Once the character creation process is completed, the player can begin to do missions for other characters. They can also receive missions through their cell phones, via textmessage. After a multitude of missions revolving around the main characters, the player receives a key to a bike, and is free to roam.
