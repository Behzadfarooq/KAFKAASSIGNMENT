WikiProject Awards

The Userbox Star is awarded to a wikipedian who makes a significant effort to WikiProject Userboxes. They may be awarded for creating an excellent or otherwise notable userbox or making a significant edit to a userbox, or other contribution to WikiProject Userboxes.

Give this to a user by leaving one of these on their talk page:
