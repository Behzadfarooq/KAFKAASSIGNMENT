Baron Darcy

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

There have been several baronies created in the name of Darcy.

  • Baron Darcy of Nocton, of 1299, abeyant since the fourteenth century
  • Baron Darcy de Knayth, of 1332, presently extant
  • Baron Darcy de Darcy, of 1509, extinct in 1635
  • Baron Darcy of Chiche, (also known as Baron Darcy of Temple Hurst) of 1551, extinct in 1640
  • Baron Darcy of Chiche, of 1613, extinct in 1737
  • Baron Darcy de Meinill, of 1641, extinct in 1778
  • Baron Darcy of Navan, of 1721, extinct in 1733.

[edit] Fiction

Lord Darcy is a fictional character in a number of stories by Randall Garrett.
