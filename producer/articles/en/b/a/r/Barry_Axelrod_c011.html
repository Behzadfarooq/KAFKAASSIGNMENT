Barry Axelrod is a sports agent from the United States.

Axelrod, a graduate of UCLA Law School[citation needed], is an attorney that specializes in sports, entertainment and business law. Axelrod has continually been active in many sports associations and fund raisers. Barry has served on the US Anti-Doping Committee; dealing with several issues surrounding drugs and sports. Axelrod has a large client list that includes: Jake Peavy, Jeff Bagwell, Craig Biggio, Matt Morris, Matt Clement, Phil Nevin, as well as San Diego Padres General Manager Kevin Towers, broadcasters Rick Sutcliffe, Mark Grace and Wally Joyner, entertainers Mark Harmon and Pam Dawber, and lastly elite figure skater Michelle Kwan.
