Bartłomiej Grzelak (born August 9, 1981 in Płock, Poland) is a Polish footballer - striker - currently playing for Legia Warszawa. He made his debut for the Polish national team in a friendly versus the United Arab Emirates in Abu Dhabi on December 6, 2006, scoring twice. He came to Legia from Widzew Łódź, where he was a star.
