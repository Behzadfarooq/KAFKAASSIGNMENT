Barry Ryan (born August 28, 1978 in Co. Clare) is an Irish football player who currently plays for St. Patrick's Athletic F.C. in the eircom league Premier Division.

Barry is a goalkeeper and his previous clubs include Dublin City FC, Shamrock Rovers and UCD. He was sacked by Shamrock Rovers during the 2003 season after testing positive for cocaine. During his time with Rovers he kept a clean sheet playing in the UEFA_Intertoto-Cup. After a 9 month ban he returned with Dublin City but couldn't forestall the Vikings' relegation and was dropped in favour of Robbie Horgan for the final handful of games.
