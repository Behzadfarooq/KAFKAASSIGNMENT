Baron St Leonards

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The title of Baron St Leonards was created in the Peerage of the United Kingdom for Edward Burtenshaw Sugden, Lord Chancellor in Lord Derby's government (1852). The title became extinct in 1985.

[edit] Barons St Leonards (1852)

  • Edward Burtenshaw Sugden, 1st Baron St Leonards (1781–1875)
  • Edward Burtenshaw Sugden, 2nd Baron St Leonards (1847–1908)
  • Frank Edward Sugden, 3rd Baron St Leonards (1890–1972)
  • John Gerald Sugden, 4th Baron St Leonards (1950–1985)
