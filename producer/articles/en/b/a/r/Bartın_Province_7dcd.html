Bartın is a small province in northern Turkey on the Black Sea, surrounding the city of Bartın. It is to the east from Zonguldak.

The town of Bartın contains a number of very old wooden houses which are no longer extant in other places.

In Bartın province is the ancient port town of Amasra (Amastris). This town is on two small fortified islands and contains many interesting old buildings and restaurants.

[edit] Districts

Bartın Province is divided into 4 districts (capital district in bold):

  • Amasra
  • Bartın
  • Kurucaşile
  • Ulus
