Barts and The London NHS Trust is an NHS Trust operating in the City of London and East London.

It runs three hospitals:

  • The Royal London Hospital in Whitechapel
  • St Bartholomew's Hospital (Barts) in Smithfield in the City
  • The London Chest Hospital in Bethnal Green
