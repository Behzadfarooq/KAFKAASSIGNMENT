Barry Hunter (born 18 November 1968, Coleraine, Northern Ireland) is a former Northern Ireland international footballer, has recently ended a two match spell as the caretaker-assistant manager at Swindon Town under Adrian Williams.

Williams and Hunter were replaced by Paul Sturrock and Kevin Summerfield.
