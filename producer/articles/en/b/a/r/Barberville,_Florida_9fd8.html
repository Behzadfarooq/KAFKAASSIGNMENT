Barberville, Florida

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Historic Barberville Central High School

Barberville is a town in Volusia County, Florida, United States. It is located at the intersection of State Road 40 and US 17.

[edit] Geography

Barberville is located at 29°11′13″N, 81°25′16″W (29.18694, -81.42111).GR1
