[edit] Include move sets?

Just coming from personal experience, often I find myself going to wikipedia, often when I am trying to find a particular move set for an MK character from google. Because I am a Mortal Kombat fan (Baraka is my personal favorite) I would like to see a list of all the move commands on a separate linked wiki-page ment to deal specifically for the move commands of all the MK characters (not on this page, of course). I think that it is a bit misleading to people who search via a search engine to the most prominant information site on the web, and not be able to get any of the raw information needed (Seriously, if you are going to list and describe every fatality, why isn't there any information on how to actually perform the fatality?). Just a thought, is all...

Wikipedia isn't a game guide, and how to perform moves simply isn't encyclopedic. Now, that said, several members of the MK WikiProject, myself included, have discussed setting up a MK-specific wiki that would have such details as moves and whatnot. Thanks. Well, at least there is something being done about it. I found it slightly misleading/annoying that there isn't any real information here for real MK fans.
