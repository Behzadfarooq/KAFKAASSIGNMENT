薔薇の聖堂 (Bara no seidou or "Rose Cathedral", in English) is Malice Mizer's fourth and final album, released on their indie label, Midi:Nette. It is also considered to be the band's darkest album in their history together. The band's lineup for the album was Mana (guitar, synthesizer) Közi (guitar, synthizer), Yu~ki (bass), (unofficial) vocalist Klaha, and several support singers and drummers. This album also marked the first time that the song's composer would also write the lyrics; previously, the singer (either Tetsu or Gackt) would pen them.
