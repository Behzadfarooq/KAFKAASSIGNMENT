Bartsch is the German name of the Barycz River in Poland. It is also the name of the Eastern-Eruopean soup, called borscht in English, in East Prussia.

People with the surname Bartsch include:

  • Jakob Bartsch (1600-1633), astronomer
  • Johann Bartsch, botanist
  • Adam Bartsch (1757-1821), scholar and artist
  • Jürgen Bartsch (1946-1976), serial killer
  • Dietmar Bartsch (born 1956), politician
