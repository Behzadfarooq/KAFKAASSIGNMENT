Bartosz Domański

Bartosz Domański (born 25 August 1980) is a Polish figure skater that used to start in Men single category. He skated for Unia Dwory club in Oświęcim, with Iwona Mydlarz-Chruścińska as a coach.

He is two time Polish Champion (2002, 2003). He skated twice at the European Championships (2002 - 31st, 2003 - 26th) and three times at the Junior World Championships (1998 - 27th, 1999 - 31st, 2000 - 29th).

He turned pro in early 2000s and has been working together with Sabina Wojtala as a coach in Opole. They both used to train each other at the same time and skated at Polish Nationals 2005/2006, where he placed 2nd. He is not related to another Polish skater; Przemysław Domański.
