Barbara Haworth-Attard (born July 25, 1953) is a Canadian children's author who lives in London, Ontario.

[edit] Works:

  • The Three Wishbells - 1995
  • Dark of the Moon - 1995
  • Home Child - 1996
  • Truth Singer - 1996
  • Buried Treasure - 1998
  • Flying Geese - 2001
  • Irish Chain - 2002
  • Theories of Relativity - 2003 (nominated for a Governor General's Award)
  • A Trail of Broken Dreams: The Gold Rush Diary of Harriet Palmer (Dear Canada) - 2004
  • Forget-Me-Not - 2005

Barbara Haworth-Attard will also author a short diary of Harriet Palmer's during the Christmas week a year or so after the original diary ended, for the Christmas treasury being released in October of 2006, from the Dear Canada series, titled A Season for Miracles: Twelve Tales of Christmas.
