Barbara Alyn Woods (born March 11, 1962 in Chicago, Illinois) is an American actress. Barbara is married to John Lind whom she met on the set of Honey, I Shrunk the Kids: The TV Show. They were married in 1999. They have two daughters.

[edit] Filmography

Barbara is most recently known for her role of Deborah Lee (formerly Deb Scott), on One Tree Hill (2003 - present). She started out recurring in season 1, and became a contract star in the second season. Many fans are familiar with her for her role as Diane Szalinski on Honey, I Shrunk the Kids: The TV Show or in the movie Striptease.

Her TV guest appearances include The Schizoid Man an episode of Star Trek: The Next Generation, Picket Fences, Ally McBeal, Wings, and American Dreams. She has guest starred on 20 different TV shows.
