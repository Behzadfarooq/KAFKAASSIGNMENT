Barbie Mini Kingdom

Barbie Mini Kingdom is a new line of miniature Barbie dolls manufactured by Mattel that are about 6" in height (about the size of the Dawn doll and Pippa Doll), featuring the main female characters from most of the Barbie movies. The line was started in 2006 with a special set of Clara from Barbie in the Nutcracker, Rapunzel from Barbie as Rapunzel, Odette from Barbie of Swan Lake, and Anneliese and Erika from Barbie as the Princess and the Pauper. The line has extended to have birthday dolls that include Annika from Barbie and the Magic of Pegasus. The dolls came with various accessories and a pet. The line also included a castle and a horse-and-carriage sized appropriately for the smaller dolls.

[edit] Trivia

  • Barbie has never been this size before, but the Bild Lilli doll, the predecessor of Barbie, was available in this size.
