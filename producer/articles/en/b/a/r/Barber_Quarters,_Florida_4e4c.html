Barber Quarters is a town in Okeechobee County, Florida, United States. It is a suburb of Okeechobee, located to its north on US 441.

[edit] Geography

Barber Quarters is located at 27°15′08″N, 80°50′22″W (27.2522, -80.8394).GR1
