Barry Roche is an Irish professional football player.

A goalkeeper, Barry signed for Chesterfield F.C. in the summer of 2005 after an impressive trial at the club. The former Nottingham Forest keeper was released by the relegated side at the end of the 2004/05 season.

A former Irish Under-21 International, Roche was mainly an understudy at Nottingham Forest to Darren Ward and Paul Gerrard, but he is expected to challenge much more for the first team shirt at Saltergate with experienced goalkeeper Carl Muggleton.

Roche is contracted to Chesterfield on a two year deal which sees him tied to the club until the summer of 2007.
