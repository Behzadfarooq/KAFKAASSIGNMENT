Barbora Špotáková (born 30 June 1981 in Jablonec nad Nisou) is a Czech javelin thrower.

Her personal best throw is 66.12 metres, achieved when she won the silver medal at the 2006 European Championships in Gothenburg.

She was a heptathlete in her early career.

Year Tournament Venue Result Extra
