[edit] SHIN

SHIN is 'TREMOR' in Japanese. It's named for a Japanese fighter plane (just as Bahamut's Neo and Zero were). SIN appears to have been a misromanisation based on the misassumption that it referred to the English concept. It's even called SHIN in the Advent Children article, so may I ask why it is being reverted? --86.144.60.11 21:45, 9 July 2006 (UTC)
