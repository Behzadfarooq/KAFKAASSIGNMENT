Barry Fuller (born August 25, 1984 in Ashford, Kent) is an English football player who currently plays for Stevenage Borough as a midfielder.

He joined Charlton as an apprentice in the 2004/05 season, but did not feature in any games for the team. In January 2006 he joined Barnet on loan. Fuller nows plays for Stevenage after signing a contract in July 2006.
