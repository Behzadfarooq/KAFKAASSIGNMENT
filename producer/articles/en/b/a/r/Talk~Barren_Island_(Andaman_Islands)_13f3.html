[edit] Coordinates of Barren Island

The webpage on Barren Island (http://www.geos.iitb.ac.in/dchandra/biexp/) says the correct coordinates of Barren Island are 12.29 N, 93.87 E, but we have a different one here. Also, the imagery from Terraserver (http://www.terraserver.com/imagery/image_gx.asp?cpx=93.85&cpy=12.266667&res=30&provider_id=340&t=&OL=) likely shows that the current Wikipedia coordinates may be wrong.
