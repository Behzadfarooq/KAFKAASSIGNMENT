Barrow Haven railway station serves the village of Barrow Haven in North Lincolnshire, England. Stopping services from Barton-on-Humber to Cleethorpes call at the station. All services are provided by Northern Rail who operate the station.

There is a shelter on the platform, with a telephone on Platform 1.

[edit] Services

Services call here between approximately 0645 and 2200 Monday to Saturday. Services call infrequently from 1030 until 1840 at this station on Sundays.
