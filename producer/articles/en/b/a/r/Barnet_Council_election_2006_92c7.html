Barnet Council election 2006

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Elections to Barnet Council were held on 4th May, 2006. The whole council was up for election for the first time since the 2002 election. The Conservative party retained overall control of the council. Overall turnout was 41.65%.

Barnet local elections are held every four years, with the next due in 2010.
