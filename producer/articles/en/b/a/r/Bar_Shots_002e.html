Bar Shots is a mixtape released in 2006 by Obie Trice and hosted by DJ Whoo Kid. It was released just after Obie's sophomore album, Second Round's On Me, in order to help the sales of his album. This mixtape features Bobby Creekwater, Cashis, Stat Quo, Kuniva, Trick Trick, 50 Cent, and Eminem.
