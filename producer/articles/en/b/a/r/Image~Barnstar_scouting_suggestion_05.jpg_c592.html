WikiProject Awards

The Scouting Barnstar is given to those individuals who have made outstanding contributions to the Scouting WikiProject and/or Scouting Portal. Recipients should be fine examples of the Scout Spirit on Wikipedia's coverage of Scouting. It can also be used to award people who have shown the principals of Scouting in their activities on Wikipedia.

This barnstar was introduced by evrik. The design was done by Mang_kiko. The Scouting barnstar was finalized on February 10, 2006.
