Thanks for dropping by my user page. I've been a long time visitor of Wikipedia, but I've only been a contributor for a few months.In my edits I mostly make grammar corrections and whatnot, but if I am particularly knowledgeable about a topic (usually a game, book, or movie) I will contribute a section or two. I suppose that's the point of Wikipedia, isn't it. My acamdemic interests center around business, politics, and history. My writing style is a bit ADHD. As soon as I hit "save page", I'll catch something that I think should be changed. I suppose making a lot of small changes is better then making no changes at all, at least until I can buckle down and get all the work done in one edit.

I love computers and information technology. I drag around my iPod, iPAQ, cell phone, and jumpdrive wherever I go. In the computer world, Mac is my first choice. I hold nothing against Windows, I am fluent in both and own several of both machines. I just like Macs better.

If you have any questions, comments, concerns, etc., feel free to post a message.

Cheers, Barbarossa
