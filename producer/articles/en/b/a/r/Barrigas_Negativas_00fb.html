Barrigas Negativas

Barrigas Negativas is a Rock band from Rio de Janeiro, That was formed in 1995 and is considered, today, one of the greatest from the region's underground scenario. The band is highly influenced by the 50's rock music. The band record your new album, with 10 new songs and release a compilation in memory of the end of the band
