[edit] I Don't Delete Pages

I'm not an administrator, so I'm unable to delete pages on Wikipedia. I watch the list of recently changed (and created) pages for vandalism and broken rules. When I find a page that's unsuitable for Wikipedia, I nominate it for deletion, and sometimes leave a note on the creator's Talk page. Shortly, an administrator will validate the deletion request and either delete the page, or remove the nomination tag if I was wrong to add it.
