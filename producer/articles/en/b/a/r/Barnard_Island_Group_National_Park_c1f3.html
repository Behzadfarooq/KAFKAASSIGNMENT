Barnard Island Group National Park

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Barnard Island Group is a national park in Queensland (Australia), 1296 km northwest of Brisbane.

[edit] Fact sheet

  • Area: 0.6 km²
  • Coordinates: 17°40′29″S, 146°10′22″E
  • Date of establishment: 1994
  • Managing authorities: Queensland Parks and Wildlife Service
  • IUCN category: II
