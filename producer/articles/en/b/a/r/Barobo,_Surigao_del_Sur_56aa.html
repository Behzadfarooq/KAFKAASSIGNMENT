Barobo, Surigao del Sur

Barobo is a 4th class municipality in the province of Surigao del Sur, Philippines. According to the 2000 census, it has a population of 34,558 people in 6,482 households.

[edit] Barangays

Barobo is politically subdivided into 21 barangays.
