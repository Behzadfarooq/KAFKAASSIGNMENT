Sometimes, text removal is the most effective editing. Occasionally known as the Deletionists Barnstar, the Editor's Barnstar Award is given to those individuals who display particularly fine deletions, omissions, reductions, reversions, blocks, bans, and other removals.

Give this to a user by leaving this on their talk page:

[[Image:Barnstar2.png|100px|left|The Editor's Barnstar|frame|Message ~~~~]]

This barnstar was introduced by Guanaco on June 18, 2004.
