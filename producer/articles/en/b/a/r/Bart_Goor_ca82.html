Bart Goor (born April 9, 1973) is a Belgian football left winger currently playing for R.S.C. Anderlecht after falling out with the Feyenoord management. He also plays for the Belgium national football team.

He was part of the Belgium team that played in their own country at Euro 2000, but despite winning the opening game against Sweden, in which Goor scored the opening goal, the Belgians crashed out in disappointing style in front of their own fans at the hands of Turkey.
