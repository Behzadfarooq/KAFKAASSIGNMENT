Barry Owens

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

UGAAWA/Tennent's monthly Merit Award

Barry Owens is an inter-county Gaelic football player for Fermanagh and Teemore.

[edit] Playing

He was awarded an All Star for Fermanagh in 2004 and 2006. He plays at Full Back for Fermanagh and Teemore.
