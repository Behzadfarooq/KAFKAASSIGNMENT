[edit] Final Proposal

Ok, this should be my final proposal. After this (and once a few more items are cleared from our plate, I will ask for yet another Vote or Die situation)

[edit] The Featured Article Medal

Category: Featured Articles

The Featured Article Medal may be awarded to an editor who makes great contributions to three or more articles that have a Featured Article status.

This award was introduced on March 14, 2005 by AllyUnion, and was designed by Ausir and Kimbar.
