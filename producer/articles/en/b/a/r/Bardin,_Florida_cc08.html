Bardin is a suburb of Palatka, Florida.

[edit] Geography

Most of Bardin in forestry and woodlands, but some of it is residential. There is one main road, Bardin Road, and many ajoining roads that lead to the residents' homes.

[edit] Demographics

A majority of the population is Caucasian. Many of these people, though not all, are what people commonly call "Rednecks".

[edit] Education

Many of the students attend Interlachen High School or Jenkins Middle School (in Palatka).
