Barney and the Backyard Gang was a home video series created from 1988 to 1991 that launched the stardom of the worldwide popular television show Barney & Friends. The first three videos of the series featured Barney with a slightly lower voice, a red tongue, and a darker color. Some of the earlier videos created in 1988 (the first episode) and 1989 (the second and third episodes) also include American actress Sandy Duncan as Mom in the first video, entitled The Backyard Show, featuring Michael and Amy wanting to make a surprise birthday for their dad. They use their imaginations and from this comes the birth of Barney. The videos are no longer in print.
