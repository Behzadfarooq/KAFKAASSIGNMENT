Barnton is a civil parish and small village in the north-west of England, just outside the town of Northwich, Cheshire. It has road connections to Runcorn and Warrington and is based largely around the ICI and Brunner Mond chemical works.

Barnton football club were founder members of the Mid Cheshire Football League.

Paula Radcliffe used to live in Barnton.
