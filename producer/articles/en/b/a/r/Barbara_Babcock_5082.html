Barbara Babcock

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Barbara Babcock (born February 27, 1937 in Pasadena, California) is an American actress.

Although she was born in the United States, she spent a large part of her childhood in Tokyo, Japan because her father Conrad Stanton Babcock, Jr. was a general in the U.S. Army. She learned to speak Japanese before English.

In 1994, she was voted one of the 50 Most Beautiful People in the World by People magazine.
