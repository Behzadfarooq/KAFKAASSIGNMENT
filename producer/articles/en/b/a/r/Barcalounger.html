A Barcalounger is a type of armchair made in the United States of America.


[edit] Cultural references

  • In "Rabbit is Rich" by John Updike, protagonist Harry Angstrom has a Barcalounger in his house.
  • In Friends Chandler and Joey both own Barcaloungers
  • In BASEketball, when Joey gives the ball he made himself the same way Coop did, the ball has a chair logo in the same place, but instead of the La-Z Boy logo, it's one for the Barcalounger
