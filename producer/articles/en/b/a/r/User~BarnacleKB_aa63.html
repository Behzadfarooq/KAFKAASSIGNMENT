[edit] Two Cent Description

BarnacleKB is a sardonic Washingtonian who mangles web sites by day for a small consultancy firm. By night, however, his superpowers reveal themselves and he typically saves the world by being immersed in motorsports.

When relaxing, he can often be found listening to loud obnoxius music, or reading books of absolutely no merit.

When all else fails, he sleeps.

He can be found on both Wikibooks and Wikipedia under the pseudonym BarnacleKB.

[edit] Professional Life

I am currently employed for a small strategic marketing consultancy as a web developer and rookie marketing wonk.

I prefer focusing on websites using Extensible Hypertext Markup Language (xHTML) + Cascading Style Sheets (CSS).

Some of my previous occupations have included gigs as a warehouseman, bartender, and bump on a log.

[edit] Recreational Life

My biggest passion in life has to do with motorsport. I love all forms, but am generally less intersted when they only turn left.

I'm fond of automobiles in general, being a huge Bimmerphile in particular.

[edit] Personal Life

I was born in the late 1970s in Winter Park, Florida, spent way too much of my life in Duvall, Washington, and now live in Shoreline, Washington.
