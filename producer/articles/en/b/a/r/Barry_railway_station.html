Barry railway station serves the town of Barry in South Wales. It is located on the Vale of Glamorgan Line, which runs from Cardiff to Bridgend via Barry, Rhoose, and Llantwit Major. A short branch off this line connects Barry to Barry Island.

Passenger services are operated by Arriva Trains Wales as part of the Valley Lines network.
