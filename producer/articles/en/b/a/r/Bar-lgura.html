A bar-lgura is a fictional demon in the Dungeons & Dragons fantasy role-playing game.

Bar-lguras are savage and cruel demons resembling fiendish orangutan. They use to live in isolated and inaccessible areas of The Abyss. Sometimes groups of bar-lgura gather up and form a tribe. They love spread destruction everywhere, even if they are often employed by powerful demon lords as scouts.

The demon lord Ilsidahur is the patron of the bar-lgura demons.
