Barb is a common and generic term for the species of fish within the genus Barbus and the famila Cyprinidae.

[edit] Variety

Genera that contain the species with the common name description of barb include:Barbichthys, Barbodes, Barboides, Barbonymus, Barbopsis, Barbus, Caecobarbus, Capoeta, Catla, Catlacarpio, Chela,, Coptostomabarbus, Cyclocheilichthys, Devario, Discherodontus, Esomus, Garra, Hypselobarbus, Labeobarbus, Leptobarbus, Megarasbora, Osteochilus, Poropuntius, Probarbus, Puntioplites, Rasbora and Sawbwa.

[edit] Origin

The root of the word 'barb' is common in cyprinid names of European languages, likely from exposure to Latin[citation needed], for example:

  • barbi from Finnish
  • barba from Spanish
  • barbeau from French and many others.
