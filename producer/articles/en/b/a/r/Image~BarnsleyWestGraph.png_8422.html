[edit] Summary

United Kingdom general election results for Barnsley West and Penistone constituency from its creation in 1983 to the present. © Jeremy Atherton 2005.

[edit] Notes

The Background colour indicates the party of the sitting MP at any given year.

Graph drawn with IGOR Pro v.5.04

Statistics gathered from:

  • http://www.psr.keele.ac.uk/area/uk/edates.htm
  • http://news.bbc.co.uk/hi/english/static/vote2001/results_constituencies/constituencies/028.stm
  • http://news.bbc.co.uk/1/shared/vote2005/html/28.stm

Parties:

  • Conservative = Conservative Party (UK)
  • Labour = Labour Party (UK)
  • Liberal = SDP-Liberal Alliance (1983–1987); Liberal Democrats (UK) (1992–present).

Data used:
