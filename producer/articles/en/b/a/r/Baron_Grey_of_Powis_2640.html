This article is about Baron Grey de Powis. For the title Baron Powis, see Earl of Powis.

Baron Grey de Powis was a title created in the Peerage of England for John Grey, who was summoned to Parliament under that title on 15 November 1482. On the death of the 3rd Baron in 1551, the barony became extinct.

[edit] Barons Grey de Powis (1482)

  • John Grey, 1st Baron Grey de Powis (1460–1497)
  • John Grey, 2nd Baron Grey de Powis (1485–1504)
  • Edward Grey, 3rd Baron Grey de Powis (died 1551)
