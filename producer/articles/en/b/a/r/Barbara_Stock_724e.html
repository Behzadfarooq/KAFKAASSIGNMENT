Barbara Stock

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia


Barbara Stock (born May 26, 1956) is an American actress, who appeared in the prime-time dramas Spenser: For Hire as Susan Silverman, and Dallas as Liz Adams.

She also appeared in an episode of Seinfeld where she seduced George Costanza off a subway, brought him to a hotel, handcuffed him to a bed and stole all of his eight dollars as well as his clothes.
