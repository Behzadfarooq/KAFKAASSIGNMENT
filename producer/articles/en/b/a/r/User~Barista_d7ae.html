[edit] A Story

Once upon a time there was a gent by the name of Mike, who was introduced to the Grande Cafe Mocha whilst attending Colorado State University. The combination of chocolate syrup added to a cappucino seemed to be the perfect pick-me-up beverage for attending early morning classes. However, one time, when he was short of cash and in dire need of caffiene infusion, he found himself repeating "Gottahavacuppamocha" ad nauseum due to the stresses of caffiene withdrawal. Years later, when he was more stable, Mike got the notion of registering a website to try out his coding skills and serve as a portfolio for his graphics, 3D, and apparel design work. He had the perfect domain name for his website: www.gottahavacuppamocha.com

[edit] Category cleanup

Ever wondered how many categories Wikipedia has? I did. Would you be surprised if it was over 68,000? I was. Lots of categories are redundant or underpopulated. Some of them probably come from typos, some from bots, some were probably started with the intention of adding more articles. At any rate, 68000 is a lot of categories. I think that with some work it can be brought down to a more manageable number (like 65,000 -lol-).
