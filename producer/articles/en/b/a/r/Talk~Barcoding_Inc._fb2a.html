[edit] Link Spam

This page is 100% link spam thinly disguised as some company's sob-story history. A fine job rising above tough circumstances but it's not that compelling. Lots of companies have (to quote the user Jehochman) "revenues exceeding $20m, and thousands of clients nationally" and lots have "won national awards" and tens of thousands have "several thousand daily visitors" to their web sites. It's highly unlikely this article "is of interest to the public" or anyone beyond the company's hired SEO.

That SEO would of course be none other than the user Jehochman, who earns his living at SEO. Nothing wrong with that, but Wikipedia wasn't created to help SEOs. Besides whoever "requested a listing of prominent Auto ID Data Capture companies" must have changed their mind since Barcoding Inc isn't linked on that page any longer, and when they were they were the only reseller (value-added or not) there. For this article's justification ("Why have a useless red link when we can have a blue one?") to be valid shouldn't some other resellers' sites be included, you know, to avoid the appearance of conflict of interest?

100% link spam.
