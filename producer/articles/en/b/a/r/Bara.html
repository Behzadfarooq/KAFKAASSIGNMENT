Bara is a male Arabic given name.

People named Bara include:

  • Al-Bara ibn Azib [1] (Bara ibn ‘Adhib)
  • Al-Bara' ibn al-Malik al-Ansari [2]
  • Nickname of Guy Herzog, Belgian cartoonist

People using it in their patronymic include:

  • Bashir ibn al-Bara' ibn Ma'rur al-Ansari [3]

Groups:

  • Bara Ibn Malik [4]

Locations:

  • Bara, East Lothian, Scotland.
  • Bara Hundred, Scania, Sweden; or the town Bara in Svedala Municipality.
  • Bara, Syria - a "dead city" and present day village in north west Syria
  • University of Eastern Africa, Baraton
  • Bara District, Nepal
  • Bara, a village in Trebišov District, Slovakia
  • Bara, a commune in Timiş County, Romania
  • Bara River, Pakistan
  • Bara, an ethnicity in central southern Madagascar speaking the Bara Malagasy dialect.
