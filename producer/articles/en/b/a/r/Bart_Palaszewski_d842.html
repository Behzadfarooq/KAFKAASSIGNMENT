Bart Palaszewski born (May 30, 1983 in Warsaw, Poland) is a professional mixed martial arts (MMA) fighter. He is currently fighting for the Quad City Silverbacks of the International Fight League.

[edit] Bio

After emigrating from Poland with his family, Palaszewski was unsure about what he wanted to do in life. He trained some, worked as a photographer and graphic designer and other computer-related jobs. Then, after seeing his first MMA event at a show in McHenry, Illinois, Palaszewski was hooked.
