[edit] As a name

  • An English given name that means "son of Tholmai" or "son of the furrows" meaning one who is rich in land.
  • An English or Scottish surname with the same onomastic meaning as the above as a given name.

Places with the name:

  • St. Bartholomew's School, Berkshire
  • Bartholomew School Eynsham
