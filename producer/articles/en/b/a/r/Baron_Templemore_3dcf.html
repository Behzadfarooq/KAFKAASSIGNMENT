Baron Templemore

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Baron Templemore is a title created on September 10, 1831 in the Peerage of the United Kingdom for Arthur Chichester. It is currently held by the Marquess of Donegall, who inherited the title in 1953.

[edit] Barons Templemore (1831)

  • Arthur Chichester, 1st Baron Templemore (1797–1837)
  • Henry Spencer Chichester, 2nd Baron Templemore (1821–1906)
  • Arthur Henry Chichester, 3rd Baron Templemore (1854–1924)
  • Arthur Claud Spencer Chichester, 4th Baron Templemore (1880–1953)
  • Dermot Richard Claud Chichester, 5th Baron Templemore (b. 1916) (became 7th Marquess of Donegall in 1975)
