Barry Yelverton, 3rd Viscount Avonmore

Barry John Yelverton, 3rd Viscount Avonmore (21 February 1790 – 24 October 1870, Dublin) was an Irish nobleman. He was the son of William Charles Yelverton, 2nd Viscount Avonmore and Mary Reade. He married, firstly, Jane Boothe, daughter of Thomas Boothe, in 1811 and had the following issue:

  • Hon. Barry Charles Yelverton (1814-1853)
  • Hon. Sydney Eloisa Yelverton (1817-1883)
  • Hon. George Frederick William Yelverton (1818-1860)
  • Hon. Mary Augusta Yelverton (1820-1843)
  • Hon. Adelaide Matilda Yelverton (1821-1884), married 1860, Lt-Gen Humphrey Lyons, Indian Army

The 3rd Viscount Avonmore married secondly, Cecilia O'Keefe, daughter of Charles O'Keefe and Letitia Yelverton, on 1 August 1822. They had the following issue:

  • Hon. Letitia Yelverton (1823-)
  • Hon. William Charles Yelverton, 4th Viscount Avonmore (1824-1883)
  • Hon. Louisa Elizabeth Yelverton (1827-)
  • Hon. Cecilia Priscilla Yelverton (1829-1854)
  • Hon. Walter Algionby Yelverton (1832-1844)
  • Hon. Anna Yelverton (1833-)
  • Hon. Maletta Yelverton (1839-)
