[edit] Summary

Photo taken on flight over Madrid Barajas Airport in 2005.

Terminals 1, 2, and 3, at center-left of the photo, are connected. Terminals 4 and 4-satelite are the two large terminals near the right of the photo. Terminal 4 (which is 1.4km long) is near the top-right, and 4-s (1km long) is below it, between the two runways.

Madrid Barajas has 4 runaways . The 2 runaways on the picture, 18R/36L and 18L/36R, are usually used for landings (configurated north) . 18R/36L is the longest commercial runaway in Europe, measuring 4,540 meters long and 60 meters wide.


Intercontinental and Non Schengen Agreement (UK,Ireland,etc) european flights arrive at T-4 Satellite. An underground automatic train connects T-4 Satellite with T-4 . The new Real Madrid training camp is the isolated square on the top-center of the image, just above the acces highway.

Terminal 4 has 39 gates, 4-S has 26, and 1, 2, & 3 together have 42; there are, in total, 107 gates, plus dozens of remote stands.


Español: Terminales 1, 2 y 3 son las 3 terminales contiguas en la parte izquierda de la imagen .

Terminal 4 y Terminal 4 satelite son las 2 Terminales gigantescas en el centro de la imagen . T-4 Satelite es la que esta entre las pistas . Solo se puede acceder a T-4 Satelite a traves de un tren subterraneo que parte de la Terminal 4 .

Terminal 1,2 y 3 tienen 37 pasarelas (fingers) . T4 tiene 39 pasarelas y la T4-S tiene 26 pasarelas. En total Madrid Barajas dispone de 107 pasarelas (finger) de embarque directo a los aviones .
