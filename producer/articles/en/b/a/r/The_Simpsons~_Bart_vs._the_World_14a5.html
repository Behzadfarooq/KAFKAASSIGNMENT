The Simpsons: Bart vs. the World
Developer(s) Imagineering
Publisher(s) Acclaim
Release date(s) December 1991
Genre(s) Action
Mode(s) Single player
Platform(s) Amiga, Game Gear, NES, Sega Master System

The game was released in 1991 and 1992 for the NES, Game Boy, Sega Master System and Game Gear, and in 1993 for the Commodore Amiga. It was developed by Imagineering and published by Acclaim Entertainment.
