Barry Clemens

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

John Barry Clemens (born May 1, 1943) is a former professional basketball player. The 6' 6" Clemens attended Ohio Wesleyan University before being drafted by the NBA's New York Knicks in 1965, and he went on to have a productive 11 year career with five teams: the Knicks, the Chicago Bulls, the Seattle SuperSonics, the Cleveland Cavaliers, and the Portland Trail Blazers. He retired in 1976 with career totals of 5,316 points and 2,526 rebounds.


[edit] Personal Life

He now lives in Cleveland, Ohio with his wife, Vivian.
