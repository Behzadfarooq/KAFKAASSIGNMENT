Barbie Griffin is the stage name for Michelle McCurry, a female nude model. She is sometimes credited as Sandy Reed, Lori Larson, Michelle M., Michelle McCurry, Barbie or Veronica Black.

During her time adult modeling, McCurry used the Barbie Griffin alias, which was owned by Mac & Bumble. When she allegedly breached her contract by using the alias on other websites, Mac & Bumble filed a lawsuit against McCurry in 2005. Since then, she has discontinued her adult-oriented modeling.[1] As for any other endeavor, McCurry promoted her website, BarbieG.com, with the claim that it would be online spring of 2005. At present, she resides in Malibu, California.
