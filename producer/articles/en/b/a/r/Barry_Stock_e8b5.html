Barry Stock

Barry Stock is the guitarist for Three Days Grace, a Canadian rock band. He joined the band after the recording of their self-titled debut.

[edit] Trivia

  • His vice is smoking.
  • His favorite drink is apple juice.
  • He has two full arm sleeves (tattoos).
  • His musical influences are Led Zeppelin, Black Sabbath, and Tool.
