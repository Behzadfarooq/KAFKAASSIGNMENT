Barack Hussein Obama, Sr. (1936 - 1982) was the father of Barack Obama. He was born in 1936 in in the Nyanza Province of Kenya, and died in a car crash in Nairobi, Kenya in 1982, leaving three wives, six sons and a daughter. All but one of his children live in Britain or the United States. He met Ann Dunham and married her in 1960 in Hawaii, but they later divorced. He is buried in the village of Nyangoma-Kogelo, Siaya District, Kenya. He studied economics toward a PhD at Harvard University after attending the University of Hawaii.
