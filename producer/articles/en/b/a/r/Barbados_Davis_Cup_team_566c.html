Barbados Davis Cup team

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Barbados Davis Cup team represents Barbados in Davis Cup tennis competition and are governed by the Barbados Tennis Association.

Barbados currently compete in the Americas Zone of Group III. They reached the Group II semifinals on their debut in 1990.

[edit] History

Barbados competed in its first Davis Cup in 1990. Barbadian players previously competed on the Caribbean/West Indies team.
