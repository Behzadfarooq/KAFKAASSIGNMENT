Barnaul State Pedagogical University (Russian: Барнаульский государственный педагогический университет) is a university in Barnaul, Russia.

Barnaul Teachers Institute was established in 1933 and renamed into Barnaul State Pedagogical Institute in 1941. In 1993 this institution of higher education received a university status.

[edit] Faculties

  • Linguistic Institute (former Faculty of Foreign Languages)
  • Faculty of Education
  • Faculty of Elementary Education
  • Faculty of History
  • Faculty of Mathematics and Informatics
  • Faculty of Philology
  • Faculty of Physical Training
  • Faculty of Physics
  • Faculty of Qualification Level Raising and Professional Retraining of Educational Workers
