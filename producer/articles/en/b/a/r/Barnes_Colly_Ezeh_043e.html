Barnes Colly Ezeh (born December 10, 1979) is a football player form Hong Kong (where he is known in Chinese as 史提夫). Originally a citizen of Nigeria, Ezeh has become a permanent resident of Hong Kong and hold a Hong Kong SAR passport. He currently plays for Xiangxue Sun Hei.
