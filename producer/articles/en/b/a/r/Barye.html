The barye (symbol: ba) is the centimetre-gram-second (CGS) unit of pressure. It is equal to 1 dyne per square centimetre.

Other names: barad, barrie, bary, baryd, baryed, barie.

1 Ba = 0.1 Pa = 0.1 N/m2
