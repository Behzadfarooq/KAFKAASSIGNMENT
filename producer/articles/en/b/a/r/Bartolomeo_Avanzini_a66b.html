Bartolomeo Avanzini

Bartolomeo Avanzini (1608-1658) was an Italian architect of the Baroque period, active mainly in Modena.

The design for the Palazzo Ducale of Modena has been attributed to Avanzini, though between 1631-4 Girolamo Rainaldi played a role in the initial plans. There appear to have been comments made on the construction by Borromini, Cortona, and Bernini. The latter viewed progress during a stop in Modena in 1665. Camillo-Guarino Guarini also played a role after 1680.
