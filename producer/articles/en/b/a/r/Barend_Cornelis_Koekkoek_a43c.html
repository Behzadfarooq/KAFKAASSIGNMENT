Barend Cornelis Koekkoek

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Barend Cornelis Koekkoek (Oct 11, 1803, Middelburg - Apr 5, 1862, Kleve) was a Dutch artist. He was the father of Johannes Hermanus Barend Koekkoek.
