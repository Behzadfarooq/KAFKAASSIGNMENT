The 2007 season is the eighth season of the Bartercard Cup run by the New Zealand Rugby League.

[edit] Notes

  • The Auckland Lions are also fielding a team in the NSWRL Premier League this year and as a result less players from the New Zealand Warriors are expected to feature in the Bartercard Cup.
  • A Saturday double header at Mt Smart Stadium number two will take place each weekend with one game screened live on Maori Television and the other one delayed on a Sunday. This replaces 2006's Monday Night Football.
  • There will only be one Split Round (Round Eight over Queens Birthday Weekend) meaning a September Grand Final.

[edit] Season Stadings

The Bartercard Cup is scheduled to commence on Saturday the 14th of April and end on the weekend of September 8-9.[1]

[edit] The Playoffs

The Top Four teams will make the Playoffs.
