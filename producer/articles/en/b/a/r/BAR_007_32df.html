BAR 007

The BAR 007 was a Formula One car for the 2005 season. The number 3 seat was taken by Jenson Button, with Takuma Sato in the number 4 seat. However, Sato was replaced by Anthony Davidson for the Malaysian Grand Prix as he had the flu. The teams test driver was Enrique Bernoldi along with Daivdson. The engine was a Honda RA005e 3.0 V10. The teams main sponsor was Lucky Strike.
