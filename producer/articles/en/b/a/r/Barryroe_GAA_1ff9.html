Barryoe GAA club is a Gaelic football and Hurling club located in the parish of Barryroe in County Cork. It draws its players from the villages of Courtmacsherry, Butlerstown, Lislevane and surounding areas. The club participates in the West Cork (Carbery) division of Cork.

[edit] Honours

  • Cork Junior Hurling Championship: Runners Up 1994
  • Cork Junior B Football Championship: 2002
  • Cork Minor B Hurling Championship: Beating Finalist 2005
  • Cork Minor C Football Championship: 2005
  • West Cork Junior A Hurling Championship: Winners 1981, 1982, 1986, 1987, 1994, 2006 Runners Up 1985, 1992, 1996, 1999, 2000, 2002
  • West Cork Junior A Football Championship: Runners-Up: 1980, 1994, 2003
  • West Cork Junior B Hurling Championship: Winners 1933, 1953 Runners-Up: 1950, 1964, 1974, 1977,
  • West Cork Junior B Football Championship: Winners 1964, 1979, 1989, 1998, 2002 Runners-Up: 1944, 1967, 1976, 1988
