Barbara Ann Crumm (born 1978, Mt. Kisco, New York) is an American artist, writer, singer, actress and former model.

She is well-known for her work as a model first with Richard Kern and most notably with her ex-fiance Dave Naz. She appears in 5 different art monographs by both.

Her own art-opening "Model, Muse, Artist, Identity" was a feature in an article in the Village Voice. She plans to put a variation of this show on tour at some point with mainly self-portraiture.

Currently is attending art school in the South, working on photography. She is also a freelance writer for Coagula Art Journal, as well as several other publications. She plays music in her new band, The New Barbara Jean (a solo project).

She was featured in a film entitled "Through the Sun " which is due to come out at the Tulsa Film Festival next year. In addition to being featured in "American Fame Part 2" by noted indie-director Cam Archer. She is rumored to be in his next film, "Pull".[citation needed]

She has been romantically linked to Guiseppe Andrews, Greg Dulli and Dave Naz.
