Barbara Tucker is an African American House music/Rhythm and Blues/Soul music singer/songwriter/choreographer born in Brooklyn on 19 March, the year is unknown. She is an popular New York based performer and promoter whose impact on the mainstream music scene is felt more in Europe than in her home country. Tucker has scored a string of hits on the Hot Dance Music/Club Play chart in the nineties and into the 21st century, including six songs that hit #1:

  • 1994 "Beautiful People"
  • 1995 "I Get Lifted"
  • 1995 "Stay Together"
  • 1998 "Everybody Dance"
  • 2000 "Stop Playing With My Mind" (Barbara Tucker featuring Darryl D'Bonneau)
  • 2005 "Most Precious Love" (Blaze presents U.D.A.U.F.L. featuring Barbara Tucker), #44* (Belgium Ultratop 50)

Barbara is currently signed to the soulful dance music label, Defected U.K.
