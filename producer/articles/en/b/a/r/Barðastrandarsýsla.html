Barðastrandarsýsla

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Barðastrandarsýsla is a county in northwestern Iceland in the Vestfirðir region. It includes Bjargtangar, the westernmost point in Iceland and westernmost point in Europe. It is named after Barðaströnd coast. It is often divided into east and west counties (Austur- and Vestur-).
