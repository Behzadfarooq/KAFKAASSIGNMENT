Barricade is a horror/splatter feature film from German director Timo Rose. It stars American actors Raine Brown and Joseph Zaso and Germany's André Reissig, Thomas Kercmar, Manoush, and Andreas Pape. It was written by Timo Rose and Ted Geoghegan.

[edit] Plot

Spoiler warning: Plot and/or ending details follow.

The film follows three friends (Brown, Zaso, and Reissig) as they travel to the Black Forest of Germany. While there, the trio is attacked by a family of deformed mountain people who murder and cannibalize anyone who ventures into their secluded territory.
