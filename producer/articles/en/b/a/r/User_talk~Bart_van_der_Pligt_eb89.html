The government, represented by Minister Verdonk, had a long debate with Parliament. In this debate Verdonk made several conflicting statements, adding even more confusion. One of these statements was that Hirsi Ali 'still has her citizenship'. I guess she meant 'Hirsi Ali still has a Dutch passport'. The government's position is that Hirsi Ali has never had Dutch citizenship and therefore her passport will be withdrawn in six weeks time.
The result of the debate was that Parliament forced Verdonk to reconsider the 'never had citizenship' part. Verdonk conceded (she will 'look into it') but meanwhile tried to convince the tv viewer that she was as firm and resulute as ever and had not made any mistake at all.
