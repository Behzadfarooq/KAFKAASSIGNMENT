[edit] Summary

Description

Iraq's Deputy Prime Minister Barham Salih conducts a press conference in the Pentagon on Sept. 14, 2006. Salih discussed some of the many problems being faced by his government and stressed that progress is being made. Salilh expressed gratitude for all America has done for his nation and remained hopeful that the U.S. will continue to stay the course with Iraq's fledgling democracy.
