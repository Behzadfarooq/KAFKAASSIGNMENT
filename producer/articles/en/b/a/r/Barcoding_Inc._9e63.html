Barcoding Inc. is an automatic identification and data capture (AIDC) systems integration company. Products and services provided use barcode, RFID, Wireless LAN and mobile computer technology. Based in Baltimore, Maryland, Barcoding Inc. has regional sales offices throughout the United States, and a European affiliate. The company has received the Inc. 500, Deloitte Technology Fast 50, Deloitte Fast 500 and VAR Business 500 awards.

As of 2005, Barcoding maintains relationships with leading hardware manufacturers, including Symbol Technologies, Intermec, and Zebra Technologies.

In 2006, Barcoding acquired Accuity ID, a Radio Frequency Identification (RFID) systems company.[1] RFID is a potential replacement for barcoding. A company executive said that RFID shows promise for future use, but barcoding is currectly less expensive and more standardized.[2]
