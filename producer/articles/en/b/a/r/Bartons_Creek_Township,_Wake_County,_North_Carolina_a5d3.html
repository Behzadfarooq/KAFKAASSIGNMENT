Bartons Creek Township, Wake County, North Carolina

Bartons Creek Township (also designated Township 2) is one of twenty townships within Wake County, North Carolina. As of the 2000 census, Bartons Creek Township had a population of 18,408, a 57.3% increase over 1990, and a population density of 0.73 people per acre (180/km²).

Bartons Creek Township, occupying 25,152 acres (102 km²) in the northwestern corner of Wake County, includes portions of the city of Raleigh.
