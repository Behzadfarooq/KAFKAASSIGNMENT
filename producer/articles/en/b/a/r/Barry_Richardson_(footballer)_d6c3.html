Barry Richardson (born on August 5, 1969 in Wallsend in the North East of England), is a professional English footballer who plays as a goalkeeper for Doncaster Rovers in Football League One.

He has played for Sunderland, Seaham Red Star (loan), Scarborough, Stockport County, Northampton Town, Preston North End, Lincoln City, Mansfield Town (loan) Sheffield Wednesday (loan), Doncaster Rovers (1st spell), Halifax Town and Doncaster Rovers (2nd spell)

During his time at Northampton, Richardson became known both as "Psycho" (for his alleged willingness to involve himself in physical disputes) and "Coco", the name of a clown and a reference to a series of mistakes he made, notably one in a 1-1 draw at Maidstone United in 1992.

Richardson is currently the goalkeeping coach at Doncaster Rovers, whilst also remaining on the clubs playing team as an emergency backup.
