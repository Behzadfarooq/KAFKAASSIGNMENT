"Barwick Green" is the theme music to the long-running BBC Radio 4 soap opera The Archers. It is a "maypole dance" from the suite My Native Heath, written in 1924 by the Yorkshire composer Arthur Wood.

Comedian Billy Connolly has said that this tune should replace "God Save the Queen" as the national anthem of the United Kingdom.
