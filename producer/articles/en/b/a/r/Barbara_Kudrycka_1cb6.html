Barbara Kudrycka

Barbara Kudrycka (born on 22 January 1956 in Kolno) is a Polish politician and Member of the European Parliament for the PD & WM with the Platforma Obywatelska, part of the European People's Party and sits on the European Parliament's Committee on Civil Liberties, Justice and Home Affairs.

Kudrycka is a substitute for the Committee on Legal Affairs and a member of the Delegation for relations with Belarus.

[edit] Education

  • Master's in Administration (1978), Master's (1985), Doctorate (1985), assistant professor (1995), professor of law (2003) at the Department of Law and Administration, University of Warsaw
  • Researcher (since 1978), head of the Dept
  • 2002: of Administrative Law at the University of Warsaw, Białystok branch
  • rector of the Białystok School of Public Administration
  • 2004: Member of the Citizens' Platform

[edit] Career

  • since 2002: Member of the board of EGPA (European Group of Public Administration
  • 2002-2004: Member of ECOSOC UN
  • 2000-2004: President of NISPAcee
  • since 2000: Vice-Chairman of the Public Administration Education Association in Białystok
