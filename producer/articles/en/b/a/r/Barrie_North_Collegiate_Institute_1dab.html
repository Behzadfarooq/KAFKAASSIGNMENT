Barrie North Collegiate Institute is a public secondary school (grades 9-12) located in Barrie, Ontario, Canada. It currently has an enrolment of about 1335 students. The administrative staff includes Chris Samis (principal), Mike Abram (vice-principal)(Moved to Collingwood Collegiate) and Diane Fletcher (vice-principal).
