Barrio Fino En Directo is an album by reggaeton artist Daddy Yankee. It includes the older songs from Barrio Fino and five new ones, including "Rompe". The album was relatively popular after being released, selling more than 500,000 copies in its first five weeks in the US. It had over 1,000,000 presales Worldwide.
