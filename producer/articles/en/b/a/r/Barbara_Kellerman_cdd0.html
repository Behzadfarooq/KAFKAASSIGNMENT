Barbara Kellerman (born Manchester, 30th December 1949) (surname at birth: Kellermann) is an English actress, noted for her roles in film and television. She trained at The Rose Bruford College.

Her film credits include: Satan's Slave, The Monster Club and The Sea Wolves.

Television appearances include: Space: 1999, The Glittering Prizes, 1990, The Professionals, The Mad Death and The Chronicles of Narnia (TV miniseries). She played the White Witch in the Lion, The Witch & The Wardrobe in 1988 and continued on to be Lady of the Green Kirtle in 1990 in the Silver Chair. She also portrayed superspy Modesty Blaise in a radio adaptation of the novel Last Day in Limbo.

She was married to Robin Scobey.
