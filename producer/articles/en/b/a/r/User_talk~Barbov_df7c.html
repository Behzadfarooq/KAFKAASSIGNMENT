[edit] Bulgarian politician stubs

Greetings Barbov

I've sorted the Bulgarian stubs looking for material for the {{Bulgaria-politician-stub}}, and my count ended at 57 articles. Since WP:WSS's standard guideline for new categories is 60 stubs, it is very very close. If you or anyone else would create, say 5 new stub articles, the new template will be sure to be approved. (It might anyway, but reaching the number 60 will make it certain.) The articles don't have to contain more information than e.g. "XYZ is a Bulgarian politician. He / she has been a member of Parliament since ... representing the ... political party." Please remember to tag any such articles with either {{Bulgaria-politician-stub}} or both {{Bulgaria-bio-stub}} and {{euro-politician-stub}}. I've used the last solution myself. If the template is approved, I will update these articles to the new template. If you create any new stubs, please tell us where you listed the proposal. I'm listing a copy of this message at WikiProject Bulgaria. Best regards. Valentinian (talk) 23:52, 22 April 2006 (UTC)

[edit] SS

Видях, че сте наясно с шведския и затова ще Ви помоля, ако знаете как се произнася двойното "SS", което се изписва в края на шведските фамилни имена- като българско "С" или като българско "Ш". Например Magnusson- Магнусон ли е или Магнушон. Например Andersson е Андершон, но тук имаме "RS", за което е казано в наредбите за транскрипции, че е "РШ", но за "SS" няма указания. Аз съм Потребител:Прон от Българската уикипедия.

Благодаря за шведското "SS". Поздрави! Прон
