Barney McAll (born 1966) is a jazz pianist and composer.

Barney McAll moved to New York City from Australia in 1997 to join Gary Bartz's band. As well as remaining to be a member of the Gary Bartz quartet, he is also plays with the Josh Roseman Unit, Fred Wesley and the JBâ€™s, Kurt Rosenwinkel's "Heartcore", and the Dewey Redman quartet. He also leads his own bands "Duty Free" and his most recent group M.O.D.A.S. He has recorded or performed with many other musicians.

Barney has performed in Russia, Lithuania, Cuba, Yugoslavia, as well as at many of the major festivals[citation needed] in numerous cities of Europe, Australia, Canada, the USA and Japan
