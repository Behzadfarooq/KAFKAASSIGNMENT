Bart Smits

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

You have new messages (last change).

Bart Smits is a Dutch heavy metal singer. He was the original singer in The Gathering, and he sang on their first album Always..., released in 1992. The following year he left them to form Wish. After three full-length albums and four EPs, the band split up in 2003. Smits' latest work is Wish's Single, released in 2003.
