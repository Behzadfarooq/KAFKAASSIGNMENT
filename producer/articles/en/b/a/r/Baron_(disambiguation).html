Baron (disambiguation)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Baron may mean:

  • Baron, a title bestowed or recognised by various rulers or governments of Europe, of Tonga and of Japan
  • Baron of beef, a cut of beef consisting of a double sirloin.
  • Baron of the Exchequer, a judge of the English Court of Exchequer, which was merged into the High Court by the Judicature Act 1873
  • Baron Services, owner of the VIPIR radar system.
  • Beechcraft Baron, an aircraft made by the Beechcraft company.
  • Baron may refer to a fictional kingdom in Final Fantasy IV.
