The Barabati Stadium[1] is a cricket venue in the east indian city of Cuttack, Orissa. The stadium has hosted ODI matches since January 1982 and Test match since January 1987.

[edit] Trivia

  • Kapil Dev bagged his 300th test wicket when he bowled Rumesh Ratnayake in January 1987
  • The Stadium hosted matches in both the World Cups hosted in the subcontinent - 1987 Cricket World Cup (Australia beat Zimbabwe by 70 runs)and 1996 Cricket World Cup (India beat Kenya by 7 Wickets)
  • Mohammad Azharuddin and Ajay Jadeja put on an unbroken 275 run partnership against Zimbabwe which was the then highest ODI partnership
    • The above partnership is the current world record for the 4th Wicket in ODI Cricket[4].
    • The above partnership is currently listed at #4 in all-time partnerships for India in ODI Cricket[5].
    • The partnership is the current world record for any unbroken partnership[6].
