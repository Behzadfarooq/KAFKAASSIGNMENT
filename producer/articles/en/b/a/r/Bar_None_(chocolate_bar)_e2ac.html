Bar None (chocolate bar)

Hershey's Bar None was a chocolate bar created by The Hershey Company and released in 1986. It went nationwide in September, 1987 after testing in California. The original formula consisted of a cocoa wafer, chocolate filling, peanuts and a milk chocolate coating. In 1993, the product was reformulated into two wafers with chocolate coating, peanuts, and caramel.
