Barry John Wallace or known by his stage name Barry Fratelli (born April 23, 1979) is the bassist in Scottish band The Fratellis.

[edit] Trivia

He is the only member of the band, to have a distinct holding in the name of the band. His mother's original surname was "Fratelli", but when she remarried, Barry followed his mother's example by taking the married name.

Barry Fratelli's real name is actually Barry John Wallace. He was born and raised in Greenfield, in the east end of Glasgow and went to school at Thorntree Primary and then Eastbank Academy. The year book of 1997 lists Barry as Barry Wallace.[citation needed]
