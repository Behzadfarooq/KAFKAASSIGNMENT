Barry Callaghan (born November 30, 1986 in Glasgow) is a Scottish footballer who currently plays as right wing-back for Dundee United in the Scottish Premier League.

He was given his only senior appearance for the club so far by then-manager Ian McCall in March 2005, as a substitute against Aberdeen. In August 2006, Callaghan joined Queen of the South, where McCall is now manager, on a six month loan transfer.

It is likely to be Callaghan's only senior appearance for the club after the club announced he, along with Gregg Burnett, would be leaving at the end of the season.[1]
