The word Barrens is used in the following contexts:

  • Pine barrens, a type of ecosystem found in the Northeastern United States.
  • The Pine Barrens (New Jersey), a well known pine barren in the state of New Jersey.
  • The Barrens, a location in the Warcraft Universe.

Barrens may also refer to:

  • Urchin barrens, where the proliferation of sea urchins has caused a massive kelp die-off.
  • Lost in the Barrens, the third Farley Mowat novel, published in 1956.
  • The Pine Barrens (book), about the history, people, and biology of the New Jersey Pine Barrens.
  • List of pine barrens which includes, but is not limited to, the New Jersey Pine Barrens.
