baroque (バロック), or brq, was a Japanese rock band, that formed in 2001 and was signed to S'Cube, later to Firewall Div. (Each divisions of Free-Will), and distributed by Sony Music.

baroque is credited with starting the Oshare Kei movement, a sub category of Visual Kei. [1] When they moved to a major label they adopted more casual clothing.

[edit] History

baroque played in Singapore's Asian Youth Music Festival in 2002.

Their first full album was released September 9, 2004, and on September 11th the band announced it was breaking up after finishing contractual obligations. The last concert was a sold out performance at Zepp Tokyo on December 25, 2004 (which holds about 4,000 people [2]).

Ryo and Kei have re-formed Kannivalism and are currently signed to Free-Will.
