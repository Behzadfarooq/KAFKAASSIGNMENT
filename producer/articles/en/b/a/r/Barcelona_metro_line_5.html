Barcelona metro line 5

Line 5, currently known as Cornellà Centre - Horta, often simply called "Línia Blava" (Blue line) is a metro line in Barcelona operated by TMB, and part of the ATM fare-integrated transport network.

It first opened in 1930. Line 5 is currently 16.6 km. long and has 23 stations, from Cornellà to Horta in the northern part of Barcelona, where it's going to be extended towards Vall d'Hebron, meeting L3.
