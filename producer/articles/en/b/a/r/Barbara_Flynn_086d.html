Barbara Flynn

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Barbara Flynn (born 5 August 1948 in Hastings, East Sussex, England) is a British actress. She became known for her appearances in several successful television comedy dramas during the 1980s. She tends to play, in her own words, "feisty strong women" (BBC interview).
