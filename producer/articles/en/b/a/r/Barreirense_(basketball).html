Barreirense Basquetebol is a professional basketball team based in Barreiro, Portugal. It plays in LCB.


[edit] Achievements

  • Portuguese Basketball League (LCB): 2

1956/57, 1957/58

  • Portuguese Basketball Cup: 2

1956/57, 1959/60, 1962/63, 1981/1982, 1983/1984, 1984/1985
