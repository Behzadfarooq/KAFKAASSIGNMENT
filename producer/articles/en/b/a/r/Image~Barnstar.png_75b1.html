Wikipedia is a collaboratively built encyclopedia, and all play their part. Some write fantastic new articles, some do research, some do copyediting and formatting, some create wonderful new images for us, some import appropriate material from other sources, some help organize content, some provide welcome and guidance. We appreciate all their help.

The Original Barnstar Award is given to recognise particularly fine contributions to Wikipedia, to let people know that their hard work is seen and appreciated, and that we thank them. To give the award to someone, just edit their user page to include the image, and say why you've given it. Don't hesitate, be bold!

You can see a list of people that have been awarded a barnstar in the list of pages linking to this image at the bottom of this page.
