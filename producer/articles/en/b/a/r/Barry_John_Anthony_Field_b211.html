Barry John Anthony Field

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Barry John Anthony Field (born 4 July 1946) is a British Conservative politician. He gained the constituency of Isle of Wight from the Liberals at 1987 general election, and held the seat until he stood down at the 1997 general election.
