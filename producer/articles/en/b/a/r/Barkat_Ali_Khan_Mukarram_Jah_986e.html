His Highness Prince Barkat Ali Khan Mukarram Jah Asaf Jah VIII born 6th October 1933 in the Hilafet Palace in Nice, France is the current pretender to the throne of Hyderabad. He is the son of Azam Jah and Durru Shehvar and the grandson of Osman Ali Khan, the last Nizam of Hyderabad state, and of the last Ottoman Caliph ‘Abdu’l-Mijid II.

Son (Azmet) and Daughter Of Barkat Ali Khan Mukarram Jah Asaf Jah VIII .
