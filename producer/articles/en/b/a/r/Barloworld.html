Barloworld (UCI Team Code: BAR) is a UCI Professional Continental cycling team based in the United Kingdom and participates in UCI Continental Circuits races and when selected as a wildcard to UCI ProTour events. They are managed by Claudio Corti with Valerio Tebaldi, Christian Andersen and Alberto Volpi assisting as directeur sportifs. Barloworld's most prominent rider is South African Robert Hunter.
