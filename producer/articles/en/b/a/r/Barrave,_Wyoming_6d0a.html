A former town in Wyoming.

Barrave was a small group of buildings used by people planning to build a new utopia. It was disbanded in 2006. In June of 2006, a group of new High school graduates from Cheyenne decided to form a new town based on utopian ideals, including those in John Lennon's song "Imagine".
