Barbary Coast (film)

Barbary Coast is a black-and-white film directed by Howard Hawks. It was released in 1935.

[edit] Main cast

  • Miriam Hopkins as Mary 'Swan' Rutledge
  • Edward G. Robinson as Luis Chamalis
  • Joel McCrea as Jim Carmichael
