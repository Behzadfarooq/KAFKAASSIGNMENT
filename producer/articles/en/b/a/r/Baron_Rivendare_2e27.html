Baron Rivendare is the final boss of the undead side of Stratholme in the World of Warcraft video game.

He is the death knight leader of the Scourge forces in the city of Stratholme, a former noble of Lordaeron who coordinates the efforts of the Lich King in the Plaguelands.

He resides in Slaughter Square, atop his fierce undead mount the Deathcharger. He kidnapped and imprisoned Ysida, an agent of the Argent Dawn.

Spoiler warning: Plot and/or ending details follow.

The undead side is reached through the main entrance of Stratholme or through the Elder Service Entrance, which is opened with the Key to the City, attained from Magistrate Barthilas . When all of the bosses are defeated and the rooms they guarded cleared, you may reach Baron in slaughter square. You must first kill several high level elite undead creatures, kill Ramstein the Gorger, kill a swarm of level 60 non-elite skeletons and zombies, and fight the number of black guard that emerges from the gate. (4 or 5). After all of this, the Baron will yell for you to enter. When he is attacked, he will attack with his sword and summon wave after wave of easy skeletons to kill your casters and healers. If these skeletons are not quickly killed, he will cast Dark Pact, killing them, and healing himself greatly - more than enough that you won't be able to kill him if you can't take down the skeletons. He also has a shadow damage aura that does 150 (resistible) damage every second to everyone near him.
