Baron Athenry

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The title of Baron Athenry was created in the Peerage of Ireland in 1172. The 19th Baron was created Earl of Louth in the Peerage of Ireland in 1749, but both titles became extinct upon his death in 1799.

[edit] Earl of Louth, 1st creation (1319)

  • John de Bermingham, 1st Earl of Louth (d. 1329), perage extinct on his death.

[edit] Earl of Louth, 2nd creation (1749)
