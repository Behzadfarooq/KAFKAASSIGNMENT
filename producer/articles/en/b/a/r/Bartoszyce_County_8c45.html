Bartoszyce County (Polish: powiat bartoszycki) is a powiat (county) in Poland, in Warmian-Masurian Voivodeship. The county seat is the town of Bartoszyce . The population numbers 61,687 (2005). Its area is 1308,54 km².

The county consists of:

  • urban communes (gminas): Bartoszyce, Górowo Iławeckie
  • urban-rural communes: Bisztynek, Sępopol
  • rural communes: Bartoszyce, Górowo Iławeckie
  • towns: Bartoszyce, Górowo Iławeckie, Bisztynek, Sępopol
