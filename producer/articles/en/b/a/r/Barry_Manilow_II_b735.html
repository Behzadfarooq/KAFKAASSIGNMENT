Barry Manilow II was the second studio Album by Barry Manilow released in 1974. It included the mega hit song Mandy, as well as "It's a Miracle".

The album's cover illustration was later parodised by Ray Stevens in the picture sleeve illustration for his 1980 single I Need Your Help, Barry Manilow.
