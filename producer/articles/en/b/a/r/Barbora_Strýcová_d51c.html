Barbora Strýcová-Záhlavová (born March 28, 1986, Plzeň, Czech Republic) is a professional female tennis player from the Czech Republic. As of Jan 29, 2007, she is 167th in the WTA rankings.

Her highest WTA Ranking has been World No. 55, a ranking she achieved on October 25, 2004.
