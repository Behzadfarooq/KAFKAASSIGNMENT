Barbie and the 12 Dancing Princesses is a direct-to-video Barbie movie, based on the fairytale "The Twelve Dancing Princesses". It was released in the United States on September 19, 2006.

[edit] Plot

Spoiler warning: Plot and/or ending details follow.

Barbie stars as a beautiful princess named Genevieve with eleven sisters, Ashlyn, Blair, Courtney, Delia, Edeline, Fallon, Hadley, Isla, Janessa, Kathleen and Lacey, who all love to dance. Along with their own personal likes and interests, all twelve love to dance and live in a beautiful castle with their widowed father, King Randolph.

Disheartened by his daughter's unladylike behavior and his inability to understand them, the King summons his cousin, Duchess Rowena to help him with their upbringing. Rowena strips the castle of all fun: no color, singing, playing, and no dancing. She forces the princesses to practice "proper" behavior, such as fanning. Disappointed with the changes, the princesses find that their mother's favorite story book, which recounts a tale of a magic land where a princess could dance for three nights, is actually true. The entrance to this magic land is in their bedchamber, on twelve tiles, each having each princess's favorite flower on it, and matching each storybook for the girls, to open the doorway they have to dance from stone to stone, oldest to youngest, and on the last stone, they must twirl 3 times. In the magic land any wish is granted by flowers that sprinkle gold dust and the princesses dance the night away. Lacey, the youngest princess, hurts her knee and discovers that water from the fountain heals the wound instantly.

The next day the royal cobbler and love interest of Princess Genevieve, Derek arrives to find that they have worn their new dancing shoes out in a single night and that they are covered with gold dust. The duchess continues to deprive them of any enjoyment and prevents them singing for their father, who has fallen ill. The doctor gives Rowena medicine for the king, of which she promptly disposes, so she can poison the king. That night, the princesses danced throughout the night.

The next day, the princesses are still exhausted, and the duchess demands to know why. Lacey tells her the truth, which upsets the duchess in disbelief. The princesses return again to their magic land after being locked into their bedroom. Derek returns, having discovered that Rowena is stealing from the king and dealing with an apothecary or pharmacist (presumably for the poison). Using the pattern of Genevieve's dancing, Derek enters the magic land and reports his findings.

Meanwhile Genevieve's talking pet, a kitten, fights with Rowena's talking pet, a monkey. The monkey determines the pattern for opening the gateway. Soon Rowena enters the magic land and discovers the ability for wishes to come true. She removes a flower that sprinkles gold dust that grants wishes and returns with it. Then her henchman destroys the stone pattern that forms the gateway.

Trapped in the magic land Genevieve wishes to know the way out and they find that the same pattern that led them in will let them out. Soon Genevieve discovers that alone she can no longer open the gateway. Only by dancing with Derek, Genevieve can open the gateway to return to their land, returning this time to their mother's dance pavillion, which surprisingly looks alike to the pavillion in the magic land.

Genevieve discovers that the king has been tricked into proclaiming Rowena acting queen until he recovers, but Rowena has poisoned him (presumably to death) and sent guards to capture any stray princesses. Using their diverse talents the princesses distract or disable the guards and enter the palace to confront Rowena. Lacey ignores Genevieve's instructions and enters the palace to give Genevieve the magic water in her vial and is seized by Rowena's henchmen.

Derek and Genevieve defeat the magically summoned suits of armor that defend Rowena but discover that Rowena has the upper hand when the henchman drags in Lacey. Noting that Genevieve is the source of most of her sister's resistance, Rowena wishes that Genevieve would dance forever and ever, but when the golden flowers spew dust Genevieve produces her fan and fans the dust onto Rowena, whose henchman joins her in dancing. The two villains then dance their way out of the castle and out of the movie.

It is revealed that Lacey saved some of the water from the magic land, and using it, Lacey revives the king, who explains that Rowena was poisoning him. Genevieve and Derek wed and the King remarks that this is truly a remarkable day, as Genevieve is finally on time for something (her wedding).

Spoilers end here.

[edit] Characters

The names of the 12 princesses start with the first 12 letters of the alphabet; for example, A for Ashlyn, B for Blair, etc. Each Princess has an associated flower - the flowers that appear on the books and the stones are on their dresses. On Ashlyn-Genevieve, it is just below the neckline of their dresses. On the twins, it is off to the side. On the triplets, it's located on the sash in the middle where the dress meets the upper part. (Much like a belt buckle.)

Genevieve (GEN-e-veev)
The graceful and good-natured heroine of the story, who intentionally tends to be late for all sorts of occasions. Genevieve is the seventh sister of twelve, who loves her family dearly. The most important thing in her life, aside from her family, is dancing. Her favourite gemstone is a pink opal and her flower is a pink rose. She has platinum blonde hair and wears a dark pink and white dress.
Ashlyn (ASH-lin)
The oldest sister, who is practical, calm, and knowledgable. She loves music and especially likes to play the flute. Ashlyn is straightforward and cares for her sisters, for whom she tries to act as a role model for. Her favourite gemstone is the garnet and her flower is a geranium. She has dark brown hair and wears a purple dress. She is the daughter of Drazena Judy 'Field' and has a younger brother.


Blair (BLARE)
The second sister, who loves horses, tends to be a bit of a dreamer, and is close to Ashlyn. She is very vocal and is the first to complain about Rowena's presence in their lives. Her flower is the larkspur and her gem is the ruby. She has black hair and wears a red dress.


Courtney (COURT-nee)
The third sister, who enjoys reading and is frequently found with her nose in a book. She is easily oblivious to everything around her as a result. Her flower is the forget-me-not and her gemstone is sapphire. She has brown hair and wears a dark blue dress. (note: "Courtney" is also a recurring character in the Barbie toy line)
Delia (DEE-lee-ah)
The fourth sister, who likes sports, especially croquet and horseback riding. She is close to Edeline. Her favourite flower is the sunflower and her gemstone is the peridot. She has blonde hair and wears a green dress.
Edeline (ED-e-line)
The fifth sister. Like Delia, she loves to play sports, especially badminton. She tends to be messy and disorganized. Her favourite flower and gemstone are honeysuckle and citrine respectively. She has light brown hair and wears an orange dress. Edeline and Blair and sisters for real

and are the daughters of actress Murielle Maple.

Fallon (FAL-len)
The sixth sister, who loves animals, plays the harp, and tends to be a romantic who likes happy endings. She likes pearls and camellia flowers. She has strawberry blonde hair and wears a pink dress.
Hadley (HAD-lee)
The eighth sister and Isla's twin. She loves to dance on stilts, something she shares with her sister. Her favourite gemstone is the topaz and narcissus flowers are her favourite. She has blonde hair and wears a turqouise dress.
Isla (AYE-lah)
The ninth sister and Hadley's twin. Athletic Isla wants to become an acrobat and enjoys dancing on stilts. Emeralds are her favourite gemstone and she likes lily of the valley flowers. She has brown hair and wears a purple dress.
Janessa (JA-ness-ah)
Janessa is the bossiest of the triplets and the tenth sister. She is interested in bugs and collecting them. Her gem is an aquamarine and her flower is a jonquil. She has brown hair and wears a light turquoise dress.
Kathleen (KATH-leen)
The eleventh sister and one of the triplets. She loves art and enjoys painting, even if it can become somewhat messy. She is close to Janessa. Her jewel is a pink diamond and her flower is a daisy. She has red hair and wears a light pink dress.
Lacey (LACE-ee)
The youngest sister, Lacey is a triplet along with Janessa and Kathleen. Lacey loves her sister Genevieve dearly. The smallest of the triplets and the most timid and clumsy, she is often uncertain of her place her large family. Her favorite stone is an amethyst and her favorite flower is a white lily. She has blonde hair and wears a lilac dress.
Derek (DER-ek)
The handsome Royal Cobbler (shoemaker), who is a friend of Genevieve's. He secretly loves her and she feels the same for him. He intends to help Genevieve stop Rowena.
Felix (Fee-liks)
Derek's pet parrot, who has the ability to speak, and a wonderful sense of humor. He is funny, sarcastic, and loves his purple feathers. He also wants Derek and Genevieve to be married, and to do this he tells Genevieve the truth Derek dosen't want Genevieve to know.
Duchess Rowena (ROW-ee-nah)
The King's cousin, who is brought into the castle in order to help him raise his twelve daughters. She strips the castle of all fun and secretly poisons the King in order to take over the kingdom. She has a monkey named Brutus, whom Fallon likes and Twyla hates.
Queen Isabella (Is-ah-BELL-ah)
The deceased mother of the twelve. She is only seen once, as a portrait.
King Randolph (RAN-dolph)
The father of the twelve dancing princesses, who is slowly being poisoned by his cousin Rowena.
Twyla (TWY-lah)
A cute kitten in the palace, who tries to act tough in front of her enemies. She is very proud and brave and does not get along with Rowena's monkey Brutus. She believes that she is a tigress (the reasons behind this belief are unknown and not explained in the film, though she seems to truly believe it), as shown when she tells Brutus that she is descended from "a line of fierce tigers from the jungles of India".
Brutus (Broo-tus)
Rowena's pet monkey. Who dosen't hide the fact that he is just as arrogant as Rowena. He loves making Twyla angry, and stays in the 12 princesses room many times to find dirt on the girls to make them suffer.
