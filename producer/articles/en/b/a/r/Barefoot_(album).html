Barefoot (Yechefa)
Studio album by Ninette Tayeb
Released September 7, 2006
Genre Pop rock
Length 43:56
Producer(s) Aviv Geffen

Barefoot is the debut album of Israeli pop rock singer Ninette Tayeb.

The album, produced by the Israeli rock singer Aviv Gefen, includes 12 tracks. The first single from the album, "Kshe'Ata Kan" ("When you're here", Hebrew: "כשאתה כאן"), was released on July the 12th, 2006.

Few weeks later the second single "Hakol Yachol Likrot" ("Anything Can Happen", Hebrew: "הכול יכול לקרות") was released.

24 hours after the album was released, it was recognized as a gold record. Two weeks later, it was certified platinum.
