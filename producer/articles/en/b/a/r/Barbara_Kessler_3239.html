Barbara Kessler is an American folk-rock singer/songwriter. She began her career performing in clubs on Cape Cod and driving an ice cream truck, then began performing at open mikes in Boston, and continues to be part of the Boston folk scene. Perhaps her best known composition is "Deep Country", which first appared on 1994's live Stranger to this Land.

  • Notion (1996)
