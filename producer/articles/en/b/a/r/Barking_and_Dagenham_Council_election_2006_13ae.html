Barking and Dagenham Council election 2006

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Elections for London Borough of Barking and Dagenham Council were held on Thursday 4 May 2006. The whole council was up for election. Barking and Dagenham is split up into 17 wards, each electing 3 councilors, so a total of 51 seats are up for election.
