Barry Thornton

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Air Marshal Barry M Thornton CB MSc BSc CEng FIMechE FCMI FRAeS RAF is the Chief of Material (Strike), Defence Equipment & Support Organisation.

Thornton was the last Commander in Chief Personnel and Training Command from 06 January 2006 until 01 April 2007, when the command was merged into Air Command.
