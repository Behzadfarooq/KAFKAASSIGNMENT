Barrie Examiner

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Barrie Examiner is the daily newspaper for Barrie, Ontario and the area.

[edit] History

The Examiner was founded in 1864 and has been publishing as a daily newspaper since then.

[edit] Present Day

The Examiner is owned by Osprey Media. The newspaper is located in one of the fastest growing communities in the country.

The newspaper publishes six days a week, Monday to Saturday. It serves the City of Barrie serving about 150,000 homes each week. In addition, every Friday The Examiner publishes a special local edition delivered to more than 10,000 homes in Innisfil, a town just south of Barrie.
