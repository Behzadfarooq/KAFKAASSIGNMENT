[edit] Barnewall of Crickstown, Co. Meath (1623)

The Baronetcy of Barnewall of Crickstown in the County of Meath, was created in the Baronetage of Ireland in 1623 for Sir Patrick Barnewall.

  • Sir Patrick Barnewall, 1st Baronet (d. 1624)
  • Sir Richard Barnewall, 2nd Baronet (1602-c. 1662)
  • Sir Patrick Barnewall, 3rd Baronet (d. aft. 1695)
  • Sir George Barnewall, 4th Baronet (d. 1735)
  • Sir George Barnewall, 5th Baronet (d. 1750)
  • Sir Thomas Barnewall, 6th Baronet (d. 1790)
  • Sir Bartholomew Barnewall, 7th Baronet (d. 1802)
  • Sir Robert Barnewall, 8th Baronet (1757-1836)
  • Sir Aylmer John Barnewall, 9th Baronet (1789-1838)
  • Sir Reginald Aylmer John Barnewall, 10th Baronet *1838-1909)
  • Sir John Robert Barnewall, 11th Baronet (1850-1936)
  • Sir Reginald John Barnewall, 12th Baronet (1888-1961)
  • Sir Reginald Robert Barnewall, 13th Baronet (b. 1924)

The Heir-apparent is the present holder's son, The Hon. Reginald Robert Barnewall (b. 26 October 1963)
