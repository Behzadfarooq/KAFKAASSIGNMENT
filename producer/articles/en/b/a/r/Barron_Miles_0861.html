Barron Miles

Barron Miles plays the position of defensive back for the British Columbia Lions in the Canadian Football League. He is 5'9" and weighs 176 lbs. He was acquired from the Montreal Alouettes in the offseason after the end of the 2004 season.

In 2004, Barron Miles received the Tom Pate Award for his outstanding contributions to the CFL and the community. In 2003, he was elected to the East Division All-Star team and tied the Alouettes' team record for career blocked kicks (6 blocked kicks). In 2002, he was a CFL All-Star and received the James P. McCaffrey Trophy. Barron Miles was also an all-star during 1998, 1999, and 2000 seasons. He received the Frank Gibson trophy in 1998.
