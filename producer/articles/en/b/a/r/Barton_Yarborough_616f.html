Barton Yarborough (October 2, 1900 â€“ December 19, 1951) in Texas, was an American actor. He was born in Texas and died in Pasadena of a heart attack.

He worked extensively in radio drama, and was probably best known for his roles as Doc Long on Carlton E. Morse's "I Love a Mystery" and Sergeant Ben Romero on Dragnet. His other work includes the role of "Skip Turner" in Adventures by Morse, also by Carlton E. Morse.[1]
