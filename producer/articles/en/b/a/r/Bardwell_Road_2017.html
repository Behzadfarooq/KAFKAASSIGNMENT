School House at the Dragon School, on Bardwell Road.

Bardwell Road is a road in North Oxford, England, off the Banbury Road. It is the location of the Dragon School, a well-known preparatory school. The Bardwell Road Centre, one of the two locations of St Clare's, Oxford, is to be found here as well.

Also off the road is the Cherwell Boathouse where punts can be rented for use on the River Cherwell.

Northmoor Road, Charlbury Road lead off Bardwell Road and Linton Road is at the far end.
