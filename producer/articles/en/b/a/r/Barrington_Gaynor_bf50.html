Barrington Gaynor (born 1966) is a retired Jamaican football defender.

Nicknamed ' Cobra', Gaynor played for Jamaican National Premier League team Harbour View F.C. and ended his active career as player/coach of Bull Bay. Also, he went through the US college system and became a three times All-American at Alderson-Broaddus College (in 1988, 1989 and 1991).

He earned several caps for the Reggae Boyz in the 1980's and early 1990's and has played many years as a fullback with Anthony Corbett in the national team defense.

Gaynor has been confirmed as the coach of 2006 National Premier League champions, Waterhouse F.C., for the 2006/07 season. Curiously, he was sent on a coaching course in England just before the start of the season. In the 2005/2006 season, he was assistant coach to Donovan Hayles at Harbour View.
