Barbara Delaplace is a Canadian science fiction writer.

Born in Vancouver, British Columbia, she immigrated to Florida, USA, when she married Jack C. Haldeman II. Delaplace won the Homer Award for best short story of 1992 for her "Black Ice", originally published in the theme anthology Aladdin: Master of the Lamp.
