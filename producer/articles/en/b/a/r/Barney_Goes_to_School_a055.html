Barney Goes to School is a Barney and the Backyard Gang home video. Released on video in 1990, it features any array of traditional Children's songs & School Songs.

[edit] Plot

Tina is lonely at school when the Barney doll is transformed into a larger than life figure. Tina and Barney meets Michael, Amy, Adam, Luci, and Derek. So Barney learns ABC, 123, Color, and other school fun! Barney & Tina says goodbye to friends.
