Barbarians (TV series)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Barbarians is a mini-series on The History Channel which tells the story of four of the most barbaric tribes of the early and late Middle Ages. These include the Vikings, the Huns, the Mongols and the Goths. Barbarians II features the Vandals, the Saxons, the Franks and the Lombards. This program tells about what the groups did and also who they conquered.
