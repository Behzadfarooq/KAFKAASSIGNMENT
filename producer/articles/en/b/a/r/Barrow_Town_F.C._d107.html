Barrow Town F.C. is a football club based in Barrow upon Soar, near Loughborough, Leicestershire, England.

In 1981, they changed their name from Barrow Old Boys F.C. to their present name. They have reached the 2nd round of the FA Vase three times in their history.

For the 2005-06 season, they are members of the Leicestershire Senior League Premier Division.
