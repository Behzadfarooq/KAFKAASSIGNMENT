Barrie Central Collegiate Institute is a public secondary school (grades 9-12) located in Barrie, Ontario, Canada. It was founded in 1843, and currently has an enrollment of about 1135 students. The administrative staff includes Russell Atkinson (principal), Mrs. Ives (vice-principal) and Ms. Barakauskas (vice-principal).
