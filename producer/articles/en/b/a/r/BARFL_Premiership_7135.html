BARFL Premiership

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The BARFL Premiership is an Australian rules football competition in England and the main league of the British Australian Rules Football League.

The BARFL Premiership began in 1990, and has been more commonly known as the London Premiership since 2001 due to a restructuring of the BARFL and the introduction of regional and Scottish divisions. It is regarded as the highest level of competition in the United Kingdom.
