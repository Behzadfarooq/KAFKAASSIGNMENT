The Barooga Football Club, nicknamed the Hawks, is an Australian Rules Football club playing in the Murray Football League.

The club is based in the town of Barooga located in the Riverina district of New South Wales.

Although Barooga was a founding member of the Murray Football League in 1931, it has spent most of its history in the Picola & District Football League. The club re-joined the Murray Football League in 1989.

[edit] Premierships

  • Murray Football League
    • 1992, 1993, 1994, 1997
  • Picola & District Football League
    • 1960, 1961, 1976, 1978, 1979, 1981, 1982, 1986, 1987
