Barbara Ann Moore (born August 21, 1968 in Spokane, Washington) is an American glamour model and actress. She was chosen as Playboy's Playmate of the Month in December 1992 and has appeared in numerous Playboy videos.

[edit] Biography

Barbara Moore was born in Spokane, Washington and raised in Seattle[citation needed] with two brothers Terry and Michael (one of two is a Marine). She was raised in a strict Christian household. By the time she was 19 years old, she was working as a flight attendant in Tennessee.

Prior to appearing in Playboy, Barbara appeared in music videos for Reba McEntire, Waylon Jennings, Hank Williams Jr., Aerosmith and Phil Collins. This led to several appearances in Playboy magazine and many guest star turns in television shows such as Married... with Children and Silk Stalkings.

After her Playmate appearance, she soon took up ballroom dancing, and became a U.S. National Pro-Am Ballroom Dance Champion.

Her degree of "celebrity" and "visibility" increased in 2004, when she began dating actor Lorenzo Lamas. Ironically, she originally met Lamas through her friend (and Playboy Playmate) Shauna Sand who was married to Lamas at the time. In July of 2005, Barbara was scheduled to marry Lamas, but the wedding was suddenly called off on the morning of the wedding date.
