Barnstar/Barnstars of National Merit

A Barnstar of National Merit may be awarded to an editor who contributes significantly to expand or improve Wikipedia's coverage of any given country.

For other variations, see Commons, the national variations.

{{subst: The Barnstar of National Merit|message ~~~~}} Geography The Barnstar of National Merit

The Barnstar of National Merit may be awarded to an editor who contributes significantly to expand or improve Wikipedia's coverage of any given country.

This barnstar was proposed by Redux on February 23, 2005, and was designed by Zscout370.
Category:Africa The Africa Award

The Africa Award may be awarded to those who have done exemplary work on Africa-related articles. This award was introduced by Wikipedia:Africa-related regional notice board at the end of 2005.
Category:Poland Polish Barnstar of National Merit

The Polish Barnstar of National Merit for articles about Poland.
Category:Canada The CanCon Award

The CanCon Award is awarded to Wikipedians with great contribution to Canada-related articles. Introduced by JTBurman.
Category:Turkey Turkish Barnstar of National Merit

The Turkish Barnstar of National Merit for articles related to Turkey.
Category:Puerto Rico Puerto Rico National Merit Medal

To be awarded to dedicated users whose contributions and dedication to articles regarding Puerto Rico are above and beyond from what is normally expected. Generally, the recipient should have at least one featured article about Puerto Rico or has created a significant amount of Puerto Rico related articles. Introduced by Tony the Marine
