Barry Ashbee Trophy

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Barry Ashbee Trophy is an annual award given by the Philadelphia Flyers to the team's Best Defenseman. The award was started during the 1974-75 season after Barry Ashbee's career-ending eye injury. The award has been given out annually ever since.

[edit] Recipients

The following players have been given the Barry Ashbee Trophy:

  • 1974-75: Joe Watson
  • 1975-76: Jim Watson
  • 1976-77: Andre Dupont
  • 1977-78: Jim Watson
  • 1978-79: Andre Dupont
  • 1979-80: Bob Dailey
  • 1980-81: Andre Dupont
  • 1981-82: Frank Bathe
  • 1982-83: Mark Howe
  • 1983-84: Frank Bathe
  • 1984-85: Brad McCrimmon
  • 1985-86: Mark Howe
  • 1986-87: Mark Howe
  • 1987-88: Mark Howe
  • 1988-89: Kjell Samuelsson
  • 1989-90: Gord Murphy
  • 1990-91: Kjell Samuelsson
  • 1991-92: Steve Duchesne
  • 1992-93: Garry Galley
  • 1993-94: Garry Galley
  • 1994-95: Eric Desjardins
  • 1995-96: Eric Desjardins
  • 1996-97: Eric Desjardins
  • 1997-98: Eric Desjardins
  • 1998-99: Eric Desjardins
  • 1999-00: Eric Desjardins
  • 2000-01: Dan McGillis
  • 2001-02: Kim Johnsson
  • 2002-03: Eric Desjardins
  • 2003-04: Kim Johnsson
  • 2005-06: Joni Pitkanen
  • 2006-07: Derian Hatcher
