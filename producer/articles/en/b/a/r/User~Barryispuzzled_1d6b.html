[edit] Biography

Puzzle Master 19:00, 3 January 2007 (UTC) is busy in real life and doesn't respond swiftly to queries.

Barry R. Clarke has written a regular puzzle column for The Daily Telegraph (UK) and is a published author with Dover, Cambridge University Press, and Mensa. In 2004, his HAIL CLIP puzzle was featured on BBC4 TV. He is a man of varied talents: with his self-published book The Shakespeare Puzzle, he is an expert on the Baconian-Shakespeare authorship theory; his comedy sketches have been broadcast on BBC and ITV; and his short film The Bath In The Garden was voted one of the Top 20 British amateur short films of 1999. He has a M.Sc. degree in quantum mechanics with published papers and is currently working on a non-probabilistic theory of the hydrogen atom. As if this were not enough, he is an accomplished guitarist, cartoonist, and can juggle 3 balls! barryispuzzled.com
