[edit] Barroso blue

Is there a reason why Barroso is blue? I understand he came from the socialist party of Portugal (PSD). If Peter Mandleson is red despite the Labour party of the UK being not remotely social democratic these days, then why would Barroso be a conservative? EddieBernard 15:12, 20 November 2005 (UTC)

This is an error and I have fixed it. Andreww 20:11, 21 November 2005 (UTC)
According to Social Democratic Party (Portugal): "The party's name can be misleading: it is a party of the centre-right, and does not advocate social democracy in any usual sense of the term. The party's delegates to the European Parliament have, since the late 1990s, sat with the European People's Party, along with the Conservative and Christian Democratic parties of many European countries." So I think the article is correct (Labour party members do not sit with this group). Andreww 20:14, 21 November 2005 (UTC)
