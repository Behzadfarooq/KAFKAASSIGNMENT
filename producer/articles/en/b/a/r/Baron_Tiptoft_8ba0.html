Baron Tiptoft

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The title of Baron Tiptoft was created on January 7, 1426 in the Peerage of England as a barony by writ for Sir John Tiptoft. It became abeyant in 1485.

[edit] Barons Tiptoft (1426)

  • John Tiptoft, 1st Baron Tiptoft (d. 1443)
  • John Tiptoft, 1st Earl of Worcester & 2nd Baron Tiptoft (1427–1470) (attainted 1470)
  • Edward Tiptoft, 2nd Earl of Worcester & 3rd Baron Tiptoft (c. 1469–1485) (restored 1471)
