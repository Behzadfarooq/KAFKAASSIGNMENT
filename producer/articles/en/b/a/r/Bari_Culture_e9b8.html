Bari Culture

Typically this term applies to the culture of Sandal Bar Area but it can also be used for whole of the bar area of punjab which includes Neeli Bar, Ganji Bar and Kirana Bar also. This area has thousands of years of history as it is comprised of the main tributaries of river Indus. Harrapa is also situated in the Neeli Bar Area. Almost all the famous romances related to the land of Punjab belong to this region, the famous epic of Heer Ranjha decsribes the romance story which came into existence on the soil of this land and them became the classic of Punjabi Language Poetry was written by one of the son of this land(Waris Shah) on the soil of this land and in the language of this land. The founder of Sikhism, Baba Guru Nanak Dev and his famous companion Bhai Mardana beloneged to the land of Bar Area. The famous freedom fighter in history of India and Pakistan Shaheed Bhagat Singh was also born here. Rae Ahmed Nawaz Khan Kharal the famous freedom fighter of the war of 1857 and Dulla Bhatti who rebelled against Mughal monarchy also belonged here. This land is still alive With its vast historical background this region presenting a colorful cultural heritage.
