Bartonia is a genus of the Gentian family (Gentianaceae), tribe Gentianeae, subtribe Swertiinae. Members of this genus are called screwstems. Bartonia was also the name of a genus in the Loasaceae family, but all of those "Bartonia" species are now generally classified under the genus Mentzelia.

[edit] Species in this genus include:

  • Bartonia aurea Lindl.
  • Bartonia paniculata (Michx.) Muhl., Branched bartonia, Twisting screwstem.
  • Bartonia texana Correll, Texas screwstem.
  • Bartonia verna (Michx.) Raf. ex Bart., White screwstem.
  • Bartonia virginica (L.) B.S.P., Yellow screwstem, Yellow bartonia.
