[edit] General Information

Barkley Inc. is an independent advertising agency based in Kansas City, MO. The agency was founded in 1964 as Fromm Inc. and later named Barkley & Evergreen, to reflect the street names of its Kansas City and Detroit offices, respectively. In 1997, the agency became a 100% employee-owned ESOP. In 4th Quarter 2006, Barkley adopted a new identity, which correlated with its move to a new headquarters in the artistic Crossroads District in downtown Kansas City. The Barkley building is the former worldwide headquarters of TWA and owner Howard Hughes. While occupying the building over 40 years ago, Hughes placed a Walt-Disney Company designed rocketship on the roof of the building. Barkley has since resurrected an exact replica of the three-story-tall rocket and adopted it as part of the agency identity and culture. Barkley is one of the top ten independent advertising agencies in the U.S. (source: Advertising Age Adweek)

Services: Advertising, Interactive, Public Relations, Sponsorships & Events, Customer Insight Management, Design, Motion Graphics, Cause Branding, Media Planning & Buying, Research

Clients Include: Blue Bunny ice cream, Build-A-Bear Workshop, Cellular One, The Coca-Cola Company, Lee Jeans, Sonic Drive-In,

Recent Awards: 2006 Communication Arts Advertising Annual, Original Juan Campaign + Negro Leagues Baseball Museum Poster; 2006 Graphis Design Annual, 40/40 Logo; 2006 Cannes International Advertising Festival Shortlist, Z-Brand Jeans Promotion; 2006 Gold National ADDY, Sonic Drive-In Graphic Standards Manual

Recent Interactive Work: Tater Tot Rejection, Make TJ Drink
