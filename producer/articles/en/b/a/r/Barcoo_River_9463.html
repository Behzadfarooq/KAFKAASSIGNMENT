Barcoo River

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Barcoo River in western Queensland, Australia rises on the northern slopes of the Warrego Range and unites with the Thomson River to form Cooper's Creek. The first European to see the river was Thomas Mitchell in 1846, who named it Victoria Stream. It was renamed by Edmund Kennedy for a name supplied by local Aborigines.
