Barjati (Hindi: बड़जाती, IAST: Baḍjātī) is a gotra (people) of Jats found in Sikar and Nagaur districts of Rajasthan in India. Literally it means 'big caste'. Barjati people are dwelling in Birania and Thethalia villages in Sikar district in Rajasthan.
