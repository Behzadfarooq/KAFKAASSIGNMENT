Barclay Hope is a Canadian actor, born on 25 February 1958 in Montreal, Quebec, Canada. He's brother of actor William Hope.

He appeared in the TV series Psi Factor: Chronicles of the Paranormal (1996-2000) as Peter Axon. His screen credits include The Wager (1998), Paycheck (2003) and a recurring role on Stargate SG-1.

Barclay Hope is married to fellow actress Lindsay Collins and they have 3 children.
