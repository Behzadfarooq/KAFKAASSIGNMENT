Bajiao Amusement Park

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Bajiao Amusement Park (八角游乐园) is a theme park in the vicinity of Beijing in China. Its full name is Beijing Shijingshan Amusement Park. It is also a stop on Line 1 of the Beijing Subway.
