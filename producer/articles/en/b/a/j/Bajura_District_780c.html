Bajura District

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Bajura district, a part of Seti zone, is one of the seventy-five districts of Nepal, a landlocked country of South Asia. The district, with Martadi as its district headquarters, covers an area of 2,188 sq km and has a population (2001) of 108,781.
