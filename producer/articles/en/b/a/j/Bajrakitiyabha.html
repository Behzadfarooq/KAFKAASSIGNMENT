Her Royal Highness Princess Bajrakitiyabha (born 7 December 1978), the first grandchild of King Bhumibol and Queen Sirikit of Thailand, is the eldest daughter of HRH Crown Prince Maha Vajralongkorn and HRH Princess Soamsavali.

[edit] Education

Princess Bajrakitiyabha received a bachelor's degree in Law from Thammasat University. She continued her master's degree and doctoral degree at Cornell University in New York State and received her J.S.D. from there in 2005.

[edit] Career

Princess Bajrakitiyabha is now working at Thai permanent mission to the United Nations, in New York City.
