Originally from Chennai, India, currently working in London. Joined Wikipedia in early 2006.

I graduated from Nanyang Technological University, Singapore in July 2005 with a bachelor's degree in Electrical & Electronic Engineering.

I am a passionate fan of Formula One racing, and a big supporter of Michael Schumacher and Ferrari. Most of my edits to date have been on articles related to F1.

I am a beginner photographer, and my only other edits have been the addition of photos to existing articles.
