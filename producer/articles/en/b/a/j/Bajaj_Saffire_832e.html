Bajaj Saffire

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

You have new messages (last change).

Saffire was a high end gear-less scooter produced in India by Bajaj Auto. It was innovative in terms that it was the first gear-less scooter to use a four stroke engine. It was also the first to come with a choice of 6 colours. It was available in two shades of blue and in red, black, green and most notably gold.

[edit] Specs

  • Engine Type 4-Stroke
  • Engine Volume 93 cm3
  • Top Speed 70 km/h
  • Acceleration 0-60km/h in 16.06s.
  • Transmission Automatic


  • Release Year 2000[1]
  • End of Sale 2003
