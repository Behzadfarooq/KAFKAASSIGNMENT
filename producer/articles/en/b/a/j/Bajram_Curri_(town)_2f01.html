Bajram Curri (town)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Bajram Curri is a town in Northern Albania on the border with Kosovo, in a remote, mostly mountainous region, water from the mountains flow into the waters of the Valbona River, famous for reported having the clearest river water in Albania. The town is named after Bajram Curri, a national hero of the early 20th century.
