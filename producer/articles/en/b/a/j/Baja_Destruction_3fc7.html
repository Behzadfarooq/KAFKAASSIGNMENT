Baja Destruction is a video game for the Playstation 2 and Wii developed by UK Developer Broadsword Interactive and published by Conspiracy Entertainment. Players take their vehicles, heavily outfitted with destructive weapons, and attempt to destroy other cars in the area. It is the companion to the title Baja Mania.
