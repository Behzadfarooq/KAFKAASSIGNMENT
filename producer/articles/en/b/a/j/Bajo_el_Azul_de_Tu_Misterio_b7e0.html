Bajo el Azul de Tu Misterio is a two-disc album recorded by Mexican rock band Jaguares. The LP was released on September 7, 1999 under the label BMG Entertainment Mexico. The first disc is a live concert album (with some songs from Jaguares' first incarnation, Caifanes); the second disc is a studio album.

Like the previous album, El Equilibrio de los Jaguares, lead singer and songwriter Saul Hernández is credited as the only member of the band. Alfonso André (drums), Sabo Romo (bass), Cesar López "Vampiro" (guitar) and Jarris Margalli (keyboards and samplers) participated in the recording of the album and toured for the live performances.
