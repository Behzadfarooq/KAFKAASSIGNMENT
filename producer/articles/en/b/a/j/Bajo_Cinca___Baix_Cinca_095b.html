Bajo Cinca / Baix Cinca

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Bajo Cinca (Catalan: Baix Cinca, Aragonese: Zinca Baxa) is an Aragonese comarca in the south-east extreme of the Huesca province in Spain.

Its capital is Fraga and it borders La Litera/La Llitera, Cinca Medio, Monegros and Bajo Aragón-Caspe/Baix Aragó-Casp in Aragon and Segrià and Ribera d'Ebre in Catalonia.

It belongs to the Catalan-speaking strip in eastern Aragon which is variously known as La Franja.
