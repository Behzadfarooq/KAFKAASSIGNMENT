The Baïse is a 188 km long river in south-western France, left tributary of the Garonne. Its source is in the foothills of the Pyrenees, near Lannemezan. It flows north through the following départements and towns:

  • Hautes-Pyrénées: Lannemezan , Trie-sur-Baïse
  • Gers: Mirande , Castéra-Verduzan , Valence-sur-Baïse , Condom
  • Lot-et-Garonne: Nérac , Lavardac

It flows into the Garonne near Aiguillon.
