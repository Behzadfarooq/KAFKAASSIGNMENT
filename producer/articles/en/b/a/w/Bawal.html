Bawal is a in Rewari district in the state of Haryana, India.

[edit] Geography

Bawal is located at 28.08° N 76.58° E[1]. It has an average elevation of 266 metres (872 feet).

[edit] Demographics

As of 2001 India censusGRIndia, Bawal had a population of 12,016. Males constitute 53% of the population and females 47%. Bawal has an average literacy rate of 66%, higher than the national average of 59.5%; with 61% of the males and 39% of females literate. 15% of the population is under 6 years of age.
