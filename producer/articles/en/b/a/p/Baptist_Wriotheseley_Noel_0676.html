Baptist Wriotheseley Noel

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Honourable Baptist Wriothesley Noel (born in Brussels, July 10, 1799, died January 19, 1873) was an English evangelical clergyman of aristocratic family.

He was minister of St John's Chapel, Bedford Row, London, from 1827 to 1848 and afterwards became a Baptist minister and President of the Baptist Union.

He was one of several people called Baptist Noel and his son was called Wriotheseley Baptist Noel.
