The Baptist Union of Victoria (BUV) is a not-for-profit organisation operating in the state of Victoria, Australia. It governs over the Baptist churches in the state, providing support and guidance as well as resources and occasionally personnel.

The Director of Ministries is Rev Alan Marr.
