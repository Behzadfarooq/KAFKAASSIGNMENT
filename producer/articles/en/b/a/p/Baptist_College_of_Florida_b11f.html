The Baptist College of Florida is located in Graceville, Florida. It is a small Christian college and is sponsored by the Florida Baptist Convention. Originally a college primarily focused on training Baptist preachers, it has begun to expand into more curricular areas.

BCF offers degrees in theology, music, counseling and education. It is nationally accredited by the National Association of Schools of Music.[1]

The Baptist College of Florida is reaching out through international study opportunities, distance learning sites, internet delivery systems and many other innovative methods.
