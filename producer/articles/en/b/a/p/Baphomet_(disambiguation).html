Baphomet (disambiguation)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Baphomet is a demonic image used in occult and historical references.

Baphomet may also be:

  • Baphomet (Dungeons & Dragons), a demon lord in the Dungeons & Dragons role-playing game
  • Baphomet (comics), a demonic entity in the Marvel Comics universe
  • A character in Clive Barker's novella Cabal
  • A minor character in the webcomic 8-Bit Theater.
  • A boss character in the MMORPG Ragnarok Online

As a song title:

  • "Baphomet", the tenth track on the album Slip by the band Quicksand
  • "Baphomet", the ninth track on the album Knights of the Cross and the second track on the single The Battle Of Bannockburn by the band Grave Digger
  • "Horns of Baphomet", the fourth track on the album The Goat of Mendes by the band Akercocke
  • "Horns ov Baphomet", the first track on the album Zos Kia Cultus (Here and Beyond) by the band Behemoth
  • "Baphomet's Throne", the sixth track on the album Ceremony of Opposites by the band Samael
  • "Day of the Baphomets", the seventh track on the album Amputechture by the band The Mars Volta
