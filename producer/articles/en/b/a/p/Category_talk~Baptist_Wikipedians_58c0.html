I won't belabor the point too much, but many Baptists, especially fundamental Baptists, would disagree with including Baptists in the "Protestant" category. The argument is that we trace our history back to the Anabaptists and other groups that always existed 'outside' the Catholic tradition. Therefore we never had to "protest" Catholicism because we were never in it.

Since, however, so many people (including many Baptists) tend to lump us in with the Protestants, I left the category as is. Roachmeister 19:53, 28 December 2005 (UTC)
