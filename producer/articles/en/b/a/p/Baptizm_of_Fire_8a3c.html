Baptizm Of Fire Is a solo album created by Judas Priest guitarist Glenn Tipton created in 1997. It was recorded and mixed at Devonshire and Ocean Studios, U.S.A. and Monnow Valley, Battery, Olympic and The Gazebo Studio, U.K. Written and recorded in years 1994-1996 Management done by Bill Curbishley and Co-ordination done by Jayne Andrews.

[edit] Musicians

Glenn Tipton-vocals and guitars
Robert Trujillo-bass
Billy Sheehan-bass
John Entwistle-bass
Cozy Powell-drums
Don Airey-keyboards
Whitfield Crane-backing vocals
