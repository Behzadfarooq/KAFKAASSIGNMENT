Inside view of Bayside Marketplace in downtown Miami, Florida. Photo: Marc Averette

Bayside Marketplace is an indoor/outdoor marketplace located in downtown Miami, just south of the AmericanAirlines Arena where the Miami Heat plays. It is similar to places such as the New Orleans Riverwalk. It was featured commonly on Miami Vice and an average of 15 million people a year visit the marketplace.
