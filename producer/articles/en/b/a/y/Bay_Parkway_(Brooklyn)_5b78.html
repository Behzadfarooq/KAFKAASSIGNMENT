Bay Parkway (Brooklyn)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

You have new messages (last change).

Bay Parkway is a street that cuts through the New York City borough of Brooklyn. It starts at Ocean Parkway and continues in a southwest direction to Lower New York Bay. It mainly runs through the Bensonhurst section of Brooklyn. Along Bay Parkway are many Chinese and Russian stores and Italian bakeries.

Bay Parkway has three New York City Subway stops:

  • Bay Parkway on the BMT Sea Beach Line (N)
  • Bay Parkway on the BMT Culver Line (F)
  • Bay Parkway on the BMT West End Line (D M)
