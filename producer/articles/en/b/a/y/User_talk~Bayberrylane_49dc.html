[edit] Great Mall/Main

I see what you are saying, however, making this station a seperate article opens up the possibility of adding information that is relavant to the transit station, but not to the mall - including other points of interest (i.e. the north county jail facility), the history of this transit hub, public art, etc.

I agree that transportation facilities that are adjacent to each other should all be part of the same article - see Diridon Station, Tamien station, Downtown Mountain View station. However, I don't think that the mere fact that this transit stop serves the Great Mall necessarily means that they belong in the same article (as an analogy, Great America theme park is a different article than Great America light rail station, etc. --Brianvdb 07:19, 5 April 2006 (UTC)
