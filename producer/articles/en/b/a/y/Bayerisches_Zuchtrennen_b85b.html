Bayerisches Zuchtrennen

The Bayerisches Zuchtrennen is a Group 1 flat horse race in Germany for three-year-old and above thoroughbreds run over a distance of 2,000 metres (approximately 1 mile 2 furlongs) at Munich Racecourse, Munich in late July / early August.

It has been sponsored by Dallmayr since 1996, and has the alternative title Grosser Dallmayr-Preis. The race was first run as a Group 1 in 1990.
