Bayside High School is a secondary school located in Virginia Beach, Virginia.

The school's most unique feature is the Health Sciences Academy; one of several magnet programs in Virginia Beach. Students throughout the city interested in a health sciences education can apply. The school has a written agreement with Eastern Virginia Medical School regarding preferential admissions for Academy graduates.[1]

Bayside is in the top 3 in Standards of Learning scores within the school district.[2] Bayside High has also achieved the highest End of Course SOL Algebra II and World History I pass rates among all schools in the district.[1]

[edit] Student activities

Extracurricular activities include: Band, Black Culture, Chess, Chorus, CHROME, Debate, Drama, Forensics, Filipino-American Culture Association, Gay/Straight Alliance, Gospel Choir, National Honor Society, National Junior Reserve Officers' Training Corps, Newspaper, Orchestra, Peer Mentor/Mediation, SCA, Scholastic Bowl, Student Athletic Trainers, as well as Freshman/Sophomore/Junior/Senior clubs and a full range of athletic activities.
