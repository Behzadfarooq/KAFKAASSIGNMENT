Bayugan, Agusan del Sur

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Bayugan is a 1st class municipality in the province of Agusan del Sur, Philippines. According to the 2000 census, it has a population of 93,623 people in 17,222 households.

Bayugan is the transportation highway nexus for the eastern part of Mindanao Island.
