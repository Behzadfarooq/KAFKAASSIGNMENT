Bayan Shireh Formation

The Bayan Shireh Formation is a geological formation in Burkhant, Mongolia, that stems from the Campanian stage of the late Cretaceous period, 83.5-70 million years ago. The fauna of this formation is similar to the nearby Iren Dabasu Formation.

Fossils found at Bayan Shireh include:

[edit] Dinosaurs

  • Achillobator, a giant dromaeosaur
  • Alectrosaurus, a tyrannosaur
  • Albertosaurus, a tyrannosaur
  • Amtosaurus, an ankylosaur
  • Enigmosaurus, a therizinosaur
  • Erlikosaurus, a therizinosaur
  • Garudimimus, an ornithomimosaur
  • Graciliceratops, a primitive ceratopsian
  • Maleevus, an ankylosaur
  • Microceratops, a primitive ceratopsian
  • Segnosaurus, a therizinosaur
  • Talarurus, an ankylosaur
  • Therizinosaurus, a giant therizinosaur
  • Tsagantegia, an ankylosaur
  • Several indeterminate fossils of ornithomimids, hadrosaurs, and theropods.
