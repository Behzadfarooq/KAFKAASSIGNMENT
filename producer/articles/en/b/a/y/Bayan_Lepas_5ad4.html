Bayan Lepas is a town in Penang, Malaysia. It is the location of Bayan Lepas International Airport, Penang's international airport. Bayan Lepas is also the industrial hub of Penang, being home to the Free Industrial Zone, where factories of many multinational companies are located. Penang's longest and biggest shopping mall, Queensbay Mall, the Bukit Jambul Complex and Penang's only Giant Hypermarket are located there. Bayan Lepas also connects the town through Jelutong Expressway. The Snake Temple was located there. It has a population of about 100,000 people.
