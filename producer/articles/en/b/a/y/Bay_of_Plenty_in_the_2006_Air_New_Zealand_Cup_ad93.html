Bay of Plenty are in Pool A for the first round of the 2006 Air New Zealand Cup and start their campaign against Wellington on the 30 July.

[edit] Bay Of Plenty Squad

The 2006 Air New Zealand Cup Bay of Plenty Squad is Ben Castle (c), Simms Davidson, Aleki Lutui, Bernie Upton, Mark Sorenson, Nili Latu, Tanerau Latimer, Colin Bourke, Warren Smith, James Afoa, Matt Wallis, Solomon King, Aidan Kuka, Aaron Rameka, Jamie Nutbrown, Garrick Cowley, Murray Williams, Mike Delany, Rena Schuster, Cory Aporo, Jeff Ierome, Hayden Reid, Anthony Tahana, Teu Nafe, Lance McDonald, Charles Baxter, Wayne Hughson

[edit] Pre-Season Games

  • 14 July vs Counties Manukau Manukau
  • 21 July vs Hawke's Bay Mount Maunganui
  • 29 July vs Waikato Hamilton

[edit] Round One

  • 4 August vs Wellington Wellington
  • 12 August vs North Harbour Mount Maunganui
  • 20 August vs Auckland Auckland
  • 26 August vs Manawatu Rotorua
  • 2 September vs Tasman Blenheim
  • 9 September vs Taranaki Mount Maunganui
