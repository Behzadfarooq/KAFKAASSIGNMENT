Bayou Country is the second album by American band Creedence Clearwater Revival, released in 1969 (see 1969 in music).

[edit] Track listing

[edit] Side one

  1. "Born on the Bayou" (J. Fogerty) – 5:16
  2. "Bootleg" (J. Fogerty) – 3:02
  3. "Graveyard Train" (J. Fogerty) – 8:37

[edit] Side two

  1. "Good Golly Miss Molly" (Robert "Bumps" Blackwell, John Marascalco) – 2:44
  2. "Penthouse Pauper" (J. Fogerty) – 3:39
  3. "Proud Mary" (J. Fogerty) – 3:08
  4. "Keep On Chooglin'" (J. Fogerty) – 7:41
