Bayezid Mosque

Bayezid II Mosque was commissioned by Bayezid II, the sultan of the Ottoman Empire from 1481 to 1512, and it is located in the Bayezid Square. Bayezid Mosque construction started in 1501 and was completed 1506 by the architect Yakub Sah bin Sultan Sah in Istanbul, Turkey.
