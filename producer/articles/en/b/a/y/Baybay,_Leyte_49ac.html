Baybay, Leyte

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

You have new messages (last change).

Baybay is a 1st class municipality in the province of Leyte, Philippines. According to the 2000 census, it has a population of 95,630 people in 19,517 households.

[edit] Barangays
