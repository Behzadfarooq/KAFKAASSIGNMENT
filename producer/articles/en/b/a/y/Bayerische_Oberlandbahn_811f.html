The Bayerische Oberlandbahn (BOB) is a private railway in Germany run by Connex. It consists of three diesel train lines operating south from Munich to the destinations Lenggries, Tegernsee and Bayrischzell which are in the Bavarian Prealps.

BOB was founded in 1998.
