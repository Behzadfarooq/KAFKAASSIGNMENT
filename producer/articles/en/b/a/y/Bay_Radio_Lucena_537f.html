Bay Radio 92.7 (DWKL - FM 92.7 MHz Lucena City) is a music FM station owned and operated by Baycomms Broadcasting Corp. in the Philippines. The studio is located at the corner of Padre Gomez and Merchan Streets, Lucena City.
