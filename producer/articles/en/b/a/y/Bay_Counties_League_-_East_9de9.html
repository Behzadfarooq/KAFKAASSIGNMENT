The Bay Counties League - East is a school athletic conference located in the East Bay Area. The league is a member of the North Coast Section, one of ten sections that comprise the California Interscholastic Federation. There are ten member schools. The athletic conference is composed of:

  • The Athenian School in Danville
  • Head-Royce School in Oakland
  • The College Preparatory School in Oakland
  • Bentley School in Lafayette
  • Chinese Christian Schools in San Leandro
  • Emery High School in Emeryville
  • University Preparatory Charter Academy in Oakland
  • Oakland Military Institute
  • California School for the Deaf in Fremont
  • Contra Costa Christian in Walnut Creek
