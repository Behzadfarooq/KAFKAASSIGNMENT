Bay Park Square also called Bay Park, Bay Park Mall or Bay Park Square Mall, is a shopping mall owned by Simon Property Group, in the Green Bay, Wisconsin suburb of Ashwaubenon, in the United States. The mall opened in 1980.

The mall is anchored by ShopKo, Kohl's and Younkers (on the site of a former Montgomery Ward department store). Elder-Beerman, which opened in 1995, closed in February of 2007 and is vacant.

It also has a Super Bowl-themed food court with 7 eateries called "The Supper Bowl". A big children's play area is located in the ShopKo wing. There are 103 specialty stores including, but not limited to Old Navy, The Gap, Hot Topic, Claire's, American Eagle Outfitters, Pac Sun, etc. Bay Park Square is located blocks away from Lambeau Field. The Elder-Beerman building used to house Bay Park Cinema until the 1990s when it moved to a new location on the corner of the parking lot behind ShopKo.

[edit] Surrounding area

Bay Park Square is situated on Oneida Street across from an area of this suburban Green Bay landscape that has many fast food restaurants like Taco Bell, McDonald's and Burger King which are all located on the same street.

A nearby strip mall known as "The Village at Bay Park" is anchored by JCPenney (which was an anchor tenant at the dead mall in Downtown Green Bay formerly known as Port Plaza) and DSW is located across from ShopKo on the Willard Drive side of Bay Park Square.
