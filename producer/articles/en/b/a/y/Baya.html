Baya

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Baya (also called Baya-Mandjia) are the largest ethnic group in the Central African Republic. They comprise 34% of the population in that country.

Baya also refers to the Islamic contract of government with a Caliph see Bayah

Baya is also the last name of some people, who follow Jainism in India.

Baya is also the name of a village in Afghanistan.

Baya is also a species of bird found in India.
