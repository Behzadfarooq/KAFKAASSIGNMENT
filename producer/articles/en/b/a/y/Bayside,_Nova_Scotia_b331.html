Coordinates: 44°32′00″N, 63°48′01″W Bayside is a small coastal community of the Halifax Regional Municipality Nova Scotia, Canada, about 19 km from Halifax. It is situated on the shore of Blind Bay (Atlantic Ocean) on the Prospect Road (Highway 333) on the Chebucto Peninsula .
