The Bayfront Convention Center is a convention center complex located in Erie, Pa. The center boasts three sides of walls with views of of the bay and will have a 28,000 square foot great hall and a 13,000 square foot ballroom.[1]

The convention center will also feature stadium seating for 4,500. The attached hotel will be an upscale Four Points by Sheraton.

The Bayfront Convention Center is scheduled to open in summer 2007 with overall construction around 84 percent at March 15, 2007. The Hotel is several months behind and will open in March 2008.[2]
