Baylor

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Baylor is a surname and the name of several places and institutions. They include:

Institutions:

  • Baylor University in Waco, Texas
  • Baylor College of Medicine, Houston, Texas
  • Baylor College of Dentistry, Dallas, Texas
  • University of Mary Hardin-Baylor, Belton, Texas
  • Baylor School, secondary school, Tennessee

People:

  • Don Baylor, Major League Baseball player, coach and manager
  • Elgin Baylor, NBA player
  • George Baylor, American soldier during the American Revolutionary War
  • Gracia Baylor, Australian politician
  • John Baylor, officer of the Confederate States of America during the American Civil War
  • Judge Robert Emmett Bledsoe Baylor, founder of Baylor University

Places:

  • Baylor County, Texas, named for Henry Weidner Baylor

Events:
