The Bay Area Baracus Rugby Football Club is a men's rugby union team based in and around San Francisco, California. The club competes in the Northern California Rugby Football Union division II.

[edit] History

BA Baracus RFC was founded in 2001 in Oakland, California by alumni of Amherst College's rugby team. The name and motto (miseror stultum) of the club come from their adopted mascot, the popular A-Team character, BA Baracus. The club began in the NCRFU division III and after a strong showing now compete in NCRFU division II.
