[edit] Genre

I changed the genre to "alternative rock" because it's a nice broad term and I hate all these millions of names for what is pretty much alternative rock anyway! Keep it simple! And keep it at "alternative rock" please Jubella 22:12, 1 May 2006 (UTC)


I disagree with you completely. If you goto their purevolume page or myspace page, they define themselves in a few genres. To just say "Alternative rock" now is way to general to describe a band. It's nearly impossible to fall under one genre, seeing as bands take roots from different places. Ex. The Smoking Popes and The Beach Boys, both are an influence to Bayside but are from different genres. Having two different types of influences causes the musician to make their own sound. Thus having more then one genre.Jarebear 19:18, 5 January 2007 (UTC)
