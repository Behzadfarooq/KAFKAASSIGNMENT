Coordinates: 44°39′0″N, 63°40′27″W Bayer's Lake Business Park is a Business Park in Halifax Nova Scotia on a plateau across Nova Scotia Highway 102 from Clayton Park . The had its beginings in the early 1980's and today occupies over 236 hectares has various retail and major warehousing, trucking, distribution, and manufacturing operations . The park is admistrated by by the Halifax Regional Municipality

[edit] Transportation

The business Park is at the junction of two major highways Nova Scotia Highway 102 and Nova Scotia Highway 103 and also Trunk 3. The park is approximately 10 kilometres from Downtown Halifax and Canadian National Railway has a rail line to the park.
