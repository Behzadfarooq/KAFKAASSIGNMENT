Bayville, New Jersey

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Bayville is an unincorporated area within Berkeley Township in Ocean County, New Jersey, United States. The area is served as United States Postal Service ZIP Code 08721.

As of the United States 2000 Census, the population for ZIP Code Tabulation Area 08721 was 17,148.
