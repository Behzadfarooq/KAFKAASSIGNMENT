Baygon is a pesticide produced by S. C. Johnson & Son.It is a Carbamate class of insecticide used for extermination and control of many household pests and is very effective for crickets, roaches, ants, carpenter ants, spiders, silverfish etc.In 1975 Baygon introduced Australiaâ€™s first surface spray for killing cockroaches and other crawling insects.

Baygon was created by the famous german company Bayer in 1975 along with the air freshener Bay Fresh, Before being sold to S. C. Johnson & Son.

[edit] Composition

Random Assortment of Baygon Products

Baygon is mainly composed of Carbamates and Propoxur. Like all other insecticide, Baygon too is highly toxic to human beings if the intake is in high levels, and would probably lead to fatal circumstances.

[edit] Products

Baygon Insecticide Concentrates, Bait and Aerosol Sprays were introduced as it's primary products. These Products include:

  • Baygon Flying Insect Spray
  • Baygon Crawling Insect Spray
  • Baygon Crawling Insect Spray/Disinfectant
  • Baygon Crawling Insect Spray Foam
  • Baygon Ant Bait Station
  • Baygon Cockroach Bait Station

  • Baygon Moth Paper
