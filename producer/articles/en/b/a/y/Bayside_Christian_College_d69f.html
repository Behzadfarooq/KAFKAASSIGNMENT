Bayside Christian College
Langwarrin South, Victoria

Bayside Christian College is an independent, small, Christian school located in Langwarrin of Melbourne, Victoria, Australia. The school focuses on teaching the students the word of God in a safe, friendly environment. Bayside is a school which teaches kids from Prep-12, founded in the 1980's. The school's current principal is Ms. Hook, has around 400 students enrolled.
