Bayless Conley is an American pastor and television personality known mostly for his program Answers With Bayless Conley which airs currently on the Church Channel. Answers runs every week on the Church Channel in over 100 countries.

The program can be found on major television channels: CNBC in Europe, North Africa and the Middle East, on Zee Prime in most of Asia, and on New Africa Networks which covers much of the African continent. Other local and regional channels also carry Answers in a multitude of other places.[[1]]


The Answers is said to bring "practical teaching of the Bible to its viewers in a non-religious way."[[2]] As a recovering druggie and alcoholic, Conley found God through a 12 year old boy. He leads Cottonwood Christian Center, which is located in Los Alamitos, CA. Currently, the church is on an effort to expand. They are constructing a facility not to far from their original location. The facility will be three-times larger.
