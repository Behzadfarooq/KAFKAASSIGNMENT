Bay Parkway (Jones Beach)

You have new messages (last change).

Bay Parkway is a 3.2 mile (5.1 km) semi-limited-access highway entirely within Jones Beach State Park. It is unsigned New York State Reference Route 909E.

Running entirely along Jones Inlet, it connects the west end of Jones Beach to the Marine theater east of Wantagh State Parkway. The only other interchange is with the Meadowbrook State Parkway and Ocean Parkway. Aside from that, it only provides access to the boat basin, fishing piers, and as a back entrance to many of the parking lots along the beach.
