Lambeth North tube station at the south end of Baylis Road.

Baylis Road is a road in Lambeth, SE1 running between Westminster Bridge Road to the south and Waterloo Road to the north.

Lambeth North tube station is located at the southern end on the junction with Westminster Bridge Road.

To the south the road continues as Kennington Road (the A23). To the north east it continues as The Cut.
