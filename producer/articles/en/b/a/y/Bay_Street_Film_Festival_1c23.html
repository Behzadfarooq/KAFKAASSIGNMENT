The Bay Street Film Festival is an independent film festival in Thunder Bay, Ontario, Canada that features local, national, and international films with the theme "Films for the People." The festival is held on 314 Bay Street in the historic Finnish Labour Temple in the heart of the Finnish quarter.

During the highly successful inaugural festival in 2005, 80 films were screened to over 3000 people. The festival also hosted 25 out-of-town film makers, musicians, and speakers, some from as far away as Israel and Finland.
