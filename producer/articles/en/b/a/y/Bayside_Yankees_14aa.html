The Bayside Yankees are a travel baseball team located in the New York area.

The team has built one of the best reputations in the history of travel baseball[citation needed]. Founder, and head coach of the Senior Americans has lead his team to many National World Championships. He and his coaches only accept players that are the best and show a love for the game that is stronger than any other. Bayside coaches' goals are to win and gain as much possible exposure to college and the MLB as possible. Bayside has players on their roster from all over the country, due to their great notoriety. Throughout colleges and the MLB, Marc Cuseta is known and is frequently called upon to send his highly talented players to their colleges, organization, etc. In the past players have earned scholarships to schools such as the University of Miami, Clemson University, Vanderbilt University, St. John's University, Duke University, Wake Forest University, University of North Carolina, University of Arizona, Georgia Tech, Notre Dame University, Oklahoma State, Florida State, Virginia Tech, Tulane University and top junior colleges.

[edit] Teams

The organization has different teams according to age and ability

  • Sophomores- Age 13-14
    • Schedule- 40 games- Coached by Diego Garcia
    • Local teams around the New York area and a trip to Ohio to play in the NABF World Series.
  • Junior Nationals-Age 15-16
    • Schedule-55 games-Hopefully coached By Charlie Delia
    • North East travel with trips to Northville, Michigan for the NABF World Series. A trip to the Astros Training Complex in Florida for the Osceola County Tournament.
  • Junior Americans-Age 15-16
    • Coached by the prestigious Joseph Kessler. He is also the Junior coordinator and VP of the Bayside Yankees. He has been coaching with this organization since 1994 and has seen nothing but success. He has a lifetime record of 575 wins 180 losses and looks to exceed the 600 win mark in the upcoming season.
    • Schedule-75 Games-
    • Travel consists of a trip to the Astros Training Complex in Florida for the Osceola County Tournament. A trip to College Station, Texas for the Premier World Series and a trip to East Cobb, Georgia for the Super Series World Series.
  • 'Senior Nationals-Age 17-18
    • Schedule-75 games-Coached by Vinny Garelick
    • North East Travel with trips to Astros Training Complex for the Osceola County Tournament. A trip to Charleston, South Carolina for the CABA Senior World Series. Another trip to Healthquest Park, New Jersey for the Don Mattingly World Series.
  • Senior Americans- Age 17-18
    • Schedule-75 Games-Coached by founder Marc Cuseta
    • Travel in all parts of the United States. Trips include Atlanta, Georgia for the East Cobb Classic. Memphis, TN for Game Day Tournament. Mobile, Alabama for the Premier BB National WW Series. A trip to Jackson, Mississippi for the NABF Senior World Series.

The teams also participate in 'Fall Ball' that takes place in August through October to help give the players more exposure to college and professional scouts. In the past trips have included Rhode Island, Arizona, North Carolina, Maryland, Virginia, Pennsylvania and South Carolina.
