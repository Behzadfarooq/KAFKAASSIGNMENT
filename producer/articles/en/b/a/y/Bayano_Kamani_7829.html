Bayano Kamani (born 17 April 1980) is a Panamanian hurdler.

Year Tournament Venue Result Extra
1999 Universiade Palma de Mallorca, Spain 2nd
2003 South American Championships Barquisimeto, Venezuela 1st
2004 Olympic Games Athens, Greece 5th
World Athletics Final Monte Carlo, Monaco 5th
2005 World Championships Helsinki, Finland 7th
World Athletics Final Monte Carlo, Monaco 6th
