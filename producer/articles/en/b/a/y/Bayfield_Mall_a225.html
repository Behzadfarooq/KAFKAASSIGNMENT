Bayfield Mall is pened in 1972 and renovated and expanded in the 90's, Bayfield is located on Bayfield Street in Barrie, Ontario, Canada. Traffic studies show the the Bayfield mall is in one of the busiest traffic corridors in the trade area.[citation needed]

[edit] Anchors

The anchors of the mall are Winners, Price Chopper, Canadian Tire, Bowlerama
