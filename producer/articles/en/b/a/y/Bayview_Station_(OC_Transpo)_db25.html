Bayview Station is a Transitway and O-Train station in Ottawa, Ontario. The bus stop is at the eastern end of Scott Street when it becomes Wellington Street. The O-Train station, the northern terminus of the current line, is located on a stub-end track branching off from the railway line, immediately under the Wellington Street and Transitway overpasses. Paved asphalt footpaths connect the two stops on opposite sides of the Transitway and the train platform.

By 2009 before its cancellation, the rail would have been extended from north and east of the station and directed towards downtown.

From the higher points of the station, one can just see the Parliament buildings across the LeBreton Flats.
