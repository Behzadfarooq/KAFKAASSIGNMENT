Bayside Acoustic EP is a self-titled EP by the Long Island Emo/pop punk band Bayside. It is comprised mostly of an intimate acoustic show recorded shortly after, and in memory of, the death of drummer John "Beatz" Holohan in a tour bus accident on October 31, 2005.

  2. "Blame It On Bad Luck" – 3:29
  3. "They Looked Like Strong Hands" – 3:28
