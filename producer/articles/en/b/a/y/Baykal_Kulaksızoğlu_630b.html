Baykal Kulaksızoğlu (born 12 May 1983) is a Turkish-Swiss football player.

In June 2006 he signed contract a to 2008 with 2nd Bundesliga club 1. FC Cologne. Formerly he played in Switzerland for Grasshopper-Club Zürich, FC Thun, and FC Basel 1893. He was played in Switzerland's U21-National squad, and featured in 2004 UEFA European Under-21 Football Championship.
