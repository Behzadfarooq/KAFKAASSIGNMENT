Bayard (ship)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Bayard was a 1,028 ton, sailing ship built for the Hall Line in 1864. In 1868 she was transferred to Sun Shipping Company and in 1881 sold to Foley and Company. [1]

On 20 August 1883 she arrived in Suva, Fiji carrying 494 Indian indentured labourers from Calcutta. She had previously carried indentured labourers to the West Indies. [2]
