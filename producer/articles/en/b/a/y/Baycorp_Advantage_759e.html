Baycorp Advantage (ASX: VEA) is the largest credit bureau in Australia and New Zealand. It provides credit reporting, credit scoring, and marketing analytics services.

The Company is soon to launch its new name of Veda Advantage. The name change was prompted by the sale of the Baycorp Collection Services which will retain the Baycorp name. The name change has already been applied to its listing on the Australian Stock Exchange.
