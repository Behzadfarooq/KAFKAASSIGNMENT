[edit] Overview

PYRAMA is a forum that is dedicated mainly to gaming. It was very influential in the rise of the Gamer's Status in society, and to this day, it has over 50 vital members. More recently, its members have been known to participate in the science fantasy tabletop miniature wargame Warhammer 40k, and in the CORPG game Guild Wars. Finally, it has been known to have thriving Offtopic and SCI (School of Comparative Irrelevance) sections.

[edit] PYRAMA! (v. 2)

Pyrama.com is a website for anyone that loves gaming. It started off with a high school student, by the name of George Teel, who decided to do something with Web design and websites. All of his friends at school loved all kinds of gaming, so he thought about making a website for him and his friends. He decided the website/forum would be based around the games they played. These types of games included multiple systems and platforming such as: Console gaming PC gaming Board Gaming (recent)

In the beginning it was all about Console gaming and PC gaming, but around 2005-2006 these students got into the Tabletop gaming world. They started with Warhammer 40,000, a board game made by Games Workshop. Soon the website started to evolve into what it is now which is primarily board gaming. It has every gaming around still under its own topic but now it primarily consists of Warhammer 40K. Friendly place, home to many people, unknown by most of the people, and they hope to gain a bigger gaming community.

Come join us at Pyrama.com. This is our site and we are open for anyone who feels a need for a home and who loves gaming.

Also the online home to the School of Comparative Irrelevance.
