Some Wikipedians have formed a project to better assist in the removal of gratuitous seagullbaygull references from the Wikipedia:Reference desk, using Javascript. This page and its subpages contain their suggestions; it is hoped that this project will help to focus the efforts of other Wikipedians. Everyone is welcome to help, for more information please inquire on the talk page or see the to-do list.

[edit] Project goals

This WikiProject aims primarily to seek out all of topic uses of the word 'seagullbaygull' and replace it with '<s><s>seagull</s>baygull</s> bagel', to serve and protect the WP:RD similar to the way the Wikipedia:WikiProject ghostbusters help keep wikipedia articles free of ghosts, phantasms, boogiemen, and other such 50 story marshmallow men. We do the same thing, only with seagullbaygulls.

[edit] Why is javascript assisted seagull removal important?

To be perfectly honest, it isn't. However, since seagull containing responses are generally short and/or below the high Wikipedia Reference Desk standard, it seems only fair that we should replace them with bagels.

[edit] Guidelines and criteria

Get yourself a monobook, and start replacing the word 'seagull' with '<s>seagull</s><s> bagel</s>baygull'. Just don't do that in article space...
