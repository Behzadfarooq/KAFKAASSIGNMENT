Bag Lady is a song from singer Erykah Badu's 2000 album Mama's Gun. She sampled the song "Xplosive" off of Dr. Dre's 1999 album 2001. The song is about a woman trying to begin anew in a relationship but she has too much emotional "baggage", and can't get close to people. The moral of the sing is to "pack light", and have hope for the future.

The song is featured on DJ-KiCKS: Vikter Duplaix.
