Bags & Trane is an album by American jazz vibraphonist Milt Jackson and saxophonist John Coltrane. Taking its title from Jackson and Coltrane's nicknames, it is the only collaborative record by the pair. It was released by Atlantic Records on January 15, 1959 (see 1959 in music).[1]

  4. "Three Little Words" (Kalmar, Ruby) – 7:27
