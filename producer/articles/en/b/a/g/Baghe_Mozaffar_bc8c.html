[edit] Description

  • Mehran Modiri,one of the most popular directors of satire and comedy TV series in Iran today, started to make this series in the autumn of 2006.
  • At first, Javad Razavian was planned to play a role in this series but instead of him, Reza Shafiei Jam appeared in Baghe Mozaffar.
  • The theme is about a family of masters living in a house named "Baghe Mozaffar".
  • Mehran Modiri has two roles in this series: He is Mozaffar Khan and also he hosts the adventures.

[edit] Plot

Mohammad Reza Hedayati & Behnoosh Bakhtiary

Mehran Modiri & Nasrollah Radesh


Siamak Ansari Stars as a Kamran, A young strange man who works in a transportation Company who is ran by Mr. Bordbaar, a fat man who easily laughs by anything. Mr Bordbar along with his son are careless people and Kamran is the only person that is helping the company. Kamran is a shy, Hard-working and Strange person to Bordbaar Family. Kamran is also in love with Nazi who is Bordbaar`s Daughter, who is in love with her old Fiance. Kamran is shy to say anything. Kamran`s dad "Mozzafar Khan Zargande" is a Khan who lives his life like old Tehran Khans.

Kamran also has a sister,Forough Khanom, who is noisy and does what she is likes to do. She is widow, and her past husbands have been killed by strange accidents that have to do something with how mean she is. They Also have a Servant, named "Heyfe Naan" Which Means Worthless. He craves for punishment and Foul language from his servants. They Also have a sloppy, worthless lawyer, who is trying to get the family to sell the garden. According to Jamshid, the Lawyer, Tehran is going to have a Highway that runs through the garden and they should sell it before the price drops drastically. Zargande Family live in a huge garden who is shared by Mozzafar Khan`s Cousin, Mansour Khan. Mansour Khan, suffers from short term memory-loss, hates his cousin, and is old with no family of his own. The story will go on about Kamran in pursue of his love.

[edit] Criticisms against this series

Mehran Modiri is one of the greatest satire directors in Iran . He has made very important projects like Pavarchin and Shabhaye Barareh . But his work in Baghe Mozaffar wasn't so interesting . His program didn't have stability as strong as Shabhaye Barareh.
Many viewers and critics have had much criticism against this series,Baghe Mozaffar.
Their criticism were about these matters:

  • This series is full of advertisements.
  • The characters of Baghe Mozaffar aren't real.All of them are imaginary.This matter has hurt the series.
  • Recommendations about tax-paying , fish-eating and safety problems spoiled Baghe Mozaffar.


[edit] The Characters of the series

  • Mozaffar Khan (acted by:Mehran Modiri)

According to the film , Mozaffar Khan is a formal person .He can't understand the meaning of love and affection.
He speaks in ancient Persian.

  • Mansour Khan (acted by:Mohammad Reza Hedayati)

Mansour Khan is Mozaffar Khan 's cousin . He suffers from short-term memory-lose .This matter makes the film more interesting.
