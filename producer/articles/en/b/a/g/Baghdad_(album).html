Baghdad was a 7-inch EP released by The Offspring in 1991. It is currently out of print, but sold 3000 copies within one week of its release. Baghdad has never been reissued on CD in its entirety, although two of the songs were later released on compilations: "Hey Joe" on Go Ahead Punk...Make My Day (Nitro Records, 1996) and "Baghdad" on Rock Against Bush, Vol. 1 (Fat Wreck Chords, 2004).

[edit] Track listing

All songs by The Offspring unless otherwise noted.

  1. "Get it Right" (an early version of the song on Ignition)
  2. "Hey Joe" (Jimi Hendrix)
  3. "Baghdad" ("Tehran" from The Offspring with slightly different lyrics)
  4. "The Blurb" (instrumental)
