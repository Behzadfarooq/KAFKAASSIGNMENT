Bagmati Zone

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Bagmati Zone is a zone of Nepal which is named after the river Bagmati. It is in Central Development Region of Nepal. The zone contains the Kathmandu Valley with its conurbation of 1,5 million inhabitants.

Districts of Bagmati

Bagmati is divided into 8 districts, listed below with headquarters in parentheses:

  • Bhaktapur District (Bhaktapur)
  • Dhading District (Dhading)
  • Lalitpur District (Patan)
  • Kathmandu District (Kathmandu)
  • Kavrepalanchok District (Dhulikhel)
  • Nuwakot District (Bidur)
  • Rasuwa District (Dhunche)
  • Sindhulpalchok District (Chautara)
