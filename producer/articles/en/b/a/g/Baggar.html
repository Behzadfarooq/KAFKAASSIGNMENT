Baggar is a town in the Jhunjhunu District of Rajasthan in India. It lies in the Shekhawati region. It is known for the Piramal Haveli with fresco paintings. This haveli is also used as a hotel.

[edit] Demographics

As of 2001 India censusGRIndia, Baggar had a population of 14,648. Males constitute 56% of the population and females 44%. Baggar has an average literacy rate of 70%, higher than the national average of 59.5%; with 64% of the males and 36% of females literate. 14% of the population is under 6 years of age.
