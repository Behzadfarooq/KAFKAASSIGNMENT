Bagno a Ripoli is a comune (municipality) in the Province of Florence in the Italian region Tuscany, located about 7 km southeast of Florence. As of 31 December 2004, it had a population of 25,528 and an area of 74.1 km².[1]

Bagno a Ripoli borders the following municipalities: Fiesole, Florence, Greve in Chianti, Impruneta, Pontassieve, Rignano sull'Arno.
