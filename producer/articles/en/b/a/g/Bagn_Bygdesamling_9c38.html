Bagn Bygdesamling

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Bagn Bydesamling is the work of Olaus Islandsmoen and consists of 12 logbuildings and a building for permanent displays. It is located 8 km south of Bagn in Sør-Aurdal.

Svartsetera, 1796
Prestgardsboligen - Stabelbygningen 1808
Telthuset, - The tent house 1813 - 1850
Stabbur Hølerseterbrøtin - Storage house Hølerseterbrøtin

The museum has also displays at the small farm Sandviken and Bagnsbergatn. The last one is a national World War II memorial location. The permanent display consists of artifacts from the stone and Iron Age, textils and clothing, furniture and kitchen items, craftmanship, hunting and fishing, farming and (?), music, school and artifacts from the war.

All the items in the museum are original and have been collected from various farms and other locations in the area. Today the museum is run as a subsidiary of Valdres Folkemuseum.
