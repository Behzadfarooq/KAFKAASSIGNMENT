[edit] Bagpiping Scotsman

Greetings. I'm Clifford Smith, and I'm a snare drummer for the Weston McEwen Pipe Band. I am, in fact, also learning the Great Highland Bagpipe, but the name was just a spur-of-the-moment thing. I'm primarily an editor of pages concerning music and the likes. I'm a member of Wikiproject Music and Wikiproject Pipe Bands. Leave a message on my talk page and I'll get back to ye.
