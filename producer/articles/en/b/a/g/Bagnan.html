Bagnan is a census town in Howrah district in the state of West Bengal, India.

[edit] Geography

Bagnan is located at 22.47° N 87.97° E[1]. It has an average elevation of 6 metres (19 feet).

[edit] Demographics

As of 2001 India censusGRIndia, Bagnan had a population of 8779. Males constitute 51% of the population and females 49%. Bagnan has an average literacy rate of 68%, higher than the national average of 59.5%; with 55% of the males and 45% of females literate. 12% of the population is under 6 years of age.
