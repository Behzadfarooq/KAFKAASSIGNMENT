Bagsy Me is the fourth album by Swedish band The Wannadies, released in 1997.

It includes the hit singles "Someone Somewhere", "Shorty", "Friends", "Hit" and "You And Me Song", the last being originally available on previous album Be A Girl. The song was not re-recorded and is present to capitalise on the bands growing fame following the tracks appearance on the soundtrack to Baz Luhrmann's 1996 film Romeo + Juliet.
