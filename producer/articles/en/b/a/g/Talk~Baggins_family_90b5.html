Dropped info from Balbo Baggins:

In earlier versions of The Lord of the Rings, Balbo is named Inigo and his wife is Pervinca Boffin and not Berylla. Only in the published book does he become Balbo. Also in the earlier versions he has three sons: Mungo, Longo (later Ponto), and Tango (later Largo) and he has no daughters. Lily was added in the second draft first as a son named Togo (Togo is later used for Lily's husband, Togo Goodbody), but changed sex into a daughter eventually; and Pansy appeared in the third draft.
