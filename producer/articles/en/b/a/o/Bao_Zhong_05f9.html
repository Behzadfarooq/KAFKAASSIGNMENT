Bao Zhong (d. 189) was an officer of the Eastern Han. He was involved in many political activities. He participated in the coalition against Dong Zhuo, along with his brother Bao Xin. He led a cavalry unit against Dong Zhuo's forces with a strength of 5,000. He was one of four officers slain by Hua Xiong at the Battle of Sishui Pass.
