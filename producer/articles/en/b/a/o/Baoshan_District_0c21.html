Baoshan District

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Baoshan (Chinese: 寶山區, pinyin:Bao Shān Qū) district of Shanghai has a land area of 424.58 km² and a population of 740,900 as of 2001.

Downtown Baoshan is the location of Baosteel's HQ. Baoshan District also is where Shanghai University has its main campus.

Baoshan District is starting to get served by Shanghai's Metro Network, although the network does not stretch north enough to serve central Baoshan.
