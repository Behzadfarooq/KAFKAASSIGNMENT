Budhana is a town and a nagar panchayat in Muzaffarnagar district in the state of Uttar Pradesh, India.

[edit] Geography

Budhana is located at 29.28° N 77.47° E[1]. It has an average elevation of 231 metres (757 feet).

[edit] Demographics

As of 2001 India censusGRIndia, Budhana had a population of 32,949. Males constitute 53% of the population and females 47%. Budhana has an average literacy rate of 49%, lower than the national average of 59.5%; with male literacy of 57% and female literacy of 40%. 18% of the population is under 6 years of age.
