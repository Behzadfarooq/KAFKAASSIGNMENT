[edit] a brief biography

I was born in the city of Hong Kong (then a British Colony) and relocated to the United States in the mid 90s. I finished my last stage of my secondary education at the University School of Nova Southeastern University. During that time, I lived in the developing community of Weston, Florida. After graduating high school, I joined Nova Southeastern University for one year as an undergraduate student, then transferred to Boston University, where I graduated with a B.A. in International Relations in 2001 and a M.A. in International Relations and International Communications in 2004.

Since 2005, I have returned to Nova Southeastern University as a dotoral student, studying for my Ph.D. in Conflict Analysis and Resolution, with particular concentration on International Conflict Resolution. I currently resides in the sunny but hurricane-prone city of Miami, Florida.
