Bud and Lou

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Bud and Lou are the pet hyenas of The Joker on the cartoon series Krypto the Superdog. They are primarily the foes of Ace the Bat-Hound, the pet of Batman, and were first introduced in the episode "The Dark Hound Strikes!" They attempt to commit crimes for their master, requiring Ace and Krypto (and Streaky the Supercat, on at least one episode) to stop them. Their names are references to the comedy duo Abbott and Costello.

[edit] Characteristics

Both hyenas have red fur with black spots and black rings around their eyes. This may be a reference to Harley Quinn, the girlfriend of The Joker. Bud wears a purple collar, has a black right front paw, and speaks in a higher voice. Lou wears a green collar, has a black left front paw and right hind paw, and speaks in a more gravelly voice. Both hyenas have a distinctive odor. This was first mentioned by Ace in the episode "The Cat and the Bat". Later on, in the episode "Funny Business", Ace told Krypto he could smell their evil.

Like their master, Bud and Lou love to laugh maniacally and play jokes on their foes. Like the other animal characters on Krypto the Superdog, they also seem to be quite intelligent (a reference to the intelligence of actual hyenas). For instance, in "Bat Hound and the Robin", while stealing pillowcases for The Joker to carry loot in, they deducted that the pillowcases made of one material would be better because pillowcases made from the other fabric would shrink in the wash, meaning less loot could be put in them. Also, The Joker has given the hyenas items to use in combatting Bat Hound. At one point in "The Dark Hound Strikes!", Bud pretended to give himself up. When Ace seized Bud's collar in his fangs, he received an electric shock. Bud then announced he had a joy-buzzer collar and laughed. In "Funny Business", the hyenas had a bubble shooter which shot special "giggle bubbles". These bubbles caused Ace to laugh uncontrollably, like a mild version of The Joker's laughing venom.

[edit] Other Media

In Batman: The Animated Series, The Joker and Harley Quinn had two or three pet hyenas. However, these hyenas, which weren't named, had pupil-less yellow eyes and tan fur with black spots. Although they were considered the pets of The Joker, it appeared that Harley was the one who cared for them. In Harley and Ivy, The Joker lamented that with Harley gone, the house was a mess, the hyenas hadn't been fed, and he couldn't find his socks. In Harlequinade, the hyenas ran to meet Harley, who fed them large pieces of meat. Later on in the episode, she referred to them as "the babies". At one point in Harley's Holiday, Harley (released from Arkham Asylum), roller-skated down the street pulled along by the leashed hyenas. She wondered why everyone was frightened, then assumed it was because of the clothes she was wearing.

Bud and Lou have also been made into toys.
