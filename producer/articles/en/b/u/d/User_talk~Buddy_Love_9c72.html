I hope you enjoy editing here and being a Wikipedian! Please sign your name on talk pages using four tildes (~~~~); this will automatically produce your name and the date. Hi Buddy. You left me a question about how you check your messages. If you are reading this, I guess it means you've figured it out, but here are the instructions:
Messages on Wikiipedia are not like e-mail, they are more like journal messages left on your Talk page. First, you log in. Then, you click on the link at the top of the page titled "my talk". The message will be there on that page. Johntex\talk 17:31, 21 September 2005 (UTC)
