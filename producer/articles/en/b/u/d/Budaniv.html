Budaniv (Ukrainian: Буданів, Polish: Budzanów, Russian: Буданов Budanov) is a village in Ternopil Oblast, Western Ukraine, near Chortkiv, Buchach. It's known since 1549. Population: 1,634 (2005).

  • Lee Strasberg, born here
