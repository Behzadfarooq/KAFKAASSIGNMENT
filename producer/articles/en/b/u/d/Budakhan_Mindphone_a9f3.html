Budakhan Mindphone is a 1999 album by Squarepusher, released on Warp Records. It follows in much the same vein as its predecessor, Music Is Rotted One Note, and is often classified as an EP due to its relative brevity - the cover refers to it as "a mini-album".

  2. "Fly Street" – 4:52

  4. "Splask" – 3:08
  5. "Two Bass Hit (Dub)" – 3:32
  6. "Varkatope" – 4:09
  7. "Gong Acid" – 4:49
