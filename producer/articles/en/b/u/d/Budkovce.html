Budkovce

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Michalovce District in the Kosice Region

Budkovce is a village and municipality in Michalovce District in the Kosice Region of eastern Slovakia.

[edit] History

In historical records the village was first mentioned in 1319.

[edit] Geography

The village lies at an altitude of 102 metres and covers an area of 19.836 km². It has a population of about 1500 people.

[edit] Ethnicity

The population is almost entirely Slovak in ethnicity.

[edit] Government

The village relies on the tax and district offices, police force and fire brigade at Michalovce although the village has its own birth registry.

[edit] Economy

The village has a post office, a Slovakian bank and insurance company and a number of food stores.

[edit] Sports

The village has a football pitch and a gymnasium.
