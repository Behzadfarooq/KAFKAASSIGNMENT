Budka Suflera

Budka Suflera (English: Prompter's Box) is a Polish rock band which was started in 1969 in Lublin by Romuald Lipko, and, after disbanding soon thereafter, resurrected by Cugowski and Romuald Lipko in 1974 and active to this day. They are perhaps best known for their 1997 song "Takie tango" (English: Such a Tango).

[edit] Members

The band's line-up has changed many times over the years. Since 2005 they are:

  • Krzysztof Cugowski - (vocals)
  • Romuald Lipko - (keyboards)
  • Tomasz Zeliszewski - (percussion)
  • Łukasz Pilch - (electric guitar)
  • Mirosław Stępień - (bass guitar)
  • Piotr Kominek - (keyboards)
  • Anna Patynek - (percussion)
  • Małgorzata Orczyk - (vocals)
  • Irena Kijewska - (vocals)
