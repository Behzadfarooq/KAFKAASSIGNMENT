Budussy is the lingering odor caused by an anatomical mixture of anal, penile, and vaginal secretions. It is most commonly reported following sexual activity.[1]


Example sentences using the word "budussy":

After the party, Shaniqua's bedroom smelled like budussy.

Damn girl, spray the Lysol, it smells like budussy in this piece.[2]


[edit] History of Budussy

The word "Budussy" originates from the 1997 Lionel Martin film, How To Be A Player. In the film, Bernie Mac exclaims, "What's that smell? It smells like Budussy! It smells like booty, dick, and pussy in here!"[3] Since then, the word appeared in the 2004 Timbaland and Magoo song Up Jumps Da Boogie (Remix): "Smell like budussy / Salt n' Pepa push me / How to be a player squeezin on your tushy," among other places. It is now a common slang term.

[edit] Perfume Line

In 2006, the local Harlem perfumery, McRae & Perri, released Budussy perfume. The tagline for the product ran, "Budussy: you wear it best when you wear nothing at all." The perfume is currently sold on street corners in the area.[4]
