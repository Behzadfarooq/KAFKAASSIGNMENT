Budapest Live is an album released in 1984 by Manfred Mann's Earth Band.

  1. "Lies (Through The 80's)" (Newman) – 4:37
  2. "Spirits In The Night" (Bruce Springsteen) – 4:30
  3. "Demolition Man" (Gordon Sumner) – 5:54
  4. "For You" (Springsteen) – 4:14
  5. "Davy's On The Road Again" (Simon, Robertson) – 6:36
  6. "Blinded By The Light" (Springsteen) – 7:38
  7. "Redemption Song (No Kwazulu)" (Bob Marley) – 3:22
  8. "Mighty Quinn" (Bob Dylan) – 4:00
  9. "Runner" (Ian Thomas) – 5:02
  10. "No Transkei" (Mann) – 5:11
  11. "Don't Kill It Carol" (Mike Heron) – 5:21

Tracks 9-11: Bonus Tracks (1999 re-issue)

  • John Lingwood - drums
