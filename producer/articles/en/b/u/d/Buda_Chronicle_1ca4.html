Buda Chronicle

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Buda Chronicle (Buda, András Hess, 1473, 70 fol., 2º.) relates the history of Hungary from the earliest times to the coronation of King Matthias and is based on mediaeval chronicles. Eleven copies of the chronicle are known to exist, two of them in Hungary: one in the National Széchényi Library and another in the Budapest University Library.
