Buddhist universities in the United States and Canada

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

You have new messages (last change).

There are several Buddhist universities in the United States and Canada, including:

  • Dharma Realm Buddhist University - located in Talmage, California
  • University of the West - located in Rosemead, California
  • Naropa University - located in Boulder, Colorado
  • Won Institute of Graduate Studies - located in Glenside, Pennsylvania
