Budli is the name of one or two legendary kings from the Scandinavian Legendary sagas.

[edit] Ásmundar saga kappabana

According to the Ásmundar saga kappabana, he was a Swedish king, and the father of Hildr. The saga relates that Hildebrand, the king of the Huns had a son named Helgi, who was married to Hildr. Helgi and Hildr had a son who was raised by his grand-father and named Hildebrand after him.

Hildebrand became a great warrior and was called the Hunnish champion. When his father Helgi had fallen in a war, his maternal grand-father, the Swedish king Budli, was killed by a Danish king by the name Alf. This Alf took Hildrebrand's mother Hild captive and gave her to the champion Aki with whom she had the son Asmund.

[edit] Völsunga saga

In the Völsunga saga, Budli was the father of Brynhildr (Brünnehilde).
