[edit] Religious Leaders' Names for Bars

I oppose the use of the name of any Religious Leader as a trade name and specifically, for a bar. As a Buddhist I'm opposed to the use of Buddha to identify a bar or a restaurant, much more to placing a Buddha statue at the premises. I think what we need to think about is how controversial it would be if someone were to start a bar with the name "Jesus Bar" or "Muhammedh Bar". Would they even be able to operate the place for sometime? I don't think so. The fact that there are so few Buddhists in the world, as compared to Christians and Muslims and even that small minority tends to be extremely tolerating and accommodating with other religions should not be taken as a grant of permission to ridicule Buddhism in any manner. I hope we can find a way to add this to the criticism section, if others also agree.

Apart from that I really like the music. 124.43.228.244 05:20, 7 February 2007 (UTC)
