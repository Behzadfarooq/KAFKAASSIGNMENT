Buda-Kashalyova

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Coat of arms

Buda-Kashalyova (Belarusian: Буда-Кашалёва, Buda-Kašalova; Russian: Буда-Кошелёво) is a town in Homyel Voblast, Belarus. It has a population of 8,800 (2005 estimate).

It was first mentioned in chronicles from the first half of 1824 as village Buda in Mahilyow Gubernia. It's population had 500 (1890's).
