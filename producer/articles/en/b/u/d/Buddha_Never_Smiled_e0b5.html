Buddha Never Smiled
Directed by Sajeed.A
Written by Sajeed.A
Starring Jeet Pal
Ashish Batra
Music by Rajesh Dass
Distributed by Inigo Motion Picture Company
Country India

Buddha Never Smiled is an Indian film. It is the story of five young men who are stranded in enemy territory during a war and how they cope with the feelings of futility and hopelessness, along with their desire to return home and leave a war in which they do not want to participate.
