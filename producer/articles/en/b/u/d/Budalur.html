Budalur

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Budalur is located in Thanjavur District in the South Indian state of Tamil Nadu. It is approximately 25 km from Thanjavur city, having more than 70 % of Kallar Community people. It is located between Vennaaru river and Kallanai kaalvaai. The name Budalur represents, "Pukuthal"(Enter)+ "Voor"(City). One of Cavery River's branch enters Budalur.
