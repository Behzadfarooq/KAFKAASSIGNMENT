Burntwood Barbarians are a rugby league club founded in 2006, who play in the Rugby League Conference, West Midlands division.

They play their games at Burntwood rugby union club in Burntwood, Staffordshire and were founded by some players who previously played for Wolverhampton Wizards.

[edit] TRL Rugby League Conference: South West and West Midlands 2006

Burntwood's first season was successful, reaching the play-offs and winning their eliminator against Plymouth. Unfortunately holiday traffic and accidents on the M5 caused them to miss their semi-final against Somerset Vikings.
