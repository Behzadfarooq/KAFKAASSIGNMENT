Burn the Priest was Burn the Priest's last album, released in 1998 by Legion Records, before changing their name to Lamb of God. This was Abe Spear's final album on guitar.

[edit] Re-release

On Mar 22, 2005, it was re-released by Epic Records. The album was remixed and remastered by Colin Richardson, it featured new liner notes by original producer Steve Austin (from heavy metal band, Today Is The Day) the cover art was changed in order to be store friendly (just silver band logo on a black background), the track "Ruiner" that was hidden at the end of track #13 ("Duane") was split into its own track (now track #14), and a live video of the song "Bloodletting," taken from the Killadelphia DVD, was put on as an enhanced portion of the disc in QuickTime format.
