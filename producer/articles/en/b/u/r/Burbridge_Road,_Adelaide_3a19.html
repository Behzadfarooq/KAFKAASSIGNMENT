Burbridge Road is an arterial road that travels east-west in the western suburbs of Adelaide, Australia.

Prior to 2001 Burbridge Road extended from West Beach to the Adelaide city centre, however most of the road was renamed to Sir Donald Bradman Drive. West Beach is now the only suburb where the road is still called Burbridge Road.
