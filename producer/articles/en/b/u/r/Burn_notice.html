Burn notice

A burn notice is an official statement issued by one intelligence agency to other agencies. It states that an individual or a group is an unreliable source of information or has become an unreliable source.

It is defined by the US Department of Defense's Dictionary of Military and Associated Terms as:

"an official statement by one intelligence agency to other agencies, domestic or foreign, that an individual or group is unreliable for any of a variety of reasons."[1]

[edit] Burned sources

A number of sources that have achieved prominence in public discourse have been the subject of burn notices. For example:

  • Ahmed Chalabi [2]
  • Curveball (informant) [2]
  • Manucher Ghorbanifar 1984 and 1986 [3][4]
