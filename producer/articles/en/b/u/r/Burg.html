Burg is the German word for castle. As such it appears in the name of many places:

  • Burg bei Magdeburg, in Saxony-Anhalt, in Germany
  • Burg an der Wupper, part of Solingen, Germany, with Schloss Burg
  • Burg Bischofstein, in Rhineland-Palatinate on the Moselle River
  • Burg in Dithmarschen, part of the Amt Burg-Süderhastedt, Schleswig-Holstein, Germany
  • Burg, Hautes-Pyrénées, a commune of the Hautes-Pyrénées département, in southwestern France
  • Burg, Switzerland is a small municipality in the canton of Aargau in Switzerland
  • Burg im Leimental, Switzerland is a small municipality in the canton of Basel-Country in Switzerland
  • BURG, a computer program for generating a fast tree parser using BURS technology
  • Johann Tobias Bürg (1766-1835), Austrian astronomer
  • Bürg (crater) on the moon, named after this astronomer
