Burroughs & Chapin

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Burroughs & Chapin Companyis a company based in Myrtle Beach, South Carolina. It owns or has had a hand in creating some of the most widely known attractions in the area. Some of these include:

  • Broadway at the Beach
  • Myrtle Waves Water Park
  • Myrtle Beach Pavilion
  • NASCAR Speedpark
  • Myrtle Square Mall
  • Coastal Grand Mall


[edit] Main Office

There office is located on Oak Street and 21st Avenue North near the Myrtle Beach Convention Center.
