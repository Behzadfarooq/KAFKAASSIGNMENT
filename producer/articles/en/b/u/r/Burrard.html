Burrard can mean many things:

  • One of the Burrard Baronets, including
  • Sir Harry Burrard, 1st Baronet of Lymington (1755–1813), a British General
  • Sir Harry Burrard-Neale, 2nd Baronet (1765–1840)
  • Burrard Inlet, an inlet in Vancouver, British Columbia, named after Harry Burrard-Neale by Captain George Vancouver (originally Burrard's Canal). And in the 19th Century, used as aba postal-address suffix, abbreviated as in Granville, B.I. and Moodyville, B.I..

The following derive from Burrard Inlet.

    • Burrard Peninsula
    • Burrard (electoral district) (1896–1904, 1917–1925), a federal electoral district
    • Vancouver-Burrard, a provincial electoral district
    • Burrard Street
    • Burrard Street Bridge
    • Burrard Station, a station on Vancouver's SkyTrain system
    • Burrard Dry Dock Shipyards in North Vancouver, BC
