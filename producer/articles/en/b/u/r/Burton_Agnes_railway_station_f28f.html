Burton Agnes railway station

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Burton Agnes railway station was a minor railway station serving the village of Burton Agnes on the Yorkshire Coast Line line from Scarborough to Hull and was opened on 6 October 1846 by the York and North Midland Railway. It closed on 5 January 1970.
