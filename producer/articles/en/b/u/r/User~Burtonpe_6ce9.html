[edit] International Travels

Idea and layout nicked from user:YCCHAN, who took it from User:Salsb, who stole it from User:Guettarda who borrowed it from User:Coolcat


I live in Ottawa, Ontario, Canada, and I've been active on Wikipedia since about September 2005. I currently work for the Canadian Nuclear Safety Commission (CNSC), an agency of the Canadian government.

I've edited many of the nuclear articles to contain some of the interesting tidbits I've come across in my job (primairly to do with CANDU or the Canadian fuel cycle, and all public-domain information), or to make them more correct and/or complete. Of course any views I may express are purely my own and don't necessarily reflect the Government of Canada or CNSC's opinions.

I've also added bits into articles on canoe and kayak (a hobby of mine), the Iron Ring Clock (a former hobby of mine), and several National/Provincial parks I've visited (Algonquin park, Kejimkujik National Park). I created the pages for the Rideau Canoe Club (needs pictures!!), The Tent Dwellers (great book), Khodynka airfield (stayed for 2 weeks not 10 minutes away and never knew it existed) and a few other small ones (Nuclear Power Demonstrator, Douglas Point, Brimstone Hill Fortress National Park).
