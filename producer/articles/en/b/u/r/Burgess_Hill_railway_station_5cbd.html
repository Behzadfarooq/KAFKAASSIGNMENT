Burgess Hill railway station serves Burgess Hill in West Sussex. It is on the Brighton Main Line, and train services are provided by Southern and First Capital Connect.

The typical service from the station is:

  • 3tph (trains per hour) to Brighton
  • 2tph to Bedford via King's Cross Thameslink and London Bridge
  • 1tph to Watford Junction via East Croydon and Clapham Junction

[edit] Station facilities

There is an office and small news stand in the station building. The car park is directly on the London-bound rail.
