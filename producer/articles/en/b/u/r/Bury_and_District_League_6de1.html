The Bury and District League is a football competition based around the town of Bury St Edmunds in England. It has a total of three divisions: Divisions One, Two and Three. Its top division sits at level 18 of the English football league system. It is a feeder to the Suffolk and Ipswich Football League and is currently sponsored by Glasswells World of Furniture.
