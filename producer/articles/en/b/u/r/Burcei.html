Location of Burcei in Italy

Burcei is a comune (municipality) in the Province of Cagliari in the Italian region Sardinia, located about 25 km northeast of Cagliari. As of 31 December 2004, it had a population of 2,943 and an area of 94.8 km².[1]

Burcei borders the following municipalities: San Vito, Sinnai, Villasalto.
