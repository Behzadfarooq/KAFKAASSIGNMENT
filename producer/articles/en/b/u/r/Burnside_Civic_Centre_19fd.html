Burnside Civic Centre

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Burnside Civic Centre houses the council chambers, community centre, library and ballroom of the City of Burnside, Adelaide, South Australia. It is located at the corner of Greenhill and Portrush Roads in Tusmore. It is opposite the popular shopping complex, Burnside Village.
