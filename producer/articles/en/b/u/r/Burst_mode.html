Burst mode may refer to:

  • Burst mode (computing), a computer term for the repeated transmission of data by a device without waiting for additional input.
  • Burst mode (firearm), a firing mode enabling a pre-determined number of rounds to be fired with a single pull of the trigger.
  • Burst mode (photography), a mode of operating a camera that allows multiple snapshots to be taken in a single click of the shutter.
  • Bursting (or burst mode), a rapid signaling mode in neurons.
  • A type of digivolution used in Digimon Savers that surpasses the Mega level
