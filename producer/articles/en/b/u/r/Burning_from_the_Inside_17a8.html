Burning From the Inside is the fourth and final album by British gothic rock band Bauhaus, released in 1983 on Beggar's Banquet Records.

During the recording of the album, singer Peter Murphy was gravely ill, leaving the rest of the band to undertake much of the recording process without him. As evidence of how much input the rest of the band had on the album, bassist David J and guitarist Daniel Ash sang lead vocals on a number of songs. This, combined with the fact that the band started recording without Peter, led to internal difficulties, and by the time the album was released, they had already broken up.

A video was made for the single "She's in Parties".
