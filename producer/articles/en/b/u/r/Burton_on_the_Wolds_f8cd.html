Burton on the Wolds

Burton on the Wolds is a village situated on the B676 3 miles west of the A46 in Leicestershire and has a population of around 800. The Parish Council of Burton on the Wolds, Cotes and Prestwold serves the village and its two neighbouring hamlets.

Burton has its own primary school, pub and shop (in the garage), with most of the village life centering at the pub and village hall, and the village is famous for its Lion's Head fountain.
