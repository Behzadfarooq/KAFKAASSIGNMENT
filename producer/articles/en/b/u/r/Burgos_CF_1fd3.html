Burgos Club de Fútbol is a football team based in Burgos in the autonomous community of Castile-Leon. Founded in 1994, it plays in the Segunda División B - Group 2. Its stadium is El Plantío with a capacity of 14,000.

[edit] Seasons

  • 2004/2005: Segunda División B 3rd
  • 2005/2006: Segunda División B 3rd
  • 2006/2007: Segunda División B
  • 1 seasons in Segunda División
  • 8 seasons in Segunda División B
  • 1 seasons in Tercera División
