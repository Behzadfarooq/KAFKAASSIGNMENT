[edit] Buraisu Naivedo

Internet Nerd, attendee of Penny Arcade Expo. I have been using the internet and have owned a computer for over 12 years now. I also been a video gamer since the release of the NES. Currently, I am going to college and majoring in International Business. My dream is to be a Video Game producer. I have been using Wikipedia for a long time but have finally made an account after I thought of something to add to the great database that is Wikipedia. I am currently planning to write a couple articles for wikipedia to add to the database. One of the first topics I am going to write about is going to be the term hotboxing and how the term is used by computer nerds that means "To use more then one computer at the same time" this term is also called duel-boxing and boxing. I have seen it done before by other nerds and my last roommate who owns four computers has done it before. It plays in a MMORPG in a group of five with himself. I also have heard the term used all of time by nerds like myself. Though, I don't do it myself because I am not into it. As a outside observer, it should be easy to write about because I am not bias on the topic. I have also seen people duel-box at Penny Arcade Expo.

Many people might know me from MMORPGs as the Infamous Buraisu or other past usernames. I have played MMORPGs and online RPGs like World of Warcraft, Phantasy Star Online, Ragnarok Online, Diablo II (and I), and a countless number of MMORPGs that I have play tested and beta tested. The main reason I am infamous because I like to counter internet trolls that flame and create hate topics to boost there ego. I am also cocky and kind of a jerk when things don't go my way. Like when people mess up in a party when playing a MMORPG or someone that doesn't know what their doing tries to lead the group of players.

Since I live in one of the central locations for the video game industry, I have a lot of experience in the subject and have even play tested for a lot of companies. I also watch anime and have other interests that might help me write some topics that might not already in on Wikipedia. I am still new to this editing stuff but I am learning fast. Hopefully one day I will make a great article that is useful to a lot of people.

Watch out, nerdcore is rising.
