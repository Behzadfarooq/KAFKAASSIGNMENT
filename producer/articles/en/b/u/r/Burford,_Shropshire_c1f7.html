Burford, Shropshire

Burford is a village and civil parish in the South Shropshire district of Shropshire, England. The village is to the north of the River Teme; on the other side of the Teme is the Worcestershire town of Tenbury Wells. To the west the A456 road bridges the Ledwyche Brook leading to the Herefordshire village of Little Hereford, while to the east is the Shropshire civil parish of Boraston. Burford House Gardens is a popular destination.

Even though Burford has never been in Herefordshire or Worcestershire, the fire station is run by the Hereford and Worcester fire service and the hospital is run by the South Worcestershire NHS Trust. Equally, the workhouse serving Burford (http://www.tenburyworkhouse.co.uk/) was in Worcestershire.

According to the 2001 census the parish had a population of 1,108.
