Burn pan! Mix, stretch and boil! (Japanese: 燃えろ寸胴！混ぜて伸ばして茹で上げろ！, - Moero Zundō! Mazete nobashite yude agero!) is episode 168 of the anime series Naruto. It is a filler episode, with a story unique to the anime series.

[edit] Episode Synopsis

Spoiler warning: Plot and/or ending details follow.

In this episode, Naruto, Chōji and Sakura volunteer to help Teuchi make the perfect ramen noodle dish in order to save his daughter Ayame from a band of ryōnin who want the recipe for themselves. The episode starts with Naruto and Choji wanting some Ramen, but the shop is closed. They go in, order some Ramen, and get Teuchi's new menu. He gets a burn, and tells them about the ryonin who have kidnapped Ayame. Naruto, Choji, and Sakura learn how to make Ramen. In the cook-off, Naruto uses the Rasengan to stir the ingredients, takes advantage of Sakura's insane strength, and Choji uses his partial multi-size technique. Naruto uses the Kage Bunshin to serve the Ramen to the 100+ ryonin. Ayame is back but is very fat. In the end, there is a cameo of nearly all the girls in the series lining up for the new diet ramen.Once Naruto walks into the store and askes Ayame for her dad, Naruto sees that Ayame's dad ate too much diet soup. Ayame's dad is so skinny you can see his bones through his skin.

[edit] Quotes and trivia

  • This episode introduces the ryōri ninja (cooking ninja), not mentioned as a regular ninja rank.
  • The crow that made regular appearances in Episode 101, saying "Stupid! Stupid!" makes a cameo.
  • Temari from the Sand Village makes a cameo in the end; she's in line to try the new Diet Ramen dish.
  • Sasame of the Fuma Clan (from Orochimaru's mansion Arc) also appears a cameo on the Diet Ramen dish line along side with Temari
