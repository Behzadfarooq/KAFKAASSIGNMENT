Bury the Hatchet was the fourth album by The Cranberries, released in 1999. All lyrics by Dolores O'Riordan, and all music by Noel Hogan and Dolores O'Riordan except tracks 3, 8, 10 and 13 by Dolores O'Riordan. In the US, the album has sold 377,000 copies as of April 2007.

This album has sold over 3,500,000 copies to date, and sold over a million copies in one month.[citation needed] It reached number one in the album charts of 17 countries.[citation needed]

The album was re-released in 2002, under the title Bury the Hatchet (The Complete Sessions 1998-1999). This version of the album featured bonus tracks; b-sides from the singles lifted off the album.

  1. Baby Blues (2:38)
  2. Sweetest Thing (3:34)
  3. Woman Without Pride (2:26)
  4. Such a Shame (4:23)
  5. Paparazzi on Mopeds (4:33)

An alternative version of this re-release also exists, featuring the bonus tracks on a separate disc and coupled up with some live tracks, it is called "Bury The Hatchet Complete Sessions", and was released in 2000, with these tracks:
