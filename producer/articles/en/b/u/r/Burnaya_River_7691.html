Burnaya River

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Burnaya (Russian: Бурная, Finnish: Taipaleenjoki) is a river in Priozersk District of Leningrad Oblast, Russia, on the Karelian Isthmus. It flows from Lake Sukhodolskoye to Lake Ladoga.
