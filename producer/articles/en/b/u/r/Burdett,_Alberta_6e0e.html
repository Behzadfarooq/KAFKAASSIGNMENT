Location of Burdett, Alberta

Coordinates: 49°49′48.9″N, 111°31′18.0″W

Burdett is a village in the Canadian province of Alberta. It lies 71 km west of Medicine Hat and 97 km east of Lethbridge, on Highway 3. It had a population of 264 as of the 2001 census.
