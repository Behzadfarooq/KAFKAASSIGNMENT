The Burlington Chiefs are Junior "A" box lacrosse team from Burlington, Ontario, Canada. The Chiefs play in the OLA Junior A Lacrosse League.

Hamilton Bengals 1976 - 1983
Bay Area Bengals 1984 - 1991
Burlington Chiefs 1992 to Present
