Burial Plot Bidding War is the first release by New York native metalcore band Every Time I Die. This EP was first released on Good Fellow Records in 2000, then reissued by Undecided Records in 2003 with different artwork.

[edit] Track listing

  1. "The Emperor's New Clothes" – 4:53
  2. "Your Touch Versus Death" – 3:35
  3. "Prom Song" – 3:51
  4. "Home Is Where You Hang Yourself" – 4:09
  5. "Morphine Season" – 3:29
