Burriana

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Burriana (or Borriana in Catalan/Valencian) is a town in eastern Spain, in the province of Castellón, part of the autonomous community of Valencia. Its population exceeds 34,000, many of them recent immigrants from North Africa and Eastern Europe (especially Romania).

The town, located by the Mediterranean Sea, has a nice beach (Platja de l'Arenal) and a former commercial harbor, now converted in a marina for small and medium sized yachts.
