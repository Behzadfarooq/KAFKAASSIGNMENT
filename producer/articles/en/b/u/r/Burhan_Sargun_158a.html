Burhan Sargun (born 11 February 1929 in Ankara) is a former Turkish footballer.

During his club career he played for Fenerbahçe, scoring a total of 112 goals. He played 8 games and scored 8 goals for the Turkey national football team, including a hat-trick in the 1954 FIFA World Cup against South Korea.
