[edit] Merge/Move

A new merge proposal came up in September, after the earlier one was removed in April. There is a considerable amount of overlap between this article, hamburger, and the others listed above. The only things in this article that is so generic as to warrant coverage seperate from the hamburger article are this article's treatment of other meats and the United Kingdom section, which . I propose that burger be moved to patty, to use the more common term, and be stripped of any reference to condiments and bread outside of its introductory paragraph, leaving that to hamburger. Should also fix redirects that point to this page that ought to point to hamburger, after the move. Would also have to clean up Hamburger's treatment of other meats/meat substitutes and introduce links to this article post-move. MrZaiustalk 21:32, 27 September 2006 (UTC)

So much for discussion. Created Patty with non-duplicated data after someone deleted Burger. Will clean up further. MrZaiustalk 20:10, 2 October 2006 (UTC)

[edit] Remerge

Proposed remerge: Several months ago, this article was deleted without discussion, ignoring the discussion of a proposed move to patty. After the article was deleted, I copied it's contents over there anyway, as all coverage of *burgers had been moved to hamburger. However, it seems to have been recreated, and with essentially the same text. I believe the redirect to hamburger should be reinstated, and that hamburger should be moved to burger.

Before the proposed move to patty, there was a proposal that burger and hamburger be merged. The data in burger that didn't really belong in the hamburger article is all duplicated in a more appropriate place now, and hamburger covers far, far more than simple hamburgers. Porkburgers, chickeburgers, etc are all covered in that article. To the best of my knowledge, only veggie burger and burgerless burger stand as seperate articles from hamburger. If this article is moved to patty again, that would free up this name as a more appropriate place for the hamburger article. Thoughts? MrZaiustalk 20:24, 8 November 2006 (UTC)
