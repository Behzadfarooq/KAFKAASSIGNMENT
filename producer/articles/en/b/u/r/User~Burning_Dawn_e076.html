What is Burning Dawn?
Burning Dawn is a Clan that is presently active in Jedi knight: Jedi Academy. Following the principles of Bushido, Burning Dawn follows the code of the Samurai and distinguishes itself from other Clans by also throwing Role Play (RP) into the loop. They can be defined as "one of the best clans in Jedi Academy", setting themselves apart with their originality, specific Ranking system, and developmental stages that members must progress through.

[edit] Branches

The Branches
In Burning Dawn there is presently 2 Branches. These include the Guntai and the Law Branch. Working respectively, both branches compliment each other and define the clan as a whole.
Guntai
The Guntai are the Army of Burning Dawn. They are the fighting force. They have seen action since there beginning, fighting in several wars with several other rival clans. They are put through a rigorous training schedule and are expected to be in prime condition every day. There are currently 2 platoons, each run by a Bansho, which is the officer is charge or the platoon leader. Each Platoon is under command of the War General, which is over complete control of the Guntai.
Law Branch
Unlike the Guntai, which is mainly incharge of fighting and war time situations, the Law Branch is a group of people who inforce the rules on and off the server. They are the Police of Burning Dawn. They take care of lamers, annoyances, and pests in order to keep the peace on the server.

[edit] The Council

What is the purpose of the Council
The Council is the brain of Burning Dawn. They toil in affairs of Clan matters and Clan organization. They are the deciding factor is most issues in burning dawn. The Council is incharge of the organization of Burning Dawn along side of addressing any current matters in the Clan. Before every meeting the Council meets in silence to discuss what will be addressed at the meeting.
Who is on the Council?
The Council Currently Consists of 5 members. These members Include:
  • Shogun Onikage Takeno
  • Hanji Hokusai Toshima
  • Yoriki Denkou Yamashiro
  • Gadamon Taisa Goemon Fukusaku
  • Jogensho Saburo Takeshi

[edit] Ranking System

Why Ranks?
The Ranks define what type of person you are, what type of leader your are, and how much you have progressed since your recruitment. Each member of Burning Dawn holds his rank highly and lives up to the rank every second they are on the server. You do not abuse your rank or the council will be sure to take swift action.
What are the Ranks?
There are several ranks in Burning Dawn. These are listed here from highest to lowest:

[edit] Bushido

What is Bushido?
Bushido means the way of the warrior or a japanese code of conduct. IT was developed between the 11th to 14th centuries. According to the Japanese dictionary Shogakukan Kokugo Daijiten, "Bushido is defined as a unique philosophy (ronri) that spread through the warrior class from the Muromachi (chusei) period."

[edit] Contact us

How do I join Burning Dawn?
There are a few ways to join burning dawn, but ultimately they all bring you to one way.
First
One way is you could get on Burning Dawns official server in Jedi Academy and talk to the men incharge of recruitment (Onikage or Ryusei). If neither is on, then talk to anyone on the server, and we will be sure to contact Onikage or Ryusei. Ultimately you will have to do the second way though.
Second
The other way (and the final way) is to go to the Burning Dawn forums and list yourself there. Go to the forums, and enter the Sub-section entitled "The Goten". Follow the directions for posting your application and someone will get back to you ASAP.
