Burren Energy

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Burren Energy plc (LSE: BUR) is a British oil exploration and production company founded in 1994 by chairman Finian Oâ€™Sullivan. The company originally began as an oil transportation company in the Caspian Sea region, but later diversified into oil production in Turkmenistan, and in 2002 acquired Tacoma Resources, a company developing oil reserves in the Republic of Congo.

The company floated on the London Stock Exchange in late 2003, and is part of the FTSE 250 index. In February 2005, the company acquired a 26% stake in Indian firm Hindustan Oil Exploration [1], and was awarded two exploration contracts in Egypt [2].

The company expanded exploration into Yemen and Oman in January 2006 [3][4].
