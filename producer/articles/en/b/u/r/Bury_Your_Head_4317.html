Bury Your Head is a song by post-hardcore band Saosin. It was one of the, if not the first song Cove Reber had written with Saosin. An unmastered version leaked to the internet around September 2004. Many versions have been done, including the one for the EP, the one for the full-length, an acoustic version for the EP and a re-done version for an Atticus compilation.
