Burton Agnes Hall is an Elizabethan manor house in the village of Burton Agnes, near Driffield in Yorkshire, originally built in 1173.

Today, the estate is managed by Hon Elizabeth Susan Whitelaw, daughter of the 1st Viscount Whitelaw and her son Simon.
