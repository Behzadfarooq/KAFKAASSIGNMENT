Burrough Green C of E Primary School is a primary school in South Cambridgeshire. It is situated next to the village green in the centre of Burrough Green, on the Cambridge/Suffolk border approximately 13 miles east of Cambridge and 6 miles south of Newmarket.

The school caters for children aged between 4 and 11 but benefits from having its own Playgroup on site and an After School Kids Club. The School enjoys good links with the local Parish Church, St Augustine's, and with the community at large.

Statement of Purpose: To provide for children of all abilities aged 4-11 years a high quality all round education within a Christian context so as to develop in each child his or her full potential. To prepare those children both for their future education and to equip them with the skills for life in society.
