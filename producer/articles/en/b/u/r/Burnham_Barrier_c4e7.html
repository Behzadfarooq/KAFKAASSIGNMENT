Burnham Barrier

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

You have new messages (last change).

The Burnham Barrier is an island in Northeast Washington, D.C. which separates the Anacostia River from Kingman Lake. The island is traversed by Benning Road and East Capitol Street. North of the Benning Road bridge on Burnham Barrier lies the Langston Golf Course.
