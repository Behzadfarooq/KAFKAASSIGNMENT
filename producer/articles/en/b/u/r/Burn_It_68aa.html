Burn It

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Burn It is a drama series shown on BBC Three following the lives of three twenty-somethings in Manchester in 2003. The series follows Carl (Chris Coghill), Andy (Keiran O'Brien) and Jon (William Ash) as three self-proclaimed Generation X-ers going through life without wives, babies and responsibilities to worry them. Two series were made running from February 2003 until 2004. Burn It also starred Lisa Faulkner and Mel B.
