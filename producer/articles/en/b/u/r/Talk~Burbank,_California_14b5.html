[edit] Airport

I clarified the statement about Burbank Airport being the second largest airport in Los Angeles county. It was too vague.

A person may interpret this in area, or total volume, instead of commercial traffic volume.

Palmdale Regional Airport is the largest airport in Los Angeles county in area, followed by LAX.

Van Nuys Airport is the busiest airport in Los Angeles County total volume wise (daily take-offs and landings), as well as in the world. LAX is second in total volume in the county.

LAX is the largest in commercial traffic volume in the County, and third in the U.S.A. Then Bob Hope Airport, as it's now called, comes in second in the county.

This will give Wikipedia users a more accurate analysis of the statement.
