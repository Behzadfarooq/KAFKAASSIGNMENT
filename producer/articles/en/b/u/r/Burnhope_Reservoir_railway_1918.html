The Burnhope Reservoir railway was an industrial narrow gauge railway built to serve the construction of Burnhope reservoir near Weardale. An extensive network of 2 ft (610 mm) gauge lines connected the North Eastern Railway branch terminus at Weardale with the dam construction site.
