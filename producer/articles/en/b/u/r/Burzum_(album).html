Burzum is the self-titled debut album by Burzum.

When this album was released, Euronymous (owner of Deathlike Silence Records) changed the name of "Ea, Lord Of The Depths" to "Ea, Lord Of The Deeps." It was later changed to the intended title on the later remaster. The album is often released on the same disc/LP as the Aske EP, probably due to Aske only having three tracks.
