Burnt to Bitz is a live album by Graham Coxon. The two-disc album is a complete recording of Coxon's gig at the London Astoria on the 25 October 2006. Copies of the limited edition were sold at the door of the performance and were avalible online soon after the gig for download. As a result, copies of the actual CD are a valuable rarity.
