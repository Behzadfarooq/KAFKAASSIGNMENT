Bureau of Primary Health Care

The Bureau of Primary Health Care is a sub program of the United States Department of Health and Human Services.

[edit] Key areas of responsibility

  1. Aging
  2. Pneumoconiosis (Black Lung)
  3. Border Health
  4. Clinical Quality
  5. Community Health Centers
  6. Federal Tort Claims Act and Health Centers
  7. Hansen's Disease
  8. Health Care for the Homeless
  9. Health Center Data
  10. Health Center Technical Assistance
  11. Healthy Communities Access Program (HCAP)
  12. Migrant and Farmworker Health
  13. Pharmacy Affairs
  14. Primary Care Associations and Primary Care Offices
  15. Public Housing Primary Care
  16. Radiation Exposure Screening and Education
  17. School-Based Health
