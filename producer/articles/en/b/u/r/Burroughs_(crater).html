Burroughs (crater)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Burroughs crater is a large crater on Mars at latitude 72.5S / longitude 243.1W, with a diameter of 104.0 kilometers. It is named after Edgar Rice Burroughs, the American science fiction novelist who wrote a series of fantasy novels set on the planet.
