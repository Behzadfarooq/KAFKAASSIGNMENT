Bury Your Dead is a five-piece Hardcore band that hails from Worcester, Massachusetts. They combine Hardcore with elements of nu metal. Their wide range of influences and their combination of sounds can be seen on the sticker that comes on their latest cd that reads "Sounds like" and then mentions bands of various genres from Lamb of God to KoRn. They are currently playing with Suicide Silence on their headline "Don't Call It A Comeback Tour" and are scheduled to play New England Metal Fest 2007.

It was announced in March 2007 that Michael Crafter, ex-vocalist for I Killed The Prom Queen and Carpathian, has taken over vocal duties for the upcoming US tour[1].
