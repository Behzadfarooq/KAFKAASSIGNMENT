Burn, Berlin, Burn! is a compilation album released by Atari Teenage Riot in 1997. Initially released only in the United States by the Beastie Boys' record label Grand Royal (Mike D quoted the music as being "the most punk rock shit he'd ever heard" [1]), this album is a collection of tracks from their first two albums Delete Yourself and The Future of War.
