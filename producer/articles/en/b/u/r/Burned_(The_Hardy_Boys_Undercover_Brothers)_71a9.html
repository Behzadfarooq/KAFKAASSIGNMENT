Burned is the 6th book in The Hardy Boys Undercover Brothers series. It was first published in October 2005 by Aladdin Paperbacks an imprint of Simon & Schuster

Spoiler warning: Plot and/or ending details follow.

ATAC send Frenk and Joe Hardy on mission to smoke out the mastermind behind and learn the structure of an illegal international CD-burning operation that employs teenagers. But the Hardys don't have to go far with one of their classmates, Julian Sanders, the prime suspect.
