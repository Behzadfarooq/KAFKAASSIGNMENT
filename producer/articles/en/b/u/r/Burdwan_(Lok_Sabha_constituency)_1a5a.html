[edit] Assembly segments

Burdwan Lok Sabha constituency is composed of the following assembly segments [2]:

  • Bardhaman North
  • Bardhaman South
  • Memari
  • Bhatar
  • Khandaghosh
  • Raina
  • Jamalpur
