Municipalities and townships of Geauga County.

Burton Township is one of the sixteen townships of Geauga County, Ohio, United States, located on the eastern part of the county. As of the 2000 census, Burton township had a total population of 4,358. The village of Burton, Ohio is centrally located within the township.
