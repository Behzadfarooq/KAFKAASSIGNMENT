Burnie United Football Club is a Football (soccer) Club which represents Burnie in the Tasmanian Northern Premier League. The club also fields teams in all junior divisions, as well as womens teams. Burnie United play their home games at Montello, in Burnie, Tasmania.

Burnie United was formed out of a unification in 1978 between Burnie Spartans and Burnie Rovers, both of which clubs played at Montello.


[edit] Honours

  • Northern Premierships: 4 times(1978,1979,1996,1997)
