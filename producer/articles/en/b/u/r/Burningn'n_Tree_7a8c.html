Burningn'n Tree is a compilation album released by Tom Jenkinson under his alias of Squarepusher. It compiles two early EPs, namely Conumber and Alroy Road Trax, which were both released under his other pseudonym Duke of Harringay on his independent label Spymania. Burningn'n Tree also contains three previously unreleased compositions by Squarepusher. The album is a fusion of drill and bass and jazz. Burningn'n Tree names all songs after their respective track index, but many of the songs were titled when released previously; those titles are parenthesized below.
