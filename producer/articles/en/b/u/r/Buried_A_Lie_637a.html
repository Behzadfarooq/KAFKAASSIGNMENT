Buried A Lie is the lead single off of Senses Fail's second album, Let It Enfold You.

[edit] Music Video

The music video of this song portrays a comical story of a boy whose girlfriend dies. It is pronounced a tragic accident but the protagonist thinks otherwise. He finds the man who poisoned her reports him to the police.
