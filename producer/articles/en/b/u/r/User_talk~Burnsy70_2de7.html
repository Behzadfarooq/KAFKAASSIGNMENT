Hi again and thanks for the email. Yes, there is a problem using this photo - Wikipedia is unable to use copyright material unless the copyright owner releases it under a licence compatible with Wikipedia's GFDL copyright licence. Only the copyright owner can agree to do this.
Most works from the U.S. (like this photo) are protected by copyright until 70 years after the death of the author/photographer. If the work is anonymous, it is protected for 120 years from the time that it was written/the photo was taken!
Sorry to have been the bearer of bad tidings so early into your time with Wikipedia - hopefully you won't be put off! Just remember - anything that you upload here, whether pictures or text, either has to be your own work, or be copyright-free in one way or another. If you have any more questions about this, please feel free to ask.
PS. Just a quick tip - it's better practice to reply to users on their Talk pages (eg. mine is here than via email)
