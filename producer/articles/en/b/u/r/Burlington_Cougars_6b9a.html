City: Burlington, Ontario

Division: West
Founded: 1952
Home Arena: Central Arena
Colours: Blue, Copper, Red, and White
Head Coach: Shawn Hudson
General Manager: Barry Rice
Farm club(s): Stoney Creek Warriors

The Burlington Cougars are a Tier II Junior "A" ice hockey team from Burlington, Ontario, Canada. They are a part of the Ontario Provincial Junior A Hockey League.
