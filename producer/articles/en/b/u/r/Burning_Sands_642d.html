[edit] Burning Sands

According to IMDB.comThis film was written by D.K Holmes and is currently in pre-production and will be released in 2008. D.K Holmes is also producing and directing the drama that is based on true events and set to start filming in April 2007.

[edit] Plot Summary

The true story of five friends forced to ban together as they embark on the treacherous task of pledging a historically black fraternity. The friends wonder if they will fulfill their destiny and become members or fold and succumb to the relentless thrashing of the fraternity brothers. Are the rewards enough to validate enduring the torture of this hellish process? Written by Anonymous

[edit] Featuring

The film is set to feature; ATL star and Diana Ross's son Evan Ross, up and comerCory Hardrict,Take the Lead actor Elijah Kelley, comedian/t.v. star Brandon T. Jackson, and Half & Half star Chico Benymon.
