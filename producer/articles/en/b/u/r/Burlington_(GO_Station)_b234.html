The Burlington GO Station is a train and bus station in the GO Transit network, located at 2101 Fairview Street in Burlington, Ontario, Canada, just south of Queen Elizabeth Way between Guelph Line and Brant Street.

It is a stop on the Lakeshore West line train service, and serves as the weekday western terminus of the rail services. Some peak-hour weekday trains also service Aldershot and Hamilton further to the west. Bus connections to/from Aldershot and Hamilton are provided for all other trains. There is a separate connecting GO Bus serving McMaster University. Weekend and holiday service is provided by bus to/from Oakville.

There are extensive parking facilities on both the north and south of the station. During weekdays, Burlington Transit serves both sides of the station, connected by wheelchair accessible tunnels under the busy CN freight tracks.
