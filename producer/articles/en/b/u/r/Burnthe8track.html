burnthe8track is a punk rock band from Winnipeg, Manitoba. The group formed in 2001 and has toured the United States, Canada, and Western Europe. The band's debut album was "The Ocean." Since then, they have released an EP, "Division," and have done much of the recording work for a new album to be released in 2006.

The band has been featured on Definitely Not the Opera on CBC Radio, in a story on their quest to get their music video "Buried Beneath Us" shot and played on MuchMusic. Several of their songs were featured in ESPN NHL 2K5.

[edit] Discography

  • The Ocean (2004)
  • Division (EP) (2004)
  • Fear of Falling Skies (working title, mid-2006 release)

[edit] Members
