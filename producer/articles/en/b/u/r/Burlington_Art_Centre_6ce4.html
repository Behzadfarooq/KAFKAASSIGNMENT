Burlington Art Centre

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Burlington Art Centre is an art centre in close proximity to Spencer Smith Park in Burlington, Ontario. It features many modern art. The Burlington Art Centre shows various exhibits throughout the year from local to national talent, including the renowned Canadian artist Robert Bateman.
