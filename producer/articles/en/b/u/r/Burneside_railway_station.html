Burneside railway station is a railway station in Burneside in Cumbria. The station is situated on the Windermere Branch Line from Oxenholme to Windermere. Burneside is a request stop. It is also one of the few stations with a manned level crossing. It only has one platform.

[edit] Services

Weekdays there is an hourly service to Windermere, and return to Oxenholme of which 6 services continue to Preston and Manchester. Sundays, there is an hourly service to Windermere and return to Oxenholme.
