Re-release of the first two albums ('Burzum' LP and 'Aske' EP, which had gone out-of-print). The S/T album is not reproduced in full, though - the track 'A Lost Forgotten Sad Spirit' is missing. This song appears here only in its 'Aske' version. Only available as a digipack and 2-LP.

Re-released in 2005 by Back on Black Records. Double black LP
