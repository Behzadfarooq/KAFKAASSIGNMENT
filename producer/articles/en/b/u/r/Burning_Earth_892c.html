Burning Earth is Firewind's second album, released in 2003. The original rhythm section of Brian Harris and Konstantine were replaced by Stian Kristoffersen and Petros Christo, respectively.

[edit] Line-up

  • Stephen Fredrick - Vocals
  • Gus G - Guitars & Keys
  • Petros Christo - Bass
  • Stian Lindas Kristoffersen - Drums
