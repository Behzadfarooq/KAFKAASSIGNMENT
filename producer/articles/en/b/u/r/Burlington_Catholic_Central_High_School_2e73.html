Burlington Catholic Central High School

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Burlington Catholic Central High School is a private, Catholic, co-ed college preparatory high school in Burlington, Wisconsin, USA, in the Archdiocese of Milwaukee. Enrollment is currently 180 students, drawing from across the southeastern Wisconsin area. The school was founded in 1924.

Students attending Catholic Central High School are given the opportunity of many various activities including, among others not listed, Key Club, NHS, Yearbook, Chinese I, Chinese II, and the newly formed Frisbee Club.

The school colors are royal blue and white, and the athletic teams are referred to as the Hilltoppers. The Boys Varsity Basketball team won the State Championship (Div. 4) in 2006. Boys baseball team was state champs in 2004-2005 (Div. 4). Football state runner ups 2004 (Div. 7). Volleyball State Champions in 2006 (Div. 3).
