Burlington Central High School is a Junior High/High School located in Burlington, Ontario. The building it now stands in was built in 1922 and was added to extensively over the years with the last major addition being made in the year 1965, when a new technical wing, and gymnasiums were added. Originally called 'Burlington High School' it is the oldest operating high school in Burlington.

The longest serving principal, Mr. W. E. Bates, started at the school in 1929 and served until 1964.

Many B.H.S. Students fought in the Second World War and are commemorated in the School's War Memorial.
