Burning Angel is the first EP from the Swedish melodic death metal band Arch Enemy. It was released in March 6, 2002. This track includes two songs from their album Wages of Sin, it is also their first album with new member Angela Gossow, who has taken Johan Liiva's place in vocals. The song Starbreaker is a Judas Priest cover from the album Sin After Sin.

[edit] Track listing

  1. "Burning Angel (Album version)" - 4:17
  2. "Lament of A Mortal Soul (Unreleased song from the "Wages of Sin" sessions)" - 4:04
  3. "Starbreaker (Judas Priest cover, recorded in 98 feat. original vocalist Johan Liiva)" - 3:25
