Burmerange (Luxembourgish: Biermereng, German: Burmeringen) is a commune and small town in south-eastern Luxembourg. It is part of the canton of Remich, which is part of the district of Grevenmacher.

As of 2005, the town of Burmerange, which lies in the south-east of the commune, has a population of 173. Other towns within the commune include Elvange.
