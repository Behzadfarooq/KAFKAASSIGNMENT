Burke Baronets

There have been two Burke Baronetcies in the Baronetage of Ireland

The first, Burke of Glinsk, County Galway, was created in 1628, the second, Burke of Marble Hill, County Galway, on 5 December 1797.

[edit] Burke of Glinsk, County Galway (1628)

  • Sir Ulick Bourke, 1st Baronet (ca. 1594–ca. 1660)
  • Sir Edmund Bourke, 2nd Baronet (died 1676/1687)
  • Sir Ulick Burke, 3rd Baronet (died 1708)
  • Sir John Burke, 4th Baronet (died 1721/1724)
  • Sir Festus Burke, 5th Baronet (died ca. 1730)
  • Sir Theobald Burke, 6th Baronet (died ca. 1740)
  • Sir Henry Burke, 7th Baronet (died 1747/1748)
  • Sir Ulick Burke, 8th Baronet (died 1759)
  • Sir Henry John Burke, 9th Baronet (died 1814)
  • Sir John Ignatius Burke, 10th Baronet (1784–1845)
  • Sir Joseph Burke, 11th Baronet (1786–1865)
  • Sir John Lionel Burke, 12th Baronet (1818–1884)
  • Lt.-Col. Sir Theobald Hubert Burke, 13th Baronet (1833–1909)

[edit] Burke of Marble Hill, County Galway (1797)

  • Sir Thomas Burke, 1st Baronet (died 1813)
  • Colonel Sir John Burke, 2nd Baronet (1782–1847)
  • Sir Thomas John Burke, 3rd Baronet (1813–1875)
  • Sir John Charles Burke, 4th Baronet (1858–1880)
  • Sir Henry George Burke, 5th Baronet (1859–1910)
  • Sir Thomas Malachy Burke, 6th Baronet (1864–1913)
  • Sir Gerald Howe Burke, 7th Baronet (1893–1954)
  • Sir Thomas Stanley Burke, 8th Baronet (1916–1989)
  • Sir James Stanley Gilbert Burke, 9th Baronet (born 1956)
