Buracona

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Buracona (Capeverdean Crioulo, ALUPEC or ALUPEK and the São Vicente Crioulo: Burakina) is a cape and a small bay located in the west of the island of Sal, the cape is approximately 4 km west of the island capital of Espargos. The point is accessed with the road linking with Palmeira and the west of the island. The cape are surrounded by palm trees

[edit] Geography and Information

The cape's soil colour are light brown, tan-red, butterschotch and rarely brown. The bay extends from Fontona north to another promontory.

[edit] Panorama

The promontory offers a panoramic of the western portion of the island of Sal as well as its mountains including most of Monte Grande.
