Burg Vischering

Burg Vischering in Lüdinghausen is one of the largest castles in northern Germany. It was seriously damaged in World War II, leading to minor alterations in the design of the castle. It consists of a small courtyard enclosed with low stone walls, a long wooden bridge over the moat, and the main building.
