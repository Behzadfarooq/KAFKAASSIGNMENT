[edit] AKA Corey Herrington

Corey James Herrington (Born December 18, 1989) is a seventeen year old male student who resides in West Michigan.

[edit] Early Life

Corey was born at North Ottawa Community Hospital in Grand Haven, Michigan on December 18, 1989 during the tv show "Wheel of Fortune." He lived in Grand Haven a couple of years and his family moved to a duplex in Nunica, Michigan. A few years after that, they moved into a duplex in Spring Lake, Michigan where he spent most of his childhood growing up.

[edit] Current Life

Corey resides with his family (father, mother and brother) in Nunica, Michigan where he has lived since 1998. He very much dislikes living there and needs somewhere to live.

[edit] Hobbies & Personal Life

At his current age of 17, Corey enjoys playing his Ibanez RG350EX electric guitar. He uses a Line 6 Spider II guitar amp anda prefers thin guitar picks. Corey is currently making demos by himself only with guitar. He is in a relationship with a schoolmate, Gloria and have been going out since mid-January. Corey also enjoys playing video games. He prefers Nintendo over Sony or Microsoft but his first gaming console was a Playstation. His two favorite games are The Legend of Zelda (series) and Metal Gear Solid (series).
