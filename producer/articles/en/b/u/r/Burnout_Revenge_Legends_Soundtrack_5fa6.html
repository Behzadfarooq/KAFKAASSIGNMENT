Burnout Revenge/Legends Soundtrack

Burnout Revenge cover
Burnout Legends cover

Below are songs from the soundtracks of Burnout Revenge and the PSP Burnout Legends. The games share some of the same songs. They are played in the menu screens and during races upon the in-game radio station "Crash FM".

All songs below are in Burnout Revenge. Songs that are included in Burnout Legends are in bold.

  • The Academy Is... - Almost Here (from Almost Here)
  • The All-American Rejects - Top of the World(from Move Along)
  • Andy hunter° - Come On(from Life)
  • Apocalyptica - Life Burns!(from Apocalyptica)
  • Asian Dub Foundation - Flyover(from Tank)
  • Avenged Sevenfold - Beast and the Harlot (shortened to 'Beast...' in the in-game listing)(from City Of Evil)
  • Billy Talent - Red Flag (from Billy Talent II)
  • Bloc Party - Helicopter(from Silent Alarm)
  • The Bravery - An Honest Mistake (Superdiscount Remix)(cannot be turned off)
  • Bullet for My Valentine - Hand of Blood(from Hand of Blood)
  • CKY - As the Tables Turn
  • Comeback Kid - Wake the Dead (from Wake The Dead)
  • The Dead 60s - Riot Radio
  • Dogs - Tuned to a Different Station
  • The Doors - Break on Through (BT remix)
  • Emanuel - The Hey Man!
  • Fall Out Boy - Dance, Dance(From From Under the Cork Tree)
  • Finch - Ink (from Say Hello to Sunshine)
  • Funeral for a Friend - All the Rage
  • Goldfinger - I Want (from Disconnection Notice)
  • Animal Alpha - Bundy
  • Infusion - Better World (Adam Freeland Remix)
  • Junkie XL - Today
  • LCD Soundsystem - Daft Punk Is Playing at My House (Soulwax Shibuya Mix)
  • Maxïmo Park - Apply Some Pressure(from A Certain Trigger)
  • Mindless Self Indulgence - Straight to Video (KMFDM Remix)
  • Morningwood - Nu Rock
  • MxPx - Heard That Sound
  • Nine Black Alps - Shot Down (from Everything Is)
  • OK Go - Do What You Want (from Oh No)
  • Pennywise - Stand Up (from The Fuse)
  • The Black Velvets - Fear And Loathing (from The Black Velvets)
  • The Outline - Shotgun
  • The Starting Line - The World
  • Timo Maas - First Day (General Midi Remix)(from Pictures)
  • Tsar - Band-Girls-Money
  • Unwritten Law - F.I.G.H.T.(from Here's to the Mourning)
  • We Are Scientists - The Great Escape
  • The Chemical Brothers - The Big Jump(from Push The Button)
  • Thrice - Lullaby
  • Yellowcard - Lights and Sounds (from Lights and Sounds)
