Burgos is the name of a number of places:

  • Spain
    • Burgos – A province
    • Burgos – The capital of Burgos province

  • Philippines
    • Burgos, Ilocos Norte
    • Burgos, Ilocos Sur

It is also the name of various people:

  • Ambiorix Burgos (born 1984) – a Major League Baseball player
  • Carl Burgos (1918-1984) – an American comic book and advertising artist
  • Javier de Burgos (1778-1849) – a Spanish writer and politician
  • José Burgos (1837-1872) – a Spanish-Filipino priest
  • Germán Burgos (born 1969) – an Argintian football player
  • Renata Burgos (born 1982) – a Brazilian freestyle swimmer
