Bureau International de l'Edition Mecanique

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

BIEM (Bureau International des Sociétés Gérant les Droits d'Enregistrement et de Reproduction Mécanique) is an organisation coordinating statutory license agreements among different countries. BIEM is based in Neuilly-sur-Seine, France, and represents 44 societies, from 42 countries.
