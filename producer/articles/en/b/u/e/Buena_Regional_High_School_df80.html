Buena Regional High School

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Buena Regional High School is a comprehensive regional public high school serving approximately 1,000 students in grades K - 12 from two communities in Atlantic County, New Jersey, United States, as part of the Buena Regional School District. Municipalities served by the district are Buena Borough and Buena Vista Township.

Students from Newfield, a non-operating school district, along with students from both Estell Manor City and Weymouth Township, attend Buena Regional High School as part of sending/receiving relationships with the respective school districts.

As of the 2004-05 school year, the school had an enrollment of 975 students.[1]

[edit] Curriculum

The curriculum offerings include Advanced Placement Program (AP) courses in AP Calculus, AP English Literature and Composition, AP European History and AP United States History.[2]
