Buena High School is a comprehensive public high school in Ventura, California. It is part of the Ventura Unified School District and serves most of the eastern portion of Ventura. The western portation of Ventura is served by Ventura High School, and in between them is Foothill Technology High School with is concitered a "magnet school" and only holds about 925 students. Buena can hold about 2725 students and Ventura can hold about 3100 students.
