Buena Vista Ferry from the east landing

The Buena Vista Ferry connects Marion County and Polk County across the Willamette River in the U.S. state of Oregon. It is located a few miles south of Independence, near the former community of Buena Vista. The river is approximately 720 feet (220 m) wide at the crossing. The ferry has a capacity of four vehicles.
