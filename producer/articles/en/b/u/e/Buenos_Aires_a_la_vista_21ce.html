Buenos Aires a la vista (English language:) is a 1950 Argentine musical drama film directed and written by Luis Bayón Herrera with Carlos A. Petit. The film premiered in 20 September 1950 in Buenos Aires.

The film is a tango based film, which is an intergral part of Argentine culture.
