Buena Park (Metrolink station)

Buena Park station is a passenger railroad station that is currently under construction in Buena Park, California. Construction started in January 2006 and completion is scheduled for May 2007 (per City of Buena Park Engineering Department).

Once open, the station will serve trains on the Metrolink Orange County Line, with service north to Los Angeles-Union Station and south to Oceanside. The approximate location of the station is on Dale Street south of Malvern Avenue. There will be a 300-car parking lot and an overpass to allow passengers to cross from one side of the tracks to the other.
