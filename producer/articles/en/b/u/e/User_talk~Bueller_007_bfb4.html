[edit] Nanking Massacre and reference cleanup

My apologies. I've been trying to work through Category:Wikipedia references cleanup and reduce the number of articles in the category by cleaning up the references on them. A lot of the articles are in pretty bad condition--numbers in brackets which refer to a badly formatted bibliography; bracket references ([1]); etc. Reading through Nanking Massacre, I noticed that it had footnotes, a further list of references, and quite a few external links, thought it was a great article and that it didn't particularly need any cleanup. Obviously, though, content disputes can be pretty vicious, and I can see why you'd need something like refernece cleanup. I imagine {{unsourced}} would be pretty much giving anybody free reign to remove content... Perhaps a template along the lines of "This article has an extensive bibliography but no footnotes; please match entries in the bibliography to parts of the text and convert these bibliography entries to footnotes" should be created...

Anyway, I've gone and reinserted the {{citation style}} tag, and again, my apologies. Jude (talk,contribs,email) 22:57, 21 April 2006 (UTC)

[edit] Image copyright problem with Image:Hakapik.jpg

Thanks for uploading Image:Hakapik.jpg. However, the image may soon be deleted unless we can determine the copyright holder and copyright status. The Wikimedia Foundation is very careful about the images included in Wikipedia because of copyright law (see Wikipedia's Copyright policy).

The copyright holder is usually the creator, the creator's employer, or the last person who was transferred ownership rights. Copyright information on images is signified using copyright templates. The three basic license types on Wikipedia are open content, public domain, and fair use. Find the appropriate template in Wikipedia:Image copyright tags and place it on the image page like this: {{TemplateName}}.

Please signify the copyright information on any other images you have uploaded or will upload. Remember that images without this important information can be deleted by an administrator. If you have any questions, feel free to contact me, or ask them at the Media copyright questions page. Thank you. - CrazyRussian talk/contribs/email 18:11, 30 April 2006 (UTC)

Please provide an edit summary when you prod an article, as anyone with the article on the watchlist may miss the nomination. And if the title is wrong, you should consider just moving the article - it is not a argument for deletion. Uppland 11:54, 29 July 2006 (UTC)
