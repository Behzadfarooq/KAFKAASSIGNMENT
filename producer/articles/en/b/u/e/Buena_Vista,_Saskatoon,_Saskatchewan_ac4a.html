[edit] Buena Vista, Saskatoon, Saskatchewan

Buena Vista neighborhood is bounded to the north by 8th Street East, to the south by Taylor Avenue. The western boundary is the South Saskatchewan River, and the eastern boundary is Broadway Avenue.

[edit] Demographics

This neighborhood has an elderly population for the most part, the age is over 65, as well the housing has been built prior to 1945. The income range is between $30,000 and $60,000.

[edit] Area Parks

  • Beuna Vista Park 6.95 acres
  • Atchison Field (This is where the Saskatoon Hilltops Football Team practice)
  • Kilburn Park 5.88 acres
  • Gabriel Dumont Park on the South Saskatchewan Riverbank.

[edit] History

Enterpreuners, W.H. Coy, A. MacDougall and F.E. Guppy promoted the development of the area of Buena Vista about 1912. [4]

[edit] Layout

Roads are laid out in a grid fashion. Streets run east and west, Avenues run north and south.
