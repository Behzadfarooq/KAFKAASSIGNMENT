[edit] Disney

Buena Vista is frequently used as a name for divisions of The Walt Disney Company, including:

  • Buena Vista Distribution, a film distributor in United States
  • Buena Vista International, a film distributor
  • Buena Vista Interactive, a software company
  • Buena Vista Motion Pictures Group, Disney's film division
  • Buena Vista Music Group, Disney's music division
  • Buena Vista Television, Disney's television syndication unit
  • Lake Buena Vista, Florida, site of the Walt Disney World resort

[edit] Miscellaneous

Buena Vista may also refer to:

  • Battle of Buena Vista, a battle in the Mexican-American War fought in 1847 in Buena Vista, Coahuila, Mexico
  • Buena Vista Social Club, a members-only music club in Havana, Cuba that was at its height during the 1940s
    • Buena Vista Social Club (album), a 1997 album of Cuban musicians who played at the Buena Vista Social Club
    • Buena Vista Social Club (film), a 1999 documentary about the album and its musicians
  • Buena Vista (song), a song by Gomez from their 2000 compilation album Abandoned Shopping Trolley Hotline
