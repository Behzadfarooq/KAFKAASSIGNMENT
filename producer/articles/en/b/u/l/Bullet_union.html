[edit] History

Bullet Union were a rock/punk/indie band from Camden Town, London who were heavily influenced by Drive Like Jehu, Sonic Youth and Unwound. Formed in late 2002 by ex-members of Dead Inside and Ursa and current members of Comet Gain and Shoe!, they disbanded in 2006 after completing a headline tour of Europe.
They played over two hundred shows alongside various acts including; An Emergency, Girls Against Boys, Balls, Hot Snakes, Dan Sartain, Blood Red Shoes, Das Oath, This aint vegas, Melt Banana, Test-Icicles, Semifinalists, Trencher, Lungfish, Hoover (band), William Elliott Whitmore, Ten Grand,The Datsuns, These Arms are Snakes, Comanechi, PRE and Part Chimp. The band also toured the UK and Europe with The Blood Brothers, and the UK with Q and not U and Black Eyes.
