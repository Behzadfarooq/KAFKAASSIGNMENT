Buldak
Hangul:
불닭
Hanja:
none
Revised Romanization: Buldalk
McCune-Reischauer: Pultak

Buldak is a Korean dish made from heavily spiced chicken. The term "bul" translates into "fire" in Korean and "dak" means chicken.
