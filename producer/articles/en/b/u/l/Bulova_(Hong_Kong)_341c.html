Bulova (Hong Kong)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Bulova is a now defunct Hong Kong football club which were dissolved after the 1984-85 season.

They won the Hong Kong FA Cup in 1981-82 and 1982-83 and were runners-up in 1979-80.

They were also runners-up in the Hong Kong First Division League in 1982-83 and 1983-84.

[edit] Notable players

  • Phil Boyer 1982-1983
  • Charlie George 1981-1982
  • Terry Conroy 1979
  • Clive Haywood (希活)
  • Tommy Hutchison
  • Derek Parlane
  • Barry Daines[1]
