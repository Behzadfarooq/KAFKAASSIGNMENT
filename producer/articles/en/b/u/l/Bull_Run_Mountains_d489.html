The Bull Run Mountains are the easternmost mountain chain in the U.S. Commonwealth of Virginia, stretching approximately sixteen miles in Loudoun, Prince William and Fauquier counties from Broad Run to Aldie. They are home to the Bull Run Mountains Conservancy Area, and to residents within Bull Run Mountain Estates. Interstate 66 and the John Marshall Highway (Va. Rt. 55) pass through the mountain at Thoroughfare Gap where the battle of Thoroughfare Gap was fought prior to the battle of Second Manassas.
