Bullrush are a British all out guitar indie rock pop band from Mexborough, Yorkshire, England. Their songs are wonderfully commercial and melodious with fantastically catchy choruses, you singing away to yourself for days. Feel good hooks a plenty and melodies that you could stop traffic with. Here is a band who command an audience with ease and produce music catchier than anything coming out of commercial or alternative radio. The songs have never meant to challenge or be fashionable, they are and will always be some blokes making music out of chords that your parents would like with guitars like a steam engine fueled on amphetamines and bass that snarls like a tiger in a lunchbox.
