The Buller Rugby Football Union (BRFU) is a rugby province based in Westport, New Zealand.

[edit] History

Buller was formed in 1894.

[edit] Clubs

Buller Rugby Football Union is made up of 6 clubs:

  • Karamea
  • Ngakawau
  • Old Boys
  • Reefton
  • Westport
  • White Star

[edit] Representative Rugby

The Buller team play from Victoria Square, Westport and in the 2006 season are in Pool A of the Heartland Championship. They are seeded 7th for the Championship as they finished 3rd in the 3rd division in 2005.

[edit] Buller in Super Rugby

Buller along with Canterbury, Tasman, West Coast, Mid Canterbury and South Canterbury make up the Crusaders Super 14 franchise.

[edit] Championships

Buller has never a Provinical Championship

Buller have never held the Ranfurly Shield.
