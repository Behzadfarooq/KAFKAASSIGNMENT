Bulqizë District

The District of Bulqizë (Albanian: Rrethi i Bulqizës) is one of the thirty-six districts of Albania. It has a population of 43,000 (2004 estimate), and an area of 718 km². It is in the east of the country, and its capital is Bulqizë.
