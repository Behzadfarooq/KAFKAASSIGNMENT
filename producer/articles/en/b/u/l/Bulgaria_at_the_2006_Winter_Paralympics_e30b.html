Bulgaria at the 2006 Winter Paralympics

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Flag of Bulgaria

Bulgaria is participating in the ninth Winter Paralympics in Turin, Italy.

Bulgaria has entered three athletes in the following sports:

  • Nordic skiing: 2 male, 1 female
