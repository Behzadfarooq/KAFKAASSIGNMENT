[edit] Plot summary

Terrorists seize an armored vehicle. The task of recovering it is entrusted to an American secret service agent. Gary Busey plays the stereotypical die-hard main character this film. He does various stunts and moves as he attempts to recover an armored vechicle.

[edit] Tagline

"An unstoppable force is about to meet an unmovable object!"
