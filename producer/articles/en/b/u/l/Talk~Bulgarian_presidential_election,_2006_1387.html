[edit] Who did the right parties support

Some of the right-wing parties were disunited at the time but still chose to support a common candidate, Nedelcho Beronov

Who did they support (if anyone) in the second round? Nil Einne 16:56, 1 November 2006 (UTC)

Although Hans-Gert Pöttering advised them to support Parvanov, they didn't officially support anyone. Most of their sympathizers, however, voted for Parvanov. Todor→Bozhinov 17:40, 1 November 2006 (UTC)

[edit] List

Why is the list organised as it is? Was this the officially numbering on the ballot or whatever? It doesn't appear to be in alphabetical order but perhaps it is in Bulgarian Nil Einne 16:58, 1 November 2006 (UTC)
