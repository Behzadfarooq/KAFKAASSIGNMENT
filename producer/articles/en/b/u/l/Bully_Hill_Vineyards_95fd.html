Bully Hill Vineyards is a wine producer based in the state of New York, United States.

[edit] History

Bully Hill Farms was started by Greyton H. Taylor and Walter S. Taylor in 1958. Walter S. Taylor represents the fourth generation of his family to be involved in grape growing and wine making. The family has been either growing grapes or producing wine since 1878.

Bully Hill Vineyards was the first small estate winery in the Keuka Lake area since the prohibition and became the cornerstone for the growth of many wineries. To date, over 11 wineries grace the Keuka Lake region.

[edit] Facilities and distribution

Growing from a small winery, with a limited number of employees, Bully Hill Vineyards now produces over 200,000 cases of wine each year. Today, Bully Hill Vineyards offer an onsite restauraunt, wine shop, two gift shops and a museum. Bully Hill offers tastings and tours year round, weather permitting. Currently Bully Hill is distributed in 30 states across the United States.
