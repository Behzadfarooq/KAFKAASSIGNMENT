Bullets in a Burning Box (born N. Matthias, Dec. 11, 1979 in Southern Oregon) is a USA-based industrial-rock artist.

A vagabond, N. Matthias wanders with a guitar, microphone and laptop strapped to his back. Most Bullets songs are written, recorded and self-produced on the road.

[edit] History

Formed in 2001, the Bullets sound mushroomed in the mountains of southern Oregon, the beaches of San Diego and the forest-jungle of Seattle.

Prior to 2001, Bullets lived in a cocoon of nu metal, progressive rock, industrial and hipohop. The influence of bands like Nine Inch Nails, Tool and Rage Against the Machine are apparent in Bullets' singing, guitar-playing and compositions.

Fear, Courage, Fulfillment
(2006)

Fear, Courage, Fulfillment, released in 2006, is Bullets' first full-length album. Five years in the making, the album contains nine tracks:

Fear, Courage, Fulfillment was partly recorded in the back of N. Matthias' car.
