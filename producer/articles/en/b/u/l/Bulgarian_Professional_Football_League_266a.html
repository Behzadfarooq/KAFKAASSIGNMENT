Bulgarian Professional Football League

The Bulgarian Professional Football League (Българска професионална футболна лига) or BPFL (БПФЛ) organizes the Bulgarian A Professional Football Group, Bulgarian B Professional Football Group and the Bulgarian Cup tournaments. It unifies the Bulgarian professional football clubs. The league's current president is Valentin Mihov and it is sponsored by TBI, a leasing company.
