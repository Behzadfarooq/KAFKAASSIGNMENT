Duck Butter is an American record label owned and operated by Tim Biskup and brother Mike Biskup.

[edit] History

Duck Butter was formed in 1986 by Tim Biskup and Mark Biskup in Clovis,California. The first recording released was “big butter EP (promo copy)” in 1988.
