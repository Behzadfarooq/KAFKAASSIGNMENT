Bulgartabac Holding Group AD or simply Bulgartabac (Bulgarian: Булгартабак) is a Bulgarian tobacco holding company and one of the leading tobacco products producers in Central and Eastern Europe, as well as a major European cigarette producer. Established in 1947 and based in the capital Sofia, it includes 22 joint-stock subsidiary companies in the tobacco-growing regions of the country.

Bulgartabac produces over 50 cigarette brands and subbrands in its modernized factories in Sofia, Plovdiv, Blagoevgrad and Stara Zagora, using both domestic and imported tobacco. Amongs the company's brands are Victory, Femina, Prestige, Slims, Bulgartabac, MM, Seven Hills, Global, Bridge, Tresor, BT, Charlie, Nevada, Orient Express, Country, etc.
