The correct title of this article is bulia byak. The initial letter is shown capitalized due to technical restrictions.

bulia byak [sic] is the lead developer of Inkscape, and most influential in the design of its user interface.

Known only by his pseudonym, the Russian expatriate is said to reside in Canada with his family, finding employment as a graphic designer and the operator of a small press.

The name is a pun in Russian.
