Bulldog Mansion (Korean: 불독맨션) is a Korean pop rock band. Their name does not translate to "Bulldog Mansion": rather, it is a phonetic approximation in Hangul, with the English name being used for their website and album art. They are most well-known in the English-speaking world for being featured in the SamBakZa Flash cartoon "There she is!! Step 2 -- Cake Dance".
