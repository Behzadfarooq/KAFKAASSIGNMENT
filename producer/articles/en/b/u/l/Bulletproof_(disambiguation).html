Bulletproof (disambiguation)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Look up bulletproof in Wiktionary, the free dictionary.

Bulletproof may refer to:

  • Bulletproof, material, or substance that is capable of resisting or stopping a bullet or high velocity missile
    • Bulletproof glass, usually transparent material that offers varying degrees of protection from small arms fire
    • Bulletproof vest, an article of protective clothing that works as a form of armour to minimize injury from projectiles
  • Bulletproof (reliability). An object or process which is extremely reliable and/or error-tolerant.
  • Bulletproof, a 1996 movie directed by Ernest R. Dickerson, starring Adam Sandler and Damon Wayans.
  • Bulletproof, a 1988 movie directed by Steve Carver
  • BulletProof Music, a record label

Bulletproof may also refer to:

  • 50 Cent: Bulletproof, a video game starring various hip-hop artists
  • Bulletproof, a mixtape made by G-Unit.
  • Bulletproof hosting is a euphemism for Internet hosting services that permit users to send spam e-mail.
  • Bulletproof Monk, a 2003 motion picture
  • Bulletproof Wallets, album
  • "Bulletproof (Hush album)" an album by Hush (rapper)
  • "Bulletproof," a 1972 album by Hard Stuff.
  • "Bullet Proof," a song by Morcheeba from their 1998 album Big Calm
  • Bullet Proof, a 1920 film starring Harry Carey
