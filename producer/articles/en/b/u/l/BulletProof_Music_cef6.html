BulletProof Music (also known as Bulletboy Music) was a Christian independent record label founded in 1997. The label was owned by gray dot, inc., alongside gray dot records. Bulletproof focused exclusively on Christian markets, and was distributed by Diamante Distribution. The label cited Ephesians 6:11-15, known as the Armor of God, as an explanation for its name.
