Bully and Billy is the third episode of the America science fiction television series Voyagers!. It was written by B.W. Sandefur and directed by Virgil Vogel and first aired on on October 24, 1982.

[edit] Plot

The Voyagers end up in Cuba in 1898 during the Spanish-American War but according to a local, Rita, the Spanish are winning. This is because Theodore Roosevelt and the Rough Riders are not there to help the revolutionaries since Billy the Kid killed Roosevelt 18 years ago. They first end up meeting Benjamin Franklin and help him with his kite flying electricity experiment in Pennsylvania, 1752. They then end up in a pit of quicksand in 1880 and Billy the Kid introduces himself as William Bonney and pulls them out with rope. They team up with Billy's gang and prevent the future US president from being shot during a stagecoach robbery but the 22 year old headstrong ”kid from New York“, tries to rally a posse to stop Billy and his outlaws. This, plus the fact that Roosevelt slapped Billy, cause him to challenge Roosevelt to a gunfight. Jeff & Bogg both know Billy is a much better shot and a final showdown has Billy attempt to shoot Bogg in the back when he intervenes. Jeff's glorification of the outlaw pushes Bogg to his limits until Jeff realizes that Billy is just a criminal and not a legend. {It is never said where they are in the Old West but it was probably New Mexico since Billy was known to be involved in the Lincoln County War in Lincoln County, New Mexico in 1880.}
