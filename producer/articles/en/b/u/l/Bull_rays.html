The genus of bull rays (Pteromylaeus) belongs to the family of eagle rays Myliobatidae. Currently, four species are recognized.

[edit] Species

  • Pteromylaeus episcopus (Valenciennes, 1844)
  • Rough eagle ray, Pteromylaeus asperrimus (Gilbert, 1898)
  • Pteromylaeus bonaparti (Duméril, 1865)
  • Bull ray, Pteromylaeus bovinus (Geoffroy Saint-Hilaire, 1817)
