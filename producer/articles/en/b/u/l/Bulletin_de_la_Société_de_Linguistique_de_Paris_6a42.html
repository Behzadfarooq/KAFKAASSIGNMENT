Bulletin de la Société de Linguistique de Paris

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Société de Linguistique de Paris publishes the Bulletin de la Société de Linguistique de Paris. The journal contains two separate volumes: one presenting articles on all linguistic domains (comparing grammatics, typology, diachronic and synchronic linguistics, theoretic and descriptive linguistics, history of linguistics) and on all language families, none excluded; The second volume is dedicated to the review of books recently appeared in the linguistic research field.
