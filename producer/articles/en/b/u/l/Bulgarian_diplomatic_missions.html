[edit] International organizations

  • Council of Europe, Strasbourg — Ivan Petkov
  • European Community (European Union and others), Brussels — Stanislav Daskalov
  • NATO, Brussels — Lyubomir Ivanov
  • United Nations, New York City — Stefan Tafrov
  • UN and other organizations, Geneva — Petko Draganov
  • UN, OSCE and other organizations, Vienna — Chavdar Zhechev
  • UNESCO, Paris — Irina Bokova
  • World Trade Organization — Dencho Georgiev
