Bullet Train to Vegas

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Bullet Train To Vegas is a Southern California-based rock band. The band put out their debut EP Profile This in 2003, then a self-titled two-song 7" in 2004. They signed with Nitro Records the following year and released their debut full-length, We Put Scissors Where Our Mouths Are, in 2005. They named themselves after a song by Drive Like Jehu, but have been criticized for sounding too derivative of At the Drive-In.
