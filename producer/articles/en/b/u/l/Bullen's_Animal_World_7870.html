Bullen's Animal World

Bullen's Animal World was a circus style theme park located at Wallacia on the outskirts of Sydney. Its exact address was 11 Park Rd, Wallacia.

It was opened in 1969 by Stafford Bullen, the son of circus founders, and operated until 1985. Its closure was to foreshadow the closure of Bullen's other nearby venture, the African Lion Safari, in 1991.
