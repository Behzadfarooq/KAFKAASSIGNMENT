Bulletproof Heart is an album by Grace Jones. It was released in 1989, making it the last studio album by Grace Jones to date. "Dream" and "Don't Cry Freedom" are CD-only tracks, not included on the vinyl release. EMI Gold released a special edition with bonus tracks in 2004, and with a slighty different track listing. The singles were "Love On Top Of Love" and "Amado Mio". "Love on Top of Love" became a Top 10 Hot Dance Music/Club Play chart hit for her. The song was produced by C + C Music Factory.
