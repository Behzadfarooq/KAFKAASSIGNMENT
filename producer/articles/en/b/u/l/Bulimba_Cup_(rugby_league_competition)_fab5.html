Bulimba Cup (rugby league competition)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Bulimba Cup was a representative rugby league competition held between Brisbane, Ipswich and Toowoomba. It was first contested in 1925 but was cancelled in 1972 due to waning public interest.

In 2005 the Queensland Rugby League issued a commemorative jersey to celebrate the 80th anniversary of this competition. [1]
