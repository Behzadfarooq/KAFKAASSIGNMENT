Bullet (disambiguation)

Bullet may refer to:

  • bullet, a metal projectile used in a gun
  • bullet (typography), a solid typographic symbol. It is called a bullet point in Britain
  • Bullet is a municipality in the district of Grandson of the Canton of Vaud, Switzerland.
  • A "bullet train" is an aerodynamically-designed rail vehicle built with speed in mind, known as Shinkansen
  • Bullet (comic), a British comic published during the 1970s
  • "Bullet" is the name of a superhero in the game Freedom Force
  • "Bullet", was the name of a dog belonging to the singer and actor Roy Rogers
in sport

The word bullet occurs in the names of many sports teams and in nicknames of individuals, typically referring to their speed in their chosen event:

  • The Brisbane Bullets, a professional basketball team competing in Australia's National Basketball League
  • The Washington Wizards were formerly known as "The Bullets"
  • "Bullet" Bob Armstrong (also known simply as "The Bullet") is a professional wrestler
  • A "bullet" can refer to a first place win in Yacht racing
  • Buckeye Bullet, an electric land speed vehicle designed and created by students at The Ohio State University
  • A low and fast American Football pass is referred to as a bullet pass.
in music
  • On music charts, a "bullet" indicates if a song has increased in points or whatever criterion used to measure the chart.
  • Bullet (album), the debut EP by punk rock band The Misfits
  • Bullet (band), a band who had a hit single in 1972 with "White Lies, Blue Eyes"
  • Bullet (band), an up and coming Swedish heavy metal band.
  • Bullets (song), a song by British indie band Editors, on the album The Back Room
  • Bullets (song), a song by the American band Creed, on the album Weathered
  • A common shortened name for Welsh metalcore band Bullet for My Valentine
  • Bullet Records, a record label
in film
  • Bullet, a 1996 film starring Tupac Shakur and Mickey Rourke
  • Bullitt (pronounced identically), a 1968 action film starring Steve McQueen, Robert Vaughn, and Jacqueline Bisset
  • Bullet is a nickname for a common spring-type clothes-pin (C47) used as a utility tool in filmmaking.
in commerce

Bullet is a fairly common name for many different commercial products, including:

  • A range of motorcycles from Royal Enfield (India)
  • A mountain biking shoe
  • An Oakley wristwatch
  • A Fender electric guitar or bass
in computer software
  • Bullet is an open sourced physics engine.
