Bulford

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Bulford (grid reference SU175435) is a village and civil parish in the Salisbury district of Wiltshire, England, close to Salisbury Plain. According to the 2001 census the parish had a population of 4,698.

The village is separated from the site of Bulford Camp, an army base, and is close to Durrington, Amesbury and Larkhill.
