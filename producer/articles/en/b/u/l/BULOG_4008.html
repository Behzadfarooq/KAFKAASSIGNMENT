BULOG

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Bureau of Logistics (Indonesian: Badan Urusan Logistik/BULOG) is a government-owned company in Indonesia which deals with food distribution and price control.

[edit] Chairman

This is the list of chairmen of BULOG:

  • Bustanil Arifin (?)
  • Beddu Amang ( - 26 August 1998)
  • Rahardi Ramelan (27 August 1998 - October 1999)
  • Jusuf Kalla (October 1999 - March 2000)
  • Rizal Ramli (March 2000 - 19 February 2001)
  • Widjanarko Puspoyo (19 February 2001 - incumbent)
