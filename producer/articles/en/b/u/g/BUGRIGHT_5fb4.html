BUGRIGHT is the followup to UVERworld's first album, Timeless. A Limited Edition CD+DVD was also released. It contains the music videos for their 4th to 6th singles and also a documentary. The album managed to hit #1 at the Oricon daily chart on 23rd Feb, and TAKUYA∞ shed the tears of joy after hearing the news.
