Buglist is an open source Eclipse plugin for viewing Bugzilla bug entries. It allows programmers/developers to run and view the Bugzilla saved searches stored in their account without leaving their integrated development environment.

Buglist requires no custom modifications to the Bugzilla instance being used, nor special backend access to the Bugzilla database.
