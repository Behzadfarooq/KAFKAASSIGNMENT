Bugs Crawling Out of People is a Canadian industrial independent record label created in 2004 by Toronto DJ and Spoken Wordcore artist Squid.

[edit] Discography

  • bcp001 v/a - revelation (2004)
  • bcp002 Dead Man's Hill - Lakes of Sacrifice (2006)
  • bcp002.1 Dead Man's Hill - Lakes of Sacrifice bonus disc (limited to 50 copies) (2006)
  • bcp003 It-clings vs Pneumatic Detach - The All Too Logical Descent into Madness (release tba)
