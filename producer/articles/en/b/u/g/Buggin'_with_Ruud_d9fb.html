Buggin' with Ruud is a television show of 13 x 60min episodes that follow the adventures of Dutch-New Zealander Ruud Kleinpaste, an insect expert. The series is broadcast in the United States on Animal Planet and, made by a small team in New Zealand at NHNZ Ltd.
