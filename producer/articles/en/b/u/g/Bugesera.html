Bugesera is one of seven districts in the new East Province, Rwanda.

It is comprised of areas south of Kigali, which were formerly in the Kigali Ngali province, around the town of Nyamata. The area is prone to droughts, and has been earmarked as the location for a possible new international airport to serve Kigali, 40km away.
