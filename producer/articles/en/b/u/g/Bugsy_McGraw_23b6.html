Bugsy McGraw

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Bugsy McGraw is the ring name of former professional wrestler Michael Davis. He is not to be confused with the other wrestler named Mike Davis.

[edit] Championships and accomplishments

[edit] National Wrestling Alliance

  • National
  • NWA American Heavyweight Championship (1 time)
  • NWA American Tag Team Championship (1 time) - with King Kong Bundy
  • NWA Canadian Tag Team Championship (Vancouver version) (2 times)
  • NWA United States Heavyweight Championship (San Francisco version) (1 time)
  • Regional
  • NWA Alabama Heavyweight Championship (1 time)
  • NWA Florida Brass Knuckles Championship (1 time)
  • NWA Florida Heavyweight Championship (1 time)
  • NWA Florida Tag Team Championship (1 time) - with Thor The Viking
  • NWA Florida Television Championship (3 times)
  • NWA Mid-Atlantic Tag Team Championship (1 time) - with Rufus R. Jones
  • NWA Pacific Coast Heavyweight Championship (Vancouver version) (1 time)

[edit] World Class Championship Wrestling

  • WCCW Television Championship (1 time)
  • WCCW Texas Brass Knuckles Championship (1 time)

[edit] World Wrestling Association

  • WWA World Tag Team Championship (1 time)

[edit] Other Titles

  • (Montreal) International Tag Team Championship (1 time)
  • SWF Tag Team Championship (1 time)
