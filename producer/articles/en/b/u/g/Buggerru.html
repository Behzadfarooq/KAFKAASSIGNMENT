Location of Buggerru in Italy

Buggerru is a comune (municipality) in the Province of Carbonia-Iglesias in the Italian region Sardinia, located about 70 km northwest of Cagliari and about 30 km northwest of Carbonia. As of 31 December 2004, it had a population of 1,124 and an area of 48.3 km².[1]

Buggerru borders the following municipalities: Fluminimaggiore, Iglesias.
