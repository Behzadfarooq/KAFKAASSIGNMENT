Bugz in the Attic

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Bugz in the Attic is a collective of DJs and producers based in West London who are prominent in the broken beat music scene. The collective includes Orin Walters (Afronaught), Paul Dolby (Seiji), Kaidi Tatham, Daz-I-Kue, Alex Phountzi, Cliff Scott, Mark Force, Matt Lord and Mikey Stirton. The group also boasts a label, BitaSweet Records and a studio, The BitaSuite. The group also runs its own Co-Op club night at Plastic People in Shoreditch, London. They have released hundreds of singles as well as remixes & compilations. Their debut studio album Back in the Doghouse was released 24th July 2006 on V2 Records.

Their single Don't Stop The Music hit #76 in the national UK chart and number 1 on the UK dance chart in 2006.
