Bug is a 2002 comedy genre film, directed by Phil Hay and Matt Manfredi, released on 28 February 2002.

[edit] Synopsis

An eclectic group of individuals in Silverlake, LA, are propelled by a series of cause-and-effect chain reactions to a common destiny.
