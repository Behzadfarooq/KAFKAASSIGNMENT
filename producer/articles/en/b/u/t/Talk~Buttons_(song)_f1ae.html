[edit] Writers

Currently this article lists Nicole Scherzinger as one of 4 co-writers of this song, however http://www.bmi.com does not list her. BMI has an official register of songwriters & music publishers, for the purpose of distributing royalties, with a searchable database. Unless there is another citable reference which includes Scherzinger as co-writer, then her name should be deleted. Design 12:00, 15 June 2006 (UTC)
