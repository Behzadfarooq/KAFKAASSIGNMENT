Butiama

Butiama is the birthplace of Julius K. Nyerere, the first president of Tanzania. It is one of the wards of the Musoma Rural District within the Mara Region.

Several things have taken the moniker Butiama because of its significance to Tanzanian history, such as the MV Butiama which is a ship that travels from Mwanza to Ukerewe and back.

According to the 2002 Tanzania National Census, the population of Butiama was 15,383.
