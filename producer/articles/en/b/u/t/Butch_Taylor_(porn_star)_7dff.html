Butch Taylor (born Boise, Idaho) was an American pornographic actor who appeared in gay and bi-sexual pornographic movies and magazines.

Taylor was a tanned and muscular blond man who performed in the bottom role. He was at one time romantically linked to fellow porn star Brad Phillips.

In 1989, he won the "Best Sex Scene" award from the "Dave" Awards for his scene with George Madera in Undercover (John Travis, director). The "Dave" Awards were awarded by Advocate Men Magazine columnist Dave Kinnick and the staff of Adam Gay Video Directory.

He retired from the porn industry and was reported to be working as a bartender and personal trainer in Boise and San Diego.
