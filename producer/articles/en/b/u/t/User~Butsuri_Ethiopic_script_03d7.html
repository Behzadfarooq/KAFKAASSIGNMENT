[edit] Ethiopic in Unicode

The "Ethiopic" range was assigned codepoints U+1200 – U+137F (decimal 4608–4991) in Unicode 3.0. The ranges "Ethiopic Supplement" U+1380 - U+ 139F (decimal 4992-5023 ) and "Ethiopic Extended" U+2D80 - U+2DDF (decimal 11648-11743) were added in Unicode 4.1:
