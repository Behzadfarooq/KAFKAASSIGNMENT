Butterflies is an episode of Malcolm in the Middle. It originally aired on April 10th, 2005 as the 17th episode of the sixth season.

[edit] Plot

Reese starts to raise butterflies despite Lois's orders to get rid of them. Meanwhile, Malcolm discovers a man named Norm hidden in the Lucky Aide who's been living there for 3 years, which shocks Malcolm. So Norm asks Malcolm to keep it a secret and in return he will give Malcolm information on the new girl at the Lucky Aide whom Malcolm likes.
