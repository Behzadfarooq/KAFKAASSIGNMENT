[edit] Live albums

Live PCPPEP
Label: Alternative Tentacles
Released: September 20, 1984
Other information: The band's first live release. Reissued on Latino Buggerveil in 2003 with the Butthole Surfers EP and four bonus tracks.
Double Live
Label: Latino Buggerveil
Released: June 1989
Other information: An independently-released live double album. Initially limited to 10,000 LPs, 7,500 cassettes and 4,750 CDs, although the album is freely available for download in MP3 form on the band's official website.

[edit] Bootlegs

Along with these albums, there are countless bootlegs floating around with songs never released on albums, rare versions of songs, and alternate versions of songs. Some of these bootlegs come in various forms of media. These include The Waco Kid, Soiled, The Peel Sessions, Chewin George Lucas' Chocolate, From the Lips Of Death, and The Texas Chainsaw Massacre, among others.
