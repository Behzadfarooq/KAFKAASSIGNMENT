The image appears replaceable to me. The {{replaceable fair use disputed}} template explains why there probably aren't any free images, but not why it's impossible to create one. ShadowHalo 04:37, 13 March 2007 (UTC)

Respectfully, I disagree. The only way to create a free image would be to attend a live Butthole Surfers performance and personally take the picture. However, the Butthole Surfers have not toured since shortly after the release of 2001's After the Astronaut, and lead singer Gibby Haynes indicated in a 2004 interview that he did not think the band would tour again unless there was a new album to promote. There is rumored to be a new Butthole Surfers album coming out this year, but nothing is definite yet. If the album is released, and the band does tour in support of it, it may be possible to create a free picture at that time. Until then (assuming the if's come to pass), there is no other free option, barring a free image donation on behalf of a fan or band member. NOTE: Have added this rationale to the {{replaceable fair use disputed}} template. Thehaikumaster 03:40, 15 March 2007 (UTC)
