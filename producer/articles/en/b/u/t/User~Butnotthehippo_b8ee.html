Bio and Interests

New editor trying to learn the ropes. I accidentally did a cannonball into this swimming pool called Wikipedia and now I am slowly learning the finer points like the backstroke. I have absolutely no interest in swimming. I am from South Florida and love it here. I also like to travel. I drive a Pontiac Grand Am GT. I enjoy camping in various state parks around Florida.

Butnotthehippo comes from a popular children's book published by renowned author, humorist, and illustrator Sandra Boynton. It seems to be the most profound children's book ever written. I enjoy watching TV and eating steak. I collect hippopotamuses. I am interested in trivia and know a little about everything. I also know a lot about some things. I am not a big fan of comedy, but Demetri Martin is really funny.
