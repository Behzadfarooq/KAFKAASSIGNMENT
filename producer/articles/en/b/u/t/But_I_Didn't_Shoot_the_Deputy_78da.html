"But I Didn't Shoot the Deputy" is the third episode of season one of the television sitcom Married... with Children. The title is a reference to the song "I Shot the Sheriff".

  • First Aired: Sunday April 19, 1987 on FOX.

[edit] Plot

Spoiler warning: Plot and/or ending details follow.

After a number of break in's around the Neighbourhood Al gets a Gun for Protection while Marcy and Steve prefer the more Humane way of protection and get a Guard Dog. But when mistaken for a Burgular Al shoots the dog and has to explain it to Marcy and Steve who are less then happy with Al.

[edit] Quotes

  • [Al to Peggy] Peg, stay at the top of the stairs. If anything happens to me get kids and lock yourself in a room. And if Kelly gets off the phone, call the cops.
