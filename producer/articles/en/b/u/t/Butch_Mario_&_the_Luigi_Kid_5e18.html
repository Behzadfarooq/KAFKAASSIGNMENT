"Butch Mario & the Luigi Kid" is the third animated episode of The Super Mario Bros. Super Show!.

[edit] Synopsis

Spoiler warning: Plot and/or ending details follow.

Kid Koopa had kidnapped Princess Peach and hidden her in a mine in the wild west. Mario, Luigi and Toad had gone after her when they stumble across a cactus with a note stuck to it. It said that Butch Mario and the Luigi Kid were outlaws and are wanted dead or alive with a reward of 10,000 gold pieces. Luigi says they should turn back because of the dangerous outlaws until Toad realizes that the Mario Bros. are the outlaws. Suddenly, the cactus turns alive (it is a Pokey) as soon as Mario reads the note, and decides to apprehend the trio. Mario sticks a plunger into the Pokey's head. Afterwards, the trio encounters Mouser, posing as the local sheriff. The Mario Bros. and Toad quickly run away with Mouser's squad on Snifits giving chase. When they encounter a cliff with a waterfall, Mario decides to jump across the logs floating in the river to get away from Mouser. The Snifits try to do the same, but fail and drown in the river.

Later, in a western town, Kid Koopa yells at Mouser for losing them. In response to being threatened by Kid Koopa, Mouser commands all the bounty hunters in the territory to come after them. Disguised and riding Ostros, Mario, Luigi, and Toad soon find out that Mouser has tripled the reward to 30,000 gold pieces (Toad admits even he'd consider turning the Marios in for this reward). A Tweeter hears Toad, and goes to inform the rest. The gang then sees a smoke signal coming from the mine, reading "HELP ME", and a signal resembling the Princess. Just when they arrive at the mine, Mouser and his squad, having been informed by the Tweeter, trap the gang. They escape, and Mouser blows up a nearby boulder, causing it to spiral downhill, chasing the Mario Bros. to the town. They take refuge in an Italian diner, were it is revealed to be a trap by Kid Koopa. He locks them up on a jailcell. By claiming they are dying of thirst, they trick Mouser long enough for Toad to sneak up inside his hat and escape.

When outside, Toad picks up a couple of Bob-Ombs, which he uses to blow up the bar Mouser is in and to free Mario and Luigi from the jailhouse. Mouser, who survived the explosion, finds them escaping, and chases them again. When they reach the mine, they try to break the wall by exploding it with a bomb, but Luigi gets trapped. When Mario gets him out, they find Kid Koopa outside waiting for them with the whole squad. Mario and Bowser get in an old-fashioned western showdown. Kid Koopa cheats and fires early, but he misses. Mario takes the advantage and starts shooting wildly at the squad. After the smoke clears, they run to find Peach. Kid Koopa comes in to blow up the place with a Bob-Omb, but the Mario Bros. start plumbing so they direct the water from the mine toward Kid Koopa, throwing them out of the cave with a big bang from the bombs. Princess Peach asks Mario if there is any way to repay them, and Mario proudly asks for all the kinds of pasta there are and garlic ice cream.

[edit] Trivia

  • Mario claims that the only time that plumbers sleep on the job is when they are working by the hour.
  • Kid Koopa makes reference of having a grandfather named "Poppa la Koopa".

[edit] Live-Action Segment: "All Steamed Up"

Sgt. Slaughter criticizes Luigi about the Steam-O-Matic.

[edit] Synopsis

Mario and Luigi are fixing Sgt. Slaughter's Steam-O-Matic, when the sergeant tells them he is on his way. Worried, Mario goes inside the steam machine to see if it's good enough. But he gets stuck inside.

When the sergeant gets to the shop, he hears a thumping sound inside the machine and commands Luigi to fix it. However, he does not know what the sound is. After much criticism of Luigi, the sergeant goes outside to fix his double-parked tank. Luigi opens the doors and sees Mario inside, but shrunk down by the machine. He wants revenge on the sergeant.
