Butch Henry

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Butch Henry (born October 7, 1968 in El Paso, Texas), is a retired professional baseball player who pitched in the Major Leagues from 1992-1999.

[edit] Teams

  • Houston Astros 1992
  • Colorado Rockies 1993
  • Montreal Expos 1993-95
  • Boston Red Sox 1997-98
  • Seattle Mariners 1999

[edit] Pitching stats

  • 148 Games
  • 33 Wins
  • 33 Losses
  • 345 Strikeouts
  • 3.83 ERA
