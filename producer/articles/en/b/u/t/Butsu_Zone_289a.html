Butsu Zone (仏ゾーン Butsu Zōn) is a manga by Hiroyuki Takei, serialized in 3 tankobon.

The series highlights the adventures of Senju, an emissary of Avalokitesvara Bodhisattva, who has to find and protect an incarnation of the Buddha Miroku. The manga hints that the savior Senju and company are seeking to find and protect may be the Shaman King.

The Butsu Zone character Anna Kyōyama also appeared in Itako no Anna, Funbari no Uta and Shaman King.
