Button (game)

Button is an early 1970s text-based computer game for BASIC designed by People's Computer Company based on the popular children's game "Button, Button, Who's Got the Button?." The game appeared on page 13 of the book What to Do After You Hit Return or P.C.C.'s First Book of Computer Games.
