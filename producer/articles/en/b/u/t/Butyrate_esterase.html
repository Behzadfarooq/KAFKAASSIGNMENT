Butyrate esterase

Butyrate Esterase is a stain which is specific for white blood cells of the Monocytic proliferation line. It is commonly used in the diagnosis of leukemia when staining touch preparation type slides of bone marrow. It is instrumental in the diagnosis ofmonocytic leukemias and myelomonocytic leukemia (type M4).
