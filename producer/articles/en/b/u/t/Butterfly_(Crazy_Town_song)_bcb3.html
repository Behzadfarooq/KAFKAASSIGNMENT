"Butterfly" is a pop song by Crazy Town from their album The Gift of Game that was released in April 2001.

The instrumental part samples the bassline from the Red Hot Chili Peppers' song "Pretty Little Ditty", with additional lyrics by Shifty Shellshock on top.

[edit] Accolades

  • Topped the charts in Australia, Germany, and the U.S. Hot 100, while reaching number three on the Canadian Singles Chart and UK Singles Chart
  • Performed at MTV's Fashionably Loud party in Cancun, Mexico during Spring Break in 2001.
  • Featured in the 2002 feature movie Orange County, and appeared on the soundtrack.
  • Covered by Richard Cheese and Lounge Against the Machine on their 2004 album I'd Like a Virgin.
  • Named the 34th "Most Awesomely Bad Song Ever" by VH1[1].
