[edit] Summary

This image is used by 20th Century Fox Home Entertainment to promote the television series, Buffy the Vampire Slayer.

It features Sarah Michelle Gellar as 'Buffy Summers' and Charisma Carpenter as 'Cordelia Chase.'

[edit] Source Information

This image was taken from the 20th Century Fox Home Entertainment Buffy website [1], found under season one > cast > Cordelia.

The television series is owned by 20th Century Fox, Mutant Enemy Inc., The WB Network, and UPN.
