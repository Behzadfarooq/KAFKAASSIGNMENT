[edit] Overview

The chronology may also be used to see how Buffy and Angel episodes can be viewed in order so that crossovers and references function correctly. All the stories considered 'canon' are bold. Many of the items on this list are not considered canon. For a canon chronology, see here. Each story listed belongs on one of the following categories:

  • Buffy television episodes - 144 shows first broadcast in 1997-2003.
  • Angel television episodes - 110 shows first broadcast in 1999-2004.
  • Buffy novels - Published from 1998-present by Pocket Books
  • Angel novels - Published from 2000-2004 by Pocket Books
  • Buffy/Angel novels - Published from 2001-2004 by Pocket Books
  • Tales of the Slayer - Four volumes of prose short stories about mythology of past slayers
  • Buffy comics - Dark Horse published from 1998-2004. Many of the comics were collected into trade paperbacks
  • Angel comics - Dark Horse published from 1998-2004. IDW are currently publishing.
  • Tales of the Vampires - Trade paperback stories. An anthology of short stories about various vampires written by Joss Whedon, and other writers of the Buffy/Angel TV shows.
  • Tales of the Slayers - Trade paperback stories. An anthology of short stories about various slayers written by Joss Whedon, and other writers of the Buffy/Angel TV shows.
  • Buffy video games. Four official Buffy video games have been released.
