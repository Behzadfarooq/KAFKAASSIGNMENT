The Buffalo Bulls Basketball team is the basketball team that represent the University of Buffalo in Buffalo, New York. The school's team currently competes in the Mid-American Conference. The team has never played in the NCAA Division I Men's Basketball Tournament. The Bulls are currently coached by Reggie Witherspoon.
