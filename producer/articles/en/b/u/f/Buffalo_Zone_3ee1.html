The cover of Buffalo Zone was an obvious takeoff of The Byrds' album where Sweethearts of the Rodeo got their name, but it did not do as well as their first two albums. There are some great songs, however, in "Uphill All the Way," "This Heart" (Billboard #25), "Hard Road to Go," and "Como Se Dice (I Love You)," although none charted very high.
