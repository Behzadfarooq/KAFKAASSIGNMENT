Buffalo Bill's is a hotel and casino located in Primm, Nevada, near the California-Nevada stateline. It has 1,242 guest rooms and suites. The hotel is home to the Desperado roller coaster, one of the tallest (225 foot drop) and fastest (95 mph) roller coasters in the world, as well as a pool in the shape of a buffalo.

The 46,000 square foot casino has over 1,700 slot machines, as well as table games, poker, and a race and sports book. Buffalo Bill's Resort and Casino is owned by MGM Mirage.
