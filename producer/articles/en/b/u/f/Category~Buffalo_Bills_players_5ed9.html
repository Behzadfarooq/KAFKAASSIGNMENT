This category is all NFL players who played for the Buffalo Bills. There are separate categories for each era of the franchise:

  • Category:Buffalo Bills (AFL) players (1960-1969)
  • Category:Buffalo Bills players (1970–)

Players should be included in each category that applies. Note, there are also separate categories for the unrelated AAFC franchise of the same name:

  • Category:Buffalo Bisons (AAFC) players (1946)
  • Category:Buffalo Bills (AAFC) players (1947–1949)
