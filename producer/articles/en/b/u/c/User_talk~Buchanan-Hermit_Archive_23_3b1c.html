Yan Stastny is Canadian

Yes, Yan Stastny does play for Team USA. But the flag is to show what nationality he is, not for who he plays for. He is from Quebec. He has to play for USA because he was raised in St. Louis and can not play for Canada because he didn't live or play there long enough. Please keep it as shown. Cheers. —The preceding unsigned comment was added by Dotcanada (talk • contribs) 19:58, 5 January 2007 (UTC).

See: [1] and [2]. Particularly item II.B.5: Dual Citizenship. Basically, he has both Canadian and U.S. citizenship and he CHOSE to play internationally as an American in the USA Hockey governed USHL and in IIHF tournaments. Essentially he pledged his American nationality to the hockey world, hence the US flag on Bruins roster. IIHF national team eligibility is complicated, but I've never stumbled upon any requirement regarding length of residence. If you stand by that statement, please back it up with a reference.
