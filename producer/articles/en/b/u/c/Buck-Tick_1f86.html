Buck-Tick

Buck-Tick is a popular Japanese rock, new wave & Gothic Rock group formed by Hisashi Imai in the early 1980s. Buck-Tick has performed with bands such as Marilyn Manson, KMFDM, Luna Sea and Soft Ballet. Buck-Tick's side-projects, Schaft and Schwein, consisted of Hisashi Imai, Atsushi Sakurai, Fujii Maki from Soft Ballet, Raymond Watts (aka "PIG"), and Sascha Konietzko from KMFDM.

[edit] Members

  • Atsushi Sakurai (櫻井敦司): vocals
  • Hisashi Imai (今井寿): guitar, electronics, and miscellaneous noise
  • Hidehiko Hoshino (星野英彦): guitar and keyboard
  • Yutaka Higuchi (桶口豊): bass
  • Toll Yagami (ヤガミ ト ル): percussion.

Although they use different surnames, Toll and Yutaka are brothers.
