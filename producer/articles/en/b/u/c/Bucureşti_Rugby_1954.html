Bucureşti are a rugby union team that competes in the European Challenge Cup. The side is a team that is formed every season to play in the Challenge Cup, consisting of footballers playing in the domestic Romanian leagues. The team has yet to make it past the group stages in the Challenge Cup, but have managed some good results.

[edit] Statistics

  • 2004/05 European Shield - Played 2, won 1, lost 1.
  • 2005/06 European Challenge Cup - Played 6, won 1, lost 5.
  • 2006/07 European Challenge Cup - Played 2, won 1, lost 1.
