The Buck Inn is an English public house situated at the north end of the high street in Northallerton, North Yorkshire.

(Needs picture)

[edit] Football Team

The football team ran from 1995 to 1998 and participated in the Wensleydale Creamery League, division 2. The team members were popularly known as the 'Buck Army'.

Their most memorable match came in the 1995-1996 season at home to their arch-rivals, Richmond Buck in the league cup quarter final. The high scoring match saw goals from David Rogers, David Tennant and Steve Grant and was eventually settled on penalties, with Buck Inn Northallerton taking the honours. Buck Inn were subsequently knocked out in the semi-finals.
