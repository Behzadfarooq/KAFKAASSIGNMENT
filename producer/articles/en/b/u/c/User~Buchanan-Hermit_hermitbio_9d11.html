  • Part-time job: Film and television extra
  • Favorite sports teams: Anaheim Ducks, Anaheim Angels

My username comes from the main building for the Faculty of Arts at the University of British Columbia, the Buchanan Building. And because I spend so much time at Buchanan, I'm practically a hermit there. So hence, Buchanan-Hermit™.

I am also a former DMOZ editor but quit after losing interest after several years, I am part of Hugh Boyd Secondary School's class of 2004 and am currently attending the University of British Columbia.
