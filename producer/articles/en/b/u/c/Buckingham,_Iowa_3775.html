Buckingham, Iowa

[edit] Location

Buckingham, Iowa is located near the interestions of Hwy D-65 and U.S. Highway 63. North of Traer, Iowa and South of Hudson, Iowa. Buckingham is located in Tama County.

The zip code for Buckingham is: 50612

Buckingham is in the Central Standard Time zone.

[edit] Facts

Buckingham is served by the Traer fire department.
