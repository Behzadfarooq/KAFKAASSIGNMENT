Buccleuch Scotch beef

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Buccleuch Beef is a beef reared on the estates of the Duke of Buccleuch and other Buccleuch farmers in Scotland. The beef has for some time been used by leading chefs and restaurants.
