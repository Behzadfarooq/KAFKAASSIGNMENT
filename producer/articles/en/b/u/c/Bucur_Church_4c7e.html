Bucur Church

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Church seen from the street.

Bucur Church is the oldest church standing in Bucharest. Legend says it was first built from wood by Bucur the shepherd. The stone construction was made by Mircea cel Bătrân (Mircea the Elder) in 1416.

[edit] Address

Radu Voda Str, no. 33
