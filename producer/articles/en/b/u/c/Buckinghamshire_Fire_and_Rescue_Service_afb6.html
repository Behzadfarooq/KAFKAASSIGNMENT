The Buckinghamshire Fire and Rescue Service, is the Fire Service serving the county of Buckinghamshire. It operates continuously manned fire stations in Aylesbury, Beaconsfield, Bletchley, Broughton, Great Holm and High Wycombe.

It also operates stations, which are manned during the day, in Amersham, Buckingham, Gerrards Cross and Newport Pagnell. Volunteer crews also man retained stations in Brill, Chesham, Great Missenden, Haddenham, Marlow, Olney, Princes Risborough, Stokenchurch, Waddesdon and Winslow.
