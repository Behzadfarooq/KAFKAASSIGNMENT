Buckley is a locality in Rochdale, in Greater Manchester, England. It is around one mile (1.6 km) north east of Rochdale town centre and one mile (1.6 km) south of Wardle.

[edit] History

Although now a locality within Rochdale, Buckley was once a small hamlet in the parish of Wardleworth. It was the site to a historic house by the name of Buckley Hall.

Today, Buckley is home to Buckley Hall Prison.
