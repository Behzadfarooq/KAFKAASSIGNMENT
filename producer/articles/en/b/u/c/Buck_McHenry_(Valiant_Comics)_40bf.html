Buck McHenry is a Valiant Comics superhero. Created by writer Jim Shooter and Bob Layton, he first appeared in Solar #10.

Buck was Earth's Geomancer from 1889 until 1992 when he passed the lineage onto his great-great-nephew Geoff McHenry. Buck battled alongside the Eternal Warrior and was eventually killed in a battle with Master Darque.
