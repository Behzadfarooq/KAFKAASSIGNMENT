Bucky Buckwalter

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Morris "Bucky" Buckwalter is a former NBA coach and executive, as well as a former coach in the ABA.

He served briefly as head coach of the Seattle SuperSonics in 1972 (on an interim basis), and then later would serve as a the head coach of the Utah Stars of the ABA, replacing Joe Mullaney.

He served as Vice President of Basketball Operations for the Portland Trail Blazers. In 1991 he won NBA Executive of the Year, as the Blazers posted a league-best 63-19 record. (The team would fail to advance to the NBA Finals that season, however; losing to the Los Angeles Lakers in the Western Conference finals).
