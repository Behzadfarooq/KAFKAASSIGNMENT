[edit] Steve Nash

If you can quote a published source that mentions Nash's defensive deficiencies, then you can restore some of what you recently added to that article. As it stands, however, your edit is nothing but POV and original research. I do agree with you, to an extent, but you need to cite an authoritative source to demonstrate that it is a commonly held opinion about Nash.

BTW, a low steals average does not necessarily mean that a player is a bad defender, so I wouldn't use that argument in the future. Lots of players get high steal totals because they take foolish chances on the defensive end. And just because someone hasn't been named to the All-Defensive team doesn't mean he's a bad defender. Zagalejo 02:25, 31 December 2006 (UTC)
