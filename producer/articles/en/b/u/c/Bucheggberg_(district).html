District of Bucheggberg, canton of Solothurn
Municipalities of Bucheggberg

Bucheggberg is one of the ten districts of the canton of Solothurn in Switzerland, situated to the southwest of the canton. Together with the district of Wasseramt, it forms the Amtei (electoral district) of Wasseramt-Bucheggberg.

Bucheggberg contains the following municipalities:
