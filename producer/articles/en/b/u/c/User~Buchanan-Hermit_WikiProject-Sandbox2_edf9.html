WikiProject Psych is an attempt to organize, categorize, and standardize articles about television show Psych. All Psych articles can be found at this index. This immense project needs many writers, editors, readers, researchers, etc. Anyone with a knowledge/interest in the Psych universe is welcome to join this project. Feel free to contact members for help getting to grips with everything.

Writing and editing can currently be done from:

  • List of Psych episodes - Episode articles
  • Checklist below - All articles
  • Pending tasks - Details of episode/non-episode articles
  • Talk page - Discussion of progress/tasks
