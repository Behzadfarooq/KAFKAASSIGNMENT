Bucky, (or "Jubaku-kun, The Twelve World History") its an anime serie created by Ami Shibata, enter the years of 1997 and 1999. In spite of not having made a lot of success in the Japan, it was exhibited at countries of Portuguese language, English and Spanish. Being the Brazil, place where more acquired fans.

[edit] History

The history happens at a place called "Parallel Planet" located above the sky and down of the sea. This planet is divided in twelve worlds, in such way that if it seems a lot with a clock. In the center of this clock, exists the "Needle tower", A place the where the time doesn't run.
Exists In each "world" a Great Child (or G.C.), that is actually the guardian of that world. The Greath Child It is always accompanied of a Spirit, an explosive companion that helps to defeat the troublemakers monsters.

Bucky, the protagonist of the history, is a normal boy that lives in the first world. He lives with a single and humble ambition: to dominate the world (in the sense of the whole planet). He is very certain and it would be capable to die to reach yours dream. Until that one day he meets with Spark, the Great Child of Cousins. Spark is very known by being the Great Child more fort of all of the worlds, and he is a successor's search. After finding with Bucky and a to talk little with him, Spark without apparent reason has just chosen yours successor. Bucky has just become a Great Child and to win the company of Jibaki, the spirit of the first world.

Since then, Bucky comes out to travel the twelve worlds and to accomplish yours dream of dominating the world, with their friends (or like him yours would say, slaves) that he finds in the world.
