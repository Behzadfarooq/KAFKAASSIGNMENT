Bucky (disambiguation)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Bucky can refer to:

  • Bucky, a Marvel Comics superhero.
  • Bucky - The Incredible Kid, a japense Anime created by Ami Shibata.
  • Bucky Badger is the official mascot of the University of Wisconsin-Madison.
  • Bucky the Beaver, a Ty Beanie Baby.
  • Bucky Covington is a musician, American Idol finalist.
    • Bucky, the eponymous album of aforementioned musician.
  • Bucky Dent was a Major League Baseball player, famous for the home run that helped the New York Yankees defeat the Boston Red Sox in a 1978 playoff game.
  • Bucky Katt is a character in the comic strip Get Fuzzy.
  • Bucky Lasek is a professional skateboarder.
  • Bucky is a nickname for Buckfast Tonic Wine, a wine made in Devon, England
  • Bucky McBadBat is a character from The Fairly OddParents.
  • Bucky O'Hare, comic book and TV show character. A green rabbit who is the captain of a ship, the Righteous Indignation.
  • Richard Buckminster "Bucky" Fuller, an American designer, architect, and inventor of the geodesic dome.
  • Ralph Bucky Phillips, convict from New York.
  • Bucky the Squirrel, a character from The Emperor's New School
  • Bucky, lead character of short-lived 1959 animated TV cartoon series Bucky and Pepito
  • Bucky bits, modifier bits in use on the MIT space-cadet computer keyboard.
