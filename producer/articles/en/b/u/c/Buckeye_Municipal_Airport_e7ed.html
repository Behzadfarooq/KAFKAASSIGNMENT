Buckeye Municipal Airport (IATA: BXK, ICAO: KBXK, FAA LID: BXK) is a general aviation airport located six miles (10 km) northwest of the central business district (CBD) of Buckeye, a town in Maricopa County, Arizona, USA, west of Phoenix. The airport averages around 100 operations a day. It is not served by any commercial airlines at this time.

[edit] Facilities

Buckeye Municipal Airport covers 706 acres and has one runway:

  • Runway 17/35: 5,500 x 75 ft. (1,676 x 23 m), Surface: Asphalt
