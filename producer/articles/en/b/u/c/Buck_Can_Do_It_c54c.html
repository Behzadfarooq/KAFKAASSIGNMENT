Buck Can Do It

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

"Buck Can Do It" is the 4th episode of season two of the television sitcom Married... with Children.

  • First Aired: Sunday October 11, 1987 on FOX.
Spoiler warning: Plot and/or ending details follow.

[edit] Plot

One of the Bundy's neighbors threaten to sue when Buck the dog mates with their dog, and asks the Bundy's to neuter Buck, which Al refuses to do.

[edit] Guest stars

Buck (In Dream) – Derek McGrath
Chainsaw – Dick Durock
Mr Pittman – Mitchell Greenberg

[edit] Episode guide

  • Writer: Michael Moye, Ron Leavitt
  • Director: Linda Day
