Location of Bucciano in Italy

Bucciano is a comune (municipality) in the Province of Benevento in the Italian region Campania, located about 40 km northeast of Naples and about 20 km southwest of Benevento. As of 31 December 2004, it had a population of 1,950 and an area of 7.9 km².[1]

Bucciano borders the following municipalities: Airola, Bonea, Moiano, Tocco Caudio.
