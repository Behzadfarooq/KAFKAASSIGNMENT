Buckfenhaim Park (Hebrew: פארק באקפנהיים) is the first novel in the Buckfenhaim Trilogy by Shai Besalel Avnon.

[edit] Translation of back cover

Intrigues, conspiracies and conflicts of powers surround Backfenhein.

Enemies from within and without are scheming to take control of the ancient, 3000-year old stardom.

Woldrok, a mighty and evil wizard, Head of House Backfenheim, is about to retire. The stardom is embarking on a phase of confusion and chaos.

Who is the one, who is worthy of leading, of facing all the dangers and the instability? Roy the powerful yet dreamy and sensitive wizard or Drall, the violent, sophisticated authoritarian warrior? An ancient spell has the power to decide – a spell whose secret has yet to be deciphered.

The first book in the Trilogy, tells the story of the Backfenheim heirs; of their lives interwoven with treacheries and murders; their enemies and friends and the women who share with them their days of love, danger and insanity.
