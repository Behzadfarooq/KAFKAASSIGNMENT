Bucloc is a 6th class municipality in the province of Abra, Philippines. According to the 2000 census, it has a population of 2,109 people in 357 households.

[edit] Barangays

Bucloc is politically subdivided into 4 barangays.

  • Lingey
  • Lamao (Pob.)
  • Labaan
  • Ducligan
