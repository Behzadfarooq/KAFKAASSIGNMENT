Buck Creek (north of US Highway 52)

Buck Creek is a small tributary of the Upper Mississippi River flowing mainly through Garnavillo and Jefferson Townnships in Clayton County, Iowa, to join the Big River at Bussey Lake, a backwater lake about 2 miles above Lock and Dam No. 10 at Guttenberg, Iowa. The Army Corps of Engineers is working to ameliorate erosion problems in the creek's drainage.

Clayton County maintains a 100 acre park on the creek.
