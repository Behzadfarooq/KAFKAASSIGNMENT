Bucky Fellini is an album by the Dead Milkmen produced and released in 1987 by Enigma, being their first not on Restless Records.

The album had one single, "Instant Club Hit (You'll Dance to Anything)." The single featured the original version and multiple "remixes" of the song, as well as previously unreleased tracks. That track and two others appeared on the 1997 compilation Death Rides a Pale Cow: The Ultimate Collection, and two on the 1998 compilation Cream of the Crop.
