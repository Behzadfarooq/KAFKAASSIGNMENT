Buckeye Leadership Society

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Buckeye Leadership Society, also known as BLS, is a honorary leadership organization at The Ohio State University in Ohio in the United States. Established on campus in late 2004, it currently has over 700 members.

The Society is affiliated with the SOURCE office which is part of the Ohio union. Buckeye Leadership Society is a chapter of Sigma Alpha Pi and is part of The National Society of Leadership and Success.

The chapter's daily duties are carried out by a group of members that serve on its advisory board. The advisory board works with chapter advisors in the SOURCE office to plan and carry out daily operations of the Society.
