Buckingham Town F.C. are a football club based in Buckingham, England. They were established in 1883 and were the founding members of the Hellenic Football League in 1953. In the 1984-85 season, they reached the 1st round proper of the FA Cup. For the 2005-06 season, they are members of the United Counties Football League Premier Division.
