Buccaneer State Park

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Buccaneer State Park is a state park in the U.S. state of Mississippi. It is located off U.S. Highway 90 at Waveland.

As of 2005, the park is closed due to damage from Hurricane Katrina.

The park is located at 30°15′41″N, 89°24′15″W GR3
