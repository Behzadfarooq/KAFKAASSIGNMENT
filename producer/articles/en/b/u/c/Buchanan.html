Buchanan is a surname of Scottish origin and is the surname of:

  • Barry Buchanan (born 1968), professional wrestler ("Bull Buchanan")
  • Bay Buchanan, sister to Pat Buchanan, President of The American Cause
  • Claudius Buchanan (1766-1815), English divine
  • Edgar Buchanan, American actor
  • Francis Buchanan, (1762-1829), Scottish surgeon, geographer and naturalist
  • Franklin Buchanan (1800-1874), Confederate admiral
  • George Buchanan (1506-1582), Scottish humanist
  • George Buchanan, British civil engineer
  • George Buchanan (1890-1955), Scottish politician
  • Ian Buchanan (born 1957). Scottish television actor
  • Jack Buchanan (1891-1957), British actor and singer
  • James Buchanan (1791-1868), President of the United States of America
  • James M. Buchanan (born 1919), economist
  • Jensen Buchanan (born 1962), U.S. actress
  • John Buchanan (born 1931), Canadian politician
  • John Buchanan, U.S. politician, Republican presidential nominee in 2004
  • John P. Buchanan (1847-1930), Governor of Tennessee
  • Jude Buchanan (1898- 1945), Abstract bee artist
  • Ken Buchanan (born 1945), Scottish boxer
  • Keisha Buchanan (born 1984), British-Jamaican singer
  • Manley Augustus Buchanan (born 1949), Jamaican musician
  • Neil Buchanan, British television presenter
  • Pat Buchanan, political pundit and Reform Party presidential nominee in 2000
  • Ray Buchanan (born 1971), American football player
  • Robert Buchanan (1813-1866), British lecturer and journalist
  • Robert C. Buchanan (1811-1878), American soldier
  • Robert Williams Buchanan (1841-1901), British poet, dramatist, and novelist
  • Roy Buchanan (1939-1988), American guitarist
  • Shomari Buchanan (born 1977), wide receiver
