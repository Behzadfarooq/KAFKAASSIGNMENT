[edit] State Debate

  • Thanks for your comment, I'd just like to point a couple things out. First, I didn't make a decision to delete the pages, I simply nominated them - it is up to the community to decide whether or not an article gets deleted. Second of all, you'll notice throughout my AfD history that I am strongly in favour of subsidiary organizations (ie. regional divisions) being included as a section of the parent article. Unless they are significantly different (ie. more than simply holding one or two different events, but something major) I simply don't see why we should waste Wikipedia space and Wikipedians' time. The question I ask myself is; "Is there any information in this page that someone from the other side of the world would want/need, and wouldn't be able to get from the larger article?" The outcome of that answer guides my actions - pm_shef 22:54, 20 June 2006 (UTC)
