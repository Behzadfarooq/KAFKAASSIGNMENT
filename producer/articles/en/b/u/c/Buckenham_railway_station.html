Buckenham railway station is a railway station in the English county of Norfolk. The station is located on the Norwich-Lowestoft Wherry Line.[1]

The station is served by 'one' with services operating between Norwich and Lowestoft. With just one train in each direction daily (Monday - Saturday) Buckenham has one of the worst (in terms of frequency) services provided to any British railway station and one of the lowest passenger usages (Strategic Rail Authority figures 2003)

RSPB Buckenham Marshes is located next to the station, with RSPB Strumpshaw Fen a short walk away. Strumpshaw Hall Steam Museum is also located in the area.
