Buckeye Border Conference

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The member schools of the Buckeye Border Conference.

The Buckeye Border Conference is a high school athletic league located in extreme northwest Ohio. The league mostly sponsors the sports of basketball and baseball/softball, with the inclusion of a few others.

The following are the current members, with the school name providing a link to the town in which it is associated with:

  • Edon Bombers^
  • Fayette Eagles
  • Hilltop Cadets^
  • North Central Eagles
  • Pettisville Black Birds
  • Stryker Panthers

^Edon and Hilltop have football teams that participate in the TAAC, but are members of the BBC for every other sport.
