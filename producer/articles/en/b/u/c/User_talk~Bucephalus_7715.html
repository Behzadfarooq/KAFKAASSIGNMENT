[edit] Deletion tags

Thank you for your work scouring the New Pages list for bad articles. I noticed that you used {delete} on CalendarHub. This tag has been deprecated as it makes extra effort on the part of the admin who actually deletes the page. If an article is worthy of speedy deletion, you should be able to give a reason why. In your edit summary you wrote advert. In the future please tag as {db|advert}. This will make it much easier on the admin and requires only three extra key strokes.
