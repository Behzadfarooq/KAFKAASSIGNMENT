Buck "Backhand" Adams (born Charles S. Allen, November 15, 1955) is a male American porn star and a Porn director. He starred in numerous films during the 1980s. The Adult Video Network has awarded him three Best Actor Awards.

Filmography as: Actor, Director, Writer, Producer.

[edit] Trivia

  • He survived several heart attacks in the fall of 1996.
  • Buck's younger sister Amber Lynn got him into porn. For months they kept their relationship secret until they were once scheduled in a scene together, forcing them to reveal the truth. Buck's relationship with Amber bounces between not speaking and friendish. [1]
  • He was married briefly during the mid-1990s to porn actress Janette Littledove.
  • He was also married to girlfriend/employee/porn actress Aspen Brock in 1999 after she visited him in the hospital following a near fatal heart attack.
  • Nicknamed "Buck-o-matic" in the 1980s, for his ability to perform sex up to five or six times a day.
