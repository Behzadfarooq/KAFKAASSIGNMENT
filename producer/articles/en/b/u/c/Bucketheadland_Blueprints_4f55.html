Bucketheadland Blueprints is the demo version to Buckethead's first album Bucketheadland.

Bucketheadland Blueprints features many early versions of the riffs featured on Bucketheadland as well as riff's which were not used at all. Notably the "park theme" is different to the one used in the album version. Although the album version to the park theme can be heard on this demo tape.
