The Buckeye Valley Local School District is located in Delaware, Ohio and is made up primarily of students from the Ashley, Ostrander and Radnor areas. The district currently consists of three elementary schools (East in Ashley, West in Ostrander and North in Radnor), one middle school and one high school. The school's colors are Brown and Gold and their mascot is the Fighting Baron.

[edit] History

The Buckeye Valley Local School District was established in 1963 as a conglomeration of the local Ashley, Ostrander, and Radnor schools. BARONS stands for Bellpoint, Ashley, Radnor, Ostrander, Norton, Scioto, all the areas in the district.

[edit] Buckeye Valley Middle School

Buckeye Valley Middle School holds grades six through eight and is located next to the high school building. Buckeye Valley Middle offers its students many of the activities that they will find at the high school. Buckeye Valley Middle School has active athletic and performing arts programs.

  • BVMS has an active student council which organizes school dances, community service projects and other events.

[edit] Buckeye Valley Elementary Schools

[edit] Buckeye Valley East Elementary

Buckeye Valley East Elementary holds grades Kindergarten through fifth grade and is located in Ashley, Ohio.

[edit] Buckeye Valley North Elementary

Buckeye Valley North Elementary holds grades Kindergarten through fifth grade and is located in Radnor, Ohio.

[edit] Buckeye Valley West Elementary

Buckeye Valley West Elementary holds grades Kindergarten through fifth grade and is located in Ostrander, Ohio.
