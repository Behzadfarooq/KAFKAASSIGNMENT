Well, my name is Joe and I am 20 years old. I was born in the capital city of Beijing, People's Republic of China on June 10, 1986. In 1988, I moved to the United States to Birmingham, Alabama. From then on I went to elementary school on until high school in Hoover where I graduated in 2004. I am currently a student at Auburn University and it is the best place in the world. My favorite television show is Charmed and I also love to travel to different places.
