A Buccaneer can be one of a number of things:

  • Buccaneer, a specific type of pirate
  • In sports:
    • Tampa Bay Buccaneers, an NFL team
    • Los Angeles Buccaneers, an NFL team
    • Buccaneer (baseball mascot), the 1995 Pittsburgh Pirates mascot.
    • Buccaneer 18, a type of light sailboat.
  • In aviation:
    • Blackburn Buccaneer, a British built strike aircraft
    • Brewster Buccaneer, a US close support aircraft
  • Buccaneer (game), a board game published by Waddingtons
  • Reading Buccaneers Drum and Bugle Corps
  • USS Sapphire (PYc-2), a United States Navy patrol vessel of World War II.
