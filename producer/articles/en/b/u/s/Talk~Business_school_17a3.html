[edit] entrance requirements _ some answers that need to be incorporated into the main article ..

Q= gre scores are valid for ? a- The GRE score is valid for five years; however most of the universities insist on a GRE score not more than 2-3 years old. ref = www.cieglobal.com/gre/gre_details.htm

Q= toefl is valid for ? A= Your TOEFL score is valid for only two years. In other words, two years after you take your TOEFL, your score will be deleted from the official TOEFL ... www.testmagic.com/toefl/faqs.htm Sanjiv swarup 03:32, 1 April 2007 (UTC)

These questions would be better answered on the pages that discuss the GRE and the TOEFL, since not all business schools use those tests, and most schools that do use them are not business schools. --Duncan 09:45, 1 April 2007 (UTC)
