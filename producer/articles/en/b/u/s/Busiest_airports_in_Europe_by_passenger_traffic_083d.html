Busiest airports in Europe by passengers/year (2006) (more than 10 million passengers/year)

  1. London Heathrow Airport, United Kingdom 67.3 mill. passengers.
  2. Charles de Gaulle International Airport, (Paris) France 56.8 mill. passengers.
  3. Frankfurt International Airport, Germany 52.8 mill. passengers
  4. Amsterdam Schiphol Airport, Netherlands 46.1 mill. passengers.
  5. Madrid Barajas International Airport. Spain 45.5 mill. passengers.
  6. London Gatwick Airport, United Kingdom 34.1 mill. passengers.
  7. Munich Airport, Germany 30.8 mill. passengers.
  8. Leonardo Da Vinci International Airport, (Rome) Italy 30.2 mill. passengers.
  9. Barcelona International Airport, Spain 30.0 mill. passengers.
  10. Paris - Orly Airport, France 25.6 mill. passengers.
  11. London Stansted Airport, United Kingdom 23.7 mill. passengers.
  12. Son Sant Joan Airport, (Palma de Mallorca) Spain 22.4 mill. passengers
  13. Manchester Airport, United Kingdom 22.1 mill. passengers.
  14. Malpensa International Airport, (Milan) Italy 21.8 mill. passengers.
  15. Dublin International Airport, Ireland 21.1 mill. passengers.
  16. Copenhagen Airport Denmark 20.9 mill. passenger.
  17. * Atatürk International Airport, (Istanbul) Turkey 19.3 mill. passengers.
  18. Zürich International Airport, Switzerland 19.2 mill. passengers.
  19. Oslo Airport, Norway 17.7 mill. passengers.
  20. Stockholm-Arlanda Airport, Sweden 17.5 mill. passengers.
  21. Vienna Airport, Austria 16.9 mill. passengers.
  22. Brussels Airport, Belgium 16.7 mill. passengers.
  23. Düsseldorf International Airport, Germany 16.6 mill. passengers.
  24. Domodedovo International Airport, (Moscow) Russia 15.4 mill. passengers
  25. Athens International Airport, Greece 15.1 mill. passengers
  26. Málaga Airport, Spain 13.1 mill. passengers
  27. Sheremetyevo International Airport, (Moscow) Russia 12.8 mill. passengers
  28. Portela Airport, (Lisbon) Portugal 12.2 mill. passengers. (est.)
  29. Helsinki-Vantaa Airport, Finland 12.1 mill. passengers.
  30. Hamburg Airport, Germany 12.0 mill. passengers.
  31. Tegel International Airport, (Berlin) Germany 11.8 mill. passengers.
  32. Ruzyně International Airport, (Prague) Czech Republic 11.6 mill. passengers.
  33. Stuttgart Airport, Germany 10.2 mill. passengers.
