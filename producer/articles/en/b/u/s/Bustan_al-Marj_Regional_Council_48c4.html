Bustan al-Marj Regional Council

The Bustan al-Marj Regional Council is a regional council in northern Israel. Its territory lies adjacent to the Jezreel Valley and north of Afula. The municipality includes four large rural settlements in the Galilee:

  • Kfar Dahi
  • Kfar Metzar
  • Kfar Nin
  • Kfar Sulam
