[edit] A reason for deleting ja

Usually, Korean people and Korean Wikipedia seperates the subway line itself from their operators. However, in Japanese Wikipedia, they use same page for subway line and operator. The previously linked Japanese page, ja:釜山交通公社, is about both Busan Subway and Busan Transportation Corporation. Currently, Busan Transportation Corporation is linked to that page. Avoding Interwiki mixing and confusion, I deleted Japanese Interwiki from this article. --peremen 10:19, 26 August 2006 (UTC)
