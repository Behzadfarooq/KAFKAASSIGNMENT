The syndrome of stress has become part of our lives and is affecting us in many ways. The modern competitive world is continuously throwing various challenges and is inducing much stress in all of us. This being the scenario, both the organization and the employees have to find ways to reduce the stress levels and improve the performance. When we are focusing on efficiency in every thing we do, there is a dire need to tackle the stress.

This seminar is basically aimed at the following; 1.Throwing light on the research studies conducted in this field, 2.Stress causing factors, 3.Coping strategies 4.Role of Counselling, Yoga and Metaphysics in stress management.

This seminar will be of great use for practicing managers, academicians, researchers and the students. Eminent and accomplished people from different walks of life will be delivering presentations during these two days.
