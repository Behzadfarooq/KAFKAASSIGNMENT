Busty Dusty (born in Van Nuys, California, USA on October 20, 1961) is the stage name of a now retired big-bust model and stripper. Before breast enhancement her stage name was Rocky Raquel, but even as Busty Dusty she increased her size after 1993.

Busty Dusty's career was at its height in the 1990s, up until her retirement in 1999. During her active phase she was one of the most popular big-bust models next to Traci Topps, SaRenna Lee, and Pandora Peaks. She never made hard core videos.

For a time she had a website with Traci Topps known as The Fantasy Cafe. The initial promo photographs for the site were shot by erotic photographer Tom Ruddock. Busty Dusty's sister Dominique was responsible for the maintenance of the website, which eventually folded when Topps parted company with the other girls. She starred in several softcore big-breast videos, including a notable appearance for Playboy in Voluptuous Vixens. Mostly Busty Dusty was seen in solo scenes.

Busty Dusty had a bra cup-size billed as 90HHH, which is obviously an exaggeration. Her measurements were reportedly 54-24-34 at her peak. Her height is 1.65 metres. In October 25, 1999, she had breast reduction surgery and announced her retirement soon after.
