Business process illustration

In order that business processes can be improved they must first be illustrated. The hardest task in business process mapping is getting everyone to agree what the process looks like. The starting point is an illustration of the process. The production of a process illustration is an iterative activity. The more widly circulated the draft illustrations then the more accurate the final, first off, illustration.

A simple form of business process illustration is the flow-chart - provided, that is, that it is kept simple.

In order that the flowchart is kept simple the author should not exceed a maximum of 6 task boxes in the flowchart. In business process illustrations it is not necessary to use conventional flow-chart symbols. Simple task boxes will suffice.
