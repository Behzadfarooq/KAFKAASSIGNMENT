Bus Verts du Calvados is the bus compagny operating cross country buses in the departement of Calvados, France. The parent company of the Bus Verts is the CETRAPEC, the old Cars du Calvados company which operated buses in and around Caen after the closure of the Tramways Electriques de Caen in 1935.
