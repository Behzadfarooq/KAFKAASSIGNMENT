Buster can mean one of several things:

  • A USA term of address: see wiktionary:buster.
  • Buster (comic), a British comic.
  • Buster (sport comic), a Swedish comic.
  • Price Busters TV, a shopping channel based in the UK who has a mascot that is a monkey called Buster.
  • Buster (film), a movie starring Phil Collins as the real-life Buster Edwards.
  • Buster Bluth, a character from Arrested Development .
  • Buster, a car test dummy used as a test subject on Mythbusters
  • Buster, a dog belonging to Roy Hattersley, who has published his diaries (ghost written by Hattersley).
  • Similarly, Buster is a dog owned by Paul O'Grady.
  • Buster (Jeffrey Wayne Cole), a star of gay pornographic films.
  • Buster is the general term for various kinds of weapons in the Mega Man universes.
  • Buster Baxter, frequently identified as "Buster the Bunny", star of Postcards from Buster and originally from the show Arthur.
  • Buster Bunny, a character from Tiny Toon Adventures.
  • Buster Poindexter, a pseudonym of David Johansen.
  • Buster, a derogatory term for a person who pretends they are gang affiliated.
  • Buster Meikle, singer and guitarist, formerly with Unit Four Plus Two and Buster & Bill.
  • Buster Merryfield, English actor; most famous for his portrayal of 'Uncle' Albert Trotter in the sitcom Only Fools and Horses.
  • Buster Keaton, an American actor from the silent film era.
  • Buster the crash test dummy, from the popular science television show, MythBusters.
  • Buster Boats, a Finnish manufacturer of high quality aluminum boats. The company is owned by Inha Works Ltd, which is a part of the Fiskars-group.
  • LITTLE BUSTERS, an album of Japanese rock band The Pillows.
  • A pratfall, often performed in comic silent movies
