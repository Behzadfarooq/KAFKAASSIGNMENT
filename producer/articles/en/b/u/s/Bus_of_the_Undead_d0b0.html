"Bus of the Undead" is the third episode of the animated series Aqua Teen Hunger Force. It was first broadcast on September 30, 2001.

[edit] Synopsis

Spoiler warning: Plot and/or ending details follow.

[edit] Cold open

Dr. Weird introduces a half-man, half-moth monster he calls Mothmonsterman, who escapes through a giant Rabbot hole in the wall.

[edit] Plot

Mothmonsterman escapes from Dr. Weird's lair and threatens trouble for the Aqua Teens if they don't leave their lights on. Master Shake suspects him to be Count Dracula, and later believes a school bus is possessed by Dracula's ghost.
