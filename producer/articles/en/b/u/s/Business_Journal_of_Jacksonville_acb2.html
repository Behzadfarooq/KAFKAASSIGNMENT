Business Journal of Jacksonville (ISSN 1527-8611 regular print; ISSN 1534-2336 online) is a weekly American City Business Journals publication containing articles about business people, issues, and events in northeast Florida and southeast Georgia. The paper, published in Jacksonville, Florida was previously named the Jacksonville Business Journal.
