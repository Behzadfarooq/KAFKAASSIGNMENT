Bush not welcome demo, Gothenburg 14 June 2001, Photographer, Per Westergård, from official website pressphotos: www.eu2001.se

The person who created this image has released all rights to it. However, according to the section about photographs in the Swedish copyright law (1994), a photographer retains the unrevokable right to be credited until 70 years after his death. This is believed to primarily concern publication in Sweden, however this is not certain.

Personen som skapade detta fotografi har släppt det fritt. Men enligt lag (1994), har fotografens alltid rätt att ha sitt namn nämnt, intill 70 år efter dennes död.
