Busted is a 1996 comedy film, starring Corey Feldman, Corey Haim, Dominick Brascia and Ava Fabian.

[edit] Plot summary

The police force of Amity decide to get crime off the streets and decide that the prostitutes are better off working out of the police station as opposed to in the streets.

[edit] Trivia

  • Dominick Brascia was in National Lampoon's Last Resort with The two Coreys
  • Marked Corey Feldman's directorial debut
