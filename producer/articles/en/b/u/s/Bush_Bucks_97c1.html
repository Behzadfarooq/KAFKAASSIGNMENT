The Bush Bucks are a South African football (soccer) club, based in East London, Eastern Cape. The club was founded in 1957.

At the end of the 2005/2006 season, the club was relegated to the Mvela Golden League after finishing second from bottom in the PSL.

[edit] Major Honours

Coca-Cola Cup Winners 1993, 1996
