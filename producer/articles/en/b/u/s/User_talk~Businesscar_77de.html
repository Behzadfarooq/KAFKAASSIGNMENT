[edit] Your new article BusinessCar

A tag has been placed on BusinessCar, requesting that it be speedily deleted from Wikipedia. This has been done under the criteria for speedy deletion, because the article seems to be blatant advertising which only promotes a company, product, group or service and which would need to be fundamentally rewritten in order to become an encyclopedia article. Please read the general criteria for speedy deletion, particularly item 11, as well as the guidelines on spam.

If you can indicate why the subject of this article is not blatant advertising, you may contest the tagging. To do this, please add {{hangon}} on the top of the page and leave a note on the article's talk page explaining your position. Please do not remove the speedy deletion tag yourself, but don't hesitate to add information to the article that would help make it encyclopedic, as well as adding any citations from reliable sources to ensure that the article will be verifiable. Feel free to leave a note on my talk page if you have any questions about this. -- AirOdyssey (Talk) 05:10, 6 January 2007 (UTC)

[edit] Your edits to North American International Auto Show and Car rental

Please do not add inappropriate external links to Wikipedia. Wikipedia is not a mere directory of links nor should it be used for advertising or promotion. Inappropriate links include (but are not limited to) links to personal web sites, links to web sites with which you are affiliated, and links that exist to attract visitors to a web site or promote a product. See the external links guideline and spam policies for further explanations of links that are considered appropriate. If you feel the link should be added to the article, then please discuss it on the article's talk page rather than re-adding it. See the welcome page to learn more about Wikipedia. Thank you. -- AirOdyssey (Talk) 05:10, 6 January 2007 (UTC)

[edit] Why was your article deleted?

In reply to your question asking why your article about BusinessCar was deleted, you might want to read the following before attempting to create a new article:

  • Wikipedia is not a soapbox
  • Criteria for speedy deletion

The fact that your username and user talk page signature say "BusinessCar" may bring some Wikipedians to believe that you are writing a page about yourself or about your own company. -- AirOdyssey (Talk) 13:04, 8 January 2007 (UTC)
