"Business Sucks (part 1)" is the 5th episode of season nine of the television sitcom Married... with Children.

  • First Aired: October 2, 1994, on FOX.


Spoiler warning: Plot and/or ending details follow.

[edit] Plot

Marcy leads a protest after Al banishes a shoe-store customer for nursing her baby. This prompts a counter-protest by NO MA'AM. Meanwhile, Peggy's trip home is delayed by a train accident.
