Busso Thoma (31 October 1899 – 23 January 1945) was a salesman.

Thoma was born in St. Blasien-Immeneich, Black Forest. In 1939, he became a consultant on the staff of the General Army Office (AHA), where, as a major, he later became an accessory to the July 20 Plot to assassinate Adolf Hitler.

On 14 September 1944, Thoma was arrested, on 17 January 1945, he was sentenced to death by the Volksgerichtshof, and on 23 January 1945, he was hanged at Plötzensee Prison in Berlin.
