[edit] Summary

A high-resolution overhead shot of a full bus yard located in New Orleans. Intersection of Chicasaw St and Metropolitan St, southwest of the I-10 crossing a canal northeast of downtown.

This is the Desire neighborhood of the 9th Ward, just west of the Industrial Canal, on the other side from the junction of w:Mississipi River Gulf Outlet, thus one of the first areas of the city to flood catestrophically.

From NOAA. See http://ngs.woc.noaa.gov/katrina/KATRINA0000.HTM for an overview of the area.

This specific image is cropped and rotated from: http://ngs.woc.noaa.gov/storms/katrina/24426569.jpg
