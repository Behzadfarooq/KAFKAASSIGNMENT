No, it is right--a category page, by definition, is a list of articles within Wikipedia related to that category. Think of it this way: the list you see on the category page is (and should be) a list of Wikipedia articles about buskers, not a list of buskers. If you want to make a page with a list of buskers, regardless of whether they have a Wikipedia article or not, Category:Buskers is not the right place to do that, but you could start a page called List of buskers which would be appropriate for that.
