The Bushmaster III is an automatic/semi-automatic cannon designed and built by Boeing. It is based on the M242 Bushmaster originally developed by Alliant Techsystems. The weapon has been selected as the primary armament of the CV90 infantry fighting vehicles (IFV) soon to enter Danish and Dutch service.
