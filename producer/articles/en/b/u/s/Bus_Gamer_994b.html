Bus Gamer (ビズゲーマー, Bus Gamer?) was a short-lived manga series, created by Kazuya Minekura. It currently only exists as a single volume 'Pilot Edition' due to difficulties in serialization. It follows the story of three young men hired by a mysterious company to participate in a business game, a way for companies to gamble against each other for corporate secrets.

Only eleven chapters of this series were ever published before the magazine in which it was serialized went under. While the artist, Kazuya Minekura, claims in the re-released Pilot Edition to still have interest in finishing the series and characters from it appear in her regularly released art books, no word has been given on when the series will be picked up again.

It is, however, notable in that, despite its short-lived run, it has managed to retain an almost cult-like popularity status amongst her fans and, due to this, has been brought to the United States by Tokyopop.
