Business process design

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

It has been suggested that Business process reengineering be merged into this article or section. (Discuss)

Business process design is a process which designs the realization of an operational business process.

The process of modifying existing processes is also known as business process reengineering.

A common methodology used for business process design is URDAD, the Use Case, Responsibility Driven Analysis and Design methodology.
