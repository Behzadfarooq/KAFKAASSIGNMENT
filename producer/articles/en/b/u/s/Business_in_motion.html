“Business in Motion” is a long-running weekly radio program broadcast by 93.3 CFMU, the radio station owned and operated by the Student Union of McMaster University.

McMaster University is in Hamilton, Ontario, Canada. CFMU started in 1978 and currently broadcasts 24/7/365 on the FM band at 93.3 and on the Internet from CFMU’s website.

Launched in September 1995, the radio shows features interviews with entrepreneurs, business owners, corporate executives, business authors, media experts and association officers.

The radio show guests discuss the lessons they learned about achieving success in business. They reveal their mistakes, motivation, triumphs and secrets.

The creator and host of “Business in Motion” is entrepreneur, author and motivational speaker, George Torok. Torok is a graduate of McMaster – BA, Economics 1979.
