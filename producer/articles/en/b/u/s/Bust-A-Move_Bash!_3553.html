Bust-A-Move Bash! (formerly known as Bust-A-Move Revolution) is a video game recently announced to be published by Majesco for Nintendo's Wii console. However, as of now nothing else about the game has been revealed, though vice president of marketing, Ken Gold, has stated that they "intend to maximize the intuitive nature of the Wii controller to create a revolutionary offering of the highly popular Bust-A-Move franchise".[1]
