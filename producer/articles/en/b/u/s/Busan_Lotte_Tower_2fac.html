Busan Lotte Tower is a mixed use tower in the southern South Korean city of Busan that is currently under construction. It will be part of the Lotte World 2 complex. It's located next to Nampo-dong station on Busan Subway Line 1

The tower will be 1,621 feet, 494 meters, once it is completed in 2008. It will be 107 stories tall. Ground was broken on the tower on December 18, 2000, but construction did not really begin until January of 2005. Another supertall is proposed in the same city as well.
