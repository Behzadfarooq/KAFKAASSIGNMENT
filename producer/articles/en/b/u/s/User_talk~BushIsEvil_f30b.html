[edit] Username policy

I know that this must be a pretty bad way to start off- and I'm sorry that you've had so many problems so far (Hope I don't add to your frustration!). RickK got the bit about your usename being unacceptable from Wikipedia:Username. If you scroll down to the Inappropriate usernames section, it says:

No inflammatory usernames: Wikipedia does not allow inflammatory or offensive user names. Inflamatory usernames are needlessly discouraging to other contributors, and disrupt and distract from our task of creating an encyclopedia.

The name "BushIsEvil" is inherantly inflammatory and it might anger some republicans (thereby being discouraging to other contributers, and [disrupting/distracting] from our task of creating an encyclopedia). Wikipedia does have a pretty big community, and if you steer clear from personal attacks, vandalism and other 'bad things,' you'll probably find that it's a really great place to be! Anyways, I understand why you might be frustrated. Please consider creating another account (how about "FirstnameLastname"?), and exploring places like Wikipedia:Community portal to find stuff to do. You can also create your own articles- just make sure that it's noteworthy!

Please understand that while we do often have (sometimes very informal) discussions on talk pages, that doesn't mean that Wikipedia is like a forum site. We have a bunch of rules and regulations that can get you into trouble if you don't follow them. If you have any questions, you can come on to the #wikipedia chat on IRC, leave a message at the village pump, or ask me on my talk page. -[[User:Frazzydee|Frazzydee|✍]] 23:32, 3 Sep 2004 (UTC)
