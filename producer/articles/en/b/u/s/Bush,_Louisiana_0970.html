Bush, Louisiana

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Bush, Louisiana is a village in north east St. Tammany Parish, Louisiana, to the south of Sun, Louisiana. Bush is on Louisiana Highway 21 between Covington, Louisiana and Bogalusa, Louisiana at 30,61° N, 89,90° W.

The United States Census Bureau appears not to keep separate data on Bush.
