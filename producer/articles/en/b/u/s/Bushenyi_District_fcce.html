Bushenyi is a district in western Uganda. Like other Ugandan districts, it is named after its 'chief town'. Produce of the district includes coffee, bananas and dairy products. It has a population of 723.427 in 2002.

Bushenyi District was part of the traditional Ankole kingdom.
