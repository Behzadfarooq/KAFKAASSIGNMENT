Bushido Blade has various meanings:

  • The Bushido Blade is a 1981 movie, featuring Richard Boone, Toshiro Mifune, Sonny Chiba, Mako and James Earl Jones.
  • Bushido Blade is a 1997 PlayStation video game featuring one-on-one combat by Squaresoft.
  • Bushido Blade 2 is the 1998 sequel to the 1997 video game, also by Squaresoft.
