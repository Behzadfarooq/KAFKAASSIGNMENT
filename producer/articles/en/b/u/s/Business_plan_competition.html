Business Plan Competitions are typically held by Universities nationwide although there are many competitions held by private organizations.

[edit] Benefits

  • Exposure to panel of judges, most of whom are partners in Venture Capital firms
  • Build up experience in pitching your plan to an audience before the real pitch to a VC
  • Gain advice and feedback from experts in the field
  • Meet fellow entrepreneurs
