[edit] Merge into FirstGroup plc?

I could be wrong, but it doesn't look as though there is enough notable information about the First Cityline service for the possibility of a decent article. Perhaps it deserves a mention on the FirstGroup plc article, but First Cityline is just one of many minor bus services owned by FirstGroup, and I'm not sure that it's significant enough to deserve its own article. Anyone agree? Tschel 20:00, 20 November 2006 (UTC)
