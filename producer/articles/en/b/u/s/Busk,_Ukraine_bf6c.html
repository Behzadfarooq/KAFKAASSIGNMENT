Busk, Ukraine

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Busk (Ukrainian: Буськ; Polish: Busk; Russian: Буск, translit.: Busk) is a city in Lviv Oblast (province) of Ukraine. Population is 8,673 (2001).
