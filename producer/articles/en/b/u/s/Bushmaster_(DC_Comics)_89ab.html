Bushmaster (DC Comics)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Bushmaster is the name of a fictional DC Comics superhero.

[edit] History

Bernal Rojas is a reptilologist who invented gadgets that mimic the abilities of reptiles, past and present. He helped Batman and Robin dismantle a bomb in Venezuela and capture a villain. Along with other Global Guardians, he was brainwashed by the Queen Bee of Bialya. Once he snapped out of it, he returned to the Global Guardians, but was killed by Fain Y'onia.
