Business International is a news program which airs on CNN International. It is anchored by Becky Anderson.

Business International has launched in 2000. It based in London headquarter, from 12noon to 1P.M. and 3 to 4P.M. (each timeslot in CET).

Features business and financial news, interviews and world news, live from Hong Kong, New York (NASDAQ MarketSite), and London Stock Exchange.

World news and weather forecast from London and CNN International's headquarter in Atlanta.

Hong Kong breau about asian business and market result. London Stock Exchange and Nasdaq reports current market news, from 8A.M. (GMT) edition hits first hour of some europe markets, and 10P.M. (GMT) slot, results of NYSE and NASDAQ.

[edit] Currently schedule

  • 8 to 9A.M. (GMT), 9A.M. in CET, 3A.M. in EST, 4P.M. in HK/SIN (Some Europe markets open at 8A.M GMT)
  • 11A.M. to 12noon (GMT) ,12noon in CET, 6A.M. in EST, 7P.M. in HK/SIN
  • 10 to 11P.M. in GMT, 11P.M. in CET, 5P.M. in EST, 4A.M. in HK/SIN (NYSE & NASDAQ closed at 4P.M. EST)
