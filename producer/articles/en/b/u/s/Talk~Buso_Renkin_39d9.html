[edit] Cancellation?

Was this really cancelled? Source: http://boards.gamefaqs.com/gfaqs/genmessage.php?board=2000121&topic=30234612

Here is a simple copy and paste incase the topic gets deleted, it was about mangaka and fan outcry:

Nobuhiro Watsuki, author of Rurouni Kenshin, is the best example of the above that I can think of.

The fate of a certain character in Rurouni Kenshin was completely altered, due to public outcry.

However, his other series, Busou Renkin, ended up being ended prematurely/canceled because he chose not to change the fate of a certain character that time around.

It was cancelled, but was that really the reason? I don't recall seeing that mentioned in any of the fan communities of the series I know of. Just that it was cancelled, but Watsuki was allowed to write a few extra chapters to finish it in the tankoubon. (and which character? I can't think of what character fans would be that upset about, the way the ending did work out...) --ACDragonMaster 22:31, 23 September 2006 (UTC)

[edit] Period and After

What is Buso Renkin Period and Buso Renkin After? They are used several times in the characters page.

--iron2000 12:31, 12 March 2007 (UTC)

That, iron2000, would be the final two chapters of Busou Renkin, Watsuki ended the manga on Shonen Jump! in Buso Renkin Final, but then he was allowed 2 more chapters the following issue and also on the 10th Tankobon. Period explains how Kazuki comes back, and After explains aftermaths of most characters.
