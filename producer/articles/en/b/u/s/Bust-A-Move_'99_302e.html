Bust-A-Move '99 (also known as Bust-A-Move 3 and Bust-A-Move 3 DX) is an arcade-style puzzle video game made by Acclaim Entertainment (which features eight characters from previous Taito games), and was released for the Nintendo 64, Sony PlayStation, Sega Dreamcast, PC, and Game Boy Color.

[edit] Character listing

  • Bub and Bob
  • Jack
  • Luna Luna
  • Marina
  • Musashi
  • Prettio
  • Sonic Blast Man
  • Twinkle
