The Bushwackers


Location Harrison, New Jersey
Division Open Class
Founded 1981
Director Jay Morlot
Championship Titles DCA: 1986, 1988, 1989, 1990, 1992, 1993
Corps Uniform magenta and navy jacket with white and black "piano-key" sash, navy blue pants and shoes, white gauntlets and aussie with white and red plume
