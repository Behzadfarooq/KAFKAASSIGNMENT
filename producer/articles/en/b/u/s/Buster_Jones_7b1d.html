Edward L. "Buster" Jones is an American voice actor.

His roles include Black Vulcan in Super Friends, Blaster in The Transformers, Doc in G.I. Joe: A Real American Hero, Lothar in Defenders of the Earth, and Winston Zeddemore (replacing Arsenio Hall) in The Real Ghostbusters.
