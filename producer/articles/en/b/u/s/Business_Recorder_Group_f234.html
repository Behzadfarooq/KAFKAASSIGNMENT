The Business Recorder Group is one of the most prominent media conglomerates in Pakistan which, in the 40 years of its history, has attained tremendous success and enjoys leadership position in every one of it's ventures.

The extensive reach to the decision makers has given the Business Recorder Group substantial influence and a special relevance to the past, present and future of Pakistan. [edit] Subsidiaries

[edit] Daily Business Recorder

Main article: Business Recorder

Business Recorder is a hugely credible and undoubtedly the most respected financial and business newspaper in the country. It has been the only publication for the last four decades that has such command and grip on providing the business community and the policy makers with valid socio-economic data and analysis on all segments of the global economy.

[edit] AAJ TV

Main article: AAJ TV

AAJ TV is the logical progression of the group's mission to dispense unadulterated information through easily accessible mediums. Extensive planning, spanning over 2 years, has gone into perfecting the organizations operations. Now AAJ TV employs over 350 employees and has fully equipped bureaus in Karachi , Lahore and Islamabad . AAJ TV is the only digital satellite channel to have an Earth Station in Pakistan that broadcasts directly to the AsiaSat satellite with a footprint of over 60 countries. Having an Earth Station provides for exceptional flexibility and control in responding to local and international events. AAJ TV caters to all tastes by providing three well defined programming blocks in the form of News, Current Affairs and Entertainment. Building on the immaculate news reporting experience of the Business Recorder, AAJ TV provides round the clock news coverage from around the world in collaboration with our partner news sources in more than 100 countries. Combining superior news reporting with unmatched analytical strength provided by the most respected social and political commentators in Pakistan accounts for in depth and precise Current Affairs programs. On the Entertainment front, industry veterans are heading the teams and using one of the best and most well equipped production facilities in the country to create ground breaking programs starring the best known faces in television.


[edit] Apex Printry

Apex Printry is arguably the most sophisticated printing facility in the country. Apart from the Government of Pakistan, Apex Printry is the only other organization that has the capability and authority to print secure financial and legal papers in Pakistan . Beyond this achievement, Apex Printry also counts the biggest names of the corporate sector as it's clients.


[edit] e-Dynamics

e-Dynamics is a high tech information solution provider. The innovative information execution by e-dynamics has made Business Recorder's online edition one of the most popular websites of Pakistan, attracting more than 250,000 visitors from over 120 countries. e-dynamics specializes in providing customized electronic marketing engines for growing enterprises along with introducing one of the most comprehensive stock tracking websites of Pakistan- www.khistocks.com. e-dynamics offers various online services to corporate and consumer sectors and is presently exploring the possibilities of extending various other unique online services to its valuable clientele. Since its initiation e-dynamics has been the essential information technology arm for Business Recorder, Apex Printry and Recorder Television Network (AAJ TV). It has maintained its tradition of integrity, dedication and service par excellence that is the mission of the group.


To meet the expectations of its valued customers e-dynamics ensures that their services meet international standards. e-dynamics high tech state of the art facilities and round the clock technical support provides our customers with timely, reliable and comprehensive solutions.

[edit] KHI-Stocks

Business Recorder Group is pleased to announce the launch of www.khistocks.com as the engine for analysis of Pakistani stocks. It has been designed to provide live and archived stock information that will serve as an invaluable resource for stock trading.
