Busalla is a comune (municipality) in the Province of Genoa in the Italian region Liguria, located about 15 km north of Genoa. As of 31 December 2004, it had a population of 5,943 and an area of 17.1 km².[1]

The municipality of Busalla contains the frazioni (subdivisions) Camarza, Sarissola, Inagea, and Salvarezza.

Busalla borders the following municipalities: Crocefieschi, Fraconalto, Isola del Cantone, Mignanego, Ronco Scrivia, Savignone, Vobbia.
