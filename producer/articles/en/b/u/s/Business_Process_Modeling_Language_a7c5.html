Business Process Modeling Language

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Business Process Modeling Language (BPML) is a meta-language for the modeling of business processes, just as XML is a meta-language for the modeling of business data. BPML was a proposed language, but now the BPMI has dropped support for this in favor of BPEL4WS.
