Bionikworld are a world class live performance and production team churning out a rolling blend of raw low slung punk-funk electro.Their live performances are renowned for creating dancefloor mayhem with its high energy sonic assault.

Their highly anticipated debut album Preset 001 was released through Rubber Records/Sony ATV in Australia in August 2003.The first single lifted from the album Schwingin, immediately gained high rotation status on Triple J, (Australias only national radio station). The last single taken from the album, Party Favors was featured on Rolling Stone magazines "Summers Hottest Acts" compilation CD in Feb 2004.

In 2003 Bionikworld released a 12 vinyl EP featuring remixes from some of Australias top producers and DJs. This EP broke precedent by having 3 of its 4 tracks chart consecutively on the ARIA (Australian Recording Industry Association) Club Chart. This was a first for any Australian electronic act. The "Black Dog/Rooting Around" 12" has sold more than 4000 copies in Europe and has received rave reviews from DJ's and media. It has been play listed on "BBC Radio 1" and has received airplay on Kiss FM and many other radio stations in Germany, France, England and Australia.

Bionikworld tracks have been played and charted by DJs across the world from many different genres including;Felix Da Housecat, Erol Alkan, Moguai, Kenny Dope Gonzales, Ewan Pearson and many others.

Bionikworld have played high profile shows and festivals across Australia, Europe, South America and Russia to thousands of people and have supported a diverse range of international acts including Miss Kitten, The Hacker, Jaques Lu Conte, Chemical Brothers, Meat Katie, Speedy J, David Holmes, Audio Bullys, Michael Mayer and more.

Bionikworld are currently living in Barcelona and are weekly residents at the famous "Fake Out" beach parties.
