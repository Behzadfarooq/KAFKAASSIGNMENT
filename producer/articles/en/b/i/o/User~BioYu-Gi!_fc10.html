[edit] Life

BioYu-Gi! was born in 1991 on July 2nd, the day exactly halfway between the beginning and the end of the year. This is made apparent by his ability to harm himself and others unintentionally. This is known as the hedgehog's dilemma His parents are currently divorced, and his father has married a woman named Kim. He has a dog named Celtie and an annoying autistic brother. He is taking all honors classes at school and maintains a GPA of 3.8. He has many interests and hobbies but not many friends, and his dream is to work at Nintendo.

[edit] Interests

BioYu-Gi! has many interests. Among these is anime. His schedule usually includes Toonami and Adult Swim on Cartoon Network, and G4TechTV's Anime Unleashed when it was on. His favorites are Naruto, Inuyasha, and anything with lots of fanservice. Going along eith the anime fandom, BioYu-Gi! has an extensive collection of manga. His favorites are Love Hina, Rurouni Kenshin, and Tsubasa: Reservoir Chronicle. Of course BioYu-Gi! is a big techno-geek, playing video games at least 3 hours a day, 6 on weekends or slow school days. He is an intuitive thinker, so he likes games that challenge him. Games such as Zelda, Mario, and Myst are his favorites. BioYu-Gi! dabbles in XBOX, not so much PS2, but is a huge Nintendo fanboy. He has already pre-ordered the Wii, and The Legend of Zelda: Twilight Princess. He does not like racing games or most shooters, except for Metroid. Of course, everyone likes music, and BioYu-Gi! is no different. His favorites are alternative and rock, with the occasional techno. His favorite bands are Gorillaz, 3 Doors Down, Green Day, and Carbon Leaf.

[edit] Hobbies

BioYu-Gi! has several hobbies as shown above. Of course he loves video games. He owns an XBOX, Gamecube, and Nintendo DS, with a plethora of games for each. He is also an avid drawer, most of which are anime characters which he then incorporates into comic books he sells. He is a fan of television like all teenagers. His favorite channels are Comedy Central, FOX, CBS, and G4TechTV. He also watches Cartoon Network on occasion. BioYu-Gi! is a bibliophile, and reads several books monthly. He only reads fiction, fantasy, and sci-fi though, no realistic junk for him.
