  • Stoat Mustela erminea

  • Grey Seal Halichoerus grypus
  • Common Seal Phoca vitulina

[edit] Lagomorpha (Rabbits and Hares)

  • Mountain Hare Lepus timidus (introduced)
  • European Hare Lepus europaeus (Possibly introduced)
  • European Rabbit Oryctolagus cuniculus (Roman and/or Norman introduction for food)

  • House Mouse Mus domesticus (Introduced)
  • Black Rat Rattus rattus (Introduced)
  • Brown Rat Rattus norvegicus (Introduced)

[edit] Primates

  • Human Homo sapiens sapiens

[edit] Marsupialia

  • Red-necked Wallaby Macropus rufogriseus (Recent, accidental introduction)
