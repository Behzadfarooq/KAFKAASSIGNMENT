[edit] Too many alcohol pages

There are too many pages for fuel alcohol and related subjects, some consolidation would be good. This page in particular could be made into a link to biofuel, and its contents distributed into biofuel, ethanol, methanol, alcohol, alcohol as a fuel, as appropriate.
Jorge Stolfi 05:16, 2 Jul 2004 (UTC)

Agree. I suggest merging to Alcohol fuel because the main point of biologically produced alcohol is fuel than anything else. --Rifleman 82 21:26, 30 April 2006 (UTC)
I'd argue 'alcohol fuel' because you can produce many of these fuels synthetically - biofuel is a subset of alcohol fuel, not the inverse. matt (no id)
