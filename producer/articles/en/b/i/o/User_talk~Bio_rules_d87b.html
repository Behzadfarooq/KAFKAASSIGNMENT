The Wikiproject for ASUE (abbrev. for A Series of Unfortunate Events) is a fairly small project in comparison with other projects such as WP:BOOK. We do not have a portal as the members decided there was no need for one. Simply sign your name on the project page and check the talk page and to do list often. The talk page is probably the most important part of the project as the members do most of our discussion and decision making there. Clamster5 20:16, 28 November 2006 (UTC)
