Bio Zombie is a Hong Kong zombie-comedy film from 1998, starring Jordan Chan. It spoofs George A. Romero's Dawn of the Dead and shows many similarities to Peter Jackson's Braindead

[edit] Plot

Spoiler warning: Plot and/or ending details follow.

Two young men give an apparently dead businessman a soft drink, which is in actuality an experimental biological weapon that turns him into a flesh eating zombie. Returning to the mall in which they work, the man escapes and begins infecting the mall population, forcing a small group of misfits to band together in order to survive.
