Biodynamic Farming & Gardening Association

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Biodynamic Farming & Gardening Association is a United States company which promotes the Biodynamic agriculture system, and has headquarters based outside of Eugene, Oregon.

[edit] History

The Association was formed in 1938 in New York state, and is a 501(c)3 non-profit organization.

[edit] Purpose

The Association has an educational focus and conducts conferences, workshops and seminars; publishes books and a bi-monthly journal, Biodynamics; supports regional, grass-roots membership groups and funds formal research and training institutions.
