The student's association Biotica vid Åbo Akademi r.f. - or just Biotica for short - is the students' association for students at Åbo Akademi University studying biochemistry or bioscience at the Faculty of Mathematics and Natural Sciences.

[edit] History

Biotica was founded in spring 1977.


In 198? the Department of Biochemistry and Pharmacy moved to its new location at the 3rd floor in Biocity, becoming a part of the growing Turku Bio-Valley (fi: Turun Biolaakso).

It's logo is a stylized tRNA molecule surrounded by a circle, inside of which the name of the association and the university it's associated with.
