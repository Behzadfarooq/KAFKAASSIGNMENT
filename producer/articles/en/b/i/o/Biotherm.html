Biotherm is a prestige skin care company whose tag line is "Be Healthy. Be Beautiful." Most Biotherm products contain a patented, mineral-rich spring water concentrate called Vitreoscilla Ferment that is used to rejuvenate the skin. BIOTHERM has purified this element through science, so that it is available in its most active form. Each product is intended to work in sync with the skin's natural biology to leave it radiant and healthy looking.

Its product line includes Source Therapie Superactiv (a skin perfecting serum); Moisture Intense Guard (Aquasource Superserum in Europe and other parts of world) (a moisturizer with Himalayan pink salt and advanced technology to retain moisture); and Celluli-Peel (a slimming complex that recontours and tones dimpled skin).

An extension of Biotherm is BIOTHERM HOMME. The BIOTHERM HOMME product line is the #1 men's line in the world (Euro Monitor 2006). Because men have thicker, firmer, and more elastic skin than women, it would benefit them to use product formulations designed specifically for their skin. BIOTHERM HOMME provides products for all male grooming needs, skin types and skin problems. They also have a well-developed anti-aging line, as well as Hydra-Detox, a line geared towards urban-based customers concerned about environmental tolls on their skin.

BIOTHERM's Make-up line is differentiated by mineral-rich makeup that is actually healthy for your skin to use, and natural-looking shades.

Biotherm is a prestige skincare line under the umbrella of L'Oréal. Its formulations specialize in using ingredients from the sea (such as plankton and kelp).
