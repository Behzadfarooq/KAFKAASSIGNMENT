Template:Biochemical Reaction

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

This page is the template for the Biochemical Reaction template. This template should be used on biological chemical pages to show the reactions that produce and use the compound, especially when part of a major biochemical pathway such as glycolysis.

This template is part of Metabolic Pathways WikiProject.
