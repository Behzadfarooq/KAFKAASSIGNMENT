The primary purpose of this list is to serve as a holding place for the identities of Bioinformatics companies, particularly those for which articles have not yet been created. Three elements would be useful to include: a link to the article, a link to the company on the web (their external homepage), and a brief description of the company.

This is an incomplete list, which may never be able to satisfy certain standards for completeness. Revisions and sourced additions are welcome.

[edit] List of Companies

  • Accelrys
  • Biomax Informatics AG homepage bioinformatics services and solutions
  • Detectorvision homepage providing the deltaMasses package for detection of protein modifications. Life Science IT consulting.
  • Ebiotics
  • Illumina, inc. homepage
  • Korilog homepage, providing KoriBlast a reliable graphical environment dedicated to BLAST data mining.
  • Rosetta Biosoftware homepage
  • Genedata
  • Genomatix, homepage offering tools for prediction of promoters; identification of transcription factor binding sites in genomic DNA
  • Korea Computer Centre Sinhung Company
  • Ocimum Biosolution, homepage offering lab information and knowledge management systems, genomics, proteomics, bioinformatics and custom contract services (India and USA).
  • Tripos Informatics homepage, pioneering breakthroughs in aiding drug discovery through its proprietary informatics and research technologies
