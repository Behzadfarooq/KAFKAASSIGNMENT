This section should not be implemented since the other appearances are not of the game but of the main character. If someone wants to talk about the main character "upa", or his sister, or other games or whatever, then this person has to make another article. This page is only about the game "bio miracle boutte upa" and NOT about Upa itself. Bragador 01:05, 16 January 2006 (UTC)

Making an Upa page would be a waste, it would only end up being merged with this article. The Wai Wai World 2 mention is very necessary since not only Upa appears in it, but two stages based on Bio Miracle Bokette Upa. And the small mention of Upa (w/ Rupa) in the 2 Parodius games isn't going to hurt anything being mentioned on this page. Upa is a part of this game, and mentioning something from this game being in another doesn't make it out of place.Buzda 02:37, 16 January 2006 (UTC)

I disagree, this is not how an encyclopedia works. FOr exemple, you will not see a Mario section in the article on Super Mario 64. If somebody want information on Bio Miracle Bokutte Upa, they are not interested in other games that may have had the same hero in them. What you could do though is make a wai wai world 2 page and mention that the character Upa is from Bio Miracle BOkutte Upa. Or just make an article on Upa. If you really think it would be pointless then it means the information is pointless and not that important. Also since Upa appears in all of these games, you section "other appearances" could be on these pages too since your information is not directly linked to the game, but to the character. Bragador 12:46, 16 January 2006 (UTC)

[edit] Comparisons with other games

I removed the graphical comparisons simply because the readers can see a picture from the game and make their own judgment. Saying it was akin to the graphics of kirby ans super mario bros was a subjective judgment which should not be included in an encyclopedia. Also the reference to doki doki panic health system, though accurate, was simply not useful to help the reader understand the principle. Also note that other games used the same system which makes the comparison less useful (especially since doki doki panic is an obscure game) Bragador 21:34, 26 January 2006 (UTC)
