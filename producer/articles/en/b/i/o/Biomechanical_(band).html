Biomechanical is an English Metal band, founded in London during the Summer of 2001.

The band takes its name from the film Independance Day witch includes mention of biomechanical.

John K. also sang with Balance of Power, but he decided to leave that band and dedicate full-time to Biomechanical. The Biomechanical-Sound is determined by hard Riffs and the complex structure of the songs. The vocals are partly distorted and bombastic-orchestral-like Synthesizer-Sounds, created by guitarist Jamie Hunt's use of a MIDI guitar, help to create the complex sound of Biomechanical. Jamie Hunt currently teaches guitar to students at the Secondary Education institution Hardenhuish situated in Chippenham, Wiltshire when not busy with Biomechanical. Matt C is often heard but seldom seen as he sits behind his drums

In 2006, the band toured Europe with Exodus.

Metal Hammer magazine tipped them as being one of the bands to watch for in 2007, which promises to bring a tour with 3 Inches of Blood, and a new album[citation needed].

The tour with 3 Inches of Blood, went ahead in March 2007
