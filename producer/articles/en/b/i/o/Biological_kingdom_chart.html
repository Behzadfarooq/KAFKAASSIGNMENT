The Biological Kingdom Chart presents the biological kingdoms in a graphic format, classifying kingdoms according to three factors. The first classification is according to cellular structure - either prokaryotic or eukaryotic. The second is by cellular organization - unicellular or multi-cellular (with the possible addition of societal). The final classification is by trophic function - with autotrophs consisting of chemosynthetic and photosynthetic varieties, and heterotrophs mobile and sessile.
