[edit] Imortal Sadness

Hey, Biozaard! I am writing to suggest a few improvements to the Imortal Sadness article that you have been developing. I can see that you're quite a new Wikipedian (welcome!), so the first thing to do I would suggest is to read carefully through the Wikipedia Tutorial. This should give you the basic idea of the editing standards of Wikipedia. After that, you can always refer to the Editing Wikipedia help section for more elaborate presentation.

After that I suggest you take a look at several other articles on similar topic - basically, any article about a musical band would be great. I recommend looking at major bands (e.g. The Prodigy, Red Hot Chili Peppers), because they usually get a lot of attention and hence are usually kept in line with the standards. After reading the articles, you can see how they are formatted, and what is different to the article you are editing. You can then try and format your article in a somewhat similar way. If you need help, do not hesitate to ask, either me at my talk page, or just put a {{helpme}} tag on your talk page along with the question, and someone will soon help you!

Finally, just a few tips: it's usually good to sign your edits at talk pages by adding four tildes (~~~~) or simply pressing the "signature" button on toolbar. Leaving an edit summary in the box just above the "Submit page" button is VERY helpful for other users to track changes to the page. Section headings do not need to be linkified, whereas article content which may have articles on them (e.g. Techno) are good candidates for linkification. Again, read help on editing - it's extensive and very interesting!

Best luck with editing! Gimlei 12:01, 4 November 2006 (UTC)
