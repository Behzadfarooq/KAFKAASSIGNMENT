Hello. I am the bigloopycookie. I am from Dayton, Ohio, but currently live in Chattanooga, Tennessee. I was born in Bell County, Texas in 1983. I went to high school in Centerville, Ohio, and graduated from Centerville High School in 2001. I have been to many different colleges including Wright State University, Northern Kentucky University, Cincinnati State and Technical College, and lately Hillsborough Community Collegeand will attend the University of Tennessee- Chattanooga..

I enjoy sports, and I was a cheerleader in the 2001-2002 school year at Wright State University. I currently enjoy following sports, and bridge research. My favorite bridges are: 1.) New River Gorge Bridge - Fayette County, West Virginia 2.) Roebling Suspension Bridge - Cincinnati, Ohio 3.) Main Street Bridge - Jacksonville, Florida 4.) Laughery Creek Bridge - Dearborn County, Indiana 5.) Newport Southbank Bridge - Cincinnati, Ohio

You can find my bridge webpage at www.flickr.com/photos/jamesadorno
