Big Jimmy Babysits is an episode of the U.S. television sitcom Yes, Dear.

[edit] Plot

Spoiler warning: Plot and/or ending details follow.

Jimmy and Christine come home from a night out. Jimmy tries to cheat the babysitter, Sarah, on her money. Big Jimmy is out visiting because he wants to see more of the grandchildren. Big Jimmy wants to take Dominic and Logan to Six Flags. Big Jimmy asks Kim and Greg if Sam can go. They’ll say they will think about it. When Big Jimmy leaves, Jimmy asks them if Sam can go and they say no. Kim and Greg tell Jimmy that they don’t trust his father.

Christine tells Jimmy that she doesn’t really trust his father. Christine says she doesn’t trust him because Jimmy was in the ER ten times before he was eight during Big Jimmy’s watch. Jimmy remembers all the things that happened to him and he has second thoughts as well. Jimmy and Christine try to figure a way to break it to Big Jimmy. Greg tells them to send Sarah with Big Jimmy. They tell Big Jimmy that Sarah is Dominic’s friends sister. He pays off Dominic and Logan to go along with the story.

Jimmy and Christine play a dirty game together, but decide to have sex instead. As they are heading out to the guesthouse, Big Jimmy returns with the kids. Big Jimmy wants to take Dominic and Logan to the zoo. The next day, Jimmy and Christine are worried because Big Jimmy hasn’t returned yet with the kids. They check the answering machine and find a message from Sarah saying that she can’t make it and a message from Big Jimmy saying that he can’t find the kids.

Jimmy and Christine rush to the zoo and find everything is fine. Big Jimmy tells them he handled everything well. Dominic remembers numerous occasions where he had to save Logan’s life because Big Jimmy wasn’t paying attention. Jimmy tells his father he is sorry and he says that he is taking the kids fishing tomorrow. Dominic tells Jimmy that they need to talk.
