Big Island

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The term Big Island can be:

  • a nickname for the island of Hawaii in the Hawaiian Islands
  • Big Island, Ontario, the name of a place in Ontario, Canada
  • Big Island, Nunavut in Canada
  • a literal translation of the surname of Naoto Ohshima, the creator of the Sonic the Hedgehog character
  • Big Island, an island over Lake Chemong.
  • a name of a popular vacationing island on Lake Minnetonka
