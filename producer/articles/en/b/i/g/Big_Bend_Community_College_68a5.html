Big Bend Community College

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Big Bend Community College
Established 1962
Type Community college
President Dr. Bill Bonaudi
Students 5,400
Location Moses Lake, Washington, United States
Campus Rural
Website http://www.bigbend.edu/

Big Bend Community College is a two-year college in Moses Lake, Washington. It offers associate's degrees in academic and vocational fields.
