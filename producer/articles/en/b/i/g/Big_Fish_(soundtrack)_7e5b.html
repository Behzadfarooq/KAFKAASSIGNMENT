Big Fish is the soundtrack, on the Sony Classical label, of the 2003 Academy Award- and Golden Globe-nominated film Big Fish starring Ewan McGregor, Albert Finney, Billy Crudup, Helena Bonham Carter and Jessica Lange. The original score and songs were composed by Danny Elfman.

The album was nominated for the Academy Award and the Golden Globe Award for Best Original Score.
