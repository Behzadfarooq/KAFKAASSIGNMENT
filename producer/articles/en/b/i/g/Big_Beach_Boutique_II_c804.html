Big Beach Boutique II is a mixed compliation album containing some of the songs which were played by Fatboy Slim and Midfield General in a live performance on Brighton Beach on July 13, 2002. There is also a DVD release which features the mix set performed by Fatboy Slim called Big Beach Boutique II - The Movie.

Track 6 contains the spoken introduction to Fatboy Slim's set.
