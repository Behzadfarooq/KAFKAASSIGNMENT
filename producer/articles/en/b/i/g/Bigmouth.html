Bigmouth / Eclipse is the second single released by Lemon Interupt, who are also known as Underworld, originally released in 1992. The track failed to make it into the UK Charts when released.

[edit] Notes

  • All Lemon Interupt releases were produced & mixed by Underworld.
  • The text 'I WAS A FUDGE HEAD ELVIS' is etched into the run-out groove of the "Bigmouth" side.
  • The text '...BORN TO BE ALIVE' appears in small print on the "Eclipse" side.
  • Elements of "Bigmouth" were later included in "Dark & Long".

[edit] Appearances

  • "Bigmouth" was previously only available as a single, until it was released worldwide in 2003 as part of the Underworld 1992-2002 Anthology.
  • "Bigmouth" appears on Progressive House Classics (FIRMCD 01, 1996).
  • An edit of "Bigmouth" appears on The Journey (Mixmag Free CD, June 1998).
