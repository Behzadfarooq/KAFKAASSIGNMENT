The Worst of Deja Voodoo Big Pile of Mud Live at The Backstage Club, Helsinki Finland


Big Pile of Mud was the fifth LP release by the Montreal-based Canadian garage-rock band Deja Voodoo. This would be their last release recorded in a studio. There is also a Greek version released by Didi Records that includes 2 bonus tracks: "Waiting for the man" a Velvet Underground cover and "I wish that cat would shut up".

[edit] Personnel

  • Tony Dewald, drums
  • Gerard van Herk, guitar/voice
