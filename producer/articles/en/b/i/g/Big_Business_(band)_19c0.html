Big Business is a stoner rock/sludge metal band currently signed to Hydra Head Records. Originally from the Seattle area, the band relocated to Los Angeles in February, 2006.

Big Business is a two piece band composed of Jared Warren of Karp and The Whip, and Coady Willis of Murder City Devils.

Big Business is currently touring as the opening act for, and as members of The Melvins, to support their new album, "(A) Senile Animal" on the Double-Drumming Rock for Peace tour. Jared Warren is filling in on bass and Coady Willis is playing drums along with Dale Crover.
