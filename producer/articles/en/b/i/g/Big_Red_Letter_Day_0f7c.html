Big Red Letter Day is a 1993 album by Buffalo Tom. The leadoff track, "Sodajerk," is probably the band's most well-known. It not only was featured on the soundtrack for the short-lived television series My So-Called Life, but also has been featured in commercials for Nike and Pontiac. "Late at Night" was also used in an episode of My So-Called Life.
