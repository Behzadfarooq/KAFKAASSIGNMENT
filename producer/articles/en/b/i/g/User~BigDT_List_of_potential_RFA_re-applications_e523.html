This is a list of individuals who have previously been considered for adminship, but who were not promoted. You may find this list helpful when seeking candidates to nominate for adminship.

By no means does inclusion on this list mean that someone would be a good admin, nor does exclusion mean that he or she would be a bad one. The selection criteria for inclusion in this list are as follows:

  • Two months have elapsed since the previous RFA.
  • The user is still an active Wikipedia user.
  • The prevailing opinion was that the candidate was suitable, but lacked experience in key areas, did not have a desired edit count, had low edit summary usage, or had other issues that appear to have been one-time mistakes rather than a continuing pattern of behavior

Beyond that, these names have not been vetted. Please examine the contributions of any user you wish to nominate.

The source for included names is User:NoSeptember/List of failed RfAs (Chronological). The number of edits in the table is as of early February 2007.

  • If you ask a user about adminship and they decline, please strike their name so that effort will not be duplicated.
  • You can help by expanding this list. Please go in reverse chronological order or make a note where you leave a gap so that there won't be duplicated effort.
