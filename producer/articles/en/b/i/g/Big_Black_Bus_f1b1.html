Big Black Bus is a cassette by Marilyn Manson and the Spooky Kids, recorded and released promotionally in summer, 1990.

[edit] Track listing

Side A

  1. "White Knuckles"
  2. "My Monkey"
  3. "Strange Same Dogma"
  4. "Red (In My) Head"

Side B of this cassette contains messages left by fans of the band on the answering machine which operated as the "Marilyn Manson Family Intervention Hotline" until the mid-1990s.
