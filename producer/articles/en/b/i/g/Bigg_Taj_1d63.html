Bigg Taj is a human beatboxer from Scotland UK

Started beatboxing at around 16 years old after hearing Scratch and Rahzel of the hip hop group "The Roots"

Bigg Taj was a finalist in the first UK beatbox championships in 2005 where he lost in the 1st round...

for more information go to http://www.myspace.com/biggtaj
