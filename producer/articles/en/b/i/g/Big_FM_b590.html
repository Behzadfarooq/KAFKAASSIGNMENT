Big FM

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Big FM is India's nationwide private FM Radio station. It is broadcasting at 92.7 Megahertz. It is owned by Indian businessman Anil Ambani.

Currently it covers 11 cities. This is the only private FM Radio which is being brodacasting from Srinagar. It announced to invest of Rs. 400 crore dedicated to transmission equipment, infrastructure and licensing, the proposed network will be the largest ever.

Its main tagline is Suno Sunao, Life Banao.

  • BangloreTagline : Keli Kelisi Life Nimmadagisi

  • Chennai Tagline : Kekkalaam Pesalaam Life Kondaadalaam
