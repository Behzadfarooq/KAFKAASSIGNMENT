[edit] Birthdays

I haven't added these because I can't be certain, but I found the birthdays of the housemates:

Shannon Cleary 8 Dec 1970, Alex Christie 21 May 1971, Turkan Aksoy 12 Dec 1971, Nathan Morris 19 June 1976, Keiran Tanner 4 May 1977, Jessica Hardy 12 June 1977, Brodie Young 6 Aug 1977, Aaron Benton 25 Nov 1977, Peter Corbett 28 Oct 1978, Sarah Kearney 7 Jan 1979, Nicole Dickmann 29 Mar 1979, Katrina Miani 9 April 1980, Mirabai Peart 19 March 1981, Damien Hoo 19 March 1981, Marty (Nathan Martin) 6 Feb 1982

I don't have their birthdays, but the ages of the other three potential intruders on entry to the house (in 2002) were:

Nicki Cahill 31 (so her birthyear should be circa 1971), Hayley 24 (birthyear circa 1978), Will 22 (birthyear circa 1980).

Those ages are sourced from http://us.geocities.com/bigbrotherzoneau/standbys.html

(Might not be reliable, but it's the best I could find). The birthdays of most of the other housemates came from IMDb, and the rest (Turkan, Jess, Peter and Katrina) from:

http://geocities.com/sacchi_80

(Again, it might not be reliable, but it confirmed the birthdays given on the IMDb pages for the other housemates).
