The Big '80s is an all '80s music channel on SIRIUS Satellite Radio channel 8 and DISH Network channel 6008. The Big '80s features all four living original MTV VJs. The Big 80's is SIRIUS's highest rated music channel.

What they play: Madonna, Prince, Bryan Adams. The Police, John Mellencamp, Huey Lewis & The News and more.
