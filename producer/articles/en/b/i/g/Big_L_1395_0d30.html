Big L is a United Kingdom based radio station broadcasting via satellite, mediumwave (using a transmitter in the Netherlands) and the Internet. The station is named after the 1960s offshore pirate radio station Radio London which was also known as Big L. Many of its jingles are derivative of the original Radio London jingles.

Using the slogan "The Heart and Soul of Rock and Roll", the station claims to broadcast the best music from the past 50 years. Presenters as of 2006 include David Hamilton, Ian Damon, Mike Read and Ray Anderson.
