Big Bumpin' is an advergaming title from Burger King. On November 19, 2006, Burger King started selling it for an additional $3.99 USD ($4.99 in Canada)with any value meal. It is one of three titles released by Burger King. Due to its immense popularity, the game continues to be sold in certain areas into 2007.[citation needed] Developed by Blitz Games (Blitz Arcade division).

[edit] Overview

Big Bumpin' is based on bumper cars, featuring characters familiar to Burger King, like Subservient Chicken and Brooke Burke. Obstacles in the arenas ("theme parks") include pits and saws. Players can gain power-ups which allow them to deal specific damages. The arenas include the Ice Box, Broiler, King's Court, The Deep, and Monsoon of Doom. The game supports up to four players using split-screen mode, as well as up to eight using Xbox Live.
