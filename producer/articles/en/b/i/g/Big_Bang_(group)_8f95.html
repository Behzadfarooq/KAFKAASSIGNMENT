Big Bang (빅뱅) is a Korean hip hop group comprised of five members. Big Bang made its debut at the YG Family concert, held on August 19th at the Gymnastics Arena in Seoul Olympic Park. Originally with six members, one was dropped in the ninth episode of their documentary episodes. Big Bang's debut album was created by the members themselves. Although all members will be involved in promotion, they will not all participate in each song; certain members are vocals, while others are rappers. They are considered the new face of YG Family.

A documentary was broadcast on GOM Player, which increased their popularity[citation needed].

[edit] Advertisements

In addition to a 6-month contract with "Skoolooks", a Korean clothing brand, Big Bang was also awarded a contract with FILA for three months. Both are reported to be worth 300 million won each.
