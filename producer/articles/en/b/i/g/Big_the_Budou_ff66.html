Big the Budou (ビッグ・ザ・武道, Big the Budou?) was a character in Yudetamago's manga and anime series Kinnikuman. His true name and identity is Neptune King (ネプチューンキング, Neptune King?).

[edit] Story

[edit] Prehistory

Once the Choujin Tournaments were dominated by a powerful being, the legendary Neptune King. However, he became disgusted as the competition shifted from strong vs. weak death matches into a commericalized sport. He then went into hibernation under in the Thames River. He remained there for millions of years until he was discovered by a Choujin named Kenkaman. Kenkaman was trying to commit suicide by jumping into the river because he couldn't find any opponents strong enough to defeat him (even Robin Mask was too easy for him). Together, the two of them plotted to bring the Choujin competitions back to its bloody roots. The Neptune King gave Kenkaman his Neptune Mask, making him Neptuneman, and created a new identity for himself- Big the Budou.
