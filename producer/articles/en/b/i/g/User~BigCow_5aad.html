[edit] Me

Computer Science student with an interest in gamng, music, movies, and books. In gaming I enjoy a good adventure game and RPG along with a little real time strategy. In music I enjoy a lot of classical stuff, a good deal of jazz, and some popular stuff I consider to be clever. In movies I enjoy science fiction and stuff which makes you think in general along with a quality comedy, and in books I am interested in economics, fantasy, and other various topics.

I've been working on a few game-related Wikibooks: Quest for Glory, and Final Fantasy VI, and Chrono Trigger. If you feel like helping out let me know, there's a lot that could be done. Recently, these have been transwiki'd over to Stategy Wiki, http://strategywiki.net

I have about 450 edits at the moment.

[edit] What I've been up to lately

  • Fixing disambiguation pages or pages where the article a term points to is less popular than some sub page, for example Casablanca vs Casablanca (film).
  • If I see a page I like I'll click edit once, run the google spellchecker from the toolbar, and re-submit with corrections.
  • Created and worked on a few minor articles on topics I found interesting but weren't listed on Wikipedia, Chicken reel most recently for example.
