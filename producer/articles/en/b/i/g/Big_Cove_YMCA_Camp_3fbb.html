[edit] About

Big Cove YMCA Camp is a historic wilderness camp dedicated to providing youth with opportunities to develop leadership skills, enhance their self esteem and foster an appreciation of the natural environment in a safe, respectful community. Founded in 1889, it is North America's oldest residential camp. It is located in Merigomish Harbour in Pictou County, Nova Scotia, Canada.

The camp runs programs for campers aged 7 to 17, divided into the follow sections: Juniors, Seniors, and Leadership.

[edit] Juniors

(one week and two weeks)

For campers aged 7 to 11, Big Cove provides a fun and welcoming atmosphere to make new friends and try new things. Campers will go on an outtrip each week, typically one night, either by canoeing or hiking.

[edit] Seniors

(one week and two weeks)

Senior campers aged 12 to 17 are given the chance to challenge themselves with new opportunities such as the high ropes course, longer outtrips of two to four nights, and the zipline.

[edit] Leadership 1 & 2

(one month)

The Leadership program at Big Cove is one of its kind in Atlantic Canada. In Leadership 1, campers are taught extensive wilderness skills through a 10-day ocean canoe trip to Port Hood, Cape Breton, as well as a 5-day intensive hike in Cape Chignecto. Participants will hone in on their kayaking, canoeing, and leadership skills.

Leadership 2 is a program designed for successful graduates of the L1 program. Participants are able to work directly with campers and counsellors as a shadow for two and five days, respectively. Many participants go on to become Big Cove staff.

[edit] Interest Groups

Each week campers select two interest groups to attend in the morning. They include: boating, archery, arts and crafts, traditional sports, drama, nature nuts, swimming, adventurers, and much more.

[edit] Staff

Big Cove is renowned for its knowledgeable and dedicated staff. All are trained in First Aid & CPR, have a minimum of Bronze Cross, and participate in a week of training prior to the summer season. Many are Big Cove veterans and Leadership 2 graduates. Big Cove also has the pleasure of hosting gap year students every spring and summer from various parts of the world such as the UK and Australia.
