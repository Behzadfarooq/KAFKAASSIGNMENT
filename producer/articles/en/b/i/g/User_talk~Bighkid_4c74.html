Just to let you know, the route shields in the "Major junctions" section of any infobox is supposed to be set as 20px. On the Chicago Skyway, Indiana Toll Road, and the Ohio Turnpike pages, the shields were 25px. Also, a wikilink is supposed to point to the other route; the shield alone is not sufficient. Check out Pennsylvania Turnpike for a infobox on a turnpike page, and if you edit any more infoboxes, please keep this in mind. Thanks! V60 22:12, 14 December 2006 (UTC)
