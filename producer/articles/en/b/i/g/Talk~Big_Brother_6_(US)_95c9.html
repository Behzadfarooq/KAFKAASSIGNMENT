[edit] Nerd Herd vs. The Friendship

The so-called "Nerd Herd" alliance was actually much more universally referred to as "The Friendship". They called themselves that, Julie called them that, and on the internet that was the more common name for the alliance. "Nerd Herd" was the term Howie and the Sovereign Six used to refer to them. The article should call the alliance by the name they called themselves. It should be changed in the article. --Mezaco 17:13, 30 August 2006 (UTC)

Actually 6 houseguests (April, Beau, Eric, Ivette, Jennifer, and Maggie) called them the Friendship, and 8 houseguests called them the Nerd Herd (Ashlea, Howie, James, Janelle, Kaysar, Michael, Rachel, Sarah). Also the internet fans for the most part called them the Nerd Herd (see Joker's Updates's archive of BB6, and Hamster Watches's archive as well). Comedy240 15:48 2 September 2006 (UTC)
