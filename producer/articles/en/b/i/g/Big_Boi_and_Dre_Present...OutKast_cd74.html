Big Boi And Dre Present...OutKast is the first OutKast greatest hits LP, released by LaFace/Arista Records in December 2001. The compilation takes five tracks from Southernplayalisticadillacmuzik, one from ATLiens, and three each from the break-out albums Aquemini and Stankonia. In addition, there are three new tracks: "The Whole World", featuring Killer Mike, "Movin' Cool (The After Party)" featuring Joi, and "Funkin' Around". The album reached platinum status and hit #18 on the Billboard album charts.
