Big Muddy Records

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Big Muddy Records is a recently-formed independent record label based in St. Louis, Missouri. Bands on the label include 7 Shot Screamers, Casey Reid, The Vultures, and Johnny O and the Jerks, all bands from St. Louis. It primarily covers genres of rock, blues, and rockabilly in its various forms.

Notably, the label released the 7 Shot Screamers' third studio album, 7 Shot Screamers In Wonderland in August of 2006.

On January 22, 2007 it was announced that Big Muddy Artists Casey Reid and the 7 Shot Screamers won the Riverfront Times' "Best of 2006" awards for Songwriter and Best Local Act Gone National, respectively.
