Big Bend State Recreation Area

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Boating on the Colorado River

Big Bend State Recreation Area is a Nevada state park in the Colorado River Valley in southern Clark County, Nevada. Located just south of the town of Laughlin, Big Bend is Nevada's newest state park, created in 1996. It is in the Southern Nevada Region of Nevada State Parks.

The recreation area, located immediately across the Colorado River from Bullhead City, Arizona, offers camping, fishing, picnicking and a boat launch.
