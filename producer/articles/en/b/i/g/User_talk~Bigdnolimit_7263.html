[edit] David Jesse Carter

David Jesse Carter sr, (1987-0000) is the third of Davids in his family but is not a jr. David Jesse Carter was born in South Phoenix in Arizona. Not much is know of David short history, but the specifics on were he attended school and why he is a person of great importance. David Carter first attended Rainbow Valley Daycare as a child at the age of 3 to 4. David then started kindergarten at Rio Vista Middleschool in the Heart of South Phoenix. David also attended Rio Vista to the third grade, which then went under a name change to Cesar Cavez Middleschool. David attended Cesar Chavez up to the 5th grade, which then transferred over to NFL YET academy, a private school in South Phoenix. David was an A+ student all the way up until his move to NFL YET where he was introduced to girls and troublemakers.Mr. Carter became very popular and well-known by all just as he was at Cesar Chavez. After the 6th grade, David seemed to have gotten his life back on track and started up again as an A+ student. David went to South Mountain High School the following year after a tough decision between South Mountain and Brophy High. David also has members of his family who are well known. The first being his uncle Sam Carter was known as one of the most well know "drug dealers of the south side." Although there is proof as to being related to Former President Jimmy Carter, the relation has been denied by all family members. David has said to have become a very quiet person when his after the death of his beloved Grandmother Jackie Marie Carter. Now here comes the twist to the man we know as David Jesse Carter. David Carter is known to have an account with millions of dollars put away for himself and family. As a kid David was blessed with hand me down stocks and a great fortune from an unknown source. Why David lives in South Phoenix and lives like the rest of us is something we would all like to know. An interview was done on him in which he claims "I have friends and I would like a girlfriend without having the money being involved, I spend what I want when I want, but it doesnt make me and I at least want my future to be secured, not just by the money I have but with my skills and what I can do, Im just proving that I can live without the money." David currently works and attends The University of Phoenix. David currently runs Carter Networking Solutions and A music production Company under the name Third Street Productionz, soon to be Deadly Soundz. Davids netwoth is said to be somewhere at about 32 to 33 million. Currently he is still living in South Phoenix with close ties to California and Texas ans has no plans on leaving South Phoenix anytime soon.
