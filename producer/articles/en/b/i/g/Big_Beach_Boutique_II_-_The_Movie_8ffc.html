Big Beach Boutique II: The Movie
DVD by Fat Boy Slim
Released 29 October, 2006
Genre Dance music
Length 90 min
Label Capitol Records (EMI)

Big Beach Boutique II: The Movie is a music DVD featuring a concert on the beach at Brighton, UK by musician Fat Boy Slim and other artists. It was recorded on July 13, 2002. 250,000 fans turned up for the huge beach party and concert. It was also released as an album, Big Beach Boutique II.
