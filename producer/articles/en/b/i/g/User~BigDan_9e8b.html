Hello, my name is Danny J. Palmer, also known as Big Dan, and I'm 22-years old and currently residing in my hometown of Scone, New South Wales. I was a Information Technology student, having done 3 years of my course at Charles Sturt University at Bathurst, New South Wales.

My main interests are television, radio, newspapers, rugby league, soccer, motor racing, basketball, music and movies. I also help edit or create topics on subjects I have been involved with, or on places where I've lived.

I'm not perfect :-), so please, if you find an article of mine that needs work, please feel free to fix it up and let me know...I'm more than willing to learn!

Also...even though I've listed myself as an Canadian wikipedian (since I like to edit articles on Canadian television and the Royal Canadian Air Farce)...I've never really been to Canada, although I'd like to one day!

Cheers, BigDan
