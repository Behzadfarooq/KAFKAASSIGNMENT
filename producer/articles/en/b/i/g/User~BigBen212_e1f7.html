My real name is Ruben Schönefeld. I was born December 2nd 1978 in Oldenburg, Lower Saxony, Germany, Europe. As of September 28, 2005 I live in Midvale, Utah. After I received my Green Card I started to work again for the Utah Department of Transportation, where I already worked before for an internship.

I found out about wikipedia after I saw a report about it on tv. My first edit has been the starting of a new article about the airport in Bremen March 3rd 2004.

Other interests besides spending way too much time on the internet or doing other things on the computer are basketball, photography and music. I like to travel and besides a few places in Europe, I've been all over Utah and a few other states (Idaho, Wyoming, Montana, Arizona, Texas, Nevada, Washington, Oregon, New York, Colorado).
