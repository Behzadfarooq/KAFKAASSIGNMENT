[edit] Welcome

Salam Big brother is always watching! Welcome to Wikipedia! Thank you for your contributions. You may benefit from following some of the links below, which will help you get the most out of Wikipedia. If you have any questions you can ask me on my talk page, or place {{helpme}} on your talk page and ask your question there. Please remember to sign your name on talk pages by clicking or by typing four tildes "~~~~"; this will automatically produce your name and the date. Finally, please do your best to always fill in the edit summary field. Don't worry about following lists of links. It's not necessary to read all of them immediately. You can get information whenever you need. Happy editing! Sa.vakilian(t-c)17:05, 22 March 2007 (UTC)
