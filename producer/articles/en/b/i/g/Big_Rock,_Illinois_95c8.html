Big Rock, Illinois

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

For other uses of "Big Rock", see Big Rock (disambiguation).

Big Rock is a small village located at 41.759308 N, 88.537617 WGR1 in Kane County, Illinois located approximately fifty miles due west of Chicago, Illinois. It is between the towns of Hinckley and Sugar Grove.

As of the 2000 census, the village had a total population of 658, although it wasn't incorporated until July 26th, 2001.

[edit] School District

Big Rock Township is a part of the Hinckley-Big Rock CUSD#429. Recently, the school board, in spite of overwhelming public opposition, voted to turn Big Rock Elementary school in to a junior high. Some residents of Big Rock have attempted to convince them to overturn this decision, but have so far been unsuccessful.[citation needed]
