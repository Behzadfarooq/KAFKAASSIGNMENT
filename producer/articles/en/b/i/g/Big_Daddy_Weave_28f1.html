Big Daddy Weave is a Christian rock band composed of Mike Weaver (lead singer), Jeremy Redmon, Jeff Jones and Joe Shirk. Their popular songs include: Every Time I Breathe, Let it Rise, In Christ, and Without You. They are signed to Fervent Records.

[edit] Tour

In both 2006 and 2007, Big Daddy Weave toured with Mark Schultz on his Broken and Beautiful Tour.

[edit] Trivia

  • All four members of the band are alumni of University of Mobile, where they met.
  • The band name came from a combination of the lead singer's last name, and the fact that he has above average weight.
