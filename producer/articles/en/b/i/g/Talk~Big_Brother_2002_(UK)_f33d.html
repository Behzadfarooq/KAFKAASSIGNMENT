[edit] Section on Jade

The entire section on Jade includes no proper citation. It's also very lengthy and to that end appears biased. There may be a need for a 'Jade' section as she acheived her notoriety from this series of big brother (and is doubtlessly one of the most famous contestants ever), but based on conjecture, hearsay, and without citation most of this section of the article seems unnecessary and needlessly negative. I'm sorry, cos I'm a wikipediaNOOB, but this still doesn't seem right. As there will be many more people visiting this page than normal, I think it's right that it is corrected. I've copied the section I disagree with below with some attempts at annotation (all in bold) :-

Jade Goody was involved in bullying fellow housemate Sophie Pritchard.

Who else was involved? Why are they not mentioned? Citation?

Jade later told Sophie it was because she was quiet and should have stood up for herself, blaming Sophie herself for her own bullying.

This is essentially true to my recollection, but surely needs more elaboration/citation to be wikified.

This led tabloid newspapers to report Jade negatively. However the week that she was nominated by every other housemate for eviction, one tabloid (The Sun, July 7th 2002) claimed that Jade was being portrayed differently on the nightly edited highlights programme, and that fellow nominee Adele was being made to look much worse by comparison.

From my recollection, true enough, but needs definitive citation. It's also worth remembering that at that point The Sun was the pro BB paper (as opposed to the mirror). I don't know what can be gained from that in a wikipedia way, just putting it out there. (ducks)

However, this was denied by a spokeswoman for Channel 4.

Citation

Despite all this Goody enjoyed considerable success as a public personality after the end of the series, admitting in Sky One's show Why reality TV is good for you that she is a millionairess.

I really do feel that if there is to be an extensive section on Jade like this, there should be sections to reflect on the other contestants in the show. As Jade acheived her fame in BB, it seems obvious to me that the other contestants played a part in that, and should somehow be noted (if only with direct relevance to Jade) on this page.

Sorry if I'm a wikipain, only trying to help. This is the only series of BB I watched in absolute totality. I slept when they slept, and awoke when they did. I'm bound to be biased (as a viewer) but I feel I can offer something to this article and I hope I have with my comments above.Harveymuso 00:14, 19 February 2007 (UTC)
