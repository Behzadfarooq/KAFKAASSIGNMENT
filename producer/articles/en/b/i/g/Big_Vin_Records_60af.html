Big Vin Records is record label, based in Arlington, Texas, United States, that was formed in February 2006 by Metal drummer and producer, Vinnie Paul. Big Vin Records has a distribution deal with Fontana Records. Vinnie became famous for being in the band Pantera and later Damageplan with his brother, the late Dimebag Darrell. He started the label a little over a year after his brother was murdered on stage while performing with Damageplan in support of their album New Found Power on December 8, 2004. His main objective is to preserve his brothers legacy and continue to release his recorded material.
