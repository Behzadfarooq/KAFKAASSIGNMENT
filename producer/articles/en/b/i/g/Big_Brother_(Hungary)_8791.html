Big Brother (Hungary)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Big Brother is one of the first reality shows. This is a very popular television program from Endemol (television production company). Endemol is the most important supplier of TV2.

The Big Brother Hungary was running on TV2.

Hosts: Claudia Liptai & Attila Till (Liptai Claudia & Till Attila)
