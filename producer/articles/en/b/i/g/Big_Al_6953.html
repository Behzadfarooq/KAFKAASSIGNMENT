Big Al

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Big Al can refer to:

  • "Big Al", a specimen of Allosaurus fragilis featured in the television documentary The Ballad of Big Al.
  • Big Al, an Australian Comedian.
  • Big Al is also the name of the elephant mascot of University of Alabama athletics teams. (AL being the postal abbreviation for Alabama).
  • Al Jefferson, a professional Basketball player on the Boston Celtics in the National Basketball Association.
  • Big Al, a character in the Ratchet & Clank series.
  • Big Al (book), a picture book by Andrew Clements. A sequel, Big Al and Shrimpy, also exists.
