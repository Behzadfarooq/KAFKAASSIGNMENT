This article is about the Tom Waits album. For the Peter Gabriel song, see Big Time (song).

Big Time is a live album of Tom Waits performances, released in 1988 on Island Records.

A movie of the same title was also released. The movie was part concert movie, part play, with elements taken from the Franks Wild Years play.
