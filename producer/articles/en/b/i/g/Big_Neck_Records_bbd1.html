Big Neck Records

Big Neck Records is an independent record label founded and operated by Bart. It was started in Buffalo, New York as an off-shoot of the Sanctuary nightclub. After being run out of town, Big Neck relocated to Reston, Virginia where it continues operating today. The label focuses mostly on garage, punk, and rockabilly acts. Big Neck's roster includes bands like The Blowtops, The Baseball Furies, The Trailerpark Tornados, Lost Sounds, The Tyrades, Concubine Forming, and The Ponys.
