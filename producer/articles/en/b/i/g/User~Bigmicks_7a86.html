If you can post a pic of what bits you have removed, I can tell you what's missing. It's common for them to come apart. Use a magnet to remove the spring & the other half of the notched tensioner. To re-assemble, you need to lock the two halves with the spring together. This is done by twisting the halves until they lock. You then refit the tensioner & the large outer threaded housing. Using a long allen key that fits in the end of the tensioner, you turn it slightly while depressing the tensioner with a long thin screwdriver. This releases the tensioner & the spring applies the correct tension to the chain. If this isn't done correctly, the chain will be either too loose or too tight & may cause damage.______
