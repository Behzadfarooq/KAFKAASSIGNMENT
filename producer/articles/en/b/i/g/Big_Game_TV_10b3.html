Big Game TV is a live phone-in quiz channel shown on its own channel on Sky Digital 849 and Living2.

Big Game TV broadcasts live daily between the hours of 22:00pm and 04:00am (Everyday). The channel encourages viewers to participate by a premium-rate telephone call or via a 'Free Web Entry' available on their website.

[edit] About Big Game TV

Big Game TV Productions released their first spin-off show, The Hallmark Channel Quiz on March 6, 2006, shown on The Hallmark Channel between 1-3pm. This show only uses 3 of the presenters.

Big Game TV Productions branched out further when The Daily Quiz! went on air on 13 March 2006. This show aired on ITV Play and on Men & Motors seven days a week from 3.00pm-4.00pm and featured a selection of Big Game TV's presenters. ITV ended their involvement with Big Game TV due to the fraud allegations in May 2006[citation needed].
