[edit] Expansion of names in Subway Infoboxes

Hey there, I noticed you have been expanding the names of the subway stations (e.g. "175th Street" --> "175th Street - George Washington Bridge Bus Terminal"). Thank you very much for your thorough contributions. I'm not so sure that this is the right thing to do, however. I believe the tradition is to keep the names matching those on the MTA's subway map - as the map is the most up-to-date resource (updated more quickly than station signs, etc). There was actually a discussion about a similar issue (Wikipedia_talk:WikiProject_New_York_City_Subway/Archive_2#42nd_Street-Grand_Central_issue), but I'm not so sure that a concensus was reached. It may be a good idea to bring this up in the Wikiproject.

Thanks again for your contributions!

-Seidenstud 17:51, 23 November 2006 (UTC)
