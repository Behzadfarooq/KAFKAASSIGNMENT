Skyler Arndt is a 17 year old bad ass residing in Guymon, Oklahoma. He lives with his mother, step father, and sister. His hobbies include drawing, playing football, listening to music, watching movies, playing drums, and hanging out with his friends and his girlfriend, Kelsey.

He was born in Amarillo, Texas on January 10, 1989 to Missie Martinez and Shawn Arndt. At that moment the song "Bad To The Bone" mysteriously started playing all over the world.

http://i84.photobucket.com/albums/k15/BiG_sKy_2006/Skyler100.jpg (The Sexy Beast himself)

In 1998 he moved to Ulysses, Kansas where he lived with his mother and step father, Charley Short, until 2005 when he moved to Guymon. He currently is in his Junior year of high school. While in Ulysses he had a kick ass band named iroN flaMes, their band consisted of: Jared Stringham - Guitar, Donovan McCauley - Bass, Skyler Arndt - Drums. iroN flaMes had one album while working together called Double-Sided Candle, the album consisted of 10 tracks that could rattle Satan's ears.

Skyler is curently not involved with a band but occasionally talks about forming one with his neighbor and fellow metalhead Albert Perales.

If anything, skyler wishes to get a band started in Guymon and name it Jigsaw.

Skyler was recently asked why he loves his music, primarily metal, so much. He simply answered: "Because, dude, if it ain't metal it ain't worth listening to, period. So get outa' my way of I'll beat your ass to death."
