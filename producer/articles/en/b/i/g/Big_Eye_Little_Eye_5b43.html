Big Eye Little Eye is the second studio album of the Canadian rock band The Golden Dogs. The album features its first single "Never Meant Any Harm".

[edit] Track listing

All songs written and arranged by Dave Azzolini. This album was engineered, mixed and co-produced by Paul Aucoin (The Hylozoists, Cuff the Duke, The Fembots) at Halla Music Studios in Toronto.

  1. Dynamo
  2. Never Meant Any Harm
  3. Construction Worker
  4. Saints At The Gates
  5. Runoutaluck
  6. Painting Ape
  7. Strong
  8. Theresa
  9. Nineteen Hundred & Eighty Five
  10. Life On The Line
  11. Force Of Nature
  12. Wheel Of Fortune
