Bignall End is a small village in North Staffordshire very near to Junction 16 of the M6 motorway.

It is a part of the Parish of Audley which comprises Audley (village), Bignall End, Wood Lane, Miles Green, Halmer End, and Alsagers Bank.

There are a few local shops; most local shopping is done in Audley village.

[edit] Facilities

  • Audley Cameo Club[1]
  • Chinese Take-away
  • Londis Shop – Off-Licence, DVDs, Newspapers, etc.
  • Barbers Shop (Male)
  • Hair Studio (Unisex)
  • The Plough Inn Public House
  • The Swan Public House (known locally as The Duck)
  • Butcher's Shop
  • Post Office
  • Cricket Club
  • Ravensmead County Primary School
