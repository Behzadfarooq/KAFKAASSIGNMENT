Big and Little Wong Tin Bar is a 1962 movie filmed in Hong Kong.

[edit] Cast

  • Jackie Chan: Played the role of a Taiwanese actress' son, whos name was Li Li-hua.
  • Sammo Hung Kam-Bo: Played an unknown role.
  • Li Hua Li: Played Li Li-hua.

[edit] Filming Information

Country: Hong Kong

Spoken Language: Cantonese

Color: Black-and-white

Sound Mix: Mono

[edit] Trivia

  • Big and Little Wong Tin Bar was Jackie Chan's first film appearance.
  • When Chan was cast for this role, he was eight years old.
  • There are no known copies of this film remaining.
