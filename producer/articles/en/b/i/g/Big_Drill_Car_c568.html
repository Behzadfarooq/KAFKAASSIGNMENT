Big Drill Car

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Big Drill Car was a melodic hardcore group from Huntington Beach, California. They were formed in 1987 and broke up in 1995. After breaking up, 2 former members Frank Daly (vocalist) and Mark Arnold (guitarist) have continued making music with their recent side project band All Systems Go!.

[edit] Line-Up

  • Frank Daly - Vocals
  • Mark Arnold - Guitar
  • Bob Thomson - Bass (1987-1992)
  • Darrin Morris - Bass (1992-1995)
  • Danny Marcroft - Drums (1987-1992)
  • Keith Fallis - Drums (1992-1995)

[edit] Discography

  • Small Block (1988, EP)
  • CD Type Thing (1989)
  • Batch (1991)
  • Toured (A Live Album) (1993, live)
  • No Worse for the Wear (1994)
