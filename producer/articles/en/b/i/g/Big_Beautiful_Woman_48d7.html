"Big Beautiful Woman" (commonly abbreviated as BBW) is a phrase most frequently used to denote sexual attraction to overweight women. BBWs are the focus of a subculture, some areas of which are sexually fetishistic in nature, including fat fetishism and feederism. Some involved with the fat acceptance movement also use the term.

The term BBW spread rapidly via the Internet during the 1990s. A large number of BBW websites are pornographic, and feature women who range from barely overweight to those who are profoundly obese. Men who are attracted to BBWs are occasionally referred to as "chubby chasers" or "fat admirers". Some men are especially fascinated or aroused by certain body parts or areas of BBWs' bodies.

BBW has many near-synonyms with varying shades of meaning. Rubenesque is a close synonym; the term refers to the art of Peter Paul Rubens, best known for portraying full-bodied women. Full-figured refers to women with a generally plump body shape, whereas voluptuous and zaftig usually connote ripeness, sensuality, and a body shape involving large breasts and wide hips.
