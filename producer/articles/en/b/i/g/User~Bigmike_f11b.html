I'm a Hereford United fan and I've essentially done the majority of the club article, and associated articles. But I'll try and sort out anything I come across that needs sorting.

I don't claim to be the biggest Hereford fan, far from it. My first match was in 1992 but such is the way things go, I've only been to relatively few matches since then (but in my defence I do live over a 100 miles from Hereford). I'm ashamed of this and aim to put it right in the coming years!
