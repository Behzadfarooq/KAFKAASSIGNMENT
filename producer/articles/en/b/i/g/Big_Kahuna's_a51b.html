The Big Kahuna, located in Destin, Florida, is a family-oriented water and land park.

Various activities are offered at The Big Kahuna, including a tubing river, an interactive water area, miniature golf, go-karts, and air rides. It covers approximately 25 acres, and is home of over 40 water attractions.

Big Kahuna's has been in the heart of Destin for 21 years. It is a family oriented water and adventure park with a Polynisian theme. It shocases various waterfalls and lush tropical gardens. It spans almost 25 acres and is kid oriented and family friendly. It is walking distance from local beaches and most local hotels.
