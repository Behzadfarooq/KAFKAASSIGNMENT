"Big Girls Don't Cry" is the confirmed fourth single from Fergie's solo album, The Dutchess. She performed the song in her AOL Music Sessions. AOL Sessions confirmed that a video is in the works. [1] Fergie explains in an interview on aol.com that as each one of her songs has a true story, Big Girls Don't Cry pertains to the hardships faced in some relationships.The quote "I Hope you know, that this has nothing to do with you. It's personal, myself and I, we got some straightning out to do."[cite this quote] suggests that even Fergie has problems with relationships.
