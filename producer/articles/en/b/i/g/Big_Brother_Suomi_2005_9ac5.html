Big Brother Suomi 2005 was the first season of the Finnish reality television series Big Brother. It aired on SubTV in Finland from 28 August 2005 to 1 December 2005.

A number of contestants (known as "housemates") lived in a purpose-built house in Espoo, and were isolated from the rest of the world. Each week, each housemate nominated two of their peers for eviction, and the housemates (two or more) to receive the most number of votes would face a public vote. Of these, one would eventually leave, having been "evicted" from the House. However, there sometimes were exceptions to this process as dictated by Big Brother, known as "twists". In the final week, there were four housemates remaining, and the public voted for who they wanted to win. Perttu Sirviö received the most votes, and won the prize money of €50,000.

[edit] Housemates

Twelve housemates entered the House at Launch. One housemate (Kathy) decided to leave the House on Day 60.

Men

  • Antti A. (b. 1980), Helsinki (2nd runner-up)
  • Antti S. (b. 1982), Lohja
  • Jani (b. 1972), Helsinki
  • Mika (b. 1977), Helsinki
  • Perttu (b. 1983), Vihti (winner)
  • Timo (b. 1977), Jyväskylä

Women

  • Hanna (b. 1978), Turku
  • Iina (b. 1978), Espoo
  • Kathy (b. 1984), Jyväskylä (left the House voluntarily) [1]
  • Minna (b. 1967), Vesilahti
  • Terhi (b. 1974), Helsinki
  • Tiina (b. 1980), Tampere (1st runner-up)

[edit] Notes

Note 1: Both Kathy and Jani received 6 nomination points. Big Brother, however, decided to draw lots and Jani was put up for eviction with Perttu. This caused some controversy.

Note 2: Big Brother punished Timo for discussing nominations by putting him up for eviction with the nominated housemates.

Note 3: After Kathy had left the House, Timo, who had received 6 nomination points, was put up for eviction with Minna.

Note 4: There were no nominations in the final week and the public were voting for housemates to win, rather than be evicted.
