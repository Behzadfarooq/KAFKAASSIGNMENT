"Big Long Now" is a song by rock band Nirvana and appears on their 1992 B-sides collection album, Incesticide. It was written by lead singer/guitarist Kurt Cobain and is rumoured to be about his ex-girlfriend Tobi Veil, while he was under the influence of drugs or alcohol.
