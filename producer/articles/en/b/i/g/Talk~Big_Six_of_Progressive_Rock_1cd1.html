Note that opinions are divided on who the "big six" actually are:

  • [1] "Pallas were one of the big six progressive rock bands that came to the fore in the early eighties, defying the big hair trend."
  • [2] "King Crimson is still respected as one of the original “big six” of progressive music’s early days, along with Yes, Genesis, Pink Floyd, EL&P, and Gentle Giant."

Note that of the 27 non-duplicate results, one of them is a wikipedia result, and almost all of the rest are about, for example, the band called "Big Six", th2 "big six" record companies, the big six psychoanalysts, "three big six-foot-tall guys", a song called "big six", etc. In fact, I only got three results (two from the same website) that were relevant to the concept in questions, in total. Three results from a websearch is nothing! Martin
