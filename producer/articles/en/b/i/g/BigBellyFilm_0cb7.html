[edit] History

BigBellyFilm, founded in 2001, is a Swedish production company. The founders are Ulf Norström and Robert P. Olsson. The name BigBellyFilm was an idea from Ulf Norström. Because both Ulf and Robert was a bit overweight at this time they made up this character BigBelly, a fat man with bading shorts and sunglasses. He is standing on a box with BigBellyFilm written in it. BigBelly is supposed to be seen as a jolly happy man not to be taken to seriusly. BigBellyFilm produces independent films and they are specializing in short drama/comedies and horrormovies.

[edit] In the works

Fyren (The Light Keeper) a Lovecraftian short movie, about a man living in fear in an abandon Lighthouse. He lives in constant light to protect himself from the evil in the darkness outside. Script is being written by Robert P. Olsson and Tommy Karlsson. This is gonna be the third Lovecraftian movie by BigBellyFilm.
