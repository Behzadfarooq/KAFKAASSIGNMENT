Biggleswade Town F.C.

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Biggleswade Town F.C. are a football club based in Biggleswade, England. They joined the South Midlands League in 1951 and were founding members of the Spartan South Midlands Football League Premier Division North in 1997. In the 2000-01 season, they reached the 3rd round of the FA Vase. For the 2006-07 season, they are members of the Spartan South Midlands Football League Premier Division.
