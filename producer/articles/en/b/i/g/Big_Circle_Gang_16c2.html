Big Circle Gang

The Big Circle Gang (大圈幫) is one of the major Chinese Organized Crime syndicates. Its Chinese designation is Tai Huen Chai (大圈仔). It was featured in the Alex Rider novel Skeleton Key. It was created by former Red Guards who fled to Hong Kong. It is active on several continents including North America, where the group is said to have an influence in Canada.[1] It has also spread to NSW, Australia particularly Parramatta.[citation needed]
