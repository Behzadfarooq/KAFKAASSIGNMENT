Big Daddy's BBQ Sauce (Pink Sheets: BIGD) is a company that produces barbecue sauce. Based in Yukon, Oklahoma, it came from the result of two friends merging their famous recipes together. In addition to producing its world-famous barbecue sauce, it also specializes in pre-cooked ribs and briskets, beef jerky, and microwavable chopped pork, chicken, and beef.

[edit] Auto Racing

A car Big Daddy's sponsored in 1999 but never ran.

The company has involved itself with several autoracing sponsorship programs such as the Indy Racing League, NHRA, along with Donlavey Racing and Larry Hedrick Motorsports in the NASCAR Winston Cup Series and Bill Catania in the American Speed Association. However, it has been discussed that they failed to pay their money. The company was the title sponsor of South Boston Speedway in 2002 and 2003. During the time the track was known as Big Daddy's South Boston Speedway.
