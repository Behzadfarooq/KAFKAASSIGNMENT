Big Reese

Big Reese (also known simply as Reese) is a hiphop/r&b producer from Atlanta, GA. He produced Lloyd's hiphop/r&b hit single You. He also produced P$C's Like A Movie from their album 25 To Life, also featuring Lloyd, and also songs for TLC, Sleepy Brown, T.I., Pink, Mystikal, Mariah Carey, Rick Ross, Young Dro, Rehab, Brooks Buford, Beanie Sigel, and Killer Mike.

He was originally part of P.A., and also was in Organized Noize.
