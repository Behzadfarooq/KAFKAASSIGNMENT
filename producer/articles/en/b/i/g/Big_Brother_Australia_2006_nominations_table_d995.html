[edit] Notes

Note 1: The Sunday before nominations, housemates were asked to nominate which two housemates they found 'most annoying'. Camilla and Anna received the most votes. They were evicted from the House and sent to the Revenge Room. They were later told by Big Brother that if they were successful in their Revenge task, they would return to the House and therefore be immune from nomination in Week 2.

Note 2: Because he failed his Insider task, Michael was automatically put up for nomination, while Karen and Krystal were exempt from nominations because they passed their task. Krystal and Karen could not vote individually and had to vote together as a single entity that was represented by Karen. The housemates up for eviction were Michael, Elise, Tilli and Ashley. After Gaelan used his Three Point Twist on Ashley, the final eviction line-up was changed to Michael, Elise, Tilli, Claire and Dino.

Note 3: Michael was exempt from nominations because he passed his Insider task. Krystal and Karen could not vote individually and had to vote together as a single entity that was represented by Krystal. Karen, Krystal, Camilla and Dino were nominated. Dino used his Three Point Twist on himself, changing the eviction line-up to Karen, Krystal and Camilla.

Note 4: Michael was exempt from nominations because he passed his Insider task. Camilla, Dino and Ashley were up for eviction. Michael used his Three Point Twist on Dino, changing the eviction line-up to Camilla, Ashley, David, Krystal and Anna.

Note 5: Camilla, Michael and David were originally up for eviction. Gaelan used his Three Point Twist on David, changing the eviction line-up to Camilla, Michael and John.

Note 6: Jade, Dino, Camilla and Krystal were up for eviction. Danielle used her Three Point Twist on Krystal, removing her from the eviction line-up. However, due to the Under the Radar vote, the public voted for John and Krystal to be added to the eviction line-up; resulting in the eviction line-up being Jade, Dino, Camilla, Krystal, and John.

Note 7: Rob, Danielle and Camilla were up for eviction. Katie used her Three Point Twist on Camilla, but it had no effect.

Note 8: Ashley, David, Jamie, and Katie were up for eviction. David used his Three Point Twist;; on Ashley, changing the eviction line-up to David, Jamie and Katie.

Note 9: Camilla, Jamie, and Rob were up for eviction. John used his Three Point Twist on Jamie, but it had no effect.

Note 10: Jamie was banned from nominating because he had discussed nominations. David, Gaelan and Krystal were up for eviction. Claire used her Three Point Twist on David, changing the eviction line-up to Gaelan, Krystal, David and Camilla. Due to a breach of the rules, Ashley and John were both removed from the house and disqualified by Big Brother, receiving no prizes.

Note 11: Perry, Darren, David and Krystal were originally up for eviction. David used his Three Point Twist on Darren, changing the eviction line-up to Perry, David and Krystal.

Note 12: Camilla, Darren and David were originally up for eviction. Darren used his Three Point Twist on himself, changing the eviction line-up to Camilla, David, Darren and Krystal.

Note 13: David, Chris and Camilla were nominated for eviction. Jamie used his Three Point Twist on Camilla, changing the eviction line-up to David, Chris, Krystal, Max and Claire.

Note 14: Max, Chris and Jamie were originally up for eviction. Jamie used his Three Point Twist on himself, changing the eviction line-up to Max, Chris, Camilla and David.
