Big Thinkers (game)

The Big Thinkers series from Humongous Entertainment was a line of educational games revolving around the characters Ben and Becky Brightly released in 1997.

Only two games were released, for the kindergarten and first grade age ranges; however, a second grade game was to have been released in the possible success of the first two. While this has not been confirmed, it is not believed that the Second Grade Big Thinkers game was ever released.
