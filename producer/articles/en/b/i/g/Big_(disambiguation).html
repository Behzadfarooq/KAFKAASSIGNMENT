Big may refer to:

In sports:

  • Big Ten Conference, the United States' oldest college athletic conference
  • Big 12 Conference, college athletic conference of twelve schools located in the central United States
  • James Worthy, college and professional basketball player from Gastonia, North Carolina who goes by the name "Big Game James"

In popular music:

  • Notorious B.I.G., (1972-1997) Popular rap artist

In Australia:

  • Australia's Big Things, loosely related set of large structures or sculptures representing much smaller objects
    • Big Banana, tourist attraction in the town of Coffs Harbour, New South Wales
    • Big Bull, large cement male Holstein cow located in Wauchope, New South Wales, Australia
    • Golden Guitar, one of the many "big" attractions that can be found around Australia
    • Big Merino, 15 metre tall cement Merino sheep located in Goulburn, New South Wales, Australia
    • The Big Pineapple, tourist attraction and working farm situated near Nambour, Queensland
    • Big Records, a record label

In other fields:

  • Big, 1988 comedy film about a teenaged boy who is aged to adulthood by a magical fortune telling machine
  • Big, The Musical a musical based on the above film.
  • Big Time, album and movie by Tom Waits
  • Big (album), album by Macy Gray to be released in 2007
  • Big the Cat, fictional character from the Sonic the Hedgehog game universe
  • Big Brothers Big Sisters of America, non-profit organization dedicated to organizing mentorships between adults and children
