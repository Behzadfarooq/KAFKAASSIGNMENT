The Big Apple Chorus is a barbershop chorus based in Manhattan, New York. The chorus, directed by Joe Hunter, was founded in 1983 and has approximately 85 members. The Big Apple Chorus has competed in the Barbershop Harmony Society's international chorus competition, with a best finish of 2nd in 1987.

For several years, the chorus has regularly participated in the holiday concert series at the South Street Seaport, including the Living Christmas Treeâ€”a group of carolers arranged in a tree, accompanying the first public Christmas tree in the city to be lit during the winter holiday season.[1]
