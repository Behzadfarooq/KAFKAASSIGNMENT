Big is the fourth studio album by American R&B/soul singer Macy Gray. It is her first new album in four years. It was released on March 27, 2007.[1]

The album was leaked onto the Internet on March 14, 2007. Following its release, it debuted at number 39 on the U.S. Billboard 200, selling about 23,000 copies in its first week.[2]
