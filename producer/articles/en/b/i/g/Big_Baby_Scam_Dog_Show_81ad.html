[edit] Plot

Ren and Stimpy are homeless and so disguise themselves as babies in order to get a new home, bribing the existing babies to leave. However, after such incidents such as Mr. Pipe scratching up Ren's face with his 5 O'clock shadow, playing upsydaisy with Ren, and finally taking a bath with the Pipe family, the boys finally see that being a baby is too much. When a cop brings Mr. Pipe his real babies, Ren and Stimpy blow their cover and ask the babies for their money back. The babies, saying "You want fifty? We'll give you fifty!!" give Ren 50 stomach punches instead of the $50 Ren gave them to get lost.

[edit] Notes, Goofs, and Trivia

  • Two scenes of this episode were cut from season one and two DVD, and airings on Spike TV:
1. A close up of Ren getting scratched by Mr. Pipe's stubble. Some copies have some of this scene it in.
2. Grandpa whistling "In the Hall of the Mountain King" to Ren and Stimpy in the bathtub. A pedophile/murderer in the 1931 film "M" constantly whistled that tune. John Kricfalusi had originally written a different compostion to be whistled by the character, but was fired before the episode was completed, and so "In the Hall of the Moutain King" was overdubbed instead.
  • When aired on Nicktoons Network, this airs with Monkey See, Monkey Don't.

[edit] Notes, Goofs, and Trivia

  • Two scenes were cut from the episode when it was aired on Nickelodeon, but were eventually restored when the DVD was released. They were:
1. When George says his name, his surname is replaced with the sound of a record being scratched.
2. Near the end, George's bobbed tail was cut because Nick thought it looked like an erection.
  • This could be a sequel to "Man's Best Friend", the episode that was banned from Nickelodeon, and was shown on Ren and Stimpy Adult Party Cartoon a decade later.
  • Second seen appearance of "That Guy" (he was also heard of other times, but not seen).
