This user has left Wikipedia, turned off by a select group of administrators.

I didn't join WP to fight userbox wars. I joined to improve some articles on Christian theology, many of which are quite simply, attrociously bad. In my time here, I have observed administrators abusing authority, inventing rules, edit warring, and vandalizing pages. Rather than enforce the rules that are there by community consensus, you vandalize templates, change the fundamental messages of templates that you don't use and don't want to use to say something completely different than what the users who use the templates want to say. What you can't achieve through community consensus, you enforce through edit warring. Best of luck. I mean no ill will to any of you whatsoever. I just feel that my views are incompatible with the way things are done here. Good day.
