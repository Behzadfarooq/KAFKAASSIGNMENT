Big Ant Studios is a melbourne based game development studio. Big Ant Studios have created games for Sony PlayStation 2, PC, and Game Boy Advance.

Big Ant Studios was previously known as Bullant Studios, and changed its name in 2006 prior to releasing its first published title Sprint Cars: Road to Knoxville.

[edit] Games Developed By Big Ant Studios

  • Sprint Cars: Road to Knoxville, (PS2, PC, 2006)
  • The Legend of Spyro: A New Beginning, (GBA, 2006) (with Krome Studios)
