[edit] ABC radio stations

Hello BigDan, good to see another Australian radio fan on Wikipedia. I've been doing a little work on ABC Radio articles, e.g. 612 ABC Brisbane and 702 ABC Sydney. I'm part way through doing a list of all ABC Local Radio stations. I see that you've done one for ABC Upper Hunter. Two things I want to ask you:

(1) I believe that ABC Local Radio stations should have titles conforming with their on-air identity, e.g. "666 ABC Canberra" instead of "2CN". Would you agree to me changing the titles of 2NC and 2UH to "1233 ABC Newcastle" and "ABC Upper Hunter"?

(2) What's the on-air identity of ABC Upper Hunter and ABC Central West? You see, when I was in Wollongong, the on-air ID was always "97.3 ABC Illawarra" yet their website, http://www.abc.net.au/illawarra shows just "ABC Illawarra". I won't complete the list of ABC Local Radio stations until I'm sure of what the on-air identities are.

Good work on the Australian broadcasting pages, and I look forward to working with you. --Humehwy 06:03, July 18, 2005 (UTC)

I'd have no problems you changing the titles to conform with ABC on-air identity...as long as you keep 2UH and 2NC as redirects...also, ABC Upper Hunter only identifies as "ABC Upper Hunter" during the mornings when it airs its breakfast program. The rest of the time, they use 2NC's on-air IDs...I'm not sure about ABC Central West, I've never been able to recieve it...BigDan 13:57, July 18, 2005 (UTC)
I've moved both the 2NC and 2UH pages to 1233 ABC Newcastle and ABC Upper Hunter. It's okay, when you move pages, redirects are put into place automatically. I think it's better for on-air names to be used rather than callsigns -- for instance, nobody would title an article "2SYD" -- but "Nova 96.9" would be perfectly acceptable for the same station! By the way, the ABC website's reception guide doesn't give a transmitter for ABC Central West in Bathurst -- the main Orange area 549AM transmitter is supposed to suffice, I guess. Anyway check out 612 ABC Brisbane -- this is the format I'd like to use for ABC stations. For the regional stations, I'd have to determine whether their on-air ID is "xxx ABC Region_Name" or just "ABC Region_Name". --Humehwy 05:40, July 19, 2005 (UTC)
Ah good...I know there's no ABC Central West transmitter in Bathurst...the 50kw transmitter in Cumnock near Orange is supposed to serve nearly all the Central West. I agree with the rest of what you've said. BigDan 11:26, July 19, 2005 (UTC)

[edit] Age category

Hello! If you are receiving this message, that means that your user page is in a specific year category. Per a recent user-category per deletion, all specific year categories are to be deleted. If you wish to continue using year categories, you have two options:

  • Using an age group category, such as Category:Wikipedians in their 30s
