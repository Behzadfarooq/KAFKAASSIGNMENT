[edit] Big Fish In Middlesex

Big Fish In Middlesex is a feature length film produced by White Lightning Productions. Written by Jonathan Straiton, Big Fish In Middlesex has won numerous awards from various Film Festivals, most notably the B-Movie Fest in upstate New York.

Big Fish in Middlesex was written and directed by Jonathan Straiton.


[edit] White Lighting Productions

White Lighting Productions is an independently funded and operated production company operated by Jonathan Straiton.
