[edit] Question on awarding of buddies

Ok, I've got a question on the way buddies are awarded in the mini games. Looking at the Jet Car Jump info, it says that you get the helicopter buddy on level 1 and the cloud buddy on level two. But for me it happened the other way around. Someone I know had it happen the way it's listed in the article. So my question is whether the order buddies are awarded for certain games is preset or if it randomizes?

Preset would make sense as it would mean that the designers wouldn't have to worry about a mini game handing out the same buddy twice, or always handing out buddies each time one plays. But it wouldn't explain why I was rewarded Jet Car Jump buddies opposite of what the article says. And the only way the randomizing could properly work is if the program was set up to check if a player already one certain buddies, pictures, music, hats, and furniture so as to not reward them a second time. -annonymous 11/19/06

It's not random. Either you had a glitch or you're simply remembering it wrong. – Someguy0830 (T | C) 05:29, 19 November 2006 (UTC)
