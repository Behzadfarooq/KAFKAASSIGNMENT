Big Muscle

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

BigMuscle (BigMuscle.com, BM) is an online community for gay, bisexual and bi-curious men over the age of 21, who have muscular, well-defined physiques. BigMuscle offers users the ability to create a free profile and post photos of themselves. The website began on February 21, 1999.

Unlike many other gay adult profile sites, BigMuscle is free; one may create or view profiles without having to sign up. Because of donations from supporting members, there is no monthly membership fee required to create a profile, post pictures, or view current members' profiles.

BigMuscle has four affiliated sites: BigMuscleBears, NormalGay, BigMuscleCollege and BigMuscleLeather.

As of March 9, 2007, BM had 20,038 members, the ages of 21 to 78 profile #68100
