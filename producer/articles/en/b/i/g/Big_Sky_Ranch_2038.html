Big Sky Ranch

Big sky ranch is a Movie Ranch located in Simi Valley, California. It has been widely used for the filming of Western Television and film productions. Some of the past Television episodes and Productions filmed there include: Rawhide, Gunsmoke, Little House on the Prairie and Highway to Heaven. A more complete list of productions can be found at The Internet Movie Database.
