Bi Community News (commonly shortened to 'BCN') is the United Kingdom's only publication serving the bisexual population.

Resulting from a workshop at the UK BiCon in 1995, it is a zine that has been consistently published since October 1995. Previous UK bisexual journals were BiFrost (1991 - 1995) and Bi Monthly in the 1980s.
