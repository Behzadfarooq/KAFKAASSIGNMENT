The simple-station Biblioteca is part of the TransMilenio mass-transit system of Bogotá, Colombia, opened in the year 2000.

[edit] Location

The station is located in southern Bogotá, facing Parque El Tunal and near the Biblioteca El Tunal, from which it gets its name. It is specifically located on Avenida Ciudad de Villavicencio with Diagonal 48 Sur.

[edit] History

In 2003, a few months after the opening of the Portal del Norte, the Avenida Ciudad de Villavicencio line was opened, including this station.
