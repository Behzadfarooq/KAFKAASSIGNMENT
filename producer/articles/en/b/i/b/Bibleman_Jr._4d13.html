Bibleman Jr.

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Bibleman Jr. is a spinoff of "Bibleman." The "junior" version is for preschoolers and is less violent than the "Bibleman" show. It features Cypher, Bibleman, and U.N.I.C.E. It features songs and animated shorts from "Guideposts Junction," a spinoff of "Guideposts Magazine."

[edit] Spinoffs

  • Bibleman
  • Bibleman: PowerSource

[edit] Episodes

  • Thankful for Jesus (2001)
  • God Loves Everyone (2001)
  • God Loves to Laugh (2003)
  • God's Beautiful World (2003)

In 2006, All Bibleman Jr. episodes were reproduced by Tommy Nelson with a whole new cover design.
