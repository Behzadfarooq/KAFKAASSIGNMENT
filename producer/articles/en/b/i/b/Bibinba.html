Bibinba (ビビンバ, Bibinba?) in a character from Yudetamago's manga and anime series Kinnikuman and its sequel Kinnikuman Nisei. She is the love interest of Suguru Kinniku, the series' protagonist.

[edit] About

  • Classification: Seigi Choujin
  • Homeland: Horumon City, Planet Kinniku
  • Age: 16
  • Height: 163 cm
  • Weight: 43 kg
  • Choujin Power: 100,000
  • Trademark Techniques: Horumon Savate, Sex Appeal Attack
  • First Appearance: Manga Chapter 82, Anime Movie 2

She refers to Kinnikuman as Suguru-sama (スグル様, Suguru-sama?). Her name is Japanese for bibimbap. (more to come)

The anime version of Kinnikuman seemed to prefer Mari Nikaidō as Kinnikuman's girlfriend over Bibinba. The final episode of Kinnikuman even implies that Bibinba and Super Phoenix got together. This was later rectified in Kinnikuman II or Ultimate Muscle anime where Bibinba was correctly depicted as Kinnikuman's wife.

[edit] Story

[edit] Beginning

Bibinba was her father Horumon Yāki's, only hope in defeating his tribe's arch-enemy, the Kinniku clan. Hormone Yāki dispatches his daughter Bibinba to kill the heir to the Kinniku throne, Kinnikuman (who was visiting his parents on Planet Kinniku). Despite being very good in her attempts to kill Kinnikuman, Kinnikuman was just too silly and ignorant of his impending death to actually die. Most of Bibinba's attempts to kill him ended with Kinnikuman helping her which eventually led her to fall in love with him. She travelled back to Earth with Kinnikuman and Meat and was the cause for Mari leaving Japan.
