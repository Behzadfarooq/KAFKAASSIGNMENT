Bible Black (バイブルブラック, Baiburuburakku?) is a Hentai anime OVA series. It is based on an eroge of the same name. The series is separated into four titles. The game was created by Active-Soft in 2000, and was adapted into an anime by the Milky Studio.

The original series, "La Noche de Walpurgis" consists of 6 episodes, 2 prequel episodes and two special "ONLY" episodes. A new season, titled "La Lanza de Longinus" (currently with 5 episodes) is currently being produced by Milky Studio as well. Both seasons of the anime -- and now the game as well -- have been translated and released in the U.S. by Kitty Media.

The whole series revolves around a certain high school rumored to have been the base of operations of a convent several years ago. The school's unofficial "witchcraft club" as they called themselves practiced dark Black Magic in the underground basement hidden away at the institute.

Teachers, students, professors, and staff practicing a dark occultic religion involving incantations, sex, and sacrifices upon their innocent victims is a common and recurring theme in all things Bible Black.

In Japan, the six chapters of La Noche de Walpurgis (aka, The Night of Walpurgis) were originally released one at a time on DVD and VHS. Between the fourth and fifth chapter of the main storyline, the backstory was released as a prequel, entitled Bible Black Gaiden (aka, Bible Black Origins).

The initial releases in North America combined two chapters per DVD, and were slightly retitled. Chapters 1 & 2 became Bible Black 1 & 2; chapters 3 & 4 became Second Sacrament 1 & 2; the two episodes of the prequel became Bible Black Origins: Black Brand & Black Altar; and chapters 5 & 6 became Revelations 1 & 2.

Plot Summary (from Anime News Network) for Chapters 1-6: Minase, a high school student, finds a book of magic in an isolated room in his school. He starts practicing black magic that has extreme sexual effects that benefit him and some of his friends. Intrigued, Minase delves deeper and deeper into using the craft, not realizing the evils that will come forth. Eventually, the origins of the book are revealed, and is the incident twelve years ago on the Night of the Walpurgis, the night when the power of evil is at its strongest. After coming to his senses, Minase struggles to get himself out of the darkness that he has put himself into.

Plot Summary (from Anime News Network) for Gaiden/Origins 1-2: Twelve years prior to its discovery by Minase, the origins of the magic book with dark, sensuous powers is revealed. Initially owned by a female student, Takashiro, who, along with other curious students form the first magic club on campus. The club begins to use the erotic spells to carry out favors for fellow students, as well as vengeance. After underestimating the powers behind the book, Takashiro is taken out of the picture, as a new leader guides the club towards a darker course, one that will force school newcomer Kitami to make a dire choice.

[edit] Stand-alone episodes: Bible Black Only

  • Bible Black Only, Volume 1 (July 25, 2005)
  • Bible Black Only, Volume 2 (April 25, 2006)

These special episodes explore the sexual misadventures of the supporting cast from the main Bible Black series. Miss Takashiro reminisces on her demonic capture, Itoh reveals her sadistic side, and Saeki is stalked by her classmates. Bible Black Only are stand-alone episodes that do not affect the continuity of the main Bible Black series. Some of the footage was previously only available on the R2 Bible Black Perfect Collection DVD.

[edit] Story Characters

  • Reika Kitami

The school doctor. Twelve years ago, when she was a student, she was chosen as a sacrificial victim for a demon-summoning ritual by the witchcraft club. During this ritual, the club director went insane and murdered all of the participating members before being stabbed by the dying Kitami. With so much blood shed, a demon was summoned, willing to grant one wish. Kitami, dying and in pain, wished to live, so the demon granted her wish -- after a fashion. Kitami's body was healed, but in twelve years time, the corruption of her soul would cause her to die and she would be sent to hell. She realized that if she were able to switch bodies with someone, her original body (and any soul inside it) would be destroyed instead. Kitami's driving need to live becomes a major plot point of the first "Bible Black" series. Kitami was also granted the ability to use dark magic, and uses her ability to preform sexual deeds, the most frequently used manifestation being the ability to transform her clitoris into a fully functional penis.

  • Minase Taki

A young student who discovers the dark magic book, "Bible Black", in an abandoned cellar. With this discovery, he becomes a pawn of powerful forces in a sinister plot. After he uses the dark magic described in the book for his own amusement, Kitami briefly charms him into serving her own dark purposes. Once free of the enchantment, Taki fights to rescue his childhood friend, Kurumi, and stop Kitami's evil plans. Although alluded to in Bible Black: La Lana de Longinus, as of the fifth episode he has not yet appeared and his fate is unknown.

  • Imari Kurumi

A female student with a crush on Minase. As a virgin, she is chosen by the school nurse, Reika Kitami, for a sinister plan. The ending is left uncertain, as although Minase completed the spell and defeated Kitami, as well as Imari seems herself later on, she proceeds to show that she has Kitami's curse to transform her clitoris into a fully functional penis, as well as various statements that imply coming from Kitami's point of view. (The original Bible Black ends with Imari hysterically laughing while raping Saeki on top of a mystical symbol after Saeki attempts to recover the "Bible Black", which is burnt by Imari). During the events of Bible Black: La Lanza de Longinus (New Testament), she is an agent for a government agency whose priority is to investigate supernatural events.

  • Hiroko Takashiro

A teacher. In her days as a student at the same school, she was a member of the witchcraft club. She was the only member of the club to survive a terrible event involving Reika Kitami, and the nurse still bears an intense grudge against her. Before the events of Bible Black: La Lanza de Longinus (New Testament), she retires from education and becomes a nun.

  • Rika Shiraki

Top of her class, one of the members of the school council, and the most popular girl of her class, as well as the daughter of a wealthy electronics manufacturer (given the nickname 'Idol of the School' in the eroge). Shiraki becomes embroiled in the sinister events revolving around the "Bible Black" when she is magically charmed by Minase. During the events of Bible Black: La Lanza de Longinus(New Testament), she is a teacher at the same school she attended.

  • Kaori Saeki

The student director of the current witchcraft club. Her hobby is, of course, dabbling into the arcane, such as the use of tarot cards and magic books. Realizing that Taki Minase had obtained a powerful book of magic, Saeki is determined to acquire it at any cost. During the events of Bible Black: La Lanza de Longinus(New Testament), Saeki is a teacher at the same school she attended and still secretly controls the witchcraft club.

  • Miyuki Nonogusa

Star of the swimming club. She is a classmate of Taki Minase.

  • Shinobu Kobayashi

The student director of the swimming club. She has a lesbian relationship with Mikimoto. Kobayashi was a character in the adventure game, but did not appear in the anime until she was featured in the Bible Black: Only short stories.

  • Mikimoto

A member of the swimming club. She has a lesbian relationship with Shinobu Kobayashi. Mikimoto was a character in the adventure game, but did not appear in the anime until she was featured in the Bible Black: Only short stories.

  • Mika Itoh

A classmate of Taki Minase. In the game, she is a member of Saeki's coven and the first victim of a spell from the Bible Black.

  • Yukiko Minase

The elder step-sister of Taki Minase. According to the Bible Black: Only short stories, at the time of the first Bible Black series, Yukiko was apparently studying to be a medical doctor (whereas in the eroge she had already completed her study and was an expert linguist). She has only a minor role in the overall story. In the anime series, Yukiko is offended by sexual advances from Minase but in the Game it has been reversed- Yukiko overly sexual towards Minase.

  • Ayumi Murai

A friend of Taki Minase and Kurumi Imari.

[edit] New Testament Release Controversy

The US release of New Testament volume 1 caused a bit of an uproar in the fan community when it was revealed that Milky had refused to give Kitty Media the Japanese language track, citing fears of more Japanese customers ignoring their release in favor of reverse importing the cheaper and uncensored R1 DVDs. It proved a hollow measure at best though, as quickly custom made versions combining the uncensored video and dub from the Kitty DVD with the audio from the Japanese DVDs and fan-made subtitles became available for download on various filesharing sites on the Internet.

Eventually it looked like Milky had backed down when it was announced that volume 2 would not only contain both Japanese and English audio, but also contain the subtitled Japanese version of the episodes from the first release. However, as the release date drew near, people with early copies started noticing errors and missing details in the animation. A further shot by shot comparison with the Japanese release [1] revealed a consistent decrease in quality between the two versions, causing cries for a boycott of the release in some online forums. So far Kitty Media has yet to comment on the matter.

[edit] Bible Black Video Game

Bible Black the Anime is based on a Hentai Game released on July 14th, 2000 by Active-Soft -- the same company that produced Discipline and Cleavage (both of which were animated into OVAs as well). An English translation was released on December 3rd, 2006 by Kitty Media.

Unlike the anime, the game is very heavily plot-oriented. While the anime series dropped about 70% worth of plot and development in favor of sex-scenes, the game is a lot more in-depth and all acts of sex are more or less in context with the plot. It features approximately 30 hours of gameplay with 12 different endings (one of which is depicted in the anime series). However, all of the hentai scenes can later be viewed sans the rest of the plot through a special mode called "Erotic Mode"

However, owing to the fact that the game is seven years old, it tends to look a bit dated. The art isn't as detailed as later hentai games and it is obvious after playing through the game that Milky Studio took a fair number of liberties while re-designing the Bible Black characters for the anime series. Rika Shiraki, for instance, looks a lot more mature in the anime as does Reika Kitami. Kozono Nami, of the Origins series, is actually blonde and unidentified in the game (she can be seen in the opening sequence that contains a ritual).
