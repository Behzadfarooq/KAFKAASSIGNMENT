Bibliography of Warhammer Fantasy Roleplay publications

The following bibliography of Warhammer Fantasy Roleplay publications aims to catalogue all officially published products containing rules relating to the various editions of Warhammer Fantasy Roleplay. The role playing game was first published in 1986 by Games Workshop, and has also been published by its subsidiary, Flame Publications, and licenced out to a third party, Hogshead Publications. The second edition of the game is currently being published through Black Industries, the role playing game imprint of Games Workshop's publishing arm, BL Publishing. All publications for WFRP 1st edition came in softback format unless stated otherwise below. All publications for WFRP 2nd edition (under Black Industries) came in hardbark format unless stated otherwise.

[edit] First edition

[edit] Published by Games Workshop

  • Warhammer Fantasy Roleplay (main rulebook, hardback, 1986)
  • The Enemy Within (campaign supplement, 1986)
  • Dungeon Rooms and Dungeon Lairs (boxed floorplan set, 1986)
  • Character Pack (1st edition, expanded rules for character generation, 1987)
  • Shadows over Bögenhafen (second part of The Enemy Within Campaign, 1987)
  • Death on the Reik (boxed edition, third part of the The Enemy Within campaign, 1987)
  • Warhammer City (Middenheim sourcebook, 1987)
  • Power Behind the Throne (fourth part of The Enemy Within campaign, 1988)
  • Death on the Reik (republished as a hardback, 1988)
  • Warhammer Campaign (hardback collection of The Enemy Within and Shadows over Bögenhafen, 1988)
  • Something Rotten in Kislev (fifth part of The Enemy Within campaign, 1988)
  • Realm of Chaos: Slaves to Darkness (first of the two Realm of Chaos volumes, joint WFRP/WFB/WH40K hardback supplement, 1988)
  • The Restless Dead (collection of scenarios previous published in White Dwarf magazine, 1989)
  • Warhammer Adventure (collection of the first three parts of the The Enemy Within campaign, 1989)
  • Warhammer City of Chaos (collection of Warhammer City and Power Behind the Throne, 1989)
  • The Empire in Flames (sixth part of the Enemy Within Campaign, 1989)
  • Warhammer Fantasy Roleplay (main rulebook republished as a softback with minor corrections, 1989)
  • Realm of Chaos: The Lost and the Damned (2nd volume of the joint WFRP/WFB/WH40K supplement, hardback, 1990)

[edit] Published by Flame Publications

  • Character Pack (2nd edition - 2nd edition of the character pack, not the game as a whole, 1990)
  • The Doomstones Campaign part 1: Fire in the Mountains (1990)
  • Lichemaster (a redesigned scenario pack from the second edtion of Warhammer Fantasy Battle, 1990)
  • The Doomstones Campaign part 2: Blood in Darkness (1990)
  • Warhammer Companion: A Grimoire of arcane knowledge (collection of scenarios and additional rules, 1990)
  • The Doomstones Campaign part 3: Death Rock (1990)
  • The Doomstones Campaign part 4: Dwarf Wars (this did not have the Doomstones name, but it is the fourth title in the series, 1990)
  • Death's Dark Shadow (scenario pack, 1991)
  • Castle Drachenfels (scenario pack based on Jack Yeovil's novel Drachenfels, 1992)

[edit] Published by Hogshead Publications

  • The Enemy Within 1: Shadow over Bögenhafen (reprint of Warhammer Campaign, 1995)
  • Apocrypha Now (additional rules, 1995)
  • The Dying of the Light (scenario pack, 1995)
  • The Enemy Within 2: Death on The Reik (reprint, 1996)
  • The Doomstones Campaign part 1: Fire & Blood (collection of Fire in the Mountains and Blood in Darkness, 1996)
  • The Doomstones Campaign part 2: Death & Wars (collection of Death Rock and Dwarf Wars, with three new pages, 1997)
  • GM's Screen & Reference Pack (1997)
  • Middenheim: City of Chaos (reprint of Warhammer City, 1998)
  • The Enemy Within 3: Power Behind the Throne (reprint with a new 14-page prologue, 1998)
  • Marienburg: Sold Down the River (Marienburg sourcebook, 1999)
  • The Enemy Within vol. 4: Something Rotten in Kislev (reprint, 1999)
  • Death's Dark Shadow (reprint, 2000)
  • Apocrypha 2: Chart of Darkness (additional rules, 2000)
  • Doomstones 3: Heart of Chaos (a new fifth part for the Doomstones campaign, 2001)
  • Realms of Sorcery (magic sourcebook)
  • Dwarfs: Stone and Steel (Dwarf scourcebook)

[edit] Second edition

[edit] Published by Black Industries

[edit] 2005

  • Warhammer Fantasy Roleplay: A Grim World of Perilous Adventure (Second Edition rulebook, including a short adventure - March 2005)
  • Character Record Pack: The Indispensable Aid to Help Players Create Mighty Heroes (50-sheet pad and black and white booklet in a colour wrap, March 2005)
  • Plundered Vaults: Scenario Pack (three classic scenarios updated from the previous edition, and three new scenarios - March 2005)
  • Game Master's Pack: The Complete Set of Charts and Tables (Four A4 colour screens and black and white booklet, April 2005)
  • Old World Bestiary (monster and NPC sourcebook - April 2005)
  • Ashes of Middenheim: Paths of the Damned (first volume of the Paths of the Damned campaign - May 2005)
  • Warhammer Fantasy Roleplay Collectors Edition (leather-bound hardback version of the Second Edition rulebook, June 2005)
  • Old World Armoury: Militaria and Miscellania (equipment sourcebook - July 2005)
  • Sigmar's Heirs: A Guide to the Empire (Empire sourcebook - August 2005)
  • Spires of Altdorf: Paths of the Damned (second volume of the Paths of the Damned campaign - September 2005)
  • Karak Azgal: Adventures of the Dragon Crag (adventure pack and dungeon sourcebook - October 2005)
  • Realms of Sorcery (magic sourcebook, including a short adventure - November 2005)

[edit] 2006

  • Forges of Nuln: Paths of the Damned (third and final volume of the Paths of the Damned campaign - February 2006)
  • Knights of the Grail: A Guide to Bretonnia (Bretonnia sourcebook - March 2006)
  • Barony of the Damned: An Adventure in Mousillon (adventure pack and Mousillon sourcebook - April 2006)
  • Children of the Horned Rat: A guide to Skaven (Skaven sourcebook - June 2006)
  • Terror in Talabheim (adventure pack and Talabheim sourcebook - July 2006)
  • Tome of Corruption (Chaos sourcebook - October 2006)
  • The WFRP Companion (collection of additional resources and rules - softback, November 2006)
  • Character Folio (adventuring journal for recording PCs' exploits - booklet form, November 2006)
  • Game Master's Toolkit (GM's screen - screen and booklet form, November 2006)
  • Renegade Crowns: Adventures Among the Border Princes (Border Princes sourcebook - December 2006)

[edit] 2007

  • Lure of the Liche Lord: An Adventure in the Border Princes (adventure pack - softback, February 2007)

[edit] Forthcoming from Black Industries

  • Night's Dark Masters (Vampire sourcebook - softback, April 2007)
  • Tome of Salvation (religion or ‘divine’ sourcebook - July 2007)
  • Realm of the Ice Queen (Kislev sourcebook - softback, November 2007)
