The simple station Biblioteca Tintal is part of the TransMilenio mass-transit system of Bogotá, Colombia, which opened in the year 2000.

[edit] Location

The station is located in southwestern Bogotá, specifically on Avenida Ciudad de Cali with Calles 30 and 33 Sur.

It serves the María Paz and Tairona neighborhoods.

The Tintal Plaza shopping center is located nearby.

[edit] History

In March of 2005, the Avenida de Las Américas line was extended along Avenida Ciudad de Cali. This station was included in that extension.

The station is named Biblioteca Tintal due to its proximity to one of the city's mega-libraries, located on the site of a former factory.
