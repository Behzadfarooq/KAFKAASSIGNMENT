Bible of Dreams is the fourth album released by the multi-genre encompassing trance group Juno Reactor.

[edit] Track listing

All tracks made by Ben Watkins with collaborators mentioned on the tracklist.

  1. "Jardin De Cecile" – 7:03 (with Mike Maguire, Paul Jackson, Xavier)
  2. "Conga Fury" – 8:06 (with Amampondo)
  3. "God Is God" – 6:46 (with Nick Burton)
  4. "Komit" – 8:12 (with Mike Maguire, Paul Jackson)
  5. "Swamp Thing" – 5:12 (with Johann Biley, Mike Maguire)
  6. "Kaguya Hime" – 6:41 (with Mike Maguire, Nick Burton)
  7. "Children of the Night" – 7:57 (with Johann Biley, Mike Maguire)
  8. "Shark" – 9:37 (with Nick Burton)
  9. "High Energy Protons (Orion Mix)" – 6:28 (with Mike Maguire, Paul Jackson. Only available on UK release)
