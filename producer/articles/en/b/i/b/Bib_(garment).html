Bib (garment)

Decorative, transparent protective bib, which is secured around the neck, protects the fine clothing from crumbs. Modeled by Mark Allyn, its designer.

A bib is a garment worn hanging from the neck on the chest to protect clothing from spilling. It may be made of cloth, plastic or other materials. Bibs are frequently used by children but also by some adults. During pregnancy, some women choose to wear a bib while eating.

The term bib may also refer to the part of a garment that covers the chest. For instance, an apron that covers the chest may be referred to as a bib apron. The part of a jumper dress or an overall that covers the chest may also be referred to as a bib.
