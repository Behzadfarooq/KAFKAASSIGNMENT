[edit] Speculation On Reika Kitami

From what has been seen in Bible Black: La Lancza de Longinus thus far, I speculate that Reika Kitami is now split into two separate beings: Kurumi Imari, the policewoman, and Jodi, a half-ghostly being.

In Bible Black: La noche de Walpurgis, the ritual that was supposed to have transferred Kitami's spirit into Imari's body may have been partially disrupted, causing Kitami's spirit to be split asunder. As a result, part of Kitami's spirit was successfully transferred to Imari while another part became an otherworldly being named Jodi, who lives outside the normal concept of life. Both beings desire to live life, but are tortured in some way, perhaps because of their split existence.

Kitami herself may be instinctively trying to become one being again, though how this would done is unknown. Perhaps the solution lies in a young detective whom she knew in his high school days - and perhaps he could heal her wounded soul and help to turn her away from evil.

Fascinating, isn't it? --Chameleon (Marc3, 2006).
