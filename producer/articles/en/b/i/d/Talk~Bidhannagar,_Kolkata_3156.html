[edit] External links

I could not decipher why the external links of Nicco Park and City Centre are being removed again and again.Could the person give any cogent reason? Amitsanyal

Hi! I removed the external links because they don't have any direct relationship with the Bidhannagar article. Nicco Park's and City Centre's official websites may be added to their respective articles. See Wikipedia:External links and Wikipedia:Spam for details. You may also like to create those articles and add the links. Hope that clarifies my stand. --Victor 19:38, 24 December 2006 (UTC)
Sorry Sir,I am not at all conviced.The determination of the relational locus is so subjective as not to be so easily commented on.To me,City Centre and Nicco Park are not so important items as to create separate articles upon.It appears to me,that they are only important in their direct locational relation to Bidhannagar.Strangely enough,you want to accord priority status to clicksaltlake.info,which is a private website and with responsibily I can vouchsafe that it contains myriads of serious mistakes(except a good interactive map) capable enough to mislead the people.Lastly,the heading contains an unpardonable mistake.Being a Bengali word,it should have been Bidhannagar instead of Bidhan Nagar.Amitsanyal
Nicco Park was already created. As for City Centre I created it. Its very difficult to scale importance, but the topics are not trivial.
As for http://www.clicksaltlake.info, I removed it because Bidhannagar Municipality's site contains sufficient information (and also a map, though not clickable).
Bidhan Nagar is an incorrect form of Bengali, maybe we should consider renaming the page Bidhannagar. Thanks Amitsanyal. --Victor 04:59, 27 December 2006 (UTC)
