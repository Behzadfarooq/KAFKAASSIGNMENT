"Biding My Time" is a composition by Pink Floyd bassist and main songwriter Roger Waters. Prior to the Relics compilation album, "Biding My Time" was an unreleased recording, heard only by fans who attended concerts where the band performed their early concept piece, The Man and the Journey, where the song appears as "Afternoon". During the guitar solo at the end of the song a tuba can be heard which is in fact being played by keyboardist Rick Wright. He also played it on "Jugband Blues".

[edit] Personnel

  • Roger Waters – bass and vocals
  • David Gilmour – guitar
  • Richard Wright – piano,tuba,organ and trombone
  • Nick Mason – drums and percussion
