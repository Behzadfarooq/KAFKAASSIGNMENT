Bijean Gayle was born in Kingston Jamaica on March 1st in 1983. Growing up on Grove Rd. in the heart of Kingston he received an early musical baptism from his father Lester Gayle Snr, who immersed the home and his children in the soulful melodies of early Roots Rock Reggae and American R & B. The voices and rhythms of iconic singers, Aretha Franklin, Nat King Cole, Dennis Brown, and Barrington Levy, came alive in the Gayle home. BiJean modeled the depth and soul of these great sounds ingrained in him, at his first talent show win, at age four.

BiJean’s tenacity and love for music grew with age. At fourteen, he formed a Group Called H.U.S.H (Honorable Unique Sound of Harmony) and enjoyed considerable success as a performing group where they entertained various audiences, including the then Governor General of Jamaica Sir Howard Cooke. The late Phillip Linton of Arrows Studio, acknowledged the groups talent by recording their first demo which was the song “Lady.”

Having decided on a solo career Bijean Gayle is committed to the process of creating rich diverse reggae music. He believes that everything you do in life leads you to your ultimate destiny. As a testimony to his own belief, BiJean has lead a life entrenched in music in hopes of achieving success as a singer and artist.

As the first step, BiJean signed a three year recording contract with EMP MUSIC LLC in October 2006. EMP hired Clive Hunt to produce the first album. Currently, they have released a three song promotional CD, entitled “Spiritual Poverty,” as a prequel to the album. Spiritual Poverty excites everyone who listens to it with its mix of hard hitting dancehall beats and sweet melodic Reggae choruses and instrumentals.

This vibrant young artiste has gained valuable experience and exposure on shows across the Island and has earned respect from his commanding performances on the various stages, including Reggae in the Hills, Island Village Showcase, and Air Jamaica Jazz & Blues Festival.

Bijean Gayle is intent on becoming a great Jamaican artist and he continuously pushes himself and his music to the next level in attainment of his musical destiny.
