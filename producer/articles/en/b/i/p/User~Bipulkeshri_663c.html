[edit] About Me

I started playing around with computers ever since I was in school. This passion that started as a hobby is now my profession. I have been working on multimedia and web design for almost 6 years. Having extensive knowledge of 3D animation, linear and interactive presentations, website design and coding in Flash (ActionScript), I have worked for a wide range of media including broadcast, CDROM, print and the web. I have also imparted training in multimedia and web design at premier training institutes.
