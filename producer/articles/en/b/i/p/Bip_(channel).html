Bip (Hebrew: ביפ‎) is a comedy channel which was founded in 2002 and is currently included on the channel pack of the Israeli digital system Hot.

As of 2006, the channel broadcasts mostly non-Israeli based television shows, such as South Park and Married... with Children, but also includes several Israeli based television shows which are available for this channel only. Most of its shows are limited for the age 14 and older.

The channel normally broadcasts for about 12 hours, beginning in 12 PM and ending in 12 AM (with up to a half hour earlier or later ending).
