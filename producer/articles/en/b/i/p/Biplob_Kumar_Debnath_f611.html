Biplob Kumar Debnath

Biplob Kumar Debnath is an Indian born author. He and his family moved to the United States in 1987, when Biplob was nine years old. He enrolled at the University of Illinois at Chicago in 1995 as a Computer Science major. Upon graduation, he realized that he did not enjoy programming and began to write a book. It is titled A Guide to the Western Civilization. According to Mr. Debnath, it is both an autobiography and a critical analysis of the issues that "could eventually lead to the demise of the West." The book is nearly complete and will be published by Mariner Books in late 2007.
