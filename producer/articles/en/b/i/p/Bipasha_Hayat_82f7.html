Bipasha Hayat (Bengali: বিপাশা হায়াং) (born March 23, 1971) is a Bangladeshi actress. She successfully acted in TV, theatre and in film and considered one of the leading actress of the country.

[edit] Carrier

During the 90’s she played many popular television dramas which established herself as one of the leading actresses of the country. Bipasha was also successful in theatre dramas but had to leave after her wedding. She was married to Taukeer Ahmed, a famous actor and film director.

[edit] Movies

She acted in two of the famous liberation war related movies Aaguner Parashmoni and Joyjatra. The first of those which was directed by Humayun Ahmed, she portrayed the role of a young girl who was blocked in her house with her family inside the city of Dhaka. Ratri as her characters name, fell in love with a guerrilla freedom fighter (played by Asaduzzaman Noor) who lived in there house during the war for shelter. Her affection towards the fighter reflects the support and contribution of the civilians to the guerrilla fighters of the war. In Joyjatra, her character was much more different as she acted as a middle aged mother who lost her son as she tried to escape from an army attack. Both of her performances however was hailed by the audiences and critics.
