Bienosaurus ("Bien's lizard") was a dinosaur from the Early Jurassic. The genus was a armored dinosaur from the Lower Lufeng Formation in Yunnan Province in China.

[edit] Discovery and species

The discoverer and person who first described the specimen was Dong Zhiming in 2001, based on a right lower jaw and teeth. Other known material includes a nearly complete right mandibular ramus with teeth and several cranial fragments.
