Bieruń-Lędziny County

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Bieruń-Lędziny County (Polish: powiat bieruńsko-lędziński ) is a powiat (county) in Poland, in Silesian Voivodeship. The county seat is the town of Bieruń. The population numbers 55,802 (2005). Its area is 156,68 km².
