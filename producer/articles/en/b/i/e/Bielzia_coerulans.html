Bielzia coerulans is species of slug from family Limacidae.

[edit] Description

It is blue slug 120 - 160 mm long. Juvenile specimens are brown.

[edit] Distribution and biotope

Endemic in Carpathian Mountains.

  • Not listed in IUCN red list - not evaluated (NE) [1]
  • The Czech Republic - vulnerable (VU) in Moravia [2]
  • Poland
  • Slovakia
  • Ukraine

Biotope: woods.
