Bivona is a mountain town of Sicily in the Province of Agrigento. The municipality has about 4,069 inhabitants.

[edit] Event

The Most important event in the Summertime of Bivona is the Peach Festival, that is organized by the local government in the second half of august, and it is the main attraction of the small mountain town. The white Peach of Bivona, in fact, represent, besides a rare deliciousness, the most common cultivation in all region of Bivona.
