[edit] Intro

The Pancho Family is a made-up group at a school, BCHS. This 'group' is also in a story (the person who created this page is creating that story). It all started on the field playing a game, Manhunt. After a few rounds, Nick yelled "Pancho!" for some reason, thus somehow creating the Pancho Family. Each person in the group was given a name (seen below).
