The Birmingham Lions are a member of the British Collegiate American Football League (BCAFL)'s Southern Conference-Central Division. The Lions broke into the BCAFL with a bang, becoming the first team to reach the College Bowl in its inaugural season, only to wait 15 years until playing in it again. While a consistent contender in the BCAFL's Southern Conference throughout the past decade (only one losing season in their past twelve), the Lions had a difficult time putting together victories in the playoffs until recently. In the 2005 playoffs, the Lions put together 4 victories, including one over the Glasgow Tigers in the College Bowl, to complete a perfect 12-0 season. Despite being upset in the Sothern Conference semifinal by eventual College Bowl winner Southampton, the Lions in 2006 became only team to complete three straight perfect (8-0) regular seasons.
