Bird Island is used to name dozens of islands throughout world.

  • Bird Island (Algoa Bay)
  • Bird Island (Isla Aves) - located in the middle of the Caribbean Sea, currently disputed by Venezuela and the Commonwealth of Dominica.
  • Bird Island (Namibia), an artificial island in Walvis Bay.
  • Bird Island, Queensland is an island within the Whitsunday Islands, Australia.
  • Bird Islands, Queensland are islands in Far North Queensland, Australia.
  • Bird Island, Seychelles
  • Bird Island (South Georgia).
  • Bird Island (Massachusetts) is an island in Buzzards Bay with a lighthouse.
  • Bird Island, North Carolina is an island in the state of North Carolina
  • Bird Island (Fox Islands) is an island in the Fox Islands group of the Aleutian Islands, Alaska, U.S.
  • Bird Island, Lake Zway is located in Ethiopia
  • Bird Island Nature Reserve in Soutafrica

Bird Island is the name of two places in the state of Minnesota in the United States:

  • Bird Island
  • Bird Island Township
