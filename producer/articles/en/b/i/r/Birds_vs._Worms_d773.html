"Birds vs. Worms" is a 1994 recorded and 1997 released single by indie rock band Modest Mouse. The single features "Birds vs. Worms" on it's A-side, and "Every Penny Fed Car" and "Four Fingered Fisherman" on it's B-side. All three songs were later released on Sad Sappy Sucker in 2001. Only 500 blue powder copies of this vinyl were ever made.[1]
