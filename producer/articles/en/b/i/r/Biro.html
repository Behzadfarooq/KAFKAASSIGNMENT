Biro

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

For other uses, see Biro (disambiguation).
Look up Biro in

Biro is a common term for a ballpoint pen, (though not in American English). The name comes from the pen's inventor, Hungarian-born Argentinian László Bíró[1].
