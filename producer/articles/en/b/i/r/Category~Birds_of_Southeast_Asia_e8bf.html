This category is for birds that are indigenous or naturalised in South-east Asia. Countries included are:

  •  Brunei Darussalam
  •  Myanmar
  •  Cambodia
  •  Timor-Leste
  •  Indonesia
  •  Laos
  •  Malaysia
  •  Philippines
  •  Singapore
  •  Thailand
  •  Vietnam

Birds that are introduced but not naturalised are not included.
