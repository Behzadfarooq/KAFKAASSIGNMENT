[edit] Plots

[edit] The Story

This is the first and shortest installment of the 3 (or more) book series. This is based when the characters are pre-teens and teens. One of the few villians in this story is Robby. Robby has threatened to destroy several cities, such as the hometowns of the few kids. The group is split into groups or leaves individually. The first person to do so is Dallas, planning to get to the top of one of the many towers. Dakota and Ryan go to another tower, planning to kill an executive that helps Robby, and destroy his helicopter. Trevor drops the two off at the tower, flies off into a window in another tower, drops a grenade and flies away. The next few are Alex, Tyler, Nick and Graham, going off to destroy the technology headquaters. The last four to come are Jose, Michael, Kris and Karim. Those four try to destroy the weapon tower; surrounded by large amounts of enemies until their friends come and help. A few of them stay to blow it up; all the rest steal as much as they can.

[edit] The Story: In The Streetz

This story is the second installment of the series. This is based on when the kids are out of jail - but on the streets. Some of the few have left for another city, but there are some new friends to replace them. Thus creating, the Pancho Family. The gang is still around for 3 years, until they decide to leave. Dakota goes to British Columbia to live there. A lot of more new friends come; yet some decide to become villians. At the end, one of the smart new people, Fuzz, creates a time warp, to go to the year 2052.

[edit] The Story: Life or Death

The 3rd part of the series. The gang has gone into their 20s, gone to the future; with some old friends. A week after going, Dakota shows up, getting a note: "Dear Dakota, Evan and them came back! Fuzz created a time warp and we went to the future. If you get this, step in. Signed, Kikyo." Dakota goes in, and finds himself in the year 2052. When he gets there, he also finds another note, saying: "Dakota! If you got here, we are in a war! World War III! Get to Toronto as soon as possible! Signed, Ryan." As soon as Dakota gets to Toronto, he finds his friends, in Elite Squad suits. Dakota joins in and fights with his old friends, in the war.

[edit] The Story: Back in Action

Not mentioned in the last piece, but the last line of the story just happens to continue the war. The gang decides to fight once again, to save their planet, and all life on it. Anyone who gets killed, gets killed in this story. No exceptions. Some people could possibly live the war. Even though some will die, they will be remembered for fighting one hell of a war.

[edit] Characters

[edit] Dakota

Dakota, in the first story, lived in a large house with his friends, but he doesn't know they are there. Jesse tells him the news about Robby, and he participates. After 4 years of jail, he creates the Pancho Family with his friends. After moving to BC for a year, Dakota returns, and goes to the future. Leader of the Riot Squad in WWIII.

[edit] Ryan

Ryan is a mutated-robot chipmunk (or squirrel) in the first story. After being fixed in the jail, he joins back with Dakota to get in the gang. Co-Leader of the Pancho Family. Ryan goes with the friends to the future after Dakota left. Leader of the Bomb Squad in WWIII.

[edit] Tyler

Tyler is one of the few melee masters. Only uses a simple handgun in the first story, though uses a wooden pole and steel nunchucks. In the third part, Tyler uses a sword and other weapons. Co-Leader of the Technology Squad in WWIII.

[edit] Blues

One of the few villians in the stories. Real name : Dan. An old friend of Fuzz and Dakota, though left for the crime business after he was knocked out of the Pancho Family. Killed by his friend Fuzz.

[edit] Trucha

Trucha is the main villian in the third and fourth story. Real name : Leo. Kills alot of the troops on the good side of the war. Formed a gang against the Pancho Family although the gang is unmentioned in the story.

[edit] Graham

Graham is also known as the great art master. Still a great artist in the third and fourth story. The graffitist in the Pancho Family and leader of the Design Squad in WWIII (Designs all the vehicles, bombs, etc).

[edit] Fuzz

Fuzz is one of the smartest people on the earth. Real name : Cheyenne. He is partially seen in the second installment, and has a bigger part in the third & fourth story. Creates the time warp to get to the future. Leader of the Technology Squad in WWIII.

[edit] Kikyo

One of the few females in the gang. Real Name : Kirsten. A good friend of Dakota & Ryan. She leaves a note for Dakota to go to the future. A member of the Bomb squad in WWIII. Gets brought back to life after being killed.
