The Birmingham Zulus are a football firm associated with Birmingham City. They took their name from a racist chant of "Zulu, Zulu" which was aimed at their predominantly black supporters by Manchester City fans in 1982, and quickly became a group of football hooligans in England.[citation needed]

The Birmingham Zulus were also featured in the 2005 film Green Street. The match shown in the film is supposedly between West Ham United and Birmingham City with a a brawl after the match between the Zulus and the Green Street Elite (GSE), the name used in the film for the Inter City Firm(ICF)[citation needed]
