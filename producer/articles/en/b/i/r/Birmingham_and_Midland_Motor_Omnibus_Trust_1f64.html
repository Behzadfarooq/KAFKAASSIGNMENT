The Birmingham and Midland Motor Omnibus Trust (BaMMOT) is a charity that runs a transport museum just outside Birmingham, at Chapel Lane, Wythall, Worcestershire, England.

The Trust was formed in 1977 and the museum site was acquired in February 1978. The museum has two halls, presenting a significant collection of preserved buses and coaches, especially Midland Red buses and Birmingham City Transport vehicles, plus a notable collection of battery electric vehicles such as milk floats.
