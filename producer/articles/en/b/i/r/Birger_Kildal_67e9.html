Birger Kildal

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Birger Kildal (1849-1913) was the Norwegian Minister of Auditing 1884-1886, as well as head of the Ministry of Postal Affairs in 1885. He was a member of the Council of State Division in Stockholm 1886-1887 and 1904-1905, Minister of Justice and Minister of Labour 1887, Minister of Labour 1887-1888, Minister of Finance 1895-1898, and Minister of Finance and Minister of Auditing 1903-1904.
