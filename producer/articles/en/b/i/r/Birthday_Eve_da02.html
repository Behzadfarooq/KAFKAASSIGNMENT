[edit] Information

This is a limited edition single by Kumi Koda, 50,000 copies available. "Birthday Eve" was used in a television advertisement for NTT DoCoMo Tokai (エヌ・ティ・ティ・ドコモ東海, enu ti ti dokomo tōkai?).

[edit] Cover

Each of the twelve singles' covers represent a traditional dress from a culture. This single's cover represents America.
