Birmingham



Birmingham is one of the major cities of the United Kingdom and the largest borough in the metropolitan county and metropolitan area of the West Midlands. This category is a subcategory of the West Midlands category, which contains further articles about things in Birmingham's metropolitan area.

For more information, see the article about Birmingham.
