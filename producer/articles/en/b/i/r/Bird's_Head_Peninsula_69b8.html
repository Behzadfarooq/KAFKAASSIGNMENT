Bird's Head Peninsula

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Bird's Head Peninsula seen from space (false color)

The Bird's Head (Indonesian: Kepala Burung, Dutch: Vogelkop) Peninsula or Doberai Peninsula is a large peninsula the makes up the northwest portion of the Province of West Irian Jaya, Indonesia, at 1.5° S 132.5° E.
