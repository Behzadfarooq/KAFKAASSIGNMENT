The Birthday Committee is an unofficial Wikipedia group that wishes Happy Birthday to members found in the listings at m:List of Wikimedians by birthday and Wikipedia:Birthday Committee/Calendar through their talk pages. More than one person from the committee may send messages to the same person. Below is a transclusion of the Birthday Committee calendar below for easy reference.
