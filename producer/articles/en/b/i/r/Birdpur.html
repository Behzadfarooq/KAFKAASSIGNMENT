Birdpur

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Birdpur is a town in Siddharth Nagar District in Uttar Pradesh in India located at 27.3833° N 83.1167° E. It is at an elevation of 92 m (301 feet).

It was an estate (Zamindari) during British Raj, it was a part of the United Province. During 1896-97, the place became widely known as excavations in a village named Piprawa yielded remains associated with Buddha.
