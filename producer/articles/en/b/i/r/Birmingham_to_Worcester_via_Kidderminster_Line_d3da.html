The Birmingham to Worcester via Kidderminster Line is a railway line from Birmingham Snow Hill to Worcester via Stourbridge and Kidderminster.

The line serves the following places:

  • Birmingham Snow Hill
  • Jewellery Quarter
  • The Hawthorns
  • Smethwick
  • Langley Green
  • Rowley Regis
  • Old Hill
  • Cradley Heath
  • Lye
  • Stourbridge
  • Hagley
  • Blakedown
  • Kidderminster
  • Hartlebury
  • Droitwich Spa
  • Worcester

Passenger services are provided by Central Trains between Birmingham and Worcester and beyond, and by Chiltern Railways between Birmingham and Kiddermister only. A triangular junction at Worcester allows a variety of service patterns (see map). Some trains terminate at Shrub Hill, whereas some reverse there before going to Foregate Street. Other trains take the direct curve to Foregate Street avoiding Shrub Hill. Some trains continue beyond Foregate Street to Great Malvern or Hereford.

Trains along this route occasionally also serviced Birmingham New Street, where they terminated. This link was removed, to much local consternation.
