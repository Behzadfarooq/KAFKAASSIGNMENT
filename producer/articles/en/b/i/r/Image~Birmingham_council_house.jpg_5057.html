In the background is the City of Birmingham Council House (built 1879) which is considered one of Birmingham's finest Victorian buildings as seen from Victoria square in the city centre.

In the foreground is the fountain known as 'The River' and the sculpture known as the 'River Goddess' by Dhruva Mistry (opened 1993) : Photo by G-Man Sept 2004. (Alternative view)
