Birmingham Convention Quarter

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Birmingham Convention Quarter is a neighbourhood in the centre of Birmingham, England. It contains the following organisations:

  • BBC
  • BRMB
  • Birmingham Repertory Theatre ("The Rep")
  • Brindleyplace
  • Broad Street
  • Galaxy
  • Heart FM
  • ITV Carlton
  • International Convention Centre (ICC)
  • The Mailbox
  • National Indoor Arena (NIA)
  • National Sealife Centre
  • Number 9 The Gallery
