Birmingham City School District, also called BPS for Birmingham Public Schools, is the public school district for Birmingham, Michigan. It has one of the top public school districts in Michigan.

Beyond Birmingham, the district provides public school services for all areas of the following municipalities: Beverly Hills, Bingham Farms, Franklin and Southfield Township, Michigan; and parts of these municipalities: Bloomfield Hills, Bloomfield Township, Southfield (city), Troy and West Bloomfield Township, Michigan.
