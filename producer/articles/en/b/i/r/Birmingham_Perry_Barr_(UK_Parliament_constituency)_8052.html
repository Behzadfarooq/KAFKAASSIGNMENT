Birmingham Perry Barr is a borough constituency represented in the House of Commons of the Parliament of the United Kingdom. It elects one Member of Parliament (MP) by the first past the post system of election.

[edit] Boundaries

The constituency covers a broad area of north-west Birmingham, adjacent to the borders with Sandwell and Walsall. Perry Barr itself is the location of a University of Central England campus. Much of the deprivation is concentrated on the Handsworth area. The constituency features a large social economic range from inner city Lozells to relatively wealthy Great Barr and the traditional working class suburb of Oscott.

[edit] Boundary review

Following the review of parliamentary representation in Birmingham and the West Midlands, the Boundary Commission for England has created a modified Perry Barr constituency, with the following electoral wards:

  • Handsworth Wood, Lozells and East Handsworth, Oscott and Perry Barr
