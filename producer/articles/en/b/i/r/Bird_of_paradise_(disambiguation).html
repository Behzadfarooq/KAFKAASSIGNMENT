Bird of paradise (disambiguation)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

A Bird of paradise is a member of the family Paradisaeidae of the order Passeriformes.

Bird of paradise may also refer to:

  • the plants named after the birds:
    • Strelitzia reginae
    • Strelitzia
    • Caesalpinia
  • Huma (mythology), the Phoenix from Persian mythology
  • Bird of paradise (card), a card in the card game Magic: The Gathering
  • Bird of paradise (Charlie Parker song), by saxophonist Charlie Parker, based on the chord changes of jazz standard All the Things You Are
  • Bird of paradise (Snowy White song) and Bird of paradise (album), by guitarist Snowy White
  • Bird of paradise (The Appleseed Cast song), a song by The Appleseed Cast on their album Low Level Owl, Volume 1
  • Bird of paradise (film), referenced in the stage production of Kiss of the Spider Woman
  • Bird of paradise (hotel), a re-appearing hotel on Lilo & Stitch: The Series
  • Bird of Paradise (Xiaolin Showdown), the title of a 2005 episode of the animated television series Xiaolin Showdown
This disambiguation page lists articles associated with the same title. If an internal link led you here, you may wish to change the link to point directly to the intended article.
