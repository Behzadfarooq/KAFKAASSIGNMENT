Birinci Lig is the highest league of football in Turkish Republic of Northern Cyprus, currently contested by 14 teams.

[edit] History

Following the formation of Cyprus Turkish Football Federation as a result of Turkish Cypriot clubs withdrawing from taking part in Cypriot national competitions, Birinci Lig was founded by Doğan Türk Birliği, Çetinkaya Türk Spor Kulübü, Mağusa Türk Gücü, Gençlik Gücü, Baf Ülkü Yurdu Spor Kulübü, and Gençler Birliği on 1955.
