Birchview Elementary School is a U.S. public primary school located in Plymouth, Minnesota which educates children in kindergarten to fifth grade. It is part of the Wayzata School District.

The mascot of Birchview Elementary School is the bobcat. As of 2005, the school principal is Dr. or Mr. Tom Koch.

Annually, the school enrolls approximately 530 students; 86% are Caucasian, 6% Asian/Pacific Islander, 5% African American, and 3% American Indian. The average student to teacher ratio at the school is 19:1. Approximately 14% of the students who attend qualify for the free or reduced lunch program, compared to the state average of 27.3%.
