The Birmingham Brummies are a British Speedway team. Accepted into the Premier League in 2007 after reformation, the club race at the Perry Barr Greyhound Stadium.

It was announced that former Argentinian and Italian National Champion Emiliano Sanchez would be racing for the team for the 2007 season.
