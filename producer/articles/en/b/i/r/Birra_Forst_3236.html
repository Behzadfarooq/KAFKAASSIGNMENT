Birra Forst, Brauerei Forst, or simply Forst, is an Italian brewing company, founded in the frazione Forst (Foresta) of Algund (Lagundo) in 1857. Forst was founded by two entrepeneurs in Merano (Meran). In 1863, the company passed to Josef Fuchs, who built the current factory in Forst, where it benefits from the fresh mountain water.

Forst has an annual production of 700,000 hl, serving primarily the domestic market. Besides producing beer in Forst, the company also maintains a factory in Palermo.
