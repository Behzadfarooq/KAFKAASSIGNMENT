Biro Funk is the debut album by Braintax, released on Low Life Records in 2001. It was recorded at Christie's own Low Life Records studio in Shepherd's Bush. The album features guest appearances from other stars of UK hip hop and scratches performed by DJ Harry Love of the Scratch Perverts.

In the long writing process for this album, Christie recorded over 40 tracks, most of which were dismissed and only the strongest made the final cut.
