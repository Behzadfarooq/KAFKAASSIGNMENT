Main article: Somali clan

Biyomaal or biimaal is a Somali clan, they live along the coastline of the southern part of the country Somalia. They belong to the main clan of Dir. Biyomal/Biimals had fought against Italian colony in 17th Centery. They became first Somali clan to stand alone against foreign colony. During Biyomal - Italian war, prominent Italian colony military cornels and leaders were killed whom their memorial menuments still can be found in Merca town of lower Shabelle region. Biyomals were very few civilised clans in 17th century and they had a sultunate that governs all coastal lines of lower Shabelle region. During last decades of Somali civil war, Biyomal became part of unarmed clans in Southern Somalia but has great influence in the policy of the failed state. Abdulahi Sheik Ismail is one of prominent veteran politicians belonging to Biyomals.
