Biyomon is the name for several fictional characters from the Digimon franchise. It is a Bird Digimon and a character from Digimon Savers. Its Japanese name is Piyomon. Its English name is a misspelling of the Japanese name, which comes from the Japanese word, piyopiyo, which is the sound of chirping. Biyomon is voiced by Haruhi Terada.

[edit] Appearance

Biyomon resembles a large, pink bird with blue-tipped feathers on his head, and a ring on one of his legs.

[edit] Description

In Digimon Savers, a male Biyomon appeared as the Digivolved form of Puwamon, who hatched from an egg that had appeared in the real world before Masaru's family. He became friends with Masaru's sister, Chika, and eventually vowed to protect her. However, when Masaru learned that if Biyomon and Chika became partners, they would be forced to join DATS. Thus, he became determined to keep Chika out of battles and vowed to send Biyomon to the Digital World as Digi-Egg; if Biyomon remained, it would become overcome by human emotions and would have run wild, like numerous Digimon had in the past. However, Masaru's battle with Biyomon was interrupted by Falcomon, and later, Mercurimon.

Mercurimon overpowered the entire DATS team, and also reverted Biyomon to a Digi-Egg. Upon returning to the Digital World, Mercurimon sensed the "human's evil" inside Biyomon's egg, and not even he could contain it. It hatched, revealing Biyomon in a dazed state and having limited memories of Chika. Mercurimon tried to make him forget the human, but Biyomon digivolved into Aquilamon and escaped, eventually appearing in the real world. There, he battled DATS, and overpowered the entire team, leaving only Masaru remaining to fight. Aquilamon then became Garudamon, and defeated GeoGreymon. When not even Chika's pleas were enough to make Garudamon come to his senses, Masaru manifested his new Digi-Soul, and allowed GeoGreymon to become RiseGreymon. RiseGreymon defeated Garudamon, and returned him to a Digi-Egg. When Chika saw it, she said that she was glad that Biyomon no longer had to suffer.

Later, when analyzing the DigiEgg, DATS discovered Mercurimon's signal mixed in with Biyomon's, and believed him to be responsible.

Biyomon once again returned later in the series. He had been protecting a group of young Digimon in the Digital World, but a strange twister sucked him and the Digimon up, tossing the Digimon directly outside Masaru's house. However, Biyomon had no memory of Chika. Chika was convinced that it was the same Biyomon, and Yoshino and the others decided to help him take care of the Digimon. When Duftmon attacked, Chika was watching the battle from her balcony and one attack got past Ravemon and Rosemon's defenses. Biyomon protected Chika from the attack by digivolving into Garudamon. After Ikuto and Yoshino decided to head back to the Digital World in search of Masaru, Biyomon stayed behind in order to protect Chika and Sayuri. When Craniummon appeared in the Real World, Biyomon mistook him as a threat and tried to fight him. After being swatted away, Chika told him not to fight, but Biyomon refused to or risk letting his manliness rot away. When Chika decided to fight with him, her Digisoul appeared and Biyomon became Garudamon again. He was still no match for Craniummon, but he soon realized that Craniummon was actually there to help BantyoLeomon.

Like the other Digimon, after the battle against Yggdrasil, Biyomon returned to the Digital World.

Attacks

  • Spiral Twister (Magical Fire): Flaps his wings, creating green flames in front of it, then shoots it out in a spiraling inferno.

[edit] Other forms

The name "Biyomon" refers only to the Rookie form of this Digimon. Biyomon has the ability to Digivolve into a number of more powerful forms, each with a different name and special attack. However, the Rookie level is his preferred form and the one he spends most of his time in.

[edit] Puwamon

Puwamon (プワモン)

Puwamon is the form that Biyomon hatches out of his Digi-Egg into. He then permanently evolves to Biyomon.

Attacks

  • Fuwa Feather: An electrified blast is sent out from the tail feathers.

[edit] Aquilamon

This section refers to the Aquilamon featured in Digimon Savers. For the Aquilamon in Digimon Adventure 02, see Aquilamon (Adventure 02).
Aquilamon (アクィラモン)

Aquilamon was Digivolved from a revived Biyomon when Mercurimon attempted to remove the "human" influence from Biyomon in vain. Aquilamon, who is confused from the ordeal and his memories of Chika, escaped from Mercurimon & returned to the real world to go on a rain of destruction, setting things on fire with his 'Blast Rings' attack while overpowering Gaogamon and Sunflowmon. Aquilamon later digivolved into Garudamon when confronted by GeoGreymon.

Attacks

  • Blast Rings (Blast Laser): Fires ring-like rays with a sharp cry from his mouth.
  • Grand Horn (Glide Horn): Aquilamon's horns, gathering energy, turn red and then he attacks, ramming the opponent with his horns.

[edit] Garudamon

This section refers to the Garudamon featured in Digimon Savers. For the Garudamon in Digimon Adventure/02, see Garudamon (Adventure/02).
Garudamon (ガルダモン)

After taking a hit from GeoGreymon, Aquilamon evolved into Garudamon. When Chika arrived at the scene, Garudamon tried to remember the happy times he had with Chika. Unfortunately, the madness Garudamon was suffering from was too strong, and he attempted to kill the girl. But when his attack rendered Masaru's mother unconscious and left GeoGreymon near death, Masaru's anger charged him with enough Digi-Soul to evolve GeoGreymon to RiseGreymon, who destroyed the possessed Garudamon. Garudamon then became a Digi-Egg and Chika was glad her friend's suffering is over. The madness was that of Mercurimon's essence. Later on, Biyomon returns and digivolves into Garudamon to protect Chika from Duftmon's attack. Garudamon then faces off against Craniummon in the real world.

Attacks

  • Wing Blade (Shadow Wing): Uses wings and blazes up and a magical bird fires on its opponent.
