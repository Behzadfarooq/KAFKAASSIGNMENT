V.M. Shukshin Biysk Pedagogical (Teachers' Training) State University or Shukshin State Pedagogical University of Biysk (Russian: Бийский педагогический государственный университет имени В. М. Шукшина) is a university in Biysk, Altai Krai.

[edit] University History

Biysk Teachers' Institute was established in 1939 and became a pedagogical institute in 1953. In 2000 this institution of higher education received a university status. In 2001 the university was named after V.M. Shukshin.
