Biala Podlaska Voivodeship

Biała Podlaska Voivodeship (Polish: województwo bialskopodlaskie) was a unit of administrative division and local government in Poland in the years 1975–1998, superseded by Lublin Voivodeship. Its capital city was Biała Podlaska. This voivodeship has the highest percentage of ethnic Belarusians in Poland.

[edit] Major cities and towns

  • Biała Podlaska (56,300 inhabitants in 1995)
  • Łosice
  • Międzyrzec Podlaski
  • Parczew
  • Radzyń Podlaski
  • Terespol
