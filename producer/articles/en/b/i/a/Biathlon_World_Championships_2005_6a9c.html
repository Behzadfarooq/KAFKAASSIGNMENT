Biathlon World Championships 2005

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The 40th Biathlon World Championships of 2005 were held in Hochfilzen, Tyrol, Austria, from 4 to 13 March. Approximately 80,000 spectators went to see the competitions.
