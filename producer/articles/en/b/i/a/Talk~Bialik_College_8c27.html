[edit] Standards

As a current VCE student of Bialik, I question the person who has decided to write this article. Is it to be a stub that is merely used as a reference for other pages, or do you actually intend on doing the research and making this a proper page? I do not consider the mention of a financial endowment much of a reference, and know there are other things that could be added. There are a number of staff members who are quite prominent in the field of education, not to mention our principal, Genia Janover, who is recognized as one of the most experienced and devoted people in her field. I would recommend that if we do not intend on writing an article - there is an abundance of material to use if this is decided - it should be deleted. Evolver of Borg 10:45, 22 April 2006 (UTC)

Thanks Evolver of Borg, I only found this article because I was covering the biographies of Georgiou and Frydenberg. I'm not at all familliar with the school, and Xtra has marked it for cleanup. Would you be interested in rewriting the page with a fuller history? It would need some referencing in any case I suspect. Please don't sacrifice your VCE just to have an A+++ page for your school. I'd be especially interested in its beginnings; and its relationships with other Jewish school around the country. Care to help? --schgooda 14:21, 22 April 2006 (UTC)

My mistake, I looked at the message before viewing the actual article...Good start guys.--schgooda 14:24, 22 April 2006 (UTC)
