[edit] Biography

Trump is the youngest of seven children and the only girl, raised in the Bay Ridge area of Brooklyn, New York. Like many in the adult industry, she started working as an exotic dancer at the age of 18 before moving on to appearing in films.

She is best known as an adult actress who has made approximately 120 adult films during her active career, which spanned from 1991 to 1999. She was best known for her willingness to perform intense oral sex scenes, involving spanking, hair pulling, and other light forms of rough sex. Her lesbian scenes also gained her fame, as she typically played a dominant role, dominating other girls during the acts, often during rough sex scenes inflicting (or seemingly inflicting) erotic pain.

After her career in pornography, she worked as an escort, allegedly commanding fees of over $1,200 USD per hour. This ended sometime in 2002.

She declared bankruptcy in November 2001 and became a tattoo artist, falling in with white supremacist groups, even though she had taken part in countless interracial sex scenes on camera with both men and women.

However, in late 2001, her silicone breast implants ruptured, and were removed.

[edit] Criminal history

In June 1995, Trump (hereafter referred to as Iwanow) and two other ex-convicts were arrested in Ft. Lauderdale, Florida, after robbing two men. She was alleged to have masterminded various crimes, her victims being those that had previously hired her through her escort service.

Her 1995 arrest came about after she relayed the information to her "employer", regarding a man who had previously hired her services. This man "paid her $200 from a secret stash of cash tucked in a pile of newspapers in his closet". Allegedly assuring her employer about the ease of any robbery attempt, the "employer" subsequently sent the two ex-convicts to this location. They came away with on $60; enraged, they threatened the two men and were subsequently apprehended.

This did not end her clash with the law. In fact, she often caused conflicts in her Palm Beach, Florida neighborhood with those of African-American heritage. Her blatant, alleged racism reportedly culminated in a beating she suffered at the hands of 15 of her fellow citizens sometime in 2001. The police did not appear to charge anyone for the assault. In reply, she sued the Palm Beach police for failure to protect her person; the suit was dismissed from court after multiple failures to appear before the Palm Beach court.

On August 14, 2002, a SWAT team arrived at her Palm Beach residence after reporting gunshots in her home. Iwanow attempted to dismiss the incident as minor, allegedly neglecting to add that she held the team at bay with a 12-gauge shotgun, a standoff lasting until the early morning of August 15. She was placed into custody for monitoring at a psychiatric hospital.

On November 7, 2003, Iwanow was arrested at an airport in Spokane, Washington on an outstanding warrant for forgery of checks. She was traveling with Aryan Nations' Richard Girnt Butler at the time, scandling him in the process (as her pornographic past was unknown to either he or his group).

Further run-ins with the law have occurred: on February 2, 2005 police in Spokane arrested Iwanow after searching her home and finding fraudulent financial paperwork and numerous types of identification and stolen Social Security cards, as well as a rifle and two handguns (which as a convicted felon she was not supposed to have). She was charged at the time with forgery and unlicensed possession of a firearm, with further charges possible.

On June 30, 2005 Iwanow was arrested again in Spokane, this time for possession for methamphetamine, when a car she was a passenger was pulled over by the police. The driver had an outstanding warrant for resisting arrest. At that time, Iwanow was identified as someone who had been carrying a firearm during a previous contact with police. Attempting to prove she did not have a gun, Iwanow emptied her purse out on the hood the car, where three golf-ball sized bundles of meth fell out.

On November 29, 2006 Iwanow was ordered to pay $602 restitution, and spend 43 months in prison, after pleading guilty to second-degree assault, second-degree kidnapping and three counts of forgery.[1]
