Białobrzegi County (Polish: powiat białobrzeski) is a powiat (county) located about 60 km to the south of Warsaw, in Mazowsze Voivodship. The county seat is the town of Białobrzegi. The population numbers 33,545 (2005). Its area is 639.28 km².
