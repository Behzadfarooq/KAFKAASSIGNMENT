Bianca is a fictional character played by Erica Rivera on the popular Disney Channel sitcom That's So Raven.

[edit] Character Information

Bianca took over for Alana as the school bully after she got sent to boot camp and took over her bullying posse (Muffy and Loca). Bianca is so bad, she got kicked out of Boot Camp. She is annoyed by Raven as much as Alana was. [1]

Bianca is only on the series for two episodes; "Boyz 'N Commotion" and "Gettin' Outta Dodge". No explanation is given for her disappearance. Raven tries to get off to a good start with her, but Bianca turns down Raven's request for friendship because she hates Raven's shoes.
