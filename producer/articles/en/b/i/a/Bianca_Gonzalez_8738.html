Bianca Monica Malasmas Gonzalez[1] (born March 11, 1983) is a Filipina TV personality. Bianca started modeling at the age of 17 and has done many product endorsements and pictorials for magazines like Candy, Seventeen, Meg, Mega, Chalk, etc. before she was discovered on TV. She is well known for her humble attitude and her friendliness to all kinds people. She is also well known for her youth advocacy. She graduated high school at the De La Salle Santiago Zobel School and graduated with a degee in AB Mass Communication at the Ateneo de Manila University.

She was one of the 14 housemates of ABS-CBN's Pinoy Big Brother: Celebrity Edition. Before she entered the Pinoy Big Brother house she was a co-host in several television shows, including the morning news show Magandang Umaga, Pilipinas (Good Morning Philippines), Y! Speak, Wazzup Wazzup, and the defunct Stardance dance reality show and MTV U-Break. She was an early crowd favorite for her friendy and down-to-earth attitude since her first day in the Pinoy Big Brother House.

She landed at 3rd place and garnered 245,594 votes or 19% during the Pinoy Big Brother: Celebrity Edition's Big Night held at the facade of the Manila Post Office last April 1. Her choice of charity is the Gabriel Symphony Foundation, an organization founded by Senator Pia Cayetano that helps children with cleft palate.

Bianca is now currently in a relationship with Lino Baker Cayetano, a pop-film director and the son of the late senator Renato Cayetano. However, her friendship with fellow housemate Zanjoe Marudo sparked controversies that led Lino to file a leave from ABS-CBN to go to France. Marudo immediately apologized to Cayetano concerning the issue.

Right now she is going to host the UpLate edition for Pinoy Big Brother: Teen Edition as Mariel Rodriguez' replacement.
