Bianca Butler

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Bianca Butler (born September 02, 1989 in Laguna Beach, California) is an American pair skater. She currently competes with Joseph Jacobsen. They are the 2007 US National junior silver medalists. They qualified for the 2005 Junior Grand Prix Final in their first year on the circuit, and placed sixth. Butler & Jacobsen are coached by former World silver medalists Jenni Meno and Todd Sand.
