Bia Figueiredo (Ana Beatriz Figueiredo, born on March 18 1985, in São Paulo) is a Brazilian female racing driver.

[edit] Career

She started her career in karting races.

After an outstanding season in Brazilian Formula Renault championship, she started competing in Formula Three Sudamericana in 2006 driving for the well-established Cesario Formula team.

In same year she drived a Red Bull Volkswagen Touareg for the PPD sports, the comapny of Pedro Diniz that hosted events of the Brazilian Formula Renault championships, Renault Speed Show.
