Bianca is a female personal name from the Italian word for "White".

It may refer to:

  • Bianca Jagger (born 1945), a social and political activist
  • Bianca Beauchamp (born 1977), a Canadian model
  • Bianca Lawson (born 1979), an American actress
  • Bianca Gonzalez (born 1983), a Filipina TV personality
  • Bianca Ryan (born 1994), an American singer
  • Bianca (Othello), a character from William Shakespeare's play Othello
  • Bianca Montgomery, the daughter of the fictional character Erica Kane on the daytime television series All My Children
  • Bianca (That's So Raven), a character on the television series That's So Raven
  • Miss Bianca, a character from the animated films The Rescuers and The Rescuers Down Under
  • Bianca (film), a 1984 film by Italian director Nanni Moretti
  • Bianca (1913 film), a silent film starring Patricia Palmer
  • Bianca (moon), one of the moons of Uranus
  • 218 Bianca, an asteroid
  • bianca.com, the first web-based chat room
