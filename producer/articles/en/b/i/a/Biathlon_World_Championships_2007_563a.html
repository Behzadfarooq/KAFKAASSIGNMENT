Biathlon World Championships 2007

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

You have new messages (last change).

The 42nd Biathlon World Championships was held in Rasen-Antholz, South Tyrol, Italy from February 2 to February 11, 2007.

[edit] Opening ceremony

  • Date / Time: Fri February 3, 2007 / 20:00 CET
    • Italian Prime Minister Romano Prodi opened the Championships, where 30 countries and 300 athletes took part.
