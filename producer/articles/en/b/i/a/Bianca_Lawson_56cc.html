Bianca Jasmine Lawson (born March 20, 1979 in Los Angeles, California, USA) is an actress. Her father, Richard Lawson was a soap star actor.

Lawson started acting at the age of nine. She has appeared in television commercials for Barbie and Revlon and in several television shows. Most prominent of these being Buffy the Vampire Slayer, where she portrays Kendra Young, another Vampire Slayer who is awakened as a result of Buffy's (temporary) death in the first season finale. She has also appeared in Saved by the Bell: The New Class and films such as Save the Last Dance and Breakin' All the Rules.

She briefly dated actor Rey-Phillip Santos. She is currently dating Ernesto Bautista, a singer signed to Virgin records.
