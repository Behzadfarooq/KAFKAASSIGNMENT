Biff Byford

Peter "Biff" Byford, (born on 5 January 1951, in Barnsley, Yorkshire, England) is an British vocalist with heavy metal band Saxon, formed in Barnsley, England in 1976, with guitarists Graham Oliver and Paul Quinn, bassist Steve Dawson, and drummer Pete Gill. He is also about the release his autobiography, entitled Never Surrender in April 2007.
