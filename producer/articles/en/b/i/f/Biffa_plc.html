Biffa plc

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Biffa plc is one of the UK's largest integrated waste management businesses, providing collection, landfill and special waste services to local authorities and industrial/commercial clients. Biffa was demerged from Severn Trent plc on 9th October 2006 giving Biffa an initial market capitalisation of £932.4m and a turn over of £712.3million in 2005/6[1]. Biffa has made good profits in the first year since flotation.[2]

The Chairman of the Biffa Group is Bob Davies.
