The Delegation Palace in Bilbao.

The Bizkaia Delegation Palace is an eclectic mansion in Bilbao, Spain. It's the seat of the executive branch of Government of Biscay, the legislative assembly meets in Gernika. It was built between 1890 and 1900 by architect Luis Aladren.
