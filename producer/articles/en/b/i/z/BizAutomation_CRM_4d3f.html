BizAutomation.com LLC is a line of business software applications company. It offers packaged and on-demand versions of its all in one business suite, which its customers - mostly small to medium enterprisesuse both on-premise as well as Software as a Service on-demand (SaaS).

[edit] History

BizAutomation.com, LLC. of Redondo Beach, Calif., founded in 2002, pioneered the concept of CRM integration with Outlook and Exchange, which includes the popular Outlook Web Access (client) aka OWA. The company started with basic CRM in 2002-2005 then in 2007 evolved its suite to include e-commerce and ERP functionality such as inventory and accounting. The company's mission is to bring all-in-one system automation to small businesses with the same ease of use and affordability that made products such as QuickBooks and ACT so popular with small businesses for the last 20 years.

[edit] Services

Now that BizAutomation.com will be releasing it's super suite as a service the company will officially be also considered a service provider rather than just a software vendor. However, many analysts agree that software vending is becoming intermingled with service provisions anyway. This is due in part to many micro businesses outsourcing back office functions.

[edit] Features

BizAutomation is a broad application covering business functions as diverse as CRM, ERP, ecommerce, customer support and marketing automation. Each of these also includes groups calendaring and tasking functions.

[edit] Implementation

BizAutomation can be simply installed or subscribed to. For the vast majority of companies it needs to be properly implemented. This has led to the rise of independent consulting firms and value-added resellers.
