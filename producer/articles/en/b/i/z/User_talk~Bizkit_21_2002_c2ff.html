[edit] Magazine covers

Hello Bizkit 21 2002. Please do not upload any additional magazine covers to serve as portraits for the people on their covers. Wikipedia policy regarding fair use, based largely from U.S. law, does not allow for the use of copyrighted magazine covers unless they are a part of critical commentary about the magazine itself. You have also had a number of other image uploads deleted recently. Please thoroughly review Wikipedia:Image use policy and Wikipedia:Fair use before uploading any additional copyrighted images. Remember, Wikipedia is a free content encyclopedia, and only uses copyrighted images in very particular circumstances, usually when a free alternative does not exist, and can not be created. If you have any questions whatsoever, please do not hesitate to ask at Wikipedia:Media copyright questions. ×Meegs 11:57, 18 June 2006 (UTC)
