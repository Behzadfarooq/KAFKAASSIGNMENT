Bishop Ready High School is a private, Catholic high school located in Columbus, Ohio. The school is part of the Roman Catholic Diocese of Columbus.

[edit] Ohio High School Athletic Association State Championships

  • Basketball - 1972,1973
  • Football – 1983
  • Wrestling – 1982,1990
  • Girls Softball – 1995
