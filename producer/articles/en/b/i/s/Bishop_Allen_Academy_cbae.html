Bishop Allen Academy (BA) is a mid sized high school located in Toronto's west end overseen by the Toronto Catholic District School Board. It was founded in the fall of 1989 on the defunct lands of Kingsmill Vocational Collegiate. The high school has about 1400 students and over 100 teachers.
