Biston is a genus of large, long-winged moths belonging to the family Geometridae. It is most notable for containing the well-known Peppered Moth.

[edit] Species

Species include:

  • Biston abruptaria
  • Biston achyra
  • Biston bengaliaria
  • Peppered Moth Biston betularia
  • Biston edwardsi
  • Biston insularis
  • Biston marginata
  • Biston multidentata
  • Biston pustulata
  • Biston recursaria
  • Biston regalis
  • Biston robustus
  • Oak Beauty Biston strataria
  • Biston strigaria
  • Biston suppressaria
  • Biston thoracicaria
