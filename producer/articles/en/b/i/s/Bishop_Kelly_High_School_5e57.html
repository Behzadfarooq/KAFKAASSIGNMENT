Bishop Kelly High School is a private high school in Boise, Idaho, USA operated by the Roman Catholic Diocese of Boise. It was established in 1964.

[edit] Notable Graduates

  • David Bieter, Mayor of Boise - Class of 1978
  • William Petersen - Class of 1972
  • Ben Ysursa, Idaho Secretary of State - Class of 1967
  • Nick Symmonds, runner (2006 USATF national championship).
