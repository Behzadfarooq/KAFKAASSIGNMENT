Bismarck is an unincorporated town in Grant County, West Virginia, USA. It lies on West Virginia Route 93 near Mount Storm Lake.

Bismarck was named in honor of Prime Minister of Prussia, Otto von Bismarck (April 1, 1815 – July 30, 1898). Because of various spellings including Bismarch and Bismark, the Board on Geographic Names officially decided upon Bismarck in 1895.
