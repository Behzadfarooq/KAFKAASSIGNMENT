Bishnupur is a census town in South 24 Parganas district in the state of West Bengal, India.

[edit] Geography

Bishnupur is located at 22.38° N 88.27° E[1]. It has an average elevation of 6 metres (19 feet).

[edit] Demographics

As of 2001 India censusGRIndia, Bishnupur had a population of 4529. Males constitute 53% of the population and females 47%. Bishnupur has an average literacy rate of 82%, higher than the national average of 59.5%; with male literacy of 86% and female literacy of 77%. 9% of the population is under 6 years of age.
