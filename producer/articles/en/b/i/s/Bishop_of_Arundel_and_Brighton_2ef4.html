The Bishop of Arundel and Brighton is the Ordinary of the Roman Catholic Diocese of Arundel and Brighton in the Province of Southwark.

The diocese covers 4,997 kmÂ² of the Counties of East and West Sussex and Surrey outside the Greater London Boroughs. The see is in the town of Arundel where the seat is located at the Cathedral Church of Saint Philip Neri.

The diocese was erected on 28 May 1965 from the Diocese of Southwark, upon the elevation of that diocese to archdiocese status. The current bishop is The Right Reverend Kieran Thomas Conry, the 4th Bishop of Arundel and Brighton.
