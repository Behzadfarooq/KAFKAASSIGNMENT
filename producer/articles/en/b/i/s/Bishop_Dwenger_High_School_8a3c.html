Bishop Dwenger High School

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Bishop Dwenger High School is a coed Catholic high school in Fort Wayne, Indiana, in the Roman Catholic Diocese of Fort Wayne-South Bend. The school was founded in 1963. Bishop Dwenger, a Diocesan Catholic high school, is founded on the life and teachings of Jesus Christ.
