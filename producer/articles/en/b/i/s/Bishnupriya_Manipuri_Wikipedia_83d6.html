Bishnupriya Manipuri Wikipedia

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The logo for the Bishnupriya Manipuri Wikipedia.

Bishnupriya Manipuri Wikipedia is the Bishnupriya Manipuri language edition of Wikipedia.

The project started on November, 2006. It passed the border of 5,000 articles on November 21, 2006 and the 10,000 mark on December 24 2006.

As of February 8, 2007 it has nearly 12 275 articles and is currently the 53th largest Wikipedia.
