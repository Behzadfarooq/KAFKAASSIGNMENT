Bishop Rawstorne C of E Language College is a school is situated in Croston, Lancashire. There are around 950 students in this school ranging from ages of 11 to 16. The first year is called Year 7 and the last year is called Year 11, when the students can leave to attend college, get a modern apprenticeship or get employment.

All students are separated into tutor groups of around 30, which are lead by a person called the tutor. The tutor takes the register each day and cares for the pupils during their five years at the school. The tutors are called A, B, C, H, M, X, such as 9H or 11C.

The school consists of around 50 to 60 rooms, split over various blocks:

  • Main Block
  • English and Media Studies Block
  • Science Block
  • Technology Block
  • Sports and Fitness Block
  • Administration Block (Which includes the main reception and library)
