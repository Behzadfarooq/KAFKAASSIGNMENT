Bismarck Municipal Airport (IATA: BIS, ICAO: KBIS) is a public airport located three miles (5 km) southeast of city of Bismarck in Burleigh County, North Dakota, USA.

A new $15 million terminal opened at the airport in May 2005. The previous terminal was in use for over 40 years and will be demolished and a parking ramp will be built on the property.
