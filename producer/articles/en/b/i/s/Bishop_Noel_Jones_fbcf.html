Bishop Noel Jones

Bishop Noel Jones, is the senior pastor of the City of Refuge Church in Gardena, California. Jones was born in Spanish Town, Jamaica January 31, 1950. The City of Refuge has about 17,000 members and was formerly the Greater Bethany Community Church. Jones is the older brother of singer and actress Grace Jones. Bishop Jones is divorced, with three children, all of whom are fully grown, the youngest being around 30 and two of which are happily married, with children of their own. Bishop Noel Jones currently lives a Christian single life, though he does have a love interest. Jones is known for his scholarly messages with a Pentecostal twist and can be seen on his television broadcast "Fresh Oil" on BET and the Church Channel.

Bishop Jones received a Bachelors of Science in Theology from Aenon Bible College. Jones is often the guest of his long time friend Bishop T.D. Jakes at his Megafest conferences. Jones has appeared on TBN, CNN, and BET for numerous occasions.

Jones founded Noel Jones Ministries in 1994.
