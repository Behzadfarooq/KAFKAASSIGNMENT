Bisco Hatori

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Bisco Hatori (葉鳥ビスコ, Hatori Bisuko?) born August 30 is a manga artist. She has worked for such magazines as LaLa. Her manga debut was A Moment of Romance in the LaLa DX magazine. Next came the cut-short Millennium Snow. The comedy Ouran High School Host Club, however, is her breakout hit.

When she's stuck thinking up names for her characters, she is inspired by loud, upbeat music (her radio is set to NACK5 FM). She loves reading all kinds of manga, but is especially fond of Please Save My Earth, a sci-fi manga, and the basketball classic Slam Dunk.

Bisco Hatori is a pseudonym, and not her real name; she states that the name has special meaning to her.

[edit] Works

  • A Moment of Romance (2001)
  • Millennium Snow (2001)
  • Ouran High School Host Club (2003)
  • Romantic Egoist
