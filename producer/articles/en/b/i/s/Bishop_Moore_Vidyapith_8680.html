Bishop Moore Vidyapith, Mavelikkara, [Kerala]], is a well known CSI school, and one of the public schools in India. It is affiliated to the ICSE board of education. It was established in 1975 in name of Bishop Rt. Rev Edward Alfred Livingstone Moore.

The current principal is Mr A C George; past principals have included the well-known educators Prof.George M Cherian etc. The school, with its adjoining chappel, is well known in south India not only for its curricular prowess but also for the extracurricular merit shown by its students. At present the school is equipped with a basketball court, Sunil Gavasker Stadium, computer lab and three science labs.

The school motto is "The Truth Shall Make You Free".

[edit] Houses

The students are divided into four houses, each named after four precious stones. They are Ruby (red), Emerald (green) and Sapphire (blue),Topaz (yellow). Competition between the houses takes place in the form of inter-house activities like sports, athletics,and Youth Festival. Meritorious houses are awarded house points and at end of year a house cup is awarded to the best House on basis of points earned.


[edit] Activities and awards

Non-competition activities take place in the form of various clubs like drama, space, nature, quiz, and various other clubs, each hosting a number of public events throughout the year.The annual interschool quiz organised by the quiz club.The students call themselves Bishop Moorians, as does the annual school magazine,The Vidyapith.

[edit] School timings

School is open on week days from Monday to Friday. The timings are, as follows:

Start -8:45 a.m.
Assembly - 8:55
Periods 1 to 3 - 9:25 to 11:25
Break - 11:25to 11:35
Periods 4and 5 -11:35 to 12:50
Lunch -12:50-1:30
Periods 6and7 -1:30-2:50
Break -2:50-3:00
8th Period - 3:00-3:30
Dispersal- 3:40

Apart from these standard timings, many extra-curricular activities are held after school (e.g. inter-class football, cricket etc matches). School is also open on Saturdays for Cubbing, Scouting , the chess club, school cricket team practice.
