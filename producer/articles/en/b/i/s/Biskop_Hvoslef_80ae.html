The Biskop Hvoslef

The Biskop Hvoslef is a veteran Norwegian sailing vessel built in 1933 that was used in search and rescue by the Redningsselskapet (Norwegian Society for Rescue at Sea) on the Norwegian coast between 1933 and 1964. It is now a privately-owned vessel.
