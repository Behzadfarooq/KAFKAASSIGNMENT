Biswanath Chariali is a town and a town area committee in Sonitpur district in the state of Assam, India.

[edit] Demographics

As of 2001 India censusGRIndia, Biswanath Chariali had a population of 16,830. Males constitute 53% of the population and females 47%. Biswanath Chariali has an average literacy rate of 80%, higher than the national average of 59.5%; with male literacy of 85% and female literacy of 75%. 9% of the population is under 6 years of age.
