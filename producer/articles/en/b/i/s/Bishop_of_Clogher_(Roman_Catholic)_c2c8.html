Bishop of Clogher (Roman Catholic)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Bishop of Clogher is the Ordinary of the Roman Catholic Diocese of Clogher. The bishop resides in the town of Monaghan.

The diocese covers much of south west Ulster, taking in most of Fermanagh and Monaghan and parts of Cavan, Leitrim and Donegal. The current bishop is the Most Reverend Dr. Joseph Duffy.

The Church of Ireland and Roman Catholic Church each have a Diocese of Clogher and each its own diocesan bishop and clergy. For details of the Church of Ireland bishops see Bishop of Clogher (Church of Ireland)
