Biskra Province

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Biskra (Arabic: ولاية بسكرة , Berber : Tibeskert) is a wilaya of Algeria. The capital city is Biskra. Tolga is one of famous Dairas of this Wilaya. Other localities include Lichoua, Sidi Okba, Sidi Khaled, El-Kantara and Ouled Djellal.
