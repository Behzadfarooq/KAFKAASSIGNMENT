Bismarck-Mandan refers to the metropolitan area (known as the "Bismarck, ND Metropolitan Statistical Area") in North Dakota that includes the twin cities of Bismarck and Mandan, the small suburb of Lincoln, and several small nearby "bedroom communities". The cities of Bismarck and Mandan straddle the Missouri River. The area has around 100,000 people. Bismarck is the capital of North Dakota.

[edit] Core cities

  • Bismarck, North Dakota
  • Mandan, North Dakota
  • Lincoln, North Dakota

[edit] Bedroom communities

  • Wilton, North Dakota
  • Flasher, North Dakota
  • New Salem, North Dakota
