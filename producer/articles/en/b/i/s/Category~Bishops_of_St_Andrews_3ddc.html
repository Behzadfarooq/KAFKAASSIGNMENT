Category:Bishops of St Andrews

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Bishops of St Andrews is a collective name for every Bishop of St Andrews (formerly ARDEPSCOP ALBAN and EPISCOPUS SCOTTORUM), the highest ranking see in the Medieval Scottish Church.
