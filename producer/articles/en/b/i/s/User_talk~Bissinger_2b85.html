[edit] Incomplete deletion

Don't be discouraged by the comments on your talk page... it's good to have editors who are willing to work on maintaining articles. But it would be good to be sure you generally understand our processes before making a larger number of changes.

I just wanted to note that regarding Ananda Saint James and Amy LePeilbet, the articles have an incomplete nomination for deletion. Nominating an article for AFD requires three steps (see How to list pages for deletion). The articles either need to have the last two steps completed, or if you've changed your mind about the deletion, then the AfD notice should be removed.

If you have any questions, feel free to ask on my talk page. --Interiot 20:14, 25 January 2007 (UTC)
