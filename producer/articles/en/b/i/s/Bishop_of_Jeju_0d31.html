Bishop of Jeju

The South Korean Catholic Diocese of Jeju was created as an Apostolic Vicariate in 1971 and became a diocese in 1977, both by Pope Paul VI. The diocese is suffragen to Archdiocese of Gwangju.

[edit] The Bishops of Jeju

  • Harold Hyun (1971-1977)
  • Michael Jung-il Park (1977-1982)
  • Paul Chang-ryul Kim (1982-2002)
  • Peter Woo-il Kang (2002-present)
