The Bishop of Kensington is one of four Suffragan bishops of the Diocese of London in the Church of England. The office is responsible for a part of Greater London including Kensington, Hounslow, Hampton, Hammersmith and Fulham, and Spelthorne. As of 2006, the current holder of the office is Rt Rev Michael Colclough.
