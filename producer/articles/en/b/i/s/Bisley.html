Bisley may refer to

  • Two villages in the United Kingdom.
    • Bisley, Surrey
    • Bisley, Gloucestershire
  • Bisley Ranges is near the Surrey village and is the headquarters of the National Rifle Association, UK (NRA). Many different types of military and civilian shooting competitions are held here, and a Combined Cadet Force competition for school pupils called the Ashburton Shield is also held annually in mid July.
There are two fullbore rifle ranges. 'Century', which can be used for Target Rifle shooting from 100 to 600 yards with 108 target lanes, and 'Stickledown', with 50 target lanes, which is used principally for long distance Target and Match Rifle shooting at distances from 700 to 1200 yards. Other ranges include 'Running Deer' and 'Cheylsmore'.
  • Bisley, the initial name for the ground attack version of the Bristol Blenheim bomber of World War II.
  • Bisley, a solitaire card game.
  • Simon Bisley, a British Comic book artist
