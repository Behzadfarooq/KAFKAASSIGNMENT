Biscayne Bay Campus

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The FIU-Biscayne Bay Campus is a regional campus located in North Miami, FL. The Biscayne Bay Campus (BBC) is part of Florida International University's main campus 40 minutes away (via Panther Express, the student bus system) in Miami, FL. It is the largest regional campus in the state of Florida and is the home to degrees such as hospitality management, marine biology and other freshmen classes. Currently, a new marine biology building is under construction as well as an expanded student union and dining hall.
