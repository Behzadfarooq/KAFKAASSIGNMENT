The Bishop of Northampton heads the Catholic Northampton Diocese, a suffragan see of the Province of Westminster, England.

The see is in the town of Northampton where the Bishop's cathedral or seat is located in the Cathedral Church of Our Lady and Saint Thomas of Canterbury.

The current bishop is the Right Reverend Peter Doyle, who was ordained bishop on 28 June 2005.
