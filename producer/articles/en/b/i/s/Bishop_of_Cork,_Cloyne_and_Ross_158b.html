Bishop of Cork, Cloyne and Ross

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The current Church of Ireland's Bishop of Cork, Cloyne and Ross is the Right Reverend William Paul Colton, B.C.L., Dip.Th., M.Phil. He was consecrated bishop at Christ Church Cathedral, Dublin, on Thursday 25 March 1999 -- the Feast of the Annunciation. He was enthroned in St. Fin Barre's Cathedral, Cork on 24 April 1999, in St. Colman's Cathedral, Cloyne on 13 May 1999, and in St. Fin Fachtna's Cathedral, Ross on 28 May 1999.
