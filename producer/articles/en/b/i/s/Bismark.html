Bismark is a town in the district of Stendal, in Saxony-Anhalt, Germany. It is situated approx. 22 km west of Stendal.

Bismark is part of the Verwaltungsgemeinschaft Bismark/Kläden, which consists of the following municipalities (population in 2005 between brackets):

  • Badingen (510)
  • Berkau (487)
  • Bismark (Altmark) (3.292)
  • Büste (Altmark) (387)
  • Dobberkau (315)
  • Garlipp (199)
  • Grassau (287)
  • Hohenwulsch (426)
  • Holzhausen (Altmark)(113)
  • Käthen (143)
  • Kläden (bei Stendal & bei Arendsee)* (741)
  • Könnigde (171)
  • Kremkau (224)
  • Meßdorf (738)
  • Querstedt (247)
  • Schäplitz (118)
  • Schernikau (458)
  • Schinne (484)
  • Schorstedt (307)
  • Steinfeld (Altmark) (328)

The seat of the Verwaltungsgemeinschaft is in Kläden.
