Bishop Borgess High School is a Catholic secondary school in the Detroit suburb of Redford, Michigan.

The Archdiocese of Detroit closed the school in 2005 after a decade of declining enrollment, but it reopened in 2006 as a Christian academy offering grades 4-12.
