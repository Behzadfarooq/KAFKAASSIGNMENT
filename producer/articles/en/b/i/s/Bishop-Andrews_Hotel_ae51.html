Bishop-Andrews Hotel

The Bishop-Andrews Hotel is a U.S. historic building in Greenville, Florida. It is located at 109 Redding Street, on U.S. 98. On June 28, 1990, it was added to the U.S. National Register of Historic Places. The building has been converted into the Grace Manor Inn, a bed and breakfast house.
