The Bismarck Blaze are a now-defunct arena football team from Bismarck, North Dakota. They were a 2000 expansion member of the Indoor Football League. During their only year of existence, the Blaze won their division and made it all the way to the Gold Cup, yet fell to the Pirates of Peoria. After the year, the IFL folded, as with the Blaze.
