Bishopsgate Institute

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Bishopsgate Institute and Ince's Umbrella Store, circa 1900.

The Bishopsgate Institute is an educational foundation located in the City of London opposite Liverpool Street station.

The Institute opened in 1894 and provides a range of educational courses. It also houses Bishopsgate Library, which offers public access to its historical collections on London and the early labour movement. Bishopsgate Institute has a wide range of meeting rooms for hire as well as large function halls for examination purposes, conferences and exhibitions. Designed by Charles Harrison Townsend, the Institute is amongst the most original buildings of the date in London.
