[edit] Miscellanea

  • Michaelangelo is heard saying "The future's so bright I gotta wear shades", which is the title of a popular 1986 song by Timbuk 3.
  • As obvious by the title, Bishop, from the last series, is pictured for the first time in this episode.
  • The bounty hunter, Torbin Zixx, is first pictured here. It is unknown if he will be pictured again.
  • Sh'Okanobo is mentioned and pictured here, his first appearance was in a hologram by Viral in "Home Invasion".
