Bishop of Saint Andrews, Dunkeld and Dunblane

The Bishop of Saint Andrews, Dunkeld and Dunblane is the Ordinary of the Scottish Episcopal Diocese of Saint Andrews, Dunkeld and Dunblane. The see is located in St Ninian's Cathedral, in Perth. The current bishop is the Right Reverend David Chillingworth, formally of the Church of Ireland.
