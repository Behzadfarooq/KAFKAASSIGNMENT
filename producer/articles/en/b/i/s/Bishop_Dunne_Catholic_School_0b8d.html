Bishop Dunne Catholic School

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Bishop Dunne Catholic School logo

Bishop Dunne Catholic School is a private Middle/High School located in the Oak Cliff area of Dallas, Texas (USA).

[edit] History and information

Bishop Dunne Catholic School was founded in the 1960s and is a part of the Diocese of Dallas. The enrollment is about 550. It is considered to be a 6-A Private School and is in the same district as sister school Bishop Lynch, Nolan Catholic, TCA, and newly added John Paul II. The school colors are red and blue. Its mascot is a Falcon.
