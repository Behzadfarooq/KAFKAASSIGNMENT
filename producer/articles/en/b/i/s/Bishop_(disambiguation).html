Bishop can refer to:

In fiction:

  • Bishop, the last name, and nickname of Tupac Shakur's character in Juice
  • Bishop, a character from the 1993 computer game Ultima Underworld II: Labyrinth of Worlds
  • Bishop, character class for Bangaas in Final Fantasy Tactics Advance for the Game Boy Advance
  • Bishop is a fictional character from the Alien series of films.
  • Bishop (cartoon), fictional villain in the 2003 Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles animated series
  • Bishop is the name of a character in Marvel Comics' X-Men books.
  • Bishop(Wizardry Character class) a character class from the game wizardry
  • In MapleStory, the 4th Job Advancement of the Magician is the Bishop.
  • In the Suikoden RPG game series by Konami, the title "Bishop" is given to religious/political leaders and army generals of the theological Kingdom of Holy Harmonia. The Harmonian head-of-state is known as the "Chief Bishop," a title currently held by Hikusaak.

In education:

  • Bishop's University in Sherbrooke, Quebec, Canada
  • Diocesan College, also known as Bishops, is a school in Rondebosch in Cape Town, South Africa

In geography:

  • Bishop, California, city in California, USA
  • Bishop Subbasin, a groundwater basin in the Livermore-Amador Valley, California, USA
  • Bernice Pauahi Bishop Estate, the largest private landowner in the State of Hawai'i

In other fields:

  • Bishop, person of authority in a Christian church
  • Bishop Publishing Co., a publishing house headquartered in London
  • Bishop, alcoholic beverage consisting of honeyed beer or honey and mashed barley fermented like beer
  • Bishop, to make a horse appear to be younger by operating on the horse's teeth
  • Bishop (artillery), British self-propelled artillery vehicle during World War II
  • Bishop (chess), a piece in the game of chess
  • In UK slang bishop can refer to the penis, particularly in the phrase bashing the bishop (= masturbating)
  • "The Bishop" the pseudonym of an American mail bomber
  • Bishop Lamont, an American rapper
  • Bishop Allen is an American indie rock band.
  • xBishopx, a Straight Edge hardcore band from Florida, featuring Mean Pete of Until the End/Remembering Never fame.
