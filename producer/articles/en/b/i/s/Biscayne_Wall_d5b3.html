The Biscayne Wall refers to a wall of skyscrapers currently under construction on the north side of downtown Miami, Florida.

They are all being built along the west side of Biscayne Boulevard.

[edit] List of buildings comprising the Biscayne Wall

  • Met 1
  • Wachovia Financial Center, 200 Block South
  • 50 Biscayne Tower, 50 North Biscayne Boulevard
  • Everglades on the Bay, 200 Block North
  • Freedom Square, 600 Block North
  • Paramount Park, 700 Block North
  • Marinablue, 800 Block North
  • 900 Biscayne Bay, 900 Block North
  • Ten Museum Park, 1000 Block North
  • Marquis Miami, 1100 Block North
