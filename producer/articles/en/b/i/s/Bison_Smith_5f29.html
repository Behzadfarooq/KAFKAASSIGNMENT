[edit] Career

Bison Smith was born on September 24, 1973, in Los Angeles, California. In 1998 he began his wrestling career. Although he only started his career in 1998, Smith has competed for WWF, APW, UPW, UWA, and other promotions. This also includes his home promotion of Pro Wrestling IRON. In addition to wrestling with his home promotion, Smith frequently tours with Pro Wrestling NOAH in Japan where he continues to climb the ladder. Smith is often referred to as the "top gajin", or foreigner, in Pro Wrestling NOAH.

[edit] Finisher and signature moves

  • Bisontenial (belly to back inverted mat slam)
  • Frankensteiner

[edit] Championships and accomplishments

  • All Pro Wrestling
  • APW Tag Team Champion (1 time) - with Boyce LeGrande
  • IWA
  • IWA Heavyweight Champion (1 time)
  • Pro Wrestling IRON
  • PWI Heavyweight Champion (1 time)
