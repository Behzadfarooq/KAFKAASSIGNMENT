[edit] Usage

The {{BIhills}} project banner template should be added (not subst:ed) to the talk page of every article within the scope of the project. While the template does not require any additional parameters, it has a number of optional ones that add various extra features to the banner. The full syntax is as follows:

{{BIhills
|class= 
}}

General parameters:

  • class â€“ a rating of the article's quality; see the assessment department for more details.
The following values may be used:
  • FA (adds articles to Category:FA-Class British and Irish hills articles)
  • A (adds articles to Category:A-Class British and Irish hills articles)
  • GA (adds articles to Category:GA-Class British and Irish hills articles)
  • B (adds articles to Category:B-Class British and Irish hills articles)
  • Start (adds articles to Category:Start-Class British and Irish hills articles)
  • Stub (adds articles to Category:Stub-Class British and Irish hills articles)
  • NA (for pages, such as templates or disambiguation pages, where assessment is unnecessary; adds pages to Category:Non-article British and Irish hills pages)
