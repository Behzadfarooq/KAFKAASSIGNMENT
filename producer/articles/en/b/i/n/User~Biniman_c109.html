Real name: Benjamin Angelo Abraha. Born: 02.08.1989. Also known as B.I.N.I. (Best italian nigga international) (Formerly: BINIMAN) and is born in Eritrea and raised in Norway. Biniman is a rapper from the Hip Hop group Loco Familia who also has build their own record label LF records. B.I.N.I. is in studio recording his first realease album soon. The album's title for his first realease album is Mom's favorite son who will featuring artists from LF records. LF Records members and producers. B.I.N.I. is of Eritrean & Italian decent.

Biography:

Benjamin Aka B.I.N.I. came to Norway with his mother late in the 80's while his father was in Eritrea as a warrior in a war between Ethiopia and Eritrea. His father came to Norway in 95 and left the family two years later, so B.I.N.I.'s mother was left to raise her children. B.I.N.I. grew up in a bad neighborhood called "HS-Town" or "HSGO (Haanes Ghetto)" where there is lots of criminality, racism (between Norwegians & Foreigners). B.I.N.I. raps about how it is to be discriminated and when he took revenge. Before B.I.N.I. was doing rap music, he actually used to play drums in bands. But after his friend & rap partner Yoshy build a record label, B.I.N.I. decided to sign and try HIP HOP music. B.I.N.I. had to show his style for rapper Yoshy to get signed by Yoshy's record label and Yoshy was satisfied and signed B.I.N.I. right a way. B.I.N.I. became the third artist to sign to LF Records. LF Records has ten artists that have signed to their record label and nobody has left the record label. B.I.N.I. did his first unreleased self title "B.I.N.I." in 2004 and in 2005 he recorded his unreleased second album "Fantasy Blue: Lion's Heart". This year he's working on his first upcoming and release album "Mom's favorite son". The album will be produced by: B.I.N.I. (Himself), Yoshy (Rap partner), Young Diaz (B.I.N.I's producer) & Jiwasour (LF Records producer).
