[edit] Javanese or Japanese

An anon recently fixed an alleged typo from Javanese to Japanese. Can somebody who's knowledgeable about this subject confirm that it was not supposed to be Javanese? Fightindaman 21:03, 10 March 2006 (UTC)

GREAT catch. I would have read that paragraph a million times without noticing it until I went to flesh it out. It's supposed to be Javanese, according to both the "Wonder Okinawa" website and the book cited. Turly-burly 23:36, 12 March 2006 (UTC)
