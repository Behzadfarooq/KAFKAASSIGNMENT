Bingham Park and Whiteley Woods

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

View of Ranmoor from Bingham Park.

Bingham Park is a park near Brincliffe and Ranmoor, Sheffield.

Bingham Park is a grassy area high on a hillside with views across the valley, to Ranmoor. A footpath, leaving Rustlings Road serpents the valley bottom towards Whiteley Woods'. The footpath ends at Forge Dam Park, passing several dams of the Porter Brook. The park possesses tennis courts, a mini golf course and a bowling green.
