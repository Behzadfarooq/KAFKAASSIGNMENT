Binnaz Uslu, born on March 12, 1985 in Ankara, Turkey is a Turkish middle distance track runner.

She participated at the 2005 Mediterranean Games in Almería, Spain and won bronze medal in 800 m with 2:02.68’. In the category 4x400 m relay, she run another bronze medal with her teammates Özge Gürler, Birsen Bekgöz and Pınar Saka at 3:40.75’.

Uslu is student at the Gazi Üniversitesi in Ankara. She is coached by Yahya Sevüktekin.
