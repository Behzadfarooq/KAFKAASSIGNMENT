Pretty happy with this, I've put a bit of work into the history of the town & illustrated it with a few pictures. Without looking stuff up in a book I'm quite hazy on a lot of the dates so dates for various events would be good. The other number I want but can't find is the census population for Bingley Urban & Bingley Rural wards.

One thing I'd like to clear up is the alcohol related disorder that some unknown IP address highlighted. I don't live there any more, just visit my parents so I don't feel in a position to say definitely how much of a problem there is. I did ask my mum's opinion & she feels that the overt police presence has brought it pretty much under control. It could of course be argued that the need for an overt police presence is a sign of an issue. On the other hand is this all mainly because Bingley has been sucsessful in attracting revelers?
