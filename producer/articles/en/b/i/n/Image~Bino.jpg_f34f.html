[edit] Summary

J. Bino ________________________________________ J. Bino(aka Jimmy Gamble) was born in Greenville, Mississippi in 1976 and moved to Houston, Texas 1982. There in H-Town he developed a love for hip-hop listening to D.J. Action (Scarface) mix-tapes in 1985. That love stayed with him through his journey back through MS and finally settling in the music rich town of Memphis, TN in 1994. J. Bino produces tracks heavily based on live instruments, bassed out with smooth samples and original drum tracks similar to the likes of Dr. Dre and Three Six Mafia. He has developed his signature sound through experimentation using extensive inspiration from old, rare classical, soul, and golden era music. ___________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________ Biography Originally making hip-hop beats from his bedroom using FL Studio, he has since graduated to Korg Triton Extreme's, Ensoniq KS-32, and Pro Tools Digi002 to become one of the more accredited producers and engineers in the underground Memphis music industry. As part of the group, The Hussahouze Kingpinz, he has gained widespread recognition and critical acclaim from hip-hoppers nationwide. The upcoming producer is set to release an Hussahouze Kingpin album entitled, Against the grain. By the help of upcoming filmmaker Darrel Jones of Lil Horse Films and the highly credited Power 99 FM radio and television personality D. J. Lomax, J. Bino landed several coveted movie soundtrack production credits on Lil Horse Films highly anticipated movies and Blackgirlonline.com's dvd release entitled, Bootyshaker vol. 1. The producer continues to produce for The Hussahouze Kingpinz along with many other hip hop artists/groups, including Joanne Gray, Acid Reign, Yung Turk and Psyhco of Black Vision. The cautious businessman is currently seeking the best distrubution/production deal to further expose the world to his melodic masterpieces. ___________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________ Selected Production Credits "Posted in da back" -Hussahouze Kingpinz featuring Jap James and Grill (Memphis) "Young Turk Live from Jail" - Young Turk (New Orleans) "U don't want that" - Jap James (Memphis) "Do me Baby" - Hussahouze Kingpinz (Memphis) Soundtrack featured in 2 DVD's, see above paragragh "Ain't tippin" - Da Gatdammitz (Memphis) "Whippin" - Da Gatdammitz "Wit It, Wit it" - Junior featuring Da Real (Memphis) "Mr. Psyhco" - Psyhco (Richmond, VA) "F%@K The World" - Pressha (Memphis) "4 Dem Gurlz Dat Get $$$" Pressha "Sex faces" remix - Joanne Gray Joetry 101 (Philidelphia, PA) "One mo 'gain" - Snipe Dosier (Memphis) "Weighin Ova" - Chuk (Chicago, IL) The Westside "Guess who's back" - Jon Doe (Orlando, FL) "Trappaz and Rappaz"- Jon Doe featuring Yo Prince "Evolution" - Acid Reign (Orlando, FL) "Who needs forever" - Acid Reign "PimpNation" - Lil Ro (Little Rock, AR) "Weekend BoyWonder" Radio Drop- D.
