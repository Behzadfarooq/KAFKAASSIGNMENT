Live Shit: Binge & Purge (1993) is Metallica's live album containing 3 CDs and 3 VHS tapes, while a newer version contains 2 DVDs from concerts in Mexico City and San Diego (on the Wherever We May Roam Tour) and Seattle (on the Damaged Justice Tour). The Seattle concert (in particular) is widely considered by Metallica fans to be perhaps the best of their concert videos due to it being shot at the peak of the ...And Justice for All era (considered by some to be Metallica's artistic prime); The setlist was comprised almost entirely of thrash metal songs.
