Binatlı Yılmaz S.K.

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Binatlı Yılmaz Spor Kulübü is a Turkish Cypriot sports club based in Güzelyurt.

[edit] Colors

The club colors are yellow and green.

[edit] Stadium

The club's home stadium is Zafer Stadı.
