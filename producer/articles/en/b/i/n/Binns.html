Binns Inc. or Binns Kitchen & Bath Design is a family managed and owned kitchen & bath design firm in Toronto, Ontario, Canada, specializing in luxury kitchen & bath designs. The company features contemporary, traditional and classical kitchens & baths.

[edit] History

The founders of Binns Kitchen & Bath Design, Joseph and Vera Binns, emigrated from Great Britain to Canada in 1957. Originally, Joseph and Vera established their business as a home design company, operating from their Scarborough, Ontario home until they had enough business to have permanent office and showroom facilities. Vera looked after the office work, while Joe did the estimating and coordination of the tradesmen. Currently, the head office is located in Pickering, Ontario.

Binns was incorporated in 1963. Today the company is under the management of Raymond and Anthony Binns. Raymond is President and CEO of the company and Anthony is Vice-President and head of the design team. Binns has a well established reputation across North America for high-end style and award-winning designs.
