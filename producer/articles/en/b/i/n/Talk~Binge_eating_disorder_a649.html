This sounds more like Bulimia than Binge Eating Disorder, but no matter what the diagnosis it's clear that you need to get some sort of help to develop a better way to maintain your weight in a healthy manner. If you're nervous about talking to a doctor about it, you might be more comfortable in a support group or possibly just reading some books regarding eating disorders.
