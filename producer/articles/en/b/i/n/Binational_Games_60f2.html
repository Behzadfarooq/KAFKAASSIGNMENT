[edit] History

Founded in International school and normal school.

[edit] Academics

Binational games are the sport match that the school team must go to the other school and they can play. They'll stay for 5 days that the school team must have play and practice for the match. The team must win or lose the Bionational games. Back to the school, the teachers, coaches and the students must listen to the principal's talking that the principal is talking about the best players. The principal tells some player and the player comes to the principal. Then, he/she'll give the player for the prize.

[edit] Athletics

Sport in the school an very important match. Bianational Games has a variety of sports, including volleyball, soccer, baseball, hockey, ping-pong, basketball, track and field, American football, and marathon. The schools are sheltering more than all teams from other schools. It's a great match that you can play it!

[edit] Tri-Association

The school was the most of the International Schools and the Normal Schools of Colombia, Carribbean and Colombian School are meeting in the week of 5 days.
