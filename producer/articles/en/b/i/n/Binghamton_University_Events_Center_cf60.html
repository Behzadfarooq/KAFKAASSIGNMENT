BU Events Center is a multi-purpose arena in Binghamton, New York. The arena opened in 2004. It is home to the Binghamton University Bearcats basketball team and can seat 5,322 fans for home games, and over 8,000 for other large scale events. Host to the 2005 and 2006 America East Conference men's basketball tournament and the 2007 conference women's tourney , the facility has also hosted commencements and concerts such as Bob Dylan, Green Day, Incubus, Ludacris, and Harry Connick Jr.

[edit] History

The Events Center was built to replace the aging West Gym. It provided a new home for Bearcats track and field, basketball, and tennis.
