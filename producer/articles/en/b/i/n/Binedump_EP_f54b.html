Binedump EP is a compilation EP released by The Flashbulb just a few months after his last album, Kirlian Selections.

All of the tracks on this EP were previously released on Kirlian Selections except for one, "Binedump (Acidwolf Mix)". Altogether this release is supposed to comprise the more hectic, traditional The Flashbulb drill n bass style songs on the album, weeding out the more ambient, unfamiliar-style songs from the mix. The Acidwolf Mix is a remix of the title track done under Jordan's new project Acidwolf, an acid house/analog-equipment-only project in a similar vein to Richard D James' recent Analord releases. Overall this compilation is meant to be very danceable.
