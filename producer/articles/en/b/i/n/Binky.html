Binky may refer to:

  • A stuffed animal or other toy that a baby or small child is more attached to than any other, and often sleeps with during bedtime as well.
  • Binky, Lavender Brown's pet rabbit in the fictional Harry Potter series.
  • Binky, Death's steed in Terry Pratchett's Discworld series.
  • Binky, the name of a bear formerly in an Alaskan zoo.
  • A trade name for a line of baby pacifiers.
  • The name of the anthropomorphic rabbit star of Matt Groening's "Life in Hell" comic strip.
  • Leave It to Binky, the name of a comic book published from the 1940s through 1970s by DC Comics.
  • Binky the Clown, a minor character in the comic strip Garfield.
  • Binky, the word used to describe the movement a rabbit makes when it flings itself around in mid air.
  • Binky Barnes is an anthropomorphized bulldog that is a character (and a virtual band) on Arthur (TV Series).
  • Binky Abdul is a fictional character in the Nickelodeon's The Fairly OddParents.
  • Binky is a paperclip similar to the Clippit Office Assistant in the comic strip Help Desk.
  • Binky a term used to describe the high flying hops that bunnies do when they are happy. There are many variations, but they usually include a spring up into the air either from a stand still or while running. Pictures and videos of binkies can be found at BinkyBunny
