Hello everyone, my name is Jonathan and I am a college student. I attend Clarion University where I study Computer Science with a minor in Information Systems. I am a strong supporter of FIRE (Foundation for Individual Rights in Education) as well as a strong supporter of the First Amendment of the United States Constitution. I will tell you right now that I can be a hypocrite some times and I am working to stop that. If you see me violating somebody’s free speech on Wikipedia please let me know right away. I believe that all human beings everywhere should have the right to free speech as well as many other freedoms (religion, the press, etc).

I am a Roman Catholic Christian and a Moderate Democrat, I live in the United States of America (the state of Pennsylvania to be exact) and someday I want to visit Japan and China.

I hope to someday be the manager of an IT Department as well as to marry and have children.

I think that all educational institutions in the United States should have something along these lines on their Wikipedia page.
