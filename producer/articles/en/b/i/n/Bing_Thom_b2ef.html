Bing Wing Thom (Chinese: 譚秉榮; born 8 December 1940) is a Canadian architect.

Born in Hong Kong, he received a Bachelor of Architecture in 1966 from the University of British Columbia and a Master of Architecture in 1970 from the University of California, Berkeley.

He was the architect of the Canada Pavilion at Expo '92 in Seville, Spain.

In 1995, he was made a Member of the Order of Canada, Canada's highest civilian honour. He is a Fellow of the Royal Architectural Institute of Canada.
