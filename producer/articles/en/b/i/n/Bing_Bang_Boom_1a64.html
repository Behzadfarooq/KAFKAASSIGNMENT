Bing Bang Boom was the first of two albums with lead singer Nikki Nelson after the departure of original member Paulette Carlson for a solo career. Unfortunately, without Carlson's distinctive voice, the Nelson-fronted group never came close to attaining the heights reached during Carlson's days as lead vocalist. The songs seemed uninspired and lacking Carlson's charm. Perhaps the best tune on the album is "The Blame" and the title song, "Bing Bang Boom."
