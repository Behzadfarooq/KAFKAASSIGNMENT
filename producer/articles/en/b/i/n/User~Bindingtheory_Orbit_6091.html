Orbit is available in the following flavors:

Flavor Market Ingredients

Original		 U.S.A. sorbitol, gum base, glycerol, mannitol, natural and artificial flavors, less than 2% of: hydrogenated starch hydrolysate, xylitol, acesulfame k, aspartame, soy lecithin, BHT (to maintain freshness)

Spearmint		 U.S.A. sorbitol, gum base, glycerol, mannitol, natural and artificial flavors, xylitol, aspartame, acesulfame K, soy lecithin, blue 1 lake, BHT (to maintain freshness), color added.

Wintermint		 U.S.A. sorbitol, gum base, glycerol, maltitol, natural and artificial flavors, xylitol, mannitol, aspartame, acesulfame k, soy lecithin, BHT (to maintain freshness)

Bubblemint		 U.S.A. sorbitol, gum base, glycerol, natural and artificial flavors, mannitol, xylitol, soy lecithin, aspartame, acesulfame K, sucralose, BHT (to maintain freshness), red 40 lake, yellow 6.

Cinnamint		 U.S.A. sorbitol, gum base, glycerol, natural and artificial flavors, mannitol, xylitol, aspartame, acesulfame K, sucralose, soy lecithin, red 40 lake, BHT (to maintain freshness), yellow 6.

Sweet Mint		 U.S.A.

Citrusmint		 U.S.A. sorbitol, gum base, glycerol, natural and artificial flavors, xylitol, mannitol, malic acid, aspartame, citric acid, acesulfame k, sucralose, fumaric acid, BHT (to maintain freshness), color added, red 40 lake.

Lemon-Lime		 U.S.A. sorbitol, gum base, xylitol, glycerol, natural and artificial flavors, less than 2% of: mannitol, citric acid, hydrogenated starch hydrolysate, soy lecithin, aspartame, acesulfame k, fumaric acid, malic acid, BHT (to maintain freshness), color (turmeric)
Crystal Mint U.S.A. sorbitol, glycerol, natural and artificial flavors; less than 2% of: xylitol, mannitol, aspartame, soy lecithin, gelatin, acesulfame K, BHT (to maintain freshness) color (blue 1 lake)
