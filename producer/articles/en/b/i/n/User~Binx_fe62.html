Binx is the user ID for a Wikipedia member from Maryland, USA. She is a recent college graduate with a B.A. in English and an M.L.S. in Archives Management. She also contributes regularly at TV.com under the user ID of Toon_Archivist. Binx is a child of the 1980's but has an interest in films that goes all the way back to the silent era. TV, movies, and Broadway shows are the topics she most frequently looks up and contributes to here at Wikipedia. This growing article lists her interests, not where she's contributed.
