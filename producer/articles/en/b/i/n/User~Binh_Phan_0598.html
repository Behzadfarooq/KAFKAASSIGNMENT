Binh Van Phan (Vietnamese: Phan Văn Bình; born in November 10th 1990 residing in Braybrook) is a male student or human being. He is the son of Phong Huynh and Mai Phan. He has 2 siblings: a brother born at the same time as him, Thai, and a sister, Truc. He is always desperately trying to catch up with homework being thrown at him by his fellow teachers at school. Binh began his schooling at the age of 5 in 1996 in primary school, where he quickly rose over the years from an infant to the current adolescent state he is in today.

[edit] Embryo to Infant: 1990-1994

As an embryo, Binh did not require to breathe, eat, excremate or any other form of necessities a fully developed baby had to endure. Finally, on November 10th, 1990, at approximately 1.00pm, he was conceived with his brother, Thai. They were named by their grandfather and their names combined, Thaibinh, literally translates into "A Lot of Peace" or "No War."

During his infancy, Binh struggled to cope with his newly developed legs and arms. Burping, excremating, vomiting and even consumption of food had to be done with assistance, usually from his mother. Eventually, he developed the art of walking and talking which he improves and continues today.

[edit] Start of Kindergarten to Primary School: 1995-2002

Kindergarten and Primary School was content with nothing that will affect or change the outcome of his health. Overall, Binh found that they were of no benefit to him whatsoever as he did not learn as much as he could have.

[edit] Life at St Paul's College: 2003-2004

Failing basically all the schools he tried out for, Binh had to settle at St Paul's College. He met some friends there such as David Tran, who grew up to be the guitarist of Under Fictious Skies. During this period, Binh had developed bigger limbs and his body developed despite his penis remaining the same size from birth.

[edit] Life at Melbourne High School: 2005-Present

Binh gained entry into Melbourne High School in 2004 and it was not until 2005 did he arrive at MHS. He persisted in his humourous and witty ways much to the despair of the teachers and colleagues. They constantly restrict his right of free speech in class and do not allow him to use basic facilities such as toilets and the canteen during class hours. He has friends and an arch enemy by the name of Marlowe. He has evil red hair and he has a bit of a moustache going, if you see him punch him from all of us here at Wikipedia.
