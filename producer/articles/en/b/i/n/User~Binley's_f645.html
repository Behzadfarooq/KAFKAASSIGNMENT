[edit] Binley's

Binley's Founded by Brian Binley, Aimee and Walter Brinzer, Binley's has been delivering specialist health and care information solutions to the public and private sector for over a decade. Binley's is the brand name given to publications and services developed and marketed by Beechwood House Publishing Ltd. Beechwood House Publishing Ltd is part of Wilmington Group Plc. Binley's enable companies to communicate, network and develop relationships with healthcare and government professionals in the UK. Their head offices can be located in Basildon, Essex in the south east of England.

Sales and Marketing Solutions

Their marketing and communication solutions include healthcare and government databases and mailing lists, mailing and fulfilment services, ETMS & CRM database management integration, market research and a growing portfolio of market intelligence services, all of which have the aim of achieving reliable, accurate and effective marketing solutions.

Binley’s reference portfolio includes a range of printed public sector directories, online modules and maps, which are used for reference, market research and business planning purposes. Their directories and maps enjoy good circulations within the healthcare community.

Binley's good reputation had been further enhanced through the frequent introduction of new products and services. They have recently made several new additions to their portfolio including a new PCT Map of England and Databases by Therapy Area. Binley’s also undertake numerous research projects on behalf of prestigious bodies within the NHS, healthcare manufacturers and pharmaceutical industry.

Binley’s in-house team of researchers verify and fully update their data up to 3 times a year. Continuous monitoring and revision of the healthcare environment combined with dedicated sales and customer service teams, means Binley’s customers enjoy absolute confidence in their product range.
