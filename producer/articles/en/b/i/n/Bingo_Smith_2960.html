Robert "Bingo" Smith (born February 26, 1946 in Memphis, Tennessee) was a Forward/Guard for the San Diego Rockets (1969-70), Cleveland Cavaliers (1970-79) and San Diego Clippers (1979-80). He helped the Cavaliers win the 1975-76 NBA Central Division.

In 11 seasons he played in 865 Games and had 22,407 Minutes Played, a .449 Field Goal Percentage (4,776 for 10,642), .798 Free Throw Percentage (1,307 for 1,637), 3,630 Total Rebounds, 1,734 Assists and 10,882 Points.

He was inducted into the University of Tulsa Athletics Hall of Fame in 1984.
