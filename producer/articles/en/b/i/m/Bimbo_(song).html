Bimbo (song)

Bimbo was a popular song variously credited to Rodney (Rod) Morris or "Pee Wee" King. The song was recorded by Gene Autry and originally released on vinyl as a 78rpm single in the 1950s. It can also be found on the 1998 album, Always Your Pal Gene Autry. This is an album of singalong cowboy music for children.

It was later recorded by Jim Reeves, for the album Up Through the Years, produced by Chet Atkins in July, 1965, on RCA Victor.

A version sung by Suzi Miller was played on the BBC radio station's Children's Favourites show during the 1950s or 1960s.
