Billy Merrell (born January 7, 1982) is an American author and poet. He published his first book Talking in the Dark with Scholastic in 2003. He also co-edited The Full Spectrum: A New Generation of Writing About Gay, Lesbian, Bisexual, Transgender, Questioning, and Other Identities for Knopf Books for Young Readers with David Levithan. It was released in 2006.
