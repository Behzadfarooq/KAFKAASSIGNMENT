Bill Gates (disambiguation)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Bill Gates (William Henry Gates III) is the co-founder of Microsoft Corporation.

Bill or William Gates is also the name of:

  • Bill and Melinda Gates Foundation
  • "Swiftwater" Bill Gates, a frontier fortune seeker who gained a measure of fame and fortune during the Klondike Gold Rush of 1897
  • Will Gates, a writer of the 1988 movie Silent Assassins
  • William Gates, a protagonist from the 1994 movie Hoop Dreams
  • William Henry Gates, Sr, a philanthropist and the father of Bill Gates, the founder of Microsoft
  • William 'Pop' Gates (1917-1999), a Harlem Globetrotter, Basketball Hall of Fame member, and one of the first black players in the NBL
  • William Thomas George Gates (1908-1990), a British banker
  • A character from the 1908 film The Greaser's Gauntlet, played by Charles Inslee
  • A rewritten version of the song "Berlin" whose lyrics consist solely of "Bill Gates"
  • William "Bill" Gates, a character in the video game Mafia: The City of Lost Heaven.
This disambiguation page lists articles associated with the same human name. If an article link led you here, you may wish to change the link to point directly to the intended article.
