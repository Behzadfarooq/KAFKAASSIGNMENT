William Edwin Burwell (March 27, 1895 - June 11, 1973) was an American right-handed relief pitcher in Major League Baseball for the St. Louis Browns (1920-21) and Pittsburgh Pirates (1928). Born in Jarbalo, Kansas, he died at age 78 in Ormond Beach, Florida and is buried nearby Daytona Beach, Florida.
