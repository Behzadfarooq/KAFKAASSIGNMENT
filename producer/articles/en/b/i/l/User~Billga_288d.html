[edit] About Me

billga

I spend my working life on Vista and my artistic life carving stone and wood. I am raising two children and trying to be the best husband, mate, and father that I can be. I believe that spelling always counts. I believe that all available evidence supports the theory of evolution, and that there is no scientific evidence in favor of intelligent design, and so I support Pastafarianism. Learning that I'm merely a vehicle for my genes and their selfish agenda was the most liberating experience of my life. I'm pro-choice and pro-life (yes, it's possible). I support drug legalization for all drugs, that includes prescription-only meds. I support every individual's right to choose whatever they want to do, as long as they take full responsibility for their choices and actions. I'm a vegetarian but even I think vegans are nuts. I firmly believe in the effects of Global Warming and am investing in property above Sea Level + 50 feet.
