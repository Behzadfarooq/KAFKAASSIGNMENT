Bill Robertie

Bill Robertie is a backgammon, chess and poker player and author. He is one of two backgammon players who's ever won the Monte Carlo World Championship Backgammon twice (in 1983 and in 1987). Robertie also won the Pro-Am at Bahamas in the year 1993 and the Istanbul World Cup in 1994. In chess, Robertie won the U.S. Speed Chess tournament.[1]

Robertie coauthored a popular series of books on tournament no-limit Texas hold'em with 1995 World Champion Dan Harrington. The books were published by Two Plus Two Publishing.

Robertie graduated from Harvard and is a systems analyst
