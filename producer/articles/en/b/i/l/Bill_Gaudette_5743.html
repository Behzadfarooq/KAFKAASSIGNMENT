Bill Gaudette (born September 14, 1981 in Hummelstown, Pennsylvania) is an American soccer player, who currently plays goalkeeper for the Columbus Crew of Major League Soccer.

Gaudette played college soccer at St. John's University from 2001 to 2004, where he finished with a 43-13-12 record.

He was drafted in the first round, 9th overall, by the Columbus Crew in the 2005 MLS Supplemental Draft.
