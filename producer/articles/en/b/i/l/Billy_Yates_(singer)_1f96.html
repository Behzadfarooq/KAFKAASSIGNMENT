Billy Yates (singer)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

You have new messages (last change).

Billy Yates is an American country singer born in Doniphan, MO. Billy first cut as a songwriter was the George Jones' smash hit, "I Don't Need Your Rockin' Chair".
