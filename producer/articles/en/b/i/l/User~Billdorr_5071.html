I was born on December 8, 1980, and I have lived all my life in Tigard, Oregon. On the Internet I usually go by the user name Korb, Korby, or MrKorb.

I am in no way related to anybody involved in the Dorr Rebellion that I know of, as my great-grandfather Dorr did not immigrate to the United States from Germany until the 1880s. I am however a direct descendant of (and this is trusting that my geneological sources are accurate) William I of England, thus making me some kind of distant cousin to Elizabeth II of the United Kingdom.
