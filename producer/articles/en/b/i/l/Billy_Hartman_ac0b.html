Billy as Terry Woods.

Billy Hartman is a Scottish actor, well known for playing the part of Terry Woods on ITV's Emmerdale since 1995.

He also played a small role in the film Highlander. He played the part of Dugal McLeod, cousin of Connor McLeod.

Hartman also appeared as Issac Talentire in the original west end production of Howard Goodall's musical 'The Hired Man'
