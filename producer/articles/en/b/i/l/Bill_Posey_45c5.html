Senator Bill Posey

Bill Posey is currently a Senator in the Florida Senate representing District 24.

[edit] Biography

Bill Posey was born December 18th, 1947 in Washington D.C. He moved to Florida in 1956. In 1969, he graduated from Brevard Community College with his Associate of Arts.

[edit] Public Service

From 1974-1976, Bill Posey worked on the Rockledge Planning Commission, from 1976-1986, he was a member of the City Council, and from 1986 to 1992, he was a member of the Business and Industrial Development Commission.


[edit] Legislative Service

Bill Posey was elected to the House of Representatives twice from 1992 - 2000. In 2000, he was elected to the Senate, and then reelected subsequently.
