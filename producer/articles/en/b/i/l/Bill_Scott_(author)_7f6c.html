Bill Scott (author)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Bill Scott (1922â€”December 24, 2005) was an Australian author, folklorist and poet.[1] He has published many anthologies of Australian verse.

Bill Scott was born in Bundaberg, Queensland. He is best know for his song "Hey Rain" and as the compiler of The Complete book of Australian folk lore. He lived toward the end of his life in Warwick in the Darling Downs.

  • The Currency Lad. (1994)
