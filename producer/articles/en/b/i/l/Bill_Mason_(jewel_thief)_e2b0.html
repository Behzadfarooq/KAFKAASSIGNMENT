Bill Mason (jewel thief)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

You have new messages (last change).

Bill Mason (born 1940 in Hundred, West Virginia) is an American jewel thief who, in his autobiography Confessions of a Master Jewel Thief, avers that he has stolen $35,000,000 in property from private residences over his life.
