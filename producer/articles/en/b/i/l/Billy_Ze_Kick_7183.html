Billy Ze Kick were a French pop band from the 1990s comprised of young members. To the non French-speaking world information about them is difficult to come by. The name of the band is taken from the title of a book by Jean Vautrin (unless they named themselves after the 1985 film of the book).[citation needed]

The music itself usually involves the repeating of an incredibly catchy refrain with melodic rap on top. The lyrics of the songs tend to be about drug use. The band's biggest hit "Mangez Moi" (Eat Me) is about psychedelic mushrooms. "OCB" another of the band's hits, is about rolling papers.
