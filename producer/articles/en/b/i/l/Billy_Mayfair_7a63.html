Billy Mayfair

William Fred Mayfair (born August 6, 1966) is a PGA Tour golfer. He was born in Phoenix, Arizona. He lives in Scottsdale, Arizona (He plays out of Estrella Mountain Range Golf Club). He went to Arizona State University and won the 1987 U.S. Amateur Championship. He turned professional in 1988 and has won five events on the PGA Tour, including the 1995 Tour Championship. He has featured in the top 50 of the Official World Golf Rankings.

On July 31st, 2006, Billy was diagnosed with testicular cancer. He had surgery on August 3rd and it has been reported that the cancer was contained. [1]
