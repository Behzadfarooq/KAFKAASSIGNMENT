Bill Ives (born in Sydney, New South Wales) was an Australian rugby league player for Glebe, Eastern Suburbs and the St. George Dragons clubs in the New South Wales Rugby League competition in Australia, his position of choice was at prop-forward.

Brother of Australian test player Clarrie Ives.
