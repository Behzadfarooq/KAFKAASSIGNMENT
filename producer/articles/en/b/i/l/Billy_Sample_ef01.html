Billy Sample

Billy Sample (born April 2, 1955 in Roanoke, Virginia), is a former professional baseball player who played in the Major Leagues primarily as an outfielder from 1978-1986.

[edit] Teams

  • Texas Rangers: 1978-1984
  • New York Yankees: 1985
  • Atlanta Braves: 1986

[edit] Batting Stats

  • 826 Games
  • 684 Hits
  • 46 Home Runs
  • 230 RBIs
  • 98 Stolen Bases
  • .272 Batting Average

[edit] Trivia

  • Sample was the last strikeout victim of Oakland Athletics right-hander Ben Callahan. {Oakland-Alameda County Coliseum -- July 1, 1983}
  • His son, Ian Sample is a Senior on the University of Hawaii football team. He is a receiver and currently having a good year on a top 25 team.
