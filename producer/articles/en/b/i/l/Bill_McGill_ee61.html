Bill McGill

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Bill McGill (born September 16, 1939 in San Angelo, Texas) is a retired American basketball player.

A 6'9" center/forward from the University of Utah, McGill was selected by the Chicago Zephyrs with the first pick of the 1962 NBA Draft. He played three seasons (1962-65) in the NBA and 2 seasons (1968-70) in the ABA. In his career, he scored 3,094 combined ABA/NBA points.
