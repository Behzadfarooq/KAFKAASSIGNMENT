William O'Brien, known as Bill O'Brien, (born 25 January 1929) is a Labour Party politician in the United Kingdom. He was returned as member of Parliament for Normanton at the 1983 general election, and re-elected at four further general elections until he retired at the 2005 election. He was previously a miner and local councillor.
