Bill Evans discography

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

This article contains a listing of the jazz pianist Bill Evans's original LP albums from early in his career. He recorded over 50 albums as a leader between 1956 and 1980 and also played as a sideman on nearly as many again. He broke new ground in many of piano trio, duet and solo recordings several which won or were nominated for Grammy Awards.
