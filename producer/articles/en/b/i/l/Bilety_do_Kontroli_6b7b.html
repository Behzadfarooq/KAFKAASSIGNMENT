[edit] Members

  • Daniel (Wróbel - pl. for sparrow) - bass guitar
  • Lechu - guitar
  • Rafał (Tobi) - drums
  • Łukasz (Kibic - pl. for supporter) - vocals

[edit] History

Bilety do Kontroli was founded in 2000 in Gdynia. Since then the band have played some successful concerts, especially supporting U.K. Subs, The Vibrators or Die Toten Hosen.

In 2002 the band managed to release its first demo album and in 2004 its first EP.

The band itself assumes to play only non-political music without any connection to either lewt or right winged part of the alternative scene.
