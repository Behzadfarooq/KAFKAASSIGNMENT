Bilohirsk raion (Ukrainian: Білогірський район, Russian: Белогорский район, Crimean Tatar: Qarasuvbazar rayonı) is one of the 25 regions of Crimea. This landlocked region is situated in the foothills of the central Crimea. The raion's centre is the historical town of Bilohirsk.
