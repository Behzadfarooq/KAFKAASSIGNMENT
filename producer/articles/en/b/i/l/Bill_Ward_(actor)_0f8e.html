Bill Ward (born 1967, Newcastle upon Tyne) is an English actor who played Charlie Stubbs in Coronation Street. He is a former advertising executive.

[edit] Selected Television Appearances

  • 2003 to 2007 Coronation Street
  • 2003 Holby City
  • 2003 The Bill
  • 2003 EastEnders
  • 2002 Footballers Wives
  • 2001 Jonathan Creek

Bill appeared in Eastenders on a one off role, as a member of the brewry, to award Alfie Moon for best barman. The story of being Cindy Beales hitman is very untrue.This has been stressed by Bill himself.

Bill also had a small cameo role in the Jonathan Creek episode "Satan's Chimney", as a man who chatted up Julia Sawalha's character Carla Borrego after briefly offending her.

He is 1.80m tall.

[edit] Coronation Street

In 2003, Bill joined the cast of Coronation Street portraying local builder Charlie Stubbs. The character has frequently been involved in controversial storylines, including an abusive relationship with former Rovers Return landlady, Shelley Unwin (portrayed by Sally Lindsay).

More recently, Charlie Stubbs has become romantically involved with Tracy Barlow again providing the series with a number of explosive scenes. An example is when Tracy discovers Charlie's affair with hairdresser Maria Sutherland (Samia Smith).

In 2006 Bill Ward announced that he was to leave Coronation Street in an episode to air during 2007. [1]

The plot device required for his exit was provided by the affair with Maria, prompting Tracy to take drastic revenge.

The shirt in which Bill's character Charlie was murdered in, is currently up for auction on eBay.
