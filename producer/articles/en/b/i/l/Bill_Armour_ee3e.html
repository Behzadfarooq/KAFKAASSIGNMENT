William Reginald Armour (September 3, 1869 - December 2, 1922) was an American manager in Major League Baseball who led the Detroit Tigers and Cleveland Indians in the early 1900s. Armour was Ty Cobb's first manager in 1905.

Bill Armour had an overall record of 382-347.
