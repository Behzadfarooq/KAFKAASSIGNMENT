Billy Gets an "A" / Yeti or Not, Here I Come is the 75th episode of The Grim Adventures of Billy and Mandy. It aired on March 2, 2007 on Cartoon Network.

[edit] Plot

[edit] Billy Gets an "A"

Billy get an "F" his big test, so he uses Grim and his scythe to turn his F into an A. However, in doing so, an alternate universe is created, where Billy is smart, Irwin is popular, Pud'n is a bully, and Sperg is a wimp. When Billy is too smart for his own good, Grim and Mandy travel through time in order to restore the natural order of the universe. However, they go through hundreds and hundreds of portals, century after century, all the way into the dinosaur era. Then the episode ends.

[edit] Yeti or Not, Here I Come

Billy, Mandy, and Grim try to capture the Abominable Snowman for a $5,000 reward. However, they find that capturing him is a challenge. First, he takes a ride on his dogs to his home, and cries to his dogs for they all will die. (resulting in the dogs chewing on Grims bones after Abom escapes) Then, he is sitting on a egg, saying he can't leave this egg because he has to warm it. Grim offers Billy to do the work, and Billy goes into a musical number with his butt caring for the egg. So Abom once again escapes while the musical number goes on, and the "egg" turns out to be a bomb and makes an explosion. So they capture him and want to show him off to the village, who speak another language. So Abom kind of translates for Billy. Billy says that he wants to take a picture and the little kids to kneel in the front and that everyone should smile real big. Abom says that Billy wants to gather up the children to work in the coal mines, and that he knocked down the satellite dish. While the village runs after Billy, Abom cuts down the bell tower on Mandy. Abom runs to the glacier, and Grim runs after him, commenting that earlier that day a glacier can move 6 feet in 24 hours, and that is was midnight and the glacier impacts on Abom. The trio returned home to hear about the Loch Ness monster, but they stayed at home.

[edit] End Credits

As a gesture of kindness, Abom decides to give Grim a replacement camcorder, to replace the one that was lost during their little scuffle. As Grim opens the box with the camcorder, he finds not a camcorder, but a bomb (which Grim mistakenly notes is a condor egg). After the bomb explodes, Abom revels in tricking Grim yet again.

[edit] Trivia

  • This is the second time Mandy and Grim try to capture a creature for money. The first time was Bearded Billy.
  • Abom is one of the few characters to both outsmart and seriously injure Mandy. However, in later shots, Mandy doesn't show any visible damage on her in any way, but she showed damage in the explosion.
  • Alternate universe changes when Grim turned Billy's "F" to a "A":
    • Billy becomes smart
    • Billy's parents' genders are switched
    • Sperg is a defenseless nerd
    • Irwin is a chick magnet
    • Pud'n is a bully
    • Ms. Butterbean is nice and seems to actually care somewhat about teaching.
    • Mandy, Grim, and Hoss Delgado are the only ones who do not change in any way.
  • While traveling through time, Mandy complains there should be a time machine. Grim says he can't afford a "hotrod 80's sports car" a reference to Back to the Future.
  • Grim Makes a reference to the song, Dude Looks like a Lady, when he sees Billy's dad in the alternate universe as a woman.
  • Mandy makes a reference to Star Wars When she says "Let's go Chewbacca."

[edit] Goofs
