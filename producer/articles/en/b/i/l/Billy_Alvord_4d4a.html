Billy Alvord

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Billy Alvord (born August 10, 1863 in St. Louis, Missouri; died April 7, 1927 in St. Petersburg, Florida) was an infielder for Major League Baseball in the 19th century.

[edit] Teams

  • St. Louis Maroons 1885
  • Kansas City Cowboys 1889
  • Toledo Maumees 1890
  • Cleveland Spiders 1891 (start)
  • Washington Statesmen 1891 (end)
  • Cleveland Spiders 1893
