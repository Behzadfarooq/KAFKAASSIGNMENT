This article is about the fictional character. For the IRA member (later Communist), see Bill Gannon (Irish Republican).

Officer Bill Gannon was a fictional character played by American actor Harry Morgan (born 1915) on the television series Dragnet 67. His long-time partner was Sergeant Joe Friday, played by actor, television producer, and writer Jack Webb (1920-1982).
