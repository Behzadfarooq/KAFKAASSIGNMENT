[edit] History

The band Bilocate was first formed in 2002. In 2005 they released their first album "Dysphoria", containing 8 songs. This also included "A Desire to Leave", a track that lasted more than 20 minutes. Dysphoria has been listed as the best local metal seller in 2006.

Bilocate are the only Jordanian metal band has been featured on Jordan's official governmental radio in a show lasted for 5 hours. The show included a full run of the album and an interview with the band's members as well.

After performing in Egypt at EgyptMetal, making them the first band to do so, Bilocate's fanbase extended from Jordan to Egypt, Saudi Arabia, Lebanon, Syria, United Arab Emirates, Libya, united kingdom, Sweden, Norway and even United states of America. In 2006 Bilocate headlined Egypt Metal gig, which took place in Cairo, performing alongside other metal bands such as Demon's Shrine and the Libyan black metal band Bath In Sin. The band performed originals such as The Tragedy Within and Days of Joys in addition to covers of My Dying Bride "She is The Dark", Katatonia "12" and Opeth "The Grand Conjuration". Bilocate had the highest rate at a voting following the show.

Bilocate is currently recording their second album.
