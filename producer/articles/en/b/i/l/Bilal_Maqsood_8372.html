Bilal Maqsood (Urdu: بلال مقصود) is the son of popular writer, actor, artist and comedian Anwar Maqsood, and is a member of the very popular Pakistani pop band Strings. He has also directed a number of music videos.

  • Maula (the last video shot by the Vital Signs)
  • Us Rah Par (featuring Junaid Jamshed)
  • Beirut (Stings) [1]
