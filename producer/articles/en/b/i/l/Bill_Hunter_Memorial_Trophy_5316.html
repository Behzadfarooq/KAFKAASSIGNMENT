Bill Hunter Memorial Trophy

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Bill Hunter Memorial Trophy is awarded annually to the Defenseman of the Year in the Western Hockey League.

It is named after one of the league's founders, Bill Hunter, who was the driving force behind the creation of the WHL. Hunter served as the owner, general manager and head coach of the Edmonton Oil Kings, and was the first Chairman of the Board of the new league.
