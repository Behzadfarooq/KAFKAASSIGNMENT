Billy Lee Riley

Billy Lee Riley was born on October 5, 1933 in Pocahontas, Arkansas, USA. He is a legendary Rockabilly-musician, who worked at Sun Records and who was rediscovered by Bob Dylan in 1992. Dylan has been a fan of Billy Lee Riley since 1956. Since 1992 Billy Lee Riley is playing Blues and Country-Blues. His album Hot Damn! (Capricorn, 1997) was nominated for the Grammy.
