William "Billy" Turley (born 15 July 1973) is an English football goalkeeper currently playing for National Conference side Oxford United.

[edit] Clubs

  • 1994-1995 : Evesham United
  • 1995-1999 : Northampton Town
    • 1998 : Leyton Orient (loan)
  • 1999-2005 : Rushden & Diamonds
  • 2005-present : Oxford United
