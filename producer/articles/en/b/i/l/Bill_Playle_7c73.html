William Rodger Playle (born 1 December 1938, Palmerston North, Manawatu, New Zealand) is a former New Zealand cricketer who has played eight Tests for New Zealand, making 151 runs in eight Tests as a specialist batsman. All his Tests were against England, and he only passed 25 once - scoring 65 in a lost cause at Wellington in 1962â€“63. His first class career started with Auckland, but he later moved to Australia, where he made his highest first class score with 122 for Western Australia.
