Bill Coffin

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Bill Coffin is a writer of novels and role-playing games. He is probably best know for his work at Palladium Books at the end of the 1990s on a number of Palladium's lines including Palladium Fantasy, Heroes Unlimited, Systems Failure (his own game) and some work on the Rifts line.

Many people consider Bill to have been one of the best writers in Palladium's history. However, due to a conflict with Kevin Siembieda he left the company around 2001, much to the disappointment of his fans. He is currently working on a new campaign setting for the D6 Space core book for WEG[1]
