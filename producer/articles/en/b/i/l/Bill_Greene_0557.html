Bill Greene

Bill Greene is the founder and President of RightMarch.com, is President of Strategic Internet Campaign Management Inc. (SICM), and is a Republican candidate for U.S. Congress in Georgia's 10th congressional district's special election to replace the late congressman Charlie Norwood.

Greene was also the host of the talk radio show "RightMarch.com Patriotism in Action" which was aired for six months on WMCA AM 570 in New York City and for one year on the CMRI Shortwave and Satelite Radio network. [1]

Greene was raised in the mountains near Hendersonville, North Carolina. He began his career teaching Political Science, American Government and Constitutional Law at Florida International University in Miami. He now resides in Braselton, Georgia with his wife of seventeen years and their two children.
