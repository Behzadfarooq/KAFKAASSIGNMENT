Billi Bruno (born Sarah Bruno in Los Angeles, California, July 20, 1997) is an American child actress who plays Jim Belushi and Courtney Thorne-Smith's daughter, Gracie, on the ABC sitcom According to Jim.

[edit] Beginning her Career

At age three, when her two sisters auditioned for acting roles, she would tag along. She'd memorize their lines (sometimes even before they did), then plead with the casting agent: "Can I say my lines now?" Her parents finally gave in to her precocious proclivities and the rest, well, will one day be history, then she got her role for Gracie on According to Jim.

[edit] Trivia

  • Is the youngest of three sisters.
  • She enjoys swimming, riding her scooter, planting flowers, drawing, coloring, taking gymnastics, diving, riding her horse, and using her imagination.
  • Billi appeared with According To Jim cast member Taylor Atelian in country music star Brad Paisley's music video, "Celebrity."
  • Currently lives in Los Angeles with her parents, and two sisters.
  • When asked what she wants to be when she grows up, her response is "I'm going to be on According to Jim forever!"
