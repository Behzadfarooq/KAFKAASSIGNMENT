Bill Atkins (born November 19, 1934) was a defensive back and punter from Auburn University who played for the San Francisco 49ers in the NFL, and in the American Football League for the Buffalo Bills, New York Titans and Jets, and the Denver Broncos. He was an AFL All-Star in 1961.
