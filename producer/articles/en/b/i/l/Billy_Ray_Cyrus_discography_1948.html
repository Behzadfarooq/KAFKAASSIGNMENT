Billy Ray Cyrus, an American country singer, has released the following albums and greatest hits compilations. Cyrus has also achieved three #1 singles and six Top Ten singles.

Some Gave All

  • Label: Mercury Records
  • Chart positions: #1 (Billboard 200, 1992); #1 (Top Country, 1992)

  • U.S. sales: Over 9 million
  • Official Singles:

    • "Could've Been Me"
    • "She's Not Cryin'
    • "Some Gave All"
    • "Wher'm I Gonna Live?"

  • Official Singles:
