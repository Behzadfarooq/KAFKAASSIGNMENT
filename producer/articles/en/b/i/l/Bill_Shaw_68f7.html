Bill Shaw, former high-paid Time Warner executive. Shaw was at one time the President of TBS Sports.

Bill Shaw, Dr. Professor of Physical Oceanography at Moss Landing Marine Laboratories. Known for his tough yet tactical approach towards teaching, Dr. Shaw inspired many students to switch to Physical Oceanography.
