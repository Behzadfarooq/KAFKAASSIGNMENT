Bill Schultz (born May 1, 1967 in Granada Hills, CA) is a retired National Football League offensive lineman.

[edit] Professional Career

Schultz played for the Indianapolis Colts between 1990 and 1993, for the Denver Broncos in 1995 and for the Chicago Bears in 1997.

[edit] College

Schultz was a fourth-round draft choice from the University of Southern California.

[edit] High School Career

Prior to USC, Schultz prepped at Kennedy High School in Granada Hills.
