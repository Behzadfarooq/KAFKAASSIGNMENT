Bill Caddick (born William Caddick, 27 June 1944, in Hurst Hill, Wolverhampton, The Black Country) is an English folk singer-songwriter and guitarist.

Former member of Home Service. Also performed with the Albion Band in 'Larkrise' at the National Theatre.

Caddick's songs, such as 'Unicorns' and 'She moves among men', have been recorded by numerous other artists on the folk scene, including June Tabor and Christy Moore.
