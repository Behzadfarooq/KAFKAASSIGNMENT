I corrected the infobox but the text is too tangled for now. From Byrne's book:

Ireland v Italy 1926-03-21 Turin: team did not include Lacey (Cannon-Brady-McCarthy-Foley-Doyle-Connolly-Flood-Grace-Watters-Fullam-Fagan)

Lacey's 3 caps were

  1. Dublin 1927-04-23 Italy 'B'
  2. Liege 1928-02-12 Belgium 1 goal
  3. Brussels 1930-05-11 Belgium

note he did not play v BEL in Dublin on 1929-04-20.

The ages need to be amended accordingly, based on the given DOB.

All books contain errors, including Byrne's and Ryan's. I've read Ryan but don't have a copy to hand, but it's all prose, whereas Byrne's lists the full lineout for every international from 1926 to 1996, so is less likely to confuse the first international with the first home international. As regards Lacey's age at the time of each match, I'm working with the DOB 1889-09-24 given in the article (I don't know the source for that) and the dates of the relevant internationals from Byrne. jnestorius(talk) 23:23, 6 November 2006 (UTC)
