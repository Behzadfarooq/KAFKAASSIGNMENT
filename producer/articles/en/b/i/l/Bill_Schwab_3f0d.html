Bill Schwab

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Bill Schwab (born 1959) is an American photographer known for his emotionally charged yet peaceful urban and natural landscapes.

Born in Detroit, Michigan he received a Bachelor of Fine Arts degree from Central Michigan University in 1983.

Having been shown in many group and solo exhibitions in the US and abroad since the early 1980s, Schwab’s work continues to become more widely known and sought after. It is represented in a growing number of private, corporate and museum collections around the world including the The Art Institute of Ohio — Cincinnati, Detroit Institute of Arts and the George Eastman House.
