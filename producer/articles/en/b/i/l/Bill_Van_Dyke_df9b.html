Bill Van Dyke (born December 15, 1863 in Paris, Illinois; died May 5, 1933 in El Paso, Texas) was an outfielder in Major League Baseball in the 19th century.

[edit] Teams

  • Toledo Maumees 1890
  • St. Louis Browns 1892
  • Boston Beaneaters 1893
