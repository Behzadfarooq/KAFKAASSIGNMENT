Bill Crow, is a jazz bassist and author.

Crow has played with bands led by Mike Riley, John Benson Brooks, Stan Getz, [[[Al Haig]], [Claude Thornhill]], Terry Gibbs, Jerry Wald, Marian McPartland, Jimmy McPartland, Gerry Mulligan, Al Cohn & Zoot Sims, Bob Brookmeyer & Clark Terry, Quincy Jones, Benny Goodman, Eddie Condon, Walter Norris, Peter Duchin, Marty Napoleon, Chris Griffin, Gene DiNovi, Doug Proper, Joe Beck, and Carmen Leggio.

Crow has played Broadway shows, including Boccaccio, The King and I, The Grand Tour, Morrisey Hall, On the Twentieth Century, and 42nd Street.
