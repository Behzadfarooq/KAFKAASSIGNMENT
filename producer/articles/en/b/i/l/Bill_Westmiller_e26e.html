William Joseph Ronald Westmiller is a North American politician. He currently resides in Thousand Oaks, California.

Westmiller was co-founder of the Libertarian Party of Canada, and he has served as National Chairman of the Republican Liberty Caucus, former National Secretary and California Chairman of the U.S. Libertarian Party, and candidate for the Republican Party nomination in California's 24th Congressional District. He is a frequent contributor to on-line journals [1] and often provides commentary on political and judicial issues [2].
