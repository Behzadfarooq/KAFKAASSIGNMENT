Greetings my fellow Wikipedians and welcome to my page. My name is Vasileios Peidis (Βασíλειος Πεïδης) and I am proudly declaring my Greek origin in my nickname, derived from God knows where (perhaps Zorba the Greek, but I'm still not sure – I was very young when I started using it). I am a high school student, but my torture will soon be over, as this will be my last school year. I was born on New Year's Day, 1989, but, contrary to what one would expect, I am not a lucky person at all. I live in Katerini, the capital of Pieria prefecture in northern Greece. It is a rather moderate-sized town of 60,000 but it has all the advantages of living in a city while lacking the disadvantages of the really big cities. This, as you can imagine, has led to my being raised in a way as to become the wonderful person I am. Finally, I am a very fluent speaker of English with two certificates of proficiency, and I am sure that this will help me to contribute more and better "to this most noble and grand project of Wikipaedia".

I came across Wikipedia last year and ever since I have been in the site for almost the whole amount of time I am online (that is, summer 2004 and summer 2005), gathering information. More serious contribution, however, I only started last June. My basic interests are architecture, history, geography and heraldry, as well as all things Harry Potter, but I usually contribute to a wide variety of articles accessed by chance, totally irrelevant from each other. In that cases, I usually conduct typo and grammar corrections and wikification. Feel free, however, to suggest anything that could be of interest to me to my talk page, either for edit/correction or reading. As my time online is limited and I mostly gather information for the time being, my edits are only around 200, although I hope that from now on I will be able to work on Wikipedia the whole year round. More recently I have started getting more interested in categorizing, and I will try to contribute more towards that direction.

I used to be a member of the AMA (Association of Members' Advocates), but I didn't actually contribute a lot to the cause, mostly due to a lack of time. Maybe I will return at a later date but for the moment I relieve myself of the burden of an Advocate's duties and shall accept no further cases.

Anyway, that's a little information about myself. I wish you a good staying in Wikipedia.
