Billy Herrington (born 14 July 1969, Long Island, New York) is a heterosexual, or "gay-for-pay," American pornographic actor who appeared in gay pornography.

Among his starring roles is the Danish film HotMen CoolBoyz (2000), produced by Lars von Trier's company Zentropa, also known for such projects as Dancer in the Dark (2000) starring Björk, Dogville (2003) starring Nicole Kidman, and the Academy Award-nominated After the Wedding (2006).

Herrington's scene in Conquered with Nino Bacci, Colton Ford, Blake Harper, and Jay Ross won the "Best Group Sex Scene" at the 2001 Adult Erotic Gay Video Awards (“Grabbys”).
