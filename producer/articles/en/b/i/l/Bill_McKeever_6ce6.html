Bill McKeever is an author on several books about Mormonism. He serves with Mormonism Research Ministry (MRM) in El Cajon, California. Bill founded MRM in 1979 with the hope of informing people about the differences between Mormonism and Christianity.

Bill's views on Mormonism has led to invitations to speak at several Universities in the United States, Russia, and the Philippines. Bill lives in the Salt Lake City area with his wife and three children.

[edit] Books by Bill McKeever

  • (1991) Answering Mormons' Questions. Bethany House Publishers. ISBN 1-55661-201-X. 
  • (1993) Dictionary of the Mormon language from Aaronic priesthood to Zion. Mormonism Research Ministry. ASIN B0006PEQEA.  (Out of print)
  • (1994) Questions to Ask Your Mormon Friend: Effective Ways to Challenge a Mormon's Arguments Without Being Offensive. Bethany House Publishers. ISBN 1-55661-455-1. 
  • (2000) Mormonism 101: Examining the Religion of the Latter-day Saints. Baker Books. ISBN 0-8010-6335-3.
