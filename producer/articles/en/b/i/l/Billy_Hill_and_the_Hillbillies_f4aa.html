Billy Hill and the Hillbillies

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Billy Hill and the Hillbillies are a musical/variety group at Disneyland in Anaheim, California. The group performs a bluegrass country-music-centered show along with classic rock and rap (performed in a country and bluegrass style). They have released some of their music on CDs.

The group had four original members, all using the stage name of Billy Hill, who performed from August 5, 1992 until November of 1994. The original members are:

  • Kirk Wall (master of ceremonies and sometime-Elvis impersonator), guitar and fiddle
  • Mario Hildago, banjo
  • Dennis Fetchet, fiddle
  • John Marshall, bass, founder of the group


Mario was later replaced before the opening inside the Golden Horseshoe on December 18, 1994 by:

  • Evan Marshall, mandolin


Evan Marshall left the band in 2003.


Today, Billy Hill and the Hillbillies perform 7 days a week inside the Golden Horseshoe Saloon as three different teams . Billy Hill members now include:

  • Kirk Wall, guitar and fiddle, five days a week
  • Dennis Fetchet, fiddle and mandolin 5 days a week
  • John Marshall, bass, 5 days a week
  • Duane Michaels, Mandolin and fiddle, five days a week
  • John Eaden, lead and guitar, two days a week
  • Rick Storey, banjo and guitar, two days a week
  • Anders Swanson, bass and fiddle, two days a week
  • John David, guitar and banjo, two days a week

[edit] Locations

  • Critter Country from 1986 until 1990: The original home of Billy and the Hillbillies.
  • Disneyland's Golden Horseshoe Saloonfrom 1992 to the present: The current home of the act.
  • Disneyland's Big Thunder Ranch: 1999-2000 while the Golden Horseshoe was occupied by the Woody's Roundup show based on Disney and Pixar's Toy Story 2.
