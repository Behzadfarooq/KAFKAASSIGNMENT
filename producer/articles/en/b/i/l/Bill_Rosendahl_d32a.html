Bill Rosendahl is a Los Angeles City Councilman, representing Council District 11, including the communities of Brentwood, Del Rey, Mar Vista, Marina del Rey, Pacific Palisades, Palms, Playa del Rey, Playa Vista, Venice, West Los Angeles and Westchester. Prior to being elected in May 2005, Rosendahl was an educator and television broadcaster, and a vice president at Adelphia.

Rosendahl is the first openly gay person elected to the Los Angeles City Council.
