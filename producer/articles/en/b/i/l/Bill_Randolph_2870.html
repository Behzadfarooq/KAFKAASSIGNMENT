Bill Randolph

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Bill Randolph (b. October 11, 1953, Detroit, Michigan) is an American actor who has starred in films and appeared on television.

His first feature film was in the horror movie Dressed to Kill (1980), but he is best known for his role in the 1981 horror movie Friday the 13th Part 2 as "Jeffrey". He also starred in the 1989 movie Penn & Teller Get Killed, his most recent movie was in the 1991 film Guilty as Charged.

He made guest appearances on Tv shows such as As the World Turns and Hill Street Blues. Bill starred in the short lived TV series Comedy Zone in 1984, and in the series Trauma Center.
