Greetings from Los Alamos, New Mexico. I'm a former nuclear physicist turned worthless line manager at Los Alamos National Laboratory, but all of my articles are submitted as a private citizen, not a lab employee. 54 years old, married with two sons. Recreational passions are hiking and backpacking (which has much to do with the things I contribute here), classical music (particularly choral music), and competitive just-about-everything. If you happen to frequent the Internet Chess Club, drop me a message there -- my handle is MeisterZinger and I run the "TriviaBot" trivia contests, for which I get lots of inspiration from Wikipedia articles!

While I contribute only occasionally here, I'm also a regular on Wikitravel and recently became an admin there. There are a few other Wikis that I hang out on as well -- this Wiki thing can be addictive!

Note that I'm unable to contribute to matters in the purview of Los Alamos National Laboratory and cannot and do not speak for the laboratory regarding the content and accuracy of Wikipedia articles.
