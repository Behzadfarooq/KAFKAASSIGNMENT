[edit] Using edit summaries

When editing an article on Wikipedia there is a small field labeled "Edit summary" under the main edit-box. It looks like this:

The text written here will appear on the Recent changes page, in the page revision history, on the diff page, and in the watchlists of users who are watching that article. See m:Help:Edit summary for full information on this feature.

Filling in the edit summary field greatly helps your fellow contributors in understanding what you changed, so please always fill in the edit summary field, especially for big edits or when you are making subtle but important changes, like changing dates or numbers. Thank you.

-Whomp 23:26, 13 May 2006 (UTC)

[edit] My Network TV and Desperate Housewives

For the second time, I've stripped the My Network TV article of references to the deal to air syndicated reruns of Desperate Housewives on local stations that just happen to be MNTV "owned and operated". The fact that you reinstated the info gives off the impression that you're unable to distinguish between programs actually provided by a broadcast television network and syndicated television programs that simply air on all or most of that network's "O&Os". Basically, saying that DH reruns will soon "be on MNTV" is like saying that Live with Regis & Kelly "is on ABC" when the truth is that Regis & Kelly is merely on many (if not all) ABC O&Os.--TwoTone 22:39, 31 May 2006 (UTC)
