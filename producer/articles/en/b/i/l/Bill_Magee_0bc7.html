William J. Magee was born in 1864 in New Brunswick, Canada.

A right handed pitcher for the 1898 Louisville Colonels, Magee had a record of 16-15. He then continued a mediocre career for five National League teams in four more seasons. He walked more than twice as many batters as he struck out.
