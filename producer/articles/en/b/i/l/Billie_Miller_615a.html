Dame Billie Antoinette Miller (born 1944) is a Barbadian politician.

Miller is currently a member of the Barbados Labour Party, she has been within the government of Prime Minister Owen Arthur since 1994. Billie Miller has served as the foreign minister of Barbados since 1994 and has also been deputy prime minister. She gave up the position of deputy prime minister during a cabinet reshuffle after the 2003 elections, in which the Barbados Labour Party maintained power. Currently Miller is a Member of Parliament for the constituency of the City of Bridgetown. It is widely suspected that once Owen Arthur retires from politics, that he will appoint either Ms. Billie Miller or Ms. Mia Mottley as the new leader for the Barbados Labour Party[1].
