William Donald Enyart (born April 28, 1947 in Pawhuska, Oklahoma) is a former American football player who played for the Buffalo Bills from 1969–1970 and for the Oakland Raiders in 1971. He played college football at Oregon State University, earning All-American honors in 1968.[1] He played in 14 games in one season in the AFL and 15 games over two seasons in the NFL. Bill "Earthquake" Enyart was inducted into the Oregon Sports Hall of Fame & Museum in 2003.
