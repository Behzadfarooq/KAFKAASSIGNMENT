Bill Saluga is an American comedian.

[edit] Raymond J. Johnson

Main article: Ray J. Johnson

Saluga is best known for his cigarette-smoking, zoot-suit-wearing television character “Raymond J. Johnson, Jr.” (also known as Ray J. Johnson).

[edit] Other work

Bill Saluga was a founding member, along with Fred Willard, Michael Mislove, George Memmoli, and Patti Deutsch of the improvisational comedy troupe Ace Trucking Company. He has appeared on several television programs, including Seinfeld, and is the co-author of Bill Saluga's Name Game Book (ISBN 0-553-20754-7).
