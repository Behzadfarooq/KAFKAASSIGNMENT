Billing Aquadrome

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Billing Aquadrome is a leisure park on the outskirts of Northampton, England. Facilities within the 235 acre (1 kmÂ²) park, which is based around various old gravel pits, include a caravan site, marina and funfair. It is also home to a variety of rallies throughout the year, which are often car or motorsport related.

At the edge of the park is the popular Billing Mill, family pub, which is housed in a converted water-mill. At one time it was reported to be one of the highest grossing pubs in the UK.
