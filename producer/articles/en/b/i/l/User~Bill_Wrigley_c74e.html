[edit] About me

I paint and sculpt, do murals, and teach about them, take photos, write for Wiki and co-manage St. Charles - Class of 1970, my elemetary school from Pierrefonds (Montreal).

For pleasure I own and restore two classic cars, cycle regularly, partake in drive-ins and regional short-track stock car racing, prepare and imbibe green cuisine, sip Islay single malt scotch, and espresso, and love anything chocolate.

I've been a Wikipedian since April 5, 2006 and currently reside in Toronto. You can contact me on my discussion page or e-mail.
