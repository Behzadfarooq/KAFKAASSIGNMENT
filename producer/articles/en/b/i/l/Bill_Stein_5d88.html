Bill Stein

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

William Allen Stein (January 21, 1947 in Battle Creek, Michigan), is a former professional baseball player who played in the Major Leagues primarily as an infielder from 1972-1985.

[edit] Teams

  • St. Louis Cardinals 1972-1973
  • Chicago White Sox 1974-1976
  • Seattle Mariners 1977-1980
  • Texas Rangers 1981-1985

[edit] Batting Stats

  • 959 Games
  • 751 Hits
  • 44 Home Runs
  • 311 RBI's
  • .267 Batting Average
