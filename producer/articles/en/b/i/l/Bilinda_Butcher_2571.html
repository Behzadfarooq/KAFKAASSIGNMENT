Bilinda Jayne Butcher (born September 16, 1961 in London) was a vocalist and guitarist with My Bloody Valentine. While Kevin Shields was MBV's principal guitarist, vocalist, producer, and songwriter, Butcher's vocals were often credited with adding greatly to the otherworldly and ethereal sound apparent in the band's work. Since the unexplained silence from the My Bloody Valentine camp, Butcher has performed collaborations with Collapsed Lung and Dinosaur Jr.
