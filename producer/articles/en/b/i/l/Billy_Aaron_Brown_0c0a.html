Billy Aaron Brown

Billy Aaron Brown as Kyle in 8 Simple Rules
Born: July 28, 1981 (age 25)
Clarinda, Iowa
Occupation: Television actor.

Billy Aaron Brown is an American actor most notable for his role as Kyle in ABC sitcom 8 Simple Rules. In 2001, Brown played "Scott" in the Mary-Kate and Ashley Olsen film, "Holiday in the Sun". He also starred in the 2003 horror movie Jeepers Creepers 2 as Andy "Bucky" Buck. He also starred in the DCOM Going to the Mat.

[edit] Biography

Billy Aaron Brown was born on July 28, 1981 in Clarinda, Iowa in the USA.

[edit] Trivia

  • He appeared on the American TV quiz show Street Smarts and won $3800.
