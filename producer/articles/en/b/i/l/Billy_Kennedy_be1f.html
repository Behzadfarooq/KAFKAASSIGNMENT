William Norton Kennedy, usually known as Billy or, from his 18th birthday onwards, Bill, was a fictional character in the Australian soap opera Neighbours, played by Jesse Spencer. He first appeared in 1994 until 2000. In 2005, Bill made a brief cameo in Annalise Hartman's documentary on Ramsay Street. His current girlfriend, Anne Wilkinson, just gave birth to their first child.
