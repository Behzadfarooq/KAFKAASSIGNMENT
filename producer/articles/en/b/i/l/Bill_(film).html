Bill is a 2007 comedy film written and directed by Bernie Goldmann and Melisa Wallick. The film was shot in St. Louis, Missouri from June 11 - July 20, 2006; the school scenes of the film were shot at Mary Institute and St. Louis Country Day School (MICDS) and additional scenes were filmed at Washington University in St. Louis. Lindsay Lohan was originally attached to the project to the play Lucy, however she left due to scheduling conflicts. Jessica Alba replaced Lohan in the film.

Bill (Aaron Eckhart) is fed up with his life, wife, and job. He finds himself the reluctant mentor of an unruly teenager. His life hits an all time low when he catches his wife having an affair with a local news anchor, but with the kid as his inspiration, he regains his lost sense of self.
