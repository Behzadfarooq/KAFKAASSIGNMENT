Bilar, Bohol

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Bilar is a 5th class municipality in the province of Bohol, Philippines. According to the 2000 census, it has a population of 16,628 people in 2,967 households.

[edit] Barangays

Bilar is politically subdivided into 19 barangays.
