Billy Rose's Jumbo
Soundtrack by Various artists
Released November 12, 1962
Label Columbia Masterworks

Billy Rose's Jumbo was the title of an album containing the music from the soundtrack of the movie of the same name. The album featured the film's cast: Doris Day, Stephen Boyd, Jimmy Durante, and Martha Raye.

It was released by Columbia Records under the Columbia Masterworks label on November 12, 1962. The catalog number was OL-5860 for the monaural LP version and OS-2260 for the stereophonic version.

  2. "Over and Over Again"—Doris Day
  3. "Why Can't I?"—Doris Day & Martha Raye
  4. "This Can't Be Love" (Richard Rodgers/Lorenz Hart)—Doris Day
  5. "The Most Beautiful Girl in the World" (Rodgers/Hart)—Stephen Boyd
  6. "My Romance" (Rodgers/Hart)—Doris Day
  7. "The Most Beautiful Girl in the World" - Reprise—Jimmy Durante
  8. "Little Girl Blue" (Rodgers/Hart)—Doris Day
  9. "Sawdust, Spangles and Dreams" - Finale—Entire Cast
