Bill Hume (? - 10 December 2005) was a football (soccer) player, born in the United Kingdom who went on to represent New Zealand and Australia.

Hume had played for New Zealand against Australia in 1958.

He moved to Australia in 1959, playing in Sydney with Gladesville-Ryde (and later, Hakoah). That year he made his two appearances for Australia (both against Scottish club Heart of Midlothian) in Sydney and Melbourne, scoring in the Melbourne match.

He returned to New Zealand in the early 1960s playing out his career with club side Hamilton Wanderers.
