Bill "Fonzie" Alfonso (born William Sierra on August 11, 1957 in Tampa, Florida) is a former referee & manager who has worked for the World Wrestling Federation (WWF), World Championship Wrestling (WCW), Jim Crockett Promotions, and Extreme Championship Wrestling (ECW). He has been the manager of Taz, Sabu, and Rob Van Dam. During his times refereeing in ECW his gimmick was that of a referee sent from the Pennsylvania State Athletic Commission. He would stop hardcore matches, among other things.

[edit] Accomplishments

  • Alfonso won the 1997 Pro Wrestling Illustrated Manager of the Year Award.
