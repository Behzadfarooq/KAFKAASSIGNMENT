Billy McAdams (born 1931 in Belfast) was a Northern Irish footballer. He played in the inside forward position. He played for Manchester City between 1953 and 1959 making 127 appearances and scoring 61 goals.

He then signed for Bolton Wanderers who hoped he could be succesor to Nat Lofthouse. In his first season with the team he scored 18 goals in 27 appearances. He was also a prolific scorer in the lower divisions of English football. He also won 15 caps for Northern Ireland national football team scoring 7 goals in the process.
