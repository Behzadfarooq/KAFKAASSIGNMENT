Bill Rogers is an American voice actor who works on the Properties of TAJ Productions.

[edit] Notable Voice Roles

  • Brock in The Mastermind of Mirage Pokémon and Pokémon from Battle Frontier
  • May's Combusken, Brock's Bonsly, and Scott in the Battle Frontier season of Pokémon; also Bonsly in King and Queen for a Day
  • Tohma Seguchi/Additional Voices in Gravitation
  • Additional Voices in The Irresponsible Captain Tylor
  • Officer Yamamoto in Boogiepop Phantom
