Bilisht

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

You have new messages (last change).

Bilisht is a city of about 12,000 people in south-east Korçë, Albania. The football (soccer) club is Bilisht Sporti.The city is 9 km from the Greek border at Kapshtica point.
