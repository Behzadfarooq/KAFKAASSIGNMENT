I live in the small village of Ashurst Wood, West Sussex in the UK. I'm a big fan of animated TV shows and so most edits you'll find from me on Wikipedia will be on those. I also have a large collection of Amiga games and will contribute to articles on that subject also. I support Tottenham Hotspur FC and am a huge fan of cricket. I have a wonderful girlfriend, Jenn who is American and lives in The States and I've spent several months over there in the past 2 years, as she has here. My job is a software developer as well as one of my hobbies. Notable projects I've produced include a comprehensive database frontend, and a Sonic the hedgehog clone.

[edit] Cricket computer games

One day I looked up Brian Lara International Cricket 2005 to find an article in a terrible state. I fixed it up a bit and then, being a fan of the previous games in the series, I decided to have a look at them. Games were missing, had the wrong names and none of them were linked to each other so I spent a couple of days organising the series into a neat and tidy bunch of articles.
