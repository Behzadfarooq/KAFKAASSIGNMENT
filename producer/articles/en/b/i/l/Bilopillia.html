Bilopillya (Ukrainian: Білопілля; Russian: Белополье, translit.: Belopol`ye) is a city, the administrative center of the Bilopilskyi Raion, in Sumy Oblast of northeastern Ukraine. It is located at around 51°8′54″N, 34°18′3″E, close to Kursk Oblast of Russia. Population is 18,384 (2001).
