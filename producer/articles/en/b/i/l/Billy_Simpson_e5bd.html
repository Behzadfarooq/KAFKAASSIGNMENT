Billy Simpson is a former Northern Irish footballer.

He signed for Rangers from Linfield for a sum of Â£11,500 in 1950. He spent nine years (1950-59) at Rangers making 239 appearances and scoring 163 goals. He represented Northern Ireland in the 1958 FIFA World Cup in Sweden. He won three championship medals and a Scottish Cup winners medal with Rangers and twelve international caps with Northern Ireland. He is a member of the Rangers F.C. Hall of Fame.
