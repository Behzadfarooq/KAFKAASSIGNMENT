William Martin Boucher (November 10, 1899 â€“ November 10, 1958) was a Canadian professional ice hockey right winger.

Born in Ottawa, Ontario, Canada, he played in the National Hockey League for the Montreal Canadiens, Boston Bruins and New York Americans. He also played for the New Haven Eagles and Bronx Tigers of the Canadian-American Hockey League.

He died of a heart attack November 10, 1958 on his 59th birthday.
