William H. Dugan (born June 5, 1959 in Hornell, New York) is a former professional American football player and collegiate All-American offensive lineman.

He earned a Bachelor of Science in Administration of Justice from Penn State University in 1986.

[edit] Personal

Dugan lives in Hornell, New York, where he is in plumbing/heating repair.
