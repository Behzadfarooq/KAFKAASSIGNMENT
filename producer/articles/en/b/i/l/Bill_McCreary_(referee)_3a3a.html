Bill McCreary (referee)

William "Bill" McCreary (born November 17, 1955, in Guelph, Ontario) is a referee in the National Hockey League. McCreary first refereed an NHL game in 1984, and since has since refereed in over 1300 NHL games. McCreary wears sweater #7.

McCreary was once called the "best referee in hockey right now" by Don Cherry.[citation needed]
