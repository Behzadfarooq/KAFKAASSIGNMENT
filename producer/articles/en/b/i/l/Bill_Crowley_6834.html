Bill Crowley is an American radio announcer. Crowley has been a news writer and announcer for radio stations in New York City, including WINS, WFAN and WCBS-AM. He is currently the afternoon and evening news announcer for Air America Radio. In addition to his hourly news updates, he could be heard at the beginning of the third hour of The Majority Report radio show discussing the events of the day, when that show aired. He also occasionally provides voice parts for comedy bits on The Randi Rhodes Show.
