Bill Beveridge

William "Bill" Beveridge (Born in Ottawa, Ontario July 1, 1909, died 13 February 1995) was a Professional Hockey Goaltender who played 9 seasons in the National Hockey League for the Detroit Cougars, Ottawa Senators, St. Louis Eagles, Montreal Maroons and New York Rangers.
