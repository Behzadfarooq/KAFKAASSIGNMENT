Biledegradable is an EP released by the industrial band Bile on March 18, 1997, on the now defunct record label, Energy Records. This EP is out of print and is highly sought after by fans of the band. Tracks one and five, "My Generation," is a cover of The Who's track of the same title. Tracks two and three were new material while track four was an early demo of the band's. "The Phantom God" is a remix of the track "Lowest Form" and "Planet Weather Control" is a remix of the track "Weather Control," both whose original versions can be found on the band's second album Teknowhore.
