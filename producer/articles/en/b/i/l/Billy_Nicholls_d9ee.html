Billy Nicholls (born February 15, 1949, White City district of Shepherd's Bush, London) is an English Singer, songwriter, Producer and musical director, whom first gained fame in the 1960's while still a teenager with his Pet Sounds influenced album, Would You Believe, originally released on Immediate Records.

Billy's recent work includes providing backing vocals on tracks on Endless Wire, the most recent album by rock legends The Who.
