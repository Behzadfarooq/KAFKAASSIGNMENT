Billboard (disambiguation)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Billboard can refer to:

  • Billboard (advertising), a large outdoor sign usually used for advertising
  • Billboard (magazine), a magazine devoted to the music industry
    • The Billboard charts, inspired by the magazine
    • The Billboard Music Award, sponsored by the magazine
  • Billboard antenna, an array of parallel antennas with flat reflectors
  • A method of rendering sprites in 3D computer graphics
  • A large Movie poster called a 'Twenty four sheet'
  • The Billboard, a level in Heroes The Tantalizing Trio PC game
