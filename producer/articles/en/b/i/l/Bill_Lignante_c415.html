Bill Lignante (March 20, 1925 in Brooklyn, New York) is an American illustrator.

Although he was initially known for his work on The Phantom for Gold Key Comics and Charlton Comics, and for his sixteen-year career as an animator for Hanna-Barbera, he later became better known in his role as a courtroom artist for ABC Network News. In the 26 years he spent with ABC, he illustrated 55 trials, including those of Sirhan Sirhan, Charlie Manson, and Patty Hearst.
