[edit] list of anti-war organizations

Hi Bill,

I reverted your addition of the Quakers to the List of anti-war organizations. As the list description implies, the list is for activist or political organizations with a primary focus on opposing war: "In order to facilitate organized opposition to war, anti-war activists have often founded anti-war organizations. These groups range from temporary coalitions to more permanent structured organizations." The Quakers don't quite fit. Some Quaker-led groups, the American Friends Service Committee and Friends Committee on National Legislation, do, and are already listed. Thanks for contributing, though, and I hope you stick around to help with the anti-war WikiProject. Kalkin 23:34, 11 April 2006 (UTC)
