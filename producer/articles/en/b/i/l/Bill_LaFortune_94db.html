Bill LaFortune, as of 2005, served as the 34th mayor of Tulsa, Oklahoma, United States.

Mr. LaFortune formerly served as District Attorney for Tulsa County, Special Judge for Tulsa County, and as Assistant Attorney General for the state of Oklahoma. He was an unsuccessful candidate for re-election on April 5, 2006 when he was defeated by former Oklahoma Secretary of Commerce and Tourism, Kathy Taylor. He served from April 1, 2002 to April 10, 2006.
