Bill Henderson (born William Randall Henderson on August 9, 1926, in Chicago, Illinois) is an American television and film actor.

Henderson's acting credits have been numerous. Beginning in the mid-1970s, and continuing to this day, he has frequently appeared on television in supporting, usually one-time roles. His film roles have followed a similar trend — minor and supporting, but often essential, roles.

Bill Henderson is also a jazz singer. He performed his own vocal tracks as King Blues in the 1983 comedy Get Crazy.
