Bill Moss (born September 4, 1933 in Luton, Bedfordshire) is a British former racing driver from England. He entered one World Championship Formula One race, the 1959 British Grand Prix, with his United Racing Stable Cooper T51, a Formula 2 car. He failed to qualify.

He is not related to Stirling Moss, winner of 16 grands prix.
