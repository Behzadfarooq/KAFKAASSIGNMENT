Billy Dare (born February 14, 1927 in Willesden, London, died April 1994) was an English footballer who played as a forward.

Dare joined West Ham United from Brentford for Â£5,000 in 1955 and, alongside Johnny Dick and Vic Keeble, played in the club's Second Division championship season of 1957-58. He played 111 league games for the club, scoring 44 goals, until his departure in 1959.

Dare later played for Southern League team Yiewsley, where he played under Bill Dodgin, Sr. and later Jackie Milburn.
