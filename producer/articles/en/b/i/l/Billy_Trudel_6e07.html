Billy Trudel

Billy Trudel is a vocalist from Los Angeles He sung backing vocals with artists such as Elton John, and lead vocals for Warpipes and most recently Public Domain.

[edit] Musical credits

  • Wag the Dog (movie-1997)
  • Elton John One Night Only-The Greatest Hits (2000)
  • Andrew W.K The Wolf (2003)
  • www.smaktones.com (2006)
