Bill Johnson (b. 1951) is an American actor most well known for his role as Leatherface in the 1986 horror film The Texas Chainsaw Massacre Part 2.

To fans of the Ultima series of role-playing games, he gained fame as the voice of the Guardian, the final antagonist of the Ultima series.
