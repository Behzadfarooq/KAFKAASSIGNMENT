William "Bill" Mosienko (Born - November 2, 1921 in Winnipeg, Manitoba, Canada - died July 9, 1994) was a Canadian hockey Right Winger who played 14 seasons in the National Hockey League for the Chicago Blackhawks. He is most famous for the fastest hat trick in NHL history, a record that still stands. On March 23, 1952, playing for the Chicago Blackhawks, he scored three goals in 21 seconds against the New York Rangers. He was inducted into the Hockey Hall of Fame in 1965.

Mosienko died of brain cancer July 9th, 1994.
