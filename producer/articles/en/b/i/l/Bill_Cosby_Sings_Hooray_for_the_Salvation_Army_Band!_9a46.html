Bill Cosby Sings Hooray for the Salvation Army Band! (1968) is the ninth album by Bill Cosby. This was his second studio album which featured his singing.

  2. Sunny (Hebb) – 3:26

  4. (I'm A) Road Runner (Dozier, Holland) – 3:33
  5. (I Can't Get No) Satisfaction (Jagger/Richards) – 2:41
  6. Get Out of My Life, Woman (Toussaint) – 2:50
  7. Hooray for the Salvation Army Band (Cosby, Smith) – 3:06
  8. Funky North Philadelphia (Lee) – 2:38

  10. Ursalena (Cosby, Smith) – 2:36

  12. Stop, Look & Listen (Lee, Telf) – 3:15
