Bilafond La

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Bilafond La
Elevation 5,450 metres (17,881 ft)
Location India
Range Eastern Karakoram Range

Bilafond La (meaning "Pass of the Butterflies"), also known as the Saltoro Pass, is a mountain pass situated on Saltoro Ridge, which sits immediately west of the vast Siachen Glacier. Currently held by India, the pass lies near the line of control dividing Indian- and Pakistani-administered territory.
