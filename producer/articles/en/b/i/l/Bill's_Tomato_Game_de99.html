Bill's Tomato Game is a 1992 puzzle, platform computer game for the Atari ST and Amiga. The game was developed and published by Psygnosis. The game requires the player to guide Bill back to the tomato truck he was in with his girlfriend which he fell of after a snag in the road. The levels consist of numerous obstacles he has to overcome using the help of various items (e.g. fans or trampolines).
