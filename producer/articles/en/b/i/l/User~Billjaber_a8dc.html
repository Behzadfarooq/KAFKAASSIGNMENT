Billal Jaber (born June 03, 1990, in New Orleans, Louisiana, USA), is an Arab-American.

[edit] History

Billal Jaber was born in New Orleans, Louisiana on June 3, 1990. Billal was raised in Chalmette, St. Bernard Parish, Louisiana. Billal has both parents, 2 sisters, a sister-in-law, a brother, and a brother-in-law. Billal went to Lacoste Elementary School from 1995-2000, N.P. Trist Middle School from 2000-2004, Andrew Jackson Fundamental Magnet High School from 2004-2005, Northshore High School 2005- 2006, and now at Chalmette High School.

[edit] Hurricane Katrina

Before Hurricane Katrina, Billal Jaber evacuated to Dallas, TX. Hurricane Katrina hit the Louisiana/Mississippi coast, flooding out St. Bernard Parish with over 10 feet in some areas. Billal's house recieved 8ft of water during the hurricane. Billal stayed in Amerisuites in Dallas, then moved the Drury Hotels in Lafayette, LA, and then moved to Slidell, LA where he is staying right now.

[edit] Acting Career

Billal began acting in 2000 in a 5th grade, school play. Hew as a Judge and he was also a Narrator. In 2004, Billal was in his 8th Grade play of The Wizard Of Oz, in which Billal was Professor Marvel and The Wizard.

[edit] TV Appearances

In late 2004 Billal was selected to appear in a local Cox Communications show called "Education Plugged In," or "EPI". In late 2005 the show was changed to "Cox Plugged In" or "CPI" The show was stopped due to Hurricane Katrina, but has began airing again.
