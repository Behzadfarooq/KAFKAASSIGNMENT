Bill Miller (pianist)

Bill Miller (1915-2006) started his whole career with Frank Sinatra in 1951 and stayed with him, playing on all of the hits until 1998 when Sinatra died. He later played on Robbie Williams Swing When You're Winning album, in tribute to Sinatra and his kin.

Bill Miller also played on the recreation of Silent Night, rerecorded after Sinatra's death with a full orchestra.
