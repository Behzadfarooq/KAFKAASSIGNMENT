[edit] Error In Title

There is an error in the title of the first Harry Potter book. Wikipedia acknowledges the book by Harry Potter and the Philosepher's Stone, when really the actual title of the book is Harry Potter and the Sorcerer's Stone. I'm neither a member nor frequent user of Wikipedia, and so I'm not sure on how this can be changed on the entire site, but if anyone could something about this error I'm sure it would be greatly appreciated!

The title given is the original British title. It was only known as Harry Potter and the Sorcerer's Stone In the U.S. edition. The custom on WIkipedia is to use british english, spelling, conventions when refering to british books or culture or art; to use U.S. conventions for U.S. things and wihn either would do whoever makes the first significnt edit gets to chose and everyone else shoudl follow their lead.
