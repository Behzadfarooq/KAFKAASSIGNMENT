Talk:Billy Klippert

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Billy Klippert was proposed for deletion. This page is an archive of the discussion about the proposed deletion. This page is no longer live. Further comments should be made on the article's talk page rather than here so that this page is preserved as an historic record. The result of the debate was KEEP

Seems non-notable (and note different spelling in article). Second runner-up in a TV contest? Fancruft. — Bill 15:49, 10 Oct 2004 (UTC)

  • Keep, on the grounds Wikipedia is Not Paper. Much as I hate them, I think that the finalists in major reality TV programmes are just about notable. Darksun 18:14, 10 Oct 2004 (UTC)
  • Redirect to Canadian Idol. Gamaliel 18:42, 10 Oct 2004 (UTC)
  • Change vote to keep based on updated version of article. Gamaliel 02:54, 11 Oct 2004 (UTC)
  • It pains me greatly to say this, but the guy has parlayed his also-ran status into an album and has had Top 40 hits. In fact, he's number two on the MuchMoreMusic charts this very week, behind only Maroon 5 -- check here if you don't believe me. Can't stand him (nor can I stand anyone associated with that whole idiotic slice of pop culture übercrap), but he's a definite keep. I ain't gonna be the one to expand the substub, but he's a valid entry. Bearcat 20:55, 10 Oct 2004 (UTC)
  • If you do decide to delete this one, please also delete the article on William Hung; otherwise keep. Auric The Rad 23:59, Oct 10, 2004 (UTC)
  • Delete. Not notable. --Improv 01:21, 11 Oct 2004 (UTC)
    • Having the current number two hit single in all of Canada doesn't make him even a bit notable? Wow. Reluctantly, I did expand the stub as a result of this vote, even though I said I wasn't going to. Bearcat 02:22, 11 Oct 2004 (UTC)
  • Keep, hit singles and a major part in a popular tv show are pretty notable —siroχo 03:55, Oct 11, 2004 (UTC)
  • Keep. Ambi 07:05, 11 Oct 2004 (UTC)
  • Keep - In my view, musicians deserve articles if they meet at least one of the following criteria:
    • hit records especially in a Wikipedia en country - this fellow has a hit record in Canada; or
    • winning significant music award - Grammy, Juno, ARIA award etc;or
    • is a member of a hall of fame for the relevant genre - rock and roll, country etc; or
    • is otherwise notable - a Google search shows nearly 5,000 entries so far [1] and has a rudimentary All Music Guide article [2] as well as appearing on a top-rated television program. Capitalistroadster 09:10, 11 Oct 2004 (UTC)
  • Keep. Like it or not, he's a star. — Gwalla | Talk 03:08, 12 Oct 2004 (UTC)
