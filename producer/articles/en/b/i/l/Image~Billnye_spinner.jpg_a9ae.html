[edit] Fair use for Battlebots

The image linked here is claimed to be used under fair use as:

  1. it is a historically significant photo of a notable individual on a notable program;
  2. it is of much lower resolution than the original (copies made from it will be of very inferior quality)

  4. Its inclusion in the article adds significantly to the article because it shows a scene from the subject of this article, the style of robots used, the set decoration and thus mood of the series, and the notable guest, Bill Nye. -- Zanimum 19:49, 9 February 2007 (UTC)
