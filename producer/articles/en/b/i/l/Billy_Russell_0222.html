William McKnight Russell (born Glasgow, September 14, 1959) is a former professional footballer who now works as Centre of Excellence Manager[1] at Hull City, where he has been caretaker manager on three occasions.

As a player he was a defender, playing for Everton, Celtic, Doncaster Rovers, Scunthorpe United and Rotherham United, making over 450 league appearances.
