Billy Martin or Bill Martin may refer to:

  • Billy Martin (1928-89), a former Major League Baseball player and manager
  • Billy Martin (percussionist), the drummer for jazz trio Medeski Martin & Wood
  • Billy Martin (tennis), a pro tennis player from the early 1970s
  • Bill Martin (b. 1938), Scottish songwriter
  • Billy Martin, a pseudonym briefly adopted by Billy Joel around 1972, to get away from what he perceived as mismanagement
  • William Dean Martin (b. 1981), a guitarist for popular American band Good Charlotte
  • William E. Martin, American songwriter and screenwriter
  • Billy Martin, the head writer and executive producer of Bill Maher's programs Politically Incorrect, Real Time with Bill Maher and Amazon Fishbowl.
