Bikku Bitti
Elevation 2,267 metres (7,438 feet)
Location Libya
Range Tibesti Mountains
Coordinates 21°59′53″N, 19°08′37″E

Bikku Bitti is the highest mountain in Libya at 2,267 m (7,438 feet). It is located in the Tibesti Mountains in southern Libya near the Chad border.'
