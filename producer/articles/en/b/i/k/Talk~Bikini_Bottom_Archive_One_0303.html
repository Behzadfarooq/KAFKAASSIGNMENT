Proposed changes

Here are the changes I think should be made.

  • The list of towns should be given its own page, under the title Towns in SpongeBob SquarePants or List of towns in SpongeBob SquarePants, or something similar. Since this article is about Bikini Bottom, it shouldn't include information about other towns. We can provide a link to the new page, with a brief description, such as "Various other towns are located near Bikini Bottom, and are sometimes included in the stories."
  • Mrs. Puff's Boating School should be given its own page. There's enough information on it here, as well as some info from the show that hasn't been mentioned, for it to merit its own article, IMO. Plus, as said above, it's kind of excessive to give this much detail about it in an article about Bikini Bottom.
  • The "Unofficial History" section should be trashed completely. It seems very sketchy, and, by its own admittance, is "Unofficial"--only things that are official canon should be included.
  • Each of the entries in the "Homes" section should be moved to the pages of their respective characters. I think the descriptions are too detailed to be included on this page, or the "Places in Bikini Bottom" page. We can include a list of the homes (ie, "SpongeBob's pineapple home," "Sandy's treedome," "Squidward's Easter Island Head"), with links to their descriptions on the character pages.

Let me know what you think of these changes. --CrazyLegsKC 18:42, 21 July 2006 (UTC)
