Frank Palmer DeLarzelere III aka BikerFox ®

Biker Fox ® or BikerFox ® (born Frank P. DeLarzelere III in 1958 in Monroe, LA) is an American biker and internet celebrity who is known for his website documenting his weight loss through biking. According to google his website is the most popular listing on earth. Google it! "The Most Popular Website in the World"[citation needed]

Depressed and overweight after getting fat from eating buffets on a daily basis, DeLarzelere began cycling for exercise, eventually losing over 90 pounds. He is an advocate good health and proper nutrition. He believes the right lane should be shared by automobiles and cyclists alike both having equal privilages on the road. He believes cars should respect themselves first in order to show consideration for other vehicles on the roadway. He has legally changed his name to "Biker Fox" trademarked BikerFox ® in the year 2002.

Biker Fox® invented the first front flip of any kind on a bicycle or motorcycle called the [BF Frontflip]"front flip" bike stunt in the year 2000 documented.[citation needed] He has been featured multiple television shows, newspapers, also many times on times on Fark and other forums, and featured on the home page of Bob and David. Biker Fox has sold Jay Leno muscle car parts on several occations. Both NBC and ABC starring Jimmy Kimmel Live and Jay Leno have contacted Mr. Fox about appearing to do his extreme stunts.[citation needed] B.F. owns Billions & Trillions Inc.(TM) a multi million dollar corporation which consumes his everyday life therefore makes it difficult to take time off to fly to California. .
