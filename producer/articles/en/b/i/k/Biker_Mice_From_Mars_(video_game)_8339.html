Biker Mice From Mars (video game)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

You have new messages (last change).

Biker Mice From Mars is a Super Nintendo Entertainment System video game based on the animates series of the same name. The European version is a big advertisement for Snickers candy bars. The object is to defeat space aliens in a series of motorcycle races. The winner gets the trophy and the losers have to start again.
