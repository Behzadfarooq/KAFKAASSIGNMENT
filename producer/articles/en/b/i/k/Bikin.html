Bikin

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Bikin (Russian: Бики́н) is a town in Khabarovsk Krai in Russia, located on the Bikin River (Ussuri's tributary) some 230 km southwest of Khabarovsk. Population: 19,600 (2005 est.); 19,641 (2002 Census); 19,000 (1967). Bikin was founded in 1885 under the name of Bikinskaya and was granted town status in 1938.
