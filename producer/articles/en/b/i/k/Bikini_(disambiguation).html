Bikini (disambiguation)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

A bikini is a type of women's bathing suit.

Bikini may also refer to:

  • Bikini (underwear), a type of undergarment showing a half naked woman
  • Bikini contest, a competition where women compete against each other in bikinis
  • Bikini waxing, a type of hair removal in the pubic region
  • String bikini, a bikini that is scantier and more revealing than traditional

In bands:

  • Bikini (band), a Hungarian rock band
  • Bikini Kill, an American punk rock band

In other:

  • Bikini Atoll, an uninhabited atoll in one of the Micronesian Islands in the Pacific Ocean
  • BIKINI state, an indication of an alert state used by the British government
  • Bikini Bottom, a city from SpongeBob SquarePants
  • A type of cloth top for an automobile that covers from the windshield back to just behind the first row of seats is referred to as a "bikini top"
