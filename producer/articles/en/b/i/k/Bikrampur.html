Bikrampur

Bikrampur is a historic region in Bengal, currently in Munshiganj District, Bangladesh. The region is famous for its cultural influences, and early Buddhist scholarship.

[edit] People from Bikrampur

  • Atisha
  • Jagadish Chandra Bose
  • Brojen Das
  • Durga Mohan Das
  • Dwarkanath Ganguly
  • Benoy Basu
  • Badal Gupta
  • Dinesh Gupta
