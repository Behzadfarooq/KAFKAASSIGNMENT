The Biq'at Hayarden Regional Council (Hebrew: מועצה אזורית בקעת הירדן‎), lit. Jordan River Regional Council (for explanation, please see Jordan Valley Regional Council), is a regional council in the Jordan River Valley of the West Bank. The municipal territory of the council reaches from Mehola in the north to Jericho in the south, and includes over twenty Israeli settlements.

Most of the settlements are located on the two major north-south roads traversing the council's territory. The Allon Road on the west and Route 90 on the east.

Dovi Tal is the head of the council (2006).
