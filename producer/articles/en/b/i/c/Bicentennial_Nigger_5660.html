Bicentennial Nigger is a comedy album by the comedian Richard Pryor. It won the 1977 Grammy Award for Best Comedy Album.

He performed "Acid" during his legendary appearance on Saturday Night Live.

[edit] Track listing

  1. "Hillbilly"
  2. "Black and White Women"
  3. "Our Gang"
  4. "Bicentennial Prayer"
  5. "Black Hollywood"
  6. "Mudbone Goes to Hollywood"
  7. "Chinese Restaurant"
  8. "Acid"
  9. "Bicentennial Nigger"
  • On cassette releases, "Acid" would be moved to side one after "Bicentennial Prayer" to make the content more even on each side of the tape.
