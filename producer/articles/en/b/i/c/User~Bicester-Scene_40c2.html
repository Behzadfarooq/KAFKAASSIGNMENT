[edit] Excessum

Excessum are an unsigned Deathcore band who set up camp in good ol' Bicester; Oxfordshire, late 2006. Their line up is made up from ex-members of local bands such as iionix (Roman collyer & Adam Verney), Taken Into Exile (Ryan Mitchell & Glenn McFarlane), CST (Tommy Plummer) and Vomit on my **** (Marc Olah).

Band members:

-Marc Olah (Vocalist)

-Ryan Mitchell (Vocalist)

-Roman Collyer (Lead Guitarist)

-Tommy Plummer (Rhythm Guitarist and Backing Vocals)

-Adam Verney (Bass Guitarist)
