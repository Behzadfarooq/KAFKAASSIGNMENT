Bickley, Western Australia

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Bickley is a suburb of Perth, Western Australia, located within the Shire of Kalamunda. Its postcode is 6076.

The Seven Network and Australian Broadcasting Corporation's Perth Television transmission towers are located here. Perth Observatory is also located here.[1]
