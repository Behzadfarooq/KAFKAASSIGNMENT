Bicester North is the main railway station of Bicester, a town in north Oxfordshire, England. It is a station on the mainline Chiltern Railways service between London Marylebone and Birmingham Snow Hill. From Bicester North, it takes roughly an hour to travel to either London or Birmingham.

There is another railway station in Bicester: Bicester Town, which is operated by First Great Western and provides a branch-line service to Oxford and is thus less busy - in terms of passenger numbers - and much more quiet than Bicester North.
