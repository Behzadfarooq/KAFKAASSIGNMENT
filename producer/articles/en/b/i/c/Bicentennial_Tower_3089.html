The Bicentennial Tower is an observation tower located in Erie, PA and features panoramic views of Lake Erie, Presque Isle State Park, and Downtown Erie. The tower was built to signify the city's 200th year in 1996. It is 187 feet tall and has 2 observation decks. [1]
