Bicknacre is a village in Essex, England. It is located approximately 10 km (5 miles) southeast from the county town of Chelmsford. The village is in the borough of Chelmsford and in the parliamentary constituency of Maldon & East Chelmsford.

[edit] Notable residents

  • Richard Robarts - A Formula One driver born in the village.
