Bitterness (emotion)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

You have new messages (last change).

Bitterness is an emotion which encompasses feelings of anger and hatred. It is often a feeling of resentment directed towards others for reasons of discrimination, neglect, jealousy, or trauma. Often times, bitterness comes after a bad experience. Most people don't tend to feel bitterness against a person, but more often towards an event. For example, a bitter divorce, or a bitter memory.
