Miffster posted this on 8/18/06:
If it's an external connector (and you're using an old TV tuner card), I believe it's probably a mini-RCA cable (with both ends terminating to a stereo headphone RCA plug). You can get those at Ace or Handyman, otherwise, just splice two mini-RCA headphone plugs so you get a cable with plugs at both ends. I used to connect the TV tuner's audio output to the soundcard's line input using this method years ago.
