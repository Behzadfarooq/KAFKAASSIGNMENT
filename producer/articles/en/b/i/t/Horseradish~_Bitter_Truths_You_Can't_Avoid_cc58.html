Horseradish: Bitter Truths You Can't Avoid is a 2007 book written by Daniel Handler, under the pseudonym Lemony Snicket. It is a book of quotes drawn mainly from Snicket's famous A Series of Unfortunate Events. [2][3]

The official synopsis from publishers HarperCollins is as follows:

Life is a turbulent journey, fraught with confusion, heartbreak, and inconvenience. Unfortunately, this book will not help.

Instead, this book of wit and wisdom offers a selection of alarming but inescapable truths from the work of Lemony Snicket, along with selections from his unpublished papers and remarks he has made at dinner parties and anarchist riots. [4]
