[edit] PT Bitratex Industries

Textile Yarn and Embroidery Fabrics manufacturers from Indonesia

[1]

[edit] Profile

[2]

PT Bitratex Industries is one of the leading Textile Yarn and Schiffli Embroidery Fabric manufacturers from Indonesia, since 1979, manufactures spun-yarn and open-end yarn of cotton, polyester, viscose and their blends. Our embroidery fabrics of laces, jacquard, motifs, allover etc. are used for home furnishing and industrial uses.
