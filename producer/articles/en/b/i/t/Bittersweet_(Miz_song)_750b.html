"Bittersweet", Miz's fifth Japanese single, is the last one set for her album Mizrock. Both songs are harder rock than Miz's past few releases. They are both new songs, and Bittersweet is completely in English.

[edit] Track listing

  1. Bittersweet
  2. Give It All Away
  3. Bittersweet - Bionic Rockers Frequency Remix
  4. Bittersweet - Dr. Shingo Remix

[edit] Promotional Video

The idea for the PV of this single is the same as Backseat Baby's. Miz is, again, in a white room. But the main colors are now black and purple, giving it a more punk-rock theme. The PV has scenes such as Miz singing in the white room, and breaking a TV with a guitar.
