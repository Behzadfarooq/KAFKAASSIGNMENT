"Bittersweet Bundle of Misery" is a song by Graham Coxon and was released as the second single from his fifth solo album Happiness in Magazines in 2004 (see 2004 in British music). It's very similar to "Coffee & TV", a song written and sung by Coxon from Blur's album 13.
