Bitburg High School

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Location of the school.

Bitburg High School is part of the Kaiserslautern School District and has a student population of approximately 300 with a faculty of thirty-five. Although Bitburg High School is located in Bitburg Air Base, some of the students that attend this school live in Spangdahlem Air Base or villages in the surrounding areas. Bitburg High School was one of the first schools in DoDDS to successfully implement and maintain the Block 8/Seminar schedule. Bitburg High School has a fully functional intranet system in which students can save files to their own server partition.

Bitburg High School offers a variety of athletics, courses, and extra curricular activities. Some of the courses offered at the school can only be taken through distance learning. However, more courses are being offered at the school every year. Athletic activities range from tennis to soccer, basketball to volleyball, cross country to wrestling, and even swimming through the local pool Cascade. Extra curricular activities come and go due to the transient student population, but some of the notable ones are National Honor Society and Student Council.

Due to the fact that Bitburg Air Base is closing, so will the high school. The planned year is 2010.
