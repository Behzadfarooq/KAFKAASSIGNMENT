Bitch Alert

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Bitch Alert is a rock band from Finland. The band has put out three albums in Finland, and in May 2004 released their debut UK album, ..rriot!. The band has three members: Heinie on vocals and guitar, Kimmo on bass, and Maritta on drums. Their style is the largely underground Riot Grrl movement.
