BITE TV is a Canadian category 2 digital cable specialty channel. BITE TV broadcasts Canadian and international short form film, videography, animation, games, and professional and amateur production that mainly target men ages 18 - 25. BITE is an interactive television channel that delivers on-air interaction, online participation, wireless downloading and PC or mobile chat-to-screen directly on the television. The channel is owned by Jeffrey Elliot of Glassbox Television Inc.

BITE has a working relation with Stomp Records, Network Ireland and MavTV in the United States

[edit] Programming Format

BITE, unlike many other television channels, has no set time blocks for its programming because of its focus on short films. Its content is programmed daily, a month in advance. BITE does this by the Broadview software which allows them to program it how they wish.

The look is also new, by telling the viewer how long that short is, and what the next short is coming up next, and the three shorts that are coming on after that one.
