I'm a high school student living in the suburbs of New York City. I'm the son of two writers. I'm Pagan, and both of my parents are too. I plan on becoming a linguist.

I have a tendency to speak in stories much of the time. I find it a very useful method of communicating, if time-consuming. It gets the point across in a way that I feel I couldn't do if I used my own words.

I, like almost any teenage boy, brag about my sex life with little to no actual activity to back it up. My philosophy is, if you believe a teenager's brags about his sex life, then you're gullible enough to deserve it.

I like to invent teams of superheroes in my mind, knowing that they'll never exist in comic book form, and especially not epic high-budget movie form, which is how I picture the battle scenes.

Unlike most teenagers, I know I'm not an adult yet. I've always been aware that I'm still a kid, which is a rare gift. Few people are that self-aware.

I'm a dancer. Dance is sacred to me, even Hip-Hop. Especially Hip-Hop. I also do tap dance, which is not actually as White a dance style as you'd think. It's really biracial: half Black, half Irish.
