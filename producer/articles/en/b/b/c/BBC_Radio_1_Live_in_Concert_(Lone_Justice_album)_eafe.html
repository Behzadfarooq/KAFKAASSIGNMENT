BBC Radio 1 Live in Concert is a live album by American roots rock band Lone Justice, released in 1994 (see 1994 in music).

[edit] Track listing

  1. "Wait" (Ryan Hedgecock, Maria McKee) – 4:37
  2. "Sweet Baby" (Little Steven & the Disciples of Soul, McKee, Benmont Tench) – 5:24
  3. "I Ain't Got No Home in This World" (Woody Guthrie) – 2:32
  4. "The Gift" (McKee) – 4:19
  5. "Wheels" (McKee) – 6:28
  6. "Shelter" (Little Steven & the Disciples of Soul, McKee) – 5:07
  7. "Belfrey" (Shane Fontaine, Little Steven & the Disciples of Soul, McKee) – 6:01
  8. "I Found Love" (McKee, Steven Van Zandt) – 4:19
  9. "Heaven" (McKee) – 6:28
  10. "Inspiration" (McKee, Gregg Sutton) – 3:52
  11. "Sweet Jane" (Lou Reed) – 9:03

[edit] Personnel

  • Maria McKee - vocals, guitar
  • Gregg Sutton - bass, vocals
  • Shane Fontaine - guitar
  • Rudy Richman - drums
  • Bruce Brody - keyboards

[edit] Production

  • Producer: Pete Dauncey
