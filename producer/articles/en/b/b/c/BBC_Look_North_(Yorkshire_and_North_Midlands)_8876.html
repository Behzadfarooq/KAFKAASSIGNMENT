Look North is a name used by the BBC for its local regional news programme in three regions in the North of England. The new corporate ident first began on 16 September 2002. The programme can be watched in any part of the UK (and Europe) on digital satellite channel 976 or 977 on the BBC UK regional TV on satellite service.

The Leeds programme covers the areas of Radio Leeds and Radio Sheffield. Due to the size of North Yorkshire, the listenership of Radio York is covered by the geographically-multitudinous Look North programmes from Leeds and Newcastle.

[edit] History

The Leeds news programme is broadcast from Emley Moor to Yorkshire and the northern parts of Derbyshire and Nottinghamshire. The programme has been broadcasting from Leeds since 25 March 1968. This was the main programme for the whole of the Yorkshire region until 11 November 2002, when a new, much more modern studio had been built in Hull, and the and the programme split in two. Look North Leeds used to be produced in a building on Woodhouse Lane, close to Leeds University. A new building was built near the West Yorkshire Playhouse on St Peter's Square, and Look North moved there on 27 September 2004. A separate independent Look North (East Yorkshire and Lincolnshire) is hosted by Peter Levy.

With Harry Gration at the helm, rugby league received equal (if not more) coverage as football. There is a lot of sport covered on the programme.

[edit] Reception issues

Homes in Southern areas of North Yorkshire (York and southwards) who have their aerials pointing at Bilsdale, receive BBC One North East. This leads to some people in this area receiving BBC One North East, with their 'local' news being mainly from the Newcastle area, whilst most households have their aerials pointing to Emley Moor receive BBC One Yorkshire, based in Leeds, seems much more 'local'.

[edit] Presenters

  • Christa Ackroyd - former presenter of Yorkshire Television's Calendar, now presents the Leeds programme. She comes from Bradford, and also worked at Pennine Radio and Radio Aire. She started on 3 September 2001.
  • Harry Gration - has presented the Leeds programme since 1982 (although took five years out in the 1990s to be a spokesman for the rugby league, then tried his hand at BBC South Today).
  • Penny Bustin - former Look East presenter
  • Ian White - Late bulletin presenter
  • Clare Frisby - Breakfast presenter
  • Danni Hewson - late night newsreader, used to read the news on Five News.
  • Charlotte Leeming - late night newsreader.
  • Cathy Killick - Politics Show presenter
  • Phil Bodmer - ex Radio York.
  • Amanda Harper

[edit] Weather presenters

  • Paul Hudson - Paul usually covers the main evening and lunchtime weather on Look North.
  • Lisa Gallagher - Lisa presents the weather usually for bulletins as part of BBC Breakfast.

[edit] Former presenters

  • Peter Levy - now the main presenter for Look North in Yorkshire and Lincolnshire based in Hull.
  • Sophie Raworth - between 1995 and 1997 - now main presenter of the BBC One O'Clock News.
  • Mike Smartt - between 1976 and 1982 - later founder of BBC News Online
  • Khalid Aziz
  • Judith Stamper - now runs a degree in Broadcast Journalism at the University of Leeds. She left in 1995.
  • Philip Hayton - BBC News 24 presenter before he left the corporation in 2005.
  • Mike McCarthy - now on Sky News.
