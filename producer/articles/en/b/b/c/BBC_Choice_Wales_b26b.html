BBC Choice Wales was a Welsh television channel started as part of BBC Wales' digital broadcasting experiments. This regional digital television channel ran for a short time in the late 1990's until the end of 2001. Just as BBC Choice UK was replaced by BBC Three, BBC Choice Wales was replaced on Welsh digital television by BBC 2W.
