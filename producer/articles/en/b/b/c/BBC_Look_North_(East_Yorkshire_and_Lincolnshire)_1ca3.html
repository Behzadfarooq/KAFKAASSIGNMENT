Look North is a name used by the BBC for its local regional news programme in three regions in the North of England.

The Hull-based programme was launched in November 2002 with Helen Fospero and Peter Levy using a studio built in the existing Radio Humberside building in Chapel Street. In 2004 Look North, along with Radio Humberside and the Online/Interactive Project moved to their current home in Queen's Gardens. Broadcast from Belmont (the tallest structure in Europe) it is one of the BBC's newest regions. Before November 11th 2002, this region was part of a larger BBC Yorkshire and Lincolnshire region, served by Look North from Leeds. The program can be watched in any part of the UK (and Europe) on digital satellite channel 977 on the BBC UK regional TV on satellite service.

Look North's main presenter in Hull is Peter Levy, with weather from Paul Hudson. Lisa Gallagher presents the weather on the Breakfast bulletins . The programme provides daily news for East Yorkshire and Lincolnshire. As well as the newsroom in Hull, it has reporters based in Lincoln. Currently it produces a half-hour programme at 18:30, a bulletin at 13:30, regular opt-ins to BBC Breakfast and bulletins at 15:25 and 22:25 every weekday, as well as one bulletin each on Saturday and Sunday. The current news editor is Dave Betts. Catherine Hearne, who previously edited BBC London News, is the head of regional and local programmes for BBC Yorkshire and Lincolnshire.

The Hull programme covers East Yorkshire, the area covered by Radio Humberside and Radio Lincolnshire. Part of west Lincolnshire is also covered by East Midlands Today.

Some households in north-west Norfolk whose television aerials point towards the Belmont transmitting station in Lincolnshire receive BBC Look North rather than BBC Look East, which the rest of Norfolk receives. The increase in households with satellite television means that many receive Look East digitally.

[edit] News and weather presenters

  • Peter Levy - presents the programme from Hull. Peter always read the Breakfast, Lunchtime and Sunday bulletins for the pre-2002 Look North from Leeds as well as hosting a weekday morning show on Radio Leeds
  • Paul Hudson - Weather forecaster from Keighley in Yorkshire. Famous for wearing loud shirts and his humour.
  • Phillip Chapman
  • Lisa Gallagher - Weather forecaster for the breakfast programme. From Bolton, and geography graduate of the University of Hull.
  • Caroline Davies
  • Hannah Moffat - Scottish geography graduate from Aberdeen University who reads the breakfast bulletins, and trained as a weather presenter.
  • Nicola Hudson
  • Simon Clark - Popular sports presenter from Hull who was formerly Sports Editor at Radio Sheffield and Viking FM in Hull.
  • Blair Jacobs - occasionally presents the breakfast and late night bulletin, and who is most well known for his lunchtime Soapbox show on Radio Humberside.

[edit] Former presenters

  • Helen Philpot - Saturday presenter on Radio Humberside.
  • Helen Fospero - Read the news on Sky News and GMTV before presenting Look North. Now presents the news on five and Sky News.
