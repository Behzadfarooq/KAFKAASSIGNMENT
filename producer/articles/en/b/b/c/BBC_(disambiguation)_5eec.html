BBC (disambiguation)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The BBC (British Broadcasting Corporation) is the national publicly funded broadcaster of the United Kingdom.

BBC is a three-letter abbreviation with multiple meanings, and may also refer to:

  • BBC, the ICAO airline code of Biman Bangladesh Airlines
  • Brown, Boveri & Cie, now Asea Brown Boveri, a multinational power/automation company headquartered in Zürich, Switzerland
  • Breakthrough Breast Cancer, a British charity
  • "BBC" (song), a song by Ming Tea written for the movie Austin Powers: International Man of Mystery
  • BBC, the English appearance of the Cyrillic letters ВВС (VVS), the acronym for the Russian Air Force
  • British Born Chinese, an alternate name for British Chinese
  • Billionaire Boys Club, the 1980s southern California investment group organized by Joe Gamsky (aka "Joe Hunt")
  • Billionaire Boys Club (clothing), the fashion label set up by Pharrell Williams/The Neptunes/Star Trak
  • Bluegrass Brewing Company, a Kentucky based microbrewery
  • (front) Bumper to Back of Cab, a key dimension of trucks
  • Big Block Chevy (see Chevrolet Big-Block engine)
  • Bionicle Based Creation, a fan-created Bionicle creation that was not a set originally marketed by the Lego Group.

In politics and government:

  • Biplobi Bangla Congress, a political party in West Bengal, India
  • Blackpool Borough Council
  • Bournemouth Borough Council, an English unitary authority
  • Belgo-British Conference

In Popular Culture

  • Acronym for Big Black Cock, in the Interracial relations circuit. The phrase was coined by Steve Reid - a New Zealand celebrity.

In education:

  • Brisbane Boys' College, an all boys day and boarding school located in Brisbane, Queensland, Australia
  • Baptist Bible College, a Christian college in Clarks Summit, Pennsylvania

In broadcasting:

  • British Broadcasting Company Ltd (1922–1926), the commercial precursor of the British Broadcasting Corporation
  • Banahaw Broadcasting Corporation, located in the Philippines
  • Biwako Broadcasting Co., Ltd., a TV station in Shiga Prefecture, Japan
  • Barrington Broadcasting Company, LLC, a U.S. TV station headquartered in Hoffman Estates, Illinois
  • Broadbank Burbcasting Company, daily program hosted by Gary Burbank on radio station WLW in Cincinnati, Ohio

In computers:

  • BBC Micro, an early home computer from the 1980s
  • Boston Business Computing, former owners of the domain name bbc.com[1]
  • Big Blue and Cousins, the Greater Victoria PC Users' Association
  • LNX-BBC, a mini-Linux distribution
  • BBCode, a markup language commonly used on message boards
  • Big Blue Cup, Adventure Game Studio's logo and mascot
