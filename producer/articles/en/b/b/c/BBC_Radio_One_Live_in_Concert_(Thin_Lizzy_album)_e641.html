BBC Radio One Live in Concert
Live album by Thin Lizzy
Released 1994
Genre Hard rock
Length 76:47
Label Griffin Music
Professional reviews
  • All Music Guide link

This recording was released in 1994 as a bootleg. This show was originally intended to be Thin Lizzy's last concert. It was at the Reading Festival in 1983 during their Farewell Tour.

Thin Lizzy, although they officially split up in 1984, they played several more concerts after this recording, including the Monsters of Rock festival in Germany a fortnight later. A similar lineup, although under the name Grand Slam (band) performed a series of concerts in 1984 and 1985, before Phil Lynott's death in 1986.
