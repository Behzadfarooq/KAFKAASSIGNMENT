Drums and Wireless: BBC Radio Sessions 77-89 (1994) is a selection of songs performed by XTC for BBC radio. Its tracks were later included on Transistor Blast: The Best of the BBC Sessions.

[edit] Track listing

All songs written by Andy Partridge, except where noted.

  1. "Opening Speech"
  2. "No Thugs In Our House"
  3. "Runaways" (Colin Moulding)
  4. "You're The Wish You Are I Had"
  5. "Poor Skeleton Steps Out"
  6. "X Wires" (Moulding)
  7. "Seagulls Screaming Kiss Her, Kiss Her"
  8. "Real By Reel"
  9. "Into The Atom Age"
  10. "Meccanik Dancing (Oh We Go!)"
  11. "Ten Feet Tall" (Moulding)
  12. "Scarecrow People"
  13. "I'm Bugged"
  14. "Dance Band" (Moulding)
  15. "Jason And The Argonauts"
  16. "One Of The Millions" (Moulding)
  17. "Roads Girdle The Globe"
