BBC East Midlands is the BBC English Region covering Nottinghamshire, Leicestershire, most of Derbyshire and parts of western and southern Lincolnshire.

Its television service includes the daily East Midlands Today news programme, the weekly regional magazine programme Inside Out and the weekly Politics Show.

The region is the controlling centre for BBC Radio Nottingham, BBC Radio Derby and BBC Radio Leicester, and the local editions of the BBC's Where I Live websites.

The regional headquarters and television centre is in London Road, Nottingham.
