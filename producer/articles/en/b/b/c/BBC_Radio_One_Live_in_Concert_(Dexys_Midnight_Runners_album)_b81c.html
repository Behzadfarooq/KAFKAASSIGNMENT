BBC Radio One Live in Concert is the only official live album by Dexys Midnight Runners, recorded for the BBC in 1982 and released in 1995. The album is unique as it is the only live recording where the Searching for the Young Soul Rebels-era horn section is present alongside the Too-Rye-Ay-era strings.
