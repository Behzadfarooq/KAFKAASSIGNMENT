[edit] Criticism

These things got a lot of criticism in the media, and moreso than usual: it cost a million quid to make a bunch of computer-generated hippos, and at a time when jobs were cut and the TV licence was being raised. Can this be mentioned in the article? --84.64.51.100 22:22, 27 October 2006 (UTC)

Certainly not without evidence to back it up. I've not heard any more critisim than usual, and things like this really grab my attention - I'd have noticed. --TheIslander 20:36, 16 November 2006 (UTC)

[edit] Sombre ident?

The rhythm and movement idents had the ballet one that only ever seemed to be used if there was a dramatic incident like a terrorist attack, or if a royal pegged it. Does anyone know if there's an equivalent circle ident that hasn't been called into service yet, or if one of the listed ones seems to be preferred to introduce the news on grim days? 12:17, 6 January 2007 (UTC)

Not sure. Moon certainly seems to fit the bill, though - it seems to be used to introduce the news quite a bit, quite rightly. I didn't think that Ballet was purposefully designed to be the 'sombre' ident, more that once it was made it was decided not to use it much as was sombre. TheIslander 18:07, 9 January 2007 (UTC)
There are as yet no unused idents; moon is the sombre ident of the current set. As time goes on, more idents will be added to the line-up while it is possible some may be withdrawn as has been the case in the past. Hope that helps. Wikiwoohoo 19:36, 9 January 2007 (UTC)
