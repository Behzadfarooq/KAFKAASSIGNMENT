BBC Radio One Sessions is a 2002 album by Cowboy Junkies. It was released only in Canada and the United Kingdom. It compiles tracks that the band recorded for BBC Radio One in the UK.

It was released on Strange Fruit Records in the UK, and Latent Records in Canada.

[edit] Track listing

All tracks written by Michael Timmins, except "You Will Be Loved Again" by Mary Margaret O'Hara and "Powderfinger" by Neil Young.

  1. "Sun Comes Up, It's Tuesday Morning" – 4:00
  2. "Thirty Summers" – 4:03
  3. "You Will Be Loved Again" – 3:08
  4. "Cause Cheap is How I Feel" – 4:20
  5. "Powderfinger" – 5:21
  6. "Black Eyed Man" – 3:14
  7. "The Last Spike" – 4:15
  8. "Townes' Blues" – 2:49
  9. "A Common Disaster" – 3:51
  10. "Just Want to See" – 4:24
  11. "Lonely Sinking Feeling" – 4:43
  12. "Lay it Down" – 4:29

Tracks 1 to 5 were recorded on 3 July 1989. Tracks 6 to 8 were recorded on 27 February 1992. Tracks 9 to 12 were recorded on 27 February 1996.

[edit] Personnel

  • Margo Timmins: Vocals (All Tracks)
  • Michael Timmins: Guitar (All Tracks), Vocals (6-8)
  • Jeff Bird: Harmonica (1-8), Mondolin/Tambourine (1-5)
  • Jaro Czerwinec: Accordion (1-5)
  • Alan Anton: Bass (9-12)
  • Peter Timmins: Drums (9-12)
