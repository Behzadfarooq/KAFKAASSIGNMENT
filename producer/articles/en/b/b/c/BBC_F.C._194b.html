BBC F.C.

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

BBC Football Club was founded in 1925 as a weekend activity for members of staff of the BBC. The club played their home matches for many years until 2003 at Motspur Park, Surrey until the BBC sold the land to Ben Dunne. Ben Dunne plans to turn the land into a leisure complex.

Colours: Amber shirts, black shorts, amber socks. Also blue and white shirts, white shorts and white socks
