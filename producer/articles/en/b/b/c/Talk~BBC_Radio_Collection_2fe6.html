[edit] Third opinion

The link in question,

  • an AudioBooks.com "Completely FREE Audio Books" sub page of
  • the Pimsleur Foreign Language Courses Audio Books main page

violates Wikipedia:External links guidelines in two ways:

  • Wikipedia:External links#Sites requiring registration
  • Wikipedia:External links#Links normally to be avoided - particularly item 3: "Links mainly intended to promote a website."

The free audio books are part of the site's commercial promotion strategy to attract buyers. The link does no harm in a talk page discussion, but it does not belong in an encyclopedia article.

There is also a user conflict of interest here, as illustrated by:

  • Audiobookworm's 07:40, February 3, 2007 (UTC) edit with the edit summary:
  • (Created page with) "I love audio books and run the website AudioBooksCorner.com where I offer audiobooks for immediate download in MP3 or WMA format."

Audiobookworm, please read the Wikipedia:Conflict of interest guideline to understand what is and is not appropriate encyclopedia editing in your position. Thank you. — Athænara ✉ 12:43, 21 March 2007 (UTC)
