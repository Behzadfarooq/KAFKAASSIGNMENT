BBC National news is the British news shown on BBC1 and BBC2 through out the UK and Britain. The BBC is a tax-funded company, which does not include any adverts.

[edit] News programs

The output of national news on the BBC1 and BBC2 includes:

  • BBC One O'Clock News
  • BBC Six O'Clock News
  • BBC Ten O'Clock News
  • BBC Breakfast
  • Newsnight


BBC News is over 50 years old and is still in high demand, with a national news channel (BBC News 24) and an advertising-based international channel BBC World.
