[edit] Summary

The logo of the British Broadcasting Corporation's Computer Literacy Project, used on all the BBC-badged computers produced by Acorn Computers Ltd.

Original image by Daniel Beardsmore (Ghiraddje) available as Illustrator 8 format at [1]; SVG conversion and image optimisation by Nicholas Shanks.
