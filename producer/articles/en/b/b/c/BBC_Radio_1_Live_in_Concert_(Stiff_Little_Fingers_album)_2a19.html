BBC Radio 1 Live in Concert is a Live Album by the band Stiff Little Fingers, released in 1994 (see 1994 in music).

[edit] Track listing

  1. "Roots, Radicals, Rockers and Reggae" (Stiff Little Fingers) – 4:03
  2. "Just Fade Away" (Ogilvie, Stiff Little Fingers) – 3:05
  3. "Safe As Houses" (Ogilvie, Stiff Little Fingers) – 5:43
  4. "Gate 49" (Ogilvie, Stiff Little Fingers) – 2:31
  5. "Hits & Misses" (Ogilvie, Stiff Little Fingers) – 3:59
  6. "The Only One" (Ogilvie, Stiff Little Fingers) – 4:23
  7. "Silver Lining" (Ogilvie, Stiff Little Fingers) – 3:06
  8. "Piccadilly Circus" (Ogilvie, Stiff Little Fingers) – 4:54

[edit] Personnel

  • Jake Burns – vocals/guitar
  • Dolphin Taylor – drums
  • Henry Cluney – guitar
  • Bruce Foxton – bass
