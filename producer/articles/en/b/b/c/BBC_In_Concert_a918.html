Eleventh release by English post-punk group Killing Joke. Songs 1-10 were recorded at the 1986 Reading and Leeds Festival in England while the final three songs were recorded during a 1985 Paris Theatre appearance. It has been reviewed as a "better-than-average bootleg" due to the sound quality.
