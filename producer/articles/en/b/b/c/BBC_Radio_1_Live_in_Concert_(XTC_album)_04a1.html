BBC Radio 1 Live in Concert (1992) is a recording of an XTC live performance from 22nd December 1980.

  5. "No Language in Our Lungs" – 4:59
  6. "This Is Pop?" – 2:49
  7. "Scissor Man" – 4:49
  8. "Towers of London" – 5:23
  9. "Battery Brides" – 7:18
