BBC Scotland Sports Personality of the Year

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The BBC Scotland Sports Personality of the Year is the most prestigious annual sport award in Scotland. It is organised by BBC Scotland.

[edit] Winners

This list is incomplete; you can help by expanding it.
  • 2005: Andrew Murray, tennis player
  • 2004: Jim Anderson, disabled swimmer
  • 2003: Chris Hoy, track cyclist
  • 1996: Stephen Hendry, snooker player
  • 1987: Stephen Hendry, snooker player
  • 1978: Kenny Dalglish, football player
  • 1977: Kenny Dalglish, football player
