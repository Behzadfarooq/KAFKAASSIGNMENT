[edit] Rationale of fair use for Bringin' on the Heartbreak

The image Bbheartbreak.JPG is being linked here; though the picture is originally copyright, I (Extraordinary Machine 01:50, 17 August 2006 (UTC)) feel it is covered by fair use because:

  1. The image is a screenshot from a promotional music video originally created by Island Def Jam Records, and intended for wide distribution.
  2. No free or public domain images have been located for this single.
  3. The image does not limit the copyright owners' rights to distribute the single or music video in any way.
  4. For an article containing discussions of a music video, a screenshot is arguably the most important image that could be included. This is a screenshot from the video, intended by Island Def Jam Records for wide distribution. As such, it has future historical significance, and is a more appropriate choice than any other image available.
  5. Its inclusion in the article is important because it suggests something of the video's style.
  6. The image is being used for informational purposes only, and its use is not believed to detract from the original single or music video in any way.
