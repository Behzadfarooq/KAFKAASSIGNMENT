[edit] Images and diagramming

If I draw a diagram I tag them as Public Domain. Photographs are tagged as GFDL. Diagrams need to be of high enough resolution to enable publication quality printing. Feel free to request diagrams to be drawn or redrawn for any page you may wish. With regard to all images let me just say:

Functional items can be beautiful --Adrian Newey

[edit] About the name

Bb3cxv came about several years back. Faced with an unnecessary registration form, mashing the keyboard with the left hand resulted in bb3cxv. It has the plus side of being pretty unique, and as such a time saver.
