BBVA Banco Francés is a financial institution in Argentina.

[edit] History

Founded in October 14, 1886 in Buenos Aires as Banco Francés del Río de la Plata (English: French Bank of the River Plate), its the oldest private Bank in Argentina. In December 1996 Spain's Banco Bilbao Vizcaya now Banco Bilbao Vizcaya Argentaria acquired the 99.90% of the social shares of Otar S.R.L. which was a major shareholder of Sud América Inversiones S.A., holder of the 30.04% of the capital of Banco Francés, thus the BBVA in its name.
