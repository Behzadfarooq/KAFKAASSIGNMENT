BBVA Bancomer is the largest financial institution in Mexico, dominating about 20% of the market.

[edit] History

Founded in 1932 in Mexico City as Banco de Comercio (English: Commerce Bank) (Bancomer). In 1982 Bancomer was nationalized when President José López Portillo nationalized the country's banking system. In July 2000 it affiliated to Spain's Banco Bilbao Vizcaya Argentaria, thus the BBVA in its name. This bank serves over 11 million customers, has 1,704 bank locations, and 4,286 ATMs.
