BBS Radio (Blogin Broadcasting Service Radio) is an internet radio station that host a variety of different shows dealing with topics such as the paranormal, extraterrestrials, UFOs, religion, spirituality, and free energy. Their main headquarters is located in Paradise, California.

[edit] Objective

This is stated on their homepage:

We are dedicated to the TRUTH, and removing fear through communication (we're all in this together!). Many of us will find comfort and relief in working together towards the common good. Together we can thrive and make a difference in the lives our our children and our selves. Alone we will fail.

[edit] Radio

[edit] Categories

BBS Radio is split into four categories:

  • BBS Daily (Live Talk Radio)
  • BBS Late Night (Live Talk Radio)
  • BBS 24-Hour Music (Sensational Sounds)
  • BBS Kanned (Original Programming)

[edit] Audio

Streaming audio speeds provided are:

  • 96K Stereo hi-speed (24-Hour Music)
  • 56K Stereo hi-speed (Daily, Late Night)
  • 32K Stereo hi-speed (Kanned)
  • 24K Mono lo-speed (Daily, Late Night)

Streaming file formats provided are:

  • iTunes (.pls)
  • Quick Time Player (.qtl)
  • Real One Player (.ram)
  • VideoLan Player (.pls)
  • Winamp (.pls)
  • Windows Media Player (.asx)
