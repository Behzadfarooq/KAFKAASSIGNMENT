[edit] Biography

Theater director, performer, theater and film actor, writer, intermedia artist, and founder of international multimedia platform Bacači Sjenki (Shadow Casters) together with intermedia artist Katarina Pejović; Project leadership of Zagreb – Cultural Kapital of Europe 3000.

[edit] Educational background:

Bachelor of Arts, Academy of Theater and Film, Zagreb

[edit] Professional background:

  • 1995.

Co-founder of the international artist group ORCHESTRA STOLPNIK; Bologna (together with the Italian artist and performer Pina Siotto) Collaboration with the United Nations Civil Police investigating atrocities and war crimes in Western Slavonia

  • 1993.

Co-founder of Flying University, a political and artistic project-school that researches issues of identity and the perception of reality

  • 1991.

Co-founder of the Antiwar campaign movement in Croatia

  • 1986.

Founder of the Theater of obvious phenomena, for which he produced several performances

[edit] Performances (Created for ORCHESTRA STOLPNIK/SHADOW CASTERS):

  • 2001 – 2005

“Shadow Casters”, Multimedia urban Workshop/Performance in framework of the Urban Festival; Zagreb (Ljubljana, Graz, Bologna, Belgrade, New York)

  • 2001

“Un’ Italia tutta per me”, Festival Infrazioni, Casalecchio di Reno; Bologna

  • 2000

“Hotel Europa” (Dramaturgy/Text by Goran Stefanovski); Bologna Cultural Capital of Europe 2000

  • 1999

“Invocation for Eldorado”, "Stagione di caccia" Festival, Bologna "Fasting of the Heart", La Biennale di Venezia; Venice

  • 1998

“B.E.N.E. – NEW ENTRY”, Teatro di Casalecchio; Bologna (for Infrazioni Festival) “Do You Still Love Me?”multimedia performance for Confini alla Deriva Festival, Calderara “XXX Document N°2”; Bologna, (for Bologna Sogna Summer Festival)

  • 1997

“XXX Document N°1”; Bologna “Road to dedem”, Installations and art performances; Rome

  • 1996

“B.E.N.E. - or The Mirror of Difference”; Bologna (presented by festivals and venues in Rome, Venezia, Zagreb, Skopje, Alessandria, etc.) “SECURITY”, Installation; Alessandria
