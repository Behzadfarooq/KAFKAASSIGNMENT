I am an administrator, so if you have any questions or need any help, feel free to let me know on my talk page. I am primarily a janitor; I am on RC Patrol during much of my free time. However, I have also written and expanded several articles, and have an extensive to-do list. Feel free to send me an e-mail; please make sure the subject line is informative.

About me

I currently very happily live in Austin, Texas. I was born and raised in Brownsville, Texas, and am an alumnus of Saint Joseph Academy. I am a proficient computer user, advanced in PHP development and knowledgeable in Objective-C. My first edit to Wikipedia was on January 27, 2005, to the article concerning one of my favorite movies, Closer. I have been an administrator since February 2006.
