BBTS Siatkarz Original Bielsko-Biała

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

BBTS Siatkarz Original Bielsko-Biała is a Polish volleyball team, based in Bielsko-Biała, playing in Polish Volleyball League (Polska Liga Siatkówki, PLS).
