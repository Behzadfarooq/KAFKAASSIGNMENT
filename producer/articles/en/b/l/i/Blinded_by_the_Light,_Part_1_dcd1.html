Blinded by the Light, Part 1

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Blinded by the Light, Part 1 (Do Your Best! Ojama Trio, Part 1) is the 87th episode of Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Monsters GX (Yu-Gi-Oh! GX).

[edit] Synopsis

Chazz is dueling like a man possessed, claming many victories with a new White Knight deck given by Sartorius himself. Jaden intends to finally free Chazz, but to do it he'll help from Chazz's spirit partners the Ojama Brothers, who decide to join him and become Heroes. As they duel, Chazz's memories of his Ojamas, as well as his newly recovered black uniform, remind him of a time he can barely remember, before he joined Sartorius.

[edit] Trivia

The title of the English-language episode could be taken from a song by Simple Plan, "Untitled," where the fourth line reads "I'm blinded by the white light."
