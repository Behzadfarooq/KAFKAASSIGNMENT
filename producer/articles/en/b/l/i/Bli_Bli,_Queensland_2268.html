Bli Bli is a locality in the Sunshine Coast conurbation in Queensland, Australia. A few kilometres inland from the Maroochy area, Bli Bli is surrounded by wetland and cane.

Its name means "many swamp oak trees" in the indigenous language.

Bli Bli is notable for the castle that has been built as a tourist attraction in the village. It is not authentically mediaeval.
