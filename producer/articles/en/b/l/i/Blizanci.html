Blizanci (Serbian: Близанци) is a Serbian musical duo. Its members are composed of twins Predrag and Nenad Jovanović, thus hence the name Blizanci (English: Twins).

[edit] Biography

Predrag and Nenad Jovanović were born on 11 January 1977 in Stuttgart, Germany. They lived in Pribojska Banja in Serbia and finished 8 years of school there. They continued their education in Germany, where they studied music in the Conservatory for Accordions in Trossingen. In the last 5 years. they've been playing music in clubs throughout Belgrade, Serbia and in other countries.

So far, they took part in 4 festivals:

  • Beovizija 2006
  • Vrnjačka Banja 2006
  • Grand Festival 2006
  • Beovizija 2007
