Blind is an album by Corrosion of Conformity. It was released in 1991 on Relativity Records. This was the only COC album with Karl Agell and Phil Swisher. This is also their third album as Technocracy is an EP.

Blind was re-released on Columbia Records in 1995 with three additional songs.
