Blind Divine

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Blind Divine is an American band from Tucson, Arizona. Classified as ambient or trance, their style is a blended mix of genres, including hard rock, alternative rock, and others.

In addition to their own, self-released albums, their music has been licensed by VH-1 and MTV, as well as appearing on the Taking Lives DVD, and the soundtrack to the independent films Orphans and Angels and Dudevision.

[edit] Members

While a few band members have changed recently, the core members, artist Daniel Martin Diaz and his wife, singer Paula Catherine Valencia, have remained. Michael Glidewell, formerly of Black Sun Ensemble, plays guitar, with Jacob Stevens on bass guitar and Amril Fladoos on drums.

In February 2006, Daniel redesigned the stage at Historic Hotel Congress in downtown Tucson.
