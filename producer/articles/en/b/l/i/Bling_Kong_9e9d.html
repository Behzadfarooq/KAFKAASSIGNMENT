The band members of Bling Kong

Bling Kong are an indie rock band from New York City consisting of eleven members; Three drummers, two guitarists, one Bass guitar player, one videographer and four cheerleaders. The band are known for their sexual but rock-driven and humorous lyrics. Their music has been compared to Peaches, The Go-Go's, The B-52's and Judas Priest. They are also launching their own clothing line as well. In the fall of 2005, the band toured to promote their second album, Do The Awesome on the Redder Records label.
