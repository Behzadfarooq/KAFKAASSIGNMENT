Blind Date is a 1987 comedy/romance film directed by Blake Edwards and starring Kim Basinger and Bruce Willis. It was Willis' film debut - his character in the film was deliberately written so as to be different from the character he had played in the TV series Moonlighting.

[edit] Trivia

The art exhibition they went to see on their first date share styles with H.R. Giger.

Originally, the two leads in the film were to be the recently married Madonna and Sean Penn, but both backed out at the last minute.
