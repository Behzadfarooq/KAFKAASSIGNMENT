The "Blind Test" is a game where you have to guess the artist and song name of a music.

[edit] Origin

Its origin comes from the Challenge, a school competition between the École polytechnique fédérale de Lausanne and the Eidgenössische Technische Hochschule Zürich in Switzerland.

This game is always presented and directed by the CAC (Comité des Anciens Challengeurs, or Former Challenger's Committee) which participate every years to this event for supporting the new committee (which is renewed every year) and for keeping alive the Challenge spirit.

[edit] The Concept

The concept is very simple. Two groups plays one against the other and a disk-joker puts different songs one after the other. The group who discover first the artist and the song title get some points. If you discover the artist, it is two points. For the song you get only one point. Wins the group with more points at the end.
