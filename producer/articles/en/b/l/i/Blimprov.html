Blimprov is a new, local comedy improvisational comedy troupe located in Fresno, California.

[edit] History

Blimprov spawned from a collaboration of Acting Students at Fresno City College. From there they added new members from other schools such as Will Smith from Fresno State and Michael Flores from UC Berkeley. Intended to be a school club, lack of administrative support forced founders Magnus Chhan and Nick Haas to take their troupe elsewhere.

[edit] Notable Appearances

On February 1, 2007, Blimprov appeared on local CBS TV47's This Morning show. Also listed on Fresno Famous during the week of their first show.

[edit] Inaugural Show

Blimprov's First show was performed on February 2, 2007 at Sunnyside High School. Where they were able to book the theatre free of charge due to members of the team being still very involved with the school.
