Blida (Arabic: البليدة‎) is a province in Algeria. Its capital is Blida. Localities in Blida Province include Beni Mered, Boufarik, Bougara, Chréa, El Affroun, Hammam Melouane, Larbaa, Mouzaia and Ouled Yaich.
