In many campaign settings for the Dungeons & Dragons role-playing game, Blibdoolpoolp is the deity worshipped by the kuo-toa race. She is also known as the "Sea Mother."

Blibdoolpoolp usually takes the form of a 20-foot tall nude human female, with a lobster's head and claws in place of humanoid parts. At close range, her gaze causes insanity.

Blibdoolpoolp's realm is called "The Murky Depths," and is located on the Elemental Plane of Water.

[edit] Clergy and Temples

Priests of the Sea Mother are called "whips" and wear pearl-colored vestments, shell helms, and drape themselves in nets as part of ceremonies. Her holy day is the new moon, and rituals are held in underwater shrines. Gems, pearls, humans, and lobsters (her sacred animal) are sacrficed to her monthly.
