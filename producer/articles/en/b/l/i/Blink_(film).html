Blink (film)

Blink is a 1994 thriller, starring Madeleine Stowe and Aidan Quinn, and is a production of New Line Cinema. Director Michael Apted was nominated for a Crystal Globe award for the movie at the Karlovy Vary International Film Festival, and screenwriter Dana Stevens was nominated for Best Motion Picture at the Edgar Allan Poe Awards. Well-known character actor Laurie Metcalf also had a role in the movie. Chicago rock band The Drovers played a support role as themselves, contributing three songs to the soundtrack. Stowe's character, Emma, is a fiddler in the group. Some scenes were filmed in Chicago, Illinois, USA.
