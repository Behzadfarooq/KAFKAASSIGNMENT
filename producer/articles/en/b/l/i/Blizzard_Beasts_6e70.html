Blizzard Beasts is the fourth album by Norwegian black metal band, Immortal. It is the last Immortal album to feature Demonaz on guitar, and the first to feature Horgh on drums.

Stylistically, this is more death metal influenced than the group's previous works (some tracks sounding akin to old Morbid Angel mixed with Immortal's traditional sound), hence the fact that the song lengths rarely go over 3 minutes, only three songs going over 3 minutes, only one of them spanning past the 6:00 mark (Immortal being more known for their lengthy songs).
