Blitz is a professional Puerto Rican wrestler who competes in the International Wrestling Association.

[edit] Career

Blitz debuted in the Internation Wrestling Association in 2002.

With Stefano and Anarchy, Blitz formed the Misfits in early 2005.

Blitz is now in a tag team with Mr. Big, and together currently feud with Super Mark.
