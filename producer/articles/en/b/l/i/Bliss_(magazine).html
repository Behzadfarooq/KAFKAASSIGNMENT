Bliss (magazine)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

You have new messages (last change).

Bliss is a monthly British magazine aimed at teenage girls and published by EMAP. It is handbag sized and comes with a free gift each month. Bliss magazine is also sold internationally at newsagents although these are usually of the previous issue.
