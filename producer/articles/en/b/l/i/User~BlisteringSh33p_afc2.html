I'm known as BlisteringSh33p, BlisteringSheep or just plain Sh33p or Sheep. I don't purposefully go looking for things on Wikipedia to do; I do things as I run across them them. Minor editing, fact correcting and updates. I add new content to existing pages when I run across something that I think would be a worthwhile contribution. Usually what happens is I see something interesting and check Wikipedia to learn more about it. If it then appears that what drove me there would add value, I add it.
