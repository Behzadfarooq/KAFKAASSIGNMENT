Blink is an episode of the British science fiction television series Doctor Who. It will be broadcast on BBC One on 2 June 2007,[1] and is the tenth episode of Series 3 of the revived Doctor Who series. It will be a single episode story, written by Steven Moffat, who has already contributed three episodes to the revived series.

This episode will be the 2007 series' "Doctor-lite" episode, similar to 2006's Love & Monsters[2]. According to Russell T. Davies, this episode will also feature a monster, described as "enough to make you shiver".
