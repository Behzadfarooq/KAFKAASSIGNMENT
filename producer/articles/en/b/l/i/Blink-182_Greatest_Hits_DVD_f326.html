The Blink-182 Greatest Hits DVD was released by Blink-182 on 1 November 2005 at the same day as their 'Greatest Hits' album (the DVD is available as a bundle with the CD).

[edit] Track listing

  1. "Dammit (Growing Up)" (Directed by Darren Doane, Ken Daurio)
  2. "Josie" (Directed by Darren Doane, Ken Daurio)
  3. "What's My Age Again?" (Directed by Marcus Siega)
  4. "All The Small Things" (Directed by Marcus Siega)
  5. "Adam's Song" (Directed by Liz Friedlander)
  6. "Man Overboard" (Directed by Marcus Siega)
  7. "The Rock Show" (Directed by The Malloys)
  8. "Stay Together For The Kids" (Directed by Sam Bayer)
  9. "First Date" (Directed by The Malloys)
  10. "Feeling This" (Directed by David LaChapelle)
  11. "I Miss You" (Directed by Jonas Akerlund)
  12. "Down" (Directed by Estevan Oriol)
  13. "Always" (Directed by Joseph Kahn)

[edit] Miscellanea

  • The videos for "M&M's", "Anthem Pt. 2", "Violence", "Not Now" and "Another Girl Another Planet" were not included on this DVD.
  • Neither version of M&M's were included on the DVD.
  • Anthem Pt. 2 was not included as it was a live video, and never a proper single.
  • The second version of STFTK (Post 9-11 version) was not included
  • Neither version of Not Now was included as, the dvd was probably made just before the video was finished being edited.
  • The same goes for Another Girl, Another Planet, the video for this was made after this Greatest Hits DVD was made available.
