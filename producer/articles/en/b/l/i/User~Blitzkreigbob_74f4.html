[edit] Bio

Welcome to my user page. My name is Callan. I live with my family in Melbourne, Australia. I am currently in year 12, working (or slaving) towards an International Baccalaureate diploma as one of the few boys attending Tintern Girls Grammar School. IB is fucking stupid. My interests include music, anarchism, cooking, art and design. I live by a vegan diet, and I am opposed to the exploitation of both human and non-human animals. I hold Anarcho-Communist ideals, on both a personal level and a social level. I believe that authority and power structures should always be examined critically to determine whether they are legitimate. I am a firm believer in the theories of gift economics and mutual aid as described by anarchist Peter Kropotkin. I believe that the capitalist system is resposible for the majority of suffering and social problems in our world. I don't believe in nation states or international borders. I don't see that any significant social change can come about through voting, and thus, I disagree with party politics. I am an advocate of the practices of direct action and direct democracy as means to undermine corporate and government power structures. I play the drums in a Melbourne punk band, and I enjoy music of the punk, folk-punk, ska, psychobilly and hardcore genres. I have a lot of respect for DIY musicians. I dislike elitism, whether it be based on class, intellect, supposed race or penis size. I believe in universal social equality and freedom. In my spare time, I play lawnbowls, read, cook, make art, take photographs of streetart and spend time with my friends and family. Some of my favourite books include George Orwell's 1984 and Homage to Catalonia. I am inspired by the writings of post-left anarchist collective, Crimethinc. Some people whose works or actions inspire me include Noam Chomsky, Nestor Makhno, Peter Kropotkin, Emma Goldman, Peter Singer, Emiliano Zapata, Mahatma Gandhi and artist Banksy. Musically, I am inspired by Joey Ramone, Joe Strummer and Thomas Gabel. I love my friends and family. I love this planet.
