Blind (company)

Blind is a skateboarding company founded by Mark Gonzales. They sell decks, wheels and accessories as well as clothes from their own clothing line. The company's logo is a stylized grim reaper with text underneath that reads "Blind".

The company first came to prominence in 1991, when they released their first video, Video Days. The video is notorious for the first appearance of Jason Lee in the world of film. Another video, titled "What if?" was released in March 2005.

Blind's skate team lineup consists of Corey Sheppard, James Craig, Jake Ducombe, Josh Maxwell, Jake Brown and Ronnie Creager.

The name "Blind" is an intentional slight to Mark Gonzales' former employer "Vision". [1]
