Blitz 5 Days is a DVD box set by Dir en grey, released exclusively for members of the band's official fan club, A Knot. Each disc features footage from one of five live shows held at Akasaka Blitz. Each performance (except for the first) was themed after one of the band's full-length albums, including the then-to-be-released Vulgar. A few minutes of backstage footage for each day are included as well.
