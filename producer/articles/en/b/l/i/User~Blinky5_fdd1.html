Blinky5

Blinky5 (born January 22, 1983) (Real name: Nick Todd). Graduated from Jackson High School in Jackson, MO and from Southeast Missouri State University. He majored in Technical Graphics. He is currently employed at Jansco Outdoor in Cape Girardeau, MO as a graphic designer. He is currently married Lesley Parres.

He likes computers, gorey movies, and all kinds of music from rap,jazz,classical,rock, to soul. His favorite bands are Metallica,Tupac, and The Temptations. He likes to read the Zits and Calvin and Hobbes comics. His favorite TV shows are anything World Wrestling Entertainment (WWE), The Screen Savers, Star Trek, and That 70's Show.
