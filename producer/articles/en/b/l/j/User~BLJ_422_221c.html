[edit] Explanation of My Username

B stands for Benjamin, my first name. It is Hebrew. In the bible, Benjamin was Joseph's youngest brother. Those Israelis who descended from him are known as the Tribe of Benjamin, or the Benjaminites. The name means "Son of my right hand."
L stands for Lucas. Lucas means light, as do the names Lucius and Luka.
J stands for my last name, Jones. Jones is a popular family name that comes from the term 'Son of John' but can also be attributed to the name 'Jonah'.
422 is my birthdate, as in 4/22/1992, or April, 22nd, 1992.
