Blood Brothers (disambiguation)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Blood Brothers can mean:

  • Blood brothers, two or more unrelated men who become as close as real brothers.
  • Blood Brothers, a musical written by Willy Russell
  • Blood Brothers (album), a 2006 album by the hip hop duo Outerspace
  • Blood Brothers (documentary), a 1996 documentary about a Bruce Springsteen & the E Street Band reunion effort
  • Blood Brothers (EP), a 1996 EP by Bruce Springsteen & the E Street Band released with the documentary
  • Blood Brothers (novel), a 1976 novel by Richard Price
  • Blood Brothers, English language name of 2004 Hong Kong gang film
  • The Blood Brothers, a post-hardcore band.
  • Blood Brothers (The Outer Limits), 1995 episode of The Outer Limits television series
  • Blood Brothers Murder, a 1997 episode of the Diagnosis Murder television series
  • Blood Brothers Machine Company, a 20th century American company
  • A song from Iron Maiden's 2000 album Brave New World
  • A song from Manowar's 2007 album Gods of War
