Blossom Valley, San Jose, California

Blossom Valley is a neighborhood in the southern region of San Jose, California northeast of the Los Capitancillos Ridge that separates it from Almaden Valley. It is located east of Cambrian and northeast of Almaden neighborhoods. It is just northeast of the Los Capitancillos Ridge and the Almaden Valley. Blossom Valley is a terrific family oriented community surrounded by nature's jewels.

The area enjoys an abundance of recreational activities as well as the eclectic lifestyle of San Jose. A number of parks provide activities such as hiking, fishing and swimming.

Blossom Valley residents can enjoy plenty of restaurants and shops placed conveniently in the community and surrounding San Jose neighborhoods. For example, the Oakridge Mall brings the major department and specialty stores right to Blossom Valley.

Blossom Valley has historically enjoyed homeowners and residents that engage in community pride as is evidenced by the environment not only in and around the natural areas. But also the general appearance of the neighborhood is evidence in its pride of residency and ownership. [1]
