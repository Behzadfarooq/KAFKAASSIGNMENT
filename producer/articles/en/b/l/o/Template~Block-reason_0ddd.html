Template:Block-reason

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

You have new messages (last change).
You have been temporarily blocked from editing Wikipedia.  Your block will expire in {{{x}}}. If you wish to make useful contributions, you are welcome to come back after the block expires. ~~~~
This template employs intricate features of template syntax.
Please do not alter it unless you are certain to understand its setup and parser functions and are ready to repair/revert all collateral damage if results are unexpected.
Any experiments should be conducted and all improvements tested in the template sandbox, or in your user space, before applying anything here.

All parameters optional.

  • x: Length of the block. (ind for indefinitely, otherwise length for temporarily)
  • r: Reason for the block. (v for vandalism, vp for vandalism to p, pa for personal attacks, s for spam, 3 for 3RR on p, sock for using sockpuppets abusively, o for other)
    • o: Another reason for the block.
  • p: Page block is based on.
