Blowfly's Punk Rock Party is the 2006 album by Blowfly and his second release for Jello Biafra's label Alternative Tentacles. On this album numerous classics of the punk rock genre by artists such as The Ramones and The Dead Kennedys are given the trademark Blowfly sex parody treatment, as well as several originally non-punk rock songs parodied in punk style and several original songs. Six "clean" radio edits are also included.
