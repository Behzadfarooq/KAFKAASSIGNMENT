"What Ya Gonna Do Now?" and "Bloody Annoying" are songs by singer-songwriter Graham Coxon. The songs were released as a limited edition double A-sided single on 23 October 2006 in promotion of Coxon's October 2006 UK tour (see 2006 in British music).[1][2] The tracks were recorded in August 2006 by Coxon and his new band.[3] The single is also Coxon's second double A-side in a row, following "I Can't Look at Your Skin" / "What's He Got?", released in July 2006.
