Blooding Mask

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Blooding Mask is a ambient, classic, atmospherical project blessed by Maethelyiah. It started in 1992 when she used to live in Italy. Blooding Mask is a mixture of dreams into music and music into dreams and magic formulae.

[edit] Discography

  • All the colors of Death (album initially produced by MBR Germany and now republished by I.A.P.)
  • What that hollow shows through (1996) (initially published by Apollyon Antiphon Germany and now republished by I.A.P.)
  • The 9 pearls on the trail (published by I.A.P.)
  • The 5 Monsters (soon to be published by I.A.P.)
