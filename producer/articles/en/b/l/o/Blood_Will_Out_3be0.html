Blood Will Out is the 5th episode of the Season 6 of the classic television show Diagnosis: Murder. The episode was originally aired October 15, 1998.

[edit] Plot

A half-dead man with a bullet in his skull roams around Community General Hospital, beating up people. The hospital is confined to a full lockdown until the man is found.

Spoiler warning: Plot and/or ending details follow.

The man is Doctor Gregory Othon, who was developing the Black Plague for the U.S. Government. He tried to go public with it and met with a reporter in a restaurant, where the NSA agents, hired by the government tried to kill him. He is thought to be dead and is sent to the pathology lab of Community General. He remains until he can figure out the antibiotic for the Plague. Dr. Mark Sloan figures out Othon's plan, and tries to help. Unfortunately, Othon dies, but Mark injects himself with the Plague to keep it alive. He was given antibiotics, after managing to test positive that the disease is truly the Plague. It is destroyed after Mark went on the news and told the world what the government was doing.

Spoilers end here.
