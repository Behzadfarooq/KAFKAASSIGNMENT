Bloody Rox, 20, is a resident of Oakland, California and a student of City College of San Francisco. She plans to major in either English, Architecture or Geology, and be a part-time artist on the side. Interests include English and European literature, and art history. Her favorite authors include Milan Kundera, Dostoevsky, Virginia Woolf, T. S. Eliot, Oscar Wilde, and Edgar Allan Poe, among many others. She also has interests in the Modernist literary era and the German Expressionist art of the 1920s.

Her Wikipedia-related activities include editing typos, fixing grammar mistakes and poorly written sentences, and adding technical information to literature stubs about books she has read.
