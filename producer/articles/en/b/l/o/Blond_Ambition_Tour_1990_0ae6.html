Blond Ambition Tour 1990 was a Germany-only 'official' DVD that was released in 2005 by Falcon Neue Medien. It includes a date from the Houston, Texas show of Madonna's landmark 1990 Blond Ambition Tour. This is seen by some as a poor quality release due to bad sound and colour. Warner Music is still to release an international official release of this tour.
