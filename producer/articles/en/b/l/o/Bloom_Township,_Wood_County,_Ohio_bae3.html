Location of Bloom Twp, Wood Co, Ohio.

Bloom Township is one of the 19 townships that make up Wood County, Ohio. It is located at the southern end of the county, forming part of the border with Hancock County. Bloom Township shares boundaries with the following townships and municipalities:

  • Allen Township, Hancock Co. (southwest)
  • Cass Township, Hancock Co. (south)
  • Henry Township (west)
  • Liberty Township (northwest corner)
  • Montgomery Township (northeast corner)
  • Perry Township (east)
  • Portage Township (north)
  • The village of Bairdstown
  • The village of Bloomdale
  • The village of Cygnet
  • The village of Jerry City

The Bloom Township House is located at the intersection of Huffman Rd. and Oil Center Rd.
