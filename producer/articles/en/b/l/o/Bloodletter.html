Bloodletter is a Star Trek: Deep Space Nine novel written by K. W. Jeter.

Spoiler warning: Plot and/or ending details follow.

Sabotage of an away mission by a Bajoran fanatic leaves Bashir trapped in the wormhole. Kira, in the Gamma Quadrant, must contend with his intention to assassinate her, without support.
