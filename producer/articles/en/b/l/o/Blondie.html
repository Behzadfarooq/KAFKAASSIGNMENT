Blondie or Blondi may mean

  • Blondie (band), a 1970s/1980s era pop music band based in New York City.

    • Blondie (album), the self-titled debut album from Blondie.
  • Alpha Blondy, a famous reggae singer.
  • Blondie (comic strip), a long-running newspaper comic strip
    • Blondie (film), a 1938 movie based on the comic strip
    • Blondie (radio), a radio comedy series based on the comic strip
  • One of the minor characters in Bloom County, a comic strip
  • Blondi, Adolf Hitler's German Shepherd dog
  • In The Good, the Bad and the Ugly, Blondie is the nickname of Clint Eastwood's character
  • Blondi (fictional), an intelligent German Shepherd dog on Kingdom Hospital.
  • Chocolate brownie, a type of sweet snack, when made using white chocolate instead of cocoa, is called a blondie.
