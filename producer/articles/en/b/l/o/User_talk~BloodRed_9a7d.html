"I just simply think that everything that exists should have a Wiki." Standards such as WP:WEB and WP:CORP exist to ensure that Wikipedia is not swamped by all manner of articles pertaining to companies and websites, notable or otherwise. A bar has to be set at some level and those entities that pass beneath said bar don't get article space on WP. If you are the holder of the copyright of the text on the website that was cut-and-pasted in to this article, then you have the right to dispose of your intellectual property as you wish. That said, a textdump on to WP, ignoring the standards and guidelines set out above it not the best way of going about it. Regards,  (aeropagitica)   (talk)  22:45, 29 August 2006 (UTC)
