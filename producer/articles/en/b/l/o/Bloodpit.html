Bloodpit

Bloodpit is a finnish rockband. Before 1998 the band was named "RIP and "Turmio". It was found in 1994 by Matthau Mikojan, who is the lead singer and Alarick, the bass player. They released their first album in 2005 ("Mental circus"). They have released many singles like "Platitude" (2005) and "Bad ass blues" (2005), the last one "Wise men don't cry" will be released in March or April 2007.

The single "Platitude" is at the EA Sports game NHL 07 soundtrack.
