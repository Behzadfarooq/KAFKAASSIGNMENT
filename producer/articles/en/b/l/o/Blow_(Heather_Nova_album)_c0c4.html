Blow is a live album by indie rocker Heather Nova, released in 1993 (see 1993 in music).

[edit] Track listing

All songs written by Heather Nova.

  1. "Light Years" – 5:24
  2. "Sugar" – 6:48
  3. "Maybe an Angel" – 6:31
  4. "Blessed" – 2:37
  5. "Mother Tongue" – 3:21
  6. "Talking to Strangers" – 5:05
  7. "Shaking the Doll" – 4:16
  8. "Frontier" – 5:17
  9. "Doubled Up" – 3:40
