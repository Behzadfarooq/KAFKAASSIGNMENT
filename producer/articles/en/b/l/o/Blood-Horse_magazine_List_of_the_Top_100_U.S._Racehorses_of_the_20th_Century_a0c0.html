Blood-Horse magazine List of the Top 100 U.S. Racehorses of the 20th Century

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

In 1999, a panel for The Blood-Horse magazine made up of distinguised horse racing people Howard Battle, Lenny Hale, Jay Hovdey, William Nack, Pete Pedersen, Jennie Rees and Tommy Trotter compiled a list of what they considered to be the top 100 racehorses of the 20th Century who had competed in the United States.

The magazine's list was published by Eclipse Press (ISBN 1-58150-024-6) in book form with complete biographies under the title Thoroughbred Champions: Top 100 Racehorses of the 20th Century.
