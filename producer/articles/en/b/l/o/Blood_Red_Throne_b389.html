Blood Red Throne is a five piece Death metal band from Kristiansand, Norway. They released their third full-length album, Altered Genesis, in January, 2005. The band features Tchort of Emperor/Green Carnation/Carpathian Forest fame as a guitarist.

[edit] Studio Releases

  • Monument of Death (2001)
  • A Taste for Blood (EP) (2002)
  • Affiliated with the Suffering (2003)
  • Altered Genesis (2005)

[edit] Demos/Splits

  • DeathMix 2000 (2000)
  • A Taste for Butchery (split with Severe Torture) (2003))
