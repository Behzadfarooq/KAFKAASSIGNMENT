[edit] Biography

[edit] College

The Breadtruck went to college.

[edit] Draft

The Breadtruck was drafted in the 3rd round by The Lil Wilts.

[edit] professional career

After being drafted by the Lil Wilts in 2003, The Breadtruck went on to play for the Rosy Palms, Uniclaytons, Hot Arevelos, Bird Flu Awareness, Odd Days, We Love LA, Huey Lewis and the Jews, and The Charles Bronson Make a Deathwish Foundation.

[edit] Legacy

The Breadtruck has played on the winning team in the following tournaments: A-Team Classic, Three Amigo, The First Imaginary Invitational, 2005 Virginia NDL Championship, 2006 NDL Orange County Championship, a bunch of LADS related tourneys and also this one charity tournament from Antelope Valley.
