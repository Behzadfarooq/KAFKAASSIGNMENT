Blocks Club with Bumpy Trot (, Blokus Kurabu with Bumpy Trot?) is a PlayStation 2 puzzle game by Irem Software Engineering. It is based on the board game of the same name (which in turn is based on the game Blokus) and features characters from Steambot Chronicles (known in Japan as Bumpy Trot). Like in Steambot Chronicles, players are able to customize the appearance of their characters.

[edit] Gameplay

Each player is given a pile of Tetris like blocks. Players must place blocks on the board starting at the corners and then extend it from the corners of the pieces they have placed. The game is over when no one can place any more pieces. The player with the fewest pieces remaining wins.
