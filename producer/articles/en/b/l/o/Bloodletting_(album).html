The third album from alternative rock band Concrete Blonde, shifting more towards gothic rock. R.E.M.'s Peter Buck and Wall of Voodoo's Andy Prieboy make guest appearances[citation needed].

  4. "The Darkening of the Light" (featuring Peter Buck)
  5. "I Don't Need a Hero"
  6. "Days and Days"
  7. "The Beast"
  8. "Lullabye"
  9. "Joey"
  10. "Tomorrow Wendy" (featuring Andy Prieboy)
