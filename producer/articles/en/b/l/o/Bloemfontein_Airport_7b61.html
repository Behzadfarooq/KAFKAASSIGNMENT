Bloemfontein Airport (IATA: BFN, ICAO: FABL) is located in Bloemfontein, South Africa.

In 2004, the airport served 225,636 passengers (+4.7% vs. '03).

[edit] Scheduled Services

  • South African Airways (Cape Town, Durban, Johannesburg, Port Elizabeth)
