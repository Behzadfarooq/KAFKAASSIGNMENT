[edit] Plot

A tragic killing of the leader of a gang (Black and Whites) leads police to believe Shane Marshell is guilty. The Marshell family just happens to be friends with the Sloanes.

[edit] Cast

  • This episode stars four Van Dykes. Dick Van Dyke as Dr. Mark Sloan, his son, Barry Van Dyke as Steve Sloan and his two sons, Carey and Shane.
  • It also stars Victoria Rowell as Dr. Amanda Bentley and Charlie Schlatter as Dr. Jesse Travis.
