Block Music is the second album from the Chicago native Shawnna. The first single was "Gettin' Some" and the second single will be "Damn" featuring Smoke of Field Mob. The album has an exclusive remix of "Gettin' Some" featuring Ludacris, Lil' Wayne, Pharrell and Too $hort. The album has so far sold over 150,000 copies in the U.S, and over 200,000 copies worldwide.
