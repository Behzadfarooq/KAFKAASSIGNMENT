Blow Pops

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Blow Pops are lollipops, originally produced by The Charms Company, which was acquired by Tootsie Roll Industries in 1988. They combine a flavored hard candy shell with a large bubble gum center. They come in many different flavors, including sour flavors.

They once had commercials with kids saying, "Pop, that's a blow pop. Say from Charms!!!"

They have many flavors, such as cherry, green apple and blue razzberry.
