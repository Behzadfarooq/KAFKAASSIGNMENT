Bloquera is an E.P. created by Maanumental, Newman, Sir Kado, Superbrush 427 (Giant Panda) and Thes One (People Under the Stairs). The group decided to take a spur of the moment road trip to Mexico as an ultimate road trip. The album was a result of a two week road trip to Mulegé, Baja California Sur, Mexico which was the inspiration for the album.

During their stay in Mulegé the group camped on the beach facing the Sea of Cortez where they fished for their meals, visited local taco stands, purchased produce from local farmers, and attended local baseball. They also filmed their trip using two Super 8 mm film cameras to document the trip. The video will be released as a bonus DVD on People Under the Stairs next album The Stepfather.

Tres Records released the album as a 12" LP in limited quanties and is now out of print. The album is still available for purchase on the iTunes Music Store.

Each member of the group also took on a unique alias for the album which is listed below:

  • Maanumental as Maanu MyGoose
  • Newman as Sandy Sand Dollar
  • Sir Kado as The Sea Troll
  • Superbrush 427 as Big Sun
  • Thes one as The Sassy Warlock
