Blood, Sweat & No Tears is the third and final album from Hip Hop group Stetsasonic. Producer Prince Paul, acclaimed for his work with De La Soul, continued a successful production career. Paul and Frukwan became members of the popular Horrorcore group Gravediggaz, along with Poetic and RZA. Daddy-O also went on producing for popular artists in the 90's.
