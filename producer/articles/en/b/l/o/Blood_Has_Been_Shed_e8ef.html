Blood Has Been Shed is a tech metalcore group from Connecticut. They are known for having two members of Killswitch Engage (Howard Jones and Justin Foley) in the band, though Blood Has Been Shed's style is more extreme. Though Jones and Foley are currently performing with Killswitch Engage, they don't intend to end Blood Has Been Shed. The band is currently in the process of recording a new album tentatively to be released sometime in 2007.
