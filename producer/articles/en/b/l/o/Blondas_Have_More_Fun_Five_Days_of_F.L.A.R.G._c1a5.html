Blondas Have More Fun/Five Days of F.L.A.R.G.

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

"Blondas Have More Fun/Five Days of F.L.A.R.G." is episode #5.6 of the Nickelodeon animated television series The Fairly OddParents. It aired on April 2, 2005.

[edit] Plot

Spoiler warning: Plot and/or ending details follow.

[edit] "Blondas Have More Fun"

Wanda and her famous sister Blonda secretly switch places for a day; Timmy does dangerous stunts while Wanda is away.

Note: Julia Louis-Dreyfus guest stars as Blonda (uncredited)[citation needed].

[edit] "Five Days of F.L.A.R.G."

Mark Chang is missing F.L.A.R.G. (a Yugopotamian celebration which is strikingly similar to Spring break), so his appendix is going to blow up on Earth. So, Timmy wishes he could celebrate a genuine F.L.A.R.G. on Earth, only on the last day Mark has to destroy the planet. He eventually decides not to; he instead destroys Fairy World.

F.L.A.R.G. stands for:

Fart
Lie
Amnesia
Ranksgiving
Good-bye

[edit] Trivia

  • The girl Carly in this episode is named after Butch Hartman's first daughter. This is Wanda's 2nd Zappy because she won one in The Zappys short.
  • In the episode Blondas Have More Fun Jorgan said that people have been waiting 18 seasons for her to kiss Dr. Poofeverwish and Blonda and the director said people have been waiting 18 years.
