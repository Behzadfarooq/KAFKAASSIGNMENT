Blossomon is a fictional character from the Digimon franchise, a Vegetation Digimon. Her name is derived from the flower, Blossom.

[edit] Description

Blossomon's head and the flowers on her tendrils are shaped like sunflowers.

[edit] Digivolution

Blossomon's Digivolution line in Digimon Adventure 02: D1 Tamers1:

  • Rookie (Child) - Floramon
  • Champion (Adult) - Ninjamon
  • Ultimate (Perfect) - Blossomon
1 Blossomon was a boss in the game, after taking a certain amount of damage it de-digivolves into Ninjamon and then into Floramon.

[edit] Special Digivolutions

  • DNA Digivolution (Mega) - Blossomon + Datamon = Eaglemon
  • DNA Digivolution (Mega) - Blossomon + Deramon = Gryphonmon

[edit] Abilities

The flower heads on Blossomon's tendrils are alive. It can also use its vines to wrap around anything. The petals around Blossomon's head can rotate for flight.

[edit] Attacks

  • Spiral Flower::The biting flowers on the tips of her tendrils are thrown out at its opponent as if they were shurikens.
  • Polinosis Shower:Throws poisonous pollen at the enemy.
  • Thorn Whip:A weaker version of Rosemon's attack.
  • Flower Poison Bite:Bites his opponents with the biting flowers of her tendrils.
  • Poison Dream:Realeses a poisonous gas upon enemy.

[edit] Appearances

Spoiler warning: Plot and/or ending details follow.

[edit] Digimon Adventure

  • Voiced by Melodee Spevack (US) & Araki Kae (Japan).

Blossomon was one of Puppetmon's playmates. She was seen briefly when T.K. was fleeing from Puppetmon, and hid in the room where Mushroomon and Blossomon were. He asked them not to say anything, but Blossomon ratted him out. When Puppetmon did not find T.K. where he was hiding, Puppetmon thought the two lied and killed them (that scene was edited out in the dub).

[edit] Digimon Adventure 02

  • Melodee Spevack reprises her role of Blossomon.

When Yolei, Kari and Ken were trapped within the Dark World, they were attacked by a large Blossomon created from some Control Spires by Arukenimon. She overpowered Aquilamon and Stingmon with her flower-headed tendrils. However, Blossomon and her flower-headed tendrils were destroyed when Aquilamon and Gatomon DNA Digivolved to Silphymon for the first time.

[edit] Digimon Savers

Gotsumon sent a large Blossomon to prevent Masaru, Agumon, Tohma, Gaomon, and Falcomon from obtaining a virus cure from Wanderer's Cape. Blossomon attempted to stop them before she ended up fighting RiseGreymon and MachGaogamon over a body of water. She ended up being destroyed by RiseGreymon.

[edit] Digital Monster X-Evolution

She was one of the Digimon that supported Yggdrasil and the original caretaker of Tokomon. Her reason for hating X-Digimon was from the fact that their fights with the Royal Knights put their children in danger. She was present at a meeting called by WarGreymon X to discuss what to do about Yggdrasil. When Omnimon dispersed the meeting, he killed her.

[edit] Digimon World 2

Blossomon digivolves from Togemon, and can digivolve further to Rosemon.

[edit] Digimon World 4

Blossomon is the first boss that the player will encounter in Death Valley.

[edit] Digimon World DS

Blossomon digivolves from Vegiemon.
