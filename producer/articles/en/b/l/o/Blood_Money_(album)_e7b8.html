Blood Money is an album by Tom Waits, released in 2002 on the Anti sub-label of Epitaph Records.

The album contains songs written for the play Woyzeck, based on the play Woyzeck by Georg Büchner. The theatrical adaptation was directed by Robert Wilson, with whom Waits had worked on two previous plays: The Black Rider and Alice, both of which resulted in soundtrack albums. The latter was released simultaneously in 2002 with Blood Money.

The play premiered at the Betty Nansen Theatre in Copenhagen, November 2000.

This album ranked at #18 in Metacritics Top 30 albums of 2002[1].
