Blood Alone is a manga by Masayuki Takano, illustrator for Boogiepop Dual. The story centers on two characters: Kuroe and Misaki. Kuroe was a writer and a private detective; Misaki was a mysterious girl who lived with Kuroe. The story revealed that Misaki was a vampire in the first chapter, and it kept unfolding the background of the characters with the story proceeding. The theme involves with crime fiction, fantasy and a "strange love story"(as mentioned on the cover of Drama CD) between Kuroe and Misaki. This manga was originally published as a dōjinshi.
