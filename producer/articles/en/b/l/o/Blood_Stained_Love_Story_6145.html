Blood Stained Love Story is an album by hard rock band Saliva which was released on January 23, 2007. This is their first album with new guitarist Jonathan Montoya after the departure of Chris Dâ€™abaldo. The entire album, including bonus tracks, was leaked onto filesharing networks on Sunday, January 21, 2007.

The album debuted on the Billboard 200 at number 19, selling 31,000 copies in its first week.[1]
