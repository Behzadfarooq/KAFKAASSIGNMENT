Blood (journal)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

You have new messages (last change).

Blood is a bimonthly medical journal published by the American Society of Hematology. It was founded by Dr William Damashek in 1946. The journal publishes clinical and basic scientific research in all areas of hematology.
