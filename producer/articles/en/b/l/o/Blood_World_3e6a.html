Blood World is the sixth release and fifth studio album of Christian metal band Mortification, released in 1994. The album focuses more on thrash metal amd contains less death metal influences then past albums. Instead of the Steve Rowe's growls, the album mainly contains his shouting style singing, but the growls do make occasional appearances.
