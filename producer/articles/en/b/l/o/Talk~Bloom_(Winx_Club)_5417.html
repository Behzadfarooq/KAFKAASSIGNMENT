[edit] October 2006 Biography discussion

Now we begin another area of contention: rewriting the winx club character biographies. I initially started rewriting blooms article with the intention that it should be written in a biographical style detailing events from the entire series and that its layout be applied to all enteries. Ultimately, the entire project just became overwhelming trying compress 40+ episodes into one single article (and probably be best left to a season synopsis). With that in mind, I would like to begin the discussion about "How should we write the articles for the winx club characters?"

  • Template Layout
When I created the template, I had in mind that only information from the winxclub.com be present to avoid a cluttered appearance and the discrepancies from other versions be list in a section in the article. However, it would be a shame to undo all the contributions from Malkinann and would like recommend that if discrepancies are added to the template they should be listed like Bold italian version/plain 4Kids version.
Another thing I would like to bring up the "gender" designation in the template: should we do away with it? Considering that I seriously doubt anyone else (from the minor character list) will ever have their own article, I have been considering changing "Gender" to "associated pixie" or something to the effect.
  • Article layout
As I have mentioned earlier, a biography from events in the series is an ambitious project and one that only the most determined should undertake. With the intention of avoiding spoilers, how about something like this:
Personal life
Magical abilities
4Kids modifications

I believe that those three sections can describe all the characters suffienctly.. If anyone has better ideas, I would like to see them.--Kenn Caesius 17:45, 15 October 2006 (UTC)

Whoops, I didn't see this when you first made it.
Adding "Associated Pixie" sounds like a good idea. The devil in me suggests perhaps adding "Token Handbag Boyfriend" as well...  ;)
Firstly: I'd suggest changing "Age" on the template to something like "Age at first appearance" - it's confusing to have their 'current' ages. On Ami Mizuno and other SM pages, we've just got an age range.
If we were to adapt the SM format to the Winx Club, you'd have something like:
  • Name + first appearance
  • Personality profile
  • Appearance/forms (civvie outfits, Winx costumes and Enchantrix descriptions go here)
  • Statistics (? - perhaps this could go in the Winx Club character template, if anywhere)
  • Magical abilities
  • 4kids changes
  • Voice actresses
But then that'd leave out the Friends/Foes section (which currently is pretty woeful for most of the characters). Maybe have a "relationships" section as well? -Malkinann 08:51, 12 November 2006 (UTC)
