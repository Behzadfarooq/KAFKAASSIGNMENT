Blockhead (disambiguation)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Wiktionary, the free dictionary.

In common use, blockhead usually refers to a stupid or stubborn person. (see Wiktionary entry for more information)

Blockhead may also refer to:

  • Blockhead, a title adopted by fans of Stephanie J. Block, a Broadway musical theatre actress
  • Blockhead, a theoretical computer system devised by Ned Block
  • Blockhead!, a block stacking game by Pressman Toy Corp.
  • Ian Dury and the Blockheads, a rock band, or the song "Blockheads" from the band's New Boots and Panties album
  • Blockhead (music), a hip-hop producer
  • Blockheads, a grindcore band from Nancy/France
  • Block-heads (Gumby), a pair of antagonists from the Gumby television series
  • Block-Heads, a 1938 film starring Laurel and Hardy
  • Human blockheads, circus sideshow performers who perform apparently dangerous acts: for example, driving nails into their noses
  • "Blockhead", a song by Devo from their album Duty Now for the Future
