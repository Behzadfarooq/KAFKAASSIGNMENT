Bloodlust (Beatta Dubiel) is a fictional mutant character in the Marvel Comics Universe.

[edit] Ficitional character biography

Bloodlust is one of the Femme Fatales, a group of female mercenaries hired to battle Spider-Man. She was a mutant with a bestial form, complete with ferocious fangs and razor sharp claws. After Decimation, Bloodlust was depowered.

[edit] Powers and abilities

Bloodlust had enhanced speed and agility as well as razor sharp claws.
