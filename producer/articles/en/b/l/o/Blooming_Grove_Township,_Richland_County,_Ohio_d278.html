Location of Blooming Grove Twp, Richland Co, Ohio.

Blooming Grove Township is one of 18 townships in Richland County, Ohio. It is part of the Mansfield, Ohio Metropolitan Statistical Area.

It borders the following Richland County townships:

  • Cass (west)
  • Butler (east)
  • Weller (southeast tip)
  • Franklin (south)
  • Jackson (southwest tip)
