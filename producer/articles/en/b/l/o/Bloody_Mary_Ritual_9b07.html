Bloody Mary Ritual is ritual where you can summon a vengeful female spirit named Bloody Mary.

[edit] The Urban Legend

According to the urban legend, You perform this in a room with a mirror and turn all the lights off (this works well in a bathroom) and usually involves no windows. You begin, in a whisper, to chant "Bloody Mary, Bloody Mary, Bloody Mary...", as you continue to chant your voice should grow louder and louder into a near scream. You should repeat the words "Bloody Mary" 13 times. While you are chanting you should be spinning around at a medium rate and taking a glimpse in the mirror at each pass. This is but one way to summon this spirit. There is a variation where instead of thirteen times you spin 3 times. What some people see in the mirror can also be different from what you see. This causes some debate on whether you actually see anything in the mirror or not. But most people still try it. Some guess some people just try it for the fun of it. Ultimately, it's just a scary mirror ritual that kids do at slumber parties to scare each other.
