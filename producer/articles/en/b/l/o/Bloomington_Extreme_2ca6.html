The Bloomington Extreme are a professional indoor football team. They joined the United Indoor Football league in 2006 as an expansion team. The team's name is derived from a naming rights deal with local auto dealer Extreme Motors.

The Extreme play at the U.S. Cellular Coliseum in Bloomington, Illinois.
