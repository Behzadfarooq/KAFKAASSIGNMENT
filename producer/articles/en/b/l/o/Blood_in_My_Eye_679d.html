Blood in My Eye is an album by rapper Ja Rule released in 2003. It is his 5th album. It is made up of mostly songs aimed toward dissing rival rappers including 50 Cent. It was done during the feud with 50 Cent. The album was named after George Jackson's radical book of the same name. The album sold 140,000 units in its first week in The U.S.

Hussein Fatal, Cadillac Tah, James Gotti, Sizzla, Black Child, Young Merc & D.O, Shadow, and Sekou 720 are featured on this album.
