Blood Sisters is a critically acclaimed 2005 book by Barbara and Stephanie Keating. The book concerns the life stories of three girls: the Irish Sarah Mackay, an Afrikaner Hanna Van der Beer and British Camilla Broughton Smith. The book follows their journey from being brought up in Kenya, until their lives divulge and their hopes and dreams are destroyed, and their bond almost with it.

The Blood Sisters are also a Reggae band, whose Ring My Bell is featured on the Reggae radio station K-JAH Radio West in popular videogame Grand Theft Auto San Andreas.
