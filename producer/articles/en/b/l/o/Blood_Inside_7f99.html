Blood Inside is an album by the Norwegian experimental band Ulver. It is the band's sixth full-length release.

[edit] Track listing

  1. "Dressed in Black" – 7:06
  2. "For the Love of God" – 4:11
  3. "Christmas" – 6:15
  4. "Blinded by Blood" – 6:22
  5. "It Is Not Sound" – 4:37
  6. "The Truth" – 4:01
  7. "In the Red" – 3:30
  8. "Your Call" – 6:07
  9. "Operator" – 6:08

[edit] Miscellanea

It Is Not Sound is heavily based on Toccata and Fugue in D Minor by Johann Sebastian Bach. The prominent melody played with keyboards can easily be found in Bach's work, but there are other extracts in the background also.

Blinded by Blood is devoted to Kristoffer Rygg's newborn son, Leon.

Blood Inside was mixed by the band and Ronan Chris Murphy
