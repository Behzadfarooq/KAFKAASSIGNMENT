Bloodties (comics)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Bloodties, a 1993 Avengers/X-Men crossover and a direct sequel to the X-Men Fatal Attractions detailing the fallout from the infamous mindwipe of Magneto and his ultimate defeat. The story is about Exodus and his attempt to kidnap Luna, the daughter of Crystal and Quicksilver and therefore, the granddaughter and heir of Magneto. This storyline was collected as a trade paperback (ISBN 0-7851-0103-9).
