I don't necessarily agree with deleting this page. If it is proposed to be removed, why not Pokey, Lakitu or Thwomp? These characters are just as minor as Blooper, if not more. Seeing as Blooper will be playable soon, he deserves more than just a paragraph in Mario series enemies. So, I'm sure we can come to some kind of agreement. Why not merge Blooper (Mario) with Gooper Blooper? Hardcore gamer 48 02:59, 24 March 2007 (UTC)
