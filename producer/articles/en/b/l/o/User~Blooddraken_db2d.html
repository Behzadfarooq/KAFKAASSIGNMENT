[edit] About Me

Well Hello everyone. This is my first complete page creation so forgive any errors. And any suggestions would be appreciated.

I suppose a little about me would be appropriate.


First off, I am an eclectic studier. I study anything and everything that catches my eye. Whether that is my current hobby of studying Scientology, which I admit I believe is a harmful cult that needs to be shut down before others get seriously hurt or quantum mechanics, or comic books, or sports (which I normally despise but then, I thank people who call me a geek hehe), various religions, or anything else.

I don't have any college experience outside of the college I went to to get my GED, but I am not bragging when I say I have the intelligence and knowledge to put alot of college professors to shame.

My hobbies include reading, writing stories, role-playing games, hunting, video games, computers, biking and swimming. I also collect bladed weapons and am working on getting a permit to get a historic gun collection.

Religiously, I identify myself as a dystheist and politically I identify myself as a conservative Republican.

Most importantly though, the one thing no-one must ever forget about me, is that I place honor above everything. My honor comes before my religion. it comes before my political beliefs. It comes before even my family and other loved ones. Without honor, one has no life.

At the moment, I am working on getting my commercial pilot's license so I can open a tour/sky diving/passenger/crop dusting business (( gotta take what I can get to pay the bills)).

I currently operate three rpg forums. They are at http://forums.delphiforums.com/Blooddraken/start http://forums.delphiforums.com/WoD2005/start http://forums.delphiforums.com/JusticeLeague1/start

I also have a blog at http://dystheist.blogspot.com/ . It's been dead lately, but that's only because I'm pretty much given up on it as no-one has commented on it.

I also support and promote this website: http://www.petakillsanimals.com
