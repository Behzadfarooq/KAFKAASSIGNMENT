Bloomington High School is a small high school in unincorporated Bloomington, California near Riverside, Rialto, San Bernadino, and Fontana. It has expanded over the years for more businesses and stores. It belongs to the Colton Joint Unified School District and is run by Principal Ignacio Cabrera. It's clubs are very diverse with many many options to choose. A new football field was purchased in recent years around 2003-4. The school is currently being remodeled and is under construction. It has a majority population of hispanic students. It's JROTC program is of the Navy branch.

It is a very small school compared to others, but it is of the right size to make the best out of the high school experience. It allows more teacher-student interaction, which is fantastic when you are trying to make the right grades for college.
