[edit] Summary

Blooming Impex first started functioning in 1995 as a Textile company. The majority of production was for export purposes. Soon it became a good name in Textiles. Ever since Blooming Impex started providing its services in healthcare in the Year 1999, it started developing itself for providing best services. Challenge was to become complete health Care partner for Hospitals and Labs. In February 2000, Blooming Impex extended its services in the field of Lab Diagnostics and emerged as your present trusted partner in Health-Care services. Blooming Impex has been listed Twice for its achievements in Times daily Magazines. Blooming Impex believe in quality of Service and Trust of its customer is at par most important for them. With the new developments in the price sensitive Indian Market, BI tries to provide its customers with best quality at good price. Based in New Delhi, Blooming Impex has network of Dealers and Distributors in all important Cities of India to support all your Health Care needs. [[['''www.bloomingimpex.com''']]]

Blooming Impex has a wide country wide network of distributors/regional offices located in all major cities to reach out to its customers in all corners of the country. BI believes that the success of its services team lies in the fact how fast they can reach to the remotest of city/town/village.

Located in New Delhi, India blooming Impex has a hierarchical modus apprentice to reach customers which includes five major representatives in North-, South-, West-, Central-, and East India, followed by state wise distributors. Together it makes an organization to serve you best.

Blooming Impex also is known for its services as providers of online help to Health care system in India & US. This is achieved by them through a number of online hosts, providing online information-statistics and new developments in medical sciences to doctors; as well sources for differential diagnosis.

Blooming Impex came to limelight with their strategical failure to market chemicals from one of the leading international chemical company (Unknown). though made a fast come back soon with another hit i.e. Analyticon, AG., Germany.
