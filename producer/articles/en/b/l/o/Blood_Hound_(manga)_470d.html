Blood Hound (夜型愛人専門店−ブラッドハウンド−DX, Yorugata Aijin Senmonten - Blood Hound - DX?) is a manga by mangaka Kaori Yuki. It is the story of naive, loudmouthed teenager Rion, who investigates a host club full of vampire hosts when she suspects they must be related to the disappearance of a lot of people in the neighborhood, especially her best friend. In the process she begins to befriend them, especially the leader Suou, who she develops a romantic relationship with. Ultimately, she figures out the vampires are not the cause of the disappearance, but her adventures with the vampires are only beginning.

There have been four chapters released so far in the Blood Hound series.

[edit] Characters

  • Rion – A teenager, who initially suspects that the Host Club Krankenhaus is behind the disappearance of several people in her community, including Rion's best friend. In the end, she figures out that the vampires are not responsible for the disappearance, but her school guidance councilor, who is a haemophiliac.
  • Suoh – The lead host of Krankenhaus, he begins to develop a romantic relationship with Rion. He also suspects that she's the reincarnation of Ellone "the one with the purest blood" whom he once loved.
