Bloody Roar 2, is a brawler video game developed by Eighting/Raizing in 1999. (Also known as Bloody Roar 2: Bringer of the New Age in Japan/Europe and Bloody Roar 2: The New Breed in the United States.)

[edit] New Features

  • Seven New Characters
  • Addition of the Beast Drive. (A devastating special attack)
  • All new Story Mode
  • All new cheats including Big Head mode.
  • 90+ drawings in game.

[edit] Playable Characters

Gado the Lion (Unlockable)
Shenlong the Tiger (Unlockable)

[edit] Trivia

  • All the character's secret start outfits are just colour swaps, beside from Uriko's, which is a completely new outfit - a white sleeveless jacket and shorts combo with a blue vest, gloves and white sneakers.
  • This was the first Bloody Roar game to be dubbed into English for its Western release.
  • Shina's name was originally Marvel in the Japanese release.
