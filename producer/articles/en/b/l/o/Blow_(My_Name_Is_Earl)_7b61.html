[edit] Synopsis

Earl takes on number 101 on his list, "Stole a girl's identity." The girl turns out to be Joy's half-sister that she never knew about and the one person that could possibly help her get sympathy from the jury. The problem, Joy and Liberty are enemies.

[edit] Trivia

  • We find out how Joy had her children. The reason why Dodge was born was because of a broken condom and Earl Jr. was born because Darnell had a cramp in his leg prohibiting him from making a "timely exit" while they were standing up (the latter would have been at the rock where Joy had later buried the silverware).
  • The episode title is the acronym for this series' fictional women's wrestling federation, Black Ladies of Wrestling. It is a reference to a real women's wrestling federation from the past - Gorgeous Ladies of Wrestling aka GLOW.
