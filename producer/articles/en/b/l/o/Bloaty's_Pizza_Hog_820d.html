Bloaty's Pizza Hog is an episode of Invader Zim produced as, and aired as episode 10B on September 7, 2001. It was written by Rob Hummel and directed by Steve Ressel. [1]

[edit] Plot

It is Dib and Gaz's only night with their father for a year when they can go out to dinner as a family. And tonight they're going to Bloaty's Pizza Hog (a parody of Chuck E. Cheese). However, when Dib runs off to battle Zim and is delayed, Gaz and Membrane are forced to wait. When Professor Membrane can't wait any longer, Gaz has to save Dib to finally eat some pizza.

Gaz finds Dib in space and rescues him from Zim, though she doesn't bother capturing Zim. They arrive back just in time to get to Bloaty's. However, Zim arrives at Bloaty's to destroy Dib once and for all, but is so horrified by the Bloaty Pig mascot and the other robot animals (a parody of Chuck E. Cheese's animatronic characters) that he runs out screaming. Dib is disappointed that he didn't get to destroy Zim, but Gaz just throws a slice of pizza into his face, suggesting that he eat it.

[edit] Trivia

  • GIR displays a possible infatuation with Gaz.
  • First time GIR reveals his impressive arsenal of built-in weaponry.
  • First episode which features Gaz in a prominent or central role.
  • First episode where it seems Gaz knows Zim is an alien.
  • First episode where Gaz sees Zim without his disguise.
  • In one shot on Zim's ship (the one that orbits the earth and looks like a crescent moon), three moons can be seen orbiting the earth.
  • This is an example of Gaz' obsession for pizza
