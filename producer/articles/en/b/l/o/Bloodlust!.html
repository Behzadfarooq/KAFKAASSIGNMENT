A group of teenagers arrive by boat to a remote island, only to find that the island seems to be deserted. Of course this is not true and a welcoming but sadistic man invites them to stay with him in his mansion on the island. He lives on the island with his wife and her boyfriend, who are secretly planning on escaping. When the teenagers arrive at the house,they are informed by the wife and her boyfriend that her husband is a murderer and that they must escape. Around this time, the man proposes that the teenage boys play a real game of man hunt in the jungle of the island, if they can out wit him, they get to live; if not, they become part of his human exhibit. With this, the teens try to escape, encountering "scary" sights such as "human skulls".
