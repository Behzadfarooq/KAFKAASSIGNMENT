Blossom Hill (VTA)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Blossom Hill is a light rail station operated by Santa Clara Valley Transportation Authority (VTA). This station is served by VTA's Alum Rock-Santa Teresa line. It was part of the original Guadalupé Line, the first segment of light rail from Santa Teresa to Tasman.

[edit] Location

Blossom Hill station is located in the median of California State Route 85, just north of Blossom Hill Road in San Jose, California.

[edit] Station amenities

  • Bicycle parking
  • Payphone
  • Park and Ride Lot
  • Wheelchair accessible
