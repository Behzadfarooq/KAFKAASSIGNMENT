Bloomfiled High School is part of Bloomfield-Mespo Schools. The school is located in North Bloomfield, Ohio, and serves approximately 350 students from the communities of Bloomfield Township, and Mesopotamia Township.

In 2006 they received a 7 out of 10 on the Great School Test Score Ratings.

The Student-Teacher Ratio: 14 students per FTE.

Ethnicity: 95 % white, 5% unspecified

Graduation Rate: 89%

Superintendent: Frank DiPiero

School mascot: Cardinal.

Who We Are... Nestled in northeastern Ohio, approximately 50 miles southeast of Cleveland, is the quaint school district of Bloomfield-Mespo. With an enrollment of 330 students, 30 teachers, and a support staff of 15 dedicated individuals, educational excellence is the main focus of Bloomfield-Mespo Schools.

Background... Bloomfield-Mespo Local School District is a consolidated district of North Bloomfield and Mesopotamia, Ohio. Legally consolidated May 30, 1968, this district has continued to grow into one of the most efficient school districts in Ohio
