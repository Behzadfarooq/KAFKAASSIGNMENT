Blood Brothers is the sophomore album from Hip Hop duo Outerspace, released on September 5, 2006 through Babygrande Records. The album features guest appearances from Vinnie Paz of Jedi Mind Tricks, Sheek Louch and Royce Da 5'9". The album's lead single is "Street Massacre" b/w "U Don't Like Me".
