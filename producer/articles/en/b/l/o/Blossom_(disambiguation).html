Blossom (disambiguation)

Blossom can refer to:

  • Blossom, the flowers of stone fruit trees and of some other small plants.
  • Blossom (mathematics), a functional for polynomials.
  • Blossom (TV series), a 1990s American television series.
  • Blossom (novel), a 1990 novel by Andrew Vachss.
  • Blümchen (Jasmin Wagner), a German singer who uses the name Blossom when singing in English.
  • Blossom Music Center in Cleveland, Ohio.
  • Blossom, a character in the American animated series Powerpuff Girls.
  • Operation Blossom - post WWII use of lengthened V-2 rockets at White Sands Proving Grounds, with four successful launches out of seven[1]
