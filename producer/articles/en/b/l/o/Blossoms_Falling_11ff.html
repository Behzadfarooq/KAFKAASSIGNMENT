"Blossoms Falling" was Ooberman's first single for Independiente, and the first to be taken from the band's debut album The Magic Treehouse. It reached #39 in the UK, the only Ooberman single to date to make the Top 40. B-side "The Things I Have Lost" was later re-recorded for the band's "First Day of the Holidays" single.
