I'm Blotto adrift, a Librarian who lives and works in the Greater Toronto Area, Ontario, Canada.

I was born in Sudbury, Ontario and am depressingly close to 40 years old. I have a Bachelors Degree from Wilfrid Laurier University, a Masters in Public Administration from the University of Western Ontario and a Masters in Library & Information Science (also from Western).

So far, my wiki interests revolve mainly around places in Ontario, including former municipalities that existed prior to mass restructuring in the 70s and 90s. Given where I work, I try to use published sources and databases as much as possible.

Now reading: Outrageous Fortune: The Rise and Ruin of Conrad and Lady Black by Tom Bower
