A blood point, in White Wolf Studio's Vampire: The Masquerade role-playing game and books, is a unit of blood (approximately half a liter). The number of blood points a vampire has depends on its generation (the farther removed the generation is, the fewer blood points the vampire has). Blood is consumed in the order of one blood point per night to survive, and also to use the Disciplines.

Up to three blood points can be taken from a human at a time without killing him or her.
