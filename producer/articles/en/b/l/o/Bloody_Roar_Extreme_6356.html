Bloody Roar: Extreme, is a brawler video game developed by Eighting/Hudson Soft in 2003. It is the second port of the PlayStation 2 game, Bloody Roar 3. The other port was to the Nintendo GameCube the year before.

[edit] Trivia

  • CGI opening and endings replaced the anime originally used in the Nintendo GameCube version.
  • There are some minor differences between the CGI opening and the original anime opening, like Jenny holding a glass of wine instead of pulling a towel from her hair and Yugo's eyes not changing to red before his transformation. Some endings have been tweaked different to how they appeared in the anime, probably due to constraints with the software.
  • The bonus character, Fang, was originally from the manga Bloody Roar: The Fang.
