[edit] Usernames

Most of my online usernames revolve around "peebrain" (for example, "peebrain1", "peebrainx", or "the_peebrain"), although just recently I started adopting the username "bleedstupid". I've also been known as "smlefo" (originating from "smellyleftfoot").

"Peebrain" came about because when I was little, I used to pee my bed and my father called me a "peebrain". I liked the name, so used it when I first started using computers (around age 11), and converted the name into a programming company, called PeeBrain Shareware, later to be renamed to Peebrain Where? The website http://pbwhere.com is where I used to host my programming projects, although at the moment it's just a server I use for random things privately.

Just recently, in November of 2005, I created the username "bleedstupid". It was created by combining two songs that I identify with, Bleed Black by AFI and Totally Stupid by Andrew W.K. - resulting in "bleedstupid". I created a fan-site for Andrew W.K. at http://bleedstupid.com as a result.

"Smlefo" was adopted sometime randomly in 2003 or 2004. It was randomly generated from a program I wrote, that combined adjectives with nouns (resulting in "SmellyLeftFoot"). I shortened it to "smlefo" because of the nickname length restriction on EFNet. I've since used it for random accounts on the internet that I don't really care about.
