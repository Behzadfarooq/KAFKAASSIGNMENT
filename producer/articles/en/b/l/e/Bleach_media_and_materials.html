[edit] Anime

See also: List of Bleach episodes

The anime version of Bleach is done by Studio Pierrot and directed by Noriyuki Abe. It began broadcasting in Japan on October 5, 2004, on TV Tokyo.

The English version the anime is licensed by VIZ Media. Bleach began broadcasting in Canada on September 8, 2006, on YTV's Bionix block and in the United States the following evening on Cartoon Network's Adult Swim.

[edit] Bleach Soul Sonic

Bleach Soul Sonic is a seiyū event. The first took place in the Summer of 2005 and another took place in the Summer of 2006. The various seiyū talk about themselves and perform in front of an audience. The 2005 event was hosted by Mitsuaki Madono, Kon's seiyū.

[edit] Bleach: Memories of Nobody

Bleach: Memories of Nobody is a film that was released in Japanese theaters on December 16, 2006. It is set right after the Soul Society arc with Ichigo back in the living world fending off hollows. The movie is centered around the activities of a strange group called the "Dark Ones," who are trying to destroy both the Soul Society and the living world.
