Blessphemy...of the Peace Beast Feastgiver and the Bear Warp Kumite is the second album by the post-hardcore band An Albatross. It was released in 2006 on CD (Ace Fu Records) and LP (Gold Standard Labs). The LP also came with a slipmat featuring the album's artwork.
