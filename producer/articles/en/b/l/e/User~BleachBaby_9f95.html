My name is Nola O'Donnell. I'm only a college student that loves to read the articles on Animes such as Gundam Wing, Bleach, and Naruto. I'm sure in time I'll branch out into more advanced fields, but for now I'm just enjoying the wonder that is Wikimedia!! :) I am also a rider for Alpha Gamma Delta in the Women's Little 500. And by the way, I wrote that article! :D I heart WIkipedia!!!

[edit] About Me

I was born in Indianapolis, IN in the USA. I lived in Singapore for 5 years and St. Brieuc, France for a few months, and have since returned to Indiana. I'm currently attending Indiana University with the hopes of getting an International Studies and French degree with a chinese minor and a business management certificate.

If you have any questions, let me know.

[edit] Autres Choses

I am fairly profficient in french, but my grammar is horrible. And I hope to soon pick up chinese. I really don't "speak" ASL, but my friend's family is deaf, so when I see it, I can usually tell what's going on and can make VERY simple responses.
