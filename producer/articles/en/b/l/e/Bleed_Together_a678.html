"Bleed Together" is a song by Seattle grunge band Soundgarden. It was the last single released by the band, after their breakup in April 1997.

The song was released on their post-breakup greatest hits album, A-Sides. It also appeared as a b-side to their 1996 single, "Burden in My Hand"

It was written by frontman Chris Cornell and recorded by Soundgarden at Studio Litho and Bad Animals Studio in Seattle.
