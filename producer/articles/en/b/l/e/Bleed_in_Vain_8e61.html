Bleed In Vain is a melodic death metal band from Italy.

They were originally established in 1999 as a symphonic black metal band called Glory and inspired by the Swedish melodic death metal scene. During the following four years they became one of the most innovative and important Italianmetalcore band and in the summer of 2005 they have been the first band from Italy who recorded at Studio Fredman with producer Fredrik Nordstrom.

[edit] Timeline

2005
Bleed In Vain enter Studio Fredman (Gothenburg, Sweden) with producer Fredrik Nordstrom (In Flames, Soilwork, Opeth, Dark Tranquillity, Arch Enemy, The Crown, Opeth...) to record guitars and bass for the new album, the album will be mixed at the studio and the mastering will take place at Goran Finnberg's the Mastering Room.

2004
After the album release and several successful shows, the band spent the entire winter composing and pre-producing new material.

Sign with Cartel Media Records (Germany) and release the first full length "One Day Left" distribuited worldwide by distributors like Soulfood Music and Century Media Distro.

2003
“International band of the month” on Swedish Metal, the famous web portal owned by Fredrik Kreem.

2002
Excellent feedback with the demo "Of Promises Decayed" (This Illusion as monicker).
