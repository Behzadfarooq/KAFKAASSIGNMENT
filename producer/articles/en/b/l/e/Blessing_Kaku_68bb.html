Blessing Kaku (born March 5, 1978 in Ughelli, Nigeria) is a Nigerian international football player, who was a member of the national squad at the 2000 Summer Olympics in Sydney. He currently plays for Maccabi Petah Tikva FC. He has also played for K.R.C. Genk Hapoel Beer Sheva, Maccabi Tel-Aviv, FC Ashdod Bolton Wanderers, and Derby County but failed to make an impact in England in his short spells with the latter two clubs.
