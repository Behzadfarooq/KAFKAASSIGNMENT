[edit] Move to Bleach: The Movie

I considered moving this page to Bleach: The Movie because that is the proper formatting of subtitles. However it is still not certain that "The Movie" was actually meant as the subtitle, so I'll leave it as it is for now. – Gunslinger47 16:32, 29 May 2006 (UTC)

This is resolved. The movie is called Bleach: Memories of Nobody. –Gunslinger47 01:41, 5 July 2006 (UTC)
Actually the title has げきじょうばん at the beginning. In other words, it's really more like Bleach, the Movie: Memories of Nobody. Dekimasu 01:49, 10 September 2006 (UTC)
Yeah "劇場版", or genkijo-ban (theatrical version). I don't believe that's actually meant to be part of the title.
It would be the same as saying "The movie, Bleach: Memories of Nobody". –Gunslinger47 01:11, 11 September 2006 (UTC)

[edit] Canon or not

Is the movie consistent with the Bleach story. I know the anime has included the Bount arc as offical unlike the Naruto fillers. But I've heard from a totally unreliable source that this movie happens right after they save Rukia and return to the human world, which is when the Bount story takes place.--Noman953

What do you mean by official? In terms of the anime, I think the movie will either retcon the Bount arc or exist as a "parallel universe" occurrence. As for the manga, neither the movie or the Bount arc are canon but the movie is "slightly" more canon because Kubo actually had a part in its writing unlike the Bount arc which only carries his name but obviously does not carry his creativity. Gdo01 00:50, 11 December 2006 (UTC)
I believe you misheard your sources. This movie is set after they save Rukia and return to the human world, that does not necessarily mean that it's meant to fit within the continuity of the manga or the anime. That said, the sources we have now do say that Tite Kubo is writing the story for the film. –Gunslinger47 08:01, 11 December 2006 (UTC)
