Bleeding Shadows are a Metal/Experimental band with an touch of math metal and trash.

Bleeding Shadows consists of 4 members Rick, Duffy, Morg and Seb. These guys are known for their energetic performances, brutal pits/wall of deaths, extreme guitar/drum solos on stage. Bleeding Shadows have played at tiny and huge venues varying from 12 Bar Club/The Cartoon Club to The Dome Upstairs. The band has now reached the stage where they will be recording their E.P which comes out in 2007. Bleeding Shadows has been in Kerrang! and on Total Rock radio! They have recently supported Head-On (Grind That Axe), Kyrbgrinder and Pure Reason Revolution (Sony) which has expanded their crowd all over the London "circuit". They began with just 2 memembers, Duffy and Rick jamming in high school and becoming good friends, now they've become a 4 piece who have to be checked out live with songs like "Death To Emo" and "Bloodshed" on the bill. This band has had good success so far considering that they've only been a full band just over 6 months starting (June 2006).
