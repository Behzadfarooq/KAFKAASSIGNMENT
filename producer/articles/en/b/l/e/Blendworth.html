Blendworth is a village near Horndean between Petersfield and Waterlooville. It has a small population, of fewer than 100 people. The church, Holy Trinity Blendworth, recently celebrated it's 150th Anniversary, and until recently, had a C of E Infant School next to it. It is part of the parish of Horndean. Blendworth also has a stable and a Brass Band.

The surrounding villages are Horndean, Chalton, Finchdean and Rowlands Castle.
