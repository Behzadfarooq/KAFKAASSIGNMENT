Blenio (district)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

District of Blenio in the canton Ticino

The district of Blenio is a district of the canton of Ticino in Switzerland. The capital of the district is Acquarossa.

[edit] Circles and municipalities

  • circle of Malvaglia: Malvaglia, Semione, Ludiano;
  • circle of di Acquarossa: Acquarossa;
  • circle of Olivone: Blenio.
