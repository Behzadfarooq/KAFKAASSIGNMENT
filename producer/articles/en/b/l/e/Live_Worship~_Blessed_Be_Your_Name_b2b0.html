Live Worship: Blessed Be Your Name is the first live album from Christian pop and rock singer Rebecca St. James.

[edit] Track listing

  1. "Lamb Of God"
  2. "Blessed Be Your Name"
  3. "Quiet You With My Love"
  4. "Let My Words Be Few"
  5. "I Adore You"
  6. "Above All"
  7. "Here I Am To Worship"
  8. "The Power Of Your Love"* (Studio Recorded)
  9. Bonus: "Shout to the Lord" (Studio Recorded)
  • Produced by Matt Bronleewe
  • Produced by Tedd T.*

[edit] Notes

  • The live songs were recorded at the Let My Words Be Few Tour.
