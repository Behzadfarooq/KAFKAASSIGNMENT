Blessed Sacrament School (Wainwright)

Blessed Sacrament School is located in Wainwright, Alberta, Canada. BSS is a K-12 school that serves to about 500 students. It presently has a Junior Kindergarten available for ages 3-5. The school team is the THUNDER. The school was originally established in 1933 but has been rebuilt recently due to fire on September 26, 2001.
