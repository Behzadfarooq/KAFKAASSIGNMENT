Ble is a pop-rock band from Greece that emerged in 1996. They revolutionized the then bland and suffering pop scene in Greece with their dark and introspective Enoches. Their first single Fovame became a huge hit single and was one of the most popular love ballads of the year in Greece. Ble's second self-titled album featured a harder and heavier sound. [1] Their third album, Ela Na Dis [2]was released in 2001.
