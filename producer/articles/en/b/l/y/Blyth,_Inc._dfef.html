Blyth, Inc. is a Greenwich, Connecticut based marketing and manufacturing company that sells personal and decorative products. The company has 4,900 full time employees and is incorporated in Delaware. It is the largest candlemaker in the United States,[1]

Subsidiaries include Candle Corporation Of America, which owns the Sterno product brand and the PartyLite direct sales business.
