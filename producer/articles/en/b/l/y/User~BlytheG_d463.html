I've been editing on Wikipedia since July 2006, and find it both educational and enjoyable. I live in Sierra Vista, Arizona, U.S., and am currenly a junior in high school. I am a very serious amateur astronomer.

I plan to attend U of A and earn a doctorate in optical engineering. I also plan on being a science author and popularizer.

Interests: Astronomy, astrophysics, optical engineering, evolutionary biology, film editing.

Heroes: Carl Sagan, Richard Dawkins, Neil deGrasse Tyson, Sam Harris, James Randi.
