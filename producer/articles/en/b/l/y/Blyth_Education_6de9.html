Blyth Education, simply referred to as Blyth, is divided into three sections. Blyth International Summers, Blyth Academy, and The View Foundation.

[edit] Blyth International Summers

The Blyth International Summer Programs are in integral part of Blyth Academy. The summer programs offer both high school credits, as well as community volunteer options. The programs, according to their website, stretch more than 17 countries, across Europe, South America, Asia, and Oceania.

[edit] Blyth Academy

The Academy can be further divided into two sections. The full time academy, which is located in downtown Toronto, or the part time program. The part time program generally is for either catching up, or for getting ahead in the summer. There are three locations in the Toronto area.

[edit] The View Foundation

Just over 10 years old, this aspect of Blyth is growing exponentially. It is incorporated as a not for profit foundation. This program leads teenagers to Ethiopia, Tanzania, and Cuba for community service work. Blyth originally started in Ethiopia, and only recently expanded to Cuba.
