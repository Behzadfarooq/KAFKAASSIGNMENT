Blåsjø is the tenth largest lake by area in the municipality of Bykle in Aust-Agder county and the municipalities of Hjelmeland and Suldal in Rogaland county. It has a surface area of 84.48 km². It lies 1055 m above sea level and has a shoreline of 200.41 km.

Blåsjø is regulated and functions as a reservoir for Ulla-Førreverkene hydroelectric power generation. The lake has an annual energy generation potential of about 7.8  TW•h.
