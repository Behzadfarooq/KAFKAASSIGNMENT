[edit] Description

Blue October play emotionally fuelled, synth electro pop, fusing electronic wizardry and guitar with underlying dance grooves.

Signed to A Different Drum ( USA ) and Cryonica ( UK ). They have released three full length albums, and been featured on several compilations.
