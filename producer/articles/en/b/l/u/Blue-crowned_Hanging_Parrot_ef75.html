The Blue-crowned Hanging Parrot, Loriculus galgulus, is a small (length: 5 inches, 13cm) parrot found in forested lowlands from Thailand to Borneo.

This common hanging parrot lays 3-4 eggs in a tree cavity.

Its diet includes flowers, buds, fruits, nuts and seeds.
