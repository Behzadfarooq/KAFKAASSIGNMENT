Blue Rock Café (Norwegian: Blå Rock Café) is a rock nightclub located in the small city Tromsø in Norway.

The café started up back in 1991, and has seens than kept the rock spirit within the house. It is said to have the best burgers you can possible get in Norway[citation needed] and they serve beer from the local Mack Brewery. One 0.4l glass of beer cost as much as 60,-NOK and it's the most expensive one in the whole city[citation needed].
