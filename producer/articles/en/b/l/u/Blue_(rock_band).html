Blue (2004-Present) is an American musical group from Washington Township, New Jersey. They have been writing, performing, and recording since 2005. With a mix of rock, grunge, and new styles, they are able to create new music. They have been influenced by The Beatles, The Doors, Pearl Jam, Nirvana, Incubus, and many more.

[edit] History

[edit] Formation of Blue

Blue, Washington Township High School Talent Show

Kyle Harrje, Gary Talvacchio, and Jon Gerwald decide to jam one day and realized they worked well together. After a short discussion of what they should name there side-project, they decided on Blue. The side-project was nothing more than a name and few songs, since Kyle Harrje, Jon Gewald, and Josh Griffis were in there own band called, Royal Landslide, and Gary Talvacchio and Bob Colozzi were in there own band. After both bands spilt, Kyle and Gary decided to reform Blue with help form Josh and Bob.

They decided to use Kyle's song "In the End" form Royal Landslide and Gary's song "So Much Better" to get the band started. After only being together for a month, they recorded there That's A Good Question EP with "In the End" and "So Much Better" with Mike Britt at Gizmotron Fortress. Not long after did Blue begin writing 6 more original songs, resulting in the LP Gulity Pleasures. Most of the instrument tracks were recorded by Talvacchio, and the vocal tracks by Britt, who also mixed and mastered the EP.

In early 2006 Josh got an eight month running battle of the bands through Jersey Shows with 252 other bands. Show after show they took the title to move on to the next round. In August 2005 at the finals, performing with Wheatus, they took fourth in the all age group.

Although Kyle went to Five Towns College the day after the battle of the bands show and Jon went Kean University, they still practice and perform shows. Currently, the band has been working with Producer/Engineer Erik Salomon on completing their first professionally produced/recorded EP. The record is expected to be completed in early 2007.
