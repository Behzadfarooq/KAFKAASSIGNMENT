Blu (company)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Blu is a manga publishing company based in Los Angeles, California. The company publishes shounen-ai and yaoi manga. It is an imprint of the popular company Tokyopop and published its first series in November 2005.

The manga published by Blu include:

  • Black Knight
  • Earthian
  • Gerard & Jacques
  • Gorgeous Carat
  • Junjo Romantica: Pure Romance
  • Love Mode
  • Love Pistols
  • Man's Best Friend
  • Menkui!
  • Shinobu Kokoro
  • Shout Out Loud!
  • Wild Rock
