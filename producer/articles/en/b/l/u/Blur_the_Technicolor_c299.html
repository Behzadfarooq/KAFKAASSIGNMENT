"Blur the Technicolor" is a song off the album Astro-Creep: 2000 by heavy metal band White Zombie. Also, a remix version of this song can be found on the EP Supersexy Swingin' Sounds. The track was the earliest song created for the album, it was even being played live back when the band was touring for La Sexorcisto. Due to legal issues, parts of the artwork on the lyric page of Blur the Technicolor had to be edited shortly after the album was first released. Also, the song was used in the 1995 movie Ace Ventura: When Nature Calls when Ace begins chasing Vincent Cadby down in the monster truck.

In 2000, the song was covered by Disarray for the White Zombie tribute album Super-Charger Hell.
