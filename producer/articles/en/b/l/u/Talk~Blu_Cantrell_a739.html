[edit] Seventh Octave and Whistle Register

In the opening track, "Waste My Time" from her debut album, So Blu.[1] Cantrell hits notes in the seventh octave. According to an interview with Neil McCormick at smh.com.au, Cantrell mentions she has a 5 octave range, as well as her struggles to get noticed in the music business, "I'd get on stage and I'd sing and hit five octaves. I coulda did a backwards flip and they woulda sat there and gone, 'Hmm, I don't know!'

The first sentence in the above is 'original research' and not referenced by an official source. Furthermore, one of the criteria to be on the seventh octave list is that the person has to have multiple recordings displaying such ability. I am also not pleased with the second sentence because it was Blu herself who declared that she has hit 5 octaves and not the interviewer him/herself declaring this. Until more suitable information is available I do not think Blu's name is to be readded. ke'ke 10/30/06 7:28am
