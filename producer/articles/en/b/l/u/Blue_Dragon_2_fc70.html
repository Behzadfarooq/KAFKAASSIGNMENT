Blue Dragon 2 (ブルードラゴン, Burū Doragon Tsū?) is the sequel to the console role-playing game Blue Dragon released in 2006 by Mistwalker for the Microsoft Xbox 360. No information has been released on this title as of yet, although in an interview on IGN with Hironobu Sakaguchi, the creator of the original Blue Dragon, he did confirm that a sequel was in the planning stages[1] and will start development presumably soon. Also, in another interview on the Computer and Video Games website, he said that he would like Blue Dragon to expand and grow as a series,[2] similar to that of Final Fantasy (the series for which Sakaguchi is most noted for creating) and Dragon Quest, even mentioning a Blue Dragon 3! The console which this game is to be released on has not yet been confirmed either, although in an interview on IGN he did say that he did expect it to arrive on the Xbox 360 like its predecessor.[3]
