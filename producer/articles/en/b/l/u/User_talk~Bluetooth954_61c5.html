[edit] A note about linking

Hello! I am a member of the Wikipedia project for disambiguating links. Links in music articles are big culprits of creating ambiguous links so I wanted to pass along some tips to help you with your contributions:

  • Rock should be (rock music) Rock
  • Metal should be (heavy metal music) Metal
  • Pop should be (pop music) Pop
  • Band should be (band (music)) Band
  • Punk should be (punk rock) Punk
  • Soul should be (soul music) Soul
  • Gothic or Goth should be (goth music) Goth
  • Remember this applies to music templates too!

Please contact me if you have any questions. Cheers. Dina 15:10, 13 September 2006 (UTC)
