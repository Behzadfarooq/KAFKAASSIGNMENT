User:Bluebot

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

This user account is a bot operated by Bluemoose (talk).

It is not a sock puppet, but rather an automated or semi-automated account for making repetitive edits that would be extremely tedious to do manually.
Administrators: if this bot is malfunctioning or causing harm, please block it.

This is a bot belonging to User:Bluemoose. This bot uses AWB to periodically perform a range of formatting tasks, as well as some maintenance tasks such as sorting some templates by date.
