Blue Meanie can refer to:

  • Blue Meanies (Yellow Submarine), fictional music-hating creatures who invade Pepperland in the movie Yellow Submarine
  • Blue Meanies, fictional cat-like creatures in Katherine Applegate's book series, Remnants.
  • Blue Meanies, self-given nickname for fans of the band Blue October
  • "Blue Meanie", one of many slang terms for police officers
  • "Blue Meanie" was also a slang term for Edinburgh traffic wardens during a period where their uniforms were blue.[1]
  • Blue Meanies, a musical band of the 1990s
  • Blue Meanies, a former engineering group within Apple Computer
  • Blue Meanies, a Mexican Beatles tribute band .
  • The ARP 2600, affectionately dubbed the "Blue Meanie"
  • Blue Meanie, A common name for a type of magic mushroom containing Psilocybin and Psilocin also known as Panaeolus (Copelandia) Cyanescens.
  • Brian Heffron, a professional wrestler more commonly known by his ring name; The Blue Meanie.
  • The Big Blue Meanie, correctly known as Greed, a monster from the Bungie game Pathways Into Darkness.
