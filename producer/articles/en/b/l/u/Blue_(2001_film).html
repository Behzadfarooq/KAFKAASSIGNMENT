Blue (2001) is a Japanese romantic drama directed by Hiroshi Ando. It is based on a Japanese manga by Kiriko Nananan, and starring Mikako Ichikawa and Manami Konishi.


It is about two high school aged girls Kayako Kirishima and Masami Endo find that their friendship is turning into something more.
