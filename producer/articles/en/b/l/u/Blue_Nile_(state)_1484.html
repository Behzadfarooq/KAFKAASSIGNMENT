Blue Nile

Blue Nile (Arabic: النيل الأزرق; transliterated: an-Nyl al-Azraq; called Al Wustá during 1991—1994) is one of the 26 wilayat (states) of Sudan. It has an area of 45,844 km² and an estimated population of approximately 600,000 (2000). Ad-Damazin is the capital of the state.
