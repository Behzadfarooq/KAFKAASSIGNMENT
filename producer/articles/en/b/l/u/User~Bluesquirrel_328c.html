History Blue Squirrel has been developing and selling software since 1995. Their original product was SQURL (Search and Query URL's) which has been replaced by WebSeeker.

Blue Squirrel's Web Site: [1]

In early 1996, Grab-a-Site and the Internet Automation System was released. In March of 1996, Blue Squirrel was acquired by The ForeFront Group, Inc. (NASDAQ: FFGI) and in 1997 became ForeFront's Internet Content Management Division responsible for development of ForeFront's entire line of Internet and Content Management products, including WebSeeker, WebWhacker, Spam Sleuth, and ClickBook. ForeFront and Blue Squirrel were acquired again in 1998 by CBT Systems (Nasdaq: CBTSY).

In June of 2000, Blue Squirrel became a privately funded independent company and acquired technologies of Wintronix, Bookmaker, and ForeFront. Blue Squirrel now develops and sells more than 15 innovative software titles direct-to-consumer, and through a variety of distributors and resellers around the world.

Their Name Their first product was called SQURL (Search and Query URL's) so Blue Squirrel used a squirrel as their company logo. They wanted their squirrel to stand out from the crowd, so they made him blue, and now their mascot is the one and only Blue Squirrel.

Product Line

CLICKBOOK prints computer files as handy booklets, pdfs, or enlarged posters and banners.

GRAB-A-SITE is an "industrial-strength" tool that can download an entire web site while retaining the original filenames and directory structure.

CLICK2PDF converts computer files into non-editable, virus-free PDFs for viewing on all computer platforms.

BANNER KIT, a low-cost package to design, print, and assemble custom interchangeable six foot vinyl banners.

SPAM SLEUTH slows the flow of e-mail spam, and cast out all Web Bugs, potential viruses, and phishing scams.

WEBWHACKER downloads Web Sites to a floppy, Zip disk, hard drive, or any storage device. You can burn CD-ROMs with Web content for distribution.

SNAPTURE captures travel itinerary, eBay listing, driving directions, product specs, an e-zine article, or a 20-page contract to your palm, pocket pc, or windows.

BLUESYNC FOR PALM syncs documents, family photos, MP3 files, Podcasts and more from an SD/MMC card or Memory Stick with a PC.

LIVE SUPPORT lets support personnel solve problems quickly by directly controlling a customer's computer.

WEBSEEKER leverages the power of many search engines, and uses a computer to refine the results, making it effortless for users to find information on the Internet.
