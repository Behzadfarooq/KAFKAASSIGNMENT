[edit] Jose Bernardo

Jose Bernardo Born in 1915, a Filipino Prewar Actor who made a film only a few. His film debut is under Sampaguita Pictures's Siya'y Aking Anak aka He is my Son and 1940's Drama Tinig ng Bayan under Joaquin Film Company.

[edit] Juan Pardo de Tavera

Juan Pardo de Tavera (born in 1901) is a Filipino film director who made his first film under Parlatone Hispano-Filipino in 1939 titled Lihim ng Dagat-Dagatan aka Secret of the Sea.

In 1940, he made his second and last movie Puso ng Isang Filipina aka A Heart of a Filipina again in Parlatone Hispano-Filipino.

Filmography

  • 1939 -Lihim ng Dagat-Dagatan
  • 1940 -Puso ng Isang Filipina
