Blue is the first album by American country singer LeAnn Rimes, released in the United States on July 9, 1996 (see 1996 in music) by Curb Records. It reached number three on the Billboard Hot 200 album chart, and number one on Hot Country Albums. The album has been certified 8x Platinum by RIAA. It is her best-selling album to date, and was released when she was only 13 years old.
