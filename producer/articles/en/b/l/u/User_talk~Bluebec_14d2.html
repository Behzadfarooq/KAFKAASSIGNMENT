[edit] Image Uploads

G'day Bec,

You may wish to read over Wikipedia:Image_copyright_tags, since if wikipedia is to use an image, it needs a license to do so. The primary way of indicating what sort of license applies to an image is by using one of the tags described in that page. For example, I may take an image and release it under the GFDL, in which case I would use the {{GFDL}} tag. See Image:Turnip.JPG for an example.

Sometimes there are images from third-party sources that can also be used by wikipedia, either because they're in the public domain, or permission has otherwise been granted. An example would be the chemical structure of solanine which was specifically released into the public domain.

I know that the map of Ghazni comes from aims.org.af, but I can't find any license on those page detailing permission to use the images or otherwise. There certainly seems to be the intent for other parties to use the maps and images supplied, but I can't find explicit permission.

Since you've got much more experience with the aims.org.af site than I do, you may wish to see if you can find notice on the site that allows the use of the maps and other images in works such as wikipedia, and if you can, add an appropriate image tag to the map of Ghazni.

Your edits have been great, and I've been learning much more about Afghanistan because of them. Keep up the fantastic work.

All the very best,

Paul (pjf 09:11, 4 Oct 2004 (UTC))
