Zion 3.0, is the next generation of Lan Emulation suites in the zion series. It is primarily used for Lan Emulation with Blizzard games. It includes features such as simple game-spamming, integration with mIRC, user ratings and much more.

Many popular figures within the "DOTA" scene are also part of the Zion Team.
