[edit] Should this article exist?

It has come to my attention that there are some who want to remove this article entirely. If so, please state why, as many of us feel that this is a noteable current event in need of mention. Note that wikipedia's policy on notability reads:

"A topic is notable if it has been the subject of at least one substantial or multiple, non-trivial published works from sources that are reliable and independent of the subject and of each other."

which this topic clearly qualifies as. That said, there may be something I am missing here. So for the time being, I am placing the hangon tag on the article. toll_booth 20:47, 10 March 2007 (UTC)

[edit] Please Refrain From Calling Zach Arend 'Killed in the Crash'

Zach Arend died a week later in the hospital. He died 'as a result of injuries sustained in the crash,' not 'killed in the crash.' In fact it seems only one fatality was immediately confirmed 'in' the crash (though there may have been more, that was the earliest report). One died in the ambulance, and another died shortly after arriving at the hospital (David Betts), at least according to media reports.

Let's also try to get the ages right. Cody Holp was 19, not 18.

→ R Young {yakłtalk} 12:17, 11 March 2007 (UTC)
