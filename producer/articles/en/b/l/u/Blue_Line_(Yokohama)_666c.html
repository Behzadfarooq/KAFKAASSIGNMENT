Blue Line (Yokohama)

The Yokohama Blue Line (ブルーライン) is a 40-km (22-mile) subway line in Yokohama, Japan, operated by Yokohama City Transportation Bureau. Line 3 transits from Azamino to Kannai, and Line 1 transits from Kannai to Shonandai. All the trains join these two lines.

[edit] Line Data

  • Total length: 40.4km
    Line 1: 19.7km
    Line 3: 20.7km
  • Gauge: 1435mm
  • Number of Stations: 32
    Line 1: 17 including Kannai Station.
    Line 3: 16 including Kannai Station
