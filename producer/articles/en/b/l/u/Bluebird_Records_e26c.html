Bluebird Records
Parent company Sony BMG
Founded 1934
Distributing label RCA Victor Group (In the US)
Genre(s) Jazz
Country of origin US
Official Website http://www.bluebirdjazz.com/

Bluebird Records is a sub-label of RCA Victor Records originally created in 1934 to counter ARC Records on the "3 records for a dollar" market. The imprint was later used for jazz releases & reissues as well as children's records in the 1950s and has been reactivated by RCA Victor Records as a jazz label.
