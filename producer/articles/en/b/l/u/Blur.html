[edit] Unfocused imagery

"Blur" generally refers to the appearance of an unfocused image. See eyeglass prescription, lens and/or the eye.

Specific terms using this meaning of "Blur" include:

  • Blur derivative
  • Bokeh, a photographic technique involving out of focus lenses.
  • Box blur, a graphic-art effect
  • Gaussian blur, a graphic-art effect
  • Motion blur, a photographic and optical effect
