Background information

BLUEMOON (former Lunar) is a Cantonese Teenagers Band group in Hong Kong since 2003. The group has 4 members, they are Adrian (Vocal/ Guitar), Gary (Bass), Tony (Guitar) and Ste (Drums). Their songs are likely between Rock and Pop. Their songs are published online and are also performed through the shows. The band also perform songs with the other bands' member(s) in some shows occasionally. All the members play for the band as partime interests.
