[edit] Gloria del Mundo

Born in 1917, Filipina Actress del Mundo made her first movie in 1940's Love Story-Drama Magbalik ka, Hirang aka Come Back Darling under Sampaguita Pictures.

Filmography

1940 -Magbalik ka, Hirang [Sampaguita]

[edit] Guillermo de Joya

Guillermo de Joya (born in 1917) is a Filipino Actor who made only a few movie in the late 1930s and are truly a remarkable supporting actor in his generation.

de Joya made his filmdebut on 1938's movie Mutya ng Sinilangan aka Muse of the East under Sinukuan Pictrues.

Filmography

  • 1938 - Mutya ng Silangan
