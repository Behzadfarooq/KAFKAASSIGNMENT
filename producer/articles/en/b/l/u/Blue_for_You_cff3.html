Blue for You is an album by English Rock band Status Quo, released in March 1976. It entered the British album chart at No. 1 and stayed there for three weeks, aking it one of their most successful long players.

'Rain', the first single from the album, reached No. 7 in the UK chart. An edited version of 'Mystery Song' was the second single and peaked at No. 11 a few months later.
