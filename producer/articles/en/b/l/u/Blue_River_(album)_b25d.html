Blue River is the 1972 album from Folk Rock musician Eric Andersen. The album was recorded in Nashville, and is a landmark recording for the Singer-songwriter/Folk Rock genre. Guest musician Joni Mitchell sings a lovely vocal on the title track "Blue River"
