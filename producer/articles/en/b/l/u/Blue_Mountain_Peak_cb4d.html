Blue Mountain Peak
Elevation 2,256 metres (7,402 feet)
Location Jamaica
Range Blue Mountains
Coordinates 18°02′N, 76°34′W

Blue Mountain Peak is the highest mountain in Jamaica at 2,256 metres (7,402 feet). It is the home of Blue Mountain coffee and their famous tours. The Blue Mountains (Jamaica) is a considered by many to be a hiker's and camper's paradise. The traditional Blue Mountain trek is the 7 mile hike to the peak with an increase in altitude of 3000 ft. Jamaicans prefer to reach the peak at sunrise so the 3-4 hour hike is usually undertaken in darkness. Hagley Gap is the small coffee farming community located near the peak and is about a 7 mile hike to the top.
