Blue Tango

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

"Blue Tango" is a popular song.

The music was written by Leroy Anderson, the lyrics by Mitchell Parish. The song was published in 1952.


An instrumental version by Leroy Anderson reached #1 on the Billboard charts in 1952. A vocal version was recorded by Alma Cogan in the United Kingdom the same year. It was also featured on the first UK singles chart in another instrumental recording by orchestra leader Ray Martin.
