Blue and white

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Blue and white may refer to:

  • The national colors of El Salvador, Finland, Greece, Guatemala, Honduras, Israel, Kosovo, Micronesia, Nicaragua, San Marino, Somalia (see also List of countries by colors of national flags#Blue and white)
  • Blue and white (porcelain) and certain types of ceramics
  • Blue-and-White Flycatcher (Cyanoptila cyanomelana), a migratory songbird from Asia
  • Blue-and-white Swallow (Notiochelidon cyanoleuca), is a passerine bird from South America
  • The Blue and White is a magazine written by undergraduates at Columbia University
  • Power Macintosh G3 (Blue & White) commonly known as the "Blue and White G3", or sometimes just the "B&W G3"
  • Goodbye Blue & White is a rarities album by Less Than Jake
  • Legend of Blue-Eyes White Dragon is the first English booster to be released in the Yu-Gi-Oh! Trading Card Game
