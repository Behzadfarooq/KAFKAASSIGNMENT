Bluestone National Scenic River

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Bluestone National Scenic River protects a portion of the Bluestone River in southern West Virginia. Little of the park is accessible via roads; one must travel via the river, trails from Bluestone State Park, or an aerial tramway from Pipestem Resort State Park.
