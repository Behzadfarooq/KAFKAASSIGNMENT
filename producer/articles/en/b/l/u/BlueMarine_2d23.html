blueMarine is an opensource photograph management application, designed to support the whole workflow of a digital photographer. Being based on a modular structure, its functions can be expanded by writing plugins.

[edit] Features

The blueMarine interface is neat and simple, modeled upon the latest UI trends such as Apple Aperture and Adobe Lightroom. It supports all the major photographic formats (JPEG, PNG, TIFF) as well as many vendor specific "camera raw" formats (NEF, CR2, PEF, ORF, SRF, CRW, MRW) and the Adobe proposed standard DNG.

blueMarine can import photos from an external storage or camera and let the user to organize them freely. The user can assign tags (both preset and user-definable) and perform queries by different criteria. Photos can be organized by Galleries and Trip; blueMarine also provide support for geotagging and is able to display Google maps in an embedded viewer.

Editing functions are not yet supported, but will delivered in a near future.
