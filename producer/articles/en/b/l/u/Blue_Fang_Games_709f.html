Blue Fang Games is a computer game developer currently working in conjunction with Microsoft Game Studios. Blue Fang is noted for the popular Zoo Tycoon series, which so far remains their only product line, although a new universe of games has been rumoured.

[edit] Games

  • Zoo Tycoon (2001)
  • Zoo Tycoon: Dinosaur Digs (2002)
  • Zoo Tycoon: Marine Mania (2002)
  • Zoo Tycoon: Complete Collection (2003)
  • Zoo Tycoon 2 (2004)
  • Zoo Tycoon DS (2005)
  • Zoo Tycoon 2: Endangered Species (2005)
  • Zoo Tycoon 2: African Adventure (2006)
  • Zoo Tycoon 2: Dino Danger Pack (2006)
  • Zoo Tycoon 2: Zookeeper Collection (2006)
  • Zoo Tycoon 2: Marine Mania (2006)
  • Zoo Tycoon 2: Extinct Animals (2007)

[edit] Trivia

  • All of Blue Fang Game's music and sound has been directed by George Valavanis since the release of Zoo Tycoon 2: Endangered Species.
  • Zoo Tycoon 2: Marine Mania, Zoo Tycoon 2: Zookeeper Collection and Zoo Tycoon 2: Extinct Animals are all in the Games for Windows series.
