[edit] Fair-use rationale for The Blues Brothers (film)

  1. The image is necessary and standard to display the film as a whole for the lead-in infobox.
  2. It is not practical to convey a visual image about the film without using the movie poster, therefore, this is a non-replaceable image.
  3. The image does not inhibit sales of the DVD or posters for The Blues Brothers (film).
  4. The image does not impede the licensing of Ruang The Blues Brothers (film).
  5. The image does no damage to the image or quality of The Blues Brothers (film). In fact its use exactly fits the purpose of a movie poster, promoting the film.
