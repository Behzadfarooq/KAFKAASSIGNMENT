Blue Ridge Community College is a community college located in the Shenandoah Valley of Virginia in Augusta County. The campus is located between Interstate 81 and US Route 11 just outside Weyers Cave. The school is a part of the Virginia Community College System and serves students from the cities of Harrisonburg, Staunton, and Waynesboro, Virginia, and the counties of Augusta, Highland and Rockingham. Blue Ridge also has sites located in Harrisonburg and the Augusta Medical Center in Fishersville, Virginia.
