  • Generally, non-commercial licences are bad because our mirrors cannot use them, which limits the "free information for everybody" mission. Thus a better licence would be the Creative Commons By Attribution Share Alike, which is tagged {{cc-by-sa-2.0}} or just By Attribution, which is {{cc-by-2.0}}. You can read more about this licences at By-Sa and By. Hopefully this is more useful. Also, you can sign your name like this ~~~~, which gives you name and datestamp.
