The Blue Album (released 21 June 2004) was the final album from the British electronica duo Orbital. It includes their final single "One Perfect Sunrise".

Blue Album's title recalls the informal names of Orbital's first two, self-titled albums, known colloquially as the Green Album and the Brown Album.
