Blueprint: Design Your Life is a bimonthly women's interest magazine published by Martha Stewart Living Omnimedia. MSLO has announce that they intend to release 2 test issues in May and August 2006, with an initial base circulation of 250,000, and plans to begin regular bimonthly publication in 2007. [1] The first issue was released May 1, 2006.

The magazine is being targeted at women age 25-40, and will compete with magazines such as Real Simple (whose former editor Tom Prince will be heading the new magazine [2]) and Domino for readers. It has been recognized as a move by the company to become more competitive in the younger demographic, and diversify its interests outside the core Martha Stewart brand. [3]

[edit] Similarly named publications

The title 'Blueprint' has historically been used for a variety of publication, including a British monthly on architecture, a political news magazine, and a Canadian Student culture magazine.
