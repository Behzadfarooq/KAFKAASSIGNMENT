"Blue Turns to Grey" is a song that was written by Mick Jagger and Keith Richards. The song first appeared in February of 1965 when both Dick and Dee Dee and The Mighty Avengers released versions of it. It was released by The Rolling Stones on their 1965 album December's Children (And Everybody's) later that year. It became a hit in the UK when remade by Cliff Richard in 1966. Don and The Goodtimes also recorded a version of this song, as did The Epics on Pye records.
