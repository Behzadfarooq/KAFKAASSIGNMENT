Bluesoleil is a Windows 2000/XP program that allows communication with Bluetooth enabled devices. It is often bundled with Bluetooth dongles. However, Bluesoleil dongles don't work with Microsoft or Widcomm stacks.

The program is also available as a standalone purchase from the vendor's website. Regardless of whether the bundled or the standalone version is purchased, the software enforces licensing restrictions which tie it to the address of a specific Bluetooth dongle.

If there is no Bluetooth dongle attached to the PC the Bluetooth logo will be grey, blue if a dongle is attached, and green connected to another Bluetooth enabled device.
