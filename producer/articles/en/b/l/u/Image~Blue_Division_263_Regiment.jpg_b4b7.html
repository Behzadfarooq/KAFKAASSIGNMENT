[edit] Licensing

This file is in the public domain, because author's rights could conceivably still exist; but should qualify for public domain under German law as the product of a Wehrmacht employee made for official purposes during the course of his official duties. However the GermanGov template is labelled with:
"Almost everything is covered by normal copyright (70 years protection after the death of the author), but there is a permission (UrhG § 5(2)) to reproduce specific official material provided that the source is credited and the material is not modified. In practice, however, this status has not been frequently applied in case law. Therefore, most pictures that fall under this licence should probably be deleted to be safe. Note: This template is deprecated and should not be used."

Please verify that the reason given above is valid!
