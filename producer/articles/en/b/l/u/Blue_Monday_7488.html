Blue Monday may refer to:

  • Blue Monday (New Order song), a 1980s dance music record, and the highest selling 12-inch single of all time
  • Blue Monday/Stitches CD Single, is the first single of the Synth rock band Orgy, also found on their album Candyass
  • Blue Monday (Fats Domino song), one of the earliest R&B songs to make the Billboard charts
  • Blue Monday (opera), the original name of a George Gershwin's one-act "Jazz opera" 135th Street
  • Blue Monday (comic), a slice of life comic book by Chynna Clugston-Major
  • Blue Monday (White Lion song), an instrumental by White Lion guitarist Vito Bratta
  • Blue Monday (Eureka Seven episode) is the 1st episode of the anime series Eureka Seven
  • Blue Monday (date), the most depressing day of the year
  • Breakfast of Champions, by Kurt Vonnegut, alternatively titled "Goodbye Blue Monday!"
  • The Monday before Shrove Tuesday and Lent; see also Clean Monday
  • The fifth and deciding game of the 1981 National League Championship Series between the Montréal Expos and the Los Angeles Dodgers where the Dodgers' Rick Monday hit a ninth-inning home run off of Montréal pitcher Steve Rogers to win the series.
