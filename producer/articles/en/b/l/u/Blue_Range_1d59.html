The Blue Range (50°50′N 115°35′W) is a mountain range of the Canadian Rockies, located on the Continental Divide in Banff National Park, Canada. The range is named for the blue appearance of the range when seen from a distance.

This range includes the following mountains and peaks:

Mountain/Peak metres feet
Red Man Mountain 2,905 9,531
Aurora Mountain 2,789 9,151
