Blunt Cliff

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Blunt Cliff is a fictional location in the Sword of Truth fantasy series by Terry Goodkind.

[edit] Geographical Characteristics

Blunt Cliff is a small trail on Blunt Mountain in Ven Forest. An off-shoot of Hawker's Trail, Blunt Cliff makes it's way onto the south face of Blunt Mountain where a traveler can get a panoramic view of the southern Ven Forest as well as the high, rugged peaks belonging to the boundary.

It was at Blunt Cliff that Richard and Kahlan faced (and defeated) their first quad.
