"Blue"
Single by A Perfect Circle
Released July 27, 2004
Genre Alternative
Length 4:13

Blue is a single by the American Alternative band A Perfect Circle. The song is from the band's 2003 second album Thirteenth Step. The song was later remixed entitled "Blue (Bird Shake Mix)" by James Iha and Geoff Sanoff for the 2004 aMOTION CD/DVD.
