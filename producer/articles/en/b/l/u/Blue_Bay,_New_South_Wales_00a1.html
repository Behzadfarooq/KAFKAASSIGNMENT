Blue Bay is a suburb located on the Central Coast of New South Wales, Australia, as part of the Wyong Shire local government area. The Central Coast is an urban region, located on the coastline north of Sydney and south of Lake Macquarie and Newcastle.[4] The Entrance itself is located approximately 97km north of the Sydney central business district.[5]
