Category:Blue-eyed soul singers

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

This category contains Caucasian artists who have made notable contributions to R&B and soul music. Blue-eyed soul is a misnomer; the artist is not required to have blue eyes, only be a white singer who has made music that credibly fits in the traditionally black world of R&B, hip-hop, and/or soul. See Blue-eyed soul for more information.
