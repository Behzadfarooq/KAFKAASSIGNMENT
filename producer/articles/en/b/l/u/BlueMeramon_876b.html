BlueMeramon is an Ultimate Level Flame Digimon that appears similar to Meramon, but blue in color and is capable of attacking with ice attacks.

[edit] Appearances

[edit] Battle of Adventurers

BlueMeramon was among the Digimon who Bio-Emerged when Mephistomon was using V-Pets to crash Earth's electronics network. He battled Renamon and Rika and had Renamon outmatched with his ice powers. She suddenly Digivolved to Kyubimon and quickly destroyed him with the attack Fox Tail Inferno.


[edit] Digimon World

Blue Meramon Appears in Ice Sanctuary,along with two Icemon. This Digimon uses Promience Beam almost exclusively,thus learning it from him is not too much trouble.

[edit] Digimon World 2

Blue Meramon can digivolve from Meramon.

[edit] Digimon World 3

BlueMeramon is one of the Fire Knight's Digimon that the player fights in the Fire Dungeon.
