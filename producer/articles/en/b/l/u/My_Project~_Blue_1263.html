A cinematic mesh of guitar and keyboards interlaced with raspy, off-kilter vocals, my project: blue is a new wave acoustic project fronted by Chad Blue. My Project: Blue has been steadily gaining a devote following all along North America's west coast. In 2005 the band released their debut EP through Boompa Records. Produced by Scott Walker of The Salteens, the EP is a synth-obscured landscape awash in shimmering guitars and throbbing percussion. The Bowie-esque space ballads are steadily anchored by Chad Blue's endearing vocal eccentricities.
