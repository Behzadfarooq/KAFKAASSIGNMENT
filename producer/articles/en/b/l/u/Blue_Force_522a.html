[edit] Synopsis

The player is a rookie police officer called Jake Ryan. His father was also a cop and the reason why the player even joined the force. Unfortunately, he was killed and his case has not yet been solved. While playing the game, Jake uncovers clues to his father's murder.

Ryan graduates at the top of his class and joins the Jackson Beach PD, the same force his dad was on. He makes several arrests in connection with a National Guard armoury break-in. Just as he is about to tie these crimes in with his father's murder, Ryan is T-boned by a car while riding his police motorcyle. After spending weeks and possibly months in rehab, his father's old partner convinces him to become his assistant at his PI firm, which Ryan accepts. Eventually, the two find a massive gun smuggling run, tied to three main individuals- a man named Bradford Green, as well as the Jackson Beach DA, Stuart Cox, and your parent's killer, Nico. Nico gets the death penalty, Green gets 20 years, and Cox gets 15 years.
