Blues Brothers is the 14th episode of the hit Nickelodeon sitcom Drake & Josh. It first aired on September 12th, 2004. Though it first aired as a 45 minute episode, in reruns it is only shown for 30 minutes.

[edit] Plot synopsis

Spoiler warning: Plot and/or ending details follow.

Walter gets Josh a job at the weather station where he works. Megan gets jealous and reminds Josh of the fourth-grade twitch, where he embarrassed himself in front of everyone. Josh gets nervous and starts twitching. Meanwhile, Drake and his band are practicing at The Premiere for the talent show, where they are singing "I Found a Way." On the talent show, the song is stolen and acted upon by someone else. Drake asks his mother to get Drake and Josh's Blues Brothers costumes, and they finally sing "Soul Man" as a tribute to the Blues Brothers. Drake and Josh win the talent show, and a girl says about a three-feat she predicted. Thus, the talent show is won by the brothers and Josh gets over his twitching problem.

[edit] Trivia

  • Drake says, at the start of the song "Soul Man," that it's a tribute to the blues brothers. However, this has not been listed.
  • One of the 2 guys at The Premiere who is teasing Josh about his twitch is actually played by a short haired Jake Morrow who in later episodes plays mulleted Gavin.
