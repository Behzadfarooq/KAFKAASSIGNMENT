BlueStar Studios

In 2006 I started work on another large project. On my 21st birthday, I announced the opening of BlueStar Studios – my own music production studio. The studio itself is where all my work takes place. It is where new songs receive their pilot versions and where the first arrangements are written. It is also there that those songs evolve into bigger projects, and finally, it’s also in this studio that they receive their final cut before entering the recording studio to record the vocals and work on the final mix. From the first days of the studios activity, I have been making sure that every song gets the finest touch possible, making each song unique and one of a kind. Just like my father, I am very dedicated to professionalism. I never go for any compromise when dealing with quality. Everything has to be “the best”! And that is probably why my work has been inspiring people from the very beginning.

Maybe BlueStar Studios is not big but as they say “size doesn’t matter”. I work on the following equipment: -A serious fast computer with loads of RAM, and ENORMOUS amounts of disk space, -A gigantic library of Virtual Instruments and Samples. -Roland Digital Piano: RD-1000 -Yamaha ns100 studio monitors And lots of equiptment like: top shelf mics from leading companies like AKG, Audio-Technica; a Yamaha mixing console; warm and transparent sounding Yamaha pre amps; lots and lots of cables from the best.

Maybe it’s not much but it’s all I need to create music. The rest of the elements that build a song like live instruments and vocals are recorded in a big production studio like the one at the F. Chopin Music Academy in Warsaw. When everything is recorded a song is mixed and you get the final effect which you can listen to using the player at the right. :-)

Although at the moment BlueStar Studios is my private recording studio, in the future it might become a place where other producers will come to work on their own music. No matter what happens, BlueStar Studios’ goal will stay the same. We will always want to give you THE BEST music and we will not settle for less! ;-)



PEOPLE

This section is dedicated to all the people who either are a part of the BlueStar Studios family or who have lent their talent to help me create another fabulous song.

David Bawiec Songwriter, Singer, Pianist/Keyboardist, Producer, Arranger, Founder of BlueStar Studios. Read more about me in the History section.

Peter Bawiec (wyBawca) Sound Engineer, Drummer My younger brother and my right hand all along. He is the only person I trust to do the recordings and mixes. Peter is a fantastic drummer that is why he helps me in creating the drum tracks. He is also the one who gets to hear new songs first and judge them. Peter is a great help in the whole “songmaking” process.
