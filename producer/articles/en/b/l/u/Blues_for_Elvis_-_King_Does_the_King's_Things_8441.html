Blues For Elvis - King Does The King's Things is a Blues album by Albert King. The songs in this album are versions of songs previously recorded by Elvis Presley.

[edit] Track listing

  1. "Hound Dog" (Leiber/Stoller) – 4:03
  2. "That's All Right" (Crudup) – 4:08
  3. "All Shook Up" (Blackwell/Presley) – 2:29
  4. "Jailhouse Rock" (Leiber/Stoller) – 3:36
  5. "Heartbreak Hotel" (Axton/Durden/Presley) – 6:05
  6. "Don't Be Cruel" (Blackwell/Presley) – 3:27
  7. "One Night" (Bartholomew/King/Steiman) – 4:18
  8. "Blue Suede Shoes" (Perkins) – 3:16
  9. "Love Me Tender" (Matson/Presley) – 5:19

[edit] Personnel

  • Albert King – Electric guitar and vocals
  • Michael Toles – Guitar
  • Marvell Thomas – Piano and organ
  • Donald "Duck" Dunn – Bass guitar
  • James Alexander – Bass guitar
  • Willy Hall – Drums
