Bluestockings Journal (Seitô-sha) was a Japanese feminist magazine founded in 1911 by Raicho Hiratsuka. Members were known as "New Women." Originally focusing on women's literature, it later shifted focus towards women's liberation and anarchism under the editorial direction of Noe Ito, until her death in 1923.
