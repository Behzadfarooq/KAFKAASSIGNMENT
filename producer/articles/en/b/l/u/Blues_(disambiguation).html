Blues (disambiguation)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The blues is a vocal and instrumental form of music based on the use of the blue notes and a repetitive pattern. The word is also often used in musical contexts to refer to the twelve bar blues, a particular blues song form.

Blues may also refer to:

  • Royal Horse Guards (The Blues), Household Cavalry regiment of the British Army
  • Blues (Mega Man), video game character from the Mega Man series
  • The Blues (film), documentary series produced by Martin Scorsese
  • A depressed mood is often referred to as the blues
  • Blues dance, a style of social dance done to blues music
  • Blues (play), a play about the homeless in America.

[edit] Music

  • Blues (album), compilation album by guitarist Jimi Hendrix
  • The Blues (album), 1999 compilation by Eric Clapton
