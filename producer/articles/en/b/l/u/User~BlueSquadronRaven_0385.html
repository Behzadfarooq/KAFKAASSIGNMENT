BlueSquadronRaven is the nom de plume of a Wikipedia contributor living in Vancouver, British Columbia, Canada. The pseudonym is a blend of raven, a family emblem, and Blue Squadron, a fictitious squadron of space fighters from the television series Battlestar Galactica. This user also appears as BlueSquadronRaven on Wikimedia Commons.
