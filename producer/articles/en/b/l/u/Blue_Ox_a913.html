Blue Ox is an energy drink brand that was produced by the Blue Ox Company of Austria. It was introduced in 2002 to compete with Red Bull. The beverage was discontinued in 2005 due to poor sales and distribution. It came packaged in 8.4 fl oz (ml) and 16 fl oz (ml) cans.

[edit] Brand portfolio

  • Blue Ox Energy Drink (light bubblegum flavor)
  • Black Cherry Real Power
  • Orange Rush
  • Citrus Max Total Impact

The company discontinued Blue Ox and started a new drink called "BLOX" in 2006.
