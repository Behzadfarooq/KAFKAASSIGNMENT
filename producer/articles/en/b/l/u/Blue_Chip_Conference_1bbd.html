The Blue Chip Conference is a high school athletic conference in southwestern Indiana, USA. The conferences members are small high schools located in Daviess, Dubois, Gibson, Knox, and Martin counties.

[edit] Member Schools

  • Barr Reeve High School Vikings, Montgomery, IN
  • Forest Park High School Rangers, Ferdinand, IN
  • Loogootee High School Lions, Loogootee, IN
  • North Knox HIgh School Warriors, Bicknell, IN
  • Northeast Dubois High School Jeeps, Dubois, IN
  • Shoals High School Jug Rox, Shoals, IN
  • South Knox High School, Spartans, Vincennes, IN
  • Vincennes Rivet High School Patriots, Vincennes, IN
  • Washington Catholic High School Cardinals, Washington, IN
  • Wood Memorial High School Trojans, Oakland City, IN

Forest Park will leave the Blue Chip Conference to join the PAC after the 2006-07 year.
