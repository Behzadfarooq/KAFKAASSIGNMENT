Blue hair

Dyed blue hair can be a shocking fashion statement.

Blue hair refers to blue-colored hair. Since blue hair does not occur naturally in humans or other mammals, it is usually the product of using blue hair dye to color one's hair. (Many mammals are indicated as having blue hair or fur, but in all cases, it will be found to be a shade of grey.)

Blue hair is often considered to be a shocking fashion statement, and is often associated with punk and counterculture. Elderly people with greying hair also sometimes tint their hair blue to counteract yellow tints in aging hair color (see: Blue rinse). Blue is also a popular hair color for animated characters, especially in anime and video games.

The term Blue Hair is also a generic term for elderly women.

[edit] Blue hair in anime and video games

As a rule, characters in anime can have hair of any color (most commonly blue, but also pink, purple, green or one of the more "normal" colors), but nobody considers it unusual. Although it is not compulsory, characters with blue hair often have personalities that are kind, reserved, shy and sometimes intellectual.
