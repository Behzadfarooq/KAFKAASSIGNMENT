Major League Gaming ("MLG") is the first successful console video gaming league that takes place on a national level. Founded in 2002 by Michael Sepso (CEO) and Sundance Digiovanni (President), Major League Gaming is a United States based organization that hosts tournaments in major cities across the country.

As of the 2006 season, games supported by Major League Gaming include Halo 2 and Super Smash Bros. Melee. In past years, Major League Gaming also held competitions for Tekken 5, Gran Turismo 3, and Soul Calibur but all three games were discontinued from tournament play due to consistant low tunout rates.

[edit] The Community

As the Major League Gaming league grew in size, its community swelled with it as well with over 100,000 members. In addition to its [forums], numerous unofficial community creations have accompanied it as well. Projects such as The Unofficial MLG PwnCast, MLG Stats, and Gaming Lessons have all come forward in an effort to support the ever-growing MLG community.
