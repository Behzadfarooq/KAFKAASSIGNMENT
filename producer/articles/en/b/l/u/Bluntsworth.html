Bluntsworth is an American acoustic rock band from Pomona, NJ. All of the members have resided at the Richard Stockton College of NJ while with the band. Gaining some mainstream success with their first album "Amby Sessions(The)", the band has gone through various line up changes over the course of their 3 year career.

[edit] Current Status

The band is currently finising a discography re-release of their original full length, that includes unreleased material "Project 2", "Fire Delight Demo(2006)", and other unreleased and recently uncovered tracks.

[edit] Themes

The lyrics of Bluntsworth touch on common themes, often seemingly dark or depressing in nature. Other interpretations of the music shows a more positive outlook.
The Woods - The woods are often discussed in relation to the refuge they allow to outcasts.
Nature - The idea of Nature and more specifically Humans effect on Nature is discussed frequently. This struggle between Nature and Society parallels the conflict between the protagonist and his past.
