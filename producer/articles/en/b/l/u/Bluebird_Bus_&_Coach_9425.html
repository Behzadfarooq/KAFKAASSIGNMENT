Bluebird Bus & Coach

Bluebird Bus and Coach is a bus operator based in Middleton in Greater Manchester. It operates a number of commercial and supported routes in the Middleton, Higher Blackley, Moston, North Manchester area, as well as a number of supported services in the south of the county.

Their main hub is Middleton Bus Station.
