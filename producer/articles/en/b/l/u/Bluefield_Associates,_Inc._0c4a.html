Bluefield Associates, Inc. is a privately held, California Corporation, with headquarters and principal laboratories in Ontario, California - USA, established in 1980s. The Company is part of the multi-billion dollar, worldwide, cosmetic and pharmaceutical industry, and is one of the primary companies which addresses and brings attention to the special skin care and treatment methods for care of non-Caucasian skin.

[edit] History

The Company's consumer products are marketed under the brand name of Clear Essence® Skin Care Systems and the Clear Essence® Collections. The brand name enjoys good penetration and consumer recognition within the target markets. Bluefield Associates, Inc. and its Clear Essence® product lines are one of America's major cosmetic exporters, so honored and recognized by various governmental and civic organizations. The Company's distribution network is impressive and covers majority of the olive to ebony skin tone world markets - Africa, Asia, Australia, the Middle East and the Americas. In addition and due to the growing immigration and world population shifts, the Company is realizing substantial sales and new distribution in the seemingly untraditional markets of Europe; namely Germany, France, England and as far north as Sweden.

Bluefield Associates, Inc. laboratories and the manufacturing plants are OTC Pharmaceutical Level Facilities, licensed to compound and package "over the counter" (OTC) pharmaceuticals, operating under the strictest quality regimes of the U.S. Food and Drug Administration (FDA), as well as the stringent guidelines and permits by the State of California. This industry credential provides the highest level of quality control, inviting contract and private label manufacture of pharmaceuticals for companies such as Bristol-Myers, Tylenol and others, both domestic and from overseas.

Business relationships and cultural awareness are the cornerstones of every global business - a description that fits the Bluefield Associates, Inc. and the Clear Essence® world brands. The Company's management, R&D laboratories, and all the marketing groups work constantly to partner with the industry, the host markets, the vendors, retailers, distributors and the Company's loyal customers to the maximize the Company’s market positions.


[edit] Ethnic Skin Specialists

Bluefield research scientists are dedicated multi-ethnic professionals whose focus and professional specialty are cosmetic dermatology and both therapeutic and holistic skin care. The company R&D efforts benefit from the medical research facilities that exist in Southern California. Although most, if not all of the research Bluefield laboratories engage in is proprietary, their proximity to the center of medical knowledge benefits and reinforces their work. The research into skin care and the many cosmetic breakthroughs by the California science community, of which the Bluefield work is a part, are making significant contributions to worldwide lifestyle wellness and beauty.

The Bluefield scientists and clinical workers are the backbone of the Clear Essence® skincare products. Their work and findings and innovative answers to skin care have resulted in a highly proprietary collections created and compounded to serve the many shades within the world’s complexion variables - and provide sensible protection, revitalization and therapy to achieve and answer the needs of today’s active populations to combat the ravages of the environmental stress.


[edit] Laboratories & Manufacturing Facilities

In terms of ancillary research, Bluefield R&D are conducting innovative investigations into Africa's and Asia's plant and fauna resources as related to their pharmaceutical and skin care applications.

One such investigation centers on cooperative engagement with specialized botanical research facility in Cameroon, on the Atlantic South Coast of West Africa. From initial indications there are several areas which may prove extremely beneficial and may ultimately enter the Clear Essence® product lines - making them even more proprietary and providing an additional Unique Selling Proposition (USP) to the Clear Essence® brand positioning.

All of the Company's laboratories and manufacturing facilities are under the supervision of the in-house Quality Control Unit (QC), as designated by the FDA and California State regulators. The manufacturing plant is located at the Company's headquarters in Ontario, California. The plant is modern and designed to the highest industry standards. Compounding to packaging, container packing and shipping is done in-house under the supervision and surveillance of the QC staff. Under the FDA guidelines, employee education and training is paramount - it is continuous and rigorous.

[edit] Achievements and Recognition

The work and contributions made by Bluefield Associates and the Clear Essence® Collections to the lifestyle quality of their clients and dealers continue to receive recognition from the public, the beauty industry and many sectors of the U.S. Government.

[edit] Multi-Cultural Motion Picture Association

In Hollywood, the motion picture industry has recognized the work of the Bluefield cosmetology staff and management for its contribution to the television and film industry by receiving honor from the Multi-Cultural Motion Picture Association.

[edit] NAFTA Support Group

In the international arena, their work of bringing skin care benefits to the six continents has been recognized and honored by the U.S. Government by being included in NAFTA Support Group and recognized in Washington D.C. by the former President of the United States, Mr. Bill Clinton, as well as the late Secretary of Commerce, Mr. Ron Brown.

[edit] Exporter of the Year

Company executives are frequently invited by the U.S. Commerce Department to participate in international trade shows and expositions as exemplary private sector representative of U.S. cosmetic and pharmaceutical industries. Most recently, the U.S. Export-Import Bank honored Bluefield Associates with one of the highest honors United States bestows on an American private company; Bluefield Associates received the coveted “United States Exporter of The Year Award.”
