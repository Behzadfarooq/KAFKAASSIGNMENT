Blue Ball, Lancaster County, Pennsylvania

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Blue Ball is a unincorporated community in Lancaster County, Pennsylvania near 40°N 76°W. Blue Ball lies approximately 2 miles east-northeast of the town of New Holland, Pennsylvania at the intersection of US 322 and PA Route 23.
