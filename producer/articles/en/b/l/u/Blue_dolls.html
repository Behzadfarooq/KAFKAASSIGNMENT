The Blue Dolls are a musical trio specializing in Italian 1940's style vocal 'close harmony' music such as Trio Lescano. Members consist of Erika Celesti and Federica Pallante and Viviana Dragani.

The group was founded by their pianist Paolo Volante. The Blue Dolls' debut cd, "Voilà les Dolls", contains only 6 traks, 3 Italian songs of Trio Lescano e 3 songs of Andrews Sisters. In february 2007 was issued their second CD, "The Blue Dolls".

The Blue Dolls are currently supported by a four-piece band featuring Paolo Volante on piano, Marco Parodi on guitar, Luca Rigazio on drums/percussion and upright bassist Riccardo Vigorè.
