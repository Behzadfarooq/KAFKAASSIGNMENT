[edit] Daniel Boone House

Hi Michael,

The image you added of the Daniel Boone house, is, I believe, not actually the Daniel Boone house. It appears to me to be the Bertolet House, a log cabin which was moved to the site of the Daniel Boone Homestead. The actual Daniel Boone home is a nearby stone house.

Here's the Daniel Boone house from the official website; here are some other awful photos. This is not the same building as in your photo.

Here is a page with a photo of the Bertolet House, which is the same building in your photo. (You have to hover your pointer over the picture to read the caption which identifies the building.)

Did you, by chance, take any photographs of the stone Daniel Boone house, where ole Dan'l was born? --Kevin Myers | (complaint dept.) 19:58, 22 July 2006 (UTC)

Kevin,
Right you are--that is indeed the Bertolet House. Chalk this up to my misinterpretation of an Historical Place marker. Thanks for bringing this to my attention! I will root through my collection; I do believe I have a photo of the actual Boone homestead somewhere. In the meantime, I'll remove the offending image.

[edit] License tagging for Image:Frederick Fleet.gif

Thanks for uploading Image:Frederick Fleet.gif. Wikipedia gets thousands of images uploaded every day, and in order to verify that the images can be legally used on Wikipedia, the source and copyright status must be indicated. Images need to have an image tag applied to the image description page indicating the copyright status of the image. This uniform and easy-to-understand method of indicating the license status allows potential re-users of the images to know what they are allowed to do with the images.

For more information on using images, see the following pages:

  • Wikipedia:Image use policy
  • Wikipedia:Image copyright tags

This is an automated notice by OrphanBot. If you need help on selecting a tag to use, or in adding the tag to the image description, feel free to post a message at Wikipedia:Media copyright questions. 18:06, 13 August 2006 (UTC)
