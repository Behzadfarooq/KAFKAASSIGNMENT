Bluedildonics are electronic sex toys that can be controlled via wireless bluetooth. This is considered one of the more recent technological developments making teledildonics and cybersex a practical reality. A product called The Toy, an SMS-activated vibrator, recently became the first bluedildonics product to hit the market.
