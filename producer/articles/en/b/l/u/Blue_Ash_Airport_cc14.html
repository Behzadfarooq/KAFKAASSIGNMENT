Blue Ash Airport (IATA: ISZ, ICAO: KISZ), also known as Cincinnati-Blue Ash Airport, is a general aviation airport located 6 miles (10 km) northeast of the city of Cincinnati in Hamilton County, Ohio, USA.

[edit] Facilities

Blue Ash Airport covers 257 acres and has one runway:

  • Runway 6/24: 3,499 x 75 ft. (1,066 x 23 m), Surface: Asphalt
