Blue Cadet-3, Do You Connect? is Modest Mouse's first EP release. The single is very short in playtime and only around 300 copies were ever made. It is a collector's item among fans. All of the tracks on this release, however, are available on their 2001 release Sad Sappy Sucker.

[edit] Track listing

  1. "Blue Cadet-3, Do You Connect?" – 1:09
  2. "Dukes Up" – 2:24
  3. "Woodgrain" – 0:30
  4. "It Always Rains on a Picnic" – 3:01
  5. "5,4,3,2,1... Lisp Off" – 0:30

[edit] Trivia

  • The first track on the EP titled "Blue Cadet-3, Do You Connect?" is obviously the name of the album and also later the name of the same track featured on Sad Sappy Sucker. However, iTunes music store does not sell this EP album, and when receiving Sad Sappy Sucker in their buy list, they entered "Blue Cadet-3, Do You Connect?" as "Blue Cadet-3, Do You Wanna Connect?". This is a case of a typo on iTunes' behalf.
