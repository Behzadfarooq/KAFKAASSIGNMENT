Blue Nun is the fourth album by Carlene Carter. It features a couple of duets with British rocker Paul Carrack: "Oh How Happy" and "Do Me Lover."

[edit] Track listing

  1. "Oh How Happy" [with Paul Carrack] (Edwin Starr) – 2:38
  2. "Love Is a 4-Letter Verb" (Carlene Carter) – 2:39
  3. "That Boy" (Carlene Carter) – 3:20
  4. "300 Pounds of Hongry" (Donnie Fritts/Eddie Hinton) – 2:40
  5. "Tougher Stuff" (Carlene Carter/Nick Lowe) – 2:40
  6. "I Need a Hit" (Carlene Carter/James Eller) – 2:44
  7. "Rockababy" (Carlene Carter) – 2:20
  8. "When You Comin' Back?" (Count Freda) – 2:26
  9. "Me and My .38" (Carlene Carter/Nick Lowe) – 2:52
  10. "Do Me Lover" [with Paul Carrack] (Carlene Carter/Nick Lowe/James Eller) – 2:49
  11. "Too Many Teardrops" (Nick Lowe/Carlene Carter) – 2:17
  12. "Billy" (Carlene Carter/Paul Carrack) – 2:38
  13. "Born to Move" [with Paul Carrack] (John Fogerty) – 2:33
  14. "Think Dirty" (Carlene Carter) – 3:52
