Blue Matter is a studio album by jazz musician John Scofield.

[edit] Musicians

John Scofield, Hiram Bullock (electric guitar); Mitchell Forman (keyboards); Gary Grainger (electric bass); Dennis Chambers (drums); Don Alias (percussion).
