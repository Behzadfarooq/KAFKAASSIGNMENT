Main - Inactive - Mini

These articles are good but need a category. The definition of a "good" article is that it is long and has lots of links. If you can't find the correct category then just ignore it.

  • After categorizing one or more articles, you can remove them from this list.
  • Please do not add the uncategorized template to these articles.
  • A good way to find the correct category is to find similar articles and see what categories they use. Another very useful tool is Duesentrieb's CategoryTree, which is exactly what it sounds like. It can be put on the sidebar in Firefox.
  • Some of these articles had categories that were removed by a vandal; it is therefore worth checking their edit histories.
  • See Uncategorised lists for a list of all the uncategorised lists. (still unfinished)
  • Also remember to leave edit summaries with your edits, a good example would be
Added categories-[[User:Bluemoose/Uncategorised_good_articles|Help categorize good articles]]
  • For articles about people, don't forget to add the categories xxxx births and xxxx deaths (where xxxx is the year). For living people, add the articles to Category:Living people instead of Category:xxxx deaths.
