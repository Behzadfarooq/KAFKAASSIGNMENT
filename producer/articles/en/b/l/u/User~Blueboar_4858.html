[edit] Welcome to Blueboar's Home Page

This user lives in New York City. Contrary to popular belief, he did not choose the name "Blueboar" because he likes a particular English Pub or Beer (although he does). The name instead derives from the legends told at the Summer Camp where he spent many happy years as a child (and where he continues to spend as much time as possible as an adult). Every good Summer Camp has an axe murderer, vengeful ghost or horiffic, blood sucking monster that hangs out somewhere near the camp... one that usually shows up on "a night just like this!" Well, this camp has Blueboar, an ogre that lives across the lake and kills bad little campers in imaginative and very gory ways.

The User Blueboar is an amateur historian. As such, most of his edits relate to historical subjects. He also edits heavily on subject of Freemasonry. More will be told about Blueboar (both the legend and the User) as time passes (ie, when I have time to update the page.)... stay tuned.

If you wish to leave Blueboar a message, please do so on his Talk Page. If you are only interested in vandalism... you can put it where the sun doesn't shine (Which fans of Terry Prattchet will know is a small village in the Ramtops)
