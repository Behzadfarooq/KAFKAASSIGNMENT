Blumenkraft is Ott's first album. It was released in 2003 on Twisted Records.

[edit] Track listing

  1. "Jack's Cheese and Bread Snack" – 12:52
  2. "Somersettler" – 7:31
  3. "Splitting an Atom" – 7:21
  4. "Escape From Tulse Hell" – 7:12
  5. "Cley Hill" – 7:32
  6. "Billy The Kid Strikes Back" – 7:13
  7. "A Load Up at Nunney Catch" – 7:18
  8. "Spannered in Pilton" – 7:07
  9. "Smoked Glass and Chrome" – 8:34
  10. "Scilly Automatic (Bonus track)" – 8:00

[edit] Miscellanea

  • The name of the track Tulse Hell probably comes from an area in London called Tulse Hill. As Ott lives in the countryside, he may have escaped from Tulse Hill, or Tulse Hell.
