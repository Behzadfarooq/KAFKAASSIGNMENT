[edit] Gradatim Ferociter

Does anyone know what the company motto, "Gradatim Ferociter", translates to in English? --Pmetzger 01:11, 4 January 2007 (UTC)

  • With my dusty latin, I would say "Step by step with spirit". Anyone has something better ? Hektor 09:15, 4 January 2007 (UTC)
  • That's pretty much my read, Hektor: "Step By Step, Courageously" seems to be the version folks are quoting the most. Please see discussion by MSNBC science editor Alan Boyle, particularly his reply ten posts down on his 1/3/07 Cosmic Log entry: http://cosmiclog.msnbc.msn.com/archive/2007/01/03/26062.aspx --Wm wikify 06:14, 8 January 2007 (UTC)
  • Of course, it could also mean something like "Step By Step, Ferociously" which has a nice ironic stubborness to it. :> --Wm wikify 06:14, 8 January 2007 (UTC)
  • Yeah, my read is "gradually, with spirit" Mwhiz 17:24, 11 January 2007 (UTC)
