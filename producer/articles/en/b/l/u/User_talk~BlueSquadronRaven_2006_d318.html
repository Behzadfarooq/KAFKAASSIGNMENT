Twilight Secret Star Guy, vandal

I've declined to block, or even warn, User:Twilight Secret Star Guy. I don't know what you think he's doing that's vandalism, but I do see that you have a funny idea of what vandalism is. Let me remind you that no one "owns" wikipedia articles. This bunch of changes to Atomic Betty is in no way vandalism. Note the difference between productive edits you disagree with and unproductive edits purposely designed to harm the encyclopedia. To deal with edits of the first type, please interact with other editors on the talk page or on their user talk page to try to reach an understanding. For the latter, leave vandalism warnings (see the top of WP:AIV) before reporting. Mangojuicetalk 18:17, 26 July 2006 (UTC)

User:Twilight Secret Star Guy was blocked indefinitely as of 2 August 2006, as a suspected sock puppet of MascotGuy, being used to circumvent the latter's permanent ban. This was precisely the reason I reported him, to prevent the inevitable string of unverifiable information that he attempts to slip into the articles he edits, in accordance with banning policy. As for the above cited example of my editing style, I will let my other edits, corrections, and additions to Atomic Betty speak for themselves. --BlueSquadronRaven 15:44, 2 August 2006 (UTC)

We have recategorized these vandals according to the simplest criteria: whether they attack the page address (pagemove), or the page content (pageblank).

Accordingly, we have redefined MascotGuy as simply a Pageblank vandal; consequently, he does not need a specific template. I will, however, refrain from reverting the template until I've heard from you. DS 00:58, 29 August 2006 (UTC)
