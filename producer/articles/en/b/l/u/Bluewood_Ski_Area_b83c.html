Bluewood Ski Area
Location: Dayton, Washington, US
Top elevation: 5,670 ft
Base elevation: 4,545 ft
Skiable area: 1,125
Runs: 24
Lift system: 2 chairs, 1 surface lift
Snowfall: 25 ft
Web site: Bluewood Ski Area

The Bluewood Ski Area is a ski resort located in southeastern Washington, United States. The base elevation is at 4,545 feet with the peak at 5,670 feet.
