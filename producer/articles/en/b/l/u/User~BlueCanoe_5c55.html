I live in Seattle, Washington, and I've also lived in Oregon, Colorado, and Minnesota. I have a M.S. in geology. I post photos on Flickr. I made my first Wikipedia edit on December 20, 2004.

I am an active contributor to BWCAWiki, a wiki about the Boundary Waters Canoe Area Wilderness in northeastern Minnesota. You can contribute!

[edit] Work on Wikipedia

[edit] Past edits

I enjoy contributing to articles on geology and geography of locations I've been to (or would like to visit):

  • on the Colorado Plateau: the Dirty Devil River, Paria River, Paria Canyon-Vermilion Cliffs Wilderness, San Rafael Swell
  • the Rocky Mountains: Pike National Forest
  • the Pacific Northwest: Ross Lake, North Cascades National Park, Alpine Lakes Wilderness, Goat Rocks
  • Minnesota: Anoka, Grand Marais, Ely, the Gunflint Trail, the Boundary Waters Canoe Area Wilderness, Boundary Waters, Voyageurs National Park, Isle Royale National Park (actually MI), Grand Portage National Monument, North Shore (Lake Superior), Superior Hiking Trail, North Country Trail
  • Canada: Quetico Provincial Park, Bowron Lake Provincial Park
  • and elsewhere: Bodø, Norway

...adding to geological articles of interest:

  • cosmogenic isotope, G. K. Gilbert

...improving articles on hobbies and interests of mine:

  • dunkel, spruce beer, Duluth pack, tumpline, the Wilderness Act of 1964, Sigurd Olson

...as well as correcting spelling and grammar on encountered pages when necessary.
