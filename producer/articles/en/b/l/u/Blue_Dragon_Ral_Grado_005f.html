Blue Dragon RalΩGrado (ブルードラゴン ラルΩグラド, Burū Doragon RaruΩGurado?) is a manga by Tsuneo Takano and Takeshi Obata. The series is based off of the Blue Dragon video game and runs weekly in Japan's Weekly Shonen Jump magazine. It was first published by Shueisha in December 2006.

The story is about a young man by the name of Ral, in the kingdom of Sphaelite (スフエライト, Suferaito?). Ral, is a special boy who has a symbiotic relationship with the shadow of a giant blue dragon named Grado.

It is divided into chapters referred to as Tales (テイル, Teiru?).

[edit] Story

In a world where demonic creatures named shadows enter our realm through their very namesakes, little is safe. However, when a young boy by the name of Ral becomes friends with the shadow inside him, he may be the last hope in saving the world. Ral lives on the island of Sphaein, in the kingdom of Sphaelite. There he has been enlisted to protect the kingdom from evil shadows that wish to destroy it. With the aid of his shadow, a large blue dragon named Grado, and his teacher, Mio, Ral can take on any challenger.

[edit] Main characters

  • Ral (ラル, Raru?)
Ral is the main character, a 15 year old boy who shares a symbiotic relationship with a shadow demon named Grado. His mother died shortly after his birth, leaving him an orphan. Upon his discovery, still an infant child, he began screaming at his saviors in very well constructed phrases. From there a large shadow demon in the shape of a blue dragon emerged from his body. The shadow destroyed his village and burned all the mountains in sight. The child was later found by a great knight named Roy. Lord Roy sealed Ral in a prison of complete darkness for 15 years. During this time he became friends with the shadow named Grado and his educator, Mio. His imprisonment was ended abruptly when the kingdom was attacked by a large number of shadow demons. Lord Roy gave the order to free Ral after Mio stated he had control over his shadow.
Ral loves women and will do anything for them. His main goal is to protect all the beautiful women of the world. Ral also has a fondness for breasts, constantly trying to fondle them.
  • Grado (グラド, Gurado?)
Grado is a shadow who takes the shape of a giant blue dragon. Once a loyal shadow, Grado chose not to follow the shadow queen, lady Bira, in her attempts to destroy the humans. Thusly, Grado has become an enemy to the shadows. Grado hopes to free the shadows of lady Bira's tyranny. Thanks to Ral's help, Grado is able to fight shadows in the human world, on a path to destroy Lady Bira.
  • Mio (ミォ, Mio?)
Ral's teacher, Mio is a beautiful and well educated woman of Sphaelite. She was the one who originally convinced Ral to fight the shadows and continues to make him focus on his goal.
  • Lady Bira (ビラ様, Bira-sama?)
Lady Bira, the queen of darkness also known as Queen Obscuria (オプスキュリア, Obusukyuria?), rules the shadows from the castle Jugil, inside the kingdom of Kabil. Bira and her lust for beauty are the reason why the shadows entered the human world. She collects and possesses beautiful girls, sucking their soul orbs from them and turning them into cripples and invalids. The shadows in her court wish for everything from her hair to her saliva in order to become stronger.

[edit] Other Hosts and Shadows

  • Marelo (マレロ, Marero?)
A host to a shadow of the first form. Like Ral, Marelo was sealed away. After seeing a demonstration of his strength, Ral asks Marelo to stay behind and protect the castle in his stead.
  • Gorbago (ゴルバゴ, Gorubago?)
Marelo's shadow partner. He claims he will do anything to see Obscuria killed so that he can return to Noir, the world of the shadows.
  • Aya (アイア, Aia?)
A host to a shadow of the first form. She was also sealed away like Ral and is thirteen years old. Ral accepts her immediately without seeing her shadow's powers because she looks so cute.
  • Kuru Kuru (クルクル, Kuru Kuru?)
Aya's chameleon-like shadow partner. He can stretch his shadow up to sixty machirs and doesn't make a sound when he walks. Aya also claims he likes it when she pulls his tongue.
  • Kafka (カフカ, Kafuka?)
Kafka the flower binder, (also known as Kafka la Chaîne Rode (シェンネロド, Shennerodo?)), is a very chivalrous knight of Stora Castle. To defend the honor of his beloved Queen, Kafka even went so far as to duel Ral.
  • Reeds (リズ, Rizu?)
Reeds is Kafka's partner shadow. Using powers that obviously derived his name, Reeds has amazing defensive capabilities and can extend thorn covered roots to constrict its enemies.

[edit] Regular Humans

  • Lord Roy (ロイ, Roi?)
A knight and lord who had found Ral as a young child. Roy imprisoned Ral in complete darkness to seal the shadow that slept within the boy. Lord Roy also gave the order that set Ral free. Ral attacked him immediately after as Ral and Grado had decided to do upon release. He was not seen as a father to Ral, who asked "What kind of father would do that to his son?"

[edit] Shadows

A shadow is a demon like creature born in the world of darkness. They have no dimensions and no form. The Humans believe them to be evil beings god sent away into the darkness. Eventually these shadows came into the human world, the world of light. They enter through the shadows of creatures and take shape. A shadow cannot take form in complete darkness. There are three forms that shadows can take in the world of light.

  • The Parasite Form - Also known as the First Form. This form is made when a creature allows a shadow to take shape in its shadow. In this form the shadow is like a symbiotic parasite, only coming out when the host gives it permission.
  • The Corroded Form - The Second Form takes place after a shadow has ate the creature from the inside out. In this form, the shadow makes the spirit and flesh of the creature its own. The shadow is free to take two separate shapes in this form, either the creatures normal shape or its shadow shape.
  • The Augmentative Form - The Third Form which is the most powerful form. In the augmentative form the shadow can eat other creatures and turn the power of the creatures into their own. The shadow can then take a shape of any creature it has eaten as well as mix shapes.
  • The Friends Form - A special fusion form which Ral uses, similar to the parasitic form in its symbiotic nature. The host creature receives the flesh and blood of the shadow itself. The host can then use the abilities of the shadow.
