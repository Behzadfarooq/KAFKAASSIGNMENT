[edit] Storyline

Blue Space is the nickname of a nascent Space Agency, the United Nations Space Agency (UNSA). This agency has a corps of astronauts headed by Ieronimus. The original seven astronauts from the Agency are Katrine, Guillermo, Hayao, Asia, Adam, Victor, and Val. They are sent to rescue the Moon base of the UNSA, the International Mobile Moon Base, which has been heavily damaged in a strange accident while trying to retrieve a container of samples. Very quickly, it appears that a mysterious organization called Trust his behind the odd events which are taking place on the Moon.
