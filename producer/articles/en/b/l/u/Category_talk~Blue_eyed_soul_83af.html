[edit] Rename

Shouldn't the category name change to something more effective and encyclopedic. Just a thought. 70.144.216.244 12:39, 20 February 2006 (UTC)

I'd wondered about that myself. "Blue Eyed Soul" fits four different subgenres, actually:
  • "White Soul", like Eric Burdon and the Box Tops
  • White people doing "soul" music - from the sublime (Van Morrison) to the ridiculous (Michael Bolton)
  • R and B singers who happen to be white - from Teena Marie to Britney Spears.
  • British "White soul" of the '80's - from Allison Moyet to Rick Astley to, yes, Boy George.

Any term that includes Alex Chilton AND Eric Burdon AND Boy George is basically meaningless.

If it's OK with all of you, I might rewrite some stuff here. Mitchberg 09:28, 25 February 2006 (UTC)
