Blue Skies is a jazz album by Cassandra Wilson. It was released on the JMT label in 1988.

  5. "Gee Baby, Ain't I Good to You" (Andy Razaf, Don Redman) – 5:04
  6. "I'm Old Fashioned" (Jerome Kern, Johnny Mercer) – 3:07
  7. "Sweet Lorraine" (Carter Burwell, Mitchell Parish) – 5:31
