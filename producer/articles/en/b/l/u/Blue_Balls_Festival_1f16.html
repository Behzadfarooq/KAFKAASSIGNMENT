The Blue Balls Festival is an annual music festival held in Lucerne, Switzerland. It has featured many famous musicians within the last few years, including KT Tunstall, Jonny Lang, UB40, Van Morrison, Jeff Healy, Jimmy Cliff, Bobby McFerrin, Billy Idol, Tracy Chapman, Jethro Tull, BB King, Bonnie Raitt, among many others.
