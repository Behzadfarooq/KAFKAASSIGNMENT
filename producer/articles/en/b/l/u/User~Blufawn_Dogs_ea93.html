[edit] My Life With Dogs

I have a great interest in dogs. I was a C-section born 4 weeks early, because if I was born on time it would mean that my mother would miss Crufts, so I was re-scheduled. I went to my first dog show at 2 weeks old and attended crufts for the first time aged 4weeks. I have been going to dog shows ever since and my son, Finley attended his first dog show at 2 weeks old also, but was 6months gone before his first Crufts. Now I am older I own two very lovely Scottish Deerhounds and a Tamaskan Dog.


[edit] How I Plan to Use my Skills for Wikipedia

I enjoy editing articles on most of the hound breeds, I have a great deal of knowlegde about some of the older, more rare breeds of hound and a few that are extinct. I may branch out into other doggy groups but I have a lot to do in hounds first of all. I have quite a number of hound referance books.

I also enjoy just randomly reading through all the differant breeds of dog I have never heard of before, and Wikipedia is a great place to find out about the more exotic kinds of dogs.
