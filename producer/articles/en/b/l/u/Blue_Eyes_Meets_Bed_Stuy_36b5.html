Blue Eyes Meets Bed Stuy
Studio album (bootleg) by The Notorious B.I.G. & Frank Sinatra
Released 2005
Genre Hip hop, Swing jazz, Mashup
Label Dirty A-Rabs
Producer(s) DJ Cappel & Smitty

Blue Eyes Meets Bed Stuy is a bootleg recording presented by Jon Moskowitz and produced by DJ Cappel & Smitty. It mixes vocals from hip hop legend The Notorious B.I.G. with the instrumentation of Frank Sinatra songs. Occasionally, Frank Sinatra vocals are thrown into the songs though.

[edit] Track list

  1. "Juicy - New York, New York"
    • Also contains samples from "Sky's the Limit" by The Notorious B.I.G.
  2. "Everyday Struggle - A Day in the Life of a Fool"
  3. "Nasty Boy - For Every Man There's a Woman"
  4. "Come On - My Way of Life"
  5. "Interlude - The World We Know (Over & Over)"
  6. "Dead Wrong - In My Room (Nancy Sinatra)"
  7. "Let's Get It On (feat. 2Pac) - Rain in My Heart"
  8. "Hypnotize - Little Green Apples"
  9. "Interlude"
  10. "10 Crack Commandments - Fools Rush In"
  11. "Runnin/Victory 2004 - A Long Night"
  12. "Unfoolish (feat. Ashanti) - Out Beyond the Window"
    • Also contains sample from "Fuckin' You Tonight" by The Notorious B.I.G.
