Blue Water Arena or Esbjerg Idrætspark is a football stadium and the home ground of Esbjerg fB. It has a capacity of 13,282.

The stadium consists of two long-side stands:

  • The Old Stand
  • The New Stand:
    • Lower area, The Claus Sørensen Section
    • Upper area consist of two sections:
      • The Vestfrost Section
      • The Viking Section
