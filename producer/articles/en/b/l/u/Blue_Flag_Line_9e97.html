[edit] Route

The service runs between Nonthaburi and Sathon districts in Bangkok. At Sathorn pier in Sathon districts is an interchange, where passengers able to change boats or change their transportaton to ferry, longtail boat, skytrain and bus.

[edit] Piers

  1. Nonthaburi (Pibul 3) - Terminal and a large market of the city
  2. Wang Lang (Siriraj) - Siriraj Hospital and ladies market
  3. Sathorn (Taksin Bridge) - Terminal, interchange and terminal of BTS Silom line
