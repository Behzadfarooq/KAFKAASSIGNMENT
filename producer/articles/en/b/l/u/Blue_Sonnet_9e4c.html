Red Fang Blue Sonnet (紅い牙 ブルーソネット, Akai Kiba Burū Sonetto?) is a 19-volume manga series by Masahiro Shibata which ran in Hana to Yume magazine from 1981 to 1987 as part of the Akai Kiba series of stories. A five episode anime OVA series was adapted from the manga and released in 1989.
