Blue Bunny is an American ice cream brand owned by Wells' Dairy, Inc. based in Le Mars, Iowa. Wells’ Dairy was founded in 1913 by Fred H. Wells when he purchased for $250 a horse, delivery wagon, a few cans and jars, and the goodwill of the business from dairy farmer Ray Bowers. This contract granted Fred H. Wells a milk distribution route, as well as a guaranteed source of raw milk from Mr. Bowers’ herd of 15 milk cows.

Around 1925, Fred H. Wells and his sons began manufacturing ice cream. Over the next few years, they distributed the frozen treats in towns throughout Iowa including Remsen and Alton and Sioux City. In 1928 Fairmont Ice Cream purchased the ice cream distribution system in Sioux City along with the right to use the Wells' name.

In 1935, the Wells family decided to sell ice cream in Sioux City again. Unable to sell their product under the name Wells, they decided to hold a “Name That Ice Cream” contest in the Sioux City Journal. A Sioux City man won the $25 cash prize for the winning entry of "Blue Bunny" after noticing how much his son enjoyed the blue bunnies in a department store window at Easter.

Still based in Le Mars, Wells’ Dairy, Inc. is the world’s largest family-owned and managed dairy processor. In addition, it has made Le Mars the “Ice Cream Capital of the World,” since more ice cream is manufactured in this one location than anywhere else on the planet

Blue Bunny has nationwide distribution of its frozen and dairy products.

[edit] Flavors

Wells’ Dairy, Inc. produces more than 500 different Blue Bunny branded ice cream flavors, frozen novelties, and fresh dairy products. The signature Blue Bunny ice cream flavor Bunny Tracks® combines chocolate-covered peanuts, peanut butter-filled chocolate bunnies, and ribbons of peanut butter, caramel, and fudge in vanilla ice cream. In addition to many other innovative ice cream flavors, Blue Bunny offers seven different varieties of vanilla.

Blue Bunny manufactures various novelties, including The Champ!® cones and FrozFruit® bars. Another Blue Bunny novelty, the Bomb Pop® is even celebrated with its own special day, National Bomb Pop Day, held annually on the last Thursday in June.

Responding to a need for lighter ice cream options, Blue Bunny has created a host of products that are low calorie, fat free, or light options. Specific lines include Sweet Freedom®, which has no sugar added; and HealthSmart®, which has no fat and no sugar added.

[edit] Blue Bunny Yogurt

Along with frozen treats, Blue Bunny also makes fresh dairy products like milk, yogurt, and sour cream. Yogurt varieties come in several product lines, including Lite 85®, Lite 85® SuperFruits™ and Sweet Freedom®. All Blue Bunny yogurts now include probiotics, stronger live cultures that can actually enhance your body's immune system and digestive health. Wells' Dairy hired Kristi Yamaguchi to help promote Blue Bunny's® new Lite 85® Superfruit™ yogurt. Taking some time off from skating after having a baby this past year, Yamaguchi got on board with Wells' Dairy about a month ago to promote the new yogurts.
