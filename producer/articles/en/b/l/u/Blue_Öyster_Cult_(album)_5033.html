Blue Öyster Cult is the self-titled debut by hard rock band, the Blue Öyster Cult, released in 1972.

  • Allen Lanier: rhythm guitar, keyboards

[edit] Notes

On the original record version of Tyranny and Mutation, one side had the original black text-on-red background style for the label of side A (called "The Black") where side B ("The Red") had the opposite - a black background with red text. On the 2001 remasters of this album and of Tyranny and Mutation, this red-on-black style is used on the disc. (Secret Treaties and Agents of Fortune use the black-on-red style.)
