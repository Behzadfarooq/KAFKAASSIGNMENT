Usage:

  • {{Wdefcon}} or {{Wdefcon|prefix=User:Zsinj/}} - Cool Cat's original version (the default one, shown at right), now in Zsinj's userspace.
    • To align the standard template, use {{wdefcon|align=<left or center, right is default>}}
  • {{Wdefcon|prefix=User:Misza13/}} - Misza13's userbox version (see talk page)
    • {{Wdefcon|prefix=User:Digitalme/}} Similar to Misza13's, but black.
  • {{Wdefcon|prefix=User:Herostratus/}} - Herostratus's version, similar size to Misza13's but different look (see talk page)
  • {{Wdefcon|prefix=User:Davidpk212/}} - Davidpk212's version, styled after a PH meter (see talk page)
  • {{Wdefcon|prefix=User:Hexagon1/}} - Hexagon1's version, very similar to Misza13's but without the comment, and appears more like a real userbox (see talk page)
    • {{Wdefcon|prefix=User talk:Hexagon1/}} - Hexagon1's second version, a plain-text level number. (see talk page)
  • {{Wdefcon|prefix=User talk:ILovePlankton/}} similar to Herostratus's but wider.
  • {{Wdefcon|prefix=User:Bugs5382/}} based off the United States Department of Homeland Security Terrorist Meter
  • {{Wdefcon|prefix=User:Herostratus/Nautical/}} based on maritime weather warning flags
  • {{Wdefcon|prefix=User:Timrem/}} - Timrem's userbox version (see talk page)
