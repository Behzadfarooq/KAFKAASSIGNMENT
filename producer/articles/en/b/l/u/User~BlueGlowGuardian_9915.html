My user name on the Wikipedia is BlueGlowGuardian, obviously. I chose the name because I enjoy the color blue, I enjoy a good glow, and the word guardian sounded amusing. I have edited pages on this grand encyclopedia from time to time. I typically search for esoteric topics and click until I stumble upon an article that I am knowledgeable of and edit any errors I find. I am a younger fellow. I am amused by how many people are unaware of things that happen everyday.

These days I tend to remain anonymous and engage in anonymous activities using pseudonyms. Few photographs exist to the public that reveal my entire face. It isn't for safety, I just think it's fun.
