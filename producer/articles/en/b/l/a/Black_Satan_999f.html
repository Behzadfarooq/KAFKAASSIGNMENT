[edit] Fictional history

The Shocker Organization has been recreated under the alias of the Black Satan.

[edit] Membership

  • Satan Bug - The leader of Black Satan.
  • Mr. Titan (1-13)/Hundred-Eye Titan (17) - A one-eyed high-rank officer of Black Satan. He and General Shadow are rivals. He can disguise himself as a normal human. He was defeated by Kamen Rider Stronger only to return later on as the Hundred-Eye Titan. In this form, he can throw his smaller eyes as if they were grenades after peeling them off. He was finally defeated by Kamen Rider Stronger again.
  • General Shadow - A high-ranking officer who is rivals with Mr. Titan. After the destruction of Black Satan, he creates the Delza Army sometime after abandoning the Black Satan organization.
  • Dead Lion - A lion monster from Egypt who is one of the high-ranking officers of Black Satan. He took over after Mr. Titan's destruction.
  • Black Satan's Soldiers - Black cat men who serve as the foot soldiers of Black Satan.

[edit] Kikaijin
