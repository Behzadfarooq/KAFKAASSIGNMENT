[edit] Pause

This nomination is "paused" at the moment. An admin expressed concern about the viability of a large batch AfD, no matter how just the nomination may be. I expect a re-formed nomination will occur shortly. Sorry for the inconvenience. As to your question, I don't think that the Web Cartoonist's Choice Awards would qualify as a primary source, and I haven't done any research as to if there are reliable independant sources that would cite the award. I'm leaning towards no, but that's just my uninformed opinion. /Blaxthos 04:13, 19 January 2007 (UTC)

[edit] WCCA and Notability

I would think that it might be good enough to meet the second point of WP:WEB. From the WCCA article: The WCCA represents a form of peer recognition since voting membership is open, only, to the sole creator, writer, artist (including colorists), or webmaster of an online webcomic.
