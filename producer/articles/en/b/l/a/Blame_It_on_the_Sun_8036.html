"Blame It on the Sun" is one of the songs of the legendary musician Stevie Wonder. "Blame It on the Sun" appeared for the first time on Stevie Wonder's famous early 1970's album Talking Book. It also appeared as the B-Side to Stevie's Innervisions single "Don't You Worry 'bout A Thing". The two songwriters were Stevie Wonder and Syreeta Wright. Lani Groves and Jim Gilstrap provided the background vocals.

[edit] American Idol Performances

"Blame It on the Sun" has been performed on American Idol only once, in Season 5. It was performed by Kellie Pickler.
