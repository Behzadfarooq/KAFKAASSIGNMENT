Blackthorne Publishing

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Blackthorne Publishing was a publisher that specialized in comic books and comic strips that existed in from about 1986-89.

Cover to Battle Beasts #1, published by Blackthorne Publishing in 1988.
