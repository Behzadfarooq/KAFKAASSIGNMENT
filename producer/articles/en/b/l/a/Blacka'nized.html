Blacka'nized

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Blacka'nized were a hip hop group from the mid 90's, comprising of Joseph Malik and A.J Nutall, hailing from Edinburgh

[edit] History

Joseph Malik was born in Glasgow but moved through to Edinburgh in the early 90’s after going to the capital for the 1993 Internationl Arts Festival and deciding not to go back to Glasgow. While in Edinburgh Joseph Malik hooked up with producer A.J Nutall, an old friend from the early 80’s hip hop scene.

MF Outa’National was the first incarnation of the collaboration between the two and under this name their track ‘Miles Out of Time’ was released on the infamous ‘Mo’ Wax Headz Vol. 1’ album.

In 1994 Malik and A.J came to the home of the Stereo MCs, and fellow DJ’s Sace and Easi label ‘Response’. It was here that Malik and A.J took the name Blacka’nized and a number of e.p’s and album projects were released to great acclaimed. In 1996 Blacka’nized left Response to set up their own label in Edinburgh called ‘Yush2K’. At the Yusk2K label they continued to produced acclaimed work and also help produced other up and coming artists such as Scotland Yard Emcees and a young KT Tunstall.
