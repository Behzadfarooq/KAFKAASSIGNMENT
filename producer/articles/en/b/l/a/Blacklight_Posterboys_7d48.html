Blacklight Posterboys is a pop/rock band formed in Atlanta, Georgia in 1996.

The Blacklight Posterboys' single "If The Animals Could Talk" was released (after it was chosen out of over 5000 songs) on the soundtrack to Road Rash:Jailbreak, the sequel to Electronic Arts' hugely popular Sony Playstation game.

One of The Blacklight Posterboys' Album Covers

[edit] Members

  • Mark Martino: Lead Guitar (Lead Vocals),
  • Reg Rogers: Drums (Vocals),
  • Curtis Clark: Lead Vocals (Rhythm Guitar),
  • James Britt: Bass (Vocals)
