Dissenting opinion

The WP:VAND policy specifically states that NPOV violations are not vandalism. Therefor it was inappropriate to use a vandalism template in response to this edit. Further, jumping directly to 'test3' with no prior warnings or explanation is also clearly inappropriate even if this had been vandalism.

Obviously Blainetologist needs to take a good look at our neutral point of view policy and his response to provocation certainly violated civility and personal attack standards, but some of that provocation was unjustified and thus a minor civility issue and a significant don't bite the newbies problem itself. We're supposed to be helping newcomers learn the ropes, not antagonizing them into becoming trolls. --CBDunkerson 13:17, 12 May 2006 (UTC)
