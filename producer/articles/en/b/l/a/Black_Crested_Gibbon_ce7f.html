The Black Crested Gibbon (Nomascus concolor), also known as the Crested Gibbon, the Black Gibbon, the White-cheeked Gibbon, or the Concolor Gibbon, is an endangered species of gibbon found in India, the Malay archipelago and Indochina. There are 4 subspecies.
