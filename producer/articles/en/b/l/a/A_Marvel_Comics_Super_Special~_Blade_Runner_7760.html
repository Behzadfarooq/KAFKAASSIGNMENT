A Marvel Comics Super Special: Blade Runner

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Cover art by Jim Steranko.

Marvel Comics Super Special: Blade Runner is the comic book adaptation of the film, Blade Runner published by Marvel Comics in 1982. It was written by Archie Goodwin with art by Al Williamson and Ralph Reese.

This was issue 22 of the Marvel Comics Super Special series of titles which by this time only printed Marvel's movie adaptations. It was reprinted in a two issue mini series but without the feature content contained in the special.

In the UK it was reprinted as the Blade Runner Annual published by Grandreams. Again, the feature content of the original special was not reprinted.
