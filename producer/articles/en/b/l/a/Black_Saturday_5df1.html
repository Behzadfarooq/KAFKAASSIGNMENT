Black Saturday

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Black Saturday refers to several events:

  • Black Saturday (1621), a term used in Scotland
  • Black Saturday (Lebanon), the December, 1975 massacre that helped precipitate the Lebanese Civil War
  • Black Saturday (1983), the day Hong Kong exchange rates fell to an all time low
  • Black Saturday (1984), a term used for when Vince McMahon raided WTBS wrestling programming
  • Black Saturday (2004), the 2004 Yanbu attack
  • Black Saturday (France), big traffic problems in France when they go on holiday
  • In The Philippines, Holy Saturday is referred to as "Black Saturday".
  • Black Saturday (Cuban Missile Crisis), possibly the closest the world got to Nuclear War.
  • Black Saturday (26 Jan 1952) After guerilla attacks on their bases in Egypt.
  • Black Saturday (Operation Agatha), after a British authorities operation in 1946 against Jewish military organizations in the Mandate of Palestine.
