Blake Morrow

Blake Morrow is a resident of Huntsville, TN, and one of the top five young persons to ever be accepted to practice law in the State of Tennessee. He entered college at age 16 and was accepted into a dual degree program. In 2006 he graduated with his BS/JD and took the bar exam and passed his first attempt.

His family has been the Mortuary business since the early 1940s and he followed suit while in School earning his Law Degree he attended Mortuary College on a part time basis and earned his Mortuary Degree and is a licensed Embalmer in the State of Tennessee. His family recently completed work on a new facility in the Commonwealth of Kentucky and he is earning his Mortuary License in the Commonwealth.

He is the grandson of prominent figures in his town and state history. He is the godson of former of US Senator Baker. His grandfather started the mortuary chain which has grown into a large business. His family has helped shape his counties history.
