The Blanchard Valley Conference is an OHSAA athletic league located in Hancock, Hardin, and Putnam Counties in northwest Ohio. Its name derives from the Blanchard River, which runs through the area in which the schools are located. The following are the current member school districts of the league (with communities in the district in parentheses after):

  • Arcadia Redskins (Arcadia)
  • Arlington Red Devils (Arlington)
  • Cory-Rawson Hornets (Mount Cory and Rawson)
  • Hardin-Northern Polar Bears (Dunkirk)
  • Leipsic Vikings (Leipsic)
  • Liberty-Benton Eagles (Liberty Township and Benton Ridge)
  • McComb Panthers (McComb)
  • Pandora-Gilboa Rockets (Pandora and Gilboa)
  • Van Buren Black Knights (Van Buren)
  • Vanlue Wildcats (Vanlue)

Leipsic and Pandora-Gilboa also have membership affiliations with the Putnam County League, although the BVC is the primary conference of both schools.
