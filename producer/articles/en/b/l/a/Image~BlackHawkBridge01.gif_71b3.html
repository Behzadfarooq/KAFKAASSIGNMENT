[edit] Caption

Black Hawk Bridge, Lansing, Iowa, 1995

[edit] Summary

This image was found at the Library of Congress HAER archive entry with the original caption: "1. 3/4 VIEW FROM TOP OF BLUFF, FROM NORTHWEST HAER IOWA,3-LANS,1-1"

It is a part of the Historic American Engineering Record collection of photos. Image taken by Joe Elliott, Summer 1995.

[edit] Note found on caption page

These photographs are Government material and are not subject to copyright. However, the courtesy of a credit line identifying the Historic American Engineering Record and the photographer would be appreciated.
