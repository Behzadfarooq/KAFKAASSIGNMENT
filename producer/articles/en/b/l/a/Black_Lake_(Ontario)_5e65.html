Black Lake is a lake located in Haliburton County, Ontario. The lake is approximately 2 kilometres long and 1 kilometre at its widest point. It has one privately owned island. Access is via two privately maintained roads leading east off of highway 35, between Norland and Minden, Ontario. There is a shared dock and approximately 100 cottages on the lake.
