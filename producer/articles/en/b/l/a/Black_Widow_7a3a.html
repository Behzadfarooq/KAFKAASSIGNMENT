Black Widow may refer to:

In wildlife:

  • The black widow spider, the common name for several species of venomous spider
  • False black widow, spiders of the genus Steatoda, often mistaken for widow spiders
  • Black widow (fish) (Stygnobrotula latebricola), a species of brotula

In literature:

  • Black Widow (Marvel Comics), two Marvel Comics characters
  • Black Widow (Timely Comics), a 1940s comic book character
  • Black Widow: A Novel, a 1981 novel by Christina Crawford
  • Black Widowers, a fictional men-only dining club created by Isaac Asimov for a series of mystery stories

In film and television:

  • Black Widow (1954 film), a film starring Ginger Rogers
  • Black Widow (1987 film), a starring Debra Winger and Theresa Russell
  • Black Widow (2005 film), a Canadian film starring Sarah Slean
  • The Black Widow (Serial), a 1947 movie serial starring Bruce Edwards
  • The Black Widow (1951 film), a British film starring Christine Norden
  • "The Black Widow" (Boston Legal episode), an episode of Boston Legal
  • "Black Widower", an episode of The Simpsons

In music:

  • Black Widow (band), a British progressive rock band
  • Criteria for a Black Widow, a 2000 album by the heavy metal band Annihilator
  • "Black Widow", a song by the rock band Mötley Crüe on the compilation Red, White and Crüe
  • "Black Widow", a song by Lita Ford on the album Dangerous Curves
  • "The Black Widow", a song by Alice Cooper on the album Welcome to My Nightmare

In video and computer games:

  • Black Widow (arcade game), a 1982 Atari arcade game
  • Black Widow (Slam Masters), a character from the Slam Masters series of games by Capcom
  • Black Widow Games, a computer games developer

Other:

  • Black Widow (truck), a monster truck that races on the USHRA circuit
  • Black Widow Spider Bike, a motorcycle featured on the reality TV show American Chopper
  • P-61 Black Widow, an American night fighter aircraft used in World War II
  • YF-23 Black Widow II, a prototype fighter aircraft designed for the United States Air Force
