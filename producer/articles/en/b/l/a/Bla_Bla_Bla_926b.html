"Bla Bla Bla" is the name of a song made by Gigi D'Agostino. He described this song as "a piece I wrote thinking of all the people who talk and talk without saying anything". The prominent but nonsensical vocal samples are taken from "Why Did You Do It", by UK band Stretch.

[edit] Song information

  • Artist: Gigi D'Agostino
  • Album: L'Amour Toujours
  • Genre: Electronic/Dance, Techno
  • Year: 1999
