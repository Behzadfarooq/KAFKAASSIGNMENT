Blac Monks is a southern rap group consisting of Mr. 3-2, Da and Awol. In 1995 they released their debut album, Secrets of the Hidden Temple, on Rap-A-Lot Records. In 1998, Raheem and Quiet Storm joined Blac Monks on the album No Mercy.
