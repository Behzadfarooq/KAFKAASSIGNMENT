Black Blade is a thriller novel written by Eric Van Lustbader. It was published in 1993.

Black Blade is also a fantasy novel by Italian writer Dario de Judicibus (La Lama Nera), and the title of a song of American band Blue Öyster Cult. The lyrics was written by Eric Bloom and British writer Michael Moorcock and is told from viewpoint of Elric, Moorcock's most famous character. In Moorcock's Elric's story, Black Blade is a nickname of the sword Stormbringer, Elric's sword, a soul-sucker sentient sapient blade. The song was featured in Blue Öyster Cult album "Cultosaurus Erectus"
