Black Country derby

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Black Country derby is most commonly the local derby between the English football teams of West Bromwich Albion F.C. and Wolverhampton Wanderers F.C.. The moniker of 'Black Country derby' can also be given to a match between either of these teams and Walsall F.C., though such matches are less common as a result of the teams' relative league positionings; as of March 2007, both Albion and Wolves had played only 13 competitive matches against Walsall, compared to over 150 games against each other.

In the 2006-07 Season the Black Country Derby results were as follows:

  • Football League Championship: WBA 3-0 Wolves, Wolves 1-0 WBA
  • FA Cup 4th round: Wolves 0-3 WBA

[edit] Played for both clubs

The following footballers have played for both West Bromwich Albion and Wolverhampton Wanderers:

  • Steve Bull
  • Don Goodman
  • Bobby Gould (also managed Albion)
  • Andy Gray
  • Andy King
  • Cyrille Regis
  • Andy Thompson
