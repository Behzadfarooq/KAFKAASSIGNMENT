[edit] Summary

Adapted from en:User:Brianski's en:Image:BlankMap-World3.svg by en:User:Canuckguy. Detailed SVG map with grouping enabled to connect all non-contiguous parts of a country's territory for easy colouring. Also there is a layer for small countries circles (currently hidden) to show smaller countries.

A thorough description of use and other instructions can be found on the talk page
