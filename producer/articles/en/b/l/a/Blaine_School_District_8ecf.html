Blaine School District No. 503 is a public school district in Whatcom County, Washington, USA and serves the communities of Blaine and Point Roberts.

As of October 2004, the district has an enrollment of 2,272 students.

Their school mascot is a Borderite. Their colors are orange and black.

Luke Ridnour of the Seattle Sonics attened Blaine.
