Blake Lively (born August 25, 1987 in Tarzana, California) is an American actress known for her roles in The Sisterhood of the Traveling Pants and Accepted.

[edit] Biography

Lively is the youngest of five children; her parents are actors Ernie Lively and Elaine Lively. She attended Burbank High School in Burbank and has participated in the National Show Choir. Lively played the love interest in the movie Accepted and her next role will be in Elvis and Anabelle, a dark romantic comedy in which she will play a beauty queen; the film co-stars Max Minghella and Mary Steenburgen.[1]
