[edit] Personal profile

My name is Marek Blahuš, I was born in 1986, come from Uherské Hradiště and presently am a student of informatics at the Masaryk University in Brno, Czech Republic. I am interested in computer programming, genealogy and languages (especially in the international language Esperanto). You can learn more about me at my personal website.

[edit] Wikipedia activities

I mostly contribute to the Esperanto Wikipedia, and somewhat also to the Czech version. I also have accounts in a few more Wikipedias. For a complete list of my accounts in other Wikipedias, see the interwiki links ("in other languages" box) at this page. If you are interested about my aid to the English Wikipedia, see the list of my contributions.
