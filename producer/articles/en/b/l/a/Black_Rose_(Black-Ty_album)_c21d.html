Black Rose is an upcoming rap album from Black-Ty aka Tyrese. It is due out in summer 2007.

[edit] Production & Features

The album will feature Jay-Z, Beanie Sigel, Eminem, Canibus, The Game, Akon, Cassidy, T.I., Chamillionaire and 50 Cent. Producers will be Just Blaze, Swizz Beatz, Dr. Dre, RockWilder and his production team, The Frontline Boyz.
