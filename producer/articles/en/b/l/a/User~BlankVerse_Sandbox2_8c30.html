[edit] Pipe trick tests

  • Articles with words in parentheses:
    • [[Los Angeles (disambiguation)|]] = Los Angeles
  • Namespaces:
    • [[Wikipedia:WikiProject Southern California|]] = WikiProject Southern California
    • [[:Image:Santacatalinaisland.jpg|]] = Santacatalinaisland.jpg
    • [[template:California-stub|]] = California-stub
    • [[User talk:BlankVerse|]] = BlankVerse
    • [[:Category:California|]] = California
    • [[User:BlankVerse|]] = BlankVerse
    • [[Help:Variable|]] = Variable
    • [[Portal:Biology|]] = Biology
  • Articles with commas:
    • [[Los Angeles, California|]] = Los Angeles, California
      • Los Angeles, California
