Black Tequila is a German punk epicore band. At present, its members are working on their next album, which will come out in 2007. Black Tequila is also playing the soundtrack for a modern adaption of The Sorrows of Young Werther at the theater in Senftenberg. With this work, they will be touring in Germany in 2007.
