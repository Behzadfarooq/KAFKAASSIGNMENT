Blaze Heatnix is a fictional character from the Mega Man X video game series. Heatnix is a reploid (a humanoid robot) in the form a phoenix, a mythical bird of fire. He acts as one of the maverick bosses (leaders of a reploid revolution). This infernal maverick flies around the boss arena, making lava rise up from all sides of the room.

Supposedly, Blaze Heatnix was a research reploid used to retreive various samples of magma or lava. The pheonix design allowed it to easily enter and exit volcanoes.

Like other reploids, Heatnix grew tired of working for humans who wouldn't give him any reward for his duty, and would commonly just treat him as equipment. He rebelled, escaping from human control to live in the shadows. Sigma approached him and offered a deal he couldn't refuse. Heatnix then went on to join Sigma's army of mavericks. He was never a fan of maverick hunters, and awaits his chance to prove he's not just a normal maverick.

[edit] Details

Some details are virtually unnoticeable to the naked eye, but some can be found easily.

One is the fact that Heatnix'es stage is rigged on a unstable volcano, meaning some of the "traps" are actually there to stabilize the volcano. Some traps like the dragon mouth that spews lava are actually expelling some lava so that the volcano cools down.

In the battle itself, the walls are rigged up with trap doors that expel magma from the volcano. Since it is in the middle of the magma chamber, lava presses down, and that is why it rushes on-screen so fast.
