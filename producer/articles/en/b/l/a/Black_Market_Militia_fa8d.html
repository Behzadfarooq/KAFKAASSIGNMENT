A rap group comprised of Killah Priest, Timbo King, Hell Razah, Tragedy Khadafi and William Cooper. They released their debut self titled album in 2005, through Nature Sounds Records. Before doing so they released two underground mixtapes, The Black Market Vol. 1, and Vol. 2: Dead Street Scrolls.
