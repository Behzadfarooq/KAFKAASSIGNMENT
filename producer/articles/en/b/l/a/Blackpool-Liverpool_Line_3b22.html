The Blackpool to Liverpool railway line is a service provided by Northern Rail for rail travel between the British coastal town of Blackpool and the city of Liverpool, in England.

The principal stops on the route are

  • Blackpool North - in Blackpool
  • Layton - Layton in Blackpool
  • Poulton le Fylde - Poulton-le-Fylde in Wyre
  • Kirkham and Wesham = Kirkham and Wesham
  • Preston - Preston
  • Leyland - Leyland
  • Euxton Balshaw Lane - Euxton
  • Wigan North Western - Wigan
  • St Helens Central - St Helens
  • Huyton - Huyton
  • Liverpool Lime Street - Liverpool
