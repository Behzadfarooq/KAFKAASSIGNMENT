Blake Gibbons

Blake Gibbons is an American actor with a recurring role as Coleman on the long running Daytime television serial General Hospital.

[edit] Roles

  • Love's Abiding Joy (2006)
  • You and I (2006)
  • A.I. Assault (2006)
  • CSI: Miami (as Rick Miller in 2006)
  • The O.C. (2006)
  • Dependency (2005)
  • The Legend of Butch & Sundance (2004)
  • Wake (2003)
  • Hollywood Homicide (2003)
  • Michelin Commercial (2003)
  • General Hospital (2002-)
  • Pepsi Commercial (2002)
  • Charmed (2001)
  • Any Day Now (2000)
  • Dr. Quinn, Medicine Woman
  • The Pretender (1999)
  • Seinfeld (1997)
  • Baywatch Nights (1992, 1994, 1995, 1996)
  • The Golden Girls (1988)
  • Murder, She Wrote (1984)
