Black Magic is an album by Swollen Members, an Canadian hip hop group. It is their fourth full length release, not including Monsters in the Closet as it was a B-sides compilation. This album boasts impressive guest vocals, however Moka Only has only been featured on two tracks. The album debuted at #22 on the Canadian billboard chart.
