[edit] Summary

If you plan on generating a new map for wikipedia by zooming in on this map and making some modifications, please consider a source map in SVG format instead, which will produce a higher quality image. These can be found in the SVG section at Wikipedia:Blank Maps.

Map marking the microstates for reference (the map is a slightly different version, but the locations of most of the small states are the same)

A blank map of the world as of 2005, with country outlines. All territories indicated in the UN listing of territories and regions are exhibited, and sovereign microstates less than 2,500 km² in area are depicted as circles (see Image_talk:BlankMap-World-v2.png for listing). The map uses the Robinson projection centered on the Greenwich Prime Meridian.

The map was made by User:Vardion and adapted by User:E Pluribus Anthony for Wikipedia.

See also Image:BlankMap-World-v3.png which has countries joined by thin lines to make easier for one-click coloring, and Image:BlankMap-World-v4.png which for visibility purposes increases the depicted size of other small countries as well.
