[edit] People

  • Black Hawk (chief), a Sauk and Fox leader of the 19th century
    • Also Black Hawk War, an Indian War led by Black Hawk
  • Antonga Black Hawk, a Ute leader known to whites as "Black Hawk"
    • Also Utah's Black Hawk War of the 1860s, an Indian War led by Antonga Black Hawk
  • Blackhawk Church -Madison, WI

[edit] Places

  • Black Hawk Bridge, a bridge over the Mississippi River between Iowa and Wisconsin
  • Black Hawk, Colorado
  • Black Hawk County, Iowa
  • Blackhawk, California
  • Black Hawk Lake, a lake in Iowa County, Wisconsin between Cobb and Highland, Wisconsin
  • Blackhawk, Mississippi
  • Blackhawk-Camino Tassajara, California
  • Black Hawk Purchase, a historical region of Iowa
  • Black Hawk College in Moline, Illinois

[edit] Things

  • USS Black Hawk is the name of four United States vessels
  • Black Hawk Statue, a statue originally named The Eternal Indian by sculptor Lorado Taft
  • S-67 Blackhawk, a type of attack helicopter made by Sikorsky
  • UH-60 Black Hawk, a type of utility helicopter also made by Sikorsky, called S-70 by the producer. Don't confuse the S-67 Blackhawk with the S-70/UH-60 Black Hawk.
  • The Black Hawk (Amtrak) passenger train between Chicago and Dubuque, Iowa
  • The Black Hawk empire, an invader region on Internet text-based adventure game, 'nation states'
  • The Common Black Hawk, a bird of prey
  • Blackhawk tools. Originally and independent producer of high quality hand tools, now a subsidiary of Stanley tools

[edit] Sports

  • Chicago Blackhawks , a National Hockey League team
  • Tri-Cities Blackhawks, former National Basketball Association team. Currently the Atlanta Hawks.
