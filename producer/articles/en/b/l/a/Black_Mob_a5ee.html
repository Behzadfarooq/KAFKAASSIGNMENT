The Black Mobb or Black Mob is an eastside Blood & People street gang that has branched all over the United States. Some Black Mob members are rivals of the street gang Folk Nation. The rivalry started when some Folk Nation members were also claiming Black Mob, and some Black Mob were claiming the street gang known as Blood.Some Black Mob members got it confused with the Blood set which is "Money over Bitches". The Black Mobb or Black Mob Black Mob are also rivals of the street gang Crip The rivalry started when Black Mob starting wearing black and killing Crips.

[edit] Gang Attire and Symbols

Black Mob members wear a certain attire, generally focusing on the color black, or black with some white or red. Some sets may not wear the black flag (bandana). Some members may even choose to wear red & black to confuse rivals & some wear shoes known as "Chucks" or the old-style Converses as a part of the uniform. The "set" or "clique" also has a gang symbol which is simply the westside symbol (hold up hand, twist up middle finger with ring finger, and hold upside down). Some crips are rivals with black mob because the MOB is also said to stand for Member of Bloods. They wear a black bandana to the right side of their clothin or body, or tied around their necks or heads. http://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/en/4/44/1151527893a3156221807b625381345l.jpg

[edit] Gang Location

The Black Mob gang has members in the Carolinas, Tennessee, Arkansas, Lousiana, Alabama, Georgia, St. Louis, Missouri, Norfolk, Virginia, Detroit, Michigan and Chicago, Illinois Minneapolis, Minnesota, Maple Grove, Minnesota,New Jersey, Los Angeles, California Currently, Black Mobb has controlled the town of Nashville, North Carolina for some time now. The gang is growin by as more as 20 a semester. Black Mobb stands for Bitches Love All Crip Killas Money Ova Bum Bitches.
