Blawenburg, New Jersey

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Blawenburg is an unincorporated area of Montgomery Township, New Jersey. It is located at the juncture of two large roads, CR 518 and 601. It contains the Blawenburg Cemetery, and the Blawenburg Reformed Church. Blawenburg is named for an early settler, Mr. Blaw.

[edit] National Register of Historic Places

The Blawenburg Historic District was added to the National Register of Historic Places in 1990, it is District #88000632. The Dutch Reformed Church of Blawenburg was added in 1985, it is building #85002004.
