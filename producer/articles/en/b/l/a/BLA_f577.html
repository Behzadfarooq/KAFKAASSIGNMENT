BLA

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

BLA is a Three Letter Acronym that could mean:

  • Black Light Activists [1]
  • Bachelor of Liberal Arts
  • Baltimore and Annapolis Railroad (AAR reporting marks BLA)
  • Balochistan Liberation Army
  • Barcelona, Venezuela airport – IATA airport code BLA
  • Bavarian Liberation Army
  • β-lactamase
  • Black Liberation Army
  • Business Licensing Authority [2]
  • Siksika language – ISO 639 language code
  • Biological License Application
  • Spelling out the letters BLA (the first 3 letters in the word "Black") is a racist epithet and a truncated abbreviation identifying one as a Black/African-American, and is a slang way to say the word "Black". It is usually uttered or whispered by those of other races behind a Black person's back. This often happens when people of a non-Black race are driving through a turnpike or toll street guarded by Black people, when those in the car are nervous and try to alert or warn each other of this difference/"clash" by uttering this epithet; this happens while the windows are closed and at most only whispered if the windows are open or on the off-chance the car is stopped or searched.
