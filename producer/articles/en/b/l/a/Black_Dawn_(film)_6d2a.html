Black Dawn is a 2005 straight-to-video action film, a directorial debut for cinematographer Alexander Gruszynski. It is a follow-up to the 2003 film The Foreigner, with Steven Seagal reprising his role as Jonathan Cold.

[edit] Plot summary

Spoiler warning: Plot and/or ending details follow.

CIA agent Jonathan Cold is working undercover to stop a group of Chechnyan terrorists acquiring and using a nuclear weapon. His cover is blown when his protégée Amanda is kidnapped, and he has to race to stop the weapon from being detonated in Los Angeles where it will cause maximum damage.

Tagline: It's always darkest before dawn.
