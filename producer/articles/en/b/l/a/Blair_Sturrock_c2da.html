Blair Sturrock (b. August 25, 1981 - Dundee, Scotland)

Sturrock is a Scottish football player currently on a short-term contract at Swindon Town where his father Paul Sturrock is currently manager.

Sturrock has played for Dundee United, Brechin City (loan), Plymouth Argyle, Kidderminster Harriers and Rochdale F.C.
