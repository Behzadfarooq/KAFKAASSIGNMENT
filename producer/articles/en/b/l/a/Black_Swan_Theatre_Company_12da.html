Black Swan Theatre Company is a theatre company based in Perth, Western Australia.

In 1991 founding artistic director Andrew Ross, under the chairmanship of Janet Holmes Ã  Court, established the theatre company.

Many productions are produced each year for audiences in Western Australia, some of the more notable productions being Cloudstreet and Plainsong.

The office and rehearsal rooms of the Black Swan Theatre Company are located in Nedlands, with productions being performed in various theatres around Perth.
