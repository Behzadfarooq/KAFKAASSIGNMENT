Blackpool Wren Rovers F.C. is a football club based in Blackpool in Lancashire.

The club ground is Bruce Park on School Road in Squires Gate, on the outskirts of Blackpool. The ground adjoins the ground of Squires Gate, and is also very close to the ground of Blackpool Mechanics. Blackpool Wren Rovers are members of the Lancashire Football Association, with their senior team playing in the West Lancashire League Premier Division, which is at the 11th level of the English football league system.

Blackpool Wren Rovers were formed in 1931. They were originally known as Wren Rovers, then later took the name Blackpool Rovers, before adopting their present name in 1998. They were founder members of the re-formed West Lancashire League in 1959, and subsequently played in the Lancashire Combination and the North West Counties League. Unfortunately financial considerations have necessitated that they drop down the football pyramid with a return to the West Lancashire League.

The club's colours are red shirts and red shorts. They do not now produce a programme for their home games. The club also operates a women's football club, Blackpool Wren Rovers L.F.C..
