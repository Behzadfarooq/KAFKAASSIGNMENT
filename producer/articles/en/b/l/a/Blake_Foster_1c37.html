Blake Foster

Blake Anthony Foster (born 29 May 1985) is an American actor.

Foster was born in Northridge, California to Patricia Rosenburg and John Foster. He has a younger sister named Callie.

Foster was the Huggies baby at the age of sixteen months. He is perhaps best known as Justin Stewart, the child who assumed the mantle of the Blue Turbo Ranger in the fifth season of Power Rangers, Power Rangers: Turbo, making his debut for the show in the movie Turbo: A Power Rangers Movie. Foster reprised the role in an episode of Power Rangers: In Space, wrapping up plot holes left behind from his abrupt departure at the conclusion of Turbo.
