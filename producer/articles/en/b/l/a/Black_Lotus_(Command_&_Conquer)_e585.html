Black Lotus

Black Lotus is a video game unit from the title Command and Conquer: Generals (and its expansion pack Command & Conquer: Generals - Zero Hour).

Black Lotus is the hero unit of China side in the game. She has three abilities:

  • She can capture enemy structures, faster than other soldiers and from distance.
  • She can disrupt enemy vehicles.
  • She can steal money from an opponent's Supply Center.

She is also stealthed when she is not using an ability.

Super Lotus

China Infantry General, Fai, in Zero Hour expansion has Super Lotus, a younger version of Black Lotus. Super Lotus has the same abilities as Black Lotus but they are faster.
