Blackbeard's Lost Treasure Train is a steel roller coaster located at Six Flags Great Adventure in Jackson, NJ. It is a smaller, family-oriented coaster.

Blackbeard's Lost Treasure Train has a single 20-car train, by far the longest in the park. Each car has a single row of two seats for a total of 40 riders. It also has one of the longest stations in the park, with an individual entrance gate for every row. Because there is only one train, no block safety system is needed and the station also serves as the main brake run.
