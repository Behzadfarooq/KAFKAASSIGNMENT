Blackhorse Road station is a railway and tube station in Walthamstow, London, England.

The station is on the Victoria Line and Silverlink Gospel Oak to Barking line, in Travelcard Zone 3. On the Victoria Line it lies between Tottenham Hale and Walthamstow Central, and on the Silverlink Gospel Oak to Barking line between South Tottenham and Walthamstow Queens Road.

The station originally opened on 19 July 1894, and the present station opened on 1 September 1968, having been rebuilt to accommodate the newly-opened Victoria Line.
