Blair Rasmussen

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Blair Allen Rasmussen (born November 13, 1962 in Auburn, Washington), is an American former professional basketball player who was selected by the Denver Nuggets in the 1st round (15th overall) of the 1985 NBA Draft. A 7'0" center from the University of Oregon, Rasmussen played in eight seasons in the NBA from 1985 to 1993. He played for the Nuggets from 1985-1991 and for the Atlanta Hawks from 1991-1993. Rasmussen's best year as a pro came during the 1990-91 season as a member of the Nuggets, when he appeared in 70 games (69 starts) and averaged 12.5 ppg and 1.9 blocks per game.
