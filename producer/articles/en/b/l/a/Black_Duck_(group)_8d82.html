Black Duck (group)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Black Duck was an Italian group whose sole single was "Whiggle In Line"


Released in 1994 by the label X Energy Records this single was an electronic Euro House style take on the hugely popular 1994 Whigfield hit single "Saturday Night".


It has an added rap element over the top and slightly different female vocals to the Whigfield original.

The video for this track featured a dancing stuffed black duck, hence the name Black Duck. The video also featured a rapper and a female "singer". The female in the video for this song was a Whigfield Lookalike possibly called Carryl Varley and thus the vocals are unlikely to be those of either Whigfield or the female purporting to sing in the video.


The track was produced by Davide Riva and Larry Pignagoli.


The track reached number 33 in the UK charts and enjoyed modest success in the US.
