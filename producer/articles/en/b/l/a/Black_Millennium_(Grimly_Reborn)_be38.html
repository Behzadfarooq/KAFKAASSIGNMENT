Black Millennium (Grimly Reborn) was Mütiilation‎'s come back album. After being away since 1995 (excluding the compilation of unreleased material by Drakkar Productions in 1999), Meyhna'ch was "resurrected" for the reason of "not being buried yet". His dead body continued to create music and to release it.

[edit] Trivia

  • The CD release was only printed in 3000 copies.
  • The LP release by End All Life Productions in 2002 was only printed in 400, hand-numbered copies.
  • The Tragic Empire Records re-release added the live show from Germendorf, Germany, July 7th, 2001, which had at the time only been partly released on 1992-2002 Ten Years of Depressive Destruction. The full show had only been circulating as a bootleg.
