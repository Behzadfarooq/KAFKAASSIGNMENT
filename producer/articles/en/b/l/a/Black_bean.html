Black bean

Black bean can mean:

  • The black turtle bean, a small, black variety of the common bean especially popular in Latin American cuisine
  • Douchi, a kind of fermented soybean especially popular in the cuisine of China
  • The urad bean or black gram
  • Any black-colored variety of bean; beans which include black-colored varieties include the soybean, the azuki bean, the mung bean and the rice bean.
  • Blackbean, a common name for Castanospermum australe, also known as the Moreton Bay Chestnut
