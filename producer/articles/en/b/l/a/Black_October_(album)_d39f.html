Black October is an album by rapper Sadat X, released on October 3, 2006. This will be Sadat's fourth solo effort, following 1996's Wild Cowboys, 2000's The State of New York vs. Derek Murphy and 2005's Experience & Education. The album features production from underground luminaries such as Da Beatminerz, Diamond D, DJ Spinna and Ayatollah. Album guests include Sadat's new crew Boss Money Gangstas, Greg Nice of Nice & Smooth and Sadat's former Brand Nubian bandmates Lord Jamar and Grand Puba.
