Black Mountain can refer to multiple places:

[edit] United Kingdom

  • Black Mountain (Belfast) a mountain beside Belfast
  • Black Mountain transmitting station the transmitter on top of the mountain beside Belfast
  • Black Mountain (hill), a peak in the Black Mountains range, on the English-Welsh border
  • Black Mountain (range), a range of mountains in South Wales, different from the nearby Black Mountains.

[edit] United States

  • Black Mountain College, founded in 1933 near Asheville, North Carolina
  • Black Mountain (Georgia), a mountain in Dawson County, Georgia.
  • Black Mountain (Kentucky), a mountain and the highest point in Kentucky
  • Black Mountain (near Milpitas, California), in Santa Clara County, California, is a mountain east of Milpitas, California.
  • Black Mountain (near Los Altos, California)
  • Black Mountain, San Diego County, California, is a mountain near Ramona in San Diego County, California.
  • Black Mountain Open Space Park contains another mountain in San Diego, California.
  • Black Mountain of Maine, a ski area in Rumford, Maine
  • Black Mountain (New Hampshire), a ski area in Jackson, New Hampshire
  • Black Mountain, North Carolina, a town in North Carolina
  • Black Mountain (West Virginia), a mountain along the Allegheny Front in West Virginia, USA
  • Black Mountain (Arizona), A mountain located in Cave Creek Arizona

[edit] Australia

  • Black Mountain National Park, a mountain near Cooktown in Queensland, Australia
  • Black Mountain (Australian Capital Territory), a mountain in Canberra the capital city of Australia
  • Black Mountain, Queensland, a suburb of Noosa
