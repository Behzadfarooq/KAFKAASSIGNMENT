Black Impact! The Moment the Black Organization Reaches Out! (ブラックインパクト! 組織の手が届く瞬間) is the 425th episode of the anime series Detective Conan. It aired in Japan on January 9, 2006 as a 2.5 hour special honoring the series's 10th anniversary.

Spoiler warning: Plot and/or ending details follow.

[edit] Overview

Conan and Ai both tell of the Black Organization and recap events that have taken place in episodes 1, 176-178, as well as 345. The recap ends with them explaining that an assassination mission is about to take place and them wondering who the Black Corps' next target is going to be.

It moves on to Chianti and Korn practicing sniping in a simulation that makes the places look like actual buildings and the people seem real. The practice shooting a famous president at different distances. Gin and Vodka come to watch, and Vodka comments that they are good, however, their limit appears to 600 yards because they begin to miss beyond that point. Gin tells them that their range of target surpasses the limit for the job, therefore, recruits them for their mission. Vermouth is seen in the city spying on a rich woman while sitting in a car.

Elsewhere, Yoko Okino introduces Conan, Kogoro, and Ran to a news reporter named Rena Mizunashi. She complains about a "mischievous doorbell", and hires detective Kogoro Mori to catch the culprit. Conan uses a piece of gum to stick a tracker to the wall. He and the others eventually find out that it was just a kid's prank.

Accidentally, Conan's tracker eventually falls off the wall and gets stuck to the bottom of Rena's shoe. He overhears her having a conversation with Gin, Vermouth, and Chianti and realizes that she's a Black Organization member that goes by the name of Kir. She mentions that Korn should be with them, and Conan is now worried because of the number of members that are in the city. To make things worse, they are planning to meet three people; one is their target, but who are they planning to kill? On top of that, Conan must find a way to reach Kir and remove the tracker before she or someone else finds it on her, which may result in them blaming Detective Moori and killing him for snooping.

While he chases Kir who is going to rendezvous with Gin and the other members, he overhears Gin informing Kir that there's a suspicious car. He tells her that they are keeping watch with their sniper rifles. Conan gets scared that they may have spotted him and his friends chasing them in the car, but he later learns that it was Jodie that Gin was referring to. Conan regroups with the FBI Agents and they must find a way to stop the Black Organization from carrying out their mission.

By using the tracker and gaining information, Conan and the FBI agents manage to catch on to the plan and target. Jodie and James Black chase Kir/Rena (believing her to be Vermouth) and cause her to crash and detain her, while Shuichi confronts Gin and the others by firing his sniper, thus, scaring them into retreating believing that more FBI agents may be involved.

The FBI have Kir kept under patrol in a hospital and keep her records of being there a secret. Conan uses his bow tie to fake an illness to the news corporation so they won't go looking for her. Vermouth is the only one that appears to know Conan's the real culprit behind thwarting their plans.

[edit] Miscellanea

  • First appearance of the new black organization members Chianti, Korn, and Kir in the anime (aside from Opening 16).
  • Chianti speaks with a distinct accent.
  • Rena Mizunashi is revealed to be the black organization member Kir. The 16th Opening already foreshadows this as she is introduced with Chianti and Korn. She accidentally crashed while trying to escape, she is currently in a coma and now she is detained by the FBI agents. Later in the manga, Conan reveals that she used to live in Osaka, and asked Heiji to investigate.
  • Before the special begins, Ai actually says that the title is "Black Impact! When the Hands of the Black Corps Reaches You!"
  • The impact of this episode is actually foreshadowed in Opening 16 starting episode 415, as it shows the new members of the BO, as well as intense action scenes with the FBI agents.
