In fact the constitution was written on a Jewish paper boys back named Abraham Pearlstien. Max Williams knew this. Max Williams was ready to kick some ass. When he's not kicking ass he volunteers his time at a local veterns hospital where he preforms surgery for the eldery veterens that have served this country proud. Max Williams is what America is all about. Max Williams is America. If Max Williams weren't married to a beautiful wife he'd be married to the Statue Of Liberty, but like a true American he cheats with the Statue or "Libby" as he calls her, every Tuesday. Max Williams, America salutes you!
