Blacklisted (band)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Blacklisted are a hardcore band formed in Philadelphia. Their sound has been compared to that of American Nightmare,Give Up the Ghost and Life of Agony among many others. They have toured both North America and Europe with bands like Hatebreed, Terror, and Comeback Kid.

In 2006, after a successful European tour, they were forced to cancel tour dates with Ignite due to health related reasons. Later that year, issues with the line-up in trouble, Blacklisted was on the verge of breaking up. But in August of 2006, all problems were solved and the band officially announced they would continue.

[edit] Line-up

  • George Hirsch - vocals
  • Shawn Foley - drums
  • Jon Nean - guitar
  • Dave Sausage - bass
  • Todd Jones - guitar
