BladeLogic, Inc. is a software company based in Lexington, MA which offers products for server provisioning, configuration and management targeted toward enterprise customers.

The company was founded in September, 2001 as a three way coming together of technology (Thomas Kraus and software from Network Shell), professional management (Dev Ittycheria, Vijay Manwani, and Vance Loiselle), and funding (Battery Ventures and Bessemer Venture Partners - $6M in first round, $29M to date).

BladeLogic makes server configuration management software that allows companies to provision, configure and manage their systems and application infrastructure. BladeLogicâ€™s Operations Manager can be used to identify, fix and prevent problems in data centers by simplifying, securing and automating the process managing change in servers.

BladeLogic products are geared toward improving the consistency of data center operations, consolidating servers, managing the roll out of new computing platforms such as Linux, controlling access to server infrastructure, applying compliance of configurations to security policies and reducing server and application downtime.

BladeLogic customers include Chicago Mercantile Exchange, Walmart, Sprint, the Federal Government and numerous Fortune 500 companies who use their software to automate their IT infrastructure.
