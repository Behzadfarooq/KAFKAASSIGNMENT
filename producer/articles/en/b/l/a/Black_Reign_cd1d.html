Black Reign
Studio album by Queen Latifah
Released 1993
Genre R&B

Black Reign is the third album from Queen Latifah. The album was released in 1993. Compared to her two previous albums, Black Reign was relatively her most successful album up to this point for Queen Latifah, peaking at #60 on the Billboard 200 and going Gold. The album also peaked at #15 on the Top R&B/Hip-Hop Albums.
