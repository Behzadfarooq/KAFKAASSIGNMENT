[edit] History

Blasthrash is a thrash metal band from São Paulo city in Brazil. They started in 1998 and since, they released two demo tapes (License To Thrash in 1999 and Beer and Mosh in 2002) and a full lentgh called No Traces Left Behind in 2005 on the independent metal label Force Majeure Records. Their sound get more close to the bay area thrash/L.A. thrash (of bands such as Vio-Lence, Evildead) then the german teutonic thrash.
