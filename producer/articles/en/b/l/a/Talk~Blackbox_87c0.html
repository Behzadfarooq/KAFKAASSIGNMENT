[edit] Old discussion about merging

bblean, xoblite, bb4win currently have almost nothing in common with blackbox. Initially, bb4win reused the graphics code from Blackbox and kept style compatibility, that's all. If they are all put together, then we should also merge here the entries for openbox, fluxbox, hackedbox and many other window managers which had something to do with Blackbox at some point in the past.

I removed bblean but it seems like the other two could be footnotes to this article. --Yath 19:45, 10 January 2006 (UTC)

Bad idea, you are talking programs for 2 differant operating systems for 1. For 2 the internal operations of both are very very differant. For example, blackboxwm uses the rc files as an X database, while the windows shells see them merely as text files. Also, the idea of plugins and what not is exclusive to bb4win and that family. While programs can interop with blackboxwm, they are not part of the shell. full disclosure I was one of the developers of bb4win and still a prominent community member running 2 of the most visted sites dealing with bb4win [lostinthebox.com and boxshots.org (formally box.crackmonkey.us)] --Tresni 23:12, 06 February 2006 (UTC -7)

VERY bad idea. BB4Win = Windows Shell Replacement, Blackbox = Linux window manager --62.47.206.215 22:28, 27 March 2006 (UTC)


I'll add to the growing chorus for this idea to be dropped, albeit as a mere anon... (194.80.32.11 01:23, 4 May 2006 (UTC))

Yes, I think for all intents and purposes this matter is closed. - Centrx 02:49, 4 May 2006 (UTC)
