I moved the page from "Blacktown" to "Blacktown, New South Wales" to follow Wikipedia place-name convention. Techelf 11:42, 9 Dec 2004 (UTC)

this is not a general place-name convention, it is only a place-name convention for american locations. there is presently a vigorous debate about whether we should apply these conventions to australian articles, see wikipedia:australian wikipedians' notice board/Archive 6#Again on Disambiguation. even though it's an archive i don't feel that the vote has been officially called yet. the general naming convention only calls for disambiguation where necessary. clarkk 09:06, 10 Dec 2004 (UTC)
