BlackOwned C-Bone

Blackowned C-Bone (Born in Atlanta, Georgia) is one of the three rappers in the hip-hop group Konkrete.

He has had some solo features, such as on OutKast's "Gangsta Shit" and on "Kryptonite", the lead single from Got Purp? Vol 2, Purple Ribbon Records's first major release.

[edit] Featured On

[edit] Solo

  • OutKast's Stankonia, Gangsta Shit
  • Backbone's Concrete Law, O.K
  • Purple Ribbon Records's Got Purp? Vol 2, Kryptonite
  • Yung Joc's New Joc City, Dope Boy Magic

[edit] In Konkrete

  • Dungeon Family's Even in Darkness, Curtains (Of 2nd Generation)
  • Big Boi's Speakerboxx, Tomb Of The Boom
  • Purple Ribbon Records's Got that Purp?, Beef
  • Purple Ribbon Records's Got that Purp?, Whatcha Wanna Do
  • Purple Ribbon Records's Got that Purp?, D-Boi Stance Feat Big Boi
  • Purple Ribbon Records's Got that Purp?, Hard In The Paint
  • Purple Ribbon Records's Got Purp? Vol 2, Shit Ya Drawers
  • Purple Ribbon Records's Got Purp? Vol 2, Lovin' This
