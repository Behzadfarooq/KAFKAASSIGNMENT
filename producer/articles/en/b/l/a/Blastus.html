Blastus

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

You have new messages (last change).

Blastus was a servant of Herod Agrippa (Acts 12:20), and a mediator for the Sidonians and Tyrians. Herod was very displeased with the people of Sidon and Tyre (Lebanon), who made Blastus their friend. Blastus was a chamberlain.
