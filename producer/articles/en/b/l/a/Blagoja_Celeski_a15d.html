Blagoja 'Billy' Celeski (born July 14, 1985 in Ohrid, Republic of Macedonia) is an Australian-raised football (soccer) player. He currently plays for Perth Glory as a central midfielder. Celeski signed for Perth Glory for the 2005/2006 A-League season from Bulleen playing 18 times and won the club's media award, but was released at the end of the season. Celeski graduated from the Victorian Institute of Sport and Australian Institute of Sport and in March 2007 Celeski was signed by Perth Glory again along with three other players.

He was on the Australian roster for the 2005 FIFA World Youth Championship, and represented Australia in the Under 23 Asian Qualifying tournament.
