[edit] 26th century

We don't have an ordr years in the 100'sinal for 0 in English. The century of years prior to 100 is the first century. The century fo is the second. The 26th century is thus the years 2500 to 2599, and the 27th century 2600 to 2699. Why did you change the re-direct?? 66.32.87.120 01:37, 24 May 2004 (UTC)

  • I'm afraid you're mistaken; The last year of a century is always the year ending in 00. The first century AD was 1 through 100, not 0 through 99. because there was no 0 AD. The 26th century page says itself: "The 26th century (Gregorian Calendar) comprises the years 2501-2600." Hence 2600 should redirect to 26th century, not 27th. blankfaze | &#9835 01:55, 24 May 2004 (UTC)
