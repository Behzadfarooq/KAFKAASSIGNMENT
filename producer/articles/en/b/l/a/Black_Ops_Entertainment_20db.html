Black Ops Entertainment is a video game developer founded in 1994. Since then is has developed eight games for several consoles includings the PS2, Gamecube, and Xbox. The company was founded in Santa Monica, California by four MIT graduates. Black Ops got their first contract with Virgin Games producing Agile Warrior.
