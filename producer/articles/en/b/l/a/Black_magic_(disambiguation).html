Black magic is a form of supernatural magic.

Black magic may also refer to:

  • Black Magic (chocolate), chocolate made by Rowntree Mackintosh
  • Black Magic (game), a party game
  • Black Magic (computer game), a 1987 computer game for Commodore 64, ZX Spectrum and Amstrad CPC by Datasoft
  • Black magic (programming), arcane computer programming techniques
  • Blackmagic Design, a manufacturer of professional video digitizing boards.
  • Black Magic and Purple Passion, a book about black plants by Karen Platt
  • Black Magic, the winning yacht in the 1995 America's Cup regatta, sailing for New Zealand, also knew as Black Magic.
  • Black Magic (DC Comics), a DC Comics horror anthology comic.
  • Tame Head (magic) magic, also known as Gong Tau, a type of black magic practised in Yunnan, Sichuan and Southeast Asia.
  • The act of a black person performing a set of tricks that lead to the victim's loss of wallet or property (slang)
  • A form of magic in the The Black Magician trilogy of books by Trudi Canavan.
  • A song from the 1983 album Show No Mercy by the thrash metal band Slayer.
  • Black Magic a Royal MODNAR Comics production which tells the story of a boy possesed by the evils of Pandora's box.
  • Black Magic is a blues album released by Magic Sam in 1968 on the Delmark label.
