Black River is a river in the Upper Peninsula of the U.S. state of Michigan. The river flows into Lake Michigan at 46°05′37″N, 85°20′30″W.

The main branch of the river rises in a marshy area in eastern Garfield Township near Cranberry Lake Bog. The East Branch rises in Hudson Township. All of the river's drainage basin is within Mackinac County, much of it within the Lake Superior State Forest.

Tributaries (from the mouth):

  • East Branch Black River
    • Borgstrom Creek
  • Peters Creek
  • Silver Creek
  • Bark Creek
