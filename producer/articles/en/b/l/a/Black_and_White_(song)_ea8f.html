"Black and White" may be one of various songs:

  • "Black & White", a 2002 song by In Flames on the album Reroute to Remain
  • "Black and White" (Three Dog Night song), a 1972 song by Three Dog Night on the album Seven Separate Fools
  • "Black and White" (Static-X song), a 2001 song by Static-X on the album Machine
  • "Black and White", a 1997 song by Sarah McLachlan on the album Surfacing
  • "Black and White", a 2006 song by The Upper Room on the album Other People's Problems
  • "Black and White People", a 2000 song by matchbox twenty on the album mad season.
  • "Black and White", a song by The String Cheese Incident on the album Outside and Inside as well as a live favorite of theirs.
  • "Black and White", a song by Deep Purple from their 1987 album The House of Blue Light
  • "Black and White", a song by Screaming Jets from their 1997 album World Gone Crazy
  • "Black and White", a song by Jackson Browne on the album Lives in the Balance
