Blair Tefkin

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Blair Tefkin (born December 9, 1959 in Los Angeles, California) is an American actress and singer. She may be best known for her role as Robin Maxwell on the 1983 science fiction miniseries V, the 1984 sequel V: The Final Battle, and V: The Series.

Tefkin also appeared in films such as Fast Times at Ridgemont High (credited as Blair Ashleigh), Three for the Road with Kerri Green and Inside Monkey Zetterland with Martha Plimpton. Finding difficulty in being cast in film projects, Tefkin recorded an album, Shocked & Devastated, which was released on her own label, Bossyboots Records. She further wrote a one-woman show called In the Land of the Giants: A Tragicomedy With Music which ran in Los Angeles.
