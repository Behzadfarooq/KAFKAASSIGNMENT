Black Ruthenia (Ruthenian: Czornaja Ruś, Belarusian: Čornaja Ruś, Polish: Ruś Czarna, Russian: Чёрная Русь) was a landlocked country in Eastern Europe, bordering principalities of White Ruthenia to its East & South, Kingdom of Poland to its West, Grand Duchy of Lithuania to its North. Its capital was Nowohorodok, and other important cities include Grodno, Słonim, Wołkowysk, Lida and Nieśwież.
