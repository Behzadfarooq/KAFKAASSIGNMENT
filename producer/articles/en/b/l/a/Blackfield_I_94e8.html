Blackfield I or just Blackfield is the eponymous debut album of the rock duo, Blackfield. It was recorded in both Tel Aviv and London, between 2001, and 2003, and released on 2004 in Israel and England and on 2005 in the United States.

Singles were released for Hello, Pain, Blackfield and Cloudy Now.
