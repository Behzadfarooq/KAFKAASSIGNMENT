[edit] National Register cat

Check it. The state historic site is just that, a state historic site. The actual entire site is not on the National Register of Historic Places, per the NRIS Database. The Register listing associated with the site in Rock Island is the Black Hawk Museum and Lodge on 46th Ave., according to the database. That site will have its own article as it is part of an MPS of historic and architectural significance. Anyway I will remove the cat. This article probably falls under the protected areas project not the National Register project, I will alter the banners to reflect this as well.A mcmurray 16:40, 20 February 2007 (UTC)
