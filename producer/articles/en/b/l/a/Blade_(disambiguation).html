A blade is a sharp cutting part, for instance of a weapon or tool.

Blade may also refer to:

  • Bladed weapon, especially a sword
  • Razor blade, used for shaving
  • Lithic blade, a type of stone archeological artefact that is at least twice as long as wide, has a more or less regular shape and a triangular or trapezoid cross-shape.
  • Prismatic blade, a type of stone archeological artefact that is long and narrow with parallel margins
  • The Blades, a common nickname for the Sheffield United football club and their fans
  • Computing blade, an interchangeable part in a computing system
  • Blade server, a self-contained computer server, designed for high density
  • The edge of an oar
