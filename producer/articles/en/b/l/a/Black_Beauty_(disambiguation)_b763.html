Black Beauty (disambiguation)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Black Beauty may refer to:

  • Black Beauty, 1877 book by Anna Sewell.
  • Gibson Les Paul Black Beauty, 1957 guitar made by Gibson.
  • Black Beauty: Live at the Fillmore West, 1970 Miles Davis live record.
  • The Adventures of Black Beauty, 1972-74 British television series inspired by (but not adapted from) the novel.
  • Black Beauty (1994 film), 1994 film adaptation of the novel.
  • Black Beauty (Horse), The shortest known horse on earth born in 1996
