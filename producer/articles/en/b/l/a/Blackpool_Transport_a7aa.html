Blackpool Transport Services (BTS) is an independent bus and tram operator running within the boroughs of Blackpool and Fylde and into the surrounding area, including Fleetwood, Lytham St Annes, Poulton le Fylde and Kirkham. BTS is a limited company wholly owned by Blackpool Borough Council, which makes it one of the few remaining municipal bus companies in the UK today.

The company's network was relaunched in April 2001 under the name Metro Coastlines. Along with the new name came a new colour-coded service: each core bus route is operated by vehicles painted in a livery heavily featuring that route's colour. There are twelve such core bus routes, and the historic tramway along the promenade also has its own variation of the livery.

[edit] Fares

Fares on Blackpool Transport depend on the distance travelled. Although there is a cap on buses to how much can be charged for one single/Return journey. Most tickets can be purchased from the bus driver of first bus boarded. On trams, most tickets can be purchased from the conductor or driver. Pensioners who hold a valid nowcard (concennenary travel card scheme in Lancashire) can travel FREE within their borough of residence, crossing a border costs 50p. Children under five also travel FREE. Drivers and conductors are happy to give change in small dominations.

Fare Adult Child (5-15)
Single £2.00 or less £1.00 or less
Return £3.80 or less £1.90 or less
Metro Saver - day ticket, one zone (bus only) £3.40 £1.70
Metro Saver+ - day ticket, all zones (bus only) £4.00 £3.00

[edit] Paint Schemes

Each line has its own individual route colour which is painted with a contrasting colour of yellow, and there are also Black generic painted buses which can be used on any line if a branded vehicle cannot be used for any reason. Each bus stop also features the line colour and timetables are also colour coded.

  • Line 1 - Orange
  • Line 2 - Green
  • Line 3 - Light Blue
  • Line 4 - Dark Blue
  • Line 5 - Red
  • Line 6 - Brown
  • Line 7 - Purple
  • Line 10 - Grey
  • Line 11 - Turquise
  • Line 14 - Dark Green
  • Line 15 - Pink
  • Line 20 - (Operated by red open top Citysightseeing buses in good weather)
