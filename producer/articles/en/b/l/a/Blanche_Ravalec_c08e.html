Blanche Ravalec

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Blanche Ravalec is a diminutive French actress most recognized for her role as Dolly, Jaws' girlfriend in the James Bond film Moonraker. She started her career in 1978 with the movie Holiday Hotel. Besides movies she has also starred in numerous TV productions.
