Black Magic Woman, or The Original Fleetwood Mac as it was known in the United Kingdom, is a 1971 compilation album by blues-rock band Fleetwood Mac. It is a double LP, comprising of songs from two Peter Green albums, Peter Green's Fleetwood Mac and English Rose.
