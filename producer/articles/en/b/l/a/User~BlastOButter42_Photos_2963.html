Pictures or photos I took and uploaded (or just other pictures I took and like). They're mostly in and around New York City, since that's where I live and I love to take photographs of it. They're not really in any sort of order, though I suppose they should be. This is now a complete list, I think.

As well as the pictures shown below, I also took a bunch of pictures of old New York City Subway cars at the New York Transit Museum. I'm not including them on this page because they're not all very attractive pictures, but they were taken by me and they're on Wikipedia. They can be seen here.

All photos taken by me were taken with my Kodak EasyShare Z730 Zoom Digital Camera.
