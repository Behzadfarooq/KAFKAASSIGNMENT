Blackrock can mean the following:

  • Blackrock is a village in County Dublin, Republic of Ireland;
  • Blackrock, County Louth is a town in County Louth, Republic of Ireland;
  • Blackrock, Cork is a suburb of Cork City, Republic of Ireland;
  • Blackrock, Monmouthshire in Monmouthshire, Wales;
  • Blackrock, New York, (sometimes referred to as 'Black Rock'), is a former town and now a neighborhood in the northwest part of Buffalo, New York
  • In the fictional Warcraft Universe:
    • Blackrock Mountain
    • Blackrock Spire
    • Blackrock Depths
    • Blackrock Realm in World of Warcraft.
  • Blackrock (Ultima) in the Ultima computer game series, is a fictional chemical substance, completely black mineral that is highly resistant to ethereal waves and thus almost completely resistant to all magic.
  • Blackrock (comics) is an adversary of Superman's in DC Comics.
  • Blackrock (play) is the name of an Australian play.
  • Blackrock (film) is the name of an Australian movie;
  • G. B. Blackrock is a character in the Transformers comic book series.
  • Blackrock is a type of limestone, with its name originating from a place in Somerset where it occurs. It is not always necessarily black, often a dark grey colour.
  • BlackRock is an investment management firm based in New York City.
