Blade-dance of the Two Lovers (Traditional Chinese: 鴛鴦刀; Simplified Chinese: 鸳鸯刀; pinyin: yuān yāng dāo) is a wuxia novella written by Jinyong. It was first published in 1961 in Ming Pao.
