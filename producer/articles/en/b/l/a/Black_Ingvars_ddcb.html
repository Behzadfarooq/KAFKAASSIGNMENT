Black Ingvars

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Black Ingvars is a Swedish humorist heavy metal band. Black Ingvars is famous for cover versions of songs from other musical styles, like pop, children's song, "dansband" music, Christmas songs and gospel. They Finished 5th in the Swedish Melodifestivalen 1998 with the song Cherie.
