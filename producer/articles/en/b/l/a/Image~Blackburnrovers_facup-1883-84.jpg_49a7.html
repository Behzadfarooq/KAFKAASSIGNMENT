Blackburn Rovers cup winners in 1883-1884. The first FA Cup win for the team. The photograph includes the East Lancashire Charity Cup; the FA Cup and the Lancashire Cup.

Back row (left to right): J. M. Lofthouse, H. McIntrye, J. Beverly, W. J. H. Arthur, F. Suter, J. Forrest, R. Birtwistle (umpire) Front row (left to right): J. Douglas, J. E. Sowerbutts, J. Brown, G. Avery, J. Hargreaves.
