Blair College

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Blair College is a for-profit college in Colorado Springs, Colorado. Blair College offers career college programs in a variety of areas. The College was founded in Pueblo, Colorado as Brown's School of Business in 1897, changing its name to Blair's Business College in 1918. In 1981, Blair Business College changed its name to Blair Junior College. In 1996, the College was acquired by Rhodes Colleges, Inc., a division of Corinthian Colleges, Inc. and the name was changed to Blair College.
