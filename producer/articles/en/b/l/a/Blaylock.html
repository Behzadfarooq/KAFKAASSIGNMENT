Blaylock is a family name that may refer to:

  • Cheryl Blaylock, puppeteer from 1989 to 1995 on Eureeka's Castle
  • Chet Blaylock (1924–1996), politician
  • Derrick Blaylock (born 1979), running back of the National Football League
  • Ed Blaylock (born 1946), voice actor and radio announcer
  • James Blaylock (born 1950), fantasy author
  • Mattie Blaylock (Celia Ann Blaylock) (1850–1888), romantic companion of Old West gambler and lawman Wyatt Earp
  • Mookie Blaylock (born 1967), former professional NBA basketball player
This page or section lists people with the surname Blaylock. If an internal link for a specific person referred you to this page, you may wish to add the given name(s) to that wikilink.
