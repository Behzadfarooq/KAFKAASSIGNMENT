Blaubeuren is a town near Ulm in Baden-Württemberg, Germany. It has 11,856 inhabitants. The town's shield shows the so-called "Blaumännle" (little blue man) - a small man, dressed in blue on a gold background.

[edit] Points of interest

  • Blautopf, source of the river Blau
  • Ruckenkreuz
  • Blaubeuren Abbey
