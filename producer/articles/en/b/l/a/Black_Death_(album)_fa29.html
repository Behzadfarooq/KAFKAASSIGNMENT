Black Death is the second full-length released by the epic metal band Brocas Helm. It was released in 1988, even though the recording sessions began in 1985. This album was re-released in 2005 by Eat Metal Records with different artwork, 1989's "Helm's Deep" demo as bonus, and also a multimedia track of a live performance. It was also re-released as a Picture LP by Doomed Planet Records in 2002, limited to 500 copies and with 2 bonustracks from the Ghost Story EP: "War Toons" and "Juggernaut".
