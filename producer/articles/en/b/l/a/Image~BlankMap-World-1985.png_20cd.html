(This is also accurate for the period between 1977 (South Africa imposes direct rule on Walvis Bay) - 1979 (US hands over Panama Canal Zone to Panama) - December 26, 1981 (Iraq-Saudi Arabia Neutral Zone partitioned between Iraq and Saudi Arabia) - May 22 1990 (re-unification of North and South Yemen))
