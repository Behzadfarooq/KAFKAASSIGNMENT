Black and Blue Bowl

The Black and Blue Bowl is an annual college football game between the University of Memphis Tigers and the University of Southern Mississippi Golden Eagles. The game gets its title from the colors of the two universities. The University of Memphis wears blue while the University of Southern Mississippi wears black. Both teams compete in Conference USA. The University of Southern Mississippi leads the University of Memphis 37-19-1. The game alternates between Memphis, Tennessee and Hattiesburg, Mississippi, but has been played in Jackson, Mississippi five times. The teams played twice in 1964. The only tie in the series occurred in 1972.
