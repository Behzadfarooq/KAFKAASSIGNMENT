Black Winter Day is an EP by the Finnish band Amorphis released in 1995.

[edit] Track listing

  1. "Black Winter Day" (Martenson, Traditional) – 3:48
  2. "Folk of the North" – 1:19
  3. "Moon and Sun" (Koivusaari) – 3:35
  4. "Moon and Sun Pt. II: North's Son" (Koivusaari, Traditional) – 5:10
