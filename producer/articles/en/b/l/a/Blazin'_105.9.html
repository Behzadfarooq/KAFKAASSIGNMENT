Blazin' 105.9 (DWLA 105.9 MHz Metro Manila) is a music FM station owned and operated by Empire Entertainment in the Philippines. The station's studio is located at Pioneer Highlands, Mandaluyong City.

This station plays today's rap, hip-hop and R & B of artists such as Beyoncé, Ice Cube, Snoop Dog, and more.

As of January 2007, the station is currently inactive.
