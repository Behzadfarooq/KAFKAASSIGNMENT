BlackPlanet.com is an interactive website which focuses on issues and concerns related to the African diaspora. The BlackPlanet website, launched on September 1, 1999, has over 16 million members. The website often features advertisements from many Fortune 500 companies. BlackPlanet allows its members to exchange notes, and to engage in real-time chat. Its executive director is Omar Wasow, an internet analyst who appears frequently on radio and television.
