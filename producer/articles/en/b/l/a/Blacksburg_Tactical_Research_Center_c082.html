Blacksburg Tactical Research Center, Inc.
Type Private
Founded Blacksburg, Virginia (1986)
Headquarters Blacksburg, Virginia, USA
Key people Greg Porter
Industry Role-playing game publisher
Products EABA, CORPS, Timelords, Macho Women with Guns, Hollyworld, F*ck This! (card game)

Blacksburg Tactical Resource Center, Inc., or BTRC is an American game publishing company most famous for publishing the Timelords and EABA role-playing games.
