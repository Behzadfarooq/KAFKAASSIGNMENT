Between the Buried and Me drummer Blake Richardson

Blake Richardson plays drums for North Carolina based metalcore band Between the Buried and Me. He also is the drummer for another North Carolina metalcore band Glass Casket with fellow member of Between the Buried and Me, guitar player Dusty Waring. Richardson joined in early 2005 replacing former Bury Your Dead drummer Mark Castillo.

Blake Richardson went to middle school at Calvary Baptist Day School in Winston-Salem. From there, he attended Mt. Tabor High School and was a member of Civitans Service Club and performed an annual drum solo for the club project named 'Spartan Spectacular'.
