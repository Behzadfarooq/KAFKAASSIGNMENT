Blair MacDonald (born 17 November 1953 in Cornwall, Ontario, Canada) is a retired professional ice hockey right winger who played in the NHL for the Edmonton Oilers and Vancouver Canucks.

MacDonald also played in the World Hockey Association and played 476 games there, scoring 171 goals and 165 assists and 336 points.
