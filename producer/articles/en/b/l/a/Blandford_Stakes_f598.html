Blandford Stakes

The Blandford Stakes is a Group 2 flat horse race in the Republic of Ireland for three-year-old and above thoroughbred fillies and mares run over a distance of 1 mile 2 furlongs (2,012 metres) at the Curragh in September.

The race was restricted to fillies and mares only from 2001, prior to which colts and geldings were also eligible to run. The present race distance also dates back to 2001 - it had been 1 mile 3 furlongs (2,213 metres) since 1994, before which it was 1 mile 4 furlongs (2,414 metres). In 1999 the race was dropped to Group 3 level, but it regained its Group 2 status in 2004.
