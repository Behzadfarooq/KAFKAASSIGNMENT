The Queen held a special children's party at Buckingham Palace on 25th June for Her Majesty's 80th birthday to celebrate British children's literature.

[edit] Charactors

Characters such as Postman Pat, Paddington Bear, Bob the Builder, Thomas the Tank Engine, the White Rabbit and Mary Poppins were attending the tea party which was in the Buckingham Palace garden.

[edit] Broadcasting

After the tea party, a special BBC show starring many of these characters was broadcasted live from Buckingham Palace on BBC1.
