Blair Betts (b. February 16, 1980 in Edmonton, Alberta, Canada) is a Canadian professional hockey centre. He currently plays for the National Hockey League's New York Rangers. He was drafted by the Calgary Flames in the 1998 NHL Entry Draft, Second Round, Thirty-third Overall. He has played for the Calgary Flames.
