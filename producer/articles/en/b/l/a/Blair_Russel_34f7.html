Blair Russel (September 17, 1880 in Montreal, Quebec - December 7, 1961) was a Canadian amateur ice hockey forward who played for the Montreal Victorias.

Blair helped the Victorias to several Stanley Cup wins during his career. He was inducted into the Hockey Hall of Fame in 1965.
