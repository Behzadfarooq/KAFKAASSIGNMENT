The titles listed here were held by these individuals on 7 July 2005, the date this picture was taken.

Front Row (L-R):

  • Lula da Silva, President of Brazil
  • Gerhard Schröder, Chancellor of Germany
  • Hu Jintao, President of China
  • George W. Bush, President of the United States
  • Tony Blair, Prime Minister of the United Kingdom (on podium)
  • Jacques Chirac, President of France
  • Manmohan Singh, Prime Minister of India
  • Vladimir Putin, President of Russia
  • Vicente Fox, President of Mexico

Back Row (L-R):

  • Paul Martin, Prime Minister of Canada
  • José Manuel Durão Barroso, President of the European Commission
  • Thabo Mbeki, President of South Africa
  • Silvio Berlusconi, Prime Minister of Italy
  • Kofi Annan, Secretary General of the United Nations
  • Junichiro Koizumi, Prime Minister of Japan
  • Paul Wolfowitz, President of the World Bank

Tony Blair with world leaders making an announcement following the July 7th 2005 terrorist attacks in London. White House photo by Eric Draper taken from [1]
