Blackie Ke Shou Liang (Chinese: 柯受良; pinyin: Kē Shòu Liáng; born 1953, China; died December 9, 2003, Shanghai, China) is famous Chinese film director, producer, stuntman and actor. His son is actor Alan Ke You Lun. Blackie died from blood poisoning.
