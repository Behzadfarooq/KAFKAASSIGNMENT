This is my junk collection. It will consist of things I run across on Wikipedia that make me think to put them on this page. Other than that I'm not going to try to justify why I put stuff on here, because that would defeat the point -- it's supposed to be completely random. The only qualification is that they have to be on Wikipedia. They might be images, articles, user pages, BJAODN, templates or userboxes, something I want to mark or flag, or anything else on Wikipedia, or excerpts from any of those things.

Please note that I don't mean that the things I put here are junk -- that refers to the fact that what I include here is completely random, based on my whims, and they will have little or nothing to do with each other.

If you'd like to alert me to something I might like to put on here, put it on the discussion page and drop be a line on my talk page. -- Robert See Hear Speak 05:08, 3 March 2007 (UTC)
