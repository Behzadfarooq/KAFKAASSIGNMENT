Black Flame is an imprint of BL Publishing, the publishing arm of Games Workshop and a sister imprint to the Black Library and Solaris Books. Black Flame is devoted to publishing cult fiction in the fields of science fiction, fantasy and horror.

[edit] Publications

Black Flame publishes novels from licensed properties: New Line Cinema and Rebellion, owners of 2000 AD comic. They have also revived the Dark Future cyberpunk series, created by Games Workshop (with both new books and reprints).

The books are typically “further adventures” using the pre-established characters, but in the case of New Line there are also a select number of film novelizations.
