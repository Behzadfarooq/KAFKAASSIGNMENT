Blac Haze is a rapper from the Florida Keys. Little is known about the rapper because of the lack of publicity. His real name is Ted Outler.

[edit] Musical Background

Blac Haze has a dark and low voice. However, he is not to be confused with Tupac Shakur. A skit from Blac Haze's album, Res-Sa-Rec-Shun, was a recorded news media coverage reporting on Blac Haze and the Tupac theory. The track, Miami Jams, explicitly stated that Blac was honored to be compared to the rap legend but said he was not infact Tupac, Blac Haze may rap like Tupac as part of a marketing strategy to sell more albums as the public becomes more convinced. On the album cover of Res-Sa-Rec-Shun, one can clearly see the difference between the facial structure of Tupac and Blac. The album cover of the single Let Me Holla at Cha, shows the tombstone and dark identity of Blac Haze to allude the themes of Tupac from the music video, Hail Mary.
