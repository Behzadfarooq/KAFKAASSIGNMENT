Black Cat Music

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Black Cat Music is a punk rock band from San Francisco Bay Area, California. They were formed in the summer of 1998 by former members of bands such as The Criminals and the Receivers. The music combined elements of punk & rock and roll with dark, poetic lyrics. After its formation, Black Cat Music released an EP & first full length record on Cheetah's records. Soon thereafter, Black Cat Music signed to Lookout! Records. The band played live shows up and down the west coast, and on several national tours. In 2004, the band released a video for the song "the Jet Trash", directed by Vincent Haycock.
