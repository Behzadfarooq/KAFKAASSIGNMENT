Blast Tyrant is the sixth full-length studio album by rock band Clutch. It was released in 2004 (see 2004 in music), and contains 15 tracks. The unabridged title of the album is Blast Tyrant's Atlas of the Invisible World Including Illustrations of Strange Beasts And Phantasms.
