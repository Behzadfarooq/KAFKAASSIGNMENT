Blackburn Rovers Ladies Football Club are an English women's football club affiliated with Blackburn Rovers. In 2006 they won the Northern Championship and thus were promoted to the Women's Premiership, where they play in the 2006-07 season.

[edit] Honours

  • FA Women's Premier League Northern Division: 2005-06 champions
  • Lancashire Women's Challenge Cup: 2003-04, 2004-05, 2005-06, 2006-2007 champions
