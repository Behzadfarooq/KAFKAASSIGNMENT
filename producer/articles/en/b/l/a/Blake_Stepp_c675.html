Blake Stepp

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Blake Roy Stepp (born February 4, 1982 in Eugene, Oregon) is an American basketball player who graduated from Gonzaga University and was drafted by the Minnesota Timberwolves in the 2004 NBA Draft. He appeared in a handful of 2004-05 preseason games with the Wolves, but did not manage to make the final cut. He then spent the 2004/05 season playing in Belgrade, Serbia with KK Partizan. During the offseason Stepp joined Valencia BC (Pamesa) in the Spanish League (ACB) for the 2005/06 season. Currently, he is a free agent awaiting an invite.

Stepp was the WCC player of the year in his Junior and Senior seasons.

Stepp attended South Eugene High School in Eugene, OR.
