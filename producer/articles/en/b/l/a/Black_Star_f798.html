Black Star

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Black star can refer to any of the following;

[edit] Astronomy

  • Saturn, is referred to as "Black Star" in ancient Judaeic beliefs
  • Dark star, a theoretical star that has a surface escape velocity that equals or exceeds the speed of light
  • Black hole, any region of space time where escape to the outside universe is impossible
  • Black dwarf, a type of degenerate dwarf star, specifically, a cold white dwarf.

[edit] Music

  • Blackstar (band), a heavy metal band formed by former members of Carcass
  • Black Star (hip hop group), a hip hop group formed by rappers Mos Def and Talib Kweli
  • Black Star (album), an album by the above group
  • Black Star (Radiohead song), a song from Radiohead's album The Bends
  • Black Star (Yngwie Malmsteen Song), a song from Yngwie Malmsteen's album Rising Force
