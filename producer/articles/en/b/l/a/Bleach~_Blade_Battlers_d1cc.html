Bleach: Blade Battlers (BLEACH: ブレイド・バトラーズ) is a video game for the PlayStation 2 based on the manga and anime series Bleach. It is a 4-player fighting game that appears to be similar in style to Capcom's Power Stone series.

[edit] Playable characters

The game offers five playable characters at first; Ichigo Kurosaki, Rukia Kuchiki, Orihime Inoue, Yasutora Sado and Uryū Ishida. As players finish off the missions in the main mission mode, more characters are unlocked depending on the ranks obtained. Acquiring an "S" ranking awards the player 3 marks (applicable only in "Captain" difficulty), getting an "A" ranking will award the player 2 marks while getting a "B" ranking awards the player with 1 mark. The player receives none if he or she obtains a "C" or a lower ranking. The more marks the player gets, the faster he or she unlocks the characters.

[edit] Game modes

  • Story: One of five original stories made for the game. With each completed episode, more stages are unlocked.
  • Mission: With two individual modes, one consisting of battles with specific objectives and/or handicaps. With points earned in this mode, you can unlock more characters. The other allows you to complete missions to unlock the ability for characters to use other character's special attacks.
  • Free Battle: As the name suggests, a free battle mode. Free for all, or Tag (2 vs 2) battles are available here. This mission can be played up to four players.
  • Training: Allows you to practice your combos and such against a dummy
  • Urahara Shoten: Allows you to buy unlockables, such as menu characters and figurines, with money earned from the other modes.
  • Options: Allows you to edit various options, including the main menu operator and difficulty level.

[edit] Other

  • The game environment appears to be destructible.
  • A Bleach: Blade Battlers OST CD with a 2007 calendar is being released along side the game.
  • Blade Battlers will be the second game to actually show Rukia's zanpakuto's shikai in full form, aside from Heat the Soul 3.
