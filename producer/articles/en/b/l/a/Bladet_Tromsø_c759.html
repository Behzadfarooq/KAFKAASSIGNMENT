Bladet Tromsø

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Bladet Tromsø is a daily newspaper published in Tromsø, Norway by Bladet Tromsø A/S.

In 2006 the paper had an average circulation of about 11000. It distributes daily, except sundays. It's competitor is Bladet Nordlys. It's goal is to be the best paper in Tromsø, both by images and text.

The newspaper holds about ten journalists, and four photographers.
