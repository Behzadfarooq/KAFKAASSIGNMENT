Blas Pérez (born 13 March 1981 in Panama, Panama) is a football forward who currently plays in Colombia for Primera A team Cúcuta Deportivo.

He made his debut for the Panama national football team in 2000 against Guatemala.

With Cucuta, Perez Won The National Tittle In 2006 Against Deportes Tolima. In 2007 Cucuta and Blas Perez made their debut in Copa Libertadores, in which Blas had currenlty scored 2 goals.
