Blazon Stone is a 6th album by German band Running Wild.

"Genocide" is a Thin Lizzy cover.
"Little Big Horn" tells about Battle of the Little Bighorn in 1876.
"Billy The Kid" tells about famous 19th century American outlaw and murderer Billy the Kid.
