This page has been created to keep track of information on the Wikipedia editor Ronald20, and to help other Wikipedia editors coordinate their efforts regarding Ronald20 (talk • contribs)/204.108.96.10 (talk • contribs).

My guess is that Ronald20 is a teenager with either a learning disability or some behavioral problem (OCD or Asperger's perhaps). He doesn't seem to respond to any attempts at communication, but I have seen some ability to learn by example. Because of that, one thing that I've been trying to do is not just doing reverts on all of his edits, but instead, for some of the articles, I also try to cleanup the article. I'll do some wikification, add more info, add references, add more links, etc.

Right now the information on Ronald20 is located in numerous places, including:

  • Wikipedia:Vandalism in progress#Registered Users (for Ronald20)
    • & Wikipedia:Vandalism in progress#IP addresses (for 204.108.96.10) Vandalism in progress reports
  • Wikipedia:Administrators' noticeboard/3RR#user:Ronald20 / user: 204.108.96.10 report of 3 Reverts violation
  • User talk:Ronald20 Ronald20's talk page
  • User talk:204.108.96.10 one of the IPs that Ronald20 has used at the Los Angeles Unified School District
  • User talk:BlankVerse#Ronald20, aka 204.108.96.10 my talk page
  • Wikipedia:Votes for undeletion#Ronald Molina discussion of his deleted vanity page
  • User talk:Lucky 6.9 #Ronald20 + SoCal WikiProject discussion with an Wikipedia editor familar with individuals with Asperger's

Note: Ronald20 has often added information that looks, at first glace, that it could possibly be true, but on further investigation proves to be false. One example was his repeated insertion of information about a "Stairway Of Death" onto the CBS Columbia Square. Another example was his insertion of the following info into the KDIS article "KDIS abandoned Radio Disney entirely in 2006, and went news." [1]. It is my opinion that the information entered by this editor can not be trusted and that absolutely everything this editor has entered on the Wikipedia probably needs verification. BlankVerse ∅ 08:35, 5 August 2005 (UTC)
