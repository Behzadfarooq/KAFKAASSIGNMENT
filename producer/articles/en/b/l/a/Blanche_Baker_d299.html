Blanche Baker (born December 20, 1956 in New York City) is an American actress. She won the Emmy Award for Supporting Actress in a Comedy or Drama Series in 1978 for her performance as Anna Weiss in the miniseries Holocaust (miniseries). She is the daughter of actress Carroll Baker and director Jack Garfein.

Baker was well known in the late 1970s and 1980s for her work in movies such as Cold Feet and Sixteen Candles.

Even today she still makes appearances on television shows such as Law & Order.
