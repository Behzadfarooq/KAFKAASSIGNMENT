Blake Clark

Blake Clark, born on February 2, 1946 in Macon, Georgia is a stand-up comedian, actor and also veteran from the Vietnam War.

He graduated from LaGrange College in 1969 with a degree in the performing arts.

Clark is frequently cast in Adam Sandler films, including The Waterboy (as "Farmer Fran"), Mr. Deeds, 50 First Dates, and Little Nicky. He also has numerous television appearances, with recurring roles on Home Improvement as Harry the hardware store owner, Boy Meets World as Shawn's father, Chet Hunter, and the first season of The Drew Carey Show.
