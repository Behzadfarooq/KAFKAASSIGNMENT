Blade Thompson (born in Montana, USA) is an American pornographic movie director. According to one interview, his father was in the military and Blade lived in Germany for a few years as a youth. He worked as a pornographic actor (porn star) who appeared in gay and bisexual porn movies in the 1990s. Blade is bald, and has worn a toupee for many of his films.

As a porn star, Thompson was known for his smooth, muscular body, his short-cropped blond hair. He is known for a large penis. He performed in both the top and bottom roles. He married in 1998 and moved into directing.
