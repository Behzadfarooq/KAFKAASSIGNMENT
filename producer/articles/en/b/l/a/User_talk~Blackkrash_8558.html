[edit] Copyrighted Images

Hi Blackkrash,

I'm afraid that Picaroon is quite correct - the Cyrax image on your user page will need to be removed as it is a breach of Wiki's Fair Use guidelines to feature the image other than to illustrate an appropriate article. It's also worth noting that first, it is not necessary to inform users in advance that they are in breach of fair use copyright, as a copyright violation is in itself grounds for Speedy Deletion (see WP:COPYVIO) and secondly, that absolutely anyone may edit any page on Wikipedia, expect for special protected pages, including individual user pages. It's just the way that Wikipedia works, I hope you understand.

As you prefer to do this yourself, perhaps you would prefer to remove the copyright infringing image yourself? Many thanks StuartDouglas 14:20, 9 March 2007 (UTC)
