Blair Mastbaum is an American writer who lives in Brooklyn, New York City.

His first novel, Clay's Way was released in 2004. The novel won a Lambda Literary Award and was recognized for excellence by the New York Public Library.[citation needed] His second novel, "Push," is being published by Carroll & Graf Publishers and will be released in 2008.[citation needed]
