Blackfield II is the second studio album by Blackfield. It was recorded in both Tel Aviv and London, and was released on February 13, 2007 in Europe and March 6, 2007 in the US.

We Put Out Records have launched a media site relating to the US release of this record.

  2. "1,000 People" (M: Geffen; L: Geffen/Wilson) - 3:54
  3. "Miss U" (M/L: Geffen) - 4:13
  4. "Christenings" (M/L: Wilson) - 4:37
  5. "This Killer" (M/L: Geffen) - 4:06
  6. "Epidemic" (M: Geffen; L: Geffen/Wilson) - 4:59

[edit] Lineup

  • Steven Wilson - vocals, lead guitar
  • Aviv Geffen - vocals, guitars, rhythm guitar, additional keyboards
  • Daniel Salomon - backing vocals, piano, keyboards
  • Seffy Efrat - backing vocals, bass guitar
  • Tomer Z - drums, percussion

[edit] Guest Musicans

  • Itamar Leshem - french horn on "1,000 People".
  • Richard Barbieri - electric piano and stylophone on "Christenings".
  • Gavin Harrison - drums on "Christenings".
  • Daniela Pick - backing vocals on "Epidemic".
  • Eran Mitelman - electric piano on "My Gift of Silence".
