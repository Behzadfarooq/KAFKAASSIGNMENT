If you cannot supply source information for these images, including Image:WhoisBond.jpg, Image:Bart2.gif, Image:Apelsin.jpg, they may be listed for speedy deletion after 7 days have passed.

If you own the copyright for any of these images (i.e., they are your own creative work or your own photographs), you can release them under one or several free licenses so that they can remain on Wikipedia. If you believe the image is under a free license or in the public domain, indicate this and why you think so on the image's page. If you obtained these images from copyrighted web sites, you may be able to assert fair use so the image may be used by Wikipedia. See Wikipedia:Fair use#Images to see if your image qualifies. If it does, you can replace the {{no source}} or {{no license}} template on the image's page with one of Wikipedia's fair use templates. If an image remains without source and license for more than 7 days, it will automatically become a candidate for speedy deletion and may be removed from Wikipedia.

[edit] Image copyright problem with Image:Vakarchuk.jpg

Thanks for uploading Image:Vakarchuk.jpg. However, the image may soon be deleted unless we can determine the copyright holder and copyright status. The Wikimedia Foundation is very careful about the images included in Wikipedia because of copyright law (see Wikipedia's Copyright policy).

The copyright holder is usually the creator, the creator's employer, or the last person who was transferred ownership rights. Copyright information on images is signified using copyright templates. The three basic license types on Wikipedia are open content, public domain, and fair use. Find the appropriate template in Wikipedia:Image copyright tags and place it on the image page like this: {{TemplateName}}.

Please signify the copyright information on any other images you have uploaded or will upload. Remember that images without this important information can be deleted by an administrator. If you have any questions, feel free to contact me, or ask them at the Image legality questions page. Thank you. Shyam (T/C) 19:47, 6 April 2006 (UTC)

[edit] Removal of deletion tags

Please do not remove speedy deletion tags from articles. If you do not believe the article deserves to be deleted, then please do the following:

  1. Place {{hangon}} on the page. Please do not remove any existing speedy deletion tag(s).
  2. Make your case on the article's talk page.

Administrators will look at your reasoning before deciding what to do with the article. Thank you.  – Tivedshambo (talk) 20:28, 13 February 2007 (UTC)
