1x02 "Ah! Can the One Who Believes be Saved?" ああっ信じる者は救われるっ？ "Aa! Shinjiru Mono ha Tsuwareru" 2005-01-13
Keiichi wishes for Belldandy to stay with him forever. In the process he is caught with a girl in a male-only dorm, and gets kicked out.
1x03 "Ah! Studying, My Family, and a Goddess!" ああっ修行と我が家と女神さまっ "Aa! Shuugyou to Wagake to Megami-sama!" 2005-01-20
A coincidental gust of wind leads Keiichi and Belldandy to a Buddhist temple. The Buddhist priest, after mistaking Keiichi for a thief, allows them to stay on the condition that they maintain the temple.
1x04 "Ah! The Queen and the Goddess!" ああっ女王さまと女神さまっ "Ah! Joou-sama to Megami-sama!" 2005-01-27
Keiichi takes Belldandy to a campus tour. Belldandy receives a lot of attention from Sayoko Mishima and many others and her non-official status at NIT starts to pose a problem.
1x05 "Ah! Living under one roof!" ああっひとつ屋根の下でっ "Aa! Hitotsu Yane no Shita de!" 2005-02-03
Fueled by social pressure, Keiichi slowly comes to a realisation of his feelings towards Belldandy. He also starts feeling under pressure, believing that he has been "enslaving" Belldandy by inadvertently forcing her to stay with him.
1x06 "Ah! Unexpectedly finding something blessed?" ああっ掘り出しものに恵アリっ？ "Aa! Hori Dashi Mono ni Megumi Ari?" 2005-02-10
Megumi Morisato (Keiichi's sister) moves to town, as she was accepted to the same university as her brother. With a little help from Belldandy, they manage to find a place to live. However the place is "haunted" by a third-class Earth spirit.
1x07 "Ah! A place to express your feelings!" ああっ想い伝える場所っ "Aa! Omoi tsutaeru bashou!" 2005-02-17
Belldandy, while getting used to Earth customs, learns about Valentine's day. Naively, she asks Sayoko Mishima about "romantic places" and is told to ask Toshiyuki Aoshima, the campus playboy, instead. Toshiyuki takes Belldandy to a hotel room.
1x08 "Ah! Taking An Examination of Love, Starting with A Deviation Value of 30!" ああっ偏差値３０からの恋愛受験っ "Aa! Hensatsuchi Sanjuu kara no Renai Juken!" 2005-02-24
While trying to find medication for Belldandy's flu-like symptoms, Keiichi meets a pleasant pharmacist - in reality, Urd in disguise.
