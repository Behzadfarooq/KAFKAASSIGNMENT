Blair Dunlop (born 11 February 1992) is an English actor who made his acting debut in "Charlie and the Chocolate Factory", directed by Tim Burton. Blair plays the young Willy Wonka, a child under strict rule by his father, a dentist, Dr. Wilbur Wonka (Christopher Lee).

Blair was also on an English television show called "Rocket Man"[1], supposedly based on a nineteenth century railway engineer. Blair played a character named Caleb McNulty for two episodes.

Blair Dunlop has a growing fan club: a testimony to his sensational talents, both as actor, and singer-songwriter.

[edit] Trivia

As an avid supporter of the football clubs Tottenham Hotspur, Real Betis and Fiorentina, he has a particular fondness for players past and present with excessively styled hair, such as Rafael Sobis, Erik Edman and Ricardo Rocha

Blair the son of folk musician, Ashley Hutchings: formerly a member of Fairport Convention, and currently of the Rainbow Chasers.
