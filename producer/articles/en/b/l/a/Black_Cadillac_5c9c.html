Black Cadillac is Rosanne Cash's album, released in 2006. The album was a reflection on the passing of Cash's mother (Vivian Liberto), father (Johnny Cash), and her stepmother, (June Carter Cash), all within a fifteen-month period. The album was nominated for a Grammy Award for Best Contemporary Folk album in 2007.
