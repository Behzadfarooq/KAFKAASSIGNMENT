Blake Tuomy-Wilhoit and Dylan Tuomy-Wilhoit (born November 29, 1990 in Los Angeles, California) are identical twin American actors, most famous for their role as Nicky and Alex Katsopolis on the popular TV show Full House.

[edit] Trivia

Blake and Dylan Tuomy-Wilhoit
  • Dylan is 19 minutes older than Blake.
  • They are cousins of actress Lisa Wilhoit.
  • Blake and Dylan share a profile on MySpace that promotes their charity based fanclub.
  • Featured in the movie "I Eat Dung Chips".
  • Dylan wants to return to professional acting unlike his brother Blake.
