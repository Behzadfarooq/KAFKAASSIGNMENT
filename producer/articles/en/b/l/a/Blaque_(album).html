Blaque's first studio album. Their self-titled debut includes their Billboard Hot 100 Chart top 10 singles "808" & "Bring It All To Me". The album earned them a nomination at the Soul Train Lady Of Soul Awards for R&B/Soul Album Of The Year, Group, Band Or Duo. Although the album did not crack the Top 50 on the Billboard 200, it remained on the chart for some time eventually going Platinum.
