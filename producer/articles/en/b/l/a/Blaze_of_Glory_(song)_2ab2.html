"Blaze of Glory" is a song by Jon Bon Jovi which hit number one on the Billboard Hot 100 in 1990, his only chart-topper away from his band Bon Jovi. The song also went to number one in Australia for six weeks. The song peaked at number two in the UK. The song is a crowd favourite with Bon Jovi fans, even though the song wasn't released as one of his band's singles.

[edit] Appearances

It first appeared in the motion picture Young Guns II, for which it was originally recorded. It later appeared as the title track on his solo album Blaze of Glory. Next, it appeared on Cross Road, Bon Jovi's Greatest Hits album. Finally, it appeared on Bon Jovi's live DVD Live From London.
