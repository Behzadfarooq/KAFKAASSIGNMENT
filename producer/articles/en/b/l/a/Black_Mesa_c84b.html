Black Mesa can have different meanings:

[edit] Real World

  • Black Mesa is the highest point in the U.S. state of Oklahoma, at 4973 feet
  • Black Mesa State Park, an Oklahoma state park which includes Black Mesa Mountain
  • Black Mesa Test Range, a US Army rocket testing facility (located at 37°22′N, 109°17′W). Pershing 1 rockets were test fired here between 1962 and 1971. [1]
  • Black Mesa, Arizona, an upland area in Navajo County, Arizona.
