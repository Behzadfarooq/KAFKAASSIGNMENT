[edit] Django-->Jango

I suggest that we officialy use "Jango" when refering to the character, as opposed to "Django".

In addition to Jango fitting the Katakana better (ジャンゴ, Jango), Jango is also more popular of a term:

Google search:
Jango "One Piece": 57,600 hits
Django "One Piece": 46,500 hits

deviantART search:
Jango "One Piece": 112 hits
Django "One Piece": 19 hits

This fits the criteria, does it not?

That's pretty fair of an assessment. To further support that spelling, the author has written the name in Roman characters on his wanted poster, and the spelling he used was indeed "JANGO". --Julian Grybowski 17:11, 16 September 2006 (UTC)
