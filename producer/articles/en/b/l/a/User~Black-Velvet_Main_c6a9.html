Greetings, and welcome to my user page (and sorry about the length, I'll get on to that later). Sakhalin Cruschev, is the psuedonym that I use with most of my interactions with Wikipedia, and it is derived from Sakhalin, the Russian island north of Japan that I have an abstract affinity for. My interests include Wikipedia, surfing the net, reading anything that will increase my academic integrity, the GTA series, and John Cale. My main activities on Wikipedia are various, and you will often see me whiling my time away stub-sorting.
