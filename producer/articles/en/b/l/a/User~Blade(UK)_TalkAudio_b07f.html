[edit] Introduction

Talkaudio is a UK based internet forum primarily offering advice, suggestions, recommendations and troubleshooting for those wishing to upgrade their automotive sound system, or car audio as it is known.

Steady growth over its 6 years of existence has placed Talkaudio as the 521st largest internet forum in the world by posts made, according to Big-Boards.com. Talkaudio is now renowned as the foremost forum for car audio within the United Kingdom.

Talkaudio's profile received a significant boost in 2004 when the website and it's members were featured in the UK's Total Car Audio magazine, which included interviews with a number of members.


[edit] History

Talkaudio was created in July of 2000, and has rapidly expanded since, and reached 2,000,000 posts by nearly 20,000 members in August 2006.

Talkaudio initially started as part of the Car Audio Direct website, but developed into a distinctly separate entity in October 2006. Following this change, Talkaudio has developed into an online car audio magazine bringing news and reviews of industry developments and new products, in addition to containing a comprehensive section of FAQ articles on the subject.


[edit] Members

Since its conception, Talkaudio has attracted a variety of members from within the UK, Europe, Asia and the USA, including notable names from within the SPL and SQ competition scene. This has resulted in coverage by UK motoring magazines Fast Car, Max Power and Total Car Audio.

Talkaudio members have set several SPL world records at UK events, and those held in European events.


[edit] Team Talkaudio

In 2003, Team Talkaudio was founded, with the aims of:

- Increasing co-operation and sharing best practice
- Encouraging new competitors by providing support
- Raising the profile of Talkaudio at shows and events

An enthusiastic core of members competing regularly under the Team Talkaudio name ensured a presence at many competitions and shows henceforth.


[edit] The TALON SPL League

Launched in 2005, the TALON SPL league provides a permanent record of the personal best SPL scores of forum members who attend the official forum "T" meets. The league is free to enter, and competitors are placed into suitable categories based on number of speakers used in the audio system, and vehicle modifications. This ensures fair and comparable scores amongst competitors. Entry to the league is completely free for all.


[edit] Industry Presence

Since its early days, the Talkaudio forum has had a significant industry presence, including representatives and owners of many car audio manufacturers and distributors. Over the period of the forum's operation, this has included Alpine, Vibe, Genesis, ShadowWorks, Directed Audio and BBG Distribution

Talkaudio has also served as a lauch platform for a number of distribution companies seeking to bring new brands of equipment into the United Kingdom. Noteable examples include Nearfield Audio, Innovation Audio, Platinum ICE and Utopia Audio.

In 2006, the forum also covered the launch of The ICE Register, an innovative service providing added security for those with aftermarket car audio systems, a first within the industry.


[edit] Trends & Fads

Despite membership being spread throughout the United Kingdom, the close-knit atmosphere of Talkaudio has resulted in a high volume of meme occurrences amongst the regular forum users.


[edit] Recent Developments

Talkaudio recently partnered with EMMA, a European sound system competition format, and now provides a dedicated sub-forum for discussion of the format.
