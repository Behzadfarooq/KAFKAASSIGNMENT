Black Earth is the first album by the Swedish melodic death metal band Arch Enemy.

[edit] Track listing

  1. "Bury Me an Angel" – 3:40
  2. "Dark Insanity" – 3:16
  3. "Eureka" – 4:44
  4. "Idolatress" – 4:56
  5. "Cosmic Retribution" – 4:00
  6. "Demoniality" – 1:19
  7. "Transmigration Macabre" – 4:09
  8. "Time Capsule" – 1:09
  9. "Fields of Desolation" – 5:31
  10. "Losing Faith" – 3:16
  11. "The Ides of March" – 1:46 (Cover Iron Maiden)
  12. "Aces High" – 4:23 (Cover Iron Maiden)
"Losing Faith" and "The Ides of March" are bonus tracks on the re-issue by Century Media, and Regain; the Regain edition also has "Aces High".
