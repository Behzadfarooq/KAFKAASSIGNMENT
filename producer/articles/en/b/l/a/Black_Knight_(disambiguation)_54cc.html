Black Knight may mean:

  • Black Knight, a knight not bound to a specific liege
  • Black Knight (Arthurian legend), any of several knights clad in black armor in Arthurian legend
  • Black Knight (comics), the name of several characters in the Marvel Comics universe
  • Black Knight (Monty Python), a fictional character in the film Monty Python and the Holy Grail
  • The Black Knight (1954 Film), a film starring Alan Ladd
  • Black Knight (film), starring Martin Lawrence
  • Black Knight (rocket), part of the British rocketry programme in the 1950s
  • Black knight (business), the unfriendly bidder in a hostile takeover
  • Black Knights, an underground hiphop group
  • Black Knight (pinball), a pinball game designed by Steve Ritchie for Williams Electronics.
  • Black Knight (racquet manufacturer), a Canadian racquet manufacturer

Black Knight may also be:

  • Arpine Lusène, a fictional villain in several Scrooge McDuck stories created by Keno Don Rosa
  • Knight of Glin, an Irish hereditary title
  • The official nickname of athletic teams at the United States Military Academy
  • A masked wrestler, either Glen Jacobs or Jeff Gaylord, who competed in the 1993 Survivor Series
  • In role-playing games, a term for a warrior who serves evil; also know as anti-paladin, Dark Knight or Blackguard.
  • On The Young and The Restless the character Jack Abbott (Peter Bergman) has nicknamed Victor Newman (Eric Braeden)"The Black Knight" due to the fact that Victor and Jack are enemies and the fact Victor has been for the most part victorious over Jack in the many battles they had.
  • Black Knight is a card from the game Magic: The Gathering with the ability Protection from White which protected it from its alterego in Magic, the White Knight.
  • The Black Knight is one of the central villains in Fire Emblem: Path of Radiance
