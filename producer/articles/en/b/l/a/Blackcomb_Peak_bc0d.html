Blackcomb Peak

View from the top of Blackcomb Glacier
Elevation 2436 m (7992 ft)
Location British Columbia, Canada
Range Fitzsimmons Range
Prominence 86 m
Coordinates 50°04′53″N, 122°52′15″W
Topo map NTC 92J/02
First ascent 1923 P. Munday, D. Munday
Easiest route class 3

Blackcomb Peak is located southeast of Whistler, British Columbia and is part of the Whistler-Blackcomb ski resort. Like Whistler Mountain, it is located on the edge of Garibaldi Provincial Park and the ski lifts are often used to access the park, particularly for The Spearhead Traverse.
