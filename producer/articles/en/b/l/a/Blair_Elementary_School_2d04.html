Archibald Blair Elementary School in Richmond, British Columbia is an elementary school.

It is located at 6551 Lynas Lane, adjacent to the Thompson Community Centre and Burnett Secondary School. As of 2006, the current school principal is Mark Porter, and the vice-principal is Jenny Martin.
