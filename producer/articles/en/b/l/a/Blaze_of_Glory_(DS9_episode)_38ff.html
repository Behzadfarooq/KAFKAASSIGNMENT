"Blaze of Glory" is an episode of the television series Star Trek: Deep Space Nine, the twenty-third episode of the fifth season.

[edit] Plot summary

Spoiler warning: Plot and/or ending details follow.

The few surviving members of the Maquis launch powerful missiles at Cardassian targets. Captain Sisko meets with the traitor Michael Eddington in order to gain information. When Eddington refuses to help, Sisko removes him from prison and brings him with on the mission. Eddington soon promises to kill Sisko.

The two go through several dangerous events, even literally fighting side by side, before the real truth is ferreted out. Eddington dies in the end, sacrificing himself for others.
