Black Army

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Black Army can refer to several different groups and affiliations:

  • The supporter club of the Swedish sports association Allmänna Idrottsklubben (AIK)
  • Several anarchist factions of the Russian Civil War, also known as the Black Guards
  • The royal army of Matthias Corvinus, a 15th century king of Hungary, mentioned in its national anthem as well
  • The Revolutionary Insurrectionary Army of Ukraine (see the article on Nestor Makhno)
  • Black Flag Army, a splinter remnant of the Taiping rebels
