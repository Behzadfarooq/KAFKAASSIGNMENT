Black Pearl (disambiguation)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Black Pearl may refer to :

  • The Black Pearl [[1]], symphonic metal band, with one of the best female voices in rock music.
  • Pelé, a Brazilian football (soccer) player, who won three World Cup medals and broke many records, also known as, The Black Pearl (Pérola Negra).
  • Eusébio, a Mozambique and Portuguese football player, also known as, The Black Pearl (Pérola Negra).
  • Pirates of the Caribbean: The Curse of the Black Pearl (2003), the Movie.
  • The Ship, the Black Pearl from the Pirates of the Caribbean movies.
  • The Black Pearl (1977), is a Drama Movie. Was released in 1977. Directed & Directed by Saul Swimmer.
  • The Black Pearl (comic book) (1996), by Eric Johnson and Mark Hamill with artwork by Bruce Patterson and H.M. Baker.
  • A black pearl is a rare tinted pearl; a hard, rounded object produced by certain animals, primarily mollusks such as oysters.
  • The Black Pearl (Scott O'Dell) (1967), is a children story book, by Scott O'Dell.
  • The Black Pearl is a book written by Nancy Mann Waddel Woodrow (1870-1935). It was re-released in 2005.
  • The Black-Pearl is a gene (blp) in Drosophila (small flies) Genome Project.
