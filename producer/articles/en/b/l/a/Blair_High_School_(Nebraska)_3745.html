Blair High School (Nebraska)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

It has been suggested that Iowa side be merged into this article or section. (Discuss)

Blair High School is a public high school in Blair, Nebraska (USA). The 140,000 square-foot building was built in 1970 at a cost of $2.2 million. Siting on 75 acres of land, it services approximately 750 students. It has been listed on the National Register of Historic Places since 1991. Blair High School received the U.S. Department of Education's Secondary School Recognition Award in 1989.
