[edit] War

[edit] Premise

During an attack on an Iraqi bunker, lead by Colonel Cane McDuff, the bunker is successfully secured, in the ensuring conflict, a black hole sweeps over the Earth causing major damage around the globe. Thousands of people suddenly disappear into thin air. They are all transported (including every soldier in Iraq) 800 years into the future where the planet is completely devoid of any human life and is ruled by an iron fist by the evil alien's known as the "Klons". Now, complete strangers must work together with extra-terristrial inhabitants of Earth

[edit] Shadow

Another new show I'm the middle of writing

[edit] Premise

In the year 2010, Tommy Sullivan is released from the state prison of California after serving five years in prison for the illegal distribution of Marijuana. On the day of his release, he is approached by the QUARTERS Foundation who offer him the chance to drive a new suped-up high-tech super-car for the government that they have been developing for the past decade. He reluctantly accepts and gains a partner, Morgan Nimmo a FBI detective and ex-newscaster, and Billy Quarters, the mechanic and son of the founder of the QUARTERS Foundation, William Quarters. Together, they solve crimes that baffle even the most trained of minds.
