Ai wo Utau yori Ore ni Oborero! (愛を歌うより俺に溺れろ!), or Blaue Rosen (the title used by Chuangyi's English translation), is a shojo manga series by Mayu Shinjo. In Japan, the short hand "Ai Ore" (愛俺) is often used for the series. "Blaue Rosen" refers to the Blue Roses, a band in the series; the term is German for "blue roses".

A drama CD has been released, and the story is adapted from vol. 2 of the manga, where the St. Nobara girls go for a school gathering, and Akira decides to tag along. Also, some dialogue from vol. 1 are featured. Another drama CD is scheduled to be released in May 2007.

[edit] Plot

Akira Shiraishi, a boy widely regarded as the "princess" in his all-boys high school, Dankaisan (男海山), decides to pursue Mizuki Sakurazaka, the "prince" of the neighbouring all-girls Saint Nobara (聖 野薔薇) High. Concealing his true sex to most of the students at St. Nobara's, he even manages to join Mizuki's band, the "Blue Roses". Mizuki, priding herself to be a tough tomboy, soon finds herself at a loss: Should she maintain her reputation as a "bishounen" and prince in her school, or should she accept Akira, who never fails to make her feel like a woman every time they were alone? Also, would Akira be able to keep his true identity a secret among the girls of St. Nobara, while his schoolmates wish to retain their princess?

[edit] Characters

[edit] Dankaisan

  • Akira Shiraishi (白石 秋羅 Shiraishi Akira; seiyu: Soichiro Hoshi) - The male lead of the series, Akira may look like an innocent bishounen on the surface; he is, after all, the "princess" of his school. However, he is actually quite calculative and cruel if he needs to, or is angered. Disregarding the odds seemingly stacked against him, he seeks out Mizuki as a date. He's also the lead vocalist of Mizuki's band, the Blue Roses (BR for short), and the shortest member. In St. Nobara, only the other members of BR know that he's a guy, until his secret becomes known to Misaki Doujima (see below). He's rather adept at drawing out Mizuki's feminine side, who in turn knows about his "dark side", as she puts it. He's also lecherous, as Mizuki finds out to her horror. Oddly, his weakness is milk; he cannot stand the smell of the liquid, and even trembles if he sees it.
He's the youngest son in the family. His father is a famous boxer, and his three elder brothers are all martial artists in their own right. Due to Akira's reputation in school, they refuse to allow any students from Dankaisan to enter their house. Akira's mother passed away soon after his birth. Despite having a "dark side", one thing is certain about Akira: he would not allow Mizuki to come to harm in any way.
  • Ran Nikkaidou (二階堂 蘭 Nikaidou Ran; Seiyu: Akira Ishida) - The chairman of Dankaisan's student council, and the only son of a police superintendent, Ran was initially displeased at Akira's pursuit of Mizuki. However, he seems to have developed feelings for Mizuki, and tries to hide it under the pretext of obtaining another "princess", the first "princess" being Akira. Unlike Rui, he is less easily swayed by Akira. Mizuki calls him "four-eyes", because of his glasses.
  • Rui Kiryuuin (鬼龍院 留衣 Kiryūin Rui; Seiyu: Takahiro Sakurai) - Ran's deputy in the student council, and the only son of the leader of the largest yazuka in Kantou, Rui is even more displeased over Akira's wooing of Mizuki. He's hot-headed, and thus frequently finds himself manipulated by Akira into helping him with his plans.

[edit] Saint Nobara

It has been implied that students attending St. Nobara largely come from rich families.

  • Mizuki Sakurazaka (桜坂 水樹 Sakurazaka Mizuki; seiyu: Mitsuki Saiga) - The female lead of the series, Mizuki has a personality which could be said to be the opposite of Akira: tough on the outside, yet gentle and sensitive beneath her tomboy facade. She's a first-year student, and the "prince" of her cohort. Despite her confidence in front of other girls, she finds herself pretty helpless when confronted by Akira's advances. Also, she's afraid of pain, so much so that her ears are not pierced. Although she likes Akira, she still has difficulty letting go of her image in front of him; this sometimes leads to quarrels between them. Initially, she harboured an intense dislike for boys, due to the experience she had with a childhood playmate, who made her feel uncomfortable whenever they played together. Ironically, that playmate turned out to be Akira.
She has a younger sister. Also, she's a fan of Sakuya Ookochi, a member of the band Lucifer from Sensual Phrase, another work by Mayu Shinjo.
  • Kaoru Naruse (成瀬 薫 Naruse Kaoru; seiyu: Shiho Kawaragi) - The former lead vocals of BR, Kaoru and Mizuki are fast friends since they were very young. The duo were often considered to be "princes" of their cohort, and both received gifts from fans in large quantities. When her family has to leave for New York, Akira managed to prove his singing skills at the farewell concert, and Kaoru decided that Akira was worthy enough to be BR's next lead vocalist. Before Akira, she's the only person who knows that Mizuki is actually a crybaby.
  • Ai Okita (沖田 愛 Okita Ai; seiyu: Yuki Kaida) - A member of the "Blue Roses", Ai is the drummer of the band. She tolerates little nonsense from Akira, who behaves like a helpless girl in front of her, despite the fact that his secret is known to the BR girls. Her identifying feature is her glasses.
  • Momoko Kidera (木寺 桃子 Kidera Momoko; seiyu: Ryoko Nagata - A member of the "Blue Roses", and the bassist of the band. She has the shortest hair in BR.
  • Misaki Doujima (堂島 美咲 Doujima Misaki; Seiyu: Romi Pak) - A third year student at St. Nobara, she's the "bishounen" and prince of her cohort. She claims to hate boys, but got more than what she bargained for when she tried to seduce Mizuki (even going to the extent of drugging Mizuki's wine); Akira got Rui to force Misaki into bed, and threatened to rape her. Although the boys were stopped by Mizuki, Misaki became the first girl outside "BR" to know Akira's secret, and vowed to expose him someday. However, as an apology to her seduction of Mizuki, Misaki gave her a voucher allowing her to stay at the deluxe suite of a hotel owned by the Doujima family.
  • Reiko Kuroki (黒木 麗子 Kuroki Reiko; seiyu: Shiho Kawaragi) - A close friend of Misaki, Reiko tried to make her advances on Akira, while Misaki did the same to Mizuki. However, she was quickly bound up by Akira.

[edit] Others

  • Shinosuke Sakaki (榊 新之助 Sakaki Shinosuke) - Shinosuke is Mizuki's neighbour, and a professional boxer. He has a crush on Mizuki, and so challenges Akira to a fight for Mizuki's hand. Although the fight was inconclusive, Shinosuke manages to raise Mizuki's doubts about having Akira as a boyfriend, claiming that the boy wasn't masculine enough. Initially shaken by Shinosuke's words, Mizuki was then reassured by Akira, who told her that he doesn't mind being mistaken for a girl by everyone else, as long as Mizuki sees him as a man. Seeing that he's beaten for now, Shinosuke decided to quit boxing. However, he has not completely given up on wooing Mizuki...
He's scheduled to appear in the second drama CD, where he is to be voiced by Kenichi Suzumura.

[edit] Trivia

  • Appearance-wise, Akira greatly resembles Kira Yamato, another character voiced by Akira's seiyu. Incidentally, in that series, Ran's seiyu also voices Athrun Zala, a close friend of Kira.
