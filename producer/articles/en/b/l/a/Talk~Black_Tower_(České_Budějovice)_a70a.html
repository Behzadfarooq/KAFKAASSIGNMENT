[edit] Survey - in opposition to the move

  1. Strongly Oppose Not primary usage; a scholarly search shows Black Towers on the Bosphorus, in Caernarvon, by Yeats, and by Bernard Benoliel, before the subject of this article. If it is moved, it will only have to be moved back; and The Black Tower, the P.D. James novel is already closer to the target than two articles ought to be. Septentrionalis PMAnderson 21:52, 28 February 2007 (UTC)
  2. Oppose It is hard to believe that the Black Tower in České Budějovice is the only notable "Black Tower", real or fictional. I suggest that Black Tower become a disambiguation page, pointing for now to this article plus The Black Tower.--orlady 21:55, 28 February 2007 (UTC)

  4. Oppose I am living in České Budějovice, but I can mot agree with idea: Black Tower in České Budějovice is only or most important on the world... Cinik 12:09, 1 March 2007 (UTC)
