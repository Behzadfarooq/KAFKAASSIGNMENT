Blanchard is a city in Traill County, North Dakota in the United States. The population was 92 at the 2000 census.

Blanchard is located near the North Dakota-Minnesota border, near the junction of North Dakota State Highway 18 and North Dakota State Highway 200.

Near Blanchard stands the KVLY-TV mast, the tallest man-made structure in the world. The KXJB-TV mast, the world's second tallest man-made structure is standing six miles from Blanchard, near Galesburg, North Dakota.

[edit] Geography

Latitude is 47.331202 and the longitude is -97.250777.

The city of Blanchard is 33 square miles (85 km²).
