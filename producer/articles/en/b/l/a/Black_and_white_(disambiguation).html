Black and white (disambiguation)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Black-and-white (or black and white) can refer to a general term used in photography, film, and other media.

Black and White, Black & White or Black+White can also refer to:

  • Black and white (colours), the color combination
  • Black and White (album), several albums
  • "Black and White" (song), several songs
  • Black & White (1998 film), a film directed by Yuri Zeltser
  • Black and White (1999 film), a film directed by James Toback
  • Black and White (2002 film), a film starring Robert Carlyle
  • Black & White (computer game), a computer game developed by Lionhead Studios
  • Black+White, an Australian art and photography magazine
  • Black. White., a reality television show on FX
  • Police car (slang), in many U.S. cities, when painted with a distinctive black and white livery
  • Black and white cookie, a large, round cookie, half-glazed with vanilla and half with chocolate
  • Black & White (whisky), a brand of Scotch whisky
  • Black-and-white thinking, as in a false dichotomy
  • Black & White Records, a record label
  • Black & White (book), a novel written by Dani Shapiro
