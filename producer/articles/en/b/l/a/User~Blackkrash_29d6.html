[edit] The user box collection

I do not have user boxes for the sake of it, but I am interested in them. I make simple user boxes and am now working on making some new ones for friends, if you are interested in help with them, then place it on the right place on my talk page with a request and I will get back to you.

[edit] The name

[edit] History

Blackkrash, a name salvaged from the results of a mental user-name brainstorm for a computer game. I have had this name for a long time, nearly as long as my other ID, Tornaddyne, however I have semi-abandonned Tornaddyne. Blackkrash takes me back over 3 years.

[edit] The use of the letter "K"

Plain and simple, I like "K"s!

[edit] Pronunciation

To answer those who have trouble with pronouncing things, here is the one, true way of saying Blackkrash.

"Black-Crash" or for an even more detailed one "Bla-ck-cra-sh"

And if you do wish to contact me, DO NOT SAY BLACKRASH! IT IS NOT A RASH!
