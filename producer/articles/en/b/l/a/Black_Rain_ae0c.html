Black Rain is the title of several works of fiction. The title is a reference to the black rain that the hibakusha reported some minutes after the atomic bombing of Hiroshima.

  • Black Rain (novel) is a 1965 novel by Japanese author Ibuse Masuji. The events of the novel are centered on the aftermath of the atomic bombing of Hiroshima.
  • Black Rain (Japanese film) (黒い雨; kuroi ame) is a 1989 Japanese film by director Imamura Shohei based on the novel of the same name by Ibuse Masuji.
  • Black Rain (film) is a 1989 American movie directed by Ridley Scott.
  • Black Rain (Tang) (黑雨) is the third novel of a 2002 Tang Haoming novel series based on Qing Military General Zeng Guofan.
  • Black Rain (album) is a 2004 Dark Lotus Album.
  • Black Rain (label) is a German Industrial music label which features Harsh EBM acts such as Feindflug.
