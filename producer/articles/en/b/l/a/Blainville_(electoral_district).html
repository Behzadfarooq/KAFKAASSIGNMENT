Blainville (electoral district)

Blainville is a provincial electoral district in Quebec, Canada that elects members to the National Assembly of Quebec. The municipalities of Blainville, Bois-des-Filion, Lorraine and Sainte-Anne-des-Plaines are situated in this district. The riding was created in 1994 from Groulx and Terrebonne.

[edit] Members of the National Assembly

  1. Céline Signori, Parti Québécois (1994-2001)
  2. Richard Legendre, Parti Québécois (2001-2007)
  3. Pierre Gingras, Action democratique du Quebec (2007-present)
