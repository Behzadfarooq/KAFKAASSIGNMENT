Black List
Compilation album by L.A. Guns
Released 2005

Black List is the second of two L.A. Guns compilation albums featuring their third singer Paul Black.

  3. Show No Mercy
  4. One More Reason To Die

  7. Wired And Wide Awake
  8. One Way Ticket To Love
  9. Name Your Poison
  10. Liquid Diamonds
  11. Love Is A Crime
  12. Winter's Fool
  13. Everything I Do
  14. A Word To The Wise Guy
  15. Roll The Dice
  16. Black City Breakdown
  17. The Devil In You (performed by Black Cherry)
