Black Women Need Love, Too!

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Black Women Need Love, Too! is the third published book by author/activist, Pearl Jr. The book was published on May 21, 2006 by Pearl Jr. Publications. Black Women Need Love, Too! (Exposing The Conspiracy To Keep Black Women Without Love) is a guide showing how to effectively rebuild the happy and successful black family.

The book is the edited version of the book How To Tame A Dawg. The motivation behind the writing of this book is author Pearl Jr.'s beliefs. She believes the only way to end racism is to talk about it in a peaceful manner, the only way to talk about in a peaceful manner is to rebuild the black community, the only way to rebuild the black community is to rebuild the black family and buying her book will show you how to rebuild the black family and subsequently kill racism.

Nicknamed, "the book with solutions," Black Women Need Love, Too! strongly shows a disgust against rap music. Since it's release, Pearl Jr. has embarked on an ambitious promotional tour. She has appeared on the radio promoting the book well over a dozen times. She was interviewed by Gigi Iam on her show, The Gigi Iam Show in Southern California.[1]

She has held many successful book signings and has promoted the books at many big events including the African Market. [2]

A commercial was made to promote the book.[3] All types of merchandise, including t-shirts, were created to promote the book.[4]

The book's official website, www.blackwomenneedlovetoo.com, won the "Best Author Site" at the 2007 Black Web Awards.[5] The book is currently on sale at www.AMAZON.com.[6]
