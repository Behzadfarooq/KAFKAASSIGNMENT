[edit] G'day!

G'day there, Blackmissionary, and welcome to Wikipedia! Good authors are always welcome on the project, and I hope you like the place and decide to stick around!

[edit] Recent edits

Hi mate. The reason behind the first edit was due to the fact that Victorian Premier Cricket is often referred to as the Victorian Premier League, although the soccer league is the most dominant comp with that name so it deserves the title. However, the Victorian (soccer) Premier League is never referred to as "Victorian Premier Cricket", so there is no need to include a link to the soccer league on that page.

For the second edit, I move Peter Moore (Australian rules footballer) to Peter Moore (footballer) due to the fact that there are no other footballers (across codes such as rugby league, soccer, gridiron etc) with that name, therefore it would make sense to use the shortest description. Hope that answered your query. Cheers, Rogerthat Talk

[edit] License tagging for Image:SeagullsDesign.png

Thanks for uploading Image:SeagullsDesign.png. Wikipedia gets hundreds of images uploaded every day, and in order to verify that the images can be legally used on Wikipedia, the source and copyright status must be indicated. Images need to have an image tag applied to the image description page indicating the copyright status of the image. This uniform and easy-to-understand method of indicating the license status allows potential re-users of the images to know what they are allowed to do with the images.
