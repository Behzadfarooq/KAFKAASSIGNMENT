Black panther (disambiguation)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Black panther may refer to:

  • In animals:
    • Black panther, a big cat of any species, but most commonly a jaguar or a leopard, whose coloration is entirely black
  • In politics:
    • Black Panther Party, a revolutionary black nationalist organization in the United States formed during the 1960s
    • Israeli Black Panthers, a member of a group of Israeli Mizrahi Jews inspired by the Black Panther Party in the United States
  • In media:
    • Black Panther, the nickname for the British criminal and murderer Donald Neilson
    • Black Panther (comics), a comic book superhero in the Marvel Comics universe, and a member of The Avengers
    • Black Panther (newspaper), an underground newspaper
  • In music:
    • Hei Bao (band) or Black Panther, a well-known Chinese rock band
    • A song by Mason Jennings from his 2000 album Birds Flying Away
  • In military units:
    • Black Panther, the symbol for the Filipino Special forces, The Scout Rangers
    • The nickname of the U.S. 761st Tank Battalion, after their unit's shoulder sleeve insignia.
  • The K2 Black Panther, a South Korean Main Battle Tank
  • In people:
    • Black Panther is a nickname for supermodel Naomi Campbell
