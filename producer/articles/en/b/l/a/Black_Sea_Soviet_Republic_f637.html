Black Sea Soviet Republic

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Black Sea Soviet Republic (March—May, 1918) was a republic of the RSFSR within the territory that corresponded to the Black Sea Guberniya of the Imperial Russia. Its capital was Novorossiysk.

It was merged into the Kuban-Black Sea Soviet Republic.
