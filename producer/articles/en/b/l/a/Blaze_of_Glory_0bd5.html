Blaze of Glory is Jon Bon Jovi's first solo album, released in 1990. It includes songs from and inspired by the movie Young Guns II. Songs from Young Guns II include #1 hit single "Blaze of Glory" and #2 "Miracle". Approximate sales were in excess of two million copies in the United States.
