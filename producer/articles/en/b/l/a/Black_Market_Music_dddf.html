Black Market Music is an album by the rock group Placebo, released in 2000. It took 9 months to record, which is the longest that Placebo have ever spent recording an album to date. It is dedicated to the Memory of Scott Piering.

[edit] Miscellanea

The U.S. release of the album adds two bonus tracks:

  • "Without You I'm Nothing" featuring David Bowie
  • "I Feel You" - Depeche Mode cover
  • A poster of the album appears in Feeder's video for "Just a Day", but the band's name and album title are censored due to copyright.

Justin Warfield of She Wants Revenge features on "Spite and Malice"
