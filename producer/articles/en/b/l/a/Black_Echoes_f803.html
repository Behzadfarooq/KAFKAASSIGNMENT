Black Echoes is a Jamaican music show, the show has been broadcasting on radio in Dublin, Ireland since 1982. The show is a diverse range of music, including reggae, ska, rock-steady and dub. The show is presented by John Public, and produced by Miss Pat and Denzil.

[edit] History

John Public has presented Black Echoes since late 1982.

In the beginning the show centred on many forms of black music but by 4 it had concentrated solely on reggae music.

Miss Pat joined the programme as producer in 1985 having moved from London, where she grew up listening to all styles of Jamaican music. Since then the programme has incorporated ska, shuffle, rock steady, early reggae, Mento etc., as well as DJ, dub, dub poetry and ragga.
