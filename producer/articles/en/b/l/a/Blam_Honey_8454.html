Blam Honey was a Industrial rock band from Japan, formed in 1995 of two members: Tatsuya (vocals, guitar, percussion) and Ryonai (programming, percussion). They've given themselves the nickname 'Industrial Gender Unit'. Their style was very heavy and their look incorporated bondage. They list NIN and Skinny Puppy as influences.

Ryonai has a side project, Suppurate System, creating ambient music, Gothic Lolita fashion accessories that has found favor by some visual kei artists.

Blam Honey released two albums: Grandiose Delusion (1998) and Typical Ingeniousness -suggest- (1999). They have since disbanded.
