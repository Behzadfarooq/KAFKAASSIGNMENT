Blatná Polianka is a village and municipality in the Sobrance District in the Košice Region of east Slovakia.

[edit] History

In historical records the village was first mentioned in 1417.

[edit] Geography

The village lies at an altitude of 104 metres and covers an area of 9.986 km². It has a population of about 170 people.

[edit] Culture

The village has a public library and a football pitch
