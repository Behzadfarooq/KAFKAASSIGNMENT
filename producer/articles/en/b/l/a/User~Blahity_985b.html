Yo! My name is David DiBattiste. I'm from Doylestown, Pennsylvania - right outside of Philadelphia - and have been a member here for about a year or so now. If you'd like to contact me for any reason, you can email me or find my IM information to the right.

I'm a web programmer for the most part, programming my own sites and running/adminning servers under the Fizor Networks brand, and also holding a position as Developer of an online game, Reign Online at the Fortyeight Network (now part of Ataya Creative), along with Ty Kauffman and Mark Caudill. I also run, as a part of Fizor Networks, the Reign-related site reign.fizor.net, along with the ReignWiki, a collection of information about playing Reign, the history of the game, and those who currently play or have played in the past.

I have a blog at dibattiste.com, originally intended to be a technology/Fizor oriented blog but now nearly entirely based on politics.

I'm far better at speaking English than any other language, and considering that most of the other languages I know are incomplete languages and somewhat mostly fictional... should you choose to contact me, I'd greatly prefer my native language. Though, if you're really, really in need of a conversation, and for some reason English or German don't work for you, feel free to email me in Klingon.

Just don't be mad at me when my spam filter deletes it!

Here, I make almost universally edits with low amounts of content and changes, save a few unique exceptions. The majority of my edits involve changing of wording or punctuation errors, or editing an article to be more uniform and less redundant if it's not needed. I also tend to screw up my own edits, so sometimes a dual edit will appear - that's me correcting something, and then me correcting myself. You'd think I'd have learned to click Show Preview by now ;)
