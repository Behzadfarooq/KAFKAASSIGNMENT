Blair Jones (born September 27, 1986) is a Canadian hockey player for the Tampa Bay Lightning of the National Hockey League.

Blair Jones scored is first NHL goal on February 6, 2007 against the LA Kings on goalie Sean Burke He currently has 4 NHL career goals.
