Blackfield & Langley F.C. is a football club based in the village of Langley, Hampshire, England. Their first FA Cup appearance was in 2001 when they were knocked out in the preliminary round. For the 2006-07 season, they are members of the Wessex League Division One. They play at the Gang Warily Ground in Blackfield. Their colours are green and white and their rivals are AFC Fawley and Hythe & Dibden.
