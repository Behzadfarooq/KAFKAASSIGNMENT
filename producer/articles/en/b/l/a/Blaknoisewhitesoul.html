Blaknoisewhitesoul are a Scottish band who describe themselves as "pop experimentalists".

Their first release was a double A-side release of "Pop Genius (song for Brian Wilson)" and "Sal The Swindler" and this was released in early 2005.

Their new single is a double A-side of songs "Dirty Darkness" and "Sometimes without the Pet Shop Boys" and is due for release in November 2005. The latter of these tracks was involved in minor controversy with Pet Shop Boys. The original track was called "Sometimes with the Pet Shop Boys" and included a sample of the lyrics from the Pet Shop Boys' single "West End girls", but this sample had to be withdrawn after an argument over royalties.
