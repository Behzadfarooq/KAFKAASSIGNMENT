Black Kaweah
Elevation 13765 ft (4196 m)
Location California, USA
Prominence 605 ft (184 m)
Coordinates 36°32′43″N, 118°30′53″W
Topo map USGS Triple Divide Peak
First ascent 1920 by Duncan McDuffie, Onis Brown, James Hutchinson

Black Kaweah is a mountain of the Kaweah peaks ridge of the California's Sierra Nevada, in Sequoia National Park.

Fulgurites can be found on the peak.
