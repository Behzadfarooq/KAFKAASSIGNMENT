The video game version of Blade was produced by Activision for the Game Boy Color and PlayStation, released November 2000. It's the adventure of Blade with help of his mentor and friend Abraham Whistler, and with old remembered enemies like Dragonetti and Mannheim.

[edit] Voice Characters

  • Reed Pepper - Blade
  • Norman Chancer - Abraham Whistler
  • Stefan Ashton Frank - Mannheim
  • William Roberts - Dragonetti
