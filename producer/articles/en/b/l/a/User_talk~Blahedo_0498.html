[edit] WikiProject?

Hi Don, how've you been? The months go by so quickly, don't they?

I'm writing because I'm thinking about starting a WikiProject for Textile Arts; the idea is to bring knitting and all the analogous crafts under one roof — along with clothing and fashion design — and to exploit the automated assessments of the Mathbot. We need to find 5 editors who are interested in contributing. Would you be willing to join? I'd be willing to do the initial technical set-up. Hoping that all's well with you, Willow 10:59, 5 February 2007 (UTC)

Thanks, Don! :) If you could sign up at Wikipedia:WikiProject Council/Proposals#Textile_Arts, that'd be great. I'm going to write a few more personal notes to see whether other editors are interested; people are editing textile articles pretty routinely, so they might be interested in an automated assessment method for them, and the possibility of their articles seeing print. Willow 12:06, 12 February 2007 (UTC)
