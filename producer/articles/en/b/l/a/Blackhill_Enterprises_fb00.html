Blackhill Enterprises

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Blackhill Enterprises was a rock music management company, founded as a partnership by the four original members of Pink Floyd, with Peter Jenner and Andrew King (winner of 2006 BACS Gold Badge Award and board member for the Performing Rights Society).

After Syd Barrett left Pink Floyd, the partnership was dissolved, and Jenner and King continued Blackhill to manage Barrett.

They also managed:

  • Marc Bolan
  • Edgar Broughton Band
  • The Clash
  • Ian Dury
  • Roy Harper
  • Alberto Y Los Trios Paranoias
