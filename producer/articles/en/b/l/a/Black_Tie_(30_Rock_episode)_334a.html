"Black Tie" is the twelfth episode in the situation comedy series 30 Rock. It first aired in the U.S. on February 1, 2007, attracting 5.71 million viewers[1]; it ranked third in its timeslot (behind February sweeps episodes of Grey's Anatomy ("Wishin' and Hopin'") and CSI ("Meet Market")[2], and tied for 69th in Nielsen's prime-time television rankings for the week.[1]

[edit] Synopsis

Spoiler warning: Plot and/or ending details follow.

Liz wonders if she's actually on a date with Jack as she accompanies him to a birthday celebration for a foreign prince. While at the ball, Liz sees a different side to Jack as he unexpectedly runs in to his ex-wife (Isabella Rossellini). Back at 30 Rock, Tracy tempts Pete with a booze- and babe-filled party in the office, while Kenneth tries to keep Pete on the straight and narrow.

[edit] Trivia

  • Will Forte is the fifth Saturday Night Live cast member to appear on the show.
  • Gerhardt is called the last male member of the Habsburg family. In real life, the Habsburg family still has many living members and Otto von Habsburg is the current head of the former ruling house.
  • Gerhardt is given the title British title of Earl rather then the German Graf or count
