This user is an undergraduate student majoring in Electrical Engineering.

Hi, my name is Blair Bonnett and I am a New Zealand Wikipedian. To be more precise I live in the South Island's largest city of Christchurch, or if you want to get really specific, in the suburb of Avonside.

At this point in time I am in my third year of four studying at the University of Canterbury for a Bachelor of Electrical Engineering. In particular, I am interested in focusing on embedded systems - that is, designing hardware around microcontrollers or Programmable Logic Devices and then programming them to serve a particular purpose. I also have an interest in integrated circuit design and telecommunications.

As an aside to all this study, I have taken up repeatedly falling off a unicycle courtesy of Unisoc, the Canterbury University Unicycle Society. I also pretend I am able to juggle.

In my spare time (to paraphrase the Tui beer ad, "Yeah, Right" [1]) I enjoy website design and general computer programming, playing and/or watching various sports like football, cricket, snooker and basketball, reading, or just hanging out with friends.

If you have any questions or would just like to get in touch with me, feel free to ask on my talk page.
