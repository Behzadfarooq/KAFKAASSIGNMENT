Blayney Wind Farm, New South Wales

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Blayney Wind Farm

The Blayney Wind Farm is a wind power station at Lake Carcoar, south of Orange, New South Wales, Australia. Blayney has fifteen wind turbines, with a generating capacity of 9.9 MW of electricity.
