Blake Woodruff (born June 19, 1995) is an American actor. He is perhaps best known for his role as Mike Baker in the film Cheaper by the Dozen.

Woodruff was born in Flagstaff, Arizona, and has two older sisters- Ayla and Raina- and a younger brother, Trevor. He decided that he wanted to be an actor at the age of six, and was cast in 2003's Cheaper by the Dozen after an uncredited role in the thriller Blind Horizon. He has since appeared on the television soap opera The Young and the Restless, and reprised his role in Cheaper by the Dozen 2. His next film role will be in the thriller, Whisper, currently scheduled for a 2006 release. Also, He has 2 sisters. Alya(b.1992) Raina(b.1993) and a younger brother Trevor (b.1998)

Woodruff enjoys rock & country music, as well as skateboarding, dancing, gymnastics, dirt bike riding, boogie boarding, kick-boxing, and rollerblading.
