Blaster Master (disambiguation)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Blaster Master may refer to:

  • Blaster Master (series) - A series of games from Sunsoft.
  • Blaster Master - The first game in the series, which was released for the Nintendo Entertainment System.
  • Blaster Master Boy - Released two years prior to Blaster Master 2 only for the handheld Game Boy.
  • Blaster Master 2 - The sequel to Blaster Master, released on the Sega Genesis.
  • Blaster Master: Enemy Below - The fourth release in the series for Nintendo's Game Boy Color.
  • Blaster Master: Blasting Again - Sunsoft's latest release in the Blaster Master series, now in three-dimensional graphics.

It may have similar name relations with:

  • Master Blaster - a type of water coaster
  • Master Blaster - Eurodance music trio [1].
  • The Master Blasters - A professional wrestling tag team in World Championship Wrestling.
  • The Blasters - A rock group formed in 1979 in Downey, California
  • Master Blasters (TV series) - A television series released in 2005 with 4 episodes.
  • Math Blaster - The first in a line of educational products created by Davidson & Associates.
  • Raster Blaster - A 1981 computer pinball game for the Apple ][.
