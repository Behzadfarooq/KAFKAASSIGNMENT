[edit] Black Flag: Who I am

I am an anarchist activist from the UK. I've been in the movement for about two decades (time flies). My log on name is, of course, the anarchist symbol (its history can be found here: http://www.infoshop.org/faq/append2.html)

As such, I'm interested in anarchist topics. I would place myself in the social strand of anarchism and am a fan of Malatesta, Kropotkin, Bakunin, Proudhon, Goldman and the likes.

I have some interest in the individualist anarchism of the likes Benjamin Tucker. I, like most anarchists, reject the idea that "anarcho"-capitalism is a form of anarchism.

If you are interested in anarchism, I would recommend "An Anarchist FAQ" at http://www.anarchistfaq.org. It has a comprehensive links page as well full of great sites.
