The Blamey Stakes is a Group 2 Australian thoroughbred horse race open run at Set Weights with Penalties over 1600 metres, at Flemington Racecourse in March each year.

[edit] Race history

  • From 1955 to 1980 the race was run at Weight for Age.
  • From 1955 to 1972 was run over 10 furlongs (approximately 2000 metres).
