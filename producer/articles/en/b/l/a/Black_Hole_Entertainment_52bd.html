Black Hole Entertainment

Black Hole Entertainment is a Budapest-based video game developer, found in 2002 by Hollywood producer Andrew G. Vajna. The company's first title was Armies of Exigo, published by Electronic Arts and released in the end of 2004. The team recently has developed Warhammer: Mark of Chaos, which was released on November 14, 2006 for the PC.
