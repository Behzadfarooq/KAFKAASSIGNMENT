Black Sheep is a New Zealand made comedy/horror film directed by Jonathan King and made in a similar style to Peter Jackson's early films such as Bad Taste and Braindead.

North American distribution rights have been acquired by The Weinstein Company and IFC Entertainment. The Weinstein Company will be releasing the film on DVD through Genius Products.

Special effects for the film were handled by Weta Workshop.

[edit] Plot

Spoiler warning: Plot and/or ending details follow.

A young Henry Oldfield lives on a farm in the countryside of New Zealand. His father specializes in sheep. However Henry develops a phobia of sheep after his brother, Angus plays a cruel prank on him involving a dead bloody rotting sheep.

15 years later, Henry Oldfield (Nathan Meister) returns home to sell his share of the family farm to his older brother Angus (Peter Feeney), as a result of their father recently dying. Unknown to Henry, Angus is carrying out secret genetic experiments. While trying to reveal Angus' experiments, a pair of environmental activists named Grant and Experience accidentally release a mutant lamb. As Grant and Experience flee from the scientists, the lamb bites Grant's earlobe off and he becomes infected. The lamb then proceeds to infect the other herds of sheep.

Meanwhile Henry and an old friend Taka decide to take a drive on the farm. Taka sees a sheep that refuses to run away, even where the car was driven right up to it. As this is happening Experience steals the rifle from the car to try and shoot them. In the end they end up teaming up with each other to investigate one of the farm houses that has smoke pouring out of it.

Inside they find one of the farmers with his neck chewed out and blood splattered along the walls. Henry sees a sheep in the hallway, and because of his phobia he quietly shuts the door and locks it. Taka and Experience realise that the sheep is ramming through the door and it ends up creating a hole. Taka shoots it and they wonder what is going on.

Back on the other end of the farm, Angus is shown driving where he sees Grant now a were-sheep. Grant bites Angus. Back in the car, Taka, Experience and Henry go off to warn Angus about the killer sheep, but a sheep hiding in the car bites Taka's foot. When their car is destroyed they go to seek refuge in the laboratory. Henry and Taka finally see that Angus is infact a horrible person, conducting experiments and being cruel to sheep. When one of the scientists see that Taka's foot has now become a sheep's foot she keeps him there for studying but shoos Experience and Henry out.

Suddenly hoards of sheep come running down the hills, they see a deep, dark pit (which is actually a waste dump and hundreds of other mutant lambs live) in there surrounded by a gate. As Henry accidently slips into the pit, and his brother refuses to help. Henry and Experience fall into the pit of rotting flesh but escape in the underground tunnels. Meanwhile, Taka transforms into a sheep, but a shot transforms him back.

Angus is shown giveing a presentation to business men about his new gentic sheep. The sheep is shown to be gorgeous but the business men are soon devoured by black sheep. When they go to warn Angus, they discover he has a sexual fetish for sheep. Disgusted they leave. Henry realises he's been infected so sheep no longer attack him. Henry and Experience go their seperate ways. Henry ends up fighting with his brother who has now transformed. A revolving blade cuts into Angus and wounds him badly, Taka comes and disinfects Angus and Henry. Experience arrives. Even though Angus is now a human being, he goes back to the sheep, bloody and severly dismantled and tells them to bite him again. The sheep, driven mad by the smell of blood just eat Angus. Eventually all the sheep are killed in a giant bonfire and the disinfectant is given to the were-sheep people.

However the audience soon finds out that the sheep-dog now makes sheep noises...

Spoilers end here.
