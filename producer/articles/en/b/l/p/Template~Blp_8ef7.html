This article must adhere to the policy on biographies of living persons. Controversial material of any kind that is unsourced or poorly sourced must be removed immediately, especially if potentially libelous. If such material is repeatedly inserted or there are other concerns relative to this policy, report it on the living persons biographies noticeboard.

This template is a self reference and should be affixed to talk pages only.

Note: To affix this message to the talk page of a living person biography it is generally preferable to use {{WPBiography|living=yes}} instead. Besides adding the living persons biography warning it will add the article to the Wikipedia 1.0 assessments list (Index · Statistics · Log).

{{Blp}} is suitable for other articles containing information on living people, such as lists.

To edit the text of this template, see {{Blp/BLPtext}}.
