Bláthnaid Ní Chofaigh

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Bláthnaid Ní Chofaigh taking part in an Irish campaign.

Bláthnaid Ní Chofaigh (born October 2, 1970) is an Irish TV personality from Ráth Cairn, Co Meath. She is most famous for presenting shows such as The Afternoon Show and Echo Island on RTÉ television. She is married with four children and currently lives in Dublin. Ní Chofaigh is also a fluent Irish-speaker and is an avid language campaigner.
