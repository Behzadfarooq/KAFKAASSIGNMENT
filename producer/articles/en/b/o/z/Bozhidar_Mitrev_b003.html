Bozhidar Mitrev (born 31/03/1987) is a Bulgarian goal keeper currently playing for Levski Sofia.

He played in Levski's 2-0 defeat to Chelsea F.C. at Stamford Bridge, making several excellent svaes, including one reaction save from a Michael Essien header. However, he received criticism from the Chelsea faithful for a bad challenge on Didier Drogba, clearly fouling him. Chelsea were not offered a penalty, however Drogba accepted the appologies of the goalkeeper.
