User Bozebus17 is a contributing Wikipedia member. His real name is Steven R. Elliott. He was born on May 6, 1983 in Warren, Michigan.


[edit] Contact Information

Email: bozebus17@sbcglobal.net. Please include wikipedia in the subject line if you choose to email Steve.


[edit] Background

Steve graduated from Oakland University in April 2005 with a Bachelor of Arts Degree in political science. He was a history major for quite some time before changing to political science. He still lives in Warren and works for the Bank of New York in their Investment Plans Division.
