Boku no Pico (My Pico) (ぼくのぴこ , Boku no Pico (My Pico)?) is a shotacon anime produced by Natural High in Japan in 2006. It was directed by Katsuyoshi Yatabe. Other notable staff members include Ishoku Dougen (character design), Yoshiten (animation character design), Goldenboy (producer), and Katsuhiko Takayama (screenplay). The OVA was released on DVD in September 2006, with a running time of 33 minutes.

It will be followed up by a second OVA titled Pico to Chico (Pico and Chico). It was originally scheduled for release in March 2007, but it's now been pushed back to April 19 2007 for unknown reasons. It will have a running time of 45 minutes. To promote the second OVA, special promotions will be held on Valentine's Day, White Day, and Children's Day.

[edit] Characters

  • Tamotsu
Nicknamed "Mokkun" by Pico, he's a young man who sexualizes Pico. While Tamotsu's job is never mentioned, his new car and spacious apartment heavily suggest he's well off. When he first seduces Pico, he is shocked to find out Pico's a boy but continues seducing him anyways. Later, he buys Pico a girl's outfit complete with collar and panties which he makes Pico wear despite protests. He mostly views Pico solely as a sexual object, though later shows true concern for Pico after he disappears. In his relationship with Pico, he is the seme.
  • Pico
A blond adolescent boy who works part-time at his grandfather's bar in the summer. He's often shown swimming, usually in the buff or in a blue speedo. Despite disliking it, he wears girls' clothing after Mokkun buys an outfit for him. Later, hurt that Tamotsu wouldn't define their relationship, he rebels by cutting off most of his hair and runs away. In his relationship with Mokkun, he is the uke.
  • Pico's Grandfather
He runs a large but usually empty bar by the beach named BeBe. When Pico visits him for the summer, he has him help out as waiter whilst wearing a frilly pink apron. When Tamotsu, a regular, attends the bar, he asks him to spend time with his grandson who has no friends in the area.
  • Chico
A brown haired boy to appear in the second OVA who develops a sexual relationship with Pico. No further information has been released.
