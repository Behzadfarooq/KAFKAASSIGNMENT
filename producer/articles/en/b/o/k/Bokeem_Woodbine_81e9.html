Bokeem Woodbine (born April 13, 1973 in Harlem, New York, USA) is an African-American film and television actor. Bokeem attended the prestigious Dalton School in New York City. He also went to LaGuardia High School of Music & Art[1] in New York. He is known for his portrayal of memorable characters in such films as Dead Presidents, Jason's Lyric, Crooklyn, Life, Ray, and Gridlock'd. His television work includes The Sopranos, CSI: Miami, and Blade: The Series.

Woodbine is currently pursuing a music career and is usually considered a fairly talented blues-rock singer and musician.[citation needed]

Woodbine made appearances in Wu-Tang video clips like Gravel Pit, Protect Ya Neck (The Jump Off), Careful (Click,Click) and in RZA's Ching Kung
