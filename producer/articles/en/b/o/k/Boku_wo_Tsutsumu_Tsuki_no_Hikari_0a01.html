Boku wo Tsutsumu Tsuki no Hikari (ボクを包む月の光)(or The Moonlight which Embraces Me) is an ongoing manga series published in Hana to Yume's monthly Betsuhana anthology and drawn and written by Saki Hiwatari. It is a sequel of her previous work, Please Save My Earth, and features the two focus characters of that series, Alice Sakaguchi (now Kobayashi) and Rin Kobayashi. They are married, and their child, 10-year-old Ren, is the main character of the series. Many of the characters from Please Save My Earth reappear in this one. It also reprises a lot of the ESP-related themes that the original series dealt with.
