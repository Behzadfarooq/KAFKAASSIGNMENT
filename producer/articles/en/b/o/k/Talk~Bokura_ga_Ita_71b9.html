[edit] Episode Count

Does anybody have any concrete evidence of the episode count? Posts on 2ch don't even mention an episode count. Once I get enough writeup done to flesh out this entry, I'll post it up there and change the episode count to reflect an unknown end.

This manga is so far 10 volumes long and still running so I strongly doubt the 13 ep count to be true. They're going at one manga chapter an ep right now, so 13 eps would probably put us at manga volume 5 (episode 8 finished up manga volume 3). I'm looking forward to this page becoming very informative since this series apparently isn't very popular (yet!).Nekotsukimi 04:52, 26 August 2006 (UTC)

According to AnimeNewsNetwork, there should be a total of 26 episodes to be released. SunsetFlare 12:07, 7 October 2006 (UTC)

According to The Triad, a group who's fansubbing the anime, there are 26 episodes, too. I am Girl 22:00, 14 October 2006 (UTC)

AnimeNewsNetwork's changed the episode count to 24 now. Anyone with other sources confirming this, or is it still majority 26? SunsetFlare 14:10, 29 October 2006 (UTC)

There are indeed 26 episodes. I possess the Triad fansubs of the entire series and just watched the final episode last night. NinjaDuck 19:42, 27 January 2007 (UTC)
