Boksitogorsk

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Coordinates: 59°28′31″N, 33°51′3″E

Boksitogorsk (Russian: Бокситого́рск) is a town in Leningrad Oblast, Russia, located on the Pyardomlya River (Syas' basin) 245 km east of St. Petersburg. Population: 18,128 (2002 Census); 21,839 (1989 Census).

Town status since 1950.
