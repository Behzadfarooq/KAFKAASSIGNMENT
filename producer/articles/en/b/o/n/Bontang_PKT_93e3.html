PS Bontang PKT (Persatuan Sepakbola Bontang Pupuk Kalimantan Timur) is an Indonesian football team located in Bontang, East Kalimantan in the island of Borneo.

Bontang PKT prides itself as the the most successful club to come out of Kalimantan. They were semi finalist of the Liga Indonesia I Dunhill in the 1994/1995 season.

[edit] History

The club is originally formed as PS Pupuk Kaltim Galatama in 1987. It went through several name changes (Pupuk Kaltim and PKT Bontang) before being known as PS Bontang PKT today.

[edit] Supporters

Bontng PKT supporters called themseves Mandau Mania. Mandau is a type of sword used by the Dayaks who inhabit most parts of the Borneo island.
