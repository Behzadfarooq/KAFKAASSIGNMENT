Bonnes Mares is a Grand Cru red wine from the village of Chambolle-Musigny in the Côte-d'Or département of Burgundy, France.

The meaning of the vineyard's name is much debated and lost in the past. Some have said the name means 'stagnant pools'[verification needed].
