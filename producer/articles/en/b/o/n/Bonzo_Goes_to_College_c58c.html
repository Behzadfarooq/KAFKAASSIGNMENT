Bonzo Goes to College (1952) is the sequel to Bedtime for Bonzo. Like Bedtime for Bonzo, it was directed by Frederick De Cordova; however, it has different writers (Jack Henley and Leo Lieberman) and lacks Ronald Reagan's presence.
