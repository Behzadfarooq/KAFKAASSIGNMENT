BONVS ONVS

"Bonus Onus" is a college student who attends Dartmouth College in Hanover, New Hampshire. He has contributed to articles in fields ranging from Environmental Science to U.S. History. His most notable contributions are to the articles Hepatitis E, Monroe Doctrine, Freedmen's Bureau, Pesticide, Crash Team Racing, Surface mining, Linguistic detoxification, Robert V. Remini, Crillon-le-Brave, and Prionus, but his tireless copyediting and fact-checking on countless other articles should also be acknowledged.

Current project: List of "largest" articles

Political battlegrounds he has done his best to neutralize: Depleted uranium, Eco-terrorism.
