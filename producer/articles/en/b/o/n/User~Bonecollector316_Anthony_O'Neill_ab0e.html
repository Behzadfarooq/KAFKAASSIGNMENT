Anthony O'Neill is A Canadian Gamer. Born in 1988, He got his first computer at the age of 8. Opening up Megatracks on his new pentium 1 pc, He was instantly hooked.


[edit] Quake 2 days

Back in his early days, when Anthony was 9 years old. He would play the Quake 2 demo online at zone.com, There he was known as super50. He had a couple close knit freidns on the community who he unfourtantley later lost contact with. Anthony than discovered mapping and started making maps for quake 2, THey become well known across the zone community with many servers playing his maps.

[edit] Halflife days

Anthony came across the halflife demo, He installed and played it, he was amazed. He became hooked on the dmeo playing it over and over, looking at all the reviews and screenshots he could. Than his birthday came, and he got halflife.

This brought anthony fully into the gaming world, completely immersing him, staying up to the wee hours of the morning just to get a few more frags.

Later discovering the mods, Anthony still plays halflife mods to this day, and doesn't plan to stop anytime soon.
