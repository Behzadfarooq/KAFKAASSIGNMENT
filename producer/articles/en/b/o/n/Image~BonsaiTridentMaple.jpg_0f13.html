  • Description:
English: Bonsai trident maple in the root over rock style.
Deutsch: Bonzaibaum im Wurzel über Fels-Stil

Part of the penjing collection at the National Bonsai and Penjing Museum, this trident maple, Acer buergerianum, has its roots growing over a rock and its foliage and stems trimmed in the shape of a dragon.

USDA photo by Peggy Greb. Image Number K10474-1.

From: http://www.ars.usda.gov/is/graphics/photos/jun03/k10474-1.htm
