[edit] Interests (related to Wiki)

[edit] Professional

I am a bioinformatician with a formal training in biochemistry. More specifically, on protein structures and evolution. I ran into a commentary on the role of experts in Wikipedia and I think it makes sense. For this reason, I do not intend to contribute specifically on topics related to my research interests, at least at the moment.

[edit] Personal

I am interested in military history, specifically on the role of technologic advancements on the way doctrines evolve. I would like to contribute on history topics at some points, once I get a good feel for the inner workings of the wikipedia project.

I am also interested in Modeling, texturing and rendering of computer digital art.
