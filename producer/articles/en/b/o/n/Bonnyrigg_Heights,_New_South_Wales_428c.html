Bonnyrigg Heights is a suburb of Sydney, in the state of New South Wales, Australia. The postcode is 2177. The elevation of this suburb is approximately between 45-80 metres above mean sea level The neighbouring suburbs of Bonnyrigg Heights are Abbotsbury, Bonnyrigg, Cecil Hills, Edensor Park and Green Valley.

[edit] Transport

Buses traverse the suburb. The T80 Liverpool-Parramatta Transitway service (Western Sydney Buses) links Bonnyrigg Heights to major centres across Greater Western Sydney including Bonnyrigg, Prairiewood, Smithfield, City of Liverpool and City of Parramatta. The hourly 833 service (Westbus) operates to Cabramatta. The hourly 836 service (Westbus) operates to Liverpool, whilst the 841 from cecil hills shopping centre is every 30 minutes to Liverpool. The newly completed transit-way goes from parramatta to miller TAFE at Hoxton park.

The M7 at cecil hills interchange takes you to M4 near parramatta, M2 at Baulkham hills (north western suburbs) or the interchange near The Crossroads for the M5 to sydney or hume highway (freeway) to Campbelltown and eventually to Melbourne
