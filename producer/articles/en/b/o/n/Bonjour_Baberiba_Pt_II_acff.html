Studio album by Bergman Rock
Released May 4, 2005

Bergman Rock's second album Bonjour Baberiba Pt II was recorded in only seven days.

  6. "Swan Song" (Bergman Rock)
  7. "Bubble Gum" (Bergman Rock)

  • John Essing - Guitar

  • Jonas Jonasson - Synth, backing vocals
  • Conny Nimmersjö - Guitar
