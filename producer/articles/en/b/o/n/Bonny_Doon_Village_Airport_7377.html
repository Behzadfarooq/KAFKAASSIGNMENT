Bonny Doon Village Airport (FAA LID: CL77) is a privately owned, private use airport located in Bonny Doon, Santa Cruz County, California, United States.

The airport is located eight nm NW of Santa Cruz, California at an elevation 2020'. The runways are 12/30 and 2200' x 50' poor quality asphalt. Left traffic for both runways, but be advised that due to trees at one end of the runway landings are on runway 30 and departures are on runway 12.

The airport is fairly easy to spot at the top of the ridge in a clearing.
