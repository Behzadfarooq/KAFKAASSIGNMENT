Bonacursus

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Bonacursus was a one time Cathar who converted to Catholicism and released a confessional report to the people of Milan exposing the nature of the Cathar heresy entitled Manefestatio haeresis catharorom quam fesit Bonacursus sometime between 1176 and 1190.[1] He also reported on the Pasagian heresy[2] as well as the Arnoldists.[3]
