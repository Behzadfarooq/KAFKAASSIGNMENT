[edit] Hello BONGU SEAN

I see you have created quite a few articles. Thanks for contributing, you're doing a good job. But there's a few things that you need to do when creating articles:

  • Please cite your sources to prove the article is notable enough to stay on Wikipedia. One example is at Bulebel Industrial Estate, please find some links to prove its importance and add them to the article.
  • If an article is short (about four or less paragraphs) then please stub them with {{malta-stub}}. That way, when other editors come to look for pages to expand, your article will be found.
  • Please categorise them in an appropriate category. A list of Malta-related categories is located at Category:Malta.
  • Please link them to any relevant articles (for example I've added Holy Week in Malta to the Culture of Malta and Holy Week pages).

Also I've listed St. Joseph Boys Junior Lyceum (Malta) for deletion because I cannot find any sources on the Internet that proves its importance. If you know of any websites or books that proves that it was the first junior lyceum in Malta, please add them to the article then tell us what you did at the deletion page so we can remove the deletion warning.

Have a good day, and once again thanks for contributing to Wikipedia. ~ ► Wykebjs ◄ (userpage | talk) 16:30, 30 January 2007 (UTC)
