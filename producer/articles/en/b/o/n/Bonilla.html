Bonilla

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Bonilla is a surname, and may refer to:

  • Bobby Bonilla – American baseball player who played from 1986 to 2001 in Major League Baseball.
  • Daryl Bonilla – Hawaiian actor, comedian, and professional wrestler.
  • George Bonilla – Film director for Zombie Planet.
  • Gerardo Bonilla – Professional race car driver.
  • Henry Bonilla – Former Congressman who represented Texas's 23rd congressional district in the United States House of Representatives.
  • Hugo Orellana Bonilla – Peruvian painter.
  • José Bonilla – Astronomer who took the earliest known photograph of an unidentified flying object in 1883.
  • Manuel Bonilla – President of Honduras from 1903 to 1907 and from 1912 to 1913.
  • Policarpo Bonilla – President of Honduras from 1894 to 1899.
  • Romain Bonilla – Zouavic musician and Wikipedian.
