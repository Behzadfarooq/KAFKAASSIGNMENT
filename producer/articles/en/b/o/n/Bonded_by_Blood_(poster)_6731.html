The popular limited edition posters for the All Blacks rugby team in New Zealand are sought after by fans as they contain a sample of every All Black player's DNA. The posters show the players performing the Haka in the Fiordland National Park.

The popularity of the phrase “Bonded by Blood” from the poster campaign has encouraged others to use it in reference of team unity, for example in the game World of Warcraft there is a guild called Bonded by Blood.
