User:Bonzo the Moon Man

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Bonzo the Moon Man is the pseudonym I use as a contributor to Wikipedia. I currently live in Alberta, Canada, though I have lived all over the country, and I contribute regularly to a variety of articles regarding various subjects. I would say that my personal expertise is with music, English and history, as I have a wealth of knowledge and experience in those areas. As for the sciences, they are definitely not my strong point and I refrain from contributing to articles with such a theme except for making very simple corrections.
