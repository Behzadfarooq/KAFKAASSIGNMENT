"Bone to Be Wild" is the twenty first episode of the television program Farscape from the first season, written by Rockne S. O'Bannon & David Kemper and directed by Andrew Prowse.

Original airdate: January 21, 2000

[edit] Synopsis

Spoiler warning: Plot and/or ending details follow.

The crew of Moya are forced to hide from the Peacekeepers in an asteroid field. There, they answer a distress call coming from what appears to be an O'Neill cylinder -type hollowed out asteroid with a breathable atmosphere filled with lush flora. The call has come from a young girl of a species unknown to them, who is fearful of a deformed monster of a different unknown species who is also in the asteroid. They soon discover, however, that the situation is not what it seems--the girl, M'Lee, is a calcivore, meaning her species eats only bones. When she is hungry, she becomes feral and grows pointy spines and sharp teeth. The "monster" is a botanist, Br'Nee, who has been collecting floral samples, and whose whole team, including his wife, has been killed by M'Lee. John discovers that Zhaan is herself a plant, and M'Lee, while a fierce creature when hungry, is at heart a frightened child whose people were deliberately placed on the asteroid by Br'Nee's species in order to wipe out all fauna. Her species was then expected to starve to death, and M'Lee was the last of them. Br'Nee takes Zhaan prisoner as the prize floral sample of his collection. Crichton rescues her, Br'Nee is accidentally killed, and the Moyans tell M'Lee that a Command Carrier will soon arrive, where she will find fresh hunting grounds, and she can eat Br'Nee in the meantime to take off the edge.

Meanwhile, Moya is having trouble bonding with her newborn baby, and asks Aeryn to help her, as she feels the baby might respond better to a Peacekeeper. It goes well and Moya asks Aeryn to name the new baby. On the PK Command Carrier, Crais finds he is losing control to Scorpius, who is increasingly challenging his authority.

[edit] Notes

  • Farscape's opening credits (with some new editing) now show Gigi Edgley as a cast regular after Anthony Simcoe: she was listed as "Guest starring" since her introduction to the series in Durka Returns.
  • Francesca Buller (M'Lee) is the wife of series star Ben Browder and she would go on to play an additional three characters over the course of the series: ro-NA in Look at the Princess Part I: A Kiss is But a Kiss & Look at the Princess Part II: I Do, I Think; Raxil in Scratch 'n Sniff; and War Minister Ahkna in the fourth season & miniseries.
  • According to the Starburst DVD's "Cool Farscape Facts," M'Lee was to return in season 5 but when the storyline was condensed from twenty-odd episodes to the events of Farscape: The Peacekeeper Wars her character had to be dropped.
  • Marton Csokas (Br'Nee) is better known for playing bad guys in xXx and The Bourne Supremacy.
  • This episode introduces Lt. Braca
  • Pa'u Zotoh Zhaan shows she can make herself invisible.
  • John discovers that Zhaan is a plant.
  • D'Argo can heal even from severe wounds.
