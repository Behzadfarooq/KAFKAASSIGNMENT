Bonita Magazine is an international men's magazine aimed at the Hispanic/Latin market based in the USA and printed in English. The magazine was first published for a consumer audience in 2005, however, the concept and format had existed as long as 3 years earlier, in 2002. In English, the Spanish word bonita means pretty or beautiful.

[edit] Creation & pre-publication

In 2002, publisher Robert E Garcia believed he noticed a gap in the magazine market, that there was little focus on Latin women as models. After considerable research he came up with Mujeres Bonita Magazine (Pretty Women). During this period he made various contacts in the business and had the magazine's first official photo shoot in 2003. It was in 2003 the name of the magazine was changed to its current name, simply Bonita Magazine.

In September 2003 the first sample issue was produced. Issue Three is pictured here featuring the work of photographer Kendal Carr of VisualCocktail

[edit] Current run/features

At the end of 2005 Bonita was released to the public and received positive feedback. Each issue has gradually improved in concept and design. The magazine currently features broad-based articles on sports, advice, health & fitness, fashion, games and any other subject interesting to a male readership. The magazine also features a selection of models with Latin American/Hispanic backgrounds and interviews with famous Latinos/Latinas.

Current features include:

  • Money Talks
  • What Women Want
  • Body & Soul
  • The Office Detective
  • Ask Dr. Love
  • News of the Weird

[edit] Competition

Bonita magazine is currently holding a competition to find the "Hottest Latinas In The World".
