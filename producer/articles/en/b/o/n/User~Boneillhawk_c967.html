Boneillhawk (b. 1985) is a student at Saint Joseph's University in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania. As can be seen in my list of created pages, I went on a run of making navigation boxes for college sports arenas. I also monitor pages related to my school.

In the future, I would like to design an infobox that can be used for stadiums and arenas. If anyone would like to join me in starting a stadiums/arenas Wikiproject, leave a note on my talk page.

Lately, I've been doing some work on SEPTA station pages and I'm starting to do a little work with the Wikipedia:WikiProject Disambiguation, although I haven't attached my name to the participants list yet.
