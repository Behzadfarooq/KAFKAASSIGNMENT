[edit] Yes and prog

Ash Soan's drumkit for The Producers at the band's second live show (7 Feb 2007)

I am best known online as a Yes fan[1] and have created a number of pages tangentially related to the band including Martyn Adelman, Deborah Anderson, Jade Anderson, Badger (band), Andrew Booker, Gonzalo Carrera, Brian Davison, GPS (band) (originally One (rock band)), Jakko Jakszyk, Luis Jardim, Jimmy Haun, Dylan Howe, Virgil Howe, Andrew Jackman, Lee Jackson, Jackson Heights (band), Gerard Johnson, Ronnie Leahy, The Lemon Trees, Stephen Lipson, Milton McDonald, Christian Nesmith, Phil Palmer, Qango, Jay Schellen, Michael Sherwood, Jeremy Stacey and Paul Stacey. I've also made significant contributions to Asia (band), The Buggles, Circa:, David Cross (musician), Michael Giles, John Payne (singer), The Producers (for which I've added my first ever picture), ProjeKct One, ProjeKct Two, ProjeKcts, Refugee (band), Ash Soan, The Syn, Ian Wallace and Alan White. My biggest project here has been creating Conspiracy (band).

In part spurred on by Pip Pyle's death in August 2006, I've been doing a lot of work on bands in the Canterbury scene, including creating Laurie Allan, Brainville, Clive Brooks, Jeff Clyne, Miquette Giraudy, Alan Gowen, Mark Hewins, In Cahoots, Isotope (band), Tony Levin (drummer), Mashu, Mirage (band), François Ovide, Geoff Richardson, Soft Heap (band) (and Soft Head), Trevor Tomkins and Alan Wakeman. I have made significant contributions to Caravan of Dreams (band), Canterbury Scene and Category:Canterbury sound, Delivery, Hatfield and the North, Pip Pyle and Dave Stewart (musician). My biggest project here has been creating Gilgamesh (band).

I've also done some other prog rock-related pages, creating Bob Drake (musician), Hail (band), Susanne Lewis, New Prog, Azalia Snail, Joe Porcaro and Fee Waybill.

[edit] Doctor Who books

I've created pages for four Doctor Who authors: Mark Clapham, Rebecca Levene, Eddie Robson and Simon Winstone. Particular books I have worked on are Anachrophobia, Combat Rock (Doctor Who), Deadfall (Bernice Summerfield), Just War (Doctor Who), The Mary-Sue Extrusion, Oh No It Isn't!, Peculiar Lives, Walking to Babylon and Genius Loci. Related articles to which I have made significant contributions include Andrew Cartmel, Jason Kane, Judge Dredd, List of Bernice Summerfield characters, Paul Magrs, Martha Jones, Bernice Summerfield and the Tenth Doctor Adventures. Also in the world of Doctor Who, I've created Cath Tregenna and significantly contributed to Out of Time (Torchwood), Smith and Jones (Doctor Who) and Judoon.
