Bongaigaon is the district headquarters of Bongaigaon District, Assam, India.

[edit] Geography

Bongaigaon is located at 26.47° N 90.57° E[1]. It has an average elevation of 54 metres (177 feet).

[edit] Demographics

As of 2001 India censusGRIndia, Bongaigaon had a population of 60,550. Males constitute 52% of the population and females 48%. Bongaigaon has an average literacy rate of 76%, higher than the national average of 59.5%; with male literacy of 81% and female literacy of 70%. 11% of the population is under 6 years of age.
