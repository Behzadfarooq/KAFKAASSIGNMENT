Bonehead

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Bonehead can refer to:

  • Bonehead, a term used to describe someone with a fixed sense of purpose to the point of being foolish.
  • Bonehead, a term for someone who appears in the background of live television (usually news reports) and draws attention to themselves.[1]
  • Bonehead, a derogatory term in the skinhead and punk subcultures, referring to Nazi-Skinheads and other racist skinheads.[2]
  • Bonehead, a derogatory term in the UK, used by traditional skinheads to describe Punk-skinheads
  • Bonehead, a parody of the character William Bodie in The Professionals, and partner of Foyle in the Comic Strip films The Bullshitters and Detectives on the Edge of a Nervous Breakdown.
  • Bonehead, nickname of Paul Arthurs, former Oasis guitarist.
