Bond (Chinese constellation)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Bond mansion (Traditional Chinese: 婁宿; Simplified Chinese: 娄宿; pinyin: Lóu Xiù) is one of the Twenty-eight mansions of the Chinese constellations. It is one of the western mansions of the White Tiger.
