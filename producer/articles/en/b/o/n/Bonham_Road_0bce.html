No.84 Bonham Road

Bonham Road (Officially Chinese: 般咸道, also 般含道) is a road in Sai Ying Pun, Hong Kong Island in Hong Kong. The road is a main road connecting Pokfulam Road in the west, near the University of Hong Kong, and Caine Road in the east, at the junction with Hospital Road and Seymour Road.

Several historical building are located at the road, including King's College and the University of Hong Kong.

The road was named after Samuel George Bonham, the third Governor of Hong Kong.
