Bonnie Doon Shopping Centre is a shopping mall in Edmonton, Alberta, Canada. It is a small sized mall and houses only an estimated 100 stores on one floor. The mall was originally an exposed shopping centre when constructed about 1960, however it was enclosed and a large Sears store added in 1967.

Its anchors are Sears, Zellers, and Safeway.

[edit] Anchors

  • Bonnie Doon Bowling Lanes (39,640 sq. ft.)
  • Canada Safeway (47,500 sq. ft.)
  • Sears (108,406 sq. ft.)
  • Sport Mart (10,001 sq. ft.)
  • Zellers (74,745 sq. ft.)

[edit] Comparable Metro Edmonton malls

Bonnie Doon Shopping Centre is amongst several major malls in Edmonton in terms of size and significance. Amongst the list of comparible malls are: St.Albert Centre, Northgate Centre, Kingsway Garden Mall, Londonderry Mall, Westmount Centre, Sherwood Park Mall, Edmonton City Centre, Commerce Place, Manulife Place, Meadowlark Mall and West Edmonton Mall.
