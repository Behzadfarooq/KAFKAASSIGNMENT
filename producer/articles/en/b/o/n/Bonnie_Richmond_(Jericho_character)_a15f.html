Bonnie Richmond is a character on the American post-apocalyptic drama Jericho. She is played by Shoshannah Stern.

Spoiler warning: Plot and/or ending details follow.

Bonnie is Stanley's younger sister and helps him run the farm. She is deaf and communicates via American Sign Language and lip reading. She is briefly taken hostage by two escaped prisoners posing as sheriff's deputies when Emily brings them, unwittingly, to her farm in search of gas. She opposes her brother's decision to allow Mimi Clark to stay at the farm, indicating in "Vox Populi" that she considers the farm to be hers and doesn't want Mimi to take it away. She is outraged to find that Stanley and Mimi have fallen in love with each other and out of spite, she starts dating a local bad boy name Sean Henthorn. She's been volunteering at the medical clinic for many years. She still struggles being around people who will not communicate with her using sign language and tends to take her anger out on Mimi. When Stanley leaves to go on the New Bern mission, she is left in Mimi's care. Mimi suspects that Bonnie is sneaking out often to meet Sean.
