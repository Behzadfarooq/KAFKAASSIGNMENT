Bonnyrigg is a suburb of Sydney, in the state of New South Wales, Australia. It lies within the Local Government Area of the City of Fairfield and its postcode is 2177. Bonnyrigg Avenue is the commercial centre of the suburb.

[edit] Location

Bonnyrigg lies in Western Sydney, approximately 30 kilometres west of Sydney's central business district as the crow flies and about 36 kilometres by road. Its closest major regional centre is Liverpool. The suburb is shaped like a diamond. The suburbs of Mount Pritchard and Cabramatta West lie to the east on the other side of Green Valley Creek. St Johns Park, Greenfield Park and Edensor Park lie to the north. Bonnyrigg Heights and Green Valley are to the west. Busby and Heckenberg lie to the south.

Bonnyrigg Avenue has a large shopping centre, Bonnyrigg Plaza, as well as several community facilities. The local primary school is adjacent to Bonnyrigg Plaza.

[edit] Transport

There are no train stations in Bonnyrigg. The closest train station is Cabramatta, on the South line and Inner West line. There are bus links to Cabramatta Station and other nearby stations.

Bonnyrigg is served by several bus routes. Westbus operate the following routes via Bonnyrigg: 826, 827, 831, 832, 833, 835 and 836. [edit] Education

There are 2 schools in Bonnyrigg. Bonnyrigg High School and Bonnyrigg Public School.
