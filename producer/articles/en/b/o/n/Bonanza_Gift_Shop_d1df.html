Bonanza Gift Shop is a landmark located on the Las Vegas Strip between the Sahara (hotel and casino) and the Stratosphere Las Vegas. It is billed as the Worlds Largest Gift Shop with over 40,000 square feet of shopping space.

The shop is listed as the The World's Greatest Gift Store by British Airways.[1] However Fodors says. OK, so it may not, in fact, be the world's largest, but it's the town's largest -- and for that matter, the town's best -- souvenir store.[2]

[edit] History

Bonanza Gifts was established in 1980.[3]

It was the Las Vegas Review-Journal's online readers pick for Best Gift Shop in 2006.[4]

[edit] Film history

The shop was featured on the PBS show Going Places.[5]
