The name Bono can refer to:

  • Bono (born Paul David Hewson), Irish musician, activist and lead singer for the band U2.
  • Sonny Bono, American record producer, singer, actor, and politician
  • Chastity Bono, activist and daughter of Sonny Bono and Cher
  • Mary Bono, politician, second wife of Sonny Bono
  • José Bono Martínez, Ex-Minister of Defence in Spain
  • Edward De Bono, writer
  • Steve Bono, former National Football League quarterback
  • Emilio De Bono, Italian general

Bono can also mean:

  • Bono, a city in Craighead County, Arkansas
  • Bono, a town in Vermillion County, Indiana
  • Bono, a city in Sardinia
  • Bono state, an Akan state which existed from the 15th to the 18th century in what is now the Brong-Ahafo region of Ghana.
  • Bono, Morbihan, a town in France
  • Bono, a cookie manufactured by Nestlé.
  • pro bono, a legal term
