Bon Voyage! is the official Disney goods specialty shop located in Urayasu.

The shop itself is shaped like an oversized bag. It is open an hour longer than Tokyo Disneyland or Tokyo DisneySea which makes it ideal shopping place for visitors.

The shop has original goods and also retails popular goods of Tokyo Disney Resort and the two parks. The shop is located outside theme parks and one can enter freely.
