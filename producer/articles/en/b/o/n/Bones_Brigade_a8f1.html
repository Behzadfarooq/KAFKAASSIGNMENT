Bones Brigade

The Bones Brigade logo.

Bones Brigade was a highly successful skateboarding team that rode under the Powell Peralta and later Powell banner. The team was created in 1979 by Stacy Peralta who picked most of the riders. They were very successful in competitions around that time. Some of the members of the team were very original, and helped shape skateboarding with their innovative tricks. By far the most famous former member is Tony Hawk.

Powell Peralta started to produce videos in 1984. To date, there have been 14 videos produced.

Under the name Powell Peralta:

  • The Bones Brigade Video Show (1984)
  • Future Primitive (1985)
  • The Search For Animal Chin (1987)
  • Public Domain (1988)
  • Axe Rated (1988)
  • Ban This (1989)
  • Propaganda (1990)
  • Eight (1991)
  • Celebrity Tropical Fish (1991)

Under the name Powell:

  • Hot Batch (1992)
  • Chaos (1992)
  • Play (1993)
  • Suburban Diners (1994)
  • Scenic Drive (1995)
  • Strip Mall Heroes (1998)

  • Bones Bearings Class of 2000 (1999)
