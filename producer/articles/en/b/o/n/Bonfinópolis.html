Bonfinópolis

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Bonfinópolis is a small town and municipality in central Goiás state, Brazil. It is located a short distance east of Goiânia and is part of the Goiânia metropolitan area.

  • Population: 6,624 in 2005 (4,908 in 2000)
  • Area: 122.7 km²
  • Elevation: 948 meters
  • Became a municipality: 1989
  • IBGE statistical microregion: Goiânia Microregion [1]
  • Postal code: 75195-000

The distance to Goiânia is 33 km. and connections are made by BR-457 / GO-010.

Neighboring municipalities are:

  • north: Goianápolis
  • south: Senador Canedo and Caldazinha
  • east: Leopoldo de Bulhões
  • west: Goiânia [2]

[edit] Economy

The economy is based on cattle raising (12500 head in 2003), milk production, agriculture, commerce, public administration, and small transformation industries.

  • Gross Domestic Product: R$18.2 million in 2003 (approx. 8.640 million dollars at April 29, 2006 rates)
  • Gross Domestic Product per capita: R$2,961 (1,401 dollars at April 29, 2006 rates)
  • Financial Institutions
  • none reporting for 2004

[edit] Main agricultural production

  • small production of oranges, coffee, and bananas
  • small production of rice, manioc, corn, and tomatoes
