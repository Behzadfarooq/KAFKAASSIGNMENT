Bongo can refer to:

  • Um Bongo, a British fruit drink marketed at children.
  • Bongo drums, a musical instrument.
  • Omar Bongo, president of Gabon.
  • The Bongo, a species of forest antelope from Africa
  • Bongo was Kuririn's Harmony Gold English Dragon Ball name dub from the 1980's.
  • The Bongo GUI development tool.
  • Bongo, the main character of Sinclair Lewis' short story Little Bear Bongo and also the name of the segment inspired by the story in the 1947 Disney animated film Fun and Fancy Free.
  • Bongo the Clown, the 'clown persona' of the American artist Glenn.
  • "Bongo," the title character of a story of a circus bear cub, featured in the Disney film, Fun and Fancy Free.
  • Bongo is a colloquial name for Dar es Salaam.
  • A generic term for truck in Asia including:
    • The Mazda Bongo, a van made by Mazda Motor Corp. of Japan, also known as the Mazda E-Series or Ford Econovan.
    • The Kia Motors Bongo series which includes the Kia Bongo Frontier
  • Bongo, a character in Matt Groening's Life in Hell.
  • Bongo Comics Group.
  • Bongo, a fictional Gungan submarine in the movie Star Wars Episode I: The Phantom Menace.
  • An alternate version of Kryten in the British sitcom Red Dwarf.
  • The Bongos, a pop music band most active in the 1980's
  • Bongo (clothing brand) is a young women's clothing brand with focus on denim
  • Bôngô (বঙ্গ), one of the Bengali names used for Bengal.
