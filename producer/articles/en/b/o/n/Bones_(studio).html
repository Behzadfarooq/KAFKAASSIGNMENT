Bones (ボンズ, Bonzu?) is a Japanese anime studio. It is noted for producing noted series such as RahXephon, Wolf's Rain, Scrapped Princess, Eureka Seven, Angelic Layer and Fullmetal Alchemist.

[edit] History

Bones was founded by Sunrise staff members Masahiko Minami, Kōji Ousaka and Toshihiro Kawamoto in October, 1998. One of their first projects was collaborating with Sunrise on Cowboy Bebop: Knockin' on Heaven's Door, a feature film based on the Cowboy Bebop TV anime series.
