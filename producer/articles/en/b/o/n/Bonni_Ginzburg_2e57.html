Bonni Ginzburg (Hebrew: בוני גינצבורג) is a former Israeli international footballer and currently works as a television personality most recently participating in Israel's verson of Dancing with the Stars. It is widely thought that Ginzburg was the first Israeli goalkeeper to play in Europe.
