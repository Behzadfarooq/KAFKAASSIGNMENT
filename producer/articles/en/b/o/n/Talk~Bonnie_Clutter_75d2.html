Thanks for the note -- you'll find the same problem with the Herbert Clutter and Kenyon Clutter articles right now, and will also find similarities between Richard Hickock and Perry Smith (murderer). I think it's worth having individual articles for each of the family members, since each is notable in his or her own right (for example, actors have portrayed them, and there are find-a-grave links to them), and other Wikipedia articles and non-Wikipedia web sites can reasonably want to link to them individually. I used some copy and paste only to get the articles started, but I hope that they will diverge as I and other people contribute more to them, until there is little or no common copy.
