[edit] Summary

Source: Flame of the Forest Publishers, Singapore and [1]

Photo of back book cover of Excuse Me, are you a Model? by deceased author and model Bonny Hicks. See below for copyright information and fair use rationale.

[edit] Preamble

The uploader of this image believes its use on Wikipedia servers in Florida falls under safety of United States fair use doctrine as contained in Title 17 of the United States Code, Circular 92 § 107, "Limitations on exclusive rights".

[edit] Purpose of use

This image serves to identify the subject visually in a way prose cannot and conveys a particular characterization and non-verbal communications about the subject as depicted by his or her appearance and demeanor in a way deemed desirable by the subject during her lifetime and by the book's publisher, Flame of the Forest Publishers, Singapore.

[edit] Character of use

The image is being used in Bonnie Hicks for the purposes of identifying the subject with critical commentary within Wikipedia, an educational encyclopedia.

[edit] Non-profit use

This image is being used in Bonnie Hicks on Wikipedia for non-profit educational purposes. Commercial users of this image under Wikipedia's GFDL license must either exclude this image, make their own fair use determination, or contact the book's publisher for usage conditions.

[edit] Nature and amount of use of the copyrighted work

This is photo of a rare and out-of-print edition of a book. The amount is only the back cover of the book.

[edit] Effect of the use of this image upon the potential market for or value of it

No market exists for a photo of the back cover of a book at low resolution and improper angle.
