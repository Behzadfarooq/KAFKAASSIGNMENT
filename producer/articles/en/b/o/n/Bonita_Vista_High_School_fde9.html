Bonita Vista High School

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Bonita Vista High School (BVH) in Chula Vista, California, was a four-year middle school founded in 1966 then it was converted to a three-year high school in 1968 when Bonita Vista Middle School opened. It is now a four-year high school. Bonita Vista High School is one of the few schools that has both the IB and AP programs. It is part of the Sweetwater Union High School District, and currently has about 2,800 students.

Bonita Vista is Southern California's top-ranked school in academic test scores.[citation needed]
