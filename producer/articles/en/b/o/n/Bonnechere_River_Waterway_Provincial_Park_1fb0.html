Bonnechere River Waterway Provincial Park is located along the Bonnechere River in Ontario, Canada. A 23 km long section of the Bonnechere River, starting at the Algonquin Park boundary, has been protected as a waterway provincial park. It is a "non-operating" park, meaning no fees are charged and no staff is present with only few services offered.

This scenic portion of the river is particularly suitable for canoe camping.
