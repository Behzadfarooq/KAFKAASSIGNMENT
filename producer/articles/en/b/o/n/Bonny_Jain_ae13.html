Bonny Jain (born June 22, 1993) is an academic competitor. He was a 2-time qualifer for the National Geographic Bee national finals, placing fourth in the 2005 event and winning the 2006 National Geographic Bee. He is also a 3-time national qualifier for the Scripps National Spelling Bee national finals, finishing 31st in 2003, 28th in 2005 and 14th in 2006. Jain lives in Moline, Illinois. Jain is a freshman at Moline High School and was selected to the 2007 Illinois High School Scholastic Bowl Coaches Association All-State 1st Team.
