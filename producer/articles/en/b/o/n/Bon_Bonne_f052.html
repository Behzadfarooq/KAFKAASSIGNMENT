Bon Bonne is a character in the Mega Man Legends series.

[edit] History

Bon is the youngest sibling in the Bonne family. Although he is quite a strong warrior throughout all three Legends games, he is really just a big baby. He is only able to talk in "Babuu's", however everyone in the Bonne family is somehow able to understand everything he says. Aside from this, Bon is more of a cameo appearance than a physical character, and thus has little to no history or character development.

[edit] Personality

Bon frequently conveys a personality in line with his perceived age- he acts and performs mannerisms similar to a young child, albeit slightly on a higher intelligence level.

[edit] Trivia

  • Bon's design seems to be based off of Baby Head from the video game Captain Commando.
  • Is one of two characters voiced by Ikue Otani who can only speak in a single word and whose voice is left unchanged in American releases; the other being Pikachu.
