Bonanza is the name of a number of places:

  • Bonanza, Arkansas, USA
  • Bonanza, Colorado, USA
  • Bonanza Creek, a tributary of the Klondike River in the Yukon Territory in Canada
  • Bonanza, Georgia, USA
  • Bonanza, Idaho, USA
  • Bonanza, Oregon, USA
  • Bonanza, Spain

Bonanza may also refer to:

  • Bonanza Bus Lines, a Providence-based bus line, bought out by Peter Pan Bus Lines
  • Beechcraft Bonanza, an aircraft
  • Bonanza, a novel by Neal Stephenson in The Baroque Cycle
  • "Bonanza", a song by The Books in their 2003 album The Lemon of Pink
  • Bonanza, the television series
  • Ponderosa/Bonanza Steakhouse, a restaurant
