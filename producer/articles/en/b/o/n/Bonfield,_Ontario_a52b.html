Bonfield is a township in northeastern Ontario, Canada on the Mattawa River in Nipissing District.

According to the Canada 2001 Census:

  • Population: 2,064
  • % Change (1996-2001): 16.9
  • Dwellings: 963
  • Area (km².): 205.75
  • Density (persons per km².): 10.0
