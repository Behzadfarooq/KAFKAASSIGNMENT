Bonar Bain

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Bonar Bain (born February 4, 1923 in Lethbridge, Alberta), a Canadian actor, is the identical twin brother of the actor Conrad Bain who starred in Diff'rent Strokes as Phillip Drummond, father to Arnold and Willis Jackson. He once played a fictional "evil" twin to Conrad ("Hank Bain") in an episode of SCTV.
