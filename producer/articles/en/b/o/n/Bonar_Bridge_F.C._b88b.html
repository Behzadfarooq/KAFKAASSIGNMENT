Bonar Bridge Football Club is a football club based in the town of Bonar Bridge in the Scottish highlands. It was formed in 1968.

The team took part in the North Caledonian Football League until the start of the 2006/07 season, when it withdrew.

The team play in green, white and black strips (uniforms).
