Bongzilla is a stoner metal group from Madison, Wisconsin, which was formed in 1995. As their name suggests, much (if not all) of their material is related to drug culture, especially Cannabis. Bongzilla are currently signed to Relapse Records. Their current bassist, "Dixie" Dave Collins, plays bass and provides vocals for fellow sludge bands Weedeater and Buzzov*en, and also plays bass for Sourvein.
