Bone (disambiguation)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Look up bone in Wiktionary, the free dictionary.

Bone or bones may refer to:

  • Bone, rigid connective organ that makes up the skeleton of vertebrates
  • Osseous tissue, a rigid mineralized tissue that is one component of bones
  • Bône, a city in Algeria, now known as Annaba
  • Bone (corsetry), a rigid vertical rib that gives a corset its shape and rigidity
  • Bone (recording), Soviet-era bootleg records, scratched into scavenged sheets of plastic
  • Bone state, an Indonesian state, existing from 1350 to 1960
  • BONES (studio), a Japanese animation studio
  • BeOS Networking Environment (BONE), a networking environment for the BeOS
  • Slang names for the games of dominoes or dice
  • Slang term for cannabis cigarettes

[edit] In music

  • Bones (instrument), an idiophonic musical instrument made of bones, wood, or other materials
  • A common slang term for the trombone musical instrument
  • Bones (drummer), the original drummer with punk rock outfit The Anti-Nowhere League
  • Bones Hillman, the bassist for Australian band Midnight Oil
  • Bone Thugs-N-Harmony, an American rap group from Cleveland, Ohio
  • "Bones", a song by Radiohead on their second album, The Bends
  • "Bones" (The Killers song), a song by The Killers from their second album, Sam's Town
  • The Bones (band), a punk rock band from Sweden
  • Bone Machine, a 1992 album by Tom Waits
  • "Bone Machine", a song on The Pixies' album Surfer Rosa

[edit] In entertainment

  • Bones, the nickname of Star Trek character Dr. Leonard McCoy, or more generally any medical doctor
  • Bone (comics), a series of comic books/graphic novels by Jeff Smith
    • An episodic video game series based on the comic: Bone: Out from Boneville, Bone: The Great Cow Race
  • Bones (film), a 2001 horror film starring Snoop Dogg

  • Bones (TV series), an ongoing crime-drama
