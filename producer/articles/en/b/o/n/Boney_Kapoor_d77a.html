Boney Kapoor is an Indian film producer. He is the son of Surinder Kapoor, who is also a film producer. His brothers are actors Anil Kapoor and Sanjay Kapoor. He married Indian actress Sridevi on June 2, 1996 and the couple have two children. He has a son and daughter from his first marriage too. He has produced many films that star both his brothers, Anil and Sanjay. His Sister Reena is married to Film Studio Biggie Sandeep Marwah of the famous Marwah Films & Video Studios at Noida Film City, NOIDA.
