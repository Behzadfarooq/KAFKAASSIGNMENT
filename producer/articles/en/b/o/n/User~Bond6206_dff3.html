Hello! I'm Bond6206. I am a James Bond fan and only edit articles having to do with James Bond. I have seen most of the James Bond films, I liked the performances of all the actors who have played the character with the exception of Daniel Craig. I found his portrayal of the character too stone-cold and he didn't have the slightest bit of humor or suaveness of the other actors that played Bond.

[edit] Favorite Bond Movies(in random order)

  • GoldenEye - My all time favorite. I liked overall plot, Pierce Brosnan's portrayal of the character, an the cast of characters.
  • The Spy Who Loved Me
  • Tomorrow Never Dies
  • A View to a Kill - Despite many claims that it is the worst Bond film, I think it is one of the best. The interesting plot, locations, and the nearly all female cast of henchmen make it one of the best films in the series.
  • The World is Not Enough
  • Licence to Kill

[edit] Least Favorite Bond Movies(in random order)

  • The Man with the Golden Gun - I disliked the plot and felt that it dragged on too long making it boring.
  • Casino Royale - I didn't like the idea of it being like a prequel since James Bond just getting his Licence to Kill and OO status. I disliked Craig's portrayal of the character as well.
  • Moonraker - Though I did enjoy some parts of it, I found it to be too 1970's. I pretty-much just didn't like it overall.
