Bond's Hospital

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Hill Street, showing Bond's Hospital (nearest the camera) and Bablake School (the half-timbered building at the end of the street)

Bond's Hospital is an almshouse situated on Hill Street, Coventry, England. It is built around the same courtyard as the old buildings of Bablake School (which are no longer used by the school).

The hospital was founded in 1506. Most of the street frontage was rebuilt in 1846.

Bond's Hospital is now a grade II* listed building.
