Bonne Bell is a cosmetics company. It was founded in 1927 in Cleveland, Ohio, by cosmetics salesman Jesse Bell, who named it after the heroine of Emerson Hough's novel "The Man Next Door".

[edit] Lip Smackers

In 1973, Bonne Bell introduced a flavored lip gloss named "Lip Smackers". The first flavor created was strawberry.

Lip Smackers comes in flavors such as fruit, candy, or soda pop. Some varieties are tinted, while others include glitter.

There are now other variations known as Lip D'Votion and Lip Lix (with added color).

[edit] Cultural references

In the movie Napoleon Dynamite, Summer Wheatley promises a "glitter Bonne Bell dispenser in all the girls' restrooms" in her bid for student body presidency.

In the movie Detroit Rock City, Christine, played by Natasha Lyonne, is seen applying a strawberry flavored lip smacker while hitching a ride with the four main characters.

Lip Smackers is mentioned by Fez in an episode of That '70s Show 'Vanstock'. In the season 2 episode, he rifles through Jackie's belongings and pulls out a sparkle tube.
