Bonnie Swanson is a fictional character from the animated television series Family Guy. She is Joe's devoted wife and mother of Kevin. She is voiced by actress Jennifer Tilly. Bonnie is rather timid and always does as Joe says (she once said "I can't say no to Joe... ever"). She gets aroused when Joe lies and likes a man who puts himself in danger. Peter claims that Joe has not touched Bonnie in a long while, however.

She has been pregnant since her debut on the show in season 1 with no signs of progress. Peter has even commented on this fact in the season 4 episode "Blind Ambition", stating that she has "been pregnant for like six years" (which is not surprising, as none of the other characters have shown any physical signs of aging). It is likely due to this fact that Bonnie dies before Joe in Stewie Griffin: The Untold Story. While being eternally with-child, she still took Tae Jitsu lessons, which inspired Lois to join the class. In the movie Rolling Courage: The Joe Swanson Story, Valerie Bertinelli played Bonnie. In the film Stewie Griffin: The Untold Story, 30 years in the future, she will have her legs given to Joe after she dies. In the season 2 episode "Love thy Trophy" Lois, perhaps accidentally, calls her Debbie.
