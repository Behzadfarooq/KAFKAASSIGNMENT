Bonoful (1899-1979), was a renowned Bengali author, a charismatic short story writer.

Bonoful, (Wildflower) was his pen name where the main name was Bolaichad Mukhopadhyay.

[edit] Works

His short stories are very short but witty. Humor and satire is found here. Every practical thing is seen by Bonoful and he sketches that perfectly.

His literary works were included in the curriculum of school level, secondary, higher secondary and graduation level Bengali Literature in Bangladesh.
