Bone Machine is an album by Tom Waits, released in 1992 (see 1992 in music) on Island Records. It won a Grammy Award for Best Alternative Music Album.

"Goin' Out West" is featured in the 1999 film Fight Club.

"Earth Died Screaming" is featured in the 1995 film 12 Monkeys.

"Jesus Gonna Be Here" is featured in the 2005 film Domino, in which Waits appears.
