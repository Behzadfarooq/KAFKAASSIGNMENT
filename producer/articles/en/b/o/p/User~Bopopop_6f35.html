[edit] Me

[edit] ♠♥♦♣

I am a human of male adolescence. Unlike most boys my age in the United States, my days are not filled with playing Grand Theft Auto or Halo. They are not filled with talking about how stupid school is, skating, or talking about how badly you want to cheat on a test. They are not filled with listening to Alternative, putting Axe on, and not taking prescription drugs. Instead, I play video games along the lines of music/BEMANI games, RPGs, and unique games. I enjoy school, and if I don't like it one day I don't whine about it because too many children are dying for an education, skateboarding looks dangerous and I don't like the subculture, and cheating on a test is in my humble opinion fear and laziness mixed up in a blender. The only Alternative band I like is Panic! At the Disco, I find axe too strong and many girls agree with me that it does the opposite of attracting women, and I believe psychiatic drugs should be a last resort.

It would be very hard to name the things I like, because like pretty much all humans we can only focus on the things that we don't like. From deep examination of my soul, my favorite color is champagne (if I didn't say color you would think that I am an alcoholic), my favourite music genre is electronica, I dislike Starbucks for very personal reasons, I have grown a likeing for alcoholic and substance abuse literature, I prefer theatre over cinema, I prefer to nourish my body without the consumption of meat, not one day goes by without me thinking of the persecuted, I like art, I speak a good amount of Japanese, and I hug a pillow when I go to sleep.

When I am older, I would like to do something with my knowledge of Japanese and my adoration of art. I would like that because I keep those kind of things close to my heart. Of course luck/kismet/destiny is not going to give me that, and instead I will land a job totally not related to the first sentence of this paragraph. I think that at this age, we should have goals and not dreams. Goals are more serious, and dreams are more of a fantasy which are no different than Cinderella and the People's Republic of China's beliefs on the Tiananmen Square protests in 1989. A goal is that I would like to write a memoir, but my dream is to be married in space.

People say I talk with elegance, and chances are I do. I have studied German, French, and Japanese. Japanese is my main studying language of choice. I prefer to use the English English spellings (like centre as opposed to the American English spelling center), and I try to pronounce things the way they were spelt. You may also notice that I put a lot of thought in what of say. I do this, as a Taoist, to make sure nobody gets hurt. That is not fun, and I have been there. When it comes to political issues, I try to strip it of its contents so that it is in the simplest form. I then find the opposite, and I make my decision from there. I am liberal. I enjoy it. And I doubt I will change.
