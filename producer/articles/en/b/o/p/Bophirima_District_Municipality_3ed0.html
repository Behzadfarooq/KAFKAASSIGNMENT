Bophirima is one of the 4 districts of North West province of South Africa. The seat of Bophirima is Vryberg. The majority of its 439 637 people speak Tswana (2001 Census). The district code is DC39.

[edit] Geography

[edit] Neighbours

Bophirima is surrounded (clockwise) by:

  • the republic of Botswana to the north
  • Central (DC38) to the north-east
  • Southern (DC40) to the south-east
  • Lejweleputswa (DC18) to the south-east
  • Frances Baard (DC9) to the south
  • Kgalagadi (CBDC1) to the west
