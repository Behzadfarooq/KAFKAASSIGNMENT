Hey there, Boyd here. I am Florida native that now lives/works in Minneapolis. I hold a tailoring degree and love to work on anything related to fiber, especially Complex Cloth. I also quilt and make garments. I am pursuing a B.A. in Religion with a history minor at Augsburg College. I love to cook, eat, read and sleep - which is most all I get to do these days. I am fanatic for history and love all things Spanish. I'm a bit of a sinophile, too. My web site where you can see my quilts and fabric is at fiberguy.com and I also have a blog too.
