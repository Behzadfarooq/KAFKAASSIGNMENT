Boyer Coe (born August 15, 1946 in Lake Charles, Louisiana) is an American former professional bodybuilder.

[edit] Bodybuilding career

Coe was 14 years of age when be first started training at the Lake Charles Gym, in fact Boyer was the first person to enroll as a member of this new gym. At the age of 15, he prophesied that he would someday become Mr. America, a dream that eventually came true. When he went to college in Lafayette, Louisiana and began to train and subsequently work at Red Lerille's gym. Red was the first man from Louisiana to win the Mr. America title and he turned out to be a great influence in perfecting Boyer's posing routine. Coe himself continued to train at Red's gym for a total of 14 years, until he opened a gym of his own in New Orleans.

Coe was married to his wife Valerie on December, 15 1979 in Huntington Beach, California on board a luxury yacht. Many other bodybuilders were present at the ceremony, with Joe Weider giving Valerie away to Boyer during the ceremony.

Coe enjoyed a very successful career in the sport, winning an unprecedented seven pro shows in 1981 alone.

Boyer is now retired from the sport and living in Southern California.
