Boys Club

Boys Club was the R&B duo of Gene Hunt and Joe Pasquale from Minneapolis, Minnesota that had a big hit with "I Remember Holding You" in 1989. Throughout the greater part of Minnesota, Boys Club was also regarded as "Minnesota's version of WHAM!."

Hunt (born Eugene Wolfgramm) was previously a member of the family group The Jets.

In 1990, Hunt briefly reunited with The Jets to record four new tracks for "The Best of The Jets" (1990) and was featured on the cover and inside album sleeve.
