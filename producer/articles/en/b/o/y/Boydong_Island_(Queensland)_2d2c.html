Boydong Island is a small islands in the Boydong cays Shelburne Bay in far north Queensland, Australia about 100 km North of Cape Grenville, Cape York Peninsula in the Great Barrier Reef Marine Park Queensland, Australia.

They a part of the East Islands group about 25km North East from Captain Billy Landing.
