Boy Waterman (born 1984-01-24 in Lelystad) is a Dutch football goalkeeper who currently plays for SC Heerenveen, where he started his career. He is currently on loan at AZ Alkmaar

On February 10, 2007 he played his first Eredivisie match for AZ against Willem II [1] He made his European debut in AZ Alkmaar's 3-3 draw against Fenerbahce (UEFA Cup) on February 14, 2007.
