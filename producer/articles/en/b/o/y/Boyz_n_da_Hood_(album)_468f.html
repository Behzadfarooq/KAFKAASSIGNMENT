Boyz n da Hood is a 2005 album by Boyz n da Hood. It was executive-produced by Sean "Diddy" Combs and Block, with co-producer Harve Pierre.

The first single from the album was Dem Boyz, featuring solo verses from Young Jeezy, Jody Breeze, and Big Duke.

The album was also released in a censored version with rather inconsistent editing that varied from verse to verse. Sometimes words like "gun" or "AK" would be censored, other times they wouldn't. Most drug content was edited and all profanity is removed.
