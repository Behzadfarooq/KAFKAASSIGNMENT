Boys Don't Cry is the title of:

  • a 1999 movie starring Hilary Swank and Chloe Sevigny: see Boys Don't Cry (film)
  • the name of the 1979 US-released album Boys Don't Cry by The Cure, featuring much the same content as the UK release Three Imaginary Boys of the same year
  • the title song by The Cure of both the (US) album and the movie: see "Boys Don't Cry (song)"
  • a band of session musicians who had a one-hit wonder in 1986 with "I Wanna be a Cowboy": see "Boys Don't Cry (band)"
  • the title of an mc chris song from his album Eating's Not Cheating
  • the title of a Plumb song from the album Beautiful Lumps of Coal
  • for the Family Guy episode, see Boys DO Cry
