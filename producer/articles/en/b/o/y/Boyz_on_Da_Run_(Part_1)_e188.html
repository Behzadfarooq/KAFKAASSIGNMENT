Boyz on Da Run is the fifth episode of the Disney Channel's animated series Shorty McShorts' Shorts.

[edit] Plot

Dem Boyz From Your Cul-De-Sac (or DBFYC as known by their fans) get a gig after their success of their album. Despite their choreagrapher Cory trying to warn them of something about to happen, DBFYC's manager Stu rushes the boys and stage, and tells them to lip-sync instead of actually singing. Then, DBFYC is exposed as lip-syncing after a technical failure (à la Milli Vanilli). They are chased out of town, and board a train to leave town. While on the train Skylar tells them that Cory would want them to stick together so they decide that they will get back together and sing using their real voices.

Songs performed: "I Would Never Lie To You", and "Never Break Us Apart"
