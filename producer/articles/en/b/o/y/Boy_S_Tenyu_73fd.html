Boy S Tenyu (2005) (Russian: Бой с тенью, Shadow Boxing) is likely the first truly Hollywood class Russian action movie.

The stars of the film include famous Russian actors such as Denis Nikiforov, Elena Panova, and Andrey Panin. Directed by Aleksey Sidorov.

[edit] Plot summary

Spoiler warning: Plot and/or ending details follow.

The Brigade has raised Artem Kolchin, but he went his own way. He became a boxer. Hir rank grew as he became title contender for the world championship and a global celebrity. But he had to return to the streets of his home town to protect his love. He has only 72 hours to settle the accounts with the people who betrayed him. He also has to save his girlfriend from hired killers and to collect a huge ransom. Just one more note: at the moment, the boxer is blind.
