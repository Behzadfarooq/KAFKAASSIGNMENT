[edit] Why is this paradoxical?

I don't see why this flask is considered paradoxical -- it looks to me like it's just a bad drawing. Water finds its own level, so the level of water in the side arm would remain level with the water surface in the main body of the flask. Why is it drawn as flowing upwards and defying gravity? You might just as well say that a drawing of Niagara Falls running backwards is a paradox! 143.252.80.110 15:31, 5 July 2006 (UTC)
