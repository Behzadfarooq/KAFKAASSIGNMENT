"Boys & Girls" is the ninth single released by Ayumi Hamasaki on July 14, 1999. It is her first single to be released as a Maxi single and also the first to sell over a million copies. In its first week of release, it ranked #2 after being beaten out for the top spot by Ami Suzuki's "Be Together". Though the following week it was able to hit #1.
