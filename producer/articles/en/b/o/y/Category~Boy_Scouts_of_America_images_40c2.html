Boy Scouts of America images

Only free images (Public Domain, GFDL, and free Creative Commons) can be in this category because it uses a gallery.

This category is intended to be used for any free use images related to the Boy Scouts of America that do not fit into any of the sub-categories. Types of free images to be included here include:

  • Photos of Scout-related events, people, places
  • Photos of 3-dimensional artwork or other objects
  • Book or magazine covers
  • Stamps
  • Certificates
  • And so forth, as long as they are free.

Fair use logos should be included under Category:Scout logos or Category:Boy Scouts of America logos. This includes:

  • Scans or photos of 2-dimensional Scout patches or awards
  • Image files depicting 2-dimensional Scout logos
  • Similar logo items

All other fair use images go in Category:Non-free Scouting images or one of its sub-categories.
