Boynton Beach Station is a Tri-Rail station in Boynton Beach, Palm Beach County, Florida.

This station is located on High Ridge Road, south of Gateway Boulevard and east of State Road 9. It officially opened January 1989 and was remodeled in 2003.

Parking is available at this station.
