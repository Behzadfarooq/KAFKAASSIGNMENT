Boy Meets Boy
Music Bill Solly
Lyrics Bill Solly
Book Bill Solly and Donald Ward
Productions 1975 Off-Broadway


Boy Meets Boy was an 1975 off-Broadway musical comedy with music and lyrics by Bill Solly, and book by Bill Solly and Donald Ward, produced by Christopher Larkin and Edith O'Hara in association with Lee Barton at the Actor's Playhouse.

The show, a light-hearted musical, features a 1940s style Astaire/Rodgers romance between two men, and a same-sex marriage.

The title refers to the much-used comedy plot-line, "Boy meets Girl, Boy loses Girl, Boy gets Girl".
