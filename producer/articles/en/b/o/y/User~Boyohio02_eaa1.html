I am a student studying Political Science at Washington State Community College in Marietta Ohio. I was born and raised in rural Ohio and have lived in Pittsburgh Pennsylvania, Knoxville Tennessee, and Las Vegas Nevada. I am 22 years old, and an active member of the Democratic Party. I work on political campaigns, and have worked to solve important issues facing America and the world. I has work as a volunteer in local, state and National democratic campaigns, and currently plan to work on Barack Obama's Presidential Campaign. I have two descendants from the Mayflower, one is Governor William Bradford the other is Edward Doty. I am also related to Rufus Putnam and Israel Putnam the Revolutionary War Colonel. Most of his descendants are from Cheshire, England, Scotland, and Belfast, Ireland.
