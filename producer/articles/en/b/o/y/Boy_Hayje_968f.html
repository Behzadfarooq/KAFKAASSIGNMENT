Johan "Boy" Hayje (born May 3, 1949, Amsterdam) is a former Formula One driver from the Netherlands. He participated in 7 grands prix, debuting on August 29, 1976. He scored no championship points.

After time spent racing saloon cars and winning the Dutch Formula Ford championship, Hayje raced in Formula 5000 and Formula 3. He drove a privately-entered Penske in his home grand prix in 1976, before a difficult period in F1 the following year driving a March for RAM Racing.

Once his Formula One career was over, Hayje raced in the European Renault 5 Turbo championship.
