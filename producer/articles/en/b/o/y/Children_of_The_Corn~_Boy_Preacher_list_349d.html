This is the profile for all of the boy preachers from the Children of the Corn films.

[edit] Preacher Proflie

Isaac (John Franklin)

The kid who startedit all. Isaac Chroner was a boy-preacher who came to a small town in Nebracka called Gatlin with a mission. To rid it of the adults who inhabitants and make way for the glorious coming of He Who Walks Behind The Rows. The Original "giver of his word", took over after the children of Gatlin began to see him as a leader figure, following him into the corn and listening to his preaching of He Who Walks Behind The Rows. Eventually, the children completely converted to his way of thinking, and murdered their own parents at his will. He has good points, as well as bad. He's loyal to He Who Walks Behind The Rows, and follows his rules. Unfortunately, we see that Isaac seems to get a little too power-hungry at the end, which causes his un-doing at the hands of his own God. (Through Isaac returns again in the si installment)

Micah (Ryan Bollman)

The second leader/preacher after Isaac, Micah is a slightly different case. While Isaac obeyed He Who Walks Behind The Rows of his own free will, Micah was possessed by him in the cornfields. Taking over Micah's body, destroyed the good in him and replaced it with the seed of evil. Sly and careful, Micah was a formidable preacher and more then a match for anyone. Obsessed with completing the mission. He'll let nothing stand in his way. Micah also brought the children to his will, but in the end Micah meet his dimisses at the blades of a corncutter.

Eli (Daniel Cerny)

Eli is a strange one to describe. Through he has a connection to He Who Wlaks Behind The Rows, and serves his will, he's a different creature. Eli appears human, but is really a huge creature with tentacle-like claws and fangs. He does one better, as he spreads the evil that started in Gatlin all the way to Chicago. He starts off by turning almost all high school students into almost-mindless drones, following the orders of He Who Walks Behind The Rows. He also carries a bible with him which acts as his "other half". His half-brother ends up destroying him with this book. (like a worm, he hd to destroy both sides body & book)

Josiah (Brandon Kleyla)
