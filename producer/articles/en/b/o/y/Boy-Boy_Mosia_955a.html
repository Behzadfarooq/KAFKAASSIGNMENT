Boy-Boy Mosia (born 1 July 1985 in South Africa) is a South African soccer player who currently plays as a midfielder for Oud-Heverlee Leuven in the Belgian Second Division. He is however still part of K.V.C. Westerlo, as he is on loan to Oud-Heverlee Leuven.
