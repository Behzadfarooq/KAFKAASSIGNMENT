Boys Town

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Boys Town and Boystown, among other variations of the name and phrase, are common terms in reference to various places, organizations, institutions and media.

[edit] Places

  • Boystown, Chicago—a neighborhood enclave of Chicago, Illinois, (part of Lakeview) known as the first gay village in the United States
  • Boys Town, Nebraska— a suburb of Omaha, Nebraska and the headquarters of the Boys Town organization
  • Boy's Town, Nuevo Laredo — a district in Nuevo Laredo, Mexico, notorious for its prostitution and also known as La Zona Rosa
  • Northalsted—see Boystown, Chicago
  • The intersection of Santa Monica Blvd. and San Vicente Blvd. in West Hollywood, California known for being the most public gay village in Los Angeles.

[edit] Institutions

  • Girls and Boys Town (previously known as Boys Town)— an organization dedicated to the housing and education of at-risk children, founded by Father Edward J. Flanagan.
  • Boys Town High School (A boarding school in Nashik District, Maharashtra, India)
  • Boys Town (juvenile detention centre)—a juvenile detention centre in Engadine, New South Wales, Australia.

[edit] Media

  • Boys Town — a film about Father Flanagan and his work starring Spencer Tracy and Mickey Rooney
