"Boy Looka Here" is the second single off the self titled album by rap artist Rich Boy. The single was produced by Polow Da Don. The video was recently released and features the Alabama A&M University marching band. This song was used on an ECW segment called Extreme Expose'. The song was also featured in the film "Rocky Balboa"
