Shawn Michaels: Boyhood Dream

Shawn Michaels: Boyhood Dream

DVD cover of "Shawn Michaels: Boyhood Dream"
Running time 120 Minutes

Shawn Michaels: Boyhood Dream is a DVD produced by World Wrestling Entertainment, looking back at some of the matches leading up to Shawn Michaels first and second WWF World Championship reigns. This is a reissue of Coliseum Video's Shawn Michaels: Boyhood Dream video, released in 1997.

[edit] Matches

1. Shawn Michaels vs. Bret Hart — (March 31, 1996, Wrestlemania XII)

2. Shawn Michaels vs. Diesel — (April 28, 1997, In Your House 7: Good Friends, Better Enemies)

3. Shawn Michaels vs. British Bulldog — (May 26, 1996, In Your House 8: Beware of Dog)

4. Shawn Michaels vs. Sycho Sid — (?)

5. Shawn Michaels vs. Vader — (August 18, 1996, Summerslam)

6. Shawn Michaels vs. Mankind — (September 22, 1996, In Your House 10: Mind Games)

7. Shawn Michaels vs. Sycho Sid — (November 16, 1996, Survivor Series)

8. Shawn Michaels vs. Sycho Sid — (January 19, 1997, Royal Rumble)
