To place an image in this category, add the tag {{BSAlogo}} to the bottom of the image's description page. If you are not sure which category an image belongs to, consult the image copyright tag page. If this category is very large, please consider adding a table of contents ({{CategoryTOC}}) or place your image in a new or existing subcategory.

This sub-category is intended to be used for "logo" images related to the Boy Scouts of America. Since these images are almost always fair use, a gallery cannot be displayed on this page.

Types of images to be included here:

  • Scans or photos of 2-dimensional BSA patches or awards
  • Image files depicting 2-dimensional BSA logos
  • Similar BSA logo items

The following "non-logo" images should be included under Category:Boy Scouts of America images:

  • Photos of BSA-related events, people, places
  • Photos of 3-dimensional artwork or other objects
  • Book or magazine covers
  • Stamps
  • Certificates
  • And so forth
