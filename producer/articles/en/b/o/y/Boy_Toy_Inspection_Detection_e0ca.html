Boy Toy/Inspection Detection

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

"Boy Toy/Inspection Detection" is episode #2.3 of the Nickelodeon animated television series The Fairly Oddparents, created by Butch Hartman. It originally aired March 8, 2002.

[edit] Plot

[edit] "Boy Toy"

Timmy is playing with Chester and A.J., destroying all their old toys, including Timmy's Crimson Chin action figure. Tootie then comes in, just before they throw water balloons at her to make her go away. Timmy is about to destroy his Crimson Chin action figure, but then decides to play with it one more time, wishing himself to toy size. Tootie then grabs Cosmo and Wanda, who are disguised as little fairy dolls, and Timmy goes to Vicky's house in his miniature size to rescue his fairies. Along the way, he confronts various perils with the Crimson Chin action figure.

[edit] "Inspection Detection"

Cosmo and Wanda have a fairy inspection; Timmy is framed for shoplifting in the Wall-to-Wall Mart. He is not really the shoplifter, Francis is.

[edit] Trivia

  • This was the first appearance of Tootie on the Fairly Oddparents TV show, after her sole appearance in the short, The Fairy Flu.
  • Wall-to-Wall Mart is a spoof of Wal-Mart.
  • Timmy's tree house interior is based upon the bridge of the USS Enterprise
  • Timmy's girl-bugging club is a parody of Calvin's club in the comic strip Calvin and Hobbes, G.R.O.S.S.. His club is also spoofed because Timmy and his club throws water balloons at girls.
  • Comedian Jay Leno voiced the Crimson Chin.
