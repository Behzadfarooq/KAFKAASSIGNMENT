Boyz 'N Commotion is an episode of That's So Raven.

[edit] Trivia

  • First appearance of the Boyz 'N Motion
  • First appearance of Bianca
  • In the past episodes (such as A Fish Called Raven and A Goat's Tale) Raven and Chelsea talk about them and worship them but we don't see the band until this episode
  • This second time Debbie Allen has directed an episode of That's So Raven.
  • Fifth appearance of Loca
  • Fifth appearance of Muffy
  • Tenth appearance of William
  • Raven had one vision, that is everyone yelling 'We hate Raven!' because of her Boyz impression.

[edit] Plot

Raven makes the Boys N' Motion quit their job when they relize that showbiz is a hard job, so they star at Raven's house. The next day, Raven goes to school and brags how the Boys are staying at her house. Bianca does not believe her, so she tells them that the Boys will perform at a concert. At home, the Boys refuse. They say that they want to quit for real. After, Raven and her crew dress up as the Boys and Bianca reveals their true identity. At home, Tanya tells the boys how dirty they were and if they tell that they have someones back, they mean it. So, the Boys show up and Raven is saved. A sub-plot is about Cory and William selling items like a used tissue paper that the boys used.
