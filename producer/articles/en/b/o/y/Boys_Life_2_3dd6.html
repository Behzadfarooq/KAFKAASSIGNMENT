Boys Life 2 is a compilation of 4 short films about being gay in America.

  • "Must Be the Music" offers a frank depiction of urban gay youth.
  • "Nunzio's Second Cousin" finds a gay policeman getting even with homophobic people.
  • "Alkali, Iowa" (directed by Mark Christopher) chronicles a Midwestern, teenaged gay male who unearths his dead father's secret.
  • "The DadShuttle" centers on the communication breakdown between a father and his son, who is gay.
