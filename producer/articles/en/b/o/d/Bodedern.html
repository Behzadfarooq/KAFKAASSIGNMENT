Bodedern

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Bodedern is a village in the west of Anglesey, North Wales, at grid reference SH334804. The Royal Mail postcode begins LL65. The population was 1,017 in 1991.

The village has a Welsh-medium secondary school, Ysgol Uwchradd Bodedern and a football team, Bodedern F.C. who play in the Cymru Alliance League.
