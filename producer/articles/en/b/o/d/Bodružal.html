The historic Church of St. Nicholas in Bodružal. The church has been reconstructed with the assistance of the U.S. Commission for the Preservation of America’s Heritage Abroad, and was blessed on September 18, 2003 by Bishop Ján Babjak of Prešov.

Bodružal (1927-1973 spelled Bodružaľ) is a municipality (village) in Slovakia in the Svidník District in Prešov Region.

The village, first mentioned in writing in 1600, is best known for housing a Baroque wooden church being a natural cultural monument in Slovakia.
