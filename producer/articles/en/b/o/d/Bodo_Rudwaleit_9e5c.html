Bodo Rudwaleit (born 3 August 1957) was a prominent football (soccer) goalkeeper in his home country East Germany. He played during most of his career for the football club Dynamo Berlin. He won the local championship ten times in a row from 1979 to 1988 with his team. He was also the goalkeeper of the East Germany national squad for over a decade before the collapse of the country between 1989 and 1990.

He was also known for being impressively tall for a football player, 2.02 meters.
