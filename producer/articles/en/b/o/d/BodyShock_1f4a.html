BodyShock is a scientific documentary series about the conditions or lives of extraordinary people. It was originally produced by redback for Channel 4 in the UK but since September 2006, it has been ArkMedia.

Image of Patrick Deuel in 2004, as featured on the Bodyshock story, 'Half Ton Man'

There have been three series since December 2003. These include (not necessarily in order):

  • The Boy who Gave Birth to his Twin (First story. Shown 12th December 2003)
  • Wild Child
  • Anatomy of a Shark Bite
  • The Man who Slept for 19 Years
  • The Man who Ate his Lover
  • Megatumour
  • Girl with the X-Ray Eyes
  • Orgasmatron
  • When Anaesthesia Fails
  • The Curse of the Mermaid
  • Half Ton Man
  • The 80 Year-old Children
  • Born with Two Heads
  • The Boy in the Bubble
  • World's Biggest Boy
