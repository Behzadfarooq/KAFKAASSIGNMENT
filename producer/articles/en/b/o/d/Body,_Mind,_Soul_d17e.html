Body, Mind, Soul is the fourth album by American singer Debbie Gibson released on the Atlantic label in 1993. The album was not successful in the US, peaking at #109 at Billboard 200 album charts and was uncertified. It would be her last album to chart on the Billboard 200 albums chart.
