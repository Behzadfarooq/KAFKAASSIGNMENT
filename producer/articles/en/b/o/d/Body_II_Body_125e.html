Body II Body, the 2nd single from Gotta Tell You, was released in fall of 2000. The chorus from David Bowie's "Ashes To Ashes' is sampled heavily in the song. The single reached the top 5 in the UK and #2 in Ireland. The single wasn't released in the US.

The single was used as background for a seatbelt awareness video entitled No Seatbelt, No Excuse, aired across Ireland.
