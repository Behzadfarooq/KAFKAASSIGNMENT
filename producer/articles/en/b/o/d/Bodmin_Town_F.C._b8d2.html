Bodmin Town F.C. is a football club based in Bodmin, Cornwall, England, in the United Kingdom.

They were established in 1889 and joined the South Western League in 1953. They have reached the 4th round of the FA Vase twice in their history. For the 2005-06 season, they were Champions of the South Western League and losing finalists in the Cornwall Senior Cup.
