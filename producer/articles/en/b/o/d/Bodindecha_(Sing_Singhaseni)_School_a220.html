Bodindecha (Sing Singhaseni) School

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Bodindecha building 2003
Bodindecha museum 2003

Bodindecha (Sing Singhaseni) School (บดินทรเดชา (สิงห์ สิงหเสนี)), commonly abbreviated as Bodin, is a Thai high school. There are two other schools in Bangkok of the same name, "Bodin 2" and "Bodin 3" These are three of the most widely respected public high schools in all of Thailand. They teach students from grade 7 up to grade 12. This school is located in Wangthonglhang, Bangkok. The number of currently registered students is approximately 4200.

This school was established in 30 April 1971. The name of the school was derived from Chao Phraya Bodindecha, whose real name is Sing Singhaseni, who was a war general under King Rama III (Phra Nangklao) of Thailand.
