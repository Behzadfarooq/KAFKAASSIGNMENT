Bodie and Brock Thoene (pronounced Tay-nee) are the authors of more than 45 works of historical fiction. Eight of their books have won Gold Medallion Awards.

Bodie has written articles appearing in "The Saturday Evening Post", "U.S. News and World Report", and "The American West".

The pair have four children and five grandchildren, and spend time living in both Nevada and London.
