Body Talk is an album by The Wallets. 1,849 vinyl albums, 1,771 cassettes, and 1,597 CDs were sold.

  6. "Skin Deep"
  7. "I Want My Body Back"
  8. "(You Sure Make Me) Feel"
  9. "100 Dragons"
