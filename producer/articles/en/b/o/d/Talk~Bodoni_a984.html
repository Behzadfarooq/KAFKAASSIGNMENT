[edit] IBM corporate identity

I put this back in because IBM do (have always?) use Bodonis in their print ads, especially for text in magazine inserts and outdoor. The well-known IBM logo is of course a typographic wordmark in a chunky slab serif style, but it's not the only public "face" of IBM. Text typography is a subtle and for some people subliminal part of corporate I.D.
