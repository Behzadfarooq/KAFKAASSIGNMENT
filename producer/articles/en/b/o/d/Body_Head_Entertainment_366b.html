Body Head Entertainment is a Pensacola based record label originally founded in 1998 by Heavyweight boxing champion Roy Jones, Jr. The title "Body Head" was derived from the term "head to body", a favourite among Roy Jones, Jr.'s arsenal of boxing maneuver.

  • Roy Jones, Jr.
  • Choppa
  • Bone Crusher
  • Boneface
  • 3D (Comprising of D.J., D-Man & Dre)

  • VI.com
  • 3rd Degree

  • Nick Knight
