BodogFight

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

BodogFight is a reality television series and mixed martial arts competition, originating from Costa Rica, and created and produced by entertainment mogul Calvin Ayre, and his Bodog.com Entertainment Group. The show is aired on Men's Outdoor and Recreation, DISH Network, The Fight Network in Canada, and webcast for free.

On February 5, 2007, ION Television announced an exclusive television alliance with Bodog Entertainment. Beginning February 13th, ION Television started to run original episodes of “BodogFight: St. Petersburg” Tuesdays and Saturdays at 11 p.m. ET/PT; this time slot, generally used for infomercials and other paid programming, has been paid for by Bodog. The series will lead up to a special event in Russia later this spring, featuring some of the biggest names in the world of mixed martial arts. [1]

Seven professional MMA fighters are situated in Costa Rica and fight to earn the right to represent the United States as part of "Team USA". In a later pay-per-view event, these victors will square off against members of the Russian Red Devils team.

The Mixed Fighting Championship also helped to produce BodogFight.

BodogFight's signature catch phrase is "Nobody getting carried away".

[edit] Results

Spoiler warning: Plot and/or ending details follow.

Episode 1: The Welterweights

  • Jorge Masvidal vs. Keith Wisniewski
  • Masvidal wins over Wisniewski in a controversial judges' decision. Though Keith dominated the 1st and 2nd rounds, Jorge still won out with a narrow points-victory: 87 over 84.

Episode 2: The Light Heavyweights

  • Mike Patt vs. Kaream Ellington
  • Ellington was forced to tap out after Patt applied an armbar, granting Patt a victory by submission.

Episode 3: The Women

  • Tara LaRosa vs. Amanda Buckner
  • LaRosa submitted Buckner by a rear choke in the 3rd round.

Episode 4: The Lightweights

  • Nick Agallar vs. Nardu Debrah
  • Agallar was the winner by referee stoppage after rendering Debrah defenseless via strikes to the head near the end of the 2nd round.

Episode 5: The Middleweights #1

  • Nick Thompson vs. Davion Peterson
  • Thompson choked out Peterson and won the fight by submission.

Episode 6: The Heavyweights

  • Mario Rinaldi vs. Mark Burch
  • Rinaldi defeated Burch via a hard-fought but unanimous judges’ decision.

Episode 7: The Middleweights #2

  • Chael Sonnen vs. Tim Credeur
  • Sonnen dominated Credeur with his ground and pound, winning a TKO victory after a referee stoppage.
