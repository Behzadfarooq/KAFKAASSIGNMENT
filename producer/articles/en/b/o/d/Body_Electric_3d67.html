Body Electric may refer to these artworks, all named after I Sing the Body Electric (Whitman), an 1855 poem from Leaves of Grass by the American poet Walt Whitman:

  • Body Electric (album), a 1999 album by Steve Roach and Vir Unis.
  • The Body Electric, a 1998 book by Robert Becker and Gary Selden.
  • "Body Electric", a 1982 song by The Sisters of Mercy.
  • "The Body Electric", a 1984 song by Rush off Grace Under Pressure (Rush album).
