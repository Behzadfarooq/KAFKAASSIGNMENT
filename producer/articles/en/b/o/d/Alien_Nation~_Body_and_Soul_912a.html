Alien Nation: Body and Soul

Alien Nation was a television show from the Fox Network that lasted a single season, and ended in 1990 with a cliffhanger series finale. Alien Nation: Dark Horizon was a television movie produced in 1994 that picked up right where that television series left off. Alien Nation: Body and Soul was the second television movie in the series, and was produced in 1995. It follows the television series format of two parallel storylines. The first plot is about a seemingly Human/Tenctonese hybrid child. The sub-plot is the budding relationship between Matt Sikes, and his Tenctonese neighbour Cathy Frankel. The idea of Matt and Cathy having a relationship was an ongoing theme of the Alien Nation television series.

Body and Soul was written by Andrew Schneider, Diane Frolov, Renee & Harry Longstreet, and was directed by Kenneth Johnson.
