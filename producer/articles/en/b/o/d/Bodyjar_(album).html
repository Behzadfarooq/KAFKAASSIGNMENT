Bodyjar was released on September 12, 2005. Bodyjar's self-titled LP is the first for drummer Shane Wakker, and independent label Shock Records following a moderately successful tenure with EMI/Capitol.

The band describe the album "as the result of a long and kinda painful process, one of losing your drummer, record label and leaving your management for no other reason than needing to feel like a real band again."[1]
