Bodelshausen

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Bodelshausen is a town in Baden-Württemberg, Germany.

[edit] Geography

[edit] Geographical Area

Bodelshausen lies south of region Rammert.

[edit] Neighboring counties

Rottenburg am Neckar, Ofterdingen, Mössingen, Hechingen ¹ and Hirrlingen

[edit] History

The oldest historical documents date back to 1100.


[edit] Politics

[edit] Mayor

The mayor is elected for 8 years. Mayor Bernd-Dieter Esslinger's term ends in 2006.

  • 1982 – 2006: Bernd-Dieter Esslinger

[edit] Sister cities

  • Soltvadkert, Hungary (1996)

Bodelshausen maintains friendly relationships with:

  • Hohburg, Saxony
  • Market town Rum, Austria


[edit] Economy and Infrastructure

[edit] Traffic

The Interstate 27 connects the town with Tübingen and Stuttgart, as well as Balingen and Rottweil.
