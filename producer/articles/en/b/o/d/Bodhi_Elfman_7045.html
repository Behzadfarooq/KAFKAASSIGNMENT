Bodhi Elfman (born July 19, 1969 in Los Angeles, California, USA) is an American actor. He is son of artist and art gallery owner Rhonda Saboff and director Richard Elfman and nephew of composer Danny Elfman. In February 1995, he married Golden Globe winning actress Jenna Elfman. He has extensive film and television credits. Bodhi is a Scientologist.
