Bodybuilding at the 2006 Asian Games

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

You have new messages (last change).

Bodybuilding was an event which took place at the 2006 Asian Games in Doha, Qatar on December 8 and 9. The competition included only men's events for eight different weight categories. All events were held at the Al-Dana Banquet Hall.[1]
