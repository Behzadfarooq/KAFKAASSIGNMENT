Bodiam Manor School

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Bodiam Manor School is an Independent Preparatory School for children aged 2 to 13 years old, very close to the East Sussex village of Bodiam. It is co-educational. It is set in 10 acres of land, and is housed in an old Manor House, hence the name.
