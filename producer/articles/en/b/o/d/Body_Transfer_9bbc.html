Body Transfer (肉体転移, Nikutai Ten'i?) is the title of a H anime by the studio Green Bunny and Silky's, consisting of two OVA episodes.

The story revolves around a group of students in the archaeology club on a summer break, when a light ray illuminated from a mysterious instrument struck them, unwillingly forcing them to switch bodies amongst themselves. The trouble begins when they are lured to masturbation and/or sexual intercourse with a male or female, and due to an orgasm, they re-shuffle their bodies with each other again.

The series exploits lesbian acts as well as masturbation and oral sex.
