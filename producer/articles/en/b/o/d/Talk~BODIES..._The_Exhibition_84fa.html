[edit] Body Worlds

If you are interested in this process you may want to look up Body Worlds. Body Worlds are exhibitions put on by IfP (Institute for Plastination). The process was invented and patented by Gunther von Hagens the purpose of his exhibitions is education about and the demystification of the human body.
