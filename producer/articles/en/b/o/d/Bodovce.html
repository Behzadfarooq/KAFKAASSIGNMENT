Bodovce is a village and municipality in Sabinov District in the Prešov Region of north-eastern Slovakia.

[edit] History

In historical records the village was first mentioned in 1427.

[edit] Geography

The municipality lies at an altitude of 450 metres and covers an area of 7.523 km². It has a population of about 325 people.
