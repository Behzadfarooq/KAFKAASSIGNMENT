Boogeymen is a horror compilation video released in 2001. Marketed as "The Killer Compilation," the film consists of seventeen scenes from notable, revolutionary horror titles, along with short screens describing the apropos villain. The films and antagonists featured are:

Blade from The Puppet Master, Candyman from Candyman, Camilla from The Guardian, Dr. Alan Feinstone, D.D.S. from The Dentist, Chucky from Child's Play, Fisherman from I Know What You Did Last Summer, Freddy Krueger from A Nightmare on Elm Street, Ghostface from Scream, Jason Voorhees from Friday the 13th, Leatherface from The Texas Chain Saw Massacre, Leprechaun from Leprechaun, Michael Myers from Halloween, Norman Bates from Psycho, Pinhead from Clive Barker's Hellraiser, Simon from The Ugly, The Tall Man from Phantasm, Djinn from Wishmaster
