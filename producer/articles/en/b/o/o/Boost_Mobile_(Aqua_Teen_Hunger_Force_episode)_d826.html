"Boost Mobile" is the second episode of Aqua Teen Hunger Force's fourth season, which originally aired on Cartoon Network on December 11, 2005. It was written by Matt Maiellaro and Dave Willis, who also voices Meatwad and Carl in the same episode. Rapper Killer Mike (under his birth name) guest-starred, providing the voice of the Boost Mobile Phone.

In the episode, Master Shake becomes the new spokesperson for real-life cell phone company Boost Mobile and attempts to advertise Boost Mobile phones to Frylock, Carl, and the audience, much to the annoyance of the former two.

[edit] Synopsis

Spoiler warning: Plot and/or ending details follow.

Frylock is shocked to find that their power bill is $2,600. As he approaches Master Shake (who is decked out in "bling-blong") on the matter, Shake plugs in a large anthropomorphic Boost Mobile phone and breaks the fourth wall by blatantly advertising the Boost Mobile phone's features to both Frylock and the audience.[1][2] After Frylock locks the Boost Mobile Phone outside, he expresses his discontent with what Shake has apparently done. The Boost Mobile Phone defenestrates itself into the Aqua Teen residence with a call from Shake's boss Josh, who threatens to remove Shake's testicles if he doesn't shape up.[3]

Frylock and Meatwad leave Master Shake and the Boost Mobile Phone to dance while they go to visit "Jeffey", who is really an inanimate garden hose. Just as Carl steps out of his house to see Frylock talking to a garden hose, Master Shake and the Boost Mobile Phone arrive and attempt to advertise Boost Mobile to Carl. However, their tactics prove ineffective, and Carl basically tells both of them to stay off his lawn.[4] Meatwad and Frylock decide to go to a barn to learn how to milk a cow. Master Shake suggests more urban activities, like going to a Ludacris concert or watching Sanford and Son, but ultimately ends up joining them in case if they need to make or receive a cellular call.

Rather than going to a barn to milk a cow, Frylock decides to kill the Boost Mobile Phone by moving it far away from any possible signal. Their journey eventually leads them to the Earth's outer core,[5] where the Boost Mobile Phone finally deactivates and bursts into flames due to poor cellular coverage. When Shake promises Frylock anything money can buy, it is then that Frylock accuses Shake of whoring the Aqua Teens to a cell phone company. They then decide to go back home before they all combust under the conditions of the Earth's core.

The Aqua Teens return home only to discover two Boost Mobile Phones inside their residence. Frylock then lures the Boost Mobile phones outside with stereotypical hip hop slang,[6] where the phones fall into a hole and are crushed by a boulder dropped from the roof. As the Aqua Teens go inside to get some sleep, the phone rings. Shake calls Josh and apologizes for what just happened as Frylock and Meatwad leave the house. The call ends with Master Shake hanging up on Josh and saying the word "crap".[7] All of a sudden, 25 angry Boost Mobile Phones transport themselves to Shake's doorstep and lynch him on the spot. An Axe Body Spray walks onto the scene looking for Shake, but doesn't need anything as he is in the credits.

Spoilers end here.

[edit] Notes

  • When riding down the highway in the Danger Cart, they pass a sign saying the highway is adopted by "Click-Click-Click-Click-Click-Click-Click", which was named by the Brownie Monsters in "The Last One".
  • The end credits places AXE body spray cans in various places.
