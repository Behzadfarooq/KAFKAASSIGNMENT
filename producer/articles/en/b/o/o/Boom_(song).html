"Boom" is a song by American pop singer Anastacia, only released in Europe and also in Asia. It was the Official FIFA World Cup song of 2002. The song is featured on the Collector's Edition of her Freak of Nature album and the soundtrack to 2002 FIFA World Cup. It was written by Anastacia and Glen Ballard, who also produced the song.

[edit] Music video

Anastacia in "Boom"

Directed by Marcos Siega, the music video for "Boom" was shot in London in 2002. It was also included on her first DVD The Video Collection, containing all the music video till "You'll Never Be Alone".

In the beginning of the video, a car is driving along and the radio plays "One Day In Your Life" in the background. Suddenly the car stops and you see a light-flash in the sky. Then the song starts and Anastacia is performing it on a small stage in front of a crowd. During the video, several people get beamed up to this party, some of them being soccer players.
