BOOM! Studios is a new independent comic book company. It was founded in June 2005 with Zombie Tales #1. The company was named Wizard Magazine's Best New Publisher of 2005.

[edit] Company History

BOOM! Studios was founded in 2005 by Ross Richie, who once worked at Malibu Comics and published titles for Atomeka Press. The company specializes in high profile projects from some of the industry's biggest names. The company focuses on various genres, such as the humor of Hero Squared, Planetary Brigade and What Were They Thinking, horror with Zombie Tales, Jenny Finn, and Giant Monster, and thrillers with 10.

BOOM! Studios received favorable attention from critics, earning 17 positive reviews from 17 websites for Zombie Tales. Since Zombie Tales, Boom! has managed to increase its comic titles to a large number for a fledgling company.

[edit] Publication

BOOM! Studios' books are often published in a prestige format, with no ads until the end of each issue. Many of their issues are deluxe format, with many short stories by several writers and artists. Their ads are from upcoming and existing comic books from the company only. Originally, the company shipped two books a month, and began branching out.

[edit] Writers

The following writers, frequently associated with Marvel Comics and/or DC Comics, and even television and Hollywood films, have written titles for BOOM! and some continue to do so.

  • Keith Giffen
  • J. M. DeMatteis
  • Mike Mignola
  • Steve Niles
  • Mark Waid
  • John Rogers
  • Andrew Cosby
  • Michael Alan Nelson
  • Johanna Stokes

[edit] Titles

  • 10 (Ten people are each given a gun and 10 bullets... and forced to hunt each other down)
  • Black Plague (The mob goes to war with a supervillain empire, while another criminal wages war on both)
  • Cthulhu Tales (Short Lovecraftian horror stories)
  • Damnation Crusade (Warhammer 40,000, by writers Dan Abnett and Ian Edginton)
  • Fear the Dead (Zombie Survival Handbook)
  • Forge of War (Warhammer, by writers Dan Abnett and Ian Edginton)
  • Fused Tales (A man becomes fused in a robotic suit)
  • Giant Monster (An astronaut arrives back on earth, turned into a giant monster)
  • GI Spy (World War II based spy comic)
  • Hero (By Yoshitaka Amano, his classic tale, translated into English at last)
  • Hero Squared (A real superhero from one dimension ends up in another.. where he didn't get superpowers and is a slacker)
  • In The Blood (Werewolf horror)
  • Jenny Finn (The rest of Mike Mignola's story)
  • Jeremiah Harm (A criminal is releasead to track down more criminals)
  • John Doe (A man wants to find the identity of an unidentified grave)
  • Planetary Brigade (Captain Valor, from Hero Squared, and his team's adventures before he left his dimension)
  • Savage Brothers (Post-apocalyptic tale)
  • Stardust Kid (formerly published by Image Comics)
  • Talent (A plane crashes, with one survivor... who inherits the memories of the others)
  • Tag (A man falls victim to a plague that is eating his body)
  • Trencher (Keith Giffen's Image comic is published as a trade by Boom!)
  • War of the Worlds: Second Wave (The Martians return to Earth)
  • What Were They Thinking (Comic books under public domain are parodied)
  • X Isle (Researchers land on a mysterious island full of beasts)
  • Zombie Tales (Short story anthology featuring zombies)
