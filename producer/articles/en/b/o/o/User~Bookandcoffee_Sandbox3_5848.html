Please do not alter it unless you are certain to understand its setup and parser functions and are ready to repair/revert all collateral damage if results are unexpected.
Any experiments should be conducted and all improvements tested in the template sandbox, or in your user space, before applying anything here.

The {{Infobox Union Country}} infobox template should be added (not subst:ed) to each of the Trade unions in country articles. There are a number of parameters, most of which are independent of each other. The full syntax is as follows:

{{Infobox Union Country
|national  = 
|government =
|legislation = 
|membership = 
|percentage =
|demographics =
|ILOmember =
|ILO-87date =
|ILO-98date =
}}

Parameters:

  • national - The article name(s) of the national trade union centre(s) in the country.
  • government - The article name(s) of related national government agency(ies).
  • legislation - The article name(s) of the primary trade union legislation.
  • membership - Preferably a single national number, and reference.
  • percentage - May contain a simple breakdown of membership ratios. (eg. 62% of formal workforce) or (35% of government workforce <br> 19% of non-government workforce).
  • demographics - May contain a simple breakdown of demographic ratios. (eg. 56% of women in workforce <br> 62% of men in workforce).
  • ILOmember - Any text in this field results in "Member of the ILO". Leaving the field empty will result in the text "Not a member of the ILO".
  • ILO-87date - The date Freedom of Association and Protection of the Right to Organise Convention, 1948 was ratified. Please add the date, it is available at the act page - leaving the field empty will result in the text "Has not ratified Convention #87".
  • ILO-98date - The date Right to Organise and Collective Bargaining Convention, 1949 was ratified. Please add the date, it is available at the act page - leaving the field empty will result in the text "Has not ratified Convention #98".
