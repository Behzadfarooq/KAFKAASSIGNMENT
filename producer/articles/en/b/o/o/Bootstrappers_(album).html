Bootstrappers is the eponymous, and only, release by the Bootstrappers, a one-time ensemble of avant-garde guitarist Elliott Sharp and the Reactionaries/Minutemen/fIREHOSE rhythm section of bassist Mike Watt and drummer George Hurley. The project recorded and released one full-length CD of improvised music, which was released on Watt's New Alliance Records label.
