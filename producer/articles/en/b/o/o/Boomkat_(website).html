Boomkat is an online music retailer based in Manchester specializing in independent music. Operating since 1998, the website serves as the online extension of Pelicanneck Records in Manchester.

[edit] Overview

Boomkat’s online store covers a wide variety of musical genres, including indie-rock, hip-hop, ambient, pop, folk-rock and IDM to name a few. Various formats are usually carried in stock, including (obviously) compact discs and vinyl for recordings, DVDs and any other artist merchandise. While Boomkat is a retailer, careful attention is given to their album reviews. While the reviews seem to steer clear of criticisms, they creatively convey what can be expected of the work, and make it easy to pick out the most impressive new albums with familiar and terse closing statements such as “Huge recommendation!” or “Absolutely essential.” These careful reviews, which primarily attempt to describe rather than critique, seem to allow Boomkat to cover a wide variety of genres fairly with ease.

The quote below comes from Boomkat’s review of Colleen’s Everyone Alive Wants Answers, an eclectic, ambient acoustic album released on Leaf Records:

“Everyone Alive Wants Answers” is the haunting work of 26-year-old Parisienne Cecile Schott. Her debut album release, she has previously released a gem of a 7" single (Babies) on Active Suspension, which brought her to the attention of The Leaf Label. An effortlessly charming album, naive instrumentals filled with warmth, melody and soul, played on a broken music box, a glockenspiel or a guitar. The recordings seem pieced together from an array of field recordings and home tapes, melodies and aroma’s slowly infused to create a homespun exercise in delicacy, beauty and a joyously moving appeal to nostalgic sensibilities and abandon. Gorgeous stuff, highly recommended.

[edit] Front Page

Every Thursday, Boomkat’s front page is updated with the week’s new shipment of merchandise. Each update is highlighted by a selection of the best releases of the new arrivals, their Albums of the Week and Singles of the Week. These selections are listed at the top of the inventory, with their entire reviews displayed.

[edit] Jukebox

A particularly attractive feature of Boomkat’s website is their preview jukebox. This player extends beyond the functionality usually available with other similar functions found with larger retailers. First, it doesn’t require a separate media player, only a recent version of Macromedia’s Flash player integrated with the listener’s browser. Additionally, the player includes a playlist and search function. Together, these functions integrated into the jukebox allow for quick simple access to every preview available on the site.

[edit] Other Features

Boomkat’s featured items sales drives focus on specific labels, artists or some other concentration, and make it easier to navigate the variety available. Finally, at the end of every year, Boomkat’s staff compiles a list of their top 50 favorite album releases. Additionally, a number of artists are polled for their lists.

In 2006 Boomkat launched a digital download store selling tracks and albums in MP3 format without DRM.
