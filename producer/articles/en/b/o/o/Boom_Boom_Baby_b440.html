Boom Boom Baby is a cd by Billy "Crash" Craddock. The songs were released in the late 1950s and early 1960s. The cd was released in 1992 on Bear Family Records. The only hit song in the United States was Don't Destroy Me. The song made it to #94 on the pop charts in November 1959. A few of the songs were hits in Australia. The songs LuLu Lee and Ah, Poor Little Baby were released on Date Records in 1958. The other tracks were released on Columbia Records in 1959-1961.
