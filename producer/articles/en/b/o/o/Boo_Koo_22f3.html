Boo Koo

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Boo Koo is an energy supplement with a lot of caffiene, similar to other energy drinks. Introduced in 2004, it was marketed with the slogan "Boo Koo Means Big" (due to the 24 fl oz can size). It is the first energy drink to be so large. "Boo Koo" is a rough anglicization of the French word "beaucoup".
