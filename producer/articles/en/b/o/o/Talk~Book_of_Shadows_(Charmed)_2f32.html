[edit] Gigantic Huge Spell List

Previous Discussion from Talk:Charmed#Book_of_Shadows_.28Charmed.29:

This morning a link to this page was added into the template, bringing this page that I hadn't noticed before to my attention. Taking a look at it, it's basically a horribly formatted version of the recently deleted "List of Charmed spells" page. It has a bit of extra information (episode names), but aside from that is a major pain on the eyes to read. I see two options for how we can deal with this:

  1. Remove all the spell information from the page, leaving just a basic description of what the BoS is.
  2. Cleanup and reformat the whole page to look pretty and be more useful than either this page, or the old List of Spells page was.

Basically, the second route is to make this page into what some of us wanted the List of Spells page to become, had it survived the AfD. Either way we go, we'll need to move on it ASAP, or this page will also get AfD'd with the state it's in now. The only mystery is how it's survived 8 months like that... Thoughts? --Maelwys 15:10, 13 April 2006 (UTC)

I think there may be a third option. The individual episode pages contain a "Book of Shadows" section, which includes a list of the spells used in that episode. The list of spells from this page could be split up and moved to the relevant episode pages, leaving this page for a general Book of Shadows description. Just throwing another idea out there. MiraLuka 22:11, 10 May 2006 (UTC)
I'd suggest removing all the lists of spells - not a collection of source material. Ziggurat 21:56, 14 May 2006 (UTC)
Individual spells: No.
Information on what the BoS is, its history, basic content & structure, differences from real life BoSes and why this all is relevant in the first place: yes.
For starters, the spells that are in the individual episode articles can be hastily removed. AdamDobay 22:56, 14 May 2006 (UTC)
I wholeheartedly agree! I have removed the wording of the various spells; context-based additions would be much more valuable. Ziggurat 23:04, 14 May 2006 (UTC)
