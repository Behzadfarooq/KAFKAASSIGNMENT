Boone High School

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

This article is about a high school in Iowa. For the high school in Florida, see William R. Boone High School.

Boone High School is a high school located in Boone, Iowa. The school serves 888 students and a faculty consisting of 90 people. The school's mascot is the Toreador.
