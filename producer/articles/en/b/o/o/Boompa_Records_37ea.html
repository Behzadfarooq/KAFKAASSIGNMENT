Boompa Records is a Canadian independent record label, established in 2003 in Vancouver, British Columbia.

Artists who have released material on Boompa include Matt Sharp (weezer, The Rentals), Leeroy Stagger, Small Sins, The Salteens, Run Chico Run, my project: blue, Hylozoists, Lullaby Baxter, Catlow, Sekiden and The Lucksmiths.

Boompa releases are distributed in Canada by EMI, in the United States by Caroline Records, in the United Kingdom by Proper Distribution, and various independent partners around the globe.
