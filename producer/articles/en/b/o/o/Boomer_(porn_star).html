Boomer (porn star)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Spencer Boomer or Boomer, also known as The Boomer or Stoolboom, was an American porn star in the late 1980s and 1990s. He appeared in a series of films by "First Class Male", namely Puppy Tales, Puppy Tales II, and Puppy Wags. His first and only appearance outside of this studio was in the Catalina Studios film, Lifeguard on Duty with Ted Cox and Matt Powers.

Although he had a long torso, he had short legs and was not well-endowed. He made up for these short comings with his outgoing personality and superior acting skills. The Boomer had all of the characteristics to become a very popular star. Lifeguard on Duty was an attempt to create a porn film with somewhat of a feasible plot line. It was also significant step from previous tawdry films in which Boomer appeared. But perhaps his most innovative film was Offenzee Pantz, SS!!!. This film, a first of its kind, featured not only gay but bi-sexual activity as well. Set in a World War II prison camp where the male prisoners cavorted among themselves as well as with the female "Nazi" guards, it featured realistic sets and a carefully written script.

With a little more coaching and experience, Boomer might have been a very successful actor. Although never famous, he did receive a certain controversial notoriety for his appearance in a gay "space" film as Dick Scobee, also the name of the astronaut who had died in the space shuttle explosion a year earlier Dick Scobee. The Boomer received the Nob Hill Theater's "CAF Award" for best supporting actor for his work in In the Middle and on Top where he appeared with the veteran actor, Dick Swan.

Spencer Boomer has spent most of his life in Texas. He currently works in the financial field.
