An Australian version of Boomerang was launched in March 2001 as part of the Foxtel Digital launch, with a lineup very similar to that of the UK version. It started previously as a 4-hour block of those T.V. shows when CN introduced 24 hour programming. Recently added are some Cartoon Cartoons such as Mike, Lu and Og and Sheep in The Big City. Every Saturday each month they air "Big Bucket" from 3pm to 6pm showcasing 3 hours of one of your favourite Boomerang shows, this month its The Smurfs.
