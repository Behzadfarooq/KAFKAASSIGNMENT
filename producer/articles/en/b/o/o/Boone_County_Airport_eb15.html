Boone County Airport (IATA: HRO, ICAO: KHRO), also known as Boone County Regional Airport, is a public airport located three miles (5 km) northwest of the central business district (CBD) of Harrison, a city in Boone County, Arkansas, USA. It serves the surrounding areas including Branson, Missouri. The airport covers 425 acres and has one runway. It is mostly used for general aviation but is also served by one commercial airline. Service is subsidized by the Essential Air Service program.
