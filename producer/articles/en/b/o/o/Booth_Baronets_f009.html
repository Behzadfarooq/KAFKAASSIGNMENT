Booth Baronets

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

You have new messages (last change).

There have been three distinct Booth baronetcies that share nothing but a surname. The first was created in the Baronetage of England, the last two in the Baronetage of the United Kingdom. The first creation is unusual in that it lacks a territorial qualifier.

[edit] Booth (1611)

  • see Baron Delamer

[edit] Booth of Portland Place (1835)

  • Sir Felix Booth, 1st Baronet (d. 1850)
  • Sir Williamson Booth, 2nd Baronet (1811-1877)
  • Sir Charles Booth, 3rd Baronet (1812-1896) (extinct on his death)

[edit] Booth of Allerton Beeches (1916)

  • Sir Alfred Allen Booth, 1st Baronet (1872-1948)
  • Sir Philip Booth, 2nd Baronet (1907-1960)
  • Sir Douglas Allen Booth, 3rd Baronet (b. 1949)
