"Boogie Woogie" is a pop – dance song written by Australian singer-songwriter Dannii Minogue and Dee Wright for EuroGroove's greatest hits album The Best Of (1995). The song features guest vocals by Minogue and was produced by Tetsuya Komuro. It was released as a Japanese–only single in June 1995 and reached number one on the Japanese singles chart.
