[edit] Stories

my brother found this tape at a sidewalk sale in brooklyn in 1993 for $2, and it turned out to be really good. that was the vast underneath. she put out another good one in 1995 and then seemed to vanish. there's never been a website about her, and i'd always meant to put one up someday. wikipedia just makes it so easy to do. it's not particularly stylish or anything, but that's alright. and it's interesting how you can create these instant links to the people she worked with, and find out that one of the drummers was in soul coughing, etc. where is she now though? for that matter, where has she been for the last 10 years? she's too good to be so obscure... ffangs 09:39, 15 October 2005 (UTC)ffangs

in answer to my own question, she's working on a new cd! i contacted Joe McGinty, who pointed out her website to me (which hadn't come up in a google search using quotes around her name). much happiness... ffangs 22:24, 16 October 2005 (UTC)ffangs
