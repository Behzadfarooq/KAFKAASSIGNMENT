Bookcert is a British training and software development company, which specialises in practical business training for bookkeepers. The name Bookcert is derived from the words bookkeeping and success [1]. Bookcert's headquarters are in Bristol, England.

[edit] History

Bookcert Limited was founded in 2006 [2] based on an apparent market opportunity (a niche in the bookkeeping training industry) for practical business training for new and existing bookkeepers. Bookcert produces training material and software which is primarily used in the private sector. The company is split into three distinct trading divisions: Bookcert Training, The Book-keepers Network and The Book-keepers Forum.

[edit] Competition

Bookcert faces stiff competition from other professional education and financial training companies within the UK, such as BPP Professional Education and FTC Kaplan.
