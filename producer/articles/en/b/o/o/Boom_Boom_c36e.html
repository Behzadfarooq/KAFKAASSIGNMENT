Boom Boom

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Boom Boom may refer to:

  • Bernie "Boom Boom" Geoffrion, a member of the Hockey Hall of Fame
  • Boom Boom (song), by John Lee Hooker
  • Boom Boom (Super Mario Bros. 3), a Boss monster in the video game Super Mario Bros. 3
  • Boom Boom My Heart, a W.A.S.P. song

  • The former alias of the Marvel comic character Meltdown (comics)
  • Name of a nasal inhalent product sold by Booming Businesses, LLC
  • Boom Boom (Cham song), by Jamaican artist, Cham
  • Boom Boom (Mabel song), by Danish artist Mabel
