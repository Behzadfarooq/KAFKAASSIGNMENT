[edit] Welcome

Hello, Booking563, and welcome to Wikipedia. Thank you for your contributions. I hope you like the place and decide to stay. If you are stuck, and looking for help, please come to the Newcomers help page, where experienced Wikipedians can answer any queries you have! Or, you can just type {{helpme}} on your user page, and someone will show up shortly to answer your questions. Here are a few good links for newcomers:

  • The Five Pillars of Wikipedia
  • How to edit a page
  • Editing tutorial
  • Picture tutorial
  • How to write a great article
  • Naming conventions
  • Manual of Style

I hope you enjoy editing here and being a Wikipedian! By the way, you can sign your name on Talk and vote pages using three tildes, like this: ~~~. Four tildes (~~~~) produces your name and the current date. If you have any questions, see the help pages, add a question to the village pump or ask me on my talk page. Again, welcome! --DV8 2XL 00:20, 20 March 2006 (UTC)

[edit] Recent article creations

Several of your recent articles have been nominated for deletion, and at least one deleted. The reason is that you are often creating very small stub articles along the line sof "a soap dispenser is a device that dispenses soap" (which qualifies as blindingly obvious). It is an interesting side-effect of the deletion debate process that some people have picked up on these articles and expanded them to real articles: that is not how it is supposed ot work. Please try to accumulate at least enough material for a couple of decent-sized paragraphs rather than just creating placeholders and relying on someone else to flesh them out. Thanks, Just zis Guy you know? 14:10, 23 March 2006 (UTC)
