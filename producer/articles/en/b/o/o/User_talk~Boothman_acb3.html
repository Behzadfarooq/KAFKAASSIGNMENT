[edit] Subst of templates

Those templates are meant to be subst:, and not left as templates in the article. See Wikipedia:WikiProject Football/Templates#Timesavers for more info. All my edits do is replace {{fc|Arsenal}} with {{subst:fc|Arsenal}} for example. The net result is the same, except the server does not have to load the same template over and over whenever the page is accessed for viewing later on. Regards. Neier 12:50, 7 April 2007 (UTC)
