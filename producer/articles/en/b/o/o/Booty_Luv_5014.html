Booty Luv are a British girl group consisting of a duo, Cherise and Nadia, who originally appeared in the mixed sex R 'n' B group Big Brovaz. Their debut single, "Boogie 2Nite" - a cover of a Tweet song - was released in the United Kingdom via the Hed Kandi label on November 27th 2006. The single received favourable reviews and become a hit with DJs. It peaked at #2 in its third week in the UK Singles Chart and spent 8 weeks in the top 10. It reached #9 in the Dutch Top 40.

[edit] Big Brovaz

The girls began their career in the R 'n' B group 'Big Brovaz' who had a considerable success following their launch in 2002. Big Brovaz hope to re-launch in 2007 with their new album "Re-Entry" and single "Big Bro Thang".
