Bootsy's Rubber Band

Bootsy's Rubber Band was a P-Funk spinoff, led by pioneering bassist Bootsy Collins. It also included his brother, Catfish Collins, Kash Waddy, Joel Johnson, Robert Johnson, Mudbone Cooper and The Horny Horns. The group was formed in 1976.
