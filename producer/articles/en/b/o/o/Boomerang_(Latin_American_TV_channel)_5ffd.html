Boomerang Latin America is the local version of Boomerang, which started in July 2001, carrying the same "classic aniamtion" format as its US sister network. In April 3, 2006, Boomerang Latin America was relaunched as a general children's network, becoming a version of Cartoon Network's sister network in India, POGO, featuring live-action programming, recent cartoons, movies, and retaining its classic cartoons in late night. Programming is "color-coded" as it follows:

  • Mini TV (preschool programming): yellow
  • Live-Action / Animation: red
  • Boomerang Movie (weekday movies): blue
    • Lights, Camera, Boomerang (weekend movies): blue/green
    • Sunday Matinee: red-orange
  • Classic Boomerang: green
