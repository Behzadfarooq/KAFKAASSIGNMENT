[edit] Locations

  • Booker, Buckinghamshire is a hamlet in the United Kingdom
  • Booker, Texas is a town in Texas, USA.

[edit] Occupations

  • Booker (music) is a person in the music industry who helps plan stages of musical tours.
  • Booker (professional wrestling) is a person in professional wrestling who sets up matches and writes storylines.

[edit] Events

  • Booker-McConnell plc was the company which was the original title sponsor of what became one of the world's most prestigious literary awards, the Booker Prize, known since 2002 as the Man Booker Prize.

[edit] People

  • Booker T is the name of several people
  • Chris Booker is a U.S. radio/TV personality
  • Cory Booker is a New Jersey Democratic politician and the current mayor of Newark
  • James Booker was a U.S. piano player and singer
