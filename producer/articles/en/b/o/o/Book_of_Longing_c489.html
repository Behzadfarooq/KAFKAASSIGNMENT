Book of Longing

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Book of Longing is a book by Leonard Cohen. First published in 2006 by McClelland and Stewart, it contains 167 previously unpublished poems and drawings, mostly written at a Zen monastery on Mount Baldy in California, where Cohen lived from 1994 to 1999, and in India, which he visited regularly during the late 1990s. The book also incorporates number of poems written after his 1976 book Death of a Lady's Man. These he left out of his 1984 Book of Mercy, which contained only psalms. Book of Longing also collects most of the lyrics to songs from the albums Ten New Songs (2001) and Dear Heather (2004). Many of these poems were first published at The Blackening Pages of The Leonard Cohen Files [1] website.
