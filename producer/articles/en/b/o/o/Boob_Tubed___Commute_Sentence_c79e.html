Boob Tubed/Commuted Sentence was the fifth episode of the second season of the Animated Television series Rocko's Modern Life.

[edit] Boob Tubed

Rocko tries to buy a modestly-priced television set, but a computer error leaves him with a gigantic entertainment system. Him, Heffer, and Filburt rent a whole bunch of videos but when they finish them, Heff continues to watch cable. He sits too close to the screen and gets his brain sucked out. Rocko and Filburt wake up to discover him in a trance-like gibberish-uttering state. The two go inside the TV to get Heff's brain back. They traverse through a powerful blizzard to TV Central where they successfully find Heff's brain among a giant pile of brains of other victims. Meanwhile, Heff strips naked and thinks he's a chicken. Rocko and Fil return and save Heff. Rocko throws away the huge TV and uses Filburt in a cardboard box as his new set.

[edit] Commuted Sentence

Rocko gets stuck in a traffic jam on his way to work. Then he fails at finding a proper parking spot, making him late much to the ire and wire-thin patience of his boss Mr. Smitty. Smitty admonishes Rocko on the importance of being on time and how it affects pay. A few days later, he parks his car in a decent spot, but Heff comes along and accidentally spills ketchup on the sidewalk, making it look like Rocko parked his car in a forbidden zone. His car is towed away. Rocko is forced to get to work via public transportation. When dangerous taxi, subway, and bus rides don't work, Rocko tries riding his bicycle, but gets mauled by Earl. An uncaring and furious Mr. Smitty scolds Rocko once again for his tardiness and tells him that if he's late one more time, he's fired. Heff comes up with the idea to shoot Rocko out of a cannon, but his aim causes Rocko to crash into the Museum of Sharp Pointy Things. Rocko tries to run the rest of the way to the comic shop, but is declared late and is fired on the spot. A police officer offers a forlorn Rocko a towing job and makes enough money to get his car back. He gets the last laugh when he tows away his boss' car as well as everyone elses.

[edit] Trivia

  • One of the many videos they bought for "Boob Tubed" said They Might Be Giants, which is also the name of a band.
  • While Heffer was channel surfing he went past a channel playing the theme song of Rocko's Modern Life.
