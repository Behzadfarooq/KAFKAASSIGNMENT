The Book of Darkness, also known as the Yami no Shō and Buch der Dunkelheit, is a fictional item from the series Magical Girl Lyrical Nanoha A's. It is a Lost Logia that has been around for centuries. As a Belkan device, it speaks German. The Wolkenritter, Vita, Signum, Shamal and Zafira, seek to complete its 666 pages to save their mistress, but Nanoha and Fate oppose their absorbing innocents to do so.

Spoiler warning: Plot and/or ending details follow.

It turns out that the Book has been completed once every few decades, killed its master rather than reviving him or her, and wiped the minds of the Wolkenritter of what it has done. However, it was originally a benevolent book called the yaten no shou/yaten no madosho, but was corrupted by its own automatic defence program. In the 5-episode climax, the Book is completed by the two "masked men" with the Wolkenritter's own Linker Cores and fused with its mistress Hayate, becoming a frightening, vengeful woman who exists only to wreak destruction on the world and then kill Hayate. The Book almost destroys Uminari City, absorbs Fate into "a dream from which she will never want to wake," and corners Nanoha when Hayate finally takes control of her body again.
