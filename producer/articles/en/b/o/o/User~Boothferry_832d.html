[edit] Waffen-SS British Free Corps Impression

Equipment Description Comments

"M36" Waffen-SS Tunic
"M40" Helmet
Erel Officer's Schirmutze
Officer's Gloves
Unterstürmführer British Free Corps Insignia
(Breeches And Field/Personal Equipment Not Pictured).

 I'll start by saying all of the items I use are reproduction apart from my Gloves. (I don't want to be wearing out relics!) All the insignia has been sewn on by my own hands and various modifications have been made to the Tunic even though it was tailored to me. It took me about 6 months to pick up everything I needed for a true impression (It's hard to find authentic looking reproduction insignia). I'll be seen wearing this very frequently, Infact you'll be unlucky if you catch me without it on.
