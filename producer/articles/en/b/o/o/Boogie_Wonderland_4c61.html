"Boogie Wonderland" is a 1979 hit disco single for Earth, Wind and Fire with the Emotions, written by Allee Willis, taken from the album I AM. It is regarded as a classic of the disco age.

The repeated line from the chorus sums up the song: "I find romance when I start to dance in Boogie Wonderland".

The song won the 1980 Grammy Award for Best R&B Instrumental Performance.

The song has been remade several times. Most recently, it was sung by Brittany Murphy for the soundtrack to the movie Happy Feet.
