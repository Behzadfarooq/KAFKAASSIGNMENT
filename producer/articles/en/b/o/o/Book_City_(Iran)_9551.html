Bookcity is a state chain store for books and other cultural materials spread throughout the city of Tehran. It is one of the distributors of English and Latin books in Iran, ranging from just-published fiction to text from Palgrave. In the country, it is perhaps the main source for Jazz and classical music from publishers such as EMI, ECM, Harmonia Mundi and others.
