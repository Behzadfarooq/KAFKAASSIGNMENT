Boonesboro, Howard County, Missouri, USA is located on Route 87 midway between Boonville and Glasgow in the historical Boone's Lick country. The community was founded in 1840 and is named for the family of frontiersman Daniel Boone. Boone's Lick State Historic Site is nearby. Noteworthy establishments include the Christian Church and the Boonville Country Store.
