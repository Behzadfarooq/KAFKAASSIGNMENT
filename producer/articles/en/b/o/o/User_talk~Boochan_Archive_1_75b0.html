[edit] Reverting vandalism

Thank you for helping to restore the UPN article to its rightful state. For future reference, you needn't manually seek out and erase each individual edit that the vandal makes. Simply click on the "history" tab, click on the link for the page revision immediately prior to the vandal's edits, click on the "edit this page" tab, and click "Save page." The vandal's changes will then be undone. —David Levy 22:05, 7 February 2007 (UTC)
