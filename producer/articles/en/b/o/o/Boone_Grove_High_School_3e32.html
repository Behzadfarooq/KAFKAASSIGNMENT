[edit] Overview

Boone Grove High School includes grades 9-12. In 1913, the first 4 year commissioned high school was built in Boone Grove. The current Boone Grove High School opened in 1995 and is located at 260 S 500 W in Valparaiso. The current principal is Garry DeRossett, who has been with the school for five years. The school's colors include royal blue and white. The Wolves of BGHS participate in the Porter County Conference (PCC).

[edit] School statements

[edit] Mission statement

The mission of Boone Grove High School is to foster the development of productive, responsible members of society through an educational partnership between school and staff and parents. The partnership will utilize critical thinking skills and problem-solving strategies across a broad-based curriculum which considers all aspects of each child's growth.

[edit] Belief statement

We are dedicated to offering integrated educational programs.
Our students will be offered opportunites to become respectful, responsible, productive members of a changing global society.
Learning is a life-long process involving problem solving.
Valuable service-oriented experiences will enhance students' academic learning.
Effective communication between administation, faculty, staff, students, families and community will facilitate student achievement.
Student success is our goal.

[edit] Academic schedule

Boone Grove High School employs 32 teachers. The faculty averages 16 years of experience. Over half of the teaching staff hold masters or doctoral degrees. Boone Grove has eleven different academic departments, they are as follows: Science, Social Studies, English, Math, Art, Life Skills, Business, Music, Industrial Technology, Physical Education, and Foreign Language. Boone Grove High School strives to meet the Indiana Academic Standards when planning out curriculum for students. Text books are chosen based on these standards. The school operates on 9 week grading periods. Courses are offered to help students achive Core 40 and the Academic Honors Diploma.
