Booth's Gin

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Booth's Gin is a famous brand of London dry gin, founded by the Booth family around 1740 and, as of March 2006, still produced today. The distinctive bottle has a reference to the Red Lion distillery where the drink was produced.

The brand is currently owned by Diageo Spirits.
