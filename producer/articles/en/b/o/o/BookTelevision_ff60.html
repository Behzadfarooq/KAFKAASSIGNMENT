BookTelevision is a Canadian category 1 digital cable specialty channel with programming relating to books and all forms of literature. It is owned by access media group a division of CHUM Limited.

[edit] History

Licensed as BookTelevision - The Channel by the CRTC on November 24, 2000; it launched on September 7, 2001 as BookTelevision: The Channel. Shortly after its launch, "The Channel" was dropped from its name to BookTelevision.
