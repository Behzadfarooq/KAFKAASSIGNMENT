[edit] Please use proper licensing tags

Wikipedia welcomes all constructive contributions to our encyclopedia, but your recent addition appears to be copyrighted text or image. Unfortunately, we cannot accept copyrighted material, for it is legally incompatible with the GNU Free Documentation License, under which Wikipedia is published. Please contribute in your own words with external material as a reference. Thank you.

Please use the proper licensing tag and provide a valid fair-use rationale whenever you upload pictures. For example, when you upload a picture of a promotional material for a tv series, please don't tag it as a "movie poster" or a "DVD cover". Wikipedia has to deal with a lot of legal issues whenever editors do that. Also, by the US/UK Fair-use/Fair-dealing laws (and Philippine copyright law as well, which applies to your additions) you must provide a fair use rationale whenever you upload a picture with a fair-use claim. Lastly, please specify the owner of the copyright of the material you upload and specify the source (please be very specific) of the image.
