Boomerang Interviú Futbol Sala is a futsal club based in Alcalá de Henares, a city in the autonomous community of Community of Madrid.

The club was founded in 1977 and her stadium is the Pabellón Caja Madrid with capacity of 4,500 seated spectators.

The club's main sponsors are El Corte Inglés and Grupo Zeta. Other sponsors include Telefónica, Movistar and Adecco.

[edit] Club Honours

  • 16 Division de Honor: 1979-80, 1980-81, 1981-82, 1983-84, 1984-85, 1985-86, 1986-87,1987-88, 1988-89, 1989-90, 1990-91, 1995-96, 2001-02, 2002-03, 2003-04, 2004-05
  • 8 Copa: 1983-84, 1985-86, 1987-88, 1989-90, 1995-96, 2000-01, 2003-04, 2004-05
  • 7 Supercopa: 1990-91, 1991-92, 1996-97, 2001-02, 2002-03, 2003-04, 2005-06
  • 2 UEFA Futsal Cup: 2003-04, 2005-06
  • 1 European Champions Cup: 1990-91
  • 2 Intercontinental Cup: 2004-05, 2005-06
  • 2 Iberian Cup: 2004, 2006
