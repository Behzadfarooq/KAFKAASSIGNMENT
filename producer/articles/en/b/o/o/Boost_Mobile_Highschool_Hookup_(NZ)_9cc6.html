Boost Mobile Highschool Hookup (NZ)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Boost Mobile High School Hookup was a music competition held by Boost Mobile in 2006 in New Zealand. The entrants had to compose an original track of any genre that was their own interpretation of the Boost Mobile (NZ) catch phrase Hook It Up. Entrants could enter as many tracks as they wanted. The competition consisted of two sections, one the people's choice award and the other the Judges choice. After the deadline there was a month long period during which the public was able to vote for their favourite track through the competition website and the judges to make their own choice.

[edit] Prizes

[edit] Grand Prize (Judges Pick)

The main prize included a two day recording and mentoring session at the highly respected Kog studios with one of NZ's most respected production teams consisting of Chong Nee, Chris Chetland and Dave Baxter - also known as Handmade Productions. The winners flights, and meals were also included along with accommodation at the prestigious Rendezvous hotel (formerly Carlton) in Auckland. The work recorded at Kog studios was to be pressed by Saatchi and Saatchi and an agreement with Telecom would be reached about the use of the wining song. In addition the winning member(s)' school also received a sound system worth $5000.

[edit] People's Choice

This was for the track with the most votes and the winners receieved a 20" Apple iMac computer with logic and a mixer.


[edit] Winners

The winners of the grand prize where 3 musicians from Dunedin known as Esdee, MC Beau and their producer Mr J with their track "A Concept Track". Chris Chetland described the track as a "clever approach to the theme"; the track was about the competition itself. Esdee's school - Bayfield High School received the school prize. Esdee, Beau and Mr J are currently putting finishing touches to the EP which shall be released as part of the prize in early 2007.

The winners of the People's Choice awards where Blue Era with their upbeat Hook It Up. Due to the large number of high quality entries a "Highly Commended" section was also added. Full Eclipse, Cam With a K and Bubbles and the Squeegy Boys received this.
