Books Kinokuniya (紀伊國屋書店, Kinokuniya Shoten?) is a Japanese bookstore chain operated by Kinokuniya Company Ltd. (株式会社紀伊國屋書店, Kabushiki-gaisha Kinokuniya Shoten?), founded in 1927, with its first store located in Shinjuku, Tokyo.

In Japan, there are 56 stores nationwide.

Since 2000, the Kinokuniya chain in the United States has capitalized on the growing popularity of anime by stocking both English and Japanese-language manga, as well as other anime-related parphenalia. The New York City branch is the best-known, encompassing, lengthwise, an entire city block.

[edit] Overseas stores

Overseas, there are 21 stores in total. They are located in:

  • Singapore
  • United States
    • New York City, Kinokuniya's largest overseas store
    • Los Angeles
    • San Francisco, California
    • San José, California
    • Seattle, Washington
    • Costa Mesa, California
    • Beaverton, Oregon
    • Edgewater, New Jersey
    • West Nyack, New York
  • Indonesia
  • Malaysia

Located on the top floor of the Suria KLCC shopping centre at the foot of the Petronas Twin Towers.

  • Thailand
  • Australia
    • Sydney, New South Wales

Located in The Galeries Victoria shopping centre in the CBD, Books Kinokuniya is Australia's largest Bookstore. Over 300,000 titles in stock specialising in Literature, Art & Design, Graphic Novels, Food, Performing Arts, Business and Children's Books. In 2006 Books Kinokuniya won the American Express Rewards for Excellence 'Best Recreational Retailer' and the Sydney City Business Awards 'Best Stationery, Books, and Music Store'.

  • Taiwan
