Bootie Brown

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Bootie Brown (real name Romye Robinson) is a West Coast rapper who was one of the founding members of the innovative hip hop group The Pharcyde. He has done very few collaborations outside of the group and is currently working with the remaining Pharcyde member Imani Wilcox on new projects with their indie label.

His collaboration with Gorillaz on their album Demon Days, the track "Dirty Harry," was released as a single on November 21, 2005.

he also appeared in the Gorillaz video Dirty Harry.
