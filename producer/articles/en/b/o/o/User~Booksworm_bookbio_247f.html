About me

Hello, I am Jean-Paul , Wikipedian from Switzerland.

I am working on a variety of Wiki projects, but, my most active one, by far, is Wikipedia in English. The other wikis I contribute to are Commons, Wikinews, Wikipédia en français, Wikipedia in het Nederlands, Wikipedia in Italiano, Wiktionary, Meta-Wiki, Wikisource, Wikiquote, Simple English and HRWiki

My main activities here are countering vandalism, making minor edits, alphabetizing lists and, often, blocking WoW sockpuppets whilst looking for new users to greet. I also greet new users. I would like to thank, RexNL and Buchanan-Hermit for the existence of their userpages which allowed mine to be what they are.
