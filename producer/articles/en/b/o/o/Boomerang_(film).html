Boomerang is a 1992 romantic comedy film, starring Eddie Murphy, and directed by Reginald Hudlin.

[edit] Synopsis

Spoiler warning: Plot and/or ending details follow.

Marcus Graham (Eddie Murphy), is a hotshot advertising executive who's also an insatiable womanizer and male chauvinist. He is thus hardly prepared for his new boss Jacqueline, played by Robin Givens. In terms of things romantic, Jacqueline is nothing more or less than a female version of Marcus, and for the first time, Graham is getting the runaround.

[edit] Trivia

  • The film also features a sculpture by acclaimed artist Munson Hunt.
  • The Star Trek episode that Angela and Marcus are watching is Episode 57: Elaan Of Troyius.
  • The hit song End of the Road by Boyz II Men was on the soundtrack to this film.
  • The popular song Hot Sex by A Tribe Called Quest was also featured on the film' s soundtrack.
  • The film stars three players from the popular James Bond film series. Geoffrey Holder played Baron Samedi in Live and Let Die (film), Grace Jones played May Day (James Bond) in A View to a Kill, and Halle Berry would go on to play Giacinta 'Jinx' Johnson in Die Another Day.
