[edit] Thanks for the welcome!

I am a new (or old turned new) contributor to Wikipedia. I used to write or edit back in 2001-2002, but that was a long time ago.

My current interests, as you see, are in Ancient Rome, specifically in the Middle Republic (circa 290 BC - 146 BC, technically, but my interests run a bit beyond that). I prefer to edit on biographies etc. Unfortunately, I no longer have access to a good university library as I did earlier, so have to depend largely on Internet-accessible resources along with the odd library book.

What I would love is feedback (from time to time - once a week, once a fortnight, once a month, whatever suits you) on the edits or new articles I do. In short, am I following Wikipedia's guidelines on NPOV etc, what do I need to change, etc? I know that some of my articles need more references, so am working on that.

If you choose to adopt me, I would be very grateful. [Should I remove that template, or is a new template added?]
