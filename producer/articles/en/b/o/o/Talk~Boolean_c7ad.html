[edit] Boolean algebra(s)

There's a discussion going on at Boolean algebra that needs to be taken into account on this page as well. Basically there's an ambiguity between "Boolean algebra" as a calculus; that is, a notation and rules for manipulating the notation, and a Boolean algebra, which is a mathematical structure satisfying certain axioms. This page assumes that we're discussing the first notion, yet links to a page primarily concerned with the second. In particular the passage

Boolean involves only the two logical values: TRUE (1) and FALSE (0)

is simply mistaken when discussing the second notion. --Trovatore 18:02, 5 September 2005 (UTC)
