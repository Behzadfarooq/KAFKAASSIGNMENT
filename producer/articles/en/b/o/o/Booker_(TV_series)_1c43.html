Booker (TV series)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

You have new messages (last change).

Booker was an hour long television drama starring Richard Grieco. It ran on the Fox Network from September 24, 1989 to May 6, 1990. It was a spin-off of 21 Jump Street.
