Hi.

I'm a transplanted Albertan who lives in Vancouver, Canada. I've been kicking around here since late 2004, and I spend most of my time at WikiProject Organized Labour these days.

I'm a blue collar worker with pro-union tendencies, although I would want to qualify that position with a discussion about political structures in general. I try to stay aware of my bias as I edit, but undoubtedly it is still there.



I have reduced the amount of time I'm spending here (or tried at least). Other parts of my life require more attention. I'll still be around, but on more of a weekly basis, rather than daily.

Cheers.

Chris
