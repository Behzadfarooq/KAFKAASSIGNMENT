The Book of Jehu is a lost text that may have been written by the Biblical prophet Jehu b. Hanani, who was one of King Baasha's contemporaries. The book is described in 2Chronicles 20:34. The passage reads: "Now the rest of the acts of Jehoshaphat, first and last, behold, they are written in the book of Jehu the son of Hanani, who is mentioned in the book of the kings of Israel."

This manuscript is sometimes called The Book of Jehu ben Hanani.
