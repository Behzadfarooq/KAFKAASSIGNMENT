Book of Divine Worship

Published in 2003, the Book of Divine Worship is an adaptation of the American Book of Common Prayer (BCP). It takes "elements of the Book of Common Prayer [which are] revised and adapted according to the Roman Rite for use by Roman Catholics coming from the Anglican tradition." Authorized by the Holy See and used primarily by Catholic converts from Anglicanism within Anglican Use, the Book of Divine Worship comprises constitutive elements of the 1928 and 1979 Episcopal Church editions of the Book of Common Prayer as well as the 1973 Roman Missal, Missale Romanum.
