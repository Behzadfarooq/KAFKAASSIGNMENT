[edit] Knowledge and Interests

I'm an anime fan, and I read manga and pay some amount of attention to Japanese music. I'm also a Nightwish fan, and slowly getting into metal. Other than that, I listen to pop, new age, rock, and occasionally folk. I dislike rap, hip-hop, and country. Classical and opera usually bore me, but I don't find them unpleasant.

I read many sci-fi, fantasy, and mystery novels (mainly ones by Agatha Christie), and occasionally write fiction and watch movies of the same types, plus comedies.

I play some video games and some online games, including, in the past, Everquest and Second Life. I have a moderate level of general computer knowledge, both of OSX and Windows, but know nothing about Linux.

I'm interested in feminism, both for women's rights and GLBTI rights. In fact, it's my major in college.

I'm moderately interested in various science, particularly biology and astronomy, as well as things having to do with aerospace history, though my knowledge isn't hugely extensive.

I've taken French for four years, plus the last three quarters in college, and a fair bit of study on my own. I think I'm pretty close to fluent in terms of reading, writing, and oral comprehension (if not actually fluent) but my speaking skills have further to go. I've started studying Japanese, but currently my skills are pretty lacking. I can read kana, and I know a few basic phrases and random words, but that's about it. It's still better than my Spanish, which was never good and has atrophied. I have also studied other languages (American Sign Language and most recently Highland Gaelic), though not in any depth.

I'm a bit of a stickler for grammar, but only in writing, so I find myself making minor edits to fix such errors.
