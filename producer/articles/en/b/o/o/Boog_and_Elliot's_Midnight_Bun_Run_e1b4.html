Boog and Elliot's Midnight Bun Run is a computer animated short film from Sony Pictures Animation. A spin-off of the animated film Open Season, Boog and Elliot's Midnight Bun Run is featured on the Open Season DVD.

[edit] Plot summary

Spoiler warning: Plot and/or ending details follow.

The campers Bob and Bobbie leave the trailer to go skinny dipping. Mr. Weenie is sleeping and they left cinnamon buns on the counter. Boog and Elliot sneak into the camp and discover there's buns in trailer. Unfortunately Mr. Weenie is guarding the door. So Boog and Elliot climb onto the roof, Boog ties Elliot to the end of a rope, and lowers through the open skylight. Elliot is swung back and forth choosing between buns with nuts and buns without nuts. he picks one with nuts but Mr. Weenie wakes up. Mr. Weenie bites Elliot on the butt and Elliot runs around the trailer acidentally pulling Boog into trailer. Bob and Bobbie hear noises from outside and think Mr. Weenie is being attacked. When Boog and Elliot discover the police are coming, Elliot runs out of the window looking for a place to hide leaving Boog in the trailer with nowhere to run. Then police officer, Gordy comes in and sees Boog holding Mr. Weenie by the tail. Then Mr. Weenie says to Boog "You are so busted" and Boog responds "Tell me about it".
