Boozed, Broozed, and Broken-Boned is a DVD released by heavy metal band Black Label Society on August 12, 2003. It was recorded at the Harpos Concert Theatre on September 14, 2002 in Detroit, Michigan. During the recording of the show, the bar allegedly ran out of alcohol. [1]
