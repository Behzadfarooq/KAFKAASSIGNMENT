This is a category to crossreference all works available for download online, including articles on e-magazines which are produced by a reputable publisher containing fiction or factual articles (including WikiBooks), but not those ezines that are email driven, political publications or those generated as a sales tool by a manufacturer or distributor. The main article for this category is e-books.
