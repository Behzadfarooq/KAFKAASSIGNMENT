Boon Lay Bus Interchange

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Boon Lay Bus Interchange, at its temporary location.

Boon Lay Bus Interchange (Chinese: 文礼巴士转换站) is a bus station located in Boon Lay at the western part of Singapore. It is integrated with the nearby Boon Lay MRT Station. As this interchange serves a myriad of passengers (from students from Nanyang Technological University to industrial workers at Jurong Industrial Estate and Tuas Industrial Estate), it is very busy. Operated by SBS Transit, this interchange is the biggest in terms of bus services and has 30 bus services.

The interchange is operating from a new temporary location from 18 June 2006 while the existing site is being built as part of the Jurong Point extension as well as a 16 storey condominium known as The Centris. A new location for the bus interchange will be constructed and continue operating until 2009.
