Boo-Boo and the Man is a short cartoon starring Boo-Boo Bear, the beloved sidekick of Yogi Bear. It was made by Ren and Stimpy creator John Kricfalusi and his company Spümcø using Macromedia Flash.

[edit] Plot outline

Boo-Boo gets beat up by the "Big Boys" (older boy bears).
