Booka Shade is a German electrohouse duo. It is made up of Walter Merziger and Arno Kammermeier, described as veterans of the Frankfurt electronic music scene. They have released two albums, Memento and Movements. Their 2005 singles "Mandarine Girl" and "Body Language" (with M.A.N.D.Y.) were widely played in clubs [1] and licensed on many mix CDs; Resident Advisor called "Body Language" "one of the biggest releases of the year."[2]

After beginnings similar to those of Underworld (namely as an overlooked early nineties synth pop duo), the band has more recently created club music. Their releases resulted on European imprints like R&S Records and Harthouse.

In 2002, they founded Get Physical Records with DJ T., Chelonis R Jones, and M.A.N.D.Y. Instead of relying upon releasing records that easily fell within the boundaries of established genres (techno, trance, house etc), the band tried to develop a sound of their own, with good reception. Pitchfork has highlighted their releases and referred to Get Physical as a new Warp Records.[3]
