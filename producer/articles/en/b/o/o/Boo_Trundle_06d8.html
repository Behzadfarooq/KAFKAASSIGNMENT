Boo Trundle

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Boo Trundle from Possible Bodies CD (1995)

Boo Trundle is a musician active in early to mid 1990's. Her style was semi-acoustic alternative indie rock.

[edit] Discography

  • The Vast Underneath (1993) (Big Deal Records) Produced by Boo Trundle and Yoram Vazan
  • Tall Sleep (EP) (1994) (Big Deal Records) Produced by Boo Trundle, Joshua Melville, and Kurt Ralske
  • Possible Bodies (1995) (Big Deal Records) Produced by Kurt Ralske and Joe McGinty

[edit] Featured Musicians

  • Guitar and Vocals: Boo Trundle
  • Guitar: Kurt Ralske
  • Keyboards: Joe McGinty
  • Bass: Akin Adams, Kurt Ralske, Chris Bowers

  • Strings: Jennifer Williams, Chrysyn M. Harp, Sarah Kapps, Jane Scarpantoni
