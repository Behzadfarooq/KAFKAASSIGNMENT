BookPeople

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

BookPeople is an independent bookstore in Austin, Texas, and the largest bookstore in the state of Texas. It was founded in 1970 and has been voted the best bookstore by the Austin Chronicle every year since 1995.[citation needed]

BookPeople was voted Publisher's Weekly's "Best Bookstore in the US 2005".[1] BookPeople is a member of the Austin Independent Business Alliance.
