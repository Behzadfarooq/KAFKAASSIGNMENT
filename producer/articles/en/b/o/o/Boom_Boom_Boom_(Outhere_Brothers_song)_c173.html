"Boom Boom Boom" is a song by The Outhere Brothers. This song was a top dance hit. The song was a United Kingdom number one in 1995.

[edit] Trivia

  • A prerecorded medley contained this song and a song by Vengaboys that was unrelated but had a similar title.
  • Played by Boston Red Sox after a player hits a homerun.
