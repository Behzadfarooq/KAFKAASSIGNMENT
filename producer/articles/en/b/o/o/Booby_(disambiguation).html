Booby or Boobies can refer to:

  • Booby (pl. Boobies), a group of seabird species that, with gannets, form the Sulidae
  • Boobs or boobies, slang term for female breasts
  • Boobie or booby, a term of endearment is derived from the Yiddish term bubele, meaning baby
  • A booby trap
  • Booby prize, a worthless or embarrassing item given as a joke to someone who loses a contest
