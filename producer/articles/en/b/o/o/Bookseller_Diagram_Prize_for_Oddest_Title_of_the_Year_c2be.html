The Bookseller/Diagram Prize for Oddest Title of the Year is a humorous literary award given each year to the book with the oddest title. It is presented each year by the British magazine Bookseller. Nominees are selected from submissions sent in by librarians, publishers, and booksellers, and the final winner is voted for by the public.
