Booba, born Elie Yaffa, is half Senegalese-half French from Boulogne-Billancourt. He began his career as a rapper in 1994. In 1996 he co-wrote, with Ali (the co-founder of Lunatic) Lunatic went on to found their own label, 45 Scientific, and in 2000 released the much vaunted Mauvais Oeil.

In 2002 he produced his first solo album Temps Mort to critical acclaim. He signs to Universal and goes on to release his second solo album Panthéon in 2004. In 2006 he released "Ouest Side".

In 2004 Booba launched his own clothing line Ünkut. It was featured in an Xbox Live Marketplace downloadable add-on for the Xbox 360 game Saints Row.

In February 2006 Booba contacted the police concerning the kidnapping of his mother and brother.

Booba is probably one of the most recognizable and famous rappers in France right now.

He recently collaborated on a song with Akon called "Gun In Hand."
