Book by category

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

A Book by category (Traditional Chinese: 類書) is one kind of reference book in ancient China.

  • First appeared in Cao Wei for the emperor Cao Pi.
  • During Song Dynasty Extensive Records of the Taiping Era and Imperial Readings of the Taiping Era,Prime Tortoise of the Record Bureau.
  • During Ming Dynasty, Yongle Encyclopedia,
  • Qing Dynasty,Gǔjīn Túshū Jíchéng.
