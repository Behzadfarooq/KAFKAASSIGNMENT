A bottle of Boone's Farm

Boone's Farm is a flavored apple wine product produced in the American state of California. Available in many flavors, its low cost makes it a favorite on college campuses. The beverage is produced by E & J Gallo Winery.

In some U.S. states, such as Minnesota and Utah, some Boone's Farm products are labelled as malt beverages and not as flavored apple/citrus wine products, as state liquor laws prohibit the sale of wine in grocery and convenience stores.
