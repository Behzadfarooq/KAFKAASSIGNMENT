"Boom Boom Boom Boom" is a song by the Dutch Eurodance group Vengaboys. It was released as a single on 23 October 1998. On 13 June 1999 it became the UK Singles Chart number one single. This song is based on Paul Lekakis's "Boom Boom", from 1987.
