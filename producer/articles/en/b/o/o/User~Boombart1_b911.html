[edit] Video games

I've been a fan of Sonic the Hedgehog for a year now. My first Sonic game was Sonic Mega Collection Plus for Xbox (I got that for my ninth birthday). My favorite Sonic character is Miles "Tails" Prower because he can fly, he's smart, and he makes terrific airplanes.

I used to hate Mario because he competed very well with Sonic, but since I got New Super Mario Bros., I became interested in him. My favorite characters in Mario are the Toads.

[edit] TV

My favorite TV channel is - yeah, you guessed it - Fox. I get Fox on channel 9 (29) in Dover (Philadelphia). My favorite show - yeah, you guessed it - The Simpsons. I also like Family Guy, American Dad, and Malcolm in the Middle.

My other favorites are ESPN, NFL Network, and NBC.
