Boomer is the pseudonym of a Dayton, Ohio area DJ on WXEG (103.9 FM). He was born on May 20, 1977 in Northampton, Massachusetts.


[edit] Radio Career

Boomer at X-Fest 11 in Dayton.

Boomer started his career as an intern at K-Rock in New York. From there, Boomer got a job in Northampton, MA, at WHMP/WLZX. He then moved to Dayton, Ohio in 2002 to be the Afternoon Drive DJ and Music Director at WXEG. He started doing the Morning Drive in 2003 before being named Assistant Program Director in 2004.

In 2005, Boomer was nominated for Music Director of the Year. He was also voted as the Best Radio Personality by the Dayton Daily News.

Boomer became a father when he and his wife, Lauren, welcomed their son, Avi, into the world on November 22, 2006 at 8:55 AM.

Boomer was the first DJ in America to play the band Seether on the radio (back when he was the Afternoon Drive DJ at WXEG).

Features on Boomer's morning show include The Morning X Sports Report, The Morning X Reality Check and Boomer's Useless Fact of the Day and contests such as writing a Wikipedia entry for prizes.
