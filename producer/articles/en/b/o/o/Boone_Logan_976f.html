Boone Logan (born August 13, 1984 in San Antonio, Texas) is a left-handed relief pitcher in Major League Baseball with the Chicago White Sox, who grew up in Helotes, Texas and attended Sandra Day O'Connor High School. He was selected in the 20th round of the 2002 amateur draft by the White Sox. He became a local sports news story after advancing from the Sox's Class A affiliate, the Great Falls White Sox, straight to the major league club during spring training in 2006.
