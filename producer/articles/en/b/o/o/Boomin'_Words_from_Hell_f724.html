Boomin' Words from Hell is the re-released of Esham Boomin' debut Words From Hell album by acid rapper Esham, released in 1990 (see 1990 in music). The original Words From Hell contains 12 tracks while the re-released Boomin' Words from Hell contain 16 tracks. Three track ("Telling It How It Is", "Don't Trip", "Watch 'Cha Back") on the original Words From Hell are missing on the re-released Boomin' words from Hell. "Watch 'Cha Back" would later resurface on the Compilation album Bootleg From The Lost Vaults. 7 extra tracks ("4 All the Suicidalist", "True", "Knockin Em Dead", "Red Rum", "Cross My Heart", "Wish You Was Down", and "Devils in the Soup") that were not on the originial Words From Hell would appear on the re-release Boomin' Words From Hell. "Red Rum" would also be on the Compilation album Bootleg From The Lost Vaults.
