Guy Boskila (Hebrew: גיא בוסקילה), commonly known by his rap name BooSkills (Hebrew: בוסקילז), is an Israeli rap artist. His rap name is a combination of his last name and the noun skills. He is part of the stable of artists on TACT Records, the label owned and run by rapper Subliminal.

[edit] Background

His childhood was spent in Los Angeles, California. During a visit to Los Angeles by Kobi Shimoni and Yoav Eliasi, a good friend and future producer of theirs, David Levy introduced them to Guy Boskila. That is how the tracks came about for two of the biggest hits on Shimoni's second album. Soon thereafter, Boskila decided to make aliyah and return to his roots in Israel.

His connection with Subliminal paid off and BooSkills is set to release his first Hebrew language album under the TACT Records label.
