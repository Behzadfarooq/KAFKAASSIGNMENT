Boca da Lapa (Capeverdean Crioulo, ALUPEC or ALUPEK and the São Vicente Crioulo: Boka da Lápa) is a rocky shoreline in the southeast the island of São Vicente. The area are linked with a road used for four-wheelers and SUVs.

[edit] Geography and Information

The beach are made up of rocks with soil colours being orange-like, tan and light brown. Another thing is the beach is not used. The mountains lie to the northern part

[edit] Panorama

The area offers a panoramic view of the southeastern part of the plain but it is blocked by its nearest mountains and Viana, it offers a view of the island of Santa Luzia.
