In the World of Greyhawk campaign setting and the default pantheon for the Dungeons & Dragons roleplaying game, Boccob is the god of Magic, Arcane Knowledge, Balance, and Foresight. He is known as the Uncaring, the Lord of All Magic, and the Archmage of the Deities. He is served by the mad demigod, Zagyg.

Boccob is known to carry the very first Staff of the Magi with him at all times. In addition, he knows every spell ever created and can travel to any time and dimension. He is the possessor of the only magical library in existence that contains a copy of every potion, spell, and magic item in existence.

[edit] Other aspects

Among the Bakluni, Boccob is known as Al'Zarad, the god of Magic and Learning. Regarded as a creation of Istus, Al'Zarad has few worshipers. Boccob is usually portrayed as a middle aged man with brown hair who wears purple robes and carries a staff of the magi (see powers)
