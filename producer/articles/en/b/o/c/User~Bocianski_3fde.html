About me
I'm a Polish user, admin of Polish Wikipedia. On wikipedia I'm writing articles on Polish topics and adding interwiki. I'm also interested in genealogy. If your ancestors came from the Lublin region, especially from the Końskowola commune, maybe we are family. If you would like, you could write me about it.
