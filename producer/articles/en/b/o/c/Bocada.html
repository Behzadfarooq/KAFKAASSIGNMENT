Bocada is a leading provider of Enterprise Data Protection Management software founded in 1999 by Cory Bear and Liam Scanlan.

The company is noted for helping create the Backup Data Protection Management category and successfully implementing their software using the Agentless Data Collection approach.

[edit] Overview

Founded in 1999 by Cory Bear and Liam Scanlan, located in Bellevue, Washington the company has built a reputation in the Enterprise Data Protection Management industry as being a quality software developer trusted by customers in the areas of Energy, Financial Services, Consumer Goods, Technology, Telecommunications and other markets. In 2005, the company shipped Bocada Enterprise 4 the seventh major version of its software. Three of the world's largest outsourced service providers (IBM, EDS and ACS) have standardized Enterprise Data Protection Management using Bocada.
