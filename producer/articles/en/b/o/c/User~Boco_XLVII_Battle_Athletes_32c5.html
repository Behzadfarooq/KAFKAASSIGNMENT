Battle Athletes (バトルアスリーテス大運動会 BATTLE ATHLETESS daiundōkai) is a series of Japanese life simulation bishōjo games by the computer game company Increment-P, though it is much more well-known by its OVA and telebi anime adaptation by AIC.
