Path of Turnpike

The Florida Turnpike passes through unincorporated suburbs west of the City of Boca Raton. The Geography and Demographics sections apply only to the incorporated city. It is not correct to state that the turnpike passes through the city proper. There is a difference between "city" and "urbanized area". -- Dalbury(Talk) 22:55, 15 December 2005 (UTC)
