Bochnia County (Polish powiat bocheński ) is a unit of territorial administration and local government in the Lesser Poland Voivodeship in Poland, created on 1 January 1999 as a result of the Local Government Reorganization Act of 1998.

[edit] Communes

Administrative division: 9 communes

  • Bochnia urban commune
  • Bochnia rural commune
  • Drwinia rural commune
  • Lipnica Murowana rural commune
  • Łapanów rural commune
  • Nowy Wiśnicz urban-rural commune
  • Rzezawa rural commune
  • Trzciana rural commune
  • Żegocina rural commune
