Category:Board game designers

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

A board game designer is a person who designs board games. This category also includes designers of card games, miniatures games, and trading card games. Designers of German-style board games are often credited on the box.

For more information, see the main articles about board games, game designers and German game designers.
