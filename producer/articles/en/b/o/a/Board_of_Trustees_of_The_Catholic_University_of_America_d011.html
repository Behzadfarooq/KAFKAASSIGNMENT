Board of Trustees of The Catholic University of America

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

As of 2005, the Board of Trustees at The Catholic University of America was composed of:

  • Carl A. Anderson, Supreme Knight of the Knights of Columbus
  • Richard D. Banziger, B.A. 1981, Managing Director, Salomon Smith Barney
  • Nancy J. Bidwill, owner, Arizona Cardinals
  • Bertha S. Braddock, Alexandria, VA philanthropist
  • Michael J. Bransfield, M.Phil 1973, Bishop of Wheeling-Charleston, WV
  • Raymond L. Burke, Archbishop of St. Louis
  • Timothy R. Busch, Esq., Orange County, California attorney
  • Charles J. Chaput, O.F.M. Cap., Archbishop of Denver
  • Paul J. Chiapparone, Executive Vice President, Electronic Data Systems
  • Robert F. Comstock, Esq., B.A. 1958, J.D. 1964, Washington, D.C. attorney
  • Robert E. Craves, B.A. 1965, Co-Founder of Costco Wholesale
  • Robert J. Crimmins, Huntington, NY
  • Edward P. Cullen, Bishop of Allentown
  • Leo A. Daly III, B.A., Washington, DC architect
  • Daniel N. DiNardo, B.A., M.A., Bishop of Houston
  • Timothy M. Dolan, Ph.D, Archbishop of Milwaukee
  • David A. Donohoe, Esq., A.B. 1959, J.D. 1962, Vice Chairman, Washington, DC attorney
  • Thomas G. Doran, Bishop of Rockford
  • Edward M. Egan, Cardinal Archbishop of New York
  • John C. Favalora, Archbishop of Miami
  • Frederick R. Favo, 1955, Oakmont, PA realtor
  • Sister Margaret Mary Fitzpatrick, S.C., President of St. Thomas Aquinas College
  • Harry J. Flynn, Archbishop of St. Paul and and Minneapolis
  • Francis E. George, O.M.I., M.Phil 1965, Cardinal Archbishop of Chicago
  • Stephanie Germack-Kerzic, Grosse Pointe Farms, MI philanthropist
  • José H. Gomez, Archbishop of San Antonio
  • Wilton D. Gregory, Archbishop of Atlanta
  • Ray J. Hillenbrand, Rapid City, SD businessman
  • Michael P. Hoffman, CEO, Changing Our World, Inc
  • William H. Keeler, Cardinal Archishop of Baltimore
  • William E. Lori, S.T.D. 1982, Chairman, Bishop of Bridgeport
  • Roger Mahony, M.S.W. 1964, Cardinal Archbishop of Los Angeles
  • Adam J. Maida, Cardinal Archbishop of Detroit
  • Theodore E. McCarrick, M.S., Ph.D., Chancellor, Cardinal Archbishop of Washington
  • William A. McKenna Jr., Saugerties, NY
  • Sandra A. McMurtrie, Archer Daniels Midland
  • William F. Murphy, Bishop of Rockville Centre
  • John J. Myers, Archbishop of Newark
  • Very Rev. David M. O’Connell, C.M., J.C.D 1987, President, Washington, DC
  • Sean P. O’Malley, O.F.M. Cap., M.A., Ph.D, Cardinal Archbishop of Boston
  • William G. Parrett, CEO, Deloitte Touche Tohmatsu
  • Joseph A. Pepe, Bishop of Las Vegas
  • Neil J. Rauenhorst, President, NJR Development Company
  • Justin F. Rigali, S.T.B. 1961, Cardinal Archbishop of Philadelphia
  • Andrea Roane, co-anchor of W*USA 9 News
  • Monsignor Walter R. Rossi, Rector, Basilica of the National Shrine of the Immaculate Conception
  • Timothy C. Scheve, CEO, Legg Mason Wood Walker
  • Rodger D. Shay, President, Shay Assets Management
  • Mark H. Tuohey III, Esq., Washington, DC attorney
  • Allen H. Vigneron, Bishop of Oakland
  • Frank G. Persico, Secretary of the Board, Chief of Staff to President O'Connell
