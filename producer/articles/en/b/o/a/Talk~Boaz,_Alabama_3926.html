[edit] Boaz, AL

Boaz is not located in Etowah County. It's located in Marshall County and the county seat is located in Guntersville, AL, not Gadsden.

Boaz is located partially in Etowah County, Alabama. The county seat for the Etowah County portion is Gadsden. The county seat for the Marshal County portion is in Guntersville. It is not uncommon for cities in Alabama to straddle county lines.
