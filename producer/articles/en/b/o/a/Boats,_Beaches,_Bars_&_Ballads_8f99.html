Boats, Beaches, Bars & Ballads

Boats, Beaches, Bars & Ballads is a four-disc (or cassette) box-set of Jimmy Buffett and the Coral Reefer Band's greatest hits, rarities, and unreleased songs. Released in 1992, the collection reached quadruple platinum.

[edit] Boats

Boats is a CD of sailor and sailing songs, true to Buffett's heart. Previously unreleased songs include Love and Luck and Take it Back. Love and Luck would later appear on the live album, Tuesdays, Thursdays, and Saturdays. The album is 66 minutes, 22 seconds long.

Companion notebook is included

[edit] Beaches

Beaches is a CD of Buffett's days in Key West, the Gulf Coast, and the Caribbean. Of the 18 beach songs, notable songs are Margaritaville, also known as the Buffett national anthem, and Cheeseburger In Paradise. Unreleased songs include Money Back Guarantee and Christmas in the Caribbean. The album is 63 minutes, 16 seconds long.

[edit] Bars

Bars is a CD of bars, songs written in bars, and plain party songs. Beginning with Fins, the album also includes Why Don't We Get Drunk and Screw and Pencil Thin Mustache. Unreleased songs include Elvis Imitators, a Buffett attempt at an Elvis Presley song, and Domino College. Fins and Cuban Crime Of Passion are cowritten with Tom Corcoran.
