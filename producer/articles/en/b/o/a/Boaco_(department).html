Boaco (department)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Boaco is a department in Nicaragua. It was formed in 1938 out of part of Chontales. It covers an area of 4,244 km² and has a population of 172,500 (2006, est.). The capital is the city of Boaco.
