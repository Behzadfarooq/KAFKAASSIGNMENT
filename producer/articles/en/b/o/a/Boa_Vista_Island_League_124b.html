Boa Vista Island League

The Boa Vista Island League is a regional championship played in Boa Vista Island, Cape Verde. The winner of the championship playes in Cape Verdean football Championships of each season.

  • Associação Académica e Operária - Sal Rei

  • Juventude do Norte - Norte

  • Sporting
