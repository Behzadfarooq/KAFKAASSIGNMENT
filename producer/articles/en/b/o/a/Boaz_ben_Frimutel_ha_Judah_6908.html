Boaz ben Frimutel ha Judah.

Fictional Prince of the Desposyni in Roman Gaul during the 4th Century AD. He was the last prince of the 'Grail Dynasty' and with his death the Principality is supposed to have passed to the Frankish House of Sicamber when his daughter Frotmund married Marcomir ex Sicamber of the Salian Franks. SPECIAL NOTE: Despite some allegations of "fictional" charachters, certain pedigrees of various european peerages including european royalty show unbroken heritage to said Boaz of this article, and previous ancestry. For those willing to consider such, certain western traditions and/or "Empire" derived dogma(s) appear to conflict with more ancient (and non-agenda driven) genealogical sources which carry a particular line of the House of David forward as a component of said european peerage and royalty. here What follows is not to dispute other lineages esteemed to be "the absolute word" though transcribed by men,; rather to serve as an alternative, not subjected to ecumenical councils and other forms of "conveyance". to wit:

 Boaz was not the son of Frimutel, but rather was the father of Frimutel also known as Frotmund I. This, to deliniate from the first's grandson Frotmund II also known as Fredemundus Desposyni. The recorded ancestry, considered by some reliable, by others not reliable follows:

Frimutel begat of Boaz ha David; begat of Titurel ben Emanuel; begat of Emanuel ben Cathaloys; begat of Cathaloys ben Aminadab; begat of Joshua ha David; begat of Joseph ben Yeshu (elsewhere known as Jesus the Nazarene); begat of Joseph ben Jacob ha David; begat of Eli ha David (brother to Joseph of Arimathea); begat of Matthat ben Levi of Arimathea; begat of Levi ben Melchi; begat of Melchi ben Janna; begat of Janna ben Joseph; begat of Joseph ben Mattathias ha David; begat of Matthias ben Naum; begat of Naum ben Esli; begat of Esli ben Nagga; begat of Nagga ben Maath; begat of Maath ha David; begat of Mattathias ben Semel (and Damaris bas Simon ha Kohen); begat of Semel ben Joseph; begat of Joseph ben Judah; begat of Judah ben Joanna; begat of Joanna ben Resa; begat of Resa ha David (and Rhoda princess of Persia); begat of Zorobabel ha David; begat of Salathiel ha David;(NOTE- the previous two cited generations are the only two in congruence to the heritage given at Matt 1:12) begat of Neri ha David; begat of Melchi ha David; begat of Addi ha David; begat of Cosan ha David; begat of Elmadain ha David; begat of Er ha Jesus; begat of Jesus ben Eliazer; begat of Eliazer ben Jorim; begat of Jorim ha David; begat of Matthat ben Levi; begat of Levi ben Simeon; begat of Simeon ben Joseph; begat of Joseph ben Jonam; begat of Jonam ha David; begat of Eliakim ha David; begat of Melea ha David; begat of Menna ha David; begat of Nathan ha David; begat of King David and Bathsheba.
