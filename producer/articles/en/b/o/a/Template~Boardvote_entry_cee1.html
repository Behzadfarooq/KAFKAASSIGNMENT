Their statements can be found here. Please take time to review their statements before voting.

Anyone who has had a legitimate User log on the English-language Wikipedia for at least three months may vote. Users from other Wikipedias may not vote for the English-language Arbitration Committee.

You may vote for as many of the candidates as you like. The two candidates with the greatest number of votes will be declared the winner. In the event of a tie, a run-off election between the leading candidates will be held.

The vote will end at 24:00 UTC on Friday, 18 December, 2004. The winners will be announced shortly afterwards, and a complete list of results will be published here.

You may vote here.

Good luck to all of the candidates!
