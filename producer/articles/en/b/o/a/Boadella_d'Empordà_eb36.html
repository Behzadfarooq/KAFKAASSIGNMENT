[[Alt Empordà]]

Boadella d'Empordà is a municipality in the comarca of the Alt Empordà in Catalonia, Spain. It is situated in the valley of the Mugà river, which is dammed to form the Boadella reservoir, a tourist attraction, and to generate hydroelectric power. Local roads link the village with Biure and Terrades.
