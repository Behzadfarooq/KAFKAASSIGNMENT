Boss Tuneage

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

You have new messages (last change).

Boss Tuneage is an independent record label based in the UK started in 1990 by Aston Stephens. The label became dormant in 1995 (when an unsuccessful relaunch as Out of Order Records took place) before being started up again in 1999. Over 150 record releases took place before a second hiatus being taken in 2005. In December 2005 the Boss Tuneage Recording Company and the Boss Tuneage Retro Series were born; the former was intended to focus on new bands whilst the latter would reissue classic releases.
