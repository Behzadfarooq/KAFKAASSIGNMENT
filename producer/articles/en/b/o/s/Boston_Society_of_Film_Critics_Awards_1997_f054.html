Boston Society of Film Critics Awards 1997

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

18th Boston Society of Film Critics Awards

December 14, 1997

Best Picture:
L.A. Confidential

The 18th Boston Society of Film Critics Awards honored the best films of 1997.

[edit] Winners

  • Best Actor:
    • Al Pacino - Donnie Brasco
  • Best Actress:
    • Helena Bonham Carter - The Wings of the Dove
  • Best Cinematography:
    • Kundun - Roger Deakins
  • Best Director:
    • John Boorman - L.A. Confidential
  • Best Documentary:
    • Fast, Cheap and Out of Control
  • Best Foreign Film:
    • Underground, France/Federal Republic of Yugoslavia/Germany/Hungary
  • Best New Filmmaker:
    • Paul Thomas Anderson - Hard Eight and Boogie Nights
  • Best Picture:
    • L.A. Confidential
  • Best Screenplay:
    • L.A. Confidential - Curtis Hanson and Brian Helgeland
  • Best Supporting Actor:
    • Kevin Spacey - L.A. Confidential
  • Best Supporting Actress:
    • Sarah Polley - The Sweet Hereafter
