Bossman born is a Baltimore, Maryland based rapper.[1]

He has appeared on mixtapes promoted by DJ Kayslay and DJ Radio with other Baltimore rappers such as Skarr Akbar, D.O.G., X.O, and Barnes and is well known through Baltimore Club music. Recently Bossman has released his debut album Law and Order and has been reported to receive a $1.5 million-plus deal with Virgin Records.[2]
