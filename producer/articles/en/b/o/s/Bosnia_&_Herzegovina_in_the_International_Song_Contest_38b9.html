Bosnia and Herzegovina entered the contest in ISC 03 and had non-stop participations since (including the special editions), failing to reach the final only two times. Bosnia's best result had been the 4th place which they have reached for 4 times

Hari Mata Hari has the odd record of holding the best and worst result for Bosnia and Herzegovina
