14th Boston Society of Film Critics Awards

December 19, 1993

Best Picture:
Schindler's List

The 14th Boston Society of Film Critics Awards honored the best filmmaking of 1993. The awards were given on 19 December 1993

[edit] Winners

  • Best Actor:
    • Daniel Day-Lewis - In the Name of the Father
  • Best Actress:
    • Holly Hunter - The Piano
  • Best Cinematography:
    • Schindler's List - Janusz Kaminski
  • Best Director:
    • Steven Spielberg - Schindler's List
  • Best Documentary:
    • Visions of Light (1992)
  • Best Foreign Film:
    • Ba wang bie ji (Farewell My Concubine), China/Hong Kong
  • Best Picture:
    • Schindler's List
  • Best Screenplay:
    • Short Cuts - Robert Altman and Frank Barhydt
  • Best Supporting Actor:
    • Ralph Fiennes - Schindler's List
  • Best Supporting Actress:
    • Rosie Perez - Fearless
