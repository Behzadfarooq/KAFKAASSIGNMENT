Bosque School is a private, non-denominational and co-educational institution in Albuquerque, New Mexico, USA. It was founded in 1994 and still continues to grow. Formerly located in a church, it has since moved to a new location on a 45 acre site by Coors Boulevard and the Manta√±o surrounded by the bosque and very close to the Rio Grande, the school emphasizes environmental concern and community involvement. The school currently has 490 students in grades 6 through 12. With 70 teaching faculty, and a 7:1 student to faculty ratio. Recently during expansion a ancient pueblo was found on the site where soccer fields and tennis courts were going to be built.

Bosque School offers courses not seen in public high schools, such as a graduation requirement of 2 years of latin and spanish.
