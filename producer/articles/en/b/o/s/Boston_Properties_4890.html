Boston Properties, Inc. (NYSE: BXP) is a self-managed real estate investment trust (REIT) based in Boston, Massachusetts. Its primary focus is "Class A" office space which it acquires, develops, and manages in the major markets of Boston, New York City, Washington, D.C., and San Francisco. It also owns two hotel properties.

It was founded in 1970 by Mortimer Zuckerman and Edward H. Linde, and became listed in June 1997.
