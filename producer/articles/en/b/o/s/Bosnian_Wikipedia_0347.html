Bosnian Wikipedia

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Logo of Bosnian Wikipedia

The Bosnian Wikipedia (Wikipedia na bosanskom jeziku) is the Bosnian language version of Wikipedia, run by the Wikimedia Foundation. As of February 3, 2007, it has 13,122 articles.
