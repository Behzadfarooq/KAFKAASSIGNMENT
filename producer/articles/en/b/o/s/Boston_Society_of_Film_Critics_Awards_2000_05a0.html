21st Boston Society of Film Critics Awards

The 21st Boston Society of Film Critics Awards honored the best in film of 2000.

[edit] Winners

  • Best Actor:
    • Colin Farrell - Tigerland
  • Best Actress:
    • Ellen Burstyn - Requiem for a Dream
  • Best Director:
    • Cameron Crowe - Almost Famous
  • Best Picture:
    • Almost Famous
  • Best Screenplay (tie):
    • Almost Famous - Cameron Crowe
    • Wonder Boys - Steven Kloves
  • Best Supporting Actor:
    • Fred Willard - Best in Show
  • Best Supporting Actress:
    • Frances McDormand - Almost Famous and Wonder Boys
