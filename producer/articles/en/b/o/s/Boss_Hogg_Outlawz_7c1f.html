Boss Hogg Outlawz
Parent company Koch Records
Founder(s) Slim Thug
Distributing label Koch Records (In the US)
Genre(s) Hiphop, Southern Rap
Country of origin United States
Official Website http://www.bosshoggoutlawz.com

Boss Hogg Outlawz is a Houston based record label that was created Slim Thug in the year 2000. The label signed a deal with Koch Records in mid 2006. The first album Serve and Collect is 15 tracks of pure genius. It has been called "the best album of the year" by Mr. Micheal Starchin of Rolling Stone magazine.
