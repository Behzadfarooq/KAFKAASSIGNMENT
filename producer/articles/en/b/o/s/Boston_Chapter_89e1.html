The New England Chapter (later called Boston Chapter) of the American Marketing Association was formally founded, and has been continuously organized since October 3rd, 1940. While the American Marketing Association was founded on January 1st 1937 through the merger of the National Association of Marketing Teachers (NAMT) and the American Marketing Society (AMS), there is some information that there may have been an earlier President for the Boston Chapter, Richard Ehrich, in 1935, Richard may have been connected with the NAMT or AMS in some way.
