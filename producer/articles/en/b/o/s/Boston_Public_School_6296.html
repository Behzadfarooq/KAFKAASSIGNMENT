Boston Public School

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Boston Public School is a feeder school to Townsend Central Public School and Waterford District High School, part of the Grand Erie District School Board. It is located in Boston, Ontario, near Waterford, Ontario, at 2993 Cockshutt Road, Waterford, Ontario N0E 1Y0. As of October 31, 2005, the school has 200 students. It offers Kindergarten to Grade 6.
