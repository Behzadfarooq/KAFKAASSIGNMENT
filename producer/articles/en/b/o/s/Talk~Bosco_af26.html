[edit] Burglary

Are we sure that Bosco was stolen? I can find no reference to it on the web that isn't using Wikipedia as its source. What's even more suspicious is that I can't find any of these pictures anywhere. So, does anyone have a source? RMoloney (talk) 10:02, 26 October 2005 (UTC)

Yes he was kinappeded from the UCD Student Bar back in 1996 or thereabouts. Bosco was returned unharmed to Donnybook Garda Station. The UCD Tribute ran with the story and it appeared on serval RTE radio shows. ant_ie 17:53, 26 October 2005 (UTC)
Ok, thanks. But was there a kidnapping in 2000, anyone? And is it true about the international photos? RMoloney (talk) 18:54, 26 October 2005 (UTC)
I don't know about 2000. I understood the photos were just made up. ant_ie 19:02, 26 October 2005 (UTC)
There were photos, however they were quite likely faked - student prank, unlikely to have the cash to bring the puppet around. I may have them here, somewhere, but my stack of "random funny crap from papers" is bloody huge... --Kiand 19:06, 26 October 2005 (UTC)
