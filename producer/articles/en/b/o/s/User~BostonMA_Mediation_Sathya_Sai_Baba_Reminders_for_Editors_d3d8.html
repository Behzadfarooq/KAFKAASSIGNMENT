This page was created as part of the mediation process for the Sathya Sai Baba and related articles. The specific purpose of this page is to keep track of open items, such as questions that have been asked, but have not been answered.

The parties to the mediation should review this page on a regular basis. The reason for this page is that sometimes questions get lost in the discussion. Sometimes questions are answered, but not in a way that makes the answer clear to other readers, such as the mediator. This page will help to keep track of those questions. Please do not edit this page and please respond to the questions in their original location -- do not respond here. The mediator will make an attempt to remove questions here after clear and unambiguous answers are posted by the original questions. If you believe that a question has been answered, but the mediator has not yet removed the question from this list, you may remind him on the Mediator Reminder Page
