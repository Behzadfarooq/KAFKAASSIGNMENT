Bose or Basu or Bosu (Bengali: বসু) is a Bengali surname for members of the Boshu kayastha clan and goutom goutro in India.

Some notable person with this surname are:

  • Subhash Chandra Bose (aka Netaji), a leader of the Indian independence movement and founder of the Indian National Army.
  • Jagdish Chandra Bose, Indian physicist
  • Satyendra Nath Bose,Indian physicist,after whom Bosons are named.
  • Rash Behari Bose,Indian freedom fighter
  • Khudiram Bose,Indian freedom fighter
  • Rajsekhar Bose,the writer
  • Amar Bose, MIT professor; founder of the Bose Corporation
  • Schindra Prasad Bose,designer of the Calcutta Flag
  • Raj Chandra Bose,statistician and mathematician
  • Rahul Bose, Indian actor
