Boston Society of Film Critics Awards 1999

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

20th Boston Society of Film Critics Awards

Best Picture:
Three Kings

The 20th Boston Society of Film Critics Awards honored the best films of 1999.

[edit] Winners

  • Best Actor:
    • Jim Carrey - Man on the Moon
  • Best Actress:
    • Hilary Swank - Boys Don't Cry
  • Best Cinematography:
    • Sleepy Hollow - Emmanuel Lubezski
  • Best Director:
    • David O. Russell - Three Kings
  • Best Documentary:
    • Hands on a Hardbody
  • Best Foreign Film:
    • Todo sobre mi madre (All About My Mother), Spain/France
  • Best New Filmmaker:
    • Kimberly Peirce - Boys Don't Cry
  • Best Picture:
    • Three Kings
  • Best Screenplay:
    • Being John Malkovich - Charlie Kaufman
  • Best Supporting Actor:
    • Christopher Plummer - The Insider
  • Best Supporting Actress:
    • Chloe Sevigny - Boys Don't Cry
