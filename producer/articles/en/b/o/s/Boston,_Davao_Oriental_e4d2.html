Boston, Davao Oriental

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Boston is a 4th class municipality in the province of Davao Oriental, Philippines. According to the 2000 census, it has a population of 10,266 people in 1,885 households.

[edit] Barangays

Boston is politically subdivided into 8 barangays.

  • Cabasagan
  • Caatihan
  • Cawayanan
  • Poblacion
  • San Jose
  • Sibajay
  • Carmen
  • Simulao
