The Bosniak Party of Montenegro (Montenegrin: Bošnjačka Stranka Crna Gore) is a Bosniak minority political party in Montenegro.

At the last legislative elections in Montenegro, 10 September 2006, the coalition of Liberal Party of Montenegro and Bosniak Party won 3 out of 81 seats in the Parliament of Montenegro. Out of this, Bosniak Party will have 1 seat.
