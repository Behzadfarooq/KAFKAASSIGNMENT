Boshengro, is a Roma word, and therefore an old word and, consequently, a good word. It means, quite literally, "one who plays the fiddle" , which I dont. Don't ask.

I was born in 1970 in Campbeltown , in Argyll, on the West Coast of Scotland and my family still live there. A lifelong victim of the wanderlust however, I now live in Sydney, Australia where my wife and I eat well, drink well and enjoy the sands and surf of Bondi Beach which sits, in all its glory, about 100 yards from my front door.

Many things interest me , a few things fascinate me but I obsess with little. Things that interest me include Winston Churchill and British Military History, things that fascinate me are often of an anthropoligical bent , in particular of a Roma, Māori and Indian nature.

As for obsession, other than Celtic Football Club, nothing really.
