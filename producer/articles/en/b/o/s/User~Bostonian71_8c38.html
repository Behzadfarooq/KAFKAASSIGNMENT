I'm an arts-lover, sports nut (go Sox!), and an Internet addict. I live in Boston, Massachusetts with my wife. I am a web developer (HTML geek), and am also going to film school part-time. Once I'm done, hopefully less than a decade from now, I want to somehow break into film editing. I find it ironic that though it's my brother who's the computer science major, all my jobs since graduation have involved computers in one form or another.

When I'm not on the web, I can be found curled up with a good book (lately I've been on a Terry Pratchett kick), watching a movie, or hanging out with my wife and/or friends. Sometimes when I'm working, I like to play (and sing along to) singer-songwriter chicks like Catie Curtis and Ani Difranco. Fortunately I work from home, where there usually isn't anyone within range. Thanks in no small part to my wife's encouragement, I have picked up some basic cooking skills, though my repertoire mostly consists of breakfast dishes like scrambled eggs and pancakes. And if all else fails, there's always chocolate.
