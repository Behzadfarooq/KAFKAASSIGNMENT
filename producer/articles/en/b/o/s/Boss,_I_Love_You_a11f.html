Boss is a 2006 Telugu film directed by V.N. Aditya. It stars Akkineni Nagarjuna, Nayantara, Poonam Bhajwa, and Shriya Saran. The film's score and soundtrack will be composed by Kalyani Malik and Harry Anand. Cinematography for the movie is handled by Siva Kumar while editing is being handled by Marthand K. Venkatesh. The film was released on September 27, 2006.

[edit] Plot

Spoiler warning: Plot and/or ending details follow.

Gopala Krishna (Akkineni Nagarjuna) is owner of GK constructions. Anuradha (Nayantara) is his secretary. She is recruited by Gopala Krishna himself in an unusual manner. She lives up to the expectations by doing very good in her job. She gradually falls in love with her Boss. When she is almost about to express her love to Gopala Krishna, he gives her a warning for work related. She decides to leave her job which in turn converts into a 1 month notice. Sruthi (Poonam Bhajwa) later replaces for Anuradha. The film takes a turn when Anuradha expresses her love to Gopala Krishna. She gets a rejection.

Spoilers end here.

[edit] Trivia

  • This is Nagarjuna's and V.N. Aditya's second time working together after delivering the hit Nenunnanu.
  • This is Nagarjuna's third time acting with Shriya Saran after working with her with hit flicks, Santosham & Nenunnanu.
