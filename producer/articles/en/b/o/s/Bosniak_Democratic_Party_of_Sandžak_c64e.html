The Bosniak Democratic Party of Sanjak (Bosnian: Bošnjačka Demokratska Stranka Sandžaka; Serbian: Бошњачка Демократска Странка Санџака or Bošnjačka Demokratska Stranka Sandžaka) is a political party in the Sandžak region of Serbia. At the final legislative elections, 28 december 2003, the party elected one seat on the list of the Democratic Party.
