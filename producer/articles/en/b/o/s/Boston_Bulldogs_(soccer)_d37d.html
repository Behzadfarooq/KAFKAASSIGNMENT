Boston Bulldogs (soccer)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

For other uses, see Boston Bulldogs.

The Boston Bulldogs were a soccer club in the A-League in 1999 and 2000. They played in the USL Pro Soccer League in 2001 before folding. The team started out as the Worcester Wildfire in 1997.
