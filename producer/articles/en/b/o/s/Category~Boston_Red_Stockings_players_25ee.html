Players for the Boston Red Stockings during the five seasons of the National Association of Professional Base Ball Players ("National Association" baseball league), 1871 â€“ 1875.

The Boston club continued to field professional teams in the National League, 1876 to 1952, before relocating to Milwaukee and later to Atlanta.

These players are commonly considered "major leaguers" although MLB does not count the NA as a major league.
