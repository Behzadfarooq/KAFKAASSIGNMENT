Boss Up Entertainment
Founded 2004
Founder Loon
Distributing label Cleopatra Records
Genre(s) Hiphop
Country US
Web address http://bossupentertainment.com/

Boss Up Entertainment is a record label founded by former Bad Boy hip hop artist Loon in 2004. Their first release was the Boss Up mixtape Vol. 1 by King Tee released in November 2005. The label also signed King Tee himself.
