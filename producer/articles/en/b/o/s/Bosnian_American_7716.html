Bosnian Americans are citizens of the United States who are of Bosnian ancestry.

[edit] Notable Bosnian Americans

  • Muhamed Sacirbey, lawyer, businessman, and former ambassador.
  • Vedad Osmanovic, college basketball player
  • Damir Markota, NBA Player
  • Sulejman Talovic, mall murderer from Utah.
  • Amer Delic, Tennis player
