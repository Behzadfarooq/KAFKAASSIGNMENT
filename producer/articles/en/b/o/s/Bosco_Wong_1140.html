Bosco Wong Chung Chak (Simplified Chinese: 黄宗泽 Traditional Chinese: 黃宗澤 Pinyin: Huáng Zōngzé), born December 13, 1980, is a Hong Kong TVB actor.

[edit] Brief Biography

This young actor's debut came in 2003, where he played a minor role in the TVB drama series "Aqua Heroes". In the same year, Bosco went on to play bigger roles in other TVB series such as "Triumph in the Skies" (衝上雲霄) and "Find The Light" (英雄﹐刀﹐少年). Wong also played the title role in "Wong Fei Hung - Master of Kung Fu" (我師傅係黃飛鴻) in 2004.

In 2005 he made his mark as a solid dramatic and comedic actor in the popular series "War of In-Laws" (我的野蠻奶奶) opposite Myolie Wu, and Hong Kong acting legend Liza Wang. War of In-Laws received high ratings and increased Bosco's popularity after its airing. Notably, Bosco's role in "War of In-Laws" earned him TVB's Most Improved Actor Award in late 2005.

Bosco Wong in 2006 was laughed at for following the footsteps of Ron Ng. BMA decided not to extend his contract because of confusion caused within the two.
