Bosco Hogan

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Bosco Hogan is an Irish actor of stage, screen and television.

He is best-known as Dr. Michael Ryan on Ballykissangel. He played Jonathan Harker in the 1977 television version of Count Dracula with Louis Jourdan. He appeared in A Cry from Heaven by Vincent Woods at the Abbey Theatre in the summer of 2005.
