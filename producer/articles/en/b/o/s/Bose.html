Bose is a Bengali, German, or Italian surname

In Bengal, the "Bose" surname belongs to the Kayastha clan of India and Bangladesh.

People with the surname "Bose":

  • Subhash Chandra Bose (aka Netaji), a leader of the Indian independence movement and founder of the Indian National Army.
  • Amar Bose, MIT professor; founder of the Bose Corporation
  • Jagdish Chandra Bose, Bengali physicist
  • Satyendra Nath Bose, Bengali physicist
  • Buddhadeva Bose, Bengali writer
  • Rahul Bose, Indian actor
  • Vivian Bose, among the founders of Scouting in India
  • Lucia Bosé, Italian actress that married the Spanish bullfighter Luis Miguel Dominguín.
  • Miguel Bosé, Spanish singer, son of Lucia and L.M. Dominguín.
  • Julius von Bose (1809-1894), a noble Prussian General
  • Tony Bose, Traditional pocketknife craftsman
  • Bose (Guangxi), a city in China
  • Sterling Bose, 1920s jazz musician

Other uses:

  • Bose-Einstein condensate, a state of matter predicted by Satyendra Bose
  • Bose Corporation, an audio company named after Amar Bose
