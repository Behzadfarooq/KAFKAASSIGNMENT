[edit] Plot

Spoiler warning: Plot and/or ending details follow.

The Prince of Ishkebar, Sanjay, visits the Tipton. He is fed up with royalty so he enlists Cody to pretend to be him during a press conference with the mayor of Boston. Sanjay goes with Zack to the mall where he is arrested for shoplifting clothing because he doesn't know any better. Zack calls Mr. Moseby who is at the press conference and is forced to tell him the truth. He comes down with Carey and Sanjay's assistant to bail them out. Sanjay demands more time to be a kid and his assistant finally agrees.

Meanwhile, London thinks that she saw a UFO outside her window so she enlists Maddie to wait for the aliens to return. London has a dream that aliens visited her and threatened to blow up Earth,but she gave them skin cream and she chants in her dream "I saved the world".. Maddie points out the UFO is a balloon from London's birthday party and thanks her for inviting her. London then realizes that Maddie has been using her.

[edit] Trivia

  • The episode title is a reference to the movie Roman Holiday.
  • US season one finale.
  • Ishkebar is a fictional country.
  • Zack is in jail for the first time.
  • Maddie sarcastically thanks London for not inviting her to her party. However in the episode Not So Suite 16 they argue about having their parties on the same day. Maybe it is that this episode is in the first season where as the other is in the second.
