Boscoppa

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Boscoppa is a large suburban area of St Austell, Cornwall's largest town. Its situation in the north-easterly direction of the town places it on the main road between Bodmin & St Austell. The area is home to St Luke's Parish Church which is housed in the local school. Boscoppa is served by two local bus routes; Western Greyhound's St Austell to Eden Project 527 service & Truronian's St Austell - Lostwithiel T24 service. However, due to the low-frequency service these buses provide, many residents commute into St Austell by car, causing significant traffic delays at peak hours.
