"Boss Luffy Returns! A Dream or Reality Lottery Trouble" is the 291st episode of the anime adaptation of the manga One Piece. It originally aired in Japan on December 17, 2006.

[edit] Plot

Spoiler warning: Plot and/or ending details follow.

[edit] Summary

In this filler episode, the Strawhat crew and Buggy's crew are in an alternate reality based on feudal Japan, called Jipang. Buggy tries to sell Orika as a slave to make up for Pandaman's debts, who Buggy thinks is Orika's father. To make matters worse, he has hired the corrupt Obugyo Mr. 3 to catch Luffy in the act of breaking the law trying to rescue her.
