Bostancı

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Bostancı is a neighborhood located on the Anatolian side of Istanbul, Turkey and which fronts the Sea of Marmara and is not far from the Princes' Islands. From the Bostancı shore, the two islands of Burgazada and Kınalıada can be seen. Bostancı is also a very crowded intersection place for the Asian side of Istanbul with the city buses, sea buses, ships, trains and dolmus.
