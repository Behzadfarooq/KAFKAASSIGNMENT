Bo Van Pelt

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Bo Van Pelt (born May 16, 1975) is an American professional golfer. He was born in Richmond, Indiana. He plays on the PGA Tour but he has not had any victories, although he has finished third twice and has featured in the top 100 of the Official World Golf Rankings.

Bo and his wife Carrie have three children together: Olivia (5), Trace (3), and Crew (newborn)

Bo Van Pelt has two sisters, Lori Juerling and Kelly Bomba.[citation needed] He also has a step-sister, Bryn-Ellyn Van Pelt.

He won the Omaha Classic on the Nationwide Tour in 2003.
