[edit] External links

The External Links section of this article seems to be attracting spam. There are naturally few sites with relavent material about Bice and many sites that are hoping to make a few bucks selling ads or soliciting donations. I'm going to informally adopt the attitude of deleting any new links that are not either (a) obviously valuable or (b) somehow justified by the editor in the edit summary. Dystopos 21:37, 19 July 2005 (UTC)

You have my full support on this, given that proper time is taken to vette out each link as it is added. Hall Monitor 21:39, 19 July 2005 (UTC)
Well. That was my proposal, not to take the time to follow a bunch of spam links to fan sites with pop-ups and other junk. We've got plenty of links to fan sites already so I'm proposing that we close the door unless an editor can explain why another link is worth having. (Of course you or anyone else is free to investigate and report, or even just to re-add it if you think I've deleted one unfairly) Dystopos 22:47, 19 July 2005 (UTC)
