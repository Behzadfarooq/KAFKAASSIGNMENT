Bo White

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Bo White is an American actor appearing in Christopher Larkin's 1974 groundbreaking film, A Very Natural Thing, the gay alternative to Love Story (1970). He plays Jason, a divorced photographer, who meets a lonely English Literature teacher David, Robert Joel, during New York's 1973 Gay Pride celebration in the second half of the film and show the potential to form a long term relationship. His brief filmography includes: Blue Summer (1973), Bible! (1974), and Urban Playground (2002).
