Bo Stief

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Bo Stief (born 15 October 1946) is a Danish jazz and rock bassist, composer, and arranger.

He has worked or recorded with Miles Davis, Stan Getz, Dizzy Gillespie, Dexter Gordon, Johnny Griffin, Jackie McLean, and Ben Webster, among many other prominent jazz musicians.
