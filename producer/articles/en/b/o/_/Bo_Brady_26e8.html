Bo Brady

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Bo Brady (full name Beauregard Aurelius Brady) (born 1961) is a fictional character on the television soap opera Days of our Lives. The role has been played by Peter Reckell (1983 to 1987, 1990 to 1992, 1995 to present) and Robert Kelker-Kelly (1992 to 1995)
