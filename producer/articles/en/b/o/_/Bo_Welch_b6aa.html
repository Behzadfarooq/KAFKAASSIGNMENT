Bo Welch

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Bo Welch (born March 19, 1963) is a former motion picture production designer turned director. He worked as a production designer on the Tim Burton films Edward Scissorhands, Beetlejuice, and Batman Returns, as well as A Little Princess and Men in Black, among other films. He made his directorial debut with The Cat in the Hat (film). He is married to actress Catherine O'Hara. His older brother, Bob Welch, is a former pitcher with the Los Angeles Dodgers and Oakland Athletics.
