Bo "Bosse" Larsson (born May 5, 1944) is a former Swedish football midfielder/striker.

Bosse Larsson was one of the national team profiles during the 1970's. In total he got 70 caps, and played at the 1970 FIFA World Cup, 1974 FIFA World Cup and 1978 FIFA World Cup.

Most of his club career he played for Malmö FF where he won the league championship several times. Between 1966 and 1969 he played professionally for VfB Stuttgart. He was awarded Guldbollen (the golden ball) in 1965 and 1973.

Today he leads a quiet life in Malmö.
