Bovenau is a small town situated in the district of Rendsburg-Eckernförde, in the German state of Schleswig-Holstein.

Bovenau has 1,046 inhabitants (2003) and lies 10 km east from Rendsburg. The current mayor is Jürgen Liebsch.
