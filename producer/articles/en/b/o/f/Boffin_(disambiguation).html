Boffin may refer to any of the following:

  • Danny Boffin, a former Belgian football international
  • A boffin is British slang for a scientist
  • Boffin is a hobbit family in J. R. R. Tolkien's The Lord of the Rings
  • Boffin is a variant spelling of the Welsh family name Baughan
  • Boffin is a platform game released on the Acorn Electron and BBC Micro.
  • Boffin was the nickname given by the Royal Navy during World War II to the Mark V mountings for the Oerlikon 20 mm cannons up-gunned with the Bofors 40 mm gun
