Bofors IK
Founded 1978
Home ice Nobelhallen
Based in Karlskoga, Sweden
Colours Blue, White, Red
League Allsvenskan

Bofors IK, is an ice-hockey club from Karlskoga in Sweden. They are currently playing in the second highest league in Sweden, Allsvenskan.
