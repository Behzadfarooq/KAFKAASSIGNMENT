Bobby Vinton's Greatest Hits is a 12-track collection that contains the first 12 singles by Bobby Vinton; it was released in 1964, just a few months after his album There! I've Said It Again. The three songs "Let's Kiss and Make Up," "Trouble Is My Middle Name," and "Tell Me Why" make their first appearance on an album.
