Bobbie Johnson (born c. 1945) is a former beauty queen from Alexandria, Virginia who held the Miss USA 1964 title and has competed in the Miss Universe pageant..

After winning the Miss District of Columbia USA crown, Johnson, from Alexandria, Virginia went on to become the first representative from the District of Columbia to achieve the title of Miss USA, at age 19. She would be the only titleholder from the district until Shauntay Hinton won the crown in 2002.

Johnson went on to compete in the Miss Universe 1964 pageant, where she made the semi-finals.

In later life, Johnson worked as an applications engineer in the computer department of General Electric.[1]
