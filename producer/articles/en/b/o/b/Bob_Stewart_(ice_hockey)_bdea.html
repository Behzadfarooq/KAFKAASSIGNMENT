Bob Stewart (Born - November 10, 1950 in Charlottetown, Prince Edward Island, Canada) is a retired Canadian Professional Hockey defenceman who played 9 seasons in the National Hockey League for the Boston Bruins, California Golden Seals, California Seals, Cleveland Barons, St. Louis Blues and Pittsburgh Penguins.

[edit] Playing career

Stewart was drafted in the 1st Round, 13th overall by the Boston Bruins in the 1970 NHL Entry Draft.
