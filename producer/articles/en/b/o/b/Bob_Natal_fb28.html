Robert Marcel Natal (born November 13, 1965, in Long Beach, California) was a Major League Baseball catcher. He is an alumnus of the University of California, San Diego.

Drafted by the Montreal Expos in the 13th round of the 1987 MLB amateur draft, Natal would make his Major League Baseball debut with the Montreal Expos on July 18, 1992, and appear in his final game on September 26, 1997.

Natal was a member of the inaugural Florida Marlins team that began play in Major League Baseball in 1993.
