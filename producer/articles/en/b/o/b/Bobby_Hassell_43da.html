Robert Hassell (born June 4, 1980 in Derby) is a professional English football player currently wearing the number 2 shirt for Barnsley.

Hassell started his career with Mansfield Town in 1996 when he signed as a trainee. He made his first team debut in September 1997 against Chester City. Bobby spent 8 years with the Stags, making 181 appearances scoring 3 goals and making an important appearance in the Division Three Play Off Final at the Millenium Stadium in May 2004.

Bobby joined Barnsley in the summer of 2004, making 43 appearances in his first season for the Tykes playing in both right back and central defensive positions. Despite missing parts of the 2005-06 season due to injury, Hassell played an important part in getting Barnsley to the League One Play Off Finals at the Millennium Stadium in Cardiff on May 27, 2006. At the time of writing, Bobby has 81 appearances and 2 goals to his name at Barnsley.
