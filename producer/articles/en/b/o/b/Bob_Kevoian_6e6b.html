Bob Kevoian (born December 2, 1950 in Los Angeles, California) hosts the nationally-syndicated radioshow The Bob & Tom Show together with his partner, Tom Griswold.

He is a 1973 graduate of California State University, Long Beach and began his radio career in Petoskey, Michigan in 1979 and came to WFBQ Radio in Indianapolis in early 1983. He married his current wife, Becky Martin, on April 2, 2005. He has a son named Toby [1] and two stepsons named Wade and Joey.

Bob participated in the Bob and Tom Radio: The Comedy Tour shows recorded in Anderson, Indiana.

[edit] Trivia

  • Kevoian is almost always seen wearing a Los Angeles Dodgers hat as a tribute to his father, who was a long time employee of the Dodgers.
  • Bob is also an avid guitar player and writes some of the lyrics and music for the show's comedic parodies.
  • Kevoian's brother-in-law (Becky's brother) is world-famous tuba player Rex Martin.
  • His mother was on Let's Make A Deal and won a Brunswick pool table, which she traded for cases of oyster stew.
