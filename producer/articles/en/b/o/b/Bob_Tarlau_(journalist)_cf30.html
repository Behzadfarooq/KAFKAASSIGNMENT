Bob Tarlau (journalist)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Robert (Bob) Tarlau (born 1944), lives in Los Angeles and is a journalist and senior producer for KTTV (FOX11) a Fox television network station. Bob is also an international correspondent for Melbourne (Australia) radio station 3AW.

[edit] Awards and recognition

In 2000 Bob was recognised by the "Greater Los Angeles Chapter of the Society of Professional Journalists" for his record of accomplishments over the course of a career in broadcast journalism that now exceeds 40 years.

Bob is also a multiple Los Angeles area Emmy winner.

[edit] Family

Bob is married to Barbara with two children, Amanda and David. The Tarlaus also have a black standard poodle "Cassie," and three cats, "Morrie," "Sheba" and "Gracie."
