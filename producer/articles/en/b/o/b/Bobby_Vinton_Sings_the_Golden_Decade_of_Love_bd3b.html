Bobby Vinton Sings the Golden Decade of Love is a two-LP collection of previously recorded songs by Vinton, released in 1975 by Epic Records. It reached #154 on the Billboard Hot 200 list of popular albums.

This collection contains mainly (apparently random-picked) cover songs and album tracks. "Over the Mountain (Across the Sea)" (which was a hit for Johnnie & Joe before Vinton) is the only single to come from this collection.
