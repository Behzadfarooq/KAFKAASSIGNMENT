Bob Snider is a singer/songwriter from Nova Scotia, Canada.

[edit] Biography

[edit] Style

Bob Snider is a folk musician, and has written a vast array of songs. When performing, he plays only his own material, with one exception being at a show during the Bear River Reunion, where he played a cover of an old blues tune.

[edit] Discography

  • Live at the Free Times Cafe (1989)
  • Caterwaul & Doggerel (1995)
  • Poetreason: The Songs of Bob Snider (1996)
  • Words and Pictures (1997)
  • Stealin' Home (2002)
