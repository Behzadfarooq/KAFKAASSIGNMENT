Bobby Morley

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

For the British film actor, see Robert Morley

Bob (or Bobby) Morley (born 20 December 1984, Kyneton, Victoria, Australia) is an Australian actor.

He is half-Filipino on his mother's side. He currently plays the role of Drew Curtis on the Seven Network drama series Home and Away. He has appeared in several stage plays in Australia and prior to Home and Away he appeared in the horror film Dead Harvest.
