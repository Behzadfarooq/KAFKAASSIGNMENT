Bobby Kildea

Bobby Kildea plays bass and guitar in the Scottish indie band Belle & Sebastian. He joined the band in 2001 to replace departing bassist Stuart David, and had previously been in V-Twin. He is the band's only Irish member and is notable for his laidback demeanor and long hair.

Though he goes by the nickname "Belfast" in the band, he was actually born in Bangor.
