Bob's Discount Furniture is a regional chain of furniture stores that began in New England, United States. As of 2007, Bob's operates 27 stores, including 5 in-store outlet centers, and several distribution centers. Stores are located in Connecticut (10), Massachusetts (6), Maine (1), New Hampshire (4), New Jersey (4), New York (1), and Rhode Island (1).

The company was founded in 1991, and is the 33rd largest furniture retailer in the United States as of 2005.

Bob Kaufman, President, and Cathy Poulin, Public Relations Director, supply their own quirky talent for the company’s ubiquitous low-production-quality television advertisements. As of 2005, Bob's was the largest television advertiser in Connecticut, and one of the largest in Massachusetts. Bob's marketing campaign positions the retailer as a family-friendly chain with everyday low prices.
