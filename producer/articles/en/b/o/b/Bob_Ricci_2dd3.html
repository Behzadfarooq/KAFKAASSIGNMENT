Bob Ricci is an American Parody and original Comedy Music Artist. Half of his songs are original words and music, and half are parodies of popular music (meaning he changes up the words to already popular songs). He is most noted for his song; "Internet Love Song" or "She Frickin' Blocked Me", a parody of "She Hates Me" by Puddle of Mudd.
