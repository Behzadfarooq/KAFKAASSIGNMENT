My name is Bob K and this is just a filler page while I get used to formatting on Wikipedia.

I was born in Perak many moons ago and grew up in Teluk Intan but am currently reside in UEP Subang Jaya or USJ as it is better known. I participate quite actively in the activities of the community portal, USJ.com.my and also maintain a blog at bobjots.org.

I am currently working with an NGO known as the Centre for Independent Journalism which is currently the secretariat for a campaign to enact a Freedom of Information legislation in Malaysia.

[edit] Interests In Wikipedia

I am primarily interested in the Malaysian entries in Wikipedia. I believe that accurate and comprehensive information in the public domain will contribute towards developing a civil society and I have a vested interest in seeing a civil society develop in Malaysia.

Considering the fact that not much objective information is available about Malaysia online, I hope to be able to contribute to this dearth of information and allow my contributions to be tempered by peer moderating and constructive dialogue.
