Bob Mancini is the head coach and general manager of the Saginaw Spirit hockey club of the Ontario Hockey League. He also has coached in the NCAA ranks at Michigan Tech and Ferris State University.

He has appeared on the Colbert Report on December 5, 2006 to discuss his team's current three game losing streak, again on January 25th, 2007 to discuss a game against rival team Oshawa Generals, and March 22, 2007 to discuss his team's upcoming playoff series.
