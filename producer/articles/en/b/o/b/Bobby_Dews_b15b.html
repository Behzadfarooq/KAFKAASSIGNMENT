Bobby Dews, Jr. (born March 23, 1939 in Clinton, Iowa, USA) is a former infielder in Minor League Baseball and a coach in Major League Baseball. He began working with the Braves' organization in 1974 and was with the team until 2006 when he retired from being the Braves bullpen coach to become a roving coach. He works with the Braves at home games and works with the minor league teams while the major league club is on the road.

Dews played baseball and basketball at Georgia Tech.[citation needed]
