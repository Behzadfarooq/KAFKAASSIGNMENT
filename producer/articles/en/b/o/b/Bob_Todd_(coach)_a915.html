Bob Todd is the head coach of the Ohio State baseball program. In his 19 seasons as the head coach of the Buckeyes, his teams have compiled a 726-363-2 record. He previously coached Kent State to a 124-82 record. He also has a career 335-175 Big Ten Conference record. He has guided Ohio State to two 50 win seasons, in 1991, and 1999. From 1991-1995, his teams have won the Big Ten championship for five consecutive years.

[edit] Awards and Honors

  • Big Ten Coach of the Year(1989,1994, 1999, 2001)
  • National Coach of the Year(1994)
