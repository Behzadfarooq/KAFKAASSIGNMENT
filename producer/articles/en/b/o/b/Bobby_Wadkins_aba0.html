Robert Edwin Wadkins (born July 26, 1951) is an American golfer.

Wadkins was born in Richmond, Virginia. He turned pro in 1973. He never won on the PGA Tour despite six runner-up finishes, but he did win on the European Tour and the Japan Golf Tour. Since turning fifty he has played on the Champions Tour, where he has won four times, including one senior major, the 2006 Senior Players Championship.

Bobby Wadkins elder brother Lanny won 21 times on the PGA Tour, including the 1977 PGA Championship.
