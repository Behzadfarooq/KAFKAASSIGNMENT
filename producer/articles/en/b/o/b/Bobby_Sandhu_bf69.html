Bobby Sandhu is an artist from Punjab, India. He is known for his pencil portraits, though he works in all the art forms: both traditional and digital. In Punjab he is more known as a TV personality as he has been hosting the longest running Live Dial-In show by the name of Dial - E -Punjab, in the history of Punjabi Television.

[edit] Biography

Bobby was born on 29th May, 1981 in Bathinda (Punjab), India. During the college his interests in Theatre and Fine Arts led him to participate in University level competitions and he participated in Mimicry, Collage and Poster Making at University level and he won the Mimicry and collage making events for 5 years straight at Punjabi University Patiala. He also represented and won first place for Punjabi University Patiala thrice at India's National Interversity Youth Festival in the event of Mimicry.

In studies he ranked first in the University in both Graduation and Post Graduation. Immediately after he finished his Post Graduation that he majored in English Literature, he got a job as a lecturer of English at P.M.N. College, Rajpura. He also started research on Popular Selected Broadway Plays (1945-2000): A Postmodern Perspective. After one year at the college, he resigned from the job to pursue his artistic interests.

[edit] Traditional and Digital artist

After he left the job, he started drawing with pencils more extensively then ever. As a self taught artist he made a name for himself as a proficient pencil artist. His initial gallery can be found here at www.bobbyrock.deviantart.com. He is mostly known for his automobile pencil portraits, but his gallery also includes pencil portraits of Hollywood celebrities. Apart from pencil portraits the gallery has many water and oil color, cartoons and caricatures, collage and Winamp skins to his credit.

Lately he learnt digital software and launched works done in Adobe Photoshop, Corel photo-Paint, Draw and Adobe Macromedia Flash. The digital works display a good control over the strokes, especially when he claims to never have used a tablet. He also moved into web designing and launched his website www.bobbysandhulive.com, that he designed on his own. Professionally he takes commissions for pencil portraits, winamp skins, digital graphics and web designs. His work has been featured in many Indian Magazines.

[edit] Television

On television he started doing Dial-E-Punjab, a show at Zee Punjabi, the biggest Punjabi channel. The show is recorded Live and is telecasted all over the world every Saturday Night. The show focuses on talking on the phone and playing the songs in between. During his two years on TV he did many events with the biggest names of the Punjabi Music Industry. Though he wishes not to be known for what he does on TV, but what he does with pencils and computers.
