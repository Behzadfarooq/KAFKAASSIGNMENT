Bob Lynch (born 12 July 1958) is a British small press comics artist who produced minicomics during the 1980s and 1990s. His self published work includes the Sav Sadness stories and Behold The Hamster. His work has also appeared in Escape Magazine, MacFormat, City Limits (London magazine), Buddies a gay humour comic similar to Viz and Fast Fiction. Also he did some drawing in Supplement. He was interviewed in Peter Pavement's comic. His first appearance on television was Blue Peter.

Bob Lynch currently works in the civil service as a Facilities Assistant.
