[edit] The Lakeland Fells

Although I live some distance away I take any opportunity to go fellwalking in the English Lake District. I am, apparently, obsessed. I find it difficult to see this as a negative characteristic- more a means of providing respite care for my family. I've completed Wainwright and am now working through Birkett, together with another list. I can't mention this due to WP:OR, but if you think about 1,000 ft nuttalls you'll get the gist.
