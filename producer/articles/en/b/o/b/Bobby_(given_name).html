Bobby (given name)

Bobby is a diminutive of the Christian names Robert (♂) and Roberta (♀) (also spelling Bobbie)

Bobby is the given name of:

  • Bobby Abreu, Professional baseball player for the Houston Astros, Philadelphia Phillies and New York Yankees
  • Bobby Allison, Nascar legend
  • Bobby Bonds, former professional baseball player, father of Barry Bonds
  • Bobby Bonilla, former baseball player
  • Bobby Brown, American singer
  • Bobby Clark, Alaska travel guide writer
  • Bobby Cox, manager of the Atlanta Braves
  • Bobby Fischer, chess grandmaster
  • Bobby Hamilton Jr., race car driver
  • Bobby Hull, former professional hockey player for the Chicago Blackhawks
  • Bobby Knight, basketball coach
  • Bobby L. Rush, Democrat Representative in Illinois
  • Bobby Labonte, a NASCAR Driver
  • Bobby Ologun, a foreign television performer in Japan, a Mixed martial arts fighter
  • Bobby Orr, former professional hockey player for the Boston Bruins
  • Bobby Rahal, race car driver and owner
  • Bobby Smith (hockey player), former NHL player for the Montreal Canadiens and Minnesota North Stars
  • Bobby Valentino (singer), an American R&B Singer.
  • Bobby Hill player for the San Diego Padres
