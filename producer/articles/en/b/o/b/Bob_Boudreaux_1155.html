Bob Boudreaux is a Vietnam veteran and former weekend news anchor for KTRK-TV in Houston, Texas.[1]

Boudreaux arrived in the U.S. Army through the Reserve Officers' Training Corps program, and was commisioned as a 2nd Lieutenant. During his four years on active duty, Boudreaux flew with the 155th Assault Helicopter Company of the 1st Aviation Brigade in the Vietnam War. He attained the rank of Captain whilst serving as a flight instructor at the U.S. Army Primary Helicopter School.[1]

[edit] Decorations

Boudreaux earned several military decorations as an aircraft commander and aviation section leader, including two awards of the Distinguished Flying Cross, the Bronze Star, the Purple Heart, and the Vietnamese Cross of Gallantry.[1]
