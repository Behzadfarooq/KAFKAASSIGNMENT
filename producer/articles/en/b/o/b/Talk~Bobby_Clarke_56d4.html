[edit] Candy

This thing about hiding candy in his uniform's pockets seems pretty far-fetched to me. For one thing, uniforms don't have pockets. Anyway, unless someone comes up with a source, that part should be deleted. Baconpatroller 04:46, 2 June 2006 (UTC)

Technically there are pockets in the pants that hold the thigh plates, at least there were in circa '80s equipment. Not that they'd be useful for easy candy access. Occasionally I'd stuff items in the cuff of my gloves (a puck, roll of tape, mouth guard), but usually not during play. I did read somewhere back when that the trainer had a tube of glucose in his kit, just in case, but it was never used. --J Clear 14:27, 22 October 2006 (UTC)
