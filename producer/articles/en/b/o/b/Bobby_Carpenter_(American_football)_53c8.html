Bobby Carpenter (born August 1, 1983 in Lancaster, Ohio) is an American football linebacker, drafted from Ohio State University by the Dallas Cowboys with the 18th pick in the first round of the 2006 NFL Draft.

He is the son of former New York Giants running back Rob Carpenter. Carpenter, a LCMS Christian, played at Lancaster High School where his father is head coach. Bobby has three younger brothers, Jonathan (a football player at the University of Cincinnati), Georgie and Nathan.

[edit] High School

Carpenter attended Lancaster High School in Lancaster, Ohio. In addition to being a football standout, Carpenter was also a member of the swim team. During his senior year, Carpenter was selected as All-Ohio first team (football), and also went to the District Finals in swimming.
