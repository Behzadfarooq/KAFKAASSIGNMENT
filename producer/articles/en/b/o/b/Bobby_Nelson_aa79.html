Bobby Nelson( ボビー　ネルソン ) is a fictional character designed for the ADK's Aggressors of Dark Kombat fighting game.

[edit] About Bobby

  • Age: 13 years
  • Height: 148 cm
  • Weight: 46 kg
  • Grappling Weight: 68 kg

Bobby is a teenage African-American basketball fan in Japan. Holding the American dream in his chest, Bobby has a big dream in his small body: to become a big star. So, Bobby goes on a journey to demostrate he's the best, in waiting for being discovered. He is the smallest character in the game.

[edit] Special Moves

  • Spinning Attack
  • Jet Uppercut
  • Flying Triple Kick
  • Big Burn Attack

[edit] Appearances

  • Aggressors of Dark Kombat

[edit] Trivia

  • His nickname is The Brown Bullet.
  • Sheen Genus is his final enemy in his story.
  • In all his moves and poses, Bobby doesn't release his basketball, even if he grabs some weapon.
  • He is a somewhat stereotypical character and was not used in any other SNK fighting title, unlike many other of their fighting game characters.
  • He appears recently with Sean Matsuda (from Capcom's Street Fighter III) as an Action card in SNK vs. Capcom: Card Fighters DS. He's one of the revived AODK characters in this game, along with Sheen and Kisarah Westfield.
