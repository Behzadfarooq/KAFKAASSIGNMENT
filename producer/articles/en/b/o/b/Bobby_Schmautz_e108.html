Robert "Bobby" Schmautz (born March 28, 1945 in Saskatoon, Saskatchewan, Canada) is a retired Canadian ice hockey forward.

Schmautz started his National Hockey League career with the Chicago Blackhawks in 1968. Schmautz would also play for the Boston Bruins, Vancouver Canucks, Colorado Rockies, and Edmonton Oilers.
