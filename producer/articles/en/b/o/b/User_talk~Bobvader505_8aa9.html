[edit] Hightower Robotics

Though your enthusiasm for your team, your school and for this page is evident I believe that the subject matter is not significant enough to warrant inclusion. I refer you specifically to the first point in Wikipedia:List of bad article ideas which states that it is probably a bad idea to begin a pages about "...Yourself or your organization - including a band of which you are a member or employee." I am unconvinced that your club has any recognition outside of your school and the comptetition which it competes in. The fact that you have written this article yourself, in the first person, is further evidence to the likelyhood that this page is not encyclopaedic but actually promotional. Therefore I believe this article should not be included in WP. This is not to denegrate your efforts, they are noteworthy, but to maintain high standards on the website.
