[edit] BoBoBo-Bo Bo-BoBo

This is the cool and incomprehensible hero, swarthy and musclebound, sporting sunglasses and a large golden afro. His hair is 24-karat gold but is usually said to be yellow or blonde; some viewers even argue about its true color. He is called Bo-bobo for short. He refuses to let people call him Bo! He fights with his nose hair using the Hanage Shinken (sometimes "Fist of the Nose Hair", other times "Snot Fou-You"), as well as simply confusing his enemies. His only weakness, which he discovered while visiting the world of Blabsalot, is the fact that he doesn't have as many deadly attacks. He believes that the Fist of the Nose Hair is sacred and that he shouldn't overuse it. He has three brothers and one sister.

[edit] History

[edit] Young Bobobo

As a kid Bobobo first learned of his ability to hear the voices of hair. At age 7, Bobobo was taken hostage by the hair hunters when his father, a humanoid-like hairball named Tsuyoshi (Pappy in dub), gave him up when the Hair Hunters came to their house and urged him to take on the Chrome Dome Empire. His first mentor was Eboji who taught him how every strand of hair was alive and doesn't like being teased. His second mentor was Master Juice (a juice can), who taught him and Captain Battleship at the time. Bobobo was also friends with Jelly Jiggler.

[edit] Bobobo and Battleship

On the day of the end of the Hair Kingdom, Captain Battleship stated that he was glad that the Hair Kingdom ended due to his lack of acknowledgment. This made Bobobo angry and ended their friendship.

  • Fistful Arm Pit Hair ("Burning Arm Pit Hair" in the Japanese manga): When unable to use his nose hair, Bo-bobo slams the enemy with his armpit hair. Used against Hagen.
  • Are You Alright?!: Bo-bobo constantly whips his enemy with both of his nose hairs, while constantly asking the enemy if he or she is all right. Once the enemy says how they feel, Bo-bobo stops attacking and explains how fighting is wrong.
  • Fistful Nose Hair - Bo-bobo slams the enemy with his nose hair.

  • Snot Fo-You: Bababa-ba Ba-baba

  • My Piano Recital in D-Minor: Attacks the enemy with a piano while Don Patch plays the piano (horribly). Used against Pana.
  • Bunch A Bananas Defense: Bo-bobo is coated with bananas.
  • Bunch A Bananas Laser: After entering banana form, Bo-bobo shoots beams from his eyes. Used against Sonic and Super Rabbit.
  • Super Fist of the Nose Hair: It's Snack and Tea Time: All recently defeated allies or enemies comes back to have snacks and tea with Bo-bobo while listening to HPSJ on the radio.
  • Super Fist of the Nose Hair: Red Card: Bo-bobo looks at a red card then summons a Mystery Box where almost anything can happen to anyone who falls in.
  • The Strong Prey Upon the Weak: A lion comes out of Bo-bobo's afro, then mauls the enemy. Used against Super Rabbit.
  • Super Fist of the Nose Hair: Sumo Bungie: Bo-bobo turns into a sumo wrestler and attacks the enemy with high-speed attacks. Used against Sonic, Unknown Sad Sack, and Super Rabbit.
  • Super Fist of the Nosehair: Superflash (ス-パ-フラシ): Bo-bobo uses a desk lamp to produce a light brighter than even the Black Sun. Used against J.
  • Super Dinosaur Kick: Bo-bobo swings then jumps off of a rope dressed as a dinosaur and kicks the enemy. Used against Giga.
  • Super Fist of School Curtain Gaurd: Bo-bobo uses a black school curtain as a shield. Used against Giga.
  • Tofu Art: Bo-bobo attacks the enemy with tofu. Used against Giga.
  • Super Fist of Nosehair: Ruff-Ruff Dog Ring Around the Poesy: Bo-bobo becomes a dog and spins towards the enemy. Used against Giga.
  • Super Fist of the Nose Hair: Best Friends Forever Umbrella: Bo-bobo shields him and his allies with a parasol.
  • Super Fist of the Nose Hair: Bo-bobo Custom Elevator: Bo-bobo summons an elevator that travels to any location.
  • Batting Practice Style Landskape Painting: My 2005 Edition: Bo-bobo becomes a portrait of himself batting baseballs at the enemy with a rainbow vapor stream following in its wake. Used againsed Giga.
  • Super Fist of the Nose Hair: Shatter the Seal: Bo-bobo's nose hair becomes a green dragon that awakens others who vandalize the universe until breathing fire upon Bo-bobo, Don Patch, and Jelly Jiggler.
  • Super Fist of the Nose Hair: Angry Bear Wedding Party: The enemy falls on a bear's wedding cake during a boat wedding party and is attacked by the stuffed bears (including the groom, who takes on the form of an acual bear).
  • Four Sisters: Freedom Fighters: Bo-bobo summons three sisters (and a male who thinks he's a girl) to fight. Used against Wild Wister.
  • Super Fist of the Nose Hair: Bo-bobo Banquet: Bo-bobo plants seed in the enemy's cloths that bloom into flowers that resemble Bo-bobo and iritates the enemy when exposed to music and explodes when it ends. Used against Wild Wister.
  • Super Fist of the Nose Hair: As the Crow Flies: Bo-bobo turns into a speedy crow that carries the enemy back in time to the creation of the wheel were they are punched by the creator. Used against Wild Wister.
  • Super Fist of the Nose Hair: 3: Bo-bobo turns into a giant three with power that exeeds the sun.

[edit] Trial Attacks

Attacks that are extensively long involving different themes and events:

  • Welcome to Planet Judge: Summons the powers of each planet. Used to defeat Torpedo Girl, but Don Patch, Jelly Jiggler, and even Bo-bobo are affected.
    • Mercury: Summons a flood and a lightning storm.
    • Venus: Drops a boulder on enemies.
    • Earth: Pushes them off a cliff into a pit of lava.
    • Mars: Bo-bobo eats really hot food and then breathing fire to burn Torpedo Girl, Don Patch, and Jelly Jiggler (but Beauty says that the food was mild).
    • Jupiter: Bo-bobo dressed as Santa Claus, running over them with a sled and reindeer.
    • Saturn: Don Patch enlarging his spikes and then spinning to attack Bo-bobo, Torpedo Girl, and Jelly Jiggler.
    • Uranus: Jelly Jiggler whacking Bo-bobo, Don Patch, and Torpedo Girl with his "nu" handkerchief.
    • Neptune: Bo-bobo using a spotlight to lift up Service Man's blanket so he flashes the others.
    • Pluto: Pluto coming down and causing a tsunami that engulfs Torpedo Girl.
  • Bo-bobo Roulette: Bo-bobo appears on a roulette wheel (Don Patch in the last spin) to randomly choose someone to be attacked in very random and ridiculous ways. Used against Beep, Haou, Megafan, and Don Patch. (Jelly Jiggler had 2 spots but never got chosen)
    • Meteorites Will Fall All Around You: This is the first Bo-bobo Roulette punishment, which involves meteorites falling near the chosen enemy. Used against Beep but Don Patch, Jelly Jiggler, and Megafan were also hit in the process.
    • 1000 Bees (Anime: 1000 Hornets): This is the second Bo-bobo Roulette punishment, which involves a thousand hornets stinging the enemy. Used on Beep and Don Patch at the same time.
    • Flatten by Bo-bobo: This is the third Bo-bobo Roulette punishment, which involves a robot Bo-bobo head to fall on the enemy but if unsuccessful, robot Bo-bobo hands clutch the enemy. Used against Haou. (This is the first punishment to affect only one person)
    • The Bo-Ninjas: This is the fourth punishment of Bo-bobo Roulette, which involves a Ninja (known as a Bo-Ninja) using the "Art of Multiplying Self" (cloning himself) and the Bo-Ninjas corner the enemy and use "Art of Hot Potato" (inflames the enemy), "Art of Stinky Gas" (blasts the enemy with a gas smoke screen), and Spyit Sushi (lightning strikes the enemy). Used on Megafan, Beep, and Haou at the same time. In the manga, when the ninjas leave, one stays and kicks Jelly Jiggler before leaving.
    • Kill Poppa Rocks!! (Anime: Destroy Don Patch): This is the fifth Bo-bobo Roulette Punishment, which involves Bo-bobo chasing Don Patch with a hammer, trying to hit him (slashes Don Patch with knife in the manga). Used on Beep but Don Patch was the one who was attacked.
    • Miss Fancey: This is the sixth Bo-bobo Roulette punishment, which causes its victim to turn into a non-violent/peace-loving girl who designs formalwear, then is attacked by Bo-bobo riding a robot fist with a picture of Miss Fancey on it. Used against Haou.
    • Super Fist of the Nose Hair: Graduation: The seventh and final Bo-bobo Roulette Punishment (with Don Patch on the roulette wheel), with Bo-bobo delivering a massive attack with his Nose Hair. Used to defeat Haou, Megafan, and Beep at the same time.
  • Super Mega Extreme Fist of the Nose Hair: It's a Wonderful Nose Hair Life(ワンダフル鼻毛7DAYS): Bo-bobo makes the enemy experience secven "ordinary" days, then slams the enemy into the ground. Used to defeat Halekulani. The days are as follows:
    • Monday: Several alarm clocks ring simultaneously as Mommy (Jelly Jiggler) wakes Bo-bobo, the family dog (Don Patch) jumps through the window, the family eats canned crab for breakfast. And Bo-bobo gets to blow up the Monday-go-round. Monday gets an A.
    • Tuesday (Barricaded Day): Several police officers who look like Bo-bobo surround the house to arrest Bo-bobo for playing video games too loud in the winter. For dinner they eat canned crab. Bo-bobo gets to blow up the roller coaster.
    • "Rock n' Roll" Wednesday (Big Bobble Head Day): An army of Bo-bobo Samurais fight to get back stolen CDs in the Tokugowa Era. Bo-bobo also rolls Halekulani off the roof. (Don Patch and Jelly exhibit their rapping abilities)
    • Thursday: It's a diary (though it looks more like a script in Kanji). Bo-bobo attempts to get fish from an outer space fish market and ends up "calmly" destroying the universe. Jelly gets thrown into Halekulani into a wall of paper with the ぬ　symbol on it. It also means 3 more days to go.
    • Friday: Chocolate-filled pies eaten by a fuzzy squirrel. Bo-bobo then attacks Halekulani for touching the squirrel because he bites. (But Beauty was petting him.)
    • Saturday (Ghost Wars): Bo-bobo, Don Patch, and Jelly Jiggler become ghost monsters with the powers of light to destroy the ghosts of darkness. (Including NO. 65: Marcel)
    • Sunday (Travel to wonderland day incognito): Bo-bobo, Don Patch, and Jelly Jiggler (along with everyone else from every other day reenact Alice in Wonderland. This ends with Bo-bobo telling Halekulani that money isn't worth much and the most important thing in this world is... money? He then slams him into the ground.
  • Bo-bobo World Tour(temporary name): Bo-bobo sends the enemy on a world tour to different, random places where each new location executes a different attack. Used against Ujikin TOKIO
  • Bobobo World Nightmare: Bo-bobo, Don Patch, and Jelly force the opponent to experience strange and scary events of their own choosing. Used against Rem.
  • Nosehair Human Express: An attack that forces the opponent to see the good of the human life. Used against Czar Baldy Bald the 3rd.
  • Bobobo TV: Used against Nechaku.

[edit] Other Attacks

This is a list of Bo-bobo's weak or "almost" sane attacks. Most of of these attacks serve their purpose as part of Bobobo's philosify of not always relying on Fist of the Nosehair:

  • Fist of the Closing CD Case - Bobobo attacks the enemy with a cd case. Used against Puppetman.
  • Fist of the Not Closing CD Case - Bobobo attacks the enemy with a cd case that cannot close due to being damaged, but it eventually does close. Used against Puppetman.
  • Armpit Chop: A weak chop attack. Used only because Bobobo didn't want to overuse Fist of the Nosehair. Used against Death Mask.
  • Bo-bobo Punch: A punch. Used against Rice.
  • Bo-bobo Chop: A chop. Used against Rice.
  • Fire Attack: Bo-bobo breathes deeply, then breathes fire. Used against Yellow River Guy and Mesopotian Guy.
  • Flickering Flames of Fire Light: When in ghost form in Saturday's Ghost War during It's a Wonderful Nose Hair Life, Bo-bobo shoots two rays of golden flames from his fingers.
  • Chicken Varechini: Bo-bobo blocks an enemy's attack with his fist. Used against Giga.
  • Angry Daddy-Long-Legs Kick: Bo-bobo extends his leag and kicks the enemy. Used against Giga.
  • Police Man's Piledriver: Bo-bobo piledrives the enemy. Used to defeat normal Giga.
  • Once, Twice, 4 Times a Man's Fist: Bo-bobo preforms limb sacrificing double punches and double kicks. Used against Giga.
  • Previously Used Teabag Attack: Bo-bobo hits the enemy with expired tea bags.
  • Pork! Chop! Punch!: Bo-bobo gains the fist of a pig and delivers a powerful punch.
