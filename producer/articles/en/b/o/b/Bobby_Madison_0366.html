Bobby Madison was an American pornographic actor who appeared in gay pornographic movies in the early 1980s.

Also known as Brian Michaels, Bobby appeared in numerous Matt Sterling and William Higgins videos, including a starring role in Brother Load.

He reportedly retired from the porn industry in 1985 following his last movie, In Heat.
