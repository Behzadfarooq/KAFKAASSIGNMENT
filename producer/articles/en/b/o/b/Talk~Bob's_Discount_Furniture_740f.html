[edit] Is it the real Bob?

I've heard people claim that the "Bob" in the commercials is in fact not the real Bob, but rather his twin brother. Word has it that the real Bob is camera shy. Is this true or mere balderdash?--RexRex84 22:02, 2 August 2006 (UTC)

It's "balderdash." I've met Bob, and visited the studio where they shoot the commercials. It's located in the "Pit" at the Manchester, CT store. Bob on TV runs the business. He doesn't have a twin brother, though he has a sister in the advertising department. They might be twins, come to think of it, but I'm not sure. Cathy Poulin, Bob's sidekick, used to be a weatherlady for a local TV station. She runs PR, but has no personal involvement with Bob (a common misperception). Jehochman (Talk/Contrib) 05:19, 3 August 2006 (UTC)
Thanks for the info. I met someone who claims to know the Kaufman family through business connections. This person claims to have met these two twins and the dubious real and stand-in Bobs. Interestingly, this person said the woman in the ads was bob's wife.--RexRex84 19:30, 3 August 2006 (UTC)
Well, I'm stuck here, because I've met Bob and Kathy and know that this stuff is completely false. Anyhow, this gossip should probably be deleted from the talk page because it's not relevant to the article. Jehochman (Talk/Contrib) 02:56, 4 August 2006 (UTC)
Fair enough. I think if there were twins and Bob was not in the ads then there should be some sort of trivia section in the article. Thanks for the clarification.--RexRex84 13:22, 4 August 2006 (UTC)

Is there any more information available on the origin of the company. Also, where was the first store opened - was it Manchester, CT?

[edit] Bob's plastic surgery accident

A few months ago I heard that Bob was undergoing plastic surgery on his face for some new commercials, and he actually died on the operating table for almost a half hour. The doctors say he experienced minor brain damage - that's why Bob's twin brother is in the commercials, and the real Bob is kept sealed away in a windowless room on the family estate. The family and company now deny Bob ever had a twin (whose name is Jimford), saying that he is the real Bob.

Please confirm?

  • Happy April Fools Day early. I live in CT, and no such news was reported here. Jehochman (Talk/Contrib) 23:08, 23 February 2007 (UTC)
