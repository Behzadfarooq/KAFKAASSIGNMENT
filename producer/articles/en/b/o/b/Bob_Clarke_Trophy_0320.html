Bob Clarke Trophy

The Bob Clarke Trophy is awarded annually to the top scorer in the Western Hockey League.

It is named after NHL Hall of Famer Bobby Clarke who was one of the WHL's early stars. Clarke won the WHL's scoring title both years he played in the league.
