Bob Menne (born February 19, 1942) is an American professional golfer who has played on the PGA Tour, the Champions Tour and the European Seniors Tour.

Originally from Gardner, Massachusetts, Menne had at least seven top-10 finishes in PGA Tour events during his career including a win at the 1974 Kemper Open. He won that tournament in a playoff with Jerry Heard. His best finish in a major was a T-30 at the 1970 PGA Championship.

After reaching the age of 50, Menne played some on the Champions Tour but with limited success. His best finish in a Champions Tour event was a T-11 at Raley's Senior Gold Rush in 1992. He has had more success playing on the European Seniors Tour.

Menne lives in Demarest, New Jersey.
