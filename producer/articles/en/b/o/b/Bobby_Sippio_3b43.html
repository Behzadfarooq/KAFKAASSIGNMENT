Robert Lee Sippio, Jr. (born December 4, 1980 in Orlando, Florida) is an Arena Football offensive specialist who plays for the Chicago Rush.

[edit] College and af2

Sippio played in college at Western Kentucky University for 3 seasons. Played in af2 with the Peoria Pirates and the Greensboro Prowlers. He finished his af2 career with 2,032 all-purpose yards.

[edit] AFL Career

2004-Played in 15 games as a rookie with the Dallas Desperados, catching 75 passes for 1,217 yards and 32 touchdowns, and recording 25.0 tackles, 2 interceptions and 3 passes broken up. made his AFL debut February 8 vs. Carolina, catching 4 passes for 78 yards and 2 TDs. Named co-Offensive Player of the Game March 6 vs. Grand Rapids Rampage after catching 6 passes for 116 yards and 3 touchdowns.


2005-Played in 16 games in his second season with Dallas, recording 61 receptions for 863 yards and 22 touchdowns…also added 30.5 tackles, 2 interceptions and 9 passes broken up.

· scored touchdowns in five different ways during the season – receiving, rushing, interception return, fumble return and kickoff return.

2006-Bobby played in nine games for the Tampa Bay Storm, catching 55 passes for 666 yards and 18 touchdowns, before being released March 28 and claimed by Chicago on March 29. Finished the 2006 season with 93 catches for 1320 yards and 35 touchdowns. Sippio posted a season high 10 catches for 136 yards and 2 TDs vs. Orlando February 19.
