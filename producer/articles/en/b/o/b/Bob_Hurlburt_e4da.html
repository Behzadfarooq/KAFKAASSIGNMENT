Bob Hurlburt (born May 1, 1950 in Toronto, Ontario) is a retired professional ice hockey player who played one game in the National Hockey League for the Vancouver Canucks.

A junior teammate of Hall of Famer Bill Barber, Hurlburt was passed over in the NHL Amateur Draft. Primarily a defensive forward, he plied his trade in minor-pro from 1970-1975, and received a one-game callup to the Canucks in the 1974-75 season.
