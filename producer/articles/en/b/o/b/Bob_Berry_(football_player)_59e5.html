Robert (Bob) Berry (born March 10, 1942 in San Jose, California) was a quarterback in the NFL for 12 years. He went to 1 Pro Bowl. Berry played in Super Bowls VIII, IX and XI as a member of the Minnesota Vikings. He has a daughter, Jennifer (b. 1973).

Currently he is a real estate agent with M. Scott Properties in Minden, Nevada, serving the greater Tahoe area (http://bobberryproperties.com/ Bob Berry Properties).
