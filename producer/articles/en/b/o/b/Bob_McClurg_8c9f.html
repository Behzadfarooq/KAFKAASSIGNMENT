Bob McClurg is an actor.

He joined the Los Angeles-based improvisational comedy team The Groundlings and remained a member for six years, working with John Paragon, Susan Barnes, Phil Hartman and Paul Reubens.

He appeared in several movies like:

  • 1987 Born in East L.A. Actor: Harry
  • 1985 Pee-Wee's Big Adventure Actor: Studio Guard
  • 1980 Cheech and Chong's Next Movie Actor: Chicken Charlie
  • 1977 Oh, God! Actor: Mechanic
  • 1977 Cracking Up Actor
  • 1976 Tunnelvision Actor
  • 1973 Magnum Force
