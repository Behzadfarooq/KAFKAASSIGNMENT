Bob Babich is an American football coach, currently the defensive coordinator of the Chicago Bears. He took over this role after the Bears decided not to renew the contract of Ron Rivera. Prior to joining the Bears, Babich worked with head coach Lovie Smith during his years with the St. Louis Rams.

He is a not related to the 1968 graduate of Miami University (Oxford, OH) and College Football Hall of Fame member of the same name.

Babich was a linebacker for the Mesa Community College in Arizona from 1979-1980, and linebacker for Tulsa from 1981-1982. He was the head coach for the North Dakota State Bison football team from 1997-2002.

[edit] Career

  • 1984 Tulsa - graduate assistant
  • 1985-1987 Tulsa - tight ends/assistant offensive line/strong safeties
  • 1988-1989 University of Wisconsin - assistant offensive line/tight ends
  • 1990 Tulsa - volunteer assistant coach/offensive line
  • 1991 Bowling Green State University - linebackers coach
  • 1992-1993 East Carolina - linebackers coach
  • 1994-1996 University of Pittsburgh - linebackers coach
  • 1997-2002 North Dakota State University - head coach
  • 2003 St. Louis Rams - linebackers coach
  • 2004-2005 Chicago Bears - linebackers coach
  • 2006 Chicago Bears - assistant head coach/linebackers coach
  • 2007-present Chicago Bears - defensive coordinator
