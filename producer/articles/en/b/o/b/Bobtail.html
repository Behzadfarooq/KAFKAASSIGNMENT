Bobtail refers originally to an animal—usually a horse, dog, or cat—whose tail is cut short or is naturally short, and has since come to be applied to other objects whose tail or back end is shorter than normal. See:

  • natural bobtail - animals carrying a gene for a naturally short tail
  • docking, a more common term for bobbing, along with cropping
  • Japanese Bobtail (cat), a type of cat
  • American Bobtail, a type of cat
  • Bobtail squid
  • Poker jargon: a bobtail straight is an open-ended straight (see also Non-standard poker hand, List of slang names for poker hands)
  • Cooper Car Company for a rear-engine racing car known as the bobtail
  • Bobcat, a wild cat named for its naturally stubby tail
  • Old English Sheepdog is sometimes called Bobtail.
Bobtail truck with sideboards
  • Bobtail truck is an informal term for a trailer-less tractor truck (Austrailian English) or a short, wide flatbed truck (American English)
