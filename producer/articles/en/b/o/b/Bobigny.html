Bobigny is a commune in the northeastern suburbs of Paris, France. It is located 9.1 km. (5.6 miles) from the center of Paris. Bobigny is the préfecture (capital) of the Seine-Saint-Denis département, as well as the seat of the Arrondissement of Bobigny.

Inhabitants are called Balbyniens.

[edit] Transport

Bobigny is served by two stations on Paris Métro Line 5: Bobigny - Pantin - Raymond Queneau and Bobigny - Pablo Picasso.
