Robert Harland Reifsnyder (1937- ) was a professional American football player. An All-American at the United States Naval Academy, he was the 1957 recipient of the Maxwell Award for College Player of the Year.[1]

He went on to play for the American Football League's New York Titans (1960-1961).

Reifsnyder was inducted into the College Football Hall of Fame in 1997.
