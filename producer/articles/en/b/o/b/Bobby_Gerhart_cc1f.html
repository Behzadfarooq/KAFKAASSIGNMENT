Bobby Gerhart (born July 21, 1958, in Lebanon, Pennsylvania), is a driver on the ARCA Re/Max Circuit. He has won four times on the circuit, three of those victories coming at the season-opening race at Daytona International Speedway. Presently, he drives the #5 Lucas Oil Chevrolet owned by his brother.

He has also driven in NASCAR, with 24 career starts in the NEXTEL Cup series with a best finish of 22nd.

He owns Bobby Gerhart's Truck World, a used truck dealership in his hometown.
