Bobby Badfingers

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Bobby Badfingers on America's Got Talent.

Bobby Badfingers (born Bob Von Merta; from San Francisco, CA) is a novelty performer whose claim to fame is that he can snap his fingers at a tremendous rate. He has appeared on television, radio and variety shows including:

  • The Paul O'Grady Show,
  • The Howard Stern Show,
  • Sábado Gigante,
  • Live with Regis & Kathie Lee
  • Tom Green Live, and others.

He has also appeared in television commercials for Fujifilm, Roadrunner High-Speed Internet, Yahoo!, and Snapple, and the Ohio Lottery's Classic Lotto.

He was a contestant on NBC's America's Got Talent. His was one of the only 4 acts in his semifinal episode to get 3 checks from the judges. He was unanimously voted through to the semifinals, but was picked as neither the judge's choice nor the viewer's choice to move to the finals during the live broadcast on July 20, 2006. The judges, however, let him advance onto the wild card show in which he failed to get voted through, thus ending his journey in the talent competition.

Merta started to become a professional finger snapper because during his childhood, he would constantly snap both of his fingers because he suffered from ADHD.

Currently lives in Huntington Beach California.
