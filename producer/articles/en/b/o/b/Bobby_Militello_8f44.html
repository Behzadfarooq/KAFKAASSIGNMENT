Robert Philip Militello, better known as Bobby Militello, Bob Militello, or Bobby M, (born 25 March 1950 in Buffalo, New York) is a U.S. jazz saxophonist and multi-instrumentalist. He has played with Maynard Ferguson and Dave Brubeck, and is active in his hometown's music scene.

[edit] Overview

Militello has done some great work with the Dave Brubeck Quartet, including:

  • (1993) Late Night Brubeck - live album (alto sax, tenor sax, shakuhachi-sounding flute)
  • (2005) London Flat, London Sharp - (alto sax, flute) [1]
