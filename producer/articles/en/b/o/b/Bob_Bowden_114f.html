Lieutenant Colonel Robert A. "Bob" Bowden (b. in Irving, Texas) is currently an assistant professor in the Chemistry department at the United States Military Academy.

Bowden received his B.A. and Ph.D. in veterinary pathobiology immunology from Texas A&M University. He is a published researcher, with articles appearing in AJP-Cell Physiology and Infection and Immunity. His most recent work is "Long-term acyclovir for prevention of varicella zoster virus disease after allogeneic hematopoietic cell transplantation--a randomized double-blind placebo-controlled study", which was published in the March 2007 issue of Blood.
