Bobsleigh at the 1948 Winter Olympics

At the 1948 Winter Olympics, two bobsleigh events were contested.

[edit] Men's events

[edit] Two man bob

Medal Team/Athlete Time
Gold Switzerland II (Felix Endrich and Friedrich Waller) 5:29.2
Silver Switzerland I (Fritz Feierabend and Paul Eberhard) 5:30.4
Bronze USA II (Fred Fortune and Schuyler Carron) 5:35.3

[edit] Four man bob

Medal Team/Athlete Time
Gold USA II (Francis Tyler, Patrick Martin, Edward Rimkus, William D'Amico) 5:20.1
Silver Belgium I (Max Houben,Freddy Mansveld, Louis-George Niels, Jacque Mouvet) 5:21.3
Bronze USA I (James Bickford, Thomas Hicks, Donald Dupree, William Dupree) 5:21.5
