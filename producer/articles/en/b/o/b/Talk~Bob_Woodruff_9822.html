[edit] Not related to Judy Woodruff

Why does someone believe the disclaimer that he is not related to Judy Woodruff is irrelevant? Since I did not know that, I think it is very relevant and also factually informative since they have the same last name and the same profession. Tbeatty 03:58, 31 January 2006 (UTC)

He's also unrelated to Robert W. Woodruff of the Robert W. Woodruff Foundation. Also unrelated to the children's book author Elvira Woodruff. Also not affiliated with Woodruff Corp., a subsidiary of Primex Plastics. Get the picture? This isn't a disambiguation page. Suggest you stop stalking and get on with productive editing of video games and WWII battleships or other subjects where you are more expert. Thanks. --68.219.203.209 04:05, 31 January 2006 (UTC)

The note about Judy Woodruff is completely relevant because they are both television news anchors/reporters. Whenever there are two people in the same field with the same last name, confusion about their relationship may occur. Crunch 13:21, 31 January 2006 (UTC)
