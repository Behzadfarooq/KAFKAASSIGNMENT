Bob Crowley (born in Cork, Ireland) is a theatre director, scenic and costume designer. He is the brother of director John Crowley. He has been nominated for the Tony Award ten times, winning three for designing the Broadway productions of Aida (2000), Carousel (1994) and The History Boys. He is a frequent collaborator of Nicholas Hytner's and besides Broadway has worked extensively at the Royal National Theatre and the Royal Shakespeare Company in London.

Most recently, Bob Crowley designed set and costume for Mary Poppins, currently playing in the West End and then opened on Broadway in November 2006. He has also become director/designer on the new Disney musical, Tarzan, currently playing at the Richard Rodgers Theatre on Broadway.

[edit] Tony Awards

  • 1987 Costume Designer (Nominee): Les Liaisons Dangereuses
  • 1987 Scenic Designer (Nominee): Les Liaisons Dangereuses
  • 1994 Scenic Designer Winner: Carousel
  • 1998 Scenic Designer (Nominee): The Capeman
  • 1999 Scenic Designer (Nominee): The Iceman Cometh
  • 1999 Scenic Designer (Nominee): Twelfth Night
  • 2000 Costume Designer (Nominee): Aida
  • 2000 Scenic Designer Winner: Aida
  • 2001 Scenic Designer (Nominee): The Invention of Love
  • 2006 Scenic Design Play Winner: The History Boys
