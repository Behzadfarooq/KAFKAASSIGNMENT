This is my page, I suppose. I would tell you all about myself but I heard it is dangerous to leak stuff like my Social Security Number and Credit Card Number, so I'll keep that confidential. I am working to be the best Wikipedian I can be by contributing frequently, and I have recently begun promoting Wikipedia by constantly announcing *goes to wiki that...* in chatrooms. Yes, wiki is a verb now. So there.

I happen to be the criminal responsible for updating the layout for and translating part of the Trinitrotoluene page, creating and translating part of the Rhein-Herne-Kanal page, and creating the Fame, Glory, and Other Things on My To Do List novel page. And I went through the embarrassment of reading that hot pink book. (It was a good book, by the way!)
