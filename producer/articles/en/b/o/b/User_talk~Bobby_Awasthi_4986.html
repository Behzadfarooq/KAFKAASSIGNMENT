[edit] Please don't cut and paste material from other websites without permission

Bobby, please read Wikipedia:Copyright policy before using material from any other website. Copying other people's content without their permission is a bad thing, since it is against copyright law, as well as Wikipedia policy. Tracking down cut-and-pastes from other websites is tedious for other editors, and may lead to your being banned from editing. We welcome any original contributions from you, and you may, of course, use any source as a reference for your contributions here (that's what "external links" references are for, to credit these sources), but please do not cut-and-paste content, or generate simple derivative works from other people's content without their explicit permission to donate their content to Wikipedia. -- Karada 08:13, 13 Apr 2005 (UTC)
