Bob Gerard (born Frederick Roberts Gerard January 19, 1914 - January 26, 1990) was a British racing driver from England. He was born in Leicester and died in South Croxton, Leicestershire. He participated in 7 grands prix, debuting on May 13, 1950. He scored no championship points.
