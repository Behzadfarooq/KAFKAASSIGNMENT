This article refers to a Liberal Member of Parliament from North Bay, Ontario. For the Progressive Conservative Member of Provincial Parliament from London, Ontario in the same era, see Bob Wood (Progressive Conservative).

Bob Wood (born March 11, 1940 in Ayer's Cliff, Quebec) is a former Canadian politician. He represented the riding of Nipissing in the Canadian House of Commons from 1988 to 2004. He was a member of the Liberal Party.

Wood did not stand for re-election in the 2004 election.
