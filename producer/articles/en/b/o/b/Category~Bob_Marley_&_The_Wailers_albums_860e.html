Category:Bob Marley & The Wailers albums

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

This category contains articles about albums released by Bob Marley, including albums released by his original band The Wailers (with Peter Tosh and Bunny Wailer) and albums released by his later solo band Bob Marley & The Wailers. In addition, it contains some posthumous compilation albums that feature live performances and recordings of songs from one or both of the bands.
