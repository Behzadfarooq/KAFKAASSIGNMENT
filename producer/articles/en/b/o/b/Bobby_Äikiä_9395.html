Bobby Äikiä was a 10 year old swedish boy who in 2006 was tortured and killed by his mother and stepfather.

Bobby died after being first tied to a chair in the house of his stepfather Eddy Larsson and mother Niina Äikä, the stepfather then threw Bobby out still tied to a chair into the snow. Bobby froze to death. Both partners in crime the day later reported Bobby missing when as they sayed he disappeard after a visit to the local stores in Jönköping. Bobbys body was found in a nearby lake the next day under the ice after the mother had confessed. The manslaughter has gotten alot of attention in Swedish media and many celebrities have shown their support for the mother and stephdad to be convicted for Bobby's death. Their was a foundation started in memory of Bobby.
