Bob Lorenz

Bob Lorenz is the primary anchor on the YES Network. He hosts the pregame and postgame shows for New York Yankees and New Jersey Nets cablecasts.

[edit] YES Network

Lorenz also hosts other shows on YES including Yankees Magazine and Yogi and a Movie, where he watches baseball-related movies with hall of fame catcher Yogi Berra. In addition, Lorenz also hosts This Week in Football a show that talks about New York Giants and New York Jets as well as the rest of the NFL, along with Yankees Hot Stove a show talking about baseball's offseason player movement.

[edit] CNN

Lorenz was formerly an anchor on CNN Sports Illustrated. Also he worked for TBS and TNT hosting various sporting event studio shows. For example he hosted Super Bowl Specials and was a fill-in host on Inside the NBA.

[edit] Trivia

  • Lorenz holds a degree in broadcast journalism from the University of Southern California in Los Angeles.
