Robert Moorehouse Coleman III, known professionally as Bobby Coleman (born May 5, 1997) is an American child actor.

Coleman, the younger sister of actress Holliston Coleman, has appeared in several film and television productions. Coleman had brief appearances in the television series Medium and JAG before moving into film roles. He appeared in the feature films Must Love Dogs and Friends with Money, before taking leading roles in the films The Good Mother and Take. He will also be seen in the title role in the upcoming film The Martian Child, his second role alongside John Cusack. Coleman also had a recurring role in the television series Surface, and it set to appear alongside his sister in Proving Ground: From the Adventures of Captain Redlocks, in which he will play the younger brother of his real-life sister.

Coleman was named after his father and grandfather, both of whom were also named Robert. He lives with his family in California.
