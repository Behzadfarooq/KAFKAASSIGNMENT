Robert "Bob" William Prittie (December 5, 1919–January 14, 2002) was a Canadian politician. He was a New Democrat Member of Parliament from Burnaby—Richmond from 1962 to 1968 and mayor of Burnaby, British Columbia from 1969 to 1973.

The Metrotown branch of the Burnaby Public Library was named after Bob Prittie.
