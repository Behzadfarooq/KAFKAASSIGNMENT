Bob Hogue

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Bob Hogue is a former sportscaster, and is a Republican member of the Hawaii State Senate representing the 24th district for six years. Hogue was the Republican nominee for U.S. Congress in Hawaii's 2nd congressional district, to replace Ed Case but lost in the general election to former Lieutenant Governor Mazie Hirono.
