Bob Moose

Robert (Bob) Ralph Moose Jr. (October 9, 1947 - October 9, 1976) was a Major League Baseball pitcher from 1967 to 1976. Moose spent his entire career with the Pittsburgh Pirates. His best season came in 1969 when he posted a 14-3 won-loss record and a 2.91 Earned Run Average working equally as a starter and reliever. His highlight game of the season was a no-hitter he pitched against the eventual world champion New York Mets on September 20.

Tragically, Moose was killed in an auto accident heading to his 29th birthday party in Martins Ferry, Ohio.
