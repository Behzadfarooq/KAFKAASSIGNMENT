Bob Geigel

Robert Geigel was an American professional wrestling promoter and also a former professional wrestler.

[edit] Profile

  • Born: Algona, Iowa
  • From: Dallas, Texas
  • Education: University of Iowa
  • Height & Weight: 5'11" and 230lbs
  • Debut: 1950
  • Previous Gimmicks: Texas, A-Bomber, Bob Geigel

[edit] Championships and accomplishments

  • American Wrestling Association
  • AWA Midwest Tag Team Championship (1 time) - with The Viking
  • AWA World Tag Team Championship (3 times) - with Hardboiled Haggerty (1), Otto von Krupp (1), and Stan Kowalski (1)
  • National Wrestling Alliance
  • National
  • NWA North American Heavyweight Championship (Amarillo version) (1 time)
  • NWA North American Tag Team Championship (9 times) - with Bob Brown (5), Bill Miller (1), The Viking (1), The Stomper (1), and Rufus R. Jones (1)
  • NWA United States Heavyweight Championship (Central States version) (2 times)
  • Regional
  • NWA Central States Heavyweight Championship (6 times)
  • NWA Central States Tag Team Championship (12 times) - with Bob Brown (5), Rufus R. Jones (2), Akio Sato (1), Bobo Brazil (1), Pat O'Connor (1), The Stomper (1), and The Viking (1)
  • NWA Southwest Heavyweight Championship (2 times)
  • NWA Southwest Tag Team Championship (4 times) - with Dory Funk (2), Mike Gallagher (1), and Boris Kalmikoff (1)
  • Other Titles
  • North Dakota Heavyweight Championship (1 time)

[edit] Job Titles

  • NWA President (1978 to 1980; 1982 to 1985; 1986 to 1987)
  • NWA Central States promoter (1958 to September 1986; February 1987 to 1988)
  • NWA St. Louis promoter (1982 to 1986)

[edit] Wrestlers Trained

  • Bart Sawyer (Steve Stewart)
  • Dick Murdoch
  • The Equalizer
  • Ox Baker (Douglas A. Baker)
