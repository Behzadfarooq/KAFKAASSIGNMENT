The Bobsleigh 1964 Winter Olympics results in Innsbruck, Austria.

[edit] Men's events

[edit] Four-man

Pos Athlete Times
Gold Canada (Peter Kirby, Douglas Anakin and John Emery and Vic Emery) 4:14.46
Silver Austria ( ) 4:15.48
Bronze Italy ( ) 4:15.60
  • The Canadian four-man bobsleigh team of Peter Kirby, Douglas Anakin and John Emery and Vic Emery upset the heavily favored Austrians.

[edit] Two-man

Pos Athlete Times
Gold Robin Thomas Dixon & Antony James Nash (GBR) 4:21.90
Silver Sergio Zardini & Romano Bonagura (ITA) 4:22.02
Bronze Eugenio Monti & Sergio Siorpaes (ITA) 4:22.63

A turn at the St. Moritz, Switzerland course is named in honor of Nash and Dixon, called the Nash-Dixon corner.
