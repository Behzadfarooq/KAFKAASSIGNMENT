Bobb was the fictional ape/monkey creature in the TV show Cro. He always talked in monkeylike sounds. Bobb is the Neanderthal on the lowest rank in the evolutionary scale, meaning he is still an ape-like creature. He doesn't speak like the rest of the Neanderthals, but makes monkey-like noises and either Gogg or Nandy translate for him. When Bobb was younger, his previous tribe (before the Neanderthals) either deserted him, got eaten, or some other reason...and Bobb was all alone!

[edit] Appearance

Bobb has gray fur despite presumably being young. He has a tanned face with a round nose and oppossbile toes. Unlike the other male Neandies, Bobb wears a brown underwear-like thing.

[edit] Old Tribe

Long ago, prior to joining the Neandies, Bobb was in a tribe with monkeys that looked just like him. They once laid down for a nap, but when Bobb woke up, however, they mysteriously vanished. Bobb looked all over for them, but they were nowhere in sight. Hence, Bobb became alone and ever since he joined the Neandies, he had a distinctive fear of being alone (even though Cro says that Bobb doesn't mind being alone).
