[edit] the telephone routine

I wonder whether Newhart borrowed the telephone routine from Hell is for Heroes; that scene is the only time I've seen him not speaking most of the "other" side of the conversation. [edit] The Driving Instructor

A recent radio programme on BBC7 said that Bob Newhart got the idea for the "The Driving Instructor" from a very similar earlier item about a driving instructor on the Al Read Show. A recording of that programme was played and there certainly were a lot of similarities. I wonder if there is any confirmation of this? --jmb 13:46, 20 August 2006 (UTC)
