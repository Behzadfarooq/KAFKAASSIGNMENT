Sir Robert Kerslake, who prefers to be known as Bob, is Chief Executive of Sheffield City Council.

[edit] Career

Sir Bob is originally from Bath, UK. After graduating in Mathematics from the University of Warwick, he held a number of posts with councils in London before becoming Chief Executive of the London Borough of Hounslow. He moved to Sheffield to take up the post of Chief Executive of Sheffield City Council in 1997.

[edit] Recognition

  • In 2003 Sir Bob was named in a Guardian list of the 100 most influential people in the public sector. [1]
  • In 2004 he received an Honorary Doctorate from Sheffield Hallam University for his "distinctive contribution to public service". [2]
  • In 2004 he received a knighthood for services to local government. [3]
