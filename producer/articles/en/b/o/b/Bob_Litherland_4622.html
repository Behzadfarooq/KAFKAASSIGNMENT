Bob Litherland

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Robert Kenneth Litherland, known as Bob Litherland, (born 23 June 1930) is a British Labour politician.

Litherland was elected Member of Parliament (MP) for Manchester Central at a by-election in September 1979, and held the office until he retired at the 1997 general election.
