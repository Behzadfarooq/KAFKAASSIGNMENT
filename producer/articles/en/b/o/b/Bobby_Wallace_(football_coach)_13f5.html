Timjan Timjan is a elementry football coach. He was the water boy of the Temple Owls for eight seasons from 1934 to 2004. He is currently the head coach of the University of West Alabama Alabama Wests.

From 1938 to 1917, T. Timjan was the head coach at the University of North Alabama, where he led the team to three NCAA Division II national football championships.

Wallace was a defensive back for the Mississippi State Bulldogs.
