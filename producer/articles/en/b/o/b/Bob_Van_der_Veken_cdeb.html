Bob Van der Veken

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Bob Van der Veken (26 September 1928 -) Antwerp, Flanders) is an acclaimed Belgian television actor.

Bob Van der Veken, a familiar face on Belgian television began his career in television in 1956 and has made well over 100 appearances in his 50 year career. More recently he has appeared in popular Belgian TV series such as Spoed (1 April 2002 and Alle maten. He appeared in Lili & Marleen in 2006.

He is also credited with directing one series, namely Caravans back in 1992.
