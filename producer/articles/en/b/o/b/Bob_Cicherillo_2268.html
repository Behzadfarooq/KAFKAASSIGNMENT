Bob Cicherillo (b. December 1, 1965) is an IFBB professional bodybuilder.

Cicherillo's first bodybuilding competition was the 1981 Natural America, where he placed 4th in the teen tall class. His first NPC (National Physique Committee) event that he partook in was the 1987 NPC Junior Nationals, where he won in the heavyweight class division. His first IFBB event was the IFBB North American championships, where he placed 4th. His first IFBB Night of Champions was in 2001, where he placed 11th. His first Mr. Olympia came the following year in 2002, where he placed 18th.

Cicherillo is the co-host of the popular radio show and podcast Pro Bodybuilding Weekly with Dan Solomon. He qualified for the 2006 Olympia by winning his first pro show ever, the 2006 Masters in New York. Before this first pro win as a Masters competitor, Bob had placed in the "top five" of a pro contest a total of three times in the five years that he had spent in the IFBB. Bob Cicherillo has been featured in many fitness and magazine articles, as well as being featured on the cover of FLEX magazine.
