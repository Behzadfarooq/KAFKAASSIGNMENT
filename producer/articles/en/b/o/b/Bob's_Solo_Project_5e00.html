Bob Bryar’s Solo Project originated when Bob Bryar was on the set for the filming of My Chemical Romance’s music video for "Welcome To The Black Parade". He was standing on the float with bandmate Frank Iero and said that he was practicing his solo project – which was all songs about MCR’s frontman Gerard Way.

Straight away when this clip appeared on TV and surfaced on youtube, My Chemical Romance fans took this message to heart and the user kirschblume created a livejournal website dedicated to his solo project. Since then a unofficial fansite has been created and he has gained fans all over the world.

Bob’s Solo Project was voted by fans the 3rd best thing about 2006 by British magazine Kerrang!, just behind My Chemical Romance and Download Festival.

Bob has little intentions of actually pursuing this side project but said in an interview with Big Cheese magazine that maybe someday if he was bored, it might materialize.
