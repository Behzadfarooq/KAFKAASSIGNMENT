Bob Francis is a talk back radio presenter on South Australian radio station FIVEaa. His program airs between 8pm and 12am weekdays and is rated as Adelaide's most popular talk back program.[citation needed]

Born to an Egyptian mother and Gypsy father[citation needed], Francis was raised in Adelaide Australia. Francis has hosted his own show on 5aa since 1981 and is the stations longest serving employee.[citation needed]

5aa's website ironically describes him as "Aggressive, loud, rude, arrogant... and those are his good points." Francis' program often causes controversy. One of his most controversial acts, also believed to have been one of the most controversial acts by any Australian talk show radio host, occurred in September 2005 whilst he was talking to a female listener - this was when Francis spoke arrogantly to her, referring to her as a 'stupid old lady' and repeatedly calling her a 'dickbrain'. He is not a very nice man.

For the record, he has publicly stated that he blames the late SA Premier Don Dunstan for everything that has gone wrong in South Australia with regards to law and order. Personally, I think it's because he couldn't find a safari suit big enough to fit him.

There is one rumour that during The Beatles visit to Adelaide in 1964, Bob Francis and the group gathered at the Goodwood Hotel for some after concert drinks. After several hours of drinking, an argument broke out over Francis calling John Lennon a 'dickbrain' over his views of world peace and harmony. He then proceeded to call Ringo Starr 'big nose', punching Paul Mcartney in the face and branding George Harrison a 'communist.'

To date, Francis is still hosting his radio program, he is an active volunteer for the Salvation Army and is the spokesman for the Red Cross's Blood Appeal. So far, they haven't found a single drop in his entire body.[citation needed]

Bob is now in his 50th year of radio broadcasting and he will be appearing naked as part of 2007 Adelaide Fringe Festival.
