You are wrong. You cannot WALK across homeplate. You can WALK to reach first base. After that you can only ADVANCE from the result of someone else's at bat. Therefore, you in fact cannot WALK across homeplate.

Let me be clear, the Cubs have not been very good since the second half of 2005, however, nearly all you read from astute baseball observers is that this is due more to poor roster management from the front office than actual in-game strategy. The Cubs would probably be best served by tearing the whole thing down and rebuilding from scratch. To do that, they need a different manager than Dusty Baker, but they also need a new front office that will spend less on questionable free agents, and invest in the farm system as teams such as the White Sox, Indians, Twins, Diamondbacks, Marlins and others have done.
