Bobby Jarzombek

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Bobby Jarzombek is a heavy metal/progressive metal drummer. He was born on September 4, 1963 in San Antonio, Texas. He began playing drums at the age of ten and got involved with various bands in his early teens. His first real claim to fame was joining Juggernaut, whom he recorded two albums with before leaving to become the drummer for the Riot reunion. Jarzombek remained with the group for quite some time, drumming on a total of seven albums. He eventually joined his brother Ron Jarzombek in the band Spastic Ink, later taking the seat behind the drum kit for Rob Halford. He is currently the drummer for Sebastian Bach, Painmuseum and Demons and Wizards. Bobby was also a member of Iced Earth from 2004 to 2006, though he did not record anything with the band but played several shows with them. Due to a conflicting schedule with current gig (Sebastian Bach) and the proposed Iced Earth recording schedule, Bobby choose to leave IE. Since then, Brent Smedley has rejoined the band. In 2004, Bobby released his drumming DVD "Performance & Technique" which is available from Alfred Publishing. He appeared on the 2005 Rob Rock album, Holy Hell.
