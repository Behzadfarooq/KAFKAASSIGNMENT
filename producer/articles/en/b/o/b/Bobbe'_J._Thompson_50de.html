Bobbe' J. Thompson is an American child actor born on February 28, 1996. He was born in Kansas City, Missouri and is also known as Bobby J. Thompson, Master Groove, and Bobbe' Jaquez Thompson. Bobbe's most recent role is Stanley in the Disney Channel Original Series That's So Raven. Bobbe' is the youngest of nine siblings. Bobbe' likes to rap. His favorite musicans are Rick James McHammer Carl Douglas the Village People LL Cool J and Sir Mixalot.
