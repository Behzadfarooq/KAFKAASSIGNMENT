Bobby Ryan (footballer)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Bobby Ryan (born May 1, 1979 in Limerick) is an Irish footballer currently playing for Dunfermline Athletic in the Scottish Premier League. He is a skilful crosser of the ball and an asset on the wing.

Ryan joined Dunfermline in January 2007 from Shelbourne. He had previously played for Limerick, Galway United and Bohemians

[edit] Honours

  • League of Ireland Championship: 2
    • Bohemians - 2002/03
    • Shelbourne - 2006
