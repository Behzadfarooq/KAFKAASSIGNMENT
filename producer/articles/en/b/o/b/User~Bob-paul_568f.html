[edit] Name

I'm Bob/Paul. Online I usually go by BobPaul as most places (including wikipedia) don't allow slashes in the user name.

It seems either someone else already registered BobPaul or I forgot my password and didn't enter my e-mail address, so here I am.

[edit] Major Work

I've not contributed much to Wikipedia, although I use it frequently. I am responsible for the majority of the origional contributions on the DD-WRT Wiki. My contributions on the DD-WRT wiki are all either anonymous or under the name Sveasoft when I remember to log in.

DD-WRT is a piece of custom software that replaces the software on Linksys's WRT54G router, greatly expanding it's available feature set.

[edit] Education

I'm a student of Computer Engineering at a State University in the USA.

[edit] Hobbies

I work for the ITS unit at my college (not the whole university) doing a little of everything from managing the Windows Server 2003 and Gentoo Linux servers. I consider this a paid hobby.

I enjoy writing bash shell scripts and simple coding in C/C++ and JAVA, as well as VHDL although I don't currently have access to the schools resources to work more with VHDL at the moment.

I'm an avid mountain biker and enjoy backpacking, hiking, canooing, kayaking, camping, winter camping and winter mountain biking. If there were more hills, I'd probably downhill ski.
