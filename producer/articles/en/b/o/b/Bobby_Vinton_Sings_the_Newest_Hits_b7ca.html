Bobby Vinton Sings the Newest Hits was Bobby Vinton's fifteenth studio album, released in 1967.

Primarily a cover album, this album contains various songs from past years, especially movie themes. Cover songs include "Call Me" (a British hit for Petula Clark in 1964) and "The End of the World" (a hit for Skeeter Davis in 1963). Movie themes include "Born Free," "The Shadow of Your Smile," and "This Is My Song." The sole single from this album is "Coming Home Soldier" (co-written by Vinton).
