Bob Sutton is the defensive coordinator of the New York Jets. He was named defensive coordinator on February 20, 2006 after the dismissal of the Jets' former defensive coordinator Donnie Henderson. He previously held the position of linebackers coach and was instrumental in molding Jonathan Vilma from a college superstar into a NFL superstar. He also coached the USMA football team, more well known as Army, in 1996 and got the team ranked 24th in the nation.
