Bob Warner is a fictional character that featured prominently in the second season of 24.

[edit] Biography

Bob is the head of a multi-national corporation and the father of Kate and Marie Warner. His wife had passed away several years prior to Day 2. His younger daughter Marie was scheduled to marry Reza Nayieer, a senior employee at Bob's company on Day 2.

Kate approached her father with concerns about Reza's activities, concern that he was not the man they thought he was, particularly after financial records supposedly linked him to the terrorist Syed Ali. Bob was unconcerned, attempting to assure her that Reza was trustworthy. Kate remained skeptical, however.

CTU was eventually brought in during Kate's investigation and began questioning Reza. Reza denied any involvement with Ali but eventually accused Bob of making the transactions. Bob denied any involvement with Ali as well and eventually admitted he freelanced for the CIA, using his company to pass information for them.

After being detained for several hours, Bob was horrified to learn that it had been Marie who was working with Ali, and that she had murdered Reza and two CTU agents when they discovered her involvement.

Marie was eventually caught and held temporarily at CTU headquarters. Bob visited his daughter and begged her to tell her why she had done the things she had done. Kate, having seen firsthand what her sister was capable of that day, told him Marie wouldn't give him any answers that he or Kate could ever understand.

[edit] 24: The Game

Bob was not seen in 24: The Game but was mentioned when Max kidnaps Kate to force Bob into helping him ship nuclear weapons out of the United States. The plan was foiled when Kate was rescued and Max was killed by Jack Bauer.
