Robert Lee Grim (born May 8, 1945 in Oakland, California) is a former NFL player who played from 1967–1977. He played high school football at Red Bluff High School and college football at Oregon State University. He was selected in the second round of the 1967 NFL Draft by the Minnesota Vikings. He played with them for five seasons before going to the New York Giants, whom he played with for three seasons. He played with the Chicago Bears for a season, and ended his career going back to the Vikings for his final two seasons. He ended his career having played in 133 total games.
