Robert "Bob" Cashell is the current Republican mayor of Reno, Nevada, elected in 2002. He served as Lieutenant Governor of Nevada from 1982 to 1986, and on the University of Nevada System Board of Regents from 1979 to 1982. He is also a prominent buissnessmann, owning the Cashell Enterprises, a hotel/casino company. He bought Bill & Effies, a truck stop, in 1967, and renamed it Boomtown. He sold it in 1988 after turning the property into a casino/resort.
