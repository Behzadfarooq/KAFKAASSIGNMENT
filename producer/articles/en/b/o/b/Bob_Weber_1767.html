Bob Weber

You have new messages (last change).

Bob Weber was the head football coach at the University of Arizona from 1969 to 1972. His record at Arizona stands at 16-26. He also served as the head coach at the University of Louisville from 1980 to 1984. At Louisville he compiled a 20-35 record.
