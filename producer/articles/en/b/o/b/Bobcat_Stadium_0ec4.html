Bobcat Stadium is a 13,500-seat football stadium in Bozeman, Montana. It opened in 1973. It is home to the Montana State University Bobcats football team.

Bobcat Stadium, formerly Reno H. Sales Stadium, was built at the cost of around a half-million dollars and the Stadium was renovated prior to the 1998 season for about $12 million. The facility was designed to accommodate further expansion in the south end zone. The Stadium's Martel Field is named after Bill Martel, the Bozeman contractor who was instrumental in the project's completion.
