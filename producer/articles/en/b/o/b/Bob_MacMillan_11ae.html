Robert Lea MacMillan (born December 3, 1952 in Charlottetown, P.E.I., Canada) is a former professional ice hockey right winger who played in the NHL for 11 seasons from 1974-75 until 1984-85.

MacMillan was drafted 15th overall by the New York Rangers in the 1972 NHL Amateur Draft.
