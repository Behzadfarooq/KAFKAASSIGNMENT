Bob Lee is a rock musician living in Burbank, California. Current drummer for Backbiter, Jon Wahl & The Amadans. Former member of Claw Hammer, Hot Damn!, Mike Watt & The Black Gang, Crawlspace, Fearless Leader, Paper Tulips, Slotnick and many others. Also founding member of punk group The Gits back in their days at Antioch college in Ohio. Bob Lee has also recorded with such rock luminaries as DEVO. In addition, Bob Lee sang lead vocals with the most recent edition of punk rock troublemakers the Rotters.
