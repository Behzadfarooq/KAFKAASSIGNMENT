[edit] Appending "(Still in Progress)"

Also in An Ed Is Born, I noticed you put "still in progress" in the body of the article. If you're actively editing an article, you can tag it {{inuse}} to prevent edit conflicts. However, it's good etiquette to do so only when you're actively editing somnething. If you plan to come back to an article later, don't use the inuse tag to "reserve" it. Feco 07:54, 28 Apr 2005 (UTC)
