Edward Robert (Bob) Burda (born July 16, 1938 in St. Louis, Missouri) is a former first baseman/right fielder in Major League Baseball who played for the St. Louis Cardinals (1962, 1971), San Francisco Giants (1965-66, 1969-70), Milwaukee Brewers (1970) and Boston Red Sox (1972). He batted and threw left handed.

In a seven-season career, Burda was a .224 hitter with 13 home runs ans 78 RBI in 388 games played.

[edit] Best season

  • 1971: Hit .291 and led National League with 14 pinch-hits
