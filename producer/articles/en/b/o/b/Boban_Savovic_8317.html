Boban Savovic

Boban Savovic (pronounced SAH-vo-vich) (born November 27, 1979) is a former college basketball player from Montenegro, Yugoslavia. Savovic played for the Ohio State University men's basketball program from 1998-2002.


[edit] Controversy

He received money from former Ohio State head coach Jim O'Brien. O'Brien was fired on June 8, 2004. Ohio State was placed on three years probation by the NCAA on March 10, 2006.
