[edit] Copyright violation

Hi there! While we appreciate your efforts to help build our encyclopedia, adding copyrighted material from external websites without permission is against our policy and may result in legal action against Wikipedia in the future. Please refrain from adding this type of material. You can either ask the copyright owner to release the material under the GFDL or a more liberal license like free use, or you can write a short article yourself with a link to the original site you used. If you continue to add copyrighted material in this manner, however, you will eventually be blocked from further editing of Wikipedia, so please do not do so.
