Bob and Brian is a morning radio show based at WHQG in Milwaukee, Wisconsin that lasts from around 5:45 a.m. - 10:20 a.m. They are also syndicated throughout Wisconsin.

[edit] Daily segments

  • Steve Czaban does a daily sports segment.
  • You (Still) Can't Win - contestants try to correctly answer 5 randomly selected trivia questions (from a pool of 26 questions)
  • One Question Line - "Where you, yes you, are granted the opportunity to ask one question...but one question only. Don't start your questions with 'Can you tell me..., Do you know..., I was wondering..., or My question is...' We reserve the right to end any call at any time for any reason." - Brian Nelson
  • This Day in History - events that happened on the date in history
  • News / Sports / Showbiz News

[edit] Occasional segments

  • Fan Letters - Listeners are given the opportunity to send in stories on various themes throughout the year. These range from Bad Break-up stories, lawn care disaster stories, home improvement gone wrong, bad camping experiences, Christmas time horror stories, and more. Every entry that is read on the show gets put into a hopper. At the end of the reading session (which usually lasts one week with two sets of stories over the course of one morning) one letter is selected at random to receive a grand prize that usually has something to do with the theme of the stories sent in. For example, when the camping stories were read in June 2006, the grand prize winner received a GPS navigation unit and Binoculars. Every entry that is read also receives a small prize as well.

[edit] Frequent guests

  • DVD Reviews - by Mark Metcalf reviews DVDs
  • Chris Capuano - the Milwaukee Brewers pitcher is interviewed every Wednesday during the baseball season
  • Santana Dotson - Former Green Bay Packer calls the show every tuesday during football season.
  • Fireman Jim - Firefighter and DJ who stops in the studio occasionally for both a funny story and a safety tip.
  • Matt Kenseth - call-in guest each Thursday, driver of the #17 DeWalt Ford Fusion in the NASCAR Nextel Cup Series, provides previews and recaps throughout the racing season.
