Bobbili Raja (Telugu: బొబ్బిలి రాజా) is a 1990 Tollywood film directed by B. Gopal and produced by Suresh Productions. It has Venkatesh in the lead role and Divya Bharati, Vanisree, and Kaikala Satyanarayana in other roles. Music was scored by Ilayaraja.

[edit] Box-office

  • The film had a 175-day run in 3 centres.It is Venkatesh's first silver-jubilee hit movie.[1]
