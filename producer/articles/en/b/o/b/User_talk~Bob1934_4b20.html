Units for CW3 Robert Lee Sykes US Army Retired.

UNITS of Robert Lee Sykes Oct 1953 to Sep 1975. Basic Training 4th Army Logan Heights Fort Bliss, TX Oct 1953 to Dec 1953. Advanced Inf. Training 63rd Inf. Div 6th Army Fort Ord, CA. Dec 1953 to Mar 1954. Leadership Training 6th Div 6th Army East Garrison Fort Ord, CA. Mar 1954 to May 1954. First Duty Station Co. G 33rd Inf. Reg & Co.E 20th Inf. Reg “SYKES REGULARS” Fort Kobbe CZ May 1954 to Sep 1956. 75th AAA Msl Bn Nike Ajax Missile Site Brandywine, MD Feb 1957 to Feb 1958. United States Army Air Defense School Student Bn Fort Bliss, TX Feb 1958 to Feb 1959. 71st Arty Nike Hercules Missile Site Waldorf, MD Mar 1959 to Dec 1962. 43rd Arty Nike Hercules Missile Site Anchorage, Alaska Dec 1962 to Jun 1964. 517th Arty Nike Hercules Missile Site Abilene,TX Jun 1965 to Jun 1966. 68th Arty Nike Hercules Missile Site Mound, MN Jun 1966 to Apr 1967. 52nd Arty Nike Hercules Missile Site Key Largo, FL Apr 1967 to Oct 1968. 44th Arty Nike Hercules Missile Site Sak Son Ni, Korea Oct 1968 to Dec 1969. United States Army Air Defense School Maintain Nike Radar Equipment in School Park. Dec 1969 to Aug 1970. Bootstrap Student at the University of Nebraska at Omaha Aug 1970 to Aug 1971. Staff and Faculty of the United States Army Air Defense School Instructor in the Computer Section for the Perimeter Acquisition Radar (PAR) Spartan/Sprint Missile System Fort Bliss, TX Aug 1971 to Aug 1972. Assigned to the Anti-Ballistic-Missile (ABM) Unit at Ft. Bliss, TX and trained with other officers to maintain the PAR site in Concrete, ND. Aug 1972 to Aug 1973. Station at Concrete, ND on the PAR site Aug 1973 to Aug 1975. Retired from the United States Army on 1 Sep 1975 at Fort Leavenworth, KA.

Ord, CA Jan 1954 to Mar 1954, Leadership School East Garrison Ft. Ord, CA Mar 1954 to May 1954, Tropic & Jungle Warfare Training Ft. Bliss, TX Aug 1967 to Oct 1967, Battery Control Officer Training Key Largo, FL Oct 1967 to Nov 1967, Chaparral/Vulcan System Technician Course No. 1-70 Ft. Bliss, TX Jul 1970 to Sep 1970, Digital Computer Technology Course No. 4-72 Ft. Bliss, TX Aug 1971 to Nov 1971, Safeguard Data Processing System Key Personnel Course No. 2-72 Ft. Bliss, TX Jan 1972 to Mar 1972, Instructor Training Course No. 5-73 Ft. Bliss, TX Oct 1972 to Nov 1972, & Safeguard Staff Officer Course No. 2-74 Ft. Bliss, TX Jan 1974 to Mar 1974.
