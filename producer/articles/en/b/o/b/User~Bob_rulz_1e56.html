Bob rulz (real name Jason) is a 16-year old from the Sugar House neighborhood of Salt Lake City, Utah. I have a variety of interests. I have minor scientific skepticism, severe arachnophobia, and a fear of flying (haven't been on an airplane since I was 18 months old, and I dread going on one again). My vote for adminship, from September 20 to September 27, 2005, failed with a vote of 7 for, 11 against, and 1 neutral. I currently plan to apply for adminship again soon.

My main scientific interests include geography, climatology, space exploration, astronomy, history (especially in a modern context), urban development, and transportation. My hobbies include video games (mostly computer games), bowling, science fiction, Dungeons & Dragons, theatre, reading, writing, television, playing the viola, the studying of music trends, and especially the listening of music. I listen to many varieties of rock and lighter metal, alternative, electronica, industrial, some punk, some pop, power pop, new wave, synthpop, some indie, and various other genres. I also follow the NFL and NBA, being a Denver Broncos and Utah Jazz fan, respectively. I'm not much into college sports, but my school would be Utah State University.

[edit] Current Wiki-status

  • Wikipedia:WikiProject Tropical cyclones - A few months ago I began a copy-editing project for the vast majority of the tropical cyclone-related articles. I put it on hiatus, but plan to return to it very soon. It's a lot of work, but I don't mind it. It's satisfying, in a way. Also, I will be paying some attention to the 2006-07 Southern Hemisphere tropical cyclone season during the Atlantic and East Pacific offseasons.
  • Wikipedia:WikiProject Utah - I really wish to get Salt Lake City, Utah to featured article status...I have been trying hard for 2 years now, and I never plan to give up until the task is accomplished. It just needs more referencing and a bit more trimming, and I'm set! Then I will do some clean-up and updating of the side articles, and then I will submit it to FAC. After that I'll probably move on to improving the core Utah article and to several of the major cities (especially Taylorsville, Utah and West Valley City, Utah, which both need significant work). Hopefully by that time both Salt Lake City, Utah and Great Salt Lake will be featured articles!
  • Wikipedia:WikiProject Meteorology - I have created the United States weather of 2006 article and continue to update it, while also updating winter storms of 2006-07. The core articles could really use some expansion, but I simply haven't had enough time to work on them. This wikiproject seems to have been kick-started and currently experiences at least a flare of activity.
  • Continued updating of 2007 in video gaming.
  • Major overhauls of the 1990s and 2000s articles.
  • My current Wikistress is moderate, so I may take some time to carry out these updates, and may periodically set some of these projects aside.
