Bobrovec

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Location of Liptovský Mikuláš District in the Zilina Region

Bobrovec is a village and municipality in Liptovský Mikuláš District in the Žilina Region of northern Slovakia.

[edit] History

In historical records the village was first mentioned in 1231.

[edit] Geography

The municipality lies at an altitude of 634 metres and covers an area of 31.132km². It has a population of about 1775 people.[1]
