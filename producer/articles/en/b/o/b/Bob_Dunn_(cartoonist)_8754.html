Bob Dunn (cartoonist)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

For other persons named Bob Dunn, see Bob Dunn (disambiguation).

Bob Dunn ( March 5, 1908 â€“ January 31, 1989 ) was an American cartoonist.

Dunn was known for his work on They'll Do It Every Time for which he won National Cartoonist Society Newspaper Panel Cartoon Award for the years 1968, 1969, and 1979 (with Al Scaduto), and their Reuben Award in 1975 for his work on the strip. He also received their Elzie Segar Award in 1975.
