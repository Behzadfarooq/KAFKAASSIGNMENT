Bob Stagner (b. July 13, 1957 in Chattanooga, Tennessee) is an American drummer and percussionist who has worked with a wide range of artists in every discipline, from Blueground Undergrass to Derek Bailey and Howard Finster. Stagner is the co-founder of the free improvisation duo the Shaking Ray Levis, and its arts education organization, the Shaking Ray Levi Society, and is involved with The Rhythmic Arts Project, founded by drummer Eddie Tuduri.
