Bob Kakaha

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Bob Kakaha, known as Bobzilla was the bassist of Damageplan (until the band's guitarist was tragically shot at a concert in Ohio). He met the late "Dimebag" Darrell Abbott and Vinnie Paul Abbott through tattooing.

Robert 'Bob' Kakaha was born in Los Angeles, California. He was raised in the Silver Lake neighborhood of Los Angeles. He now is a resident of Dallas, Texas.
