Robert "Bob" Rickard founded Fortean Times, The Journal of Strange Phenomena, in 1973 to continue the work of Charles Fort. Rickard has written several books and hundreds of articles on a wide range of Fortean topics. In 1981, he was a founding member of ASSAP and is also the founder of the Charles Fort Institute.
