Bobbysocks (album)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

You have new messages (last change).

Bobbysocks was the first an album from Norwegian pop duo Bobbysocks. The album was released in 1984 and in a new version in 1985. The re-release became popular in many parts of the world, and by early 1986 the album had sold around 75 000 copies in Norway.
