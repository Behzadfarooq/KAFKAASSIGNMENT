Bobby Frank Cherry (June 20, 1930 in Mineral Springs, Alabama - November 18, 2004 at Kilby Correctional Facility, Montgomery) was convicted in 2002 for the 16th Street Baptist Church bombing. A former Ku Klux Klan member, Cherry always denied his involvement in the 1963 crime, which killed four African-American girls.
