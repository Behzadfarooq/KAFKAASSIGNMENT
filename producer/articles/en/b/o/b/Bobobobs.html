Bobobobs was an animated children's television series. It was produced by the Catalan company BRB Internacional in 1988. In Catalan it was called 'Els Bobobobs', but when it was translated into English by Telefilm Canada it was merely entitled Bobobobs. It was also translated into Dutch, German and Italian. It was directed and adapted by Tim Reid, based on the characters created by Henk and Nerida Zwart. Each episode was 23 minutes long, and 26 episodes were made.

[edit] Plot

A race of miniature people called Bobobobs on a far away planet set a ship off to find the Earth. The ship is called the Bobular Quest. The ship is designed in the style of the old wooden ships. Along the way they have many adventures encountering pink space monsters, giant crabs and pirates, among many others. The Bobs also have the amazing ability to make themselves invisible and do other magical things if they join hands and concentrate.

[edit] Characters

Little Bob, the captain, Blip and Cornelius
  • Captain Bob Wouter - the kindly and level-headed captain of the Bobular Quest, he has a wooden leg.
  • Little Bob - The Captain's son, who often gets into mischief.
  • Blip - a floating purple head with arms, it is Little Bob's best friend.
  • Cornelius - a wisened old man, who has a long white beard. He wears a purple wizards hat, and is an astronomer.
  • Wilbur - the dim witted carpenter. Petronella's brother.
  • Petronella - a mean woman who is constantly thinking of her own (non-existent) beauty.
  • A.D. - Petronella's long suffering husband.
  • Fritz - The chef who can't cook, a real grump.
  • Aunt Agatha - A kindly old lady who does a lot of baking.
  • Ein - A scientist, Stein's brother.
  • Stein - A scientist, Ein's brother.
  • Peter - A young man, who often helps out Cornelius.
  • Doc Bone - The Doctor.
  • Nurse Miny - Doc Bone's assistant.
