Bob Gagliano (born September 5, 1958 in Los Angeles, California), was a former American Football player. He began his career playing quarterback for Glendale College, Southwestern Louisiana. He then played for United States International University (now Alliant International University) in San Diego, and Utah State University. He was drafted by the Kansas City Chiefs in 1981 and, until his playing career ended in 1995, he played for six NFL teams as well as the USFLâ€™s Denver Gold.
