Bob Suter (born May 16, 1957 in Madison, Wisconsin was an American ice hockey defenseman.

Bob is best known for being a member of the Miracle On Ice 1980 U.S. Olympic hockey team won the gold medal team. Though he was drafted by the Los Angeles Kings of the NHL and the New England Whalers of the WHA, he never played an NHL game. Bob's brother Gary played in the NHL. His son, Ryan, is enjoying a successful early career in the NHL's Nashville Predators organization.

Bob attended the University of Wisconsin.
