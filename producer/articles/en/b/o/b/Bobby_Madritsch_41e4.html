Bobby Madritsch born February 28, 1976 in Oak Lawn, IL is a current Major League Baseball Player. He is a pitcher.

[edit] Life

Madritsch is one of only a two current Native American MLB players. The other is Kyle Lohse. He attended Point Park College in Pennsylvania.

[edit] Baseball career

Madritsch has pitched for the Seattle Mariners and Kansas City Royals.
