Bob Lazarus

Bob Lazarus is an American stand-up comedian and film actor.

Lazarus attended Boston College and graduated from the University of Massachusetts.[1]

He has appeared on Showtime and Comedy Central. Lazarus has toured the United States with Steven Wright.[2] He has also appeared with Robin Williams, Jay Leno, Rob Schneider, Denis Leary, Judy Tenuta, Sinbad, Paula Poundstone and Ray Romano.[3]

Lazarus is married and has a daughter.[4] He lives in Massachusetts when not touring.
