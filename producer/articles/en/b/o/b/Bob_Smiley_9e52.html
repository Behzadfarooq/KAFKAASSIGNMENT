Bob Smiley is a Christian comedian. He has been featured at the Spirit West Coast and Festival Con Dios music festivals. Bob also recently opened for Switchfoot in front of crowds reaching sixty thousand people.

Smiley sends an infrequent newsletter that contains jokes and information about upcoming events and albums.
