Bobbie Rosenfeld Award

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Bobbie Rosenfeld Award is an annual award given to Canada's best female athlete. The award is named after the late Bobbie Rosenfeld, a Olympic medal track and field athlete. The award, first given in 1933, is voted upon by members of the Canadian Press.
