[edit] About

Wikipedia is one of the greatest internet ideas ever! I hope to contribute to it and give back to what Wikipedia has gave to me, a never ending amount of free knowledge!

[edit] Wikipedia Interests

My favorite interests include articles about Canada and Australia, especially on their human geography, such as cities, places of interest etc. Although I'm from Canada, I did a tour of Australia for almost one month in July 2006 and absolutely love the place. Other interests of mine include vexillology (Canada and Australia of course), ice hockey, Canadian numismatics to name a few. I try to help out Category:Category needed when I can by listing sub-par articles for Category:Candidates for speedy deletion.
