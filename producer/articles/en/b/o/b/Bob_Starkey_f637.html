Bob Starkey

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Bob Starkey (born September 7, 1959) is the interim head coach of the LSU Lady Tigers basketball team. He was announced as the interim head coach after Pokey Chatman resigned on March 7, 2007 and then stepped down immediately on March 8, 2007. In his first month as a head coach, Starkey led the Lady Tigers in the NCAA Women's Basketball tournament to its fourth straight Final Four. Starkey has stated that he has no desire to become LSU's head coach on a permanent , and hopes that his successor will keep him on as an assistant coach.[1]

[edit] Coaching career

  • 1984-87: Assistant Coach, West Virginia State University
  • 1988-89: Women's Assistant Coach, Marshall University
  • 1990-1996: Assistant Coach, LSU Men's Basketball
  • 1996-1997: Assistant Coach, LSU Men's & Women's Basketball
  • 1998-2007: Assistant Coach, LSU Women's Basketball
  • 2007-: Interim Head Coach, LSU Women's Basketball
