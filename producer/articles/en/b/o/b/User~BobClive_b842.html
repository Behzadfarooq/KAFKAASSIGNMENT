I am BobClive. Fond of Dupleix, I have chosen to live in Paris. In addition to English and French, I am more or less familiar with two more European and half a dozen Indian languages. My main subject is pre-Gandhian freedom movement in British India. I am also interested in comparative literature and linguistics, philosophy and ethnomusicology.
