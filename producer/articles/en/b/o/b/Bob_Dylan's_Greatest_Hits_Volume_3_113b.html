Bob Dylan's Greatest Hits Volume 3 is the third official compilation album by Bob Dylan, released in 1994.

All songs are by Bob Dylan, except where noted.

  1. "Tangled Up in Blue" – 5:42
  2. "Changing of the Guards" – 6:36
  3. "The Groom's Still Waiting at the Altar" – 4:03
  4. "Hurricane" (Dylan/Levy) – 8:34
  5. "Forever Young" – 4:58
  6. "Jokerman" – 6:16
  7. "Dignity" (First release of this song which was recorded during sessions for 'Oh, Mercy' album) – 5:58
  8. "Silvio" (Dylan/Hunter) – 3:07
  9. "Ring Them Bells" – 3:02
  10. "Gotta Serve Somebody" – 5:25

  12. "Brownsville Girl" (Dylan/Shepard) – 11:04

  14. "Knockin' on Heaven's Door" – 2:30
