[edit] Bob Girard

Bob Girard (born April 12, 1949 in Montreal, Quebec) is a retired Canadian ice hockey player.

He was signed as a free agent in 1973 by the California Golden Seals. After playing a season in California, he followed the team when it relocated to Cleveland. After parts of two seasons with the Barons, he played parts of three seasons with the Washington Capitals.

[edit] Bob Girard

(born October 27, 1949 in New Jersey) is a musician in Charlottesville, Virginia

He attended the University of Virginia and became a musician. He was a main focus of a documentary on the music scene in Charlottesville with many other local musicians, including his close friend Charlie Pastorfield. The film premiered at the Paramount theater on October 28th, 2006 as part of the Virginia Film Festival. He still lives in the Charlottesville area with his wife and three children.
