[edit] Early Life

Bobby Catalano - South America

Robert Anthony Catalano also known as Bobby Catalano was born in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania. Catalano first gained fame on the television program Dance Party USA. Starting as a dancer on the show and than later becoming the host of the hit dance show. He was considered in these times a teen idol who became very popular and was well known for his famous sunglasses which he only removed once a year on the Valentines Day show. Vogue Magazine reporter Lisa Robinson once reported, "He gets over 1000 letters a week. He has a sexy gravely voice, and has taken off his trade-mark sunglasses only once." Model magazine offered this comment. "As the records spin, the viewers wondered if Bobby Catalano will take off his omni-present shades." The Philadelphia Inquirer had this to say: "If only Bobby Catalano would take off his sunglasses, just imagine thousands of lovestruck girls would be so much happier." One sample quote from a teen magazine from the 1990's "Any discussion of the most gorgeous guys on any TV show wouldn't be complete without Bobby Catalano. Girls just go wild for a glimpse of Bobby's eyes behind those mysterious shades." In 1993 he was cast in the film Zero Cool directed by Isaac Zepel. Not much is really known about him since he always seemed to be a bit of a mystery.

[edit] Recent Work

Bobby Catalano - Sorany Chavez

More recently Bobby Catalano has created a network of model websites similar to what you would see in Playboy. One of his most famous sites is Selena Spice which he produced and created in 2005. Selena Spice also known as Andrea Rincon is now one of the top internet sex symbols as a result of Catalano's production, she is often compared to Vida Guerra. Catalano also has a number of other very popular model websites in his portfolio, such as Karla Spice 2007 and La Zona Modelos 2004. Currently he is an actor and producer in South America.
