Bobby Rackard

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Bobby Rackard (born 1927 - 1996) was an Irish hurler.

Robert Rackard was born in Killanne, County Wexford on January 6, 1927. He played senior inter-county hurling with Wexford between 1945 and 1957, as well as playing hurling for his local Rathnure Club. He was the brother of Nicky Rackard and Billy Rackard with whom he won two All-Ireland hurling medals in 1955 and 1956. He also played on the Wexford team that lost the All-Ireland finals in 1951 and 1954. Rackard also won National Hurling League and Railway Cup medals. He was a skillful defender noted for his style and sportsmanship, whose career ended prematurely because of injury.
