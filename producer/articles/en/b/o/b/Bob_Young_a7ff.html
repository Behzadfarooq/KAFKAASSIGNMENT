Bob Young

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

For other persons named Bob Young, see Bob Young (disambiguation).

Robert "Bob" Young was born in Ancaster, Ontario, Canada[1] and graduated from the University of Toronto.

He created the ACC Corporation which merged with Red Hat in 1995. From the merger to 1999 Bob Young was Red Hat's CEO.

After leaving Red Hat he started Lulu.com, a self-publishing web-site that claims to be the world's fastest-growing provider of print-on-demand books. He is Lulu.com's CEO.

He also owns the Hamilton Tiger-Cats, Hamilton, Ontario's Canadian Football League franchise.
