Bobby Hamilton (born July 1, 1971) is a National Football League defensive end for the New York Jets. He was released by the Jets on February 28, 2007, and re-signed by the team on March 6, 2007.

[edit] College career

Hamilton attended Southern Miss, where he was a four year letterman and a two-year starter. He finished his career with 18 sacks and 173 tackles(18 for losses). He is also a Super Bowl champion twice over with the New England Patriots. Hamilton was a four year letterman in football, basketball, and track at East Marion High School in Columbia, Mississippi from 1985 -1989.
