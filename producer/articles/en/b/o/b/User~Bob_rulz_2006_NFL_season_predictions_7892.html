Here are my slightly biased NFL predictions for the 2006 season. These are my probably completely wrong pre-season predictions. I am now officially locking the standings table from my own personal editing. These are my final pre-season predictions. Playoff predictions will come sometime during the week before the playoffs start, and I will also make a Super Bowl prediction some time in the week before the Super Bowl. I may also make midseason predictions, if I feel so inclined. Feel free to leave comments on my predictions on the talk page. Note that these predictions are purely for fun, so enjoy, praise, and ridicule them all you want!
