[edit] Top tips for any wikipedian

[edit] Be bold

Many users on wikipedia say this and so will I: if you think something need changing on Wikipedia, then change it! You won't destroy anything if you mess up; thousands of other users can fix any problems you leave behind. If you're really scared of what's about to happen, then at least post something on the talk page.

[edit] Use the preview

It took me around 5 edits to get spoiler tags into one article correctly. This would not have happened had I previewed my edits...
