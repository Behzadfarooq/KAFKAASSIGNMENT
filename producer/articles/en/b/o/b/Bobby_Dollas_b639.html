Bobby Dollas (born January 31, 1965 in Montreal, Quebec) is a former professional ice hockey player.

Dollas played for the Winnipeg Jets, Quebec Nordiques, Detroit Red Wings, Mighty Ducks of Anaheim, Edmonton Oilers, Pittsburgh Penguins, Calgary Flames, Ottawa Senators and San Jose Sharks. He currently coach senior hockey for the Sorel-Tracy Mission of the Ligue nord-américaine de hockey.
