Robert "Bob" Johnson or Mr. Johnson is a fictional character from the animated series, Squirrel Boy. He is voiced by Kurtwood Smith.

[edit] Profile

Mr. Johnson is the father of Andy and husband of Lucille Johnson (Mrs.Johnson). He hates Rodney. In one episode where Rodney enters a Manizo Pet Shampoo contest he mentions paste being edible and misses a full head of hair which he got a full body a hair when Rodney made a hair grown formula and only to become bald. In "Yer Out!" He coached a Little League team called "The Fighting Worms" but got replaced by Rodney and strated to coach the rival team "The Worm Crushers". News reporters made an embarrassing report on him when Rodney replaced him. He also raced in the Melon Crate Derby to get revenge on a Scottish guy who always cheated when he was little. He also lost his voice while watching a comedy about a cow when supposed to give an important speech to the Badger Scouts. He also made a dream lawn which Rodney, Andy, Salty Mike, and Kyle destroyed. He is often referred to as Mr. J by Rodney.
