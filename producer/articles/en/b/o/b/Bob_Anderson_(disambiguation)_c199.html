Bob Anderson (disambiguation)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Bob Anderson has been the name of several people:

  • Bob Anderson, deceased former Formula One driver.
  • Bob Anderson (darts player), world professional darts champion, 1988.
  • Bob Anderson (The Simpsons director), animation director for The Simpsons.
  • Bob Anderson (photographer), Canadian stock photographer and photojournalist.
  • Bob Anderson (fencer), legendary swordmaster and stunt double for Darth Vader in the Star Wars movies.
  • Bob Anderson (baseball), Major League Baseball pitcher from 1957-1963.
  • Bob Anderson (engineer), one of the pioneers of cardiac pacemaking.
  • Bob Anderson (football), member of the College Football Hall of Fame.
  • Bob Anderson (runner), Runner's World magazine founder.
