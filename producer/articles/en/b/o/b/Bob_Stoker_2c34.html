Bob Stoker is a Ulster Unionist Party politician and former Member of the Northern Ireland Forum for South Belfast.

He was elected to Northern Ireland Forum for Belfast South in May 1996.

Stoker was elected as Councillor to represent the Balmoral Electoral Area in the by-election of May 1995. He was re-elected as Councillor in May 1997 and June 2001.

He was elected Deputy Lord Mayor in June 1998 and Lord Mayor of Belfast in June 1999.

Bob Stoker is currently a candidate in the 2007 Assembly elections standing in South Belfast.
