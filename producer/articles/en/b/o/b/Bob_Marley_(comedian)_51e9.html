This article is about the comedian named Bob Marley. For the popular reggae musician, see Bob Marley.

Bob Marley is a comedian from Portland, Maine. He delivers jokes mostly about life in New England with an overemphasized New England accent. Bob Marley's first television appearance was on Comedy Central. He also appeared as Detective Greenly in the movie Boondock Saints. He graduated from Deering High School in Portland, Maine in 1985.

As of 2007 he has performed on many television shows including:

  • The Tonight Show with Jay Leno
  • The Late Show with David Letterman
  • Late Night with Conan O'Brien
  • The Late Late Show with Craig Kilborn
  • MAD TV
  • Prime Time Live with Diane Sawyer
  • NBC's Comedy Showcase
  • Rodney Dangerfield's Comedy Cure on TBS
  • Mars and Venus with Cybill Shepard
  • The Martin Short Show
  • Comedy Central Presents
