Bobby Vinton's All-Time Greatest Hits is a two-LP collection of previously recorded songs by Bobby Vinton, released in 1972 by Epic Records. It reached #119 on the Billboard Hot 200 list of popular albums. It contains 11 of Vinton's previous hit singles and one album track ("I'll Make You My Baby").
