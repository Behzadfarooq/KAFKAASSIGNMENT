Bob Peak

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Movie poster Camelot

Robert "Bob" M. Peak (May 30, 1927 â€“ August 1, 1992) was a pioneer in the development of the modern movie poster. His paintings and illustrations were enormously influential.

His artwork has been on the cover of Time magazine, TV Guide, and Sports Illustrated. He also did many advertisements and even some stamps. Some of the movie posters he has done are: Apocalypse Now, Star Trek I to VI, Superman: The Movie, West Side Story, Rollerball, My Fair Lady, Camelot and In Like Flint.
