Brian and Bobbie Houston

Bobbie Houston is a senior pastor of the Hillsong Church in Sydney, Australia. She is married to Brian Houston, the church's other senior pastor.

Bobbie Houston's ministry is mainly aimed at women. She is the author of the books I'll have what she's having and Heaven is in this house.

Her husband credits her with a significant portion of the early growth of Hillsong Church.

She was born in New Zealand and married Brian Houston in February 1977.

Recently, she has been helping to raise money for the victims of the Indian Ocean tsunami of 2004.
