Bobby Deen (born April 28, 1970 in Albany, Georgia) is a chef and businessman. He is best known as one of the sons of Paula Deen, and together with his brother Jamie runs her restaurant, The Lady & Sons, in Savannah, Georgia. He also frequently appears on her show, Paula's Home Cooking, and with brother Jamie Deen, has been hosting his own show, Road Tasted, since July 11, 2006.

Deen was also named one of the 50 Most Elgible Bachelors by People Magazine in the 2006 issue.
