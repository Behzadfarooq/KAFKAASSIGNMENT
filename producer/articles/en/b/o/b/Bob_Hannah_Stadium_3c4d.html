Bob Hannah Stadium is a baseball stadium located at the University of Delaware. It plays host to the Delaware Blue Hens Baseball team. The stadium's namesake, Bob Hannah, retired as head coach in 2000. The stadium seats 1,300 people for baseball. Features of the stadium include an enclosed press box, an outdoor batting cage, and banners on the outfield fence signifying Delaware's numerous conference titles and NCAA appearances. The field dimensions are 330 down the left and rightfield lines, 365 in right center and left center, and 400 to center field.
