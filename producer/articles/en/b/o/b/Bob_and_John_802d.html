Bob and John is a thoroughbred horse. A foal of 2003, he ran in the 750,000 dollar wood memorial and finished first in front of Jazil and Keyed Entry. Later he ran in the Kentucky Derby and finished in 17. He also ran in the Belmont Stakes and finished 8th

[edit] Connections

Bob and John is owned by Stonerside Stable. He is trained by Bob Baffert. He is ridden by Garrett Gomez. Bob and John was bred in Kentucky by Stonerside Stable.

[edit] Breeding

Bob and John is the son of Seeking The Gold out of the mare Minister's Melody. His sire is a son of Mr. Prospector and his dam is from Deputy Minister.
