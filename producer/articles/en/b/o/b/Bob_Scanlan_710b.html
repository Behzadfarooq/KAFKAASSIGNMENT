Bob Scanlan

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Robert Guy Scanlan Jr. (born August 9, 1966 in Los Angeles, California), is a former professional baseball player who pitched in the Major Leagues from 1991-2001.

[edit] Teams

  • Chicago Cubs 1991-1993
  • Milwaukee Brewers 1994-1995, 2000
  • Detroit Tigers 1996
  • Kansas City Royals 1996
  • Houston Astros 1998
  • Montreal Expos 2001

[edit] Pitching Stats

  • 290 Games
  • 20 Wins
  • 34 Losses
  • 17 Saves
  • 245 Strikeouts
  • 4.63 ERA
