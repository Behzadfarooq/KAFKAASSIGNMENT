Bob Ryan is a meteorologist, currently forecasting for WRC-TV. He has served as the station's chief meteorologist since 1980, and was previously the Today Show's first on-air meteorologist. He releases an annual meteorology almanac that details weather events of the past year. It also contains information such as average highs and lows for each day of the year and significant weather events that happened on that date in history.
