Robert "Bob" Reginald Hope is a fictional character on the ITV soap opera Emmerdale. He is played by Tony Audenshaw.

[edit] About Bob

Bob arrived in Emmerdale in 2000 as a representative for Naughty Nylons. Viv Windsor and he were attracted to each other and got together. Bob moved in with her and tried to make friends with her daughter Donna. He proposed to her when he was drunk, but they still got married. Bob then had to deal with his daughter Dawn turning up and getting engaged to his friend, the much older Terry Woods. Viv and Bob were divorced in 2005 but in the same year became engaged again and married on February 15, 2006.

[edit] Wives

Bob has been married six times to four women, he was first married to Jean Hope, who he married twice, the mother of his eldest children Dawn and Jamie, but the marriage ended in divorce when he walked out. His second wife was Barbara Hope, again they had two children Josh and Carly but this marriage again ended in divorce when he walked out. His third marriage was to American exotic dancer, Vonda, this was a disaster as they were only married for 24 hours, again this ended in divorce but Vonda gave birth to a daughter, Roxy that he didn't know about until she was tracked down by Viv. Finally his fourth and final wife was Viv Hope, who he married for the first time in 2001 but it ended in divorce in 2005, but they remarried on 15th February 2006.
