Bobby Shaw (born 4/23/1975) is a NFL wide receiver who is currently a free agent. Shaw has played for 5 NFL teams: Seattle Seahawks, Pittsburgh Steelers, Jacksonville Jaguars, Buffalo Bills, and San Diego Chargers.
