Bob Young (news anchor)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Bob Young was a TV news journalist for ABC News. He served as the anchor of The ABC Evening News (now, World News) from October 1967 to May 1968. His only noteworthy broadcast would be on April 4, 1968, when he anchored ABC's coverage of the assassination of Dr. Martin Luther King Jr.
