Welcome to my user page
My name is Bobo192. I am 21 years old, and I am an English born-and-based Wikipedian. I am interested in sports, including soccer, baseball, and Formula One motor racing, and sleeping (something about which my darling fiancée will claim that I am something of an authority figure...)
Me and Wikipedia
I started contributing to Wikipedia in 2004, when I was accidentally sent here by a friend trying to Google me into information I couldn't find elsewhere. I was immediately hooked and, 554 days later, became an administrator.
 
What have I done?
I have written over 1,350 Wikipedia articles, mostly about cricketers and soccer players.
 
What have I not done?
Taken over the world. But seriously, I have a whole list of articles in my creation queue which I have not yet started. There's plenty for me to be getting along with there.
