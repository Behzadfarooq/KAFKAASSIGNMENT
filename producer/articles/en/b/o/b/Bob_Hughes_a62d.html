Dr. Robert "Bob" Hughes. M.D. is a fictional character on the American soap opera, As The World Turns. Bob was original played by Bobby Alford from 1956 to April 1958 then it was actor Ronnie Welch from June 1958 to September 1960 and finally and currently by actor Don Hastings since October 7th, 1960 to the present.
