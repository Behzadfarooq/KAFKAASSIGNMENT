Robert Charles 'Bob' Sweikert (born May 20, 1926 Los Angeles, CA, died June 17 1956 Salem, Indiana) was an American racing driver, best known as the winner of the 1955 Indianapolis 500 and 1955 National Championship. He died in 1956 after crashing a Sprint car at Salem Speedway.

[edit] World Championship career summary
