Robert Douglas Johnson (born September 18, 1946 in Cleveland, Tennessee) is a former American football center who played twelve seasons with the Cincinnati Bengals first in the American Football League, and then in the National Football League. He was the last original Bengal to retire. His uniform number 54 was retired by the team.

Johnson played college football at the University of Tennessee and in 1989 was inducted into the College Football Hall of Fame.
