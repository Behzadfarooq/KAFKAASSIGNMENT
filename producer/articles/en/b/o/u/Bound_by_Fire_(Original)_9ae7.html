Debut album from Zimmer's Hole. This edition of the CD may be out of print. Some copies (still sealed) surface on eBay quite often. It was later re-issued on Century Media Records with bonus tracks. Devin Townsend produced this CD underneath the alias "Dr.Skinny" (Also the name he uses for Punky Brüster)
