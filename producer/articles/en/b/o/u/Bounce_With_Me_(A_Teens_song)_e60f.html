Bounce With Me was an A*Teens special song for the promotional campaign of a new drink called Pop Drinks in Sweden.

The song was recorded during the "Pop 'Til You Drop!" recording sessions. At one point, it was even on the track list of the album, but it was eventually removed for contractual reasons.

The song was released on radio and on TV spots, but it never had a commercial release, although thousands of promotional copies were distributed within Scandinavia.
