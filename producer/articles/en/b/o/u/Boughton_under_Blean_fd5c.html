Boughton under Blean is a village and civil parish in the Swale district of Kent, England, with a population of 1,865 according to the 2001 census.

Part of Swale district, the village is situated on the A2 road, between Faversham and Canterbury.

There is a village web site at: [http://www.boughton-under-blean.org.uk ]
