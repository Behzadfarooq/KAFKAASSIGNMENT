Bourassa-Sauvé is a provincial electoral district in Quebec, Canada that elects members to the National Assembly of Quebec. The district is located in Montreal and consists of most of the former city of Montreal North. The riding was created in 2003 from parts of Bourassa and Sauvé.

[edit] Members of the National Assembly

  1. Line Beauchamp, Liberal (2003-present)
