Bouère is a commune of the arrondissement of Château-Gontier in the Mayenne département of the Pays-de-la-Loire région of France.

[edit] Geography

In the South East of Mayenne, it is an old country of Anjou, located between Grez-en-Bouère and Saint Denis d'Anjou. City located on marble careers having taken part in the development of Basilica of the Sacré Cœur, and the station of the Gare Saint-Lazare in Paris.
