Bournda is a national park in New South Wales (Australia), 345 km southwest of Sydney.

[edit] Fact sheet

  • Area: 26 km²
  • Coordinates: 36°48′36″S, 149°55′07″E
  • Date of establishment: April 24, 1992
  • Managing authorities: New South Wales National Parks and Wildlife Service
  • IUCN category: II
