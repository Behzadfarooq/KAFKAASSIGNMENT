Bounce Back is the original first single from Stacie Orrico's Say It Again EP. It was released to radio in August 2002, but when Virgin Records epxressed interest in Stacie the album was re-recorded so the album could be more mainstream. Say It Again EP became the Stacie Orrico album with a new version of Bounce Back.
