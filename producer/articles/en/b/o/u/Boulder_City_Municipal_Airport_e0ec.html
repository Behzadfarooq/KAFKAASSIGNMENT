Boulder City Municipal Airport (IATA: BLD, FAA LID: 61B) is a public airport located one mile (1.6 km) southwest of the central business district of Boulder City, in Clark County, Nevada, USA.

[edit] History

Boulder City Municipal Airport replaced Boulder City Airport that started out as Bullock Airport.

[edit] Facilities

Boulder City Municipal Airport covers an area of 530 acres (214 ha) which contains three runways:

  • Runway 9L/27R: 2,200 x 60 ft. (671 x 18 m), Surface: Asphalt
  • Runway 9R/27L: 4,800 x 75 ft. (1,463 x 23 m), Surface: Asphalt
  • Runway 15/33: 3,850 x 75 ft. (1,173 x 23 m), Surface: Asphalt

[edit] Airlines and destinations

  • Papillon Grand Canyon Helicopters (Grand Canyon)
  • Scenic Airlines (Grand Canyon)
