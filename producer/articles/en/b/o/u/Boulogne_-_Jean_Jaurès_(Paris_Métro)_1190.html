Boulogne - Jean Jaurès (Paris Métro)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Boulogne - Jean Jaurès is a station on the Paris Métro, serving Line 10, in Boulogne-Billancourt, on the Boulevard Jean Jaurès. Jean Jaurès (1859–1914) was a French Socialist leader.

East of this station Line 10 splits into separate eastbound and westbound sections until Javel - André Citroën station.
