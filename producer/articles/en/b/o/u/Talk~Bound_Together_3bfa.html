[edit] ReBound Edition

Bound Together: ReBound has been released. For some reason, the page is next to impossible to find. (It was a miracle that I even found the site) Most, if not all, of the existing links to the Bound Together site don't work. Even a google search will give the old (defunct) link. I added the correct link to the list at the bottem of the article and modified the track listing to include the new tracks. There are a few additional touches that need to be made to update this article.

1. I marked the new tracks with bullets, but this makes the track list look messy. Maybe there is an alternate way to indicate the new tracks?

2. The "ReBound" section is out of date. I don't know enough about the project to write it myself, so I am leaving it to somebody who knows what they are doing.

3. I did my best to determine the source tracks for each of the new tunes, but there were a few for which I wasn't sure. I have marked those tracks with question marks. If someone could confirm them, I would appreciate it.

4. The credits don't currently include contributors to ReBound. I don't know who did what, so that needs to be filled out by somebody else.
