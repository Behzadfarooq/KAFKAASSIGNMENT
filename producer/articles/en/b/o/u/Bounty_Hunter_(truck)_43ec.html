Bounty Hunter is a monster truck currently racing in the USHRA Monster Jam series. It is owned and driven by Jimmy Creten, a former mud racer. The truck is sponsored by CSK Auto. It has been to the final round of three Monster Jam World Finals racing events and the truck was the 2005 Monster Jam Freestyle champion. This is not t be confused with a truck of the identical name driven by Brian Bellini back in the early 1990's.
