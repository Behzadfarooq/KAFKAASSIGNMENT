Bou Inania

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Court of the Bou Inania madersa

Bou Inania (Arabic: المدرسة البوعنانية‎ - Al-madrasa Al-abū `inānīya) is a madrasa (Islamic learning center) founded by the Marinid ruler Abu Inan Faris in Meknes, Morocco in 1350.

The madrasa is considered as the best monument the Marinids have left. It is one of the marvels of Islamic architecture. The columns and doors have wonderful decorations, including the writings of carved ornaments.

The monument contains a prayer hall, a place for wudu, classrooms and a large patio.
