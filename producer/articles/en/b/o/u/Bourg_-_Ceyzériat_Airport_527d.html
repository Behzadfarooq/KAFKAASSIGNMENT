Bourg - Ceyzériat Airport (French: Aéroport de Bourg - Ceyzériat) (IATA: XBK, ICAO: LFHS) is an airport located 5 km east of Bourg-en-Bresse and northwest of Ceyzériat, both communes of the department of Ain in France.
