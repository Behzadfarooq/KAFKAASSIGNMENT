Bound 4 Da Reload (Casualty) was a 2000 single and so far the biggest success for British group Oxide & Neutrino, members of the So Solid Crew, which peaked at No. 1. The repetitive and electronically-mixed track features a sample from the theme tune to the TV show Casualty.

While the record appeared on Top of the Pops in its Number One week, many radio stations refused to play "Bound 4 Da Reload" because of its controversial spoken lyrics ("Oh, ****, I've been shot! I can't believe this/ Could everyone stop getting shot?"). These lyrics were sampled from the film Lock, Stock and Two Smoking Barrels.
