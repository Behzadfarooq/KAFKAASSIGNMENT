Bourvari

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

 This Iran location article is a stub. You can help Wikipedia by expanding it.

Bourvari (Armenian: Բուրւարի) is a collection of villages in Iran, between the city of Khomein (Markazi Province) and Aligoodarz (Lorestān Province).

Bourvari was mainly populate by Armenians who were brought to the region by Shah Abbas of the Safavid dynasty in 1603 and 1604.

The following is a list of villages, which were part of Bourvari:

  1. Dehno
  2. Khorzend
  3. Farajabad
  4. Bahmanabad
  5. Sangesfid
  6. Khakvar
