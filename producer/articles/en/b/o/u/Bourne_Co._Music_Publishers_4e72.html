Bourne Co. Music Publishers

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Bourne Co. Music Publishers is an American publisher of sheet music.

The company was founded in the 1920s and is one of the largest privately held international music publishers in the world. Its office is in New York City. Its copyrights consist of classical, modern, standards, Latin American Pop, Spirituals, Jazz, Country, Seasonal, Film, and Children's music.

Its subsidiaries include:

  • BOURNE MUSIC CANADA LIMITED
  • BOURNE MUSIC FRANCE
  • BOURNE MUSIC LTD. (U.K.)
