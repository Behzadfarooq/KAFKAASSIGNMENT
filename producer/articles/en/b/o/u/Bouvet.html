[edit] Places

  • Bouvet Island - an uninhabited island in the South Atlantic.

[edit] People

  • Jean-Baptiste Charles Bouvet de Lozier (1705–1786) - French explorer, discovered Bouvet Island.
  • François Joseph Bouvet de Précourt (1753–1832) - French admiral.
  • Pierre François Étienne Bouvet de Maisonneuve (1775–1860) - French admiral.
  • Gustave Bouvet, French anarchist and attempted assassin.

[edit] Things
