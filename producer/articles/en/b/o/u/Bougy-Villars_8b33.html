Bougy-Villars

Bougy-Villars is a municipality in the Swiss the canton of Vaud, located in the district of Aubonne. This village has around 700 inhabitants if you count the surrounding houses. Numerous fancy villas and rich neighborhoods group around this community because of its exceptional view of the Lake Leman. This village also houses the famous "Tristan chocolatier" which is considered by many one of the finest chocolate in Switzerland. The village is situated between Rolle and signal de bougy.
