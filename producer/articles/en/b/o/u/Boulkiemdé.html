Boulkiemdé

Boulkiemdé is one of the 45 provinces of Burkina Faso.

The capital of Boulkiemdé is Koudougou.

Departments of Boulkiemdé

Boulkiemde is divided into 13 departments:

  • Bingo
  • Imasgho
  • Kindi
  • Kokologho
  • Koudougou
  • Nanoro
  • Pella
  • Poa
  • Ramongo
  • Sabou
  • Sigle
  • Sourgou
  • Thyou
