Boundary Road (Vancouver)

Beginning of Boundary Road. North Shore Mountains in the background, the Second Narrows Ironworkers Memorial Bridge on the left

Boundary Road is one of the most important traffic corridors in Greater Vancouver, British Columbia, Canada. It runs south from Burrard Inlet to the Fraser River separating Vancouver from Burnaby.

Most of the heavy traffic along Boundary Road moves between Hastings Street and Marine Way near the Fraser River. The northern end of Boundary Road has the least traffic as it provides no access to the Ironworkers Memorial Bridge.
