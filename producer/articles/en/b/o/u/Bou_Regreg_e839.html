Bou Regreg

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Valley of the Bou Regreg near Rabat, Morocco

The Bou Regreg (Arabic: أبو رقراق) is a river located in Morocco, between Rabat and Salé.

The river is 240kms long. Its average discharge is 23 m3/s and can reach 1500 m3/s period of flooding. The source of the river originates from the Middle Atlas mountains at an altitude of 1627 meters on the level of Jbel Mtourzgane (Province of Khemisset) and of Grou (Province of Khénifra) and throws itself in the Atlantic Ocean with its mouth separating the cities of Rabat at south and Salé at north.
