Boucle du Mouhoun Region

Boucle du Mouhoun is one of Burkina Faso's 13 administrative regions. It was created on 2 July 2001 and had a population of 1,353,607 in 2002. The region's capital is Dédougou. Six provinces make up the Boucle du Mouhoun region - Balé, Banwa, Kossi, Mouhoun, Nayala, and Sourou.
