[edit] History

Boulder City Airport was dedicated on December 10, 1933. Commerical air service arrived at the airport on June 15, 1936 with Grand Canyon Airlines. Trans World Airlines opened a terminal at the airport on May 20, 1938. The terminal building was sold in 1958 to the Boulder City Elks Club.[1]
