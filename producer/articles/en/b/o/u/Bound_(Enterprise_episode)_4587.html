"Bound" is the title of a Star Trek: Enterprise television episode from season four.

Archer falls under the spell of Navaar, an Orion slave girl. (This is a production publicity photo; Navaar wears a different costume in the broadcast version.)

[edit] Plot outline

Spoiler warning: Plot and/or ending details follow.

Jonathan Archer finds himself taking custody of three Orion Slave Girls with a hidden agenda, while Trip Tucker and T'Pol discover a deeper aspect to their relationship.

This was the final regular standalone episode of Star Trek: Enterprise, the finale being considered part of the "Birth of the Federation" arc.

[edit] Background Information

  • "The Gorn Hegemony" is mentioned by Harrad-Sar, the Orion ship captain. This is the first mention of the Gorn on Enterprise.
  • This episode reveals a secret about Orion females.
  • At the start of the episode, Archer states in his log that the ship is en route to the Berengaria system. Later in the episode, we find that one of the Orion females is called Navaar. The two names are actually connected - Berengaria of Navarre was the Queen Consort of Richard Lionheart, whom she married in 1191.
