Bourkelands
Wagga Wagga, New South Wales
LGA: City of Wagga Wagga

Bourkelands is a rapidly developing southern suburb of Wagga Wagga in southern New South Wales, Australia. The suburb consists predominantly of "display homes" and as a large amount of RAAF (Royal Australian Air Force) and Australian Army housing. The suburb borders Red Hill Road and is within walking distance of the Tolland shopping centre and Jubilee Park/Oval.

A large Mormon church was constructed in the suburb in 2005.
