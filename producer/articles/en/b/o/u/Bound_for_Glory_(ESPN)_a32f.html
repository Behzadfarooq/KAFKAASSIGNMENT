Bound for Glory (ESPN)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Bound for Glory was a television show on ESPN, from October 2005 to December 2005. This show featured former Chicago Bear Dick Butkus coaching the Robinson, Pennsylvania-based Montour High Spartans. The Spartans were a perennial Pennsylvania state champion contender in the 1940s but have had consistent losing records since. ESPN and Dick Butkus came in with the intention of turning around their post-millennium losing ways.
