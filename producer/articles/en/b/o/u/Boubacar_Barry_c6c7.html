Boubacar "Copa" Barry, known as Boubacar Barry, or just Barry (born December 30 1979) is a Côte d'Ivoire football goalkeeper who currently, as of May 2006, plays for K.S.K. Beveren.

He has 7 caps for the national team, and was called up to the 2006 World Cup.
