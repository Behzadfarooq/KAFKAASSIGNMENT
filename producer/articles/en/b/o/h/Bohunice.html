Bohunice is a village and municipality in Ilava District in the Trenčín Region of north-western Slovakia.

[edit] History

In historical records the village was first mentioned in 1229.

[edit] Geography

The municipality lies at an altitude of 276 metres and covers an area of 7.036 km². It has a population of about 752 people.
