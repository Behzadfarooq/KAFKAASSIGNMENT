Bohushi

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Bohushi (Ukrainian: Богуші; Yiddish: Bohush) is a village near Berezne, Tynne, south of Sarny and Klesiv, in the Bereznivs'kyy rayon (Березнівський район), Rivnens'ka oblast', Volhynia, Ukraine.
