"Bohemian Like You" is a single by The Dandy Warhols, released in 2000 (see 2000 in music). It is featured on their third studio album (also released in 2000), Thirteen Tales From Urban Bohemia. It was also featured on a Vodafone advertisement. In 2006, a mash-up between "Bohemian Like You" and "Horny" was released as "Horny as a Dandy".

The song's video is controversial and gets little daytime airplay, since it features full-frontal male nudity and topless female nudity (with the woman's vagina covered by her leg).

The song has also been featured in the 2002 movie Igby Goes Down, Flushed Away, and Man of the Year (2006 film).
