Bohemian (disambiguation)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Look up bohemian, Bohemian in Wiktionary, the free dictionary.

The term Bohemian may refer to any of the following:

  • Bohemian, a resident of the former kingdom of Bohemia, nowadays the western half of the Czech Republic
  • Bohemianism, a cultural movement
    • Bohemian style and boho-chic, styles of fashion associated with the movement
  • FC Bohemians Praha, a famous Prague football (soccer) club, founded in 1905
  • Bohemian F.C., is a Dublin football team founded in 1890
  • Bohemian, a low-end beer made by Molson and distributed in Canada
