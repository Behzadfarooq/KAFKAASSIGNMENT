Bohemia Manor Middle School is located in the historic town of Chesapeake City in Cecil County, Maryland. This school is operated by Cecil County Public Schools and shares its campus with Bohemia Manor High School. It houses grades 6 through 8 and has a student population of 541.

[edit] History

Bohemia Manor Middle School opened in 1996. Before this time, grades 7 through 12 were taught in Bohemia Manor High School. Currently, the middle school is housed in the east wing, the high school in the west wing.

[edit] Principals

  • Steve Asplen, 1996-2001
  • Robert Buckley, 2001-2006
  • Berkely Orr, 2006-Present

[edit] Notable Events

  • In 2001, Bohemia joined the Green School Program that helps educate students about the Chesapeake Bay.

[edit] General Information

  • Principal: Berkely Orr
