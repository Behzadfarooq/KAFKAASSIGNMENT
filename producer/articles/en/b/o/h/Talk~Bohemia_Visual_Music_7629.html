[edit] Factual Dispute

I dispute the accuracy of this srticle, since Portland's KORS-CA is an LPTV, and that article makes no mention of BVM. I also dispute the involvement of Houston's KHLM. Furthermore, BVM's website only mentions KPHE, which no longer carries the service. -- azumanga 18:11, 1 January 2007 (UTC)

I'm searching for a source about KORS-CA. Their latest STA application to the FCC refers to broadcasting on digital channel 16, [1] which is where I got the notion that it was a digital subchannel, just as 123lkik had claimed, however, their FCC change application [2] seems to indicate that it's an analog station on channel 16, which is why I didn't restore the reference. BVM's website does mention KORS-CA and claims to broadcast on digital subchannel 16.2, [3] but I'm trying to find an independent source to verify the claim. dhett 19:29, 1 January 2007 (UTC)
Verified by the FCC - KORS-CA is broadcasting in digital per STA at the maximum 15 kW allowed. See FCC Query (2nd entry). Still seeking independent verification for BVM on KORS-CA. dhett 22:57, 1 January 2007 (UTC)
