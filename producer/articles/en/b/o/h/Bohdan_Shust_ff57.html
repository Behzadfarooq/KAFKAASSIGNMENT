Bohdan Shust (born on March 4, 1986 in Sudova Vyshnya, Ukraine) is a professional Ukrainian football player for Shakhtar Donetsk. He was a part of Ukraine's 2006 World Cup Squad as well as a member of the Ukraine national under-21 football team.
