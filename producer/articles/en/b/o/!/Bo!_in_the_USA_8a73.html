Bo! In The USA is the fourth series of Channel 4 comedy Bo' Selecta in which Avid Merrion (Leigh Francis) and his wife Sacha Merrion (Barunka O'Shaughnessy) own a hotel in Hollywood, California where celebrities stay.[citation needed] Other characters include The Bear, Keith Lemon, The Mexican Wolf Boys, Ralph, Carlito and more.[citation needed]
