Bolívar Province (Peru)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Bolívar Province is a Peruvian province located in the La Libertad Region. It is one of the tweleve provinces that make up that region.

[edit] Boundaries

  • North Amazonas Region
  • East San Martín Region
  • South Patáz Province.
  • West Sánchez Carrión Province, Cajamarca Region

[edit] Political Division

The province has an area of 1,718.86 square kilometers and is divided into six districts:

  • Bolívar
  • Bambamarca
  • Condormarca
  • Longotea
  • Uchumarca
  • Ucuncha

[edit] Population

The province has an approximate population of 19,000 inhabitants

[edit] Capital

The capital of this province is the city of Bolívar.
