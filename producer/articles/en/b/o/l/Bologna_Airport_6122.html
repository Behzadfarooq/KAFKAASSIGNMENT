Bologna Airport (Italian: Aeroporto di Bologna) or Guglielmo Marconi Airport (IATA: BLQ, ICAO: LIPE) is an airport serving the city of Bologna in Italy. It is approximately 6km (3.75 miles) northwest the town center in the Emilia-Romagna region of Italy, about 200km (125 miles) east of Milan.

The airport is named after Bologna native Guglielmo Marconi, an Italian electrical engineer and Nobel laureate.
