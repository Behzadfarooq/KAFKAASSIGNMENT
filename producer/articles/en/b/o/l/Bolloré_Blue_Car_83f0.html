Bolloré Blue Car in Geneva Auto Show 2005

The Bolloré Blue Car is a small electric car built by the battery manufacturer Bolloré from Quimper, France. The bubble shaped car, originally code named VBE1, was designed with help by engineers from Renault and was built by the manufacturer to showcase a new range of electric battery power cells.
