Bollschweil Priory was a Cluniac nunnery at Bollschweil (formerly Bolesweiler) in the district of Breisgau-Hochschwarzwald, Baden-Württemberg, Germany.

It was founded by Saint Ulrich of Zell in or after 1087 to complement the monastery he had founded at Grüningen (later moved to Zell), but for some reason, probably unsuitability of the site, the nunnery was moved to nearby Sölden in 1115.
