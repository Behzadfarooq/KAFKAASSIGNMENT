Boldizsár Bodor

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Boldizsár Bodor (born April 27, 1982 in Pécs) is a Hungarian football (soccer) player, who plays for Roda JC since the summer of 2004. Prior to that he served K.F.C. Germinal Beerschot in Belgium for four years.
