Boljun
Boljun (Croatia)

Boljun is a village in central Istria, Croatia, in the area of Boljunsko Polje, 20 km northeast of Pazin; elevation 110 m. Boljun is located on the regional road Učka - Labin. The main occupation is farming.

The village is located on the low slopes of Učka mountain.
