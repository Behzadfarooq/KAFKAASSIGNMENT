[edit] Failed Good Article nomination

I feel that the article in its current state does not meet Good Article criteria for the following reasons:

  1. The lead section needs improvement; see WP:LEAD for guidance
  2. The Family and early life setion is very confusing, especially the first paragraph. It just generally needs to be reworded in a manner that focuses more on the subject (Bolli Bollason). For example, it should have started out by mentioning when he was born and to whom, before going into the story about his father's death.

I will say that the article is well-sourced with ample inline citations, which is an excellent start (and too rare on Wikipedia). It just needs some work to make it more readible. --JerryOrr 02:09, 15 February 2007 (UTC)
