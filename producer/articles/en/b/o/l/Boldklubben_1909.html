Boldklubben 1909 is a Danish football club currently playing in the Danish 2nd Division West. They play at Odense Atletik Stadion in Odense on Funen, which has a capacity of 8,000.

From the 2006-07 season, they will merge with B 1913 and Dalum IF, and the new team will play under the name of FC FYN.

[edit] Achievements

  • Danish championship titles: 1959, 1964
  • Danish Cup: 1962, 1971
