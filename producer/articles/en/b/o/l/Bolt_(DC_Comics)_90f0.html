Bolt is a DC Comics small time supervillain with the powers of teleportation and energy blasts. He first appears in Blue Devil #6 in battle with Blue Devil and The Trickster.

Bolt appears in Suicide Squad Vol. 1 #64-66 as part of a more villainous version of the Squad propping up the dictatorship in the Bermuda Triangle island of Diabloverde. Amanda Waller and her Squad take out him and his colleagues while attempting to remove the dictator.

He joined the third incarnation of the Suicide Squad and apparently died on his first mission alongside Killer Frost, Putty, Eliza and Larvanaut. He fell through a shaft, broke his leg and was attacked by killer ants. He was seen dead in the hands of Killer Frost. He later turned up alive again in the pages of Identity Crisis #1 and was badly injured by two street kids, suffering a punctured lung and two punctured kidneys. He has since joined The Society.
