Boler Mountain, is the home of the London Ski Club in London, Ontario. The mountain is located in the western suburbs of London and is used for skiing, snowboarding and snow tubing in the winter and for mountain biking in the summer. The facility is a small one, with a vertical drop of 115 feet and 50 skiable acres. There are five lifts and 9 trails. The club is undergoing a major expansion which will double the skiable area when it's finished. Although the expansion was originally to be completed by 2000, as of 2007 it has not yet been completed.
