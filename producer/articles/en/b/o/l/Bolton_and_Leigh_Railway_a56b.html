Bolton and Leigh Railway

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

You have new messages (last change).

The Bolton and Leigh railway was built in 1828 two years before the Manchester and Liverpool Railway. It was Britain's first public passenger railway [citations needed].

The railway operated from Bolton Bolton Great Moor Street railway station to Kenyon Junction near Warrington.

Leigh is the biggest town in England without a railway station [citations needed]
