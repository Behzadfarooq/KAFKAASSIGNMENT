"Bole Chudiyan" is a song from the Bollywood film Kabhi Khushi Kabhie Gham, directed by Karan Johar.

[edit] About the song

The music was composed by the Jatin Lalit, lyrics were given by Sameer and sung by Alka Yagnik, Kavita Krishnamurthy, Udit Narayan, Sonu Nigam and Amit Kumar.

[edit] Music Video

The song comes when Rahul(Khan), Anjali(Kajol), Rohan(Roshan) and Pooja(Kapoor) are celebrating the festival of Karwa Chauth. The song is a true indian wedding type song with colorful decorations and dresses which relfects festivals of India.

[edit] Trivia

  • The song is compiled on sets which is quite expensive.
  • The part where all the cast is in one shot, Karan Johar saw why the film is going to be a success and wished he could also be there.
