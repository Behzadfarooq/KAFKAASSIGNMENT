There have been elections for both houses of the National Congress of Bolivia - the Senate and the Chamber of Deputies. If no candidate in the Presidential election has an absolute majority, the newly-elected Congress apparently elects from the two leading candidates. Whilst Qurioga has said he won't accept the Presidency, I'm not sure what happens if Senators and Deputies ignore this and vote him in anyway (and that doesn't seem inconcievable to me).
