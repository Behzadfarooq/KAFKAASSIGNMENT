The Bolivarian Youth is a revolutionary anti-capitalist youth organization. The organization was founded on Mayday (May 1st), 2005 in Miami and was born from the local Bolivarian Circle. Inspired by Venezuela's peaceful and democratic Bolivarian Revolution, the "Youth" was organized to educate and mobilize revolutionary minded young people in South Florida for unity, solidarity, and political action. The Bolivarian Youth has chapters at Florida International University and Florida Atlantic University. The official website of the Bolivarian Youth is www.bolivarianyouth.com.
