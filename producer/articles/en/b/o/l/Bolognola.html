Location of Bolognola in Italy

Bolognola is a comune (municipality) in the Province of Macerata in the Italian region Marche, located about 70 km southwest of Ancona and about 40 km southwest of Macerata. As of 31 December 2004, it had a population of 152 and an area of 25.8 km².[1]

Bolognola borders the following municipalities: Acquacanina, Montefortino, Sarnano, Ussita.
