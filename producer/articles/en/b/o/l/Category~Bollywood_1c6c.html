Category:Bollywood

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Indian Hindi language film industry is nicknamed Bollywood. The 'B' in Bollywood is for the city of Bombay (Mumbai) where the industry is based, the rest of the name being an obvious pun on Hollywood. It produces more than 200 movies a year.
