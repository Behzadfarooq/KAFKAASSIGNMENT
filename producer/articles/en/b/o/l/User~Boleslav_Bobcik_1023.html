My name with diacritic is Boleslav Bobčík. Common nickname is Bolek.

I am a programmer in Prague branch of T-Systems. Although most of my time I work using Perl programming language, I feel equally confident in several other programming languages, handful of RDBMS (such as Oracle, PostgreSQL) and other technologies.

I graduated from Masaryk university, Faculty of Computer Science in 2004. Since then, I have worked at the golf course in my hometown for several months (as a break from office stereotype). Starting January 2005, I moved to Prague.

Contrary to Czech stereotype, I have moved several times during my life. I lived in Uherský Brod since birth; after several years our family moved to Ústí nad Labem. In 1991, we moved to our present hometown, Nová Bystřice.

I am interested in almost all areas related to computers:

  • Natural language processing
  • Information systems analysis, design and evaluation
  • Cryptography
  • Computational geometry
  • Analysis of algorithms
  • DTP and typography
  • and more

My other hobbies include:

  • Japanese (language, culture, anime)
  • Golf
  • Wikipedia
  • Photography
