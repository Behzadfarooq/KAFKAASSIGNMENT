Bologna Motor Show

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Bologna Motor Show is an auto show held in Bologna, Italy. It is recognized as an international show by the Organisation Internationale des Constructeurs d'Automobiles.

[edit] 2006

The 2006 show runs from Dec 5 through Dec 17.

  • Chevrolet Lacetti diesel
  • DR 3
  • DR 5
  • Fiat Panda Panda
  • Ford C-MAX facelift

  • Lamborghini Murcielago LP640 Roadster (Europe introduction)
  • Maserati MC12 Corsa
  • Opel Astra MY2007

  • Škoda Roomster Scout
