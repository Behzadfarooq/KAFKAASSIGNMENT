[edit] Cleanup

I removed some entries that are dictionary entries, not needed in a disambig page:

  • To go to flower, usually in the case of a vegetable plant like broccoli, which may cause the flavour to change.
  • To run suddenly, as in bolting for the exit.

I removed entries that had no article to link to, or which were self-promoting and/or non-notable:

  • The internet website Bolt.com, which is a social netwroking site for young adults.
    • A DC Comics character affiliated with the Suicide Squad; see Bolt (DC Comics).
    • A Marvel Comics character who was partnered with Maverick and later assumed the Maverick identity himself.

It may not always be a good idea but there is a Wiki convention which frowns on verbs in titles (Wikipedia:Naming conventions#Use gerund of verbs). So along the lines of your thinking it would be Bolting (botany). I agree that this might be the best answer but it is also normal though perhaps not rigidly fixed, that parenthesis be used only where there is already an article called for example Bolting. Here therefore, Bolting would be the convetional form of the title.
