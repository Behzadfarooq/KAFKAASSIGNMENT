Bolton Council election 2006

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

You have new messages (last change).

Elections to Bolton Council were held on 4 May 2006. One third of the council was up for election and the council stayed under no overall control. Overall turnout was 34.3%.

After the election, the composition of the council was

  • Labour 22
  • Conservative 21
  • Liberal Democrat 17
