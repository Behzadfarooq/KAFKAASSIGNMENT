Bollebygd Municipality is a Municipality in Västra Götaland County, in western Sweden. Its seat is in Bollebygd (pop. 3,200).

The municipality Bollebygd was established first in 1952, but with the municipal reform of 1971, it became part of its eastern neighbour Borås Municipality, until it in 1995 once again formed a separate municipality.
