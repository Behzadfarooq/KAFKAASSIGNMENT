Bolero Records

Bolero Records is an independent record label established in March of 2002 by world renowned Nuevo Flamenco guitarist, Armik, based in Tarzana, California. In addition to Armik, their artist roster includes guitarists Young & Rollins, Romero, and Jason McGuire.
