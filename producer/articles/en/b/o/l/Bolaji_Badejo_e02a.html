Bolaji Badejo

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Bolaji Badejo (born in Nigeria) is a former design student and one-time actor known for his portrayal as "The Alien" in the acclaimed science fiction movie Alien. He is Nigerian and a member of the Yoruba tribe. Badejo stands at 7'2" tall.
