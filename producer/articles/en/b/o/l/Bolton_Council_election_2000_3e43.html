Bolton Council election 2000

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Elections to Bolton Council were held on 4th May, 2000. One third of the council was up for election and the Labour party kept overall control of the council.

After the election, the composition of the council was

  • Labour 34
  • Conservative 15
  • Liberal Democrat 11
