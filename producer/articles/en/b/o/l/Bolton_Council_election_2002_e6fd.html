Bolton Council election 2002

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Elections to Bolton Council were held on 2 May 2002. One third of the council was up for election and the Labour party kept overall control of the council.

After the election, the composition of the council was

  • Labour 31
  • Conservative 16
  • Liberal Democrat 13
