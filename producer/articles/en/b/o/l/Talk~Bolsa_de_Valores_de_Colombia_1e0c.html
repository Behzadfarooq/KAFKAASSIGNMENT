[edit] list of companies on the BVC

--Nickcarroll 22:17, 27 March 2007 (UTC) I think this is the appropriate page for including the list of public colombian companies, as a subsection. As I understand it all corporations are listed on the BVC??

There are two relevant pages, "List of Colombian Companies" and "Companies of Colombia". They are both incomplete lists.

Perhaps the best approach is to ditch the two incomplete pages mentioned above, and have a page "colombian companies" containing the following:

Colombian law allows the following types of Companies: -Corporation -Limited Liability Company -Branch of a foriegn corporation -General Partnership -Limited Partnership -Limited Partnership with shares etc....

Under each, a description, and under Corporation, a link to the list of companies on the BVC.
