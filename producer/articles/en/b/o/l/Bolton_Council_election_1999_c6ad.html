Bolton Council election 1999

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

You have new messages (last change).

Elections to Bolton Council were held on 6th May, 1999. One third of the council was up for election and the Labour party kept overall control of the council.

After the election, the composition of the council was

  • Labour 41
  • Conservative 10
  • Liberal Democrat 9
