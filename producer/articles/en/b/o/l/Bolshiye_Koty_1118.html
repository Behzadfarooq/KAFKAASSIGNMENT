Bolshiye Koty (Russian: Больши́е Коты́; lit. Big Cats) is settlement in Irkutsk Oblast, Russia situated on the shores of Lake Baikal. Bolshiye Koty river runs near the settlement, falling into Baikal. It is accessible during the summer by ferry from Irkutsk or Listvyanka and in the winter via roads across the lake ice.
