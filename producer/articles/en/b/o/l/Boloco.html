Boloco (formerly The Wrap) is a chain of restaurants that features inspired burritos, wraps, salads, and smoothies in its menu. It has 11 restaurants in the greater Boston area, spread out through the financial district, the campuses of many city-based colleges, and the hospital district. In addition, it has a location open in Hanover, New Hampshire. There are burritos representing Mexican, Japanese, Thai, and Mediterranean cuisines.


[edit] History

The Wrap was incorporated in 1996 as the combined brainchild of co-founders Adam Liebman, Gregg Harris, John Pepper, and Jason Hutchinson as a wrap and smoothie restaurant. In 1997, the founders opened the first restaurant (called "Under Wraps" for the first few months) in Boston's Back Bay. In 1998 they acquired a competing 2-restaurant business with locations in Harvard Square and Cleveland Circle (Boston College area). All three locations were re-branded under the new name, The Wrap and Smoothie Bar (Under Wraps had to be abandoned due to legal reasons). Over the following years, there were a few setbacks and unsuccessful business decisions (a challenging merger with another 4-unit Juice Company in 2000, for example), but the team overcame those and continued growing (Water Street '99, Newbury Street '00, Northeastern University '00, Children's Hospital '03, Pearl Street '03, Dartmouth College '04, Federal Street '04, Tufts Univ '06, Davis Square '07). Today, the team operates 12 locations in New England, with two additional expected to open in 2007.
