Bolulla

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Bolulla is a Valencian town and municipality located in the comarca of Marina Baixa, in the province of Alicante, Spain. Bolulla has an area of 13.6 km² and, according to the 2002 census, a total population of 355 inhabitants. The economy of Bolulla is exclusively based on agriculture. The most important monument in the town is the Catholic church of Sant Josep (Saint Joseph), built in the 18th century.
