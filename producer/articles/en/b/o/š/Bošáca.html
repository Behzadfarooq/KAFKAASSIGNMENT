Bošáca is a village and municipality in Nové Mesto nad Váhom District in the Trenčín Region of western Slovakia.

[edit] History

In historical records the village was first mentioned in 1380.

[edit] Geography

The municipality lies at an altitude of 235 metres and covers an area of 19.598km². It has a population of about 1330 people.
