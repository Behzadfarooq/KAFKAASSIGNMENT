Bogdan Zdrojewski

Bogdan Zdrojewski (born May 18, 1957) is a Polish politician who was the first president of Wrocław after the fall of communism in Poland, and held the seat in the years 1990 to 2001. Afterwards, he has been a senator and member of the Polish Sejm.
