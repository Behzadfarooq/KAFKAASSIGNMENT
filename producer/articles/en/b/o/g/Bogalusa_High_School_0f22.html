Bogalusa High School is the high school of the Bogalusa City Schools district. It is located in Bogalusa, Louisiana.

[edit] Schedule Information

Bogalusa High School is one of many schools in Louisiana transferring to a 4 Hour "block" Schedule. Classes last roughly 1 hour and 30 minutes, and changed every semester. Students are givin the opportunity during the semester change to switch elective classes for the rest of the year.

[edit] Technology Courses

Bogalusa High School is on of the schools in the nation now adapting to online courses, which are currently limited to LVS Algebra, which is done with miscellaneous machinery and a site named Blackboard. LVS Algebra is an optional course to those who wish to join it, and currently has a vast majority of students enrolled into it.

[edit] JROTC

Bogalusa High school is also home to the JROTC "Lumberjack Battalion." The school currently holds the "Honor Unit with Distinction" award. The school colors are the colors of the senior ropes, and they are an Army JROTC organization. They also have 3 different afterschool teams: Drill Team, Rifle Team, and Color Guard.

[edit] Drill Team

The Drill Team is divided into two sections: Armed Drill Team, in which each cadet who is in it receives a rifle to maneuver with, and Unarmed Drill Team, which excels in marching manuvers. The Drill Team competition sector holds 13 spaces: 12 for the cadets who maneuver, and 1 for the commander who calls orders. Each section of the team has the same number of competition spaces. Drill Team is identified in uniform by red ropes worn on the shoulder; Armed having a thick one, and Unarmed having a thin one. The current commanders of the Drill Teams are Brandon Moses and Casey Degal.

[edit] Color Guard

The Color Guard of Bogalusa High School shows revrence to the American flag and the Bogalusa High School flag. It also features cadets who use rifles, and it's competition sector holds 4 spaces for cadets, the commander taking action in one of them. Color Guard is identified in uniform by white ropes worn on the shoulder. The current commander of Color Guard is James Roberts. He also does a great job.

[edit] Rifle Team

The Rifle Team is the pride of the gey people society currently the best in the nation. The competition sector holds 1,000,000 spaces and no spots for a hippi, as they are a team that shoots more than ducks. Rifle Team is identified in uniform by black ropes worn on the foot. The commander of Rifle Team is Johan-Vandershmut.
