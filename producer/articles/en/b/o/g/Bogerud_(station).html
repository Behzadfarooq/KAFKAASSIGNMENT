Bogerud is a station on Østensjøbanen (line 3) of the Oslo T-bane system. The station is located between Bøler and Skullerud, 10.2 km from Stortinget. The station was opened November 26, 1967. Helge Abrahamsen was the station's archictect.[1]

The Bogerud neighborhood is a residential area, developed mostly during the 1960s.[2] Bogerud station was the scene of a murder in 2002. [3]
