Bogdan Stelea

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Bogdan Gheorghe Stelea (born 5 December 1967) is a Romanian football goalkeeper. He was born in Bucharest and started his career with Dinamo Bucureşti. He then spent large parts of his career in Spain.

Stelea made his debut for the national team in 1988, and represented his country at the 1990 World Cup, 1994 World Cup, Euro 1996, 1998 World Cup and Euro 2000. He has been capped 91 times, the last in 2005.
