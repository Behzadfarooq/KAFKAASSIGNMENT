Dr. Bogdan C. Maglich (born ca. 1930) is the leading advocate of a purported non-radioactive aneutronic fusion energy source. Maglich's Migma fusion would use colliding ion beams. Maglich has raised a few eyebrows by actually suing his scientific critics.
