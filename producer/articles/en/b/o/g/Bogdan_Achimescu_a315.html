Bogdan Achimescu

Bogdan Achimescu (born in 1965 in Timişoara, Romania) is a Polish-Romanian visual artist.

His work mainly consists of drawings and installations. It explores themes of genetic inheritance, political dystopia and, more recently, imaginary architecture and artifacts. Through his images, Achimescu "elaborates on human identity through its absence" [1] and mocks established values from a humorous perspective rooted in his nomadic biography. [2] Achimescu represented Romania at the 2001 Venice Biennial as a participant in the Context Network project. He collaborated with the Berlin-based Urban art group on bucharest-buchawork, an artistic project researching Romania's transition on the threshold of EU integration.
