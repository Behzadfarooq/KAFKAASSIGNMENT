Bogomil Dyakov (Bulgarian: Богомил Дяков) (born 12 April 1984) is a Bulgarian footballer. He currently plays as a defender for PFC Spartak Varna.Bogomil plays in the right of defense. His first club was PFC Levski Sofia.In June 2001 Levski loaned Dyakov for two years to Pirin and he played in this team between 2001 and 2003 in Bulgaria's second division - B PFG.Between 2003 and 2005 Levski again loaned Dyakov, but to another club - Rodopa.In Smolyan, Bogomil played very well and was called up to the Bulgaria U21 in 2004.He signed for Spartak in early 2007 for fee of 40 000 €.
