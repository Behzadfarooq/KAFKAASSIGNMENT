Bogusław Wyparło (born on November 29, 1974) is a Polish footballer (goalkeeper) playing currently for ŁKS Łódź. He was in the ŁKS squad in the 1997/1998 season when the club won the championship of Poland. Wyparło played three times for Poland national football team. He is a trainee of Stal Mielec.He debuted in I liga in the age of 16 .
