Head of the Warszawa Centrum and national tourism from 1994-1998, Bogdan Tyszkiewicz was arrested in 2000 for openly displaying an illegal weapon.

Frequently testifying against members of the Pruszków mafia for the Central Investigative Bureau, he was arrested on charges of corruption stating that he aided other gangsters evade the law. He was killed on August 2nd 2005, allegedly by his Belarussian driver Andrei.

[edit] Trivia

  • A referee in his spare time, Tyszkiewicz served as the leader of the Polish Hockey Federation
