Bogatyńskie

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Bogatyńskie (German: Tüngen) is a village in Poland, in the Warmian-Masurian Voivodship, in Lidzbark County, in Orneta Commune, about 6 km south-west of Orneta.

The family of Nicolaus von Tüngen, bishop of Warmia from 1467 until 1489, came from there.
