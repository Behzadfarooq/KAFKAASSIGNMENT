Bogdan Lobonţ (born January 18, 1978, in Hunedoara, Romania) is a goalkeeper for FC Dinamo Bucuresti and the Romanian national team.

Lobont first played for FC Corvinul Hunedoara, and he remained with the team until 1997, when he moved to Rapid Bucureşti. He enjoyed success with Rapid Bucharest, and was picked up by Ajax in 2000. However, injury and inexperience combined to keep him out of a starting role, and he was loaned to Dinamo Bucureşti for the next season. When he returned to Ajax, though, he was the team's primary goalkeeper. Although he has struggled with injuries from time to time since, he has played well when available in both the Eredivisie and the Champions League.

In January 2006, Lobont was sold from Ajax to Italian Serie A club Fiorentina, which was looking for a goalkeeper after the injury of Sebastien Frey.

Lobont is also an important part of the Romanian national team, and he has earned 38 caps, his first in 1998. He was part of his country's Euro 2000 squad.

In 2007 Bogdan Lobont return to "his love" F.C Dinamo Bucharest to win championship,play in the Uefa Champions League and to be again in the 11 start in National Team Of Romania .
