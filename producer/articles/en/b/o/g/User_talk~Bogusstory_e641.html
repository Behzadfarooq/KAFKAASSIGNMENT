[edit] Removal of links to the article

A few users are deleting and redirecting my links to 9/11 open questions. Please stop. Deleting links to an article while it is being voted on for deletion affects the election outcome, especially after so many votes came in from those links prior to major changes. Although there is no explcit rule against this, it could easily be argued as sabotage and vandalism of the Wiki Election by a voter. Wiki deletion policy allows for those links to be redirected after the page has been deleted.

[edit] All links removed now

All links to 9/11 open questions that existed at the beginning of the voting, have been deleted by the voters and RickK, in a tag-team fashion. Based on the 3 revert rule limit that I have, the tag-team has won the edit wars and votes are now trickling on the VfD page as compared to the initial volume. The edit wars for links to my page mostly began after I improved the page significantly. After I lost the edit wars, I started emailing editors of related pages to see if they might want to take a look at my page to either vote on it or harvest the links to strengthen their pages. Bogusstory 21:35, 29 Mar 2005 (UTC)
