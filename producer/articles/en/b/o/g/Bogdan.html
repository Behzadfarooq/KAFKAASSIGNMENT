Bogdan

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Bogdan, a Slavic name meaning "given by God" and largely corresponding to Greek Theodore, Hebrew Nathanael and Jonathan, Latin Deodatus, may refer to:

Rulers of Moldavia:

  • Bogdan I of Moldavia (1359 - 1365)
    • Bogdania was an early name for the principality of Moldavia (named after Bogdan I).
  • Bogdan II of Moldavia (1449 - 1451)
  • Bogdan III cel Orb (1504 - 1517)
  • Bogdan IV of Moldavia (1568 - 1572)

Other people:

  • Bogdan Filov, Bulgarian politician
  • Bogdan Kacmajor
  • Bohdan Khmelnytskyi, Cossack hetman, great military and political leader of Ukraine and Poland in 17th century
  • Bogdan Maglich
  • Bogdan Petriceicu-Hasdeu, Romanian philologist
  • Bogdan Raczynski, Polish musician
