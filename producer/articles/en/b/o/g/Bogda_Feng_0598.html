Bogda Feng (sometimes referred to as Mount Bogda) (博格达峰) is the highest mountain in the eastern Tien Shan mountain range, China, at 5,445 m (17,864 ft). Its sides slope at angles of between 70° and 80°, which helps to explain why it was not successfully climbed until 1981.
