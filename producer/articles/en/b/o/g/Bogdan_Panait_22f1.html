Bogdan Panait, (born April 12, 1983 in Ploieşti), is a Romanian football player, currently playing for FC Vaslui as a fullback.

Panait made his professional debut in 2001 at Petrolul Ploieşti and also played between 2003 and 2006 for FC Vaslui.

Despite being a fullback, Panait has a great scoring appetite. Last season he scored four goals in 26 league appearances.

He was part of Steaua's UEFA Champions League 2006-07 squad during 2006.

In winter 2006, a trade between Steaua Bucharest and FC Vaslui took place: Marius Croitoru to Steaua in exchange for Panait at FC Vaslui and a sum of money.
