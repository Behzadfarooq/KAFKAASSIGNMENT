Boxes (Icehouse album)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Boxes
Studio album by Icehouse
Released 1985
Recorded Trash Studios, Sydney, Australia
Genre Post Punk/New Wave
Label Festival Records/Chrysalis Records
Producer(s) Iva Davies
Bob Kretschmer

Boxes is an album by Australian band, Icehouse. The work was originally conceived for the Sydney Dance Company performance of Boxes. It was conceived by its composers, in collaboration with choreographer Graeme Murphy as a dance work. The first live performance of Boxes was given by the Sydney Dance Company together with Iva Davies, Bob Kretschmer, Masaki Tanazawa in the Opera Theatre of the Sydney Opera House on November 7th 1985.
