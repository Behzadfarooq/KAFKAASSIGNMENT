Boxing at the 2003 Pan American Games

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

You have new messages (last change).

The Boxing Tournament at the 2003 Pan American Games was held in Santo Domingo, Dominican Republic from August 8 to August 15. It served as a qualification tournament for the 2004 Summer Olympics in Athens, Greece. The number one and two earned a ticket for the Olympic tournament.
