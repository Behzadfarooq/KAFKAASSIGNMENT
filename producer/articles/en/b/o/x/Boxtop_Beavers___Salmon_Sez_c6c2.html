Boxtop Beavers / Salmon Sez

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Boxtop Beavers / Salmon Sez was an episode of the Nickelodeon animated series The Angry Beavers.

The Angry Beavers are collecting cereal boxes for toys. Dag goes right for the toys inside the boxes, which are tiny and cheap. Norb uses boxtops to get bigger, better toys. Dag gets in on the boxtops and tries to save enough for a streetsweeper.

  • Written By: John Derevlany
  • Storyboard: Joel Seibel, Mitchell Schauer, Michael R. Gerard
  • Directed By: Robert Hughes

[edit] Salmon Sez

Various salmon keep swimming into the beavers' dam causing destruction. Dag tries to fight them off. The salmon want to spawn, but Dag thinks it stands for "Smashing Property and Whomping Norb."

  • Written By: Victor Wilson
  • Storyboard: Chris Dent, Mario D'Anna
  • Directed By: Justin Michael

[edit] Voice Cast

  • Stuart Pankin (Destructo)
  • Philece Sampler (Girl, Mother)
  • Gregg Berger (TV Reporter, Bear)

[edit] Trivia

  • In Boxtop Beavers Daggett is swimming around in the cereal with only his fins his showing. This is a reference to the movie Jaws.
