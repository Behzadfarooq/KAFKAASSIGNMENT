Boxing Hefner is a compilation of singles, b-sides, and live versions of songs by the British indie rock band Hefner. Released on Too Pure in 2000, it collected various early songs, as well as BBC sessions and a cover of the Jonathan Richman song "To Hide a Little Thought."
