Boxing at the 2000 Summer Olympics

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

You have new messages (last change).

The boxing competition at the 2000 Summer Olympics in Sydney was held over a period of sixteen days at the Sydney Convention and Exhibition Centre in Darling Harbour. Three hundred and twelve men representing 74 nations competed in twelve weight categories, with 28 athletes in each category except for the heavyweight and super heavyweight divisions, where there were sixteen each.
