The Bantamweight class in the boxing at the 2004 Summer Olympics competition was the third-lightest class. Bantamweights were limited to those boxers weighing more than 54 kilograms.

[edit] Participants

  • David Munyasia (Kenya) was thrown out of the 2004 Athens Games on just four days before the draw after becoming the first competitor to test positive in Athens for a banned drug. He was tested positive for cathine.
  • Petit Ngnitedem (Gabon) replaced at the last moment winner of second African qualification Mohamed Abdelsayed (Egypt).
