Boxing at the 1995 Pan American Games

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Boxing Tournament at the 1995 Pan American Games was held in Mar del Plata, Argentina from March 11 to March 27. It served as a qualification tournament for the 1996 Summer Olympics in Atlanta, Georgia. The number one and two earned a ticket for the Olympic tournament.
