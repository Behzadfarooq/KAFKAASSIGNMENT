Boxing at the 1964 Summer Olympics - Welterweight

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

You have new messages (last change).

The welterweight class in the boxing at the 1964 Summer Olympics competition was the fifth-heaviest class. Welterweights were limited to those boxers weighing less than 67 kilograms.
