The Featherweight class in the boxing at the 2004 Summer Olympics competition was the fourth-lightest class. Featherweights were limited to those boxers weighing more than 57 kilograms.

[edit] Participants

  • Daniel Brizuela (Argentina) qualified for the Athens Games as a lucky semifinal loser in the final tournament in Rio de Janeiro. He took the place of Aaron Garcia (USA), which quota was dropped by the USA Boxing Association due to unsuccessful presentation at USA Olympic trials.
