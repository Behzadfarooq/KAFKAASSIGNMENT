Box of Scorpions
Box set by Scorpions
Released 2004
Genre Hard rock

The Box of Scorpions is a triple-CD compilation album by the German hard rock band Scorpions, released in May 25, 2004. It contains songs from their albums between the years 1972 and 2001 as well as Live songs.
