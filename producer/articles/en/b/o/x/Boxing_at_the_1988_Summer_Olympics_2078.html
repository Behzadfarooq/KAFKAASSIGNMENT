Boxing at the 1988 Summer Olympics

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

You have new messages (last change).

Boxing at the 1988 Summer Olympics took place in the Chamshil Students' Gymnasium in Seoul. The boxing schedule began on September 17 and ended on October 2. Twelve boxing events (all men's individual) were contested, with the participation of 441 athletes and 159 officials from 106 countries.
