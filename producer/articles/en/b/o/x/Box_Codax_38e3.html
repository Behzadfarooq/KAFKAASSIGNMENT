Box Codax

Box Codax is an bunch of troubadours from Glasgow, Scotland. Their musical style is tagged as experimental, DIY, anarchic melodiac, but their music draws from a wide array of influences. Supposedly, they are made up of Nick McCarthy more famous for being the guitarrist of alternative band Franz Ferdinand and Alexander Ragnew.
