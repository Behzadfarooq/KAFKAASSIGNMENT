Boxely (Layout engine)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

You have new messages (last change).

Boxely is an XML/CSS rendering engine, adapted for rendering UI Chrome (like Gecko does with XUL). It is developed by AOL and is used in AIM Triton and AOL Explorer.
