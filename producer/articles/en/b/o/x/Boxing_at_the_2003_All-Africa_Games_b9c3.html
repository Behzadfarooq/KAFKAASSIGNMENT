Boxing at the 2003 All-Africa Games

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Boxing Tournament at the 2003 All-Africa Games was held in Abuja, Nigeria from October 4 to October 13. It served as a qualification tournament for the 2004 Summer Olympics in Athens, Greece. The number one and two earned a ticket for the Olympic tournament.
