Boxing at the 1984 Summer Olympics

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

You have new messages (last change).

Boxing at the 1984 Summer Olympics took place in the Memorial Sports Arena in Los Angeles, California. The boxing schedule began on July 29 and ended on August 11. Twelve boxing events (all men's individual) were contested.
