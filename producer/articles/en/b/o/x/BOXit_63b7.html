BOXit is a simple Puzzle game, created by Frank Hollwitz in 2001.

[edit] Game rules

The gaming field is a grid filled with colored pieces (numbered as dice). The goal of the game is to clear the field to the greatest degree possible as quickly as possible. You can move a piece over other pieces in the same row or column, replacing them. One piece replaces another if they share a color or number.

[edit] Releases

BOXit is a free software, released under GNU GPL license. Versions are available for most Java enabled operating systems.
