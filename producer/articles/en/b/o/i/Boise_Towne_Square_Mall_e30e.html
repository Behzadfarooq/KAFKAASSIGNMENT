Boise Towne Square Mall is a mall in Boise, Idaho and is the largest mall in Idaho.

[edit] History

The history of the mall can be traced back to 1983 when Boise Mall at Westpark was proposed.[1] The mall's name changed to Westpark Mall in 1985[1] and Boise Towne Square Mall in 1987. The mall was built in 1987 and opened in 1988. The mall was expanded in 1998 to add Dillard's, 100 new stores, a food court, and more space for The Bon Marchè. ZCMI asked for an approval to build a store in 1999 but due to all ZCMI stores closed on Sundays it was never approved. In 2000, another expansion was made to expand Sears and add more stores. In 2006, the mall was expanded to add 35,000 square feet of retail and restaurant space. Stores and restaurants include Borders Books & Music and The Cheesecake Factory.
