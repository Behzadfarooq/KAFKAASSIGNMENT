Boiţa

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Boiţa (Hungarian: Bojca) is a commune in Sibiu County, Transylvania, Romania, at the foothills of the Cindrel Mountains, 22 km south of the county capital Sibiu, in the Mărginimea Sibiului ethnographical area, on the main road between Sibiu and the southern part of Romania, the National road 7/European route 81, at the entrance of the Olt River defile. In 1910 the village had 1657 inhabitants.
