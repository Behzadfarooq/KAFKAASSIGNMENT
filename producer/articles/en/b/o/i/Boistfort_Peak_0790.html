Boistfort Peak

Boistfort Peak, also called Baw Faw Peak is a 3,061 foot peak in the Willapa Hills of Washington State. Its coordinates are 46°29′19″N, 123°12′56″W. It was once surmounted by a fire lookout [1]. Today it has communication antennae including the N7PRJ 2-meter ham repeater. It also is the site of station BOW in the Pacific Northwest Seismograph Network [2].
