[edit] Geography

Boisar is located at 19.8° N 72.75° E[1]. It has an average elevation of 10 metres (32 feet).


Boisar is located 45 km north of Virar on the Western Railway line of Mumbai Suburban Railway To reach Boisar, one can catch Shuttle/MEMU trains from Virar. Long Distance trains also stop. By Road, It can be reached from National Highway NH-4, 22 km off from Pelhar Phata. There are regular state transport buses from Palghar, Thane, Bhiwandi and Wada.

[edit] Demographics

As of 2001 India censusGRIndia, Boisar had a population of 14,684. Males constitute 56% of the population and females 44%. Boisar has an average literacy rate of 77%, higher than the national average of 59.5%; with male literacy of 83% and female literacy of 69%. 15% of the population is under 6 years of age.


[edit] Industrial Area

Tarapur Industrial Estate, the premium industrial area, is located nearby. The huge industrial area accommodates various specialty chemical, bulk drugs, steel and alloy and textile manufacturing companies.

The working environment is good and basic infrastructure like roads, power, water and availability of manpower is available.

[edit] Nuclear Power Station

India's first nuclear power plant, Tarapur Atomic Power Station (TAPS), is located 12km away from Boisar. There are four units currently operational at this location. Two units, named 1&2 are the oldest commercial nuclear reactors in India. These two units are of Boiling Water Reactor (BWR) type. These were commissioned in 1969 and generate 160MWe each. The other two units (3&4) were put in service in 2005 and generate 540MWe each. The newer units of 3&4 are indigenously built and are largest Pressurized Heavy Water (PHWR) reactors built in India

Bhabha Atomic Research Centre (B.A.R.C) is also located close by. This location consists of the waste immobilisation plant and the fuel reprocessing plant.

[edit] Gas Fired Power Station

Tarapur Industrial Estate has proposed a gas fired power station at Tarapur Industrial Area.

[edit] Residential

Boisar is fine residential location. Very good schools, shopping complexes, multiplexes and hotels are located here.


[edit] Schools

Boisar has some of the best schools as compared to its demographics. One of the oldest Private English schools in Boisar was TVM or Tarapur Vidya Mandir. There was a government run English school for the kids who's parents were employed in BARC or TAPS. Many schools came in the course of the year but the only private school that has grown and expanded is the TVM. Ramchandra Hiraji Save at Tarapur is a good Marathi Medium school.

[edit] Public Transport

Rented cars/taxis, state transport buses (Maharashtra State Transport Corporation) and auto rickshaws are the general mode of transport. Boisar also has many good beaches, the most loved one is "Chinchani". These beaches are not crowded and clean for people to enjoy! There are many chiku farms that one can visit.
