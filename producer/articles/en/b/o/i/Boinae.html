Boinae is one of three subfamilies of the family Boidae, a group of snakes commonly referred to as boas. The subfamily includes seven genera.

[edit] Genera

  • Acrantophis, Madagascar ground boas.
  • Boa, boa constrictors.
  • Candoia, Pacific island boas.
  • Corallus, tree boas.
  • Epicrates, rainbow boas, Antillean boas.
  • Eunectes, anacondas.
  • Sanzinia, Madagascar tree boas.

[edit] Taxonomy

In some sources the genera Acrantophis and Sanzinia are synonymized with the genus Boa.

[edit] See also

  • Erycinae, sand boas, rubber boas, rosy boas, and calabaria.
  • Pythoninae, some sources classify Pythoninae under Boidae, others classify it under Pythonidae.
