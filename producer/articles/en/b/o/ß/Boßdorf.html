Boßdorf (or Bossdorf) is a community in Wittenberg district in Saxony-Anhalt, Germany.

[edit] Geography

[edit] Location

Boßdorf lies about 15 km north of Lutherstadt Wittenberg. The community belongs to the administrative community (Verwaltungsgemeinschaft) of Elbaue-Fläming, whose seat is in the town of Zahna.

[edit] Politics

[edit] Mayor

The honorary mayor Werner Matthes was first elected on 16 October 1994.

[edit] Economy and infrastructure

[edit] Transport

Boßdorf is connected to Federal Highway (Bundesstraße) B 2, which lies 6 km east of the community, and joins Berlin and Wittenberg.
