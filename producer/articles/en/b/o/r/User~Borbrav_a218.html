I am a grade 11 student in the International Baccalaureate programme, from Calgary, Alberta. My interests include mathematics, particularly puzzles, logic, and discrete mathematics, physics, astronomy, and computer programming, particularly in C.

I first came to Wikipedia doing research sometime in spring 2005, and became a Wikipedian on June 21, 2005.

Possible areas in which I can improve Wikipedia:

  • Parks and natural areas in and around Calgary.
  • Mathematical puzzles/terms/general things.
  • Puzzles of various sorts.
  • Computer programming problems (i.e. for Wikibooks)
