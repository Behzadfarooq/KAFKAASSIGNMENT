Borgetto

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Borgetto is a small town in the province of Palermo in Sicily. As of 2007 Borgetto had an estimated population of 6,875. [1]

[edit] History

Borgetto was founded in the 9th century by Arabs. Borgetto literally means "small tower" in Arabic.
