Born Again (Warrant album)

Born Again
Studio album by Warrant
Released 2006
Genre Rock

Born Again is Warrant's eleventh studio album and the first Warrant album to feature singer Jaime St. James.

There is a video for the song "Bourbon County Line".

[edit] Track listing

  1. "Devil's Juice"
  2. "Dirty Jack"
  3. "Bourbon County Line"
  4. "Hell, CA"
  5. "Angels"
  6. "Love Strikes Like Lightning"
  7. "Glimmer"
  8. "Roller Coaster"
  9. "Down In Diamonds"
  10. "Velvet Noose"
  11. "Roxy"
  12. "Good Times"

[edit] Credits

  • Jaime St. James: Lead Vocals

  • Erik Turner: Guitar

  • Steven Sweet: Drums
