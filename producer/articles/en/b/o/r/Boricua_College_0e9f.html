Boricua College

Boricua College is a post-secondary educational institution located in New York City. The college was designed to serve the educational needs of Puerto Ricans and other Hispanics.

The school employs a large bilingual faculty of 130 full-time and 100 part-time faculty and staff that serves a student population of 1,200.
