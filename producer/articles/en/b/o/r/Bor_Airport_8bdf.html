Bor Airport (ICAO: LY89) (Serbian Аеродром Бор, Latinic: Aerodrom Bor) is an airport in Serbia, near the town of Bor.

The airport is said to be partly used as a training camp by the Serbian special police units, called Žandarmerija, but also stay as a civil one to handle tourists during the season.

[edit] Reconstruction and development (2006)

Belgrade Nikola Tesla Airport (in Belgrade) is creating a regional airport network in Serbia. Bor airport will soon be reconstructed and will be able to accept public aircraft. Bor Airport has a good runway, modern control tower and hangars. Belgrade Airport would transport some equipment from Terminal 1 from Belgrade and would reconstruct the runway. This is planned for 2006.
