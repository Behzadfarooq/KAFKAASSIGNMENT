Born again (disambiguation)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Being born again is a concept associated with salvation in Christian theology. The phraseology derives from Jesus' conversation with Nicodemus in St John's Gospel at chapter 3. Many works have used Born Again as or in a title. These include:

In music:

  • Born Again (Randy Newman), a 1979 album by Randy Newman
  • Born Again (Black Sabbath), a 1983 album by Black Sabbath
  • Born Again (The Notorious B.I.G.), a 1999 posthumous album of songs by Notorious B.I.G.
  • Born Again (album), other albums by this name
  • "Born Again", a song by Marilyn Manson on the album Holy Wood (In the Shadow of the Valley of Death).
  • "Born Again", a song by Starsailor on the album Silence is Easy.

In other fields:

  • Born Again, a book by Charles W. Colson, who was implicated in Watergate, about his subsequent experience of becoming a born again Christian (ISBN 0-8007-9377-3).
  • Born Again (film), a 1978 film dramatization of Colson's Watergate experience and religious conversion.
  • Born Again, a stage musical version of Eugene Ionesco's Rhinoceros directed by Peter Hall at the Chichester Festival Theatre in 1990, with music by the then young British composer Jason Carr, and lyrics by Peter Hall
  • Born Again, a software written by Brochner Software that allows legacy computers to run Mac OS 8.1
  • Daredevil: Born Again, a story from the comic book Daredevil by Frank Miller and David Mazzucchelli
  • "Born Again" (The X-Files episode), an episode of The X-Files

See also:

  • Björn Again, an ABBA tribute band
  • Bourne Again, a Unix shell
