[edit] Merzbow Collaborations

Boris have a well-established history of collaboration with Japanoise artist Merzbow. Though this may seem an unusual relationship at first, it becomes clear upon listening that it is the experimental elements in each other’s sound that bond them together as recording partners. Thus far, three of the collaborations with Merzbow have been full-length albums, and the fourth is a 12" EP.

[edit] Compilation appearances

[edit] Exclusive Songs

These songs are available only on the compilations.

  • "Water Porch" on Take Care of Scabbard Fish (1994) Scabbard Fish (CD)

The above song was actually Boris's recorded debut.

  • "Vacuuum" on From Koenji to Eternity (1996) Inoxia (CD)
  • "Me and the Devil Blues" on Up Jumped the Devil: Tribute to Robert Johnson (2000) P-Vine (CD)
  • "Dronevil" on Mangrove2002 (2002) Mangrove (CD)
  • "Froggie Bee Baa" on Merzbow: Frog Remixed & Revisited (2003) Misanthropic Agenda (2xCD)

[edit] Samplers

These are tracks taken from other Boris albums.

  • "The Sinking Belle (Blue Sheep)" on Mind The Gap Vol. 65 (2006) Gonzo Circus (CD) Originally from Altar (with Sunn O)))
