Born Yesterday is a 1993 remake of the 1950 film based on Born Yesterday, a play by Garson Kanin. The 1993 film stars Melanie Griffith, John Goodman and Don Johnson. It was adapted by Douglas McGrath and directed by Luis Mandoki.

Griffith was nominated for the 1993 Golden Raspberry Award for Worst Actress for her performance in the film.
