Boris Pašanski (often spelled "Pashanski" in English language media) is a professional tennis player on the ATP Tour. He was born in Belgrade, Serbia on November 3, 1982.

He is right-handed and turned pro in 2001. He reached his career-high ranking of 55th on February 27, 2006. He is currently 102nd in the ATP rankings.

At the 2006 Wimbledon Championships, Pašanski lost his opening-round match to Andre Agassi in Agassi's last tournament at Wimbledon.
