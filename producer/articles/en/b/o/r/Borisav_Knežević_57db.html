Borisav Knezevic, born in 1971. in Belgrade, coordinator for external relations in the Public Procurement Office[1] of the Government of the Republic of Serbia[2]. Previously,he was working as senior adviser in the Serbia and Montenegro European Integration Office, and was member of the SAA [3] Working group for visas, asylum, migration and integrated border management, and TAIEX [4]coordinator for Serbia and Montenegro as well. Graduated from the Faculty of Economics in Belgrade[5].In 2003. he successfully completed the Master on Local Development for the Balkans-University of Trento[6] and Free University of Bozen-Bolzano[7][8],Italy (recently renamed in Joint European Master in Comparative Local Development [9]). Borisav Knezevic is also the lecturer in the Novi Sad Business School [10]on the following courses : “Contemporary tendencies in public administration“ and "Economics of Public Sector" He is the author of a number of articles and brochures on EU matters, local development, integrated border management [11], matters related to Kosovo and Metohia situation, etc. Borisav Knezevic is also member of the Center for European Integration-Serbia.[12]
