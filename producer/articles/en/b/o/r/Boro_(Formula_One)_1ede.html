Boro (Formula One)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Boro was a Formula One team from Netherlands. The car were actually built by the Ensign team, but was renamed Boro after their main sponsor HB Bewaking ended up as proprietors of the car after a legal dispute with Mo Nunn of Ensign. They entered a total of 8 Grands Prix between 1976 and 1977, but failed to cause a lasting impression.
