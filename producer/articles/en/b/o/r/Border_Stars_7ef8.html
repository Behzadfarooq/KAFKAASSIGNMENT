Border Stars, also known as Windsor Border Stars, are a Canadian soccer team, founded in 2004. The team is a member of the Canadian Soccer League, the highest professional soccer league in Canada, and play in the National Division.

The team currently play their home games at Windsor Stadium in the city of Windsor, Ontario, although occasionally games are played at Notre Dame Preparatory High School just across the border in Pontiac, Michigan . The team's colors are black, white and orange.

The Border Stars entered the CPSL in 2004, and won the Open Canada Cup in both 2004 and 2005. The Border Stars also played one season in AISL (American Indoor Soccer League) and won it in 2005.
