[edit] A few matters

Hi Pankaj,

Interesting to see your contributions at wiki. A few matters:

  • One cannot copy-paste text material from websites into the wiki-articles, as that counts as violation of copyright. Its generally better with a resume of the text, and a linked reference to the full text.
  • You've uploaded several photos. You may upload photos at Wikimedia Commons. Then the pictures will be availible for all wikipedias, not just the English-language one. There is a Tripura page and a Tripura Category.
  • Can you have a look at the flag at the Indigenous Nationalist Party of Twipra. I'm quite sure about the colours, but not 100% on the orders of the colours.

--Soman 13:00, 26 July 2006 (UTC)

Please do not add commercial links or links to your own private websites to Wikipedia, as you did in Banamalipur. Wikipedia is not a vehicle for advertising or a mere collection of external links. You are, however, encouraged to add content instead of links as long as the content abides by our policies and guidelines. If you feel the link should be added to the article, then please discuss it on the article's talk page rather than re-adding it. See the welcome page if you would like to learn more about contributing to our encyclopedia. Thanks. A link to the edit I have reverted can be found here: link. If you believe this edit should not have been reverted, please contact me. --AbsolutDan (talk) 12:32, 1 September 2006 (UTC)
