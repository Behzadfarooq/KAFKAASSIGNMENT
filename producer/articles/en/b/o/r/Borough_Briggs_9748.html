Borough Briggs is a football ground in the Scottish city of Elgin, Morayshire. It is the home ground of Elgin City F.C., who currently play in the Scottish League Third Division. Borough Briggs opened in 1921 and has a capacity of 3927 (478 seated), and thus is some way short of Premier League criteria.
