Borja Valero Iglesias (born 12 January 1985 in Madrid) is a Spanish football (soccer) midfielder for Real Madrid Castilla. He made his senior debut against Écija Balompié, substituting Javi García at the 60min. mark in a Copa del Rey match on 25 October 2006.

He made another appearance in a Champions League match against Dynamo Kyiv, replacing Miguel Ángel Nieto in the 74 min.

He wears No. 7 in Castilla and No. 39 in the first team.

Next season he will play for RCD Mallorca in La Liga
