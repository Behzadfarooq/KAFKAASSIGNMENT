[edit] Hugh Southey

The article Hugh Southey has been or soon will be deleted from Wikipedia. This happened because the article seems to be about a subject but it does not indicate how or why the subject is notable. If you can indicate why the subject is really notable, you are free to re-create the article, making sure to cite any verifiable sources. Feel free to leave a note on my talk page if you have any questions about this. You might also want to read our criteria for speedy deletion, particularly item 7 under Articles. -- Merope 21:18, 27 October 2006 (UTC)

[edit] Emile Peynaud

In the future, please do not copy and paste articles in order to move them. We need to preserve the edit history of the article, and copying the text and pasting it elsewhere makes it look like you're the one who contributed the article. I've fixed this page move. You can read Help:Moving a page for more information. -- Merope 21:47, 31 October 2006 (UTC)
