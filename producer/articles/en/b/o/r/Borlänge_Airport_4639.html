Dala Airport, or Borlänge Airport, is situated outside Borlänge, Dalarna, Sweden and 20 min from Falun.

[edit] History

The airfield was built during the 2nd World War, the field was modernized in 1961. In mid 1960 the first civil airlines arrived at Dala Airport.1972 was the new terminal building finished and Dala Airport AB took over the airport. The airfield is still owned by the Swedish miliitary.
