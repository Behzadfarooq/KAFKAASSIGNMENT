Born Twice is the title of an album by Randy Stonehill, which was released in 1971.

[edit] Track listing

All Songs Written By Randy Stonehill, except "Hand In The Hand" By McClellan, "I Need You" By Jimmy Owens, And He's Got The Whole World" And "He Is A Friend Of Mine" are Public Domain-Words & Arr. By Randy Stonehill.

[edit] Side one

  1. "I Need You"
  2. "Hand In The Hand"
  3. "He's Got The Whole World"
  4. "Help Me Lord"
  5. "Thank You"

[edit] Side two

  1. "Never Can Repay"
  2. "All Right Now"
  3. "Passing Stranger"
  4. "I Love You"
  5. "He Is A Friend Of Mine"
  6. "Norman's Kitchen"

Some versions of the LP replace "He Is A Friend of Mine" with a track called "Christmas Time."

There is also a double-LP version of the album, which is called Judgement Day at Speedee Mart. That version includes the following track list.

[edit] Black LP Side One

  1. "Forget Your Hexagram" (by Larry Norman)
  2. "Why Don't You Look Into Jesus?" (Norman)
  3. "Lonely By Myself" (Norman)
  4. "God's Not Dead"
  5. "I Am A Servant" (Norman)

[edit] Black LP Side Two

  1. "Never Can Repay"
  2. "All Right Now"
  3. "Passing Stranger"
  4. "I Love You"
  5. "Christmas Time"
  6. "Norman's Kitchen"

[edit] Blue LP Side One

  1. "I Need You"
  2. "Hand In The Hand"
  3. "He's Got The Whole World"
  4. "Help Me Lord"
  5. "Thank You"

[edit] Blue LP Side Two

  1. "Norman's Kitchen"
  2. "Pardon Me" (Norman)
  3. "Sunset Road To Nowhere"
  4. "Hasty Heart"
  5. "He Is A Friend Of Mine"

[edit] Personnel

  • Randy Stonehill: lead vocals, lead guitar, rhythm acoustical guitar, kitchenware.
  • Fred Bova: lead guitar.
  • Glenn Salwitz: bass guitar.
  • "Hilly" Hillman: drums and Felix.
  • Larry Norman: piano, organ, mouth claves, harmonies.

[edit] Production notes

  • Arranged, Produced & Photography by Larry Norman.
  • mixed at Fat Chance Studios.
