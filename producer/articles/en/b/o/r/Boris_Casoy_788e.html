Boris Casoy

Boris Casoy, a Brazilian journalist, was born in 1941 in São Paulo of Jewish Ukrainian immigrant parents. He has spent most of his professional life in TV journalism and is currently one a Brazilian TV anchorman.

Casoy was fired from TV Record in late December 2005 - officially, for disagreements with changes that the station wanted to make on his program. However, he had revealed that there was massive pressure from the Worker's Party, since he and his crew were unrelenting in investigating and exposing corruption in the Lula government. [1]
