[edit] About Me

My interests are in epistemology, ontology, social/political philosophy, history and literature. I am also particularly interested in learning more about life during the communist era in Eastern, Central, and Southern Europe.

The areas that I primarily contribute in are literature and philosophy, with particular focus on Southern Slavic (SFRY) literature and philosophy. I am currently colaborating on a series of articles on the Praxis school of thought.

See also a blog featuring some of my short papers in philosophy and literary criticism.
