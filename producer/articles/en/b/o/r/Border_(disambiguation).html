Border can refer to:

  • A border can consist of a margin around the edge of something, such as a lawn, garden, photograph, or sheet of paper.
  • Borders define geographic boundaries of political entities or legal jurisdictions, such as governments, states or countries.
  • A herbaceous border comprises a narrow strip of plants along the edge of a garden path or a lawn or fence.
  • Something can belong on the border if it lies in a grey area at the edge of a category or condition or near the borderline between two different situations but not clearly in one or the other.
  • The Border country consists of an area of Lowland Scotland on the border between Scotland (see also Scottish Borders) and England.
  • Border, Allan, an Australian cricket player
  • ITV Border, the local ITV franchise for the south of Scotland, Cumbria, Berwick-upon-Tweed and the Isle of Man.
  • Border (1997): A war film based on the Indo-Pakistani War of 1971. Stars Sunny Deol, Jackie Shroff, Sunil Shetty, Akshaye Khanna, Pooja Bhatt, Rani Mukherjee and Tabu.
  • The Border, a 1982 film
