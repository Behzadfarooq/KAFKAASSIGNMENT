Borgholm Castle

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Drawing of Borgholm Castle from 1634
The Ruins in 1921

Borgholm Castle in Borgholm, Sweden, is today only a ruin of the fortress that was first built here in the second half of the 12th century and many times rebuilt in later centuries.[1] It is linked to Halltorps estate, somewhat to the south. The castle was destroyed in a fire October 14, 1806.
