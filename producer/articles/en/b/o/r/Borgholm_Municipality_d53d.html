Borgholm Municipality is a Municipality in Kalmar County, in south-eastern Sweden, located on the island of Öland in the Baltic Sea. Its seat is located in the town of Borgholm with 3500 inhabitants. Notable historic sites in this municipality are Borgholm Castle and Halltorps Estate.

Other towns (urban areas) include Köpingsvik and Björkviken.

[edit] Sister cities

Borgholm has four sister cities (aka twin towns):

  • Rockford, Illinois USA
  • Łeba Poland
  • Zelenogradsk, Kaliningrad
  • Korsnäs , Finland
