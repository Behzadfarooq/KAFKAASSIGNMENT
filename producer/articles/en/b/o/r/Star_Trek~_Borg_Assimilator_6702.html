Star Trek: Borg Assimilator was a video game originally slated for release by Activision in 2001/2002. In this game the player is the Borg. Upon inspection of the game product, Activision placed the game on hold due to its inconsistency with the Star Trek universe and technology. It is believed that this game will never be released and there are no current plans to do so.

It is highly probable that the game, as developed earlier, will never be released due to improvements in game and PC system technology since then and would be inferior to current games similar in nature. There are no current plans to redevelop the game or create a new one with the same theme.
