Boris and Tomsk is a split 7" EP by Japanese bands Boris and Tomsk, released on red vinyl and limited to 500 copies.

[edit] Track listing

  1. Boris - "Mass Mercury" - 6:09
  2. Tomsk - "Dumpsite" - 1:48
  3. Tomsk - "Rape-Active-Hate" - 00:21
  4. Tomsk - "Bloodlink" - 1:04
  5. Tomsk - "Me" - 1:32
