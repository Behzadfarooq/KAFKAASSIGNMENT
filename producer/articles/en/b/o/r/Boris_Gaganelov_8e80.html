Boris Gaganelov

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Boris Gaganelov (Bulgarian: Борис Гаганелов) (born 7 October 1941) is a former Bulgarian football player and later coach.

He represented Bulgaria at the FIFA World Cups in 1966 and 1970.
