BORN is Okuda Miwako's 8th single. It was released on August 30, 2006, by BMG JAPAN INC. This single was originally contained on Okuda Miwako's second album, Kimi wo omou.

  2. love you
  3. 雨と夢のあとに (Ame to yume no ato ni) [After the rain and the dream]
  4. BORN (Instrumental ver.)
  5. love you (Instrumental ver.)
