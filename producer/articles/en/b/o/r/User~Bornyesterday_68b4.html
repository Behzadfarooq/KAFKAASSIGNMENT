Born Yesterday

  1. Adjectival phrase. A self-applied description in response to a statement by another person indicating the user's knowledge of the information which the other individual has just stated; often delivered in the interrogative statement "Do you think I was born yesterday?" with a sarcastic tone of voice if the user considers the prompting statement to be obvious information.
  2. Noun. Internet username. Also often seen as a single word "bornyesterday."
