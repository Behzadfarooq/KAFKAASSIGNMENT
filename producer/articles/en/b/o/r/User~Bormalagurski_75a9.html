My name is Boris Malagurski, I live in Vancouver, BC, Canada. I write articles mostly for Serbian Wikipedia by the name of Бормалагурски.

I was born on August 11th in Subotica, Yugoslavia, most likely the most boring place on Earth. But, I have very good friends there, and I like to visit my hometown every year, because one should never forget his origins.

Now I'm in Vancouver, where I have decided to live for a while. I'm very interested in almost everything (I'm not joking), especially History (but not ancient History, more like 20th century History).

Feel free to contact me about almost anything, by leaving a message on my talk page or by sending me an e-mail.
