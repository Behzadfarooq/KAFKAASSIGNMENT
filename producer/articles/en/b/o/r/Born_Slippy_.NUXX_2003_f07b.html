Born Slippy .NUXX 2003 is a re-released version of Born Slippy .NUXX by Underworld, to promote the album 1992-2002, originally released in 2003. The original 1996 version was a huge hit, and new remixes were commissioned for this release, along with a new video.

One of the mix on the track is extensively used by TV Asahi for their station opening sequence.
