[edit] Famous BTs section

I propose we delete entries in the Famous BT section having to do with advertisements. These dogs hardly qualify as "famous." Also, the entry about several border terriers in a Tarzan film could also be removed. Again, you wouldn't call them "famous." The section could be retitled "Exhaustive list of all Border Terrier appearances in all forms of media," but that does not seem like something that belongs in an encyclopedia. Thoughts? --Faith healer 14:09, 21 August 2006 (UTC)

[edit] Toto from Return to Oz

Someone removed Toto (of Return to Oz) from the list of famous border terriers (and it has since been re-added). Perhaps the one who removed it thought it referred to Toto from the original Wizard of Oz, who was a Cairn terrier. Although I am not the one who added this item, it does seem from Google searches (and Toto's wiki entry that the dog in Return to Oz was in fact a border terrier, so I thought I would leave this note to for future reference. --Faith Healer 21:22, 16 August 2006 (UTC)

Yes it was actually a Border Terrier. HuskyMoon 11:41, 17 January 2007 (UTC)

[edit] Request for a picture

If anyone has one, I'd love to see a picture of a Border Terrier that isn't kept stripped. I think that would help people who see them on the street as pets to be able to better identify them as 'Border Terrier,' as I know from experience that a stripped terrier often looks very different from a clipped dog. Am I out of line here? I just think that that would be a good addition to the article. —The preceding unsigned comment was added by Mythicaldog (talk • contribs) .

Sounds good to me. If you see one, take a photo and upload it to wikipedia! :-) Elf | Talk 01:02, 11 March 2006 (UTC)

Nice job, done. Good one. Cute. HuskyMoon 23:11, 16 January 2007 (UTC)
