Borgholzhausen is a town in the district of Gütersloh in the state of North Rhine-Westphalia, Germany. It is located in the Teutoburg Forest, approx. 20 km north-west of Bielefeld.

[edit] Geography

Borgholzhausen lies in the middle of a clearing in the Teutoburger Wald on the northern edge of the Westfälischen Bucht. The city's centre lies about one kilometer east of Johannisberg. Borholzhausen is sister cities with New Haven, Missouri.
