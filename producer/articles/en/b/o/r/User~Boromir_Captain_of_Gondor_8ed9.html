[edit] Infantry

Skirmisher - a light rifleman good against musketeers but week against cavalry

Musketeer - a good, all around unit with no particular weaknesses

Pikeman - an archaic infantry good against cavalry but weak against ranged units and artillery

Halberdier - a heavily armoured infantry good against cavalry but weak against ranged units and artillery

Highlander - a mercenary musketeer

Doppelsoldner - an elite, two handed swordsman. unique unit to the German civilization

Longbowman - a long range, anti-infantry archer. a unique unite to the British civilization

Corsair - a mercenary from the Barbary Coast armed with a sword

Janissary - the Ottoman version of the Musketeer, except stronger and tougher but slower


[edit] Cavalry

Hussar - a standard, light cavalry unit. good against artillery and light infantry

Dragoon - an anti-cavalry cavalry unit

Uhlan - a hard hitting unit armed with cold steel. weak hitpoints. German unique unit

Cossack - a weak, cheap cavalry unit available only to the Russians

Cavalry Archer - an anti-cavalry cavalry availible to the Russians and Ottomans


[edit] Artillery

Culverin - light cannon. good against infantry


Grenadier - artillery with hard hitpoints and good against infantry


Mortar - artillery good against buildings
