Boris Valabik (born February 14, 1986 in Nitra, Slovakia) is an ice hockey player for the Chicago Wolves of the AHL. He was drafted 10th overall as a defenseman in the 2004 NHL Entry Draft by the Atlanta Thrashers.

[edit] Playing career

  • Member of 2003 Slovak Under-18 National Team
  • Member of 2004 Slovak Under-18 National Team
  • Played in 2004 CHL Top Prospects Game
