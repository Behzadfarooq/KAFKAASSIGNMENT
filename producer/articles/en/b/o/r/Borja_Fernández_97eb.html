Borja Fernández Fernández, often shortened to Borja, (born January 14, 1981 in Ourense) is a Spanish footballer, currently at Valladolid. His position is defender and defensive midfielder.

Borja joined the academy in 2000 and after impressive displays for the Real Madrid C team and Real Madrid Castilla, he was promoted to the senior squad in 2003. He appeared in fifteen La Liga games that season and eight in his second season. He has struggled to break into the first team at the club and moved to the second division in 2006.
