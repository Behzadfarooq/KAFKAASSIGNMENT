Born Too Late is the third album by the American doom metal/stoner metal band Saint Vitus, which was released in 1987. This was the first Saint Vitus album featuring former Obsessed singer Scott "Wino" Weinrich.

In 1990, SST released Born Too Late on CD and included the Thirsty And Miserable EP tracks.
