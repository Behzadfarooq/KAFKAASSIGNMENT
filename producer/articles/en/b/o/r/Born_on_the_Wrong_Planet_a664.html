Born on the Wrong Planet is the 1996 debut album of Colorado based Jam band, String Cheese Incident. Re-released in 1998 the album includes studio versions of many of the most popular songs in the band's regular live rotation , including "Black Clouds," "Land's End," "Texas," and "Jellyfish."
