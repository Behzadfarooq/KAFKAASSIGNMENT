Communes of Borgou

Borgou is one of the twelve departments of Benin. Borgou borders the country of Nigeria and the departments of Alibori, Atakora, Collines and Donga. The capital of Borgou is Parakou and the total area is 25,310km.² or 9,772 mi.².

Borgou is divided into the communes of Bembéréké, Kalalé, Kandi, N'Dali, Nikki, Parakou, Pèrèrè, Sinendé, and Tchaourou.
