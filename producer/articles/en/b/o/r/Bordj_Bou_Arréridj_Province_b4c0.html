Bordj Bou Arréridj Province

Bordj Bou Arréridj (Arabic: ولاية برج بوعريريج) is a province and in the east Algeria around 200 km away from the capital Algiers. It is famous for its many electronic industries and for its kind and generous people. There is also a city Bordj Bou Arreridj. Other localities include Bir-Kasd-Ali and Glela.
