Borodianka

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Borodianka (Ukrainian: Бородянка) is a town in Kiev Oblast (province) of Ukraine. It's the administrative center of Borodianskyi Raion. Population is 12,535 (2001).

The town is notable for its private aviation airfield (one of the few in Ukraine) and "Borex" excavator-manufacturing company (being the only important industry in Borodianka).
