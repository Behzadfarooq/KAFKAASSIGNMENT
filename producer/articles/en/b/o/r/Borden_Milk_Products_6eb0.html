Borden Milk Products

Borden Milk Products LP was founded in 1856 by Gail Borden and is now owned by Dallas-based Milk Products LP. MPLP is privately held and located near National Dairy Products LP.

Most of Borden's products are sold in the United States. The condensed milk line began in 1856 was sold to Eagle Family Foods Incorporated in 1998.

It sublicenses the Borden name, as well as its mascot, Elsie the Cow.
