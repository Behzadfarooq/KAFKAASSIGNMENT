Bora Zivkovic (born September 4, 1974) is a Danish professionel football player, who currently plays for the Danish club Vejle Boldklub. He is on contract with the club until July 1, 2007. Even though he is a defender, he has a certain skill to score goals often by heading. This has resulted in him scoring in all the clubs he has ever played for and in his debut for both Herfølge Boldklub on March 14, 2004 and for Vejle Boldklub on July 19, 2006. He came close to do the same in his debut for Fredrikstad FK on July 25, 2004 but missed a penalty. Zivkovic didn't score after his debut match for Herfølge Boldklub and the status is so far the same in Vejle Boldklub.

He was one time asked to join the squad for the Danish National Team but he never debuted.

[edit] Honours

  • Danish champion with Silkeborg IF 1993-94
  • Danish Cup Winner with Silkeborg IF 2001
