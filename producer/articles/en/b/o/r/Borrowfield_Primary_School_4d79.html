Borrowfield Primary School

Borrowfield is a Primary School in Montrose, Angus. It has a pupil roll of around 300 pupils with staff numbering around 30.

[edit] School buildings

There are three main building in the school grounds and these are as follows: Infant Department- P.1 to P.3/4, Upper Department- P.4 to P.7 and the Office Block- Gym hall, ICT Room, First Aid room and Canteen.

There are 14 rooms altogether in the Infant and Upper Departments.

[edit] P.S.A

There are sevral P.S.As (Primary School Assistant) on hand at Interval and Lunchtime as well as in the Canteen and the First Aid Room. These people are there to ensure the playgrounds are as safe as possible and to punish any pupil who misbehaves.

[edit] Card systems

P.S. As have the authority to issue warning cards to any pupil who misbehaves in the playground and the cards are as follows: Smile Card- Awarded to a pupil who behaves in a manner that is helpful to other Pupils or to the P.S.As. Yellow Card- This is a warning card given to a pupil who misbehaves in the playground like being in a restricted area. Red Card- This is the most serious card given to a pupil who has committed a serious offence like swearing or fighting or has misbehaved again after receiving the yellow card. The pupil or pupils who get issued with the red card must return to his/her class patio and stay there until the bell rings.
