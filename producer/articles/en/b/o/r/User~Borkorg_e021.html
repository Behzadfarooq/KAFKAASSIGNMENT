[edit] BORKORG

Unleashed public servant/inconoclast [who] has, indeed, endured [and endeared?] much racial intolerance, repugnant consternation [of the masses]... and plaguing small mountain hamlets with dieseased locusts... He is often interpreted as a resurrected nephilim, and under this guise he is named 용태규 (Yong Taegyu). Borkorg distributed much of his recalcitrant propaganda using this epithet during his brief lodging in South Korea. He also used several other pseudonyms; the "invidious moniker" he adopted after being reinstated as a "leviathan denizen" of Yamato was Jigoku Shounetsu (焦熱地獄, Shounetsu Jigoku), which was deciphered recently by linguistic guildsman Dr. Richard Hindmarsh. It means "burning inferno" in the English tongue... how ironic? No.

Damien Borkorg, under the pseudonym of Heath Ledger, commenced his first 'malevolent assault' (which was cited as Operation Blackhawk) by vying to eliminate over ten thousand key delegates during the clandestine DetroitRockCity convention in 1978. He was aided by notorious political scientist, Keiji Haino, who often implored that he "find a more suitable vocation"... However, Damien was a negligent knave during this period of counterculture eradication (labelled the "Epoch Of Capitalist Waste"); his once rational creeds became belligerent hallucinations of brutal warfare and the haggard profiles of deceased commanders and perishing infantrymen... these increasingly bizarre visions culminated in a violent catharsis which was often referred to as the Tank Man incident. Over time, the conceited wretch became an established percussionist in the Defiled Prog movement, and was a member of several collusive "musical consortiums" which were mustered from the enslaved pagans of the era; such bands were often cited as chaotic, unhinged, nugatory, masochistic, otiose, injudicious, judicious, and irreverent. Jejune Motherfire was perhaps the group he was most renowned for; they produced over twenty thousand "enmity infestations" during their convoluted career, including Execration Upon Those Cursed, Desolate Horrors, Merciless Battlestar Of Mephisto's Lust, Embrace Of The Nautical Violence, Rancor Of The Maligned Pestilence and their five hour improvisation, Convulsing Betwixt The Inquity Of Lucifer Volition Und Pernicious, Vile Putrefaction Of Infernal Cadaver, which included Rotting Karlyle, Repulsive Hydra, Ravaged Slut and Infuriated Revulsion Of Zionism, the latter of whose "forces" were massacred on three consecutive days following the performance. Damien Borkorg departed to an alternate dimension for four years, but frequently commuted to his erstwhile dwelling in Pit, Los Angeles.

Afterwards, his body began to decay. Unfortunately it has never been fully restored, despite the fact that it evokes memories of a dark childhood...
