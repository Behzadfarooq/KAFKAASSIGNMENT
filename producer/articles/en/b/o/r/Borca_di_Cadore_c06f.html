Location of Borca di Cadore in Italy

Borca di Cadore is a comune (municipality) in the Province of Belluno in the Italian region Veneto, located about 110 km north of Venice and about 35 km north of Belluno. As of 31 December 2004, it had a population of 820 and an area of 27.0 km².[1]

The municipality of Borca di Cadore contains the frazione (subdivision) Villanova di Borca di Cadore.

Borca di Cadore borders the following municipalities: Calalzo di Cadore, San Vito di Cadore, Selva di Cadore, Vodo Cadore, Zoldo Alto.
