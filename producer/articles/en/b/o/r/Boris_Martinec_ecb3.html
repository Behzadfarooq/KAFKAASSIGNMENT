Boris Martinec (born November 27, 1988 in Zagreb, Croatia) is a Croatian figure skater. He is the 2004, 2005, and 2007 Croatian national champion. He is a two-time competitor at the World Junior Figure Skating Championships, although he has never made it out of qualifying. His highest placement in an ISU Championship is 22nd at the 2007 European Figure Skating Championships.
