Borås Municipality [bu'roːs], is a Swedish municipality where the city of Borås is seat.

[edit] Sister cities

The municipality has four sister cities and one other international town it cooperates with.

  • Espelkamp, Germany
  • Molde, Norway
  • Mikkeli, Finland
  • Vejle, Denmark

[edit] Cooperation town

  • Modena, Italia
