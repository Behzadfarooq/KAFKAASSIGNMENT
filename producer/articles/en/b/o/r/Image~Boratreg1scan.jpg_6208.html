[edit] Summary

A scan of the DVD cover for Borat! Cultural Learnings of America for Make Benefit Glorious Nation of Kazakhstan. Scan made by OverlordKain, scaled down by self.

A rough translation of the back of the DVD, made by Kain using www.translate.ru:

"The official Ministry of Information of Kazakhstan represents the film of Borat Sagdiev, the fourth most well-known journalist of Kazakhstan. Look its travel on U, S, and A and its knowledge about the greatest country of the world. It meets many-many interesting people in the well-known country of democracy and pornography. Borat studies to drive a car, behave on etiquette, to lower water in a toilet bowl, and too how to look after for the sexually attractive, yellow-haired woman, Pamela the Anderson. As film contains abusive language, unnecessary application of force and violence, as well as the staff of a close up of man’s Bishkek, the present film cannot be shown to children of less 3-years age. We wish pleasure!"
