Born Reckless is a 1930 American crime comedy directed by Andrew Bennison and John Ford written by Donald Henderson Clarke based on a novel by Louis Beretti. The film starred Edmund Lowe and Catherine Dale Owen.

[edit] Plot

Hoping to use the publicity to get re-elected, a judge sentences a notorious gangster to fight in the war.
