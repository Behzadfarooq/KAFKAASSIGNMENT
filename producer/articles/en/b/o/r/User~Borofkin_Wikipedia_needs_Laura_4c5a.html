User:Borofkin/Wikipedia needs Laura

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

< User:Borofkin

Laura is a very intelligent, well read, and well travelled young woman. Her skills extend far beyond mere mastery of the English language. Apparently she is quite handy with a nailgun and cordless drill, as well.

The Wikipedia Community has started a campaign to convince Laura to become an active contributor, as most are certain that the very survival of the project is dependant on this occurring.

[edit] Why Wikipedia needs Laura

Wikipedia has thousands of contributors, and while everybody has some knowledge to contribute, some have a less than perfect command of the language. There are typographical errors, poorly worded sentences, and embarrassing grammatical errors throughout. The need for professional and skilled editors is obvious.

[edit] Why Laura should join Wikipedia

  • Wikipedia is a volunteer organisation, and just like any volunteer organisation, they are always in need of new members
  • Wikipedia has noble intentions. Copyrighted encyclopedias, while a good source of information, are only available to a very small number of wealthy people. The goal of Wikipedia is to make knowledge available to everyone, regardless of their ability to pay.
  • Believe it or not, contributing to Wikipedia is fun

[edit] Where to start?

  • Copyedit an article. There are thousands of articles that need to be cleaned up
  • Join a project
  • Check out the Australian wikipedians' 'notice board or the U.S. Wikipedians' notice board
