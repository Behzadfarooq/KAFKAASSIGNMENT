Borat's Television Programme is a 2 part compilation spin-off of Da Ali G Show by British television station Channel 4, featuring Borat Sagdiyev (Cyrillic: Борат Сагдиев) in America learning about the local culture and customs, much in the vein of the later movie Borat: Cultural Learnings of America for Make Benefit Glorious Nation of Kazakhstan. The 2 episodes contain Borat and Bruno segments from Ali G In Da USA, along with new & unseen interviews and shenanigans.

[edit] Episodes

No. Episode Air Date
1.01 "Episode 1" August 20, 2004
1.02 "Episode 2" August 22, 2004

Both Episodes were repeated November 1 2006 on Channel 4 in preparation for the movies release on November 3 2006.
