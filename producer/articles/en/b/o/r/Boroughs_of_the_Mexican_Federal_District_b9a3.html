Boroughs of the Mexican Federal District

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Mexican Federal District is divided into 16 boroughs (delegaciones) for local government and administrative purposes. These do not have regulatory powers, which are mostly centralized in the Federal District government. The head of a borough is called a jefe delegacional in Spanish.
