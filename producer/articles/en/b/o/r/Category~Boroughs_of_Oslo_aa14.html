Category:Boroughs of Oslo

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

There are currently 15 boroughs in the city of Oslo (Between 1988 and 2004 the number was 25). The boroughs are self administrated to a large degree. Marka and Sentrum are parts of Oslo, but not boroughs with an administration. There are also a variety of neighbourhoods without administrative status, some of which formerly had borough status.
