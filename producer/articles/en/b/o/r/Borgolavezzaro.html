Borgolavezzaro is a comune (municipality) in the Province of Novara in the Italian region Piedmont, located about 80 km northeast of Turin and about 15 km southeast of Novara. As of 31 December 2004, it had a population of 1,909 and an area of 21.2 km².[1]

Borgolavezzaro borders the following municipalities: Albonese, Cilavegna, Nicorvo, Robbio, Tornaco, Vespolate.
