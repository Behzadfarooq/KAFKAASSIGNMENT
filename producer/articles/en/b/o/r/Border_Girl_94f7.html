Border Girl is Mexican singer Paulina Rubio's English-language debut album and her sixth solo studio album. The album was released in North America by Universal Records on June 18, 2002. Border Girl includes Rubio's most successful single in English, "Don't Say Goodbye", that opened doors for Rubio in new markets such as the United Kingdom. Besides, the next three singles as "The One You Love" and "I'll Be Right Here (Sexual Lover)" failed to gain airplay due the medium promotion of the album.
