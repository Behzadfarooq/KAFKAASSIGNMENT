Location of Borgo Pace in Italy

Borgo Pace is a comune (municipality) in the Province of Pesaro e Urbino in the Italian region Marche, located about 100 km west of Ancona and about 60 km southwest of Pesaro. As of 31 December 2004, it had a population of 674 and an area of 56.0 km².[1]

Borgo Pace borders the following municipalities: Badia Tedalda, Carpegna, Mercatello sul Metauro, San Giustino, Sansepolcro, Sestino.
