Boronia Park Public School is a state primary school in the bushland and waterfront suburb of Hunter's Hill, 2110, in the Municipality of Hunters Hill, in Sydney, Australia. The school is run by the New South Wales Department of Education and Training.

As part of a special parent-funded programme, the school runs weekly Italian language classes for all students.

The school boasts a training band and three choirs.
