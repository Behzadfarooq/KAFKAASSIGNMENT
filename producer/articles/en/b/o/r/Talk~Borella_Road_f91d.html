[edit] Borella Road

While the information is facturally accurate and verifiable, it is trivial and adds nothing to the sum of human knowledge. If one travels along Borella Road as I have, one will observe takeaways, the hospital, the airport, but so what - not notable: Delete. AYArktos 09:17, 25 May 2005 (UTC)

the road, contrary to the description is not the 2nd most major road in Albury. It is already mentioned in the article about Albert Chalmers Borella VC after whom the road was named. If one was going to augment the Albury article with descriptions of roads there would be at least half a dozen roads or streets mentioned before Borella Road.--AYArktos 09:22, 5 Jun 2005 (UTC)
  • Describing Albury, New South Wales requires describing its major roads. Merge with that or keep. Kappa 09:24, 25 May 2005 (UTC)
  • Merge. Radiant_* 10:09, May 25, 2005 (UTC)
  • Delete: First, it's not going to prove to be a unique name. Second, it's a stranded detail (merge would be fine). Third, the road needs a character to be described at all; being a trafficked piece of asphalt is not sufficient. Fourth, the road would need to be known outside of its city to be sought. Geogre 14:27, 25 May 2005 (UTC)
  • Not worth an article on it's own, could be a disamb problem. Merge into Albury, New South Wales and remove trivial stuff on celebs. It doesn't make a road any more important. Mgm|(talk) 18:46, May 25, 2005 (UTC)
  • Delete per Geogre. Quale 00:39, 26 May 2005 (UTC)
  • Keep or Merge, and if necessary disambiguate the redirect. --SPUI (talk) 01:25, 26 May 2005 (UTC)
  • Merge into Albury, New South Wales. Zzyzx11 (Talk) 03:59, 26 May 2005 (UTC)
  • Delete or Merge Vegaswikian 05:52, 26 May 2005 (UTC)
  • Merge with Albury, New South Wales and disambiguate the redirect later if needed, per SPUI. Sjakkalle 07:07, 26 May 2005 (UTC)
  • Merge with Albury, New South Wales. I'm all for having articles on prominent streets, but I'd like to see a better claim to notability. Ambi 11:37, 4 Jun 2005 (UTC)
