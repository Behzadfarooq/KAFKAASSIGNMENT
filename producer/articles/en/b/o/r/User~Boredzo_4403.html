Peter Hosey, aka Mac-arena the Bored Zo, is:

  • a developer on the Adium and Growl projects
  • an expert programmer in C, Objective-C, and Python, and a novice programmer in Perl
  • an expert Mac OS X user (currently on Tiger, with Panther on a separate partition)
  • thoroughly pedantic
  • a fan of Unicode and hyperlinks

The Bored Zo corrects the following on sight:

  • any spelling error
  • use of "--" where "â€”" is meant
  • double-spaces after a full stop
  • lists where a space does not occur between the "*" and the item text
  • "However" not being followed by a comma when it should be (for example, "However some people choose to go to Subway instead of McDonald's")
  • improperly-formatted links ([[x|x]], [[x|xs]], etc.)
  • "seperate"

If you want to talk to the Bored Zo live, he typically is available on freenode, under the nickname Mac-arena or BoredZo.
