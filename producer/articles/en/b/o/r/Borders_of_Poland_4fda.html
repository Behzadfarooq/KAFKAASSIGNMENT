Polish borders with length

Borders of Poland are 3582 kilometers long[1]. The neighboring countries are Germany to the west, the Czech Republic and Slovakia to the south, Ukraine and Belarus to the east, and Lithuania and the Russian province of Kaliningrad to the northeast. To the north, Poland is bordered by the Baltic Sea.

Breakdown of border lenghts per country:

  • sea: 528km[1]
  • Russia (Kaliningrad): 210[1]
  • Lithuania: 103[1]
  • Belarus: 416[1]
  • Ukraine: 529[1]
  • Slovakia: 539[1]
  • Czech Republic: 790[1]
  • Germany: 467[1]
