Born to Reign is the third solo studio album released by Will Smith. The album was released on Columbia Records in the United States on June 25, 2002 and was considered a drop from his previous level of succes, having only reached Gold status by the RIAA. Whereas "Big Willie Style" and "Willenium" both reached multiplatinum status. Smith's 2005 album, "Lost and Found" (which reclaimed the success the aforementioned albums had) addressed this with the title and numerous references to him focusing on acting over rapping.

This album includes the semi-successful tracks "Willow is a Player" and "Black Suits Comin'" which is also the lead single from the soundtrack to the major motion picture Men in Black 2.
