Borovany (České Budějovice District)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

See other places named Borovany.
Location of Borovany in the Czech Republic

Borovany is a small town in the South Bohemian Region of the Czech Republic, near České Budějovice. It has cca 3,700 inhabitants.

Hamlets Vrcov, Hluboká u Borovan, Dvorec, Třebeč, Radostice and Trocnov are administrative part of Borovany.
