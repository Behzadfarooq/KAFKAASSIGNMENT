Borovce is a village and municipality in Piešťany District in the Trnava Region of western Slovakia.

[edit] History

In historical records the village was first mentioned in 1262.

[edit] Geography

The municipality lies at an altitude of 160 metres and covers an area of 10.879km². It has a population of about 913 people.
