Borut Bilač

Borut Bilač (born 14 April 1965) is a retired Slovenian long jumper, best known for finishing ninth at the 1992 Olympic Games. His personal best is 8.24 metres, achieved in July 1990.

He is married to high jumper Britta Bilač.
