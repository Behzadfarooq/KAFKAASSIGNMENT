Peppers

Why was the article on Brian Peppers removed?

Because, really, there's nothing particularly notable about him.

What is there to say about him? What did he ever do?

He's ugly. He's so ugly that, when his picture was being passed around online, some people thought it had been Photoshopped. But it was real. It turns out he's pathologically ugly. It's called "Crouzon's syndrome", and because of it, he lives in a nursing home. Snopes investigated, and it turns out that he got arrested for sexually touching an employee at the home, except maybe it was all a misunderstanding, we really don't know, it's not public information. The picture everyone's seen is his mug shot. YTMND made fun of him a lot.

Annnnnnnd.... that's it. Being investigated by Snopes isn't particularly special; investigating is what Snopes does. Being made fun of on YTMND isn't particularly special; making fun of stuff is what YTMND does.

Early versions of the article got deleted for, basically, saying "HE'S A FUCKING UGLY PERVERT HA HA HA HA HA HA BRIAN PEPPERS BRIAN PEPPERS BRIAN PERVERT FUCKING UGLY DICK HA HA HA HA!"; eventually a more serious version was constructed, but even then there was nothing particularly special about him.

It's not like he's Joseph Merrick, the Elephant Man. He was pathologically ugly too, but he was the subject of a major motion picture and an award-winning play. Lots of people are pathologically ugly.

It's not like he's Ghyslain Raza, the Star Wars kid. He got made fun of on the Internet a lot too, but he also got made fun of in major media - on talk shows, in Newsweek, on TV cartoons, on the Jumbotron at various sporting events... and then there was a huge lawsuit. Lots of people get made fun of on the Internet.

It's not like he's Kenneth Pinyan. He was a sex offender too, but he wound up being grotesquely killed in a way that resulted in new laws being passed. Lots of people are sex offenders.

It's not like he's Lois McMaster Bujold. She's from Ohio too, but she's a bestselling author who's won multiple awards. Lots of people are from Ohio.

What's the big deal about Brian Peppers? DS 01:12, 21 February 2007 (UTC)

The big deal is he IS notable. Not for being from Ohio or for molesting someone, but there he is a MASSIVE internet phenomenon now and he rightly deserves a place here. Seems there are others who agree with me on this one. Boris Allen 12:21, 21 February 2007 (UTC)
We have specific criteria for online phenomena; Mr Peppers does not meet those criteria. DS 13:23, 21 February 2007 (UTC)
Even though Brian Peppers does not have a page because of libel concerns, privacy concerns, and family requests WP:BLP, he does have a mention at List of Internet phenomena. Is this sufficient? --Kevinkor2 14:18, 21 February 2007 (UTC)
