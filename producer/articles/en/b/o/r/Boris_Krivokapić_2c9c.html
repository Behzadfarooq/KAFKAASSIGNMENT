Prof. Boris Krivokapić, LL.D. (born 16 November 1958 in Belgrade) is af ull-professor of Public International Law as well as of Human Rights with the Megatrend University in Belgrade.

He graduated at the Faculty of Law of the Belgrade University in 1979, where also got his M.A. in 1985 and LL.D. in 1988.

He is the author of 23 books (of them 11 as co-author) and over 100 scientific articles, published in Serbian (Yugoslav) and international scientific journals in a dozen of countries of Europe, Asia and America; some 20 scientific projects, over 300 reviews, etc.

He received a number of awards, including: (2005) Award class A1 for distinguished results in science (awarded by Serbian Ministry of Science; (2006) Award “Prof. dr Borislav T. Blagojevic” for the best book on Comparative and European Law, published in Serbia and Montenegro in the period 2004-2005.
