"Born to Be Wild" is the first SpongeBob SquarePants episode from season four.

Spoiler warning: Plot and/or ending details follow.

Brief Summary: SpongeBob hears that a very bad motorcycle gang is coming to Bikini Bottom. He tries to warn the other people in Bikini Bottom but with no avail, Mr Krabs wants to host the gang in The Krusty Krab.

Time/Date: April 24

Songs: Unknown

[edit] Plot

SpongeBob is jellyfishing. Some men on motorcycles drive past him, and SpongeBob holds on to one of the men's jackets, ripping a 'W' off of the jacket. He looks at the jacket when he has to let go of it. He presumes it says 'The ild Ones', but he soon notices the 'W' in his hand and figures out that they are called 'The Wild Ones'. After someone tells him about the Wild Ones, SpongeBob runs to tell Mr. Krabs. He does not listen, so he tells Patrick. Mr Krabs puts up a banner saying 'WELCOME WILD ONES!' on the Krusty Krab and raises his prices in order to get them to eat there after hearing Spongebob describes them as "ravenous". After Patrick says that if they dressed up like bikers, they would be the big scary bikers, SpongeBob and Patrick decide to dress in biker attire and ride into town on a paddle-unicycle and a paddle-tricycle, respectively. After the bikers arrive at The Krusty Krab, it turns out that they are old folk. The actual name of the biker gang is 'The Mild Ones'. SpongeBob had the 'M' upside-down. The biker that had the "M" ripped off of his jacket gets his "M" back from SpongeBob, and The Mild Ones ride away. Shortly after this, it is revealed that Squidward decided to go with them.

[edit] Trivia/Goofs

  • Once again, Patrick is seen wear different clothes on a non-fancy occasion, with one of the first being in My Pretty Seahorse.
  • This episode aired in Germany and UK before it aired in the United States.
  • Patrick's Goofy Goober underwear can be seen.
  • Born To Be Wild was mistaken on many sites to be called Burn To Be Wild.
  • Old Man Jenkins is one of The Mild Ones
  • This is the third time that the letters W and M were switched around; the first being "No Weenies Allowed", second being "Mermaid Man and Barnacle Boy IV".
  • The German title, in English, means, strangely, "Young and Wild".
  • German Title: Wilde Jungs
  • When the Mild Ones are driving around Squidward, when you watch that in slow motion, you can see that the men's jackets say "Mile Ones" instead of "Mild Ones".
  • If you look closely, you'll be able to see that the bikers are actually old people even though they are riding really fast.
  • Patrick and SpongeBob are chopped up by a lawn mower into tiny stars and tiny sponge cubes.
  • SpongeBob's biker outfit consists of: a red and white striped long sleeved top, a denim waistcoat, jeans, brown work boots and a round helmet with a spike and a skull. His bike is his paddle-unicycle.
  • Patrick's biker outfit consists of: a yellow-ish t-shirt, a black leather jacket with a high collar, blue denim shorts with a big belt with a skull on it and a black cap. His bike is a paddle-tricycle.
  • Patrick and SpongeBob drink blood and 'diet' blood.
  • When SpongeBob takes the "shortcut", he breaks the "Fourth Wall" by running into an invisible wall.
  • The episode reveals Mr. Krabs really doesn't care about SpongeBob and Patrick, as he says he will let the biker gang beat them up, eat, beat them up again, eat, beat them up again, ect, just to make money.
  • This episode will be aired Thursday 29 March 2007 in Latin America.
