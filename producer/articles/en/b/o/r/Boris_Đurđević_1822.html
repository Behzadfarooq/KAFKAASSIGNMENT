Boris Đurđević (b. March 15, 1973 in Vinkovci) is Croatian musician, best known as the member of Colonia dance music band.

After experimenting with rap and disco music in his youth, Đurđević became DJ in Vinkovci in early 1990s. After meeting with Tomislav Jelić and Indira Vladić, he founded Colonia, a band for which he writes music and lyrics.

Boris Đurđević is also known as the author of theme songs for Croatian version of Big Brother reality show and songwriter for the Hrvatski Idol winner's single Treba Mi Snage Za Kraj (I Need Strength For The End).
