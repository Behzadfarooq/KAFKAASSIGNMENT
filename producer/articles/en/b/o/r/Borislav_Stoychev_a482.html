Borislav Stoychev

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Borislav Stoychev (Bulgarian: Борислав Стойчев) (born November 4, 1986 in Haskovo, Bulgaria) is a Bulgarian professional football player who currently plays for Levski Sofia as a defender. He began his career in hometown, playing for FC Haskovo. He was bought by Levski Sofia in January, 2006. He is a Bulgaria national under-21 football team international.

  • Height - 1.87 m.
  • Weight - 78 kg.
