Borgone Susa is a comune (municipality) in the Province of Turin in the Italian region Piedmont, located about 35 km west of Turin. As of 31 December 2004, it had a population of 2,310 and an area of 5.0 km².[1]

Borgone Susa borders the following municipalities: Condove, San Didero, Villar Focchiardo, and Sant'Antonino di Susa.
