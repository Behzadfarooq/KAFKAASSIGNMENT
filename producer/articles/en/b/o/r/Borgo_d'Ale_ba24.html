Location of Borgo d'Ale in Italy

Borgo d'Ale is a comune (municipality) in the Province of Vercelli in the Italian region Piedmont, located about 40 km northeast of Turin and about 30 km west of Vercelli. As of 31 December 2004, it had a population of 2,629 and an area of 39.3 km².[1]

Borgo d'Ale borders the following municipalities: Alice Castello, Azeglio, Bianzè, Borgomasino, Cossano Canavese, Maglione, Moncrivello, Settimo Rottaro, Tronzano Vercellese, and Viverone.
