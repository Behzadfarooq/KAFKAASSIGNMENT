Borsodi Jégkorong Liga

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The "Borsodi Jégkorong Liga" (Borsodi Ice Hockey League) is the first line of the Hungarian Ice Hockey Championship. It has 6 Hungarian teams and 1 Romanian (Transylvanian club with Hungarian players) team in the 2006 - 2007 season.

[edit] The teams (2006-2007)

  • Alba Volán Fevita (Székesfehérvár - Hungary)
  • Fehérvári titánok (Székesfehérvár - Hungary)
  • DAB.extra.hu (ex Dunaferr S.E.) (Dunaújváros - Hungary)
  • Ferencvárosi TC (FTC)(Budapest IX. - Hungary)
  • Miskolci Jegesmedvék Jégkorong Sportegyesület (Miskolc - Hungary)
  • Újpesti TE (ex Újpesti Dózsa)(Budapest IV. - Hungary)
  • Csíkszeredai Sport Club jégkorong csapata (Csíkszereda - Transylvania)
