Born to Be Beavers / Up All Night was the first episode of the first season of the Animated Television series The Angry Beavers.

[edit] Born to Be Beavers

In a mendacious documentary, entitled "Beavers on the Go", a family of beavers are living happily until the mother gives birth to a second litter. This means the first litter is now ready to leave the lodge and live on their own. Norbert, the oldest by 4 minutes, is ready, but Daggett is reluctant. Norb tells him if they just kick back and relax, the job will get done. Dag tries to build a den, but he catches Norb goofing off in a chicken house. Norb claims this would make a good dwelling. The farmer catches them, and chases them into his truck. The truck goes down a hill, through a forest, hitting every tree in its path, over a cliff, into a pond, forming our heroes home.

  • Written By: Mitchell Schauer
  • Storyboard: Mitchell Schauer, Michael R. Gerard
  • Directed By: Robert Hughes

[edit] Up All Night

The beavers can't sleep, and since they're not living with their parents anymore, they decide to stay up all night. Throughout the night they have a jumping contest, do prank calls, watch horror movies, exercise, play tether ball, pillow fight, and battle the mysterious Crawling Spleen (or is it just a leaky faucet?). The beavers notice the clock which says 10:54. Dag sees that the clock isn't plugged in, and they rush outside, to their blow, they've stayed up a ba-zillion years.

  • Written By: Victor Wilson
  • Storyboard: Mitchell Schauer, Michael R. Gerard
  • Directed By: Robert Hughes

[edit] Notes/Trivia

  • First appearances (Born to Be Beavers): Norbert, Daggett, Mrs. Beaver, Mr. Beaver, Stacey & Chelsea (unnamed at this point), Bill Lickings (voice-over), (Up All Night) The Crawling Spleen, Oxnard Montolvo.
  • Series creator Mitch Schauer, is the voice of the farmer.
  • Up All Night, would get a follow-up in Season 3 entitled, "Up All Day".
  • Up All Night is the first episode where the beavers are seen eating jalepenos peppers.
  • When Dag and Norb's parents first appear in the black and white documentary, their mom is a light tone, and their dad is a dark tone, but in subsequent episodes, their dad is yellow, and mom is brown.
  • When Dag is first seen in "Born to Be Beavers", he has two eyebrows, but afterwards, they become a unibrow, and stay that way for the remainder of the show (except for a brief moment in "Long in the Teeth" and "Food of the Clods").
