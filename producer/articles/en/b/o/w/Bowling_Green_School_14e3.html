Bowling Green School

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Bowling Green School is a Pre-K through 12th Grade, college prep, private school located in Franklinton, Louisiana, USA, in Washington parish.

The school colors are Green, Gold and White. The school mascot is the Buccaneer.
