Bowling Bowling Bowling Parking Parking is a live EP released by Green Day in 1996. The songs were recorded on different dates. The tracklisting and recording dates are as follows:

[edit] Track listing

  1. "Armatage Shanks" (recorded live March 26, 1996, Spoytinalle, Prague)
  2. "Brainstew" (recorded live March 26, 1996, Spoytinalle, Prague)
  3. "Jaded" (recorded live March 26, 1996, Spoytinalle, Prague)
  4. "Knowledge" (recorded live March 11, 1994, Jannis Landing, St. Petersburg)
  5. "Basket Case" (recorded live January 27, 1996, Harumi Arena, Tokyo)
  6. "She" (recorded live March 26, 1996, Spoytinalle, Prague)
  7. "Walking Contradiction" (recorded live March 26, 1996, Spoytinalle, Prague)
