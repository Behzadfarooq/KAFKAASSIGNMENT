Bowl used for smoking marijuana

Bowl is used by smokers of cannabis to indicate one of two different things:

  • The bowl-shaped receptacle in which marijuana is placed prior to smoking which forms part of a marijuana pipe or bong. It can also be used to refer to the removable slide found in many bongs.
  • The quantity of marijuana necessary to fill this receptacle. As the size of these receptacles will vary depending on what the marijuana is being smoked through, this is not an exact measure and is not in any way standardized.
