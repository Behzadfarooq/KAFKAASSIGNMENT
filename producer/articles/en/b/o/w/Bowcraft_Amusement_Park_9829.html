Bowcraft Amusement Park

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Bowcraft Amusement Park or Bowcraft Playland is located on Route 22 West in Scotch Plains, New Jersey, United States. It is a medium size family amusement park with a large selection of rides for children and teens, including the Dragon roller coaster. Bowcraft Amusement Park is open weekends from April through October and daily June through Labor Day. Parking is $5 and admission is free. Rides are priced using multiple tickets. The opening scene from the 1994 movie North was filmed at Bowcraft.
