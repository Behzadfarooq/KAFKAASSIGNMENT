Bowenfels Railway Station

Bowenfels is a disused railway station on the Main Western line of New South Wales, Australia. It consists of a historic sandstone building. The station opened in 1869 with the construction of the railway[1] and was electrified in the 1950's as part of the Blue Mountains line electrification programme. Passenger services were withdrawn in 1974 and the station was closed. The station was restored in 1994 and now operates as the Lithgow tourist information office[2].
