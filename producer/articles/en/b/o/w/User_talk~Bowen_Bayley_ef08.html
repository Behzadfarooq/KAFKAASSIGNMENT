[edit] Fate/Stay Night

Every other wiki article does NOT have spoilers on the main page, only the poorly written ones or the ones that have a small enough amount of content that seperate pages wouldn't make sense. A good example of how a Wiki page of the type Fate should be is to look at the Bleach pages. Hence why major spoilers for Fate are being put on the character pages instead of the main page. There's nothing worse than browsing the main article for something to get a general idea about something and having spoilers like "Saber is King Arthur!" or "Archer is Shirou!" right there. Spoiler tags don't do a thing when they're right before a section like that, since you'll see them as soon as you see the tags. You're more than welcome to rewrite the article as you see fit, but I'm just as welcome to go in and edit afterwards. A little common courtesy towards ALL those who visit the site goes a long way, so I ask you to please keep major spoilers to a minimum and put the others on character pages. It just solves so many problems before they start. On a side note, I certainly hope you weren't the one who thought Aoko Aozaki was in Fate and that she was Caster(Assassins Master). ^_^;;; Nezu 06:19, 6 March 2006 (UTC)

I'm a registered user, so no I didn't make that edit. The Bleach page has gone downhill IMO. Take a look at any of the Harry Potter, Die Hard or Lost articles. They all contain plot synopses, I'm certain it's the same for every other TV series, movie, book and manga. Sure spoilers are bad but this is an encyclopedia not a message board and as such a plot synopsis is sorely needed on the F/S N page which I'm sure you've been discouraging people to make with your actions.
