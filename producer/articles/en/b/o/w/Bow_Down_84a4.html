Bow Down is an album by Westside Connection. The group formed because they felt that the East Coast artists lacked respect for the West Coast, which had fallen that year with the death of 2Pac and the collapse of Death Row Records. At first, the album received a fair amount of attention, but eventually became seen by some as a Hip-Hop classic with hits like "Bow Down" and "Gangsta's Make The World Go Round". It peaked at #2 on the Billboard 200 on November 9 shipping 145,000 units[1], 1996 and eventually went platinum, selling 1.7 million copies[2].
