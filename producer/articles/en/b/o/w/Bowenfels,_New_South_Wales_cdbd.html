Bowenfels is a small town on the western outskirts of Lithgow, New South Wales, Australia.

[edit] History

The town was founded in the 1830's to service travellers along the new road to Bathurst, which opened in 1832[1]. The town was the first settlement in the valley and pre-dated Lithgow by 40 years. The railway opened in 1869[2], and was electrified in the 1950s, although passenger services no longer service Bowenfels station.
