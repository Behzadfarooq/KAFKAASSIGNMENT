Bowling at the 2006 Asian Games

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

You have new messages (last change).

Bowling took place for the men's and women's individual, doubles, trios, and team events at the 2006 Asian Games in Doha, Qatar from December 3 to December 10. All events were held at the Qatar Bowling Centre.
