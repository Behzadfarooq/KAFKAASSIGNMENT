Picture taken in August 2003 from the Trans-Canada Highway. Bow River cascades below as mountains from Banff National Park shadow overhead.

The picture shows Morant's Curve, a popular Canadian Pacific Railroad photography spot. It appears in many railroading books and calendars, as well as older Canadian Pacific travel materials.
