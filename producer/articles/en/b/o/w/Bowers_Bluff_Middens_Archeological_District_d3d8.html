Bowers Bluff Middens Archeological District
(U.S. Registered Historic District)
Location: Lake County, Florida
Nearest city: Astor
Coordinates: 29°06′19″N, 81°30′12″W
Added to NRHP: February 1, 1980

The Bowers Bluff Middens Archeological District is a U.S. historic district (designated as such on February 1, 1980) located approximately five miles southeast of Astor.
