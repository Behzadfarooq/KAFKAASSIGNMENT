Bow is a fictional character in the animated television series She-Ra: Princess of Power. He is a member of The Great Rebellion. In one episode he is referred to as the best archer in all of Etheria[1]. He is famed for the accuracy of his shot. He works along side Arrow, his horse. Aside from his skill as an archer Bow is also musically gifted[1][2]. He often writes songs to serenade Adora; his instrument of choice is usually the harp.

[edit] Character information

Spoiler warning: Plot and/or ending details follow.
Bow

Bow is one of the original Rebels; he would often have to disguise his face when leaving The Whispering Woods, to prevent the Horde from identifying him. Bow, like Glimmer, is a rather impetuous member of the Rebellion. He is far too eager to rush into battle with the Horde, which more often than not would get him into trouble. Bow appears to have known Rebellion member Kowl for quite a long time. They have a friendly love/hate relationship. Kowl has on many occasions voiced his dislike of Bow's musical abilities, but Adora seems to enjoy his songs. Bow has also shown some ability as an illusionist. At different times he has made birds appear out of nowhere,[3] and made himself disappear[4].

[edit] Powers and abilities

Bow is Etheria's most skillful archer, described as the best shot in all of Etheira. Bow is also a talented storyteller and has knowledge of old legends to entertain the children[5].

[edit] Arrow

Arrow is Bow's stallion. Arrow's toy figure is a blue pegasus, but in the TV series it is a normal orange horse. Arrow appears in many TV episodes, along Bow.
