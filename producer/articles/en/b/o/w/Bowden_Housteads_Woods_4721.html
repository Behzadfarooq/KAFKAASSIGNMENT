Bowden Housteads Woods

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Bowden Housteads Woods are woods in Darnall, Sheffield. the woods are encircled by Sheffield Outer Ring Road, Sheffield Parkway and Handsworth Road.

The woods are classified as ancient woodlands, having been in existence since the 1600s, the Car Brook flow through the wooded area. Spring brings a carpet of bluebells.
