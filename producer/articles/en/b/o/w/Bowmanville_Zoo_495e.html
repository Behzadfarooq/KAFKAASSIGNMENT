Bowmanville Zoo

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Bowmanville Zoo is a zoo in Bowmanville, Ontario, Canada. It is one of the largest suppliers of animals for Hollywood movies and television programs. It is Canada's oldest private zoo; founded in 1919, it now hosts over 300 animals. The zoo allows children to ride on the back of an elephant or a camel.

Some of the animal talent includes:

  • Baghera, the black jaguar from Peter Benchley's Amazon
  • a bevy of camels from The 13th Warrior starring Antonio Banderas
  • Caesar, the lion from The Ghost and the Darkness
  • Billy, the white bengal tiger from the TV series Animorphs
  • the late Bongo, the lion from the movie George of the Jungle
  • Maggie the Macaque, known for her Stanley Cup Playoffs predictions
