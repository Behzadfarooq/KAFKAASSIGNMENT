Bowie Tsang (Traditional Chinese: 曾寶儀; Simplified Chinese: 曾宝仪; pinyin: Céng Bǎoyí born February 21, 1973 is a Taiwanese female Mandopop singer, actress, TV host and author. She is better known by her nickname a*baw (Traditional Chinese: 阿寶; Simplified Chinese: 阿宝; pinyin: Ā bǎo). She holds a bachelor degree in Sociology from National Taiwan University. She speaks Mandarin, Cantonese, English, and a little Japanese. She loves singing and speaks very quickly. Her hobbies include watching movies, eating good food, sleeping and singing.

[edit] Family

Bowie has a younger sister and two younger brothers. Her father is popular Hong Kong actor, Eric Tsang.

While her father stays in Hong Kong for his acting career, Bowie now lives in Taiwan with her mother and her siblings. Although they see each other when they can, she primarily communicates with her father over the phone.
