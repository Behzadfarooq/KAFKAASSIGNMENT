Bow Wow (That's My Name) is the 2nd single off of Lil' Bow Wow's debut album Beware of Dog It samples "Dernier Domicile Connu" originally recorded by Francois De Roubaix and "Atomic Dog" by George Clinton.[1],[2]

[edit] The music video

The video was directed by Dave Meyers[3]. It was voted #1 Video on BET.

  • 2001 : Won Billboard Music Award
    • Category Rap Single of the Year for "Bow Wow (That's My Name)"
  • 2001 : Won BET Award
    • Category Viewer's Choice for "Bow Wow (That's My Name)"
