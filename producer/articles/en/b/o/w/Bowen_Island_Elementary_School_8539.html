Bowen Island Elementary School

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

It has been suggested that this article or section be merged with Bowen Island Community School. (Discuss)

Bowen Island Community School (commonly referred to as "BICS", pronounced as one word as though it were spelled "bicks") is a K-7 school located on Bowen Island, British Columbia, Canada. The school, while being on Bowen Island is part of the West Vancouver school district. Once students graduate from elementary school they must commute daily by ferry to secondary schools or they must go to a private school called Island Pacific School (IPS) that goes from grades 6-9.
