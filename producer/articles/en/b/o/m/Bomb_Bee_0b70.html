Bomb Bee
Developer(s) Namco
Publisher(s) Namco
Release date(s) 1979
Genre(s) Breakout
Mode(s) Up to 2 players, alternating turns
Platform(s) Arcade
Input Paddle; 1 button
Arcade cabinet Upright, cabaret, and cocktail
Arcade display Vertical orientation, Raster, 224 x 272 resolution

Bomb Bee is an arcade game that was released by Namco in 1979. It is the sequel to Gee Bee, which was released in the previous year.
