Bombay High (2007) is a forthcoming high budget Hindi film directed by debutant, Nupur Ashtana. Madhavan is paired with Tabu in the flick. Vinod Mehra's daughter, Soniya Mehra will aso play a role. Music is set to be rendered by Sandeep Chowta. Shooting will start in March 2007 and end in September 2007 for release. The film was previously titled Hello Zindagi.
