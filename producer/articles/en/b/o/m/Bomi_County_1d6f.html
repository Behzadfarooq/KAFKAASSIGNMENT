Bomi is one of Liberia's 15 counties. Tubmanburg is the county's capital. The county has a population of approximately 48570 and is divided into four districts:

  • Dewoin District
  • Klay District
  • Mecca District
  • Senjeh District

Bomi has an area of 755 sq. miles and a density of 36.6 persons per sq. mile.[1]
