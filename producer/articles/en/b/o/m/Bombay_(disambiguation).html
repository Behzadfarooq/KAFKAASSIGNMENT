Bombay (disambiguation)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Bombay might refer to:

  • Places
    • Bombay is the former name of the Indian city of Mumbai.
    • Bombay State is the name of a former state in India, partitioned in 1960 into Gujarat and Maharashtra. It was previously a province known as Bombay Presidency in British India.
    • Isle of Bombay is the name of one the Seven islands of Bombay that was merged to create city of Bombay.
    • Bombay, New York is the name of a town in Franklin County, New York in the United States.
    • The Bombay Hills, New Zealand mark the traditional southern boundary of Auckland, New Zealand. There is a small village in them also named Bombay.
  • Film and television
    • Bombay (film) is a 1995 Tamil film by Mani Ratnam.
    • Bombay Dreams is a musical produced by Andrew Lloyd Webber, composed by A. R. Rahman.
    • Gordon Bombay is the head coach and protagonist of the Mighty Ducks film trilogy.
    • Bombay Boys is a 1998 film by Kaizad Gustad
    • Dr. Bombay is a character in the television series Bewitched
  • Food and drink
    • Bombay mix is a traditional Indian snack.
    • Bombay Gin and Bombay Sapphire are widely-distributed brands of gin.
  • Others
    • Bombay Company (and Bombay Outlet) is a chain of shops selling household goods and furniture
    • The Bristol Bombay was a British bomber aircraft named after the Indian city.
    • The Bombay (cat) is a breed of domestic cat
    • The Bombay blood group is a rare blood type except in parts of India where a frequency of 1 in 7,600 has been observed.
  • Possible confusions
    • A bomb bay is the area of a bomber aircraft where the bombs are stored.
