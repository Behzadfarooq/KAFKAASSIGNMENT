Bombardier Global 5000
Bombardier BD-700 Global 5000 takes off

The Bombardier Global Express 5000 private jet is a slightly shortened version of its bigger brother, the Global Express XRS. It can fly close to 5000 nautical miles without a stop at a speed of Mach 0.85, making it one of the fastest long range jets available today. Typical configuration features 13 passenger seats including fully berthable seats and an aft lounge/bedroom. It weighs in at 89,700 lbs (typical configuration including optional SB. It is manufactured by Bombardier in Toronto, Canada, and flown "green" to Montreal for final completion.
