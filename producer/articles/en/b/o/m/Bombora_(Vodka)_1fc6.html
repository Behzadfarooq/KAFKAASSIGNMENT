Bombora Vodka is premium brand of vodka. It is distilled in the Barossa Valley using an assortment of premium Australian grapes. The name, Bombora, is taken from the Aboriginal word for "reef", which fits the spirit and imagery that make up the Bombora identity. Premiered in Kansas City, Missouri in September of 2006 and in St. Louis, Missouri in October of 2006, Bombora Vodka has widened its distribution to the states of Colorado, Illinois, Florida, California and Kansas.

[edit] Firsts

Bombora Vodka is the first "Premium Vodka" to be distilled from grapes in the world[attribution needed]. It is also the first "Premium Vodka" distilled from grapes to be imported into the United States[attribution needed].
