Bomshel is an American female country music duo, consisting of lead singer Buffy "Buf" Lawson and fiddle player Kristy O.

The duo got its start at Tootsie's Orchid Lounge in Nashville, Tennessee. They would set up on Tuesday nights to play their songs. Kristy got the idea for forming the duo after attending a Fan Fair in 2004.[1]

The duo's song "Bomshel Stomp" was #6 on Club Connection's list of Top Country Dance Hits of 2005.[2] Due to the song's success in dance clubs, "Bomshel Stomp" has been released as the duo's third single.
