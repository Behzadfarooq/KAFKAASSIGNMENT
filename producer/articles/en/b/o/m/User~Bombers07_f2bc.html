BRAZOS VALLEY BOMBERS

The Brazos Valley Bombers are the newest team to join the Texas Collegiate League (TCL). The Bombers are a summer collegiate baseball team that help NCAA baseball players gain knowledge and experience by living like a minor league player. The Bombers come from different schools all over the nation to live with host families and gain extra exposure in front of major league scouts.

The Bombers official logo, Texas with a Bomber flying through it, was chosen because it represents the state of Texas as well as the team name. The official team colors are navy blue and Vegas Gold. The Bombers did not want to use traditional baseball colors.

[edit] Entertainment at the Game

While this is a team within a summer collegiate league, the Bombers will be hosting more entertainment than most summer leagues. The game-day entertainment will be comparible to a minor league team. There will be live bands before several games, FIREWORKS after 7 of the 24 home games, along with t-shirts tosses, mascot races, a Kids Zone, Beer Deck and much more.

The Bomber want to bring summer fun to the Brazos Valley.

[edit] 2007 Roster

The 2007 Roster will include players from Texas A&M University, Rice University, University of Houston, Notre Dame University, Duke University, Long Beach State,

The Bomebers are the 10th team to enter into the TCL and will play against teams in Coppel, Wichita Falls, Weatherford, Duncanville, Denton, Euless, McKinney and Mineral Wells.

[edit] Travis Field

The Bombers will play at legendary Travis Field. Travis has hosted several teams over the last 60 years, including the semi-professional Bryan Bombers team in the 40's and 50's. Since then, the field has seen a lot of high school and men's league games. Travis is located right off of Carson Drive and Bomber Lane in Bryan, Texas.
