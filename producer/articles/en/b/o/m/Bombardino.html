Commonly used ingredients:

  • 1 part Advocaat (or eggnog)
  • 1 part Black coffee


Bombardino is a drink popular in Italy during the Winter, especially in the skiing resorts. It is made by mixing 1/3 Advocaat or eggnog, 1/3 Whiskey, and 1/3 black coffee. Its name is derived from the warm feeling experienced by the drinker, due to its temperature (approximately 30Â°) and high alcohol content.
