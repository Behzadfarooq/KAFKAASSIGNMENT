Bombon, Camarines Sur

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Bombon is a 5th class municipality in the province of Camarines Sur, Philippines. According to the 2000 census, it has a population of 12,843 people in 2,335 households.

[edit] Barangays

Bombon is politically subdivided into 8 barangays.

  • San Antonio
  • San Francisco
  • San Isidro (Pob.)
  • San Jose (Pob.)
  • Pagao (San Juan)
  • San Roque (Pob.)
  • Siembre
  • Santo Domingo
