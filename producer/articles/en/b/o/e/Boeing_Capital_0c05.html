Boeing Capital, is a subsidiary of The Boeing Company, which provides asset based leasing and lending services. Boeing Capital, is made up of 2 divisions. These divisions are Aircraft Financial Services and The Space & Defense Financial Services

[edit] History

Boeing Capital was Founded in 1968 as McDonnell-Douglas Finance, but this name was changed to Boeing Capital in 1997, when Boeing bought the McDonnell-Douglas Company.
