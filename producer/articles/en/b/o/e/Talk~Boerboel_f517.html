THE STATUS OF THE BOERBOEL PEDIGREE

It has come to my notice that there is a editor namely Psenffner who is repeatedly vandalising the entry for the South African Boerboel, with miss information, concerning the Boerboels pedigree status. As the breed stands today the only internationally recognised pedigree the dog has, is issued solely under the auspices of the South African Boerboel Breeders association (SABT/SABBA) the historical Boerboel association of South Africa (HBSA) and the elite Boerboel association (EBBSA) all three which are South African organisations recognised international amongst all Boerboel owners and breeders.

This misinformation has been brought to the attention of the above organisations, pending appropriate action.

Kind regards
