Boerne Independent School District

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Boerne Independent School District is a public school district based in Boerne, Texas (USA).

Located in Kendall County, portions of the district extend into Bexar and Comal counties. In addition to Boerne, the communities of Fair Oaks Ranch and Scenic Oaks also lie within the district.

[edit] Awards and honors

Boerne High School has been listed in national rankings as one of the most successful schools in the nation when it comes to student participation in the College Board's Advanced Placement Program. Over 700 AP exams were administered to BHS students in the last year for which statistics have been made available, at a school with less than 2,000 students.

For the 2006-2007 school year, the Boerne ISD Teacher of the Year is Paul Yoder, who works at the high school. The year before, the honor went to Rhonda Pratka of Fabra Elementary School.
