Boevange-sur-Attert (Luxembourgish: Béiwen-Atert, German: Böwingen) is a commune and small town in eastern Luxembourg, in the canton of Mersch. It is situated on the river Attert.

As of 2005, the town of Boevange-sur-Attert, which lies in the north of the commune, has a population of 554. Other towns within the commune include Brouch, Buschdorf, and Grevenknapp.
