Bojan Marović (Montenegrin: Бојан Маровић) (born May 21, 1984 in Titograd, Montenegro, SFRY) is a Montenegrin singer and actor. He is from and currently resides in Podgorica.

[edit] Biography

Bojan was born on May 21, 1984 in Titograd (now Podgorica). On his 18th birthday, his mother, Sanja, gave him his song "Vise te nema", which won him 3rd place in the Sunčane Skale festival. He has a younger brother, Boris, who is about 11 years old. Boris is Bojan's inspiration, according to an interview.
