"Bojangles" is the first single of rapper Pitbull's 2006 album El Mariel. It was produced by Lil Jon. The remix features Lil Jon and the Ying Yang Twins. "Bojangles" features a controversial video featuring the Ying Yang Twins and Lil Jon. The meaning of Bojangles is a female with a big chest, nice legs, and nice arms; hence when one of the Ying Yang Twins said a Bojangles is a girl with breast, legs and wings. This is not to be confused with the restaurant. Bojangles was also the nickname of Bill Robinson, a famous African-American dancer/entertainer in the 1920's.
