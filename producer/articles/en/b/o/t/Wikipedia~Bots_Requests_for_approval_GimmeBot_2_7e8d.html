[edit] GimmeBot

tasks • contribs • count • logs • page moves • block user • block log • flag log • flag bot

Operator: Gimmetrow

Automatic or Manually Assisted: supervised, manually assisted

Programming Language(s): pyWikipedia

Function Summary: Converting FA and other review-related templates to {{ArticleHistory}}

Edit period(s) (e.g. Continuous, daily, one time run): Batches of 25-50 pages, a couple times a day

Edit rate requested: 3 edits per minute

Already has a bot flag (Y/N): yes

Function Details:

The bot will be converting various review-related talk-page templates to the new {{ArticleHistory}}, including {{featured}}, {{facfailed}}, {{farcfailed}}, {{formerFA}}, and {{formerFA2}}. It also includes info from {{peerreview}}, {{oldpeerreview}}, {{GA}}, and some others when encountered. Initial priority are the 850 or so pages left with the featured template. Enough oddities happen that this is manually supervised. Pages will be handled in groups of 25-50, up to about 100 pages per day. See contributions for some trial runs. Gimmetrow 14:01, 14 February 2007 (UTC)
