Bottoms of Barrels is the sophomore album by Omaha, Nebraska's Tilly and the Wall. It was released on 2006-05-23 on Team Love Records.

This album is the 9th release of Team Love Records.

Its Australian release is set somewhere in May, 2007 by Dew Process.

  • Clark Baechle: electronic beats (tracks 4, 7, 9)

  • Dan McCarthy: accordion (track 3)
  • Jesse McKelvey: drums (track 2)
  • A.J. Mogis: organ (track 2)
