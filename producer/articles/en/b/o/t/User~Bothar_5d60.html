[edit] Contributions

[edit] Approach

I initially just used Wikipedia for quick referencing, but then got fascinated by the its concept of editing. Since then I've added a few things, but I don't think I'll ever be one of the advanced (or even intermediate) editing users. Still, when I see something that needs fixing, I do my part to improve Wikipedia. I would like to contribute more photos South African related articles, and started slowly with this mini project.

[edit] I started the following pages

  • Airports Company South Africa because my company tendered for work there.
  • The Category "Words of Afrikaans origin"
  • Amphitheatre, Drakensberg where I once had a marvelious holiday.
  • Bunny Chow (Film) a small start, but the film haven't been released yet.
  • Casper de Vries, my favourite Afrikaans comedian.
  • Wally Hayward, one of my childhood heroes when I was into road running (this article existed, but I had to move it from an obscure location and redo it from a few sentences to what it is now)
  • Jukskei, an Afrikaner folk game which I can see by just looking out of my office window.
