Bottomley is a surname, originating in West Yorkshire, and may refer to:

  • Gordon Bottomley
  • Henry Bottomley
  • Horatio Bottomley, British fraudster
  • James Bottomley (Usenet innovator)
  • Sir James Bottomley, KCMG, British diplomat
  • Jim Bottomley, baseball player
  • John Bottomley, a Canadian singer-songwriter
  • Norman Bottomley, senior Royal Air Force commander
  • Peter Bottomley, a British Conservative politician
  • Richard Bottomley
  • Virginia Bottomley
