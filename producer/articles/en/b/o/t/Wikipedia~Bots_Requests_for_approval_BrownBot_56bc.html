[edit] BrownBot

Operator: Cbrown1023

Automatic or Manually Assisted: Automatic, but normally supervised

Programming Language(s): AutoWikiBrowser

Function Summary: AWB-editor: newsletter distrubtion and other AWB tasks

Edit period(s) (e.g. Continuous, daily, one time run): Continuous: Always at then end of the month (for newsletter distribution) but may also be used for other AWB tasks.

Edit rate requested: 10 edits per minute

Already has a bot flag (Y/N):

Function Details: The bot primarily uses AutoWikiBrowser to do a number of tedious tasks at a faster rate than a human editor. The bot distributes the montly WikiProject Films newsletter at the end of each month. Other than that, the bot edits sporadically to substitute templates that need to be substituted, adds WikiProject Banners to talk pages, and removes the AfD notice/adds an {{oldafdfull}} tag for mass-nominations that close as Keep or No consensus. Distribute notice of Current Wikipedia:Cinema Collaboration of the Week to voters
