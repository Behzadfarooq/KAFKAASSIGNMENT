Boticas (pron. IPA: [bu'tikɐʃ]) is a municipality in Portugal with a total area of 322.0 km² and a total population of 6,116 inhabitants.

The municipality is composed of 16 parishes, and is located in the district of Vila Real.

The present Mayor is Fernando Pereira Campos, elected by the Social Democratic Party.

The municipal holiday is November 6.
