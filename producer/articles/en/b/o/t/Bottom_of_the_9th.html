Rating(s) ESRB: Everyone (E)

Bottom of the 9th is a baseball game by Konami for the Sega Saturn, PlayStation and the Nintendo 64. Featuring Chipper Jones hitting 732 foot bombs!! It is a heavily updated version of Konami's 1989 arcade game Bottom of the Ninth [1].

Was originally released for the PLAYSTATION.

Brian L. Hunter is by far the best player in this Konami title.
