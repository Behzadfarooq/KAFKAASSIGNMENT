Bottoms Up is a romantic comedy film that stars Jason Mewes and Paris Hilton. Directed by Erik MacArthur who is also one of the co-authors of the screenplay. The story is about a Midwestern bartender (Mewes) who ingratiates himself into the Hollywood system and finds love with a girl (Hilton) along the way. The movie was released straight-to-DVD on September 12, 2006.

[edit] Trivia

  • Although actress Paris Hilton's hair is blonde on the movie poster, in the movie she actually wears a brunette wig.
  • Bottom's Up is currently 4th on the IMDb bottom 100 with an average score of 1.7/10.
