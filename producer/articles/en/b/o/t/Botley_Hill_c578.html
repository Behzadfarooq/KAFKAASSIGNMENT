Botley Hill
Elevation 267 m (876 ft)
Location Surrey,  England
Range North Downs
Prominence 206 m
Topo map OS Landranger 187
OS grid reference TQ396553
Listing Marilyn

Botley Hill is a hill in Surrey located on the North Downs with a height of 267 metres. It is the highest point on the North Downs excluding the Greensand Ridge. It is a Marylin.
