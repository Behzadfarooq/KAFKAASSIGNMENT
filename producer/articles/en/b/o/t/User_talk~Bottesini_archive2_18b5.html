[edit] Piping links

I've looked over your past edits, and you seem to be replacing several valid links with piped links. You may not realize that links like [[talk]]ing work the same exact way as [[talk|talking]], and are actually preferred. Both will produce a link with the text "talking" linking to the page "talk" (talking). Note that this works for any suffix, not only -ing. —Mets501talk 22:16, 5 May 2006 (UTC)
