Botha is a surname, especially common among people from South Africa. People named Botha include:

  • Louis Botha (1862-1919), first Prime Minister of South Africa
  • Pieter Willem Botha (1916-2006), also known as P.W., South African prime minister from 1978 to 1984 and state president from 1984 to 1989
  • Roelof Frederik "Pik" Botha (b. 1932), South African foreign minister in the later years of Apartheid
  • Roelof Botha, former Chief Financial Officer of Paypal
  • Luki Botha (born 1930), South African Formula One driver
  • Hendrik Egnatius "Naas" Botha (born 1958), South African rugby player
  • Francois Botha (born 1968), South African boxer and kickboxer.
  • Andre Botha (born 1975) is a Johannesburg-born Irish cricketer.
  • John Philip "Bakkies" Botha (born 1979), South African rugby player
  • Johan Botha (born 1982), South African cricketer
  • Johan Botha (Opera)
