"Bottle Living" is a song by Depeche Mode vocalist David Gahan and is the third single from his 2003 debut album, Paper Monsters. It was released on 27 October 2003 in UK and on 4 November in the USA. This is the last single from the album. It's a double A-side, the other song being "Hold On". The rhythm of this song is rockier, in tone with the entire albume, and is in line with the first single, Dirty Sticky Floors. Still, this single has a B-side song, in fact a remix of another track from Paper Monsters: "Hidden houses". This track can be found on the limited edition Maxi-CD, launched in the UK.
