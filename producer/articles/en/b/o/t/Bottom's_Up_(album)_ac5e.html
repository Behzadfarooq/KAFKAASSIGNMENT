Bottom's Up is Obie Trice's 3rd studio album, due out late 2007. Obie stated that his fans will no longer have to wait as long as they did in-between Cheers and Second Round's On Me. He said to expect this one as early as 2007.[citation needed]


[edit] Confirmed Tracks

  • "Shady Baby" (featuring Eminem) (Produced by Eminem)
  • "Damn" (featuring Tony Yayo, Lloyd Banks & Nate Dogg) (Produced by Eminem)
  • "Y'all Haters" (featuring Stat Quo) (Produced by Dr. Dre)
  • "Bottom's Up" (featuring Akon) (Produced by Akon)
  • "Could Be" (featuring D12) (Produced by Eminem)
  • "Shady's Greatest" (featuring D12, 50 Cent, Stat Quo, Cashis & Bobby Creekwater)
  • "Show Me Love" (featuring Carl Thomas)
