This page lists the users who are authorized to approve or deny bots requests for approval. (Hence the name, Bot approvals group.) However, only bureaucrats are technically capable of flagging bots. There is currently no formal process to add new members to this group or to remove existing members, and requests for addition and removals have been handled case-by-case. A new process is being discussed on this page's talk page.
