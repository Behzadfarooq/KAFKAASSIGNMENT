The Botswana national football team, nicknamed The Zebras, is the national team of Botswana and is controlled by the Botswana Football Association. They have never qualified for the finals of the World Cup nor the African Nations Cup.

[edit] World Cup record

  • 1930 to 1990 - Did not enter
  • 1994 - Did not qualify
  • 1998 - Did not enter
  • 2002 to 2006 - Did not qualify

[edit] African Nations Cup record

  • 1957 to 1992 - Did not enter
  • 1994 to 2006 - Did not qualify
