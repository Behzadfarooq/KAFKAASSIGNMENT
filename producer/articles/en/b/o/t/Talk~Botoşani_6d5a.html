[edit] Eminescu and Botosani

As far as I remember from Romanian Literature manual from highschool, Eminescu was born in Botosani and grew up in Ipotesti.

[edit] Etymology

Another possible origin of the name is that of a Mongol chief, Batu Khan, grandson of Genghis Khan, who occupied this region in the 13th century.

That's a very unlikely etymology. I am not aware of any other Mongol placenames anywhere in Romania and the Romanian ending -ani makes it even more unlikely. If anyone finds a reliable source mentioning it (see Wikipedia:Attribution), it can be readded. bogdan 15:58, 1 March 2007 (UTC)
