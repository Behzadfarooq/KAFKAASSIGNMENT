Bottle toke

A Bottle Toke, commonly referred to as a "B.T.","Bot", or simply "Goodness" is a method of smoking Hashish, Hash oil and Tees by which a small hole is made near the base of a beer or plastic bottle large enough to allow a cigarette access without touching the bottle. Next a small amount of Hash or oil is placed on the cherry of the lit cigarette (unfortunate circumstances have led to the development of the "dusty bottle", whereby when users are lacking cigarettes they used buts from the ashtray to brew their "bots", making the extra dust from a stale cigarette a little more than spicy). Then the lit end of the 'ciggy' is then placed inside the bottle, allowing it to fill with smoke. Once all the hash or oil is burnt off, the cigarette is removed, the cap of the bottle is removed, and the smoke is inhaled through the mouth of the bottle.

Another method is making "finger hash" or "kief tees", out of marijuana crystals. Marijuana is put into a coffee grinder, and grinded up. Then you collect the THC(crystals) from the coffee grinder, and make "B.T.'s" out of the crystal, or alternatively you can smoke it in a bong, pipe or a rolled joint.

This method is used almost exclusively in Ontario (Southern Ontario, Burlington)and other English-speaking areas of Canada. BTs have also spread to the island of Bermuda through a man known locally as 'Coco'.

In times of desperate need, coffee grinders can be busted without any marijuana present. This is known as the "empty bust", or "phantom bust".
