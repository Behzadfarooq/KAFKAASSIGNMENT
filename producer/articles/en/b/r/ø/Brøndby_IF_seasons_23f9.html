Brøndby IF seasons

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

This is a list of seasons played by Danish football club Brøndby IF from the 1981-82 season, when Brøndby IF entered the top-flight Danish football championship, to the present day. It details the club's achievements in major competitions, in both Danish football and European tournaments.
