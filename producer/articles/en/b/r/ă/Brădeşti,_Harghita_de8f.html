Brădeşti (Hungarian: Fenyéd) is a commune in Harghita County, Romania. The commune consists of 2 villages:

  • Brădeşti/Fenyéd
  • Târnoviţa/Küküllőkeményfalva

Satu Mare/Máréfalva is now an independent commune.

[edit] Demographics

The commune has an absolute Székely (Hungarian) majority. According to the 2002 census it has a population of 3,561; of which 97.92% or 3,487 are Hungarian.
