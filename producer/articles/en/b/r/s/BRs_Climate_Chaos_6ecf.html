BRs Climate Chaos is made by "The Super Flash Bros." and sponsored by Armour Games, and the music is by "Dustball". It stands for Blue Rabbit's Climate Chaos. You can play it at the Ugoplayer website (Previously known as Flashplayer). This game was posted on November 14, 2006. You should always try the tutorial first if you haven't played yet. Graphics are quite good in this game.

[edit] Plot

The storyline is about a Blue Rabbit and his friend visiting and island (hotel island). There are actually four islands in the game by taking a tour/boat/walk and you have to collect an element disk for each one and put it in a statue to stop the chaotic weather. The people don't like the weather, so you decide to do something about it by finding all four disks. The disks are the snow, sun, rain, and thunder. There are four islands in the game also, the snow, sun, rain, and thunder. The statue where you put the disks in is located on the shrine island.

[edit] How to get the disks

Spoiler warning: Plot and/or ending details follow.

Snow Disk-Get it on the thunder island when you enter the native island. Sun Disk-Get it by taking the green strange native scale from the native woman and use it to unlock the treasure chest next to the reindeer on the spring island. Take the antique watch and give it to the guy in the hotel room. He will give you the sun disk. Thunder Disk-After taking a picture of the giant shell on the snow island with your camera, show it to the shell contest manager. He will give you a free coupon for a souvenir in the gift shop. Buy they map and take the bucket and shovel from the dancing seal in the front of the hotel. Start digging around on the bottom left side corner close to the shells of the spring island by rapidly clicking the bucket/shovel and walking around. You should find the thunder disk, and the map is just for indicating where to dig although it is not clear. You will not need to trade the map in again for another item, although it looks as if it disappears. Rain Disk-Take a picture of the hotel manager (wolf-looks like native people) and show it to the native chief when you get to the rain island. He will then give you the rain disk. (The guy you meet in the beginning).

[edit] Hints to get disks

Snow-A guy at the boat offers you a tour after you go in to the hotel It's obvious to get it, you can't not get it unless you barely played. Sun-The native woman gives you a free strange native key. The guy says you need a key to unlock the treasure chest on the thunder island. If you get the antique clock, the guy in the hotel says he likes money and time. Thunder-A guy on the sun island (hotel) that island+bucket/shovel+map=treasure. The treasure map that you get looks like the shape of the thunder island, and the sun marking points out the treasure. Rain-The native chief says his son went to another island, and he never came back. In the hotel lobby there is a picture clue. Just use the same strategy for the gaint shell for this one.

[edit] The End

In points in the game you can save whenever you want. Put all the disks in the statue on the snow island and you will win the game. After the credits will be shown.
