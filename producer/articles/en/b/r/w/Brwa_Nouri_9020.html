Brwa Hekmat Nouri (born January 23, 1987 in Urmia, Iran) is an Iranian-born Swedish footballer, a promising midfielder who won JSM (Swedish Junior tournament) with AIK in 2004. He was given a chance to play senior football in the three last games of 2005 when promotion was already secured.

Nouri has been a regular at junior level for Sweden, but has not been developing as expected during the last year or two. Was loaned to Väsby United under the 2007 season to get more experience and get vital senior team football under former AIK youth team coach Mikael Stahre, now manager at Väsby. Rumours have been circulation the reasons behind him being loaned out (Åtvidabergs FF in 2006) is bad discipline and unprofessional behaviour etc, although this haven't been officially confirmed from either club or player.
