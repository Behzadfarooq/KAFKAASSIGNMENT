Brętowo

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Brętowo is one of the quarters of the city of Gdańsk, Poland. It includes 2 housing estates Niedźwiednik and Matemblewo that are located inside Oliwa forests. The old (now-closed) railway track from Wrzeszcz to Kartuzy goes through the quarter with few destroyed railway bridges. The bridges are used by alpinists to train.
