[edit] Plot

Spoiler warning: Plot and/or ending details follow.

Greg loses his job and is forced to take a job playing the mascot at Chuck E. Cheese's, a popular children's pizza parlor. This is after he discovers that his house is filled with toxic mold and must be torn down due to termites. Greg ends up staying with Jimmy after swallowing his own pride.

[edit] Notes

  • Season Five Finale
  • Suppose to be the series finale since chances didn't look good for the show to be renewed for a sixth season, but in late 2005 in a surprising move CBS picked up the show for season six.
  • This episode begins with the surprise revelation that all of a sudden, Greg has no job. This was not explained until the following episode, The Radford Reshuffle (which was to be aired before this episode, but was pre-empted by a press conference and was aired the following September)
