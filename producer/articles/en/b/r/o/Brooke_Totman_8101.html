Brooke Totman (born December 27, 1972) is an American comic actress. Totman is most notable for her membership in the recurring cast of comedians on sketch comedy series MADtv.

[edit] Biography

Brooke Totman was born on December 27, 1972 in Eugene, Oregon. She received a scholarship in 1991 from The America's Junior Miss. Totman attended the Groundlings school, where she performed in such shows as "Lake Waterford Crystal Filter Water Show."

[edit] Career

[edit] MADtv

Totman joined the cast of MADtv in 1999, as a featured performer, for the fifth season. Totman was noted for her uncanny celebrity impressions of Ann-Margaret and Jennifer Love Hewitt. Totman's tenure on MADtv was limited to only one season. At the end of Season Five, in 2000, her contract was not renewed.

[edit] Other entertainment projects

After her tenure on MADtv, Totman made appearances on several television shows. She appeared in King of Queens, (2002), Less Than Perfect (2003) and Judging Amy (2003)
