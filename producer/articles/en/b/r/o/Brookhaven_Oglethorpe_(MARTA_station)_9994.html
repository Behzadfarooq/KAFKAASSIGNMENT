Brookhaven/Oglethorpe is a station in the MARTA rail system. It is an above-ground station that provides access to the Brookhaven area and is a mile south of Oglethorpe University.

[edit] Parking

Brookhaven/Oglethorpe has 1252 daily and long term parking spaces available for MARTA users which are located in paved parking lots. Daily parking (less than 24 hours) is free; long term parking is charged at US$4 per day, including the first day.
