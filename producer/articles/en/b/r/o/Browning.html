The term "browning" (to make brown) has a number of applications.

Several famous people were/are called Browning:

  • Christopher Browning, American historian focusing on the Holocaust
  • Dolly Kyle Browning, Claims to be a former mistress of US President Bill Clinton
  • Elizabeth Barrett Browning, English poetess
  • Emily Browning, Australian actress
  • Sir Frederick Arthur Montague Browning, English World War II lieutenant general
  • John Browning, American firearms inventor
  • Jonathan Browning, the American pioneer and firearms inventor (father of John Browning)
  • Kurt Browning, Canadian skater
  • Martin Browning, Professor of economics
  • Miles Rutherford Browning, American World War II rear admiral
  • Robert Browning, English poet
  • Tod Browning, horror movie pioneer
  • Val A. Browning, American firearms inventor and industrialist (son of John Browning)
  • Phil Browning, Fluvanna Flying Flucos Head Football Coach and Athletic Director

Locations:

  • Browning, Illinois
  • Browning, Montana
  • Browning, Wisconsin
  • Browning Hill, in Brown County, Indiana; sometimes called Browning Mountain

Definitions:

  • The chemical process of browning food
  • A small handgun (Europe)
  • A process to darken the metal on firearms.

Companies:
