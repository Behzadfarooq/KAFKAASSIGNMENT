The Browning BLR is a lever action hunting rifle. It is manufactured by Browning, and comes in many different variations: Chambered from 22-250 Rem to 325WSM to 450 Marlin, barrel length from 20"-24", weighing from 6lb 8oz, to 7lb 12oz. The image at right shows a Browning lever action BLR Lightweight '81, chambered for the .308 Win, with the addition of a Leupold 2-7x33 scope, mounted directly onto the receiver.

A Browning BLR .308, with the addition of a scope

[edit] Uses

The BLR can be used for anything from plinking to hunting to security. Since the BLR can be chambered for so many different cartridges, it has very many uses. the .243 Win can be used for small game such as coyotes, the .308 is very sutable for deer or elk hunting.
