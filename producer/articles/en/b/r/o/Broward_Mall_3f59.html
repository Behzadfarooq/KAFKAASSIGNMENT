Broward Mall is a one-level 1.0 million sf shopping mall in Plantation, Florida, a suburb of Fort Lauderdale The mall has recently completed a multi-million-dollar renovation, and is operated and co-owned by the Mills Corporation. The center features 125 retail and service shops, four department stores, and a variety of casual dining venues.
