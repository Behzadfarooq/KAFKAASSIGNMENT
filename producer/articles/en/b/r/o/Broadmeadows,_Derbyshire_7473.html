Broadmeadows, Derbyshire

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Broadmeadows is a newly built housing estate in the town of South Normanton, Derbyshire. The estate is situated in the Bolsover district and is not far from the town of Alfreton. The housing estate is also in close proximity to the town and cities of Chesterfield, Derby, Sheffield, Mansfield and Nottingham.
