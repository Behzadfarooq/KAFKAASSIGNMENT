Broken Saints is an action-adventure video game developed by Gnosis Games, based on the Broken Saints animation. The game is being developed in two iterations; one for all current generation consoles Xbox 360, Playstation 3, and the Wii[1], and another for handhelds. Both versions will utilize the artistic style found in the animated comics.
