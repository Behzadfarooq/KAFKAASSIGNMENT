Brooke Hunter (born March 14, 1970 in Los Angeles, California) is an American pornographic actress and director. She married Don Hollywood (also known as Ron Miller) on January 15, 2000. Before Brooke became a porn star she worked as an accountant.[1]

With her manager and husband Don Hollywood, who also operates the Erotica L.A. conventions, she works on movies for Wildlife Productions. The first series from Wildfire is Brooke Hunter's Sexual Antics.[2] Hunter also hosts a television series called Dirty Newcomers, produced by Wildfire.

Currently Hunter and Hollywood host a show on http://www.KSEXradio.com titled "Tits and Asshole" airing every Monday night 9PM (PST).

Her contract with Wildfire is not exclusive, however, as she also works for Vivid Entertainment, Wicked Pictures and VCA Pictures.
