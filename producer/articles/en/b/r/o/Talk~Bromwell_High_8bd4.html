[edit] Inactive character

Does anyone know anything about the woman on the far right (glasses, hair in a bun)? As far as I know, she hasn't said a word in the entire series, just wondering if anyone may have info on her?

Nope, no idea who she is. My guess is she was a planned character who made it past the design stage and was then removed from the show, so they left her in the publicity stills anyway. She could also be there just to make up numbers. Anyway, unless she's in the background of the staff room or something like that at any point, she's not really canon so we can ignore her. [edit] dunno who finds this show funny!

I think the show is not funny in the least. It tries hard to be funny but frankly is pathetic. Maybe one reason why I change the channel when it is on is because I watch it on the Comedy Network in North America and so the show is sandwiched between Family Guy and South Park. If only Bromwell High was even 1% as funny as these two other shows it might be worth watching!
