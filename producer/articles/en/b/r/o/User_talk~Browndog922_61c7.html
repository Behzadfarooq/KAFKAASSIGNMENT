[edit] License tagging for Image:MaryElizabethWinsteadasWendyChristensen.jpg

Thanks for uploading Image:MaryElizabethWinsteadasWendyChristensen.jpg. Wikipedia gets thousands of images uploaded every day, and in order to verify that the images can be legally used on Wikipedia, the source and copyright status must be indicated. Images need to have an image tag applied to the image description page indicating the copyright status of the image. This uniform and easy-to-understand method of indicating the license status allows potential re-users of the images to know what they are allowed to do with the images.

For more information on using images, see the following pages:

  • Wikipedia:Image use policy
  • Wikipedia:Image copyright tags

[edit] Insertion of uncited, unverified Lost material

Please do not insert such things as Kate Austen as the flashback for the first episode of Season 3. This has happened with other people on this same subject. There's no official source that verifies this yet, so your work WILL be reverted. -- PKtm 04:58, 3 September 2006 (UTC)

Please refrain from undoing other people's edits repeatedly. If you continue, you may be blocked from editing Wikipedia under the three-revert rule, which states that nobody may revert a single page more than three times in 24 hours. (Note: this also means editing the page to reinsert an old edit. If the effect of your actions is to revert back, it qualifies as a revert.) Thank you. -- PKtm 05:27, 3 September 2006 (UTC)


This message is on the talk page, but maybe you didn't see it so I'll bring it to you:

This page has a policy concerning future episodes, limiting their descriptions to published information from official sources. The policy was voted on from November 17 - November 24, 2005. For more information on the poll voting, see the archived poll section. The policy states:
  • Information on this page will be limited to official broadcasts, information taken from the ABC website, official LOST websites, official episode descriptions, and interviews with cast/producers/writers/directors.
  • Information extrapolated from commercials or previews, or spoiler websites will NOT be included on this page. This includes unverified episode titles, plot elements or flashback information.

If you continue to violate this policy you will be blocked from editing. -- Ned Scott 05:29, 3 September 2006 (UTC)
