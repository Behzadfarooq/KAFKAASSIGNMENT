[edit] "Biased" article topics

You may want to read through Wikipedia:Neutral point of view. In a nutshell, though, Wikipedia presents all sides of a dispute without asserting that any one is (in)correct. So for articles like liberal media, it would be presented that many right-wingers feel the media has a liberal bias, without actually suggesting that this is the correct or incorrect view. Andre (talk) 19:09, Dec 30, 2004 (UTC)
