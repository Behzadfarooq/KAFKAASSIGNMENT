Brothers is an unincorporated community in Deschutes County, Oregon, United States on U.S. Route 20. Vast tracts of uninhabited land covered with sagebrush surround Brothers, which are often used for model and high power rocket launches. [1] It is part of the Bend, Oregon Metropolitan Statistical Area. As of the 1990 census, Brothers had a population of 41 people.
