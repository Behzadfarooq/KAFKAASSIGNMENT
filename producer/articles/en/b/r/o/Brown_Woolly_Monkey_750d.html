The Brown Woolly Monkey or Common Woolly Monkey, Lagothrix lagotricha, is a woolly monkey from Colombia, Ecuador, Peru and Brazil. It lives in groups of 2 to 70 individuals, usually splitting the group into smaller subgroups while in activity.
