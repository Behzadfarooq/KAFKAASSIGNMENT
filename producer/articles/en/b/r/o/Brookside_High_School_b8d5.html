Brookside High School

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Brookside High School is a public high school located at 1812 Harris Road in Sheffield, Ohio, 20 miles west of Cleveland, Ohio. It is part of Sheffield-Sheffield Lake City Schools.

The school colors are red and black. The school mascot is the Cardinal. The school is a member of the Patriot Athletic Conference (PAC-10).

[edit] School Songs

[edit] Alma Mater

Hail unto our High School,
A symbol of the right
To her we owe our thanks
For those days both fair and bright.
May this dear school
In all our memories ever lie
That we may cherish ever,
Our dear old Brookside High.

[edit] Fight Song

(Tune: Ohio State's "Fight the Team Across the Field")
Fight the team across the field,
Show them that Brookside's here!
Send the earth reverberating
With a mighty cheer, RAH! RAH! RAH!
Hit them hard and see how they fall,
Never let that team get the ball,
Hail! Hail! The gang's all here,
And we'll beat that old _____ High!
