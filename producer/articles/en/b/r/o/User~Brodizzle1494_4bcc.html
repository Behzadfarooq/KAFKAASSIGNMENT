[edit] Brody Miller

{Brody Miller |- ! Born August 29, 1994 ! Born in Cleveland, Ohio


Brody Miller was bron in Cleveland, Ohio. He moved to Simi Valley, California and now lives in Medford, New Jersey. He is a two sport phenom. He is the starting catcher on the Medford Indian District baseball team. He contains a spectacular .356 batting average. He had led exultantly this team to two district titles back-to-back. He also is a star small forward on his basketball team in which he stars with a 13.2 ppg average. Also so 9.8 rpg average, 6.8 apg average, and 2.0 bpg average. He led this underdog team to an upset win to the #3 team 32-31 in which he dropped a clutch deep jumper and stole a huge pass which he took down with a sick spin move for a layup. Down 31-30 football sensation Brad Rieger put up a huge steal n fast break layup to win with 13.4 left in the game.

He is most known for being the brother of Quinton Miller, star prospect pitcher. Brody says, "'Q' is a great athlete, but I've been telling myself I can take him anyday, ive dunked over him countless times and always beat him in everything we've competed in. Even my mother loves me most." The confident but maybe arrogant comment led Quinton to say, "Im gonna kill that piece of wannabe garbage, ill beam him next time I see him!'

Brody in his most recent tournament had to endure an extreme task of catching 15 innings and led the team to a 2-1 record in the tournament with the game winning double to beat Pinehill to right center. In a recent survey 96% of people say Miller is the most inspiring athlete they've ever seen.

Brody Miller is currently being scouted by Cincinnati Reds scouts.
