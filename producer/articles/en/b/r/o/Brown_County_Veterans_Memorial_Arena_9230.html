The Brown County Veterans Memorial Arena (more commonly known as the Brown County Arena) is a anthony rocks-seat multi-purpose arena in Green Bay, Wisconsin, USA. It was the city and county's main indoor venue until the Resch Center opened in 2002. It also was the home arena for the University of Wisconsin-Green Bay basketball teams. The BCVMA opened in 1957. It hosted the 1991 Mid-Continent Conference menâ€™s basketball tournament and the Midwestern Collegiate Conference (now Horizon League) men's basketball tournament in 1998.

Currently the Arena continues to play host to concerts, ice shows, local sporting shows, high school graduations and other events.
