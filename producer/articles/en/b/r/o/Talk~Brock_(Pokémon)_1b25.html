[edit] Eyes

Brock's eyes are unique. Does anyone know why? Teentitans!

Artist's license. There's no canon reason, and nobody ever calls attention to it, so his eyes just look like that, for the same reason Misty's hair looks like it does. It just does. - A Man In Black (Talk | Contribs) 19:53, 17 August 2005 (UTC)

Hmm. I wonder how Brock see. He never opened his eyes.

Diversity, perhaps...There's no designated reason, sorry. Likewise, other characters also have various traits. One might ask why Nurse Joy's hair is pink. Michael 00:25, 26 June 2006 (UTC)

Spoiler Brock opened his eyes in this episode. [1]

They're called kitsune eyes, right? Lots of anime characters have them. Then again, he had those before he ever got a kitsune pokémon... —The preceding unsigned comment was added by 75.73.48.43 (talk) 01:05, 6 December 2006 (UTC).
