Brotherhood among the Sahaba

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

A system of Brotherhood among the Sahaba was created in the Islamic prophet Muhammad, each Muslim being paired with another one.

[edit] List

A list includes:

[edit] Mecca

  • Umar - Abu Bakr [1].

[edit] Medina

  • Umar - Utban ibn Malik [1]
  • Abu Darda - Salman the Persian [2]
  • Muhammad ibn Maslamah - Abu Ubaydah ibn al-Jarrah [3].
