The Brooklyn Blue Devils are a semi-pro football team located in Brooklyn, NY. The Brooklyn Blue Devils are in the Metro South Division.

[edit] Team History

The Brooklyn Blue Devils was Started by #32 Chester Joseph who was a former Samuel J. Tilden HS player. He wanted to have a team for players that didn't play pro or college football. The team is family based, anyone who has played for them feel as if their part of a family. Beginning their rookie year in 2006 in the NAFL they feel that it will be soon that they will be taken the national trophy in florida home any day.
