Broadcast Film Critics Association Awards 1999

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

5th Broadcast Film Critics Association Awards

2000

Best Picture:
American Beauty

The 5th Critics' Choice Awards, honoring the best filmmaking of 1999, were given in 2000.

[edit] Top 10 Films

(alphabetical)

  1. American Beauty
  3. Being John Malkovich
  4. The Cider House Rules
  5. The Green Mile
  6. The Insider
  7. Magnolia
  8. Man on the Moon
  9. The Sixth Sense
  10. The Talented Mr. Ripley
  11. Three Kings

[edit] Winners

  • Best Picture:
    • American Beauty
  • Best Actor:
    • Russell Crowe - The Insider
  • Best Actress:
    • Hilary Swank - Boys Don't Cry
  • Best Supporting Actor:
    • Michael Clarke Duncan - The Green Mile
  • Best Supporting Actress:
    • Angelina Jolie - Girl, Interrupted
  • Best Director:
    • Sam Mendes - American Beauty
  • Best Screenplay - Adapted:
    • The Green Mile - Frank Darabont
  • Best Screenplay - Original:
    • American Beauty - Alan Ball
  • Best Young Performer:
    • Haley Joel Osment - The Sixth Sense
  • Best Animated Film:
    • Toy Story 2
  • Best Family Film:
    • October Sky
  • Best Foreign Language Film:
    • Todo sobre mi madre (All About My Mother), Spain
  • Best Song:
  • "Music of My Heart" - Music of My Heart
  • Best Composer:
    • Gabriel Yared - The Talented Mr. Ripley
