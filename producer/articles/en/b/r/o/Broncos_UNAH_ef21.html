Broncos-UNAH is an Honduran football club based in Choluteca, Choluteca. Currently the club plays in Liga Nacional de Honduras.

Broncos has had many financial problems. One of the clubs most recent problems were the changing of its name and the changing of its board of directors. The team is not playing well as of late and is in serious trouble of descending to Liga de Ascenso de Honduras. The club has had to change it's location numerous times since the stadiums has been to big to get as much players they needed. They've been at Danli, Tegucigalpa and as of now Choluteca.
