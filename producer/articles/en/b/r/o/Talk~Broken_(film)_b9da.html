[edit] Trivia section removal

The sole piece of trivia, "The movie was filmed in black-and-white, except for the "Gave Up" video (which was in color)," mistakenly protrays the "Gave Up" video from Closure as being part of the Broken movie. This was incorrect and in contradiction with the rest of the article. Since there was no factually-accurate trivia, I deleted the entire section. -- Rynne 16:51, 20 November 2006 (UTC)

[edit] YouTube links

This article is one of thousands on Wikipedia that have a link to YouTube in it. Based on the External links policy, most of these should probably be removed. I'm putting this message on the talk page, to request the regular editors take a look at the link and make sure it doesn't violate policy. In short: 1. 99% of the time YouTube should not be used as a source. 2. We must not link to material violating someone's copyright. If you are not sure whether the link on this article should be removed or if you would like to help spread this message, contact us on User talk:J.smith/YouTube Linklist. Give me any non-imdb source that Robert Patrick is the cop? I've seen many arguments that it's actually Richard, and no proof anywhere that it's Robert, apart from speculation because he's dressed as a cop before. Year 0 22:45, 15 January 2007 (UTC)

That's not Robert Patrick as the cop. I showed the movie to a friend who is a huge Robert Patrick fan and she said "No way" when I showed her (and she was hoping it was him.)
