[edit] Userboxes: a note

As you can see, I have a plethora of userboxes on my user page. Many of them were obtained through the categories on Wikipedia’s userbox page, but a number of them were “borrowed” from other users’ pages, with a bit of editing here and there in some cases. Unfortunately, I never kept track of which userboxes I took from which pages, so I can’t properly give credit where it is due. Therefore, I’m taking the easy way out and absolving myself of all culpability:

If you find a userbox on this page that you believe was originally your creation, please do not take offense that I have made use of it on my own user page. The only reason I would add a userbox to my user page is if I feel that it genuinely applies to me, so I hope you take it as a compliment that I have borrowed your work. Should you wish that I remove it from my user page or that some other action be taken, please notify me on my talk page and we will work something out.
