Brown Mountain Power Station, New South Wales

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

You have new messages (last change).

Brown Mountain Power Station is a hydroelectric power station working off Rutherford Creek and Cochran Dam, New South Wales, Australia. Brown Mountain has five turbo generators, with a generating capacity of 4.5MW of electricity.

The power station was completed in 1943. It is currently undergoing an upgrade that will increase the capacity to 4.95MW.
