Brooklyn Faith Stepp (September 22, 1989 was born to Eddie and Amy Stepp in Richmond, Kentucky.

After living with her grandparents Elmer Gene and Laverne Riddell for 5 years after birth, she moved to Waco, Kentucky and attended schools there. When she started attending Clark Moores Middle School in Richmond, Kentucky, she began to become to distant to her parents and pretend to be sick. Her parents were never around most of the time and played in country bars on the weekends. Following, the sixth grade, she began attending a christian academy which did not improve her behavior. She then started attending Oneida Baptist Institute where she was exposed to kids with drug and behavioral problems. When she was attending the school, her parents seperated and her father wanted nothing to do with his child.

[edit] Teenage Life

Her freshman year of high school she attended Estill County High School in Irvine, Kentucky, then she moved and started her sophomore year, at Powell County High School. Ther she met a man named Raymond Caskey who introduced to a life of drugs, partying and eventually many attempts toward suicide. After a bout with cutting herself, her mother checked her into the Charter Ridge Rehab Facility in Lexington, Kentucky. She was treated for depression and substance abuse. When she was discharged from the rehab facility, she and her mother returned to Estill County. After she returned back, she met Joseph Aaron Morrison. The couple started dating on March 9, 2005. She began to become happy and say things like:

I'm happy again. I want to see the world.
