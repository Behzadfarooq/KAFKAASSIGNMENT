Category:Broad gauge (7 feet) railway locomotives

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The largest fleets of broad gauge locomotives were operated by the:

  • Bristol and Exeter Railway
  • Great Western Railway
  • North Devon Railway (using locomotives mainly purchased from the Bristol and Gloucester Railway
  • South Devon Railway
  • Vale of Neath Railway

[edit] Replica and surviving locomotives

There are four 7 feet gauge locomotives in the United Kingdom:

  • Fire Fly - a replica Great Western Railway 2-2-2
  • Iron Duke - a replica Great Western Railway 4-2-2
  • North Star - a non-working replica Great Western Railway 2-2-2
  • Tiny - a surviving South Devon Railway 0-4-0vb
