Brownie may refer to:

  • Brownie, chocolate cake, also known as 'Boston' or 'chocolate' brownie
    • Alice B. Toklas brownie, brownies containing cannabis (also known as hash, bud, magic or special brownie)
  • Brownie points, social currency
  • Brownie (elf), a kind of elf or tomte
  • Brownie (camera), a Kodak camera, one of the first affordable box cameras
    • Brownie (ブローニー) film, 120 film, especially in Japan
  • The Brownies (Girl Guides), junior Girl Guides in Britain and Canada, or junior Girl Scouts of the USA
  • The Brownies, fictional creatures in the books of Palmer Cox
  • Brownies, a nickname for the Cleveland Browns, an American football team
  • Brownie (slang), a sexual slur for a homosexual man
  • Michael D. Brown, head of FEMA 2003-5, famously referred to as 'Brownie' by President George W. Bush
  • Brownie (guitar) was the nickname of one of Eric Clapton's guitars that he used in the early 70's primarily on the Layla and Other Assorted Love Songs album
  • A "brownie" is a nickname given to residents of Brown College at Monroe Hill, a residential college at the University of Virginia
  • A "brownie" is a disobedient young man
  • Brownies (film), an Indonesian film produced in 2005 by Hanung Bramantyo.
