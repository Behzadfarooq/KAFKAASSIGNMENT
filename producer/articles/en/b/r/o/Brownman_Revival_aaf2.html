Brownman Revival

Brownman Revival is a Filipino reggae band. The band was started in late 1994 and was founded by Dennis and Dino Concepcion, who also founded Indio I in 1993. The group draws heavily from the music of UB40, Big Mountain, and Bob Marley.

[edit] Histography of Performances

1995

  • June 9 - ORSEM Night '95 Freshman Orientation variety show - Ateneo de Manila University
  • August 5 - “BOA Night” Business Organizations of the Ateneo party
  • December 2 - "SHINDIG" Ateneo Junior Marketing Association concert – Ateneo de Manila University
  • December 2 - Management Economics Organization party – Ateneo de Manila University
  • December 7 - CERSA OPENHOUSE '95 (w/ Indio I, Put3ska) – Ateneo de Manila University
  • Brownman Revival is also a much-anticipated act at the annual UP Fair, a weeklong slate of concerts, rides and other novelty experiences, one of the most popular campus events of the University of the Philippines-Diliman where they have been regularly making their appearances even prior to their first mainstream reggae album "Steady Lang".
