Brocton F.C. is a football club based in Brocton, near Stafford, Staffordshire, England. They were established in 1937 and joined the Staffordshire Senior League in 1991. The league was renamed the Midland League in 1994. Currently they are members of the Midland Football Combination Premier Division, while their reserves now play in the Staffordshire League. http://broctonfc.wordpress.com/
