Broughton and Milton Keynes (village) is parish grouping in Milton Keynes, England.

The group includes the two parishes of Broughton and Milton Keynes, which cover Atterbury, Brook Furlong, Broughton, Fox Milne, Middleton (Milton Keynes village), Northfield, Oakgrove, and Pineham. According to the 2001 census the two parishes have a population of 1,367. (MK 10 Postcode)
