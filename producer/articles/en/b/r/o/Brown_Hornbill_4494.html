The Brown Hornbill Ptilolaemus austeni, also known as Austen's Brown Hornbill, is a species of hornbill found in South Asia. It was recently split from the Rusty-cheeked Hornbill P. tickelli.

The name commemorates the naturalist Henry Haversham Godwin-Austen.
