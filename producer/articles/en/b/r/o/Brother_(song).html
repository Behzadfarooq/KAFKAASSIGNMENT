Brother is track 3 on the Stereophonics album Language. Sex. Violence. Other?.

Lead singer Kelly Jones has said before playing this song live before that the song was inspired by an incident that happened to him in London.

This track fades into track 4 Devil.

It is also the title of a song by Alexis Korner's CCS & a song by rock group Alice in Chains
