Brockville Lions Steel Band

The Brockville Lions Steel Band is a band of Trinidadian origin, composed chiefly of tuned percussion instruments fashioned from steel oil drums. Formed March 24th, 1996, with approx. 30 Teens ageing from 13-19 the band performs all over North America including Canada and the USA. The Band plays all kinds of variations of music. Whether it be standard Calypso, Rock, Ballads and of course traditional Holiday tunes.
