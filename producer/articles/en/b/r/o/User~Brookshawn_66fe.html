Told by his teacher that Wikipedia was not a reliable source, Brookshawn disregarded Wikipedia for months. Later on, he came to find Wiki again and embrace the market of ideas that is Wikipedia.

[edit] Delete Shortcuts

Adapted from Acebrock. I am an Inclusionist as I have probably mentioned about 3 other times in this article. I use these delete shortcuts simply for deleting nonsense articles on the Recent Changes: New Articles board.

  • {{db-bio}} If it's a page about how cool they are, it's gone.
  • {{db-band}} If it states that it plays to small audiences or the only link on the page is to MySpace, Kiss it goodbye.
  • {{db-attack}} Obvious
  • {{nonsense}} All caps or incoherent
  • {{db-empty}} Empty page or very short page
  • {{db-contact}} Reserved for ads
  • {{db-reason|my reasons here}} For If nothing else fits
