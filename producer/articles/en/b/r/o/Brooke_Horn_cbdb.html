Brooke Horn

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Lauren Brooke Horn (born October 29, 1991 in Gainesville, Florida, USA) is an American actress.

[edit] Career

Horn started her career at the age of 4 appearing in numerous prints and commercials. Her most notable role is Gwen in Little Men (film) (2005). In May 2006, it was announced at her official website that she will be playing Amanda in the upcoming mini-series Modele.

[edit] Personal life

She enjoys swimming, snorkeling, wake tubing, and rollerblading. Horn currently takes Taekwondo classes. She has one sister, Jordan Horn who is also an actress. She is close friends with Michelle Dion and Jose Joaquin Noriega.
