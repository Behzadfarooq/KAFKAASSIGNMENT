Brooke Point High School is a public high school located in Stafford, Virginia serving students in grades nine through twelve in the eastern portion of Stafford County, Virginia.

Brooke Point was completed in 1993 as the third high school in Stafford County. The colors are red and black and the mascot is the Black Hawk.

The current principal is Cynthia Holder.
