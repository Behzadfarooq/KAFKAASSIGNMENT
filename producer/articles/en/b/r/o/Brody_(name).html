[edit] Brody as a surname

Bródy is a surname of Hungarian people:

  • Imre Bródy
  • Sándor Bródy
  • Zsigmond Bródy
  • Daniel Bródy
  • János Bródy
  • Michael Bródy

The "ó" is a long o [oh] in Hungarian and the "y" indicates a "from": from Bród

Brody is a surname of Russian or Ukrainian Jews, mostly those originating from the Ukrainian city of Brody. Brody is also a surname of Irish people, while Brodie is a Scottish name.

  • Adam Brody
  • Adrien Brody
  • Alan Brody
  • Dylan Brody
  • Francine Brody
  • Larry Brody
  • Leonard Brody
  • Marcus Brody, fictional character
  • Mona Brody, US artist
  • Morton A. Brody, American judge
  • Nathan Brody
  • Neville Brody
  • Tal Brody
  • William R. Brody

[edit] Brody as a given name

Brody is also a given name of Irish people:

  • Brody Dalle
  • Brody Hutzler
