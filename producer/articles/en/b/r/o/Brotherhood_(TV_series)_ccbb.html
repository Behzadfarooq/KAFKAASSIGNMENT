Brotherhood (有情有义) is a modern television serial jointly produced by MediaCorp (Media Corporation of Singapore) and a China television production company. Starring Hongkong actor Jordan Chan, Singaporean celebrity couple Fann Wong and Christopher Lee as well as China actresses Huang Yi and Hao Lei, the serial is set in modern-day Shanghai and Singapore, telling of the inter-generational feud between two twin brothers.
