Affiliations The Beyond Corporation©
Abilities Above human combat abilities (FightPersona XP™)

The Broccolli Men are the foot soldiers of the Beyond Corporation© in Marvel Comic's Nextwave: Agents of H.A.T.E.. They are robots made partially from slabs of genetically modified kelp with motor oil blood with Doombot bodies wrapped in off the rack business suits. Their combat AI is the Beyond Corp's FightPersona XP™, an apparently ineffective package judging by the number of them that are killed by the Nextwave squad. Their speech abilities appear to be on average quite limited as well.
