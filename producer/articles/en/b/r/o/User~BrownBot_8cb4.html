Brownbot is a Wikipedia bot account run by Cbrown1023 talk that primarily uses AutoWikiBrowser to do a number of tedious tasks at a faster rate than a human editor. The bot distributes the monthly WikiProject Films newsletter at the end of each month. Other than that, the bot edits sporadically on a wide array of tasks.
