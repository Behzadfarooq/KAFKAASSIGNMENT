Brock Huard (born April 15, 1976 in Seattle, Washington) is an American football quarterback in the NFL.

[edit] High School Years

Huard attended Puyallup High School (PHS) in Puyallup, Washington. Huard enjoyed an illustrious career in high school.

[edit] Collegiate and Professional Career

Huard was a member of the Seattle Seahawks from 2000-2001 and of the Indianapolis Colts in 2003. Currently he is an unrestricted free agent. He attended the University of Washington. He is the younger brother of current NFL player Damon Huard.

[edit] Post Football Years

Brock does numerous radio and television gigs, and sources say that he is currently being considered for a position with FSN to color on their PAC-10 games. He is now announcing for ESPN Pop Warner football specials live from Disney's Wide World of Sports.
