Brooks Field (IATA: RMY, ICAO: KRMY) is a general aviation airport located south of Marshall in Calhoun County, Michigan, USA.

[edit] Facilities

Brooks Field was officially dedicated in 1931, although the first plane landed there on November 9, 1929[1] . It has 1 runway. The airport is staffed from 8AM until dusk, except for major holidays.

[edit] Runways

  • Runway 10/28: 3,500 x 75 ft. (1,067 x 23 m), Surface: Asphalt

[edit] Transit

  • The closest highway is I-69, which is approximately 2 miles west of the airport. I-94 is also very close to the airport. The airport is accessible by road from Kalamazoo St.
