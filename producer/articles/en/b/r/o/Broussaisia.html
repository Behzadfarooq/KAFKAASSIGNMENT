Broussasia is a monotypic genus of flowering plant in the family Hydrangeaceae. It consists of the single species Broussaisia arguta, known commonly as Kanawao.

Kanawao is a perennial plant native to Hawaii. A relative of the garden Hydrangea, it is a widespread species occurring in wet forests on all Hawaii's main islands. It is an evergreen shrub growing to 4 m tall, with opposite, ovate leaves 15-30 cm long with a serrated margin.
