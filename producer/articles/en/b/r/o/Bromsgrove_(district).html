Bromsgrove is a local government district in the West Midlands of England. Its council is based in the town of Bromsgrove.

Other places in the district include Aston Fields, Blakedown, Catshill, Hagley and Stoke Prior.

The current district was formed on April 1, 1974 by the merger of Bromsgrove urban district and Bromsgrove Rural District.
