I am currently away
Me at the 2005 Independence Bowl.

I'm from Jacksonville, FL, but I'm currently a second-year business student at the University of South Carolina in Columbia, SC. I play the tenor saxophone in the marching band at USC. I'm mainly a contributor to Stargate and NASCAR-related articles, though I have a few others on my watchlist. I'm a member of the NASCAR WikiProject, the Stargate WikiProject, and the IROC WikiProject. I also use far too many userboxes.

The username BrotherFlounder comes from my Kappa Kappa Psi nickname. (Flounder, Spring 06, Zeta Chi chapter - AEA!)
