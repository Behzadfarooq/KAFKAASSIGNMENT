You claim Perth is a former city. Perth is still a city.

No, Perth is a former city. The Scottish Parliament approved list of cities is:
  • Glasgow
  • Edinburgh
  • Aberdeen
  • Dundee
  • Inverness
  • Stirling
It is explained here, at section 1.2.2 "Wider Reach: the City and Region". A direct quote:
"Towns such as Perth and Elgin commonly use the title of 'city' as a survival from earlier history."
