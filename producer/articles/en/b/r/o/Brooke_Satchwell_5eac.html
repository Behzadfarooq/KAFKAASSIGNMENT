Brooke Kerith Satchwell (born 14 November 1980 in Melbourne) is an Australian actress best known for playing Anne Wilkinson in the soap opera Neighbours.

She first appeared on Australian television on 19 November 1996. She won the Best New Talent at the 1998 Logie Awards, and a People's Choice Award for Favorite Teen Idol in 1999.

She has appeared in:

  • Dangerous (2007) (TV)
  • Tripping Over (2006) (TV)
  • Small Claims: White Wedding (2005) (TV)
  • Play School (2005-current) (TV)
  • Right Here Right Now (2004)
  • "White Collar Blue" (2002) (TV)
  • Radio Samurai (2002) (V)
  • "Water Rats" (2000-2001) (TV)
  • "Neighbours" (1996-2000) (TV)

[edit] Personal life

Satchwell's five-year relationship with Matthew Newton ended in 2006 after claims he assaulted her (see here).
