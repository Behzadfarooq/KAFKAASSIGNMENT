Brooktown High Senior Year is an upcoming dating sim video game for the PlayStation Portable. It is being developed by Backbone Entertainment and published by Konami. It is set to release in May of 2007. It is a complete rewrite/remake of Konami's Tokimeki Memorial series designed and scripted with Western audiences in mind.

[edit] Gameplay

Being toted as "your chance to re-live your high school days," Brooktown High Senior Year places the gamer in the shoes of either a male or female high school student. This character is customizable, but what this entails has not been released yet. The player then gets to interact with the 20 other students at Brooktown, who have their own social circles and distinct personalities, in order to become part of the different cliques.

The main goal is to find a boyfriend or girlfriend, whom you can then take on dates to various locations such the movies, the beach, and the mall.

Various mini-games will be used to reach the goals mentioned above.
