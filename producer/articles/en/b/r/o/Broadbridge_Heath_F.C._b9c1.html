Broadbridge Heath F.C.

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Broadbridge Heath F.C. are a football club based in Broadbridge Heath, near Horsham, England. They were established in 1919 and were the founding members of the Sussex County Football League Division Three in 1983. In the 2005-06 season, they are members of the Sussex County Football League Division Two.
