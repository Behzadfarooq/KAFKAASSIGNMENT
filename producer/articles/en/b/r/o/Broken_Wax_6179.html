Broken Wax is an EP, released in 2006 by Black Milk, a rapper and hip hop producer from Detroit, Michigan. It featured appearances from Mr. Porter of D12, Phat Kat, T3, Elzhi, Fat Ray and Nametag.

[edit] Track listing

  1. Broken Wax
  2. Pressure
  3. Keep It Live (Feat. Mr. Porter)
  4. U's A Freak Bitch
  5. Tell 'Em (Feat. Nametag)
  6. Danger (Feat. T3 & Phat Kat)
  7. S.O.T.C. (Feat. Fat Ray & Elzhi)
  8. Outro
