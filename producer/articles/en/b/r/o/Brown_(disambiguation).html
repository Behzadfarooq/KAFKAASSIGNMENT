Brown is a color. It can also refer to:

  • Brown (surname), a common English family name
  • Brown University, an American Ivy League university
  • Brown, an abbreviation of Brown v. Board of Education, a landmark decision of the United States Supreme Court which explicitly outlawed racial segregation of public education facilities
  • Brown, a nickname for the United Parcel Service (UPS), originating from an advertising campaign highlighting the color of its delivery trucks and uniforms
  • Brown (album), a P.O.D. album
  • Brown County, various counties in the United States with that name
