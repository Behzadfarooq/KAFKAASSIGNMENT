Broken Equipment Productions are Twinn the Rap Rocky and Fatt Kidd. They are from the Westbank of New Orleans, Louisiana. They are the makers of "Dipset the Movie" cartoons.

Broken Equipment also made the parody song many people believe is Lil Wayne and Birdman called "Make Up Sex". And they made a parody of Lil Wayne again with Juelz Santana called "I Can't Feel My Dick".

Their slogans are "Stop what you doin, this is Broken Equipment".

They were featured in GIANT Magazine.
