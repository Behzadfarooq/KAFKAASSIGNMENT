Brooklyn Junior High School is a public junior high school located in Brooklyn Park, Minnesota on 7377 Noble Avenue North.

[edit] School Overview

The school is one of four public junior high schools in the Osseo Area School District 279 and is one of two located in Brooklyn Park. The school has a population of 1097 which is 1% Native American, 5% Hispanic, 23% Asian, 33% Black, and 38% white.

[edit] Sports Teams and Rivalries

They are the Brooklyn Bulldogs, with rivalries being the North View Knights, Osseo Orioles, and Maple Grove Mustangs. The juniorr high school rivalry reflects that of the senior high rivalry between Park Center Senior High School, Osseo Senior High School, and Maple Grove Senior High School.
