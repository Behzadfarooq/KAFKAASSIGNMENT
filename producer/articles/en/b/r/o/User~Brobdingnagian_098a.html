This account is a relatively new one, being used by someone who has been hanging around Wikipedia for quite some time.

[edit] LANGUAGE

When not reading up on things like String Theory, I study any of the nine languages that I am interested in. Among these languages are French, German, Russian, Finnish, and Icelandic.

[edit] ET CETERA

I have a great deal of passion for Science, Language, and Art. Indeed, previous teachers in these fields have dubbed me a "Renaissance Man," something that I take great pride in.

I have no specific political party that I affiliate myself with. However, the vast majority of my political ideals can be catagorized as being "left-wing," so plan accodingly.

My greatest "non-serious" hobby in playing the Guitar. Of the five instruments that I can play (Viola, Clarinet, Piano, Guitar, Bass Clarinet), I enjoy the versatility that the Electric Guitar gives.
