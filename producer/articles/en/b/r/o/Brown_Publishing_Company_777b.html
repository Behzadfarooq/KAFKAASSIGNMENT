Brown Publishing Company

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

You have new messages (last change).

The Brown Publishing Company is an Ohio newspaper business started by Congressman Clarence J. Brown in 1920. The company is still a family-owned business that publishes 18 daily newspapers, 27 weekly newspapers, and 26 free weeklies. The current CEO is Brown's grandson, Roy Brown. The current chairman of the board is Roy's brother, actor Clancy Brown.
