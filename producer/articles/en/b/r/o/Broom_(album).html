Broom is a full-length album from indie pop/rock band Someone Still Loves You Boris Yeltsin. It was released in the United States in 2005.

[edit] Track listing

All tracks by John, Tom, Will and Phil.

  1. Pangea
  2. I Am Warm + Powerful
  3. What'll We Do
  4. Travel Song
  5. Oregon Girl
  6. House Fire
  7. Yr Broom
  8. Anna Lee
  9. Anna Elephant
  10. Gwyneth

[edit] Notes

  • Additional parts by Lauren, Jonathan, Beth and Gwyn.
  • Layout and photography designed by Aaron Scott.
  • The Polyvinyl LP-reissue includes three bonus songs: Let's Get Tired, Warm + Powerful (Acoustic) and Song W.
