[edit] Reception

Rolling Stone (10/26/00, p.112) - 3 stars out of 5 - "The rare rap-metal act willing to admit that good times actually exist...Rap metal has found its Motley Crue". Q (10/00, p.119) - 3 stars out of 5 - "Focused and mature...they possess the wherewithal to express their anger and frustration musically..." CMJ (8/21/00, p.27) - "Weaves down-tuned guitars with booty-licious beats...and hardcore raps that are littered by more cussing than a sailor." Melody Maker (8/26/00, p.59) - 4 stars out of 5 - "Brilliant, about as black as this pimp-rock is gonna get, hip-hop credible in a way no one in the field has been since Urban Dance Squad...it's a great album".

[edit] Trivia

  • The CD contains bonus track "Bad Dream" but it can't be accessed by a normal CD Player. It can be played on computer after turning CD Flash Menu and typing a code: "bring pain to the powers that be".
  • Waiting To Die" features East Bay Ray of The Dead Kennedys.
