Brookville High School is part of Brookville Local Schools located in Brookville, Ohio. The school mascot is the blue devil. The motto of Brookville is "...to inspire all students to achieve excellence and prepare them to become self-directed, productive members of society."

Brookville has been rated as "Excellent" since 2002 by the Ohio Department of Education. The school met all 12 of the state indicators for the 2005-2006 school year.[1].

[edit] Ohio High School Athletic Association State Championships

  • Boys Baseball – 1935 [2]
  • Boys Track and Field – 1973 [3]
