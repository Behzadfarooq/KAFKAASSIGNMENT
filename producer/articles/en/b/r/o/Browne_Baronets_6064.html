Browne Baronets

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

There have been ten creations of baronets with the surname Browne (as distinct from Brown and Broun).

Contents

  • 1 Browne of Walcot, Northants (21 September 1621)
  • 2 Browne of Molahiffe, Kerry (16 February 1622)
  • 3 Browne of Kishack, Dublin (30 March 1622)
  • 4 Browne of Bettesworth Castle, Surrey (7 July 1627)
  • 5 Browne of The Neale, Mayo (21 June 1636)
  • 6 Browne of Deptford, Kent (1 September 1649)
  • 7 Browne of Kiddington, Oxon (1 July 1659)
  • 8 Browne of London (22 July 1660)
  • 9 Browne of Caversham, Oxon (10 May 1665)
  • 10 Browne of Palmerston, Ireland (8 December 1665)
  • 11 See also

[edit] Browne of Walcot, Northants (21 September 1621)

Created in the Baronetage of England

  • Sir Robert Browne, 1st Baronet
  • Sir Thomas Browne, 2nd Baronet
  • Sir Robert Browne, 3rd Baronet

Extinct on his death
