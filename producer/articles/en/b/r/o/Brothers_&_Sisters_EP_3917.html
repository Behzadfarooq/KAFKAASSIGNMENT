Brothers & Sisters EP
EP by Coldplay
Released April 26, 1999
Recorded Station Studios, February 1999
Genre Rock
Length 10:55
Label Fierce Panda NING 68CD
Producer(s) Mike Beaver
Coldplay

Brothers & Sisters EP is the second release by English band Coldplay. Recording was completed in four days.

The A-side, "Brothers & Sisters", was already a live favourite before the release of the EP.

The atmospheric backdrop to "Easy to Please" was created by setting up microphones on a wet road outside the studio.

Of the CD, only 2500 original copies are in existence, and can regularly be found on eBay. It was re-issued in 2002.

[edit] Track listing

  1. "Brothers & Sisters"
  2. "Easy to Please"
  3. "Only Superstition"
