Broadhall Way

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Broadhall Way is a multi-use stadium in Stevenage, England. It is currently used mostly for football matches and is the home ground of Stevenage Borough F.C.. The stadium has a capacity of 7,100 people.

It was improved in 1996 when Stevenage Borough F.C. were denied entry to the Football League because the stadium did not meet the Football League standards.
