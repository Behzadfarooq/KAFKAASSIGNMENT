'Brotha From the Third Rock is an episode from the television series Class of 3000. It first aired on January 26, 2007 on Cartoon Network.

[edit] Plot

Spoiler warning: Plot and/or ending details follow.

Philly Phil tries to get the others to believe aliens are real by launching into space. Something goes wrong and he lands back in Atlanta. He's kiddnapped by government agents who think he's an alien and wish to boil his brain. The others come to look for him and have a run in with the agents who attempt to erase their memories. The group escapes and the agents erase their own minds by mistake. The group go to Sunny for help and come up with an elaberate plan involving them dressing up and acting like ninjas, Eddie's father's fleet of black hawk choppers,cool theme music, and getting ice cream afterwards. The plan gets changed to Sunny and Li'l D sneeking in as delivery workers. The plan appears to go perfect but Sunny and Li'l D grab an real alien which looked like Philly Phil. The alien leaves right as the real Philly Phil is released after the agents discovered he wasn't an alien. Phil no longer believes in aliens, until Sunny calls some to pick him up.

Spoilers end here.

[edit] Trivia

  • The government agents are likely a reference to the Men in Black, they even have a memory eraser.
  • The person who yells "Ow, my eye!" when an item is tossed off screen is different than before. It sounds more like Madison.
  • Tamika references The Planet of the Apes.
  • They did the music video in the transmission in the plan.
  • In the Cinema (which can be seen briefly when they are ninjas and Sunny is talking not in the Music Video) the movie title is Brotha from the 3rd rock a sci-fi movie parody of the title, probably.
  • When Sunny bags the alien (not Philly Phil) in the alien room you can very briefly see his face.
  • SUMA stands for Shut Up and Move Along.

[edit] Goofs

  • When the SUMA agents are at the warehouse door the sign says: nothing here sense RossWell and the door is colored but *When Sunny and Lil'd stand there there is no sign and the door is rusted or rust colored.
  • When the real alien is captured in the bag its muffled screaming sounds like Philly Phil's
  • "Third Rock" in the title of the episode is possibly a reference to the Earth, being the "third rock" away from the Sun.
  • Roswell is also a level from Tony Hawk's Pro Skater what Sunny said, "I think you're thinking of another Roswell..."
  • Eddie's father's hair is blonde in this ep. but brown ( burnette ) in the episode "Eddie's Money.
