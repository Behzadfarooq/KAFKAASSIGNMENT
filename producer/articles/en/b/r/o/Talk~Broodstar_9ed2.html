For people interested, this is why I removed the Broodstar + Soul Foundry 'combo': it doesn't work because the "Affinity for Artifacts" ability reduces the mana cost. The Soul Foundry, however, asks for the CONVERTED mana cost. Since it doesn't reduce the converted mana cost, creating a copy of Broodstar using Soul Foundry costs 10 colorless mana. (Which is a lot, and in tournaments the game usually is already over before any player has that much mana.)

I think this article can be deleted completely, as Broodstar has a small section on the Mirrodin page. John 04:42, 28 March 2006 (UTC)

Except that very little info is given there. You wouldn't delete the article about Magic: the Gathering just because there's an article about TCGs in general, either, would you? VDZ

We don't need a card for every Magic card that impacted play. If it bothers you that much, can't you just take the most important parts of this article and put them into Broodstars' Mirrodin section? John 14:11, 30 March 2006 (UTC)
Why not an article for every important card? The more information on Wikipedia, the better.VDZ
The Power Nine has only enough notability for a single article; by keeping this article, you're saying Broodstar is more important to the game than the Power Nine. That clearly is not so. -- Grev 02:52, 26 April 2006 (UTC)
That is because the Power Nine is a specific group of cards, each of which has enough information on Wikipedia to write an article about them. Since there's no 'affinity cards' article, this article doesn't fit in with any other article. The two closest would be Ravager Affinity, but Broodstar didn't appear in that affinity type (because of the Ravager), or Mirrodin, where there's too much info to put in just the notable cards section. Maybe somebody should make an 'important affinity cards' article. VDZ
