Browns Island is the name of several places, including:

  • Browns Island (West Virginia), an island in the Ohio River
  • Browns Island, a 595-acre wetland in the San Jaoquin-Sacramento Delta which lends its name to a park within the East Bay Regional Park District in the San Francisco Bay Area
  • an island in manmade Lake Celilo on the Columbia River
  • an island in Massachusetts
  • an island in Utah's Great Salt Lake
  • Browns Island, Auckland, a small volcano off the coast of Auckland
  • a small island, also known as Tahoramaurea Island, near the southern end of Kapiti Island
  • an island in the Ahaura River, Westland
