Bronislovas Lubys

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Bronislovas Lubys (born on October 8, 1938 in Plungė) is a Lithuanian entrepreneur, a former Prime Minister of Lithuania and second-richest Lithuanian, according to the Lithuanian magazine Veidas.
