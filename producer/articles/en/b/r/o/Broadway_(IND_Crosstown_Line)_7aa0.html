Broadway is a station on the IND Crosstown Line of the New York City Subway. One of the icon tiles on the uptown platform has the station name incorrectly spelled as "Brodaway." The mezzanine has a crossover at the south end. The north end has been sealed up. It currently holds offices, storage, and once led to the South Fourth Street station shell of the IND Second System. There are vent chambers present.
