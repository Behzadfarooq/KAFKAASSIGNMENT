Brontosaurus (actually a junior synonym of Apatosaurus) is one of the most famous dinosaurs.

[edit] Film and television

  • The 1925 silent movie The Lost World features an epic Willis O'Brien special effects battle between a Brontosaurus and an Allosaurus.
  • The Skull Island of the King Kong story features a Brontosaurus (which kills several men by chomping but not eating them) in the original and involved in a stampede and pile-up in the 2005 remake.
  • The "Age of the Dinosaurs" segment of the Rite of Spring sequence in Fantasia features Brontosaurs.
  • Fred Flintstone's favourite food was Brontosaurus steak.
  • British comedy group Monty Python's 1974 season includes a sketch involving a "Miss Anne Elk" whose trivial theory about the Brontosaurus parodied the controversy of the time.
  • The 1985 film Baby: Secret of the Lost Legend involves a baby Brontosaurus.
  • The 1988 film The Land Before Time features an Apatosaurus named Littlefoot who is often mistaken as a Brontosaurus.
  • The 1980s cartoon Dinosaucers, starring anthropomorphic dinosaurs, included a character named Bronto-Thunder; however, said character had a girlfriend named Apatty Saurus, likely making his name intentionally "incorrect".

[edit] Toys

  • Sludge is a Dinobot Brontosaurus from the 1984 Transformers toy line.
  • Brontosaurus from the Dino-Riders toyline.
  • Thunder the Brontosaurus is a Fisher-Price Ecovore.
  • Bronty the Brontosaurus is a Beanie Baby.

[edit] Music

  • "Brontosaurus Stomp" (1960) was an Australian #10 hit for The Piltdown Men.
  • Proto-metal track "Brontosaurus" (1970) was a UK #7 hit for The Move.
  • David Bellamy once recorded a song called "Brontosaurus Will You Wait For Me?".
  • Music Group "Da Vinci's Notebook" releases an A Capella album titled "Brontosaurus" (2002)
  • Walking in Your Footsteps by The Police
  • Brontosaurus Chorus are a popular London based indie pop band.

[edit] Other

  • Sinclair Oil uses a Brontosaurus as its logo.
  • Bronto Software uses a Brontosaurus as its logo.
  • "9949 Brontosaurus" is an asteroid.
  • The Little Blue Brontosaurus (1983) is a children's book from Byron Preiss.
  • Danny and the Dinosaur is a children's book by Sid Hoff.
