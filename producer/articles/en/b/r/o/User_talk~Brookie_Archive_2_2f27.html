Los Tres Reyes Magos/The three Wise Men

I would like to share with my three best friends in Wikipedia, Brookie, David Cannon and Linuxbeak a Puerto Rican Christian tradition and wish you three a "Happy Dia de Reyes", a celebration that we still celebrate in Puerto Rico every January 6. The night before the children of the island fill a little box with grass for the kings camel and place it under the Christmas tree.
