Brooke Catherine Vincent (born 4th June 1992) is a child actor in the popular British soap opera Coronation Street. In 2004, Brooke took over from Emma Woodward in playing Sophie Webster in Coronation Street.

[edit] Career

Brooke is two years older than the character she plays and, shortly after landing the role of Sophie in "Coronation Street", she appeared on "Children in Need".

Brooke's first television appearance was as a guest star, Casey Emma Glass, in the TV series, "The League of Gentlemen". She joined Coronation Street in April 2004 as the third actress to play Sophie Webster, replacing Emma Woodward who decided to leave Corrie at the end of 2003. Brooke's first appearance was shown on 12th May.

In 2005 she appeared in the Coronation street Christmas Pantomime.

[edit] Personal life

She lives in Denton, Manchester and has two half sisters called Georgia and Holly.

Brooke attendes Fairfield High School for Girls. When she is not filming, Brooke loves to shop with good mate Helen Flanagan, who plays Brooke's older sister in Coronation Street. Brooke also loves to play football and supports Manchester United.
