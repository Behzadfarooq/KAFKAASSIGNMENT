[edit] Current Info

Broadcast Media was approved by the FCC to purchase the Univision group of television stations. The purchase is pending final details and the paying of fine by Univision.

[edit] Radio

San Antonio, Texas is the 29th largest radio market in the country as ranked by Arbitron. The following is a list of radio stations serving the San Antonio area.
