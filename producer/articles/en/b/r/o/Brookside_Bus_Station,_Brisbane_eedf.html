The Brookside Bus Station, at Mitchelton, is serviced by TransLink bus routes. It is part of the Brookside Shopping Centre and is close to Mitchelton Railway Station. It is in Zone 3 of the TransLink integrated public transport system.

[edit] TransLink bus routes servicing Brookside

[edit] Stop A

  • 354 - Chermside : Local via Everton Park and Stafford Heights (Monday - Saturday) (BT)
  • 358 - Toombul : Local via Stafford and Wooloowin (Monday - Saturday) (BT)
  • 599 - Great Circle Line - Clockwise : Loop via Chermside, Toombul, Cannon Hill, Carindale, Garden City, Sunnybank, Indooroopilly, Toowong, Brookside (Mitchelton) and Chermside (Monday - Saturday) (BT)

[edit] Stop B

  • 360 - City : All Stops via Fortitude Valley, Enoggera and Everton Park (Monday - Saturday) (BT)
  • 361 - City : All Stops via Fortitude Valley, Newmarket, Gaythorne and Mitchelton (Monday - Friday) (BT)
  • 362 - The Gap : Local via Keperra (Monday - Saturday) (BT)
  • 390 - City : All Stops via Kelvin Grove, Newmarket and Enoggera (Every day) (BT) - 1 service continues to Ferny Hills during PM peak
  • N390 - Ferny Grove : NightLink via Kelvin Grove, Newmarket, Enoggera and Mitchelton (Late night Friday - Saturday) (BT)
  • 397 - Ferny Grove : All Stops via Bunya Road (Monday - Friday) (BT) - 1 service continues to City during AM peak
  • 398 - Ferny Grove : All Stops via Arana Hills (Monday - Friday) (BT)
  • 598 - Great Circle Line - Anti-clockwise : Loop via Chermside, Brookside (Mitchelton), Toowong, Indoorooopilly, Sunnybank, Garden City, Carindale, Cannon Hill, Toombul and Chermside (Monday - Saturday) (BT)
