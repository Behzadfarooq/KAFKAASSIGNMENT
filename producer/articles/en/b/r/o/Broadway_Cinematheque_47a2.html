Broadway Cinematheque

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Broadway Cinematheque (Traditional Chinese: 百老匯電影中心) is a cinema in Yau Ma Tei, Hong Kong. Located within a private housing estate Prosperous Garden, the cinema, run by Broadway Circuit, provides wider spectrum of films than other cinemas in Hong Kong. The cinema hosts four houses with 640 seats. It also has a book store, Kubrick, about films and a café in adjacent.
