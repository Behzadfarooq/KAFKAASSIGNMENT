Broadway High School

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

This article is about Broadway High School in California and should not be confused with the one in Virginia, although it redirects here. For the defunct Seattle high school, see Seattle Central Community College.
Entrance to Broadway High School in San Jose, California.

Broadway High School is an alternative secondary school located in San Jose, California which serves students within the San Jose Unified School District. The school was first opened in 1976.

The school offers a number of intern programs, such as health services and vocational occupations, as well as afternoon and evening adult education classes. They also offer a NASA-sponsored nationally-recognized Robotics program which won national honors in the 1999 NASA Ames Regional FIRST Robotics Competition. [1]

Average annual enrollment is approximately 300 pupils, in contrast with over 1000 in most other San Jose Unified high schools.
