Brook Islands National Park

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Brook Islands is a national park in Queensland (Australia), 1246 km northwest of Brisbane.

[edit] Fact sheet

  • Area: 0.9 km²
  • Coordinates: 18°08′29″S, 146°16′56″E
  • Date of establishment: 1994
  • Managing authorities: Queensland Parks and Wildlife Service
  • IUCN category: II
