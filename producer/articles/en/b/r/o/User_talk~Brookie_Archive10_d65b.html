Cristian Vogel is a composer and music producer, known for his experimental DJ and Live performances, studio composition and production. Born in Chile 1972 but growing up in the UK, he now lives and works in Barcelona. 

He studied 20th Century Music at Sussex University, under the tutor-ship of the British composers, Johnathan Harvey and Martin Butler. Over more than a decade he has recorded a number of albums and released on eminent experimental techno labels such as Tresor Berlin, Novamute and Mille Plateaux. He has toured consistently since 1992 as DJ and live performer, both as a solo artist and with the group 'Super_Collider'. As composer for dance, he has scored four pieces for Gilles Jobin, the renound Swiss choreographer. As remixer and producer, he has been invited to reinterpret songs by groups such as Radiohead, Maximo Park and Oval and has has been album producer for Chicks On Speed amongst others.
