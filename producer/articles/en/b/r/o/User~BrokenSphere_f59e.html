[edit] Current projects

Priorities tend to shift depending on how I feel about certain projects. I typically tend to focus on a particular anime and fill in missing information or pictures, but lately have been bouncing around as soon as something new or in need of workup catches my eye.

[edit] Contributions

I started editing under this username on October 23, 2006. My contributions tend to fall under the following:

  • Adding the appropriate Wikiproject banners to talk pages. I have probably done this for a couple thousand articles and counting.
  • Recent changes patrol, including reverting vandalism, tagging pages for speedy deletion, reporting vandals, etc. This is a great way to contribute to Wikipedia without creating articles yourself.
  • Copy edits, usually a random article found here, or whenever I spot the need, e.g. typos, layout, wording, adding or revising content, etc., etc.
  • Uploading images, see below for more info.
  • Current edit count - this is just a number.
