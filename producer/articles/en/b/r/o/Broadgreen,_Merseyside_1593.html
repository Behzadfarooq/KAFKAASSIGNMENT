Broadgreen is a suburb of Liverpool, Merseyside. Located on the eastern edge of the city, it is bordered by Knotty Ash, Childwall and, further east, Huyton-with-Roby, part of the borough of Knowsley.

[edit] History

This section is a stub. You can help by expanding it.

Until 2004, it was a Liverpool City Council Ward.

[edit] Description

Broadgreen is primarily a residential area, made up of semi-detached and detached houses. As a small district, it often merged with Childwall, a larger suburb of the city. It is home to Broadgreen Hospital.

The Broadgreen area covers 2.97 square kilometres and in 2001 had a population of 14,889.

[edit] Transport

  • It is served by Broad Green railway station where regular trains depart for Liverpool city centre, Manchester, Wigan and St Helens.
  • It is situated close to the end of the M62.
