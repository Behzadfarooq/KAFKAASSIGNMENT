Bronius Kutavičius

Bronius Kutavičius (born 1932) is a Lithuanian composer. He has written numerous oratorios and operas, among them The Last Pagan Rites, Lokis the Bear and The Gates of Jerusalem. He won the Lithuanian State Prize in 1995, and the prize of the Probaltica Festival in Torun (Poland) in 1996.

[edit] Works

  • Dzukian Variations (1978)
  • The Last Pagan Rites(oratorio,1978)
  • From the Jatvingian Stone (1983)
  • The Gates of Jerusalem(1991-95)
  • Epitaphium temporum pereunti (1998)
  • Lokis the Bear (opera, 2000)
  • Joys of Spring (2005)
