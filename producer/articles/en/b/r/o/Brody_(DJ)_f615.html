Brody (born 1976 in Oakland, New Jersey) is an American radio personality and music director in Raleigh, North Carolina for WDCG-FM 105.1, the only Top 40 radio channel in the Raleigh-Durham metropolitan area. He hosts his own midday radio show every weekday from 10:00 a.m. to 3:00 p.m. EST.

In 2006, Brody moved from nights to middays on G-105. He was replaced at night by Geller, a deejay who used to work in eastern North Carolina before going to Omaha, Salt Lake City, and Atlanta before coming to G-105.
