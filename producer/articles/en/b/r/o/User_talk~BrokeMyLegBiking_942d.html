[edit] Your Wikipedia Page

Hi. I noticed you wanted a page to experiment on and test how Wikipedia works. The commonly accepted method is to make your own "sandbox" (an area for you to test out edits in). Here's how to do it.

You can add /sandbox after your username in the URL bar above. For example, you'd go to:

http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/User:BrokeMyLegBiking/sandbox

It'll ask you to make a page, and you can then create a page for testing.

The difference between making a sandbox as stated above and making an article (as you did) is that the main space of Wikipedia should be for encyclopedia articles only (we call that "articlespace" or "mainspace"). But since the sandbox is under your name (so to speak), you can use it for testing as you wish (we call that "userspace").

If you have any more questions about this, feel free to leave a message on my talk page. Welcome to Wikipedia! .V. [Talk|Email] 15:17, 6 February 2007 (UTC)
