Brown Bear's Wedding was a one off cartoon that was produced by Central Television aired on ITV at Christmas 1991.

The cartoon featured an all star cast of voices including Joss Ackland as the voice of Brown Bear, Helena Bonham Carter as the voice of White Bear and Hugh Laurie as the voice of Brown Bear's best friend Owl.
