[edit] School Overview

Brooklyn Center High School is the only high school serving ISD #286, and has approximately 850 students in grades 7-12. One of the highest in the state, BCHS' open enrollment percentage is 33%. District 286 the district is the smallest district in the state in terms of geographical size

[edit] Trivia

  • School fight song is the University of Purdue fight song
  • School rivals are Columbia Heights High School


[edit] Notable Alumni

  • Marcus Harris, Fred Biletnikoff award winner as a receiver at Wyoming
  • Liz Alabi, class of 2002, holds Gopher record in hammer throw and is also 2-time team captain
