Brother Love's Travelling Salvation Show is the name of Neil Diamond's fourth studio album. Four months after the title cut became a hit (#22), Diamond recorded and released a new single, Sweet Caroline (#4). Because of it's popularity, the song was tagged on to the end of later pressings of the album, which was also given a new sleeve (see illustration below right). All songs written by Neil Diamond.
