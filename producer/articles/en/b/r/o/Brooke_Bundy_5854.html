Brooke Bundy

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Brooke Bundy (b. August 8, 1944 in New York City, New York, USA) is an American actress who has appeared in film and on television. Brooke has appeared in many TV movies and some films. Her well known feature film role is in the 1987 hit horror film A Nightmare on Elm Street 3: Dream Warriors and in the following 1988 movie A Nightmare on Elm Street 4: The Dream Master as Elaine Parker.

She has starred on serials like Days of Our Lives as Rebecca North from 1975-1977 and in General Hospital as Diana Maynard Taylor R.N. from 1977-1981.

Brooke has made guest appearances on many television shows, some of those appearances range from Gunsmoke, Bonanza, Rawhide, and Star Trek: The Next Generation as Chief Engineer Sarah MacDougal in the season 1 episode "The Naked Now".

Brooke Bundy has been married to Peter Helm since the mid 1960s and have a daughter, Tiffany Helm who is also an actress. She is now an acting coach and manager and is a part of the NUSTARS program for new actors and actresses.
