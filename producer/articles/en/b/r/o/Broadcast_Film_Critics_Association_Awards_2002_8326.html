8th Broadcast Film Critics Association Awards

January 17, 2003

Best Picture:
Chicago

The 8th Critics' Choice Awards, honoring the best filmmaking of 2002, were given on 17 January 2003.

[edit] Top 10 Films

(alphabetical)

  1. About Schmidt
  2. Adaptation.
  3. Catch Me If You Can
  4. Chicago
  5. Far from Heaven
  6. Gangs of New York
  7. The Hours
  8. The Lord of the Rings: The Two Towers
  9. The Pianist
  10. Road to Perdition

[edit] Winners

  • Best Picture:
    • Chicago
  • Best Actor:
    • Jack Nicholson - About Schmidt
  • Best Actress:
    • Julianne Moore - Far from Heaven
  • Best Supporting Actor:
    • Chris Cooper - Adaptation.
  • Best Supporting Actress:
    • Catherine Zeta-Jones - Chicago
  • Best Director:
    • Steven Spielberg - Catch Me If You Can
  • Best Writer:
    • Charlie Kaufman - Adaptation. and Confessions of a Dangerous Mind
  • Best Young Actor/Actress:
    • Kieran Culkin - Igby Goes Down
  • Best Ensemble Cast:
    • Chicago
  • Best Animated Feature:
    • Spirited Away
  • Best Family Film (Live Action):
    • Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets
  • Best Foreign Language Film:
    • Y tu mamá también (And Your Mother Too), Mexico/USA
  • Best Documentary:
    • Bowling for Columbine
  • Best Picture Made for Television:
    • Door to Door
  • Best Song:
    • "Lose Yourself" - 8 Mile by Eminem
  • Best Composer:

John Williams - Catch Me If You Can and Minority Report

  • Best Digital Acting Performance:
    • Gollum - The Lord of the Rings: The Two Towers
