[edit] Broke By Tuesday

Broke By Tuesday are an unsigned, four-piece punk rock band that originated from Texas, in the United States.

[edit] The Current Band Setup

Tony Salazar - Guitar/Vocals

Chris Roe - Drums

Danny Chapman - Bass Guitar / Vocals

Brian Mccartney - Guitar/Keyboard/Vocals

[edit] Overview

Broke By Tuesday are a punk rock band that play their own songs. The style they mostly play is punk and punk rock with tons of influences from all over the musical spectrum.

They live in Austin, Texas...in the United States and regularly play in gigs in the Austin and San Marcos area. The band is hoping to raise enough money from their local gigs to be able to record their debut album, to be named....hah we don't have a name for it yet.

[edit] History

The band was first created under the name 'Number Six', by James Pearce, Jason Reichl, and Tony Salazar.

After searching for a drummer, James suggested they put up an add made out out of makeup we found at Jason's house. We put it up and didn't hear anything for several weeks. We eventually found a drummer, and since then the band has gone through different members as well as band names. Past members include: Jimmy Keeton on drums, George Dell on guitar, Jason Reichl on bass guitar, Faf on drums, Anthony on Drums, another Anthony on drums, Chris on drums, Steve on bass guitar, Jon on bass guitar, Shaun on guitar, and Jose on drums. As for band names: Number Six, Godzilla Gorilla, Short Bus Heroes, and The Legos.


Where are they now


Jason Reichl went on to perform in Another Antigone and The Bends.
