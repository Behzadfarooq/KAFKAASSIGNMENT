Broadway Shopping Centre

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Broadway Shopping Centre in Sydney, Australia is a large shopping centre containing a Kmart, Coles, BiLo, food court, Hoyts 12 screen cinemas, Target, and well over 100 other shops. It is located on a street by the same name in Broadway. In April 2007 a new 4th opened containing 35 new stores.
