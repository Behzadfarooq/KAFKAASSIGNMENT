Brodek u Přerova is a village in the Olomouc Region of the Czech Republic. It is located 9 km northwest of Přerov and has a population of 2,058 (2006). The first written mention of Brodek dates back to 1301. Today the village is known for bellmaking. Bells of many churches in the Czech Republic have been cast in local workshop od Dytrych family, founded in 1951.
