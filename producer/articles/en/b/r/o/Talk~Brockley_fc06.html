[edit] The stone circle

The article says the stone circle was made by "a group of local artists". Does anyone know who this group is? BobFromBrockley 16:41, 19 March 2007 (UTC)

Not sure without checking - but they did publish a booklet about the whole enterprise - used to be available in the ceramics shop at the bottom of Vicars Hill. NickW 09:48, 22 March 2007 (UTC)

There is indeed a very good booklet published by the Brockley Society in 2000. which tells the whole story of the circle, which goes back to 1997 and involved a group of local artists involved in the Brockley Open Studios. The Open Studio event takes place each summer and is a good chance to meet local artists and see their work. See Also the Art House in Lewisham way. Jon G March 25.
