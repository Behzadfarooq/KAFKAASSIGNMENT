Brown Peak is a peak located in Kern County, California. The peak is located on King Solomons Ridge at the head of Haight Canyon, 3.4 km (2.1 mi) southwest of Liebel Peak and 20 km (12.5 mi) north of Loraine.

Brown Peak is named for Charlie Brown, a miner who lived in the area during the early 1900's.
