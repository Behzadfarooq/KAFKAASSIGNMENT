  • Broken Heart, Mylon LeFevre's band during the period of 1982-1991
  • "Broken Heart," a song by Spiritualized from their 1997 album Ladies and Gentlemen We Are Floating in Space
  • "Broken Heart," a song by White Lion from their 1991 album Mane Attraction
