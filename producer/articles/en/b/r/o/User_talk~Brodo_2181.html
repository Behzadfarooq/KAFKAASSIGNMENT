[edit] Gonzo

Hi, Brodo. The reference to Gonzo as "Gonzales" in the Steve Martin episode is a one-joke situation. Kermit says that he doesn't want to see Gonzo's dancing cheese audition. He tells Gonzo to get lost, and then asks Scooter who's up next. Scooter announces the next audition as "Gonzales and Yolanda," and then we see Gonzo and a wedge of cheese dressed in Spanish costumes, dancing to flamenco guitar music. You wrote on the Gonzo page that his name is "said to be short for Gonzales," but the characters don't say that.

In another episode, Fozzie performs a magic act as "The Great Fozzini," but that's not supposed to be his real name -- it's just a one-time alias, like "Gonzales." I hope that makes my edit more clear. Toughpigs 04:36, 5 December 2005 (UTC)
