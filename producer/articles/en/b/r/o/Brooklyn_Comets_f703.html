The Brooklyn Comets are an expansion team for the American Basketball Association (ABA) in the New York City borough of Brooklyn. The team began play as the Brooklyn Wonders and was owned by Leigh Sutton and their general manager was Ron Eford before an ownership change.

The team was purchased in mid-December 2006 by Daisy Brand (mother of Los Angeles Clippers star Elton Brand and his brother Artie Brand, who will be GM) and plans to resume play on February 3, 2007, going by the name Brooklyn Comets. [1]
