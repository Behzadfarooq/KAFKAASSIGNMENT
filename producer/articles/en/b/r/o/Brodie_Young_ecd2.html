Brodie Young

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Brodie Young hopsting Quizmania (on 2007-03-01)

Brodie Young is a television personality in Australia and host of Quizmania.

Brodie was first seen on television on Channel Ten's hit reality show Big Brother in 2002 as an intruder. He was voted off the program after two weeks. He then had guest roles on Ten's Totally Wild and Wicked Science, and Nickelodeon children's program SNTV. Brodie has also appeared in commercials for Mitsubishi Motors, Nestle and Jetstar.

He had a lead role in a television commercial for RACV. In 2005, he hosted Noise TV on SBS. He is currently host of Channel Nine's Quizmania. As well as television, Brodie has done many public events.
