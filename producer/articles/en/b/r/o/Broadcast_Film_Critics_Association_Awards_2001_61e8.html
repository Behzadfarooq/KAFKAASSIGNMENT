7th Broadcast Film Critics Association Awards

January 11, 2002

The 7th Critics' Choice Awards, honoring the best filmmaking of 2001, were given on 11 January 2002.

[edit] Top 10 Films

(alphabetical)

  1. Ali
  2. A Beautiful Mind
  3. In the Bedroom
  4. The Lord of the Rings: The Fellowship of the Ring
  5. The Man Who Wasn't There
  6. Memento
  7. Moulin Rouge!
  8. Mulholland Drive
  9. The Shipping News
  10. Shrek

[edit] Winners

  • Best Picture:
    • A Beautiful Mind
  • Best Actor:
    • Russell Crowe - A Beautiful Mind
  • Best Actress:
    • Sissy Spacek - In the Bedroom
  • Best Supporting Actor:
    • Ben Kingsley - Sexy Beast
  • Best Supporting Actress:
    • Jennifer Connelly - A Beautiful Mind
  • Best Director (tie):
    • Ron Howard - A Beautiful Mind
    • Baz Luhrmann - Moulin Rouge!
  • Best Screenplay:
    • Memento - Christopher Nolan
  • Best Young Performer:
    • Dakota Fanning - I Am Sam
  • Best Ensemble Cast:
    • Gosford Park
  • Best Animated Film:
    • Shrek
  • Best Family Film:
    • Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone
  • Best Foreign Language Film:
    • Le Fabuleux destin d'Amélie Poulain, France
  • Best Picture Made For Television:
    • Life with Judy Garland: Me and My Shadows
  • Best Song:
    • "May It Be", Enya - The Lord of the Rings: The Fellowship of the Ring
  • Best Composer:
    • Howard Shore - The Lord of the Rings: The Fellowship of the Ring
