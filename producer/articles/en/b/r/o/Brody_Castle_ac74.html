Brody Castle

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Brody Castle (Ukrainian: Бродівський замок, Brodivskyi zamok) is a castle located in the city of Brody, part of Lviv Oblast, Ukraine. The structure was built between 1630 and 1635 by Stanisław Koniecpolski, based on a design of a French engineer.
