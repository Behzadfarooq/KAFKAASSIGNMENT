Brogan-Fletcher Enterprise is a large blue-chip company established in Pensacola, Florida in 1990. Originally called the Brogan Project, the name changed after Ted Brogan, the founder, recruited a young associate Brad Fletcher to help expand his global business empire.

[edit] The Brogan Project Days

Ted Brogan's initial ideas and ambitions for his newly established company, the Brogan Project, were curtailed by his financial difficulties. By downsizing in the IT department, Brogan was able to afford a plane ticket to Texas to find a business partner. He had heard favourable talk of a young entrepreneur, Brad Fletcher, who been very successful in the Texas Oil Industry. At first, Fletcher was unwilling, but agreed after a lengthy meeting with Brogan and his colleagues. The business relies on the match between Brogan's idealism and Fletcher's financial capability.

"At first Brad seemed unimpressed with my unconventional idealism towards the management of a large company, but after a meeting with Refreshment Technition Jack Daniels, he was more willing" -Ted Brogan, December 4, 1990

[edit] The rise and diversification of the business

Brogan Fletcher Ent. started off by investing USD $15 million into the rapidly growing pharmaceuticals industry. With the overwhelming profits made by the development of topical anasthetic gelatin, Brogan Fletcher Ent. expanded into the military technology industry. They established firm links with the Pentagon by developing the highly acclaimed model 226 automatic slide operated pistol.

After the company's success in the fields of military engineering and pharmaceutical development, Brogan and Fletcher expanded the business, based on their philosophy of diversity, by starting several new subsidiary companies.

The first expansion of many was their investment in a law firm, with the sole purpose of helping low-income individuals fight insurance claims. Many insurance companies have talented and well-paid lawyers at their disposal, and it can be very difficult to produce a strong lawsuit against them without suitable legal aid. The newly-formed Kitcheman, Kerr and Sons law firm aimed to be an affordable solution.

Shortly afterwards the Brogan-Fletcher Enterprise expanded further, by founding an investment firm. Freedom Financial was a financial planning firm geared towards helping parents prepare for their children's college or university education. With their Slogan "Start Early, For Tomorrow's Sake", Freedom Financial soared to the be one of the top Financial Firms in the Miami area.

[edit] Current status

Brogan Fletcher Enterprise's current situation is a very positive one, with gross earnings of around USD $5 billion per annum, and a constant search for expansion. Upon the release of their 2007 projected earnings, the company's stock rose to USD $57.30 and is expected to pass USD $70.00 by November 2007.
