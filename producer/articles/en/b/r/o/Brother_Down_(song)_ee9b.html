Brother Down is Canadian musician Sam Roberts' first single. It was first featured on the album Brother Down and later on the Juno Award winning album We Were Born in a Flame.

[edit] Awards & Nominations

  • 2003 - Nominated for Single of the Year at the Juno Awards.
  • 2003 - Won Best VideoFACT at the MuchMusic Video Awards.
  • 2003 - Nominated for Best Pop Video and Best Independent Video and the MuchMusic Video Awards.
