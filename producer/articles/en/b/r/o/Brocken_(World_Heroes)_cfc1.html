Brocken is a fictional character designed for the ADK's World Heroes game series. Brocken is part-robot, and his moveset contains techniques involving mechanically-extended limbs and missile 'fists'.

[edit] About Brocken

The Ultimate Soldier of the XXIII century. He alone is able to defeat an army. Invited to defeat Geegus and acepted by orders of his superiors. Thanks to Professor Brown, his power has increased, and even though his body is an ice-cold machine, his arrogant heart has not even cooled down.

[edit] Trivia

  • Apparently based on Brocken Jr. from Kinnikuman and to some extent Adolf Hitler[citation needed]. Also could be based in Terminator.[citation needed]
  • Brocken is also compared to Street Fighter II's M. Bison and Dhalsim, due to the former's appearance and the latter's extendable limbs.
  • He appears in stages related to the WWII. In World Heroes, he appears in an industrial elevator, and the player can see Messerschmitt fighters and tanks (probably Panzers). In World Heroes 2 and World Heroes 2 Jet, he appears inside a building (possibly a military depot). During the battle, a huge Jagdpanther tank tears down a wall. In World Heroes Perfect, he appears in a cave that serves as shelter for U-Boats (the U-Boat featured bears the name U-209 in its conning tower).
