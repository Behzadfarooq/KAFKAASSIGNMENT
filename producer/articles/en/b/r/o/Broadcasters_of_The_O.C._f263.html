This article contains information regarding the international broadcast of the American television series The O.C.. The series concluded on February 22, 2007. It has also premiered in other countries around the world including non-English speaking countries.

[edit] Australia

In Australia, The O.C. airs on Channel Ten at 9:30pm Tuesdays. It originally aired on 8:30pm Tuesday nights. However, during Season 4, it moved to a later timeslot of 9:30pm. Replays of The O.C. screen on pay television station, Arena on Wednesday nights. The Australian broadcast of the fourth season of The O.C. on Channel Ten started simultaneous to FOX in the United States, airing only four days after, but is currently broadcast 2 weeks after it has been shown in the US. Channel Ten aired the series finale of the fourth season in a double episode at 8.30pm on February 23 2007, only hours after the US. Due to Channel Ten's AFL commitments in Adelaide the series finale aired a day later than the rest of Australia on February 24 at 8.00pm.

[edit] United States

The first season of The O.C. premiered on Fox in the United States on August 5, 2003 at 9:00pm ET. The series finale of The O.C. is scheduled to air on February 22, 2007. The cable network SOAPnet purchased the syndication rights to The O.C. On Monday, April 9, 2007 the network will begin airing reruns of the show as part of an afternoon, high school drama line up with Beverly Hills, 90210 at 4 P.M. ET, One Tree Hill at 5 P.M. ET and The O.C. at 6 P.M. ET.[1]
