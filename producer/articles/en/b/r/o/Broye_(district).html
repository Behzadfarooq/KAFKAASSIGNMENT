The District of Broye is a one of the seven districts of the Canton of Fribourg, Switzerland, lying in the vicinity of Lake Neuchâtel. Its territory is non-contiguous and Broye is fragmented into four parts, three of which are exclaved from the canton of Fribourg. Broye consists of the following municipalities, of which Estavayer-le-Lac is the capital:
