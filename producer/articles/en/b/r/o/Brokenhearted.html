"Brokenhearted" is an R&B song written and produced by Keith Crouch, and Kipper Jones for Brandy's self-titled debut studio album, Brandy (1994). A remix version of the song, produced by duo Soulshock & Karlin and including vocals by Boyz II Men lead singer Wanya Morris, was released as the album's fourth and final single in August 1995 (see 1995 in music). In the United States, the song peaked at number 9 on the Billboard Hot 100, becoming Brandy's third top ten hit domestically.
