Brooke Howard-Smith (born October 1972) is a New Zealand broadcaster, featuring on radio and TV3's Target consumer affairs' show. He is also the co-founder of Senate and England clothing skate brands and of the Poynter streetwear label, and continues to have a following among the skateboarding and rollerblading community in the US Europe and Canada. His girlfriend Amber peebles is a former Miss New Zealand and presents for MTVs Full Tank.

Brooke has a band with James Reid of the Feelers called Fierce Asian Gang.
