  • Hello, I'm Bronxigar. I joined Wikipedia on November 28th, 2005 but only recently have asserted a name for myself. I am currently a student in business *attending Brevard Community College, and a proud member of the USAF.

[edit] Vandalism policy

I revert vandalism on sight. I also regularly monitor the recent changes. I always warn after vandalism (unless the vandal is already blocked/reported on WP:AIV, or if someone warns before me for vandalism I reverted), and expect other users to do the same when they revert vandalism. I always assume good faith, and will treat different types of vandalism differently. I will generally go through the test1-4 process, unless the user has been warned before or is obviously a blocked vandal who just switched IP's, in which case I will be much less lenient.
