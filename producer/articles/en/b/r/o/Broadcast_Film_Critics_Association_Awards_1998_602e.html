Broadcast Film Critics Association Awards 1998

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

4th Critics' Choice Awards

1999

The 4th Critics' Choice Awards given by the Broadcast Film Critics Association in 1999 honored the finest achievements in 1998 filmmaking.

[edit] Winners

  • Best Picture:
    • Saving Private Ryan
  • Best Actor:
    • Ian McKellen - Gods and Monsters
  • Best Actress:
    • Cate Blanchett - Elizabeth
  • Best Supporting Actor:
    • Billy Bob Thornton - A Simple Plan and Primary Colors
  • Best Supporting Actress:
    • Kathy Bates - Primary Colors
  • Best Director:
    • Steven Spielberg - Saving Private Ryan
  • Best Screenplay - Adaptation:
    • A Simple Plan - Scott Smith
  • Best Screenplay - Original:
    • Shakespeare in Love - Tom Stoppard and Marc Norman
  • Best Child Performance:
    • Ian Michael Smith - Simon Birch
  • Best Animated Feature (tie):
    • A Bug's Life
    • The Prince of Egypt
  • Best Family Film:
    • A Bug's Life
  • Best Foreign Film:
    • La vita Ã¨ bella (Life Is Beautiful), Italy
