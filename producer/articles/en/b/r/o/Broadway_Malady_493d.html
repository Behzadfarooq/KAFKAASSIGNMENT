"Broadway Malady" is the eleventh episode in the first season of American mystery series Murder, She Wrote. It first aired on January 13, 1985 and guest stars Milton Berle.

[edit] Synopsis

Broadway legend Rita Bristol and her daughter star in a musical produced by her son, not knowing that murder is lurking in the wings.
