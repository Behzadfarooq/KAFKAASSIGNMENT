Brownstown, Lancaster County, Pennsylvania

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Brownstown, Pennsylvania is located in Lancaster County, Pennsylvania.

[edit] Geography

The town has a total land area of 0.66 square kilometers. It is situated southwest of Ephrata and northeast Lancaster city. Pennsylvania Route 772 (South State Street) runs through the center of town. It is located in Lancaster and there is a lot of farming.

[edit] Demographics

The population is approximately 937.
