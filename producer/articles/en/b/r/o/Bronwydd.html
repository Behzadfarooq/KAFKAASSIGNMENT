Bronwydd

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Bronwydd is a village in Carmarthenshire, Wales. It is situated about three miles north of Carmarthen town in the valley of the River Gwili.

Brownwydd gives its name to a community comprising the Bronwydd village, a couple of nearby hamlets and a number of working farms in the surrounding area.
