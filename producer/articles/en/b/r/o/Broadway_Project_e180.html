Broadway Project is musician Dan Berridge from Bristol. Reviewers often describe the music as "cinematic" and it combines elements of ambient, jazz and hip hop. The epic nature of his records has led to him being commissioned to write scores for British television programs. He has released three albums to date.

[edit] Discography

  • Compassion (2000, Memphis Industries, Cat no.: MI014CD)
  • The Vessel (2003, Memphis Industries, Cat no.: MI031CD)
  • In Finite (30 May 2005, Grand Central Records, Cat no.: GCCD140)

A second version of Compassion was released in 2002, with an additional disc containing early singles and rarities (Cat no. MI014CD2)
