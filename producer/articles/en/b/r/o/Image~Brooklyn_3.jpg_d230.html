== Summary for Template:Brooklyn Mattingly


T-girl author from San Francisco, At age 22 Brooklyn Mattingly is quickly moving up the ranks of Penguin Authors in the United States with two movie scripts to her credit that were used from her first two novels written. The novels (THE ROOKIE BOOKIE IN 2002), and (LEAPING DEATH in 2006) which is in current movie casting for production in early 2008. She is currently working on a transgendered fiction novel and a novel about a teenage romance. Was born in Richmond, BC. Canada on August.29/1984 as Howard(Hugh)Hawkins and legally had his name changed to Brook Lynn Mattingly on April.17/2006 as a citizen of both Canada and the United States. Is not legally allowed to change gender status unless an assignment surgery is performed. As of right now Brooklyn does not wish to partake in any of those so she is still considered a male on all identification. Moved to San Francisco in April/06 where she resides currently. Has four step-brothers and two-step sisters and is the youngest out of all of them.
