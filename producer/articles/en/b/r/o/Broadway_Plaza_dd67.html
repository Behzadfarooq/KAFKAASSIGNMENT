Broadway Plaza is an upscale shopping mall located in downtown Walnut Creek, California. It is managed by The Macerich Company. The mall is anchored by Macy's & Nordstrom's. David M. Brian, The Gap, Banana Republic, Ann Taylor, Victoria's Secret and Build-A-Bear Workshop are a few of the retailers that draw a large and diverse market to the plaza.
