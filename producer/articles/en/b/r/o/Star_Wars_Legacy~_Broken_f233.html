Star Wars Legacy: Broken is a six-part story arc in the Star Wars: Legacy series of comic books written by John Ostrander & Jan Duursema. The first issue was published on 21 June 2006 by Dark Horse Comics. The story is set in the Star Wars galaxy approximately 100 years after the end of the Yuuzhan Vong War in the novel series The New Jedi Order, and 130 years after the Battle of Yavin in the movie Star Wars Episode IV: A New Hope.
