Broadway Bill is a horse-racing comedy film from 1934, directed by Frank Capra and starring Warner Baxter and Myrna Loy. The film was one of Capra's personal favourites, and he remade it himself in 1950 as Riding High.

In later years, the distributor of Riding High, Paramount Pictures, would acquire the rights to Broadway Bill.

In the UK the film was released as Strictly Confidential.
