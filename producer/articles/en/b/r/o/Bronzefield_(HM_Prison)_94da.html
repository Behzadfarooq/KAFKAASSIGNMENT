Bronzefield (HM Prison)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

You have new messages (last change).

HM Prison Bronzefield at Ashford, Middlesex, England has been designed specifically to reflect women prisoners' needs. Opened in June 2004 to accommodate 450 women, it includes a mother and baby unit and a special section for women with behavioural problems.

HM Prison Bronzefield is the largest female prison in Europe and is currently the only prison to house cat-A prisoners in the female estate.

Bronzefield is a privately run prison operated by United Kingdom Detention Services and is staffed by approx 160 prison officers which has a ratio of 60% female and 40% male officers to reflect the need for a women's prison.
