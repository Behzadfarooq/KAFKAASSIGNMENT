Brown is the second album by rock group P.O.D.. The album was released on Rescue Records. It was initially released in 1996, and sold out quickly. It was remastered and re-released in 2000 by Diamante.

[edit] Notes

  • Tracks 1, 4, 5, 7, 9, 11 and 15 have no lyrics listed in the CD booklet, even though most of the tracks actually have lyrics.
  • Track 14 contains a sample of the N.W.A. song 'Gangsta Gangsta'; "And here's the plot, takin' niggas out with a flurry of buckshots".
  • This albums was commended in Alternative Press's list of being the top 10 most influential Hardcore Christian albums in a 2005 Issue also featuring Zao and underøath on the cover.
