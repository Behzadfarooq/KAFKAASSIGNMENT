Brotherhood is the first studio album released by group 3T. It was coproduced by their uncle Michael Jackson and was released on his label MJJ Music. It reportedly sold around three million copies worldwide. Several singles were released : the international hit "Anything", and the european and asian well-received singles "24/7", "Why" (a duet with Michael Jackson), "I Need You" (with Michael Jackson on backing vocals) and "Gotta Be You".

It is dedicated in memory of the band members' mother, Delores "Dee-Dee" Jackson.
