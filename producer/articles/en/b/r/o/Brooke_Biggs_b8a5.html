Brooke Biggs (born September 28, 1983[1] in Gainesville, Florida) is a pornographic actress and nude model.

Biggs entered the adult film industry in 2002 at age 19 and has appeared in over 184 films.[2] She has posed for many magazine layouts.[3] She has been featured on the cover of dozens of video boxes and has made films for well known adult film distributors like Vivid, Sin City, Legend, Simon Wolfe, Digital Playground and Private.[2] In 2004 she was nominated for the CAVR (Cyberspace Adult Video Reviews) MVP award. [4]


Brooke began her porn-career making web movies for her now defunct website "Mean Streets of Brook-Lynn" and "AOV" (a Canadian Amateur Site) in which she modeled (under the name Brook-Lynn) for webcam quality videos with a man who in the videos identifies himself as her boyfriend. The date of some of the content on the site is early 2001, before Brooke turned 18.[5]

Brooke's porn career almost ended before it even got going. In 2003, while shooting a hardcore scene with Vince Voyeur, Brooke refused to perform an oral sex act on Voyeur.
