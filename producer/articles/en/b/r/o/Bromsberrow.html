Bromsberrow

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Bromesberrow Heath is the largest settlement in the parish

Bromesberrow or Bromsberrow is a village and civil parish in the Forest of Dean district of Gloucestershire, England. According to the 2001 census it had a population of 411. The village is close to the meeting point between Gloucestershire, Herefordshire, and Worcestershire. The nearest town is Ledbury, about four miles north in Herefordshire.

St. Mary's Church is in Bromesberrow itself
