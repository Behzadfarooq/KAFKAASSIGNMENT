Ribbon for the Bronze Star Medal

  • The ribbon is 1 3/8 inches wide and consists of the following stripes:
  • 1/32 inch White 67101;
  • 9/16 inch Scarlet 67111;
  • 1/32 inch White;
  • center stripe 1/8 inch Ultramarine Blue 67118;
  • 1/32 inch White;
  • 9/16 inch Scarlet; and
  • 1/32 inch White.
