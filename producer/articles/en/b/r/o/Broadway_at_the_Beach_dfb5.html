Broadway at the Beach

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Broadway at the Beach is a shopping center located in Myrtle Beach, South Carolina. Since its inception in 1996, it has grown to be one of Myrtle Beach's premier shopping destinations. The site is built upon 350 acres, which includes 3 hotels, 11 nightclubs, 20 restaurants, 15 attractions, and over 100 specialty stores, all surrounding a 23-acre lake created from the drainage of the former Withers Swamp.

[edit] Restaurants

Many restaurants are located in and around the Broadway at the Beach complex. Included are:

  • The Crab House
  • Hard Rock Cafe
  • Joe’s Crab Shack
  • Jimmy Buffett's Margaritaville
  • Key West Grill
  • KISS Coffeehouse
  • Landry’s Seafood
  • Liberty Steakhouse and Brewery
  • NASCAR Cafe
  • Planet Hollywood
  • Tony Roma’s
  • Uno Chicago Grill

[edit] Attractions

Broadway at the Beach is home to a number of attractions. A listing of these Include:

  • Ripley's Aquarium, the first aquarium ever by Ripley's Entertainment
  • NASCAR Speedpark
  • IMAX Theater
  • Magiquest
  • The Palace Theatre, a performing arts center
  • Myrtle Waves Water Park
  • Carmike Cinemas
  • Dragon's Lair Putt-Putt Course

[edit] Marathon Usage

During the Bi-Lo Marathon in February, the park is used for the course for the Friday 5k run, and forms the second to fourth miles of the marathon course.
