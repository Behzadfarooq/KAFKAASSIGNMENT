[edit] Franchise history

Established by students at SUNY Brockport in 1999. Currently the Golden Eagles are governed by the National Collegiate Roller Hockey Association and compete in the Eastern Collegiate Roller Hockey Association (ECRHA) at the Division II level. The club has made steps of improvement since the beginning. The club has made two consecutive trips to the National Championships and has high hopes of returning this season.
