Broadcast Film Critics Association Awards 2005

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

11th BFCA Critics' Choice Awards

January 9, 2006

Best Picture:
Brokeback Mountain

The 11th Critics' Choice Awards are given on January 9, 2006 to honor the finest achievements in 2005 filmmaking.

[edit] Top 10 Films

(alphabetical)

  1. Brokeback Mountain
  2. Capote
  3. Cinderella Man
  4. The Constant Gardener
  5. Crash
  6. Good Night, and Good Luck.
  7. King Kong
  8. Memoirs of a Geisha
  9. Munich
  10. Walk the Line

[edit] Winners

  • Best Picture:
    • Brokeback Mountain
  • Best Actor:
    • Philip Seymour Hoffman - Capote
  • Best Actress:
    • Reese Witherspoon - Walk the Line
  • Best Supporting Actor:
    • Paul Giamatti - Cinderella Man
  • Best Supporting Actress (tie):
    • Amy Adams - Junebug
    • Michelle Williams - Brokeback Mountain
  • Best Director:
    • Ang Lee - Brokeback Mountain
  • Best Screenplay:
    • Crash - Paul Haggis and Bobby Moresco
  • Best Young Actor:
    • Freddie Highmore - Charlie and the Chocolate Factory
  • Best Young Actress:
    • Dakota Fanning - War of the Worlds
  • Best Ensemble Cast:
    • Crash
  • Best Animated Film:
    • Wallace and Gromit: The Curse of the Were-Rabbit
  • Best Family Film:
    • The Chronicles of Narnia: The Lion, the Witch and the Wardrobe
  • Best Foreign Language Film:
    • Kung fu (Kung Fu Hustle), China/Hong Kong
  • Best Documentary:
    • March of the Penguins
  • Best Picture Made For Television:
    • Into The West
  • Best Song:
    • "Hustle & Flow" - Hustle & Flow
  • Best Composer:
    • John Williams - Memoirs of a Geisha
  • Best Soundtrack:
    • Walk the Line
