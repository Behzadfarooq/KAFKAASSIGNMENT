[edit] About me

I'm an anal-retentive grammar nazi from Mankato, Minnesota. I suppose I could also be considered a WikiGnome, which probably stems directly from the former. In real life I'm an environmental health specialist with a past history that includes teaching high school science and degrees in English and biology education. Although I no longer work in the classroom, I maintain my teaching license and education is a big part of my current profession.

I'm married with three kids, two beagles, two cats, and a 55-gallon aquarium (currently in my office).
