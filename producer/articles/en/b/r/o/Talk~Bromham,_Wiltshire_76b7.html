[edit] Amenities

This is a revised version of the Amenities section. It still needs sources and citations as well some further revision and added information. If anybody could see to bringing this up to encyclopedia standard, I would be much obliged. :) (Dead-In-Horrorwood 21:16, 22 February 2007 (UTC))

[edit] Costcutters

Costcutters is a small local convenience store located at the Chantry. It's open every day of the week from early till late. The store also runs a DVD-rental service.

[edit] H.F Stiles & Sons

H.F Stiles & Sons is a family owned-and-run butcher shop served by it's own abattoir. The butcher shop is located in the High Street. The abbatoir also provides to other butchers outside of Bromham.

[edit] Post Office

The local Post Office is located on New Road and the proprietor is Dave Price.

[edit] Top Set

Top Set is the village's only hairdressers and is located at The Pound. The salon caters to both male and female customers.

[edit] Mark Wilkinson Furniture

Mark Wilkinson Furniture is the main employer in the village. The company produces high-quality kitchens and other furniture. Mark Wilkinson has two factories located at Jockey Lane and The Common and the head offices and showroom located at the Highstreet.

[edit] Bromham Growers

Bromham Growers is a family owned-and-run business. They package locally grown vegetables and supply them to markets and supermarkets.

[edit] V&P Collins Farm Shop

V&P Collins is another family owned-and-run business. They sell all their own produce at their Farm Shop located on Devizes Road and in various farmer's markets around the area.

[edit] Sandridge Farm House Bacon

Sandridge Farm House is a bacon factory producing bacon from their own pigs to traditional old-Wiltshire recipes.

[edit] Greyhound Inn

Situated in the Highstreet, the Greyhound Inn is a public house serving drinks and providing catering. They are supplied mainly by Devizes-based brewery, Wadworths & Co.

[edit] Oliver Cromwell Public House

Located in St. Edith's Marsh, The Oliver Cromwell is named after the military and political leader by the same name best known for making England a republic and leading the Commonwealth of England.

[edit] Westbrook Inn

Westbrook Inn can be found at Westbrook on the Melksham-To-Calne Road. As well as beverages the Inn also provides food to hungry patrons.

[edit] Smithy Clinic

A pysiotherapy Clinic located at Horse Pool. It caters to the needs of sport injury victims as well as muscular and back ailments.
