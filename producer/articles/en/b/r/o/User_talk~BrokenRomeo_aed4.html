[edit] Sign your comments

Hello. In case you didn't know, when you add content to talk pages and Wikipedia pages that have open discussion, you should sign your posts by typing four tildes ( ~~~~ ) at the end of your comment. You may also click on the signature button located above the edit window. This will automatically insert a signature with your name and the time you posted the comment. This information is useful because other editors will be able to tell who said what, and when. Thank you!--Crossmr 20:02, 22 August 2006 (UTC)

[edit] Stop abusing the hangon tag

The hangon tag is not to be left indefinitely on an article. Its to be placed solely while you write your reason on the talk page that you feel the article should not be deleted. You've done so already so stop putting it back on that article. An admin will read the talk page before deleting.--Crossmr 20:04, 22 August 2006 (UTC)
