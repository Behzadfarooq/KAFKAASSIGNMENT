[edit] brokeNbored

The History

brokeNbored was originally a Pop Punk Band from Chelsea Ma once the band was disbanded Enrique Orellana aka Rike decided to keep the name and promised himself to make this name a well known name world by whether good or bad. After much debate in 2002 Rike asked his longtime best friend and future roommate to join the band, Xavier Santa aka X joined the band as the Guitarist and Lead singer for the band. At this point in time in the end of 2002 bNb had only a guitarist and a bassist and were looking for a drummer of some sort to complete the band as a trio. After going through many drummers who just couldn't take the long days of no practice and video game playing bNb met a possible candidate named Oscar Castro aka Da O.C. B-Boy by day and rock with your thing out machine by night. After a couple of months together Da O.C. was drafted by the Armed Forces to protect the country. He left the band and was shipped off to the war and hasn't been heard of since. To this date the existing members of bNb misses their best drummer ever.

The Story Begins a summary of the first few episodes/issues Spoiler Warning

The members of bNb get a shipment in the mail of boxes, these boxes contained instruments and recording equiptment. Included in these packages was a letter from a man named Guy Nolastname that explained that he wanted to help them creat a band to help this world they lived in with good solid music. Little did they know what was to follow as they agreed to have Guy as their band manager.
