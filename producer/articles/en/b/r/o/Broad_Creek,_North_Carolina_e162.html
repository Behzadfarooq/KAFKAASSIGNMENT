[edit] Waterways and sounds:

Broad Creek, Gales Creek, & Bogue Sound

Broad & Gales Creek empties into Bogue Sound, which is separated from the Atlantic Ocean by Bogue Banks, part of North Carolina's barrier islands known as the Southern Outer Banks.
