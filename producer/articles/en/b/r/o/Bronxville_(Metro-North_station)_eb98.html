Bronxville (Metro-North station)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

You have new messages (last change).

The Bronxville Metro-North Railroad station serves the residents of the village of Bronxville, New York via the Harlem Line. It is 15.3 miles (24.6 km) from Grand Central Terminal.

As of August 2006, daily commuter ridership was 3,109 and there were 331 parking spots. [1]

This station is located in the Zone 3 Metro-North fare zone.
