[edit] Locations

Brooklyn is a borough of New York City.

In addition, Brooklyn is the name or part of the name of the following places:

In the United States of America:

  • Brooklyn, Connecticut
  • Brooklyn, Illinois
    • Brooklyn, Saint Clair County, Illinois
    • Brooklyn, Schuyler County, Illinois
  • Brooklyn, Indiana
  • Brooklyn, Iowa
  • There is a section of Baltimore, Maryland, called Brooklyn
    • Brooklyn Park, Maryland
  • Brooklyn, Michigan
  • Brooklyn was an early name for the settlement that eventually became Minneapolis, Minnesota.
  • Brooklyn, Ohio
  • Brooklyn, Wisconsin
    • Brooklyn, Dane County, Wisconsin
    • Brooklyn, Green County, Wisconsin
    • Brooklyn, Green Lake County, Wisconsin
    • Brooklyn, Washburn County, Wisconsin
  • Brooklyn Center, Minnesota (derived from Minneapolis, see above)
  • Brooklyn Park, Minnesota (derived from Minneapolis, see above)
  • Brooklyn, Portland, Oregon

In Canada

  • Brooklyn, Yarmouth County, Nova Scotia, a small village north of Yarmouth
  • Brooklyn, Queens County, Nova Scotia, a village of Liverpool

In South Africa:

  • Brooklyn, Pretoria, a suburb of Pretoria, Gauteng Province
  • Brooklyn, Cape Town, a suburb of Cape Town, Western Cape Province

In Australia:

  • Brooklyn, Victoria, a suburb of Melbourne
  • Brooklyn, New South Wales, a suburb of Sydney
  • Brooklyn, Tasmania
  • Brooklyn Park, South Australia

In New Zealand:

  • Brooklyn, New Zealand, a suburb of Wellington
  • Brooklyn Valley, New Zealand, near Motueka in the South Island

[edit] Popular Culture

  • Brooklyn is a Broadway musical.
  • Brooklyn is also the name of a character on the Walt Disney animated series Gargoyles.
  • In the anime series Beyblade, Brooklyn is an extremely strong Beyblader who uses the great power of his mind to energize his beyblade, Zeus.
  • Brooklyn is a term used in ten-pin bowling to describe a hit in the pocket opposite (left [pins 1,2] for right-handers, right [pins 1,3] for left-handers) from the one that is attempted.
