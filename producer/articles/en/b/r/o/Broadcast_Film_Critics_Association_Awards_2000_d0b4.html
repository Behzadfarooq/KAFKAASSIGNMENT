6th Broadcast Film Critics Association Awards

2001

The 6th Critics' Choice Awards, honoring the best filmmaking of 2000, were given in 2001.

[edit] Top 10 Films

(alphabetical)

  1. Almost Famous
  2. Billy Elliot
  3. Cast Away
  4. Crouching Tiger, Hidden Dragon
  5. Erin Brockovich
  6. Gladiator
  7. Quills
  8. Thirteen Days
  9. Traffic
  10. Wonder Boys
  11. You Can Count on Me

[edit] Winners

  • Best Picture:
    • Gladiator
  • Best Actor:
    • Russell Crowe - Gladiator
  • Best Actress:
    • Julia Roberts - Erin Brockovich
  • Best Supporting Actor:
    • Joaquin Phoenix - Gladiator and Quills
  • Best Supporting Actress:
    • Frances McDormand - Almost Famous and Wonder Boys
  • Best Director:
    • Steven Soderbergh - Erin Brockovich and Traffic
  • Best Screenplay - Adapted:
    • Traffic - Stephen Gaghan
  • Best Screenplay - Original:
    • Almost Famous - Cameron Crowe
  • Best Young Performer:
    • Jamie Bell - Billy Elliot
  • Best Animated Film:
    • Chicken Run
  • Best Family Film:
    • My Dog Skip
  • Best Foreign Language Film:
    • Crouching Tiger, Hidden Dragon, China, Hong Kong
  • Best Song:

"My Funny Friend and Me", Sting - The Emperor's New Groove

  • Best Composer:
    • Hans Zimmer - Gladiator and The Road to El Dorado
  • Best Inanimate Object:
    • Wilson the Volleyball, Cast Away
