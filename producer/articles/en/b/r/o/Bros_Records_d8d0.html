Bros Records is a Central European record label owned by the major record company Sony BMG Music Entertainment. The label is based in Germany and is the home to several successful chart acts, including E-Rotic, Maarja Kivi, Bad Boys Blue, Gracia, Chris Norman and Virus Incorporation. The label was also the home of the girl group Vanilla Ninja from mid-2004 to late 2005, before the group parted company with Bros on bad terms.

Bros Records is the primary home of all artists managed by David Brandes, who has achieved a large amount of publicity in recent years due to his actions, which include fixing chart positions and stealing material from other artist's music videos without their consent.
