Brother André CHS conducts classes for grades 9 through 12, with Catholic teaching included in the curriculum. It is named after Blessed Andre Fresette, known as Brother Andrew, a 20th century Catholic Brother in Montreal responsible for the building of the Saint Joseph's Oratory in that city. It is located in east-central Markham, and its feeder schools include:

  • All Saints Catholic Elementary School (CES)
  • Kateri Tekakwitha CES
  • St. Edward CES
  • St. Joseph CES (Markham)
  • St. Julia Billiart CES
  • St. Patrick CES (Markham)
  • St. Mark CES (Stouffville)
  • St. Brigid CES (Stouffville)

[edit] Features of the School

The restaurant hosted by hospitality students, equipped with a kitchen and a computer lab.

The school is renowned for its AP (Advanced Placement) and PACE program. The school is also unique to a cafetorium, a restaurant host by hospitality students named Chez Andre, and a school band.

[edit] Buildings of the school

One of Brother Andre Catholic High School's classroom on the lower floor.

There are three major buildings in the school, one is the main building, one is the remote building, and the other one is the portable. The main building and the remote building are permanent, separated by a parking lot, and the portable is remote.
