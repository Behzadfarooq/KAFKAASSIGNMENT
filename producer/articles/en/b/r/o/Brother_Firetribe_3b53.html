Brother Firetribe

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Brother Firetribe is an AOR band formed in 2002 in Finland. They describe their music as easy going rock. It is notable that they work without the addition of drums, an instrument that is usually seen as a must in heavy metal music.

The band have recently changed their name from False Metal to Brother Firetribe; however, the album is called 'False Metal'.

They take their name from an in-joke as a perfect description for their music is 'tennis heavy' and the name of a well known Finnish tennis player Veli Paloheimo translates into English as 'Brother Firetribe'. The album title 'False Metal' comes from a slogan coined by heavy metal group Manowar, 'Death To False Metal'.

They released their first album on Spinefarm Records on June 7, 2006.
