BRT Bangkok

BRT Bus Rapid Transit is a new transit system in Bangkok, Thailand. It will look like the TransJakarta. The plan has been approved to built 5 bus routes, the first one will be completed early 2008. The five BRT routes will cover just over 110 kilometres and cost nearly Bt13 billion. The routes can accommodate 50,000 passengers a day in the first year.

Bus Routes Kilometers Finished
Chong Nonsi - Rajaphruek 16.5km 2008
Don Muang - Min Buri - Suvarnabhumi 38km 2009
Mor Chit - Pak Kred 18.7km 2009
Min Buri - Sri Nakharin - Sukhumvit 107 25km 2009-2010
Bang Na - Suvarnabhumi 15.6km 2009-2010
