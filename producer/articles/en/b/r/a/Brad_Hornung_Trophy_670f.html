Brad Hornung Trophy

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Brad Hornung Trophy is awarded annually to the most sportsmanlike player of the Western Hockey League.

It is named after former Regina Pats player Brad Hornung who was paralyzed during a game on March 1, 1987.
