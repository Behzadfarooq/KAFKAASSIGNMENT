Brant Bjork and the Operators is the self-titled album by desert rocker Brant Bjork. Though, it's credited as "Brant Bjork and the Operators" it is basically his second solo album with Brant singing, and playing guitar, bass and drum. He is joined by friends to lend guitar and vocals.

[edit] Notes

  • "My Ghettoblaster" is Brant's first single to have a music video.
  • "Electric Lalli Land" gathers it's title from Jimi Hendrix's album Electric Ladyland. The song features Mario Lalli on guitar.
