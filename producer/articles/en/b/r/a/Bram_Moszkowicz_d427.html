Abraham (Bram) Moszkowicz (b. June 26, 1960, Maastricht) is an influential Dutch lawyer.

Abraham Moszkowicz, his father Max, and two of his brothers run the law firm (Moszkowicz, Moszkowicz, Moszkowicz & Moszkowicz).

Moszkowicz is also a crime editor for show business program RTL Boulevard. In 2005 Moszkowicz presented a talent show for young lawyers at AVRO television station, called De Nieuwe Moszkowicz (The New Moszkowicz).

Moszkowicz has had many notable clients, including the assassinated criminal Cor van Hout, former Surinamese coup leader Desi Bouterse and singer Patty Brard.

Moszkowicz resigned as lawyer for Willem Holleeder, who is suspected of the extortion of Amsterdam real estate magnates, and involvement with the murder of some of them.
