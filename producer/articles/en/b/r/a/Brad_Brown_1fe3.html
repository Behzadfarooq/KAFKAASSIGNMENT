Brad Brown (born December 27, 1975 in Baie Verte, Newfoundland, Canada) is a defenceman currently (2005) playing for the Toronto Marlies of the AHL.

[edit] Playing career

Brad Brown was drafted in the first round, eighteenth overall, by the Montreal Canadiens in the 1994 NHL Entry Draft. He has played for an assortment of teams in the National Hockey League including the Montreal Canadiens, Chicago Blackhawks, New York Rangers, Minnesota Wild, and the Buffalo Sabres.
