Brad Kotz

Bradley 'Brad' Kotz was a four-time All-American NCAA lacrosse player at Syracuse University from 1982 to 1985, with Kotz leading the Orange to the NCAA Men's Lacrosse Championship in 1983 and two additional appearances in the finals in 1984 and 1985.

Brad played professionally from 1989 to 1991 with the Major Indoor Lacrosse League Philadelphia Wings, winning two championships with the club.

Kotz was named the USILA player of the year in 1983, and entered the National Lacrosse Hall of Fame in 2001.
