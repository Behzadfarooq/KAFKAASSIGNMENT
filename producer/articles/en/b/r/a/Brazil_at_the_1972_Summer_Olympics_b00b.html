[edit] Medals

Brazil finished in 41st position in the final medal rankings, with two bronze medals.

[edit] Bronze

  • Nelson Prudencio — Athletics, Men's Triple Jump
  • Chiaki Ishii — Judo, Men's Half Heavyweight (93 kg)
