[edit] Where was he born?

The lead sentence describes Jones as being born in Armadale, but there are (at least) two places with that name in Australia, and one more place with a similar name differently spelled. I suspect that Jones was born in Armadale, Western Australia, but I would like an editor who knows for sure to make the change. Darcyj 23:28, 3 February 2007 (UTC)
