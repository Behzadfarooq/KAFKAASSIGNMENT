Brache Sparta F.C. are a football club based in Luton, England. They were established in 1960 and joined the South Midlands League in 1975. In the 1997-98 season, they reached the 4th round of the FA Vase. For the 2005-06 season, they are members of the Spartan South Midlands Football League Division One. They are most likely the subject of the song "Theme From Sparta F.C." by The Fall.[citation needed]
