Brandon Hancock (born June 13, 1983) is an american football fullback who played for the University of Southern California Trojans. He quit do to health risks from knee injuries.

[edit] College Career

He majored in communications at the University of Southern California. He is currently getting his Masters degree in communications and works part time at ESPN Radio 710 AM for USC Trojans games.

[edit] High School Years

Hancock attended Clovis West High School (Fresno, California) and was a student and a letterman in football. In football, he won All-League and All-CIF honors. Brandon Hancock graduated fromm Clovis West High School in 2002 as Class Valedictorian.
