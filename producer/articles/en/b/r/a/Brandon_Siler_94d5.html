Brandon Siler (born December 5, 1985) is an American college football player, currently the starter at middle linebacker for the Florida Gators. Siler has announced that he will enter the 2007 NFL Draft.

[edit] College career

A star at Orlando's Evans High School, Siler chose the Gators over Tennessee and Maryland. His impact came early, as he started six games as a freshman for Florida. His play during his junior season in 2006 helped the Gators win the Southeastern Conference for the first time in six years and earned him All-SEC honors from the AP. He also has a sibling named Devante Walton who he says motivates him all the time and gives him credit for his strong persona. [1]
