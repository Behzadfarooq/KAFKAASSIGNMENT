The word Brat has several meanings:

  • Spoiled brat, a child indulged and not disciplined.
  • Military brat, a person who grew up with one or both parents in the military. Because the United States is the only country that has widely studied these people as an identifiable demographic, there is a more comprehensive article on the American phenomenon at military brat (U.S. subculture).
  • Brat Pack, a group of young American film actors who came to prominence in the 1980s.
  • Da Brat, a rapper popular in the 1990s.
  • Brat, an isometric computer puzzle game released in 1991 in which you must guide your character safely through the level.
  • Brat and Brat-2, Russian crime films.
  • Bratz is the name of a line of dolls which started production in 2001.
  • Brats, a 1930 Laurel and Hardy film
  • An abbreviation of bratwurst.
