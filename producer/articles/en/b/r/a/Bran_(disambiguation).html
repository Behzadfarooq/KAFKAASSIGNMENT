Bran is the hard outer layer of cereal grains.

Bran may also refer to:

Places:

  • Bran Castle, a national monument and landmark of Romanian tourism
  • Bran, Charente-Maritime, a commune in the Charente-Maritime département in France
  • Bran, Braşov, a town in Romania, Braşov County, near Bran Castle

Characters:

  • Bran the Blessed, a character in Celtic mythology; his sister Branwen
  • Bran Mak Morn, the last king of the Picts in Robert E. Howard's fiction
  • The protagonist of The Voyage of Bran, a tale from Irish mythology
  • a tribe or region in Africa, mentioned in Spanish colonial texts from Latin America (origin of slaves)
  • Bran of House Stark in George R. R. Martin's A Song of Ice and Fire book series.
  • Bran Davies, also called the Pendragon, a main character in the last two books of Susan Cooper's The Dark Is Rising
