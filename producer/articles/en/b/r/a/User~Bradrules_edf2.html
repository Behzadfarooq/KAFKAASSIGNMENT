My name is Brad, and I rule.

I was born in Pittsburgh, but have lived in the San Francisco Bay Area since I was about five years old. I am part Lithuanian and part Sicilian, and used to be a competitive swimmer for about 15 years.

I am interested in sports, enough so that I used to be a sportswriter for a local paper until I quit because of awful pay. I also have experience in the banking and software industries, so a lot of my contributions here will probably end up being about sports, money and computers.

I set up a Wikipedia account months and months ago, but it was only recently that I started using it often, so I'm still getting the hang of a few things.
