Brad Vering

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Brad Vering is a United States Olympic Greco-Roman wrestler.

[edit] High School Years

Vering attended Howells High School in Howells, Nebraska and was a letterman in football and wrestling. In wrestling, he was a three-time State Champion.
