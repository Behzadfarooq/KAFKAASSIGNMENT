Bracknell is a parliamentary constituency represented in the House of Commons of the Parliament of the United Kingdom. It elects one Member of Parliament (MP) by the first past the post system of election.

[edit] Boundaries

Bracknell is based around the town of Bracknell and the Bracknell Forest authority. It borders the constituencies of Wokingham, Maidenhead, Windsor, Surrey Heath, Aldershot and North East Hampshire.

[edit] Members of Parliament

The current and only Member of Parliament Bracknell has had so far since the constituency's creation is Andrew MacKay for the Conservative Party.

  • 1997 — present: Andrew MacKay, Conservative
