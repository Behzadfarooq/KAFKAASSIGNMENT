Bradley "Brad" Carbunkle is a fictional character created for the Nickelodeon animated series My Life as a Teenage Robot. He and his little brother Tuck are Jenny Wakeman's next door neighbors and best friends. Brad is a typical teenager, and Jenny often looks up to him when she needs advice on how the average teen lives. Unfortunately, Brad often causes more trouble for Jenny than he's worth.

Brad once briefly joined the Skyway Patrol, but quit after being inundated with paperwork.

Although it is sometimes hinted on the show that Brad and Jenny secretly have romantic feelings for each others, the relationship between them is merely platonic.[1] However, it is somewhat evidenced at the end of the episode "Bradventure", where Jenny is congratulating Brad after he unexpectedly rescues her from a mad scientist. Also, in Escape From Cluster Prime, it's only when Brad (and Tuck) shows up that Jenny decides to go back to Earth even after how accustomed she's become to the Cluster's home planet (although it really could be just because she didn't want to leave all her friends)

In the pilot featured on Oh Yeah! Cartoons, Brad was voiced by Melissa Denton. On the series itself, he is voiced by Chad Doreck.
