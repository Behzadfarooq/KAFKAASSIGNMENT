Bramford Primary School is a primary school located in Woodsetton, Sedgley, which is in the West Midlands of England. It was opened during the 1950s to serve the expanding Woodsetton area and was recently expanded to include a new sports hall and several classrooms. The current head teacher is Mr. Len Hazlehurst, who was appointed in September 2006 to succeed the retiring Mr. Derek Noakes.
