Brad Lukowich (born August 12, 1976 in Cranbrook, British Columbia) is a Canadian ice hockey defenceman who plays for the New Jersey Devils of the NHL.

Lukowich was a 4th round selection, 90th overall by the New York Islanders in the 1994 NHL Entry Draft. On March 9, 2006, Lukowich was traded from the New York Islanders to the Devils for a third round draft pick in 2006. He has won 2 Stanley Cups in his career with the Dallas Stars in 1999 and with the Tampa Bay Lightning in 2004.
