Brampton Fire and Rescue

Brampton Fire and Rescue respond to fire calls in the City of Brampton in Peel Region of Ontario, Canada. It operates eight fire halls and co-oridinates with other emergency services in Peel Region and the Greater Toronto Area:

  • Mississauga Fire and Rescue
  • Toronto Fire Services

A list of fire halls and locations:

  • Hurontario Street and Steeles Avenue
  • Vodden Street East and Main Street North (Hurontario)
  • Mayfield Road and Highway 10 (Hurontario)
  • Rutherford Road South and Queen Street East (Highway 7)
  • Bramalea Road south of Balmoral Drive
  • Williams Parkway East and Chrysler Drive
  • The Gore Road south of Fitzpatrick Drive
North: Halton Hills Fire, Caledon Fire
West: Milton Fire Brampton Fire and Rescue East: Fire Services in York Region
South: Mississauga Fire and Greater Toronto Airport Authority Fire and Emergency Services
