Brandon Becker

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Brandon Becker (born April 22, 1980) is an American novelist known namely by his work titled, Life as a Dream.

The skiier Brandon Becker was born in 1986. He has appeared in such ski movies as First Light, Speshul, Breathe and Stop, and Teddy Bear Crisis, Push and Pull. He grew up in New Meadows, Idaho, and skiied his home mountain Brundage.
