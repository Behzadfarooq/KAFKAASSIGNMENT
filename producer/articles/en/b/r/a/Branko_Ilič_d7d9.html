Branko Ilič (born February 6, 1983) is a Slovenian footballer of Serbian origin. His position is defender. He can play as central defender or right full back. He currently plays for Real Betis in the Spanish La Liga on loan from NK Domžale.

On February 4, 2007 he played his first La Liga match for Real Betis against Athletic Bilbao [1]
