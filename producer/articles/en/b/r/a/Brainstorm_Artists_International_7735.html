Brainstorm Artists International (B.A.I.) formerly Broken Records was a record label founded in 1987 by Gene Eugene and Ojo Taylor. The label closed in 1997.

Located in Fullerton, California, B.A.I. became an important label in the development of the West Coast Christian alternative music scene. The label focused primarily on modern rock, rap, and hip-hop.
