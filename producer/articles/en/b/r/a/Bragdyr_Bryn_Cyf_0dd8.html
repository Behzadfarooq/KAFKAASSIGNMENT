An independent brewery founded in 2005 by Geraint Roberts and Euros Jones-Evans on a trading estate in Denbigh North Wales.

[edit] Brief Details

The brewery produces cask ale and from summer 2006 bottle conditioned ale.

[edit] Availability

The cask ales are available in the Denbigh area.

[edit] Beers

The brewery produces three regular cask ales and from summer 2006 two bottle conditioned ales.
