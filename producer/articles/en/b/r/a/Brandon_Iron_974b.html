Brandon Iron (born July 14, 1968) is a pornographic actor and director.

Iron was born in in Alberta, Canada. He now lives and works in California. Iron is the creator of two rough sex video franchises, Slap Happy and I Love it Rough.

Iron directed many movies for Platinum X Pictures. He created the lines for Baker's Dozen, Intensities in 10 Cities, A Good Source of Iron, I Love it Rough, Photographic Mammaries, Ten Little Piggies and 50 to 1. In some of his older work, he is credited as "Brandon Irons" or "Alek James Hidell". The latter name was an alias of Lee Harvey Oswald.

Iron has travelled and shot movies in Prague, Budapest, Montreal, St. Petersburg, Paris, London, Brussels, Hamburg, Berlin, Lanzarote, Barcelona, Maui, New York, Victoria, Ottawa, Boston, and Belgrade.[citation needed]

Braincash and Iron launched "covermyface.com", "spermcocktail.com" and "loadmymouth.com." in 2006.
