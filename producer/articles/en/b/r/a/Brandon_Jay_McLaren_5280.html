Brandon Jay McLaren (born in Vancouver, British Columbia, Canada) is a Canadian actor. He is best known by his role of Jack Landors, the Red SPD Power Ranger, on Power Rangers: SPD. He is currently dating fellow Power Ranger star, Emma Lahana. He was a guest star in the episode "Bloodlines" of Blade: The Series. Mclaren is of the African American descent.
