Bravo Airlines

You have new messages (last change).

Bravo Airlines is an airline based in Madrid, Spain operating long-haul passenger and cargo services between Europe and Africa. Its main base is Madrid Barajas International Airport[1].

[edit] Code data

  • IATA Code: BQ
  • ICAO Code: BBV [1]

[edit] History

The airline was created in 2004 and started operations in October 2006. It was founded with the aim of operating scheduled flights within Africa and connections between Europe and Africa. It has 400 employees[1].

[edit] Destinations

Bravo Airlines operates long-haul services from Madrid, Brussels and Paris to Kinshasa and Brazzaville. Flights connect with domestic and regional services operated by sister company Bravo Air Congo[1].
