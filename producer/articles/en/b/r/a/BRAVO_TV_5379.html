BRAVO TV is a German television program which aired from January 1985 till December 1986 on Sat.1 and from May 1993 till December 2002 on RTL II. In February 2003 the ZDF took over the show, but it was cancelled due to bad ratings in November 2004. The show was later revived by Pro 7 and aired again for the first time on November 5, 2005. The host of the show is now singer Ben.

The television show covers similar topics as the print magazine of BRAVO. It usually includes detailed music charts, prominent guests, sexual education topics and a music video selected by the viewers at the end of the show.

The following people have hosted BRAVO TV since 1993:

  • Kristiane Backer (1993-1995)
  • Heike Makatsch (1995-1996)
  • Jasmin Gerat (1996-1997)
  • Lori Stern (1997-1998)
  • Nova Meierhenrich with Kerstin Kramer and Florian Wahlberg (1998-1999)
  • Enie van de Meiklokjes (1999-2001)
  • Collien Fernandes and Sebastian Höffner (2001-2002)
  • Mia Aegerter (2004)
  • Ben (2005-2006)
  • Gülcan (2006-current)
