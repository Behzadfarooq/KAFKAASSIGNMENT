Brachypelma klaasi is a theraphosid spider that occurs in Mexico.

[edit] Appearance

The setae of this species have a uniformly rusty appearance. Adults have a body length of about 6 cm.

[edit] Name

Named in honor of Peter Klaas, the collector of the species.
