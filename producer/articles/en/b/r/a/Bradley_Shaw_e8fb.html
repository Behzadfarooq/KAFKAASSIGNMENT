Bradley Shaw (born February 13, 1983 in Christchurch) is a field hockey player from New Zealand, who earned his first cap for the national team, nicknamed The Black Sticks, in 2004 at the Champions Trophy against The Netherlands. He's an up and coming defender. Like his older brother Hayden, Shaw is a drag flick specialist. He has the ability to distribute the ball with accuracy, according to the New Zealand team management.

[edit] International Senior Tournaments

  • 2005 – Sultan Azlan Shah Cup, Kuala Lumpur
  • 2006 – Commonwealth Games
  • 2006 – World Cup
