Bravenet is a website, which was started in 1997. Its purpose is to provide free and premium web hosting and all the resources needed to build and make a web site for potential webmasters of all skill levels.[1]


[edit] Spyware

According to spywaredb.com, Bravenet.com leaves "dangerous cookies" on your computer, that "crucial data could be endangered or even lost."[2] Tenebril.com states aswell, that the Bravenet.com cookie is spyware, also identifying it as a "tracking cookie" [3]

[edit] Interface

Bravenet has had many major interface updates, 1 every 2 years up to 2003, and then another one every year after that (demonstrated by several examples to the left.) The current interface is a orange, green and silver colour scheme
