Braindance is a subgenre of jungle that was first coined together by Rephlex Records that started in the 90's. It was first used to describe Aphex Twin's style of music that includes:

  • Intelligent dance music
  • Gabber
  • Experimental music
  • Industrial

It was first coined by Rephlex Records as a response to the majority of people labeling Aphex Twin's music as IDM. It has only been used to describe music on Rephlex Records. Examples of BrainDance can be found on many Aphex Twin albums incuding Come To Daddy and Classics.
