Brandon Kroeger (1971-2006) was the original drummer for Nickelback. He was an original member of the band along with his first cousins Mike and Chad. His work is included on the albums Hesher and Curb. He was replaced by Ryan Vikedal who in turn was replaced by Daniel Adair, the current Nickelback drummer.
