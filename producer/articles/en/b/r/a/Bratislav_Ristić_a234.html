Bratislav Ristić (born 21 January 1980 in Niš) is a Serbian football (soccer) midfielder. Currently plays for Málaga CF in Segunda División.

He also played for FC Metalurh Donetsk (2003-06), Club Brugge K.V. (1998-2003) and FK Radnički Niš (1990-1998).

He also holds a Belgium passport.
