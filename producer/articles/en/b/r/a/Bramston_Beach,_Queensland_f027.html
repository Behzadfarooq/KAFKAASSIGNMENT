Bramston Beach.

Bramston Beach ( 17°21′S, 146°01′E) is a town and located in Queensland, Australia. Its location is about 60 kilometres south of the regional centre of Cairns. It is located 17 kilometres east of the Bruce Highway.

The Eubenangee Swamp National Park and Queensland's highest mountain, Mount Bartle Frere are nearby.

It is a popular spot with caravaners and is a good fishing location and has a slightly older than average population. The population of Bramston Beach is 260.
