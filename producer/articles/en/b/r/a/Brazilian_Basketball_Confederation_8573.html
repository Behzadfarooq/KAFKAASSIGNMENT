Confederação Brasileira de Basketball or BCC is the national confederation of Basketball for the nation of Brazil which belongs to FIBA. The Brazil national basketball team is currently rank 6 by FIBA. The current president of the BBC is Mr. Bozikis. There are 720 Registered Clubs, 36000 Licensed Female Players, 96000 Licensed Male Players, and 42000 Unlicensed Players. It was affiliated with FIBA in 1935.
