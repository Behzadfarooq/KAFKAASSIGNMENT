Bratz Kidz

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

You have new messages (last change).

Bratz Kidz are the child equivalent to the popular Bratz doll line. The dolls are 6 inches tall and are proportioned to be shorter than the normal Bratz. So far the Bratz Kidz only come in the female gender, but "Boyz Kidz" are rumoured for 2007.

[edit] Popularity and Publicity

The Bratz Kidz are marketed to represent the innocence of children that normal Bratz and Babyz do not have and are modeled after their main fanbase, eight to twelve year old girls. With long skirts and printed t-shirts, the kidz have separated themselves from the Bratz and Bratz Babyz lines.

[edit] Slogans

  • "The Kidz with a passion For Fun!"
  • "Bigger than Babyz! Gonna be teens! We're Bratz Kidz, we're in between!"

[edit] Price and Value

The Bratz Kidz were first sold at 15.99 for the original four, with the Kidz sisters 2-pack, as well as the playsets, sold for around $20 - $30 in the U.S.

[edit] Bratz Kidz Film

The Bratz Kidz movie is set to be released in Spring 2007 and will be 3-D animation. Like the other 3-D Bratz films it will be released straight to DVD and will most likely spawn a soundtrack and and extra merchandise. The film will be based on the Sleep Over Adventure line.

  • Cloe - Haley Ramm
  • Jade - Jodelle Ferland
  • Yasmin - Marlene Lawston
  • Sasha - Chantal Strand
  • Meygan - Britt McKillip
