The Brantford Civic Centre is a 2,981 seat arena in Brantford, Ontario. It was built as a Centennial Project in 1967. The arena hosted the Brantford Alexanders of the Ontario Hockey League from 1978-1984. The OHL all star game was also hosted here in 1982
The Brantford Smoke(1991-1998) of the CHL, Colonial Hockey League. Moved to Asheville until 2002.

The arena is home to the Brantford Blast of Major League Hockey, and the Brantford Golden Eagles of the Ontario Hockey Association. The arena will be the host arena when Brantford hosts the Allan Cup in 2008.
