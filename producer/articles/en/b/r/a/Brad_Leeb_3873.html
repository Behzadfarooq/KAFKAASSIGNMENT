Brad Leeb (b. August 27, 1979) is a Canadian ice hockey player, who currently plays for the Toronto Marlies in the American Hockey League.

Leeb started his career playing for the Red Deer Vipers in the Alberta Midget Hockey League. He also spent five seasons in the Western Hockey League. In 1999-2000 he was signed by the Vancouver Canucks as a free agent. While he was with the Canucks for two seasons, he only appeared in 4 games. The rest of the seasons he played for minor league teams.

In 2002-2003 he was traded to the Toronto Maple Leafs for Tomas Mojzis. He only played one game for the Maple Leafs. He was then sent to the Toronto Marlies, where he currently plays.
