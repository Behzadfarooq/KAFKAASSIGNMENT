Branch Ave is a Washington Metro station in Prince George's County, Maryland on the Green Line. It is the southeastern end of the Green Line. The Branch Avenue rail yard lies just beyond this station.

The station is located near the intersection of Branch Avenue and Old Soper Road. Service began on January 13, 2001.
