RE:Brand was a British documentary television program that aimed to take a challenging look at cultural taboos. It was conceived, written and hosted by Russell Brand and was shown on the digital satellite channel UK Play in 2002.

The episode "Nazi Boy" was recorded to have 0 views much to Russell's distress.[1]

[edit] Episodes

  1. "Dad Fight" - Brand challenges his father to a boxing match.
  2. "Nazi Boy" - Brand meets Mark Collett the leader of the Youth BNP.
  3. "Homeless James (Part 1)" - Brand invites a homeless man to live in his house.
  4. "Homeless James (Part 2)"
  5. "My Old Tart?" - Brand takes a pensioner away for a dirty weekend.
  6. "Eddie Kidd Rides Again" - Brand meets daredevil Eddie Kidd, who was left paralyzed after a stunt went wrong.
  7. "Wanky Wanky" - Brand challenges his own heterosexuality by masturbating a gay man in a pub toilet, but with some apprehension.
