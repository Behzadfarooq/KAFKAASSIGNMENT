Brad Halls is a software developer, percussionist, and cub scout den leader. He currently resides in Michigan with his wife Heather and 2 children, Emily and Cameron.

He is manager of the Teamcenter Visualization 3D Analysis group at UGS (formerly Unigraphics Solutions) in Southfield, Michigan.

[edit] Interests

[edit] Software Engineering

I manage the Teamcenter Visualization 3D Analysis group at UGS (formerly Unigraphics), in Southfield, Michigan. Our group was originally a part of Engineering Animation, Inc., before being purchased by UGS.

[edit] 3D Computer Graphics

Some of the products that my group works on are:

  • Measurement
  • Cross Section
  • Clearance
  • Parasolid integration
  • Path Planning
  • Swept Volume
  • Animation
  • Behaviors
  • Presenter
  • Direct Model (JT)
  • Appearance Editor
  • Immersive Display

[edit] Website Development

I develop & maintain websites for a hobby. Here are a few examples of my work:

  • Michigan State Spartan Marching Band
  • Waterford Coalition for Youth

[edit] Percussion, Drum Corps, & Marching Band

In my younger days, I played snare drum in the Michigan State University Marching Band and several Drum and Bugle Corps.

  • Spartan Marching Band (member, 1981-86)
  • Phantom Regiment (member, 1983-84)
  • Blue Devils (member, 1985)
  • Cavaliers (staff, 1988-92)
