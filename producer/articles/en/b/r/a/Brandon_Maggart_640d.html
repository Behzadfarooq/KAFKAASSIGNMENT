Brandon Maggart (born Roscoe Maggart, Jr. on 12 December 1933 in Carthage, Tennessee) is an American actor.

He appeared has half of "Buddy and Jim" sketches with James Catusi in the first season of Sesame Street, in 1969.

In 1970, he was nominated for a Tony Award for Best Performance by a Featured Actor in a Musical for his role in Applause.

Maggart is the father of actor Garett Maggart, singers Fiona Apple, and Maude Maggart, and writer/director Spencer Maggart
