[edit] Trivia

  • Brandon has been spotted on several occasions surfing with Kyle Grove on the beaches of Malibu
  • According to several sources around the Internet [citation needed], Boyd's ethnic background is suggested or listed as Irish, Scottish, Mexican, and Native American. This has yet to be confirmed by the singer himself.
  • Brandon's book, White Fluffy Clouds can be purchased at: http://www.brandonboydbooks.com/
  • "From the Murks of the Sultry Abyss", the follow-up to "White Fluffy Clouds," is to be released in December 2006.
  • Brandon's favorite drink is IBC Root Beer[citation needed]
  • Brandon's favorite colors are purple and black[citation needed]
  • Brandon is extremely open about his sexuality and has talked in interviews and magazines about his bisexuality[citation needed]
  • Brandon's first artistic love remains drawing. Before the band decided to concentrate on music full time, he and Jose had attended Moorpark College full time as art students.
  • Brandon's younger brother, Jason, was the lead singer of the rock band Audiovent.
