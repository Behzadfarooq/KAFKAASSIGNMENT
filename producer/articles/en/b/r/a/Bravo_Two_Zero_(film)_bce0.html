Bravo Two Zero is a 1999 television film based on a book of the same name by Andy McNab. The film covers the real life events - from McNab's perspective - of the British SAS patrol Bravo Two Zero, who were charged with finding Iraqi Scud missile launchers during the Gulf War. A previous film about the patrol, The One That Got Away, based on Chris Ryan's book of the same name, was broadcast in 1996.

Filmed on location in South Africa, the film features many local acting luminaries, and breathtaking locations.
