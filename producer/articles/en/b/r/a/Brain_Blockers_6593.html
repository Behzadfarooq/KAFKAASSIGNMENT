Brain Blockers is a 2005 horror film, directed by Lincoln Kupchak.

The movie Brain Blockers is a mad scientist/horror film about a college professor Edwin Craig who develops a potion that turns his students into blood thirsty zombies. It is up to a couple of grad students Timmi Cragg and Matt Shevin as well as a newspaper reporter Ned Liebl to uncover the secrets and stop his plan.
