[edit] About Me

I am a Louisville native. I was in the public school system from kindergarten until I graduated from Jeffersontown High School in 2005. I currently attend Bellarmine University here in Louisville, and am employed by Jefferson County Public Schools in the Security and Investigations Unit.

[edit] Areas of Interest

I got started on Wikipedia by contributing to articles related to The Sims 2. Currently, I spend most of my time with the Louisville WikiProject. My areas of concentration for that project are education and law enforcement. I added most of the information in the Jefferson County Public Schools (Kentucky) article and created the Louisville Metro Police Department article -- though they would not be as good as they are were it not for other editors. I'm currently working on a navigation box for Jefferson County Public Schools, as well as looking into a restructuring plan for the Education in Louisville category -- as an exclusionist, I would rather not see all these stubs on elementary schools.

I'm hoping to get the JCPS and LMPD articles up to the feature article level.

One of these days, I may decide to drive around the city and take pictures of all these schools so that there is something to put in these articles.

[edit] September 16, 2006 Update

I've added WikiProject Mathematics to my interests. I've created some graphics for geometry-related articles. I'm currently helping out on improving Polar coordinate system from GA to FA.

I'm also working on the Wikification backlog. I've wikified the following articles:

  • El Paso County Constable - 2006-09-16
  • Juvenile Delinquents Act - 2006-09-16
  • New York City Police Department Combat Cross - 2006-09-16
  • New York City Police Department Medal for Valor - 2006-09-16

[edit] For what it's worth

I've started contributing to the Mathematics Wikia, so I've taken up learning Latex. If you have any equation requests, let me know.
