Brandon Thomas

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

See also: Brandon Thomas (musician)

Brandon Thomas (December 24, 1850 - June 19, 1914), British actor and playwright who wrote the hit play Charley's Aunt (1892).

His full name was Walter Brandon Thomas. He died in London and is buried in Brompton Cemetery in West London.
