Brad Logan, or Brad Minus as he sometimes goes by, is a famous punk/hardcore guitarist from Los Angeles who also owns and operates Blacknoise Recordings. Brad was the frontman and guitarist for the band F-Minus but is most famous for his work in the group Leftöver Crack.

"Brad Logan" is also the title of a song written about him by Rancid, and featured on the Chef Aid Episode/Compact Disc from South Park.

He has roadie'd for hundreds of bands, including Rancid,Social Distortion and Alkaline Trio. He has also been a substitute guitarist for the Unseen and many other punk/hardcore bands .


He is married to Kansas artist and clothing designer Kristen Ferrell .
