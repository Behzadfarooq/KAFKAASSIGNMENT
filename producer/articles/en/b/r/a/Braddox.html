Location

Braddox (sometimes incorrectly spelt Bradox or Braddocks)is a townland in County Monaghan, Republic of Ireland. It is located approximately 2 miles from the County Armagh, Northern Ireland border.

Amenities

Amenities include a post office and a Presbyterian church with hall.

Population

The population consists mainly of cattle and sheep with some people and small animals

Tourism

Information can be found at the Monaghan Tourism office in nearby Monaghan Town. There are currently no guided tours.
