Braemar Elementary is a primary school for children from grades kindergarten to grade 7. Braemar has many sports programs for the senior grades in the school such as, basketball, volleyball and track and field. Braemar is situated at 3600 Mahon Avenue in proximity to Balmoral Junior Secondary School. Braemar is a bilingual school and is proud to have just created the late French immersion program for older children. There are currently 500 children attending this school and 43% of the student body are capable or will be capable to speak French at a very high level.
