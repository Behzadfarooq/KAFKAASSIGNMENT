Brasmex - Brasil Minas Express was a cargo airline based in Brazil.

[edit] Code Data

  • ICAO Code: BCA
  • Callsign: Brasmex

[edit] History

The airline was established in 2001 and ceased operations in February 2004.

[edit] Services

Brasmex operated cargo services from Belo Horizonte, Rio de Janeiro and São Paulo to Luxembourg, Manaus, Miami and Milan.

[edit] Fleet

The Brasmex fleet included 1 McDonnell Douglas DC-10-30 aircraft.
