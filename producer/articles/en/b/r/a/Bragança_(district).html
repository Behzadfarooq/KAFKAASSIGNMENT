The District of Bragança (pron. IPA: [bɾɐ'gɐ̃sɐ] or [bɾɐ'gãsɐ]) is located in the Northeast of Portugal. The district capital is the city of Bragança. Mirandela and Macedo de Cavaleiros are also important centres. Its area is 1,173 km².

The district contains 12 municipalities:

  • Alfândega da Fé
  • Bragança
  • Carrazeda de Ansiães
  • Freixo de Espada à Cinta
  • Macedo de Cavaleiros
  • Miranda do Douro
  • Mirandela
  • Mogadouro
  • Moncorvo
  • Vila Flor
  • Vimioso
  • Vinhais
