Brave Yester Days is a 2-disc best-of album from Katatonia. It contains the material from Katatonia's older era, mainly consisting of Doom/Death metal material, and a few depressive rock.

[edit] Included Material

This compilation features selections taken from the following albums:

  • Jhva Elohim Meth (1991-Demo)
  • Dance of December Souls (1993)
  • W.A.R. Compilation Vol. 1 (1994-Compilation)
  • For Funerals to Come (1995-EP)
  • Brave Murder Day (1996)
  • Sounds of Decay (1997-EP)
  • Saw You Drown (1998-EP)

Brave Yester Days also contains the track Untrue, a previously unreleased song from the Sounds of Decay Sessions.
