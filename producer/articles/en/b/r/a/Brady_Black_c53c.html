Brady Black

Brady Black (full name Brady Victor Black) is the son of John Black and Isabella Toscano on Days of Our Lives and the heir to Greek tycoon Victor Kiriakis.

He was played by Kyle Lowder from 2000 to 2005 until the character's departure with on-screen wife Chloe Lane (Nadia Bjorlin). Lowder was the only contract actor to ever play the role, but as a child Brady was played by recurring guest stars Alex, Max and Dash Lucero (1992 to 1994) and Eric and Brandon Billings (1994 to 1999).
