Brandon Spencer Lee Call (born November 17, 1976) is an American actor. He portrayed J.T. Lambert on the ABC sitcom Step by Step and Hobie Buchannon during the first season of Baywatch. Call also briefly endorsed the popular early 90's beverage, Boku.

Call was born in Torrance, California to Richard Call and Elyse Pollack. He has two sisters, Dee Anne and Tandi, and a brother, Dustin.

While leaving the set after a taping session for Step by Step in September 1996, Call was attacked by an unknown gunman. It appears that this was a random attack, and the gunman did not know whom he was attacking. Though he was shot in both arms, Call was not seriously injured, and he made a full recovery.[1]

Call currently resides in Vista, California where he owns and operates a gas station and car wash. It was Call's choice to put aside his acting career after Step By Step and live a quiet life with his daughter in California.[citation needed]
