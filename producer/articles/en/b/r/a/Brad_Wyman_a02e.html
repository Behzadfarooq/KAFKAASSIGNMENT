Brad Hibbs Wyman (born May 13, 1963 in Los Angeles, California) is an American film producer, best known for producing Monster. His first film was White of the Eye, and later worked on Freeway, Trees Lounge, The Dark Backward, The Chase, and Barb Wire. He currently lives in Los Angeles, California, with his wife Salomé Wyman, the director of Fast Sofa, and his two kids. He currently just finished filming Normal Adolescent Behavior in Rhode Island, and just finished Homo Erectus directed by Adam Rifkin, and Look, with Barry Schuler.

[edit] Work with Adam Rifkin

Wyman has been known to often work with director Adam Rifkin. His second film, Never on Tuesday was Rifkin's first film. He later worked on The Dark Backward, The Chase, and Denial among others with Adam Rifkin. He is also making two upcoming films with Rifkin (Homo Erectus, Look).
