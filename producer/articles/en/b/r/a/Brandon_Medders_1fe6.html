Brandon Edward Medders is a baseball pitcher for the Arizona Diamondbacks franchise. He was born on 26th January 1980 in Tuscaloosa, Alabama.

Medders pitched full-time out of the bullpen for the 2006 season. He was very effective and consistent for the majority of the season. 2007 looks bright for Medders, as he looks to improve upon his solid 2006 campaign.
