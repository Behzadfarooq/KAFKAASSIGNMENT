Brandon Bevan (June 8, 1988) is an Native American actor and martial artist. He was born in Los Angeles, California, and currently lives in Hollywood, California.

[edit] Biography

[edit] Career

Bevan has made his way up to Hollywood, California where he met a good friend who happends to be a Stunt Man and worked with Jackie Chan, Will Smith, Tom Cruise and many many more actors.


[edit] Personal life

Bevan's Is of Native American and his hometown is in Los Angeles, California. He began studying martial arts at the age of four. He has studied 13 different martial arts, including Taekwondo, Shenkito, Judo and Kenpo. A sports enthusiast, Bevan enjoys outdoor sports, especially football, soccer, hiking and baseball. He graduated from New Philadelphia High School in New Philadelphia, Ohio and also attended Maysville High School (Ohio) in South Zanesville, Ohio for a couple of year and He also attended Newark High School in Newark, Ohio.[verification needed]


[edit] Martial arts

Bevan is a 9th Degree Black Belt as of 2007 with more than a total of 15 years experience in the martial arts.
