[edit] Cleanup

I have provisionally tagged with Cleanup, article subject may or may not be truly noteworthy. The article reads far too much as a promotional piece - too much marketing speak such as "a uniquely-designed interactive game board" and "Brain Chain is an educational game well-designed for family play because the rules are simple; no one is eliminated, it is fast-paced, and because the winning chain length can be adjusted to allow for a game between 30 minutes and 90 minutes long". It appears the article author has now sprouted a child article, Brain Pill, which should frankly just be deleted - a board game counter is not notably enough to have a wikipedia article (unless it played a major part in saving the life of a head of state or something). In short, this whole situation looks decidedly spammy and marketing based - I will give it a couple of weeks to see if it changes, then I think it is fair to give it a run through AfD to get a wider editors view. SFC9394 01:25, 25 December 2006 (UTC)
