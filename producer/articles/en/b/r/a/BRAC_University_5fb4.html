BRAC University is a private university located in Dhaka, Bangladesh. The University was established by BRAC in 2001. BRAC University had its first convocation in January, 2006.

The University offers undergraduate courses in Computer Science & Engineering, Computer Science, Electronics &Communication Engineering, Architecture, Economics, BBA, English, Law and Physics. It also offers MBA, MBM, MDS, MPH and Post Graduate Programs in Disaster Management degrees.

BRACU a.k.a BU is located in 66,Mohakhali,Dhaka-1212. It has also another campus which is called TARC(Training And Resource Center) at Savar in Dhaka. Students of 1st semester need to live there throughout that semester only for the purpose of training, education, community living, etc. BRACU a.k.a BU called this semester as "Residential Semester".
