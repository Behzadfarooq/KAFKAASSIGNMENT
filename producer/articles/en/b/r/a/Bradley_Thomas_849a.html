Bradley Thomas is a professional footballer currently on loan to Boston United from Yeovil Town. Born in Forest Green, he is a defender standing at 6ft 2in.

His previous clubs include, Peterborough United, Kettering Town, Aldershot Town, Heybridge, Weymouth, Eastleigh and Tamworth F.C.
