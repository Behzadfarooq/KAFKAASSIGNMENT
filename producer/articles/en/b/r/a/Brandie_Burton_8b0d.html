Brandie Burton (b January 8, 1972, San Bernardino, California) is an American professional golfer.

Burton attended Arizona State University. In 1989 she won the United States Girls Junior Amateur Golf Championship. She was Rookie of the Year on the LPGA Tour in 1991. As of May 2005 she has won five times on the tour, including two majors, the 1993 and 1998 du Maurier Classics. Her best finish on the money list is 3rd in 1993. She was a member of the United States Solheim Cup team in 1992, 1994, 1996, 1998 and 2000.
