Here is a list of Bratz songs, some can be found on the Rock Angelz or Genie Magic CDs, others may be bonus songs in the other places, or can be in the Bratz's future album.

  • "Look Around" is a song by Christina Milian & Verbal from "m-flo", which is said to be sang with the Bratz.
  • "Show Me What You Got" is a song from the Korean singer's, BoA's remix album, Next World. In the music video Bratz appear in Tokyo as dolls and dancers, who are look only like Bratz.
  • "Bein' Who We Are" is an exclusive song in the doll collection, Live in Concert! This is the first song the Bratz girls themselves sang.
