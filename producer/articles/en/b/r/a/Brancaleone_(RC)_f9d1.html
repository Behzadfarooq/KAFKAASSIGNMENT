Location of Brancaleone in Italy

Brancaleone is a comune (municipality) in the Province of Reggio Calabria in the Italian region Calabria, located about 110 km southwest of Catanzaro and about 40 km southeast of Reggio Calabria. As of 31 December 2004, it had a population of 3,923 and an area of 35.9 km².[1]

Brancaleone borders the following municipalities: Bruzzano Zeffirio, Palizzi, Staiti.
