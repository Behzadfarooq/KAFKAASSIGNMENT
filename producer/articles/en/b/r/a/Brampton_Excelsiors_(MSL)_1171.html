The Brampton Excelsiors are Senior "A" box lacrosse team from Brampton, Ontario, Canada. The Excelsiors play in the Major Series Lacrosse Senior "A" Lacrosse League.

Brampton Excelsiors 1912 - 1941
Mimico-Brampton Combines 1942 - 1943
Brampton-Lakeshore Combines 1945
Brampton Excelsiors 1944, 1946 - 1960
Brampton Ramblers 1961 - 1962
Brampton Sealtests 1963 - 1964
Brampton Excelsiors 1965 - 1968
Bramalea Excelsiors 1969
Brampton Excelsiors 1970 to Present
