Brandon Choi is an American comic book author best known as one of the co-creators of the series Gen¹³ which enjoyed high popularity in the middle and late 1990s. He also wrote several other titles for Wildstorm Comics. Mr. Choi grew up in St. Louis, Missouri and is a long-time friend of famed comic book artist and Wildstorm founder Jim Lee.
