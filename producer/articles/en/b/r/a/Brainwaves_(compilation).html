Brainwaves (compilation)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Brainwaves
Compilation album by various artists
Released 2006 November 17
Genre experimental
Label Brainwashed Recordings
BRAIN 012

Brainwaves is a compilation album put together by Brainwashed.com to commemorate the Brainwaves festival. The album is a 3 CD set, limited to 500 copies (one for each audience member of the Brainwaves festival) and comes with a Brainwaves logo badge. This set contains many exclusive songs by the musical groups.

The sleeve is blank and so it comes without any track listing or other details printed on the packaging.

According to Brainwashed.com:
"Celebrating ten years of Brainwashed.com, Brainwaves is a three day event in November, 2006. Brainwaves the souvenir is a set of three CDs, ten songs each. These were made for the Brainwaves fest. There is no printing on the packaging whatsoever. It is intended for autographs."
