[edit] About User

[edit] General facts

I am 18 years old and live in Trondheim, Norway. I go to high school and study maths, physics and chemistry. In the future i want to get a doctor's degree and become a scientist, hopefully ending up developing something that becomes important to many people.


[edit] Interests

I enjoy playing computer games, especially MMORPG's like World of Warcraft and A3.

History has also been a subject that would interest me. Periods like the Roman Empire, the medieval age and World War II fascinate me, and of course, weapons used during these time periods are fun to learn more about.

[edit] Works on Wikipedia

I am a quite new user and editor of Wikipedia, and have so far only tested out the tools and gotten familiar with the setup. My first (and only) article is Mads Eriksen. As soon as I feel more at home here, I will probably edit spelling that I find as well as translating between Norwegian and English Wikipedia.
