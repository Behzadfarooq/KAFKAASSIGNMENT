Brandenburg state election, 1999

The Brandenburg state election, 1999, was conducted on September 5, 1999, to elect members to the Landtag (state legislature) of Brandenburg.

[edit] Post-election

SPD lost their overall-majority, but Manfred Stolpe (SPD) formed an SPD-CDU coalition and remained Minister-President.
