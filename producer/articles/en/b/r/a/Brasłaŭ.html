Brasłaŭ

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Braslaw (Belarusian: Браслаў; Russian: Браслав, Breslav; Lithuanian: Breslauja; Polish: Brasław) is a city in the Vitebsk Province of Belarus, an administrative center of the Braslaw district.

[edit] History

Town was first mentioned in 1065.
