The Bradenton Juice is a future team of the South Coast League, to be based in Bradenton, Florida. The team will not be affiliated with Major or Minor League Baseball. They will play at Robert C. Wynn Baseball Field, starting in the 2007 season.[1] The name "Juice" refers to the city of Bradenton's location as the corporate headquarters of Tropicana and to the orange groves in the surrounding area.
