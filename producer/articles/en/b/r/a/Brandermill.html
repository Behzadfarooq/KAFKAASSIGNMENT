Brandermill

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Brandermill is a major suburban residential and commercial development in the Southside of Richmond, Virginia. It is located near Midlothian, Virginia at the southern terminus of the Powhite Parkway and is centered around the Swift Creek Reservoir.

In 1977, Brandermill developers East West Partners (then known as "The Brandermill group [1] were recognized for their work when Brandermill was named "The Best Planned Community in America" by Better Homes and Gardens and the National Association of Home Builders. [2]
