Bradford Christian School is based in Bradford, West Yorkshire, England. It has been running since 1993 and is a private school. It keeps a high standard of discipline and moral standards with many lessons being taught around Christian principles. The headmaster is Phil Moon who helped found the school at the beginning. It helps sponsor trips to Romania every year for older students.

The starting point in Bradford Christian School, is the simple fact that God loves the individual child. The teachers seek to share that love in all they do. That love is inspired by faith in Jesus Christ who gave his life for us. From that faith springs hope, which becomes an anchor to the soul as we go through life. The school seeks to impart Godly wisdom to the children, giving them a Christian world view or a perspective on life and relationships. Bradford Christian School offers a lasting foundation to your child's life based on Godly wisdom and a Biblical presentation of knowledge and understanding.
