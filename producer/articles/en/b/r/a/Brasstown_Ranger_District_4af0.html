Brasstown Ranger District

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Map showing the ranger districts of the Chattahoochee National Forest

The Brasstown Ranger District is one of the five ranger districts of the Chattahoochee National Forest. The district includes land located in Lumpkin, Towns and Union counties, Georgia. Comprised of approximately 157,000 acres, the district's features include Geogia's highest peak, Brasstown Bald, and Sosebee Cove.

The headquarters for the Brasstown Ranger District is located in Blairsville, Georgia.
