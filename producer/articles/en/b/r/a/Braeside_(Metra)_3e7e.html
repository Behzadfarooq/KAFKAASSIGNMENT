Braeside (Metra)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Braeside Metra Station.

Braeside is a railroad station near Highland Park, Illinois serving Metra's Union Pacific/North Line. It is located at 10 North St. Johns Avenue, just off Lake-Cook Road. In Metra's zone-based fare schedule, Braeside is in zone E. Cook County Forest Preserves' Turnbull Woods and William N. Erickson Preserve are adjacent to the station, and the Chicago Botanic Garden is about a mile away.

The station consists of two side platforms and a waiting room, but does not contain a ticket agent booth. Northbound trains stop on the west platform and southbound trains stop on the east platform. Trains go south to Chicago's Ogilvie Transportation Center, and as far north as Kenosha, Wisconsin.
