Bradford North is a constituency represented in the House of Commons of the Parliament of the United Kingdom. It elects one Member of Parliament(MP) by the first past the post system of election.

[edit] Boundaries

This constituency covers the northern part of Bradford.

Following the review of parliamentary representation in West Yorkshire by the Boundary Commission for England, Bradford North has been significantly altered and will be fought at the next UK General Election as Bradford East.
