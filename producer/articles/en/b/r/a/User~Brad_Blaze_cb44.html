Well, this article is about me, so I think I'll describe myself a little. I'm a columnist on a wrestling website. and I run a website. However I don't really fit in with the typical Internet. I flat out refuse to use any of the slang crap on the Internet and I don't use Myspace or any other place like that. I absolutely detest Internet video because of its stupidity. This name is actually a psuedonym. I am confident, determined, and I don't back down from anybody in real life, traits that I know will get me to the top of whatever I choose to do. I'm also very level-headed. There's no better way to succeed than to remain calm. Here on Wikipedia, I am interested in expanding the content in areas of interest to me. I'm a wrestling fan and I believe strongly that non-fans should stay out of all Wrestling-related articles and let the fans sort the section out.
