Brandy Ledford

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Brandy Ledford (born February 4, 1969) is an American actress, model and former Penthouse Pet of The Year, best known for her role as Doyle in the science fiction TV series Andromeda. She is sometimes credited as Jisel (the name she used when she appeared as a Penthouse Pet) or Brandy Sanders (her name when she was married to Damian Sanders, one of the pioneers of freestyle snowboarding). Ledford is a devout Christian who describes Jesus as "the greatest love of my life".[1]

In addition to Andromeda, Ledford starred in Season 10 of Baywatch Hawaii, and the SciFi Channel series, The Invisible Man; she has also guest starred in a number of other science fiction TV series.

Ledford was born in Denver, Colorado, but moved to Redondo Beach, California at the age of four. She has also lived in: Utah; Japan; Huntington Beach, California; Lake Tahoe, California; New York; Toronto, Ontario; Hawaii; San Diego, California; Vancouver, British Columbia; and Los Angeles County, California. She is five feet and six inches tall, has blond hair, green eyes and weighs 120 pounds (54 kg). She loves snowboarding, reading, watching movies, shopping, travel and kickboxing. She also likes to watch tennis, football and hockey.

Before she became a Christian, Brandy appeared in explicit solo and girl-girl pictorials in Penthouse, Genesis, Club International, and Hustler, as well as other adult men's publications. She also appeared as part of a threesome in a home-made hardcore porn film with musician Vince Neil and porn actress Janine Lindemulder that was distributed without her knowledge or consent. After her adult entertainment career, she made several non-pornographic adult films before establishing herself as a mainstream television actor.

She is reported to have a pictorial calendar coming out for 2007.[2]
