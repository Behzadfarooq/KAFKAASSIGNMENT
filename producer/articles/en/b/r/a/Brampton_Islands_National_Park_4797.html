Brampton Islands National Park

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Brampton Islands is a national park in Queensland, Australia, 834 km northwest of Brisbane. The park covers much of Brampton Island and all of Carlisle Island.

[edit] Fact sheet

  • Area: 10 km²
  • Coordinates: 20°47′15″S, 149°17′08″E
  • Date of establishment: 1968
  • Managing authorities: Queensland Parks and Wildlife Service
  • IUCN category: II
