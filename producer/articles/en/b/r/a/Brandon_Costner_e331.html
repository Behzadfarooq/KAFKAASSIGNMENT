Brandon Costner is an American collegiate basketball Power Forward for the NC State Wolfpack basketball team. He sat out his first season because of an injury. 2007 was not technically his first season but it was till his freshman season as he was redshirted. His highest scoring game was in the 2007 ACC Tournament against Duke. Brandon Costner is the son of NBA player Tony Costner.
