[edit] People

See Bray (surname)

[edit] In England:

  • Bray, a village near Maidenhead, Berkshire, England [1]. Its name probably comes from Old French bray or bray meaning "marsh". It is home to two of the top twenty restaurants in the UK according to Restaurant magazine. One is Heston Blumenthal's The Fat Duck, the other is the Roux brothers' Waterside Inn.
