Braemar Hill (Traditional Chinese: 寶馬山) is a hill and a residential area at the south of North Point on the Hong Kong Island, Hong Kong.

The hill is largely residential. Choi Sai Woo Park (賽西湖公園) is the major recreation park on the hill. A private tertiary institution Hong Kong Shue Yan University along with Chinese International School are erected high on the hill. Besides residential uses, this area has a lot of schools, in which some of them is famous among the district.

[edit] Education

As the area is surrounded by many schools, the above bus routes 81A, 81S and 529P are the Special School Routes. Routes 23B, 25, 27, 41A and 529 also provide Special Routes.
