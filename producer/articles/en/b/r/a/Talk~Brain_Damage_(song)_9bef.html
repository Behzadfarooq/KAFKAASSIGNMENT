[edit] Speculation

Roger Waters sings this song, probably because he was beginning to feel that it was important that he should sing the songs he wrote.

I removed the above speculative text from the article because it doesn't sound factual to me, however, I have a feeling that its addition was done in good faith, and I do think it might be appropriate to acknowledge this in some manner.
