Bray lock, Berkshire

Bray lock is a lock and weir situated on the River Thames near Bray and Maidenhead, Berkshire, England. It is owned and managed by the Environment Agency. The lock is located on the northen bank, with the lock keepers cottage on an island between the lock and the weir. It is a manned lock.
