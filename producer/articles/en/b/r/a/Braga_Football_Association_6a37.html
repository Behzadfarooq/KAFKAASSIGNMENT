Braga Football Association

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Braga Football Association (Associação de Futebol de Braga, abrv. AF Braga) is the district governing body for the all football competitions in the Portuguese district of Braga. It is also the regulator of the clubs registered in the district.

[edit] Notable clubs in the Braga FA

  • Sporting de Braga
  • Vitória de Guimarães
  • Gil Vicente FC
  • Moreirense FC
