Brazilian Order of Scientific Merit

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Great Cross of the National Order of Scientific Merit

The Ordem Nacional do Mérito Científico (National Order of Scientific Merit, in Portuguese language) is a honor bestowed upon Brazilian and foreign personalities recognized for their scientific and technical contributions to the cause and development of science in Brazil.
