Brazosport Independent School District is a school district based in Freeport, Texas (USA).

The district serves the cities, towns, and villages of Clute, Freeport, Jones Creek, Lake Jackson, Oyster Creek, Quintana, Richwood, and Surfside Beach.

It was formed in the year 1944 when all the existing school districts merged into one.

Brazosport Community College serves communities served by Brazosport ISD.
