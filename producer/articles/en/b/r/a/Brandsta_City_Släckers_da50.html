Brandsta City Släckers is a swedish band with some firefighters who has becomed very popular in Sweden.

The band singed "Kom och ta mig" in Melodifestivalen 2002 and they also performed in Melodifestivalen 2003 with "15 minuter". The groupmembers usally work as firefighters and perfrom dressed as such. They also received the next highest vote from the televoting audience in melodifestivalen 2002.

Leadsinger Glenn Borgkvist has also done a duet with Lotta Nilsson in Melodifestivalen 2004, and hosted the rescue show "På liv och död" on TV4.
