Bragadiru is a town in the southwestern part of Ilfov County, Romania.

[edit] Demographics

According to the 2002 census, Bragadiru had a population of 8,165, of which 98.19% were Romanians, 1.45% were Roma, and 0.36% of other ethnic backgrounds.
