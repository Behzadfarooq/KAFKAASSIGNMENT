Branislav Šoškić (born 19 November 1922, Gusinje, Montenegro) was the President of the Socialist Republic of Montenegro's Presidency in 1985-1986. He was a member of the League of Communists of Montenegro. Noted economist, he is member of Montenegrin Academy of Sciences and Arts, whose president he was from 1981 until 1985. He currently resides in Belgrade,Serbia.
