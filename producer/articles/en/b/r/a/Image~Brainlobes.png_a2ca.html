Diagram showing the lobes of the human cerebral cortex and the cerebellum (blue). The brain is seen from the right side, the front of the brain (above the eyes) is to the right.

Key:
Color Item Location on diagram
yellow occipital lobe leftmost
orange parietal lobe top-left
red frontal lobe rightmost
green temporal lobe third from bottom, approximately central
black brain stem bottommost
blue cerebellum second from bottom
