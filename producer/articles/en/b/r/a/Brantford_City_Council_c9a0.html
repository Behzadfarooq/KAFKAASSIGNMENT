The Brantford City Council is the governing body of Brantford, Ontario.

The council consists of the Mayor of Brantford and 10 Councillors(2 per ward)

[edit] Members

  • Mayor - Mike Hancock
  • West Brant, Downtown, Ward 1 - Jennifer Kinneman and Mark Littell
  • North Brant, Ward 2 - Vince Bucci and John Sless
  • Brier Park, Ward 3 - Greg Martin and Dan McCreary
  • Branlyn Community, Ward 4 - Richard Carpenter and James Calnan
  • Eagle Place, Ward 5 - John Bradford and Marguerite Ceschi-Smith
