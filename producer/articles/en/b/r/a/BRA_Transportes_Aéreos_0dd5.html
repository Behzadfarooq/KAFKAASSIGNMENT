BRA (Brasil Rodo Aéreo) Transportes Aéreos is a low-fare airline based in São Paulo, Brazil, operating both domestic and international services and charter flights. Its main base is Guarulhos International Airport[1]. BRA is the third largest airline in Brazil with 4.19% of the domestic Brazilian market, as of August 2006[citation needed]

[edit] History

The airline was established and started operations in 1999. It was formed as the domestic charter airline Brasil Rodo Aéreo by Panexpress Viagens e Turismo and transformed itself into a low-fare airline from March 2006 rebranded as BRA Transportes Aéreos[1]
