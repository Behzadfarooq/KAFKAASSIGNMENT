Bradd W. Szonye is a software engineer, artist, musician, and gamer. He was awarded the degree of Bachelor of Science in Engineering (Computer Engineering) by the University of Michigan, Ann Arbor in 1993. He was born in Michigan and lived near Detroit until 1998. He currently lives in San José, California with his daughter.
