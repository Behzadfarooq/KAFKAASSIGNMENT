Brad Is Dead was a rare demo 7 inch vinyl EP by Five Iron Frenzy. The title of the record derives from their website's fan forum, where a rumor started that their trumpet player Brad had died, after Reese said he had when Brad wasn't able to play a show. The rumor later spawned FIF songs such as "The Untimely Death of Brad," and parts of "That's How the Story Ends."
