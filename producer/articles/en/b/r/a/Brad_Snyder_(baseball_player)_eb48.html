Brad Snyder (baseball player)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Bradley Michael Snyder (born May 25, 1982 in Sandusky, Ohio) is a Major League/Minor League baseball player. He made his debut on a Major League roster as a September 2006 call-up with the Cleveland Indians. He attended Ball State University from 2002 through 2003. During his 111 NCAA games, Snyder hit 27 homeruns with an aluminum/metal/composite material bat.
