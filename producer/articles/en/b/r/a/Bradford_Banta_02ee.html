Dennis Bradford Banta, sometimes called Brad Banta (born December 14, 1970 in Baton Rouge, Louisiana), is a retired National Football League tight end.

[edit] Professional career

Banta played from 1994 to 2004 for the Indianapolis Colts, New York Jets, Buffalo Bills and Detroit Lions.

[edit] College career

Banta graduated from the University of Southern California in 1994.

[edit] Personal

His father Jack Banta also played for the USC Trojans and in the NFL.
