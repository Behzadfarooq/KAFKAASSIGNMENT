Brandon Barnes is an American musician and producer from Denver, Colorado. He is best known as the drummer for the hardcore punk band, Rise Against. Brandon began studying music in university, and quickly realized this was definitely what he wanted to do with his life. He played in his school's jazz band and also took private lessons. Through the years, he managed to develop his own unique drumming style, compiled by the influence of his drumming heroes such as: Neil Peart, Terry Bozzio, and Dave Lombardo. His sensational drumming brings the mighty Rise Against to a new level of music.


[edit] Equipment

While touring in the United States, he uses Pro-Mark drumsticks, Tama drums, Evans heads, and cymbals from both Sabian and Zildjian.
