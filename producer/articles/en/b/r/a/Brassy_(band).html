Brassy (band)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

You have new messages (last change).

Brassy is a band which features Muffin Spencer, the sister of Jon Spencer (of Jon Spencer Blues Explosion) as its lead vocalist. The band has released two albums to date: Gettin' Wise and Got It Made.

[edit] Members

  • Muffin Spencer - vocals, guitar
  • Stefan Gordon - guitar
  • Karen Frost - bass
  • Jonny Barrington - drums
