Brayan E. Peña (born January 7, 1982 in Havana, Cuba) is a back-up catcher for the Atlanta Braves. He was called up to the Majors early in the 2005 season because of an injury to Eddie Pérez. He was a member of the Cuban National 18 and under team as a youth. He graduated from Espa Julio Trigo Junior College in Costa Rica.
