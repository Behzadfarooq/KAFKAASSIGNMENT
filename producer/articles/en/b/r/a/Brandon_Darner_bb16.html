Brandon Darner

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Brandon Darner (born February, 1977), was a replacement percussionist for Slipknot. He was recruited after the firing of Greg Welts, which left Slipknot in need of a second percussionist. He served as Joey Jordison's drum tech and Mick Thomson's guitar tech. He was replaced by Chris Fehn, current member. Darner did have short success with (Slipknot founder) Shawn Crahan's other band To My Surprise, but has now left the band. He is currently a guitarist in Iowa based band The Envy Corps.
