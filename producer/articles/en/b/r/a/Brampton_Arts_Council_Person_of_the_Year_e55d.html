Brampton Arts Council/Person of the Year

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Brampton Arts Council Arts Person of the Year is a prize to honour the Brampton, Ontario citizen who exemplifies continued patronage to any sector of the arts: visual, dance, dramatic or musical.

[edit] Past Winners Include

  • 2005 - Alan & Sylvia Gibson
  • 2004 - John Cutruzzola
  • 2003 - Paulette Murphy
  • 2002 - Marion Fralick-Bartlett
  • 2001 - David & Catherine Harmsworth
  • 2000 - Rhoda Begley
  • 1999 - Conrad Mieschke
  • 1998 - Peter Richards
  • 1997 - Alan Heatley & John Setterfield
  • 1996 - Jan Stapleton
  • 1995 - Gail & Ken Watson
  • 1994 - Andrew Dittgen
  • 1993 - Joseph Gomes
  • 1992 - Keith Moreau
  • 1991 - Moira Lumley
  • 1990 - L. Greta Capps
  • 1989 - Jack Reid
