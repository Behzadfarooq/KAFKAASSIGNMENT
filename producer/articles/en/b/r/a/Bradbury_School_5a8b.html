The Bradbury School of Hong Kong is one with a environment which provides children with an all-round education of the very highest quality, where joyful learning is important. The caliber of all the staff is positive, encouraging an approach in classrooms, which produces outstanding results. Children at the Bradbury School are encouraged to become independent thinkers who are curious about the world around them, and are taught to develop the skills to become learners and leaders in a 21st century global environment.
