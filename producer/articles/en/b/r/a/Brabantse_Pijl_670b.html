The Brabantse Pijl (English:Brabant Arrow, French:Flèche Brabançonne) is an annual road bicycle race held annually in Flemish Brabant, Belgium. Since 2005, the race has been organised as a 1.1 event on the UCI Europe Tour. Edwig Van Hooydonck holds the record for most wins in the race with four between 1987 and 1995.
