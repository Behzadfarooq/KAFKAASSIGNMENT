Bragador is a nickname created for Dungeons and Dragons in the mid 90s by me :D The full name is actually Bragador Delekhan and is a berserker I played for 2 or 3 games only. Since I thought the name sounded "cool", I decided to keep it for my online life.


[edit] About Me

I'm a graduate student in psychology. I'm extremely curious so I tend to read tons of scientific magazines and follow what is happening in the world. I'm also an adventurer at heart so I love to trek. Let's just say I want to learn everything and see everything.
