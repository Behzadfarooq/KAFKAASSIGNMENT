Bratz Babyz is a brand name under which a series of dolls are made.

It is a subset of the main Bratz line in which all of the dolls are babies. The brand is split into two lines, the regular Bratz Babyz and the Bratz Big Babyz (which include a "birth certificate" and other accessories).

[edit] Bratz Babyz

In 2004, the first Bratz Babyz were made. The babyz were Cloe, Sasha, Yasmin and Jade (the first Bratz.) The dolls came with a changing bag, a nap blanket, bottle on a chain and a pet based on the girls nicknames (e.g. Sasha's nickname is Bunny Boo, so she has a bunny.) Later on, in 2005, Meygan, Fianna, Cameron and Dana join the Bratz Babyz group.

In 2005, they released the 'Hair Flair' collection. Instead of having plastic hair, the dolls had real hair like regular Bratz dolls. Of course, this hair could not come off.

[edit] Bratz Triiiplets

Oriana is a rock star with a guitar. She is Punk. Valentina is a fasion designer. She is Pretty. Siernna is a tennis player. She is Kickin'. A 3-in-1 doll pack all with each a different flavor and attitude.

[edit] Bratz Big Babyz

Soon after, they made Bratz Big Babyz, dolls about 5 times the size of regular Babyz dolls. In 2006 the Bratz Big Babyz Bubble Trouble Dolls have been appeared, that laughed, burped, and blew bubble when you fed them soda. A half year after the Bratz Babyz Hair Flair, the Big Babyz has the realistic hair version, "Bratz Big Babyz Hair Flair".

[edit] Itsy Bitsy Bratz

Itsy Bitsy Bratz are the smallest version of Bratz Babyz.

[edit] Controversy

The Bratz Babyz have been the target of complaints from some parents about the sexualization of children's toys, especially the "Babyz Night Out" fashion pack, the "Brattoo Parlor" playset, and the fact that the Big Babyz wear something under their skirts that looks like a thong (which actually serves the functional purpose of keeping the skirt from riding up and over the doll's hips).

[edit] Alleged Profanity

On Christmas Day 2006, Miami resident Kristina Acre received the Jade Singing Bratz doll as a gift from Santa Claus. According to her father, Luis Acre, Kristina made him aware of that the doll was saying "lots of bad words". According to Luis Acre these include "the f- and b-words". [1] Later MGA Entertainment responded on their website about the report. The actual lyrics, which do not contain profanity, are posted on the Bratz.com website in the Videos & Music section under Bratz Babyz The Movie - Music.

[edit] Bratz Babyz: The Movie (2006)

In September 2006, Bratz Babyz: The Movie was released. The collection was made up of a lot of dolls. First of all, in the regular Babyz collection, there were Babyz Lil' Dancers. These dolls danced when you clapped your hands, played music, tapped or snapped, or pressed the button on the stand. They also had a concert stage that played music from the movie. In Big Babyz, they had Dancing and Singing Karaoke Babyz. These dolls came with a Karaoke machine that played all 5 songs from the Babyz Movie. A redhead, green eyed skateboarding boy also was released, named Harvey. It was his first appearance. A video game of the movie was released for PC and GameBoy Advance (not yet released).
