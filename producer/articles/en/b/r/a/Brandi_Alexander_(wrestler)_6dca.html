Brandi Alexander is a professional wrestler. During her decade long plus career, she has wrestled in many wrestling federations such as, WWE, WCW, NWA, AWA, WLW, PGWA, FWA, CWF, and LAW among others.

[edit] Championships & accomplishments

  • 1-time FWA Women's Title
  • 1-time CWA Continental Women's Title
  • 2-time SSW Women's Championship
  • 1-time ICW Women's Title
  • 1-time APW Cruiserweight Champion
  • 1-time LAW Intercontinental TV Title
  • 1-time UCW Women's Title
  • 1-time CWA Women's Title
  • 1-time NEW Women's Title
