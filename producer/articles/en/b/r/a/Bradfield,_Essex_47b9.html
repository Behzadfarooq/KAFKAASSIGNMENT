Bradfield is a village and civil parish in Essex, England. It is located about five kilometers (3 miles) east of Manningtree and is fifty-one kilometers (31 miles) northeast from the county town of Chelmsford. The village is in the district of Tendring and the parliamentary constituency of North Essex. There is a Parish Council.[1] Its population is 1,000.[citation needed]

The church is dedicated to Saint Lawrence.

The vilage is close to the River Stour.
