Branko Tomovic

Branko Tomović (Serbian cyrillic: Бранко Томовић) (born June 17, 1977 in Münster) is a German-born actor of Serbian extraction.

Tomović studied acting at the prestigious Lee Strasberg Theatre Institute in New York City and was first seen on the screen in the lead role of the film Remote Control, produced by the American Film Institute which was among the finalists for a Student Academy Award in 2002. In 2007, he will appear in the new Ken Loach feature These Times and The Bourne Ultimatum, directed by Paul Greengrass. He currently lives in London.
