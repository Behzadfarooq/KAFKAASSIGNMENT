Bramcote Park Business & Enterprise School is a mixed state school in the county of Nottinghamshire in the East Midlands. It teaches children from 11 to 16 - Years 7-11. It is located in Bramcote. The headteacher is Chris Teal.

[edit] History

[edit] Business Status

Bramcote Park became a business and enterprise school in 2004. Since then many subjects have improved, including ICT, Art, Science and History.

[edit] New Logo

Soon after the school got its business status, it announced a new logo, consisting of red, white and blue.

[edit] Federation

As of 2007 the school is part of a three school federation, aimed at sharing staff and resources.
