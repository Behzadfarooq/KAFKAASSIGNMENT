BrainPOP

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

BrainPOP web site

BrainPOP is an educational website with Flash based videos about math, technology, health, science, social studies, arts & music, and English. Most of the videos feature the site's recurring characters, Tim and Moby.

[edit] About the site

On the site, students can watch the BrainPOP movies and exchange messages with the cast of Brainpop characters. There are also interactive quizzes and games, comic strips, and experiments. The bulk of content is available only by subscription, although there are a number of free movies.

About 25% of U.S. school districts subscribe to BrainPOP and use it with school curricula as it aligns to state standards. BrainPOP has won multiple education and media awards including The Flash Film Festival District Administration Curriculum Award and Forbes Best of the Web.

[edit] Characters

[edit] Tim

Tim

Tim (full name Timothy) is a smart and useful teenager who does most of the talking in the movies. He has brown hair and usually wears a white T-shirt with a picture on it which relates to the movie he's in. In a voting video it is implied that Tim and Moby live in New York.

Tim is twelve years old, although he sounds much older (around 16 years of age). According to a movie about immigration, he is of Welsh-Japanese descent. It is interesting to note that he is left-handed.

His birthday is October 31st, according to his ID during the movie about blood types.

In the Dyslexia movie it is hinted that Tim has Dyslexia.

In the Braces movie, it shows he owns a retainer.

He thinks it's freaky how the letters he gets in the movies reflect what's going on in his life, as he notes in the computer viruses movie.

Tim's favorite sport is baseball, as he himself mentions in the Baseball movie.

[edit] Moby

Moby

Moby is an orange robot who communicates in bleeping noises (similar to R2D2). He is Tim's friend but loves to drive him insane. While Tim does most of the talking during a movie, Moby usually just stands to the side and helps out by fetching things for Tim, asking questions, and so on. As a robot, he can do lots of things that Tim is unable to do (e. g. changing his hand into a freeze-ray, sending himself back in time). Most of the movies end humorously, often with Tim getting angry with Moby or vice versa. It is occasionally implied that Moby is from an extrasolar planet. Sometimes Moby says too much about the subject and erases Tim's memory. There is a part when he has a clone brother in the Twins movie.

[edit] Cassie and Rita

Cassie and Rita

Cassie and Rita are two crazy teenage girls, who are best friends and are mainly featured in comics which accompany most of the movies. They also occasionally appear in the movies, and even narrate some of them.

Rita tends to be more composed and intelligent, while Cassie tends to be more absent-minded. A common theme in the comic strip is Cassie doing something silly and Rita helping her. It has also been revealed that Tim has a crush on Rita but we are not sure if Rita likes him back. In one of the movies Tim reads Cassie's diary, finds out, and blushes.

It is noted that Rita has a ginger cat named Pickle, which Tim has to take care of in one movie.

[edit] Bob, The Ex-Lab Rat

Bob the Ex-Lab Rat

Bob is featured in experiments called "Experiment with Bob the Ex-Lab Rat," which relate to the movies.

[edit] GARY and gary

GARY and gary are also featured in some comics on BrainPOP, called "How To With GARY and Gary". The capitalized "GARY" is the father, and "gary" is the son.

[edit] BrainPOP Jr.

BrainPOP Jr. was launched on September 4, 2006. It is similar to BrainPOP, but the movies are geared towards grades K-3(age 6-9). They star Moby and a little girl named Annie. Annie may be related to Tim or know him at least.
