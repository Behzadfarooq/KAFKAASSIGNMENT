Bradley (Brad) Ronald Hazzard is an Australian politician and member of the New South Wales Legislative Assembly.

Hazzard has a BA (Science) and Diploma of Education from Macquarie University, an LL.B. from the University of New South Wales and a LL.M. from the University of Sydney. He was a science teacher from 1974 to 1977 and was admitted as a solicitor in 1977. He is married with two teenage sons.[1]

Hazzard has represented Wakehurst for the Liberal Party of Australia since 1991.[2]
