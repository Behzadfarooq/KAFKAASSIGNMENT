Bradley Ryan Fullmer was born January 17, 1975 in Chatsworth, California. Fullmer bats left handed and throws right handed.

Fullmer last played at the Major League level during the 2004 season with the Texas Rangers. He spent nearly all of 2005 out of baseball healing from injuries before joining the Charlotte Knights near seasons end. The Knights are the Triple-A Minor League affiliate of the Major League Baseball team, the Chicago White Sox.

His career in the Majors has seen him play thus far with the Montreal Expos (1997-1999), the Toronto Blue Jays (2000-2001), the Anaheim Angels (2002-2003), and the Texas Rangers (2004). Fullmer was a member of the World Series Champion Anaheim Angels in 2002.
