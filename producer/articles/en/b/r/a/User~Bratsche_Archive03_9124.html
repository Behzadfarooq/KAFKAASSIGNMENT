I have received permission to use two logos from Southern Utah University. The grantor only indicated that I could use them here and did not specify any limitations. I have written back to the grantor to concerning which tag should be used and expect a response, but until then I was hoping I could get these marks on the SUU page, with appropriate tags.

Other that, the only guidelines I have found come from here, where it says:

As with the Wordmark, the Birdmark may not be altered in any way. Also, the trademark (TM) symbol must appear in all usages of the Birdmark and its secondary marks.

The Birdmark image contains the trademark symbol, and I assume (in good faith, of course) that it is understood that the image would be placed in a template, as I gave other university articles (with templates) as examples.

I'm thinking that {{PermissionAndFairUse}} might be the best one, though currently I have selected {{logo}} for both. If there is a better one please let me know.

[edit] Sony Mavica copyvio

Hi Bratsche,

The article acquired the copyvio in August. All reversions prior to that are copyvio-free (or, at least not copied from the source that you gave). I've deleted the article and restored all the older versions. I also noted this on WP:CP. I've been bold because I hate to see a perfectly good article buried under a copyvio tag just because someone inserted a paragraph of copyvio text recently.

Greetings,  grm_wnr Esc 22:57, 11 September 2005 (UTC)

Deleting certain revisions means deleting the whole article, then going to Special:Undelete/<articlename>, ticking the little boxes of all revisions that are good, and then hitting undelete. The "bad" revisions will stay deleted, the rest will be back. Of course, this is only fun with articles that have a relatively short history =) -- grm_wnr Esc 02:54, 12 September 2005 (UTC)
