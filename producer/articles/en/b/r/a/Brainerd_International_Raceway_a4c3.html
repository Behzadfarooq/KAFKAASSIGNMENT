Brainerd International Raceway is a 3-mile road course, drag strip nad kart track in Brainerd, Minnesota. It hosted a 2 heat USAC Indy Car race in 1969. The heats were won by Gordon Johncock and Dan Gurney [1].

When opened in 1963 it was as Donnybrooke Speedway. A change of ownership in 1973 caused the change of name.
