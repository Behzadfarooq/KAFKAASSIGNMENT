Brann Dailor

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Brann Dailor is an American drummer, best known as a member of Mastodon, a heavy metal band based in Atlanta, Georgia.

His playing is influenced by jazz and prog-rock drumming styles, polyrhythmic and very fill-heavy. His drumming is considered one of Mastodon's major appeals to listeners. He has stated in interviews that his favourite albums are Stevie Wonder's Innervisions and Genesis' The Lamb Lies Down on Broadway. His drumming has been receiving acclaim as Mastodon has become more well-known, and he performed at the Modern Drummer Festival 2006. Dave Grohl recently claimed he thought Dailor was the best drummer in the world.[citation needed] Before forming Mastodon, he and fellow Mastodon member Bill Kelliher were members of the Rochester-based band Lethargy, and they played on the album In the Eyes of God by Today Is the Day.

He is married to Susanne Gibboney, guitarist and singer of Lust, drummer of Catfight, and bassist of Tiger!Tiger!.
