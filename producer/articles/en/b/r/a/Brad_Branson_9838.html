Brad Branson

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Bradley Alexander Branson (born September 24, 1958 in Harvey, Illinois) is a former American NBA player.

After being selected by the Detroit Pistons in the 1980 NBA Draft, Branson played in two NBA seasons for the Cleveland Cavaliers (1981-82) and Indiana Pacers (1982-83).
