Brandywine Creek (Ohio)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Brandywine Falls

Brandywine Creek is a tributary of the Cuyahoga River that is partly contained in Cuyahoga Valley National Park in Ohio. Brandywine Falls was the site of the now abandoned Village of Brandywine. The river runs into the Cuyahoga River in Sagamore Hills Township near the now-abandoned Jaite Paper Mill.

Brandywine falls

The Brandywine Creek watershed begins in the northern portion of Hudson, and includes the cities of Hudson, Macedonia and Twinsburg, and the townships of Boston, Sagamore Hills, and Northfield Center.
