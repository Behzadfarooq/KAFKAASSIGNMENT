Brant Page Hansen (born October 11, 1969) is an American radio personality. Brant is currently morning show host at WAY-FM in West Palm Beach, FL.

[edit] Family

Brant and his wife, Carolyn, have two children.

[edit] Radio career beginnings

Brant began his radio career as a talk radio host in his home state of Illinois. He left talk radio for contemporary Christian WBGL-FM in Champaign, IL. Brant left WBGL for KSBJ-FM in Houston, TX.

[edit] Trivia

  • Brant invented a politically-satirical game “Clintonopoly”.
  • Brant sang and played in modern-rock band "Farewell to Juliet"
  • Brant is originally from Assumption, Illinois. Population 1,000
