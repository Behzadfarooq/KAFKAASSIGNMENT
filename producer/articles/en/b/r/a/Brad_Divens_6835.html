Brad Divens

Brad Divens was the bassist and lead vocalist of Souls At Zero (now disbanded), formerly known as Wrathchild America. His vocal style often draws comparisons to James Hetfield (Metallica). Previously, Brad played guitar with the Maryland band Kix on their Cool Kids album.

He has since worked as front of house sound engineer for acts such as Linkin Park, Cyndi Lauper, and Slayer.

[edit] Partial discography

With Kix:

  • Cool Kids

With Wrathchild America:

  • Climbing The Walls
  • 3-D

With Souls At Zero:

  • Souls At Zero
  • Six-T-Six (EP)
  • A Taste For The Perverse
