Brandchannel (brandchannel.com) is a website about branding launched in 2001.[1] Its goal is to provide a global perspective on brands. The website is produced by global branding agency Interbrand, but maintains editorial independence. It is published weekly and features web reviews, brand profiles, reader polls, international directory of industry conferences and practitioners as well as archives of brand papers written by industry experts. Brandchannel's office is based in New York City.
