Branded By Fire are an American Heavy Metal band from San Antonio, TX that was formed in 2006 by former bassist and currently lead and rhythm guitarist kylethestyle, lead guitarist AllenX and drummer Andrew "The Animal" Clarke. They have released one self-titled EP and are currently working on a second one entitled "There are no Roses in Hell". The band's name is derived from the Capcom videogame Demon's Crest. The playable character's name is Firebrand.
