Brandon Smith

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Brandon Mychal Smith (born May 29, 1989) is an American actor.

Smith started acting at the age of 8 years old. Soon, he booked his first national commercial for Nike with Tiger Woods. At that point he knew what he wanted to do in life - act - and has been ever since. Throughout his career, Brandon has landed comedic and dramatic roles.
