This article is about a railway station in England. For Braintree station in Massachusetts, see Braintree (MBTA station).

Braintree railway station is a railway station serving the town of Braintree in Essex. The station is the terminus of the Witham-Braintree line.

The station is served by 'one'. All services are operated by Class 321 and occasionally Class 322 electrical multiple units. Most services operate direct to London Liverpool Street, joining the Great Eastern Main Line at Witham.

There is typically an hourly weekday service to London Liverpool Street (64mins), on Sundays there is a shuttle to/from Witham.
