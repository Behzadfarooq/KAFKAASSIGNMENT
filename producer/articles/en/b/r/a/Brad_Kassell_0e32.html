Brad Kassell (born January 7, 1980 in Llano, Texas) is an American football player who currently plays linebacker for the New York Jets.

[edit] High School Years

Kassell attended Llano High School in Llano, Texas and was a letterman in football as a quarterback. In football, as a senior, he rushed for 1431 yards and 20 touchdowns and passed for 839 yards and eight touchdowns, and was named the Class 3A All-West MVP, and won All-State second team honors. Brad Kassell graduated from Llano High School in 1999.

[edit] College and Pro Career

He was a four-year letterman who played in 44 games at North Texas University and was named Sun Belt Conference Defensive Player of the Year during his junior and senior season. Kassell received a unanimous first-team selection to the conference teams and was team MVP in his final two seasons. He ranks third on the school's all-time tackle list. in 2002, he was signed as a free agent by the Tennessee Titans. In 2006, he was signed as a free agent to the New York Jets
