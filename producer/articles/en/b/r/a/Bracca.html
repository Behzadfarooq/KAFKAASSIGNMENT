Location of Bracca in Italy

Bracca is a comune (municipality) in the Province of Bergamo in the Italian region Lombardy, located about 60 km northeast of Milan and about 14 km northeast of Bergamo. As of 31 December 2004, it had a population of 827 and an area of 5.5 km².[1]

Bracca borders the following municipalities: Algua, Costa di Serina, San Pellegrino Terme, Zogno.
