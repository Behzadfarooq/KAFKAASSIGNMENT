Bradley Imes Smith also known as Brad Imes (born March 13, 1977) is an American mixed martial arts fighter. He currently fights as a heavyweight in the Ultimate Fighting Championship.

[edit] Biography

Brad comes from a football background, having played offensive tackle for the University of Missouri, and the Iowa Barnstormers of the Arena Football League.

[edit] Martial arts background

Brad came into the The Ultimate Fighter reality series with training in Big Arts.
