Brahui are a tribal society settled in Balochistan in South western Asia. Balochistan region is divided among Iran, Afghanistan and Pakistan.

The following is a partial list of major Brahui tribes.

B

  • Bangulzai
  • Banulzai

L

  • Lango (tribe)
  • Lehri

M

  • Mashwani
  • Mirwani
  • Muhammad Shahi
  • Muhammed Hasni
  • Muhammed Shahi

R

  • Raisani

S

  • Sarpara
  • Sasooli
  • Shahwani
  • Sumalani

Z

  • Zehri
