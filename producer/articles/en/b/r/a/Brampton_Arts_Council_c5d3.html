Brampton Arts Council

The Brampton Arts Council is a non-profit, charitable organization, which was formed to promote and develop the arts in the city. The BAC annually recognizes a Brampton resident for their contribution to the arts (Arts Persons of the Year).

Groups include:

  • Visual Arts Brampton
  • Theatre Brampton
