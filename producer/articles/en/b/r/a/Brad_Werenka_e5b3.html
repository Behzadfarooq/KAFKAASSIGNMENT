Brad Werenka

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Brad Werenka (born February 12, 1969 in Two Hills, Alberta) is a former professional ice hockey defenseman. He was drafted by the Edmonton Oilers in the second round of the 1987 NHL Entry Draft, 47th overall. In addition to minor pro experience, Werenka went on to play for the Oilers, Quebec Nordiques, Chicago Blackhawks, Pittsburgh Penguins, and Calgary Flames.
