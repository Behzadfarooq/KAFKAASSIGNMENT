Brain Cell (Kevin) is a fictional character, a mutant in the Marvel Comics Universe. His first appearance was in Uncanny X-Men #292.

[edit] Ficitional character biography

Brain Cell was found to be the one responsible for instigating the riots in the Morlocks tunnels. The Professor X was able to calm him down and thus halt the fighting. However, the fate of Kevin was never revealed. He presumably died during the flood or later on the Hill.

[edit] Powers and abilities

Psi-link with anyone he comes into physical contact with, allowing him to read their thoughts and constantly broadcast his own into their minds.
