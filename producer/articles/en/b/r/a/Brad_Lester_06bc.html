Brad Lester (born 24 October, 1985), is a American football running back who currently plays for Auburn University. He was born to Phillis and Willie Miller and grew up in Lilburn, Georgia [1]. Lester attended Parkview High School. At Parkview, Lester played alongside current Atlanta Braves outfielder Jeff Francoeur.
