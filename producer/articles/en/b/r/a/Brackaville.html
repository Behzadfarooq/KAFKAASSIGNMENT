Brackaville

Brackaville is a village in County Tyrone, Northern Ireland. It is in the parish of Donaghenry in the Coalisland district, on the western side of Lough Neagh.

[edit] Sport

Brackaville is the home of the Gaelic football club Brackaville Owen Roes GFC.
