Bramley-Moore Dock

The Bramley-Moore Dock is a dock, on the River Mersey and part of the Port of Liverpool it is situated in the northern end of the dock system, connected to Sandon Half Tide Dock to the north and Nelson Dock to the south.

It opened in 1848.
