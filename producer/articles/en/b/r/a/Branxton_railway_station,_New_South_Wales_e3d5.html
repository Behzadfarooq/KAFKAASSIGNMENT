Branxton is a railway station on the Main North Line of New South Wales, Australia. CityRail operates diesel self propelled railcars through Branxton as part of its Hunter Line regional service from Newcastle. is a railway station in the town of Branxton, New South Wales, Australia. Historically, the next outbound station is Belford, however this station was closed on 4 September 2005.
