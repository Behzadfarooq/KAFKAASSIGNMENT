Braives is a municipality located in the Belgian province of Liège. On January 1, 2006 Braives had a total population of 5,579. The total area is 44.00 km² which gives a population density of 127 inhabitants per km².visit site:braives.be Fallais is one of the 8 villages composing the entity of Braives. Fallais is famous for its wonderful medieval castle and many other curiosities like: a water mill, a former wheelmakershop, the river Méhaigne and the Ravel cycling path

Braives has got eight villages: Ciplet, Avennes, Latinne, Tourinne, Fallais, Fumal, Ville-en-Hesbaye and Braives. More over,three nice attached hamlets are to be mentionned:Hosdent [1] to Latinne,Pitet to Fallais and Foncourt to Fumal.
