Brabys

A.C. Brabys is a South African company that produces B2B directories and operates an online portal and directory of organizations and services for consumers and businesses. The company has diversified into maps and tourist information. The site serves all of Southern Africa including:

  • Angola
  • Botswana
  • Lesotho
  • Malawi
  • Mauritius
  • Mozambique
  • Namibia
  • Seychelles
  • South Africa
  • Swaziland
  • Tanzania
  • Zambia
  • Zimbabwe

Brabys' service is affiliated with the websites of Business Day, Tiscali, Sunday Times and Trade World.

Brabys has purchased Ananzi, a South African search engine.
