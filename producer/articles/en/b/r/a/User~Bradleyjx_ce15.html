[edit] general information

My name is J. Bradley Baker, a native of Madison, WI but currently at college in Eau Claire, WI. I am a dedicated Magic person, both as a limited player and as a DCI Judge. I run my own site, JX Productions, a site mostly dedicated on holding my major writings and as a provider of information towards tournaments I run or help organize.

If you want an interesting quirk about me, it would be that I am a "good" poetry writer (at least to educators who look at my work) who despises that which I create. My issue is that I feel that the more open forms of poetry are simply too easy to write; I write these works to express how I feel against them by showing how easy they can be to write.
