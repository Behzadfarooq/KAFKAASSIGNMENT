Brayton College

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Brayton College is a medium sized high school in Selby, North Yorkshire. The College is an 11-16 establishment with approximately 1200 students. Brayton High School opened in 1979, and the school was awarded Technology College status in 2001. The name was changed in recognition of the new status.

The College is actually located within the town boundaries of Selby though its catchment area is primarily the nearby village of Brayton.
