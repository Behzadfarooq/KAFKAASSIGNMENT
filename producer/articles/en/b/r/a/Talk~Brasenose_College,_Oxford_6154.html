[edit] Archer

His status is unclear (and his own accounts are notoriously fictitious). After one of his bankruptcies, he was offered a room in College by the Principal, a friend of his. He may have enrolled for a PGCE (though, as he had no first degree, this is dubious in one sense or another), but certainly didn't leave with any qualification. Whether he counts as a former student is pretty unclear. --Mel Etitis (Μελ Ετητης) 09:36, 21 March 2006 (UTC)

Archer was a student at Brasenose for 3 years after which he was awarded an Oxford University endorsed qualification ( a diploma, not a degree) - the title of this section is "notable former students". He is notable, and he was a student as Brasenose. Either he is reinserted, or else the title is changed to reflect a list of only those sitting for Oxford degrees (whether obtained or not).--jrleighton 16:34, 21 March 2006 (UTC)
