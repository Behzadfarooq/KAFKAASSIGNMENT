Brad Blum (born 1954) was the CEO of Burger King from December 2002 to July 2, 2004. He joined from Darden Restaurants where he had headed the Olive Garden unit, but left after only 18 months citing strategic differences with Burger King's board. Blum's successor, Greg Brenneman, was appointed on August 1, 2004. Blum's hometown is Cincinnati, Ohio; he attended Denison University (BA) and received an MBA from the Kellogg School at Northwestern University. His hobby is auto racing
