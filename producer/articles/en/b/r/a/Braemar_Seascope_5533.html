Braemar Seascope Group plc is a publicly listed British shipbroking company which was created through the merger (2001) of Braemar (shipbroker) (founded 1983) and Seascope (founded 1972). In 2003 it acquired Cory Brothers Shipping Agency. Its headquarters are NEAR PADDINGTON, London. The London Stock Exchange symbol is BMS.
