Brad Isbister (born May 7, 1977 in Edmonton, Alberta, Canada) is a professional ice hockey left wing who currently plays for the New York Rangers of the NHL.

Isbister was drafted 67th overall by the Winnipeg Jets in the 1995 NHL Entry Draft.

Brad Isbister played his first NHL game on October 1, 1997 for the Phoenix Coyotes (formerly Winnipeg Jets) against the Chicago Blackhawks. His first NHL point was an assist, in a 3-2 Phoenix win over the Colorado Avalanche on October 11, 1997. His first NHL goal came 10 days later on October 21, in a 4-3 Phoenix loss to the Mighty Ducks of Anaheim.

Isbister has spent most of the 2006-07 season in the AHL. However, he did appear in two games for the Rangers in December, 2006, and recorded his 100th NHL goal in a game against the Florida Panthers.
