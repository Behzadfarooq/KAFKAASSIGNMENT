The Braille Institute (Braille Institute of America) is a private non-profit organization whose mission is to eliminate blindness and severe sight loss as a barrier to a fulfilling life.

Braille Institute achieves its mission with five regional centers – in Los Angeles, Santa Barbara, Orange County, Rancho Mirage, and San Diego – each of which provides services to the surrounding area's population of blind and visually impaired persons. Each center typically contains a braille/cassette library, youth department, technology store, and cafeteria.

[edit] Services

[edit] Youth Department

Each regional center provides a vast array of activities for blind and visually impaired youth ranging anywhere from arts and crafts to white-water rafting. The staff divide the youth into three main age ranges. The preschool and below age group receive in-home consultations and focus on parent resources. The elementary to middle school and high school age groups all participate in a rich set of activities outlined below.
