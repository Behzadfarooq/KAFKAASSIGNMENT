Brancaccio & Aisher

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Brancaccio & Aisher are a duo of house music producers Luke Brancaccio and Bruce Aisher. As remixers they have a large catalog of hits. They hit #1 on both the Hot Dance Music/Club Play chart and the UK Dance Chart in 2002 with the track "It's Gonna Be... (A Lovely Day)".
