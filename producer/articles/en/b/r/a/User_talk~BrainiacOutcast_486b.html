[edit] Clearing an autoblock

Please note that we cannot unblock you at this time because you have not given us the information we need to look into your block. It is very likely that you are not personally blocked. If you are prevented from editing, it may be because you are autoblocked or blocked because of your IP address. Without further details there is nothing further we can do to review your block or lift it. If you are still autoblocked by the time you read this message, please follow these instructions:

  1. If you have a Wikipedia account, please ensure that you are logged in.
    Your account name will be visible in the top right of this page if you are.
    If it isn't, try bypassing your web browser's cache.
  2. Try to edit the Sandbox.
  3. Copy the {{unblock-auto|...}} code generated for you under the "Autoblocked?" section.
  4. Paste the code at the bottom of your user talk page and click save.

If you are not blocked from editing the sandbox then the autoblock on your IP address has already expired and you can resume editing. --  Netsnipe  ►  17:49, 2 October 2006 (UTC)

[edit] Just a reminder: Wikipedia is an encyclopedia

Hi, BrainiacOutcast.

I've been watching your contributions for the past month or so. You seem to have grasped wikicode fairly well, judging by your userpage and subpages.

I would like to take this opportunity to remind you that Wikipedia is an encyclopedia first and foremost. If you would like to be an editor here, you should edit articles, like Australia, John Locke, Fish, and Global warming, just to give a few examples. You should not merely edit your user page and your friends' talk pages. I see that you and your friends are still using talk pages occasionally to communicate. While it's great that you've cut down on it, I would suggest that you use a website like MySpace.com or livejournal.com to keep up with each other. There are also instant messanger services like the chat feature on Gmail and AOL Instant Messanger.

You need to start contributing to our encyclopedia. Wikipedia is not a place to design a page about yourself (AKA your userpage) and to talk to your friends (AKA your talk pages). Our mission is to build an encyclopedia. Find a topic that interests you, type it in the search bar, and find an article on it. Improve that article: fix the grammar or the information. You can even revert vandalism (go to Recent changes and then Recent changes patrol).

Editors in good standing are beginning to become concerned that you and several of your friends are using server space without contributing to the encyclopedia. With your knowledge of Wikicode, I think you could make large and valuable contributions to the encyclopedic content we host. Srose (talk) 18:03, 7 October 2006 (UTC)
