Brad Badger (born January 11, 1975 in Corvallis, Oregon) is an American football player who currently plays offensive tackle for the Oakland Raiders.

[edit] High school career

Badger attended Corvallis High School in Corvallis, Oregon and starred in football, basketball, and baseball.

[edit] College career

At Stanford University, Badger was named to the All Pac-10 first-team as a senior, where he started 34 of 35 games dating back to his sophomore year.

[edit] Professional career

He was drafted in the fifth round of the 1997 NFL Draft by the Washington Redskins. He also played for the Minnesota Vikings and currently plays for the Oakland Raiders.

[edit] Personal Life

Brad is currently married to Mercedes Badger, daughter of Henry Cisneros. They dated and met at Stanford University .
