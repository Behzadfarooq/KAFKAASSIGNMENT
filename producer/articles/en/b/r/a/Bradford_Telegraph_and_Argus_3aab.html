The Telegraph & Argus is the local evening newspaper for Bradford, West Yorkshire, England. It is published six times each week, from Monday to Saturday inclusive. Locally, the paper is known as the "T&A".

Although predominantly Bradford-focused, separate editions cover the Aire Valley, Otley & Ilkley, and the Spen Valley. The newspaper is published and printed in Bradford city centre, in the newspaper's distinctive glass-fronted building on Hall Ings.

Founded in 1868, the paper was a broadsheet until 1989 when it became tabloid. It features a range of news, features, sport, lifestyle articles and classified advertising, with weekly supplements on motoring and property.

The Telegraph & Argus is owned by Newsquest, the second largest publisher of regional newspapers in the United Kingdom. The current editor is Perry Austin-Clarke.
