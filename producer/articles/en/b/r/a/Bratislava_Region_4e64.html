Bratislava region

The Bratislava Region (Slovak: Bratislavský kraj) is one of the administrative regions of Slovakia. Its capital is Bratislava. Total area: 2,053 square kilometres. Total population (2001): 599,015. Population density: 301/square kilometre.

It comprises the following okresy, i.e. districts:

  • Bratislava I
  • Bratislava II
  • Bratislava III
  • Bratislava IV
  • Bratislava V
  • Malacky District
  • Pezinok District
  • Senec District
