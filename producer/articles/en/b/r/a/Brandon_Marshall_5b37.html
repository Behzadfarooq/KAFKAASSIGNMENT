Brandon Marshall (born March 23, 1984) is a wide receiver out of UCF, who was selected in the 4th round, with the 119th overall pick of the 2006 NFL Draft by the Denver Broncos. In his collegiate career Marshall amassed 112 receptions, 1,634 receiving yards, and 13 touchdowns. He was a second team All-C-USA selectee in the 2005 season. Marshall's best career game came in the 2005 Hawaii Bowl, where he had 11 catches for 210 yards, and three touchdowns.
