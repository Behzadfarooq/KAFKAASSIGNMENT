Branimir Petrovic (Serbian Cyrillic: Бранимир Петровић), (born June 26, 1982 in Užice) is a Serbian football player, who, as of 2006 was playing for FK Zeta, his first professional club. He also played for FK Partizan and Shandong Luneng.

He was part of the Serbian and Montenegrin 2004 Olympic football team, who exited in the first round, finishing fourth in Group C behind gold-medal winners Argentina, Australia and Tunisia.
