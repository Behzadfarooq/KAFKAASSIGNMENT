[edit] Claims of Ending

Someone edited the guide to claim that the series was ending in August of this year, with only 30 episodes airing. I have yet to hear any news of this and since the 29th episode is airing in March, it doesn't make sense that the series would end in August of 2006 with only 30 episodes. Can someone explain where exactly the claims came from, and if the ending of the series cannot be proven, shouldn't it be removed from the synopsis?

Ok, the 30th episode is airing on March 31st, so under that logic, I don't believe the series could end in August with only 30 episodes aired. I've removed that segment from the Wikipedia article, though if anyone has any supporting logic in regards to if the series is really being cancelled, feel free to share it in this discussion page.
