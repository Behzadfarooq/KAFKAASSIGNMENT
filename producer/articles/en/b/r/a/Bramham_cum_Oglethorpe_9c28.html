Bramham cum Oglethorpe

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Bramham cum Oglethorpe, more well known as just "Bramham", is a village and civil parish in the English county of West Yorkshire. According to the 2001 census the parish had a population of 1,715.

It is located 19 km (12 miles) north west of Leeds and 6 km (4 miles) west of Tadcaster. Bramham Park (at SE410417), two miles to the west of the village, is home to the Leeds Festival, an annual music and arts festival.

It is administered as part of the City of Leeds; being part of the city council's Wetherby ward.
