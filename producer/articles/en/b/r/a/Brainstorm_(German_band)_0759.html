Brainstorm (German band)

Country Germany
Years active 1989–present

Power metal
Thrash metal
Labels Metal Blade

Brainstorm is a power/thrash metal band from Germany, formed in 1990. The band features American born lead singer Andy B. Franck, who is also famous for his other band, Symphorce. They are known to play a somewhat darker style of music than most power metal groups.
