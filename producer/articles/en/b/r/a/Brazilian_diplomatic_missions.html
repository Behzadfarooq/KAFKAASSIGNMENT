No other Latin American country has as many diplomatic missions as Brazil. It has diplomatic representations in over 106 countries across the five continents. Special ties link the country with the other seven member-nations of the Community of Portuguese Language Countries. Brazil has recently intensified its relationship with China, Russia, India and other developing countries.
