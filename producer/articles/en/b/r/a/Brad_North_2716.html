Brad North (born January 21, 1985) is an American soccer player from The Woodlands, Texas. A 6'1", 190 lb. forward from Northwestern University, North was a two time All Big Ten first team selection, scoring 29 goals over a four year career. North was the first player from Northwestern U. to be picked in the MLS SuperDraft, being selected 24th overall (11th in the second round) by D.C. United.
