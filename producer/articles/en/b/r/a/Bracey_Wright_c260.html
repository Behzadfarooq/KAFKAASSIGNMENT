Bracey Wright (born July 1, 1984, The Colony, Texas) is an American professional basketball player for the NBA's Minnesota Timberwolves. Wright attended The Colony High School in The Colony. He was drafted by the Minnesota Timberwolves in the 2005 NBA Draft, after a college career at Indiana University. The Timberwolves designated him to the Florida Flame of the D-League for the 2005-06 season. The Timberwolves exercised his contract option on June 30, 2006 which means he will remain on the team's roster for the 2006-07 season.
