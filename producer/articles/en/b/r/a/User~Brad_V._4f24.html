[edit] Career

I am currently enlisted in the United States Air Force, stationed at Elmendorf Air Force Base in Alaska. I am a Senior Airman (E-4), but I'm working on that big promotion to Staff Sergeant.

[edit] Assignment History

A quick list of where I've been in my time in the military:

  • Lackland AFB, Texas - 320th Training Squadron for basic military training (20 August 2002 - 27 October 2002).
  • Lackland AFB, Texas - 344th Training Squadron for Electronic Principles technical training (27 October 2002 - 20 January 2003).
  • Sheppard AFB, Texas - 365th Training Squadron for Avionic Sensors System Apprentice course (20 January 2003 - 24 July 2003).
  • Elmendorf AFB, Alaska - 3rd Component Maintenance Squadron (24 July 2003 - present).
    • Kwangju AB, Republic of Korea - 3rd Air Expeditionary Group (3 September 2004 - 6 January 2005).
  • Kunsan AB, Republic of Korea - 8th Maintenance Squadron (projected 7 September 2006).

[edit] Awards and Decorations

Some of my military awards and decorations I've gotten in my time in the Service.

  • Air Force Outstanding Unit Award plus one bronze oak leaf cluster
  • Air Force Good Conduct Medal
  • National Defense Service Medal
  • Global War on Terrorism Service Medal
  • Korean Defense Service Medal
  • Air Force Expeditionary Service Ribbon
  • Air Force Training Ribbon
