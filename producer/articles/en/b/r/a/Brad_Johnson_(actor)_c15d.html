Brad Johnson (actor)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia


Brad Johnson (b. October 24, 1959 in Tucson, Arizona) is an American actor and former Marlboro Man. His first acting credit was in the low-budget biker film The Nam Angels, and shortly after he had his first co-starring role in a major film when he played the role of Ted Baker in the 1989 movie Always, directed by Steven Spielberg.

His other movie credits include Flight of the Intruder, Philadelphia Experiment II, and Blind Obsession.

He also played the role of Rayford Steele in the Left Behind Series, and played Dr. Dominick O'Malley on Melrose Place.
