Brazilian Imperial Regalia

The Brazilian Imperial Regalia consists of the Imperial Crowns: the Crown of Pedro II and the Crown of Pedro I; the Imperial Sceptre; and jewelry belonging to the Emperors and Empresses of Brazil. Most are displayed at The Imperial Museum of Brazil in Petrópolis and at The National Museum of Brazil in Rio de Janeiro. Other items are stored at the National Treasury of Brazil in Brasília.

Imperial Sceptre

Imperial insignias: Order of Pedro I; Imperial Order of the Cross; and the Order of the Rose
