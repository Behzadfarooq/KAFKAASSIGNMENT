Parliamentary elections were held in Brazil in 1945. They were won by the Social Democratic Party, who took 151 of the 286 seats.

[edit] Results

Party Seats % Votes
Social Democratic Party 2,531,944 42.7% 151
National Democratic Union 1,575,375 26.6% 81
Brazilian Labour Party 603,500 10.2% 22
Brazilian Communist Party 511,302 8.6% 14
Reformer Party 219,562 3.7% 9
PPS 107,321 1.8% 4
Christian Democratic Party 101,636 1.7% 2
Progressive Renewal Party 70,675 1.2% 2
PL 57,341 1.0% 1
Others 145,960 2.5% 0
Total 5,924,616 100% 286
