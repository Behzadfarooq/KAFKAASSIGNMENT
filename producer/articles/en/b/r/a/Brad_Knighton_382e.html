Brad Burton Knighton (born February 6, 1985) is an American soccer goalkeeper who currently plays for the New England Revolution of Major League Soccer.

Knighton played four years of college soccer at UNC-Wilmington, and for Indiana Invaders in the USL Premier Development League. Undrafted by MLS, he attended a New England tryout in early 2007 and was good enough to be invited on trial for the preseason. He impressed during the trial, winning a spot as the Revolution's third-choice keeper and a developmental contract with the club[1].
