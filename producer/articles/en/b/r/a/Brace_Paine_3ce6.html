Brace Paine aka Nathan Howdeshell is the guitarist of American band The Gossip. He is from Arkansas.

Paine is an artist, poet, musician and author from Center Hill, Arkansas. His art has been shown in London, New York, Montreal, Portland, Sweden and Arkansas. His "mini novelettes" (or "warm novelettes") have been reviewed in Vice, i-D and various other high end publications. His subject matter tends to dwell on the dark and funny side of life.

He has also published work under the names Amy Snow, Druxx, T.r. Ash and Raquel Medina-Cleghorn a.k.a. "Slow Jams" and recorded music under the group/monikers: Die Monitr Batss, XEAXX XEAXX (with noise artist John Wiese), So Soft + The Tests and The Gossip. His heroes are Henry Darger, Ray Johnson, Yoko Ono, Yayoi Kusama and Marcel Duchamp
