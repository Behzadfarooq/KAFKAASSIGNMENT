Bramwell

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

For other uses, see Bramwell (disambiguation).

Bramwell is a British television series starring Jemma Redgrave as Dr. Eleanor Bramwell, a woman challenging the domination of men in the medical establishment who runs a free hospital for the poor in the East End of London, during the Victorian era (1895).

The series by Carlton Television was shown in Britain on ITV in five series from 1995. It was subsequently shown in other countries (US, PBS from 1996 to 2000.
