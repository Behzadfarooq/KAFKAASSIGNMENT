Brandon Mcleod (born July 18, 1991) is an American R&B singer, dancer, and actor. He is in the studio now working on his upcoming album "It's Over Now" with singer/songwriter Chris Brown It's due to hit store's in the fall of 2007. His new single "Better Off" is Scheduled to air On 2007.

[edit] Early life

Brandon was born in Atlanta, Georgia but moved to a small town of Warsaw, virginia when he was 12. He discovered his love of music when he heard the voices of artists like Usher and Aaliyah at a younger age. He first noticed he could sing when he was 11 years old, when his mother took him to local recording studios in Charlotte, North Carolina and Atlanta, Georgia. He was discovered when he was walking down the halls of a local mall in Charlotte, North Carolina, singing Love By Musiq Soulchild by Co-owner of Jive Records. From then he was introduced to a mate of Jive records.

[edit] Music career

Mcleod got his big break when a co-owner of Jive Records heard him singing in a Charlotte mall. Brandon then flew to New York City and auditioned for Def Jam VP Tina Davis. He was then sent to Sherman Oaks, California to perform for L. A Ried. He was signed to Jive Records on the spot. Brandon was introduced to fellow artist Chris Brown to record an album together. They are in the studio working on an album called It's Over Now. Scheduled for release November 2007.
