Brad Richardson (born February 4, 1985 in Belleville, Ontario) is a professional ice hockey player who currently plays center and left wing for the National Hockey League's Colorado Avalanche. He was drafted by the Colorado Avalanche in the 2003 NHL Entry Draft. He was the chosen 163rd overall in the 5th round.
