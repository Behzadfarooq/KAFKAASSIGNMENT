Branko Baletić (15 June 1946 Belgrade, Serbia) is a Serbian-Montenegrin film director and executive producer.

Films include Granica (1990) (producer), Stela (1990) (executive producer) and the most famous Balkan Express (1983), a comic tale of Serbian bootleggers and thieves making their way during the German occupation in WW2.
