Braithwaite railway station

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Braithwaite railway station was situated on the Cockermouth, Keswick and Penrith Railway between Penrith and Cockermouth. It served the village of Braithwaite. The station opened to passenger traffic on 2 January 1865, and closed on 18 April 1966.
