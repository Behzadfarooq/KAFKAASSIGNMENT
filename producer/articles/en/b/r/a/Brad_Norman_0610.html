Born: January 31, 1987
St. John's, Newfoundland, Canada
Occupation: Student and novelist
Genres: Adventure, mystery
Website: BradNorman.com

Brad Norman (born January 31, 1987) is the Canadian author of Business Trip and his latest release, The Oris. After writing many poems throughout high school, some of which were published in a book along with poetry by his friend known as Ginny, Brad started writing fiction once in university. His first ever novel, Business Trip is told from the first person vantage point and is therefore commonly thought to be somewhat autobiographical, although Brad says this is not true and claims the story is entirely fictitious [1].

According to his website, Brad is currently studying science at the Memorial University of Newfoundland [2].
