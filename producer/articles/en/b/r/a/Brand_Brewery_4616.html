Brand Brewery is a Dutch brewery founded in 1871 by the Brand family in Wijlre (municipality Gulpen-Wittem), Netherlands. It is now owned by Heineken.

[edit] Brief details

The name comes from the Brand family which in 1871 purchased the Netherlands oldest brewery (1341) and remained in management after its acquisition by Heineken.

[edit] Beers

Beers produced are:

  • Brand Pils (sold in the United States as Royal Brand Beer (5.0% ABV)
  • Brand Urtyp a 5.5% ABV premium Pilsner. Urtyp stands for 'original'.

  • Brand Oud Bruin (Brand Old Brown) A mild-sweet maltbeer. Dark colored and a low alcoholpercentage (3,5%)

Beers are not pasteurised and have long lagering periods.
