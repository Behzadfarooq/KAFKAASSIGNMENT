Brno Philharmonic Orchestra (Czech: Statni filharmonie Brno) is one of the most notable orchestras in the Czech Republic.

[edit] Principal conductors

  • Petr Altrichter (2002–)
  • Aldo Ceccato
  • Leoš Svárovský (1991–1995)
  • Petr Vronský (1978–1991)
  • Frantisek Jilek
  • Jiri Pinkas
  • Jiri Waldhans
  • Martin Turnovský (1959–1963)
  • Otakar Trhlik (1956)
  • Břetislav Bakala (1956)

[edit] Conductor laureate

  • Sir Charles Mackerras (2007–)
