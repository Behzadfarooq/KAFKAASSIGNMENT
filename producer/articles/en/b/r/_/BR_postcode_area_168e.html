The BR postcode area, also known as the Bromley postcode area[2], is a group of eight postal districts in south east Greater London which are subdivisions of seven post towns.

The main sorting office is in Bromley and the area served includes part of the London Borough of Bromley. The northern part of the Kent district of Sevenoaks is also covered by the BR8 postal district.
