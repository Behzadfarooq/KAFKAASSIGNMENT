BRB Internacional is a Spanish animation studio which is best known for producing 1980s cartoon hits such as Around the World with Willy Fog, Dogtanian and the Three Muskehounds and The World of David the Gnome.

It was created by Claudio Biern Boyd in 1973 as merchandising company.

Unusually for a studio based in a spanish-speaking country, all text appearing in their productions, from street names to the credits, is written entirely in English. The reason may be for ease of exporting to other languages since English is considered an international language, or due to an underlying, yet known, perception among Spanish speakers that things originally made in English and later translated are of better quality.
