I've been using Wikipedia for a couple of years, but just joined it last year. I'm a 22 year old from Kentucky who recently graduated from Asbury College with a Media degree.

I made the first version of the World Gospel Mission article, as 65.164.148.226. I remembered to sign in about one second after I clicked Save Page. Other topics of interest to me are Geography and anything about Honduras, as well as many other random things. Most of my edits are fixing typos and minor inaccuracies, or NPOV issues. Lately I've also been trying to expand on the articles on Choluteca, Honduras and started the article on the Choluteca River. You're more than welcome to help with these!
