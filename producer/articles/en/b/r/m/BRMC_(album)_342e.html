BRMC is the self-titled debut album by Black Rebel Motorcycle Club, released on Virgin Records on April 3, 2001. This first release made an immediate impact through the music world, making them part of the 'garage revolution' that The Strokes spawned in the early 2000's. Although the album wasn't as popular as the New Yorkers' debut, it earned the band a stable fanbase.
