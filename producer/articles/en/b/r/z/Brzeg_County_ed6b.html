Brzeg County (Polish: powiat brzeski ) is a powiat (county) in Poland, in Opole Voivodeship. The county seat is the town of Brzeg. The population numbers 92,637 (2005). Its area is 876.52 km².

  • Brzeg
  • Grodków
  • Lewin Brzeski
