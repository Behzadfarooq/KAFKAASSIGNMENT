Brzesko County (Polish powiat brzeski ) is a unit of territorial administration and local government in the Lesser Poland Voivodeship in Poland, created on 1 January 1999 as a result of the Local Government Reorganization Act of 1998.

[edit] Communes

Administrative division: 7 communes

  • Borzęcin rural commune
  • Brzesko rural commune
  • Czchów rural commune
  • Dębno rural commune
  • Gnojnik rural commune
  • Iwkowa rural commune
  • Szczurowa rural commune
