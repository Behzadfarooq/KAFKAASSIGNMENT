[edit] Re: You (Kumi Koda song)

Hello! Since You (Kumi Koda song) has only one history item, you should be able to move You (song) to You (Kumi Koda song) without problem, overwritting it. To do it, go to You (song), and click the move tab that is next to history. You will end up here. Now, in the To new title: textbox, write the name of the new page (in this case, You (Kumi Koda song)) and write an explanation in the Reason box. Click Move page, and it should move the page and create a redirect from You (song) to You (Kumi Koda song). Then, just redirect You (song) wherever you want. If it asks you confirmation to overwrite the old page, just click Ok, Yes, or whatever the option to overwrite is.

If you need more help, I can do it. However, it is better if you do it yourself, to learn and prevent more involuntary mistakes. -- ReyBrujo 15:23, 15 July 2006 (UTC)

ReyBrujo, thank you for your help! In the end I needed an admin to move the article since I already had created the You (Kumi Koda song) article, so now I know this too :) Best regards, Brz7 22:23, 15 July 2006 (UTC)
