Brześć Kujawski Voivodeship (Polish: województwo brzesko-kujawskie) was a unit of administrative division and local government in Poland from 14th century to the partitions of Poland in 1772-1795. Part of Kuyavia region and the Greater Poland province.

Governor seat:

  • Brześć Kujawski

Voivodes: Jakub Szczawinski (1622-1637), Andrzej Kretkowski (1637-1643), Jan Szymon Szczawiński (1643-1652)

Regional council (sejmik) seats:

  • Brześć Kujawski

Other towns:

  • Kowal
  • Kruszwica
  • Przedecz
  • Radziejów

Neighbouring Voivodeships:

  • Inowrocław Voivodeship
  • Rawa Voivodeship
  • Łęczyca Voivodeship
  • Kalisz Voivodeship
  • Gniezno Voivodeship (since 1768)
