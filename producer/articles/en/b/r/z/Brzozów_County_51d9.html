Brzozów County (Polish powiat brzozowski ) is a unit of territorial administration and local government in the Subcarpathian Voivodship in Poland, created on 1 January 1999 as a result of the Local Government Reorganization Act of 1998.

[edit] Communes

Administrative division: 6 communes

  • Brzozów urban-rural commune
  • Domaradz rural commune
  • Dydnia rural commune
  • Haczów rural commune
  • Jasienica Rosielna rural commune
  • Nozdrzec rural commune
