Brändö is an island municipality of Åland, Finland. It has a population of 526 and covers an area of 103.08 km² of which 0.2 km² is water. The population density is 5.0 inhabitants per km². The municipality is unilingually Swedish and 91 percent of the population are Swedish speakers.
