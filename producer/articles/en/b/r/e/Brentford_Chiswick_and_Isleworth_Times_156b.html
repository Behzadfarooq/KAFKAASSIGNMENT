The Brentford, Chiswick and Isleworth Times (including Hounslow, Feltham and Hanworth) is a weekly local newspaper covering news and events across the whole London Borough of Hounslow in London, United Kingdom.

[edit] Publication

It is published as a Broadsheet format every Friday. The paper is owned by Newsquest Media Group, and along with the other newspapers in the local group has an average circulation of 15,675.[1]

The paper is based at offices in Richmond, London, where the Richmond and Twickenham Times is also produced.

As of September 2006, Christine Briddon is news editor for the paper while Faye Duxberry and Stephen Abbott act as reporters covering the whole of Hounslow.
