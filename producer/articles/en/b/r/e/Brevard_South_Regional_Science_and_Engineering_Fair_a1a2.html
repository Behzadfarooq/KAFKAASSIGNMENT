Brevard South Regional Science and Engineering Fair

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

You have new messages (last change).

Brevard South Regional Science and Engineering Fair is an annual fair held at the Melbourne Square Mall in Melbourne, Florida. This year's fair was held from February 1-3, 2007. The first day is always setting up the boards while the second and third days are judging with an awards ceremony at Eau Gallie High School the night of day three. The majority of first place winners will be attending the Florida State Science and Engineering Fair in April of 2007.
