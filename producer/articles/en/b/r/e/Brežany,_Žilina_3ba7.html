Brežany is a village and municipality in Žilina District in the Žilina Region of northern Slovakia.

[edit] History

In historical records the village was first mentioned in 1393.

[edit] Geography

The municipality lies at an altitude of 390 metres and covers an area of 3.656km². It has a population of about 460 people.
