Brett Alan Tucker (born May 21st, 1972 in Melbourne, Australia) is the Australian actor and singer. The son of Ken and Janice Tucker, he grew up in the Yarra Valley of Victoria [1] with his three siblings - Nicky, Mark and David.[2] He attended Melbourne's National Theater Drama School and made his acting debut in a 1994 episode of Snowy River: The McGregor Saga.

Brett is best known for playing Dave Brewer on the drama McLeod's Daughters. He apprenticed with an real country veterinarian to research his role. [3]

Brett's onstage credits include The Woman in Black in 2006. [4]

He is currently pursuing his career in the US.
