Brendan James Lawley (born September 18, 1988 in Victoria) is an Australian football (soccer) player, currently a train on reserve player for the Melbourne Victory in the Australian A-League.

Brendan Lawley (Born 18/9/88) is an attacking midfielder. Touted as a future Melbourne Victory first team player in the Australian premier football competition, the A-League, Lawley is a long term prospect for the future and likely replacement for the outgoing Harry Kewell in the Socceroos [1]. Lawley is currently in talks with the Melbourne University and Monash University Football clubs about the prospect of a scholarship [2].
