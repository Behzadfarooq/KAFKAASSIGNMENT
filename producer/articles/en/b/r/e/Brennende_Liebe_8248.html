"Brennende Liebe" (German for Burning love) is the second single from German industrial metal group OOMPH! off of Wahrheit oder Pflicht (second edition). At first, the single has been released as a b-side single like previously Niemand was, but later the song appeared on the re-edition of Wahrheit oder Pflicht. The song features Sonja Kraushofer from L'Ame Immortelle doing about half of lines.

[edit] Music video

The music video is Frankenstein-themed. Dero, Crap and Flux are scientists working in a castle, bringing monsters to life. They have already created a Frankenstein-like monster, and now they are working on a female. In the video, the male monster works as an assistant and accidentally chooses the wrong brain for the female monster which the band is working on bringing to life. When the female monster is brought to life, she falls in love with the male monster and commands him to kill their creators (the band).
