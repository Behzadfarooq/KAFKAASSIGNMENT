Brett "Gank" Jungblut is an American professional poker player, based in La Jolla, California.

Jungblut began playing tournament poker in 2002. Since then he has over twenty in the money tournament finishes including first place finishes at the Bicycle Casino's Winnin' o' the Green limit hold'em event and the World Series of Poker (WSOP) Omaha Hi-Lo championship in 2004.

Jungblut was a former member of "The Crew" alongside Scott Fischman, Russ "Dutch" Boyd and others.

As of 2006, his total live tournament winnings exceed $350,000.
