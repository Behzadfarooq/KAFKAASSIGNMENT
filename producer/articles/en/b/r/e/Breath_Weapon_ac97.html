A Breath Weapon is the cone or line shaped weapon exhaled by dragons in the fictional Dungeons & Dragons game. Each type of dragon has a different breath weapon. The Chromatic (evil) dragons have one breath weapon and the Metallic (good) dragons have two. Other dragons and semi-dragons frequently have breath weapons. One example is the Dragon Turtle's cone of steam breath weapon.

[edit] Form

Breath weapons typically come in one of three forms.

  • Line: Does damage in a straight line. For example, the Blue dragon's line of lightning.
  • Cone: Does damage in a wide cone shape. For example, the Red dragon's cone of fire.
  • Cloud: Does damage with a cloud of gas. For example, the Green dragon's cloud of chlorine gas.

[edit] Composition

Breath weapons typically are composed of one of several materials.

  • Fire: Magical fire is used by Gold Dragons and Red dragons.
  • Electricity: Lightning is exhaled by Blue and Bronze dragons.
  • Liquid: The Black dragon exhales a line of acid.
  • Gas: The Green dragon's breath weapon is a cloud of chlorine gas.
