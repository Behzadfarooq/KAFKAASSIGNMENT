Brett Seymour (born September 27, 1984 in Mackay, Australia) is an Australian rugby league player for the Cronulla Sharks club in the National Rugby League competition. His position of choice is in the halves.

The former Brisbane Broncos halfback was sacked by the club late in 2006 for player behaviour which was later cleared. He subsequently joined the Cronulla Sutherland Sharks to which he has signed a 2-year contract, effective 2007.
