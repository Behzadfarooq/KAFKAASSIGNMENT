Breathe No More was a demo recorded for Evanescence's debut mainstream album Fallen but did not make it onto the final record. A demo version was leaked onto the Internet however and a live version was used on the live DVD Anywhere but Home. Breathe No More is track 08 on the DVD and features drums and acoustic guitar, instruments not featured on the version appearing on the Elektra soundtrack.
