Brent Tate (born March 3, 1982 in Roma, Queensland) is an Australian rugby league player for the Brisbane Broncos in the National Rugby League competition. He also represents Queensland and Australia. His position of choice is at centre but can also play on the wing. Due to an ongoing neck problem Tate plays with a special brace.

Recently there has benn speculation that Tate may switch codes and sign with the Queensland Reds in 2008

[edit] Club Career

  • Junior Club: Redcliffe
  • First Grade Debut: Round 21, Brisbane v. Melbourne at Colonial, July 29, 2001
  • First Grade Record: 104 appearances scoring 36 tries
  • Premiership with the all star 2006 Brisbane Broncos

[edit] Representative games

  • State Of Origin: Nine games for Queensland between 2002 and 2006
  • International: Eleven tests for Australia
