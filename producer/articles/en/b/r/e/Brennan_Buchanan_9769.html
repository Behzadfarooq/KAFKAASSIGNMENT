Brennan "Bree" Buchanan is a character of One Life to Live. She was born on April 28, 2006, her parents are Nash Brennan and Jessica 'Tess' Buchanan. She is actually the daughter of Tess but Tess wanted Brennan safe so she let Jessica raise her. Her step-father is Antonio Vega. Her nickname is Bree, for short for Brennan.
