Brent Reilly (born 12 November 1983) is an Australian rules footballer in the Australian Football League.

Debuting with the Adelaide Football Club in 2003 as a 19-year-old, he took time to develop (like many current players at Adelaide) and struggling to retain his spot in the side. He played every Premiership game in the 2005 season, before injuring his hamstring and missing the last two finals.

Neil Craig's coaching has further helped Reilly's development, with the youngster earning selection in most matches (albeit starting on the bench).
