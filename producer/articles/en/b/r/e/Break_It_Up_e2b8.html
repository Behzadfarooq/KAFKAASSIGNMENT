Break It Up

Break It Up were a key band in the UK hardcore punk scene from 2003 until their demise in September 2006. Consisting of former members of Voorhees and Thirty Seconds Until Armageddon, the band recorded a self-titled EP on Anger Management Records and also an album with the UK's Dead & Gone Records entitled No Sides. Inspired by 80's hardcore like Uniform Choice, Insted and Negative Approach, Break It Up played regularly with fellow bands like Bold, 7 Seconds, Walk The Plank, Underdog, Justice, Mental, Insted, Marked For Death, Annhillation Time and many others.

[edit] Band Members

Demo line-up:

  • Lins (vocals)
  • Sean Readman (guitar)
  • Pete Falkous (bass)
  • Chris Fay (drums)

"No Sides" album line-up:

  • Lins (vocals)
  • Sean Readman (guitar)
  • Graeme Nicholls (lead guitar)
  • Ian Sidaway (bass)
  • Chris Fay (drums)

Later members:

  • Paul Rugman-Jones(bass)
  • Peet (guitar)
