Bred 2 Die Born 2 Live is the debut album from rapper Lil' Scrappy. The album was released on December 5, 2006.

Guests include Yung Joc, Stayfresh, Young Buck, Young Dro, Bohagon, Lil' Jon, Three 6 Mafia, Playboy Tre', Lloyd, Yo Gotti, Lil' Chris, Nook, Olivia & 50 Cent. Production came from Lil' Jon, 50 Cent, Kadis & Sean, Sha Money XL, Jazze Pha, Drumma Boy, Jonathan "JR" Rotem & Isaac "Ike Dirty" Hayes among others.

The first single is called "Money in the Bank" featuring Young Buck and is produced by Isaac "Ike Dirty" Hayes. The second single is Gangsta Gangsta featuring and produced by Lil' Jon. The third single is called "Oh Yeah (Work)" featuring E-40 and Sean P of The YoungBloodZ, and is produced by Lil' Jon. The album debuted at #24 on the Billboard 200 with about 82,000 copies sold.[1]
