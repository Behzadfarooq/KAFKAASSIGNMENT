Brent Seabrook (born April 20, 1985, in Richmond, British Columbia) is a Canadian professional ice hockey defenceman playing for the Chicago Black Hawks in the NHL.

[edit] Playing career

Brent Seabrook was selected 14th overall, in the 2003 NHL Entry Draft. He was selected as the 1st choice of the Chicago Blackhawks. He also represented Canada at the 2004 and 2005 World Junior Championships.
