Brent and Harrow is a constituency represented in the London Assembly.

It consists of the combined area of the London Borough of Brent and the London Borough of Harrow.

In elections to Westminster the London Borough of Brent is traditionally a Labour-voting area while the London Borough of Harrow is a traditionally Conservative-voting area.

The equivalent Westminster seats are:

  • Brent North (Labour)
  • Brent East (Liberal Democrat)
  • Brent South (Labour)
  • Harrow East (Labour)
  • Harrow West (Labour)
