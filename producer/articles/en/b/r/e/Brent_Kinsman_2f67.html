Brent Kinsman (born November 13, 1998) is an American child actor who typically plays a rambunctious twin alongside his identical twin brother Shane Kinsman. He played Nigel Baker in the 2003 film Cheaper by the Dozen and its 2005 sequel Cheaper by the Dozen 2. He has a starring role as Preston Scavo on the popular ABC television series Desperate Housewives.
