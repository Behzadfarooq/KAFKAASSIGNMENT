[edit] Seeing Brett Scallions with the band called "The Xs"

After seeing Fuel on tour opening for Aerosmith, I was quite shocked to see Brett Scallions in a band as a background singer, at a small bar, opening for a local band, not to mention that there were probably less than 100 people there to watch them. As a fan, I used to think of Brett as one of those people you were lucky just to see from 30ft. away. My feelings have changed dramatically since I had the oportunity to talk to him at this bar. My friend and I went over to where Brett was sitting. We figured it was a good oportunity to try and talk to him because no one else was around him anyway. He seemed okay at first with talking to us. We were asking him questions about what happened with Fuel and wondering how he felt about going from something really big to what he's now doing. He seemed a little uncomfortable with our questions. We then told him that he had an amazing voice and we just loved the song hemorrhage. We asked if he would sing a couple lines from the song for us. He then told us that we were f---ing nuts, got up and walked away from us. I was so insulted by his remark that I no longer see him as this idol. In fact I have a lot more respect for the lead guitar player in Bretts new band "The Xs". This band member sat with my friend and I at the bar. He was very polite and actually looked like he was enjoying talking to us. We asked him some questions about himself, also for the story on Brett leaving Fuel and he handled himself respectfully. I have to say, that band member is going to have a good handle on how to treat his fans when or if they make it big. I would like to thank that lead guitar player from "The Xs" for taking the time to talk to us and treating us with respect!
