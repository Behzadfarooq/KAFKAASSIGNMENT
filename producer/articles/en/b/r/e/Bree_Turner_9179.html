Bree Turner (born March 10, 1977) is an American film actress/theater actress, commercial model and dancer. She has appeared as a dancer in films including My Best Friend's Wedding (1997), The Big Lebowski (1998), She's All That (1999) and Austin Powers: The Spy Who Shagged Me (1999).

Turner was born in Palo Alto, California. She has starred in ads for companies such as Gap, Dr. Pepper, Hyundai, Toshiba and Budweiser.

As an actress, Turner has appeared in minor supporting roles in films such as The Wedding Planner, American Pie 2, Deuce Bigalow: Male Gigolo and Sorority Boys. She also had a co-starrring role in the independent film Backyard Dogs in 2000 with Walter Emanuel Jones (of Mighty Morphin' Power Rangers fame) and Scott Hamm.

In 2004, she starred as the main antagonist Tina Hammersmith in the straight to video sequel of the 2000 film Bring It On entitled Bring It On Again. In 2005, she starred in the Masters of Horror episode "Incident On and Off a Mountain Road" by Don Coscarelli. In 2006, Bree Turner costarred in the Lindsay Lohan vehicle Just My Luck.

In addition to her movie roles, Turner was also a cast member of MTV's late night series Undressed and a recurring character in the police drama Cold Case and the sitcom Spin City. She also starred in 2004's Good Girls Don't. In 2006, Turner starred in the first and/or pilot episodes of Love Monkey, Masters of Horror, Sex, Love & Secrets, Flirt, Standoff and Traveler. She also played a minor supporting role in the movie The TV Set and costarred in Jekyll + Hyde during the same year.

Turner will appear in the upcoming Firehouse Dog.
