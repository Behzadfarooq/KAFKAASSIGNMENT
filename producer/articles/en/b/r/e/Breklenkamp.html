Breklenkamp

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia
Breklenkamp in the municipality of Dinkelland.

Breklenkamp ( 52°27′N, 6°59′E) is a town in the Dutch province of Overijssel. It is a part of the municipality of Dinkelland, and lies about 16 km north of Oldenzaal.

The statistical area "Breklenkamp", which also can include the surrounding countryside, has a population of around 200.[1]
