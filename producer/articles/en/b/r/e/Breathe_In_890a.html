This article is about the Lucie Silvas album. For band, see Breathe In (band).

Breathe In is the second studio album by British pop singer-songwriter Lucie Silvas, released via Universal/Mercury Records on October 11, 2004 (see 2004 in music) in the United Kingdom, on March 29, 2005 (see 2005 in music) in the Netherlands and in April 2006 worldwide.
