Breann McGregor (born May 11, 1985 in New Orleans, Louisiana) is an American model who has appeared in many of the Special Edition issues of Playboy.

She is the oldest of three children. Growing up, Breann took an early interest in acting. Often as a child, she put her mother's camera on her dresser and pretended she was being filmed. Her acting Credits include Waiting, which came out in 2005 and another movie that will be released in 2006.

Her love of acting was only second to her desire to one day pose for Playboy. She got the idea when one day she discovered the magazine in a stack of her dad's mail. At five years old, she was curious about the book's plastic covering. Breann discovered that within the pages of Playboy there was absolutely gorgeous woman, and from that day forward she wanted to be one of them.

About four months before Hurricane Katrina hit New Orleans, Breann broke her neck in a car accident and had to wear a neck brace for three months. Three weeks after her full recovery, she was displaced by Hurricanes Katrina and Rita. She was forced away from her home for several months. Breann is now back in the city and and better than ever, she posed for the cover of Playboy's Lingerie April/May 2006 issue and was picked as Cyber Girl of the Month September 2006. She was chosen as Cyber Girl of the Year 2007.
