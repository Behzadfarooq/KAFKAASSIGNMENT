Brendon Paul Bracewell (b. 14 September 1959) in Auckland. He is the younger brother of John Bracewell. He frequently suffered injuries throughout his playing career.

At present Brendon Bracewell operates a private cricket coaching academy "Bracewell Cricket Academy" based Rathkeale College in Masterton. Academy provides Overseas Cricket Development Programme, Pre-Season Coaching Camp, Festival of Cricket etc.
