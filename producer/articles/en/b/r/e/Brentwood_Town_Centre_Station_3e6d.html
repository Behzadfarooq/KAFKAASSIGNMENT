Brentwood Town Centre Station (sometimes abbreviated as Brentwood Station) is located on an elevated portion of the Millennium Line. The station is part of Vancouver's Skytrain light rail rapid transit system, run by Translink and is one of the busier stations on the Millennium Line. The electronic fare machines at this station do not have Interac card access.

The station is located on Lougheed Highway and Willingdon Avenue in Burnaby, British Columbia, and is directly adjacent to Brentwood Town Centre, a mid-size shopping mall, with stores like Zellers, Sears Canada and London Drugs.

[edit] Design

On May 11, 2002, Busby + Associates Architects, designers of the station, were honoured for their work with a Governor General's Medal in Architecture.
