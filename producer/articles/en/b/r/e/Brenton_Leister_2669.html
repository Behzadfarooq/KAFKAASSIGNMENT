Brenton Leister (born September 3, 1985) is an English football player who currently plays for St. Patrick's Athletic F.C. in the eircom league Premier Division.

Leister is a midfielder, who can also play in defence and joined St. Pats in July 2006. His previous clubs include Leeds United and Halifax Town.
