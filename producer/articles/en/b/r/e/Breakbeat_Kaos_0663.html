Breakbeat Kaos

Breakbeat Kaos is an independent drum and bass record label based in London, UK. It is the result of a merger between Fresh's and Adam F's record labels and has released singles from artists such as Pendulum, Fresh, Adam F and several others.

Dogs On Acid and Under Construction are sub-label of Breakbeat Kaos.
