Brent Moloney (born 28 January 1984) is an Australian rules footballer.

Originating from Warrnambool, Moloney played U/18s in the TAC Cup for the Geelong Falcons. He was drafted in the 2003 pre-season draft, and made his debut with Geelong Football Club in round 14, 2003. Moloney was traded to the Melbourne Demons following the 2004 AFL season.

He is known for his hardness at the ball, ability to win kicks and his preference for long driving kicks into the forward line. Injury sidelined him for much of the 2006 season.
