Brett Holman (born March 27, 1984 in Bankstown) is an Australian football (soccer) player. He currently plays as a striker for the Dutch club NEC. He made his debut for Australia against Bahrain on February 22, 2006 in an Asian Cup qualifier. He previously played for Parramatta Power in Australia before moving to Dutch football club Feyenoord Rotterdam in 2002. Once in the Netherlands he played for Excelsior Rotterdam on loan before NEC signed him in 2006. He was educated at Christian Brothers' High School, Lewisham. He scored his first international goal against China in a friendly on the 24th March 2007.
