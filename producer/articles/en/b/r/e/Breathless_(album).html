Breathless is the sixth studio album by saxophonist Kenny G. It was released by Arista Records in 1992, and reached number 1 on the Contemporary Jazz Albums chart and number 2 on the Billboard 200 and R&B/Hip-Hop Albums charts.[1] The track "Forever in Love" won Best Instrumental Composition at the 1993 Grammy Awards.[2]
