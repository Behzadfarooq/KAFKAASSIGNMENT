Brent Mather Swan
Born June 23, 1973
Cincinnati, Ohio
Occupation Film producer/Consultant

Brent Mather Swan is a writer and film producer consulting with premiere entertainment companies in the LA area. As a former Screen Actors Guild agent and talent manager, he is currently developing interactive storytelling technology.

The Mather family is one of the first American families. Three of the most eminent and influential Puritan clergymen in colonial Massachusetts advised the founding fathers on religious policy in the early drafting of The United States Declaration of Independence. Cotton Mather, Increase Mather, Richard Mather.

Brent learned to ride horses at the Culver Academies, our nation's oldest standing cavalry school. He was trained as an Elite Rough Rider and Mounted Color Guard for the Black Horse Troop. He currently rides and trains Morgan horses in the LA area with his fianc√©e.

Brent attended The Gow School, Muskingum College and several other schools in his ongoing education.

Brent is currently a producer and writer of the feature film The Eye of the World, based on the bestselling book series The Wheel of Time by Robert Jordan[citation needed].

His recent consulting clients have included: ActiVision, The Highlands, Shapiro-Lichtman Literary Agency, The Coralie Jr. Agency, Ross World Wide Media, Digital Cinema Initiatives, Highman Investments and THQ.

As a storyteller, Brent credits Alfred Hitchcock, Rod Serling, William Shakespeare, Anne McCaffrey, Robert A. Heinlein, William Butler Yeats, Lewis Carroll, Bill Watterson, Mary Shelly and Edgar Allan Poe as significant influences.
