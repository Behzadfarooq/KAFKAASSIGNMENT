"Breathe on Me" is a pop song from Pop singer Britney Spears released from the album In the Zone, released during the second quarter of 2004.

[edit] Song Information

"Breathe on Me" was originally set as the fourth single from In the Zone. However, it was aborted and was replaced by "Outrageous". When filming the video for "Outrageous," Spears underwent a knee operation, causing her to cancel the releases of the next scheduled singles, including this and "(I Got That) Boom Boom."

[edit] Music video

Spears performing "Breathe on Me" at The Onyx Hotel Tour (2004)

The video shoot was supposed to be directed by Sanaa Hamri in Chicago, Illinois. However, Spears was still in the hospital at the time, so the shoot was cancelled, and "Breathe on Me" and "(I Got That) Boom Boom" were released without proper videos. Her performance at the "The Onyx Hotel Tour" was used as the video.

[edit] Chart performance

Fans were clamoring for the release of more singles from In the Zone after "Outrageous" was only released in the US and Japan. "Breathe on Me" was still given very minimal airplay on the radio, but her upcoming nuptials and the lack of interest in further In the Zone promotion halted any progression.
