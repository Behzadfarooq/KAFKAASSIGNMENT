[edit] Description

Brean is the largest amusement park in the southwest of England. Open from March to October every year, the leisure park has also been a concert venue for artists such as DJ Casper and Peter Andre, as well as Jason Donovan and The Wurzels. Brainiac: Science Abuse also filmed "Funfair Physics" at the park.

[edit] Attractions

  • Magic Mouse

This ride is a typical spinning wild mouse ride. It follows the normal spinning style track layout and is one of the park's bigger rides. Was previously known as "crazy mouse" until the start of the 2007 season.
Photo Ride Info on RCDB

  • Shockwave

A pinfari ride, that is typical for Pinfari, rouch, fast and unusually, has a loop!
Ride Info on RCDB Photo

  • Xtreme

No doubt by far the biggest/tallest ride at the park. Thrills only for the strong hearted! Voted scariest ride at the park by Brainiac.
Photo

  • XFactory

This ride is very similar to the traditional Miama Trip ride found within most mobile fun fair with the extra added bonus of water jets that shoot water up at the riders when they are in mid air. Another difference that makes this ride interesting is that the seats tilt downwards so riders get a better view of the water and ground below.

  • Wipe Out

This ride is the same as Vortex which is located in Thorpe Park. The ride swings riders left and right on the end of a large arm which holds riders in a rotating gondola. Photo
