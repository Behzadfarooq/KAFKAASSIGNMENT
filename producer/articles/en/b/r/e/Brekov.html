Brekov is a village and municipality in Humenné District in the Prešov Region of north-east Slovakia.

[edit] History

In historical records the village was first mentioned in 1314.

[edit] Geography

The municipality lies at an altitude of 145 metres and covers an area of 9.725 km². It has a population of about 1265 people.
