Brezovica is a village and municipality in Tvrdošín District in the Žilina Region of northern Slovakia.

[edit] History

In historical records the village was first mentioned in 1580.

[edit] Geography

The municipality lies at an altitude of 700 metres and covers an area of 19.229km². It has a population of about 1290 people.
