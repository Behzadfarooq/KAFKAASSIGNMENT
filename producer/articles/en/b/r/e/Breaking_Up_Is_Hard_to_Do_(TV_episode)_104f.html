"Breaking Up Is Hard to Do" is an episode of the UPN sitcom The Parkers.

[edit] Synopsis

While newlyweds Jerel and Kim prepare to leave for Paris, Jerel's mom, Clarise, shows up with a surprise that could threaten their marriage. Jerel is only 17. Kim decides not go with him to Paris. Meanwhile, Professor Oglevee has a falling out with Veronica. So he takes T's advice to date another woman but it turns out he only wants Veronica. After his date, he realizes that she's from a dating service and he has to pay for the date. No one could make this tight-wad pay....except for his very, very, very, very, very huge accountant. He was a BIGGGGG threat!!!!!!!!!!!!

[edit] Notes

  • Adele Givens guest starred on three episodes of Moesha as a concert usher (in two episodes) and a police officer (in one episode).
  • Mo'Nique and Adele Givens later performed stand-up comedy with Sommore and Laura Hayes in the 2001 film The Queens of Comedy.

[edit] Trivia

How could Kim be 18 in this episode? In Season 1; Episode 14: Love Is A Royal Pain, it was mentioned that she was 19.
