Brendan Coogan

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Brendan Coogan (1970 -) is a British television presenter, from Middleton, Greater Manchester, best known for previously presenting Top Gear on the BBC and for leaving the show following a drunk driving conviction. He is the brother of Actor/Comedian Steve Coogan and former Mock Turtles Frontman Martin Coogan.

In April 2006, he became a presenter for the Sky One motoring show, Vroom Vroom
