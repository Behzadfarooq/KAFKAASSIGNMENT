Breitenburg is a municipality in the district of Steinburg, in Schleswig-Holstein, Germany. It is situated on the river Stör, approx. 4 km southeast of Itzehoe.

Breitenburg is also the seat of the Amt ("collective municipality") Breitenburg, which consists of the following municipalities (population in 2005 in parentheses):

  • Breitenberg (390)
  • Breitenburg (1061)
  • Kollmoor (35)
  • Kronsmoor (192)
  • Lägerdorf (2.756)
  • Moordiek (123)
  • Moordorf (16)
  • Münsterdorf (1948)
  • Oelixdorf (1709)
  • Westermoor (371)
