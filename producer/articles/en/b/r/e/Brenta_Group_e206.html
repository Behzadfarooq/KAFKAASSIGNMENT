Brenta Group

The Brenta Group is a mountain range in the Italian Alps. It is part of the Southern Limestone Alps. It is located in the province of Trento.

The Brenta Group is separated from the Ortler Alps in the north by the Noce valley; from the Adamello-Presanella group in the west by the Campo Carlo Magno Pass and the river Sarca; from the Fiemme Alps in the east by the Adige valley.
