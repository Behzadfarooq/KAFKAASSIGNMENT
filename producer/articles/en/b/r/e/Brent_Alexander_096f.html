Brent Alexander (born July 10, 1971) was a free safety in the NFL. He was signed as a undrafted free-agent by the Arizona Cardinals in 1994. In his career he played for the Arizona Cardinals, Carolina Panthers, Pittsburgh Steelers, and New York Giants. On February 25, 2006 he announced his retirement from playing in the NFL. On March 1, 2006, his agent said that Brent doesn't want to retire yet.
