Bredgar and Wormshill Light Railway

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Bredgar & Wormshill Light Railway is located near Wormshill in Kent, just South of Sittingbourne. It is a 2 ft (610 mm) gauge narrow gauge private railway that is half a mile in length.
