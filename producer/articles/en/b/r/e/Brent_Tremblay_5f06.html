Brent Tremblay

Brent Francis Tremblay (born November 1, 1957 in North Bay, Ontario) is a retired Canadian ice hockey defenseman who was drafted in 1977 by the Washington Capitals. Tremblay played only 10 games with the Capitals before he retired from professional hockey in 1979.
