Brett Pitman

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Brett Pitman (born 31 January 1988 in Jersey) is an English professional footballer who plays as a striker for AFC Bournemouth. He was signed as a 16 year-old after being spotted playing for Jersey league side St. Saviour.
