Breda (disambiguation)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Breda may refer to the following:

  • Breda a city in southern Netherlands
  • Breda a municipality in Selva (comarca), Girona (province), Catalonia, Spain.
  • Breda is a city in Iowa, United States.
  • a salticid spider genus.
  • A number of related Italian industrial manufacturing companies including bus and streetcar maker Ansaldobreda, S.P.A. and small arms maker Breda Meccanica Bresciana.
  • Toussaint Bréda, better known as Toussaint L'Ouverture.
  • Breda Royal Beer, generally known as simply Breda. A Dutch lager sold almost exclusively in the Channel Islands.
