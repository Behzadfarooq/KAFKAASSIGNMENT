Bretton Woods Mountain Resort is a ski area located in Bretton Woods, New Hampshire across from the Mt. Washington hotel.

 This ski or skiing-related article is a stub. You can help Wikipedia by expanding it.

Location North of Rt. 302 in New Hampshire, it is accesible from all major highways in the NorthEast. Set Up Composed of 434 Acres of a natural wildlife reserve, this mountain is enough to satisfy the most expert skiers and snowboarders. My personal experience is that it is a great place to learn to either ski or snowboard, and the lesson program is top notch. The resortt is expanding and will soon cover three mountain peaks. 97% of the trails have snowmaking, so the fun never ends. There are 101 trails currently and the resort recieves an average of 200 inches of snow per year. The uphill lifts include 9 Lifts, four of them are high speed quads. They run at a capcity of 14,000 people per hour. Provides day/night skiing, low prices, rentals, food, lodge, beautiful scenery of Mt. Washington, easy commuter trams to the Mt. Washington Hotel and other commoditites. The Mountain is 3100 feet tall, easily making it one of the tallest in the northeast.
