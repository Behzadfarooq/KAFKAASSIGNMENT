Brent Spencer Follett QC (1809 – 1886) was a British Conservative politician.

The son of Benjamin Follett and Ann Webb, he was made a Queen's Counsel on 11 July 1851 and became a Bencher of Lincoln's Inn on 3 November of the same year. From 1852 to 1857, he was Member of Parliament (MP) for Bridgwater, and from 1862 to 1886 Registrar of the Land Registry.

On 14 October 1814, he married Caroline Amelia Skirrow, daughter of Walter Skirrow. They had two sons.
