Brett Allen

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

For the Australian Football League official, see Brett Allen (umpire).

Brett Allen (born 12th May 1982), in Hertfordshire, is a British television presenter and Journalist.

Before going to university he worked mostly on shows for Children's BBC, including some appearances in ChuckleVision and Grange Hill.

Whilst at university he made the newspapers and TV in the UK and Australia, after placing a bet, for a joke, that he could date Kylie Minogue.

After university he returned to television, appearing on, BBC, ITV and Sky Digital.

Brett is also a Motoring Journalist, and has contributed to motoring magazines and TV programmes.
