The Brevard County Manatees are a minor league baseball team that plays its 70-game home schedule at the Space Coast Stadium in Viera, Florida. The team is a member of the Florida State League, and has been the A-Advanced affiliate of the Milwaukee Brewers since 2005. Previously the Manatees were affiliated with the Montreal Expos and Florida Marlins.

The Manatees will be affiliated with the Milwaukee Brewers through 2010 as the teams signed a four-year Player Development Contract (PDC) this last fall.
