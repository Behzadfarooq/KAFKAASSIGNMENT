Bredsten

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Bredsten is a small village in Egtved municipality, Denmark. Bredsten is situated 11 kilometres west of Vejle, and it has approximately 1500 inhabitants. The village is quite old, indicated by the church which dates back to the Middle Ages though modified by the local entrepreneur Lichtenstein i the 1740's. Bredsten is known for a few famous persons, among which you find the businessman Harry Motor, Jens Madsen, Grand Champion of the quiz show, Jeopardy, Lars Døhr, former soccer player in Vejle Boldklub and the sister of former soccer legend John Sivebæk. Furthermore Bredsten is famous for its status as one of the most boring villages in the Southern part of Jutland.
