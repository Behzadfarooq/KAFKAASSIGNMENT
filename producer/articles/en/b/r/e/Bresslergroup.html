Bresslergroup is a full service product development firm founded in 1970 by industrial designer, Peter Bressler. They are headquartered in Philadelphia, PA in the Marketplace Design Center. The staff of 25 multidisciplinary professionals includes user research experts, industrial designers, graphic and interface designers, mechanical and electrical engineers. According to the company website, they "create innovative new product solutions via integrated user research, product design and engineering services."

The firm has experience across diverse markets including work for Black & Decker, Becton Dickinson, Air Products, J&J and Schick. They have won more than 80 major design awards including the Industrial Designers Society of America (IDSA) IDEA, Appliance Design, Japanese G-mark, and iF Hanover. They have authored more than 100 design and utility patents. Employees affiliated with; IDSA, PDMA and AIGA.

[edit] Services:

  • Design and user research, including ethnographic research
  • Human factors expertise that includes user interface design and universal design
  • Ideation / structured brainstorming
  • Industrial Design
  • Graphic and packaging design
  • Mechanical and electrical engineering
  • Parametric solids / CAD modeling using Pro/E and SolidWorks
  • Finite Element Analysis
  • Modelmaking and prototyping
  • Production engineering and documentation
  • Extensive knowledge of materials and production processes
  • Domestic and off-shore production sourcing
