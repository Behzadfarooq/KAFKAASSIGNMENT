Brent Catchpole is a New Zealand politician. He is a member of the New Zealand First party. He was elected to Parliament as a list MP in the 2002 elections, but lost his seat in the 2005 elections. He was his party's spokesperson on Communications & IT, Environment, Biosecurity, Internal Affairs, and Tourism portfolios. Before entering politics, Catchpole worked first as an accountant and then as a marketing director for a tourism company. He now works as a political lobbying consultant.
