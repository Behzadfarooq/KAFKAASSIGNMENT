Brendan Kelly (musician)

Brendan Kelly (born September 8, 1976) is the bassist/vocalist of the Chicago-based punk band The Lawrence Arms, as well as guitarist/lead vocalist in The Falcon. Kelly's former bands include Slapstick and The Broadways. He is often known for his raspy vocals and drunken demeanor. Kelly also appeared on The Daily Show in a correspondent piece by Ed Helms entitled "The Clash."

He attended Northwestern University in Evanston, IL.
