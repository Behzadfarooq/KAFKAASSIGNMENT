Break of Dawn is an album by Do As Infinity, released in 2000. This is the only album cover where Dai Nagao is included in along with the other two members.

  2. "Standing on the Hill"
  3. "Oasis"

  5. "Kokoro no Chizu" (心の地図, "Kokoro no Chizu"? Map of the Heart)
  6. "Heart"
  7. "Raven"
  8. "Welcome!"
  9. "Painful"
  10. "Tangerine Dream"
  11. "Yesterday & Today"
