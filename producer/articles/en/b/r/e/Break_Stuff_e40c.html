"Break Stuff" is a song by rapcore/nu metal band Limp Bizkit. Break Stuff is the band's fourth and final single that resulted from their studio album Significant Other. The single was released along side "N 2 Gether Now".

The video contains cameos by Jonathan Davis, Eminem, Dr. Dre, Pauly Shore and Snoop Dogg.
