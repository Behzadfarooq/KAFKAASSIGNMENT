Brent E. Erwin

Brent E. Erwin is a 16-year veteran of the Comics Industry, having opened his first Comic Shop in Waco, Texas at age 19. Brent went on to become a Sales Representative for Diamond Comics Distributors, working with Acclaim Comics in New York City. Leaving Diamond Comics Distributors Brent became a Marketing Coordinator for the direct market working for Fabian Nicieza the Publisher of Acclaim Comics. Brent has also worked for Wizard Entertainment, Publisher of the leading comics industry magazine Wizard the Comics Magazine, as Senior Advertising Account Manager.

In 1998 Brent launched SmallPressComics.com, an on-line community devoted to Small Press and Independent Comics. In 2003 Brent along with comic's devotees David Hedgecock and Mike Hall founded Ape Entertainment publishers of high-quality original graphic novels, comic books, and illustrated fiction.
