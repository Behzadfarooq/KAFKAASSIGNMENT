Brennan Mulwray is a character on the television series Mutant X. He is portrayed by Victor Webster.

Brennan Mulwray is one of the genetically engineered superhumans called "New Mutants". He has the ability to generate and project electricity; in later seasons of the show he was also given the ability to fly. Brennan is a member of the benevolent organization Mutant X, and has a romantic history with fellow mutant Shalimar Fox.
