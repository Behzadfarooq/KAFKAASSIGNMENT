Breeveld

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia
The statistical district of Breeveld in the municipality of Woerden.

Breeveld ( 52°6′N, 4°56′E) is a village in the Dutch province of Utrecht. It is a part of the municipality of Woerden, and lies about 4 km northeast of the city centre.

The statistical district "Breeveld", which covers the village and the surrounding countryside, has a population of around 130.[1]
