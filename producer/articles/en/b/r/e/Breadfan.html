Breadfan is a heavy metal song originlly recorded by Budgie. Metallica released a version of the song in September of 1988 as a B-side to their "Harvester of Sorrow" single. It was later included on their 1998 album Garage Inc.

[edit] Trivia

  • The song is rumored to be about greed in the music industry.
  • At the very end of the Metallica version of the song, you can hear a child say "Mommy, Where's Fluffy?"
  • The intro to the song was reused in the Red Hot Chili Peppers song Around the World.
