Brent Stait (born September 9, 1959) is a Canadian actor, best known for his roles as Rev Bem in the science fiction television series Andromeda and Louis Ferretti in Stargate SG-1. He was born in Snow Lake, Manitoba.

Stait left Andromeda due to allergies from his heavy prosthetics use for his character on the show.

He later played the body of Michael Kenmore in his Wraith form in "Allies"; however, Connor Trinneer continued to provide the voice.
