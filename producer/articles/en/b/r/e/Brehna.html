Brehna

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Brehna is a town in the district of Bitterfeld, in Saxony-Anhalt, Germany. It is situated southwest of Bitterfeld. It is part of the Verwaltungsgemeinschaft ("collective municipality") Bitterfeld.Important in this city is the church where Katharina von Bora, the wife of Martin Luther lifed.
