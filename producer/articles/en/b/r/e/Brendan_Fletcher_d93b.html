Brendan Fletcher is a Canadian actor born on December 15, 1981 in Comox Valley, British Columbia. His acting debut was in 1996 in the CBC television movie Little Criminals, earning him a Leo Award. which His filmography includes Tideland, Freddy vs. Jason, Ginger Snaps Back, Ginger Snaps 2: Unleashed, Anatomy of a Hate Crime, The Five Senses, Air Bud, Eighteen, Paper Moon Affair and Law of Enclosures. He also played Stirling Patterson (nickname: Stink) in The Adventures of Shirley Holmes and in the MTV miniseries Anatomy of a Hate Crime.
