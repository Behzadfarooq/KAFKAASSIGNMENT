Brenda C. Barnes is the president, chairman and chief executive of Sara Lee, and previously was the first female CEO of Sara Lee. She has been listed in Forbes power rankings of The World's 100 Most Powerful Women since 2004.

Barnes, a graduate of East Leyden High School, Augustana College and Loyola University, has moved Sara Lee's headquarters out of downtown Chicago to suburban Downers Grove.

[edit] Career

  • 1976: Business Manager, Wilson Sporting Goods
  • 1981: VP of Marketing, Frito-Lay
  • 1984: Group VP of Marketing, Pepsi-Cola;
  • 1991: President, Pepsi-Cola South/West
  • 1993: COO, Pepsi-Cola North America
  • 1996 - 1998: CEO, Pepsi-Cola NA
  • Nov 1999 - Mar 2000: Interim President & COO, Starwood Hotels & Resorts
