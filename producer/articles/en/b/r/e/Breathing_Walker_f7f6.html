Breathing Walker

Breathing Walker was a rock band formed by Tonie Joy (guitar), Jonathan Vance (vocals), Mark Laurence (drums), Tim Horner (violin, bass), Zak Fusciello (drums and percussion) and Alex Badertscher (guitar) after the break up of Joy, Vance and Laurence's previous band, Moss Icon. The band only released one album, which was self-titled and released on the Vermin Scum record label. The band produced music that sounded somewhat like Moss Icon, but had more of a "tribal" vibe to it.
