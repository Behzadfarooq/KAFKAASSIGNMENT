Hey the name's Bren and if you're reading this it shows how easy I am to find on the internet. I'm into gridiron, podcasting, and a few other interests so that's mainly where you'll find me here on Wikipedia.

I'm also into photography and support Commons with images where I can. Feel free to check out my Commons contributions or my Flickr site. If you see anything you would like to use on Flickr but has an incompatible licence (most of my images were uploaded with a CC-nc component), please message me and I'll adjust it. I'm also living in Sydney and travel regularly throughout the NSW Central West so if you need a picture taken out in the field I'm open for ideas.
