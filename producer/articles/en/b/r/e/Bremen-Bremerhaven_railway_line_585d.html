The Bremen-Bremerhaven railway line is a railway line connecting the German cities Bremen and Bremerhaven with each other. The only major station inbetween is situated in Osterholz-Scharmbeck. The line was fully opened in 1862 and today mostly sees freight traffic and local passenger trains. The last long-distance trains were InterRegio trains on the line Cuxhaven-Luxembourg that were discontinued in 2001.
