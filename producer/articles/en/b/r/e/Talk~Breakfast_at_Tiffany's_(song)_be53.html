[edit] Keep the article here

The song was a major hit in 1995. Other wiki articles are devoted to individual songs. I think that because the song title is also a title of a major motion picture, a play, and the famous novella by Truman Capote that it should have it's own entry. Perhaps the article it needs to be fleshed out. --Sp3lly 07:29, 21 September 2006 (UTC)

Do not merge I agree that the article should remain. This is a song by a one-hit wonder and many people might be searching for the song title trying to figure out the artist. Hopefully the article can be expanded. Vivaldi (talk) 04:01, 28 September 2006 (UTC)
In fact, I just came here for exactly that purpose Sherurcij (Speaker for the Dead) 17:29, 29 December 2006 (UTC)
