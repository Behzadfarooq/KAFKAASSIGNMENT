Brent Hughes (born April 5, 1966, in New Westminster, British Columbia) is a former ice hockey left wing. He played in the National Hockey League for the Winnipeg Jets, Boston Bruins, Buffalo Sabres, and New York Islanders.

In his NHL career, Hughes played in 357 games, scoring 41 goals and 39 assists.
