Breaking Kayfabe
(2006)


Breaking Kayfabe is the debut album by Canadian rapper Cadence Weapon, released in 2005 on Upper Class Recordings. It will be released in the United States by Epitaph Records on March 13, 2007.

The album was shortlisted for the inaugural Polaris Music Prize in 2006. However, he lost to Final Fantasy's He Poos Clouds.
