Brenda Costa (born on November 8, 1982 in Rio de Janeiro) is a Brazilian fashion model and former swimmer. She is 177 cm (5 feet 9.5 inches) tall. Her model career started when she was 16 years old [1], after she was discovered in the Ipanema neighborhood by Agência Mega [2].

She was born deaf.[3]

Brenda Costa is placed 23 in the Models.com Top 25 Sexiest Models [4].

She was on the cover of the April 2004 French edition of ELLE magazine.
