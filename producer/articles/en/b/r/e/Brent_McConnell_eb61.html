Brent McConnell (born November 12, 1980) is an Australian rugby league player. He made his First Grade Debut for the North Queensland Cowboys against the South Sydney Rabbitohs in Round 17, 2006. In that match McConnell scored the match-winning try against Souths to win the game for North Queensland 16-14 with only five minutes left. He only played two games in 2006, the other being against the Penrith Panthers in Round 19.

For the 2007 season, McConnell has moved to the Brisbane Broncos.
