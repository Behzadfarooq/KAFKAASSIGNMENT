Brett Clark (born on December 23, 1976 in Wapella, Saskatchewan) is a professional ice hockey defenceman who currently plays for the Colorado Avalanche of the NHL.

Clark was drafted in the 6th round, 154th overall by the Montreal Canadiens in the 1996 NHL Entry Draft. On February 24th, 2007, Clark played in his 300th career NHL game against the Los Angeles Kings.
