Brent Olson is a writer living near Clinton, Minnesota. His first three books are The Lay of the Land, A View from the Prairie, Letters from a Peasant, and Still Whistling, the only sane response to a complicated world. The Midwest Independent Publishers Association awarded Brent Olson the award for Best Regional Book for Letters from a Peasant

His weekly column, Independently Speaking, is syndicated around the world.
