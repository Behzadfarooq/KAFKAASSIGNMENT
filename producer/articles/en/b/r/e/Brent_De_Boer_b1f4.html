Brent De Boer

Brent "Fathead" De Boer is a drummer and backup vocalist with The Dandy Warhols. He replaced founding member Eric Hedford in 1998 and is a cousin of Warhols' frontman Courtney Taylor-Taylor. He graduated from Lakeridge High School in Lake Oswego, Oregon in 1994.
