Breton Wikipedia

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Breton Wikipedia is the Breton language version of Wikipedia, run by the Wikimedia Foundation. As of November 14, 2006, it has 10,925 articles, making it the 52nd largest Wikipedia.
