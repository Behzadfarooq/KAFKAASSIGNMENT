Brett Ebert (born November 18, 1983) is an Australian rules footballer.

The son of Port Adelaide legend Russell Ebert, Ebert began his career with the Port Adelaide Magpies in the SANFL and was drafted by the Port Adelaide AFL side under the father/son rule at the 2002 AFL Draft. Winning a Magarey Medal in 2003, Ebert made his AFL debut in Round 2, 2004, winning an AFL Rising Star nomination in 2004.
