Bret Wolfe

Bret Wolfe is an American porn star and a film and theatre actor who appeared in gay pornographic movies and now mainstream movies. He was born in San Francisco, California.

He has won ten porn industry awards, including the "People's Choice" for Rookie of the Year at the 2002 Gay Entertainment Awards, and the 2003 GayVN and Grabby "Newcomer of the Year" awards.

Prior to the porn industry Wolfe performed in 36 stage plays, including The Normal Heart, Our Town, and Night of the Iguana. He toured with two shows: Hello, Dolly! and Jesus Christ Superstar. He was ranked first in Dramatic Interpretation on the college-level competition circuit, representing the state of California in the national competition. He studied Theatre History and Theology in College, and worked as a staff producer with Dick Clark Communications. Acting has always been his true passion.

Wolfe also writes a monthly political column "Wolfe's Howl" chronicling his journey, from politics and porn to acting and relationships. He is currently writing his first novel, and starring in his twenty-first and final video. He is also a regular cast member of the gay soap opera Wet Palms. His career is now focused on crossing over into mainstream entertainment. Wolfe performed in the independent feature film Long Term Relationship, which was released in Fall 2006. He is set start shooting is second feature film "Back Soon" late summer 2006. He lives in Hollywood,CA.
