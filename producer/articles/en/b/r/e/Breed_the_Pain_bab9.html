Breed The Pain is the third album by New Zealand metal band 8 Foot Sativa. It was released in New Zealand on 11 January 2005 by Intergalactic Records. It was later released worldwide on 7 March 2005 by Black Mark Records, and in Australia on 18 April 2005 by Roadrunner Records. This album was the first and and only album to feature vocalist Matt Sheppard.
