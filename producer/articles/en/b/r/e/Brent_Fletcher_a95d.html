Brent Fletcher

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Brent Fletcher is an American stuntman and television writer. He has performed stunts in films such as Blank Check, Clean Slate, Suicide Kings, The Watcher, Miss Congeniality 2: Armed & Fabulous and Mr. & Mrs. Smith. He has also written episodes of the television series Lost and Angel.[1]
