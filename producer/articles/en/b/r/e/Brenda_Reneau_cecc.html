Brenda Reneau is a Republican politician from the US state of Oklahoma. Reneau served as the Oklahoma Commissioner of Labor for three consecutive terms. First elected in 1994, she is the first and only woman to serve as Labor Commissioner. She failed to be reelected, and her term ended in January 2007.
