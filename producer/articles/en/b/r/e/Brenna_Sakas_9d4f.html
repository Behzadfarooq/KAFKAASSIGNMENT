Brenna Katheleen Sakas (born June 4, 1984) is a beauty queen from Chandler, Arizona who has competed in the Miss USA pageant.

Sakas was crowned Miss Arizona USA on November 6, 2005 in Phoenix, Arizona. It was her first attempt at this title, although she had previously competed in the Miss Arizona 2004 pageant. On April 21, 2006 Sakas represented Arizona in the Miss USA 2006 pageant broadcast live from Baltimore, Maryland, placing in the top 15. This was only Arizona's second placement since 1998. The nationally televised pageant was won by Tara Conner of Kentucky.

Sakas is a 2002 graduate of Seton Catholic High School in Chandler, Arizona and is now senior at Arizona State University pursuing a degree in Human Communication with an emphasis in speech communication and a minor in Family and Human Development. She would like to also obtain a Masters Degree in Family and Human Development.

When not involved in her studies, Sakas is a refreshment cart attendant at the Seville Golf and Country Club.

Sakas is passionate about community involvement and has volunteered with a number of organizations, using her title to advance worthy causes, particularly the Arizona chapter of Habitat for Humanity.

She is also heavily involved with music, dance and drama. She has had roles in numerous shows including Oklahoma! (Ado Annie), Fiddler on the Roof, Miss Me Kate, Grease (musical), Lucky Stiff, and in Little Mary Sunshine as Nancy Twinkle.

Her other interests include learning to play golf, public speaking, reading and Reality TV.
