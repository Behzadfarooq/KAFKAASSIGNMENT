Brent "The Disciple" Cooper (b. October 28, 1973, Nashville) is a professional boxer. He is also a devout Christian and works as a personal trainer. The vast majority of his professional fights have been contested in Nashville.

[edit] Boxing career

He was a contestant on the reality TV show The Contender. On the show, he was placed on the East Coast team and fought Anthony Bonsante in the sixth First Round fight. This was a surprise match, and couldn't have helped him in his preparation.
