Brent Harding (born March 3, 1967) is a bass player for the famous California punk group Social Distortion.

Harding playing bass in Social Distortion. photo by Erika Harding
Harding with Social Distortion in Hamburg, Germany 2005. photo by Erika Harding
Harding with Social Distortion in New York City at the Nokia Theater, October, 2005. photo by Erika Harding
Harding with Sylvain Sylvain of the New York Dolls in New York City at the Nokia Theater, October, 2005. photo by Erika Harding

He joined Social Distortion in November 2004 and replaced Matt Freeman, who is best known for working as the bass player of Rancid.

Harding was the bass player for Social Distortion's Mike Ness during Ness' Cheating At Solitiare tour and recorded with Ness on his second solo offering Under the Influences. His previous bands include Deke Dickerson And The Ecco-Fonics, The Eugene Edwards Band, The Lucky Stars, The Sleepwalkers and The What-Me Worry? Jug Band. He also plays stand up for an insurgent bluegrass band called Whiskey Chimp in his home town of Ventura, Ca.
