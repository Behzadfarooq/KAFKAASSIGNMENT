BRender

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

BRender
Developer: Argonaut Software
OS: Windows, MS-DOS, Playstation
Use: Game Engine
Website: The official BRender page

BRender (abbreviation of Blazing Renderer) is a development toolkit and a real-time 3D graphics engine for computer games, simulators and graphic tools. It was developed and licenced by now defunct Argonaut Software. The engine had support for Intel's MMX instruction set and it supported Microsoft Windows, MS-DOS and Sony PlayStation platforms. Support for 3D hardware graphics accelerator cards was added.

[edit] Games using BRender engine

  • 3D Movie Maker
  • Aladdin in Nasira's Revenge
  • Carmageddon
  • FX Fighter
  • FX Fighter Turbo
  • Croc: Legend of the Gobbos
  • Dr. Who, Destiny Of The Doctors
  • Independence War and its add-on campaign Defiance
  • Pete Sampras Tennis 97
  • Play with Teletubbies (on Win 95/98)
  • Privateer 2: The Darkening
