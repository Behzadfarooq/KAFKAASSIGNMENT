Brent South is a parliamentary constituency represented in the House of Commons of the Parliament of the United Kingdom. It elects one Member of Parliament (MP) by the first past the post system of election.

Since its creation on 1974, the constituency has consistently elected Labour MPs with large majorities. At the next general election, Brent South will be dissolved and split between nearby Brent North and two newly-created constituencies: Brent Central and Hampstead and Kilburn.
