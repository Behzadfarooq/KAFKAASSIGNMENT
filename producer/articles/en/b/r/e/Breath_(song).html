"Breath" is the second single and the third track off of post-grunge band Breaking Benjamin's third album, Phobia. It is the band's highest charted song ever.

It is currently #1 on the US Mainstream Rock Tracks chart, making it Breaking Benjamin's first No.1 Hit. It is also #4 on the US Modern Rock Tracks chart. It has also reached #2 on Billboard's Bubbling Under Hot 100 chart, equivalent to #102 on the US Hot 100.

A contest in which fans create a music video for "Breath," called the Breath Fan Video Contest, has begun, and the band has selected 4 videos as finalists. A winner will be announced shortly.
