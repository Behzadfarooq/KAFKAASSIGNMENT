Breakfast at Sweethearts was the second studio, and third overall album by Australian pub rock band Cold Chisel, released in February of 1979.

All songs by Don Walker, except as noted

  2. "Merry-Go-Round"
  3. "Dresden"
  4. "Goodbye (Astrid Goodbye)" (Walker, Jimmy Barnes)
  5. "Plaza"
  6. "Shipping Steel"
  7. "I'm Gonna Roll Ya"
  8. "Showtime"
  9. "Breakfast at Sweethearts"
  10. "The Door"
