"Breakin' There's No Stoppin' Us" was the single from Ilanda feat. Joanne.

[edit] Song Information

Ilanda teamed up with Joanne in 2004 with the release of Breakin' There's No Stoppin' Us, released on March 20, 2004. It debuted and peaked at #38 on the ARIA Single Charts.

Breakin' There's No Stoppin' Us was the theme to the movie The Wog Boy.

[edit] Track Information

Breakin' There's No Stoppin' Us features three versions on its single.

The single contains the tracks:
Track #01: Breakin' There's No Stoppin' Us [Radio Edit] 3:57
Track #02: Breakin' There's No Stoppin' Us [Extended Radio Mix] 5:37
Track #03: Breakin' There's No Stoppin' Us [Club Mix] 6:54
Track #04: Close To You 3:28
Track #05: Move 3:41
Track #06: Starlite Cafe 3:40

Breakin' There's No Stoppin' Us can be found on the soundtrack album to The Wog Boy.
