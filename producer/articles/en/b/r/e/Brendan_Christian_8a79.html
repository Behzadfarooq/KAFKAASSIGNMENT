Brendan Christian

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Brendan Christian (born December 11, 1983 in Antigua) is a sprinter from Antigua and Barbuda who specializes in the 200 metres.

He is best known for winning a silver medal in this event at the 2002 World Junior Championships, where he also finished sixth in the 100 metres race.

He then competed at the 2004 Summer Olympics, reaching the quarter-final, and the 2006 Commonwealth Games where he reached the semi-final.
