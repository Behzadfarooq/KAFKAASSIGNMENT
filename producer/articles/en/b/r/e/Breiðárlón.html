Breiðárlón is a glacier lake at the south end of the Icelandic glacier Vatnajökull.

It is not far from Skaftafell National Park and the better known glacier lake Jökulsárlón. From the glacier lake Breiðárlón a little river flows into the Fjallsárlón.
