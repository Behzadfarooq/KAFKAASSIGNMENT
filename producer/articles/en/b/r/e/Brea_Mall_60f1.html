The Brea Mall is a shopping mall located in the northern Orange County community of Brea, California. Brea is within minutes of California State University Fullerton. It is owned and operated by the Simon Property Group, and is home to four major department stores, over 175 specialty shops and boutiques, and a food court. It is approximately 1,310,000 square feet (1.31 million).
