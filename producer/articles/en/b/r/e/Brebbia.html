Brebbia is a comune (municipality) in the Province of Varese in the Italian region Lombardy, located about 60 km northwest of Milan and about 14 km west of Varese. As of 31 December 2004, it had a population of 3,250 and an area of 6.3 km².[1]

Brebbia borders the following municipalities: Belgirate, Besozzo, Ispra, Malgesso, Travedona-Monate.
