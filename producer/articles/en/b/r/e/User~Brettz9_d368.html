[edit] MORE IMMEDIATE WISHLIST FOR WIKIPEDIA

It'd be nice to have the option to save notes here to yourself as well for private viewing only (e.g., pages one frequently wishes to visit (but not necessarily those one is watching), etc.) This could be useful not only for privacy but just for hiding away things probably not of interest to one's visitors.

It'd also be cool to have such preferred items show up as bookmarks in the margin or somewhere.

It'd be nice to have check-in-check-out as in Dreamweaver, to at least know if someone has already checked an article out to edit it (if not be prevented from saving it).
