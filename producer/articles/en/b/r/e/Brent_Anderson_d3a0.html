Brent Eric Anderson (born 1955 in San Jose, California) is an American comic book artist.

Anderson is best known for illustrating the X-Men graphic novel God Loves, Man Kills and his co-creation (with writer Kurt Busiek and cover artist Alex Ross), Astro City.

He began working for Marvel Comics in 1979 with Ka-Zar The Savage [1], written by Bruce Jones. He has also been the artist for Strikeforce: Morituri, Somerset Holmes (also with Jones) and on spin-offs of J. Michael Straczynski's Rising Stars.
