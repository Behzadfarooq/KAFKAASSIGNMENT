[edit] The Beginning of Something Great

On a dismal December night in 1985, Brett Clouser graced the world with his presence for the first time. Ever since that momentous occasion he has continued to bless and impact the world in unimaginable ways. Throughout his life he has been known for his creativity, ambition, along with his musical and mathematical prowess. With his innovative mind, Clouser, at the age of thirteen began to outlet his creativity. This creativity sprung forth with his two and three dimensional creations. His impeccable craftsmanship was matched by no other through his intricate woodworkings.

[edit] High School Years (2000-2004)

His interests began to transpose into other fields. He began to transition from a focus on woodworkings to drafting on the computer. Continuing his passion for design, Brett Clouser, took classes allowing him to be an architect and engineer. He created detailed sketches of houses of his own design, as well as designed and produced engineering mechanisms. During these years, he ignited his passion for mathematics as well as music.



[edit] The College Years (2005-Present)

After spending a year at home attending the local junior college, Brett Clouser left his home of eighteen years to the land with year-round sun, a thriving economy, endless opportunities and gorgeous women. He departed on his way to Califoria to continue his higher education. His high school years were a period where he lacked the foresight into his future but was confident and comfortable that Califoria was to be the setting for the next chapter in his life. With a blink of an eye, school began. After bouncing around from major to major not fully comprehending what was in store for Brett, he decided to major in Business Administration. He has continued to hold onto his interests and passions in life. The creativity, ambition, drive, mathematical intuition, and musical prowess has kept with him throughout his life thus far. The future looks bright for Brett Clouser as his dreams transcend to reality.
