Brescia University is a coeducational Catholic university located in Owensboro, Kentucky, in the United States. It was founded in 1950 by the Ursuline Sisters of Mount Saint Joseph as Brescia College. It became an accredited university in 1998 with the addition of Masters degree programs in Curriculum and Instruction and Management.
