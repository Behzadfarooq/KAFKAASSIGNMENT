Brewer twins

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Derek and Keith Brewer, known as the Brewer Twins, are famous male modeling twins.

[edit] Biography

They were born on September 21, 1973) at Torrance Memorial Hospital in Torrance, California. Derek was born fifteen minutes before Keith â€” neither their mother nor the doctors knew a second baby was to be expected.

During their modeling career, the twins worked for clients such as GAP, Guess?, Yves Saint Laurent and Armani.

They have worked with photographers such as Bruce Weber, Steven Meisel, Tiziano Magni, Claus Wickwrath, Randall Mesdon, Marko Realmonte and also with model Cindy Crawford. They have appeared on the covers of Glamour, Esquire, Mizz, Sassy, Dolly, Student Life, Vogue, Fortune amongst others.

There are several nude pictures of the twins in circulation that were taken by Weber which were originally published in Joe's Magazine in 1998. They depict the twins naked, wrapped in each other's arms, fully entwined, in the woods, and in bed. This photoshoot has been noted as being inspirational for similar shoots by the Carlson Twins in their Abercrombie and Fitch campaign.
