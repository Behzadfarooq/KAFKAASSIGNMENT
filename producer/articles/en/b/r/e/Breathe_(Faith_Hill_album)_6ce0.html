Breathe is the fourth album by country artist Faith Hill, released in 1999 (see 1999 in music). It won a Grammy Award for Best Country Album. Hill was one of the earliest of the late-1990s country divas that were popular in the US, including Shania Twain, sheDaisy and the Dixie Chicks. Her husband, Tim McGraw, with whom she duets on "Let's Make Love", is also a successful country singer.
