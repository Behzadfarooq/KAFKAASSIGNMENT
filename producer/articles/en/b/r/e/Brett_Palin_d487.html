Brett Palin (born June 23, 1984 in Nanaimo, British Columbia, Canada) is a Canadian ice hockey defenseman currently playing for the Omaha Ak-Sar-Ben Knights of the American Hockey League. He previously played Major Junior in the WHL Kelowna Rockets organization winning the Memorial Cup once in 2003-2004.
