Breakthru Publishing

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Breakthru Publishing is a private[1] publishing company based out of Houston, Texas. The company is mainly focused on producing dieting audiotapes and books, but also published[2] Jane Self's 60 Minutes and the Assassination of Werner Erhard, in 1992.

[edit] Published Books

  • 2002 - Who Taught You That: Answers to 101 Commonly Asked Questions in Matters Related to Sex, Leah Schwartz
  • 1992 - 60 Minutes and the Assassination of Werner Erhard, by Jane Self, Ph.D.
  • 1992 - The One Hour Orgasm: A New Approach to Achieving Maximum Sexual Pleasure, Bob Schwartz, Ph.D.
  • 1990 - Diets Still Don't Work...How to Lose Weight Step-By-Step Even After You've Failed at Dieting, by Bob Schwartz, Ph.D.
  • 1991 - The 14 Day Stress Cure...A New Approach for Dealing With Stress That Can Change Your Life, by Mort Orman, M.D.
  • 1984 - Diets Don't Work...The Secrets of Losing Weight Step-By-Step When All Else Fails, by Bob Schwartz, Ph.D.
