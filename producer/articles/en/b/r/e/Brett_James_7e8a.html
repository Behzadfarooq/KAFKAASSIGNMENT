Brett James is an American country music singer-songwriter, best known for co-writing Carrie Underwood's country single "Jesus, Take the Wheel".

Brett James has also written several singles for other artists. These include:

  • Jessica Andrews - "Helplessly, Hopelessly", "Who I Am"
  • Chris Cagle - "Wal-Mart Parking Lot"
  • Kenny Chesney - "Keg in the Closet", "You Save Me"
  • Kenny Chesney with Uncle Kracker - "When The Sun Goes Down"
  • Billy Ray Cyrus - "You Won't Be Lonely Now"
  • Dusty Drake - "Say Yes"
  • Emerson Drive - "November"
  • Sara Evans - "Cheatin'"
  • Josh Gracin - "I Want To Live", "Stay With Me (Brass Bed)"
  • Pat Green - "Feels Just Like It Should"
  • Lonestar - "With Me"
  • Martina McBride - "Blessed"
  • Tim McGraw - "Drugs Or Jesus", "Telluride"
  • Ashley Monroe - "I Don't Want To"
  • Rascal Flatts - "Love You Out Loud"
  • Collin Raye - "You Still Take Me There"
  • Carrie Underwood - "Jesus, Take the Wheel"
  • Rhonda Vincent & The Rage - "I've Forgotten You"
  • Mark Wills - "When You Think Of Me"
  • Chely Wright - "Never Love You Enough"
  • Wynonna - "What The World Needs"
