Bread of Life Christian Church in Northern Sydney (Traditional Chinese: 北雪梨靈糧堂) is a church organization based in northern Sydney, Australia. The church currently only having manderin services, and they are planning to have a youth bilingual service in 2007.

[edit] History

On 31st of July the church starting with prayer cell group meetings in Paster Li's home. Then the first church services start on the 1st of September 2002 with only 10 people.
