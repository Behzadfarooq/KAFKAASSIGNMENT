Brett Leon Szabo (born February 1, 1968 in Postville, Iowa) is an American former professional basketball player.

A 6'11" center, Szabo played for the NCAA Division II's Augustana College Vikings and played one season for the National Basketball Association's Boston Celtics during the 1996-97 season playing 70 games while averaging 2.2 points and 2.4 rebounds.
