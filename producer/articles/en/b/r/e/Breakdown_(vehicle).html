Breakdown (vehicle)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Main article: Automobile repair shop

A vehicle breakdown is a mechanical failure. Generally, when a breakdown happens a tow truck helps the driver and, if necessary, tows the vehicle to the automobile repair shop.

The tow service can be included in the vehicle insurance.
