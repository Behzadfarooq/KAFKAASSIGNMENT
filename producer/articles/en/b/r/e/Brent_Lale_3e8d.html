[edit] About the Host

Rounding out Rogers Televisionâ€™s powerhouse broadcast team is Brent Lale who returns for his sixth season as the host of OHL Prime Time. A native of St. Thomas, Ontario he is an avid and long-time London Knights fan. Brent also lends his talents as the sports reporter on Rogers Television's daily news program, First Local, which airs daily at 5:00 and 5:30 PM.

Through out each game, Brent brings Knights fans engaging interviews with top OHL players and coaches. Lale has hosted a local radio sports show and been a regular columnist in the St. Thomas Times Journal.
