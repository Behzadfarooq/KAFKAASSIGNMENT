[edit] Relationship between Bremen and Bremerhaven and administration of "overseas port"

As I understand it, the following hierarchichal and treaty relations exist:

  1. the Land: "Freie und Hansestadt Bremen" comprises the cities of Bremen and Bremerhaven, which are not geographically contiguous (about 60 km apart).
  2. The sea port known as the Überseehafen ("Overseas Port") located geographically at Bremerhaven belongs to the city of Bremen, though there is a treaty between Bremen and Bremerhaven which provides that it shall be administered by Bremerhaven.
  3. There was also an "overseas port" geographically located at Bremen, but I think this was closed because, as a river port, it was no longer suitable for large container ships.

--Boson 22:01, 17 November 2006 (UTC)
