Breaker is the third album released by the speed metal band Accept. It was recorded from December 1980 to January 1981 at Delta-Studio, Wilster. All compositions by Accept.

[edit] Track listing

  1. "Starlight"
  2. "Breaker"
  3. "Run If You Can"
  4. "Can't Stand The Night"
  5. "Son Of A Bitch"
  6. "Burning"
  7. "Feelings"
  8. "Midnight Highway"
  9. "Breaking Up Again"
  10. "Down And Out"
