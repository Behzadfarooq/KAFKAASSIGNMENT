Brent Wilson is the former bassist for Panic! at the Disco. He was replaced by new bassist, Jon Walker.[1] Contrary to the band's statements, Wilson claims to have played on the band's debut album.


Currently, Wilson is pursuing legal action against his former bandmates for his percentage from the album sales.[2]
