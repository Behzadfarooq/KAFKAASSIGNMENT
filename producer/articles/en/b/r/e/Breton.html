Breton

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Breton can refer to:

  • Brittany, as an adjective for this historical province of France
  • The Breton language, a Celtic language spoken by some of the inhabitants of Brittany and Loire-Atlantique
  • A Breton person, part of a Brythonic ethnic group inhabiting the region of Brittany
  • André Breton (1896-1966), French author and surrealist theorist
  • Jules Adolphe Aime Louis Breton (1827-1906), French realist painter
  • Thierry Breton (born 1955), the French Minister of Economy, Finance, and Industry
  • Nicholas Breton, 16th century poet
  • Bretons, a race in The Elder Scrolls game series who are descendants of men and Elves
  • Breton is an alternative name for wine made with the grape Cabernet Franc in the Loire Valley.
