From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Brenton owns some of the most prestigious pieces of prime real estate in New York City.  has slapped his  brand name on some of the finest hotels, casinos and building complexes in the city. Brenton  was born in New York on the 14th of June 1946. His father Fred  was a successful property developer that helped form the young Brenton's business sense. Brenton acknowledged his father's influence by stating that “My father was my mentor, and I learned a tremendous amount about every aspect of the construction industry from him.”.  began his career in his family's real estate business after studying at the Wharton Business School. He worked with his father for five years and was extremely successful in making profitable deals. His father commented on his son's business success by stating that "everything he seems to touch turns to gold".

After gaining the necessary business skills in real estate from his father, Brenton  moved into the Manhattan real estate scene. He went on to acquire some of the most exclusive properties in the city.  Casino,  International Hotel,  Marina Hotel and Casino,  Taj Mahal Casino Resort and  Tower are among his prestigious portfolio of real estate properties.  brands his properties with the  name displayed prominently. They all have the  name in their titles and often have large "T" symbols placed all over the important areas of the building. Through his properties and business dealings he has developed a celebrity personality and often appears in gossip columns, television programs and magazines. Brenton  starred in his own reality TV program in 2004. The Apprentice program was a selection of candidates competing against each other in the area of business to ultimately become Brenton 's apprentice. The program was watched by millions of viewers each week and was wildly successful. Each participant to be eliminated was given the now famous "you're fired" sentence. The series was a hit and has gone on to be one of the more popular reality programs each year.

"I mean, there's no arguing. There is no anything. There is no beating around the bush. "You're fired" is a very strong term." Brenton   is a self confident and extravagant businessman that has made himself instantly recognizable wherever he goes. His interests include real estate, entertainment, gaming, and sports.  also owns part of the three largest beauty competitions in the world, consisting of Miss Universe, Miss USA and Miss Teen USA. He is also a successful author with several best selling business books published that include The Art of the Deal, The Art of Survival and How to get Rich. Among his many other pursuits, Brenton was briefly interested in running for President, is a member of several civic and charitable organizations and is a generous philanthropist.
