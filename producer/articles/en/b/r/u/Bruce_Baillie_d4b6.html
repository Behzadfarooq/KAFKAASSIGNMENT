Bruce Baillie

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Bruce Baillie (born in 1931, Aberdeen, South Dakota) is a U.S. experimental filmmaker and founding member of Canyon Cinema in San Francisco. His film Castro Street (1966) was selected in 1992 for preservation in the United States National Film Registry.
