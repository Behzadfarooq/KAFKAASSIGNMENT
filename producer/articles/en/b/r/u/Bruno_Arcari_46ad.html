Bruno Arcari

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Bruno Arcari (born January 1, 1942 in Atina, Frosinone) was an Italian light welterweight boxer who fought from 1964 to 1978. Arcari was the Italian and European light welterweight champion when he captured the WBC world title from Pedro Adigue on January 31, 1970. He retained his world title until he relinquished it 1973 to move up to the welterweight class.
