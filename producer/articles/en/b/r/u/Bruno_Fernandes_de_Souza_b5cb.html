Bruno Fernandes das Dores de Souza (born 1979), commonly known as Bruno, is a Brazilian footballer who plays goalkeeper for Flamengo.

In 2006, he formally joined Flamengo after being released by Corinthians. He replaced incumbent Diego as first choice goalkeeper for a series of summer friendlies and turned in a series of impressive performances, cementing his starting position between the posts for the season.

[edit] Honours

  • Taça Guanabara - 2007
  • Brazil Cup - 2006
