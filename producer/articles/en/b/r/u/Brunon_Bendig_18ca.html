Brunon Bendig

Bronze Rome 1960 Bantamweight

Brunon Bendig (October 6, 1938 in Chełmno – September 15, 2006 in Gdańsk) was a bantamweight amateur boxer from Poland, who tied for bronze with Australian Oliver Taylor at the 1960 Summer Olympics.
