Bruna Lombardi

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Bruna Patrizia Maria Teresa Romilda Lombardi (b. August 1, 1952, São Paulo) is a Brazilian poet, writer, model and film and TV actress of Italian descent. She is daughter of Italian film producer Ugo Lombardi.

She is married to actor Carlos Alberto Riccelli and has a son, Kim Riccelli. They currently reside in Los Angeles, California, USA. She is considered one of the most beautiful women in Brazil.
