Brunswick High School

Brunswick High School is a public high school in Brunswick, Ohio. It is the only high school in the Brunswick City Schools district.

The school colors are royal blue and white. The mascot is the Blue Devil. The school is a member of the Pioneer Conference.

[edit] Bomb Threats

Over the past 2 years Brunswick High School has been plagued by bomb threats. The first threat was made on May 17, 2006. Students were evacuated to the football stadium and eventually sent home. The second one was made in the fall of 2006. The threat. made by a Freshman male, was left on a school answering machine. The message was received before buses arrived at the school, so buses were sent back and faculty sent home. The school was searched and no explosives were found. The Third bomb threat was called February 7,2007. Buses were called and student were sent home 40 minutes after school began. The fourth was made February 8, 2007. School was never opened. School was also called off February 9th.

The off-school period from February 3-11, 2007 because of snow days, bomb threat and two weekends, has been named by students "Bomb Threat Break".
