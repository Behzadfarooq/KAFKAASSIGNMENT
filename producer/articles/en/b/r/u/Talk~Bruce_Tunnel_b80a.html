[edit] Second longest navigable?

Some information in this article or section is not attributed to sources and may not be reliable.
Please check for inaccuracies, and modify and cite sources as needed.

It says, on the Main Page no less, that this is the second longest navigable canal tunnel in the UK. However, if I read the respective WP articles right then Dudley Tunnel and Standedge Tunnel are both longer and both navigable. Moreover, Standedge Tunnel was not navigable 20 years ago when the book cited on this page as a reference was published.
