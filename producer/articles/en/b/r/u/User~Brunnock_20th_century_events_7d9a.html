[edit] Events

[edit] 1900s

  • 1901: Federation of Australia
  • 1901: William McKinley is assassinated and Theodore Roosevelt becomes President of the United States.
  • 1901: First Nobel Prizes are awarded.
  • 1902: Second Boer War ends when the British Empire defeats the Transvaal Boers.
  • 1903: Panama declares independence from Colombia with the support of the United States which gains control of the Panama Canal Zone.
  • 1904: The Entente Cordiale ends centuries of conflict between France and England.
  • 1904-07: Herero Wars between Hereros and German colonists in Namibia
  • 1905: Japan defeats Russia in the Russo-Japanese War.
  • 1905: Norway declares independence from Sweden.
  • 1908: Petroleum is discovered in the Middle East at Masjed Soleyman.
  • 1908: Bulgaria declares independence from the Ottoman Empire
  • 1908: Young Turks lead a successful rebellion against the Sultan of the Ottoman Empire and rule it until 1918.
  • 1909: NAACP founded.

[edit] 1910s

  • 1910: Union of South Africa
  • 1910: Japan annexes Korea.
  • 1910: Portugese Republican Revolution
  • 1910-28: Mexican Revolution
  • 1910: Henry Ford begins mass producing Model T automobiles.
  • 1911-12: Xinhai Revolution results in the end of the Qing Dynasty and the formation of the Republic of China.
  • 1912: International Opium Convention- first international agreement to curb the drug trade
  • 1912: France establishes a protectorate over Morocco.
  • 1912: The RMS Titanic sinks.
  • 1912-13: Balkan Wars
  • 1912: African National Congress formed in South Africa.
  • 1914-19: World War I
  • 1914: Panama Canal opens.
  • 1917: Russian Revolution
  • 1917: Finland declares independence from Russia.
  • 1918: Lithuania, Estonia, Belarus, Hungary, Latvia and Poland declare independence.
  • 1918-19: Spanish flu pandemic
  • 1918-19: German Revolution leads to the Weimar Republic.

[edit] 1920s

  • 1929-39: Great Depression

[edit] 1930s

  • 1939-45: World War II

[edit] 1940s

  • 1945: United Nations established.
  • 1947-91: Cold War
  • 1947-79: Indochina Wars
  • 1947-99: Indo-Pakistani Wars
  • 1948: The 1948 Arab-Israeli War touched off the ongoing Arab-Israeli conflict.

[edit] 1950s

  • 1950-53: Korean War
  • 1955-68: African-American Civil Rights Movement
  • 1957-75: Space Race
  • 1958-62: Great Leap Forward

[edit] 1960s

  • 1963: John F. Kennedy is assassinated
  • 1968: Riots followed the assassination of Martin Luther King, Jr. in the US. (see 1968 Washington, D.C. riots)
  • 1969-76: Cultural Revolution in China

[edit] 1970s

  • 1972-75: Watergate scandal in the US
  • 1973 oil crisis
  • 1975-79: The Khmer Rouge kill over 25% of the population of Cambodia (2 million people).
  • 1979: Iranian Revolution

[edit] 1980s

  • 1980-88: Iran-Iraq War
  • 1989: Berlin Wall is dismantled.

[edit] 1990s

  • 1994: Rwandan Genocide
  • 1996-99: Kosovo War
  • 1998-2003: Second Congo War
