Bruno Pittermann

Dr. Bruno Pittermann (born September 3, 1905 in Vienna; died September 19, 1983 in Vienna) was an Austrian politician and chairman of the Social Democratic Party of Austria from 1957 to 1967 and Vice Chancellor of Austria from 1957 to 1966. From 1964 to 1976, he was president of the Socialist International.
