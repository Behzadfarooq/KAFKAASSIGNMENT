Bruce Bruce (born as Bruce Church on 1965 in Atlanta, Georgia) is an African-American actor/stand-up comedian from Atlanta, Georgia. He has performed stand-up on various shows on Comedy Central and even performed on Comedy Central Presents in 2003. Also played an important role in promoting def comedy jam, a black stand up comedy in the all stars volume 11. He also had roles in the films XXX: State of the Union, The Wash, Larry the Cable Guy: Health Inspector and most recently Outkast's new big screen debut Idlewild. He had a short run on a memorable series of "Popeye's Chicken" commercials.


[edit] Singing Career

In 1997 Bruce Bruce had a short lived singing career in which he sang memorable hits such as "Baby I Love Your Way" and "Dirty Pop". His singing career abrubptly ended when he was shot in the neck causing him to lose his beautiful voice.
