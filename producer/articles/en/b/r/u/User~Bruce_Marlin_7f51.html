From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Bruce Marlin, a noted amateur entomologist, educator, and professional photographer specializing in macro photography of the natural world; arthropods in general and insects and spiders in particular. My work can be seen at North American Insects and Spiders, Calphotos (the Berkeley Digital Library Project), and Bugguide.net, among other places.
