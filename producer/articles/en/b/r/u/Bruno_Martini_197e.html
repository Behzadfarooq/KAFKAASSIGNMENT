Bruno Martini (born in Nevers, January 25, 1962) was a former football (soccer) player in goalkeeper role.

In career (1979–1999) he played for clubs AJ Auxerre, AS Nancy and Montpellier Herault Sport Club. He got 31 caps with France national football team and participated at two editions of UEFA European Football Championship in 1992 and 1996.
