[edit] Gulf Oil

Bruce, be assured that the song referred to "Bantry Bay" and not "Bounty Bay". It is possible that the word Bantry was incorrectly transcribed at some point as Bounty and then published. The reference is to Gulf's oil trans-shipment terminal at Whiddy Island, Bantry Bay. See Betelgeuse incident. I like your addition to the Gulf article, but adding the full words of the song to the Betelgeuse article might seem a little frivolous. best wishes. Bob BScar23625 08:12, 23 November 2006 (UTC)
