Bruce Glover (born May 2, 1932 in Chicago, Illinois) is an American actor most famous for his role of assassin Mr. Wint in the 1971 James Bond film Diamonds Are Forever. He is also the father of actor Crispin Glover.

  • Night of the Scarecrow (1995)
