Brutally Live

Brutally Live is a live Alice Cooper DVD that captures the artists performance at the Hammersmith Apollo, London, England on June 8, 2000 on the first leg of the Brutal Planet tour.

The 2003 re-issue came with a bonus live CD of the same show.
