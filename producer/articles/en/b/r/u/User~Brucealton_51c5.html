MAKING FULLER USE OF MONEY A better understanding of theoritical axioms and functional realities of moey usgae would give money more freedom to work iots magic.

1. Trade expands to meet the velocity and volume of money in circulation.

   Access to money enables trade. Trade means every transaction or activity that circulates money, consumerism and all fiscal dealings.

2. Levels of material wealth are set by and can only originate from levels of trading. 3. Trading is created and sustained by the continuous wating of goods and service.

  Without wasting there cannot be an economy. The indoctrination that wasting must be somehow harming our well being is completely false. Ridding ourselves of this one false indoctrination would go a long way in strengthening the foundation and management of economies.

4. Living standarsd stem from an adequate velocity and volume of money in circulation. 5. The application of all knowledge enables money circulation and produces goods and services to trade. 6. The level of trade sets the level of the economy. Example: Since hermits do not trade, hermits have no economy or transferable wealth. 7. Labor cannot be stored for future use. Labor only has money circulation and wealth generation abilities while laboring. 8. The circulation of tax money makes it possible for individuals and businesses to earn and acquire a large portion of their wealth. Circulating tax money creates national wealth, like roads and social improvements. Restricting and inhibiting the volume of optimum taxation curtails economies. 9. Nations that discard inhibiting money use indoctrinations enable themselves to make far greater use of money. Although they cannot tell you why, most citizens, politicians and economists unwittingly curtail the freeedom of money to fulfill its fuller potential. 10. Individuals, corporations including financial institutions can "only" be custodians as opposed to being owners of money and property wherein their name is attached. As custodians of a nations money, they are oblidged to support their economies through adequate money circulation, including passing sufficient monies through taxation. 11. Velocity and volume of money circulation are the "keys" of economics. The velocity rate (turn over time) and volume of money being circulated allows citizens, corporations and nations to create,sustain and expand economies. 12. By decreasing the turn over time of taxation monies, governments would be able to provide more services with the same amount of taxation monies.

By giving money freedom to work its woders, greater materialistic and more just civilizations can be attained.
