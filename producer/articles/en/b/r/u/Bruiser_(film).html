Bruiser is a 2000 horror film, released directly to DVD. It stars Jason Flemyng as Henry Creedlow, a man who wakes up one morning to discover he is missing his face only to find that it has been replaced by a white featureless mask. Written and directed by George A. Romero, Bruiser is the first film he had directed since the 1993 film The Dark Half. The film also features legendary horror punk band The Misfits.
