The Brunner Baronetcy, of Druids Cross in the County of Lancashire, was created in the Baronetage of the United Kingdom on 27th July 1895 for the industrialist and Liberal Party Member of Parliament (MP) John Tomlinson Brunner.

[edit] Brunner Baronets of Druids Cross, Lancashire (27 July 1895)

  • Sir John Tomlinson Brunner, 1st Baronet (1842–1919)
  • Sir John Fowler Leece Brunner, 2nd Baronet (1865–1929)
  • Sir Felix John Morgan Brunner, 3rd Baronet (1897–1982)
  • Sir John Henry Kilian Brunner, 4th Baronet (born 1927)
