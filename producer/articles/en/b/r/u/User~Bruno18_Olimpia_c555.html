[edit] List of Club Olimpia footballers

Name Nationality Position Years at Olimpia Notes/Achievements
Sila Godoy ? 1902-? One of the founders of the club
Junio Quinto Godoy ? 1902-? -
Pedro Caballero ? 1902-? -
César Mena Porta ? 1910s-? Captain of the first Olimpia team to win the Paraguayan league championship in 1912.
Axel Sirvent ? 1920s-? The club has an annual award with his name to the best athletes
Oscar López de Filippis ? 1920s-? -
Antonio Brunetti GK 1920s-? Paraguayan league champion in 1927, 1928, 1929.
Luis Vargas Peña FW 1920s-? Paraguayan league champion in 1927, 1928, 1929, 1936, 1937, 1938.
Scored the first ever goal for Paraguay in a World Cup (1930).
Rogelio Etcheverry ? 1920s-? Paraguayan league champion in 1927, 1928, 1929, 1936, 1937, 1938.
Pilot for the Paraguayan army during the Chaco War.
Aurelio González MF/FW 1920s-? Paraguayan league champion in 1927, 1928, 1929.
Soldier for the Paraguayan army during the Chaco War.
Would become a successful coach for Olimpia and Paraguay.
Flaminio Silva FW 1930s-? Paraguayan league champion in 1936, 1937, 1938.
Holds the Paraguayan all-time record for most goals in a single season (34 goals, in 1936)
