Bruce Smith (Pennsylvania politician)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Bruce Smith is a former Pennsylvania State Representative in the 92nd District which covers parts of York and Cumberland counties. [1] Smith was elected in 1980, and retired in 2006.

Smith is a native of Harrisburg, Pennsylvania. Smith is a graduate of John Harris High School. Smith graduated from Elizabethtown College in 1956, and is the College's first alumni elected to the Pennsylvania State House. [2]
