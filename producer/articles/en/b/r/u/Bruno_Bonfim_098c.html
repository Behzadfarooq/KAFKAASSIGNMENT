Bruno Bonfim

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Bruno Bonfim (born May 18, 1979 in Goiânia, Goiás) is a middle-distance freestyle swimmer from Brazil, who competed for his native country at the 2004 Summer Olympics in Athens, Greece. A year earlier, he won the bronze medal at the Pan American Games in Santo Domingo, Dominican Republic.
