Bruna Erhardt (born 1 January 1988) is a Brazilian fashion model. She was born in Tubarão, Santa Catarina, Brazil, and her ancestry is German-Brazilian. She has appeared on the cover of the Brazilian Vogue. Fellow Brazilian models Caroline Trentini and Cintia Dicker are her good friends and roommates in New York City. Bruna or "Brux" as her friends call her, has signed with major agencies such as Marilyn São Paulo and New York, Select Model ManagementLondon, Women Milan, and Colors Modeling Agency Barcelona.
