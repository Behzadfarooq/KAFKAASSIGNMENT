Bruno Milligan is a fictional character on ITV British dramas Footballers' Wives and Footballers' Wives: Extra Time, played by English actor Ben Richards.

Spoiler warning: Plot and/or ending details follow.

[edit] Season 4

Bruno was first introduced on the trip to spain. He has a wife, and daughter. He had sex with katie Jones, who later accused him of raping her.

When Lucy runs off with Giles, Bruno takes a gun with Conrad to his house. However Giles no longer lives their. Bruno Goes through the back and then accidently shoots Conrad, mistaking him for Giles.

[edit] Extra Time

Bruno quickly leaves the house where he has just mistakenly shot Conrad, as the owner of the house calls for an ambulance. Bruno has just had time to wipe down the gun and return it to the display cabinet when he is interrupted by the door bell. A distraught Lucy, in full wedding regalia returns home with Angelica and pleads with Bruno to forgive her. By this time, Conrad's shooting is all over the evening news, now livid; Bruno tells Lucy that she must provide him with an alibi. Finally putting two and two together she agrees. The police however are looking no further than Giles. They're convinced he's the one responsible for the shooting. Bruno's two unknown twins then shew up at his house and demanded to stay. Bruno got along with Yasmin but disliked Reece. After Angelica takes drugs she steels from Reece, Bruno sends him onto the streets. Reece then calls his mum Joly Salter and tells her what Bruno has done. Joly arrives at the training ground and tells Bruno to buy the twins their own flat. As she uses their marrige certificate as bribary. This revealing that he and Lucy were never truly married. Soon after Bruno gives Joly a dimond ring. Seeing this Lucy leaves Bruno and takes her and Angelica to spain. Bruno then leaves to get Lucy and Angelica back.

[edit] Season 5

Bruno returned from spain with Bruno and Angelica. When they arrive in their house he askes to renew their vows. He askes Joly for a devorce, so that he and Lucy will be legally married, however Lucy find out about this and leaves him at the alter. Amber Gates then suduces him, as she thinks that he killed Conrad. She digs up the gun Bruno used and makes Bruno think he is seeing things. When Amber propears a meal for Bruno, Bruno gets suspicious. He tells her to have some first but she refuses. She tells him she knows that he killed Conrad. Later at a party, Amber akes a gun and tries to kill Bruno. But she is stopped by Lucy. Bruno reveals to Lucy that he still has feelings for her. After a drunken night Lucy becomes pregnant, and they begin dating. However at the end of the series it is reveled that it could be Bruno's or Tremaine Gidigbi's baby.
