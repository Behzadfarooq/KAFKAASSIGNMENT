Bruce Dorminey

Bruce Dorminey is an award-winning science journalist who writes about astronomy and astrophysics. He is a former Hong Kong bureau chief for Aviation Week & Space Technology magazine and a former Paris-based technology correspondent for the Financial Times newspaper. A frequent contributor to Astronomy magazine, he is the author of the book Distant Wanderers: The Search for Planets Beyond the Solar System.
