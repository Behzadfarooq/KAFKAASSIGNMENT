Bruce Clark (March 31, 1957) was an American football defensive lineman who played eight seasons in the National Football League for the New Orleans Saints and Kansas City Chiefs. He was drafted in 1980 by the Green Bay Packers, but chose to play in the Canadian Football League for the Toronto Argonauts. He later played one year for the World League of American Football Barcelona Dragons.

Clark played college football at Penn State, where he was and All-American and won the Lombardi Award as the best college defensive lineman in 1978.
