Bruce Reitherman

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Bruce Reitherman (born September 15, 1955 in Burbank, California), is an American child actor and the son of film director Wolfgang Reitherman.

[edit] Life and work

He provided the voices of Mowgli in The Jungle Book, and Christopher Robin in The Many Adventures of Winnie the Pooh.

[edit] Filmography

[edit] Voice

  • The Many Adventures of Winnie the Pooh (1977) (voice)
  • The Jungle Book (1967) (voice)
  • Winnie the Pooh and the Honey Tree (1966) (voice)

[edit] Director, Writer, Producer

  • Nature (1982) TV Series (episode "The Living Edens: Big Sur - California's Wild Coast")
