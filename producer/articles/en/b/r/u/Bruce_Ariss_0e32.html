Bruce Wallace Ariss Jr. (August 10, 1911 â€“ September, 1994) was an American artist. He has been quite influential in Monterey, with streets and theatres and such named after him. In addition, he has numerous murals there, at least some of which were WPA projects in the 1930s. He also has some illustration work in published books.
