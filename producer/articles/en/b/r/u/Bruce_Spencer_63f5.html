Bruce Spencer is an American record producer, songwriter, and drummer, best known for his work with the rock and roll trio, The 77s.

Aside from his work with the 77s, Spencer has also played the drums for artists like Charlie Peacock, Brent Bourgeois, Larry Tagg, and others. Spencer also spent some time as the drummer for the rock group called Vector in the early 1980s, along with guitarist Jimmy Abegg and Steve Griffith.
