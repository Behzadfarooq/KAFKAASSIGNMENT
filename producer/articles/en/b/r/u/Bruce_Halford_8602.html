Bruce Halford (May 18, 1931 - December 2, 2001) was a British racing driver from England. He was born in Hampton-in-Arden, at that time within Warwickshire, and died in Churston Ferrers, Devon. He participated in 9 World Championship Formula One grands prix, debuting on July 14, 1956. He scored no championship points.
