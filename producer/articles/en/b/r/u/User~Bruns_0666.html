[edit] About Me

First off, I am a transgendered individual. I don't like using the term transsexual due to the fact I believe that sexuality and gender are two totally separate things. I sometimes also use gender queer as a way of describing myself. Please try to respect my choices of names and pronouns when you talk or refer to me.

I'm an experienced Linux systems administrator with a background in building and operating Internet Service Providers. I also have an extensive background in customer support positions.

[edit] Activities

I am currently involved with the SOSDG and AHBL, and work on various activism type projects including abolishing the death penalty and LGBT rights.
