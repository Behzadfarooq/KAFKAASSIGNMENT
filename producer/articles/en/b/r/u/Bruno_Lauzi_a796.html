Bruno Lauzi (August 8, 1937 – October 23, 2006) was an Italian singer-songwriter.

Lauzi was born in Asmara, then part of the Italian Eastern Africa. He wrote songs for Mia Martini, Georges Moustaki, Ornella Vanoni.

He died at Peschiera Borromeo from Parkinson's disease, aged 69.
