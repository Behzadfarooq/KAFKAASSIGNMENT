I grew up in a small town named Brive, in France. After high-school, I moved to a suburbs of Paris for my undergrad at ESIEE. I now live in Pasadena, California and go to school as a PhD student. I am doing some signal processing of fMRI recordings of human brains.

Most of my edits on Wikipedia are on the English website. Some are translations or adaptations about topics related to France but I also do other edits. Since English is not my first language, please do check my syntax and spelling.

My pet articles:

  • The Vichy 80, Brive-la-Gaillarde, ESIEE, Paris Chamber of Commerce, CNED
  • False discovery rate, Canonical correlation, Rayleigh quotient, Tikhonov regularization
  • California High School Exit Exam, Baker percentage, Car battery, James Wilson

I stopped editing Wikipedia for lack of time and inherent problems with this entreprise. I fix vandalism and spam once in a while, but that's about it.
