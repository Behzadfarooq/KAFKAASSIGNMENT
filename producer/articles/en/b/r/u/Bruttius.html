Bruttius

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Bruttius was a Roman nomen.

  • Bruttius Sura, general in the time of Sulla
  • Quintus Bruttius Balbus, a candidate for duovir of Pompeii, mentioned in political graffiti (from Walls of Pompeii)
  • Lucius Bruttius Maximus, proconsul of Cyprus in 80. His family came from Volceii, Lucania, Italy. From him, descended:
  • C. Bruttius Praesens L. Fulvius Rusticus, consul 139
  • His son, Lucius Fulvius Gaius Bruttius Praesens Laberius Maximus, consul 153, 180
  • His granddaughter, Bruttia Crispina
  • His grandson, Lucius Bruttius Quintius Crispinus, consul 187

Later generations:

  • Gaius Bruttius Praesens, consul 217
  • Gaius Bruttius Crispinus, consul 224
  • Gaius Bruttius Praesens, consul 246
