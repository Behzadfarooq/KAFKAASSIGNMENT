Bruce Maiman

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Bruce Maiman, aka, Dr. Maimes is a radio personality who has appeared on KDON in Monterey, California, on WKRQ in Cincinnati, Ohio and on KCCL in Sacramento. He was also a long time producer for Scott Shannon and Ross Brittain on the legendary New York City station Z100. Maiman is now working in Sacramento at KFBK as a talk radio host. [1]

His show at KDON, Dr. Maimes & MG, in Salinas, CA was the first-ever radio program to feature UFO Phil (1997-1998) and High-Pitched Eric (1998-1999), who would later appear on the Howard Stern Show.

Has been an NYC cab driver. [2]
