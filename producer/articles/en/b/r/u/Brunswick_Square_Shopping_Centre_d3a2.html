Brunswick Square Shopping Centre is a multi-level shopping mall located in the heart of Saint John, New Brunswick, Canada. It has more than 60 stores, and opened in 1978.

The mall is connected via the city's pedway system to the City Market, the Delta Brunswick Hotel, City Hall, the Aquatic Centre, Harbour Station, the Saint John Trade and Convention Centre and Market Square. The mall doesn't have a major anchor, but includes a number of unique locally owned store such as Dreskin's, which has been selling ladies wear in Saint John for more than 80 years. The mall also holds a health club and the local studio of Global Television.
