Your talk page is intended to serve as a place to discuss your contributions. It ends up being a sort of a history of the work you've done and the conflicts you've been in, or the praise you've recieved. It's alright to archive comments or warnings, but blanking it is not considered acceptable, as it is not yours. Please read WP:USERPAGE and WP:TALK. I've read your argument about how the content is "libel", but as a law student you should know that someone publishing libelious material does not justify you going in and destroying their presses or all copies of their work. The proper thing to do is go through the proper channels. As for the threat to file a complaint, believe me, you're not the first. We even have a policy concerning legal threats, WP:LEGAL, which you might like to read. Please continue to contribute constructively to the encyclopedia, and please don't continue to blank other users' comments. --Daniel Olsen 04:59, 22 November 2006 (UTC)
