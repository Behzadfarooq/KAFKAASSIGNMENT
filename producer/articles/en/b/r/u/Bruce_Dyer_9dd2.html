Bruce Antonio Dyer (born 13 April 1975 in Ilford, London) is an English professional football player, who plays as a striker. He is currently playing at Doncaster Rovers.

Dyer started his career at Watford in 1994, before being sold on to Crystal Palace. This transfer made Dyer the first ever teenager in English football to be transferred for at least Â£1 million. He played in the Premiership and the Football League Division One with Palace for several seasons before moving to Barnsley.

When Barnsley were relegated to the Football League Division Two in 2003, Watford snapped up Dyer on a free transfer. He later played 12 games for Stoke City, where he enjoyed a 10-game loan spell at Millwall before transferring to Sheffield United in January 2006.

Having made over 300 appearances in the Football League and scored over 100 goals in his career so far, Dyer joined Doncaster Rovers on 2 June 2006 on a free transfer. He had a loan spell with Bradford City in February 2007.
