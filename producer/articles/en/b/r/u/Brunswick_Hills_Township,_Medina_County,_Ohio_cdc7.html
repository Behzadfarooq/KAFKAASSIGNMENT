Brunswick Hills Township, Medina County, Ohio

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Municipalities and townships of Medina County.

Brunswick Hills Township is one of 17 townships in Medina County, Ohio, United States.

It borders the following Medina County townships:

  • Liverpool (west)
  • Hinckley (east)
  • Granger (southeast tip)
  • Medina (south)
  • York (southwest tip)
