Brusnica

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Location of Brusnica within Slovakia

Brusnica is a village and municipality in Stropkov District in the Prešov Region of north-eastern Slovakia.

[edit] History

In historical records the village was first mentioned in 1408.

[edit] Geography

Brusnica

The municipality lies at an altitude of 199 metres and covers an area of 14.283 km². It has a population of about 415 people. The village has a significant Rusyn minority (32%) and smaller Roma minority (2%), along with the majority of Slovaks (65%).
