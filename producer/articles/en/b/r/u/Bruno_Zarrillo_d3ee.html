Bruno Zarrillo (born September 5, 1966, in Winnipeg, Manitoba, Canada) is a Italian-Canadian retired professional ice hockey winger.

[edit] Achievements

  • 1990 - Serie A Champion with HC Bolzano
  • 1995 - Serie A Champion with HC Bolzano
  • 1997 - NLA Champion with SC Bern
  • 2004 - Serie A Champion with HC Milano Vipers

[edit] International play

Bruno Zarrillo played a total of 170 games for the Italian national ice hockey team.
