[edit] Any reason why only military pictures are used?

US military pictures are public domain, and hence can be used on Wikipedia. I suspect it is difficult to find other free photographs. — Matt Crypto 14:52, 6 August 2006 (UTC)

Jedibob5, if you look at many of the biography pages, it lists the birthday and the (age) is the current age. If the bio is of a deceased person, then it does not have the age.
