Bruce Gerald Woloshyn was born March 22, 1964 in North Battleford, Saskatchewan to Gerald & Phyllis Woloshyn.

Mr. Woloshyn started his career in 1984 working as an editor and technical director for CBC and CTV television affiliates in western Canada.

Mr. Woloshyn is currently a visual effects artist/supervisor at Rainmaker Animation & Visual Effects in Vancouver.

[edit] Television credits

His visual effects television credits include

  • Stargate SG-1,
  • Stargate Atlantis,
  • Andromeda,
  • Highlander: The Series,
  • Dark Angel,
  • Millennium,
  • The pilot to Smallville.

[edit] Film credits

His feature film credits include

  • Night At The Museum,
  • Antitrust,
  • 'Mission to Mars
  • 3000 Miles to Graceland.
