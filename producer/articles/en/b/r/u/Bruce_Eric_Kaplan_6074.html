Bruce Eric Kaplan

Bruce Eric Kaplan, known as BEK, is an American cartoonist whose single panel cartoons frequently appear in The New Yorker. Kaplan is also a screenwriter and has worked on Seinfeld and on Six Feet Under.

Kaplan joined the writing staff of Six Feet Under during the first season in 2001 and served as Executive Producer during the fifth and final season. Kaplan wrote 7 episodes for the series.

[edit] Six Feet Under episodes

  • The Foot (2001)
  • The New Person (2001)
  • The Invisible Woman (2002)
  • The Secret (2002)
  • The Trap (2003)
  • The Dare (2004)
  • The Silence (2005)

[edit] Seinfeld episodes

  • "The Merv Griffin Show", (1997)
  • "The Cartoon" (1998)
  • "The Puerto Rican Day", (1998)
