Bruno Labbadia (born February 8, 1966 in Darmstadt) is a former German footballer of Italian heritage.

In an illustrious career, Labbadia recorded 103 goals in 328 league games having played for some of Germany's top Bundesliga clubs. He had two caps for the Germany national football team. He is currently the manager of Regionalliga-Süd side SV Darmstadt 98.
