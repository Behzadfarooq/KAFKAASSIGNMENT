Bruce Seth Green A. C. E is a director who has worked on several major television productions in the last three decades, including Buffy the Vampire Slayer, Dawson's Creek, and Gilmore Girls.

Despite their very similar names and both working on Buffy the Vampire Slayer, he is no relation with actor Seth Green.
