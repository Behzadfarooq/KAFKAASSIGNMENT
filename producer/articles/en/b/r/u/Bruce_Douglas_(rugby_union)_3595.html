Bruce Douglas (rugby union)

Bruce Andrew Ferguson Douglas (born 10 February 1980) is a Scottish rugby union footballer. He plays prop for Border Reivers.

He has represented Scottish Schools, Scottish Districts and Scotland at Under 16, Under 18, Under 19 and Under 21 levels.

He completed his contract with NEC Harlequins and then joined The Borders in their debut season. Douglas won his first cap in the 37-10 win against Romania in November 2002. He played in all three Scotland midweek victories in the 2002 summer tour of North America.

In 2003 Douglas scored his first try for Scotland in the 30-22 victory over Wales. He toured South Africa in 2003 and his work-rate earned him recognition on the 2004 tour of Australia.
