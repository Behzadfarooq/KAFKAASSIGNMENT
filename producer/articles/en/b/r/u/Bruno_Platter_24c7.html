Bruno Platter

Bruno Platter, Th.D. (born 21 March 1944) is the Hochmeister (Grand master) of the Teutonic Order.

Platter was born 1944 in Unterinn am Ritten in South Tyrol, Italy. He joined the Teutonic Order in 1964 and became the Grand Master on 26 August 2000 and became an abbot on 29 October 2000.
