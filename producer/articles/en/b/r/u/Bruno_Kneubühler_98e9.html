Bruno Kneubühler (born December 3, 1946) is a Swiss former Grand Prix motorcycle road racer. During his career, he competed in every Grand Prix class from 50cc to 500cc. While he never won a championship, he finished in second place three times. His lengthy Grand Prix career saw him compete against many champions from Giacomo Agostini to Mick Doohan.
