Bruno Söderström (October 28, 1881 - January 1, 1969) was a Swedish athlete who competed mainly in the Javelin throw free style and pole vault.

He competed for a Sweden in the 1906 Summer Olympics held in Athens, Greece in the Pole Vault where he won the Silver medal. He followed this up with the Javelin throw free style where he won the Bronze medal.

Two yeas later he returned for the 1908 Summer Olympics held in London, Great Britain in the Pole Vault but this time he only managed to win the Bronze medal.
