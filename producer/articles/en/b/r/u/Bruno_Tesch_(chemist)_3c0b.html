Bruno Tesch (chemist)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

You have new messages (last change).

Bruno Emil Tesch (14 August 1890 - 16 May 1946 in Hameln) was a German chemist and entrepreneur, condemned and executed on 16 May 1946 for his involvement in the production of Zyklon B.

Not to be confused with Bruno Guido Camillo Tesch.
