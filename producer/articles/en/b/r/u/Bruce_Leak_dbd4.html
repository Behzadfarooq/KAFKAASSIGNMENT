Bruce Leak

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

You have new messages (last change).

A graduate of Stanford University, Bruce Leak was the engineering team leader of the first version of QuickTime by Apple Computer. He co-founded WebTV Networks which was acquired by Microsoft.

He also held positions at Rocket Science Games and General Magic Inc.
