[edit] Political

Referring to the German history:

  • since 1235 the Duchy of Brunswick-Lüneburg
  • 1269 – 1814 the Principality Brunswick-Wolfenbüttel
  • 1814 – 1918 the Duchy of Brunswick
  • 1918 – 1921 the Socialist Republic of Brunswick
  • 1921 – 1933 the Free state of Brunswick
  • 1978 – 2004 the Braunschweig (region)
  • since 2005 the Metropolitan area Hannover-Braunschweig-Göttingen
