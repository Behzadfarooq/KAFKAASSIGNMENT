A folklorist by trade.


I am distantly related to Susan B. Anthony, Judy Blume, and the real Hy Zaret.

Another cousin was one of the main seismic engineers in the construction of the Torre Latinoamericana.


I live in Bound Brook, New Jersey. And yes, I suffer from bruxism.


Articles started: Applied folklore, American Folklore Society, Don West, Jim Corbett, Kenneth A. Gibson, Komagata Maru, Mixe, and Public folklore.
