Bruce Rushin from Brundall in Norfolk, is an art teacher at Flegg High School in Norfolk, the United Kingdom.

Bruce had always been interested in art, ever since he was a child, being his favourite subject when he was at school. In 1997, he entered a competition by submitting his design for the new two pound coin. Little did he know he would be the winning contestant.

His current claim to fame is designing the 1997 British Two Pound coin.[citation needed]
