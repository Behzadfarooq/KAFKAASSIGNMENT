Brutality and Bloodshed for All is the final studio album by GG Allin. Released in 1993, all of the songs on this album were written while Allin was in prison.

[edit] Miscellanea

While many people think that the band Anal Cunt named themselves after the third track on this album, they actually formed in 1988, five years before this album was released. GG originally wanted Anal Cunt to be his back-up band but due to traveling issues it never happened.
