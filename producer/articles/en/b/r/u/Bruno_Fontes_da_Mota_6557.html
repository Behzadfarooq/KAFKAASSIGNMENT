Bruno Mota fullname Bruno Fontes da Mota (24 October 1987 in Geneva) is a Swiss football (soccer) midfielder. Currently plays for U.C. Sampdoria. His lastname Fontes da Mota is portuguese, because of his father's family.

He also played for FC Chiasso (2005-06) and made 13 appearances. He played youth football in Servette FC (2000-05) and Athlétique Régina (1995-2000).
