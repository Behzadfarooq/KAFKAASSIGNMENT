Bruce Daniels

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

You have new messages (last change).

Bruce Daniels is an American stand-up comedian and actor. His primary claim to fame comes from the fact that he toured from 2003 to 2005 as the opening act for his friend, comedienne Margaret Cho. They also co-star together in the film Bam Bam and Celeste.
