Bruce Horn

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

You have new messages (last change).

Bruce Horn, a programmer with Apple Computer, was the creator of the Macintosh Finder. His signature is amongst those molded to the case of the Macintosh 128K.

He now owns, and programs software for, Ingenuity Software.
