Bruno Bucciarati is a fictional character from the Japanese manga JoJo's Bizarre Adventure. Bruno is one of the hero's of part 5, defecting from Diavlo's gang and leading a group of rouge gangsters.

[edit] Stand

Sticky Fingers is the name of Bruno's stand. It has the ability to turn anything it touches to open with a zipper. For example, Sticky Fingers can hit a wall and open it up and walk through it then zip it up again. It can also mismatch things up when they are unzipped.

Bruno's trademark line is delivered while beating his opponents senseless with his fists. He begins stringing "Ari, Ari, Ari" to his rapid punches and finishes with "Arrivederci" -which is "goodbye" in Italian- during his finishing blow.
