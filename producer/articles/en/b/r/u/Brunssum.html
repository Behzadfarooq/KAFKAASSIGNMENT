Brunssum (pronunciation (help·info)) is a municipality and a town in the southeastern Netherlands. It's the home to NATO Joint Forces Command Brunssum.

  • Brunssum

[edit] Notable people born in Brunssum

  • Patrick Dybiona (born 1963), freestyle swimmer
  • Kevin Hofland (born 1979), football (soccer) defender
  • Bob Altena (born 1986), decathlete
