Brunswick Dock

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Brunswick Dock is a dock, on the River Mersey and part of the Port of Liverpool it is situated in the southern dock system, connected to Coburg Dock to the north, Toxteth Dock to the south. The dock was opened in 1832, designed by Jesse Hartley, specifically as a timber dock.

The Royal Navy Headquarter (RNHQ) Merseyside is on Brunswick Dock the current commander is Cdre JEV Madgwick (John). The Dock is home to a small squadron of Minesweepers.

This and the other docks in the southern system are owned by British Waterways, part of the dock is set aside for moorings.

In 1854 Cato & Miller built ships at Brunswick Dock.
