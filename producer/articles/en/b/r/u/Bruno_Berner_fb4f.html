Bruno Berner (born November 21, 1977) is a Swiss football player.

He has spent most of his career playing for Grasshopper Zürich, SC Freiburg, and FC_Basel[1] .

Berner moved to Blackburn Rovers on a free transfer from FC_Basel on January 30, 2007.[2] He has been given the squad number 25.

On February 10, 2007 he played his first Premiership match for Blackburn against Everton F.C. [3]

He played for Switzerland national football team and was a participant at the 2004 UEFA European Championship.[4]
