Bruno Tonioli

Bruno Tonioli is a dancer and professional choreographer. He is best known as a judge on both the British television series Strictly Come Dancing and its American adaptation, Dancing with the Stars.

As a toddler Bruno would dance in front of the radio to entertain people.

Bruno was director and choreographer of the 2000/01 Flying Music UK national tour of "What A Feeling" starring Gwen Dickey from Rose Royce and Limahl of Kajagoogoo.[citation needed]

Bruno was one of the main dancers in Elton John's video I'm Still Standing.[citation needed] He has also worked with Paul McCartney, Michael Jackson, and other big names in the music world.[citation needed]

He is fluent in English, Spanish, French and Italian.[1]
