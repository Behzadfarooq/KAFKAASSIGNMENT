[edit] Factual Dispute: Designation of Road Code M1

First of all - I'm a newbie and this is the first time I've labelled an article like this. If I've done it wrong, please excuse me and fix it!

While I believe that the Bruce Highway is still part of National Highway 1, I know that in South-East Queensland (I think all of the Bruce Highway) has been re-designated to M1, rather than National Highway 1. I think this information needs to be updated on the article, in it's various images, as well as several other articles linking to, and relating to this topic. --DanielBC 10:14, 31 May 2006 (UTC)
