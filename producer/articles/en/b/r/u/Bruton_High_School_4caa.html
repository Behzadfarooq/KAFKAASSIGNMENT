Bruton High School is a public high school located east of Williamsburg, Virginia in the Lightfoot community in York County, Virginia. It is part of the York County School Division and opened in 1976. Athletic teams compete in the Virginia High School League's AA Bay Rivers District in Region I.

[edit] Accreditation

Bruton High is fully accredited by the Virginia Department of Education[1] and has been accredited by the Southern Association of Colleges and Schools since 1976. [2]

[edit] Athletics

Bruton High fields teams in these sports:

  • Volleyball
  • Cross-Country
  • Field Hockey
  • Golf
  • Cheerleading
  • Football
  • Basketball (Boys and Girls)
  • Swimming (Boys and Girls)
  • Wrestling
  • Indoor Track
  • Baseball
  • Soccer (Boys and Girls)
  • Softball
  • Tennis (Boys and Girls)
  • Track (Boys and Girls)

[edit] Notable alumni

  • Bryan Randall, former quarterback for Bruton and for Virginia Tech. He now plays for the NFL's Pittsburgh Steelers. He played previously for the Tampa Bay Buccaneers.
