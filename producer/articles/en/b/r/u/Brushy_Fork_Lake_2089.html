Brushy Fork Lake

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Brushy Fork Lake is an 18-acre impoundment on the South Branch South Fork Potomac River located three miles south of Sugar Grove in southeastern Pendleton County, West Virginia, USA. Brushy Fork Lake lies in the Dry River District of the George Washington National Forest.
