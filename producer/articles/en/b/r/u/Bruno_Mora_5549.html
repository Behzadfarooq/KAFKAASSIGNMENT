Bruno Mora (born 29 March 1937 in Parma, Italy; died 10 December 1986) was an Italian footballer.

During his career he played for U.C. Sampdoria, Juventus F.C. and A.C. Milan. He earned 21 caps and scored 4 goals for the Italy national football team, and played in the 1962 FIFA World Cup.
