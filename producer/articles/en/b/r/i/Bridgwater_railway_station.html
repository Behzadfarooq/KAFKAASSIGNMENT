Bridgwater railway station serves Bridgwater in Somerset, England. The station is operated by First Great Western. Built to the designs of Isambard Kingdom Brunel, the station is now a Grade II listed building.

Outside the station are the loading facilities for nuclear flasks from Hinkley Point nuclear power station
