Brittany Curran (born on June 2, 1990 in Boston Massachusetts) is American actress. She has guest starred on television shows like Complete Savages, Drake & Josh, Mad TV, Power Rangers Wild Force and The Suite Life of Zack & Cody. She has also had minor roles in feature films including 13 Going on 30, Akeelah and the Bee and also co-starred in the Disney Channel Original Movie Go Figure.
