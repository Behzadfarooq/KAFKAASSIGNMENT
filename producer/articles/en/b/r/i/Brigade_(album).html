Brigade is an album released by the popular rock/pop band Heart in 1990 after a three year gap between albums. The album managed to make number three on the U.S. Billboard 200.

  2. "All I Wanna Do Is Make Love To You"
  3. "Secret"
  4. "Tall, Dark, Handsome Stranger"

  6. "The Night"
  7. "Fallen from Grace"
  8. "Under The Sky"
  9. "Cruel Nights"
  10. "Stranded"
  11. "Call Of The Wild"
  12. "I Want Your World To Turn"
  13. "I Love You"

[edit] Japan Special Edition 3" CD Bonus Tracks

  1. "Cruel Tears"
  2. "Never Stop Loving You"
  3. "The Will to Love"

The track "You're the Voice" was recorded for Brigade and released on a promo-only CD single but didn't appear on a proper Heart album until Greatest Hits 1985-1995.
