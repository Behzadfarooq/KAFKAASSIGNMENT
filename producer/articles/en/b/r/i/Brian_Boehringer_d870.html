Brian Edward Boehringer (born January 8, 1970 in St. Louis, Missouri) is an American former Major league baseball pitcher and a current minor league pitcher in the Detroit Tigers organization.

In 2006, Boehringer went 2-4, with a 2.75 ERA with the Omaha Royals. He also played with the Toledo Mud Hens, where he went 3-1, with a 4.83 ERA.
