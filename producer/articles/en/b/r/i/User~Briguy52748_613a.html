[edit] Current projects

[edit] Country music history

My biggest project right now is creating a series of articles chronicling the history of country music. Pattered after the Years in Music, this series of articles about country music details year-by-year events, songs, awards, etc., in the genre.

This is a work in progress, and will be completed as time allows. I'll let you know how I'm progressing.

[edit] List of newspapers in Iowa/Illinois

This is also a work in progress, but eventually, these two articles will eventually list all the daily, weekly and specialty newspapers published and circulated in both Iowa and Illinois.

I'd greatly appreciate help in expanding the Illinois list, but I think I have the Iowa list under control.

[edit] List of school districts in Iowa

Like the list of newspapers in Iowa, I am creating a list of all Iowa public school districts. If you have anything to add, so long as it is a public school, just add it, so long as its verifiable. If the district has a Web site (not all do, amazinginly), please add it as well.
