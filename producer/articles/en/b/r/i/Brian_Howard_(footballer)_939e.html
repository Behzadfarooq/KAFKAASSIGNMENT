Brian Howard (born January 23, 1983 in Winchester) is a professional footballer who currently plays as an Attacking Central Midfielder for Barnsley and wears the number 11 shirt.

Brian started his career as a trainee with Southampton but never made a full appearance before signing for Swindon Town in 2003 making his debut on the 9 August 2003 against Sheffield Wednesday. He is a former England Under-20 international.

Brian signed for Barnsley from Swindon Town in the summer of 2005 for 2 years. Howard has successfully stepped into the hole left by the departed Stephen McPhail and is regarded by many fans as a Barnsley hero in the making. Barnsley's supporters are often heard to sing 'There's Only One Brian Howard' at matches.

At the time of writing, Brian has made 43 appearances for the Tykes scoring 10 goals.
