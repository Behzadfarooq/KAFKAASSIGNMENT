Brian Nankervis

Brian Nankervis is an Australian comedian. He is best known for his character Raymond J. Bartholomeuz, who was popular during the 1980s and 90s as a regular guest on Hey Hey It's Saturday. Bartholomeuz also appeared weekly on Paul Hester's ABCTV series Hessie's Shed (1999), and in Bob Franklin's sitcom Introducing Gary Petty (2000).

Brian starred as Dr Ray Good in the stage and TV versions of Let The Blood Run Free (TV series: 1990-1992), and appeared in the ensemble cast of the sketch comedy series Jimeoin (1994-1995).

He frequently appears as himself on television and stage, and is the regular warmup man and audience wrangler for The Panel (1998-).

Most recently, Brian created the SBS music trivia game show Rockwiz (2005-), which he also produces and appears in as scorekeeper.
