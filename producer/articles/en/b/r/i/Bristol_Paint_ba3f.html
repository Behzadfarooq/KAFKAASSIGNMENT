Bristol Paint

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

For other things called Bristol, see Bristol (disambiguation)

Bristol Paint is an Australian decorating supplies company. It has 120 stores throughout the country, which employ 500 staff.

Bristol Paint is part of Barloworld Coatings Australia.

[edit] External links

  • Bristol Paint's website

Corporate Information: Bristol Paint is part of Barloworld Coatings Australia manufacturers of leading brands Bristol, Taubmans and White Knight Paints. Barloworld Coatings has been manufacturing paint in Australia for over 100 years. As part of this group of companies Bristol enjoys the resources to manufacture the highest quality paints and research new paint formulas for specific markets.

As well as manufacturing capabilities, Bristol has a chain of over 120 Bristol Paint and Decorator Centres nationwide. . It is the largest chain of paint and wallpaper outlets, supplying paint and other decorative products and accessories to trade and retail customers in Australia.

Bristol's growth, under the Barloworld banner, has led them to become known in many countries throughout the Asia Pacific region. Export of this quality Australian made paint now makes up a significant and important part of the corporation's future.

Products Bristol has a range of paint and accessory products for the home DIYer (Do It Yourself) as well as the trade painter. Complimentary Brands Bristol also stock a number of products from Taubmans and WhiteKnight Paints, both part of the stable of brands held by Barloworld Coatings Australia
