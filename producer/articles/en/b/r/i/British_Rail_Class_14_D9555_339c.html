British Rail Class 14, number D9555, is a preserved 0-6-0 diesel-hydraulic shunting locomotive. This locomotive is historic as it was the last ever locomotive to be built at Swindon Works in 1965.

Most of its British Rail life was spent on the Severn and Wye route hauling coal trains. Today the locomotive privately owned and is operating on the preserved Dean Forest Railway, Gloucestershire, its original route.
