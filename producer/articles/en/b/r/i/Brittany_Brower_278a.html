Brittany Brower (born May 6, 1982) is an American fashion model and actress from Tallahassee, Florida. She was a contestant on the fourth season of America's Next Top Model.

[edit] Before Top Model

Before Top Model, Brower worked with Marsha Doll Models and Promogirl.com for over eleven years. She was part of NYC Model Camp Models of the South where she won Model of the Year.

[edit] Modeling/Acting career

Brower signed with Next Model Management in L.A., Passport in San Francisco and Reel Management. Her runway shows include: San Francisco Fashion Week Presents "Emerging Stars" (with Michelle Deighton and Christina Murphy), Meghan Spring 2006, Frederick's of Hollywood Spring 2006, Ellegirl presents Dare To Be You: Wal-Mart Meets America's Next Top Models 2005, and JLo. Her modelling credits include: Von Dutch, Carlos Santana Shoes, GQ magazine, US weekly magazine, Maxim magazine and others. [1] [2] [3] [4] [5] [6]

Brower was part of Bravo's Battle of the Network Reality Stars. She has guest starred on an episode of Love, Inc. [7] [8] For her work on reality TV, Maxim listed her in their Hottest Women of Reality TV chart [9].

[edit] Trivia

  • Brower dated Survivor contestant Burton Roberts.[citation needed]
