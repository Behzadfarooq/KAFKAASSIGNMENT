Bristol City Council Elections 1986

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

You have new messages (last change).

One third of seats were up for election. The election in Brislington West was a by-election. There was a general but small swing away from the Conservatives. Labour regained their majority on the Council, which they would not lose until 2003.
