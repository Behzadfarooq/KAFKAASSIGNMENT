Brian Klemm

Brian Klemm (born May 21, 1982) is the guitarist for the Orange County-based third-wave ska band Suburban Legends.

He also lends his voice to both versions of the song "Desperate", "This Cherry", and a song so far only played live, "Your Girlfriend's Pretty." He also sang a live cover of "Can't Take My Eyes Off You" with the band at the band's September 2006 and January 2007 performances at Disneyland's Tomorrowland Terrace stage.

[edit] Trivia

  • His guitar once belonged to Aaron Barrett, the lead singer and guitarist of fellow ska band Reel Big Fish.
  • He was once an interviewee - also known as a "street savant" - on the game show Street Smarts.
