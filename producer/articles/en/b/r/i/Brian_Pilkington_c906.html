Brian Pilkington, born February 12, 1933 in Leyland, England is a former professional footballer and England international who played as a midfielder.

During his career, Pilkington played for Burnley, Bolton Wanderers, Bury, Barrow and Chorley. He won 1 England cap whilst at Burnley. His only cap was on October 2, 1954 in a 2-0 victory over Northern Ireland.
