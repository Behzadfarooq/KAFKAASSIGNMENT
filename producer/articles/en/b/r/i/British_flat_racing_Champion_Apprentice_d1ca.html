British flat racing Champion Apprentice

The Champion Apprentice of flat racing in Great Britain is the apprentice jockey who has ridden the most winning horses during a season. The list below shows the Champion Apprentice and the number of winners for each year since 1922.

  • 1922 - R. A. Jones - 58
  • 1923 - Charlie Elliott - 89
  • 1924 - Charlie Elliott - 106
  • 1925 - Charlie Smirke - 70
  • 1926 - Charlie Smirke - 71
  • 1927 - Sam Wragg - 38
  • 1928 - G. Baines / L. Cordell - 33
  • 1929 - C. Adley - 35
  • 1930 - J. Simpson - 28
  • 1931 - F. Rickaby - 44
  • 1932 - F. Rickaby - 37

  • 1935 - Eph Smith - 76
  • 1936 - W. Wing - 37

  • 1942 - K. Mullins - 7

  • 1946 - J. Sime - 40
