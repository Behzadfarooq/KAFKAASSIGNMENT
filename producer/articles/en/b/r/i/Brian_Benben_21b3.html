Brian Benben (born on June 18, 1956 in Winchester, Virginia) is an American actor.

He is perhaps best-known for his leading role in the 1990-1996 HBO sitcom Dream On. He had a starring role in the 1994 film Radioland Murders playing Mary Stuart Masterson's estranged husband. He later starred in The Brian Benben Show in 1998, but it was quickly canceled.

He married Madeleine Stowe in 1982, with whom he has two children. They live on a ranch in Texas.
