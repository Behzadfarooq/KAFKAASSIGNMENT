Brittany Andrews (born August 13, 1973 in Milwaukee, Wisconsin as Michelle Carmel Barry) is an American pornographic actress and exotic dancer. She started her career as an exotic dancer, and while living in Texas, she became a popular feature dancer after undergoing rhinoplasty and breast augmentation, a procedure she now publicly regrets [1].

She began doing photo shoots for men's magazines, but moved to adult film after meeting Jenna Jameson in 1995 during a photo shoot for Hustler magazine in Jamaica. She starred in her first film Internal Affairs in 1997, for which she was nominated for an AVN award.

In addition to adult film she hosts several TV shows, producing one and running her own production company, Britco Pictures in Los Angeles, California. She is also actively involved in several adult websites.

In December, 2003, Brittany Andrews was appointed to the Women In Adult Board of Directors as the Talent Liaison.
