Brian Oulton (February 11, 1908â€”April 13, 1992) was a British character actor.

Born in Liverpool, he made his acting debut in 1939 as a lead actor. During World War II he served in the army, and returned to acting playing character roles in 1946; he made a name for himself playing the same pompous character in numerous films, and he went on to appear in several Carry On films.

In 1969 he appeared as an eccentric psychic medium in Randall and Hopkirk (Deceased) in the episode Never Trust a Ghost. He was also a stage actor and playwright, writing and starring in productions such as Births, Marriages and Deaths (1975) and For Entertainment Only (1976). He also appeared in The Young Ones as Neil's dad.
