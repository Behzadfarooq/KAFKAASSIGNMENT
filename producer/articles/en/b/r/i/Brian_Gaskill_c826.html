Brian Gaskill

Brian Richard Gaskill (born 22 January 1970 in Honolulu, Hawaii, raised in Neptune Township, New Jersey) is an American actor.

His television debut was in the series Models Inc. in 1994. In 1995, he was cast as Bobby Warner in the soap opera All My Children, in which he appeared until 1997.

In 2001 he appeared in the role as angel/vampire hunter Rafe Kovich on the serial Port Charles. He continued with the role until the show was canceled in 2003.

After Port Charles was canceled, he then appeared as Oscar Marone on The Bold and the Beautiful from 2003 to 2004.

From September 2005 to December 2005, Gaskill briefly joined the cast of As the World Turns as B.J. Green. Gaskill and his wife, Tonya Watts, welcomed their first child, Alabama Zoe, on September 26, 2005.

In March 2007, Gaskill joined the cast of Guiding Light as Dylan Lewis, the son of Reva Shayne and Billy Lewis and the estranged father of Daisy Lemay.

[edit] Television filmography

  • Guiding Light as Dylan Lewis (#2) (March 2, 2007 to present)
  • As the World Turns as B.J. Green/Byron Glass (September 27, 2005 to December 28, 2005)
  • The Bold and the Beautiful as Oscar Marone (September 11, 2003 to summer 2004)
  • Port Charles as Rafe Kovich (August 17, 2001 to October 3, 2003)
  • All My Children as Robert 'Bobby' Thorne Warner (aka Roberto) #3 (1995 to July 1997)

He attended the performing arts program at Red Bank Regional High School.
