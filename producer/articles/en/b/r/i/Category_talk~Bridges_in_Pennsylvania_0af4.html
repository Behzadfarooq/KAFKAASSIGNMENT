[edit] PA bridge category confusion

Hi PA bridge people, I just caused a bit of extra work for folks by removing the PA bridges category from several Pittsburgh area bridge articles. A glance at Wikipedia:WikiProject_Bridges shows that the current policy is to include bridge articles in both the PA bridge cat and other cats like toll bridges or Pittsburgh bridges. So...what about the Lancaster county covered bridges? Ought those articles be in the PA bridge cat too?

I would say that category should be deleted entirely. None of the covered bridges in Lancaster County seem to be noteworthy. Cacophony 04:27, 4 April 2006 (UTC)
