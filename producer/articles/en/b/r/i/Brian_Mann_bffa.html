[edit] High school years

Brian Mann attended Xaverian Brothers High School in Westwood, Massachusetts, and was a good student and a three-sport captain in football, basketball, and baseball. In football, he led his team to two consecutive state titles and set school records.

[edit] College career

Mann attended Dartmouth College, Brian Mann was a four-year letterman in football. As a senior, he passed for a school single-season record 2,913 yards, was the team MVP, and was an All-Ivy League selection.

[edit] Movie Career

Through his career as a profesional quarterback, Brian has landed roles as a body double for quarterback roles such as Adam Sandler's double in the remake of The Longest Yard and the role as the quarterback in "Invincible".
