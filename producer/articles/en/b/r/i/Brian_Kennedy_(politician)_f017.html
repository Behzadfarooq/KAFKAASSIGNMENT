Brian Kennedy (b. 1964) is the former state Republican Party chairman in the U.S. State of Iowa.

In 2006, he lost the primary for Iowa's first Congressional District Seat in 2006, the seat being vacated by eight term Republican Congressman Jim Nussle who is running for governor. He was defeated by businessman Mike Whalen.

He lives in Bettendorf, Iowa, with his wife and son.
