Brian Cushing is a strongside linebacker and LEO for the University of Southern California Trojans football team. He attended Bergen Catholic High School in Oradell, New Jersey. He shares the #10 with starting quarterback John David Booty. Cushing currently resides in Park Ridge, New Jersey.

On January 1, 2007, Cushing was named the Defensive MVP of the Rose Bowl Game, after recording 2½ sacks and forcing a third-quarter fumble that changed the momentum of the game.[1]
