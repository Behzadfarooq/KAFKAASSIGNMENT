Bristol City Council Elections 1991

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

You have new messages (last change).

One third of seats were up for election. Two seats were contested in Windmill Hill due to an extra vacancy occurring. There was a general swing against the Conservatives. In Lockleaze, the Labour Party failed to field a candidate because of a nomination papers error.

The party standings following the election:
