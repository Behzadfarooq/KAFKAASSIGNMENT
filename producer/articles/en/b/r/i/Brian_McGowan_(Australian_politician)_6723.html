Brian McGowan (b. 1 January 1938, Barham, New South Wales) was an Australian politician, elected as a member of the New South Wales Legislative Assembly.

[edit] Football career

McGowan played Australian rules football with the South Melbourne Football Club, playing 118 games during the 1960s. He was just 165cm tall and played as a rover.
