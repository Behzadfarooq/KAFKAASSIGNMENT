Brian A. Skiff

Brian A. Skiff is an American astronomer.

He has discovered a number of comets, including the periodic comets 114P/Wiseman-Skiff (with Jennifer Wiseman and 140P/Bowell-Skiff.

He has discovered a number of asteroids, including the Trojan asteroid (15398) 1997 UZ₂₃.

Working on the LONEOS project, he re-discovered the long-lost 69230 Hermes in October 2003 and discovered the Apohele asteroid 2004 JG₆ in May 2004.

The asteroid 2554 Skiff was named in his honour.
