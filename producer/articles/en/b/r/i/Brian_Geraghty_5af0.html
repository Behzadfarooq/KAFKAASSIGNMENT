Brian Timothy Geraghty (born 1975) is an American film and television actor.

Geraghty was born in Toms River, New Jersey. He graduated from Toms River High School East in 1993 and then the Neighborhood Playhouse School of Theatre before beginning his professional career in New York City; he later moved to Los Angeles. In his free time, he enjoys surfing.
