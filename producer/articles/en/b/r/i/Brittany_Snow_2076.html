Brittany Anne Snow (born March 9, 1986 in Tampa, Florida) is an American television and film actress. Snow began modeling at the age of three in a print ad for Burdines. She is a 2004 graduate of Gaither High School in Tampa, Florida.

[edit] Career

Snow had a popular role at age twelve as troubled teen Susan LeMay on the soap opera Guiding Light from 1998 to 2001. She is perhaps best known for playing Meg Pryor on NBC's American Dreams and Ariel, a neo-Nazi high school student on the third season of Nip/Tuck.

In 2005, Snow appeared in The Pacifier with action star Vin Diesel. In 2006, Snow was in the film John Tucker Must Die, and voiced Namine in the video game Kingdom Hearts II and Shizuku in the Hayao Miyazaki film, "Whisper of the Heart." Brittany Snow will be in Hairspray, a film adaptation of the Broadway musical, alongside Zac Efron and John Travolta in 2007. She will be playing Amber Von Tussel

[edit] Personal Life

Snow had been dating actor Kyle Searles since 2003, but said in an interview about The Pacifier that they were no longer together after 2-and-a-half-years. Currently she is dating musician Michael Johnson, the drummer for boy band Natural.

She is best friends with actresses Sophia Bush, Arielle Kebbel, Ashanti, Bethany Joy Galeotti, Ashley Tisdale and Hayden Panettiere. Her favorite actresses are Meg Ryan, Natalie Portman and Reese Witherspoon.

[edit] Trivia

  • Played the nurse in the video "The Phrase That Pays" by The Academy Is...
  • Snow became the sixth victim and the seventh star ever to be featured on Punk'd more than once. Her first appearance is when she was tricked by Ashton Kutcher after she crashed into a portapotty with her car, making one man sick. Her second is when she tricked One Tree Hill's Sophia Bush after Bush pushed a waiter with her butt and crashed into a tank full of lobsters.
  • In a 2003 interview on her official website, Snow admitted to her fans of being a notorious blusher as she embarrasses far too easily.
  • In a 2005 interview, Snow admitted to receiving 8 detentions while in high school. Snow did not receive a single detention during her freshman and sophomore years but she was given 2 during her junior year (for tardiness and talking in class) and 6 during her senior year (passing notes, talking in class, tardiness, skipping a class, unexcused absences). [1]
  • Snow has done numerous anti-smoking public service announcements[2]. However, Snow has been caught in public smoking, showing she is a smoker, or at least a "social smoker".[3]
