[edit] Biography

Brian M. Reading

Brianreading the Wikipedian is known as Brian Reading in real life. Brian was born on August, 30 1985 in Lubbock, Texas. He is an American university student majoring in Information Systems. Brian currently resides in Houston.

[edit] Political Views

Brian is a Libertarian by ideology. He believes that the less government intervention on both social and economic matters, the better. He breaks on specific issues concerning foreign policy with the Libertarian Party (as well as with Liberals), as he feels they hold the wrong stance on The War on Terror and Gulf War II. Thus, he is a Neolibertarian.

He tends to side with Conservatives on most political issues, as the current Republican Party of the United States has an agenda that relies on States' rights versus Federal rights and low government intervention on economic matters. He tends to break with Conservatives however, based on their religious agendas and social intervention.

[edit] Religious and Philosophical Views

Brian is an Atheist who can sometimes be a bit of an Agnostic. He believes in a Nietzschean philosophy of Individualism and rational self-preservation, as well as a practical philosophy of Pragmatism. Brian believes in a natural social stratification among socio-economic classes.
