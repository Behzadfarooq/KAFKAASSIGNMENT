Bria Myles (born May 21, 1984) is an American Hip hop model and actress from North Hollywood, Los Angeles, California.

Bria attended Fairfax High School located in Los Angeles, California, near the border of West Hollywood in the Fairfax District. Bria realised early that she wanted to go into entertainment and she decided to take the plunge with the encouragement, advice, and guidiance of a few close friends.

[edit] Career

Bria Myles in a photo shoot.

Bria made her debut in the Rhymefest/Kanye West video "Brand New" in 2005. She followed this up with a lead performance in Twista's "Girl Tonight" video, which featured Trey Songz. In order to avoid the overexposure that typified several other Hip hop models, Bria took a sabbatical from music video performances that lasted until early 2006.

Bria resumed active work by appearing in Nelly Furtado's "Promiscuous" video and then delivering a noteworthy performance in the Pharrell Williams/Clipse music video "Mr. Me Too." More recently, Bria was featured on the June 2006 cover of King Magazine with other notable models in the hip hop industry, hosted the Ne-Yo BET Awards party, and also featured in the second July 2006 issue of Show Magazine.

Her most recent music video appearance is in Dem Jeans, a song by Chingy featuring Jermaine Dupri.
