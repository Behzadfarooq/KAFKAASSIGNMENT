Briargrove Park, Houston, Texas

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Briargrove Park is a subdivision in Houston, Texas, United States.

Briargrove Park is located north of Westheimer Road and inside of Texas State Highway Beltway 8.

[edit] Education

Westside High School

Briargrove Park residents are zoned to Houston ISD schools:

  • Walnut Bend Elementary School
  • Revere Middle School
    • Option: West Briar Middle School
  • Westside High School
