British NVC community OV4 (Chrysanthemum segetum - Spergula arvensis community) is one of the open habitat communities in the British National Vegetation Classification system.

It is a widely-distributed community. There are two subcommunities.

[edit] Community composition

The following constant species are found in this community:

  • Corn Marigold (Chrysanthemum segetum)
  • Annual Meadow-grass (Poa annua)
  • Common Knotgrass (Polygonum aviculare)
  • Corn Spurrey (Spergula arvensis)

No rare species are associated with the community.

[edit] Distribution

This is a widely distributed community, most commonly found in western Britain.

[edit] Subcommunities

There are two subcommunities:

  • the so-called typical subcommunity
  • the Ranunculs repens - Sonchus asper subcommunity
