Brian Hall (actor)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Brian Hall (November 20, 1937 in Brighton â€“ September 17, 1997 in Worthing, West Sussex) was an English actor perhaps best known for his role in the British sitcom Fawlty Towers where he played the hotel chef Terry. He also played leading roles in police drama Softly, Softly (1966) and sitcom You Must Be The Husband (1987). He also played several guest starring roles including The Professionals, The Long Good Friday, The Bill and The Sweeney. He died in September 1997 after a long battle with cancer.
