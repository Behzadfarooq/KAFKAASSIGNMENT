Brian Jones may refer to:

  • Brian Jones, founding member of The Rolling Stones
  • Brian Jones (aeronaut), balloonist
  • Brian Jones (poet), British poet
  • Brian Keith Jones (also known as Mr. Baldy), Australian convicted child sex offender
  • Brian Jones (basketball), University of North Dakota men's basketball coach
