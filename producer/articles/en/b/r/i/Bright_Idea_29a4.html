Bright Idea is the debut album by the Hollywood band Orson. Originally self-released in 2005, it was released commercially on May 29, 2006 in the UK and was released internationally on June 13, 2006. This album was recorded for just $5000 in Hollywood, funded by the band themselves, and produced by Noah Shain. It contains the hit singles "No Tomorrow", "Bright Idea", "Happiness" and the soon to be released "Already Over". Bright Idea charted at #1 on the official UK album chart on June 4, 2006, selling 47,542 copies.
