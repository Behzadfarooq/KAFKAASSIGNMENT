Bring 'Em Out
EP by Hawk Nelson
Released December 20, 2005
"Bring Em Out" may also refer to a 2004 rap single by T.I.

Bring 'Em Out from the MGM Motion Picture Yours, Mine, and Ours is a 3-song EP from the Canadian Christian punk band Hawk Nelson. It is only available on the iTunes store for $2.97. It was released on December 20, 2005.

[edit] Track listing

  1. "Bring 'Em Out"
    • Featuring Drake Bell
  2. "Things We Go Through"
  3. "Bring 'Em Out (Album Version)"

[edit] Misc.

  • "Things We Go Through" is from their 2004 album Letters to the President.
  • "Bring 'Em Out" is from their 2006 album Smile, It's The End of the World.
  • The first track, featuring Drake Bell, was written for and performed on the motion picture Yours, Mine and Ours, in which Hawk Nelson is the band playing at the party.
