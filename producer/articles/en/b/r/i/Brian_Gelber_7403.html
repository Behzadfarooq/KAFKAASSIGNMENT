Gelber Group President and CEO Brian Gelber (seated).

Brian Gelber is an options trader, Chairman and President of Gelber Group, a trader on the CBOT exchange floor, managed a large commodity fund, and directed a successful venture capital business. Brian is the founder, chairman and president of Gelber Group a company that provides trade execution, clearing, and risk management services for major global electronic exchanges.
