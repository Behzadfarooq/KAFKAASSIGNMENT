Brigitte Acton

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Brigitte Acton (born on November 30, 1985 in Sault Ste. Marie, Ontario, Canada) is a Canadian alpine skier.

Acton competed at the 2006 Winter Games, where she earned 11th in giant slalom, 17th in giant slalom, and 10th in downhill combined.

She finished first in Slalom in the 2006 Canadian National Champinoships, second in Giant Slalom and seventh in Super G.

Acton's mother competed in the 1972 Olympics in Sapporo, Japan.
