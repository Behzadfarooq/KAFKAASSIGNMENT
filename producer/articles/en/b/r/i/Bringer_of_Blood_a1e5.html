Bringer of Blood is Six Feet Under's seventh studio album. (Tenth album if you count live albums). Bringer of Blood contains fan favorites such as Amerika the Brutal, Murdered in the Basement, When Skin Turns Blue, and Bringer of Blood.

The German limited edition digipack CD in slipcase comes with a bonus DVD called "The Making of Bringer of Blood" and two bonus tracks. (See Bringer des Blutes, and Unknown)
