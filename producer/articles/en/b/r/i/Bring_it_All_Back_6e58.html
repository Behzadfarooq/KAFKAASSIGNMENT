"Bring it All Back" is a pop song written by S Club 7, Eliot Kennedy, Mike Percy and Tim Lever for S Club 7's debut album S Club (1999). The song was produced by Kennedy, Percy and Lever and received a mixed reception from music critics. It was released as their first single in the summer of 1999 and reached the top spot in the United Kingdom and became certified platinum. It also reached the top ten in Sweden and Ireland. "Bring it All Back" was released in North America on January 25, 2000 after their television show Miami 7 became popular in the United States, but it failed to make an impact on the Billboard Hot 100.
