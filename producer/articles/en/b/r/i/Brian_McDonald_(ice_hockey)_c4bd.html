Brian McDonald (born March 23, 1945, in Toronto, Ontario) is a former professional ice hockey centre. He played just twenty games in the National Hockey League with the Chicago Black Hawks and Buffalo Sabres. Meanwhile, he played several seasons in the World Hockey Association with the Houston Aeros, Los Angeles Sharks, Michigan Stags, and Indianapolis Racers. McDonald appeared in 304 WHA games in his career, scoring 90 goals and adding 100 assists.
