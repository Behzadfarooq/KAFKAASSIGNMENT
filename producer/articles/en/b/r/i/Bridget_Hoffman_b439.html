Bridget Hoffman (also Brigette Hoffman) is a voice actor. She is also credited as Ellen Wilkinson, Ellen Arden, Jane Arden, Karen Woods, Ruby Marlowe, Serena Kolb, and Tessa Ariel. She also serves an ADR scriptwriter and ADR director.
