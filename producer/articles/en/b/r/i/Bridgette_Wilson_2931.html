Bridgette Leann Wilson (born September 25, 1973), also known as Bridgette Wilson-Sampras, is an American actress.

[edit] Biography

[edit] Early life & acting career

She was born in Gold Beach, Oregon. Wilson-Sampras was crowned Miss Teen USA in 1990 in Biloxi, Mississippi; she is the second contender from Oregon to win this crown. After her reign as Miss Teen USA, Wilson-Sampras became an actress and appeared in: Saved By The Bell (playing Ginger), Last Action Hero (playing Arnold Schwarzenegger's daughter), Billy Madison (playing Adam Sandler's teacher/girlfriend), Mortal Kombat (playing Sonya Blade), I Know What You Did Last Summer (playing Sarah Michelle Gellar's elder sister) and The Wedding Planner. In addition to acting, Wilson-Sampras sings; her music is popular in Japan,[citation needed] and she has starred in The Rocky Horror Show (off-Broadway).

[edit] Personal life

The sturdy Wilson-Sampras is 5' 9" (175 cm), often does her own stunts, and wears size 12 shoes. Her sister, Tracy Wilson, is also an actress.

She married tennis champion Pete Sampras on September 30, 2000. They have two sons, Christian Charles (b. November 21, 2002) and Ryan Nikolaos (b. July 29, 2005).

[edit] Recent events

Wilson-Sampras was a judge on the 2006 Miss Universe competition.
