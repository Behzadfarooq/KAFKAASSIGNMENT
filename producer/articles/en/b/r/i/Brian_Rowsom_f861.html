Brian Rowsom

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Brian Rowsom (born October 23, 1965 in Newark, New Jersey), is an American former professional basketball player who was selected by the Indiana Pacers in the 2nd round (34th overall) of the 1987 NBA Draft. A 6'9" forward from the University of North Carolina at Wilmington, Rowsom played in 3 NBA seasons from 1987 to 1990. He played for the Pacers and Charlotte Hornets.

In his NBA career, Rowsom played in 82 games and scored a total of 457 points. On December 2, 1989 as a member for the Hornets, he scored a career high 20 points against the San Antonio Spurs.
