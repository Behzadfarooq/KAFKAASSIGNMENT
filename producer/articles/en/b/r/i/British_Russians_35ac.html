British Russians

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

You have new messages (last change).

British Russians are people of Russian origin who were born or raised in the United Kingdom. There are estimated to be around 200-300,000 Russians living in the UK, most based in London. Many are a mixture of businesspeople, proffessionals, young families and students.


[edit] Famous British Russians

  • Dame Helen Mirren, actress
  • Sir Peter Ustinov, actor, writer, & raconteur
  • Lilia Kopylova, international professional dancer
  • Alexandra Tolstoy, descendant of the Tolstoy dynasty
