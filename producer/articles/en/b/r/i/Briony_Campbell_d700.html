Doctor Briony Campbell was a minor fictional character in the popular BBC soap opera EastEnders. She was played by Rae Hendrie.

Briony was the paediatrician who looked after Freddie Mitchell when he was injured and had blood on the brain.

She interviewed Oliver Cousins, Walford's GP and boyfriend of Freddie's mother, Little Mo Mitchell. He told her that Mo had once nearly hit Freddie.

Briony then informed the police and social services, who investigated Mo. When Mo found out that Oliver had told Briony, she ended their relationship.

After more extensive tests, Freddie was found to have a low platelet count, and Mo was cleared of all allegations.

Mo later thought that Briony and Oliver were a couple, but Briony informed her that Oliver still liked her.
