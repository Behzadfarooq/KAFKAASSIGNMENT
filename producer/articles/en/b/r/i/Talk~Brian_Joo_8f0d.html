[edit] This name thing..

This name problem keeps on coming up again. I just asked some fans what the "G" stands for in "Brian G. Joo." Apparently, the "G" stands for Jintaek, a name that he really hates. This contradicts what many sources say, because many of them cite Joo Minkyu as his Korean name. Perhaps his English full name is Brian Jintaek Joo while his Korean equivalent is Joo Minkyu. I don't know. mirageinred 07:02, 9 December 2006 (UTC)
