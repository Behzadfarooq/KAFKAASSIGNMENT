  • Host: Jonathan Ross

[edit] Television, radio and film

Winners first; nominees indented

  • Best Comedy Series: The Fast Show
  • Best BBC Sitcom: One Foot In The Grave
  • Best ITV Sitcom: The 10%ers
  • Best Channel 4 Sitcom: Father Ted
  • Best Children's Comedy: Woof!
  • Best Comedy Film: Babe
  • Best Radio Comedy: People Like Us
  • Best TV Comedy Drama: Outside Edge
  • Best TV Entertainment Series: TFI Friday
  • Best International Comedy: Frasier
  • Best New TV Comedy: The Thin Blue Line

[edit] People and performances

  • Best TV Comedy Actor: Dermot Morgan - Father Ted
  • Best TV Comedy Actress: Pauline McLynn - Father Ted
  • Best ITV Entertainment Presenter: Cilla Black
  • Best BBC1 Entertainment Presenter: Ruby Wax
  • Best Channel 4/BBC2 Entertainment Presenter: Chris Evans - TFI Friday
  • Best Male Comedy Performer: Paul Whitehouse - The Fast Show
  • Best Female Comedy Performer: Caroline Aherne - The Mrs Merton Show
  • Best TV Newcomer: James Dreyfus - The Thin Blue Line
  • Best Stand-up Comic: Eddie Izzard
  • Best TV Comedy Personalities: Vic Reeves and Bob Mortimer
  • Writer's Guild Lifetime Achievement Award: Johnny Speight
  • Lifetime Achievement Awards: Cilla Black, Dave Allen, Johnny Speight
