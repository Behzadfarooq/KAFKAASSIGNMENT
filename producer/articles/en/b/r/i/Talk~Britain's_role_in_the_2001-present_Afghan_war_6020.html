[edit] Overlap with Operation Herrick

The Operation Herrick article sets out to covers all British military activity in Afghanistan since 2002. My concern is that this article, apart from the 2001 element (Operation Veritas), is a duplication. Unless I've missed something, this page should just be a disambiguation page to Operation Veritas and Operation Herrick. Greenshed 16:01, 20 December 2006 (UTC)
