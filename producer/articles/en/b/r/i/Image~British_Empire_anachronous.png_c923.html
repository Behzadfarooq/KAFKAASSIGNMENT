This map was the exact same anachronous map used in the British Empire page. The only difference is that some areas have been coloured in the appropriate colours. The new areas that have been shaded in are: Afghanistan, Rwanda, Burundi, Cuba and the Philippines. This is an anachronous map of the British Empire and England showing all the territories ruled from 1770 to 1948 (British Empire) and from 1066-1707 (England). Territories lost by 1900 in red (British) and lavender (English only), greatest extent in pink, wartime occupied territories in light pink and postwar occupied territories of defeated Axis in salmon.
