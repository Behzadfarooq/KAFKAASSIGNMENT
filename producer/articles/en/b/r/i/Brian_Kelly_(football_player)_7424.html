Brian Kelly (born January 14, 1976 in Las Vegas, Nevada, United States) is a National Football League cornerback currently playing for the Tampa Bay Buccaneers.

[edit] Professional Career

He was drafted by the Bucs in the 2nd round of the 1998 NFL Draft. He wears the number 25 jersey and helped the winning Super Bowl Team with an impressive 8 interceptions in 2002, which tied for the league lead. He currently starts on the other side of Ronde Barber, making them one of the best cornerback duos in the NFL.

[edit] College Career

Kelly graduated from USC in 1997.
