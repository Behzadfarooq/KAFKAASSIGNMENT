Brian Keich

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Brian "Buck" Keich is a mountain bike racer. He won the 2005 Single Speed World Championship in State College, PA. In 2006 he traveled to 2006 race held in Sweden to defend his World Championship title but came in second. Buck's current primary racing sponsor is Independent Fabrication.
