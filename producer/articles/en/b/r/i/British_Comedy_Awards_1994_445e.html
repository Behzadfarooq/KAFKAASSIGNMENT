[edit] Television, radio and film

Winners first; nominees indented

  • Best BBC Comedy Series: Red Dwarf
    • Absolutely Fabulous
  • Best ITV Comedy: Time After Time
  • Best Channel 4 Comedy: Drop The Dead Donkey
  • Best Entertainment Series: Barrymore
  • Best TV Comedy Series: Drop The Dead Donkey
  • Best Comedy Series: Murder Most Horrid
  • Best Comedy Drama: Outside Edge
  • Best New TV Comedy: Knowing Me, Knowing You... With Alan Partridge
  • Best Comedy Film: Four Weddings and a Funeral
  • Best Radio Comedy: A Look Back to the Future

[edit] People and performances

  • Best TV Comedy Actor: Stephen Tompkinson - Drop The Dead Donkey
  • Best TV Comedy Actress: Brenda Blethyn - Outside Edge
    • Jennifer Saunders - Absolutely Fabulous
  • Best TV Comedy Newcomer: Chris Morris - The Day Today
  • Best Male TV Performer: Steve Coogan - Knowing Me, Knowing You... With Alan Partridge
  • Best Female Comedy Performer: Tracey Ullman - Tracey Ullman Takes On New York
  • Best TV Comedy Personality: Steve Coogan
  • Best ITV Entertainment Presenter: Michael Barrymore - Barrymore
  • Best BBC Entertainment Presenter: Noel Edmonds - Noel's House Party
  • Best Channel 4 Entertainment Presenter: Chris Evans - Don't Forget Your Toothbrush
  • Best Theatre Variety Performer: Billy Pearce
  • Best Stand-up Comic: Phil Kay
  • Best Comedy Writer: Jack Rosenthal
  • Special Award: Armando Iannucci
  • Lifetime Achievement Award: Spike Milligan, June Whitfield
