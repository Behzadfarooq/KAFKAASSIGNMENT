Brian Lynch

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Brian Lynch (born June 21, 1973) is an American writer, best known for writing and directing the movie Big Helium Dog. He is currently writing the comic, Spike: Asylum (Angel comic) for IDW, and resurrected the villain Slyde for an issue of Spider-Man Unlimited. He was also responsible for uncredited writing work on Scary Movie 3.
