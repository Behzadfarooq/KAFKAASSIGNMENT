Brian Wilson (born 9 May 1983) is an English footballer currently playing for Bristol City F.C. in Football League One.

Wilson was born in Manchester and had previously played for Cheltenham Town F.C. and Stoke City F.C. He can play as a defender as well as a midfielder.
