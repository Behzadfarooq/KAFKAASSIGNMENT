British Championship Wrestling is a Scottish Professional wrestling promotion based in Scotland. The promotion opened in 2003 and features some of the most talented stars from Scotland. They have also had many international names pass through their doors, including Mick Foley, Raven, Steve Corino, Honky Tonk Man, Marty Jannetty, Chris Hero, Tracy Smothers, and more. The group promotes shows in the west of Scotland, in East Kilbride, Carluke, and Kilmarnock.
