[edit] Requested move

British Accountant → Accountancy qualifications in the United Kingdom – "British Accountant" is not a recognised qualification or a term in general use in the UK accountancy profession. The proposed title would better reflect the article's subject. THJames 21:07, 19 August 2006 (UTC)

[edit] Objection & Other idea

I suggest we can use both title British Accountant and Accountancy qualifications in the United Kingdom. Because in general practise many people would like to call British Accountant as the description to all qualified accountancy qualifications in the United Kingdom. So it is benefit to reserve the article name British Accountant in Wikipedia.

Lewislams 2:11, 21 August 2006 (UTC)

I think British qualified accountants may also be the good and appropriate title for the current page of British Accountant

[edit] Other idea

May be British accountancy or British accountancy qualifications is better than the suggested title Accountancy qualifications in the United Kingdom. But I still think British Accountant is one of acceptable title under the same reason stated by Lewislams as the above.

FrankL906 2:38, 21 August 2006 (UTC)
