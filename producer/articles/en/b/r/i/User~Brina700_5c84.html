[edit] About Me

I am self employed and have provided legal services to attorneys for over 20 years. I specialize in document processing and am highly skilled in research, editing, formatting, etc.

My college education is in psychology, though the study of Early Civilizations with an emphasis on Western Development has been a steady pursuit. I also have a passion for mythology, ancient spiritual practices, and the occult (though not as a "believer" or "follower" of said practices, but in how they relate to the development of ancient cultures).

My hobby is writing, fiction and nonfiction. Some of my work has been published on a small scale.
