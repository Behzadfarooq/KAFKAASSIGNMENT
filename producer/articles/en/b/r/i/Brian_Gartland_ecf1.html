Brian Gartland (born 4 November, 1986) is an Irish footballer who is currently playing for Shelbourne in the FAI First Division. Brian signed for Shels from Bray Wanderers on 27 February 2007. Gartland was the Bray Wanderers' Under-21 Player of the Year for 2006.

He made his debut for Shelbourne on 9 March 2007 in a league fixture against Kildare County, scoring the first of Shelbourne's goals in a 2-2 draw.
