Brian Augustyn is a comic book editor and writer.

He was born in 1954. He has often worked, as both an editor and writer, with Mark Waid.

During the late 1980s and early 1990s he was an editor for DC Comics where he edited The Flash, Justice League and the !mpact Comics line of titles. He discovered his love of comics with DC's Showcase #4, which starred The Flash.

As a writer he has worked on titles such as Batman: Gotham by Gaslight, The Flash, Imperial Guard, Out There, and Crimson.

He has been recognized for his work in the industry with the Wizard Fan Award for Favorite Editor in 1994.

Currently the Managing Editor for Visionary Comics Studio.
