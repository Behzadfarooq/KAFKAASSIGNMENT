Brickell Station is a Metrorail and Metromover changeover station in the western Brickell district of downtown Miami, Florida, USA.

The station is located near the intersection of Southwest First Avenue and 10th Street. It opened to Metrorail on service May 20, 1984 and to Metromover May 26, 1994. It is named for the district it is located in, which in turn, is named after the Brickell Family, a founding family of Miami.
