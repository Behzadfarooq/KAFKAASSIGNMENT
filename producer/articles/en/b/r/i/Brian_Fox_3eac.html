Brian Fox

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

You have new messages (last change).

Brian Fox is a free software programmer. He was the original author, in 1987, of the GNU Bash shell. Fox is also the author of:

  • The GNU components makeinfo, info, finger, and readline
  • The BuddyCast peer-to-peer streaming media protocol (with Mel Beckman and Denison Bollay)
  • Meta-HTML
