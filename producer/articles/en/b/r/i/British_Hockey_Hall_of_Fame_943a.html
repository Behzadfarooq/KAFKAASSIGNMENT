The British Ice Hockey Hall of Fame, was founded in 1948 and is the third oldest Hockey Hall of Fame in the world behind the Russian and Soviet Hockey Hall of Fame also founded in 1948 and the International Hockey Hall of Fame founded in 1943. The Hall serves to honor those individuals that have contributed to the sport of hockey in Britain. The Hall has displays and memorabilia depicting the significant contributions of players, coaches, referees and other important figures in the sport.
