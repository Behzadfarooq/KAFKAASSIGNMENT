Brian Mikkelsen

Brian Arthur Mikkelsen (born January 31, 1966) is a Danish politician. He is a member of the Conservative People's Party, and has been a member of parliament (Folketinget) since September 21, 1994.

He has been the Danish Culture Minister since 27 November 2001, as member of the Cabinet of Anders Fogh Rasmussen I and II.

Brian Mikkelsen has since 2002 been a member of the World Anti-Doping Agency, and is the current vice-chairman of the organization.

He wants the 2020 Summer Olympics to be held in Copenhagen[1].
