[edit] Jakes?

Why is his name pronounced "Jakes"? His name is from the French, should it not be pronounced as such?

Mr. Jacques is British, so at some point perhaps his ancestors changed the pronunciation. He has specifically stated how his name is pronounced, so there's no ambiguity here. ~MDD4696 00:19, 11 July 2006 (UTC)

Most English speaking people with that last name pronounce it "Jakes", myself included. 160.84.253.241 21:04, 22 March 2007 (UTC)
