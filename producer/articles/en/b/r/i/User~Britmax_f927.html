I have just noticed that most season records in the English Football League link to Bournemouth F.C (The Poppies), based at Victoria Avenue, as the relevant club. Unfortunately this club has never played league football: the correct club is A.F.C. Bournemouth, who play across town at the Fitness First Stadium, still known locally as Dean Court. I will gradually change all the relevant records but if someone does some of it I'd be grateful as it is quite a repetitive task. Britmax 23:41, 25 February 2007 (UTC)
