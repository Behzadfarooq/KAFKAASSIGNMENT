Bridge Street Middle School is an Ohio County, West Virginia public school housing grades 6-8. It is located in Elm Grove, a neighborhood of Wheeling, West Virginia. Elm Grove and Middle Creek Elementary schools are the "feeder" schools for Bridge Street Middle.
