Brian Wilson (disambiguation)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Brian Wilson may refer to:

  • Brian Wilson, the American musician, member of the Beach Boys
  • Brian Wilson (song), a song, referring to the musician, by the Barenaked Ladies
  • Brian Wilson (systems) (born 1933), British academic, co-developer of soft systems methodology (SSM)
  • Brian Wilson (politician) (born 1948), the British Labour Party politician
  • Brian Wilson (Northern Ireland politician), joined the Green party in 2005
  • Brian Wilson (Fox News correspondent) (born c.1960?), D.C. bureau chief and vice president of Fox News Channel
  • Brian Wilson (Australian rules footballer) (born 1961), Brownlow Medal winner 1982
  • Brian Wilson (poker player) (born 1968?), bracelet winner at the 2005 World Series of Poker
  • Brian Wilson (baseball player) (born 1982), Major League Baseball player
  • Brian Wilson (footballer) (born 1983), Football (Soccer) player currently playing for Bristol City F.C.
  • Brian M. Wilson, the American radio talk show host.
  • Brian S. Wilson, instructional designer.
