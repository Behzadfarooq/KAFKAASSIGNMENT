Brian Ireland

Brian Ireland was (born November 1980) in Dana Point, California. He is currently the drummer for the Geffen Records/Drive-Thru Records band, Something Corporate. Brian is one of four members of Something Corporate. His bandmates are Andrew McMahon, Kevin "Clutch" Page, and Josh Partington. Ireland is currently working on a side project with former Something Corporate member, William Tell. On December 10, 2006 it was announced that Brian would also be joining the New York based band Streamline as their drummer, on top of his three other drumming positions.

[edit] Discography

  • Ready...Break (2000, an independent CD, as Something Corporate)
  • Audioboxer (2001 as Something Corporate)
  • Leaving Through the Window (2002 as Something Corporate)
  • North (2003 as Something Corporate)
  • You Can Hold Me Down (drummer and producer for William Tell's 2007 album)
