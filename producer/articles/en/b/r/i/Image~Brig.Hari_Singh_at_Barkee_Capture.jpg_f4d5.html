[edit] Summary

Brig. Hari Singh A.V.S.M.

This picture is of my maternal grandfather Brig. Hari Singh Deora A.V.S.M (Ati Vishisht Sewa Medal), 18th Cavalry (Indian Army).

This picture was taken during the 1965 Indo-Pak War when the Indian Army made a successful attempt through the bridge in the village of Barki, just east of Lahore to cross over the Ichhogil Canal.

For more information, contact me through www.AbhinayRathore.com
