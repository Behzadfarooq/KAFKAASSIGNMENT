Brianne Chierighino Siddall (Born August 25, 1963 in West Virginia) is a voice actress. She is usually cast in the short kid role of a timid young boy. She is also a well-known voice actress during the Power Rangers era who was active in the franchise until it moved to New Zealand in 2003. Although she voiced many different monsters, one of her best-known roles was the voice of an ally, that being Circuit in 2001's PR incarnation, Power Rangers: Time Force (and in 2002 during the Wild Force/Time Force team-up, Reinforcements From the Future, was able to reprise her role as Circuit's voice).

She is sometimes credited as "Ian Hawk" or "Jetta E. Bumpy" .
