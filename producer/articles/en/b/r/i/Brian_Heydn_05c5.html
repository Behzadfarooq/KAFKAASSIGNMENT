Brian Heydn

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Brian Heydn is an Artist and Graphic Designer, best known for his work on album covers and CD packaging for artists like The 77s, Daniel Amos, and The Lost Dogs.

Heydn began working with the 77s in 1999 on the artwork and packaging design of their EP CD. Since then, he has went on to work on dozens of other CDs and Book Sets.

Heydn currently lives in Missouri with his wife and children.
