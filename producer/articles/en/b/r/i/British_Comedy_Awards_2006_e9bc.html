[edit] Television, film and stage

[edit] Best Comedy Entertainment Programme

  • Harry Hill's TV Burp (Avalon Television, ITV1)
    • Have I Got News for You (Hat Trick Productions, BBC One)
    • Ant & Dec Christmas Takeaway (ITV1)

[edit] Best Comedy Film

  • Wallace & Gromit: The Curse of the Were-Rabbit
    • Confetti
    • Little Miss Sunshine

[edit] Best International Comedy TV Show

  • Curb Your Enthusiasm (HBO, More4)
    • Everybody Hates Chris (Five)
    • The Office: An American Workplace (ITV2)

[edit] Britain's Best New TV Comedy

  • Star Stories (Objective Productions, Channel 4)
    • Suburban Shootout (Five)
    • That Mitchell and Webb Look (BBC 2)

[edit] Best TV Comedy

  • Peep Show (Channel 4)
    • Extras (BBC 2)
    • The Thick of It (BBC 4)

[edit] Best Stage Comedy

  • Little Britain Live
    • The Mighty Boosh
    • The Two Faces of Mitchell and Webb

[edit] People's Choice Award

This award is voted for by the public:

  • Ant & Dec's Saturday Night Takeaway (ITV1) (1st Place)
    • Green Wing (Channel 4) (2nd Place)
    • Friday Night with Jonathan Ross (BBC 1) (3rd Place)
    • That Mitchell and Webb Look (BBC 2) (4th Place)
      • Extras (BBC 2) (Long List)
      • 8 Out of 10 Cats (Channel 4) (Long List)
      • Little Britain (BBC 1) (Long List)
      • Northern Lights (ITV1) (Long List)
