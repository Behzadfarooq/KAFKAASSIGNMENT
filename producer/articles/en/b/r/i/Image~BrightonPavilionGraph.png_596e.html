Election results for Brighton Pavilion constituency from its creation in 1950 to the 2005 general election. © Jeremy Atherton 2006.

[edit] Notes

Graph drawn with IGOR Pro v.5.04

The Background colour indicates the party of the sitting MP at any given year.

Parties:

  • Conservative = Conservative Party (UK)
  • Green = Green Party of England and Wales
  • Labour = Labour Party (UK)
  • Liberal = Liberal Party (UK) (1950–1979); Social Democratic Party (UK) (1983–1987); Liberal Democrats (UK) (1992–present).
