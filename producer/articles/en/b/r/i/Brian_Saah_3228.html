Brian Saah (born December 16, 1986) is a 6'1" professional footballer for Leyton Orient in League One of the Football League. Despite starting as a central midfielder Saah has recently converted to a central defender and won praise for his displays deputising for first team regulars John Mackie and Gabriel Zakuani during the 2005/6 season. He has since gained further praises with his highly rated performances for Orient in the 2006/7 season. By many Brian Saah is seen as a future star, and is expected by some to join Gabriel Zakuani at the top of the football pyramid. Recently Saah has agreed to extend his Leyton Orient contract until the summer of 2009.
