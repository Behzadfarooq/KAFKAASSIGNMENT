Brian Allsop was a rugby league player in the Australian Competition, the New South Wales Rugby League(NSWRL).

A wing-threequarter, Allsop played for 2 Sydney clubs during his career, 80 matches for the Eastern Suburbs club in the years (1955-59), and 74 for Manly Warringah(1960-64).

An Easts junior Allsop made it into the first grade side in 1955, in that season he was Easts leading try scorer, In one match that year against the Parramatta Eels he scored 5 tries.

Allsop was a representative of Sydney and NSW. The speedy winger scored a total of 59 tries for Easts before departing for Manly were he scored a further 36 in his time there.
