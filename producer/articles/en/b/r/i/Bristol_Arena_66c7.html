The Bristol Arena is a 10,500-seat multi-purpose arena in Bristol, England that is currently in planning. It is due to open in 2009 although negotiations are still ongoing between the city council, regional development agency and developers with regard to how it will be financed. Local residents are due to be consulted towards the end of 2006 - for more information on that process see [1]. Once completed, it will host concerts and sporting events.
