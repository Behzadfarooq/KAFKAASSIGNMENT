Bristol and Suburban Association Football League

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Bristol and Suburban Association Football League is a football competition in England. This league has a total of seven divisions, the highest of which, the Premier Division One, sits at level 12 of the English football league system. It is one of three feeders to the Gloucestershire County League.

In the 2005-06 season, St Aldhelms won the title. For the 2006-07 season, 15 clubs are competing in the Premier Division One. The league's other divisions are Premier Division Two and Divisions One to Five.
