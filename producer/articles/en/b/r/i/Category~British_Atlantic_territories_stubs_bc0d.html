This category is for stub articles relating to the British Overseas Territories in the Atlantic (that is, Bermuda, the Falkland Islands, Saint Helena and its dependencies, and South Georgia and the South Sandwich Islands). You can help Wikipedia by expanding them.

To add an article to this category, use {{UK-Atlantic-stub}} instead of {{stub}}.

For geographical articles relating to the United Kingdom's Atlantic territories, use {{UK-Atlantic-geo-stub}}. Bermuda has its own geographical stub category - {{Bermuda-geo-stub}}.
