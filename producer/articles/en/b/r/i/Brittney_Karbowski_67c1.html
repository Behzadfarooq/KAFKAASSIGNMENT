Brittney Karbowski (born June 20, 1986) is an American voice actress. She currently works for ADV Films. Her most notable anime role is Fuko from Gilgamesh.

[edit] History

Brittney got her big break in entering ADV Films when Matt Greenfield spotted her in the audience during a musical in Houston and asked her to be auditioned as a voice actor. After her auditions, she was able to land her first role in Gilgamesh with other roles in Le Chevalier D'Eon and Pani Poni Dash. [1]

Brittney is a Theater major at the University of Houston. [1]
