Brighu Lake

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Brighu Lake
Location Kullu district
Lake type High altitude lake
Surface elevation 4235 m
References Himachal Pradesh Tourism Dep.

Brighu Lake is a high altitude lake which is located in the district Kullu. It is about 4235m above the sea level. It lies east of the Rohtang Pass and is about 6kms. from the village of Gulaba. This lake is held sacred to the sage, Brighu. It lies on the various trek routes of the region.[1]
