British Columbia Amateur Hockey Association

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

BCAHA Emblem

The British Columbia Amateur Hockey Association is the governing body of all ice hockey in British Columbia and the Yukon Territory, Canada. The BCAHA is a branch of Hockey Canada.

[edit] Leagues

  • British Columbia Hockey League Tier II Junior "A"
  • Kootenay International Junior Hockey League Junior "B"
  • Pacific International Junior Hockey League Junior "B"
  • Vancouver Island Junior Hockey League Junior "B"
