Brian Tee

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Brian Tee (born March 15, 1977 in Okinawa, Japan) is an Asian American actor of half Korean and half Japanese descent. At the age of two, he moved to Hacienda Heights, CA and was raised there ever since. He attended Glen A. Wilson High School and was ASB president and a star captain of the football team. He is most famous for his appearance as D.K. in The Fast and the Furious: Tokyo Drift. He speaks Korean, Japanese, Chinese and English. In his spare time, he enjoys working out, playing sports, and practicing martial arts.
