The Brighton Tsunami are a member of the British Collegiate American Football League (BCAFL)'s Southern Conference-Southern Division. The Tsunami have been one of the more successful recent additions to the BCAFL, finishing their first two seasons with a winning record. Brighton is still seeking their first playoff appearance.

  • 2004-05 - 3-4-1
