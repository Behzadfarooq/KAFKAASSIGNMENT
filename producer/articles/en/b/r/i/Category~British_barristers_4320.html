Category:British barristers

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

A very large number of British MPs were called to the bar and had brief and unimportant legal careers before entering politics. These politicians account for a large majority of the British barristers with Wikipedia articles. Thus this category will be more useful if only individuals whose legal career was itself notable are placed in it.
