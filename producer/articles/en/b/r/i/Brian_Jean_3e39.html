Brian M. Jean (born February 3, 1963) is a Canadian politician.

Born in Kelowna, British Columbia, has a Bachelor of Science degree, Master of Business Administration degree and a Bachelor of Laws degree. He worked as a farm hand, logger, publisher, businessperson, teacher, and lawyer before being elected as a Conservative to the Canadian House of Commons for the riding of Fort McMurray—Athabasca in 2004.
