Brihan Maharashtra

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Brihan Maharashtra is an organisation supporting natives of the Indian state of Maharashtra who live elsewhere. Benefiting from its educational programme, many of its members follow service-oriented careers. The community has formed several Maharshtra Mandals to preserve its culture.

The community has Indian branches in New Delhi, Ahmadabad, Bhopal, Hyderabad, Bangalore and other cities, and overseas branches in the USA, Canada, Russia, Japan, Australia, Tanzania, Kenya, the UAE and elsewhere.
