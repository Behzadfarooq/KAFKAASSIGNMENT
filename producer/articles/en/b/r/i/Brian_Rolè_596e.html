Brian Rolè

Brian Rolè is a Maltese magician and illusionist. He was born on Malta's sister island Gozo in 1972 and became a full time professional magician in 2000. Currently he performs with his wife Lola under the stage name The Palmers.

In 2004 he was elected as President of the International Brotherhood of Magicians' Ring 202 in Malta for one year, having previously served for three years as the Ring's secretary.

[edit] Awards

Brain Rolè came first in the Parlour Magic Competition in 2002 and was runner up in the Close-up Magic Competition in 2004.

[edit] Performances

Brian performed extensively across the world, from South America to Asia, as well as several countries in Europe and at the Emirates Towers in Dubai. He also became the first illusionist to perform at the ruins of the Royal Opera House in Valletta, more than 50 years after its destruction.

[edit] On television

Brian Rolè performed on various Maltese television shows, namely Tlaqna (in which he performed street magic) and Raxx (in which he performed grand illusions), both of which aired on local tv station TVM. Apart from that he also appeared on television shows, such as Showtime Ma Aceline, Is-Sibt, Hello Ray amongst others as a special guest.

[edit] Recently

Most recently he launched the new James Bond film, Casino Royale at the Intercontinental Hotel in Malta for the special premier screening at The Eden Century Cinemas on the 16th of November 2006.
