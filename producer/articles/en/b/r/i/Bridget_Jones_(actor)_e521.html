Bridget Jones (actor)

Bridget Jones is a writer and performer for Mystery Science Theater 3000. She was an occasional presence for the first few seasons before being made a full-time writer by the Sci Fi Channel era. [1] On camera she first appeared in episode 316, Gamera vs. Zigra, as Helen, a young Coke-loving Japanese girl. She has played characters as diverse as Mr. B Natural and Lisa Loeb but is probably best known for two roles: Nuveena, Mike Nelson's singing love interest (in episodes 524, 12 to the Moon, and 614, San Francisco International), and Flavia, an evil Roman matron and Pearl Forrester's nemesis in a multi-episode arc in season eight. [2]

She is married to fellow MST3K writer Michael J. Nelson. They have two sons. [3]
