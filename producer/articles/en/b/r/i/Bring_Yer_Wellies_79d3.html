Bring Yer Wellies is the sixth album by Celtic band Gaelic Storm. It was released on July 25, 2006.

[edit] Personnel

Gaelic Storm

  • Patrick Murphy (accordion, spoons, lead vocals)
  • Steve Twigger (guitar, bouzouki, mandolin, lead vocals)
  • Ryan Lacey (drums, percussion)
  • Ellery Klein (fiddle, vocals)
  • Peter Purvis (Highland bagpipes, Uilleann pipes, Deger pipes, whistles)

Additional personnel

  • Jeff May (bass)
  • Rob Forkner (bodhran)
  • Michael Ramos (accordion)
  • Lauren DeAlbert (didgeridoo)

[edit] Miscellanea

"Wellies" is a nickname for Wellington boots, which feature prominently in the lyrics of "Kelly's Wellies" and on the album cover.
