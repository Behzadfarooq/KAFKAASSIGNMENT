Brian Prestowitz (born February 15, 1977, in Sendai, Japan (Miyagi prefecture)) is a music composer and computer technician with a degree in multimedia. Commonly known as "Burner" or as "The Mac Surgeon" in the Lake Tahoe Area. Grew up near Washington, D.C. and moved to Northern, CA in 1998. He has made himself a name in both the music industry and computer tech industry.

He has a total of 164 composed songs and over 20 independent albums. It is also rumored that he has several years of martial art training in the art of Kenpo Karate and Jiu-Jitsu.

After 3 small jobs and going to a community college for 4 years while in the multimedia field, he trained and worked at Apple Computer. This would later be the cause of his move to the Lake Tahoe area. There he is currently writing more and better music while still working on people's Macintoshes and PCs. Few people recogize him as more than being a computer tech but also a music composer as well.

His compositions mainly consist of Japanese pop music. Every now and then he throws in a slow song or a jazz song.

Though it is not confirmed yet he may have been in a few student film productions back since 1994 while he was living in the Washington, D.C. metropolitan area. However, it is confirmed that over 7 of his compositions have been used in other people's films.

TRIVIAL FACTS: Brian Prestowitz's native language is English while he is able to comprehend and speak Japanese as well. His Japanese name is Kimura Kenji. In Japan the last name is spoken first and the first name is spoken last. Brian Prestowitz is also adopted hence his Polish last name.
