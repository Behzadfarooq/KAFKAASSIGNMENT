    Matthew Brian Meadows (born November 21, 1975) is a relief pitcher and is currently a free agent.

    Meadows graduated from Charles Henderson (AL) High School, where he played baseball. In the offseason he designates time and effort in support of the Garth Brooks' Teammates for Kids Foundation. Meadows is also involved in the March of Dimes program in Alabama. He was originally signed by Florida scout Bill Singer.
