Brian Marcil

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Brian Marcil was a professional football player in the Canadian Football League.

Marcil graduated from Loyola College in Montreal, where he starred on the Warriors undefeated 1968 team (which has since been inducted into Concordia University's Sports Hall of Fame.)

The CFL was home to a 6 year career, mostly with the Calgary Stampeders (5 years and 46 games from 1970 to 1974) where he played on the Grey Cup winning 1971 team. He also had a short stint with the Montreal Alouettes (9 games in 1972) and finished his career with 2 games for the Ottawa Rough Riders in 1975.

Later Marcil would be Vice President of the Alouettes Alumni Asscociation, play for the Montreal Irish rugby club, and go into the insurance business.
