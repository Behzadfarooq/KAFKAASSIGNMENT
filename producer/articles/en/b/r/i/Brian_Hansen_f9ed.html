Brian Dean Hansen (born October 20, 1960 in Hawarden, Iowa) is a former National Football League punter. He played from 1984 to 1999 for the New Orleans Saints, New England Patriots, Cleveland Browns, New York Jets, and Washington Redskins. He originally signed on with New Orleans in 1984 as an undrafted free agent.
