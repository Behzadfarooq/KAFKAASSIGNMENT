Brigade was a comic-book published by Image comics created, plotted and written by Rob Liefeld and illustrated by Marat Mychaels.

[edit] Series history

The original team was led by Battlestone, who had been a former member of Youngblood, as well as members of Image Comics' 'Golden Age' team The Allied Supermen of America. The original team was killed off during Extreme Sacrifice, one of Extreme Studios company wide events. They were replaced by more recognizable characters from the Image Comics universe, including Jim Valentino's Shadowhawk and Erik Larsen and Gary Carlson's Vanguard.

A new Brigade was formed and published by Awesome Entertainment, including Jeff Matsuda's Ka-Boom and Youngblood's Badrock,
