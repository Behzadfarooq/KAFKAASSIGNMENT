[edit] Television, radio and film

  • Best Entertainment Series: Alas Smith and Jones
  • Best BBC Sitcom: One Foot In The Grave
  • Best ITV/Channel 4 Sitcom: Drop The Dead Donkey
  • Best New TV Comedy: Have I Got News For You
  • Best Comedy Film: Home Alone
  • Best Radio Comedy: On The Hour, The Million Pound Radio Show

[edit] People and performances

  • Best TV Entertainment Presenter: Clive Anderson - Whose Line Is It Anyway
  • Best TV Comedy Actor: Richard Wilson - One Foot In The Grave
  • Best TV Comedy Actress: Patricia Routledge
  • Best British Entertainment Performers: Mel Smith and Griff Rhys Jones
  • Best TV Comedy Newcomer: Angus Deayton
  • Best Stage Newcomer: Jack Dee
  • Best Comedy Club Performer: Jeremy Hardy
  • Best Variety Performers: Vic Reeves and Bob Mortimer
  • Best Radio Comedy Personality: Phil Holden
  • Best Comedy Writer: John Sullivan
  • Lifetime Achievement Award: Beryl Reid, George Burns
