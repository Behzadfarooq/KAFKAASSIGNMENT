The Brisbane Cup is a Group 1 Australian thoroughbred horse race for 3 year olds and over, run under handicap conditions over a distance of 3200 metres at Eagle Farm Racecourse, Brisbane in June. 2006 Prizemoney is AUD$500,000.

[edit] Race history

  • 1866 run over 18 furlongs (approx 3600 metres).
  • 1883 & 1888 run over 14 furlongs (approx 2800 metres).
  • 1946 run over 12 furlongs (approx 2400 metres).
