Category:British MPs

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

This is a category listing current and former Members of the United Kingdom House of Commons, and the parliaments of the predecessor states: the Parliament of England and the Parliament of Scotland.

MPs should not be added directly to this category. They should instead be categorised under one or more of the appropriate sub-categories:

before 1707
  • Category:Members of the pre-1707 English Parliament
  • Category:Members of the pre-1707 Scottish Parliament
1707–1800
  • Category:Members of the Parliament of Great Britain
1801—present
  • Category:Members of the United Kingdom Parliament
