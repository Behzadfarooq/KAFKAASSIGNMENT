Britt Fredriksen (born 1 October 1945 in Norway) was Playboy magazine's Playmate of the Month for its June 1968 issue. Her centerfold was photographed by Pompeo Posar.

Before taking a break from college, Britt had been a mathematics major. At the time of her photo shoot, she was a bunny at the Playboy Club in St. Louis, Missouri. She stayed in modeling after appearing in Playboy.
