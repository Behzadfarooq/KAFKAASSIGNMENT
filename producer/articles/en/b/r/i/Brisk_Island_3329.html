Brisk Island

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Brisk Island and surrounds (click to zoom).

Brisk Island is one of the islands in the Great Palm Island group. The nearest island is Great Palm Island, after which the group is named. The Aboriginal name for Brisk Island is Culgarool Island.

The island is situated in Steamer Passage, Halifax Bay approximately 2 kilometers South East of Palm Island, Queensland in Queensland Australia.
