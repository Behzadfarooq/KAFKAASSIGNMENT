Brian Berrebbi was born May 17th, 1978 in Brooklyn New York.

Brian is a writer and actor. He has written for The Andy Milonakis Show and is a frequent performer at The Upright Citizens Brigade Theatre. He has appeared in numerous commercials, including commercials for Nabisco/Triscuits, Spike TV, and Advance Internet. He is most recognizable for Iappearing in a commercial for T-Mobile entitled "Busted", but more commonly known as "the 'Secret Lovers' commercial", which began to air in October 2006.
