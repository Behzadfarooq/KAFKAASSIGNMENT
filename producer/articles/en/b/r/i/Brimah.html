The Brimah family is a very large migrant African family that originated several generations ago in the Saudi Arabian kingdom, and has since then migrated stopping for generations at a time, through the great African kingdom: Timbuktu in Mali, an on to Nigerias Northern border state, Sokoto, residing in Birnin Kebi, and then were a part of the famous Fulanis who were invited to Kwara state capital, Ilorin to help the Ilorin Yorubas fight and ward off the Oyo empire regions who came to capture Ilorin and make it a part of the Oyo empire.

The Brimah family, along with the Gambari family and the now Alao family came as leaders with other fulani warriors and fought off the Oyo army. Ilorin, its name meaning 'grind Iron', hence became part of the Northern empire, as these fulanis stayed and ruled Ilorin.


After the first Emir(Northeren emirate title for Chief) ruling ilorin, from the Gambari family died, this single family decided to rule as a monarchy and transfer the title automatically to the next in kin. Brimah opposed this and protested for an election system instead. An election was held, the Brimah candidate lost, and the Gambari son won, hence, knowing the repercussion, Brimah left with about 13 family members on a self exile in the middle of the night. His spirtualist had told him he would not/should not stop his journey till he gets to a river he cant cross, hence Brimah travelled through the countries of west African coast, through Benin republic, through Togo to Ghana where he got to the great river and stopped. Brimah then settled with his family and started trade in Ghana.


His great success, selling kolanuts and other imports and exports, and his good strong character quickly raised him in status in this Accra region of then Gold Coast in today Ghana. Soon Brimah was selected by the government as the leader of all foreign moslem migrants in Ghana(Zongo community), he was thus chosen as a king over all hausas, fulanis and Yoruba immigrants in Ghana.

Brimah was given a wife from Brazil by the Ghanian government whose name was Fatima Peregrino. He had fur wives, and all his kids by this wife got the suffix attached to their name -Peregrino-Brimah.

His family is accredited with many achievements including commissioning of the first Tap, pipe borne water in Ghana, and building the first story building, also a son of Brimah, Umar, bought the first Lincoln car in Ghana and was sang about something like this...'Umaru..Umaru lincoln'

The current Chief, Chief Amida Peregrino Brimah volunteered during WWII and fought in Burma, gratification was accorded him in the memorial gates Trust foundation, [1] [2] , by Queen Elizabeth of England for his selfless service and volunteering efforts as one of many great Africans.

CHIEF AMIDA PEREGRINO BRIMAH

He also was part of the protest with other WWII veterans that led to the liberation of Ghana from colonial rule, which was the first west African nation to gain independence, hence spearheading west Africas liberation from colonial rule. The Brimahs all through the generations remain great achievers in foreign lands world wide. I the author, am the meekest.
