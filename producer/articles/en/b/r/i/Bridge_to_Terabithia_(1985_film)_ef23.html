Bridge to Terabithia was made into a short tv movie filmed in Edmonton, Alberta, Canada for PBS in 1985, starring Annette O'Toole, Julian Coutts and Julie Beaulieu. The film is based on the children's novel Bridge to Terabithia by Katherine Paterson.
