Brihthelm

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Brihthelm, a possible Bishop of Selsey.

He may have received a grant of land from King Eadwig in 956, but the charter has been condemned as spurious [1] [1].

He possibly issued a charter in 957 [2], but that document is also supicious [1]
