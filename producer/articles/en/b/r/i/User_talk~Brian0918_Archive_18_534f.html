[edit] Columbia encyclopedia

Hi, Brian. I think the Columbia encyclopedia topic list you have was ordered to be deleted by Jimbo because he was worried about copyright problems. People have tried to get around it by combining lists from other encyclopedias. The Columbia topics are included in that large list. It makes them harder to use, though. I recompiled the Weisstein articles myself, kept them offline and deleted them after I had done all I could. I used the preview function to find out which ones already had articles so it wouldn't be stored on the site. I removed those topics by hand, but for this bigger list I came up with a way to remove them using Microsoft Word based on color (that list is from multiple sources). Terms with more than one meaning may get lost that way, though. So, I guess you could leave the list as it is, combine it with at least one other list, keep it offline or use the gigantic combined list. -- Kjkolb 06:50, 19 December 2005 (UTC)
