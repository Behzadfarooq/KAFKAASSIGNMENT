Bristol-D (ブリストル-D ) is a fictional character designed for the Power Instinct game series.

[edit] Profile

Bristol-D is the demonic form of Bristol Weller. After defeating the original form, Bristol-D rips out of the fallen regular Weller's body.

[edit] Special Moves

  • Faerie Kamikaze
  • Satanic Upper
  • Demon Fire
  • Hellbound Kicks
  • Energy Expulsion
  • Horrid Swarm

[edit] Quotes

  • "The fear is tearing your heart... just close your eyes!!"
  • "Now you understand... you can't feel the pain, cause you are already dead."
  • "There is no one who has seen the true darkness and lived to regret it."
  • "Your flesh and blood are ours."
