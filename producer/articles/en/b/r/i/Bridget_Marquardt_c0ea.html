Bridget Christina Marquardt (born Bridget Sandmeier September 25, 1973 in Tillamook, Oregon) is an American television personality, model, and actress. Marquardt is mostly known for starring in the popular TV show The Girls Next Door, which features Hugh Hefner's three girlfriends Holly Madison, Kendra Wilkinson and herself.

[edit] Biography

Bridget Marquardt was born on September 25, 1973 in the town of Tillamook, Oregon. The family moved there after living in Lodi, California. Shortly after her birth, Marquardt's mother moved the family back to California. Her parents would eventually divorce, but her mother would get married again. Marquardt lived with her mother, sister Anastasia and younger brother Eddie at their stepfather's ranch.

Marquardt attended Galt High School for her freshman and sophomore years. She then transferred to Lodi High School. Marquardt graduated from high school in 1990, two months after her 17th birthday.

Some of her male friends thought that she should pose for Playboy magazine. Marquardt sent a letter to Playboy inquiring how to become a Playmate.

Then, Marquardt enrolled at San Joaquin Delta Junior College. After graduating with an A.A., Marquardt transferred to California State University, Sacramento. In 1998 at the age of 25, Marquardt entered Playboy's Millennium Playmate search. She had a successful photo shoot and was sent to the Playboy Mansion for another test shoot. Later, Marquardt graduated from CSU Sacramento with a B.A. in Communications and an emphasis on Public Relations.

In 2001, Marquardt moved to Los Angeles after earning her Master's Degree in Communications from the University of the Pacific in Stockton, California. She immediately attracted the attention of modeling and acting agents and began appearing in small roles for movies, soap operas and graduate films. She also found herself attending parties at the Playboy Mansion, where she became friends with a number of Hugh Hefner's girlfriends. In March 2002, Bridget went on her first date with Hugh Hefner, and moved into the mansion and became one of Hefner's girlfriends late that same year.

[edit] In Media

Marquardt was featured on the E! TV show The Girls Next Door, and appeared in a pictorial in Playboy magazine's November 2005 issue as well as the September 2006 issue. She has appeared in several films as well including the horror/comedy Kottentail in which she portrays a scientist doing genetic research on rabbits.

She appeared in an episode of Curb Your Enthusiasm as herself along with Holly Madison and Hefner in 2005.

She also appeared in an episode of Celebrity Paranormal Project which aired on November 19, 2006. In the episode, she had the strongest connection with ghosts of anyone else in the group. She also claimed to have taken an online paranormal course because of her interests in paranormal activity.
