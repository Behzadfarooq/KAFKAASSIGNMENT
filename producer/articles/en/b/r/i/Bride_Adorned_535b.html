Bride Adorned

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Bride Adorned are a symphonic power metal band from Finland. Bride Adorned's music is characterized by extensive usage of choir vocals and grandiose arrangements. Similar bands include Therion and Rhapsody of Fire.
