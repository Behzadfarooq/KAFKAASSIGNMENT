Brill is a fairly uncommon family name, most seen in the Dutch language.

  • Abraham Brill, an Austrian-born American psychiatrist and disciple of Jung.
  • Debbie Brill, a Canadian high-jump athlete.
  • Diane Brill, a cosmetics merchant and manufacturer.
  • Dusty Brill, a drummer.
  • E. J. Brill, founder of Brill Publishers, a Dutch publishing company based in Leiden.
  • Eric Brill, a computer scientist.
    • Brill Tagger, one of Eric Brill's more notable inventions.
  • Fran Brill, a voice actress and puppeteer best known for work on Sesame Street.
  • Hermann Brill, a German politician who opposed the rise of Nazism.
  • J. G. Brill, founder of J.G. Brill and Company.
  • Jehiel Brill, Russian journalist ([1])
  • John Frederick Brill, a World War II mural artist.
  • Ludwig Brill, a co-creator with Max Noether of Brill-Noether theory, a branch of mathematics.
  • Paul and Mattheus Brill, 16th century Dutch landscape painters.
  • Steven Brill
    • Steven Brill (law writer), the founder of American Lawyer magazine, Court TV, Contentville, and Brill's Content.
    • Steven Brill (scriptwriter), actor, director, and screenwriter of films such as the Mighty Ducks films.
