[edit] Hi.

Hi. I am user:82.45.15.247.

I have edited the "bristol urban culture" post so that it is a little more consistent with reality. I know this is unpopular, but there you go. I am however happy for my own edit to be edited on grounds of not wanting to cause offence, but this wholesale roll-back of changes has to stop.

The fact is that Bristol and it's "urban culture" does have another, less salubrious side to it, and I am not happy to see this whitewashed out of existance by self-serving, self-congratulatory nonsense. Bristol Indymedia in particular seem to be fond of censoring facts and points of view they don't like while spamming themselves everywhere and covering up less pleasant episodes in their history.

In general the posted article seems to be very much something for the tourists, the slummers, and the over thirty-fives. Again, I don't mince words, so I'm happy to have my somewhat acerbic comments moderated but not censored wholesale. I have in fact made several changes to this edit, could someone actually check it out this time rather than just silence other viewpoints?
