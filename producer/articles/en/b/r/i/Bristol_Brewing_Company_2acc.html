Bristol Brewing Company

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia


Bristol Brewing Company is an American brewery founded in 1994 by Mike Bristol in Colorado Springs, Colorado.

[edit] Brief details

Mike Bristol has stated that his mission is to "make beer that brings three things to mind: quality, purity, and sanity"[1].

[edit] Beers

The brewery produces a range of beers brewed in a variety of styles. The most popular regular beers are Laughing Lab Scottish Ale, a 5.3% abv, which has received several brewing awards; Red Rocket Pale Ale, a 5.2% abv American pale ale; Beehive Honey Wheat, a 4.4% abv wheat beer; and Mass Transit Ale a 5% abv pale ale.

Winter Warlock Oatmeal Stout, a 6.5% abv sweet stout is a popular winter seasonal.

[edit] Availability

Beers are available on draught in bars and restaurants in Colorado, in jugs to take away at the brewery, and in bottles.
