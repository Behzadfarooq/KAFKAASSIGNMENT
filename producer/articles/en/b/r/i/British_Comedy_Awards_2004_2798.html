  • Date: December 22, 2004
  • Location: London Studios, London

[edit] Television and film

Winners first; nominees indented

[edit] Best Comedy Entertainment Programme

  • Ant and Dec's Saturday Night Takeaway (Granada Television, ITV1)
    • Have I Got News For You (Hat Trick Productions, BBC One)
    • I'm a Celebrity, Get Me Out of Here! (LWT Factual, ITV1)

[edit] Best Comedy Film

  • School of Rock
    • Shaun of the Dead
    • Shrek 2

[edit] Best International Comedy TV Show

  • The Simpsons (Twentieth Century Fox, BBC Two, Sky One, Channel 4)
    • Curb Your Enthusiasm (HBO, BBC Four, E4)
    • Sex and the City (HBO, Channel 4)

[edit] Best TV Comedy

  • Little Britain (BBC Two, BBC Three)
    • Nighty Night (Baby Cow Productions, BBC Three)
    • The Office Christmas Special (BBC One)

[edit] Best TV Comedy Drama

  • Doc Martin (Buffalo Productions, ITV1)
    • Jonathan Creek (BBC One)
    • Shameless (Company Pictures, Channel 4)

[edit] Best New TV Comedy

  • Nighty Night (Baby Cow Productions, BBC Three)
    • Doc Martin (Buffalo Productions, ITV1)
    • Life Begins (Granada Television, ITV1)

[edit] People's Choice Award

  • Little Britain (BBC Two) - 41% of the public vote
    • Ant and Dec's Saturday Night Takeaway (ITV) - 28%
    • Green Wing (Channel 4) - 16%
    • Friday Night with Jonathan Ross (BBC One)

[edit] People and performances

[edit] Best TV Comedy Actor

  • Matt Lucas and David Walliams - Little Britain (BBC Two)
    • Martin Clunes - Beauty, Doc Martin (ITV1)
    • Ricky Gervais - The Office Christmas Special (BBC One)

[edit] Best TV Comedy Actress

  • Caroline Quentin - Life Begins, Von Trapped (ITV1)
    • Catherine Tate - The Catherine Tate Show (BBC Two)
    • Julia Davis - Nighty Night (BBC Two)

[edit] Best TV Comedy Entertainment Personality

  • Ant McPartlin, Declan Donnelly - Ant and Dec's Saturday Night Takeaway, I'm a Celebrity, Get Me Out of Here! (ITV1)
    • Jonathan Ross - Friday Night with Jonathan Ross (BBC One)
    • Paul Merton - Have I Got News For You (BBC One), Room 101 (BBC Two)

[edit] Best TV Comedy Newcomer

  • Catherine Tate - The Catherine Tate Show (BBC Two)
    • James McAvoy - Shameless (Channel 4)
    • Julian Barrett and Noel Fielding - The Mighty Boosh (BBC Three)

[edit] Writer of the Year

  • Stephen Merchant and Ricky Gervais

[edit] Outstanding Contribution to Comedy Award

  • Matt Groening
  • Dawn French and Jennifer Saunders
