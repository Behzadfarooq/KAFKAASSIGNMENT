[edit] Television and film

Winners first; nominees indented

  • Best British TV Comedy: A Bit of a Do
  • Best ITV/Channel 4 Sitcom: A Bit of a Do
  • Best BBC Sitcom: Only Fools and Horses
  • Best US Sitcom: Cheers
  • Best New TV Comedy: Drop The Dead Donkey
  • Best Comedy Film: Shirley Valentine

[edit] People and performances

  • Best TV Comedy Actor: David Jason - A Bit of a Do
  • Best TV Comedy Actress: Jean Boht
    • Pauline Quirke - Birds of a Feather
  • Best Entertainment Performer: Rowan Atkinson
  • Best Entertainment Presenter: Clive James
  • Best TV Comedy Newcomer: Pauline Quirke - Birds of a Feather
  • Best Radio Comedy: Victor Lewis-Smith
  • Best Live Stand-up: Victoria Wood
  • Best Variety Act: Russ Abbott
  • Best Stage Comedy Newcomer: Mike Doyle
  • Best British Film Actor: Griff Rhys Jones
  • Best British Film Actress: Pauline Collins
  • Best US Film Performer: Eddie Murphy
  • Best Comedy Writer: David Nobbs
  • Lifetime Achievement Award: Ronnie Barker
  • Lifetime Achievement Award for Stage: Norman Wisdom
  • Lifetime Achievement Award for Radio: Roy Hudd
  • Lifetime Achievement Award for Film Comedy: Peter Rogers and the Carry On films team
