[edit] Service Announcements

From March 2007 the following routes will cease from London Gatwick Airport: Athens, Hassi Messaoud, Kiev, Newcastle, and Riga. In replacement new services will commence to Dresden, Newquay, and Sarajevo. All three routes will use Boeing 737 300/400/500 series aircraft.

From March 2007 a new longhaul service from London Gatwick Airport begins with a three times weekly service to Port of Spain, Trinidad using a Boeing 777-200 aircraft. [1]
