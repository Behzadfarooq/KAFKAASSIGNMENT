Brightlight Pictures

Brightlight Pictures Inc. is a Canadian film production and television production company based in Vancouver, British Columbia, Canada. The company was founded in 2001.

  • Hunter: The Reckoning (2007)
  • Slither (2006)
  • Far Cry (2006)
  • In the Name of the King (2006)
  • The Wicker Man (2006)
  • 88 Minutes (2006)
  • How to Make a Canadian Film (2005)
  • The Long Weekend (2005)
  • Marker (2005)
  • Alone in the Dark (2005)
  • White Noise (2005)
  • Johnny Tootall (2005) (TV)
  • Severed (2005)
  • Pink Ludoos (2004)
  • Going the Distance (2004)
  • Lies Like Truth (2004)
  • Cable Beach (2004) (TV)

  • Alienated (2003)
  • House of the Dead (2003)
  • Punch (2002)
