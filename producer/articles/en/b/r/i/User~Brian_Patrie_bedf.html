Brian Patrie is a 1.7m tall, male homo sapien. He was born July 29, 1965 (age 41). He currently resides in northeast Alabama USA.

He formally joined Wikipedia, after having anonymously submitted a handful of edits (typically linking articles to other articles).

He cohosts, and produces Off Track, a weekly music and banter programme, on the W0KIE Satellite Radio Network.

[edit] Quirks

He pronounces

  • vi vye (because it's not v.i.)
  • Linux lee-nooks (as Linus does)

He tends to spell

  • i in lowercase (just as me, je, moi, ich, yo)
  • various english words in international fashion (e.g. favour, realise)
