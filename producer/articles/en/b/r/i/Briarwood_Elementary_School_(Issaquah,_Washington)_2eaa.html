Briarwood Elementary School is located in the East Renton Highlands and serves grades kindergarten through 5th. Briarwood is part of the Issaquah School District and is a feeder school for Maywood Middle School and Liberty Senior High School.

With only 425 students, Briarwood enjoys a sense of community and a rich mix of special programs, including a science/technology magnet program and full day kindergarten.
