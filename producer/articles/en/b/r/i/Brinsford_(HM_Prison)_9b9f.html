HMP & YOI Brinsford is a penal establishment in the Wolverhampton area of the English West Midlands.

Brinsford holds two categories of prisoner - Juveniles (those aged between 15 and 18) and Young Offenders (those aged over 18). The establishment was opened in 1991 as a YOI and Remand Centre.
