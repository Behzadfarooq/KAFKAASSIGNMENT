Brian Deck

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Brian Deck (Born 1976 Toronto, Ontario)

Chief Technology Officer of ChannelAssist Inc. 2006 to present.
Founder and Executive Director of Digital Understanding Inc. 2000 to present.
President of Creative Image 1995 - 2000
Studied at Ontario College of Art and Design 1995-2000



Brian Deck is an American music producer and member of band Red Red Meat. He co-founded Idful Studios in 1988. In the early 2000's, Deck produced bands including Califone, Fruit Bats, The Grates, Holopaw, Iron & Wine, and Modest Mouse.
