Bridezillas

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Bridezillas is a reality television show on WE: Women's Entertainment, which debuted in 2005. It follows the lives of engaged women, casting their busy schedules in an emphatic and sometimes humorous fashion. In its second set of shows, Bridezillas became the highest rated original programming on the WE Network.[citation needed] In the summer of 2006, the third set of shows continued setting viewing records for the channel.[citation needed] A fourth set of shows has been tentatively approved.[citation needed]
