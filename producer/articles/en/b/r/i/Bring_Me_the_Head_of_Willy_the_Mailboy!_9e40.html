Bring Me the Head of Willy the Mailboy! Is the 5th Dilbert book. It features 9 months of Dilbert comic strips and an introduction by Scott Adams. Its genre is humour. It sells at the retail pice of Â£6.99 in the UK

[edit] Plot

It features around the stories of;

Dilbert, The main character and engineer and his pet Dogbert, plus minor appearances from Bob and Dawn, the dinosaurs

Most of these strips are set in Dilbert's home in this set while the later strips are mostly based out of the office and thus contain different humor because of different situations and characters. The few years of the strip used a different style also with the scenery and characters being drawn in a more boldish way and many of the minor and major characters having appearances altered.
