Bridget Regan

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Bridget Regan (b. June 6, 1975, Detroit, Michigan) is an American musician. She splits her time between homes in the United States and Ireland. She plays the fiddle, tin whistle, and uillean pipes.

Bridget started to play the violin when she was about three years old but never learned to read music notes.

She plays music for and contributes backing vocals to the seven-piece Los Angeles-based Celtic Punk band Flogging Molly.
