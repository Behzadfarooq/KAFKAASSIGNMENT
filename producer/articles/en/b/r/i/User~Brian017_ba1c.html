[edit] Education

In 1992, Brian started school at a local Primary School. Within 2 weeks, he was skipped up a year due to his astounding knowledge he showed to his teacher. During this time Brian had many friends, and was very clever for his age. He frequentely was top of the class in all subjects, even scoring a perfect 100% in a history exam. His teachers were often astonished at Brian's cheery mood he brought to the classroom and also at his enthusiasm in adversity. It was this adversity that saw him win the Student of the Year award at his Primary Schools Graduation in 1999. He recieved a piece of Kilkenny Crystal and a engraved gold medal. An honour which reduced Brian to tears, as well as the other students, parents and teachers during the standing ovation which he recieved. However, this was to proof Brian's finest hour as the coming years saw a downturn in his academic studies. The pressures of Secondary school resulted in his grades spiralling and he found it hard to make friends in his new school. Despite his poor results Brian sat his Junior Certificate exams in 2002 and his Leaving Certificate exams two years later in 2004 (Brian is the only member of the family who has done this). In October of that year, he was offered a place on the Thelogy and Arts course at NUI Maynooth. Brian intially accepted the offer but upon futher assessment of the situation, he decide he was not ready for university yet. Brian's family put pressure on him to reconsider and this caused Brian a lot of stress and he had a failed attempt at suicide. Brian is currently evaluating the situation and will attend university in the future.

[edit] Sporting Interests

Brian is a very keen sports follower. Brian likes many sports including Hurling, Gaelic Football, Australian Football, Boccia and Soccer amongst others. Brian supports many teams in different sports; in soccer he supports Manchester Utd, in his national sport hurling he cheers on Kilkenny, while in Australian Football he barracks for Collingwood. Brian also has won numerous medals for various Track and Field events. Brian also competed as goalkeeper for his team in an All-Ireland indoor soccer competition.
