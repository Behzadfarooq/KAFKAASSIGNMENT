Why is this page here? Is there any such as place as "Bridgewater England"? Should it redirect to Bridgwater? If there is such a place, it should be Bridgewater, England anyway. Angela 01:17, 30 Aug 2003 (UTC)

A gazetteer search doesn't turn anything up, but it seems to be a very common misspelling for Bridgwater. WarofDreams

I don't think Bridgwater and Bridgewater are the same place. Is there any requirement for "England" to be appended on anyway (unless there's other bridgewaters, and that page is a disambiguation) ? This page is *definitely* misnamed though. -- Steinsky 01:39, 30 Aug 2003 (UTC)
Actually, I've just looked it up in the AA GB atlas, there is no "Bridgewater". The only pages that link here are the Bridgewater disambiguation page, the Bridgwater page, and some user_talk/ page, and they all link to the redirect (Bridgewater England) page. -- Steinsky 01:44, 30 Aug 2003 (UTC)
Thanks for doing the research. Bridgewater, England and Bridgewater England now redirect to Bridgwater. I didn't want to do it without being sure and end up upsetting someone who lives in Bridgewater, England! Angela 01:56, 30 Aug 2003 (UTC)
