Britt Strandberg (born March 31, 1934). is a former Swedish cross country skier who competed in the 1960's. She won three Winter Olympic medals in the 3 x 5 km with a gold in 1960 and silvers in 1964 and 1968.

Sandberg also won 3 x 5 km medals at the FIS Nordic World Ski Championships with a silver in 1962 and a bronze in 1966.
