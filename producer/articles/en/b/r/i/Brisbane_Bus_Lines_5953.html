Brisbane Bus Lines is a bus operator, servicing places between Ferny Grove and Samford.

Brisbane Bus Lines operates a service under the TransLink banner, in which they are based out of Ferny Grove Station

It also operates railbuses for QR CityTrain in case of line maintenance or other last minute reasons, and they provide charter and school services.
