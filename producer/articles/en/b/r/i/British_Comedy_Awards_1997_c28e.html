[edit] Television, radio and film

Winners first; nominees indented

  • Best BBC Sitcom: One Foot In The Grave
  • Best ITV Sitcom: Faith in the Future
  • Best C4 Sitcom: Father Ted
  • Best New TV Comedy Series: Knowing Me, Knowing You... With Alan Partridge
  • Best TV Comedy Show: The Fast Show
  • Best TV Comedy Series: Only Fools and Horses
  • Best Comedy Film: The Full Monty
  • Best New TV Comedy: Harry Hill
  • Funniest Comedy Moment: Only Fools and Horses
  • Best International Comedy: The Larry Sanders Show
  • People's Choice Award: Only Fools and Horses
  • Best BBC Comedy Drama: The Missing Postman
  • Best ITV Comedy Drama: Cold Feet
  • Best Children's Comedy: My Dad's a Boring Nerd
  • Best Entertainment Programme: An Evening With Lily Savage
  • Best Radio Comedy: People Like Us

[edit] People and performances

  • Best BBC1 Comedy Personality: Caroline Aherne - The Mrs Merton Show
  • Best ITV Comedy Personality: Cilla Black
  • Best BBC2/Channel 4 Comedy Personality: Paul Whitehouse
  • Best TV Comedy Actor: David Jason - Only Fools and Horses
    • Nicholas Lyndhurst - Only Fools and Horses
  • Best TV Comedy Actress: Dawn French - The Vicar of Dibley
    • Caroline Quentin - Men Behaving Badly
    • Helen Baxendale - Cold Feet
  • Best Comedy Newcomer: Graham Norton
  • Best Stand-up Comic: Jack Dee
  • Writer's Guild Lifetime Achievement Award: Ray Galton and Alan Simpson
  • Lifetime Achievement Award: Stanley Baxter
