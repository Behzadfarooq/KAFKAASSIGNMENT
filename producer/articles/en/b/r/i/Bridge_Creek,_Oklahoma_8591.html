Bridge Creek is an unincorporated community in Grady County, Oklahoma, United States. As of 2005, the town is estimated to have a total population of 34.

[edit] Geography

Bridge Creek is located at 35°14′6″N, 97°44′10″W (35.235, -97.736)GR1.

The incorporated town of Bridge Creek (established 2000) is only the center of much larger, rapidly growing area colloquially known as "Bridge Creek". Bridge Creek is sometimes considered to be part of a rapidly growing area of northern McClain and Grady Counties known as the "Tri-City Area" with Newcastle, Tuttle and Blanchard.

[edit] May 3, 1999, Tornado Outbreak

Bridge Creek was one of several areas hardest hit by a tornado in the deadly May 3, 1999, tornado outbreak. At least 12 died in the Bridge Creek area.
