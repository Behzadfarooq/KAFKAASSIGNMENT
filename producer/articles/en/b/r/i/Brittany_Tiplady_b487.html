Brittany Alexandra Tiplady (born January 21, 1991 in Richmond, British Columbia) is a Canadian child actress best known for her role as Jordan Black in the television series Millennium (1996-1999).

Won a 1998 Young Artist Award for "Best Performance in a TV Drama Series - Supporting Actress."

Her brother Avery is also a child performer.
