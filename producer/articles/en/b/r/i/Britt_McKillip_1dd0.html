Britt McKillip is a Canadian child actress born in British Columbia, Canada on 18 January 1991, best known for her role as Reggie Lass in the cable series Dead Like Me.

Her father is the producer Tom McKillip and mother, songwriter Lynda McKillip. She has an elder sister named Carly McKillip, who is also an actress.
