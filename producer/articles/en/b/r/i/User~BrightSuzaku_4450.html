[edit] Text, oh, Text!

Hello. I am BrightSuzaku, and I've only just become a member here at Wikipedia!

I have long used Wikipedia as a source for imformation and research, and have found it very useful, indeed. As a result, I decided to take the next step up, and joined the Wikipedian community. Hopefully, I'll get better at this whole "make your own/edit your page" thing as time passes.

I enjoy reading, writing, playing videogames and drawing. I also like history (a lot), the sciences, and physics. I am also an avid anime/manga fan, especially shōjo (girl's) manga.


I have made no significant -or insignificant!- contributions to Wikipedia yet, but I will assuredly get there at some point. If I feel motivated to do so, I can research for hours straight. I suppose the only think I could wish for was the ability to do such research at school. Either way, so long as I'm here, I might as well have a good time while I'm at it! I'm all tense right now, but I'll get over it, eventually.
