Brittle Days - A Tribute To Nick Drake
Tribute album by Various artists
Released 1992
Label Imaginary

Brittle Days - A Tribute To Nick Drake is a 1992 compilation album released by Imaginary Records in the UK. The album features contemporary artists performing cover versions of songs by Nick Drake.

[edit] Tracklisting

1. Changelings - Riverman
2. The High Llamas - At The Chime Of A City Clock
3. Loop - Pink Moon
4. No Man - Road
5. The Walkabouts - Cello Song
6. Shelleyan Orphan - Joey
7. Scott Appel - From The Morning
8. The Times - Fruit Tree
9. Martyn Bates - Know
10. Swinging Swine - Voice From The Mountain
11. Nikki Sudden & The French Revolution - Time Has Told Me
12. Tracy Santa - Fly
13. Clive Gregson - Northern Sky
14. Scott Appel - Hazy Jane
15. R. Stevie Moore - Riverman

Catalogue number ILLCD 026
