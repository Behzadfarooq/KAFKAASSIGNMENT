British 232nd Infantry Brigade

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The 232nd Infantry Brigade was formed from the 2nd Malta Brigade. It was based in Malta and Egypt .

[edit] Commander

  • Brig. F.A.J.E. Marshall

[edit] Component Units

  • 2nd Bn, The King's Own Malta Regiment
  • 3rd Bn, The King's Own Malta Regiment
  • 2nd Bn, Royal Irish Fusiliers
  • 8th Bn, King's Own Royal Regiment
  • 8th Bn, The Manchester Regiment
