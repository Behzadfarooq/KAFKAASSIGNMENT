[edit] BrickViking

This article introduces the entity BrickViking to the rest of the wikipedia at large. Be aware that any of this information is subject to change at the entities' whim... in short, take everything you read with a healthy dose of skepticism.

I was born in 1967, in Wellington, New Zealand. I currently live in Christchurch, New Zealand with wife, no children, three computers and a cat.

I am currently interested in operating systems such as Linux, BSD, Solaris, plan 9, and many others. I'm also into the IRC community and enjoy talking to different users about their respective problems, and occasionally solving problems too. Some of my other interests include music, astronomy, amateur radio, computer games, computer programming and natural languages.
