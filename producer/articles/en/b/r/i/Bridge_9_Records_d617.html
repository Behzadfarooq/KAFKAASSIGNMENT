Bridge 9 Records is a contemporary hardcore punk record label located in Boston, Massachusetts. Bridge 9 is owned by Chris Wrenn, who began the label in 1995 and produced its first release in 1996. The label gets is named as a metaphor for what Wrenn wanted to do in creating the label, bridge all parts of the hardcore scene into one label and then put his lucky number (nine) at the end. The label currently has 5 employees, 15 artists and 41 bands.
