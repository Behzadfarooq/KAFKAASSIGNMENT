[edit] Comebacks

[edit] Biggest Comeback Win

  • Recovered from a 18-points deficit to win. Trailed Canberra 18-0 after 43 minutes to win 30-28 at Suncorp Stadium (April 29, 2006)

[edit] Worst Collapse

  • Surrended a 18-point lead. Led Newcastle 22-4 after 44 minutes to lose 26-22 at Marathon Stadium (June 4, 2000)
