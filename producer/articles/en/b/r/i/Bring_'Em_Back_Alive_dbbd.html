Bring 'Em Back Alive is an adventure television series starring Bruce Boxleitner, Cindy Morgan and Ron O'Neal. The series was shown in the United States from September 1982 to May 1983. Set in Singapore, it was one of several shows like Tales of the Gold Monkey to try and capture the success of Raiders of the Lost Ark. In reality, Frank Buck was a real big game trapper who was very famous in the 1930s. He wrote a book entitled "Bring 'em back Alive." He appeared in some movies and is remembered by serial fans as the star of the cliffhanger Jungle Menace.

Original Cast:

1) Bruce Boxleitner as Frank Buck, Big Game Trapper and Collector of Wild Animals
2) Cindy Morgan as Gloria Marlowe, United States Consul, Singapore
3) Clyde Kusatsu as Ali, Buck's Friend and No. 1 Boy
4) Ron O'Neal as H.H., His Royal Highness, the Sultan of Jahore
5) Sean McClory as Myles Delaney, Manager of the Raffles Hotel
6) John Zee as G.B. Von Turgo, Smuggler and Kingpin of the Singapore Underworld
7) Harvey Jason as Bhundi
