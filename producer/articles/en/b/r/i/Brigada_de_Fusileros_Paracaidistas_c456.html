Brigada de Fusileros Paracaidistas

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Official seal of the BFP



The Brigada de Fusileros Paracaidistas (Infantry Parachutists Brigade, BFP) is a paratrooper unit belonging to the Mexican Air Force and created in 1969. Their headquarters were established in Mexico City. In 1999 the Centro de Adiestramiento de Paracaidismo (Airborne Training Center) was created. A battalion can be deployed rapidly to any part of the country.

Structure:

Headquarters

3 Infantry Parachutists Battalions

1 Special Forces Airmobile Group

1 Logistics Company
