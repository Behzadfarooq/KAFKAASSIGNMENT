  • Britney Spears has the record for the most retired videos on the TRL countdown with 13 retired videos, plus one honorary video. "I'm A Slave 4 U" could not retire because "I'm Not A Girl, Not Yet A Woman" had already debuted on the TRL countdown while "Slave" had not yet acquired the requred number of days to retire. However, it was retired in 2005 when it was selected from six near-retired videos to be retired, giving her the HR award.
  • Britney had a winning streak with all of her videos retiring consecutivley on the countdown until I'm A Slave 4 U in 2001. This is with the exception of Born To Make You Happy and I Love Rock 'N Roll which they werent released in the US, so the videos werent counted there.
