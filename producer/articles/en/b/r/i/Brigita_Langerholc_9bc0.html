Brigita Langerholc

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Brigita Langerholc (born July 23, 1976) is a Slovenian middle distance runner who specializes in the 800 metres.

She finished fourth at the 2000 Olympics in a personal best time of 1:58.51 minutes, which still stands. In 2001 she won the silver medal at the Mediterranean Games and a gold medal at the Universiade. She then finished fifth at the 2006 European Athletics Championships in Gothenburg and sixth at the 2007 European Indoor Championships.

She has competed at the World Championships in 1999, 2001, 2003 and 2005 without ever reaching the final.
