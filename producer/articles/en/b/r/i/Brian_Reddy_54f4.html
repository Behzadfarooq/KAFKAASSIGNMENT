Brian Reddy is an American actor who has had minor roles in a number of hit films and nationally syndicated television programs.

[edit] Filmography

  • Farm Sluts (2003) .... Larry's Father
  • Lost Souls (2000) .... Father Frank Page
  • Ali: An American Hero (2000) (TV)
  • O Brother, Where Art Thou? (2000) .... Pappy's Staff
  • Traces of Insanity (1998) (TV)
  • Ivory Tower (1998) .... Bob Martell
  • Dante's Peak (1997) .... Les Worrell
  • Runaway Car (1997) (TV) .... Carsten
  • The Bachelor's Baby (1996) (TV)
  • Primal Fear (1996) .... Insp. Woodside
  • The Birdcage (1996) .... TV Editor
  • Casino (1995) .... Board Investigators
  • Outbreak (1995) .... Tracy's Father
  • Clean Slate1994) .... Young Man
  • Cosmic Slop (1994) (TV) .... V.P. (segment "Space Traders")
  • Hart to Hart: Hart to Hart Returns (1993) (TV) .... Eric Hayden
  • In the Shadows, Someone's Watching (1993) (TV) .... Dr. Fenton Silver
  • Ghostwriter (1993) (tv series) mr miller
  • Prophet of Evil: The Ervil LeBaron Story (1993) (TV) .... Michael Leary
  • What About Bob? (1991) .... Carswell Fensterwald, M.D.
  • Say Yes (1986) .... Thief

Reddy has also held minor roles in such varying and notable TV productions as Law & Order: Special Victims Unit, Enterprise, Saved By the Bell: The New Class, The X Files and Buffy the Vampire Slayer. He played Dan (the high-talker) on Seinfeld.
