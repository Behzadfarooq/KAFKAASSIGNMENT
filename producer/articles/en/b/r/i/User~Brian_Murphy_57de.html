User:Brian Murphy

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Brian Murphy is a political science major at the Ohio State University. He is an expert on the history of Ohio State football and an avid fan of the Cleveland Indians. He is a Democrat and has come under fire for some of his opinions, for example, his opposition to capital punishment.
