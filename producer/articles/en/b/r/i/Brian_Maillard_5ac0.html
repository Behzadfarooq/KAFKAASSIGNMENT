Brian Maillard was the voice of K in the anime Gravitation. He also was the voice of a soldier in the Xbox game Fable.

Additionally, he was one of the "Applebee's Guys" in the series of 2006 Applebee's commercials featuring a musical duo singing about food. He played bass.
