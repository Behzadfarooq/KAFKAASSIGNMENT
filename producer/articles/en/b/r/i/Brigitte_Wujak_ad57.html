Brigitte Wujak (born March 6, 1955 in Karl-Marx-Stadt) is a retired long jumper who represented East Germany. She won the silver medal at the 1980 Summer Olympics in Moscow with a personal best jump of 7.04 metres. She retired after the 1984 season.
