Bribie Island Coaches is a bus operator, based at Bribie Island north of Brisbane. It provides services between Caboolture and Bribie Island.

Bribie Island Coaches operates a small number of services under the TransLink banner, in which many services service Bribie Island and the surrounding suburbs. They also operate a Link service which connects to train services at Caboolture railway station.

They also provide charter & school services in the region.

[edit] TransLink services

  • 640 - Bribie Island - Caboolture Station : Link via Woorim, Sandstone Point and Ningi (Every day) (BIC)
  • 641 - Banksia Beach - Bribie Centre : Local via Bellara (Every day) (BIC)
  • 642 - Woorim - Sandstone Point : Local via Bribie Centre (Every day) (BIC)
