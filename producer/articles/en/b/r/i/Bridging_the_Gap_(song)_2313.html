"Bridging the Gap", the secong single off of Nas' Street's Disciple features his father. It samples music from Muddy Waters and Howling Wolf's "Mannish Boy" composition. Olu Dara provides the hook of the song by talking about his path and how Nas was born. Nas and Olu Dara performed the song many times before the release of Street's Disciple. It was the only buzz the album had as its release came near. The song is referenced in the title track of The Game's song: "The Documentary".

[edit] Music video

The video shows Nas and Olu Dara on a stage with women dancing in the background, it also shows a few shots that are supposed to be Nas in school. The video had high rotation on MTV and BET.
