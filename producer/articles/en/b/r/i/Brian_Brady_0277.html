Brian Brady

Brian Brady (died 10th September 1949) was an Irish politician, born to a prominent pro-republic family in Killybegs, County Donegal. He represented Donegal in Dáil Éireann as a member of Fianna Fáil from 1932 until his death in 1949. His son, Hugh Brady, was also a politician.

He is buried in Killybegs.
