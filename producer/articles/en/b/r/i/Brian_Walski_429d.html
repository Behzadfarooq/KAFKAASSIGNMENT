Brian Walski

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

You have new messages (last change).

Brian Walski is a professional photographer who was accused in 2003 of altering some news photographs. Until the incident, he was a staff photographer at the Los Angeles Times. Previously, he had won the California Press Photographers Association's 2001 Photographer of the Year.
