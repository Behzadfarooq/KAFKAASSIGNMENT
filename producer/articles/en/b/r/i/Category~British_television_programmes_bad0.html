This category requires continual maintenance to avoid becoming too large. It should list very few—if any—article pages directly, and contain primarily sub-categories.
Please diffuse articles into relevant subcategories as needed.

This category includes TV programmes made and shown in the United Kingdom: it does not include programmes made outside the UK and imported.

Wherever possible, articles should not be placed directly into this category, but rather into the subcategories.

Stub articles in these categories should be tagged with {{UK-tv-stub}}, not {{Tv-stub}} or {{stub}}.
