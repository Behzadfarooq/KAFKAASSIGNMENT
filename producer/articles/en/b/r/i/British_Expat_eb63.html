British Expat is an Internet-based portal for British expatriates worldwide and for would-be British emigrants. It offers news, information and advice via articles and its various discussion forums. It also contains sections on property, expatriate employment, tax, insurance and other financial services, and a number of lifestyle columns including a child psychologist Q&A. There are also travel features, restaurant reviews and various price comparison searches and online booking facilities. Other facilities provided include web-based email and an opt-in email newsletter.

The website BritishExpat.com was formally launched on 1 April 2000, although the company British Expat Ltd was not formed until 2001[1]. It has remained a privately owned company registered in the United Kingdom.
