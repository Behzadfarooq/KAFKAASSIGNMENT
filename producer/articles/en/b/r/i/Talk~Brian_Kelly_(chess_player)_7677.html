[edit] Rating

http://www.icu.ie/ratings/list.php?filter=Kelly&list=200609&club=&sort=rating is one link that shows Brian Kelly's rating. He currently occupies Board 2 for Ireland. This is behind the originally Russian, but now Irish, Alexander Baburin, Grandmaster. The references I provided to seem to me to allow any user to find out that he is the strongest player in Ireland at the minute. (Barring Alexander Baburin) and the article by Tim Harding gives an historical overview. Don't forget that only Mark Orr, Mark Heidenfeld and Mark Quinn have ever obtained the IM title, Brian has a higher rating than all of them.
