Brix (computer game)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Brix is a puzzle game for the IBM PC. It was developed and produced by Epic MegaGames in 1992.

It is a clone of Taito's Puzznic, and thus bears strong graphical and some gameplay similarities to Flipull/Plotting, also produced by Taito.
