Briercrest College is a Christian, private college located in Caronport, Saskatchewan, Canada, which offers courses in theology, arts, and Christian ministry. There are around 650 students, 500 of whom are in residence. It is a part of Briercrest Family of Schools - a Christian educational organization consisting of three schools: Briercrest College, Briercrest Seminary, and Caronport High School. It also operates a distance learning program.

The college has been accredited by The Association for Biblical Higher Education since 1976.[1]

From 1995 to 2006, the college operated its own radio station, CJOS-FM. The station is now defunct.
