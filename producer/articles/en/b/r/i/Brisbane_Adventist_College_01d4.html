Brisbane Adventist College is a Christian independent school on the border of the suburbs of Mansfield and Wishart in Brisbane, Australia.

The school is from Prep - 12 and is run by the Seventh-day Adventist Church.

Brisbane Adventist College recently celebrated its 40th anniversary.
