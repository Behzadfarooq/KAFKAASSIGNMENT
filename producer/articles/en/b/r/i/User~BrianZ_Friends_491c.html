This is a page for friendly people I've run into on Wikipedia:

  1. Monkeyman - The original Wikipedian to offer me a plausible solution to External Links inclusion in certain articles. I'll always remember that.
  2. GraemeL - The first admin I ran into on Wikipedia. very helpful, but not until he realized I wasn't a spammer. He's the reason I originally created an account. In doing so, I was treated less like an anonymous troll. :)
  3. Xyrael - Although he turned me down for VandalProof due to the age of my account or lack thereof, as of June 2006, he's a good guy and offered to be a mediator in my first edit war with Tommac2, See My Enemies. Even though we didn't actually need his help, offering it was cool. I awarded him a WikiThanks for it.
  4. Bob the Cannibal - He's currently a Cavalry Scout in the U.S. Army. I was a Scout in the U.S. Army from 1991 to 1994. Any Scout is a friend of mine.
