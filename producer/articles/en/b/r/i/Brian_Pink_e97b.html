Brian Pink

Brian Pink is the Australian Statistician, the head of the Australian Bureau of Statistics, beginning March 2007.[1]

Prior to September 1999 Brian Pink was ABS's Statistical Support Group Manager, when he was appointed as the New Zealand Government Statistician and chief executive of Statistics New Zealand.[2]
