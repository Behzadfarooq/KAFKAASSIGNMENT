Brian Koppelman

Brian Koppelman is an U.S. screenwriter, producer, director, and actor. His father is Charles Koppelman, the chairman of Martha Stewart Living Omnimedia, and his sister is radio personality Jennifer Hutt.

[edit] Works

  • Rounders (1998)
  • Knockaround Guys (2001)
  • Runaway Jury (2003)
  • Walking Tall (2004) (screenplay)
  • Tilt (2005)
  • The Untouchables: Capone Rising (2006)
  • Ocean's Thirteen (2007)
