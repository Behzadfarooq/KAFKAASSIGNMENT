Created by Robert J Williamson in 1994, the British Shakespeare Company have become the UK's leading open air Shakespeare Company. The 2007 shows are Henry V and as You Like it and will tour Norway, Windsor, Leeds and Oxford. With a cast of sixteen professional actors and including original music, beautiful locations, stunning costumes, these shows are simply perfect for a summers evening's entertainment.

They have performed over 700 shows with star names from film, television and stage. However it is the quality of the support cast which makes British Shakespeare Company's performances unmissable. [1]
