Brigitte Habersatter-Totschnig (born August 30, 1954 in Filzmoos, Salzburg) is a former Austrian alpine skier.


[edit] Achievements

1976 Winter Olympics/World championships in Innsbruck:

  • Silver at alpine skiing Downhill

Alpine skiing World Championship 1978 in Garmisch-Partenkirchen:

  • seventh place at Downhill

7 World Cup race victories at Downhill

1 World Cup race victory at Giant Slalom

Two time Downhill World Cup winner 1975/76 and 1976/77
