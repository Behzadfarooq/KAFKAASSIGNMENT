"Bringing Home the Beacon" is an episode from the fourth season of the American television series Farscape, written by Carleton Eastlake and directed by Rowan Woods.

[edit] Synopsis

Spoiler warning: Plot and/or ending details follow.

The women of Moya arrive on a market attached to a dead leviathan in order to purchase a cloaking device for Moya - one which mimics the energy signatures of other vessels, fooling sensors. While waiting for the device to be completed, they run into trouble--Braca, Grayza, and other Peackeepers are on base for a secret meeting with Scarran War Minister Akhna to sign a non-aggression treaty. Braca has told his men to keep an eye out for a Nebari, so Chiana, along with Noranti, get temporary genetic modifications to make them look like a different species. Aeryn and Sikozu, meanwhile, follow Grayza to see what she and Akhna are doing. When Aeryn hears Grayza "give" the Luxan empire to the Scarrans as part of the treaty, she resolves to kill Grayza to prevent the treaty from being signed. Unfortunately, she doesn't get a clear shot, and the treaty is signed. Immediately afterward Akhna goes back on her word and takes Grayza prisoner. Aeryn and Sikozu join together to stop the Scarrans and free Grayza. Aeryn stays behind while telling everyone else to go back to their shuttle--if she hasn't returned in a half-arn, they are to leave without her. She just makes it, and they return to Moya. Unfortunately, upon arrival, they discover there is a location beacon transmitting their location to a Scarran ship chasing them. Aeryn suggests that she and John leave Moya in a shuttle, since they're really the ones the Scarrans would want, anyway. John asks Aeryn "But what about the baby?" Aeryn aeems confused and doesn't appear to know what John is talking about. John eventually realizes it's not Aeryn, and shoots her in the face, whereupon a horrified John sees that it's not Aeryn, but a bioloid replica--Aeryn has been taken by the Scarrans to points unknown, for torture and interrogation, and they have no way of knowing where she is.

Spoilers end here.
