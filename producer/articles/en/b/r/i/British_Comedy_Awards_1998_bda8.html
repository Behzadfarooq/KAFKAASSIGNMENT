  • Host: Jonathan Ross

[edit] Television, radio and film

Winners first; nominees indented

  • Best TV Sitcom: I'm Alan Partridge
  • Best TV Comedy Series: Goodness Gracious Me
  • Best TV Comedy Drama: Underworld
  • Best New TV Comedy: The Royle Family
  • Best Light Entertainment Series: Who Wants to Be a Millionaire?
  • Best Children's Comedy: Sooty and Co
  • Best Comedy Film: Lock, Stock and Two Smoking Barrels
  • People's Choice Award: One Foot In The Grave
  • Best Radio Comedy: Old Harry's Game

[edit] People and performances

  • Best TV Comedy Actor: Steve Coogan I'm Alan Partridge
  • Best TV Comedy Actress: Emma Chambers The Vicar of Dibley
    • Dawn French - The Vicar of Dibley
  • Best TV Comedy Newcomer: Dylan Moran How Do You Want Me?
  • Best BBC1 Comedy Personality: Harry Enfield
  • Best BBC2/Channel 4/Channel 5 Comedy Personality: Steve Coogan
  • Best ITV Comedy Personality: Michael Barrymore
  • Best Stand-up Comedian: Tommy Tiernan
  • Best Writers: Denis Norden and Frank Muir
  • Lifetime Achievement Award: Thora Hird
