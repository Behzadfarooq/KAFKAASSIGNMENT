Brian Wiseman (born July 13, 1971 in Chatham, Ontario, Canada) was a Canadian ice hockey forward and college assistant coach.

Wiseman started his NHL career with the Toronto Maple Leafs in 1996. After he was done playing professional hockey he was an assistant coach for Princeton University in 2003-2004.

He has also played for the St. John's Maple Leafs (AHL), Chicago Wolves (IHL), and Houston Aeros (IHL). He helped the Houston Aeros win a Turner Cup in the 1998-1999 season.
