"Bring the Pain" was the first single from rap artist Method Man's debut album Tical. Industrial music band Mindless Self Indulgence covered it in their single "Bring the Pain/Tornado".

Comedian Chris Rock named his 1996 tour and television special "Bring the Pain" after this song. Method Man is thanked in the special's closing credits.
