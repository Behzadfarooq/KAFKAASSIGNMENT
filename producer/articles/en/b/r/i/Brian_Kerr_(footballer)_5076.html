Brian Kerr (born 12 October 1981, in Bellshill) is a Scottish footballer, having won 3 caps for the national side. He currently plays for SPL side Motherwell.

Kerr joined Motherwell from Newcastle United in the summer of 2004. However, having been with the club for less than a month, his career took a bad set back when he suffered cruciate ligament damage in a pre-season friendly with St Mirren. This left him out of football for 8 months.

Since recovering from his injury, Kerr has re-established his place in Motherwell's first-team squad, and scored his first goal for the club, as well as the club's first goal of season 2005-06 with a spectacular strike in a 4-4 draw with Celtic.
