BRI is a three-letter abbreviation with multiple meanings, as described below:

  • Banque des Règlements Internationaux, = BIS in English as 'Bank for International Settlements'
  • The IATA airport code for Bari Airport in Italy
  • Basic Rate Interface, an ISDN configuration
  • Bathroom Reader's Institute
  • Behavioral Risk improvement, developed by MARSH,USA. [1]
  • BioSciences Research Institute
  • Brewers Retail Inc., operator of The Beer Store chain in Ontario
  • Brigade de recherche et d'intervention, a French police unit
  • The National Rail code for Bristol Temple Meads railway station in the UK
  • Bank Rakyat Indonesia
  • Bristol Royal Infirmary
  • The Biblical Research Institute of the Seventh-day Adventist Church
  • The Association of American Railroads reporting mark of the fallen flag Burlington-Rock Island Railroad
