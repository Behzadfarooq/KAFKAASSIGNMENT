Brian Lee Cardinal (born May 2, 1977 in Tolono, Illinois) is an American professional basketball player who currently plays for the Memphis Grizzlies of the NBA.

At Purdue University, he led the Boilermakers to an Elite Eight in 2000 his senior season. He was also a member of Theta Chi Fraternity. Cardinal was selected in the second round by the Detroit Pistons in the 2000 NBA Draft. He has also played for the Washington Wizards and Golden State Warriors. Currently, "the janitor", plays for the Memphis Grizzlies. He has averaged 8.1 points per game throughout his career.
