The Bristled Grassbird, Chaetornis striatus is a Old World warbler belonging to the genus Chaetornis. Chaetornis striata is endemic to the Indian subcontinent, where it is patchily and locally distributed in India, Nepal, and Pakistan. Formerly described as common in at least Gujarat, Andhra Pradesh and West Bengal (India) and parts of Bangladesh, it has declined.
