United Kingdom general election results for Sheffield Brightside constituency, 1885–2005. © Jeremy Atherton 2005.

[edit] Notes

The Background colour indicates the party of the sitting MP at any given year. The striped area between 1897 and 1900 represents Frederick Maddison who stood as liberal-labour.

Graph drawn with IGOR Pro v.5.04

Parties:

  • Conservative = Conservative Party (UK).
  • Labour = Labour Party (UK).
  • Liberal = Liberal Party (UK) (1885–1979); SDP-Liberal Alliance (1983–1987); Liberal Democrats (UK) (1992–present).
