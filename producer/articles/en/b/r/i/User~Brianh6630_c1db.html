[edit] Who am I?

I am a proud card carrying member of the human race.

I am a Texan by birth, but a Californian by force.

My genetic structure strongly resembles others of my species, although I foolishly aspire to be more than my genes.

I consume red beef and carbonated beverages on a regular basis. (This should cover the infamous "You are what you eat." angle.)

[edit] What do I do?

I explore my enviroment for signs of intelligent life. As soon as I find it I will submit my discovery to a Wikipedia discussion board, that will create a featured article about it.

Update I have found a promising lead at a local 7-11. As soon as I am able gather more information on the subject I will update this page. --Brian 05:47, 28 September 2005 (UTC)
