Bright Lights, Big City (Fat Pat album)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

You have new messages (last change).

Bright Lights, Big City is Fat Pat's first album with unheard verses. Guest include Big Hawk, Trae, Paul Wall, Bun B, Z-Ro, and more.

[edit] Confirmed Tracks

  • "Top's Drop (Remix)" (Featuring Bun B, Paul Wall, & Big Hawk)
  • "Where Have You Been" (Featuring Z-Ro)
  • "Too Much Lean In My Cup" (Featuring Big Hawk)
  • "Many Niggaz Pass Me By" (Featuring Paul Wall & Big Hawk)
  • "Lift Up That Trunk" (Featuring Trae, Willie D, & Scarface)
