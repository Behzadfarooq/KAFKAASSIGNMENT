Brina M. Palencia (Born February 13, 1984 in Oklahoma) is a Texas-based voice actress who primarily works on the properties of Funimation. Brina is a graduate of the University of North Texas. Her most well known voice role is that of Eve, on Black Cat, in addition to Eve she has had roles on Fullmetal Alchemist, Negima!, Trinity Blood and BECK: Mongolian Chop Squad.

Palencia's voice range gets her cast in a variety of roles, apart from being able to do normal female voices she is skilled in doing younger females and younger males as well.
