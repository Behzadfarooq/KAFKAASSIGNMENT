Bristol Manor Farm F.C. are an English football team who play in the Western Football League. They play their home games at The Creek, Bristol.

They were formed in 1960 and played in the Somerset Senior League. Playing in the Western League Division One from 1977, the team achieved promotion to the Premier Division after winning the division one title in the 1982/83 season. They were relegated in 2001 before regaining Premier Division status in 2004.

The reserve team has played in the Bristol & Surburban League since gaining promotion to the Premier Division 2 in the 2004/05 season. The youth team, who were formed in 2003, achieved promotion in their inaugural season to the Somerset Floodlight Youth League Premier Division and were beaten Somerset Cup finalists in 2005. The youth team won the Somserset Cup the following year [2006] beating Mangotsfield United in the Final.

[edit] Club details

  • First team manager: Steve Fey
  • Assistant manager: Nigel Gillard
  • Biggest victory: 6-0 versus Bitton (2006)
