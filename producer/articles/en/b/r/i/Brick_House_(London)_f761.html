The Brick House in West London, England was completed in May 2005 and was designed by the architect Caruso St John and constructed by Harris Calnan Construction with service engineering by Mendick Waring and structural engineering by Price & Myers. It was nominated for the 2006 Stirling Prize

The project inserted a new house, accessed through an archway into the end of a Victorian city-centre street.

[edit] Site and Brief

The site was back-land development in the shape of a horses head.
