Britannia may refer to:

  • Britannia, a goddess and the national personification of the United Kingdom.
  • Roman Britain, the province of the Roman Empire called Britannia.
  • Britannia Building Society, a British mutual institution.
  • Britannia coin, a British bullion coin issued since 1987.
  • Britannia Industries, a biscuitmaking company in India.
  • Britannia metal, an alloy of pewter.
  • Britannia Secondary School, a school in Vancouver, Canada.
  • Britannia silver, an alloy of silver.
  • Britannia Stadium, the home of the English association football team Stoke City F.C.

In geography:

  • Britannia (Ottawa), a neighbourhood in Ottawa, Canada.
  • Britannia Beach, British Columbia, a small town near Vancouver, Canada.

In games:

  • Britannia (Ultima), a land in the Ultima game series.
    • Britannia (Ultima Online), the setting of the game Ultima Online.
  • Britannia (board game), a historical board game.

In transportation:

  • Britannia Airways, a UK based charter airline.
  • HMS Britannia, several Royal Navy warships.
  • HMY Britannia, a British Royal Yacht.
  • RMS Britannia, an ocean liner of the British Cunard Steamship Lines.
  • BR standard class 7, the British Railways "Britannia class" steam locomotives.
    • 70000 Britannia, the first of the class.
  • Bristol Britannia, an airliner built by the Bristol Aeroplane Company in 1952.

In publications:

  • Britannia (magazine), a publication for expatriate Britons.
  • Britannia (newspaper), a newspaper of the suffragettes (WSPU) renamed during World War I.

Britannia can also refer to:

  • Headley Britannia, an eventing mare born in 1993.
  • Pugnaces Britanniae, an extinct breed of fighting dog.
