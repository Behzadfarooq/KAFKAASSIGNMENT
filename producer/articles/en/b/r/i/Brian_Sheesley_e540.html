Brian Sheesley

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

You have new messages (last change).

Brian Sheesley is an animation director who has worked on several comedy shows, including directing duties on The Grim Adventures of Billy and Mandy, two episodes of The Critic, nine episodes of Futurama and two episodes of King of the Hill.[1][2] Sheesley also worked as an animation timer on Rugrats Go Wild! (in 2003) and as an animator on A Wish for Wings That Work in 1991. Brian is currently an animation director and supervising director on Camp Lazlo.
