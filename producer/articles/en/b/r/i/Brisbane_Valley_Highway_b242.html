Brisbane Valley Highway
Length 91 kilometres
General direction: North-South
From: 8 km east of Moore, Queensland
To: Ipswich, Queensland
Towns along highway: Esk, Toogoolawah

The Brisbane Valley Highway is a state highway in Queensland. It leaves the Warrego Highway near Ipswich and travels in a northwesterly direction, following the Brisbane River until it reaches the D'Aguilar Highway, where it ends. Route 17 continues with the D'Aguilar Highway and eventually, the Burnett Highway. State Route 85 joins the highway from its beginning off the D'Aguilar Highway, and leaves the highway turning west at Esk.

The highway crosses the Wivenhoe Dam.

[edit] List of towns along the Brisbane Valley Highway

  • Ipswich
  • Fernvale
  • Esk
  • Toogoolawah
