Bring 'Em In is the name of the first album from the Swedish band Mando Diao, released 2002 in Sweden.

In 2003 the album was released as both a CD and a LP internationally, and was well-received by critics.

Their debut single was "Sheepdog".

In the year 2005 Mando Diao followed Bring 'Em In with their second album, titled Hurricane Bar.
