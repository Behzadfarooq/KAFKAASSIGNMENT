The Bribri are a small indigenous tribe, around 10,000 members, living in the Talamanca canton inside of the Lim칩n Province in Costa Rica. They speak the Bribri language.

El Puente [1] (which means The Bridge in English) is a non-profit organization working with the Bribri people, offering educational assistance, food, and micro-loans. Their goal is to help families and individuals become self-sufficient. Each program by itself is very powerful. When used together, they provide broad support for a family working together toward self-sufficiency. El Puente also offers educational working volunteer opportunities.
