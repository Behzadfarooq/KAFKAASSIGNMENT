Brian Wellman

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Brian Wellman (born September 8, 1967) is a former triple jumper from Bermuda.

He became indoor world champion in 1995, setting a new championship record (CR) of 17.72. His personal best outdoors was 17.62 metres. Both these marks are Bermudan records. In addition he has a wind-assisted (+7.1 m/s) mark of 17.75 metres. [1]

Brian competed collegiately for the University of Arkansas where he won two NCAA Outdoor triple jump titles.
