  • Nightscream, I understand your issue. As part of Wikiproject New Jersey, I have personally worked on creation -- or extensive updating -- of pages for every single one of the 120 members of the New Jersey Senate and the New Jersey General Assembly, including Stack's page. Most of New Jersey's state legislators are primarily serving in that role. For these members it is obviously relevant for those reading an article to see who else represents the same district and have an opportunity to link to the pages for the other two members representing the same district.
