British Columbia Christian Academy is a small private Christian school in Port Coquitlam, British Columbia. BCCA houses students from K-12 with an additional preschool section, located within a dedicated portable. All classes and grade were originally housed within the VERY long hallway, but due to increasing enrollment within the French school, BCCA was pushed off several former areas. The primary grades (K-3) and head office continue to be located in the hallway connected to the French school, even though it's been cut shorter now. Intermediate grades (4-7) are placed in a separate annex, which also houses the music/chapel room. The high school (8-12) are now located in a series of portables, formerly of the "Talmud Talons High School (source: wooden sign in hockey court, since removed), the main one housing math, English, science, French, sports, student services/cafe and computer lab, while a small nearby portable houses the social studies and CAPP(Career and Personal Planning)/CP (Christian Perspectives).
