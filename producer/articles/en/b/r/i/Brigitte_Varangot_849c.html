Brigitte Varangot

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Brigitte Varangot (born May 1, 1940) is a French golf champion.

Principal victories:

  • 1957 : British Girls Amateur Golf Championship
  • 1959, 1960, 1961: French International Junior Girls Golf Championship (Troph√©e Esmond)
  • 1963, 1965, 1968: British Ladies Amateur Golf Championship
  • 1961, 1962, 1964, 1965, 1966, 1973: French International Ladies Golf Championship
  • 1959, 1961, 1963, 1970: French Ladies National Championship
  • 1970: Italian International Ladies Golf Championship

In addition, she was part of the French team that won the European Championship in 1959, 1961, 1969 and a member of the French team that won the first Espirito Santo Trophy in 1964.
