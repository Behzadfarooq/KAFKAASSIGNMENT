Brian Thomas Krause (born February 1, 1969) is an American actor. He is best known for his role as Leo Wyatt on the WB Network TV series Charmed from 1998 to 2006.

Krause was born in El Toro, California and made his debut as Richard Lestrange in the romance adventure sequel Return to the Blue Lagoon in 1991. A year later, he also starred opposite Alice Krige, playing Charles Brady in the horror movie Sleepwalkers.

Brian and his wife Beth had their first child, Jamen, in 1996, but later divorced in 2000.
