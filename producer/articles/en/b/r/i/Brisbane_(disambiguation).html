Brisbane may refer to the following places:

  • Brisbane Ranges National Park, Victoria

In Sport:

  • Brisbane Broncos
  • Brisbane Lions
  • Brisbane Bullets

In the United States:

Brisbane may also refer to the following people:

  • James Brisbane (1774-1829), British naval officer
  • Albert Brisbane (1809-1890), American author and theorist
  • Arthur Brisbane (1864-1936), American newspaper editor
