Bridlewood Community Elementary School is a Canadian elementary and middle school located on 63 Bluegrass Drive, in Kanata, Ontario. It is situated in the Zone 2 school board district [1], with Cathy Curry as its trustee, in the Kanata neighbourhood of Bridlewood. It has many classes, ranging from junior kindergarten to grade 8. There are also two core French classes, a computer lab, and a library with a collection of over 15,000 books. There are also six intermediate classes, including a gifted grade 7/8 class. Bridlewood Community Elementary School also supports a Special Support Unit, an ESL class, and a Dual Diagnosis class. The school, operated by the Ottawa-Carleton District School Board, currently has a student attendance of 424 (2006-2007) students, and 50 staff members (including non-teaching staff).

Division of the students are as follows:

  • Grades 7-8: 181 students
  • Grades 4-6: 102 students
  • Grades 1-3: 142 students

The current principal of Bridlewood C.E.S is Mrs. Pauline McKenna-Upshaw, and its acting vice-principal is Mr. Jeff Gervais.

[edit] Band

The school is notable for its excellent band. The band is led by grade 7/8 music teacher, Mr. Williamson. Since 1994, the Senior band (consisting mostly of grade 8 students, with the exeption of a few talented grade 7 students) has made an annual trip to Toronto. The focus of this three day trip has included competitive performances at Canada's Wonderland and Ontario Place, and going to tourist attractions such as Niagara Falls, Marineland, Ontario, and Clifton Hill.

Band instruments available in the Bridlewood Band are as follows:

  • Clarinet/Bass Clarinet
  • Flute
  • Trombone (no Bass Trombones)
  • Bass Guitar (limited amplifiers)
  • Percussion (limited)
  • Saxophones (Alto, Tenor, Baritone)
  • Trumpet
  • Mallet Percussion
  • Tuba
  • Baritone

[edit] Sports

The school does well in local sports tournaments, especially basketball ones. For example, last year, the school won two gold medals in local tournaments and one silver in the famous Glashan Tournament.

Other teams at the school are:

  • Soccer team
  • Volleyball team
  • Badminton team
  • Football team
  • Bordenball team
  • Track and Field team
  • Basketball team
