Brian Newth was a New Zealand athlete. his specialty was the Modern Pentathlon. Newth was one of the eight members of the New Zealand contingent who particiapted at the Games of the XXII Olympiad (Moscow) in 1980.

Newth carried the flag of the New Zealand Olympic and Commonwealth Games Association at the Opening Ceremony of the Games.

In the Modern Pentathlon competition, Newth scored the following:

23rd in the Equestrian (1018 points), 42nd in the Fencing (532 points), 38th in the Shooting (846 points), 39th in the Swimming (1048 points), and 28th in the Cross-country running (1042 points). Newth totalled 4486 points, placing him 40th overall.
