[edit] Television, radio and film

Winners first; nominees indented


[edit] Best TV Sitcom

  • The Royle Family
    • Gimme Gimme Gimme
    • Spaced

[edit] Best New TV Comedy

  • dinnerladies
    • The League of Gentlemen
    • People Like Us

[edit] Best TV Comedy Drama

  • Cold Feet
    • Jonathan Creek (Christmas Special)
    • Ted and Ralph

[edit] Best TV 'Broken Comedy'

  • Big Train
    • The League of Gentlemen
    • Smack the Pony

[edit] Best Comedy Game Show

  • Have I Got News For You
    • Families at War
    • Never Mind the Buzzcocks

[edit] Best Comedy Talk Show

  • So Graham Norton
    • Room 101
    • Ruby's American Pie

[edit] Best Comedy Entertainment Programme

  • Comic Relief
    • Stars in Their Eyes
    • Two Ronnies Night

[edit] Best International Comedy TV Show

  • The Larry Sanders Show

[edit] Best Comedy Film

  • Notting Hill

[edit] Best Radio Comedy

  • The Sunday Format


[edit] People and performances

[edit] Best TV Comedy Actor

  • Ricky Tomlinson - The Royle Family
    • James Nesbitt - Cold Feet
    • Paul Whitehouse - Ted and Ralph

[edit] Best TV Comedy Actress

  • Caroline Aherne - The Royle Family, Mrs Merton and Malcolm
    • Fay Ripley - Cold Feet
    • Kathy Burke - Gimme Gimme Gimme

[edit] Best Comedy Entertainment Personality

  • Paul Merton - Have I Got News For You, Room 101
    • Graham Norton - So Graham Norton
    • Michael Barrymore - Strike It Lucky, My Kind of Music

[edit] Best Live Stand-up

  • Bill Bailey

[edit] Best Male Comedy Newcomer

  • Sacha Baron Cohen - The 11 O'Clock Show
    • Craig Cash - The Royle Family
    • Simon Pegg - Spaced, Big Train

[edit] Best Female Comedy Newcomer

  • Jessica Stevenson - Spaced, The Royle Family
    • Daisy Donovan - The 11 O'Clock Show
    • Sally Phillips - Smack the Pony

[edit] Best Writer

  • Richard Curtis

[edit] Lifetime Achievement Award

  • Barry Humphries, Ronnie Barker, Ronnie Corbett
