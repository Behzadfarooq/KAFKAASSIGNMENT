Brigid Brannagh (born August 3, 1972) is an American actress of Irish ancestry. She's been credited as Brigid Brannagh, Brigid Walsh, and Brigid Conley Walsh. She is a frequent guest star on various television programs. She starred in Kindred: The Embraced and Over There. She also had the reoccurring role of Virginia Bryce, Wesley Wyndam-Pryce's girlfriend, during the second season of Angel.

Brannagh lives in Los Angeles, California with her husband Justin Lyons.

Brannagh guest star on both Charmed and Angel in the second seasons sixth episodes.
