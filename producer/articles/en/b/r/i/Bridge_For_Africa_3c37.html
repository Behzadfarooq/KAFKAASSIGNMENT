Bridge For Africa is a 501(c)(3) non-profit, Fair Trade organization. It was created to promote self-sufficiency in rural Africa. The organization works with African artist groups to design and create hand-made African items. The artists are paid a living wage for their work. Fundraising is used to reinvest in community development, training, and job creation.
