Women's Snowboarding
Bronze 1998 Giant Slalom

Brigitte "Gitti" Köck (born May 18, 1970 in Innsbruck) is an Austrian snowboarder and Olympic medalist. At the 1998 Winter Olympics in Nagano, Japan she won bronze in the Giant Slalom competition.

World Championships:

  • 1x 2nd rank (1997)
  • 2x 3rd rank (1999, 2000)
  • 3x 4th rank (1999, 2001, 2002)

European Championships:

  • 1x 1st rank (1995)
  • 3x 2nd rank (1995, 1998, 1999)
  • 1x 3rd rank (1996)

Worldcup / World Pro Tour:

  • 45 times top-3
