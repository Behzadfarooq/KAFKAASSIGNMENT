Brian Irwin (also known as The Space Cowboy[1]) is a country/folk rock singer from Vancouver. Having toured the world and performed in Dublin, Paris, Seoul and Montreal, he now lives in Los Angeles.[2]

[edit] Discography

Brian Irwin has released the following albums:[3]

  • Northern Girl
  • Blues for Hanuman
  • How I Pass My Day
  • Christmas In LA
  • Nude
  • The Dode
