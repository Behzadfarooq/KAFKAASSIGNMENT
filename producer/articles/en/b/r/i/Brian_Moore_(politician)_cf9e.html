Brian Moore (politician)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Brian Moore

Brian Moore is an independent candidate who ran on an anti-war campaign for Democratic incumbent Bill Nelson's Senate seat in Florida's 2006 Senate election.

Moore has been highly critical of Nelson, who continues to support the U.S. occupation of Iraq, which Moore describes as a quagmire.[1]

In September 2006, the Green Party of Florida officially endorsed Brian Moore. He finish 4th of 6 candidates receiving 19,695 votes, for 0.4% of the total vote in the election.[2]
