Brimfield State Forest

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Brimfield State Forest is a Massachusetts state forest located in near the town of Brimfield in Hampden County, Massachusetts. The park is managed by the Department of Conservation and Recreation.

[edit] Description

Brimfield State Forest has over 20 miles of roads and trails primarily used for hiking and horseback riding. The forest includes Dean Pond Recreation Area, a popular spot for fishing, picnicking and swimming.
