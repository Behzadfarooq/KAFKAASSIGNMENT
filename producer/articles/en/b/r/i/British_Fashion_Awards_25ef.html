The British Fashion Awards is a ceremony held annually in the United Kingdom to present awards to those who have made the most outstanding contributions to British clothing design during the year. The awards include that of British Designer of the Year, as well as awards to the 'most-fashionable' celebrities in various fields. The awards are organised by the British Fashion Council and run as part of London Fashion Week each February.
