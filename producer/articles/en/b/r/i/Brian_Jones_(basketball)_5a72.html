Brian Jones (basketball)

Brian Jones is the current head coach for the men's basketball team at the University of North Dakota. He is a graduate of University of Northern Iowa.

[edit] Career

  • 1996-1998 University of Nebraska-Omaha - assistant coach
  • 1999 Southwest Missouri State University - administrative assistant coach
  • 2000-2001 University of Iowa - administrative assistant coach
  • 2002-2006 University of Iowa - assistant coach
  • 2007- University of North Dakota - head coach
