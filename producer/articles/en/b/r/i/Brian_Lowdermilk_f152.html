Brian Lowdermilk

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Brian Lowdermilk is an American musical theater lyricist and composer.

[edit] Biography

Brian Lowdermilk is a lyricist and composer of musicals. He is also a music director, arranger, vocal coach, and pianist.

Lowdermilkâ€™s most recent musicals have been collaborations with Kait Kerrigan. Their collaborations include The Woman Upstairs, The Unauthorized Autobiography of Samantha Brown (with Zach Altman) and Wrong Number. Lowdermilk and Kerrigan are currently working on TheaterworksUSA's adaptation of Henry and Mudge, which will premiere Off-Broadway in 2006 at the Lucille Lortel Theatre.

Lowdermilk has received a Richard Rodgers Award, an Alan Menken Award, and a Jonathan Larson Memorial Fellowship at the Dramatists Guild.

A fully staged production of The Woman Upstairs is in talks to be presented at the University of the Arts, in Philadelphia, PA, eventually.
