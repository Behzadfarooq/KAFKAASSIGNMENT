British Balls Magazine, known by readers as 'The Balls' is a weekly street press magazine published in Australia, by What Media Group.

[edit] Magazine Style and Content

British Balls is a news and sport based publication aimed at British and Irish backpackers living in Australia. The magazine contains humorous and sardonic reports of news from 'up over', hollywood gossip, the latest football and sports results from the UK, as well as info regarding most aspects of Australian travel. The magazine is free to pick up in bars, hostels and most traveller's haunts all around Australia.

British Balls has a very distinctive style both in editorial and in design - the front covers of the magazine usually feature a humorous montage of a celebrity or sportsperson, generally in a ridiculous situation based upon a topical story. The magazine is one of the few street press titles that is printed in full CMYK colour throughout the body of the magazine.

[edit] Background

British Balls was established in 1999 in Sydney as a competitor to other backpacker lifestyle publications (such as TNT Magazine and The Word (magazine)). The magazine currently sits around 130 pages.
