Brimington is a civil parish within the borough of Chesterfield in north-east Derbyshire, England. The town of Staveley is to the east, and Hollingwood is nearby.

[edit] Library

Brimington has a small library. It provides all the usual facilities expected of a library in the UK.

[edit] Businesses

Brimington has three chip shops and a petrol station.

There is an old pub called the Ark Tavern which is beside what is now a care home. The building housing the care home used to be called Sutton Lodge (formerly Sutton Villa) and dates from about 1780 -1790.
