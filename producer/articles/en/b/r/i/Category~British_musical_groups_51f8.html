The British musical groups category is for groups which are composed of members who are notably from the United Kingdom. It does not apply to groups which do not contain British musicians, but have had their main success in the United Kingdom.

If the group identifies themselves as being from a constituent country of the UK make use of Category:English musical groups,Category:Welsh musical groups, Category:Northern Irish musical groups, Category:Scottish musical groups. Otherwise please categorise them here.
