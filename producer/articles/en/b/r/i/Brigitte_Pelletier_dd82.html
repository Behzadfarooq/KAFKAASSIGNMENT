Brigitte Pelletier (born circa 1966) is a Quebec lawyer and associate deputy minister of the Quebec Ministry of Justice. She is a graduate of Laval University.

She was a former chief of staff for Quebec premier Bernard Landry in 2002-2003.

Following Landry's defeat in 2003, she was appointed as Associate Deputy Minister of Justice.
