Category:British Atlantic territories geography stubs

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

This category is for stub articles relating to the geography of the British Overseas Territories in the Atlantic (that is, Bermuda, the Falkland Islands, Saint Helena and its dependencies, and South Georgia and the South Sandwich Islands). You can help Wikipedia by expanding them.

To add an article to this category, use {{UK-Atlantic-geo-stub}} instead of {{stub}}.

Use {{Bermuda-geo-stub}} and {{Falklands-geo-stub}} for geography articles related to Bermuda and the Falkland Islands, respectively.
