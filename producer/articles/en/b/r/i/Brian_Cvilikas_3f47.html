Brian Cvilikas (born March 16, 1984 in Highlands Ranch, Colorado) is a striker currently playing for American USL First Division side Virginia Beach Mariners. Cvilikas attended Old Dominion University and was drafted by the MetroStars in the 2006 MLS Supplemental Draft, but failed to make the team.
