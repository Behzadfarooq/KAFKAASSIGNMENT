[edit] Tank, Infantry, Mk I, Matilda I (A11)

Total production 1937-40: 140.

[edit] Tank, Infantry, Mk II, Matilda II (A12)

Total production 1937-43 of Matilda II Mks II, IIa, III, IV: 2,987.

[edit] Tank, Infantry, Mk III, Valentine

Total production 1939-45 of Valentine Mks I, II, III and IV: 8,275.

[edit] Tank, Light, Mk VII, Tetrarch (A17)

Total production: 177.

[edit] Tank, Light, Mk I, (A9)

Total production: 125.

[edit] Tank, Cruiser, Mk II, (A10)

Total production 1938-40: 175.

[edit] Tank, Cruiser, Mk III, (A13)

Total production 1938-39: 65.

[edit] Tank, Cruiser, Mk IV, (A13 Mk II)

Several converted from Mk III, variant Mk IVA: 665.

[edit] Tank, Cruiser, Mk V, Covenanter (A13 Mk III)

Total production: 1,700. Note that no A13 Mk III saw combat as all were stationed in Great Britain during the war.

[edit] Tank, Cruiser, Mk VI, Crusader (A15)

Total production 1940-43 of Crusader Mks I, II and III: 5,300.

[edit] Tank, Cruiser, Mk VII, Cavalier (A24)

Total production 1941-43: 500.

[edit] Tank, Cruiser, Mk VIII, Centaur (A27L)

Total production 1942-43: 950.

[edit] Tank, Cruiser, Mk VIII, Challenger (A30)

Total production 1943-44: 200.

[edit] Tank, Cruiser, Comet I (A34)

Total production 1944-45: 1,200.

[edit] Tank, Cruiser, Centurion I (A41)

Total production 1944-45: 6.
