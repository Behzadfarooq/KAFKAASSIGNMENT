Brink Lindsey

Brink Lindsey is a senior fellow and the Director of the Center for Trade Policy Studies at the Cato Institute. Mr. Lindsey is also a contributing editor to Reason Magazine. Lindsey received a Bachelor of Arts from Princeton University, and a Juris Doctor from Harvard Law School.

[edit] Publications

  • Brink Lindsey, Daniel J. Ikenson (2003). "Antidumping Exposed: The Devilish Details of Unfair Trade Law"
  • Brink Lindsey, (2002). Against the Dead Hand: The Uncertain Struggle for Global Capitalism
