Brian Reed

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia


Brian Reed (born July 1, 1973 in Anderson, Indiana) is an American comic book and video game writer, currently residing near Atlanta, Georgia. Prior to becoming a full time writer, Reed was a video game designer.
