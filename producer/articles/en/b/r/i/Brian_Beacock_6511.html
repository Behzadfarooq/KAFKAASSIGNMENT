Brian Beacock

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Brian Beacock is an anime voice actor. He is best known for playing Takato Matsuki in Digimon Tamers, and Yamato Delgado in Battle B-Daman. He also has appeared on CSI: Crime Scene Investigation.

[edit] Anime Roles

  • Battle B-Daman - Yamato Delgado, Joshua
  • Bleach - Yumichika Ayasegawa
  • Bobobo-bo Bo-bobo - Dengaku Man, Additional Voices
  • Digimon Tamers - Takato Matsuki, Gallantmon (shared)
  • Digimon Frontier - Bokomon (Frontier)
  • Duel Masters - Aizen, Boy George (Season Three)
  • Idaten Jump - Arthur
  • Naruto - Sakon, Yashamaru
  • Zatch Bell! - Byonko, Jobin, Additional Voices
