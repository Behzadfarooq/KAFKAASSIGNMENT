Bristow, or Bristowe, is the surname of:

  • Allan Bristow, American basketball coach
  • Benjamin Helm Bristow (1832-1896), American lawyer and politician
  • Chris Bristow (1937-1960), British racing driver
  • Deem Bristow (1947-2005), American actor and voice actor
  • Eric Bristow (born 1957), British darts player
  • George Bristow (c.1862-1947), British taxidermist and gunsmith, a central figure in the Hastings Rarities affair
  • George Frederick Bristow, American composer
  • Grant Bristow (born 1958), Canadian security agent
  • Gwen Bristow (1903-1980), American author and journalist
  • Henry William Bristow (1817-1889), English geologist
  • Joseph L. Bristow, American politician
  • Laurie Bristow, British diplomat
  • Patrick Bristow, American actor
  • Richard Bristow, English Catholic writer
  • Sam Bristow, British poker player
  • W.S. Bristowe (1901-1979), English naturalist
  • A P (Andy) Bristow (Born 1963), Soldier, sailor and leader of Antarctic Expedition
