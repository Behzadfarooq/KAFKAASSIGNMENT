Brienne Pedigo

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Brienne Pedigo is an auto racing pit reporter for ESPN and ESPN on ABC for the Indy Racing League. She was formally introduced as the newest member of the Indy Racing League Telecast crew on March 7, 2007.[1] She is taking over as pit reporter for Jamie Little, who has moved to ESPN's NASCAR Busch Series coverage. She was formerly a pit reporter for The Outdoor Channel's USAC sprint and midget car races, and a reporter for CBS' coverage of the NCAA Final Four. Her father, Gary, is also a join owner in an IndyCar Series team, Panther Racing. Pedigo and General Hospital star Tyler Christopher have been engaged since October 2006.
