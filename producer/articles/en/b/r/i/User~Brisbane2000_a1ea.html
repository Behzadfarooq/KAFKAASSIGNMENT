[edit] Welcome

Hi,

My username is funny because I use the same username for everything I have to log on for; I chose this one in 2000 when I happened to be in Brisbane, so there you go.

I really like wikipedia and spend quite some time reading articles and whenever I want to know something I look it up here. Given the nature of the wikipedia project I wanted to contribute some myself so joining the League Of Copyeditors seems like a good idea. Also when I feel the article could be expanded relatively easy I try to do so.

English is not my native language, which may appear odd for someone wanting to copy edit, however I have a decent command over it and my insurance policy is my housemate who is an english teacher.

If anyone needs help on an article; I consider myself knowledgeable about a few subjects:

  • Army fire support and related subjects.
  • Military intelligence.

Cheers!
