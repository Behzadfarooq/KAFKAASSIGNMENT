Ornithologists' from the United Kingdom.

People who are ornithologists practice Ornithology, the study of birds. They may also be birdwatchers although the latter may not necessarily be ornithoogists. Ornithology is a branch of natural history, zooology and biology. See also the list of ornithologists.
