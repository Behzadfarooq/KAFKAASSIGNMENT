[edit] Spelling

Some slang words are shared between British and American English, with a difference in spelling only. Slang naturally has no fixed orthography, and is written as whatever comes readily to mind. This can generate confusion with readers who do not pronounce letters the same way. One particular concerns the letter "r", which British slang assumes isn't emphatically pronounced.

British American
erm um
nur nah
larf (as an exaggeration) laugh
arse ass
