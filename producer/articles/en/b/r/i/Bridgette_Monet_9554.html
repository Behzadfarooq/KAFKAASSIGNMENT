Bridgette Monet (born Dana Kunath in 1959) is an American pornographic actress who was active in the porn industry in the early 1980s.

Regarded as one of the few "high class" porn actresses, she had a personal pictorial in the March 1984 issue of Playboy magazine. In contrast to such porn stars of the same era as Marilyn Chambers and Serena - who usually portrayed sexually aggressive women in adult movies - Monet often portrayed 'sexual awakening' and 'the seduced innocent' roles in her adult movie career, capitalizing on her girl next door attractiveness and on-screen persona. During her porn career, she went to California State University of Long Beach to study medicine.

Under the name Sarah Hampshire she also worked as a BDSM model for magazines and videos.
