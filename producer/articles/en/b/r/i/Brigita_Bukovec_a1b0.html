Brigita Bukovec

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Olympic medal record
Women's athletics
Silver 1996 Atlanta 100 m Hurdles

Brigita Bukovec (born January 25, 1970) is a retired Slovenian hurdler who won a surprising Olympic silver medal in 1996. During the Olympics she set a personal best time with 12.59 seconds.

She stopped competing on top level after the 1998 season.
