Brier Elementary School

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Brier Elementary School is an elementary school in Fremont, California. It is located at 39201 Sundale Drive 94538[1]. It is one of 28 elementary schools in the city belonging to the Fremont Unified School District. Brier is part of the Kennedy Attendance area, and goes to John F. Kennedy High School and Walters Junior High School[2].
