Brinkerhoff is a webcomic by Gabe Strine about a rabbit named Brinkerhoff. The strip starts with Brink's wife Karen asking him for a divorce. Brink learns she's been seeing another man, Mark, and things proceed to go downhill for him. The strip was written after Strine's own divorce.

On 2006-11-07 it was announced that Brinkerhoff would be published on Viper Comics' website[1].

[edit] Characters

  • Brink: The eponymous protagonist of the strip.
  • Karen: Brink's ex-wife.
  • Hank: Brinks best friend (never married). He gets himself into odd situations and starts the weekly "wave day."
  • Brink's Mom: Brink initially moves back in with his mother.
  • Mark: He was Brink and Karen's marriage counselor before he and Karen started seeing each other. He is utterly ineffectual in reigning in Brink and Karen's children.
