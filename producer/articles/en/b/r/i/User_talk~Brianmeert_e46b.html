[edit] Movie Filler

I've added the "{{prod}}" template to the article Movie Filler, suggesting that it be deleted according to the proposed deletion process. All contributions are appreciated, but I don't believe it satisfies Wikipedia's criteria for inclusion, and I've explained why in the deletion notice (see also Wikipedia:What Wikipedia is not and Wikipedia:Notability). Please either work to improve the article if the topic is worthy of inclusion in Wikipedia, or, if you disagree, discuss the issues raised at Talk:Movie Filler. If you remove the {{dated prod}} template, the article will not be deleted, but note that it may still be sent to Wikipedia:Articles for deletion, where it may be deleted if consensus to delete is reached. Pascal.Tesson 03:38, 15 November 2006 (UTC)

Hi. I see that you have removed the deletion tag. (Which you have every right to do) Nevertheless, I believe that the article should still be deleted for a number of reasons. First, as I said in my deletion rationale, the article is constructed as an advertisement for your website. We try to avoid such conflicts of interest. Moreover, the website in question does not seem to meet our guideline for inclusion of web-related content. You also claim that "movie filler" is becoming increasingly used in that sense. Frankly, I'm not convinced: judging by my search on Google, this is not widely used as you claim. Even if it was, then that would probably not be worth a Wikipedia article and would be best suited for Wiktionary.

Cheers, Pascal.Tesson 04:04, 15 November 2006 (UTC)
