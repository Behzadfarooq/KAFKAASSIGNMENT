Brian Marcee Williams (born December 17, 1972 in Dallas) is a retired National Football League linebacker.

[edit] Professional Career

Williams played for the Green Bay Packers, Detroit Lions and New Orleans Saints between 1995 and 2002.

[edit] College Career

Williams played college football at the University of Southern California.
