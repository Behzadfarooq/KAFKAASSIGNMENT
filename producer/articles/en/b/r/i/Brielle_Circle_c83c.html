Brielle Circle

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Brielle Circle was a traffic circle in Wall Township, New Jersey, United States, located where Route 34, Route 35, and Route 70 meet. It was the southern end for the never-built Route 18 extension. Around 2000 the circle was replaced by an at-grade intersection with jughandles.
