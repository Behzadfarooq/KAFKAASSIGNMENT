Brian Worth is an American who was the runner-up on the television series Average Joe:Hawaii. The show featured "Average Joes" vying for the attentions of Larissa Meek. Later in the show, a group of attractive suitors were brought in to compete against the Joes. Brian made it to the final against Gil Hyatt, but Larissa chose Gil. Brian also participated in the show Battle of the Network Reality Stars.
