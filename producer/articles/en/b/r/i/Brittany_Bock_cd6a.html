Brittany Bock

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Brittany Bock (born April 11, 1987) is an Association football player from USA. She plays mid-fielder and forward for University of Notre Dame and FC Indiana.[1] Bock is also a member of the US U-20 national women's under 20 soccer team.

Brittany's full name is Brittany Christine Bock. She is the daughter of Brian and Kelly Bock. Her birthplace is Naperville, Illinois and she attended Neuqua Valley High School. Brittany is a sophomore enrolled in Mendoza College of Business at the University of Notre Dame.[2]

In the 2006 NCAA Div-1 Women's Soccer National Finals on December 3, Notre Dame lost 2-1 to the University of North Carolina. Brittany scored the only goal for the Irish in the 81st minute. [3] Previously, she played for "Windy City Pride" from 2002-2004. [4]
