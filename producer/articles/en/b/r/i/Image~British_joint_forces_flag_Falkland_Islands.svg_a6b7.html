[edit] Summary

This is the joint forces flag used by the en:Falkland Islands Defence Forces. The base stripped pattern is from old Yugoslav Civil Ensign and the map is from av SVG map of the Americas. FOTW was used for the colors and the CIA World Factbook was used for the placement of the Falklands themselves.
