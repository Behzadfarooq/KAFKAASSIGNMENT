Bridgeton High School is a comprehensive community public high school that serves the city of Bridgeton, in Cumberland County, New Jersey, United States, as part of the Bridgeton Public Schools, an Abbott District

Students from Downe Township and Lawrence Township attend the high school as part of a sending/receiving relationship.[1]
