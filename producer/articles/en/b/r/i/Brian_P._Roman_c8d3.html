Brian P. Roman

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Brian P. Roman is an American astronomer.

He discovered a number of asteroids, and also co-discovered the periodic comets 111P/Helin-Roman-Crockett, 117P/Helin-Roman-Alu and 132P/Helin-Roman-Alu.

The asteroid 2516 Roman was likely named in his honour.
