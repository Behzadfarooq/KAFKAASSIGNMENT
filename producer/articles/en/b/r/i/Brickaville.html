Brickaville

Brickaville is a city in Toamasina Province, Madagascar. It is located at around 18°49′0″S, 49°4′0″E. Brickaville, also known as Ampasimanolotra, is located on the road linking Toamasina to Antananarivo. Its main industries revolve around sugar.
