[edit] Usage

Add {{British Columbia regional districts map|map=Census divisions BC.png}} in articles pertaining Regional districts of British Columbia.

The map parameter is optional, it can be changed with equal map images, the default is Census divisions BC.png.

A regonal district map with linked labels will be rendered.
