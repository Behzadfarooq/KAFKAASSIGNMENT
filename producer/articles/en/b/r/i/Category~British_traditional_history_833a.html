Category:British traditional history

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

This category lists articles related to the pre-Anglo-Saxon history of Britain and the Britons, as accounted by Geoffrey of Monmouth and others. It contains articles on a mixture of mythical and historical subjects, and on some topics which fall somewhere between the two.
