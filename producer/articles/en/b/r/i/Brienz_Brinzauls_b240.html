Brienz/Brinzauls

Brienz/Brinzauls is a municipality in the district of Albula in the canton of Graubünden in Switzerland.

The majority of the population is German-speaking, with a large Romansh-speaking minority.

The village is located north of the Albula River on the road from Lenzerheide to Davos. The area is 13.38 km² and the population 114.
