British Rail Class D1/1

British Rail Class D1/1 was a class of locomotive commissioned by British Rail in England. It was a diesel locomotive converted from the DY1 class in the pre-TOPS period.

[edit] Specification

  • Numbers: D2950-D2952 (formerly 11500-11502)
  • Wheel arrangement: 0-4-0 shunter
  • Introduced: 1955
  • Builder: Hunslet Engine Company
  • Engine: Gardner 6L3 of 153 bhp (114 KW)
  • Transmission: Mechanical, four-speed
