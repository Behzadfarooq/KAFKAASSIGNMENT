Britney Spears' other songs

Here is a list of other songs that Britney recorded, but are not on any official U.S. versions of her albums. It also includes b-side songs and oversea versions of albums, etc.

1998 (...Baby One More Time era)

  • I'm So Curious ("Sometimes" CD single)
  • Deep In My Heart (...Baby One more Time European version)
  • I'll Never Stop Loving You (...Baby One More Time European version)
  • Autumn Goodbye (...Baby One More Time European version)

1999/2000 (Oops!... I Did It Again era)

  • Girl In The Mirror (Oops!... I Did It Again European version)
  • Heart (Oops!... I Did It Again European version)
  • You Got It All (This song was originally recorded for the ...Baby One More Time album, however, it did not make the cut beyond a small clip featured on a Backstreet Boys bonus CD and we can found this song on the limited Bonus CD of ...Baby One More Time; it is also mentioned in the initial pressings of the ...Baby One More Time North American tour book. Oops!... I Did It Again European version)
  • Walk On By ("Stronger" single)
  • I've Got The Urge (Herbal Essence Promotional Jingle)
  • Joy Of Pepsi (Pepsi Promotional Jingle)

2001 (Britney era)

  • Intimidated ("I'm a Slave 4 U" single)
  • I Run Away (Britney international editions)
  • Before The Goodbye (Britney international editions)
  • She'll Never Be Me (this song can only be found within a minute long clip, however there are many unofficial extended mixes floating on the net)
  • When I Found You (This song can be found on Britney European Version)
  • Mystic Man (Performed live on the 2nd leg of the Dream Within A Dream Tour)
  • Weakness (Performed live on the 2nd leg of the Dream Within A Dream Tour)
  • You Were My Home (Performed live on the 2nd leg of the Dream Within A Dream Tour)
  • Pepsi Generation (Pepsi Promotional Jingle)
  • Right Now (Taste The Victory) (Pepsi Promotional Jingle)

2002/2003 (In The Zone era)

  • Don't Hang Up (In the Zone European edition)
  • The Answer (This song can be found on the In The Zone European Version)
  • Girls & Boys (This one can be found on the European Version of In The Zone DVD)
  • Get It (aka Can You Give Me My Soul) (Demo version only, the official title of this song in not known, what so ever)
  • Early Mornin' (Alternate Version) (This song is only available in a short clip from a producer's website, it features slightly different vocals and lyrics, however, the clip is only around 45 seconds)
  • Sin City (This is an unofficial song title, there is no official title - this song is the full length version of the PUNK'D rap Britney did in 2002)
  • "Like a Virgin"/"Hollywood"/"Work It" medley (Madonna Featuring Christina Aguilera, Britney Spears, and Missy Elliot) (This song is only available in a 'live' format, but can be purchased on Madonna's Remixed & Revisited compilation)
  • "We Will Rock You" (Featuring P!nk & Beyoncé) (Pepsi Promotional Jingle)

2004/2005 (Chaotic / My Prerogative era)

  • Mona Lisa (Britney originally premiered this song in early 2004 on KISS FM, it was a slightly different demo version - the official studio version came out on the Britney and Kevin: Chaotic bonus disk)
  • Chaotic (it is rumored that "Chaotic" and "Over To You Now" were originally recorded for the In The Zone album, but did not make the cut - there is no official evidence for 'Over To You Now' however)
  • Over To You Now
  • Tell Me What You're Sippin' On (There's only a little clip floating on the net)
  • Someday (I Will Understand) (This may have been Britney's only recording in the year of 2005, it can be found on the Britney and Kevin: Chaotic bonus disk)

2005/2006

  • Luv The Hurt Away (Feat. Full Force) [Many speculate that this song was actually recorded during the ...Baby One More Time sessions)
  • Little Me (also known as "Just Yesterday" or "For My Sister" - this song premiered on BritneySpears.com with an accompanied video. This song has only leaked in a 45 second clip)
  • Rebellion (this song premiered on BritneySpears.com under the 'Just Britney' section and can only be found within a 51 second clip)
  • Crazy (Kevin Federline featuring Britney Spears) (Leaked on the Internet on October 28, 2006, and can be found on Federline's debut album, Playing With Fire.
