The name Brian is of Celtic origin and its meaning may be "hill" or "strong, noble, and high". There is no strong evidence regarding any potential origin, only conjecture, although the Gaelic word "brígh"/"brìgh" ("context", "elixir", "essence", "interpretation", "meaning", "pith", "significance") offer a possible root. Brian means a matter strength, dignity, and honor. Another meaning for this name is "majestic and powerful in Irish rule."

A common definition is "honor, strength, virtue".
