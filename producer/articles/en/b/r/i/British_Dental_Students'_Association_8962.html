The British Dental Students' Association is a representative body of the dental students studying at British dental schools. The association wes established in 1942.

[edit] Positions

The Executive Committee are made up of five positions at present: President, Treasurer, Communicatons, Secretary & Sportsday Organiser; Conference Organiser, and Past President (only advisory role).
