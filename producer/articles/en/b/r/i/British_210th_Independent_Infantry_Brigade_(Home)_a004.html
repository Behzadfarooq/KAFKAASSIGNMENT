British 210th Independent Infantry Brigade (Home)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The 210th Independent Infantry Brigade (Home) was created on 10 October 1940 in the United Kingdom as a Home defense static brigade. It converted to the 38th (Irish) Infantry Brigade (United Kingdom) on 13 January 1942.

[edit] Structure

(As the 38th Infantry Brigade)

  • 1st Battalion, The Royal Irish Fusiliers
  • 2nd Battalion, The London Irish Rifles
  • 6th Battalion, The Royal Inniskilling Fusiliers
  • 2nd Battalion, The Royal Inniskilling Fusiliers
