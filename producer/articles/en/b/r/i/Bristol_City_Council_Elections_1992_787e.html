Bristol City Council Elections 1992

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

You have new messages (last change).

One third of seats were up for election. Two seats were contested in Hartcliffe due to an extra vacancy occurring. The elections were held just weeks after John Major's unexpected 1992 General election victory, and mirroring that result there was a strong swing from Labour to Conservative.

The party standings following the election:

Party Cllrs Change
  Labour Party 40 -5
  Conservative Party 22 +4
  Liberal Democrats 6 +1
