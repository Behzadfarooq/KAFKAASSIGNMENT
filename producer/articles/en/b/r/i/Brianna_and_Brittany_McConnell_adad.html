Megan Brianna and Morgan Brittany McConnell (they use their middle names) are two United States child actresses, born August 20, 1993. They are twins.

They played Belle Black on Days of Our Lives from 1995 to 1998. They also played Lexi in the movie Baby Geniuses, and Arnold Schwarzenegger's baby in Junior.
