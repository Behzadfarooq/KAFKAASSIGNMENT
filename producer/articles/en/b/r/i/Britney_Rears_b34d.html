Britney Rears is a character in pornography movies, currently portrayed by Hillary Scott.

Britney Rears came to prominence in the spring of 2005. Director Will Ryder noticed Jessica Sweet, who bears some resemblance to Britney Spears, as the actress who could play the role of Britney. Britney Rears: Wild Back Stage Sex Party was a huge seller for the company VCA, as well as put Jessica Sweet on the porn map.

Sweet would reprise the role in Britney Rears 2: I Wanna Get Laid. The plot was loosely based on Britney Spears marriage to Kevin Federline, as Rears had recently married a man named Kevin. Instead of being distributed by VCA, Hustler financed the movie. They would also distribute Britney Rears 3: Britney Gets Shafted.

Due to Sweet being difficult to work with, Hustler and Will Ryder replaced her with a co-star from Britney Rears 2, Hillary Scott. Scott was able to fit the role, due to her new 34D breasts, the similar size of Jessica Sweet's all natural ones. The plot of the film was completely off course from the first two. Rears purchases a time traveling machine, and travels back to 1974. It was released August 22 to positive reviews.

The Howard Stern Show has played the theme song from Wild Back Stage Sex Party several times.
