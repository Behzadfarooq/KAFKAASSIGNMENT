[edit] List of Catholic priests

Wikipedia's policy on capitalization says that you should "not capitalize second and subsequent words" of article names. Further, the manual of style entry on job titles says that they should be lowercase "When used generically", as is the case with this page. Catholic priests are priests, not Priests. I moved and edited the article to reflect that. Regards, Whosyourjudas (talk) 19:42, 25 Oct 2004 (UTC)
