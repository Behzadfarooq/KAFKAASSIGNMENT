Brian O Donoghue is an inter-county Gaelic football goalkeeper for Galway. His father won a Hurling League medal with Galway in 1975. He has battled with Alan Keane for the position of goalkeeper

[edit] Playing Career

He made his debut for Galway in 2003 and has won 3 connacht medals. He also was part of the Galway under21 team to win the All-Ireland in 2002.

Brian escaped a serious head injury in 2006 after a heavy fall for his club, Claregalway but made a full recovery and played in the Connaght final 2 weeks later.
