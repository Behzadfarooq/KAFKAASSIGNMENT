Brittany Petros

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Brittany Petros (born September 9, 1974 in Robbinsdale, Minnesota) is an American actress, who first gained widespread notice being a contestant on the first season of the American version of the hit reality TV show Big Brother, broadcast by CBS in 2000. Subsequent to the show, she's had various TV and film roles.

[edit] Actress

Starting in 2003 she's had a recurring guest role in the long running soap opera Days of Our Lives, playing character Denise. She's also had guest appearances on Suspect: True Crime Stories in 2002, and Dead Last.

Movies she's done include I-See-You.Com (2005), Screech of the Decapitated (2004), and Zombie Rights! (2003).

[edit] Casting

Brittany has worked as a casting director for TV and film. She did casting work for The Lyon's Den (as an intern), Switched!, Suspect, Dismissed, and CSI: Crime Scene Investigation (as an intern).

[edit] Big Brother

Brittany was one of ten Big Brother contestants, who were confined in a house together, with the objective of outlasting one another. Each week TV viewers voted out one of the contestants. Brittany was one of the most noticed contestants, due to her brightly colored hair. Brittany was the fourth contestant to be evicted, leaving the house on day 57.
