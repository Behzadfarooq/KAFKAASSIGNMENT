My wikilife started September 16, 2005.

I joined Wikipedia because it appeals to my anal organizational tendencies. I love the idea of articles grouped by categories, everything in its place.

I also enjoy the fact that it's impossible to use Wiki and not learn something new, whether it be HTML for Wiki, Wikipolicy, or just some new bit of information contained in the pages of Wiki.

[edit] My Background

My parents are from the South, both raised in Paducah, Kentucky. I am, however, firmly Northern, with the exception of living in Orange and Victoria, Texas for approxiamately two years, around the ages of four and five.

Almost all of the branches of my family tree that have been researched were in America by the early 1700s or before which is really cool. Basically, half of the family settled in Massachusetts, Maryland and Rhode Island and the other half settled in Virginia and North Carolina. Ethnically, I am mostly Anglo (British and Irish); I'm also 1/16th Cherokee and a certain part Swiss and Ukranianand it is interesting to know where I come from because some people don't know where they come from or who their parents are.

Due to the fact that even my parents haven't lived in the South in 35 years, I am effectively a Northern WASP with Southern roots. I like to think I have a Southerner's charm and the WASP sensibility and intelligence.

[edit] A Little Bit About Me

I was born May 3, 1979, in Wilmington, North Carolina.

I've moved around a lot, especially as a child growing up. Most of my memorable elementary school years were spent in Geneva, Switzerland. I attended the International School of Geneva, La Grande Boissière campus.

Back in the US, I graduated from a well-respected school outside of Philadelphia, Pennsylvania and went on to a dual-major in psychology and behavioral science at a small, private college in Delaware.

I speak both English and French fluently.

Aside from the locations mentioned above, I have lived in the following places: Aiken, South Carolina; Wilmington, Delaware; Chadds Ford, Pennsylvania; West Chester, PA; State College, Pennsylvania and Newark, Delaware.

I work in the legal profession.
