Bright and Shiny (album)

Doris Day sings early Sherman Brothers song as title song of 1960 LP.

Bright and Shiny was an album released by Columbia Records, featuring Doris Day backed by Neal Hefti's orchestra, on March 20, 1961. It was released in two forms; a monaural LP (catalog number CL-1614) and a stereophonic LP (catalog number CS-8414). A song of the same name was composed especially for this album.

  2. "I Want to Be Happy" (Vincent Youmans/Irving Caesar)
  3. "Keep Smilin', Keep Laughin', Be Happy"
  4. "Singin' in the Rain" (Nacio Herb Brown/Arthur Freed)
  5. "Gotta Feelin'"
  6. "Happy Talk" (Richard Rodgers/Oscar Hammerstein II)
  7. "Ridin' High"
  8. "On the Sunny Side of the Street" (Jimmy McHugh/Dorothy Fields)
  9. "Clap Yo' Hands"
  10. "Stay with the Happy People"
  11. "Twinkle and Shine"
