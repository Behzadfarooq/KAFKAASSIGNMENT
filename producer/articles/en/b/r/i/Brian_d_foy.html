brian d foy (born 1970) is the publisher and editor of The Perl Review, co-author of books on Perl including Learning Perl and Intermediate Perl, founder of Perl Mongers, and a veteran of the Iraq War. [1] He is the author of multiple Perl modules on CPAN. He is a partner at Stonehenge Consulting Services.

He prefers that his name be rendered all lowercase with no punctuation. When spoken, the 'd' is silent. [2]
