Brian Diaz

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

You have new messages (last change).

Brian Diaz was the lead singer for Edna's Goldfish, a ska band popular in the late 90s, hailing from Long Island, NY. From 2000 to 2003 he sang and played bass for The Reunion Show, a pop-punk band also from Long Island whose "Kill Your Television" record came out on Victory Records in 2002.

Born October 14, 1976 in New York City, was raised in NYC and Long Island. Currently resides in Chicago, and has been for past few years touring with bands such as Brand New and Motion City Soundtrack as a production manager and guitar tech.
