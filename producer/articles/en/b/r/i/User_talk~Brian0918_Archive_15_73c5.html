[edit] Unsourced

There is no consensus that the unsourced template belongs on article pages, and there is almost universal agreement that it does not belong at the top of articles. This is a template meant for editors, not for readers, and per the template guidelines it belongs on the talk page. If you have serious questions about the accuracy of an article, which our readers do need to be warned about, please use the verify tag. - SimonP 15:53, 1 December 2005 (UTC)
