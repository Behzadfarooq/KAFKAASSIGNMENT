[edit] Episode summary

Week 1

The 12 fresh faced girls are picked up across London on a Routenaster London Bus and are taken to meet their host for the competition, Lisa Butcher. They also meet their stylist Hilary Alexander and their personal trainer Gideon. The arguments begin and the claws come out.

  • Marina was eliminated

Week 2

The girls get their makeovers, but not everyone is happy with their new look. The girls participate in a makeup challenge. The winners go on a date with 3 male models, the losers work and serve in a resteraunt. The girls have a photo shoot with Nicky Johnston. While at the shoot more fights break out and a rift is caused between the girls.

  • Claire was eliminated

Week 3

The girls meet choreographer Andy Turner. They learn a routine and have to put their new skills to the test in a pop video. Edwina and Naomi once again are separated after being accused of making comments on the other girls skills. The girls undergo a close-up beauty photo shoot.

  • Shauna was eliminated

Week 4

This week the girls learn acting at the theatre. The girls are delighted to perform with Paul Danan from Hollyoaks. The girls have to audition for an ice-cream commercial, with the winners going on a date with Paul Danan and Scott Wright. The fights between the girls get even worse. The girls shoot a showergel commercial. And one girl has had enough and splits to two.

  • Due to Anne's decision to leave nobody was eliminated. Lucy and Stephanie were in the bottom two.

Week 5

The girls are tutored in nutrition and attitude. They model designer Maria Grachvogel’s designs in a catwalk challenge. The winners get a spa treatment and the losers have to model Liz Hurley’s beachwear in the window of a store. But they are the real winners when they get to meet Mohammed Al Fayed. When they rub it into the other girl’s faces, things get tense. The theme of the photo shoot this week is ‘Fairytale Princess’. But having to pose with a bullfrog is too much for Tashi, causing her to break into tears.

  • Hayley was eliminated.

Week 6

The girls have a yoga session with Howard Napper. They meet ‘Heat’ magazine’s Lucie Cave. They have an interview challenge. The winners go for a dinner with Tamara Beckwith, Orla O’ Rourke and Angela Dunn. They have a photo shoot for DKNY. One girl brings another to tears.

  • Marisa was eliminated.

Week 7

The girls go on a go-see in Paris. The girls task is to buy an outfit for £30. The winners get a visit from their boyfriends. They visit Karen Diamond at Models 1, the winners representative agency. The girls learn they will be going to Milan and try their best on this weeks couture photshoot as there are only 5 seats on the plane.

  • Stephanie was eliminated.

Week 8

This episode goes back to the beginning of the competition and shows us how much each of the girls have changed.

Week 9

The five remaining contestants arrive in Milan. The rooms in the hotel cause problems. This weeks challenge is a go-see with Paola at Beatrice Agency. They struggle with a photoshoot with top Milanese designer, Stefano. This week sees two eliminations.

  • Naomi and Tashi were eliminated. Naomi came fifth and Tashi came fourth.


Week 10

In the finale, the girls do a topless photo shoot with male models. And 'Britain's Next Top Model' is finally chosen.

  • Lucy is Britain's Next Top Model.
  • Jenilee and Edwina were eliminated. Jenilee came third and Edwina came second.

Week 11

This episode goes back to beginning and shows us Lucy's journey throughout the competition towards becoming 'Britain's Next Top Model'.

[edit] And The Winner Is...

Final
The judges go through Lucy and Edwina’s portfolios and watch clips of the catwalk show. Edwina continues to criticise herself for her performance but the judges feel that they both looked like they had been modelling for years. Lucy is named Britain’s Next Top Model.

Lucy has now won a modelling contract with Models1. Along with this new contract, Lucy has also been given the cover of an edition of 2005's B magazine as well as a spread and interview with the magazine about life before the show and what it will be like now its finished.
