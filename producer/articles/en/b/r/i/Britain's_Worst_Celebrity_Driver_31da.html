Britain's Worst Celebrity Driver was a Five reality television show in which six British celebrities had to drive from John o' Groats and perform various challenges. The British public then decide who will be 'awarded' the title of Britain's Worst Celebrity Driver. The series is presented by Quentin Willson and Jenni Falconer. The series was eventually 'won' by Joel Ross.

The celebrities that took part were:

  • Joel Ross (DJ) -Britain's worst celebrity driver
  • Suzanne Shaw (singer) -runner up
  • Brian Blessed (actor) -eliminated 4th
  • Antonia Okonma (actress) -eliminated 3rd
  • John Noakes (presenter) -eliminated 2nd
  • Erik Estrada (actor) -eliminated 1st
