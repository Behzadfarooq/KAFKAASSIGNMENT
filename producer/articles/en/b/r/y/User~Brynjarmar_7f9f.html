BMV, born 12.19.1978 in Reykjavik Iceland as Brynjar Mar Valdimarsson. I began my music career in 1992 when i started my first band Kenya, as a guitar player and lead singer. Shortly afterwards, i started recording songs as a solo artist, but still using Kenya as my artist name. During the following five years, i recorded few songs on my own and released my first single in 1995. Around that time i also had a band with my best friend called Tiramizu, together we released 3 singles in 1995/96. During the following two years i was playing with many bands, as well as writing and recording songs. In 1998 i was hired as a lead singer in a band that had been playing on concerts and bars for many years. We started playing all around Iceland 2-3 times a week, as well as recording songs. We released 3 singles together in 1999. Shortly thereafter i left the group, wanting to do music on my own. In 2000, i started working in radio, not just as a DJ, but as a program director and owner. Radio programming took most of my time the following years, and playing as a DJ in nightclubs all weekends. In 2005, i formed the Pop/Hiphop band Snooze with a female rapper called Kido. Together we released a couple of singles in 2005/06 and we are still working together as a side project. Today i am working as a program director and a DJ on a Top40 station in Iceland (FM957), and also a DJ in nightclubs all weekends. I have teamed up with a producer in America for my music, and i am making two styles of music, Pop/Hiphop and soft pop. I hope to release my first solo album in 2007.
