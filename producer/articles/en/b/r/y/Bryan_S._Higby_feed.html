Bryan S. Higby

Bryan S. Higby is U.S. novelist and short story writer. His two published novels include The Diary of a Logos and a follow up: A Logos at Large. He currently resides in Jamestown, NY, where he works as the multi-media instructor/adviser to the students of Jamestown High School. A graduate of SUNY Potsdam with a BA in Theatre, Higby is also one of the founding members of the Bunbury Theatre, and has regularly appeared on stage in the company's performances.

Higby has also produced the following films:

  • Mr. Tree Companion
  • Speaking But Not Actually
  • Billy Goes Home
  • Pleasure
  • Amuse
  • The Tunnel

Higby's forthcoming novel, Genetic Tree will be published in 2007
