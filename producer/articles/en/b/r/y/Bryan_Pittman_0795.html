Bryan Pittman

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Bryan Pittman (born January 20, 1977) is a National Football League long snapper for the Houston Texans.

[edit] Junior College Career

At Walla Walla Community College, Pittman was the team's longsnapper and also started for two years at tight end. He then transferred to the University of Washington.
