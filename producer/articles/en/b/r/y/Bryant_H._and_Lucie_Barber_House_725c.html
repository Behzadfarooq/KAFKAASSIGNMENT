Bryant H. and Lucie Barber House
(U.S. National Register of Historic Places)
The Italianate Bryant and Lucie Barber House.
Location: Polo, Ogle County, Illinois, USA
Coordinates: 41°59′13″N, 89°34′38″W
Architectural style(s): Italianate
Added to NRHP: February 10, 1993[1]
Reference #: 92001849

The Bryant H. and Lucie House is a Registered Historic Place in the Ogle County, Illinois city of Polo. It is one of six overall sites and three homes in Polo listed on the Register. The other two homes listed on the National Register of Historic Places in Polo are the John McGrath House and the Henry D. Barber House. The Bryant and Lucie Barber House joined the Register in 1993.
