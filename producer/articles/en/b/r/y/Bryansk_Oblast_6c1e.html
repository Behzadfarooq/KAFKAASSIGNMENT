Bryansk Oblast (Russian: Бря́нская о́бласть, Bryanskaya oblast) is a federal subject of Russia (an oblast). Its administrative center is the city of Bryansk. Population: 1,378,941 (2002 Census); 1,474,785 (1989 Census).

[edit] Time zone

Bryansk Oblast is located in the Moscow Time Zone (MSK/MSD). UTC offset is +0300 (MSK)/+0400 (MSD).

[edit] Administrative divisions

Main article: Administrative divisions of Bryansk Oblast
Administrative divisions of Bryansk Oblast

Bryansk Oblast is divided into 27 districts (raions, Russian: районы) and 4 cities (города), which are further divided into 12 towns (города), 24 urban-type settlements (посёлки городского типа), and 224 selsovets (сельсоветы).
