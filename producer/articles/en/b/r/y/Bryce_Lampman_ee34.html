Bryce Lampman (born August 31, 1982) in Rochester, Minnesota is an American ice hockey player who is currently under contract with the New York Rangers of the National Hockey League and plays for the Hartford Wolf Pack of the American Hockey League. Lampman was drafted 113th overall by the Rangers in the 2001 NHL Entry Draft. He has spent most of his professional career with the Rangers' AHL affiliate the Hartford Wolf Pack, though he has played 9 times in the NHL, all for the Rangers.
