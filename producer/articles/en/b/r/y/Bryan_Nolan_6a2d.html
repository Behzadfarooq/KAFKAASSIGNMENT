Bryan Nolan was a fictional character in the popular BBC soap opera EastEnders. He was played by Mark Springer.

[edit] History

Charge Nurse Bryan Nolan works in the special care baby unit at Walford General Hospital. He looked after Petal Mitchell when she was born with Down syndrome. Petal's parents Billy and Honey Mitchell warmed to Bryan, and Honey was upset when he was on an NHS training course. Bryan heard that Honey wanted to see him, and left the training course. He found Billy and Honey's address and went to their house to visit Honey, convincing her to give Petal a chance. He gave Petal a hat when she left hospital to go home. He later appeared on Christmas Eve, and spoke to Billy when Petal was ill in hospital again.
