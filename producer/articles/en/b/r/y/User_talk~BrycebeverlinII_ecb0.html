[edit] Hello!

Welcome to Wikipedia, Bryce! There's a bit of a learning curve to be sure. One principle we follow is not writing autobiographical articles, and many of your first articles face deletion because of this.

As someone who has written several articles on small artists, I can assure you that our guidelines on musical performer articles are inclusive of virtually any artist that has done a tour in the United States. We just can't be having people write about themselves. I'm sure someone will think enough of you to put up an article sometime soon.

I would encourage you to continue editing Wikipedia on other topics that are of interest to you. The Wikipedia community appreciates contributions on topics you have knowledge of. Even tedious chores like spelling corrections and deleting vandalism earn you respect. So go out there and be bold! --AlexWCovington (talk) 02:21, 3 February 2006 (UTC)
