Bryan Watson (b. 18 April 1969) is the 1999-2006 DanceSport World Champion in latin dancing, professional category.

City: Durban, South Africa

Favourite Dance: Samba

Current Partner: Carmen Vincelj

South African Coach: Dave Campbell
