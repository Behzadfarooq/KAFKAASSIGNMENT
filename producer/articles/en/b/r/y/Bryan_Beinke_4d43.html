Bryan Beinke (born August 5, 1975) is a former Australian rules footballer in the Australian Football League.

He was recruited by the Adelaide Crows in the 1998 AFL Draft at pick 34 and went on to play with the Adelaide Crows between 1999 and 2002 before he retired early at just 27.
