Bryan Johnson (singer)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

For other persons of the same name, see Bryan Johnson (disambiguation).

Bryan Johnson (1936-1995) was an English singer.

The brother of the late [attribution needed] singer, Teddy Johnson, he emulated his brother and sister-in-law Pearl Carr, by coming second in the Eurovision Song Contest in 1960, with "Looking High, High, High".
