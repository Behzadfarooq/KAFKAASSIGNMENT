Yes, I've added you to my userpage. Ok:

  1. What do you want to do to your userpage? If you don't have an idea in mind, you can look at other people's userpages and steal what bits you like. Certainly my userpage is a result of me seeing various userpages on my travels and thinking "Ooh, I'd like that"!

  3. I don't know if Queerclickis a acceptable article for Wikipedia. It makes no assertion of notability - an article on Wikipedia has to have been covered by several non-trivial reliable sources. Are there any on Queerclick? And if so, then you can use the information from them to write your article and it won't be a problem. At the moment, because you run it, it's original research, which some editors can be very aggressive about removing.
