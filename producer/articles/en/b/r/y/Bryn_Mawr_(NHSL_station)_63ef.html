Bryn Mawr (NHSL station)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

This article is about the station on SEPTA Route 100. For the station on the SEPTA R5, see Bryn Mawr (SEPTA station).

Bryn Mawr Station is an interurban rapid transit station on the SEPTA Norristown High Speed Line (Route 100). The station is located at Glenbrook Avenue and County Line Road in Radnor Township, Pennsylvania. Local, Hughes Park Express, and Norristown Express trains stop at Bryn Mawr. During rush hour, some trains coming from 69th Street terminate at Bryn Mawr. The station lies near Bryn Mawr Hospital. This station is in the overlap of the two fare zones, meaning that it is a one zone ride to any other station on the route. The station lies 5.4 track miles from 69th Street Terminal. The station has off-street parking available.
