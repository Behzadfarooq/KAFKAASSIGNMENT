Bryn Mawr station

Bryn Mawr Station is an above-ground commuter rail station located in the western suburbs of Philadelphia at Morris & Bryn Mawr Aves, Bryn Mawr, Pennsylvania 19010 [1]. It is served by most R5 Paoli-Thorndale trains with the exception of a few "Limited" and express trains.

The ticket office at this station is open weekdays 6:05 a.m. to 6:05 p.m. excluding holidays. There are 46 parking spaces at the station.

This station is in fare zone 3 and is 10.1 track miles from Suburban Station. In 2003, the average total weekday boardings at this station was 822.
