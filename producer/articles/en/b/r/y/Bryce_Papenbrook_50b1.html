Bryce Papenbrook (February 24, 1986 - ) is an American voice actor. He is the son of Debbie Rothstein and the late Bob Papenbrook, both of whom are also voice actors. Like his parents, Bryce does voices primarily in anime dubbing and video games. He is sometimes credited as Bryce Rothstein.
