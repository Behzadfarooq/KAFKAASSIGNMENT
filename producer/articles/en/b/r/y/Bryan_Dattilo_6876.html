Bryan Ronald Dattilo (b. July 29, 1971, Kankakee, Illinois) is an American actor. He is best known for his role as Lucas Roberts on the NBC daytime soap opera Days of our Lives, a role he played from 1993 to 2001 and returned to in 2002.

Besides his regular appearance on the soap, he starred in a number of independent films, including the award-winning short film, Gaydar.

[edit] Biography

[edit] Early Life

Dattilo was born in Illinois, subsequently moving to Los Angeles at the age of 7. He eventually began to tag along with his older sister at acting class, though reluctlantly, this began his first experience within the business.

[edit] Current Life

Dattilo currently resides in North Hollywood as a single father raising his son, Gabe. He is currently playing the role of "Lucas Roberts", a role which he originated in 1993, on Days of Our Lives.

[edit] Major role to date

  • 1993 to March 27, 2001
  • February 2002 to July 2002;
  • September 2002 to present
