Bryn Gunn (born August 21, 1958 in Kettering) is a former footballer who played as a full-back for a number of clubs between 1975 and 1992. He is best remembered for his time with Nottingham Forest, where he won the European Cup in 1980. His daughter, Jenny, plays cricket for England, and was part of the Ashes winning team of 2005.
