Bryan Rennie (1954 - ) is the Vira I. Heinz Professor of Religion at Westminster College (PA). Known for his groundbreaking works on Romanian scholar Mircea Eliade, Rennie was awarded the Mircea Eliade Centennial Jubilee Medal for contributions to the History of Religions by Romanian president Traian Basescu in 2006.
