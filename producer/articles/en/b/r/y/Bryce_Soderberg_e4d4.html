Bryce Soderberg

Bryce Dane Soderberg (born April 10, 1980 in Victoria, British Columbia) is the bassist for Los Angeles rock band Lifehouse.

Soderberg is also the bassist/vocalist for independent LA band Tomorrow And Everyday After,(on hiatus) and a former member of the band AM Radio. Currently, Bryce is living in Los Angeles working on Lifehouse's fourth album.
