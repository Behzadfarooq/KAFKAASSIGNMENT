Bryn Mawr (disambiguation)

Bryn Mawr (Welsh for 'big hill') may refer to the following places:

  • Brynmawr - a market town in Blaenau Gwent, Wales
  • Bryn Mawr - a farm near the Welsh town of Dolgellau that belonged to Rowland Ellis, a Quaker who emigrated to Pennsylvania
  • Bryn Mawr, Pennsylvania - a city in Pennsylvania, USA
  • Bryn Mawr, California
  • Bryn Mawr, Minneapolis - an official neighborhood of the City of Minneapolis, Minnesota, USA
  • Bryn Mawr-Skyway, Washington - a census-designated place
  • Bryn Mawr Historic District, a section of the Edgewater community area in Chicago, Illinois, USA
  • Bryn Mawr Station, a rapid transit station on the Chicago 'L' serving the Bryn Mawr neighborhood


Bryn Mawr is also the name of the following schools:

  • Bryn Mawr College - a women's liberal-arts college located in Bryn Mawr, Pennsylvania, USA
  • Bryn Mawr School - a college-preparatory school in Baltimore, Maryland, USA
