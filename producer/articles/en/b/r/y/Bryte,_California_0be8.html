Bryte is a former town in Yolo County, California, now forming part of West Sacramento. It is located just west of the Sacramento River in the eastern portion of the county. Bryte's ZIP Code is 95605.

Bryte was named after Mike Bryte an early dairy farmer on whose land the town grew up. [1]
