Brynn Laurel Thayer (born October 4, 1949 in North, Dallas, Texas) is an American actress who has had numerous roles in a variety of television series, most notably Matlock portraying Matlock's daughter, Leanne MacIntyre. She is the daughter of William Paul Thayer, a Lieutenant Commander in the US Naval Reserve, and Margery Schwartz Thayer.
