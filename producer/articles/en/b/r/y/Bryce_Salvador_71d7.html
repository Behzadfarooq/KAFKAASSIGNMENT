Bryce Salvador (born February 11, 1976 in Brandon, Manitoba) is a professional ice hockey defenceman who currently plays for the St. Louis Blues of the NHL.

Salvador was drafted in the 6th round, 138th overall by the Tampa Bay Lightning in the 1994 NHL Entry Draft.
