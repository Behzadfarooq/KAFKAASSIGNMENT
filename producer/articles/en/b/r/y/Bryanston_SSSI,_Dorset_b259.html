Bryanston SSSI, Dorset

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Bryanston SSSI (grid reference ST974070) is a 0.3 hectare biological Site of Special Scientific Interest in Dorset, notified in 1977.

It is used as a roost site by Greater Horseshoe bats.
