My name is Bryan. Bryan the Magnificent is something I started calling myself a while ago. I copied it from Suleiman the Magnificent. I live in Gresham, Oregon, which is the largest suburb of Portland, Oregon and fourth largest city in Oregon. It is a town of almost 100,000 people. I’m fifteen and a freshman. I go to a Christian private school. I learn a lot and enjoy going there. We have three days a week Tuesday-Thursday but we have a lot of homework and projects to make up for the two days off. The school has some notable faculty but not worthy of Wikipedia articles.

My main interests are history, Portland and Gresham Oregon, Native American tribes, and sports. Unfortunately, I don’t know anything about history or Native Americans that’s not already in Wikipedia so I can’t contribute. I can contribute to sports though, mainly basketball.
