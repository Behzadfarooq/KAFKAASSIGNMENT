Bryan Vetell born (July 26, 1977 in New York, New York) is an American professional mixed martial arts (MMA) fighter. He is currently fighting for the New York Pitbulls of the International Fight League.

[edit] Bio

Vetell was a Philosophy major at Hofstra University. He began wrestling in high school but didn't get the itch to compete again until a few years after college. On June 30, 2006 he fought in his first pro bout against Chris Clarke and won by a keylock submission.
