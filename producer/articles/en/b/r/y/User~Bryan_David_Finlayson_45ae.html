Bryan David Finlayson (1985 - ) Born in the snowy morning of February 5th in Arlington, Virginia. He is the son of David Finlayson - a prominent classical trombonist with the New York Philharmonic - and Debra Finlayson, a New Jersey elementry school teacher. Bryan's parents divorced in 1990 when Bryan was five years old. His father moved to a 65th street and Broadway appartment in New York and later remarried to Cathrine Toth, a freelance clothing disigner. Cathrine was the mother of two boys, Christian and Sasha Toth. from her previous marrage. In the mean time Bryan attended public school in Oakland, NJ for one year before transfering to Ridgewood, NJ in second grade. He went on to graduate from Ridgewood High School with the class of 2003. After graduating from High School, Bryan attended William Patterson University where he studied Classical Guitar Performance with Gabriel Handy. After his year at William Patterson University, Bryan was accepted as a Freshman to North Carolina School of the Arts, an prestigeous performing arts school where he studdied Classical Guitar Performance with classical guitar virtuoso Gerry Klickstien. Feeling the stresses of an music performance concentration, Bryan began to develope a strong intrest in Journalism. Bryan left North Carolina school of the Arts at the end of the accademic year in May of 2005. He is now attending Rowan University as a Junior Transfer student. Bryan is happy with his current life as a Journalism student and writes frequently for the school student newspaper The Whit.
