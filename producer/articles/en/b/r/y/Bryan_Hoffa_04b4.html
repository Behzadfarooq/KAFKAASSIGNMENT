Bryan Hoffa

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

For other uses, see Hoffa (disambiguation).

Bryan Hoffa is an American studio engineer who has worked with Camper Van Beethoven, Cracker, Carbon Leaf, Patrick Phelan, South, Tanakh, The Saints, as well as Neil Hagerty & The Howling Hex.

Hoffa is also a stakeholder in Richmond, Virginia's Sound of Music Studios, which was founded by David Lowery, John Morand, and Miguel Urbiztondo.
