Bryan Paul Bullington (b. September 4, 1980, in Indianapolis, Indiana) is a Major League Baseball right handed pitcher who currently plays for the Pittsburgh Pirates, and attended Ball State.

Bullington was the first overall pick in the 2002 draft by the Pirates. He was the first player from the Mid-American Conference to be selected with the first pick in a major sport's draft.

He has only pitched 1.1 innings in the majors as a number of injuries have prevented him from playing much early in his career. He is currently on the Disabled List recovering from surgery.
