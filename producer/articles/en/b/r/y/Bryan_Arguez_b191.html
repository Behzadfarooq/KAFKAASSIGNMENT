Bryan Arguez (born 13 January 1989 [1]) is an American soccer from Miami [2] who currently plays in MLS for D.C. United.

[edit] Career

[edit] DC United

Bryan was selected in the 2007 MLS SuperDraft in the first round (11th overall) [3]

[edit] International

Bryan started 2006 with the U-17 U.S. Men's National Team and made six appearances before he was moved up to the U-20 U.S. Team. With the U-20's he has earned five caps, three of which were starts [4].
