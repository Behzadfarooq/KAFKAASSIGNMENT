[edit] Singer's sexual orientation

Various anon IP editors keep removing the mention of Singer's homosexuality from the "Biography" section. I'm not sure why — he's not closeted, and even discusses how growing up gay influenced his interpretation of the X-Men films in the BBC interview linked at the bottom of the page. Sure, it's not the be-all and end-all of his identity, but it's verifiable and relevant and ought to remain in the article. —Josiah Rowe (talk • contribs) 22:27, 14 November 2005 (UTC)

Just keep restoring it. Since they never explain why they are deleting that particular detail, who knows what they are up to. AlistairMcMillan 04:44, 15 November 2005 (UTC)
It's probably just because they think it's not true, because it usually is vandalism.--86.43.64.115 16:48, 1 March 2006 (UTC)
An intext note would probably help.--Fallout boy 23:47, 16 March 2006 (UTC)
Good idea. —Josiah Rowe (talk • contribs) 03:46, 17 March 2006 (UTC)
