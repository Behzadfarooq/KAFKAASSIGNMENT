Bryan Larsen (born February 12, 1975) is an American artist - specifically a painter in the realm of contemporary romantic realism. As is appropriate to this style, many of his works depict the characters (or representations) from the novels by Ayn Rand

He studied illustration at Utah State University with Damon Denys.

In his own words:

My goal is to portray the heroic and romantic in human nature and human achievement in a realistic style and a modern setting. I place particular emphasis on composition, technique, realistic detail, proper craftsmanship and consistency of style

[edit] Art

Some of his works include:

  • Self Absolution of the Titan
  • Motive Force
  • By the Fountain
  • Young Builder
  • Born With Wings
