Brynäs IF is a Swedish football club, and the football section of Brynäs IF, located in Gävle. The club, formed 1912, has played one season in the highest Swedish division, Allsvenskan (1974), and has won the Swedish Championship once, in 1925. The club merged with Gefle IF and played under the name Gefle IF/Brynäs between the 1979 and 1981 seasons, but the merger was split up in 1982.

[edit] Achievements

  • Allsvenskan:
    • Best placement (14th): 1974
  • Svenska Mästerskapet:
    • Winners: 1925
