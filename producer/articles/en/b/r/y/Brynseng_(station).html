Brynseng is a station on the Oslo T-bane system located in the Gamle Oslo borough. The station is shared by three lines, Østensjøbanen (Line 3), Furusetbanen (Line 2) and Lambertseterbanen (Line 1 and 4). The station has four platforms. The two northernmost platforms are for trains on Østensjøbanen and Furusetbanen. Lambertseterbanen uses the two other platforms before turning south and leaving the other two lines. At Brynseng station are train yards for the subway company.
