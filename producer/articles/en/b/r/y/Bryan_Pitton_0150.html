Bryan Pitton is an ice hockey goaltender for the Brampton Battalion of the Ontario Hockey League. He is also a prospect for the Edmonton Oilers of the National Hockey League.

Bryan was born January 26th 1988 in Mississauga, Ontario. He is 6 feet and 1 inch tall and weighs 167 pounds. Bryan enjoys working out, fishing and listening to music. His favourite player is former goaltender Patrick Roy and his favourite team are the Toronto Maple Leafs.

He had great statistics for a rookie goaltender in the OHL this year with 16 wins and 4 losses, including a save percentage of .904 %. Pitton had a very good pre-season with the Oilers but got sent back down to develop, especially with his young age, he is far from being ready for the NHL.

He was drafted in the 5th round, 133rd overall by the Edmonton Oilers in the 2006 NHL Entry Draft in Vancouver.

His older brother Jason played for the Guelph Storm, where he was traded to from the Sault Ste. Marie Greyhounds. Jason is a prospect of the New York Islanders.
