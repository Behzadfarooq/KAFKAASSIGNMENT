I have make extensive changes to our Lord of the Rings article in an attempt to work it up to Featured Article status. In order to find more suggestions I have listed it under Wikipedia:Peer Review. Under the guidelines to nominations I am required to notify others of knowledge in the area to review it. I have decided that the best group for this would be our WikiProject. [edit] Kurdistan

Hi, and thank you for the message at my talk page.

It is indeed a relevant question which articles should be under the category. My immediate answer would be any entity and subcategory related to the region of Kurdistan. However, for now I find it very important to counter attempts to simply empty the category. The category has previously had some quite open opposition. One user listed the entire category for deletion, see the debate at Wikipedia:Categories_for_deletion/Log/2006_March_3#Category:Kurdistan. When it seemed clear that there was a vast majority for keeping the category, the same user stated : This vote means little to me. It is inflated to begin with. No other category vote has this many votes. Also there is a pov infestation. I am not accusing anyone of anything but I find it odd that the majority of the keep votes came from Kurds and Greeks. So no I dont care about this vote at all. I have no reason to keep nonsense like this on wikipedia, I will eventualy get it deleted, watch me. --Cool CatTalk|@ 11:48, 10 March 2006 (UTC). Such explicit attempts to obstruct a decision taken after a debate must be countered, and I believe all Wikipedians do have a responsibility in implementing decisions.

May I suggest a more general discussion at Category_talk:Kurdistan, and in the meantime keeping an eye on the related articles. Cheers. Bertilvidet 13:23, 1 February 2007 (UTC)
