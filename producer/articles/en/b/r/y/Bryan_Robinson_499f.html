Bryan Robinson (born June 22, 1974 in Toledo, Ohio) is an American professional football player who currently plays for the Cincinnati Bengals of the National Football League. A 6'4", 304 lbs. defensive end from Fresno State, Robinson has also played for the St. Louis Rams, Chicago Bears, and Miami Dolphins during his NFL career.

Robinson made one of the most memorable and emotional plays in recent Chicago Bears history on November 7, 1999. Playing at Green Bay in the first Bears game since the death of Walter Payton, he blocked a 28-yard Ryan Longwell Field Goal attempt in the final seconds to preserve a 14-13 victory over the archrival Green Bay Packers.
