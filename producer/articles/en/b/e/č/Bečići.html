Bečići (Бечићи) is a town within the municipality of Budva, Montenegro. It is located north-east of Budva, and has around 800 permanent residents (2003 census).

Bečici beach

It has a 1,950m long sandy beach, which is among most beautiful beaches in Montenegro and the southern Adriatic. In 1935 it won the "Grand Prix" in Paris as the most beautiful beach in Europe.

The town of Bečići has received a large amount of investment in recent years, most notably with the building of the luxurious "Splendid" hotel, which cost 70 million euros to build. In addition, other large hotels were mostly refurbished.
