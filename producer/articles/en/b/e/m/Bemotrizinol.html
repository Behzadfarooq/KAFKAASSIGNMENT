Bemotrizinol (USAN, trade name Tinosorb® S, INCI bis-ethylhexyloxyphenol methoxyphenyl triazine) is an oil soluble chemical which is added to sunscreens to absorb UV rays. It is a broad spectrum UV absorber, absorbing UVB as well as UVA rays. Currently it is not approved by the United States Food and Drug Administration, but is approved in the European Union and some other parts of the world.[1]
