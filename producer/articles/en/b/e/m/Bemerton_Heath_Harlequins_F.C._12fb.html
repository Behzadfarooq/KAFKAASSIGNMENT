Bemerton Heath Harlequins F.C. is an English association football club based in Salisbury, Wiltshire. For the 2006-07 season, they are members of the Wessex League Premier Division.

[edit] History

The club was established in 1989 by the merger of three clubs, Bermerton Athletic of the Wiltshire League, Moon F.C. of the Salisbury & District League, and Bemerton Boys of the Mid-Wilts League. The new club joined the Wessex League in the same year and have remained in this league to the present day. In the 1998-99 season, they reached the 5th round of the FA Vase.

[edit] Club records

  • Best league performance: 3rd in Wessex League (single division at this time), 1992-93
  • Best FA Cup performance: 3rd qualifying round, 1992-93
  • Best FA Vase performance: 5th round, 1998-99
