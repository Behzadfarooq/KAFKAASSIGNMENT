Bega channel

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Bega Channel is the first navigation channel built in Romania to serve the city of Timişoara. Its name comes from the Bega river. It crosses the territory of Timiş County in western Romania and proceeds into the territory of Serbia, flowing into the river Tisa.
