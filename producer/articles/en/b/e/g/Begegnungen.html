Begegnungen is a compilation album by Brian Eno, Dieter Moebius, Hans-Joachim Roedelius, and Conny Plank, collecting various album tracks released elsewhere.

[edit] Track listing

  1. Roedelius - Johanneslust (4:58)
  2. Dieter Moebius & Conny Plank - Two Oldtimers (6:58)
  3. Eno-Moebius-Roedelius - The Belldog (6:14)
  4. Dieter Moebius - Nervös (3:26)
  5. Moebius-Plank-Neumeier - Pitch Control (6:21)
  6. Cluster - Dem Wanderer (3:52)
  7. Cluster & Eno - Schöne Hände (3:02)
