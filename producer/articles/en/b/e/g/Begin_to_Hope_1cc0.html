Begin to Hope is the fourth album by Regina Spektor. It was released June 13, 2006. The album debuted at number 70 on the Billboard 200, but due to the popularity of the single "Fidelity" the album rose to number 20 and was labeled a "pace setter" by Billboard. Rolling Stone magazine has named it the 21st best album of 2006.

[edit] Single Success

In 2006, Regina Spektor's first single, "Fidelity," premiered on VH1. Soon after, VH1 included her among "You Oughta Know: Artists on the Rise" [1].

Regina Spektor performed "Fidelity" on Late Night With Conan O'Brien in July 2006.

Begin to Hope has sold 380,000 copies so far in the U.S., 75,000 copies in the UK, over 185,000 copies in Canada, and more than 1,200,000 copies worldwide making it Regina's best-selling album to date.[citation needed]
