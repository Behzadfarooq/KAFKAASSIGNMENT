Beginnings is the title of Yes guitarist Steve Howe's first solo album. It was released in 1975.

[edit] Track listing

  1. Doors of Sleep (4:08)
  2. Australia (4:13)
  3. The Nature of the Sea (3:57)
  4. Lost Symphony (4:41)
  5. Beginnings (7:31)
  6. Will o' the Wisp (6:00)
  7. Ram (1:53)
  8. Pleasure Stole the Night (2:57)
  9. Break Away From It All (4:19)
