Beggen, in northern Luxembourg City.

Beggen is a quarter in northern Luxembourg City, in southern Luxembourg.

In 2001, the quarter had a population of 2,297 people. Beggen is home to FC Avenir Beggen, an historically successful football club that now plays in the second-tier Division of Honour.
