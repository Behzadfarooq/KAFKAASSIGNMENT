Simpleflow2007 00:45, 4 November 2006 (UTC)== Summary == Name: Simple Flow

Genre: Rap/Hip-Hop

Albums: Beginner's Luck Release (November 17, 2006

Website: www.Myspace.com/btbb4life

Short Biography: Tommy J. Capozziello Jr. born 1991 in bridgeport Ct, grew up as a "white kid" and went to Blackham School from Pre k to 8th grade. In 4th grade at the age of 9 he found an old copy of an eminem CD and played it in his stereo. he says that "eminem was what motivated him to write lyrics". that year he started to write his own lyrics not only about himself but his situation and what he seen. He loved to write music but he figured he wasn't that great but kept writing. By the time he hit 11 he had a notebook fill of raps and of course they got better and he showed improvement. In 2005 when he turned 14 he found out a lot about music since he's been writing for 5 years and new how to record music and got the feel of it. So that winter he bought himself an Mp3 player with a microphone built in. this gave him the opportunity to record songs. so he jumped on the computer and downloaded a couple instrumentals and tried it out. The beat and his voice didn't come in so clear but it was a start. He did it all winter and by spring 2005 he had two songs out on his myspace page. "stressed Out" and "T-cap Freestyle Mix" at that time his name was T-Cap. When the summer of 06' hit he realized he needed to start real recording with real equipment. So for about a year until the following summer he saved about 700 dollars. At 15 years old he spent 700 dollars with enough faith in himself and his future career he bought a mic and amp, headphones wires and cables software and more. Finally when he got it too work about a week he after installing it and getting it ready he hopped in his closet and started recording. His first full mic recording was "Ahead Of Life" which brought a lot of attention from friends and family but only about 20 people. Now in November 2006 he is working on his Demo "Beginner's Luck" with 7 songs at a Official Release Date of 11/17/06 for approx. $6. he hopes to sell 50 copies which will bring him about 300 dollars and enough for some professional studio time and some real made beats and maybe some left over to buy 50 more CDs to make another $300!

So if you wanna listen to him go to www.myspace.com/btbb4life and check out the songs videos, pictures and new album releases.
