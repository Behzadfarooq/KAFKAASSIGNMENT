[[Baix Llobregat]]

Begues is a municipality in the comarca of the Baix Llobregat in Catalonia, Spain. It is situated in the south-west of the comarca, and the municipal territory covers most of the Garraf massif including the peaks of el Montau (658 m) and la Morella (594 m). It is an important tourist centre, and is connected with Avinyonet del Penedès and with Gavà by a local road.
