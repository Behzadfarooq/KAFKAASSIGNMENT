Benjamin Nicholas (born December 10, 1987, in Adelaide, Australia) is an Australian actor. He played Scott "Stingray" Timmins on the Australian Soap opera Neighbours. Raised in Adelaide, South Australia, he currently resides in Melbourne, Australia where Neighbours is filmed. According to co-star Matthew Werkmeister, some of Stingray's more individual expressions such as "hufter" and "Belgium" (as an expression of dismay or irritation) were coined by Ben himself. It was rumoured that Ben had planned on leaving Neighbours for some time due to wanting to further his career in movies. His last performance as 'Stingray' was aired in Australia on 23rd March 2007. Nicholas travelled from Australia to the UK to perform Pantomime over the Christmas period.
