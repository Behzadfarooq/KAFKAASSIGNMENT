Eric Charles 'Ben' McKay (29 December 1918 in Cambridge, Tasmania - 11 July 1976, Hobart) was an independent member of the Tasmanian Legislative Council from 9 May 1959 until his death in the Electoral division of Pembroke.

In the following by election he was succeeded by his son Peter McKay.
