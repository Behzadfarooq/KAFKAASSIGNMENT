Ben Griffin (born March 7, 1986 in Brisbane, Queensland) is an Australian football (soccer) player who plays as a central defender. He currently plays for the Queensland Roar in the A-League competition. He represented Australia at Under 17, Under 20 and Under 23 level. While he did not play for Roar in the 05/06 season, he was a monumental defender for the most part of the Roars' 2006-07 season.

He went to school at Marist College Ashgrove in Brisbane.
