Beniaján is a nice village located in the Garden of Murcia (Spain), beside the mountains that close the Valley of Segura. It has a population around 10,000 inhabitants and it's close to the capital, Murcia. Beniaján is known worldwide by the abundant production of citrus fruits that its factories exports around the world, the most important industrial activity in this city.
