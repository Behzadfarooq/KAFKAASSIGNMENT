[edit] Summary

This picture is taken on the 23rd-2-2006 in the city of Benghazi following a protest against police killing 13 people following the 17th-2-2006 protest in front of Italian embassy in Benghazi. This pictur is taken in front of the main emergency hospital (Al Jala).
