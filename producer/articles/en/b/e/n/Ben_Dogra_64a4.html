Widely regarded as one of the brightest and most successful football agents in the nation, Ben Dogra recently joined Tom Condon to head-up the Football Division of Creative Artists Agency. In 2006 Dogra led all NFL agents by representing four first round draft picks -- including No. 1 pick Mario Williams -- the third straight year that Dogra at least tied for the most first-round picks. CAA represents over 120 NFL players, including Peyton Manning, Eli Manning, Drew Brees, Matt Leinart, Marvin Harrison, Tony Romo and Shaun Alexander.

Dogra graduated from George Mason University with a B.S in Economics prior to earning his law degree from St. Louis University School of Law.

Creative Artists Agency is the largest talent agency in the world.CAA also has substantial talent resources, repping most of the town's A-list actors, writers and directors. Founded in 1975, CAA represents some 2,500 clients working in film, television, theater, music, sports and literature.
