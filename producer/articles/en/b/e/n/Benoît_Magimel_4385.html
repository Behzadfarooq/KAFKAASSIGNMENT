Benoît Magimel (born May 11, 1974) is a French actor.

  • 1994: L'Incruste, directed by Emilie Deleuze
  • 1995: La Fille seule, directed by Benoît Jacquot
  • 1995: La Haine, directed by Mathieu Kassovitz
  • 1996: Les Voleurs, directed by André Téchiné
  • 1996: Quinze sans billets, directed by Samuel Tasinaje
  • 1997: Papa, directed by Laurent Merlin
  • 1998: Déjà mort, directed by Olivier Dahan
  • 1998: Warning, directed by Nicolas Klein
  • 1998: Une minute de silence, directed by Florent Emilio Siri
  • 1999: Elle et lui au 14e étage, directed by Sophie Blondy

  • 1999: Les Enfants du siècle, directed by Diane Kurys
