Ben Cullum

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Ben Cullum is a UK based musician, composer and producer of Anglo-Burmese background, brother of Jamie Cullum. He acquired a music degree in London[citation needed] and worked for a short time as a session bass guitarist. Soon after, he joined a UK-based video games developer, Rare Ltd, and worked for 4 years as a composer for console games, providing the voice for characters including Falco Lombardi from the Star Fox series. Currently Ben is working as a full-time songwriter/programmer/producer/musician. His first single "Canâ€™t Get Better Than This" was released in the spring of 2005, on house label DTPM Recordings and has been remixed by Dab Hands and Ashley Beedle. His music is published by Chrysalis Records, and he is the composer of "These Are the Days", the debut single by his brother Jamie Cullum.

Ben is currently working and touring with The Egg. Although he has been hard at work collaborating with various musicians. He has 2 cuts on Ferry Corsten's hit album L.E.F, they are 'Beautiful' and 'Forever'. Three songs written by Ben can be found on Kyle Eastwood's new album, 'Now'. He has also recorded bass, organ and guitar on three of the All Saints tracks.
