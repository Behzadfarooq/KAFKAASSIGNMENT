Benji Radach (born in Castlerock, Washington) is an American professional mixed martial arts (MMA) fighter. He is currently fighting for the Los Angeles Anacondas of the International Fight League.

[edit] Bio

As a former UFC fighter, Radach spent the last couple of years away from mixed martial arts after suffering a severely broken jaw in a fight against Chris Leben. He is well-known in the fight world as a promising young prospect whose career was halted by an unlikely injury. Radach is now fighting at middleweight for the Los Angeles Anacondas and has won his first two IFL fights.

[edit] Outside MMA

In 2006 Radach foiled an armed robbery attempt in Vancouver, Washington by pummeling and disarming a gunman who was trying to hold up a restaurant that Radach was eating in.[1]
