[edit] AfD

Ah, I see now! You're missing out on Step II on this page. Here's the procedure:

  • Tag the article with {{subst:afd1}} (you did that)
  • Save the page (you did that)
  • Now read the box that appears. It says something like "please discuss this on this article's entry". That line is in red. Click it!
  • You get taken to a blank page. In there, type: {{subst:afd2 | pg=Romeo (song) | text=Reason}} ~~~~ replacing "Reason" with why you think the article should be deleted.
  • Save that page
  • Now do what you were doing before: adding {{subst:afd3 | pg=Romeo (song)}} to the bottom of the page of today's articles for deletion.

Hope that helps. The process is a bit cumbersome, but it needs to be for transparency. ➨ ЯEDVERS 16:02, 12 August 2006 (UTC)
