Bent Christensen is the name of several persons:

  • Bent Christensen (1929-1992), a Danish film director.
  • Bent Christensen (1963), a former Danish football (soccer) player for Lyngby Boldklub and the Denmark national football team.
  • Bent Christensen Arensøe (1967), nicknamed "Turbo", another former Danish football player, most notable for winning Euro 1992, and playing for Brøndby IF.
