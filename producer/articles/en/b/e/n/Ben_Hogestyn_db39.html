Ben Hogestyn (born April 27, 1983 in Petaluma, California) is an American soap opera actor.

Hogestyn landed the role of Lucas Jones on General Hospital from September 2005 to June 2006.

Hogestyn began appearing on The Bold and the Beautiful in a contract role of Harry Jackson beginning August 28, 2006, but was soon demoted to recurring status in early November 2006.

Hogestyn is the stepson of Days of our Lives star Drake Hogestyn.

Hogestyn began his career under his birth name Ben Schieferstein, but later began using Hogestyn professionally.
