Ben Krass (1919 - June 7, 2004) was co-owner of Krass Brothers clothing store on South Street in Philadelphia.

He was known throughout the Philadelphia area for appearing in numerous zany television commercials for the store, from the 1960s to the 1980s. The four commercials below were taped at Channel 17 studios in Philadelphia in the late 1990s.
