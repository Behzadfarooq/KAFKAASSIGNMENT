Benjamin Stoloff (6 October 1895, Philadelphia, Pennsylvania, USA - 8 September 1960, Hollywood, California) best known as Ben Stoloff was an American film director and producer. He began his career as a short film comedy director and gradually moved into feature film directing and production later in his career.
