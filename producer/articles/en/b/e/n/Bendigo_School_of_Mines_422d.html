Bendigo School of Mines

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Bendigo School of Mines is located in Bendigo, Victoria, Australia and was established in 1873.

[edit] Notable past students

  • John Scaddan, c.1889-1895, later Premier of Western Australia
  • Edward Heitmann c.1896, later Australian M.P.
  • John Michael Higgins (metallurgist) c.1882
