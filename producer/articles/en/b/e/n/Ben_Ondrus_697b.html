Ben Ondrus (born June 25, 1982 in Sherwood Park, Alberta, Canada) is a professional ice hockey player. He plays right wing.

Ondrus was the former captain of the Swift Current Broncos, where he was junior teammates with fellow Toronto Maple Leafs' prospects Ian White and Jeremy Williams. He was signed to an American Hockey League contract after a fine performance at the Leafs' 2003 rookie camp, and earned a contract with Toronto following an encouraging rookie AHL season with the St. John's Maple Leafs in 2003-04. Ben made his National Hockey League debut on Tuesday, March 7, 2006, versus the Montreal Canadiens. He resides in North York, Ontario with his childhood friend, author Wilson McCutchan.
