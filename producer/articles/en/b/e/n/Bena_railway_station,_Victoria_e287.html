Bena was a railway station on the South Gippsland line in South Gippsland, Victoria, Australia. The station was opened during the 1890s, Bena was later closed during the 1980's along with White Law Station.

With the current re-alignment of the South Gippsland Highway, the site of the former station will be the location of a "rail over road" bridge, due to this some services on the South Gippsland Tourist Railway have been suspended until its completion.
