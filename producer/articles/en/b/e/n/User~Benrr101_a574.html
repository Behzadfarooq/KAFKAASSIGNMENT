[edit] My Views on the Wikipedia

From my humble opinion, I feel that the Wikipedia should be a place where people can post information for the good of common knowledge. I feel that it is important to post useful information to the Wikipedia -- not information that will do people no good. I feel that information on the Wikipedia should be correctly backed up or be from a knowledgeable source. The information on the Wikipedia should be free from vandalism and all incorrect or just messed up information. I hope that someday anyone who needs to know something can turn to the Wikipedia (and other Wikimedia Foundation Projects) as the giant brain of the world.
