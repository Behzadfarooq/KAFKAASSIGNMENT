This article is about Beneath the Surface, the album by GZA. For the Stargate SG-1 episode, see Beneath the Surface (Stargate SG-1)

Beneath the Surface is a 1999 (see 1999 in music) album from Wu-Tang Clan member GZA, an American hip hop musician. It was his second album released as a member of Wu-Tang and his third overall.
