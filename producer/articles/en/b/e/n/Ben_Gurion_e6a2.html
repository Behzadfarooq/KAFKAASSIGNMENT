David Ben-Gurion was the first Prime Minister of Israel.

Several things in Israel are named after him:

  • Ben Gurion International Airport, the largest international airport in Israel.
  • The Ben-Gurion University of the Negev, with the main campus located in Be'er Sheva.
  • Midreshet Ben-Gurion - settlement in the Negev.
  • The Ben Gurion tank - a locally modified Israeli version of the Centurion tank.
  • Ben Gurion elementary schools in Rehovot and Netanya.
