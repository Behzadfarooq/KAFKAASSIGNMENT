Benjamin Diskin

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Benjamin "Ben" Diskin (born August 25, 1982 in Los Angeles County, California) is an American voice actor. He is best known as the voices of Numbuh 1 and Numbuh 2 in the animated series Codename: Kids Next Door. He also provides voices for Wilbur, a member of the Six-Gum Gang in episodes R.O.B.B.E.R.S. and E.L.E.C.T.I.O.N.S. He has appeared in The Wonder Years, Hey Arnold!, Mr. Saturday Night, Kindergarten Cop, Baby Boom, and Just Like Dad. He also voiced Hahn in Avatar: The Last Airbender. He voiced Eddie J. Squirrel in Squirrel Boy.

In video games, he voiced Jupis Tooki McGanel in Rogue Galaxy.

He can currently be seen on Blood+ playing Kai Miyagusuku.
