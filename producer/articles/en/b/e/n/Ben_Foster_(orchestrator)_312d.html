Ben Foster (orchestrator)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Ben Foster

Ben Foster is a British orchestrator and conductor who is best known for his work as an orchestrator on the BBC series Doctor Who.

[edit] Career

Since 2006, he has served as an orchestrator for the BBC series Doctor Who, and has collaborated with the show's musical director Murray Gold on several other projects (most notably Torchwood). [1]

He also conducted "Doctor Who: A Celebration", which was held at the Wales Millennium Centre on November 19, 2006 in aid of Children In Need, which was accompanied by a CD release of the Doctor Who Soundtrack, released December 11, 2006 on Silva Screen Records. [2]
