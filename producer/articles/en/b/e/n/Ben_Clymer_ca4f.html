Ben Clymer (born April 11, 1978 in Bloomington, Minnesota) is a winger currently (2005) with the Washington Capitals of the NHL. He attended and played hockey for Bloomington Jefferson High School and the University of Minnesota.[1]

He was formerly with the Tampa Bay Lightning and was part of the 2004 Stanley Cup Championship team. He also played one season with the EHC Biel in the second Swiss division.
