Benešov (disambiguation)

Benešov is name of several locations in the Czech Republic:

  • Benešov, a town in Central Bohemian Region and the capital of Benešov District
  • Benešov, a village in South Moravian Region (Blansko District)
  • Benešov nad Černou, a village in South Bohemian Region (Český Krumlov)
  • Benešov nad Ploučnicí, a town in Ústí nad Labem Region (Děčín District)
  • Benešov u Semil, a village in Liberec Region (Semily District)
  • Dolní Benešov, a town in Moravian-Silesian Region (Opava District)
  • Horní Benešov, a town in Moravian-Silesian Region (Bruntál District), until 1926 named just Benešov
This disambiguation page lists articles about distinct geographical locations with the same name. If an internal link led you here, you may wish to change the link to point directly to the intended article.
