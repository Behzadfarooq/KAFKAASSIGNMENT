Benito Armando Archundia Téllez (* 21 March 1966 in Tlalnepantla, Mexico) is a Mexican football arbitrator recognized by FIFA. He started his international arbitrator career on 1 January 1993. He officiated his first international match in May of 1994 with the USA against Greece. During the FIFA world championship 2006, he officiated the meetings between Brazil against Croatia, France against South Korea, Tschechien against Italy and the round of the last sixteen between Switzerland and Ukraine.
