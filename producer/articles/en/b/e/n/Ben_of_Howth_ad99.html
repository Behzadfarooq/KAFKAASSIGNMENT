The Ben of Howth is a hill on Howth Head. It is 171 metres high, lying approximately 1Â½ km to the south of Howth. The nearest road is Windgate Road, from which a path leads west past Green Hallows quarry.

It is sometimes confused with Shielmartin Hill, which lies approximately 1 km to the west.
