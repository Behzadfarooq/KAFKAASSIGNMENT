[edit] The Wikipedia:WikiProject Musicians/Categorization guidelines

  • As part of a bit of a clean-up of Category:Welsh musicians, created 22 various Welsh music related categories (including Category:Welsh classical musicians, Category:Welsh harpists, Category:Welsh musicians by genre and Category:Welsh pianists. (Only one Welsh musician left in the top-level category to place: Syd Howells - can you help?!) I then discovered the categorization guidelines, and was inspired...
  • All oboists (50 or so names) were previously in Category:Oboists with no sub-categories. I put them into appropriate national / genre sub-categories, and discovered the existence of Category:Rock oboists! 35 new categories.
  • All violists (70 or so names) were previously in Category:Violists with no sub-categories. I put them into appropriate national / genre sub-categories, and this time found I had to create Category:American blues violists! 76 new categories were needed (but getting quicker this time with practice)
  • Similarly, Category:Bassoonists - 25 categories - and, after a though-provoking comment by User:Badagnani, I added List of bassoonists to try and retain the usefulness of being able to see all bassoonists on Wikipedia on one page, as you could before the category was "processed".
  • Category:Harpists - 47 categories
  • Various other categories created for other instruments of multi-instrumentalists as I go through - 22 for flautists, 9 saxophonists, 7 violinists, 5 clarinetists, 2 trombonists - more work needed in these areas.
  • Plus 41 "parent" categories for these to fit into e.g. Category:Fooian classical musicians by instrument or Category:Fooian musicians by genre
  • Plus some "tidying" of the existing category trees when I spot problems.
