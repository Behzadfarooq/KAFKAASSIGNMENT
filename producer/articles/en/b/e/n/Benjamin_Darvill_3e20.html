Benjamin Darvill

Benjamin Darvill (born January 4, 1967, in Winnipeg, Manitoba) plays harmonica, mandolin, acoustic and electric guitar, and more for the Canadian rock band, Crash Test Dummies.

Since 2000, he has been busy with his solo project "Son of Dave," which resulted in three albums.
