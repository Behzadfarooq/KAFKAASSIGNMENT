[edit] Info

Hi, this is Ben 10. I've been using Wikipedia for a couple of years now, but I still don't have much of a working knowledge about how to do everything. I usually have to request things like pictures in an article, so please be patient with me.

Here is some info about me:

  • I am 18 years old.
  • I'm a guy.
  • I'm a Christian, and I love every minute of it.
  • I am dangerous with a whoopie cushion.
  • I am an expert in the practical use of movies. My favorite method is when my little brother, known to Wikipedia as Bass.exe, is playing a game on his laptop and he's supposed to be taking a bath. All I have to do is pop just about any movie with "boy/girl interaction" (don't worry, I always keep it G-rated) into the DVD player and he's in the tub before you can say "yuck." He especially hates Bambi or Lady and the Tramp, which is a big part of why I like them so much. Will it work for others? Let me put it this way: I haven't needed to do it in months. If you use this method, let me know.
  • I'm a cryptozoologist, and believe that Mokele-mbembe may have a connection with the Biblical Behemoth.
  • I am a Zoo Tycoon addict, especially of the expansion packs. I often plan downloads of my own, even though I never learned to use the A.P.E. and the original game is technically "outdated."
  • I prefer truth over being politically correct, and I generally call 'em as I see 'em. So sorry if I offend you, but facts are facts. The way I see it, there is no such thing as a closet Christian, and it's pretty much impossible to be openly Christian these days without offending someone.
  • I never swear if I can avoid it.
  • I support President George W. Bush.
  • I am a dragon fanatic and, although I don't believe in astrology, I was born in the year of the dragon on the Chinese zodiac. By the way, would someone please tell me how to ditch the link at the bottom that says I'm intersted in Chinese Astrology? Because the truth is, I'm not.
  • I coined the use of the term Double-take in reference to a scene in a sequel, prequel, or midquel that is similar to one in the original.
  • I'm into comic strips, especially ones like Garfield or Calvin and Hobbes. I even draw a comic in my high school's newspaper based on the latter, and hope to set up a website ASAP.
  • I tried to make an article for a book series I am working on about a dragon named Arredd, but no dice. I promise to knock it off until the books are official and a website exists. My apologies to anyone I gave a hard time. However, anyone curious can post questions on the talk page.
