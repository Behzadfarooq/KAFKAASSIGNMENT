Beniardá (Valencian: Beniardà; Spanish: Beniardá) is a municipality in the comarca of Marina Baixa in the Valencian Community, Spain. The economy of Beniardá is exclusively based on agriculture. The most important monument in the town is the Catholic church of Sant Joan Babtista, built in the 16th century.
