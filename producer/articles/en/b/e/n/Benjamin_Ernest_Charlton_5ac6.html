Benjamin Ernest Charlton

Benjamin Ernest Charlton born: 12 April, 1835 in Brant County, Upper Canada. died: 12 March, 1901. He was mayor of Hamilton, Ontario from 1867 and 1873-1874. He was also a teacher, manufacturer, politician. Buried in Hamilton Cemetery. [1]

[edit] Tribute

Charlton Avenue, a street in the lower city of Hamilton, Ontario was named after him.
