Benjamin Maurice Silverstone (born London, April 9, 1979) is a British actor.

Silverstone's biggest movie is Get Real, in which he played a 16-year-old gay boy named Steven Carter who falls in love with high-school jock John Dixon. Carter later discovers that Dixon is also hiding his sexuality. This has become a very popular "coming-out" movie. When he was age 14, he played Taplow in the remake of the The Browning Version.

He is also a stage actor, and has appeared in productions such as The Tempest by William Shakespeare. Silverstone also played Jack Kipling in a 2004 production of My Boy Jack.

His last project is Jump!, in which he plays a young Jew, Philippe Halsman, charged with the murder of his father.
