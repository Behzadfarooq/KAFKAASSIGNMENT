Benton Community School Corporation

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Benton Community School Corporation administers the one high school/middle school and three elementary schools in Benton County, Indiana. Its offices are located in the county seat of Fowler, Indiana.

[edit] History

In the fall of 2006, the students of Fowler Elementary School and Oxford Elementary School began attending a new consolidated school named Prairie Crossing located in Fowler.
