Bentall Centre (Vancouver)

Bentall Centre logo.

The Bentall Centre is the collective name for five office towers, plus the basement mall, located in downtown Vancouver, British Columbia. The fifth tower is being constructed in two phases: the first phase was completed in September of 2002, and the second phase is currently under construction.

[edit] Services

Under the complex, there is a shopping mall, including a food court. The mall has a direct connection the the Burrard SkyTrain station.

[edit] One Bentall Centre

One Bentall Centre.

One Bentall Centre is located at 505 Burrard Street in Vancouver.

It stands at 86 m or 22 storeys tall and completed in 1967.

[edit] Two Bentall Centre

Two Bentall Centre.

Two Bentall Centre is located at 555 Burrard Street in Vancouver.

It stands at 70 m or 18 storeys tall and completed in 1969.

[edit] Three Bentall Centre

Three Bentall Centre.

Three Bentall Centre is located at 595 Burrard Street in Vancouver.

It stands at 122 m or 32 storeys tall and completed in 1974.

[edit] Four Bentall Centre

Four Bentall Centre.

Four Bentall Centre is located at 1055 Dunsmuir Street in Vancouver.

It stands at 138 m or 35 storeys and was completed in 1981. Currently it is the 7th tallest building in the city.

[edit] Bentall 5 (Five Bentall Centre)

This article or section contains information about expected future buildings or structures.

Bentall 5.
Bentall 5 from Burrard Station.

Bentall 5 (or Five Bentall Centre) is located at 550 Burrard Street in Vancouver. When completed in Spring 2007, it will stand at 140 m [1] or 33 storeys (construction is ongoing), making it the 7th tallest building in the city. The first 22 storeys were completed in September of 2002 by Musson Cattell Mackey Partnership and the floors above the 22nd floor are currently being constructed by EllisDon Construction.
