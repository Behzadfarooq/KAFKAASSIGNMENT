Bentley with Arksey

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Bentley with Arksey was an urban district in the West Riding of Yorkshire from 1911 to 1974, containing Bentley and Arksey [1]. Since 1974 it has formed part of the Metropolitan Borough of Doncaster in South Yorkshire. Prior to this it had been a township.
