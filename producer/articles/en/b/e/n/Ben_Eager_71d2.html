Ben Eager (born January 22, 1984 in Ottawa, Ontario) is a professional ice hockey left winger who currently plays for the Philadelphia Flyers of the NHL. He attended Immaculata High School in Ottawa.

[edit] Playing career

Eager was drafted in the 1st round, 23rd overall by the Phoenix Coyotes in the 2002 NHL Entry Draft. He was later traded by Coyotes along with Sean Burke and Branko Radivojevic for centre Mike Comrie.
