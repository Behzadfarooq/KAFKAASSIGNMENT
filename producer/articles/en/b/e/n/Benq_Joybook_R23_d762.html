Benq Joybook R23

The Benq Joybook R23 is a laptop computer from electronic manufacturer Benq.

[edit] Features

It has a 15" XGA TFT screen and 1024 mebibytes DDR2 memory. Storage can be done on a 80 gigabyte harddisk and the processor is a Mobile AMD Turion 3200+ MT.
