This short essay is about the proposed Wikipedia policy Wikipedia:Offensive

I support this proposal because WP:NOT states clearly that Wikipedia is NOT censored. [1] I also believe that removing content because it is offensive to some people would distort Wikipedia's NPOV policy, and you should also remember that it would remove information from Wikipedia. Wikipedia is a resource for providing free information.

I could personally take the article on Westboro Baptist Church offensive myself because of my sexual orientation, but I don't. Why? It is an encyclopedia. It is providing information on Westboro Baptist Church. And the article should provide all of the information it can provided it remains NPOV.

[edit] What should we do?

I say we should simply accept the proposal as policy. There's not much more to say apart from that.
