Beneath the Valley of the Ultra-Vixens is a satirical sexploitation film starring Uschi Digart and Kitten Natividad written and directed by breast fetishist Russ Meyer. Released in 1979, this was Meyer's penultimate and most explicit film. The screenplay was written by Roger Ebert.

The glam metal band Faster Pussycat released an album called Between the Valley of the Ultra Pussy, a play on this movie's title, considering their band is named after another Russ Meyer film, Faster, Pussycat! Kill! Kill!.
