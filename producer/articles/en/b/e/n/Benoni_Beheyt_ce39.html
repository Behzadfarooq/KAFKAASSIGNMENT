Benoni Beheyt

Benoni Beheyt (born 27 September 1940, Zwijnaarde) is a Belgian former professional road bicycle racer who raced from 1962 to 1968. Beheyt won 22 races over his career and is most famous for winning the 1963 World Cycling Championships Road Race and a stage win at the 1964 Tour de France.
