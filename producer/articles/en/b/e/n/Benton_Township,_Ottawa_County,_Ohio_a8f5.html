Benton Township, Ottawa County, Ohio

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Location of Benton Twp, Ottawa Co, Ohio

Benton Township is one of the 12 townships that make up Ottawa County, Ohio. It is located in the western end of the county, and forms part of the boundary with Lucas County. Benton Township borders the following townships and municipalities:

  • Allen Township (northwest)
  • Carroll Township (east)
  • Clay Township (southwest)
  • Harris Township (south)
  • Jerusalem Township, Lucas Co. (north)
  • Salem Township (southeast)
  • The village of Rocky Ridge is completely surrounded by the township.

Benton Township is also the location of the Ottawa National Wildlife Refuge and Crane Creek State Park.
