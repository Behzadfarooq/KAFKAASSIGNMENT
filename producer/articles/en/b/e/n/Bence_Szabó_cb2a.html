Bence Szabó

Olympic medal record
Men's fencing
Gold 1988 Seoul Sabre team
Gold 1992 Barcelona Sabre individual
Silver 1992 Barcelona Sabre team
Silver 1996 Atlanta Sabre team

Bence Szabó (born 13 June 1962) is a Hungarian fencer, who has won four Olympic medals in the sabre competitions.
