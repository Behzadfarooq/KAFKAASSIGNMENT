Benasque

Benasque (Benás, in the local dialect) is a town in the province of Huesca (Spain), located in the heart of the Pyrenees, 143 km north-east of Huesca. It is part of the Ribagorzan shire.

Local Patués dialect is transitional between Catalan and Aragonese.
