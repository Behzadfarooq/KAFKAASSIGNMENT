Not to be mistaken with Hong Kong actor Benny Chan Ho Man.

Benny Chan is a Hong Kong movie director, producer and writer.

Benny Chan was nominated for "Best director" at the 24th Annual Hong Kong Film Awards for the movie New Police Story.

Filmography As A Director:

  • A Moment Of Romance (1990)
  • Son on the Run (1991)
  • What A Hero! (1992)
  • The Magic Crane (1993)
  • A Moment of Romance 2 (1993)
  • Happy Hour (1995)
  • Man Wanted (1995)
  • Fist of Fury (1995)
  • Big Bullet (1996)
  • Who Am I? (1998)
  • Gen-X Cops (1999)
  • Gen-Y Cops (2000)
  • Heroic Duo (2003)
  • New Police Story (2004)
  • Divergence (2005)
  • Rob-B-Hood (2006)
