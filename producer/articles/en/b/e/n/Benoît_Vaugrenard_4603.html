Benoît Vaugrenard (born January 5, 1982 in Vannes) is a French cyclist who races for Française des Jeux in the UCI ProTour.


[edit] Major achievements

2006 – Française des Jeux
  • Best Young Rider, After Stages 1, 2 & 6 – Tour de France
  • Best Young Rider, After Stage 2 – Paris-Nice
