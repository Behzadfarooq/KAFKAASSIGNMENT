Benoit Cheyrou (born 3 May 1981) is a footballer from France who currently plays as midfielder for AJ Auxerre in the French Ligue 1.

[edit] Trivia

  • Benoït's older brother Bruno currently plays for Rennes, having previously played for Liverpool.
