Benton, Mississippi

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Benton, Mississippi, is a small town in Yazoo County in the U.S. state of Mississippi. Mississippi Highway 433 encircles the community. Benton is also served by the Zip Code 39039. Benton was the county seat of Yazoo County from 1829 to 1848. It is home to Benton Academy, one of only two major private schools in Yazoo County.
