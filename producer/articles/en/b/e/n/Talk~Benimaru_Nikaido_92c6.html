[edit] Are these viable sources?

I noticed that my trivia on the Polymar/Benimaru connection was removed. Now since Hurricane Polymar is a ridiculously obscure series that I doubt many have watched I couldn't find much for evidence with the exception of two things. First is an FAQ on Tatsunoko Fight over at GameFAQS which is here: http://db.gamefaqs.com/console/psx/file/tatsunoko_fight.txt.This is an old PSX game that featured many Tatsunoko characters one of which is Polymar. Scroll down to his section and you can see that of the four moves listed the three that have been given to Beni are listed (one of which is mispelled but can be rectified with the second source). The second source is the Holy Blood-Hurricane Polymar OVA that came out in 1996 and is infinitely easier to track down than anything TV series related. In the OVA Polymar does a variant of the Shinkuu Katategoma although he never says the name and towards the end of the second Episode he does the Gen'ei Hurricane and does yell out the attack name which helps to fix the error in the FAQ. Phew. Now are these two things good enough for sources?

Oh and the JoJo connection is stated in the KOF 10th Anniversary site in Beni's profile although the comic and character are never named but its an obvious and well known fact.

Ammended on my talkpage. -ZeroTalk 04:45, 1 June 2006 (UTC)
