Ben Wilkinson (born 25 April 1987 in Sheffield, England) is a professional footballer, who currently plays for Hull City. Wilkinson is the son of former Sheffield Wednesday and Leeds United manager Howard Wilkinson. He formerly played with Sheffield United and Derby County and appeared in FIFA 07. He also appears on Football Manager 2007 and has very good dribbling stats.
