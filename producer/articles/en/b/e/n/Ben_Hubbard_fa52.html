Ben Hubbard

Ben Hubbard is a minor fictional character from the Superman films. He is a neighbor who looks after Martha Kent in Smallville while Clark is in Metropolis. The character was mentioned in the first Superman movie, but appeared in the movie Superman Returns, played by James Karen.

His role in Superman Returns was very small, especially since his main scene was cut from the theatrical release.
