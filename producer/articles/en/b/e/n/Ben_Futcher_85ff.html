Ben Futcher (born 4 June 1981 in Bradford) is an English professional footballer currently playing for Peterborough United.

Futcher, a defender, joined Peterborough from Grimsby Town on 24 August 2006 for an undisclosed fee believed to be around Â£50,000

Futcher's other previous clubs are; Oldham Athletic, Lincoln City, Doncaster Rovers and Stalybridge Celtic.

He is the son of ex-Manchester City, Oldham Athletic and Grimsby Town defender Paul Futcher.
