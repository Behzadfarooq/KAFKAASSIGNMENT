Bent Gestur Sivertz, O.B.E., B.A. (Victoria, British Columbia August 11, 1905 - October 4, 2000 Mayne Island) was a Canadian sailor, teacher, soldier, and civil servant.

He was Commissioner of the Northwest Territories from July 12, 1963 to January 16, 1967.
