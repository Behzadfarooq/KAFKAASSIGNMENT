Benjamin C. Dawkins, Sr. (19 July 1881-22 August 1966)[1]

Appointed to the United States District Court for the Western District of Louisiana, 5th Circuit by President Calvin Coolidge in 1924.[1]

[edit] Education and Career

  • Completed his law degree at Tulane University, 1906[1]
  • Private practice, Monroe, Louisiana, 1906-1912[1]
  • Judge, Louisiana District Court, 1912-1918[1]
  • Associate justice, Supreme Court of Louisiana, 1918-1924[1]
