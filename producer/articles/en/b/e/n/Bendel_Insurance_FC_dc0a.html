Bendel Insurance Football Club, also known as Insurance of Benin Football Club, is a soccer club based in Benin City, Nigeria. They ended their 2004/5 season 12th in the 20-member Nigeria Premier League [1]. Their venue, Samuel Ogbemudia Stadium, has a capacity of some 20,000 [2]

[edit] Achievements

  • Nigeria Premier League: 2

1973, 1979

  • Nigerian FA Cup: 3

1972, 1978, 1980

  • CAF Cup: 1

1994
