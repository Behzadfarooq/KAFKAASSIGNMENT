Benjamin F. Jonas

Benjamin Franklin Jonas (1834 – December 21, 1911) was a politician in Louisiana and an officer in the Confederate States Army during the American Civil War.

Jonas was born in New Orleans, Louisiana. He enlisted in the army during the early days of the Civil War and initially served in the Fenner Artillery. He was later promoted to the rank of major. He was a member of the Louisiana state house of representatives in 1865.

Following the war, he served as a U.S. Senator during Reconstruction as a Democrat from 1879–85.
