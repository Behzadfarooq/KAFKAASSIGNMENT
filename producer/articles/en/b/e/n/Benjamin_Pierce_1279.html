Benjamin Pierce may refer to:

  • Benjamin Pierce (governor) (1757-1839), a governor of New Hampshire in the 1820s, and father of U.S. President Franklin Pierce
  • Benjamin Pierce, (1841-1853), the last surviving son of U.S. President Franklin Pierce; died in a train accident just before his father's inauguration
  • Benjamin C. Pierce, a professor of computer science at the University of Pennsylvania
  • Benjamin Franklin Pierce, a fictional character, Hawkeye Pierce, in the film M*A*S*H and television series M*A*S*H
