Benkovce

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Location of Vranov nad Topľou District in the Prešov Region.

Benkovce is a village and municipality in Vranov nad Topľou District in the Prešov Region of eastern Slovakia.

[edit] History

In historical records the village was first mentioned in 1363.

[edit] Geography

The municipality lies at an altitude of 138 metres and covers an area of 8.239km². It has a population of about 544 people.
