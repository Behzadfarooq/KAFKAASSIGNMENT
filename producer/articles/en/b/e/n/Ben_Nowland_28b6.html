Ben Nowland (born May 27, 1980 in Jacksonville, Florida) is an American football player who plays center, most recently for the Denver Broncos. Nowland played collegiately with Auburn before signing as an undrafted free agent with the Denver Broncos. Nowland played with the Grand Rapids Rampage of the AFL prior to the 2006 NFL season.
