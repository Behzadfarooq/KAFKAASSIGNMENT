[edit] the MODFATHER of CIRCUITBENDING and audio mastermind

BENT MONKEY CAGE, CIRCUIT BENDING ,a California DIY Synthesizeer ODDITORIUM of LIVE Noise [Circuit Bent] Performances and FREE Circuit Bent Instruction*

His daily walk, and/or derive, involving circuit bending, is from conception, to design, to creating, to producing, to directing, and to live performances in California.

Lee Daniel Perry at bMc has been modifying Electronic Musical Instruments for 42 years. His platform is 3rd generation trained in the realm of electronic wizardry. Lee also did his engineering thesis (University of Louisville 1977)on CIRCUIT Warping. He then went on tour as a Professional Drummer, traveling many major venues in the USA as a UNION Musician."DEVOID of LOGIC", the FIRST BOOK ever written on Circuit Bending copyright USA library of congress 2003

This disciple of noise, bMc, has added "tech" to the word BENDING on the planetary map. Electronic syncopated NOISE WITH THE USE OF CIRCUIT BENT MUSICAL INSTRUMENTS, is becomming a mandatory obsession, in the alternative Music and Theatre Production genres.
