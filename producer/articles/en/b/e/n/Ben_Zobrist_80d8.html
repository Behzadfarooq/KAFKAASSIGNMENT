Benjamin T. Zobrist (born May 26, 1981 in Eureka, Illinois) is an American baseball player and currently plays in Major League Baseball for the Tampa Bay Devil Rays. He is a switch-hitter but throws right-handed. Zobrist usually plays shortstop.

Ben played college baseball for Dallas Baptist University.
