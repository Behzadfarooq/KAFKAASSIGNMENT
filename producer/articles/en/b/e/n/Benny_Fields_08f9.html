Benny (Bennie) Fields (born Benjamin Geisenfeld, June 14, 1894 in Milwaukee - died August 16, 1959 in New York City) one half of the legendary Vaudeville team of Blossom Seeley and Benny Fields. Their lives were made into a famous movie, Somebody Loves Me (1952) with Betty Hutton and Ralph Meeker.
