Ben Laurie

Ben Laurie is a founding director of The Apache Software Foundation, a core team member of OpenSSL, a member of the Shmoo Group, a director of the Open Rights Group and Director of Security at The Bunker Secure Hosting. He wrote Apache-SSL, the basis of most SSL-enabled versions of the Apache HTTP Server and is a co-author of OpenPGP:SDK.

Ben also writes articles and papers, many of which can be found on his home page or his blog and is interested in Ideal Knots.
