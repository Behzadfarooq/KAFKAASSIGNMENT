[edit] Mission statement

I pledge above all to serve God and make the most of every opportunity He presents to me. I acknowledge that Jesus Christ died on the cross for my sins, and that He did it because He loves us in the way He wants us to love each other. I pledge to do what is right, what is honorable, and what is good, and to keep doing so whether people want me to or not, dagnabbit.
