I'm Ben Mills. My home is in Odell, a village in North Bedfordshire, England. Between 2004 and 2006 I was studying (Natural Sciences) at King's College, Cambridge. I've had a hard time with depression in the past few years, so much so that I decided to leave the stress of Cambridge and transfer to Bristol in 2007, to continue my studies in chemistry.

In the meantime, I am working at Sharnbrook Upper School, my former school.

Most of my editing is to chemistry articles. I've been adding images of the molecular structures of some chemical compounds, especially to articles which previously lacked an appropriate image. I use Wikipedia all the time to find the structures of molecules, so I feel I am returning the favour by adding structures where none existed before!

I also have my own website, Benjamin-Mills.com.

And remember: wherever you are, whoever you are, look around you.
