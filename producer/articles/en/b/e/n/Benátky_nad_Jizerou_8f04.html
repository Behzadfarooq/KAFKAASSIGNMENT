Benátky nad Jizerou (IPA: [ˈbɛna:tkɪ ˈnadjɪzɛroʊ̯], German: Benatek) is a town on the Jizera river in the Central Bohemian Region of the Czech Republic, between the cities Stará Boleslav and Mladá Boleslav.

The city was the site of a castle and observatory built by astronomer Tycho Brahe.
