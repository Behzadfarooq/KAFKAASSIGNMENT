For a while now I've been quite inactive, the stress of multiple jobs college etc was taking its toll and I guess the final nail in the coffin was my personal laptop blowing up. But I am back now with a new laptop and much more free time, although on my return I seem disappointed in some of the issues that seem to be occurring. I think perhaps people need to learn to chill a bit and rember were all here for one purpose, writing an encyclopaedia. People can contribute to this in multiple ways, be it maintaince based like AFD vandal fighting or content addition or a mixture of both. What doesn’t help us build an encyclopaedia is in-fighting, bickering over trivial issues or using wikipedia as an online battleground. This has the opposite effect it simply pushes contributors out and encourages drama, which does nothing to aid our ultimate goal; it makes us MySpace.
