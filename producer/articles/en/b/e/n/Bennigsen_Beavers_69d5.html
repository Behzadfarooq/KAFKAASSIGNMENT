The Bennigsen Beavers is a German Baseball and Softball team in the Springer Ortsteil Bennigsen, southwest from Hannover.

The Bennigsen Beavers were created in 1993 as a department of FC Bennigsen and, as of 1 January 2004, has become an independendent sporting team. In the 2005 season, altogether 7 teams participated in various competitions. As of the middle of 2005, its various teams have 97 Members.
