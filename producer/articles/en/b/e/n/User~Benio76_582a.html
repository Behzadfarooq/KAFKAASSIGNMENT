[edit] I was born in Rome...

Roma, il Pantheon


I was born in Rome thirty years ago. I've been educated as a real Humanist: I studied Latin, Ancient Greek, History, Music and finally Philosophy, which is my current field of specialisation. My editings will be mostly related to these fields, but not only... who knows?

There is an ancient sculpture in Rome, called "la Bocca della Verità" (the Mouth of Truth). It can be found in the portico of Santa Maria in Cosmedin, the church where my parents got married. The legend tells that those who will put their hand in its mouth and lie, will have their hand cut off! I already tested it and my hands are both at the end of my arms. And you?
