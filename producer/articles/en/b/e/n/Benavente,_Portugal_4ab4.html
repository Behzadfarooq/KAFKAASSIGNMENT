Benavente (pron. IPA: [bɨnɐ'vẽt(ɨ)]) is a municipality in Portugal with a total area of 521.0 km² and a total population of 25,266 inhabitants.

The municipality is composed of 4 parishes, and is located in the district of Santarém.

The present Mayor is António José Ganhão, elected by the Unitarian Democratic Coalition.

The municipal holiday is Ascension Day.

[edit] Economy

Agriculture, livestock farming, forestry, and food industries are the main actvities. The Companhia das Lezírias is headquartered in the municipality, in Samora Correia.
