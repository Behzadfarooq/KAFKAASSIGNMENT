[edit] Basic Information

Hi, I'm Ben here, a history major from Singapore.

Intellectual influences include Max Weber, Edward Said, Thomas Jefferson, AJP Taylor, *李敖 and Samuel P. Huntington. An unapologetic relativist but accept that some views are more true in varying extent than others. Interests include postwar Southeast Asian nationalism, societal issues, history of Christianity, football, nature amongst others.
