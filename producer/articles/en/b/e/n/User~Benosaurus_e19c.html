Hello! My name is Benosaurus, and I evolved from a Synapsid reptile. But that was a really long time ago. Right now, I am 14 years old. My name on all other Wikis is "V-Rex" (except for Lyriki, where I am known as "MusicalSpinosaurus").I am the co-founder of the Club Penguin Wiki.

You may call me a "Nibble-Editor." I put in small things here and there in Wikipedia. I've also been trying to beef up some of the dinosaur articles. One of my goals is to put pictures on the dinosaur articles to give people a better understanding of them. See my contributions at http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Special:Contributions/Benosaurus.
