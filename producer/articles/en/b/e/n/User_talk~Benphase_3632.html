[edit] Article deletion

Your article was deleted under CSD-A7. CSD-A7 means "Criteria for Speedy Deletion #7 - Unremarkable people or groups. An article about a real person, group of people, band, or club that does not assert the importance or significance of its subject. If the assertion is disputed or controversial, it should be taken to AFD instead. (See Wikipedia:Deletion of vanity articles for further guidance on this criterion)."

Wikipedia is not a collection of random information and weblinks. It is an encyclopedia. As such, Wikipedia has rules on the notability of articles it contains, including guidelines such as WP:WEB for the significance of websites that gain their own articles. Your forum is not significant or notable under those rules.
