Ben Gill (born 9 October 1987 in Harrow[1]) is an English central midfielder who is currently plays for Watford. Ben Gill attended John Lyon School.

He started his career as a youth player at Arsenal but was released in July 2005 and joined Watford's Academy. Whilst still an Academy scholar he made his first team debut in a League Cup victory against Wolverhampton Wanderers on 20 September 2005, and has gone on to make two further cup appearances.

In October 2006 he joined Conference Cambridge United a month's loan, in order to cover injuries sustained by the Yellows.[2] He was involved in 4 matches, starting 3 times.
