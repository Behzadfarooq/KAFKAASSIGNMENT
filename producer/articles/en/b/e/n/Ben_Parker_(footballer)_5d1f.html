Ben Parker (born November 15, 1987) is a young English footballer who is currently on a season long loan from Leed United at Bradford City. He is a defender in the centre back position though can also play at left-back.

Parker has yet to make his competitive debut for the Leeds first team, however played in the majority of their 2006-07 pre-season fixtures.

On 5 October 2006 Leeds caretaker manager John Carver said that if Ben's appearances were being limited at Bradford then the club would rather he came back to Leeds and fought for a 1st team place there than sitting on the bench at Valley Parade[1]. However, Parker's loan spell was extended in late December 2006, indicating new Leeds manager Dennis Wise wants Parker to gain as much experience as possible before plunging him into the new look Leeds side. Parker has yet to make his competitive debut for Leeds.
