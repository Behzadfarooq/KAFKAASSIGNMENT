Benjamin Köhler (born August 4, 1980 in Berlin) is a German footballer playing with Eintracht Frankfurt in Bundesliga. Köhler who is left-footed can play flexibly on all offensive positions often plays on the left wing. After getting just one appearance with Hertha BSC Berlin he had a one year loan stint with Duisburg he finally moved to Regionalliga side Rot-Weiss Essen where he scored 13 goals. After season Eintracht Frankfurt eyed him and finally signed him. In the promotion season he netted 7 goals in 29 games. In the next season he had as many games while scoring 3 goals, one which was among the goal of the week choices.
