Ben Helfgott (b. 22 November 1930) is a survivor of the World War II holocaust.

Born in Poland, Helfgott emigrated to England after the war. He would eventually become a campaigner for Holocaust Memorial Day.

He represented Great Britain at weightlifting in 1956 Summer Olympics at Melbourne, Australia and was a bronze medal winner at The 1958 British Empire and Commonwealth Games held in Cardiff, south Wales July 18-26 1958.

He was a guest on UK's BBC Radio 4's Desert Island Discs programme on 1st April 2007.
