The Ben Boeke Arena (often shortened to "The Boeke") is an 1,100-seat multi-purpose arena in Anchorage, Alaska. The arena opened in 1974. It is named after former Anchorage City Clerk Benjamin W. Boeke, who served from 1947-1967, under 11 mayors and 8 city managers. The arena is owned by the Municipality of Anchorage and operated by SMG, a nationwide property management company. It is also located approximately next-door to the Sullivan Arena.
