Ben Guerens (born 24 December, 1979, in Melbourne, Australia) is an actor.

Geurens graduated from NIDA in 2004. At drama school his credits include The Return, Romeo and Juliet, Much Ado About Nothing and Who's Afraid of the Working Class.

Guerens, best known for playing Toby Mangel on the soap opera Neighbours from 1990-1993 has also appeared in television shows The Man from Snowy River, Body Melt and Blue Heelers.

He currently plays Ash Nader in Home And Away and is appearing in the Melbourne Theatre Company's production of Entertaining Mr Sloane, in which he plays the lead role of Mr Sloane.
