Benjamin J. Rabin (June 3, 1896 - February 22, 1969) was a Democratic member of the United States House of Representatives from New York.

Rabin was born in Rochester, New York. He served in the United States Navy during World War I from May 30, 1917 until January 1919. He graduated from New York University in 1919. He was elected to Congress in 1944 and served from January 3, 1945 until his resignation on December 31, 1947. He served on the New York Supreme Court from January 5, 1948 until his death in Palm Beach, Florida.
