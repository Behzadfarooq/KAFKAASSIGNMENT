Description

en:Benjamin Franklin National Memorial is located in the rotunda of the en:Franklin Institute in en:Philadelphia, Pennsylvania at approximately 39°57′30″N, 75°10′23″W. The 20 foot high marble statue was sculpted by en:James Earle Fraser circa 1938. Image cropt from a en:National Park Service file. [1]
