Ben Georgia

Ben Georgia (born May 5, 1941) is an award-winning abstractionist painter and art gallery owner.[1] An art specialist, since 1986 Georgia has been listed in Art in America's Guide To Museums, Galleries & Artists.[2] Important collectors have purchased Georgia—including multiple works by Jack Welch and Abigail Johnson—throughout the United States, Canada, Germany, Netherlands and UK. His work hangs in the galleries of prominent corporate art collections, including those of Castrol, Schering-Plough and Ricoh. His work is in galleries in Toronto, Houston and St. Louis. His West Orange gallery is a 3,000-square-foot (280 m²) space that exhibits things.
