Benidorm Club Deportivo is a Spanish football team based in Benidorm in the autonomous community of Land of Valencia. Founded in 1964, they play in Segunda División B - Group 3. Their stadium is the Estadio Municipal de Foietes, which has a capacity of 6,000.

[edit] Seasons

  • 2004/2005: Segunda División B 12th
  • 2005/2006: Segunda División B 7th
  • 2006/2007: Segunda División B
  • 14 seasons in Segunda División B
  • 14 seasons in Tercera División
