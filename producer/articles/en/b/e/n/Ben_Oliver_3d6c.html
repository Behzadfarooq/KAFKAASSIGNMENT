Ben Oliver

Ben Oliver is a comic book artist who has worked for 2000 AD on Judge Dredd as well as providing art for The Authority, The Losers and Ultimate X-Men

[edit] Bibliography

  • Judge Dredd (with John Wagner):
    • "You're a Better Man Than I Am, Gunga Dinsdale" (in 2000 AD #1210, 2000)
    • "Bring Me the Heart of P. J. Maybe" (in 2000 AD #1211, 2000)
    • "Dead Ringer" (in Judge Dredd Megazine, #3.68, 2000)
  • The Authority: Human on the Inside (with John Ridley, Wildstorm, 2004, ISBN 1-4012-0069-9)
  • The Losers (with Andy Diggle, collected in Close Quarters, collects #20-25, 144 pages, 2006 ISBN 1-4012-0719-7)
  • Tom Strong #33: "The Journey Within" (with Joe Casey, 2005, collected in Tom Strong Book 6, hardcover, December 2006, ISBN 1-4012-1108-9)
  • Ultimate X-Men (with Robert Kirkman, Marvel Comics):
    • "Phoenix?" (in Ultimate X-Men #69-71, collected in Ultimate X-Men Volume 14: Phoenix?, 2006)
    • "Cable" (in Ultimate X-Men #75-78?, 2006)
