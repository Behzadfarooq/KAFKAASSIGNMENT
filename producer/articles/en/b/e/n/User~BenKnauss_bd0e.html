Benjamin Knauss, born Jason Knauss, (17 July 1971-Present); is an American mystic, hedonist, and sexual revolutionary.

Knauss himself claims to be a Elks member, a Cub Scout Leader and an avid Rock Climber.

[edit] Early years

Benjamin Knauss was born in Cameron Memorial Hospital, Angola, Indiana, Indiana, between 4:00am and 6:00am on 17 July 1971.

His father, James Knauss, worked as an electrician in a brass works. His mother, Margory Knauss, was a stay at home mother until he was five, and the family seperated. His mother was Baptist, member of the most extreme wing of the Southern Baptists.

Benjamin attended Grace Baptist Academy, Angola, Indiana, for his first 5 years of schooling, later moving to Lakewood Park Christian School in Auburn, Indiana for sixth grade and Howe Military School in Howe, Indiana to complete his schooling.

[edit] Software Development Years

Ben worked on such products as Microsofts Windows 2000, the Informix SE Database Sierra Online's Police Quest 3, Informix Wingz and the Apple IPod.


[edit] Later years

Ben made his living as a Software Engineer and Project Manager.
