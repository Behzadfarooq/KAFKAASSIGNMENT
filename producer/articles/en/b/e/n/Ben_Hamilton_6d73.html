Benjamin Thomas Hamilton (born August 18, 1977 in Minneapolis, Minnesota) is an NFL offensive lineman currently playing for the Denver Broncos. He played in the prep ranks at Wayzata High School in Minnesota and in college at the University of Minnesota, where he earned a Bachelor's Degree in civil engineering.

While at the University of Minnesota, he was selected as a Playboy All-American, but refused to pose for a picture with the team due to his religious convictions. Ben's father, Wes Hamilton is a former offensive linesman for the Minnesota Vikings.
