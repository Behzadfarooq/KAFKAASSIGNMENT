At Benchmark Hospitality International, things are different. Each of our vast collection of "resorts", "hotels" and conference centers is truly unique, and offers its guests a one-of-a-kind experience in its own distinctive style and setting. One thing, however, all Benchmark properties share is an unwavering commitment to setting the standard for quality and service. It is the difference that makes people choose Benchmark time and time again.


[edit] About us

Benchmark Hospitality International has set the standard for integrity, quality and service in management and marketing services for resorts, conference centers, conference hotels and golf clubs. In our twenty-five year history, we've learned a great deal about our business, about what makes a great conference center, hotel or conference resort and what doesn't. Early on in our quest to become the company we are today, we developed a philosophy for our endeavors, a formula for success that you'll find at each of our properties, throughout our literature and as the foundation of our logo.

“It has become the Benchmark legacy, creating the balance of Living, Learning and Leisure™.” We realized early on that the core function of a Benchmark conference hotel or conference resort was to provide an environment that would facilitate idea exchange, problem-solving and retention of learning. The accomplishment of this threefold task within a professional business environment free from the distractions and interruptions of daily business life, requires that the property be designed, managed and marketed to meet the specific needs of a meeting planner's objectives for the company's meeting. Many metropolitan hotel properties and resort destinations do not wish to meet these criteria, nor do they plan the facilities or management focus to accommodate them. It is an area of specialization that requires a unique and dedicated approach. It is the niche market within the hospitality industry in which we choose to take the lead, win recognition among our peers and earn accolades from our customers.

[edit] Owners & Developers

Benchmark's growth philosophy is based upon the conviction that maximum long-term profitability is the result of an optimum balance of financial considerations, product and guest experience, and human resource considerations. The policies and procedures in place throughout all Benchmark operations reflect this balance.

Benchmark Hospitality continues to grow under the following guidelines:

•Benchmark concentrates its efforts on the management of projects in targeted areas having a strong corporate presence and/or areas having strong destination characteristics

•Management staff and systems strengths are main­tained through the hiring of key personnel as well as the promotion of in-house management staff whenever possible. Every management and marketing system is evaluated on a regularly scheduled basis to guarantee and maintain Benchmark's competitive edge.

•Benchmark restricts the number of new project openings to those that can be effectively supervised and managed. This insures the level of quality and the continuity of service to our customers.

•Due to our success in meeting the goals of financial institutions and individual owners in the takeover of underachieving properties, Benchmark continues to evaluate the potential of other distressed properties that are better served by our marketing and management strength.

•When applicable, Benchmark introduces financial resources, developers and projects to each other in order to create successful partnerships.
