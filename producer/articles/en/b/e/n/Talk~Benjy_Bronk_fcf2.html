[edit] Marks Friggin as a source

Is it acceptable to use Marks Friggins Howard Stern Show site as a source for Howard Stern related wikipedia articles?

Unfortunately, no. LilDice 11:16, 14 March 2007 (UTC)
A Mark's Friggin or Rundown source is better than no source at all, but if you can find a more reliable source use it. I think both would probably be considered fan sites or vanity sites. Mark's Friggin especially. Also take a look at the talk page archives, they go over using mark's friggin, and some other issues. I think some of the stuff they say may referring to copying and pasting whole sections of Mark's Friggin instead of paraphrasing. I've been using rundowns to cite show events and to create an timeline on show people, but that is because I can't find a better source.(Optigan13 05:03, 15 March 2007 (UTC))
