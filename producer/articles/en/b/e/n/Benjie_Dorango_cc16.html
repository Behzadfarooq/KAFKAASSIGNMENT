Benjie Dorango

Benjie Dorango - He did the voice of Fei of Cooking Master Boy, Sid of Final Fantasy Unlimited, Grown-up Edward and the first voice of Arthur of Lady Lady, Doumyouji of Hana Yori Dango and Shinn Asuka of Mobile Suit Gundam Seed Destiny on Hero TV. He is one of the dubbers of Meteor Garden 1 & 2. Currently, he supplies the voice of Tracy of Tokyo Underground.
