Location of Benna in Italy

Benna is a comune (municipality) in the Province of Biella in the Italian region Piedmont, located about 60 km northeast of Turin and about 4 km southeast of Biella. As of 31 December 2004, it had a population of 1,168 and an area of 9.4 km².[1]

Benna borders the following municipalities: Candelo, Cossato, Massazza, Mottalciata, Verrone.
