Benjamin Marc Ramaroson

Bishop Benjamin Marc Ramaroson (born 25 April 1955) is the bishop of the Diocese of Farafangana in Farafangana, Madagascar. He was ordained priest on 15 August 1984 for the Congregation of the Mission. He was appointed and confirmed as bishop in November 2005.
