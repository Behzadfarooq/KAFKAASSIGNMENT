Benton Academy is an independent, co-educational college preparatory school in Benton, Mississippi. It is member of the MPSA. The headmaster of the school is Jamie Carr.

[edit] Athletics

For the athletic periods of 2006-2009 Benton Academy will participating in the Class A North District 4-A Group. Its historical athletic rivals include Central Holmes Academy, Tri-County Academy, Sharkey-Issaquena Academy, and Humphreys Academy. Tamara Vandevere serves as the Athletic Director.

[edit] Driving Directions

Take Interstate 55 to the Canton/Yazoo City exit 124. This will be Hwy 16. You will go approximately 15 miles west towards Yazoo City. Benton Academy will be on your right. You may also take Hwy 49 to Yazoo City. You will see a Benton exit. That will be Hwy 16. You will go approximately 7 miles. Benton Academy will be on your left.
