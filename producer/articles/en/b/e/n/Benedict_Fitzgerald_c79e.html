Benedict Fitzgerald

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Benedict Fitzgerald (born 1949) is an American screenwriter who co-wrote the screenplay for The Passion of the Christ with Mel Gibson. His other writing credits include a television screenplay of Moby Dick in 1998 and Wise Blood in 1979.

His latest project is Myriam, Mother of the Christ due for release by MGM at Easter 2008.
