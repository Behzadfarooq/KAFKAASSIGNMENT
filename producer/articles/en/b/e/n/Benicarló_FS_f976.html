Benicarló Futbol Sala is a futsal club based in Benicarló, city of the province of Castellón in the autonomous community of Valencian Community.

The club was founded in 1996 and her stadium is Estadio Pabellón Municipal with capacity of 2,000 seaters.

The club has the sponsorship of Ayuntamiento de Benicarló and Grupo Poblet.
