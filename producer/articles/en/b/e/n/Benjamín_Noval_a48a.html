Benjamín Noval (born 23 January 1979 in Mieres), is a professional road bicycle racer from Spain. He currently rides for the UCI ProTeam Discovery Channel Pro Cycling Team. His wide body provides his team leader a great slipstream in which to conserve their energy. He is signed with Discovery Channel through to the end of the 2007 season.
