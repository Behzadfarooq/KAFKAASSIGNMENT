My name is Benjamin Bruce, and I'm a member of the church of Christ. I'm proud to be a Texan (as you may have guessed!), and I live in a small town in Parker County.

My interests include languages & linguistics, and nature, as well as numismatics and philately. I am learning three languages (Spanish, Koine Greek, and Latin), and although I'm not yet fluent in Spanish, I am practising and hope to be in the near future.

I've got several websites, and if you'd like to look at my blog, you can see it here: BenjaBlog

To contact me, please leave a note on my talk page or e-mail me.

[edit] Projects

[edit] Language games

Currently I am working on a project to categorise as many language games as I can, and feature them on Wikipedia. I have done extensive research on language games across the world, and now I am working on organising my information and writing it up on Wikipedia. I am doing this in my spare time, of which I have very little (I publish a bimonthly newsletter and make websites, plus I have my schoolwork to do!).

[edit] Native American languages

I have done lots of research on the languages of the Native American tribes in Oklahoma for my Hello Oklahoma! project, and I am adding some of that information to Wikipedia. At the moment I am working on a table for the Cherokee language article, which can be seen on my Under construction page.
