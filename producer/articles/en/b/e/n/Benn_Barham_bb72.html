Benn Barham, born in Ashford, Kent, England (6 February 1976) is a professional golfer on the PGA European Tour [1], club pro at Chart Hills Golf Club.

In the 2006 Barclays Scottish Open at Loch Lomond, Benn achieved his highest placing on the European Tour, finishing in fifth place, with rounds of 71, 68, 65 and 70, winning Â£79,440.
