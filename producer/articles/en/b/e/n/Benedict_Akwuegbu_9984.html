Benedict Akwuegbu

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Benedict Akwuegbu (born 3 November 1974) is a Nigerian football player.

He played for a few clubs, including RC Harelbeke (Belgium), Grazer AK (Austria), Shenyang Haishi (China), FC Kärnten (Austria), FC Wacker Tirol (Austria) and Sportfreunde Siegen in Germany. He now plays for Tianjin Teda in China.

He plays for Nigeria national football team and was a participant at the 2002 FIFA World Cup.
