Benn Levy

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Benn Levy (7 March 1900 – 7 December 1973) was a Labour Party Member of Parliament in the House of Commons of the Parliament of the United Kingdom. He was first elected at the 1945 general election, for the Eton and Slough constituency, and stood down at the 1950 general election.
