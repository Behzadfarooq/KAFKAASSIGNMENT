Benoit

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Benoit is a very common catholic French male given name, as well as family name. It is sometimes spelled Benoît or, less frequently, Benoist.

Pronounced ben-wa, the name is rooted in the French word benit which means blessed. Benoit is also a common surname, often pronounced by English speakers as /bɛn.ɔɪ.ɪt/ or /bɛn.ɔɪt/.

The female form of the name is Benoîte, or Bénédicte.
