Ben & Jerry's ice cream flavors are frequently changed, with seasonal and limited batch blends (such as American Pie) as well as annual variations.

[edit] Just released

  • Stephen Colbert's AmeriCone Dream - Vanilla Ice Cream with Fudge Covered Waffle Cone Pieces & a Caramel Swirl (March 2007)
  • Willie Nelson’s Country Peach Cobbler - Peach Ice Cream with Cinnamon-Sugar Shortbread pieces (April 2007)
  • The Gobfather - Chocolate Ice Cream with Fudge Covered Almonds & a Nougat Swirl
  • Cinnamon Buns - Caramel Ice Cream with Cinnamon Bun Dough & a Caramel Cinnamon Struesel Swirl
  • Crème Brûlée - Sweet Custard Ice Cream with a Carmelized Sugar Swirl
  • S'mores - Chocolate Ice Cream with Fudge Chunks & Toasted Marshmallow & Graham Cracker Swirls

[edit] Legend

  • SE = Flavor contains pieces manufactured on shared equipment with products that contain nuts.
  • DE = Manufactured Kosher on Dairy Equipment.
  • SS = Scoop Shop only

[edit] Ice cream

  • American Pie - Apple pie ice cream with apples and pie crust pieces.
  • Bananas on the Rum – (SS) – Banana ice cream with a buttery rum-brown sugar swirl.
  • Banana Split - (Walnuts) - Banana and strawberry ice creams with walnuts, fudge chunks, and a fudge swirl.
  • Black & Tan - Chocolate & Cream Stout Ice Creams Topped with a Cream Stout Ice Cream Head
  • Bovinity Divinity - (Discontinued) Chocolate and white chocolate ice cream swirled with little chocolate and white chocolate cows.
  • Brownie Batter - Brownie Batter Ice Cream with a Rich Brownie Batter Swirl
  • Butter Pecan – (Pecans) – Rich buttery ice cream with roasted pecans.
  • Caramel Chew- Vanilla ice cream with swirls of caramel and small caramel filled chocolates.
  • Cherry Garcia – Cherry ice cream with cherries and fudge flakes.
  • Chocolate – Chocolate ice cream.
  • Chocolate Chip Cookie Dough – Vanilla ice cream with globs of chocolate chip cookie dough.
  • Chocolate Fudge Brownie – Chocolate ice cream with fudge brownies.
  • Chocolate Peanut Butter Swirl – (Peanuts, SS) – Milk chocolate ice cream with a thick peanut butter swirl.
  • Chubby Hubby - (Peanuts) - Fudge-Covered Peanut Butter-Filled Pretzels in Vanilla Malt Ice Cream Rippled with Fudge & Peanut Butter.
  • Chunky Monkey – (Walnuts) – Banana ice cream with fudge chunks and walnuts.
  • Coconut Almond Fudge Chip – (Almonds, SS) – Coconut ice cream with fudge chips and roasted almonds.
  • Coffee – Coffee ice cream made with Fair Trade Certified coffee extract.
  • CoffeeCoffee BuzzBuzzBuzz – (SS) – Coffee ice cream made with Fair Trade Certified coffee extract, with espresso bean fudge chunks.
  • Coffee Heath Bar Crunch - Coffee Ice Cream with Chunks of HEATH Bar Crunch (made with Fair Trade Certified coffee)
  • Dave Matthews Band Magic Brownies - Vanilla Ice Cream with Swirls of Raspberry & Brownies
  • Dublin Mudslide – Irish cream liqueur ice cream with chocolate chocolate chip cookies and a coffee fudge swirl.
  • Everything But The... – Vanilla HEATH Bar Crunch / New York Super Fudge Chunk / Peanut Butter Cup. A Collision of Chocolate Ice Cream & Vanilla Ice Cream mixed with HEATH Bar Chunks, White Chocolatey Chunks, Peanut Butter Cups & Chocolatey-Covered Almonds.
  • Fossil Fuel - Sweet Cream Ice Cream with Chocolate Cookie Pieces, Fudge Dinosaurs and a Fudge Swirl
  • Half Baked (2Twisted!) - (Chocolate Fudge Brownies meets Chocolate Chip Cookie Dough) A Crazy Concoction of Chocolate & Vanilla Ice Cream mixed with Fudge Brownies & Gobs of Chocolate Chip Cookie Dough
  • Honey I'm Home - Always soft vanilla with a hint of honey ice cream with little honey nuggets.
  • Karamel Sutra - A Core of Soft Caramel Encircled by Chocolate & Caramel Ice Creams & Fudge Chips
  • Mint Chocolate Chunk - Mint ice cream with fudge chunks.
  • Mint Chocolate Cookie - Peppermint Ice Cream with Oxford Creme Cookies
  • Neapolitan Dynamite - Cherry Garcia and Chocolate Fudge Brownie ice creams
  • New York Super Fudge Chunk – (Assorted Nuts) – Chocolate ice cream with white and dark fudge chunks, pecans, walnuts, and fudge-covered almonds.
  • Oatmeal Cookie Chunk – Sweet cream cinnamon ice cream with chunks of oatmeal cookies and fudge.
  • Peanut Butter Cup – (Peanuts) – Peanut butter ice cream with real peanut butter cups.
  • Phish Food – (SE) – Chocolate ice cream with gooey marshmallow and caramel swirls and chocolate fish.
  • Pistachio Pistachio - (Pistachios) - Pistachio Ice Cream with Lightly Roasted Pistachios
  • Pumpkin Cheesecake - (SS) - Pumpkin cheesecake ice cream with a thick graham cracker swirl.
  • Strawberry – (SS) – Strawberry ice cream with frozen strawberries.
  • Strawberry Cheesecake – Strawberry cheesecake ice cream with strawberries and a thick graham cracker swirl.
  • Sublime Key Lime Pie - (SS) - Key lime ice cream with a tangy lime twist, fluffy meringue swirl and pieces of pie crust.
  • Sweet Cream & Cookies – (SS) - Sweet cream ice cream with whole and broken chocolate sandwich cookies.
  • Triple Caramel Chunk – (SE, SS) – Caramel ice cream with a swirl of caramel and fudge covered caramel chunks.
  • Turtle Soup – (Cashews) – Vanilla ice cream with fudge-covered caramel cashews and a caramel swirl.
  • Vanilla – Vanilla ice cream with vanilla bean specks.
  • Vanilla Caramel Fudge - Vanilla Ice Cream with Swirls of Caramel & Fudge
  • Vanilla Heath Bar Crunch – (Almonds) – Vanilla ice cream with Heath® Bars.
  • Vermonty Python - Coffee liqueur ice cream with a chocolate cookie crumb swirl & fudge cows

[edit] Low fat frozen yogurt

  • Black Raspberry - (SS) - Black Raspberry Low Fat Yogurt with Thick Black Raspberry Swirls
  • Cherry Garcia – Cherry low fat frozen yogurt with cherries and fudge flakes.
  • Chocolate Fudge Brownie – Chocolate low fat frozen yogurt with fudgy brownies.
  • Half Baked – Chocolate & vanilla low fat frozen yogurts with fudgy brownies and chocolate chip cookie dough.
  • Phish Food – Chocolate Frozen Yogurt with Gooey Marshmallow, a Caramel Swirl & Fudge Fish
  • Vanilla – Creamy low fat vanilla yogurt.

[edit] Sorbet

  • Berried Treasure - Chunky Blueberry and Blackberry Sorbet Swirled with Zesty Lemon Sorbet.
  • Berry Berry Extraordinary – (DE) – Swirls of blueberry fruit sorbet and raspberry fruit sorbet.
  • Jamaican Me Crazy - (DE) Chunky pineapple sorbet with a passion fruit swirl.
  • Lemonade Stand – (DE, SS) – Lemon sorbet.
  • Mango Lime – (DE, SS) – Mango sorbet with a hint of lime.
  • Strawberry Kiwi – (DE) – Strawberry & kiwi fruit sorbet.

[edit] No sugar added

  • Vanilla Fudge Chip – (SS) – Vanilla ice cream with fudge chips, sweetened with Splenda.

[edit] Organic

  • Chocolate Fudge Brownie - Organic Chocolate Ice Cream with Organic Fudge Brownies
  • Strawberry - Organic Strawberry Ice Cream
  • Sweet Cream & Cookies - Organic Sweet Cream Ice Cream with Organic Chocolate Sandwich Cookies
  • Vanilla - Organic Vanilla Ice Cream

[edit] Novelties

  • Chocolate Fudge Brownie Milkshake - A milk beverage all shook up with rich chocolate and other natural flavors.
  • Chunky Monkey Milkshake - A milk beverage swirled with bananas, chocolate and other natural flavors.
  • Cherry Garcia Milkshake - A milk beverage swirled with cherry, chocolate and other natural flavors.
