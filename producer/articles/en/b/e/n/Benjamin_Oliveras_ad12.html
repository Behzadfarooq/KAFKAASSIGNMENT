Benjamin Oliveras

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Benjamin Oliveras (born February 7, 1981 in Perpignan) is a French football defender currently playing for French Championnat National side Nîmes Olympique.

His previous clubs include Bordeaux and Stade Reims.
