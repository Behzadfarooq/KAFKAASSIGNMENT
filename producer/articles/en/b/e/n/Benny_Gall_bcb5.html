Benny Gall (born March 14, 1971) is a Danish football goalkeeper playing for F.C. Copenhagen. He plays with the no. 31, and is reserve for Jesper Christiansen.

Gall came to FCK from Esbjerg fB in 2001, but before that he played for Fremad Amager, KB, Brønshøj BK, Dordrecht'90, De Graafschap and Shrewsbury Town.

Gall is also manager of FCK's 2nd Division East team. In the summer 2007 he leaves FCK both as player and manager, as his contract expires[1]
