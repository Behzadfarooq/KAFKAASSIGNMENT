About Me

Hey...

I'm a British writer and broadcaster.

Only recently registered after the new regulations, but I've been doing wiki stuff for years in an unregistered identity, mainly on football, pop music, crime and British telly and radio, but I know all sorts of useless odds and sods which have gone on here...

I'm male, in my 30s, married, a journalist, a French speaker, a dog and cat lover, a supporter of Hull City, an apologist for the 1980s, politically conservative and a Guinness drinker.

I'm in favour of privatisation, capitalism, corporal punishment in schools, a reformation of the discipline laws in football and a responsible Press.

I'm against the death penalty, racism, homophobia, inheritance tax, speed cameras, bans on smoking in public and the identification of people accused of sexual offences prior to conviction.

Irrespective of views with which I disagree or lack sympathy, I am an advocate of absolute free speech.

I use Mozilla Firefox and have no skill on wikipedia beyond writing and basic editing. I am not a designer or creator of tables or illustrations.

Thank you. I'm here all week.
