Benny Boom

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Benny Boom (born on July 22, 1971) is a music video director and current director of Gorilla Flix, Inc. He trained at Temple University's School of Radio, Television, & Film, and also served as a protégé of veteran director Little X and Hype Williams. He has also directed music videos for Ciara, Cassidy, P. Diddy, Amerie, Nas, Nelly, Kelly Rowland, Lil' Kim, LL Cool J, The Pussycat Dolls and many others.

The rap video director has created political ads against George W. Bush in the past, and he is quoted as saying, "We've got to get George W. Bush out of office, and it's very important that kids understand what a serious condition the world is in with this madman." Most of the ads end with the phrase "George Bush, he's not on our side."[citation needed]

Boom is a member of Alpha Phi Alpha, the first intercollegiate Greek-letter fraternity established for African Americans. [1]
