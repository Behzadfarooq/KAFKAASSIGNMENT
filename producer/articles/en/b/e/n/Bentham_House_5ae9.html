Bentham House

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Bentham House on Endsleigh Gardens is the home of University College London's reputable and prestigious Faculty of Laws. Named after the famous jurist and political philosopher Jeremy Bentham, the acclaimed spiritual father of the University, the Faculty produces scores of high achieving law students, including the recently retired Lord Chief Justice, Lord Woolf of Barnes.
