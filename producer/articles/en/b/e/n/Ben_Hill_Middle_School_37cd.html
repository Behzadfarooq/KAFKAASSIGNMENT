Ben Hill Middle School was opened in 1986 on Ehrlich Road in Tampa, Florida.

The school is named in honor of Ben Hill, a former Hillsborough County, Florida school board member.

The school colors are garnet and gray.

The school mascot is the Bear.

Ben Hill is a higher-class school with a low minority population. The demographics are almost completely opposite from their rival school, Buchanan Middle School. While 63.44% of Buchanan's population is non-White, only 48.26% of Ben Hill's population is non-White. This causes much racially-motivated tension when students transfer to Gaither High School.
