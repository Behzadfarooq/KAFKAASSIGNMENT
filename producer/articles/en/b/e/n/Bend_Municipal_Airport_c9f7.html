Bend Municipal Airport (IATA: BDN, ICAO: KBDN), is a public airport located five miles (8 km) northeast of the city of Bend in Deschutes County, Oregon, USA.

Bend serves as the home to Columbia Aircraft and Epic Aircraft. In 2006 a restaurant returned to the field creativly titled after the airport elevation; 3456.
