[edit] Image copyright problem with Image:BrechinDiocese.JPG

Thanks for uploading Image:BrechinDiocese.JPG. However, the image may soon be deleted unless we can determine the copyright holder and copyright status. The Wikimedia Foundation is very careful about the images included in Wikipedia because of copyright law (see Wikipedia's Copyright policy).

The copyright holder is usually the creator, the creator's employer, or the last person who was transferred ownership rights. Copyright information on images on Wikipedia is signified using copyright templates. The three basic license types on Wikipedia are open content, public domain, and fair use. Find the appropriate template in Wikipedia:Image copyright tags and place it on the image page like this: {{TemplateName}}.

Please signify the copyright information on any other images you have uploaded or will upload. Remember that images without this important information can be deleted by an administrator. You can get help on image copyright tagging from Wikipedia talk:Image copyright tags. --OrphanBot 06:48, 21 January 2006 (UTC)

[edit] regarding your edits to Dundee

Please be careful when editing, your recent edits to this article while appreciated where quite problematic in a number of areas. for instance:-

  • your edits to the universities section removed a number of references and footnotes. Note that per wikipedia:verifiability all information should be properly sourced. Please do not remove relevant references, they enable readers to double check facts and aid in research.
  • A lot of your contributions duplicated material that was already in the article. For instance in the schools section you added a section that on the high school of Dundee even though there was already a paragraph on it earlier on in that section. in the media section you also duplicated mention of the city's radio stations even though they were already covered in the music section.
  • sub articles - a lot of the material you added more properly belonged in sub-articles, for instance the history section under politics and government should have been covered in the main Politics of Dundee article first. the section that the main Dundee article should remain a summary of the former per Wikipedia:Summery style the same also applies to many of the areas of Dundee which I have helpfully moved to the appropriate articles
  • Referencing - as the Dundee article is currently a featured article candidate it is important that information in it is well referenced. for example your sentence "As of February 2006, Median weekly earnings are £409, an increase of £33% since 1998, making them on a par with the Scottish median." should if possible be accompanied by a footnote (which you can read about at WP:FN) detailing where this figure comes from.

otherwise please don't let this put you off contributing.  YDAM TALK 21:40, 8 November 2006 (UTC)


Thanks for those improvements. That reference you provided in the economy section had tons of usefull information. Keep up the good work.  YDAM TALK 10:15, 11 November 2006 (UTC)
