Benjamin Edwards (artist)

Benjamin Edwards (born 1970) is an American visual artist and writer. He was born in Iowa City, Iowa, graduated from UCLA, and earned a Masters from the Rhode Island School of Design. His work deals mainly with "found imagery" collected from media (magazines, the internet, newspapers, video games, photographs, blueprints, logos and graphics) and then manipulated using 2d and 3d graphic software. This imagery is then massed into a "virtual space" on canvas, creating immense depth and vibrant colors. Benjamin Edwards is represented by Greenberg Van Doren in New York City.
