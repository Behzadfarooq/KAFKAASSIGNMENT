JR-West Bentenchō Station

Bentenchō Station (弁天町駅 Bentenchō-eki) is a train station in Minato-ku(Minato Ward), Osaka, Japan.

[edit] Layout

[edit] JR Bentenchō Station

  • There are two side platforms and two tracks on the third floor.
1 ■Osaka Loop Line Inner track for Shin-Imamiya, Tennoji, Kansai Airport, Wakayama and Nara
2 ■Osaka Loop Line Outer track for Nishikujo and Osaka

[edit] Osaka Municipal Subway Bentenchō Station

  • There are two side platforms and two tracks over the Osaka Loop Line.
1 ■Chuo Line (eastbound) for Hommachi, Tanimachi Yonchome, Morinomiya, Nagata, Ikoma and Gakken Nara-Tomigaoka
2 ■Chuo Line (westbound) for Osakako and Cosmosquare

[edit] Establishments around the station

  • Modern Transportation Museum (交通科学博物館)
  • ORC 200 Bldg.
    • the head office of Radio Osaka (ラジオ大阪)
