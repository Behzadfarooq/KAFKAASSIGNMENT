Benperidol is a drug which is a butyrophenone derivative. It is an antipsychotic, which can be used for the treatment of schizophrenia[1], but it is primarily used to control deviant, antisocial hypersexual behaviour[2], and is sometimes prescribed to sex offenders as a condition of their parole, as an alternative to anti-androgen drugs such as cyproterone.[3]

Benperidol was discovered at Janssen Pharmaceutica in 1961.
