Benoît Lecouls

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Benoît Lecouls (born 22 March 1979) is a French rugby union footballer. He currently plays for Biarritz Olympique in the top division of French rugby, the Top 14 competition. He usually plays as a prop. Prior to playing with Biarritz, Lecouls played for Toulouse and SU Agen.
