[edit] Military Rank

  • MG = Major General

  • Col = Colonel
  • Ltc = Lieutenant Colonel
  • Maj = Major
  • Cpt = Captain
  • Lt = Lieutenant
  • Sgt = Sergeant

[edit] Other

  • w = wounded
  • mw = mortally wounded
  • k = killed
