Ben Richards

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Ben Richards is also the name of the protagonist in the story The Running Man.

Ben Richards (born March 27, 1972, in Rustington) is a British actor. He is best known for playing Bruno Milligan in series 4 and 5 of the British TV Drama Footballers' Wives and in series 1 and 2 of its spin-off Footballers' Wives: Extra Time. He also appeared in the final ever mini-episode of Footballers' Wives shown on 17 July 2006 as part of Sport Relief night. He is now starring in Guys and Dolls with Claire Sweeney.

In 2006 he originally became a judge on Strictly Dance Fever, hosted by Graham Norton. He was alongside choreographers Stacey Haynes, Arlene Phillips and Wayne Sleep.

He was in Holby City for a few episodes in 2006. He was also in Stars in their Eyes and was Ricky Martin. He is married to Helen and they were meant to be having twins.
