Beninese diplomatic missions

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Embassy of Benin in Ottawa

Owing to its small size, the Republic of Benin maintains a very modest diplomatic network abroad. Its paramount relationship is with France, the former colonial power.

Honorary consulates are excluded from this list.
