Ben Jorgensen

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Ben "Slork" Jorgensen (born July 4, 1983 in Teaneck, New Jersey) is the lead singer and guitarist of the New Jersey band Armor for Sleep. He wrote all of the first Armor For Sleep CD by himself which was recorded for $100 at a local recording studio. He lives in Teaneck, New Jersey with his parents. He is of German, Danish, and Japanese descent, and is also Jewish.

He made a cameo appearance in Fall Out Boy's "Dance, Dance" video.
