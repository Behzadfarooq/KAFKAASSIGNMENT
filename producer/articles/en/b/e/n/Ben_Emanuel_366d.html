Benjamin F. Emanuel (born on June 18, 1982 in Texas City, Texas) is a player in the National Football League. He plays the free safety position. He went to UCLA. He attended Clear Brook High School.

[edit] Pro career

Emanuel was drafted by the Carolina Panthers in the 5th round (171st overall) in the 2005 NFL Draft. He was then signed by the San Francisco 49ers. He currently plays for the Cleveland Browns.
