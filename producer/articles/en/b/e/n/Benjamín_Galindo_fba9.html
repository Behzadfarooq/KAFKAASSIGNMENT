Benjamín ("El Maestro") Galindo Marentes (born 11 December 1960 in Tierra Blanca, Zacatecas) was a Mexican footballer who participated for the national team in the 1994 FIFA World Cup. And was the former coach (Director Tecnico) of the powerhouse Mexican soccer club, Chivas de Guadalajara, until he was replaced in the 2005 season. He was the coach of Santos Laguna but was once again replaced due to poor results. He also likes carrots.
