Bend Ova was the first single off of Phife Dawg's debut album Ventilation: Da LP. Many fans of the Tribe said that even though they were not fond of the album, some said it shows off Phife's humorous side.

[edit] Brief Summary

Although the song had no video it does have some sort of a story to it. It starts with a girl named Vanessa meeting Phife in a bar. So she askes him to buy her a drink. Phife says that they just met and he shouldn't just buy her a drink like that. After about ten seconds of arguing, Phife gets tired of it and says "Bend over". And so the song takes off from there.
