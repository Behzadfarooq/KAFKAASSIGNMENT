Beneath the Sky is a sextet Deathcore band, consisting of vocalist Joey Nelson, guitarists Chris Profitt and Jeff Nelson, bassist/vocalist Nick Scarberry, keytarist Matt Jones and drummer Brandon Sowder.

[edit] History

Based out of Cincinnati, Ohio, Beneath the Sky formed in mid 2004 after the collapse of their former band Blind Judgment. Through constant promotion and regional touring Beneath the Sky have gained fans all across the U.S. and in many other parts of the world. Beneath the Sky's intense live performance has been impressing audiences all across the Midwest and Eastern United States. Beneath the Sky's music can be described as intense metalcore. In January 2005 Beneath the Sky released a 4-song demo cd that distributed over 5000 copies before its retirement in late 2005. The band is currently under management with Hardcore Marketing,Inc a firm based out of Cleveland, Ohio that has worked with many of today's leading artists. Beneath the Sky completed work on a new album at Track Six Studios in Cleveland,OH with producer Don Debiase. Beneath the Sky continues to fill up venues with small town attitude and big time hearts. Letting fans go as far as joining the band on stage for a little choir-style performance.

On May 13, 2006 Beneath the Sky released their debut EP More Than You Can Handle....

In August 2006 Beneath the Sky signed with Victory Records and recorded their first full-length album in October 2006.

On January 16, 2007 Beneath the Sky filmed the video for their first single, "7861", in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania.

On January 23, 2007 Beneath the Sky released their debut album entitled What Demons Do to Saints.
