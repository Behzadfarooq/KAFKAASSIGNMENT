Ben Sandford (born March 12, 1979 in Rotorua) is a male skeleton racer from New Zealand, who took part in the 2005/2006 Skeleton World Cup and qualified for the 2006 Winter Olympics. He was the highest ranked New Zealand male on the 2005-2006 world cup circuit as well as the highest ranked male from the southern hemisphere.

[edit] World Cup 2005/2006 Results

  • 8th on November 10, 2005, Calgary Canada
  • 13th on November 17, 2005, Lake Placid, New York, United States
  • 10th December 8, 2005, Igls Austria
  • 8th January 12 2006, Koenigssee Germany
