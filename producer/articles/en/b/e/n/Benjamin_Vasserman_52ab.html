Benjamin Vasserman (born June 11, 1949 in Sovetsk, Kirov Oblast, Russia in the family of an employee deported from Estonia) is an Estonian graphic artist and printmaker.

  • He is the husband of Naima Neidre.

[edit] Education

In 1979, Vasserman graduated from the Estonian Academy of Art, Tallinn.

[edit] Artistic career

  • Began to exhibit in 1978
  • Vasserman has been a freelance artist since 1990

[edit] Membership

  • 1989 Estonian Artists' Association
  • 1992 Association of Estonian Printmakers
