Welcome to my user page. I'm a late-blooming 40-something male who thought he wanted to go to grad school to learn to be a professor of Portuguese and Brazilian Studies, and found out that he would rather teach middle school Spanish and Social Studies in a Jewish community day school - so that's what I do. Originally from Ft. Worth, Texas, I lived in Austin, Texas for a long time before moving to Providence, Rhode Island.
