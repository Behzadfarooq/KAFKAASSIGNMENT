[edit] Benjamin Christopher Smith

Born 24th April 1991

FAMILY

Ben Smith, as he is known to his friends, was born in Sitaard, Holland 1991. When he was 1 he and his family moved to a small town outside of Lyon, France where his brother, Paul John Smith, was born. His family moved back to Manchester, England shortly before his third birthday, His older sister, Gemma Rose Smith, and younger sister, Lizzie Anne Smith, have been born here. His father James Smith was born in Scotland and moved to Felixstowe, East Anglia when he was six and graduated at Lancashire Universitywhen he was 21. This was where James met Ben Smith's mother Alison Smith, maiden name hawkins, who is also a graduate of the Lancashire University.

ACHIEVEMENTS AND ASPIRATIONS

Ben is currently a student of Parrs Wood Technology and Arts College in Manchester. He has only achieved minor awards during his short life such as a nomination for young citizen of the year and playing on match for a cricket club (played the match at Cheadle Kingsway but now goes to Didsbury CC). Many people would consider Ben to be clever as he is intelligent in many subjects, especially maths where he his one of the highest achievers in the year, but he is not known for his sporting ability only for his academic. He supports Manchester United FC and Lancashire CCC.He dreams of either being a cartoonist or a proffesional cricketer.
