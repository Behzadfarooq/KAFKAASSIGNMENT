Ben Andrews (born in Cleveland, Ohio, United States, 1985) is a American gay porn actor (porn star) who performs in pornographic movies. He is 6'2", weighs 165 pounds, and is best known for his exceptionally large penis, which reportedly measures 11 inches long, 9 inches in circumference and is uncircumcised. In terms of sexual role, Andrews is strictly a top only. [1]

Andrews' studio, Lucas Entertainment, boasts of Andrews having the largest penis in the gay porn industry. Lucas Entertainment set up a website, Whosisbigger.com, where viewers were invited to view a video clip of Andrews and largest-in-the-biz Chad Hunt and vote for who had the largest penis - Ben won 71% to 29%. In March 2007, Ben received mainstream exposure alongside Falcon Studios' Erik Rhodes in a national ad campaign for Loehmann's department store. [2]

Andrews is currently signed to a 3 year-long exclusive contract with New York City based Lucas Entertainment. [3]
