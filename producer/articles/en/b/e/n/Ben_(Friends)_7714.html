Ben (Second name is never decided. Could possibly be Geller, Willick or even Bunch)(born May 11, 1995) is a fictional character played by Cole Sprouse on the hit TV sitcom Friends. He is the son of Ross Geller and Carol Willick and the half brother of Emma Geller-Green the daughter of Ross and Rachel Green. Before he was born his mother and father divorced and Carol, who had recently come out as a lesbian, moved in with her girlfriend Susan Bunch [1]. He lives primarily with his two mothers for the entirety of the series.
