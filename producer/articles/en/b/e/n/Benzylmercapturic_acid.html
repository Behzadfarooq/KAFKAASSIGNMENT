Benzylmercapturic acid
2-acetylamino-3-benzylsulfanyl-propanoic acid
Chemical formula C12H15NO3S
Molecular weight 253.318
Metabolism None
Elimination half-life
Excretion Renal

Benzylmercapturic acid is a minor metabolite of toluene in humans. It is used in the diagnosis of toluene exposure. As its name indicates, it contains a benzyl moiety and a mercaptan moiety.
