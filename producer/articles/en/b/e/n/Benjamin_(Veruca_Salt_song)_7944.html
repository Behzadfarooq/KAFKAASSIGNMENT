Benjamin (Veruca Salt song)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The cover for Bejamin

Benjamin was the third single off Veruca Salt's sophomore record, Eight Arms to Hold You. Due to the poor success of Shutterbug, a video for Benjamin was never made. The single was released on Geffen Records

[edit] The White Rabbit

The White Rabbit on the cover of the Benjamin single has caused much speculation. The most popular theory is that Benjamin was Nina Gordon's pet rabbit who died or ran away.

[edit] Track listing

  1. "Benjamin" (Gordon)
  2. "The Speed of Candy" (Gordon)
  3. "Never Met Her" (Post)
  4. "Swedish Fish" (Post)
