Bender Qassim International Airport (IATA: BSA, ICAO: HCMF), also known as Bossaso Airport or Bosasso Airport, is the second largest airport in Somalia. It is located on the outer edge of the city of Boosaaso at Longitude 49° 9' 0" E Latitude 11° 16' 59" N.

[edit] Scheduled Services

  • Daallo Airlines (Addis Ababa, Burao, Djibouti City [via Burao], Hargeisa, Jeddah, Dubai, London, Paris via Djibouti)
  • Djibouti Airlines (Djibouti City, Hargeisa, Dubai, Sharjah)
