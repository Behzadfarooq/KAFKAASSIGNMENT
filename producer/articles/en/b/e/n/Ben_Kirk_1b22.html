Ben Ian Kennedy Kirk was a fictional character in the Australian soap opera Neighbours. He was played first by Noah Sutherland upon his birth until his departure in 2003. When Ben returned to the show in 2004, he was played by Sean Berends. Ben was the infant son of Drew Kirk and Libby Kennedy.
