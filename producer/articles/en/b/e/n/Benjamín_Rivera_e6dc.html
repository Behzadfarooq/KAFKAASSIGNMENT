Benjamín Rivera

Benjamín Rivera is a Latin American voice actor. He is best known for voicing Goemon Ishikawa XIII in the Mexican/Latin American dub of Lupin III. He is married to Fujiko Mine's Latin American voice actress, Belinda Martinez.
