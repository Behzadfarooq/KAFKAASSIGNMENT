Benjamin Solari Parravicini (1898-1974), born in Buenos Aires, Argentina on August 8, was a painting artist. He was professor at the Liceo de España, manager of the Department of Arts at the Banco Municipal of Buenos Aires and director of the Exhibition Gallery of the Municipality of Buenos Aires, Secretary of the Association for the development of Arts in 1948. There is a popoular thinking that he was a prophet and was taught to paint by an angel, it is also believed he knew about the indigo children before they became popular and he prophetised a ´´Gray man´´ who is going to save Argentina(probably a Politic or spiritual leader). Parravicini also promoted several arts related projects in Buenos Aires.
