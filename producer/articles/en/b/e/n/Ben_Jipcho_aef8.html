Ben Jipcho (born March 1, 1943) is a former athlete from Kenya, who won the silver medal in the 3.000 metres Steeplechase at the 1972 Summer Olympics, behind his legendary teammate Kipchoge Keino.

Jipcho won 400 metres race in the 1973 All-Africa Games. He also won the golden medal at the 1974 Commonwealth Games in Christchurch, New Zealand.
