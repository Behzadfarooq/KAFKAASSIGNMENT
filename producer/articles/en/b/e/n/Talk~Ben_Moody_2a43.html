Ben Moody has many extended family members that reside in Wimberley, TX and other places around the US. He also has a brother who could be considered a piano virtuoso and who is the leader singer for the up and coming band Rigby Fawkes, and a cousin named Brian K. Moody and an uncle named Keith Moody who both reside in Wimberley, TX. Brian moody is part if an up-and-coming band called Beastiality who are based out of Wimberley, but will be touring with the band The Poet and Animality around Austin, San Antonio, as well as Gerogetown. The guitarist for Beastiality is named Greg Gibson, and he also resides in Wimberley.

[edit] Name

It's "Ben Moody III", stop changing it to the second.


I wonder about the advisability of posting this here, but it is incontrovertible proof of his real name, to my mind. Property records from a county where he owns a home list the owner's full name as Moody, Ben Robert II. The address for the owner is a California address, and it is safe to assume that this is Ben Moody the musician rather than his father. His father's home is listed as well, with his mother Melissa Moody, who is a relatively well-known photographer, listed as co-owner. It's clear to me that Ben Robert Moody is his father, Melissa Moody is his mother, and he is Ben Robert Moody II. Also, the Evanescence Reference wiki page on Ben Moody lists his full name as Ben Robert Moody II, and it is probably the most accurate source of Evanescence information on the internet, since it is essentially a joint project of EvBoard and Evthreads. As I said, this is pretty much incontrovertible evidence. —The preceding unsigned comment was added by 64.132.242.232 (talk) 17:57, 29 December 2006 (UTC).
