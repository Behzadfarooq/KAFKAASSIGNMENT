Benny Lautrup (born June 25, 1939) is a professor in theoretical physics at the Niels Bohr Institute at the University of Copenhagen. He has published a textbook, Physics of Continuous Matter (http://www.lautrup.nbi.dk/continuum) and is also known for his part in the Nakanishi-Lautrup formalism, a concept in quantum field theory.
