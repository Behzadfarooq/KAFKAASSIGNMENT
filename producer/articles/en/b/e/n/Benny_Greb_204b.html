Benny Greb (born on June 13, 1980 in Augsburg, Germany) is a young, up-and-coming drummer. He began playing drums at the age of six. He began taking professional lessons at the age of twelve. The young drummer sponsored by Meinl Percussion is a very active clinician, and has recently been working on his solo career. He currently plays for the bands Jerobeam and Otterpost, and has played alongside the likes of Wayne Krantz, Bobby McFerrin and Jo Jo Mayer. He is best known for his amazing musical solos. He often plays a melody with his feet, with the use of two high-hats, a double bass pedal, and sometimes several octobans also played with his feet, and plays impressive solos with his hands over the top.
