Benefactor is the second studio album by American new wave band Romeo Void, released in 1982. It was released on CD in 2006 by Wounded Bird Records, with their Never Say Never EP as four bonus tracks. The first version of "Never Say Never" is a shorter, "clean" edit suitable for general radio broadcast.
