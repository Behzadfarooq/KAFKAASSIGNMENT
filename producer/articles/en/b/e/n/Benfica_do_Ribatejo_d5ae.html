Benfica do Ribatejo (pron. IPA: [bɐ̃ĩ'fikɐ] or [bẽ'fikɐ du ʁibɐ'tɛʒu]) is a town and a parish located in the Municipality of Almeirim, Portugal.

  • Population: 3,117 habitantes (2001)
