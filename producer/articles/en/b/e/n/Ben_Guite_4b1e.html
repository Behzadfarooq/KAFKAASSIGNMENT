Ben Guite (born July 17, 1978 in Montreal, Quebec is an Canadian ice hockey player who plays for the Colorado Avalanche organization.

[edit] Playing Career

  • June 21, 1997 - Drafted by the Montreal Canadiens 172nd overall in the 7th round.
  • August 1, 2001 - Signed by the New York Islanders as a free agent.
  • March 19, 2002 - Traded to the Anaheim Mighty Ducks along with Bjorn Melin for Dave Roche.
  • August 15, 2005 - Signed by the Boston Bruins as a free agent.
  • July 12, 2006 - Signed by the Colorado Avalanche as a free agent.
