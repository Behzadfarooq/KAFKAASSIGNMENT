Ben Phillips

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Benjamin Gerard Phillips (born 9 July 1989, Truro, Cornwall, United Kingdom) is a Cornish entraprenuer-come-celebrity, locally known for his good deeds, wealth, and eccentricity. He is a modest inventor, though to date has not greatly profited from his inventions, riding on his Northern Irish families' name as inventors.

[edit] Early life

He was born on 9 July 1989 in Treliske hospital, and grew up in the surrounding area. He was private school educated, and becoming an inventor was always his childhood passion, strongly enouraged by his parents; inventors themselves.

[edit] Education

He received an offer to study at Cambridge in 2007, though has noted that if he accepts this offer, his inventions will take priority.

[edit] Trivia

  • Phillips can be noted for his obsession with Soviet Russia.
  • Phillips holds the Cornish record for fastest time in completing a rubix cube, approximating 12 seconds.
  • Phillips is locally known as "Ben 'The Coat' Phillips".
