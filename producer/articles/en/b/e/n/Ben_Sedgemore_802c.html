Ben Sedgemore (born August 5, 1975 in Wolverhampton) is an English footballer, currently playing on loan for Havant & Waterlooville from Rushden & Diamonds.

He previously played for Northampton Town, Mansfield Town, Peterborough United, Mansfield Town, Macclesfield Town, Lincoln City, Canvey Island and Cambridge United, and signed for Rushden & Diamonds in November 2006.

In January 2006 he joined Conference South side Havant & Waterlooville on loan as cover for Ian Baird's injury-hit side [1].
