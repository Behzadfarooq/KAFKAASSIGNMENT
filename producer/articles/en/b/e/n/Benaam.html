Benaam (Hindi: बेनाम, Urdu: بینام) is a Hindi/Urdu Bollywood film currently under production (as of May 2006). The film is directed by Anees Bazmee.

[edit] Cast

  • Ajay Devgan
  • Bhoomika Chawla

  • Vijay Raaz
