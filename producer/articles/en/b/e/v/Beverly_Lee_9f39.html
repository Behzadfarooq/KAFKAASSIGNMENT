Beverly Lee

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

You have new messages (last change).

Beverly Lee was a member of the all girl vocal pop group, The Shirelles. She was born on August 3, 1941, in Passaic, New Jersey.

Today, she holds the trademark for the Shirelles' name.
