Beverly McDonald

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Olympic medal record
Women's Athletics
Silver 2000 Sydney 4x100m relay

Beverly McDonald (born February 15, 1970 in Saint Mary, Jamaica) is a Jamaican sprinter. Her accomplishments include winning the silver medal in 4x100m relay at the 2000 Olympics.

Beverly McDonald is the sister of Michael McDonald.

[edit] Personal bests

  • 100 metres - 10.99 (1998)
  • 200 metres - 22.22 (1999)
