Beverley Busson

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Beverley Ann Busson LLB, COM, OBC is the current interim Commissioner of the Royal Canadian Mounted Police. She was appointed on December 15, 2006. She is the first female to hold this post.

Born in Halifax, Nova Scotia, she joined the Royal Canadian Mounted Police in 1974.

In 2004, she was made a Commander of the Order of Merit of the Police Forces. In 2006, she was awarded the Order of British Columbia.
