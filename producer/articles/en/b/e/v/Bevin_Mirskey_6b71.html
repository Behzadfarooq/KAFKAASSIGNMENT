Bevin Mirskey is a recurring character on CW show One Tree Hill. She is a cheerleader and Skills' girlfriend. She is portrayed by Bevin Prince.

[edit] Season One

Bevin first appeared in the episode "Spirit in the Night" as a Tree Hill chearleader.

[edit] Season Two

In Season 2, Bevin appeared at Nathan and Haley's wedding reception. Then at Haley's bachelorette party. She is a friend of Brooke Davis. She was manipulated by Erica Marsh so that she wouldn't vote for Brooke as Student Council President by telling her Brooke was running for the US Presidency. The not so bright Bevin was scared since she believes Brooke is the best cheerleader captain ever.

[edit] Season Three

In season 3, however, she had become more important. She was, just like Mouth, a way Rachel Gatina found to get Brooke mad, by for instance making her a new haircut or trading their spot for the fantasy boy draft. She was amazed by Rachel's dancing skills, which also got Brooke angry. During the fantasy boy draft she chose Skills as her dream boy and they've been together ever since, although Skills has some trouble with Bevin's sometime stupid actions and the fact that she is very rich. For instance one day they got lost in the forest and Bevin told him on different occasions that North was in conflicting directions.

[edit] Season Four

In season 4, when Skills asked her to gather information about Brooke for Lucas, she ran to tell him that Brooke had dumped Lucas, which both Lucas and Skills obviously knew. She was also one of the cheerleaders to go with Haley James Scott to the hospital when she got sick at the state championship. She then celebrated the victory with her boyfriend Skills. She also pushed him to dance in front of everyone during the stripping contest promising to then dance for him. Recently, the characters of Tree Hill had to pair up for an assignment and Bevin was paired with Rachel. Rachel made a comment about Bevin being dumb. Bevin replied with how she may seem dumb but she's not stupid and she only acts the way she does because she (Rachel) and the other cheerleaders need her to feel better about themselves. She also said that she can change who she is after high school while people like Rachel cling to high school as a way to stay above people. Then she takes a picture of Rachel, with Rachel out of focus. She does this to show the high school not Rachel it shows how Rachel clings to high school.
