Bev Bighead
Rocko's Modern Life character
Age 53
Gender Female
Species Toad
Job None
First appearance No Pain No Gain
Voice actor Charles Adler

In Rocko's Modern Life, Beverly Bighead is Ed's wife, but it's clear that she wears the pants in the family. She's loud, domineering, and she keeps Ed in line. But beneath her ugly exterior and grating voice, she's really a closet party animal. She doesn't have every bit of control over Ed, she just straightens him up when she needs to. Although it may seem like she and Ed have love/hate relationship, they actually have more love than hate in it.
