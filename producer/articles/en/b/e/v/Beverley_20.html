Beverley 20

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Beverley 20 is a walk in the East Riding of Yorkshire, England that runs via various routes between Beverley Minster and the Humber Bridge. All the routes pass through the local villages of Skidby and North Ferriby. It also clips the edge of Walkington.

The paths themselves are also used by other longer routes. So the section from the Humber Bridge to North Ferriby is used by the Yorkshire Wolds Way, High Hunsley Circuit and the Trans Pennine Trail.
