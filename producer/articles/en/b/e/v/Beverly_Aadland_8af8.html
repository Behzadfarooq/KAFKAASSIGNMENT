Beverly Aadland

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Beverly Aadland (born September 17, 1943, Los Angeles, USA) was an American actress of the 1950s.

[edit] Filmography

  • 1951 Death of a Salesman
  • 1958 South Pacific
  • 1959 Cuban Rebel Girls

She dated actor Errol Flynn in the last two years of his life.
