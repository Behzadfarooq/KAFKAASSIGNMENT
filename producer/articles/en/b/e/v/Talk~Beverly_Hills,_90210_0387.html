[edit] When did it become a soap opera?

Although there is no argument that for most of its run, 90210 was a soap opera, I remember seeing the first few episodes and at least during that first year, it was anything but a soap opera. In fact it was more a family comedy-drama along the lines of Our House more than anything. I think this is worth noting, however I stopped watching after the first few months, and the next time I paid any attention to the show it was a couple of years later and it had become the soap opera. I think it should be noted when the format changed over. 23skidoo 03:30, 4 August 2006 (UTC)
