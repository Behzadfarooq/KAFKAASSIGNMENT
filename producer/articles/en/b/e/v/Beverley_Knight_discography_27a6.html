[edit] Albums

[edit] Studio

Album cover Album information
The B-Funk
  • Release Date: 2 November 1995 (UK)
  • UK Chart peak: #145
  • BPI Sales Certification: Not certified
  • Singles: "Flavour Of The Old School", "Down For The One", "Moving On Up (On The Right Side)", "Mutual Feeling", "Cast All Your Cares".
Prodigal Sista
  • Release Date: 17 August 1998
  • UK Chart peak: #42
  • BPI Sales Certification: Gold
  • Singles: "Made It Back" (Featuring Redman), "Rewind (Find A Way)", "Sista Sista", "Made It Back '99" (Featuring Redman), "Greatest Day".
Who I Am
  • Release Date: 11 March 2002
  • UK Chart peak: #7
  • BPI Sales Certification: Gold
  • Singles: "Get Up", "Shoulda, Woulda, Coulda", "Gold", "Shape Of You (Reshaped)" (Featuring Hollywood).
Affirmation
  • Release Date: 28 June 2004
  • UK Chart peak: #11
  • BPI Sales Certification: Gold
  • Singles: "Come As You Are", "Not Too Late For Love", "Keep This Fire Burning".
Music City Soul
  • Release Date: 7 May 2007
  • Singles: "No Man's Land"
