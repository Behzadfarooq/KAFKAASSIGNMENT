Beovizija 2007 was the 5th edition of Beovizija and the first national selection for the newly independent Serbia in the Eurovision Song Contest. It was held on 7 March (semi-finals) and 8 March (finals). The winner of the contest, Marija Šerifović, will represent Serbia in the Eurovision Song Contest 2007 in Helsinki.
