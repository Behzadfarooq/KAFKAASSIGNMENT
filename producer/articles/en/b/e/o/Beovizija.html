Beovizija

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Flamingosi feat. Louis performing at Evropesma

Beovizija is a Serbian pop music festival first held in 2003.

In 2004, it became the Serbian semi-final to choose songs for the final, Evropesma. The winner of Evropesma was to represent Serbia and Montenegro in the Eurovision Song Contest. Beovizija is organized by RTS, the Serbian national broadcaster. The other semi-final used to be Montevizija, organized by the Montenegrin broadcaster RTCG.

As of 2007, Beovizija has been the national selection for Serbia, now separate from Montenegro, in the Eurovision Song Contest.

[edit] Winners

  • 2003: Toše Proeski
  • 2004: Negative
  • 2005: Jelena Tomašević
  • 2006:Flamingosi featuring Louis
  • 2007: Marija Šerifović
