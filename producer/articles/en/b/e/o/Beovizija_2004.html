The Beovizija 2004 was the second edition of Beovizija. This festival was some sort of semi-final for Evropesma 2004, the festival where the song that represented Serbia and Montenegro in the Eurovision Song Contest 2004 was chosen. It was held on February 20th at the Sava Center in Belgrade, Serbia, hosted by Aleksandar Srećković and Ksenija Balaban. The top 4 songs qualified for the final - they were chosen by 9 jury members (8 jurors and televoting/SMS voting being the 9th member). Negativ, Leontina, Boris Režak and Nataša Kojić qualified for Evropesma. Jelena Jevremović was the winner of the televote/SMS vote.
