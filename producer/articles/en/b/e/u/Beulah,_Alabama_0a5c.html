Beulah, Alabama is an unincorporated community in the northeast corner of Lee County, Alabama, just south of Valley, Alabama. It is part of the Auburn Metropolitan Area. It is bounded by Chambers County on the north, the Chattahoochee River on the east, and the Halawaka Embayment of Lake Harding on the south.
