Beulah
can refer to one of the following places:

  • The Land of Beulah is a location in Isaiah 62:4 in The Bible. It means "Married."
  • Sweet Beulah Land, a hymn about the above location
  • any of these United States cities:
    • Beulah, Alabama
    • Beulah, Michigan
    • Beulah, Mississippi
    • Beulah, North Dakota
  • In the United Kingdom
    • Beulah: a village in Ceredigion, Wales
    • Beulah: a village in Powys, Wales
  • In Australia
    • Beulah, Victoria

Also:

  • Beulah is a proper feminine name.
  • Beulah McGillicutty was a professional wrestling valet.
  • Beulah was a 1940s radio show about an African American housekeeper named "Beulah". that later became an early television show, the first sitcom to star an African-American, in the 1950s. The show was criticized for its alleged stereotypical content, and identifying a black woman as "Beulah" is considered a racial slur (see Aunt Jemima).
  • Beulah is an indie rock band associated with the Elephant Six Collective.
  • Beulah is a UK based female singer-songwriter who has been described as the UK's answer to Norah Jones.
  • Beulah is the name of a short street in San Francisco [1]
