Beulah, Kansas

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

You have new messages (last change).

Beulah is a small community in Crawford County, Kansas. Beulah was founded in 1874 by a colony of Methodists. The Methodist church was completed in 1881. The post office opened December 31, 1874, and closed March 15, 1955.
