The District of Beja (pron. IPA: ['bɛʒɐ]) is located in the South of Portugal, the district Capital is the city of Beja.

The district is composed of 14 municipalities:

  • Aljustrel
  • Almodôvar
  • Alvito
  • Barrancos
  • Beja
  • Castro Verde
  • Cuba
  • Ferreira do Alentejo
  • Mértola
  • Moura
  • Odemira
  • Ourique
  • Serpa
  • Vidigueira
