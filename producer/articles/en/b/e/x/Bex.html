Bex

Bex is a municipality in the canton of Vaud, Switzerland, located in the district of Aigle. It has an area of 96.60 km², an altitude of 424 m, and 5709 inhabitants.

Bex is connected to Les Diablerets by the mountain pass Col de la Croix.
