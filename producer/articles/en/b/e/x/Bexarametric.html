Bexarametric, the performing pseudonym of Kevin James Deal, was an American electronic music artist of his own label Metric Style Records. He specializes in the electronic music genres of drum and bass, Intelligent Dance Music, and trip hop, with a lot jazz influence.

Bexarametric (pronounced like the weather term, "barometric") has been writing music for about 10 years, starting on guitar then working his way into electronics. It all started with jazz and rock then fused into electronic music. He spent a few years developing his own sound until releasing his first LP.

The majority of his music is featured on his ACIDplanet.com profile, including recordings of his live gigs, remixes of other artists and collaborations. He has since stopped making music, and not updated or renewed subscriptions to his website or acidplanet page for some time.
