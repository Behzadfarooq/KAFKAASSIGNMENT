Be Bad is a Canadian noise rock band, who have travelled around North America in their van freaking out kids and going hungry while bashing out some killer jams.

[edit] Name

The name is in reference from a pre-setting for a clunky 1980's guitar effect pedal. It was also chosen as it is a common mantra often shouted in many early rock and roll songs.
