"Be My Love" is a popular song.

It was written by lyricist Sammy Cahn and composer Nicholas Brodzsky and published in 1950.

The song was featured in a 1950 movie, The Toast of New Orleans, where it was sung by Mario Lanza. The Lanza recording of the song (released by RCA Victor Records as catalog number 20-1561) was a million-seller and a Billboard #1 charting hit.
