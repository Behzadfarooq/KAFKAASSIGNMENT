Be Easy is the second single off T.I. album Trap Muzik. The song is about the life of a man in the projects.

[edit] Video Information

T.I. in the "Look What I Got" music video

The video is about a man who was shot, and left for dead. The video starts off with the man receiving CPR from paramedics. The video then begins and the whole incident begins in reverse, from the man getting CPR to the man leaving his house.

The beginning of the video is where T.I. was "playing" the piano to beat of the song and appears to be walking and doing everything backwards.

The second portion of the video "Look What I Got" contained cameo appearances such as Eightball & MJG, Killer Mike, P$C & Cee-Lo. The scene appears to be a parking lot with a crowd of people.
