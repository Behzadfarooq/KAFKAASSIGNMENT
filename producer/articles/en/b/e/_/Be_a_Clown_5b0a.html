"Be a Clown" is a song written by Cole Porter for the 1948 film The Pirate. The song "Make 'Em Laugh" in the film Singin' in the Rain was largely plagiarized from "Be a Clown".

The song was featured in an episode of Land of the Giants and also in the 2004 film De-Lovely.
