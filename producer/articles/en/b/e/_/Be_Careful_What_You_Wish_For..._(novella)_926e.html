Be Careful what you wish for is the 12th novella in R.L. Stine's best-selling Goosebumps series of spooky books for children and teenagers.

[edit] Directly from R.L. Stine

Samantha Byrd is a klutz. An accident waiting to happen. She's the laughingstock of the girl's basketball team and that mean, rotten Judith Bellwood is making her life miserable off the court. But that all changed when this: One afternoon. When Sam gets off the bus from school. She almost runs into Clarissa, a creepy lady (known as a witch) who could grant her three wishes. Only she wasn't careful when she made those wishes, and now that they're coming true, her life is turning into a living nightmare!

[edit] Characters

  • Samantha Byrd: A young girl who is always miserable.
  • Clarissa: A witch in disguise; could grant Sam 3 wishes.
  • Judith Bellwood: A girl at Samantha's school who keeps tormenting her.
  • Punkin:a mutt dog of Samantha Byrd
  • Ron Byrd: Brother of Samantha
  • Anna Frost: Friend of Judith Bellwood
  • Cory Blinn: Best friend of Samantha Byrd

[edit] Television Episode

In the television series based on the Goosebump books, this story was adapted into a one part story.
