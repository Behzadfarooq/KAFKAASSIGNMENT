"Be Afraid of the Dark" is the 38th episode of the American animated television series Ben 10. It will air on April 14, 2007.

[edit] Plot

Continued from "The Return", Ben is forced to travel into space in order to prevent Ghostfreak from taking over the world by plunging Earth into eternal darkness.

[edit] Notes

  • This episode aired in the UK on April 6, 2007.
  • Ben gains back Ghostfreak in the Omnitrix, as well as Doctor Vicktor's form. This marks the first time Ben gains two different aliens in a single episode.
