From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Be With You may refer to the following songs

  • "Be with You (Enrique Iglesias song)", a song and single from the Enrique (1999 album) by Enrique Iglesias.
  • "Be with You", a song from the Feels So Good album by Atomic Kitten.
  • "Be With You (MAX)", a cover of Atomic Kitten's song of the same name released as a Japanese language single by the girl group, MAX.
  • Be with You is the English title to the 2004 Japanese movie Ima, Ai ni Yukimasu.
  • "Be With You", a Carmen Rasmusen's music available on Pride and Prejudice: A Latter-Day Comedy Soundtrack.
