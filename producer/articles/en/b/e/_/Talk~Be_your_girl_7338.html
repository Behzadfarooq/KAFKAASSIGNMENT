[edit] Copyright Violation

Whoever marked this article for violation went overboard, I'm not clear on whether including song lyrics is prohibited. We should have just removed the copyrighted lyrics, as I have done on the temporary subpage. Brad T. Cordeiro 21:51, 14 December 2006 (UTC)

Including copyrighted lyrics is forbidden (see Wikipedia:WikiProject_Songs#Lyrics_and_music_videos), and Wikipedia:Copyright problems clearly states that if all previous versions of an article include the copyright violation (which is the case here), it should be blanked just the way I did. So I didn't go overboard at all, I did exactly what has to be done in a case like this. --Fritz Saalfeld (Talk) 22:44, 14 December 2006 (UTC)
See Wikipedia:Copyright problems#Alternatives to deletion. "Rewrite the article, excluding copyrighted text." Flagging the entire article as copyvio could have been avoided by just deleting the lyrics from the article. Brad T. Cordeiro 00:56, 21 December 2006 (UTC)
Guess you should have read the sentence after that one, too: "This is done on a temporary page at Talk:PAGENAME/Temp so that the original, copyright-infringing version can be deleted by an administrator and the rewrite copied over." --Fritz Saalfeld (Talk) 10:16, 21 December 2006 (UTC)
