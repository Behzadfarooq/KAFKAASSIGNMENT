"Be Prepared" is a fourth season episode of That's So Raven.

[edit] Plot

Maria Shriver (who guest stars on this episode) decides to get Donna Cabonna's help for a new commercial of a safety plan for fires. Donna and Maria need a band to be in the commercial, so Raven decides to get the Boyz n' Motion (her old friends in Boyz 'N Commotion). However, when she decides to go through the wardrobe with them, their personalities clash and cause the group to break up, just one day before the commercial starts shooting. Now Raven must try to get the band back together in time. Meanwhile, Cory and Victor try to work through their safety plan at home.

[edit] Production

The episode premiered on May 12, 2006. This is the second appearance of the fictional musical group Boyz n' Motion. Michael Copon guest stars as Boyz n' Motion member Ricky. Ryan Hansen guest stars as Boyz n' Motion member JJJ (the third J' is silent). Columbus Short guest stars as Boyz n' Motion member Tre.

This episode was made as a fun, educational method to inform children and families to be prepared in case a disaster occurs. During the period that this episode aired, the United States Department of Homeland Security encouraged American households to be prepared and ready in case of disaster. [1] [2]

[edit] Trivia

  • Raven refers to Coco as a girl. However, the gender is frequently changed through the episode.
  • Aired in Asia as the penultimate episode.
