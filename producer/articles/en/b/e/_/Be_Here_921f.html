Be Here is the fourth solo album by New Zealand-born Australian country singer Keith Urban.

The album produced three #1 singles: "Days Go By", "Making Memories of Us" and "Better Life". Nine of the album's songs were penned by Urban. It achieved triple-platinum sales, reached #8 on the Canadian album chart, #1 on the Billboard Hot Country Albums chart and #3 on the Billboard 200.
