Be Somebody is the third single from Dre, off his upcoming album The Trunk. Although it has not garnered much mainstreem attention, it is one of the deepest and most thought provoking rap songs of 2006, touching on everything from the evolution of rap, their hip-hop heroes, and Cool & Dre's careers to growing up in Miami, struggles of the street, and Hurricane Wilma. This is a stark contrast to their previous single, "Chevy Ridin' High", which describes cars and materialism.

[edit] Song Information

The song features Keyshia Cole and contains a sample of Aretha Franklin's "You Make My Life". There has not been much hype around the song.
