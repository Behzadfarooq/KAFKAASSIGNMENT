Be Stiff is the second EP by New Wave musicians Devo, released in 1978 (see 1978 in music). It consists of six self-produced 4-track demo recordings, four of which had been previously released on independent singles on the band's own Booji Boy imprint. All six of these tracks would go on to be re-recorded by the band during the sessions for Q: Are We Not Men? A: We Are Devo! though the title track and "Social Fools" were not issued on the album. This EP was distributed only in the United Kingdom and Japan.
