"Be My Girl" is a 1986 ballad single from New Kids On The Block. Written and produced by Maurice Starr, it was the groups first release from their debut album New Kids On The Block. Although it received marginal airplay in their hometown of Boston, it was largely ignored nationally and failed to chart.
