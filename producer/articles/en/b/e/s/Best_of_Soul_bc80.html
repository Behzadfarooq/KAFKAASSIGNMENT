BEST OF SOUL was BoA's first album compilation of her Japanese singles. It hit number one on the United World Chart and became the first album of 2005 to sell over one million copies. This album also put her in Japanese history for being the first foreigner to have more than one album sell over a million copies in Japan. It also had a limited edition version which contained a DVD with the music videos.
