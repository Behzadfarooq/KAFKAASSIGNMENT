Highest Level Student Council (極上生徒会, Gokujō Seitokai?) is an anime series produced by Konami and J.C.STAFF, which started airing on TV Tokyo in April 2005. The development staff includes composer Yoko Shimomura of Kingdom Hearts fame.

It has been licensed for North American release by ADV Films. The release date for volume one was January 9, 2007.

[edit] Story

The story takes place at Miyagami Gakuen, a mysterious all-girls high school, its student council, the Miyagami Gakuen Maximum Authority Holders Highest Level Student Council, also known as the Gokujou Seitokai (Gokujou Student Council), having Assault and Covert divisions. The council has more authority than even the faculty and staff.

All Gokujou Seitokai members have some kind of special ability, which helps them to make Miyagami Gakuen a place where students are not restricted by the outside (adult) world.

Gokujou Seitokai features wacky humor and a notable amount of yuri or shoujo-ai (subtext and canon). It can be seen as a mixture of elements from Azumanga Daioh and Maria-sama ga Miteru. Gokujou Seitokai's emphasis is humor, but more profound subjects like finding one's way in life and friendship come into the focus on occasion.

Randou Rino, who is on her own since the death of her mother, transfers to Miyagami Gakuen, on the recommendation of her penpal, Mr. Poppit. Shortly after her arrival, Rino surprisingly becomes a member of the Gokujou Seitokai, the prestigious student council of Miyagami High. Rino learns that Jinguji Kanade founded Miyagami Gakuen as a place where students can live free from restrictions and that the Gokujou Seitokai was formed to ensure that freedom.

The Gokujou Seitokai is divided into the three main divisions: Executive, Assault and Covert plus the Vehicle Squad.

Each of its members has her own special ability, such as special fighting techniques or weaponry such as cards and a yo-yo, or, in Rino's case, a living hand puppet called Pucchan.

Rino's life at Miyagami Gakuen is paved with hilarious events, but every now and then, the series undertakes a much more serious and philosophical undertone.

[edit] Executive Members

  • Jinguji Kanade (神宮司奏, Jinguuji Kanade?) (Voice:Hitomi Nabatame)

Kanade is the executive director and founder of Miyagami Gakuen, president of the Gokujou Seitokai, and the successor to the Jinguji conglomerate. She takes a special interest in Rino and takes care of her, later found to be at the request of Rino's mother, a relative of hers. She is able to telepathically communicate with other people. Also of note is that Kanade is later revealed to be Mr. Poppit, Rino's penpal at the beginning of the series. When she was just a kid, she had a wish to go to school and so Miyagami High School was built.

After her graduation from high school, Kanade returns in the last episode as the director of the school.

  • Kinjo Nanaho (金城奈々穂, Kinjō Nanaho?) (Voice:Junko Noda)

Nanaho is one of the two vice presidents of the Gokujou Seitokai and also the leader of the Assault Squad, using a yo-yo as her weapon. She secretly has a great love for "girly" things, which can be seen from the decor of her room. Nanaho is also an old friend of Kanade and protects and advises Kanade in various matters by her own free will, and not because of the obligations that have been bestowed to the Kinjo family to protect Jinguji family from generations ago. She has 6 brothers and she is the youngest child of her family. Since childhood she was trained to protect Kanade, which caused her to bear a grudge towards the other girl until Kanade told her that she was free to do as she wanted.

  • Ginga Kuon (銀河久遠, Ginga Kuon?) (Voice:Kaori Shimizu)

Kuon is the other vice president and oversees the Covert Squad. During the Kuon-centered episodes (15 and 16), it is revealed that she is actually a spy for another organization, on a mission to discover the hidden powers of the Jinguji family. However, this was actually forced on her as the company run by her father was supported by the organization. She is a specialist in gathering information, surprising Nanaho during her transfer interview to Miyagami Gakuen. Kuon is also well-trained in self-defense, as can be seen when she performs throws on attackers larger than her during the series.

Kuon is also one quarter non-Japanese (unspecified from Konami's website) on her paternal grandfather's side, and comes from a wealthy family.

  • Randou Rino (蘭堂りの, Randŏ Rino?) (Voice:Yukari Tamura)

Rino is the main character of the series and, on her first day, is given the title of Secretary of Gokujou Seitokai. She has inherited the powers of the Jinguji family but does not know about it. She is almost always seen with Pucchan, her hand puppet companion. She is also capable of sending her voice outwards to everyone in a large radius around her, when her "powers" awaken late in the series. Her mother was a member of the Jinguuji family but ran away because she disliked the ways of the family.

  • Ichikawa Mayura (市川まゆら, Ichikawa Mayura?) (Voice:Miyuki Sawashiro)

Mayura is the accountant for the Gokujou Seitokai. She is usually busy trying to fit all the needs of the council into the budget, and has appeared in almost every episode working with an abacus.

[edit] Assault Squad

The Assault Squad (遊撃部, Yŭgekibu?) is in charge of the safety of the school by eliminating any potential threats.

  • Hida Sayuri (飛田小百合, Hida Sayuri?) (Voice:Ayako Kawasumi)

Sayuri fights with her wooden sword using the Hida Kasseiryuu. She is an old friend of Rein. Before she was invited to Miyagami Gakuen, she was referred to as a genius swordsman who inherited the pure blood of Hida. An incident that caused her to leave a senior swordsman permanently injured caused her to feel deep guilt ever since, and she sends him flowers daily.

When Sayuri's glasses are taken off, she has a hard time seeing, and ends up muttering "megane, megane..." ("glasses, glasses") without ever being able to locate them.

  • Tsunomoto Rein (角元れいん, Tsunomoto Rein?) (Voice:Yuki Matsuoka)

Rein uses playing cards as her weapon and is a childhood friend of Sayuri The Hida family took her in after her father disappeared. She enjoys playing card games and does not mind gambling, due to her familiarity with handling cards.

Rein has an extremely maternal side that is shown when an abandoned baby turns up at Gokujou-ryou. This is said to be due to the fact that her father abandoned her when she was very young, making her understand well how it feels to not be loved by anyone.Sometimes Pucchan oftenly refers her as little kid senpai due to her childish acts and nature.Reinj also cheats quite a lot because she doesn't want to lose(consecutive loses means seperation)

Though she has expressed a dislike for her father, claiming to want to hit him if her ever shows up again, she actually still loves him deep in her heart. Rein finally sees her father during episode 26.

  • Izumi Kaori (和泉香, Izumi Kaori?) (Voice:Chiwa Saitō)

Kaori fights bare-handed and is a classmate of Rino and Ayumu. She seems to have a crush on Kanade and resents Rino because of her close relationship with Kanade.

She has two brothers and one sister, which is why she does not live in the Gokujou-ryou like the other members of the student council. This is only made known to the rest when a series of events cause them to stumble upon her house and see Kaori's siblings.

[edit] Covert Squad

The Covert Squad (隠密, Onmitsu?) specializes in gathering intelligence and performs espionage and surveillance operations. An example is seen in Episode 15: When a new student plans to transfer to Miyagami Gakuen and there is reason for suspicion, a background check is conducted by the Covert Squad.

  • Katsura Seina (桂聖奈, Katsura Seina?) (Voice:Kumi Sakuma)
Seina from episode 26

Seina is the captain of the Covert Squad and Minamo's elder sister. Constantly seen smiling, she can also be very serious when necessary. Seina is first known simply as an old friend of Kanade, but is revealed later in the series to be a relative of hers. While Seina is of Jinguji blood, she did not inherit any powers, and as a result, had her last name changed.

Near the end of the series, she is revealed to be the new chairman of V, a group that oversees the workings of the Jinguuji family and controls even the family's leader (which was the main reason that Kanade had trouble rebelling against the family). This position was taken by Seina in an effort to help Kanade.

  • Kutsugi Kotoha (矩継琴葉, Kutsugi Kotoha?) (Voice:Kana Ueda)
    Kotoha from episode 26

Kotoha is the best member of the Covert Squad and uses shurikens as her weapon of choice. She views Kuon with great suspicion and as a threat when her identity is revealed, but later comes to accept her as her leader once again. She is said to never smile, but did towards Kuon at the end of episode 16.

Kotoha is also a master of unarmed combat and disguise, posing as the caretaker of Gokujou-ryou in episode 11 during a soccer match between the Gokujou Seitokai and the student council of another school. The game was later won almost single-handedly by her, despite the other team possessing a huge lead in the earlier parts of the match.

  • Ohme Ayumu (桜梅歩, Oume Ayumu?) (Voice:Eri Sendai)
    Ayu from episode 26

Ayumu is Rino's best friend, classmate and the one to introduce her to the Gokujou Seitokai. She is later found to be from the Ohme clan of ninja, who left them because she disliked the rules and policies that prohibited her from acting or dressing how most girls would. She has a twin brother named Urato. In an episode centered on her, Ayumu is discovered by a talent scout and planned to enter showbiz as AYU-AYU.

  • Katsura Minamo (桂みなも, Katsura Minamo?) (Voice:Ayumi Tsuji)
Minamo from episode 26

Minamo is Seina's little sister and also inherited no powers from the Jinguji blood within her. She is physically very weak, though this is hidden by her constantly playing pranks and general acts of mischief (this has included making a mess of the seitokai hostel and throwing Nanaho's foreign-imported teddy bear into pouring rain). She likes doing scripts and drawings and was the one behind projects such as the Gokujou Seitokai card game featuring the members of the student council and the (poorly received) play Space Alien. Even though Minamo has a weak heart, she doesn't want others to feel pity for her, causing her to constantly push herself to the extent of danger to her health.

[edit] Vehicle Squad

  • Manabe Cyndi (シンディ真鍋, Shindi Manabe?) (Voice:Tomoko Kawakami)

Cyndi is the only shown member of the Vehicle Squad and speaks in a combination of English and Japanese, though her lines have usually been short, such as "me too" and "roger". She addresses Kanade as "Boss".

She is in love with Pucchan. and later confesses to him (it should be noted the Pucchan never turned her down).

Cindy is later found to be, in fact, quite adept and aware of the situation around her. In episode 25, Cindy can speak Japanese fluently but it is later revealed that she never spoke it properly because her mother (who speaks in a highly rude manner much like gangsters picking a fight) told her she was not speaking it right.

[edit] Trivia

Spoiler warning: Plot and/or ending details follow.
  • In the opening sequence, Kuon and Seina is originally accompanied by two shadows, signifying the secrecy of the Covert Squad. In episode 16 and onward, the right side is revealed to be Kotoha, and from episode 21 onward, the left side is revealed to be Ayumu.
  • Also in the opening sequence, Itami Yukimi's name is originally spelt Itam Yukina. This error is only present in the first episode.
  • In episode 23, near the end of the episode when Rino goes out to find the Landlady (the scene after she exits Cyndi's car), she passes a person on a bicycle who has an uncanny resemblance to Takemoto from the anime Honey & Clover during his soul-searchin cycling trip across Japan. J.C. Staff animated both Honey & Clover and Gokujou Seitokai and they were both aired during the same time.
  • In Episode 7, Rein and Sayuri make a comment about the man Mayura goes to meet, who is named Shimon. This is likely a reference to the protagonist named Shimon(or Simon in the English) in the game franchise Castlevania by Konami. It is interesting that Konami was also the main sponsor for Gokujou Seitokai.
Spoilers end here.
