Bessemer Airport (IATA: EKY, ICAO: KEKY) is a public airport located three miles (4.8 km) southeast of the central business district (CBD) of Bessemer, a city in Jefferson County, Alabama, USA.

[edit] Facilities

Bessemer Airport covers 380 acres and has one runway:

  • Runway 5/23: 5,700 x 100 ft. (1,737 x 30 m), Surface: Asphalt

An Instrument Landing System was installed in 2000. The airport has received funding to expand the runway to 6,001 feet.
