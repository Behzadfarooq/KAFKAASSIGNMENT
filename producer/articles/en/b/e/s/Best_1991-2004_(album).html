Best 1991-2004
Compilation album by Seal
Released 2004

Best 1991-2004 is a 2004 compilation album by Seal, released by Warner Bros. Records containing 14 tracks. Three separate versions of this album were released: one containing Seal's greatest hits, another with an additional disc of acoustic versions of Seal's greatest hits, and a third release which included a DVD-Audio with surround sound mixes of both CD's as well as a collection of music videos.
