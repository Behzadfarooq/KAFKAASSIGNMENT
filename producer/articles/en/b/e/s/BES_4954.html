The BES (Bureau of Engineer Surveyors) was established over 40 years ago as a specialist organisation for engineer surveyors to keep up-to-date with relevant technical information. With regulatory pressures being introduced on a seemingly endless and increasing basis BES has never enjoyed a more important role.

Although BES members are predominantly engineer surveyors, this Professional Sector of the SOE (Society of Operations Engineers) is also of interest to building services engineers, technical health and safety professionals and those involved in engineering facilities.
