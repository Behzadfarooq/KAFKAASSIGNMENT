Best of the Best 2 is a 1993 martial arts movie. The plot follows three of the characters from the original film, and was the last film in the series to have seen a theatrical release.

[edit] Plot

Spoiler warning: Plot and/or ending details follow.

After returning home from South Korea to great acclaim, three members of the U.S. National Karate Team set up their own martial arts studio in Las Vegas. When one member of the team turns up dead after taking part in an illegal underground fighting competition, it is up to the two remaining team members to investigate the murder and exact revenge upon those responsible. They are assisted by an old rival from Team Korea.
