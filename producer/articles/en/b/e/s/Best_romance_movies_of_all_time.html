Best romance movies of all time

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Opinions on what is the best romance movie of all time varies, but top romance film lists do contain some similarities. The movies Casablanca and Singin’ in the Rain are on the following lists and popular romance actors include Cary Grant, Ingrid Bergman and Kate Winslet.

[edit] Best Romance Movies of all Time Lists

[edit] IMDB's Top Rated Romance Titles

Ranked by rating and user votes.

  1. Casablanca (1942) starring Humphrey Bogart and Ingrid Bergman. Directed by Michael Curtiz. This film also placed #6 on IMDB's top 250 movies and received over 93,000 user votes.
  2. Shindler's List (1993) starring Liam Neeson and Ben Kingsley, directed by Steven Speilberg. This film received nearly 140,000 votes and placed #7 overall.
  3. Sunset Blvd (1950) starring William Holden and Gloria Swanson, directed by Billy Wilder. This movie received over 25,000 votes and placed #30 in IMDB's top 250 movies.
  4. Lage Raho Munnabhai (2006) starring Sanjay Dutt and Arshad Warsi, directed by Rajkumar Hirani. This film received nearly 2,000 votes.
  5. It's a Wonderful Life (1946) starring James Stewart and Donna Reed, directed by Frank Capra. This movie received nearly 57,000 votes and placed #29 overall.
  6. Le Fabuleux Destin d'Amélie Poulain, Le (2001) Staring Audrey Tautou and Mathieu Kassovitz, directed by Jean-Pierre Jeunet. Received over 92,000 user votes and placed #31 in IMDB's top 250.
  7. Vertigo (1958) starring James Stewart and Kim Novak, directed by Alfred Hitchcock. received nearly 48,000 votes and placed #35.
  8. Eternal Sunshine of the Spotless Mind (2004) starring Jim Carrey and Kate Winslet, directed by Michel Gondry. received nearly 100,000 votes and placed #39 overall.
  9. Ballada o soldate (1959) starring Vladimir Ivashov and Zhanna Prokhorenko, directed by Grigori Chukhrai. This film received over 1,000 user votes.
  10. Metropolis (1927) starring Alfred Abel and Gustav Fröhlich, directed by Fritz Lang. This movie received over 18,000 user votes and placed #63 overall.

[edit] Rotten Tomatoes Best of Romance

All movies received 100% fresh tomatoes on Rotten Tomatoes critics tomatometer.

  1. Sense and Sensibility (1995)starring Emma Thompson and Kate Winslet.
  2. North by Northwest (1959) starring Cary Grant and Eva Marie Saint.
  3. Singin' in the Rain (1952) starring Gene Kelly and Debbie Reynolds.
  4. The Philadelphia Story (1940) starring Cary Grant and Katharine Hepburn.
  5. A Fish Called Wanda (1988) starring John Cleese and Jamie Lee Curtis.
  6. Red (1994) starring Irène Jacob and Jean-Louis Trintignant.
  7. Before Sunrise (1995) starring Ethan Hawke and Julie Delpy.
  8. The Umbrellas of Cherbourg (1964) starring Catherine Deneuve and Nino Castelnuovo.
  9. Laura (1944) starring Gene Tierney and Dana Andrews.
  10. Bull Durham (1988) starring Kevin Costner and Susan Sarandon.

[edit] TopTenREVIEWS Top Movies by Genre, Romance

Ranked by critical review score, based on a four out of four scoring system.

  1. Singin' in the Rain (1952) starring Gene Kelly and Debbie Reynolds. Scores 3.9 of 4 and 3rd in best of all time.
  2. Casablanca (1942) starring Humphrey Bogart and Ingrid Bergman. Scores 3.9 of 4 and 5th in best movie of all time list.
  3. Gone with the Wind (1939) starring Clark Gable and Vivien Leigh. Scores 3.85 and 14th overall.
  4. Annie Hall (1977) starring Woody Allen and Mia Farrow. Scores 3.6 and 43rd overall.
  5. Notorious (1946) starring Cary Grant and Ingrid Bergman. Scores 3.57 and 55th overall.
  6. Rebecca (1940) starring Laurence Olivier and Joan Fontaine. Scores 3.57 and 56th on the best of all time list.
  7. The Philadelphia Story (1940) starring Cary Grant and Katharine Hepburn. Scores 3.5 and lands 64th overall.
  8. Titanic (1997) starring Leonardo DiCaprio and Kate Winslet. Scores 3.5 of 4 and 72nd overall.
  9. Shakespeare in Love (1998) starring Joseph Fiennes and Geoffrey Rush. Scores 3.45 of 4 and 86th overall.
  10. Before Sunrise (1995) starring Ethan Hawke and Julie Delpy. Scores 3.4 and 89th overall.
