Best Friends

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

For other uses, see Best Friends (disambiguation).

Best Friends is a 1982 feature film, starring Goldie Hawn and Burt Reynolds. They are screenwriters who are romantically involved. Over Christmas vacation, they go visit their families for the first time as a couple.
