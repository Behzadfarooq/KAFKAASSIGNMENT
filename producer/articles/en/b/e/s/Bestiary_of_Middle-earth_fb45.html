This article is a bestiary of the people, creatures and mystical beings of Middle-earth as written about in the legendarium of J. R. R. Tolkien.

[edit] Ainur

Ainu

  • Valar and Valier
    • Aratar - chiefs of the Valar and Valier
  • Maiar (see also Balrogs)

[edit] Dragons (Worms)

Dragons

  • Cold-drakes - those who could not breathe fire
  • Fire-drakes (Urulóki) - those who breathed fire. Both included:
    • Long-worms - legless dragons
    • Legged, non-flying dragons
    • Winged dragons

[edit] Dwarves

Dwarves

  • Durin's folk (Longbeards)
  • Other six kindreds
  • Petty-dwarves (Noegyth Nibin, Nibin-noeg, Nibin-Nogrim)

[edit] Elves (Quendi)

Elves

  • Calaquendi:
    • Eldar
    • Teleri
      • Noldor
      • Vanyar
  • Moriquendi:
    • Avari
    • Sindar and
    • Nandor - Both included the
    • Wood-elves
  • Half-elven
