Besson

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Etymology: In central France dialects, the word "besson" or "bisson" means "twin" and comes from Latin adverb "bis" (two times).

Besson may refer to:

  • Besson (company), a manufacturer of brass instruments
  • Besson, Allier, a commune of the Allier département in France
