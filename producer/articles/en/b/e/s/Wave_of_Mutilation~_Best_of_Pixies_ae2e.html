Wave of Mutilation: Best of Pixies is a compilation album by the Pixies. It was released in May 2004 alongside a companion DVD featuring a live show, promotional videos and two documentaries. Early batches of the record feature a fault on the track "Hey", where Frank Black's opening shout of "Hey" is missing.
