Bessaker

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Bessaker is a village in Roan municipality in northern Sør-Trøndelag, Norway. The village has about 200 inhabitants. Bessaker was previously visited regularly by the Hurtigruten, but today the ships call here on special occasions only, such as the annual fisheries festival (Fiskefestivalen).
