DVD cover of The Best of The Bloodiest Brawls, Vol. 1

The Best of The Bloodiest Brawls, Vol.1 is a DVD showcasing the most hardcore matches in Total Nonstop Action Wrestling's history. It was released on March 14, 2006.

It is a one disc DVD, and is hosted by Mike Tenay and Don West

Matches are shown in full.

[edit] Matches on DVD

1. Monsters Ball Match: Abyss vs. Raven vs. Monty Brown.

  • This was the first ever Monsters' Ball match.

2. Six Sides of Steel match: Triple X vs. America's Most Wanted.

  • The match stipulated that the losing team must disband.
    • This match was voted "2004 Match of The Year."

3. Final Assault match: Abyss vs. Jeff Hardy

  • Aside from the Destination X 2005 DVD, this match can be seen on Enigma: The Best of Jeff Hardy.

4. Six Sides of Steel match: Abyss vs. AJ Styles

  • The winner becomes #1 contender for the NWA World Heavyweight Title.

5. House of Fun match Raven vs. Sean Waltman

  • Raven was originally scheduled to face Jeff Hardy, but he no-showed, and was replaced by Waltman.

6. Dog-Collar match for the NWA World Heavyweight Championship Abyss vs. Raven

7. Rhino & Jeff Jarrett vs. Sabu & Raven

  • This match also shows the TNA return of Jeff Hardy, who was suspended in May.

8. Monsters Ball match: Rhino vs. Abyss vs. Sabu vs. Jeff Hardy

9. Jeff Jarrett & Americas Most Wanted vs. Rhino & Team 3D

10. Barbed Wire Massacre: Abyss vs. Sabu

  • This match won Match of the Year, as voted by the fans on tnawrestling.com

Note: On the dvd cover it says Hardy vs. Abyss is full metal mayham it is not, it is falls count anywhere.

[edit] Bonus Features

1. Special Ringside footage of the Barbed Wire Massacre

2. Father James Mitchell shearing Raven's head after Raven lost a Hair Match to Shane Douglas.

[edit] Other Edition

"Best of Bloodiest Brawls" was originally released in 2003 and was on sale via TNA Wrestling's official website. The difference between the original, out-of-print edition and this edition is that the original consisted of two-discs, and the match range encompassed 2003.
