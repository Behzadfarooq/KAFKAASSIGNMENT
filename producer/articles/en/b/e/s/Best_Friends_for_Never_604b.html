Best Friends for Never: A Clique Novel is the second novel in The Clique Series. It was written by Lisi Harrison who was formerly a head writer for MTV Production.

[edit] Plot summary

Spoiler warning: Plot and/or ending details follow.

The book begins at Massie Block's house, during William Block's birthday dinner. Massie receives a phone call from Alicia, who informs her that two girls from their school have been gossiping about her and suggesting that her social status at school is declining. Alicia has also found out that they are planning to throw a Halloween party in which both boys and girls are invited. Massie hangs up and returns to the party, where Claire's younger brother Todd spills grape juice on Massie's new skirt, claiming it was an accident. When Massie is angry at Todd, Claire speaks in his defense and tells Massie it's "just a skirt". Massie retaliates, and eventually she and Claire start a bet that Massie can't go shopping for a month and Claire can't wear the same outfit for a month. The loser has to wear Massie's old winterwear for a week.

Massie then decides to beat Becca and Liz at throwing a boy-girl Halloween party. She strikes up a deal with Claire on the terms that Claire helps Massie convince her parents to let her throw a Halloween party, Massie will help Claire convince her parents to let her have a cell phone. Massie's mother Kendra Block agrees to Massie's proposition if Claire cohosts the party. Massie does not do her share of the deal, and Claire does not get a cell phone. Massie tells her friends, Kristen, Dylan, and Alicia her plans to throw a party. They nastily inform Becca and Liz, as well. She plans for Kristen, Dylan, Alicia and herself to design and wear provocative "Dirty Devil" costumes, but later on Massie's mother forces Massie to include Claire in the costume plans.

Massie hires a prestigious party planner to assist her. Massie instructs Claire to send invitations to the girls who she considers "losers", with the intention that the invitations will appear to be from Claire only, to discourage them from attending. Claire instead signs the invitations with Massie's name and phone number. All of the "losers" RSVP to Massie. As revenge, when Massie brings the "Dirty Devil" costumes to school for her friends to wear, she purposefully does not bring Claire's costume, further alienating Claire. At school, Claire asks Massie why Massie didn't include her in wearing their costumes to school. Massie replies that she never wanted Claire to be part of their plan, anyway. Claire runs to the nurse's office to find solace, and while she's talking to the nurse, a girl runs in. She explains that while trying to copy Massie's costume by cutting her sweater, but accidentally cut herself in the process. The nurse realizes other students are also copying Massie's skimpy costume and promptly tells the principal. Massie and her friends are sent to the principal's office where they are lectured about their inappropriate costumes and their parents are informed. The principal forces them to change into unflattering clothing from the school Lost & Found.

At Massie's party, Massie is excited to see the boy she has a crush on, Cam Fisher. Kristen and Dylan are crushing on the same boy, Derrick "Derrington" Harrington. Kristen's mother does not allow Kristen to attend the party after receiving the phone call from the school. This leaves Dylan free to flirt with Derrington. Alicia meets a new friend, an empty-headed girl named Olivia, who returns to OCD after having a nose job. Massie's crush Cam meets Claire at the party, unbeknownst to Massie. She likes him and it is assumed that he likes her back. They win a dance contest together. Claire gives her part of the prize to him.

When they get return to school the following Monday, they find out from the principal that OCD is adopting a uniform policy after the Halloween costume incident. Massie takes refuge from her angry peers in a bathroom. Claire finds her and suggests that she ask to design the new school uniform. The fashion department manager refuses, but agrees to hold a school-wide uniform design contest and have the winner's design made into a uniform.

The competition begins. Massie and Claire participate, along with many of their classmates. However Massie's housekeeper mistakenly disposes of their creations. Claire suggests they restart their projects and work together, and if Massie works with her she will call their bet off. Massie agrees and asks Dylan and Kristen to be their models. Claire finally feels like a part of Massie's group of friends.

At the design contest their main competition is Olivia and Alicia. Olivia and Alicia's design is inspired by Ralph Lauren's Blue Label and includes jeans, a tweed blazer, strappy sandals and a necktie used as a belt (an idea stolen from Massie). Massie, Claire, Dylan and Kristen are last to present. Their uniform comes in four different colour schemes designed to flatter the wearer's skin tone and general colouring. Their presentation is well-received.

The two finalists are Alicia and Olivia's design and Massie and Claire's design. The audience and participants vote by placing a thimble in the box of the finalist they want to win. Massie paints her thimble purple for good luck. Alicia and Olivia are announced as the winners. Crushed, Massie inspects the boxes and finds her purple thimble in Alicia and Olivia's box and realizes that Alicia and Olivia have switched their boxes. Massie decides to wait for revenge against them and "pounce" when the time is right.

After the show, Massie finds out that Derrington likes her. Both Massie and Claire's families go out for dinner, and Claire receives a note from Cam via her brother Todd (who goes to the same school as Cam) saying that he likes her. Claire decides to show Massie the note later (unaware that Massie likes Cam), and the novel ends.
