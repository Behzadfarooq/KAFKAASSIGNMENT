The Bestival 2007 is the upcoming fourth installment of the Bestival a boutique music fesival at Robin Hill on the Isle Of Wight. It will be held between September 7 - September 9. Tickets went on general sale on Friday March 2.

Many of the estabished features will be retained including the Bollywood Bar, The Farmers Market, Come Dancing Tent and the Fancy dress parade. here are some exciting new features announced including -

  • Swim To Bestival - A charity initiative involving swimming the Solent with the assistance of Royal Navy lifeguards for protection.
  • Club Dada - A cabaret/circus themed club tent, which will aim to embosy the spirit of the festival in a nutshell.
  • Laughter Clubs - Inspired by the influx of laughter clubs of India, the many laughter stations will encourage the festival goer to appreciate the benefits of laughter. [1]
