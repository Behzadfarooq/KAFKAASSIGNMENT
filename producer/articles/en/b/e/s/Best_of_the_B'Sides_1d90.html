Best of the B'Sides is a compilation of b-sides by the British heavy metal band Iron Maiden, released on 4 November 2002. Best of the B'Sides is a collection of b-sides that were recorded by the band over the years. Best of the B'Sides was released in 2002 as a part of Eddie's Archive.
