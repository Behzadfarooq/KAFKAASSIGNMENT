Best in Show (2000) is a mockumentary film following five entries in a prestigious conformation dog show. The film shows how much dog owners love their pets, and how carried away they can get. Christopher Guest directed, having written the script with Eugene Levy.

Many of the people involved also made This Is Spinal Tap (1984), The Return of Spinal Tap (1992), Waiting for Guffman (1996), A Mighty Wind (2003), and/or For Your Consideration (2006).

The film won American, British, and Canadian Comedy Awards.

This film is number 38 on Bravo's "100 Funniest Movies".
