Q: What is your name, rank, and serial number?

Erik Söderlund, div2, 666.

Q: How did you get into Quake?

I actually got into Quake thanks to an old friend of mine, Circle. I didn't have a connection, so I played against Frogbots a lot and sometimes on LAN, never online though... That was way back, around the year 1999. I got back into Quake about one and an half years ago when I got my first internet connection.

Q: What is it about the great game that you like?

Speed, that's something that I find very attractive among many other things, like solidarity between all the Quake players, like everyone knows each other, having fun and talking. The teamplay has a big factor in it too, I won't forget tourneys like EQL, Duelmania, Ride My Rocket and other tourneys.

Q: Who is/are your favourite player/s?

Hum, hard one... I would like to say ParadokS as an 4on4 player with his amazing teamplay skills and the way he plays. My favourite duel player is Griffin without a doubt. Just watch that guy, heh?

Q: How did you get into making videos?

I downloaded some Quake movie, think it was some QHLAN movie and thought it was cool. So I dowloaded a video program that some guy recommended called Sony Vegas Video and started making small QW clips, adding a few transitions and effects, and it didn't go my way. I released my first movie December 9, 2004 called Noobpov and liked the editing part alot, so I continued.

Q: What is your favourite Quake video and why?

My favourite Quake video would be Apartment Huntin', I just love the whole idéa, a whole new category in movie making. And how well done it is, in every way.

Q: Who is your favourite Quake filmmaker and why?

Depends, pez is the best frag-movies maker, with his knowlage in 3D programs and in Adobe After Effects, he's just great. And vb for his tricking movies. And new idéas like tweaking the gun and stuff like that, always cool to watch his movies. Come back?

Q: What is some of the software have you used when making your videos?

As I mentioned above, I'm using Vegas Video 5.0 (editing stuff), and besides that I'm using photoshop 9.0 for picture editing, name making and so on. VirtualDub (compressing), Particle Illusion, Java .pk3/.md3 model viewer, QView. I think that's all.

Q: Which of your own videos is your favourite and why?

I don't like any of them ;D, but if I have to choose it would be Showdown. Don't know why actually, maybe 'cause I put so much editing time into it. Around 70 .png's, movie clips & sound files.

Q: What else are you interested in, apart from Quake?

I don't have a life apart from Quake... Nah, I'm a big movie fan, so much of my spare time goes to watching movies and being with my girlfriend and friends.

"Soon comes a new movie that I have vaent laengta for ja vet itt hur länge".

That's all from me.
