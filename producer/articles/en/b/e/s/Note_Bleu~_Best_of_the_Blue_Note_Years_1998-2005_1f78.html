Note Bleu: Best of the Blue Note Years 1998-2005 is a greatest hits compilation from Medeski Martin & Wood featuring songs from all of their albums released on Blue Note Records. (The band has since started an independent label, Indirecto Records.)

A Deluxe Edition CD was also released, featuring 3 bonus tracks and a bonus DVD.
