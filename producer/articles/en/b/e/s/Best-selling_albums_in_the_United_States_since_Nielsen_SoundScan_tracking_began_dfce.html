Best-selling albums in the United States since Nielsen SoundScan tracking began

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

This is a Top ten best-selling albums of sound scan era in the United States according to the Recording Industry Association of America. Nielsen SoundScan began tracking sales data for Billboard on March 1, 1991. The list of albums sales are between 1991 to 2006 (see below):
