BEST of UETOAYA -Single Collection- is Aya Ueto's first best album. It sold 28,213 copies in its first week and peaked at #5. This album contains only one new song, "Shimokita Ijou Harajuku Miman" which is a cover of the theme song of the drama Shimokita Sundays (starring Aya herself) performed by Fumiya Fujii. It became her first Weekly Top 5 entry in over 3 years.
