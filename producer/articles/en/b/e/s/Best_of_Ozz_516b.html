Best of Ozz is a compilation album by Ozzy Osbourne, released in 1989. This album was released without his consent and is a record company greatest hits rather than an artist approved greatest hits.

[edit] Track listing

  1. "Over The Mountain"
  2. "Secret Loser
  3. "Goodbye To Romance"
  4. "Shot In The Dark"
  5. "Mr. Crowley"
  6. "Bark At The Moon"
  7. "Crazy Train"
  8. "Centre Of Eternity"
  9. "Diary Of A Madman"
  10. "The Ultimate Sin"

[edit] Notes

  • This compilation doesn't have any songs for the previous album No Rest for the Wicked.
