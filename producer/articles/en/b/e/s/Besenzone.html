Location of Besenzone in Italy

Besenzone is a comune (municipality) in the Province of Piacenza in the Italian region Emilia-Romagna, located about 120 km northwest of Bologna and about 20 km southeast of Piacenza. As of 31 December 2004, it had a population of 982 and an area of 23.9 km².[1]

Besenzone borders the following municipalities: Alseno, Busseto, Cortemaggiore, Fiorenzuola d'Arda, Villanova sull'Arda.
