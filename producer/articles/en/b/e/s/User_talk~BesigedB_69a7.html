[edit] Commons pics

Hi from Adrian! It's an interesting question whether I need to have pity on the 56K people (I've got 2 megabit broadband from TeleWest which is eighteen times quicker than 56K). My pics straight off the camera are about 800K but I find little effect on the quality of the pic if I compress them down in Photoshop to between 300K and 350K so I'll carry on doing that.

On the subject of Commons upload , I'll do a trial. I'll upload my next five pics (all of Nissan cars) onto it and then I'll see how well it displays my image info and my copyright notice. If the user has to click on something like "Click here to see Image Information" then I probably won't do it. If I find I can still have my image info below the pic (as on en) then I will. I'll let you know - Adrian Pingstone 22:01, 31 Jan 2005 (UTC)

  • Fun Fun Fun. By default the commons copyright notice isn't displayed on en:, but you can edit the page on EN to display it yourself. An example is on Image:Protest 0008.JPG - the image is on commons but I've inserted the copyright notice onto en manually. In future versions hacks like that probably won't be needed, but for now it's a fair enough workaround. --BesigedB (talk) 22:17, 31 Jan 2005 (UTC)
