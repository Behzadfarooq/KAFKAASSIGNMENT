Bessie is both a surname and a woman's first name. The name Bessie comes from the English origin. In English The meaning of the name Bessie is: Diminutive of Elizabeth, meaning my god is bountiful, or god of plenty, used since the 16th century. Sometimes used as an independent name.

Bessie may refer to:

  • Bessie (lake monster) — a lake monster in lake Erie
  • Bessie Smith — blues singer.
  • Alvah Bessie — screenwriter blacklisted as part of the Hollywood Ten
  • Diamond Bessie — a murdered prostitute
  • Bessie Coleman — first African-American female aviator
  • In the science fiction television series Doctor Who, Bessie was the third Doctor's souped-up yellow Edwardian roadster.
  • In popular culture the name of a cow.
  • Bessie is also the name of an asphalt-paver in the movie Cars.
