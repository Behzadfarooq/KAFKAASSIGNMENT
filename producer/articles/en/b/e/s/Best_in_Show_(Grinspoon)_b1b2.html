Best in Show is a compilation of Australian band Grinspoon's best ofs, and was released to coincide with the ten year anniversary of the band. The first song, 'Sweet As Sugar', was recorded for the purposes of the best of and appropriately is a return to the classic style of the band that was abandoned for their latest album, Thrills, Kills & Sunday Pills. After the first song, the tracks are listed chronologically, from the song "Champion" recorded in 1995, to Hard Act to Follow recorded in 2004. The linear notes contain photography of the band from their beginnings to the present, and details the history of the band in the form of a fairy tale about the 'Knights of Grinspoon' from the 'Land of Oz.'
