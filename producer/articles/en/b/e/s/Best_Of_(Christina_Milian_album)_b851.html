Best Of is the first compilation album from R&B singer Christina Milian. The album includes her hit singles and songs that she hand picked herself from her first three studio albums, Christina Milian, It's About Time, and So Amazin', though, only one track, Say I, was chosen from So Amazin', possibly due to the fact that it is her lowest selling album. L.O.V.E., Down For You, and Spending Time were all released as singles in Japan and charted despite not having videos.

The album was only released in Japan and some other Asian countries on November 22, 2006, but it is unknown if there will be a release in the U.S. or Europe. An original 2007 mini-calendar is included, with a different picture of Milian for every four months.
