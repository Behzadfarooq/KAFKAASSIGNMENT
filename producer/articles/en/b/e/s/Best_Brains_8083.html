Best Brains, Inc. was a television production company, based in Eden Prairie, Minnesota. It is best known for its creation of the comedy/sci-fi television program Mystery Science Theater 3000, aka MST3K (1988â€“1999). The company ceased producing TV shows in 1999 and closed its studio; however, it still sells MST3K merchandise.

During the KTMA season of MST3K, the company called themselves Hair Brain Productions.
