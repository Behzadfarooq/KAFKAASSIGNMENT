Eternity: Best of 93 - 98
Studio album by Takara
Released 1998
Genre Hard rock
Label Point Music & Saraya Recordings
Producer(s) Jeff Scott Soto

Eternity: Best of 93 - 98 was the 1st compilation album by hard rock band Takara released in 1998 on Point Music & Saraya Recordings.

  14. "Don't Wanna Be Alone"
  15. "Blind in Paradise"
  16. "Say U'll Stay"
  17. "Restless Heart" (Acoustic)
  18. "Your Love" (Acoustic)

  • Gary Schutt – bass
  • Carl Demarco – bass
