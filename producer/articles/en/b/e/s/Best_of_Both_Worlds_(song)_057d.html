"Best of Both Worlds" is a song performed by Miley Cyrus for the character Hannah Montana. It is Cyrus' first hit song. It is also the theme song for the Disney Channel Original Series Hannah Montana. The song debuted on the Billboard Hot 100 at number nonety-two in early August 2006.[1] It is included as the first track on the Hannah Montana soundtrack.

The song is about having the "best of both worlds". On the television show, Miley Stewart loves a great life as a regular girl, with great friends and a nice family. And on stage, she is a pop idol.

This song is also featured on the GirlNext CD.

On 26 February 2007, "Best of Both Worlds" entered the British charts at number forty-three.
