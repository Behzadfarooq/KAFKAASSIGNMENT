Besos en la frente (English language:Kisses in the Front) is a 1996 Argentine drama film directed and written by Carlos Galettini. The film premiered in 5 September 1996 in Buenos Aires and was nominated for two Silver Condor Awards in 1997. The film starred Leonardo Sbaraglia.
