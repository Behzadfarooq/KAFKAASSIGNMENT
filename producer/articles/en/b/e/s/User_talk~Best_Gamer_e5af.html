[edit] Posting an Image

Thanks, but the bigger problem is that I don't know how to post good images. Can you help me out? Best Gamer 25 July 2006

Well - I can't guarantee that the images will be good - that's up to you! But to put an image onto Wikipedia, select the 'Upload file' link in the toolbox on the left of your browser window. That takes you to a dialog - hit the 'Browse' button and select the image file that you'd like to upload. You can add comments to the picture at that time. When Wikipedia displays your image, you'll see a title at the top that will be something like 'Image:A Car and A Video Game.jpg' (the name will depend on what you told Wiki to call it - or whatever it guessed by itself). You need to remember that title - write it down someplace. Now, when you want to insert that picture into an article, use something like:
 [[Image:A Car and A Video Game.jpg|right|thumb|250px|This is a photo of a car and a video game]]
The image will appear as a thumbnail image to the right of the text and will be resized to 250 pixels.
MOST IMPORTANT: Make sure you have the legal rights to upload the picture - you must take care about this. You cannot just scan in an image from a book or magazine or grab a photo from someone's web page without permission. Photos that you took yourself are usually OK though. I can't stress this enough.
SteveBaker 16:12, 28 July 2006 (UTC)
