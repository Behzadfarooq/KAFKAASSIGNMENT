Beside Yourself is a compilation album by Australian progressive rock band The Church, combining the iTunes Exclusive Tracks EP, the Forget Yourself United States bonus disc, tracks from the Australian "Song In Space" single, and unreleased tracks from the Forget Yourself sessions. It was released in 2004 on the

The artwork includes reproductions of several original drawings/paintings by Steve Kilbey.

The eclectic compilation is an oddity in the Church discography, although it bears a passing resemblance to the band's 1990 collection of b-sides and outtakes, A Quick Smoke at Spot's.

The collection was limited to a pressing of 500 copies in stores, though the band's official web store may have copies available from time to time.
