Bessatsu Margaret (Betsuma for short) is a shōjo manga magazine published monthly in Japan by Shueisha. It is targeted at girls aged roughly 12-17.

[edit] Mangaka and series serialized in Bessatsu Margaret

  • Kazune Kawahara
    • Koukou Debut
    • Sensei!
  • Kira
    • Massugu ni Ikou
  • Masami Nagata
    • Renai Catalogue
  • Aya Nakahara
    • Lovely Complex
  • Kaoru Tada
    • Ai Shite Knight
    • Itazura na Kiss
  • Mitsuba Takanashi
    • Akuma de Sourou (The Devil Does Exist)
    • Beniiro Hero (Crimson Hero)
