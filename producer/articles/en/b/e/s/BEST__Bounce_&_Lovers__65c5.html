BEST ~Bounce&Lovers~ is the third best-of album by Japanese pop star, Kumi Koda. The tracklisting for the CD will only contain Koda's ballads, and feature a track penned by Hideaki Tokunaga, while the DVD will feature certain music videos reedited to show just the dancing scenes. The album is a time limited purchase and can be purchased in any method from March 2007 to May 2007. It is released to coincide with Koda's sixth anniversary since debut.
