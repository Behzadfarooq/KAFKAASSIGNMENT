Starr Struck: Best of Ringo Starr, Vol. 2 is Ringo Starr's second official compilation album, released in 1989 through the Rhino Records label. The successor to 1975's Blast From Your Past, it rounds up Starr's highlights from 1976's Ringo's Rotogravure to 1983's Old Wave and encompasses label stays with Polydor, Atlantic, Portrait and RCA.

Starr Struck: Best of Ringo Starr, Vol. 2 was only released in the US. Consequently, it saw the first introduction of four tracks from Old Wave which had not seen issue there up to that time.

Never charting anywhere, Starr Struck: Best of Ringo Starr, Vol. 2 went out of print during the 1990s.
