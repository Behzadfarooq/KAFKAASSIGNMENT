Best Denki

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Best Denki store at Ngee Ann City, Singapore.

Best Denki Co., Ltd. (株式会社ベスト電器, Kabushiki-gaisha Besuto Denki?) (TYO: 8175 ) is a Japanese electronics retailer with outlets across Japan, Singapore, Malaysia, Hong Kong, Indonesia and Taiwan. Best Denki headquarters is located in Fukuoka. Previously, in Singapore, it was known as "Yaohan Best", when it started operations there in 1985. After Yaohan's bankruptcy, the store was changed to its current name. The store has also a service sector that does repair, transportation of goods and after sale service for its franchise stores. In Singapore, the store is located in Ngee Ann City, a high-end shopping centre in Singapore, which it its flagship among the nine Singapore stores. Recently it opens its newest flagship store in Jakarta at Senayan City. The store is the largest Best Denki store outside Japan.
