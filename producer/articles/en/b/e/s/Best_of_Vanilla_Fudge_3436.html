Best of Vanilla Fudge is a best of album by the American psychedelic rock band Vanilla Fudge. It was released by Atco Records in 1982.

[edit] Track listing

  1. "Illusions Of My Childhood (Part 1)"
  2. "You Keep Me Hangin' On"
  3. "Illusions Of My Childhood (Part 2)"
  4. "Shotgun"
  5. "Some Velvet Morning"
  6. "Ticket To Ride"
  7. "Take Me For A Little While"
  8. "Illusions Of My Childhood (Part 3)"
  9. "Where Is My Mind"
  10. "Season Of The Witch"

The album is currently out of print.
