Best (compilation)

Best! is a greatest hits compilation for 1990's pop band Jellyfish. The album was released in 2006 and featured twenty tracks. Eight of the tracks were non-album while the other twelve consisted of songs pulled from the bands two (and only) albums, Bellybutton and Spilt Milk. The song "Ignorance is Bliss" was taken from a Nintendo games complitation entitled "Nintendo: White Knuckle Scorin'" and is inspired by the video game Super Mario Brothers. The song "No Matter What" is a live cover of a Badfinger song.
