Chronicle: Best of the Works is a Dr. Dre greatest hits album released by Death Row UK/Ron Winters Productions, the UK branch of Death Row Records, on May 26, 2002. It contains all of Dre's hit singles from that era as well as those he produced for other artists on Death Row. A U.S. version of this album was released in 2006.
