Best of the Chrysalis Years is a compilation album made up of tracks from the Ramones five albums on Chrysalis Records, Brain Drain, Mondo Bizarro, Acid Eaters, ¡Adios Amigos!, and Loco Live. It was released on May 28, 2002, by EMI International, and is not available in the United States. The album was re-released in 2004 with a new tracklisting as, The Best of The Ramones.
