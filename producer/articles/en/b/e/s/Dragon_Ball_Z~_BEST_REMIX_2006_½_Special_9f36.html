Dragon Ball Z: BEST REMIX 2006 ½ Special (ドラゴンボールZ BEST REMIX 2006 ½ スペシャル) is a remix compilation album of theme and image songs from the anime Dragon Ball Z. It was released on December 20, 2006 by Columbia Records in Japan only.
