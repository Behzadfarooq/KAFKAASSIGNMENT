Time Pieces: Best of Eric Clapton is a greatest hits album by a blues songwriter Eric Clapton. It was released in May 1982 (see 1982 in music) with his smash hit singles except from No Reason to Cry and Another Ticket. The album cover features Eric Clapton's favourite guitar, Blackie.

[edit] Track listing

  1. "I Shot The Sheriff" (from 461 Ocean Boulevard)
  2. "After Midnight" (from Eric Clapton)
  3. "Knockin' On Heaven's Door" (released as a single)
  4. "Wonderful Tonight" (from Slowhand)
  5. "Layla" (from Layla and Other Assorted Love Songs)
  6. "Cocaine" (from Slowhand)
  7. "Lay Down Sally" (from Slowhand)
  8. "Willie And The Hand Jive" (from 461 Ocean Boulevard)
  9. "Promises" (from Backless)
  10. "Swing Low, Sweet Chariot" (from There's One in Every Crowd)
  11. "Let It Grow" (from 461 Ocean Boulevard)
