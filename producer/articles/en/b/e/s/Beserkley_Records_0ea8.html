Beserkley Records was an independent record label based in Berkeley, California.

The label was founded circa 1975 by Matthew King Kaufman and featured numerous recording artists including Earth Quake, The Rubinoos, The Smirks, Jonathan Richman & The Modern Lovers and most notably the Greg Kihn Band.

Beserkley was licensed to Rhino Records in 1986.
