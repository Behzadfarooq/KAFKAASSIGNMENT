Best Of Def Leppard is a compilation of some of Def Leppard's greatest songs, including Pour Some Sugar On Me, Love Bites, and Bringin' On The Heartbreak.

Best Of replaces 1995's Vault as the definitive collection of Def Leppard's work. In fact, Best Of is basically Vault with the addition of "Long Long Way To Go".

A 2CD limited edition was released in a slipcase with more obscure fan-favourite album cuts on the second disc. In the UK, a cover of The Kinks "Waterloo Sunset" was included as a bonus. This was a teaser for the band's 70s cover album Yeah!

"Best Of" was released in a number of territories around the world, except Canada and the US. A 2CD best of called Rock of Ages was issued there instead.
