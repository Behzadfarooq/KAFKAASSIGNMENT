Beyond the Astral Skies is the third album from Uli Jon Roth's Electric Sun band. It was released in 1985 on EMI.

[edit] Track listing

  • All songs composed by Uli Jon Roth
  1. The Night the Master Comes 4:16
  2. What Is Love? 3:24
  3. Why? 4:51
  4. I'll Be There 5:02
  5. Return (Chant of Angels) 3:27
  6. Icebreaker 2:39
  7. I'm a River 4:45
  8. Angel of Peace 3:43
  9. Eleison 6:56
  10. Son of Sky 2:21

[edit] Participants on this Electric Sun album:

  • Uli Jon Roth - Lead/harmony vocals, guitars, keyboards, bass
  • Clive Bunker - Drums, Orchestral Percussion
  • Michael Flexig (as Michael Flechsich) - Lead vocs on 2, 9, 10, harmony vocs on 1-4, 6-10
  • Ule Ritgen - Bass guitar, harmony vocs on 2, 4
  • Elizabeth Mackenzie - Soprano and Alto on 9
  • Robert Curtis - Violin and Viola on 9
  • Jenni Evans - Harmony vocs on 1, 5, 6, 8
  • Dorothy Patterson - Harmony vocs on 5, 6
  • Nicky Moore - Vocals on 1
  • Zeno Roth - Harmony vocs on 1, 3, 6, 8
  • Rainer Przywara - Harmony vocs on 2, 3, 4, 7, 9, 10
