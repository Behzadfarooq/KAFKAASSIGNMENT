Beyond Thee Infinite Beat
Studio album by Psychic TV
Released 1990

Beyond Thee Infinite Beat (subtitled Ravemaster Mixes) is a remix album by Psychic TV. The album is compromised of remixes songs from Towards Thee Infinite Beat.
