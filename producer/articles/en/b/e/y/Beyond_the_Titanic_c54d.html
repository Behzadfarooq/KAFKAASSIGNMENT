Beyond the Titanic is a text adventure game written by Scott Miller for MS-DOS, published by Apogee Software (now 3D Realms). In the game, the player has to escape from the sinking ship Titanic.

The game was originally distributed as shareware. It was one of the two games (the other being Supernova) that were released by Apogee not using the Apogee Model, but as traditional shareware.

Beyond the Titanic was re-released as freeware by Apogee to hang in on the hype around the Titanic movie on March 10, 1998. See also list of commercial games released as freeware.
