Beyond Tomorrow was also the name of the US-made show that was created by the then-new Fox Network as one of it's debut shows. It used the same format as Beyond 2000. (as well as it's research team, IIRC) It only lasted two seasons, but it preped me for seeing 2000 when it started appearing on US cable a couple years later. --12.107.104.2 15:22, 28 July 2005 (UTC)
