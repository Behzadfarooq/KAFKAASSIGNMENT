Beyond Logic Records

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Beyond Logic Records is a popular Mumbai based psy-trance record label. It has been instrumental in bringing quality psy-trance to Indian scenario.Beyond Logic tied up with several international record labels to form a team to distribute their albums in India.

[edit] History

Formed in 2003, Beyond Logic is dedicated to music and culture, through music that captures the positivity of your life-force. Beyond Logic creates a space for people to have good times, celebrate life and provide refreshing and memorable new productions of full-on music.

[edit] Mission statement

Beyond Logic envisages to spread positivity and happiness through the trance dance experience, using meditative states of bliss and freedom to reveal worlds within our world. Beyond Logic also promotes deserving artists
