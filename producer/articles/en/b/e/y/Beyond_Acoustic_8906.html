Beyond Acoustic is a music album of Philippine acoustic band MYMP (Make Your Momma Proud). It was released under Ivory Records.

[edit] Track listing

  1. "Get Me"
  2. "Tell Me Where It Hurts" (cover of Tell Me Where It Hurts by Kathy Troccoli)
  3. "Kailan" (cover of Kailan by Smokey Mountain)
  4. "Magical Feeling"
  5. "Jam (Set Your Spirits Free)"
  6. "When I Dream" (cover of When I Dream by Crystal Gayle)
  7. "Would You Be My Girlfriend"
  8. "Talaga Naman"
  9. "Rush"
  10. "Fast Car" (cover of Fast Car by Tracy Chapman)
  11. "Ginoo, Walay Sukod" (Praise Song)
  12. "Sa Kanya" (original version by Ogie Alcasid)
