Beyond The Magic Door was a children's television show on WBBM-TV in Chicago, Illinois in the early 1980's. The show was a sequel of sorts to the earlier program The Magic Door Television Theatre

The theme song began with the following lyrics

If your mind is open
And free to explore
I know a place called Dreidl
It lies beyond the magic door
It's over the rainbow, through the mighty seas,
...
Come follow me, through the magic door

Dan Castellaneta briefly played the role of Detective Farblonget. Other characters included J. P. Potter, a disembodied hand wielding a cigar.
