Beyond The Blue Mosque
Compilation album by Muslimgauze
Released 1997 April 24
Label Staalplaat STCD 116

Beyond The Blue Mosque is a compilation album of Muslimgauze material. The compilation was released in 1997 then repressed in 2003.

[edit] Song origins

  • Tracks 1 & 4 from Izlamaphobia (MUSLIMLIM 001)
  • Track 2, 7 & 8 from Azzazin (MUSLIMLIM 003)
  • Track 3 from Return Of Black September (MUSLIMLIM 004)
  • Track 5 from Deceiver (MUSLIMLIM 008)
  • Tracks 6 & 9 from Zealot 10" (MUSLIMLIM 006)
    • Track 6 is supposed to be an edit of Baseej from the Zealot 10" EP, but is actually a completely different track.
