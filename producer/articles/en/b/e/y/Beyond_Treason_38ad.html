Beyond Treason is a 2005 documentary by William Lewis and Joyce Riley. The filmmakers show that the American government continue to orchestrate a large cover-up of the causes of Gulf War syndrome.The illness is caused by exposure to depleted uranium (DU) munitions - both live and spent, poor GMP practices and sloppy science by the US pharmaceutical industry, and medical experimentation on American soldiers.

The documentary features interviews by Doug Rokke (Director Of The U.S. Army Depleted Uranium Project), Dennis Kyne (NBC Medical Specialist Veteran's Advocate), Joyce Riley (Spokesperson, American Gulf War Veterans Association ), Mark Zeller (SSGT, Veteran - Op. Desert Storm ) and Bob Jones (SSGT RET. Veteran - Op. Desert Storm )

Beyond Treason won the Grand Festival Award at the 2005 Berkeley Film Fest.
