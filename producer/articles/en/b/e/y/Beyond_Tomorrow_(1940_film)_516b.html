Beyond Tomorrow is a 1940 fantasy film directed by A. Edward Sutherland. In it, a trio of ghosts try to help two young lovers whom they knew when alive.

In 2004, an alternate color version of the black and white film was produced by Legend Films and released by 20th Century Fox, under the title Beyond Christmas.
