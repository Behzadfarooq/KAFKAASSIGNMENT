Pitfall 3D: Beyond the Jungle is a video game released in 1998 for the Sony PlayStation by Activision.

In this adventure, Harry meets a girl named Mira who enlists his aid in freeing her people from "The Scourge", an evil force bent on controlling her world.

One of the most distinctive features of this game is the voice of Bruce Campbell as Pitfall Harry.

Just as in its predecessor Pitfall: The Mayan Adventure, the original Pitfall! for the Atari 2600 is available as an Easter Egg.
