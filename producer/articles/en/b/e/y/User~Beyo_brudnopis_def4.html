[edit] Society

There are thousands of us...but we are ruled by the three (Quinn to Blackwood)

Mor-Taxan society is divided into three functional casts:ruling class,scientists and lower class. Ruling class is represented on Earth by the triad called The Advocacy while their leaders known as The Council (never seen on screen) remain on Mor-Tax to supervise evacuation from their dying planet. To lower class belong mainly so called 'field units' who are undercovered in human society while most of scientist stay in Advocacy Headquaters at Nevada Desert to provide medical and engeenering service.

There is little known about life on Mor-Tax. The planet has garden-like environment, rich with vegetables and plants with some their forms being sentient.We don't know any name or locations of alien cities as well as continents or seas.There are some indication of alien love to art provided by Renegade Quinn whoose works are very valuable among humans.
