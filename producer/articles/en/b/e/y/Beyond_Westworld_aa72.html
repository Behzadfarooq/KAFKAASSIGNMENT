Beyond Westworld was a short-lived 1980 TV show that carried on the stories of the two feature films, Westworld and Futureworld. It features John Moore as Security Chief of the Delos Corporation. The story revolves around John Moore having to stop the evil scientist, Quaid, as he plans to use the robots in Delos to try and take over the world. Only five episodes were aired.

[edit] Episode Synopsis

Spoiler warning: Plot and/or ending details follow.

[edit] Westworld Destroyed

'Writer - Lou Shaw'
'Director - Ted Post'

John Moore is assigned to hunt down Quaid who has some androids that he proposes to use to further his own ends.

[edit] My Brother's Keeper

'Writer - Lou Shaw and Haward Dimsdale'
'Director - Rob Holcomb'

Quaid blackmails the owner of an oil company and John and Pamela must find the android in the company's ranks.

[edit] Sound of Terror

'Writer - Martin Roth'
'Director - Paul Stanley'

Quaid gets his hands on some uranium and John and Pamela must find another android who is hiding in a rock band.

[edit] The Lion

'Writer - Martin Roth and David Bennett Carren'
'Director - Jack Starrett'

Delos circuitry is found in a car explosion and John and the rest of the Delos team must try and work out Quaid's new plan.

[edit] Take-over

'Writer - Gregory S. Dinallo, Steven Greenberg and Aubrey Solomon'
'Director - Don Weis'

Quaid's target is the police officer in charge of the state Governor's visit. John and Pamela must stop Quaid and identify the police officer who is actually an android.
