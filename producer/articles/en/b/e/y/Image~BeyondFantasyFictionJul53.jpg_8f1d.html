Cover of July 1953 issue of Beyond Fantasy Fiction. Artist is Richard Powers. Copyright is either Powers, or more likely Galaxy Publishing Corporation, who published the magazine.

[edit] Rationale of fair use

It is believed to be unlikely that an alternate free licensed work currently exists or can be created.

The Beyond Fantasy Fiction article links to this image. Though the picture is originally copyright, I Mike Christie (talk) feel it is covered by fair use because:

  1. No free or public domain images have been located for any issues of this magazine.
  2. The image does not limit the copyright owners' rights to distribute the book in any way, and the magazine is no longer in print in a version with this cover
  3. The article discusses the magazine, and this is an image of the magazine. Hence the article forms a commentary on the image.
  4. The cover is being used for informational purposes only, and its use is not believed to detract from the original magazine in any way.
