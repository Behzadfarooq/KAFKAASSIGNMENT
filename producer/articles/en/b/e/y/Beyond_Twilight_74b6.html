Beyond Twilight is a Progressive Metal band from Denmark with symphonic elements to their sound being the use of keyboards and harmonic vocals but are high pitched and usually found in most power metal bands, which can be used as another genre classification to their style. The band was formed in 1990 under the name of Twilight, but the band changed its name to Beyond Twilight in 1999.

The band released three studio albums so far, The Devil's Hall of Fame on July 23rd, 2001, Section X on March 29th, 2005 and For The Love Of Art And The Making on April 21st, 2006 under the Massacre Records Label.

Beyond Twilight play Progressive Metal with the influence of Horror films. Fans often categorize the band as Cinematic Metal.
