Beyond Witch Mountain

Beyond Witch Mountain was filmed in 1982, and was the second sequel to the 1975 Disney film Escape to Witch Mountain. It was originally intended to be the pilot for a TV series, but no networks thought that viewers would take the show seriously, and so a second episode was never made.

The film is rarely shown on The Disney Channel or any other station, and has never been released on home video.
