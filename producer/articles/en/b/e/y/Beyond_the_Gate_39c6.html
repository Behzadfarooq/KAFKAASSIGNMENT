Beyond the Gate is the third album, and first mini album from the band Moi dix Mois. This album was created after the band members Juka, Kazuno, and Tohru left the band in 2005. The only known band members featured on this album are Mana, K (brought in during the Invite to Immorality tour in 2005), and Seth (the new lead vocalist). The album was released on March 1st, 2006.

The album was released in two editions. Like the other albums before it, it as released in regular and limited editions. Both the regular edition and limited edition were released in a standard jewel case with the CD. The limited edition includes a sticker, poster, 3-page fold out jacket, and has a different album cover. The regular edition comes with a 16 page booklet.
