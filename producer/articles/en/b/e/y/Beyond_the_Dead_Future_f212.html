Beyond the Dead Future is the first non-Bemani album of Takehiko Fujii, under the alias SLAKE. It was distributed by Eddie Lehecka, AKA djparanoia of bemanistyle.com, in July 2006. The cover was designed by DJ_Yuz ,now a member of the site's design team, during an album cover contest held in March 2005. Every copy was distributed through mail personally.[original research?]
