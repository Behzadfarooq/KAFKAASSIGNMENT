[edit] Precedent is to have one article per song, not one per separate recording or version

Again, as I told you on User talk:Irreplaceable, the precedent on Wikipedia is to have one article on every song, not one on every separate recording or version or single release. That means one article on the Paragons, Blondie and Atomic Kitten versions of "The Tide Is High", but two articles on Lily Allen's "Shame for You"/"Alfie" double A-side: one on "Shame for You", and one on "Alfie". Please stop creating separate articles on double A-side singles and cover versions that duplicate information already in other articles. Blanking pages is unacceptable as well. Extraordinary Machine 22:33, 15 February 2007 (UTC)
