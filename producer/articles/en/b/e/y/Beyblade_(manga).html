The Beyblade manga is VERY different from the anime. The English version is poorly translated by Viz and has some Engrish in it.

Chapter 1:Special: The King-
Introduces Tyson, Chief, Hurricane Jin, and the Blade Shark members along with Tyson's first blade: Dragoon Megaro Arm and his Holy Monster: Seiryuu Blue Dragon.

Chapter 2: Let it Rip!-
Tyson enters the Beyblade community Battle Tournament and fights the second Blade Shark, Carlos.

Chapter 3: Never Give Up!-
The Chief runs off with Tyson's Megaro Arm to find out the secret of Seiryuu's power which he used to defeat Carlos. When the Blade Shark four (including Carlos) take the Chief hostage for the Megaro Arm, Tyson must rescue him from the Blade Shark's hideout in the Hiwatari Construction Yard and face Carlos once again!

Chapter 4: Legendary!-
The Blade Shark's boss is introduced: Kai Hiwatari. When the mysterious shinobi Huricane Jin appears, Tyson and Kai must battle for control of Megaro Arm. Megaro Arm has trouble dealing with Kai's Ultimate Frostic Dranzer and it's Fire Bomb Spin attack.

Chapter 5: Secret of the Blue Dragon-
After Kai and Tyson's fight, Tyson came out victorious but with his Megaro Arm shattered. Tyson tries to repair his Megaro Arm with a new beyblade his older brother Hitoshi hid when he was younger, but Hurricane Jin arrives to say that the new blade is still no match for Ultimate Frostic Dranzer. Jin tells Tyson and Chief about the Holy Monster's and their power. Tyson's Holy Monster is Seiryuu of the East and Kai's Holy Monster is Suzaku of the South. Soon Tyson's Megaro Arm evolve's into Dragoon Grip Attacker.

Chapter 6: Max-imum!-
Tyson and the Chief meet a foreign blader from the U.S. named Max, who's father owns a Spinning Top Hobby Store.

Chapter 7: Tyson versus Max-
Tyson's Grip Attacker and Max's Protoshell face off with Tyson losing. After Max tells Tyson that his strategy is too obvious, Tyson develops a Super Attack called the Vanishing Attack just in time for the Beyblade Super Battle tournament.

Chapter 8: A Meeting of the Powers-
The Beyblade Super Battle Tournament has begun. The Chief must face Kai and his new blade: Auto-Change Balancer. This new blade has an impressive gimmick which allows it to switch from offensive to defensive in a heart beat. Tyson must battle the Blade Shark four and deafeat Carlos one last time!

Chapter 9: Introducing...Draciel!-
Max and Kai battle to decide who advances to the finals. Max uses an improved defensive strategy with his new blade: Draciel Metal Ball and his Holy Monster Genbu. The Chief mets a mysterious man named Mr. B who is actually a BBA Scientist.

Chapter 10: The Fourth Legend!-
Tyson and the Chinese blader Ray must face off in the semi-finals! Ray's Driger S has a terrifying secret: it has a Spin Gear System! Mr. B encourages the Chief to create a Spin-Gear System for Tyson. Tyson's Spin-Gear equipped beyblade is then known as Dragoon S.

Chapter 11: Tyson versus Kai!-
Tyson and Kai face off in a match with Tyson having the upper hand.

Chapter 12: Begin the Ultimate Battle!-
Tyson continues fighting Kai. Kai's mysterious past is revealed and Jin is actually Tyson's older bro Hitoshi! Tyson becomes the Beyblade Champ of Japan!

Chapter 13:Journey to the land of Legend!-
Tyson,Max,Chief and Kai all take a trip to China to see the project that Hitoshi and Tyson's dad been digging up. Ray shows up and tells them that China is the birth place of the Directional Series Holy Beast. Tyson meets up with a Chinese tribe warrior named Lee who has an old score to settle with Ray.

Chapter 14:Tyson vs. the White Tigers-
The Japan team face off against the China team to see who gets Ray's allegiance.

Chapter 15:Demolition Boys-
A team of Russian beyblade killers appear known as the Demolition Boys. They take everyone at the Tate Hobby Store hostage. Ray battles Tala only to get his Driger S destroyed and Tyson fights Tala only to lose and end up with a shattered Dragoon S. The Commissioner tells them about the Team Wolrd Championships coming up and the Chief has a plan for Tyson and Ray. Max receives the Draciel S.

Bonus Chapter:Special Section Introducing USA's Ace Micheal-
Tyson and Max travel to Boston,Massachusetts to see America. They get separated and Tyson meets the strongest Beyblader in the U.S.A, Micheal Summers.

Chapter 16:Beyblade World Championship!-
The World event begins! Tyson and Ray receive new blades:Dragoon F and Driger F respectively. They defeat the Brazilian team and Kai has no intention of joining the team.

Chapter 17:Rematch!Tyson vs. Micheal-
The Japan team fights the American team and Tyson must face Micheal again with the help of a new Hyper Attack: Phantom Hurricane.

Chapter 18:The Super Attack Foiled!-
The China team drops out of the championship because of their loss to the super strong European team! Kai fights Johnny and when he looses, Robert agrees to fight Kai to see the strength of his pride.

Chapter 19:Four-Fold Power!-
Max and Kai get Draciel F and Dranzer F respectively and are ready to fight the Euro team in a decisive rematch.

Chapter 20:A Battle of Pride!The Euro match-
Kai and Robert fight one last time. When Tyson must fight Johnny,the Demolition Boys invade the World Championships and challenge everyone on their private isle in Russia!

Chapter 21:The Final Arena-
The Beybladers of the world are ready to take the Russian team on, but fighting on their Russian war-like territory might be the biggest mistake in history! With everyone fighting the Demolition Shock Troopers, Tyson must fight Tala with a new part his bro gave him. Near the end of the battle,Dragoon F transforms to the Dragoon V.

Explosive Shoot Special Section:Daichi-
A sidestory that tells of young Daichi and his quest to become no.1 is no joking matter.

Part 1:Introducing Daichi-
Daichi Sumeragi leaves Japan Mountain after the death of his father to do what his father asks him to do: become the best in Japan! With a beyblade given to him by his dad before his passing,Daichi sets out to become No.1!

Part 2:Blader Spirit-
Daichi becomes the gang leader of a local Beyblade team and fights Ray only to lose, but he comes to realize that Beyblade is about fun not winning.

Part 3:The Birth of Strata Dragoon-
Daichi fights Carlos with the power of his dad's dragon friend. The dragon which will be born in strata:Strata Dragoon.
