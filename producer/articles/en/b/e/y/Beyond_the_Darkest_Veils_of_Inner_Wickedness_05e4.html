Beyond the Darkest Veils of Inner Wickedness is a title of the second demo released on a cassette by Swedish heavy metal band Therion. Limited to 500 copies.

[edit] Track listing

  1. "Macabre Declension"
  2. "Megalomania"
  3. "Paroxysmal Holocaust"

[edit] Credits

  • Peter Hansson - guitar
  • Oskar Forss - drums
  • Erik Gustafsson - bass guitar
