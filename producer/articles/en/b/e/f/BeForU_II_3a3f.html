BeForU Second Album- BeForU II is the second Japanese studio album by all female J-Pop group BeForU, released February 14, 2006.

[edit] Song Details

[edit] シナリオ (album version)

"シナリオ" (scenario) is originally a song from the Bemani video game "GuitarsFreaks V & DrumMania V". It is the first, and only song to feature the three newer members, BeForU NEXT, exclusively.

  • Genre: J- Rock

[edit] Freedom (album version)

Freedom originates from "Dance Dance Revolution Party Collection. The original is close to J-Trance and is credited to the entire group, although only Riyu is heard. The album version is sung again by Riyu Kosaka with rock elements.

[edit] I am...(album version)

Originally featured in "beatmaniaIIDX12 HAPPY SKY", "I am" is by Miharu Arisawa. Originally written by NAOKI for TËЯRA, it was given to Miharu as a solo.

[edit] Under the Sky ～SAYAKA's solo new version～

"Under the Sky" was a J-Eurotrance song first appearing on beatmania IIDX12 HAPPY SKY by Sayaka Minami with platoniX. platoniX consists of composer Tatsuya Shimizu and vocalist Junko Hirata. Sayaka's new version is slower, and its instrumentals consist of guitar instead of synths.

[edit] KI・SE・KI(album version)

"KI・SE・KI" was the first BeForU song to feature all of the newly added members for a total of six and first appeared on their "KI・SE・KI" single. The version featured here has more rock elements.

[edit] Morning Glory

"Morning Glory" is an exclusive song and was later featured in Dance Dance Revolution SuperNOVA. It contains elements from both rock guitar and J-Pop genres.

[edit] Eternal

"Eternal" is an exclusive song for this album sung by Yoma Komatsu.

[edit] BLACK OUT～I want to be・・・!! (album version)

"BLACK OUT" is a song from the Bemani video game "GuitarFreaks V2 & DrumMania V2". The original version contains different lyrics and is sung and by Noria and rapping by Riyu Kosaka. This version features vocals from all six members.

  • Bemani Genre: J-Rock

[edit] HONEY♂PUNCH

"HONEY♂PUNCH" is an exclusive song for this album sung by Riyu Kosaka, and later featured in "Dance Dance Revolution SuperNOVA" in edited form. The edited form was featured in Pop'n Music 13 Carnival CS as an exclusive CS song. It is also to be used as a beginner-only difficulty track in the upcoming beatmania IIDX 14 GOLD.

  • Bemani Genre: HAPPY DANCE POP.
  • Also can be found on:
  1. pop'n music 13 Carnival CS for Play Station 2
  2. Beatmania IIDX 14 GOLD

[edit] PEACE(^^)v

"PEACE(^^)v" is an exclusive Dance song for this album, and later included in "Dance Dance Revolution SuperNOVA".

[edit] 摩天楼

"摩天楼" (Matenrou) means "Skyscraper" and is an exclusive song for this album sung by Risa Sotohana. She is also the composer and plays the keyboard.

[edit] DIVE 2 0 0 6

"DIVE" was BeForU's first song, which debuted in Dance Dance Revolution 5th Mix. Since then, it has had many reincarnations. The full version of the original can be found on their first album. This new version features vocals from all six members and more rock guitar and piano elements.

  • Other iterations:
  1. DIVE
  2. DIVE ~more deep & deeper style~
  3. DIVE ~into your heart~ [NAOKI feat. Paula Terry]
  4. DIVE TO THE NIGHT [Riyu Kosaka]
  5. DIVE TO THE NIGHT (TËЯRA Works Remix) [Riyu Kosaka]
  6. DIVE TO THE NIGHT (NM Rock style) [Riyu Kosaka]

[edit] 初恋

"初恋" (Hatsukoi) means "First Love" and is an exclusive song for this album sung by Sayaka Minami.

[edit] HAPPY HAPPY BIRTHDAY

HAPPY HAPPY BIRTHDAY is an exclusive song for this album sung by Noria Shiraishi.
