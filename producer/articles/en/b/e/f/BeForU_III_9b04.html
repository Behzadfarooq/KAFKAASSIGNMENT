"BeForU III", is the third studio album by the hit Japanese pop group BeForU. It was released on March 14,2007. It will be a cd and a dvd, and will feature three of BeForU's singles, Red Rocket Rising, Get set GO!! 〜BeForU Astronauts Set〜, and Strike Party!!!. It also includes new songs, such as ⑥Rainbow and 僕の中にある僕じゃないボクだけのボクと僕 (Rough Translation: My center there is not me only me and me)
