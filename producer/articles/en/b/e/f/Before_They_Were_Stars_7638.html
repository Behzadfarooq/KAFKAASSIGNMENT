Before They Were Stars was a 1996 30-minute American ABC television show which was hosted by former Happy Days actor Scott Baio.

The term "Before They Were Stars" has since become widely used by television shows and magazines when featuring segments and articles on famous actors in their lesser-known performances (and non-entertainment jobs).

[edit] Description

As the name implies, this TV series showed film clips, commercials, television clips and screen tests of celebrities before they became famous.

Baywatch TV and film actress Yasmine Bleeth appeared in episode number 4, season number 1. She described the 1980 childhood film called Hey Babe! in which she starred with actor Buddy Hackett when she was only 12 years old. Her character's name was Theresa O'Brian.

In episode number 7, season number 1, Yasmine Bleeth again appeared on Before They Were Stars in clips from the popular soap opera Ryan's Hope when she was only 16 years old. She starred on the soap opera from 1985-1989. Her character's name was Ryan Maeve Fenelli Hyde. Her fellow Ryan's Hope actor, Grant Show also appeared in the clip.
