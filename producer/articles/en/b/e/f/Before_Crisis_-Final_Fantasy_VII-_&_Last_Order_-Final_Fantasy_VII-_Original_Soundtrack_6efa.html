Before Crisis -Final Fantasy VII- & Last Order -Final Fantasy VII- Original Soundtrack

BEFORE CRISIS -FINAL FANTASY VII- & LAST ORDER -FINAL FANTASY VII- Original Soundtrack
Soundtrack by Takeharu Ishimoto
Released May, 2007
Genre Soundtrack
Label Square Enix

Before Crisis: Final Fantasy VII & Last Order: Final Fantasy VII Original Soundtrack is a soundtrack featuring music from the mobile action RPG, Before Crisis: Final Fantasy VII, and the OVA Last Order: Final Fantasy VII, composed and produced by Takeharu Ishimoto, to be released on May, 2007 and priced at ¥2100.
