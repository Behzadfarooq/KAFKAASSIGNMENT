Before Hell Freezes Over is the eighth studio album from rapper Busta Rhymes. It is due out sometime during spring of 2007. The album features production from Timbaland, Dr. Dre, Focus, Pharrell, Swizz Beatz, and J Dilla, among others. And also may feature appearences from 50 Cent, The Game, Spliff Star & DMX.

Busta said on MTV News that he'll be working with a lot of longtime collaborators as well as some people he's never had the chance to work with before. "You're gonna get your traditional Busta Rhymes-and-Pharrell collabo," he said. "My man Focus from the Aftermath crew; Dr. Dre; the late, great J Dilla got work on the album. It's gonna be great â€” look forward to the new bang-out."[1]

Busta Rhymes mentioned that he's trying to get 50 Cent and The Game on the same track for this upcoming album.[2][3] Due to the recent diss track "Body Bags" by The Game, there probably won't be the collaboration with 50, Game, and Busta. The Game disses Tony Yayo and 50 Cent on the song. There are also rumours that Timbaland or one of his productions will appear on the album, after a video surfaced on YouTube. [4]
