Star Wars: Black Fleet Crisis #1: Before The Storm is the first in book in The Black Fleet Crisis trilogy and is set in the fictional Star Wars universe. It is authored by Michael P. Kube-McDowell and was published by Bantam Books on March 1, 1996.
