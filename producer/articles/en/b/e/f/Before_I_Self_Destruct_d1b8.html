Before I Self Destruct will be 50 Cent's fourth and final album in his contract with Interscope, excluding his actual last album which will be a Greatest Hits album. Before I Self Destruct is "two songs from completion" and will be released on February 4, 2008 which was confirmed by 50 Cent in an April 4 interview with Hot 97.[1]
