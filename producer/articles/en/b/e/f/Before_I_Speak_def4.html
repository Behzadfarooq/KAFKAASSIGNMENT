Before I Speak
Studio album by Kyle Riabko
Released 19 April 2005
Recorded  ???
Label Columbia

Before I Speak is Kyle Riabko's new album. The album is in R&B style with elements of funk, soul and rock. Liz Phair and Riabko sing the Chemistry together.
