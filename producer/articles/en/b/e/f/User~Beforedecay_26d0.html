From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

I'm only getting started with Wikipedia, and my contributions are more through talks and suggestions than direct modifications. I have been contributing to The Internet Movie Database more extensively, bringing both informative updates and personal reviews. My interests include photography (both "old school" and "new school"), filmmaking and cinematography (both "old school" and "new school"), illustration (both "old school" and "new school"), and repeating myself whenever necessary. I also like learning the various alphabets of the world that are in use today, and being able to understand other cultures in the original language. Though I have taken part in several worldwide art projects and published many of my works, I am not pursuing an artistic career at the moment. I have a very good knowledge of Super-8 filmmaking and equipement as well as their manufacturers, of world cinema History of the 1970s to the 1990s, of film music and film music composers, the history of cinematography and film cinematographers (also called directors of photography), of creation-related equipment such as writing instruments, and paper, as well as their manufacturers, and of the operating system Mac OS 9. My name is Fabrice and I live in France.
