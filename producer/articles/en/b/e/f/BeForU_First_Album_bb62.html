BeForUファーストアルバム (BeForU First Album-BeForU) is the first Japanese studio album by all female J-Pop group BeForU, released in 2003.

[edit] Song Details

[edit] チカラ (LIVE BAND style)

"チカラ" (Chikara) means "power" or "strength" in Japanese. Originally a song from "GuitarsFreaks 9th Mix & DrumMania 8th Mix, the lead voice in this song is by Riyu and chorus by the others members.

  • Bemani Genre: GIRLS ROCK EXTREME.
  • Also can be found on:
  1. pop'n music ee'mall 2nd Avenue and pop'n music 9 CS
  2. Dance Dance Revolution SuperNOVA

[edit] ♥LOVE² シュガ→♥

"♥LOVE² シュガ→♥" is pronounced as "Love Love Sugar". It originates from "pop'n music 8" and is a solo by Noria.

  • Bemani Genre: EUROBEAT
  • Also can be found on:
  1. Dance Dance Revolution EXTREME

[edit] DIVE

"DIVE" was the very first song BeForU ever performed, with which they debuted in Dance Dance Revolution 5th Mix.

  • Bemani Genre: J-POP

[edit] ヒマワリ

"ヒマワリ" or "Himawari is the Japanese word for Sunflower. It is a solo song by Riyu Kosaka which first debuted in "GuitarsFreaks 10th Mix & DrumMania 9th Mix".

  • Bemani Genre: J-HAPPY HARDCORE
  • Also can be found on:
  1. beatmaniaIIDX 12 HAPPY SKY
  2. Dance Dance Revolution ULTRAMIX 3, and later Dance Dance Revolution SuperNOVA

[edit] BABY LOVE

"BABY LOVE" is an exclusive song for this album sung by Noria, and later appeared in beatmania IIDX 10th style.

  • Bemani Genre: ITALO POP

[edit] LOVE♥SHINE

"LOVE♥SHINE" is a solo song by Riyu appearing in Dance Dance Revolution EXTREME. This is a longer version.

  • Bemani Genre: J-HAPPY HARDCORE
  • Also can be found on:
  1. beatmaniaIIDX 9th style

[edit] BRE∀K DOWN! (LIVE BAND style)

"BRE∀K DOWN!" is a song originating in "DDRMAX 2:Dance Dance Revolution 7th mix" and a similar version, BRE∀K DOWN! -GF&DM STYLE- appeared in "GuitarFreaks 8th Mix & DrumMania 7th Mix. This is a longer version with Live style instrumentals.

  • Bemani Genre: GIRLS ROCK from DDR

[edit] Silvia Drive (extended)

"Silvia Drive" is a song appearing on beatmania IIDX 9th style.

  • Bemani Genre: HAPPY HANDBAG

[edit] Firefly

"Firefly" was the second song that appeared in Dance Dance Revolution in its sixth iteration "DDRMAX:Dance Dance Revolution 6th mix".

[edit] ☆shining☆ (LIVE BAND style)

"☆shining☆" is a song originally from "pop'n music 9" and another version, ☆shining☆（GF&dm style）is featured in "GuitarFreaks 11th Mix & DrumMania 10th Mix"

  • Bemani Genre: GIRLS ROCK
  • Also can be found on:
  1. Toy's March 2

[edit] SHOOTING STAR

SHOOTING STAR is an exclusive song for this album sung by Riyu Kosaka, and later featured in "beatmaniaIIDX 10th style".

  • Bemani Genre: J-POP

[edit] TEARS

"TEARS" is trance song from "Dance Dance Revolution EXTREME". It is a solo by Noria under the alias "EK". It is notable for being the only trance song any BeForU member has ever done.

  • Bemani Genre: TRANCE

[edit] true...(2003 album version)

"true..." first appeared in "DDRMAX:Dance Dance Revolution 6th mix". This song also appeared in Riyu's first single "true...".

  • Bemani Genre: J-POP.

[edit] GRADUATION～それぞれの明日～ (unplugged version)

GRADUATION～それぞれの明日～, or ～sorezore no ashita～ means "～for each tomorrow～" is from Dance Dance Revolution EXTREME. The full original version can be found as the "Staff Roll" on the DDR EXTREME OST. This version is an unplugged, piano interpretation.

  • Bemani Genre: ソツギョウ (Sotsugyo)
