"Because of You" is a 2007 song by singer and songwriter Ne-Yo, produced and co-written by StarGate. It is the first single from his sophomore album, of the same name, Because of You. The single started radio play the week of February 4, 2007. [1] [2]

[edit] Song information

Five other versions of the song have surfaced, one with an extended ending, one featuring rapper AC, S-One, Joe Budden and another with Bone Thugs-N-Harmony. The song debuted on the Billboard Hot 100 at number 84 in March.[1] The music video was premiered on Access Granted on March 14, 2007. A first look of the video was shown on TRL also. Also, the video has garnished over 1.5 million views on Youtube. This song was also featured in the film Are We Done Yet?.
