Becky Griffin

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Becky Griffin (born on December 21, 1977 in Givatayim, Israel) is an Israeli model, Presenter and actress. She was born to Jewish Parents, Yemenite mother and American father. Becky was first discovered as a commentator in the sport channel for basketball games. She also worked as a reporter in the "Erev Tov" show and in "In Out". Becky also starred in the movie "Matana Mi Shamayim".

In May 12, 2003, she won the yearned job oppertunity of becoming a MTV VJ in MTV Europe. Until 2005 Griffin stayed in Britain and appeared on the channel as the host of the European chart.

In December 2003 she was chosen to lead the campaign of the fashion network "Castro".
