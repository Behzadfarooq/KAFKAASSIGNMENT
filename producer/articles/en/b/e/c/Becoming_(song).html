"Becoming" is a song by the heavy metal group Pantera from the album Far Beyond Driven. The song features a unique guitar riff by Dimebag Darrell which involves a DigiTech Whammy pedal. Vinnie Paul uses extensive double bass in this song, also. The song hints at Phil Anselmo's childhood.
