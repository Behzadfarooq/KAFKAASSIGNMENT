"Because We Want To" is a UK Number One pop song by English singer-turned-actress Billie, which topped the charts in the summer of 1998. With this song Piper became the youngest ever female singer (just 15 at the time) to debut at the number one spot in the UK charts.

However, the success of the song was attributed to the England football team being knocked out of the 1998 FIFA World Cup in France. "Three Lions 98" had been re-recorded by the Lightning Seeds, along with David Baddiel and Frank Skinner had been No1 for two weeks, but with England's demise against Argentina, the song dropped down the charts.

[edit] Billie looks Back

As stated in her new autobiography, Growing Pains, Billie Piper says that the song had a 'Kiddy Anthem', and that the video was slightly strange, with a cgi kid that comes to life from a billboard and dances with her.
