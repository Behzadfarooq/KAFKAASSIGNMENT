Beckenham Hill railway station is in the London Borough of Lewisham in south London. It is in Travelcard Zone 4, and the station and all trains are operated by Southeastern. It serves the mainly residential area of Southend and parts of Bellingham and Downham.

[edit] Service

The typical off-peak service is:

  • 2 trains per hour (tph) on Monday to Saturday to London Blackfriars calling at all stations via Peckham Rye
  • 2tph on Sunday to London Victoria calling at all stations via Peckham Rye
  • 2tph to Sevenoaks via Bromley South and Swanley.
