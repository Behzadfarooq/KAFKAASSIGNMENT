Becca Gardner

Becca Gardner (born 1990 in Bellevue, Washington) is an American actress. Since age 2 she lived in Lake Tahoe, Nevada. Gardner lives together with her parents, brother and two sisters.

[edit] Filmography

  • What Ever Happened to Alice (2003) playing Bonnie
  • The Practice playing Anna Viorka in episode Victims' Rights aired November 30 2003
  • An Unfinished Life (2005) playing Griff Gilkyson
  • Grey's Anatomy playing Bex in episode Begin the Begin aired January 15, 2006
