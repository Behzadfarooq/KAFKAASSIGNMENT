Become You is the eighth studio album by the Indigo Girls, released in 2002.

  1. "Moment Of Forgiveness" (Amy Ray) – 3:13
  2. "Deconstruction" (Emily Saliers) – 4:15
  3. "Become You" (Ray) – 3:46
  4. "You've Got To Show" (Saliers) – 4:44
  5. "Yield" (Ray) – 2:48
  6. "Collecting You" (Saliers) – 4:31
  7. "Hope Alone" (Saliers, Roboff) – 3:56
  8. "Bitterroot" (Ray) – 2:46
  9. "Our Deliverance" (Saliers) – 4:12
  10. "Starkville" (Ray) – 4:22
  11. "She's Saving Me" (Saliers) – 5:04
  12. "Nuevas Señoritas" (Ray) – 4:34

[edit] Credits

Amy Ray - Vocals, acoustic guitar, mandolin, bouzouki, harmonica
Emily Saliers - Vocals, 6 & 12 string acoustic guitar, classical guitar, electric guitar, bouzouki, mandolin
Carol Isaacs - Hammond B-3, Wurlitzer, piano, accordion, penny whistle, recorder, percussion
Clare Kenny - Acoustic and electric bass
Brady Blade - Drums and percussion

Guest musicians: Michelle Malone (vocals on "Moment Of Forgiveness" and "Hope Alone", vocals and harmonica on "Bitterroot"), Dan Higgins (saxophone on "You've Got To Show") and Jerry Hey (string arrangement on "Hope Alone").
