Becca Lish

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Rebecca "Becca" Lish (born December 26, 1959 in San Francisco, California) is an American voice actress.

Lish is a graduate of Yale University, where she received a degree in philosophy. She married fellow actor Andrew Mutnick in 1992;[1] they have two children.
