Becky! is an e-mail client published by Rimarts. Unlike most conventional e-mail clients, which are aimed for light-users by being easy-to-use, Becky! tends to be more complicated than usual. In fact, its main design is to be more functional than other clients.

Becky! has a plugin ability which can be created by someone out of the dev team and can be added to the functions of the software. One of such plugin which has gained Becky's popularity is a Hotmail receiver.
