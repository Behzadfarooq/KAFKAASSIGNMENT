Beckwith, Ontario

Beckwith is a township in eastern Ontario, Canada, in the County of Lanark on the Mississippi River. It is located within Canada's National Capital Region.

According to the 2001 Statistics Canada Census:

  • Population: 6,046
  • % Change (1996-2001): 10.7
  • Dwellings: 2,490
  • Area (km².): 240.12
  • Density (persons per km².): 25.2
