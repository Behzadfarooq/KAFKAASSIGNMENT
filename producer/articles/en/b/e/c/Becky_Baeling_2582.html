Rebecca "Becky Baeling" Bahling (born May 1, 1977, Rochester, Michigan) is an American Dance-pop and dance music singer.

She began having success on Billboard's Hot Dance Music/Club Play chart with her debut album, Becstasy, which was released on Universal Records in 2003. Baeling's most successful single to date has been "Getaway," which hit #1 on the U.S. dance chart.

She won the Best New Artist category at the 2003 International Dance Music Awards held in Miami, Florida. She holds a BFA in musical theatre from The University of Michigan. She is also an aspiring actress and comedian, having had her TV sitcom debut on UPN's "ALL OF US" in 2005.

She is also a TV host/music journalist and can currently be seen on virtually continuous rotation on DirecTV's CD USA program, "The 101". Fans patiently wait for her sophomore album once she announces one.

She sang the National Anthem at a NASCAR Nextel Cup Race.
