Becker High School is located in Minnesota Public School District 726 in Becker, MN. Other schools in the district are Becker Prinary School, Becker Intermediate School, and Becker Middle School. It provides education for grades 9-12.

[edit] Sports

Becker High School's team sports are known as the Becker Bulldogs. Becker High School provides the following sports:
