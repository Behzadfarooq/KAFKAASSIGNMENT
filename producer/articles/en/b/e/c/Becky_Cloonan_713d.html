Becky Cloonan (born 23 June 1980 in Pisa, Italy) is an American comic book creator known for her manga-influenced artwork and extreme prolificacy. She created minicomics and was part of the Meathaus collective before collaborating with Brian Wood on Channel Zero: Jennie One in 2003. Since then, her profile (and workload) has steadily risen; her best-known work to date has been the 12-issue comics series Demo (2004), also with Wood.

Cloonan's first solo graphic novel, East Coast Rising Volume 1, was released by Tokyopop in 2006. She is also currently collaborating with writer Steven T. Seagle on the Vertigo comics series American Virgin.
