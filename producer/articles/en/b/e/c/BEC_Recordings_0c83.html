BEC Recordings is a Christian rock record label that is a subsidiary of Tooth and Nail Records.

The label is home to many bands that, while falling under the rock/alternative umbrella, tend to appeal to a broader, more mainstream audience, as opposed to Tooth and Nail or its other subsidiaries Solid State Records and Uprok Records, which tend to be more focused on young adult and teenage audiences. BECs major artists include The O.C. Supertones, Kutless, and Jeremy Camp.
