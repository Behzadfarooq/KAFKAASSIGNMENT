Beckenham Train Station, is a Transperth train station 13.8km apart from Perth Train Station, in Western Australia, on the Armadale Line. Openned in 1968 it was originally named Higham Railway Station. Due to its proximity to the original Cannington Train Station, the later was moved to a new site near Cecil Avenue during the upgrade of the rail line from Diesel to Electric as there needed to be further distances between stations. Thornlie Line trains do not stop at Beckenham Train Station.
