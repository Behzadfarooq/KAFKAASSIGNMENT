Because I Love It Vol. 2 will be the second mixtape to be released by RnB singer Amerie. It will be featured in her MySpace this Spring 2007, according to the "Outro" track in the Vol. 1 of the said mix tape, before the album is officially released.

Some tracks that are rumored to be in the Vol. 2 mix tape are "Some Like It" and "Crazy Wonderful", both confirmed earlier.

If the album is due to be released in April, this mixtape should be out very soon.
