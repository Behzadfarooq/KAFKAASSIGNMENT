Becky Mantin

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Becky Mantin is a British television presenter

Mantin started her media career at Norwich based radio station Broadland 102, and then transferred to television with Anglia Television, firstly solely as a weather girl and then reporting and presenting regional programmes including the short-lived consumer show Late Night Checkout.

She then started to appear on the ITV National Weather, Channel Five, Sky Television and on This Morning as an occasional reporter. From 25 February 2005 she ceased duties with Anglia, and permanently transferred to the ITV National Weather team working alongside Martyn Davies and Sian Lloyd and replacing Chrissie Reidy who was moved to regional programming with ITV in London.
