Becky Johnson

Becky Johnson (born February 14, 1978) is a Canadian comedian, writer, actor and improviser, best know for her work in the improv comedy duo Iron Cobra (with Graham Wagner). She has been nominated at the Canadian Comedy Awards for her work in improvised theatre work and has also done a fair amount of work producing comedy shows and improv festivals in her hometown of Toronto.

Aside from live theatre, Johnson has done sporradic work in independent films. Some titles include: Secondary High (2002); The Water Game (2002); The Affected Turtleneck Trio (2001); I'm Not Martin! (2000); and Uranium (2004).

Becky Johnson has also had some attention for her work as a playwright, although not much is known of her work outside of Toronto and there appears to be nothing published. She publishes her own zines and underground comics, reviewes of which pop up in Canada's Broken Pencil Magazine from time to time.
