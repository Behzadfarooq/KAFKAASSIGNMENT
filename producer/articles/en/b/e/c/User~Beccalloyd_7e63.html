Rebecca Lloyd has attracted worldwide attention for her original compositions for films, television and ensembles. Most notably, Rebecca managed to stage a major coup in 2002 by securing a commission from China's largest TV Network that saw her compose, conduct and arrange the music for the "World History Series” which went on to be screened to an audience of over 1 billion people. Her compositions and arrangements have been performed to entertain the likes of Rudy Giuliani to Australian Prime Minister John Howard. For further information about Rebecca, please visit: www.rebeccalloyd.com

In addition to her own projects, Rebecca Lloyd has recently joined the staff at the New York post-production studio “World Wide Audio Inc” as a resident composer. Since moving to New York in June 2006, Rebecca has already scored music for two Warner Bros Feature Films. “Twisted Fortune” featuring Charlie Murphy, Dave Attell, Carol Alt as well as “And Then Came Love” featuring Vanessa Williams and Eartha Kitt. Most recently, her music was used on Oprah.

In addition to Rebecca’s composing abilities, she is also an experienced conductor and producer. As a classically trained performer herself, Rebecca has a brilliant repour with musicians and is well respected by fellow performers and her ability to bring a real orchestra to life in minimal amount of time.

Inspired by dance and creative movement, Rebecca composes music for film, television and performance with rhythm and drive, emphasising particular synchronicity with her subject. Fuelled by passion and flair this modern classical composer is a fresh young face to be watched carefully, with pieces ranging from pure classical to cross over classical and now moving into experimentation with modern contemporary music and collaborating with the likes of rock and dance producers, Rebecca is set for big things world wide.

Rebecca has over 100 original compositions played by classical instrumentalists. With 8 major releases, including 2 solo albums and 4 collaborative. Later in 2006, 2 meditations CDs which Rebecca wrote the music for will be released internationally. Rebecca also has many other credits to name, including numerous compositions for International Award Winning Company “Messages on Hold”, to add to this Rebecca’s compositions can be heard regularly on ABC and FM stations throughout Australia.

As a producer, Rebecca has released 8 albums of her compositions, her ability to work with the musicians allows her the flexibility to take any project from beginning to end with assurance of a world class product.

To gain a better understanding of all facets of the film and editing process, Rebecca has studied post graduate studies in “Film and Television” and is well acquainted with ‘Final Cut Pro’ and similar editing and graphics programmes.
