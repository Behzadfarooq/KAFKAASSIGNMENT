Bechtle

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Bechtle is a German technology company listed on the TecDAX stock index.

Established in 1983, the company operates from over 60 locations throughout Europe, with the HQ in Germany and offices in Switzerland, UK, Italy, Netherlands, France, Austria, Spain and Belgium. As of 2005 it employed 3,900 people and has an annual turnover of approximately 1.2 billion euros. The Head Office, known as Bechtle Platz 1, is located in Neckarsulm in the South of Germany.

Bechtle are one of the largest IT resellers in Germany and they, along with their UK, French and Swiss offices, are Microsoft Gold Certified partners.
