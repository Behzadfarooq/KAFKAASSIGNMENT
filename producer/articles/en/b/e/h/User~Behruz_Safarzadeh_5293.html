Behruz Safarzadeh (بهروز صفرزاده in Persian) (born April 25, 1969 in Tehran, Iran) is an Iranian scholar, graduated from The University of Tehran, 1992.

He works now at The Iranian Academy of Persian Language and Literature, Department of Lexicography, Tehran, Iran.
