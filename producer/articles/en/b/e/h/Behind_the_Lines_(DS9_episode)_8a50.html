"Behind the Lines" is a sixth-season episode of Star Trek: Deep Space Nine.

Spoiler warning: Plot and/or ending details follow.

Captain Benjamin Sisko creates a risky plan to disable a critical sensor array of the Dominion; while on Terok Nor, Kira, Jake, Rom and Odo seek to undermine the Cardassian/Dominion Alliance. Kira soon comes to believe Odo has betrayed the Federation; he has physically and mentally joined with an enenmy shapeshifter. Sisko has to deal with being promoted out of the central command seat. Dax has taken over the USS Defiant. Additionally, the self-replicating mine-field which Rom designed and which prevents enemy backup from coming through the wormhole is in danger of being disabled.
