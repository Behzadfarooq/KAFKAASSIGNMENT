Behrouz A. Forouzan

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Behrouz A. Forouzan (born 1944) is a Professor at the Computer Information Systems department of DeAnza College. He has written many textbooks about computer science, networking, programming and databases. He graduated at the University of California, Irvine.
