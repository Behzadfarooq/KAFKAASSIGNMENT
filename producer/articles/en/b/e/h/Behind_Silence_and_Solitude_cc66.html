Behind Silence and Solitude is All That Remains's first full length album. It was released on March 26, 2002 by Metal Blade Records.

The album is different from the bands future efforts as this one is void of any clean vocals and even contains elements of melodic death metal. The album is difficult to find in record stores but available on some bittorent websites.
