The full name Behdad (Persian: بهداد) prefers to be called by is Behdad Esfahbod (Persian: بهداد اسفهبد), though his legal (passport) name is much longer.

Behdad got his MSc from the department of computer science at the University of Toronto in 2006. He is currently working for Red Hat.
