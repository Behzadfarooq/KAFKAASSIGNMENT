Behind the Harmony is a 2006 rap album by Bone Thugs-N-Harmony, featuring Lil Jon.

[edit] Track listing

  1. "Do You Remember?" (Recorded for "Thug World Order" under the name "Remember Yesterday")
  2. "Cause I'm Bone, Bone" (featuring Bobby Brown) (Recorded for "Thug World Order" under the name "I'm Bone")
  3. "When The Lord Comes" (featuring K-Mont, Kaymont & LaReece) (Recorded for "Thug On Da Line" under the name "Judgement Time")
  4. "Yall Ain't Know" (real name is "Don't Fuck Around")
  5. "I Am Not That Nigga" "(Recorded for "Thug World Order" under the name "Not Dat Nigga")
  6. "Paradise" (Recorded for "Gemini: Good Vs. Evil")
  7. "Let Me Hold Something"
  8. "Here We Go" (Recorded for "Thug On Da Line" under the name "Niggaz-N-Bitchez")
  9. "Down To Die" (featuring Asu) (Recorded for Asu's "Dangerous Language")
  10. "Blowin N Rollin" (real name is "As We Roll")
  11. "Tear Da Club" (featuring Lil' Jon & Apollo Kreed (real name is "Shut The Club Down")
  12. "So Krayzie" (Recorded for "Gemini: Good Vs. Evil")
  13. "These Last Dayz" (Recorded for "Thug World Order" under the name "Last Days")
  14. "First Round Knockout" (featuring Asu, Wish Bone & Young Dre)
  15. "Everybody Wants A Thug" (real name is "Everybody Wanna Thug")
