This article is about the death metal band Beheaded. For an article about decapitation and beheading see Decapitation

Beheaded is a death metal band from Malta. They were formed in 1991, by vocalist Marcel Scalpello, guitarist David Bugeja, and drummer Chris Brincat and are still playing. They have performed live in Malta, Europe and the US and have released several albums.
