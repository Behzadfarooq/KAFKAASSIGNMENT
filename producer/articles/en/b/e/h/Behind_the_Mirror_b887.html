"Behind the Mirror" is a single by German thrash metal band Kreator released in 1987. It was released as a limited edition 12" picture disc.

[edit] Track listing

  1. "Behind the Mirror" - 3:44
  2. "Gangland" (Tygers Of Pan Tang cover) - 3:25
