[edit] Leonard Salomon Ornstein

I just noticed some inconsistencies in the article on Leonard Salomon Ornstein. First it says on November 29, 1941, Ornstein withdrew ... and a few lines later Ornstein died on May 20, 1941, six months after being barred from University. I suppose that the first date should read November 29, 1940, but I didn't want to mess with it since I don't have access to the source article.
Another issue is that of thesis supervisor: According to the list of Ph.D. students of H.A. Lorentz: 1881-1921 on the website of the Lorentz Institute was Lorentz his supervisor, not Ehrenfest. If the date of Ornstein's thesis defense menstioned there is correct (1908) Ehrenfest's involvement would be unlikely: At that time Ehrenfest was still in St Petersburg; he hadn't moved to Leiden yet.
Good artikel overigens; mijn complimenten! JdH 13:55, 29 March 2007 (UTC)
