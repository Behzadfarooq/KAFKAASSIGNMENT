Behexen

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Behexen are a Finnish black metal band founded in 1994. They play traditional, old school black metal with a raw sound. Their first full-length release was "Rituale Satanum", released in 2000 and followed by "By the Blessing of Satan" in 2004. The latter is considered by many to be their opus magnum thus far. They have also released a split with Horna (12" LP limited to 500 and CD 1000 pieces) and a 3-vinyl set "From the Devil's Chalice" (also limited to 500).

Behexen toured Europe in 2005, as a support act to Archgoat. [1] [2]
