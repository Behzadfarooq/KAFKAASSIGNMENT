Behringer

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

BEHRINGER Spezielle Studiotechnik GmbH (Behringer) is a German company, founded in its present form in 1989.

[edit] Products

Behringer UBB1002 battery powered mixer

The company designs and manufactures audio products. The company recently introduced a large number of products including stompboxes and even more recently; digital pianos.

[edit] Copyright Controversy

Many users and industry professionals claim that the designs and features of Behringer products copy that of other manufacturer's. The manager of the company has denied this, claiming that all the products were designed by the company.[1]
