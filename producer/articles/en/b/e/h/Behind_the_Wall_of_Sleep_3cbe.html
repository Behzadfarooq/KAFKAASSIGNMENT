Behind the Wall of Sleep is an EP by Macabre released in 1994 by Nuclear Blast Records. It contains three new tracks and one cover of Black Sabbath's song Behind the Wall of Sleep from their 1970 debut album Black Sabbath.

This EP is included on the re-release of the Sinister Slaughter album; however the title is misspelled as Behind the Walls of Sleep as well as the title of the song is misspelled in the same way.

[edit] Track listing

  1. "Fishtales" – 1:48 - Albert Fish
  2. "Behind the Walls of Sleep" – 3:52
  3. "Slaughter Thy Poser" – 2:14
  4. "Freeze Dried Man" – 3:59 - Jeffrey Dahmer
