[edit] Music Videos Source

Do you have and sources stating that Bebe and Cece shot all these music videos? I saw Jingle Bells on YouTube. Jgcarter 02:36, 19 December 2006 (UTC)

Well, for starters, check out http://www.amazon.com/Capitol-Video-BeBe-CeCe-Winans/dp/6302360625/ and look closely at the cover. There are videos listed for "Heaven", "Celebrate New Life", "Addictive Love", "I'll Take You There", and "It's OK" (I actually have them on LaserDisc believe it or nor). The video for "Celebrate New Life" is here on YouTube. As for the others, from "Jingle Bells" on down the list, I remember seeing them on BET in the 90's, but they aren't on any VHS/DVD collection. I'm not completely certain about "Love Of My Life" though. Souldier77 04:50, 19 December 2006 (UTC)
