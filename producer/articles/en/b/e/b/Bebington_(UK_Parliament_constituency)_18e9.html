Bebington (UK Parliament constituency)

Bebington was a parliamentary constituency in the United Kingdom, which existed from 1950 to 1974. The constituency was centred on the town of Bebington on the The Wirral Peninsula in Merseyside, England.

[edit] History

Created for the 1950 general election, it ceased to exist with the implementation of the 1974 boundary changes and was replaced for the February 1974 election by the Bebington and Ellesmere Port Constituency.
