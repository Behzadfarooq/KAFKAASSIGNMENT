Bebin Tv, Bebin (ببین) in Persian means "look", is an online TV network created by Persians living in the west with 4 channels: Bebin, Music, Life and Vpod. English and Persian are spoken with equal frequency throughout the network, some programs do offer English subtitles. One of the features that makes this network unique is the ability given to all watching to create his/her own video and upload it for everyone else's entertainment in the Vpod channel. Also, unlike regular TV, this network allows the viewer to change programs at will, if you don't like whats on, you can simply change it and watch what pleases you most. This network does not deal with politics and news, it mostly offers programming related to entertainment, music, food and culture.[[1]]
