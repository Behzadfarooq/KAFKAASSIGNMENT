Bebop to the Boolean Boogie: An Unconventional Guide to Electronics Fundamentals, Components, and Processes is a technical book written by Clive Maxfield. The guide is an overview of a wide array of electronic and logic topics, such as the history of number systems, memory circuits and logic design.

It is aimed at laymen, suitable for under-graduates. It lacks a bibliography.
