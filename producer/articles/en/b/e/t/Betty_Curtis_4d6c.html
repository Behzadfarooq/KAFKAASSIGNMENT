Betty Curtis

Betty Curtis (b. as Roberta Corti Milan, March 21, 1936 – d. Lecco, June 15, 2006) was an Italian singer active from 1958 to 1969.

Winner of Sanremo Music Festival in 1961 with Luciano Tajoli.
