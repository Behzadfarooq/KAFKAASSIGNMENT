Bethel, Clarke County, Virginia

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Bethel is an unincorporated community on the Shenandoah River in Clarke County, Virginia, USA. It lies at the crossroads of Swift Shoals and Kennel Roads. Bethel takes its name from the Old Bethel Church there.
