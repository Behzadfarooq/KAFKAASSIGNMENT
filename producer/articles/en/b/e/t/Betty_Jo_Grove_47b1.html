Betty Jo Grove (b. 1953, Camp Hill, Pennsylvania) is a beauty queen and model.

In 1973 Grove won the Miss Maryland USA title and represented her state in the 1973 Miss USA pageant, televised live from New York City. She placed 4th runner-up to eventual winner Amanda Jones of Illinois.

Grove attended the University of Maryland from 1972. While on campus she did advertisements for the Maryland football team during the 1973-1974 season. She appeared on the sports pages in newspapers at as far away as Philadelphia.
