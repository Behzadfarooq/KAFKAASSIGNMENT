Beta Tau Fraternity (ΒΤ) was founded on September 29, 1957 in Potsdam, New York as a Clarkson University fraternity. Beta Tau has remained an active social fraternity since that time. Although mainly a social fraternity, Beta Tau is also active in other respects including community events and interfraternity sports.

The Beta Tau alumni are extremely active and close knit. The Washington DC area has a high concentration of relocated Alumni.
