Betty Trask Award

The Betty Trask Award for a first novel given by the Society of Authors to citizens of the Commonwealth under the age of 35. The awards have been established since 1984 and total £25,000 each year.[1] The prizes are awarded to traditional or romantic novels, while those of an experimental nature are excluded.[2] Awards are given for both published and unpublished works.[3]
