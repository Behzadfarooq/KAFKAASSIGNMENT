[edit] School Name Changes

The school's offical name is Beths Grammar School, which is the title of the article. Beths is no longer an accrnym for Bexley and Erith Technical High School as most of that information is now false. Thus, the accronym should be mentioned purely in a historical context, not in the introduction.

(Corrected: May 10, 2006)

[edit] Prefect Teams

[edit] Location categories

  • It is unnecessary to have two categories on the same article, one of which entails the other. If you take a look at Category:Grammar schools in England you will see that it does not contain all the grammar schools in England, or even try to. It contains the smaller-area subcategories, which subsequently contain individual articles. As well as that, it contains articles for schools which do not fall into any of the areas for which there are geographical subcategories (since those subcategories are not exhaustive). We don't need to put both categories on. Jdcooper 22:44, 5 January 2007 (UTC)
