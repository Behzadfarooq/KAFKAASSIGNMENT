Bettye Ackerman

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Bettye Ackerman as Dr. Maggie Graham and Vince Edwards as Ben Casey

Bettye Ackerman (b. February 28, 1924, Cottageville, South Carolina - d. November 1, 2006, Columbia, South Carolina) was an American actress primarily known for her television work.

Ackerman grew up in Williston, South Carolina, one of four children. She graduated from Columbia College in South Carolina in 1945 and left for New York City soon after. She played Dr. Maggie Graham on the medical drama, Ben Casey, from 1961 until 1966. She played Anne Frazer on Bracken's World, and played the original Constance MacKenzie on the daytime program Return to Peyton Place.

Ackerman married Sam Jaffe on June 7, 1956. Although there was a 30+ year difference in their ages, they had a very successful and happy marriage until Jaffe died of cancer in 1984. They had no children.

She sold her home in Beverly Hills and returned to South Carolina to be near her large extended family in 1998. Shortly afterwards she was diagnosed with Alzheimer's disease and died, aged 82, following a stroke.

Bettye Ackerman Jaffe was also a noted artist, who had numerous exhibits in both Beverly Hills and Columbia, South Carolina.
