[edit] Academics

For world languages, Bethel offers four years of French and Spansish, as well as three years of German. AP classes offered are Statistics, Calculus, Literature and Composition, Studio Art, Government and Politics, and Biology.

[edit] Activities

ASB, Speech/Debate, DECA, Drama, FBLA, FCCLA, French Club, National Honor Society, Key Club, Native American Club, Natural Helpers, SAFTYE, S.A.V.E, Spanish Club, and United Cultural Congress.

[edit] Athletics

Bethel is part of the South Division of the South Puget Sound league in Washington's West Central District. Recent team state tournament results:

  • Boys Basketball- 2nd in 2000, 3rd in 2004, and 1st in 2005.
  • Football- 2nd in 2000
  • Volleyball- 1st in 1996, 5th in 2000

Other sports are soccer, girls basketball, cross country, track and field, swim and dive, baseball, fastpitch softball, tennis, golf, wrestling, and bowling. It has cheerleading and a drill team.
