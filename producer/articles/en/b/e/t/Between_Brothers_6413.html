Between Brothers was an American television sitcom-comedy that premiered September 11, 1997 on the FOX, and ran for two seasons. The first season aired on FOX from September 11, 1997 to January 29, 1998 and the second season was picked-up by UPN, and was aired from February 9, 1999 to March 2, 1999. The series starred Kadeem Hardison and Dondre Whitfield as brothers sharing an apartment. The final episode was aired on March 2, 1999.
