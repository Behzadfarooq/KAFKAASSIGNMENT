Sally McCluskey (b. in Omaha, Nebraska) is an American writer of romance novels as Bethany Campbell and Lisa Harris.

[edit] Biography

Sally McCluskey was born and raised in Omaha, Nebraska. One of the best things about growing up in Omaha was that, like it or not, every school child was herded at least once yearly through the city's sumptuous art museum, Joslyn. Omaha also had a great central public library, not far from Joslyn. In college Sally majored in English and minored in art.

Sally worked ass a freelancer for several greeting card companies as a writer and doing rough art. She sold her first romance novel in 1984 and has won three Ritas, three Romantic Times Reviewer Awards, a Maggie Award, and the Daphne du Maurier Award of Excellence.
