Betty Lynn

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Betty Lynn (Born August 29, 1926 in Kansas City, Missouri) is an American television actress. She is best known for playing Thelma Lou (Barney Fife's girlfriend) in The Andy Griffith Show.
