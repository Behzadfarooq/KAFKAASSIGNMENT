Betrothal in a Monastery (Obrucheniye v monastïre in transliteration) is Sergei Prokofiev's 6th opera with an opus number.

[edit] Background

Written in four acts to a Russian libretto by the composer and his second wife Mira Mendelson, the opera is based on Richard Brinsley Sheridan's comic opera libretto The Duenna.

[edit] Premiere

03-Nov-1946, Kirov Theater, Leningrad, conducted by Boris Khaikin.

[edit] Performance history

The opera was performed in 1989 at the Wexford Festival and in 2006 at Glyndebourne.

[edit] Summer Night

Summer Night (Op. 123) is an orchestral suite with music drawn from Betrothal in a Monastery.

[edit] Movements

The suite, lasting around 20 minutes, consists of five movements:

  1. Introduction: Moderato, ma con brio
  2. Serenade: Adagio
  3. Minuet
  4. Dreams (Nocturne): Andante tranquillo
  5. Dance: Allegretto
