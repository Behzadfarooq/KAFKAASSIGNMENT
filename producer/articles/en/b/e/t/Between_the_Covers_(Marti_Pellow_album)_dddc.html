Between the Covers is a cover album by Wet Wet Wet frontman Marti Pellow. It was released on November 17, 2003.

The album's sole offspring single was Neil Young's "A Lot of Love".

[edit] Track listing

  1. "A Lot of Love" (Neil Young)
  2. "Brand New Start" (Paul Weller)
  3. "Fire and Rain" (James Taylor)
  4. "Follow You, Follow Me" (Genesis)
  5. "Creepin'" (Stevie Wonder)
  6. "Mary Jane's Last Dance" (Tom Petty)
  7. "Brass in Pocket" (The Pretenders)
  8. "The River" (Joni Mitchell)
  9. "Grandma's Hands" (Bill Withers)
  10. "Suzanne" (Leonard Cohen)
  11. "Don't Let Me Down" (Pellow, Difford, Caine)
