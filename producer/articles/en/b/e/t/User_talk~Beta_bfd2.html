[edit] Formatting articles

A word of advice when creating articles. Wiki formatting really hates lines that start with a space, especially on long lines, and ends up formatting them like this:

 * Vision in the Curriculum:An evaluation ofthe Fis Project in Primary schools in Ireland, Fis Project/Dept of Education and Science, 2005.

Instead, always start at the left margin. To indent, add colons (:) to the left.

  • Vision in the Curriculum:An evaluation ofthe Fis Project in Primary schools in Ireland, Fis Project/Dept of Education and Science, 2005.
  • Vision in the Curriculum:An evaluation ofthe Fis Project in Primary schools in Ireland, Fis Project/Dept of Education and Science, 2005.
  • Vision in the Curriculum:An evaluation ofthe Fis Project in Primary schools in Ireland, Fis Project/Dept of Education and Science, 2005.

Hope this helps. Fan1967 20:36, 29 June 2006 (UTC)
