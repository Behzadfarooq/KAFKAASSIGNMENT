Betsy Jones-Moreland (April 1, 1930 â€“ May 1, 2006) was an American actress.

She was born in Brooklyn, New York, and began her career in small roles in the mid-1950s, appearing in several Roger Corman films, including a lead role in Last Woman on Earth (1960). Subsequently she appeared mostly on television through 1975. In the early 1990s, she appeared in a recurring role as a judge in a series of "Perry Mason" television films.
