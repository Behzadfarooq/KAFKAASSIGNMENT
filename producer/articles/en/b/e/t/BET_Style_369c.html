BET Style was an entertainment show, much like syndicated Entertainment Tonight, that focuses on African Americans in the entertainment industry. It aired on Black Entertainment Television at 7:30e-p/6:30c on Thursdays. Its hosts were usually Big Tigger and Melyssa Ford. Its last episode was on July 6, 2006.
