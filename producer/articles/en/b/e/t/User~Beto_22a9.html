Beto Boullosa

Beto Boullosa is a Computer Scientist, graduated from Universidade Federal da Bahia (Brazil), with a specialization in Computer Networks from Universidade Federal da Paraíba (Brazil). He has a M.Sc. in Systems Engineering from Universidade Federal do Rio de Janeiro (Brazil) and a Master in Cognitive Science from Universidad de Barcelona (Spain).

He worked as a teacher on databases and computer languages in Universidade Federal de Bahia, Universidade Católica de Salvador and UNIFACS, in Salvador (Brazil), as well as in Universidade Estácio de Sá, in Rio de Janeiro (Brazil).

Currently, he works as a researcher at the GLICOM - Computational Linguistics Group - in Universidade Pompeu Fabra (Spain) and is a D.Sc. student in Cognitive Science in Universidad de Barcelona.

His interests are many, specially on computers, internet, linguistics, psychology, football, Formula 1 and videogames.
