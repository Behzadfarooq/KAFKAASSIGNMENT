Bet That' is the first single off of Trick Daddy's seventh studio album Back by Thug Demand. The song was released in October 2006. It features Chamillionaire and Gold Ru$h. The song was produced by The Runners; who has produced for various artists including Rick Ross. It premiered on BET's 106 & Park where it was the New Joint of the day on October 30, 2006. A remix has been released featuring Chamillionaire & Ja Rule. It is currently on the Billboard Bubbling Under Hot 100 Singles chart at number 4 which equals to 104 on the Hot 100.
