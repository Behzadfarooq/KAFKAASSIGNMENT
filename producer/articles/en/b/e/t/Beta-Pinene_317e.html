Beta-Pinene

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The correct title of this article is beta-Pinene. The initial letter is shown capitalized due to technical restrictions.

beta-Pinene (Î²-pinene) is a colorless liquid, soluble in alcohol, but not water. It has a woody-green pine-like smell. It occurs naturally in rosemary, parsley, dill, basil and rose.
