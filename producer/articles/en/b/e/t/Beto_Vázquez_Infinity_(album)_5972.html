Beto Vázquez Infinity is the first album by Beto Vázquez Infinity, released the 4 of March of 2001.

[edit] Guests

  • Tarja Turunen featuring on tracks 1, 2, 4, 10 & 11.
  • Candice Night featuring on tracks 6, 7, 9 & 11.
  • Sabine Edelsbacher featuring on tracks 2, 10 & 11.
  • Fabio Lione featuring on track 3.
