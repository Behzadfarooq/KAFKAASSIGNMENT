Beth Israel Medical Center is a hospital in New York City. It has four major locations providing health services. It acts as University Hospital and Manhattan Campus for the Albert Einstein College of Medicine of Yeshiva University. The main hospital location is at First Avenue and 16th Street, near Stuyvesant Square.

Beth Israel Medical Center is a member of the Continuum Health Partners, a nonprofit hospital system that consists of four other institutions: Roosevelt Hospital, St. Luke's Hospital, Long Island College Hospital, and the New York Eye and Ear Infirmary.

Together, the Continuum Health Partners offer a wide range of medical services to patients.

The medical center is affiliated with the NewYork-Presbyterian Healthcare System.

The current CEO and president of Beth Israel Medical Center is David J. Shulkin, MD.
