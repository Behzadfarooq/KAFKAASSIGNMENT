Beto Vazquez Infinity is an Argentinian Metal band from Buenos Aires, originally assembled in 2000.

[edit] Biography

The band was founded by Beto Váquez, bassist of Nepal. They played supporting bands like Nightwish, Tierra Santa, Angra, Labyrinth, Vision Divine.
