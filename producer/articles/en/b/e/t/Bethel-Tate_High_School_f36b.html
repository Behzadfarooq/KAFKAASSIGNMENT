Bethel-Tate High School is a public high school located in the village of Bethel, Ohio. It is the only high school in the Bethel-Tate Local School District.

[edit] Extra Curriculars

Bethel-Tate offers a wide array of extra curricular activities for the student body. The participation fee for these groups is generally free or very inexpensive. Many members of the student body participate.

[edit] Sports

Bethel-Tate is part of the Southern Buckeye Academic and Athletic Conference (SBAAC).
