"Bet'cha Gonna Need My Lovin'" is the first single from the 1984 album Heart Don't Lie, the third album by American singer La Toya Jackson. The song was released on 7" and 12" singles without a B-side track, and peaked at #25 (Billboard Hot R&B/Hip-Hop Singles & Tracks) and #55 (Billboard Hot Dance Music/Club Play).
