Beth Toussaint (born 25 September 1962) is an American actress, best known for her television performances.

She was a regular cast member in Dallas as Tracy Lawton from 1988 to 1989 and has also appeared in Cheers, Star Trek: The Next Generation, Melrose Place, Matlock, Babylon 5, Savannah and Martial Law. In July 2006, she made a guest appearance on The Young and the Restless as Hope.

Toussaint appeared in one episode of Star Trek: The Next Generation as the sister of Tasha Yar.

She is married to actor Jack Coleman.
