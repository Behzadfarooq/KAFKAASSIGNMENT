Bettisons Folly, Hornsea

Bettisons Folly is in Hornsea, East Yorkshire, England. It is also referred to as Bettisons Tower and was built some time between 1829 and 1853 by William Bettison.

At the time, William Bettison was a Hull based brewer who built the folly on what was then his garden.

Nowadays, Bettisons Folly is a Grade II listed building and stands in the middle of a new housing estate off Newbegin Road.

It is very rarely open to the public.
