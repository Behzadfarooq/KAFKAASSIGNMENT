Beta Beta Beta

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Beta Beta Beta, or TriBeta, is a National Biological Honor Society. It was founded in 1922 at Oklahoma City University by Dr. Frank G. Brooks. The society is intended primarily for undergraduate students, and it has more than 175,000 lifetime members since being founded. There are over 400 chapters in the United States and Puerto Rico.

The national office is located in Florence, Alabama, currently headed by Dr. Gary Wolgamott of the Department of Biology at Southwestern Oklahoma State.
