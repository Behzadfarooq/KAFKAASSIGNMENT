Betsucomi (ベツコミ), formerly known as Bessatsu Shōjo Comic, is a monthly Japanese manga magazine published by Shogakukan, originally aimed at young girls, but increasingly marketed to older teens and young women. It is released on the 13th of each month. Its competitors are Betsuma, Princess and Lala.
