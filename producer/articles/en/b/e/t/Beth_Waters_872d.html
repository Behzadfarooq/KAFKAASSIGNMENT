Beth Waters is an American singer/songwriter based in San Francisco, California.

Waters' 2006 This Little Piggy was named one of the Best Albums of 2006 for Singer/Songwriters by Apple's iTunes[1]. Her songs have been used in such television programs as ABC's "One Life To Live," Lifetime Television's "Recipe For A Perfect Christmas" and "1-800-Missing," and the WB Network's "One Tree Hill."[2]
