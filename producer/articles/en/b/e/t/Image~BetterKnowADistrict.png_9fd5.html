[edit] Summary

I created this PNG from a screenshot of ArcExplorer showing the congressional district shapefiles (available from census.gov) and selecting the 31 that have been covered on Better Know A District so far. It has since been updated as of March 21, 2007, to cover the 55 districts "Better Known".
