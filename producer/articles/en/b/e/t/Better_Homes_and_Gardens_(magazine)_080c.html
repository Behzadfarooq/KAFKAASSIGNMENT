Better Homes and Gardens is one of the most widely circulated magazines in the United States. Better Homes and Gardens focuses on interests regarding homes, cooking, gardening, crafts, healthy living, decorating, and entertaining. The magazine is published 12 times per year by the Meredith Corporation.

The magazine was founded in 1922 by Edwin Meredith, the United States Secretary of Agriculture under Woodrow Wilson.

The magazine shares its name with an Australian magazine of similar nature. A computer program has been released in Australia under the name MVH - MyVirtualHome.
