BET Hip Hop Awards

You have new messages (last change).

The 1st Annual Hip Hop Awards were held Sunday, November 12, 2006 in Atlanta at the Fox Theatre and was aired November 15 at 9 p.m. on BET hosted by comedian Katt Williams.

  • 2006: Katt Williams

[edit] Presenters

  • 2006: Monica, Busta Rhymes, Flavor Flav, Delishuz, Keyshia Cole, Melyssa Ford, Big Tigger, Rick Ross & Nelly

[edit] Performers

  • 2006: Ludacris,Jermaine Dupri,Young Jeezy, Lil Jon,Rick Ross, Lil Wayne, Birdman,Ludacris,Young Jeezy, The Game, Snoop Dogg, T.I.,Young Dro,Big Kuntry King, B.G., Young Jeezy, Jim Jones, Juelz Santana, & DJ Unk
  • 2006: Cyphers: Remy Ma,Lupe Fiasco,Sway DaSafo,Saigon,Styles P, Papoose & Rhymefest

[edit] BET Hip Hop Awards of 2006 winners

  • Hip Hop Video Of The Year: T.I. - "What You Know"
  • Element Award: Video Director Of The Year: Hype Williams
  • Hip Hop Track Of The Year: "It's Goin Down" - Yung Joc featuring Nitti - Produced by Nitti
  • Rookie Of The Year: Chamillionaire
  • Element Award: Producer Of The Year: Jermaine Dupri
  • Hip Hop MVP Of The Year: T.I.
  • Element Award: Lyricist Of The Year: Common
  • Element Award: Best Hip Hop Dance Of The Year: Snap
  • Best Hip Hop Movie: ATL - Directed by Chris Robinson
  • Best Live Performance: Busta Rhymes
  • Best Hip Hop Collabo: Touch It (remix) - Busta Rhymes feat. Mary J Blige,Rah Digga,Missy Elliot,Lloyd Banks,Papoose, & DMX
  • Hip Hop CD Of The Year: King - T.I.
  • Hip Hop Hustler Award: Jay Z
  • Alltel Wireless People's Champ Award: Chamillionaire feat. Krayzie Bone - "Ridin"
  • Best UK Hip Hop Act (BET International): Sway
  • BET Hot Ringtone Award: Young Dro feat. T.I. - "Shoulder Lean"
  • I Am Hip-Hop Icon Award: Grandmaster Flash
