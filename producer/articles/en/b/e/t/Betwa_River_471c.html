The Betwa (Vetravati) is a river in Northern India, and a tributary of the Yamuna. This river is mentioned in the epic Mahabharata along with the Charmanwati river. Both are tributaries of Yamuna. Vetravati was also known as Shuktimati. The capital of Chedi Kingdom was on the banks of this river.

It is being connected to the Ken River in Madhya Pradesh as the first link in the ambitious river linking project in India.
