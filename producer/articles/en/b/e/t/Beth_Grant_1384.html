Beth Grant (born September 18, 1949, in Gadsden, Alabama) is an American actress. She is known for playing characters who are very strict, conservative, religious zealots and sticklers for rules.

She has appeared in dozens of films, including Donnie Darko, Sordid Lives, A Time to Kill, Little Miss Sunshine, Daltry Calhoun and Factory Girl. Grant has also appeared in many TV shows, including Malcolm in the Middle, The X-Files, Friends, Six Feet Under, My Name Is Earl,King of the Hill, and Judging Amy. In 2006, she appeared as a recurring character in nine episodes on the CBS drama Jericho.

[edit] Personal

Grant is an alumnus of East Carolina University. She is married to actor Michael Chieffo, with whom she has one child.
