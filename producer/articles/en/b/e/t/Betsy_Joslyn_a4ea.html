Betsy Joslyn

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Betsy Joslyn (born April 19, 1954 in Staten Island, New York) is a Broadway musical and dramatic actress and soprano.

[edit] Broadway credits

  • High Society (1998)
  • The Goodbye Girl (1993)
  • A Few Good Men (1989)
  • Into the Woods (1987)
  • Les Misérables (1987)
  • Sunday in the Park with George (1984)
  • A Doll's Life (1982)
  • Sweeney Todd (1979)

[edit] Major touring credits

  • Beauty and the Beast (1996)
  • City of Angels (1992)
  • Camelot (1984)
