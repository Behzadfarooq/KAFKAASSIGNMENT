Between the Buried and Me is the debut album from the band of the same name.

[edit] Track listing

  1. More of Myself to Kill – 6:50
  2. Arsonist – 4:52
  3. Aspirations – 5:47
  4. What We Have Become – 5:09
  5. Fire for a Dry Mouth – 6:07
  6. Naked by the Computer – 5:36
  7. Use of a Weapon – 4:53
  8. Shevanel Cut a Flip – 9:27

[edit] Band line-up

  • Tommy Rogers - Vocals/Keyboards
  • Paul Waggoner - Lead/Rhythm Guitar
  • Nick Fletcher - Rhythm Guitar
  • Will Goodyear - Drums/Percussion
  • Jason King - Bass guitar
  • The song "Arsonist" was written about the Westboro Baptist Church in Topeka, Kansas that supports the site www.godhatesfags.com
  • The name Shevanel that is used in the album comes from somewhat of a famous homeless man who lives in Raleigh, NC, he also has a dog named Plip.
