Bettina S. is a Finnish talk show, hosted by Bettina Sågbom and produced by FST. The show is also broadcast on YLE2. However, the show is ten minutes longer on FST5.

The show premiered in 2003 and in the same year won the Venla prize for Best Entertainment Show of 2003.
