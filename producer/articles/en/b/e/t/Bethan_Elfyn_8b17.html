Cardiff-born Bethan Elfyn (IPA: [ˈbɛθan ˈɛlvɨn]) is a Welsh radio and television presenter; she currently co-presents the Wales programme on BBC Radio 1 with Huw Stephens as part of the station's regional output every Thursday evening.

Bethan joined Radio 1 in 1999 as part of the aforementioned new regional output, she has also produced other programmes for the station.

Bethan is a fluent Welsh speaker and has appeared as a panellist on Welsh language channel S4C's music talent show Waw Ffactor, she also co-hosted Radio 1's first ever Welsh language show as part of the oneclick strand in August 2005 with Aled Haydn-Jones from The Chris Moyles Show.
