Beth Littleford (born July 17, 1968 in Nashville, Tennessee) is a comedian and television personality best known for her being a correspondent on the popular Comedy Central satirical news series, The Daily Show from 1996 to 2000. She lives in Los Angeles, California with her husband, Rob Fox, and their son, Jackson Oliver Fox, born 30 July 2005. She has guest-starred on numerous television programs during the late 1990s and 2000s such as Spin City, The West Wing, Family Guy, and Frasier. Littleford was also a celebrity commentator on VH1's I Love the 80s Strikes Back in 2003, I Love the 90s in 2004, and I Love the 90s: Part Deux in 2005.
