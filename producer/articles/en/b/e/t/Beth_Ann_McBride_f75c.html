Beth Ann McBride is an American talk radio personality. She hosts The Ride with McBride weekday afternoons on talk radio station WSJS in Winston-Salem, North Carolina. In addition to her radio program, McBride is program director of the CBS Radio-owned station.

McBride is the former producer of The Don and Mike Show, a syndicated radio program originated by Westwood One, based at WJFK-FM in the Washington, DC area. During this time, she was on the receiving end of many jokes (e.g. how she would use a mirror to watch herself while masturbating), but was noted for her strong work ethic. She once revealed on the show that she traveled 6 hours to take her dog to a vet in North Carolina that had previously treated the animal.

McBride has also worked at radio stations in Buffalo, Charlotte, Erie, Pennsylvania and St. Croix in the U.S. Virgin Islands.
