Bethlehem (disambiguation)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Bethlehem is commonly referred to a city in the West Bank area (which is otherwise known as Judea).

Bethlehem may also refer to:

[edit] Places

In Palestine and Israel:

  • The Bethlehem Governorate in the West Bank
  • Bethlehem of Galilee, an ancient city in the Galilee and a modern moshav (agricultural settlement) in Israel

In the United States:

  • Bethlehem, a small town in Connecticut
  • Bethlehem, a small town in Georgia
  • Bethlehem, a small town in Maryland
  • Bethlehem, a small town in New Hampshire
  • Bethlehem, a town in New York
  • Bethlehem, a small town in North Carolina
  • Bethlehem, a town in Pennsylvania
  • Bethlehem, a small town in West Virginia

In other countries:

  • Bethlehem, a small village in Netherlands, in the province of Groningen
  • Bethlehem, a suburb of the city of Tauranga, Bay of Plenty region, New Zealand
  • Bethlehem, a town in the Free State province of South Africa
  • Bethlehem, a city district in Bern, the capital of Switzerland
  • Bethlehem, a a tiny farming village in Carmarthenshire, West Wales, United Kingdom
