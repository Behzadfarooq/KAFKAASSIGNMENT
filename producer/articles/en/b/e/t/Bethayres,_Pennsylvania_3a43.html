Bethayres, Pennsylvania

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

You have new messages (last change).

Bethayres is a small village in Lower Moreland Township, Pennsylvania. It is named for Elizabeth Ayres. It is an unincorporated place, and it is served by the Huntingdon Valley, Pennsylvania post office. Because of this, it is often said to be a section of Huntingdon Valley.

Bethayres has a stop on the SEPTA R3 West Trenton Regional Rail line, providing access to Philadelphia and New Jersey.
