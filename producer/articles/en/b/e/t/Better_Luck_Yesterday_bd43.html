Better Luck Yesterday is a dark comedy independent movie produced in 2006. It was written and directed by Max Dubinsky.

[edit] Plot

Spoiler warning: Plot and/or ending details follow.

Four friends (a comedian, a doctor, a suicidal depressive, and an alcoholic) discuss over dinner how bad their days have been. As each one relates their story, however, they begin to discover that each of them caused the others' bad days. The movie is a series of flashbacks from earlier in the day.
