Better Than A Thousand was a hardcore band with former Youth of Today singer Ray Cappo and Ken Olden from Battery fame. It was a side project of Shelter, Ray originally intended just to record a couple songs with some friends and it spawned into three world tours and two 'classic' albums. Better Than A Thousand sounds like a modern hardcore version of Youth Of Today.
