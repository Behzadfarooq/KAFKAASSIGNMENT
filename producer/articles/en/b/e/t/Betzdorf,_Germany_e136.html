Betzdorf is a town, municipality and Verbandsgemeinde in Rhineland-Palatinate, Germany. It is part of the district of Altenkirchen. Betzdorf is located on the river Sieg, approx. 15 km south-west of Siegen. The Verbandsgemeinde Betzdorf consists of the following municipalities:

  1. Alsdorf (Westerwald)
  2. Betzdorf (Sieg)
  3. Grünebach
  4. Scheuerfeld
  5. Wallmenroth
