Beau "One Man Army™" Taylor is an American mixed martial arts fighter with an undefeated record of 3-0.


Born December 25th 1978, Beau Taylor grew up in Malibu, and then moved to San Luis Obispo, California, where he was an All-American amateur wrestler for Cuesta College and also wrestled for California Polytechnic University. While living in San Luis Obispo, Beau trained extensively with UFC Light Heavyweight Champion Chuck Liddell at SLO Kickboxing Academy. He also trained in Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu under Cesar Gracie, Jake Shields, and Dave Terrell.


Taylor then moved to Tulsa, Oklahoma, where he currently lives with his wife and three children. Beau was a wrestling coach at Cascia Hall Preparatory School in Tulsa and trained with famed Lion's Den fighter Ken Shamrock before joining up with Tulsa Top Team, where he is a grappling and mixed martial arts coach.


Beau is also a multiple time medalist in submission wrestling tournaments, and a well respected cornerman, having cornered such standout fighters as Jake Shields, Matt Wiman, and Jason Brazeal. His "OneManArmy™" character is also incredibly popular on the forums of MMA.tv.
