Hmm.. I'm wondering how accurate this page is. Check Your Head and Ill Communication both say "Grand Royal" on their records. They're listed as "Capitol Records" here. Which is the truth?

Also, I've never heard of the "Don't Mosh" CD. Does it really exist? Could someone who knows the answers to these questions correct the article? --rlwelch

They produced the albums under Grand Royal but were manufactured and distributed by Capitol, if you check the album inserts. Popular bands on indie labels tend to do this if their albums are big sellers because smaller labels can't handle the volume. Beasties smaller releases like 'Aglio' could be released on GR, since demand is not as high (bands like Bad Religion whose members started and still manage Epitaph, signed with Atlantic to release their stuff). 'Don't Mosh' was an unreleased interactive CD-ROM from '94. Beastiemania.com is a good comprehensive source for all things beastie.
