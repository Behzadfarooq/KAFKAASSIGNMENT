Bear Creek Country Club is a private golf club and neighborhood located in Woodinville, Washington. It's east of Seattle and located very near Redmond, home to Microsoft. The Golf Club hosts weddings, golf tournaments, receptions, and a host of other events for the greater Seattle community, although golf club membership is semi-exclusive. The neighborhood would be defined as a gated community and is home to approximately 200 families. The neighborhood is a short drive away from Wilder Elementary School and Overlake High School.
