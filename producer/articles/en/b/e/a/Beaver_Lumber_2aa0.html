Beaver Lumber was a former Canadian building supply chain owned by Molson. It was once Canada's 4th largest building supply chain with 138 stores. In 2000 it was purchased by Home Hardware, a cooperative of over 1000 independent Canadian hardware stores. Beaver Lumber stores were rebranded as Home Building Centres.
