Beast of the Bonzos (cover) — Beast of the Bonzoes (record)
Compilation album by Bonzo Dog Band
Released 1971
Genre Rock
Label United Artists Records UAS 5517
Compiled by Martin Cerf, John Mendelsohn

This American best of album differs from the British The Best of the Bonzo's in having different cover art, an extra flap with an article about Bonzo by John Mendelsohn, and about half different songs.
