[edit] MI*SB

Created by myself; Jamie Green and a good friend Max Ellaway, the comic book looks into a world where people live in fear of the government, and secret conspiracies. However, in this world, one Kingdom is seemingly immune to the absoulute corruption smothering the human race; San Baeyeux. And one woman now stands a chance, to make a difference. Her name is; Beatrix Knight, the Queen Of San Baeyeux.

[edit] The Voice of Comics

I act as a self appointed moderator of many Marvel Comic data pages here on wikipedia. My main base of work is on the X-men, centering around my favourite character, Rogue.

Currently, i am working on my own comic book/graphic novel, under the name; "MI*SB" (see above for details)
