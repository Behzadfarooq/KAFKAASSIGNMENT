Beatrice Jepchumba

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Beatrice Jepchumba (born 25 November 1983 in Nandi) is a Kenyan runner.

[edit] Achievements

Year Tournament Venue Result Extra
2000 World Junior Championships Santiago, Chile 1st 3000 m
2005 World Cross Country Championships Saint-Étienne, France 7th Short race
2nd Team competition
2006 World Cross Country Championships Fukuoka, Japan 6th Short race
2nd Team competition
African Championships Bambous, Mauritius 7th 1500 m

[edit] Personal bests

  • 1500 metres - 4:16.08 min (2006)
  • 3000 metres - 8:45.07 min (2001)
  • 3000 metres steeplechase - 10:11.5 min (2005)
  • 5000 metres - 15:32.08 min (2006)
