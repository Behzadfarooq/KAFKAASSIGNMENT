The Beaumont Drillers are a professional indoor football team. They're currently a member of the NIFL. They play their home games at Ford Arena. They originally began playing as the Louisiana Rangers in the Professional Indoor Football League in 2000 when they replaced the Louisiana Bayou Beast. They originally played their home games at the Rapides Coliseum in Alexandria, Louisiana. After the league collapsed, the Rangers moved in to the NIFL. After two seasons, the franchise moved to Beaumont, Texas, and became the Drillers. So far in their overall history, the franchise has made the playoffs three times, yet they fell in Round 1 in each of them.
