Beate Uhse Erotic Museum

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Beate Uhse, founder of the museum.

The Beate Uhse Erotic Museum (German: Beate Uhse Erotik-Museum) in Berlin, Germany, opened in 1996. The museum was opened by Beate Uhse, the early stunt pilot and entrepreneur, who started the world's first sex shop after World War II. It claims to be "the world's largest erotic museum".
