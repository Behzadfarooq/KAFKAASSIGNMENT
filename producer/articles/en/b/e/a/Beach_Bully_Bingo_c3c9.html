"Beach Bully Bingo" is the twenty-ninth sub-episode of Tom and Jerry Tales.

[edit] Episode Summary

Jerry shows Tom a picture in his sketchbook that shows them being friends and going to the beach. Thus, they agree and go there, but Butch and Spike arrive first. Tom and Jerry team up while Butch and Spike are the rivals in a game. Tom controls Butch, and Jerry controls Spike. Tom and Jerry are actually trying to get the bullies off the beach, though. Finally, both Butch and Spike are driven away from the beach, and Tom and Jerry play happily there.

[edit] Trivia

  • Butch's third appearance on the show, the first two being "Polar Peril" and "City Dump Chumps".
