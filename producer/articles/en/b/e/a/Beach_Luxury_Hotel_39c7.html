The Beach Luxury Hotel is situated on M. T. Khan Road, in Karachi, Pakistan. Beach Luxury hotel is one of the major hotel in Pakistan and It is a part of Avari Hotels Limited. The five-story hotel situated on 6 landscaped acres, situated at the water front facing the cool sea breeze only minutes away from business, commercial & shopping centers.
