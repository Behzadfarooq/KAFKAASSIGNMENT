Beat Boy is an EP by the New Romantic band Visage, released on Polygram Records in August, 1984.

The title track is available in Visage's album Beat Boy.

[edit] Track listing

7" version:

  1. "Beat Boy" (Single edit) – 3:30
  2. "Beat Boy" (Dance dub) – 3:48

[edit] Personnel

  • Steve Strange (vocals)
  • Rusty Egan (drums, electronic drums programming)
  • Steve Barnacle (bass)
  • Dave Formula (synthesizer)
  • Andy Barnett (guitar)
  • Marsha Raven (backing vocals)
  • Karen Ramsey (backing vocals)
  • Rose Patterson (backing vocals)
