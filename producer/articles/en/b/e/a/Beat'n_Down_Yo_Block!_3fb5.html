Beat'n Down Yo Block is the debut album from Atlanta based rapper Unk. It was released October 3, 2006. If features the song Walk It Out, which had been featured in the 2006 film Happy Feet.

"Beat'n Down Yo Block" features many prominent southern rappers, among them D.G. Yola, Baby D and Dem Franchize Boyz. It also features production by Jazze Pha.
