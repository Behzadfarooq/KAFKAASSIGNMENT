Beat Seitz (born 28 October 1973) is a Swiss bobsledder who competed in the bobsleigh events at the 1998 Winter Olympics in Nagano, Japan. Beat Seitz won a silver medal in the four-person bobsleigh event with teammates Marcel Rohner, Markus Nüssli and Markus Wasser.
