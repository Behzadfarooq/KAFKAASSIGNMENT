This article is about the movie. For the glam metal band, see Beautiful Creatures (band), for the self-titled album by the same band see Beautiful Creatures (album). For the solo album by Juliana Hatfield, see Beautiful Creature.

Beautiful Creatures is a 2000 black comedy/thriller film directed by Bill Eagles and starring Susan Lynch and Rachel Weisz. Lynch received a British Independent Film Award nomination for her role.
