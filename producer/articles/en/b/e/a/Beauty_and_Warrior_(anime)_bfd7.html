Beauty and Warrior is an Indonesian animated film directed by Sukma Romadhon and produced by Pt. Unggul Cipta Piranti. The English dubbing was made by Prathivi Recording. It is about the story of two brothers, Mega and Batara, who have flying horses and search for a powerful sword. It is 41 minutes long and intended for children. It was made in 2002 and was released in the US market in 2005 by its distributor, IFD Films and Arts Ltd., in 2005 at retailers such as Wal-Mart.
