Bear Rock Cafe is chain of restaurants headquartered in Cary, NC. The restaurants feature a rustic lodge decor, several with near-life-size bear wood carvings near the entrance. The menu continues with the outdoors theme featuring such sandwiches as "Mojave BBQ Chicken" and "Mountain Bird."

The chain opened October, 1997 in Raleigh, NC. Since then it has grown to 38 locations in 11 states.
