Beautiful Katamari

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

This article or section contains information about an unreleased video game.
It is likely to contain information of a speculative nature, and the content may change dramatically as more information becomes available.

Beautiful Katamari is a recently announced game for PlayStation 3, Xbox 360 and Wii.[1] It will be the fourth game in the Katamari series of games by Namco. Following, Katamari Damacy, We Love Katamari and Me & My Katamari. It has been speculated that the version for the PlayStation 3 and Xbox 360 will come out in October 2007 whereas the Wii version will come out in November 2007.
