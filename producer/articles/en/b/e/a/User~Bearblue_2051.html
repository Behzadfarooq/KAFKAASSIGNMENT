[edit] Why I edit the way I do

Someone may look at my contributions page and wonder why I make so many small edits. And the answer is that it is because I believe in Save, save often. So small edits = remembering to save before losing the edit. Also, it gives me a chance to check against a previous entry.

Even if I used a word processor to edit material, I would end up doing the same thing.
