[edit] Potter local history and film

Beatrix Potter was born in "The Boltons", a street in Kensington. Just round the corner, on Brompton Road is a plaque showing coloured portraits of Peter Rabbit and Jemima Puddleduck. In 2005 it was announced that Rene Zellweger and Ewan McGregor would be playing the parts of Beatrix and Warne in a film version of Beatrix Potter's life. —The preceding unsigned comment was added by 193.122.47.146 (talk • contribs) 16:07, 15 October 2005 (UTC).
