In 2001, Sam the Kid released an instrumental album entitled Beats Vol 1: Amor. Using resources like vinyl, discs, soap-operas, videos and recorded phone calls among others, STK created a superb and unique audiophilic world, inspired by the love of his parents, to whom he dedicated the album.
