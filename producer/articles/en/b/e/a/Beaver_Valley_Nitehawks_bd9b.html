The Beaver Valley Nitehawks are a Canadian 'B' Junior ice hockey team from Fruitvale, British Columbia. They play in the Kootenay International Junior Hockey League.

NHL Players Adam Deadmarsh and Barrett Jackman played for the Nitehawks.

In 2006, Nitehawk player Kevin Koopman was drafted by the Ottawa Senators in the 2006 NHL Entry Draft.

[edit] History

  • KIJHL League Champions (1997, 1999, 2001, 2003, 2004)
  • Cyclone Taylor Cup Winners (1997, 2001)
  • The Fernie Ghostriders beat them in the 2006/2007 playoffs.
