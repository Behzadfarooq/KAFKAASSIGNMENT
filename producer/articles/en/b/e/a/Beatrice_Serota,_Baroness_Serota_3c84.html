Beatrice, Baroness Serota, DBE (b. 15 October 1919 - d. 21 October 2002) was a British Government minister and a Deputy Speaker of the House of Lords.

[edit] Family

Her son is Sir Nicholas Serota.

[edit] Education

  • John Howard School
  • London School of Economics (Honorary Fellow, 1976)

[edit] Career

  • Hampstead Borough Council, 1945-49
  • London County Council for Brixton, 1954-65 (Chairman, Children's Committee, 1958-65)
  • Greater London Council for Lambeth, 1964-67 (Chief Whip)
  • Baroness in Waiting, 1968-69
  • Minister of State (Health), DHSS, 1969-70
  • BBC Complaints Commission, 1975-77
  • Governor, BBC, 1977-82
  • Principal Deputy Chairman of Committees, and Chairman, European Communities Select Committee, House of Lords, 1986-92
