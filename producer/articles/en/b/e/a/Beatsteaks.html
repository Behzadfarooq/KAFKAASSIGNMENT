The Beatsteaks are a punk band from Berlin, Germany, formed in 1995. At the MTV Europe Music Awards of 2004, they won an award for "Best German Act". They made their debut to American audience on the MTV television program, Viva La Bam and are making an appearance on his compilation CD entitled Viva La Bands with their Smack Smash album track "I Don’t Care as Long as You Sing". They have also appeared on various Punk-O-Rama compilations.
