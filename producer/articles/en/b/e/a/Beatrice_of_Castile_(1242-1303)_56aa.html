Beatrice of Castile (1242-1303)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Beatrice of Castile (1242 - 1303) was the second Queen consort of Afonso III of Portugal.

She was an illegitimate daughter of Alfonso X of Castile and Maria de Guzman.

She was married to Affonso III in 1253. The bride was about eleven years old and the groom was thirty-two years old. They had the following children:
