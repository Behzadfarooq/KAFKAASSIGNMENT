Beastie is a steel family roller coaster made by Pinfari of Italy. The coaster is located in the Adventure Land area of Alton Towers in Staffordshire, England. The coaster, although a family ride, does pick up some speed on the drops and features a tunnel, with a worryingly low height, for taller riders. Like the Runaway Mine Train it does two laps of the circuit, and repeat rides are possible on quiet days.
