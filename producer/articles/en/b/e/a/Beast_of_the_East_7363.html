The Beast of the East is a high school and collegiate wrestling tournament that is held annually in Newark, Delaware. Originally called the Delaware Winter Nationals, the name "Beast of the East" stuck when the tournament joined the ranks of the toughest tournmanets in the country. The high school tournament is considered to be one of the toughest in the United States. The tournament draws some of the best teams in the country every year including perennial powerhouse Blair Academy. Another supreme national power house that enters the tournament, is the Nazareth Blue Eagles out of Nazareth, PA (Nazareth was the top ranked public school this season). Other teams include; St. Edward High School, Massillon Perry High School and St. Paris Graham High School. Beast of the East champions have gone on to high accomplishments in NCAA Division I wrestling, including a number of national champions. The tournament was originally started to provide scholarship money for Delaware high school wrestlers and continues that tradition today. Vic Leonard was the original tournament director, Bob Shaw has taken over that responsibility in recent years.
