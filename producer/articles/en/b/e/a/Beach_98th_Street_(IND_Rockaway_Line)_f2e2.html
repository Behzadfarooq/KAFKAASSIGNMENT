Beach 98th Street, sometimes referred as Beach 98th Street–Playland, is a station on the New York City Subway's IND Rockaway Line. The station is built on a concrete viaduct. New lights have been installed. There is a crossunder to the tile mezzanine. The "Playland" name came from the former Rockaways' Playland, which was closed in the mid-1980s. No trace of the park remains other than the station name. The southbound platform is longer that the northbound one, and has an extra exit at the north end which is now sealed. Canopies, mezzanine, and side walls are similar to Beach 90th Street.

[edit] Bus connections

  • Local service: Q21 and Q22
  • Limited stop service: Q53
  • Manhattan express service: QM16
