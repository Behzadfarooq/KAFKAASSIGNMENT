Bear Creek (Oregon)

Bear Creek is a tributary of the Rogue River in Jackson County, Oregon, United States. The main population centers in the Rogue Valley in Jackson County (Medford and Ashland) are actually located in the Bear Creek branch of the valley.

The umbrella company of Harry and David and Jackson & Perkins, the Harry and David Operations Corp., was formerly named "Bear Creek Corporation". It is located in Medford, and was named for the creek.

The Bear Creek Greenway is a biking/hiking path that, when complete, will extend from Central Point to Ashland.
