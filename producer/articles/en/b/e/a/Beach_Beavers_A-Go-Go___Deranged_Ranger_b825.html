Beach Beavers A-Go-Go and Deranged Ranger are episode 5a and 5b, respectively, of season one of The Angry Beavers.

[edit] Beach Beavers A-Go Go

Dag wishes to migrate to a sunny beach to avoid the cold winter. Norb is eventually persuaded, and the two journey, and arrive to a beach, ironically called A Sunny Beach.

  • Written By: Victor Wilson
  • Storyboard: Mark O'Hare
  • Directed By: Robert Hughes

[edit] Deranged Ranger

Ranger Phil has had it with the forest animals and their complaints. He quits and takes off to be a figure skater. Dag remarks, "Any half-wit can do your job." With Dag being the said comment, the forest elect Dag the new ranger. All goes well, at first, until bossing the other animals around goes to his head and starts making insane laws, such as "No Eating." Norb and the rest band together to get revenge.

  • Written By: John Oerevlany
  • Storybaord: Chris Dent
  • Directed By: Patti Shinagawa

[edit] Notes/Trivia

  • The title Beach Beavers A-Go Go, may be a spoof of the B-Movie Monster A Go-Go.
  • "Beach..." is the first episode where Dag and Norb wear their swimsuits, Dag's blue trunks, and Norb's black speedo. This is what they wear during the end credits, to the tune of Baby Elephant Walk.
  • First song heard in the Beach episode, "Beach beaver A-Go Go", sung by Nick Bakay.

norb is kissed in this episode.

  • The voice of Ranger Phil is Ed Winter, who'll later become the voice Scientist Number One.
