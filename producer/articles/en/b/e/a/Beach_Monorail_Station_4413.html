Beach Monorail Station

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Exterior view of Beach Station, Sentosa
Platform view of Beach Station, Sentosa

Beach Station is the fourth and the last station along the completed Sentosa Express in Singapore. It is located in between Siloso Beach and Palawan Beach in Sentosa.

The working name for this station was Palawan Station. The station opened on January 15, 2007. Visitors can alight and transfer to beach trams to the beaches, including Tanjong Beach.

Another station may be added further south of the station, though plans have not been firmed up as yet.
