Beaconsfield–Baie-D'Urfé

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia


Beaconsfield–Baie-D'Urfé is a former borough in the West Island area of Montreal, Quebec. It was composed of the former municipalities of Beaconsfield and Baie-D'Urfé.

Forced merger: January 1, 2002 On June 20, 2004, both Beaconsfield and Baie-d'Urfé voted to return to being independent municipalities, effective January 1, 2006.
