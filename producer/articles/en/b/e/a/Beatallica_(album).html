Beatallica (known by fans as The Grey Album) is the second E.P. from Beatallica. It contains 8 tracks, made from combinations of Beatles and Metallica songs

[edit] Track listing

  1. Blackened the USSR ("Back in the U.S.S.R." / "Blackened")
    • Musical References:
      • "Hey Jude"
    • Lyrical References:
      • "Hit the Lights"
      • Lemmy from the band Motörhead
  2. Sandman ("Taxman" / "Enter Sandman")
    • Lyrical References:
      • "Seek and Destroy"
      • Don Dokken/Dokken
  3. And I'm Evil ("And I Love Her" / "Am I Evil?" (Diamond Head cover))
    • Lyrical References:
      • "Ain't My Bitch"
      • "Die Die My Darling"
      • The Misfits (A Glenn Danzig sound-alike provides backing vocals half-way through the song)
  4. Got to Get You Trapped Under Ice ("Got to Get You Into My Life" / "Trapped Under Ice")
    • Lyrical References:
      • MTV
      • Bananarama
  5. Leper Madonna ("Lady Madonna" / "Leper Messiah")
    • Musical References:
      • Contains a sample from the infamous online faux-copy of Madonna's American Life
    • Lyrical References:
      • "Orion"
  6. Hey Dude ("Hey Jude" / "Nothing Else Matters")
    • Lyrical References:
      • "For Whom the Bell Tolls"
      • "Eye of the Beholder"
      • Diamond Head
      • Kip Winger
      • New Wave of British Heavy Metal
      • "Flying V" Guitars
  7. I Want to Choke Your Band ("I Want to Hold Your Hand")
    • Lyrical References:
      • Don't Tread On Me
      • C.C. DeVille
      • White Lion
      • Warrant
  8. We Can Hit the Lightz ("We Can Work It Out" / "Hit the Lights")
    • Lyrical References:
      • Trixter
      • No Life 'Til Leather
    • Musical References:
      • "Orion" (bass line during the "life is very short" section)
