                                            The Full Biography on Jasmine R. Kauffman
                                                      February 25th, 1993-Present



                                                  Jasmine: The Early Years

Jasmine Rachael Kauffman was born February 25th, 1993 to Herbert B. Kauffman and Debra A. Tillou. She had two older siblings: Abe at four and Sasha at one. At a very young age, Jasmine learned how to both walk and talk at age one. Her first memory was going to family pictures at the age of three. She has been doing those every year since. Also at the age of three, Jasmine recieved a new family member, Eli. Skipping preschool, she learned how to read at four and a half and began kindergarten at five. Although she skipped preschool, Jasmine was very smart. She was always in the advanced spelling and reading groups. This difference from the other kids did not bother Jasmine at all.

                                                  Jasmine: School and Present

Jasmine attended Miller School kindergarten through third grade. After third grade, she was homeschooled. I interviewed Jasmine on homeschooling. Question: Was homeschool fun? Answer: Yes, its pretty fun. Although I get the same amount work done in homeschooling as public schooling, it takes a lot less time. You can play in the snow when everyone else is at school! Question: Was homeschooling better then public? Answer: Our public schools were horrible, so yeah. Question: Could you sleep in? Answer: I have to get up at 9:00, so kind of. Question: Do your siblings get homeschooled? Answer: My little brother does. He has since kindergarten... he's a kindergarten dropout. Sasha was homeschooled but before this summer she wen't back to school, and Abe was homeschooled 7th grade- 9th grade. Question: Did you ever go back to public school? Answer: No. In 2001, Jasmine got her first horse, St. Nicholas. In 2003, she got another horse, Zinger. Her two horses are well taken care of and horseback riding is one of her hobbies. Other hobbies are softball, swimming, and kayacking. Jasmine told us that her interests are... "I love horses and horse-back riding, Softball (This year my team placed first!!=D)Swimming in our pool, Hanging with friends, Being on the computer, Hanging out with my friends, Kayaking, Fairs(Me my sis and my friend like going on the most dangerous and stupidest rides)Jelly filled donuts =)Listening to music, Dancing like no one's watching, Singing while blasting music, Annoying my siblings."

Jasmine Kauffman currently lives in Maine.
