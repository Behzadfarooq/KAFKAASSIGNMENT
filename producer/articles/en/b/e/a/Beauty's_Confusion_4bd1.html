Beauty's Confusion

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Beauty's Confusion is a trip-hop duo from Philadelphia, Pennsylvania, USA.

Since releasing their debut album "Breathe In" (named after the second track on Frou Frou's critically-acclaimed 2002 album "Details"), Beauty's Confusion has gone on to produce soundtrack music for video games released on Microsoft's Xbox, and provide background score music for author and independent filmmaker Michael W. Dean's documentary on legendary author Hubert Selby Jr. In addition, the duo has remixed many artists, from Björk to Azure Ray (most recently), Scotland's Kosheen, Public Enemy, Tears for Fears and Madonna.
