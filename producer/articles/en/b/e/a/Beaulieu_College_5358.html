Beaulieu College is a private, coeducational day school founded in 2001. It is located in Kyalami, Gauteng, South Africa.

[edit] History

The school was started by the local community who saw a need for an school founded on community principles. Mr Robert Clarence was appointed head.

In 2005 Mr Andrew Cook (previously Deputy Rector at Michaelhouse) was appointed as headmaster.

[edit] Heads

  • Robert Clarence (2001 - 2004)
  • Vivienne Jones (Acting) (2004)
  • Andrew Cook (2005 - present)

[edit] Academics

Beaulieu College school-leavers write the Independent Examinations Board exams.

Most leavers go on to attend South African universities such as the University of Johannesburg and University of Pretoria.
