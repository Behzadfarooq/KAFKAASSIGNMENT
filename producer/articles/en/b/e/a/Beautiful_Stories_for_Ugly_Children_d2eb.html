Beautiful Stories for Ugly Children, vol. 1 (June 1989)

Beautiful Stories for Ugly Children was a surreal, kooky non-superhero comic book series written by Dave Louapre and illustrated by Dan Sweetman, published by DC Comics under the Piranha Press imprint from June 1989 until September 1992, a total of 30 issues in the run (and a couple of anthologies published separately).

In late 2006, Dave Louapre announced there would be a collected edition published but the Canadian publisher they were in talks with went belly up later that year. Sweetman was last heard to be storyboarding the Get Smart remake starring Steve Carrell and Louapre was seeking other publishing alternatives.

[edit] Stories from BSFUC

  • Issue #1: A Cotton Candy Autopsy, in which psychotic clowns burn down the big-top and go on a drunken rampage
  • Issue #2: The Dead Johnsons' Big Incredible Day, in which a family of corpses has a big incredible day.
  • Issue #3: Diary of a Depressed Tap Dancer
  • Issue #4: The Black Balloon
  • Issue #5: The Crypt of The Magi
  • Issue #6: Happy Birthday to Hell
  • Issue #7: Ricky The Doughnut Boy
  • Issue #8: Die Rainbow Die
  • Issue #9: By The Light of The Screaming Moon
  • Issue #10: Safari Jim's Last Chance Gas
  • Issue #11: The Daffodils of Plague Town
  • Issue #12: Death Takes A Holiday, in which Death decides to hang out with an old man who looks interesting.
  • Issue #13: A Cotton Candy Autopsy II, a sequel to the first issue. Involves clowns.
  • Issue #14: Dangerous Prayers
  • Issue #15: The Pagan Tourist
  • Issue #16: The Santa of Demolition Street
  • Issue #17: A Conspiracy of Sweaters
  • Issue #18: The Neutered Beast, a tale about a cupid.
  • Issue #19: Nice Girls Don't Massacre Ants
  • Issue #20: Arnold: Confessions of a Blood Junkie, pretty sure this involves a fat vampire guy.
  • Issue #21: Dances With Cows
  • Issue #22: Psychotronic Virgin
  • Issue #23: Tiny Slimy, Writhing Thing
  • Issue #24: I Am Paul's Dog
  • Issue #25: Legion of Ogs
  • Issue #26: Dead Like Me
  • Issue #27: The No-Wax Killing Floor, a tale involving coackroaches if we recall correctly.
  • Issue #28: The Guilty Orphan
  • Issue #29: Gravity Sucks
  • Issue #30: The Dream is Dead, Gone, Shot Off, All Squashed Flat, the final issue.
