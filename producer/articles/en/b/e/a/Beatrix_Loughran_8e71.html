Beatrix S. Loughran (born June 30, 1900 in New York City; died December 7, 1975) is an American figure skater. She is the only American to win three Olympic medals in figure skating (1924, 1928, 1932). She is a six-time National champion (1925-1927 in singles, 1930-1932 in pairs).

In 1997, Loughran was inducted into the United States Figure Skating Hall of Fame.
