[edit] Early life

Beaver grew up on a farm out side of the Riverina town of Berrigan

Beaver attended Berrigan public school and Finley high school in 2003 he studied Certificate iii in agriculture at Murrumbidgee college of agriculture, Yanco.

[edit] Career

Upon completing college Beaver worked around Berrigan till 2005 when he moved into a cottage exactly half way between Jerilderie and Berrigan, till December when he moved to Finley were he lives to this day.

Beaver owns and drives a 1989 Holden Jackaroo called The Beavermobile, The Beavermobile a transgender, considers itself a ute stuck in a wagons body, The Beavermobile has encountered a small amount of discrimination, an example of this was when it was not allowed to enter the ute area at last years Deni ute muster.
