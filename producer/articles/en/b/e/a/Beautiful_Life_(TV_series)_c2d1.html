Beautiful Life (ビューティフルライフ) is a drama of "Toshiba Nichiyo Gekijo (東芝日曜劇場)" on TBS Television. It was first shown in Japan from 16 January to 26 March 2000.

[edit] Characters

  • Shuji Okishima – A hairstylist. His father wanted him to take over the Okishima hospital but he ignored his family. He becomes a Top Stylist and is picked up in magazines occasionally.
  • Kyoko Machida – Works at a library. The incidence of her disease came when she was 17 and since then, she has had to use a wheelchair.
  • Sachie Tamura – Kyoko's best friend who also works at the library. She encourages Kyoko all the time.
  • Takumi Okabe – Shuji's assistant. He respects Shuji.
  • Masao Machida – Kyoko's brother. He lives with his parents and works at the liquor store they own. He is close to Kyoko and is protective of her.
  • Satoru Kawamura – A hairstylist. He works at the same shop as Shuji and is very popular.
  • Kozo Miyama – Does volunteering. He likes Kyoko and comes to the library often.
  • Mayumi Ozawa – Top Stylist. She works at the same shop as Shuji.


[edit] Theme song

"Konya tsuki no mieru oka ni" by B'z. The CD single was released on February 9, 2000.

[edit] Subtitling

Beautiful Life was fansubbed by Japan-TV, a fansubbing group dedicated to providing Japanese television dramas in English.
