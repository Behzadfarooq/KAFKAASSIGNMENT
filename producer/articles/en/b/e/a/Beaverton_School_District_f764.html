Beaverton School District

The Beaverton School District is a school district in suburban Beaverton and Portland, Oregon. It is the third largest in the state of Oregon with an estimated enrollment of 35,329 students as of 2005. For 2004-05, the district had a total budget of $428 million.

The district employs 2,016 teachers at its 31 elementary, 8 middle, and 8 high schools.
