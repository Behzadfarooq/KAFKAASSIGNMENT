Beaver Lake (Kentucky)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Beaver Lake is a 158-acre artificial lake in Anderson County, Kentucky. Created in 1963, it is owned by the Kentucky Department of Fish and Wildlife Resources.[1]
