Beauharnois (provincial electoral district)

Beauharnois is a provincial electoral district in Quebec, Canada that elects members to the National Assembly of Quebec. The riding was created in 2003 from parts of Beauharnois-Huntingdon, Châteauguay and Salaberry-Soulanges.

[edit] Members of the National Assembly

  1. Serge Deslières, Parti Québécois (2003-present)
