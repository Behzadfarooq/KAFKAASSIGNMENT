Beatrisa "Bebe" Liang (梁靖賢 born March 31, 1988 in Tarzana, California), is an American figure skater. She trains at the Toyota Sports Center in El Segundo, California. Her coaches are Frank Carroll and Ken Congemi.

Liang has been competitive as a senior-level skater since she was thirteen years old. She has consistently placed in the top ten at the United States Figure Skating Championships and has competed for three years on the senior ISU Grand Prix of Figure Skating circuit.

Liang was one of four up-and-coming figure skaters featured on the 2006 TLC series, Ice Diaries. She is also a pianist. Liang attends college online.

Liang was originally coached by Tiffany Chin, who trained her for ten years. Then she worked with Christy Ness for a year, before switching to Frank Carroll and Ken Congemi in May, 2005.[1]
