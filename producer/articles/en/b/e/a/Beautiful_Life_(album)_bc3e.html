Beautiful Life is the second album by Guy Sebastian. It was released October 18, 2004 on the Sony BMG Australia label. It was followed by his "The Beautiful Life" Australian concert tour. The album reached 'Platinum' status in Australia selling more than 70,000 copies - this being significantly lower than approximately 420,000 copies of his debut Just as I Am.
