Beautiful Game Studios

Beautiful Game Studios is a computer game development studio based in London, England. The studio is an internal development team within Eidos and was set up in late 2003 to develop Championship Manager 5 (and future games in the series).

Championship Manager 2007 is their most recent project, released in October 2006.
