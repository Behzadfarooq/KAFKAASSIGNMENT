Beastorizer is a one-on-one beat 'em up arcade game released by 8ing/Raizing in 1997. The player has the choice between eight unique fighters with the ability to morph into powerful 'werebeasts' and battling their way to the final battle against Uriko, the deadly werechimera.

Beastorizer when released onto the PlayStation would be renamed as Bloody Roar.

[edit] Playable Characters

Yugo the Wolf
Alice the Rabbit
Long the Tiger
Gado the Lion
Bakuryu the Mole
Mitsuko the Boar
