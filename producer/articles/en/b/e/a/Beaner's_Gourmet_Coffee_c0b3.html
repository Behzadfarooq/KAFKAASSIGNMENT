Beaner's Gourmet Coffee is a coffee franchise based in the Midwestern United States. Founded in March, 1995 by Robert Fish and Mary Roszel, the first Beaners opened its doors in East Lansing, Michigan near the campus of Michigan State University on March 15, 1995. A second store opened in October of 1997 in Lansing, Michigan, one block from the Michigan State Capitol.

Beaners began franchising in 1999. As of August 1, 2006, Beaner's has 52 stores[1] in Alabama, Florida, Georgia, Illinois, Indiana, Michigan, Ohio, South Carolina and Wisconsin. Many of its stores feature complimentary Wi-Fi access for its patrons.
