Beauty and the Breakdown is an album by the hardcore band Bury Your Dead. The album was released on July 11, 2006 on Victory Records. The artwork is like a storybook and the song titles are from fairy tales. Prior to its release the album leaked to various P2P networks as well as torrent sites as of June 12, 2006.

Much like the second album, Cover Your Tracks, it has a theme to the entire CDs song titles. All the songs titles have to do with a children's tale, such as; "let down your hair"- Rapunzel, "Enchanted Rose"- Beauty and the Beast, etc. The second one was based on one of today's famous actors Tom Cruise. This one is based on a woman and her abuser as evidenced in the booklet that comes with the album and what else but hardcore themes of self-betterment to blend into it.
