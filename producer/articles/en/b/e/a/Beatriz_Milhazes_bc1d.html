Beatriz Milhazes was born in, Rio De Janeiro Brazil in 1960.

Technique

In her first UK exhibition (2001 from 4th April – 13th May) This had paintings where she uses Brazilian cultural imagery and references to western modernist painting, she creates smooth surfaces, this is produced from the artist's use of plastic sheeting onto which paint is applied and then peeled off onto canvases.
