Beauty from Pain is the fifth album released by the Christian pop rock band Superchic[k]. This is the first CD the band has released with an official drummer, Brandon Estelle. Christian rapper tobyMac appears in the song "Stories (Down to the Bottom)", which is also heard on his CD Welcome to Diverse City.
