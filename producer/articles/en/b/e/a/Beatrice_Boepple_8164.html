Beatrice Boepple (b. February 7, 1962 in Bennington, Vermont, USA) is an American actress best known for her role in the 1989 horror movie A Nightmare on Elm Street 5: The Dream Child as Amanda Krueger. She also starred in the 1987 hit movie Stakeout.

Beatrice made a guest appearance on Neon Rider andThe Beachcombers.
