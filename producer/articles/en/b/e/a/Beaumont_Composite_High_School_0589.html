Ecole Secondaire Beaumont Composite High School is a Canadian school in Beaumont, Alberta. It is located in the south west corner of town, and features an open atrium, a welding lab, several mechanics bays, a beauty culture lab, and a design lab. It also operates on a dual track system, which provides both an English language education, and also a French immersion program (where French is the primary language of instruction). It is a combined junior (grade 9) and senior (10-12) high school.

Students at the school can take select college credit courses, through a partnership with Athabasca University.
