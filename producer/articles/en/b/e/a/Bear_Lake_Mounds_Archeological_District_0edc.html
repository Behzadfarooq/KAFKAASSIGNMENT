Bear Lake Mounds Archeological District
IUCN Category V (Protected Landscape/Seascape)
Location: Monroe County, Florida, USA
Nearest city: Flamingo, Florida
Coordinates: 25°10′29″N, 80°56′13″W
Area: 3986 acres
Established: November 5, 1996
Governing body: National Park Service

The Bear Lake Mounds Archeological District is a U.S. National Historic Landmark District (designated as such on November 5, 1996) located north of Flamingo, Florida, east of Bear Lake.

It suffered extensive damaged from hurricane Wilma on October 24, 2005 and it, as well as the trail and surrounding boardwalk has been closed to the public since then.
