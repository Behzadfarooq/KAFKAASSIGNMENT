Beans & Weenies is episode 10b of the animated series, Camp Lazlo

The campers are tired of Chef McMuesli's "hot dogs" and the Jellies have to help him make them. The Jellies don't do the best job and he leaves. Lazlo decides to make their own hot dogs made with his weenies, Raj's baked buns, and Clam's mustard. Lazlo, Raj and Clam become famous throughout camp for making outstanding hot dogs. They get tired of doing it every Monday and Raj and Clam argue about which is better--buns or mustard. Lazlo tries to mediate, but the Jelly Beans split up to pursue unsuccessful solo careers.
