(ビュティ) - Beauty is the female heroine of Bobobo's group and usually the only girl when Suzu isn't around. Unlike the puns used for many of the characters, she received her name because the manga writer was looking at a video tape of the movie American Beauty when he created her character. A stereotypical teenage manga heroine, she is one of the few sane characters who is the first to join Bo-bobo and was the only citizen to still have hair after the attack of the town she lived in. She initionally wanted to join Bobobo because she seeked a life of adventure. Bobobo let her join him, but rejected Pickle at the same time. She has feelings for Gasser after he rescued her (the first time they met), but doesn't say anything about them. She is often exasperated by Bo-bobo's odd behavior or the behavior or actions of others, usually with the same face. She's the only person in the group that doesn't fight, but still criticizes the others' fighting styles. There are a few rare occurrences where she was forced to fight to defend herself, but its only when Bobobo isn't around to protect her. Beauty worries about her friends' safety and finds them as her own family and though finds Bo-bobo crazy she idolizes him greatly.

[edit] Trivia

  • Beauty's hobbies are shopping and cooking.
  • She likes hamburgers and dislikes crude (or for that matter any nonsensical) humor.
  • Her shoe size is 6 1/2.
  • It is revealed in episode 5 that Beauty has a VERY bad temper behind her pretty face when the Mean Green Soup Alien hits Don Patch with his soup craving power, she starts yelling because everyone was acting really stupid.
  • In Shinsetsu Bobobo-bo Bo-bobo, Beauty's wrist bands have hearts on them and her heart belt buckle is turned right.

[edit] Relationship with Heppokomaru

Beauty and Heppokomaru have a very romantic but quiet relationship. She loves him dearly and gets sad when he or her other friends are hurt. Her first encounter with him was in the forest after she is kidnapped by Tarashi. He uses 'Stink Bomb' and defeats Tarashi. Beauty was never able to thank him or ask for his name. Bo-bobo thinks Heppokomaru is evil, since the Mean Green Soup Alien was after him for not eating rice soup, but Beauty explains to Bo-bobo that he saved her hair from the evil of Tarashi. She and Heppokomaru have had some very romantic scenes like when Captain Battleship tried to attack Beauty with the attacking ducktail nose hair, Gasser pushed her out of the attack, which led to much blushing along with Gasser asking if Beauty was alright and Beauty thanking him. Also in the Hair Kingdom arc, they tell each other how they feel and kiss. In Shinsetsu Bobobo-bo Bo-bobo she cries when they incounter him only to know that he is now apart of the Hair Hunt troops (he still has his hair though), and is out to kill Bo-bobo. Right before Heppokomaru is about to swallow the demon pill, she slaps him so he can stop the evil he is doing, but instead he hits her aside and swallows the pill. Beauty has the tendency to make people cry for some reason because even while he is in demon form, Heppokomaru turns back to normal and begins to cry for hurting Beauty and Bo-bobo. She is then attacked by another enemy (possibly a Hair Hunt troop), but she is defenseless since Bo-bobo is injured, but Heppokomaru gets in front and takes the attack even though he is punched severely and begins bleeding. This made Beauty very sad and she began crying for him to recover. So you can see that she loves Heppokomaru very much.

[edit] Forms

  • Bride: While Gasser was in the haunted area of the A-Block Amusment Park, he realizes he has to get married (though it was arranged before he even entered the Haunted House). He then imagines Beauty in a wedding dress (this was also the second time you can see that he has a crush on Beauty) and she is approving to this surprise marraige.
  • Panda: When Rice trapped Beauty in his giant rice bowl, the result of being in there too long would turn you into a panda. However, unlike the luckless Hair Hunter who was caught in there, a panda suit simply came out which Beauty slipped on
  • Child: When she and Gasser enter the 'Kid's Area' in Holy Guacomole Land, they turn into little kids. In this form she is small, her clothes shrink along with her, and her eyes are bigger (seeing she is a small child).
