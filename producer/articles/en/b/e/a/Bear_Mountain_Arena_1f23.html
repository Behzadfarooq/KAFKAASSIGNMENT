Bear Mountain Arena

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Bear Mountain Arena is in Colwood, British Columbia, Canada. The arena was built in 2004, and its capacity is 2300. It is the home of the Victoria Shamrocks and the Victoria Grizzlies. It was the home of the Victoria Salmon Kings for the 2004-2005 ECHL season. It has played host to minor lacrosse teams in Victoria, and has hosted BCLA provincial lacrosse championships.
