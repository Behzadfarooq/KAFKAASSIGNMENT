[edit] Mergefrom affiliates of Beal Bank

The creator of the article on Beal Bank has created a page each for its affiliates, CSG Investments, Inc., Loan Acquisition Corporation, and Beal Mortgage Services. While Beal Bank passes WP:CORP by virtue of news coverage, its affiliates fail WP:CORP. A Lexis search shows no significant news coverage on the 3 affiliates, and I could find neither Beal Bank nor its affiliates in a Yahoo stock search. So the article on each of the 3 affiliates needs to be merged, or deleted. Pan Dan 18:00, 9 September 2006 (UTC)
