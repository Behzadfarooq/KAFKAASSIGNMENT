Beauty and the Beast (disambiguation)

Beauty and the Beast can refer to:

  • Beauty and the Beast, the traditional folktale
    • Beauty and the Beast (1946 film), by Jean Cocteau, also known as La Belle et la Bête
    • Beauty and the Beast (1991 film), an animated Disney version
      • "Beauty and the Beast" (1991 song), from the 1991 film
      • Beauty and the Beast Live on Stage, theme park show based on the animated movie
      • Beauty and the Beast (musical) based on the animated movie
    • Beauty and the Beast (1992 film), a Golden Films production
    • Beauty and the Beast (TV series)
  • Beauty and the Beast (TV show), Australian panel show
  • "Beauty and the Beast" (song), by David Bowie
  • Beauty & the Beast (X-Men episode), an episode in the 1994 X-Men animated series.
  • Beauty and the Beast, a frequent name used to designate an aesthetic tendency of the Gothic metal genre.
