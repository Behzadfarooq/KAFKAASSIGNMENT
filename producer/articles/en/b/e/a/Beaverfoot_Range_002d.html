Beaverfoot Range

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Beaverfoot Range ( 51°08′N, 116°38′W) is a mountain range of the Canadian Rockies, located in southeastern British Columbia. The range extends from Cedared Creek near Spillimacheen north to the Kicking Horse River.

This range includes the following mountains and peaks:

Mountain/Peak metres feet
Kapristo Mountain 2,722 8,930
Castle Mountain 2,545 8,350
Coral Mountain 2,460 8,071
