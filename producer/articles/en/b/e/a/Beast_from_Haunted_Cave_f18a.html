Beast from Haunted Cave is a 1959 horror/gangster/heist film directed by Monte Hellman and starring Michael Forest, Frank Wolff, Richard Sinatra, and Sheila Carroll. Filmed in South Dakota, it tell the story of bank robbers fleeing in the snow who run afoul of a beast that feeds on humans.

[edit] Trivia

Chris Robinson , who later starred on General Hospital, both constructed and portrayed the "beast".
