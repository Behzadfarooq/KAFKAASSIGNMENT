Beautiful Collision is the second solo album by New Zealand artist Bic Runga, released on July 1, 2002 in New Zealand, July 15, 2002 in Australia, and November 5, 2002 in the USA.

This album went eleven times platinum in New Zealand, and was named Highest Selling Album at the New Zealand Music Awards.

RIANZ ranked Beautiful Collision first in the New Zealand Top 50 Albums of 2003. It charted for 49 weeks. It ranked 19th in the New Zealand Top 50 Albums of 2004 for a further 28 weeks.
