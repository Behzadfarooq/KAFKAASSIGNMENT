Beau M. Billingslea is an American actor and voice actor. He is best known as the voice of Jet Black from the very popular anime Cowboy Bebop. In addition to voice acting, Billingslea has appeared in many popular television series as a prolific guest actor. Before he got into acting, he played football at the University of Connecticut. He had also been drafted out of high school by the Kansas City A's (now Oakland A's).
