Beautiful Love

Beautiful Love was the hit single from I Wish We All Could Win, the debut album by Christian rock band The Afters.

[edit] Critical Success

  • Certified Gold by the RIAA for Digital Sales in 2006
The single's presence in the TV show 8th & Ocean prompted a six-week surge in which Beautiful Love was downloaded 90,000 times
  • Nielson Soundscan named Beautiful Love as the top downloaded song on the inspirational chart for 2006.
  • No. 1 R&R Christian CHR single
  • Nominated for a Dove Award
  • "Beautiful Love" won the mtvU Award for Best Streaming Video in 2005
  • Beautiful Love spent 10+ weeks on iTunes' top-100 downloaded songs chart (peaking at #14)

[edit] Charts

  • Peaked on the Billboard Hot 100 chart at #55
  • Peaked on the Billboard Pop 100 chart at #43
  • Peaked on the Billboard Hot Digital Songs chart at #23

[edit] Television

  • "Beautiful Love" was used as the theme song for the MTV reality series 8th & Ocean.
