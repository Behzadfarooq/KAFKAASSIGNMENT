Beauty Point, New South Wales

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Beauty Point is a piece of land protruding into the Middle Harbour of Sydney Harbour, Australia. It was originally known as Billy Goat Point, but was renamed Beauty Point when it was subdivided to be sold. The name is used to refer to the surrounding area as an urban locality in the suburb of Mosman.
