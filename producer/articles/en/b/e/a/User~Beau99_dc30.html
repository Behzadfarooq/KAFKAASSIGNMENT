I am a 20-year-old former college student from Phoenix, Arizona. I am a fan of music; particularly hard rock, but I also like some country music. I am also an avid anime and cartoon fan. I also show enough symptoms to be diagnosed with Asperger syndrome, but self-diagnosis is good enough for me... especially since I was misdiagnosed with Attention Deficit Disorder.
