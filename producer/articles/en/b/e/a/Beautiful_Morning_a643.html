Beautiful Morning

Beautiful Morning was the lead single for Ace of Base's 2002 record Da Capo. Beautiful Morning was released in the middle of September 2002 and did very well on European radio especially in Germany where the song was the fastest climbing song on the radio during that year. On the charts the song was a minor hit.
