Beatties is a British department store group with 12 stores across the Midlands. In 2005 James Beattie was acquired by House of Fraser. On 14 January 2006, the Birmingham store closed.

  • Dudley
  • Huddersfield
  • Northampton
  • Solihull
  • Sutton Coldfield
  • Telford
  • Wolverhampton (former Head Office)
  • Worcester
