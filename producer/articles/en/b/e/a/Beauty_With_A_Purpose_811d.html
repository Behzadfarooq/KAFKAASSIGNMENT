Beauty With A Purpose (formerly known as Miss World Scholarship) is an event established in 2001 that is celebrated during the activities prior to the Miss World pageant. It awards the contestant with the most relevant and important charity project in her nation. Since 2005 the winner earns a spot in the semifinals.
