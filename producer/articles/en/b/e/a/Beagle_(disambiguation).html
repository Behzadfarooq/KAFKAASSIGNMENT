Beagle is a dog breed.

Beagle is also the name of:

  • HMS Beagle, the ship in which Charles Darwin undertook the travels during which he made many observations which became important for his formulation of his theory of evolution
    • For other Royal Navy ships named Beagle, see HMS Beagle (disambiguation)
  • The Beagle Channel in extreme southern South America near the Strait of Magellan, named after HMS Beagle which explored it twice
  • The Beagle Gulf in the vicinity of Australian city of Darwin, Northern Territory, named after HMS Beagle which explored it in 1839
  • Beagle 2, a failed British Mars lander named after HMS Beagle. It crashed on 25 December 2003
  • The Soviet Ilyushin Il-28 bomber aircraft, which had the NATO reporting name of Beagle
  • Beagle Aircraft, a former British aircraft manufacturer
  • Beagle (software), a desktop search service for GNU/Linux users.
  • Beagle (crater), the crater on Mars explored by the Opportunity rover
  • Peter S. Beagle, fantasy writer
  • Beagle (beer), an Argentinean beer
