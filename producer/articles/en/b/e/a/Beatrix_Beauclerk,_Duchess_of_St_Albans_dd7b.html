Beatrix Beauclerk, Duchess of St Albans GBE DGStJ (25 March 1877â€“5 August 1953) was a daughter of the 5th Marquess of Lansdowne and his wife, Maud.

On 16 October 1897, she married the 6th Marquess of Waterford (becoming the Marchioness of Waterford) and they later had six children. Her husband died in 1911 and on 19 August 1918, she married the Osborne Beauclerk, 12th Duke of St Albans.
