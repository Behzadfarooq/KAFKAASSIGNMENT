"Beach Day"[1] is an upcoming episode of the third season of the U.S. version of The Office, and according to Jenna Fischer will be the 50th episode[2], though it is unknown if this is referring to production or airing order. It will be directed by acclaimed film director Harold Ramis[1], who previously directed the episode A Benihana Christmas.

[edit] Synopsis

Spoiler warning: Plot and/or ending details follow.

The workers head out to the beach, where Michael forces them to compete in Survivor-like challenges to find out who among them is the best employee.
