Beat Hefti (born February 3, 1978 in Schwellbrun) is a bobsledder from Switzerland, who competed in the bobsleigh events at the 2006 Winter Olympics in Turin, Italy. Hefti, paired with Martin Annen, won the bronze medal in the Men's Two-person bobsleigh event. Hefti also won bronze in the four-person bobsleigh event with teammates Martin Annen, Thomas Lamparter, and Cedric Grand.
