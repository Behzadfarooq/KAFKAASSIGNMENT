The Beatles' Beatles for Sale (No 2) EP was released June 4, 1965. The EP was only released in Mono and never released in Stereo. It's catalogue number is Parlophone GEP 8938.

[edit] Track listing

Side A:

  1. "I'll Follow the Sun"
  2. "Baby's in Black"

Side B:

  1. "Words of Love"
  2. "I Don't Want to Spoil the Party"
