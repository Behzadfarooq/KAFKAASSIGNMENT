Beaver Dam Middle School is a secondary school serving grades six through eight. It is part of the Littlefield Unified School District.

Beaver Dam Middle School maintains a population of just over 130.

[edit] History

Beaver Dam Middle School started in 2005 for the 2005-2006 school year. It houses grades 6th, 7th and 8th. The School currently got its first elective classes: Speech and Debate, Choir, Theater,and Jounalism. It also got a new class Reading for the first time which is a required class which every students takes. More history later on.
