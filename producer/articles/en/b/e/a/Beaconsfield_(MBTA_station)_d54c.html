Beaconsfield (MBTA station)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Beaconsfield is a stop on the D branch of the MBTA's Green Line. It is located on Beaconsfield Rd. in Brookline, a short walk away from the Dean Road stop on the C branch.

[edit] Accessibility

This station is not wheelchair accessible.
