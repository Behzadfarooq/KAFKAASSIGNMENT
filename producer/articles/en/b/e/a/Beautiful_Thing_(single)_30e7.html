"Beautiful Thing" is the second single of Dutch pop singer Do from her second album, Follow Me.

[edit] Video

The video was shot in Bonaire. It is the continued story of Beautiful Thing where Do is now sick of the games her lover plays and cannot take it any more. In Beautiful Thing she was singing about how good love is; in Sending Me Roses she is over that.
