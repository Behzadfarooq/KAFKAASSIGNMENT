Beacon Hill (Chinese: 煙墩山 or 筆架山) is a hill in the Kowloon Tong area of Hong Kong's Kowloon peninsula. It is the 17th highest hill of Hong Kong. It is 417 m tall.

Beacon Hill is located within the Lion Rock Country Park.
