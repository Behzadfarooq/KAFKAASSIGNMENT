Beautiful Savior Lutheran Church (Portland, Oregon)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Beautiful Savior Lutheran Church is a congregation of the Lutheran Church - Missouri Synod (LCMS) located in Portland, Oregon. It is part of the Synod's Northwest Dictrict, and was formed in 1955. With 942 members as of 2006, it is the largest LCMS congregation in Oregon and the ninth largest in the district, which also includes churches in Alaska, Idaho and Washington.
