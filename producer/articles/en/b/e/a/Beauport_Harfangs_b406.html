The Beauport Harfangs ("Snow Owls") were a team in the Quebec Major Junior Hockey League from 1990 to 1997. They played their home games at the Aréna Marcel Bédard in the Quebec City suburb of Beauport, Quebec. They moved from the suburbs to the big city in 1997, becoming the second edition of the Quebec Remparts.
