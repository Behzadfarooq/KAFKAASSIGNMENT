[edit] Reference by real name (Animated series)

Isn't Cyborg the first male Titan to be called by his real name in the animated series? (Season 3, episode 1: "Deception"). He infiltrates the organization, run by Brother Blood, under the alias "Stone". Stone is his actual family name, his full name being: Victor "Vic" Stone. -Hecko 17:55, 12 December 2005 (UTC)

They never indicated that was his real name, only his supervillain alias. KramarDanIkabu [[User talk:KramarDanIkabu|(speak)]] 17:56, 12 December 2005 (UTC)
I see your point. Kind of when Robin's alternate dimension/universe self (the little guy in the episode "Fractured" from Season 2), was called Nosyarg Kcid (Dick Grayson, Robin's real name, spelled backwards). -Hecko 21:10, 13 December 2005 (UTC)
