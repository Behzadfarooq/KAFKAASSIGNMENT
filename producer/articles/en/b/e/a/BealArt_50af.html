[edit] Programs

Bealart has ten separate fully equiped studio facilities which are augmented by a computer lab, art library, and courses in art history and drawing. Developed by practising artists, the program provides professional instruction with a hands-on approach.

The foundation programs are offered as half day or full day courses. The programs provide students the opportunity to immerse themselves in an intensive, studio based experience.

A specialist (SPECIAL ART) year allows students to develop expertise in two of a possible ten selected disciplines.

BealArt is one of the few vocational highschools that offers students with the opportunity to develop their fine art skills in a professional atmosphere.

[edit] Studios

Painting acrylic, oil, encaustic, watercolour, gouache Printmaking etching, lithography, relief, silkscreen, papermaking and book binding

3-D Design carving, casting and construction, functional and nonfunctional forms

Ceramic Technology pottery, ceramics, sculpture, glaze and firing technology

Photography 35mm, medium format, digital, studio and darkroom

Video/Animation video production, classical animation and film studies

Textiles/Fabric Arts weaving, felting, knitting, dye painting, embelishing, screenprinting

Commercial Design advertising, illustration, digital design

Drawing observational, analytical, expressive and figure drawing

Art History art criticism, cultural history, the business of being an artist


[edit] Year End Show

BealArt's Year End Show (YES) is a showing of work by BealArt students over the year. It is a juried show and prizes are awarded. Some awards are cash prizes and scholarhips. It is a three day event in which thousands of spectators attend throughout the weekend.
