Beautiful Assassins is the solo project of American drummer Matt Walker. Matt's solo work is very melodic in structure, yet directed towards electronica and is very percussion-oriented. Beautiful Assassins was created to serve as an outlet for Matt's enormous backcatalogue of unreleased music over the past decade or so.
