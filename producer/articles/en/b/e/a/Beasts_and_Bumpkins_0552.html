Beasts and Bumpkins is a 1997 real-time strategy PC game developed by Worldweaver Productions and published by Electronic Arts.

Stripped of your lands and exiled by the king, you must rebuild your empire from scratch with just a handful of peasants at your command. Manage your limited resources carefully to increase the population of your servants and defeat the monstrous minions of the Dark Lord Sabellian terrorizing the countryside.
