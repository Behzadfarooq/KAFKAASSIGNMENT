[edit] Localisation of the school

About the localisation of Beauxbatons: it is interesting to notice that there is a bus station near Poitiers (in Mignaloux-Beauvoir exactly: cf. the article in french) which is called Chemin de Beaubaton ([1]). In fact Beaubaton is an industrial district of Mignaloux-Beauvoir. The Academy of Beaubâtons could be there... not far from the château de Lusignan, Melusine's castle!

Caesius, January 2, 2005
