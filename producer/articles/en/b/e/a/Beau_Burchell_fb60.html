Beau Burchell (December 18th, 1978) is a guitarist for the Capitol Records band Saosin. He plays a Gibson Les Paul like the other members.

[edit] Live Setup

Beau uses Gibson Les Paul Standards and SG Standards into a Hughes and Kettner Triamp Mk.II through a Marshall 1960BV. He shares a rack with Chris, which is mostly identical to Justin's, plus Chris's amp head and wireless unit. Instead of the GCX controller and Ground Control unit present in Justin's rig, Beau just uses the Hughes potwat foot controller. He uses Ernie Ball .012-.054 strings.
