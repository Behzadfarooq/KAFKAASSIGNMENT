"Beautiful World" was the third single to be released from the her forthcoming debut album, "R3MIX". She has had 2 singles "Shock To The System" and "Dirty Business" both of which have been number one on the UK dance and club charts. "Beautiful World" was released on March 13, 2006. It is the title track for Sara's new album, set for a release soon after. This single reached number one in the UK dance and club charts.

Sara Jorge in the music video for her single "Beautiful World"

Promo music video for "Beautiful World" by Sara Jorge.

The video for the song features Sara walking through what at first appears to be grey and worn out urban area, but as she sings the place flowers and everything turns green and multicoloured.
