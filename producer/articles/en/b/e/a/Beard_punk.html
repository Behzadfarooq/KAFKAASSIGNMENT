Beard Punk is a sub-genre of music which draws heavy influences from punk, post hardcore, indie, folk, emo, and pop punk. Beard punk is commonly associated with Gainesville, Florida based independent record label No Idea Records.

Some notable bands include Hot Water Music, Against Me!, Small Brown Bike, and Glass and Ashes.The reason for this being called, beard punk is because a few members of said bands had beards.
