Beaterator is a music mixer game to be released in Summer 2007 by Rockstar Games for the PlayStation Portable. Beaterator is being developed by Rockstar Leeds, which was also responsible for developing the Grand Theft Auto games Liberty City Stories and Vice City Stories for the PSP as well. The game will also be co-produced by noted music producer Timbaland.

The game is based off a music mixing tool released on the Internet in 2005 by Rockstar and is expected to contain all-new music, and other audio mixing features, as well as new content and modes such as "rhythm games" and other challenges.
