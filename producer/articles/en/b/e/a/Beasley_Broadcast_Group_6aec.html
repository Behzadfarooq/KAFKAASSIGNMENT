Beasley Broadcast Group, Inc. NASDAQ: BBGI, based in Naples, Florida, is an owner/operator of radio stations in the United States. As of November, 2006, the company owned 16 AM stations and 26 FM stations. [2]

[edit] History

The company was founded in 1961 by George G. Beasley. On February 11, 2000 the Group completed its IPO.
