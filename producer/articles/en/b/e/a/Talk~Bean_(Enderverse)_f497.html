[edit] Anton

The bolded part of the following sentence doesn't make sense:

"The other embryos were locked away, until they were stolen by Volescu, who used turned Anton's Key in them."

And, BTW, who is Anton? --Jose Ramos 08:26, 13 Feb 2004 (UTC)

Anton is the man whose work laid the groundwork for Bean's creation. His work was for the effects of genetics on intelligence. He was served with an order of inhibition, meaning he could not talk about the subject without inducing a panic attack. He got around this by communicating his findings in a nonchalant way and by using religion as a vehicle. —The preceding unsigned comment was added by 70.25.138.179 (talk • contribs) 12:59, 7 July 2005 (UTC).
