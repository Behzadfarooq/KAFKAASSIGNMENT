Beast is often a word used to describe a large, often dangerous animal. It can also mean a bullying, often violent person.

Beast or The Beast can also refer to:

  • Beast (Beauty and the Beast), a fictional character and main hero of the 1991 animated film Beauty and the Beast
  • Beast (comics), a Marvel Comics superhero
  • Beast (B.C. Rich Guitar), a guitar model by B.C. Rich
  • Beast (video game), an ASCII game made in 1984
  • The Beast (Krull), a fictional character and main villain of the 1983 sci-fi fantasy Krull
  • The Beast (Poltergeist), a fictional character and main villain of the 1982-88 Poltergeist film series. It was seen as a large spider-like creature in the first movie (only appearing briefly near the end), but took on the human form of Reverend Henry Kane in the second and third films.
  • The Beast (1988 film)
  • The Beast (1996 film)
  • The Beast (game), an alternate reality game created to promote the film A.I.: Artificial Intelligence
  • The Beast (Homeworld), the primary antagonist in the computer game Homeworld: Cataclysm
  • The Beast (book), a biography of Aleister Crowley by Daniel P. Mannix
  • The Beast (newspaper), a Buffalo, New York newspaper
  • The Beast (roller coaster), a wooden roller coaster located at Paramount's Kings Island near Cincinnati, Ohio
  • The Beast (wrestler), the stage name of Yvon Cormier, a retired Canadian professional wrestler
  • The Beast (TV series), a very short-lived 2001 TV series
  • Dan "The Beast" Severn, American mixed martial artist and professional wrestler
  • Daily Beast, a fictional newspaper in the novel Scoop
  • The Beast, a fictional demon, possibly Satan himself, featured in Doctor Who.
  • Satan is often referred to as "The Beast"
  • Júlio Baptista, Arsenal Midfielder, nicknamed "The Beast"
  • Aleister Crowley, English occultist, nicknamed "The Beast"
  • Miguel Angel Nadal, retired Spanish footballer, nicknamed "The Beast of Barcelona"
  • Max Mirnyi, Belarusian Tennis Player, nicknamed "The Beast Of Belarus"
  • Shadow of the Beast, a scrolling action game often abbreviated to "Beast"
  • Daigo Umehara, a professional versus fighting games player nicknamed "The Beast"
  • Episode 2 of the anime series Neon Genesis Evangelion.
  • Milwaukee's Best, a beer, is often called beast by American college students.
  • Television series Buffy the Vampire Slayer and Angel feature:
    • The Beast (Buffyverse), a fictional demon in Angel and
    • Glorificus, a goddess in Buffy the Vampire Slayer also referred to as "The Beast."
