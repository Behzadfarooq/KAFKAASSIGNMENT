Beaumont-en-Auge is a commune of the Calvados département in the Basse-Normandie région in France.

[edit] Eminent people

  • Jean-Charles Langlois, painter, born at Beaumont.
  • Pierre-Simon Laplace, mathematician, physicist, astronomer and philosopher, born at Beaumont-en-Auge.
