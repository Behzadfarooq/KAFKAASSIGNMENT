Beauty & the Beast is an episode in the 1994 animated TV series X-Men Animated Series.

Spoiler warning: Plot and/or ending details follow.

[edit] Characters

X-Men: Professor X, Cyclops, Wolverine, Storm, Rogue, Gambit, Beast, Jean Grey, Jubilee

Supporting Characters Magneto

Villains: Graydon Creed

[edit] Synopsis

This episode begins in a Laboratory where Beast is working on a cure for blindness, for his patient, Carlie, a young lady who has grown fond of Beast. Suddenly there is an explosion. Beast carries Carlie out of the building and then the Friends of Humanity members and Graydon Creed, who were responsible for the vandalism, attack Beast. But then the cops come and the mob disperses. This attack angers Wolverine, but Jean Grey calms him down by telling that this is not the X-Men way. Then he goes away. Later he finds a Friends of Humanity hideout and injures himself and says that it was a mutant, to get inside the building. Carlie's father hates mutants and creates a scene at the hospital when he hears that a mutant saved his daughter. So Beast to leaves. Later Carlie gets her operation, but Friends of Humanity kidnap her. So, Beast calls the X-Men for help. Since Wolverine was already at the Friends of Humanity HQ, he frees Carlie and lets the other X-Men inside. Wolverine had asked the X-Men to bring a hologram, and shows all the Friends of Humanity members that Sabretooth is actually Graydon Creed's father. Hearing that their leader's father is a mutant, all the friends of Humanity members leave, then the X-Men also leave, and take Carlie to his father. After this, her father seems to have gotten rid of his prejudice against mutants.

Meanwhile, in Savage Land, where Professor X and Magneto are trapped, get caught in a net set up by the Savage Land Mutates, while they were crossing the river. Amphibious stuns them, and then they wake up tied up on a raft. Amphibious is taking them to the new ruler of Savage Land, but later Magneto and Xavier escape from the raft.
