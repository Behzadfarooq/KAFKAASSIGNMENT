The Beachside Sharks are an Australian rules football club formed in the western Los Angeles area. Formed in 2007, the Beachside Sharks compete in the Southern California Australian Football League.


[edit] History

The Sharks were founded by Adam Howarth in 2007. The Sharks being a new team have yet to accumlite much history, but they are poised to begin the work.
