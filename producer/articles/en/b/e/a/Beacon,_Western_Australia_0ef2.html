Beacon is a town in Western Australia. It is located in the Wheatbelt region with agriculture being one of the major occupations in the area. it is located in the Shire of Mount Marshall. Beacon is 42 km north of Bencubbin and 333 km northeast of Perth, lying on the northeast border of the wheatbelt. The population of Beacon is estimated to be between 73 [1] and 100 [2].
