Beromünster

Beromünster is a municipality in the district of Sursee in the canton of Lucerne in Switzerland.

Beromünster is a famous site of a transmission facility for medium wave and shortwave with the famous landmark Blosenbergturm. There is also a castle that held the first printing press in Switzerland in the 1470s.
