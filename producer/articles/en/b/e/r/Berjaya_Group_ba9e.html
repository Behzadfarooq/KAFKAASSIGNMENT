Berjaya Group Berhad (KLSE: BJGROUP) is one of the most diversified conglomerates in Malaysia and is headed by Tan Sri Dato' Seri Vincent Tan Chee Yioun. The group's main activities includes Property Investment & Development, Gaming & Lottery Management and Vacation Time-Share, Travel, Hotels & Resorts Development & Management.

Today, Berjaya Group is a major Malaysian conglomerate with an annual revenue in excess of RM2.93 billion. Through a series of acquisitions, organic growth and the entrepreneurial leadership of Tan Sri Dato' Seri Vincent Tan, Berjaya Group today has a total employee of over 17,000, and is engaged in a variety of core activities. This include, property and construction, vacation, hotels & resorts, education, investment holdings, food & beverages and industrial businesses.

Main companies under Berjaya Corporation listed on Bursa Malaysia Securities Berhad are:

  • Berjaya Land Berhad
  • Berjaya Capital Berhad
  • Berjaya Sports Toto Berhad
  • Cosway Corporation Berhad
  • Dunham-Bush (Malaysia) Bhd

[edit] History

The Berjaya Group was established 1984 when Vincent Tan acquired a major controlling stake in the share capital of Berjaya Kawat Berhad from its founders, BHP Ltd, Australia and National Iron & Steel Mills, Singapore.
