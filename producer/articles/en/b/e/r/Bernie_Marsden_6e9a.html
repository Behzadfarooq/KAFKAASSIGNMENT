Bernie Marsden

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Bernie Marsden (born Bernard John Marsden, 7 May 1951, in Buckingham, Buckinghamshire) is an English guitarist and a former member of the Hard rock band Whitesnake. In more recent years, Bernie has toured with his band Bernie Marsden's Whitesnake and performed with one of the UK's most celebrated singers Elkie Brooks.
