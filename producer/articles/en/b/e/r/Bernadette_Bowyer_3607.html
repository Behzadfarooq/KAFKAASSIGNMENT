Bernadette Bowyer

Bernadette Bowyer (born January 23, 1966 in Brampton, Ontario) is a former field hockey player from Canada, who represented her native country at the 1992 Summer Olympics in Barcelona, Spain.

The Canadian National Team finished seventh in the competition.
