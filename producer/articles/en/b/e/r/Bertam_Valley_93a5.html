Bertam Valley

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Bertam Valley, is an agricultural based village in the Cameron Highlands, near to Ringlet. Its entry is located just at the Ringlet. At the side of the village, is the famous water dam in Cameron Highlands. Now a new road is making connect to Kuala Lipis.
