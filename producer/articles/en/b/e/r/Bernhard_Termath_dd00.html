Bernhard Termath (born August 26, 1928 and died March 24, 2004) was a German football coach and former player.

He earned 7 caps and scored 4 goals for West Germany from 1951 to 1954.

He was most notable for coaching the Karlsruher SC football team from February 19, 1968 to June 30, 1968.

According to a 2001 article on Termath in the German magazine Kicker, German-American actor Steve Termath is a distant relative of Termath.
