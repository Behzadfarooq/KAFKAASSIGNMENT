Bert Bates

Bert Bates is one of the authors of the book "Head First Design Patterns" published by O'Reilly Media and the partner of Kathy Sierra.

[edit] Publications

  • Freeman, Eric; Elisabeth Freeman, Kathy Sierra, and Bert Bates (2004). Head First Design Patterns. O'Reilly Media, 637. ISBN 0-596-00712-4.
