I'll wait a day or so before deleting, in case you can change my mind <g>. Zora 02:38, 11 July 2005 (UTC)

[edit] The SPICE linkspammer

Dear Bernard, thanks for the work you did reverting the work of the linkspammer. We get that often in the Bollywood articles. If you have the time and energy, there are additional steps you can take.

  • Look at the contrib list for the spammer's IP -- it will point you to spammed articles that aren't on your watchlist
  • Post a warning on the talk page for the IP saying that the user will be banned if he/she doesn't stop linkspamming.

Usually the warning is enough to stop the spamming. When it's clear that the spam will be reverted quickly and that banning will follow, the spammer usually gives up. Sometimes you have to follow through with reporting vandalism on the WP:VIP vandalism page, but if you don't want to mess with that, message me. If I'm online (which I am all too much of the time) I'll take care of the details.

P.S. Someone from bharatmatrimony.com has been slowly, slowly posting links to various actors' articles. It's a commercial site, and the film links are clearly just there to attract customers, but they actually seemed useful. What do you think? Keep or delete? Zora 22:16, 18 July 2005 (UTC)
