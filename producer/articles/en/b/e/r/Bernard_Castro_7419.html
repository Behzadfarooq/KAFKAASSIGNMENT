Bernard Castro

Bernard Castro (1904 - August 24, 1991) was the inventor of the convertible couch.

Castro was born in Sicily. He arrived in USA in 1919.

He died as a millionaire in 1991. His wife Theresa Castro whom he married on Valentine's Day 1942 died at the age of 85 in March 2002 ([1]).
