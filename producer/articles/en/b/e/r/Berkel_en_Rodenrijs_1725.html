Berkel en Rodenrijs is a town and former municipality in the western Netherlands, in the province of South Holland. The former municipality had a population of 19,251 on January 1, 2006 (source: CBS), and covered an area of 18.91 km² (7.30 mile²) of which 0.29 km² (0.11 mile²) is water. On January 1, 2007, the town was merged with neighbouring towns Bergschenhoek and Bleiswijk to form the new municipality Lansingerland.
