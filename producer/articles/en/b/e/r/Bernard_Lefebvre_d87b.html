Bernard Lefebvre

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

See also: Lefèvre

Bernard Lefebvre, known as Ellebé, is a French photographer (1906 - 1992). He was a member of the Rouen Academy, and president of the Rouen Photo-club from 1937-1941 and 1951-1977.
