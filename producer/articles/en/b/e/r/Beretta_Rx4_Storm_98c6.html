The Beretta Rx4 is a a rifle developed by Fabbrica d'Armi Pietro Beretta of Italy. The action is a Gas-operated, rotating bolt that is self adjusting. The rifle uses STANAG compatible magazines (ex. M16 magazines). The rifle's stock has five points of adjustment. This rifle is in the same family as the Beretta Px4 Storm pistol and the Cx4 Storm Carbine. It is currently available for civilians and many police forces currently use these as well. It is not in military use.The Beretta Rx4 Storm is the latest member of the Total Solutions for Law Enforcement family. This semiautomatic .223 special purpose rifle emphasizes individual performance with multiple stock configurations, ergonomic adjustment, and ambidextrous controls.

The first .223 caliber carbine to join the Beretta Storm family emphasizes ergonomics, individual performance, modularity, customization, and adaptability for different applications and users, while continuing to be the finest in firearms reliability and accuracy. With homeland security and personal officer protection in mind, the Rx4 Storm comes standard with a reversible cross bolt safety and ambidextrous bolt release. It accepts standard M4, M16, and AR15 magazines, but unlike some other weapon systems, the magazine release button is reversible and may be operated from either side.

The new requirements of homeland security demand the ability to fire a higher performance and longer distance ammunition, in a firearm with the ability to be modified to each operator. Available in two models, a 12.5” barrel and a 16 inch barrel version, both are lightweight with simplified controls, with a standard 10 round magazine included. The 12.5 inch barrel is threaded and features a removable muzzle brake. The Rx4 Storm accepts the Beretta vertical grip, or other after market grips. The MIL-STD-1913 “Picatinny” top rail is standard, with side and bottom and fore-end rails optional. The optional rails allow for mounting at the 12,3,6, and 9 o’clock positions.

Designed by Beretta and Benelli engineers, the Rx4 Storm incorporates the most advanced technology in firearms. Using the ARGO (Auto Regulating Gas Operating) system perfected by Benelli, the gas port is located just forward of the chamber where the gases are hotter and cleaner, resulting in less fouling and more reliable cycling. The action bars are required to move only ½” for complete cycling which results in fast operation with minimum vibration, making it easier to handle and operate. Additionally, the position of the gas valves allows for the compact and lightweight design of this rifle.

Emphasizing the popular individualized performance and modularity of the Beretta Storm family, the Rx4 Storm has nine attachment points for slings for multiple carry options, making it more individualized than your typical rifle. Just like its brothers, the Rx4 includes a technopolymer design with a simplified disassembly system that does not require tools. When the last round fires, the bolt locks to the rear for additional user safety. The five position telescoping stock allows the operator to adjust the length of pull for size, stature, and use of body armor. Also optional are a fixed pistol grip stock or traditional rifle stock to meet the preferences of different users. Included are combat ghost ring sights with protective ears, adjustable for windage and elevation.
