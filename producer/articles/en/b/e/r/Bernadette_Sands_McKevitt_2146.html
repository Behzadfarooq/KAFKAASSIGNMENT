Bernadette Sands McKevitt

Bernadette Sands McKevitt is an Irish Republican from Belfast, Northern Ireland. She is a former leading member of the 32 County Sovereignty Movement.

She is the younger sister of late IRA hunger striker Bobby Sands. Her husband, Michael McKevitt, was the quartermaster general of the Provisional IRA and later a founding member of the Real IRA, an anti-Good Friday Agreement splinter group.
