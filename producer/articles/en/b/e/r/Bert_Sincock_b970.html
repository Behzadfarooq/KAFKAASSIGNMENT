Herbert Sylvester Sincock (born on September 8, 1887 in Barkerville, British Columbia, Canada - died on August 1, 1946) is a former Major League Baseball player.

Sincock made his one and only major league appearance on June 25, 1908. In that game he pitched 4 2/3 innings and allowed 2 runs on 3 hits. He also struck out one batter.

He was also the first British Columbian to make it to the major leagues. It would take another 47 years for another British Columbian to make his major league debut.
