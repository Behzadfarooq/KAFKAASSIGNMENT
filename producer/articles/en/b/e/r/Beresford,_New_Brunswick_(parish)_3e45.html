Beresford is a parish in northeastern New Brunswick, Canada in Gloucester County on the Chaleur Bay.

It's defined in the Territorial Division Act as: West by the County line; east by the Chaleur Bay and south by the easterly prolongation of the south line of Restigouche County.

According to the Canada 2001 Census:

  • Population: 6,622
  • % Change (1996-2001): -3.0
  • Dwellings: 2,568
  • Area (km².): 456.00
  • Density (persons per km².): 14.5
