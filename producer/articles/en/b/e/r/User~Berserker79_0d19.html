Hello and welcome to my user page! :)

I came on Wikipedia around December 2004 looking for info on chemicals for work purposes. My search was rewarded with a well written article, so I was compelled to try to help people who comes here seeking for information in the same fashion, by writing articles as best as I can.

I usually write articles dealing with subjects related to my work, so these are mainly chemical compounds or biology articles. I'm also a computer games enthusiast, so I contribute to a number of computer game articles.
