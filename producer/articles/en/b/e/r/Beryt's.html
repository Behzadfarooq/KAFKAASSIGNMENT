Beryt's

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Beryt's is a Lebanese food franchise located in Geneva, Switzerland. As of time of writing, there are two locations, both in the city center, although only one is mentioned on the official website.

The service is similar to a fast food restaurant in that food is served quickly, sometimes already prepared in the case of popular items and can be eaten on the spot, to go or delivered. Meals eaten on the spot are served with solid glasses, plates and utensils, whereas take-away or delivered meals are served in throwaway recipients and with plastic knives and forks.

The locales feature tables inside and outside, some couches and bar-style seats. There are several TVs per restaurant, usually displaying music or sports channels. Newspapers and local magazines are also a courtesy.

The menu ranges from typical fast food items like hamburgers, french fries and soda to Lebanese salads, falafels and meats. Alcoholic drinks are also served.
