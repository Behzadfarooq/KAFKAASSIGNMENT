Murray Bert Olmstead (born September 4, 1926 in Sceptre, Saskatchewan) was a Canadian professional ice hockey forward who played for the Montreal Canadiens, Chicago Blackhawks and Toronto Maple Leafs in the National Hockey League. He also served as coach of the Oakland Seals. Nicknamed "Dirty Bertie", he was inducted into the Hockey Hall of Fame in 1985.

Bert won the Stanley Cup five times.
