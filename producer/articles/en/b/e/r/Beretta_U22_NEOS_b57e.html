Beretta U22 NEOS

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The U22 Neos is a firearm made in the USA or Italy by Beretta. It takes .22LR ammunition only. The barrel is available in three lengths as indicated in the model name: 4.5", 6", and 7.5". Two 10 round magazines come standard.

Models of the Beretta U22 Neos are:

  • U22 Neos 4.5
  • U22 Neos 4.5 INOX
  • U22 Neos 6.0
  • U22 Neos 6.0 INOX
  • U22 Neos 6.0 DLX
  • U22 Neos 6.0 INOX DLX
  • U22 Neos 7.5 DLX
  • U22 Neos 7.5 INOX DLX.


[edit] Servicing

The firearm is disassembled and reassembled in the same order. When you pull back the slide it locks in. The magazine release is on the right side of the frame which may make it difficult for users who take their finger off the trigger to not drop the magazine.


[edit] Carbine Conversion Kit

In 2004, Beretta developed a conversion kit that allows the user to convert their U22 Neo into a carbine rifle. The kit includes a skeletonized buttstock, a fiberglass-reinforced polymer grip, a 16-1/4" barrel, adjustable rear sight, Tru-Glo fiber optic front sight, and an integrated Weaver-Style Rail. No gunsmithing is required to assemble.
