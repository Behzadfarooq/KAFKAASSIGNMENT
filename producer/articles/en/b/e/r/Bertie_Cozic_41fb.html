Bertrand Cozic (born May 18, 1978 in Quimper) is a French footballer playing in England. A midfielder, Cozic first came to England with Team Bath F.C., the football team of the University of Bath, in 2002. After a successful first season, he moved into the professional game, having spells with Cheltenham, Hereford, Northampton, Kidderminster and Aldershot, before returning to Team Bath in late 2005. In the summer of 2006, he transferred to Exeter City, following manager Paul Tisdale.
