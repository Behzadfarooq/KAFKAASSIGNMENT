Bernard Schuiteman

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Bernard Schuiteman (born October 3, 1973 in Garderen, Gelderland) is a retired football (soccer) defender from the Netherlands, who started his professional career in the 1993-1994 season playing for Bayer Leverkusen. He later served Feyenoord Rotterdam, FC Utrecht, Grazer AK, FSV Mainz 05, SpVgg Unterhaching, Apollon Limassol and Cambuur Leeuwarden.
