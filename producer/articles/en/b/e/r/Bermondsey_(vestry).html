Bermondsey (vestry)

The Vestry of St. Mary Magdalen, Bermondsey was a local government institution created in 1854 and succeeded by the Metropolitan Borough of Bermondsey in 1900.

The population of the Vestry in 1896 was 84,632 and it had adopted the Public Libraries Act 1850 in 1887. For electoral purposes the Vestry was divided into four wards and had 120 members.
