Berlin Township, Erie County, Ohio

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Location of Berlin Twp, Erie Co, Ohio.

Berlin Township is one of the 9 townships that make up Erie County, Ohio. It is part of the Sandusky, Ohio metropolitan statistical area.
