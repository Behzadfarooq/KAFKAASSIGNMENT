[edit] yes, performed

I am, in fact, a part of a world-renowned symphony orchestra. They have performed in the past at Carnegie Hall, New York and Parma, Italy. Soon they will perform in Beijing, China. As part of an NB-wide series of concerts, they performed in the BMHS auditorium. Check out the website: it's www.nbyo-ojnb.ca. I'm serious. Wikiisawesome 11:30, 2 April 2007 (UTC)
