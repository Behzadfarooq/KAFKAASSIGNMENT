Bernardino Tamames Alonso, (born August 15, 1973 in Lleida), is a Spanish basketball player. The 2.06 m and 107 kg center currently plays for Plus Pujol Lleida of the Spanish LEB.

[edit] Player career

  • 1992/95 FC Barcelona
  • 1995/06 Bàsquet Andorra
  • 1996/00 CB Gran Canaria
  • 2000/01 Caja San Fernando
  • 2001/07 Plus Pujol Lleida

[edit] Honours

FC Barcelona

  • Spanish Champions: 1
    • 1995
