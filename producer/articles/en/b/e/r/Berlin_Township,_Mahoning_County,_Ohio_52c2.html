Berlin Township, Mahoning County, Ohio

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Map of Mahoning County's municipalities and townships

Berlin Township or Berlin Center is one of the fourteen townships of Mahoning County, Ohio, located in a rural area and centered at the intersection of U.S. Route 224 and State Route 534 about twenty miles southwest of Youngstown, Ohio. As of the 2000 census, the township had a total population of 2,240.
