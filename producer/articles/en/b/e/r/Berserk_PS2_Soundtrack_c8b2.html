Berserk Millennium Falcon Arc: Chapter of the Holy Demon War original soundtrack (ベルセルク 千年帝国の鷹篇 魔戦記の章~ Original Game Soundtrack) is based on the second video game (for PlayStation 2) of the popular heroic fantasy Berserk anime TV series first broadcasted in Japan from 1997 to 1998.

Since then the TV series was released on DVD and DVD Box and distributed in Asia, America and Europe. Sammy's PlayStation 2 game was released in both Japan and Korea, but this OST was only sold in Japan.

Susumu Hirasawa, who did the anime music, composed Sign as the opening movie theme, and also made a remix of it for the end theme entitled Sign-2. Both tracks were produced by Chaos Union. However, the majority of the game's soundtrack was created by other composers namely, Shinya Chikamori, Hiroshi Watanabe, Yasushi Hasegawa and Tomoyo Nishimoto, all of them are Sound Ams produced artists.

[edit] Miscellanea

  • Both game and soundtrack are only sold in Asia.
  • ULF Records stands for "Underground Liberation Force Records", a Japanese record company publishing both international and domestic artists through various labels such as "Sten Och Flod" (a game soundtrack label) or "Fuji Label" (a drama CD label).
