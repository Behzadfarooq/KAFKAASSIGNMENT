Bernie Bonvoisin

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Bernie Bonvoisin (left) and Norbert Krief (right)

Bernard "Bernie" Bonvoisin (born July 9, 1956) is a French hard rock singer and film director. He is best known for having been the singer of Trust.
