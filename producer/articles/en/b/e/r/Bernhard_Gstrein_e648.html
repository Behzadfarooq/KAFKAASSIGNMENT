Silver 1988 Calgary Alpine combined

Bernhard Gstrein (born September 19, 1965 in Vent). He competed from 1984 to 1996 and won one world cup slalom competition. At the 1988 Olympics in Calgary. He won a silver medal in the men's alpine combined.
