Bert Monroy

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Burt Monroy hosting PixelPerfect on the Revision3 network

Bert Monroy is an American artist best known for his skill in using Adobe Photoshop.

Monroy is considered by some to be one of the pioneers of digital art. Much of his work is photorealistic. He has published several books on the use of Photoshop, and is also an accomplished lecturer. He was a frequent guest on The Screen Savers, where he gave brief Photoshop tutorials. In 2004 he was inducted into the Photoshop Hall of Fame. He also hosts a web TV show, PixelPerfect, on Revision3.
