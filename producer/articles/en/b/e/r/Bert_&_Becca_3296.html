Bert & Becca is an in-prodcution movie is about a retired old-time movie star and singer. His name is Bert and he is played by Ernest Borgnine. He comes to have a very close relationship with his granddaughter, Becca (played by Juliette Goglia) after her mother his hospitalized. She meets all of his other retired famous friends and decides she wants to be famous. She sings a song to get into the school pageant and she gets in. She sings "Go Tell it on the Mountain" with a chorus. Bert comes to give his granddaughter flowers for a marvelous job.

[edit] Filming

Filming will last for five weeks. It is filmed at Devonshire School, California. It is scheduled to be a Christmas movie, due in December, 2007, and on the Hallmark Channel.
