Berlin, Berlin was a television series from ARD-Werbung. It aired in Germany from 2002 to 2005 Tuesdays through Fridays at 6:50PM on the German TV network Das Erste.

[edit] Plot

The series tells the story of Lolle (played by Felicitas Woll) who, after school, follows her boyfriend, Tom, from Malente to Berlin. Once there, she finds that Tom has cheated on her with another girl. Rather than move back home, Lolle decides to stay in Berlin with her cousin, Sven (played by Jan Sosniok), his best friend, Hart, and Rosalie.


[edit] Awards

In 2004 the series won an Emmy in the category "Best Comedy" for Episode47.
