Bernardo Rodríguez Arias nicknamed Berni Rodríguez (born June 7, 1980, 1.97m (6 ft, 05in)) is a Spanish professional basketball player for Unicaja Málaga.

He played with the Spanish team in the 2006 FIBA World Championship.
