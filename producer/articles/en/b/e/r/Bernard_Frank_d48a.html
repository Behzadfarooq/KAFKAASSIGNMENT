Bernard Frank

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

See also Bernard Frank (journalist) (born 1929), French journalist, or Bernhard Frank, WW2 SS Officer

Bernard Frank (February 28, 1927 - October 15, 1996) was a French oriental scholar (especially Japanese literature) and writer. Japanologist, orientalist.

He was the second son of Jacques Frank and Suzanne Planchenault.
