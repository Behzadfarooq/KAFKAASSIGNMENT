Bernhard Gothic is a geometric sans serif typeface designed by Lucian Bernhard in 1930 for the American Type Founders (ATF). Bernhard Gothic is more organic and less regular than other geometric sans-serif typefaces, including Futura, Kabel, and Century Gothic showing influence of Bernhard's earlier more experessionistic faces.
