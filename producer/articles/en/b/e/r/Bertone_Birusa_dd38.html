Bertone Birusa

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Bertone Birusa
Manufacturer Bertone, BMW
Class Concept car
Body style Coupe
Engine V8
Transmission 6 speed manual
Length 440 cm (173 in)
Width 190 cm (75 in)
Related BMW Z8

The Bertone Birusa is a design study based on the BMW Z8. The Birusa features a 5.0-liter V8 with 400 bhp, and also has 11 Bose speakers.
