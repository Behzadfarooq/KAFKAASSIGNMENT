Bermudez Biscuit Company Limited

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Bermudez Biscuit Company Ltd. is a Trinidadian company located in mount Lambert Trinidad and Tobago. The products are the famous round white, wholewheat, multigrain crix including the crix biscuit-tins and dixie crackers, domino-chocolate, vanilla, strawberry sweet biscuits and others.

Bermudez is a "key food processor" in the eastern Caribbean according to the United States Department of Agriculture[1] The company also operates in Barbados and Jamaica.[2]
