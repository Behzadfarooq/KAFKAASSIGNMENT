[edit] What is it with Penguins?

All of a sudden I know as much about penguins as your most experienced ornithologist. I don’t remember if there is a cute or amazing creature that is all the rage every year; I don’t remember what last year’s was. But I do remember koalas and everything else Australian were the fad back in the…when was it? Then dinosaurs were cool for a while – for a pretty long while. Unicorns had ther five minutes. But now it’s the penguins turn.

I don’t particularly care much for cute critters; but these birds on March of the Penguins were pretty tough. If only I had such determination to go to the gym, or stop binge drinking, or something that they say is “good for you.‿ Once in a while I tune in to the Monterey Bay Aquarium webcams to check out the critters while goofing off at work - and they have the South African type there. http://www.mbayaq.org/efc/efc_splash/../../media/strm/mba_peng.asx And on another one of those webcams (I think it was the kelp forest) you could actually hear a live presentation on the penguin exhibit. I learned a lot. For example: Did you know there are 14 different varieties of penguin? And that the Emperor is about 4’ tall? And that they eat five times a day about a third of their body weight in fish? Wow! Yeah? Well, what good does this information do me? I can’t use this information at work to get me a promotion, or at least make me more productive. I’m never going to have a pet penguin. I called all the pet stores already; they don’t even know where to begin looking for one. So it’s information that’s just taking up space in my brain. But I’m probably going to check out Happy Feet eventually. Damn penguins, they’re everywhere!
