Berwick, Iowa

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Berwick is a hamlet in Iowa, United States. The population was 370 at the 2000 census.


[edit] Demographics

As of the census of 2000, there were 370 people, 177 households, and 105 families residing in the city.

The racial makeup of the city was 98.65% White, 0.27% Asian, and 1.08% from two or more races.


The average household size was 2.09 and the average family size was 2.71.

In the city the population was spread out with 20.8% under the age of 18, 5.7% from 18 to 24, 30.0% from 25 to 44, 27.0% from 45 to 64, and 16.5% who were 65 years of age or older. The median age was 40 years. For every 100 females there were 84.1 males. For every 100 females age 18 and over, there were 76.9 males.
