Berlin state election, 2006

The Berlin state election, 2006, was held on September 17, 2006, to elect members to the Abgeordnetenhaus (House of Deputies) of Berlin. The Social Democratic Party under Wowereit and the Left Party will go together for a next coalition in Berlin.
