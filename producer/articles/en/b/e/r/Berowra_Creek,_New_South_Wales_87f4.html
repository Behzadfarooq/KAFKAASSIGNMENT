Berowra Creek, New South Wales

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Berowra Creek is a remote northern suburb of Sydney, in the state of New South Wales, Australia[1]. It is located near and around the 30 km long Berowra Creek watercourse, is spread across several different waterfront locations, and borders Hornsby Shire. Access to Berowra Creek is via boat only.
