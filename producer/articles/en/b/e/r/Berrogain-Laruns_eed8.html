Berrogain-Laruns

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Berrogain-Laruns (Basque: Berrogaine-Lahüntze) is a small village and commune in the Pyrénées-Atlantiques département of southwestern France.

It is located in the former province of Soule.
