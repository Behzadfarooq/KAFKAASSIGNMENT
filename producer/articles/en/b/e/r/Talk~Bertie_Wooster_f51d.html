Talk:Bertie Wooster

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Yes, Bertie's family is filthy rich. However, Bertie does not have to rely on an allowance from his Aunt Agatha. Rather, as stated in several places in the stories, Aunt Agatha's hold is only her innate dominance and fierceness. Bertie states categorically that she does not hold purse strings.
