Berry Linux is a LiveCD Linux distribution based on Fedora Core. The current version is 0.78 (August 29, 2006) and is in English and Japanese. The medium version (v0.64) includes OpenOffice.org version 2.0 and is about 480 MB, while the mini version (v0.54) is about 162.5 MB.
