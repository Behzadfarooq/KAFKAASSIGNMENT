Bergens Tidende is Norway's fourth largest newspaper and largest regional newspaper with a circulation of about 90,000 copies (2002 numbers). Founded in 1868, it is a Norwegian language newspaper published daily in Bergen. It reaches approximately 260,000 readers every day (2005), mainly in the counties of Hordaland and Sogn og Fjordane.
