Bernard Riley

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Bernard Riley (born January 12, 1981) is an american football lineman.

[edit] Pro Career

Riley played for the National Football League Tampa Bay Buccaneers and Arena Football League for Los Angeles Avengers and currently Columbus Destroyers.

[edit] College Career

Riley played college football at the University of Southern California.

[edit] High School Career

Riley prepped and was a High School All-American at Los Alamitos High School.
