Berrocal de Salvatierra

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Berrocal de Salvatierra is a village and municipality in the province of Salamanca, western Spain, part of the autonomous community of Castile-Leon. It is 45 kilometres from the provincial capital city of Salamanca and has a population of 125 people.

It lies 900 metres above sea level.

The post code is 37795.
