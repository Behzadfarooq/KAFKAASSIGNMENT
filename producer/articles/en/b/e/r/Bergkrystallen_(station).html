Bergkrystallen is the end station on Lambertseterbanen, after Munkelia, of the Oslo T-bane system. It is located in the Nordstrand borough. Bergkrystallen is a road just north of the station. The area is mainly residential. Originally it was planned to extend Lambertseterbanen to Mortensrud, but Østensjøbanen was extended instead.
