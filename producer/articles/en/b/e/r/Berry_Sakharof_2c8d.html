Berry Sakharof (Hebrew: ברי סחרוף) (born July 7, 1957) is an influential Israeli rock guitarist/singer. He first came to public attention as a founding member of Minimal Compact - the only Israeli rock band to have achieved any significant success outside Israel.

In 2002, Sakharof collaborated with Erez Aizen and Amit Duvedevani of Infected Mushroom to create the Birthday EP.
