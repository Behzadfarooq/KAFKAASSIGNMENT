Bernard C. Schoenfeld (August 17, 1907 - April 25, 1980) was a film screenwriter. He wrote for over twenty films and television series including Phantom Lady (1944), The Dark Corner (screenplay based on the Cornell Woolrich novel, 1946), Caged (1950), Macao (1952), and "The Twilight Zone" episode From Agnes - with Love.
