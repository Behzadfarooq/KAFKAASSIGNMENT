Bering Strait is the self-titled debut album from the country music band Bering Strait.

Early versions of the album did not have "When Going Home," but instead had two additional tracks. The album originally opened with "Magic" and also had another multilingual song called, "We Are The Same." In the end, the two tracks were removed in favor of "When Going Home."

[edit] Music videos

Music videos were produced for "When Going Home," "Jagged Edge of a Broken Heart" and the 2002 Grammy Award Nominee "Bearing Straight." Ultimately, "Bearing Straight" was aired on national television as a country music video, whereas "Jagged Edge" was only distributed as a digital file on the CD album itself. "When Going Home" was never publicly distributed.
