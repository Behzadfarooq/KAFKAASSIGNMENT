Beriln is an unincorported community located in Roger Mills County, Oklahoma. The Beriln Post Office opened September 2, 1896, and closed May 5, 1967. The 1905 Oklahoma Territorial Census gave the population for the town as sixty.

The Goodwin-Baker Archaeological Site is located nearby.
