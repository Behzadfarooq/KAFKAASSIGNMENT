Berg is a station on Sognsvannsbanen (line 3) of the Oslo T-bane system. It lies between Ullevål stadion and Tåsen and is the first station after the northern circle line leaves Sognsvannsbanen. The station is located 6.1 km from Stortinget. Berg is among the original stations on the line, and was opened October 10, 1934.[1]

The area around the station is mainly residential. A high school, Berg videregående skole, is located only 100 m from the station.
