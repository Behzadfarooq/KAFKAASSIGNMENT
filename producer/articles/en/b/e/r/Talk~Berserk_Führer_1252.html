[edit] Berserk Fury vs Liger Zero

I'm sure many people have noticed, but from what I saw if Vega did not eject the Berserk Fury would have blasted the Liger Zero away. When the charged particle beam was fired and Zero's Laser Claw was in Berserk Fury's neck, the Fury slowly came lower as he clawed Zero's leg with his arms. At that moment Vega came to and ejected, shutting down the functions of the Berserk Fury. —The preceding unsigned comment was added by Desert Spada (talk • contribs) 02:34, 7 December 2006 (UTC).

  • Yes, the Berserk Fury started to fight back, but the Liger Zero was holding fast. The beam would probably have disgorged eventually, and no doubt the recoil of the beam pushing down on its legs would've put it under terrible stress. The Berserk Fury took a shitload of damage during the fight but the Liger Zero didn't take any at all, it still would have been able to win. Also, Bit's remarks of "hang in there, Liger", and "almost got him!" seem to imply that the Berserk Fury's fortitude was starting to wane. Either way, it doesn't change the fact that the Berserk Fury was defeated by the Liger Zero, so why quibble over it? K00bine 10:19, 8 December 2006 (UTC)
  • I was just stating what I saw. Bit said "hang in there, Liger", and "almost got him!" to encourage Liger because he was starting to be pushed back. From the beginning of the fight between the Liger Zero and the Berserk Fury Liger was winning. After Vega was knocked out the Fury took total control and started to get the upper hand. Of course, this was at the end of the battle and Vega soon woke up. If he had not ejected the Fury would have won. Oh well, he is still one of the best zoids out there! Desert Spada 04:44, 9 December 2006 (UTC)
  • No. If the Berserk Fury had fired its CPC at point blank like that it would have killed itself AND the Liger. And the BF was clearly on the recieving end of the beating from the Liger; the BF took a ton of damage, the Liger didn't take any. No matter what way you slice it, the BF would've lost. K00bine 09:31, 3 February 2007 (UTC)
