Beryozovsky (Russian: Берёзовский) is a town in Sverdlovsk Oblast, Russia, located on the Beryozovka River (Pyshma's tributary), 13 km north-east of Yekaterinburg. Population: 46,744 (2002 Census); 47,944 (1989 Census).

It was founded in 1752 as a gold-mining settlement. Town status since 1938.
