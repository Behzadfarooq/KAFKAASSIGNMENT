Bernd Krauss (born May 8, 1957 in Dortmund) is a retired German-Austrian football player and later a football manager.

He played for clubs such as Borussia Dortmund (Germany), Rapid Wien and Borussia Mönchengladbach (Germany).

He was a member of the Austrian national team, for which he played 22 matches.

He was a participant at the 1982 FIFA World Cup.

As a football coach he coached for example Borussia Mönchengladbach, Real Sociedad San Sebastian (Spain), RCD Mallorca (Spain) and Pegah Gilan F.C. (Iran). He now coaches CD Tenerife in Spain.
