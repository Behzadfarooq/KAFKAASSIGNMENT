Welcome to the Costa Rica WikiProject. We are a group dedicated to improving Wikipedia's coverage of articles related to Costa Rica.

Goals
  • The goal of this WikiProject is to provide complete coverage on subjects related to Costa Rica, by expanding articles on people, places, and things related to Costa Rica.
Scope
  • This project proposes to work with all pages related to the state of Costa Rica, its history, geography, and people. We will strive to ensure that each of these articles also is placed in the Category:Costa Rica.

    [edit] New articles

    Please feel free to list your new Costa Rica-related articles here (newer articles at the top, please). Any new articles that have an interesting or unusual fact in them should be suggested for the Did you know? box the Main Page. Please also make sure that each new article created is put into one of the categories for Costa Rica, so that the articles will appear there. Also, if possible, add each new article to the page Wikipedia:WikiProject Costa Rica/Articles (still under construction) so that they can be viewed for recent changes.

    [edit] Stubs

    {{CostaRica-stub}} - Costa Rica stub notice; to be added to the bottom of all relevant article pages.

    NOTE: Stubs are managed by the Stub-sorting WikiProject. Before creating a new stub, you should propose it at Wikipedia:WikiProject Stub sorting/Proposals. See Wikipedia:Stub for general guidelines on proposing stub templates and categories. DO NOT simply create new stub templates, as these will be deleted.

    [edit] Other templates

    {{WikiProject Costa Rica}} should be placed on the talk pages of relevant articles to put them under the project's scope.


    {{WikiProject Costa Rica}} looks like this:

    		 This page is within the scope of WikiProject Costa Rica, an attempt to build a comprehensive and detailed guide to articles on Costa Rica on Wikipedia. If you would like to participate, you can edit the article attached to this page, or visit the project page, where you can join the project and/or contribute to the discussion.
