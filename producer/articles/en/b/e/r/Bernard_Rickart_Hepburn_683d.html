Bernard Rickart Hepburn (May 27, 1876 â€“ February 23, 1939) was born in Picton, Ontario. A businessman, he became a Member of the Canadian House of Commons in 1911 as a Conservative representative for Prince Edward, before representing the Unionist for the same district upon the Conservative Party's merger with segments of the Liberal Party of Canada. He remained a member of the Commons until 1921.
