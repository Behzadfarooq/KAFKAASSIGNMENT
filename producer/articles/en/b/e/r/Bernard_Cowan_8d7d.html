Bernard Cowan

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Bernard Cowan (1922–July 17, 1990) was a Canadian actor, announcer and writer. He was born in Toronto, Ontario.

Cowan worked as the announcer on many CBC television series such as Front Page Challenge and Wayne and Shuster. He also worked on such animated series as Spider-Man, The Incredible Hulk, Rocket Robin Hood and the Christmas special Rudolph the Red-Nosed Reindeer.
