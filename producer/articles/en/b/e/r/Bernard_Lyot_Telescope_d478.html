Bernard Lyot Telescope

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

You have new messages (last change).

The Bernard Lyot Telescope is a 2 m Cassegrain telescope operating in the visible domain. It is located at 2878 m elevation on the Pic du Midi in the French Pyrenees.
