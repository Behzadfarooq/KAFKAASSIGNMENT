Berkeley Vale is a suburb located on the Central Coast of New South Wales, Australia, as part of the Wyong Shire local government area It is located South of Tuggerah Lake, West of Tumbi Umbi and East of Chittaway Point. Wyong Road is the main road that runs through Berkeley Vale, although Lakedge Ave, which runs along Tuggerah Lake is a popular road to take. Berkeley Vale is contains two public schools Berkeley Vale Campus, which is apart of Tuggerah Lakes Secondry Collage and Berkeley Vale Primary School. There is also a private hospital on the lake side of Wyong Road.
