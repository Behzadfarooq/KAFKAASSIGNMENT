Bernard Casoni, (born 4 September 1961, in Cannes, France) is a football manager, currently managing SC Bastia.In his playing career he played for Olympique Marseille and France at Euro 1992.

[edit] Titles

  • French championship in 1991 and 1992 with Olympique Marseille
  • UEFA Champions League runner-up in 1991 with Olympique Marseille
  • UEFA Champions League in 1993 with Olympique Marseille
  • Ligue 2 in 1995 with Olympique Marseille
