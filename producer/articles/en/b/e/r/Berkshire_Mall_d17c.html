Berkshire Mall

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

You have new messages (last change).

The Berkshire Mall is located in Lanesborough, Massachusetts, located outside of Pittsfield. The mall was built in 1988, and can be reached from both Route 8 and U.S. Route 7. There have been many changes in the mall's anchors since its opening. The mall is owned by The Pyramid Companies.

[edit] Current anchors

  • Best Buy (30,000 square feet. opened 2002)
  • JCPenney (51,000 sq. ft.)
  • Linens N Things (27,000 square feet, opened 2002)
  • Macy's (110,000 square feet, converted from Filene's 2006)
  • Old Navy (opened 1999)
  • Regal Cinema
  • Sears (122,422 sq. ft)
  • Target (126,000 square feet, opened 2006)

[edit] Former anchors

  • Ames: Closed 2002, demolished in 2005, Target on site 2006
  • Borders Books and Music[citation needed]
  • Filene's: Converted to Macy's in 2006
  • Hills: 80,000 square feet, converted to Ames in 1998
  • Service Merchandise: Closed in 1996. Now Best Buy and Linens N Things.
  • Stiegers: Closed in 1994 and became Filene's
  • Woolworth: Later Borders Books and Music.[citation needed]
