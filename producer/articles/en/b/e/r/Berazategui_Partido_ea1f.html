Berazategui is a partido in Buenos Aires Province, Argentina. With a surface of 188 km² and a population of 287,913 (2001 census [INDEC]), its located at the southeast of the Gran Buenos Aires urban conglomerate, and its capital is Berazategui city.

It was part of the Quilmes Partido until 1960.

The majority of the population are concentrated in the city of Berazategui which has an important commercial district with a pedestrianised centre.

Berazategui is also home to Asociación Deportiva Berazategui, a football club that play in the regionalised 5th division of Argentine football.
