Bernstein is a commune in Burgenland in the district Oberwart in Austria with 2.441 inhabitants. The Hungarian name of the commune is Borostyánkő.

[edit] Geography

Parts of the commune are Dreihütten, Redlschlag, Rettenbach and Stuben.

[edit] Politics

Mayor is Robert Marth of the SPÖ. In the representation (23 mandates) the SPÖ has 14 mandates and the ÖVP 9.
