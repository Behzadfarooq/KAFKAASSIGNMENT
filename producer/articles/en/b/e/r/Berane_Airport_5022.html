Berane Airport (Montenegrin: Aerodrom Berane) (IATA: IVG, ICAO: LYBR) is an airport located in the Berane Municiaplity in Montenegro also known as Dolac Airport. The airport is used for General Aviation and can't handle larger aircraft.

Berane Airport is in awaiting reconstruction for expansion and possible status as an international airport. The reconstruction was given 15 million euros.

[edit] History

On 21 July 1961, the Belgrade-Ivangrad line was opened by JAT Yugoslav Airlines. know it sucks

[edit] Name

The airport was originally known as Ivangrad Airport until Ivangrad was renamed back to Berane, causing the airport to also be renamed Berane Airport.
