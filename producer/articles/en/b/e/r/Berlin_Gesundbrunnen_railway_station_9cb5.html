Berlin Gesundbrunnen is a railway station in Berlin, Germany. It is situated in the northern Berlin borough of the same name and is an interconnection point between the Ringbahn and the Nord-Süd-Bahn lines of the Berlin S-Bahn, as well as a regional and long distance station on the Deutsche Bahn network. The station fell into disuse after 1961 and was closed between 1980 and 1984. After German reunification, it was extensively modernised.

U-Bahn station Gesundbrunnen

The station also has a Berlin U-Bahn station located on the U8.
