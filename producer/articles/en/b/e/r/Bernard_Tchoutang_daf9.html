Bernard Tchoutang

Bernard Tchoutang (born 2 September 1976) is a Cameroonian football player who, as of 2006, was playing for Hapoel Petah Tikva FC.

He was part of the victorious Cameroonian 2000 African Nations Cup team. He also took part at the 2001 FIFA Confederations Cup.

[edit] Clubs

  • 1994 : Tonnerre Yaounde
  • 1994-1997 : Vanspor
  • 1997-2002 : Roda JC
  • 2002-2003 : Metalurh Donetsk
  • 2003-2004 : Viborg FF
  • 2004-2005 : Metalurh Donetsk
  • 2005-2006 : Pahang FA
  • 2006-present : Hapoel Petah Tikva
