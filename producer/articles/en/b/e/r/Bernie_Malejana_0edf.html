Bernie Malejana - Currently, he dubs characters in Yakitate!! Japan. He supplied the voice of Sugoroku Mutou, Ryo BakuraYami Bakura, and the second voice of Maximillion Pagasus of Yu-Gi-Oh on ABS-CBN and Rock Lee,Kabuto,Kankuro, and Jiraiya of Naruto. He also did the voice of Kyousuke Jin of Crush Gear Turboon ABS-CBN. He did the voice behind Yajirobi, Piccolo, and Trunks of Dragon Ball Z/Dragon Ball GT and he is the 2nd replacement dubber for Doraemon.
