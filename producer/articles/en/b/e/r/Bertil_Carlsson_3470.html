Bronze 1927 Cortina d'Ampezzo Individual large hill

Bertil Carlsson is a Swedish ski jumper who competed in the 1920's. He won a bronze medal in the individual large hill at the 1927 FIS Nordic World Ski Championships in Cortina d'Ampezzo.
