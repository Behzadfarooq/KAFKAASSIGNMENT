Bernard Panafieu

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Bernard Cardinal Panafieu (born January 26, 1931 in Châtellerault; pronunciation (help·info)) is a French Roman Catholic bishop.

Styles of
Bernard Cardinal Panafieu
Reference style His Eminence
Spoken style Your Eminence
Informal style Cardinal
See Marseilles

He is currently Archbishop of Marseilles and a Cardinal Priest of the Catholic Church. He was one of the cardinal electors who participated in the 2005 papal conclave that selected Pope Benedict XVI.
