Bertram Hector 'Bernard' MacDonald (May 25, 1902 - Dec 28, 1965) was a British long-distance runner.

He competed for Great Britain in the 1924 Summer Olympics held in Paris, France in the 3000 metre team where he won the silver medal with his team mates Harry Johnston and George Webber.
