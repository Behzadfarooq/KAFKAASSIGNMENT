Berwick

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Berwick-upon-Tweed is a border town, now in England, formerly in Scotland. North Berwick in Scotland is named in contrast to it.

Other places called Berwick are:

  • Berwick, Victoria, Australia
  • Berwick, Nova Scotia, Canada
  • Berwick, Sussex, England
  • Berwick, Louisiana, United States
  • Berwick, Maine, United States
  • Berwick, Pennsylvania, United States

[edit] People

  • Dennison Berwick, Author and nomad
  • James FitzJames, 1st Duke of Berwick
  • Christopher Berwick, Count of Mystki-Rzym
  • Berwick family, heirs to the Polish szlachta family Mystkowski
