Bernd Thijs

Bernd Thijs (born June 28, 1978 in Hasselt, Belgium) is a Belgian football midfielder who, as of 2005, is playing for Borussia Mönchengladbach. His former clubs include Standard Liège, Racing Genk and Trabzonspor. Thijs played 5 times with Belgium and was in the team for the 2002 World Cup.
