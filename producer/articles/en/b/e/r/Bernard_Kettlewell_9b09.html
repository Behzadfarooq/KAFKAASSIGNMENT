Bernard Kettlewell

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Dr Henry Bernard Davis Kettlewell (24 February 1907 - 1979) was a British lepidopterist and medical doctor.

He was educated at Charterhouse School.

Kettlewell is notable for his experiments on the peppered moth.

He died from a drug overdose in 1979. Though recorded as accidental, it has been suggested that it was a suicide.[citation needed]
