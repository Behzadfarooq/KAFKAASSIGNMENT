Berengaria was the name of several medieval women, notably

  • Berengaria of Navarre (c.1165–1230), queen consort to Richard I of England
  • Berengária of Portugal (c. 1195–1221), daughter of King Sancho I of Portugal, queen consort to Valdemar II of Denmark

Also:

  • RMS Berengaria, an ocean liner
  • Berengaria VII, a world on which Mr. Spock says he has seen dragons (Star Trek: The Original Series, "This Side of Paradise")
