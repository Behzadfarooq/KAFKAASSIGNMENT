Berjaya Air is an airline based in Shah Alam, Selangor, Malaysia. It operates domestic scheduled and charter services and regional flights. It's main base is Sultan Abdul Aziz Shah Airport, Subang, Malaysia[1].

[edit] History

The airline was established and started operations in 1989. It is owned by the Berjaya Group (through Berjaya Land) and was formerly known as Pacific Air Charter[1].
