Bernhard "Berni" Klodt (born 26 October 1926 in Gelsenkirchen-Bismarck; died 23 May 1996 in Garmisch-Partenkirchen) was a German football (soccer) player.

Between 1950 and 1959, he played 19 times and scored 3 goals for the Germany national football team. He was part of the 1954 FIFA World Cup-winning squad, and also played in the 1958 FIFA World Cup. For his club career, he played for Schalke 04.
