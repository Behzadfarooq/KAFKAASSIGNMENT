Berwick High is a high school in Berwick, Louisiana the school is academically ranked 17th out of 165 public high schools in Louisiana. The school offers many athletic programs. The tennis team has recently had state level victories. The quiz bowl team is a two time defending state champion. Berwick High also has a football team.
