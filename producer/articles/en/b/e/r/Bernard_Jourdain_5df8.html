Bernard Jourdain (born August 16, 1950 in Brussels) is a Belgian born Mexican former race car driver. He started two Indianapolis 500's (1989 Indianapolis 500, 1991 Indianapolis 500). He was named the Indy 500 Co-Rookie of the Year in 1989 with racer Scott Pruett. Jourdain is the uncle of racer Michel Jourdain, Jr..
