Bertalan Székely

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Bertalan Szekely (May 8, 1835 - August 21, 1910) as a Hungarian Romantic painter of historical themes. "The Discovery of Louis Il's Dead Body", "Women of Eger", "Mohács", "Ladislas V" are among the most important of his history paintings. Szekely is also known for his many murals.
