Bernardo Daniel Romeo (born 10 September 1977 in Tandil) is an Argentine football striker, currently plays for Osasuna.

Romeo has played for a number of clubs including Estudiantes de La Plata and San Lorenzo in Argentina, Hamburg in Germany, Real Mallorca and CA Osasuna in Spain.

Bernardo Romeo has been capped 4 times by Argentina. He wore the number 19 shirt, early in his career.

[edit] Honors and awards

  • Topscorer Primera Division Argentina Clausura 2001
  • FIFA World Youth Championship: 1997
