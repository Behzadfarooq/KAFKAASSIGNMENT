Bernard Allison

Bernard Allison was born on 25 November, 1965 in Chicago, Illinois. He is a blues guitarist based out of Minneapolis, Minnesota. His father, Luther Allison was a Chicago blues legend in both the United States and abroad. Bernard is the youngest of nine children and had many different musical influences while growing up.

  • 1995 -- Funkifino (Ruf Records)
  • 1997 -- Born With The Blues (Ruf Records)

  • 2000 -- Across The Water (Tone-Cool)
  • 2002 -- Hang On (Ruf Records)
  • 2003 -- Kentucky Fried Blues (live) (Ruf Records)
  • 2005 -- Higher Power (Ruf Records)
  • 2006 -- Energized. Live in Europe, CD + DVD (Ruf Records)
