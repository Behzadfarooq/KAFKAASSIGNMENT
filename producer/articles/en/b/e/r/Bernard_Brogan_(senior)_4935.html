Bernard 'Bernie' Brogan is a former Dublin Gaelic football player from Ireland.

[edit] Playing career

Bernie has received an allstar for his performances in 1979. He was on the all-Ireland senior football winning side for Dublin in 1976 against rivals Kerry by a scoreline of 3-8 to 0-10. He then went on to win his next all-Ireland title the following year in a final win over Armagh by a scoreline of 5-12 to 3-6. He also has two national football league division one medals for Dublin which he received in 1978 against Mayo and in 1976 in the final in which he appeared as a substitute against Derry. Bernie is the father of current Dublin and St Oliver Plunketts footballers Bernard and Alan Brogan.
