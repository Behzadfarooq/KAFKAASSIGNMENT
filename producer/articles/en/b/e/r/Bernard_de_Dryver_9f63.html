Bernard de Dryver (born September 19, 1952) is a racing driver from Belgium. He entered 2 Formula One grands prix, in Belgium in 1977 and 1978. In 1977 he entered a non-works March, but failed to make the grid. The following year he entered a privately run Ensign but did not qualify for official practice.

He raced a full season in the Aurora UK Formula One Championship in 1979, driving a Fittipaldi, scoring a number of podiums and finishing 4th in the championship.

He is still involved in motor sport, most recently in GT Racing.
