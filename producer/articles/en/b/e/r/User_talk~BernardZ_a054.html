[edit] Tiger I

Hi there. I was just wondering if you had a source to back up the assertion that the main role of the Tiger was as a tank killer. Based on my knowledge this is a slighlty misleading impression - Tigers were heavy tanks, designed to lead breakthroughs and conduct crucial defences, not simply to find and destroy enemy tanks (which was the role of AT guns and tank destroyers). Even if we change that sentence, your points about kill ratios are still basically valid. The Land 11:04, 31 December 2006 (UTC)

The tigers were orginaly designed to be as you say as an offensive weapon but by the time they came onto the field, the military situation had changed dramatically so other then a few times, their main use was defensively where they proved very effective against enemy tanks.

My source was listed on the page which is p190 of Sledgehammers: Strengths and Flaws of Tiger Tank Battalions in World War II (Paperback) by Christopher W. Wilbeck, Otto Carius (Preface), Viktor Iskrov (Epilogue), Ray Holt
