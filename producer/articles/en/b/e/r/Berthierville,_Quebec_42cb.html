Berthierville, Quebec

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Berthierville is a Canadian town located between Montreal and Trois-Rivières on the north shore of the St Lawrence River in the province of Quebec. Population (2001 census) 3,939. Berthierville is the seat of D'Autray Regional County Municipality, and is served by Quebec Autoroute 40, and is the junction of Provincial Highways 138 and 158.

Berthierville is the birthplace of Formula One race car driver Gilles Villeneuve.
