Berlin Brandenburgish dialects

Berlin-Brandenburgish is a group of dialects spoken in Brandenburg (Brandenburgisch) and Berlin (Berlinerisch). The original East Low German dialects of Berlin have been strongly influenced by East Central German dialects, so that Berlinerische today is often classified as East Middle German.
