The Universität der Künste Berlin, UdK (Berlin University of the Arts) is a German university founded in 1975 with the merger of the Berlin State School of Fine Arts and the Berlin State School of Music and the Performing Arts. Its root institutions date back to the founding of the Akademie der Künste in 1696. The university has four colleges specializing in fine arts, architecture, media and design, music, and the performing arts with a student body of near 4,500. A Hochschule until November 2001, the university is one of the largest institutions of music and arts in the world.
