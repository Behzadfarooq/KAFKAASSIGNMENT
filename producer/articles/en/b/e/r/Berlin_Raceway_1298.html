Berlin Raceway

Berlin Raceway is a 0.438 mile long paved oval motor racetrack in Marne, Michigan, near Grand Rapids. It opened in 1950.

[edit] National Events

It is a stop on the ARCA tour. The track was also a stop on the American Speed Association tour before the series folded.
