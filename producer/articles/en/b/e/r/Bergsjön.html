Bergsjön Centrum

Bergsjön is a district in the north-eastern part of Gothenburg in western Sweden.

[edit] Public transit

This suburb is served by two tram lines (7 and 11), which partially run through tunnels. The tramway originally was a part of the premetro-metro project (hence the tunnels). However, as the costs for this conversion grew, the project was abandoned. Bergsjöbanan is still premetro-based, though. The stops located in Bergsjön are Komettorget (Comet Sq; terminus), Rymdtorget (Space Sq), Teleskopgatan (Telescope St) and Galileis Gata (Galileis St).
