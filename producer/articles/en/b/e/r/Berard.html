Berard (Bérard) is a surname, and may refer to:

  • Al Berard, Cajun musician
  • André Bérard, Canadian businessman and President, CEO, and Chairman of the National Bank of Canada
  • Berard of Carbio, Franciscan monk
  • Bryan Berard, American ice hockey player
  • Joseph Frédéric Bérard, French physician and philosopher
  • Léon Bérard, French politician and lawyer
  • Roxane Berard, Belgian actress
  • Ryan Berard, Canadian junior ice hockey player
  • Victor Bérard (1864-1931), French classical scholar
  • St. Berard of Carbio
