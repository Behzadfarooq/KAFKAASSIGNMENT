The Czech Republic has its own version of the Deal or No Deal format for TV Prima, called Ber nebo neber. Pavel Zuna is the host and the set is dead-on to the US set. Players can win from as little as 1 Kč (Czech koruna) (about 4¢ US, €0.03, 2p, and ¥5) to as big as 5,000,000 Kč (about US$232,000, €177,000, £119,000, and ¥27,000,000).
