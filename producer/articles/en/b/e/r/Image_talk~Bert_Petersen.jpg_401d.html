[edit] Fair Use Image Dispute

The image used in the bert petersen article has been placed under dispute. Be advised that there are currently no other alternative sources for an image of this subject, until such time as I receive a response from the subject himself providing a free use image.

Such being the case, the following criteria are met under the Wikipedia Fair Use policy:

  1. No free equivalent is available or could be created that would be of acceptable quality
  2. The material is not used in a manner that would likely replace the original market role of the original.
  3. The image was previously published
  4. The image contributes significantly to the article (identifies the subject of an article)
  5. This image cannot be replaced by a different one, while still having the same effect.
