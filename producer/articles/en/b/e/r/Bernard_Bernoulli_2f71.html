Bernard Bernoulli is a fictional character in the Maniac Mansion series of adventure games. He is a stereotypical nerd and has the usual nerd look of glasses, a dress shirt, gray slacks pulled way over his navel, and a bow tie.

Bernard is one of several playable characters in Maniac Mansion and appears as the main protagonist in Day of the Tentacle.

[edit] Maniac Mansion

Main article: Maniac Mansion

Bernard made his first appearance in Maniac Mansion where he was one of the 7 playable characters within the game. He has the ability to dismantle complex electronics. However, he has a fear for Green Tentacle until someone makes friends with him.

[edit] Day of The Tentacle

Main article: Day of the Tentacle

Out of the 6 playable characters in Maniac Mansion, Bernard is the only character who made it into the next game. Only this time he plays a starring role. He and the 2 other playable characters in the game (Hoagie and Laverne) must travel back in time to stop Purple Tentacle from achieving World domination.

[edit] Other appearancess

Bernard appears three times in Sam & Max Hit the Road behind the counter of Snucky's. Each of the three are subtly different in appearance.
