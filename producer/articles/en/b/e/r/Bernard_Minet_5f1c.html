Bernard Minet (born December 28, 1953 as Bernard Wantier) is a French singer and actor.

He started his career in the Pas-de-Calais in 1969 and arrived in Paris in 1970, where he was part of several bands during his studies: "Pop", the "Baloches" and the "Golf Drouot." In 1974 he won first prize in percussion at the Conservatoire national de Paris. In 1983 he started a collaboration with Dorothée and joined the band les Musclés as drummer. He also pursued a career as a solo artist.

He also played in 482 episodes of the Salut les Musclés and la Croisière Foll'Amour sitcoms.
