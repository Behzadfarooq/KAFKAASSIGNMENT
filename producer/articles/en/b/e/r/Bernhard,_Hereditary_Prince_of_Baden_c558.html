Bernhard, Hereditary Prince of Baden

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Bernhard, Hereditary Prince of Baden (Bernhard Max Friedrich August Gustav Ludwig Kraft, born 27 May 1970 in Schloss Salem, Salem, Baden-Württemberg, Germany. He is the eldest son of Maximilian, Margrave of Baden and Archduchess Valerie of Austria.

[edit] Marriage and children

He married with Stephanie Kaul (b. 27 June 1966) in a civil ceremony on 22 June 2001 and a religious ceremony the following day. The couple have three children:

  • His Grand Ducal Highness Prince Leopold Bernhard Max Michael Ernst-August Friedrich Guillaume of Baden (b. 18 May 2002 at Ravensburg)
  • His Grand Ducal Highness Prince Friedrich Bernhard Leopold Christian Berthold Christoph of Baden (b. 9 March 2004 at Ravensburg)
  • His Grand Ducal Highness Prince Karl-Wilhelm Bernhard Max Alexander Ernst-August Heinrich-Donatus Mathais of Baden (b. 11 February 2006 at Ravensburg)

Bernhard is 466th in the line of succession to the British Throne.
