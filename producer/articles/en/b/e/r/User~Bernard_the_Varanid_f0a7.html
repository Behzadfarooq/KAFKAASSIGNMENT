I am a student of Mathematics at the University of Colorado at Boulder. Currently I am trying to finish me degree while I live and train with the US National Cycling Team. My interests include pure mathematics (e.g. topology, set theory, logic, and number theory), science, go, and the sport of cycling. I compete professionally in the sport of track cycling for the Spike Pro Cycling team and I also compete with the US National team (in international competitions). My disciplines are the keirin (pronounced KAY-rin, and not to be confused with Kirin beer) and sprints.
