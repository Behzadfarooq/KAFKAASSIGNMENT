Bernardo Redín

Bernardo Redín (born 26 February 1963 in Cali) is a former Colombian footballer. He earned 40 caps and scored 5 goals for the Colombia national football team from 1987 to 1991, and scored 2 goals in the 1990 FIFA World Cup.

[edit] Club career

  • 1981-1990 Deportivo Cali
  • 1990-1991 CSKA Sofia
  • 1992-1996 América de Cali
  • 1996-1999 Quindio Armenia
  • 2000 Atletico Huila Neiva
  • 2000 Oriente Petrolero
  • 2001 Atletico Huila Neiva
