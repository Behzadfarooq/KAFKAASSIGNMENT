Berezayka (Russian: Березайка) is a river in Novgorod Oblast and Tver Oblast of Russia. Berezayka starts from the Berezay Lake in the Valdai Hills at 57°45′20″N, 33°19′00″E, flows through a number of lakes including the southeastern part of the Piros Lake, and joins the Msta River at Berezovsky Ryadok village at 58°05′50″N, 34°29′10″E.

It is a popular rafting route. The main tributary of Berezayka is Valdayka River.
