Bernhard Modern is a serif typeface that was designed by Lucian Bernhard in 1937. It is considered to be the best-known of all of Bernhard's fonts.

The typeface is known for its extreme extenders. It is somewhat unusual in that it is equally useful for both text and headings.

Some of its best-known usages include Pepperidge Farm products, the credits for the Twilight Zone television series and the closing credits of the Gilmore Girls television series.
