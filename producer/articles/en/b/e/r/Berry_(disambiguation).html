Berry can have several meanings:

  • A berry is a type of fruit (see also pome).
  • The Berry is the student political newspaper at Cambridge University.
  • Berry College is a liberal arts college, located in Mount Berry, Georgia, USA.
  • A female crustacean is described as being "in berry" when she is carrying external eggs.
