Bernard Stasi

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Bernard Stasi (born July 4, 1930 in Reims) is a French politician. He is the son of Italo-Mexican immigrants. Stasi served as Minister of France's Overseas Department and Territories from April 2, 1973 to February 27, 1974.

From 1998 to 2004 he was the Ombudsman of the French Republic.
