Small Question: What's "Groupe Bruxelles Lambert [...] acquired control of Bertelsmann AG." supposed to mean? Bertelsmann is owned by three major shareholders - the Bertelsmann Foundation, The Mohn family, and Bruxelles Lambert. The Mohn family and the Bertelsmann Foundation are pretty much the same thing (BVG) and control 75% of Bertelmann AG. Which means, Bruxelles Lambert has voting rights (25%), but that's not really important, as there are only two parties that are allowed to vote, and the other party's vote weighs in at 75%. I'd say Bruxelles Lambert owns 25.1%, but has _no_ control...?
