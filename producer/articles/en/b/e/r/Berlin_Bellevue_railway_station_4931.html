Bellevue is a railway station on the Berlin Stadtbahn in Berlin, Germany. It is served by Berlin S-Bahn trains.

[edit] History

Work on the station started in 1875 and it was opened together with most other Stadtbahn stations on February 7, 1882. Today, it is one of the two Stadtbahn stations still in the original state. The other station is Hackescher Markt. The station is a listed building since 1987.
