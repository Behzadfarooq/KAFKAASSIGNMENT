Berry Brothers and Rudd

Berry Brothers and Rudd is British's oldest wine and spirit merchant, which began operations in the 17th century. Its flagship store is located on 3 St. James's Street, London, United Kingdom since 1698.

It is known for offering wines Bordeaux and Burgandy wines en primeur and providing wine storage services. In addition to selling wines en primeur, it also sells a number of wines and spirits under its own label, "Berry's Own Selection". It also created the Cutty Sark whisky, and created the vintage concept for The Glenrothes single malt Scotch whisky.

Berry Brothers also have offices in Dublin, Ireland and Hong Kong, two wine schools and a fine dining venue in the cellars in St. James's Street.
