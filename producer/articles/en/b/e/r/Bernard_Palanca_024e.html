Bernard Palanca

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Bernard Palanca, Jr. is the grandson of Filipino actor Armando Goyena (Jose Revilla in real life) and Pacquita Roces, the first Camay girl in the Philippines. His mom is Pilar Revilla-Jocson, the widow of the racecar driver Bernard Palanca. His younger brother Miko Palanca is also an actor. [1]

Palanca is currently single and expecting the arrival of his firstborn with actress Meryll Soriano, niece of Maricel Soriano and eldest daughter of Willie Revillame. [2]

A member of ABS CBN's Star Magic stable, he was part of The Hunks together with Piolo Pascual, Jericho Rosales, Diether Ocampo, and Carlos Agassi and the defunct band Bizkit Factory that was featured in the movie Kahit Isang Saglit.
