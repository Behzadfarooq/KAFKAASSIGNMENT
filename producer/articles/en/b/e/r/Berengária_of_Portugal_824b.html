Berengária of Portugal (pron. IPA: [bɨɾẽ'gaɾiɐ]) was a Portuguese infanta, later Queen consort of Denmark. She was the fifth daughter of Portuguese King Sancho I and Dulce Berenguer. She married Danish King Valdemar II and was the mother of Danish kings Eric IV, Abel and Christopher I.

She was born c. 1195 and married Valdemar II in 1214. She died in 1221.
