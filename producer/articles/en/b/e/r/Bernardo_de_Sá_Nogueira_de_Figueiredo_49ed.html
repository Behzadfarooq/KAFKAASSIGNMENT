Bernardo de Sá Nogueira de Figueiredo, Marquess of Sá da Bandeira (pron. IPA [sa dɐ bɐ̃'dɐiɾɐ]) was the Prime Minister of Portugal from November 5, 1836 - June 2, 1837.
