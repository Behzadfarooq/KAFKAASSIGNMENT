Bernard Boissier (born 3 October 1952, in Nîmes (Gard, France) is a former French football player.

The defender played successively in Nîmes Olympique (from 1964 to 1981), Lyon (1981-1982), Toulon (from 1982 to 1986) and Le Grau-du-Roi (from 1986 to 1988).

On 26 April 1975, he got his first and last cap with the French national team against Portugal in a 0-2 defeat but played only for two minutes.
