[edit] First president

In the list of club presidents, the first name to occur is M. Samil Osmanoglu, while the club web site states Mehmet Şamil Bey. Was Bey president for a very short time or is there some other explanation for this discrepancy? /Dcastor 23:31, 11 November 2006 (UTC)

'Bey' means 'Mr.'
Ah, simple explanation. Thankyou. /Dcastor 01:12, 23 November 2006 (UTC)
