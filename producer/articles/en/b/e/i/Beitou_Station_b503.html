Beitou Station (Traditional Chinese: 北投站; pinyin: běitóu zhàn) is a Taipei Rapid Transit System station on Red Line (Danshui Line and Xinbeitou Branch Line), located in Beitou, Taipei.

The position of station could be traced back the same name station of defunct TRA Tanshui Line and Hsin Peitou Line.

[edit] Layout
