Beirut International Exhibition & Leisure Center

The Beirut International Exhibition & Leisure Center, frequently referred to as BIEL, is a large multi-purpose facility in downtown Beirut. Lebanon's largest such complex, it hosts exhibitions, conferences, concerts, and private events. It opened on November 28, 2001.
