Yosef Dov Soloveitchik (1820-1892) was the author of Beit HaLevi and the great-grandfather of Rabbi Joseph B. Soloveitchik. For more information, see Brisk yeshivas and methods. In turn, he was the great-grandson of Rav Chaim of Volozhin.
