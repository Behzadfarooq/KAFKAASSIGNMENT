Beira Alta

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Antiga província da Beira Alta

Beira Alta (pron. IPA: ['bɐiɾɐ aɫtɐ]) was a Portuguese province in the Northern Part of the country. In the past, the area was connected with two more creating the Beira region.
