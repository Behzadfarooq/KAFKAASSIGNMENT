Beijing Tiyu Guangbo

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Beijing Tiyu Guangbo (Chinese: 北京外语广播) is also known as the "Beijing Sports Station". The radio station broadcasts at 102.5 FM and 927 AM in Beijing, China. The station is part of the Beijing Ren Min Guangbo Dian Tai group.

The recent focus of this radio station has been the World Cup in Germany and the Beijing Olympic Games for 2008.

The sports that are broadcast often on this station are:

  • F1
  • Chess
  • Volleyball
  • Football_(soccer)
  • Tennis
  • Ping_pong
  • Basketball
  • China Soccer
