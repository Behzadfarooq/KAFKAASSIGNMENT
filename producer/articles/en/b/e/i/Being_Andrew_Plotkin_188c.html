Being Andrew Plotkin

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Being Andrew Plotkin is a piece of interactive fiction written by J. Robinson Wheeler as a light-hearted parody of both the film Being John Malkovich (1999) and of the successful interactive fiction author Andrew Plotkin and his games. It won the Best Game and Best Non-Player Characters awards at the 2000 Xyzzy Awards, and was a finalist for four other categories.
