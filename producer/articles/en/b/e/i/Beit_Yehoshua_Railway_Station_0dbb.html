Beit Yehoshua Railway Station (Hebrew: תחנת הרכבת בית יהושע‎, Taḥanat HaRakevet Beit Yohoshua) is an Israel Railways passenger station located in the village (moshav) of Beit Yehoshua and serves the village and the southern part of the city of Netanya, with its large industrial zones, as well as other small communities in the area.
