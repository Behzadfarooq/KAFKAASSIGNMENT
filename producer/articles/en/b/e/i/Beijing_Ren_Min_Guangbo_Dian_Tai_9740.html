Beijing Ren Min Guangbo Dian Tai (translated as the Beijing People Broadcasting Station) (Simplified Chinese: 北京人民广播电台), is a family of radio stations that also include news, music, and sports in Beijing.

Beijing Radio broadcasts in Beijing on the 97.4 and 103.9 MHz as well as through their official website at http://www.bjradio.com.cn/
