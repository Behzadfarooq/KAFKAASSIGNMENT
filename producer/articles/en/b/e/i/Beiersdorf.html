Beiersdorf AG (FWB: BEI) is a multinational corporation based in Hamburg, Germany, manufacturing personal care products. Its brands include:

  • 8x4 website
  • atrix
  • Curad website
  • Elastoplast website
  • Eucerin website, makers of Aquaphor
  • Florena website
  • FUTURO website
  • Hansaplast website
  • JUVENA website
  • Labello website
  • la prairie website
  • Marlies Möller website
  • NIVEA website
  • SBT: Skin Biology Therapy website
  • tesa website

Beiersdorf's corporate headquarters are located in Hamburg.

Although its shares are publicly listed, Beiersdorf is controlled by Tchibo Holding AG, which directly owns 50.49 percent of shares. Tchibo Holding is held by members of the Herz family. Tchibo [1] is known as a coffee company and on-line and mail-order retailer.
