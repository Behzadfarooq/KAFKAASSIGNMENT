Bei Yan (卑衍) was an officer serving under Gongsun Yuan, duing the Three Kingdoms Era.

At one time, Bei Yan led Gongsun's troops against Sima Yi's but ultimately lost. After he retreated from defeat, he once again reassembled his forces and attempted a second attack, but after several skirmishes, he was finally slain by the Wei officer Xiahou Ba.
