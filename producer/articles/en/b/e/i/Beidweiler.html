Beidweiler (Luxembourgish: Beidler) is a small town in the commune of Junglinster, in central Luxembourg. As of 2001, the town has a population of 214.

Beidweiler is the site of the 2000 kilowatt-transmitter for transmitting the French-speaking programme of RTL.
