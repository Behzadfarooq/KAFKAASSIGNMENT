[edit] Your photos

First of all, let me say that your photos of Israeli cities are excellent and add a lot to Wikipedia. However, there's one problem - all of them have a 'bievushtang' signature at the bottom-right. While I don't think this is against policy, it's discouraged on Wikipedia for many reasons, including copyright, etc. I will not attempt to remove your images but someone else may because of the signature, thinking they are copyrighted by someone else or something. I think you should remove the signature and add that you created the photographs in the image summary if you want credit. Thanks, Ynhockey (Talk) 17:18, 1 December 2006 (UTC)
