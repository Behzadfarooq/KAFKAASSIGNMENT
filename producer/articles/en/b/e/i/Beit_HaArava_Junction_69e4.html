Beit HaArava Junction

The Beit HaArava Junction is the junction of Israel's Highway 1 and Highway 90. The Beit Ha'arava Junction is situated near Israel's eastern border with Jordan directly east of Jerusalem, and just a few minutes north of the Dead Sea and south of Jericho. Its trempiada serves traffic northward through Bet She'an until the Tsemach Junction, southward past the Dead Sea to Eilat, and west to Jerusalem.
