Giuseppe "Beppe" Gabbiani (born January 2, 1957 in Piacenza) is a former Formula One driver from Italy. He participated in 17 Grands Prix, debuting on October 1, 1978, and scored no championship points.

In Formula 2, he won the 1983 Eifelrennen.

In sports car racing, he scored 3rd in the 2003 1000km Spa.
