[edit] BEPS International School – Brussels

Avenue Franklin Rooseveltlaan 23 1050 Brussels Tel +32 (0)2 648 43 11 Fax + 32 (0)2 687 2968 e-mail: brussels@beps.be

A highly-respected independent primary school which has served the international community for over 33 years. As an IB World school, it is authorized to offer the International Baccalaureate Primary Years Programme, which provides a dynamic enquiry-based world-recognized curriculum. Children aged 2 ½ to 11 enjoy learning in a happy, safe and exciting environment. French is taught from pre-school. A garderie and school clubs are available. School busing to most sections of greater Brussels. Head of School: Nicola Clare

[edit] BEPS International School – Limal

Rue Léon Deladrière 13 1300 Limal Tel +32 (0)10 41 72 27 Fax +32 (0)10 40 10 43 e-mail : limal@beps.be

Superb rural green world environment for children 2 ½ - 11 years, located 20 km south of Brussels with ideal access from Overijse, La Hulpe, Rixensart, Genval,and Lasne. It offers an exciting international curriculum with a strong French programme. Facilities include a children’s Museum of Interesting Things, a theatre, and pony-riding. There is a “Little Bear’s” crèche for toddlers 18 months to 3 years. School busing available. Head of School: Henny de Waal

[edit] BEPS International School – Waterloo

Steenweg op Waterloo 280 1640 Sint-Genesius-Rode Tel +32 (0)2 358 56 06 Fax +32 (0)2 358 31 32 e-mail : office@wis.be

Combines with World International School (WIS) in ensuring educational excellence for students 2 ½ to 16 years. It offers a dynamic international curriculum allowing for a smooth transition from primary into middle years based on the International Baccalaureate Primary Years and Middle Years curricula. Superb facilities, a safe and spacious green world environment, conveniently located between Waterloo and Sint-Genesius-Rode. Special needs assistance arranged. School busing available Head of School: Chris Mordue
