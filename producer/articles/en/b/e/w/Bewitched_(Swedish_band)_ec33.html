Bewitched (Swedish band)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Bewitched is a Swedish retro thrash band.

[edit] Members

[edit] Current members

  • Marcus Norman (Vargher) : guitars/vocals (see also Havayoth, Naglfar)
  • Hellfire : guitars
  • Kristofer Olivius (Wrathyr) : bass/vocals (see also Naglfar)
  • Ulf Andersson (Stormlord) : drums (see also Nocturnal Rites)

[edit] Former members

  • Anders Nyström (Blackheim) : guitars/vocals (see also Bloodbath, Diabolical Masquerade, Katatonia)
  • Fredrik Degerström (Spider) : guitars (see also Guillotine)
  • Reaper : drums (see also Deathwitch)
