Bewdley Town F.C. is a football club based in Bewdley, Worcestershire, England. They joined the West Midlands Regional League Division One South in 1999. For the 2006-07 season, they are members of the West Midlands Regional League Premier Division. They play at Ribbesford Meadows and wear a blue and yellow kit. Their reserve team plays in the West Midlands Regional League Division Two.

[edit] History

Prior to joining the West Midlands League they won a number of league titles and cups in local football, including the Kidderminster Senior Cup

They also won the Worcestershire Junior Cup in 2003-04 and again in 2004-05
