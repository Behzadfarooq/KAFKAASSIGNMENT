Beware of Idols (アイドル密室殺人事件) is an episode of the anime series Case Closed. It first aired in Japan on January 22, 1996.

[edit] Plot

The Idol Yoko Okino asks for Kogoro's help and tells him that someone is watching her and that she gets silent phone calls and that someone was chasing her last night. Her manager, Eichi Yamagishi, says that they should make this investigation private because if everyone knew about this her image will be ruined. Then Kogoro accepted the Case and goes to her house with Conan and Ran. But Conan's primary school friends Ayumi, Mitsuhiko and Genta secretly came along with them. When Yoko opened the door of her house she saw a dead body and screamed. Conan and Kogoro came inside inside to examine the body, and told Ran to contact the police about the murder. Inspector Megure came after they investigated a bit they found out that Yoko has a rival named Yuuko, and the two looks just like each other. So the police contacted Yuuko and asked her to come to the crime scene. In the end, Conan collected enough evidence and solved the case. He then knocked Kogoro out with an ash tray before using the Voice-Changing Device to copy Kogoro's Voice and pointed out to everyone that this was a suicide and the dead man was actually Yoko's boyfriend.
