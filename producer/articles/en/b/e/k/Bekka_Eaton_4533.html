Bekka Eaton, the current Director of Theater at Miami University-Hamilton in Hamilton, Ohio, got her start as part of the The Second City improvisational comedy troup in Chicago. She went on to be cast as the Female DJ in Sixteen Candles and a larger role as Delta Burke's sister in the tv movie Day-O. She is also the founder of the Greater Cincinnati teen improvisational group Dot.Comedy.
