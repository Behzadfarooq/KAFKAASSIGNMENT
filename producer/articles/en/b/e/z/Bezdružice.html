Bezdružice (IPA: [ˈbɛzdrʊʒɪtsɛ]; German: Weseritz) is a town in Pilsen Region of the Czech Republic. It lies some 30 km (19 mi) to the north-west from the region capital of Pilsen.

Bezdružice is also the seat of the Municipality with Commissioned Local Authority within the Stříbro Municipality with Extended Competence.
