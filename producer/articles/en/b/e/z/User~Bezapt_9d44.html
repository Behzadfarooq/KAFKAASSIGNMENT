No prizes for guessing where I'm proud to be from; my interests include (but aren't limited to) ancient and modern history (have a postgraduate degree in Egyptology), technology, and breathtakingly aromatic arabica coffee. Mmmm, coffee!!

My username, Bezapt, is an Arabic language word meaning "exact(ly)" or "precise(ly)". The emphasis is on the second syllable. It's more fun if you say the ZAPT! part with some emphasis.
