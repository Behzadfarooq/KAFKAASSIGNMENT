Bezpartyjny Blok Współpracy z Rządem

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Bezpartyjny Blok Współpracy z Rządem (Polish for Non-partisan Block of Collaboration with the Government), abbreviated BBWR, was a non-political organization which existed from 1928 until 1935, closely affiliated with Piłsudski and his Sanacja movement.
