Bezdekov nad Metuji

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Bezdekov nad Metuji is a village in Náchod District, Czech Republic. There are about 500 people living there. The village is situated on a hill about 480 metres over sea; the location provides nice panoramas around the village. In Bezděkov there is a nice baroque church designed by architect Kilián Ignác Dientzenhofer. Bezdekov nad Metuji was founded about year 1241.
