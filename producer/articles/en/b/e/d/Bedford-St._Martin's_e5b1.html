Bedford/St. Martin's is a United States publishing company, specializing in college humanities textbooks. It is owned by the Stuttgart, Germany-based Georg von Holtzbrinck Publishing Group.

It publishes, among other things, The Bedford Reader, popular among high school Advanced Placement English Language and Composition teachers. St. Martin's Press is related.
