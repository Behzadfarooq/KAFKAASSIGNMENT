I'm a UK based wikipedian with an interest in Cricket, Grimsby Town, North Lincolnshire and Railways.

After a mere handfull of edits on here, I seem to think I'm qualified to start my own wiki! Head over to http://ukrailways.wikia.com and take a look. Initial work is starting with the use of wikipedia articles to seed it. Contributions, comments and criticism welcome.

I've been working on a few pages of current and ex-Grimsby Town footballers recently and want to in the future, I'll list here to keep track. Mainly adding infoboxes, but beefing up a few as I go. Paul Futcher is my own creation and I intend to spend some more time on it in the near future
