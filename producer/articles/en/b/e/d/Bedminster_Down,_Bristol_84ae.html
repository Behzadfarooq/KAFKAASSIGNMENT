Bedminster Down is an area of Bristol, England.

Bedminster Down is a suburb of Bristol. Much of the area was built in the 1930s. It extends from Bedminster to Ashton, Highridge and Uplands, and is a largely residential area. Much of the area is built over disused coal mines.

The Parish Church is dedicated to St Oswald and was built in 1927.
