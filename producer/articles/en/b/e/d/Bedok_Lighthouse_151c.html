Bedok Lighthouse

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Coordinates: 1°18′54″N, 103°56′06″E

Bedok Lighthouse (Chinese: 勿洛灯塔) is a lighthouse facility located at the South Eastern coast of Singapore. The lighthouse itself is notable for being located on top of a 26 story condominium at Lagoon View Block 5000L Singapore 449293, Marine Parade Road.
