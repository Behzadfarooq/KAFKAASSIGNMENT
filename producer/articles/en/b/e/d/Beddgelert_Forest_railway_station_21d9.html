Beddgelert Forest railway station is a new railway station on the Welsh Highland Railway. As of May, 2006, the platform is accessed through the Beddgelert Forest Campsite and laid with Tarmac complete with a concrete base for the waiting shelter.

The station is being built to serve the popular Beddgelert Forest Campsite, run by the Forestry Commission in association with The Camping and Caravanning Club. The station will have a waiting shelter, with the possibility of ticket sales, and will hopefully will be an alternative to going to Caernarfon or Porthmadog by car.
