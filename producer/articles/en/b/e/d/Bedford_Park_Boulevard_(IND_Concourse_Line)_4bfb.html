Bedford Park Boulevard station has three tracks with two island platforms.

At the south end, one can exit to the underpass or go up stairs to the Grand Concourse. Tile colors are lawn green with a darker green border, and there is no name tablet.

This station at one time had a full length mezzanine this was closed long ago and is now home to a master tower, which controls the entire line down to 145th Street. There is also an equipment room over the south end of the northbound platform. There is also a short, one-car length platform just north of the southbound platform after a gap of about one or two car lengths.

B Trains terminate here in the rush hours, and D trains often lay up here as well.
