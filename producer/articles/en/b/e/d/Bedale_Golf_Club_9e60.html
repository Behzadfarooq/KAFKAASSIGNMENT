The market town of Bedale is located in the heart of the picturesque North Yorkshire countryside on the edge of the Dales and only a few minutes drive from the attractive and ancient towns of Northallerton and Richmond. The current course is set on a parkland landscape which benefits from a wealth of mature trees. The club was founded in 1894 and has be located at Bedale Park, and at Firby Road, But since 1967 we have been located at our current site on Leyburn Road.

The course at Bedale has everything one would expect from a high quality golfing venue in the beautiful Yorkshire Dales. From teasing dog - legs and a meandering beck, water hazards, mature trees and strategically placed bunkers and rolling greens.

From the competition markers Bedale Golf Club is a testing 18 hole, 6610 yard, par 72, SSS. 72 golf course.Its reputation continues to grow as new features are introduced and has now become a regular venue for many prestigious regional competitions.
