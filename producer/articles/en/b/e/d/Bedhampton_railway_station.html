Bedhampton railway station is a railway station serving the district of Bedhampton, which lies a mile west of the centre of Havant, in Hampshire.

It is located on the Portsmouth Direct Line which runs between London (London Waterloo) and Portsmouth (Portsmouth Harbour).

Although Cosham is the nearest station to the west, no services directly connect these stations. Passengers must travel to the next station to the south, at Hilsea and change trains.
