Bedburg-Hau

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Coat of arms Map

Bedburg-Hau is a municipality in the district of Cleves in the state of North Rhine-Westphalia, Germany. It is located approx. 5 km south-east of Cleves. The town is populated by 12,813 people.

Moyland Castle is situated in Bedburg-Hau
