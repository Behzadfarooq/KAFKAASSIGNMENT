The Bedfordshire Football League is a football competition in England. It will run with four divisions for the 2006/07, including the Bedfordshire Football League Premier Division. The Premier Division is at step 7 (or level 11) of the National League System. The top club in the league may apply for promotion to the United Counties Football League or the Spartan South Midlands League.

In the 2005-06 season, Caldecote were the league champions.

[edit] History

Prior to the 2006/07 season, the league was known as the Bedford & District League. Along with the name change, the league reorganized its five divisions by incorporating the two primarily reserve divisions (Associate Division One and Two) into the three first team divisions, ultimately creating three divisions of 16 clubs and a bottom division of 17 clubs.
