Bedarra Island

Bedarra Island is in the middle of the Family Islands group and located approximately 10km East of Tully Heads. The alternate name is Richards Island. Regular flights are available from all major Australian capital cities to Dunk Island via Cairns. A 20 minute launch service between Dunk Island and Bedarra Island operates daily and meets all flights into Dunk Island.

[edit] Bedarra Island Resort

Bedarra Island Resort, operated by Voyages Hotels and Resorts, is located on the island.
