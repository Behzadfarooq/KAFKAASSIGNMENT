Bed, Bath & Beyond is the seventh episode of season four of the television situation comedy Will & Grace.

Spoiler warning: Plot and/or ending details follow.

[edit] Plot

Grace refuses to get out of bed in the aftermath of her break up with Nathan. So Will tries his best to get her back into life, including inviting over Jack and Karen - an idea he later regrets.

[edit] Episode info

  • Season number: 4
  • Episode number: 7
  • First aired: November 8, 2001
  • Writer: Jhoni Marchinko
  • Director: James Burrows
  • Producers: Tracy Poust, Jon Kinnally and Tim Kaiser
  • Executive producers: David Kohan & Max Mutchnick
