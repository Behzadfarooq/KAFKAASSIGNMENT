Bełchatów (IPA: [bɛwˈxatuf] (help·info)) is a city in central Poland, with 62,437 inhabitants (2004). It is located in Łódź Voivodeship and is the capital of Bełchatów County.

[edit] Industry

Near Bełchatów there is the largest thermal power plant in Europe, the Elektrownia Bełchatów.

[edit] Sports
