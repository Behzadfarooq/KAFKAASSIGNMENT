Beňadikovce is a village and municipality in Svidník District in the Prešov Region of north-eastern Slovakia.

[edit] History

In historical records the village was first mentioned in 1402.

[edit] Geography

The municipality lies at an altitude of 250 metres and covers an area of 12.084km². It has a population of about 238 people.
