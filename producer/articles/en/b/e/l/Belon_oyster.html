Belon oysters

Belon oyster is a species of oyster from Brittany, France with a metallic flavor that is considered excellent for eating raw on the half shell. It ranges from 1½ to 3½ inches across. Although originally from France, it is now being aquacultured in California, Maine, and Washington.
