[edit] New Logo

The old Giant's logo has been replaced with a new one, which includes the Coor's sponsorship of the team. I'm not sure how encyclopedic this is; and would like a discussion on reverting to the old logo without the corporate logo included. Any ideas anyone. Y control 13:05, 25 January 2006 (UTC)

Question i would have is if the logo with the coors added is the official logo that they use, or just a protional one. --Boothy443 | trácht ar 07:34, 26 January 2006 (UTC)
Both logos (the old and the current one) in the article are near identical; apart from the introduction of the coors logo. I personally think that this looks cheap and unconventional, however I wanted to guage opinion before taking any action. Y control 09:42, 26 January 2006 (UTC)
