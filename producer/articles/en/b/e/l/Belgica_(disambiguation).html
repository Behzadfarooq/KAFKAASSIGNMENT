Belgica (disambiguation)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Belgica ordinarily denotes the Roman province of Gallia Belgica.

  • In the Roman province, a mixture of Germanic and Gaulish peoples were referred to by Romans as Belgae
  • The Seventeen Provinces of the Low Countries, later divided in:
    • Belgica Foederata or the federal Dutch Republic
    • Belgica Regia or the royal Southern Netherlands
  • The kingdom of Belgium revived the toponym in a new form in 1830.
  • RV Belgica has been the name of two Belgian scientific research vessels, of 1884 and 1984.
    • The Belgian Antarctic Expedition was named after the first Belgica.
  • Belgica (genus), a genus of flies
  • The Belgica Mountains in Antarctica were named after the first research vessel.
  • The Belgica Mounds are seamounts in the Porcupine Bight in the NE Atlantic. They were named after the RV Belgica which investigated these features during a scientific cruise in 1997.
