Belleville High School is located in Belleville, Michigan and the only high school in the Van Buren School District.

[edit] Building

Belleville High School is a fairly old and large school. The original building was built in 1899, and some of the current building was constructed from that building's frame. In 1953 a fire destroyed the third floor. Plans for a new school have been discussed but were rejected.
