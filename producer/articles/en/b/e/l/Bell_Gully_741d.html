Bell Gully
Bell Gully's Auckland premises

Bell Gully is one of New Zealand's largest law firms. Founded in 1840, it practises all areas of commercial law, including banking and finance, corporate law, mergers and acquisitions, property law, taxation law, commercial litigation and employment litigation.

Bell Gully has won numerous awards in recent years, all of which indicate that it is New Zealand's premier law firm. Its achievements include the prestigious New Zealand Law Firm of Year award from respected global publisher International Financial Law Review. Bell Gully has won this award for four consecutive years (2004, 2005, 2006 and 2007).

In addition, Who's Who Legal in 2006 named Bell Gully Law Firm of the Year for New Zealand. This award was published in The International Who's Who of Business Lawyers 2006.

Bell Gully's partners are also often recognised by various international organisations as leading legal practitioners in New Zealand.

Bell Gully has offices in both Auckland and Wellington.
