[edit] Crew Information

Can someone tell me where the information for Bellamy's other crewmembers (besides Sarquiss) came from? I don't even remember their names being mentioned. Sigmasonic X 22:40, 1 August 2006 (UTC)

I believe there was a SBS question where Oda did name them. I'll go searching for it at some point. Angel Emfrbl 21:00, 12 August 2006 (UTC)
Page 84 of One Piece Blue. Here's essentially what it says:
Ross: Fighter. Wears a hat and a large coat.
Eddy: Navigator. Has a log-pose on his right hand.
Huett: Cook. Has a tattoo of the number 21 on his forehead.
Rivers: Sniper. Large man with a hat. Seems to have no shoulders.
Mani: Female fighter. Sitting next to the large man.
Myure: Doctor. She has a beauty spot under her eye and cross boots.
Then it also has stuff about Lily (the girl Sarquiss was with). But that's all the info... Murasaki Seiko 04:10, 23 August 2006 (UTC)
Also, just thought I'd mention this, these names might not be the correct romanizations, what was here before might be better, I didn't look at 'em. ^^; Murasaki Seiko 06:01, 23 August 2006 (UTC)
Okay I have a copy now of the Blue Book + Red book One Piece data books, I was lucky enough to find a copy at a imported books shop. I had knew I had seen their names somewhere but wasn't sure, it turns out they were on a few example pages I once saw of those books somewhere on the internet. My Japanese is limited so I can't confirm these names either nor any info about the crew. Now its been proven though where they are at least, we can have them back on the page.
