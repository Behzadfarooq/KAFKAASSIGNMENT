Belize Premier Football League

The Belize Premier Football League (BPFL) is the premier division of football in Belize sanctioned by the Football Federation of Belize. Its teams are eligible for international competition.

The current commissioner of the BPFL is Omario Perdomo.

[edit] Belize Premier Football League - 2007 Season

The BPFL is currently in its sixteenth season as of late February 2007 with a twelve-team league.

[edit] Returning Clubs

  • Wagiya (Dangriga-sub-champions 2006)
  • FC Belize (Belize City- champions 2006)
  • Hankook Verdes (formerly Real Verdes, also Hankook Verdes United)
  • Belmopan Bandits
  • Alpha
  • Pickstock Lake I United

[edit] New Teams

  • Toledo United, Toledo
  • Juventus Sugar Boys, Orange Walk
  • Santels, Santa Elena
  • Benque Viejo del Carmen United
  • San Pedro Nairi's
