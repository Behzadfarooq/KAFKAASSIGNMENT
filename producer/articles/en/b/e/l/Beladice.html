Zlaté Moravce District in the Nitra region

Beladice is a village and municipality in Zlaté Moravce District of the Nitra Region, in western-central Slovakia.

[edit] History

In historical records the village was first mentioned in 1156.

[edit] Geography

The municipality lies at an altitude of 170 metres and covers an area of 22.408 km². It has a population of about 1525 people.

[edit] Ethnicity

The population is roughly 98.5% Slovak.

[edit] Facilities

The village has a small public library a gym and football pitch.
