Belinda Martinez

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

You have new messages (last change).

Belinda Martinez is a Latin American voice actress who was the Spanish voice of Fujiko Mine in the Mexican/Latin American dub of Lupin III. She also was the Mexican/Latin American voice of Queen Beryl and Haruka Tenou/Uranus in Sailor Moon.

She's married to fellow voice actor Benjamín Rivera-
