The Bell County Expo Center is a 6,559-seat multi-purpose arena in Belton, Texas. It is home to the CenTex Barracudas (Intense Football League) and the former home of the now defunct Texas Bullets (Professional Indoor Football League) and the Central Texas Stampede (Western Professional Hockey League).
