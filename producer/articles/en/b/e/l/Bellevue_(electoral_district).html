Bellevue (electoral district)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Bellevue is a provincial electoral district for the House of Assembly of Newfoundland and Labrador, Canada. As of 2003 it had 7310 eligible voters.

It contains the communities of:

  • Blaketown
  • Old Shop
  • South Dildo
  • Chapel Arm
  • Norman's Cove
  • Long Cove
  • Tornlea
  • Bellevue
  • Chance Cove
  • Arnolds Cove
  • Come by Chance
  • Sunnyside
  • Goobies
  • North Harbour
  • Garden Cove
  • Swift Current
  • Monkstown
  • Terrenceville
  • Grand le Pierre
  • English Harbour East
  • Harbour Mille
  • Little Harbour East
  • Little Bay East
  • Bay L'Argent
  • Jacques Fontaine
  • St. Bernard's
  • Long Harbour
  • Mount Arlington Heights
  • Fair Haven
  • Southern Harbour
