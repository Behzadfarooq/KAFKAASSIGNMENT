Bellevue Public Schools operates 14 elementary schools (K-6--some schools also offer prekindergarten programs), two middle schools (7-8), and two high schools (9-12) in Bellevue in the U.S. state of Nebraska. The district has 574 teachers (FTEs) serving 8,886 students.
