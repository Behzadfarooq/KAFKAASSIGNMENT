Belarius is the online nickname of Greg Guichard Jensen, born March 4th, 1981. He has used this nickname consistently since 1992. He has a degree in Psychology from (and is currently employed as a research assistant at) Reed College.

His broader interests include games of all kinds (role-playing games, primarily the World of Darkness and D20 systems, being at the forefront), current events, science fiction and fantasy, technology, and (unsurprisingly) psychology, particularly of the cognitive and behavioral flavor.
