Bellova Ves

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Location of Dunajská Streda District in the Trnava Region

Bellova Ves is a village and municipality in the Dunajská Streda District of the Trnava Region of south-west Slovakia.

[edit] History

In historical records the village was first mentioned in 1951.

[edit] Geography

The municipality lies at an altitude of 119 metres and covers an area of 6.929 km². It has a population of about 187 people.
