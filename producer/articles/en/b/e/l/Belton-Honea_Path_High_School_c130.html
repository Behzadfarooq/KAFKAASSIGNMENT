Belton-Honea Path High School is a comprehensive, co-educational, public secondary school located in Belton, South Carolina, United States. BHP is currently the only open public high school serving Honea Path and Belton.

[edit] History

Belton-Honea Path opened in 1966. This new school was formed by the merger of Belton High School and Honea Path High School. The mascot is the bear.

[edit] Awards, Academic

2005:

SC State Department of Education gave BHP an Excellent rating

[edit] Awards, Sports

Varsity Football:

2004: AAA State Champions
2000 to 2006: REGION 1-AAA Champions

[edit] Student enrollment

Belton-Honea Path had a total enrollment of approximately 1,002 students.

[edit] Extracurriculars

Belton-Honea Path students participate in a wide range of extracurricular activities ranging from marching band and athletics to various cultural and community service clubs like Key Club. It has the following sports teams: soccer, golf, wrestling, basketball, track and field, baseball, and football.
