Believe in Angels... Believe in Me is the solo debut album of Angel, former lead singer of No Secrets. It was released on August 31, 2004 by Midas Records. Although the album failed to chart, it did receive multiple reviews from well-known sources and the singles all charted on the Billboard Hot Dance Tracks chart.
