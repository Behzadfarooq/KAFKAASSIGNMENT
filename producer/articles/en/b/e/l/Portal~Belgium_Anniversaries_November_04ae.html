November 1

All Saints Day, public holiday

  • 1700: death of Charles II of Spain, ruler over most of the current territory of Belgium
  • 1943: birth of Salvatore Adamo, singer
  • 1948: birth of Charles Picqué, Minister-President of the Brussels-Capital Region

Edit November 1 anniversaries • November 1 anniversaries on English Wikipedia

November 2

All Souls' Day

  • 1083: death of Matilda of Flanders (pictured), queen consort of the Kingdom of England
  • 1953: death of Émile Cammaerts, poet, translator and professor
  • 2004: death of Gustaaf Joos, priest, made cardinal by John Paul II with whom he had studied and had become befriended

Edit November 2 anniversaries • November 2 anniversaries on English Wikipedia

November 3

  • 1685: birth of François Roettiers, late Baroque painter
  • 1746: death of Maurus Dantine, monk and chronologist
  • 1879: death of Joseph Poelaert, architect of the Law Courts of Brussels
  • 1891: the first edition of the Gazet van Antwerpen appears
  • 1901: birth of Leopold III, fourth king of Belgium

Edit November 3 anniversaries • November 3 anniversaries on English Wikipedia

November 4

  • 1576: Spain captures Antwerp in the Eighty Years' War and start the Sack of Antwerp: the loss of 8,000 lives and the destruction and looting is known as the Spanish Fury
  • 1652: death of Jean-Charles de la Faille, mathematician
  • 1918: the Battle of the Sambre, one of the final Allied offensives of World War I

Edit November 4 anniversaries • November 4 anniversaries on English Wikipedia

November 5

  • 1530: the St. Felix's Flood washes away large parts of Flanders and Zeeland
  • 1899: Adrien de Gerlache and the Belgian Antarctic Expedition in the RV Belgica (pictured) return to Antwerp after having spent the winter in Antarctica

Edit November 5 anniversaries • November 5 anniversaries on English Wikipedia

November 6

  • 1479: birth of Joanna of Castile, mother of Charles V, Holy Roman Emperor
  • 1661: birth of Charles II of Spain, ruler over most of the current territory of Belgium
  • 1792: in the Battle of Jemappes, the French Revolutionary army defeated the Austrians
  • 1814: birth of Adolphe Sax, inventor
  • 1956: birth of Marc Dutroux, kidnapper and abuser of six girls, murderer of four of them, in 1995 and 1996
  • 1974: birth of Frank Vandenbroucke, cyclist, winner of Paris-Nice and Liège-Bastogne-Liège
  • 2001: national airline SABENA is declared bankrupt

Edit November 6 anniversaries • November 6 anniversaries on English Wikipedia

November 7

  • 2001: final flight for bankrupt national airline SABENA

Edit November 7 anniversaries • November 7 anniversaries on English Wikipedia

November 8

  • 1171: death of Baldwin IV, Count of Hainaut
  • 1578: Pacification of Ghent of the Netherlands against Spain in the Eighty Years' War
  • 1867: birth of Léon Houa, cyclist, winner of the first three editions of Liège-Bastogne-Liège
  • 1890: death of César Franck (pictured), composer
  • 1949: death of Cyriel Verschaeve, writer

Edit November 8 anniversaries • November 8 anniversaries on English Wikipedia

November 9

  • 1641: death of Cardinal-Infante Ferdinand, governor of the Netherlands, in Brussels
  • 1956: birth of Lei Clijsters, soccer player, winner of the European Cup Winners Cup, played 40 times for the Belgium national football team: he is also the father of tennis player Kim Clijsters
  • 1985: final and most bloody attack by the Nijvel gang, killing 8 people at a supermarket in Aalst
  • 2005: death of Muriel Degauque, first Belgian female suicide bomber, in Iraq

Edit November 9 anniversaries • November 9 anniversaries on English Wikipedia

November 10

  • 1433: birth of Charles I, Duke of Burgundy, ruler over most of the current Belgian territory
  • 1917- World War I: end of the Battle of Passchendaele, one of the bloodiest battles of World War I with about 3 quarters of a million casualties, after three months of fierce fighting, Canadian forces take Passchendaele
  • 1934: birth of Lucien Bianchi, race car driver, winner of the 1962 12 Hours of Sebring and the 1968 24 Hours of Le Mans
  • 1967: birth of Rémy Belvaux, film director, also producer and actor in Man Bites Dog

Edit November 10 anniversaries • November 10 anniversaries on English Wikipedia

November 11

Armistice Day

  • 1893: birth of Paul van Zeeland, politician, Prime Minister of Belgium between 1935 and 1937
  • 1918: Armistice Day: the end of World War I
  • 2003: death of Paul Janssen, pharmacologist, founder of Janssen Pharmaceutica

Edit November 11 anniversaries • November 11 anniversaries on English Wikipedia

November 12

  • 1723: death of Joseph Clemens of Bavaria, prince-bishop of Liège
  • 1867: death of Charles Frédéric Dubois, naturalist
  • 1914: birth of Edward Schillebeeckx, theologian
  • 1921: death of Fernand Khnopff, Symbolist painter
  • 1966: birth of Alberto Rey, pornographic actor

Edit November 12 anniversaries • November 12 anniversaries on English Wikipedia

November 13

  • 1904: Land speed record set in Ostend to 168.22 km/h
  • 1909; death of Francis, Baron Dhanis, administrator in Congo Free State

Edit November 13 anniversaries • November 13 anniversaries on English Wikipedia

November 14

  • 1863: birth of Leo Baekeland (pictured), chemist, inventor of Bakelite
  • 1888: death of Jan van Beers, poet
  • 1961: birth of Lucas Belvaux, film director and actor
  • 1985: birth of Thomas Vermaelen, soccer player with AFC Ajax and the Belgium national football team

Edit November 14 anniversaries • November 14 anniversaries on English Wikipedia

November 15

Holiday of the German-speaking Community (German: Tag der Deutschsprachigen Gemeinschaft Belgiens)

  • 1194: death of Margaret I, Countess of Flanders
  • 1559: birth of Albert VII, Archduke of Austria, Governor of the Habsburg Netherlands
  • 1872: foundation of the Benedictine Maredsous Abbey, known for its beer and cheese
  • 1902: attempted assassination of King Leopold II of Belgium by Gennaro Rubino, an Italian anarchist
  • 1913: birth of Arthur Haulot, journalist
  • 1926: birth of François Craenhals, comics artist, best known for Chevalier Ardent and Les 4 As

Edit November 15 anniversaries • November 15 anniversaries on English Wikipedia

November 16

  • 1945: birth of Jan Bucquoy, film director, comics author, and anarchist
  • 1964: birth of Josip Weber, soccer player, three time top scorer of the Belgian competition, played 8 times for the Belgium national football team

Edit November 16 anniversaries • November 16 anniversaries on English Wikipedia

November 17

  • 1905: birth of Queen Astrid, wife of Leopold III of Belgium
  • 1933: birth of Roger Leloup, comics artist best known for Yoko Tsuno
  • 1969: birth of Jean-Michel Saive, table tennis player, former World number 1

Edit November 17 anniversaries • November 17 anniversaries on English Wikipedia

November 18

  • 1522: birth of Lamoral, Count of Egmont, general and statesman
  • 1930: birth of Marcel Marlier, illustrator of the Martine children's book series
  • 1980: birth of François Duval, rally driver, winner of the 2005 Rally Australia

Edit November 18 anniversaries • November 18 anniversaries on English Wikipedia

November 19

  • 1404: the St. Elizabeth's flood washes away large parts of Flanders and Zeeland
  • 1595: death of Hubert Waelrant, Renaissance composer
  • 1949: death of James Ensor, early expressionist and surrealist painter
  • 2002: death of Alexandre de Merode, head of the medical commission of the International Olympic Committee

Edit November 19 anniversaries • November 19 anniversaries on English Wikipedia

November 20

  • 1518: death of Pierre de La Rue, composer
  • 1662: death of Archduke Leopold Wilhelm of Austria, governor of the Spanish Netherlands
  • 1834: inauguration of the Free University of Belgium, later renamed to Université Libre de Bruxelles, and in 1970 divided into the Université Libre de Bruxelles and the Vrije Universiteit Brussel
  • 1841: birth of Victor D'Hondt (pictured), mathematician
  • 1887: death of Louis Gallait, painter

Edit November 20 anniversaries • November 20 anniversaries on English Wikipedia

November 21

  • 1783: first manned gas-filled balloon flight by professor Jan-Pieter Minckeler from the lawn of Arenberg Castle (pictured)
  • 1898: birth of René Magritte, surrealist painter
  • 1946: birth of Jacky Lafon, actress in the VTM soap Familie

Edit November 21 anniversaries • November 21 anniversaries on English Wikipedia

November 22

  • 1914: end of the First Battle of Ypres, stopping the advance of the German troops through Belgium
  • 1967: birth of Bart Veldkamp, speed skater, gold medalist at the 1992 Winter Olympics for the Netherlands, bronze medalist at the 1998 Winter Olympics for Belgium
  • 2005: death of Madani Bouhouche, convicted for two murders and suspected of connections with the Nijvel gang

Edit November 22 anniversaries • November 22 anniversaries on English Wikipedia

November 23

  • 1862: birth of Théo van Rysselberghe, neo-impressionist painter
  • 1891: birth of Pierre Ryckmans, head of Belgian Congo from 1934 to 1946
  • 1912: death of Charles Bourseul, sometimes credited as the inventor of the telephone
  • 1965: death of Queen Elisabeth, wife of King Albert I of Belgium, patron of the arts

Edit November 23 anniversaries • November 23 anniversaries on English Wikipedia

November 24

  • 1192: death of Albert of Liège, prince-bishop and saint
  • 1492: death of Lewis de Bruges, Flemish knight who became the Earl of Winchester in 1472
  • 1886: birth of Georges Vantongerloo, sculptor and painter, founding member of De Stijl
  • 1938: birth of Willy Claes, former Secretary General of NATO

Edit November 24 anniversaries • November 24 anniversaries on English Wikipedia

November 25

  • 1824: birth of Michel Marie Charles Verlat, painter
  • 1936: birth of Lucien Olieslagers, soccer player, Golden Shoe for the best Belgian soccer player of 1959
  • 1961: death of Hubert Van Innis, archer, winner of two golden and one silver medal at the 1900 Summer Olympics in Paris
  • 1972: birth of Stefan Everts, 10 times World Champion motocross and winner of 101 Grand Prix

Edit November 25 anniversaries • November 25 anniversaries on English Wikipedia

November 26

  • 1675: death of Francisco Castel Rodrigo, Governor of the Habsburg Netherlands
  • 1866: death of Adrien-François Servais (pictured), called "the Paganini of the cello" by Hector Berlioz
  • 1921: birth of Alex Close, cyclist, winner of the Dauphiné Libéré and the Tour of Belgium
  • 1922: birth of Etienne Gailly, athlete, winner of the bronze medal in the marathon at the 1948 Summer Olympics in London after a dramatic finish
  • 1943: birth of Joël Robert, 6 times motocross World Champion

Edit November 26 anniversaries • November 26 anniversaries on English Wikipedia

November 27

  • 511: death of Clovis I, first king of the Franks
  • 639: death of Saint Acarius, bishop of Tournai
  • 1382: in the Battle of Roosebeke, the French and Castilian forces, led by King Charles VI of France, defeated the Flemish and English troops: Philip van Artevelde, commander of Ghent, is one of the main victims
  • 1474: death of Guillaume Dufay, composer
  • 1674: death of Franciscus van den Enden, teacher of Baruch de Spinoza and an early promotor of democracy
  • 1769: birth of Erasme Louis Surlet de Chokier, first regent of Belgium before the accession of King Leopold I
  • 1819: birth of Paul Jean Clays, painter
  • 1830: death of André Parmentier, early American landscape architect
  • 1916: death of Emile Verhaeren, early Symbolist poet

Edit November 27 anniversaries • November 27 anniversaries on English Wikipedia

November 28

  • 1640: birth of Willem de Vlamingh, sea captain, explorer of the southwest coast of Australia
  • 1849: birth of Albert Thys, businessman in Congo Free State
  • 1886: death of Charles John Seghers, archbishop of Portland, founder of the Alaska missions
  • 1916: birth of Lilian Baels (pictured), second wife of King Leopold III of Belgium
  • 1944: death of Lode Zielens, writer and journalist
  • 1965: birth of Erwin Mortier, author

Edit November 28 anniversaries • November 28 anniversaries on English Wikipedia

November 29

  • 1338: birth of Lionel of Antwerp, 1st Duke of Clarence, later governor of Ireland
  • 1780: death of Maria Theresa, Holy Roman Empress, Archduchess of Austria and Queen of Hungary and Bohemia, ruler over the Austrian Netherlands, which comprised most of present-day Belgium

Edit November 29 anniversaries • November 29 anniversaries on English Wikipedia

November 30

  • 1959: birth of Bart Peeters, singer, drummer, presenter and actor
