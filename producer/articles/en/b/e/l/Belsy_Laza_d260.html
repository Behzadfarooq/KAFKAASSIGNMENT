Belsy Laza

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Belsy Laza (born 5 June 1967) is a retired Cuban shot putter.

Her personal best put was 20.96 metres, achieved in May 1992 in Mexico City. This is the current North America, Central America and the Caribbean (NACAC) record.[1]
