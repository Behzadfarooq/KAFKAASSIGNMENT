Saint Valentine's Day is celebrated

  • 1894: death of Eugène Charles Catalan (pictured), mathematician, stated Catalan's conjecture
  • 1946: birth of Jan Decleir, actor
  • 1950: birth of Raymond van het Groenewoud, singer
  • 1952: death of Maurice De Waele, cyclist, winner of the 1929 Tour de France
  • 1955: birth of Ronald Desruelles, athlete, 1986 European Indoor Champion over 60 metres
