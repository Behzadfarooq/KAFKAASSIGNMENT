From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

< Portal:Belgium | Anniversaries | March
  • 659: death of Gertrude of Nivelles, saint and first abbess of the Benedictine abbey of Nivelles
  • 1822: death of Lieven Bauwens, early industrial spy
  • 1862: birth of Silvio Gesell, theoretical economist, social activist, anarchist and founder of Freiwirtschaft
  • 1948: the Treaty of Brussels is signed, creating the Western European Union (WEU) between Belgium, the Netherlands, France, Luxemburg, and the United Kingdom
