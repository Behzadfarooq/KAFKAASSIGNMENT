Bellabeg

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Bellabeg (Gaelic: Am Baile Beag) is a small roadside settlement in Strathdon, Aberdeenshire, Scotland.

Latitude: 57.1991°N, Longitude: 3.0658°W.

Bellabeg lies on the north side of the River Don, Aberdeenshire opposite the village of Strathdon.
