Old Nye County Courthouse in Belmont, Nevada

Belmont Courthouse State Historic Park is a Nevada state park in the Toquima Mountains of Nye County, Nevada. It is located in Belmont.

The area has no services.

[edit] History

The courthouse, open to the public, was purchased by Nye County for use as a courthouse in 1868.

The Courthouse was deeded to the Nevada State Parks in 1974.
