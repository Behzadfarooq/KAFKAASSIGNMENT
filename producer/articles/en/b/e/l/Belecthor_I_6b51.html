Belecthor I is a fictional character in J. R. R. Tolkien's legendarium.

Belecthor was the fifteenth Ruling Steward of Gondor. He succeeded his father, Húrin II, at his death in 2628 Third Age. The Corsairs of Umbar were the major threat to Gondor at the time, although no major events are recorded.

Belecthor had two children: his daughter, Morwen, and son Orodreth.

He was succeeded by his son Orodreth at his death in 2655.
