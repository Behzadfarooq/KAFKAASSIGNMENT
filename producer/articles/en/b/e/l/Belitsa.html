Belitsa (Bulgarian Белица) is a town in Southwestern Bulgaria. It is located in Blagoevgrad oblast and is close to Yakoruda. Home of the last remaining "dancing" bears, although they are enjoying a much more relaxing life in their new sanctuary. You can find out more about the Dancing Bears by visiting the Four Paws website Four Paws or you can download a free PDF booklet here
