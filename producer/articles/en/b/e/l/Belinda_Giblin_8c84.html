Belinda Giblin

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Belinda Giblin (b. 1950) is an Australian actress, born in Tamworth, New South Wales.

She is best known in Australia for her roles as Kay Webster in the risqu√©, satirical 1970s soap opera The Box and as the scheming Alison Carr in the 1980s melodrama Sons and Daughters. Other television roles include appearances in The Sullivans, Alvin Purple (1976), Bluey (1976), Heartbreak High, Skyways, Home and Away and Good Guys, Bad Guys.

She starred in the feature film Petersen in 1974. Today she remains busy in the theatre.
