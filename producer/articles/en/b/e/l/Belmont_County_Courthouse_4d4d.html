Belmont County Courthouse

The Belmont County Courthouse is located at 101 West Main Street, St. Clairsville, Belmont County, Ohio, United States. It sits on the highest point in the St. Clairsville area and can be seen from Interstate 70.

[edit] County Government

The main positions the courthouse houses are as follows:

  • Clerk of Courts: Randy Marple
  • Common Pleas Court: Judge Jennifer S. Sargus & Judge John M. Solovan II
  • County Auditor: Joseph A. Papanno
  • County Commissioners: Mark Thomas II, Walter Longshaw, Charles Probst, Jr.
  • County Engineer: Fred F. Bennett
  • Juvenile Court: Judge J. Mark Costine
  • Probate Court: Judge J. Mark Costine
  • Recorder: Mary C. Nixon

[edit] Court Annexes

[edit] Court Annex-St. Clairsville

An annex to the Courthouse is located at 147 West Main Street in St. Clairsville, west of the main complex. This building houses the following positions:

  • Prosecuting Attorney: Christopher Berhalter
  • Western Division Court: Judge Harry W. White

[edit] Court Annex-Bellaire

Another annex is located at 400 West 26th Street, Suite 100 in Bellaire, Ohio. It houses the following positions:

  • Eastern Division Court: Judge D. William Davis

[edit] Court Annex-Martins Ferry

Yet another annex is located at 401 Walnut Street in Martins Ferry, Ohio. It houses the following positions:

  • Northern Division Court: Judge Frank Fregiato
