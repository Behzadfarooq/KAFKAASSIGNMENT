Belur, West Bengal

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

For other uses see Belur

Belur is a town on the west bank of the Hooghly River in the district of Howrah, West Bengal, India. Situated in Belur is Belur Math, a religious abbey, containing a complex of temples. Belur Math is famous for being the headquarters of the Ramakrishna Math, a monastic order, as well as that of the related Ramakrishna Mission, both founded by Swami Vivekananda.
