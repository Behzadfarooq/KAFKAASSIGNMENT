Belen

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Belén (or Belen) is the Spanish expression for nativity scene, and also the name for the town of Bethlehem in that language. It may also refer to:

  • Places
    • Belén, a town in the Argentine province of Catamarca
    • Belén de Umbría, is a town in the Risaralda department of Colombia
    • Belén, a municipality in the Honduran department of Lempira
    • Belén, a town in northern Peru
    • Belén, a town in western Spain
    • Belen, a town in New Mexico, United States
  • Others
    • Belen, a character in the mythical universe of J.R.R. Tolkien, the son of Bëor the Old
    • Belén López, a character in Spanish TV show Aquí no hay quien viva
    • Belen Jesuit Preparatory School in Miami, Florida (United States)
    • Colegio de Belén which stood in Havana (Cuba) until the communist government of Fidel Castro, an alumnus of the school himself, took power. It moved to Miami, Florida. See Belen Jesuit Preparatory School
    • Belen, a pastoral, nomadic people in northern Ethiopia.
    • Marta Belen, American opera singer.
