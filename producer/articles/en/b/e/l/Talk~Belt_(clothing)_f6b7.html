[edit] illegal in india ??

I'm an Indian , living in India .The idea of leather belts being "illegal" in India is simply ridiculos ... Almost all the major brands have showrooms in India and their business seems to be doing pretty well! .. please check/change this

I have checked this. The prohibition on leather goods is technically correct as it originates from the Sale of Leather Goods Ordinance (No.645 of 1929), however since independence in 1947 the Indian government has not enforced the ban, but nor has it actually repealed the legislation. Royal Mail 23:33 7th June 2006 (UTC)
