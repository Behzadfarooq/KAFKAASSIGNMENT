March 1

Image:Dick norman.jpg
  • 1597: birth of Jean-Charles de la Faille, mathematician
  • 1876: birth of Henri de Baillet-Latour, third president of the International Olympic Committee from 1925 till 1942
  • 1934: birth of Jean-Michel Folon, artist, illustrator, painter, and sculptor
  • 1945: birth of Wilfried Van Moer, soccer player, three times winner of the Belgian Golden Shoe
  • 1956: birth of Jan Van der Roost, composer
  • 1971: birth of Dick Norman, tennis player (pictured)

March 2

  • 855: death of Lothair I, Holy Roman Emperor
  • 1127: death of Charles I, Count of Flanders
  • 1871: birth of Mathieu Crickboom, violinist, founder of the Crickboom Quartet with Pablo Casals
  • 1962: death of Charles Jean de la Vallée-Poussin, mathematician

Edit March 2 anniversaries • March 2 anniversaries on English Wikipedia

March 3

  • 1819: birth of Gustave de Molinari, economist (pictured)
  • 1875: Jacques Bouhy, baritone, stars as Escamillo in the first performance of Carmen by Georges Bizet
  • 1972: birth of Yasmine, singer and tv presenter
  • 1976: birth of Joos Valgaeren, soccer player, former player of Celtic F.C. and of the Belgium national football team
  • 1990: death of Gérard Blitz, sporter and businessman, winner of the bronze medal in waterpolo at the 1936 Summer Olympics in Berlin, founder of holiday company Club Med
  • 1983: death of Hergé, comics author of The Adventures of Tintin
  • 2004: Belgian brewer Interbrew and Brazilian rival AmBev agreed to merge in a $11.2 billion deal that formed InBev, the world's largest brewer

March 4

  • 1906: birth of Georges Ronsse, cyclist, World Champion in 1928 and 1929
  • 1976: birth of Thierry Renaer, field hockey defender
  • 1984: birth of Tom De Mul, footballer, currently plays as a right winger for Ajax Amsterdam

March 5

  • 1576: death of Luis de Zúñiga y Requesens, Governor of the Habsburg Netherlands
  • 1592: death of Michael Coxcie, painter
  • 1939: birth of Pierre Wynants, chef of the Comme chez soi restaurant
  • 1945: birth of Erwin Van Den Daele, soccer player, selected 32 times for the Belgium national football team
  • 1971: birth of Filip Meirhaeghe, mountain biker, silver medalist at the 2000 Summer Olympics in Sydney and World Champion in 2003
  • 2001: death of Franz De Mulder, cyclist, winner of the 1960 Vuelta a España

Edit March 5 anniversaries • March 5 anniversaries on English Wikipedia

March 6

  • 1447: death of Saint Colette, founder of the order of Colettine Poor Clares
  • 1925: Belgium annexes Eupen, Malmedy, and Sankt Vith
  • 1949: birth of Melchior Wathelet, Minister-President of Wallonia between 1985 and 1988
  • 1957: birth of Roland Liboton, cyclo-cross racer, four times World Champion
  • 1987: The British ferry M/S Herald of Free Enterprise capsizes in about 90 seconds after leaving the harbour of Zeebrugge, Belgium en route to Dover, England across the English Channel, killing 193

March 7

  • 1877: death of Victor Scheppers, founder of the Congregation of the Brothers of Scheppers

  • 1979: death of Gérard Blitz, sportsman, winner of two silver and one bronze medal at the 1920 Summer Olympics in Antwerp and the 1924 Summer Olympics in Paris

March 15

  • 44 BC: death of Julius Caesar, Roman conqueror of what is currently Belgium and the first to mention the "Belgae"
  • 1086: death of Richilde, Countess of Mons and Hainaut
  • 1912: birth of Louis Paul Boon, writer
  • 1946: demolition of the IJzertoren
  • 1971: death of Jean-Pierre Monseré, cyclist, 1970 World Champion
  • 1972: birth of Filip Dewulf, tennis player, semi-finalist at the French Open

Edit March 15 anniversaries • March 15 anniversaries on English Wikipedia

March 16

  • 1815: Prince Willem of the House of Orange-Nassau proclaimed himself King of the United Kingdom of the Netherlands
  • 1878: birth of Émile Cammaerts, poet, translator and professor
  • 1881: birth of Pierre Paulus, painter, designer of the rooster on the Walloon flag
  • 1911: birth of Pierre Harmel, Prime Minister of Belgium between 1965 and 1966

Edit March 16 anniversaries • March 16 anniversaries on English Wikipedia

March 17

  • 659: death of Gertrude of Nivelles, saint and first abbess of the Benedictine abbey of Nivelles
  • 1822: death of Lieven Bauwens, early industrial spy
  • 1862: birth of Silvio Gesell, theoretical economist, social activist, anarchist and founder of Freiwirtschaft
  • 1948: the Treaty of Brussels is signed, creating the Western European Union (WEU) between Belgium, the Netherlands, France, Luxemburg, and the United Kingdom

  • 1973: birth of Tom Nuyens, Mister World 1996

  • 1866: death of Jean-Baptist David, professor Dutch and history, president of twe first two "taelcongressen" (language congresses)
