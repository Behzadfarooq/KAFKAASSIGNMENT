Belaya Kalitva

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Coat of arms of Belaya Kalitva

Belaya Kalitva (Russian: Бе́лая Кали́тва) is a town in Rostov Oblast, Russia. It is located at around 48°10′30″N, 40°47′22″E. Population: 47,347 (2002 Census).
