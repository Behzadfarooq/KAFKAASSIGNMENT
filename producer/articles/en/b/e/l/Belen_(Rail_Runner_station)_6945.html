Belen is the southern terminus of the New Mexico Rail Runner Express commuter rail line in Belen, New Mexico.

It is located in the center of the town of Belen, near the intersection of Reinken Avenue and Wisconsin Street. The station serves residents of Belen and surrounding communities in Valencia County, New Mexico. Service began on February 2, 2006.

The station has free parking, with 220 spaces.

Each of the Rail Runner stations contains an icon to express each community's identity. The icon representing this station is the Harvey House in Belen, which is near the station.
