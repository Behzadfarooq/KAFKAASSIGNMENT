[edit] About me

My first logged-in edit to Wikipedia was on 6 April, 2004. I was made a Wikipedia administrator on 4 September, 2005.

I am now living in Boston and working for Yokel, a startup which is building a search engine to help people find products for sale in stores near them. This is taking up a lot of my time and brainpower, so my Wikipedia work has slowed down somewhat recently. Feel free to leave a message, though; apologies if it's a few days or more before I can get back to you.

I also have a digital camera, and I love wandering around the city, so if you need a picture of anything in the Boston area, let me know. -- Beland 18:19, 14 January 2006 (UTC)
