Bela Vista Futebol Clube is a Brazilian football team from the city of Niterói, Rio de Janeiro state, founded on May 10, 1977.

[edit] Stadium

The home stadium Alziro Almeida has a capacity of 4,000 people. The stadium is also known as Alzirão.

[edit] Colors

The official colors are red and white.
