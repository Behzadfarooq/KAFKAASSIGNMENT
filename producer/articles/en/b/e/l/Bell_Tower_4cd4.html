Bell Tower

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Not to be confused with a bell tower on a church or other building.

Bell Tower is an office tower in Edmonton, Canada. It stands at 130 metres (426 feet) or 31 storeys tall and was completed in 1982. Tenants include Bell Canada, Bishop & McKenzie LLP, & Great-West Life Assurance.
