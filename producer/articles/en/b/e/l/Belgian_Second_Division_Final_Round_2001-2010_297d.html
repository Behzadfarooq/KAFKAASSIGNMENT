[edit] 2002

[edit] Qualifiers

The following teams qualified for the 2002 final round:

  • K. Heusden-Zolder as winner of the second period
  • K.S.V. Ingelmunster as second of the second division
  • R.A.E.C. Mons as third of the second division
  • Cercle Brugge K.S.V. as sixth of the second division

Periods 1 and 3 were won by champion K.V. Mechelen and Heusden-Zolder finished 4th of the second division.

[edit] 2003

[edit] Qualifiers

The following teams qualified for the 2003 final round:

  • K. Heusden-Zolder as winner of the first and second periods
  • K.A.S. Eupen as second of the second division
  • S.V. Zulte-Waregem as fourth of the second division
  • F.C. Denderleeuw as fifth of the second division

Period 3 was won by champion Cercle Brugge K.S.V. and Heusden-Zolder finished 3rd of the second division.

[edit] 2006

[edit] Qualifiers

The following teams qualified for the 2006 final round:

  • Lierse S.K. as 17th of the Jupiler League
  • K.V.S.K. United Overpelt-Lommel as 2nd of the second division
  • K.F.C. Verbroedering Geel as 3rd of the second division
  • K.V. Red Star Waasland as 4th of the second division

The period winners were not qualified as it was not decided before the beginning of the final round whether the points raised by clubs against Beringen-Heusden-Zolder were to be retired or not.

[edit] 2007

[edit] Qualifiers

The following teams are qualified for the 2007 final round:

  • F. C. Verbroedering Dender E.H. as winner of the first period
  • Royal Antwerp FC as winner of the second period
  • The winner of the third period
  • The team ending in position 17 in the Belgian League

Note: as long as the season is not over, all nominated teams are provisional, as teams who have won a period could still become champions and thus qualify directly. In which the next highest finishing team in the league will take their place.
