Bel-Korhadris

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Bel-Korhadris the Scholar King is a fictional character from Warhammer Fantasy.

Bel-Korhadris started and built the White Tower of Hoeth.

Timeline

All dates are based on the Sigmarite Calendar].

c -679 - Construction of the White Tower begins.

c -290 - The Tower is half completed, and the Loremasters make a small town around it.

c 497 - The Order of the Sword Masters of Hoeth is made as the White Tower is completed.
