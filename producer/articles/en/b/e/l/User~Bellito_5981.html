[edit] Italian

I can survive in Italy for about 2-3 days probably. Then I better make my way to England where I speak the way they do— in English. Until this gets to a level 2, you get: NOTHING! Yay, nothing! Isn't that great...Not. That's probably because I learn the majority of my Italian from hearing it and looking the discernable words up in an English-Italian dictionary. Oh, well.

[edit] Spanish

It's slipping away from me, little by little. Pretty soon, I'll be hitting 2. Not good. Here's some level three Spanish...while it lasts.

  • Como estas? Bueno, y tú? Muy bueno tambien. Que estas haciendo? Soy preparando el desayuno por mi familia. Sí? Sí. Que es el desayuno? Huevos. Porque huevos? A mi me gusta los huevos. O, sí.
  • Translation: How are you? Good, and you? Very good as well. What are you doing? I'm making breakfast for my family. Really? Yes. What is breakfast? Eggs. Why eggs? I like eggs. Oh.

Kind of a boring conversation, but what can you expect? I'm at about a 2.5 level of it!
