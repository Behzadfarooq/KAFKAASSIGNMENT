The Bellingham SLAM are a 2005 expansion team in the Ron Boone Division of the American Basketball Association. They were originally expected to play in Everett, Washington, but were relocated to Bellingham, Washington under the ownership of John Dominguez. The head coach is Rob Ridnour, father of Luke Ridnour.

[edit] Inaugural season

The SLAM won their first two playoff games in 2005, but lost in the Great Eight Tournament in Rochester to the San Jose SkyRockets by a score of 138-119 on March 22, 2006.
