Believer is a Christian heavy metal band from the late 1980s and early 1990s, that played a hybrid mixture of death, thrash, and progressive metal.[1] The two primary members of the band were vocalist/guitarist Kurt Bachman and drummer Joey Daub, who were joined by several others after their 1989 debut album, Extraction from Mortality. The band was jointly signed to Roadrunner Records, and the Christian label R.E.X. Music. Believer disbanded in 1994; however, in March 2005, Daub announced that he and Bachman were in the process of reforming the band, and are planning to release a new album sometime within late 2006 or early 2007.
