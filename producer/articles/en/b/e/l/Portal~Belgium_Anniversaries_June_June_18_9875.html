  • 1464: death of Roger van der Weyden, painter
  • 1815: at the Battle of Waterloo, Napoleon Bonaparte is defeated by the combined troops of the United Kingdom, the United Netherlands, and Prussia
  • 1848: birth of Jacques Bouhy, baritone, one of the main stars in the first performance of Carmen by Georges Bizet
  • 1865: death of Antoine Wiertz, Romantic painter and sculptor
  • 1930: foundation of the INR, the national broadcasting company, forerunner of the VRT and the RTBF
  • 1971: birth of C.J. Bolland, music producer and remixer
  • 1975: birth of Marie Gillain, actress and model
  • 1997: death of Edmond Leburton, politician, Prime Minister of Belgium between 1973 and 1974
