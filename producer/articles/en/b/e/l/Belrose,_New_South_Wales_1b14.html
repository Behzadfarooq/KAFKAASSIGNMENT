Belrose, New South Wales

Belrose is a suburb on the Northern Beaches of Sydney, New South Wales, Australia. It is part of the Warringah local government area. Primarily a residential area, it also accommodates Austlink Business Park, two shopping centres, a theatre, a library, and many playing field areas including Lionel Watts Oval which is a sporting ground for kids and Frenchs Forest Showground. It borders Terrey Hills, Davidson, and Frenchs Forest. Its postcode is 2085.
