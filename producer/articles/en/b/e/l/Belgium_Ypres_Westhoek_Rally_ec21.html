The Belgium Ypres Westhoek Rally (BYWR) founded by Fanz Thevelin in 1965 is one of the most famous rally's in the European Rally Championship and the International Rally Challenge.

[edit] Trivia

In its history has had some famous Rally pilots and Co-pilots participating like Didier Auriol, Colin Mcrae, Alister McRae, Juha Kankkunen, François Duval, Michele Mouton, Jean Todt, Armin Schwarz and Ari Vatanen among others.

Is hosted by A.C. Targa Florio.
