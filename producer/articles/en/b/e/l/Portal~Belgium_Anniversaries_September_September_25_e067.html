You have new messages (last change).
  • 1506: death of Philip I of Castile, ruler over most of current Belgium, father of Charles V, Holy Roman Emperor
  • 1630: death of Ambrosio Spinola, marqués de los Balbases, Spanish general in Belgium during the Eighty Years' War, victorious at the Siege of Ostend
  • 1946: birth of Dirk Sterckx, politician, former journalist for the VRT, former chairman of the VLD in 2004
  • 1955: birth of Ludo Coeck, soccer player, played 46 times for the Belgium national football team
  • 1983: death of King Leopold III of Belgium, King of the Belgians from 1934 to 1951
  • 2005: Tom Boonen wins the World Cycling Championship in Madrid
