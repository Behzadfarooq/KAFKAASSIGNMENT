Belhurst Castle

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Belhurst Castle is a former private residence on the shores of Seneca Lake in Geneva, New York.

[edit] History

Architect Albert W. Fuller designed the imposing stone mansion in the Romanesque Revival style, popular in the last two decades of the nineteenth century. Supplemented by modern facilities, Belhurst Castle now serves as a restaurant and inn. Belhurst Castle has been used as a casino, supper club, and speakeasy.
