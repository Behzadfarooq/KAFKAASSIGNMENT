Belarusian Wikipedia (Belarussian: Беларуская Вікіпэдыя) - is the Belarusian language version of Wikipedia.

The Belarusian Wikipedia started on August 12, 2004. It passed the milestone of 5,000 articles on September 29, 2006. As of February 7, 2007 it has 6,148 articles.
