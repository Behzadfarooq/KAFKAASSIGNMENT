Belial's Brood

One of the antagonists in Vampire: The Requiem, Belial's Brood is a loose confederation of Satanists, demon-worshippers and overt miscreants. The Covenant claims that the Damned originate from Hell itself, and exalt in the spread of misery and pain. They are one of the two "enemy only" covenants in the core rulebook, the other being the VII.

They are intended to be more straightforward enemies than other antagonists that might be fought against in The Requiem, as their goals (spreading wanton misery and violence, heedless of whether or not they expose the existence of vampires) are antithetical to nearly any character.
