Below Par Records is an Australian independent record label established in 2000 by three sixteen year old Sydney high school students.

In 2002 Below Par Records won the Nescafe Big Break competition[1] and in 2003 Below Par Records went into a label development deal with Eleven owned by John Watson and Melissa Chenery.
