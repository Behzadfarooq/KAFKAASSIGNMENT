Located in Belgrade, Minnesota BBE High School has all 7th-12th grade students in the Belgrade-Brooten-Elrosa School District.

[edit] History

This school was formerly with the Belgrade-Elrosa school district. When this school did not have enough children to keep the school running, they merged with the Brooten school district. They combined in 1987. In 2005, BBE had their first state team participant, as they won the 6AA championship in volleyball. They lost in the first round at state against Stewartville and then lost in the first consolation match against St. Cloud Cathedral.


[edit] Students

There are 380 students, 51% of which are male. Grade 7 has 71 students, grade 8 has 61 students, grade 9 has 64 students, grade 10 has 55 students, there are 66 juniors, and 63 seniors. The student:teacher ratio is 1:16. 2% of all students are hispanic, and 98% are white. This data was taken from 2001-2003

[edit] Teachers

There are 24 teachers teaching at this school.
