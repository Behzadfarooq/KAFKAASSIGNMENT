Bell's Amusement Park was an old-fashioned East Coast style amusement park, in Tulsa, Oklahoma. It was located near the Southwest corner of Expo Square, and just West of the International Petroleum Exposition building. The company's 10 acre lease with the Tulsa County Public Facilities Authority expired on November 8, 2006.

On November 9, 2006, the Tulsa County Fair Board offered no new lease for the park, and the amusement park was given until March 8, 2007 to remove its rides and attractions from Expo Square.

Later, the date was pushed back to June 15, 2007, for the complete removal of all property owned by the amusement park. The entire parkful of rides and attractions are being removed at this time.

The park was founded in 1951, and was open for the last time in October of 2006.
