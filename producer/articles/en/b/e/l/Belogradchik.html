Belograchik (Bulgarian: Белоградчик listen (help·info)) is a town in northwestern Bulgaria. It is located in Vidin Province and is close to Dimovo. The Belogradchik Observatory is located nearby.

The town is a popular toursit destination. Important landmarks are the mediaeval Belogradchik Fortress and the Belogradchik Rocks, bizarre rock formations reaching up to 200 m in height.
