I saw your article The Polar Bear Club. The game that they play sounds a lot like Petals Around the Rose. However, the term "Polar Bear Club" is usually associated with ice swimming. If you can provide reliable sources to verify the activities of The Polar Bear Club that you wrote about, then The Polar Bear Club can be a disambiguation page.
