Saint Nicholas Eve

  • 1355: death of John III, Duke of Brabant
  • 1495: birth of Nicolas Cleynaerts, grammarian
  • 1671: birth of Joseph Clemens of Bavaria, prince-bishop of Liège
  • 1907: death of Charles Leickert, landscape painter
  • 1951: birth of Anne-Mie van Kerckhoven, artist
  • 1966: death of Sylvère Maes, cyclist, winner of the Tour de France in 1936 and 1939
