Belokranjska povitica

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Belokranjska povitica is a Slovene national dish.

Ingredients (recipe yields: 10 portions)

[edit] the dough

  • 1/2 kg of flour
  • 1/4 l of tepid water
  • salt
  • 1/2 tea spoon of vinegar
  • 1 spoon of oil

[edit] the filling

  • 1/2 kg of curd
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 dcl of thick cream
  • 5 dkg of butter

Preparation:

We put the salt into the water, then we make the dough. We separate the dough into 2 parts and let it stay for half an hour. When the dough is ready, we add the filling, roll everything together and roast it.
