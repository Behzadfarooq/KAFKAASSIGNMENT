Bellevue (French, meaning beautiful view) is the name of many places in many countries:

  • Australia
    • Bellevue, Western Australia
    • Bellevue Hill, New South Wales
  • Canada:
    • Bellevue, Alberta
    • Bellevue, Newfoundland and Labrador
    • Bellevue, Ontario
    • Bellevue, Prince Edward Island
    • Bellevue, Saskatchewan
  • France:
    • Château de Bellevue, a small château built for Madame de Pompadour near Paris in 1750 overlooking the Seine and demolished in 1823.
  • Germany:
    • Schloss Bellevue is the official residence of the President of Germany, being a château near the center of the city of Berlin.
  • Sweden:
    • Bellevue (Göteborg), a town square in Gamlestan, Göteborg
  • Switzerland
    • Bellevue, Switzerland, a municipality in the Canton of Geneva
    • Bellevue (Zurich), a town square in Zurich
  • United Kingdom
    • Bellevue, Belfast
  • United States of America:
    • Bellevue (LaGrange, Georgia), a historic house in LaGrange, Georgia
    • Bellevue (Florida), a historic house in Tallahassee, Florida
    • Bellevue Baptist Church, in Memphis, Tennessee
    • Bellevue, Kentucky
    • Bellvue, Colorado
    • Bellevue, Michigan
    • Bellevue, Nebraska
    • Bellevue, Ohio
    • Bellevue, Oregon
    • Bellevue, Pennsylvania, a suburb of Pittsburgh
    • Bellevue, Texas
    • Bellevue, Washington
    • Bellevue, Washington, D.C., a neighborhood in Washington, DC
    • Bellevue, Wisconsin
