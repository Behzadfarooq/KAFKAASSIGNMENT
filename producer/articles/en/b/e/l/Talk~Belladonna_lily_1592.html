This is probably the weirdest software bug I've seen on Wikipedia, and that's saying something. :)

I was going over this article preparing to delete it because it was listed on VfD over a week. I see in the history that it purports to have been created by IP# 209.98.3.203, which is impossible. (Except for the title, and I didn't create the title either, I'd previously never even heard the term "belladonna lily.")
