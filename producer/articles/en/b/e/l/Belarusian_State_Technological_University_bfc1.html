Belarusian State Technological University (Russian: Белорусский государственный технологический университет; Belarusian: Беларускі Дзяржаўны Тэхналагічны Універсітэт) is a University in Minsk, Belarus specialized in Engineering and Technology.

  • 1930 stablished in Gomel
  • 1946 transferred to Minsk

[edit] Structure

  • 47 departments
  • 9 faculties
  • Dean's office for foreign students
  • Pre-University courses
  • Negoreloe forestry experimental station
  • Botanical garden
  • Meteorological station
  • Educational-production forest-processing complex
  • Borisov educational-scientific experimental station
  • Technological gymnasium

[edit] Prominent alumni

  • Yuri Puntus - U-21 Belarus and FC MTZ-RIPO teams chief coach
