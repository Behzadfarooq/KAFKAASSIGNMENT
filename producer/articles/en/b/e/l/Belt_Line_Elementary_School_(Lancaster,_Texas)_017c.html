Belt Line Elementary School is a public school in Lancaster, Texas (USA). It is part of the Lancaster Independent School District.

Located at 1355 West Belt Line Road in central Lancaster, the school serves students in grades pre-kindergarten through five.

Prior to the 2006-2007 school year, the campus served as the district's intermediate school.

[edit] School uniforms

All Lancaster ISD students are required to wear school uniforms [1].

Pre-Kindergartners and Elementary schoolers may wear:

  • Red, White, and Blue solid polo shirts, turtlenecks, vests, and sweatshirts
  • Solid color jackets and windbreakers
  • White Oxford shirts
  • Khaki and Navy blue bottoms and Black slacks
