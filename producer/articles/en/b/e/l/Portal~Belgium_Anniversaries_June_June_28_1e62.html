You have new messages (last change).
  • 1550: birth of Johannes van den Driesche, Orientalist and exegete
  • 1577: birth of Peter Paul Rubens, the most popular and prolific Flemish and European painter of the 17th century, proponent of an exuberant Baroque style which emphasized movement, colour, and sensuality
  • 1598: death of Abraham Ortelius, cartographer and creator of the first modern atlas
  • 1919: Eupen, Malmédy and Sankt-Vith are annexed from Germany by Belgium after World War I, due to the Treaty of Versailles
  • 1954: birth of Benoît Sokal, video game designer and comics artist, best know for Canardo
  • 1994: death of Idel Ianchelevici, sculptor
