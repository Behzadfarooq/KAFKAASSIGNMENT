Portal:Belgium/Anniversaries/February/February 21

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

< Portal:Belgium | Anniversaries | February
  • 1944: birth of Herman De Coninck, poet and literary critic
  • 1946: World War II collaborator José Streel is executed by firing squad
  • 1947: birth of Hugo Coveliers, politician
  • 1954: birth of Ivo Van Damme, middle distance runner, double silver medalist at the 1976 Summer Olympics in Montreal, remembered in the annual Memorial van Damme athletics meeting in Brussels
  • 1957: birth of Stijn Coninx, film director, best known for Daens
  • 1986: birth of Prince Amadeo of Belgium, Archduke of Austria-Este, first child of Princess Astrid of Belgium and her husband Prince Lorenz
