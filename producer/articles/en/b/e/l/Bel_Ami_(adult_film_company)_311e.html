Bel Ami is a Bratislava-based pornographic film company established by George Duroy, who is Slovak ("Duroy" is a pseudonym based on the main character of Guy De Maupassant's novel Bel Ami). "Bel Ami" means "beautiful friend" in French.

It specializes in gay pornographic films and photographs featuring young men in their late teens and early to mid-twenties. Its products range in style from soft-core (Moments With Johan) to harder-core (An American in Prague).

An American in Prague (1997) is a contender for the best-selling gay pornographic film of all time.
