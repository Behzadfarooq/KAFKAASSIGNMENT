[edit] District Demographics

This school district covers about 700 square miles in the Stearns, Pope, and Kandiyohi counties in Minnesota. There are about 822 students enrolled in the district. The teacher to student ratio is 1:15. This school district covers the towns of Belgrade; Brooten; Elrosa; Greenwald; Regal; Padua; and Sedan.

[edit] Elementary School

Located in Brooten, there are 396 students and 27 teachers. The teacher:student ratio is 1:15.
