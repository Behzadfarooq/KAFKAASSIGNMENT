Belarus
Broadcaster: BTRC
First Entered Junior Eurovision: 2003
Number of Entries: 4
Won: 1 Scoring (since 2003)
Highest Score (2005): 149
Lowest Score (2004): 9

Belarusians have been remarkably successful in the Junior Eurovision Song Contest. Belarus won in 2005 and has had minimum poor results, such as the disappointing results of 2004. Because of their overall success, however, it will be surprising if they do not participate in 2007.
