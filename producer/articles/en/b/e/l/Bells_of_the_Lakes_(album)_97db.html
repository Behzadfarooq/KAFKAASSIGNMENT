Bells of the Lakes is an album recorded and released in 2000 by Bells of the Lakes. It was their first album, recorded at the McKnight Auditorium at the Minnesota History Center[1]. It features a wide variety of handbell music, including a piece arranged by the director of Bells of the Lakes, W. H. (Bill) Mathis[2].
