Belgrave (Puffing Billy) railway station is situated in Belgrave, a suburb of Melbourne in the Australian state of Victoria. It is the inner terminal of the famous Puffing Billy steam tourist railway. It is adjacent to, and forms an interchange with, Belgrave railway station which is the outer terminal of the Belgrave line of Melbourne's broad gauge (5ft 3in) electric suburban network.
