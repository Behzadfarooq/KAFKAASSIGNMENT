[edit] Overview

Gareth Millar and Mark Helferty close in for a tackle.

Coached by HC Rod Thompson, the team was founded in 2002. The Bulls currently compete in the IAFL in a kitted, 11 a side capacity, and in their second season won the league, and narrowly lost in the Shamrock Bowl final. Their offence features Gareth Millar, Mike Calo, Charlie Campalani and Bjorn Blythe in addition to possessing one of the biggest offensive line units in the IAFL. The defence is strong, featuring Mark Helferty, Marty McKeever and Tim Jebb.

[edit] 2006

They competed well in 2006, despite losing some experienced players to various different circumstances. They narrowly lost in Shamrock Bowl final of 2005.

[edit] 2006 Player Awards

  • Most Valuable Player - Mike Calo
  • Offensive Player Of The Year - Charlie Campalani
  • Defensive Player Of The Year - Mark Helferty
  • Rookie Of The Year - Tim Jebb
  • Most Improved Player - Andy Black
