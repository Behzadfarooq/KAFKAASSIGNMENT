Belfast-Pownal Bay

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Belfast-Pownal Bay is a provincial electoral district for the Legislative assembly of Prince Edward Island, Canada.

[edit] Members

The riding as elected the following Members of the Legislative Assembly:

  Member Party Term
     Wilbur MacDonald Progressive Conservative 1996-present
