Hi, my nickname is Beltz. I am french-canadian and near fluent in english. I occasionally try to fix typos or formatting errors in new articles. I especially make efforts to remove bias.

[edit] Contributions

  • An article on the anime tv-series database website, animenfo.com.
  • An article on the videogame Rebelstar: Tactical Command. It's a videogame from the same mind who created the legendary X-COM series.
  • An article on Limnic Eruptions, an extremely rare natural disaster that has killed over 1800 people in it's two observed occurences.
