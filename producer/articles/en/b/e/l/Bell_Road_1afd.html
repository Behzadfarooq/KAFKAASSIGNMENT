Bell Road is the busiest arterial road in the city of Phoenix, Arizona. Bell Road runs east-west through northern Phoenix connecting the suburbs of Glendale and Scottsdale (where Bell is known as Frank Lloyd Wright Boulevard). Bell Road intersects several freeways that serve the Phoenix metropolitan area including (from east to west) Arizona State Route 101, Interstate 17, and Arizona State Route 51. Several other notable routes intersect Bell Road such as U.S. Route 60 and Arizona State Route 303 which both meet Bell Road in Surprise northwest of Phoenix.
