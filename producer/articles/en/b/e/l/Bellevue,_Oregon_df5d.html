Bellevue is an unincorporated, rural community in Yamhill County, Oregon, United States. It is named for the French words for "beautiful view". Oregon Geographic Names notes that it is not known who named the community, but it was settled in about 1860, and a previous post office in the area was named "Muddy". Bellevue post office operated from 1869 to 1904.

[edit] Geography

Bellevue is located on Oregon State Route 18, eight miles south of McMinnville and four miles north of Sheridan. Bellevue is also the western terminus of the Bellevue-Hopewell Highway.
