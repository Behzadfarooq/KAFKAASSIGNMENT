Belleville (French, literally "beautiful town") is the name of several places in the world.

[edit] North America

Belleville is the name of several places in North America:

  • Belleville, Ontario, Canada
  • Belleville, Arkansas, United States of America
  • Belleville, Illinois, United States of America
  • Belleville, Kansas, United States of America
  • Belleville, Michigan, United States of America
  • Belleville, New Jersey, United States of America
  • Belleville, New York, United States of America
  • Belleville, Pennsylvania, United States of America

There are also a number of places named Bellville in the United States of America, with a different spelling.

[edit] Europe

Belleville is the name or part of the name of several communes in France:

  • Belleville, in the Meurthe-et-Moselle département
  • Belleville, in the Rhône département
  • Belleville, in the Deux-Sèvres département
  • Belleville-en-Caux, in the Seine-Maritime département
  • Belleville-et-Châtillon-sur-Bar, in the Ardennes département
  • Belleville-sur-Loire, in the Cher département
  • Belleville-sur-Mer, in the Seine-Maritime département
  • Belleville-sur-Meuse, in the Meuse département
  • Belleville-sur-Vie, in the Vendée département
  • St-Martin-de-Belleville, in the Savoie département
  • Belleville is also the name of a neighbourhood of Paris anciently the center of an independent commune (see Belleville (commune)) annexed by the City of Paris in 1860

[edit] Other

  • Belleville (fictional city) is also a fictional and satirical city in North America in the French animated movie Les Triplettes de Belleville, aka Belleville Rendez-vous, possibly named after the song "Belleville" by Django Reinhardt.
  • Belleville can also refer to a type of spring, called Belleville washer
  • Belleville Shoe A boot manufacturer and the main supplier of boots to the US Armed Forces.
