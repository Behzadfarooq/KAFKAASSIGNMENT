Bell, Oklahoma

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Bell, Oklahoma, could refer to:

  • Bell, an extinct post office in Le Flore County, Oklahoma. The post office existed from April 13, 1891, until September 23, 1897. It was named for George W. Bell, a judge for Jack's Fork County, Choctaw Nation.[1]
  • Bell, a loosely settled unincorported community built up around the rural Bell Elementary School in Adair County, Oklahoma. (see Bell School District (Oklahoma).[2][3]
  • Bell CDP (Oklahoma), a statistical entity established by the US Census Bureau in Adair County, Oklahoma.[4]
