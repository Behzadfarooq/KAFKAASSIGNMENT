From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

< Portal:Belgium | Anniversaries | February
  • 1370: death of Jean Le Bel, chronicler
  • 1775: death of Peter Dens, Roman Catholic theologian
  • 1809: birth of André Dumont, geologist
  • 1847: death of Germinal Pierre Dandelin, French-Dutch-Belgian mathematician and engineer
  • 1849: death of Pierre François Verhulst, mathematician
  • 1913: birth of Willy Vandersteen, comics artist, best known for Spike and Suzy and De Rode Ridder
  • 1961: crash of Sabena Flight 548, killing all 72 persons on board and one on the ground
  • 1968: birth of Axelle Red, singer
  • 1978: birth of Tia Hellebaut, European high jump champion in 2006
  • 2002: foundation of SN Brussels Airlines, successor of DAT
