The Belgian Medical Informatics Association or Belgian Society for Medical Informatics is a Belgian non-profit organization, which was established in 1974, to promote and develop medical information science and technology in Belgium.

As a scientific society, the focus of its activities is on improving communication among researchers and developers in the field of medical computing and telematics. Within the organization, the role of medical informatics in society and its ethical aspects are also discussed.
