Beloyarsky (Russian: Белоярский) is a town in Khanty-Mansi Autonomous Okrug in Russia, located on the Kazim River (Ob's tributary), northwest of Khanty-Mansiysk. Population: 19,500 (2005 est.)

Economy of the town is based on oil and natural gas extraction.
