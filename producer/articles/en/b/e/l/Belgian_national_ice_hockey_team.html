Belgian national ice hockey team

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Belgian national ice hockey team has participated in the IIHF European Championships from 1910 to 1932. They have won 2 bronze medals (1910, 1911), 1 silver medal (1927), and 1 gold medal (1913).

  • First match: March 4, 1905, Brussels, Belgium. Belgium 3-0 France
  • Largest victory: March 21, 1989, Geel, Belgium. Belgium 21-2 New Zealand
  • Largest defeat: March 13, 1993, Bled, Slovenia. Ukraine 37-2 Belgium

Source: International Hockey Forums
