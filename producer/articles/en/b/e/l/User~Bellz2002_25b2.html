Caveman Style is an amateur English football club created by co-workers at Fujitsu-Siemens in Sheffield.

The club currently operates from within a Goals Soccer Centre located in the north side of Sheffield although there are rumours to break away from this and start in a district league, but as of March 30 2007 no solid foundations have been put into place.

The club was founded in the spring of 2006 by three employees of Fujitsu-Siemens in order to have a extra-curricular activity outside of work. Within one week of the idea being fronted, a strip had been bought, a logo had been designed (there were four initially drafted), a squad of twelve had been assembled and entry into the Tuesday league was finalised.
