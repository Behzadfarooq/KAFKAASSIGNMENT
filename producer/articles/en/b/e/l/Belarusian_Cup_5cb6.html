Belarusian Cup

The Belarusian Cup (Belarusian: Кубак Беларусі) is the main "knockout" cup competition in Belarusian football.

The competition is a knockout (single elimination) tournament.


[edit] Finals

  • 1992 - F. C. Dinamo Minsk 6-1 FC Dnepr Mahilyow
  • 1993 - FC Neman Grodno 2-1 Vedrich Rechitsa
  • 1994 - F. C. Dinamo Minsk 3-1 Fandok Babruysk
  • 1995 - Dinamo-93 Minsk 1-1 Tarpeda Mahilyou (aet, 7-6 pen)
  • 1996 - MPKC Mazyr 4-1 F. C. Dinamo Minsk
  • 1997 - FC Belshyna Babruisk 2-0 Dinamo-93 Minsk
  • 1998 - FC Lakamatyu Vitsebsk 2-1 F. C. Dinamo Minsk (aet)
  • 1999 - FC Belshyna Babruisk 1-1 FC Slavia Mozyr (aet, 4-2 pen)
  • 2000 - FC Slavia Mozyr 2-1 Tarpeda-MAZ Minsk
  • 2001 - FC Belshyna Babruisk 1-0 FC Slavia Mozyr (asdet)
  • 2002 - FC Gomel 2-0 FC BATE
  • 2003 - F. C. Dinamo Minsk 2-0 FC Lakamatyu Minsk
  • 2004 - FC Shakhtyor 1-0 FC Gomel (aet)
  • 2005 - FC MTZ-RIPO 2-1 FC BATE
  • 2006 - FC BATE 3-1 FC Shakhtyor (aet)
