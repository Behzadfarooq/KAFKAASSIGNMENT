The Belmeken dam lake

The Belmeken (Bulgarian - Белмекен ) dam lake is situated in the Rila Mountains and is the highest dam lake on the Balkan pensinsula. There is a Steam Accumulative Hydroelectric Power Plant built on the lake (SAWPP) (Bulgarian : ПАВЕЦ). It is part of the huge cascade Belmeken-Sestrimo-Chaira. Part of the water used in the SAWPP (ПАВЕЦ) is returned by its pumps, while the rest is sent through tunnels in the Sestriomo WPP (Water Power Plant). From there it flows again through tunnels to the third and last dam with SAWPP - Chaira. The construction of a forth dam, Iadenitsa, is in progress and will be finished in the coming years.
