Belpre Township is one of the twenty-two townships of Washington County, Ohio, United States. No other township in Ohio is named Belpre Township.

It borders the following townships:

  • Dunham Township: north
  • Decatur Township: west
  • Troy Township, Athens County: southwest

To the south and east, Belpre Township borders West Virginia across the Ohio River.
