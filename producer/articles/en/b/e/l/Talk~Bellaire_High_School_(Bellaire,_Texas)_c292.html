Bellaire Class of 2006 College Admissions

Here is the latest college admissions information (those who got accepted into the top 20 world universities based on The Times Higher Education Supplement and/or the top 10 America's Best Colleges based on the U.S. News and World Report--from the 2006 rankings) obtained from a college survey from Bellaire High School senior Class of 2006:

9 were admitted to Berkeley, 1 was admitted to Caltech, 2 were admitted to Columbia, 14 were admitted to Cornell, 2 were admitted to Dartmouth, 11 were admitted to Duke, 1 was admitted to Harvard, 1 was admitted to MIT, 1 was admitted to Oxford, 5 were admitted to Princeton, 6 were admitted to Stanford, 6 were admitted to U Chicago, 7 were admitted to U Penn, and 2 were admitted to Yale.

The total scholarship (including financial aid packages) received by these students from these universities was around $1,274,000 US Dollar. ...above information was provided by 510077401, a PTO member of Bellaire High School
