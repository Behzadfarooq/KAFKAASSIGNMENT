Belleville High School is a four-year comprehensive community public high school that serves students in ninth through twelfth grade from Belleville, in Essex County, New Jersey, United States, as part of the Belleville School District.

[edit] Notable alumni

  • Belleville is the hometown of the band My Chemical Romance. Band members Gerard Way (vocals) and his brother, Mikey Way (bass) attended Belleville High School.[1]
