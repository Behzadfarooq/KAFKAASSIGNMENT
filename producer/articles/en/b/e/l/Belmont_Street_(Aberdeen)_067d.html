Belmont Street Farmers Market

Belmont Street is a north-south street in the centre of Aberdeen, Scotland.

Belmont Street runs perpendicular to Union Street and is known for its vibrant nightlife. The street is also host to various shops, restaurants and a cinema.

There is a farmers market, Aberdeen Country Fair held on the last Saturday of every month.[1]
