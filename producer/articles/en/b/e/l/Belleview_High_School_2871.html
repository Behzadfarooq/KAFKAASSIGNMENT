Belleview High School is a school in Marion County, Florida. It serves the Belleview area. The school mascot is the Diamondback Rattler, and the school colors are blue, silver, and white. The school offers the AICE [1] (Advanced International Certificate of Education) program. The eighty acre campus boasts a stadium with seating for 2,000 people, and a Gymnasium capable of holding 1,500 fans. The school also has a large Auditorium and six large, two story buildings.[2]
