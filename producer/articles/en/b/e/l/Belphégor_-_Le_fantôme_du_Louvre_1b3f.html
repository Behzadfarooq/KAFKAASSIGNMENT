Belphégor - Le fantôme du Louvre ("Belphegor, Ghost of the Louvre") is a 2001 French feature film directed by Jean-Paul Salomé. It is based on a novel by Arthur Bernède.

[edit] Cast

  • Sophie Marceau - Lisa/Belphégor
  • Michel Serrault - Verlac
  • Frédéric Diefenthal - Martin
  • Julie Christie - Glenda Spender
  • Jean-François Balmer - Bertrand Faussier
