Belboon is commonly known as an advertising network or affiliate network which originates from Berlin in Germany. Commonly advertising that is served from a belboon.de domain name is a result of an advertising partner offering rewards to publisher/affiliate sites in return for a view, click, sale or lead. This is otherwise referred to as affiliate marketing

All advertising media from belboon is served on the basis that should a user click on the served media and then continue to purchase and item or create a lead then the affiliate or referring partner will be paid a pre agreed sum with the affiliate partner, using belboon as the middle man or service to track such relationship.

Belboon is an online marketing service of YOC AG, a pan european mobile phone marketing company. Belboon is seated within a 6.5 billion pound affiliate industry. Belboon at the time of this wikipedia documents creation facilitates advertising media distribution online for more than 100 mainly german advertisers on more than 5000 german portals and web communities.

[edit] Privacy

The Belboon adserving network serve localised cookies which allows them to facilitate tracking of the users actions on any of the publisher sites and merchants sale or lead confirmation pages. Whilst the cookies are placed in the users browser there is no real threat to privacy as the cookie contains nothing more than click information such as time, date and the merchant or advertisers site that you visit.
