Bellonda is a fictional character from the Dune universe created by Frank Herbert. She is a Reverend Mother and the chief Mentat-Archivist councillor and a Watchdog of Mother Superior Taraza. She is a main character in Heretics of Dune, Chapterhouse Dune, and Hunters of Dune.

[edit] Heretics of Dune

[edit] Chapterhouse Dune

In Chapterhouse Dune, during a conversation with the Duncan Idaho ghola it is revealed that Bellonda is a descendant of Anteac, an important Reverend Mother from the time of the God Emperor Leto II.

Bellonda then retrieves the following information about the origins of Mentats from Other Memory:

Order of Mentats, founded by Gilbertus Albans; temporary sanctuary with Bene Tleilax who hoped to incorporate them into Tleilaxu hegemony; spread into uncounted "seed schools"; suppressed by Leto II because they formed a nucleus of independent opposition; spread into the the Scattering after the Famine.

[edit] Hunters of Dune

Bellonda is one of the few Bene Gesserits with access to sensitive breeding Records. Furthermore, only she, Taraza, Hesterion, Odrade, Murbella, Sheeana, and Doria have the memories of all the Mother Superiors. [1] [2]

Bellonda suspects the Honored Matres were originally Revered Mothers sent out in the Scattering. She suspects that melange withdrawal and hypnosis caused the Honored Matres to deny their origins.

Thirteen years after the no-ship Ithaca escaped from Chapterhouse, Bellonda was killed in a duel by her Spice Operations Director partner and Murbella's former Honored Matres assistant Doria. Murbella was outraged and forced Doria to Share with Bellonda and made her the sole Spice Operations Director. [3]
