Bellavista is a rock band from San Francisco, California, comprised of Rex Shelverton, Jeremy Bringetto and Cary LaScala, all childhood friends who grew up around Half Moon Bay, in Northern California. Bellavista are otherwise remembered as three of the final members of The Vue.

The Vue formed in the late 1990’s and released two albums on Sub Pop and an EP on RCA. Between 2001 and 2004, Vue performed with The Rolling Stones, Black Rebel Motorcycle Club, Franz Ferdinand, The Faint and …The Trail Of The Dead. At the end of 2004, the band went on hiatus, returning as Bellavista in 2006.

Bellavista’s self-titled, self-produced debut is scheduled for release on Take Root Records in May ’07.
