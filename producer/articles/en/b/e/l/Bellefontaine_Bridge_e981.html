The Bellefontaine Bridge is a four-span truss BNSF railroad bridge over the Missouri River between St. Charles County, Missouri and St. Louis County, Missouri.

It has four 440 foot spans. Construction started on July 4, 1892 and it opened on December 27, 1893.
