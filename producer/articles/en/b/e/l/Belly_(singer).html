Belly (singer)

Belly, also known as Rebellyus, is a Canadian Palestinian-born rapper and hip hop artist. Belly was born in Palestine on April 7, 1984, and grew up surrounded by war and violence. He shortly after moved to Ottawa, Ontario, Canada where he currently resides. So far, Belly has two chart topping singles, one of them being labelmate Massari's "Rush the floor", and the other Pressure which featured Ginuwine. An older video, which did not chart at all, called Getaway feat. Massari was released from an EP the duo released as The Capital Prophets. Also, he has released three successful mixtapes (Death Before Dishonour, Vol.1 Kool Kid, Vol.2 DJ Kay Slay, Vol.3 DJ Big Mike). His music has an Arabic feel to it, and in it he speaks about "the things going on in [his] life". The music video of Pressure features Brooke Hogan and Hulk Hogan. Pressure reached no.18 on the charts.[citation needed] He has also been featured in Massari's "Don't let go".

Belly is currently signed with Capital Prophets records. Beat Merchant is the producer on his first single Pressure. His upcoming album, The Revolution, is expected in the near future.
