Bellingwedde (pronunciation (help·info)) is a municipality in the northeastern Netherlands.


[edit] Population centres

Bellingwolde, Blijham, Klein-Ulsda, Oudeschans, Rhederbrug, Veelerveen, Vriescheloo, Wedde and Wedderveer.
