The BEL20 is the benchmark index of Euronext Brussels. Since March 1, 2007 it has contained the following companies[1] [2]:

  • Ackermans & van Haaren (weight=1.14%) - industrials
  • Agfa-Gevaert (weight=1.93%) - technology
  • Bekaert (weight=0.95%) - engineering
  • Belgacom (weight=4.21%) - telecom
  • Cofinimmo (weight=0.94%) - real estate
  • Colruyt (weight=2.00%) - retail
  • Delhaize Group (weight=4.71%) - retail
  • Dexia (weight=11.07%) - finance
  • Fortis (weight=15.00%) - finance
  • Groupe Bruxelles Lambert (weight=5.71%) - holding
  • InBev (weight=8.27%) - brewery
  • KBC Groep (weight=13.18%) - finance
  • Mobistar (weight=1.59%) - telecom
  • NPM/CNP (weight=1.50%) - finance
  • Omega Pharma (weight=0.84%) - pharmaceutical
  • Solvay (weight=5.33%) - chemistry and pharma
  • Suez (weight=15.00%) - utilities
  • UCB (weight=4.01%) - chemistry and pharma
  • Umicore (weight=2.60%) - basic resources
