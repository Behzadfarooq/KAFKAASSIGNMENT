Portal:Belgium/Anniversaries/January/January 10

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

< Portal:Belgium | Anniversaries | January
  • 1480: birth of Margarete of Austria, second Governor of the Habsburg Netherlands
  • 1635: birth of Alexander Farnese, Prince of Parma, Governor of the Habsburg Netherlands
  • 1664: death of Antonius Sanderus, historian, author of Flandria Illustrata (other sources give January 16 as the date)
  • 1882: birth of Eugène Joseph Delporte, astronomer, discovered 66 asteroids including 1221 Amor
  • 1929: beginning of the first of The Adventures of Tintin by Hergé in Le Petit Vingtième: Tintin in the Land of the Soviets
  • 2005: death of Jan Pieter Schotte, Roman Catholic cardinal and official of the Roman Curia
