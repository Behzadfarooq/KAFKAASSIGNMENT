Beldgabred

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

You have new messages (last change).

Beldgabred was a legendary king of the Britons as accounted by Geoffrey of Monmouth. He was preceded by Sisillius III and succeeded by his brother Archmail. It is reported that Beldgabred surpassed all other musicians on every kind of instrument. He was claimed by many to be the god of minstrels.
