Belokurikha (Russian: Белоку́риха) is a town and a balneological resort in Altai Krai, Russia, located on the Bolshaya Belokurikha River some 250 km south of Barnaul. Population: 14,700 (2005); 14,533 (2002 Census); 5,700 (1968).
