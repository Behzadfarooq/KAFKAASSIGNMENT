Belle may refer to:

  • Southern belle, a refined woman (from the French belle, "beautiful")
    • Southern Belle (KCS), a passenger train operated by the Kansas City Southern Railway
    • Southern Belle (LBSC), a passenger train operated by the London, Brighton and South Coast Railway
    • Southern Belle (game), a computer game
  • Belle, Missouri, a town in the United States
  • Belle (chess machine), an early chess computer
  • Belle (Peanuts), is character in the Peanuts comic strip
  • Belle de jour, a 1967 French film starring Catherine Deneuve
  • Celebration Belle, a riverboat
  • Belle Gunness (1859-c1931), a Norwegian-American female serial killer
  • Memphis Belle (B-17), a World War II bomber, or one of two films about her:
    • Memphis Belle: A Story of a Flying Fortress, a 1944 documentary film about the real aircraft and crew on its final mission
    • Memphis Belle (film), a 1990 fictionalized film inspired by the 1944 documentary
  • Belle of Louisville, a restored Ohio/Mississippi River sternwheeler riverboat
  • Northern Belle, a ship that ran aground near London in 1857
  • Belle (Disney), the fictional heroine of the 1991 animated film Beauty and the Beast
  • Belle (magazine), an Australian design magazine

Belle may also refer to:

  • Bailleul, Nord, a town in France which original Dutch name was Belle
  • Belle Isle, one of several places
  • Belle et Sébastien, a French children's story
  • Belle & Sebastian, Scottish pop group named after the story
  • Belle Experiment, a particle accelerator experiment
  • Belle, a slang word in the RBAP online role-playing game
