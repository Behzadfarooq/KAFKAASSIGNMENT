Belvedere (ETS)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Belvedere Station was the original northern terminus of the LRT (route 201) line which opened April 22, 1978 in Edmonton, Alberta, Canada. It is located approximately at Fort Road and 129 Avenue, between Clareview Station and Coliseum Station.
