Beeliar, Western Australia

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Beeliar is a suburb of Perth, Western Australia, located within the City of Cockburn. Its postcode is 6164. The name refers to the Beeliar people, a tribe of Indigenous Australians who had land usage rights over the southern half of Perth's metropolitan area.
