Beeson Carroll

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Beeson Carroll is a tall, dark haired actor with about 20 credits to his name. He has not been seen for some time, since the 1998 film Rounders, where he played Judge Kaplan.

He is the first actor to play Donald Penobscot, Major Margaret "Hot Lips" Houlihan's first husband on the TV series M*A*S*H, but was not available for the character's second appearance, and lost the role to former Tarzan and football player Mike Henry.
