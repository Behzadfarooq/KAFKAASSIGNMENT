Bee may refer to:

  • Bee, a monophyletic group in the insect superfamily Apoidea
  • Honeybee which is kept for Honey
  • Bees figure more prominently in myth than any other insect: see Bee (mythology).
  • Bee (gathering), a social gathering of people for work or competition
  • Bee (VB), a commune in northern Italy
  • Another word for the nautical term, bee block
  • The English spelling of the letter B
  • Jaymz Bee, a Canadian musician, writer and radio personality
  • Several rock bands by the name of The Bees
  • An acronym for Black Economic Empowerment a South African government programme to redress past inequalities
  • In England, Brentford Football Club is known as The Bees.
  • The Bee (magazine) was a literary magazine started 1759.
  • The Bee (radio station) is a radio station based in the English town of Blackburn.
  • Any of three The McClatchy Company newspapers in California: The Sacramento Bee, The Fresno Bee, or The Modesto Bee
  • Bee, a brand of playing card manufactured by the United States Playing Card Company
  • Crazy Bee, a fictional character featured in the Chinese horror film Bio Zombie
