Beedles Station, Ohio

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

You have new messages (last change).

Beedles Station was the first settlement in what was to become Warren County, Ohio. A blockhouse was established there in 1795 in what is now Section 28, Town 4, Range 3 of the Between the Miami Rivers Survey in western Turtlecreek Township. This is on the west side of State Route 741 about a mile south of Otterbein.
