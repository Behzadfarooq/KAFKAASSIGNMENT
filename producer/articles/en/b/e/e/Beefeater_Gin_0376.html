Beefeater Gin is a brand of gin bottled, and distributed in the United Kingdom, by the company of James Burrough. It is a 40% alcohol product (80 proof) in the UK, and a 47% alcohol product (94 proof) in the US. This gin is distilled from "100% grain spirit". The bottle bears an image of a Yeomen Warder. The manufacturer has been in business since 1820 and is located in London, England.
