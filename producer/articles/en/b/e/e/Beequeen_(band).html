Beequeen (band)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

You have new messages (last change).

Beequeen is a collaboration between Dutch musicians Freek Kinkelaar and Frans de Waard, who work primarily in drone and dream pop/ambient music. They have released material on prominent independent labels such as Korm Plastics, Staalplaat, Ant-Zen, RRRecords, and Mille Plateaux.
