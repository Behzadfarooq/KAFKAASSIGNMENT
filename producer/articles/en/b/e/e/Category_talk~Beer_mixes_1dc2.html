[edit] Merge and redirect to Category:Cocktails with beer

The two categories are the same. In addition, "Beer mix" and "beer mixes" are terms used in the brewing industry - see [1]. Creating a category with the name Beer mix is thus misleading.

The term cocktail mean a mix - such as a fruit cocktail. The category "Cocktails with beer" contains articles which are about cocktails with beer. SilkTork 02:21, 21 January 2007 (UTC)

I would add that I was against having having mixed drinks not involving a spirit/distilled beverage categorised along with normal cocktails: Wikipedia_talk:WikiProject Mixed Drinks/Archive2006#Sorting_out_what_should_be_included_under_.22Cocktails.22 SilkTork 03:13, 21 January 2007 (UTC)
