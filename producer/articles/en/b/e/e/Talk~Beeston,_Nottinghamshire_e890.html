I believe the Humber Works was in operation way after 1907. My father worked there as a young man and he was born in 1902. He eventually moved to Coventry with them but I think that was in the 20's. He returned to Beeston to run Wood's Greengrocery store on Queens Road for 45 years. (Leslie Wood)
