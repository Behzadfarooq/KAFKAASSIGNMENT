[edit] Strasbourg ISU day & Yuri's Night 2006 Planning

12th of April is celebrated around the world as a special day dedicated to the spirit and adventure of space travel and activities. Yuri's Night events every year map an orbiting party in honour of Yuri Gagarin, the 1st Space Shuttle flight and the 12th is also the founding day of ISU.

This year the celebrations are coming to Strasbourg, France! The preparations are underway and the aim of this article is to update the Local Organizing Committee on the progress and status but also log the lessons learnt/better approaches for a bigger version of the celebrations in the coming years.

Hello Bee!--Beex 15:16, 22 March 2006 (UTC) [Paul Reilly]

[edit] 2006 Events

The current plan is as follows:

11th April Afternoon - Open event in town for children, enthusiasts other interested people. --> See point (1)

12th April Afternoon (4/5 pm) - ISU Day celebrations: BBQ at ISU for students/families and staff. --> (2)

12th April Evening (10pm onwards) - Yuri's night club party - at Club JAVA or similar venue - including DJ sets, live music (volunteers from ISU?) or any other famous act? As a warm up: I have a yuri's night special game ready. --> (3)


Why is this a multi-event celebration?

  • In line with the motto and agenda of Space Generation and Yuri's night, involving the public outside the space community is essential in this celebration of human space flight.
  • This would help gain a similar support as 'physics on stage' the Students who attended this event were extremely interested and motivated by the talks and demonstrations given.
  • Many of our Masters students have kids and families this year. Such committments may mean that a club party is not possible for them to attend. Hence, a BBQ would give a good chance for them and their families (we are going to input fun things for kids too!)
  • Club celebrations are the traditional way of celebrating yuri's night, these would be weblinked and broadcasted across all the parties to make a non-stop world space party.
