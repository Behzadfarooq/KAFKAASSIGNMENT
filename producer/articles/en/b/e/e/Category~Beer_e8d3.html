Welcome to Wikipedia's Beer Category.

If you are looking for a beer, a brand, or a brewery, or for information about beer in a particular country, try looking in Category:Beer and breweries by region which is categorised into regions such as Category:Beer and breweries in Europe, which are further broken down into smaller regions such as Category:Beer and breweries in Germany.

Articles on the different types or styles of beer, such as IPA, Lager, Stout and Lambic can be found in Category:Beer styles.

If you are looking for information on brewing, including homebrewing, different types of breweries, equipment, containers, and beer ingredients such as hops and malt, look in Category:Brewing.

If you are looking for information on the culture connected to beer such as glassware, festivals, beer organisations, or rating beer, look in Category:Beer culture. General articles on drinking can be found in Category:Drinking culture.

Information on bars, pubs and beer cafes can be found in Category:Drinking establishments.

Miscellaneous unclassified beer articles can be found below.

Questions may be asked at Wikipedia talk:WikiProject Beer.
