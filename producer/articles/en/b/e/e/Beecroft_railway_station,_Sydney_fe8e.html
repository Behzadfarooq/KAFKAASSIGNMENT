Beecroft is a CityRail railway station on Sydney's Northern Line. It serves the suburb of Beecroft, a residential area. Beecroft has an island platform in the middle of a reverse curve, and is located on a steep 2.5% gradient.

[edit] History

Beecroft station was opened on September 17, 1886[1]. The original Beecroft station was located futher south of the existing platform, approximately adjacent to the Beecroft tennis courts. The disused down platform was demolished in the early 1990s.

Beecroft station in its existing location was opened on March 7, 1892. A disused dock platform for produce is located opposite the down platform.

Currently, the station is accessed via an underpass and staircase. The ticket office used to be located in the underpass at the foot of the staircase, but was relocated onto the platform.

[edit] Accessibility

The station is accessed via an underpass and staircase. There is no elevator, thus no Easy Access for wheelchairs.
