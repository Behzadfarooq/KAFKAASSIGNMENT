Beechcroft High School is a 4 year high school (grades 9-12) located on the north side of Columbus, Ohio. It is a part of Columbus Public Schools. Beechcroft was first opened in 1976 as a six year junior/senior high school.

Beechcroft is known to some as "Croft". The school colors are Gold and Brown. There is some discussion of plans to change the school colors to Black and Gold. The school nickname is the Cougars.

[edit] Sports and activities

The football program at Beechcroft is one of the best the Columbus City League. Beechcroft has reached the playoffs 12 times in football, including a 5 season run from 1999-2003.

In 2004 the Beechcroft football team went 9-1, but did not reach the playoffs. This was a disappointing end to the season. The only loss was to Brookhaven High School, who finished 15-0 and won the Division 2 State Championship. With Beechcroft in Division 3, many thought they would have won the state title in Division if they had made the playoffs.

In 2005 Beechcroft made it's return to the playoffs after an 8-2 regular season, losing to Brookhaven and Licking Valley (Newark). The team faced Canfield HS in the opening round. Losing 28-0.
