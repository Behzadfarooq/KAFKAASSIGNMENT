Bee Hives is a 2004 album by Broken Social Scene. It is a collection of B-sides and rarities.

[edit] Track listing

  1. "(untitled)" – 0:37
  2. "Market Fresh" – 3:57
  3. "Weddings" – 7:02
  4. "hHallmark" – 3:53
  5. "Backyards" – 8:14
  6. "Da Da Dada" – 7:09
  7. "Ambulance for the Ambiance" – 5:18
  8. "Time = Cause" – 5:07
  9. "Lover's Spit" – 7:34

Note that "hHallmark" is not a typographical error. The extra h is an allusion to Brendan Canning's prior band, hHead. Also, Leslie Feist contributed vocals to the band's signature single "Lovers' Spit" as a B-side.
