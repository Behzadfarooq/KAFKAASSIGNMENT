Beechbone in Peter Jackson's The Two Towers

Beechbone is a fictional character in J. R. R. Tolkien's The Lord of the Rings. He is an Ent who died in the Fall of Isengard when he was lit on fire and burned alive. In Peter Jackson's film adaptation, an Ent presumed to be Beechbone is briefly seen extinguishing himself in the flood that engulfs Isengard and therefore is presumed to be alive; unlike the Beechbone of the book.
