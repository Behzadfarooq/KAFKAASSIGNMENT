[edit] Plot

Spoiler warning: Plot and/or ending details follow.

Deep in South America, an earthquake awakens a giant robot beetle - a robot whose sole purpose is to prepare the earth for his people, a race of alien insectoids. Joinedby by Bea, Max finds himself hard-pressed to distract the single-minded servant, at least until he realizes the link between the robot and the insects of Earth.
