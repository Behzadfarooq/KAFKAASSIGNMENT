"Been Gone" is the first single from Canadian R&B/Pop singer Keshia Chanté's second studio album "2U". The single was written by Chanté and produced by Philadelphia production company The Matrax. The music video premiered on MuchMusic at 5:00pm (EST) on September 13th, 2006. The song features a sped up section of Antonio Vivaldi's "Winter" which plays throughout most of the song. It peaked at 10 on the Canadian BDS Radio Airplay chart.
