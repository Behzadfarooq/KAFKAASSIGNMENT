[edit] Romanization

The Japanese guide book has the romanization diffrent, ie Kissu=Kiss, Slade=Sled, Shagi=Shaggy, Gurineed=Grinide. According to the Wikipedia:Manual of Style (Japan-related articles) the romanization used by Viz should be the main romanization used in this article, but I think that we should also include the official Japanese romanization in the character profile for reference. [edit] Story Question

I've haven't read the manga, only seen some of the anime, and... aren't the Zenon Warriors destroyed in the very first chapter and give their Saigas to Beet? Beet creates the Beet Warriors group, joined by Poala and later other friends.

Yup, the Zenon Warriors are dying and so is Beet, so they combine their lifeforces and saigas so that Beet can live. The Zenon warriors then die. After the prolouge chapter, Beet leaves home and learns how to use five different saigas, the ones the Zenon Warriors had. It is supposed to be considered uncommon to have multiple saigas. And yes, later on he makes the Beet Warriors.

While the Zenon Warriors were destroyed as a team, the members of the team have been shown to be or hinted to be alive, as Cruss was found at one point and a character who may be Zenon has repeatedly appeared. The fates of the others are still in question, but presumed alive after the discovery of Cruss.
