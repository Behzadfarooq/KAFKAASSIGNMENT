Beechboro, Western Australia

Beechboro is a suburb of Perth, Western Australia, located within the City of Swan. Its postcode is 6063.

[edit] Community points of interest

[edit] Education

  • 3 public primary schools, Beechboro primary, East Beechboro primary and West Beechboro
  • 2 private primary school, Beechboro Christian School and John Septimus Roe Anglican Community School
  • Swan education district office

[edit] Leisure

  • several parks/reserves in Beechboro, these include Altone Park, Maguire Oval, Ottowa Park and many smaller parks.
  • 1 skate park located at Altone Park
  • an aquatic centre
  • 1 Scout hall and 1 Guides hall
  • 1 family centre
  • 2 community centres
  • Altone Park Golf Course
  • a recreation centre located at Altone Park
  • Bosnian centre

[edit] Religion

  • 4 churches
