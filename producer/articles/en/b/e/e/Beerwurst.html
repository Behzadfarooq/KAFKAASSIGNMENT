Beerwurst is a German cooked, smoked sausage with a garlicky flavor and dark red color. It is seasoned with black peppercorns, paprika, and mustard seeds for flavor.

Beerwurst is a very popular item in German dishes. It is usually sold as sandwich meat.

Fresh beerwurst will last for two days in the refrigerator. Precooked beerwurst will last for 5-7 days.
