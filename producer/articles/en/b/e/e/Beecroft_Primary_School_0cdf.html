Beecroft Primary School is a primary school located in Beecroft, Sydney, New South Wales, Australia. It was Established in 1897. It provides education to children from the Beecroft and Cheltenham area of North Western Sydney. The Principal is Jennie Ryan.

[edit] History

The original school was a single school room that taught up to 60 children by two teachers. That room is now named the "Heritage Room". An additional classroom was built in 1904, which is now used as the Dental Clinic. In 1926 a Headmaster's Office and a storeroom were also added. They are now used as a music room and a pottery room.

[edit] School uniforms

Beecroft requires its students to wear school uniforms [1] [2].
