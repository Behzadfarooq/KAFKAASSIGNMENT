Beetham (disambiguation)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Beetham may refer to:

  • Beetham, a village in the north of England.
  • Edward Beetham (1905-1979), one time Governor of Trinidad and Tobago
  • Beetham Highway, a major highway in Trinidad and Tobago named after Edward Beetham
  • Beetham Estate Gardens, a neighbourhood in eastern Port of Spain, Trinidad and Tobago near the Beetham Highway
  • Michael Beetham (1923-), Marshal of the Royal Air Force
  • Bruce Beetham (1936-1997), an academic and politician from New Zealand
