[edit] Bring Me The Horizon

Your comments at Wikipedia:Articles for deletion/Bring Me The Horizon about Arctic Monkeys are correct, but largely irrelevant to the AfD discussion on Bring Me The Horizon. The primary difference is that the Arctic Monkeys article was not noticed until the band gained notability while your band's article was created during a time of increased scrutiny due to other recent events. If the report that the band will release an album in Spring 2006 comes true, then you may request undeletion of the article at that time by visiting Wikipedia:Deletion review and providing a source (link to a news story on the band or some similar third-party reference) showing that the band has gained the level of notability needed by WP:MUSIC. --Allen3 talk 16:56, 6 December 2005 (UTC)

[edit] Test Switch Isolator

The article Test Switch Isolator has been speedily deleted from Wikipedia. This has been done because the article seems to be about a person, group of people, band, club, company, or web content, but it does not indicate how or why the subject is notable, that is, why an article about that subject should be included in Wikipedia. Under the criteria for speedy deletion, articles that do not assert notability may be deleted at any time. If you can indicate why the subject is really notable, you are free to re-create the article, making sure to cite any verifiable sources.

Please see the guidelines for what is generally accepted as notable, and for specific types of articles, you may want to check out our criteria for biographies, for web sites, for bands, or for companies. Feel free to leave a note on my talk page if you have any questions about this. -- Merope 16:57, 8 January 2007 (UTC)
