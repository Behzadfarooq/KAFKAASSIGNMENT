Beetham Tower is a name shared by several high-rise buildings in separate cities in the United Kingdom, financed and owned by the Beetham Organization. They are primarily residential buildings, but two also have hotel and conference facilities.

The towers and their respective cities are:

  • Beetham Tower, Manchester
  • Beetham Tower, Birmingham, also known as the 10 Holloway Circus
  • Beetham Tower, Liverpool
  • Beetham Tower West, Liverpool
  • Beetham Tower, Brighton
  • Beetham Tower, London

As of 2006, Liverpool's Beetham Tower West is under construction.
