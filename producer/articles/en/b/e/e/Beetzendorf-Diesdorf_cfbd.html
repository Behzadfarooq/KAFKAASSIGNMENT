Beetzendorf-Diesdorf

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Beetzendorf-Diesdorf is a Verwaltungsgemeinschaft ("collective municipality") in the Altmarkkreis Salzwedel (district), in Saxony-Anhalt, Germany. It is situated southwest of Salzwedel. The seat of the Verwaltungsgemeinschaft is in Beetzendorf.
