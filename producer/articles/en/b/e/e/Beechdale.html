Beechdale is a housing estate in Walsall, England, mostly developed during the 1950s.

The estate was served by Beechdale Primary School until its closure at the end of the 2005/06 school year with demolition of the premises began on January 24, 2007. Hatherton Lane Primary School became the only primary school in the area. The January 30 2007 edition of local newspaper, the Express and Star, reported that the land was to be redeveloped into luxury residential properties with the local council selling the site to a property developer for a reported cost in excess of Â£20 million.

Beechdale Football Club, the local football team, play and train on a football pitch within the estate.

Noddy Holder, the lead singer of Slade, grew up on the estate.
