[edit] Origins

The Beer Launching Fridge was a device created by John W. Cornwell, an electrical engineer who recently graduated from Duke University (Pratt '06). He is a member of Delta Kappa Epsilon (EP chapter at Duke University). After video of the device was released to the public in February of 2007 and featured on both YouTube and Metacafe, the Launcher and its creator became an overnight internet celebrity.

[edit] Operation

The device works by loading a beer from the refrigerator to an elevator leading outside of the fridge. From there, the beer is loaded to a catapult. The prototype device is controlled by a remote control which can determine and lock the speed, launch angle, and direction just by the press of a button. Then the catapult launches the beer to the target user on command.

[edit] Publicity

The beer launching fridge was covered on several major web sites include Slashdot and Digg.

It was also shown on the Colbert Report on the 13th of March, 2007.
