Beefeater is a chain of pub restaurants in the UK, owned by Whitbread plc.

[edit] History

Set up by then Whitbread Brewery in the 1970's, Beefeater has continued to expand throughout the UK. Originally famous for its Prawn Cocktails and Steaks - Beefeater became the place to go for any family occasion, a tradition that continues today

[edit] B2 or Beefeater 2

In the early part of the millennium, Whitbread PLC (Now a hospitality company, having sold the brewery in the late 90's) have invested Millions of pounds refurbishing all of its Beefeater outlets in a project known internally as "B2".

The refurbishment works involved ripping all Beefeater buildings back to their shells and starting again.

The new restaurants described by Whitbread as "Warm, Modern and Stylish" have been mostly successful and embraced by Loyal Beefeater Consumers - Main changes included introducing Chargrills to all outlets, and having open plan grill areas so guests can see their meals being prepared (one of the few exceptions is the Coldra Beefeater, Newport, South Wales - Where due to Building shape restrictions the Kitchen remained "Back Of House")

The "B2 Programme" will be completed by Mid-2007


[edit] Beefeater & Premier Travel Inn

Despite the success of the B2 Programme, Beefeater Restaurants not adjoined to a Premier Travel Inn (also owned and operated by Whitbread plc) simply weren’t performing. So in late 2006, Whitbread made the decision to sell off the majority of Stand-alone Beefeaters (Beefeaters where No Premier Travel Inn was present and had no potential for a new Build PTI). These were sold to rival Restaurant Company M&B. Since the sale the retained estates financial performance has improved vastly.

Whitbread have recently merged its Beefeater Brand with its Brewers Fayers Brand and while consumers will not see any change, behind the scenes both brands are now Run by Whitbread’s Pub/Restaurants department - this change has also seen Whitbread sell its Pizza Hut and TGI Fridays brands - With the idea in mind of simply concentrating on Beefeater, Brewers Fayre and its latest Country Dining Brands.
