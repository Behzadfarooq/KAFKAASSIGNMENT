[edit] Dealing with vandals of Stoke-on-Trent

Hello, I've noticed your quick and welcome work in reverting some recent vandalism of Stoke-on-Trent. I've put appropriate warnings on the user's talk page and then, following the procedure, reported the IP address to administrators who may take appropriate action. I wondered if you might like to consider doing this yourself when you deal with vandalism? If so, you need to go to Wikipedia:Administrator intervention against vandalism and read the advice in the big green box, and then make use of the appropriate warning templates (link given in the big green box) until the trigger level is reached.  DDS  talk 01:33, 19 November 2006 (UTC)
