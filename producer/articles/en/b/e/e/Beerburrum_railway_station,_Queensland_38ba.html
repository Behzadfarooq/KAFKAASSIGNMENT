Beerburrum Station is a railway station on the Nambour and Gympie North Line of South East Queensland, Australia. It is part of the QR CityTrain network.

The station is in Zone 10 of the TransLink integrated public transport system.

Beerburrum has a short platform and can only accommodate three cars at a time. Passengers wishing to use Beerburrum should always ensure that they are in the front three cars of any train.
